space capsule

noun
manned spacecraft for human transport
Pronunciation: / speɪs ˈkæpsəl / (AmE), / speɪs ˈkæpsjuːl / (BrE)
A spacecraft designed to carry people and support human life during travel in outer space, often returning safely to Earth.

Examples

A space capsule design
Engineers are testing new space capsules that could carry astronauts to Mars in the future.
The space capsule safely splashed down in the Pacific Ocean after re-entry.
The Apollo missions used a space capsule to transport astronauts to and from the Moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spacecraft - a vehicle designed for travel or operation in outer space
