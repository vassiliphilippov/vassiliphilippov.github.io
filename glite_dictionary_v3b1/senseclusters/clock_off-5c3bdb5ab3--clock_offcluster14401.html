clock off

verb
record leaving work time
Pronunciation: / klɑk ˈɔf / (AmE), / klɒk ˈɒf / (BrE)
to record the time when you leave work, usually by using a special machine

Examples

To clock off work
Don't forget to clock off before you leave the building.
I usually clock off at five o'clock every weekday.
She clocked off early to attend her daughter's school play.
