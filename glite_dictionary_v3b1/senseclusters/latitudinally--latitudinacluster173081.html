latitudinally

adverb
by latitude
Pronunciation: / ˌlætɪˈtuːdənəli / (AmE), / ˌlætɪˈtjuːdənəli / (BrE)
in a direction or manner relating to latitude or along lines of latitude

Examples

Change latitudinally
The temperature changes latitudinally as you move from the equator toward the poles.
Species diversity often varies latitudinally across different regions of the world.
The researchers mapped the data latitudinally to study climate patterns.

Related Terms
