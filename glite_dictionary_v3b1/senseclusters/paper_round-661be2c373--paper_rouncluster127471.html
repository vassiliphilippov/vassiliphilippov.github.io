paper round

noun
route for delivering newspapers
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpər raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə raʊnd / (BrE)
a regular route along which newspapers are delivered, typically by a young person as a part-time job

Examples

A paper round route
Delivering a paper round was his first job as a teenager.
He got up early every morning to complete his paper round before school.
She saved her money from her paper round to buy a bicycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paper route - the route taken to deliver newspapers (North American)
