man crush

noun
non-sexual admiration of another man
Pronunciation: / mæn krʌʃ / (AmE), / mæn krʌʃ / (BrE)
A strong but non-sexual admiration or affection that a man feels towards another man, often a celebrity or notable figure.

Examples

A man crush on actor
During the interview, Tom joked about his man crush on the famous director.
He admitted he had a man crush on his favorite football player.
It's pretty common for fans to develop a man crush on inspiring celebrities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bromance - a close but non-romantic friendship between two men
