Maya Lin

proper noun
American architect and sculptor
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪə lɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪə lɪn / (BrE)
(born 1959) An American sculptor and architect best known for designing the Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, D.C.
