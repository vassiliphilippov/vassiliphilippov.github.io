racialization

noun
process of assigning race
8.4
Pronunciation: /reɪ.ʃə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /reɪ.ʃə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of assigning racial identity or significance to people, groups, or things, often leading to social distinctions or discrimination

  • synonym: discrimination - unfair treatment based on group membership
  • synonym: racial identity - a person's identification with a particular racial group
  • synonym: stereotyping - the act of assigning generalized traits to a group
