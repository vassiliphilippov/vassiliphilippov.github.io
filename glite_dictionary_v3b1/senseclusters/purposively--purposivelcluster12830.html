purposively

adverb
with intention
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrpəsɪvli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is done with intention or for a specific purpose

Examples

To purposively choose
The researchers purposively chose participants who met specific criteria for the experiment.
She purposively avoided mentioning the topic during the meeting.
The decorations were purposively arranged to create a welcoming atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidentally - in a way that is not planned or intended
  • synonym: deliberately - in a careful and planned manner
  • synonym: intentionally - in a deliberate or planned way
