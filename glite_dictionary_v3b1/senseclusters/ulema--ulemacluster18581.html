ulema

noun
body of Muslim religious scholars
9.7
Pronunciation: / uːˈleɪmə / (AmE), / uːˈleɪmə / (BrE)
the body of Muslim scholars who are recognized as having specialist knowledge of Islamic sacred law and theology

Examples

The ulema scholars
He consulted with the ulema for guidance on religious matters.
The ulema played a crucial role in shaping the legal and educational systems of the Ottoman Empire.
Throughout history, the ulema have served as guardians of Islamic law and doctrine.
