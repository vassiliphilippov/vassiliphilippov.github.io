segregant

noun
result of separation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡrɪɡənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡrɪɡənt / (BrE)
an individual or substance that has resulted from a process of separation, especially in genetics or chemistry

Examples

A genetic segregant
Each segregant in the experiment showed a different genetic trait.
The scientist analyzed the chemical properties of each segregant after the reaction.
Researchers recorded the characteristics of every segregant produced by the cross.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mixture - a combination of different substances or things
  • synonym: offspring - an individual's child or descendant, especially in genetics
