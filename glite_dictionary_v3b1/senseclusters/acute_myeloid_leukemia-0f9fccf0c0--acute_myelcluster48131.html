A type of cancer of the blood and bone marrow characterized by an excess of immature white blood cells, most common in adolescents and young adults.

Examples

After being diagnosed with acute myeloid leukemia, she began treatment immediately.

Synonyms

    5.7 0.01/m

  • myeloblastic leukemia
    6.8 0.00/m
    myeloblastic leukemia shows an excess of myeloblasts in the bloodstream, while acute myeloid leukemia refers to the wider disease of immature myeloid white cells.
