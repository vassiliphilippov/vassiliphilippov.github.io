acute myeloid leukemia

noun
type of blood cancer
7.5 4.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkjuːt ˈmaɪ.ə.lɔɪd luː.ˈkiː.mi.ə/ /; / /ə.ˈkjuːt ˈmaɪ.ə.lɔɪd luːk.ˈiː.mi.ə/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkjuːt ˈmaɪ.ə.lɔɪd luː.ˈkiː.mi.ə/ /; / /ə.ˈkjuːt ˈmaɪ.ə.lɔɪd luːk.ˈiː.mi.ə/ (BrE)
A type of cancer of the blood and bone marrow characterized by an excess of immature white blood cells, most common in adolescents and young adults.

Examples

Acute myeloid leukemia diagnosis
After being diagnosed with acute myeloid leukemia, she began treatment immediately.
Researchers are looking for new therapies to combat acute myeloid leukemia in young adults.
Symptoms of acute myeloid leukemia often include fatigue, fever, and frequent infections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chronic myeloid leukemia - a slower-progressing cancer of bone marrow with excessive immature white blood cells
  • synonym: leukemia - cancer of blood-forming tissues, hindering the body's ability to fight infection
