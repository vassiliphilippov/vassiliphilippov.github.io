slate-colored junco

noun
North American grey-plumaged songbird
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsleɪt ˌkʌlɚd ˈdʒʌŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsleɪt ˌkʌləd ˈdʒʌŋkəʊ / (BrE)
a common North American songbird with mostly grey plumage and dark brown eyes, belonging to the genus Junco
