mound

noun
a pile or heap of objects
2.1 4.12/m
Pronunciation: / maÊŠnd / (AmE), / maÊŠnd / (BrE)
a heap or pile formed by gathering objects or substances on top of each other

Examples

A mound of sand
She placed a mound of clothes on the bed to sort through.
The children built a mound of sand at the beach.
There was a mound of paperwork on his desk after the holiday.

Synonyms

  • heap
    1.1 4.93/m
    a heap is a messy pile put down without order, while a mound is more shaped and often rounded or made of earth.
