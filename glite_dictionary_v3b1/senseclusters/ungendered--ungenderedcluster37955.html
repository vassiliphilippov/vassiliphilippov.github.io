ungendered

adjective
not linked to gender
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛndərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛndəd / (BrE)
not assigned, associated with, or specific to any gender

Examples

Ungendered clothing
The school introduced ungendered uniforms so all students could feel comfortable.
Some languages use ungendered pronouns to refer to people without specifying gender.
The organization aims to create an ungendered environment where everyone is treated equally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gender-neutral - not specific to any gender
  • antonym: gendered - associated with a specific gender
