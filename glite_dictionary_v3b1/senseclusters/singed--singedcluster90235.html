singed

adjective
slightly burned
3.7
Pronunciation: / sɪndʒd / (AmE), / sɪndʒd / (BrE)
slightly burned or scorched on the surface, usually without being completely destroyed by fire or heat

Examples

Singed edges
The singed paper smelled faintly of smoke but was still readable.
After standing too close to the campfire, his jacket sleeves were singed at the edges.
She noticed her hair was slightly singed after using the curling iron on high heat.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: charred - burned until blackened
  • synonym: scorched - burned the surface of something slightly
  • antonym: unburned - not burned or affected by fire
