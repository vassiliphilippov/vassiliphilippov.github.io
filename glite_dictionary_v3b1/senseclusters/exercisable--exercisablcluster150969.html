exercisable

adjective
able to be used
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈek.sər.saɪ.zə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈek.sə.saɪ.zə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be put into effect or used, especially referring to rights or options

Examples

Exercisable rights
The employee's stock options are exercisable after a certain period of time.
Only rights that are currently exercisable can be transferred to another party.
The contract states when the privileges become exercisable by the holder.
