musicless

adjective
without musical ability
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪkləs / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkləs / (BrE)
lacking musical ability, sense of rhythm, or appreciation for music

Examples

Musicless person
Although his family loved to sing, he felt completely musicless and avoided joining in.
Her musicless childhood made her appreciate concerts even more as an adult.
Some people are naturally gifted, while others remain musicless despite their efforts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: musical - having a talent for or appreciation of music
  • synonym: tuneless - not able to produce musical notes in tune

Senses in This Cluster
