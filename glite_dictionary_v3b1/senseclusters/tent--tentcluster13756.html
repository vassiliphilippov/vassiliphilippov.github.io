tent

verb
to cover like a tent
6.8
Pronunciation: / tɛnt / (AmE), / tɛnt / (BrE)
to cover, drape, or shape something so that it resembles a tent, often to protect or enclose it

Examples

To tent food
They tented the food with foil to keep it warm before serving.
The workers tented the house to protect it during fumigation.
She tented the blanket over the sleeping child to create a cozy space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drape - to cover or hang loosely with cloth
  • antonym: uncover - to remove a cover from something
