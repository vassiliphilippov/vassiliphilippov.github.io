sagittate

adjective
arrowhead-shaped (of leaves)
5.8 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsæ.dʒɪ.teɪt/ /; / /sə.ˈdʒɪ.teɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsæ.dʒɪ.teɪt/ /; / /sə.ˈdʒɪ.teɪt/ (BrE)
(of a leaf) shaped like an arrowhead, with a pointed tip and the two basal lobes flaring outward.

Examples

Sagittate leaf shape
Botanists often use the term sagittate when classifying certain aquatic plants.
The distinctive, sagittate leaves help to quickly identify this species in the wild.
The plant's leaves are sagittate, giving them a striking arrowhead appearance.
