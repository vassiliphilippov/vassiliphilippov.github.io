go-ahead

noun
readiness to start new ventures
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ əˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ əˌhɛd / (BrE)
Eagerness or readiness to start new projects or undertake bold new ventures.

Examples

Go-ahead spirit
She has the go-ahead needed to lead the team into new territory.
Their go-ahead attitude helped the company grow rapidly.
Without a little go-ahead, innovative ideas are rarely put into action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enterprise - willingness to initiate new projects
  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or initiative to undertake new ventures
