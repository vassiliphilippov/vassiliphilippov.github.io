virtuoso

adjective
showing exceptional skill or mastery
Pronunciation: / ˌvɝː.tʃuˈoʊ.soʊ / (AmE), / ˌvɜː.tʃuˈəʊ.səʊ / (BrE)
showing or revealing exceptional skill or mastery, especially in a particular field or activity

Examples

Virtuoso performance
He delivered a virtuoso speech that captivated everyone in the hall.
Her virtuoso performance on the piano stunned the entire audience.
The chef's virtuoso approach to cooking turned every meal into a work of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consummate - showing a high degree of skill or flair; complete or perfect
  • synonym: masterful - showing great skill or expertise
