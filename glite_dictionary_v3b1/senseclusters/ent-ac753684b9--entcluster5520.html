ENT

noun
ear, nose, throat doctor
3.5
Pronunciation: / iː ɛn tiː / (AmE), / iː ɛn tiː / (BrE)
a medical specialist who diagnoses and treats diseases of the ear, nose, and throat; an otolaryngologist

Examples

ENT doctor
She was referred to an ENT after experiencing chronic sinus infections.
The ENT performed a minor surgery to correct his nasal problem.
If you have persistent throat pain, you should see an ENT for evaluation.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: audiologist - a specialist in hearing disorders
  • synonym: otolaryngologist - a doctor specializing in diseases of the ear, nose, and throat
