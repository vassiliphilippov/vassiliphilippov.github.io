raise an eyebrow

verb
express surprise or skepticism
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪz ən ˈaɪˌbraʊ / (AmE), / ˈreɪz ən ˈaɪbraʊ / (BrE)
to show surprise or skepticism, often by lifting one's eyebrow(s) as a visible gesture

Examples

To raise an eyebrow in surprise
It's not uncommon to raise an eyebrow when you hear such an unbelievable story.
She raised an eyebrow at his unusual explanation, clearly not convinced.
When the manager announced the new policy, several employees raised an eyebrow in confusion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: be skeptical - to doubt or question something
  • synonym: be surprised - to feel or show surprise
