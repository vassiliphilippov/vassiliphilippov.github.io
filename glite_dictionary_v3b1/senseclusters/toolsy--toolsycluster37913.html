toolsy

adjective
foolish or obnoxious
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːlzi / (AmE), / ˈtuːlzi / (BrE)

Examples

Don't be so toolsy—just relax and be yourself around new people.
Her toolsy behavior at the event made everyone feel uncomfortable.
