library catalog

noun
systematic listing of library holdings
5.7 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.brə.riː ˈkæt.ə.lɔːg/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.briː ˈkæt.əl.ɒg/ (BrE)
a systematic list or database of the materials held in a library, such as books, periodicals, and other resources
