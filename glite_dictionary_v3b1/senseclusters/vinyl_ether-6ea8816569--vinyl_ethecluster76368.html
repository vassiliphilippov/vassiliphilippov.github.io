vinyl ether

noun
a volatile liquid anesthetic (chemical)
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪnəl ˈiːθər / (AmE), / ˈvaɪnɪl ˈiːθə / (BrE)
a colorless, volatile, highly flammable liquid formerly used as an inhalation anesthetic
