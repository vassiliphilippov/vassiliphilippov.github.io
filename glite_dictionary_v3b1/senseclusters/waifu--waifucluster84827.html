waifu

noun
idealized anime/manga female partner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪfu / (AmE), / ˈwaɪfu / (BrE)
A fictional female character from anime, manga, or related media, regarded as someone’s ideal partner or object of affection.

Examples

Anime waifu poster
He proudly displays his collection of waifus in the form of posters and figurines.
In online forums, people often debate which waifu is the best.
Many fans consider Rem from Re:Zero to be the ultimate waifu.
