crew

noun
group of people working together
2.7
Pronunciation: / kruː /; / kru / (AmE), / kruː /; / kru / (BrE)
a group of people who work together, especially to operate a vehicle (such as a ship, aircraft, or train) or do practical tasks

Examples

Ship crew
A crew of electricians arrived to fix the power lines after the storm.
The construction crew finished building the bridge ahead of schedule.
The film crew worked long hours to capture every scene perfectly.
After dinner, the ship's crew gathered on deck for a meeting.
The airplane crew handled the emergency calmly and efficiently.
The train crew rotated shifts during the long overnight journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passenger - a person who travels in a vehicle but is not part of the crew
  • synonym: staff - the employees of an organization or business
  • synonym: team - a group of people working together toward a common goal
