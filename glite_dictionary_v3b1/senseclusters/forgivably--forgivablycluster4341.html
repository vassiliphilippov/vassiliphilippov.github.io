forgivably

adverb
in an excusable or forgivable manner
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɪvəbli / (AmE), / fəˈɡɪvəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that can be forgiven or excused; deserving excuse or forgiveness.

Examples

To be forgivably late
Arriving late was forgivably due to the unexpected traffic jam.
His mistake was forgivably overlooked by his generous teacher.
She was forgivably nervous during her first public performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excusably - in an excusable manner or to an excusable degree
  • antonym: unforgivably - in a manner not deserving forgiveness or excuse
