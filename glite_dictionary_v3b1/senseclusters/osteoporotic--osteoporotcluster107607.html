osteoporotic

adjective
affected by osteoporosis
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstiəpəˈrɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊpəˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by osteoporosis, a condition in which bones become weak and brittle

Examples

Osteoporotic bones
The doctor explained that osteoporotic bones are more likely to break after a minor fall.
She takes medication to prevent her condition from becoming osteoporotic.
Regular exercise can help reduce the risk of developing osteoporotic fractures in old age.
