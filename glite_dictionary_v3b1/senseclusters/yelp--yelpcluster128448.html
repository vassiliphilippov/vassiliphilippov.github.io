yelp

verb
bark or cry out sharply (animal)
6.8 1.7
Pronunciation: /jelp/ (AmE), /jelp/ (BrE)
(of an animal) to bark or cry out in a sharp, high-pitched tone, usually expressing pain or excitement.

Examples

To yelp in pain
The dog began to yelp in pain after stepping on a thorn.
The frightened fox yelped and darted back into the bushes.
We heard the puppies yelping excitedly as they played in the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bark - to make a sharp explosive cry, typical of a dog
  • degree higher: howl - make a long loud wailing cry, like a dog or wolf
