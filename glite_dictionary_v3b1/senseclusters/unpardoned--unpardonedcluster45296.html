unpardoned

adjective
not forgiven
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɑrdənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɑːdənd / (BrE)
not forgiven or not officially excused for an offense or wrongdoing

Examples

An unpardoned mistake
He lived for years with the burden of his unpardoned mistakes.
The law does not allow for any unpardoned offenses to be overlooked.
She felt isolated because of her unpardoned actions in the past.
