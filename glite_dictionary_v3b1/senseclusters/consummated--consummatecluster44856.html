consummated

adjective
brought to completion
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsəˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsjʊˌmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
brought to completion; fully completed or finalized, especially in a formal or legal context.

Examples

A consummated contract

The merger was officially consummated after both companies signed the agreement.
They celebrated their consummated marriage during the wedding reception.
