truelove

noun
a deeply loved person; romantic partner
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːˌlʌv / (AmE), / ˈtruːˌlʌv / (BrE)
a person deeply loved by another, especially as their romantic partner

Examples

Even after all these years, she still regards him as her truelove.
He wrote a heartfelt letter to confess his feelings to his truelove.
In fairy tales, the hero sets out on a quest to rescue his truelove.
