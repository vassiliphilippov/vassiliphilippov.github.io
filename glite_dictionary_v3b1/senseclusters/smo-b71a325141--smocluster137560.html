SMO

noun
senior medical officer
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈəʊ / (BrE)
a senior doctor with clinical and managerial responsibility in a hospital or health service

Examples

Hospital SMO
The SMO attended the emergency meeting to discuss the hospital's response plan.
After years of experience, she was promoted to SMO at the city hospital.
The SMO oversees both patient care and administrative duties in the department.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CMO - chief medical officer, the highest-ranking medical authority in an organization
  • antonym: junior doctor - a doctor who is not yet in a senior position
