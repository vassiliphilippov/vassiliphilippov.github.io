powerhouse

noun
an energetic, dynamic person
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚˌhaʊs /; / ˈpaʊərˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.əˌhaʊs /; / ˈpaʊəˌhaʊs / (BrE)
