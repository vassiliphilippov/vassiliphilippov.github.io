Synonyms

  • character assassination
    7.3 0.62/m
    character assassination is the common phrase for harming someone's reputation with lies or rumors, while assassination sounds shorter and more dramatic.
  • blackwash
    9.9 0.00/m
    blackwash is an attempt to destroy someone's reputation with false allegations, while assassination is a more figurative, dramatic term for that harm.
  • defamation
    7.1 2.50/m
    defamation is the legal term for false harmful statements about someone, while assassination is a strong, nonlegal word for similar reputation attacks.
  • smear campaign
    5.5 0.64/m
    smear campaign is an organized effort to spread false accusations over time, while assassination can mean a single, sharp reputational attack.
  • mudslinging
    6.5 0.00/m
    mudslinging is nasty, often political name-calling and accusations, while assassination implies a deliberate, sometimes planned, attack on reputation.
  • vilification
    8.4 0.56/m
    vilification is harsh public abuse and strong insults, while assassination suggests targeted false claims meant to ruin someone's reputation.
  • whisper campaign
    7.5 0.05/m
    whisper campaign spreads secret rumors quietly to hurt someone, while assassination is a broader, often public or direct reputation attack.
  • besmirchment
    9.0 0.01/m
    besmirchment is a literary word for staining someone's name, while assassination is the common, stronger metaphor for attacking reputation deliberately.
  • detraction
    8.6 0.05/m
    detraction is a single unfair remark that lowers someone's standing, while assassination is a full, deliberate attack on their reputation.
