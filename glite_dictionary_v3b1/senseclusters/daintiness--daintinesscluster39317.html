daintiness

noun
delicate beauty or refinement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪnti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪnti.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being delicately beautiful or attractive, often in a refined or graceful way

Examples

The daintiness of china
He admired the daintiness of the china teacups on the table.
The daintiness of her handwriting made every letter pleasing to read.
There was a certain daintiness to the way she arranged the flowers in the vase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicacy - the quality of being delicate in form; fineness
