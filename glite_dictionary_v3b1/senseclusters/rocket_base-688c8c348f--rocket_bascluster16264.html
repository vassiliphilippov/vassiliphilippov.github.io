rocket base

noun
military site for rocket missiles
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪt beɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt beɪs / (BrE)
A military installation where rocket missiles are stored, launched, or maintained.

Examples

A rocket base facility
