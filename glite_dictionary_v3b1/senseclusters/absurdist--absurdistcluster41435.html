absurdist

noun
believer in absurdism
8.2
Pronunciation: /əb.ˈsɜːr.dɪst/ /; / /æb.ˈsɜːr.dɪst/ (AmE), /əb.ˈsɜː.dɪst/ /; / /æb.ˈsɜː.dɪst/ (BrE)
a person who believes in or follows the philosophy that human life is inherently meaningless and absurd

Examples

An absurdist thinker
As an absurdist, he often questioned the purpose of daily routines and traditions.
The novel was written by a famous absurdist who challenged conventional ideas about meaning.
Many absurdists find comfort in accepting the lack of inherent purpose in life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: existentialist - a person who believes that individuals create meaning in an otherwise meaningless world
  • synonym: nihilist - a person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles
