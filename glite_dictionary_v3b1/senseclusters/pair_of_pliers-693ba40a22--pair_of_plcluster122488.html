pair of pliers

noun
gripping hand tool with hinged arms
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛr əv ˈplaɪərz / (AmE), / ˈpeə əv ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
A hand tool with two hinged arms and serrated jaws, used for gripping, bending, or cutting objects.
