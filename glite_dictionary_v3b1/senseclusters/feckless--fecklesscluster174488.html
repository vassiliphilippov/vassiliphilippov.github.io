feckless

adjective
lacking effectiveness or responsibility

Pronunciation: /ˈfek.ləs/ /; / /ˈfek.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈfek.ləs/ /; / /ˈfek.lɪs/ (BrE)

Examples
