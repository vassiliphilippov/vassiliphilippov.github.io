candle-wasting

adjective
wasting time or resources; unproductive
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌweɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkændlˌweɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
spending time or resources unproductively or in vain, often through habits that are wasteful

Examples

candle-wasting evenings
He regretted his candle-wasting evenings spent idly by the fire.
Her candle-wasting behavior eventually led to missed opportunities.
They described the meeting as a candle-wasting exercise with no clear outcomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: productive - achieving or producing a significant amount; constructive
  • synonym: wasteful - using resources carelessly or to no purpose
