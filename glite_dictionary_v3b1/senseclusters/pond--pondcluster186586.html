pond

noun
small body of still water
5.4
Pronunciation: / pɑnd / (AmE), / pɒnd / (BrE)
a small area of still water, typically smaller than a lake, either natural or artificial

Examples

A small pond
A family of ducks made their home near the edge of the pond.
In winter, the pond freezes over and kids skate on it.
We spent the afternoon fishing at the pond behind the farmhouse.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: lake - a large body of water surrounded by land
  • degree lower: pool - a small and usually artificially created body of still water
