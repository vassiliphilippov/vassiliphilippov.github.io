prosthodontist

noun
dental specialist in artificial replacements
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑsθəˈdɑntɪst / (AmE), / ˌprɒsθəˈdɒntɪst / (BrE)
a dentist who specializes in the replacement of missing teeth and related dental structures with artificial devices

Examples

A prosthodontist specialist
A skilled prosthodontist can greatly improve the comfort and function of artificial teeth.
After losing several teeth in an accident, she consulted a prosthodontist for dental implants.
The prosthodontist recommended a set of custom-made dentures for the patient.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dentist - a person qualified to treat the diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums
  • synonym: orthodontist - a dentist specializing in correcting irregularities of the teeth
