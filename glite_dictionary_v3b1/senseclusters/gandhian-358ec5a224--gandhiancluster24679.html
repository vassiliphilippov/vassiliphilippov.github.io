Gandhian

adjective
relating to Gandhi or his teachings
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡændiən / (AmE), / ˈɡandiən / (BrE)
Relating to Mahatma Gandhi or his teachings, especially his principles of nonviolence and civil disobedience.

Examples

Gandhian principles
He wrote a thesis on Gandhian economics and its relevance today.
Her philosophy is inspired by Gandhian principles of peace and truth.
The protestors adopted a Gandhian approach of nonviolent resistance.
