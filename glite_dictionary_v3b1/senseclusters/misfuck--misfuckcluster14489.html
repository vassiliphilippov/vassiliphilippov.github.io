misfuck

verb
ruin or botch something completely (slang, vulgar)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈfʌk / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈfʌk / (BrE)
(Vulgar slang) To ruin or make a mess of something badly; to mishandle or spoil completely.

Examples

To misfuck a project
He really misfucked that project with his careless mistakes.
If you misfuck this deal, we could lose everything.
She realized she had misfucked her chances by saying the wrong thing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fix - to repair or correct something
  • synonym: mess up - to spoil, ruin, or make a mistake in something
  • synonym: screw up - to make a serious mistake or botch something (informal)
