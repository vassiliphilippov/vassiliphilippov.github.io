tipless

adjective
without a tip
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈtɪpləs / (BrE)
lacking a tip or pointed end, especially referring to objects or clothing with the end part removed or absent

Examples

Tipless gloves
He wore tipless gloves so he could use his phone.
The artist preferred using a tipless brush for certain painting techniques.
Some people buy tipless pens to avoid ink smudging on their hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunt - having a rounded or flat end instead of a sharp one
  • antonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapered end
