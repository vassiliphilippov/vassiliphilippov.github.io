feather star

noun
marine animal with feathery arms
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðɚ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə stɑː / (BrE)
A free-swimming, stalkless marine crinoid with ten feathery arms, found on muddy sea bottoms.

Examples

A feather star animal
At the aquarium, we watched a feather star cling to a rock with its feathery appendages.
Scientists discovered a new species of feather star in the deep sea.
The feather star gracefully moved its arms as it drifted along the ocean floor.
