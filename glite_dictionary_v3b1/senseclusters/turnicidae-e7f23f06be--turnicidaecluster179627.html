Turnicidae

noun
family of small Old World birds: buttonquails
Pronunciation: / tɜːrˈnɪsɪˌdiː /; / tɜrˈnɪsɪˌdi / (AmE), / tɜːˈnɪsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of small Old World birds, called buttonquails, that look like true quail but are not closely related to them
