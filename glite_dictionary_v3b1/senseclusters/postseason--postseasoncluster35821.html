postseason

noun
games after regular season
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌsiːzən / (BrE)
the period of games or competitions after the regular sports season to determine a champion

Examples

The postseason begins
The team worked hard all year to qualify for the postseason.
Many fans travel long distances to watch their favorite teams in the postseason.
Ticket prices usually increase during the postseason because of higher demand.
The team was excited to reach the postseason after a strong regular season performance.
Many fans travel to watch their favorite teams compete in the postseason each year.
Winning in the postseason is often considered more important than regular season victories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playoffs - games played after the regular season to decide the champion
  • antonym: regular season - the main part of a sports season before playoffs or postseason
