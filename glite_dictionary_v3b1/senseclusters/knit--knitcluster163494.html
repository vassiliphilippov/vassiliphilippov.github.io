knit

noun
fabric produced by knitting
4.1
Pronunciation: / nɪt / (AmE), / nɪt / (BrE)
a type of fabric or an item made by interlacing yarn with needles or by machine

Examples

A cotton knit
He chose a lightweight knit for the summer top.
The sweater is made from a soft wool knit.
This shop sells both woven and knit fabrics.
She admired the intricate knit of the handmade blanket.
The sweater's tight knit made it especially warm in winter.
This scarf has a loose knit that gives it a casual look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knitting - the action or process of knitting yarn into fabric
  • antonym: weave - a fabric or cloth created by interlacing threads at right angles
  • antonym: woven - fabric made by weaving threads over and under each other
