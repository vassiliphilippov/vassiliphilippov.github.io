twaddler

noun
person who talks or writes nonsense
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɑːd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwɒd.lə / (BrE)
A person who talks or writes nonsense or trivial chatter.

Examples

A twaddler talks nonsense
Instead of offering constructive ideas, he played the twaddler during the entire meeting.
She dismissed her colleague as nothing more than a harmless twaddler.
The group quickly grew tired of the constant musings of the office twaddler.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - to talk rapidly and continuously in a foolish way
  • synonym: prattler - a person who talks at length in a foolish or inconsequential way
  • synonym: twaddle - trivial or foolish talk or writing
