backsword

noun
stick used for fencing practice
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌsɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌsɔːd / (BrE)
A stick used in place of a sword for fencing practice, especially in historical contexts.

Examples

Wooden backsword
During training, a backsword was used to prevent injury.
He collected antique fencing equipment, including an old backsword.
The students practiced their moves with a backsword before handling real blades.
