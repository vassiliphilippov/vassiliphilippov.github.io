fascia

noun
dashboard or instrument panel in vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃiə / (AmE), / ˈfæsiə / (BrE)
the dashboard or instrument panel of a car or airplane, containing dials and controls

Examples

Car fascia design
Aircraft designers paid special attention to the layout of the fascia to improve usability.
He reached across the fascia to adjust the air conditioning controls.
The new model features a digital display in the center of the fascia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dashboard - the panel facing the driver of a vehicle, containing controls and instruments
