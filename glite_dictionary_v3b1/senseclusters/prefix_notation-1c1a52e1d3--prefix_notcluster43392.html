Examples

Prefix notation in math
In computer science, prefix notation is also known as Polish notation.
Mathematicians sometimes use prefix notation to avoid parentheses in complex formulas.
The calculator was set to operate using prefix notation rather than the standard infix method.

Synonyms

  • Polish notation
    8.6 0.00/m
    Polish notation is the common name used in math and computing for parenthesis-free operator-before-operand expressions, while prefix notation is the general label.
  • Lukasiewicz notation
    9.9 0.00/m
    Lukasiewicz notation is an older name often used in logic history and papers, while prefix notation is the neutral descriptive term.
