infector

noun
disease transmitter
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛktər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛktə / (BrE)
a person, animal, or thing that spreads a disease or infection to others

Examples

Mosquito infector
The mosquito is a well-known infector of malaria in tropical regions.
Doctors worked to identify the original infector in the outbreak.
Proper hygiene can help prevent becoming an infector of common colds.
The mosquito is a common infector of malaria in tropical regions.
Doctors identified the patient as the primary infector in the outbreak.
Proper sanitation can help prevent contact with any potential infector of disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carrier - a person or animal that transmits a disease without showing symptoms
  • antonym: host - an organism that harbors a parasite or pathogen
  • synonym: vector - an organism, typically an insect, that transmits a pathogen
