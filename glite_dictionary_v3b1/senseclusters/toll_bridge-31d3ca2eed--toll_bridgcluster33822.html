toll bridge

noun
bridge where a toll is charged
Pronunciation: / toʊl brɪdʒ / (AmE), / təʊl brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A bridge over which a fee is charged for passage.

Examples

A toll bridge fee

The new toll bridge reduced travel time but increased daily expenses for commuters.
We had to pay a few dollars to cross the toll bridge into the city.
