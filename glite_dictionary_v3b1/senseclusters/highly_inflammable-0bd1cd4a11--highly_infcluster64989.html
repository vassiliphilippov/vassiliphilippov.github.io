highly inflammable

adjective
very easy to catch fire
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli ɪnˈflæməbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli ɪnˈflæməbl / (BrE)
Very easily capable of catching fire and burning quickly.

Examples

Highly inflammable liquids
Please do not smoke near highly inflammable substances to avoid any risk of fire.
The warehouse stores highly inflammable chemicals that require strict safety precautions.
The warning label indicates that the gas is highly inflammable and should be handled with care.
