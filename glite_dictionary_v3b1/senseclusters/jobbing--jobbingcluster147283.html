jobbing

noun
small print jobs
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒbɪŋ / (BrE)
small-scale commercial printing work or the printed items produced, such as cards, leaflets, or invoices

Examples

Jobbing print order
The print shop handled all the jobbing for local businesses, including flyers and business cards.
He found steady work in jobbing, producing invoices and leaflets for small companies.
Many small printers rely on jobbing to keep their businesses running throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printing - the process of producing text and images on paper
  • synonym: publishing - the business of producing and distributing printed material
