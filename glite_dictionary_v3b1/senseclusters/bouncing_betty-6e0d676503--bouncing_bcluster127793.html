bouncing betty

noun
a type of explosive land mine
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˈbɛti / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˈbɛti / (BrE)
a type of antipersonnel land mine that launches upward before exploding, designed to cause injury by dispersing shrapnel at about waist height

Examples

A bouncing betty mine
A bouncing betty can cause devastating injuries due to its upward explosion.
During the war, the presence of a bouncing betty was a constant threat on the battlefield.
The soldiers carefully avoided areas suspected of hiding bouncing betties during their patrol.
