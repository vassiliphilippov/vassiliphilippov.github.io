carding

noun
ID check for age
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of checking a person's identification card to verify their age or identity, especially before selling age-restricted goods

Examples

Carding at the bar
The bartender is strict about carding everyone who orders alcohol, no matter their appearance.
Some stores have a policy of carding all customers who buy tobacco products.
After several warnings, the shop was fined for not properly carding underage buyers.
