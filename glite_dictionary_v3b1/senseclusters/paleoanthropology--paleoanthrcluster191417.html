paleoanthropology

noun
the study of human fossils
1.0 8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪlioʊˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpæliəʊˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi /; / ˌpalɪəʊˌanθrəˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of human fossils and the origins and evolution of early humans.

Examples

Paleoanthropology study fossils
