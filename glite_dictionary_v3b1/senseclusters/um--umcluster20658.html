um

interjection
hesitation sound
4.1
Pronunciation: / ʌm / (AmE), / ʌm / (BrE)
used to show hesitation, a pause, or uncertainty while speaking

Examples

Say um hesitantly
She paused and said um before answering the difficult question.
During his speech, he often used um when searching for the right words.
If you are unsure, it is common to say um while you think.
She said, 'um, I am not sure how to answer that question.'
During his presentation, he used 'um' several times while thinking.
If you listen closely, you will notice he often says 'um' when he is nervous.
"Um, could you repeat the question?" he asked nervously.
She paused and said, "um, I am not sure about that answer."
If you need more time to think, just say um before you respond.
