indelible ink

noun
permanent ink that cannot be erased
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛləbəl ɪŋk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛlɪbəl ɪŋk / (BrE)
a type of ink that cannot be removed, erased, or washed away

Examples

Indelible ink mark
Many countries use indelible ink during elections to prevent people from voting multiple times.
She marked her tools with indelible ink so her name would never fade.
The document was signed using indelible ink to ensure the signature lasted.
