unknit

verb
to relax the brow
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈnɪt / (BrE)
to relax or smooth a knitted or furrowed part of the body, especially the brow or forehead

Examples

To unknit your brow
She tried to unknit her brow when she realized the mistake was forgiven.
After hearing the good news, he managed to unknit his worried expression.
It took a moment for her to unknit her forehead and smile again.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frown - to contract the brow in displeasure or concentration
  • synonym: relax - to make less tense or tight
