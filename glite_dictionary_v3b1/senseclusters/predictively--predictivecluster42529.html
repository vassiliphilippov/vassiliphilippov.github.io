predictively

adverb
in a predictive way
4.9
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɪktɪvli / (AmE), / prɪˈdɪktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or relates to making predictions about future events or outcomes

Examples

To predictively analyze data
The software analyzes data predictively to forecast future sales trends.
Doctors can use certain tests predictively to assess a patient's risk of disease.
The team acted predictively, preparing for possible challenges before they arose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prophetically - in a way that predicts or foretells the future
  • antonym: retrospectively - in a way that looks back on or reviews past events
