acolyte

noun
loyal follower
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæk.ə.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈæk.ə.laɪt/ (BrE)
a person who assists or loyally follows a leader or important person, often as a supporter or helper

Examples

Political acolyte
The young acolyte eagerly carried the books for the professor during the lecture.
Many political acolytes gathered to support their candidate at the rally.
As a loyal acolyte, she always defended her mentor's decisions in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disciple - a devoted follower or student of a teacher or leader
  • synonym: follower - a person who supports and admires a particular person or set of ideas
  • antonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group or organization
