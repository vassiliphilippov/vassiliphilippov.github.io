he-huckleberry

noun
a deciduous shrubby plant with white flowers
9.3
Pronunciation: / hi ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri / (AmE), / hi ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub with many branches, producing dense, downy panicles of small, bell-shaped white flowers.

Examples

The he-huckleberry shrub
Botanists identified several varieties of he-huckleberry growing along the hillside.
The he-huckleberry attracts bees and other pollinators with its bell-shaped flowers.
The forest path was lined with he-huckleberry shrubs in full bloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: huckleberry - a small, round, edible berry or the shrub producing it
