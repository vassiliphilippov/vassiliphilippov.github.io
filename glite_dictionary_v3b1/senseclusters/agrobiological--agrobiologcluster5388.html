agrobiological

adjective
relating to agrobiology
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡroʊbaɪ.əˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌaɡrəʊbaɪ.əˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of agrobiology, especially as it concerns the relationships between soil, plants, and microorganisms in agriculture

Examples

Agrobiological science study
An agrobiological approach considers both ecological and agricultural principles.
Researchers are developing agrobiological methods to improve sustainable farming.
The agrobiological properties of the soil were analyzed before planting the crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agrobiology - the study of plant nutrition and soil management in agriculture
