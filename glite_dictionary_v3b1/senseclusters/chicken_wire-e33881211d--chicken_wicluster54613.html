chicken wire

noun
hexagonal wire mesh used for fencing
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən waɪr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn waɪə / (BrE)
A type of light, flexible galvanized wire mesh with a hexagonal pattern, used especially for making fences for poultry or gardening.

Examples

Chicken wire fence
Storms had torn sections of chicken wire loose from the animal enclosure.
The old coop was patched up with some chicken wire and wooden posts.
We used chicken wire to build a fence around the vegetable garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wire mesh - a net made of thin metal wire
