horseless carriage

noun
early term for an automobile
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsləs ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsləs ˈkærɪdʒ / (BrE)
an early term for an automobile, especially used to describe the first motor cars of the late 19th and early 20th centuries
