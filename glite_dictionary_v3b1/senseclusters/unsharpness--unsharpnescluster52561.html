unsharpness

noun
lack of clarity
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɑːrpnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɑːpnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being blurry, unclear, or lacking focus (in images, perception, or thought)
