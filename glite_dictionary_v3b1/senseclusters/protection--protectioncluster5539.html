protection

noun
support, endorsement, or guidance
5.3
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of giving support, endorsement, or guidance, especially in a way that helps someone succeed

Examples

Protection by the UN
The organization operates under the protection of the United Nations.
The young artist thrived under the protection of a wealthy benefactor.
With the protection of her influential mentor, she advanced quickly in her career.
