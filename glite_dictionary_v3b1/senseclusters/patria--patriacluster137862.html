patria

noun
native country
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtriə / (AmE), / ˈpætriə / (BrE)
a person's native country or homeland, often regarded with deep affection or sentiment

Examples

Return to patria
After years of living abroad, she longed to return to her patria and see her family.
The poet wrote passionately about his patria and its beautiful landscapes.
Many immigrants maintain strong ties to their patria through language and traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreign land - a country that is not one's own
  • synonym: homeland - the country where someone was born or feels they belong
  • synonym: motherland - one's native country, especially when regarded with affection
