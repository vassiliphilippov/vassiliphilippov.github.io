ergotic

adjective
relating to ergot (fungus/drug)
Pronunciation: / ɜːrˈɡɒtɪk / (AmE), / ɜːˈɡɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by ergot, a fungus affecting cereals and used medicinally.

Examples

Ergotic fungus
Historic outbreaks of ergotic rye caused significant illness in medieval Europe.
Some pharmaceuticals use ergotic alkaloids for their vasoconstrictive properties.
The patient developed symptoms consistent with ergotic poisoning after consuming contaminated rye.
