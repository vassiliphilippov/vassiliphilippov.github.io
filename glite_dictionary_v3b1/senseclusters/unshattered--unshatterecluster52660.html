unshattered

adjective
not emotionally broken
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃætərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃætəd / (BrE)
not emotionally broken or ruined; remaining strong or unaffected after a traumatic event or shock

Examples

Unshattered spirit
Despite the harsh criticism, his confidence remained unshattered throughout the ordeal.
She walked away from the experience surprisingly unshattered and ready to face new challenges.
Their friendship was tested by rumors but stayed unshattered in the end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resilient - able to recover quickly from difficulties
  • antonym: shattered - emotionally broken or devastated
  • synonym: unbroken - not defeated or destroyed; still whole
