shiner

noun
a food fish of the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪnə / (BrE)
an important food fish of the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean, with a greenish-blue body, dark bars, and few or no scales

Examples
