crenellation

noun
building battlements with gaps
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɛnəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɛnɪˈleɪʃən /; / ˌkrɛnɪˈleɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a row of battlements with regular gaps (crenels) along the top of a wall, especially on a castle, used for defense or decoration
