spottiness

noun
unevenness or spots
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːtinəs / (AmE), / ˈspɒtinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being covered with spots or being uneven in appearance or quality

Examples

The spottiness of paint
The spottiness of the paint job was obvious in the sunlight.
She was concerned about the spottiness on her face before the big event.
The report highlighted the spottiness in the team's performance throughout the season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patchiness - the quality of being uneven or irregular in coverage or quality
  • antonym: uniformity - the quality of being the same or consistent throughout
