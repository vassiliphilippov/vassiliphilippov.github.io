sprig

noun
small stem or branch
Pronunciation: / sprɪɡ / (AmE), / sprɪɡ / (BrE)
a small stem or branch, especially one that is freshly cut or used for decoration or cooking

Examples

A sprig of mint
A single sprig of rosemary can add a lot of flavor to a roast.
He wore a sprig of holly in his buttonhole during the holidays.
She placed a sprig of mint on top of the dessert for decoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twig - a small thin branch of a tree or bush
