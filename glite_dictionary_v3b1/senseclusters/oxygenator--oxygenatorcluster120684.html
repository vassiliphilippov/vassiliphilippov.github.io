oxygenator

noun
oxygen-adding plant or device
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksɪdʒəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒəˌneɪtə / (BrE)
a plant or device that adds oxygen to water, especially to improve water quality in ponds or aquariums

Examples

Aquarium oxygenator
Adding an oxygenator to the aquarium helps keep the fish healthy by increasing oxygen levels.
The gardener recommended planting an oxygenator to improve the water quality in the small pond.
Many aquatic oxygenators are easy to care for and benefit both plants and animals in the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerator - a device that introduces air into water or soil
