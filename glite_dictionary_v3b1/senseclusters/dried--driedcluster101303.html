dried

adjective
no longer wet
2.2
Pronunciation: / draɪd / (AmE), / draɪd / (BrE)
no longer wet; having had all moisture removed or evaporated.

Examples

Dried mud
He brushed away the dried mud from his shoes before entering the house.
She hung the towels outside until they were completely dried.
The dried paint began to crack and peel off the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid; not wet
  • antonym: wet - covered or saturated with water or another liquid
