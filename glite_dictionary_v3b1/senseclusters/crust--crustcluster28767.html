crust

verb
to form a hard outer layer
Pronunciation: / krʌst / (AmE), / krʌst / (BrE)
to form or become covered with a hard outer layer

Examples

To crust over
As the wound healed, it began to crust over.
If you leave the soup out overnight, it may crust on top.
Mud can crust as it dries in the hot sun.
After several hours in the sun, the mud began crusting on the boy's shoes.
If you leave the soup uncovered, a thin layer will start crusting on top.
The wound was slowly crusting over as it healed.
