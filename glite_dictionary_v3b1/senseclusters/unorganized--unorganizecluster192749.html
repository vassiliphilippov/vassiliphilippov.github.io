unorganized

adjective
not structured; lacking organization
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɔr.ɡəˌnaɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈɔr.ɡə.naɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔː.ɡə.naɪzd /; / ˌʌnˈɔː.ɡə.naɪzd / (BrE)
not having or belonging to a structured or systematic whole; lacking formal arrangement or organization.

Examples

Unorganized files
Some files remained unorganized on her computer for months.
The charity's efforts were hampered by an unorganized distribution network.
The meeting dissolved into chaos because the agenda was completely unorganized.
Many unorganised workplaces struggle with efficiency and productivity.
Several unorganised groups operate independently without any oversight.
The files were left in an unorganised heap on the desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disorganized - lacking order or method; confused or chaotic
  • antonym: organized - having or belonging to a structured whole; arranged in a systematic way
