prostatodynia

noun
prostate pain
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːstətoʊˈdɪniə / (AmE), / ˌprɒstətəʊˈdɪniə / (BrE)
pain or discomfort in the prostate gland without evidence of infection

Examples

Prostatodynia pain
The doctor diagnosed him with prostatodynia after ruling out infection.
Men experiencing prostatodynia may report pelvic pain and urinary discomfort.
Treatment for prostatodynia often focuses on relieving symptoms rather than curing an infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prostatitis - inflammation of the prostate gland, often with pain
