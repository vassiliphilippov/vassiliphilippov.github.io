legal fee

noun
fee paid for legal services
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl fi / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl fiː / (BrE)
an amount of money paid to a lawyer or law firm for professional legal services

Examples

Pay legal fee
Her legal fee was higher than she expected after the lengthy trial.
The client refused to pay the legal fee until the work was completed.
The contract outlined how the legal fee would be billed by the attorney.
