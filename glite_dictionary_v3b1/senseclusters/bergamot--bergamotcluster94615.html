bergamot

noun
Mediterranean citrus tree, fruit used in perfumes/teas
9.8 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːr.gə.mɑːt/ /; / /ˈbɜːr.gə.mət/ (AmE), /ˈbɜː.gə.mɒt/ /; / /ˈbɜː.gə.mət/ (BrE)
