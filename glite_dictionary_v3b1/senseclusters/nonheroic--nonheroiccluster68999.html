nonheroic

adjective
lacking heroism
6.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.hɪ.ˈroʊ.ɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.hɪ.ˈrəʊ.ɪk/ (BrE)
not showing heroic qualities such as bravery, nobility, or grandeur; ordinary or unheroic

Examples

A nonheroic character
The film takes a nonheroic approach, focusing on the daily struggles of its main character.
Her nonheroic actions showed that courage can be quiet and unremarkable.
Many modern novels feature nonheroic protagonists who deal with ordinary life challenges.
His response to the emergency was practical but nonheroic.
The film focused on the nonheroic aspects of everyday life.
She made a nonheroic decision to stay home and care for her family.
