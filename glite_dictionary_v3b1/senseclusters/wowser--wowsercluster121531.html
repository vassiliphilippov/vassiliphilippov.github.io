wowser

noun
strict moralist
Pronunciation: / ˈwaʊzər / (AmE), / ˈwaʊzə / (BrE)
a person who is very strict about moral behavior and disapproves of fun, pleasure, or enjoyment

Examples

Old wowser complains
The wowser complained about the music and insisted the party end early.
Some people called her a wowser because she never approved of any fun activities.
He was known as a wowser for his strict views on drinking and entertainment.
The town's wowser tried to stop the festival because he thought it was too wild.
Some people called her a wowser because she opposed dancing and parties.
He was labeled a wowser after campaigning against the new bar opening in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hedonist - a person who pursues pleasure as a goal
  • synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • antonym: libertine - a person who behaves without moral principles
  • synonym: puritan - a person with strict moral or religious beliefs
