monument

noun
officially protected historic site
2.8 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːn.jə.mənt/ /; / /ˈmɑːn.jʊ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈmɒn.jʊ.mənt/ /; / /ˈmɒn.jə.mənt/ (BrE)
a large or important structure or place built to honor a person, event, or history

Examples
