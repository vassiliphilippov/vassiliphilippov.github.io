monument

noun
officially protected historic site
3.4 13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀên.j…ô.m…ônt /; / Ààm…ëÀênj…ôm…ônt / (AmE), / Ààm…ín.j ä.m…ônt /; / Ààm…ínj äm…ônt /; / Ààm…ín.j…ô.m…ônt / (BrE)
