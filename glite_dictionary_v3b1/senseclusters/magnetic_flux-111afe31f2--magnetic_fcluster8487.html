magnetic flux

noun
measure of magnetic field through an area
Pronunciation: / məgˈnɛtɪk flʌks /; / mægˈnɛtɪk flʌks / (AmE), / məɡˈnɛtɪk flʌks /; / mæɡˈnɛtɪk flʌks / (BrE)
the total amount of magnetic field passing through a given area (used to measure the strength of a magnetic field)
