order Helotiales

noun
an order of fungi with disk- or goblet-shaped fruiting bodies
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ˌhɛloʊtiˈeɪliˌiz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌhɛlətɪˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi characterized by having spores (asci) in disk- or goblet-shaped apothecia

Examples

Order Helotiales fungi
Fungi in the order Helotiales are distinguished by their disk-shaped fruiting bodies called apothecia.
Several plant pathogens belong to the order Helotiales, affecting crops worldwide.
The order Helotiales includes many species found on decaying wood and leaves.
