catechistic

adjective
of or relating to a catechism
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkætɪˈkɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˈkɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or resembling a catechism, especially in method or rigor

Examples

A catechistic method
His catechistic style ensured that every lesson followed a strict question-and-answer format.
She used a catechistic approach, drilling each answer until it was perfect.
The teacher's catechistic questions helped the students memorize important facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catechetical - relating to religious instruction by means of question and answer
