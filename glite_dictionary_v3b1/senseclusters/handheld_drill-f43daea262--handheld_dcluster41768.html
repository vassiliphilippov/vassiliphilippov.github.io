handheld drill

noun
portable power drill operated by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændhɛld drɪl / (AmE), / ˈhændhɛld drɪl / (BrE)
a small, portable drill designed to be held and operated by hand, used for making holes in materials such as wood or metal

Examples

A handheld drill tool
He used a handheld drill to make holes in the wooden shelf.
Make sure the handheld drill is fully charged before starting your project.
The technician prefers a handheld drill over a bulky drill press for smaller tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: power drill - an electric or battery-powered drill used to make holes
