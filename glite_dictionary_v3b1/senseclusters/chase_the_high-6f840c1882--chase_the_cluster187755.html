chase the high

verb
seek intense excitement or pleasure, often repeatedly
6.0
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪs ðə haɪ / (AmE), / tʃeɪs ðə haɪ / (BrE)
to continually seek the intense excitement or pleasure, especially from drugs or risky behavior, usually after having already experienced it once

Examples

To chase the high again
After his first experience with skydiving, he kept chasing the high with more extreme sports.
No matter how successful she became, she was always chasing the high of her early days of fame.
People struggling with addiction often find themselves chasing the high that the first use gave them.
