lantern fly

noun
brightly marked tropical insect with snout-like projection
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlæntərn faɪ / (AmE), / ˈlæntən faɪ / (BrE)
a large tropical insect with bright markings and a snout-like projection, once believed to emit light

Examples

The lantern fly insect

The lantern fly is known for its vivid colors and unusual appearance.
