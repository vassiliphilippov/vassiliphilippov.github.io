marsh hen

noun
North American marsh-dwelling bird, especially the coot
Pronunciation: / mɑrʃ hɛn / (AmE), / mɑːʃ hɛn / (BrE)
a small marsh-dwelling bird, especially the American coot or similar birds with a frontal shield, found in North America
