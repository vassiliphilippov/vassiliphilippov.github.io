round-the-clock patrol

noun
continuous, nonstop patrol
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɑk pəˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɒk pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
a patrol that operates without interruption throughout the day and night

Examples

A round-the-clock patrol team
Due to the recent incidents, police have established a round-the-clock patrol in the neighborhood.
The security company maintains a round-the-clock patrol to protect the premises.
Volunteers organized a round-the-clock patrol to keep watch over the evacuated area.

Related Terms
