scritch

verb
to make scraping sound
Pronunciation: / skrɪtʃ / (AmE), / skrɪtʃ / (BrE)
to make a harsh, scraping or scratching sound by rubbing against a surface

Examples

To scritch the door
The cat began to scritch at the door, wanting to be let in.
I could hear branches scritching against the window during the storm.
She scritched her pencil across the paper, making a sharp noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scrape - to rub or scratch a surface, often making a noise
  • synonym: scratch - to make a slight, harsh noise by rubbing something rough
