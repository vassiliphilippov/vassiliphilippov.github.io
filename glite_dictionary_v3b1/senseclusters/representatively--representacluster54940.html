representatively

adverb
on behalf of others
5.8
Pronunciation: /re.prɪ.ˈzen.tə.tɪv.liː/ /; / /re.prə.ˈzen.tə.tɪv.liː/ (AmE), /re.prɪ.ˈzen.tə.tɪv.liː/ /; / /re.prə.ˈzen.tə.tɪv.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that acts on behalf of or as an official representative of someone or a group

Examples

Representatively speak for a group
The ambassador spoke representatively for her country at the international summit.
He acted representatively when signing the agreement on behalf of the committee.
The students were chosen representatively to voice the concerns of their classmates.
