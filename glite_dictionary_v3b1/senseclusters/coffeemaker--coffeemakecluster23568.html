coffeemaker

noun
person who makes coffee
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔfiˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who prepares or serves coffee, especially in an informal or domestic setting

Examples

Family coffeemaker
During family gatherings, my aunt is always the coffeemaker and keeps everyone happy.
He became the unofficial coffeemaker at work, brewing fresh pots every morning.
As the coffeemaker for our book club, she always brings her special blend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barista - a person who prepares and serves coffee drinks, especially in a café
