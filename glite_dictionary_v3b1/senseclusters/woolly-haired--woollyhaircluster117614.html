woolly-haired

adjective
having dense, curly, wool-like hair
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊliˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈwʊliˌheəd /; / ˈwʊliˌhɛəd / (BrE)
having hair or fur that is dense, curly, or matted, like wool
