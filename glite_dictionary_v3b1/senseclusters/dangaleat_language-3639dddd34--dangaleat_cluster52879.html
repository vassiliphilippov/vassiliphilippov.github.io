Dangaleat language

noun
Chadic language of Chad
Pronunciation: / ˌdæŋɡəˈleɪət ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌdaŋɡəˈleɪət ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Chad, notable for its seven vowels and distinctions in vowel length.
