olfactory modality

noun
sense of smell; scent perception
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɑlˈfæktəri moʊˈdælɪti / (AmE), / ɒlˈfæktəri məʊˈdælɪti / (BrE)
the sensory faculty that enables the perception and distinction of scents or odors

Examples

Olfactory modality sense
Damage to the olfactory modality can result in a reduced or lost sense of smell.
Researchers studied how age affects the olfactory modality in elderly participants.
The olfactory modality allows humans and animals to detect and distinguish a wide variety of scents.

Related Terms
