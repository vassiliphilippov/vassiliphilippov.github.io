extra point

noun
point scored after touchdown in American football
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkstrə pɔɪnt /; / ˈɛk.strə pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrə pɔɪnt /; / ˈɛk.strə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a point scored in American football by kicking the ball through the uprights after a touchdown; more generally, an additional point awarded in a game as a bonus or after a specific achievement
