gamepad

noun
video game controller
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmpæd / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmpæd / (BrE)
a handheld device with buttons and joysticks used to control video games on a console or computer

Examples

A gamepad controller
He bought a new gamepad to play racing games on his computer.
Some players find a gamepad more comfortable than a mouse and keyboard for certain games.
The console comes with a wireless gamepad that charges via USB.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - a device used to operate or control a machine or system
  • synonym: joystick - a lever used to control movement in video games or machines
  • antonym: keyboard - a set of keys used to input data into a computer
