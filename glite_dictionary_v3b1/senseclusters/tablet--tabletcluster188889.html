tablet

noun
a small flat compressed cake of a substance
Pronunciation: / ˈtæblət / (AmE), / ˈtæblət /; / ˈtæblɪt / (BrE)
a small, solid, compressed piece of a substance, especially a dose of medicine, meant to be swallowed or used individually

Examples

A tablet of medicine
Each tablet contains the exact dosage required for your treatment.
She placed a tablet of soap in the bathroom dish before the guests arrived.
The doctor told me to dissolve a tablet in water and drink it once a day.
He accidentally dropped the tablet on the floor and had to get a new one.
Take one tablet every morning with a glass of water.
The doctor prescribed a new tablet to help with her headaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsule - a small container with medicine, usually gel-like and easy to swallow
  • synonym: pill - a small round mass of medicinal substance for swallowing
