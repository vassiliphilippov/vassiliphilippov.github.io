muchacha

noun
female domestic worker
9.8
Pronunciation: / muˈtʃɑtʃə / (AmE), / muˈtʃɑːtʃə / (BrE)
a female domestic worker or maid, especially in Spanish-speaking or Spanish-influenced contexts

Examples

A muchacha cleans
They hired a muchacha to help with cleaning and cooking while they worked long hours.
The muchacha arrived early every morning to tidy the house and prepare breakfast.
In some countries, it is common for families to employ a muchacha for household tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maid - a female domestic worker
