Synonyms

  • collectivization
    9.6 0.35/m
    collectivization is the state joining farms or factories into shared units, while communization means organizing society under communal or state ownership.
  • communalization
    9.1 0.00/m
    communalization is moving private property or services to local community control, while communization also includes state takeover and communist reorganization.
