giant tortoise

noun
a very large tortoise species
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɔr.təs / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɔː.təs / (BrE)
a very large tortoise native to the Galapagos and Seychelles islands, known for its great size and longevity

Examples
