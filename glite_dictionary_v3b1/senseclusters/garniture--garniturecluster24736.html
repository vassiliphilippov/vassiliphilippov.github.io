garniture

noun
decorative accessory set
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪtjʊə / (BrE)
a decorative accessory or set of ornaments, especially arranged on a table or mantelpiece

Examples

Mantelpiece garniture
The marble garniture on the fireplace added elegance to the living room.
She arranged a garniture of porcelain vases on the dining table for the party.
Collectors often seek antique garniture sets to display in their homes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoration - something that adds beauty or adornment
  • synonym: ornament - an object used to decorate something
