papal bull

noun
formal pope-issued proclamation
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpəl bʊl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəl bʊl / (BrE)
an official document or proclamation issued by the pope, typically sealed with a leaden seal

Examples

A papal bull document
Historians studied the papal bull for insights into the politics of the period.
Many medieval laws were enacted by means of a papal bull.
The pope issued a papal bull to address the growing concerns within the church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encyclical - a papal letter sent to all bishops of the Roman Catholic Church
