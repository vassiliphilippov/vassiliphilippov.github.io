brute

noun
a cruel or violent person
5.1
Pronunciation: / brut /; / bruːt / (AmE), / bruːt / (BrE)
a person or animal who is cruel, violent, rough, or lacking in compassion or sensitivity

Examples

A cruel brute
Even his friends admitted that he could be a brute when he was angry.
He acted like a brute towards anyone who disagreed with him.
The prison guard was known as a brute who showed no mercy to the inmates.
The wrestler was known as a brute in the ring, intimidating all his opponents.
She called him a brute after he broke the chair in anger.
Despite his size, the dog was no brute and was actually very gentle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beast - an animal, especially a large or dangerous one; also used metaphorically for a cruel person
  • antonym: gentleman - a polite or kind man, in contrast to a brute
  • synonym: monster - a person who is inhumanly cruel
  • synonym: savage - a person regarded as primitive or violent
