-ies

x
plural noun suffix
1.0
Pronunciation: / iz / (AmE), / iz / (BrE)
a suffix used to form the plural of nouns ending in 'y' by replacing 'y' with 'ies'

Examples

Cities and countries
The plural of 'city' is 'cities', following the rule for nouns ending in 'y'.
When a noun ends in a consonant plus 'y', you change the 'y' to 'ies' to make it plural.
Words like 'puppy' and 'party' both form their plurals with the 'ies' ending.
