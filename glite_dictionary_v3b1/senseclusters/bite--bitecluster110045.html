Synonyms

    nibble is a small, gentle bite often taken carefully, while bite is the general action or mark made by teeth.
  • chomp
    5.3 0.3/m
    chomp is a loud, vigorous chewing or biting action, while bite is the basic act of using teeth to grip or tear.
  • insect bite
    2.5 0.3/m
    insect bite is a wound from an insect that can itch or hurt, while bite is any mark or wound made by teeth.
