neocortical

adjective
relating to the neocortex of the brain
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈkɔrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈkɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the neocortex, the part of the cerebral cortex involved in higher brain functions
