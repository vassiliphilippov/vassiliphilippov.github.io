charcuterie

noun
shop or products specializing in cured meats
8.0 6.8
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrˈkuːtəri / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈkuːtəri / (BrE)
a shop or establishment specializing in the sale of cured meats, such as sausages, hams, and pâtés; also, the products sold in such a shop

Examples

Charcuterie shop
He learned how to prepare pâtés and terrines while working at a famous charcuterie in Paris.
She ordered a selection of meats from the local charcuterie for the party.
The new restaurant features a charcuterie board with artisan cheeses and sausages.
