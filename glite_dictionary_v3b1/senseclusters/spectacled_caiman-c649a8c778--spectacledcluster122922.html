spectacled caiman

noun
a caiman species with bony eye ridges
9.7
Pronunciation: / spɛk.tə.kəld ˈkeɪ.mən / (AmE), / spɛk.tə.kl̩d ˈkeɪ.mən / (BrE)
a species of caiman with bony ridges above the eyes, native to regions from southern Mexico to Argentina
