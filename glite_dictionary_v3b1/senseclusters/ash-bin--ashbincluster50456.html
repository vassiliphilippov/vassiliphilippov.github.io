ash-bin

noun
container for holding rubbish
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæʃ bɪn / (AmE), / ˈæʃ bɪn / (BrE)
A container for holding rubbish, especially household waste, until it is collected.

Examples

The ash-bin container
He carried the bag out to the ash-bin at the end of the alley.
Old newspapers and kitchen waste were thrown into the ash-bin.
The ash-bin was emptied by the collectors every Friday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dustbin - a container for household rubbish
  • synonym: trash can - a container for garbage or rubbish
