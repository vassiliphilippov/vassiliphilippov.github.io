warriorism

noun
support for military force
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːriərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈwɒriərɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in, promotion of, or quality of being a warrior or supporting warrior-like values, attitudes, or the use of military force
