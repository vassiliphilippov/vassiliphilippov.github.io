unbudging

adjective
firm and unyielding
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbʌdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
not willing to move, change position, or change one's opinion

Examples

Unbudging attitude
No matter how much we pleaded, her unbudging attitude did not change.
The committee remained unbudging on their decision despite public protests.
His unbudging stance made negotiations very difficult for everyone involved.
