white feldspar

noun
a white-colored feldspar mineral
5.2
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈfɛldˌspɑr / (AmE), / waɪt ˈfɛldˌspɑː / (BrE)
a commonly occurring feldspar mineral, typically white in color, that is a major component of many rocks

Examples
