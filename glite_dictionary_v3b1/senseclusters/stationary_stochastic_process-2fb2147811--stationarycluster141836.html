stationary stochastic process

noun
stochastic process with time-invariant distribution
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsteɪʃəˌneri stəˈkæstɪk ˈproʊsɛs / (AmE), / ˌsteɪʃənəri stəˈkæstɪk ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a stochastic process whose statistical properties, such as the distribution of random variables, do not change over time

A white noise sequence is a classic example of a stationary stochastic process in statistics.
