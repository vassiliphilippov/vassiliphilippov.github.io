Electric Boogaloo

noun
humorous phrase for an unnecessary or playful sequel
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈbuɡəˌlu / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈbuːɡəluː / (BrE)
a humorous or ironic phrase used as a subtitle or reference to a sequel, especially one perceived as unnecessary, exaggerated, or playful

Examples

Electric Boogaloo sequel
Many fans dubbed the movie's unnecessary sequel 'Electric Boogaloo' on social media.
They joked that the annual meeting was turning into 'Annual Meeting II: Electric Boogaloo'.
Whenever a project is restarted, someone inevitably calls it 'Electric Boogaloo' in the group chat.
