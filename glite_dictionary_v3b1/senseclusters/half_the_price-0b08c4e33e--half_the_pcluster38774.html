half the price

noun
costing 50% less than original
Pronunciation: / hæf ðə praɪs / (AmE), / hɑːf ðə praɪs / (BrE)
costing 50% less than the original price; at a price reduced by half.

Examples

Half the price sale
All items in the store are now available at half the price.
He bought a second ticket for half the price during the holiday sale.
If you wait until tomorrow, you might get it for half the price.

Related Terms

  • synonym: half-price - at half the usual or original cost
