glander

noun
infected individual
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlændər / (AmE), / ˈɡlændə / (BrE)
a person or animal infected with glanders, a contagious disease that mainly affects horses and other equines

Examples

A sick glander
The veterinarian quickly separated the glander from the rest of the herd.
Authorities ordered the destruction of any glander to prevent an outbreak.
It is important to identify a glander early to control the spread of the disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carrier - an individual who harbors a disease and can transmit it
  • antonym: healthy - not affected by disease
