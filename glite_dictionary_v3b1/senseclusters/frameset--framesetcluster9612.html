frameset

noun
group of animation frames
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪmˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈfreɪmˌsɛt / (BrE)
a group or sequence of individual frames used together in animation, video, or graphics to create motion

Examples

Animation frameset
The animator adjusted the frameset to make the character's walk cycle smoother.
Each frameset in the video game controls a different action, like jumping or running.
To create a realistic effect, she carefully designed every frame in the frameset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sequence - a set of related items arranged in order
  • synonym: sprite - a two-dimensional image or animation integrated into a larger scene
  • antonym: still - a single static image, not part of a sequence
