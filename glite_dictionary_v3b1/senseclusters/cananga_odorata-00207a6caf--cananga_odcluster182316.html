Cananga odorata

noun
tree producing ylang-ylang oil
Pronunciation: / kəˈnæŋɡə oʊˈdɔrətə / (AmE), / kəˈnaŋɡə əʊˈdɔːrətə / (BrE)
An evergreen Asian tree with fragrant greenish-yellow flowers, known for producing ylang-ylang oil, widely cultivated in tropical regions.

Examples

Cananga odorata tree
Many gardens in Southeast Asia feature Cananga odorata for its beautiful and aromatic flowers.
Studies show that Cananga odorata has calming properties due to its fragrant oil.
The essential oil extracted from Cananga odorata is widely used in perfumery.
