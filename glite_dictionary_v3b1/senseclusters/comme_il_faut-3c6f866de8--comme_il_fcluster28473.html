comme il faut

adjective
proper; socially correct
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / kʌm il ˈfoʊ / (AmE), / kɒm iːl ˈfəʊ / (BrE)
Proper or correct according to accepted standards or social conventions; appropriate or decorous.

Examples

A comme il faut manner
He always insists on behaving in a comme il faut manner at official dinners.
Her attire was perfectly comme il faut for the formal reception.
Such frankness was not considered comme il faut in aristocratic circles of the time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: improper - not in accordance with accepted standards; unsuitable
  • synonym: proper - in accordance with recognized standards; appropriate
