bottom-feeder

noun
fish that feeds on the bottom
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəm ˌfidər /; / ˈbɑːtəm ˌfiːdər / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm ˌfiːdə / (BrE)
a fish or animal that lives and eats food found at the bottom of a body of water, usually by scavenging

Examples
