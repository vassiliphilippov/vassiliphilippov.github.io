Dardic

noun
a group of Indic languages spoken in South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrdɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɑːdɪk / (BrE)
any of a group of Indic languages spoken in parts of Kashmir, eastern Afghanistan, and northern Pakistan

Examples

Dardic languages
Linguists have studied the Dardic languages to trace the development of Indo-Aryan dialects.
Many Dardic speakers live in remote valleys of northern Pakistan.
The Dardic branch includes languages such as Kashmiri and Shina.
