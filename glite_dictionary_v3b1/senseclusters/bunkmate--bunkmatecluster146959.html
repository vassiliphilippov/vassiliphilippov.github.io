bunkmate

noun
a person sharing your sleeping quarters
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋkˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who shares the same sleeping quarters, especially sharing a bunk bed or a room with you

Examples

My bunkmate snores
After the lights went out, his bunkmate started snoring loudly.
During summer camp, my bunkmate and I stayed up late telling ghost stories.
She quickly became close friends with her bunkmate during their first week at university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roommate - a person who shares a room or apartment with another
