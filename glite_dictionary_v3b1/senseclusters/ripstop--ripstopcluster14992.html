ripstop

adjective
tear-resistant fabric
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpˌstɑp / (AmE), / ˈrɪpˌstɒp / (BrE)
made of fabric that is woven with a reinforcing technique to resist tearing and ripping

Examples

Ripstop fabric jacket
The jacket is made from ripstop material, making it ideal for outdoor adventures.
Many camping tents use ripstop fabric to prevent small tears from spreading.
He bought a pair of ripstop pants for hiking in rough terrain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: durable - able to withstand wear, pressure, or damage
  • antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
