Pannonic

adjective
of the Pannonian Basin
Pronunciation: / pəˈnɑnɪk / (AmE), / pəˈnɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Pannonian geological stage or to the sediments and deposits of the Pannonian Basin from the late Miocene period

Examples

Pannonic sediments
The Pannonic sediments provide valuable information about the climate of the late Miocene.
Researchers discovered fossils in Pannonic layers beneath the surface of the basin.
The Pannonic deposits are important for understanding the geological history of Central Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pannonian - relating to the Pannonian Basin or its geological period
