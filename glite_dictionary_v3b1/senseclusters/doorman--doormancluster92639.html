doorman

noun
man guarding or attending a building entrance
1.0 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdɔːr.mən/ /; / /ˈdɔːr.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈdɔː.mən/ /; / /ˈdɔː.mæn/ (BrE)
