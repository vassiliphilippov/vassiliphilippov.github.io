loud holler

noun
a very loud shout or cry
Pronunciation: / laʊd ˈhɑlər / (AmE), / laʊd ˈhɒlə / (BrE)
a very loud shout or cry, often expressing strong emotion or calling attention

Examples

A loud holler
A sudden loud holler woke everyone in the quiet camp.
His loud holler echoed across the valley, startling the birds.
The coach let out a loud holler from the sidelines when they scored the goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shout - a loud call or cry expressing strong emotion or seeking attention
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very softly, without using the vocal cords
