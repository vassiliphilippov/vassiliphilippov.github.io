slow march

noun
procession or walk at a slow pace
2.3 4.0
Pronunciation: /sloʊ mɑːrtʃ/ (AmE), /sləʊ mɑːtʃ/ (BrE)
a walk or procession carried out at a deliberately slow pace, often for ceremonial or formal purposes

Examples

The mourners proceeded in a slow march behind the hearse.
