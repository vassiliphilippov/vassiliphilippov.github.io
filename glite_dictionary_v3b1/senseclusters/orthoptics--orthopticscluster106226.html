orthoptics

noun
nonsurgical treatment of binocular vision defects
2.7 8.1
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.ˈθɑːp.tɪks/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈθɒp.tɪks/ (BrE)
the treatment of defects in binocular vision, such as strabismus or amblyopia, using nonsurgical techniques, especially eye muscle exercises

Examples

Orthoptics treatment
