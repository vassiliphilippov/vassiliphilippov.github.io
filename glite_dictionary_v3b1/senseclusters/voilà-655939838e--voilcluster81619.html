voilà

interjection
attention to result
8.6
Pronunciation: /vwɑː.ˈlɑː/ /; / /vɑː.ˈlɑː/ /; / /vwʌ.ˈlɑː/ (AmE), /vwɑː.ˈlɑː/ /; / /vwæl.ˈɑː/ /; / /vwʌ.ˈɑː/ (BrE)
used to draw attention to something that has just appeared, been completed, or revealed (meaning 'here it is' or 'there you are')

Examples

Voilà here
I pressed the button and, voilà, the lights turned on instantly.
She finished the painting and, voilà, a beautiful landscape appeared.
Mix the ingredients together and, voilà, you have a delicious cake.
She handed him the wrapped gift and said, 'voilà! I hope you like it.'
After hours of work, he finished the puzzle and exclaimed, 'voilà!'
The chef placed the dish on the table and announced, 'voilà, dinner is served.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: behold - used to call attention to something, especially something impressive
  • synonym: here you go - used when giving something to someone
  • synonym: ta-da - used to announce something completed or revealed
