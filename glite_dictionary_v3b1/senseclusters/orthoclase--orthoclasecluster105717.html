orthoclase

noun
a mineral in the feldspar group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrθəˌkleɪs / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəkleɪz / (BrE)
a white or colored mineral of the feldspar group, often found in igneous rocks, with a monoclinic crystal structure

Examples

Orthoclase mineral
Orthoclase can be found in many types of igneous rocks, such as granite and syenite.

The granite countertop contains visible crystals of orthoclase along with quartz and mica.
