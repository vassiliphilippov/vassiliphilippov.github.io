kiss goodbye

verb
accept or let go of something lost
Pronunciation: / kɪs ɡəˈdbaɪ / (AmE), / kɪs ɡəˈdbʌɪ / (BrE)
To accept the loss of something or to let go of something, often with regret or resignation.

Examples

To kiss goodbye hopes
After the failed exam, she had to kiss goodbye to her scholarship hopes.
If we miss this final train, we'll have to kiss goodbye to getting home tonight.
When the funding was cut, the team kissed goodbye to their research project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: give up - to stop trying to achieve something; relinquish hope
