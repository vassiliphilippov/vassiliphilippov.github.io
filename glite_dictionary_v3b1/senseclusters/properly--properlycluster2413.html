properly

adverb
in the correct or appropriate way
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.pɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈprɒp.ə.li /; / ˈprɒp.əli / (BrE)
in a correct, suitable, or appropriate way

Examples

To properly wash hands
It is important to wash your hands properly before eating.
