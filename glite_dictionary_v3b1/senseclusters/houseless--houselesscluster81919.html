houseless

adjective
without houses
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsləs / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsləs / (BrE)
having no houses or permanent buildings, especially referring to a place or area

Examples

Houseless land
After the fire, the once busy street was left completely houseless.
The explorers crossed a vast, houseless plain that stretched to the horizon.
Following the storm, the village became houseless and deserted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: populated - having people living in a place
  • synonym: uninhabited - not lived in by people
