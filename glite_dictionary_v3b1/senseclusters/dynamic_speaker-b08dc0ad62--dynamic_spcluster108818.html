dynamic speaker

noun
type of loudspeaker
3.4 5.5
Pronunciation: / daɪˈnæmɪk ˈspiːkər / (AmE), / daɪˈnæmɪk ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
A type of loudspeaker that produces sound by using a lightweight diaphragm attached to a voice coil which moves through a magnetic field.

Examples

A dynamic speaker unit
A dynamic speaker converts electrical signals into vibrations that produce sound waves.
Replacing the old cone with a modern dynamic speaker made the music sound much better.
The new sound system uses a high-efficiency dynamic speaker for improved audio clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loudspeaker - device that converts electrical signals into sound
