unit of ammunition

noun
single charge of ammunition
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnɪt əv ˌæməˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪt əv ˌaməˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
A single charge of ammunition, such as a cartridge or shell, prepared for firing one shot.

Examples

One unit of ammunition
Each soldier was issued one unit of ammunition before the training exercise.
He carefully counted every unit of ammunition left in the box.
The weapon can only be fired once per unit of ammunition loaded.
