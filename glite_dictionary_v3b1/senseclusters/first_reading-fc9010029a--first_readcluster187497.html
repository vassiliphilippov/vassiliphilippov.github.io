first reading

noun
initial presentation of a bill in legislature
1.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /fɜːrst ˈriː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /fɜːst ˈriː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
The initial presentation of a bill in a legislative body, typically before formal debate or examination begins.

Examples

The first reading of a bill

During the first reading, the bill is introduced but not debated in detail.
The first reading of the education reform bill will take place in Parliament next Monday.
