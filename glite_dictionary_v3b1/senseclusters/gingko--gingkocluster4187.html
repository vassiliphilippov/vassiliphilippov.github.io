gingko

noun
ornamental Chinese tree with fan-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
A deciduous Chinese tree (Ginkgo biloba) with fan-shaped leaves and fleshy yellow seeds, often grown as an ornamental street tree.

Examples

Gingko tree leaves
She collected fallen gingko leaves for her science project.
The gingko is valued both for its beauty and its resilience in urban environments.
The city lined its main avenue with gingko trees, which turn bright yellow in autumn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maidenhair tree - another name for ginkgo, especially used in horticulture
