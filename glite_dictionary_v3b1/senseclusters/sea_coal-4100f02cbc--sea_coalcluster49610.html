sea coal

noun
type of pulverized bituminous coal used in foundries
8.3
Pronunciation: / siː koʊl / (AmE), / siː kəʊl / (BrE)
Pulverized bituminous coal, especially used as a facing in metal foundries.

Examples

Pulverized sea coal
During the 19th century, sea coal was an essential material in many industrial processes.
The foundry workers applied sea coal as a facing to the molds before pouring the molten metal.
They ordered several tons of sea coal for the steel plant's casting operations.
