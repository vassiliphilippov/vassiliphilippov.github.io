interdependently

adverb
with mutual reliance
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərdɪˈpɛndəntli / (AmE), / ˌɪntədɪˈpɛndəntli / (BrE)
in a manner where two or more people or things rely on each other for support or success

Examples

Work interdependently with others
The two companies operated interdependently to maximize their resources and reach new markets.
Ecosystems function interdependently, with each species playing a vital role in the balance of nature.
The departments must work interdependently to ensure the project's overall success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperatively - in a way that involves working together for a common purpose
  • antonym: independently - in a way that does not rely on others
