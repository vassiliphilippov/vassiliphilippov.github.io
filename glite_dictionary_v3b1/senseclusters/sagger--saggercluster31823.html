sagger

noun
kiln pottery container
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæɡər / (AmE), / ˈsæɡə / (BrE)
a protective container made of fire-resistant material used in kilns to shield pottery during firing

Examples

Kiln sagger container
The ceramic bowls were placed in a sagger to protect them from direct flame in the kiln.
Each sagger was carefully stacked to maximize space during the pottery firing process.
Using a sagger helps prevent ash and debris from damaging delicate porcelain pieces.

  • synonym: kiln - a furnace or oven for firing pottery or bricks
