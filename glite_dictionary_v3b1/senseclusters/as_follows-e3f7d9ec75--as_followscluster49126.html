as follows

adverb
introduces list or explanation
Pronunciation: / æz fəˈloʊz / (AmE), / æz fəˈləʊz / (BrE)
used to introduce a list or explanation that comes next

Examples

As follows: the list
The answers to the exam questions were as follows: A, C, B, and D.
The process for submitting your application is as follows: fill out the form and send it to the office.
The terms of the agreement are as follows: both parties must adhere to the schedule.
