spinneret

noun
fiber-forming device
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspɪn.ər.ət/ (AmE), /ˈspɪn.ə.rət/ (BrE)
a small device with many tiny holes used to form continuous filaments by forcing liquid material through it, especially in making synthetic fibers

Examples

Synthetic fiber spinneret
The spinneret is essential for producing fine nylon threads in textile factories.
Technicians cleaned the spinneret to prevent blockages during the fiber manufacturing process.
A damaged spinneret can cause uneven thickness in synthetic filaments.
