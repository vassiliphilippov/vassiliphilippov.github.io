reformational

adjective
of Reformational philosophy
Pronunciation: / rɪˌfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / rɪˌfɔːˈmeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to Reformational philosophy, a 20th-century Christian philosophical movement associated with Dooyeweerd and Vollenhoven.

The university offers a course on reformational approaches to ethics and society.
Many scholars debate the impact of reformational thought on modern Christian education.
