historicize

verb
to make historical
8.1
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔːrɪˌsaɪz / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪsaɪz / (BrE)
to give something the appearance, style, or character of a past historical period

Examples

To historicize a building
The architect wanted to historicize the building by adding classical columns to its facade.
They chose to historicize the festival with costumes and decorations from the 18th century.
The film director decided to historicize the set to match the era of the story.
