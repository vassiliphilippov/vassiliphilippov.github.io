Ethernet

noun
local area network technology
6.6 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.θər.net/ (AmE), /ˈiː.θə.net/ (BrE)
a system for connecting computers in a local area network using protocols and cables that allow data transfer at high speed

Examples

Ethernet cable connection
Many modern laptops now omit Ethernet ports to save space.
The computers in our office are connected via Ethernet for greater reliability.
To set up a wired connection, simply plug the Ethernet cable into your router and device.
