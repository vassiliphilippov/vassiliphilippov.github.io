nodulated

adjective
having or forming nodules
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑdʒəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɒdjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having nodules or formed in the shape of nodules; characterized by small lumps or swellings.

Examples

Nodulated thyroid gland
Doctors found that her thyroid gland appeared nodulated on the ultrasound scan.
The roots of leguminous plants are often nodulated due to the presence of nitrogen-fixing bacteria.
This mineral sample is highly nodulated, giving it a rough, uneven surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nodular - having the form of a nodule; knobby or lumpy
