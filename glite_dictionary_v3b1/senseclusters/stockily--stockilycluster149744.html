stockily

adverb
in a stocky manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːkɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɒkɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is broad, solid, and usually short in build or form.

Examples

Walk stockily
He walked stockily across the room, shoulders hunched.
She stood stockily beside her taller friends at the ceremony.
The dog was stockily built, giving it a sturdy appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stocky - broad and solidly built; thickset
