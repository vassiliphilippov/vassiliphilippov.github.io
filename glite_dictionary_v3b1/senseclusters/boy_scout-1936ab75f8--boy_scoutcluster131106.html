boy scout

noun
member of youth organization (Scouts)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ ˌskaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ skaʊt / (BrE)
A member of a youth organization dedicated to developing good citizenship, practical skills, and personal responsibility, known for being helpful and prepared.

Examples

Boy scout camp
After joining as a boy scout, he quickly learned first aid and camping skills.
She thanked the boy scout for guiding her through the unfamiliar trail.
The boy scout earned a badge for helping his community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Girl Scout - a girl member of the corresponding youth organization
  • synonym: Scout - a member of the Scouts organization, regardless of gender
