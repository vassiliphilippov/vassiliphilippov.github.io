carbapenem

noun
potent antibiotic
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrbəˈpiːnəm / (AmE), / ˌkɑːbəˈpiːnəm / (BrE)
a powerful antibiotic used to treat serious bacterial infections, especially those resistant to other antibiotics

Examples

A carbapenem antibiotic
Doctors may prescribe a carbapenem for infections resistant to other antibiotics.
The hospital used a carbapenem to treat the patient's severe bacterial infection.
Some bacteria have developed resistance even to carbapenems, making treatment more difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cephalosporin - a class of antibiotics similar to penicillins
  • synonym: penicillin - a group of antibiotics used to treat bacterial infections
