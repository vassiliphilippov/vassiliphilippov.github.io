carbapenem

noun
potent antibiotic
7.2
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.bə.ˈpen.əm/ (AmE), /kɑː.bə.ˈpen.əm/ (BrE)

Examples

A carbapenem antibiotic

Some bacteria have developed resistance even to carbapenems, making treatment more difficult.
