wallow

verb
(of animals) roll about in mud or water
5.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑː.loʊ/ /; / /ˈwɔː.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈwɒ.ləʊ/ (BrE)
(of animals) to roll about or lie in mud, water, or dust for refreshment, pleasure, or to keep cool.

Examples

To wallow in mud
After the rain, the cattle were seen wallowing in the wet field.
Buffalo often wallow in shallow water to cool off.
The piglets wallowed happily in the mud under the hot sun.
