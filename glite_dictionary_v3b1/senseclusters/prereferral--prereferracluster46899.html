prereferral

adjective
before formal referral
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː.rɪˈfɜːr.əl / (AmE), / ˌpriː.rɪˈfɜː.rəl / (BrE)
occurring or implemented before a formal referral, especially in educational or support processes

Examples

Prereferral intervention plan
The school developed prereferral interventions to help students before considering special education services.
A prereferral meeting was held to discuss strategies for supporting the child in the classroom.
Teachers are encouraged to use prereferral processes to address learning difficulties early.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postreferral - occurring after a formal referral
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before something more important
