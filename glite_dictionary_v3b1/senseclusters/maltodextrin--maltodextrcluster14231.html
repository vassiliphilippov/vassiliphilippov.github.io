maltodextrin

noun
food carbohydrate additive
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmæltoʊˈdɛkstrɪn / (AmE), / ˌmaltəʊˈdɛkstrɪn / (BrE)
a white powder made from starch, used as a food additive to provide energy, thicken, sweeten, or bulk up processed foods and supplements
