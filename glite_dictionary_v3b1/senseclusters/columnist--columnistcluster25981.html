columnist

noun
journalist who writes opinion articles
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑləmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɒləmnɪst / (BrE)
a journalist who regularly writes articles giving opinions or commentary, typically in a newspaper or magazine

Examples

A columnist writes articles
Many readers look forward to the advice columnist's weekly insights.
She worked as a columnist for the city newspaper for more than twenty years.
The political columnist sparked controversy with his latest editorial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editorialist - a journalist who writes editorials
