anadama bread

noun
New England bread with cornmeal and molasses
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈdæmə brɛd / (AmE), / ˌænəˈdæmə brɛd / (BrE)
A traditional yeast bread made with white flour, cornmeal, and molasses, originating in New England.
