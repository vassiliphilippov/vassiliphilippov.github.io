verticil

noun
whorl of leaves or organs on a stem
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrtɪsɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪsɪl / (BrE)
A ring of leaves or other organs arranged in a circle around the stem of a plant.

Examples

A verticil of leaves
Each verticil of leaves emerges at regular intervals as the plant grows.
Researchers noted the number of verticils on each specimen during the study.
The flowers are arranged in a distinct verticil along the stem.
