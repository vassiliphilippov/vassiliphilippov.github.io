limner

noun
portrait painter or illustrator
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmnər / (AmE), / ˈlɪmnə / (BrE)
an artist who paints or draws portraits, especially one working before the modern era

Examples

A limner paints portraits
Before photography, people relied on a limner to capture their appearance for posterity.
Many colonial American limners left behind unsigned but detailed likenesses of their subjects.
The wealthy merchant hired a limner to paint his family's portrait.
