champion

noun
winner of a competition
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæm.pi.ən /; / ˈtʃæmpiən / (AmE), / ˈtʃæm.pi.ən /; / ˈtʃæmpiən / (BrE)
a person who has won first place or is the best in a competition, contest, or field

Examples

Tennis champion
After years of hard training, she became the champion of the national tennis tournament.
Every champion remembers the moment they first held their trophy.
The reigning champion defended his title in an exciting final match.
He became the champion of the national chess tournament after years of practice.
She is regarded as a champion in the field of medical research.
The champion of the spelling bee was interviewed by several newspapers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loser - a person who loses in a competition
  • synonym: winner - a person who wins something
