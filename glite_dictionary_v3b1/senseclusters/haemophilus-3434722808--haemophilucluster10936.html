Haemophilus

proper noun
bacterial genus
Pronunciation: / hiˈmɑːfɪləs / (AmE), / hiˈmɒfɪləs /; / hɪˈmɒfɪləs / (BrE)
a genus of bacteria that can cause infections in humans, especially respiratory infections
