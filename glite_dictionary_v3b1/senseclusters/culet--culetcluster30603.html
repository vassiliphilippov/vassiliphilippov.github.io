culet

noun
gemstone bottom facet
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːlɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːlɪt / (BrE)
the small, flat facet at the bottom of a cut gemstone, especially a diamond

Examples

Diamond culet
The jeweler examined the culet to ensure the diamond was cut correctly.
A large culet can sometimes be seen as a tiny dot when looking through the top of a gemstone.
Some modern diamonds are cut without a culet to improve brilliance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facet - a flat surface on a cut gemstone
