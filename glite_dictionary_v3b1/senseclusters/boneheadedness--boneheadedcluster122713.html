boneheadedness

noun
foolish stubbornness
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnˌhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very foolish or stubborn, especially in a way that leads to mistakes

Examples

His boneheadedness mistake
His boneheadedness caused the team to lose an easy game.
She laughed at the boneheadedness of forgetting her own birthday.
The manager's boneheadedness was obvious when he ignored all the warnings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstinacy - stubborn refusal to change one's opinion or action
  • synonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or common sense
  • antonym: wisdom - the ability to make good decisions and judgments
