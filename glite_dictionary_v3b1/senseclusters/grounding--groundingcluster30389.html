grounding

noun
mindfulness coping technique
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈgraʊn.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgraʊn.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
a psychological coping technique using sensory or mindfulness-based exercises to bring attention to the present and reduce anxiety, panic, or dissociation.

Examples

Grounding exercises
During the anxiety attack, she practiced grounding by focusing on her five senses.
The therapist taught him a simple grounding exercise to stop dissociation during stressful moments.
When panic rose, he used grounding techniques like naming objects to feel present again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coping strategy - a method or technique used to manage stress, anxiety, or difficult emotions
  • synonym: mindfulness - the practice of focusing on the present moment, often to reduce stress or improve awareness
