conglomerate

verb
to combine into a group
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡlɑːmɚˌeɪt / (AmE), / kənˈɡlɒməreɪt / (BrE)
to gather or combine different things into a single mass or group

Examples

To conglomerate companies
The small businesses decided to conglomerate in order to compete with larger companies.
Over time, dust and debris conglomerate in the corners of the old house.
Several organizations conglomerated their resources to launch the new community project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregate - to collect or gather into a whole
  • antonym: separate - to set or keep apart
