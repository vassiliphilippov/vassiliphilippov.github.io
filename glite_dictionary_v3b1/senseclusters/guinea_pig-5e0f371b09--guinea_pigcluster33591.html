guinea pig

noun
person used in an experiment or trial
8.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪn.ɪ.ə pɪg/ (AmE), /ˈgɪn.ɪ.ə pɪg/ (BrE)
a person who is used as a subject in an experiment or test; someone who tries something new so others can observe the results

Examples

A human guinea pig
I'm not sure I want to be your guinea pig for testing that software.
She felt like a guinea pig during the clinical trial for the new vaccine.
The students volunteered to be guinea pigs for the psychology experiment.
During the training session, she volunteered as a guinea pig so others could learn from her experience.
I don’t mind being a guinea pig if it will help improve medical treatments for others.
When the company launched its new software, the employees were the first guinea pigs to try it out.
