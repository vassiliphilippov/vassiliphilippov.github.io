shootdown

noun
aircraft destruction
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
the act of bringing down an aircraft by shooting at it, especially in a military context

Examples

The shootdown of a plane
The military confirmed the shootdown of the enemy fighter jet during the conflict.
After the shootdown, rescue teams searched for the pilot in the remote area.
International tensions rose following the shootdown of the surveillance aircraft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash - an accident in which an aircraft falls to the ground
  • synonym: downing - the act of causing an aircraft to fall from the sky
