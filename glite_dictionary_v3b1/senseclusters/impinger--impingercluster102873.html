impinger

noun
one who interferes
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪndʒər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person or thing that forcefully affects, encroaches upon, or interferes with something else

Examples

Impinger on rules
The new regulations acted as an impinger on small businesses, reducing their operating hours significantly.
As an impinger, the loud construction disrupted the peaceful atmosphere of the library.
Some critics see the policy as an impinger on personal freedoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interferer - someone who gets involved in a situation where they are not wanted
  • synonym: intruder - a person who enters a place or situation without permission
  • antonym: supporter - someone who helps or encourages a cause or person
