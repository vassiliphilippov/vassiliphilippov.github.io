unreviewable

adjective
not subject to review
9.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Àå ån.r…™ÀàvjuÀê.…ô.b…ôl / (AmE), / Àå ån.r…™ÀàvjuÀê.…ô.b…ôl / (BrE)
not able to be examined, reconsidered, or judged by a higher authority or court

Examples

Unreviewable decision
The judge declared the administrative decision unreviewable under current law.
Some government actions are considered unreviewable and cannot be challenged in court.
The contract included a clause making the arbitration result unreviewable by any party.

Synonyms

  • nonreviewable
    7.9 0.01/m
    nonreviewable is used in official rules and documents to say something cannot be reviewed, while unreviewable is the general adjective for that idea.
  • unappealable
    8.8 0.03/m
    unappealable means a decision cannot be changed by an appeal in court, while unreviewable covers any refusal to be examined or judged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appealable - able to be challenged or reviewed by a higher authority
  • synonym: final - not able to be changed or reversed
