selfie

noun
self-taken photo
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlfi / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfi / (BrE)
a photograph that you take of yourself, usually with a smartphone or webcam, often to share on social media

Examples

Take a selfie
She posted a selfie from the top of the mountain to share her adventure with friends.
Before the concert started, he took a quick selfie with the stage in the background.
Many tourists like to take a selfie in front of famous landmarks during their travels.
He took a selfie in front of the Eiffel Tower to remember his trip.
Before the concert started, she snapped a quick selfie with her friends.
Many people post at least one selfie a day on their social media accounts.

Many celebrities share selfies with their fans on social media platforms.
