unflatteringly

adverb
in an unattractive way
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflætərɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈflætərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not make someone or something look good or attractive

Examples

Unflatteringly shown
The photograph showed him unflatteringly, highlighting every wrinkle and flaw.
She was described unflatteringly in the review, which focused on her mistakes.
The movie portrays the city unflatteringly, emphasizing its problems rather than its beauty.
