node

noun
swelling or thickened enlargement
5.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /noʊd/ (AmE), /nəʊd/ (BrE)
a swelling or point on a plant stem from which leaves, branches, or flowers grow

Examples

Plant stem node
Knots or nodes can be seen along the stem of certain plants.
The biopsy confirmed the enlargement was a benign node.
The doctor found a small node on her thyroid during the examination.
Buds and branches can also develop at a node.
Each leaf grows from a node along the plant's stem.
The distance between each node is known as the internode.
