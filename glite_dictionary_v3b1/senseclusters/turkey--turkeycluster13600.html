turkey

noun
complete failure (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːki / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki / (BrE)
an event, performance, or thing that fails completely or is very unsuccessful

Examples

Turkey at the box office
His first attempt at baking a cake was a complete turkey.
Many critics called the movie a turkey because of its poor script and acting.
The new play turned out to be a turkey and closed within a week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dud - something that fails to work or is unsuccessful
  • synonym: flop - a complete failure, especially a theatrical production
  • antonym: success - the achievement of an aim or purpose
