modalist

noun
composer using modes
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊdəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈməʊdəlɪst / (BrE)
a musician or composer who uses musical modes or emphasizes modal harmony in their work

Examples

Modalist composer
The famous modalist drew inspiration from medieval church music for his latest composition.
As a dedicated modalist, she often experiments with unusual scales in her pieces.
Many modern composers admire the work of a modalist for its unique harmonic language.
