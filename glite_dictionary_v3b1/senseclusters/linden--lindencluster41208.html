linden

noun
deciduous tree of the genus Tilia
7.4 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪn.dən/ (AmE), /ˈlɪn.dən/ (BrE)
A deciduous tree of the genus Tilia, having heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant yellowish flowers; some species provide valuable timber.

The furniture was crafted from linden wood, known for its fine grain and light color.
