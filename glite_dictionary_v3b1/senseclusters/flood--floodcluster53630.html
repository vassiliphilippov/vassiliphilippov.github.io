flood

noun
a large, overwhelming quantity
2.4 5.89/m
Pronunciation: / flÊŒd / (AmE), / flÊŒd / (BrE)
a very large number or amount of things that arrive or appear suddenly, often more than can be handled

Examples

A flood of emails
After the product launch, there was a flood of customer inquiries.
She was met with a flood of support from her friends during the difficult time.
The company received a flood of applications after advertising the job opening.
After the announcement, a flood of messages poured into her inbox.
He was overwhelmed by a flood of emotions during the ceremony.
The river burst its banks and sent a flood of water through the town.
