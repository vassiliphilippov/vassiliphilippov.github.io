crescent-cell anemia

noun
anemia with crescent-shaped red blood cells
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛsənt sɛl əˈniːmiə / (AmE), / ˈkrɛsnt sɛl əˈniːmiə /; / ˈkrɛsənt sɛl əˈniːmiə / (BrE)
A hereditary or congenital form of anemia, primarily affecting people of African descent, in which red blood cells become crescent-shaped and break down prematurely.
