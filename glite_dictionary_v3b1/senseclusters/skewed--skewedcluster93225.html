skewed

adjective
slanting or oblique in position
5.2
Pronunciation: / skjud / (AmE), / skjuːd / (BrE)
Not straight or level; having a slanting or oblique direction or position.

Examples

A skewed view
His view of the situation was skewed by personal bias.
Make sure the shelves are not skewed before you fix them to the wall.
The painting hung slightly skewed on the wall, irritating her perfectionist eye.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight - having no angle or curve; not slanting
