noncombustion

noun
absence of burning
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəmˈbʌs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəmˈbʌs.tʃən / (BrE)
a process or condition in which burning or combustion does not take place

Examples

Noncombustion process
Electric vehicles use noncombustion energy sources to reduce emissions.
The new heating system operates through noncombustion methods, making it safer for indoor use.
Researchers are developing noncombustion engines to minimize air pollution in cities.
