gateball

noun
ball for gateball sport
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small, hard ball used in the sport of gateball, typically made of wood or plastic and struck with a mallet

Examples

A gateball ball
He polished his gateball before the match to ensure a smooth roll.
Each player must bring their own gateball to the tournament.
The referee checked the gateball for any cracks before the game started.
