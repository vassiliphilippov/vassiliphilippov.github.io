frost-weed

noun
a tall perennial herb with white flowers
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑst wid /; / ˈfrɑstˌwid / (AmE), / ˈfrɒst wiːd /; / ˈfrɒstˌwiːd / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to the eastern United States, known for producing ice or frost-like formations at its base or stems during the first frosts, and for its clusters of flowers
