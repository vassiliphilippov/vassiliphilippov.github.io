pin on

verb
attribute blame or responsibility to someone
5.1
Pronunciation: / pɪn ɑn / (AmE), / pɪn ɒn / (BrE)
to attribute blame or responsibility for something, often unfairly, to a person

Examples

To pin on blame
It's wrong to pin on someone else what you have done yourself.
The committee tried to pin on her the responsibility for the mistake.
They always look for a scapegoat to pin on any problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blame - to assign responsibility for a fault or wrong
  • antonym: exonerate - to officially absolve someone from blame or fault
