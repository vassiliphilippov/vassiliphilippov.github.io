confederation

noun
state of being allied; alliance
4.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /kən.fə.də.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kən.fed.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kən.fed.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kən.fə.də.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a group of states, organizations, or people that join together for a common purpose, while keeping their independence

The successful confederation of several tribes led to increased security for the region.
