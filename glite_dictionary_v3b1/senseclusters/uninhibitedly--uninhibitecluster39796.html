uninhibitedly

adverb
freely, without restraint
7.5
Pronunciation: /ʌ.nɪn.hɪ.ˈbɪt.ɪd.liː/ /; / /ə.nɪn.hɪ.ˈbɪt.ɪd.liː/ (AmE), /ʌ.nɪn.hɪ.ˈbɪt.ɪd.liː/ /; / /ə.nɪn.hɪ.ˈbɪt.ɪd.liː/ (BrE)

He expressed his opinions uninhibitedly, never afraid to speak his mind.
