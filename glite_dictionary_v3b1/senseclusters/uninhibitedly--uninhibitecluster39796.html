uninhibitedly

adverb
freely, without restraint
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈhɪbɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈhɪbɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a free and unrestrained manner, without holding back feelings or actions

Examples

Laugh uninhibitedly
She laughed uninhibitedly at the comedian's jokes, not caring who was watching.
The children played uninhibitedly in the park, running and shouting with joy.
He expressed his opinions uninhibitedly, never afraid to speak his mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freely - without restriction or limitation
  • synonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden
  • antonym: reservedly - in a restrained or cautious manner
