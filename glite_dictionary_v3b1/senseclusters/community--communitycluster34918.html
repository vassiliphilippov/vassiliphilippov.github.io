community

noun
a group sharing interests or goals
1.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəti /; / kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
a state of sharing or having something in common, such as interests, values, or ownership

Examples

Community spirit
A sense of community was built through shared goals and open discussion.
The two companies formed a community of interest to promote new technology.
They discovered a community of purpose that made collaboration easy.
Monks in the abbey lived in a community of property and purpose.
The two families developed a strong community of interests over the years.
True friendship is often based on a community of values and beliefs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discord - lack of agreement or harmony
  • synonym: joint ownership - ownership shared by two or more people
  • synonym: solidarity - unity or agreement of feeling or action, especially among individuals with a common interest
