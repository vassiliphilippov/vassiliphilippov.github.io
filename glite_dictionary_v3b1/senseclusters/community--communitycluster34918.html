Synonyms

  • communality
    5.8 0.13/m
    communality describes people sharing goods, duties, or life closely in a group, while community means having things or interests in common.
  • commonality
    4.6 3.17/m
    commonality names shared features or attributes among people, while community describes a group that shares interests, values, or ownership.
  • collectiveness
    5.7 0.01/m
    collectiveness refers to group action or ownership as a principle, while community is a social state of sharing interests or possessions.
