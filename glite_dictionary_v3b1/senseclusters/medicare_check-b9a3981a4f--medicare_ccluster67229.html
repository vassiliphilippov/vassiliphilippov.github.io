Medicare check

noun
check for Medicare medical expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛd.ɪˌker tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈmɛd.ɪˌkeə tʃɛk / (BrE)
a check sent to an elderly person as payment or reimbursement for medical expenses under the Medicare program in the United States

Examples

A Medicare check
After her surgery, Jill waited anxiously for her Medicare check to cover the hospital costs.
He received his Medicare check in the mail and deposited it at the bank the same day.
Many elderly Americans rely on a Medicare check to help pay for their prescription medications.
