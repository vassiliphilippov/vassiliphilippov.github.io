second-stringer

noun
non-starting sports team member
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkəndˈstrɪŋər / (AmE), / ˌsɛkəndˈstrɪŋə / (BrE)
a sports player who is not a regular starter on a team and usually plays only as a substitute

Examples

A second-stringer player
After the injury, the coach called in a second-stringer to fill the position.
Although he was a second-stringer last season, he worked hard to earn a spot in the starting lineup.
Every good team needs reliable second-stringers to step up when needed.
