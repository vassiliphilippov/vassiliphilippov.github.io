cystogram

noun
bladder X-ray test
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəɡræm / (BrE)
a medical X-ray test of the bladder using contrast dye, or the X-ray image produced by this test

Examples

A cystogram test
The doctor ordered a cystogram to check for any leaks after the patient's bladder surgery.
A cystogram can help diagnose injuries to the bladder following an accident.
Before removing the catheter, the nurse performed a cystogram to ensure proper healing.
The doctor reviewed the cystogram to look for any signs of bladder damage.
A cystogram can help detect leaks or injuries after bladder surgery.
Before the procedure, the nurse explained how a cystogram is performed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cystoscopy - a procedure to examine the inside of the bladder with a camera
  • synonym: urogram - an X-ray of the urinary tract using contrast dye
