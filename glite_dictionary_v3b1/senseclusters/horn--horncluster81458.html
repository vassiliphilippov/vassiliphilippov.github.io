horn

noun
the tough material covering animal horns and forming hooves or claws
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /hɔːrn/ /; / /hoʊrn/ (AmE), /hɔːn/ (BrE)
the hard, tough material mainly composed of keratin, covering the horns of certain animals and forming hooves, claws, and nails

Examples

Horn material
Claws, hooves, and horn are all composed mainly of keratin.
Rhino horn is prized in some cultures, though it is made from the same material as human fingernails.
The blacksmith examined the horn of the horse's hoof for signs of disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keratin - the fibrous protein forming the main structural constituent of horn, hair, feathers, hooves, and claws
