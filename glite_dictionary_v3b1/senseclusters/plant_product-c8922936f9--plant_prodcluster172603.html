plant product

noun
product made from plant material
Pronunciation: / plænt ˈprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
a product made from plant material, such as food, fibers, or extracts derived from plants

Examples

Plant products from nature

Some medicines are developed from plant products with natural healing properties.
Soy milk is a plant product commonly used as a dairy alternative.
