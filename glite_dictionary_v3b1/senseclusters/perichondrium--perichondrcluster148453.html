perichondrium

noun
cartilage covering tissue
9.9
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪ.ˈkɑːn.drɪ.əm/ /; / /pɛr.ɪ.ˈkɑːn.drɪ.əm/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.ˈkɒn.drɪ.əm/ /; / /pɛr.ɪ.ˈkɒn.drɪ.əm/ (BrE)
a layer of dense connective tissue that surrounds cartilage in the body

Examples

The perichondrium layer
The perichondrium supplies nutrients to the cartilage it surrounds.
Injury to the perichondrium can slow down the healing of cartilage.
