elaborator

noun
processing machine
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæbəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈlæbəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a machine or device that processes raw materials or ingredients into a more refined or usable form

Examples

Juice elaborator
The company invested in a new elaborator to improve the quality of their fruit juice products.
After installing the elaborator, the plant could process raw materials much more efficiently.
The elaborator separates impurities from the crude oil before further refinement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: processor - a machine that prepares or treats materials for use
  • synonym: refiner - a machine or person that purifies or improves a substance
