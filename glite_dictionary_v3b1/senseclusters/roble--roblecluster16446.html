roble

noun
tropical tree with yellow flowers and large pods
6.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊb.leɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊb.leɪ / (BrE)
A large tropical tree of Trinidad and Guyana with odd-pinnate leaves, violet-scented clusters of yellow flowers, and long smooth pods; often grown as a specimen in parks and gardens.
