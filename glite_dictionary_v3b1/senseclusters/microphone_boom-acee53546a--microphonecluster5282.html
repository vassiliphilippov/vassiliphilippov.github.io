microphone boom

noun
pole holding overhead microphone on set
9.4 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.krə.foʊn buːm/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krə.foʊn boʊm/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krə.fəʊn buːm/ (BrE)
