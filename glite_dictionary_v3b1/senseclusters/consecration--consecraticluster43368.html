consecration

noun
solemn dedication to a purpose or goal
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsɪˈkreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsɪˈkreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of formally dedicating your life, time, or efforts to a valued purpose or goal

Examples

Consecration to a cause
He regarded his consecration to social service as his greatest source of fulfillment.
Her consecration to scientific research inspired everyone in the laboratory.
The artist's consecration to his craft led to a lifetime of remarkable achievements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dedication - the act of devoting time or effort to a particular task or purpose
