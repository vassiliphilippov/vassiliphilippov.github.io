olivine

noun
a green mineral made of magnesium iron silicate
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːləˌvin / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪviːn / (BrE)
a greenish mineral consisting of magnesium iron silicate, commonly found in igneous rocks and used as a source of magnesium

Examples

Green olivine mineral
Geologists identified olivine crystals in the volcanic rock sample.
Magnesium is often extracted from minerals like olivine in industrial processes.
The green hue of the sand comes from an abundance of olivine grains.

Related Terms
