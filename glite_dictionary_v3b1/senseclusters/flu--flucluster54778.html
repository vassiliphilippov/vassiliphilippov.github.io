flu

noun
common viral infection (influenza)
Pronunciation: / fluː /; / flu / (AmE), / fluː /; / flu / (BrE)
a common, highly contagious viral illness (influenza) that causes fever, aches, and respiratory symptoms

Examples

The flu virus
Doctors recommend getting vaccinated every year to protect against the flu.
His body ached and he had a high fever, classic signs of the flu.
She stayed home from work because she had the flu.
Every winter, hospitals see an increase in flu cases.
She had to stay home from work because she caught the flu.
The flu vaccine can help prevent serious illness.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cold - milder viral infection with similar symptoms
  • synonym: influenza - technical or full name for flu, viral illness
