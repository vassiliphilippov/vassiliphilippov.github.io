unhabituated

adjective
not accustomed
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnhəˈbɪtʃueɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnhəˈbɪtʃueɪtɪd / (BrE)
not used to or familiar with something; not accustomed.

Examples

Unhabituated animals
The wild birds remained unhabituated to people and quickly flew away.
She was unhabituated to the cold climate and found it difficult to adjust.
Many animals in the reserve are still unhabituated and avoid human contact.

Related Terms

  • antonym: habituated - used to or familiar with something
  • synonym: unaccustomed - not familiar with something through experience
