parietal

adjective
of body walls
Pronunciation: / pəˈraɪətəl / (AmE), / pəˈraɪətəl / (BrE)
relating to the walls of a body part, organ, or cavity

Examples

Parietal membrane lining
The parietal membrane lines the inside of the chest cavity.
Damage to the parietal lobe can affect a person's sense of touch.
Doctors examined the parietal surface of the organ during surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: visceral - relating to the internal organs
