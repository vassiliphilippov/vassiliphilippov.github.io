vagueness

noun
unclearness or lack of precision
4.3 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈveɪg.nəs/ /; / /ˈveɪg.nɪs/ (AmE), /ˈveɪg.nəs/ /; / /ˈveɪg.nɪs/ (BrE)
the quality of being unclear, imprecise, or lacking definite form or meaning

Examples

The vagueness of meaning
Critics complained about the vagueness of the official statement on policy changes.

His instructions were marked by such vagueness that no one knew what to do.
The vagueness of the outline made the figure difficult to recognize.
