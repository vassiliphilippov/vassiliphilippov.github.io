jackass

noun
a foolish or stupid man
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækˌæs / (AmE), / ˈdʒakˌas / (BrE)
a man who behaves in a very stupid or foolish way; an incompetent or senseless person

Examples

He acts like a jackass
Don't be such a jackass and listen to what I'm telling you.
He acted like a complete jackass at the meeting, embarrassing everyone.
Sometimes, his jokes go too far and make him sound like a jackass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or senseless behavior
