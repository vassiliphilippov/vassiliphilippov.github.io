sulfur mustard

noun
toxic blistering chemical warfare agent
9.5 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfɚ ˈmʌstɚd / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
a toxic chemical compound used as a warfare agent, causing severe blisters and damage to the eyes and lungs; there is no known antidote
