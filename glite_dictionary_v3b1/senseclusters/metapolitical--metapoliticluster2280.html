metapolitical

adjective
influencing politics indirectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to ideas, actions, or efforts that go beyond traditional politics, often by influencing culture or society to shape political outcomes indirectly
