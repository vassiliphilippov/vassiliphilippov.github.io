monopolistically

adverb
in a monopoly-like way
9.7
Pronunciation: /mə.nə.ˈpɑː.lɪs.tɪk.li/ (AmE), /mə.nə.ˈpɒl.ɪs.tɪk.li/ (BrE)
in a manner that involves or resembles having exclusive control over a market or industry

Examples

Operate monopolistically
The company operated monopolistically, making it difficult for new businesses to enter the market.
Critics argued that the industry was behaving monopolistically by setting unfair prices.
Regulators investigated whether the firm acted monopolistically to eliminate competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitively - in a way that involves or encourages competition
  • synonym: exclusively - in a way that excludes others
