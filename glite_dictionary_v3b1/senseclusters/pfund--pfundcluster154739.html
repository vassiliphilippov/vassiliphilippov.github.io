pfund

noun
German weight unit
Pronunciation: / pfʊnt / (AmE), / pfʊnt / (BrE)
a unit of weight used in German-speaking countries, approximately equal to a pound

Examples

A pfund of sugar
The old recipe asks for one pfund of sugar, which is about 500 grams.
In some German markets, cheese is still sold by the pfund.
He bought a pfund of apples at the local store while visiting Austria.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kilogram - the base unit of mass in the metric system
  • synonym: pound - a unit of weight equal to 16 ounces
