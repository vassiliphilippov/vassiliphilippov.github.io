Hakenkreuz

noun
Nazi emblem of bent-armed cross
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.kənˌkrɔɪts / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.kənˌkrɔɪts / (BrE)
the official emblem of the Nazi Party and the Third Reich, consisting of a cross with its arms bent at right angles in a clockwise direction (swastika)
