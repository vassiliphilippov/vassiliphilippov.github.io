dictation

noun
speaking for someone else to write down
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of saying words aloud for someone else to write down, or the words or passage that are dictated

Examples

A dictation exercise
Accurate dictation is an important skill for administrative assistants.
She gave a dictation to her secretary every morning to prepare her correspondence.
The students had trouble keeping up with the dictation during the language lesson.
After the meeting, the secretary typed up the dictation from her notes.
She saved each dictation in a separate folder for easy reference.
The manager reviewed the dictation before signing the official letter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dictate - to say or read aloud for someone else to write down
  • synonym: transcription - a written or printed version of material originally presented in another medium
