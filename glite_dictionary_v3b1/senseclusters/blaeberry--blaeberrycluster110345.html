blaeberry

noun
a European blue-black berry; whortleberry
Pronunciation: / bleɪˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbleɪbəri / (BrE)
A small, sweet, blue-black berry growing on an erect shrub native to Europe, closely related to the blueberry.

Examples

A blaeberry bush
Children used to stain their fingers picking blaeberry in the summer woods.
On our walk through the highlands, we gathered handfuls of blaeberries from the shrubs.
The tart flavor of blaeberries makes them perfect for homemade jams and desserts.

Related Terms
