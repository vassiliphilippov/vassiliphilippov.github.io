fairly

adverb
to some extent; moderately
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrli / (AmE), / ˈfeəli / (BrE)
to some extent or degree; moderately but not completely or extremely.

Examples

Fairly easy task
His explanation was fairly clear, though some parts were confusing.
That movie was fairly entertaining, but I've seen better.
The task was fairly easy, so she completed it in just an hour.
