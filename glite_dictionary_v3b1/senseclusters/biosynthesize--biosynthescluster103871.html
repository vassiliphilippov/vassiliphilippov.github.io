biosynthesize

verb
to produce compounds biologically
8.1
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.ˈsɪn.θə.saɪz/ /; / /baɪ.ə.ˈsɪn.θə.saɪz/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.ˈsɪn.θə.saɪz/ /; / /baɪ.ə.ˈsɪn.θə.saɪz/ (BrE)
to produce complex chemical compounds from simpler substances within a living organism

Examples

To biosynthesize vitamins
Certain bacteria can biosynthesize essential vitamins needed by humans.
Plants biosynthesize glucose during the process of photosynthesis.
Some animals are unable to biosynthesize specific amino acids and must obtain them from their diet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decompose - to break down into simpler parts
  • synonym: synthesize - to combine different elements to form a whole
