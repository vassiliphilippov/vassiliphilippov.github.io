satrapy

noun
area of control
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪtrəpi / (AmE), / ˈsætrəpi / (BrE)
an area, organization, or sphere where someone has complete authority or control, often used disapprovingly

Examples

Corporate satrapy
The CEO ran the company as his personal satrapy, making all decisions without consulting others.
Critics accused the mayor of turning the city council into a satrapy loyal only to him.
After years in power, she treated the department like her own satrapy and resisted any reforms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democracy - a system where power is shared and not held by one person
  • synonym: domain - an area or field under someone's control
  • synonym: empire - a large group of territories or organizations controlled by one ruler
