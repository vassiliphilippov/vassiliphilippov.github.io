status asthmaticus

noun
severe, unresponsive asthma attack
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtəs æsθˈmætɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈsta.təs asˈma.tɪ.kəs / (BrE)
A prolonged and severe asthma attack that does not respond to standard treatments and may be life-threatening.

Examples

status asthmaticus attack
Doctors recognized the signs of status asthmaticus and immediately began advanced therapy.
Prompt medical intervention is crucial in cases of status asthmaticus to prevent respiratory failure.
The patient was admitted to the intensive care unit with status asthmaticus after repeated treatments failed.
