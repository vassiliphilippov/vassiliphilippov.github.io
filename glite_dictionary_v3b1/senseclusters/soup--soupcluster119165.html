soup

verb
give illegal drugs to a racehorse
9.9
Pronunciation: / suːp / (AmE), / suːp / (BrE)
to secretly administer drugs or stimulants to (a racehorse) in order to improve its performance illegally.

Examples

To soup a horse

The trainer was banned after he tried to soup several racehorses before a major event.
There are strict penalties for anyone caught attempting to soup a racehorse.
