Midlander

noun
person from Midlands
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdləndər / (AmE), / ˈmɪdləndə / (BrE)
a person who comes from the Midlands region of a country, especially the United Kingdom

Examples

A proud Midlander
As a proud Midlander, she often visited Birmingham.
Many Midlanders have a distinct accent that sets them apart from other regions.
He moved to London but still identified himself as a Midlander at heart.
