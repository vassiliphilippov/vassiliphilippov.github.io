baby's tears

noun
creeping plant with tiny round leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiz tɪrz / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbɪz tɪəz / (BrE)
a creeping Corsican plant (Soleirolia soleirolii) with dense, moss-like mats of small round leaves, often grown as a houseplant or ground cover.
