supersensible

adjective
beyond physical senses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈsɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈsɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
beyond what can be perceived by the senses; not detectable by ordinary human senses

Examples

The supersensible world
The philosopher argued that supersensible truths exist beyond our physical experience.
Some people believe in supersensible forces that cannot be measured by science.
Art can sometimes express supersensible ideas that words cannot fully capture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imperceptible - impossible to perceive or notice
  • antonym: sensible - able to be perceived by the senses
  • synonym: supernatural - beyond the laws of nature or scientific explanation
