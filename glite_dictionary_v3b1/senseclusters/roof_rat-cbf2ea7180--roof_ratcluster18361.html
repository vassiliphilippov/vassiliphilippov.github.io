roof rat

noun
a common pest rat species living in roofs
7.3
Pronunciation: / ruf ræt / (AmE), / ruːf ræt / (BrE)
a species of rat (Rattus rattus), originally from Asia, that is a common household pest found worldwide, often living in roofs or upper parts of buildings

Examples

A roof rat nest
After hearing strange noises above the ceiling, they discovered a nest of roof rats.
The roof rat is smaller and more agile than the common brown rat.
We had to call an exterminator after a roof rat was spotted in the attic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black rat - another name for the roof rat, Rattus rattus species
