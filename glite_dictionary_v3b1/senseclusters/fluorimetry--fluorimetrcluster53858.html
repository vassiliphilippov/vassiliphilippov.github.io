fluorimetry

noun
fluorescence measurement technique
8.5
Pronunciation: / flʊˈrɪmətri / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɪmətri / (BrE)
a scientific method that measures fluorescence (glowing light) to identify or determine the amount of a substance in a sample
