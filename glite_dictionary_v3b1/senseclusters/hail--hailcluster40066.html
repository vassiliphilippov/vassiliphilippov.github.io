hail

verb
originate or come from (a place)
3.1
Pronunciation: / heɪl / (AmE), / heɪl / (BrE)
(of a person) to come from or have been born in a particular place; to originate from.

Examples

Hail from London
Many jazz musicians hail from New Orleans.
Several of the team members hailed from different countries across Europe.
She proudly hails from a small fishing village on the coast.
