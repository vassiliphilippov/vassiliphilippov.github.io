Pharisee-like

adjective
hypocritically self-righteous; morally superior
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfæ.rɪ.siː laɪk/ (AmE), /ˈfæ.rɪ.siː laɪk/ (BrE)
Hypocritically self-righteous or morally superior, especially in a way that is insincere or judgmental toward others.

Examples

Pharisee-like attitude
He adopted a Pharisee-like tone, lecturing everyone about their faults.
Her Pharisee-like behavior made her unpopular among her colleagues.
People were uncomfortable with his Pharisee-like insistence on moral perfection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypocritical - Pretending to have virtues or moral beliefs one does not possess
  • synonym: sanctimonious - Making a show of being morally superior to others
