acoustic music

noun
music without electronic amplification
Pronunciation: / əˈkuːstɪk ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪk ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
Music performed without electronic amplification, using only natural sound from instruments and voices.

Examples

Acoustic music performance
I love listening to acoustic music because it feels more natural and intimate.
They hired a small trio to play acoustic music at the wedding reception.
This café features live acoustic music every Friday evening.
