iron manganese tungsten

noun
iron-manganese tungstate mineral; tungsten ore
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərn ˈmænɡəniːz ˈtʌŋstən / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˈmaŋɡəniːz ˈtʌŋstən / (BrE)
a mineral composed of iron and manganese tungstate, occurring in crystalline form and serving as the principal ore of tungsten

Examples

Large iron manganese tungsten crystals
Large crystals of iron manganese tungsten can be found in association with granite.
The extraction of tungsten often relies on deposits of iron manganese tungsten.
The vein was rich in iron manganese tungsten and promised a profitable mining operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wolframite - a mineral consisting of iron and manganese tungstate; chief ore of tungsten
