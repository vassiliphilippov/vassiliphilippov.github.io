diffusive

adjective
spreading by diffusion
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjusɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːsɪv / (BrE)
Tending to spread by diffusion; characterized by or relating to diffusion.

Examples

A diffusive process
A diffusive process distributes heat evenly throughout the material.
Gases mix through diffusive transport across membranes.
The diffusive movement of molecules causes the spread of ink in water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diffuse - to spread widely or thinly; to scatter
