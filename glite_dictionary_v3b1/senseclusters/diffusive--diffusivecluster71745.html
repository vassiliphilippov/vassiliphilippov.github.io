Examples

A diffusive process

Gases mix through diffusive transport across membranes.
The diffusive movement of molecules causes the spread of ink in water.

Synonyms

    7.9 0.10/m

  • disseminative
    8.4 0.00/m
    disseminative tends to mean spreading widely, often of ideas or things, while diffusive specifically refers to spreading by physical diffusion.
