Synonyms

  • expatriation
    8.2 0.04/m
    expatriation describes choosing to live abroad, often for work or a new life, while emigration is leaving one's country to settle permanently.
  • outmigration
    7.2 0.14/m
    outmigration means people leave a region or area to live elsewhere, sometimes within the same country, whereas emigration is leaving a country to settle abroad.
