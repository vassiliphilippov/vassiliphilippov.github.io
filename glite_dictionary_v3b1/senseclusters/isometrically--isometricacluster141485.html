isometrically

adverb
preserving distances
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that preserves distances exactly, especially in mathematics when a transformation keeps the metric unchanged

Examples

Mapped isometrically
The shapes were mapped isometrically so that all distances remained the same.
In geometry, two figures are isometrically related if one can be transformed into the other without stretching.
The software allows you to move objects isometrically across the grid.
