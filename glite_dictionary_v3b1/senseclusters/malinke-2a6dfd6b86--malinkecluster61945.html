Malinke

proper noun
West African ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪŋkeɪ / (AmE), / məˈlɪŋkeɪ / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group or the language spoken by this group, primarily in Mali, Guinea, and surrounding countries

Examples

A Malinke speaker

Many Malinke people live in the savanna regions of West Africa.
She is studying the Malinke language as part of her research on African cultures.
