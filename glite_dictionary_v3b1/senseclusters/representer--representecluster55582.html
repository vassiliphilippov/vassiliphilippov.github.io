representer

noun
person acting for others
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntər / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntə / (BrE)
a person who speaks, acts, or stands on behalf of another person or group

Examples

Group representer
The team selected a representer to attend the international conference on their behalf.
As the official representer, she voiced the concerns of the entire community.
Each department must appoint a representer for the upcoming negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agent - a person who acts on behalf of another
  • antonym: principal - a person who is represented by another
  • synonym: representative - a person chosen or appointed to act or speak for others
