alabastrine

adjective
resembling alabaster; pale and smooth
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈbæstraɪn / (AmE), / ˌaləˈbastrɪn / (BrE)
resembling or made of alabaster, especially in color or smoothness; having a pale, translucent appearance

Examples

Alabastrine pale skin
Painters often describe perfect, pale skin as having an alabastrine quality.
She admired the alabastrine complexion of the ancient statue.
The room was bathed in an alabastrine glow from the soft lamp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alabaster - a fine-grained, translucent form of gypsum, often white and used in sculpture
