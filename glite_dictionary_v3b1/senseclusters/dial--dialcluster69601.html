dial

noun
face of a clock or watch
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.əl /; / ˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
the face or display of a clock, watch, or measuring instrument that shows information with hands or numbers

Examples

Clock dial
She glanced at the dial and realized she was late for her meeting.
The antique clock's dial was beautifully decorated with gold accents.
The luminous dial allows you to read the time even in the dark.
She adjusted the pressure using the gauge’s dial until it reached the correct level.
The old radio had a large round dial to select stations.
The speedometer dial was clearly visible behind the steering wheel.

  • synonym: gauge - instrument for measuring and displaying a value
