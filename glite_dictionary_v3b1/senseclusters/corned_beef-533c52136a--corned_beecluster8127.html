corned beef

noun
beef preserved in brine
Pronunciation: / kɔrn(d) bif /; / kɔrn ˈbif / (AmE), / kɔːnd biːf /; / kɔːnd ˈbiːf / (BrE)
beef that has been cured or pickled in brine, often served as a dish or in sandwiches

Examples

A corned beef sandwich
She prefers her corned beef served warm with potatoes.
The traditional dish for St. Patrick's Day is corned beef and cabbage.
We made delicious sandwiches with slices of corned beef and mustard.
He made a delicious sandwich with corned beef and mustard on rye bread.
Many people enjoy eating corned beef and cabbage on St. Patrick's Day.
The deli sells both pastrami and corned beef by the pound.
