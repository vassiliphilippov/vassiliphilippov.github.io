Tubifex

noun
aquatic worm genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbɪˌfɛks / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbɪˌfɛks / (BrE)
a genus of small, aquatic worms commonly found in freshwater and often used as food for aquarium fish

Examples

Many aquarium owners feed their fish with live Tubifex to provide extra nutrition.

Researchers studied the pollution tolerance of Tubifex in urban waterways.
