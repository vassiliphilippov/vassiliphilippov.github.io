doxy

noun
informal name for doxycycline (antibiotic)
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑksi / (AmE), / ˈdɒksi / (BrE)
Informal short form of doxycycline, a broad-spectrum tetracycline antibiotic used to treat various bacterial infections.
