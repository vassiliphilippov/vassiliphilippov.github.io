hyalinize

verb
to become glassy
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.ə.lɪ.naɪz/ /; / /haɪ.ə.ˈlɪ.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.ə.lɪ.naɪz/ /; / /haɪ.ə.ˈlɪ.naɪz/ (BrE)
to become or cause to become glassy and translucent, especially in biological tissue

Examples

To hyalinize tissue
Under certain conditions, the tissue may hyalinize and lose its normal structure.
Pathologists observed that the blood vessels had hyalinized after years of high blood pressure.
Prolonged stress can cause some cells to hyalinize and appear glassy under a microscope.
