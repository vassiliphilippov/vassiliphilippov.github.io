antiballistic missile

noun
missile that intercepts ballistic missiles
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntibaˈlɪstɪk ˈmɪsaɪl / (AmE), / ˌæntɪbəˈlɪstɪk ˈmɪsaɪl / (BrE)
A missile designed to intercept and destroy incoming intercontinental ballistic missiles.

Examples

An antiballistic missile system
An antiballistic missile system can intercept hostile warheads before they reach their targets.
Recent treaties have restricted the development of antiballistic missile technology.
The country deployed several antiballistic missiles to enhance its national defense system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intercontinental ballistic missile - a missile with a long range designed to deliver nuclear warheads over continents
