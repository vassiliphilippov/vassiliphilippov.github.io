roundel

noun
round piece of armor for armpit protection
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊn.dəl / (AmE), / ˈraʊn.dəl / (BrE)
a round piece of armor plate that protects the armpit, used especially in medieval armor

Examples

Medieval roundel armor
Collectors often display a medieval roundel as part of their armor exhibits.
The knight's roundel shielded his armpit from the opponent's sword.
Without a roundel, that area would be especially vulnerable during battle.
