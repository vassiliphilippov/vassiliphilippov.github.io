celebrative

adjective
relating to celebration
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlɪˌbreɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪbrətɪv / (BrE)
expressing, relating to, or done as part of a celebration or festivity

Examples

Celebrative atmosphere
The parade had a celebrative atmosphere, with music and dancing in the streets.
Her speech was celebrative of the team's achievements over the past year.
They organized a celebrative dinner to mark the end of the project.
The team organized a celebrative event to honor their coach's retirement.
A celebrative mood filled the hall after the announcement of the winners.
They enjoyed a celebrative dinner with friends and family after the graduation ceremony.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solemn - serious and not cheerful or celebratory
