snagger

noun
object that catches
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡər / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡə / (BrE)
an object or projection that catches, tears, or becomes caught on things such as clothing or fabric

Examples

A fence snagger
The broken fence post acted as a snagger, shredding her coat as she climbed over it.
Be careful of the snagger on the door frame, or it might rip your sleeve.
He fixed the carpet to remove the snagger that kept catching people's socks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hook - a curved device used for catching or holding things
  • synonym: snag - a sharp or rough part that sticks out and may catch or tear
