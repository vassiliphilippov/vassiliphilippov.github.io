prestudy

noun
preparatory assignment
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌstʌdi / (BrE)
a short assignment or material completed before a course or meeting to help participants prepare

Examples

A prestudy assignment
Please finish the prestudy before attending the first lecture next week.
The prestudy included several articles to read and a short quiz.
Completing the prestudy helped everyone participate more actively in the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homework - schoolwork assigned to be done outside the classroom
  • synonym: preparation - the action of making ready or being made ready for use
