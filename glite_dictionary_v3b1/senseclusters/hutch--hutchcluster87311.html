hutch

noun
storage furniture
Pronunciation: / hʌtʃ / (AmE), / hʌtʃ / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with shelves or drawers, often used for storage or displaying items such as dishes

Examples

Kitchen hutch
She displayed her favorite plates in the dining room hutch.
The antique hutch in the kitchen holds all of our glassware and cookbooks.
They bought a new hutch to organize their dishes and silverware.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabinet - a piece of furniture with doors and shelves for storage
  • synonym: sideboard - a piece of dining room furniture for holding dishes and serving food
