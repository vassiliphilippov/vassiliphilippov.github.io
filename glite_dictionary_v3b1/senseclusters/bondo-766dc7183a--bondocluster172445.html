Bondo

noun
West African women's society
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒndəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional women's initiation society found in parts of West Africa, especially among the Mende people

Examples

Bondo women's society
The Bondo society plays an important role in the cultural life of the community.
Young girls undergo special training and rituals as part of the Bondo initiation.
Anthropologists have studied the traditions of the Bondo society in Sierra Leone.
