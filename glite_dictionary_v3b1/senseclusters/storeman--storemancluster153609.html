storeman

noun
supply manager
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrmən / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmən / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing, receiving, storing, and issuing goods or supplies, especially in a warehouse, store, or service setting

Examples

The storeman checks stock
The storeman checked the inventory every morning to ensure nothing was missing.
As a storeman on the ship, he was trusted with all the food and equipment supplies.
The warehouse hired a new storeman to organize and track incoming goods.
The storeman checked the inventory before making deliveries.
As a storeman, he ensures all goods are properly labeled and stored.
The company hired a new storeman to manage the warehouse supplies.
The storeman checked the inventory list before distributing the new equipment to the team.
As a storeman, he was trusted to keep track of all the supplies in the warehouse.
During the operation, the storeman ensured that every soldier received the correct gear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clerk - a person who keeps records or manages inventory
  • synonym: quartermaster - an officer responsible for supplies and equipment, especially in the military
  • synonym: warehouseman - a person who works in or manages a warehouse
