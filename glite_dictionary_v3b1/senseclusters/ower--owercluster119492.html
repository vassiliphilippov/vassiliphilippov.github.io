ower

preposition
Scots for above/across
Pronunciation: / ˈuːər / (BrE)
a dialectal or Scots preposition meaning above, across, or beyond something

Examples

Ower the bridge
They walked ower the bridge into the next village despite the rain.
The bird flew ower the fields as the sun was rising.
He jumped ower the wall to fetch the lost ball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: over - above or across something
