quartet

noun
musical piece for four performers
8.5
Pronunciation: / kwɔrˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɔːˈtɛt / (BrE)
a musical composition written for four performers or instruments

Examples

A string quartet
Beethoven's late string quartets are considered masterpieces of chamber music.
She composed a quartet for piano, violin, viola, and cello.
The ensemble performed a modern quartet that challenged traditional harmonies.
