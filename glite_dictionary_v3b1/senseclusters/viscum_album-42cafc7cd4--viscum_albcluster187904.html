Viscum album

noun
European mistletoe shrub
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪskəm ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈvɪskəm ˈal.bəm / (BrE)
A species of parasitic shrub native to Europe, known as the traditional Christmas mistletoe, with greenish stems, leathery leaves, and white berries.
