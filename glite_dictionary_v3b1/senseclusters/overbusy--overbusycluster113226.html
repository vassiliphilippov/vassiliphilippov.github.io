overbusy

adjective
excessively busy
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbɪzi / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɪzi / (BrE)
Too busy; excessively occupied or engaged.

Examples

Too overbusy day

Many parents become overbusy juggling their children's schedules and their own responsibilities.
The overbusy manager rarely found time to sit down for lunch.
