psychoactive drug

noun
drug affecting mood or perception
2.4 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈæktɪv drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈæktɪv drʌɡ / (BrE)
A drug that affects mental processes, producing changes in mood, behavior, or perception.

The use of psychoactive drugs can have profound effects on mood and cognition.
