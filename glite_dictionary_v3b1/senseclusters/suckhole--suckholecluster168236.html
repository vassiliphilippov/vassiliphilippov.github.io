suckhole

noun
flattering subordinate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈsʌkˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a person who flatters or fawns over someone in authority to gain favor or advantage

Examples

Office suckhole
Everyone in the department knew him as the suckhole who always complimented the manager.
She refused to be a suckhole just to get a promotion at work.
The new intern quickly gained a reputation as a suckhole for constantly praising the team leader.
