tying

noun
act of binding things together
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of fastening or binding things together with a string, rope, or similar material

Examples

The tying of knots

Proper tying of the knots is essential for safety.
