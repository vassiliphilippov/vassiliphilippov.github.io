immunochemistry

noun
study of chemical processes in immunology
6.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.mjuː.noʊ.ˈke.mɪ.striː/ (AmE), /ɪ.mjuː.nəʊ.ˈke.mɪ.striː/ (BrE)
the branch of chemistry that deals with the chemical structure and processes of the immune system, especially the interactions between antigens and antibodies
