Cortinarius gentilis

noun
a poisonous mushroom species
Pronunciation: / kɔːrˌtɪnˈɛəriəs ˈdʒɛntɪlɪs / (AmE), / kɔːˌtɪnˈɑːrɪəs ˈdʒɛntɪlɪs / (BrE)
a species of poisonous mushroom with a bright yellow-brown cap and a long cinnamon-colored stalk, found in temperate forests
