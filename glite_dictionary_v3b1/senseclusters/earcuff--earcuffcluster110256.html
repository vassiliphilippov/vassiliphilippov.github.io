earcuff

noun
jewelry worn on the edge of the ear
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌkʌf / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌkʌf / (BrE)
a piece of jewelry designed to be worn on the outer edge of the ear without the need for a piercing

Examples

A earcuff jewelry
Many fashionistas now prefer the bold look of an earcuff to complement their outfits.
She wore a sparkling earcuff that wrapped elegantly around her entire ear.
Unlike a traditional earring, an earcuff requires no piercing to stay in place.
