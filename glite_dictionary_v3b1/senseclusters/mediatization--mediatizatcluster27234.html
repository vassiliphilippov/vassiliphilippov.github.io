mediatization

noun
adapting for media
8.2
Pronunciation: /miː.di.ə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /miː.di.ə.taɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of adapting or transforming something so it can be distributed or presented through mass media or digital channels

Examples

Mediatization of an event
The mediatization of the event allowed people worldwide to watch it live online.
Modern education often involves the mediatization of lectures through video and interactive platforms.
The mediatization of traditional art forms has made them more accessible to younger audiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to transmit content by radio, television, or other media
  • synonym: digitization - the process of converting information into a digital format
