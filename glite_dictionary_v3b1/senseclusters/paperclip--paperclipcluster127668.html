paperclip

noun
wire or plastic clip for papers
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpɚˌklɪp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌklɪp / (BrE)
a small piece of bent wire or plastic used to hold sheets of paper together

Examples

A paperclip holds papers
He bent a paperclip to unlock the stuck drawer.
She used a paperclip to keep the documents organized.
There is a paperclip attached to the top of the report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binder clip - a metal or plastic clip for holding a stack of papers
  • synonym: staple - a short piece of wire used to fasten sheets of paper together
