posthuman

noun
enhanced future being
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
a hypothetical or future being whose abilities have been so greatly enhanced or changed that it is no longer considered an ordinary human

Examples

A posthuman being
Some scientists believe that a posthuman society could emerge through advanced technology.
The novel explores the daily life of a posthuman living in a world without disease or aging.
Debates about ethics often arise when discussing the rights of a posthuman individual.

Related Terms

  • antonym: human - a member of the species Homo sapiens
  • synonym: transhuman - a being whose abilities are enhanced beyond current human limits
