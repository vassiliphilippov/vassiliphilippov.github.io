frazzled

adjective
worn or frayed
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfræzəld / (AmE), / ˈfræzəld / (BrE)
worn out, frayed, or damaged at the edges due to long use, rubbing, or heat

Examples

Frazzled edges
After years of use, the rug's edges became frazzled and started to unravel.
The frazzled rope finally snapped during the storm.
His favorite jeans looked frazzled at the cuffs from constant wear.
