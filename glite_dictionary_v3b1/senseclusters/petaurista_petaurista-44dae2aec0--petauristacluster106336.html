Petaurista petaurista

noun
species of large Southeast Asian flying squirrel
Pronunciation: / pɛˌtɔrɪstə pɛˌtɔrɪstə / (AmE), / pɛˌtɔːrɪstə pɛˌtɔːrɪstə / (BrE)
