pool

noun
total sum of bets or stakes
4.2 1.79/m
Pronunciation: / pul / (AmE), / puÀêl / (BrE)
the total amount of money bet by all players in a game or competition, to be awarded to the winner or winners

Examples

A pool of money
A large pool was collected before the poker game began.
Everyone contributes to the pool and the winner takes it all.
The pool grew quickly as more people joined the office lottery.
