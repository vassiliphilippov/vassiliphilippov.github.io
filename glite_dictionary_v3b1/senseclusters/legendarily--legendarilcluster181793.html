legendarily

adverb
famously; as in legend
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒəndˌɛrəli /; / ˈlɛdʒəndɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒəndərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is famous, celebrated, or exceptional, as described in legends or stories

Examples

To be legendarily famous
The mountain is legendarily difficult to climb, attracting adventurers from around the world.
He is legendarily generous, always helping those in need without hesitation.
The city is legendarily known for its vibrant nightlife and music scene.
The chef is legendarily skilled, attracting food lovers from around the world.
Her generosity is legendarily well known in the community.
The mountain is legendarily difficult to climb, even for experienced hikers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exceptionally - to an unusually high degree
  • synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • synonym: famously - in a way that is well known by many people
  • degree lower: notoriously - in a way that is famous, usually for something bad
  • antonym: ordinarily - in a normal or usual way
