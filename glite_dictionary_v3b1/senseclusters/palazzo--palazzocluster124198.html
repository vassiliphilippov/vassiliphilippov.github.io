palazzo

noun
wide-legged trousers
8.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɑːtsoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈlæt.səʊ / (BrE)
a pair of women's loose, wide-legged trousers made from lightweight, flowing fabric
