hypersensitive

adjective
very easily affected
6.7
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.ˈsen.sɪ.tɪv/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.ˈsen.sɪ.tɪv/ (BrE)
easily affected or strongly responsive to physical, emotional, or environmental stimuli

Examples

Hypersensitive to criticism
He is hypersensitive to criticism and takes even small comments very personally.
Some children are hypersensitive to loud noises and bright lights.
Her skin became hypersensitive after using the new facial cream.
