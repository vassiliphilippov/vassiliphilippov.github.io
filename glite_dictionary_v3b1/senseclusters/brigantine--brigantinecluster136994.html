brigantine

noun
two-masted sailing vessel
9.6 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɪ.ɡən.tɪn/ /; / /ˈbrɪ.ɡən.taɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbrɪ.ɡən.tiːn/ /; / /ˈbrɪ.ɡən.taɪn/ (BrE)
