bagatelle

noun
trivial or unimportant thing
Pronunciation: / ˌbæɡəˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˌbaɡəˈtɛl / (BrE)
a thing of little importance or value; a trivial matter

Examples

A small bagatelle
Compared to the challenges we faced last year, this setback is a bagatelle.
For him, losing a few dollars was a bagatelle, hardly worth mentioning.
She dismissed the broken vase as a mere bagatelle and didn't worry about it.
