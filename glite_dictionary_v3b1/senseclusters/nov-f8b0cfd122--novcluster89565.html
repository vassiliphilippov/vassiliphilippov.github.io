Nov

noun
abbreviation for November (month)
1.0
Pronunciation: / nɑv / (AmE), / nɒv / (BrE)
The standard abbreviation for the month of November, following October and preceding December.

Examples

Nov month
Her birthday is in Nov, just after Halloween.
