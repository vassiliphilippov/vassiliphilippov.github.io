revenue

noun
total income before deductions
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛv.əˌnu / (AmE), / ˈrɛv.juː / (BrE)
the total income generated by a business or organization before any expenses or deductions are taken out

Examples

Company revenue
The company's revenue increased significantly last quarter due to higher sales.
They reported annual revenue of over two million dollars before taxes and expenses.
Tourism is a major source of revenue for the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: income - money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or through investments
