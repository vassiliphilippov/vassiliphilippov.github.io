upgrade

verb
move to a higher class of service (travel)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɡreɪd /; / ˈʌpˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌpɡreɪd / (BrE)
to move to a better or higher class of service, especially in travel or hotels

Examples

To upgrade to first class
After arriving early at the airport, she was upgraded to business class.
Passengers can upgrade their seats for a fee depending on availability.
When his flight was overbooked, he was lucky to be upgraded to first class.
Passengers with frequent flyer status are more likely to be upgraded to first class.
The airline upgraded me to business class when the economy seats were overbooked.
We were pleasantly surprised when the hotel staff upgraded our room at no extra cost.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downgrade - to move to a lower class of service
