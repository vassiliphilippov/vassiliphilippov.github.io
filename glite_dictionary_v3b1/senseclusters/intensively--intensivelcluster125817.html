Examples

To study intensively
He prepared for the exam intensively to ensure he understood every detail.
She trained intensively for the marathon, running every day for months.
The crops were grown intensively to maximize the use of available land.

Synonyms

  • strenuously
    5.5 1.04/m
    strenuously is used for hard, physical or determined effort, while intensively means working with close focus and sustained attention.
  • intensely
    3.1 8.27/m
    intensely describes very strong feeling or force, while intensively describes focused work or study done with close attention.
  • vigorously
    3.4 7.83/m
    vigorously implies strong physical action or energy, while intensively implies concentrated effort or attention over time.
