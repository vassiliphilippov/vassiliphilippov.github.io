shortsightedness

noun
inability to see distant objects clearly; myopia
6.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /ʃɔːr.ˈsaɪ.tɪd.nəs/ (AmE), /ʃɔː.ˈsaɪ.tɪd.nəs/ (BrE)
the condition of being unable to see distant objects clearly because of a defect in the eye's focusing ability; myopia

Examples

Eye shortsightedness
Despite his shortsightedness, he refused to wear his prescription glasses.
Her shortsightedness made it difficult to read signs from a distance.
Modern medicine offers several ways to correct shortsightedness, such as glasses or laser surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farsightedness - condition of being able to see distant objects better than close ones (hyperopia)
  • synonym: myopia - medical term for shortsightedness; inability to see distant objects clearly
