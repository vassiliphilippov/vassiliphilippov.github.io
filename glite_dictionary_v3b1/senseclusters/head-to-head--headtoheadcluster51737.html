head-to-head

adjective
between two people; private
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛd tə ˈhɛd / (AmE), / ˌhɛd tə ˈhɛd / (BrE)
(of a meeting, discussion, etc.) involving only two people, especially in an intimate or private setting.

Examples

Head-to-head meeting
The two leaders engaged in a head-to-head debate behind closed doors.
Their head-to-head discussion helped resolve the misunderstanding quickly.
We had a head-to-head meeting to talk about our concerns honestly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tête-à-tête - private conversation between two people
