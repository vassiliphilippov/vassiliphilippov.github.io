responsible

adjective
having duty or accountability; trustworthy
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspɒn.sə.bəl/ (BrE)
having the duty to deal with something or having control over someone or something, and being accountable for the outcome; deserving to be trusted to do what is right or required

Examples

Responsible for the team
As a manager, he is responsible for overseeing all daily operations.
It is responsible to recycle your waste and reduce environmental impact.
Parents are responsible for the safety and well-being of their children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accountable - required or expected to justify actions or decisions; responsible
  • antonym: irresponsible - not showing a proper sense of responsibility
  • synonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
