Examples

As a manager, he is responsible for overseeing all daily operations.

Synonyms

  • accountable
    4.6 17/m
    accountable is required to explain actions to an authority, while responsible includes both control and being trusted to do what is right.
  • answerable
    6.4 0.55/m
    answerable means being legally or morally required to report to someone, while responsible also covers having control and being trusted to act.
  • duty-bound
    5.8 0.36/m
    duty-bound shows a strong inner obligation to act, while responsible adds formal control and being trusted to manage outcomes.
