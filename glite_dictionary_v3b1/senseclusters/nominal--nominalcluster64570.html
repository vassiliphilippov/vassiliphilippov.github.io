nominal

adjective
relating to nouns or noun phrases (grammar)
7.2 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…ëÀê.m…ô.n…ôl / (AmE), / Ààn…ím.…™.n…ôl / (BrE)
Relating to a noun or to a word group that functions as a noun, especially in grammar or linguistics.

Examples

Nominal phrase
A nominal group may contain determiners and adjectives as well as the noun itself.
In the sentence 'Swimming is fun,' 'swimming' is a nominal word.
Linguists often analyze the role of nominal phrases in sentence structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noun - a word used to identify any of a class of people, places, or things
