vita

noun
academic résumé
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈviːtə / (AmE), / ˈviːtə / (BrE)
a brief written account of a person's life, achievements, or career, especially used in academic or professional contexts

Examples

An academic vita
She attached her academic vita to the job application.
The professor updated his vita to include his latest publications.
Applicants are required to submit a detailed vita with their credentials.
The conference program included a short vita for each keynote speaker.
She submitted her vita along with her application for the art residency.
A brief vita of the author appeared on the back cover of the book.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autobiography - a biography written by the person it is about
  • synonym: biography - a detailed written account of someone's life
  • synonym: curriculum vitae - a detailed written summary of a person's education and work experience
  • synonym: obituary - a published notice of a person's death with a short biography
  • synonym: résumé - a brief summary of a person's education, qualifications, and previous experience
