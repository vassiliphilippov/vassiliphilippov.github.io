sphericity

noun
quality of being spherical
4.8
Pronunciation: / sfɛˈrɪs.ə.ti / (AmE), / sfəˈrɪs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being spherical; the degree to which an object resembles a sphere

Examples

Measure sphericity degree
Scientists measure sphericity to assess how closely objects resemble perfect spheres.
The sphericity of the ball ensured it rolled smoothly across the table.
The planet's slight deviation from perfect sphericity is due to its rotation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ellipticity - the degree of being elliptical rather than spherical
  • synonym: roundness - the quality of being round or curved
