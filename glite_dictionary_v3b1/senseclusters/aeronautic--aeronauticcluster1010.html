aeronautic

adjective
pertaining to aeronautics
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˈnɔtɪk / (AmE), / ˌeərəˈnɔːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the science or practice of building or flying aircraft

Examples

Aeronautic technology
Advances in aeronautic technology have made flight safer and more efficient.
Her dream was to work in the aeronautic industry.
The university offers several aeronautic engineering programs.

  • degree higher: astronautic - relating to the science of space flight
