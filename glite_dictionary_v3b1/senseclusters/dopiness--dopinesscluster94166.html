dopiness

noun
sleepy state
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊpinəs / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpinəs / (BrE)
the state of feeling sleepy, drowsy, or lacking alertness

Examples

Afternoon dopiness
His dopiness after lunch made it hard to concentrate on his work.
The medicine caused a feeling of dopiness that lasted for several hours.
She apologized for her dopiness during the early morning meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertness - the state of being awake and attentive
  • synonym: drowsiness - a feeling of being sleepy or half-asleep
