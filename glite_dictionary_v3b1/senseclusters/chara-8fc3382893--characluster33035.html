Chara

noun
genus of green algae in freshwater lakes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.ə / (AmE), / ˈkeə.rə / (BrE)
a genus of green algae found in freshwater lakes, especially in areas with limestone, often forming dense underwater growths

Examples

Chara algae growth
Chara can often be seen covering the bottom of clear, calcareous lakes.
Scientists studied the effects of pollution on Chara populations in the region.
The presence of Chara is considered an indicator of good water quality.
