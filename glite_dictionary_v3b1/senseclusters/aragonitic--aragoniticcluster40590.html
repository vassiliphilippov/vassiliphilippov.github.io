aragonitic

adjective
containing aragonite
Pronunciation: / ˌærəɡəˈnɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌærəɡəˈnɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing aragonite, a crystalline form of calcium carbonate

Examples

Aragonitic coral skeleton
The coral skeleton is primarily aragonitic, giving it a unique crystal structure.
Scientists analyzed the aragonitic layers in the mollusk shell to study environmental changes.
Some pearls are formed from aragonitic material, which affects their luster and durability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calcitic - relating to or containing calcite, another form of calcium carbonate
