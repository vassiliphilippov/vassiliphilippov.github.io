book cloth

noun
material used for covering books
Pronunciation: / bʊk klɔθ / (AmE), / bʊk klɒθ / (BrE)
a strong woven fabric, usually made from cotton, used to cover and bind books

Examples

Durable book cloth
Many libraries choose durable book cloth to protect their most frequently borrowed volumes.
The artisan applied the book cloth smoothly over the cardboard cover.
The rare edition was bound in a beautiful red book cloth with gold lettering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookbinding - the process of physically assembling a book from ordered paper sheets
