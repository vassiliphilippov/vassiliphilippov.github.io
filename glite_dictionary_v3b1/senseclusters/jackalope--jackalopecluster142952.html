jackalope

noun
mythical horned rabbit
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈdʒækələʊp / (BrE)
a mythical animal from American folklore, depicted as a jackrabbit with antelope horns

Examples

Legendary jackalope
Tourists often search for the legendary jackalope when visiting the American West.
A stuffed jackalope hung on the wall of the old roadside diner.
Stories about the jackalope have been told for generations in rural communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: griffin - a mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle
