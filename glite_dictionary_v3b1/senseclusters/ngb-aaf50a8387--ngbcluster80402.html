NGB

noun
U.S. National Guard administrative agency (acronym)
8.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / …õn d íi bi / (AmE), / …õn d íiÀê biÀê / (BrE)
abbreviation for National Guard Bureau, the agency responsible for administering the Army National Guard and Air National Guard, and coordinating between the Army, Air Force, and National Guard units in the United States.

Examples
