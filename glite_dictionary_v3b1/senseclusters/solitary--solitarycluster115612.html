solitary

noun
person who lives alone
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːləˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.ɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
a person who lives alone and tends to avoid the company of others

Examples

A solitary person
After years as a solitary, he found it difficult to adjust to city life.
In the story, the solitary built a cabin deep in the woods.
The old solitary rarely spoke to anyone in the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hermit - a person living in solitude, often for religious reasons
