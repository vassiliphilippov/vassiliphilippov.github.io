solitary

noun
person who lives alone
7.2 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀêl…ôÀåt…õri / (AmE), / Ààs…íl.…™.t…ôr.i / (BrE)
a person who lives alone and tends to avoid the company of others

Examples

A solitary person
After years as a solitary, he found it difficult to adjust to city life.

The old solitary rarely spoke to anyone in the village.
