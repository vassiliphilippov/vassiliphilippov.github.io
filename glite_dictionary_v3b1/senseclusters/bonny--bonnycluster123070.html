bonny

adjective
attractive; pleasing in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːni / (AmE), / ˈbɒni / (BrE)
attractive or pleasing to look at, especially in a healthy and lively way

The old sailor spoke fondly of his bonny home by the sea.
What a bonny child you have!
