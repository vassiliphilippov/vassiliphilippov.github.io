attire

verb
dress in special clothes
5.9
Pronunciation: / əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
to dress in special or formal clothes, especially to look attractive or impressive

Examples

To attire a child
He attired the children in matching outfits for the holiday photo.
She attired herself in a beautiful gown for the ceremony.
They attired themselves in traditional costume for the annual festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress - to put on clothes, especially formal or special clothes
  • antonym: undress - to remove clothes from oneself
