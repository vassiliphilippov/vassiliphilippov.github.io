butcher

verb
kill animals for food
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə / (BrE)
to kill animals, usually for food consumption

Examples

To butcher a pig
Farmers often butcher their own livestock during the autumn season.
The chef insisted on butchering the meat himself to ensure its quality.
They butchered a pig and used every part for food.
