wormlike

adjective
resembling a worm
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːmlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape, appearance, or movement of a worm

Examples

Wormlike creature
The strange sea creature had a wormlike body that twisted as it swam.
Some larvae are wormlike in appearance, making them hard to distinguish from real worms.
The roots of the plant grew in a wormlike pattern through the soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serpentine - having a shape or movement like a snake
  • degree lower: worm - a small, soft-bodied animal with a long, thin body and no legs
