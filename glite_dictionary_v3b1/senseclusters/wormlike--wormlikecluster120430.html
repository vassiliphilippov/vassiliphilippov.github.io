wormlike

adjective
resembling a worm
3.6 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…úrmla…™k / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀêmla…™k / (BrE)
resembling or having the shape, appearance, or movement of a worm

Examples

Wormlike creature
The strange sea creature had a wormlike body that twisted as it swam.
Some larvae are wormlike in appearance, making them hard to distinguish from real worms.
The roots of the plant grew in a wormlike pattern through the soil.

Synonyms

  • worm-shaped
    3.0 0.01/m
    worm-shaped describes something long and thin in form, while wormlike also suggests the moving, wriggling shape of a worm.
  • vermiform
    9.6 0.00/m
    vermiform is a formal scientific word for a long, cylindrical shape, while wormlike is a general everyday word that also suggests movement.
