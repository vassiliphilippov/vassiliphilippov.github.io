semiconductor unit

noun
electronic component made from semiconducting material
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmɪkənˈdʌktər ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪkənˈdʌktə ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a piece of equipment or component made from semiconducting material, used to control electrical current in electronic devices

Examples

A semiconductor unit failure
A failure in one semiconductor unit can disrupt the entire circuit.
Modern computers rely on semiconductor units to process and store data efficiently.
The factory produces thousands of semiconductor units each day for use in electronic gadgets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: semiconductor - a substance whose electrical conductivity is between that of a conductor and an insulator
