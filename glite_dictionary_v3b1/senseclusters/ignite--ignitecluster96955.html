ignite

verb
arouse strong feelings or passions
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnaɪt / (BrE)
to arouse or excite strong feelings or passions in someone

Examples

To ignite passion
The documentary ignited a national conversation on climate change.
The leader's passionate speech ignited a wave of enthusiasm among the crowd.
Unfair treatment can ignite feelings of resentment within a community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflame - to provoke or intensify emotions or feelings
  • synonym: stir up - to provoke or incite strong feelings
