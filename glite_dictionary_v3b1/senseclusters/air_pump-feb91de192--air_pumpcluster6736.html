air pump

noun
device for moving air
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌpʌmp / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌpʌmp / (BrE)
a device used to move air into or out of an object or space

Examples

An air pump inflates tires
He used an air pump to inflate the bicycle tires before riding.
The aquarium relies on an air pump to keep the water oxygenated.
The old soccer ball needs an air pump to fill it up again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pump - a device for moving liquids or gases by mechanical action
