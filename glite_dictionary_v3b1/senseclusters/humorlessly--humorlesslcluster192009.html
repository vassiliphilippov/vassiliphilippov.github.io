humorlessly

adverb
without showing humor
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmɚləsli /; / ˈhjuːmərləsli / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmələsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows no amusement or sense of humor.

Examples

To act humorlessly
He humorlessly dismissed the team's playful suggestions.
She listened humorlessly as he told yet another joke.
The manager responded humorlessly to the lighthearted comment.
He humourlessly recounted the incident, focusing only on the facts.
She responded humourlessly to his attempt at a joke, showing no sign of amusement.
The rules were enforced humourlessly, leaving little room for flexibility or fun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humorously - in an amusing or humorous manner
  • synonym: seriously - without humor or playfulness
