crepe jasmine

noun
a tropical flowering shrub with wavy white flowers
Pronunciation: / kreɪp ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / kreɪp ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
A tropical shrub with glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers that have crimped or wavy corollas, native to South and Southeast Asia.

Examples

The crepe jasmine flower
Botanists admire the crimped petals of the crepe jasmine for their unique beauty.
The crepe jasmine in her garden bloomed every evening, filling the air with its sweet fragrance.
We planted a crepe jasmine next to the porch because its glossy leaves stay green year-round.
