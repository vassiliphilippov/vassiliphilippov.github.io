snidery

adjective
mocking or sarcastic
Pronunciation: / ˈsnaɪdəri / (AmE), / ˈsnaɪdəri / (BrE)
showing or characterized by snide, mocking, or disparaging remarks or attitude

Examples

Snidery remarks
His snidery comments made the meeting uncomfortable for everyone involved.
She could not hide her snidery attitude when discussing her rival's achievements.
The review was written in such a snidery tone that the author felt personally attacked.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kind - having a friendly, generous, or considerate nature
  • synonym: mocking - making fun of someone or something in a cruel way
  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
