flexuous

adjective
having bends or windings
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛkʃuəs / (AmE), / ˈflɛksjuːəs / (BrE)
having many bends, curves, or windings; winding or sinuous in form or movement.

Examples

The flexuous path
She admired the flexuous lines of the artist's sculpture.
The flexuous path through the forest made the hike both challenging and beautiful.
The river followed a flexuous course through the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sinuous - having many curves and turns
