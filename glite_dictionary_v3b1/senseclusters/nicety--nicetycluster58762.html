nicety

noun
fine distinction or subtle point
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪsəti / (AmE), / ˈnaɪsəti / (BrE)
a subtle or fine distinction in meaning, opinion, or attitude; a small but important detail or point

Examples

A legal nicety
She explained the legal nicety that made the difference between two similar cases.
The discussion involved the niceties of philosophical theory rather than practical concerns.
Understanding the niceties of etiquette can be challenging when visiting a new country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broadness - lack of precision or detail; generality
  • synonym: nuance - a subtle difference in meaning or opinion
  • synonym: subtlety - the quality of being delicate or precise
