hash

verb
chop food into small pieces
6.5
Pronunciation: / hæʃ / (AmE), / hæʃ / (BrE)
to cut food, especially meat or potatoes, into small pieces, usually for cooking

Examples

To hash potatoes
Many recipes call for you to hash leftover turkey and vegetables for a quick meal.
She used a sharp knife to hash the onions before adding them to the stew.
The chef hashed the potatoes to make a classic breakfast hash.
She spent the morning hashing leftover roast with potatoes to make a hearty breakfast.
He quickly started hashing the vegetables for the stew.
After hashing the meat, she added it to the pan with onions and spices.

  • synonym: mince - to cut food, especially meat, into very small pieces
