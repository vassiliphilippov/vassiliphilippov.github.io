absentminded

adjective
lost in thought; forgetful
4.8 4.8
Pronunciation: /æb.sənt.ˈmaɪn.dɪd/ (AmE), /æb.sənt.ˈmaɪn.dɪd/ (BrE)
easily distracted or lost in thought, often forgetting things or not paying attention to what is happening around one

Examples

An absentminded person
He grew so absentminded that he forgot where he put his keys every morning.
She laughed at her absentminded mistakes during the meeting.
The absentminded professor walked right past his own office without noticing.
