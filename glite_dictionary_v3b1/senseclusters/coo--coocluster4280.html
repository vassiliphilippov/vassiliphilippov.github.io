coo

verb
speak softly or lovingly
5.5 5.5
Pronunciation: / kuː / (AmE), / kuː / (BrE)
to make a soft, gentle, or loving sound, like a dove or when speaking affectionately

Examples

To coo sweetly
He cooed words of affection into her ear during the dance.
She gently cooed to her baby as she rocked him to sleep.
The couple sat by the fire, cooing sweet nothings to each other.
I heard a dove coo outside my window early this morning.
Pigeons coo to each other as they nest on the rooftop.
The baby cooed softly in her mother's arms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: croon - sing or say something in a soft, low voice
  • synonym: murmur - to make a soft, low sound
  • synonym: whisper - speak very softly with breath but no vocalization
