kora

noun
West African harp
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrə / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrə / (BrE)
a West African stringed musical instrument with 21 strings, resembling a harp and played by plucking

Examples

Play the kora
The musician played a beautiful melody on the kora during the concert.
She learned to play the kora while living in Senegal for a year.
The sound of the kora filled the room with a gentle, rhythmic harmony.
