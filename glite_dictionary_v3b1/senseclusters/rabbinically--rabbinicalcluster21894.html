rabbinically

adverb
in a rabbinic way
Pronunciation: / rəˈbɪnɪkli / (AmE), / rəˈbɪnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to rabbis or rabbinic teachings or traditions

Examples

Interpreted rabbinically

He approached the debate rabbinically, referencing traditional Jewish law.
