bizz

noun
low buzzing sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪz / (AmE), / bɪz / (BrE)
a short, low buzzing or humming sound, often made by an electronic device or insect

Examples

A bizz from the fridge
The refrigerator gave a low bizz as it hummed through the silent night.
She heard a faint bizz coming from the old lamp in the corner.
A sudden bizz startled him as a fly zipped past his ear.
