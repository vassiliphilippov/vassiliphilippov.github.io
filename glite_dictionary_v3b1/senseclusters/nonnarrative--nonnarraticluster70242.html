nonnarrative

adjective
not story-based
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈnærətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈnærətɪv / (BrE)
not telling a story or not organized in the form of a narrative

Examples

Nonnarrative style
The documentary used a nonnarrative approach, focusing on images and sounds rather than a storyline.
Some modern art films are intentionally nonnarrative to challenge traditional storytelling.
Her essay was nonnarrative, presenting facts and analysis without telling a personal story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: descriptive - giving details about how something looks, feels, or works
  • antonym: narrative - telling a story or structured as a story
