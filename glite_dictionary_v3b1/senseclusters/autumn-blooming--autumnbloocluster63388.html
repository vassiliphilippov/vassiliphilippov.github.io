autumn-blooming

adjective
flowering in autumn
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtəm ˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm ˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) flowering or coming into bloom during the autumn season.

Examples

Autumn-blooming flowers

Some autumn-blooming plants thrive even after temperatures begin to drop.
The autumn-blooming sedum provides nectar for bees in September.
