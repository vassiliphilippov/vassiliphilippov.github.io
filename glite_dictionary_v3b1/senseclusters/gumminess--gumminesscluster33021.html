gumminess

noun
sticky, cohesive quality
6.4 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌ.mɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈgʌ.mɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
The quality of being sticky and cohesive, often referring to a texture that tends to adhere to surfaces or itself.

Examples

The gumminess of pudding
Chefs often try to avoid excessive gumminess in desserts like puddings.
The gumminess of the candy made it hard to get off my teeth.
We measured the gumminess of the bread to ensure it would be pleasant to eat.
