cottony-white

adjective
pure white like cotton
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtəni ˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒtəni ˈwaɪt / (BrE)
Extremely white and soft in appearance or texture, like cotton.

Examples

Fluffy cottony-white clouds
Fluffy, cottony-white clouds drifted lazily across the summer sky.
She wore a cottony-white dress that shimmered in the sunlight.
The mountaintops were covered in a layer of cottony-white snow.
