woo woo

noun
pseudoscientific or mystical nonsense
Pronunciation: / ˈwuˌwu / (AmE), / ˈwuː ˌwuː / (BrE)
Relating to beliefs, claims, or ideas that are considered pseudoscientific, mystical, or lacking scientific credibility; often used to dismiss something as nonsense.

Examples

The woo woo ideas
He rolled his eyes at the woo woo theories being discussed.
Some people are drawn to woo woo healing practices despite a lack of scientific evidence.
The documentary tries to separate genuine research from woo woo speculation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - ideas that are absurd or meaningless
  • antonym: rational - based on or agreeable to reason; logical
