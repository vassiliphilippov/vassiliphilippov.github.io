signature tune

noun
melody identifying a person or program
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃɚ tuːn /; / \u02c8s\u026a\u0261.n\u0259.t\u0283\u025a tu\u02d0n / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃə tjuːn /; / \u02c8s\u026a\u0261.n\u0259.t\u0283\u0259 tju\u02d0n / (BrE)
a short, distinctive piece of music used to identify a performer, show, or brand, especially at the start or end of a broadcast or in advertising
