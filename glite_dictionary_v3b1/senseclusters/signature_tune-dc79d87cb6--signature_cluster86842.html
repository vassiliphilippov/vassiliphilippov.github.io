signature tune

noun
melody identifying a person or program

Pronunciation: /ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃər tuːn/ /; / /ˈsɪɡ.nɪ.tʃər tjuːn/ (AmE), /ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃə tjuːn/ /; / /ˈsɪɡ.nɪ.tʃə tʃuːn/ (BrE)
