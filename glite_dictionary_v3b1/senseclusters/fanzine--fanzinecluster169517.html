fanzine

noun
fan-made magazine
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌziːn / (AmE), / ˈfænziːn / (BrE)
a small magazine created and published by fans about a shared interest, often produced informally

Examples

A fanzine about music
She started a fanzine to share her love of indie music with other fans.
Many science fiction fanzines were exchanged by mail in the 1980s.
The fanzine featured interviews with underground comic artists and exclusive artwork.
She contributed artwork to a local fanzine dedicated to indie rock bands.
The fanzine featured interviews with actors from cult science fiction shows.
Many fans discovered new writers through the pages of the monthly fanzine.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: magazine - a periodical publication containing articles and illustrations
  • synonym: zine - a small, self-published magazine
