fanzine

noun
fan-made magazine
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfæn.ziːn/ /; / /ˈfæn.zɪn/ (AmE), /ˈfæn.ziːn/ /; / /ˈfæn.zɪn/ (BrE)

Examples
