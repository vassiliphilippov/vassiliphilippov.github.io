Nitrobacter

noun
genus of soil bacteria
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪtroʊˈbæktər /; / naɪtroʊˈbæktər / (AmE), / ˌnɪtrəʊˈbæktə /; / naɪtrəʊˈbæktə / (BrE)
