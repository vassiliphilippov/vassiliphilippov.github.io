smartweed

noun
water-loving plant
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑrtˌwid / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtˌwiːd / (BrE)
a plant with green leaves and small flowers, often found near water or in fields, belonging to the knotweed family and sometimes considered a weed
