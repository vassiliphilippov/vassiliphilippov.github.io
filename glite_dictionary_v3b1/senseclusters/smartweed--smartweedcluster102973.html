smartweed

noun
water-loving plant
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑrtˌwid / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtˌwiːd / (BrE)
a plant with green leaves and small flowers, often found near water or in fields, belonging to the knotweed family and sometimes considered a weed

Examples

Smartweed plant
We found a patch of smartweed growing along the edge of the pond.
Some birds feed on the seeds of smartweed in wetland areas.
The gardener removed the smartweed to make space for other plants.
The farmer spent hours pulling smartweed from the vegetable garden to protect his crops.
After the heavy rain, smartweed quickly spread across the empty lot.
Many gardeners struggle to control smartweed because it grows so rapidly among other plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dock - a plant with broad leaves often found near water
  • synonym: knotweed - a plant of the same family with jointed stems
  • synonym: weed - an unwanted wild plant that grows where it is not wanted
