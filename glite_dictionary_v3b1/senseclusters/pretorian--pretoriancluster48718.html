pretorian

adjective
relating to a Roman praetor
Pronunciation: / priˈtɔriən / (AmE), / priˈtɔːriən / (BrE)
of or relating to a Roman praetor, an elected magistrate in ancient Rome.

Examples

The pretorian guard
Ancient texts often mention pretorian commands and privileges.
The senator was accused of abusing his pretorian authority.
These laws were established during the pretorian era of Roman governance.
