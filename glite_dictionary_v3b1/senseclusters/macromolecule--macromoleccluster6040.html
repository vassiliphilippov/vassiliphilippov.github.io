macromolecule

noun
very large molecule, e.g., protein or DNA
4.1 5.8
Pronunciation: /mæ.krə.ˈmɑː.lə.kjuːl/ (AmE), /mæ.krə.ˈmɒl.ɪ.kjuːl/ (BrE)
