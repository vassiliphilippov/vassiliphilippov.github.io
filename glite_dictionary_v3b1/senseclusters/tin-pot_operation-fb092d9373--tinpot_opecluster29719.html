tin-pot operation

noun
insignificant or unprofessional business
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪn ˌpɑt ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɪn pɒt ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a business or organization that is regarded as insignificant, amateurish, or poorly managed

Examples

A tin-pot operation business
After seeing how disorganized the staff were, I realized it was just a tin-pot operation.
He refused to invest in such a tin-pot operation with no track record.
The scandal revealed that the charity was no more than a tin-pot operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fly-by-night - an unreliable or untrustworthy business
  • antonym: legitimate business - a reputable and properly run company
