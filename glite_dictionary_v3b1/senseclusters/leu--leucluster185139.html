leu

noun
Romanian currency unit
Pronunciation: / leɪˈuː / (AmE), / leɪˈuː / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Romania, equal to 100 bani.

Examples

Romanian leu value
Foreign investors are watching the value of the leu closely.
She exchanged her euros for Romanian leu at the airport.
The price of lunch was 30 leu at the restaurant.
