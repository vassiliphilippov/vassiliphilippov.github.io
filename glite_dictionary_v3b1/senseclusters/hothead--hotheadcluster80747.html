hothead

noun
impulsive, quick-tempered person
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who gets angry easily and acts without thinking

Examples

A hothead person
Despite being a hothead in his youth, he learned to control his temper over time.
Everyone knows Mike is a hothead, so try not to upset him during meetings.
The coach warned the young player not to act like a hothead on the field.
Jake can be a real hothead when things don’t go his way.
She tried to calm the hothead before the argument got out of control.
The coach warned that being a hothead would only hurt the team's chances.
