Euglena

noun
genus of single-celled green algae
8.9
Pronunciation: /juː.ˈɡliː.nə/ (AmE), /juː.ˈɡliː.nə/ (BrE)
A genus of single-celled green algae with a single flagellum, found in fresh and saltwater, known for their ability to photosynthesize and move.
