incivil

adjective
not civilized
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪvəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪvəl / (BrE)
not civilized; lacking cultural refinement or social development; barbarous (archaic)

Examples

Incivil tribes
In ancient times, travelers often feared entering incivil lands far from their own cities.
The novel describes an incivil society untouched by modern customs or laws.
Historians debated whether the region was truly incivil or simply different in its traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbarous - cruel or uncivilized; primitive
  • antonym: civilized - having an advanced or refined culture
  • synonym: uncivilized - not advanced socially or culturally
