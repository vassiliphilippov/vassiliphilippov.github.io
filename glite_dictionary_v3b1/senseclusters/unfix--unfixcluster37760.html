unfix

verb
to unsettle emotionally
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪks / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪks / (BrE)
to disturb or unsettle someone mentally or emotionally, causing them to lose stability or composure

Examples

Bad news unfixed her
The shocking news seemed to unfix her, leaving her unable to concentrate for the rest of the day.
A sudden change in routine can unfix even the most organized person.
He tried to remain calm, but the criticism clearly unfixed him during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - to make someone peaceful or relaxed
  • synonym: disturb - to interrupt the normal state or calm of someone
  • synonym: unsettle - to make someone feel anxious or disturbed
