wine tosser

noun
a person clumsy with wine
8.6
Pronunciation: / waɪn ˈtɔsɚ / (AmE), / waɪn ˈtɒsə / (BrE)
A person who spills wine or behaves clumsily when handling wine, especially at social events.

Examples

The wine tosser spills
As the designated wine tosser of the group, he wasn't allowed near the white tablecloth.
Everyone at the dinner avoided the wine tosser after he knocked over two glasses.
Julia laughed and called herself a wine tosser after spilling her second glass.
