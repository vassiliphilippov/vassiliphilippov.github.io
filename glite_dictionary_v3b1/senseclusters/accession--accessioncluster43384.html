accession

verb
record addition to a collection
9.2 7.2
Pronunciation: /ək.ˈse.ʃən/ /; / /æk.ˈse.ʃən/ /; / /ə.ˈse.ʃən/ (AmE), /ək.ˈse.ʃən/ /; / /æk.ˈse.ʃən/ /; / /ə.ˈse.ʃən/ (BrE)
to formally record an item as an addition to a collection, especially in a library or museum

Examples

To accession a book
Each book donated to the library must be accessioned before it can be issued to readers.
New artifacts are carefully accessioned into the museum's collection after being cleaned and documented.
The process of accessioning historical documents ensures their preservation and traceability.
