infrequent

adjective
not happening often
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfriːkwənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfriːkwənt / (BrE)
not happening often; not occurring regularly or at short intervals

Examples

An infrequent visitor
His apologies were so infrequent that she doubted his sincerity.
Scheduled bus service to the village is infrequent, so many residents own cars.
Severe storms are infrequent in this region compared to the tropics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frequent - happening often or at short intervals
  • synonym: rare - not occurring very often; uncommon
