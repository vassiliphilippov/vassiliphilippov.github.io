bostan

proper noun
Saadi's Persian poem
9.9
Pronunciation: / buːˈstɑːn / (AmE), / buːˈstɑːn / (BrE)
the title of a famous 13th-century Persian didactic poem by Saadi, often translated as 'The Orchard'

Examples

Saadi's Bostan poem
Many students of Persian literature study the bostan to understand Saadi's moral teachings.
The bostan is celebrated for its wisdom and poetic beauty in Persian culture.
Translations of the bostan have introduced Saadi's ideas to readers around the world.
