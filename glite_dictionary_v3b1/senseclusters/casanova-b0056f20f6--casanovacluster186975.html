Casanova

noun
man noted for many love affairs
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsəˈnoʊvə / (AmE), / ˌkæsəˈnəʊvə / (BrE)
a man who is known for having many romantic or sexual relationships with women

Examples

A real Casanova
Don't trust him with your heart; he's a real Casanova.
Everyone at the office knew him to be a bit of a Casanova.
He had a reputation as a Casanova in his younger years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philanderer - a man who frequently has affairs with women
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who often engages in casual sexual relationships with women
