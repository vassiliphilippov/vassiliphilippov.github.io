graverobber

noun
one who steals from graves or tombs
7.1 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈgreɪv.rɑː.bər/ (AmE), /ˈgreɪv.rɒ.bə/ (BrE)
a person who illegally removes bodies or valuables from graves or tombs, often to sell them
