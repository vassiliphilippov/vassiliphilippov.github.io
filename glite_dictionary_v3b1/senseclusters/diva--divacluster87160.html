diva

noun
demanding, temperamental celebrity
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.və/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.və/ (BrE)
a famous or glamorous celebrity (originally female) who behaves in a demanding, self-important, or temperamental way.

Examples

Pop diva
The pop star's on-tour attitude earned her a reputation as a diva.
Her manager complained that the actress behaved like a diva during rehearsals last week.
Fans tolerated his talent but criticized the musician's diva demands for private flights and dressing rooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prima donna - a leading female singer in an opera company; used similarly for someone who is demanding.
  • antonym: team player - someone who cooperates with others; often contrasted with a demanding or self-important person.
