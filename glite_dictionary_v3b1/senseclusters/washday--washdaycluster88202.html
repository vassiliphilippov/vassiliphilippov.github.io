washday

noun
a day for doing laundry
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃ.deɪ / (BrE)
A day, typically each week, on which household laundry is usually done.

Examples

A washday day
Monday was always washday when I was growing up.
On washday, the house was filled with the smell of soap and clean linens.
She scheduled her meetings around washday so she could finish the laundry.
