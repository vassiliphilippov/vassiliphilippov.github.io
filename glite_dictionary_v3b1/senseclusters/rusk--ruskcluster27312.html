rusk

noun
sweet, twice-baked crisp bread or biscuit
4.5
Pronunciation: / rʌsk / (AmE), / rʌsk / (BrE)
a hard, dry biscuit or slice of sweet raised bread that has been baked again until it is crisp and brown

Examples

A rusk biscuit
She dipped a rusk into her tea for breakfast.
The bakery sells delicious almond rusks in the morning.
To soothe the baby, he was given a rusk to chew on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biscotti - an Italian almond biscuit that is baked twice for extra crispness
