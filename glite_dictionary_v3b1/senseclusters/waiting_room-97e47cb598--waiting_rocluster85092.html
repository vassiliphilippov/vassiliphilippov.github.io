waiting room

noun
room for people to wait in public places
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtɪŋ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
A room in a public place, such as a hospital, station, or airport, where people sit and wait for an appointment or event.

Examples

Hospital waiting room
Please remain in the waiting room until the doctor is ready to see you.
She sat quietly in the waiting room until her name was called.
The airport waiting room was filled with travelers reading and talking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lounge - a comfortable room in a public place for sitting and relaxing
