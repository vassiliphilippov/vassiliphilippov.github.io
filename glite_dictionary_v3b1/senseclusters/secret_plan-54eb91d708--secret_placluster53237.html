secret plan

noun
scheme kept hidden, often for dishonest aims
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkrət plæn / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt plæn / (BrE)
a scheme or arrangement that is kept hidden, especially to do something dishonest or illegal

Examples

A secret plan
She discovered their secret plan only after it was too late.
The group worked together on a secret plan to surprise their boss.
The police uncovered a secret plan to hack the company's computers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspiracy - a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful
  • synonym: plot - a secret plan to achieve something, often illegal or harmful
  • synonym: scheme - a secret or dishonest plan
