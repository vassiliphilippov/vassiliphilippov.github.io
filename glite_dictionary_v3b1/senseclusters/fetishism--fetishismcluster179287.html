fetishism

noun
unusual fixation
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛtɪˌʃɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfɛtɪʃɪzəm / (BrE)
a strong and unusual fixation or sexual attraction to a particular object, material, or body part

Examples

Coin fetishism
The collector's fetishism for rare coins led him to travel the world.
Some people develop a fetishism for certain materials, like leather or silk.
Her fetishism with shoes was reflected in her impressive and unusual collection.
