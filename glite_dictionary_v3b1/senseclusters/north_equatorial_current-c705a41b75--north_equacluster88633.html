North Equatorial Current

proper noun
ocean current north of the equator
6.2
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ ɪˌkwɛtʃəriəl ˈkɝːənt / (AmE), / nɔːθ ɪˌkwɛtʃəriəl ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
