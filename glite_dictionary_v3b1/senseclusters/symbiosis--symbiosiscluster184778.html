symbiosis

noun
mutual human–technology relationship
5.5

(in technology and AI) a close, mutually beneficial relationship or integration between humans and machines or artificial intelligence, in which each augments the other's abilities.

Examples

Symbiosis with AI
Researchers argue that a close symbiosis between doctors and AI can improve diagnostic accuracy in hospitals.
The company's design aims for a symbiosis of human creativity and machine learning in product development.
Philosophers debated whether human–AI symbiosis threatens autonomy or actually extends our cognitive abilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: human–AI collaboration - the partnership of humans and AI systems working together to perform tasks or make decisions
  • synonym: human–computer symbiosis - a close, cooperative relationship between humans and computers or software systems
