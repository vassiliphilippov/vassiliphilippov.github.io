crusted

adjective
having a hardened crust as a covering
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrʌstɪd / (BrE)
covered with or having a hardened crust on the surface.

Examples

Crusted with mud
Her shoes were crusted with dried mud after the hike.
The old bread was crusted and hard to chew.
The patient's wound became crusted over as it started to heal.
