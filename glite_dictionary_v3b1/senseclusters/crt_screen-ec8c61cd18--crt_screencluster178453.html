CRT screen

noun
cathode-ray tube display
4.7
Pronunciation: / si ɑr ti skrin / (AmE), / si ɑː tiː skriːn / (BrE)
a display device that uses a cathode-ray tube to create images on its screen

Examples

Old CRT screen
He replaced his bulky CRT screen with a modern LED monitor.
Many gamers claim that playing classic games on a CRT screen offers more authentic graphics.
The old computer in my office still has a CRT screen instead of a flat panel display.

  • synonym: monitor - an electronic visual display for computers
