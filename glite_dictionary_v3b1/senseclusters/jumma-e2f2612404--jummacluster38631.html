Jumma

proper noun
Islamic prayer; Bangladeshi people
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmə / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmə / (BrE)
the Friday congregational prayer in Islam, or a member of the Jumma indigenous peoples of Bangladesh

Examples

Jumma at the mosque
Every Friday, Muslims gather at the mosque for Jumma prayers.
The Jumma people have a rich cultural heritage in the Chittagong Hill Tracts.
He invited his friends to join him for Jumma at the local mosque.
