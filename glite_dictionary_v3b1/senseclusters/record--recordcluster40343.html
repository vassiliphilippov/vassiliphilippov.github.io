record

noun
disc for playing recorded music
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a flat, circular disc used to store and play back recorded music or sounds, typically played on a phonograph or turntable

Examples

A music record
He put his favorite record on the turntable and listened to it all evening.
Many collectors search for rare jazz records at auctions and flea markets.
The sound of a record playing has a warmth that many people still appreciate.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: CD - compact disc; a later technology for storing and playing music
  • degree higher: cassette - a magnetic tape audio storage format, similar use for music playback
  • synonym: vinyl - a phonograph record made from polyvinyl chloride, commonly used as a synonym for record
