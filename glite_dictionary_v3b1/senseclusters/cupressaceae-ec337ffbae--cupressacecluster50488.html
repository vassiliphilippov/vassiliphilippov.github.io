Cupressaceae

noun
family of coniferous trees (cypresses, junipers, cedars)
Pronunciation: / kuːˌprɛˈseɪʃiː /; / kuːˌprɛsˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / kjuːˌprɛˈsiːsiː /; / kjuːˌprɛsˈiːsiː / (BrE)
a family of coniferous trees and shrubs that includes cypresses, junipers, and many cedars

Cupressaceae family trees
Many garden hedges are made from species within the Cupressaceae.

The Cupressaceae are widespread, occurring on every continent except Antarctica.
