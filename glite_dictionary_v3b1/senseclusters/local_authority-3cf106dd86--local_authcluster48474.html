local authority

noun
a local governing body
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəl ɔːˈθɔːrəti / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ɔːˈθɒrɪti / (BrE)
a governing body responsible for administering public services and local affairs in a specific geographic area

Examples

The local authority office
Many parks are maintained by the local authority to provide recreational spaces for residents.
She contacted the local authority to apply for a building permit.
The local authority is responsible for waste collection and recycling in this area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: municipality - a city or town with its own local government
