branding

noun
promoting a public identity
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrændɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of creating and promoting a public, corporate, or personal identity (a 'brand') to influence how people perceive a product, service, or person.

Examples

Personal branding
In the digital era, personal branding is essential for freelancers seeking credibility online.
The startup invested heavily in branding to make its product stand out in crowded markets.
Good branding can change how customers perceive a company and increase willingness to pay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brand - a distinctive name, symbol, or identity used to identify a product, company, or person
  • synonym: marketing - the activity of promoting, selling, and distributing products or services; closely related to branding
