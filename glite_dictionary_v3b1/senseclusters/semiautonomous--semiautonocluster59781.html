semiautonomous

adjective
partly independent
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌɔtəˈnɑməs / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌɔːtəˈnəʊməs / (BrE)
partly able to operate, function, or govern itself independently

Examples

A semiautonomous division
The company created a semiautonomous division to handle its overseas operations.
Many modern cars now include semiautonomous driving features for added safety.
The region was granted semiautonomous status, allowing it some control over local affairs.

Related Terms
