blot

noun
small mark or stain
5.0
Pronunciation: / blɑt / (AmE), / blɒt / (BrE)
a small mark or stain, especially one made by ink, dirt, or another substance

Examples

An ink blot
A single blot on the white wall caught everyone's attention.
She wiped the blot of mud from her skirt before the event.
There was an ink blot on the contract that made it hard to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smudge - a dirty mark or smear
  • synonym: stain - a spot or discoloration left on a surface
