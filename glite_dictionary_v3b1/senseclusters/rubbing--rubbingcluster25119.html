rubbing

noun
act of moving with pressure
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving one object over another with pressure, typically to produce heat, clean a surface, or relieve soreness

Examples

Muscle rubbing for relief
After hours of rubbing, the tarnish was finally removed from the silver.
She felt a rubbing on her ankle where the new shoes were too tight.
The rubbing of the stones together eventually started a small fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friction - the resistance that one surface or object encounters when moving over another
