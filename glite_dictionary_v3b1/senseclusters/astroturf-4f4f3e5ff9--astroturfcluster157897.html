AstroTurf

noun
fake grassroots campaign
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈæs.troʊ.tɜːrf/ (AmE), /ˈæs.trəʊ.tɜːf/ (BrE)
a campaign or movement that is made to appear as grassroots support but is actually organized or funded by outside interests

Examples

Fake AstroTurf campaign
