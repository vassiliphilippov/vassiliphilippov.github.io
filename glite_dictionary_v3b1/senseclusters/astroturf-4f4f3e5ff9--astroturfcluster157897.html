AstroTurf

noun
fake grassroots campaign
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæstroʊˌtɝf / (AmE), / ˈæstrəʊˌtɜːf / (BrE)
a campaign or movement that is made to appear as grassroots support but is actually organized or funded by outside interests

Examples

Fake AstroTurf campaign
The protest was revealed to be AstroTurf organized by a major corporation.
Many politicians accused the campaign of being AstroTurf rather than genuine public support.
Journalists uncovered evidence that the online movement was actually AstroTurf funded by lobbyists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: front group - an organization that appears independent but is controlled by another group
  • antonym: grassroots - a movement or campaign that originates from ordinary people
