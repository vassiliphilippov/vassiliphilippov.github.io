chairless

adjective
without a leader
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃer.ləs/ /; / /ˈtʃeə.r.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈtʃeə.ləs/ (BrE)
lacking a chairperson or presiding officer; without someone appointed to lead or preside over a group or meeting

Examples

Chairless committee
The committee remained chairless after the unexpected resignation of its leader.
Important decisions were postponed because the board was still chairless.
A chairless meeting can quickly become disorganized without someone to guide the discussion.
