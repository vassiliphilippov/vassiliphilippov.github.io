jab

noun
a sudden poke with finger or elbow
Pronunciation: / dʒæb / (AmE), / dʒæb / (BrE)
a quick, sharp poke or push, especially with a finger, hand, or object

Examples

A sharp jab
A sudden jab from his finger made everyone laugh.
He gave his friend a playful jab with his elbow.
She felt a sharp jab in her side during the crowded train ride.
He gave me a warning jab with his finger when I interrupted him.
She silenced the barking dog with a gentle jab of her umbrella.
The boxer's quick jab caught his opponent off guard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poke - a quick, sharp push with something pointed
  • synonym: thrust - a sudden and forceful push forward
