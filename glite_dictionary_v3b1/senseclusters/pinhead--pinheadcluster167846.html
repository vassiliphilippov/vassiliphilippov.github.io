pinhead

noun
foolish or ignorant person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌhɛd / (BrE)
An ignorant or foolish person.

Examples

He is a pinhead
Don't be such a pinhead—read the instructions before you try to fix it.
He felt like a complete pinhead after forgetting his own anniversary.
Sometimes my brother acts like a real pinhead when he's tired.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person
  • synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person
