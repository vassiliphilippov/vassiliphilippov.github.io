slugging

noun
skincare moisturizing method
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡɪŋ / (BrE)
a skincare practice of applying a thick layer of moisturizer, often petroleum jelly, to seal in moisture overnight

Examples

Overnight slugging routine
Many people have started slugging to help their skin stay hydrated during winter.
After her evening routine, she finished by slugging to lock in all the moisture.
Some dermatologists recommend slugging for those with very dry or sensitive skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exfoliation - the process of removing dead skin cells from the surface
  • synonym: moisturizing - the act of applying products to add or retain skin moisture
