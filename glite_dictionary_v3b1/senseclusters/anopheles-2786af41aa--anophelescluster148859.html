Anopheles

noun
genus of malaria mosquitoes
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑː.fəˌliːz /; / əˈnɑfəˌliz / (AmE), / əˈnɒ.fəˌliːz /; / əˈnɒfəˌliːz / (BrE)
a genus of mosquitoes that can transmit malaria, with adults resting head downward and larvae that do not have breathing tubes
