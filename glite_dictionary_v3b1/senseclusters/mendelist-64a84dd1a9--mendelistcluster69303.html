Mendelist

noun
follower of Mendel
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛndəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛndəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or studies the genetic theories of Gregor Mendel

Examples

A famous Mendelist scientist
The Mendelist argued that inheritance follows predictable patterns based on Mendel's laws.
As a dedicated Mendelist, she spent years researching pea plant genetics.
Many early 20th-century scientists identified as Mendelists during debates about heredity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Darwinist - a person who supports the theories of Charles Darwin
  • synonym: geneticist - a scientist who studies genetics
