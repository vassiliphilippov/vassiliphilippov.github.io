nondevelopmental

adjective
not from early development
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
not originating during early growth or developmental stages, but occurring later in life, especially in medical or psychological contexts

Examples

Nondevelopmental disorder in adults
The doctor explained that her condition was nondevelopmental and appeared only in adulthood.
Researchers are studying the causes of nondevelopmental language disorders in teenagers.
Unlike congenital issues, nondevelopmental symptoms often emerge after the early years of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acquired - developed after birth, not present from the beginning
  • antonym: congenital - present from birth
