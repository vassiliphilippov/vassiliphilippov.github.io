comedogen

noun
substance that causes acne by clogging pores
9.0 8.7
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmiː.də.dʒən/ /; / /ˈkoʊ.miː.ə.dʒən/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmiː.də.dʒən/ /; / /ˈkəʊ.miː.ə.dʒən/ (BrE)
A substance that tends to cause the formation of acne by clogging pores on the skin.

Examples

Comedogen substance
Dermatologists often test cosmetics for comedogen content before recommending them to acne-prone patients.
If you have oily skin, check the ingredients for any comedogen that might exacerbate acne.
Many skincare products avoid using any comedogen to reduce the risk of breakouts.
