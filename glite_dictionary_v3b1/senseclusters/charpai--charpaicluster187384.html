charpai

noun
woven rope bed
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɑːrˈpaɪ / (AmE), / tʃɑːˈpaɪ / (BrE)
a traditional South Asian bed with a wooden frame and a surface woven from rope or tape

Examples

A charpai bed
Villagers often gather and sit on a charpai in the courtyard during the evening.
He took a nap on the old charpai after working in the fields all morning.
The guesthouse provided a comfortable charpai for travelers to rest on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cot - a small, simple bed, often portable
  • synonym: hammock - a hanging bed made of fabric or netting
