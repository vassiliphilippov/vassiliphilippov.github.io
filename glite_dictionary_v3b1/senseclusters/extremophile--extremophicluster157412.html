extremophile

noun
lover of extremes
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstriːməˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːməˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who seeks out, enjoys, or thrives on extreme conditions or experiences

Examples

An extremophile athlete
As an extremophile, he loved running ultramarathons in the desert and climbing the highest mountains.
The documentary followed a group of extremophiles as they explored the coldest places on Earth.
Her friends called her an extremophile because she always sought out the most challenging adventures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurer - a person who seeks excitement and new experiences
  • antonym: cautious person - someone who avoids danger or risk
  • synonym: risk-taker - someone who is willing to take risks
