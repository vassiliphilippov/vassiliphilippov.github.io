curling

adjective
hair that forms curls
6.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɜːr.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkɜː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of hair) forming curls or having been made to curl; characterized by the presence of curls.

Examples

Curling hair
Over time, his curling locks became his most distinctive feature.
She admired her daughter's curling hair as it gleamed in the sunlight.
The stylist used a hot iron to create curling waves in her hair.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: wavy - (of hair) forming slight curves or waves, not straight or tightly curled
