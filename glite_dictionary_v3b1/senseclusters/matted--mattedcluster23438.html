matted

adjective
tangled into a dense mass
Pronunciation: / ˈmætɪd / (AmE), / ˈmætɪd / (BrE)
(of hair, fur, or vegetation) tangled into a dense, compact mass, often becoming twisted or stuck together.

Examples

Matted hair
After the rain, hikers found the trail blocked by matted leaves and branches.
Her hair became matted after swimming in the lake without a cap.
The dog's matted fur needed to be carefully brushed out by the groomer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having a flat, even surface, not tangled or rough
  • synonym: tangled - twisted together in a disorderly mass
