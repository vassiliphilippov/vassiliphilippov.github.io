yearly

noun
annual reference book or report
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjɪrli / (AmE), / ˈjɪəli / (BrE)
a book or publication that is issued once every year, typically containing information or records for the preceding year

Examples

A science yearly
Each library keeps a copy of the science yearly for reference.
I look forward to reading the agricultural yearly every spring for the latest farming trends.
The financial yearly provided a comprehensive review of the company's performance.
