grand duchess

noun
female royal rank; wife of grand duke or woman of that rank
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd ˈdʌtʃ.ɪs / (AmE), / ɡrænd ˈdʌtʃ.ɪs / (BrE)
a woman who is the wife of a grand duke or who holds the rank of grand duke in her own right

Examples

The grand duchess wore a tiara
During the gala, the grand duchess wore a dazzling tiara passed down through generations.
Olga Alexandrovna was a celebrated grand duchess of the Russian imperial family.
She became a grand duchess after marrying the heir to the throne.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: duchess - the wife or widow of a duke, or a woman holding ducal rank in her own right
  • synonym: grand duke - a ruler or male of high rank, below king but above duke
