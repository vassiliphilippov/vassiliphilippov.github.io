huerta

noun
irrigated farmland
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛrtə / (AmE), / ˈwɛətə / (BrE)
an irrigated area of farmland, especially found in Spain or Spanish-speaking regions, used for growing fruits and vegetables

Examples

A huerta farm
The village is surrounded by a lush huerta that supplies fresh produce.
Farmers in the huerta use traditional irrigation methods to water their crops.
Tourists enjoyed walking through the huerta and sampling local fruits and vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: field - an area of open land for farming or pasture
  • synonym: orchard - a piece of land planted with fruit trees
