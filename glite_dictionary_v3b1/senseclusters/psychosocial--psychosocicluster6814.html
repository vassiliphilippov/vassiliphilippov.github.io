psychosocial

adjective
social and psychological
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
relating to the influence of social factors on an individual's mind, emotions, or behavior

Examples

Psychosocial factors
The counselor considered the psychosocial factors affecting the patient's recovery.
Schools play an important role in supporting children's psychosocial development.
The study examined the psychosocial impact of unemployment on families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychological - relating to the mind or mental processes
  • synonym: social - relating to society or its organization
