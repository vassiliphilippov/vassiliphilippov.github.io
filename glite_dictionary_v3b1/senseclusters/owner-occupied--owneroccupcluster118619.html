owner-occupied

adjective
lived in by the owner
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjupaɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊnə ˈɒkjupaɪd / (BrE)
(of a house, apartment, etc.) lived in by the person who owns it, rather than rented to someone else.

Examples

Owner-occupied house
Applicants must intend the property to be owner-occupied in order to qualify for the loan.
Only two of the houses on this street are owner-occupied; the rest are rented.
This building contains both owner-occupied units and rental apartments.

Related Terms
