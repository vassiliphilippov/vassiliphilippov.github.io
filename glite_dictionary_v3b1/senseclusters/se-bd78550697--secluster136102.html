SE

noun
southeast compass point
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ˈi / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˈiː / (BrE)
the compass point midway between south and east, corresponding to 135 degrees

Examples

The SE direction
The city lies to the SE of the capital, about 50 kilometers away.
The hiking trail continues in a SE direction towards the coast.
Winds from the SE are expected to bring heavy rain overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: southeast - the direction between south and east
