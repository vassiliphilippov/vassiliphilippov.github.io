opening

noun
sequence of opening chess moves
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
a recognized sequence of moves made at the beginning of a game of chess, typically following established theory

Examples

Chess opening
He spent hours studying the opening variations to improve his chess game.
She was surprised when her opponent played an unfamiliar opening.
The Ruy Lopez is a popular opening among grandmasters.
