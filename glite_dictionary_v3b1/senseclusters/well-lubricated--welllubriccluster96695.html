well-lubricated

adjective
properly greased or oiled
Pronunciation: / wɛl ˈluːbrɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈljuːbrɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Properly greased or oiled to ensure smooth operation, especially of machinery or mechanical parts.

Examples

A well-lubricated machine
A well-lubricated gear system reduces wear and extends machinery life.
Before starting the engine, make sure all moving parts are well-lubricated.
The technician checked that the conveyor belt was well-lubricated for optimal performance.

  • synonym: oiled - treated or coated with oil for smoothness or preservation
