funk hole

noun
a safe retreat or shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋk hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋk həʊl / (BrE)
A place, especially a dugout or shelter, used as a safe retreat during times of danger or anxiety.
