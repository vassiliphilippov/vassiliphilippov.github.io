shuffle

noun
mixing playing cards
1.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈʃʌf.əl/ (AmE), /ˈʃʌf.əl/ (BrE)
a way of mixing or arranging things, especially cards or songs, in a random order

Examples

A shuffle mode
A good shuffle is essential to ensure a fair game.
After each round, he performed a quick shuffle of the deck.
She taught her younger brother how to do a proper shuffle.
I turned on shuffle to listen to my playlist in random order.
Her phone played the whole album on shuffle, so tracks came in no particular order.
When studying, he prefers to set the lecture recordings to shuffle for varied review each day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: repeat - a playback mode that repeats items in the same order; often the opposite of random play
  • synonym: riffle - a method of shuffling cards by interleaving them
  • synonym: shuffle (verb) - to play or arrange items in a random order
