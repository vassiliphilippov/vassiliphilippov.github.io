circum-

x
prefix meaning around
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkəm / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəm / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'around' or 'about', used to form words that indicate surrounding or encircling something

Examples

The circum- prefix
The prefix circum- appears in words like 'circumference' and 'circumnavigate'.
Adding circum- to a word often gives the sense of going around something.
In 'circumvent', the circum- prefix means to go around an obstacle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peri- - a prefix meaning 'around', especially in medical terms
