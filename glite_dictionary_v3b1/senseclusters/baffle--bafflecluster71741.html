baffle

verb
restrain emission of sound, fluid, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈbæfəl / (AmE), / ˈbæfəl / (BrE)
to restrain the emission of sound, fluid, or other substances, typically by using a barrier or device

Examples

To baffle the noise
A filter can baffle the flow of air to reduce noise.
Engineers installed panels to baffle the sound in the concert hall.
The speaker box was designed to baffle unwanted vibrations.
