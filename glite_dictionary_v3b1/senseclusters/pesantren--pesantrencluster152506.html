pesantren

noun
Indonesian Islamic boarding school
Pronunciation: / pɛˈsæntrɛn / (AmE), / pɛˈsantrɛn / (BrE)
A boarding school in Indonesia run by Muslim religious leaders, focusing on Islamic education and moral instruction.

Examples

A pesantren school
Many young Indonesians receive their education at a pesantren in their local community.
Parents appreciate the discipline and values taught at the pesantren.
The pesantren offers both religious and secular subjects to its students.
