counterblast

noun
a forceful response or reaction
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌblæst / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəbˌlɑːst / (BrE)
a strong and forceful reaction or response, especially one made in opposition to another person's words or actions

Examples

A counterblast statement
After the newspaper article was published, the company issued a public counterblast defending its practices.
His controversial statement met with an immediate counterblast on social media.
The senator's remarks provoked a fierce counterblast from her opponents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outburst - a sudden and strong expression of emotion or reaction
  • synonym: rejoinder - a reply, especially a sharp or witty one
  • synonym: retort - a quick, sharp, or witty reply
