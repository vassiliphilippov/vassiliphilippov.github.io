knapsack

noun
bag with straps carried on the back
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈnap.sæk / (BrE)
a bag with straps, carried on the back or over the shoulder, used for carrying personal belongings

Examples

A knapsack bag
After traveling all day, her knapsack felt surprisingly heavy.
She packed her water bottle and lunch into her knapsack before leaving for the hike.
The student slung his knapsack over his shoulder as he left the library.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back with shoulder straps
