savage humor

noun
brutally honest or harsh comedy
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsævɪdʒ ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈsævɪdʒ ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
comedy or wit that is brutally honest, harsh, or biting in its criticism or delivery

Examples

Savage humor style
Her savage humor divided the crowd—some laughed, others squirmed at the brutality of her jokes.
The comedian is known for his savage humor that spares no one in the audience.
The novel's narrative is dark, laced with savage humor about the absurdity of modern life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentle humor - mild, light-hearted comedy
  • synonym: sarcasm - the use of irony to mock or convey contempt
  • synonym: wit - clever and humorous expression
