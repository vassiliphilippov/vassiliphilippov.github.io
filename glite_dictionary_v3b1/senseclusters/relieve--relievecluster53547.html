Synonyms

  • ease
    2.1 14/m
    ease makes something less difficult or painful and often acts gently, while relieve means to reduce a pain or burden.
  • alleviate
    5.3 4/m
    alleviate reduces pain, suffering, or problems in a clear way, while relieve is a more general word for easing discomfort.
  • assuage
    7.7 0.9/m
    assuage calls for calming or satisfying a strong feeling like guilt, while relieve focuses on making pain or stress less.
  • allay
    6.9 0.6/m
    allay reduces fears, doubts, or anger quickly, while relieve covers easing physical pain or a heavy burden more broadly.
  • palliate
    9.0 0.01/m
    palliate reduces symptoms of a disease without curing it, while relieve is a general verb for lessening pain or stress.
  • soothe
    3.4 3/m
    soothe calms someone and can ease pain with soft touch or words, while relieve simply makes discomfort less.
  • comfort
    1.0 8/m
    comfort gives support or kind words to a person in pain, while relieve refers to making the pain or burden smaller.
  • lighten
    2.5 1/m
    lighten makes a load or mood less heavy, sometimes by small steps, while relieve means to reduce or remove serious burden.
