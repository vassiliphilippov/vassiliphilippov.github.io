bloom of youth

noun
prime time of youth
Pronunciation: / bluːm əv juθ / (AmE), / bluːm əv juːθ / (BrE)
the period in a person's life when they are young and at their physical or emotional prime

Examples

The bloom of youth period
He longed for the bloom of youth that had faded from his face.
In the bloom of youth, she achieved remarkable things that shaped her entire career.
Poets often praise the bloom of youth for its vitality and beauty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prime of life - the period when one is at the peak of health, strength, or vigor
