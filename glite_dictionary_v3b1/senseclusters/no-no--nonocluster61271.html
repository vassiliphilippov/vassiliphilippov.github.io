no-no

noun
prohibited or frowned-upon thing
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.nəʊ / (BrE)
an action, behavior, or thing that is not allowed or is strongly discouraged

Examples

A no-no rule
It's a big no-no to use your phone during meetings in this company.
Tracking mud into the house is always a no-no at my grandmother's place.
Wearing jeans to the formal dinner was considered a no-no.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptable - something allowed or approved of
  • synonym: taboo - something prohibited by social custom
