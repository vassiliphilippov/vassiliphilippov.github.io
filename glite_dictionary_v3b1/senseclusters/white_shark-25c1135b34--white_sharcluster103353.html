white shark

noun
large predatory shark species
1.2
Pronunciation: / waɪt ʃɑrk / (AmE), / waɪt ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large, aggressive shark (Carcharodon carcharias) found in warm seas, sometimes known to attack humans

Examples

A white shark
Researchers tagged a white shark to track its migration patterns across the Pacific Ocean.
Swimmers are warned to be cautious because a white shark was recently spotted in these waters.
The white shark is one of the ocean's most feared predators due to its size and power.
