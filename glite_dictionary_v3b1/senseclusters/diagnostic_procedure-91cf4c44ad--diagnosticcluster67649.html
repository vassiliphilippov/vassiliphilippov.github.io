diagnostic procedure

noun
method used to diagnose illness
4.4 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / da…™…ôgÀàn…ëst…™k pro äÀàsiÀêd í…ö / (AmE), / Àåda…™…ôgÀàn…íst…™k pr…ôÀàsiÀêd í…ô / (BrE)
a medical process or test carried out to determine the nature or cause of a disease or condition

Examples

A diagnostic procedure test
An MRI scan is a common diagnostic procedure used to examine internal organs.
Before recommending treatment, physicians usually perform at least one diagnostic procedure.
The doctor ordered a diagnostic procedure to identify the cause of her symptoms.

Synonyms

  • diagnostic test
    3.5 0.79/m
    a diagnostic test is a single, specific medical test that gives clear results, while a diagnostic procedure covers any process to find a disease.
  • diagnostic technique
    4.0 0.13/m
    a diagnostic technique is a practical method used to examine patients, while a diagnostic procedure is a formal process that may include tests.
