diagnostic procedure

noun
method used to diagnose illness
Pronunciation: / daɪəgˈnɑstɪk proʊˈsiːdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəgˈnɒstɪk prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a medical process or test carried out to determine the nature or cause of a disease or condition

Examples

A diagnostic procedure test
An MRI scan is a common diagnostic procedure used to examine internal organs.
Before recommending treatment, physicians usually perform at least one diagnostic procedure.
The doctor ordered a diagnostic procedure to identify the cause of her symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagnosis - the identification of the nature of an illness or other problem
  • antonym: treatment - medical care given to a patient for an illness or injury
