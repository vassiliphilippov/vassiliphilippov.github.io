law of nations

noun
laws governing relations between nations
Pronunciation: / lɔ ʌv ˈneɪʃənz / (AmE), / lɔː əv ˈneɪʃənz / (BrE)
the body of laws governing the conduct and relations between independent nations; international law

Examples

Law of nations rules
His research focuses on the history and development of the law of nations.
Many consider violations of the law of nations to be serious international offenses.
The law of nations provides the legal framework for diplomatic relations and treaties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: international law - the set of rules generally regarded and accepted as binding in relations among countries
