serum

noun
liquid for treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪərəm / (BrE)
a clear liquid, often used in skincare or medical treatment, that contains active ingredients or antibodies

Examples

Face serum
She applies a serum to her face every morning to keep her skin hydrated.
The doctor injected the serum to help the patient fight the infection.
Many people use a vitamin C serum as part of their daily skincare routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lotion - a liquid preparation applied to the skin
  • synonym: ointment - a smooth substance applied to the skin for healing
