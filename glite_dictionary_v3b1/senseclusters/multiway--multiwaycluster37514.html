multiway

adjective
involving several options
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌweɪ / (BrE)
involving or allowing more than two possible ways, directions, or options

Examples

Multiway valve
The engineer installed a multiway valve to control the flow in different directions.
A multiway conference call allows several people to join the discussion from various locations.
The multiway test provides students with several possible answers to choose from.
