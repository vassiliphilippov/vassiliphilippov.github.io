northerly

adjective
oriented toward the north
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðəli / (BrE)
oriented toward or situated in the north; facing or moving toward the north

Examples

Northerly direction
The hikers set off in a northerly direction to reach the mountain summit.
These plants thrive best on slopes with a northerly aspect due to the shade.
We could feel a cool northerly breeze blowing across the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: northern - located in or toward the north
  • antonym: southerly - oriented toward or situated in the south
