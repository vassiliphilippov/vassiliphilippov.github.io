apricot liqueur

noun
sweet alcoholic drink made from apricots
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.rɪ.kɑt lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a sweet alcoholic beverage made from apricots, often served as a dessert drink or used in cocktails
