hay-scented

adjective
having a scent like hay
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪ sɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈheɪ sɛntɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a scent reminiscent of hay, especially referring to the hay-scented fern native to eastern North America.

Examples

Hay-scented fern
As I brushed past the hay-scented plants, the familiar smell brought back memories of childhood hikes.
Gardeners often plant hay-scented varieties for their pleasant fragrance and lush appearance.
The woods were filled with the sweet aroma of hay-scented ferns on a hot summer day.
