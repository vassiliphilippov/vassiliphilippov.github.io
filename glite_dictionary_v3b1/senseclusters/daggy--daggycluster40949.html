daggy

adjective
shaggy or dirty
Pronunciation: / ˈdæɡi / (AmE), / ˈdæɡi / (BrE)
shaggy, dirty, or matted, especially with clumps of dried material or dirt sticking to the surface.

Examples

Daggy sheep
After running through the muddy field, the sheep looked especially daggy.
The old sweater became daggy after years of use and little washing.
He noticed his dog was daggy from playing outside in the rain and dirt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt or impurities
  • synonym: shaggy - having long, untidy, or messy hair or fibers
