atomic number 81

noun
element thallium (Tl), metallic, toxic
Pronunciation: / əˌtɑmɪk ˈnʌmbɚ ˈeɪti wʌn / (AmE), / əˌtɒmɪk ˈnʌmbə ˈeɪti wʌn / (BrE)
Thallium; a soft, grey, malleable metallic element (symbol Tl) that resembles tin but discolors on exposure to air. Highly toxic, it is used in rodent and insect poisons and occurs in zinc blende and some iron ores.
