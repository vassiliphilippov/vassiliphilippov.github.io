grated cheese

noun
cheese shredded by grating
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtɪd tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪd tʃiːz / (BrE)
Cheese that has been shredded or reduced to small pieces by rubbing it against a grater, typically used as a topping or ingredient.

Examples

A handful of grated cheese
She added a handful of grated cheese to the omelette mixture.
Sprinkle grated cheese over the pasta before serving.
The recipe calls for a cup of grated cheese as a topping.
