blogroll

verb
to add to blog list
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːɡˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈblɒɡˌrəʊl / (BrE)
to add a blog or website to a list of recommended links on one's own blog

Examples

To blogroll a site
She decided to blogroll several new writers to help raise their online profiles.
If you enjoy my content, please blogroll my site on your homepage.
Many bloggers blogroll their favorite sites to share useful resources with readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: link - to create a hyperlink to another website
  • synonym: recommend - to suggest something as worthy or good
