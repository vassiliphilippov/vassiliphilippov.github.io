pickle relish

noun
chopped pickle condiment
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkəl ˈrɛlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəl ˈrɛlɪʃ / (BrE)
a condiment made from chopped pickles, often sweetened and used as a topping or flavoring
