spartanly

adverb
simply or strictly
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈspɑːr.tən.li/ (AmE), /ˈspɑː.tən.li/ (BrE)
in a way that is very simple, strict, and lacking in comfort or luxury

Examples

Spartanly decorated room
The cabin was spartanly decorated, with only the bare essentials for living.
He lived spartanly during his training, avoiding any unnecessary comforts.
Meals were served spartanly, with just bread and water on the table.
They lived spartanly during their expedition, carrying only the bare essentials.
The room was furnished spartanly, with just a bed and a small table.
He accepted the challenges of training spartanly, never asking for special treatment.
