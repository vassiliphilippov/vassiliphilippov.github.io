tore

noun
lowest molding at a column base
Pronunciation: / tɔr / (AmE), / tɔː / (BrE)
The lowest, usually rounded, molding at the base of a column in classical architecture.

Examples

The tore of a column
Careful restoration preserved the original marble tore on the temple's columns.
The tore is an essential feature in many Doric and Ionic column designs.
The architect specified a prominent tore to accentuate the base of each column.
