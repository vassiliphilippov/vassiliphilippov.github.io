bungee cord

noun
elasticized rope with hooks
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌn.dʒi kɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.dʒi kɔːd / (BrE)
a strong, elasticized rope with hooks at each end, used to secure or fasten objects

Examples

A bungee cord strap
He used a bungee cord to secure the luggage to the roof of his car.
Make sure the bungee cord is not stretched too tightly, or it might snap.
The tent poles were tied together with a strong bungee cord.
