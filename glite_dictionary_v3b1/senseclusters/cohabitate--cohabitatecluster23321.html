cohabitate

verb
to live together
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhæbɪteɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈhæbɪteɪt / (BrE)
to live together in the same home, especially as an unmarried couple

Examples

Couples cohabitate before marriage
Many couples choose to cohabitate before deciding to get married.
They have been cohabitating for three years and share all household responsibilities.
Some cultures discourage young people from cohabitating before marriage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cohabit - to live together as a couple, usually without being married
  • antonym: separate - to stop living together as a couple
