tabernacle

noun
a Mormon place of worship
Pronunciation: / ˈtæbɚˌnækəl / (AmE), / ˈtæbənækəl / (BrE)
a large building used for worship and other religious gatherings by members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, especially the Salt Lake Tabernacle

Examples

Mormon tabernacle
Many visitors tour the famous Mormon tabernacle for its unique architecture and acoustics.
The annual conference is held at the LDS tabernacle downtown.
The choir performed in the historic tabernacle in Salt Lake City.
