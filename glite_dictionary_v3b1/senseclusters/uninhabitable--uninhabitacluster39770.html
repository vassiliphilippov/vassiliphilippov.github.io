uninhabitable

adjective
not fit to live in
5.6 5.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪn.ˈhæb.ɪ.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪn.ˈhæb.ɪ.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
