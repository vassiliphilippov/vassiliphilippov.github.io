roadie

noun
fan who follows band
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdi / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdi / (BrE)
a fan who travels to many concerts by the same band or performer, often following them from place to place

Examples

Superfan roadie travels
As a dedicated roadie, she attended every show on the band's national tour.
The roadie made friends with other fans while traveling from city to city.
He became a roadie after seeing his favorite band perform live for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groupie - a fan who follows a band or celebrity closely, often attending many events
