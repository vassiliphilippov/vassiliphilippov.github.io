sketch

noun
short written account or description
Pronunciation: / skɛtʃ / (AmE), / skɛtʃ / (BrE)
a short written or spoken account or summary that gives the main features of something

Examples

A sketch of history
Each chapter begins with a brief sketch outlining the main historical events.
Her memoir includes a moving sketch of her childhood in the countryside.
The journalist wrote a compelling sketch of the famous artist's life.
At the start of the meeting, he gave a sketch of the project's background.
Her biography included a brief sketch of each important period in her life.
The journalist wrote a sketch of the day's major events for the evening news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outline - brief summary or general account of something
  • synonym: summary - a brief statement or account of the main points of something
