truther

adjective
conspiracy theory-related
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːθər / (AmE), / ˈtruːθə / (BrE)
relating to people who believe or promote conspiracy theories, or to their claims and ideas

Examples

Truther claims
The truther websites quickly spread rumors after the news broke.
She dismissed the truther claims as unfounded and misleading.
Many documentaries with a truther perspective question the official explanation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mainstream - representing widely accepted ideas or beliefs
