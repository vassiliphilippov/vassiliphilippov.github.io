peanut gallery

noun
group making trivial or unwelcome comments
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnʌt ˌɡæləri /; / ˈpinət ˌɡæləri / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt ˌɡæləri /; / ˈpiːnət ˌɡæləri / (BrE)
a group of people who make comments or criticisms, often loudly and without much authority or respect

Examples

Comments from the peanut gallery
Her suggestion was met with sarcastic laughter from the peanut gallery.
I wish the peanut gallery would keep their opinions to themselves during the meeting.
Ignore the comments from the peanut gallery and focus on your work.
Please ignore the peanut gallery and focus on your work.
She made a joke that drew laughter from the peanut gallery.
Whenever a decision is made, the peanut gallery always has something to say.

  • synonym: hecklers - people who interrupt performances with unwanted remarks
