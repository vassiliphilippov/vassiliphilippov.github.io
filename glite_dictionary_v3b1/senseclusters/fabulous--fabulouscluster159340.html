fabulous

adjective
relating to myth or fable
Pronunciation: / ˈfæbjələs / (AmE), / ˈfæbjʊləs / (BrE)
Relating to or told in fable or mythology; described in traditional stories and not based on historical fact.

Examples

Fabulous creatures
Some claimed to have seen a fabulous beast deep in the forest, but there was no real evidence.
The ancient Greeks believed in fabulous creatures like the phoenix and the minotaur.
The tale describes a fabulous land of golden palaces and eternal youth.

  • synonym: legendary - described in legend; famous in traditional stories
  • synonym: mythical - existing only in traditional stories; of myth
