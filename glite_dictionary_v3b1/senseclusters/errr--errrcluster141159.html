errr

interjection
mild disgust sound
Pronunciation: / ɜːr / (AmE), / ɜːr / (BrE)
used to express mild disgust, distaste, or disapproval in reaction to something unpleasant

Examples

He tasted the old milk and muttered errr, refusing to drink any more of it.
When she saw the bug crawling on her sandwich, she said errr and pushed the plate away.
The smell from the garbage made everyone go errr as they walked by.
