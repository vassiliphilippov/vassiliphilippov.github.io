hard sauce

noun
sweet butter sauce for desserts
3.9
Pronunciation: / hɑrd sɔs / (AmE), / hɑːd sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweet, creamy sauce made from butter and sugar, often flavored with brandy or other spirits, served with hot desserts such as pudding.

Examples

A hard sauce dessert
She made hard sauce using butter, sugar, and a splash of brandy for extra flavor.
The chef recommended serving the warm cake with a generous spoonful of hard sauce.
Would you like some hard sauce on your Christmas pudding?
