quinsy

noun
painful tonsil infection (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪnzi / (AmE), / ˈkwɪnzi / (BrE)
A painful, pus-filled inflammation of the tonsils and surrounding tissues, usually as a complication of tonsillitis.

Examples

Severe quinsy infection
Doctors diagnosed her with quinsy when she complained of intense throat pain and difficulty swallowing.
The patient developed quinsy after a severe case of tonsillitis.
Without prompt treatment, quinsy can lead to serious complications.
