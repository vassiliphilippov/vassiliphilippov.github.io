lithological

adjective
relating to rocks
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the physical characteristics, composition, or type of rocks

Examples

The lithological characteristics
The lithological differences between the two regions explain their unique landscapes.
Geologists use lithological data to classify and map rock formations.
A lithological survey was conducted before the construction of the dam.
