graywater

adjective
wastewater reuse
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
relating to the collection, treatment, or use of wastewater from sinks, showers, or washing machines, not including toilet waste.

Examples

Graywater treatment unit
The new building features a graywater system to irrigate the landscaping and flush toilets.
Many eco-friendly homes use graywater pipes to reduce their overall water consumption.
A graywater treatment unit can help conserve water in areas facing drought.
