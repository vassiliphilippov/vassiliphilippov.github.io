meme

noun
viral online image or media item
3.4
Pronunciation: /miːm/ (AmE), /miːm/ (BrE)
An image, video, piece of text, or other digital media item, often humorous or satirical, that is copied and shared widely online and modified by many users.

Examples

Funny meme online
The meme about the distracted boyfriend spread across social media overnight.
Teenagers often create new memes by combining familiar pictures with funny, topical captions.
Marketing teams sometimes try to produce a meme that will go viral and boost engagement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: viral content - online material that spreads rapidly and widely between users.
