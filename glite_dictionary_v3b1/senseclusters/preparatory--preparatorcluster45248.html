preparatory

adjective
serving as preparation
4.2
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpɛrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / prɪˈparətəri / (BrE)
done in order to prepare for something; serving as a preparation or introduction.

Examples

Preparatory class
She enrolled in a preparatory class to get ready for the exam.
The students took preparatory courses before starting the main program.
We need to complete some preparatory work before the meeting tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concluding - serving to finish or bring to an end
  • synonym: introductory - serving as an introduction to something
