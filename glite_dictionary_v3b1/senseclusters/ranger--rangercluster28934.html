ranger

noun
official managing a forest or park
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒə / (BrE)
an official employed to manage, protect, and oversee a forest, park, or nature reserve

Examples

Park ranger
A team of rangers patrols the forest every day to prevent illegal logging.
After the storm, the ranger checked the trails for fallen trees.
The ranger explained the rules of the national park to the visitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poacher - a person who illegally hunts or fishes on protected land
  • synonym: warden - a person responsible for supervising a particular place or area
