Examples

To pulsate with music
Bright lights pulsated above the dance floor, creating an electric atmosphere.
His temples pulsated with pain after the stressful meeting.
The crowd felt the music pulsate through the floor during the concert.
Her heart began to pulsate rapidly as she waited for the results.
The city streets pulsate with energy during the annual festival.
The lights in the club pulsated to the rhythm of the music.

Synonyms

  • pulse
    4.2 2.35/m
    pulse moves or expands in a steady beat like a heart, while pulsate describes regular widening and shrinking motion.
  • throb
    3.1 0.80/m
    throb is a strong, often painful beat felt in the body, while pulsate is a regular, gentler rhythmic movement.
