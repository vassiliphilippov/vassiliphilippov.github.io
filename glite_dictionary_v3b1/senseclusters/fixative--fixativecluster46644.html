fixative

noun
chemical to prevent smudging of art
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪksətɪv /; / ˈfɪk.sə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɪksətɪv /; / ˈfɪk.sə.tɪv / (BrE)
a chemical substance used to preserve, protect, or stabilize materials such as artwork or biological samples

A good fixative can help artwork last much longer without fading or damage.
