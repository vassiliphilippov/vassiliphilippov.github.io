balm for a troubled mind

noun
something that soothes or comforts the anxious
Pronunciation: / bɑm fər ə ˈtrʌbəld maɪnd / (AmE), / bɑːm fə ə ˈtrʌbəld maɪnd / (BrE)
something that provides comfort or relief to someone who is anxious or distressed

Examples

A balm for a troubled mind
For many people, taking a peaceful walk in nature is a balm for a troubled mind.
Her kind words were a balm for a troubled mind during his difficult times.
Listening to gentle music always acts as a balm for a troubled mind after a stressful day.
