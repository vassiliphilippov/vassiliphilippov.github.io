handicraftsman

noun
skilled manual artisan
Pronunciation: / ˈhændiˌkræftsmən / (AmE), / ˈhændɪˌkrɑːftsmən / (BrE)
a person skilled in making decorative or practical objects by hand

Examples

A handicraftsman making baskets
The handicraftsman displayed his woven baskets at the market.
Each handicraftsman in the village specializes in a different traditional technique.
A skilled handicraftsman can turn simple materials into beautiful works of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in a craft, especially one that involves making things by hand
  • synonym: craftsman - a person who is skilled at making things by hand
