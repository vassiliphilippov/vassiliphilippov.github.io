deadened

adjective
made less intense
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdənd / (AmE), / ˈdɛdənd / (BrE)
(of a feeling, sensation, or emotion) made or become less intense or less strong.

Examples

Deadened emotions
After years of exposure, his senses became deadened to the noise of the city.
Painkillers left her with a deadened sensation in her arm.
The shock of the news was deadened by her calm demeanor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - to make (something) less intense or sharp
  • antonym: intensified - made or become stronger or more intense
