rapaciousness

noun
predatory or greedy quality
8.1
Pronunciation: / rəˈpeɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / rəˈpeɪʃəsnɪs /; / rəˈpeɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
extreme greed or a strong, predatory desire to get more than is needed or fair
