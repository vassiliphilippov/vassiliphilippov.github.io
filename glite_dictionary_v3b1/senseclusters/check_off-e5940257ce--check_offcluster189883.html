check off

verb
mark as completed on a list
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk ɔf /; / tʃɛk ɑf / (AmE), / tʃɛk ɒf / (BrE)
to mark an item on a list to show it has been completed, dealt with, or verified

Examples

To check off items
As I packed my bag, I checked off each item on the list.
Please remember to check off all completed tasks by the end of the day.
She carefully checked off the students' names as they entered the classroom.
He quickly checked off all the names on the attendance sheet.
Please check off the boxes next to the items you have packed.
She checked off each task as she finished it.
Please check off each item on the inventory list as you inspect it.
I will check off the tasks as I finish them throughout the day.
She forgot to check off the last box on the form before submitting it.

  • synonym: tick off - to mark an item on a list as done, often used in British English
