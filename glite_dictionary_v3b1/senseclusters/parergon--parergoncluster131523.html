parergon

noun
secondary work
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɜːrɡɑn / (AmE), / pəˈrɜːɡɒn / (BrE)
an accessory or secondary work, especially one that complements or surrounds a main work in art or literature

Examples

A parergon in art
The introduction was treated as a parergon to the main text of the novel.
In classical architecture, the decorative border is often considered a parergon rather than part of the structure itself.
Scholars debated whether the footnotes should be seen as a parergon or as essential to understanding the essay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appendix - a supplementary section at the end of a book or document
  • antonym: main work - the primary or central work of art or literature
