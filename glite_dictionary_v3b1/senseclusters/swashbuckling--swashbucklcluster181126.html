swashbuckling

adjective
daring and flamboyantly adventurous
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈswɑːʃ.bʌk.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈswɒʃ.bʌk.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
