postnational

adjective
beyond nation-states
9.2
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ˈnæʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ˈnæʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)
relating to a situation, era, or perspective that goes beyond the traditional nation-state or national boundaries

Examples

Postnational society
The rise of global organizations reflects a postnational approach to solving international problems.
In a postnational society, cultural identity is shaped more by global influences than by national borders.
Some political theorists discuss the challenges of governance in a postnational era.

Related Terms

  • antonym: national - relating to a nation or country
  • synonym: transnational - extending or operating across national boundaries
