bittern

noun
a marsh-dwelling heron with a booming call
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtɚn / (AmE), / ˈbɪtən / (BrE)
a small, compact, tawny-brown heron found in marshes, known for its nocturnal habits and distinctive booming cry

Examples

The bittern bird
Conservationists are working to protect the wetland habitats essential for the bittern.
The elusive bittern is rarely seen but often heard thanks to its distinctive call.
We watched a bittern emerge silently from the reeds at dusk.

Related Terms
