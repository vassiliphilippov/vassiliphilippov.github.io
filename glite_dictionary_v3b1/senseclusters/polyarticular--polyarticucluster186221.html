polyarticular

adjective
involving many joints
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliɑrˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌpɒliɑːˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
affecting or involving many joints at the same time

Examples

Polyarticular arthritis
The patient was diagnosed with polyarticular arthritis, which caused pain in several joints.
Children with polyarticular juvenile arthritis may experience swelling in both knees and wrists.
Treatment for polyarticular conditions often requires a combination of medications and physical therapy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monoarticular - affecting a single joint
  • antonym: oligoarticular - affecting a few joints
