applanation

noun
flattening for measurement
Pronunciation: / ˌæpləˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæpləˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the flattening of a surface, especially as a method for measuring pressure in the eye

Examples

Applanation tonometry
The ophthalmologist performed applanation to check the patient's intraocular pressure.
Accurate applanation is essential for diagnosing certain eye conditions.
Modern devices use applanation techniques to ensure reliable eye pressure measurements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flattening - the act of making something flat
