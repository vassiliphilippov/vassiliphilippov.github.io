purposefully

adverb
with determination or clear aim
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrpəsfəli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəsfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows determination or has a clear aim; done with a particular purpose.

Examples

Walk purposefully forward
He walked purposefully toward the exit, determined to leave the meeting early.
She purposefully ignored his comments, focusing on her work instead.
The artist arranged the objects purposefully to achieve the perfect composition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidentally - in a way that is not planned or intended; antonym of 'purposefully'
  • synonym: deliberately - in a careful and intentional way; synonym of 'purposefully'
