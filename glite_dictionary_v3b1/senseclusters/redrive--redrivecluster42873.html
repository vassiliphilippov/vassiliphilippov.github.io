redrive

noun
repeated attempt
8.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêÀådra…™v / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêÀådra…™v / (BrE)
an act of driving, sending, or processing something again, especially after a previous attempt has failed

Examples

System redrive event
The system automatically initiates a redrive if the first data transfer fails.
After the error, a redrive of the process was scheduled for the next morning.
Engineers monitored the redrive to ensure all messages were delivered successfully.

Synonyms

  • retransmission
    4.4 0.21/m

  • retry
    3.3 0.09/m
    retry is a fresh attempt to do an action again after it fails, while redrive focuses on driving or processing the item again.
  • reattempt
    3.5 0.00/m
    reattempt means trying something again after an interruption or failure, while redrive emphasizes sending, driving, or processing the same thing again.
  • resubmission
    4.1 0.07/m
    resubmission is sending a document or form again, usually after changes, while redrive means re-sending or reprocessing without that correction focus.
  • reemission
    7.9 0.00/m
    reemission is issuing or sending something again in formal or technical settings, while redrive is a general act of driving or processing again.
