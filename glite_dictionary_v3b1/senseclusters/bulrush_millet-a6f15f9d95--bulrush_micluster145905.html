bulrush millet

noun
a tall grain-bearing grass used for food and forage
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊl.rʌʃ ˈmɪl.ət/ /; / /ˈbʊl.rʌʃ ˈmɪl.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈbʊl.rʌʃ ˈmɪl.ət/ /; / /ˈbʊl.rʌʃ ˈmɪl.ɪt/ (BrE)
a tall grass cultivated in Africa and Asia for its edible grain and in the United States mostly as animal forage; sometimes used in brewing beer

Examples

Some African beers are brewed using fermented bulrush millet.
The grain from bulrush millet is a traditional food staple in parts of Africa.
