left the building

verb
departed a place; has gone
Pronunciation: / lɛft ðə ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / lɛft ðə ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone has departed or exited a place, often unexpectedly or finally

Examples

He left the building quickly
By the time security arrived, the suspect had already left the building.
Once the star has left the building, the fans start to disperse.
The boss left the building without saying goodbye to anyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrive - to reach a place, especially at the end of a journey
  • synonym: depart - to leave, especially to go on a journey
