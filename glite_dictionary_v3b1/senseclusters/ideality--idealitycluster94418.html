ideality

noun
the state of being ideal or perfect
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪdiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌaɪdɪˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being ideal; the state of being perfect or representing the best possible standard

Examples

The ideality of art
His art sought not realism, but a sense of ideality and transcendence.
Philosophers have debated whether ideality can exist in the real world.
The ideality of her vision inspired everyone who heard her speak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perfection - the condition or quality of being free from all flaws or defects
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
