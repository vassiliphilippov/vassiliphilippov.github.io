waitlist

noun
list of people waiting
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈweɪtˌlɪst / (BrE)
a list of people waiting for a place, service, or opportunity when spaces are limited

Examples

On the waitlist
After applying to the university, she was placed on the waitlist for admission.
The restaurant was so busy that we had to join the waitlist for a table.
He checked his status on the waitlist every day, hoping for a spot to open up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: queue - a line or list of people waiting for something
  • antonym: reservation - an arrangement to secure a place in advance
