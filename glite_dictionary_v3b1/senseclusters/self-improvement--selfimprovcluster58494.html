Synonyms

  • personal growth
    4.0 0.5/m
    personal growth stresses gradual self-knowledge and inner change through reflection, while self-improvement focuses on active skill and habit work.
  • self-cultivation
    6.7 0.01/m
    self-cultivation means disciplined study and practice to shape character and skills, while self-improvement covers many small personal efforts.
  • self-help
    3.6 2/m
    self-help refers to books, techniques, or courses people use to solve problems, while self-improvement is the wider process of getting better.
  • self-reformation
    6.8 0.00/m
