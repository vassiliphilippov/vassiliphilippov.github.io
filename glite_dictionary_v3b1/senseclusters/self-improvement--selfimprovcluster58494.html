self-improvement

noun
the process of making yourself better
1.0
Pronunciation: / sɛlf ɪmˈpruvmənt /; / ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpruvmənt / (AmE), / sɛlf ɪmˈpruːvmənt /; / ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpruːvmənt / (BrE)
the process of making yourself better in knowledge, character, skills, or habits through your own efforts

Examples
