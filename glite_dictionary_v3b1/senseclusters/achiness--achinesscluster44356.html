achiness

noun
dull pain or longing
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪkinəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪkinəs / (BrE)
a dull, persistent feeling of pain or longing, often emotional or physical in nature

Examples

Emotional achiness
She felt a deep achiness in her heart after saying goodbye to her childhood home.
The achiness in his muscles lingered for days after the long hike.
There was a quiet achiness in the room as everyone remembered their lost friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ache - a continuous or dull pain
  • antonym: comfort - a state of physical or emotional ease
  • synonym: yearning - a strong feeling of longing or desire
