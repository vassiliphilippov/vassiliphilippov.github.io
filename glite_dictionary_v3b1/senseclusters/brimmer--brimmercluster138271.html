brimmer

noun
full glass or cup
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪmər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪmə / (BrE)
a container, especially a glass, that is filled to the very top

Examples

A brimmer of beer
He raised a brimmer of ale to toast his friends.
The bartender poured a brimmer for each guest at the celebration.
She handed him a brimmer of water after his long run.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empty glass - a glass with nothing in it
  • synonym: full glass - a glass filled to the top
