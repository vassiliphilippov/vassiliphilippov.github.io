unsufferably

adverb
to an unbearable degree
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsʌfərəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsʌfərəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely difficult or impossible to endure

Examples

An unsufferably hot day
The room became unsufferably hot as the sun beat down all afternoon.
He found her arrogance unsufferably annoying during the entire meeting.
The noise from the construction site was unsufferably loud every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intolerably - in a way that is impossible to accept or endure
  • antonym: tolerably - in a way that can be endured
  • synonym: unbearably - in a way that cannot be tolerated
