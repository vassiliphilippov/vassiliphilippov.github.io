congressperson

noun
member of Congress
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡrɛspɜːrsən /; / ˈkɑŋɡrɛsˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡrɛspɜːsən /; / ˈkɒŋɡrɛsˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
