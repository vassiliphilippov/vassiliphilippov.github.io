lawfulness

noun
quality of conforming to law
4.7 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…îf(…ô)ln…ôs / (AmE), / Ààl…îÀêf(…ô)ln…ôs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being in accordance with the law; legality

Examples

The lawfulness of actions
Public trust in institutions is strengthened by the lawfulness of government decisions.
The lawyer emphasized the lawfulness of her client's actions in court.
We must question the lawfulness of such surveillance measures.

Synonyms

  • legality
    4.1 3.03/m
    legality is the formal legal status used in law and official documents, while lawfulness describes acting in line with the law.
  • licitness
    9.9 0.00/m
    licitness denotes strict conformity with legal rules in formal discussion, while lawfulness is the general quality of following the law.
