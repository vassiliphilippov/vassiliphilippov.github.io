cake

noun
small, flat mass of ground or chopped food
1.0
Pronunciation: / keɪk / (AmE), / keɪk / (BrE)
A small, flat mass of chopped or ground food, often shaped and cooked to be eaten as a single serving.

Examples

A fish cake
Each cake is made from finely ground fish mixed with spices.
She ordered a cake of fried potato with her meal.
The restaurant serves a delicious crab cake as an appetizer.
