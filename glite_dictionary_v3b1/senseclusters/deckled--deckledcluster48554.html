deckled

adjective
with a rough or untrimmed paper edge
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkəld / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəld / (BrE)
(of paper) having a rough or untrimmed edge, typically resembling that of handmade paper.

Examples

Deckled paper edges
Collectors appreciate books printed on deckled pages for their vintage charm.
The artist chose deckled paper to give the invitation a handcrafted appearance.
The stationery's deckled edges set it apart from standard office paper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; opposite of deckled
