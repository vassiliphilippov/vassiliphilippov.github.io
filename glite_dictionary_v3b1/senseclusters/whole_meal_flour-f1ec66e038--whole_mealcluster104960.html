whole meal flour

noun
flour from the entire wheat grain
3.6 2.9
Pronunciation: /hoʊl miːl flaʊr/ /; / /hoʊl miːl ˈflaʊ.ər/ (AmE), /həʊl miːl ˈflaʊ.ə/ (BrE)
