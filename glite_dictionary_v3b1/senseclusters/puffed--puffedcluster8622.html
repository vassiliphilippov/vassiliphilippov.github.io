puffed

adjective
gathered for a rounded, protruding shape
5.9
Pronunciation: / pʌft / (AmE), / pʌft / (BrE)
(of a part of clothing or fabric) gathered so as to form a rounded, swollen shape that sticks out.

Examples

Puffed shoulders
She chose a blouse with puffed shoulders for the vintage-inspired look.
The costume's puffed skirt created a dramatic silhouette on stage.
The dress featured puffed sleeves that added an elegant touch to her outfit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puffy - swollen or rounded in shape, often due to air or soft materials
