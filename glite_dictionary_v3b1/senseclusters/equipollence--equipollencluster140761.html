equipollence

noun
equality of power or value
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːləns /; / ˌiːkwəˈpoʊləns / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈpɒləns /; / ˌiːkwɪˈpɒlɪns / (BrE)
the state of being equal in power, force, value, or significance (including in geometry, when vectors have the same magnitude and direction)

Examples

In mathematics, equipollence describes when two vectors have the same length and direction.
The concept of equipollence is fundamental in vector theory and geometry.
