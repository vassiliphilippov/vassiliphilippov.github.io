uncertainty

noun
state of doubt or unpredictability
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɜrtənti / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtənti / (BrE)
the state of being unsure, in doubt, or not confident; lack of certainty or predictability

Examples

Market uncertainty

Her uncertainty about the decision was obvious from her hesitant tone.
The economic forecast is filled with uncertainty due to unpredictable market trends.
