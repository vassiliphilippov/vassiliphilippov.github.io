unsoothable

adjective
impossible to calm
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsuːðəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsuːðəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to calm or comfort, no matter what is done

Examples

An unsoothable child
No matter what she tried, her toddler remained unsoothable throughout the night.
The dog's anxiety during thunderstorms made him completely unsoothable.
After hearing the bad news, he was unsoothable for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inconsolable - unable to be comforted or consoled
  • antonym: soothable - able to be calmed or comforted
