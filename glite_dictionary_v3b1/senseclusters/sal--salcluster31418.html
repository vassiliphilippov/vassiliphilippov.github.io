sal

noun
Indian hardwood tree
9.6
Pronunciation: / sɑl / (AmE), / sɑːl / (BrE)

Examples

Sal tree wood

Dense sal forests provide important habitats for wildlife in northern India.
Villagers collected resin from the sal tree to use in traditional medicines.
