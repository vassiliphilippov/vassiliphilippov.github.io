tip

noun
extreme end of something
Pronunciation: / tɪp / (AmE), / tɪp / (BrE)
the pointed or extreme end of something, such as an object or a mountain

Examples

Tip of the pen
Be careful not to touch the tip of the knife.
The tip of the pencil broke off while I was writing.
You can see snow on the tip of the mountain from here.
Climbers finally reached the tip of the mountain after a grueling ascent.
The ice at the tip of the sculpture began to melt in the sun.
You can see the lighthouse perched on the tip of the rocky peninsula.

  • synonym: peak - the pointed top of a mountain or ridge
  • synonym: point - sharp or tapered end of something
