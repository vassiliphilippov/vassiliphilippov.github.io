torsion bar

noun
metal spring bar for vehicle suspension
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrʒən bɑr / (AmE), / ˈtɔːʃən bɑː / (BrE)
a metal bar that functions as a torsional spring, commonly used in the suspension systems of some vehicles to absorb shocks and support the chassis

Examples

The torsion bar spring
During the repair, the mechanic adjusted the torsion bar to correct the vehicle's ride height.
Many off-road vehicles rely on a torsion bar setup for their rugged suspension systems.
