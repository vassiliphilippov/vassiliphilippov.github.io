turn

verb
to change color
1.9
Pronunciation: / tɝn / (AmE), / tɜːn / (BrE)
(of a thing or surface) to change to a different color, especially as a result of aging, ripening, or exposure.

Examples

Leaves turn yellow
Every autumn, the leaves turn red, orange, and yellow before falling to the ground.
His face turned pale when he heard the shocking news.
If you leave bananas out too long, they turn brown and spotty.
