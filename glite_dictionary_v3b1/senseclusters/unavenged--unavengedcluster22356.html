unavenged

adjective
not yet avenged
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈvɛndʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈvɛn(d)ʒd / (BrE)
(of a wrongful act or harm) not yet compensated or punished by vengeance or retribution.

Examples

An unavenged wrong
He could not rest while his brother's death was still unavenged.
The crime remained unavenged for years, haunting the community.
The people demanded justice for the unavenged victims of the conflict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avenged - (of a wrongful act) for which vengeance has been taken
