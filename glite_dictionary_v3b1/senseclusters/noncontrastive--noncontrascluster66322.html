noncontrastive

adjective
not distinguishing meaning
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈstræstɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈstræstɪv / (BrE)
not serving to distinguish meaning between words or sounds in a language

Examples

Noncontrastive sounds
In Japanese, pitch is noncontrastive in some dialects and does not affect word meaning.
The linguist explained that vowel length is noncontrastive in English but contrastive in Finnish.
Aspiration of consonants is noncontrastive in French, so it does not change the word.
