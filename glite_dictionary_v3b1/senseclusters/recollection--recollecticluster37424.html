Synonyms

  • memory
    1.3 98/m
    memory holds past events or facts in the mind, while recollection names a specific memory or the act of remembering it.
  • recall
    2.8 5.35/m
    recall is the active process of bringing information to mind, while recollection refers more to a remembered image or the ability to remember.
  • reminiscence
    6.0 1.49/m
    reminiscence is a warm or shared story about the past, while recollection can mean any memory or the act of remembering.
  • remembrance
    3.9 1.26/m
    remembrance is a formal or public act of remembering people or events, while recollection is a personal memory or the power to remember.
