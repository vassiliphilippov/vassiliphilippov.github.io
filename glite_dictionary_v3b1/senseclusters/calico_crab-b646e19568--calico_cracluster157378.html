calico crab

noun
spotted crab of Atlantic US beaches
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkælɪˌkoʊ kræb / (AmE), / ˈkælɪkəʊ kræb / (BrE)
a brightly spotted crab found on sandy beaches along the Atlantic coast of the United States, known for its mottled shell
