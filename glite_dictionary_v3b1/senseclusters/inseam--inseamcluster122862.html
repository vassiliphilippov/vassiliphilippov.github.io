inseam

noun
pant leg measurement
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsiːm / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌsiːm / (BrE)
the length of the inner seam of pants, measured from the crotch to the bottom hem

Examples

Inseam length
The store assistant asked for my inseam to find the right size of jeans.
If your inseam is too short, the pants may look awkward when you sit down.
He checked the inseam measurement before ordering custom-made trousers online.
Before buying new jeans, it is important to know your inseam for the best fit.
The tailor asked for my inseam measurement to adjust the length of the trousers.
He checked the inseam on the label to make sure the pants were long enough.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outseam - the length of the outer seam of pants from the waistband to the hem
