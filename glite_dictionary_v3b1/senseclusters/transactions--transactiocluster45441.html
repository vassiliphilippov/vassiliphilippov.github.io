transactions

noun
written record of meeting proceedings
5.7
Pronunciation: / trænˈzækʃənz / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃənz / (BrE)
a written record or published account detailing the proceedings or activities that took place at a meeting or within an organization, often used as the title of academic society publications.

Examples

The transactions report
Researchers often refer to the transactions of professional organizations when citing key scientific discoveries.
The annual transactions of the society include detailed reports from both the chairman and the treasurer.
The secretary compiled the transactions to provide an official record of the meeting's outcomes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minutes - a summarized written record of what was said and decided at a meeting
