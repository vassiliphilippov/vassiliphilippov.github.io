Agapanthus

noun
genus of flowering plants (African lilies)
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡəˈpænθəs / (AmE), / ˌaɡəˈpanθəs / (BrE)
a genus of South African evergreen or deciduous flowering plants, sometimes known as African lilies, often grown as ornamentals.
