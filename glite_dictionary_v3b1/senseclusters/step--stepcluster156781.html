step

noun
distance of a single stride
Pronunciation: / stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp / (BrE)
the distance covered in a single step, or a very short distance

Examples

A long step
A typical step for an adult is about 75 centimeters in length.
He measured the garden by counting each step from the fence to the shed.
She marked the trail with a step of exactly one yard each time.
It's just a step down the hall to the conference room.
She lives only a step away from the train station, making her commute very short.
The beach is just a step from our hotel, so we can easily walk there.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pace - a single step taken when walking; also a measure of distance
