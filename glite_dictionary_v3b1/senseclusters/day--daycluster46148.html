day

noun
period of Earth's rotation relative to a star
3.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /deɪ/ (AmE), /deɪ/ (BrE)
a period of 24 hours, especially from midnight to midnight, or the time it takes a planet to make one complete rotation on its axis
