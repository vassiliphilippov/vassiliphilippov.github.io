ogive

noun
pointed arch; cumulative graph
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdʒaɪv / (AmE), / ˈəʊdʒaɪv / (BrE)
a pointed arch in architecture or a graph showing cumulative frequency in statistics

Examples

Gothic ogive
The medieval cathedral features a beautiful ogive above its main entrance.
In statistics class, we learned how to draw an ogive to represent cumulative data.
The window's ogive shape is typical of Gothic architecture.
