abdominal nerve plexus

noun
nerve network in the abdomen
3.7
Pronunciation: / əbˈdɑmɪnəl nɜrv ˈplɛksəs / (AmE), / əbˈdɒmɪnəl nɜːv ˈplɛksəs / (BrE)
a large network of sympathetic nerves located in the abdomen, situated behind the stomach

Examples

The abdominal nerve plexus
Injury to the abdominal nerve plexus can lead to severe digestive issues.
Surgeons must exercise caution to avoid damaging the abdominal nerve plexus during abdominal operations.
The abdominal nerve plexus plays a crucial role in controlling abdominal organs.
