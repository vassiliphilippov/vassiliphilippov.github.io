concert

verb
to plan or arrange together
6.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝːt / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːt / (BrE)
to arrange or plan (something) by mutual agreement with others; to act together in a coordinated way

Examples

To concert a plan
He concerted with his allies to issue a joint statement.
Several nations concerted their efforts in response to the crisis.
The two companies concerted a strategy to enter the new market together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coordinate - to arrange or organize something so that its parts work together effectively
