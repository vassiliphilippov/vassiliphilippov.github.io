Labrador

proper noun
dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈlæbrədɔr / (AmE), / ˈlæbrədɔː / (BrE)
a breed of large, friendly dog known for intelligence, loyalty, and a gentle temperament

Examples

Labrador puppy
Their family adopted a playful Labrador from the animal shelter last year.
A well-trained Labrador can make an excellent guide dog for people with visual impairments.
The children laughed as their Labrador splashed in the lake during summer vacation.
