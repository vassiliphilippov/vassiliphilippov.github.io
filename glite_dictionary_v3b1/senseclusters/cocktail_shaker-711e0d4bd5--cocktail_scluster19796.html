cocktail shaker

noun
container for mixing cocktails
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑk.teɪl ˌʃeɪ.kər / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl ˌʃeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a container with a tight-fitting lid used for mixing cocktails by shaking the ingredients together with ice
