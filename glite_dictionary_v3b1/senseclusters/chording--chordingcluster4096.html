chording

noun
key combination input
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the method of entering characters by pressing combinations of keys at the same time on a special keyboard

Examples

Chording keyboard input
Many stenographers use chording to quickly record spoken words during court sessions.
Some computer keyboards are designed for chording to help users type with fewer keys.
Learning chording can greatly increase your typing speed on a chorded keyboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stenography - the practice of writing in shorthand, often using a special machine
  • synonym: typing - the act of inputting text using a keyboard
