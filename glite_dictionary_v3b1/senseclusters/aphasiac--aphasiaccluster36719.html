aphasiac

noun
person with language loss
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈfeɪziæk / (AmE), / əˈfeɪziæk / (BrE)
a person who has lost the ability to use or understand language due to brain injury or disease

Examples

An aphasiac patient
The aphasiac struggled to find the right words during the conversation.
After the accident, the hospital provided special support for each aphasiac in recovery.
Family members learned new ways to communicate with their loved one who became an aphasiac.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphasic - a person affected by aphasia
  • synonym: stroke patient - a person who has suffered a stroke
