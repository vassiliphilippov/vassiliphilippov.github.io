ticker

noun
stock symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkər / (AmE), / ˈtɪkə / (BrE)
a short code or symbol used to identify a stock or security on a financial exchange

Examples

Ticker symbol TSLA
The ticker for Tesla is TSLA on the NASDAQ exchange.
Investors often search for a company's ticker before buying shares.
Each stock has a unique ticker that appears on financial news sites.
The ticker for Alphabet Inc. is GOOGL.
Investors often search for a company's ticker before buying shares online.
Each stock on the exchange is listed under a unique ticker.

  • synonym: security - a financial asset such as a stock or bond
  • synonym: stock symbol - a unique code representing a publicly traded company
  • synonym: symbol - a letter or group of letters representing a stock on an exchange
