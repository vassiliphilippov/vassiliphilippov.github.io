malignant neoplastic disease

noun
malignant tumor or cancer
8.9 5.9
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪɡ.nənt niː.oʊˈplæstɪk dɪˈziz / (AmE), / məˈlɪɡ.nənt niː.əʊˈplæstɪk dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease characterized by a malignant tumor or growth that arises from uncontrolled and abnormal cell division, potentially spreading to other body parts via the lymphatic system or bloodstream.
