hypodermal

adjective
relating to the hypodermis, under the skin
8.1 6.0
Pronunciation: / haɪpəˈdɝːməl / (AmE), / haɪpəˈdɜːməl / (BrE)
relating to or situated beneath the outer layer of the skin (hypodermis), especially in anatomical or biological contexts.

Examples

Hypodermal skin layer
Botanists observed a hypodermal layer in the plant stem beneath the outer covering.
Some medications are delivered through hypodermal injections for quicker absorption.
The hypodermal tissue contains fat cells that provide insulation for the body.
