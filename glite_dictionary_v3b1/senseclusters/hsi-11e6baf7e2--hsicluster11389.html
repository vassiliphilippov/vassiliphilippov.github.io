HSI

noun
hyperspectral imaging
8.5
Pronunciation: / eɪtʃ ɛs aɪ / (AmE), / eɪtʃ ɛs aɪ / (BrE)
a remote-sensing technique called hyperspectral imaging that captures many narrow spectral bands to identify materials or detect conditions invisible in standard images

Examples

HSI analysis of rocks
Scientists used HSI to analyze the mineral composition of the rock samples from the field.
With HSI, researchers can detect crop diseases before symptoms appear to the naked eye.
The satellite's HSI system provided detailed data for environmental monitoring projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multispectral imaging - a technique capturing images in several broad spectral bands
  • synonym: remote sensing - the use of satellite or aerial sensor technologies to detect and classify objects on Earth
