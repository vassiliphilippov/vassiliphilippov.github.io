soft-shelled crab

noun
edible crab recently molted, with a soft shell
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑft ˌʃɛld kræb /; / ˈsɔft ʃɛld kræb / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌʃɛld krab /; / ˈsɒft ʃɛld kræb / (BrE)
