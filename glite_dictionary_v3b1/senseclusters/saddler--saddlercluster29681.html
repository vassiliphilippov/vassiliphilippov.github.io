saddler

noun
maker or seller of horse saddles
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈsæd.lə / (BrE)
a person who makes, repairs, or sells saddles and other riding equipment for horses

Examples

A saddler repairs saddles
A saddler is essential for maintaining the quality and safety of riding equipment.
After years of experience, she became an expert saddler with her own workshop.
The village saddler crafted beautiful leather bridles and saddles by hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saddlery - equipment and accessories for horse riding
