clammy

adjective
unpleasantly damp and cool
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmi / (AmE), / ˈklæmi / (BrE)
unpleasantly damp, sticky, and typically cool to the touch

Examples

Clammy hands
After running in the rain, his hands felt clammy when he shook mine.
She woke up with a clammy feeling on her skin after the humid night.
The walls of the cave were cold and clammy to the touch.
