stigmatic

adjective
relating to or resembling stigmata
Pronunciation: / stɪgˈmætɪk / (AmE), / stɪgˈmatɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling stigmata; having marks or characteristics similar to stigmata.

Examples

Stigmatic wounds
In the Middle Ages, individuals believed to be stigmatic often attracted both admiration and suspicion.
Some saints are described as stigmatic, displaying mysterious wounds similar to those of Christ.
The stigmatic appearance of her hands drew the attention of the local church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stigmatized - marked by stigma or social disapproval
