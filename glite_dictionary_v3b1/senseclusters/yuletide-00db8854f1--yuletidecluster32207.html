Yuletide

noun
the Christmas season
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːl.taɪd / (AmE), / ˈjuːl.taɪd / (BrE)
The Christmas season, especially the period from December 24 to January 6.

Examples

The Yuletide season
During Yuletide, people exchange gifts and enjoy festive meals.
Families gather from far and wide to celebrate Yuletide together.
The town square was beautifully decorated for Yuletide festivities.
