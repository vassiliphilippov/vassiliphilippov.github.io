peludo

noun
South American armadillo species
9.5
Pronunciation: / peˈluːdoʊ / (AmE), / pɛˈluːdəʊ / (BrE)
A South American armadillo (Chaetophractus villosus) characterized by six movable bands and hairy underparts, native to Argentina and neighboring regions.

Examples

The peludo armadillo
Scientists in Argentina are studying the habitat range of the peludo.
The peludo is distinguished by its thick fur and flexible armor bands.
Unlike other armadillos, the peludo can be quite hairy underneath its shell.

Related Terms
