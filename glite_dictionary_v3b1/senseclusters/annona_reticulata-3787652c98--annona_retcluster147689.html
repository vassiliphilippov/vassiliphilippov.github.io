Annona reticulata

noun
tropical tree with heart-shaped fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnoʊnə ˌrɛtɪkjuːˈleɪtə / (AmE), / əˈnəʊnə ˌrɛtɪkjʊˈlɑːtə / (BrE)
