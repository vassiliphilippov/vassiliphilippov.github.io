chink

verb
make cracks or fissures
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
to make cracks or narrow openings in something

Examples

To chink the wood
Prolonged exposure to sunlight can chink the wooden beams.
Repeated freezing and thawing may chink the concrete over time.
The painters were careful not to chink the fresh plaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crack - to break or fracture without complete separation
