gauze

noun
thin fabric used in bandages
5.2 3.0
Pronunciation: /gɔːz/ (AmE), /gɔːz/ (BrE)
a thin, transparent fabric with a loose open weave, often used for bandages, dressings, or other purposes

Examples

Sterile gauze pad
Always keep some sterile gauze in your first-aid kit.
He wrapped a piece of gauze around his finger to stop the bleeding.
The nurse carefully replaced the old gauze with a fresh, sterile one.
He bought several meters of white gauze for his art project.
The gauze curtains allowed sunlight to filter softly into the room.
The nurse carefully placed a piece of gauze over the wound to keep it clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to bind a wound or protect an injured part of the body
  • synonym: muslin - a lightweight cotton cloth with a plain weave, often used for dressmaking or curtains
