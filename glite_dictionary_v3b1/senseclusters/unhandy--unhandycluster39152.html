unhandy

adjective
physically clumsy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhændi / (AmE), / ʌnˈhændi / (BrE)
physically awkward or clumsy, especially in handling objects or performing tasks with the hands

Examples

An unhandy person
He was so unhandy that he struggled to assemble even simple furniture.
Because she is unhandy, she prefers to hire someone for home repairs.
The unhandy student accidentally spilled paint all over the floor during art class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkward - lacking skill or grace in movement
  • synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • antonym: handy - skillful or useful with the hands
