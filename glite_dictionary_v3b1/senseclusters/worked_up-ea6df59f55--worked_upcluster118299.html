worked up

adjective
overly agitated or emotional
6.5 0.72/m
Pronunciation: / w…ùkt  åp / (AmE), / w…úÀêkt  åp / (BrE)
Very upset, excited, or agitated, especially due to strong emotions such as anger, worry, or excitement.

Examples

To be worked up
He was so worked up after the meeting that he couldn't sleep.
She tends to become worked up over small problems.
Try not to get too worked up about the test results.

  • wound up
    5.2 0.54/m

  • agitated
    4.1 2.20/m

  • stirred up
    4.4 0.01/m

  • keyed up
    6.9 0.13/m

  • excited
