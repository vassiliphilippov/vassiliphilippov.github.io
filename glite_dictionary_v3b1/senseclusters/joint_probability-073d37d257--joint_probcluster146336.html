joint probability

noun
probability two events occur together
Pronunciation: / dʒɔɪnt prɑːbəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dʒɔɪnt prɒbəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the probability of two events happening at the same time or together; a statistical measure of the likelihood that both events occur.

Examples

The joint probability of events
The joint probability of rolling two sixes with a pair of dice is 1/36.
Understanding joint probability is essential for analyzing the relationship between two random variables.
We used a chart to visualize the joint probability of rain and high winds occurring on the same day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conditional probability - the probability of an event occurring given that another event has occurred
