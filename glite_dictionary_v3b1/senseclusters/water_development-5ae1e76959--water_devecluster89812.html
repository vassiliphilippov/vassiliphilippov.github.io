water development

noun
improving use of water resources
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ dɪˌvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə dɪˌvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
The process of improving or managing the use of water resources in an area to make them more beneficial or effective.

Examples

Effective water development projects
Effective water development projects can transform arid regions into productive farmland.
International aid organizations often support water development initiatives in drought-prone communities.
The government has invested heavily in water development to ensure access to clean drinking water in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irrigation - the artificial application of water to land to assist in crop production
  • synonym: water management - the activity of planning, developing, distributing, and managing the optimum use of water resources
