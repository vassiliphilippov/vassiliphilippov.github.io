skeptically

adverb
in a doubtful or skeptical manner
7.3 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛptɪkli / (AmE), / ˈskɛptɪkli /; / ˈskɛptɪkəli / (BrE)
In a manner showing doubt or disbelief; with skepticism.

Examples

To listen skeptically
He listened skeptically as the salesman made his pitch about the miracle product.

The proposal was received skeptically by many members of the committee.
He listened sceptically to their claims about the miracle cure.
She glanced sceptically at the proposal, unsure if it would succeed.
The audience reacted sceptically when the magician announced his next trick.
