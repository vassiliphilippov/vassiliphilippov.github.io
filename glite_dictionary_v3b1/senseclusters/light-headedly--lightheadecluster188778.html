light-headedly

adverb
in a giddy or dizzy manner
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪtˈhɛdɪdli / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈhɛdɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows dizziness or a giddy feeling, as if feeling faint or unsteady.

Examples

Walk light-headedly
After the roller coaster ride, they walked light-headedly toward the exit.
He danced light-headedly around the room, laughing uncontrollably.
She stood up light-headedly after sitting for too long.
