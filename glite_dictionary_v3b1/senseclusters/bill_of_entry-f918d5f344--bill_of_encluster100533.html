bill of entry

noun
official customs document listing imported/exported goods
3.6
Pronunciation: / bɪl əv ˈɛntri / (AmE), / bɪl əv ˈɛntri / (BrE)
an official document listing goods being imported or exported, submitted to a customs house for clearance

Examples

A bill of entry document
Every consignment must be accompanied by a bill of entry to ensure proper documentation.
The customs official reviewed the bill of entry before allowing the shipment into the country.
The importer submitted a bill of entry listing all the goods and their values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill of lading - official document listing details about goods shipped
