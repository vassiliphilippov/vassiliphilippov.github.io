snigger

noun
a quiet, disrespectful laugh
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈsnɪɡə / (BrE)
a quiet or disrespectful laugh, especially one expressing scorn or amusement at someone

Examples

A quiet snigger
A snigger escaped from the group as the speaker made a mistake.
He tried to hide his snigger when the teacher stumbled on the words.
Her rude comment drew a loud snigger from the back of the classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snicker - a suppressed or disrespectful laugh; chiefly American English
