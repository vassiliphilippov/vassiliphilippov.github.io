Yuchi

proper noun
Taiwanese township
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈjuːtʃi / (BrE)
a township in Nantou County, central Taiwan, known for its proximity to Sun Moon Lake and as a tourist destination

Examples

Yuchi Township
Many tourists visit Yuchi each year to enjoy the beautiful scenery of Sun Moon Lake.
The local markets in Yuchi offer a variety of traditional Taiwanese foods and crafts.
We took a boat tour from Yuchi to explore the islands on Sun Moon Lake.
