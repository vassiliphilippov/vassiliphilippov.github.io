puritanically

adverb
in a strict or prudish manner
Pronunciation: / ˌpjʊrɪˈtænɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpjʊərəˈtænɪkli / (BrE)
In a very strict, moral, or prudishly proper manner, especially regarding sexual conduct or pleasure.

Examples

Raised puritanically strict
His parents raised him puritanically, forbidding even the smallest indulgence.
She spoke puritanically about the importance of modest dress in society.
The new policy was enforced puritanically, leaving no room for flexibility or enjoyment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prudishly - in a manner that is excessively proper or modest, especially about sex
