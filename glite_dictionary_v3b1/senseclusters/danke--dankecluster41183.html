danke

x
German for thank you
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑŋkə / (AmE), / ˈdaŋkə / (BrE)
an informal way to say 'thank you', borrowed from German and sometimes used in English conversation

Examples

Say danke politely
After receiving her coffee, she smiled and said danke to the barista.
He waved goodbye and called out danke as he left the party.
When someone held the door open for him, he replied with a cheerful danke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thank you - a polite expression used when acknowledging a gift or service
  • synonym: thanks - an expression of gratitude
