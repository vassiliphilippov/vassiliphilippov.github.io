subacute bacterial endocarditis

noun
chronic infection of heart lining and valves
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌb.əˈkjuːt bækˈtɪr.i.əl ˌɛn.doʊ.kɑːrˈdaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌsʌb.əˈkjuːt bækˈtɪə.ri.əl ˌɛn.dəʊ.kɑːˈdaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
a chronic bacterial infection of the inner lining of the heart and its valves, with symptoms that develop gradually

Examples
