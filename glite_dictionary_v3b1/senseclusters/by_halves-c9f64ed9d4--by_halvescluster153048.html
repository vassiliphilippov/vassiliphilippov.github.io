by halves

adverb
partially; incompletely
Pronunciation: / baɪ ˈhævz / (AmE), / baɪ ˈhɑːvz / (BrE)
in an incomplete or partial way; not thoroughly or wholeheartedly.

Examples

Work by halves
Don't approach your training by halves if you want real results.
She never does things by halves; she always gives her best effort.
When he started the project, he promised not to do it by halves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: half-heartedly - without enthusiasm or commitment
  • antonym: thoroughly - completely and with great attention to detail
