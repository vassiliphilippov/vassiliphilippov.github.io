Mt Orizaba

proper noun
the highest mountain in Mexico
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ɔːrɪˈzɑːbə / (AmE), / maʊnt ɒrɪˈzɑːbə / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in southern Mexico, located between Mexico City and Veracruz; the highest peak in Mexico at 18,695 feet (5,696 meters)

Examples

Mt Orizaba summit
Many climbers attempt to reach the summit of Mt Orizaba each year.
Scientists have studied the geology of Mt Orizaba to learn more about extinct volcanoes.
The snow-capped peak of Mt Orizaba is visible from hundreds of miles away on a clear day.
