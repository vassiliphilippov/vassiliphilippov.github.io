multispeed

noun
device with multiple gears
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌspiːd / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌspiːd / (BrE)
a device, vehicle, or machine that has more than one speed or gear setting

Examples

A multispeed bike
He bought a new multispeed to help him ride up steep hills more easily.
The mechanic recommended a multispeed for better performance on different terrains.
Many cyclists prefer a multispeed because it offers more flexibility during long rides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geared - having gears that allow for different speeds
  • antonym: single-speed - a device or vehicle with only one speed or gear
