springlike

adjective
resembling spring
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics typical of spring, such as mild weather or freshness

Examples

Springlike weather
The air felt springlike with its gentle breeze and blooming flowers.
After days of rain, the sun brought a springlike warmth to the city.
Her mood was as springlike as the bright, clear morning outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vernal - relating to or characteristic of spring
  • antonym: wintry - characteristic of winter; cold or bleak
