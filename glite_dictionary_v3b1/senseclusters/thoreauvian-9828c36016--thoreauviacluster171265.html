Thoreauvian

adjective
relating to Henry David Thoreau or his ideas
Pronunciation: / θɔːˈroʊviən / (AmE), / θɔːˈrəʊviən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Henry David Thoreau, his works, ideas, or philosophy, especially concerning simplicity, nature, and individualism.

Examples

Thoreauvian lifestyle
Her Thoreauvian lifestyle reflects a deep commitment to simplicity and connection with nature.
Many modern environmentalists are inspired by Thoreauvian principles in their advocacy.
The writer adopted a Thoreauvian perspective in his essay on solitude and self-reliance.
