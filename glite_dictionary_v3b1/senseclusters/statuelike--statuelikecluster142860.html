statuelike

adjective
calm and dignified
Pronunciation: / ˈstætʃuˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstætʃuːˌlaɪk /; / ˈstætʃuˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance, stillness, or dignity of a statue

Examples

Statuelike stillness
She stood in the doorway with a statuelike grace that caught everyone's attention.
His statuelike composure made him seem unshakable during the tense meeting.
The model held a statuelike pose for the artist, barely moving for hours.
The model remained statuelike as the artist painted her portrait.
He stood statuelike in the garden, lost in thought.
The children were so still and statuelike during the game that no one could tell they were there.

Related Terms

  • antonym: animated - full of movement or lively
  • synonym: majestic - having grandeur or dignity
  • synonym: regal - resembling a king or queen; dignified
  • synonym: statuesque - tall, graceful, and attractive like a statue
