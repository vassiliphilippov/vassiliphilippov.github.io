black mamba

noun
highly venomous African snake
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈmæmbə / (AmE), / blæk ˈmambə / (BrE)
a large, highly venomous snake of southern and eastern Africa, known for its speed and potent bite

Examples

A black mamba snake
Despite its name, the black mamba is usually olive or grayish-brown in color.
The black mamba is feared for its aggressive behavior and deadly venom.
We saw a documentary about the speed and danger of the black mamba.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mamba - any of several highly venomous African snakes
