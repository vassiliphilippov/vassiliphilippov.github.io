archesporial

adjective
relating to spore-forming cells in a sporangium
Pronunciation: / ˌɑrk.ɪˈspɔr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌɑː.kɪˈspɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the cells in a sporangium that give rise to spores

Examples

Archesporial cells
Researchers studied the development of archesporial tissue in various plant species.
The archesporial cells divide repeatedly to produce the spores within the sporangium.
The archesporial layer is essential for the formation of fertile spores in ferns.
