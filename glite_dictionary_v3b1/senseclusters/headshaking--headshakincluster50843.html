headshaking

noun
side-to-side head movement to indicate denial or disbelief
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.ʃeɪ.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhed.ʃeɪ.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
