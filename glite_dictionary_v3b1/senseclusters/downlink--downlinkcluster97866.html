downlink

verb
to transmit data
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌlɪŋk / (BrE)
to send or transmit data from a satellite or central transmitter to a receiving device or ground station

Examples

To downlink data
The satellite will downlink images to the ground station every hour.
Engineers need to downlink the latest software updates to all remote devices.
During the test, the system failed to downlink the necessary data to the user terminal.
The satellite will downlink the weather data to the ground station every hour.
Engineers monitored as the probe downlinked high-resolution images of the planet's surface.
After the experiment, all results were downlinked for immediate analysis by the research team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transmit - to send data or signals from one place to another
  • antonym: uplink - to send data from a user device to a central network node
