tinted

adjective
slightly colored or influenced
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪn.tɪd/ /; / /ˈtɪn.təd/ (AmE), /ˈtɪn.tɪd/ /; / /ˈtɪn.təd/ (BrE)
having a small or slight amount of color added, often to make something less transparent or to change its appearance slightly

Examples
