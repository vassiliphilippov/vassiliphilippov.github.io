tinted

adjective
slightly colored or influenced
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪntɪd / (BrE)
having a small or slight amount of color added, often to make something less transparent or to change its appearance slightly

Examples

Tinted windows
The windows were tinted to reduce glare from the sun.
Her voice was tinted with sadness as she spoke about the past.
The painting is tinted with warm shades of orange and red.
The car has tinted windows to block sunlight.
She wore tinted glasses to protect her eyes from the bright light.
The artist preferred using tinted paper for her sketches.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - without any color or tint
  • synonym: colored - having color added
  • antonym: pure - not mixed with any other color or quality
  • synonym: tinged - having a small amount of a color or quality added
