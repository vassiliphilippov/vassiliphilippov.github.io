handwritten

adjective
written by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌrɪtən / (AmE), / ˈhændˌrɪt(ə)n / (BrE)
written by hand rather than printed or typed

Examples

Handwritten document
Please submit your essay as a handwritten document, not a typed one.
She cherished the handwritten letter she received from her grandmother.
The invitation was handwritten in beautiful calligraphy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: printed - produced using a printing press or printer
  • antonym: typed - produced using a typewriter or computer keyboard
