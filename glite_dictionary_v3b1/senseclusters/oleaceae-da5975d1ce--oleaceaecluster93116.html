Oleaceae

noun
a plant family including olive, ash, and jasmine
9.9
Pronunciation: /oʊ.li.ˈeɪ.siː/ /; / /ə.li.ˈeɪ.siː/ (AmE), /əʊ.li.ˈeɪ.siː/ /; / /ə.li.ˈeɪ.siː/ (BrE)
a family of flowering plants that includes trees and shrubs such as the olive, ash, jasmine, privet, and lilac, typically bearing berries, drupes, or capsules as fruits

Examples

Oleaceae plant family

Lilacs and olives are both members of the Oleaceae.
The Oleaceae family contains many popular ornamental and fruit-bearing plants.
