progressively

adverb
increasing or developing gradually
4.1
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪvli / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that increases or develops gradually over time.

Examples

Increasing progressively
Her responsibilities at work increased progressively as she gained experience.
The symptoms worsened progressively over the course of several weeks.
The temperature in the room dropped progressively as night fell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gradually - in a slow, step-by-step manner without sudden changes
  • antonym: suddenly - quickly and unexpectedly, without warning
