EEC

noun
Thailand's Eastern Economic Corridor
Pronunciation: / iː iː siː / (AmE), / iː iː siː / (BrE)
(in Thailand) abbreviation for Eastern Economic Corridor, a government development program and special economic zone in the eastern provinces designed to attract investment and promote industrial growth.

Examples

Thai EEC project
The Thai government announced incentives under the EEC plan to boost eastern growth.
Several foreign manufacturers have set up factories inside the EEC to access better logistics and ports.
Investors praised tax benefits offered in the EEC, saying the zone will speed regional industrial development.
