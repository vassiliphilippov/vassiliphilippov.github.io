pons Varolii

noun
part of the brainstem linking medulla, cerebellum, and midbrain
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑnz vəˈroʊli.aɪ / (AmE), / pɒnz vəˈrəʊlɪ.aɪ / (BrE)
a broad band of nerve fibers in the brainstem that links the medulla oblongata and the cerebellum with the midbrain
