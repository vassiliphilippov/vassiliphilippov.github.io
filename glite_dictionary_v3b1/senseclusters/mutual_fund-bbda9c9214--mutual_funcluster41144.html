mutual fund

noun
investment fund pooling investors' money
3.5 4.30/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêt Éu…ôl f ånd /; / ÀàmjuÀê.t Éu.…ôl f ånd / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt Éu…ôl f ånd /; / ÀàmjuÀê.tju.…ôl f ånd / (BrE)
an investment fund that pools money from many investors and is managed by professionals, investing in a variety of securities

Examples

A mutual fund investment
Many people choose mutual funds for their retirement accounts.
She invested her savings in a mutual fund to diversify her portfolio.
The mutual fund saw steady growth over the past five years.
Many people choose a mutual fund for its professional management and lower risk.
She invested most of her savings in a mutual fund to diversify her portfolio.
The value of a mutual fund can fluctuate depending on the market performance of its assets.

Synonyms

  • open-end investment company
