animal tissue

noun
biological tissue found in animals
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl ˈtɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈtɪsjuː / (BrE)
the tissue that forms the organs and other body parts of animals, composed of cells and their products

Examples

Animal tissue sample
Scientists examine animal tissue samples under a microscope to study cell structure.
The disease affects animal tissue and can spread rapidly if untreated.
Veterinarians often analyze animal tissue to diagnose infections in livestock.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plant tissue - the tissue in the bodies of plants
  • synonym: tissue - an aggregate of similar cells and cell products forming a definite kind of structural material
