metric system

noun
decimal system of measurement
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrɪk ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪk ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A decimal system of measurement based on the meter, kilogram, and second, used worldwide.

Examples

Most countries have adopted the metric system for their official measurements.
The United States still relies largely on customary units rather than the metric system.
