sustainable living

noun
lifestyle aimed at lowering environmental impact
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / səˈsteɪnəbəl ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnəbl ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
a lifestyle that seeks to reduce an individual's or society's use of Earth's natural resources and personal resources by making environmentally conscious choices

Examples

Sustainable living practices
She decided to embrace sustainable living, reducing waste and conserving water at home.
The community prides itself on practicing sustainable living through renewable energy and local food sourcing.
Workshops on sustainable living are offered to help people lower their carbon footprint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: green living - choosing a lifestyle that is environmentally friendly
  • synonym: sustainability - the ability to maintain ecological balance without depleting resources
