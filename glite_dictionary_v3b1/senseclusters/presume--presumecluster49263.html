to act with unwarranted confidence or take liberties without permission

Examples

To presume permission
Don't presume that you're welcome to join the meeting without an invitation.
He tends to presume on our hospitality without asking.
I wouldn't presume to tell you how to do your job.
He presumed to enter her office without knocking, which annoyed her.
I would not presume to speak for the whole group without their consent.
You presume too much when you make decisions for others unasked.

Synonyms
