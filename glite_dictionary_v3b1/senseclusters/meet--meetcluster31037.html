meet

verb
compete against an opponent
2.5
Pronunciation: / mit / (AmE), / miːt / (BrE)
to compete against an opponent in a sporting event, game, or contest

Examples

To meet a rival team
England and France met in the semifinals of the tournament.
He was excited to meet his longtime rival on the tennis court.
The two teams will meet in the championship game next month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to stay away from or not compete with an opponent
  • synonym: face - to confront or oppose, especially in a competition or challenge
  • synonym: play - to participate against another in a game or sport
