chukka

noun
ankle-high suede/leather boot or shoe
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkə / (BrE)
An ankle-high boot or shoe, typically made of suede or leather, laced through two or three pairs of eyelets.

Examples

Chukka boots
He wore a pair of brown suede chukkas to the office with his jeans.
Leather chukkas are a versatile choice for both casual and semi-formal outfits.
These chukkas are both stylish and comfortable for everyday wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desert boot - a type of chukka boot with a crepe sole, originally worn by British soldiers in the desert
