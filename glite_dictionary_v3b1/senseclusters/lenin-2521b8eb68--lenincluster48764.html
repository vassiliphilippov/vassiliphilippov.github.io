Lenin

proper noun
Russian revolutionary leader (1870–1924)
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈle.nɪn/ /; / /ˈle.nən/ (AmE), /ˈle.nɪn/ /; / /ˈle.nən/ (BrE)
