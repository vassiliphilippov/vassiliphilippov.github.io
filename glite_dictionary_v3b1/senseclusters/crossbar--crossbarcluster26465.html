crossbar

noun
horizontal bar to be jumped or vaulted over in sports
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌbɑː / (BrE)
a horizontal bar used in sports that athletes must jump or vault over, especially in high jump or pole vault events

Examples

High jump crossbar
If you touch the crossbar during your jump, the attempt does not count.
Officials raised the crossbar to a new height after each successful jump.
The athlete cleared the crossbar with ease during the pole vault competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a long, rigid piece of metal or wood
