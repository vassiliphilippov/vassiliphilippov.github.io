uncollectable

adjective
not worth collecting
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or desirable to be collected, especially by collectors.

Examples

Uncollectable stamp
Because of its poor condition, the stamp was considered uncollectable by most enthusiasts.
Many mass-produced items are simply uncollectable due to their lack of rarity or value.
The damaged vase was deemed uncollectable at the antique fair.
