Examples

Dominating role
Her dominating presence in the debate made her arguments hard to ignore.
That match was the dominating event of the entire tournament.
The company has maintained a dominating position in the smartphone market for years.

Synonyms

  • dominant
    4.1 0.11/m
    dominant describes someone or something with the most power, while dominating often focuses on the active process of keeping control.
  • preponderant
    8.7 0.17/m
    preponderant is a formal word for having greater influence, while dominating is more general and used in everyday contexts.
  • hegemonic
    9.1 2.68/m
    hegemonic describes wide cultural or political control, while dominating is a general word for having most power in any situation.
  • reigning
    4.9 0.01/m
    reigning means currently holding power or a title, while dominating describes having the most influence in a group or situation.
  • ascendant
    7.9 0.48/m
    ascendant suggests rising influence or growing importance, while dominating shows already having the most power or influence.
  • ascendent
    7.9 0.00/m
    ascendent is an alternative form used to show rising or current influence, while dominating focuses on holding the most power in a situation.
