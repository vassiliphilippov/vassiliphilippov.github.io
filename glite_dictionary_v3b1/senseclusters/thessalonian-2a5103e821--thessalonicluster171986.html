Thessalonian

noun
a person from Thessalonica
Pronunciation: / ˌθɛsəˈloʊniən / (AmE), / ˌθɛsəˈləʊniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Thessalonica, an ancient city in Macedonia

Examples

A Thessalonian citizen
As a proud Thessalonian, he often spoke about his city's history.
Paul wrote two letters to the Thessalonians in the New Testament.
The ancient city attracted many Thessalonians searching for trade opportunities.
