sweet fern

noun
aromatic shrub with fern-like leaves
2.9
Pronunciation: / swit fɝn / (AmE), / swiːt fɜːn / (BrE)
a low, shrubby plant with sweet-scented, fern-like leaves, native to North America (not a true fern)
