becoming

noun
process of change
5.3
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of changing or developing into something different or new

Examples

The becoming of a leader
The artist's work explores the becoming of identity through different stages of life.
In philosophy, the concept of becoming is often contrasted with being.
The city's rapid becoming into a technology hub surprised many residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evolution - the gradual development of something
  • antonym: stagnation - a state of not changing or developing
  • synonym: transformation - a complete change in form or appearance
