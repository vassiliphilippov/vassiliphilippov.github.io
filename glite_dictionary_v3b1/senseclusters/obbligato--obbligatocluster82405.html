obbligato

noun
essential musical part in a score
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ /; / ˌɑːbliˈɡɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
