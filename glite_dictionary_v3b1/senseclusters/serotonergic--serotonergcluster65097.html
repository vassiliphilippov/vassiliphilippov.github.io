serotonergic

adjective
affecting serotonin
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛrətoʊˈnɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛrəʊtəˈnɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affecting serotonin, a neurotransmitter in the body

Examples

Serotonergic effect
The new medication has a strong serotonergic effect, which can help treat depression.
Researchers are studying how serotonergic pathways influence mood and behavior.
Some antidepressants work by increasing serotonergic activity in the brain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dopaminergic - relating to or affecting dopamine
  • synonym: serotonin - a neurotransmitter involved in mood and other functions
