verb
to become visible or to appear

Examples

Synonyms

  • appear
    1.0 86/m
    appear is used when something comes into sight or begins to be seen, while come out means to become visible.
  • emerge
    3.5 31/m
    emerge is used when something comes out from a hidden place and becomes seen, while come out means to appear.
  • surface
    3.9 10/m
    surface means to rise to the top or become visible after hiding, while come out means simply to appear.
  • come forth
    4.2 0.27/m
    come forth often suggests appearing suddenly or formally in public, while come out is more general for becoming visible.
  • come on
    2.1 2.53/m
    come on is often used for lights or a performance starting to appear, while come out means to become visible.
  • show up
    1.4 63/m
    show up is used for people arriving or appearing at a place, while come out focuses on simply becoming visible.
  • turn up
    2.0 5.52/m
    turn up often means arriving or appearing by chance, while come out simply means to become visible or appear.
  • issue
    6.7 3.37/m
    issue in formal use often means to flow out or originate from a source, while come out means to appear.
