if only

conjunction
expressing a wish or regret
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪf ˈoʊnli / (AmE), / ɪf ˈəʊnli / (BrE)
used to express a strong wish or regret about something that did or did not happen

Examples

If only I had
If only I had remembered to bring my umbrella, I wouldn't be soaked now.
He thought about the promotion and sighed, 'if only I had worked harder.'
She whispered, 'if only he knew how much I cared.'
