deicer

noun
device or substance for removing ice
7.2 4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdiˌaɪsər / (AmE), / ˈdiːˌaɪsə / (BrE)
A device or substance used to remove ice or frost from surfaces such as windshields, refrigerators, or airplane wings.

Examples

A deicer device
Make sure the deicer is working before traveling in snowy conditions.
She turned on the deicer to clear the frost from the windshield.
The plane's wings were equipped with a special deicer to prevent ice buildup.
