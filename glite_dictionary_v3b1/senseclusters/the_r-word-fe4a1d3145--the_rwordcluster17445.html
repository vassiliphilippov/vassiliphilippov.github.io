the r-word

noun
euphemism for an offensive slur
Pronunciation: / ði ˈɑr ˌwɝd / (AmE), / ði ˈɑː ˌwɜːd / (BrE)
a euphemism for a highly offensive term referring to intellectual disability; used to avoid uttering the slur itself
