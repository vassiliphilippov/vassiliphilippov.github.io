mushily

adverb
vaguely or indistinctly
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃɪli / (BrE)
in a vague, indistinct, or confused manner, lacking clear form or detail

Examples

Mushily remembers details
He remembered the event mushily, unable to recall any specific details.
The painting was mushily colored, with no sharp lines or clear shapes.
Her explanation was mushily worded, leaving everyone confused about her main point.
