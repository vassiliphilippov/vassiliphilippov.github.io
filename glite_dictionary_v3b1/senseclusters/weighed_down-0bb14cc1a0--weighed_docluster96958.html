weighed down

adjective
burdened with weight or responsibility
1.8 3.7
Pronunciation: / weɪd daʊn / (AmE), / weɪd daʊn / (BrE)
burdened or oppressed by something heavy, either physically (carrying weight) or figuratively (worries, responsibilities, etc.)

Examples

Weighed down with books
His backpack was weighed down with books and supplies for his classes.
She felt weighed down by the pressure to succeed at work.
The old branches were weighed down with snow after the storm.
After losing his job, he seemed weighed down with worry and uncertainty about the future.
She felt weighed down by the pressure of her deadlines at work.
The backpack was so heavy that he walked slowly, weighed down by all his books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burdened - carrying a heavy load or responsibility
  • synonym: overburdened - Having too much to carry or deal with
  • antonym: unburdened - not burdened; free from a load or responsibility
