deer hair

noun
hair of a deer
Pronunciation: / dɪr hɛr /; / ˈdɪr hɛr / (AmE), / dɪə hɛə /; / ˈdɪə hɛə / (BrE)
the coarse hair or fur from a deer, often used in fishing lures, crafts, or decorations
