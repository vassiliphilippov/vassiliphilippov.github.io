snowmaker

noun
artificial snow producer
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a machine or person that produces artificial snow, especially for ski slopes or events

Examples

A snowmaker machine
The ski resort invested in a new snowmaker to ensure good conditions throughout the winter.
During warm spells, the snowmaker helps keep the slopes covered for skiers.
A skilled snowmaker can create realistic snow for movie sets and special events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snow gun - a device that sprays water and air to create artificial snow
