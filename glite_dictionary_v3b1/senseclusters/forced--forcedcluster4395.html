forced

adjective
produced by external force
7.5 3.8
Pronunciation: /fɔːrst/ (AmE), /fɔːst/ (BrE)
(of a process or substance) produced by the application of external force rather than occurring naturally or spontaneously.

Examples

Forced air system
A forced circulation system moves water through the heating pipes more efficiently.
The laboratory used forced ventilation to maintain air quality during experiments.
The technician explained how forced convection improves heat transfer in the device.
