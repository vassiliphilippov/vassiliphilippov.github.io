aestheticist

noun
lover of beauty
Pronunciation: / ɛsˈθɛtɪsɪst / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who has or claims to have refined taste in beauty or the arts

Examples

Famous aestheticist in art
As an aestheticist, he spent hours admiring the details of classical paintings.
The interior designer was a true aestheticist, always seeking harmony and elegance in every room.
Many aestheticists believe that art should be valued for its beauty alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connoisseur - a person with expert knowledge and refined taste, especially in the arts
  • antonym: philistine - a person who lacks appreciation for art or culture
