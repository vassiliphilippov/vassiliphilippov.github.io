aestheticist

noun
lover of beauty
8.7
Pronunciation: /iːs.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ /; / /e.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /iːs.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ /; / /e.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

Famous aestheticist in art
As an aestheticist, he spent hours admiring the details of classical paintings.
The interior designer was a true aestheticist, always seeking harmony and elegance in every room.
Many aestheticists believe that art should be valued for its beauty alone.
