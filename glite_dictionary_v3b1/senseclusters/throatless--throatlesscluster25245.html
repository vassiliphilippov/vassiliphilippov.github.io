throatless

adjective
unable to speak
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊtləs / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊtləs / (BrE)
unable to speak or make a sound, or lacking a means of expression or voice, especially figuratively.

Examples

Throatless voice
She felt throatless in the meeting, unable to share her true feelings.
The throatless cry of the crowd went unheard by those in power.
After the accident, he was left throatless and could only communicate through writing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressive - able to express thoughts or feelings clearly
  • synonym: silent - not making any sound
  • synonym: voiceless - unable to speak or lacking a voice
