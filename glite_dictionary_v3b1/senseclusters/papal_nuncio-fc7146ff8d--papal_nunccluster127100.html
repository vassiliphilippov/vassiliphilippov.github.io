papal nuncio

noun
Pope's diplomatic representative
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpəl ˈnʌnsiˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəl ˈnʌnsɪəʊ / (BrE)
a diplomatic representative of the Pope with ambassadorial status, serving as an official liaison between the Vatican and a foreign government

Examples

papal nuncio to France
As papal nuncio to France, he played a key role in diplomatic negotiations.
The conference was attended by the papal nuncio and several cardinals.
The newly appointed papal nuncio presented his credentials to the president.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambassador - official diplomatic representative sent by a country
  • synonym: envoy - a diplomatic agent or messenger
