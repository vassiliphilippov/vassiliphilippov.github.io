macchiato

noun
espresso with milk
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːkiˈɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmakɪˈɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
a coffee drink made by combining espresso and milk, usually with one in a small amount to create a layered effect

Examples

A macchiato coffee
He ordered a caramel macchiato to enjoy with his breakfast pastry.
The barista carefully poured milk into the macchiato to create a perfect spot on top.
If you prefer a stronger coffee flavor, try a macchiato instead of a latte.
She ordered a caramel macchiato at the coffee shop before heading to work.
The barista carefully poured the espresso into the steamed milk to make a perfect macchiato.
Many people enjoy a macchiato for its balance of strong coffee and creamy milk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: americano - a coffee drink made by adding hot water to espresso
  • synonym: cappuccino - a coffee drink with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam
  • synonym: espresso - a strong black coffee made by forcing steam through ground coffee beans
  • synonym: latte - a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk
