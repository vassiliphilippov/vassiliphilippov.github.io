artificialness

noun
quality of being man-made
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəlnəs / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being made or produced by humans rather than occurring naturally

Examples

Artificialness of plastic fruit
The artificialness of the plastic fruit made it easy to spot among the real ones.
Some people dislike the artificialness of processed foods compared to fresh produce.
The artificialness of her smile suggested she was not truly happy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artificiality - the state of being artificial
  • antonym: naturalness - the quality of being natural or not artificial
