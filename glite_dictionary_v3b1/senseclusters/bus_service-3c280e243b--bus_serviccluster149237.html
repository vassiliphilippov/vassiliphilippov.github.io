bus service

noun
public bus transportation system
Pronunciation: / bʌs ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / bʌs ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a system or arrangement in which buses operate on regular routes to transport passengers, usually as part of public transport

Examples

A bus service route
A reliable bus service is essential for people without private transportation.
Due to heavy snowfall, the bus service was temporarily suspended.
The city expanded its bus service to include routes to the new shopping center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus line - a company or route for scheduled bus services
  • degree lower: taxi service - personal road transport by licensed vehicles
  • degree lower: train service - scheduled railway passenger transportation
