safety concern

noun
issue posing a risk to safety or well-being
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti kənˈsɝːn / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)

Examples

The playground was closed due to safety concerns raised by parents.
