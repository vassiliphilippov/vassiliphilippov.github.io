kerfuffle

noun
a minor commotion or fuss
Pronunciation: / kərˈfʌfəl / (AmE), / kəˈfʌf(ə)l / (BrE)
A commotion or fuss, especially one caused by conflicting views or a minor disturbance.

Examples

A kerfuffle at meeting
After the kerfuffle, everyone quickly returned to their seats as if nothing had happened.
The new policy announcement caused quite a kerfuffle among the staff.
There was a bit of a kerfuffle outside the office when the delivery arrived late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commotion - a state of confused and noisy disturbance
  • synonym: uproar - a loud and impassioned noise or disturbance
