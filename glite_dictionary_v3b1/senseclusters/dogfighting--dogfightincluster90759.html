dogfighting

noun
illegal dog fights
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔgˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɒgˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
an illegal and cruel activity in which dogs are forced to fight each other for entertainment or gambling

Examples

Illegal dogfighting event
Authorities raided a warehouse where illegal dogfighting events were being held.
Many animal rights groups campaign against dogfighting and work to rescue abused dogs.
Laws have become stricter to prevent dogfighting and punish those involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal cruelty - the act of causing suffering to animals
  • synonym: cockfighting - an illegal activity where roosters are forced to fight each other
