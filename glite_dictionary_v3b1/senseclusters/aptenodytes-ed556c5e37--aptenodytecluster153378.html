Aptenodytes

proper noun
penguin genus
Pronunciation: / æpˌtɛnəˈdaɪtiːz / (AmE), / æpˌtɛnəˈdaɪtiːz / (BrE)
a genus of large penguins that includes the emperor penguin and the king penguin

Examples

Genus Aptenodytes
The genus Aptenodytes contains the largest species of penguins in the world.
Scientists study Aptenodytes to learn more about how emperor and king penguins survive in cold climates.
Both the emperor and king penguins belong to the Aptenodytes genus.
