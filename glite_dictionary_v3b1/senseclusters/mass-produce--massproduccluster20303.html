mass-produce

verb
manufacture in large quantities
Pronunciation: / ˌmæs prəˈdus / (AmE), / ˌmæs prəˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to manufacture goods in large quantities, usually using machines and standardized processes

Examples

To mass-produce cars
Many companies mass-produce cars to meet the high demand of consumers.
The factory mass-produced toys for the holiday season.
Using new technology, manufacturers can mass-produce complex items with great efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manufacture - to produce goods, especially on a large scale using machinery
