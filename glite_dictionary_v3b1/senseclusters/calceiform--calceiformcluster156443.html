calceiform

adjective
slipper-shaped (of blossoms)
Pronunciation: / ˈkælsiˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪɪfɔːm / (BrE)
(of blossoms) shaped like a slipper; especially used of certain orchid flowers.

Examples

Calceiform orchid blossoms
Her garden features rare calceiform blossoms that resemble tiny slippers.
Several species in the Cypripedium genus have distinctive calceiform flowers.
The orchid's calceiform lip attracts pollinators with its unique shape.
