cookstove

noun
stove for cooking (esp. wood or coal)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌstoʊv / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌstəʊv / (BrE)
a stove used for cooking food, especially one that burns wood or coal

Examples

A cookstove burns wood
Many rural families relied on a cookstove for both heating and preparing meals.
She learned to bake bread in her grandmother’s wood-fired cookstove.
The old farmhouse kitchen still had a cookstove that burned firewood.
