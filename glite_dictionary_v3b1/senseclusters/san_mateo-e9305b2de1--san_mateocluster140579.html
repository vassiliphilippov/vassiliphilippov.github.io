San Mateo

proper noun
city in California south of San Francisco
Pronunciation: / sæn məˈteɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / san məˈteɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
A city in California, located south of San Francisco, known for its residential areas and proximity to Silicon Valley.

Examples

San Mateo city
