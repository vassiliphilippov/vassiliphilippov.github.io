respirator

noun
device providing long-term artificial breathing
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛspəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈrɛspɪreɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to provide artificial respiration, especially for patients unable to breathe unaided over an extended period

Examples

Hospital respirator machine
New guidelines recommend regular maintenance of the respirator in intensive care units.
Strict sterilization protocols must be followed when handling a hospital respirator.
The patient was placed on a respirator after having difficulty breathing on her own.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ventilator - a machine that moves air in and out of the lungs for patients who cannot breathe unaided
