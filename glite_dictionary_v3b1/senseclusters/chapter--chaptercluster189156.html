chapter

noun
distinct period in history or someone's life
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæptɚ /; / ˈtʃæp.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃæptə /; / ˈtʃæp.tə / (BrE)
a distinct period or episode in a person's life or in history, often marking a significant change or development

Examples

A new chapter in life
Moving to a new city marked an exciting chapter in her life.
The country's independence was a significant chapter in its history.
They closed the chapter on their failed business and started again.
The accident marked a difficult chapter in his life.
The war was a tragic chapter in the nation's history.
Their move to another country began a new chapter for the whole family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phase - a stage in a process or period of development
