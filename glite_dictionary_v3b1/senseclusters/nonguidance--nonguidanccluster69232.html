nonguidance

noun
not official advice
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɡaɪdəns / (AmE), / nɒnˈɡaɪdəns / (BrE)
a document, notice, or statement that is explicitly stated not to be official guidance or advice

Examples

Nonguidance statement
The agency released a nonguidance statement to clarify that it was not an official policy.
Lawyers reviewed the nonguidance document to ensure it did not imply any legal obligation.
The notice was labeled as nonguidance to avoid confusion with formal instructions.
