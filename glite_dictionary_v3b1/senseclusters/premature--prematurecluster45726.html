premature

adjective
born before full term of pregnancy
4.2 2.13/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåpriÀêm…ôÀàt É är / (AmE), / ÀåpriÀêm…ôÀàtj ä…ô / (BrE)
(of a baby) born before the end of the normal period of pregnancy.

Examples

Premature baby
A premature birth can lead to various health complications.
She was worried when her daughter delivered a premature infant.
The hospital has a special unit for premature babies.
