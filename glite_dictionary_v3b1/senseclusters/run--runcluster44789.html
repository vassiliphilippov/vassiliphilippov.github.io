run

noun
a flow or pouring of liquid
4.4
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
an act or instance of a fluid pouring or flowing forth continuously

Examples

A run of water
After the storm, there was a run of muddy water down the hillside.
The syrup producer monitors the run of sap each spring.
There was a brief run of oil from the broken pipe before it was sealed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flow - the movement of a liquid in a steady, continuous stream
