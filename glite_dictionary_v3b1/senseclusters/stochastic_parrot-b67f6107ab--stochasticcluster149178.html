stochastic parrot

noun
AI model that unpredictably imitates human language
Pronunciation: / stoʊˈkæstɪk ˈpærət / (AmE), / stəʊˈkæstɪk ˈpærət / (BrE)
a system, especially an artificial intelligence, that imitates human outputs in a random or uncomprehending way, producing responses that may be unpredictable, repetitive, or lacking genuine understanding

Examples

A stochastic parrot system
Some experts describe large language models as a kind of stochastic parrot that mimics human responses without comprehension.
The research paper argued that calling an AI a stochastic parrot highlights its inability to understand meaning.
When criticized for errors, the developers admitted their chatbot could sometimes act like a stochastic parrot in unpredictable ways.
