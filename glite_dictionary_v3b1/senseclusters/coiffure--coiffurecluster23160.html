coiffure

noun
arrangement or style of hair
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈfjʊr / (AmE), / kwɑːˈfjʊə / (BrE)
A style or arrangement of hair, especially one created by a hairdresser.

Examples

A fancy coiffure
Every era has its own distinctive coiffure that reflects the fashion of the time.
His mother visited the salon weekly for her favorite coiffure.
The elegant coiffure she wore to the gala drew many admiring glances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hairstyle - a particular way in which a person's hair is cut or arranged
