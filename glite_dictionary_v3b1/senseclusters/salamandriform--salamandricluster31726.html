salamandriform

adjective
having the shape of a salamander
Pronunciation: / səˈlæm.ən.drɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / səˈlam.ənd.rɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
Having a shape or form resembling a salamander.

Examples

A salamandriform shape
In paleontology, a salamandriform skull indicates adaptation to an aquatic lifestyle.
Researchers described the animal’s movements as salamandriform, closely resembling those of modern newts.
The fossil exhibits a distinctly salamandriform body structure, with an elongated trunk and short limbs.
