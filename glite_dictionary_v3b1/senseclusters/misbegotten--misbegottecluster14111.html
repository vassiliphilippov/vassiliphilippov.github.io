misbegotten

adjective
badly conceived
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɑtən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɒtən / (BrE)
badly planned, conceived, or created in a wrong or foolish way

Examples

Misbegotten plan
The committee quickly realized their misbegotten proposal would not solve the problem.
He regretted investing in such a misbegotten business venture.
Their misbegotten plan led to more confusion than progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ill-conceived - badly planned or thought out
  • antonym: well-conceived - carefully and thoughtfully planned
