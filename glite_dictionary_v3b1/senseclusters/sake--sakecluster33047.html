sake

noun
purpose or benefit (usually in phrases)
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / seɪk / (AmE), / seɪk / (BrE)
the purpose, reason, or benefit for doing something (especially in phrases like 'for the sake of')

For your own sake, you should get some rest tonight.
She moved to the city for the sake of her career.
They made sacrifices for the sake of their children.
