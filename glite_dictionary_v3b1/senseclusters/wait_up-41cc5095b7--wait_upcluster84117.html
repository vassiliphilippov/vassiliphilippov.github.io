wait up

verb
stay awake for someone's return
1.0
Pronunciation: / weɪt ʌp / (AmE), / weɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to stay awake until someone arrives, usually at home, especially to welcome or meet them

Examples

To wait up at home
Don't wait up—I'll be working late at the office.
Please wait up for me if I come home late tonight.
She was so worried that she decided to wait up until her son got home.
