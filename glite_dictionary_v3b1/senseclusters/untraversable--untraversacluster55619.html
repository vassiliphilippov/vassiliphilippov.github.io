untraversable

adjective
impossible to cross or travel through
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.trəˈvɜr.sə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.trəˈvɜː.sə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to travel across or through

Examples

An untraversable road

Dense undergrowth and fallen trees made the forest path completely untraversable by vehicles.
Engineers declared that section of the riverbank untraversable for construction equipment.
