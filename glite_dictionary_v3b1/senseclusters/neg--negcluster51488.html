neg

verb
to subtly insult
Pronunciation: / nɛɡ / (AmE), / nɛɡ / (BrE)
to give someone a backhanded compliment or mild insult to undermine their confidence, often as a manipulative flirting tactic

Examples

To neg a girl
He tried to neg her by making a sarcastic comment about her hairstyle.
Some people believe that using a neg can make them seem more attractive, but it often backfires.
She recognized his attempt to neg her and responded with confidence.
