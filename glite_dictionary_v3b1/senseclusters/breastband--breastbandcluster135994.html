breastband

noun
bird's chest marking
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌbænd / (BrE)
a band of feathers of a different color running across the breast of a bird

Examples

Bird's breastband
The robin's red breastband makes it easy to identify among other birds.
Some finches have a bright yellow breastband that stands out during mating season.
The guide pointed out the distinctive breastband on the rare warbler in the tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collar - a band or marking around the neck of a bird
  • synonym: plumage - the layer of feathers covering a bird
