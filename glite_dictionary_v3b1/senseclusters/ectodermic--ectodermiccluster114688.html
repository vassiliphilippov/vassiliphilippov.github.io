ectodermic

adjective
relating to the outer embryonic layer
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.toʊˈdɝː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛk.təʊˈdɜː.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from the ectoderm, the outermost layer of cells in an embryo

Examples

Ectodermic layer
Certain skin disorders can be traced back to ectodermic abnormalities.
Melanocytes are derived from ectodermic cells in early development.
The nervous system develops from ectodermic tissue during embryogenesis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endodermic - relating to the endoderm, the innermost embryonic layer
  • antonym: mesodermic - relating to the mesoderm, the middle embryonic layer
