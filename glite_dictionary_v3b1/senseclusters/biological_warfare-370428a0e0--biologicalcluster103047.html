Synonyms

  • biological attack
    5.5 0.20/m
    biological attack is a single use of germs in an attack, while biological warfare is a broader military strategy using such agents.
  • biologic attack
    7.8 0.00/m
    biologic attack is an alternative term often used for a single incident, while biological warfare describes planned, long-term military use of such agents.
  • bioattack
    9.3 0.01/m
    bioattack is a short, specific event using bacteria or toxins, while biological warfare is planned, long-term use of such weapons in war.
  • germ warfare
    6.8 0.19/m
    germ warfare is an older phrase for using microbes to cause disease, while biological warfare also includes toxins and food or water contamination.
