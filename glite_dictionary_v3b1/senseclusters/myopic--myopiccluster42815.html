Examples

Synonyms

  • nearsighted
    3.8 0.54/m
    nearsighted is unable to see distant objects clearly and sees nearby things better, while myopic names the medical condition of poor distance vision.
  • shortsighted
    3.8 0.05/m
    shortsighted means unable to see far without correction and is often used like nearsighted, while myopic refers to the clinical term for that vision problem.
