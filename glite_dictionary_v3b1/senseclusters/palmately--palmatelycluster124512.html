palmately

adverb
in a palmate (hand-like) manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːmətli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːmə tli / (BrE)
In a manner resembling or arranged like the open hand with the fingers spread; in a way that is palmate, particularly in reference to the arrangement of veins or lobes in leaves or other structures.

Examples

Leaves palmately veined
Several species in this genus have palmately veined foliage, making them easy to identify.
The flower clusters are palmately arranged, spreading outwards from a single stem.
The leaves are palmately lobed, radiating from a central point like fingers from a hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palmate - having parts arranged like the fingers of a hand
