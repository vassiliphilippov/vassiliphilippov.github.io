antilock

adjective
prevents wheel locking
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌlɒk / (BrE)
designed to prevent wheels from locking during braking, especially in vehicles

Examples

Antilock brakes
Modern cars often come with antilock braking systems to improve safety on slippery roads.
The mechanic recommended installing antilock brakes for better control during sudden stops.
Many drivers feel more confident in winter conditions with antilock features on their vehicles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-skid - designed to prevent sliding or skidding
