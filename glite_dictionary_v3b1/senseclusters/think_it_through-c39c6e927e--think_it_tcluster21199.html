think it through

verb
consider something carefully
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /θɪŋk ɪt θruː/ /; / /θɪŋk ə θruː/ (AmE), /θɪŋk ɪt θruː/ /; / /θɪŋk ə θruː/ (BrE)
