coarctation

noun
narrowing of a vessel in the body
Pronunciation: / koʊˌɑrkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / kəʊˌɑːkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an abnormal narrowing or constriction of a vessel, canal, or duct in the body, especially a blood vessel like the aorta
