on first-name terms

x
having a friendly, informal relationship
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈfɜrst neɪm tɜrmz / (AmE), / ɒn ˈfɜːst neɪm tɜːmz / (BrE)
having a friendly and informal relationship, shown by addressing each other by first names rather than titles or surnames

Examples

Be on first-name terms
After only a few weeks, the new colleagues were on first-name terms.
Her boss insists on being on first-name terms with everyone in the office.
It took some time, but eventually we were on first-name terms with all our neighbors.
