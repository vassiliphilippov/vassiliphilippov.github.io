ribband

noun
decorative ribbon or award ribbon
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.ənd / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.ənd / (BrE)
a ribbon used for decoration, especially as an ornament or award

Examples

A blue ribband
He received a blue ribband for winning first place in the contest.
She tied her hair with a bright red ribband for the celebration.
The soldier wore a ribband on his chest as a mark of honor.

Related Terms
