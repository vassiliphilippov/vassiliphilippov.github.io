ironed

adjective
smoothed with a hot iron
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɚnd / (AmE), / ˈaɪənd / (BrE)
(of clothes or linens) made smooth and free of creases by being pressed with a hot iron.

Examples

A freshly ironed shirt
He wore a freshly ironed shirt to the interview.
She prefers her bed sheets ironed for extra comfort.
The hotel provided clean, ironed towels every morning.
