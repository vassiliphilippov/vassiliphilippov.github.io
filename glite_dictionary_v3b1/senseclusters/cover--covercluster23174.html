cover

verb
(of a bird) to sit on eggs for incubation
8.2 1.12/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆkÊŒvÉš / (AmE), / ËˆkÊŒvÉ™ / (BrE)
(of a bird or other animal) to sit on eggs in order to keep them warm until they hatch.

Examples

A bird covers eggs
If disturbed, the female may briefly leave but quickly returns to cover the eggs again.
Parents often take turns to cover the nest and keep the eggs safe.
The hen will cover her eggs for weeks until they hatch.

Synonyms

  • brood
    6.0 0.13/m
    brood is used for a bird sitting on eggs to keep them warm until they hatch, while cover also applies to other animals.
  • incubate
    5.1 0.33/m
    incubate involves sitting to keep eggs warm so they can develop and hatch, while cover just means sitting on eggs to warm them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brood - to sit on eggs to hatch them (of a bird)
