Flodden

proper noun
the 1513 battle between England and Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑdən / (AmE), / ˈflɒdən / (BrE)
the Battle of Flodden, fought in 1513 between England and Scotland, in which the English defeated the Scots and King James IV of Scotland was killed

Examples

Battle of Flodden
James IV of Scotland lost his life at the Battle of Flodden in 1513.
Many legends and songs have been inspired by the tragic outcome of Flodden.
The defeat at Flodden marked a significant turning point in Scottish history.
