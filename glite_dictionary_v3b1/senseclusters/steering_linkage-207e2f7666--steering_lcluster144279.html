steering linkage

noun
mechanism connecting steering to wheels in vehicles
5.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪr.ɪŋ ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈstɪr.ɪŋ ˈlɪŋ.kə.dʒ/ (AmE), /ˈstɪə.rɪŋ ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈstɪə.rɪŋ ˈlɪŋ.kə.dʒ/ (BrE)
the system of rods and levers connecting the steering gear to the front wheels of a motor vehicle, allowing the wheels to turn in response to steering

Examples

The steering linkage system
A worn steering linkage can make your car difficult to control on the road.
Proper maintenance of the steering linkage is crucial for safe vehicle handling.
The mechanic inspected the steering linkage for signs of damage after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steering gear - mechanism that transmits the driver's steering movements to the steering linkage
