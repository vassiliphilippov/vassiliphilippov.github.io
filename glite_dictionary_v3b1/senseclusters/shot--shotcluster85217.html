shot

verb
to fire a weapon
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt / (AmE), / ʃɒt / (BrE)
to fire a gun or other weapon, or to hit something by firing a projectile

Examples

To shoot a target
The soldier shot at the enemy from behind the wall.
She shot the basketball and scored the winning point.
The police officer shot into the air to warn the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire - to discharge a gun or weapon
  • antonym: miss - to fail to hit a target
