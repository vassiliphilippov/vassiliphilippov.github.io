drapery

noun
a curtain or decorative hanging
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈdreɪpəri / (BrE)
a piece of cloth, especially used as a curtain or for decoration, often hung in loose folds

Examples

Drapery curtains
He pulled back the drapery to look out at the garden.
She chose a floral drapery to match the room's vintage decor.
The heavy drapery in the living room kept out the morning sunlight.
She admired the flowing drapery that hung from the ceiling, giving the room a touch of elegance.
Sunlight filtered through the heavy drapery at the windows, casting soft shadows on the floor.
The artist skillfully painted the drapery of the figure's robe, capturing every delicate fold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtain - a piece of cloth that hangs across a window or opening to block or obscure light or vision
  • synonym: drape - to arrange cloth or fabric loosely or gracefully
