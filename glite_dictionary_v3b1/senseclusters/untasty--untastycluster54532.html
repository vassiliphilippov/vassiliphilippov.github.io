untasty

adjective
not flavorful
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪsti / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪsti / (BrE)
not having a pleasant flavor; lacking taste or flavor

Examples

An untasty meal
The pasta was overcooked and untasty, so no one wanted to eat it.
He found the snack untasty and left most of it on his plate.
Despite its appearance, the dish was surprisingly untasty and bland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flavorless - having no flavor
  • synonym: tasteless - lacking flavor or taste
  • antonym: tasty - having a pleasant flavor
