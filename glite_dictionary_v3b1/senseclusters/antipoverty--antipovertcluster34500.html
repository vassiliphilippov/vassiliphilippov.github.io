antipoverty

noun
poverty reduction efforts
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈpɑːvərti / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈpɒvəti / (BrE)
actions, programs, or policies designed to reduce or prevent poverty

Examples

New antipoverty measures
The government introduced new antipoverty measures to help low-income families.
Many organizations focus on antipoverty initiatives to improve living conditions in poor communities.
Effective antipoverty programs can lead to better education and health outcomes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poverty alleviation - efforts to make poverty less severe
  • synonym: welfare - government support for those in need
