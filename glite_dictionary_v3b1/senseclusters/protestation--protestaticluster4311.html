protestation

noun
formal declaration of objection
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.tɛˈsteɪ.ʃən /; / ˌproʊ.t̬əˈsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.tɛˈsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a strong or formal declaration of objection, disagreement, or denial

Examples

His protestations fell on deaf ears, as no one believed him.
She submitted a formal protestation against the unfair decision.
