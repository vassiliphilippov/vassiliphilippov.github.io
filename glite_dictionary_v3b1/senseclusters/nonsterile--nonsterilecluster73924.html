nonsterile

adjective
not germ-free
5.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstɛrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈstɛraɪl / (BrE)
not completely free from germs or microorganisms; not sterile

Examples

Nonsterile medical tools
You should not use nonsterile equipment for surgery.
The bandages were found to be nonsterile and had to be replaced.
Using nonsterile gloves increases the risk of infection during medical procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sterile - completely free from germs or microorganisms
