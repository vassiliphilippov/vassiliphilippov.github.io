namespace

noun
resource grouping area
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪmˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈneɪmˌspeɪs / (BrE)
a system or area in computing that organizes and separates names or resources to avoid conflicts

Examples

Each project was assigned its own namespace to keep files organized and secure.
