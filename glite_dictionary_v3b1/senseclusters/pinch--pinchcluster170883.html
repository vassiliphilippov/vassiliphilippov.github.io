pinch

verb
use two-finger gesture to zoom
3.7
Pronunciation: /pɪntʃ/ (AmE), /pɪntʃ/ (BrE)
(in touchscreen devices) to use a two-finger gesture, moving the fingers together or apart on the screen, to zoom in or out of content.

Examples

To pinch the screen
When viewing photos, you can pinch the screen to zoom in on small details.
She pinched the map to zoom out and find the train station faster.
If you accidentally pinch the page, the article will enlarge and shift layout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zoom - to change the magnification of an image or view on a screen
