smear paint

verb
to spread paint messily
Pronunciation: / smɪr peɪnt / (AmE), / smɪə peɪnt / (BrE)
to spread paint onto a surface in a messy or uneven way

Examples

To smear paint messily
Children love to smear paint all over their hands and faces during art class.
He used a sponge to smear paint across the surface of the old table.
She tried to smear paint on the canvas, creating an abstract effect.
