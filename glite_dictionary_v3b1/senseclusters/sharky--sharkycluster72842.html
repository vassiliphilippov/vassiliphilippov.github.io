sharky

adjective
resembling a shark
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːki / (BrE)
resembling a shark in appearance, especially having sharp teeth, a pointed snout, or a fin-like shape

Examples

Sharky design
The designer created a sharky logo with sharp angles and a pointed fin.
His costume had a sharky look, complete with jagged teeth and a dorsal fin.
The new car model features a sharky front end that stands out on the road.
