lenticularis

adjective
lens-shaped
9.9
Pronunciation: /len.tɪ.kjʊ.ˈlær.ɪs/ /; / /len.tɪ.kjuː.ˈlær.ɪs/ (AmE), /len.tɪ.kjuː.ˈlær.ɪs/ /; / /len.tɪ.kjʊ.ˈlær.ɪs/ (BrE)
having the shape of a lens, with both sides convex and flattened in the middle

Examples

Lenticularis shape
The cloud had a lenticularis appearance, resembling a flying saucer in the sky.
Some seeds are described as lenticularis due to their flattened, lens-like shape.
The geologist noted the lenticularis form of the mineral crystal under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biconvex - having both surfaces curved outward, like a lens
  • antonym: concave - curved inward like the inside of a bowl
