Navy Cross

noun
US Navy decoration for heroism
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvi krɑs / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi krɒs / (BrE)
a United States Navy decoration awarded for extraordinary heroism in action against an enemy

Examples

The Navy Cross medal
Earning the Navy Cross is considered a great honor among service members.
She was awarded the Navy Cross for her bravery during the mission.
The Navy Cross is the second-highest military decoration for valor in the United States Navy.
