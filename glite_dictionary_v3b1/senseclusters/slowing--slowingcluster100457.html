slowing

noun
reduction in speed or rate
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A reduction in the speed, rate, or pace of something.

Examples

A slowing in speed
Recent data suggest a slowing in the rate of technological innovation.
The slowing of traffic due to construction caused major delays.
There has been a noticeable slowing in the growth of the economy this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceleration - a reduction in speed; the act of slowing down
