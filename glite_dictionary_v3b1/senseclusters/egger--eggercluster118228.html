egger

noun
bird egg collector
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡər / (AmE), / ˈɛɡə / (BrE)
a person who collects or steals the eggs of birds, often illegally or for personal interest

Examples

Bird egger
The park rangers arrested the egger for taking eggs from the rare bird's nest.
In the past, an egger might have sold eggs to collectors or museums.
Strict laws now punish any egger caught disturbing protected wildlife.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservationist - a person who works to protect wildlife and natural resources
  • synonym: poacher - a person who illegally hunts or captures animals
