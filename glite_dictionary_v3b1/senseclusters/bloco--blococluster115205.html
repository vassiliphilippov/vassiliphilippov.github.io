bloco

noun
building material block
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈblɒkəʊ / (BrE)
a large solid piece of material, such as stone or concrete, used in building or construction

Examples

Concrete bloco wall

A single concrete bloco can be very heavy to move by hand.
