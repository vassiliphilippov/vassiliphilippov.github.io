dermal

adjective
relating to the skin
5.1 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɜːr.məl/ (AmE), /ˈdɜː.məl/ (BrE)
relating to the skin or its layers (such as the dermis or outer protective layers)

Examples

Dermal layer
