clergy

noun
official religious leaders (Christianity)
3.6 9.90/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkl…ùÀêd íi / (AmE), / Ààkl…úÀêd íi / (BrE)
The official leaders of religious services in Christianity, such as priests, ministers, or pastors, collectively, as distinct from regular church members.
