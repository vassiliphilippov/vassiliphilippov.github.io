glareless

adjective
without a hostile stare
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɛrləs / (AmE), / ˈɡleələs / (BrE)
not showing an angry, intense, or hostile stare; lacking a glaring look

Examples

Glareless eyes
Her glareless eyes made everyone feel at ease during the tense meeting.
He greeted the newcomer with a glareless expression, showing no sign of anger.
Despite the criticism, her response remained calm and glareless throughout the discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glaring - showing an angry or fierce stare
  • antonym: hostile - showing or feeling opposition or dislike
