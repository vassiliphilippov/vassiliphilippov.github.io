incomer

noun
new resident
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌkʌmər / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who has recently arrived in a place to live or work, especially someone new to a community

Examples

New incomer in town
The small town welcomed every incomer with open arms.
As an incomer, she quickly made friends in her new neighborhood.
Some traditions are unfamiliar to an incomer at first.
The small town was friendly to every incomer who decided to settle there.
As an incomer, she found it challenging to adapt to the local customs at first.
Many incomers bring new ideas and energy to the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: local - a person who has lived in a place for a long time
  • synonym: newcomer - a person who has recently arrived somewhere
