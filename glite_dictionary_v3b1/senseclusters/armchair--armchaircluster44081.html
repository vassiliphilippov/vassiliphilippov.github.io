armchair

noun
comfortable chair with arm supports
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmˌtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈɑːmtʃeə / (BrE)
A comfortable chair with supports on each side for a person's arms.

Examples

A comfortable armchair
He sat comfortably in the armchair by the fireplace, reading a book.
She prefers the old leather armchair in the corner for her afternoon nap.
The living room was furnished with a sofa and a matching armchair.

Related Terms
