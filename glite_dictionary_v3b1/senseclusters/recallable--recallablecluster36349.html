recallable

adjective
able to be remembered
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔləbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːləbəl / (BrE)
able to be remembered or brought back to mind when needed

Examples

Recallable facts
The most important facts should be recallable during the final exam.
She organized her notes to make the information more recallable later.
Memories from childhood are not always easily recallable as we grow older.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgettable - easily forgotten; not memorable
  • synonym: memorable - easy to remember because it is special or unusual
