spinning machine

noun
textile machine for spinning yarn or thread
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used in the textile industry for spinning fibers into yarn or thread
