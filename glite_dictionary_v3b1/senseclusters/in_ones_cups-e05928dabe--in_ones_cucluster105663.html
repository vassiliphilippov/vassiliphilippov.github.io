in one's cups

adjective
intoxicated; drunk
Pronunciation: / ɪn wʌnz kʌps / (AmE), / ɪn wʌnz kʌps / (BrE)
drunk; under the influence of alcohol

Examples

He tends to let secrets slip when in his cups.
Though usually reserved, she became outspoken in her cups at the party.
