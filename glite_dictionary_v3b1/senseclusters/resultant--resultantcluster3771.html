resultant

noun
something produced as a result
5.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzʌltənt / (AmE), / rɪˈzʌltənt / (BrE)
a thing produced as a result in mathematics or science, especially a vector that is the sum of other vectors

Examples

Resultant vector
Mathematicians often calculate the resultant of two polynomials to determine their common roots.
The combined forces produce a resultant that points to the right.
When these chemicals react, the resultant is a harmless gas.
The resultant is obtained by adding the two force vectors together.
The engineer drew the resultant to represent the combined effect of the applied forces.
When calculating the resultant of several vectors, direction must also be considered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: result - an outcome or consequence of an action or process
  • synonym: vector - a quantity with both magnitude and direction
