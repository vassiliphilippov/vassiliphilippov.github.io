glace

noun
candied fruit or nut
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑːˈseɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːseɪ / (BrE)
a sweet food, such as fruit or nuts, that has been coated with a sugary glaze or preserved in sugar syrup
