rivetter

noun
worker who inserts and hammers rivets
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvətər / (AmE), / ˈrɪvɪtə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to insert and hammer rivets, especially in construction or manufacturing

Examples

Shipyard rivetter
During the war, she became a skilled rivetter at the local shipyard.
Many factories once employed hundreds of rivetters to assemble machinery.
The rivetter fastened each section of the metal frame firmly in place.
