twittery

adjective
like bird chirping
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪtəri / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtəri / (BrE)
making short, high-pitched sounds or resembling the light chirping of birds

Examples

Twittery bird sounds
The garden was alive with the twittery calls of sparrows early in the morning.
She loved the twittery sounds coming from the trees outside her window.
A twittery melody drifted through the open field as the sun rose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirpy - making cheerful, high-pitched sounds
  • degree higher: melodious - having a pleasant musical sound
