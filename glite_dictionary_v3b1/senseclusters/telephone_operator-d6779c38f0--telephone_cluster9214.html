telephone operator

noun
person who connects telephone calls
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.ə.foʊn ˈɑː.pə.reɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈtel.ə.fəʊn ˈɒ.pə.reɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.fəʊn ˈɒ.pə.reɪ.tə/ (BrE)
