slim-bodied

adjective
having a slender body
6.7
Pronunciation: / slɪm ˈbɑdiːd / (AmE), / slɪm ˈbɒdid / (BrE)
having a body that is slender or thin in shape or appearance

Examples

Slim-bodied birds
Many birds of prey are slim-bodied, allowing them to soar swiftly through the sky.
She admired the slim-bodied sculpture on display at the gallery.
The slim-bodied fish darted quickly through the reeds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fat-bodied - having a body that is fat; opposite of slim-bodied
  • synonym: slender - gracefully thin, especially of a person or animal
