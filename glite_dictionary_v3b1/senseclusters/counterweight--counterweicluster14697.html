counterweight

noun
a balancing weight
6.1 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.tər.weɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.tə.weɪt/ (BrE)
a weight used to balance another weight, especially in mechanisms or machines

Examples

Crane counterweight
A heavy counterweight was attached to the crane to keep it stable during lifting operations.
Adding a counterweight to the seesaw helped the children balance perfectly.
The elevator system relies on a counterweight to offset the weight of the cabin.
