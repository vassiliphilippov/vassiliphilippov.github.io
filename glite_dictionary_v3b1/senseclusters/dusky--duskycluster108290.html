dusky

adjective
dimly lit as by twilight
2.7 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌ.skiː/ /; / /ˈdʌ.skɪ/ (AmE), /ˈdʌ.skɪ/ /; / /ˈdʌ.skiː/ (BrE)
