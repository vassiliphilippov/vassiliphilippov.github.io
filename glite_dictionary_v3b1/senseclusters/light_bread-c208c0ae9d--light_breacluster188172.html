light bread

noun
soft, pale wheat bread
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt ˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌbrɛd / (BrE)
bread made from finely ground and usually bleached wheat flour, typically soft and pale in color

Examples

A slice of light bread
He prefers light bread to whole wheat because of its soft texture.
Many bakeries in the South are known for their traditional light bread.
She served sandwiches made with light bread and ham at the picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: white bread - bread made from wheat flour that has had the bran and germ removed
