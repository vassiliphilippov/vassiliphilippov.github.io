American plan

noun
hotel plan with all meals included
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛɹɪkən plæn / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkən plæn / (BrE)
A hotel pricing plan that includes three meals a day with the cost of the room.

Examples

An American plan hotel
Many resorts offer an American plan, which covers breakfast, lunch, and dinner in the room rate.
Under the American plan, guests enjoy three daily meals as part of their accommodation.
We opted for the American plan to keep our holiday costs predictable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: European plan - a hotel plan that includes room only, no meals
