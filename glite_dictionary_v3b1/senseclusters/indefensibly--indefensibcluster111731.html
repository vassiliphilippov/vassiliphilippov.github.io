Examples

Act indefensibly wrong

It is indefensibly wrong to discriminate against people based on their background.
The law was applied indefensibly in this particular case, causing public outrage.

Synonyms

  • inexcusably
    6.9 0.11/m
    inexcusably is used when an action clearly deserves blame and no excuse fits, while indefensibly focuses on being impossible to defend.
  • unjustifiably
    7.0 0.12/m
    unjustifiably means lacking good reason and often shows weak grounds, while indefensibly stresses that no strong defense exists for it.
  • unwarrantably
    9.9 0.00/m
    unwarrantably means lacking reasonable grounds or justification, while indefensibly stresses that something cannot be defended as right.
  • insupportably
    8.3 0.01/m
    insupportably often means something cannot be supported or is unbearable, while indefensibly means it cannot be defended as right.
