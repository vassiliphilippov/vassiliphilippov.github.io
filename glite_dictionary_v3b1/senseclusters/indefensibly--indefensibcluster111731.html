indefensibly

adverb
without justification
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be justified or defended as right or reasonable

Examples

Act indefensibly wrong
The manager indefensibly dismissed the complaints without any investigation.
It is indefensibly wrong to discriminate against people based on their background.
The law was applied indefensibly in this particular case, causing public outrage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensibly - in a way that can be defended or justified
  • synonym: unjustifiably - in a way that cannot be justified
