arbitrator

noun
person who settles disputes
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrbɪˌtreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɑːbɪˌtreɪtə / (BrE)
a person officially appointed to settle a dispute between parties outside of court, making a binding decision

Examples

An arbitrator settles disputes
An experienced arbitrator was chosen to oversee the labor negotiations.
Each company nominated an arbitrator to resolve the commercial dispute.
The contract stated that any disagreement would be settled by an arbitrator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbiter - person with power to decide a dispute or issue
  • degree lower: mediator - person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement
