outrebound

verb
to get more rebounds
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊt.rɪˈbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˌaʊt.rɪˈbaʊnd / (BrE)

Examples

To outrebound the opponent
The visiting team managed to outrebound their rivals in the second half.
If we outrebound them tonight, we have a good chance to win.
Despite being shorter, she outrebounded everyone else on the court.
