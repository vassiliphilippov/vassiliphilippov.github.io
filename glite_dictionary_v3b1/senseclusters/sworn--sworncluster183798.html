sworn

adjective
formally bound by oath or promise
5.2
Pronunciation: / swɔrn / (AmE), / swɔːn / (BrE)
having made a solemn promise or oath; formally bound by oath

Examples

She is a sworn witness in the trial.
They became sworn brothers after taking an oath of loyalty.
As a sworn witness, she gave her testimony in court.
He considered her his sworn enemy ever since the betrayal.
They became sworn friends after completing the challenging journey together.

  • antonym: unsworn - not having taken an oath
