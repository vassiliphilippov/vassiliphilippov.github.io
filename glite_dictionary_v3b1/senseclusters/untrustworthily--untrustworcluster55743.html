untrustworthily

adverb
without reliability
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈtrʌstwɝːðɪli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtrʌstwɜːðɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of trustworthiness or reliability

Examples

Behave untrustworthily
He behaved untrustworthily by sharing confidential information with outsiders.
The documents were handled untrustworthily, leading to a loss of important data.
She acted untrustworthily when she broke her promise to the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dishonestly - in a way that is not honest or truthful
  • antonym: reliably - in a way that can be trusted or depended on
