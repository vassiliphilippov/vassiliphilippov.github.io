Gallia Aquitania

proper noun
historical region in southwestern France
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæliə ˌækwɪˈteɪniə / (AmE), / ˈɡaliə ˌakwɪˈteɪnɪə / (BrE)
A historical region of southwestern France, located between Bordeaux and the Pyrenees, known as Aquitania under Roman rule.

Examples

Gallia Aquitania region
Archaeologists have uncovered numerous artifacts in the region once known as Gallia Aquitania.
