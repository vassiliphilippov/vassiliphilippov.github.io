sparerib

noun
a cut of pork ribs with little meat
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛrˌrɪb / (AmE), / ˈspɛə.rɪb / (BrE)
a cut of pork ribs from which most of the meat has been trimmed away, leaving mainly bone and some attached meat, often used in cooking and barbecuing

Examples

Grilled spareribs
My grandmother's specialty was roasting spareribs with a sweet and tangy sauce.
The recipe calls for marinating the spareribs overnight for better flavor.
We ordered a plate of spareribs at the barbecue restaurant.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: baby back rib - a cut of pork ribs from the upper part of the ribcage, more tender and meaty than spareribs
