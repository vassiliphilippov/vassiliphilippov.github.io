bucket list

noun
things to do before you die
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkɪt lɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt lɪst / (BrE)
a list of experiences or achievements that a person wants to accomplish during their lifetime, especially before they die

Examples

A bucket list of goals
He made a bucket list after his diagnosis and started traveling the world.
Visiting the Grand Canyon has always been at the top of my bucket list.
Writing a novel was one of the few things still left on her bucket list.
He made a bucket list of things he wants to do after retiring.
Visiting Antarctica is at the top of my bucket list.
Writing a novel is something I hope to check off my bucket list one day.

  • synonym: wish list - a list of desired items or experiences
