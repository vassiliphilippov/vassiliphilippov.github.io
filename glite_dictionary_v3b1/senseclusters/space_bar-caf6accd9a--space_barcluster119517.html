space bar

noun
key on a keyboard for inserting spaces
3.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /speɪs bɑːr/ (AmE), /speɪs bɑː/ (BrE)
the long horizontal key on a typewriter or computer keyboard that inserts a space between words or characters when pressed

Examples

Press the space bar
Her finger hovered over the space bar as she searched for the right words to type next.
Press the space bar to insert a space between the words.
The space bar on my keyboard is not working properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enter key - key on a keyboard used to input commands or start a new line
