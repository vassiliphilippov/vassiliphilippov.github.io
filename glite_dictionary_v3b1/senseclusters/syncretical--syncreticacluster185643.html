syncretical

adjective
relating to syncretism
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈkrɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪŋˈkrɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by syncretism; combining different beliefs, systems, or practices.

Examples

Syncretical approach
A syncretical approach to philosophy seeks to unite Eastern and Western thought.
Her art displays a syncretical fusion of various cultural styles.
The region's religion is highly syncretical, blending local traditions with imported beliefs.
