vagabonding

verb
to wander long-term
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈvæg.ə.bɑːn.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈvæg.ə.bɒn.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
to travel or live from place to place without a permanent home, often for extended periods

Examples

To go vagabonding abroad
After college, she spent a year vagabonding through Southeast Asia with just a backpack.
Many people dream of vagabonding across Europe, exploring new cities without a set plan.
He wrote a blog about his experiences vagabonding and meeting people from all walks of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roam - to move about or travel aimlessly or unsystematically
  • antonym: settle - to establish a permanent home
