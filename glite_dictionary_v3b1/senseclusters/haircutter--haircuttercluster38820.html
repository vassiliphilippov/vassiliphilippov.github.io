haircutter

noun
hair cutting tool
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈheəˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a tool or machine, especially an electric clipper, used for cutting or trimming hair

Examples

Electric haircutter
He used a haircutter to give his brother a quick trim before the party.
The new haircutter comes with several attachments for different hair lengths.
Many people bought a haircutter to cut their own hair during the lockdown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clipper - an electric tool for cutting hair
  • synonym: scissors - a hand-operated tool for cutting hair or other materials
