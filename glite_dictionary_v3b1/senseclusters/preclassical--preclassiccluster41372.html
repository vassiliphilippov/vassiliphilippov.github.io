preclassical

adjective
before classical era
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈklæsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈklæsɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the period before the classical era in art, music, literature, or history

Examples

Preclassical period art
The exhibit displays preclassical sculptures that influenced later Greek artists.
Researchers study preclassical music to understand the origins of Western harmony.
Many preclassical texts provide insight into early philosophical thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archaic - very old or old-fashioned; from an earlier period
  • antonym: classical - relating to the classical period in art, music, or history
