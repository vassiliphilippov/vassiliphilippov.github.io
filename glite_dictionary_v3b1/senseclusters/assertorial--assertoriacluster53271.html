assertorial

adjective
confidently forceful
Pronunciation: / əˌsɜːrˈtɔːriəl /; / ˌæsərˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / əˌsɜːˈtɔːriəl /; / əˌsɜːˈtɔːrɪəl /; / ˌæsəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
stating or claiming something as true or with confidence

Examples

An assertorial statement

In logic, an assertorial proposition claims something is the case.

In logic, an assertorial proposition differs from one that expresses necessity or possibility.
Her argument was based on assertorial judgments rather than hypothetical scenarios.
