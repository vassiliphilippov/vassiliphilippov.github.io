lacquerware

noun
decorative wooden object coated with lacquer
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlækɚˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈlakəˌwɛə / (BrE)
Decorative objects made of wood and coated with lacquer, often inlaid with ivory or precious metals.

Examples

Chinese lacquerware
