heart and soul

noun
essential or most vital part of something
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ənd soʊl / (AmE), / hɑːt ənd səʊl / (BrE)
the most essential, vital, or important part of something; the central core

Examples

Heart and soul of the team
He is considered the heart and soul of the team, holding everyone together.
The heart and soul of her argument was the need for equal opportunity.
The city centre is truly the heart and soul of the community.
