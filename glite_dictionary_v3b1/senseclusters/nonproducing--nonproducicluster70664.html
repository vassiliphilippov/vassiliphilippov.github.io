nonproducing

adjective
not generating output
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprəˈdusɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprəˈdjuːsɪŋ / (BrE)
not producing or not capable of producing anything, especially goods, resources, or results

Examples

Nonproducing assets
The company sold off its nonproducing assets to focus on growth.
Farmers abandoned the nonproducing land after several years of poor harvests.
The oil well was classified as nonproducing after it ran dry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: productive - producing or able to produce large amounts or results
  • synonym: unproductive - not producing useful results or goods
