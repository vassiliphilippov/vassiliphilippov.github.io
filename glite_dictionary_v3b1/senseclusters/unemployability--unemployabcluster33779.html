unemployability

noun
inability to work
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˌplɔɪəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˌplɔɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state or condition of being unable to get or keep a job

Examples

Permanent unemployability
His injury resulted in permanent unemployability and he could no longer support his family.
The program aims to reduce unemployability among young adults by offering job training.
Long-term illness can lead to unemployability for some people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employability - the quality of being suitable for paid work
  • synonym: unemployment - the state of not having a job
