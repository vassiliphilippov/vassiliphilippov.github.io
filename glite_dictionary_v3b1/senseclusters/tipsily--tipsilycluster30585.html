tipsily

adverb
in a drunk manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpsɪli / (AmE), / ˈtɪpsɪli / (BrE)
in a slightly drunk or unsteady manner

Examples

Laugh tipsily
She laughed tipsily at his joke, her cheeks flushed from the wine.
After a few drinks, he danced tipsily across the living room.
The couple walked tipsily home, holding hands and singing softly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunkenly - in a way that shows someone is drunk
  • antonym: steadily - in a firm or stable manner
