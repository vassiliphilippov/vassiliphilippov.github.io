ancillary

noun
supporting person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈænsəˌlɛri / (AmE), / ænˈsɪləri / (BrE)
a person or thing that provides necessary support to the main activities or operation of an organization or system

Examples

An ancillary worker
The hospital could not function without its dedicated ancillaries who support the medical staff.
Many schools employ ancillaries to help with administrative and maintenance tasks.
The company hired several ancillaries to assist during the busy holiday season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone in their work
  • antonym: main - the most important or principal part
