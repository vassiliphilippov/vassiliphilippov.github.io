Milontin

noun
anticonvulsant drug (trade name)
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈlɑːntɪn / (AmE), / mɪˈlɒntɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked anticonvulsant drug used to treat petit mal epilepsy (absence seizures).

Examples

Milontin medication
After several weeks, Milontin helped reduce the frequency of his seizures.
Patients with absence seizures may be prescribed Milontin as part of their treatment regimen.
The neurologist discussed the side effects of Milontin with the patient's family.

Related Terms
