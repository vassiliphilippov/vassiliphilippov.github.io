rag

noun
a small piece of cloth
Pronunciation: / ræɡ / (AmE), / ræɡ / (BrE)
a small piece of old, often torn cloth, typically used for cleaning or wiping.

Examples

A dirty rag
Please throw that dirty rag in the laundry basket.
She used an old rag to wipe the muddy footprints from the floor.
The mechanic kept a rag in his pocket for greasy hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloth - a woven or felted fabric made from wool, cotton, or a similar fiber
