shaming

verb
to make feel ashamed
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel ashamed or embarrassed, often by criticizing or humiliating them publicly.

Examples

To shame someone publicly
The group was shaming people online for not recycling their waste properly.
She regretted shaming her friend in front of the whole class.
Many activists warn against shaming individuals instead of encouraging positive change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humiliate - to make someone feel very embarrassed or ashamed
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for someone
