reforest

verb
to plant trees in deforested land
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈfɔːr.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfɒr.ɪst / (BrE)
to plant trees on land where a forest has been cut down or destroyed

Examples

To reforest burned land
After the wildfire, it took years to fully reforest the region.
The government plans to reforest areas that were damaged by illegal logging.
Volunteers worked all weekend to help reforest the valley.
