Spanish onion

noun
large, mild, juicy onion variety
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
A large, mild, and juicy variety of onion, often eaten raw in salads or on sandwiches.

Examples

A Spanish onion salad

You can serve burgers with rings of raw Spanish onion for extra flavor and crunch.
