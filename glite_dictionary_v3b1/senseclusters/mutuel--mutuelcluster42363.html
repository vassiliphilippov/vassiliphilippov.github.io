mutuel

noun
pooled betting system
9.8
Pronunciation: /mjuː.ˈtjuː.əl/ /; / /mjuː.ˈtjuːl/ (AmE), /mjuː.ˈtjuː.əl/ /; / /mjuː.ˈtjuːl/ (BrE)
a system of betting in which all bets are pooled and winners share the total amount after deductions

Examples

Mutuel betting system
He placed his wager at the mutuel window at the racetrack.
The mutuel system ensures that all winning bettors share the prize pool fairly.
Many horse racing fans prefer the mutuel method over fixed-odds betting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed-odds - a betting system where the payout is agreed at the time the bet is placed
  • synonym: pari-mutuel - a betting system where all bets are pooled and winners share the pot
