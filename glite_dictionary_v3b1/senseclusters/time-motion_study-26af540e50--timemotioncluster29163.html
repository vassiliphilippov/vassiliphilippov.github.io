time-motion study

noun
analysis to improve work efficiency
4.8 5.0
Pronunciation: /taɪm ˈmoʊ.ʃən ˈstʌd.ɪ/ (AmE), /taɪm ˈməʊ.ʃən ˈstʌd.ɪ/ (BrE)
an analysis of a job or work process to determine the most efficient way to perform it, often by observing and timing each movement involved

Examples

A time-motion study analysis
A time-motion study revealed unnecessary steps in the workers' daily routines.
He specialized in performing time-motion studies for manufacturing firms.
The company conducted a time-motion study to increase efficiency on the assembly line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: time study - the analysis of the time taken to complete each step of a task
