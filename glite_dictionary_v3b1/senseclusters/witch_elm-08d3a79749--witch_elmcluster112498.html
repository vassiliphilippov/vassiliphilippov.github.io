witch elm

noun
a Eurasian elm tree (Ulmus glabra)
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ ɛlm / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ɛlm / (BrE)
A species of elm tree (Ulmus glabra) native to Europe and often planted as a shade tree.

Examples

A witch elm tree
A beautiful witch elm stood at the center of the old park, offering shade in the summer.
Several witch elms were planted along the avenue to create a natural walkway.
The witch elm is prized for its broad canopy and resistance to wind.
