romaine lettuce

noun
lettuce with long, crisp leaves
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmeɪn ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / rəʊˈmeɪn ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a type of lettuce with long, crisp, dark-green leaves, often used in salads
