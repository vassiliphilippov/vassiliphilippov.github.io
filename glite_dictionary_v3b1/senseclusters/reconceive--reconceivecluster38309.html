reconceive

verb
to become pregnant again
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkənˈsiːv / (AmE), / ˌriːkənˈsiːv / (BrE)
to become pregnant again after a previous pregnancy or conception.

Examples

To reconceive a child

Doctors advised her to wait before trying to reconceive after the miscarriage.
Many couples find it emotionally challenging to reconceive following a loss.
