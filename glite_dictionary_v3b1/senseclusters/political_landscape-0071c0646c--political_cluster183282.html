political landscape

noun
current state of political affairs
6.1
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈlændskeɪp / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
the overall state or conditions of political affairs in a country, region, or organization, especially as these are influenced by major events or trends

Examples

The political landscape today
Social media has become an influential factor in today's political landscape.
Sudden policy changes can disrupt the stability of a region's political landscape.
The recent election significantly altered the political landscape of the country.
