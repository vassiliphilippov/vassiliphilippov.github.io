Lathyrus hirsutus

noun
annual pea plant, cover crop
Pronunciation: / ləˈθaɪrəs hɪrˈsuːtəs / (AmE), / ləˈθaɪrəs hɪəˈsjuːtəs / (BrE)
A weak-stemmed annual plant of the pea family, native to the Mediterranean region and established in the southern United States, cultivated as a cover and pasture crop.
