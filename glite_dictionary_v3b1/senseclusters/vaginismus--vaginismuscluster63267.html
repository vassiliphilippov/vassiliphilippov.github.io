vaginismus

noun
involuntary vaginal muscle contraction
Pronunciation: / vəˌdʒɪnɪzˈməs / (AmE), / vəˌdʒɪnɪzˈməs / (BrE)
a condition in which involuntary muscle contractions cause the vagina to close, often making sexual intercourse or medical examination painful or impossible

Examples

Vaginismus muscle spasm
Many women with vaginismus feel anxious about sexual activity because of the discomfort it causes.
The patient was diagnosed with vaginismus after experiencing pain during routine gynecological exams.
Treatment for vaginismus may include physical therapy and counseling.
