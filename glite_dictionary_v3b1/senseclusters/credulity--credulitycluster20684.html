credulity

noun
tendency to believe too easily
8.1 0.54/m
Pronunciation: / kr…ôÀàdjuÀêl…ôti / (AmE), / kr…ôÀàdjuÀêl…ôti / (BrE)
a tendency to believe things too easily or quickly, with little evidence or skepticism

Examples

High credulity
His credulity made him an easy target for internet scams.
She was warned that too much credulity could lead to disappointment.
The magician relied on the credulity of the audience to amaze them with tricks.
