agent bank

noun
bank managing syndicated loan
2.7 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.dʒənt bæŋk/ /; / /ˈeɪ.dʒənt beɪŋk/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.dʒənt bæŋk/ /; / /ˈeɪ.dʒənt beɪŋk/ (BrE)
a bank that acts on behalf of other banks or lenders, especially to manage loans or financial transactions

Examples

The agent bank manages loans
