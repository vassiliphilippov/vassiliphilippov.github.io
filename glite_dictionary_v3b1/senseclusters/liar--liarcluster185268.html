liar

noun
a person who tells lies
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who tells lies, especially habitually

Examples

A liar tells lies
It's hard to trust anything a liar says after being caught so many times.
She called him a liar when he denied breaking the vase.
The politician was branded a liar by his opponents in the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibber - a person who tells trivial or harmless lies
  • antonym: truth-teller - a person who tells the truth
