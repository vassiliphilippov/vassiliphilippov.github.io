congregation

noun
group attending religious worship
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːŋɡrəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
A group of people who regularly gather for religious worship, especially in a church.

Examples

Church congregation
A small congregation gathers at the old chapel each Sunday.
The congregation stood to sing the final hymn together.
The priest thanked the congregation for their generous donations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: audience - group of people gathered to watch or listen to something
  • synonym: parishioners - members of a particular parish or church
