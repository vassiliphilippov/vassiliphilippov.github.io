Synonyms

    2.3 2/m
    cart is used for moving heavy things on wheels, while haul often means moving heavy loads by vehicle or pulling.
  • transport
    1.4 18/m
    transport moves people or goods by vehicle or service, while haul focuses on moving heavy loads often by pulling.
  • pull along
    3.0 0.5/m
    pull along means dragging something with effort, while haul covers both dragging and moving heavy loads by vehicle.
  • lug
    4.4 1/m
    lug means carrying or dragging something heavy with effort by hand, while haul can involve vehicles or pulling.
  • tote
    6.0 3/m
    tote means carrying something heavy or awkward, often by hand or shoulder, while haul suggests larger loads or vehicles.
