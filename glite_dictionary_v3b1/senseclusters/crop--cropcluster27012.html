crop

noun
the yield of plants in a season
Pronunciation: / krɑp / (AmE), / krɒp / (BrE)
plants grown and harvested for food or other use, or the total amount produced in a season

Examples

Wheat crop
After the drought, the corn crop was significantly reduced.
Farmers depend on a good crop to support their families.
The wheat crop was especially abundant this year.
Each crop requires specific conditions to grow successfully.
The farmers planted a new crop of corn this year.
Wheat is the main crop in this region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fallow - land that is plowed but left unsown to restore fertility
  • synonym: harvest - the process or period of gathering in crops
