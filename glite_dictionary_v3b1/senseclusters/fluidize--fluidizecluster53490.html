fluidize

verb
to make solid flow
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːɪˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfluːɪdaɪz / (BrE)
to make a solid behave like a fluid by passing a gas or liquid through it

Examples

To fluidize powder
Industrial processes often fluidize powders to improve chemical reactions and heat transfer.
The engineers fluidized the bed of sand by pumping air through it.
To achieve even mixing, the system must fluidize the catalyst particles efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liquefy - to make or become liquid
  • antonym: solidify - to make or become solid
