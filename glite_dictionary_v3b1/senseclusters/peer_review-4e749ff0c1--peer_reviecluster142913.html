peer review

verb
evaluate a colleague's work professionally
7.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /pɪr rɪ.ˈvjuː/ /; / /piːr rɪ.ˈvjuː/ (AmE), /pɪə rɪ.ˈvjuː/ /; / /piːə rɪ.ˈvjuː/ (BrE)
To evaluate or assess the quality and validity of a colleague's work, especially in academic or professional settings, by other experts in the same field.

Examples

To peer review a paper
All submitted manuscripts are peer reviewed before they are accepted for publication.
She is peer reviewing several articles for the journal this month.
We will peer review the proposal to ensure its scientific merit.
