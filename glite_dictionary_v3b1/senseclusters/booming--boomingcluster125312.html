Examples

  • resounding
    4.9 1.80/m
    resounding is a loud clear sound that echoes in a space, while booming focuses on depth and bass.
  • boomy
    7.3 0.01/m

  • thunderous
    4.1 1.33/m
    thunderous is an extremely loud, powerful noise like thunder, while booming is deep and echoing but less storm-like.
  • thundering
    3.2 0.00/m

  • roaring
    3.4 1.00/m

  • bellowing
    5.9 0.19/m

  • ringing
