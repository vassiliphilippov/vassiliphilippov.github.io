diatomaceous

adjective
from diatom remains
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪətəˈmeɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌdaɪətəˈmeɪʃəs / (BrE)
consisting of or relating to the remains of diatoms, which are microscopic algae with silica cell walls

Examples

Diatomaceous earth
The laboratory used a diatomaceous filter to remove impurities from the water sample.
Farmers sometimes spread diatomaceous earth in their fields to control insect pests naturally.
The rock layer was rich in diatomaceous material, indicating an ancient lake environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: siliceous - containing or resembling silica
