mahout

noun
elephant driver or keeper
9.0
Pronunciation: / məˈhaʊt / (AmE), / məˈhaʊt / (BrE)
a person who works as the driver and keeper of an elephant, especially in South or Southeast Asia

Examples

A mahout with elephant
At the sanctuary, a mahout explained how he communicates with his elephant.
Each mahout develops a close bond with his elephant over many years.
The skilled mahout guided the elephant gently through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elephant trainer - person who trains elephants
