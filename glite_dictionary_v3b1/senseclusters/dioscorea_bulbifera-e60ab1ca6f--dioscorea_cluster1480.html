Dioscorea bulbifera

noun
species of yam cultivated for its tubers
9.9
Pronunciation: / di.ɑːsˈkɔːri.ə bʌlˈbɪfərə / (AmE), / dɪ.ɒsˈkɔːri.ə bʌlˈbɪfərə / (BrE)

Examples
