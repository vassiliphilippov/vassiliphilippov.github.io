sacralize

verb
to fuse with sacrum
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪkrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsakrəlaɪz / (BrE)
to cause a vertebra or other structure to fuse with the sacrum, making it anatomically part of the sacral region

Examples

To sacralize a vertebra
The X-ray showed that the L5 vertebra had sacralized, limiting the patient's lower back movement.
Some people are born with a sacralized lumbar vertebra, which can affect posture.
The doctor explained how trauma could sacralize a vertebra over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuse - to join two or more things together to form a single entity
