worldbuilding

noun
creating imaginary worlds
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrld.bɪl.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːld.bɪl.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of creating an imaginary world or setting, especially for a story, game, or other creative work

Examples
