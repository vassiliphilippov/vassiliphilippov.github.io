pasteboard

noun
stiff cardboard made of layered paper
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪstˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a stiff, strong cardboard made by gluing together several layers of paper

Examples

A pasteboard box
He carefully cut the pasteboard to fit the frame for his project.
The artist mounted her sketch on a piece of pasteboard for display.
We used pasteboard to make durable signs for the school fair.
