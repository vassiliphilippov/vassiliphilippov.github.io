balloon seat

noun
round, bell-shaped chair seat
Pronunciation: / bəˈluːn siːt / (AmE), / bəˈluːn siːt / (BrE)
A seat of bell-like rounded shape, found on some 18th-century chairs.

Examples

A balloon seat chair
Furniture collectors often seek out pieces with original balloon seats from the Georgian period.
The balloon seat was popular among 18th-century cabinetmakers for its comfort and graceful design.
The antique chair featured a distinctive balloon seat that gave it an elegant look.
