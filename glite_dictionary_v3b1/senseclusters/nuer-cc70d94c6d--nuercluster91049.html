Nuer

adjective
of the Nuer people
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːə / (BrE)
relating to the Nuer people, their language, or their culture

Examples

Nuer traditions
The Nuer community celebrates many traditional festivals throughout the year.
She studied Nuer customs as part of her anthropology research.
Many Nuer families live along the Nile in South Sudan.
