psig

noun
pressure unit (gauge)
Pronunciation: / piː ɛs aɪ ˈdʒiː / (AmE), / piː ɛs aɪ ˈdʒiː / (BrE)
a unit of pressure that measures pounds per square inch relative to atmospheric pressure

Examples

psig pressure unit
The pressure gauge showed 80 psig after the system was fully pressurized.
Always check the psig reading before operating the equipment to ensure safety.
The manual recommends maintaining a minimum of 30 psig for optimal performance.
