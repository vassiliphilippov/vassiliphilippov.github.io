purpuric

adjective
purple-colored
9.8
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.ˈpjʊr.ɪk/ /; / /pɜːr.ˈpjuːr.ɪk/ (AmE), /pɜː.ˈpjʊ.rɪk/ /; / /pɜː.ˈpjuː.rɪk/ (BrE)
having a purple or purplish color, especially relating to discoloration of the skin or tissues, often due to purpura (a condition causing purple spots on the skin)
