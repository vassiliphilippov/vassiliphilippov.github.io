unproportioned

adjective
lacking balance
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈpɔrʃənd / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈpɔːʃənd / (BrE)
not having correct or balanced proportions in size, shape, or arrangement

Examples

An unproportioned sketch

Some buildings in the city look oddly unproportioned next to their modern neighbors.
Her sculpture was criticized for being unproportioned and lacking harmony.
