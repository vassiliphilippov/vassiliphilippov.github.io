Burmese-Yi language

noun
Tibeto-Burman language of northern Myanmar and Yunnan
Pronunciation: / bɜrˌmiz ˈji ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bɜːˌmiːz ˈjiː ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken in northern Myanmar and Yunnan province of China
