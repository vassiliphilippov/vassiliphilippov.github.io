companionship

noun
the state of being with someone
2.2
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / kəmˈpænjənʃɪp / (BrE)
the pleasant feeling of being with someone and having their company

Examples

Friendly companionship
After moving to a new city, she found companionship in a local book club.
Dogs are known for providing loyal companionship to their owners.
For many elderly people, companionship is just as important as medical care.
