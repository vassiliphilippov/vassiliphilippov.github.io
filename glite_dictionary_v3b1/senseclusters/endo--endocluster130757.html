endo

noun
forward bicycle fall
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛndəʊ / (BrE)
a sudden forward fall on a bicycle or motorcycle, caused when the front wheel stops and the rider is thrown over the handlebars

Examples

Bike endo accident
He broke his arm after an endo when the bicycle's front wheel suddenly stopped.
She did an unexpected endo while riding down the steep hill.
After hitting the rock, his bike stopped and he went over the handlebars in a dramatic endo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash - an accident in which a vehicle hits something
  • synonym: wipeout - a sudden fall or accident, especially in sports
