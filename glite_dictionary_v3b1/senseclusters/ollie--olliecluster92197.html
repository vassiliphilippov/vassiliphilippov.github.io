ollie

noun
skateboarding jump trick
Pronunciation: / ˈɑli / (AmE), / ˈɒli / (BrE)
a skateboarding trick in which the rider jumps into the air with the board without using their hands

Examples

A perfect ollie
He landed his first ollie after weeks of practice at the skate park.
The crowd cheered when she performed a high ollie over the stairs.
Learning to ollie is an essential skill for beginner skateboarders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kickflip - a skateboarding trick where the board flips in the air
  • synonym: manual - a skateboarding trick balancing on two wheels
