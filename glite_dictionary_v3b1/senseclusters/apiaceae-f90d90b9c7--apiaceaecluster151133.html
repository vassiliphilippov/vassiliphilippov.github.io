Apiaceae

noun
the parsley or carrot family of plants
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.piˈeɪ.si.i /; / ˌeɪpiˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.pɪˈeɪ.siː.iː /; / ˌeɪpɪˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
a large family of plants with umbrella-shaped clusters of small flowers, including celery, carrot, parsley, dill, and similar species
