subhuman

adjective
less than human; undignified or inhumane
7.5 6.5
Pronunciation: /sʌb.ˈhjuː.mən/ /; / /sʌb.ˈjuː.mən/ (AmE), /sʌb.ˈhjuː.mən/ /; / /sʌb.ˈjuː.mən/ (BrE)
regarded as less than human or not deserving the rights or dignity of a human being

Examples

Subhuman treatment
Many activists protest the subhuman living conditions found in overcrowded refugee camps.
Some early scientists classified certain primates as subhuman, though this view is now considered outdated.
The dictator's regime treated its political prisoners as subhuman beings, denying them basic rights.
