PPA

noun
progressive language disorder
9.9
Pronunciation: / pi pi eɪ / (AmE), / piː piː eɪ / (BrE)
a neurological syndrome characterized by a gradual and progressive loss of language abilities

Examples

PPA diagnosis
The neurologist explained that PPA would gradually make it harder for her to find the right words.
Support groups for people with PPA can help families understand the condition.
Early symptoms of PPA often include difficulty naming everyday objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphasia - a disorder affecting the ability to communicate
  • synonym: dementia - a decline in cognitive function, including memory and thinking
