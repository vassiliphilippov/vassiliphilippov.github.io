Lakeland terrier

noun
breed of wire-haired terrier dog
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪk.lənd ˈteɹ.i.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪk.lənd ˈteɹ.i.ə / (BrE)
a small, hardy breed of wire-haired terrier, originally from England's Lake District, used for hunting and as a companion dog

Examples

A Lakeland terrier dog
She adopted a lively Lakeland terrier from the animal shelter last year.
The Lakeland terrier excelled in the dog show for its agility and temperament.
Their family has raised Lakeland terriers for several generations.
