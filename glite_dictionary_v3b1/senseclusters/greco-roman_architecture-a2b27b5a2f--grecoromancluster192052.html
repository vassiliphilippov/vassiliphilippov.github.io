Greco-Roman architecture

noun
architecture inspired by ancient Greece and Rome
Pronunciation: / ˌɡriːkoʊˈroʊmən ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃər /; / ˌɡreɪkoʊˈroʊmən ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃər / (AmE), / ˌɡriːkəʊˈrəʊmən ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃə / (BrE)
a style of architecture influenced by the designs and principles of ancient Greece and Rome, characterized by columns, arches, and symmetry
