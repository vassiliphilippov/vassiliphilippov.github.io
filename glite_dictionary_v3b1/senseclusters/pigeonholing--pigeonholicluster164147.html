pigeonholing

noun
the act of categorizing simplistically
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒənˌhoʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒənˌhəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or practice of categorizing people or things, often in a simplistic or restrictive way

Examples

Avoid pigeonholing people
Some people resist pigeonholing based on nationality or background.
The pigeonholing of artists makes it hard for them to explore new styles.
The report criticized the pigeonholing of students into rigid academic tracks.
