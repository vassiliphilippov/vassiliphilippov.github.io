rand

noun
South African currency unit
Pronunciation: / rænd / (AmE), / rænd / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in South Africa, equal to 100 cents

Examples

South African rand
She exchanged her dollars for South African rand before traveling.
The rand strengthened against the dollar after the announcement.
The price of the new car is 300,000 rand.
