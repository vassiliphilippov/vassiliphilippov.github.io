wood rat

noun
small rodent with furry tail and large ears
Pronunciation: / wʊd ræt / (AmE), / wʊd ræt / (BrE)
A small rodent of the Northern Hemisphere, typically with soft grey-and-white fur, a furry tail, and large ears, belonging to the genus Neotoma or related genera.

Examples

A wood rat animal
A wood rat may sometimes be referred to as a pack rat because of its habit of collecting objects.
Researchers found that the wood rat is a host for certain species of ticks.
The wood rat constructs elaborate nests from twigs and other debris.
