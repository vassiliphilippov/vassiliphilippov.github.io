crum

noun
small food fragment
Pronunciation: / krʌm / (AmE), / krʌm / (BrE)
an old or dialect word for a small piece or fragment of bread or food

Examples

A crum of bread
He searched the table for a single crum to satisfy his hunger.
Not a crum remained after the children finished their meal.
She brushed every crum off the counter before cleaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crumb - a very small piece of bread or cake
