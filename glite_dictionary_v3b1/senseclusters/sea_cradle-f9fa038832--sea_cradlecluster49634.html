sea cradle

noun
marine mollusk with eight shell plates
6.8
Pronunciation: / siː ˈkræd.əl / (AmE), / siː ˈkreɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
a primitive, elongated marine mollusk with bilateral symmetry and a mantle covered by eight calcareous plates; also known as a chiton

Examples

The sea cradle mollusk
Marine biologists study the sea cradle to understand mollusk evolution.
The sea cradle slowly grazed on algae on the submerged boulder.
While exploring the tide pools, we found a sea cradle attached to the rocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chiton - a marine mollusk with a shell of eight overlapping plates
