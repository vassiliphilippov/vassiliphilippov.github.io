trawlable

adjective
can be trawled
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɔləbəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɔːləbəl / (BrE)
able to be fished or harvested using a trawl net

Examples

Trawlable waters
The fishing company only operates in trawlable waters where their nets can reach the seabed.
Environmental regulations protect some areas that are not trawlable to preserve marine habitats.
Maps show which parts of the ocean floor are considered trawlable for commercial fishing.
