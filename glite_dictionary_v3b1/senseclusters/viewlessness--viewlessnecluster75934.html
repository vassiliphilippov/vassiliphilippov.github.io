viewlessness

noun
lack of opinion
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈvjuː.ləs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈvjuː.ləs.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of having no opinion, position, or point of view on a particular matter

Examples

Viewlessness in debates

In the debate, his viewlessness was seen as a sign of indecision rather than open-mindedness.
