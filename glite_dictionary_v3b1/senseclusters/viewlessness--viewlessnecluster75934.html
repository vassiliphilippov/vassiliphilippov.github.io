viewlessness

noun
lack of opinion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvjuːləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈvjuːləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of having no opinion, position, or point of view on a particular matter

Examples

Viewlessness in debates
Her viewlessness on controversial topics made her a trusted mediator among her friends.
The politician's viewlessness disappointed voters who wanted clear positions on important issues.
In the debate, his viewlessness was seen as a sign of indecision rather than open-mindedness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bias - a strong opinion or preference for one side
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: neutrality - the state of not supporting or helping either side in a conflict or disagreement
