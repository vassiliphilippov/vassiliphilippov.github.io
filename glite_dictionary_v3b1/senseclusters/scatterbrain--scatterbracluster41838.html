scatterbrain

noun
disorganized, forgetful person
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskæt̬ərˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈskætəˌbreɪn / (BrE)
a person who is forgetful, disorganized, or unable to concentrate

Examples

A scatterbrain person
Despite her talent, Jane is a bit of a scatterbrain and often misses appointments.
Don't leave the planning to him—he's too much of a scatterbrain.
I'm such a scatterbrain that I forgot my keys again this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airhead - a silly or unintelligent person
  • antonym: organizer - a person who is systematic and orderly
  • synonym: space cadet - a person who is absent-minded or inattentive
