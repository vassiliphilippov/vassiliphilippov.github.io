unstopping

verb
to remove a blockage
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
removing a stopper or obstruction from something so that it is no longer blocked

Examples

Unstopping a drain
The plumber's careful unstopping of the sink pipe prevented further damage.
After unstopping the drain, the water flowed freely again.
She spent the afternoon unstopping the old fireplace to let the air circulate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: block - to obstruct or stop something
  • synonym: unblock - to remove an obstruction from something
