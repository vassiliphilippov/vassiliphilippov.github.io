ABV

noun
alcohol percentage
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ biː ˈviː / (AmE), / ˌeɪ biː ˈviː / (BrE)
a measure showing the percentage of alcohol in a drink by volume

Examples

ABV of wine
The wine has an ABV of 12%, which is typical for this type.
Always check the ABV on the label before buying a new beer.
Drinks with a higher ABV can affect you more quickly.
