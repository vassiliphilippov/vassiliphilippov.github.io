Synonyms

  • in front
    1.0 120/m
    in front shows position near the front relative to another object, while ahead means at or toward a further forward place.
  • forward
    1.0 15/m
    forward points toward the front or in the direction of movement, while ahead means at or to a position further forward.
  • dead ahead
    3.4 0.3/m
    dead ahead means directly straight in front with no angle, while ahead can mean simply more forward or further along.
  • out front
    3.4 0.4/m
    out front often means leading or in charge at the front, while ahead focuses on simple forward position or movement.
  • in front of
    1.0 130/m
    in front of is used with an object to show location before it, while ahead often stands alone to mean forward.
