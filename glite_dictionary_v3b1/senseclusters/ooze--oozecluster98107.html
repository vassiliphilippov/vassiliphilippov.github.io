ooze

noun
slow seepage or movement of a substance
5.2 6.0
Pronunciation: / uz / (AmE), / uːz / (BrE)
the slow movement or seepage of a liquid or substance, especially through small openings or porous material

Examples

A slow ooze
A thick ooze started to spread across the floor after the container broke.
Slow ooze from the cave walls created glistening streaks along the limestone.
The ooze of water from the cracked pipe was barely noticeable at first.
