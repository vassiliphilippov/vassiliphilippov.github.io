walk-on

adjective
nonspeaking acting role
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔk ˌɔn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːk ɒn / (BrE)
(of a part or role in a play, film, etc.) not involving speech or spoken lines; having no spoken dialogue.

Examples

Walk-on role
He started his acting career with a walk-on role in a local theatre production.
Many actors begin with walk-on appearances before landing speaking parts.
She had a walk-on part in the movie, appearing without any lines.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bit part - small acting role with little or no lines
  • synonym: nonspeaking - (of a role) not involving spoken lines
