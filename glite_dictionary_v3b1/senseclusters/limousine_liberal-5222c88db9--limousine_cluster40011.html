limousine liberal

noun
wealthy liberal seen as out of touch
8.5 9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪməˌzin ˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˌlɪməˌziːn ˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
A wealthy person who supports liberal political views or causes, often regarded as being out of touch with the needs and problems of ordinary people.

Examples

A limousine liberal
Critics accuse her of being a limousine liberal who has no idea what ordinary families go through.
Some voters resent advice from limousine liberals who have never faced real financial hardship.
The mayor's expensive lifestyle led many to label him a limousine liberal.

Related Terms
