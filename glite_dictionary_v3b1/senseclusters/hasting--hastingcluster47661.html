hasting

noun
act of hurrying
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hurrying or moving quickly, especially in an archaic or literary context

Examples

Sudden hasting
Their hasting to the station left many small tasks undone in the house.
In his hasting, he forgot to lock the door behind him.
The poem describes the hasting of travelers eager to reach their destination before nightfall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delay - the act of making something late or slower
  • synonym: haste - speed or urgency of movement or action
