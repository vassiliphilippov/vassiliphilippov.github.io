avatar

noun
incarnation of a Hindu deity
2.9 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈævəˌtɑr / (AmE), / ˈævətɑː / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) a manifestation of a deity or released soul in bodily form on earth; an incarnation, especially of Vishnu.

Examples

Hindu avatar
According to legend, Krishna is considered an avatar of the god Vishnu.
Many ancient stories describe different avatars sent to restore balance on earth.
The avatar of Vishnu appeared to save the world from destruction.
