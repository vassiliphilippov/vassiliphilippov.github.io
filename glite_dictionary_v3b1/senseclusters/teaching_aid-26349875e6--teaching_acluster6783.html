teaching aid

noun
material or equipment for teaching
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːtʃɪŋ eɪd / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃɪŋ eɪd / (BrE)
an object, material, or piece of equipment used by teachers to facilitate learning and improve the teaching process

Examples

A teaching aid tool
He created a simple teaching aid out of colored paper for the math lesson.
Interactive whiteboards are a popular teaching aid in modern classrooms.
The teacher used a teaching aid to help explain the lesson to her students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visual aid - materials, such as charts or models, used to enhance learning visually
