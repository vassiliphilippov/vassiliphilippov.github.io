garderobe

noun
cloakroom or wardrobe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdəˌroʊb / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdəˌrəʊb / (BrE)
a room or cupboard for storing coats and other clothing, especially in public places like theaters

Examples

Theater garderobe
She left her coat in the theater's garderobe before taking her seat.
The museum provides a garderobe for visitors to store their bags and jackets.
Please collect your belongings from the garderobe before the building closes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloakroom - a room where coats and other belongings may be left temporarily
  • synonym: wardrobe - a large cupboard or closet for storing clothes
