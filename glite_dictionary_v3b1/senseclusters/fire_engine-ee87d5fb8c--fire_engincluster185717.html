fire engine

noun
vehicle for transporting firefighters and equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ˌɛn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌɛn.dʒɪn / (BrE)
a large vehicle equipped with firefighting equipment that is used to transport firefighters and tools to the scene of a fire

Examples

A fire engine arrives
A fire engine arrived within minutes to put out the blaze.
He dreamed of becoming a firefighter and riding in a bright red fire engine.
The children waved excitedly as the fire engine raced down the street with its siren blaring.
