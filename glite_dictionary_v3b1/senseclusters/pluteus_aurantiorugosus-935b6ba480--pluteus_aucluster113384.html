Pluteus aurantiorugosus

noun
brightly colored scarlet-cap mushroom species
Pronunciation: / ˈpluːti.əs ɔːˌrænti.oʊruːˈɡoʊsəs / (AmE), / ˈpluːtjuːəs ɔːˌræn.ti.əʊ.ruːˈɡəʊ.səs / (BrE)
A rare species of mushroom with a striking scarlet cap and slender stem, found on decaying wood.

Examples

The Pluteus aurantiorugosus mushroom
Because of its vivid scarlet color, the Pluteus aurantiorugosus is a favorite subject for mushroom photographers.
Mycologists were excited to find a Pluteus aurantiorugosus growing on a decaying log in the forest.
The brilliant cap of the Pluteus aurantiorugosus makes it easily distinguishable among woodland mushrooms.
