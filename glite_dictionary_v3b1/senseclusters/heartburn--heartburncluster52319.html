heartburn

noun
burning chest sensation caused by acid reflux
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌbɝn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌbɜːn / (BrE)
A painful burning sensation in the chest caused by stomach acid rising into the esophagus, often occurring after eating.

Examples

Severe heartburn pain
Eating spicy foods sometimes gives me heartburn in the middle of the night.
If you often experience heartburn, you should consider seeing a doctor.
She bought some antacids to relieve her heartburn after dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acid reflux - backflow of stomach acid into the esophagus
  • synonym: indigestion - discomfort in the stomach associated with difficulty in digesting food
