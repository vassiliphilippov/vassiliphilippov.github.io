keep

noun
means or cost of self-support
Pronunciation: / kip / (AmE), / kiːp / (BrE)
the cost or means of supporting oneself, especially in terms of food, shelter, or other necessities

Examples

Earn your keep
After losing his job, he struggled to provide his children's keep.
The hostel provides food and accommodation, but you must work to earn your keep.
With rising costs, he found it hard to pay for his own keep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: livelihood - means of securing the necessities of life
  • synonym: support - maintenance or subsistence provided for someone
