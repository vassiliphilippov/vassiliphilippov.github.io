CFI

noun
flight instructor
7.8
Pronunciation: / si ɛf aɪ / (AmE), / siː ɛf aɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Certified Flight Instructor, a person qualified to teach others how to fly aircraft

Examples

CFI flight instructor
After earning his license, he worked as a CFI at a busy airport.
The CFI explained the safety procedures before the first flight lesson.
Many pilots remember their first solo flight with their CFI watching from the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flight instructor - a person who teaches others how to fly aircraft
  • antonym: student pilot - a person learning to fly an aircraft
