Synonyms

  • Golgi body
    6.3 0.00/m
    Golgi body is an alternative common name for the organelle, while Golgi apparatus is the formal scientific term for the same structure.
  • Golgi complex
    4.7 0.01/m
    Golgi complex names the same membranous network in cells, while Golgi apparatus is the widely used standard term in biology.
  • dictyosome
    9.9 0.00/m
    dictyosome refers to one Golgi unit in plant and animal secretory cells, while Golgi apparatus names the whole organelle.
