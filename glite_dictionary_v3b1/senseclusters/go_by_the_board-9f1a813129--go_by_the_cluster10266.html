go by the board

verb
be disregarded or ignored
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ baɪ ðə bɔrd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ baɪ ðə bɔːd / (BrE)
(of a plan, proposal, or rule) to be rejected, ignored, or disregarded.

Examples

Plans go by the board
All safety precautions went by the board during the emergency.
Her earlier promises go by the board once she gets what she wants.
Many traditional customs have gone by the board in recent years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disregard - to pay no attention to; ignore
  • synonym: ignore - to refuse to take notice of
