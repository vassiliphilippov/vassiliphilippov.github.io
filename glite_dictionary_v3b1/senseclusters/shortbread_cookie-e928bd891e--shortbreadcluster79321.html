shortbread cookie

noun
rich, buttery biscuit/cookie
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt.brɛd ˈkʊk.i / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.brɛd ˈkʊk.i / (BrE)
A rich, thick biscuit made with a large amount of butter, sugar, and flour, typically crisp and crumbly.

Examples
