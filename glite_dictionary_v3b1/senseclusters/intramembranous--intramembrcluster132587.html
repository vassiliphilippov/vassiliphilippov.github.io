intramembranous

adjective
within a membrane
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˌmɛmˈbreɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˌmɛmˈbreɪnəs / (BrE)
occurring or taking place within a membrane

Examples

Intramembranous ossification
Bone develops through intramembranous ossification in parts of the skull and jaw.
The process of intramembranous bone formation is essential during fetal development.
Researchers studied intramembranous pathways to understand how certain bones grow.
