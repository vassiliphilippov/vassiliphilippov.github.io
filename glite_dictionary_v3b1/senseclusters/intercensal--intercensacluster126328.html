intercensal

adjective
between censuses
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɛnsəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛnsəl / (BrE)
occurring or relating to the period between two official population censuses

Examples

Intercensal population data
The agency published intercensal data to help track demographic changes between official counts.
Researchers used intercensal estimates to analyze population growth trends.
Planning decisions often rely on intercensal statistics when recent census data is unavailable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: census - an official count or survey of a population
