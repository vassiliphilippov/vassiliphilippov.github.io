understandably

adverb
reasonably; justifiably
3.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈstæn.də.bliː/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈstæn.də.bliː/ (BrE)

Examples

He was understandably upset
He was understandably upset when he heard the disappointing news.
The team was understandably proud of their hard work and success.
She reacted understandably when her flight was suddenly canceled.
