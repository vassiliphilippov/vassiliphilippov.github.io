glowy

adjective
having a glowing or radiant appearance
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.i / (BrE)
having a soft, radiant appearance, as if glowing with light or health.

Examples

A glowy finish
He prefers a glowy finish to his makeup rather than a matte look.
Her skin looked especially glowy after using the new moisturizer.
The city skyline was glowy with lights during the festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or sheen
  • synonym: radiant - sending out light; shining or glowing brightly
