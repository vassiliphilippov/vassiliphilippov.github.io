robot bomb

noun
jet-propelled unmanned missile with a bomb
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌbɑt bɑm / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbɒt bɒm / (BrE)
a jet-propelled unmanned missile with wings, designed to deliver an explosive payload to a target

Examples

A robot bomb missile
Allied forces developed new defenses to intercept the incoming robot bombs before they reached their targets.
During World War II, London was attacked repeatedly by the German robot bombs.
The museum has a display featuring an early robot bomb and its guidance system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flying bomb - an unmanned aerial vehicle designed to deliver bombs
  • synonym: guided missile - a missile whose path can be controlled during flight
