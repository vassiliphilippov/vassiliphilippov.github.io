dichotomist

adjective
dividing into two parts
9.6
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈkɑː.tə.mɪst/ /; / /daɪ.ˈkɔː.tə.mɪst/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈkɒt.ə.mɪst/ (BrE)
characterized by dividing or viewing things as two opposing or mutually exclusive parts.

Examples

Dichotomist thinking style
The article took a dichotomist approach, presenting only two possible outcomes.
Her dichotomist thinking made it hard for her to see any middle ground.
Critics argue that a dichotomist perspective oversimplifies complex social issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binary - involving two parts or options
  • antonym: nuanced - showing subtle differences or distinctions
