revegetation

noun
natural plant regrowth
5.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ve.dʒə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.ve.dʒə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ve.dʒə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.ve.dʒə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of plants growing again in an area where they were lost, either naturally or with human help

Examples

The revegetation of the burned forest took several years and required careful planning.

Successful revegetation can attract wildlife back to previously barren land.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deforestation - the removal of trees and plant life from an area
  • synonym: reforestation - the process of planting trees to restore a forest
  • synonym: regeneration - the process of renewal or restoration, especially of plant life
  • synonym: restoration - the act of returning something to its original condition
