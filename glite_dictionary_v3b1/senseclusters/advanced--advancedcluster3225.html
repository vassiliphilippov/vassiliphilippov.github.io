advanced

adjective
positioned ahead; leading
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈvænst / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnst / (BrE)
positioned ahead of others in place or time; situated in the front or leading position.

Examples

Advanced team
The advanced team arrived hours before the main group to prepare the site.
Their advanced position gave them a strategic advantage over the rest of the army.
We set up camp at the most advanced point reached by the expedition.
