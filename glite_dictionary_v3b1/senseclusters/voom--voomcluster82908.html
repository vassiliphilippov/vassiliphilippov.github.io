voom

noun
rushing sound or movement
9.9 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / vuÀêm / (AmE), / vuÀêm / (BrE)
a sudden rushing movement or the loud sound made by something moving very quickly, especially a vehicle or engine

Examples

A loud voom
The car sped by with a loud voom that startled everyone on the sidewalk.
As the rocket launched, a deep voom echoed across the valley.
We heard a sudden voom as the train rushed through the station.

Synonyms

  • vroom
    8.8 0.59/m
    vroom is the loud roaring sound of a fast-running engine, while voom can mean either that noise or a sudden rushing movement.
  • whoosh
    4.2 1.16/m
    whoosh is a quick rushing sound of air or liquid, while voom often refers to vehicle or engine movement and its loud sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whoosh - a rushing sound made by something moving fast
  • synonym: zoom - a buzzing or humming sound of something moving quickly
