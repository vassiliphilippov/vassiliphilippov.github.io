offshore drilling rig

noun
platform for extracting oil at sea
4.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːf.ʃɔːr ˈdrɪl.ɪŋ rɪg/ /; / /ˈɑːf.ʃɑːr ˈdrɪl.ɪŋ rɪg/ (AmE), /ˈɒf.ʃɔː ˈdrɪl.ɪŋ rɪg/ (BrE)
A large platform located at sea, used for extracting oil or gas from beneath the seabed.

Examples

An offshore drilling rig
After months at sea, the offshore drilling rig finally struck a productive oil reserve.
Strict safety protocols are enforced on every offshore drilling rig to prevent accidents and oil spills.
The crew boarded the offshore drilling rig early in the morning to begin their shift.
