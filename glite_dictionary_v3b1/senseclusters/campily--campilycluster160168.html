campily

adverb
exaggeratedly theatrical
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpɪli / (AmE), / ˈkæmpɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is exaggerated, theatrical, or deliberately artificial, often for humorous effect

Examples

To act campily
The comedian danced campily across the stage, making the audience laugh with every move.
She decorated her apartment campily, using bright colors and flashy ornaments everywhere.
The host greeted the guests campily, waving his arms and speaking in an exaggerated voice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subtly - in a delicate or understated way
  • synonym: theatrically - in a dramatic or exaggerated manner
