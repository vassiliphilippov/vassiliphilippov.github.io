kneehole

noun
space under desk
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈniːˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈniːhəʊl / (BrE)
an open space beneath a desk or table designed to accommodate the knees of a seated person

Examples

A kneehole desk
The antique desk features a large kneehole for comfortable seating.
She placed her chair in front of the kneehole to start working on her laptop.
A modern office table usually has a wide kneehole to allow easy movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat surface for writing or working
  • synonym: table - a piece of furniture with a flat top and one or more legs
