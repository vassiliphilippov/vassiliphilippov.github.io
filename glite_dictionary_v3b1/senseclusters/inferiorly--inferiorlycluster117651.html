inferiorly

adverb
in a lower position
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriɚli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriəli / (BrE)
in a position lower or beneath something else, especially in anatomical contexts

Examples

Inferiorly placed organ
In the diagram, the liver is situated inferiorly to the heart.
The wound was located inferiorly to the knee joint.
The kidneys are positioned inferiorly relative to the lungs in the human body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: below - in a lower position than something else
  • antonym: superiorly - in a position higher or above something else
