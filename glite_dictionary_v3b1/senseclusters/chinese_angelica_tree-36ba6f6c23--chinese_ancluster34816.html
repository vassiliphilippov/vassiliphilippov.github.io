Chinese angelica tree

noun
tree species native to China
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪˌniz ænˈdʒɛlɪkə tri / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪˌniːz anˈdʒɛlɪkə triː / (BrE)
a small tree native to China, similar to the American angelica tree but with fewer prickles, cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
