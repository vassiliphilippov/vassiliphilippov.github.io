ombrellino

noun
ceremonial umbrella
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːmbreˈliːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɒmbreˈliːnəʊ / (BrE)
a small ceremonial umbrella used in certain Catholic religious processions

Examples

Ceremonial ombrellino
During the procession, the bishop held the ombrellino above the sacred relic.
The ornate ombrellino was decorated with gold and red fabric.
In some ceremonies, an ombrellino is carried to honor the presence of the Eucharist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: umbrella - a device for protection from rain or sun
