unthought-of

adjective
not considered or imagined
8.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɔt ʌv / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɔːt ɒv / (BrE)
not considered or imagined; so unexpected as to have not occurred to someone before.

Examples

An unthought-of surprise
His promotion came as an unthought-of surprise to everyone in the office.
She found the answer in an unthought-of source while researching.
The treasure was discovered in an unthought-of corner of the old library.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreseen - known in advance or predicted
  • synonym: unanticipated - not anticipated or foreseen
  • synonym: unexpected - not expected or anticipated
