addictive

adjective
causing addiction or dependence
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪktɪv / (AmE), / əˈdɪktɪv / (BrE)
causing or likely to cause addiction; producing a compulsive need or habit.

Examples

addictive habit
Many people find social media to be surprisingly addictive.
Nicotine is the most addictive substance found in cigarettes.
Some prescription medications can be highly addictive if not used correctly.
