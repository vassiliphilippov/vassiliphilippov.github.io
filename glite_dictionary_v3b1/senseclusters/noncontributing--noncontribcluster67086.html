noncontributing

adjective
not making payments
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtrɪbjutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
not supplying required payments or contributions, especially to a fund, organization, or group effort

Examples

Noncontributing pension employee
Employees who are noncontributing to the pension plan may not receive full retirement benefits.
The charity asked all noncontributing members to consider making a donation this year.
After becoming noncontributing, he lost his voting rights in the union.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contributing - supplying required payments or support
