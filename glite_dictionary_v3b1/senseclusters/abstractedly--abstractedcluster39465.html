abstractedly

adverb
in a distracted or absentminded way
4.2 6.2
Pronunciation: / əbˈstræktɪdli / (AmE), / əbˈstraktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows someone is thinking deeply about something else and not paying attention to what is happening around them; absentmindedly.

Examples

To think abstractedly
During the meeting, Sarah stared abstractedly out the window, thinking of her holiday plans.
He drummed his fingers abstractedly on the table, lost in thought.
She nodded abstractedly while reading her book, barely noticing his question.
