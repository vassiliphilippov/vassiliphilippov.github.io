unmistakable

adjective
impossible to mistake; obvious
3.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.mɪ.ˈsteɪ.kə.bəl/ /; / /ə.n.mɪ.ˈsteɪ.kə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.mɪ.ˈsteɪ.kə.bəl/ /; / /ə.n.mɪ.ˈsteɪ.kə.bəl/ (BrE)
