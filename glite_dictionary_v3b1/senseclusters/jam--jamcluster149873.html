jam

noun
a favorite song or musical performance
Pronunciation: / dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
(informal, slang) A song or musical performance, especially one that is a favorite or particularly enjoyable.

Examples

My favorite jam
That new single is definitely my jam right now.
They played an old jam that got everyone singing along.
Whenever his jam comes on, he can't help but dance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: track - a single song or piece of music on an album
  • synonym: tune - a piece of music, especially a memorable or popular song
