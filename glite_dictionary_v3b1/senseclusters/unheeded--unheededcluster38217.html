unheeded

adjective
not noticed or disregarded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhiːdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhiːdɪd / (BrE)
(of advice, warnings, requests, etc.) not noticed or disregarded; not given attention.

Examples

An unheeded warning
He shouted for help, but his cries remained unheeded.
Her advice was often unheeded by her colleagues.
The warning signs went unheeded until it was too late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ignored - not given attention; disregarded
  • antonym: noticed - given attention; observed
