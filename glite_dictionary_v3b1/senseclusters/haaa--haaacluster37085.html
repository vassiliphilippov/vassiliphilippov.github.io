haaa

interjection
written laughter
Pronunciation: / hɑː / (AmE), / hɑː / (BrE)
an informal written expression used to show laughter or amusement, especially in text messages or online

Examples

Text haaa for laughter
She replied with haaa when her friend sent a funny meme.
After hearing the silly story, he just typed haaa in the chat.
Whenever something makes him laugh online, he writes haaa instead of using emojis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haha - an informal written expression of laughter
  • synonym: lol - an abbreviation for 'laugh out loud', used to show amusement
