nutritiousness

noun
quality of being nourishing
2.8
Pronunciation: / nuˈtrɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being nourishing and promoting healthy growth

Examples

The nutritiousness of food
Comparing the nutritiousness of different snacks can help you make healthier choices.
She always chooses cereals based on their nutritiousness rather than just their taste.
The nutritiousness of a meal is an important consideration for athletes and children.

Related Terms

  • antonym: malnutrition - lack of proper nutrition
  • synonym: nutrition - the process of obtaining food necessary for health and growth
