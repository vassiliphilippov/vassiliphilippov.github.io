dough-faced

adjective
having a pale, lifeless face
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈdəʊˌfeɪst / (BrE)
having a pale, dull, or lifeless facial appearance, often as if tired or worn out.

Examples

A dough-faced look
After staying up all night, she appeared dough-faced at breakfast.
He always looked a bit dough-faced during the gloomy winter months.
The office workers returned from the late shift looking dough-faced and exhausted.
