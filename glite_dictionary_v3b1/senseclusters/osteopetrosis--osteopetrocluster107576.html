osteopetrosis

noun
rare inherited bone disorder with increased density
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…ëÀêstio äp…ôÀàtro äs…™s / (AmE), / Àå…ísti…ô äp…ôÀàtr…ô äs…™s / (BrE)
A rare inherited disorder characterized by abnormally increased bone density, which can lead to obliteration of the bone marrow cavity and other complications.

Examples

Rare osteopetrosis disorder
Researchers are studying genetic mutations responsible for osteopetrosis in hopes of finding a cure.
The diagnosis of osteopetrosis was confirmed by X-ray imaging showing unusually dense bones.
Treatment options for osteopetrosis are limited, often requiring careful management of symptoms.

Synonyms

  • Albers-Schonberg disease
    9.6 0.00/m
    Albers-Schonberg disease describes an inherited condition with marked bone density increase and possible marrow cavity loss, while osteopetrosis is the general rare disorder term.
  • marble bones disease
    8.9 0.00/m
    marble bones disease means bones become very dense and hard and may lose marrow cavities, while osteopetrosis is the rare inherited disorder that causes this.
