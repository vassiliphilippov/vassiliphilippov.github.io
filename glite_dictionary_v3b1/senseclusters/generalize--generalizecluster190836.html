generalize

verb
ML: perform well on unseen data
Pronunciation: / c8d9bn59r59ccla6az / (AmE), / c8d9bn59r59la6az / (BrE)
(in machine learning and AI) to apply patterns learned from training data so a model performs well on new, unseen data.

Examples

AI generalizes to data
The neural network was trained to generalise to new images, not just the training data.
If the model generalises poorly, it will fail when presented with real-world examples.
After adding more data, the classifier generalised and correctly labeled unseen samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalize - US spelling of 'generalise'; same verb form used in technical writing.
  • antonym: overfit - to model the training data too closely so that performance on unseen data is poor; often considered the opposite of good generalisation in ML.
