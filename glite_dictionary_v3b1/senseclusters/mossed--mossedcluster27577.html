mossed

adjective
covered in moss
5.1
Pronunciation: / mɔst / (AmE), / mɒst / (BrE)
covered with or overgrown by moss.

Examples

Mossed stone surface
The ancient statue was almost completely mossed after years in the damp forest.
We walked along a mossed path that felt soft under our feet.
The roof of the cottage became mossed over many rainy seasons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - not covered by anything; lacking moss or vegetation
  • synonym: mossy - covered with or resembling moss
