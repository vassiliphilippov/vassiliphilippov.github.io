attunement

noun
process of adjustment
7.9
Pronunciation: / əˈtunmənt / (AmE), / əˈtjuːnmənt / (BrE)
the process of adjusting, aligning, or becoming receptive to achieve harmony or connection

Examples

Musical attunement
The attunement of the choir's voices created a beautiful harmony during the performance.
Proper attunement of the radio allowed us to hear the broadcast clearly.
Therapists often speak about the importance of emotional attunement in healthy relationships.
Effective communication requires attunement to the feelings and needs of others.
The musician's attunement to the orchestra allowed for a flawless performance.
Therapists often work on attunement with their clients to build trust and understanding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptation - the process of adjusting to new conditions
  • synonym: alignment - the arrangement in a straight line or in correct relative positions
  • antonym: discord - lack of harmony or agreement
