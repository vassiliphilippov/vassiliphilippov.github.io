bliss

verb
to feel great joy
Pronunciation: / blɪs / (AmE), / blɪs / (BrE)
to experience a state of extreme happiness or joy, often in a peaceful or contented way.

Examples

To bliss in silence
After the ceremony, she blissed for an hour, content and smiling without speaking.
He blissed out on the beach, listening to the sound of the waves.
They blissed together in the quiet garden, enjoying the peaceful afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delight - to take great pleasure or joy
  • synonym: rejoice - to feel or show great joy or delight
  • antonym: suffer - to experience pain or distress
