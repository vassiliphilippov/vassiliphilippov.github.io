tatou

noun
South American armadillo
Pronunciation: / tæˈtuː / (AmE), / taˈtuː / (BrE)
a type of armadillo native to South America, especially the giant armadillo known for its large protective shell

Examples

The tatou armadillo
The tatou is rarely seen during the day because it is mostly nocturnal.
Researchers studied the habitat of the tatou in the Amazon rainforest.
The giant tatou can dig deep burrows to escape predators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armadillo - a burrowing mammal with a hard, protective shell
