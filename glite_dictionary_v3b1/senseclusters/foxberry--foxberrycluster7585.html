foxberry

noun
low evergreen shrub with red edible berries

A low evergreen shrub of northern temperate regions, especially in Europe, Asia, and America, that bears small red edible berries.

Examples

The foxberry bush
In northern forests, animals often feed on the abundant foxberries in autumn.
The tart flavor of the foxberry makes it perfect for jams and desserts.
We picked foxberries from the shrubs growing along the rocky hillside.
