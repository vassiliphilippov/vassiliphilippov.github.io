let simmer

verb
allow to cook at low heat
Pronunciation: / lɛt ˈsɪmɚ / (AmE), / lɛt ˈsɪmə / (BrE)
to allow food to cook slowly at a low temperature, just below boiling

Examples

To let simmer stew
After you bring the stew to a boil, let simmer for at least an hour to develop the flavors.
Please let simmer until all the vegetables are tender and fully cooked.
The recipe said to let simmer uncovered so the sauce would thicken.
