nonseriousness

noun
lack of seriousness
6.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɪriəsnəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɪəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not being serious, earnest, or sincere

Examples

Nonseriousness at work
Her nonseriousness made it difficult for the team to focus on the project.
The teacher was frustrated by the students' nonseriousness during the important discussion.
He tried to hide his nerves with a display of nonseriousness at the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frivolity - lack of seriousness or sense
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious or earnest
