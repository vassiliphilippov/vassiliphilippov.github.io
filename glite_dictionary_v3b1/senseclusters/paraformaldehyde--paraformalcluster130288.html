paraformaldehyde

noun
chemical tissue preservative
9.4
Pronunciation: /pær.ə.fɔːr.məl.ˈdeɪ.haɪd/ /; / /pær.ə.fɑːr.məl.ˈdeɪ.haɪd/ (AmE), /pær.ə.fɔː.məl.ˈdeɪ.haɪd/ (BrE)
