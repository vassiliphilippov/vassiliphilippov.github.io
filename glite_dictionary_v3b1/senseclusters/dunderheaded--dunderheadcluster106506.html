dunderheaded

adjective
very foolish
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌndərˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʌndəhɛdɪd / (BrE)

Examples

A dunderheaded mistake
It was a dunderheaded mistake to leave the keys in the car.
His dunderheaded comments made everyone in the meeting roll their eyes.
She realized her dunderheaded decision had caused more problems than it solved.
