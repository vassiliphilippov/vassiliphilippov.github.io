bloodweed

noun
plant with red sap
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌwiːd / (BrE)
a common name for several plants that have reddish sap or red-colored flowers

Examples

Red bloodweed plant
The bloodweed added a splash of color to the otherwise green meadow.
Gardeners often admire the striking red flowers of bloodweed in the summer.
Some species called bloodweed are known for their bright, reddish sap.

Related Terms
