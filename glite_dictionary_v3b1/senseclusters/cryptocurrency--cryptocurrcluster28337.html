cryptocurrency

noun
digital currency using cryptography
1.5 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪp.toʊ.kʌr.ən.siː/ /; / /ˈkrɪp.tə.kʌr.ən.siː/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪp.təʊ.kʌr.ən.siː/ /; / /ˈkrɪp.tə.kʌr.ən.siː/ (BrE)
A digital currency in which transactions are verified and records maintained by a decentralized system using cryptography, rather than by a centralized authority.

Examples

A cryptocurrency coin
Bitcoin was the first widely recognized cryptocurrency to gain global attention.
Many people invest in cryptocurrency hoping its value will increase over time.
Regulations around cryptocurrency vary significantly from country to country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital currency - a form of currency stored and transacted online
  • antonym: fiat currency - government-issued currency not backed by a commodity
