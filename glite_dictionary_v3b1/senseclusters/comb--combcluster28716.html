comb

verb
search thoroughly
Pronunciation: / koʊm / (AmE), / kəʊm / (BrE)
to search a place or area very carefully in order to find something

Examples

To comb the files
Detectives have combed through hundreds of files without finding a clue.
The police combed the entire park for evidence after the incident.
Volunteers combed the beach, hoping to find the lost dog.
