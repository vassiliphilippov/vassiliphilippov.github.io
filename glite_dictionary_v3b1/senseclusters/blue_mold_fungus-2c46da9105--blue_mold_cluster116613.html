blue mold fungus

noun
fungus causing blue mold in tobacco
8.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /bluː moʊld ˈfʌŋ.gəs/ (AmE), /bluː məʊld ˈfʌŋ.gəs/ (BrE)
a fungus that causes a serious disease in tobacco plants, producing bluish-grey mildew on the undersides of leaves
