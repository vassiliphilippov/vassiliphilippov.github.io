blue mold fungus

noun
fungus causing blue mold in tobacco
Pronunciation: / blu moʊld ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / bluː məʊld ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes a serious disease in tobacco plants, producing bluish-grey mildew on the undersides of leaves

Examples

Blue mold fungus on leaves
Blue mold fungus is particularly destructive during humid, rainy weather.
Researchers are developing resistant strains to combat blue mold fungus.
The outbreak of blue mold fungus threatened the region's entire tobacco crop.
