punt

verb
kick the ball before it hits the ground
5.3
Pronunciation: / pʌnt / (AmE), / pʌnt / (BrE)
(in sports) to kick the ball by dropping it from the hands and striking it before it touches the ground, especially in football or rugby.

Examples

To punt the ball
She watched the player expertly punt during the rugby match.
The quarterback decided to punt the ball on fourth down.
They punted the ball deep into the opponent's territory.

  • synonym: kick - to strike with the foot
