Russian Revolution

noun
1917 Bolshevik-led revolution in Russia
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌ.ʃən rev.ə.ˈluː.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈrʌ.ʃən rev.ə.ˈluː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the revolution led by the Bolsheviks under Lenin in November 1917, resulting in the overthrow of the Russian government and the establishment of Soviet rule

Examples

The Russian Revolution of 1917
Lenin played a key role during the Russian Revolution of 1917.
Many books have been written about the causes and aftermath of the Russian Revolution.
The Russian Revolution dramatically changed the course of Russian and world history.
