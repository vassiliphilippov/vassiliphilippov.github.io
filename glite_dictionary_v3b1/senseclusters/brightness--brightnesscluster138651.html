brightness

noun
quality of being luminous or reflecting light
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being bright, especially how much light something gives off or how close it is to white
