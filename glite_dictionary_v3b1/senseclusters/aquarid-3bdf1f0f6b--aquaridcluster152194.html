Aquarid

noun
meteor shower name
Pronunciation: / ˈækwərɪd /; / əˈkwɛrɪd / (AmE), / ˈakwərɪd /; / əˈkwɛərɪd / (BrE)
a meteor shower (or a meteor from it) named after the constellation Aquarius, such as the Eta Aquarids or Delta Aquarids
