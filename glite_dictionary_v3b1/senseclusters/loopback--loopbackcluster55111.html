loopback

noun
testing connector
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈluːp.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈluːp.bæk/ (BrE)
a connector, cable, or configuration that routes a device's output signal back into its input for testing or diagnosis

Examples

Loopback cable test
The engineer used a loopback cable to check if the network port was functioning correctly.
A loopback test can help identify problems in communication hardware without external devices.
Before shipping the equipment, technicians performed a loopback to ensure all signals were transmitted properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagnostic - a tool or procedure for identifying faults
  • synonym: self-test - a test performed by a device on itself
