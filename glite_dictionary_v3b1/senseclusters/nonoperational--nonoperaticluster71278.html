nonoperational

adjective
not functioning
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɑpəˈreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
not working, functioning, or currently in use

Examples

Nonoperational equipment
The security cameras were nonoperational during the power outage.
Due to maintenance, the swimming pool is currently nonoperational.
Several machines in the factory became nonoperational after the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: functional - working or able to operate
  • synonym: inoperative - not working or not able to function
