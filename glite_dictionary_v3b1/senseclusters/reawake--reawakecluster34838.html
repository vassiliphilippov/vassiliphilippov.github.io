reawake

verb
to become active again
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈweɪk / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈweɪk / (BrE)
to cause someone or something, such as a feeling or interest, to become awake or active again

Examples

To reawake old memories
The old song managed to reawake memories she thought she had forgotten.
After years of silence, the movement began to reawake in the community.
The teacher's passion for science helped reawake his students' curiosity.
