antirevolutionary

adjective
opposed to revolution
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌrɛvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
opposed to revolution or major political change, especially in a political context

Examples

Antirevolutionary government
The party's antirevolutionary policies appealed to those who feared rapid change.
During the protests, several leaders made antirevolutionary statements to calm the public.
He was criticized for his antirevolutionary attitude in a time of political upheaval.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterrevolutionary - actively opposing a revolution or seeking to reverse its results
  • antonym: revolutionary - favoring or promoting revolution or major change
