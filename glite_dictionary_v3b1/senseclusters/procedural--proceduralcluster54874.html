procedural

noun
crime or law drama
5.5
Pronunciation: / prəˈsiːdʒərəl / (AmE), / prəˈsiːdʒərəl / (BrE)
a television show, movie, or book that focuses on the step-by-step process of solving crimes or legal cases

Examples

Procedural drama
She enjoys watching a procedural about detectives solving crimes.
The new procedural on television follows a team of lawyers handling difficult cases.
Many viewers like a procedural because it shows how police work step by step.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detective story - a story about solving a crime, usually featuring a detective
  • synonym: legal drama - a drama centered on legal cases and courtroom proceedings
