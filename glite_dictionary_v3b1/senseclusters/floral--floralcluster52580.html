floral

noun
flower pattern
3.3 0.72/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfl…îr…ôl / (AmE), / Ààfl…îÀêr…ôl / (BrE)

Examples

Bold florals

Many spring dresses feature delicate florals in soft pastel colors.
