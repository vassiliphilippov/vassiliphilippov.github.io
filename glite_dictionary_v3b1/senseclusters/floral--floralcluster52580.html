floral

noun
flower pattern
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrəl / (BrE)
a design, pattern, or fabric featuring images or motifs of flowers

Examples

Bold florals
The curtains in her living room have bold florals that brighten the space.
He wore a shirt covered in colorful florals to the summer party.
Many spring dresses feature delicate florals in soft pastel colors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: geometric - a design based on geometric shapes
