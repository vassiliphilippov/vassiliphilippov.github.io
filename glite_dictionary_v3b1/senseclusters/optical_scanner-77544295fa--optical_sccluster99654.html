optical scanner

noun
device converting text/images to digital data
Pronunciation: / ˈɑptɪkəl ˈskænər / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl ˈskænə / (BrE)
a device that reads printed or written text and images by converting them into digital form for storage or processing by a computer

Examples

An optical scanner device
An optical scanner can greatly reduce filing and paper storage needs.
She used an optical scanner to save all her sketches on her computer.
The office is equipped with a high-speed optical scanner for digitizing documents.
