bait

noun
food or material used to attract prey
5.3
Pronunciation: / beɪt / (AmE), / beɪt / (BrE)
food or other material used to attract fish or animals so that they can be caught or trapped.

Examples

Fish bait
She put fresh bait on the hook before casting her line again.
The hunter set out some bait to attract wild animals to the trap.
Worms are commonly used as bait when fishing in rivers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoy - an object used to distract or lure animals into a trap
  • synonym: lure - something used to entice animals or fish to a trap or hook
