Synonyms

  • indigene
    9.4 0.2/m
    an indigene is a person born among the area's original people, while a native is anyone belonging to that local indigenous group.
  • aboriginal
    7.6 0.2/m
    an aboriginal is an original inhabitant of a region before colonization, while a native is a general term for someone from that place.
  • aborigine
    8.6 0.3/m
    an aborigine is a member of a region's original people often used for specific groups, while a native is the broader local person.
