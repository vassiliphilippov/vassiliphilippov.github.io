hypoglycemic

adjective
relating to low blood sugar
4.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /haɪ.poʊ.ɡlaɪ.ˈsiː.mɪk/ /; / /haɪ.pəʊ.ɡlaɪ.ˈsiː.mɪk/ (AmE), /haɪ.pəʊ.ɡlaɪ.ˈsiː.mɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or having hypoglycemia (an abnormally low level of glucose in the blood)

Examples

Hypoglycemic agents
Doctors often prescribe hypoglycemic agents to manage diabetes.
She felt dizzy and faint due to her hypoglycemic condition.
The patient experienced a hypoglycemic episode after skipping breakfast.
Certain medications can induce a hypoglycaemic state in diabetic patients.
Symptoms such as dizziness and sweating may indicate a hypoglycaemic episode.
The doctor prescribed a hypoglycaemic agent to manage her blood sugar levels.
