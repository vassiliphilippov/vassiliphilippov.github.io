Bunsen burner

noun
laboratory gas burner with air valve
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌnsən ˌbɝːnər /; / ˈbʌn.sən ˌbɝː.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌnsən ˌbɜːnə /; / ˈbʌn.sən ˌbɜː.nə / (BrE)
a small adjustable gas burner used in laboratories, with an air valve to control the flame, used for heating and experiments
