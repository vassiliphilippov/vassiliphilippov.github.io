schismatical

adjective
relating to or promoting schism
9.9 9.4
Pronunciation: /skɪz.ˈmæt.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /skɪz.ˈmæt.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of schism; involved in or promoting division, especially within a religious or organizational context

Examples

Schismatical sects
Certain schismatical sects were excluded from participating in official rites.
Historians wrote about the schismatical conflicts that divided the community in the seventeenth century.
The council condemned the schismatical movement for threatening the unity of the church.
