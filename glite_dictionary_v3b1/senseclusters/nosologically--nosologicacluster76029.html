nosologically

adverb
relating to disease classification
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːsəˈlɑːdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnɒsəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the classification of diseases

Examples

To categorize nosologically
The diseases were nosologically categorized according to international standards.
Researchers analyzed the data nosologically to identify patterns among different conditions.
The report grouped the disorders nosologically rather than by their symptoms.
