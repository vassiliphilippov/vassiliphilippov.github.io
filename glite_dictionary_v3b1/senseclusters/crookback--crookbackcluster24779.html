crookback

adjective
person with a hunched back
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊkˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkrʊkˌbak / (BrE)
having a curved or hunched back, especially as a result of kyphosis; suffering from an abnormal spinal curvature.

Examples

Crookback old man
He was cruelly mocked as a crookback in his youth.
Legends often featured a crookback living at the edge of town.
The old man was a crookback who walked with great difficulty.
