grindingly

adverb
extremely harshly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely harsh, tedious, or relentless

Examples

Grindingly slow process
The movie was grindingly slow, making it hard to stay interested until the end.
He worked grindingly long hours at the factory every day.
The weather remained grindingly cold throughout the entire winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pleasantly - in a way that is enjoyable or pleasing
  • synonym: relentlessly - in a way that does not stop or become less intense
  • synonym: tediously - in a way that is boring and repetitive
