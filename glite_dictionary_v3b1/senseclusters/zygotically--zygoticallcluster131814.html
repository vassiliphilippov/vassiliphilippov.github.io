zygotically

adverb
relating to zygote stage
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈɡɑtɪkli / (AmE), / zaɪˈɡɒtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or occurring at the zygote stage of development

Examples

Develop zygotically

The mutation is expressed zygotically, affecting the earliest stages of growth.
