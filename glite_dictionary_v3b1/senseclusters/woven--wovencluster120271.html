woven

adjective
made by interlacing threads or elements
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwoʊ.vən / (AmE), / ˈwəʊ.vən / (BrE)
made or constructed by interlacing threads, strips, or other elements together into a unified whole

Examples

A woven basket
She admired the beautiful woven baskets at the artisan market.
The rug was woven from natural wool fibers, making it both durable and soft.
Traditional tales are often woven into the fabric of local culture.
