therapeutic

adjective
relating to treatment or therapy
Pronunciation: / ˌθɛrəˈpjutɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the treatment of illness or having healing or beneficial effects

Examples

Therapeutic treatment plan
A therapeutic approach can be beneficial for those struggling with anxiety.
Some people find painting to be a deeply therapeutic activity.
The doctor recommended a therapeutic exercise program to aid recovery.
Many people find spending time in nature to be therapeutic for both body and mind.
The doctor prescribed a therapeutic massage to help relieve the patient's back pain.
The new drug has shown remarkable therapeutic effects in clinical trials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curative - having the ability to cure or heal
  • antonym: harmful - causing injury or damage
  • synonym: remedial - intended to cure or provide a remedy
