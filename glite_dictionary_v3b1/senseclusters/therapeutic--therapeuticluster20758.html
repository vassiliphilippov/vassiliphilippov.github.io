Synonyms

  • curative
    6.2 0.8/m
    curative is able to cure disease directly, while therapeutic relates to treatment and can include easing or supporting recovery.
  • remedial
    5.3 0.3/m
    remedial aims to fix a health problem or correct damage, while therapeutic covers treatments that heal or bring benefit.
  • healing
    2.6 0.00/m
    healing means restoring health or comfort over time, while therapeutic describes any treatment or effect that helps recovery.
  • medicinal
    4.0 4/m
    medicinal describes substances used as medicine, while therapeutic describes treatments or actions that help healing and well-being.
  • restorative
    5.4 1/m
    restorative makes someone feel stronger or refreshed, often after rest, while therapeutic relates specifically to medical treatment and healing.
  • restitutive
    7.9 0.00/m
    restitutive restores health or strength in a formal, old-fashioned way, while therapeutic is the common modern word for treatment.
