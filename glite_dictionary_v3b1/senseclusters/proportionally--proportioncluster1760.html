proportionally

adverb
in proportion; to a corresponding degree
5.4
Pronunciation: / prəˈpɔrʃənəli / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that corresponds in size, amount, or degree to something else; to a proportionate extent.

Examples

Grow proportionally
As the project grew, the resources allocated to it expanded proportionally.
The bonus was distributed proportionally among all employees based on their sales performance.
The punishment will increase proportionally with the severity of the offense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proportionately - to a proportionate degree; in a way that matches a corresponding ratio
