trichologist

noun
hair and scalp specialist
Pronunciation: / trɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / trɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a specialist who studies or treats hair and scalp problems

Examples

Trichologist treats hair
After noticing unusual hair loss, he decided to consult a trichologist for professional advice.
The trichologist recommended a special shampoo to treat her scalp condition.
Many people visit a trichologist when they experience persistent dandruff or hair thinning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dermatologist - a doctor who specializes in skin, hair, and nail conditions
