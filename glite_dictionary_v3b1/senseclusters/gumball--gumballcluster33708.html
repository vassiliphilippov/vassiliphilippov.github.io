gumball

noun
round candy with gum center
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small, round candy with a hard outer shell and a chewy, gum-like center, often sold in vending machines

Examples

A sweet gumball
The children raced to the machine to buy a colorful gumball after school.
She popped a blue gumball into her mouth and started chewing happily.
A single gumball rolled across the floor when the machine was bumped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chewing gum - a sweet, flavored gum for chewing
  • synonym: jawbreaker - a hard, round candy that dissolves slowly in the mouth
