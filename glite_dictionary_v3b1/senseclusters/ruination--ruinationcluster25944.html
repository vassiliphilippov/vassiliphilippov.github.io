ruination

noun
complete destruction; devastating loss
6.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ruː.ɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ruː.ɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act, process, or state of being completely destroyed, ruined, or losing all value, hope, or reputation
