chop

noun
a small rib-in piece of meat
Pronunciation: / tʃɑp / (AmE), / tʃɒp / (BrE)
a small, thick piece of meat with a bone, cut from the rib or loin of an animal

Examples

A pork chop
Each chop was seasoned with herbs before being placed on the barbecue.
He ordered a grilled lamb chop with a side of vegetables for dinner.
The butcher recommended the pork chops for a quick and tasty meal.
She ordered two lamb chops for dinner at the restaurant.
The chef seasoned the pork chops before grilling them to perfection.
We bought fresh chops from the butcher for our weekend barbecue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutlet - a small piece of meat, often similar in size or cut
  • antonym: steak - a thick slice of meat, usually without a bone
