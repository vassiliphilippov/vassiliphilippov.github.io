postseason

adjective
after regular season
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌsiːzən / (BrE)
relating to or occurring after the regular sports season, especially during playoffs or championship games

Examples

Postseason games
The team wore special postseason uniforms for the championship game.
He scored his first postseason goal in last night's match.
Many fans travel long distances to attend postseason games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playoff - a series of games after the regular season to determine a champion
  • antonym: regular season - the main part of a sports season before playoffs
