aciduria

noun
organic acids in urine
9.9
Pronunciation: /æs.ə.ˈdʊr.iː.ə/ (AmE), /æs.ə.ˈdʊə.riː.ə/ (BrE)
a hereditary metabolic disorder in which abnormal amounts of organic acids are excreted in the urine

Examples

Inherited aciduria disorder
The newborn was diagnosed with aciduria after abnormal results appeared in the screening test.
Early detection of aciduria can help prevent serious health complications in children.
Special diets are often recommended for patients with aciduria to manage their symptoms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alkaluria - excretion of alkaline urine
  • synonym: aminoaciduria - excretion of amino acids in the urine
