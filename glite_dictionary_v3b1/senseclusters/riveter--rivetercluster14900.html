riveter

noun
machine for driving rivets
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvətər / (AmE), / ˈrɪvɪtə / (BrE)
a machine used for driving rivets into materials to fasten them together

Examples

Pneumatic riveter
A pneumatic riveter makes joining metal plates much more efficient.
He trained the workers on how to use the automatic riveter safely.
The factory installed a new riveter to speed up the assembly line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rivet - a short metal pin or bolt used for fastening
