banking system

noun
all banks in a country or region
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
all the banks in a country or region, considered collectively as an organized system for managing money and financial transactions

Examples
