nongonococcal urethritis

noun
sexually transmitted urethral inflammation (not from gonorrhea)
6.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌɡɑnəˈkɑkəl jʊˌriːˈθraɪtɪs / (AmE), / nɒnˌɡɒnəˈkɒkəl jʊəˌriːˈθraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the urethra, usually caused by chlamydia, and not by gonorrhea; a sexually transmitted infection

Examples

Nongonococcal urethritis infection
Chlamydia trachomatis is a leading cause of nongonococcal urethritis in young adults.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with nongonococcal urethritis after ruling out a gonorrhea infection.
Treatment for nongonococcal urethritis often includes antibiotics to address the underlying bacterial infection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gonococcal urethritis - urethritis caused by gonorrhea infection
  • synonym: urethritis - inflammation of the urethra
