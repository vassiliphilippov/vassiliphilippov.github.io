myoclonic

adjective
muscle jerking
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪəˈklɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəˈklɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to sudden, involuntary muscle jerks or twitches

Examples

Myoclonic muscle jerks
The doctor noted several myoclonic movements in the patient's arms during the test.
Some types of epilepsy are characterized by frequent myoclonic jerks.
After the medication, her myoclonic symptoms became less severe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spasmodic - relating to sudden, brief muscle contractions
  • antonym: tonic - relating to continuous muscle contraction
