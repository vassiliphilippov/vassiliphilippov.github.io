refurnish

verb
provide with new or different furniture
Pronunciation: / riˈfɜrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfɜːnɪʃ / (BrE)
to provide a room or building with new or different furniture

Examples

To refurnish a room
After the renovation, they decided to refurnish the entire apartment with modern pieces.
She spent the weekend shopping to refurnish her bedroom after moving in.
We plan to refurnish the office to create a more comfortable working environment.
