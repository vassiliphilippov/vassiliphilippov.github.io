Dicentra canadensis

noun
plant with cream-colored flowers, corn-like roots
Pronunciation: / daɪˈsɛntrə kəˈnædənsɪs / (AmE), / daɪˈsɛntrə kəˈnædənsɪs / (BrE)
a North American woodland plant with delicate, cream-colored flowers and tuberous roots resembling kernels of corn
