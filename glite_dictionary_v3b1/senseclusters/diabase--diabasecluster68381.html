diabase

adjective
relating to diabase rock
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəbeɪz / (BrE)
made of or relating to diabase, a hard, dark igneous rock used in construction and road building
