crateful

noun
quantity a crate can hold
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪtfʊl / (AmE), / ˈkreɪtfʊl / (BrE)
The amount that a crate can hold.

Examples

A crateful of books
She opened a crateful of old books found in the attic.
The farmer delivered a crateful of apples to the market each morning.
They donated a crateful of canned food to the local shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxful - the quantity contained in a box
  • synonym: crate - a large container for transporting or storing goods
