nothing to speak of

x
of little or no importance
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ tə ˈspiːk ʌv / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ tə ˈspiːk ɒv / (BrE)
used to say that something is insignificant, unimportant, or barely noticeable

Examples

Nothing to speak of in budget
After expenses, there was nothing to speak of left in the budget.
His injuries were minor—just a few scratches, nothing to speak of.
The weather was fine, with nothing to speak of in terms of rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: not much - very little, hardly any
