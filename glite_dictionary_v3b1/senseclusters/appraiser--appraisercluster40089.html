appraiser

noun
person who assesses value or authenticity
3.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpreɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpreɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to assess or officially estimate the value, quality, or authenticity of something, especially property, goods, or works of art

Examples

An appraiser estimates value
An experienced appraiser can often detect subtle signs of forgery in artwork.
She hired an appraiser to confirm the authenticity of the painting.
The antique vase was examined by a renowned appraiser before being auctioned.
A qualified appraiser must assess the jewelry for insurance purposes.
Before purchasing the artwork, she consulted a professional appraiser.
The appraiser inspected the house carefully before determining its market value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assessor - a person who evaluates the quality, importance, or value of something
  • antonym: owner - a person who possesses something, as opposed to one who appraises it
  • synonym: valuator - a person who estimates the worth or value of something
  • synonym: valuer - a person who assesses or estimates the value of something
