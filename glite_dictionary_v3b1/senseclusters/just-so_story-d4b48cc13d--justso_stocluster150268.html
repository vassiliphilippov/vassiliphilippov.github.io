just-so story

noun
fanciful explanatory tale
8.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd í åst Ààso ä Àåst…îri / (AmE), / Àåd í åst Ààs…ô ä Àåst…îÀêri / (BrE)
a fanciful or imaginative tale, often invented to explain a natural phenomenon or the origin of something, usually presented as if it were the true explanation

Examples

A just-so story
He launched into a just-so story to explain the mysterious disappearance of the cookies.
Sarah entertained her nieces with a just-so story about why zebras have stripes.
Scientists sometimes warn against accepting a just-so story without evidence.

Synonyms

  • fairytale

    a fairytale is an implausible, excuse-like story often told to mislead, while a just-so story pretends to explain how something began.
