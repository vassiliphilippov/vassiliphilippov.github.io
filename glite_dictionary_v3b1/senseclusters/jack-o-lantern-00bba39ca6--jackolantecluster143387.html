jack-o'-lantern

noun
mysterious marsh light in folklore
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæk əˌlæn tɚn / (AmE), / ˈdʒæk əˌlæn tən / (BrE)
A pale, mysterious light sometimes seen at night over marshy ground, believed in folklore to be caused by spirits or natural phenomena.

Examples

Marsh jack-o'-lantern light
Legends say the jack-o'-lantern leads travelers astray at night.
Scientists have tried to explain the jack-o'-lantern as a result of marsh gases.
We caught a glimpse of a jack-o'-lantern flickering above the bog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: will-o'-the-wisp - a phosphorescent light seen at night over marshy ground, synonymous with this sense
