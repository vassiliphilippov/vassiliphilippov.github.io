life-sized

adjective
of the same size as the original
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf.saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf.saɪzd / (BrE)
having the same size as the person or thing represented; as large as life.

Examples

A life-sized statue
She ordered a life-sized cutout of her favorite movie star for the party.
The museum exhibited a life-sized model of a Tyrannosaurus rex.
We were amazed by the life-sized sculptures in the park.
