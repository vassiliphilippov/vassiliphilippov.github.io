clearing house

noun
organization that facilitates transactions
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈklɪərɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
an organization or institution that collects and distributes items, information, or payments, especially to facilitate transactions between other organizations

Examples

Information clearing house
All checks must pass through a clearing house before the funds are transferred.
The central bank acts as a clearing house for all interbank payments.
The nonprofit organization served as a clearing house for job listings in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brokerage - a firm that arranges transactions between buyers and sellers
