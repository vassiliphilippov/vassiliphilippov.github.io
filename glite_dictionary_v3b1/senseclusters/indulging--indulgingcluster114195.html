indulging

noun
the act of gratifying a desire
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌldʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌldʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of allowing oneself or others to satisfy a desire or wish

Examples

His indulging of whims
His indulging of every whim soon became a problem.
She found herself indulging in a piece of cake despite her diet.
Too much indulging in sweets can harm your health.
