duckbilled platypus

noun
Australian egg-laying mammal with a bill and webbed feet
5.7 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌk.bɪld ˈplæt.ɪ.pəs/ /; / /ˈdʌk.bɪld ˈplæt.ə.pʊs/ (AmE), /ˈdʌk.bɪld ˈplæt.ɪ.pəs/ (BrE)
a small, egg-laying mammal native to Australia and Tasmania, with dense fur, a broad, flat bill, a flat tail, and webbed feet; the only species in the family Ornithorhynchidae
