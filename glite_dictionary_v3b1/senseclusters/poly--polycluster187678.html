poly

noun
polyester fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑli / (AmE), / ˈpɒli / (BrE)
an informal term for polyester fabric or a garment made from polyester

Examples

Poly fabric
She bought a dress made of poly because it was easy to wash and quick to dry.
The team uniforms were all poly, which made them lightweight but not very breathable.
He prefers natural fibers, but sometimes wears poly shirts for sports.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cotton - a natural fabric made from cotton fibers
  • synonym: polyester - a synthetic fabric made from polyester fibers
