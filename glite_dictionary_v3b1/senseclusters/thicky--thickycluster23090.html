thicky

noun
slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪki / (AmE), / ˈθɪki / (BrE)
an informal and often insulting term for a person who is not very intelligent

Examples

A thicky in class
They laughed and called him a thicky after he failed the simple quiz.
It is rude to label someone a thicky just because they made a mistake.
She hated being called a thicky by her classmates at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dunce - a person who is slow to learn or not intelligent
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
