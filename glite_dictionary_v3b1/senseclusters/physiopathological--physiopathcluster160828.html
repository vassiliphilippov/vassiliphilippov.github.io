physiopathological

adjective
disease-related processes
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪziˌoʊˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəʊˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the abnormal physiological processes associated with disease or injury

Examples

Physiopathological mechanisms
The study focused on the physiopathological mechanisms underlying chronic inflammation.
Doctors examined the physiopathological effects of the virus on lung tissue.
Understanding physiopathological changes is crucial for developing new treatments.
