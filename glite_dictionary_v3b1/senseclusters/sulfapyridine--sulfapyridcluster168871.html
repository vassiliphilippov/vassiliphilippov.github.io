sulfapyridine

noun
sulfa drug, antibacterial compound
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌlfəˈpaɪrəˌdin / (AmE), / ˌsʌlfəˈpɪrɪdiːn / (BrE)
A sulfa drug derived from pyridine and sulfanilamide, formerly used to treat bacterial infections.

Examples

Sulfapyridine drug
Because of adverse side effects, sulfapyridine is rarely prescribed today.
Researchers studied the effectiveness of sulfapyridine against various bacterial strains.
The development of sulfapyridine marked a major advance in treating bacterial pneumonia.
