stanza

noun
group of lines in a poem
4.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstæn.zə/ (AmE), /ˈstæn.zə/ (BrE)
A group of lines forming a unit within a poem, typically separated from others by a space; a verse.

Examples

A stanza in a poem

Every stanza in this poem follows the same rhyme scheme.
The poet uses each stanza to express a different stage of emotion.
