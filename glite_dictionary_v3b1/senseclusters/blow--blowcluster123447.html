blow

verb
deposit eggs (of insects)
9.9
Pronunciation: / bloʊ / (AmE), / bləʊ / (BrE)
(of certain insects) to deposit eggs, especially on meat or carrion.

Examples

Flies blow meat
Bluebottle flies often blow meat left uncovered outdoors.
Certain insects may blow wounds in livestock, causing infection.
The sheep carcass was quickly blown by swarming insects.
