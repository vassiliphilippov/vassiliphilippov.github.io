yelper

noun
one who yelps
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛlpər / (AmE), / ˈjɛlpə / (BrE)
a person or animal that makes a short, sharp cry, often from pain, surprise, or fear

Examples

A scared yelper
The frightened yelper skittered under the couch after the thunderstorm.
Every time the doorbell rang, the little yelper would make everyone jump.
The vet gently calmed the injured yelper before examining its paw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barker - an animal, especially a dog, that barks
  • synonym: crier - someone or something that cries out loudly
