petrography

noun
study of rocks
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛtrəˈɡræfi / (AmE), / ˌpɛtrəˈɡræfi / (BrE)
the scientific study of rocks, focusing on their composition, structure, and classification

Examples

Petrography study of rocks
She specialized in petrography to analyze the mineral content of ancient stones.
A detailed report in petrography revealed the volcanic origin of the rock samples.
Modern petrography uses microscopes to study the fine structure of rocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mineralogy - the scientific study of minerals
  • synonym: petrology - the scientific study of the origin and structure of rocks
