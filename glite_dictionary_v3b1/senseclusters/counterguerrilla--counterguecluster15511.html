counterguerrilla

noun
anti-guerrilla fighter
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərɡəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəɡəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
a person or military unit that conducts operations to combat guerrilla fighters

Examples

Counterguerrilla unit
The government sent a counterguerrilla team to restore order in the conflict zone.
A counterguerrilla force was trained to track and neutralize insurgent groups in the mountains.
He joined the counterguerrilla unit after years of serving in the regular army.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guerrilla - a member of a small independent group fighting against larger regular forces
  • synonym: soldier - a person who serves in an army
