carbonated

adjective
containing dissolved carbon dioxide gas

(of a drink or liquid) containing dissolved carbon dioxide gas, especially added artificially to make it fizzy.

Examples

Carbonated soft drinks
Many children enjoy drinking carbonated soft drinks with their meals.
She prefers carbonated water over still water at restaurants.
The carbonated beverage fizzed as soon as I opened the can.

  • fizzy

  • aerated
