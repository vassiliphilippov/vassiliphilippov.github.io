moonish

adjective
dreamy or sentimental
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnɪʃ / (BrE)
dreamy, absent-minded, or lost in romantic or fanciful thoughts

Examples

Moonish daydreams
He looked out the window with a moonish expression, lost in his own thoughts.
Her moonish daydreams often distracted her from her work.
The poet's moonish mood inspired a series of romantic verses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamy - having a gentle, romantic, or absent-minded quality
  • antonym: practical - concerned with real situations rather than ideas or feelings
  • synonym: sentimental - showing or affected by tender feelings or emotions
