scoliometer

noun
spinal curvature device
Pronunciation: / ˌskoʊliˈɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌskəʊliˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to measure the degree of curvature in the spine, especially for detecting scoliosis

Examples

A scoliometer device
The school nurse used a scoliometer during the annual spine screening for students.
A scoliometer helps doctors quickly assess the severity of spinal curvature in patients.
After noticing uneven shoulders, the physician measured the angle with a scoliometer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protractor - an instrument for measuring angles
  • synonym: scoliosis - a medical condition involving abnormal curvature of the spine
