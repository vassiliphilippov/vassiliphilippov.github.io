pillwort

noun
European aquatic fern with round fruiting bodies
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈpɪlˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a small European water fern (Pilularia globulifera) growing on the margins of ponds or in wet acidic soil, with small round fruiting bodies (sporocarps)

Examples

The pillwort fern
Conservationists are working to restore areas where pillwort has declined due to habitat loss.
The shallow edges of the pond are an ideal habitat for pillwort.
We identified several rare plants during our survey, including pillwort and water violet.
