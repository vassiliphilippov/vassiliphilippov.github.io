pillwort

noun
European aquatic fern with round fruiting bodies
7.7 9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪl.wɑːrt/ /; / /ˈpɪl.wɔːrt/ (AmE), /ˈpɪl.wɔːt/ (BrE)
a small European water fern (Pilularia globulifera) growing on the margins of ponds or in wet acidic soil, with small round fruiting bodies (sporocarps)
