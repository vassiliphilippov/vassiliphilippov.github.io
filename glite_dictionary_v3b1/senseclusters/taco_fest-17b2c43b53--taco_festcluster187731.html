taco fest

noun
festival celebrating tacos and related food
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːkoʊ fɛst / (AmE), / ˈtakəʊ fɛst / (BrE)
A festival or public event featuring tacos and other taco-related foods, typically including food vendors, tastings, and entertainment.
