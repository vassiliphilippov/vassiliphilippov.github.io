genus Foeniculum

noun
genus of aromatic European herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs fɪˈnɪkjʊləm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs fɪˈnɪkjʊləm / (BrE)
A small genus of aromatic European herbs with finely divided leaves and yellow flowers, including the fennel.
