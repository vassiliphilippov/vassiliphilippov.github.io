Girl Scout

noun
female member of the Girl Scouts
1.8
Pronunciation: /gɜːrl skaʊt/ (AmE), /gɜːl skaʊt/ (BrE)
A girl who is a member of the Girl Scouts, an organization that promotes personal development and community service among girls and young women.

Examples

A Girl Scout troop

She earned her badge as a Girl Scout by volunteering at the local food bank.
The Girl Scout troop sold cookies to raise money for community projects.
