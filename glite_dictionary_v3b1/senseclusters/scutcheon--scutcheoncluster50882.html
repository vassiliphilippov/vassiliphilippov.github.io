scutcheon

noun
protective covering around a keyhole or handle
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskʌtʃ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈskʌtʃ.ən/ (BrE)
A flat plate or protective covering, especially around a keyhole or door handle, designed to prevent soiling or damage to the surrounding surface.

Examples

Door scutcheon plate
He replaced the old scutcheon on the front door to prevent fingerprints from marking the wood.
Please ensure the scutcheon matches the new doorknob in both color and style.
The brass scutcheon around the keyhole gleamed in the sunlight.
