An activity or gift that is intended to help people in need or benefit the public at large.

Examples

Give to charity
After the earthquake, many people donated money and clothes to charity.
Organizing a food drive is a wonderful form of charity.
The local community center relies on charity to fund its activities.

Synonyms

  • donation
    1.9 21/m
    donation is the act of giving money, goods, or time to help others, while charity covers activities or gifts that help people in need.
  • contribution
    2.5 28/m
    contribution is an amount of money given to a fund or cause, while charity is a broader activity or gift to help people.
  • offering
    3.7 5.10/m
    offering is a sum given often to a church or cause, while charity is any activity or gift meant to help those in need.
  • gift
    1.0 19/m
    gift is something given without payment in many situations, while charity is giving aimed specifically to help people or the public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: selfishness - lack of consideration for others; concern only with one's own advantage or pleasure
