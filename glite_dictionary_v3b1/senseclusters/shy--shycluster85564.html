shy

verb
throw quickly or suddenly
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪ / (AmE), / ʃaɪ / (BrE)
to throw something quickly or suddenly, especially with a quick movement of the arm

Examples

To shy a ball
He shied a snowball at his friend during the fight.
She shied her keys onto the table in frustration.
The boy shied a stone at the dog before running away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fling - to throw something suddenly with force
  • synonym: toss - to throw something lightly or casually
