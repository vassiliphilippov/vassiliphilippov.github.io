saddle shoe

noun
low-heeled shoe with a contrasting middle band
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl ʃu / (AmE), / ˈsædəl ʃuː / (BrE)
a type of low-heeled oxford shoe, typically with a plain toe and a contrasting band, or 'saddle', across the middle of the shoe

Examples

A pair of saddle shoes
My grandfather still treasures his old black-and-white saddle shoes from his college days.
She wore a pair of classic saddle shoes with her school uniform.
The dance floor was filled with teens in poodle skirts and saddle shoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxford - a type of lace-up shoe with a low heel
