least bittern

noun
a small North American heron
9.5
Pronunciation: / list ˈbɪtɚn / (AmE), / liːst ˈbɪtən / (BrE)
a very small species of heron found in North and Central America, noted for its secretive behavior and slender build

Examples

The least bittern bird
During migration, several least bitterns were observed resting in the wetlands.
The least bittern is often difficult to spot due to its camouflaged plumage and reed-dwelling habits.
The distinctive call of the least bittern can sometimes be heard at dawn in marshy areas.
