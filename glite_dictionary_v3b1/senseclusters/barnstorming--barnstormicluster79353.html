barnstorming

noun
political campaign tour
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrnˌstɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːnˌstɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
a series of energetic political speeches or appearances in different places to gain support for a candidate or cause

Examples

Barnstorming campaign
The senator's barnstorming across rural towns helped energize her supporters before the election.
After weeks of barnstorming, the candidate's popularity increased in several key regions.
His barnstorming tour included speeches in more than twenty cities in just ten days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campaigning - actively working to gain support for a cause or candidate
  • synonym: rally - a large public meeting to support a cause or candidate
