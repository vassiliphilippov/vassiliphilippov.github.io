scraggy

adjective
rough or uneven in surface or outline
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskræɡi / (AmE), / ˈskræɡi / (BrE)
having a rough, uneven, or jagged surface or outline; rugged or irregular in appearance

Examples

Scraggy rocks
After the landslide, the mountain trail became much more scraggy and difficult to climb.
The old tree had scraggy branches reaching in every direction.
We hiked along the scraggy cliffs overlooking the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jagged - having sharp projections or a harsh outline
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
