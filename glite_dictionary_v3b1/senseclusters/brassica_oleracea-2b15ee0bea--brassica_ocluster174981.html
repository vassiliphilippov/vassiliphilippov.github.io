Brassica oleracea

noun
species of plant including cabbage, broccoli, etc.
Pronunciation: / brəˌsɪkə oʊˈlɛəriə / (AmE), / brəˌsɪkə əʊˈlɪərɪə /; / brəˌsɪkə əʊˈlɪəriə / (BrE)
a plant species of the cabbage family that includes both wild and cultivated vegetables such as cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, kale, and Brussels sprouts
