unposted

adjective
not recorded in accounts
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpoʊstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊstɪd / (BrE)
not yet entered or recorded in official financial records or an accounting ledger

Examples

Unposted invoice
The accountant found several unposted invoices that needed to be entered before the end of the month.
Please check if there are any unposted transactions before finalizing the financial report.
The bank statement showed an unposted deposit that would appear in the next cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pending - awaiting action or completion
  • antonym: recorded - entered into official records or accounts
