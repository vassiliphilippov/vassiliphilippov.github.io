styling

noun
design appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the way in which something is designed or arranged to look attractive or fashionable

Examples

Modern styling
The new smartphone's styling makes it stand out from other models on the market.
Many people choose a car based on its styling as much as its performance.
The interior styling of the restaurant creates a warm and inviting atmosphere.
The styling of the new smartphone makes it stand out from its competitors.
She admired the elegant styling of the living room furniture.
Car enthusiasts often discuss the styling of different vehicle models at length.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the way that someone or something looks
  • synonym: decoration - the act of making something look more attractive
  • synonym: design - the plan or arrangement of the appearance of something
  • antonym: plainness - the quality of being simple and without decoration
