mercifully

adverb
in a compassionate or forgiving way
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.sɪ.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.sɪ.fəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows compassion or forgiveness toward someone in distress or deserving punishment

Examples

Spoke mercifully to child
She spoke mercifully to the frightened child, calming his fears.
The judge decided to treat the young offender mercifully because it was his first mistake.
The wounded soldier was mercifully rescued from the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassionately - in a compassionate manner
  • antonym: harshly - in a cruel or severe manner
