dentifrice

noun
tooth-cleaning substance
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəˌfrɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɛntɪfrɪs / (BrE)
A substance, such as a paste or powder, used for cleaning the teeth.

Examples

A tube of dentifrice
Her travel bag always includes a small tube of dentifrice and a toothbrush.
Many modern dentifrices contain fluoride to help prevent cavities.
The dentist recommended using a gentle dentifrice for sensitive teeth.
