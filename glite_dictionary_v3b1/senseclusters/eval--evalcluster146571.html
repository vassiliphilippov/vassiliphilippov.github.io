eval

noun
informal assessment
Pronunciation: / ˈiːvæl / (AmE), / ˈiːvæl / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for evaluation, meaning an assessment or review of something or someone

Examples

A quick eval
The teacher gave a quick eval at the end of the lesson to check our understanding.
Before starting the project, we had an eval to discuss our strengths and weaknesses.
She scheduled a short eval with her supervisor after the training session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evaluation - the process of assessing or judging something
  • synonym: review - an examination or assessment of something
  • synonym: test - a procedure to assess knowledge, ability, or performance
