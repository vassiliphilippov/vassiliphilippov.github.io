phono

adjective
relating to phonographs
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnəʊ / (BrE)
relating to phonographs or to audio signals at the level produced by a phonograph turntable, especially before amplification.

Examples

Phono input jack
Connect the record player to the phono input on your stereo system for the best sound quality.
Some modern amplifiers no longer include a phono stage for turntables.
He bought a special cable to link his vintage turntable to the phono port on the receiver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audio - relating to sound or the reproduction of sound
