squad car

noun
police patrol car with radio
Pronunciation: / skwɑd kɑr / (AmE), / skwɒd kɑː / (BrE)
a car used by police officers while patrolling, usually equipped with a radio for communication with headquarters

Examples

Squad car with police
A pair of officers stepped out of their squad car to investigate the disturbance.
She saw a squad car parked outside the store late at night.
The squad car raced down the street in pursuit of the suspect.
