rising

adjective
increasing; becoming higher or greater
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
increasing or becoming higher or greater in degree, value, or status

Examples

Rising prices
Sales figures showed a rising trend throughout the past year.
The rising cost of living is a concern for many families.
There is a rising interest in renewable energy sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: falling - decreasing or becoming lower in degree or value or status
  • synonym: increasing - becoming greater in size, amount, or degree
