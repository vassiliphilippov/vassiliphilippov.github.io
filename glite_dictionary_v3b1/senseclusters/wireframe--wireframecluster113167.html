wireframe

verb
to outline interface
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌfreɪm / (BrE)
to create a basic schematic or outline of a user interface, showing structure and layout without detailed design

Examples

To wireframe a page
Before starting the visual design, the team will wireframe each page of the website.
She likes to wireframe her ideas on paper before using any design software.
Developers often wireframe apps to plan the user experience early in the process.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: design - to create the plans or drawings for something
  • synonym: sketch - to make a simple drawing or outline
