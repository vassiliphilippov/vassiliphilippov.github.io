prune

verb
remove unwanted parts
Pronunciation: / pruːn / (AmE), / pruːn / (BrE)
to remove unwanted or unnecessary parts from something, especially to improve its quality or efficiency

Examples

To prune a list
It's time to prune your email inbox and delete old, unnecessary messages.
She regularly prunes her to-do list to stay focused on what matters most.
The company decided to prune several underperforming products from its lineup.
