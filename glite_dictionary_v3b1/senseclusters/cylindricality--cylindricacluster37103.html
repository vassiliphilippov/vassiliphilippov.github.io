cylindricality

noun
quality of being cylindrical
Pronunciation: / sɪˌlɪndrɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / sɪˌlɪn.drɪˈkæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being shaped like a cylinder, especially in reference to the exact roundness and parallelism of a 3-dimensional cylinder.

Examples

The cylindricality of a shape
Any deviation from perfect cylindricality can cause mechanical inefficiency in industrial machinery.
Strict cylindricality is required for the piston to function properly in the cylinder.
The cylindricality of the engine parts was measured with precise instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cylindricity - the degree to which an object approaches a perfect cylinder
