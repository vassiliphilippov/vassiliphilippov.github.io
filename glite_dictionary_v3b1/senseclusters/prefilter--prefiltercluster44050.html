prefilter

noun
initial filtering device
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌfɪltər / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌfɪltə / (BrE)
a device or process that removes unwanted material before the main filter in a system

Examples

A prefilter screen
The vacuum cleaner's prefilter traps hair and dust before the main filter is used.
Replacing the prefilter regularly helps extend the life of your water filtration system.
Many air purifiers include a washable prefilter to capture larger particles like pollen and pet dander.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filter - a device that removes impurities from air, water, or other substances
