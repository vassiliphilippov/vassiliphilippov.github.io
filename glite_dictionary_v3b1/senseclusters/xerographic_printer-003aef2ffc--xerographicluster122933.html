xerographic printer

noun
printer using the xerographic process
Pronunciation: / ˌzɪɹəˈɡɹæfɪk ˈprɪntɚ / (AmE), / ˌzɪərəˈɡræfɪk ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
A type of page printer that produces images using the xerographic process, commonly used in photocopiers and laser printers.

Examples

A xerographic printer
Maintaining a xerographic printer involves regularly replacing its toner cartridge.
Photocopiers and many laser printers are types of xerographic printers.
The office upgraded to a xerographic printer for faster and higher-quality document production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laser printer - a type of printer that uses a laser beam and xerographic process to produce text and graphics
