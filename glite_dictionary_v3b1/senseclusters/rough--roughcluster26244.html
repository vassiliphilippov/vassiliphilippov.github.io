rough

adjective
lacking refinement or finesse (of people/behavior)
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) lacking refinement, politeness, or finesse; coarse or unpolished.

Examples

Rough manners
Although he had a rough style, his intentions were always kind.
His rough manners often offended guests at formal events.
She tries to hide her rough edges in professional settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarse - lacking refinement or delicacy (of people or manners)
  • antonym: polished - showing refinement, sophistication, or good manners
