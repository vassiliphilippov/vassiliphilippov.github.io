abri

noun
protective shelter
Pronunciation: / æˈbri / (AmE), / ˈæbri / (BrE)
a man-made shelter or dugout that protects people from weather, bombing, or other danger

Examples

A concrete abri
During the storm, the hikers found safety in a small abri built into the hillside.
Soldiers waited in the abri until the shelling stopped.
The old railway abri now serves as a museum exhibit about wartime life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bunker - a reinforced underground shelter, especially for protection during attacks
  • synonym: dugout - a shelter dug into the ground, often used in war
  • antonym: exposure - the state of being unprotected from danger or the elements
