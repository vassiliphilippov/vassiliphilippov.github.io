lankly

adverb
in a limp way
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋkli / (AmE), / ˈlæŋkli / (BrE)
in a long, thin, and limp manner, often appearing straight and without body or volume

Examples

Hair hangs lankly
After swimming, her hair hung lankly down her back.
The old flag draped lankly from the pole in the humid air.
He watched the vines grow lankly along the garden fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limply - in a way that lacks firmness or stiffness
  • antonym: thickly - in a dense or full manner
