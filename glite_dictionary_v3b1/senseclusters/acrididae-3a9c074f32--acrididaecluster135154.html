Acrididae

noun
family of short-horned grasshoppers
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪdɪˌdiː /; / ˌækrɪˈdaɪdiː / (AmE), / əˈkrɪdɪˌdiː /; / ˌakrɪˈdɪːdiː / (BrE)
