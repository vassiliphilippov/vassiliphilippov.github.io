curare

noun
toxic muscle relaxant from South American plants
7.4
Pronunciation: / kjuˈrɑːri / (AmE), / kjʊəˈrɑːri / (BrE)
a poisonous substance obtained from certain tropical South American plants, used as a muscle relaxant, especially in medical and biological contexts

Examples

A dose of curare
During the surgery, a small amount of curare was administered as a muscle relaxant.
Scientists study curare to understand its effects on nerve-muscle transmission.
The indigenous people used curare on their arrow tips while hunting.
