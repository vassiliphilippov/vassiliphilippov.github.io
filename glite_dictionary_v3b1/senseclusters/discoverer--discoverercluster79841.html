discoverer

noun
person who finds something first
3.7 2.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈskʌ.vər.ər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈskʌ.və.rə/ (BrE)
a person who is the first to find, observe, identify, or invent something new

Examples

The discoverer of a place
As the discoverer of the hidden cave, she was invited to speak at the conference.
Christopher Columbus is often called the discoverer of America, although others arrived before him.
The discoverer of the new species was awarded a prestigious prize.
Every great invention has a discoverer behind it.
Marie Curie is celebrated as the discoverer of radium.
The discoverer of the new plant species received international fame.
