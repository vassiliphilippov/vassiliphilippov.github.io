classer

noun
item sorter or grader
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsər / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sort or grade items into classes, especially in an industrial setting

Examples

Wool classer
The wool classer inspected each fleece for quality before it was processed.
As a classer, she was responsible for sorting the cotton by grade.
Every classer must be trained to recognize subtle differences in material quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grader - a person who sorts or evaluates items by quality or size
  • synonym: inspector - a person who examines items for quality or standards
