beforetime

noun
former times
9.5
Pronunciation: / bɪˈfɔːrˌtaɪm / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔːtaɪm / (BrE)
a time in the past; former times or earlier days

Examples

The beforetime days
In the beforetime, people wrote letters instead of sending emails.
Stories from the beforetime are often told by grandparents to their grandchildren.
Many traditions from the beforetime have faded away in modern society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nowadays - the present time, as opposed to the past
  • synonym: yesteryear - the past, especially a time fondly remembered
