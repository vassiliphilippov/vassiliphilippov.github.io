barbershop

noun
shop for men's haircuts and grooming
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.bər.ʃɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.bə.ʃɒp/ (BrE)
A shop where men have their hair cut, beards trimmed, and other grooming services performed, typically by a barber.

Examples

The barbershop haircut
