Jebus

proper noun
ancient name for Jerusalem
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːbəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːbəs / (BrE)
an ancient name for the city of Jerusalem, used in the Bible and early historical sources

Examples

Ancient Jebus city
The ancient city of Jebus was later conquered by King David.
Historians believe that Jebus was located on the site of present-day Jerusalem.
Biblical texts often refer to Jerusalem as Jebus before it became the capital of Israel.
