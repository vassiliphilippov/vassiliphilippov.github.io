cozy

verb
to make comfortable
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi / (BrE)
to make oneself or someone else warm, comfortable, and relaxed

Examples

To cozy up on the sofa
After a long day, she cozied up on the sofa with her favorite movie.
They cozy together by the fireplace whenever it snows outside.
He cozied himself under the thick blanket to escape the chilly air.
On cold evenings, I love cozying up by the fireplace with a cup of tea.
They spent the afternoon cozying their living room with soft blankets and pillows.
After a long day, she enjoys cozying into bed with her favorite novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snuggle - to settle or move into a warm, comfortable position
  • antonym: unsettle - to make someone feel anxious or uncomfortable
