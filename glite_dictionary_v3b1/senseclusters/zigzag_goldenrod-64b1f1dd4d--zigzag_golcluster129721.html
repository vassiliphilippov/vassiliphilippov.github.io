zigzag goldenrod

noun
North American woodland goldenrod species
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡ ˈɡoʊl.dənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡ ˈɡəʊl.dənˌrɒd / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant (Solidago flexicaulis) with zigzag-shaped stems and clusters of small yellow flowers, found in woodlands.

Examples

zigzag goldenrod plant
Gardeners often use zigzag goldenrod to add color to shaded woodland areas.
She identified the plant as zigzag goldenrod because of its distinctive stem pattern.
The bright flowers of the zigzag goldenrod attract many pollinators in late summer.
