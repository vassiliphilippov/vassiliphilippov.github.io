unstocked

adjective
lacking inventory
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒkt / (BrE)
not supplied with stock, goods, or necessary items; empty or lacking inventory

Examples

Unstocked shelves
After the holiday rush, many store shelves remained unstocked for several days.
The refrigerator was completely unstocked when they returned from vacation.
Some rural pharmacies are often unstocked with essential medicines during winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled
  • antonym: stocked - supplied with goods or inventory
