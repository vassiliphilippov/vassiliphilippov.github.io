picket

verb
protest by standing or marching outside
9.9 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.ɪt/ /; / /ˈpɪk.ət/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.ɪt/ /; / /ˈpɪk.ət/ (BrE)
to stand or march outside a place of work or other site as a form of protest, typically to dissuade others from entering or to express disagreement

Examples

To picket a building

Security guards were hired after union members began to picket the office building.
The workers decided to picket the factory gates early in the morning.
