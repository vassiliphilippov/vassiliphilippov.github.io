fetidly

adverb
in a disgusting way
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that smells very unpleasant or foul

Examples

To fetidly smell bad
The garbage had been left to rot fetidly in the alley for days.
The politician spoke fetidly, spreading lies and stirring up hatred among the crowd.
Corruption spread fetidly through the organization, tainting every decision made.
The abandoned food decayed fetidly in the back of the refrigerator.
A pile of wet leaves lay fetidly in the corner of the garden after the rain.
The air in the closed room hung fetidly after days without ventilation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disgustingly - in a manner that causes strong dislike or revulsion
  • antonym: fragrantly - in a way that smells pleasant
  • synonym: malodorously - in a way that has a bad smell
  • synonym: offensively - in a way that causes offense or disgust
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a way that is pleasing or agreeable
