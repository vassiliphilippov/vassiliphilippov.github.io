thirty-second

adjective
ordinal number; 32nd in order
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːrti ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
Ordinal number coming after thirty-first and before thirty-third, representing the position of 32nd in a sequence.

Examples

They celebrated their thirty-second wedding anniversary last month.
This is the thirty-second chapter of the book, right before the conclusion.
