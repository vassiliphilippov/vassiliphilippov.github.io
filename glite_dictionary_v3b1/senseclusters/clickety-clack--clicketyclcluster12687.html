A fast, rhythmic series of clicking and clacking sounds, often used to describe the noise made by machines or hard objects striking together in rapid succession.

Examples

The clickety-clack sound
He found the clickety-clack of the old typewriter oddly satisfying as he worked late into the night.
The clickety-clack of the train wheels lulled her to sleep during the journey.
The horse's hooves made a gentle clickety-clack as they trotted down the cobblestone street.

Synonyms

  • clickety-click
    8.6 0.02/m
    clickety-click is a light, continuous rapid clicking sound from small machines, while clickety-clack suggests louder alternating clicks and clacks.
  • click-clack
    6.3 0.01/m
    click-clack is a quick, sharp alternating series of sudden strikes, while clickety-clack feels more rhythmic and fast in sequence.
