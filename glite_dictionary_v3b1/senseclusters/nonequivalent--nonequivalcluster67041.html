nonequivalent

adjective
not equal or interchangeable
Pronunciation: / ˌnʌnɪˈkwɪvələnt / (AmE), / ˌnʌnɪˈkwɪvələnt / (BrE)
not equal or interchangeable in value, quantity, or significance.

Examples

The nonequivalent atoms
In chemistry, certain hydrogen atoms may be nonequivalent due to their positions in the molecule.
The two proposals are nonequivalent in their impact on employees.
Their offers, though similar on the surface, are actually nonequivalent in terms of benefits.
