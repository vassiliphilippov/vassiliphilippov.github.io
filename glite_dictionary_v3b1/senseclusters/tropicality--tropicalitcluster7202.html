tropicality

noun
representation of tropics
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɑːpɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the way the tropics are represented or imagined in culture, literature, or politics, often highlighting exotic or stereotypical qualities

Examples

Cultural tropicality
The novel explores how tropicality shapes Western perceptions of Caribbean islands.
Scholars have criticized the use of tropicality in travel writing for reinforcing stereotypes.
Museums sometimes display artifacts in ways that reflect outdated ideas of tropicality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stereotype - a fixed, oversimplified idea about a group or place
