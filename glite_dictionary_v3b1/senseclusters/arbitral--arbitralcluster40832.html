arbitral

adjective
relating to arbitration
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrbɪtrəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːbɪtrəl / (BrE)
relating to arbitration or to an arbitrator's decision or authority.

Examples

An arbitral tribunal
An arbitral tribunal was established to handle the commercial disagreement.
Several international organizations prefer arbitral procedures to court litigation.
The two companies agreed to abide by the arbitral ruling in their contract dispute.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbitration - the process of resolving a dispute outside of court by an impartial third party
