flat

noun
large level area of land
Pronunciation: / flæt / (AmE), / flæt / (BrE)
a large area of level, open land, usually with few or no trees

Examples

Open flat
The horses ran swiftly across the open flat before reaching the hills.
They explored the famous salt flats during their trip to Utah.
Vegetation is sparse on this flat, making it easy to see for miles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - a large area of flat land with few trees
