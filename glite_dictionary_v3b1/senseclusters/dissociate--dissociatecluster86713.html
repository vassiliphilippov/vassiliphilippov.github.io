dissociate

verb
regard as unconnected
3.4 7.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsoʊʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈsəʊsiˌeɪt / (BrE)

Examples

To dissociate ideas

She could not dissociate the sound of rain from her childhood memories.
The company tried to dissociate itself from the scandal by issuing a statement.
