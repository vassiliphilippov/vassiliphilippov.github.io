snottiness

noun
arrogant attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑːtinəs / (AmE), / ˈsnɒtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being rude, arrogant, or showing disdain toward others

Examples

His snottiness showed
His snottiness was obvious when he refused to talk to anyone he considered beneath him.
People were put off by the snottiness she displayed at the party.
If you drop the snottiness, you might find it easier to make friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - an attitude of superiority and self-importance
  • antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: rudeness - impolite or disrespectful behavior
