extended family

noun
wider family including relatives beyond parents and children
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈstend.əd ˈfæm.ə.liː/ /; / /ɪk.ˈstend.əd ˈfæm.liː/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈstend.ɪd ˈfæm.ɪ.liː/ /; / /ɪk.ˈstend.ɪd ˈfæm.liː/ (BrE)
