lithographically

adverb
by lithography
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or using lithography, especially in printing or producing images

Examples

Printed lithographically
The invitations were lithographically printed to achieve high-quality images.
Many old maps were produced lithographically before digital printing became common.
The artist prefers to reproduce her work lithographically for its unique texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: digitally - in a manner using digital technology
  • synonym: mechanically - in a manner using machines or mechanical processes
