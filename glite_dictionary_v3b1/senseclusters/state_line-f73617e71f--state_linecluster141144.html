state line

noun
boundary between two states
Pronunciation: / steɪt laɪn / (AmE), / steɪt laɪn / (BrE)
the boundary separating two states within a country, especially in the United States.

Examples

The state line sign
A sign on the highway marked the state line between Texas and Oklahoma.
Police jurisdiction often ends at the state line.
We crossed the state line just before noon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - the official line that separates two political or geographical areas
  • degree lower: county line - the boundary between two counties
