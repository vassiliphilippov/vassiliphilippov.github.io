milter

noun
male breeding fish
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪltər / (AmE), / ˈmɪltə / (BrE)
a male fish, especially a salmon or trout, during the breeding season

Examples

Spawning milter
The biologist identified the milter by its brighter colors during spawning season.
During the breeding run, each milter competes to fertilize the eggs.
Fishermen can often spot a milter by its distinctive hooked jaw.

Related Terms

  • antonym: female - the female fish, especially during breeding
  • synonym: spawner - a fish that releases eggs or sperm during breeding
