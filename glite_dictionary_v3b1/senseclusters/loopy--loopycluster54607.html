loopy

adjective
covered with or having loops
Pronunciation: / ˈluːpi / (AmE), / ˈluːpi / (BrE)
having many loops, or covered with loops, especially in texture or pattern.

Examples

Loopy handwriting
She admired the loopy handwriting in the letter, full of big curls.
The artist created a loopy wire sculpture for the exhibition.
The scarf had a loopy pattern that made it look extra cozy.
