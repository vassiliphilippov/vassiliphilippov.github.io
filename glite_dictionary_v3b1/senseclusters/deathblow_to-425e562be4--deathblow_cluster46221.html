deathblow to

noun
decisive end or destruction of something
2.4 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθˌbloʊ tu / (AmE), / ˈdɛθˌbləʊ tʊ / (BrE)
an event or action that completely destroys, defeats, or puts an end to something

Examples

A deathblow to hope
Losing their biggest sponsor proved to be the deathblow to the team's championship hopes.
The new law delivered a deathblow to the outdated policy.
The resignation of the CEO was the deathblow to the troubled company.
