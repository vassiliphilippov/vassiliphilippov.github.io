northeasterly

adjective
oriented toward the northeast
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈistərli / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːstəli / (BrE)
(of a place or direction) situated in or oriented toward the northeast.

Examples

Northeasterly corner
A northeasterly window lets in the morning light.
The hikers took the northeasterly trail through the mountains.
They built the house on the northeasterly side of the lake for better sunlight.
