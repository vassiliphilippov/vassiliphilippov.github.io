establishment

noun
authority figures; ruling group
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈstæblɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the people or groups collectively exercising authority or control in an organization, institution, or society, especially those who are seen as maintaining the status quo

Examples

The political establishment
He was eager to join the academic establishment and influence future research.
She often challenged the ideas promoted by the establishment.
The new policy drew sharp criticism from the political establishment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administration - the group of people who manage or direct an organization
  • synonym: governance - the act or process of governing or overseeing control
