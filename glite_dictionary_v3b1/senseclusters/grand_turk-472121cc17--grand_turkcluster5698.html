Grand Turk

proper noun
historical ruler, esp. Ottoman Sultan
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd tɝk / (AmE), / ɡrænd tɜːk / (BrE)
the ruler of a Muslim country, especially the Ottoman Empire; used historically for the Sultan of Turkey

Examples

Grand Turk ruler
During the sixteenth century, the Grand Turk commanded respect and fear across Europe.
In European writings, the Grand Turk was often depicted as a mysterious and powerful figure.
The ambassador was received by the Grand Turk at the imperial palace in Istanbul.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emperor - sovereign ruler of great power and rank, especially one ruling an empire
  • synonym: sultan - a Muslim sovereign, especially of the former Ottoman Empire
