religiousness

noun
extreme conscientiousness; devout attentiveness
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəsnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being extremely conscientious or meticulous, especially as if guided by religious devotion.

Examples

Religiousness in work
His religiousness in following safety procedures kept everyone calm during the emergency.
She approached her daily exercise routine with a kind of religiousness that impressed her friends.
The chef's religiousness about cleanliness ensured the kitchen was always spotless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or thoroughness
  • synonym: devotion - deep commitment or enthusiasm for a cause or activity
