employment

noun
the act of hiring someone
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of giving someone a job or the process of hiring someone

Examples

Employment of new staff
Her duties included the employment of seasonal workers during the busy holiday period.
Regulations ensure the fair employment of qualified candidates from diverse backgrounds.
The company’s recent employment of several new managers has changed the team dynamic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dismissal - the act of removing someone from their job
  • synonym: recruitment - the process of finding and hiring new employees
