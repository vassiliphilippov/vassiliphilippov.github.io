friendly

adjective
easy to use or understand
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛn(d)li / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn(d)li / (BrE)
Easy to use, understand, or access; designed to be simple or helpful for the user or consumer.

Examples

Friendly website design

The new software is very friendly for beginners with its simple interface.
We chose a more friendly option for people who aren't tech-savvy.
