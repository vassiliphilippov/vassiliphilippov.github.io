rotisserie

noun
oven with a rotating spit for meat
7.7
Pronunciation: / roʊˈtɪsəri / (AmE), / rəʊˈtɪsəri / (BrE)
an oven or broiler fitted with a rotating spit where meat is cooked as it turns

Examples

Rotisserie oven
He roasted the chicken in the rotisserie for a perfectly crispy skin.
Many supermarkets sell pre-cooked rotisserie chickens near the deli section.
The chef demonstrated how to use a rotisserie to cook lamb evenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spit - a long, thin rod used to hold meat while it is roasted over a fire or in an oven
