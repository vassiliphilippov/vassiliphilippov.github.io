trace

verb
draw or outline lines on a surface
1.0
Pronunciation: / treɪs / (AmE), / treɪs / (BrE)
to draw lines or marks on a surface, often following or outlining a shape

Examples

To trace a line
He traced the map with his finger, following the path they would take.
She carefully traced the outline of the maple leaf onto her notebook.
The artist traced a series of elegant lines across the canvas to form the sketch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draw - to make marks or a picture with a pen or pencil
  • synonym: sketch - to make a quick drawing
