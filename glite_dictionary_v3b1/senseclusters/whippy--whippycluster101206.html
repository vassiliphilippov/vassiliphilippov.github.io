whippy

adjective
flexible; bends and springs back
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpi / (AmE), / ˈwɪpi / (BrE)
flexible and able to bend and spring back easily without breaking

Examples

A whippy walking stick
He preferred a whippy walking stick made from young hazel wood.
The fishing rod was so whippy that it bent almost double when she caught a big fish.
These branches are whippy and won't break easily under pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigid - not able to bend or be forced out of shape; stiff
