inheritance

noun
genetic transmission of traits
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhɛrətəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns / (BrE)
In genetics, the process by which characteristics are passed from parents to offspring through their genes.

Examples

Genetic inheritance
Eye color is determined by inheritance from both parents.
Gregor Mendel studied patterns of inheritance in pea plants.
Many genetic diseases occur through inheritance rather than environmental factors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heredity - the passing of traits from parents to offspring
