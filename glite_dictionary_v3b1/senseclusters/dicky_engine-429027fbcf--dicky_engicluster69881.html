dicky engine

noun
an unreliable or faulty engine
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪki ˈɛnʤɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɪki ˈɛnʤɪn / (BrE)
an engine that is unreliable or faulty, often breaking down or failing to run properly

Examples

A dicky engine
After months of trouble, she finally replaced the dicky engine with a newer model.
My old van still runs, but the dicky engine makes every journey a gamble.
The mechanic warned that a dicky engine could leave us stranded on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faulty engine - an engine not working correctly
  • antonym: reliable engine - an engine that consistently works well without failure
