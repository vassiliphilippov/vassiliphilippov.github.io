mysterious

adjective
difficult to explain or understand
2.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /mɪ.ˈstɪr.ɪ.əs/ /; / /mə.ˈstɪr.ɪ.əs/ (AmE), /mɪ.ˈstɪə.rɪ.əs/ /; / /mə.ˈstɪə.rɪ.əs/ (BrE)
difficult or impossible to understand, explain, or identify; having an obscure or puzzling quality

Examples

A mysterious noise

The abandoned house at the end of the street has always been a mysterious place to the villagers.
