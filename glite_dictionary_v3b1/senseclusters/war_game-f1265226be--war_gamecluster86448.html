war game

noun
military simulation exercise
Pronunciation: / wɔr ɡeɪm / (AmE), / wɔː ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a simulation of a military operation used for training, demonstration, or testing strategies.

Examples

A war game simulation
Military commanders participate in war games to prepare for possible real-life conflicts.
The annual war game involved multiple countries' forces and lasted two weeks.
The army conducted a war game to test their new battle strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: simulation - imitation of a process or situation for training or study
