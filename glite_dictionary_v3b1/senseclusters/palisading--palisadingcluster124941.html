palisading

noun
fence of stakes
Pronunciation: / ˌpælɪˈseɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpælɪˈseɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
a fence or barrier made of closely set strong stakes or posts, used for defense or enclosure

Examples

The wooden palisading was reinforced to withstand attacks during the siege.
