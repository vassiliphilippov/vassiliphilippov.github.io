plumply

adverb
heavily or solidly
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmpli / (AmE), / ˈplʌmpli / (BrE)
in a way that is sudden, heavy, or solid, often producing a soft sound when sitting, falling, or placing something

Examples

Sat plumply down
She sat plumply on the sofa, exhausted after her long day at work.
The cat landed plumply on the soft cushion after jumping from the table.
He placed the package plumply on the counter, making a gentle thud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavily - in a way that involves great weight or force
  • antonym: lightly - in a way that involves little weight or force
