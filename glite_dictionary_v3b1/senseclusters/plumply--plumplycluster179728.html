plumply

adverb
heavily or solidly
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈplʌm.plɪ/ /; / /ˈplʌm.pliː/ (AmE), /ˈplʌm.plɪ/ /; / /ˈplʌm.pliː/ (BrE)
