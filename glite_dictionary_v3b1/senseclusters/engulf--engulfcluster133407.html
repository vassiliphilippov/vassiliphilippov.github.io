engulf

verb
to absorb or involve fully
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡʌlf / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡʌlf / (BrE)
to absorb or involve someone completely; to devote fully to a task or activity

Examples

To engulf in work
Passion for chess engulfed him during his teenage years.
She allowed grief to engulf her after the loss of her friend.
Sometimes, work can engulf us so completely that we forget to take breaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engage - to occupy or involve fully
  • synonym: immerse - to involve deeply; absorb fully
