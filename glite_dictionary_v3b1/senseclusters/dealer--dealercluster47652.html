dealer

noun
person selling illegal goods
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːlər / (AmE), / ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person who buys and sells illegal or illicit goods, especially drugs or stolen items

Examples

Drug dealer
Authorities are cracking down on dealer networks operating in the city.
He was known in the neighborhood as a dealer who sold stolen electronics.
The police arrested the suspected dealer during a late-night raid downtown.
