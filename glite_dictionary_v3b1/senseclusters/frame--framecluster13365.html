frame

noun
system shaping meaning or behavior
Pronunciation: / freɪm / (AmE), / freɪm / (BrE)
a structure of assumptions, standards, or ideas that shapes or gives meaning to behavior or understanding

Examples

Legal frame
The legal frame of society shapes what is considered acceptable conduct.
The sociologist analyzed how the cultural frame influences people's attitudes and actions.
Within this theoretical frame, all behaviors are interpreted as rational responses to the environment.
