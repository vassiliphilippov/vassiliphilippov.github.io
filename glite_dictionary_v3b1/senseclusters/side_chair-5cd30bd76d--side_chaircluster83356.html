side chair

noun
armless, straight-backed chair
1.0
Pronunciation: / saɪd tʃɛr / (AmE), / saɪd tʃɛː / (BrE)
A chair with a straight back and no arms, designed to be placed at the side of a table or room.

Examples

A side chair without arms
Each guest was shown to a side chair placed neatly along the wall.
The side chair was upholstered in a bright, cheerful fabric.
We added a classic side chair to the dining room for extra seating.

Related Terms

  • antonym: armchair - a chair with supports for the arms
