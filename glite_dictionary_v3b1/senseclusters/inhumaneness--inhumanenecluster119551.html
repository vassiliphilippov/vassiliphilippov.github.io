inhumaneness

noun
lack of compassion or humanity
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌhjuːməˈneɪnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˌhjuːməˈneɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking compassion or consideration for others; cruelty or lack of humanity

Examples

The inhumaneness of cruelty
Her inhumaneness became apparent when she refused to help those in need.
Many were shocked by the sheer inhumaneness of his actions.
The court condemned the inhumaneness of the treatment inflicted upon the prisoners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humaneness - the quality of compassion or kindness
  • synonym: inhumanity - cruel or barbarous behavior; the state of lacking compassion
