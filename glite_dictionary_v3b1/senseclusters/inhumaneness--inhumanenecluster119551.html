inhumaneness

noun
lack of compassion or humanity
4.0 7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌhjuːməˈneɪnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˌhjuːməˈneɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking compassion or consideration for others; cruelty or lack of humanity

Examples

The court condemned the inhumaneness of the treatment inflicted upon the prisoners.
