abandonment

noun
the act of giving something up
3.6 3.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbæn.dən.mənt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbæn.dən.mənt/ (BrE)
the act of giving something up completely; the process of leaving or discarding something or someone.

Examples

Abandonment of plans

She felt a deep sense of loss at the abandonment by her closest friends.
The abandonment of the old house left it to decay for years.
