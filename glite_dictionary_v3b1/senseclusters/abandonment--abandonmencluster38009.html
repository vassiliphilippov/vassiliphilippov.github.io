abandonment

noun
the act of giving something up
Pronunciation: / əˈbændənmənt / (AmE), / əˈbændənmənt / (BrE)
the act of giving something up completely; the process of leaving or discarding something or someone.

Examples

Abandonment of plans
After much thought, he announced the abandonment of his plans to move abroad.
She felt a deep sense of loss at the abandonment by her closest friends.
The abandonment of the old house left it to decay for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: renunciation - the formal rejection of something, typically a belief or claim
  • antonym: retention - the act of keeping possession of something
