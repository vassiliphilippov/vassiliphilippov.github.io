sticking plaster

noun
adhesive bandage for wounds
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkɪŋ ˌplæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɪkɪŋ ˌplɑːstə / (BrE)
A small piece of adhesive material used to cover and protect minor wounds.

Examples

A sticking plaster strip
Do you have a sticking plaster in your first aid kit?
He put a sticking plaster on his cut finger to stop the bleeding.
She covered the blister with a sticking plaster before going for a run.
