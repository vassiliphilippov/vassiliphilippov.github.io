cartridge

noun
replaceable module for a device
1.7 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.trɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈkɑːr.trədʒ/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.trɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈkɑː.trədʒ/ (BrE)
a small container or module designed to be inserted into a larger device, typically to provide a replaceable medium, substance, or component such as ink, tape, or data storage

Examples

Ink cartridge
He carefully slid the new tape cartridge into the player and pressed play.
I need to replace the ink cartridge in my printer before I can print more documents.
The video game console requires you to insert a cartridge to play different games.
