Synonyms

  • abetment
    9.5 0.01/m
    abetment involves active help or support in committing a crime, while incitement focuses on urging others by speech to do wrong.
  • instigation
    7.5 0.48/m
    instigation means provoking or starting trouble, while incitement specifically means urging people to commit illegal or violent acts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deterrence - the act of discouraging an action through fear or doubt
  • antonym: deterrent - something that discourages someone from taking action
  • antonym: discouragement - the act of trying to prevent something by making it seem difficult or by making someone less willing to do it
  • synonym: encouragement - the act of giving someone support or confidence to do something
  • synonym: instigation - the act of causing an event or action to happen, often something negative
  • synonym: provocation - the action of provoking or stirring up
