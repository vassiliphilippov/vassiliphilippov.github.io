grinch

verb
to spoil enjoyment
8.9
Pronunciation: /grɪntʃ/ (AmE), /grɪntʃ/ (BrE)
to spoil or prevent someone else's enjoyment, especially by being negative or unwilling to join in celebration

Examples

To grinch the party
Please don't grinch the holiday spirit by complaining about the decorations.
He tried not to grinch the fun, but he just wasn't in the mood to celebrate.
Nobody wants someone to grinch their birthday party with negative comments.
