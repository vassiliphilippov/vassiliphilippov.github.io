zona

noun
shingles rash
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊnə / (AmE), / ˈzəʊnə / (BrE)
a medical term for shingles, a painful skin rash with blisters caused by the varicella-zoster virus

Examples

Zona rash
The doctor diagnosed her with zona after she developed a painful rash.
Older adults are at higher risk of developing zona due to weakened immune systems.
Antiviral medication can help reduce the severity of zona if taken early.
