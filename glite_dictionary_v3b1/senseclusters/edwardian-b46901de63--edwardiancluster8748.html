Edwardian

adjective
of the era of Edward VII (1901–1910) in Britain
8.0 6.5
Pronunciation: / ɛdˈwɔrdiən / (AmE), / ɛdˈwɔːdɪən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the period in Britain during the reign of Edward VII (1901–1910), especially in terms of style, culture, or society.

Examples

Edwardian style furniture
She inherited a beautiful set of Edwardian furniture from her grandmother.
The film is set in Edwardian England and captures the elegance of the time.
The house was decorated in a distinctly Edwardian style with ornate moldings and high ceilings.
