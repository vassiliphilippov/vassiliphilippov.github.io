evocatively

adverb
in a vivid way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɑːkətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈvɒkətɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that brings strong images, feelings, or memories to mind

Examples

Evocatively describes nature
The author writes evocatively about the beauty of the countryside.
Music can evocatively transport listeners to another time or place.
The painting evocatively captures the mood of a stormy evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vividly - in a way that produces powerful feelings or clear images
