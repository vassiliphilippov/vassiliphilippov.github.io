brail

verb
gather a sail in with ropes
3.7 9.3
Pronunciation: / breɪl / (AmE), / breɪl / (BrE)
(of a sail) to gather in or take up with ropes for furling or securing, especially using a brail.

Examples

To brail a sail

The sailors began to brail the mainsail as the storm approached.
You must brail the sail quickly to avoid damage in high winds.
