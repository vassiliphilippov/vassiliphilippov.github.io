sociodrama

noun
drama about social issues
7.5
Pronunciation: /soʊ.sɪ.oʊ.ˈdrɑː.mə/ /; / /soʊ.sɪ.oʊ.ˈdræ.mə/ (AmE), /səʊ.sɪ.ə.ˈdrɑː.mə/ (BrE)
a group activity or performance where people act out social situations to explore or understand social issues, relationships, or conflicts, often for education or therapy

  • synonym: psychodrama - a dramatic activity focusing on personal psychological issues
  • synonym: role-play - acting out roles in a particular situation as a learning or therapeutic technique
