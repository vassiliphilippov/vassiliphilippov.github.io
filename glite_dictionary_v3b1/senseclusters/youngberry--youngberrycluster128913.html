youngberry

noun
hybrid berry shrub
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌŋˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈjʌŋˌbɛri / (BrE)
a thorny shrub, a hybrid of blackberry and dewberry, cultivated for its dark red edible fruit

Examples

Youngberry bush
The farmer planted several youngberry bushes along the fence for their delicious fruit.
Each summer, we pick fresh youngberries to make homemade jam.
A youngberry shrub can produce fruit for many years if properly cared for.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackberry - a dark purple-black edible berry from a bramble bush
  • synonym: loganberry - a hybrid berry that is a cross between a raspberry and a blackberry
