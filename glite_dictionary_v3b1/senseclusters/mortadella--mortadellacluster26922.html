mortadella

noun
Italian pork sausage
Pronunciation: / ˌmɔrtəˈdɛlə / (AmE), / ˌmɔːtəˈdɛlə / (BrE)
a large Italian sausage made from finely ground pork, often flavored with spices and cubes of fat

Examples

Slices of mortadella
She made a sandwich with slices of mortadella and cheese.
At the deli, he ordered a thick slice of mortadella for his lunch.
The chef added diced mortadella to the pasta salad for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bologna - a type of cooked, smoked sausage similar to mortadella
  • synonym: salami - a cured sausage made from fermented and air-dried meat
