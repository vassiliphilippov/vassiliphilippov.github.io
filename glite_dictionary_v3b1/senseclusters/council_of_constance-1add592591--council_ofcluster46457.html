Council of Constance

proper noun
historic church council (1414–1418) ending the Great Schism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl əv ˈkɑn.stəns / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl əv ˈkɒn.stəns / (BrE)
the ecumenical council held from 1414 to 1418 that ended the Great Schism in the Roman Catholic Church by resolving disputes over papal succession
