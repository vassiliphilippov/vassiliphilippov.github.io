retroviral

adjective
relating to retroviruses
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊˈvaɪrəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈvaɪərəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by retroviruses, which are viruses that use RNA to reproduce inside host cells

Examples

A retroviral test is often used to detect HIV in its early stages.
