larder

noun
small room or cupboard for storing food
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrdər / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdə / (BrE)
a small room or cupboard used for storing food, especially before refrigerators were common

Examples

A kitchen larder
After restocking the larder, they had enough supplies for the week.
She went to the larder to get some cheese for lunch.
The cottage had a cool, stone-walled larder to keep food fresh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pantry - a small room or closet in which food, utensils, and kitchen supplies are stored
