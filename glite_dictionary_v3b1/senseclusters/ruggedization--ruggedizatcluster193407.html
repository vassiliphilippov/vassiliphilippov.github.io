ruggedization

noun
making equipment more robust/durable
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌɡɪdəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌrʌɡ.ɪ.daɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌrʌɡɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌrʌɡ.ɪ.daɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of making equipment stronger and more durable, especially to withstand harsh use or conditions

Examples

Military laptops undergo ruggedisation to ensure functionality in extreme environments.
