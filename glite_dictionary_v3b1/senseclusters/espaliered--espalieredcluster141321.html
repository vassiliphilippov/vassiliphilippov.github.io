espaliered

adjective
grown flat on support
Pronunciation: / ɪˈspæliɚd / (AmE), / ɪˈspalɪəd / (BrE)
trained to grow flat against a wall or support, typically for decorative or space-saving purposes

Examples

Espaliered fruit trees
The gardener carefully pruned the espaliered pear trees along the brick wall.
Visitors admired the espaliered roses that decorated the courtyard entrance.
Fruit is easier to harvest from espaliered plants because their branches are spread out flat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free-standing - growing naturally without support or shaping
  • synonym: trained - grown in a controlled shape or direction
