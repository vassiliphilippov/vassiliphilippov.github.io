heehaw

interjection
mocking exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːˌhɔ / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhɔː / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating a donkey's bray, used to express loud laughter or to mock someone

Examples

A loud heehaw of laughter
The audience burst into a loud heehaw after the comedian's joke.
He let out a heehaw to mock his friend's silly mistake.
Whenever something really funny happened, she would shout heehaw and clap her hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boo - an exclamation used to show disapproval or mockery
  • synonym: haha - an exclamation used to show laughter
