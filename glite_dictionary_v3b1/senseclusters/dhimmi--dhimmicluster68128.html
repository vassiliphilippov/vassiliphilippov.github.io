dhimmi

noun
offensive insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmi / (AmE), / ˈdɪmi / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person seen as weak or overly submissive, especially toward Muslims or Islamic influence

Examples

Called a dhimmi insultingly
The blogger was criticized for calling his opponent a dhimmi during the heated debate.
Some commentators use the word dhimmi as an insult in political discussions.
He was labeled a dhimmi for supporting policies that favored religious minorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appeaser - a person who tries to please or pacify others, often seen as weak
  • antonym: resister - a person who opposes or stands up against something
