glory

verb
rejoice with pride; take great pleasure
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔri / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri / (BrE)
to take great pride or pleasure in something; to rejoice proudly

Examples

To glory in success
He glories in his independence and unique style.
Many ancient warriors gloried in their victories on the battlefield.
She glories in every opportunity to help others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boast - to talk with excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • antonym: despair - to lose hope; opposite of feeling pride or joy
