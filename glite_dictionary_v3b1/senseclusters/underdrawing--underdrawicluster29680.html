underdrawing

noun
preliminary sketch
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌdrɔɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
a preliminary sketch or outline made on a surface before the final painting is applied

Examples

In Renaissance paintings, the underdrawing often guided the placement of colors and details.
