rayon

noun
synthetic fabric similar to silk
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.ɒn / (BrE)
a synthetic fiber made from regenerated cellulose, often used as a fabric resembling silk

Examples

Rayon fabric
Because rayon is breathable, it's often used in summer clothing.
She prefers rayon shirts for their comfort and affordable price.
The dress was made from rayon, giving it a smooth, silky feel.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: silk - a natural fiber known for its softness and sheen, produced by silkworms
  • synonym: viscose - a type of rayon fabric made from cellulose
