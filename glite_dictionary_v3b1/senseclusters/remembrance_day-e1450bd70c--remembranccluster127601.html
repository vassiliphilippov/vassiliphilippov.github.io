Synonyms

  • Remembrance Sunday
    4.6 0.01/m
    Remembrance Sunday is the ceremony on the Sunday nearest November 11 to honour war dead, while Remembrance Day refers to the same event.
  • Poppy Day
    3.9 0.00/m
    Poppy Day is the informal British name when people wear poppies to remember soldiers, while Remembrance Day is the formal name for the same observance.
