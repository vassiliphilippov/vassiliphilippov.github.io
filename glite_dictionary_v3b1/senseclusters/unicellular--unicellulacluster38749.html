unicellular

adjective
having or consisting of a single cell
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈsɛljələr / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈsɛljʊlə / (BrE)
(of an organism or structure) having or consisting of a single cell.

Examples

Unicellular organism
Bacteria are classic examples of unicellular organisms.
Some algae are unicellular, while others form colonies or multicellular structures.
The scientist observed a unicellular organism swimming swiftly under the microscope.
