neuraminidase

noun
viral enzyme
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊrəˈmɪnɪˌdeɪs /; / nʊˌræməˈnɪdeɪs / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəˈmɪnɪˌdeɪz /; / njʊəˌræmɪˈneɪdeɪs / (BrE)
