biological group

noun
plants or animals classified together
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl gruːp/ (AmE), /baɪ.ə.ˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl gruːp/ (BrE)
a set of plants or animals that are classified together based on shared biological characteristics

Examples

A biological group of animals
A biological group may include only plants, only animals, or a mixture of both.
Species that share certain traits are placed in the same biological group.
The conference discussed the evolution of each major biological group.
