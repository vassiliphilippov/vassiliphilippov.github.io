fixing

noun
preservation process in histology
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪk.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪk.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process in histology of preserving and hardening a tissue sample so that it retains its structure as in the living body

Examples
