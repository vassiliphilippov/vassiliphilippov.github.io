mining engineer

noun
engineer specializing in mining
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnɪŋ ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnɪŋ ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person trained in the engineering principles applied to the design, construction, and operation of mines

Examples

Mining engineer designs mines
As a mining engineer, he traveled around the world to assess mineral deposits.
She trained as a mining engineer before joining a large construction firm.
The company hired a mining engineer to oversee safety at the new mine.
