nickname

verb
give a nickname to
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkˌneɪm / (AmE), / ˈnɪk.neɪm / (BrE)
to give a nickname to someone or something; to call by a familiar or informal name instead of the real name

Examples

To nickname a friend
Everyone at school nicknamed him 'Speedy' because he ran so fast.
The press quickly nicknamed the new leader 'Iron Lady'.
They affectionately nicknamed the city 'The Big Apple'.
