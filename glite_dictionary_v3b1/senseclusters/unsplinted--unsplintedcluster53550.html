unsplinted

adjective
without a splint
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsplɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsplɪntɪd / (BrE)
not supported or immobilized with a splint, especially referring to a bone or injury.

Examples

Unsplinted broken arm
The doctor examined the unsplinted arm to check for further swelling.
Leaving the fracture unsplinted could delay the healing process.
The nurse noticed that the patient's finger was still unsplinted after the accident.
