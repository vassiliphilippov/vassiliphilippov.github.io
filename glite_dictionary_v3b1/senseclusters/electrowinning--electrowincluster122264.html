electrowinning

noun
metal extraction process
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈwɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈwɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
a process that uses electricity to extract metals from a solution

Examples

Electrowinning process
Copper is often recovered from solution using electrowinning in modern mining operations.
The company invested in new electrowinning equipment to improve metal recovery rates.
Zinc production facilities rely on electrowinning to obtain pure metal from leach solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrolysis - chemical decomposition by passing an electric current through a solution
  • antonym: smelting - extracting metal from ore by heating and melting
