wrongdoing

noun
illegal or immoral behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔŋˌduːɪŋ /; / ˈrɔŋˌduɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒŋˌduːɪŋ / (BrE)
illegal or immoral behavior; actions that break laws or moral standards

Examples

Serious wrongdoing

Corporate wrongdoing was uncovered in the financial audit.
She denied any wrongdoing in connection to the missing funds.
The committee investigated the allegations of wrongdoing by public officials.
