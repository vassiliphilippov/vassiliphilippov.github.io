nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide

noun
biological coenzyme involved in metabolism
7.4
Pronunciation: / nɪˌkoʊtɪnəˌmaɪd əˈdiːn ˌdaɪˌnuːkliəˈtaɪd / (AmE), / nɪˌkəʊtɪnəˌmaɪd əˈdiːn ˌdaɪˌnjuːklɪəˈtaɪd / (BrE)
a coenzyme found in most living cells, derived from vitamin B3, which functions as a reductant in various metabolic reactions
