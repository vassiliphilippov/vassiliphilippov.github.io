indecorousness

noun
lack of decorum or propriety
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛkərəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛkərəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being improper or lacking good taste and decorum in behavior or appearance

Examples

Clear indecorousness behavior
Her constant interruptions showed a clear indecorousness that surprised everyone at the meeting.
The indecorousness of his jokes made the formal dinner rather awkward.
They were criticized for the indecorousness of their attire at the solemn event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decorum - behavior in keeping with good taste and propriety
  • synonym: impropriety - failure to observe standards of proper behavior
