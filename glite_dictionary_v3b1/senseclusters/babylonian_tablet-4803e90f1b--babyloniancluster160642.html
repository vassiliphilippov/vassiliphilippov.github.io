Babylonian tablet

noun
ancient clay artifact from Babylonian civilization
5.0
Pronunciation: / bəˌboʊˈloʊniən ˈtæblət / (AmE), / bəˌbəʊˈləʊniən ˈtæblət / (BrE)
an ancient clay tablet inscribed with cuneiform writing from the Babylonian civilization, often bearing records, laws, or literary texts

Examples

An ancient Babylonian tablet
Archaeologists discovered a Babylonian tablet detailing ancient trade transactions.
Students studied a Babylonian tablet to learn about early astronomical observations.
