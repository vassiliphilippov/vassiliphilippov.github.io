Micrococcus

noun
genus of spherical bacteria
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɑkəs /; / ˌmaɪkrəˈkɑːkəs / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪkrəˈkɒkəs /; / ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒkəs / (BrE)
a genus of round (spherical), usually harmless bacteria found in soil, water, dust, and on human skin
