given

noun
assumed fact or condition
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvən / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvən / (BrE)
a fact, condition, or piece of information that is accepted as true or certain in a particular situation, especially as a starting point for reasoning or discussion

Examples

A math given
Before solving the equation, write down all the givens provided in the problem.
In this experiment, temperature and pressure are treated as givens.
The teacher asked the students to identify the givens before attempting the proof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assumption - something that is accepted as true without proof
  • synonym: premise - a statement or idea that is accepted as true and used as the basis for reasoning
