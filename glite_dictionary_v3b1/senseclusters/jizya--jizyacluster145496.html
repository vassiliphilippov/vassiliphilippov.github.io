jizya

noun
tax on non-Muslims
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪziə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪziə / (BrE)
a tax historically imposed on non-Muslims living under Islamic rule

Examples

Pay jizya tax
Under Islamic law, non-Muslim citizens were required to pay the jizya as a form of tax.
The collection of jizya was an important source of revenue for some medieval Islamic states.
Historical records show that the jizya varied in amount depending on the region and period.
