underrate

verb
estimate as less than real value; underestimate
1.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.də.ˈreɪt/ /; / /ən.də.ˈreɪt/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈreɪt/ /; / /ən.də.ˈreɪt/ (BrE)
to estimate the value, ability, or importance of someone or something as less than it actually is; to underestimate

Examples

To underrate ability
Critics often underrate her performance in that film, but it was truly exceptional.
Don't underrate the power of small daily habits to transform your life over time.
Many people underrate the risks involved in starting a new business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underestimate - to judge something as less than its actual value or importance
