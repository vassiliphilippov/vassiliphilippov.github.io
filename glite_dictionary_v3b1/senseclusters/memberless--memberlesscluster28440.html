memberless

adjective
without members
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmbərˌlɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɛmbələs / (BrE)
Having no members; lacking any individuals belonging to it.

Examples

A memberless group
A memberless organization cannot carry out its intended functions.
After the last person resigned, the committee stood memberless for several months.
The online forum became memberless after persistent inactivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled or occupied
