postal order

noun
money order issued by a post office
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstl ˌɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstl ˌɔːdə / (BrE)
a written document purchased at a post office allowing the stated sum to be paid to a named person at another post office

Examples

A postal order form
Please send your application together with a postal order for the registration fee.
She received a postal order for her birthday from her aunt.
You can pay the fee by postal order or cheque.

Related Terms

  • synonym: money order - an order for the payment of money, similar to a postal order but issued by a bank or other entity
