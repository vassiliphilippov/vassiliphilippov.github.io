ethnological

adjective
relating to ethnology
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθ.nəˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌɛθ.nəˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of the characteristics and differences of various peoples and the relationships between them (ethnology).

Examples

An ethnological study
An ethnological approach can provide valuable insights into the dynamics of cultural change.
Her ethnological research focused on the traditions of indigenous communities in South America.
The museum boasts a large collection of ethnological artifacts from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethnology - the study of the characteristics and differences of peoples
