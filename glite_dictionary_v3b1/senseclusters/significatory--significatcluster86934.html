significatory

noun
sign or indicator
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkətəri / (BrE)
a person or thing that signifies, indicates, or serves as a sign of something

Examples

A significatory object
The red light acted as a significatory for drivers to stop at the intersection.
In ancient cultures, the eagle was often a significatory of power and freedom.
The badge on his uniform served as a significatory of his rank in the organization.
