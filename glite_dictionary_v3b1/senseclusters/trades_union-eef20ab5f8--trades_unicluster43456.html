trades union

noun
an organization of employees for collective bargaining
2.7 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / tre…™dz ÀàjuÀêni…ôn / (AmE), / tre…™dz ÀàjuÀêni…ôn / (BrE)
an organization of workers formed to protect and advance their rights and interests through collective bargaining with employers

Examples

The trades union meeting
Many members of the trades union attended the meeting to vote on the pay proposal.
She joined a trades union to help campaign for better working conditions.
The company negotiated a wage agreement with the local trades union.

Synonyms

  • trade union
    2.8 3.27/m
    trade union is the common name for worker organizations that negotiate with employers, while trades union is a less used variant.
  • labor union
    2.7 3.79/m
    labor union is the American term for worker groups that bargain with employers, while trades union is a British-style phrase.
  • union
    2.3 71/m
    union is a short, general word for a workers' organization that bargains with employers, while trades union is a fuller phrase naming that group.
  • brotherhood
    3.5 15/m
    brotherhood is a fraternal-style worker organization often tied to one trade or male membership, while trades union is a general workers' group.
