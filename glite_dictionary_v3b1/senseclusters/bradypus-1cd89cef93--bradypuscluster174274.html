Examples

Bradypus genus
The Bradypus genus is well known for its extremely slow movement and tree-dwelling lifestyle.
Scientists have identified several species within the Bradypus genus living in rainforests.
Unlike two-toed sloths, members of the Bradypus genus have three toes on each limb.
