Dioon

noun
genus of cycads from Mexico and Central America
Pronunciation: / diˈoʊ.ɒn / (AmE), / dɪˈəʊ.ɒn / (BrE)
A genus of arborescent cycads native to Mexico and Central America, sometimes included in the family Cycadaceae.
