evaporation

noun
the process of liquid turning into vapor
3.2 2.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌvapəˈreɪʃən /; / ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which a liquid (especially water) changes into vapor, usually because of heat or exposure to air
