out of keeping

adjective
not appropriate or consistent
6.2
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv ˈkipɪŋ / (AmE), / aʊt əv ˈkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
not consistent with what is considered correct, appropriate, or expected in a particular situation

Examples

Out of keeping with style
Her rude remark was completely out of keeping with her usual politeness.
His casual clothes were out of keeping with the formality of the event.
The loud music seemed out of keeping with the quiet atmosphere of the restaurant.
