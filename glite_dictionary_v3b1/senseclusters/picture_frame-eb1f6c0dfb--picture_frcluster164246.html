picture frame

noun
electronic device for displaying photos
Pronunciation: / c8p6akt835a fre6am / (AmE), / c8p6akt8359 fre6am / (BrE)
An electronic device with a screen that displays digital photographs, often able to run slideshows and receive images from memory cards or the internet.

Examples

Digital picture frame
She bought a digital picture frame to display rotating family photos on her mantel.
The new picture frame connects to Wi‑Fi so grandparents receive new photos instantly.
During the party, he set the picture frame to slideshow so guests could see baby pictures.
