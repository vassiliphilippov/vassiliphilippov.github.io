hipshot

adjective
with misaligned hip
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɪpˌʃɒt / (BrE)
having a hip that is out of alignment or deformed, especially as a result of injury or abnormality

Examples

Hipshot dog
The old dog became hipshot after years of running on rough ground.
A hipshot horse may have difficulty walking or standing for long periods.
The veterinarian explained that the animal was hipshot due to a previous injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lame - unable to walk properly due to injury or deformity
