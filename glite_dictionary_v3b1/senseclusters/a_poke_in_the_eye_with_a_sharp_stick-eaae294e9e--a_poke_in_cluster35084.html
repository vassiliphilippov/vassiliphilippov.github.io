a poke in the eye with a sharp stick

noun
something very unpleasant (humorous expression)
Pronunciation: / ə poʊk ɪn ði aɪ wɪð ə ʃɑrp stɪk / (AmE), / ə pəʊk ɪn ði aɪ wɪð ə ʃɑːp stɪk / (BrE)
a humorous phrase used to describe something very unpleasant or to say that even an undesirable situation is better than something far worse

Examples

A poke in the eye with a sharp stick phrase
Losing your keys is inconvenient, but it's not as bad as a poke in the eye with a sharp stick.
She admitted that the job wasn't great, but it's better than a poke in the eye with a sharp stick.
When asked about the bland dinner, he joked that it was still better than a poke in the eye with a sharp stick.
