foliar

adjective
of plant leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊliər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊliə / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the leaves of a plant

Examples

Foliar fertilizer
The gardener applied a foliar fertilizer to help the plants grow stronger.
Researchers studied the foliar structure to understand how the plant absorbs sunlight.
A foliar disease can quickly spread through a field if not treated early.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leafy - having many leaves or covered with leaves
