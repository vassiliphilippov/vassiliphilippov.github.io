orangish

adjective
somewhat orange in color
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.ɪn.dʒɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒr.ɪn.dʒɪʃ / (BrE)
having a color that is somewhat like orange, between red and yellow.

Examples

An orangish color
She wore an orangish dress that stood out in the crowd.
The sky turned an orangish hue as the sun began to set.
The walls were painted an orangish color to brighten the room.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: yellowish - somewhat yellow in color
