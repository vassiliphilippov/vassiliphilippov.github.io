thatcher

noun
person who thatches roofs
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈθætʃər / (AmE), / ˈθætʃə / (BrE)
a person skilled in making roofs from straw, reeds, or similar plant materials

Examples

A thatcher makes roofs
In some rural villages, a skilled thatcher is still in high demand.
She apprenticed with a local thatcher to learn the traditional craft.
The thatcher repaired the old cottage roof with expertly layered straw.
