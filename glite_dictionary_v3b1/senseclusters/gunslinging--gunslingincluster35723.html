gunslinging

noun
aggressive behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
aggressive or confrontational behavior, especially in speech or debate, intended to intimidate or dominate others

Examples

Political gunslinging
The political debate turned into a contest of gunslinging rather than thoughtful discussion.
She was tired of all the gunslinging at the office meetings and wanted a more respectful environment.
Instead of gunslinging, the team decided to focus on finding solutions together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diplomacy - the skill of dealing with people in a sensitive and effective way
  • synonym: hostility - unfriendly or aggressive behavior
