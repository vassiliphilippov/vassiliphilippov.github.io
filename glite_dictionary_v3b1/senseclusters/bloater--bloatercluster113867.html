bloater

noun
lightly smoked whole herring
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊtər / (AmE), / ˈbləʊtə / (BrE)
a large herring that has been lightly salted and briefly smoked without being gutted

Examples

Smoked bloater fish
I had a bloater for breakfast, freshly smoked from the local fishmonger.
She remembered her grandfather preparing bloaters over the fire on cold mornings.
The restaurant specializes in traditional dishes such as bloater and kippers.
