butt

noun
thicker end of an object or handle
7.4
Pronunciation: / bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
the thicker or base end of something, such as a handle or the bottom of a plant

Examples

The butt of a hammer
He gripped the butt of the hammer tightly as he worked.
She carved her initials into the butt of the old walking stick.
The butt of the axe was chipped from years of use.
After the storm, only the butt of the once-tall pine remained above ground.
Foresters measured the diameter of the butt before deciding where to make the cut.
The butt of the old oak still sprouted new shoots each spring.
