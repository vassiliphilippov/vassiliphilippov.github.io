Bambusa vulgaris

noun
species of highly vigorous bamboo
8.4
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbuːsə vʌlˈɡɛrɪs / (AmE), / bamˈbuːsə vʌlˈɡɛːrɪs / (BrE)
A species of bamboo with vigorous growth and thin-walled culms, usually striped green and yellow, widely cultivated in tropical regions.

Examples

Bambusa vulgaris bamboo plant
