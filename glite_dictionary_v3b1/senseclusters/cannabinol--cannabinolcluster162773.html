cannabinol

noun
cannabis compound
Pronunciation: / kəˈnæbɪnɔl / (AmE), / kəˈnæbɪnɒl / (BrE)
a mildly psychoactive chemical compound found in cannabis, sometimes formed by the breakdown of THC
