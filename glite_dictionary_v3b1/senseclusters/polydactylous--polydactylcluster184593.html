polydactylous

adjective
having extra fingers or toes
3.6 8.4
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.ˈdæk.tɪ.ləs/ /; / /pɑː.lɪ.ˈdæk.tə.ləs/ (AmE), /pɒ.lɪ.ˈdæk.tɪ.ləs/ /; / /pɒ.lɪ.ˈdæk.tə.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a person or other vertebrate) having more than the usual number of fingers or toes.

Examples

A polydactylous hand

Some genetic conditions in humans can result in a polydactylous appearance of hands or feet.
The newborn kitten was found to be polydactylous, possessing extra toes on its front paws.
