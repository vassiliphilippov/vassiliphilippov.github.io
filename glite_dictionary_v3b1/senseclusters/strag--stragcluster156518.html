strag

verb
to fall behind
9.9
Pronunciation: / stræɡ / (AmE), / stræɡ / (BrE)
to fall behind or move away from a group, especially while walking or traveling together

Examples

To strag behind
During the long hike, a few children began to strag at the back of the group.
If you strag too far behind, you might get lost in the crowd.
The coach encouraged the players not to strag during the morning run.

Related Terms

  • antonym: keep up - to move at the same speed as others
  • synonym: lag - to move or progress more slowly than others
