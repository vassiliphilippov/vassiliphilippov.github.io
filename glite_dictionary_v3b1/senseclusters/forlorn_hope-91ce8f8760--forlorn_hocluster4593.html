forlorn hope

noun
a hopeless or desperate enterprise
Pronunciation: / fərˈlɔrn ˈhoʊp / (AmE), / fəˈlɔːn ˈhəʊp / (BrE)
a venture or undertaking in which success is extremely unlikely; a desperate or hopeless enterprise

Examples

A forlorn hope venture
Rescuing the stranded hikers in such dangerous weather was a forlorn hope, yet they tried anyway.
Starting his own business with so much debt seemed like a forlorn hope to many, but he persisted.
The soldiers volunteered for the forlorn hope, fully aware they might not return.
