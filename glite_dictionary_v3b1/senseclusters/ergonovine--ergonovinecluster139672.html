ergonovine

noun
alkaloid used to contract uterus
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…úÀêr…°…ôÀàno ävin / (AmE), / Àå…úÀê…°…ôÀàn…ô äviÀên / (BrE)
