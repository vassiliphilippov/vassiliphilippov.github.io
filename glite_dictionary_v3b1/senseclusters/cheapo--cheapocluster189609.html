cheapo

noun
stingy person
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːpəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is very unwilling to spend money

Examples

Mark is a cheapo
Everyone knows Mark is a cheapo who never tips at restaurants.
My friends call me a cheapo because I always look for discounts.
She didn't want to seem like a cheapo at the group dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheapskate - a person who hates spending money
  • synonym: miser - a person who hoards money and spends as little as possible
  • antonym: spender - a person who spends money freely
