squeezy

adjective
cramped or crowded
Pronunciation: / ˈskwiːzi / (AmE), / ˈskwiːzi / (BrE)
informal: (of a place) uncomfortably small or crowded; cramped.

Examples

Squeezy room
The elevator was so squeezy that we could barely move our arms.
It felt squeezy in the tiny café during the lunch rush.
We found the hotel room a bit too squeezy for three people and all our luggage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spacious - having plenty of space
