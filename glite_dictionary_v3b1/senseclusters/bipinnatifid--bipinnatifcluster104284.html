bipinnatifid

adjective
leaf with deeply divided, twice-lobed segments
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪˌpɪn.əˈtaɪ.fɪd / (AmE), / ˌbɪˌpɪn.əˈtɪf.ɪd / (BrE)
(of a leaf) pinnatifid with the segments also pinnatifid; having twice-divided lobed segments arranged on either side of a common axis.

Examples

Bipinnatifid leaf segments
A botanist identified the rare plant by its unique bipinnatifid foliage.
Some species display bipinnatifid leaf structures, distinguishing them from their relatives.
The fern's bipinnatifid leaves made its fronds look especially delicate.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: pinnatifid - (of a leaf) with lobes cut toward the midrib but not reaching it
