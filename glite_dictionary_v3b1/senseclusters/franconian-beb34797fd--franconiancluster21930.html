Franconian

adjective
of the Franks
Pronunciation: / fræŋˈkoʊniən / (AmE), / fræŋˈkəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the Franks, an early medieval Germanic people, or to their culture, language, or institutions

Examples

Franconian law code
The historian specialized in Franconian law and its influence on medieval Europe.
Several Franconian dialects are still spoken in parts of Germany today.
The exhibition featured Franconian jewelry and weapons from the early Middle Ages.
