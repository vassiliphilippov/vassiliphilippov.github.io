tweener

noun
tennis shot between legs
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈtwiːnə / (BrE)
a shot in tennis where the player hits the ball between their legs, usually while facing away from the net

Examples

Tennis tweener shot
The crowd cheered when he hit a perfect tweener to win the rally.
She practiced the tweener for weeks before using it in a real match.
It takes great skill and timing to execute a tweener successfully during a game.
