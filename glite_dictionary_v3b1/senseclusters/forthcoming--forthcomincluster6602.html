Synonyms

  • open
    1.0 30/m
    open is willing to share ideas and feelings, while forthcoming means ready to give information when asked.
  • upfront
    4.3 3/m
    upfront is bluntly honest about intentions or feelings, while forthcoming emphasizes readiness to talk and give information.
  • candid
    4.6 4/m
    candid is openly honest, even about hard facts, while forthcoming focuses on being willing to speak and share information.
  • forthright
    5.5 1/m
    forthright is direct and sometimes blunt in speech, while forthcoming is more about being willing to answer or talk openly.
  • straightforward
    3.6 2/m
    straightforward is plain and clear, often without emotion, while forthcoming stresses readiness to give information and be communicative.
  • transparent
    4.1 3/m
    transparent is fully open and clear, often used about systems or people, while forthcoming focuses on being willing to talk.
