indiscipline

noun
lack of discipline or self-control
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˈdɪsəplɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪnˈdɪsɪplɪn / (BrE)
lack of discipline or control, especially resulting in disobedience or disorder.

Examples

Workplace indiscipline
Management is addressing indiscipline among employees with stricter rules.
Repeated acts of indiscipline led to his suspension from the team.
The teacher was concerned about the growing indiscipline in the classroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discipline - the practice of training people to obey rules or a code of behavior
  • synonym: self-control - the ability to control one's emotions and behavior
