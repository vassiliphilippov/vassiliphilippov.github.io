attaboy

interjection
praise or encouragement
Pronunciation: / ˈætəˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈætəˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express praise or encouragement, especially to a male

Examples

Attaboy after a win
When his friend finished the race, he cheered, 'attaboy!'
The coach shouted 'attaboy' after the player made a great pass.
You fixed the computer yourself? Attaboy!

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravo - an exclamation used to show approval or praise
  • synonym: well done - an exclamation expressing approval or praise
