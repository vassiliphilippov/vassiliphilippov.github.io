laugh track

noun
recorded laughter in TV/radio show soundtracks
4.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /læf træk/ (AmE), /lɑːf træk/ (BrE)
a recording of laughter added to the soundtrack of a radio or television show to simulate or reinforce audience response

Examples

A laugh track recording
She preferred comedies without a laugh track because they felt more authentic.
Some sitcoms use a laugh track to make the jokes seem funnier.
The absence of a laugh track gave the show a more realistic feel.
