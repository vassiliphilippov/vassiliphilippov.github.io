methhead

noun
slang for addict
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛθˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɛθˌhɛd / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who is addicted to methamphetamine

Examples

A methhead addict
The police found evidence that the house was used by methheads for drug activity.
He was labeled a methhead after years of struggling with addiction.
Neighbors complained about the noise coming from the methhead living next door.
