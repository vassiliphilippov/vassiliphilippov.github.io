remonstrative

adjective
expressing protest
8.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɑːnstrətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈmɒnstrətɪv / (BrE)
expressing protest, objection, or disapproval, often in a forceful or earnest way

Examples

Remonstrative gestures
His remonstrative gestures showed he was unhappy with the new policy.
The student's remonstrative letter to the principal outlined several concerns about the rules.
She gave her friend a remonstrative look when he interrupted the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accepting - willing to receive or agree without protest
  • synonym: complaining - expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: protesting - showing strong disagreement or objection
