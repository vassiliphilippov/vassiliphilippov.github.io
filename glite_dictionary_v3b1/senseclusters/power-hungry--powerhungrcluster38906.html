power-hungry

adjective
uses a lot of electrical power
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.ər ˈhʌŋ.griː/ /; / /ˈpaʊr ˈhʌŋ.griː/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.ə ˈhʌŋ.griː/ /; / /ˈpaʊ.ə ˈhʌŋ.grɪ/ (BrE)
(of a device, system, or software) using or requiring a large amount of electrical power or energy.

Examples

Power-hungry device
The gaming laptop is power-hungry, draining the battery after only an hour of play.
Updating multiple background apps made her phone feel slow and power-hungry by midday.
Large AI models can be power-hungry, requiring specialized cooling and significant electricity to run.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energy-efficient - using little energy for the service provided; opposite of using a lot of power
  • synonym: energy-hungry - needing or using a lot of energy
  • synonym: power-intensive - requiring a large amount of electrical power to operate
