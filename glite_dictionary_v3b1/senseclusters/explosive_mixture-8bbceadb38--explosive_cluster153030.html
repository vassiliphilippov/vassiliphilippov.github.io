explosive mixture

noun
mixture capable of exploding
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊsɪv ˈmɪkstʃər / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊsɪv ˈmɪkstʃə / (BrE)
a combination of substances that is capable of exploding when ignited or subjected to heat or shock

Examples

An explosive mixture of chemicals
Always keep chemicals apart that could combine to create an explosive mixture.

The factory had strict safety protocols to prevent any explosive mixture from forming in the storage tanks.
