Examples

Synonyms

  • scrunch
    6.4 0.11/m
    scrunch is the sharp, squashing sound you hear when you press or step on soft things, while crunch often involves teeth.
  • munch
    2.6 3.18/m
    munch is steady, audible chewing of food with a rhythmic jaw movement, while crunch often means biting hard or crushing underfoot.
