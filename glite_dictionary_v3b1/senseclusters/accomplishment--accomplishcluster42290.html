accomplishment

noun
acquired skill or ability
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑːmplɪʃmənt / (AmE), / əˈkʌmplɪʃmənt / (BrE)
an ability or skill that has been acquired through training or practice

Examples

A musical accomplishment
He listed piano as one of his accomplishments on his résumé.
Language is an accomplishment that opens doors to new cultures.
Singing is an accomplishment she developed through years of practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: achievement - a thing done successfully, often through effort or skill
  • synonym: skill - the ability to do something well, usually as a result of experience and training
