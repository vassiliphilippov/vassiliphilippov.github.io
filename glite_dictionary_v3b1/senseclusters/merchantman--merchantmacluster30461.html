merchantman

noun
cargo-carrying ship
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜːrtʃəntˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.tʃənt.mən / (BrE)
a ship designed or used for carrying cargo in trade, especially in international waters; a merchant ship.

Examples

A merchantman ship
Old paintings often depict a merchantman with tall masts and numerous sails.
Pirates attacked the merchantman as it sailed through dangerous waters.
The British merchantman was loaded with tea and spices bound for India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freighter - a ship or aircraft designed to carry goods rather than passengers
  • antonym: warship - a naval ship built and primarily intended for combat
