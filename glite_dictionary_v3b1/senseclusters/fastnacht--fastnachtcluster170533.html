fastnacht

noun
fried doughnut eaten on Shrove Tuesday
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːst.nɑːkt / (AmE), / ˈfæst.naxt / (BrE)
A doughnut or similar cake traditionally eaten on Shrove Tuesday, especially in Pennsylvania Dutch communities.

Examples

A fastnacht doughnut
Every year, my grandmother makes homemade fastnacht on Shrove Tuesday.
Many bakeries sell fastnachts only during the week before Lent.
We enjoyed a warm, sugar-coated fastnacht with our morning coffee.
