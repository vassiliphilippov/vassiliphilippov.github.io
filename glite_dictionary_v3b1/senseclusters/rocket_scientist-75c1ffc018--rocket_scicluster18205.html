rocket scientist

noun
engineer specializing in rockets
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkɪt ˌsaɪəntɪst /; / ˈrɑːkət ˌsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
an engineer or expert who designs, builds, or tests rockets and spacecraft

Examples

You don't have to be a rocket scientist to fix this problem—it's actually quite simple.
