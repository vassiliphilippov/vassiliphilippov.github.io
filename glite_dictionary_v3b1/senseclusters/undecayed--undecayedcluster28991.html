undecayed

adjective
not decayed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈkeɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈkeɪd / (BrE)
not having undergone decay, especially radioactive decay; still in its original state.

Examples

Undecayed atoms
The sample contained several undecayed atoms that scientists could still measure.
After many years, the artifact was surprisingly undecayed and well preserved.
Researchers analyzed the proportion of undecayed material to estimate the sample's age.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decayed - having undergone decomposition or breakdown
  • synonym: intact - not damaged or changed; complete
