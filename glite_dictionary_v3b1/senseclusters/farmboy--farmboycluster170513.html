farmboy

noun
boy working on farm
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːrmˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man who lives or works on a farm, often seen as coming from a rural background

Examples

The farmboy works
The farmboy woke up before sunrise to help with the chores.
As a farmboy, he learned the value of hard work at a young age.
The city girl was fascinated by the stories the farmboy told about rural life.
The young farmboy gazed in wonder at the tall buildings of the city.
Despite being a farmboy, he adapted quickly to university life in the city.
People often underestimate a farmboy because of his simple background.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city boy - a boy or young man from a city
  • synonym: country boy - a boy or young man from the countryside
  • synonym: farmhand - a person who works on a farm
