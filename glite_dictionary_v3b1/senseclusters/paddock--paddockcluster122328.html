paddock

noun
enclosed animal field
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpæ.dək/ (AmE), /ˈpæ.dək/ (BrE)
a small enclosed field where animals such as horses or cows are kept, exercised, or grazed

Examples

Sheep paddock
The farmer led the sheep into the paddock for the night.
After the race, the horses were cooled down in the shady paddock.
Children watched the cows grazing peacefully in the paddock near the farmhouse.
The horses grazed peacefully in the paddock.
After the race, the winning horse was led back to its paddock.
The farm has a large paddock where the cows spend most of their day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pasture - a large area of grass where animals graze
  • synonym: pen - a small enclosure for animals
