arc

noun
a curved shape or section
3.1
Pronunciation: / ɑrk / (AmE), / ɑːk / (BrE)
a curved shape or a part of the edge of a circle or other curve

Examples

A rainbow arc
A rainbow forms a bright arc across the sky.
She drew an elegant arc on the paper with her pencil.
The story follows the arc of the hero's journey from childhood to adulthood.
The bridge has an elegant arc stretching over the river.
The rainbow formed a perfect arc across the sky.
To draw a circle, start by marking an arc with a compass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chord - a straight line joining two points on a curve
  • synonym: curve - a continuously bending line without angles
  • antonym: line - something long and straight, the opposite of a curve
  • synonym: segment - the area between a chord and an arc of a circle
