dhobi

noun
South Asian laundry worker
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊbi / (AmE), / ˈdəʊbi / (BrE)
a person in South Asia whose job or traditional role is to wash and iron clothes

Examples

The dhobi washes clothes
The dhobi collected clothes from every house in the village each morning.
Her family has been dhobi for generations, known for their careful laundry work.
A local dhobi set up a small shop near the railway station.
The dhobi delivered the freshly pressed shirts to our house.
Every week, the dhobi collects laundry from the neighborhood and returns it clean.
In many Indian villages, the dhobi is an essential part of daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: launderer - a person who washes and irons clothes for a living
  • synonym: washerman - a person whose job is to wash clothes
