Indian language

noun
language of Indigenous peoples of the Americas
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the languages spoken by Indigenous peoples of the Americas, especially in North, Central, and South America.

Examples

Indian language families
Many scholars have devoted their careers to documenting the grammar and vocabulary of Indian language families.
The treaty was signed after lengthy negotiations conducted in an Indian language.
This region is home to several distinct Indian languages that are still spoken today.

Related Terms
