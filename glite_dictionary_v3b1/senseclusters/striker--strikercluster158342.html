striker

noun
person who hits something
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkə / (BrE)
a person who hits something, especially in sports, work, or other contexts where forceful striking is involved

Examples

Cricket striker
In cricket, the striker aimed for a powerful shot to the boundary.
She was known as a fierce striker on the school's hockey team.
The blacksmith was an excellent striker, shaping horseshoes with precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hitter - a person who hits, especially in sports
