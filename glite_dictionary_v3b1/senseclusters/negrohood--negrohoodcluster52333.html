negrohood

noun
state of being a Negro
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈniːɡroʊhʊd / (AmE), / ˈniːɡrəʊhʊd / (BrE)
the state or condition of being a Negro; blackness (now considered offensive and archaic)

Examples

Negrohood as identity
Writers in the 19th century sometimes discussed the concept of negrohood in their works.
The term negrohood is now recognized as both offensive and outdated.
Historical texts may refer to negrohood when describing racial identity, but it is no longer used today.
