whore oneself out

verb
degrade oneself for gain
Pronunciation: / hɔɹ wʊnˈsɛlf aʊt / (AmE), / hɔː wʊnˈself aʊt / (BrE)
to compromise one's principles or dignity for personal gain, especially for money or publicity, in a way regarded as degrading
