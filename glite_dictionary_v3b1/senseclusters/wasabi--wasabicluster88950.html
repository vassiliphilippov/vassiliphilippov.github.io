wasabi

noun
Japanese spicy green plant root
2.7
Pronunciation: / wəˈsɑːbi / (AmE), / wəˈsɑːbi / (BrE)
a Japanese plant of the mustard family with a thick, pungent green root, used as a spice in Japanese cuisine

Examples
