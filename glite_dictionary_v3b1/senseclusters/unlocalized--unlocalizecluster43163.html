unlocalized

adjective
not region-specific
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈləʊkəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not adapted or translated for a specific region, language, or culture

Examples

Unlocalized software version
The website showed an unlocalized warning that confused users from different countries.
Many video games are released with unlocalized menus, making them hard to understand for non-English speakers.
He noticed that the software's help section was still unlocalized and only available in English.

Related Terms

  • antonym: localized - adapted or translated for a specific region or language
  • synonym: untranslated - not translated into another language
