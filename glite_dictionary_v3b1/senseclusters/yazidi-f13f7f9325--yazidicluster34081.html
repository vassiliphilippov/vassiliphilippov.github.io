Yazidi

noun
religious minority member
9.6
Pronunciation: / jəˈziːdi / (AmE), / jəˈziːdi / (BrE)
a member of a religious minority group with ancient roots, mainly living in northern Iraq and nearby regions

Examples

A Yazidi woman
The Yazidi community has preserved its unique traditions for centuries.
Many Yazidi families have faced persecution and displacement in recent years.
A Yazidi festival was celebrated with music and dancing in the village.
