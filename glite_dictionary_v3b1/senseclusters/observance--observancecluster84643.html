observance

noun
the act of observing or complying with rules or customs
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝːvəns / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːvəns / (BrE)
the act of watching, noticing, or paying careful attention to something

Examples

Silent observance
He watched the ritual in silent observance, showing deep respect.
Strict observance of the rules is required during the ceremony.
The observance of traditional customs is still important in this region.
His observance of the rule saved him from making a costly mistake.
Sharp observance of her boss's instructions ensured the project was a success.
The teacher praised the students for their careful observance during the safety demonstration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disregard - lack of attention or care
  • synonym: observation - the action or process of observing something carefully
