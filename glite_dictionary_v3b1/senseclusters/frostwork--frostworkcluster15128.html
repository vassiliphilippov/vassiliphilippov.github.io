frostwork

noun
frost-like decoration
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstwɜːk / (BrE)
a delicate, often intricate pattern that looks like frost, found on surfaces such as glass, metal, or textiles (either naturally formed or used as decoration)

Examples

Window frostwork
The windows were decorated with intricate frostwork that sparkled in the morning light.
She admired the delicate frostwork pattern etched onto the silver tray.
The scarf featured a subtle frostwork design that reminded her of icy winter mornings.
On winter mornings, the windows were decorated with beautiful frostwork that looked like lace.
She admired the delicate frostwork covering the car windshield before scraping it away.
The intricate frostwork on the garden fence sparkled in the early sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: etching - a decorative design made by cutting into a surface
  • synonym: filigree - delicate ornamental work made from fine wire
  • synonym: hoarfrost - a white, crystalline deposit of frozen water vapor
