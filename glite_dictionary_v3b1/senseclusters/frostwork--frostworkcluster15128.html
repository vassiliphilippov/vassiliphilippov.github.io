frostwork

noun
frost-like decoration
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɑːst.wɜːrk/ /; / /ˈfrɔːst.wɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈfrɒst.wɜːk/ (BrE)
a delicate, often intricate pattern that looks like frost, found on surfaces such as glass, metal, or textiles (either naturally formed or used as decoration)
