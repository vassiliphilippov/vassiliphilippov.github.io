self-esteem

noun
confidence and pride in oneself
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ɪˈstim / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪˈstiːm / (BrE)
confidence and satisfaction in yourself; a feeling of pride and self-respect.

Examples

High self-esteem
Criticism from others can seriously damage a child's self-esteem.
He spoke with such low self-esteem that it was clear he doubted his abilities.
The exercise program helped improve her self-esteem and overall mood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-confidence - a feeling of trust in one's abilities, qualities, and judgment
  • antonym: self-doubt - lack of confidence in oneself and one's abilities
