sexualize

verb
make sexual or attribute sexuality to
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something sexual in character or quality; to regard or present something or someone in sexual terms
