glaive

noun
medieval bladed weapon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡleɪv / (AmE), / ɡleɪv / (BrE)
a sword or polearm with a long blade, used as a weapon in medieval times

Examples

Medieval glaive
The knight raised his glaive as the enemy approached the castle walls.
In the museum, a medieval glaive is displayed alongside other ancient weapons.
Legends tell of a hero who wielded a magical glaive in battle.
