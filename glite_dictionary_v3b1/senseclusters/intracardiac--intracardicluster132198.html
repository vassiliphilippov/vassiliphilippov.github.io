intracardiac

adjective
within the heart
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈkɑːrdiæk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈkɑːdiæk / (BrE)
occurring within or administered into the heart

Examples

Intracardiac catheter
The surgeon used an intracardiac catheter to monitor the patient's heart function.
An intracardiac injection may be necessary in certain emergency situations.
Doctors detected an intracardiac mass during the echocardiogram.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extracardiac - located or occurring outside the heart
  • synonym: intravenous - occurring within or administered into a vein
