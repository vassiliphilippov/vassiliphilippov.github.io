salat

noun
Islamic ritual prayer, performed five times daily
7.6
Pronunciation: / sɑˈlɑt / (AmE), / sɑːˈlɑːt / (BrE)
the ritual prayer of Muslims, performed five times daily facing Mecca, and constituting the second pillar of Islam

Examples

Daily salat prayer
During the midday salat, worshippers gather at the mosque to pray together.
Muslims are required to perform salat at specific times each day.
She taught her children the steps and recitations of salat.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hajj - the fifth pillar of Islam, pilgrimage to Mecca
  • synonym: prayer - the act of communicating with a deity, especially as part of a religious ritual
  • degree lower: zakat - the third pillar of Islam, giving of alms to the poor
