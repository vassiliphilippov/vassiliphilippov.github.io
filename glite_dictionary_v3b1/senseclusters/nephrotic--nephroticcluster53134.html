nephrotic

noun
person with nephrosis
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfrɑtɪk / (AmE), / nɛˈfrɒtɪk / (BrE)
a person who is affected by nephrosis or nephrotic syndrome, a kidney disorder causing protein loss in urine

Examples

A nephrotic patient
The doctor explained the treatment plan to the nephrotic and their family.
A nephrotic may experience swelling in the legs and around the eyes.
Support groups can help a nephrotic cope with the challenges of their condition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy person - a person not affected by illness
  • synonym: patient - a person receiving medical care
