windsurfing

noun
sailing on a board
2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪnd.sɜːr.fɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪnd.sɜː.fɪŋ/ (BrE)
a sport in which a person stands on a board with an attached sail and uses the wind to move across water

Examples

Windsurfing on the lake
He took up windsurfing because he loves both the water and the wind.
Many tourists try windsurfing when they visit the coastal resort each summer.
Learning the basics of windsurfing can be challenging but very rewarding.
