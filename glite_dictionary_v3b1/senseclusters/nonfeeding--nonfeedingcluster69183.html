nonfeeding

adjective
not providing nourishment
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈfiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
not providing food or nourishment, especially to offspring or others who depend on it

Examples

Nonfeeding stage
The nonfeeding stage of the insect's life cycle lasts several weeks.
After surgery, the animal entered a nonfeeding period and required special care.
Some birds have a nonfeeding phase while they are incubating their eggs.
