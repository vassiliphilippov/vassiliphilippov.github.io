ritual dance

noun
dance performed in a religious ritual
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəl dæns / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuəl dɑːns / (BrE)
a dance performed as part of a religious or ceremonial ritual

Examples

Ritual dance ceremony
Anthropologists studied the ritual dance at the ancient temple.
Each year, the community gathers for a sacred ritual dance honoring their ancestors.
The tribe performed a ritual dance to celebrate the harvest.
