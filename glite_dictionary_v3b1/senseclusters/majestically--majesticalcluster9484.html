majestically

adverb
in a majestic manner
2.7
Pronunciation: / məˈdʒɛstɪkli / (AmE), / məˈdʒɛstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great dignity or grandeur.

Examples

To walk majestically
She walked majestically into the room, turning every head with her poise.
The ancient castle stood majestically on the hill, overlooking the valley below.
The orchestra performed the symphony majestically, captivating everyone in the hall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humbly - in a humble or modest manner
