mesogleal

adjective
of the mesoglea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈɡliːəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəˈɡliːəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the mesoglea, the jelly-like layer found in some invertebrates such as jellyfish

Examples

Mesogleal jelly layer
The mesogleal layer provides structural support in many species of jellyfish.
Researchers studied the mesogleal composition to understand how these animals move.
Certain cells within the mesogleal region are responsible for producing collagen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mesoglea - the jelly-like layer between cell layers in some invertebrates
