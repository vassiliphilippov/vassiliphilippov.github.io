nongovernmental organization

noun
organization not part of any government
2.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.gʌv.ərn.ˈmen.təl ɔːr.gən.əˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /nɒn.gʌv.ən.ˈmen.təl ɔː.gə.naɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an organization that operates independently from local, state, or federal government, typically working to address social, political, or humanitarian issues

Examples

Several nongovernmental organizations
Several nongovernmental organizations submitted reports to the United Nations.
She works for a nongovernmental organization focused on promoting education in rural areas.
The earthquake relief efforts were coordinated by a nongovernmental organization specializing in disaster response.
