deformed

adjective
having an abnormal or ugly shape
3.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / dɪˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
Having an unnatural or abnormal shape, often in a way that is considered unattractive or ugly.

Examples

Deformed limbs
Some animals show deformed limbs due to genetic mutations.
The sculpture depicted a deformed figure with exaggerated features.
Years of disease had left his hands deformed and stiff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distorted - twisted out of the normal shape
  • synonym: malformed - abnormally formed
  • synonym: misshapen - badly shaped or deformed
