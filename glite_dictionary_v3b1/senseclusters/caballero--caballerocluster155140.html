caballero

noun
Spanish gentleman
Pronunciation: / kɑːbəˈjeɪroʊ / (AmE), / kæbəˈjeərəʊ / (BrE)
a Spanish or Latin American gentleman or man of high social standing, especially one who is courteous or chivalrous

Examples

A true caballero
He acted like a true caballero, always opening doors for others.
The caballero greeted everyone with a warm smile and a polite bow.
In the story, a brave caballero rides across the countryside to help those in need.
The caballero greeted everyone with a polite bow at the entrance.
In the old town, a respected caballero was known for his generosity and honor.
She admired the way the caballero treated others with such respect and dignity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knight - a man awarded a non-hereditary title of honor for service or merit
