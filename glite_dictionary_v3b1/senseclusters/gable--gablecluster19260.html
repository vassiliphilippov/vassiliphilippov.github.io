gable

noun
triangular wall section of a roof
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ.bəl / (BrE)
the triangular part of a wall that is enclosed between the sloping sides of a pitched roof.

Examples

A gable wall
A small window was set high in the gable above the front door.
Many houses in the village feature a red brick gable facing the street.
The old church has a stone gable with intricate carvings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pediment - the triangular upper part of the front of a building in classical style, typically surmounting a portico
