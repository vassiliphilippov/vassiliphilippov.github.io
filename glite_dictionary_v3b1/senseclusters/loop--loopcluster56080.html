loop

noun
an inner group with special access or influence
7.5 1.99/m
Pronunciation: / luÀêp / (AmE), / luÀêp / (BrE)
a group of people with access to privileged information or decision-making, especially within a government, organization, or business; an inner circle of advisors

Examples

In the loop at work
After the executive reshuffle, she remained in the loop and continued to advise the CEO.
He was kept in the loop about all changes affecting the project.
Those outside the loop often felt excluded from key decisions.

Synonyms
