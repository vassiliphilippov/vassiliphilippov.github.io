tenure

noun
length of time holding a position
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnjɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnjə / (BrE)
the period of time during which someone holds a job, position, or office

Examples

University tenure
During his long tenure at the university, he mentored hundreds of students.
Her tenure as CEO was marked by significant growth for the company.
The governor's tenure ended after just one term in office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appointment - the act of assigning a job or position, or the conditions of holding it
  • antonym: resignation - the act of leaving or giving up a job or position
  • synonym: term - a fixed or limited period for which something lasts
