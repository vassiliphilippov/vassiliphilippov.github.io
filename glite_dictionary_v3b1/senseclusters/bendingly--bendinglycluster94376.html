bendingly

adverb
with bending motion
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbɛndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves bending or curving the body or an object

Examples

Bendingly move across
The gymnast moved bendingly across the mat, impressing the judges with her flexibility.
Branches swayed bendingly in the strong wind during the storm.
He reached bendingly to tie his shoes before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that bends easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigidly - in a stiff or inflexible manner
