Hallowmass

noun
Feast day honoring all saints
Pronunciation: / ˈhæloʊˌmæs / (AmE), / ˈhaləʊˌmæs / (BrE)
A Christian feast day honoring all the saints, traditionally celebrated on November 1st.

Examples

The Hallowmass feast
