dustheap

noun
pile of rubbish
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌhiːp / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌhiːp / (BrE)
a pile or heap of dust, rubbish, or discarded material

Examples

A dustheap pile
The children found an old toy buried in the dustheap behind the building.
After cleaning the attic, he threw all the broken items onto the dustheap.
The alley was lined with a large dustheap that needed to be cleared away.

  • synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of waste or discarded material
