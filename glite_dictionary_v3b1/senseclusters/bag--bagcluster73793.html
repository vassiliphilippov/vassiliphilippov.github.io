bag

noun
number of game animals caught or killed
Pronunciation: / bæɡ / (AmE), / bæɡ / (BrE)
the quantity of game, birds, or fish captured or shot by one person in a specified period

Examples

A hunter's bag
Authorities set a daily bag limit to protect wildlife populations.
She was proud of her bag after a successful day of fishing.
The hunter's bag included three rabbits and a pheasant.
