bombilla

noun
mate drinking straw
Pronunciation: / boʊmˈbiːjə / (AmE), / bɒmˈbiːjə / (BrE)
a metal straw with a filter at one end, used for drinking mate

Examples

Mate bombilla straw
She carefully cleaned her bombilla after finishing her morning mate.
The traditional way to drink mate is with a bombilla and a gourd.
He forgot his bombilla at home, so he could not enjoy mate with his friends.
