sphere

verb
to make round
7.6
Pronunciation: / sfɪr / (AmE), / sfɪə / (BrE)
to form something into a round shape or enclose it within a sphere

Examples

To sphere the clay
The artist carefully sphered the clay to create a perfect model of the planet.
Tiny bubbles sphered in the liquid, reflecting the light beautifully.
The magician sphered the mist, making it float above the stage like a glowing orb.
