nut

noun
enthusiast or obsessive person
9.6
Pronunciation: / nʌt / (AmE), / nʌt / (BrE)
a person who is extremely enthusiastic about or obsessed with a particular activity, subject, or thing; a fanatic

Examples

A sports nut
As a movie nut, he has an impressive library of classic films.
My uncle is a real car nut who spends every weekend working on his collection.
She's such a fitness nut that she goes to the gym twice a day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive and single-minded zeal
  • synonym: freak - a person with a very strong interest in a particular subject
  • synonym: junkie - a person with an obsessive enthusiasm for something
