aplastic

adjective
emotionally unresponsive
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
showing little or no emotional response or initiative; unresponsive or inert in behavior

Examples

Aplastic personality
After the accident he became emotionally distant and remarkably aplastic in social situations.
Her aplastic demeanor made it difficult for others to know how she was feeling.
Despite the exciting news, he remained aplastic and showed no visible reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling little or no interest or emotion
  • antonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively
