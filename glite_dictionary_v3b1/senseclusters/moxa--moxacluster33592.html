moxa

noun
acupuncture herb
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑksə / (AmE), / ˈmɒksə / (BrE)
dried plant material (especially mugwort) that is burned on or near the skin in acupuncture or traditional East Asian medicine to apply heat for healing
