symphysis

noun
growing together of body parts, especially bones
8.4 8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪm.fə.sɪs /; / ˈsɪmfəsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪm.fɪ.sɪs /; / ˈsɪmfɪsɪs / (BrE)
the joining or fusion of two bones or body parts, usually by cartilage
