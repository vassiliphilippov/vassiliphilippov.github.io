compensator

noun
device reducing recoil
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmpənˌseɪtər /; / ˈkɑːmpənˌseɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpənˌseɪtə / (BrE)
a device that reduces or counteracts unwanted movement, effects, or forces, especially in machinery or firearms
