at death's door

adverb
very close to death
4.8
Pronunciation: / æt dɛθs dɔr / (AmE), / ət dɛθs dɔː / (BrE)
very close to death; in a critical condition or near dying

Examples

Be at death's door
After the severe infection, she was at death's door for several days.
During his illness, many feared he was at death's door.
He described feeling at death's door when the fever was at its worst.
