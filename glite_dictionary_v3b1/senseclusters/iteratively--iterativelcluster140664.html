iteratively

adverb
by repeating steps
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪt̬əˌreɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈɪtərətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves repeating a process several times to improve or refine the result

Examples

Test iteratively
The engineers tested the new design iteratively to ensure it met all safety standards.
By working iteratively, the team was able to quickly identify and fix problems in the project.
The software was developed iteratively, with each version improving on the last.

Related Terms

  • antonym: once - one single time
  • synonym: repeatedly - again and again; many times
