Gucci

noun
luxury brand item
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈɡuːtʃi / (BrE)
a luxury item, especially a bag or accessory, made by the Gucci fashion brand

Examples

A Gucci bag
She saved for months to buy an authentic Gucci handbag from the boutique.
His favorite accessory is a vintage Gucci belt he found at a thrift store.
Many celebrities are often seen carrying the latest Gucci designs at events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: designer - a product made by a famous fashion brand
  • synonym: luxury - something expensive and of high quality
