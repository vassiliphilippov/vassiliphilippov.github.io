glimmering

noun
a faint idea or hint
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪmɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪmərɪŋ / (BrE)
a slight suggestion or vague understanding of something

Examples

A glimmering of hope
A glimmering of hope returned when the rescue team arrived.
She had a glimmering of what the answer might be, but wasn't certain.
There wasn't even a glimmering of understanding on his face during the lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inkling - a slight knowledge or suspicion; a hint
  • synonym: notion - a general or vague idea
