deerstalking

noun
hunting deer by stalking
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrˌstɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪəˌstɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or sport of stalking and hunting deer, especially by following them silently in their natural habitat.

Examples

Deerstalking in the Highlands
He spent the afternoon deerstalking in the Scottish Highlands.
Many tourists visit the estate for traditional deerstalking experiences.
Proper clothing is essential when engaging in deerstalking to blend into the environment.
