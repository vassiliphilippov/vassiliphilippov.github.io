Boyne

proper noun
a 1690 battle in Ireland between William III and James II
9.8 0.23/m

the Battle of the Boyne, a decisive conflict in 1690 near the River Boyne in Ireland, where forces of William III defeated those of the deposed James II, ending the attempt to restore Catholic rule in England.
