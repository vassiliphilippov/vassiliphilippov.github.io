gatewaying

noun
providing access
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌweɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌweɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of providing entry or access to a place, system, service, or opportunity

Examples

Gatewaying new members
Her volunteer work focused on gatewaying refugees into essential community services.
The new policy is aimed at gatewaying small businesses into the international market.
Effective gatewaying can help students access higher education opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrier - something that blocks or prevents access
  • synonym: facilitation - the act of making something easier or helping it happen
