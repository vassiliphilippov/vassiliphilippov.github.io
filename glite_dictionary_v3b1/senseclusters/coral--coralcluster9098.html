coral

noun
marine animal forming reefs
4.4 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.əl/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.rəl/ (BrE)
a small marine animal that forms reefs, or its hard skeleton, often used in jewelry and ornaments

Examples

Coral reef
Colorful fish swim among the branches of coral in the shallow waters.
Divers are warned not to touch the coral to protect it from damage.
The Great Barrier Reef is made up of billions of tiny coral polyps.
She wore a beautiful necklace made of coral.
The museum displayed an ancient ring set with rare red coral.
The shop sells earrings carved from Mediterranean coral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coralline - related to or resembling coral, especially in color
  • synonym: pearl - a hard, lustrous spherical mass formed within the shell of a pearl oyster; used in jewelry
  • synonym: reef - a ridge of jagged rock, coral, or sand just above or below the surface of the sea
  • synonym: shell - the hard, protective outer layer created by an animal that lives in the sea
