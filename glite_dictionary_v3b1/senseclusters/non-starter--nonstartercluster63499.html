non-starter

noun
unfeasible idea or plan
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstɑɾɚ / (AmE), / nɒnˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
an idea, plan, or proposal that has no realistic chance of being successful

Examples

A non-starter idea
The board rejected the proposal, calling it a non-starter from the outset.
They realized the merger would be a non-starter after preliminary discussions failed.
With no clear funding sources, the new project was a non-starter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: failure - lack of success
  • antonym: success - accomplishment of an aim or purpose
