elfstone

noun
magical stone in fantasy
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlfstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛlfstəʊn / (BrE)
a magical stone associated with elves, often possessing special powers in fantasy stories

Examples

A magical elfstone
The wizard handed the hero an elfstone to help guide him through the darkness.
Legends say that an elfstone can heal wounds and reveal hidden paths.
She wore an elfstone around her neck as a symbol of protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amulet - an object believed to have magical powers and protect its owner
  • synonym: talisman - an object thought to bring good luck or provide protection
