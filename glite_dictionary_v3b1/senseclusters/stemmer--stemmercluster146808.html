stemmer

noun
algorithm for reducing words to their stem
8.3 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstem.ər/ (AmE), /ˈstem.ə/ (BrE)
an algorithm that removes inflectional and derivational endings from words to reduce them to their base or stem form

Examples

Text stemmer
A stemmer allows search engines to handle different word forms, such as 'run' and 'running'.
The Porter stemmer is widely used in English language text processing applications.
While using a stemmer, some words may be reduced to non-dictionary stems.

  • synonym: lemmatizer - an algorithm that reduces words to their dictionary or base form (lemma)
