buffalo grass

noun
mat-forming lawn grass
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəloʊ ɡræs /; / ˈbʌfəˌloʊ ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a low, hardy grass native to North America, often used for lawns and landscaping because it needs little water
