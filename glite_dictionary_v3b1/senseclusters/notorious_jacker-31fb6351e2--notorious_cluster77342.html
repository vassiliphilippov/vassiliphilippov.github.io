notorious jacker

noun
well-known thief or hijacker
Pronunciation: / noʊˈtɔriəs ˈdʒækər / (AmE), / nəʊˈtɔːriəs ˈdʒækə / (BrE)
A person who is well known for stealing or hijacking, especially in a bold or repeated manner.

Examples

The notorious jacker caught
After many successful heists, the notorious jacker finally ended up behind bars.
Everyone warned me that the area was home to a notorious jacker wanted by the police.
The notorious jacker struck again last night, stealing three cars from the neighborhood.
