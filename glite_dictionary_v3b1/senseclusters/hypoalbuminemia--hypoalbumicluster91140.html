hypoalbuminemia

noun
low blood albumin
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˌælbjəˈmiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊˌalbjuːˈmiːmɪə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the level of albumin in the blood is abnormally low

Examples

Low hypoalbuminemia level
The doctor diagnosed the patient with hypoalbuminemia after reviewing the blood tests.
Severe hypoalbuminemia can lead to swelling in the legs and feet.
Treatment for hypoalbuminemia often involves addressing the underlying cause of protein loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hyperalbuminemia - a condition with abnormally high levels of albumin in the blood
  • synonym: hypoproteinemia - a condition with abnormally low levels of protein in the blood
