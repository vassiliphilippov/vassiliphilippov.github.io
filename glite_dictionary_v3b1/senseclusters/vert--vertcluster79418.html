vert

adjective
vertical ramp sports
Pronunciation: / vɜrt / (AmE), / vɜːt / (BrE)
relating to skateboarding, BMX, or snowboarding performed on a vertical ramp or surface, especially involving aerial tricks.

Examples

Vert tricks
He is known for his incredible vert tricks on the half-pipe.
The competition featured both street and vert events for skateboarders.
She prefers vert skating because she loves flying high above the ramp.

Related Terms

  • antonym: street - relating to skateboarding or BMX performed on flat ground or urban obstacles
