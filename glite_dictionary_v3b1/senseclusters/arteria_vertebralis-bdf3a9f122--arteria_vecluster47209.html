arteria vertebralis

noun
vertebral artery of the neck

Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪə.rɪ.ə vɜːr.tə.ˈbreɪ.lɪs/ /; / /ɑːr.tɪ.ˈriː.ə vɜːr.tə.ˈbreɪ.lɪs/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.rɪ.ə vɜː.tə.ˈbreɪ.lɪs/ /; / /ɑː.tɪ.ˈriː.ə vɜː.tɪ.ˈbreɪ.lɪs/ (BrE)
The vertebral artery; the first branch of the subclavian artery, supplying blood to the posterior part of the brain and divided into four parts.

Examples
