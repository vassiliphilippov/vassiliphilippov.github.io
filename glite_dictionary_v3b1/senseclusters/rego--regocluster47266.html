rego

noun
vehicle registration (Australia)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒəʊ /; / ˈrɛɡəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term (especially in Australia) for a vehicle's registration, the registration process, or the registration fee

Examples

Car rego fee
I have to pay my car's rego before the end of the month.
She forgot to renew the rego and got a fine from the police.
How much is the annual rego for a small vehicle in your state?
You need to renew your rego before driving your car on public roads.
The university sent a reminder about the deadline for course rego next week.
He forgot to bring proof of rego when he went to pick up his new license plate.
