Beckettian

adjective
like Samuel Beckett's style
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɛˈkɛtiən / (AmE), / bɪˈkɛtiən / (BrE)
relating to or resembling the style of Samuel Beckett, especially marked by bleakness, minimalism, dark humor, or existential themes

Examples

Beckettian style
The director's new film has a distinctly Beckettian sense of isolation and dark humor.
Her writing style is often described as Beckettian because of its minimalism and existential themes.
The empty stage and sparse dialogue gave the performance a Beckettian atmosphere.
The director's new film has a distinctly Beckettian atmosphere, with long silences and minimal dialogue.
Critics described the novel's bleak humor and sparse setting as highly Beckettian.
Her play, full of existential questions and absurd situations, was praised for its Beckettian qualities.
