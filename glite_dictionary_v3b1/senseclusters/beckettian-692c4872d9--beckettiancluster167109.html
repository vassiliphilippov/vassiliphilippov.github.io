Beckettian

adjective
like Samuel Beckett's style
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbek.ə.tɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈbek.ɪ.tɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈbek.ə.tɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈbek.ɪ.tɪ.ən/ (BrE)
