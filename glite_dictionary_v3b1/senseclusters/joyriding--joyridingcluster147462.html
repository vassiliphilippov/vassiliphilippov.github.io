joyriding

noun
stealing vehicle for fun
5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɔɪ.raɪ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɔɪ.raɪ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)

After a night of joyriding, the stolen vehicle was found abandoned on the outskirts of town.
