dart player

noun
person who plays darts
Pronunciation: / dɑrt ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / dɑːt ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of darts, either professionally or as a hobby

Examples

A dart player competes
Becoming a great dart player requires both practice and sharp concentration.
Many professional dart players travel internationally to participate in major championships.
She is a skilled dart player who competes in local tournaments every weekend.

Related Terms
