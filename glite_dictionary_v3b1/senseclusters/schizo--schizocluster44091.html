schizo

adjective
offensive slang insult
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪtsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈskɪtsəʊ / (BrE)
offensive slang for someone who is erratic, mentally unstable, or associated with schizophrenia; considered highly inappropriate

Examples

A schizo remark
Using the word schizo to describe someone is offensive and should be avoided.
The teacher explained why calling someone schizo is disrespectful and hurtful.
He was upset when his classmates referred to him as a schizo because of his behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - informal term for mentally unstable
  • antonym: sane - mentally healthy and stable
