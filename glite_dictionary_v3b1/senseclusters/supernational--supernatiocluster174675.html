supernational

noun
authority above nations
9.3
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈnæʃ.əl/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈnæʃ.əl/ (BrE)
an organization or authority that operates above the level of individual nation states and has powers or influence over them

Examples

A supernational body
The European Union is often considered a supernational that can set policies for its member states.
A new supernational was established to coordinate disaster response across several countries.
Some critics argue that a supernational may undermine national sovereignty.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: international - involving two or more countries
  • antonym: national - relating to a single nation
  • synonym: supranational - an organization with authority above individual nations
