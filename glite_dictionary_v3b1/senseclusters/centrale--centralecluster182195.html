centrale

proper noun
Milan train station
Pronunciation: / tʃɛnˈtrɑːleɪ / (AmE), / tʃɛnˈtrɑːleɪ / (BrE)
the main railway station in Milan, Italy, commonly referred to as 'Centrale' in informal contexts.

Examples

Milan centrale station

Many tourists use centrale as their starting point in Milan.
