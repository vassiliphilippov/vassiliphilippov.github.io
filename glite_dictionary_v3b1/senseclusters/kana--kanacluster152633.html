kana

noun
Japanese syllabic script
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
a Japanese syllabic writing system, including the hiragana and katakana scripts

Examples

Japanese kana system
Before learning kanji, students often start with kana to understand basic Japanese writing.
The textbook includes charts for both hiragana and katakana kana characters.
He practiced writing kana every day to improve his reading skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiragana - a component of the Japanese kana system used for native words
  • antonym: kanji - Japanese logographic characters of Chinese origin
  • synonym: katakana - a component of the Japanese kana system used for foreign words
