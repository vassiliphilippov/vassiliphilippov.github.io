discernability

noun
quality of being easy to perceive
6.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˌsɜrnəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / dɪˌsɜːnəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being easy to perceive or distinguish.

Examples

High discernability between options
High discernability between options is essential for making informed choices.
In low light conditions, the discernability of the road signs decreases significantly.
The discernability of the logo ensures it stands out even from a distance.
