Turnerian

adjective
of or like Turner
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɜrˈnɪriən / (AmE), / tɜːˈnɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of someone named Turner, especially the artist J. M. W. Turner

Examples

Turnerian painting style
The landscape was painted in a distinctly Turnerian style, with swirling clouds and dramatic light.
Her use of color and atmosphere in the piece is very Turnerian.
Art critics praised the Turnerian qualities of the new exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Turneresque - in the style of the artist J. M. W. Turner
