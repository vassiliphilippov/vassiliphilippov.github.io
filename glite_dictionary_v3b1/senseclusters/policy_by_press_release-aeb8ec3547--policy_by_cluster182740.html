policy by press release

noun
announcing policy via media statements
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑləsi baɪ ˈprɛs rɪˌlis / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪsi baɪ ˈprɛs rɪˌliːs / (BrE)
the practice of announcing official guidelines or decisions to the public through the media, often without prior internal discussion or formal process

Examples

Policy by press release practice
Critics argue that policy by press release undermines transparency and proper debate.
Many officials were frustrated when they first learned of the new initiative through policy by press release.
The government was accused of governing by policy by press release rather than thoughtful planning.
