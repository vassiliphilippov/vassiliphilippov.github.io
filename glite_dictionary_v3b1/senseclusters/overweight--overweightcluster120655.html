overweight

verb
to give excessive emphasis
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.weɪt/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.weɪt/ (BrE)
to give too much weight, importance, or emphasis to something when considering or evaluating it

Examples

To overweight recent events
The report overweights recent trends while ignoring long-term data.
Analysts warned against overweighting short-term profits in their investment decisions.
She was criticized for overweighting anecdotal evidence when drawing her conclusions.

  • antonym: underweight - to give too little importance to something
