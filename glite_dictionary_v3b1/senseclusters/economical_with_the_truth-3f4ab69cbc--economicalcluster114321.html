economical with the truth

adjective
deliberately not fully honest or straightforward
7.2 6.8
Pronunciation: /e.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪ.kəl wɪð ðə truːθ/ /; / /iː.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪ.kəl wɪð ðə truːθ/ /; / /e.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪ.kəl wɪθ ðə truːθ/ (AmE), /iː.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪ.kəl wɪð ðə truːθ/ /; / /e.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪ.kəl wɪð ðə truːθ/ /; / /iː.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪ.kəl wɪθ ðə truːθ/ (BrE)

Examples

Some writers are economical with the truth to create suspense or protect privacy.
