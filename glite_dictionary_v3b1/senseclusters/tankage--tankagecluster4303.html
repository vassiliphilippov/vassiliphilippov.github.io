tankage

noun
storage of substances in tanks
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋ.kɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtaŋ.kɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process or act of storing a substance in tanks, especially large containers for liquids such as oil or chemicals
