mock turtleneck

noun
sweater or shirt with a short, upright collar
Pronunciation: / mɑk ˈtɝt̬l̩ˌnɛk / (AmE), / mɒk ˈtɜːtl̩ˌnɛk / (BrE)
A sweater or shirt with a short, close-fitting collar resembling a turtleneck but not folding over.

Examples

A mock turtleneck sweater
His favorite winter top is a black mock turtleneck because it's comfortable and neat.
She wore a stylish mock turtleneck under her blazer for the meeting.
The store had several colors of mock turtlenecks on display near the entrance.
