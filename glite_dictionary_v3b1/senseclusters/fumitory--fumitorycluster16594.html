fumitory

noun
European herb with purplish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuː.məˌtɔɹ.i / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.mɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
A delicate European herb with greyish leaves and spikes of purplish flowers, formerly used medicinally.
