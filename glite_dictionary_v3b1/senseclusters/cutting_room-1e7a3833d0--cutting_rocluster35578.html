cutting room

noun
room for editing films or tapes
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtɪŋ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
a room where films or tapes are edited, especially by removing unwanted sections

Examples

The cutting room floor
Many important scenes ended up on the cutting room floor and never made it to the audience.
The director spent hours in the cutting room perfecting the final version of the film.
The sound editor joined her colleagues in the cutting room to work on the documentary.
