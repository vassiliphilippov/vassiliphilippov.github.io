short-arse

noun
derogatory term for a short person
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt ɑrs / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ɑːs / (BrE)
A derogatory slang term for a person of short stature.

Examples

A short-arse person
Despite being a short-arse, she climbed the rock wall faster than anyone else.
He was always the butt of jokes for being a short-arse at the football club.
They called him a short-arse in school, but he never let it bother him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shorty - informal term for a short person
  • antonym: tall - having a greater than average height
