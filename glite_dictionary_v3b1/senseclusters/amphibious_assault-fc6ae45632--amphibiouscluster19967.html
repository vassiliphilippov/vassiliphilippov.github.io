amphibious assault

noun
military attack from sea to land
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiəs əˈsɔlt / (AmE), / æmˈfɪbɪəs əˈsɔːlt / (BrE)
a military attack on a land target that is carried out by troops who are brought ashore by naval ships

Examples

An amphibious assault attack
During World War II, the D-Day invasion was the largest amphibious assault in history.
The marines launched an amphibious assault on the fortified beach at dawn.
The success of the operation depended on a swift and decisive amphibious assault.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assault - a violent attack
  • synonym: naval landing - a military disembarkation from ships onto a shore
