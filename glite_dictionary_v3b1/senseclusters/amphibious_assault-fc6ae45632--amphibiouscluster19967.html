amphibious assault

noun
military attack from sea to land
6.2 8.4
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiəs əˈsɔlt / (AmE), / æmˈfɪbɪəs əˈsɔːlt / (BrE)
a military attack on a land target that is carried out by troops who are brought ashore by naval ships
