éclair

noun
cream-filled pastry
Pronunciation: / eɪˈklɛr / (AmE), / eɪˈklɛə / (BrE)
a long, thin pastry filled with cream and topped with icing, often chocolate

Examples

Chocolate éclair
She ordered a chocolate éclair at the bakery for dessert.
The bakery displayed fresh éclairs filled with vanilla cream and topped with chocolate icing.
He enjoyed a delicious éclair with his afternoon coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cream puff - a round pastry filled with cream
  • synonym: doughnut - a sweet fried or baked dough pastry, often with a filling or topping
