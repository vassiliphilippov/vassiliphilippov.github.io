hob

verb
cut or shape with a hob (engineering)
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /hɑːb/ (AmE), /hɒb/ (BrE)
(of a tool or machine) to cut or shape a workpiece using a hob, typically in manufacturing gears and similar parts.

Examples

To hob gear teeth
After the blank is cast, workers hob the teeth for accuracy.
The machinist hobbed the gears to precise specifications.
The shop is equipped to hob helical gears as well as spur gears.
