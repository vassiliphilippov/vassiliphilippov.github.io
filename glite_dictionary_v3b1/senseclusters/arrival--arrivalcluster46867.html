arrival

noun
the act of coming to a place
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪvəl / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl / (BrE)
the act of coming to a place, especially at the end of a journey

Examples

Arrival at the airport
Please notify us upon your arrival at the hotel.
The arrival of spring is always a welcome change after winter.
We were excited about her arrival at the airport last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coming - the act of arriving at a place
  • antonym: departure - the act of leaving a place
