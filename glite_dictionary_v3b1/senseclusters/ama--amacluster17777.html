ama

noun
Ayurvedic toxin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
in Ayurveda, a toxic substance believed to form from improper digestion and cause illness in the body

Examples

Ayurveda ama buildup
According to Ayurveda, the buildup of ama in the body can lead to various health problems.
The doctor recommended a special diet to help reduce ama and improve digestion.
Practitioners believe that removing ama is essential for restoring balance and wellness.
