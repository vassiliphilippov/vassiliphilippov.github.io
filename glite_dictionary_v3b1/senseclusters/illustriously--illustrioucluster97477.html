illustriously

adverb
in a distinguished or eminent manner
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlʌstriəsli / (AmE), / ɪˈlʌstriəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is highly distinguished or famous; with great eminence or brilliance.

Examples

To serve illustriously
He served illustriously in the military for over three decades.
The author was illustriously remembered in literary circles long after his death.
The scientist was illustriously awarded for her groundbreaking discoveries.
