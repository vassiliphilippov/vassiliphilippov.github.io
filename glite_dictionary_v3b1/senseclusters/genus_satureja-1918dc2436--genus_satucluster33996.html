genus Satureja

noun
a genus of herbs (savory) in the mint family
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːnəs sæˈtʊəriə / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəs sæˈtjʊərɪə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic herbs in the mint family, commonly known as savory, including species used as culinary herbs.
