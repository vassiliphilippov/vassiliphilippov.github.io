trackback

verb
to notify linked site
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈtrækˌbæk / (BrE)
to send an automatic notification to another website to inform it that you have linked to its content

Examples

To trackback a post
If you mention another blog post, you should trackback the original author to give them credit.
Many bloggers trackback sources to let readers find related discussions easily.
She always remembers to trackback when referencing articles in her own blog posts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ping - to send a signal or notification to another computer or website
