weapon system

noun
integrated set of weapons and equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛpən ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛpən ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a combination of weapons and related equipment used together as an integrated unit, especially in military operations

Examples

An effective weapon system
An effective weapon system integrates firepower, targeting, and defense technologies in a single platform.
Engineers spent years testing each component of the weapon system to ensure it met military standards.
The new submarine is equipped with an advanced weapon system capable of targeting multiple threats simultaneously.
