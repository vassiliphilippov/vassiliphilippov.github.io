to return to a former, usually bad, way of behaving, especially after making progress; to relapse into old habits or moral failings

Examples

To backslide into habits
After months of healthy eating, she began to backslide into her old junk food habits.
Despite promising to change, he would sometimes backslide and repeat his mistakes.
It's natural to occasionally backslide, but persistence is key to long-term progress.
