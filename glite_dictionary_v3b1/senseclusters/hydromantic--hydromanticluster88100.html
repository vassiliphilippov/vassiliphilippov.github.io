hydromantic

adjective
divination by water
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
relating to divination or fortune-telling by interpreting water or its movements

Examples

Hydromantic rituals
The ancient ceremony included several hydromantic rituals to predict the coming year's harvest.
A hydromantic reading was performed by observing the ripples on the lake's surface.
Some cultures have hydromantic traditions that involve interpreting the flow of rivers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prophetic - relating to predicting the future
