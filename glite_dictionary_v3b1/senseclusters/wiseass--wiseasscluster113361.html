wiseass

adjective
sarcastic and smug
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪzˌæs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪzˌɑːs / (BrE)
showing an irritatingly smug, clever, or sarcastic attitude that annoys others

Examples

Wiseass remarks
His wiseass comments during the meeting annoyed everyone in the room.
She gave a wiseass answer when asked about her homework.
Don't be so wiseass when your parents are talking to you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness and respect
  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
  • synonym: smart-aleck - someone who is irritatingly clever or sarcastic
