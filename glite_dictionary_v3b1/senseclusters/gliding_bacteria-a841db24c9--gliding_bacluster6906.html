gliding bacteria

noun
bacteria that move along surfaces by gliding
8.5 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɡlaɪ.dɪŋ bæk.ˈtɪr.i.ə/ (AmE), /ˈɡlaɪ.dɪŋ bæk.ˈtɪə.ri.ə/ (BrE)
bacteria that move by gliding along surfaces, often forming colonies in slime; commonly found in moist soils, decaying plant matter, or animal waste.
