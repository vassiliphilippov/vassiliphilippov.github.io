subagency

noun
secondary agency
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a smaller or subordinate agency that operates under the authority of a main agency

Examples

Government subagency
The government created a subagency to handle environmental regulations in the region.
Each subagency reports its findings to the main office every month.
Funding for the new project was managed by a specialized subagency within the department.
The government created a new subagency to oversee environmental regulations in rural areas.
Each subagency is responsible for a specific aspect of the parent agency's mission.
Budget cuts forced the main agency to merge two of its subagencies into one.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: agency - an organization that provides a particular service
  • synonym: branch - a local office or division of a larger organization
  • synonym: department - a division of a larger organization with a specific function
  • antonym: main agency - the primary or central agency in charge
  • synonym: subsidiary - a company controlled by another company
