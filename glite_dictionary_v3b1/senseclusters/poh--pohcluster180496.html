poh

interjection
expression of contempt
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊ / (AmE), / pəʊ / (BrE)
an old-fashioned exclamation used to express contempt, disbelief, or dismissal

Examples

Poh expression
"Poh! I do not believe a word of it," she replied with a wave of her hand.
He dismissed the rumor with a loud poh and walked away.
When told of the supposed miracle, the old man simply said, "Poh, that's impossible."
