Pronunciation: / məˈθɑksi / (AmE), / mɪˈθɒksi / (BrE)

Examples

Methoxy group

Adding a methoxy group can change the chemical properties of a compound.
The presence of a methoxy substituent often increases the solubility of organic molecules.
