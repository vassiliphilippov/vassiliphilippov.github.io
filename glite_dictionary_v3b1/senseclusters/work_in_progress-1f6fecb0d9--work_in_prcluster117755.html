work in progress

noun
uncompleted project or task
2.0
Pronunciation: / wɝːk ɪn ˈprɑːɡrəs / (AmE), / wɜːk ɪn ˈprəʊɡrəs / (BrE)
a project or piece of work that is not yet completed

Examples

A work in progress
Her novel remains a work in progress, but she hopes to finish it soon.
The exhibition showcases several works in progress by local artists.
The new highway is still a work in progress and won't be open until next year.
