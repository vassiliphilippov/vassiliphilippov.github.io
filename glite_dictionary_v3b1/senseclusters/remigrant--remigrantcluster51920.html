remigrant

noun
returning emigrant
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌmaɪɡrənt / (AmE), / ˈriːˌmaɪɡrənt / (BrE)
a person who returns to their country of origin after living abroad

Examples

A remigrant returns
After ten years in Canada, the remigrant found it challenging to readjust to life in her homeland.
The government created special programs to help each remigrant reintegrate into society.
As a remigrant, he brought new skills and perspectives back to his community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emigrant - a person who leaves their own country to settle in another
  • synonym: returnee - a person who returns to a place, especially their home country
