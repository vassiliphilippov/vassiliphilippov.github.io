Riparia riparia

noun
species of swallow; sand martin or bank swallow
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛəriə rɪˈpɛəriə / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛːrɪə rɪˈpɛːrɪə / (BrE)
A small swallow of the Northern Hemisphere, also known as the sand martin or bank swallow, that nests in tunnels dug in clay or sand banks.
