durn cat

noun
colloquial expression for an annoying cat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝn kæt / (AmE), / ˈdɜːn kæt / (BrE)
Used, often humorously or with mild annoyance, to refer to a cat that is causing trouble or frustration.

Examples

The durn cat sleeps
Grandpa grumbled about the durn cat sleeping on his favorite chair.
I can't believe that durn cat knocked over my coffee again.
Whenever the durn cat sneaks outside, it takes me hours to find her.
