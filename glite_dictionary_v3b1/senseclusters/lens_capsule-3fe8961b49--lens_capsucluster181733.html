lens capsule

noun
membrane surrounding the eye's lens
8.1
Pronunciation: / lɛnz ˈkæp.səl / (AmE), / lɛnz ˈkæp.sjuːl / (BrE)
the thin, transparent membrane that surrounds the lens of the eye

Examples

The lens capsule membrane
A tear in the lens capsule can lead to complications during eye surgery.
During cataract surgery, the surgeon carefully opens the lens capsule to remove the cloudy lens.
The integrity of the lens capsule is crucial for implanting an artificial intraocular lens.
