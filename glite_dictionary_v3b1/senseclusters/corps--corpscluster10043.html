corps

noun
large military unit
Pronunciation: / kɔːr / (AmE), / kɔː / (BrE)
a large military unit, usually consisting of two or more divisions and their support

Examples

Army corps
A new corps was formed to provide rapid response in emergencies.
Several divisions make up a corps in most armies.
The general commanded the entire corps during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: division - a military unit typically smaller than a corps
