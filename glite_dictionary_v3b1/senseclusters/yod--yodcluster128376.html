yod

noun
astrological aspect pattern
9.9
Pronunciation: / joʊd / (AmE), / jɒd / (BrE)
in astrology, a triangular aspect pattern formed when two planets are each about 150 degrees from a third, often associated with tension or fate

Examples

Astrological yod pattern
The astrologer explained that a yod in your birth chart can indicate a unique life purpose.
Many believe that having a yod brings both challenges and opportunities for personal growth.
Her interest in astrology grew after she discovered a yod connecting three major planets in her chart.
