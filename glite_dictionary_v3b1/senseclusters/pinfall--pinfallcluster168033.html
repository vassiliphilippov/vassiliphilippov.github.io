pinfall

noun
wrestling victory
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌfɔːl / (BrE)
a victory in wrestling achieved by holding an opponent's shoulders on the mat for a set period of time

Examples

A wrestling pinfall
The wrestler celebrated his pinfall after a dramatic comeback in the final seconds.
She won the championship match with a decisive pinfall over her opponent.
A quick pinfall can end a wrestling match in just a few moments.
