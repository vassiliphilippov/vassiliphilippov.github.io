half-price

adverb
for half the usual price
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈpraɪs / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈpraɪs / (BrE)
At or for half the usual or regular price.

Examples

Half-price sale
If you order before noon, you can get your second coffee half-price.
Many items in the store are on sale at half-price until the end of the week.
She was thrilled to find designer shoes for half-price during the clearance event.
