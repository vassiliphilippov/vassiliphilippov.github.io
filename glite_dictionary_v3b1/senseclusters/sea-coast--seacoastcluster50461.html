sea-coast

noun
shore of a sea or ocean
1.1 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀåko äst / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêÀåk…ô äst / (BrE)
the land or shore along the edge of a sea or ocean

Examples

The sea-coast line

Synonyms

    1.0 0.50/m

  • seashore
    1.0 1.94/m

  • coast
    1.0 35/m

  • shore
    1.0 33/m
    shore is the land beside any large body of water like a lake or sea, while sea-coast refers specifically to a sea's edge.
