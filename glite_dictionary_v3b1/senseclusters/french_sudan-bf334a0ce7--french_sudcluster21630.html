French Sudan

proper noun
former name for Mali (colonial era)
8.8
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ suːˈdæn / (AmE), / frɛntʃ suːˈdæn / (BrE)
the former name of present-day Mali during its time as a French colonial territory in West Africa, before independence in 1960

Examples

French Sudan colony
During the colonial period, French Sudan was administered as part of French West Africa.
Historical documents refer to Bamako as the capital of French Sudan before independence.
The territory known as French Sudan gained independence from France in 1960 and was renamed Mali.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mali - the modern country that succeeded French Sudan after independence
