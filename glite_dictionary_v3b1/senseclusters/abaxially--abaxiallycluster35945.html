abaxially

adverb
on the side away from the axis (e.g., of a leaf)
9.9 9.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbæk.si.ə.liː/ /; / /ə.ˈbæk.sɪ.ə.liː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbæk.sɪ.ə.liː/ /; / /ə.ˈbæk.si.ə.liː/ (BrE)
In an abaxial manner; on or relating to the side away from the axis, especially the underside of a leaf or organ.

Examples

Leaves abaxially hairy
The petals are often pubescent abaxially but smooth on the upper side.
The plant's bracts are purple abaxially and green adaxially.
The stomata are found abaxially on the lower surface of the leaf.

Related Terms
