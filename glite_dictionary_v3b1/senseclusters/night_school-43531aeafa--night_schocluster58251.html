night school

noun
school with evening classes
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt skuːl / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt skuːl / (BrE)
a school or program offering classes in the evening, typically for adults or people who cannot attend during the day

Examples

Attend night school
After his day job, he goes to night school to study accounting.
Many working adults attend night school to improve their qualifications.
She enrolled in night school to finish her high school diploma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evening class - a class held in the evening, often for adult learners
