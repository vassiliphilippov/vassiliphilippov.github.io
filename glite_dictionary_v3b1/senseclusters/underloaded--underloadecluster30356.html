underloaded

adjective
less than full load
5.7
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈloʊ.dɪd/ /; / /ʌn.dər.ˈloʊ.dəd/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈləʊ.dɪd/ /; / /ʌn.də.ˈləʊ.dəd/ (BrE)
having less than the usual or required amount of load or weight

Examples

An underloaded truck

If the machine is underloaded, it may not operate efficiently.
The ferry left the dock underloaded because fewer passengers boarded than expected.
