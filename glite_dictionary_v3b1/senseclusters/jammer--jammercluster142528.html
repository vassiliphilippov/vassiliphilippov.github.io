jammer

noun
device that blocks communication signals
5.8 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒæ.mər/ (AmE), /ˈdʒæ.mə/ (BrE)
a device that transmits signals to interfere with or block communications, especially radio, radar, or wireless signals

Examples

Cell phone jammers
Cell phone jammers are illegal in many countries due to their interference with emergency services.
Security teams installed a jammer to prevent unauthorized drone flights over the area.
The military used a jammer to disrupt enemy radio communications during the mission.
