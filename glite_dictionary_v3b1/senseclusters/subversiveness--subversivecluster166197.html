subversiveness

noun
quality of being subversive
7.7
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɜrsɪvnəs / (AmE), / səbˈvɜːsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of seeking to undermine or overthrow an established system, authority, or institution

Examples

Subversiveness against authority
Authorities monitor the internet for signs of subversiveness among dissident groups.
Her art was both admired and censured for its subversiveness.
Some critics worried about the subversiveness of his political writings.
