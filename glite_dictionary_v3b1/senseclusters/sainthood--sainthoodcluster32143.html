sainthood

noun
saints collectively; community of saints
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪnthʊd / (AmE), / ˈseɪnthʊd / (BrE)
All saints collectively, regarded as a group or community.

Examples

The sainthood prays

Medieval artwork often depicted the sainthood as a gathering in paradise.
Throughout history, the sainthood has played a crucial role in the development of many religious traditions.
