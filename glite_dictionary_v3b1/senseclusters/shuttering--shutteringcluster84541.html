shuttering

noun
closing of business
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈʃʌt.ər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈʃʌt.ər.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of permanently or temporarily closing a business, factory, or public service.

Examples

Shuttering a business
The shuttering of the local library disappointed many residents who relied on its services.
After years of declining sales, the shuttering of the factory was inevitable.
Many employees lost their jobs due to the sudden shuttering of the restaurant chain.
