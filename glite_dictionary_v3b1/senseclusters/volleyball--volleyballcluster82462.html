volleyball

noun
team sport with ball and net
2.8 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈvɑː.lɪ.bɔːl/ /; / /ˈvɑː.lɪ.bɑːl/ (AmE), /ˈvɒl.ɪ.bɔːl/ (BrE)
