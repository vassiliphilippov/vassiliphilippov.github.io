benignantly

adverb
in a kind and gentle manner
7.1
Pronunciation: / bəˈnaɪnəntli / (AmE), / bɪˈnɪɡnəntli / (BrE)
in a kind, gentle, and benevolent manner; showing goodwill or kindness.

Examples

To benignantly forgive
He benignantly forgave the mistake, understanding it was accidental.
She smiled benignantly at the children as they played in the garden.
The teacher listened benignantly to the student's concerns, offering helpful advice.
