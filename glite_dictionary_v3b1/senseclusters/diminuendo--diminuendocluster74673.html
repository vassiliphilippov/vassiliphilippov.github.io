diminuendo

noun
gradual decrease in loudness (music)
Pronunciation: / dəˌmɪn.juˈen.doʊ / (AmE), / dɪˌmɪn.juˈen.dəʊ / (BrE)
In music, a gradual decrease in loudness or intensity.

Examples

Diminuendo passage
She marked a diminuendo in the score to indicate the volume should gradually decrease.
The choir's diminuendo added a delicate touch to the final bars.
The orchestra played a beautiful diminuendo at the end of the piece.
