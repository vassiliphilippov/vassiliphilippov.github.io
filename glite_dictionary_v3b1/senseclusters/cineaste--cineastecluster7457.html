cineaste

noun
film enthusiast or maker
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪniˌæst / (AmE), / ˈsiːniːˌæst / (BrE)
a person who is passionate about or knowledgeable in cinema or filmmaking

As a true cineaste, she can discuss directors and genres for hours.
Several cineastes gathered to debate the best films of the decade.
