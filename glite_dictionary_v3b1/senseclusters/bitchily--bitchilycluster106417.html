bitchily

adverb
spiteful manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃɪli / (BrE)
in a spiteful, mean, or malicious manner, often intended to hurt or offend someone

Examples

To bitchily criticize
She bitchily criticized her coworker's presentation in front of everyone.
He responded bitchily when asked about his recent mistake.
The comments on the post were written bitchily and upset many readers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kindly - in a friendly or generous manner
  • synonym: maliciously - in a way that shows a wish to harm someone
  • synonym: spitefully - in a way that shows a desire to hurt or upset someone
