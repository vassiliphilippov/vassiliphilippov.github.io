tapper

noun
worker who cuts screw threads with a tap
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpər / (AmE), / ˈtæpə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to use a tap to cut screw threads inside holes in metal or other materials

Examples

Metal tapper
As a tapper, he worked with various tools to shape metal components in the workshop.
Every tapper must pay close attention to detail to achieve a flawless finish.
The experienced tapper ensured each hole had precise threads for the assembly.
