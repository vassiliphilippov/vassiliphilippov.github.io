caravan

verb
travel in a convoy or group
Pronunciation: / ˈkær.ə.væn / (AmE), / ˈkær.ə.væn / (BrE)
to travel together in a group, especially with vehicles or animals, as a caravan

Examples

To caravan together
A group of tourists caravaned through the desert to reach the ancient ruins.
They decided to caravan across the country with friends for the summer.
We often caravan to music festivals, traveling together for safety and fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convoy - to travel together, especially for protection
