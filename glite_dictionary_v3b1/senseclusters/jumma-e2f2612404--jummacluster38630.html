Jumma

proper noun
Friday in Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmə / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmə / (BrE)
Friday, especially as the day of communal prayer in Muslim communities.

Examples

Jumma prayers
Many Muslims gather for prayers on Jumma every week.
Shops often close early on Jumma in some countries to allow for prayer.
We had a special family meal after Jumma prayers at the mosque.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Friday - the fifth day of the week in many calendars
