tudung

noun
Malaysian Muslim women's headscarf
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːdʊŋ / (AmE), / ˈtuːdʊŋ / (BrE)
A scarf worn by Muslim women in Malaysia that covers the hair but leaves the face uncovered.

Examples

A tudung scarf
At the event, she chose a light blue tudung to complement her dress.
Many Malaysian women wear the tudung as a symbol of modesty and faith.
The school has a policy allowing students to wear the tudung during classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hijab - a headscarf worn by many Muslim women
  • synonym: khimar - a type of Islamic head covering
  • degree higher: niqab - a veil covering the face, worn by some Muslim women
