spoiled

adjective
no longer safe to eat (food)

(of food) no longer safe or fit to eat because it has decayed or gone bad.

Examples

Spoiled food
Any spoiled vegetables should be removed from the refrigerator immediately.
He threw out the spoiled milk because it smelled sour.
The picnic was ruined when we discovered most of the food had become spoiled.
A spoilt apple can ruin the whole basket if not removed quickly.
After the power cut, all the meat in the freezer was spoilt.
We had to throw away the spoilt milk because it smelled sour.

  • tainted

  • soured
