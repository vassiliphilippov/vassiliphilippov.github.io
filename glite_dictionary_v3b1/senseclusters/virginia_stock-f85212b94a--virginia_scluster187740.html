Virginia stock

noun
fragrant flowering herb used as an ornamental plant
Pronunciation: / vərˈdʒɪniə stɑk / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪniə stɒk / (BrE)
an erect, branching herb (Malcolmia maritima) with fragrant white, pink, red, or lilac flowers, cultivated as a garden ornamental

Examples

Virginia stock flowers
Virginia stock is easy to grow from seed and attracts bees to the garden.
He grew Virginia stock along the path for their sweet fragrance and colorful display.
The Virginia stock in her garden bloomed profusely throughout the spring.
