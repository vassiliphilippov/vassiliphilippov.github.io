uncharitableness

noun
lack of kindness
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃærɪtəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃærɪtəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unkind, harsh, or lacking generosity toward others

Examples

Her uncharitableness shown
Her uncharitableness made it difficult for her to maintain friendships.
The uncharitableness of his remarks surprised everyone at the meeting.
People often regret their uncharitableness after realizing the impact of their words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charitableness - the quality of being kind and generous to others
  • synonym: unkindness - the quality of being not kind or considerate
