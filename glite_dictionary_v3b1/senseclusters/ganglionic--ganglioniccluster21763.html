ganglionic

adjective
of a nerve ganglion
Pronunciation: / ɡæŋˈɡliːɒnɪk / (AmE), / ɡæŋˈɡliːɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a ganglion, especially in nerves or nerve cells

Examples

Ganglionic nerve cells
The doctor noted ganglionic changes in the patient's nervous tissue.
Researchers studied the ganglionic structure to understand nerve cell organization.
Damage to ganglionic regions can affect muscle control and sensation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neuronal - relating to nerve cells
