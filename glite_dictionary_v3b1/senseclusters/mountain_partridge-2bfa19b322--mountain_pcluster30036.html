mountain partridge

noun
a North American bird, also called California partridge
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntn ˈpɑr.trɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈpɑː.trɪdʒ / (BrE)
A bird native to western North America, also known as the California partridge, slightly larger than the California quail.

Examples

The mountain partridge bird
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the mountain partridge.
The mountain partridge is distinguished by its larger size compared to the California quail.
We spotted a mountain partridge while hiking in the Sierra Nevada foothills.
