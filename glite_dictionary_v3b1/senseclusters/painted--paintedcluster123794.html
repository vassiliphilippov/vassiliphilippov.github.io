painted

adjective
artificial or insincere (emotion, appearance)
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd / (BrE)
(of an emotion, expression, or appearance) lacking genuine feeling, appearing artificial or insincere as if merely applied or performed superficially.

Examples

Painted smile
Despite his painted enthusiasm, everyone sensed his lack of interest.
She wore a painted smile at the meeting, hiding her true feelings.
The politician's painted concern did little to reassure the public.
