Indian

adjective
relating to Native Americans
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən / (BrE)
relating to the indigenous peoples of the Americas, especially those of North America, or to their cultures or languages

Examples

Indian tribe
Many museums display Indian artifacts, such as pottery and arrowheads.
She studied Indian languages at the university for her anthropology degree.
The festival celebrates traditional Indian music and dance from various tribes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Native American - a member of the indigenous peoples of the Americas, especially the United States
