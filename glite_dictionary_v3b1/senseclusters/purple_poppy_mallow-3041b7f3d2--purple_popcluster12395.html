purple poppy mallow

noun
hairy perennial plant with crimson-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.pəl ˈpɑː.pi ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈpɒp.i ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
