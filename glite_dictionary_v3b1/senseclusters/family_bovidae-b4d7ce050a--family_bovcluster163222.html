family Bovidae

noun
family of hoofed mammals (cattle, sheep, goats, antelopes)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli boʊˈvɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli bəʊˈvɪdiː / (BrE)
A large family of hoofed mammals including cattle, sheep, goats, and true antelopes.
