touch

noun
informal request for money
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ / (AmE), / tʌtʃ / (BrE)
an informal request for money, especially as a gift or loan

Examples

A touch for money
After losing his job, he was always looking for a touch from friends.
She realized that his visit was just another touch for cash.
The scam artist tried to make a touch in every bar he entered.
