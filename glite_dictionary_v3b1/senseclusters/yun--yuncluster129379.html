yun

noun
Mandarin for cloud
Pronunciation: / jʊn / (AmE), / jʊn / (BrE)
the transliteration of the Mandarin word for 'cloud', often used in names, art, and cultural references.

Examples

A yun in art
The artist painted a delicate yun floating above the mountains in her landscape.
Many Chinese poems use the word yun to symbolize freedom and change.
The vase was decorated with a swirling yun pattern inspired by traditional art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud - a visible mass of condensed water vapor in the sky
