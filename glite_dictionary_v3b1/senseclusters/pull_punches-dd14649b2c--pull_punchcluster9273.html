pull punches

verb
restrain criticism or effort
9.6 5.4
Pronunciation: /pʊl ˈpʌn.tʃɪz/ /; / /pʊl ˈpʌn.tʃəz/ (AmE), /pʊl ˈpʌn.tʃɪz/ /; / /pəl ˈpʌn.tʃɪz/ /; / /pʊl ˈpʌn.tʃəz/ (BrE)
