llareta

noun
Andean cushion plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʝaˈɾeta / (AmE), / ʝaˈɾeta / (BrE)
a cushion-shaped, slow-growing plant native to the high Andes mountains of South America

Examples

The llareta plant
The llareta can live for thousands of years in the harsh Andean climate.
Scientists study the llareta to understand how plants survive extreme conditions.
Local communities sometimes use llareta as a traditional fuel source.
