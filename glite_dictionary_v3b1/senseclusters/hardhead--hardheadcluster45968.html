hardhead

noun
fish with tough head
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌhɛd / (BrE)
a common name for several species of fish, especially those with a bony or tough head, such as certain catfish or marine fish
