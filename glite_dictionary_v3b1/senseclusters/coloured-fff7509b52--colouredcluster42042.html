Coloured

noun
offensive mixed-race term
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person of mixed race, especially in South Africa; now considered inappropriate and outdated

Examples

The Coloured community

Many people find the word Coloured deeply hurtful due to its historical context.
Modern language guides advise against using Coloured to describe anyone's racial background.
