commensurateness

noun
state of being proportionate
7.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛn.sjɚ.ətnəs / (AmE), / kəˈmɛn.sjʊərətnəs / (BrE)
the state of corresponding in degree, size, or amount; the quality of being proportionate or commensurate

Examples

The commensurateness of punishment

The commensurateness of resources allocated to each project ensures fairness.
There was a clear commensurateness between the risk taken and the reward offered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disproportion - lack of proper relation in size, amount, or degree
  • synonym: proportion - a part considered in relation to the whole; balance among the parts
