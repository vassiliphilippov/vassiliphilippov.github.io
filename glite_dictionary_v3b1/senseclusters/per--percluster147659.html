per

preposition
by means of
Pronunciation: / pɚ / (AmE), / pɜː / (BrE)
by means of, through, or according to a particular method, channel, or standard

Examples

Per email
Please send the signed form per email rather than as a paper copy.
The instructions were given per your request.
All employees must submit their reports per company policy.
