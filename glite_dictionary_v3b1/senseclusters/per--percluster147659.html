per

by means of
1.0 10/m

by means of, through, or according to a particular method, channel, or standard

Examples

Per email
Please send the signed form per email rather than as a paper copy.
The instructions were given per your request.
All employees must submit their reports per company policy.

Synonyms

  • via
    1.0 65/m
    via is used to show a route or medium, while per is often used to mean according to a rule or method.
  • by way of
    2.9 7.61/m
    by way of is a phrase for using a process or route, while per is more neutral and often means according to.
