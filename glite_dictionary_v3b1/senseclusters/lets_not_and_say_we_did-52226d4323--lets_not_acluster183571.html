x

9.9
Pronunciation: / lɛts nɑt ənd seɪ wi dɪd / (AmE), / lɛts nɒt ənd seɪ wi dɪd / (BrE)
used humorously to refuse an activity or dismiss a suggestion by pretending to have done it already

Examples

Let's not and say we did
She suggested we go hiking in the rain, so I said, 'let's not and say we did.'
When my friends wanted to clean the garage, I joked, 'let's not and say we did.'
Whenever someone proposes something boring, he always replies, 'let's not and say we did.'
