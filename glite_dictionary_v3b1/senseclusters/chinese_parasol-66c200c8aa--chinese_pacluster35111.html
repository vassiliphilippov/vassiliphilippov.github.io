Chinese parasol

noun
Asian ornamental tree with maple-like leaves
2.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈpæ.rə.sɑːl/ /; / /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈpæ.rə.səl/ (AmE), /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈpæ.rə.sɒl/ /; / /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈpæ.rə.səl/ (BrE)
A deciduous tree (Firmiana simplex) native to China, grown for its attractive, maple-like leaves and long clusters of yellow-green flowers, followed by unusual leaflike pods.
