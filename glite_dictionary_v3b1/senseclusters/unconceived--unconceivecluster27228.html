unconceived

adjective
without children
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsiːvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsiːvd / (BrE)
not having produced children or offspring; without descendants.

Examples

Unconceived heirs
The property was inherited by a distant relative because the owner died unconceived.
In old legal documents, a person described as unconceived had no direct heirs.
The title passed to another branch of the family since the nobleman remained unconceived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childless - not having any children
  • antonym: fertile - able to produce offspring
