Synonyms

    2.7 0.8/m
    brighten up makes something or someone more cheerful or brighter, while light up often means sudden happiness or making a place bright.
  • light
    1.0 40/m
    light means to start a fire or make something shine, while light up often adds suddenness or shows a face becoming very happy.
  • illuminate
    4.1 2/m
    illuminate means to shine light on and make something visible, while light up is more general and used in everyday speech.
  • glow
    1.0 4/m
