rough-leaved aster

noun
a North American plant with rough leaves and purple flowers
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌf liːvd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈrʌf liːvd ˈastə / (BrE)
A North American perennial herb of the daisy family (Eurybia radulina), characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and clusters of purple or lavender flowers.
