psyop

noun
online influence campaign

(informal) a coordinated information campaign, especially online, using social media, fake accounts, bots, or other techniques to influence public opinion or behaviour.

Examples

Online psyop campaign
Analysts claimed the viral social-media campaign looked like a state-sponsored psyop aimed at dividing voters online.
The research paper described how bots and fake accounts helped run a psyop across dozens of platforms.
After the election, journalists investigated whether the trending hashtags were part of a coordinated psyop.
