transmission system

noun
vehicle mechanism transmitting engine power to wheels
1.7 3.1
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the mechanism in a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels, typically involving gears and shafts

Examples

Vehicle transmission system
Electric vehicles often have a simplified transmission system compared to traditional petrol cars.
Regular maintenance of the transmission system can help prevent costly repairs in the future.
The car's transmission system developed a fault that made it difficult to shift gears.
