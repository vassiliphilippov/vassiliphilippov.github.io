inflect

verb
vary the pitch of voice
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɛkt / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɛkt / (BrE)
to change the pitch or tone of one's voice during speech

Examples

To inflect your voice
Actors must learn how to inflect their lines to convey different emotions.
She tried to inflect her voice to sound more enthusiastic during the presentation.
When reading stories to children, he would inflect his speech to make the characters come alive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modulate - to adjust or vary the pitch, tone, or volume of the voice
