subacromial

adjective
beneath shoulder acromion
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbəˈkroʊmiəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbəˈkrəʊmiəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath the acromion, the bony prominence on the top of the shoulder blade

Examples

Subacromial bursitis pain
The doctor diagnosed the patient with subacromial bursitis after examining his shoulder pain.
Physical therapy can help reduce inflammation in the subacromial space.
An injection was given to relieve discomfort in the subacromial region.
