Three Stooges

proper noun
comedic trio known for slapstick and clumsiness
Pronunciation: / θri ˈstuːdʒɪz / (AmE), / θriː ˈstjuːdʒɪz / (BrE)
a famous group of three comedians known for their slapstick humor and clumsy antics, often used allusively to describe people behaving foolishly together

Examples

Three Stooges comedy group
He did a funny impression of the Three Stooges slipping on banana peels.
The party turned chaotic, with everyone running around like the Three Stooges.
Whenever those three work together, they end up acting like the Three Stooges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clown - a comic entertainer; someone who behaves comically or foolishly
