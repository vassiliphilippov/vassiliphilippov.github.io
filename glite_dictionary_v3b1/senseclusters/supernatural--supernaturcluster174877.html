supernatural

adjective
beyond natural laws; unexplainable by science
2.5 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.ɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.ər.əl / (BrE)
(of a phenomenon, event, or being) not able to be explained by natural laws or science; attributed to a force beyond scientific understanding or the laws of nature.

Examples

Supernatural powers
Many people believe that supernatural forces are responsible for unexplained events.
She claimed to have experienced a supernatural vision that changed her life.
The story is filled with supernatural creatures and mysterious powers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranormal - denoting phenomena beyond normal scientific explanation
