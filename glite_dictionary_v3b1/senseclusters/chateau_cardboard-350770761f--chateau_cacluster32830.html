Chateau Cardboard

noun
humorous term for cheap housing
Pronunciation: / ʃæˈtoʊ ˈkɑrdˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ʃaːˈtoː ˈkɑːdbɔːd / (BrE)
A humorous term for a cheap, shabby, or low-quality place to live, often used ironically to refer to modest or rundown housing.

Examples

A Chateau Cardboard home
After he lost his job, Mark had to move into a Chateau Cardboard on the edge of town.
Even though their Chateau Cardboard was tiny and old, they made it feel like home.
They threw a party at their new place, jokingly calling it Chateau Cardboard.
