gene mutation

noun
change in a gene's DNA sequence
Pronunciation: / dʒiːn mjuˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒiːn mjuːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a permanent alteration in the DNA sequence of a gene, resulting in a variant form that may be transmitted to subsequent generations
