touch-me-not

noun
a plant with yellow/orange flowers, grows in wet soil
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃ mi nɑt /; / ˈtʌtʃ mi ˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ mi nɒt /; / ˈtʌtʃ mi ˌnɒt / (BrE)
a plant whose seed pods or fruit burst open and release seeds when touched, especially species in the genus Impatiens or Ecballium
