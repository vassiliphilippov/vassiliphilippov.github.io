bacalao

noun
salted cod dish
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːkəˈlaʊ / (AmE), / ˌbakəˈlaʊ / (BrE)
a dish made from salted and usually rehydrated cod, prepared in various regional styles, especially in Spanish and Portuguese cuisine

Examples

Traditional bacalao dish
My grandmother prepares her famous bacalao every Christmas Eve for the whole family.
The restaurant's signature dish is bacalao served with olives and peppers.
He learned to cook traditional bacalao while traveling through Spain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacalhau - Portuguese term for salted cod dishes
  • synonym: salt cod - codfish preserved by salting and drying
