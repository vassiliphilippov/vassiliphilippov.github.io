Korahitic

noun
descendant of Korahites
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈrɑː.hɪ.tɪk/ (AmE), /kə.ˈrɑː.hɪ.tɪk/ (BrE)
a person who belongs to or is descended from the biblical Korahites, a Levitical family associated with certain Psalms
