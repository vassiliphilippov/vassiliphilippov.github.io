sacramentally

adverb
in a sacramental way
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or resembling a religious sacrament

Examples

To sacramentally bless bread
The bread and wine were sacramentally blessed during the ceremony.
She approached the altar sacramentally, with deep reverence and devotion.
The community gathered sacramentally to mark the important religious occasion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: profane - in a way that is not sacred or religious
  • synonym: ritually - in a way that follows a religious or formal ceremony
