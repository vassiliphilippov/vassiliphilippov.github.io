fry

verb
become very hot or overheated
5.8
Pronunciation: / fraɪ / (AmE), / fraɪ / (BrE)
(informal) To become very hot, especially due to high temperatures or exposure to strong sunlight.

Examples

To fry in the sun
If you forget your hat at the festival, you’ll fry out there all afternoon.
She felt like she was going to fry during the heatwave last July.
We were starting to fry in the midday sun before we found some shade.
