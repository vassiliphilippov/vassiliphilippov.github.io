stallion

noun
uncastrated adult male horse
Pronunciation: / ˈstæliən / (AmE), / ˈstaljən / (BrE)
an adult male horse that has not been castrated, especially one kept for breeding

Examples

An adult stallion
Ranchers often separate the stallion from the rest of the herd to prevent fights.
The prize-winning stallion was admired for its strength and beauty.
They chose a purebred stallion to mate with the mares this season.

  • antonym: mare - an adult female horse
