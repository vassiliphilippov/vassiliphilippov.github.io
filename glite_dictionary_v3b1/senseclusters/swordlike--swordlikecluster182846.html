Shaped like a sword blade; long, narrow, and pointed.

Examples

A swordlike shape
He admired the swordlike elegance of the skyscraper's silhouette against the sky.
Her swordlike glare made it clear she was not pleased.
The leaves of this plant are swordlike and taper to a sharp point.

Synonyms

  • sword-shaped
    5.3 0.03/m
    sword-shaped describes leaves or objects with a clear sword-blade form, while swordlike is a more general adjective for similar shapes.
  • ensiform
    9.9 0.00/m
    ensiform is a technical, often botanical word for sword-blade shapes, while swordlike is everyday and more widely used.
  • bladelike
    6.3 0.02/m
    bladelike refers to a narrow flat shape like a knife or sword blade, while swordlike specifically evokes a sword's long pointed blade.
