warning bell

noun
signal of potential problem or danger
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrnɪŋ bɛl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ bɛl / (BrE)
an event or signal that alerts people to a potential problem, danger, or issue

Examples

Health warning bell
His irregular heartbeat was a warning bell for the doctor to investigate further.
The drop in sales rang a warning bell for the management team.
When deadlines were repeatedly missed, it set off a warning bell for the project's sponsors.
