heap on

verb
to add excessively or overburden
3.7 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / hip …ën / (AmE), / hiÀêp …ín / (BrE)
to give or add something in large quantities, especially so much that it becomes a burden or excessive

Examples

To heap on blame
It's not helpful to heap on blame without offering solutions.
Please don't heap on criticism when someone is trying their best.
The manager started to heap on more work even though we were already overwhelmed.
