tuskless

adjective
without tusks
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌskləs / (AmE), / ˈtʌskləs / (BrE)
lacking tusks, especially referring to animals that normally have them

Examples

Tuskless elephant
The tuskless elephant was less likely to be targeted by poachers.
Scientists studied a population of tuskless elephants in the national park.
Some warthogs are born tuskless, which affects their ability to dig for food.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tusked - having tusks
