league

noun
association for common action
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡ / (AmE), / ˈliːɡ / (BrE)
an association of states, organizations, or individuals formed for a common purpose

Examples

Student league
He was president of the student league at his university.
Several European countries joined the league to promote peace and cooperation.
The cities formed a league to increase their bargaining power.
