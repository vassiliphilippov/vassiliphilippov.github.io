league

noun
association for common action
2.8 9.82/m

an association of states, organizations, or individuals formed for a common purpose

Examples

Student league

Synonyms

  • confederation
    6.0 3.09/m
    a confederation is a formal union of states that keep their independence, while a league is a looser association.
  • confederacy
    7.4 0.65/m
    a confederacy is a political union often linked to historical or rebel states, while a league is a general association for many groups.
  • coalition
    4.3 42/m
    a coalition is a temporary alliance of different parties for a specific goal, while a league is usually a more lasting association.
