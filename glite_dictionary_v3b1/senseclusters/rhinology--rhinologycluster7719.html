rhinology

noun
study of the nose
Pronunciation: / raɪˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / raɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that studies the nose and its diseases

Examples

Rhinology medicine
After years of training, she became an expert in rhinology and treated many patients with nasal disorders.
The hospital opened a new department dedicated to rhinology and sinus surgery.
Advances in rhinology have improved the treatment of chronic sinus infections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dermatology - the branch of medicine dealing with the skin
  • synonym: otolaryngology - the medical specialty dealing with the ear, nose, and throat
