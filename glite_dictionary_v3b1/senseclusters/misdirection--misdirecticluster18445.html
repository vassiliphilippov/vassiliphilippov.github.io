misdirection

noun
careless or poor management
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
careless or inefficient management of resources, people, or efforts

Examples

Misdirection of resources
Her efforts were hampered by the misdirection of the project team.
The company's failure was blamed on the misdirection of its limited resources.
Years of financial misdirection left the organization in debt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mismanagement - ineffective, careless, or corrupt management
