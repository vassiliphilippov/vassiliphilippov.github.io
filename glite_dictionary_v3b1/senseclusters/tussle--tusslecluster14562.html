tussle

noun
a brief, disorderly fight or struggle
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌsəl / (BrE)
a short, vigorous struggle or fight, typically involving disorder or confusion

Examples

A quick tussle
After a brief tussle, the security guards managed to separate the crowd.
The two players got into a tussle over the ball during the game.
There was a noisy tussle outside the pub late last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawl - a noisy, rough fight in a crowd
  • synonym: fight - an act of fighting with physical force
  • synonym: scuffle - a short, confused fight or struggle at close quarters
