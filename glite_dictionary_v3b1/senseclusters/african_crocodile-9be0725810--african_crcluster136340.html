African crocodile

noun
a large crocodile species found in Africa
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈkrɑː.kəˌdaɪl / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈkrɒk.ə.daɪl / (BrE)
a large, dangerous species of crocodile native to Africa, known for its broad distribution across the continent

Examples

The African crocodile basking
During their safari, they spotted an enormous African crocodile basking on the riverbank.
Many villagers are cautious when close to water because African crocodiles are known to attack unexpectedly.
The African crocodile is one of the largest and most feared predators in the continent's rivers.
