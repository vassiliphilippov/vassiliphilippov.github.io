shrimp-fish

noun
slender tropical fish with transparent plates
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪmpˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪmpˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A slender tropical fish found in shallow East Indian waters, characterized by a body covered with transparent plates; commonly belonging to the family Centriscidae.

Examples

A shrimp-fish near reefs
Divers spotted several shrimp-fish gliding vertically near the coral reefs.
The aquarium added a beautiful shrimp-fish to its tropical exhibit last week.
The transparent plates of the shrimp-fish make it difficult to see in the water.
