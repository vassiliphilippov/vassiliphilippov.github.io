quadricycle

noun
small four-wheeled vehicle
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːdrɪˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɒdrɪˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
a small four-wheeled vehicle, usually powered by pedals or a small motor, similar to a bicycle or a low-powered car

Examples

Ride a quadricycle
The city allows quadricycles on certain roads where regular cars are not permitted.
He bought a lightweight electric quadricycle to commute short distances in the city.
Some countries require only a basic license to drive a quadricycle due to its low speed.
They rented a quadricycle to explore the park together.
The new quadricycle model is designed for families who want to ride together.
Children laughed as they pedaled their bright red quadricycle along the boardwalk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bicycle - a two-wheeled vehicle powered by pedals
  • degree higher: car - a motor vehicle with four wheels used for transportation
  • synonym: microcar - a very small, lightweight car
  • synonym: tricycle - a vehicle with three wheels powered by pedals
