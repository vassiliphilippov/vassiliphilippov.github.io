Ricinus

proper noun
plant genus
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪsɪnəs / (BrE)
a genus of plants that includes the castor bean, known for producing castor oil

Examples

Ricinus castor bean
The seeds of Ricinus are used to produce castor oil.
Ricinus plants can grow very tall in warm climates.
Scientists study Ricinus because its seeds contain both useful oil and toxic compounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: castor bean - the seed of the Ricinus plant, used to make castor oil
