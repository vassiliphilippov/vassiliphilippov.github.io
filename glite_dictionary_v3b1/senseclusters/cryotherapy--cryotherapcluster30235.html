cryotherapy

noun
cold treatment therapy
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌkraɪəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a treatment that uses very cold temperatures to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, promote healing, or improve appearance

Examples

Athlete cryotherapy session
Many athletes use cryotherapy to speed up recovery after intense workouts.
The clinic offers cryotherapy sessions to help reduce joint pain and swelling.
Doctors sometimes recommend cryotherapy for treating certain skin conditions or injuries.
Many spas now offer cryotherapy sessions to help clients achieve firmer, more youthful-looking skin.
After her workout, she booked a cryotherapy treatment to reduce muscle soreness and puffiness.
Some people use cryotherapy facials to refresh their appearance before important events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold therapy - the use of cold to treat pain or injury
  • antonym: heat therapy - the use of heat to treat medical or cosmetic conditions
  • antonym: thermotherapy - the use of heat in medical treatment
