unpretentious

adjective
not showy; modest
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs / (BrE)
not trying to impress others; simple, modest, and genuine

Examples

An unpretentious style
Although the restaurant is very popular, it remains unpretentious and friendly.
She wore an unpretentious dress that suited the occasion perfectly.
The author’s unpretentious style makes the book accessible to a wide audience.
Despite his success, he remains friendly and unpretentious in all his interactions.
The restaurant has an unpretentious atmosphere and serves delicious home-cooked meals.
They chose an unpretentious wedding ceremony with only close family and friends.
Despite her success, she remains genuinely unpretentious and approachable.
The restaurant has an unpretentious atmosphere, with simple decor and friendly staff.
Their unpretentious wedding focused on family, friends, and good food rather than lavish decorations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modest - not boastful or showing off; having or showing a moderate opinion of oneself
  • antonym: ostentatious - characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress
  • antonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or impressive than one is
