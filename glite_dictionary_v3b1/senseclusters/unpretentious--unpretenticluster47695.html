unpretentious

adjective
not showy; modest
6.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.prɪ.ˈten.ʃəs/ /; / /ʌn.prə.ˈten.ʃəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.prɪ.ˈten.ʃəs/ /; / /ʌn.prə.ˈten.ʃəs/ (BrE)
not trying to impress others; simple, modest, and genuine
