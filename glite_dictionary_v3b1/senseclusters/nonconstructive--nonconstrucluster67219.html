nonconstructive

adjective
not helpful
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈstrʌktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈstrʌktɪv / (BrE)
not helpful or not intended to produce a useful or positive result

Examples

Nonconstructive criticism
His nonconstructive comments made the meeting less productive for everyone.
We need feedback that is useful, not nonconstructive criticism.
The teacher asked students to avoid nonconstructive remarks during group discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: constructive - helpful and intended to produce a positive result
  • synonym: unhelpful - not providing useful assistance
