transmigrant

noun
person in new body
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmaɪɡrənt / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪɡrənt / (BrE)
a person whose soul or consciousness is believed to have moved into another body or life, often in stories or beliefs

Examples

A transmigrant soul
In the novel, the main character becomes a transmigrant after a mysterious accident.
Some cultures have legends about a transmigrant who remembers past lives.
The fantasy series follows a transmigrant adapting to life in a new world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: migrant - a person who moves from one place to another
  • synonym: reincarnate - a person who is believed to have been born again in a new body
