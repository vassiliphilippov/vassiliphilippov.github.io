curvilineal

adjective
having or forming curved lines
Pronunciation: / ˌkɝː.vɪˈlɪn.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌkɜː.vɪˈlɪn.i.əl / (BrE)
characterized by or following a curved line rather than a straight one

Examples

A curvilineal shape
Botanical illustrations sometimes show the curvilineal growth patterns of climbing plants.
The architect designed the building with curvilineal walls to create a sense of movement.
The artist favored curvilineal forms over straight lines in her latest sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curvilinear - characterized by or following a curved line
  • antonym: linear - consisting of or resembling a straight line
