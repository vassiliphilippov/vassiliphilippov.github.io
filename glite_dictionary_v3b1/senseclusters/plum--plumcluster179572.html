plum

noun
a small sweet fruit with a pit
1.8 1.0
Pronunciation: /plʌm/ (AmE), /plʌm/ (BrE)
a small, round or oval fruit with smooth skin, sweet flesh, and a single hard stone, produced by various trees of the genus Prunus.

Examples

A ripe plum
For dessert, he served a delicious tart filled with plums and cream.
She picked a fresh plum from the tree and bit into its juicy flesh.
The market was offering ripe plums at a very reasonable price this week.
