supergun

noun
very large gun
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌɡʌn / (BrE)
an extremely large or powerful gun, especially one designed for military use and capable of firing over long distances

Examples

Military supergun
The military displayed its new supergun at the international defense exhibition.
Engineers worked for years to develop a supergun capable of reaching distant targets.
News reports described the supergun as a breakthrough in long-range artillery technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artillery - large-caliber guns used in warfare on land
  • synonym: cannon - a large, heavy gun used in warfare
  • antonym: handgun - a small gun designed to be held in one hand
