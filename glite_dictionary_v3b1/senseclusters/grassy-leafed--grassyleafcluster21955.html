grassy-leafed

adjective
having grass-like leaves
4.7 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræsi liːft / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsi liːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that resemble grass in appearance or texture.

Examples

Grassy-leafed plants
Gardeners often choose grassy-leafed species to add texture to flower beds.
Some lilies are grassy-leafed, making them easy to mistake for true grasses at a glance.
The grassy-leafed plant thrived in the sunlit meadow next to the real grasses.
