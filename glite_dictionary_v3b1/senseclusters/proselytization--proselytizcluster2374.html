proselytization

noun
attempt to convert
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːsələˌtaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɒsəlɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of trying to persuade someone to change their religion or beliefs

Examples

Proselytization efforts
The organization faced criticism for its proselytization efforts in rural areas.
Some countries have laws restricting proselytization to protect religious harmony.
He viewed proselytization as an important part of his faith.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversion - the process of changing one's religion or beliefs
  • synonym: evangelism - the act of preaching or spreading a particular faith
  • antonym: tolerance - acceptance of different beliefs or practices
