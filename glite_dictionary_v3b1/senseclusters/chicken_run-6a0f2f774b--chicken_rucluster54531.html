chicken run

noun
enclosed yard for keeping poultry
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪk.ən ˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˌrʌn / (BrE)
an enclosed outdoor area where chickens or other poultry are kept, often attached to a chicken coop

Examples

A chicken run enclosure
Foxes can be a problem if your chicken run isn't properly secured.
She bought a new chicken run to give her hens more space to roam.
The children helped their grandfather repair the old chicken run in the backyard.
