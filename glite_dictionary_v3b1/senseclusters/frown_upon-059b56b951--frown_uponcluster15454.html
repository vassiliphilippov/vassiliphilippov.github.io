frown upon

verb
show disapproval of
5.7
Pronunciation: / fraʊn əˈpɑn / (AmE), / fraʊn əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to disapprove of something or someone, or consider it unacceptable

Examples

To frown upon bad behavior
In her culture, it is frowned upon to speak loudly in public.
Many parents frown upon letting children watch too much television.
Most companies frown upon employees using social media during work hours.
It is frowned upon to speak loudly in libraries.
Smoking in public places is generally frowned upon these days.
Their company frowns upon employees using social media during work hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approve of - to think that someone or something is good or acceptable
  • synonym: disapprove of - to have an unfavorable opinion about something
