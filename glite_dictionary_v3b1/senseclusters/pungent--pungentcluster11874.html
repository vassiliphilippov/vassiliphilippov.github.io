pungent

adjective
sharply expressive; caustic
8.2 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌn.dʒənt/ (AmE), /ˈpʌn.dʒənt/ (BrE)
(of speech, remarks, or writing) sharply expressive or capable of wounding emotionally; caustic or biting.

Examples

Pungent comment
Her pungent wit often left her audience stunned and silent.
She delivered a pungent satire of modern politics in her latest column.
The critic's pungent review stung the playwright deeply.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - (of remarks, etc.) lacking strong features or characteristics; not sharp
  • synonym: caustic - (of speech or writing) severely critical or sarcastic
