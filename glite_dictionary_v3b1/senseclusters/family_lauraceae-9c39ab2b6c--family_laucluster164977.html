family Lauraceae

noun
botanical family including laurels and avocados
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli lɔːˈreɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæməli lɔːˈreɪsiː / (BrE)
a large family of mainly tropical and subtropical trees and shrubs, including laurels, cinnamons, and avocados.

Examples

Family Lauraceae trees
Avocado trees, which belong to the family Lauraceae, are native to Central and South America.
Cinnamon comes from the bark of certain trees in the family Lauraceae.
The family Lauraceae includes over fifty genera and more than two thousand species worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laurel - an evergreen shrub or tree of the family Lauraceae
