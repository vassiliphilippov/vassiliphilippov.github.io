canso

noun
Canadian patrol aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈkænsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkæn.səʊ / (BrE)
a Canadian-built version of the PBY Catalina flying boat used for maritime patrol and rescue missions

Examples

During World War II, the canso played a vital role in search and rescue operations.
A team of volunteers worked for years to rebuild the historic canso for public display.
