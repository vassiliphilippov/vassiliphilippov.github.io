brilliant

adjective
clear, sharp, ringing (of sound)
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪljənt / (AmE), / ˈbrɪliənt / (BrE)
(of sound) clear, sharp, and ringing; having a bright, penetrating quality.

Examples

Brilliant sound
Her singing voice was known for its brilliant clarity and resonance.
The conductor praised the brilliant sound produced by the string section.
The trumpet solo had a brilliant tone that stood out in the orchestra.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bright - (of sound) clear and distinct in quality
  • antonym: dull - (of sound) lacking sharpness or clarity
