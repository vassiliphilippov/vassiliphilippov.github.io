pat

noun
sound of a gentle blow
2.1
Pronunciation: / pæt / (AmE), / pæt / (BrE)
the gentle sound made by a light or soft blow

Examples

The pat of feet
She heard the pat of small feet on the wooden floor.
The raindrops made a soft pat against the windowpane.
There was a gentle pat as he clapped his hands together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - a light touch or blow, often creating a soft sound
