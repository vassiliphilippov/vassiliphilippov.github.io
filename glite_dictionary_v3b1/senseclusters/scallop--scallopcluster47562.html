scallop

noun
rounded projection or notch along an edge
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskæl.əp / (AmE), / ˈskæl.əp / (BrE)
a small, rounded projection or indentation along the edge of something, such as a leaf, piece of cloth, or shell

Examples

Scallop edge
Each leaf displayed a distinctive scallop pattern along its outer edge.
The craftsman carved a decorative scallop into the wooden frame.
The dress was trimmed with a delicate scallop along the hem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serration - a jagged or tooth-like projection along an edge
