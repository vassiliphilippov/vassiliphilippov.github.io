festoon

verb
decorate with strings of flowers or garlands
7.5 1.20/m
Pronunciation: / f…õÀàstuÀên / (AmE), / f…õÀàstuÀên / (BrE)
to decorate a place with chains, garlands, or strings of flowers, ribbons, or similar decorations, especially by draping them in loops.

Examples

To festoon a room
She festooned the hallways with paper chains and balloons for the celebration.
The town square was festooned with colorful banners for the festival.
The trees along the avenue were festooned with twinkling lights for the winter holiday.

Synonyms

  • wreathe with
    6.9 0.04/m
    wreathe with means to encircle something with a round garland like a crown, while festoon means to drape long strings or garlands in loops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorate - to make something look more attractive by putting things on it
