stinging

noun
sharp, sudden pain or sensation
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, sudden pain or sensation similar to being stung

Examples

A sudden stinging
He was surprised by the stinging of the icy wind against his face.
She felt a sudden stinging after touching the jellyfish.
The stinging from the paper cut lasted for several minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sting - a sharp pain caused by a stinger or similar object
