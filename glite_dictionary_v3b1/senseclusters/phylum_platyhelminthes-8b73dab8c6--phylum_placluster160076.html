phylum Platyhelminthes

noun
phylum of flatworms
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.ləm plæ.tɪ.hel.ˈmɪn.θiːz/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ləm plæ.tɪ.hel.ˈmɪn.θiːz/ (BrE)
