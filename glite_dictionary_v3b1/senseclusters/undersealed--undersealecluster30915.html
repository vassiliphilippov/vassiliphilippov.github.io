undersealed

adjective
having rustproof coating applied underneath (vehicles)
7.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àå ånd…öÀàsiÀêld / (AmE), / Àå ånd…ôÀàsiÀêld / (BrE)
(of a motor vehicle) having a protective coating of tar or other rustproof material applied to the underside to prevent corrosion.

Examples

An undersealed car
Before resale, all the vehicles were thoroughly cleaned and undersealed for added protection.
He checked if the used truck had been undersealed before making an offer.
The classic car was carefully undersealed to prevent rust during the winter months.
