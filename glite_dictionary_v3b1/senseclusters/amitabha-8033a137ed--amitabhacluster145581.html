Amitabha

proper noun
male Indian name
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːmɪˈtɑːbə / (AmE), / ˌæmɪˈtɑːbə / (BrE)
a male given name of Indian origin, meaning 'infinite light', often used in honor of the Buddha

Examples

Amitabha as a name
They named their son Amitabha to honor their family's Buddhist heritage.
At the ceremony, Amitabha received blessings from the monks.
The teacher explained the meaning of the name Amitabha to the class.
