entangle around

verb
twist or weave intricately around
5.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtæŋɡəl əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɪnˈtæŋɡl əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to twist or weave something so that it winds intricately around something else

Examples

To entangle around roots
As the roots entangled around the stone, they became impossible to separate.
Her hair would always entangle around her necklace if she wasn’t careful.
The cables began to entangle around the legs of the chair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entangle - to twist together so as to make it difficult to free; to ensnare
  • antonym: unwrap - to remove what is wrapped or twisted around something
