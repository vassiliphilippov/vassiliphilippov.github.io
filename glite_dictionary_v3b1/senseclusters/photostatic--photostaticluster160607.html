photostatic

adjective
relating to photocopies
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈstætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by the process of making photocopies, especially using a photostat machine

Examples

Photostatic copy
The lawyer requested a photostatic copy of the contract for the case file.
All photostatic reproductions must be clearly marked as copies, not originals.
She found a photostatic version of the old letter in the archives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original - not copied; the first or earliest version
  • synonym: photocopied - produced by a photocopier
