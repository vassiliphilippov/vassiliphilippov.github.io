ice-skater

noun
person who ice skates
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌskeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in the sport or activity of ice skating

Examples

A ice-skater on ice
After spending years practicing on frozen ponds, he became a skilled ice-skater.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an Olympic ice-skater.
The professional ice-skater performed a flawless triple axel during the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skater - a person who skates, especially on ice
