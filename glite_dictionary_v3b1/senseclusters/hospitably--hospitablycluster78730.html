hospitably

adverb
in a hospitable or welcoming manner
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈspɪtəbli / (AmE), / hɒˈspɪtəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows friendly and generous treatment of guests or strangers.

Examples

To hospitably welcome guests
Despite the bad weather, the guests were received hospitably at the inn.
She was treated hospitably by her host family during her exchange program.
The villagers welcomed the tourists hospitably when they arrived in the remote town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hospitable - friendly and generous to guests or strangers
  • antonym: inhospitably - in an unfriendly or unwelcoming manner
