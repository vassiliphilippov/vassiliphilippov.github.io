bristle

verb
be thickly covered with bristles
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsəl / (BrE)
(of an object or surface) to have or be thickly covered with bristles or something resembling bristles.

Examples

The wall bristled with spikes
Her anger bristled on the surface, visible in every tense muscle.
The cactus bristled with long, sharp spines that deterred animals from eating it.
The old paintbrush bristles with dried streaks of color from years of use.

  • synonym: throng - to be filled or crowded with something
