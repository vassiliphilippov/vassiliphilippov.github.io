imaginal

adjective
adult insect stage
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪnəl / (AmE), / ɪˈmædʒɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to the adult stage of an insect's life cycle or to imagination

Examples

The imaginal stage
The butterfly emerged in its imaginal form after metamorphosis.
Scientists studied the imaginal development of beetles in the laboratory.
The imaginal stage is when the insect reaches full maturity and can reproduce.

Related Terms

  • antonym: larval - relating to the immature, early stage of an insect
  • antonym: pupal - relating to the stage between larva and adult in insects
