antirestoration

noun
opponent of restoration
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntaɪˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who opposes the restoration of a previous system, order, or condition

Examples

An antirestoration leader
The antirestoration argued that bringing back the old laws would harm progress.
As an antirestoration, he spoke out against returning to previous political systems.
Many antirestoration activists attended the rally to protest the proposed changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opponent - a person who is against something
  • antonym: restorationist - a person who supports restoring a former system or order
