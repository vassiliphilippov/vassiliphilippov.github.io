Tom Paine

proper noun
English-born writer & political activist, revolutionary thinker
Pronunciation: / tɑm peɪn / (AmE), / tɒm peɪn / (BrE)
(1737–1809) An English-born American political activist and writer whose works supported the American Revolution and the French Revolution.
