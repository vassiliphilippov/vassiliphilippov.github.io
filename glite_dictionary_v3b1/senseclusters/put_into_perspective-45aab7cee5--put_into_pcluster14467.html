put into perspective

verb
understand the true importance by comparison or context
3.2
Pronunciation: / pʊt ˌɪntu pɚˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / pʊt ˌɪntuː pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
to help someone see or understand the true importance, size, or nature of something by comparing it with other things or by giving context

Examples
