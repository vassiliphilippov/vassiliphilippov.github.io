illegibly

adverb
in an unreadable manner
2.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈledʒ.ə.bliː/ /; / /ɪ.ˈledʒ.ɪ.bliː/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈledʒ.ɪ.bliː/ /; / /ɪ.ˈledʒ.ə.bliː/ (BrE)
In a way that is difficult or impossible to read.

Examples

Write illegibly on paper
If you write illegibly on the exam, the teacher might not be able to grade your answers properly.
She filled out the form illegibly, making it hard for anyone to understand her responses.
The prescription was written so illegibly that the pharmacist had to call the doctor.
