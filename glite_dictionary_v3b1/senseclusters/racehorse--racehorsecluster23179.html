racehorse

noun
horse bred for racing
4.3 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪs.hɔrs /; / ˈreɪsˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈreɪs.hɔːs /; / ˈreɪsˌhɔːs / (BrE)
a horse that is bred and trained to compete in horse races

Examples

A racehorse trainer
She owns several champion racehorses that have won prestigious races.
The retired racehorse was adopted by a loving family.
Training a young racehorse requires patience and expertise.
After retiring from competition, the racehorse was kept as a breeding stallion.
She has always dreamed of owning a champion racehorse.
The racehorse thundered down the track towards the finish line.
