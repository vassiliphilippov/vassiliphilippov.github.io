whistle

noun
simple fipple flute instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪsl̩ / (BrE)
A simple, inexpensive flute with a fipple mouthpiece, commonly used in folk music.

Examples

A tin whistle
