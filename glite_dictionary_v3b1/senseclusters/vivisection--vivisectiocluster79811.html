vivisection

noun
surgical operation on living animals for research
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /vɪ.vɪ.ˈsek.ʃən/ (AmE), /vɪ.vɪ.ˈsek.ʃən/ (BrE)
the practice of performing surgical operations on living animals for scientific research, especially to study physiological or pathological processes

Examples

Vivisection on animals
Many medical advances in the nineteenth century came about through the use of vivisection.
Some universities have reduced or eliminated their vivisection programs due to public pressure and changes in research guidelines.
The ethical controversy surrounding vivisection has sparked debates among scientists and animal rights groups.
