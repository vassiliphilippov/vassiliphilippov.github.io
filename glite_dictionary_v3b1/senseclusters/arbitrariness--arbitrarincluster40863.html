arbitrariness

noun
quality of being random or unpredictable
5.5 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːr.bɪˈtrer.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɑː.bɪˈtrɑː.rɪ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system

Examples

The arbitrariness of decisions
Critics argued that the law was enforced with arbitrariness, undermining public trust.
Scientists seek to eliminate arbitrariness from their experiments by following strict protocols.
The manager's arbitrariness made staff unsure of what to expect each day.

  • antonym: reasonableness - the quality of being fair and sensible
