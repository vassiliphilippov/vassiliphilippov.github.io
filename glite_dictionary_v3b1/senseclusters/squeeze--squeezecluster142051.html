squeeze

noun
marketing page for collecting contacts
7.6
Pronunciation: /skwiːz/ (AmE), /skwiːz/ (BrE)
(in online marketing) a web page created specifically to collect visitors' contact information (for example, email addresses), usually by offering content or an incentive in exchange.

Examples

Squeeze page signup
The marketer created a squeeze page to collect emails from interested visitors.
They A/B tested different squeeze designs to improve the sign-up conversion rate.
Without a clear call to action, the squeeze failed to convert visitors into subscribers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landing page - a web page designed to receive visitors and encourage a specific action, such as signing up or purchasing.
  • synonym: lead capture page - a web page whose primary purpose is to obtain a visitor's contact details (leads).
