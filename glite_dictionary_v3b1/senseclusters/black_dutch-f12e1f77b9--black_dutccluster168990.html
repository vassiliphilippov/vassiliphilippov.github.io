Black Dutch

noun
term for people of mixed ancestry (U.S. historical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈdʌtʃ / (AmE), / blak ˈdʌtʃ / (BrE)
a term historically used in the United States to describe people of mixed ancestry, often of African, Native American, and European descent, with unclear or ambiguous origins
