wild dog

noun
undomesticated dog-like canid
2.8
Pronunciation: / waɪld dɔg / (AmE), / waɪld dɒg / (BrE)
any of various non-domesticated mammals of the dog family (Canidae) that resemble domestic dogs, as distinguished from jackals or wolves

Examples

A pack of wild dogs
A pack of wild dogs was spotted near the river yesterday.
African wild dogs are known for their striking coat patterns and highly social behavior.
Unlike wolves or jackals, wild dogs tend to hunt in open grasslands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jackal - a wild dog-like mammal of Africa and Asia
  • antonym: wolf - large wild carnivorous mammal of the dog family
