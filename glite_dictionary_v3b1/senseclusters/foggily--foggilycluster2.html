foggily

adverb
unclearly or vaguely
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːɡɪli / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is unclear, vague, or as if covered by fog

Examples

To foggily remember
He could foggily recall the events of that strange night.
The instructions were foggily written, making them hard to follow.
After waking up, she foggily remembered her dream.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • synonym: vaguely - in a way that is not clear or definite
