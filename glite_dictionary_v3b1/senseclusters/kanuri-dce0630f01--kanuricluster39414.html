Kanuri

noun
West African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈnʊri / (AmE), / kəˈnʊəri / (BrE)
a member of the Kanuri people, an ethnic group native to the Lake Chad region of West Africa

Examples

A Kanuri person
The Kanuri have a rich history and are known for their traditional music and crafts.
Many Kanuri communities are found in northeastern Nigeria and surrounding countries.
A Kanuri leader played an important role in the region's history during the 19th century.
