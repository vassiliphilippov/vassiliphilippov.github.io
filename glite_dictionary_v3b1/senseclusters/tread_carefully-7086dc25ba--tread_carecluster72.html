tread carefully

verb
proceed cautiously; act with care
4.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /tred ˈker.fə.lɪ/ /; / /tred ˈker.fə.liː/ (AmE), /tred ˈkeə.fə.lɪ/ /; / /tred ˈkeə.fə.liː/ (BrE)
