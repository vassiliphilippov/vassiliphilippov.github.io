stock

verb
supply with livestock
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: / stɑk / (AmE), / stɒk / (BrE)
to provide, supply, or keep something available for use or sale (such as goods, animals, or fish)

Examples

They always stock fresh produce even in winter.
