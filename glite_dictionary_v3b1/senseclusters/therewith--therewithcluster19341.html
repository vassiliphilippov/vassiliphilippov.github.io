therewith

adverb
with that or this; as a result
4.2
Pronunciation: / ðɛrˈwɪθ / (AmE), / ðeəˈwɪθ / (BrE)
with that, with this, or with it; immediately following or as a consequence of something just mentioned.

Examples

Troubled therewith
He received the letter and was much troubled therewith.
If he brings a gift, let him come therewith.
She finished her speech and departed therewith.
