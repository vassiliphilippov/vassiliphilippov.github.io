liftable

adjective
able to be removed
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪftəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪftəbəl / (BrE)
able to be removed, ended, or revoked, especially referring to rules, bans, or restrictions.

Examples

Liftable ban
The curfew is liftable if the situation improves in the coming weeks.
Many restrictions are only liftable after the government reviews the latest health data.
The ban on large gatherings is not liftable until further notice from the authorities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanent - lasting or intended to last indefinitely
  • synonym: revocable - able to be cancelled or withdrawn
