Giovanni Maria Mastai-Ferretti

proper noun
birth name of Pope Pius IX
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊˈvɑːni məˈriːə mæˈstaɪ fəˈrɛti / (AmE), / dʒəʊˈvɑːni məˈriːə masˈtaɪ fəˈrɛtti / (BrE)
The birth name of Pope Pius IX, the Italian pope from 1846 to 1878 who declared the dogma of the Immaculate Conception in 1854.

Examples

Giovanni Maria Mastai-Ferretti pope name
Giovanni Maria Mastai-Ferretti was later known as Pope Pius IX after his election to the papacy.
Many historians study Giovanni Maria Mastai-Ferretti to understand the changes in the Catholic Church during the 19th century.
The declaration of the Immaculate Conception in 1854 was a significant act by Giovanni Maria Mastai-Ferretti.
