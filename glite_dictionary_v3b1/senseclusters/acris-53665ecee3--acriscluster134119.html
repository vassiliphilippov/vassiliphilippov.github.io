Acris

noun
genus of small North American frogs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.krɪs / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.krɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small North American frogs commonly known as cricket frogs, belonging to the family Hylidae.

Examples

Acris frog genus
Field guides list Acris as one of the most widespread frog genera in the United States.
Scientists often study Acris populations to monitor environmental changes in wetlands.
The mating call of Acris is similar to the sound of crickets.
