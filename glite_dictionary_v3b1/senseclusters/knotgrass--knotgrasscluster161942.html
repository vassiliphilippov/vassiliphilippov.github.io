knotgrass

noun
a low, weedy plant with spikelets on stems
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑtˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈnɒtˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a low-growing weedy plant with slender stems and small spikelets forming along the leaf stems, commonly found in fields and waste places

Examples

Common knotgrass plant
Botanists have studied the medicinal properties of common knotgrass for centuries.
Some gardeners find knotgrass difficult to remove due to its tough roots and spreading habit.
The knotgrass quickly spread across the edges of the field, making it hard for crops to grow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polygonum - a genus of flowering plants that includes knotgrass
