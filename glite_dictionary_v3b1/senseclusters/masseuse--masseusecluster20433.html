masseuse

noun
female massage therapist
Pronunciation: / məˈsus / (AmE), / mæˈsɜːz / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to give people massages

Examples

A masseuse at spa
After a long flight, I booked an appointment with a masseuse to help me relax.
He recommended his favorite masseuse at the spa downtown.
The masseuse used aromatic oils during the massage session.
