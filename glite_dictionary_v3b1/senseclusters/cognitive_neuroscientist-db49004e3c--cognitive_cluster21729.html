cognitive neuroscientist

noun
scientist studying brain and mental processes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːɡnəˌtɪv ˌnʊroʊˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnətɪv ˌnjʊərəʊˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies how brain processes relate to thoughts, feelings, and mental functions

Examples

Cognitive neuroscientist studies brain
As a cognitive neuroscientist, she investigates how memory is encoded in the brain.
He became interested in becoming a cognitive neuroscientist after taking a psychology class in college.
The university hired a cognitive neuroscientist to lead the new brain research center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cognitive science - the scientific study of mind and its processes
  • degree lower: neuroscientist - a scientist who studies the nervous system
