cognitive neuroscientist

noun
scientist studying brain and mental processes
5.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːɡ.nɪ.tɪv njuː.rə.ˈsaɪ.ən.tɪst/ (AmE), /ˈkɒɡ.nɪ.tɪv njuː.rəʊ.ˈsaɪ.ənt.ɪst/ (BrE)
