Planckian

adjective
following Planck's law
Pronunciation: / ˈplæŋkiən / (AmE), / ˈplæŋkiən / (BrE)
relating to or having properties that follow Planck's law, especially in reference to blackbody radiation or spectra

Examples

Planckian spectrum
The scientist analyzed the Planckian spectrum emitted by the heated object in the laboratory.
A Planckian distribution describes the intensity of radiation from a perfect blackbody at a given temperature.
The data showed a nearly Planckian emission curve, supporting the theoretical predictions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackbody - an idealized physical body that absorbs all incident electromagnetic radiation
