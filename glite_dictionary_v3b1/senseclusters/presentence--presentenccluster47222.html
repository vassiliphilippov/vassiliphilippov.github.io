presentence

adjective
before sentencing
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈsɛntəns / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈsɛntəns / (BrE)
relating to the period or procedures before a court's sentencing decision

Examples

Presentence investigation report
The court requested a presentence investigation to gather more information about the defendant.
A presentence report can influence the judge's final decision on punishment.
Lawyers often submit presentence memoranda to highlight mitigating factors before sentencing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsentencing - relating to the period after a court's sentencing decision
  • synonym: pretrial - relating to the period before a trial begins
