technicism

noun
focus on technique
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtek.nɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈtek.nə.sɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈtek.nɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈtek.nə.sɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
an emphasis on technical skill or detailed technique, often at the expense of emotion or meaning

Examples

Artistic technicism
The artist's technicism impressed the judges, but some felt the work lacked passion.
In music competitions, too much technicism can make performances seem cold or mechanical.
Her writing style is marked by technicism, focusing on structure rather than emotional impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: technicality - a detail or aspect related to technique or procedure
