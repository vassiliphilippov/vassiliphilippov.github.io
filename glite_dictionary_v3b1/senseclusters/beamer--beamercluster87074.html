beamer

noun
image projector
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːmər / (AmE), / ˈbiːmə / (BrE)
a device that projects images or video onto a screen, commonly used for presentations or movies

Examples

A beamer projector
The teacher used a beamer to display the lesson slides on the classroom wall.
We rented a beamer for the outdoor movie night in the park.
Please connect your laptop to the beamer before starting your presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: projector - a device that projects images or video onto a surface
