cosplay

verb
dress up as a media character, esp. in anime
4.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊsˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒsˌpleɪ / (BrE)
to dress up as a character from a film, book, or other media, especially from Japanese anime, often as part of a fan event or convention

Examples

To cosplay anime characters
Every year, thousands of fans cosplay their favorite characters at Comic-Con.
Many people cosplay in groups to recreate scenes from anime or games.
She decided to cosplay as Sailor Moon for the convention this weekend.
