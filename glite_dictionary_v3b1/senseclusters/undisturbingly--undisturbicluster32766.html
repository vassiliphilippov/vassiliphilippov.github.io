undisturbingly

adverb
calmly, without worry
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈstɜrbɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈstɜːbɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that does not cause disturbance, worry, or upset

Examples

Undisturbingly quiet
The landscape appeared undisturbingly quiet after the storm had passed.
She spoke undisturbingly, helping everyone feel at ease during the meeting.
The results were undisturbingly normal, which reassured the entire team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed way
  • antonym: disturbingly - in a way that causes worry or upset
  • synonym: peacefully - in a way that is quiet and free from disturbance
