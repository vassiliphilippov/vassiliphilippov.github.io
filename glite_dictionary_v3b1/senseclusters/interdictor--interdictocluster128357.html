interdictor

noun
blocker or preventer
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdɪktər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdɪktə / (BrE)
a person or thing, especially a military aircraft, that prevents or blocks the movement or progress of something

Examples

Military interdictor jet
The military deployed an interdictor to prevent enemy supplies from reaching the front lines.
An advanced interdictor aircraft can disrupt communication between opposing forces.
The role of an interdictor is crucial in stopping illegal shipments across borders.
