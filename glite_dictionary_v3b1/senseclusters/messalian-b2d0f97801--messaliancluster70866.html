Messalian

adjective
of Messalian sect
Pronunciation: / mɛˈseɪliən / (AmE), / mɛˈseɪliən / (BrE)
relating to the Messalian sect, known for their focus on continuous mystical prayer and rejection of church authority.

Examples

Messalian beliefs condemned
The council condemned several Messalian beliefs as heretical in the fifth century.
Her writings reflect a distinctly Messalian emphasis on personal spiritual experience over formal rituals.
Historians debate the true influence of Messalian practices on later mystical movements.
