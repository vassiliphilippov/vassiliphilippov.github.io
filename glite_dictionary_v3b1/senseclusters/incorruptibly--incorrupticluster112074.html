incorruptibly

adverb
in an untamperable way
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəˈrʌptəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəˈrʌptəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be changed, tampered with, or made dishonest; showing complete honesty or integrity

Examples

The official performed his duties incorruptibly, refusing all offers of bribery.
She managed the charity incorruptibly, ensuring every donation was used properly.
Throughout his long career, he acted incorruptibly and earned the public's trust.
