Georgian

noun
the language of Georgia (Caucasus)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔrdʒən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒiən / (BrE)
the language of Georgia, a country in the Caucasus, spoken by about 3 million people and known for its long literary tradition

Examples

Speak Georgian
Many students at the university are studying Georgian as a foreign language.
She is fluent in both Russian and Georgian.
The novel was originally written in Georgian before being translated into English.
