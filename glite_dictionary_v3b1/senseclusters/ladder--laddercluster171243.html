ladder

noun
stages or levels of progression
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlædɚ / (AmE), / ˈladə / (BrE)
a series of stages or levels through which someone or something can progress, especially in a career or organization

Examples

Career ladder
Many people strive to climb the social ladder to improve their status.
Reaching the top of the academic ladder requires years of study and research.
She quickly moved up the ladder in her company through hard work and dedication.

Related Terms
