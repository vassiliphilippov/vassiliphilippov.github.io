jaguarundi

noun
small wildcat of Central and South America
Pronunciation: / dʒæɡwəˈrʌndi / (AmE), / dʒæɡjʊəˈrʌndi / (BrE)
a long-bodied, long-tailed wildcat native to Central and South America, with a uniform coat and short legs

Examples

The jaguarundi cat
Scientists are studying the habitat of the jaguarundi in southern Texas.
The jaguarundi is rarely seen in the wild due to its elusive nature.
Unlike other wildcats, the jaguarundi has a uniform coat rather than spots.
