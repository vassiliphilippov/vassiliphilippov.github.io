overinflation

noun
excess air in lungs
7.4
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ɪn.ˈfleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /əʊ.vər.ɪn.ˈfleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an abnormal condition in which too much air is trapped in the lungs or a lung region, causing enlargement

Examples

Lung overinflation
The doctor explained that overinflation of the lungs can make breathing more difficult for some patients.
Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease may lead to overinflation in certain areas of the lungs.
A CT scan revealed significant overinflation in the upper lobes of his lungs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atelectasis - collapse or closure of a lung resulting in reduced air
  • synonym: hyperinflation - excessive accumulation of air in the lungs
