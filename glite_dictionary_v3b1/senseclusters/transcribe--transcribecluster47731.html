transcribe

verb
convert DNA into RNA (biology)
4.3
Pronunciation: / trænˈskraɪb / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪb / (BrE)
(in molecular biology) to convert the genetic information in a strand of DNA into a complementary strand of RNA, especially messenger RNA.

Examples

To transcribe DNA to RNA
During gene expression, the enzyme RNA polymerase transcribes the DNA sequence into messenger RNA.
Some viruses can transcribe their genetic material directly from RNA without the need for DNA.
The process by which DNA is transcribed into RNA is essential for protein synthesis.
