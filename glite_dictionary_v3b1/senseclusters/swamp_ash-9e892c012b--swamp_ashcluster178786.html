swamp ash

noun
ash tree from southeastern US swamps
9.9
Pronunciation: / swɑmp æʃ / (AmE), / swɒmp æʃ / (BrE)
A type of ash tree (Fraxinus species) native to swampy areas of the southeastern United States, often valued for its lightweight wood.
