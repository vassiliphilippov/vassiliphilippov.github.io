Gandhara

proper noun
ancient South Asian region
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɡænˈdɑːrə / (AmE), / ɡænˈdɑːrə / (BrE)
an ancient region in South Asia, famous for its Buddhist art and cultural influence, located in present-day Pakistan and Afghanistan

Examples

Ancient Gandhara region
The ancient kingdom of Gandhara played a key role in the spread of Buddhism.
Many museums display artifacts from Gandhara that reflect a blend of Greek and Indian artistic styles.
Scholars study Gandhara to understand the cultural exchanges between East and West in ancient times.
