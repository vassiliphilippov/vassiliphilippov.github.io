teacup

noun
cup used for drinking tea
Pronunciation: / ˈtiː.kʌp / (AmE), / ˈtiː.kʌp / (BrE)
A small cup with a handle, typically made of china or porcelain, used for drinking tea.

Examples

A porcelain teacup
He accidentally chipped the edge of his favorite teacup.
She poured her tea carefully into the delicate teacup.
The elegant set came with matching saucers and a floral teacup.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: mug - a larger cup, often without a saucer, used for hot drinks
  • synonym: saucer - a small shallow dish for holding a cup
