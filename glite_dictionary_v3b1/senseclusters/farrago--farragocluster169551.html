farrago

noun
a confused mixture of things
Pronunciation: / fəˈrɑɡoʊ / (AmE), / fəˈrɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A confused mixture; an assortment of diverse or incongruous things.

Examples

A farrago of ideas
His explanation was a farrago of contradictions that left everyone confused.
The author’s new book is a farrago of facts, fiction, and personal anecdotes.
The exhibit presented a farrago of styles, ranging from classical to modernist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hodgepodge - a jumbled mixture of things
  • synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • synonym: mixture - a combination of different things
