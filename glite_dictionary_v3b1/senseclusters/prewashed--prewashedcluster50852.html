prewashed

adjective
washed before sale
3.5
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈwɑːʃt/ /; / /prɪ.ˈwɑːʃt/ (AmE), /priː.ˈwɒʃt/ /; / /prɪ.ˈwɒʃt/ (BrE)
washed during manufacturing to reduce shrinkage, fix dye, or give a softer or faded appearance

Examples

Prewashed jeans
She bought a pair of prewashed jeans that felt soft and comfortable from the first wear.
The prewashed fabric did not shrink after being laundered at home.
Many people prefer prewashed clothing because it looks and feels broken in.
