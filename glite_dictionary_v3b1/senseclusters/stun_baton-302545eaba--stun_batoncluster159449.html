stun baton

noun
nonlethal electric shock weapon
1.0
Pronunciation: / stʌn bəˈtɑn / (AmE), / stʌn bəˈtɒn / (BrE)
a weapon that temporarily disables a person by delivering a nonlethal high-voltage electric shock

Examples

A stun baton weapon
He demonstrated how a stun baton can temporarily immobilize an attacker.
Police used a stun baton to subdue the aggressive suspect.
The security guard carried a stun baton for personal protection.
