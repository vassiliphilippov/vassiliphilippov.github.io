Tutelo language

noun
extinct Siouan language of the Tutelo people
9.9
Pronunciation: / tuːˈtɛloʊ ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tjuːˈtɛləʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Siouan language formerly spoken by the Tutelo people, indigenous to the eastern United States.
