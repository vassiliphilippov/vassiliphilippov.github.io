green

adjective
not fully developed or ripe
2.5
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
(of fruit, wood, or other substances) not fully developed or mature; not ripe.

Examples

Green fruit
It's hard to work with green wood because it still contains a lot of moisture.
Some fruits are picked while still green so they ripen during transport.
The recipe calls for green tomatoes, which are firmer and more tart than ripe ones.
