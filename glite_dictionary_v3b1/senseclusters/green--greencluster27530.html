green

adjective
not fully developed or ripe
2.1 5.30/m
Pronunciation: / …°rin / (AmE), / …°riÀên / (BrE)
(of fruit, wood, or other substances) not fully developed or mature; not ripe.

Examples

The recipe calls for green tomatoes, which are firmer and more tart than ripe ones.
