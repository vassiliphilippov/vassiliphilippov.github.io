nonrepresentation

noun
absence of representation
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of not acting as a representative for someone, especially in legal or formal situations

Examples

Nonrepresentation in court
The defendant's nonrepresentation in court led to a delay in the trial proceedings.
Due to nonrepresentation, the organization could not participate in the final vote.
Her case was dismissed because of nonrepresentation by legal counsel at the hearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absence - the state of being not present or not available
  • antonym: representation - the act of speaking or acting on behalf of someone
