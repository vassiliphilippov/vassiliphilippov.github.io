supernaturalistic

adjective
relating to supernaturalism or the supernatural
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˌnætʃ.ɚ.əˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˌnætʃ.ə.rəˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)

Examples
