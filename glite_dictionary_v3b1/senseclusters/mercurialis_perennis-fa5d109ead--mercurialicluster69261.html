Mercurialis perennis

noun
European woodland plant with greenish flowers
Pronunciation: / mərˌkjʊəriˈælɪs pəˈrɛnɪs / (AmE), / mɜːˌkjʊəriˈælɪs pəˈrɛnɪs / (BrE)
A European perennial plant of the spurge family, with greenish flowers, commonly found in woodland and known as dog's mercury.
