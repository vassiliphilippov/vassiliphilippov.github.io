gassing

noun
exposure to gas; process of applying gas
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡasɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of exposing something to a gas, especially for a chemical or industrial purpose

Examples

Industrial gassing process
Improper gassing can lead to hazardous conditions in the plant.
Industries use gassing procedures to sterilize certain products.
The gassing of the laboratory samples is essential for this experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasification - the process of converting solid or liquid into gas
