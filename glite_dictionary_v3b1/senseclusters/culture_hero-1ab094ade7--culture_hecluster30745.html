culture hero

noun
person admired for culture contributions
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃər ˌhiroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃə ˌhɪərəʊ / (BrE)
a person who is greatly admired for making significant contributions to the culture or society

Examples

A culture hero figure
After her innovative work in film, she was recognized as a culture hero in the industry.
Many young artists view him as a culture hero for his commitment to creativity and social progress.
Maya Angelou became a culture hero for her influence on literature and civil rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icon - a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol or as worthy of veneration
  • antonym: villain - a person who harms or opposes the values of a culture or community
