pineal

adjective
relating to the pineal gland
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪniəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪniəl / (BrE)
relating to the pineal gland, a small endocrine gland in the brain that produces melatonin.

Examples

Pineal gland

The pineal gland is responsible for producing melatonin.
