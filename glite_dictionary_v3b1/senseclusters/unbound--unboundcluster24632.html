unbound

adjective
not bound (esp. book or manuscript)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbaʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a book or manuscript) not secured within a cover; not bound.

Examples

Unbound pages
Collectors sometimes prefer unbound editions for custom binding.
She kept an unbound copy of her thesis for easier editing.
The manuscript was delivered as an unbound stack of pages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bound - (of a book) secured within a cover
