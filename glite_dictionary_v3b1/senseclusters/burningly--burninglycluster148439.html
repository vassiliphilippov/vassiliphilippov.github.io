burningly

adverb
intensely; with passion
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is very intense or full of strong emotion or sensation, like burning.

Examples

Her eyes shone burningly with excitement as she shared her news.
The debate continued burningly late into the night, with neither side willing to give up.
