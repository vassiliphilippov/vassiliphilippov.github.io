raucous

adjective
loud, harsh, and noisy

Examples

A raucous concert

  • jarring

  • strident
    7.2 2.13/m

  • cacophonous
    8.4 0.27/m

  • rackety
    7.4 0.02/m
    rackety is noisy and wild, often from parties or machines, while raucous highlights harsh, loud sound or a rough atmosphere.
  • harsh
    3.9 4.64/m

  • unmelodious
