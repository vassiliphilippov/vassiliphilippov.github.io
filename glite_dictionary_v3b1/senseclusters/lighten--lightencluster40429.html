lighten

verb
make more cheerful or bright with color
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtən / (BrE)
to make or become brighter or lighter in color or illumination

Examples

To lighten a room
A colorful rug will lighten the mood of your living room instantly.
Choosing white curtains can lighten a dark hallway and make it feel more spacious.
They repainted the office in yellow to lighten the atmosphere for the staff.
As the clouds parted, the sky began to lighten above the hills.
Over time, his hair will naturally lighten in the sun.
The painter chose a color that would lighten the dark hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brighten - make or become more bright or cheerful
  • antonym: darken - to become darker or less bright
