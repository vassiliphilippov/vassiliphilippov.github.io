Northumbrian

noun
person from Northumbria
8.4
Pronunciation: / nɔrˈθʌmbriən / (AmE), / nɔːˈθʌmbriən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Northumbria, a historic region in northern England

Examples

A Northumbrian person
The proud Northumbrian shared stories about the region's ancient castles and landscapes.
As a Northumbrian, she spoke with a distinctive northern accent.
Many Northumbrians celebrate their unique cultural heritage every year at local festivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geordie - a person from the Tyneside area of northeast England
  • antonym: southerner - a person from the south of England
