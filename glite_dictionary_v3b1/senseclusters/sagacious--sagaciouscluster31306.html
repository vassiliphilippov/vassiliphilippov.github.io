sagacious

adjective
wise; having keen judgment
6.6
Pronunciation: / səˈɡeɪʃəs / (AmE), / səˈɡeɪʃəs / (BrE)
having or showing keen mental discernment and good judgment; wise or shrewd

Examples

Her sagacious advice helped the company avoid a costly mistake.
