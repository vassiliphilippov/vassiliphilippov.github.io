kabala

noun
esoteric mystical tradition
8.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːlə / (BrE)
an esoteric mystical tradition or occult matter, especially one resembling the Kabbalah and often kept secret

Examples

Study of kabala texts
He devoted years to the study of ancient texts associated with kabala.
Some believe the teachings of kabala hold the key to spiritual enlightenment.
The secret society was rumored to practice a form of kabala in their ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kabbalah - the Jewish mystical and esoteric tradition
