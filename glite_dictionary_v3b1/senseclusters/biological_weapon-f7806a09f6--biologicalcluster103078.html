biological weapon

noun
weapon that uses living organisms to harm
4.7 4.6
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl ˈwep.ən/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.ˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl ˈwep.ən/ (BrE)
A weapon that uses bacteria, viruses, or other organisms to harm or kill people, animals, or plants in warfare.
