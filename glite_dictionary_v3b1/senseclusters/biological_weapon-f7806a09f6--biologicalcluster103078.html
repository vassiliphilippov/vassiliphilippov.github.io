biological weapon

noun
weapon that uses living organisms to harm
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈwɛpən / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈwɛpən / (BrE)
A weapon that uses bacteria, viruses, or other organisms to harm or kill people, animals, or plants in warfare.

Examples

Biological weapon use
Some countries have signed treaties banning the use of biological weapons.
The threat of biological weapons has made biodefense a high priority for many nations.
There was international outrage after reports of biological weapons being developed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical weapon - weapon that uses chemicals to cause harm
  • synonym: nuclear weapon - weapon that uses nuclear reactions to cause destruction
