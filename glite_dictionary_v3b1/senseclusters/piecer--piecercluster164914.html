piecer

noun
quilt maker
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːsər / (AmE), / ˈpiːsə / (BrE)
a person who joins pieces of fabric together, especially to make quilts or patchwork items

Examples

Quilt piecer
The skilled piecer carefully matched the colors and patterns of each fabric square.
As a dedicated piecer, she spent hours assembling intricate patchwork designs for her quilts.
Every piecer in the group brought their own unique style to the community project.
