disinflationary

adjective
reducing inflation rate
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to or causing a reduction in the rate at which prices increase

Examples

Disinflationary measures
The government introduced disinflationary measures to slow down the rapid rise in consumer prices.
Analysts expect a disinflationary trend to continue as demand weakens in the economy.
A disinflationary environment can help stabilize the cost of living for many households.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: deflationary - causing or relating to a decrease in the general price level
  • antonym: inflationary - causing or relating to an increase in prices
