Brigid

proper noun
Celtic goddess of fire and wisdom
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪdʒɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdɪd / (BrE)
In Celtic mythology, the goddess of fire, fertility, agriculture, household arts, and wisdom, later identified with Saint Bridget.

Examples

Celtic goddess Brigid
Brigid is often depicted holding a flame, symbolizing her association with fire and inspiration.
Many traditions during Imbolc are dedicated to Brigid and the coming of spring.
The ancient Celts prayed to Brigid for a good harvest and healthy livestock.
