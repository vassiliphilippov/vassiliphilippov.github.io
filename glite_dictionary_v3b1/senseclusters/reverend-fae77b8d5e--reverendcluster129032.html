Reverend

noun
title for a Christian clergyman
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvərənd / (AmE), / ˈrɛvərənd / (BrE)
a title of respect used before the name of a member of the Christian clergy

Examples

The Reverend Smith
Reverend Smith will be leading the Sunday service this week.
She invited Reverend Brown to speak at the community event.
The congregation welcomed Reverend Thomas as their new pastor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pastor - Christian minister or priest in charge of a church
  • synonym: Priest - ordained minister of the Catholic, Orthodox, or Anglican Church
