water flea

noun
tiny freshwater crustacean
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌfli /; / ˈwɔtər ˌfli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌfliː /; / ˈwɔːtə ˌfli / (BrE)
