swail

noun
shallow marshy channel
9.9
Pronunciation: /sweɪl/ /; / /swaɪl/ (AmE), /sweɪl/ /; / /swaɪl/ (BrE)
a low, marshy area or shallow channel that temporarily carries water, especially after rain.

Examples

Children splashed in the shallow water that filled the swail every spring.
