booze

noun
informal: alcoholic drink
Pronunciation: / buz / (AmE), / buːz / (BrE)
Strong alcoholic drink, especially distilled spirits; alcohol, especially in informal speech.

Examples

A bottle of booze
He hid a bottle of booze in his desk drawer at work.
She decided to give up booze after her health scare.
They served plenty of booze at the New Year's Eve party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drink - An alcoholic beverage
  • synonym: liquor - Strong alcoholic drink; distilled spirit
  • antonym: soft drink - A drink without alcohol
