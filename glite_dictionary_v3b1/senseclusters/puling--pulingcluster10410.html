puling

noun
weak complainer
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpjuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who is weak, cowardly, or constantly complains, often in a whiny manner (derogatory)

Examples

She was tired of being called a puling just because she expressed her worries.
