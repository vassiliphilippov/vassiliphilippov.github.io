anticonscription

noun
opposition to the draft
8.6
Pronunciation: /æn.ti.kən.ˈskrɪp.ʃən/ (AmE), /æn.ti.kən.ˈskrɪp.ʃən/ (BrE)
opposition to compulsory military service, or the movement against requiring people to join the armed forces by law

Examples

Many students joined anticonscription rallies to protest the new draft law.
Public debates about anticonscription highlighted concerns over personal freedom and government authority.
