Wilms tumour

noun
malignant kidney tumor in children
6.7
Pronunciation: / wɪlmz ˈtumɚ / (AmE), / wɪlmz ˈtjuːmə / (BrE)
a malignant tumor of the kidney occurring mainly in young children, often presenting with abdominal mass and sometimes blood in the urine

Examples

Wilms tumour in children
Doctors monitor for relapse in children who have recovered from a Wilms tumour.
The child was diagnosed with a Wilms tumour after presenting with abdominal swelling.
Treatment of Wilms tumour usually involves surgery and chemotherapy.
