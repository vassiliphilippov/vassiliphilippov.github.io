agree to disagree

verb
accept differing opinions without conflict
Pronunciation: / əˈɡri tə dɪˈsɡri / (AmE), / əˈɡriː tʊ dɪsˈɡriː / (BrE)
to accept that people have different opinions and decide not to argue about them

Examples

To agree to disagree peacefully
After hours of debate, they decided to agree to disagree and move on to another topic.
Let's agree to disagree about politics so our dinner can stay peaceful.
Sometimes it's healthier for friends to agree to disagree than to continue arguing.
