marble cake

noun
cake with marbled light and dark batter
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrbəl keɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.bəl keɪk / (BrE)
A cake made by lightly blending light and dark batters to create a marbled appearance.

Examples

A marble cake slice
She brought a delicious marble cake to the party, impressing all the guests.
The bakery is famous for its moist and flavorful marble cake.
Would you like a slice of marble cake with your tea?
