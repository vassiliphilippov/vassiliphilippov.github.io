dyer

noun
person who dyes cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to dye cloth, thread, etc.

Examples

The dyer colors cloth
In medieval times, the dyer was an important member of the textile guild.
She apprenticed with a skilled dyer to learn traditional methods of coloring cloth.
The dyer prepared vibrant colors for the fabric in his workshop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes, repairs, or alters clothing
  • synonym: weaver - a person who weaves cloth
