Darwinist

adjective
supporting Darwin's theories
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːr.wɪ.nɪst/ (AmE), /ˈdɑː.wɪ.nɪst/ (BrE)
relating to or supporting the theories of Charles Darwin, especially the theory of evolution by natural selection

Examples

Darwinist theory
The scientist presented a Darwinist explanation for the diversity of species found on the island.
Her Darwinist perspective influenced the way she taught biology to her students.
Some critics argue against the Darwinist view of human origins in public debates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creationist - believing that life was created by a divine being
  • synonym: evolutionary - relating to the process of evolution
