brownish yellow

noun
muted yellow with brown tinge
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a yellow color that is tinged with brown, resulting in a muted or earthy shade

Examples

A brownish yellow shade
Her eyes had a unique brownish yellow shade that caught everyone’s attention.
The autumn leaves turned a brownish yellow before finally falling from the trees.
The walls were painted a subtle brownish yellow to create a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: brown - a dark color produced by mixing red, yellow, and black
  • degree lower: yellow - a bright color like that of lemons or gold
