co-administration

noun
joint management
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the joint management or operation of an organization, service, or function by two or more parties

Examples

Co-administration of a company
The two companies agreed on co-administration of the new joint venture to ensure equal input.
Effective co-administration requires clear communication between all participating organizations.
During the transition period, the hospital operated under a co-administration model shared by both boards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others
  • synonym: partnership - a relationship where two or more parties work together
  • antonym: sole administration - management by a single person or entity
