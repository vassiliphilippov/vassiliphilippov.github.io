handball

noun
game where players hit a ball with hands against a wall
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhandˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game played in a walled court or against a single wall where players strike a small rubber ball with their hands

Examples

Handball court game

In handball, quick reflexes and agility are essential to win.
We watched a fast-paced handball match at the local gym.
