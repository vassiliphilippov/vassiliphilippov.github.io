cheerily

adverb
in a cheerful or happy manner
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪərɪli / (BrE)
In a cheerful, happy, or good-humoured manner.

Examples

To smile cheerily
Despite the rain, he waved cheerily from the porch.
She greeted her new colleagues cheerily on her first day at the office.
The children sang cheerily as they walked to school in the sunshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful or glad way
  • antonym: gloomily - in a sad or depressed manner
