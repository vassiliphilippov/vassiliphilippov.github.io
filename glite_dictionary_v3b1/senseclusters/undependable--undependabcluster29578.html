undependable

adjective
not reliable; untrustworthy
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈpɛn.də.bəl /; / ˌʌndɪˈpɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈpen.də.bəl /; / ˌʌndɪˈpɛndəbl / (BrE)
not reliable or trustworthy; likely to fail, disappoint, or be wrong

Examples

An undependable friend
After several missed deadlines, his behavior became increasingly undependable.
She found his excuses undependable and stopped relying on him for help.
They replaced the old, undependable engine with a newer model.
Due to his undependable memory, he often forgot important details.
Many critics find the study's conclusions to be undependable.
The information provided by that website is notoriously undependable.

  • synonym: unreliable - not able to be relied upon
