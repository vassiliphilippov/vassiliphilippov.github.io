sez

verb
informal 'says'
Pronunciation: / sɛz / (AmE), / sɛz / (BrE)
an informal or humorous spelling of 'says', used to imitate the way some people pronounce it in speech

Examples

He sez jokingly
"He sez he will be here soon," she joked with a grin.
Whenever he tells a story, he always sez it in a funny accent.
The comic strip character often sez things in a playful way.
