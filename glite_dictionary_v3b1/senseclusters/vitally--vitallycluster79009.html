vitally

adverb
to a vital or essential degree
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪtəli / (AmE), / ˈvaɪtəli / (BrE)
to an extremely important or necessary degree; in a way that is crucial or essential.

Examples

Is vitally important
Good nutrition is vitally needed for a healthy lifestyle.
It is vitally important that you attend the meeting tomorrow.
The information provided was vitally relevant to our research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crucially - in a way that is extremely important
  • synonym: essentially - to an essential or necessary degree
