sensitive

adjective
likely to upset; flagged content
4.1
Pronunciation: / c8s5bns59t6av / (AmE), / c8s5bns59t6av / (BrE)
Likely to cause distress, offense, or strong emotional reaction; requiring caution or a warning before viewing, often used for online images, videos, or posts that platforms may flag or hide.

Examples

Sensitive content warning
The platform flags some images as sensitive to warn users about distressing content.
Journalists avoided the sensitive topic in the report to prevent upsetting readers.
You must confirm you want to view the sensitive post because it may contain graphic images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicate - requiring careful handling because it could upset or offend
  • synonym: disturbing - causing worry, upset, or emotional discomfort
