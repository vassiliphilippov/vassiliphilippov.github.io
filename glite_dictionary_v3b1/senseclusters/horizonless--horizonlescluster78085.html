horizonless

adjective
without a visible horizon
Pronunciation: / həˈraɪzənləs / (AmE), / həˈraɪzənləs / (BrE)
having no visible or defined horizon; appearing limitless or boundless

Examples

Horizonless ocean view
The ocean seemed horizonless as it stretched endlessly into the distance.
They gazed across the horizonless plain, feeling both awe and insignificance.
A horizonless sky made the landscape feel vast and mysterious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundless - without limits or boundaries
  • synonym: infinite - having no end or limit
  • antonym: limited - having boundaries or restrictions
