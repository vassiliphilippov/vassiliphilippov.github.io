Armed Forces Day

noun
US holiday to honor the armed forces
Pronunciation: / ɑrmd ˈfɔrsɪz deɪ / (AmE), / ɑːmd ˈfɔːsɪz deɪ / (BrE)
an annual holiday in the United States observed on the third Saturday in May to honor members of the armed forces

Examples

Armed Forces Day events
He volunteers at the air force base during Armed Forces Day events each year.
On Armed Forces Day, many families attend ceremonies at the veterans' memorial.
The local city council holds a parade every Armed Forces Day to celebrate military personnel.
