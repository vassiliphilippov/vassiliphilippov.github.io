unfooted

adjective
without metrical feet
9.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfʊt.ɪd/ /; / /ən.ˈfʊt.ɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfʊt.ɪd/ /; / /ən.ˈfʊt.ɪd/ (BrE)
