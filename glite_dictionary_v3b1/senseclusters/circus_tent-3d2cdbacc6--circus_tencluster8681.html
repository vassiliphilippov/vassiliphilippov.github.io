Synonyms

  • big top
    3.5 0.15/m
    big top is the common name for the main circus tent used for performances and audiences, while circus tent is the general term.
  • top
    5.3 1.10/m
    top is a shorter, informal name for the big circus tent used for shows, while circus tent is the full standard name.
  • round top
    8.1 0.00/m
    round top is an older canvas tent mainly for the audience at shows, while circus tent covers performers, performances, and audiences.
