sickling

verb
to cut with a sickle
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪk.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈsɪk.əl.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsɪk.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈsɪk.əl.ɪŋ/ (BrE)

Examples

To sickle the grass

Farmers were sickling the wheat before the rain started.
The workers took turns sickling the overgrown weeds by the riverbank.
