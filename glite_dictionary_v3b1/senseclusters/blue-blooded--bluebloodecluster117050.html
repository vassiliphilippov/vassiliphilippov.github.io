blue-blooded

adjective
of noble or aristocratic descent
Pronunciation: / ˌbluˈblʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˌbluːˈblʌdɪd / (BrE)
Belonging to or characteristic of the nobility or aristocracy.

Examples

A blue-blooded noble
As a blue-blooded member of the royal family, she was accustomed to a life of privilege.
Many believed his blue-blooded background made him a natural leader in society.
Though she was not blue-blooded, her manners impressed even the aristocrats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aristocratic - belonging to the aristocracy; noble
  • antonym: common - not belonging to the nobility; of ordinary people
  • synonym: patrician - of or belonging to the upper class or nobility
