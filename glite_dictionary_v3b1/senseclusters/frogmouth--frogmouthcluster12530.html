frogmouth

noun
nocturnal bird with a wide mouth
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔɡˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
A nocturnal insect-eating bird of Australia and Southeast Asia with a broad, flat mouth resembling that of a frog.

Examples

A frogmouth bird
A frogmouth hunts insects at night using its wide beak.
Several species of frogmouth are found in the forests of Southeast Asia.
The frogmouth blends perfectly with tree bark during the day.
