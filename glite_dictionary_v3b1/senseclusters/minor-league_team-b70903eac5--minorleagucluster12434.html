minor-league team

noun
sports team in a minor league
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnɚ ˌliːɡ ˈtiːm / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə ˌliːɡ ˈtiːm / (BrE)
a team that competes in a minor league, which is a lower-level professional or semi-professional sports league below the major leagues.

Examples

A minor-league team player
After being drafted, he played for a minor-league team to gain more experience.
Many athletes start their careers on a minor-league team before moving to the big leagues.
The city takes great pride in its local minor-league team.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: major-league team - a team that plays in a major league
  • degree lower: minor league - a sports league below the top professional level
