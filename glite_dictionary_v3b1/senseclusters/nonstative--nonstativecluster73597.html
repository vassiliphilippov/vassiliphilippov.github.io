nonstative

adjective
action verb type

Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsteɪtɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈsteɪtɪv / (BrE)
describing a verb that expresses an action or process rather than a state or condition

Examples

Nonstative verb example
In the sentence 'She runs every morning,' 'runs' is a nonstative verb.
Most action verbs in English are considered nonstative.
Teachers often explain the difference between stative and nonstative verbs to language learners.
