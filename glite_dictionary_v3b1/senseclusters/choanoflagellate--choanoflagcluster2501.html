choanoflagellate

adjective
of choanoflagellates
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˌænəˈflædʒəˌleɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˌanəˈflædʒɪlət / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of choanoflagellates, which are microscopic single-celled organisms with a collar of flagella.
