Azilian

adjective
late Paleolithic culture
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈzɪliən / (AmE), / əˈzɪliən / (BrE)
relating to a late Paleolithic culture in western Europe known for its distinctive artifacts and cave art

Examples

Azilian cave art
Archaeologists discovered Azilian tools and painted pebbles in the ancient cave.
The Azilian period is marked by a shift in artistic expression and toolmaking.
Researchers studied Azilian remains to learn more about prehistoric European societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Epipaleolithic - relating to the final phase of the Paleolithic period
  • antonym: Magdalenian - relating to an earlier Upper Paleolithic culture in western Europe
