basso relievo

noun
sculpture with slight projection from the background
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːsoʊ rɛˈljɛvoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbæsəʊ rɛˈljɛvəʊ / (BrE)
A sculptural technique in which the figures are only slightly raised from a flat background, with no undercutting.

Examples

Basso relievo sculpture
In art class, we studied how basso relievo differs from higher forms of relief sculpture.
She enjoyed creating basso relievo panels because of the subtlety they offered.
The museum's ancient frieze features a beautiful example of basso relievo depicting a parade of horses.
