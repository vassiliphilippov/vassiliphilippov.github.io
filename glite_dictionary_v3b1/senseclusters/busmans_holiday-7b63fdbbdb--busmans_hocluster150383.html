busman's holiday

noun
holiday spent doing usual work
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌs.mənz ˈhɑː.lə.deɪ/ /; / /ˈbʌs.mənz ˈhɑː.lɪ.deɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbʌs.mənz ˈhɒl.ɪ.deɪ/ /; / /ˈbʌs.mænz ˈhɒl.ɪ.deɪ/ (BrE)
A holiday or period of leisure in which someone spends their time doing the same kind of work activity as they do in their normal job.

Examples

A busman's holiday period
Even on his busman's holiday, the chef enjoyed cooking elaborate meals at home.
It was a real busman's holiday when the teacher spent her vacation volunteering at a summer camp.
She spent her busman's holiday organizing books for a local library.
