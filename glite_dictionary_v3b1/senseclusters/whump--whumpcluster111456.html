whump

interjection
sound of dull impact
9.9
Pronunciation: /wʌmp/ (AmE), /wʌmp/ (BrE)
an exclamation used to represent a sudden dull impact, thud, or heavy fall

Examples

Whump! A heavy fall
The box hit the floor with a loud whump that startled everyone in the room.
As the snow slid off the roof, it landed with a heavy whump on the ground below.
He dropped his backpack onto the couch, making a soft whump as it landed.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from an impact
  • degree lower: tap - a light touch or sound
  • synonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
