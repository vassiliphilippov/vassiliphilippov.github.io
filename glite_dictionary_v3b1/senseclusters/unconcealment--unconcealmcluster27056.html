unconcealment

noun
making visible
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsiːlmənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsiːlmənt / (BrE)
the act or state of making something no longer hidden; the process or result of revealing something

Examples

The unconcealment of documents
The unconcealment of the documents led to a major political scandal.
His sudden unconcealment of the secret shocked his friends.
The artist's work is about the unconcealment of hidden emotions.
The unconcealment of the ancient ruins attracted historians from around the world.
After years of secrecy, the unconcealment of the documents changed public opinion.
The artist's intention was to create a sense of unconcealment in her latest exhibition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concealment - the act of hiding or keeping something secret
  • synonym: disclosure - the act of making something known or public
  • synonym: exposure - the state of being uncovered or made visible
  • synonym: revelation - the act of revealing or making known something hidden
