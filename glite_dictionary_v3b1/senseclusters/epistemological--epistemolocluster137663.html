epistemological

adjective
relating to the theory of knowledge
Pronunciation: / ɪˌpɪstəməˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˌpɪstɪməˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the theory of knowledge, especially with regard to its methods, validity, and scope.

Examples

Epistemological theory of knowledge
Her research is grounded in epistemological analysis of scientific reasoning.
The philosopher raised several epistemological questions about the nature of truth.
This debate touches on deep epistemological issues within the field of psychology.
