antispyware

adjective
protecting against spyware
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈspaɪˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈspaɪˌweə / (BrE)
designed to prevent, detect, or remove spyware from computers or devices

Examples

Antispyware protection software
You should install an antispyware program to keep your computer safe from unwanted tracking.
Many antispyware tools update automatically to protect against new threats.
The company recommends using antispyware software alongside your regular antivirus protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antivirus - designed to protect against computer viruses
  • antonym: spyware - software that secretly collects user information
