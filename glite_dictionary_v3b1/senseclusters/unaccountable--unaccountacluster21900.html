Examples

Unaccountable reason
For some unaccountable reason, the radio stopped working overnight.
She felt an unaccountable sense of sadness that she couldn't describe.

Synonyms

  • inexplicable
    7.8 3.27/m
    inexplicable often describes mysterious events with no clear cause, while unaccountable sounds more formal and stresses lacking any explanation.
  • unexplainable
    7.0 0.69/m
    unexplainable feels plain and is used for things people cannot explain, while unaccountable sounds more formal and is less common.
  • inscrutable
    8.9 1.40/m
    inscrutable suggests something or someone seems mysterious and hard to read, while unaccountable means it cannot be explained.
  • incomprehensible
    8.8 4.12/m
    incomprehensible describes language or ideas that are too hard to understand, while unaccountable means they cannot be explained or given a cause.
