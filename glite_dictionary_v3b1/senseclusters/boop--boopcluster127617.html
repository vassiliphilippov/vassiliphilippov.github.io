boop

interjection
playful exclamation
Pronunciation: / buːp / (AmE), / buːp / (BrE)
an exclamation used playfully when lightly touching or tapping someone, often on the nose or face

Examples

Boop! on the nose
She giggled and said boop as she gently tapped his nose.
Whenever he walks by, his friend greets him with a cheerful boop and a smile.
The puppy touched my finger with its nose, and I couldn't help but say boop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bop - a light, playful hit or tap
