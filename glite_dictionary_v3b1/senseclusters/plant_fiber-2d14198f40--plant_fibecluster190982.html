plant fiber

noun
fiber material from plants
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / plænt ˈfaɪbɚ / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈfaɪbə / (BrE)
a natural material consisting of fibers derived from plants, used in textiles, ropes, and also as dietary fiber.
