United Mexican States

proper noun
official name for the country of Mexico
Pronunciation: / juˈnaɪtɪd ˈmɛksɪkən steɪts / (AmE), / juˈnaɪtɪd ˈmɛksɪkən steɪts / (BrE)
the official name of Mexico, a republic in southern North America that gained independence from Spain in 1810

Examples

The United Mexican States country

International treaties sometimes refer to Mexico by its official name, the United Mexican States.
The constitution of the United Mexican States was adopted in 1917 and remains in effect today.
