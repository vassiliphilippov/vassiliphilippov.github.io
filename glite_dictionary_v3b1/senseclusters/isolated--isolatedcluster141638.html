isolated

adjective
rare or separate in time
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.səˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.səˌleɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Occurring rarely or separately, not close together in time or frequency.

Examples

Isolated incidents
There have been only isolated incidents of theft in the area during the past year.
These errors are not typical and appear to be isolated cases.
We received isolated reports of power outages after the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frequent - happening often and regularly
  • synonym: occasional - happening from time to time
