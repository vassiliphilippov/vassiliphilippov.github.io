unsympathetic

adjective
not open-minded or receptive
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
not willing to consider or accept new ideas or perspectives; not open-minded or receptive

Examples

Unsympathetic to ideas
He remained unsympathetic to arguments that challenged his beliefs.
Her unsympathetic attitude made it difficult to discuss new approaches.
The committee was unsympathetic to proposals for major change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflexible - unwilling to change or compromise
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas; unprejudiced
  • antonym: receptive - willing to listen to or accept new ideas and suggestions
