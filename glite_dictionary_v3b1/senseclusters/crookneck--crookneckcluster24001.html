crookneck

noun
type of yellow squash with curved neck
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊkˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈkrʊk.nɛk / (BrE)
A variety of yellow squash with a thin, curved neck and slightly warty skin, commonly grown as a vegetable.

Examples

Yellow crookneck squash
Many recipes call for crookneck squash because of its tender flesh and mild flavor.
The farmer's market had an abundance of fresh crookneck available last week.
We grew several crookneck squashes in our backyard garden this summer.
