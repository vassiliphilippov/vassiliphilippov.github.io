bill

noun
a bird's projecting mouthpart
Pronunciation: / bɪl / (AmE), / bɪl / (BrE)
the hard, projecting mouthpart of a bird, used for eating, grooming, and other functions

Examples

A bird's bill
Many birds use their bill to build nests by carrying twigs and grass.
The pelican caught a fish in its large bill and swallowed it whole.
The woodpecker has a strong bill for pecking at tree bark.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beak - the hard, pointed mouthpart of a bird
