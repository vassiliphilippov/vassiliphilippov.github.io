Descurainia pinnata

noun
a North American herb with bitter leaves
Pronunciation: / dɛs.kjʊˈreɪ.ni.ə pɪˈneɪ.tə / (AmE), / dɛs.kjʊˈreɪ.ni.ə pɪˈneɪ.tə / (BrE)
