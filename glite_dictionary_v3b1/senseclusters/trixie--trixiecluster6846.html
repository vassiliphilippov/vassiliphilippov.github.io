trixie

noun
slang insult for women
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪksi / (AmE), / ˈtrɪksi / (BrE)
a derogatory term for a young, fashionable woman who is considered shallow or overly concerned with material things

Examples

A trixie girl
Some people use trixie to insult women they see as superficial or obsessed with fashion.
Calling someone a trixie is considered rude and disrespectful.
The term trixie is often used in a negative way to stereotype young women.
