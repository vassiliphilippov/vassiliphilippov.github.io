harmonizer

noun
musician who creates musical harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːməniˌzaɪə /; / ˈhɑːmənaɪzə / (BrE)
a musician who sings or plays musical parts that create harmony with others
