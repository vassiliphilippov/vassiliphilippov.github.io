gun

noun
large, mobile artillery weapon
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn / (AmE), / ɡʌn / (BrE)
a weapon that shoots bullets or shells at high speed, such as a pistol, rifle, or cannon

Examples

A gun fires bullets
During the battle, the artillery gun was moved forward by a team of horses.
The museum displays a historical field gun used in the nineteenth century.
The soldiers positioned the gun on the hillside to cover the valley below.
She learned how to safely use a gun at the shooting range.
The antique gun on display dates back to the 18th century.
The police found a loaded gun under the suspect's jacket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cannon - a large, heavy gun typically mounted on wheels, used in warfare
  • synonym: firearm - a portable gun, such as a rifle or pistol
  • degree lower: pistol - small, handheld firearm
  • degree lower: rifle - a long-barreled firearm designed to be fired from the shoulder
