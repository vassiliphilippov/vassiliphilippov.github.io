approach

noun
becoming nearer in time
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
the act or process of something becoming nearer in time or occurrence

Examples

The approach of winter
Everyone was anxious about the approach of the deadline.
The approach of spring brought new life to the garden.
With the approach of dusk, the air grew noticeably cooler.

  • synonym: arrival - the act of reaching a place or moment
