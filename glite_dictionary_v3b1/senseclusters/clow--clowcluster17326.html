clow

noun
ditch or drain
9.9
an old term for a ditch or open drain, especially one for carrying rainwater

Examples

A roadside clow
The rainwater flowed down the clow beside the road.
Farmers would clear the clow each spring to prevent flooding in the fields.
Children played near the old clow that ran behind the village houses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ditch - a long narrow channel dug in the ground for drainage
  • synonym: gutter - a shallow channel at the side of a road for carrying off rainwater
