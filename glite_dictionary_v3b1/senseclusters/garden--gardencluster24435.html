garden

noun
personal online notes collection
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑːr.dən/ (AmE), /ˈgɑː.dən/ (BrE)
a personal, publicly accessible online collection of notes, ideas, or drafts that is curated and allowed to grow and evolve over time (often called a 'digital garden').

Examples

A digital garden
She maintains a garden of short essays on her website to develop research ideas over time.
When applying for the fellowship, he linked to his garden so reviewers could read related project notes.
After years of note-taking, her garden now contains hundreds of interlinked observations and drafts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital garden - a curated, interlinked personal website of notes and ideas; essentially synonymous with this sense.
  • synonym: personal knowledge base - a collection of notes, documents, or resources organized by an individual for personal use and development.
