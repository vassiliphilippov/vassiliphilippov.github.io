scraping

noun
automated extraction of website data
Pronunciation: / ˈskreɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskreɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of automatically extracting data from websites or online sources, typically by using scripts or tools to collect and aggregate information for analysis or reuse.

Examples

Web scraping
The research team relied on scraping to gather prices and customer reviews from many online stores.
Many startups use scraping to build large datasets for training machine-learning models and market analysis.
Journalists used scraping to compile public records from government sites for an investigative report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data extraction - the process of retrieving data from various sources for processing or analysis
  • synonym: web scraping - the automated technique of extracting information from websites, often using specialized software or scripts
