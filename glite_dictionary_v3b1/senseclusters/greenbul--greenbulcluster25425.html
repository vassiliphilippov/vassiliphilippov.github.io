greenbul

noun
African songbird
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnbʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnbʊl / (BrE)
a type of African songbird with greenish feathers, belonging to the bulbul family

Examples

A greenbul bird
We spotted a greenbul perched quietly among the dense leaves.
The distinctive call of the greenbul echoed through the African rainforest.
Birdwatchers were excited to see a rare greenbul during their morning walk.
