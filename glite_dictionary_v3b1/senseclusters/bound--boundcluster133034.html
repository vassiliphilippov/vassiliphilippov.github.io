bound

noun
a light, springing movement
5.1
Pronunciation: / baʊnd / (AmE), / baʊnd / (BrE)
a light, springing movement upwards or forwards

Examples

A quick bound
The child crossed the puddle in one energetic bound.
The deer took a bound over the fallen log and disappeared into the trees.
With a single bound, the gymnast landed perfectly on the mat.
