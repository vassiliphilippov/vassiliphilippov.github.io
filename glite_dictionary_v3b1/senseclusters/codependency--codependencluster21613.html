codependency

noun
unhealthy mutual dependence
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi / (AmE), / ˌkəʊdɪˈpɛndənsi / (BrE)
a relationship where one or both people are overly dependent on each other in unhealthy ways, often enabling harmful behavior
