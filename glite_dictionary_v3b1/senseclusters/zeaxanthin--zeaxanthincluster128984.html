zeaxanthin

noun
a yellow plant pigment, carotenoid
9.7 8.6
Pronunciation: /ziː.ə.ˈzæn.θɪn/ (AmE), /ziː.ə.ˈzæn.θɪn/ (BrE)
a yellow carotenoid pigment found in many plants, especially corn, that is isomeric with lutein and important in human nutrition and vision

Examples

Yellow zeaxanthin pigment
Leafy greens are a rich source of both lutein and zeaxanthin.
Many nutritional supplements contain zeaxanthin to support eye health.
Scientists studied the concentration of zeaxanthin in different varieties of corn.
