Blackburnian warbler

noun
a North American warbler with orange-and-black head
Pronunciation: / ˌblækˈbɝːniən ˈwɔːrblər /; / ˌblækˈbɜːrniən ˈwɔːrblɚ / (AmE), / ˌblækˈbɜːniən ˈwɔːblə / (BrE)
