precious stone

noun
valuable gemstone used in jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃəs stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs stəʊn / (BrE)
a rare and valuable gemstone, such as a diamond, ruby, sapphire, or emerald, often used in jewelry

Examples

The necklace was set with a single large precious stone that sparkled in the sunlight.
The jeweler inspected the precious stone carefully before appraising its worth.
The necklace was set with a dazzling precious stone at its center.
Throughout history, rulers have coveted rare precious stones for their beauty and value.
