buckhorn

noun
deer horn or plant
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌhɔːn / (BrE)
the horn of a male deer, or a plant whose leaves resemble such horns

Examples

A buckhorn plant
The knife had a handle made from polished buckhorn.
We spotted a rare buckhorn plant growing near the edge of the forest.
Collectors often seek out old tools with buckhorn grips for their unique appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antler - the branched horn of a deer or similar animal
