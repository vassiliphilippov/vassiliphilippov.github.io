image

noun
figurative language that evokes a mental picture
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a mental picture or a word/phrase that creates a picture in your mind, especially in literature

Examples

A mental image
Shakespeare skillfully creates a vivid image of the night in this passage.
The poem uses the image of a storm to express emotional turmoil.
Writers often use image and symbolism to deepen meaning in their works.
She closed her eyes and let the image of the ocean calm her.
Sometimes a troubling image from the past would appear unexpectedly.
The novel vividly evoked powerful images in the reader's mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagery - the use of vivid or figurative language to represent objects, actions, or ideas
  • synonym: imagination - the ability of the mind to create images and ideas
  • synonym: visualization - the process of forming a mental image
