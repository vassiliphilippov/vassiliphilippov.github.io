leaf blade

noun
broad, flat part of a leaf
2.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /liːf bleɪd/ (AmE), /liːf bleɪd/ (BrE)
the broad, flat part of a leaf, distinct from the stalk or petiole, especially in grasses and many other plants
