undepressed

adjective
not sad or hopeless
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈprɛst / (BrE)
not feeling sad, hopeless, or affected by depression

Examples

Undepressed mood

He remained undepressed despite the many challenges he faced at work.
The support from friends kept him undepressed during difficult times.
