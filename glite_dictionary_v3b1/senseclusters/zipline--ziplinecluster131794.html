zipline

noun
cable ride activity
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪpˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈzɪpˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a strong cable or rope stretched between two points, used for sliding down with a harness or pulley, usually for fun or transport
