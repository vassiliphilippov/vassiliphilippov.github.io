land tax

noun
tax on assessed value of land
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlænd ˌtæks / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˌtæks / (BrE)
a tax imposed on real property, based on the assessed value of the land, usually levied by local governments or municipalities

Examples

Land tax payment
He consulted a financial advisor about how land tax might affect his investment property.
Many homeowners were surprised by the rise in their annual land tax bills.
The local government increased the land tax to fund public infrastructure upgrades.
