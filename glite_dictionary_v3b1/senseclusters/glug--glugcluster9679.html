glug

noun
gurgling liquid sound
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌɡ / (AmE), / ɡlʌɡ / (BrE)
a gurgling or gulping sound made when liquid is poured from a bottle or container

Examples

A glug of water
The glug of wine being poured into the glass was loud in the quiet room.
She heard the familiar glug as he emptied the last of the juice from the carton.
With each glug, the bottle became lighter and the sound grew softer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gurgle - a bubbling or rippling sound made by liquid
  • synonym: splash - a sound made when something hits liquid
