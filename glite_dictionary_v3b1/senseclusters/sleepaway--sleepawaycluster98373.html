sleepaway

adjective
overnight camp
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsliːpəˌweɪ / (BrE)
describing a camp or program where participants stay overnight away from home

Examples

Sleepaway camp
Many children look forward to their first sleepaway camp experience during the summer.
Parents often prepare a packing list for their kids attending a sleepaway program.
She made lifelong friends at a sleepaway camp in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: day camp - a camp where participants return home each day
  • synonym: overnight - lasting or staying for one or more nights
