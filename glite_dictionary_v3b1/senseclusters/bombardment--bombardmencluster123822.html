bombardment

noun
continuous stream of communication
6.1 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / b…ëmÀàb…ërdm…ônt / (AmE), / b…ímÀàb…ëÀêdm…ônt / (BrE)
a rapid and continuous stream of spoken or written communication, such as questions, messages, or criticisms

Examples

Bombardment of questions
After the press conference began, the politician faced a bombardment of questions from reporters.
She was unprepared for the bombardment of criticism she received on social media.
The customer service team received a bombardment of emails regarding the product recall.

Synonyms

  • barrage
    6.8 2.99/m
    a barrage is a sudden heavy stream of questions or criticism, while a bombardment implies steady, repeated communication over time.
  • onslaught
    7.9 0.95/m
    an onslaught is a strong, often violent flood of attacks or complaints, while a bombardment usually means repeated messages or questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrage - a rapid outpouring of something, especially words or questions
