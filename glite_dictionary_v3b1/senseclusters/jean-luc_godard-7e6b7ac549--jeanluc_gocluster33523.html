Jean-Luc Godard

proper noun
French director; French New Wave innovator
Pronunciation: / ʒɑ̃ lyk ɡoʊˈdɑr /; / ʒɑn luk ɡoʊˈdɑr / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ lyk ɡəʊˈdɑː /; / ʒɑ̃ lyk ɡɔˈdaʁ / (BrE)
a French film director and screenwriter (1930–2022), known for his innovative style and as a pioneer of the French New Wave cinema movement
