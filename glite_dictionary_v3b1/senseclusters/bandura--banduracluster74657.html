bandura

noun
Ukrainian string instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / bænˈdʊrə / (AmE), / bænˈdjʊərə / (BrE)
a traditional Ukrainian stringed musical instrument with a rounded body and many strings, similar to a lute

Examples

A bandura instrument
The musician captivated the audience with a beautiful melody on her bandura.
He learned to play the bandura from his grandfather, who was a folk artist.
At the cultural fair, visitors could try strumming a traditional bandura.
