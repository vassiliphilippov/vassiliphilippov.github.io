registration

noun
the act of enrolling
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdʒəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of enrolling or signing up formally for something, such as a course, event, or organization

Examples

Registration for classes
Registration for the summer program begins next week and spaces fill up quickly.
Students completed their registration online before the start of the semester.
The conference registration includes access to all workshops and keynote sessions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enrollment - the act of signing up or enrolling in a course or organization
  • antonym: withdrawal - the act of removing oneself from enrollment or participation
