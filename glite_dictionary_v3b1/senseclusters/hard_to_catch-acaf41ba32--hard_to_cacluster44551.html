hard to catch

adjective
difficult to apprehend or defeat
Pronunciation: / hɑrd tə kætʃ / (AmE), / hɑːd tə kætʃ / (BrE)
difficult to apprehend, defeat, or outsmart; not easily caught or overcome.

Examples

Hard to catch cat
Her elusive cat is hard to catch whenever it escapes into the garden.
The answer to this tricky riddle is hard to catch for most people.
The suspect is hard to catch because he frequently changes his appearance and location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elusive - difficult to find, catch, or achieve
