addict

noun
person dependent on substance or activity
2.6 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.dɪkt/ /; / /ə.ˈdɪkt/ (AmE), /ˈæ.dɪkt/ /; / /ə.ˈdɪkt/ (BrE)
a person who is unable to stop using a substance or engaging in an activity, especially one that is harmful, due to a physical or psychological dependence

Examples

A drug addict
After years of substance abuse, he finally admitted he was an addict.
Many former addicts credit support groups with helping them recover.
She became an addict after experimenting with prescription painkillers.
