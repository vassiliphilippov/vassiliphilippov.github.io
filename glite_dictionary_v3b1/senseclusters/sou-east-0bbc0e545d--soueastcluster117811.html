sou'-east

noun
compass direction between south and east
8.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / sa äÀàist / (AmE), / sa äÀàiÀêst / (BrE)
the compass point that lies midway between south and east, at an angle of 135 degrees from north.

Examples

Sou'-east wind
The storm was approaching from the sou'-east, bringing heavy rains.
The wind shifted suddenly from sou'-east to due south overnight.
We sailed towards the sou'-east, keeping the rising sun on our left.
