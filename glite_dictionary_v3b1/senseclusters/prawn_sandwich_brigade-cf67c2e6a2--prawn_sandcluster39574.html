prawn sandwich brigade

noun
group seen as pretentious or snobbish eaters
8.5 8.1
Pronunciation: / prɔn ˈsændwɪtʃ brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / prɔːn ˈsænwɪtʃ brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
A group of people, especially in sports or social settings, considered snobbish or pretentious because of their selective, often fussy eating habits or perceived lack of genuine enthusiasm.

Examples
