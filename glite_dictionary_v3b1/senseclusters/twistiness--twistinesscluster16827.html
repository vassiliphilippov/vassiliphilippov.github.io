twistiness

noun
unexpected complexity
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtwɪs.tɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈtwɪs.tə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈtwɪs.tɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈtwɪs.tə.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of having many unexpected changes, complications, or surprises, especially in a story, argument, or situation

Examples

Twistiness of a story
The twistiness of the movie kept the audience guessing until the very end.
She enjoyed the twistiness of the detective novel, which was full of surprises.
The twistiness of the negotiations made it hard to predict the outcome.
