treacly

adjective
overly sweet or sentimental
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːkli / (AmE), / ˈtriːkli / (BrE)
excessively sweet or sentimental, resembling treacle in taste or character

Examples

A treacly voice
She couldn't stand his treacly compliments, which sounded insincere.
The dessert had a treacly flavor that overwhelmed the other ingredients.
The movie's ending was so treacly that it made some viewers cringe.
