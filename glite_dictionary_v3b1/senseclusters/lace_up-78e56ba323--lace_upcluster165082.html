lace up

verb
fasten with laces
Pronunciation: / leɪs ʌp / (AmE), / leɪs ʌp / (BrE)
to fasten or secure something, especially shoes or clothing, by threading laces through eyelets or holes and tightening them

Examples

To lace up shoes
He stopped to lace up his shoes before jogging across the park.
Please lace up your boots tightly before hiking up the mountain.
She quickly laced up her skates at the start of her skating lesson.
