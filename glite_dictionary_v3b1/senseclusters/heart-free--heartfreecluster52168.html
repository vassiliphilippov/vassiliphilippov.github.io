heart-free

adjective
free from emotional attachment
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌfri / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌfriː / (BrE)
Free from emotional attachment or burdens; not emotionally involved or troubled.

Examples

Heart-free and happy

He wandered through life heart-free and open to every new adventure.
She felt heart-free after letting go of all her worries.
