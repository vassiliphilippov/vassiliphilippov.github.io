recognition

noun
formal acknowledgement of a government or nation
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the explicit and formal acknowledgement by one government of the existence or sovereignty of another government or nation

Examples

Diplomatic recognition
Guatemala's recognition of Belize in 1991 helped settle longstanding territorial disputes.
Many nations refused to grant recognition to the self-declared republic until it met international standards.
The country's diplomatic status changed after the United Nations voted for its recognition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acknowledgement - the act of accepting or admitting the existence of something
