fertility drug

noun
drug to increase fertility
4.0 3.0
Pronunciation: / fərˈtɪləti drʌɡ / (AmE), / fəˈtɪl.ɪ.ti drʌɡ / (BrE)
a medication taken to increase a woman's ability to conceive or improve reproductive fertility

Examples

A fertility drug treatment

Her doctor discussed the risks and benefits of using a fertility drug before beginning treatment.
Some fertility drugs work by stimulating the ovaries to release eggs.
