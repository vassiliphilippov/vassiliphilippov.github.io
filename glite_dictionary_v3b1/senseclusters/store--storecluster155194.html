store

noun
a supply kept for future use
1.0 7.33/m
Pronunciation: / st…îr / (AmE), / st…îÀê / (BrE)
a supply of something kept for future use; a reserve or stock

Examples

A store of food
A considerable store of patience is needed to work with young children.
She keeps a store of canned food in case of emergencies.
They found a hidden store of supplies in the basement.
