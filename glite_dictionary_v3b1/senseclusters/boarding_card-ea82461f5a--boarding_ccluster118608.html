boarding card

noun
official card for boarding a plane or ship
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
an official document or card that passengers must show before being allowed to board a plane or ship
