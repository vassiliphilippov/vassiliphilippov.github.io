belayer

noun
crew member handling ropes
9.9
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈleɪ.ər/ /; / /bə.ˈleɪ.ər/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈleɪ.ə/ /; / /bə.ˈleɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a crew member on a ship responsible for securing or fastening ropes to pins or cleats

Examples

Ship belayer
The belayer quickly took in the slack and secured the halyard to the belaying pin.
During the storm, the belayer made sure all the ropes were tightly fastened.
Every belayer must be attentive to avoid accidents on deck.
