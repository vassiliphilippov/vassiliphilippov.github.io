fight down

verb
suppress or control (a feeling or reaction)
Pronunciation: / faɪt daʊn / (AmE), / faɪt daʊn / (BrE)
To struggle to control or suppress (a feeling, urge, or reaction), often with difficulty.

Examples

To fight down tears
Despite feeling deeply emotional, she fought down her tears during the speech.
He managed to fight down his anger and responded calmly to the criticism.
She tried to fight down the panic rising inside her as the power went out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: give in - to yield or surrender
  • synonym: suppress - to put an end to the activities or existence of; to restrain
