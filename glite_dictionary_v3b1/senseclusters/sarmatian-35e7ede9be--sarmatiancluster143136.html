Sarmatian

proper noun
ancient Eastern European people
Pronunciation: / sɑːrˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / sɑːˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a member of an ancient people who lived in Eastern Europe, especially between the 5th century BCE and 4th century CE

Examples

Sarmatian tribes
The Sarmatian tribes played a significant role in the history of Eastern Europe.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts belonging to a Sarmatian settlement near the river.
Legends say that the Sarmatian warriors were skilled horsemen and archers.
