Aegypti

proper noun
of Egypt (Latin)
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdʒɪptaɪ / (AmE), / aɪˈdʒɪptaɪ / (BrE)
the Latin genitive form meaning 'of Egypt', used in scientific names to indicate origin from Egypt

Examples

Aedes Aegypti
The yellow fever mosquito is scientifically named Aedes Aegypti.
Several species with the name Aegypti are native to Egypt.
The Latin term Aegypti appears in many biological classifications.
