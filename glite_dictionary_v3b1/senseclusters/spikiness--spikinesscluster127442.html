spikiness

noun
irritable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪkinəs / (AmE), / ˈspaɪkinɪs / (BrE)
a brusque, sharp, or easily irritated manner or tone in a person's behavior or speech

Examples

Spikiness in conversation
Her spikiness in meetings put colleagues on edge and discouraged open discussion.
Despite his spikiness, he was respected for his honesty and directness.
The manager's spikiness sometimes made it difficult for employees to approach her with concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being peaceful and not easily upset
  • synonym: gruffness - a rough or stern manner or voice
  • synonym: irritability - the quality of being easily annoyed or angered
