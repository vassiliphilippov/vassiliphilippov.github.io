cot

noun
a small bed for a baby with high sides
1.0 0.34/m
Pronunciation: / kÉ‘t / (AmE), / kÉ’t / (BrE)
a small bed with high sides, usually for a baby or young child, to prevent them from falling out

Examples

Baby cot
Make sure the cot rails are raised before leaving the room.
The baby slept soundly in her cot all night.
We set up the cot in the corner of the nursery.

Synonyms

  • crib
    1.0 4.54/m
    crib has slatted sides and is often made to keep a baby safe when they sleep, while cot is a small bed with high sides.
  • baby's bed
    1.3 0.01/m
    baby's bed is a plain everyday phrase used to refer to any small infant bed, while cot names a small bed with high sides.
