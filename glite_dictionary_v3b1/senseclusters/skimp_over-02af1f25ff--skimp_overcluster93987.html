skimp over

verb
deal with superficially or insufficiently
5.4 6.0
Pronunciation: /skɪmp ˈoʊ.vər/ (AmE), /skɪmp ˈəʊ.və/ (BrE)
to deal with something superficially or insufficiently, especially in a hurried or careless way

Examples

You shouldn't skimp over the safety instructions if you want to avoid accidents.
