thong

verb
to whip with thong
9.9
Pronunciation: / θɔŋd / (AmE), / θɒŋd / (BrE)
to beat, whip, or lash someone with a thong, especially as a form of punishment.

Examples

To thong a prisoner
In the historical account the prisoner was thonged for breaking the governor's rules.
The guard thonged the runaway slave as punishment for his escape attempt.
During the ceremony, offenders were sometimes thonged in front of the entire village.
