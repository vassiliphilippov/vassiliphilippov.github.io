apocopate

adjective
final part omitted
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːkəˌpeɪt / (AmE), / əˈpɒkəˌpeɪt / (BrE)
having the final sound or syllable omitted, especially as a result of apocope

Examples

Apocopate form
The word 'photo' is an apocopate form of 'photograph'.
In poetry, an apocopate ending can help maintain the meter of a line.
Some dialects use apocopate forms in everyday speech for convenience.
