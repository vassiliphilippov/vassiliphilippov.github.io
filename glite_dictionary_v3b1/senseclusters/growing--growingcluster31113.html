growing

noun
process of an organism increasing in size or complexity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
The process by which an individual organism increases in size or complexity over time, especially as a natural biological development.

Examples

Healthy growing in children
Adequate nutrition is essential for healthy growing in young children.
Scientists studied the growing of bacteria under different conditions.
The growing of plants requires both sunlight and water.
