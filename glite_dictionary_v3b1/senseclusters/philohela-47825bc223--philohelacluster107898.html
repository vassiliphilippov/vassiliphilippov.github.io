Philohela

noun
genus of American woodcocks
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪloʊˈhiːlə / (AmE), / ˌfɪləʊˈhiːlə / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the family Scolopacidae, comprising the American woodcock.

Examples

The Philohela bird
Field guides often list the Philohela among North American shorebirds.
Scientists debated whether the American woodcock should remain in the genus Philohela or be reclassified.
The Philohela is distinguished by its cryptic plumage and woodland habitat.
