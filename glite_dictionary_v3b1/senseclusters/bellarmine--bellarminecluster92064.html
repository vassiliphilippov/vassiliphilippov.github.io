bellarmine

noun
decorated stoneware jug with a bearded face
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbel.ər.maɪn/ /; / /ˈbel.ə.maɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbel.ə.maɪn/ (BrE)
a stoneware jug from the 16th and 17th centuries with a long neck and a bearded face, often a caricature of Cardinal Bellarmine, used mainly for storing and serving drinks
