shooter

noun
a person who uses a gun, esp. a killer
3.6 16/m
Pronunciation: / Àà ÉuÀêt…ôr / (AmE), / Àà ÉuÀêt…ô / (BrE)
a person who uses a gun, bow, or other weapon to shoot, especially judged by their skill or accuracy (can also refer to a professional killer)

Examples

Skilled shooter
As a notorious shooter, he was feared throughout the city underworld.
The film tells the story of a professional shooter hired for a dangerous mission.
The police are searching for the shooter responsible for the crime.
As a novice shooter, she practiced at the range every weekend.
He is an excellent shooter and rarely misses his target.
The team relied on their best shooter to score the winning point.
