clintonia

noun
genus of temperate-flowering plants
Pronunciation: / klɪnˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / klɪnˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
A plant of the temperate genus Clintonia, with broad basal leaves and white, yellowish, or purplish flowers followed by blue or black berries.

Examples

The clintonia plant
Botanists often study clintonia for its unique flower and leaf structure.
The berries of clintonia are a striking deep blue in the late summer.
We found a patch of clintonia blooming near the forest stream.
