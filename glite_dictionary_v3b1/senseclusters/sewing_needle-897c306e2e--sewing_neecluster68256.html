sewing needle

noun
tool for sewing and stitching
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (BrE)
a slender, pointed metal tool with a hole (eye) for thread, used in sewing to pass thread through fabric

Examples

A sharp sewing needle
A sharp sewing needle is essential for hand-stitching fabrics.
He pricked his finger while pushing the sewing needle through thick material.
She carefully threaded the sewing needle before starting her embroidery project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: needle - a small, slender, pointed tool with an eye for sewing
