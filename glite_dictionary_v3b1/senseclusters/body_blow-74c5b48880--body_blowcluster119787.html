body blow

noun
severe setback or disappointment
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi bloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi bləʊ / (BrE)
an event or occurrence that causes great difficulty, disappointment, or emotional shock

Examples

A body blow to hope
The factory closure dealt a body blow to the local economy.
The new tax regulations were a body blow to small business owners across the country.
When his trusted friend betrayed him, it came as a real body blow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boost - something that helps or improves; an event with a positive impact
  • synonym: setback - a reversal or check in progress
  • synonym: shock - a sudden upsetting or surprising event
