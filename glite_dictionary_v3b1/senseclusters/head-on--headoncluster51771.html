head-on

adjective, adverb
directly in front; with the front foremost
5.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhɛd.ɑːn/ /; / /ˈhɛd.ɔːn/ (AmE), /ˈhɛd.ɒn/ (BrE)
with the front part leading or facing directly; in a direct or frontal way, especially in collisions or confrontations
