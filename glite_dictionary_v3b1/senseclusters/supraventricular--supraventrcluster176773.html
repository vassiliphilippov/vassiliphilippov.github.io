supraventricular

adjective
above heart ventricles
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəvɛnˈtrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəvɛnˈtrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring above a ventricle, especially of the heart (or brain)

Examples

Supraventricular heart arrhythmia
The doctor explained that the arrhythmia was supraventricular in origin and not from the ventricles.
A supraventricular tachycardia can cause a rapid heartbeat that starts above the heart's ventricles.
Medications were prescribed to control her supraventricular rhythm disturbances.
The doctor noted a supraventricular arrhythmia on the patient's ECG results.
A supraventricular tumor was detected during the neurological examination.
Researchers studied the effects of supraventricular stimulation on heart function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atrial - relating to the atria of the heart
  • antonym: ventricular - relating to the ventricles of the heart
