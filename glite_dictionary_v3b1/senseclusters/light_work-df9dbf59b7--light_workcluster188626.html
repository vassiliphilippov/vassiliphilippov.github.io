light work

noun
easy or manageable task
Pronunciation: / laɪt wɝːk / (AmE), / laɪt wɜːk / (BrE)
A task or activity that is easy to do or takes little effort.

Examples

Light work task
She made light work of organizing the entire event by herself.
The team made light work of assembling the furniture in under an hour.
These exercises should be light work for anyone who trains regularly.
