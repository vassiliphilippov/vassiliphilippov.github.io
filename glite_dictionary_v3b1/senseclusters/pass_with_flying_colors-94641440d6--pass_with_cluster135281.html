pass with flying colors

verb
to succeed brilliantly, especially in a test
5.2
Pronunciation: / pæs wɪð ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkʌlɚz / (AmE), / pɑːs wɪð ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkʌləz / (BrE)
