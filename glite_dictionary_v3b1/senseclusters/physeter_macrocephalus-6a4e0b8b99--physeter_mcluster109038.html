Physeter macrocephalus

noun
sperm whale species
Pronunciation: / faɪˈsiːtər ˌmækroʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / fɪˈsiːtə ˌmækrəʊˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the sperm whale, a large marine mammal known for its massive head and deep diving abilities

Examples

The Physeter macrocephalus whale
The Physeter macrocephalus can dive to great depths in search of squid.
Researchers tracked a pod of Physeter macrocephalus off the coast of New Zealand.
The massive head of the Physeter macrocephalus contains the largest brain of any animal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orca - a large, black-and-white predatory whale also known as the killer whale
  • synonym: sperm whale - a large toothed whale with a massive head, found in oceans worldwide
