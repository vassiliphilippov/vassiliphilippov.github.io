brick trowel

noun
mason's tool for spreading mortar
Pronunciation: / brɪk ˈtraʊəl / (AmE), / brɪk ˈtraʊəl / (BrE)
a hand tool with a flat, pointed blade, used by masons for picking up and spreading mortar

Examples

A brick trowel tool
A good brick trowel is essential for any bricklaying project.
After mixing the cement, she used a brick trowel to apply it evenly between the bricks.
The mason expertly spread mortar with his brick trowel as he built the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trowel - a small handheld tool with a flat blade, used for spreading or smoothing plaster, mortar, or concrete
