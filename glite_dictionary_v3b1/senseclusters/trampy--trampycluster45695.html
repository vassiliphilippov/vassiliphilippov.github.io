trampy

adjective
shabby or untidy
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmpi / (AmE), / ˈtræmpi / (BrE)
looking shabby, untidy, or resembling a vagrant in appearance or manner

Examples

Trampy clothes
His trampy clothes made him look like he had slept outside.
The abandoned house had a trampy feel, with broken windows and overgrown weeds.
She worried her outfit looked too trampy for the formal dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat - tidy and in order
  • synonym: shabby - in poor condition through long use or lack of care
  • synonym: unkempt - not neat or tidy; messy in appearance
