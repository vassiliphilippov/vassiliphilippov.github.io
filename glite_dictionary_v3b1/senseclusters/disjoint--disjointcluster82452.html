disjoint

verb
separate at the joints
8.0
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
to separate (meat or a body) at the joints; to cut apart at the joints

Examples

To disjoint a chicken
Before roasting the bird, you should disjoint it into manageable pieces.
It can be tricky to disjoint a rabbit without practice.
The butcher expertly disjointed the carcass in just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joint - to cut (meat) into joints
