barrier to progress

noun
obstacle to improvement
Pronunciation: / ˈbæriər tə ˈprɑːɡrɛs / (AmE), / ˈbæriə tə ˈprəʊɡres / (BrE)
something that prevents improvement or advancement.

Examples

A barrier to progress
Effective leadership is needed to overcome any barrier to progress in a project.
Lack of funding is often the biggest barrier to progress in scientific research.
Outdated technology can pose a serious barrier to progress for modern businesses.
