V-1

noun
German World War II jet-propelled missile
9.9 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / vi w ån / (AmE), / viÀê w ån / (BrE)
a German jet-propelled missile used during World War II; it carried an explosive warhead and was the first guided missile used in warfare

Examples

The V-1 missile
During World War II, London was targeted by numerous V-1 attacks launched from continental Europe.
Engineers studied the technology behind the V-1 to develop later cruise missiles.
The display at the museum includes an actual V-1 missile recovered after the war.
