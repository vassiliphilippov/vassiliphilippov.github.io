throw a wobbly

verb
suddenly lose one's temper
8.6
Pronunciation: / θroʊ ə ˈwɑbli / (AmE), / θrəʊ ə ˈwɒbli / (BrE)
to suddenly become very angry or upset in a dramatic or uncontrolled way

Examples

To throw a wobbly suddenly

Julia threw a wobbly when she saw her birthday cake was ruined.
Teenagers sometimes throw a wobbly over the smallest things.
