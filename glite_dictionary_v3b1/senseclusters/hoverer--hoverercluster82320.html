hoverer

noun
one who stays nearby
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌvərər / (AmE), / ˈhɒvərə / (BrE)
a person, animal, or thing that remains suspended in one place in the air or stays nearby without moving away

Examples

A hoverer drone
The hoverer circled above the field, searching for a place to land.
At the party, he was a quiet hoverer near the snack table, rarely joining conversations.
A small drone acted as a hoverer, monitoring the area from above.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drifter - a person or thing that moves from place to place
  • synonym: observer - a person who watches something attentively
