Potassium acid carbonate in baking
Because it reacts with acids to release carbon dioxide, potassium acid carbonate is often found in baking powder.
Doctors sometimes recommend potassium acid carbonate to neutralize stomach acid and relieve indigestion.
Many bakers use potassium acid carbonate as a leavening agent in recipes requiring low-sodium alternatives.
