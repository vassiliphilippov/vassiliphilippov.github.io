overbrush

noun
dense upper vegetation
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)

Examples

Thick overbrush
The hikers struggled to move through the thick overbrush along the trail.
Firefighters cleared the overbrush to prevent the wildfire from spreading quickly.
Birds often build their nests high in the overbrush where they are harder to spot.
