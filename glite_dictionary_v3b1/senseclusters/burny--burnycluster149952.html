burny

adjective
causing burning sensation
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrni / (AmE), / ˈbɜːni / (BrE)
hot or chemically corrosive enough to cause a burning sensation or injury to skin or tissue

Examples

Burny chemical liquid
Be careful with that chemical; it is extremely burny and can damage your skin.
The soup was so burny that I had to wait several minutes before tasting it.
After touching the plant, she felt a burny sensation on her fingers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caustic - able to burn or corrode organic tissue
  • synonym: irritating - causing discomfort or inflammation
  • antonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect
