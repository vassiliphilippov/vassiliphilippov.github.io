Confucianist

noun
follower of Confucius's teachings
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈfjuːʃənɪst / (AmE), / kənˈfjuːʃənɪst / (BrE)
A person who believes in or follows the teachings of Confucius.

Examples

A Confucianist teacher
During the debate, the Confucianist explained the importance of moral integrity.
Many Confucianists gather annually to commemorate Confucius's birthday.
She considers herself a Confucianist and practices the values of respect and filial piety.
