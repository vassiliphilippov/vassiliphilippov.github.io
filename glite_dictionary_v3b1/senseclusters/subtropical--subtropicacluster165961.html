subtropical

adjective
relating to the subtropics
4.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /sʌb.ˈtrɑː.pɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /sʌb.ˈtrɒp.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the geographic regions immediately north or south of the tropics, typically with hot, humid summers and mild winters

Examples

The region's subtropical climate makes it a popular destination for tourists year-round.
Winters in subtropical zones are generally mild and rarely experience frost.
