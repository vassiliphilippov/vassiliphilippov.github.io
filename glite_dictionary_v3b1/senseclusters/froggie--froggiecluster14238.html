froggie

noun
small frog (childish)
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːɡi / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡi / (BrE)
a small or young frog, often used by or for children in a playful or affectionate way

Examples

A cute froggie
The little boy was excited to find a froggie hopping near the garden.
She drew a picture of a smiling froggie for her school project.
After the rain, we saw a tiny froggie sitting on a leaf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frog - a small tailless amphibian with long hind legs for leaping
  • antonym: toad - a similar amphibian with drier, bumpier skin
