carve

verb
cut food, especially meat, into pieces
1.5 3.56/m
Pronunciation: / k…ëÀêrv / (AmE), / k…ëÀêv / (BrE)
to cut (meat, a piece of meat, etc.) into slices or pieces, especially before serving

Examples

To carve the turkey
