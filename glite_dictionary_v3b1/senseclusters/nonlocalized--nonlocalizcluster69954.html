nonlocalized

adjective
not adapted for region
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈləʊkəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not adapted, translated, or customized for a specific language, country, or cultural market

Examples

Nonlocalized software version
The software remained nonlocalized, making it difficult for users in other countries to understand the instructions.
A nonlocalized product may not meet the needs of customers in different regions.
They received complaints because the app was still nonlocalized and only available in English.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generic - not specific to any particular group or place
  • antonym: localized - adapted or translated for a specific region or culture
