in love

adjective
experiencing romantic affection
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn lʌv / (AmE), / ɪn lʌv / (BrE)

Examples

To be in love

She walked around all day smiling as if she were completely in love.
They met at college and quickly fell in love with each other.
