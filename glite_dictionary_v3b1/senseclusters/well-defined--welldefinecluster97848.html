well-defined

adjective
having clear, distinct boundaries or form
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛl dɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl dɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
having clear and distinct edges, boundaries, or form; precisely delineated or easy to distinguish

Examples

Well-defined lines
Her painting style is characterized by well-defined lines and shapes.
The doctor examined the X-ray and noted the well-defined edges of the tumor.
The mountain range had well-defined peaks visible against the sky.
