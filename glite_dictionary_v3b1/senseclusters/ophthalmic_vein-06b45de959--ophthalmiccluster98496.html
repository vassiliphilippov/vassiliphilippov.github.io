ophthalmic vein

noun
vein draining blood from the eye
Pronunciation: / ɑfˈθælmɪk veɪn / (AmE), / ɒfˈθælmɪk veɪn / (BrE)
either of two veins that drain blood from the eye and empty into the cavernous sinus

Examples

The ophthalmic vein drains
A blood clot in the ophthalmic vein can lead to serious complications affecting vision.
Inflammation sometimes spreads to the ophthalmic vein, causing swelling around the eye.
The surgeon carefully located the ophthalmic vein during the procedure.

  • synonym: vein - blood vessel that carries blood to the heart
