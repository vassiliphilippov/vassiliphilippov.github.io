little finger

noun
the smallest, outermost finger of the hand
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɾl̩ ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈfɪŋ.ɡə / (BrE)
the smallest finger on the human hand, located farthest from the thumb

Examples

The little finger
He wore a gold ring on his little finger.
She injured her little finger during the volleyball game.
Your little finger is also called the 'pinky' in some countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinky - informal synonym for little finger
  • antonym: thumb - short, thick first digit on the hand
