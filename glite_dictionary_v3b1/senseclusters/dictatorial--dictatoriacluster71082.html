dictatorial

adjective
having absolute, authoritarian power
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪk.təˈtɔːr.i.əl /; / ˌdɪk.təˈtɔri.əl / (AmE), / ˌdɪk.təˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
having or showing complete power and expecting others to obey without question; behaving in an autocratic or domineering way
