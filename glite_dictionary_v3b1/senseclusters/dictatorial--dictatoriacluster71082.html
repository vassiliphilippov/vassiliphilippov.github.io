Synonyms

  • autocratic
    8.0 2/m
    autocratic describes a single ruler who holds complete formal power in government, while dictatorial means demanding unquestioning obedience.
  • authoritarian
    7.5 8/m
    authoritarian expects strict obedience and enforces rules across society, while dictatorial emphasizes one person's absolute control.
  • despotic
    8.4 0.8/m
    despotic rules use cruel, absolute power over people, while dictatorial focuses on forcing obedience and dominating others.
  • tyrannical
    7.9 2/m
    tyrannical leaders use power cruelly and unjustly, while dictatorial leaders demand obedience and act in a domineering way.
  • tyrannic
    9.1 0.01/m
    tyrannic describes harsh, absolute rule often cruel, while dictatorial stresses expecting obedience and controlling people.
  • oppressive
    6.3 4/m
    oppressive refers to harsh systems that limit people and cause suffering, while dictatorial refers to a person demanding total obedience.
  • iron-fisted
    7.6 0.1/m
    iron-fisted rulers use severe, strict measures to control people, while dictatorial means expecting unquestioning obedience from others.
  • overbearing
    5.8 1/m
    overbearing people act bossy and push others around in personal situations, while dictatorial implies formal absolute power and control.
