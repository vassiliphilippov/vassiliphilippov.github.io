offworld

adjective
outside Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌwɜrld / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌwɜːld / (BrE)
located on, relating to, or originating from a place outside the planet Earth, especially in space or on another planet.

Examples

Offworld colony
The scientists are developing offworld habitats for future Mars missions.
She dreams of working in an offworld research facility one day.
The movie features an offworld colony struggling to survive on a distant planet.

Related Terms
