In a way that cannot be separated or parted; so closely linked as to be impossible to divide.

Examples

Inseparably linked
Love and trust are often inseparably linked in a strong relationship.
The history of the city is inseparably connected with the river that runs through it.
Their lives had become inseparably intertwined over the years.

Synonyms

  • indissolubly
    8.2 0.08/m
    indissolubly describes ties that cannot be broken in law or feeling, while inseparably means simply closely joined.
  • inextricably
    8.0 2.52/m
    inextricably shows things are so tangled you cannot get them apart, while inseparably simply means they cannot be separated.
  • indivisibly
    5.7 0.03/m
    indivisibly means something cannot be divided into parts, while inseparably focuses on things that stay joined together.
