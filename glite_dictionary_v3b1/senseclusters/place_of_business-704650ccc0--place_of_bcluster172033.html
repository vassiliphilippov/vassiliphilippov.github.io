place of business

noun
a location for conducting business
Pronunciation: / ˌpleɪs əv ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˌpleɪs əv ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
a building or location where commercial activities such as sales, manufacturing, warehousing, or services are conducted
