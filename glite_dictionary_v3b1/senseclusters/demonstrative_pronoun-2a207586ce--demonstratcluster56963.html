demonstrative pronoun

noun
pronoun that points to something specific
4.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑnstrətɪv proʊˈnaʊn / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒnstrətɪv prəˈnaʊn / (BrE)
a pronoun used to indicate or point to a specific person, place, thing, or idea, such as 'this', 'that', 'these', or 'those'

Examples

Demonstrative pronoun this
In the sentence 'This is delicious,' the word this is a demonstrative pronoun.
Teachers often explain that these and 'those' are examples of demonstrative pronouns.
Use those when referring to objects that are far away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstrative - a word or pronoun that indicates or singles out the thing referred to
  • antonym: personal pronoun - pronoun used to refer to a particular person or thing
