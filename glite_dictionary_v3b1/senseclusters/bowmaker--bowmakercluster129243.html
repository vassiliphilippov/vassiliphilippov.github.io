bowmaker

noun
maker of bows
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes bows, especially for stringed musical instruments or for archery

Examples

A bowmaker for violins
The famous bowmaker crafted bows for some of the world's best violinists.
She apprenticed with a skilled bowmaker to learn the art of making archery bows.
A high-quality bowmaker can greatly influence the sound of a string instrument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: archer - a person who uses a bow to shoot arrows
  • synonym: luthier - a person who makes or repairs stringed instruments
