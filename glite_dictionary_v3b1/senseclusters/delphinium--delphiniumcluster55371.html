delphinium

noun
a flowering plant of the genus Delphinium
7.4
Pronunciation: / dɛlˈfɪniəm / (AmE), / dɛlˈfɪniəm / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Delphinium, noted for its tall spikes of brightly colored, spurred flowers and deeply divided leaves; many species are highly toxic

Examples

Tall delphinium flowers
Be careful when handling delphinium plants, as many parts are toxic if ingested.
Each spring, she eagerly waits for the delphiniums to flower along the fence.
The garden was filled with tall delphinium blooms in every shade of blue and purple.
