prizewinner

noun
winner of a prize
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪzˌwɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpraɪzˌwɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that has won a prize in a competition or contest

Examples

The prizewinner accepts trophy
The prizewinner smiled proudly as she accepted her trophy on stage.
Each prizewinner was given a certificate and a small cash award.
The science fair prizewinner developed an innovative new water filter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: champion - a person who has won first place in a competition
  • antonym: loser - a person who does not win in a competition
