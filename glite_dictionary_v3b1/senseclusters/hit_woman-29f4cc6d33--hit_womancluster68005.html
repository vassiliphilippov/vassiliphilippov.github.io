hit woman

noun
a female hired assassin
Pronunciation: / hɪt ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / hɪt ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A female professional killer, especially one who is hired to murder someone with a firearm.

Examples

A hit woman killer
In the movie, the lead character is a hit woman with a mysterious past.
She was once a feared hit woman before deciding to leave her criminal past behind.
The mob boss hired a notorious hit woman to eliminate his rival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hitman - a man who is hired to kill someone
