head blight

noun
disease of cereal crop heads
Pronunciation: / hɛd blaɪt / (AmE), / hɛd blaɪt / (BrE)
a fungal disease affecting the heads of cereal crops, causing discoloration and reduced yield

Examples

Head blight disease
Farmers are concerned about head blight damaging this year's wheat harvest.
Proper crop rotation can help prevent head blight in barley fields.
The wet spring created ideal conditions for head blight to spread among the cereal crops.
