circumflex femoral vein

noun
vein near hip/thigh area
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkəmˌflɛks fəˈmoʊrəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəmˌflɛks fɪˈməʊrəl veɪn / (BrE)
either of two veins that accompany the circumflex femoral arteries, serving the hip and thigh

Examples

The circumflex femoral vein
An injury to the circumflex femoral vein can lead to significant bleeding in the upper thigh region.
Radiological imaging revealed a blockage in the circumflex femoral vein near the patient's hip joint.
The surgeon carefully identified the circumflex femoral vein during the procedure to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femoral vein - major vein of the thigh draining blood from the leg
