sausage party

noun
gathering with mostly men
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈsɒsɪdʒ ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
An informal gathering attended mostly or exclusively by men, often implying a lack of women present.

Examples

A sausage party night
He complained that every poker night was just another sausage party.
Our team events shouldn't always be a sausage party; let's try to invite more colleagues.
The tech conference turned into a real sausage party after the lunch break.
