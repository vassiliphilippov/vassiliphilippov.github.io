maloca

noun
Amazon communal house
Pronunciation: / məˈloʊkə / (AmE), / məˈləʊkə / (BrE)
a large communal house used by some indigenous peoples of the Amazon for living and gatherings

Examples

A maloca house
The villagers built a new maloca to accommodate their growing community.
Ceremonies and important meetings are often held inside the maloca.
Each family has its own space within the large maloca shared by the tribe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: longhouse - a traditional communal dwelling of some indigenous peoples
