multistate

adjective
involving several states
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌsteɪt / (BrE)
involving, relating to, or existing in more than one state, especially within a country

Examples

A multistate lottery
The multistate lottery is played in dozens of regions across the country.
A multistate investigation was launched to track the source of the outbreak.
Several companies joined a multistate partnership to improve transportation infrastructure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interstate - involving or connecting two or more states
  • antonym: intrastate - existing or occurring within a single state
