Keatsian

adjective
of John Keats
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːtsiən / (AmE), / ˈkiːtsiən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the English poet John Keats or his poetry, especially its rich imagery and sensuous style

Examples

Keatsian poetry style
The novel's lush descriptions and emotional depth give it a distinctly Keatsian quality.
Her paintings evoke a Keatsian sense of beauty and melancholy.
Critics praised the film's Keatsian atmosphere and poetic language.
