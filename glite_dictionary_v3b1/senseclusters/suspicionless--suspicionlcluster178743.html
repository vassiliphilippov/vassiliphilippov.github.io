suspicionless

adjective
without prior suspicion
8.4
Pronunciation: / səˈspɪʃənləs / (AmE), / səˈspɪʃənləs / (BrE)
done without any prior suspicion or reason for doubt; not suspected of wrongdoing

Examples

Suspicionless security check
The airport security conducted a suspicionless screening of all passengers entering the terminal.
Some people argue that suspicionless searches violate personal privacy rights.
The new policy allows for suspicionless checks at public events to ensure safety.
The police conducted a suspicionless search to ensure public safety.
After a thorough investigation, the employee was found to be completely suspicionless.
The audit confirmed that all transactions were suspicionless and above board.

  • antonym: targeted - directed at a specific person or group based on suspicion or reason
