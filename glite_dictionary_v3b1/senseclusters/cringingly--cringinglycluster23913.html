cringingly

adverb
in a submissive way
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows excessive deference, submission, or a tendency to grovel

Examples

Cringingly agree to demands
He cringingly agreed to every demand, hoping to avoid conflict with his boss.
She cringingly apologized for a mistake that was not her fault.
The student cringingly accepted the harsh criticism without defending himself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidently - in a way that shows self-assurance and assertiveness
  • synonym: obsequiously - in an overly eager or servile manner
  • synonym: submissively - in a way that shows willingness to yield to others
