DFT

noun
testable circuit design
Pronunciation: / di ɛf ti / (AmE), / diː ɛf tiː / (BrE)
engineering practices that make electronic circuits easier to test for faults during manufacturing

Examples

DFT features in chips
The engineers incorporated DFT features to simplify the testing process of the new microchip.
By following DFT guidelines, the team reduced the time needed to identify manufacturing faults.
Modern electronic products often rely on DFT to ensure quality and reliability during production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: design for manufacturability - engineering practices to make products easier to manufacture
  • synonym: testability - the ease with which a system can be tested
