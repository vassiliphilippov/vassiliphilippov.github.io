swatting

noun
quick striking action
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hitting or striking something quickly, especially to kill or drive away insects

Examples

Swatting flies
He spent the afternoon swatting mosquitoes that buzzed around the campsite.
Her constant swatting at flies made it hard to concentrate on the picnic.
The loud swatting of insects echoed through the kitchen every summer evening.
