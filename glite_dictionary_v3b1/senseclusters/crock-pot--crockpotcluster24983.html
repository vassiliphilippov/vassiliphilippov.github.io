crock-pot

noun
slow-cooking appliance
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑkˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈkrɒkˌpɒt / (BrE)
an electric cooking pot that cooks food slowly over several hours at a low temperature

Examples

Electric crock-pot
She left the soup to simmer all day in the crock-pot while she was at work.
A crock-pot is perfect for making tender stews and roasts with minimal effort.
He bought a new crock-pot to prepare meals for his busy family.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pressure cooker - a sealed pot that cooks food quickly using steam pressure
  • synonym: slow cooker - an electric pot for cooking food slowly at low temperatures
