straight man

noun
serious partner in a comedy act
5.3 6.0
Pronunciation: / streɪt mæn /; / ˈstreɪt ˌmæn / (AmE), / streɪt mæn /; / ˈstreɪt ˌman / (BrE)
a performer in a comedy act or duo who plays a serious role to set up jokes for the comedian
