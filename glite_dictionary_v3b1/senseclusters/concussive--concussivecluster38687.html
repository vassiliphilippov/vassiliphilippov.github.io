concussive

adjective
relating to concussion
Pronunciation: / kənˈkʌsɪv / (AmE), / kənˈkʌsɪv / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a concussion, especially describing symptoms or effects of a sudden brain injury

Examples

Concussive injury
The player was removed from the game after a concussive blow to the head.
Doctors monitored her for concussive symptoms following the accident.
Repeated concussive injuries can have long-term effects on brain health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benign - not harmful or not causing injury
  • synonym: traumatic - relating to or caused by trauma or injury
