nonunion

noun
worker not in union
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈjuːniən / (AmE), / nɒnˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a worker or group that does not belong to a labor union

Examples

Nonunion workers
The company prefers to hire nonunion employees for temporary positions.
Many nonunion workers do not receive the same benefits as union members.
A group of nonunion staff raised concerns about their working conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonmember - a person who is not a member of a group or organization
  • antonym: union member - a worker who belongs to a labor union
