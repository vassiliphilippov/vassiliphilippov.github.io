dry one's eyes

verb
stop crying; regain composure
1.0
Pronunciation: / draɪ wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / draɪ wʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to stop crying and regain one's composure, especially after being upset

Examples

To dry his eyes quickly
A comforting word from her friend helped him dry his eyes.
Once the sad movie ended, he took a moment to dry his eyes.
She tried to dry her eyes before leaving the room.
