gateball

noun
team mallet sport
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtbɔːl / (BrE)
a team sport in which players use mallets to hit balls through gates on a field, similar to croquet

Examples

Gateball match
The local club organizes gateball matches every Saturday morning.
She learned the rules of gateball quickly and joined a team.
Many seniors enjoy playing gateball because it is social and not too physically demanding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: croquet - an outdoor game where players hit balls through hoops with mallets
