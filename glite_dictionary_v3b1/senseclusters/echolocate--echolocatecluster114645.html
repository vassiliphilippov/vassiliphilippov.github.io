echolocate

verb
to find by echoes
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkoʊˌloʊkeɪt /; / ˌɛkoʊˈloʊkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊˌləʊkeɪt /; / ˌɛkəʊˈləʊkeɪt / (BrE)
to find or detect the position of objects by making sounds and listening to the echoes that return
