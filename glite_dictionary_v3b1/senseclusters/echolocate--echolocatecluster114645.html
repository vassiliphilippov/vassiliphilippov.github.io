echolocate

verb
to find by echoes
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkoʊˌloʊkeɪt /; / ˌɛkoʊˈloʊkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊˌləʊkeɪt /; / ˌɛkəʊˈləʊkeɪt / (BrE)
