conservancy

noun
commission overseeing fisheries/navigation
3.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝː.vən.si / (AmE), / kənˈsɜː.vən.si / (BrE)
an official body or organization responsible for managing and protecting natural resources such as rivers, forests, or fisheries

Examples

A conservancy organization
A new fisheries conservancy was established to protect local resources.
The conservancy imposed strict rules to maintain safe navigation channels.
The Port of London Conservancy regulates shipping traffic along the river.
A local conservancy manages the maintenance of the riverbanks and surrounding forests.
The national conservancy is working to restore the wetland habitats in the region.
Volunteers joined the conservancy to help plant native trees in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservation - the act of preserving and protecting the environment
