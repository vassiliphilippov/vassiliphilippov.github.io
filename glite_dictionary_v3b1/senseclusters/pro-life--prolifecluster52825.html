pro-life

adjective
opposing abortion; supporting embryo/fetus rights
7.8
Pronunciation: / proʊ ˈlaɪf / (AmE), / prəʊ ˈlaɪf / (BrE)
opposed to the legalization of abortion and supporting legal protection for embryos and fetuses

Examples

Pro-life views
He wrote an article expressing his pro-life views and advocating for stricter abortion laws.
Many pro-life activists marched in the rally downtown last weekend.
The senator is known for her strong pro-life stance on abortion.
