jungly

adjective
overgrown with tropical vegetation
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋɡli / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡli / (BrE)
(of land or vegetation) thickly overgrown, especially with tropical plants; resembling a jungle.

Examples

A jungly garden
That corner of the garden is so jungly that you can barely see the fence.
The old plantation had become completely jungly after years without maintenance.
We hacked our way through the jungly undergrowth, searching for the river.
