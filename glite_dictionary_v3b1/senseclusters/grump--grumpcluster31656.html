grump

noun
a bad-tempered person
2.3 4.3
Pronunciation: /grʌmp/ (AmE), /grʌmp/ (BrE)
A person who is easily annoyed or in a bad mood, often complaining or irritable.

Examples

Even though she's a bit of a grump, her friends know she means well.
My grandfather can be a real grump if he doesn't get his morning coffee.
