grump

noun
a bad-tempered person
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌmp / (AmE), / ɡrʌmp / (BrE)
A person who is easily annoyed or in a bad mood, often complaining or irritable.

Examples

A morning grump
Don't be such a grump—come join the party and have some fun!
Even though she's a bit of a grump, her friends know she means well.
My grandfather can be a real grump if he doesn't get his morning coffee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful person - a person who is happy and good-natured
  • synonym: grouch - a person who complains a lot or is habitually irritable
