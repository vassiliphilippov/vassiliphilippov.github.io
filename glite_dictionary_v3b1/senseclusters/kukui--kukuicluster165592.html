kukui

noun
Hawaiian tree symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈkuːi / (AmE), / kuːˈkuːi / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to Hawaii, also known as the candlenut tree, important in Hawaiian culture

Examples

Kukui tree in Hawaii

The kukui tree is an important symbol in Hawaiian culture.
People use oil from the kukui nut for cooking and skin care.
