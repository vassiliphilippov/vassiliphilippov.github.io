rooker

noun
swindler or cheat
Pronunciation: / ˈrʊkər / (AmE), / ˈrʊkə / (BrE)
an old or rare word for a person who cheats or swindles others

Examples

A rooker cheats
In the old city, a clever rooker would often target unsuspecting visitors.
The story warned travelers to beware of any rooker offering too-good-to-be-true deals.
He was called a rooker after he tricked several merchants out of their goods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheat - a person who acts dishonestly to gain an advantage
  • antonym: honest person - someone who tells the truth and does not cheat
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats others out of money or assets
