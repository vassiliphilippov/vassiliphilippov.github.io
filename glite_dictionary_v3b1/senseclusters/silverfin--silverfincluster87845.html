silverfin

noun
fish with silvery fins
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌfɪn / (BrE)
a type of fish with silvery fins, used as a common name for several different species

Examples

A silverfin fish
The lake is famous for its abundant silverfin that shimmer in the sunlight.
Fishermen often catch silverfin early in the morning when the water is calm.
Children watched as a school of silverfin darted through the clear stream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carp - a type of freshwater fish, sometimes called silverfin
  • synonym: minnow - a small freshwater fish, often with shiny scales
