reengagement

noun
resumption of conflict
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of resuming conflict, fighting, or hostile contact after a pause or interruption

Examples

Reengagement of forces
The sudden reengagement of the two armies caught the peacekeepers by surprise.
Diplomats worked hard to prevent a reengagement after the fragile truce was broken.
Following several days of calm, there was a rapid reengagement between the rival factions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ceasefire - a temporary suspension of fighting
  • synonym: renewal - the process of beginning something again after a pause
