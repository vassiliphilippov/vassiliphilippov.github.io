Viburnum lantana

noun
European shrub with berries; the wayfaring tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈbɜrnəm lænˈteɪnə / (AmE), / vaɪˈbɜːnəm lænˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
A vigorous deciduous European shrub, commonly found along roadsides, with red berries that turn black; also known as the wayfaring tree.
