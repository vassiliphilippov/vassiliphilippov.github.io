landing strip

noun
simple airfield or runway
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ ˌstrɪp / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ ˌstrɪp / (BrE)
a runway or area where aircraft can land and take off, usually with minimal facilities

Examples

A landing strip runway

Emergency crews cleared the landing strip following a minor incident.
The new airport will include an additional landing strip to accommodate more flights.
