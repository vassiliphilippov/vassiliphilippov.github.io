rechart

verb
to chart again
7.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈtʃɑːrt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈtʃɑːrt/ /; / /rə.ˈtʃɑːrt/ (AmE), /riː.ˈtʃɑːt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈtʃɑːt/ /; / /rə.ˈtʃɑːt/ (BrE)
to appear again on a sales or popularity chart, especially after having dropped off previously

Examples

Song recharts in charts
After a surge in popularity, the single managed to rechart in the top ten.
The album did not perform well at first but later recharted thanks to a viral video.
It is rare for a song to rechart years after its original release.
