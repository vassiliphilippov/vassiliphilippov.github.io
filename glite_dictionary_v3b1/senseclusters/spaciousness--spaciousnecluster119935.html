spaciousness

noun
the quality of being spacious or roomy
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈspeɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being large, open, and having plenty of space, especially inside a building or room

Examples

The spaciousness of rooms
The spaciousness of the hotel lobby made guests feel comfortable and relaxed.
The designer used large windows to enhance the sense of spaciousness in the living room.
We admired the spaciousness of their new apartment compared to the old one.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crampedness - the quality of being small, crowded, or confined
  • synonym: roominess - the quality of being comfortably large or having a lot of space
