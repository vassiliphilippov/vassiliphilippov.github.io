Islamofascist

adjective
combining fascism and Islam
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪsləmoʊˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪsləməʊˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
describing a movement, policy, or ideology that combines elements of fascism with Islamic beliefs or practices, often used as a political label
