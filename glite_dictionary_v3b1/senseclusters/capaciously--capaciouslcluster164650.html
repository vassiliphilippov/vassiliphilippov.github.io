capaciously

adverb
spaciously
Pronunciation: / kəˈpeɪʃəsli / (AmE), / kəˈpeɪʃəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows great space or capacity

Examples

Capaciously arranged room
The conference room was capaciously arranged to accommodate all the guests comfortably.
Books and decorations were capaciously displayed on the wide shelves.
The new theater is capaciously built, allowing for large audiences and easy movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrowly - in a way that is limited in space
  • synonym: spaciously - in a way that provides a lot of space
