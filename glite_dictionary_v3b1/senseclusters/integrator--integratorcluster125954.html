integrator

noun
one who unifies parts
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntəˌɡreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɪntɪɡreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings different parts together to function as a whole

Examples

Project integrator
The integrator played a key role in merging the two departments into one team.
As a system integrator, she made sure all the components worked together seamlessly.
A good integrator can help different groups collaborate more effectively on a project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coordinator - a person who organizes people or groups so that they work together efficiently
  • antonym: divider - a person or thing that separates or keeps things apart
  • synonym: unifier - someone who brings things or people together into a single group or system
