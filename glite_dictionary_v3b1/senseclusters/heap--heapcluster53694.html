heap

verb
give in large amounts
5.2
Pronunciation: / hip / (AmE), / hiːp / (BrE)
to give or deliver something in large or excessive amounts, often used with objects like praise, scorn, work, etc.

Examples

To heap blame
After the accident, the media heaped blame on the organizers.
The critics heaped praise on the actor for his remarkable performance.
They heaped gifts upon the winner to celebrate her achievement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pile - to give or put things in a large quantity or pile; to load heavily
