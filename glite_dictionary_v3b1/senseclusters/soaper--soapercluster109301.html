soaper

noun
informal for soap opera
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpə / (BrE)
an informal term for a soap opera, a television or radio series with ongoing dramatic stories about personal relationships

Examples

A popular soaper
She never misses her favorite soaper that airs every weekday evening.
Many people get hooked on a soaper because of its dramatic storylines.
He joked that his life felt like a real-life soaper sometimes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soap opera - a serialized drama focusing on domestic and emotional situations
