mile

noun
Swedish unit of 10 kilometers
2.8
Pronunciation: / maɪl / (AmE), / maɪl / (BrE)
(in Sweden) a unit of length equal to 10 kilometers, used in Swedish measurement.

Examples

A Swedish mile
In Sweden, a mile is commonly understood to mean ten kilometers, not the standard English mile.
She ran a mile in Sweden and was surprised to learn it was much longer than she expected.
The race covered a distance of two Swedish miles, which is about twenty kilometers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mile - a unit of length equal to 1,760 yards or approximately 1,609 meters (standard mile)
