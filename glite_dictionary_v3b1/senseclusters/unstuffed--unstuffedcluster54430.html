unstuffed

adjective
not full after eating
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstʌft / (AmE), / ʌnˈstʌft / (BrE)
not feeling full or satisfied after eating; not having eaten enough to feel satiated.

Examples

Still unstuffed after dinner
Even after the main course, she still felt unstuffed and wanted dessert.
He left the table unstuffed, wishing he had ordered a larger meal.
If you are unstuffed after dinner, you can always have a snack later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hungry - feeling the need or desire to eat
  • antonym: satiated - having had enough to eat; fully satisfied
  • antonym: stuffed - feeling very full after eating
