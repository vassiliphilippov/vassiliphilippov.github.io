unhoped-for

adjective
unexpected; not even imagined
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhoʊpt fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈhəʊpt fɔː / (BrE)
not expected or not even imagined as possible; unexpected, especially in a positive sense

Examples

An unhoped-for result
Her recovery after the accident was truly unhoped-for and amazed all the doctors.
The explorers discovered an unhoped-for passage through the mountains, making their journey much easier.
Winning the lottery was an unhoped-for stroke of fortune for the struggling family.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hoped-for - desired or anticipated
  • synonym: unexpected - not expected or anticipated
