glass dome

noun
transparent dome-shaped glass structure
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs doʊm / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs dəʊm / (BrE)
a transparent, dome-shaped structure made of glass, often used to cover and protect an area or object

Examples

A glass dome cover
He placed the antique clock beneath a glass dome to protect it from dust.
The botanical garden is housed under a stunning glass dome that lets sunlight pour in.
The observatory's glass dome slid open to reveal the night sky.
