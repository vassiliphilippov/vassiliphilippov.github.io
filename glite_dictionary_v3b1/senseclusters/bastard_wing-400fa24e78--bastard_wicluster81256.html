bastard wing

noun
tuft of small feathers on a bird's wing digit
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstərd wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd wɪŋ / (BrE)
a small, stiff tuft of feathers on the first digit of a bird's wing, also known as the alula
