Flying Dutchman

noun
legendary ghost captain of a phantom ship
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈdʌtʃ.mən/ (AmE), /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈdʌtʃ.mən/ (BrE)
a legendary ghost ship and its captain, doomed to sail the seas forever as a curse; often seen as an omen by sailors

Examples

The Flying Dutchman legend
Legends say the Flying Dutchman can never make port and is cursed to roam the seas for eternity.

The legend of the Flying Dutchman has inspired countless novels and operas.
