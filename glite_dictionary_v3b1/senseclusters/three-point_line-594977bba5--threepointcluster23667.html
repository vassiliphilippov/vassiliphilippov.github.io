three-point line

noun
basketball court line for three-point shots
Pronunciation: / θriˈpɔɪnt laɪn / (AmE), / θriːˈpɔɪnt laɪn / (BrE)
the arc-shaped line on a basketball court beyond which a successful shot scores three points

Examples

The three-point line on court
High school and professional courts have different distances for the three-point line.
She made an incredible shot from behind the three-point line just before the buzzer.
The defense pressured him as he stood near the three-point line looking for an open teammate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basketball court - the playing surface for basketball games
  • synonym: three-pointer - a basketball shot made from beyond the three-point line
