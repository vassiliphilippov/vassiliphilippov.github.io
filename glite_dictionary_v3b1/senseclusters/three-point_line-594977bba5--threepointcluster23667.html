three-point line

noun
basketball court line for three-point shots
3.6 2.6
Pronunciation: /θriː pɔɪnt laɪn/ (AmE), /θriː pɔɪnt laɪn/ (BrE)
the arc-shaped line on a basketball court beyond which a successful shot scores three points
