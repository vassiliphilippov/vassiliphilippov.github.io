attribute

noun
quality or characteristic of someone or something
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈætrɪˌbjut /; / əˈtrɪbjut / (AmE), / ˈætrɪˌbjuːt /; / əˈtrɪbjuːt / (BrE)
a quality, feature, or characteristic that belongs to or identifies someone or something

Examples

A key attribute
Honesty is an important attribute for any leader.
Patience is a valuable attribute when working with children.
The painting's most striking attribute is its vibrant use of color.
Honesty is a valued attribute in a friend.
One attribute that sets her apart is her remarkable creativity.
Patience is an important attribute for anyone working in customer service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: characteristic - a typical feature or quality that makes something different
  • synonym: feature - a distinctive attribute or aspect of something
  • synonym: property - a quality or feature that belongs to something, especially a physical object
  • synonym: trait - a distinguishing quality, typically of a person
