vocationally

adverb
in relation to vocational training or occupation
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to training or education for a specific occupation or trade

Examples

To be vocationally trained
He is vocationally oriented and prefers hands-on training over academic study.
Many students in the program are vocationally trained to enter the workforce directly after graduation.
The school is focused on preparing students vocationally for careers in technology and engineering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: professionally - in a professional manner; relating to a profession
