crape

noun
a very thin pancake
7.9
Pronunciation: / kreɪp / (AmE), / kreɪp / (BrE)
A very thin pancake, typically made from wheat flour, eggs, and milk, and served with sweet or savoury fillings.

Examples

A sweet crape
He learned to make the perfect crape during his trip to Paris.
She ordered a chocolate crape filled with strawberries for dessert.
The cafe specializes in savory crapes with cheese and ham.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crêpe - A very thin pancake; standard French spelling for the dish
