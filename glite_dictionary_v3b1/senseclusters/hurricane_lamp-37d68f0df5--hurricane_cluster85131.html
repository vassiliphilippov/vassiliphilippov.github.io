hurricane lamp

noun
lamp with chimney protecting flame from wind
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝː.ɪ.keɪn læmp / (AmE), / ˈhʌr.ɪ.kən læmp / (BrE)
an oil lamp fitted with a glass chimney and a perforated metal lid to shield the flame from wind

Examples

A hurricane lamp light
During the storm, they relied on a hurricane lamp for light.
She cleaned the glass chimney of the hurricane lamp before lighting it.
The old cabin was lit by a single hurricane lamp hanging from the ceiling.
