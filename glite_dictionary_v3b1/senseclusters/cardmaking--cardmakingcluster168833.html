cardmaking

noun
making greeting cards
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of making decorative greeting cards by hand, either as a hobby or for sale

Examples

Cardmaking hobby
Many people find cardmaking to be a creative and relaxing pastime.
She started cardmaking to give personalized birthday cards to her friends.
There are online communities where enthusiasts share their cardmaking designs and techniques.
She started cardmaking as a hobby and now sells her handmade cards at local markets.
Many people enjoy cardmaking because it allows them to express creativity and make personalized gifts.
The art supply store offers workshops on cardmaking for beginners and experienced crafters alike.
