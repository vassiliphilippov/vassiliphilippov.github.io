pleuritic

adjective
of pleura inflammation
9.6
Pronunciation: / plʊˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / plʊəˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by inflammation of the pleura, the membrane surrounding the lungs

Examples

Pleuritic chest pain
The patient complained of pleuritic chest pain that worsened with deep breathing.
Doctors often check for pleuritic symptoms when diagnosing respiratory illnesses.
A pleuritic cough can be a sign of inflammation in the lining of the lungs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleurisy - inflammation of the pleura causing chest pain
