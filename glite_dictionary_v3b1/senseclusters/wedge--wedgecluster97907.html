wedge

noun
large sandwich on a long roll (US regional)
Pronunciation: / wɛdʒ / (AmE), / wɛdʒ / (BrE)
(US, regional) A large sandwich made from a long crusty roll split lengthwise and filled with meats, cheese, and often vegetables and condiments; known by different names in various regions.

Examples

A turkey wedge
At the deli, I ordered a turkey wedge with all the classic toppings.
For lunch, we shared a gigantic Italian wedge and a bag of chips.
Some people call it a sub, but in Westchester it's always a wedge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - a large sandwich made of a long roll; New York regional term
  • synonym: hoagie - a large sandwich made of a long roll split lengthwise and filled with various ingredients; US regional term
  • synonym: sub - a sandwich made from a long roll, filled with meats, cheese, and vegetables; US regional term
