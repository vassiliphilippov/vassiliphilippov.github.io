elflike

adjective
small and delicate like an elf
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlf.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛlf.laɪk / (BrE)
Having a small, delicate, and graceful appearance, resembling that of an elf.

Examples

A elflike face
He described the small, elflike creature he saw in the garden.
The dancer moved with an elflike grace, almost floating across the stage.
With her elflike features and bright eyes, she captivated everyone in the room.
