subflooring

noun
support layer under floor
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌflɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌflɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer of material placed under the finished floor to provide support and a base for flooring materials

Examples

The subflooring layer
The builders checked the subflooring for damage before installing the new carpet.
Water leaks can cause the subflooring to rot if not repaired quickly.
After removing the old tiles, they discovered the subflooring needed to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: flooring - the finished surface layer that people walk on
  • synonym: underlayment - a material installed beneath flooring for support or insulation
