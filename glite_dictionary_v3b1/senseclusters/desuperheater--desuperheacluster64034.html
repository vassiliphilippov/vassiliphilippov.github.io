desuperheater

noun
steam cooling device
Pronunciation: / diːˈsuːpərˌhiːtər / (AmE), / diːˈsuːpəhˌhiːtə / (BrE)
a device used to lower the temperature of superheated steam by injecting water into it

Examples

A desuperheater device
The engineer installed a desuperheater to ensure the steam temperature stayed within safe limits.
A desuperheater helps protect equipment by cooling superheated steam before it enters the system.
Regular maintenance of the desuperheater is essential for efficient plant operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condenser - a device that turns steam or vapor into liquid
  • antonym: superheater - a device that increases the temperature of steam above its boiling point
