functionalistic

adjective
focused on functions
8.4
Pronunciation: /fʌŋk.ʃən.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /fʌŋk.ʃə.nə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /fʌŋk.ʃən.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /fʌŋk.ʃə.nə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or based on the theory that emphasizes the functions or roles of parts within a system

Examples

Functionalistic theory approach
The sociologist adopted a functionalistic perspective to analyze how education supports society.
A functionalistic design values the purpose of each component in the system.
Her research takes a functionalistic approach to understanding family roles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: structuralist - relating to a theory emphasizing structure over function
