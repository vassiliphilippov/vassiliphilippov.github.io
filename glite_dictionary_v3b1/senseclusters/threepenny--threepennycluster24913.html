threepenny

adjective
having a size of threepenny (nail)
5.4 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈθriː.pə.nɪ/ /; / /ˈθrʌ.pə.nɪ/ (AmE), /ˈθriː.pə.nɪ/ /; / /ˈθrʌ.pə.nɪ/ (BrE)
(of a nail) having a size designated as threepenny, typically 1 1/4 inches long.

Examples

Threepenny nail

The carpenter asked for a box of threepenny nails to finish the trim.
