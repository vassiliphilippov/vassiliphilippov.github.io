unretentive

adjective
having poor memory retention
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈtɛntɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈtɛntɪv / (BrE)
(of a person's memory) not good at retaining or remembering things; lacking the ability to remember.

Examples

Unretentive memory
Although he tries to recall names, his unretentive memory makes it difficult.
Because his memory was unretentive, he often forgot important appointments.
She found her unretentive mind struggled with learning new vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forgetful - tending to forget; having a bad memory
  • antonym: retentive - having the ability to remember easily; good at remembering
