psoriatic

noun
person with psoriasis
Pronunciation: / ˌsɔːriˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɒriˈætɪk / (BrE)
a person who is affected by psoriasis, a chronic skin condition

Examples

A psoriatic patient
A psoriatic may need to use special creams to manage their symptoms.
Support groups can help a psoriatic cope with the emotional impact of the disease.
Doctors often advise a psoriatic to avoid known triggers like stress or certain foods.
