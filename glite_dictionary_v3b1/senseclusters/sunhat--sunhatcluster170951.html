sunhat

noun
broad-brimmed hat for sun protection
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌhæt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌhæt / (BrE)
A hat with a wide brim worn to protect the face and neck from the sun.

Examples

A sunhat with brim
Don't forget your sunhat if you're going to spend the day at the beach.
She wore a large sunhat to protect herself from the harsh afternoon rays.
Wide-brimmed sunhats are especially popular during summer festivals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cap - a soft, close-fitting head covering
  • synonym: straw hat - a hat made of straw, often worn in sunny weather
