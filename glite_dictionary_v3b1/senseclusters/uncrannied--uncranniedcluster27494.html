uncrannied

adjective
having no cracks or small openings
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrænid / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrænid / (BrE)
(of a surface or structure) having no cracks or small openings; without chinks or crannies.

Examples

An uncrannied vault
Only an uncrannied vault could protect the treasures from moisture.
She admired the seamless, uncrannied finish of the sculpture.
The wall was carefully plastered to remain uncrannied through harsh winters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crannied - having small cracks or crevices
