Fukkianese

noun
Chinese dialects from Fujian province
9.9
Pronunciation: / fuːˌkiːəˈniːz / (AmE), / fuːˌkiːəˈniːz / (BrE)
any of the Chinese dialects, especially Min, spoken in Fujian (Fukien) province or by its emigrants

Examples

Fukkianese dialect
Many overseas Chinese communities in Southeast Asia speak Fukkianese at home.
She learned to write Mandarin characters but always spoke Fukkianese with her grandparents.
The shop owner greeted us in Fukkianese before switching to Mandarin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Min - a group of Chinese dialects spoken in Fujian province and nearby areas
