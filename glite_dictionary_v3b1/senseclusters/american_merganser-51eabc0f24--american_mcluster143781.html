American merganser

noun
North American diving duck
7.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən mɚˈɡænzər / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən məˈɡænzə / (BrE)
A common North American diving duck, considered a variety of the European goosander.

Examples

The American mergansers swim
Birdwatchers spotted several American mergansers on the river this morning.
The American merganser can often be seen hunting for fish in northern lakes.
Unlike other ducks, the American merganser has a slender, serrated bill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goosander - large diving duck also known as the common merganser
