optogenetic

adjective
light-controlled genetics
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑptəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk /; / ˌɑptəˌdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒptəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the use of light to control cells or organisms, usually by genetic modification, especially in scientific research
