unreached

adjective
not reached; inaccessible
7.5 0.04/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàrit Ét / (AmE), /  ånÀàriÀêt Ét / (BrE)
not reached; inaccessible or situated in a place that has not been attained or arrived at

Examples

Still unreached places
Many of the planet's deepest caves are still unreached by explorers.
The mountaintop stood unreached despite many attempts to climb it.
The remote island remains unreached by modern transportation.

Synonyms

  • unreachable
    4.9 0.75/m
    unreachable is seen as impossible or very hard to get to, while unreached often means simply not yet reached.
  • inaccessible
    7.9 3.38/m
    inaccessible cannot be entered or easily used by people, while unreached focuses on places or goals that remain unattained.
  • out of reach
    4.0 2.77/m
    out of reach means you cannot get or touch something now, while unreached describes places or aims not yet reached.
  • unvisited
    4.1 0.13/m
    unvisited means no one has been to a place, while unreached highlights that the place or person was not attained.
  • untraveled
    6.3 0.04/m
    untraveled describes roads or areas not explored by people, while unreached means they were not arrived at or attained.

  • antonym: reachable - able to be reached
