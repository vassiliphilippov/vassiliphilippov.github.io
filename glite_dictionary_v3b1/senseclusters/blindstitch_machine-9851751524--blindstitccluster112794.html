blindstitch machine

noun
sewing machine for invisible stitches
4.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ mə.ˈʃɪn/ (AmE), /ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
