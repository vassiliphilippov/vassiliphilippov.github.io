blindstitch machine

noun
sewing machine for invisible stitches
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A sewing machine designed to join two pieces of fabric with stitches that are invisible or nearly invisible on the surface.

Examples

A blindstitch machine sews fabric
Investing in a blindstitch machine can speed up professional garment production.
She prefers to finish her curtains with a blindstitch machine for a clean, polished edge.
The tailor used a blindstitch machine to hem the trousers without visible threads.
