Merina

proper noun
Malagasy dialect
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈriːnə / (AmE), / məˈriːnə / (BrE)
the main dialect of the Malagasy language spoken in Madagascar's central highlands, forming the basis of standard Malagasy

Examples

Merina dialect
Many people in Antananarivo speak the Merina dialect as their first language.
The Merina dialect is widely used in education and media across Madagascar.
