malleolus

noun
ankle bone prominence
Pronunciation: / məˈliːələs / (AmE), / məˈliːələs / (BrE)
the rounded bony prominence on either side of the human ankle

Examples

Swollen malleolus
The doctor examined the swelling around his malleolus after the fall.
A fracture of the malleolus can make walking very painful.
