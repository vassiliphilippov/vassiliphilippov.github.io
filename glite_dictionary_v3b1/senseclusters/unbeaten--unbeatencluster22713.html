unbeaten

adjective
not defeated or conquered
1.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbitn̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbiːtn̩ / (BrE)
not having been defeated or conquered; not overcome in a contest or conflict.

Examples

An unbeaten team
Despite facing many enemies, the city stood unbeaten for centuries.
Her record is still unbeaten after ten years of fierce competition.
The team remained unbeaten throughout the entire season, winning every match they played.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conquered - having been overcome or defeated
  • synonym: undefeated - not having been beaten; never lost in competition
