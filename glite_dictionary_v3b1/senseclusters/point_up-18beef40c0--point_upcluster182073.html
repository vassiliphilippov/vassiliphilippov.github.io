point up

verb
emphasize or highlight something
3.7
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt ʌp / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ʌp / (BrE)
to emphasize or make something more noticeable, especially by highlighting or drawing attention to it

Examples

To point up risks
Her quick reaction pointed up the risks of ignoring safety procedures.
The new study points up several issues with the current approach.
The report points up the urgent need for reform in the system.
Her comments pointed up the main issues we need to address.
Several mistakes in the report point up the need for better training.
The investigation helped point up the weaknesses in their security system.

  • synonym: emphasize - to give special importance or prominence to something
  • synonym: highlight - to emphasize or make something stand out
