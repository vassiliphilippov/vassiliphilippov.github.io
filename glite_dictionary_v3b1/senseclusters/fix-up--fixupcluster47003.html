fix-up

noun
arranged date
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪksˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈfɪksˌʌp / (BrE)
an arrangement where two people are introduced, usually with the hope that they will start a romantic relationship

Examples

A fix-up date
She was nervous about the fix-up her friend had arranged for her on Friday night.
After their fix-up, they discovered they had a lot in common and started dating.
He agreed to the fix-up even though he usually preferred to meet people naturally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blind date - a romantic meeting between two people who have not met before
  • synonym: set-up - an arrangement to introduce two people for a romantic purpose
