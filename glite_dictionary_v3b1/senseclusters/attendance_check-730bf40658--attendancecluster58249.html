attendance check

noun
checking who is present (in class)
1.9
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛn.dəns tʃɛk / (AmE), / əˈtɛn.dəns tʃɛk / (BrE)
an act of calling names to see which students are present in a classroom or at an event

Examples

An attendance check in class
Every morning, an attendance check is conducted before lessons start.
Please arrive on time, as the attendance check is taken promptly at nine.
The teacher began the class with an attendance check to see who was missing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: register - an official list or record of names
  • synonym: roll call - the act of reading aloud a list of names to check attendance
