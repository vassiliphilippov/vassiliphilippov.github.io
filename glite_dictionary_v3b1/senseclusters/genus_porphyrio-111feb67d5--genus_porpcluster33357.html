genus Porphyrio

noun
a genus of purple gallinule birds
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs pɔːrˈfɪr.i.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs pɔːˈfɪə.rɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
