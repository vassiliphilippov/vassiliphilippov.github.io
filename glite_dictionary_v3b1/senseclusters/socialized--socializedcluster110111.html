socialized

adjective
under group or government control
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃə.laɪzd /; / ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃə.laɪzd /; / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a service, industry, or institution) brought under group or government ownership or control for the benefit of society

  • antonym: privatized - (of a service or industry) transferred from public to private ownership or control
