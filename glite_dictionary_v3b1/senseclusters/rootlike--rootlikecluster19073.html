rootlike

adjective
resembling a root
Pronunciation: / ˈruːtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈruːtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a root, especially in shape or function

Examples

Rootlike tendrils
The plant developed rootlike tendrils that helped it cling to the rocks.
Some fungi produce rootlike structures to absorb nutrients from the soil.
The coral's rootlike base anchors it firmly to the ocean floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rooted - having roots or being firmly fixed like a root
