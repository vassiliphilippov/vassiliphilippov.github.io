lakeshore

noun
the edge or shore of a lake
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪkˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈleɪkˌʃɔː / (BrE)
the land along the edge of a lake

Examples

The lakeshore land
After swimming, they relaxed on the sandy lakeshore and watched the boats go by.
Cottages lined the quiet lakeshore, offering a peaceful retreat for visitors.
We enjoyed a picnic on the lakeshore as the sun set behind the hills.
