downstate

noun
southern part of state
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪt / (BrE)
the southern or less urban part of a state, or the people living in that region

Examples

Life in downstate
Many people from the city travel to downstate for a quieter lifestyle on weekends.
The university attracts students from both downstate and upstate regions.
Farming is a major industry in downstate due to its open land and mild climate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countryside - rural areas outside cities and towns
  • antonym: upstate - the northern or more rural part of a state
