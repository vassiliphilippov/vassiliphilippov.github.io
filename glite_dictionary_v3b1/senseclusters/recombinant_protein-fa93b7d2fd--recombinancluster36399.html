recombinant protein

noun
protein made by genetic engineering
5.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑːmbɪnənt ˈproʊtin / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒmbɪnənt ˈprəʊtiːn / (BrE)
a protein produced by genetic engineering, using recombinant DNA technology

Examples

Recombinant protein production
Insulin is now often produced as a recombinant protein using bacteria.
Scientists use recombinant protein technology to create vaccines for various diseases.
The study analyzed the activity of the recombinant protein in cell cultures.
