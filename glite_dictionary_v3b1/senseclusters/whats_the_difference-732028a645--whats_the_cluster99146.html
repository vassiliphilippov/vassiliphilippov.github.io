what's the difference

x
asking for distinction or importance of difference
Pronunciation: / wʌts ðə ˈdɪfərəns / (AmE), / wɒts ðə ˈdɪfərəns / (BrE)
used to ask about the distinction or variation between two or more things, often implying the distinction may not matter

Examples

What's the difference between models
What's the difference between this model and the old one?
I don't see what's the difference if we eat here or at home.
She shrugged and said, 'what's the difference? They're both delicious.'
