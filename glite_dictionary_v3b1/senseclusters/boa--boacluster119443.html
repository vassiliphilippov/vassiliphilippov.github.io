boa

noun
large tropical constrictor snake
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A large, chiefly tropical, non-venomous constrictor snake, often with vestigial hind limbs, belonging to the family Boidae.

Examples

A giant boa snake
A boa can suffocate its prey by constriction before swallowing it whole.
She was fascinated by the spotted pattern on the boa at the reptile house.
The boa slowly wrapped itself around the branch, blending into the rainforest foliage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: python - large non-venomous constrictor snake of the family Pythonidae
