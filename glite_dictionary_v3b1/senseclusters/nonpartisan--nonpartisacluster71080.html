nonpartisan

noun
person with no political party affiliation
5.1 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpɑrtɪzən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpɑːtɪzən / (BrE)
a person who does not support or belong to any political party or group

Examples

A true nonpartisan
As a nonpartisan, she prefers to evaluate policies on their merits rather than party loyalty.
Many voters identify as nonpartisan because they do not feel represented by any major party.
The debate was moderated by a nonpartisan to ensure fairness for all candidates.
