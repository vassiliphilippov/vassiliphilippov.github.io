patterning

noun
precise layout process
Pronunciation: / ˈpætərnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpætənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of creating precise arrangements or layouts on a surface, especially in manufacturing or technology

Examples

Chip patterning
Modern chip factories use extreme ultraviolet light for precise patterning of circuits on silicon wafers.
The patterning of textiles requires careful control to ensure each design is consistent.
Advances in patterning technology have enabled the production of smaller and more powerful electronic devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrangement - the act of organizing or placing things in a particular order
  • synonym: etching - the process of using chemicals to cut designs into a surface
