amateurism

noun
participation in activities for pleasure, not profit
8.6 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.mə.tʃɜːr.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈæ.mə.tə.rɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈæ.mə.tə.rɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈæ.mə.tʃə.rɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the belief or practice of participating in sports, the arts, or other activities for pleasure rather than for financial gain or professional status.

Examples

Amateurism in sports
He defended amateurism, insisting that sports should not be influenced by money.
Some critics argue that amateurism preserves the purity of sports.
The Olympic Games were once strictly governed by a spirit of amateurism.
