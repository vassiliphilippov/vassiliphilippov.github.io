God the Father

proper noun
Christian concept of God as Creator and Father
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd ðə ˈfɑðɚ / (AmE), / ɡɒd ðə ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
In Christian theology, the first person of the Trinity; God conceived as the creator and father of all.

Examples

God the Father in Trinity
Belief in God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit forms the doctrine of the Trinity.
Many prayers in Christian tradition are addressed to God the Father as the creator of all things.
She found comfort in her faith, trusting in God the Father during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: God the Holy Spirit - the third person of the Trinity
  • synonym: God the Son - the second person of the Trinity, referring to Jesus Christ
