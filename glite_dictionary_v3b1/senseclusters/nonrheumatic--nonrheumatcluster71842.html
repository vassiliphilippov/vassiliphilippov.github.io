nonrheumatic

adjective
not related to rheumatism
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnruˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnruːˈmatɪk / (BrE)
not caused by or related to rheumatism or rheumatic disease

Examples

Nonrheumatic condition
The doctor confirmed that the condition was nonrheumatic and unrelated to any previous joint issues.
She was relieved to learn her heart problem was nonrheumatic in origin.
Researchers are studying the causes of nonrheumatic fever in children.
