leg

noun
stage or segment of a journey
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ / (AmE), / lɛɡ / (BrE)
a stage or section of a journey, race, or course, especially between two points

Examples

The final leg of the race
After completing the toughest leg of the race, the runners stopped for a rest.
The final leg of the journey will be by boat across the lake.
The first leg of our trip took us from Paris to Rome.
After tacking east for several hours, they finished the longest leg of the race.
The first leg of our voyage took us across the open sea.
We completed each leg of the course with careful navigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: segment - a distinct part cut off from a larger whole, especially in relation to journeys
  • synonym: stage - a part of a journey or process
