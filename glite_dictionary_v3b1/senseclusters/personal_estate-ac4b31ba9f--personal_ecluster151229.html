personal estate

noun
movable property (not land or buildings)
6.1 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…úr.s…ôn.…ôl …™Ààste…™t / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.s…ôn.…ôl …™Ààste…™t / (BrE)
movable property owned by an individual, as opposed to real estate such as land or buildings.

Examples

The personal estate of a person
Debts must be paid from the personal estate before the remainder is distributed to heirs.
The inventory includes everything from vehicles to jewelry as part of the personal estate.
Under her will, all of her personal estate was left to her grandchildren.

Synonyms

  • personal property
    3.1 1.36/m
    personal property names everyday movable belongings like furniture and jewelry, while personal estate is the formal legal term for the same movable goods.
  • personalty
    9.9 0.01/m
    personalty is a strict legal term for movable possessions, while personal estate is a formal phrase for those same items.
  • private property
    2.8 4.64/m
    private property refers to things owned privately and stresses ownership, while personal estate specifically names movable goods not land.
