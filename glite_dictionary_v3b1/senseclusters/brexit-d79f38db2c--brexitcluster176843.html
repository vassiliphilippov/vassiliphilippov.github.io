Brexit

verb
to leave the EU
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛksɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrɛksɪt / (BrE)
to cause or undergo the withdrawal of the United Kingdom from the European Union, or to leave an organization in a similar manner
