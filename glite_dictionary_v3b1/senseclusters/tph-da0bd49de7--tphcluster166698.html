TPH

sym
petroleum hydrocarbon measure
Pronunciation: / ti pi eɪtʃ / (AmE), / tiː piː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for total petroleum hydrocarbons, a group of chemical compounds found in petroleum used as a measure of contamination in environmental science

Examples

TPH in water
The laboratory analyzed the water sample for TPH to assess pollution levels.
Environmental regulations require regular monitoring of TPH in industrial waste.
High TPH readings in the soil indicated a recent oil spill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contaminant - a substance that makes something impure or polluted
  • synonym: hydrocarbon - an organic compound consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon
