lignocellulosic

adjective
plant fiber material
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪɡnoʊˌsɛljəˈloʊsɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɪɡnəʊˌsɛljʊˈlɒsɪk / (BrE)
relating to plant material composed of cellulose, hemicellulose, and lignin

Examples

Lignocellulosic plant material
Scientists are studying lignocellulosic materials to produce renewable biofuels.
The factory processes lignocellulosic biomass into valuable chemicals.
Many agricultural wastes are considered lignocellulosic because they contain both lignin and cellulose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cellulosic - relating to or made from cellulose
  • synonym: woody - consisting of or resembling wood
