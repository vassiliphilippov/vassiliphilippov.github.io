decry

verb
express strong public disapproval
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkraɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈkraɪ / (BrE)
to publicly express strong disapproval of or criticize (something or someone)

Examples

To decry poor funding
Many experts decry the lack of funding for public education in the country.
She has always decried violence as a means to resolve political disputes.
The senator did not hesitate to decry the policy as unfair and damaging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condemn - to express complete disapproval of, typically in public
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration of
