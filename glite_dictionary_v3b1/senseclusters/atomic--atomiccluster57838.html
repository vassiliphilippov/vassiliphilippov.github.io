atomic

adjective
relating to energy from atoms (weapons)
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑmɪk / (AmE), / əˈtɒmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using the energy released from atoms, especially in weapons or bombs

Examples

Atomic bomb
Many nations signed treaties to limit their atomic weapons arsenals.
The devastation caused by an atomic explosion is immense and long-lasting.
The threat of an atomic bomb terrified the world during the Cold War.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuclear - relating to nuclei or the energy released from nuclear reactions, especially in weapons or power
