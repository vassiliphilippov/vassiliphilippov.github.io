combing

noun
removed hairs or fibers
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊmɪŋ / (BrE)
hairs or fibers that have been removed by combing, often collected from a brush or comb

Examples

Dog combing leftovers
After brushing her dog, she threw away the combing left in the brush.
The hairdresser swept up the combing from the salon floor at the end of the day.
Some people use the combing from their hairbrush to make crafts or test for hair health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shedding - the process of losing hair or fur naturally
