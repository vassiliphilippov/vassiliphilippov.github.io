simulant

noun
imitation material
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmjələnt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmjʊlənt / (BrE)
a substance or material made to imitate another, especially for scientific or testing purposes

Examples

Lunar soil simulant
The scientists used a lunar soil simulant to test the new equipment.
Cubic zirconia is a common diamond simulant in jewelry.
Before launching the mission, engineers practiced with a Mars rock simulant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitation - something made to look like another thing
  • antonym: original - the thing that is being imitated or copied
  • synonym: replica - an exact copy or model of something
