fingerpaint

noun
jelly-like paint used with fingers
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌpeɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌpeɪnt / (BrE)
A type of paint with a jelly-like consistency, designed to be spread with the fingers, especially by children.

Examples

Children's fingerpaint
Make sure the fingerpaint is non-toxic before giving it to toddlers.
Red and blue fingerpaint stained his hands after the project.
The children covered the paper with bright fingerpaint during art class.
