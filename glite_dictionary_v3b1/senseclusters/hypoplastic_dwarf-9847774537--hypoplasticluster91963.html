hypoplastic dwarf

noun
person with genetic short stature and normal development
8.7 9.0
Pronunciation: / haɪpəˌplæstɪk ˈdwɔrf / (AmE), / haɪpəˌplæstɪk ˈdwɔːf / (BrE)
A person of unusually small stature whose body parts and mental and sexual development are normal, due to a genetic condition causing underdevelopment of bones; especially one with achondroplasia.

Examples

Hypoplastic dwarf condition
Research into the genetic causes of hypoplastic dwarf conditions has led to new therapies.
The doctor explained that the patient was a hypoplastic dwarf with normal intellectual development.
Unlike other forms of dwarfism, a hypoplastic dwarf may have normal proportions and sexual development.

  • synonym: achondroplastic dwarf - person with short stature due to a specific bone growth disorder
