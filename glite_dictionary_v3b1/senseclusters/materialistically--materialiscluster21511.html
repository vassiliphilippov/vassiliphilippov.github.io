materialistically

adverb
in a materialistic way
1.0
Pronunciation: / məˌtɪriəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / məˌtɪə.ri.əˈlɪs.tɪk.li / (BrE)
In a way that is overly concerned with material possessions or wealth rather than spiritual or intellectual values.

Examples

Think materialistically about money
He tends to view success materialistically, focusing more on possessions than personal growth.
Some people approach relationships materialistically, valuing gifts over emotional connection.
The company was criticized for operating materialistically at the expense of ethical considerations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: materialistic - overly concerned with material possessions
