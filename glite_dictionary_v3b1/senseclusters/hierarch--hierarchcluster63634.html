hierarch

noun
person of high rank in a hierarchy
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪərˌɑrk /; / ˈhaɪəˌrɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərɑːk / (BrE)
a person who holds a high position in a hierarchy, especially in a religious or organizational context

Examples

Several senior hierarchs gathered for the important synod.
The Orthodox hierarch presided over the ceremony with great solemnity.
