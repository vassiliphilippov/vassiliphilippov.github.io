Jesu

proper noun
archaic form of Jesus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːzuː / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪzjuː / (BrE)

Examples

Sweet Jesu

Many poets used Jesu in their verses to express deep faith.
