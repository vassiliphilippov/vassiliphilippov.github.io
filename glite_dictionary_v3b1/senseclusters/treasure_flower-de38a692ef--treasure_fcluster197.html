treasure flower

noun
South African ornamental plant with bright orange flowers
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛʒər ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A South African perennial plant with short, densely leafy stems and orange flower rays marked with black eyespots at the base, commonly grown for ornamental purposes.

Examples

The treasure flower plant
Because it tolerates drought, the treasure flower is popular in xeriscaping designs.
The treasure flower adds a bright splash of color to many gardens.
We planted treasure flowers along the garden path for their vibrant blooms.
