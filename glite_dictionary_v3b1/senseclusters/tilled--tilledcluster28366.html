tilled

adjective
prepared land for planting
Pronunciation: / tɪld / (AmE), / tɪld / (BrE)
(of land) prepared for planting by plowing, harrowing, or hoeing.

Examples

Tilled farmland
After the fields were tilled, the farmers began sowing seeds.
Only tilled soil will provide a good environment for young plants.
The garden looked neat and tilled after hours of hard work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: untilled - (of land) not prepared for planting
