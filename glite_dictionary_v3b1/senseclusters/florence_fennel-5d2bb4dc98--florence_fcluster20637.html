Florence fennel

noun
aromatic edible stem base (vegetable)
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔr.əns ˈfɛn.əl /; / ˈflɔːrəns ˈfɛnəl / (AmE), / ˈflɒr.əns ˈfɛn.əl /; / ˈflɒrəns ˈfɛnəl / (BrE)
a type of fennel with a swollen, aromatic bulbous stem base that is eaten as a vegetable, either cooked or raw
