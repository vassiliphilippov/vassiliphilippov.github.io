structuration

noun
process of organizing
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌstrʌktʃəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌstrʌktʃəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of organizing or arranging parts into a unified or coherent structure or pattern

Examples

Structuration of ideas
The structuration of the curriculum made the course easier to follow.
Effective structuration of the team led to better communication and results.
The structuration of data is essential for efficient information retrieval.
The sociologist analyzed the structuration of communities in rapidly growing cities.
Effective structuration of the curriculum helps students understand complex subjects more easily.
The structuration of the new company allowed for better communication between departments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorder - a lack of order or organization
  • antonym: disorganization - the state of lacking order or structure
  • synonym: organization - the act of arranging or structuring things systematically
  • synonym: structure - the arrangement of and relations between the parts of something complex
