banker's dozen

noun
thirteen items given instead of twelve
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkərz ˈdʌzən / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkəz ˈdʌzən / (BrE)
Thirteen items, especially given instead of twelve as an extra, often in sales or special offers.

Examples

A banker's dozen of cookies
For their anniversary sale, the store offered every customer a banker's dozen of cookies.
The promotion includes a banker's dozen of locally grown apples for the price of twelve.
You often get a banker's dozen when buying items in bulk during holiday sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baker's dozen - thirteen items, especially a group of thirteen baked goods
