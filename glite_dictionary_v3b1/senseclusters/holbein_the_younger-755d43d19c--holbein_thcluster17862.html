Holbein the Younger

proper noun
German portrait painter of the English court
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlbaɪn ðə ˈjʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈhɒlbaɪn ðə ˈjʌŋə / (BrE)
(1497–1543) German painter and engraver renowned for his remarkable portraits, especially those of Henry VIII and the English court.
