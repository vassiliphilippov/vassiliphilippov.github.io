sobole

noun
plant shoot or sprout
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊboʊl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbəʊl / (BrE)
a single shoot or sprout that grows from the base of a plant, often forming a new stem or branch

Examples

A sobole shoot
A sobole appeared at the foot of the ancient oak after the spring rains.
Gardeners often remove each sobole to encourage the main stem to grow stronger.
The botanist studied the sobole to understand how the plant reproduced vegetatively.
