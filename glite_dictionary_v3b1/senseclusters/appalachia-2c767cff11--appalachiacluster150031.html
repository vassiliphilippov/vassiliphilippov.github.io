Appalachia

proper noun
region in eastern U.S. linked to Appalachian Mountains
6.8
Pronunciation: /æ.pə.ˈlætʃ.iː.ə/ /; / /æ.pə.ˈleɪ.tʃə/ /; / /æ.pə.ˈlætʃ.ə/ (AmE), /æ.pə.ˈleɪ.tʃə/ /; / /æ.pə.ˈlætʃ.ə/ (BrE)
