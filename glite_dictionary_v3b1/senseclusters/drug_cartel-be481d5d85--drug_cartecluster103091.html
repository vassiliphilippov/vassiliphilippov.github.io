drug cartel

noun
organized group controlling illegal drug trade
1.0
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ kɑrˈtɛl / (AmE), / drʌɡ kɑːˈtɛl / (BrE)
an organized group that illegally controls the production and distribution of narcotic drugs, often using violence and corruption to maintain its operations.

Examples

A drug cartel leader
He was accused of laundering money for a notorious drug cartel.
Rival drug cartels frequently clash over territory and distribution routes.
The police made several arrests as they tried to dismantle the drug cartel operating in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartel - an association of manufacturers or suppliers that maintains prices and restricts competition
