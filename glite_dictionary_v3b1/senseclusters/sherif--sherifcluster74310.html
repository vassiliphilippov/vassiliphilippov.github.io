sherif

noun
law officer or noble
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈrɪf / (AmE), / ʃəˈrɪf / (BrE)
a variant spelling of 'sheriff' (a law officer) or 'sharif' (a Muslim noble or descendant of Muhammad), used in some historical or regional contexts

Examples

The sherif kept order
The sherif maintained order in the town during a difficult period.
As a respected sherif, he was both a leader and a judge in the community.
The title of sherif was passed down through generations in his family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sharif - a Muslim noble, especially a descendant of Muhammad
  • synonym: sheriff - an official responsible for law enforcement in a county or region
