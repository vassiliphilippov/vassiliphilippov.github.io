S

noun
unit of electric conductance (siemens)
7.5
Pronunciation: / siː / (AmE), / siː / (BrE)
a unit of electric conductance equal to one siemens, which is the reciprocal of an ohm

Examples

3 S conductance
A value of 3 S indicates higher conductance than 1 S.
The instrument measured the sample's conductance as 1 S.
The resistance of the circuit was 2 ohms, so its conductance was 0.5 S.
