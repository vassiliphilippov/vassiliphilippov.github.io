generate

verb
produce or supply
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnəreɪt / (BrE)
to produce, create, or provide something as a result of a process or action

Examples

Generate a profit
Her investments generate a steady income every month.
The factory generates enough electricity to power the entire region.
The new marketing strategy generated a lot of interest in the product.
Solar panels can generate enough electricity to power a whole house.
The announcement generated much excitement among fans around the world.
This app will generate a report based on your recent activity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consume - to use up (resource, supply)
  • antonym: destroy - to put an end to the existence of something by damaging or attacking it
  • synonym: produce - to make or bring forth
  • synonym: yield - to produce or provide (a natural, agricultural, or industrial product)
