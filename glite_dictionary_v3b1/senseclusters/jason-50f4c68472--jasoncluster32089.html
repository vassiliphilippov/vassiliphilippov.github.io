Jason

proper noun
hero in Greek mythology; leader of the Argonauts
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪsən / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪsən / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the leader of the Argonauts and husband of Medea, famous for his quest to obtain the Golden Fleece.
