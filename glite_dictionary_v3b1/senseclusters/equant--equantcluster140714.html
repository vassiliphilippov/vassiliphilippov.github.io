equant

adjective
nearly equal dimensions
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàiÀêkw…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêkw…ônt / (BrE)
having nearly equal dimensions in all directions, especially referring to crystals, grains, or particles that are almost equidimensional

Examples

Equant crystal shape
The mineral sample was classified as equant because its length, width, and height were almost the same.
Under the microscope, the sand grains appeared equant rather than elongated or flattened.
Geologists often describe crystals as equant when they are nearly spherical in shape.
