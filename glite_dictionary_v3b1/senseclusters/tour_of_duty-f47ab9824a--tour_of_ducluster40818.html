tour of duty

noun
period of military service at a posting
1.9 6.0
Pronunciation: / tʊr əv ˈduːti / (AmE), / tʊə əv ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
a period of time spent by someone, especially a member of the military, working in a particular place or role before being transferred or given leave

Examples

A tour of duty overseas
After his tour of duty overseas, he returned home to his family.
Each soldier must complete a tour of duty before being eligible for leave.
Sergeant Miller’s tour of duty in Afghanistan lasted for twelve months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deployment - the movement of troops to a place for military action
  • antonym: leave - period when someone in the military is permitted to be away from duty
