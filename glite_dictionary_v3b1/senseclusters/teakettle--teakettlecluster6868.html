teakettle

noun
kettle for boiling water for tea
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːˌkɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌkɛtl̩ / (BrE)
a kettle, usually with a spout and handle, used for boiling water, especially for making tea

Examples

A teakettle on stove
Please wash the teakettle before using it for the first time.
She put the teakettle on the stove to boil some water for tea.
The old-fashioned teakettle whistled loudly when the water was ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kettle - a container for boiling water or cooking food over a fire
