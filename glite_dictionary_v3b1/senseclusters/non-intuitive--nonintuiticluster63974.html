non-intuitive

adjective
not obvious
8.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ɪn.ˈtuː.ɪ.tɪv/ /; / /nɑːn.ɪn.ˈtjuː.ɪ.tɪv/ (AmE), /nɒn.ɪn.ˈtjuː.ɪ.tɪv/ /; / /nɒn.ɪn.ˈtʃuː.ɪ.tɪv/ (BrE)
not easy to understand, use, or predict because it does not match common sense or expectations

Examples
