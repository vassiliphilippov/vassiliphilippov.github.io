antianxiety drug

noun
drug used to relieve anxiety
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiænˈzaɪəti drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌæntiænˈzaɪəti drʌɡ / (BrE)
A drug used to relieve anxiety and reduce tension and irritability.

Examples

An antianxiety drug dose
He found that taking an antianxiety drug significantly improved his daily functioning.
Some antianxiety drugs can cause drowsiness as a side effect.
The doctor prescribed an antianxiety drug to help her cope with stress.
