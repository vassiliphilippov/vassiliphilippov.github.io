sitting

noun
act of being seated
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of sitting down or remaining seated for a period of time

Examples

A long sitting
After a long sitting, her legs began to ache.
He finished the entire novel in one sitting.
Let's take a break after this sitting and stretch our legs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stand - the act of standing up or remaining upright on the feet
