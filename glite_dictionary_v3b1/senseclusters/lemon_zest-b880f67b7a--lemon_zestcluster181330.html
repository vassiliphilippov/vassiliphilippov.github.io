lemon zest

noun
grated outer peel of a lemon used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmən zɛst / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən zɛst / (BrE)
the thin, brightly colored outer layer of a lemon's peel, grated or peeled off and used to add flavor to food and drinks
