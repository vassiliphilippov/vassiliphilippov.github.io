sukuma

noun
East African leafy green
8.9
Pronunciation: / suˈkuːmə / (AmE), / suˈkuːmə / (BrE)
a leafy green vegetable, similar to collard greens, commonly used in East African cooking

Examples

Fresh sukuma leaves
In Kenya, sukuma is often cooked with onions and served with ugali.
She bought fresh sukuma from the market to prepare a traditional meal.
Many families enjoy eating sukuma as a healthy side dish.
