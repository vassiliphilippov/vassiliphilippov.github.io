Examples

Books occupy space
Her collection of books occupies all the available shelves in the room.
The city completely occupies the island, leaving no space for forests.
Water occupies every part of the valley during the rainy season.
The exhibition occupies the entire first floor of the museum.
The renovation work occupied nearly two months of our time.
This project will occupy most of my weekend.
