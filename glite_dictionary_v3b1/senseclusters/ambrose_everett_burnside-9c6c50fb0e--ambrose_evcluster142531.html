Ambrose Everett Burnside

proper noun
US Civil War general (1824–1881); source of 'sideburns'
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.bɹoʊz ˈɛv.ɚ.ɪt ˈbɝːn.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈæm.brəʊz ˈev.rɪt ˈbɜːn.saɪd / (BrE)
(1824–1881) United States general in the American Civil War, known for his defeat at the Battle of Fredericksburg and for giving his name to 'sideburns'.
