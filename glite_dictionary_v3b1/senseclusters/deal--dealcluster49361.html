Synonyms

  • agreement
    1.4 66/m
    agreement is a formal, often written promise between parties, while deal is a more general arrangement often used in business.
  • business deal
    2.4 0.93/m
    business deal is an agreement about buying or selling between companies or people, while deal is a general term for an arrangement.
  • bargain
    2.0 13/m
    bargain is a negotiated offer that often focuses on a low price, while deal is any agreement reached after negotiation.
  • arrangement
    2.5 28/m
    arrangement is a plan that sets time or place between people, while deal is a negotiated agreement, especially in business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically binding arrangement between parties
  • synonym: bargain - an agreement between parties on the terms of a transaction or exchange
  • synonym: contract - a written or spoken agreement enforceable by law
  • antonym: disagreement - failure to reach an arrangement or deal
  • antonym: rip-off - an excessively overpriced deal or unfair agreement
  • synonym: transaction - an instance of buying or selling something
