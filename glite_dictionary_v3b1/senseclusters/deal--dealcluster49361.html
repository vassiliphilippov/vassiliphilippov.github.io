Synonyms

  • agreement
    1.4 80/m
    agreement is a formal, often written promise between parties, while deal is a more general arrangement often used in business.
  • business deal
    2.4 1/m
    business deal is an agreement about buying or selling between companies or people, while deal is a general term for an arrangement.
  • bargain
    2.0 15/m
    bargain is a negotiated offer that often focuses on a low price, while deal is any agreement reached after negotiation.
  • arrangement
    2.5 30/m
    arrangement is a plan that sets time or place between people, while deal is a negotiated agreement, especially in business.
