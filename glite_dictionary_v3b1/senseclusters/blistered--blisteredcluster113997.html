blistered

verb
to form blisters
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪstərd / (AmE), / ˈblɪstəd / (BrE)
formed blisters or caused something to develop blisters or bubbles, often due to heat or friction.

Examples

Her heels blistered
After a long hike in new shoes, her heels blistered painfully.
The paint on the old fence blistered in the hot summer sun.
His hands blistered from working without gloves all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bubble - to form bubbles on a surface
  • antonym: crack - to break or split without complete separation
