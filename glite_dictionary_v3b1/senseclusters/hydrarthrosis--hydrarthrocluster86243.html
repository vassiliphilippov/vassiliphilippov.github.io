hydrarthrosis

Examples

Joint hydrarthrosis
After the accident, a buildup of synovial fluid led to hydrarthrosis in his elbow joint.
The doctor explained that hydrarthrosis often requires draining the excess fluid to relieve swelling.
The patient suffered from hydrarthrosis in her knee, making movement painful and difficult.
