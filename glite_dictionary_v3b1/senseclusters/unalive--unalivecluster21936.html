unalive

verb
to cause death
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈlaɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈlaɪv / (BrE)
to kill or cause to die; used informally online as a euphemism, especially for suicide or death.

Examples

To unalive someone
Some people use the word unalive online to avoid triggering sensitive topics.
The character threatened to unalive his enemies if they betrayed him.
She joked that her plants would unalive if she forgot to water them again.
