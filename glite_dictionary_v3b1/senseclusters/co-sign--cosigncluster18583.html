co-sign

verb
publicly endorse; agree with
4.6
Pronunciation: / koʊˈsaɪn / (AmE), / kəʊˈsaɪn / (BrE)
informal: to publicly express support for or agree with a statement, idea, proposal, or person.

Examples

To co-sign a petition
Many celebrities decided to co-sign the petition to publicly endorse the charity's reforms.
I completely co-sign her view that the new policy will improve work–life balance.
After the speech, three senior researchers co-signed a joint statement supporting the study.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endorse - to express public approval or support for something
  • antonym: reject - to refuse to accept or agree with something
