footstone

noun
foundational principle
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a fundamental principle or basis on which something is established, used figuratively

Examples

Footstone of trust
Trust became the footstone of their partnership, guiding every decision they made together.
Honesty is often seen as the footstone of a strong community.
Education serves as the footstone for personal and professional growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cornerstone - an essential or foundational element
  • synonym: foundation - the underlying basis or principle for something
