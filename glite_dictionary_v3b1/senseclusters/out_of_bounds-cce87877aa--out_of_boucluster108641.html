out of bounds

adjective
not within permitted limits; not allowed
6.1
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv baʊndz / (AmE), / aʊt əv baʊndz / (BrE)
not within the permitted limits or area; not allowed or inappropriate

Examples

Out of bounds area

The soccer ball was declared out of bounds and play stopped immediately.
