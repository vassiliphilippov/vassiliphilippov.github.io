harpoon

verb
strike or kill with a harpoon
Pronunciation: / hɑrˈpun / (AmE), / hɑːˈpuːn / (BrE)
to strike, wound, or kill (an animal, especially a whale) with a harpoon.

Examples

To harpoon a whale
Early whalers would often harpoon several animals during a single voyage.
Modern regulations have limited the number of whales that can legally be harpooned.
They attempted to harpoon the massive whale from their small boat.
