synthesize

verb
combine elements to form a whole
7.3 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.θə.saɪz/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.θə.saɪz/ (BrE)
to combine different things, ideas, or substances to form a complete or more complex whole, especially in scientific or intellectual contexts
