Dobsonian

adjective
of Dobson telescopes
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑbˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / dɒbˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a Dobson telescope or its simple altazimuth mount design

Examples

Dobsonian telescope design
The club members built a large Dobsonian telescope for stargazing events in the park.
A Dobsonian mount allows for easy movement and setup, making it popular among amateur astronomers.
She preferred the simplicity of a Dobsonian design when observing the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Newtonian - relating to Newtonian telescopes, a type of reflecting telescope
