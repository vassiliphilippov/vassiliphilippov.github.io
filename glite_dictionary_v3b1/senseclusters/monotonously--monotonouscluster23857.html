monotonously

adverb
in a monotonous or unvarying manner
4.7 6.2
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːtənəsli / (AmE), / məˈnɒtənəsli / (BrE)
in a dull, repetitive, or unvarying way that lacks interest or variety

Examples

Speak monotonously
Tasks in the factory were performed monotonously, leading to boredom among the workers.
The lecturer spoke monotonously for nearly an hour, making it hard for students to remain attentive.
The rain fell monotonously against the window, creating a soothing but unchanging rhythm.
