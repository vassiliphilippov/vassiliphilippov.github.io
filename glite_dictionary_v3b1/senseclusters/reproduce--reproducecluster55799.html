reproduce

verb
produce offspring; procreate
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˈduːs / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdjuːs / (BrE)
(of an animal, plant, or microorganism) to produce offspring or new individuals of the same kind.

Examples

To reproduce offspring
Endangered animals may struggle to reproduce in captivity.
Many fish can reproduce several times throughout their lives.
Under the right conditions, yeast cells reproduce rapidly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procreate - to produce offspring, especially by sexual reproduction
