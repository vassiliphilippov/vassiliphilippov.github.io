Ciliophora

noun
class of protozoa with cilia
Pronunciation: / sɪl.iˈɑf.ə.rə /; / sɪliˈɑfərə / (AmE), / sɪl.iˈɒf.ə.rə /; / sɪliˈɒfərə / (BrE)
a class of protozoa (single-celled organisms) that have cilia (tiny hairlike structures) on their surface at some stage of their life
