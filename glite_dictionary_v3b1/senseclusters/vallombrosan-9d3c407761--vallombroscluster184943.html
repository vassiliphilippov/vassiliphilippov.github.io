Vallombrosan

noun
member of monastic order
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvæləmˈbroʊzən / (AmE), / ˌvæləmˈbrəʊzən / (BrE)
a monk or member of the Vallombrosan religious order founded at Vallombrosa Abbey in Italy

Examples

A Vallombrosan monk
The Vallombrosan greeted the pilgrims and offered them simple bread and water.
As a Vallombrosan, he dedicated his life to prayer and service at the abbey.
The history of the Vallombrosan order dates back to the eleventh century in Tuscany.
