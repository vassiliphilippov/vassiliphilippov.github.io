arriver

noun
person who arrives
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪvər / (AmE), / əˈraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who arrives at a place, especially someone who has just come or reached a destination

Examples

The first arriver
The first arriver at the party helped set up the decorations.
Late arrivers were asked to wait outside the hall.
Each arriver received a welcome packet at the registration desk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: departed - a person who has left a place
  • synonym: newcomer - a person who has recently arrived in a place
