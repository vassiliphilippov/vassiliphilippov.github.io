boulevard

noun
wide city street, often tree-lined
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːləˌvɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbuːləvɑːd / (BrE)
A wide street in a city, often lined with trees, typically designed for traffic, pedestrians, or both.

Examples

A boulevard street
During the parade, crowds gathered on both sides of the boulevard to watch the festivities.
The trees along the boulevard provided much-needed shade on hot summer days.
Their apartment overlooks a busy boulevard lined with shops and cafes.

  • degree lower: street - a public road in a city or town
