rung

noun
crosspiece between chair legs
8.2 2.5
Pronunciation: /rʌŋ/ (AmE), /rʌŋ/ (BrE)
a horizontal bar that connects the sides of a ladder or chair, used as a step or support

Examples

The lowest rung
He rested his feet on the lowest rung of the chair.
One of the rungs on this old chair is broken.
The craftsman carefully shaped each rung to fit between the chair legs.
He carefully placed his foot on the next rung and climbed higher up the ladder.
She painted each rung of the old wooden ladder a different color.
The ladder was missing a rung, making it unsafe to use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rail - a horizontal bar or support on furniture, often similar in function to a rung
  • synonym: step - a flat surface for placing the foot, as in a staircase
