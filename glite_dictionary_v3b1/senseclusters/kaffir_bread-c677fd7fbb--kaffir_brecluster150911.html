kaffir bread

noun
South African cycad with edible starchy pith
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkæf.ɚ brɛd / (AmE), / ˈkæf.ɪə brɛd / (BrE)
A South African cycad (Encephalartos species), whose starchy pith from the fruit is used as food.

Examples

Kaffir bread plant
Botanists studied the growth patterns of kaffir bread in different soil types.
In some regions, people still harvest kaffir bread for its edible qualities.
The starchy pith of the kaffir bread was once an important food source for indigenous South African communities.
