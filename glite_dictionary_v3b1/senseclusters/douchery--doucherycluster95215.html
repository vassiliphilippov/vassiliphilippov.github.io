douchery

noun
obnoxious or rude behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈduːʃəri / (AmE), / ˈduːʃəri / (BrE)
Obnoxious or rude behavior, especially that which is arrogant or inconsiderate.

Examples

His douchery behavior
His blatant douchery during the meeting offended everyone present.
She called him out on his douchery, refusing to let the rude comments slide.
Some people online are famous for their douchery and constant trolling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jerkiness - behavior characterized by being a jerk
  • antonym: politeness - behavior showing good manners, opposite of rudeness
