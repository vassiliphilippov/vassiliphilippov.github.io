obs

noun
vital sign measurements
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɑbz / (AmE), / ɒbz / (BrE)
informal abbreviation for 'observations', meaning measurements of a patient's vital signs such as pulse, blood pressure, and temperature

Examples

Patient obs chart
The nurse recorded the patient's obs at the start of every shift.
After surgery, the doctor asked for regular obs to monitor the patient's recovery.
Student nurses learn how to take obs as part of their basic training.
