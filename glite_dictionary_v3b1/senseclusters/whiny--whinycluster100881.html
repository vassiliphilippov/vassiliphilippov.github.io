whiny

adjective
inclined to complain in a childish way
4.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪ.niː/ /; / /ˈhwaɪ.niː/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪ.niː/ /; / /ˈhwaɪ.niː/ (BrE)
complaining too much in a way that seems childish or difficult to please; given to whining.

Examples

A whiny voice
His whiny tone made it difficult for his friends to take him seriously.
She was too whiny about her workload for anyone to sympathize.
The whiny child refused to eat his vegetables and kept protesting loudly.
