lightbox

noun
image overlay feature
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪt.bɑːks/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪt.bɒks/ (BrE)
a web or app feature that displays an image or video in a centered overlay while dimming the background content

Examples

Photo lightbox online
Clicking the image will open it in a lightbox for a better view.
The website uses a lightbox to display product photos without leaving the page.
A lightbox effect helps users focus on the video by dimming the rest of the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modal - a pop-up window that appears on top of the main content
  • synonym: popup - a small window that appears suddenly on a computer screen
