hankerer

noun
person who longs
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋkərər / (AmE), / ˈhæŋkərə / (BrE)
a person who strongly desires or longs for something, especially persistently or repeatedly

Examples

A hankerer for success
As a lifelong hankerer after knowledge, she spent hours reading every day.
The hankerer in him could not resist the call of distant lands and new adventures.
Many hankerers find it hard to settle for what they already have.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contented person - a person who is satisfied and does not long for more
  • synonym: craver - a person who has a strong desire for something
  • synonym: yearner - a person who longs or wishes for something deeply
