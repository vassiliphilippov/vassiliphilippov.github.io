welcome mat

noun
mat for wiping shoes at an entrance
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəm mæt / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəm mæt / (BrE)
a mat placed outside a door or entrance for people to wipe their shoes on, often with a welcoming message to greet visitors

Examples

A welcome mat outside
He brushed the dirt from his boots on the welcome mat before stepping inside.
Please leave your shoes on the welcome mat when you come in.
The new welcome mat outside their door reads 'Home Sweet Home.'
Every visitor was greeted by a cheerful welcome mat at the entrance.
Putting out the welcome mat is a simple way to make guests feel at home.
You can wipe your shoes on the welcome mat before coming in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doormat - a mat placed in front of a door for wiping shoes
