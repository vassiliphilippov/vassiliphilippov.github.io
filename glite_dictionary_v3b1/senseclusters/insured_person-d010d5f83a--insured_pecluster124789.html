insured person

noun
person protected by an insurance policy
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈʃʊrd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔːd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose interests are protected by an insurance policy, or who is covered under an insurance contract against specified losses or risks

Examples

The insured person dies
If the insured person passes away, the policy pays out to their beneficiaries.
Only the insured person can file a claim for health benefits under this plan.
Under the policy, the insured person is entitled to receive compensation in case of accidental damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: policyholder - a person who owns an insurance policy
