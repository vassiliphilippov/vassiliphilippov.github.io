haphazard

adjective
lacking order or care
Pronunciation: / hæpˈhæz.ərd /; / hæpˈhæzərd / (AmE), / hæpˈhæz.əd /; / hæpˈhæzəd / (BrE)
done with no particular order, plan, or organization; random or careless

Examples

Haphazard arrangement
He arranged the books in a haphazard fashion on the shelf, without any logical order.
Reports were kept in a haphazard manner, making it difficult to find important information.
Their haphazard approach to planning led to many mistakes during the event.
Decorations were hung in a haphazard way, giving the party a quirky and spontaneous feel.
The city grew in a haphazard fashion, with little planning or regulation.
Their haphazard approach to organizing the files made it nearly impossible to find anything quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orderly - arranged or organized in a systematic way
  • synonym: random - made or done without method or conscious decision
  • synonym: slapdash - done too hurriedly and carelessly
  • antonym: systematic - done or acting according to a fixed plan or system
