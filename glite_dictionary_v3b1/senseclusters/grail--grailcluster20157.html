grail

noun
the legendary cup from the Last Supper
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪl / (AmE), / ɡreɪl / (BrE)
the cup or chalice said to have been used by Jesus at the Last Supper and sought after in medieval legend, especially in the quest for the Holy Grail

Examples

The Holy grail
Some believe that the grail holds mystical powers.
The knights embarked on a quest to find the legendary grail.
The story of King Arthur often involves the search for the grail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Holy Grail - the cup believed to have been used by Christ at the Last Supper, also representing a sought-after object
