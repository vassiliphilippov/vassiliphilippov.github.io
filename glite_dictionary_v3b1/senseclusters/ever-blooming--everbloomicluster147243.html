ever-blooming

adjective
flowers continuously
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
producing flowers continuously or over a long period of time

Examples

Ever-blooming roses
The ever-blooming jasmine filled the garden with fragrance from spring to autumn.
Gardeners love ever-blooming varieties because they provide color for months.
She planted an ever-blooming rose bush that flowered from May until the first frost.

Related Terms

  • antonym: annual - a plant that completes its life cycle in one year
  • synonym: perennial - a plant that lives for several years and blooms repeatedly
