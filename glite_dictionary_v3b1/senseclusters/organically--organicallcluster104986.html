organically

adverb
as an integral or inherent part
2.3
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈɡænɪkli / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡænɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner such that something serves as an inherent or integral part of the whole.

Examples

Organically developed ideas
In her compositions, every theme is developed organically rather than being artificially added.
The arches blend organically into the overall design of the cathedral.
The supporting characters are connected organically to the main plot, enhancing the story's unity.
