Examples

Used improperly
If the machine is assembled improperly, it could be dangerous to use.
The report was rejected because it was improperly formatted.

Synonyms

  • unsuitably

  • incorrectly

    incorrectly means with factual or technical errors, while improperly often suggests a wrong method, poor fit, or social unacceptability.
