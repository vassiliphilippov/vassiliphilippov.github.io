polar

adjective
at opposite extremes; completely opposed
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlə / (BrE)
completely different or opposite in every way; at the extreme ends of a range or spectrum

Examples

Polar opposites
His reaction was polar to what I expected; instead of anger, he smiled.
The two scientists held polar viewpoints on the cause of climate change.
Their political beliefs are polar opposites, making compromise very difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diametric - completely opposed; at opposite extremes
  • synonym: opposite - being completely different in nature, direction, or meaning
