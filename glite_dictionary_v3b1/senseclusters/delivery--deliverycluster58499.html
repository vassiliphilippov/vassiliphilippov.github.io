Synonyms

  • legal transfer
    4.2 0.06/m
    legal transfer is a formal move of ownership under the law, while delivery is the general act of giving title or possession.
  • conveyance
    8.2 0.15/m
    conveyance is the formal legal process or document used to move property, while delivery refers to the act of transferring title or possession.
  • handover
    3.9 0.75/m
    handover is the physical passing of possession or responsibility between people or groups, while delivery focuses on legally transferring title or ownership.
