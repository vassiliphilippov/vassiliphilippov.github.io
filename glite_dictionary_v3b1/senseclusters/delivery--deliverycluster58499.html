delivery

noun
voluntary transfer of possession or title
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvəri / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)
the act of voluntarily transferring something, such as title or possession, from one party to another

Examples

Legal delivery
Proper delivery of the deed is essential for the transfer of property ownership.
She arranged the delivery of the documents to her lawyer.
The delivery of shares was completed electronically between the two companies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transfer - the act of moving something from one person or place to another
