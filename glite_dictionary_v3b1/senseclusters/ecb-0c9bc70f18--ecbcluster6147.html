ECB

noun
European Central Bank
Pronunciation: / iː siː biː / (AmE), / iː siː biː / (BrE)
the European Central Bank, the main institution responsible for managing the euro and monetary policy in the eurozone

Examples

ECB interest rates
The ECB announced a change in interest rates to address inflation in the eurozone.
Many economists closely follow decisions made by the ECB each month.
The ECB plays a key role in maintaining financial stability across Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: European Central Bank - the central bank for the euro and eurozone countries
  • synonym: Federal Reserve - the central bank of the United States
