frozen custard

noun
a creamy dessert like ice cream, made with custard
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzən ˈkʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən ˈkʌstəd / (BrE)
A cold dessert similar to ice cream, made with a base of boiled custard typically containing eggs and cream.

Examples

A frozen custard dessert
Many people prefer frozen custard to ice cream because of its rich, smooth texture.
The bakery makes delicious frozen custard in a variety of flavors during the summer.
We stopped at the famous roadside stand to get some frozen custard on a hot afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice cream - a sweet frozen food made from cream or milk and sugar, often with flavoring
