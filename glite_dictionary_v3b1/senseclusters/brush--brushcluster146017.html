brush

noun
electrical conductor in motors or generators
5.7
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ / (AmE), / brʌʃ / (BrE)
a device that conducts electrical current between stationary and moving parts of a generator or motor

Examples

A carbon brush
A carbon brush was replaced during the motor repair.
Most small electric motors use a brush to transfer current to the rotor.
Worn brushes can cause sparking and reduced efficiency in generators.
