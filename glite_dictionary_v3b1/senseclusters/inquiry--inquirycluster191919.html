Examples

Make an inquiry
After receiving the report, the committee began its inquiry into the incident.
The judge launched a formal inquiry into the company's financial activities.
We made an inquiry about the new policy to better understand its impact.
After her inquiry, he explained all the project details.
Please direct any inquiries about the course to the admissions office.

After much enquiry, the truth finally came to light.

Synonyms

  • investigation
    2.9 112/m
    an investigation is a careful, formal search for facts, while an inquiry can mean asking a question or a wider examination.
  • examination
    2.9 3.18/m
    an examination is a formal, systematic questioning often in official or school settings, while an inquiry covers asking or investigating more broadly.
  • questioning
    3.2 0.20/m
    questioning is the act of asking many questions to gather information, while an inquiry can also mean a formal investigation.
  • research
    3.4 481/m
    research is a long, careful study to discover new facts, while an inquiry can be a short question or an investigation.
  • query
    3.7 3.74/m
    a query is a single question often sent to an official or company, while an inquiry can be broader or investigative.
  • question
    1.0 426/m
    a question is one sentence used to ask for information, while an inquiry can mean a formal investigation or general asking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: question - a sentence worded or expressed so as to elicit information
  • synonym: research - systematic investigation to establish facts
