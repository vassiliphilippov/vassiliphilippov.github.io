hypokinetic

adjective
reduced movement
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by decreased movement or reduced physical activity

Examples

Hypokinetic lifestyle
The doctor noted the patient's hypokinetic movements during the neurological examination.
A hypokinetic lifestyle can increase the risk of certain health problems.
Some forms of Parkinson's disease are classified as hypokinetic disorders due to reduced motion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hyperkinetic - relating to or characterized by excessive movement
  • synonym: inactive - not engaging in physical activity
