Synonyms

  • unconversant
    8.4 0.01/m
    unconversant is not familiar with a subject and sounds formal or bookish, while ignorant means lacking knowledge generally.
  • unaware
    2.9 15/m
    unaware does not notice or know about a fact or event, while ignorant means lacking knowledge more broadly.
  • unacquainted
    6.5 0.07/m
    unacquainted means not familiar or not having met or used something, while ignorant implies a lack of knowledge.
  • uninitiated
    8.0 0.83/m

  • unenlightened
    7.0 0.56/m

  • uneducated
    2.8 3.72/m
    uneducated lacks formal schooling or training and often means less taught, while ignorant refers to lack of knowledge or awareness.
