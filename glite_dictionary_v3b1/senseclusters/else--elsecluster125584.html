else

adverb
otherwise; if not
Pronunciation: / ɛls / (AmE), / ɛls / (BrE)
used after 'or' to mean 'otherwise' or 'if not', often introducing a negative consequence or alternative.

Examples

Or else trouble
You must leave now, else you will miss the train.
Study hard, else you might fail the exam.
Call me when you arrive, else I will worry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: otherwise - in a different way or if not
