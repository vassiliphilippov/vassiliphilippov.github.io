izar

noun
long loose cotton garment for women
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈzɑr / (AmE), / ɪˈzɑː / (BrE)
a long, loose cotton outer garment, usually white, traditionally worn by Muslim women in North Africa and the Middle East, covering the entire body

Examples

A white izar
Her izar kept her comfortably cool under the hot desert sun.
The woman wore a crisp white izar as she walked through the marketplace.
Traditional ceremonies often require participants to dress in a flowing izar.
