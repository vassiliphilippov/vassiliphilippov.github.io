tinea cruris

noun
fungal infection of the groin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪniə ˈkrʊrɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɪnɪə ˈkrʊərɪs / (BrE)
A fungal infection affecting the skin of the groin, commonly known as jock itch, especially prevalent in men.

Examples

tinea cruris infection
Athletes are particularly susceptible to tinea cruris if they wear tight, damp clothing for extended periods.
To prevent tinea cruris, it is important to keep the groin area clean and dry, especially after exercise.
Treatment for tinea cruris typically involves applying topical antifungal creams to the affected area.
