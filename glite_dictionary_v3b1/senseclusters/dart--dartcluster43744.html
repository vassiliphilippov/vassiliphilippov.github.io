dart

noun
small pointed missile
Pronunciation: / dɑrt / (AmE), / dɑːt / (BrE)
a small, narrow, pointed missile that is thrown or shot, often as a weapon or in games

Examples

Throw a dart
He threw the dart at the center of the target and scored a bullseye.
She pulled the dart from the board and handed it to the next player.
The children played a game of dart throwing at the fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrow - a shaft with a pointed tip shot from a bow
  • synonym: missile - an object that is thrown or projected to strike a target
