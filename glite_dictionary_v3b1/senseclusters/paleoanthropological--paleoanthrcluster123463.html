paleoanthropological

adjective
relating to the study of human fossils
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪlioʊˌænθroʊpəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpalɪəʊˌænθrəpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
of or relating to the scientific study of human fossils and ancient human ancestors

Examples

Paleoanthropological study
He attended an international paleoanthropological conference to discuss recent fossil discoveries.
Her paleoanthropological research focuses on the evolutionary development of bipedalism in ancient hominins.
The paleoanthropological evidence suggests that early humans migrated out of Africa much earlier than previously thought.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: anthropological - relating to the study of humans and their societies
  • synonym: archaeological - relating to the study of ancient societies through material remains
