Synonyms

  • extolment
    9.9 0.01/m
    extolment gives very high, formal praise in writing or speech, while glorification means giving great honor or exaltation publicly.
  • valorization
    9.1 0.1/m
    valorization praises or raises the value of something often in cultural or economic terms, while glorification focuses on honor and admiration.
  • praise
