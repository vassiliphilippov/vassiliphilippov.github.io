bar

noun
official prohibition, restriction
6.1 13/m
Pronunciation: / b…ër / (AmE), / b…ëÀê / (BrE)
an official or legal prohibition or restriction; something that prevents a particular action or event

Examples

A bar to progress
His criminal record was a bar to being hired in the financial sector.
Legal changes removed the bar on women working in certain professions.
There is no bar to him joining the committee if he wishes.
