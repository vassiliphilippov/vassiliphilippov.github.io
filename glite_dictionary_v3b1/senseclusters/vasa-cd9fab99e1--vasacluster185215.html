Vasa

proper noun
Swedish royal dynasty
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːsə / (BrE)
the royal House of Vasa, a Swedish dynasty that ruled Sweden and parts of Poland in the 16th and 17th centuries

Examples

Vasa royal family
The Vasa dynasty played a crucial role in shaping Swedish history during the 16th century.
Many Polish kings in the 17th century were members of the Vasa family.
Historians often compare the Vasa dynasty to other European royal houses of the era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Habsburg - a powerful European royal dynasty that ruled Austria and other territories
  • synonym: Romanov - the Russian royal family that ruled from 1613 to 1917
