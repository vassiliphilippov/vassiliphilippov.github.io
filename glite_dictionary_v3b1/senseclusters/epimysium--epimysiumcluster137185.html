epimysium

noun
muscle outer covering
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈmɪziəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈmɪziəm / (BrE)
a layer of connective tissue that surrounds and protects an entire muscle

Examples

The epimysium layer
The epimysium provides structural support and protection for the entire muscle.
During surgery, the doctor carefully separated the epimysium from the underlying muscle tissue.
Damage to the epimysium can affect the function and healing of a muscle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endomysium - connective tissue surrounding individual muscle fibers
  • synonym: perimysium - connective tissue surrounding bundles of muscle fibers
