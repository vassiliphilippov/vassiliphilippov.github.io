lining

noun
attaching an inner fabric layer
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of attaching an inner layer of fabric to the inside of a garment, curtain, or similar item

Examples

Dress lining process
Proper lining ensures that a dress retains its shape and lasts longer.
She learned the process of lining curtains in her sewing class.
The lining of the coat was expertly attached by the tailor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lining - a piece of cloth sewn inside garments
