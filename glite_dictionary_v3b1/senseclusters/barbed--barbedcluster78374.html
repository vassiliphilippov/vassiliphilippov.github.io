barbed

adjective
having sharp points or barbs
5.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /bɑːrbd/ (AmE), /bɑːbd/ (BrE)
having or covered with sharp points or barbs, such as those on wire, feathers, or certain animals and plants.

Examples

The hedgehog's barbed spines protect it from predators.
