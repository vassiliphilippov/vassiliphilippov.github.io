bed rest

noun
continuous rest in bed for medical reasons

The act of staying in bed continuously for a period of time, usually as prescribed for medical reasons such as illness, injury, or pregnancy.

Examples

To stay on bed rest
During his recovery from the flu, he was advised to stay on bed rest.
Pregnant women with complications are sometimes put on bed rest to reduce risk.
The doctor prescribed bed rest for two weeks after her surgery.
