tengu

noun
Japanese mythical creature
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛŋɡu / (AmE), / ˈtɛŋɡu / (BrE)
a supernatural creature in Japanese folklore, often shown with bird-like features and associated with mountains and forests

Examples

A mountain tengu
The villagers believed a tengu lived deep within the forest near their town.
In many stories, a tengu is portrayed as both a protector and a trickster.
Tourists visit the shrine hoping to catch a glimpse of a legendary tengu.
