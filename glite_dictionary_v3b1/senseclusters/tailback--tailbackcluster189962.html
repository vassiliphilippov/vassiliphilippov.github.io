tailback

noun
American football running position
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈteɪlbæk /; / ˈteɪlˌbæk / (BrE)
(in American football) the player who lines up farthest from the line of scrimmage, usually the main ball-carrier
