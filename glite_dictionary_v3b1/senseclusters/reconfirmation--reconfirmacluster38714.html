reconfirmation

noun
renewal of vows
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌkɑːnfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌkɒnfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal act or ceremony in which a person reaffirms their faith, vows, or commitment, especially in a religious context

Examples

Religious reconfirmation ceremony
The church held a special reconfirmation ceremony for members wishing to renew their faith.
After many years, she participated in a reconfirmation to reaffirm her spiritual commitment.
The community gathered to witness the reconfirmation of their long-standing traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confirmation - a ceremony in which a person affirms their faith or commitment
  • antonym: renunciation - the formal rejection of a belief or claim
