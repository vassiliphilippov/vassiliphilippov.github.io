tool

noun
means used to achieve something
1.0
Pronunciation: / tuːl / (AmE), / tuːl / (BrE)
a thing, method, or person used by someone to achieve a purpose, often in a figurative or negative way

Examples

Tool for success
Education is a powerful tool for social change.
The internet has become an essential tool in modern business.
They used humor as a tool to break the tension in the room.
He didn't realize he was just a tool in their scheme.
She resented being treated like a tool to further their ambitions.
The corrupt official was exposed as a political tool for his superiors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: means - a method or way of doing something
  • synonym: puppet - a person who is controlled by others
