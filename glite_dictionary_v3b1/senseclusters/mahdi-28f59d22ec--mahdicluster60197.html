Mahdi

noun
prophesied Islamic messianic leader
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.diː/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.diː/ (BrE)
(in Islam) the prophesied messianic leader who Muslims believe will appear before the end of the world to restore justice and true religion.

Examples

The Mahdi leader
Many Muslims await the coming of the Mahdi as a sign of the end times.
Some sects in Islam have different interpretations of who the Mahdi will be.
The concept of the Mahdi plays a significant role in Shia theology.
