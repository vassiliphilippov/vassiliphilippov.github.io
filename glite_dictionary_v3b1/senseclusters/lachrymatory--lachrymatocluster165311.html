lachrymatory

adjective
causing or relating to tears
Pronunciation: / ˈlækrəˌmɛˌtɔri / (AmE), / ləkˈrɪm.ə.t(ə)ri / (BrE)
Causing or relating to the production of tears.

Examples

Lachrymatory gas
Exposure to the lachrymatory gas caused everyone to start crying uncontrollably.
Onions release a lachrymatory substance that makes your eyes water when you cut them.
Police used lachrymatory agents to disperse the protesters in the square.

  • synonym: tearful - feeling or showing tears
