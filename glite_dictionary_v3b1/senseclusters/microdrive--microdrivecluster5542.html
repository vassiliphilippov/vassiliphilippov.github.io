microdrive

noun
miniature hard drive
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌdraɪv / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdraɪv / (BrE)
a very small hard disk drive designed for use in portable electronic devices

Examples

MP3 microdrive
The early MP3 player used a microdrive to store hundreds of songs.
Some digital cameras relied on a microdrive for additional memory capacity.
He replaced the faulty microdrive in his portable device to restore its storage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flash drive - a small portable device for storing digital data using flash memory
  • synonym: hard disk - a device for storing and retrieving digital information using magnetic storage
