overlimit

noun
excess over a limit
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlɪmɪt / (BrE)
the amount by which something exceeds a specified or allowed limit

Examples

Above the overlimit
The truck was fined because its cargo was above the overlimit allowed by law.
If your credit card balance is in overlimit, you may face extra fees.
The inspector recorded the overlimit before instructing the team to remove the extra weight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excess - an amount that is more than necessary or allowed
  • antonym: shortfall - an amount by which something is less than what is needed or expected
