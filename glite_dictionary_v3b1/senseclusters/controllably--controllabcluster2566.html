controllably

adverb
in a manageable way
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊləbli / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊləbli / (BrE)
in a manner that can be directed, managed, or kept within limits

Examples

Controllably adjust temperature
The temperature in the laboratory rose controllably thanks to the new cooling system.
She laughed controllably, able to stop herself when the meeting resumed.
The drone flew controllably even in strong winds due to its advanced design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uncontrollably - in a way that cannot be managed or kept within limits
