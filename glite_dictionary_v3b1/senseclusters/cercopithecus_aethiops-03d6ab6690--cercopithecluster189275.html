Cercopithecus aethiops

noun
East African monkey species
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɚˌkoʊpɪˈθikəs iːˈθaɪ.ɑps / (AmE), / səˌkɒpɪˈθiːkəs iːˈθaɪ.ɒps / (BrE)
A species of East African monkey with white and olive green fur and long white tufts of hair beside its face; also known as the vervet monkey.

Examples

The Cercopithecus aethiops monkey
In East Africa, the Cercopithecus aethiops often interacts with human settlements.
Researchers observed a troop of Cercopithecus aethiops foraging near the riverbank.
The Cercopithecus aethiops is well known for its expressive face and agile movements.
