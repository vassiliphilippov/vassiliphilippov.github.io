brine shrimp

noun
small saline water crustacean (Artemia)
6.2
Pronunciation: / braɪn ʃrɪmp / (AmE), / braɪn ʃrɪmp / (BrE)
a small crustacean (genus Artemia) that lives in salt or brackish water, often used as food for fish and other aquatic animals

Examples

Brine shrimp food
Aquarists often use brine shrimp as nutritious food for tropical fish.
Researchers studied the life cycle of brine shrimp in the Great Salt Lake.
The brine shrimp can survive in extremely salty environments that few other animals tolerate.
