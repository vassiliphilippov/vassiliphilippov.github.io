dark-brown

adjective
deep brown color
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑrkˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˌdɑːkˈbraʊn / (BrE)
of a color that is deep or rich brown, resembling that of wood or earth

Examples

A dark-brown horse
A dark-brown horse galloped across the field in the early morning light.
She wore a dark-brown dress that matched her eyes perfectly.
The table was made from dark-brown oak, giving it an elegant look.
