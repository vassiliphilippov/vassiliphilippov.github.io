repaste

verb
to glue again
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpeɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpeɪst / (BrE)
to stick or glue something again after it has come off or become detached

Examples

To repaste a poster
After the poster fell down, he had to repaste it to the wall.
The label kept peeling off, so she repasted it several times.
If the stamp comes loose, you can repaste it with a little glue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detach - to remove or separate something
  • synonym: reapply - to put something on again
