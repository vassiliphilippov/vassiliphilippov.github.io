positionless

adjective
without official role
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɪʃənləs / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃənləs / (BrE)
not having an official job, post, or assigned role within an organization or group

Examples

Positionless employees
After the merger, several employees became positionless and were unsure about their future in the company.
He felt positionless in the new team, as no one assigned him any specific duties.
The restructuring left her positionless, performing various tasks without a clear title.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unassigned - not given a specific role or task
