steam ejector

noun
device that uses steam to create vacuum or move fluids
Pronunciation: / stiːm ɪˈdʒɛktəɹ / (AmE), / stiːm ɪˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
A device that uses steam under pressure to create a vacuum or move fluids by entraining them with the steam flow.

Examples

A steam ejector device
A malfunction in the steam ejector caused a drop in system efficiency.
Many refineries rely on the steam ejector for reliable vacuum generation.
The laboratory uses a steam ejector to maintain a high vacuum in their equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eductor-jet pump - pump using a jet to create suction
  • synonym: injector - device using a fluid jet to move another fluid
