Amphitrite

proper noun
ship name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæmfɪˈtraɪti / (AmE), / ˌæmfɪˈtraɪti / (BrE)
the name given to various ships and boats, especially historical vessels, often inspired by the sea goddess Amphitrite in Greek mythology

Examples

The ship Amphitrite
The historic Amphitrite sailed across the Atlantic in the early 1800s.
Visitors admired the restored figurehead of the Amphitrite at the maritime museum.
Records show that several naval vessels were named Amphitrite over the centuries.
