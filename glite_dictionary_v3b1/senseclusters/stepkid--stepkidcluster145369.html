stepkid

noun
spouse's child
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌkɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌkɪd / (BrE)
a child of one's spouse or partner from a previous relationship, not biologically related to oneself

Examples

A stepkid in family
She always makes sure her stepkid feels included in family activities.
After the wedding, he became a proud stepkid in a blended family.
The teacher treated every stepkid with the same kindness as the other students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biological child - a child related to a parent by birth
  • synonym: stepchild - a child of one's spouse from a previous relationship
