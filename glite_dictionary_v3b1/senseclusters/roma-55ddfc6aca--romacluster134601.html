Roma

noun
member of the Roma people

A member of a people of traditionally itinerant lifestyle, originally from northern India, now living mainly in Europe, North Africa, and North America, who speak the Romany language.

Examples

Synonyms

  • Romani
    7.3 0.04/m
    Romani is an ethnic-group term that highlights their culture, while Roma stresses being a member who speaks the Romany language.
  • Romany
    8.5 0.01/m
    Romany refers to the group's language or a person of that culture, while Roma specifically names a member of that people.
