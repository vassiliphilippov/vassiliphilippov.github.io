Roma

noun
member of the Roma people
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊmə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊmə / (BrE)
A member of a people of traditionally itinerant lifestyle, originally from northern India, now living mainly in Europe, North Africa, and North America, who speak the Romany language.

Examples

Roma community
Many Roma faced discrimination when migrating to Western European countries.
She studies the language and history of the Roma in Eastern Europe.
The cultural traditions of the Roma have enriched music across the continent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gypsy - now often considered offensive term for Roma people
  • synonym: Romani - the language spoken by the Roma people
