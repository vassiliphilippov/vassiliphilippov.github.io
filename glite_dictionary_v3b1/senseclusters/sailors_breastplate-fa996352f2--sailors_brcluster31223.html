sailor's breastplate

noun
knot used for dragging a gun carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlərz ˈbrɛstˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈseɪləz ˈbrɛstˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a knot tied in a rope that is used for dragging a gun carriage, especially in naval contexts
