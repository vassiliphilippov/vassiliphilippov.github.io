printing

noun
process of producing text/images on paper
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of producing text or images by applying ink to paper, especially for publication

Examples

Printing process
