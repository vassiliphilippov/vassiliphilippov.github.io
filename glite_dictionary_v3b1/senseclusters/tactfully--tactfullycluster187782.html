tactfully

adverb
in a tactful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtæktfəli / (AmE), / ˈtæktfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows skill and sensitivity in dealing with difficult or delicate situations without offending others

Examples

To tactfully refuse
He tactfully declined the invitation without causing any offense.
She tactfully changed the subject when the conversation became uncomfortable.
The manager handled the criticism tactfully to avoid hurting anyone's feelings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tactless - in a way that shows a lack of sensitivity or consideration; without tact
