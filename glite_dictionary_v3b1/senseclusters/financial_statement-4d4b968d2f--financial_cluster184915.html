financial statement

noun
official record of financial accounts
1.4
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃəl ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃl ˈsteɪtmənt /; / faɪˈnanʃəl ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an official document or record that shows the financial position and activities of a company, person, or other entity
