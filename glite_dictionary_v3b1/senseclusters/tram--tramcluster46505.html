tram

noun
cable-suspended passenger or freight vehicle
3.6
Pronunciation: / træm / (AmE), / træm / (BrE)
a vehicle for carrying passengers or goods in cabins suspended from cables and supported by towers, often used at ski resorts or across valleys

Examples

Mountain tram ride
During the summer, the tram offers rides across the wide valley.
The ski resort installed a new tram to carry visitors to the summit.
We took the tram up the mountain for a breathtaking view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gondola - an enclosed cabin suspended from a cable, used to transport passengers
