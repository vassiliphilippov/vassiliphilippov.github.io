oceanographic

adjective
relating to ocean science
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊʃiənoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊʃiənəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of the ocean and its phenomena

Examples

Oceanographic instruments
The team used oceanographic instruments to measure water temperature and salinity.
She is pursuing an oceanographic career to help protect marine environments.
The oceanographic data revealed new information about deep-sea currents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marine - relating to the sea or ocean
  • antonym: terrestrial - relating to the land
