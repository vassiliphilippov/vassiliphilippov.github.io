quira

noun
hard, heavy red wood from the quira tree
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːrə / (AmE), / ˈkiːrə / (BrE)
A hard, heavy red wood obtained from the quira tree, used especially in carpentry and construction.

Examples

Quira wood furniture
Artisans often choose quira when they need wood that will last for generations.
Many builders prize quira for its durability and weight in construction projects.
The furniture was crafted from solid quira, giving it a distinctive reddish hue.
