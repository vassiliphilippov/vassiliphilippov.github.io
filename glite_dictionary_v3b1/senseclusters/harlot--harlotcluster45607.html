harlot

noun
prostitute (archaic/literary)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrlət / (AmE), / ˈhɑːlət / (BrE)
a woman who engages in sexual intercourse for money; prostitute (archaic or literary)

Examples

A harlot woman
He accused her unjustly, calling her a harlot in a fit of jealousy.
In the medieval city, tales of the harlot were whispered among the townsfolk.
The novel depicts a harlot who seeks redemption despite her past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtesan - a female prostitute, especially one with wealthy or upper-class clients
  • synonym: prostitute - a person who engages in sexual activity for payment
