resistor

noun
electrical component that limits current
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstər / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstə / (BrE)
An electrical component that limits or regulates the flow of electrical current in a circuit.

Examples

A resistor component
Each color band on a resistor indicates its resistance rating.
The engineer replaced the faulty resistor to fix the circuit.
You can control the brightness of the LED by adjusting the resistor value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capacitor - an electrical component that stores energy in an electric field
  • antonym: conductor - an object or substance that allows the flow of electrical current
