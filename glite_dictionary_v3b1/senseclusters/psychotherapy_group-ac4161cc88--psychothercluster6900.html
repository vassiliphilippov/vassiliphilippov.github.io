psychotherapy group

noun
group meeting for psychotherapy
2.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /saɪ.koʊ.ˈθe.rə.piː gruːp/ /; / /saɪ.kə.ˈθe.rə.piː gruːp/ (AmE), /saɪ.kəʊ.ˈθe.rə.piː gruːp/ /; / /saɪ.koʊ.ˈθe.rə.piː gruːp/ (BrE)
a group of people who meet together to receive or participate in psychotherapy, often guided by a professional therapist

Examples

A psychotherapy group session
After joining a psychotherapy group, he found it easier to open up about his feelings.
She attends a psychotherapy group every week to help manage her anxiety.
The psychotherapy group provided a safe space to discuss personal struggles with others facing similar challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: group therapy - a form of psychotherapy in which a group of patients discuss their problems together under supervision
  • degree lower: support group - a group of people who meet to support each other, usually in relation to a shared issue or concern
