resynthesize

verb
to reconstruct by synthesis
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɪnθəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɪnθəsaɪz / (BrE)
to recreate or reconstruct a sound, image, or signal by means of synthesis, often using analyzed data

Examples

To resynthesize a song
The software can resynthesize a song from its individual instrument tracks for better sound quality.
Scientists used advanced algorithms to resynthesize the missing parts of the ancient manuscript.
After analyzing the voice recording, the engineer managed to resynthesize the original speech.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decompose - to break down into smaller parts or elements
  • synonym: synthesize - to produce something by combining different elements, especially electronically
