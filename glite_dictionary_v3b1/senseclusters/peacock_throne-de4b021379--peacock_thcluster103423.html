Peacock Throne

noun
golden throne of Mughal emperors, later Persian symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.kɑːk θroʊn/ /; / /ˈpiː.kək θroʊn/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.kɒk θrəʊn/ /; / /ˈpiː.kək θrəʊn/ (BrE)
