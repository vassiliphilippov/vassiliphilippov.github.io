saliency

noun
measure/visualization of input importance in AI
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪliənsi / (AmE), / ˈseɪliənsi / (BrE)
(in machine learning and computer vision) a numerical measure or visualization that shows which parts of an input (for example, pixels in an image) most influence a model's output; often presented as a saliency map.

Examples

Saliency map
The model's saliency showed which parts of the photo influenced the classification most.
Researchers generated a heatmap to visualize the saliency of each pixel in the image.
Before trusting predictions, we check the saliency maps to ensure the model focuses on relevant features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salience - the state or quality of being prominent or standing out
  • synonym: saliency map - a visual heatmap showing saliency values across an input, highlighting influential regions
