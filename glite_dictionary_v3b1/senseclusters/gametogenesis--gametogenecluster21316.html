gametogenesis

noun
formation of sex cells by meiosis
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɡəˌmitəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ɡəˌmiːtəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (BrE)
the process in which sex cells (gametes) develop and mature through meiosis

Examples

Gametogenesis process
Gametogenesis occurs in the reproductive organs of animals and results in sperm and eggs.
Scientists study gametogenesis to better understand genetic inheritance and variation.
The failure of proper gametogenesis can lead to infertility in both males and females.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fertilization - fusion of gametes to initiate development of a new organism
  • synonym: meiosis - type of cell division producing gametes
