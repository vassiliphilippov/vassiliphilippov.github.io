dude ranch

noun
resort offering ranch activities
Pronunciation: / ˈduːd ræntʃ / (AmE), / ˈdjuːd ræntʃ / (BrE)
A holiday resort, especially in the western United States, where guests can experience ranch life and activities such as horseback riding and camping.

Examples

A dude ranch holiday
He always dreamed of visiting a dude ranch to experience the cowboy lifestyle.
Many families spend their summer vacation at a dude ranch in Colorado.
The dude ranch offered horseback riding and nightly campfires for the guests.

  • synonym: guest ranch - ranch that accommodates paying guests
