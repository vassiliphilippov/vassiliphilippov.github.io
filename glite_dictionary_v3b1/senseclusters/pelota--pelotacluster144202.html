pelota

noun
Spanish game ball
Pronunciation: / pɛˈloʊtə / (AmE), / pɛˈləʊtə / (BrE)
a small ball used in traditional Spanish or Latin American ball games, especially in sports like jai alai or pelota vasca

Examples

A pelota ball
In the traditional Basque sport, players catch and throw the pelota at high speeds.
The children played with a pelota in the village square every afternoon.
A new pelota was needed after the old one wore out during the tournament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ball - a round object used in games and sports
