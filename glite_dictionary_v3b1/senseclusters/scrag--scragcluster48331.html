scrag

verb
remove or discard something unwanted
Pronunciation: / skræɡ / (AmE), / skræɡ / (BrE)
to remove, discard, or eliminate something unwanted or outdated

Examples

To scrag old things
Every spring, he scrags unwanted items from the attic to make space for new things.
The IT department scragged obsolete software to streamline operations.
We decided to scrag all the broken chairs from the garage before the move.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispose - to throw away or get rid of something
