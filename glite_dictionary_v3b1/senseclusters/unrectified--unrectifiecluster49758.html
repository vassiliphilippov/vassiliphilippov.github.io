unrectified

adjective
not purified by distillation
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛktɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛktɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not purified or concentrated by rectification, especially by distillation; not refined by repeated distillation.
