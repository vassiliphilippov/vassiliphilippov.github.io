unrectified

adjective
not purified by distillation
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛktɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛktɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not purified or concentrated by rectification, especially by distillation; not refined by repeated distillation.

Examples

Unrectified alcohol
The laboratory used unrectified alcohol for the initial stage of the experiment.
Some traditional recipes call for unrectified spirits to achieve a stronger flavor.
Because it was unrectified, the chemical contained more impurities than the refined version.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raw - in a natural, unrefined state
  • antonym: rectified - purified or concentrated by distillation
