Marineland

noun
marine-themed aquarium with animal shows
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛrɪnˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈmarɪnˌland / (BrE)
A commercial aquarium or facility featuring marine animal shows, especially with trained dolphins.

Examples

Marineland dolphin show
Many tourists enjoy spending the afternoon at Marineland, watching the trained dolphins perform tricks.
The children were excited for their school trip to Marineland last Friday.
