vellum

noun
creamy heavy paper resembling parchment
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈvel.əm/ (AmE), /ˈvel.əm/ (BrE)
a heavy, creamy-colored paper resembling parchment, often used for fine documents or artistic purposes

Examples

Vellum paper sheet
Old manuscripts were often written on vellum due to its durability and texture.
The artist chose to use vellum for her watercolor illustrations because of its smooth surface.
The invitation was printed on elegant vellum to give it a luxurious feel.
