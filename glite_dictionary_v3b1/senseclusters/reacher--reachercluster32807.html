reacher

noun
device for grabbing
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈriːtʃə / (BrE)
a tool or device (or sometimes a person) that is used to reach or pick up things that are out of reach

Examples

A reacher tool
After her surgery, she used a reacher to grab items from the top shelf.
The hospital provided each patient with a reacher to help them pick up dropped objects.
He invented a new type of reacher that can extend even further than standard models.
After her surgery, she used a reacher to pick up items from the floor.
The elderly man kept a reacher nearby to help him grab things on high shelves.
A reacher can make daily tasks easier for people with limited mobility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grabber - a tool used to pick up or grasp objects
  • synonym: picker - a device or person that picks up things
  • synonym: tongs - a tool with two arms used for picking up objects
