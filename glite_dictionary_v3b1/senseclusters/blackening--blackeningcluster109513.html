blackening

noun
process of turning black or darker
Pronunciation: / ˈblæk.ən.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblæk.ən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of becoming black or darker in color.

Examples

The blackening of silver
The blackening of the silver was due to a chemical reaction.
The chef demonstrated the blackening of fish using a hot cast-iron skillet.
Years of smoke caused a gradual blackening of the ceiling.
