torchlight

noun
portable electric light
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtʃˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a portable electric light powered by batteries, commonly known as a flashlight in American English

Examples

A torchlight beam
She used a torchlight to find her way in the dark.
The campers packed a torchlight in case they needed to walk at night.
He shone his torchlight under the bed to look for his missing shoe.
