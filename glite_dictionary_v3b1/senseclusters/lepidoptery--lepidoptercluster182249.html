lepidoptery

noun
study of butterflies and moths
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˈdɑptəri / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptəri / (BrE)
the scientific study of butterflies and moths; the branch of entomology dealing with Lepidoptera

Examples

Lepidoptery study
An interest in butterflies led her to study lepidoptery at university.
He devoted his career to lepidoptery, discovering several new species of moths.
The museum held an exhibit on the history of lepidoptery and its prominent researchers.
