picture

noun
visible part of a TV broadcast
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
the visible part of a television broadcast as received on a screen, especially as opposed to the sound

Examples

TV picture quality
Can you adjust the antenna to get a better picture?
The picture froze on the screen while the audio continued.
When the storm hit, the picture turned fuzzy but the sound was clear.
