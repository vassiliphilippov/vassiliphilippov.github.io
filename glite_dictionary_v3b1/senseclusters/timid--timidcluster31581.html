timid

noun
fearful, cautious people
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪmɪd / (BrE)
People who are fearful and lack courage or confidence, often avoiding bold actions or dangerous situations.

Examples

The timid avoid danger
Only the timid stayed behind when the others explored the cave.
The haunted house was too frightening for the timid.
Whitewater rafting is not for the timid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bold - people who are brave and confident
