viscountess

noun
female noble of viscount rank
9.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪˌkaʊntəs / (AmE), / vɪsˈkaʊntɪs / (BrE)
a noblewoman who is the wife or widow of a viscount, or who holds the rank of viscount in her own right
