react

verb
undergo a chemical change
1.2
Pronunciation: / riˈækt / (AmE), / riˈækt / (BrE)
(of substances) to undergo a chemical change when mixed with another substance, especially under certain conditions.

Examples

Chemicals react together
Carbon dioxide does not react with nitrogen under normal conditions.
The two chemicals will only react at high temperatures.
When sodium and water react, hydrogen gas is produced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combine - to join or mix together, often chemically
  • antonym: decompose - to break down into simpler substances through a chemical reaction
