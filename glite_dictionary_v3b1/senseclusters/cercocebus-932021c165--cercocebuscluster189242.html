Cercocebus

noun
a genus of Old World monkeys (mangabeys)
Pronunciation: / sərˈkoʊsɪbəs / (AmE), / səˈkəʊsɪbəs / (BrE)
A genus of Old World monkeys, known as mangabeys, found in central Africa.

Examples

Cercocebus monkey
Researchers observed a group of Cercocebus feeding high in the forest canopy.
The endangered Cercocebus populations are threatened by habitat loss in central Africa.
Unlike other monkeys, Cercocebus species have cheek pouches for storing food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mangabey - a type of Old World monkey of the genus Cercocebus or Lophocebus
