unfactual

adjective
not factual
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæktʃuəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfæktʃuəl / (BrE)
not based on facts; incorrect or untrue

Examples

Unfactual claims
The article was criticized for its unfactual claims about the event.
Spreading unfactual information can lead to misunderstandings and confusion.
His argument was dismissed because it was largely unfactual and unsupported by evidence.
