intuitive feeling

noun
an instinctive sense or understanding
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtuː.ɪ.t̬ɪv ˈfiː.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtjuː.ɪ.tɪv ˈfiː.lɪŋ / (BrE)
an understanding or sense of something that arises instinctively, without conscious reasoning

Examples

An intuitive feeling about outcome
His intuitive feeling about the outcome proved to be correct.
Often, an intuitive feeling guides artists in their creative process.
She had an intuitive feeling that something was wrong, even though there was no evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intuition - the ability to understand something instinctively
