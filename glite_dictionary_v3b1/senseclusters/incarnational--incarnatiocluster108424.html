incarnational

adjective
relating to embodiment
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkɑːrˈneɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnkɑːˈneɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to embodiment in human or physical form, especially in a religious or theological context

Examples

An incarnational approach
The church's teachings emphasize an incarnational approach to faith and daily life.
Her poetry explores the incarnational aspects of love and human experience.
The artist's work has an incarnational quality that connects with viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embodied - expressed or represented in physical form
  • antonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit rather than physical form
