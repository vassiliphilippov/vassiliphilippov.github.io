accordant

adjective
in agreement; consistent
3.5
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔrdənt / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdənt / (BrE)
in agreement or harmony with something; consistent or conforming

Examples

Accordant with rules
His actions were accordant with his stated beliefs.
The policy is accordant with international regulations.
Their decisions were not accordant with company values.
His decision was accordant with the values of the company.
The music played in the background was accordant with the peaceful atmosphere of the garden.
Their views on climate policy were completely accordant, leading to a successful partnership.

  • antonym: discordant - not in agreement or harmony
