Corrodentia

noun
order of insects: booklice and bark-lice
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˌroʊˈdɛnʃiə / (AmE), / kəˌrəʊˈdɛnʃiə / (BrE)
an order of insects that includes booklice and bark-lice, formerly used in entomological classification

Examples

Corrodentia insects
Although Corrodentia is now considered obsolete, it once grouped together booklice and bark-lice.
Recent taxonomic revisions have redistributed members previously assigned to Corrodentia into other insect orders.
The entomologist specialized in the study of Corrodentia and their evolutionary history.
