Potomac

proper noun
major river in the eastern United States
4.3
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtoʊ.mək/ /; / /pə.ˈəʊ.mək/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtəʊ.mək/ /; / /pə.ˈtəʊ.mæk/ (BrE)
a major river in the east central United States, rising in the Appalachian Mountains of West Virginia and flowing east into the Chesapeake Bay, forming part of the boundary between Maryland and Virginia.
