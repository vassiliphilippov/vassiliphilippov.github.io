cholo style

noun
Mexican-American urban fashion style
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊ.loʊ staɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊ.ləʊ staɪl / (BrE)
A distinctive fashion and aesthetic associated with Mexican-American urban culture, often featuring baggy clothing, bandanas, tattoos, and specific hairstyles.

His cholo style was evident in the crisp white T-shirt, baggy khakis, and carefully groomed mustache.
