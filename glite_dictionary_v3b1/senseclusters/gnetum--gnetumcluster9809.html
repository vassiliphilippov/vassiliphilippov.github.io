gnetum

noun
a tropical tree or shrub genus; edible leaves and seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈniː.təm / (AmE), / ˈniː.təm / (BrE)
A small tropical tree or shrub of the genus Gnetum, with tiered branches and broad glossy dark green leaves, whose young leaves and seeds are used as food.

Examples

The gnetum tree
Botanists are studying the distribution of gnetum species across Southeast Asia.
Farmers collect edible seeds from the gnetum tree to produce flour.
The young leaves of gnetum are often added to soups in West African cuisine.
