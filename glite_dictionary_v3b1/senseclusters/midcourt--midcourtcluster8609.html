midcourt

noun
center of court
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌkɔːt / (BrE)
the central area of a sports court, such as in basketball, tennis, or netball

Examples

Across midcourt
The player dribbled the ball across midcourt before passing to a teammate.
She attempted a long shot from midcourt as the buzzer sounded.
The coach called a timeout when the team reached midcourt during the fast break.
She volleyed from midcourt, surprising her opponent with a well-placed shot.
The player moved quickly to midcourt to intercept the pass during the rally.
Standing at midcourt gave him a good view of the entire game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baseline - the boundary line at each end of a court
  • synonym: center court - the middle part of a sports court
