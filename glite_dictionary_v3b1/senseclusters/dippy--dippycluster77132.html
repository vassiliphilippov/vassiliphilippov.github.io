dippy

adjective
suitable for dipping
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpi / (AmE), / ˈdɪpi / (BrE)
having a soft or runny consistency that makes it suitable for dipping food into

Examples

Dippy eggs
The chef prepared a dippy cheese fondue for guests to enjoy with bread.
Kids love eating vegetables with a dippy ranch dressing on the side.
She made a dippy chocolate sauce perfect for dipping strawberries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saucy - having a sauce-like or liquid consistency
  • antonym: thick - having a dense or solid consistency, not suitable for dipping
