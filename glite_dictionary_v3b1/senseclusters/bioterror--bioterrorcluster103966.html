bioterror

noun
biological terrorism
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌtɛrər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌtɛrə / (BrE)
the use or threat of biological agents to intentionally cause harm, fear, or disruption

Examples

Bioterror attack
The government increased security measures to prevent bioterror attacks at major events.
Experts warn that bioterror could pose a serious threat to public health.
After the incident, the city developed new protocols to respond to bioterror emergencies.
