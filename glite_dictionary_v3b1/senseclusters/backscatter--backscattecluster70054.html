backscatter

noun
unsolicited bounced emails
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌskætər / (AmE), / ˈbækˌskætə / (BrE)
(in email security) unsolicited bounce messages returned to forged or spoofed sender addresses when those addresses are used by spammers or automated systems.

Examples

Email backscatter alerts
The security team investigated backscatter to locate compromised mail servers used by spammers.
Many ISPs now filter backscatter automatically to reduce the load on customer inboxes.
After the phishing campaign, company logs recorded massive backscatter coming from forged sender addresses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounce (email) - an automated message returned when an email cannot be delivered
