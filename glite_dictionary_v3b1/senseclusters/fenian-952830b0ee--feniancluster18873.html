Fenian

noun
offensive Irish slur
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːniən / (AmE), / ˈfiːniən / (BrE)
an offensive term for an Irish nationalist, especially used as a slur in historical or sectarian contexts

Examples

Called a Fenian
The politician was criticized for calling his opponent a Fenian during the debate.
Some fans chanted Fenian as an insult at the football match, causing controversy.
The term Fenian is considered highly offensive in many parts of the UK and Ireland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their nation's interests
  • antonym: unionist - a person who supports the political union between Great Britain and Northern Ireland
