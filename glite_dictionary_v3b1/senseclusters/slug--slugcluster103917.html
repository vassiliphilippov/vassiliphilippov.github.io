slug

noun
lazy or slothful person
Pronunciation: / slʌɡ / (AmE), / slʌɡ / (BrE)
A person who is habitually lazy or avoids work.

Examples

A lazy slug
On weekends, he turns into a real slug, barely moving from the couch.
She called her brother a slug when he slept past noon again.
Stop being such a slug and help us clean the garage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idler - a person who avoids work or activity
  • synonym: sluggard - a habitually lazy person
  • antonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
