rock drill

noun
machine for drilling into rock
Pronunciation: / rɑk drɪl / (AmE), / rɒk drɪl / (BrE)
a powered tool or machine for drilling holes into rock, commonly used in mining and construction

Examples

A rock drill machine
A pneumatic rock drill increased the speed of excavation considerably.
He operated the rock drill during the construction of the tunnel.
The miners used a rock drill to bore through the solid granite wall.
