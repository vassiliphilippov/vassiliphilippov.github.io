gringa

noun
slang for foreign woman
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a slang term in Latin America for a foreign, especially white, woman from an English-speaking country

Examples

A foreign gringa
During her trip to Mexico, some people called her a gringa because of her accent.
The group of friends joked about being the only gringa in the small village.
She was surprised when someone referred to her as a gringa at the market.
