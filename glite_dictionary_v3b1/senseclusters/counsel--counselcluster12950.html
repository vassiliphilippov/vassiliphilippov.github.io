counsel

noun
lawyer in court
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl / (BrE)
a lawyer who represents and advises someone in a court of law

Examples

Defense counsel
She hired experienced counsel to represent her during the trial.
The defense counsel presented a compelling argument to the jury.
The judge addressed both counsel before announcing the verdict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attorney - a person appointed to act for another in legal matters
  • synonym: barrister - a lawyer entitled to represent clients in higher courts
  • synonym: solicitor - a lawyer who gives legal advice and prepares legal documents
