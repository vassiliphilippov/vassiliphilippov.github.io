unsustainable

adjective
cannot be maintained
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈteɪnəbl /; / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnəbl / (BrE)
not able to be continued or maintained over time

Examples

An unsustainable growth
The company's rapid growth was unsustainable and eventually led to financial problems.
Relying on fossil fuels is considered unsustainable for the environment in the long term.
His argument became unsustainable when new facts were revealed during the debate.
The current rate of deforestation is unsustainable and threatens many species.
An unsustainable business model will eventually lead to failure.
Using so much water in a dry region is clearly unsustainable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indefensible - impossible to defend or justify
  • antonym: sustainable - able to be maintained or continued
  • synonym: untenable - not able to be maintained or defended
  • synonym: unviable - not capable of working successfully
