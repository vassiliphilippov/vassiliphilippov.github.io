chili pepper

noun
hot, spicy fruit used as seasoning
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli ˌpɛpər /; / ˈtʃɪli ˌpepər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli ˌpɛpə /; / ˈtʃɪli ˌpepə / (BrE)
a small, usually red or green, spicy pod (or the plant that produces it) used to add heat and flavor to food
