chili pepper

noun
hot, spicy fruit used as seasoning
1.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪl.iː ˈpep.ər/ /; / /ˈtʃaɪ.liː ˈpep.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪl.iː ˈpep.ə/ /; / /ˈtʃaɪ.liː ˈpep.ə/ (BrE)
