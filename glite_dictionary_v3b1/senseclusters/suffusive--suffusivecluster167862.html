suffusive

adjective
spreading through or over
8.1
Pronunciation: / səˈfjuːsɪv / (AmE), / səˈfjuːsɪv / (BrE)
spreading gradually or diffusely through or over something, especially in a way that fills it with color or emotion.

Examples

A suffusive glow
A suffusive glow radiated from behind the mountains at sunrise.
Her cheeks were bathed in a suffusive blush when he complimented her.
The room was filled with a suffusive warmth from the crackling fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diffuse - spread out over a large area; not concentrated
  • synonym: pervasive - spreading widely throughout an area or a group of people
