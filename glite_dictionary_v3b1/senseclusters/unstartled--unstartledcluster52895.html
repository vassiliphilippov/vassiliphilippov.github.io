unstartled

adjective
not surprised
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑrtəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɑːtld / (BrE)
not surprised or alarmed by something unexpected

Examples

Remain unstartled
Despite the loud crash, she remained unstartled and continued reading her book.
The dog was unstartled by the thunderstorm, unlike the other pets in the house.
He walked into the room unstartled by the unexpected applause from his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or surprise
  • antonym: startled - suddenly surprised or alarmed
  • synonym: unfazed - not disturbed or affected by something unexpected
