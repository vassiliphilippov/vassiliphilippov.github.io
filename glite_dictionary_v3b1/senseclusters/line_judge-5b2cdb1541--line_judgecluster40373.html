line judge

noun
football official assisting the referee
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪn ˌdʒʌdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn ˌdʒʌdʒ / (BrE)
an official in football who assists the referee, especially by keeping track of the official time during a game

Examples

The line judge watches time
A quick decision by the line judge prevented any confusion on the field.
During the game, the line judge communicated closely with the referee.
The line judge signaled the play was out of bounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referee - the main official overseeing a game and enforcing the rules
