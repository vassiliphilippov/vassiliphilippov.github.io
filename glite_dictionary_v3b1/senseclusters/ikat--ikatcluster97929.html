ikat

noun
tie-dye weaving method
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.kæt/ /; / /ˈɪ.kæt/ (AmE), /ˈiː.kæt/ /; / /ˈɪ.kæt/ (BrE)
a textile technique in which yarns are tie-dyed before weaving to create distinctive, often blurred patterns in the fabric

Examples

Ikat weaving technique
The artisan demonstrated how to create ikat patterns by dyeing the threads before weaving.
She bought a beautiful scarf made with traditional ikat techniques from the market.
Many Southeast Asian cultures are known for their unique ikat textiles and designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batik - a method of dyeing fabric using wax to create patterns
  • synonym: tie-dye - a technique of dyeing fabric by tying sections to resist the dye
