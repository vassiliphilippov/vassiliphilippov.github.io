DBH

noun
tree trunk diameter
Pronunciation: / diː biː eɪtʃ / (AmE), / diː biː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
the diameter of a tree trunk measured at 4.5 feet (1.3 meters) above the ground

Examples

Tree DBH measurement
The DBH of each tree was recorded to estimate the forest's total biomass.
Foresters use DBH as a standard measurement when assessing tree growth and health.
A tree with a large DBH is often much older than its neighbors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: girth - the measurement around the circumference of something, especially a tree trunk
