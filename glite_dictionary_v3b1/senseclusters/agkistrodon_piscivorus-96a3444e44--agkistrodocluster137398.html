Agkistrodon piscivorus

noun
venomous semiaquatic snake (cottonmouth) of the southern US
Pronunciation: / æɡˌkɪstrəˌdɒn pɪˈsɪvərəs / (AmE), / æɡˌkɪstrəˌdɒn pɪˈsɪvərəs / (BrE)
