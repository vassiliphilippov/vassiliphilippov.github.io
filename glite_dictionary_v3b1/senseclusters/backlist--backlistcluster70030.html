backlist

adjective
older published titles
Pronunciation: / ˈbæklɪst / (AmE), / ˈbæklɪst / (BrE)
relating to books or titles that are older and still available in a publisher's catalogue, not newly published.

Examples

Backlist novels
The publisher decided to reprint several backlist novels due to renewed interest.
Many backlist titles continue to sell steadily years after their initial release.
She manages the backlist section, ensuring older books remain available to readers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frontlist - relating to newly published books or titles
