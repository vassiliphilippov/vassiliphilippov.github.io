theo-

x
prefix: God, divinity
Pronunciation: / θiːoʊ / (AmE), / θiːəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'God' or relating to divinity, used in words about religion or deities

Examples

Theo- prefix God
The prefix theo- appears in words like 'theology' and 'theocracy' to show a connection to God.
Many English words about religion use the prefix theo- to indicate something related to divinity.
In the word 'theocentric', the prefix theo- means that God is at the center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deus- - a Latin prefix meaning 'god', used in words about deities
