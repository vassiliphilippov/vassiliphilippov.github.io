unyoked

adjective
freed from control
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjoʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈjəʊkt / (BrE)
freed from control, obligation, or close association; no longer bound to a system, partnership, or authority

Examples

Unyoked from rules
After years of strict rules, she finally felt unyoked and able to make her own choices.
The country became unyoked from colonial rule and established its independence.
He lived a happier life once he was unyoked from his former business partner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bound - tied or restricted by obligation or control
  • synonym: liberated - freed from restrictions or control
