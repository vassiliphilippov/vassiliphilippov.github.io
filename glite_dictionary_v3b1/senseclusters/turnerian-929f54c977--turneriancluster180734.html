Turnerian

noun
admirer of Turner
Pronunciation: / tɜrˈnɪriən / (AmE), / tɜːˈnɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who admires, follows, or imitates the style of the artist J. M. W. Turner

Examples

A Turnerian at the gallery
As a passionate Turnerian, he traveled to see every major exhibition of Turner's paintings.
The art club included several Turnerians who discussed the influence of light in landscape painting.
Her Turnerian approach was evident in the swirling clouds and bold colors of her artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Turnerite - a follower or admirer of J. M. W. Turner
