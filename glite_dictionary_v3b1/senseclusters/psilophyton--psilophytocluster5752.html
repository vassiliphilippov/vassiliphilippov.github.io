psilophyton

noun
an extinct genus of early vascular plants
Pronunciation: / saɪləˈfaɪtən / (AmE), / sɪləˈfʌɪtən / (BrE)
any plant or fossil belonging to the extinct genus Psilophyton, known from the Devonian period and significant in the evolution of early vascular plants.

Examples

Fossil psilophyton plant
Scientists discovered a fossilized psilophyton in the Canadian arctic, shedding light on early land plants.
Several species within the genus psilophyton have helped researchers understand the evolution of vascular systems in plants.
The structure of psilophyton stems suggests adaptation to life on land during the Devonian period.
