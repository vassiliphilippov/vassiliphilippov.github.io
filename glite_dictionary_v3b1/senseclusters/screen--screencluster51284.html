screen

noun
surface for projecting images
1.0 88/m
Pronunciation: / skrin / (AmE), / skriÀên / (BrE)
a surface or electronic display where images, videos, or data are shown (such as in a cinema, on a computer, or TV)

Examples

A computer screen
He set up a projector and screen in the conference room for the meeting.
Please lower the screen so we can start the presentation.
The movie was shown on a huge screen in the middle of the park.
A message appeared suddenly on the television screen while I was watching the news.
He stared at the computer screen for hours, trying to finish his project.
The movie looked fantastic on the big screen at the theater.

Synonyms

  • display
    2.5 24/m
    display usually refers to the electronic device that shows images or data, while screen refers to the surface or panel where images appear.
  • video display
    3.4 0.22/m
    video display is an electronic device that shows moving images or video, while screen is the surface or panel where those images appear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - a device or surface for showing images or data
  • synonym: monitor - an electronic visual display for computers or televisions
