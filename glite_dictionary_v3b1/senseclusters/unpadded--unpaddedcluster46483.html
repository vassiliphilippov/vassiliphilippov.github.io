unpadded

adjective
without extra cushioning
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpædɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpædɪd / (BrE)
not containing any extra soft material for comfort or protection

Examples

Unpadded seat
The unpadded bench made the long wait at the station uncomfortable.
She preferred unpadded envelopes for sending simple letters.
Wearing unpadded gloves in winter left his hands cold and stiff.

Related Terms

  • antonym: padded - containing extra soft material for comfort or protection
