amalgamator

noun
one who arranges company amalgamations
Pronunciation: / əˈmælɡəˌmeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈmalɡəmeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or company that arranges or effects the merger of two or more commercial companies

Examples

An amalgamator of companies
As an experienced amalgamator, she successfully negotiated several complex corporate mergers.
The amalgamator played a crucial role in bringing the rival firms together.
The company hired an amalgamator to oversee the fusion of their business with a competitor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: merger - the combination of two or more companies into one
