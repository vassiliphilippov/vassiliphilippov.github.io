chiropractor

noun
musculoskeletal specialist, spinal manipulator
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪrəˌpræktər / (AmE), / ˈkaɪərəʊˌpræktə / (BrE)
a healthcare professional trained to diagnose and treat musculoskeletal disorders, especially by manual adjustment or manipulation of the spine

Examples

The chiropractor adjusts spine
Her chiropractor recommended daily stretching exercises to improve her posture.
I visited a chiropractor for my persistent back pain.
Many athletes rely on a chiropractor to help maintain their spinal health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physiotherapist - a healthcare professional who treats injury or dysfunction with physical methods
