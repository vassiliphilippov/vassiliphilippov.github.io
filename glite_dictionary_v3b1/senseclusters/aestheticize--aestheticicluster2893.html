aestheticize

verb
to make artistic
Pronunciation: / ɛsˈθɛtɪˌsaɪz / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪsaɪz / (BrE)
to make something appear more beautiful or artistic, often by focusing on its visual or aesthetic qualities
