unism

noun
art theory of unity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪzəm / (BrE)
a theory or artistic style that emphasizes unity and wholeness in composition

Examples

Unism in painting
The painter's commitment to unism is clear in the seamless blending of shapes and colors.
Many modern artists have explored unism to create a sense of harmony in their work.
The exhibition featured several pieces inspired by unism and its principles of wholeness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragmentation - the process of breaking into separate parts
  • synonym: minimalism - an art style using simple forms and limited elements
