kayak

verb
to travel in a small canoe
4.5 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkaɪ.æk/ (AmE), /ˈkaɪ.æk/ (BrE)
to travel or move in a kayak, a small, narrow boat propelled by a double-bladed paddle.

Examples

To kayak a river

She loves to kayak on the lake early in the morning when it's quiet.
They kayaked down the river, enjoying the scenery and wildlife on both banks.
