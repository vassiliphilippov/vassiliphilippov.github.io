bagger

noun
machine that puts things into bags
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈbaɡə / (BrE)
a machine for placing objects or substances into bags, especially in industrial or commercial settings

Examples

Automatic bagger machine
A bagger automatically fills each bag with the correct weight of flour.
The factory installed a new bagger to improve packaging efficiency.
They called the technician when the bagger malfunctioned during production.
