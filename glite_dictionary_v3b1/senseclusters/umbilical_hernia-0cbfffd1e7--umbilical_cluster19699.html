umbilical hernia

noun
abdominal bulge near navel (medical)
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl ˈhɜrniə / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl ˈhɜːniə / (BrE)
a condition in which part of the intestine bulges through the abdominal wall near the navel

Examples

An umbilical hernia bulge
Her umbilical hernia was monitored closely, but no complications developed.
Surgery is rarely required for an umbilical hernia in young children, as it often resolves naturally.
The doctor diagnosed the infant with an umbilical hernia that would likely heal on its own.
