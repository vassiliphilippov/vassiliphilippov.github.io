Synonyms

  • luminance
    7.1 0.33/m
    luminance measures how bright a surface appears to the eye, while brightness level refers to the overall light emitted or reflected by an object.
  • radiance
    6.8 0.30/m
    radiance describes light power leaving or arriving at a surface point in specific directions, while brightness level measures visible light amount on an object.
  • light intensity
    3.9 0.23/m
    light intensity is the measured amount of visible light sent from a source in one direction, while brightness level is about emitted or reflected light on objects.
  • luminosity
    7.3 1.30/m
    luminosity means the total light power a body gives off, whereas brightness level refers to how much light an object emits or reflects nearby.
