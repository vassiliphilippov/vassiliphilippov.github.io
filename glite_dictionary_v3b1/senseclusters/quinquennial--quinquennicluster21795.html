quinquennial

noun
five-year period
9.6
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˈkwɛniəl / (AmE), / kwɪnˈkwɛnɪəl / (BrE)
a period of five years, or an event that occurs every five years

Examples

A quinquennial meeting
The committee meets for a quinquennial to review the progress of the project.
Every quinquennial, the city hosts a festival to celebrate its founding.
The organization publishes a quinquennial report summarizing its achievements over the past five years.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: biennial - an event or period lasting two years or occurring every two years
  • degree higher: decennial - an event or period lasting ten years or occurring every ten years
