radiotherapeutic

adjective
using radiation therapy
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊˌθɛr.iˈpjuː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊˌθɛr.ɪˈpjuː.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or used in the treatment of disease with radiation

Examples

Radiotherapeutic treatment
The doctor recommended a radiotherapeutic approach to treat the patient's cancer.
Advances in radiotherapeutic techniques have improved survival rates for many diseases.
A radiotherapeutic procedure was scheduled to target the affected area precisely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chemotherapeutic - relating to the treatment of disease with chemicals or drugs
  • synonym: radiation therapy - the treatment of disease, especially cancer, using radiation
