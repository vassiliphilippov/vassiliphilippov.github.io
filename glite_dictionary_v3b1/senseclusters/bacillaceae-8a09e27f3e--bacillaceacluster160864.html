Bacillaceae

Examples

Bacillaceae family bacteria
Identifying bacteria within the Bacillaceae family requires Gram staining and microscopic examination.
Some species in the Bacillaceae family are used industrially for enzyme production.
The Bacillaceae are known for their ability to survive extreme conditions by forming spores.
