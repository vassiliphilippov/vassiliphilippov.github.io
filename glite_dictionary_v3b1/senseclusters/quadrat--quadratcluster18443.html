quadrat

noun
sampling frame in ecology
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːdræt / (AmE), / ˈkwɒdrət / (BrE)
a square frame or plot of land used by scientists to sample or study the distribution of plants or animals in a specific area

Examples

Researchers use a quadrat to estimate the population density of small animals in a habitat.
