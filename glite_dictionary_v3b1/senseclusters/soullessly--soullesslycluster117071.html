soullessly

adverb
in a lifeless or emotionless way
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊl.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.ləs.li / (BrE)
in a manner that lacks emotion, spirit, or human feeling; in a mechanical or lifeless way

Examples

To act soullessly
Rows of identical houses stretched soullessly across the horizon.
She answered the questions soullessly, barely looking up from her papers.
The speech was delivered soullessly, as if he didn't believe a word he was saying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotionlessly - without showing emotion
  • synonym: mechanically - in a mechanical, automatic way, without feeling
