soullessly

adverb
in a lifeless or emotionless way
5.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.ləs.liː/ /; / /ˈsoʊ.les.liː/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.ləs.liː/ /; / /ˈsəʊ.les.liː/ (BrE)
