coloratura

noun
lyric soprano specializing in ornate vocal music
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.ər.əˈtʊr.ə / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.ər.əˈtjʊə.rə / (BrE)
A lyric soprano singer who specializes in performing elaborate vocal music with florid, ornamented passages.

Examples

A coloratura soprano
As a gifted coloratura, she captivated audiences with her agile and expressive singing.
Many coloratura sopranos master the complex trills and runs required in Mozart's operas.
The soprano's brilliance as a coloratura earned her roles in several major opera houses.
