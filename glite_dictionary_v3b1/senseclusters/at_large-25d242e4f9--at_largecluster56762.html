at large

adverb
in a general way; broadly
6.9
Pronunciation: / ət ˈlɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ət ˈlɑːdʒ / (BrE)
in a general or broad way; as a whole, especially referring to groups or the public

Examples

The escaped prisoner is still at large and considered dangerous.
These issues concern society at large, not just a single group.
