village-like

adjective
resembling a village
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪlɪdʒˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈvɪlɪdʒˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a small rural community, especially in atmosphere or appearance

Examples

Village-like atmosphere
The new housing development was designed to have a village-like feel, with small shops and open spaces.
Residents appreciated the village-like charm of their quiet neighborhood.
Despite being in the city, the area maintained a village-like sense of community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than the city
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
