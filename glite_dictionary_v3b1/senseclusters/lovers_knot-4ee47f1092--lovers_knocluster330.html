lovers' knot

noun
decorative knot symbolizing love
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvɚz nɑt / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəz nɒt / (BrE)
a stylized or decorative knot symbolizing love or the bond between lovers, often used as an emblem or motif

Examples

A lovers' knot symbol
Many couples exchange jewelry featuring a lovers' knot to symbolize their eternal bond.
She admired the delicate lovers' knot pattern embroidered on the wedding handkerchief.
The antique brooch was shaped like a lovers' knot encrusted with small pearls.
