cryogenically

adverb
at very low temperatures
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkraɪəʊˈdʒɛnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner involving very low temperatures, especially for preservation or storage purposes

Examples

Stored cryogenically
The laboratory stores samples cryogenically to keep them viable.
Some seeds are preserved cryogenically for future research and restoration projects.
The patient’s body was cryogenically frozen in hopes of future revival.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: frozen - preserved by being made very cold until solid
  • degree lower: refrigerated - kept cool or cold for preservation
