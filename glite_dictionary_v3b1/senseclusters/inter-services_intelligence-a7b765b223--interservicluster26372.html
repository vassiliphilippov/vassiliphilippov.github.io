Inter-Services Intelligence

proper noun
Pakistan's main intelligence agency
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntər ˈsɜːrvɪsɪz ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns / (AmE), / ˌɪntə ˈsɜːvɪsɪz ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns / (BrE)
the principal intelligence agency of Pakistan, influential in political and military affairs, and known for its activities both domestically and internationally.
