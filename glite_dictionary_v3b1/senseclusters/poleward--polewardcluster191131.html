poleward

adjective, adverb
toward a pole
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlwɚd /; / ˈpoʊlwərd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlwəd / (BrE)
toward or closer to either the North or South Pole of the Earth (used as an adjective or adverb)
