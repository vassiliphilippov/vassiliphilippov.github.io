starchiness

noun
formality or stiffness
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtʃinəs / (BrE)
a quality of being very formal, stiff, or lacking warmth in manner or style

Examples

Social starchiness
His starchiness made it difficult for colleagues to relax around him at work.
The starchiness of the ceremony gave it a very serious and formal atmosphere.
Despite her starchiness, she was respected for her professionalism and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formality - the quality of being formal and proper
  • synonym: stiffness - the quality of being rigid or not relaxed
  • antonym: warmth - friendliness and kindness in manner
