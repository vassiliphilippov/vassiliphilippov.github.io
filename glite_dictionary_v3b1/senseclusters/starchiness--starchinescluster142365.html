starchiness

noun
formality or stiffness

Pronunciation: / Ààst…ërt Éin…ôs / (AmE), / Ààst…ëÀêt Éin…ôs / (BrE)

Examples

Social starchiness

The starchiness of the ceremony gave it a very serious and formal atmosphere.
Despite her starchiness, she was respected for her professionalism and attention to detail.
