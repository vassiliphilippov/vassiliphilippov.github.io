hiplife

adjective
Ghanaian music genre
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɪpˌlaɪf / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Ghanaian music genre that blends hip hop with highlife.

Examples

Hiplife artist
The festival featured several hiplife artists who energized the crowd with their unique sound.
She wore a hiplife inspired outfit to celebrate Ghanaian culture at the concert.
Many young people enjoy the hiplife style for its blend of rap and traditional rhythms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hip hop - a genre of popular music developed in the United States featuring rhythmic speech and beats
