unfrugal

adjective
wasteful with money
9.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàfruÀê…°…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàfruÀê…°…ôl / (BrE)
not careful with money or resources; spending or using things wastefully

Examples

An unfrugal spender
Her unfrugal approach to shopping left her with little money at the end of the month.
The company suffered losses due to its unfrugal spending on unnecessary projects.
He realized his unfrugal lifestyle was unsustainable and decided to make changes.

Synonyms

  • unthrifty
    8.6 0.00/m
    unthrifty is commonly used to mean careless with money, while unfrugal sounds rarer and also means wasteful with spending.
  • thriftless
    8.5 0.01/m
    thriftless says someone lacks thrift or economy in general, while unfrugal refers more to wasteful spending or using things carelessly.
  • wasteful
    3.0 3.71/m
    wasteful is using more of something than needed in any setting, while unfrugal specifically means careless with money or resources.
