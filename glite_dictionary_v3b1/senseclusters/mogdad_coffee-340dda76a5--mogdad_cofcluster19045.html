mogdad coffee

noun
a tropical shrub used as coffee adulterant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑɡdæd ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / ˈmɒɡdad ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a tropical shrub (Cassia occidentalis) with malodorous leaves, whose seeds are sometimes used as an adulterant for coffee

Examples
