dive-bomb

verb
attack by bombing in a steep dive
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪv bɑm / (AmE), / ˈdaɪv bɒm / (BrE)
To attack (a target) by dropping bombs from a steeply diving aircraft.

Examples

To dive-bomb a target
During the war, pilots were trained to dive-bomb strategic targets.
Several gulls dive-bomb unsuspecting beachgoers in search of food.
The fighter planes dive-bombed the enemy supply trucks at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strafe - attack repeatedly with bombs or machine-gun fire from low-flying aircraft
