snark

noun
cryptographic proof
9.9
Pronunciation: /snɑːrk/ (AmE), /snɑːk/ (BrE)
a short, non-interactive cryptographic proof that allows one party to prove possession of information without revealing it, used for efficient verification in computer science and blockchain systems
