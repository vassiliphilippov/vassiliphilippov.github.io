pry bar

noun
iron lever tool used for prying
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪ bɑr / (AmE), / ˈpraɪ bɑː / (BrE)
A heavy iron bar with one end shaped like a wedge, used to force objects apart or to remove nails.

Examples

A pry bar tool
A pry bar can be handy when removing old, stubborn nails.
She grabbed the pry bar from the toolbox to open the stuck window.
The mechanic used a pry bar to lift the heavy crate off the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crowbar - a similar iron bar used as a lever
