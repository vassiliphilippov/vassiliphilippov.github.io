noun
person who operates a balloon

Examples

Synonyms

  • ballooner
    6.1 0.01/m
    A ballooner pilots hot air balloons for sport or events, while a balloonist is the general term for any balloon pilot.
  • aeronaut
    9.6 0.14/m
    An aeronaut pilots or travels in airships or balloons on lighter-than-air craft, while a balloonist specifically flies or operates balloons.
