araneiform

noun
spider-like formation
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪniəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / əˈreɪnɪəˌfɔːm / (BrE)
a structure or formation that resembles a spider or spider web, especially a radial surface feature on Mars formed by seasonal gas activity

Examples

A Martian araneiform
Scientists mapped each araneiform on the polar surface to study seasonal gas eruptions.
The rover captured images of a unique araneiform pattern on the Martian ground.
Researchers believe the araneiform features are created by carbon dioxide gas escaping beneath the ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radial - arranged like rays or radii from a center
  • synonym: spiderweb - a web spun by a spider
