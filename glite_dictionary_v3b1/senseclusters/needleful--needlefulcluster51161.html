needleful

noun
amount a needle holds
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdlfʊl / (AmE), / ˈniːdlfʊl / (BrE)
the amount of thread, yarn, or other material that a needle can hold at one time

Examples

A needleful of thread
She threaded a needleful of blue silk for the next stitch.
He carefully pulled a needleful of thread through the fabric to mend the tear.
After using up one needleful, she paused to rethread before continuing her embroidery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handful - the amount that can be held in one hand
  • synonym: spoonful - the amount that a spoon can hold
