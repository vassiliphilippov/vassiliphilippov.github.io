crest

noun
heraldic device on a helmet
5.2
Pronunciation: / krɛst / (AmE), / krɛst / (BrE)
(heraldry) a distinctive device or emblem originally used on a helmet in medieval times, often forming part of a coat of arms.

Examples

Family crest
Many noble families have a unique crest as part of their heraldry.
The crest atop his armor made him easily identifiable on the battlefield.
The knight's helmet was adorned with a silver crest shaped like a lion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coat of arms - heraldic design on a shield or escutcheon
