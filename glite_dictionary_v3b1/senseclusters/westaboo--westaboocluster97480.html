westaboo

noun
person overly admiring Western culture
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstəˌbu / (AmE), / ˈwɛstəˌbuː / (BrE)
a person who has an excessive admiration for Western culture, customs, or products, especially to the point of favoring them over their own culture

Examples

A westaboo person
Growing up in Asia, he was often teased for his obvious westaboo interests in cuisine and fashion.
Her westaboo tendencies showed in her collection of American pop music and movies.
Some critics accused the director of being a westaboo for prioritizing Western storytelling styles.
