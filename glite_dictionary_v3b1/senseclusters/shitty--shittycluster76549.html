shitty

adjective
very bad (vulgar)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈʃɪti / (BrE)
Very bad; of extremely poor quality or standard, often used as a vulgar term to express strong disapproval or disappointment.

Examples

A shitty day
He apologized for his shitty behavior last night.
I had a shitty day at work and just want to go to bed.
The movie was so shitty that we left before it ended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excellent - extremely good, of high quality
  • synonym: lousy - very bad or poor in quality
