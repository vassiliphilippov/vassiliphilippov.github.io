differential

noun
mechanical device allowing wheels to turn at different speeds
8.7 6.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.fər.ˈen.ʃəl/ (AmE), /dɪ.fə.ˈren.ʃəl/ /; / /dɪ.fər.ˈen.ʃəl/ (BrE)
A mechanical device, especially in a vehicle, that allows two shafts or wheels to rotate at different speeds, typically used in the drive axles.

Examples

Car differential gear
A limited-slip differential helps improve performance when cornering at high speeds.
Modern trucks use a locking differential for better traction on slippery roads.
The car's differential was replaced after it started making unusual noises.

  • synonym: gearbox - mechanism transmitting power through gears in a vehicle
