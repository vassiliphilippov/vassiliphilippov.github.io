Examples

Feel light-headed

Synonyms

  • lightheaded
    4.4 0.52/m
    lightheaded is the single-word form common in modern informal and medical notes, while light-headed names the dizzy, weak feeling.
  • woozy
    5.7 1.02/m
    woozy feels unsteady and hazy, often with dizziness, while light-headed describes a specific dizzy, weak feeling as if one might faint.
  • dizzy
    1.0 11/m
    dizzy has a spinning or whirling sensation and balance trouble, while light-headed also includes general weakness and a faint feeling.
  • faint
    2.7 2.05/m
    faint is feeling close to losing consciousness or actually losing it, while light-headed stresses dizziness and light weakness before fainting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dizzy - feeling as if you might lose your balance or fall
  • synonym: faint - to feel weak and as if you are about to lose consciousness
