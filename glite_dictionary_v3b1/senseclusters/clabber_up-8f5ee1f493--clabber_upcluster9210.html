clabber up

verb
to curdle or thicken milk
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklæbər ʌp / (AmE), / ˈklæbə ʌp / (BrE)
(of milk or cream) to sour, curdle, or thicken into clabber.

Examples

To clabber up milk
If you leave fresh milk out in the heat, it will clabber up overnight.
She forgot about the jug, and it started to clabber up on the windowsill.
The recipe called for the cream to clabber up before being churned into butter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curdle - to form curds or lumps, especially in milk
  • synonym: sour - (of milk) to become acidic or spoiled
