yarmulke

noun
Jewish skullcap worn at prayer
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrməlkə / (AmE), / ˈjɑːmʊlkə / (BrE)
A small, round skullcap traditionally worn by Jewish men, especially during prayer or religious ceremonies.

Examples

A yarmulke cap
He placed his yarmulke on his head before entering the synagogue.
Many people wore a yarmulke at the bar mitzvah ceremony.
Some Jewish students wear a yarmulke to school every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kippah - another term for a Jewish skullcap
