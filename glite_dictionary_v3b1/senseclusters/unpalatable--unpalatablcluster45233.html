unpalatable

adjective
not pleasant to taste or accept
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæl.ə.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpæl.ə.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not pleasant to taste or difficult to accept or agree with

Examples

The unpalatable fact
He had to face the unpalatable fact that he might never walk again.
She found his arrogant attitude completely unpalatable.
The medicine left an unpalatable aftertaste that lingered for hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: palatable - pleasant to taste or acceptable to the mind
