cretaceous

adjective
abounding in or containing chalk
8.0
Pronunciation: / krɪˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / krɪˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of rock or soil) abounding in chalk; consisting mainly of chalk.

Examples

Cretaceous rock
Much of the local farmland is situated on cretaceous soils that favor certain crops.
The cliffs in that region have a distinctly cretaceous composition.
The geologist examined the cretaceous layers for evidence of ancient sea life.
