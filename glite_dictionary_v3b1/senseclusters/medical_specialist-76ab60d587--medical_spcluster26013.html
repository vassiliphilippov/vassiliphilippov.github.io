medical specialist

noun
doctor with expertise in one field
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈspɛʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈspɛʃəlɪst / (BrE)
A doctor who has expert training and practices in a particular branch of medicine.

Examples

A medical specialist doctor
After months without answers, she finally saw a medical specialist for her rare symptoms.
Becoming a medical specialist often requires years of additional training beyond medical school.
The hospital referred him to a medical specialist in cardiology for further evaluation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: general practitioner - a doctor who is trained in general medicine rather than a specialized area
  • synonym: specialist - a person who is an expert in a particular field
