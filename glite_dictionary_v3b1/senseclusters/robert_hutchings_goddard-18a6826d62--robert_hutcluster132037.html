Robert Hutchings Goddard

proper noun
U.S. physicist and rocket pioneer
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑː.bərt ˈhʌtʃ.ɪŋz ˈgɑː.dərd/ (AmE), /ˈrɒ.bət ˈhʌtʃ.ɪŋz ˈgɒd.əd/ (BrE)
(1882–1945) United States physicist who developed the first successful liquid-fueled rocket, regarded as a pioneer of modern rocketry.
