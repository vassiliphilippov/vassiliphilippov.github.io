reindeer lichen

noun
a grey, branching Arctic lichen
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪn.dər ˈlaɪ.kən/ /; / /ˈreɪn.dɪr ˈlaɪ.kən/ (AmE), /ˈreɪn.dɪə ˈlaɪ.kən/ /; / /ˈreɪn.dɪə ˈlɪtʃ.ən/ (BrE)
