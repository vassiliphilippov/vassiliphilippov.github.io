parkinsonian

adjective
like Parkinson's disease
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑrkɪnˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌpɑːkɪnˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
showing symptoms or characteristics similar to those of Parkinson's disease, such as tremors or slow movement

Examples

Parkinsonian symptoms
The patient developed a parkinsonian gait after years of medication.
Doctors noticed several parkinsonian features during the neurological examination.
Her handwriting became smaller and shaky, a common parkinsonian symptom.
