to shine with a brief or small flash of reflected light, sometimes as if wet or glossy

Examples

To glint in the sun
A hint of gold glinted in her hair when she stood by the window.
His watch glinted as he raised his hand to check the time.
The sun made the dewdrops on the grass glint in the early morning light.

Synonyms

  • gleam
    1.9 9.49/m
    gleam is a soft, steady shine on smooth surfaces, while glint is a quick, small flash of reflected light.
  • twinkle
    2.3 0.41/m
    twinkle is a repeated quick sparkle like stars, while glint is a single brief flash of reflected light on a shiny surface.
  • flash
    1.0 8.36/m
    flash is a sudden bright burst of light, often from a source, while glint is a small reflected flash on a shiny surface.
