high-stakes game

noun
game with serious consequences
Pronunciation: / haɪ steɪks ɡeɪm / (AmE), / haɪ steɪks ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game or situation in which the outcome has significant consequences or risks for the participants

Examples

Negotiating the merger turned into a high-stakes game between rival companies.
The championship final was a high-stakes game that kept fans on the edge of their seats.
