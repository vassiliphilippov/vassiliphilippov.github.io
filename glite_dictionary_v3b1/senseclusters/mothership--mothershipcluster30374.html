mothership

noun
central organization
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəʃɪp / (BrE)
a central organization, company, or base that provides support, direction, or resources to smaller groups or subsidiaries, often used figuratively.
