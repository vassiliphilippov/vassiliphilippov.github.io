Canadian bacon

noun
bacon from cured pork loin
9.1 2.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪdiən ˈbeɪkən / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈbeɪkən / (BrE)
A type of bacon made from cured, boneless pork loin, usually leaner and rounder than streaky bacon.

Examples

Canadian bacon slice
Canadian bacon is much leaner than the traditional American style.
He ordered eggs Benedict with a slice of Canadian bacon on top.
The pizza was topped with pineapple and Canadian bacon, creating a savory-sweet flavor.
