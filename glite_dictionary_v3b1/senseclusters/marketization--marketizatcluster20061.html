marketization

noun
introduction of market principles
7.4 0.34/m
Pronunciation: / Àåm…ërk…ôt…ôÀàze…™ É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåm…ëÀêk…™ta…™Ààze…™ É…ôn / (BrE)
the process of introducing or increasing market principles, such as competition and private ownership, or making something available for sale in a commercial market

Examples

Synonyms

  • commercialization
    6.3 2.17/m
    commercialization focuses on bringing products or ideas into sale to make profit, while marketization covers wider shifts to market rules and ownership.
  • commodification
    8.4 0.77/m
    commodification turns ideas, services, or relationships into things to buy, while marketization means wider introduction of competition and private ownership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercialization - the process of bringing something to the market for profit
  • synonym: liberalization - the removal of restrictions to allow for more competition and free-market activity
  • antonym: nationalization - the transfer of private assets into public ownership
  • synonym: privatization - the transfer of ownership from public to private hands
