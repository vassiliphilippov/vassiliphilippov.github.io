Abies bracteata

noun
fir tree species native to California
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz ˌbrækˈtiː.ə.tə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.biː.iːz ˌbrækˈtiː.ə.tə / (BrE)
A pyramidal fir tree native to southwestern California, noted for its spiny pointed leaves and cone scales with long spines.
