titan

noun
person of great influence or importance
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.t̬ən / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.tən / (BrE)
a person of great importance, power, or influence in a particular field

Examples

A titan of industry
Among the writers of her generation, she is considered a true titan.
He was a titan of the technology industry, shaping the way we use computers today.
The art world mourned the loss of a titan whose influence spanned decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giant - a person or thing of great size or importance
