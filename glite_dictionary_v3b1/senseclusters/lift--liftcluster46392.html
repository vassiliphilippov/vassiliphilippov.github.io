lift

verb
take words/ideas without credit
Pronunciation: / lɪft / (AmE), / lɪft / (BrE)
to take and use words or ideas from someone else's work without proper acknowledgment, especially in writing or speech

Examples

To lift text
He was accused of having lifted entire paragraphs from a published article.
Some students lift phrases from online sources without giving credit.
The speech sounded original, but much of it was lifted from famous authors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plagiarize - to copy someone else's work and pretend it is your own
