unsuspended

adjective
not stopped or hung
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈspɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈspɛndɪd / (BrE)
not temporarily stopped, hung, or kept from falling; continuing or remaining in place without interruption

Examples

Unsuspended service
The train service remained unsuspended despite the heavy snowfall.
All privileges were unsuspended after the investigation found no wrongdoing.
The bridge was left unsuspended, supported by solid pillars instead of cables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: active - currently in operation or effect
  • antonym: suspended - temporarily stopped or interrupted
