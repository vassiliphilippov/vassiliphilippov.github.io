glassie

noun
small drinking glass
9.9
an informal term for a small drinking glass or tumbler

Examples

A small glassie
She poured some juice into a glassie for her little brother.
After dinner, he washed his glassie and put it back in the cupboard.
Could you hand me that glassie on the counter, please?

Related Terms

  • antonym: mug - a large cup with a handle, used for hot drinks
  • synonym: tumbler - a drinking glass with straight sides and no handle
