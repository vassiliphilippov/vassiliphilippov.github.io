Old Norse

noun
medieval Scandinavian language
Pronunciation: / oʊld nɔrs / (AmE), / əʊld nɔːs / (BrE)
an extinct North Germanic language spoken in Scandinavia and Iceland from about 700 to 1350

Examples

Old Norse language
