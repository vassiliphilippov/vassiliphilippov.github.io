ironworker

noun
steel construction worker
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.ərn.wɜːr.kər/ /; / /ˈaɪrn.wɜːr.kər/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.ən.wɜː.kə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to build or repair structures using iron or steel, such as buildings or bridges

Examples

Skilled ironworker builds
The ironworker carefully welded the beams on the new bridge.
Every ironworker on the site wore a safety harness while working at great heights.
After years as an ironworker, he became a supervisor for large construction projects.
The ironworker carefully secured the steel beams high above the city streets.
Many ironworkers wear safety harnesses while working on skyscrapers.
Becoming an ironworker requires both physical strength and technical skill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steelworker - a person who works with steel, especially in construction
  • synonym: welder - a person who joins metal parts together by heating
