incredible

adjective
impossible to believe; astonishing
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkrɛdəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛdɪbəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to believe; extraordinary or astonishing.

Examples

An incredible speed
