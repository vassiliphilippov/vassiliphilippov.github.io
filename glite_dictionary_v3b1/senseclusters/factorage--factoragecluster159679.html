factorage

noun
business of a factor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæktərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfæktərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the business or office of acting as a factor, handling and selling goods on behalf of others for a commission

Examples

A factorage office
He inherited the family factorage and quickly expanded its client base.
The merchant relied on a reputable factorage to handle his overseas shipments.
Many small producers use a factorage to sell their goods in foreign markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agency - a business that represents others in commercial transactions
  • synonym: brokerage - the business of a broker who arranges sales or purchases for others
