nontreatment

noun
absence of treatment
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / nɒnˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
the condition or decision of not giving medical or therapeutic treatment

Examples

The nontreatment group
The nontreatment group was monitored to compare outcomes with those receiving the new drug.
Researchers found that the nontreatment participants reported fewer side effects overall.
In clinical trials, a nontreatment group helps determine the effectiveness of a therapy.
The ethics committee discussed whether nontreatment was appropriate in this end-of-life situation.
Some families choose nontreatment to prioritize the patient's comfort over aggressive medical procedures.
Doctors must carefully document the decision for nontreatment in the patient's medical records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: control group - a group in a study that does not receive the experimental treatment
  • synonym: palliative care - medical care focused on comfort rather than cure
  • antonym: treatment - medical care or intervention given to a patient
