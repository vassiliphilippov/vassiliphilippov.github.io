jones

noun
strong craving
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊnz / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊnz / (BrE)
an informal term for a strong desire or craving for something, especially one that feels like an addiction

Examples

A jones for sweets
After quitting coffee, she developed a jones for chocolate in the afternoons.
He had a serious jones for adventure and was always planning his next trip.
Whenever I see a bakery, I get a jones for fresh bread.
