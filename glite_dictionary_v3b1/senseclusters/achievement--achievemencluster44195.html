achievement

noun
digital badge for completing a task
3.4 1.78/m
Pronunciation: / 59c8t83ivm59nt / (AmE), / 59c8t83id0vm59nt / (BrE)
(in computing and video games) a digital badge, token, or record awarded by a game or online platform to show that a specific milestone, task, or challenge has been completed.

Examples

Game achievement unlocked
He unlocked the achievement after completing all in-game challenges in a single session.
The game's rare achievements were displayed on his profile and impressed his friends.
She earned an achievement for completing the tutorial without using hints.

Synonyms

  • badge
    1.4 14/m
    a badge is a visible object or icon showing membership or earned tasks, while an achievement is a game's digital record of completing a milestone.
  • award
    1.0 85/m
    an award is a prize or recognition given for excellence, while an achievement is a digital badge or record for a game milestone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: badge - a symbol or token that shows that someone has achieved or belongs to something; often a digital award in apps and games
  • synonym: trophy - an award or prize given for victory or achievement; used metaphorically for virtual awards in games
