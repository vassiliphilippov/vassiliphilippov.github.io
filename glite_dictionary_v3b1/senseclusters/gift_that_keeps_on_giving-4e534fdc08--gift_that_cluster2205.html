gift that keeps on giving

noun
something with lasting benefits
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪft ðæt kips ɑn ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɪft ðæt kiːps ɒn ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
