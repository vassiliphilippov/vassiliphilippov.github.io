ultrasonogram

noun
ultrasound image
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈsɑːnəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɒnəˌɡræm / (BrE)
an image of the inside of the body made using ultrasound, usually for medical diagnosis

Examples

An ultrasonogram image
The technician showed the patient the ultrasonogram of her abdomen on the screen.
Doctors often use an ultrasonogram to monitor the growth of a fetus during pregnancy.
After the injury, an ultrasonogram helped reveal the extent of the internal damage.
The doctor reviewed the ultrasonogram to check for any abnormalities in the patient's liver.
During her pregnancy, she received an ultrasonogram to monitor the baby's development.
A clear ultrasonogram helped the surgeon plan the upcoming procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sonogram - an image produced by ultrasound, especially in medical contexts
  • synonym: ultrasound - a medical imaging technique using high-frequency sound waves
  • antonym: x-ray - an image produced by X-rays to view inside the body
