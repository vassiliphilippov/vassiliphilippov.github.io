museumgoer

noun
person visiting museums
Pronunciation: / mjuˈziəmˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / mjuˈziːəmˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who visits or frequently goes to museums

Examples

A museumgoer visits exhibits
The museumgoer spent hours exploring the ancient artifacts on display.
Many museumgoers attended the opening night of the new science exhibit.
As a frequent museumgoer, she enjoys learning about art and history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patron - a regular customer or supporter of a place or activity
  • synonym: visitor - a person who goes to a place for pleasure or interest
