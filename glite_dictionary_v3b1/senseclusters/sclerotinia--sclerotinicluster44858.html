sclerotinia

noun
a plant-pathogenic fungus genus
Pronunciation: / sklɛrəˈtɪniə / (AmE), / sklɛrəˈtɪnɪə / (BrE)
a fungus of the genus Sclerotinia, some species of which cause brown rot diseases in plants

Examples

Sclerotinia fungus disease
Scientists are researching methods to control sclerotinia infections in sunflowers.
Several crops were damaged by sclerotinia during last year's growing season.
The presence of sclerotinia can cause brown rot on fruits and vegetables.
