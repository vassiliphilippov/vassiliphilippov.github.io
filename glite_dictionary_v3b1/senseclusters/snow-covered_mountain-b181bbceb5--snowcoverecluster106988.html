snow-covered mountain

noun
mountain covered with snow
5.2
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ˈkʌvɚd ˈmaʊntɪn / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈkʌvəd ˈmaʊntɪn / (BrE)
a mountain whose surface is entirely or partially covered with snow

Examples

A snow-covered mountain
Early in the morning, the sun painted the snow-covered mountain in brilliant shades of pink and gold.
From the window, she admired the distant snow-covered mountain rising above the valley.
The climbers prepared for their ascent of the treacherous snow-covered mountain.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: mountain - a large natural elevation of the earth's surface
  • synonym: snowcapped - having a covering of snow on the top
