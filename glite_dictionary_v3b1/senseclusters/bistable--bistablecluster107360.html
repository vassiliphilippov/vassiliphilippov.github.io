bistable

noun
device with two states
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbaɪ.stə.bəl/ /; / /baɪ.ˈsteɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈbaɪ.stə.bəl/ /; / /baɪ.ˈsteɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
a device or component that has two stable states and can be switched between them, such as an electronic flip-flop

Examples

An electronic bistable
The technician tested the bistable to ensure it could reliably switch between its two states.
A bistable is often used in digital circuits to store a single bit of information.
When the bistable failed, the entire memory module stopped working correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flip-flop - an electronic circuit with two stable states
  • synonym: multivibrator - an electronic circuit used to implement a variety of simple two-state systems
