entremets

noun
multi-layered mousse dessert
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːn.trə.ˈmeɪ/ /; / /ən.trə.ˈmeɪ/ (AmE), /ɒn.trə.ˈmeɪ/ /; / /ən.trə.ˈmeɪ/ (BrE)
(in modern pastry) a multi-layered, often mousse-based plated dessert finished with glaze and decorative elements, served as a dessert course.

Examples

Chocolate entremets
The pastry chef presented elegant entremets made of chocolate mousse and raspberry gel.
Guests praised the restaurant's seasonal entremets finished with mirror glaze and edible flowers.
Her baking blog teaches how to assemble layered entremets with sponge, mousse, and crunchy praline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dessert - a course of sweet food eaten at the end of a meal
  • synonym: mousse - a light dessert made with whipped cream or egg whites, often used as a layer in entremets
