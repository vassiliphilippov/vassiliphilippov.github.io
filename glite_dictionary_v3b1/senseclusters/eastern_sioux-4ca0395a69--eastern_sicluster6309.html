Eastern Sioux

noun
member of the eastern branch of the Sioux
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərn suː / (AmE), / ˈiːstən sjuː / (BrE)
A member of the eastern branch of the Sioux people, a Native American group.

Examples

The Eastern Sioux tribe
He studied the traditions of the Eastern Sioux for his anthropology research.
Many Eastern Sioux were relocated during the 19th century due to westward expansion.
The Eastern Sioux have a distinct dialect within the Sioux language group.
