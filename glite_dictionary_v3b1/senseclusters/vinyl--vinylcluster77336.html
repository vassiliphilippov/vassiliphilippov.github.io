vinyl

noun
shiny, flexible plastic material
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈvaɪnɪl / (BrE)
a shiny, flexible, and tough plastic material, used especially for floor coverings, upholstery, and records.

Examples

Vinyl record collection
He prefers listening to music on old vinyl records rather than digital files.
The chair was covered in red vinyl that looked almost like leather.
We chose a vinyl floor for the kitchen because it's easy to clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PVC - polyvinyl chloride, a common type of vinyl plastic
