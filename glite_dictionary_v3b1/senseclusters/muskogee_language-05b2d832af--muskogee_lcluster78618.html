Muskogee language

noun
language of the Muskogee people
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌsˈkoʊɡi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / mʌsˈkəʊɡi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The language of the Muskogee people, belonging to the Muskogean language family, traditionally spoken in parts of the southeastern United States.

Examples

Muskogee language spoken
Linguists have documented over 70% of the Muskogee language’s traditional vocabulary.
Preservation of the Muskogee language is important to maintaining cultural heritage.
Students enrolled in the course are learning to speak the Muskogee language.
