balladic

adjective
like a ballad
Pronunciation: / bəˈlædɪk / (AmE), / bəˈlædɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a ballad, especially in style, form, or storytelling

Examples

Balladic style music
The composer wrote a balladic piece that captured the spirit of old folk tales.
Her poetry often takes on a balladic quality, weaving stories with emotional depth.
The film's soundtrack features several balladic songs that enhance its romantic atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lyrical - expressing emotions in an imaginative and beautiful way
  • synonym: narrative - telling a story or describing a sequence of events
