thermostatically

adverb
in a way controlled by a thermostat
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner controlled or regulated by a thermostat.

Examples

To thermostatically control heating
Heating systems nowadays often operate thermostatically, automatically adjusting to room temperature.
The laboratory is thermostatically controlled to ensure accurate results.
The storage tanks are thermostatically maintained at a constant temperature.
