loup

noun
jeweler's magnifier; eye mask
9.9
Pronunciation: / luːp / (AmE), / luːp / (BrE)
a small magnifying glass used by jewelers or a half-mask that covers the eyes

Examples

Jeweler's loup
The jeweler inspected the ring with a loup to check for flaws.
At the masquerade ball, she wore a delicate black loup over her eyes.
He always keeps a loup in his pocket for examining gemstones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a covering for all or part of the face
