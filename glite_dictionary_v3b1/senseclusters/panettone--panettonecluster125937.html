panettone

noun
Italian Christmas bread
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpænəˈtoʊni / (AmE), / ˌpanɛˈtɒneɪ / (BrE)
a sweet Italian bread loaf with dried fruit, traditionally eaten at Christmas

Examples

A panettone loaf

With a cup of coffee, a slice of panettone makes a perfect winter breakfast.
