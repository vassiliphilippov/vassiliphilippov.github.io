hummer

noun
small hovering bird
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmər / (AmE), / ˈhʌmə / (BrE)
a small bird, also called a hummingbird, known for its ability to hover and rapidly beat its wings

Examples

A tiny hummer bird
A bright green hummer darted from flower to flower in the garden.
We watched a tiny hummer hover in front of the window, sipping nectar.
The rapid wing movement of a hummer makes a soft buzzing sound in the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hummingbird - a tiny bird that can hover in place by rapidly beating its wings
