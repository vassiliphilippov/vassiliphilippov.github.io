suey

interjection
calling pigs
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːi / (AmE), / ˈsuːi / (BrE)
an interjection used to call or summon pigs, especially on a farm

Examples

To shout suey for pigs
The farmer called out suey to gather the pigs for feeding time.
With a loud suey, she signaled the animals to come closer to the pen.
Children laughed as they tried shouting suey to see if the pigs would respond.
