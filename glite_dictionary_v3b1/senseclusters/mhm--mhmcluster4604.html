mhm

interjection
skeptical response
Pronunciation: / m̩hm̩ / (AmE), / m̩hm̩ / (BrE)
a short sound used to express doubt, disbelief, or skeptical disagreement, often with a questioning or sarcastic tone

Examples

Skeptical mhm
When he claimed he could run a mile in four minutes, she just replied with a skeptical mhm.
I told my brother I would clean the kitchen, and he gave me a doubtful mhm.
After hearing the unbelievable story, everyone in the room responded with a sarcastic mhm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hmm - a sound expressing hesitation or uncertainty
  • antonym: uh-huh - a sound expressing agreement or acknowledgment
