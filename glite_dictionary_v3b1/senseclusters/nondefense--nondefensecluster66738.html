nondefense

noun
civilian government spending
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
government spending, programs, or items that are not related to military or defense purposes

Examples

Nondefense budget
Congress debated whether to increase nondefense spending on education and healthcare.
The budget proposal included significant cuts to nondefense programs to reduce the deficit.
Analysts compared the growth of nondefense expenditures to military spending over the past decade.

Related Terms
