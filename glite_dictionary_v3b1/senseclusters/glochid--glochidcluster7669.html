glochid

noun
barbed bristle on cacti
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊkɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒkɪd / (BrE)
a small barbed spine or bristle, often tufted, found on cacti and related plants

Examples

Small glochids on cactus
Protect your hands when handling cacti to avoid contact with glochids.
She used tweezers to remove the tiny glochid embedded in her finger.
The glochids on the cactus can easily stick to your skin and cause irritation.
