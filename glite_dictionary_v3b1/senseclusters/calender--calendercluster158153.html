calender

verb
press material with rollers for smoothing or glazing
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləndər / (AmE), / ˈkælɪndə / (BrE)
to press paper, cloth, or other material between rollers or plates to smooth, glaze, or thin it into sheets

Examples

To calender cloth
Before packaging, the manufacturer calendered the paper for extra gloss.
The factory calenders the fabric to achieve a smooth finish.
They are calendering the sheets to make them thinner and more flexible.
