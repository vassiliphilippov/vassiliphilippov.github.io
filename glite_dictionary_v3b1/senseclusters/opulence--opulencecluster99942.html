opulence

noun
great wealth or luxuriousness
7.7 0.9/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀê.pj…ô.l…ôns / (AmE), / Àà…íp.j ä.l…ôns / (BrE)
great wealth or luxuriousness; the state of having or displaying immense riches, often in a lavish or extravagant manner

Examples

Great opulence
Despite his opulence, he lived a surprisingly modest life among the wealthy elite.
Her wedding dress embodied opulence, adorned with precious jewels and intricate lacework.
The royal palace was a symbol of opulence with its gilded halls and marble staircases.

  • luxuriousness

    luxuriousness means extreme comfort and rich materials, while opulence suggests very large wealth and public display.
  • lavishness
    6.6 0.09/m
    lavishness means giving or spending in large amounts, while opulence focuses on great wealth shown in rich style.
  • sumptuosity
    9.9 0.00/m
    sumptuosity is an old word for extreme costly luxury, while opulence is more common and means great wealth or lavish display.
  • affluence

  • luxury
    4.2 20/m

  • splendor
