astronautics

noun
science of space travel and navigation
Pronunciation: / ˌæstrəˈnɔtɪks / (AmE), / ˌæstrəˈnɔːtɪks / (BrE)
The science and technology of navigating and traveling beyond Earth's atmosphere, including the design and operation of spacecraft.

Examples

Astronautics technology
He is pursuing a degree in astronautics to work on spacecraft design.
Many remarkable discoveries in astronautics have transformed our understanding of space travel.
The university offers advanced courses in astronautics and aerospace engineering.
