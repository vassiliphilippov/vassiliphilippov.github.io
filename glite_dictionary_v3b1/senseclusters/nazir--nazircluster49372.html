nazir

noun
institutional overseer
Pronunciation: / nɑːˈziːr / (AmE), / nɑːˈziːə / (BrE)
an official or overseer in some Muslim or South Asian institutions responsible for administration or supervision

Examples

Nazir of the mosque
The nazir of the mosque inspected the repairs and arranged new funding.
A new nazir was appointed to oversee the school’s daily operations.
The nazir ensured that all administrative tasks were completed on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person responsible for running an organization
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees or manages others
