pak choi

noun
Chinese leafy vegetable
Pronunciation: / pæk tʃɔɪ / (AmE), / pæk tʃɔɪ / (BrE)
A Chinese vegetable with crisp white stalks and dark green leaves, commonly used in Asian cooking.
