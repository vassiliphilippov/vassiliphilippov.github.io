Synonyms

  • intraocular lens
    4.6 0.01/m

  • IOL
    6.1 0.04/m
    IOL is the short medical abbreviation doctors use in notes, while lens implant is the common everyday phrase patients see.
  • interocular lens implant
    5.1 0.00/m
