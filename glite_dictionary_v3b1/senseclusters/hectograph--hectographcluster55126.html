hectograph

noun
duplicating device using gelatin plate
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛktəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈhɛktəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a duplicating device that uses a gelatin plate from which ink can be transferred to make copies

Examples

A hectograph machine
Early illustrators sometimes relied on the hectograph to duplicate their sketches.
The hectograph was eventually replaced by more modern copying technologies.
The teacher produced worksheets for the class using a hectograph.
