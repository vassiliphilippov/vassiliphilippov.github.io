nasty habit

noun
unpleasant or undesirable behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈnæsti ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsti ˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
a behavior that is unpleasant, annoying, or considered socially undesirable

Examples

Nasty habit of gossiping
His nasty habit of gossiping often gets him into trouble at work.
It's important to break any nasty habit before it becomes too ingrained.
She has a nasty habit of biting her nails when she's nervous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bad habit - a regular practice that is considered undesirable or harmful
  • antonym: good habit - a regular practice that is considered beneficial or desirable
