Cardamine bulbifera

noun
a European perennial plant with a knotted white rootstock
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑːrˈdæmɪn bʌlˈbɪfərə / (AmE), / kɑːˈdamɪni bʌlˈbɪfərə / (BrE)
A European perennial plant of the mustard family, known as coralroot bittercress, characterized by its knotted white rootstock and clusters of pale lilac flowers.
