Purana

noun
ancient Hindu religious text
Pronunciation: / puˈrɑːnə / (AmE), / pjʊˈrɑːnə / (BrE)
any of a group of ancient Hindu texts containing myths, legends, and religious teachings, composed between the 1st and 11th centuries CE.

Examples

Ancient Purana texts
Scholars have translated many Purana texts into English for global audiences.
The Purana contains stories about Hindu gods and the origins of the universe.
The children listened as their grandmother recited tales from the Purana.
