backside

noun
rear side of an object
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbæk.saɪd / (BrE)
the rear side or surface of something, such as a building, document, or card

Examples

Backside of the card
Please check the backside of the building for the delivery entrance.
The package label was on the backside of the box, not the front.
There was a small tear on the backside of his shirt.
Please check the backside of the document for additional notes.
The artist wrote his signature on the backside of the canvas.
There is a label attached to the backside of the photo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rear - the back part or side of something
  • synonym: reverse - the opposite or back side of something
