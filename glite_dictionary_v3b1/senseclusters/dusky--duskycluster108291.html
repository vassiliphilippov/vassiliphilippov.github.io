dusky

adjective
having a dark skin complexion
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌski / (AmE), / ˈdʌski / (BrE)
(of a person's skin) naturally having a dark complexion.

Examples

Dusky skin tone
His dusky features are often highlighted in the family’s photo albums.
She wore a vibrant scarf that contrasted beautifully with her dusky skin.
The artist admired the model's dusky complexion as he painted her portrait.

  • synonym: swarthy - having a dark complexion
