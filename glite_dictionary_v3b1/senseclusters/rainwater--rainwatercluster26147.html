rainwater

noun
water that falls as rain
1.0 2.27/m
Pronunciation: / Ààre…™nÀåw…ët…ö / (AmE), / Ààre…™nw…îÀêt…ô / (BrE)
Water that falls from the clouds as rain and collects on the ground, usually considered fresh water.

Examples

Rainwater collection
After a heavy storm, rainwater pooled along the roadside.
Many people install systems to harvest rainwater for household use.
The rainwater collected in barrels was used for watering the garden.

Synonyms

  • pluvial
    8.9 0.00/m
    pluvial is a formal technical word for water produced by rainfall and stored, while rainwater is the common everyday name.
  • rain
    1.0 79/m
    rain falls from clouds as small drops, while rainwater is the water that collects on the ground and is used.

Related Terms
