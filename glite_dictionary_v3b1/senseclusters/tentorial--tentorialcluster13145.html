tentorial

adjective
tent-shaped
9.5
Pronunciation: / tɛnˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / tɛnˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
having the shape of a tent or relating to a tent or tent-like structure.

Examples

The museum displayed a model of a tentorial dwelling used by ancient nomads.
