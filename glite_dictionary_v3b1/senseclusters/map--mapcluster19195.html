map

noun
diagram of an area; earth's surface
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /mæp/ (AmE), /mæp/ (BrE)
A diagrammatic representation of an area of the earth's surface, showing features such as roads, cities, or countries.

Examples

A road map
He marked their route on the map before setting out on the trip.
She unfolded the map to help them find the nearest town.
The map on the wall shows all the countries of the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atlas - a book of maps or charts
  • synonym: chart - a sheet of information in the form of a table, graph, or diagram; also a nautical or aeronautical map
  • synonym: globe - a spherical representation of the earth
