sculpturally

adverb
in a sculptural way
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌlptʃɚəli / (AmE), / ˈskʌlptʃərəli / (BrE)

Examples

The furniture in the lobby is sculpturally arranged to create a sense of movement and flow.
