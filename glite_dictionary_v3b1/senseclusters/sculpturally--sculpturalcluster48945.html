sculpturally

adverb
in a sculptural way
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌlptʃɚəli / (AmE), / ˈskʌlptʃərəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to, resembles, or is characteristic of sculpture or sculptural form

Examples

Designed sculpturally
The museum's new wing is sculpturally designed to catch the light at different times of day.
Her hair was arranged sculpturally, creating a dramatic and elegant look for the event.
The furniture in the lobby is sculpturally arranged to create a sense of movement and flow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artistically - in a way that shows creative skill or art
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned way
