synonymity

noun
the state of being synonymous
9.3 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəˈnɪməti / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəˈnɪmɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of two words having the same or nearly the same meaning.

Examples

The synonymity of words
Linguists debate the synonymity of certain technical terms across different disciplines.
Some argue that perfect synonymity is rare, as words usually carry slightly different connotations.
