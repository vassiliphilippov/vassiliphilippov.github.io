to the tune of

preposition
amounting to a specified sum
Pronunciation: / tə ðə tun ʌv / (AmE), / tə ðə tjuːn ɒv / (BrE)
amounting to a specified sum, especially a large amount of money

Examples

to the tune of a large amount
The charity raised funds to the tune of several hundred thousand dollars.
The company was sued to the tune of two million dollars for breach of contract.
The renovations cost us to the tune of £50,000.
