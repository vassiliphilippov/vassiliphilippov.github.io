waterproofness

noun
imperviousness to water
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌprufnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːfnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being impervious to water; the condition of preventing water from passing through or into something.

Examples

High waterproofness level
A high level of waterproofness is essential for watches used by divers.
The waterproofness of the jacket makes it perfect for hiking in heavy rain.
We had to test the waterproofness of the tent before our camping trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterproof - not allowing water to pass through
  • synonym: watertightness - state of being so tightly sealed no water can enter or escape
