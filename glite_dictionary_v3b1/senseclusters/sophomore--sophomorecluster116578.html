sophomore

noun
second-year student (college/university, US)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːf.moʊr / (AmE), / ˈsɒf.mə / (BrE)
A student in the second year of a course at a college or university, especially in the US.

Examples

A college sophomore
After completing her freshman year, Maria returned in the fall as a sophomore.
He became president of his club during his sophomore year at college.
The university organized a special event for all sophomore students.
