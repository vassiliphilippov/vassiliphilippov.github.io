Evangelical United Brethren Church

noun
former Methodist denomination
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌivænˈdʒɛlɪkəl ˈjuːnaɪtɪd brəˈðrɪn tʃɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˌiːvanˈdʒelɪkəl ˈjuːnaɪtɪd brəˈðriːn tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
