shake hands

verb
to greet or congratulate by clasping hands
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk hændz / (AmE), / ʃeɪk hændz / (BrE)
to take someone's hand and move it up and down as a gesture of greeting, agreement, or congratulation

Examples
