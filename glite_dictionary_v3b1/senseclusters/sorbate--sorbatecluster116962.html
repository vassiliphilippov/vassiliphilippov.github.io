sorbate

noun
food preservative compound
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːrbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːbeɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of sorbic acid, commonly used as a food preservative to prevent spoilage

Examples

Potassium sorbate preservative
Potassium sorbate is commonly added to foods to prevent spoilage.
Many baked goods contain sorbate to extend their shelf life.
The label lists sorbate as one of the main preservatives in the product.
