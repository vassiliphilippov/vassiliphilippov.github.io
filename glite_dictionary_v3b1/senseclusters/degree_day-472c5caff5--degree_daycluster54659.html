degree day

noun
university graduation ceremony day
Pronunciation: / dɪˈɡri deɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈɡri deɪ / (BrE)
the day on which university degrees are officially awarded to graduates, typically during a formal ceremony

Examples

Degree day ceremony
Guests are invited to the campus for the degree day ceremony next Friday.
On his degree day, James wore his gown and cap with pride.
The entire family attended Emily's degree day to celebrate her graduation from university.
