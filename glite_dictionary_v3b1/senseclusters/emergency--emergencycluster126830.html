emergency

noun
state of crisis with special powers invoked
1.3 1.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈmɜːr.dʒən.siː/ /; / /ə.ˈmɜːr.dʒən.siː/ /; / /ˈiː.mər.dʒən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈmɜː.dʒən.siː/ /; / /ə.ˈmɜː.dʒən.siː/ (BrE)
An official state in which normal procedures are suspended and extraordinary powers, such as martial law, may be enacted to deal with a crisis.

Examples

State of emergency
After the earthquake, the president declared an emergency to restore order and deploy the military.
During the emergency, civil liberties were temporarily suspended in the worst-affected areas.
The city remained under emergency rule until the crisis was fully resolved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: martial law - military control of normal civilian functions
