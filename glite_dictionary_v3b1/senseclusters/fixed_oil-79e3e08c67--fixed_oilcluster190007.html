fixed oil

A nonvolatile oil derived from animals or plants, that does not evaporate easily and is commonly used in food, cosmetics, or medicine.

Examples

A fixed oil sample
Olive oil is a well-known example of a fixed oil used in cooking and cosmetics.
Pharmaceutical ointments often use a fixed oil as a carrier for active ingredients.
Unlike volatile oils, a fixed oil does not evaporate when exposed to air.
