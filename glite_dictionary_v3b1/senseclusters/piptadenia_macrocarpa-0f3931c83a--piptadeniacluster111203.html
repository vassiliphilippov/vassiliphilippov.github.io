Piptadenia macrocarpa

noun
Brazilian shrub with pinnate leaves and spicate flowers
Pronunciation: / pɪp.təˈdiː.ni.ə ˌmæ.krəˈkɑːr.pə / (AmE), / pɪp.təˈdiː.ni.ə ˌmæ.krəˈkɑː.pə / (BrE)
A Brazilian shrub with doubly pinnate leaves and small flower spikes, producing flat or irregularly constricted pods; sometimes classified within the genus Piptadenia.
