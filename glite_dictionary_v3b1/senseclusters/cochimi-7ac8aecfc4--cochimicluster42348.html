Cochimi

noun
member of an Indigenous group from Baja California
Pronunciation: / koʊ.tʃiːˈmiː / (AmE), / kəʊ.tʃɪˈmiː / (BrE)
A member of an Indigenous people formerly living in central Baja California, Mexico.

Examples

A Cochimi person
