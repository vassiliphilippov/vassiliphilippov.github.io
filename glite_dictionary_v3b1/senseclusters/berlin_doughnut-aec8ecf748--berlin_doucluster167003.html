Berlin doughnut

noun
jelly- or jam-filled doughnut
6.7 5.2
Pronunciation: / bɜːrˈlɪn ˈdoʊ.nʌt / (AmE), / bɜːˈlɪn ˈdəʊ.nʌt / (BrE)
A doughnut made from sweet yeast dough, deep-fried and filled with jelly or jam, often dusted with sugar.

Examples

A Berlin doughnut with jam
At breakfast, Lisa enjoyed a fresh Berlin doughnut dusted with sugar alongside her coffee.
I bought a Berlin doughnut from the bakery, and it was filled with raspberry jam.
The cafe is famous for its Berlin doughnuts, which are made with homemade berry preserves.
