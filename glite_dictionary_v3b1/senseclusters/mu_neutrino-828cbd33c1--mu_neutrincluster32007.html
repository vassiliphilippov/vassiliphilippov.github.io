mu neutrino

noun
neutrino associated with the muon
9.9
Pronunciation: / muː nuˈtriːnoʊ / (AmE), / mjuː njuːˈtriːnəʊ / (BrE)
a type of neutrino associated with the muon, one of the three flavors of neutrinos in particle physics

Examples

Mu neutrino particle
Each mu neutrino is electrically neutral and interacts only via the weak nuclear force.
Scientists detected the mu neutrino to investigate its properties in high-energy experiments.
The mu neutrino is produced when cosmic rays interact with the earth's atmosphere.
