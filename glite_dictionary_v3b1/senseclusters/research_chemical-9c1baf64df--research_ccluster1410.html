research chemical

noun

an informal term for a designer or psychoactive drug sold as a 'research chemical' for human use, often with unknown effects, safety, and legal status.

Examples

Research chemical drug
Some vendors market research chemical products as safe for recreational use despite unknown risks.
Authorities warn that ingesting a single dose of a research chemical can cause unpredictable side effects.
He bought a research chemical labelled 'not for human consumption' from an online seller.

Related Terms
