lankily

adverb
awkwardly, thinly
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋkɪli / (AmE), / ˈlæŋkɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is tall, thin, and awkward in movement or appearance

Examples

He walks lankily
The teenager moved lankily through the crowded hallway, his height making him stand out.
She danced lankily, her long arms and legs moving in unexpected directions.
The puppy ran lankily across the yard, still growing into its big paws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable way
  • antonym: gracefully - in an elegant and smooth way
