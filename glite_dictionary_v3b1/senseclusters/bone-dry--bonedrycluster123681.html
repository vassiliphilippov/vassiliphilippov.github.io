bone-dry

adjective
completely dry, no moisture
7.1
Pronunciation: / boʊn ˈdraɪ /; / boʊnˈdraɪ / (AmE), / bəʊn ˈdraɪ /; / bəʊnˈdraɪ / (BrE)
