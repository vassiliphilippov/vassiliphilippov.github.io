heavy-laden

adjective
carrying a heavy load
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛviˌleɪdən / (AmE), / ˈhɛviˌleɪdən / (BrE)
carrying a heavy load; weighed down with a physically heavy burden.

Examples

Heavy-laden with snow
After the storm, the tree branches were heavy-laden with snow.
The donkey climbed the steep hill, heavy-laden with sacks of grain.
The old truck, heavy-laden with bricks, struggled down the bumpy road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loaded - filled with as much as possible; heavily carrying something
