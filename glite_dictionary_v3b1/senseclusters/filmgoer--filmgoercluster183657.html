filmgoer

noun
cinema attendee
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly goes to the cinema to watch movies

Examples

A filmgoer at cinema
As a passionate filmgoer, she never misses a new release at the local cinema.
The festival attracted thousands of filmgoers from around the country.
Every Friday night, the group of friends became regular filmgoers at the downtown theater.
The filmgoer bought tickets for the latest blockbuster on opening night.
Many filmgoers prefer to watch movies on the big screen rather than at home.
As a dedicated filmgoer, she never misses a new release at her local cinema.
