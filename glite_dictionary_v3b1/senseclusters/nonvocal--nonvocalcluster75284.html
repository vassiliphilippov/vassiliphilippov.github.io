nonvocal

adjective
not outspoken
6.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvoʊkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈvəʊkəl / (BrE)
not expressing opinions or feelings openly; reserved or not outspoken

Examples

Nonvocal participant
Despite her strong beliefs, she remained nonvocal during the heated discussion.
His nonvocal nature made it difficult for others to know his true feelings.
In group projects, some students are nonvocal and prefer to listen rather than speak.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outspoken - expressing opinions or feelings freely and openly
  • synonym: reticent - unwilling to speak or express thoughts freely
