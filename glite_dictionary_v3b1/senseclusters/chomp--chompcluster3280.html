chomp

verb
to chew noisily or vigorously
Pronunciation: / tʃɑmp / (AmE), / tʃɒmp / (BrE)
to chew food with loud or vigorous movements of the jaws

Examples

To chomp on food
She chomped her gum noisily during the entire movie.
The child began to chomp on his apple as soon as he left the kitchen.
We could hear the horses chomping grass in the field nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: munch - to chew with steady or audible action
