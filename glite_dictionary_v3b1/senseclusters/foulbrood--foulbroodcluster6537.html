foulbrood

noun
bee larval disease
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfaʊl.bruːd/ (AmE), /ˈfaʊl.bruːd/ (BrE)
a bacterial disease that affects and kills the larvae of honeybees, often destroying entire colonies

Examples

Foulbrood disease
The beekeeper lost several hives to foulbrood last season.
Early detection of foulbrood is crucial to prevent the spread of the disease among bee colonies.
Veterinarians recommend burning infected equipment to control foulbrood outbreaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: European foulbrood - a bacterial disease of honeybee larvae caused by Melissococcus plutonius
