silkie

noun
seal-human shapeshifter
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪl.kiː/ /; / /ˈsɪl.kɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsɪl.kiː/ /; / /ˈsɪl.kɪ/ (BrE)
a mythical creature in Scottish and Irish folklore, said to be a seal that can shed its skin to become human on land

Examples

A silkie from legend
In the old story, a silkie left the sea to live among humans for many years.
Legends say that if you find a silkie's seal skin, you can control whether it stays on land.
The children listened in awe as their grandmother described the mysterious silkie who visited the village.
