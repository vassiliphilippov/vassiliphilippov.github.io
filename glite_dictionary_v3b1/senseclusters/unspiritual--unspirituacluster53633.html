unspiritual

adjective
not spiritual
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɪrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɪrɪtʃuəl / (BrE)
not interested in or connected to spiritual, religious, or moral matters; focused on material or practical things

Examples

An unspiritual attitude
His unspiritual attitude made him uninterested in the temple's traditions.
The novel explores the contrast between unspiritual ambition and a search for deeper meaning.
She found the business environment too unspiritual for her personal values.
His unspiritual attitude made him focus only on practical solutions.
The discussion was entirely unspiritual, centered on facts and logic.
She preferred an unspiritual approach to life, avoiding religious topics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: materialistic - focused on material possessions and physical comfort
  • synonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
  • antonym: spiritual - concerned with religious or higher moral values
  • synonym: worldly - concerned with ordinary life rather than spiritual matters
