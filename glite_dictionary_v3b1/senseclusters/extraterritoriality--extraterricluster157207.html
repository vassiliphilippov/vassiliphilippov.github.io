extraterritoriality

noun
jurisdiction beyond borders
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrətɛˌrɪtɔriˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrətɛˌrɪtɒriˈælɪti / (BrE)
the principle or practice of a state applying its laws or jurisdiction beyond its own territorial borders

Examples

Extraterritoriality of laws
The issue of extraterritoriality often arises in international business disputes.
Some countries object to the extraterritoriality of foreign laws affecting their citizens.
The treaty granted extraterritoriality to diplomats working abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jurisdiction - the official power to make legal decisions and judgments
  • antonym: sovereignty - supreme power or authority of a state to govern itself
