the answer to someone's prayers

noun
something that fulfills one's earnest wish
4.2
Pronunciation: / ði ˈæn.sɚ tu ˈsʌmˌwʌnz ˈprɛrz / (AmE), / ði ˈɑːn.sə tu ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈpreəz / (BrE)
a thing or person that provides exactly what someone needs or desires, often unexpectedly or after a long wait

Examples

Be the answer to their prayers
For many students, online learning has been the answer to their prayers.
Her promotion was the answer to her prayers after years of hard work.
The sudden arrival of fresh water was truly the answer to their prayers after days at sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: godsend - a very helpful or timely event or person
  • synonym: miracle - an extraordinary event, especially one that brings great benefit
