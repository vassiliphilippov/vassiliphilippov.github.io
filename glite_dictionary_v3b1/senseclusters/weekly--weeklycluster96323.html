weekly

adjective
happening every week
1.0 26/m
Pronunciation: / Ààwikli / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêkli / (BrE)
Happening, produced, or appearing once every week.

Examples

A weekly meeting
She writes a weekly column for the local newspaper.
The gardener makes a weekly visit to tend the plants.
We have a weekly meeting every Monday at 10 a.m.

Synonyms

  • every week
    1.0 11/m
    every week is the common spoken phrase for repeated events, while weekly is the single-word form used in timetables and reports.
  • each week
    1.0 7.08/m
    each week refers to individual weekly occurrences, while weekly is a single-word label often used in schedules and notices.
