questioning

noun
the act of asking questions
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of asking questions; a process or period during which questions are asked to gather information.

Examples

Police questioning

His persistent questioning made everyone uncomfortable during the meeting.
