aeroembolism

noun
medical: gas bubbles in body due to pressure change
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˈɛmbəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɛərəʊˈɛmbəlɪzəm /; / ˌeərəʊˈɛmbəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a medical condition where air or gas bubbles enter the blood and block blood vessels, often caused by rapid changes in pressure or medical procedures
