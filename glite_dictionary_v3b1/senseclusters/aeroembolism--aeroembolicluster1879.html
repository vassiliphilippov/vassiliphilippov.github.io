aeroembolism

noun
medical: gas bubbles in body due to pressure change
8.4 8.8
Pronunciation: /e.r.oʊ.ˈem.bə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /eə.rəʊ.ˈem.bə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
