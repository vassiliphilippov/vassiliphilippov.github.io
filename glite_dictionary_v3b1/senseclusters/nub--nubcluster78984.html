nub

noun
small lump or protuberance
Pronunciation: / nʌb / (AmE), / nʌb / (BrE)
a small lump, knob, or piece, especially one that sticks out or is left over

Examples

A small nub
He felt a small nub on the surface of the fabric.
Over time, a nub of metal formed from repeated use.
The statue had a nub where the arm used to be.
At the bottom of the box was only a nub of soap left.
He tossed the nub of chalk into the bin after finishing the lesson.
She chewed on the nub of her pencil as she thought about the answer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knob - a rounded lump or handle on a surface
  • synonym: protuberance - a thing that protrudes from a surface
  • synonym: stub - the short end left after the rest has been used
