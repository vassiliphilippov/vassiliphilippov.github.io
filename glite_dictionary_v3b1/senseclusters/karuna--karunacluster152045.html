karuna

noun
Buddhist compassion
Pronunciation: / kəˈruːnə / (AmE), / kəˈruːnə / (BrE)
compassion or mercy, especially as a central concept in Buddhism involving empathy for the suffering of others

Examples

The karuna of Buddha
The monk emphasized the importance of karuna in helping those who are suffering.
Practicing karuna can lead to greater understanding and kindness in daily life.
Many Buddhist teachings focus on developing karuna toward all living beings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassion - sympathetic concern for the suffering of others
  • antonym: cruelty - behavior that causes pain or suffering to others
  • synonym: empathy - the ability to understand and share another person's feelings
