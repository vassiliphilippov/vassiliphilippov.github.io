kissingly

adverb
in a kissing manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves, resembles, or suggests a kiss, either by showing affection or by touching lightly and gently

Examples

She smiled kissingly
She smiled kissingly at her partner before leaving for work.
He waved kissingly to his daughter as she boarded the school bus.
The puppy licked its owner's face kissingly after being reunited.
The breeze passed kissingly over her face as she walked along the shore.
Sunlight fell kissingly on the tops of the mountains at dawn.
He brushed her hand kissingly before saying goodbye.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affectionately - in a loving or caring way
  • synonym: gently - in a soft or mild manner
  • antonym: roughly - in a harsh or forceful manner
