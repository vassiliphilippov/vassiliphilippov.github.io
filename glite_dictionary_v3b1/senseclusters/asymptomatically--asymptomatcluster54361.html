asymptomatically

adverb
without symptoms
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪsɪmp.təˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌeɪsɪmp.təˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no symptoms of disease or illness

Examples

To be asymptomatically positive
The patient was found to be infected but remained asymptomatically positive for several weeks.
Many viruses can be spread asymptomatically, making them harder to control.
She recovered asymptomatically and never experienced any signs of illness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: symptomatically - in a way that shows symptoms of disease
