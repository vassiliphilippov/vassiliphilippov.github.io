inner workings

noun
internal mechanisms or processes
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnɚ ˈwɝːkɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈɪnə ˈwɜːkɪŋz / (BrE)
the internal mechanisms, processes, or operations that are not visible or known to outsiders, especially of an organization, system, or machine

Examples

The inner workings of government
Books about politics often reveal the inner workings of government.
Few people understand the inner workings of the human brain.
The journalist was determined to uncover the inner workings of the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mechanism - the structure or arrangement of the parts of a machine or system
  • synonym: operation - the way in which a machine or system functions
  • antonym: outer workings - the external aspects of how something functions
