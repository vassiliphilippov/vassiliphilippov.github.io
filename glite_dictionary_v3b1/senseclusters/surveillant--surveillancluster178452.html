surveillant

noun
person who monitors
8.1
Pronunciation: / sɚˈveɪənt / (AmE), / sɜːˈveɪənt / (BrE)
a person who watches over others, especially to ensure that rules or procedures are followed

Examples

A surveillant in school
A surveillant walked quietly through the classroom to make sure everyone was following the rules.
During the exam, the surveillant checked each student's identification before they began.
The museum hired a surveillant to monitor visitors and protect the artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invigilator - a person who supervises students during an exam
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees others' work or behavior
