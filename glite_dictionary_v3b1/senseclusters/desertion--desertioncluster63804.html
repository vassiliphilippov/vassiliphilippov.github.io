desertion

noun
the act of abandoning or giving up

the act of leaving, abandoning, or withdrawing support from someone, a place, or a responsibility, especially when you are expected to stay or help

Examples

Synonyms

    4.9 1.77/m

  • forsaking

  • dereliction
    8.2 0.17/m
    dereliction is failing in duty or neglecting care over time, while desertion is actively leaving someone who needs help.
