desertion

noun
the act of abandoning or giving up
3.4 5.0
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈzɜːr.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈzɜː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of leaving, abandoning, or withdrawing support from someone, a place, or a responsibility, especially when you are expected to stay or help

Examples

The desertion of the soldiers caused panic among their comrades.
There was widespread anger at the desertion of the community by local officials.
