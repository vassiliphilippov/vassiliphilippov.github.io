gently

adverb
in a soft or light manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntli /; / ˈdʒɛn.tli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntli /; / ˈdʒɛn.tli / (BrE)
in a soft, careful, or mild way; not harsh or forceful
