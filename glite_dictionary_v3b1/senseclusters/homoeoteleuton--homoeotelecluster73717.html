homoeoteleuton

noun
copying omission error
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmiˌoʊtəˈluːtən / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiəʊtɛˈluːtɒn / (BrE)
an error in writing or copying where a section of text is accidentally omitted because two lines or phrases end similarly
