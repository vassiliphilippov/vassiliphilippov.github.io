twofold

adjective
twice as great; double
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtuˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtuːfəʊld / (BrE)
twice as great or as many; double in amount or degree.

Examples

Twofold increase
She received a twofold benefit from the new policy: better pay and flexible hours.
The company's profits saw a twofold increase over the past year.
The risk became twofold after the regulations were relaxed.
