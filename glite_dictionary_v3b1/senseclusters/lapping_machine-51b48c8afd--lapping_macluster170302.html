lapping machine

noun
machine for lapping surfaces with abrasive
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈlæpɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used in the lapping process, where two surfaces are rubbed together with an abrasive material to achieve a high degree of smoothness or flatness
