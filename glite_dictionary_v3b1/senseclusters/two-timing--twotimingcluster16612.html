two-timing

adjective
cheating on a partner
5.0
Pronunciation: / tuːˌtaɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / tuːˌtaɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) having a romantic or sexual relationship with someone other than their committed partner; cheating.

Examples

A two-timing partner
She broke up with her two-timing boyfriend after discovering his affair.
The novel tells the story of a two-timing husband who finally gets caught.
There was no excuse for his two-timing behavior in the relationship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithful - loyal or trustworthy in a relationship
  • synonym: unfaithful - not remaining loyal to a partner
