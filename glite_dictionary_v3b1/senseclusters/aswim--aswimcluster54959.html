aswim

adjective
covered with liquid
Pronunciation: / əˈswɪm / (AmE), / əˈswɪm / (BrE)
full of or covered with liquid, especially water, or appearing to be swimming or floating

Examples

Fields aswim with water
After the heavy rain, the fields were aswim with puddles and mud.
Her eyes were aswim with tears as she listened to the sad story.
The boat appeared aswim in the moonlit lake, gently rocking on the water.

  • antonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid
