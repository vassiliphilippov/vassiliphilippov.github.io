gargoyle

noun
grotesque carved waterspout on buildings
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrˌɡɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːɡɔɪl / (BrE)
a grotesquely carved stone figure, often used as a decorative waterspout on buildings (especially in Gothic architecture)
