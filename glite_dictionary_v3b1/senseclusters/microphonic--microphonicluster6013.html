microphonic

noun
early microphone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkrəˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
an early or rare term for a microphone, a device that converts sound into an electrical signal

Examples

A microphonic device
The scientist demonstrated how the microphonic could pick up even the faintest sounds.
In early radio broadcasts, a microphonic was essential for capturing live performances.
Collectors sometimes find a vintage microphonic among old audio equipment.
