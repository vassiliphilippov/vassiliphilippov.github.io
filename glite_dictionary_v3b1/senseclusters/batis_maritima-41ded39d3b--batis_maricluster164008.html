Batis maritima

noun
coastal shrub native to the Americas
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪtɪs məˈrɪtɪmə / (AmE), / ˈbætɪs məˈrɪtɪmə / (BrE)
A low-growing, strong-smelling shrub native to coastal areas of the Americas, with unisexual flowers in cone-like spikes and thick, succulent leaves.
