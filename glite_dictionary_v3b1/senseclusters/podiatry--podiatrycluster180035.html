podiatry

noun
branch of medicine for feet
7.3
Pronunciation: / poʊˈdaɪətri / (AmE), / pəˈdaɪətri / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases and disorders of the feet

Examples

Podiatry clinic
Advances in podiatry have made it easier to manage sports injuries affecting the feet.
She scheduled an appointment with the podiatry clinic to treat her foot pain.
The hospital offers specialized podiatry services for diabetic patients.
