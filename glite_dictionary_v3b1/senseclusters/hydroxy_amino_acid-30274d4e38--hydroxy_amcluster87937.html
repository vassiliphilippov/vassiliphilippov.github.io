hydroxy amino acid

noun
an amino acid with a hydroxy group
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑksi əˈminoʊ ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒksi əˈmiːnəʊ ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
an amino acid containing a hydroxy group in its side chain, such as serine or threonine

Examples

Hydroxy amino acids in enzymes
