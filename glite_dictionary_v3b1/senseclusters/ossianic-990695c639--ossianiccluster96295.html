Ossianic

adjective
of Ossian's poetry
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːsiˈænɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒsiˈænɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Ossian or his poetry, especially in style or mood

Examples

Ossianic poetry style
The novel's Ossianic atmosphere gave it a sense of ancient mystery and melancholy.
Critics praised the Ossianic imagery that evoked wild landscapes and heroic legends.
Her music has an Ossianic quality, filled with longing and echoes of Celtic tradition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classical - relating to ancient Greek or Roman art and literature
  • synonym: romantic - having qualities of emotion, imagination, or adventure
