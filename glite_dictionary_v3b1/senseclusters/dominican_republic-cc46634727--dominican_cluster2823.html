Dominican Republic

proper noun
country in the Caribbean on Hispaniola
Pronunciation: / dəˌmɪnɪkən rəˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / dəˌmɪnɪkən rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
a country in the Caribbean occupying the eastern two-thirds of the island of Hispaniola, sharing the island with Haiti
