logical relation

noun
relation between propositions in logic
1.7 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl riː.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples
