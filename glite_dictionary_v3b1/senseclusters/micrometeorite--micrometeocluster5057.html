micrometeorite

noun
very small meteorite reaching Earth
6.1 7.5
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈmiː.ti.ɔːr.aɪt/ /; / /maɪ.krə.ˈmiː.tə.r.aɪt/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈmiː.tɪ.ə.raɪt/ /; / /maɪ.krə.ˈmiː.ti.ə.raɪt/ (BrE)
a very small meteorite that enters the Earth's atmosphere and reaches the ground without being intensely heated

Examples

A micrometeorite lands
A micrometeorite can land on Earth without burning up due to its tiny size.
Museum exhibits sometimes include micrometeorites collected from Antarctic ice.
Scientists found several micrometeorites on the roof of the urban building after a heavy rain.
