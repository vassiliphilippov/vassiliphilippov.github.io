moldability

noun
ease of shaping
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊldəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌməʊldəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of a material that allows it to be easily shaped or formed

Examples

Moldability of clay
The moldability of clay makes it popular for pottery and sculpture.
Engineers test the moldability of new materials before using them in manufacturing.
High moldability is essential for plastics used in injection molding processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plasticity - the ability of a material to be shaped or molded
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not easily shaped
