inner tube

noun
inflatable tube inside a tire
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnər tuːb / (AmE), / ˈɪnə tjuːb / (BrE)
an inflatable rubber tube placed inside a pneumatic tire to hold air

Examples

A inner tube repair
After repairing my bike, I replaced the old inner tube before inflating the tire.
Mountain bikers often carry a spare inner tube on long rides in case of a flat.
The mechanic found a puncture in the car’s inner tube causing the slow leak.
