zoot suit

noun
flashy men's suit from the 1930s–40s
9.5 8.0
Pronunciation: /zuːt suːt/ /; / /zuːt sjuːt/ (AmE), /zuːt sjuːt/ /; / /zuːt suːt/ (BrE)
