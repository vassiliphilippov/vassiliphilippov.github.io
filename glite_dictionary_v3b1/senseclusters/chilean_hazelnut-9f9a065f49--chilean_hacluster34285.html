Chilean hazelnut

noun
Chilean shrub with edible hazelnut-like seed
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈliən ˈheɪzəlˌnʌt / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən ˈheɪzəlnʌt / (BrE)
A Chilean shrub (Gevuina avellana) bearing coral-red fruit with an edible seed that resembles a hazelnut.

Examples

The Chilean hazelnut fruit
Botanists study the Chilean hazelnut for its potential as a food crop in temperate regions.
She added chopped Chilean hazelnuts to the salad for a tasty crunch.
The Chilean hazelnut is prized in South America for its rich, flavorful seeds.
