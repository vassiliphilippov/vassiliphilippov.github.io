suffragan

noun
assistant or subordinate bishop
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌfrəɡən / (AmE), / ˈsʌfrəɡən / (BrE)
a bishop appointed to assist and act as a subordinate to a diocesan bishop in certain Christian churches

Examples

A suffragan bishop
A new suffragan was consecrated to support the expanding church in the region.
As a suffragan, she worked closely with the diocesan bishop on pastoral matters.
The suffragan was responsible for overseeing several parishes within the diocese.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: archbishop - a bishop of higher rank or office
  • degree higher: bishop - a senior member of the Christian clergy, in charge of a diocese
