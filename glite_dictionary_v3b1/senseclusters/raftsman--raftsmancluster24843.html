raftsman

noun
a man who travels on a raft
Pronunciation: / ˈræftsmən / (AmE), / ˈrɑːftsmən / (BrE)
a man who travels on or steers a raft, especially as part of his work

Examples

A raftsman steers a raft
In the 19th century, a raftsman would often be responsible for transporting timber to distant markets.
The crew included a skilled raftsman who knew how to navigate treacherous waters.
The seasoned raftsman steered his raft steadily down the wide, swirling river.

  • synonym: rafter - a person who travels by raft or constructs rafters
