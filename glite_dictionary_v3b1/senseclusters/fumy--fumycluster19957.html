fumy

adjective
sullenly resentful
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːmi / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmi / (BrE)
showing or feeling anger or irritation in a sullen or resentful way

Examples

Fumy mood
She became fumy after being left out of the group discussion.
His fumy attitude made it difficult for others to approach him.
After the argument, Mark sat in the corner looking fumy and silent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
  • synonym: sulky - bad-tempered or resentful
