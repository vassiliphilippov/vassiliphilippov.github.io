widescreen

noun
wide-aspect display or TV
Pronunciation: / c8wa6ad ccskrin / (AmE), / c8wa6ad ccskrid0n / (BrE)
a television, monitor, or other display that has a wide aspect ratio (wider than the traditional almost-square format).

Examples

A widescreen television
She upgraded to a new widescreen to enjoy movies on a cinema-sized display.
The store had several widescreens on display, each with different smart features and resolutions.
He preferred the ultrawide widescreen monitor for editing videos and multitasking across apps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monitor - a screen used with a computer for displaying images and text; often used for computer displays.
  • synonym: television - an electronic device for receiving and displaying broadcast or recorded moving images; often used to mean a TV set.
