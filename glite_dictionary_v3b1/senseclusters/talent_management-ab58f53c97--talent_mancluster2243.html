talent management

noun
strategies for managing employee talent
Pronunciation: / ˈtæl.ənt ˈmæn.ədʒ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈtælənt ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the systematic process of attracting, developing, and retaining skilled employees within an organization

Examples

Effective talent management strategy
Effective talent management can give companies a competitive edge in their industry.
Many organizations invest in talent management programs to develop their best employees.
Modern talent management involves using technology to identify and train future leaders.
