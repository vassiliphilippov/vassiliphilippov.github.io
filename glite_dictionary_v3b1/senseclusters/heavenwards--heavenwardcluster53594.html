heavenwards

adverb
towards heaven or upwards
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənˌwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənˌwədz / (BrE)
Towards heaven; in or toward the sky or upward direction, either literally or figuratively.

Examples

Drift heavenwards slowly
As the choir sang, incense drifted heavenwards in the old cathedral.
The birds soared heavenwards, disappearing into the afternoon sky.
With a silent prayer, she gazed heavenwards as the sun set behind the hills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downwards - toward a lower place or position
  • synonym: upwards - toward a higher place or position
