greenbottle fly

noun
a blowfly with a metallic green body
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌbɑtəl flaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnbɒtl flaɪ / (BrE)
a common blowfly with a metallic green body, found in many parts of the world

Examples

The greenbottle fly insect
A metallic sheen helped the scientist identify the insect as a greenbottle fly.
After leaving the window open, a greenbottle fly buzzed noisily around the kitchen.
The greenbottle fly is often attracted to decaying meat and garbage.

Related Terms
