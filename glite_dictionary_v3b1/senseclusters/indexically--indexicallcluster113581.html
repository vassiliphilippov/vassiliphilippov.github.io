indexically

adverb
by means of an index
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that arranges, locates, or refers to items by means of an index or reference system

Examples

Indexically arranged terms
The glossary was organized indexically to make it easier for users to find terms.
In the database, records are stored indexically for faster searching and retrieval.
The topics in the report are listed indexically rather than by date or importance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alphabetically - in order according to the letters of the alphabet
  • antonym: randomly - in no specific order or pattern
