indexically

adverb
by means of an index
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that arranges, locates, or refers to items by means of an index or reference system

Examples

The topics in the report are listed indexically rather than by date or importance.
