beefiness

noun
muscular strength
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːfinəs / (AmE), / ˈbiːfinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being muscular, strong, or thick in build

Examples

Engine beefiness
The beefiness of the new car's engine made it popular among racing fans.
His beefiness was obvious after months of intense weight training at the gym.
The sandwich's beefiness satisfied even the hungriest customers at the diner.
