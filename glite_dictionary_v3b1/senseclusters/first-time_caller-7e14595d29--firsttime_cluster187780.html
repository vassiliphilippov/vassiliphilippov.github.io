first-time caller

noun
person calling for the first time
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst taɪm ˈkɔlər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst taɪm ˈkɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who is calling a radio or television show for the first time

Examples

A first-time caller on air
As a first-time caller, he was nervous to speak on air but quickly relaxed.
That was our third first-time caller tonight, which is always exciting for the show.
The host welcomed the first-time caller and asked where she was from.
