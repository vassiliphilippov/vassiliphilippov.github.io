younger

adjective
less old
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈjʌŋɡə / (BrE)
having less age than another person or thing, especially in comparison between two or more

Examples

Younger sister
My younger sister started high school this year and is very excited.
He is the younger of the two brothers, but he is taller.
Many people look up to their younger colleagues for fresh ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junior - younger or lower in rank or age
  • antonym: older - having more age than another person or thing
