Situated in or relating to the inland part of a country or region, away from the coast or borders.

Examples

Interior region
After living in a port city for years, she moved to an interior town surrounded by mountains.
Many of the interior provinces experience hotter summers and colder winters than coastal areas.
The interior tribes have traditions very different from those on the coast.

Synonyms

    2.7 3.65/m

  • upcountry
    6.8 0.06/m

  • midland
    4.8 0.00/m
    midland refers to the central middle part of a country, while interior refers more broadly to inland areas away from the coast.
