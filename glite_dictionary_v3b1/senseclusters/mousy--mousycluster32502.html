mousy

adjective
infested with mice
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsi / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsi / (BrE)
(of a place) infested with mice.

Examples

Mousy old house
After months of neglect, the abandoned cabin became mousy and uninhabitable.
Despite her efforts, the cellar remained mousy throughout the winter.
The old farmhouse was so mousy that traps had to be set in every room.
