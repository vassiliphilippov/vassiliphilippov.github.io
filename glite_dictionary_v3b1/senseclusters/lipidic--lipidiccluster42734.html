lipidic

adjective
containing lipids
Pronunciation: / lɪˈpɪdɪk / (AmE), / lɪˈpɪdɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing lipids, which are fats or fat-like substances found in living organisms

Examples

Lipidic cell membrane
The scientist studied the lipidic composition of the cell membrane.
Certain vitamins are stored in lipidic tissues within the body.
The lipidic layer helps protect the skin from dehydration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatty - containing or composed of fat
