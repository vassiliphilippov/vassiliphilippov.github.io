conga

verb
to dance the conga
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡə / (BrE)
to dance the conga, a Latin American dance in which people form a line and follow each other around the room while performing simple steps

Examples

To conga around
At the wedding, everyone congaed happily around the dance floor.
She was congaing with her friends at the party last night.
We conga in a line every New Year's Eve to celebrate the holiday.
