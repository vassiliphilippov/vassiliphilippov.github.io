nit

noun
egg or young of a parasitic insect
6.0
Pronunciation: / nɪt / (AmE), / nɪt / (BrE)
the egg or young form of a parasitic insect, especially a louse, often attached to hair or clothing

Examples

A nit in hair
She found a single nit attached to the back of her collar.
Teachers often check students for signs of lice and nits during outbreaks.
The doctor used a special comb to remove every nit from the child's hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: louse - a parasitic insect that lives on mammals and feeds on blood
