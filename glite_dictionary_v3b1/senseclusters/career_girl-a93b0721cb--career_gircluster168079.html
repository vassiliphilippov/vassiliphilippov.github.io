career girl

noun
woman focused on her career
4.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr ɡɜrl / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman whose main interest and ambition is in pursuing a career, especially as opposed to a traditional domestic role
