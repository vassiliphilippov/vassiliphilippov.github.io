appraisal

noun
expert estimation or assessment
8.8 3.3
Pronunciation: / əˈpreɪzəl / (AmE), / əˈpreɪzəl / (BrE)
an assessment or written report estimating the value, quality, or condition of something

Examples

A property appraisal

The annual performance appraisal helps employees understand their strengths and areas for improvement.
The bank required an independent appraisal of the property's value before approving the loan.
A recent appraisal revealed the painting was worth far more than expected.
She handed the bank an appraisal to secure the loan against her jewelry.
The insurance company required an appraisal of the house before issuing the policy.
