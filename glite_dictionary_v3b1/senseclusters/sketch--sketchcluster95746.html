sketch

verb
make a rough drawing
Pronunciation: / skɛtʃ / (AmE), / skɛtʃ / (BrE)
to make a quick, rough drawing of something, often as a preliminary study

Examples

To sketch a face
During the lecture, she quickly sketched the diagram on her notepad.
He spent the afternoon sketching flowers in the park.
The artist decided to sketch the city skyline before painting it.
