silo

noun
cylindrical tower for storing silage or grain
9.2 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪ.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪ.ləʊ/ (BrE)
A tall, cylindrical structure, typically made of concrete or metal, used for storing silage or grain on a farm.

Examples

A silo of grain
After the harvest, the workers inspected the silo to ensure it was properly sealed.
Modern farms often have more than one silo to store different types of feed.
The farmer filled the silo with freshly harvested corn for the winter.
