eyeleted

adjective
with reinforced holes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪəlɛtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪəlɛtɪd / (BrE)
having one or more small holes reinforced with a metal or sewn ring, often for decoration or fastening

Examples

Eyeleted curtains
The curtains were eyeleted so they could easily slide along the rod.

The designer used eyeleted fabric to add texture to the summer dress.
