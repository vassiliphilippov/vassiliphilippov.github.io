as a rule

adverb
usually; in general
Pronunciation: / æz ə ruːl / (AmE), / æz ə ruːl / (BrE)
usually; in general; typically or as is normally the case.

Examples

As a rule usually
As a rule, I take a walk after dinner to relax.
As a rule, children learn languages faster than adults.
As a rule, we don't accept applications after the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generally - in most cases; usually
  • antonym: rarely - not often; infrequently
