greasy spoon

noun
cheap restaurant serving fried food
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡri.si ˌspun /; / ˈɡrisi ˈspun / (AmE), / ˈɡriː.si ˌspuːn /; / ˈɡriːzi ˈspuːn / (BrE)
a small, inexpensive restaurant or cafe that serves simple, often fried or grilled food
