avaunt

verb
to drive away
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈvɔnt / (AmE), / əˈvɑːnt / (BrE)
to drive away, banish, or command to depart, especially used as an exclamation to order someone or something to go away

Examples

To avaunt a spirit
The wizard raised his staff and shouted, 'avaunt, foul creature!'
In old tales, heroes would avaunt evil spirits with a single word.
The knight commanded, 'avaunt from this place, lest you face my sword!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: banish - to send someone away from a place as a punishment
  • antonym: summon - to call someone to appear or be present
