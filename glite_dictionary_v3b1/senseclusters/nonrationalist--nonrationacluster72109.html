nonrationalist

noun
person not guided by reason
9.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈræ.ʃə.nəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈræ.ʃə.nəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who does not base their beliefs or theories solely on reason or rational thought

Examples

A nonrationalist philosopher
As a nonrationalist, she valued intuition and emotion over strict logic in her philosophy.
The debate featured a nonrationalist who challenged the idea that reason alone leads to truth.
Some religious thinkers are considered nonrationalists because they emphasize faith over reason.
