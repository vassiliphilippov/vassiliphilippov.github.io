unconvicted

noun
people not found guilty
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɪktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɪktɪd / (BrE)
people who have not been found guilty of a crime, especially as a group.

Examples

Rights of the unconvicted
The rights of the unconvicted must be respected during any legal process.
Advocates argued that the unconvicted should not be treated as criminals.
Special facilities were provided for the unconvicted awaiting trial.

  • synonym: innocent - not found guilty of a crime
