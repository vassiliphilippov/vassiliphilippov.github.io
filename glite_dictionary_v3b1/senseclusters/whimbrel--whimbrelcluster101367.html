whimbrel

noun
wading bird species
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪm.brəl/ (AmE), /ˈwɪm.brəl/ (BrE)
a medium-sized wading bird with a long, down-curved bill, found in coastal areas

Examples

A whimbrel bird
We watched a whimbrel searching for food along the muddy shoreline.
The distinctive call of the whimbrel could be heard across the salt marsh.
During migration, flocks of whimbrels stop to rest on coastal beaches.

During migration, flocks of whimbrels can be seen resting on coastal mudflats.
