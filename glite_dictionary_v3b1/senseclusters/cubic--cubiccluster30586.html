cubic

adjective
involving the cube (third power)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɪk / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk / (BrE)
(of an equation or expression) involving the cube (third power) and no higher power of a quantity or variable.

Examples

Cubic equation
A cubic polynomial has the highest degree of three.
Finding the roots of cubic equations can be more complex than solving linear or quadratic ones.
The mathematician explained how to solve a cubic equation.
