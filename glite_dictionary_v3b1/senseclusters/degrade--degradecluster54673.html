degrade

verb
to treat with disrespect or reduce in dignity
2.5 4.1
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈɡreɪd/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈɡreɪd/ (BrE)
to treat or describe (someone or something) in a way that causes a loss of dignity or respect; to lower in character or quality

Examples

Words degrade people
Nobody should degrade another person because of their background.
She felt degraded by the unfair remarks made in the meeting.
The manager was criticized for degrading his staff in front of customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dignify - to treat with respect or add dignity
  • synonym: humiliate - to make someone feel ashamed or foolish by injuring their dignity
