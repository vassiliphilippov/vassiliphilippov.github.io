myringa

noun
membrane of the ear; eardrum
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /mɪ.ˈrɪŋ.ɡə/ /; / /maɪ.ˈrɪŋ.ɡə/ (AmE), /mɪ.ˈrɪŋ.ɡə/ /; / /maɪ.ˈrɪŋ.ɡə/ (BrE)
