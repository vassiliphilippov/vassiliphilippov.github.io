The act or ceremony of placing a dead body in a grave, usually with rites or rituals.

Examples

A burial ceremony

Traditional burial practices differ widely across cultures and religions.

Synonyms

  • interment
    8.4 0.36/m
    interment is a formal ceremony of placing a body in a grave or tomb, while burial is the common everyday term.
  • inhumation
    9.9 0.02/m
    inhumation is the technical or legal word for placing a body in the earth, while burial is the everyday term.
  • entombment
    8.9 0.19/m
    entombment is placing a body in a tomb or vault above ground, while burial often means putting a body in the ground.
  • sepulture
    9.9 0.00/m
    sepulture is an old or literary word for burying a body with rites, while burial is the modern common term.
