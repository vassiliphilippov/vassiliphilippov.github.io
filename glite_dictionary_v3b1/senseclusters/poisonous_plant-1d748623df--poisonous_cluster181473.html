poisonous plant

noun
a plant that is harmful or deadly
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzənəs plænt / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that is harmful or fatal if touched or ingested in sufficient quantity

Examples

A poisonous plant
Children should be taught to recognize and avoid any poisonous plant growing in the wild.
Some gardeners use gloves when handling a poisonous plant to avoid skin irritation.
The berries of the nightshade are from a poisonous plant and should not be eaten.

Related Terms

  • antonym: edible plant - a plant safe to eat, not harmful
  • synonym: toxic plant - a plant containing toxins, harmful if eaten or touched
