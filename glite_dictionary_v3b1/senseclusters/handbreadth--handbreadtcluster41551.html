handbreadth

noun
unit of length equal to a hand's width
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbrɛdθ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbrɛdθ / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to the width of a human hand, used especially historically or informally

Examples

A handbreadth of fabric
He measured the fabric by pacing out each handbreadth.
The table was only a handbreadth wider than the doorway.
There was just a handbreadth of space left between the wall and the cabinet.
