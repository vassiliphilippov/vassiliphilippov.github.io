mina

noun
tropical Asian starling
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ nə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ nə / (BrE)
A type of tropical Asian starling, known for its loud calls and often kept as a cage bird.

Examples

Pet mina bird
The mina is prized for its ability to mimic human speech.
The pet shop had several mina birds for sale, each chattering noisily.
We saw a flock of minas feeding in the rice fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mynah - an alternative spelling and common variant for this bird
