silver thatch

noun
a fan palm of southern Florida and Cuba
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər ˌθætʃ /; / ˈsɪlvər θætʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌθætʃ /; / ˈsɪlvə θatʃ / (BrE)
a small palm tree native to southern Florida, Cuba, and the West Indies, known for its silvery leaves
