bowstring hemp

noun
houseplant with sword-shaped leaves; fiber source
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌstrɪŋ hɛmp / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌstrɪŋ hɛmp / (BrE)
a tropical African plant (Sansevieria trifasciata), cultivated as a houseplant for its long, mottled, sword-shaped leaves or grown for its strong fiber

Examples

Bowstring hemp houseplant
Fibers from bowstring hemp have traditionally been used to make cords and ropes.

The bowstring hemp thrives indoors with little water or sunlight.
