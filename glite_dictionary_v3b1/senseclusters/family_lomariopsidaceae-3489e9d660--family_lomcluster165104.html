family Lomariopsidaceae

noun
family of mostly climbing ferns
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪli loʊˌmɛəriˌɑːpsɪˈdeɪsiː / (AmE), / fəˈmɪli ləʊˌmarɪˌɒpsɪˈdiːsiː / (BrE)
A small family of usually climbing (scandent) ferns, found mainly in tropical regions.
