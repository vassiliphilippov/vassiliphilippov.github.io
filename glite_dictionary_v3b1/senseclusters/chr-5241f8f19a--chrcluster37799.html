Chr

noun
abbreviation for Christ
9.8
Pronunciation: / siː eɪtʃ ɑːr / (AmE), / siː eɪtʃ ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Christ, used especially to refer to Jesus Christ in writing

Examples

Chr the Savior

In some historical documents, Chr is used instead of writing out Christ fully.
The artist inscribed Chr on the painting to honor Jesus Christ.
