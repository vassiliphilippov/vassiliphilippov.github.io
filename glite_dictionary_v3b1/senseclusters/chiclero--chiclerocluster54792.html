chiclero

noun
chicle harvester
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈkleɪroʊ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈklɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a person who collects chicle, the natural gum used in making chewing gum, especially in Central America

Examples

The chiclero collects gum
The chiclero skillfully climbed the tall sapodilla tree to extract the valuable gum.
Many chicleros work in the forests of Central America, supplying chicle for chewing gum.
Becoming a chiclero requires knowledge of the jungle and the right harvesting techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gum collector - a person who gathers natural gum from trees
  • synonym: harvester - a person who gathers crops or natural products
