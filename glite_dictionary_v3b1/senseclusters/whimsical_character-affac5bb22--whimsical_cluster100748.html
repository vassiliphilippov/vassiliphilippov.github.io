whimsical character

noun
amusingly odd or fanciful figure
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmzɪkəl ˈkærɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪmzɪkəl ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
a fictional figure or persona that is amusingly odd, fanciful, or imaginative

Examples

A whimsical character in stories
Her children's stories often feature a whimsical character with magical hats.
In the play, the most memorable role was a whimsical character who danced on tables.
The novel introduces a whimsical character who communicates only in riddles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantastical character - fictional figure with imaginative, magical, or unreal traits
  • antonym: serious character - fictional figure with a sober, earnest, or solemn nature
