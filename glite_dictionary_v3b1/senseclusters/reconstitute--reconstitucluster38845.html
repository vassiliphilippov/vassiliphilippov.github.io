reconstitute

verb
to form again
5.4
Pronunciation: /riː.kən.ˈstɪ.tuːt/ /; / /riː.kən.ˈstɪ.tjuːt/ (AmE), /riː.kɒn.ˈstɪ.tjuːt/ /; / /riː.kɒn.ˈstɪ.tuːt/ (BrE)
to restore something, especially food or powder, to its original state by adding water or another liquid

Examples

To reconstitute soup powder
