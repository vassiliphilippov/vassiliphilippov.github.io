megaphone

verb
to announce loudly
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈme.gə.foʊn/ (AmE), /ˈme.gə.fəʊn/ (BrE)
to broadcast or announce something loudly, as if using a megaphone

Examples

To megaphone news
The coach megaphoned instructions to the players across the noisy field.
During the parade, the organizer megaphoned safety reminders to the crowd.
She megaphoned her excitement so everyone at the event could hear her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amplify - to make something louder or more powerful
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very quietly
