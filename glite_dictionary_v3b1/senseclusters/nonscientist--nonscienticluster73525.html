nonscientist

adjective
not a scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
not trained in or working in the sciences; relating to people who are not scientists

Examples

Nonscientist readers
The article was written in a way that even a nonscientist could easily understand.
During the conference, several nonscientist members of the public asked insightful questions.
The museum offers nonscientist tours that explain complex topics in simple terms.

  • antonym: scientific - relating to or characteristic of science or scientists
