bandana

noun
colorful large handkerchief
Pronunciation: / bænˈdænə / (AmE), / bænˈdænə / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored handkerchief, typically with printed patterns, often worn around the neck or head as a scarf.

Examples

A bandana scarf
He tied a bandana over his hair to keep the sweat away while working.
She wore a red bandana around her neck as a fashion statement.
The pirate's costume included an eye patch and a spotted bandana.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handkerchief - square of cloth for wiping the nose or face
