sentencer

noun
judge giving sentence
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.tən.sər/ /; / /ˈsen.təns.ər/ (AmE), /ˈsen.tən.sə/ /; / /ˈsen.təns.ə/ (BrE)
a person, especially a judge, who decides and announces a legal sentence in court

Examples

The sentencer in court
The sentencer carefully reviewed the facts before announcing the punishment.
After hearing both sides, the sentencer delivered a fair and just decision.
In some countries, the sentencer has the authority to choose between different types of penalties.
