commercial traveler

noun
traveling salesperson or representative
5.7 3.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɜːr.ʃəl ˈtræv.əl.ər/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɜː.ʃəl ˈtræv.ə.lə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to travel to different places to sell goods or solicit orders for their company

Examples

Commercial traveler meets clients
As a commercial traveler, she spent most of her time on the road meeting clients.
The commercial traveler visited several small towns every week to take orders from local shops.
The life of a commercial traveler can be demanding, but it offers great opportunities for building customer relationships.
As a commercial traveller, she spent most weeks on the road visiting clients across the country.
In the past, being a commercial traveller involved long journeys by train or coach.
The commercial traveller arrived every few months with samples of the latest fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sales representative - a person who sells products or services for a company, often by visiting clients
  • synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is to sell things
  • synonym: traveling salesman - a salesperson who travels to visit customers
  • synonym: travelling salesman - a person whose job is to travel to different places to sell products
