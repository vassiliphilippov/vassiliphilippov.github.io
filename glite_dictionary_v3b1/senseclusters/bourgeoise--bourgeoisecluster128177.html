bourgeoise

noun
middle-class woman
8.1
Pronunciation: / bʊrˈʒwɑːz / (AmE), / bʊəˈʒwɑːz / (BrE)
a woman who belongs to the middle class, especially in terms of social or cultural status

Examples

The bourgeoise woman
The bourgeoise enjoyed spending her afternoons at the local café with friends.
As a proud bourgeoise, she valued education and cultural activities.
Many novels of the era depict the daily life of a bourgeoise in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aristocrat - a member of the highest social class
  • synonym: bourgeois - a person belonging to the middle class
