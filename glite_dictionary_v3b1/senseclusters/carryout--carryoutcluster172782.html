carryout

adjective
for taking away
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkæriˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈkæriˌaʊt / (BrE)
intended to be taken away from the place of purchase rather than eaten or used there

Examples

Carryout menu

Many places provide discounts on carryout orders during the week.
