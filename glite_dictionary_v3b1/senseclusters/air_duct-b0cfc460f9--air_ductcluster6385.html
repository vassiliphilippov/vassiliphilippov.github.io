air duct

noun
a passage or pipe for air ventilation
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌdʌkt / (BrE)
a passage or pipe for moving air to provide ventilation, especially in buildings, vehicles, or industrial settings

Examples

The air duct system
Miners rely on the air duct system to provide fresh air underground.
The air duct needs to be cleaned regularly to ensure good indoor air quality.
The contractor installed a new air duct to improve airflow in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duct - a tube or passage for fluids, air, or cables
  • synonym: ventilation shaft - vertical or inclined passage for air in mines or buildings
