air duct

noun
a passage or pipe for air ventilation

Examples

The air duct system

The air duct needs to be cleaned regularly to ensure good indoor air quality.
The contractor installed a new air duct to improve airflow in the office.

Synonyms

  • air passage
    3.5 0.23/m
    an air passage is a wide channel for moving fresh or stale air, while an air duct is a pipe for building or vehicle ventilation.
  • ventilation shaft
    4.5 0.13/m
    a ventilation shaft is a large passage that moves fresh air through a building, while an air duct is usually a smaller pipe for rooms or vehicles.
  • air shaft
    4.7 0.16/m
    an air shaft is a mostly vertical opening that moves air in a building or mine, while an air duct is a pipe used in vehicles or buildings.
  • airway
    4.5 0.04/m
    an airway is a passage that moves air, often in mines, while an air duct is a pipe for general ventilation in buildings or vehicles.
  • vent
    2.7 7.48/m
    a vent is a small opening that lets air or gas escape, while an air duct is a pipe that moves air for building or vehicle ventilation.
