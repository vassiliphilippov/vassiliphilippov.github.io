braziletto

noun
red dye from wood
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbræzɪˈlɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbræzɪˈlɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a red or crimson dye or pigment made from the wood of the braziletto tree, or the reddish color produced by it
