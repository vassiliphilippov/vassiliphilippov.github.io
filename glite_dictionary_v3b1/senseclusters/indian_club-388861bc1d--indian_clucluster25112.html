Indian club

noun
bottle-shaped exercise club
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən klʌb / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪən klʌb / (BrE)
a bottle-shaped wooden club used as an exercise implement in fitness training and physical therapy

Examples

Swinging Indian club
He swung the Indian club in wide circles to warm up his shoulders.

Victorian-era gyms often featured exercises with the Indian club.
