busy-bodied

adjective
officious; meddlesome
8.1 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪziˌbɑdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɪziˌbɒdɪd / (BrE)
Intrusively meddlesome or officious; inclined to interfere in others' affairs in a way that is annoying or inappropriate.

Examples

A busy-bodied person
His busy-bodied questions made everyone at the party uncomfortable.
No one appreciated the busy-bodied approach she took to organizing other people's tasks.
She found her new neighbor insufferably busy-bodied, always prying into private matters.
