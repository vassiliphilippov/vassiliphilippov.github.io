Jinghpaw language

noun
language of the Jinghpaw people in Myanmar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋpɔː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋpɔː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Jinghpaw people, belonging to the Kachinic branch of the Tibeto-Burman language family, spoken mainly in northern Myanmar
