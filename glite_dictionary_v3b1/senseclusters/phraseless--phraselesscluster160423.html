phraseless

adjective
without clear phrasing
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzləs / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzləs / (BrE)
not divided into or showing clear musical phrases; lacking distinct melodic phrasing.

Examples

Phraseless music
The modern composition was intentionally phraseless, creating a continuous stream of sound.
Some listeners found the phraseless melody difficult to follow without clear musical breaks.
Her improvisation was so phraseless that it felt like a single, endless line of music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unstructured - lacking a clear or organized structure
