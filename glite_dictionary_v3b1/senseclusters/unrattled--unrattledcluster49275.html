unrattled

adjective
not shaken or disturbed
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrætəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈrætəld / (BrE)
not shaken, disturbed, or made to rattle; remaining steady or unaffected by shock or impact

Examples

Unrattled glassware
The glassware on the shelf remained unrattled even after the heavy truck passed by.
Despite the turbulence, the pilot's hands were unrattled as he controlled the plane.
The old clock stayed unrattled during the minor earthquake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shaken - disturbed or made to move back and forth quickly
  • synonym: steady - firm, stable, or not easily moved
