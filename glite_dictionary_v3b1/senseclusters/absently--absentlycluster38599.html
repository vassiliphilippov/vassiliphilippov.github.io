absently

adverb
in an absentminded way
Pronunciation: / ˈæbsəntli / (AmE), / ˈabsəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of focus or attention; in an absentminded or distracted manner.

Examples

To absently nod
He nodded absently as he read the report.
She stirred her coffee absently while thinking about her meeting.
The child gazed absently out the window during the long car ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absentmindedly - in a distracted or inattentive manner
