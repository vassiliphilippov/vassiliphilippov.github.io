codeword

noun
slogan or label
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdwɝːd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdwɜːd / (BrE)
a word or phrase used to represent a particular idea, movement, or policy, often as a slogan or label

Examples

Political codeword
The campaign adopted 'change' as its main codeword to inspire supporters.
In the meeting, 'efficiency' became a codeword for cutting costs.
Activists used a special codeword to signal the start of the protest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzzword - a fashionable word or phrase used to impress or persuade
  • synonym: catchphrase - a well-known phrase associated with a particular group or idea
  • synonym: slogan - a short and memorable phrase used to promote an idea or cause
