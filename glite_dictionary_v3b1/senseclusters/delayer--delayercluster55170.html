delayer

noun
a person who delays
5.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈleɪər / (AmE), / dɪˈleɪə / (BrE)
a person who delays or puts things off until later

Examples

A chronic delayer
As a chronic delayer, she always waits until the last minute to book her flights.
He is a notorious delayer when it comes to turning in assignments.
The project's progress was hindered by one persistent delayer on the team.
