second fiddle

noun
subordinate role; secondary position
6.9 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənd ˈfɪd.əl /; / ˌsɛkənd ˈfɪdəl /; / ˈsɛkənd ˈfɪdəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ənd ˈfɪd.əl /; / ˌsɛkənd ˈfɪdəl /; / ˌsekənd ˈfɪd.əl / (BrE)
a person or role that is less important or supports someone else; being in a subordinate position
