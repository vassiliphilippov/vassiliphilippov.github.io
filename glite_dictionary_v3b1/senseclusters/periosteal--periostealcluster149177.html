periosteal

adjective
around bone membrane
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛriˈɑstiəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛriˈɒstiəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated around the membrane that covers a bone

Examples

Periosteal membrane
The doctor noted swelling in the periosteal region of the patient's leg.
A periosteal reaction can indicate an underlying bone injury or infection.
During surgery, the periosteal layer was carefully separated from the bone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bone - the hard, dense connective tissue forming the skeleton
  • synonym: periosteum - the membrane covering the outer surface of bones
