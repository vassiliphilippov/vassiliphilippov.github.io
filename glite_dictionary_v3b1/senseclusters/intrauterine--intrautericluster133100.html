intrauterine

adjective
within the uterus
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈju.t̬ər.aɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈjuː.tər.aɪn / (BrE)
Situated or occurring within the uterus (womb).

Examples

An intrauterine infection
An intrauterine infection can pose serious risks to both the mother and the baby.
She chose an intrauterine device (IUD) as her preferred method of birth control.
The doctor monitored the intrauterine development of the fetus during each prenatal visit.
