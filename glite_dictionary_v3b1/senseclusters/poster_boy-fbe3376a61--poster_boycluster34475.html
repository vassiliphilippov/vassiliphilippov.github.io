poster boy

noun
male symbol or prime example
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstər bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a man or boy who is seen as a perfect or prominent example of something, often used as a symbol for a cause, movement, or quality

Examples

The poster boy for change
He became the poster boy for environmental activism after his campaign went viral.
In the media, he was unfairly portrayed as the poster boy for corporate greed.
That athlete is often called the poster boy for clean living and hard work.
After his successful recovery, he became the poster boy for the hospital's new treatment program.
He never wanted to be the poster boy for environmental activism, but his work made him one.
The young entrepreneur has turned into the poster boy for innovation in the tech industry.
