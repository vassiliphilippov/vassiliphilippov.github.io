worm lizard

noun
limbless burrowing reptile resembling a worm
Pronunciation: / wɜrm ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / wɜːm ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
A limbless, burrowing reptile of the family Amphisbaenidae, resembling a worm and native to warm or tropical regions.
