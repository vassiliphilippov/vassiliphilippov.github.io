rhinologist

noun
nose specialist
Pronunciation: / raɪˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / raɪˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases of the nose

Examples

Rhinologist appointment
After months of sinus problems, she finally saw a rhinologist for a thorough evaluation.
The rhinologist recommended surgery to correct the patient's chronic nasal obstruction.
If you have persistent nosebleeds, a rhinologist may be able to help.

Related Terms

  • antonym: general practitioner - a doctor who treats common medical conditions
  • synonym: otolaryngologist - a doctor specializing in diseases of the ear, nose, and throat
