incline bench press

noun
weightlifting exercise on an inclined bench
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈklaɪn bɛntʃ prɛs / (AmE), / ɪnˈklaɪn bɛntʃ prɛs / (BrE)
an exercise in which a barbell or dumbbells are pressed upwards while lying on a bench set at an incline, targeting the upper chest muscles

Examples

The incline bench press exercise
He added the incline bench press to his workout to build upper chest strength.
Many trainers recommend performing an incline bench press at a 30-degree angle.
She lifted heavier weights during the incline bench press this week compared to last.
