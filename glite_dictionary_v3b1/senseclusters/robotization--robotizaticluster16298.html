robotization

noun
use of robots
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊbətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrəʊbətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of replacing human labor with robots or automated machines

Examples

Robotization of factories
The robotization of the car industry has led to faster and more efficient production lines.
Many fear that increased robotization will result in fewer jobs for factory workers.
As robotization spreads, companies are investing in new technologies to stay competitive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automation - the use of machines to perform tasks without human intervention
  • antonym: manual labor - work done by hand or with physical effort
  • synonym: mechanization - the process of using machines to do work previously done by people or animals
