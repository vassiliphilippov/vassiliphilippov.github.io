overvoting

noun
excess votes error
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈvoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈvəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
a situation in an election when more votes are recorded than the number of ballots or eligible voters, usually indicating an error

Examples

Election overvoting
The election was investigated after officials noticed overvoting in several precincts.
A technical glitch led to overvoting that affected the final results.
Strict procedures are in place to prevent overvoting during ballot counting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballot stuffing - illegally adding extra ballots to increase vote totals
  • antonym: undervoting - a situation where fewer votes are cast than possible in an election
