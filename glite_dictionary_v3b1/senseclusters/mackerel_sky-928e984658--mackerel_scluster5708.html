mackerel sky

noun
sky with rows of rippled clouds
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmækɚəl skaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmæk(ə)rəl skaɪ / (BrE)
a sky covered with rows of cirrocumulus or small altocumulus clouds, often resembling the markings on a mackerel's back

Examples

A mackerel sky
A mackerel sky decorated the horizon, its rippled clouds glowing pink at sunrise.
Photographers love to capture a mackerel sky because of its striking textures and patterns.
Sailors took the presence of a mackerel sky as a sign that rain was on the way.
