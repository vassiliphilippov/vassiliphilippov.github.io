toxicologist

noun
expert in poisons and their effects
Pronunciation: / tɑksɪˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / tɒksɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the effects, detection, and treatment of poisons

Examples

A toxicologist studies poisons
After years of research, the toxicologist concluded that the chemical was highly dangerous to humans.
As a toxicologist, she is often called to testify in court cases involving poisoning.
The police consulted a toxicologist to determine the cause of the mysterious illness.
