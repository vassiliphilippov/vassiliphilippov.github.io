mark up

verb
increase the price of something
3.5 0.4/m
Pronunciation: / m…ërk  åp / (AmE), / m…ëÀêk  åp / (BrE)
to increase the price of something, especially so it can be sold at a profit

Examples

To mark up prices
Some retailers mark up products by as much as 50%.
The store decided to mark up all electronics before the holiday shopping season.
They often mark up imported goods to cover shipping and customs fees.
