Kalahari Desert

proper noun
desert region in southwestern Africa
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɑː.hɑː.ri ˈdɛz.ɚt / (AmE), / kəˌlɑː.hɑː.ri ˈdɛz.ət / (BrE)
a large desert region in southwestern Africa, mainly located in Botswana, with parts in Namibia and South Africa

Examples

The Kalahari Desert
Scientists have studied climate change by examining ancient lake beds in the Kalahari Desert.
The Kalahari Desert is home to a diverse range of wildlife, despite its arid conditions.
Tourists are often fascinated by the beauty and solitude of the Kalahari Desert.
