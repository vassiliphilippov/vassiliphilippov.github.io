communalistic

adjective
relating to communalism
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of communalism or communal living, emphasizing shared ownership or group cooperation

Examples

Communalistic lifestyle
The community's communalistic values encouraged everyone to share resources and responsibilities.
A communalistic lifestyle can help reduce individual expenses and promote cooperation.
Their communalistic approach to farming meant that all members worked together and shared the harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - done by people acting as a group
  • antonym: individualistic - favoring independence and self-reliance over group cooperation
