Bernese mountain dog

noun
Swiss breed of large working dog
Pronunciation: / bərˈniːz ˈmaʊntən dɔɡ / (AmE), / bɛːˈniːz ˈmaʊntən dɒɡ / (BrE)
a large, powerful breed of Swiss working dog with a long black coat, tan or rust markings, and white on the chest, feet, and face.

Examples

A Bernese mountain dog breed
A Bernese mountain dog requires regular grooming due to its long, thick coat.
Her family owns two friendly Bernese mountain dogs that love hiking in the mountains.
The Bernese mountain dog is known for its gentle temperament and loyalty.
