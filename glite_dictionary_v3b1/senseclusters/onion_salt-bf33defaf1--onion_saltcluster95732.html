onion salt

noun
seasoned salt with onion flavor
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌnjən sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən sɒlt / (BrE)
a condiment made from ground dried onion mixed with salt, used to flavor food

Examples

Onion salt seasoning

The recipe calls for a teaspoon of onion salt.
