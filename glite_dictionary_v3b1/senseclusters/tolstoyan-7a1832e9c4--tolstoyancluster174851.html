Tolstoyan

adjective
of Tolstoy's ideas
Pronunciation: / tɔlˈstɔɪən / (AmE), / tɒlˈstɔɪən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, lifestyle, or followers of the Russian writer Leo Tolstoy, especially his beliefs in simplicity and nonviolence

Examples

Tolstoyan lifestyle
She adopted a Tolstoyan approach to life, focusing on simplicity and moral integrity.
The community was founded on Tolstoyan principles of nonviolence and self-sufficiency.
His Tolstoyan beliefs influenced his decision to live without luxury or excess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gandhian - relating to the ideas or followers of Mahatma Gandhi, especially nonviolence
