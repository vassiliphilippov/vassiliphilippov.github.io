germination

noun
beginning or origin of development
5.4 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd í…úrm…ôÀàne…™ É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåd í…úÀêm…ôÀàne…™ É…ôn / (BrE)
the process of something beginning or developing; the origin or earliest stage of a development, often used figuratively

Examples

Germination of an idea
Her interest in science found its germination in childhood experiments in the kitchen.
The germination of new ideas often comes from unexpected conversations.
The germination of the movement can be traced back to a small group of activists.
