antichristian

noun
opponent of Christianity
9.1
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈkrɪs.tʃən/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈkrɪs.tʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Some writings were considered antichristian and banned by the authorities.
