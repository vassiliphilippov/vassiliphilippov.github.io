antichristian

noun
opponent of Christianity
Pronunciation: / ˌæntikrɪs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntikrɪs.tʃən / (BrE)
a person or group that is opposed to or hostile toward Christianity or its teachings

Examples

An antichristian group
The group was labeled antichristian for protesting outside the church.
Some writings were considered antichristian and banned by the authorities.
He faced criticism for his openly antichristian views in a religious community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Christian - a person who follows the religion based on the teachings of Jesus Christ
  • synonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of God or gods
