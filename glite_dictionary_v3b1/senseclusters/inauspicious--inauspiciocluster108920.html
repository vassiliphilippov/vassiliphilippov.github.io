inauspicious

adjective
not suggesting success or good fortune
4.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ɔː.ˈspɪ.ʃəs/ /; / /ɪn.ɑː.ˈspɪ.ʃəs/ (AmE), /ɪn.ɔː.ˈspɪ.ʃəs/ (BrE)
not giving a good chance of success; suggesting bad luck or an unpromising outcome

Examples

Some considered beginning the project on a Friday to be an inauspicious choice.
The wedding was delayed by an inauspicious series of setbacks.
