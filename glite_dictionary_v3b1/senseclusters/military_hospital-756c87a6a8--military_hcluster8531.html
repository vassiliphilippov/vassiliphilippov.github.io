military hospital

noun
hospital for soldiers and military personnel
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈhɑːspɪtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈhɒspɪtl̩ / (BrE)
A hospital operated by the armed forces, providing medical care to soldiers and other military personnel.

Examples

A military hospital ward
Her father works as a surgeon in a military hospital overseas.
The military hospital provides specialized care for veterans.
The injured soldiers were rushed to the nearest military hospital after the battle.
