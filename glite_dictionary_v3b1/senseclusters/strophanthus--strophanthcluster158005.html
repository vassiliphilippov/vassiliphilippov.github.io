strophanthus

noun
genus of tropical shrubs or small trees with showy flowers
8.2
Pronunciation: / stroʊˈfænθəs / (AmE), / strəʊˈfænθəs / (BrE)
a genus of tropical African and Asian shrubs or small trees with whorled leaves and showy, clustered flowers, some species having poisonous seeds

Examples

Strophanthus poisonous seeds
Botanists study strophanthus for its medicinal and toxic properties.
Some species of strophanthus have striking, star-shaped flowers that attract gardeners.
The seeds of the strophanthus plant were used traditionally as arrow poison.
