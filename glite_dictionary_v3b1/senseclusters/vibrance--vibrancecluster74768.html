vibrance

noun
color intensity adjustment
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrəns / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrəns / (BrE)
an image-editing adjustment that increases the intensity of muted colors without oversaturating already vivid ones

Examples

Photo vibrance adjustment
Adjusting the vibrance in the photo made the dull colors stand out without making the reds too strong.
She prefers to use vibrance instead of saturation to enhance her landscape photos naturally.
Increasing the vibrance brought out the subtle blues in the sky while keeping the bright colors balanced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saturation - the intensity or purity of a color in an image
