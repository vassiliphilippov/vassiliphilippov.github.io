new-car smell

noun
distinctive scent of a new car
Pronunciation: / ˌnuː ˈkɑr smɛl / (AmE), / ˌnjuː ˈkɑː smɛl / (BrE)
a distinctive scent associated with newly manufactured or purchased automobiles, often perceived as pleasant

Examples

A new-car smell in the car
After a few months, the new-car smell began to fade from the interior.
I love that new-car smell when I get into a brand new vehicle.
Many air fresheners try to imitate the new-car smell for older vehicles.
