agglomerate

noun
a collection of objects piled together

A mass or collection of things gathered together into a clump or heap, especially objects laid on top of each other.

Examples

A agglomerate of bones
A paleontologist carefully examined the fossil agglomerate containing bone fragments from several species.
The artist used a random agglomerate of household items to create an abstract sculpture.
The volcanic agglomerate was visible as a rough, rocky mound at the base of the cliff.
