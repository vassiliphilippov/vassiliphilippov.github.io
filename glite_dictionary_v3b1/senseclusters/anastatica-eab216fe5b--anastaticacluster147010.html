Anastatica

noun
genus of plant known as the rose of Jericho
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.əˈstæt.ɪ.kə /; / ˌænəˈstætɪkə / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈstæt.ɪ.kə /; / ˌanəˈstatɪkə / (BrE)
a genus of plant in the mustard family (Brassicaceae), known for the rose of Jericho (resurrection plant), which can survive extreme dryness and revive when watered
