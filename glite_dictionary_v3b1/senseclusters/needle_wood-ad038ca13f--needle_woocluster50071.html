needle wood

noun
Australian shrub with pointed leaves and white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəl wʊd / (AmE), / ˈniːdəl wʊd / (BrE)
a large bushy shrub of central and eastern Australia, with pungent pointed leaves and creamy white flowers

Examples

Tough needle wood branches
Farmers use the tough branches of needle wood as natural fencing.
The needle wood thrives in the arid regions of central Australia.
The creamy white flowers of the needle wood bloom in late spring.
