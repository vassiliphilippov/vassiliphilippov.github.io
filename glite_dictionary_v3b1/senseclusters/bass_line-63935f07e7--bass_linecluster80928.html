bass line

noun
underlying musical rhythm or melody (in bass)
Pronunciation: / beɪs laɪn / (AmE), / beɪs laɪn / (BrE)
the underlying rhythm or melody in a piece of music played by the bass instruments, typically forming the harmonic and rhythmic foundation
