stir

verb
move slightly or faintly
1.0 6/m
Pronunciation: / st…ùÀê / (AmE), / st…úÀê / (BrE)
to begin to move slightly or to cause something to move after being still

Examples

Leaves stir gently
Not a leaf stirred in the hot, still afternoon.
She did not stir when he called her name.
The animal stirred slightly in its sleep, but did not wake up.
After hours of silence, the crowd started to stir restlessly.
He did not stir until the smell of breakfast reached his room.
The camp was quiet until the sun rose and people began to stir.
