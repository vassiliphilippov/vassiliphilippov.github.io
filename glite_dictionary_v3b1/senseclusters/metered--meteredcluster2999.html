metered

adjective
with regular rhythm
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtərd / (AmE), / ˈmiːtəd / (BrE)
arranged with a regular rhythmic structure, especially in poetry, having a defined poetic meter

Examples

Metered poetry lines
The poem's metered verses created a pleasing musical effect for the audience.
She prefers writing in metered forms rather than free verse.
The teacher asked the students to identify the metered lines in the passage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhythmic - having a regular repeated pattern of sounds or movements
  • antonym: unmetered - not arranged with a regular rhythmic structure
