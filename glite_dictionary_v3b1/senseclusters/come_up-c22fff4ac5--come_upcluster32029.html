come up

verb
produce or suggest (idea, plan, etc.)
Pronunciation: / kʌm ʌp / (AmE), / kʌm ʌp / (BrE)
to produce or bring forth (an idea, suggestion, plan, etc.), especially something desirable or useful

Examples

Come up with an idea
Can you come up with a better way to organize the files?
During the meeting, several employees came up with suggestions to improve productivity.
She always manages to come up with creative solutions to difficult problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suggest - to put an idea or plan forward for consideration
