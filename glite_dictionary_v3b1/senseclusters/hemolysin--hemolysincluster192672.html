hemolysin

noun
substance that destroys red blood cells
Pronunciation: / hɪˈmɑːləsɪn /; / hiːˈmɑlɪsɪn / (AmE), / hɪˈməʊlɪsɪn /; / hiːˈmɒlɪsɪn / (BrE)
a substance that causes the destruction of red blood cells and the release of hemoglobin
