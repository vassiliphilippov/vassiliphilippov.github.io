bounty hunter

noun
person who finds bugs for reward
4.0 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / c8ba8anti cch8cnt59r / (AmE), / c8ba8anti cch8cnt59 / (BrE)
A person who seeks out and reports software vulnerabilities in order to earn monetary rewards offered by a company's or platform's bug bounty program.

Examples

A bounty hunter finds bugs
A skilled bounty hunter reported a critical remote-code-execution bug to the company's bounty program.
After months of searching, the freelance bounty hunter received a large payout for the exploit.
Many startups advertise rewards to attract an ethical bounty hunter who finds security flaws quickly.

Synonyms

  • bug-hunter
    4.0 0.00/m
    bug-hunter searches broadly for software bugs and vulnerabilities, often in public bounty programs, while bounty hunter targets security flaws to earn company rewards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug hunter - a person who searches for software bugs or vulnerabilities, especially to report them for rewards
  • synonym: security researcher - someone who investigates software or systems to find and analyse security vulnerabilities
