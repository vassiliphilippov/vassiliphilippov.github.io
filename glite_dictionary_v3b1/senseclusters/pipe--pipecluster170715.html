pipe

noun
smoking instrument; tube with bowl
Pronunciation: / paɪp / (AmE), / paɪp / (BrE)
A tube with a small bowl at one end, used for smoking tobacco.

Examples

Tobacco pipe
After dinner, he enjoyed smoking his favorite pipe on the porch.
He packed his pipe with tobacco and lit it by the fireplace.
The old fisherman always carried a pipe in his jacket pocket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cigar - A roll of tobacco wrapped in a tobacco leaf for smoking.
  • synonym: cigarette - A thin cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking.
