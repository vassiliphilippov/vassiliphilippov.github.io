cross-cultural understanding

noun
awareness and appreciation of different cultures
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˈkʌltʃɚəl ˌʌndɚˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / krɒs ˈkʌltʃərəl ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
the knowledge, awareness, and appreciation of differences and similarities among cultures, enabling effective and respectful interaction

Examples

Cross-cultural understanding skills
Cross-cultural understanding is essential for global companies working with diverse teams across different countries.
His travels fostered a deep cross-cultural understanding that influenced his approach to international business.
Programs promoting cross-cultural understanding can help reduce prejudice and build stronger communities.
