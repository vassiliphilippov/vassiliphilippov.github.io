baldish

adjective
partly bald
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔldɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːldɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat bald or partially lacking hair, but not completely bald

Examples

A baldish man
He was already looking a bit baldish in his thirties.
The man had a baldish patch on the top of his head.
Her father is only baldish, with some hair still around the sides.
