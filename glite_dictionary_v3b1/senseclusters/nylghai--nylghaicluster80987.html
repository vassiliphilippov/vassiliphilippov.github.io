nylghai

noun
large Indian antelope species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪlˌgaɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɪlɡʌɪ / (BrE)
A large antelope native to India; the male is blue-grey with white markings and horns, while the female is brownish and lacks horns.

Examples

A nylghai antelope
A herd of nylghai moved quietly through the tall, dry grass at sunrise.
In the wildlife sanctuary, we saw a female nylghai with her young calf.
The nylghai is often found grazing in the grasslands of northern India.
