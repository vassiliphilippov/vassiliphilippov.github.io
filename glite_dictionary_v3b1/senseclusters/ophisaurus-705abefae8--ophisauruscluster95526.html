Ophisaurus

noun
a genus of legless lizards (glass lizards)
Pronunciation: / oʊˈfɪsɔːrəs /; / ˌoʊfɪˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / ˌɒfɪˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of legless lizards, commonly called glass lizards, that look like snakes but are actually lizards
