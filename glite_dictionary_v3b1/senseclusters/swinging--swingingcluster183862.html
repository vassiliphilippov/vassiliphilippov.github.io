swinging

adjective
having a lively rhythm or movement
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
having a lively and rhythmic movement or beat, as in music or walking.

Examples

Swinging music
She walked with a swinging stride down the boulevard.
The band played a swinging tune that got everyone dancing.
The dancer's swinging movements added energy to the performance.
