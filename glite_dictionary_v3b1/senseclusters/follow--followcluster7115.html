Synonyms

  • travel along
    1.6 0.5/m
    travel along means moving along a set route or path, while follow can also mean going after someone or copying their direction.
  • trail
    2.5 2/m
    trail means secretly following someone, often to catch them, while follow can simply mean walking or moving behind without that intent.
  • track
    2.3 50/m
    track means watching or recording someone's movement to get information, while follow often means physically going behind them.
