Andrei Sakharov

proper noun
Soviet physicist and human rights activist
Pronunciation: / ˈændreɪ ˈsɑːkərɒf /; / ˈændreɪ ˈsɑːkəˌrɔv / (AmE), / ˈændreɪ ˈsækhərɒf /; / ˈændreɪ ˈsakhɑːrɒf / (BrE)
(1921–1989) Soviet physicist, Nobel Peace Prize winner, and dissident known for developing the hydrogen bomb and for advocating nuclear disarmament and human rights
