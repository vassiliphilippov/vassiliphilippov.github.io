light

adjective
(phonetics) pronounced with little or no stress
7.4
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
(of a vowel or syllable) pronounced with little or no stress; not strongly emphasized.

Examples

Light syllable
A light syllable is often a target for reduction in rapid speech.
In some languages, a light syllable may never appear at the end of a word.
The first vowel in the word 'banana' is typically a light syllable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heavy - (of a syllable) strongly stressed or prominent
  • synonym: unstressed - not having strong emphasis in pronunciation
