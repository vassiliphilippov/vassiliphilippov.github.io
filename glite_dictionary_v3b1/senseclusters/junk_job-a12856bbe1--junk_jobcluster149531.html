junk job

noun
low-status or unfulfilling job
7.0
Pronunciation: / dʒʌŋk dʒɑb / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk dʒɒb / (BrE)
a low-status or undesirable job, especially one that is unfulfilling or perceived as having little value

Examples

A junk job at a call center
After graduating, she took a junk job at a call center to pay the bills.
He was tired of working junk jobs with no chance for advancement.
Some people are forced to accept any junk job they can find in a tough economy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dead-end job - a job with few prospects for advancement
  • antonym: dream job - a job that is highly desirable or ideal
