add together

verb
combine numbers by addition
Pronunciation: / æd təˈɡɛðər /; / æd təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / æd təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to calculate the total of two or more numbers or amounts by combining them

Examples

To add together numbers
If you add together 15 and 20, you get 35.
When we added together everyone's scores, it was clear who had won.
You need to add together all the expenses to find out the total cost.
Please add together all the numbers in the second column.
The teacher asked the students to add together their test scores and find the average.
When you add together the costs of rent and utilities, the total is quite high.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subtract - to take a number or amount away from another
  • synonym: sum - the total amount resulting from the addition of numbers
