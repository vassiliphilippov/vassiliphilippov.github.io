somatization

noun
unexplained physical symptoms
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊmətaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsoʊmətəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsəʊmətaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsəʊmətʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the experience or process of having physical symptoms caused by psychological or emotional problems, without a medical explanation

Examples

In some cultures, somatization is a common way to express emotional pain.
Therapists often help patients recognize the link between somatization and unresolved psychological issues.
