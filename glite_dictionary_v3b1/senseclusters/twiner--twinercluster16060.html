twiner

noun
person or thing that entwines or twists
Pronunciation: / ˈtwaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈtwaɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that entwines or forms something by twisting or interlacing threads, fibers, or strands

Examples

Skilled twiner
In traditional basketry, the twiner interlaces reeds to form sturdy containers.
