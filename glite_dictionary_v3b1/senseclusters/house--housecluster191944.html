house

noun
astrological division of the zodiac
Pronunciation: / haʊs / (AmE), / haʊs / (BrE)
In astrology, each of the twelve equal divisions of the zodiac, believed to represent different aspects of life and personality.

Examples

The first house in astrology
He spent years studying how each house affects a person’s destiny.
In her birth chart, the Sun is located in the tenth house, suggesting a focus on career.
Many astrologers believe that the seventh house governs relationships and partnerships.
