motel

noun
a hotel for motorists with parking
Pronunciation: / moʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / məʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A roadside hotel designed to provide accommodation for motorists, typically with parking areas in front of the rooms.

Examples

A motel with parking
The motel had convenient parking right outside each room.
They stopped at a small motel just off the highway for the night.
We booked a room at the motel because it was more affordable than a hotel.
