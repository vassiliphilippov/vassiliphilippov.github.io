magnanimously

adverb
in a generous or forgiving manner
5.9
Pronunciation: / mægˈnænɪməsli / (AmE), / maɡˈnænɪməsli / (BrE)
In a generous and forgiving way, especially towards a rival or someone less powerful.

Examples

To magnanimously forgive
Even after the heated argument, she magnanimously offered her help to her opponent.
He magnanimously accepted the apology, showing no sign of resentment.
The losing team magnanimously congratulated their rivals on a game well played.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generously - in a way that shows a willingness to give more than expected
