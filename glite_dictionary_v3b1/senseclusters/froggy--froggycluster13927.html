froggy

adjective
like a frog
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːɡi / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡi / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a frog, often in a cute or playful way

Examples

Froggy slippers
The child giggled at the froggy slippers with big green eyes.
He made a froggy face to make the baby laugh.
Her backpack had a froggy design with little webbed feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frog-like - having qualities similar to a frog
