overcoat

verb
to cover excessively
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkoʊt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkəʊt / (BrE)
to cover something with an additional or excessive layer, especially of paint or another substance

Examples

To overcoat with paint
If you overcoat the cake with icing, it may become too sweet.
The artist accidentally overcoated the canvas, hiding the original colors.
Try not to overcoat the furniture with varnish, or it will look uneven.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coat - to cover a surface with a layer of something
  • antonym: strip - to remove a layer from a surface
