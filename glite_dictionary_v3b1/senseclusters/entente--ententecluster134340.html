entente

noun
informal alliance between countries
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈtɑnt / (AmE), / ɒnˈtɒnt / (BrE)
an informal agreement or alliance between countries or political powers for cooperation or mutual understanding

Examples

The Entente agreement
During the early 20th century, the Triple Entente played a crucial role in global politics.
Negotiators sought an entente rather than a formal treaty to avoid provoking other countries.
The two nations formed an entente to promote regional stability.
A secret entente between the nations shaped the course of the negotiations.
The entente allowed the governments to coordinate their foreign policies without a formal treaty.
The two countries formed an entente to promote peace and stability in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alliance - a formal agreement between countries or groups to work together
