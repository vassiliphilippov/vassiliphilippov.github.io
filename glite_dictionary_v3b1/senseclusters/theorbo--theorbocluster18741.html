theorbo

noun
large early string instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / θiˈɔrboʊ / (AmE), / θɪˈɔːbəʊ / (BrE)
a large, long-necked stringed instrument from the Renaissance and Baroque periods, similar to a lute and used for accompaniment
