pivotally

adverb
crucially; centrally
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətəli / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətəli / (BrE)
in a way that is crucially important or central to the outcome of something

Examples

Pivotally important role
She was pivotally involved in the project's success from the very beginning.
The decision made by the board pivotally changed the direction of the company.
His advice pivotally influenced the outcome of the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crucially - in a way that is extremely important
  • synonym: essentially - in a way that is absolutely necessary
  • antonym: marginally - in a way that is only slightly important
