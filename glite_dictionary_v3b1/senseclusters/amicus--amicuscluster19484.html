amicus

noun
legal adviser to court

a person or group not directly involved in a court case who offers information or support to assist the court's decision (legal term)

Examples

Amicus brief

Synonyms

  • amicus curiae
    9.9 0.14/m
    amicus curiae is the formal Latin name lawyers file with the court, while amicus is a shorter, general label.
  • friend of the court
    7.9 0.13/m
    friend of the court is the plain English phrase journalists and speakers use, while amicus is the formal legal term.
