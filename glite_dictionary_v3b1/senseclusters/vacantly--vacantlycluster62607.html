vacantly

adverb
in an empty or expressionless manner
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪkəntli / (AmE), / ˈveɪkəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no interest, thought, or emotion; with an empty or expressionless manner.

Examples

Look vacantly ahead
He gazed vacantly at the white wall, lost in thought.
She answered the question vacantly, not really listening to the conversation.
The child looked vacantly out the window during the long drive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blankly - in a way that shows no expression or emotion
