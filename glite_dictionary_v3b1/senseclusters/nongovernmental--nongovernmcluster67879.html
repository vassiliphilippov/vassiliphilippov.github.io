nongovernmental

adjective
not government-run
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɡʌvənˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
not controlled, owned, or run by a government

Examples

Nongovernmental organizations
Many nongovernmental organizations work to improve education in developing countries.
The project was funded by a nongovernmental source rather than the state.
A nongovernmental committee reviewed the environmental impact of the new policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: governmental - controlled or run by a government
  • synonym: private - not owned or controlled by the government
