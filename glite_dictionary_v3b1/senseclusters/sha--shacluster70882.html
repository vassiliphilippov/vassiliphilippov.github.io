sha

noun
cryptographic hash function
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʃɑː/ (AmE), /ʃɑː/ (BrE)
any of a family of cryptographic hash functions, such as SHA-1 or SHA-256, used to generate a fixed-size data digest

Examples

SHA algorithm

Many modern applications rely on sha algorithms for data integrity and security.
When verifying file downloads, a sha checksum is often provided to ensure authenticity.
