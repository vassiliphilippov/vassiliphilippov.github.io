dark

adjective
lacking enlightenment or knowledge
3.9
Pronunciation: / dɑrk / (AmE), / dɑːk / (BrE)
connected with evil, ignorance, secrecy, or being hidden from knowledge; morally bad, lacking enlightenment, or concealed

Examples

There were dark rumors about his involvement in the crime.
