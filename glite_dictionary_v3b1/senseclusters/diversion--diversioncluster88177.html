diversion

noun
entertaining or distracting activity
3.9 2.85/m
Pronunciation: / da…™Ààv…úÀêr í…ôn / (AmE), / da…™Ààv…úÀê É…ôn / (BrE)
an activity that entertains, amuses, or distracts people from their usual concerns

Examples

A fun diversion
Chess was his favorite diversion after a long day at work.
For many people, reading serves as a pleasant diversion from daily stress.
The community center offers many diversions for local youth.

Synonyms

  • entertainment
    2.1 47/m

  • amusement
    1.8 5.53/m

  • recreation
    2.7 9.17/m
