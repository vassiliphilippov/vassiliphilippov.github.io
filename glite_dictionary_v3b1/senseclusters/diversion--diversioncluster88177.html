diversion

noun
entertaining or distracting activity
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɜːrʒən / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
an activity that entertains, amuses, or distracts people from their usual concerns

Examples

A fun diversion
Chess was his favorite diversion after a long day at work.
For many people, reading serves as a pleasant diversion from daily stress.
The community center offers many diversions for local youth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pastime - an activity that someone does regularly for enjoyment rather than work
  • synonym: recreation - an activity done for enjoyment when one is not working
  • antonym: work - activity involving mental or physical effort done to achieve a purpose or result
