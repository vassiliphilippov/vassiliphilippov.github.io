fascinator

noun
decorative headpiece
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a small, decorative headpiece or hat, often worn by women at formal events or social occasions

Examples

A stylish fascinator
She wore a sparkling fascinator to the royal garden party.
Many guests at the wedding chose a colorful fascinator instead of a traditional hat.
The boutique displayed an elegant fascinator decorated with feathers and beads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hat - a covering for the head, often with a brim
  • antonym: helmet - a hard or protective head covering
  • synonym: veil - a piece of fine material worn by women to protect or conceal the face
