champ

verb
chew or bite the bit impatiently (of a horse)
8.8 6.3
Pronunciation: /tʃæmp/ /; / /ʃæmp/ (AmE), /tʃæmp/ /; / /ʃæmp/ (BrE)
to chew or bite something noisily and forcefully (literally or figuratively, as with impatience or eagerness)

Examples

To champ the bit
