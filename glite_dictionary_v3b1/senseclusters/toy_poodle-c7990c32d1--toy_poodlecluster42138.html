toy poodle

noun
very small breed of poodle
Pronunciation: / tɔɪ ˈpuːdəl / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈpuːdl / (BrE)
A breed of very small poodles, typically kept as a companion dog and prized for its intelligence and hypoallergenic coat.

Examples

A toy poodle dog
Many families choose the toy poodle as a pet for their children.
She adopted a toy poodle because of its friendly temperament and minimal shedding.
The toy poodle excelled in agility training despite its small size.
