bicker

verb
argue or quarrel over petty things
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɪkə / (BrE)
to argue or quarrel about trivial matters, especially continuously and in a petty way

Examples

To bicker over small things
My siblings always bicker about who gets to sit in the front seat of the car.
The two colleagues would bicker endlessly over minor details in the project.
Their constant bickering made it difficult for the team to focus on the task.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agree - to have the same opinion or be in harmony
  • synonym: quarrel - to have an angry dispute or disagreement
