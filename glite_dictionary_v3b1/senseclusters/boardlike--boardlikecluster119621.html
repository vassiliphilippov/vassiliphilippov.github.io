boardlike

adjective
stiff or expressionless
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
showing little or no emotion or flexibility; stiff or expressionless in manner or appearance

Examples

Boardlike face
Her boardlike posture made her seem unapproachable during the meeting.
He answered the questions in a boardlike tone, without any hint of emotion.
Despite the joke, his face remained boardlike, showing no reaction.

  • synonym: expressionless - showing no emotion or facial expression
  • synonym: rigid - not flexible; stiff
