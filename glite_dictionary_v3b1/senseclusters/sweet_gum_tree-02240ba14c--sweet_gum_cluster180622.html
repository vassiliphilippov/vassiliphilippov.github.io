sweet gum tree

noun
tree with prickly fruit and fragrant sap
9.5
Pronunciation: / swit ɡʌm tri / (AmE), / swiːt ɡʌm triː / (BrE)
A North American tree of the genus Liquidambar, known for its prickly round fruit clusters and fragrant sap.

Examples

The sweet gum tree fruit
Every spring, the sweet gum tree fills the air with its pleasant, resinous scent.
The yard was dotted with sweet gum trees, their spiky seed balls covering the ground in autumn.
They planted a sweet gum tree for its shade and vibrant red leaves in the fall.
