sweet gum tree

noun
tree with prickly fruit and fragrant sap
Pronunciation: / swit ɡʌm tri / (AmE), / swiːt ɡʌm triː / (BrE)
A North American tree of the genus Liquidambar, known for its prickly round fruit clusters and fragrant sap.
