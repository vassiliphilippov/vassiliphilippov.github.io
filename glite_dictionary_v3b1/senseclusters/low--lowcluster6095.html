low

adjective
having small height or elevation
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
at or having a small elevation or height, especially relative to the ground or a reference point

Examples

Low clouds
A blanket of low clouds covered the entire city.
Early in the morning, the sun hangs low on the horizon.
The apartment has low ceilings, which make the rooms feel smaller.

Related Terms

  • antonym: high - being at or having a great elevation or height
