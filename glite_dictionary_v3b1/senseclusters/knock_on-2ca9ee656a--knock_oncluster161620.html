knock on

noun
rugby: foul of knocking ball forward
5.3 6.6
Pronunciation: / nɑk ɑn / (AmE), / nɒk ɒn / (BrE)
In rugby, an instance of illegally knocking the ball forward with the hand or arm while attempting to catch it.

Examples

A knock on in rugby
