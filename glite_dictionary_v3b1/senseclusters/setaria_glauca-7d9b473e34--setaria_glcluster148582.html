Setaria glauca

noun
a common weedy grass (yellow foxtail)
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛˈtɛəri.ə ˈɡlɔːkə / (AmE), / sɪˈtɛːrɪə ˈɡlaʊkə / (BrE)
A species of common, bristly grass, also called yellow foxtail, found extensively in temperate regions and often considered a weed.
