Eucalyptus amygdalina

noun
Tasmanian eucalyptus tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˈkʌlɪptəs æˌmɪɡdəˈlaɪnə / (AmE), / juːˈkælɪptəs æˌmɪɡdəˈlʌɪnə / (BrE)
A species of eucalyptus tree, known as the black peppermint or red gum, native to Tasmania, Australia.

Examples

Eucalyptus amygdalina tree
Botanists conducted a survey of Eucalyptus amygdalina distribution across southeastern Australia.
The forests of Tasmania are home to the towering Eucalyptus amygdalina.
The leaves of Eucalyptus amygdalina are often used in traditional remedies.
