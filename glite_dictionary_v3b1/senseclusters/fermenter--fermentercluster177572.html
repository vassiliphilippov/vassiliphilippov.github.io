fermenter

noun
vessel for fermentation
5.5
Pronunciation: / fərˈmɛntər / (AmE), / fəˈmɛntə / (BrE)
a container or device used for carrying out fermentation, especially in industrial or laboratory processes

Examples

A beer fermenter
The brewery uses a large fermenter to produce its signature beer.
After adding the yeast, the mixture was placed in the fermenter for several days.
Technicians cleaned the fermenter thoroughly before starting the next batch.
