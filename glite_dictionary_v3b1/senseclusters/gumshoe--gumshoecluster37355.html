gumshoe

verb
to investigate discreetly
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌʃuː / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌʃuː / (BrE)
to investigate or search for information in a manner similar to a detective, often discreetly.

Examples

To gumshoe for clues
She spent the afternoon gumshoeing around the neighborhood for clues.
He has been gumshoeing online to find out more about the mysterious company.
After gumshoeing for hours, they finally discovered the missing document.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detect - to discover or identify the presence of something
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
  • synonym: investigate - to carry out a systematic or formal inquiry
