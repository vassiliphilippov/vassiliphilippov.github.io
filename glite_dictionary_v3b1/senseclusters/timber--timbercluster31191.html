timber

noun
sound quality in music or voice
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbə / (BrE)
the quality of a musical sound or voice that distinguishes different types of sound production, such as voices or musical instruments

Examples

Timber of a voice
Each singer in the choir has a slightly different timber, creating a rich blend of voices.
Sound engineers pay close attention to the timber when mixing music tracks.
The unique timber of the violin made the solo especially beautiful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tone - the general quality or character of a musical or vocal sound
