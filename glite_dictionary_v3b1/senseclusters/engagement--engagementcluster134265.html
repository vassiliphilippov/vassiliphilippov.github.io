engagement

noun
an arrangement for a meeting or appointment
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
an arrangement to meet someone or to perform at a particular time, such as an appointment or scheduled event

Examples

A dinner engagement
He checked his calendar to make sure he wasn't double-booked for the engagement.
I'm sorry, I have a previous engagement and won't be able to attend the dinner.
She had an important engagement at the law office that afternoon.
She couldn't attend the party because she had a prior engagement at the theater.
The band has an engagement at the jazz club next Friday night.
Their tour included several one-night engagements across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appointment - an arrangement to meet or visit someone at a particular time
  • synonym: booking - an advance arrangement to employ a performer or group
  • synonym: gig - a job or engagement for a musician or performer
