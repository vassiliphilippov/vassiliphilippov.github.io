deionize

verb
remove ions from (a substance)
7.3
Pronunciation: / diˈaɪ.əˌnaɪz /; / diˈaɪəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / diˈaɪ.ə.naɪz /; / diˈaɪənaɪz / (BrE)
to remove ions from a substance, especially water, to make it purer

Examples

To deionize water
Laboratory technicians deionize the water before using it in experiments.
The company installed a system to deionize all incoming process water.
You should deionize the equipment after each cycle to prevent mineral buildup.
The laboratory uses special equipment to deionize water before conducting sensitive experiments.
You should always deionize the solution to avoid unwanted chemical reactions.
After they deionized the samples, the results were much more accurate.

  • synonym: demineralize - remove mineral salts from (a substance)
  • synonym: purify - to make something clean or free from impurities
