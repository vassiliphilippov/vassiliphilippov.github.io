Synonyms

  • stick out
    3.2 4.64/m
    stick out is plain and common for something extending beyond a surface, while overhang implies hanging over an edge or lip.
  • protrude
    5.8 4.35/m
    protrude sounds more formal and describes a part pushing beyond its surface, while overhang often suggests hanging over an edge.
  • jut
    4.8 2.61/m
    jut suggests a sharp or dramatic projection from a line, while overhang often means something hangs out over an edge.
  • project
    4.3 2.43/m
    project is a formal term for extending outward from a surface, while overhang often implies hanging over an edge or lip.
