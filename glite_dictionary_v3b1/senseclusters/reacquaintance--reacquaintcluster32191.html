reacquaintance

noun
renewed familiarity
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈkweɪntəns / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈkweɪntəns / (BrE)
the process or instance of becoming familiar with someone or something again after a period of separation or absence

Examples

Reacquaintance after years
Their reacquaintance after many years was a joyful occasion.
The trip allowed him a reacquaintance with his childhood hometown.
After months apart, the team's reacquaintance brought renewed energy to their project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: introduction - the act of meeting someone for the first time
  • synonym: reunion - the act of coming together again after being apart
