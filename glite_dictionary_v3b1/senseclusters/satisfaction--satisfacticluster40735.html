satisfaction

noun
payment or fulfillment of a legal obligation
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsæt.ɪsˈfæk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsæt.ɪsˈfæk.ʃən / (BrE)
(law) The payment of a debt or the fulfillment of an obligation, especially as a legal settlement.

Examples

Satisfaction of a debt
After years of negotiation, the company finally provided satisfaction for its outstanding debts.
His claim was dismissed upon satisfaction of the agreed settlement amount.
The court required full satisfaction of the contract before closing the case.

Related Terms

  • synonym: settlement - An official agreement intended to resolve a dispute or claim
