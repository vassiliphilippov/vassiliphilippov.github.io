natural talent

noun
innate ability or skill
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚl ˈtælənt / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ˈtalənt / (BrE)
an inborn or innate ability or skill, especially in a particular activity or field

Examples

Natural talent for music
He has a natural talent for leadership, making him an excellent team captain.
Her natural talent with languages allowed her to learn Spanish in just a few months.
The young pianist's natural talent impressed the judges at the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acquired skill - skill developed through practice rather than inborn
  • synonym: aptitude - natural ability to do something
  • synonym: gift - natural ability or talent
