Saluki

noun
tall, swift hunting dog breed
Pronunciation: / səˈluki / (AmE), / səˈluːki / (BrE)
a breed of tall, slender, swift hunting dog with a smooth or silky coat, originally from Egypt and southwestern Asia

Examples

A Saluki dog
She adopted a Saluki from a rescue organization specializing in sighthounds.
The Saluki is prized for its speed and graceful appearance in dog shows.
Traditionally, the Saluki was used by nomads to hunt game across desert landscapes.
