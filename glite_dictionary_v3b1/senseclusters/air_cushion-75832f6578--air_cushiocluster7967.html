air cushion

noun
layer of trapped air supporting a hovercraft
5.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /er ˈkʊʃ.ən/ (AmE), /eə ˈkʊʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a layer of trapped air that supports a hovercraft a short distance above the surface of water or ground
