Synonyms

  • collateral
    6.7 2/m
    collateral refers to something secondary or supporting, while incidental means a minor side effect or result rather than the main thing.
  • nonessential
    4.4 0.5/m
    nonessential means not necessary or central, while incidental describes a minor or secondary result that is not the main thing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned

  • antonym: primary - of first importance, main
  • synonym: resultant - occurring as a result or consequence
  • synonym: secondary - not primary, of minor importance
  • antonym: significant - important and meaningful
