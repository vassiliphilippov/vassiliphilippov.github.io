conical

adjective
having the shape of a cone
4.8 1.15/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀên…™k…ôl / (AmE), / Ààk…ín…™k…ôl / (BrE)
Having the shape of a cone; relating to or resembling a cone.

Examples

A conical hat
He wore a large, conical hat that protected his face from the sun.
Some species of trees have a conical shape to help snow slide off their branches.
The artist created a conical sculpture that towered over the nearby park benches.

Synonyms

  • conic
    7.2 0.05/m
    conic is used often in math and geometry for cone-related shapes, while conical is the general everyday adjective.
  • cone-shaped
    2.8 0.36/m
    cone-shaped is a clear, informal way to say an object looks like a cone, while conical is more formal.
  • conelike
    4.9 0.02/m
    conelike sounds rare or old-fashioned when used to describe forms, while conical is the standard modern adjective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conelike - shaped like a cone
  • antonym: cylindrical - having the form of a cylinder
