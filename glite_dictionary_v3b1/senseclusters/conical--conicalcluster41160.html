conical

adjective
having the shape of a cone
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒnɪkəl / (BrE)
Having the shape of a cone; relating to or resembling a cone.

Examples

A conical hat
He wore a large, conical hat that protected his face from the sun.
Some species of trees have a conical shape to help snow slide off their branches.
The artist created a conical sculpture that towered over the nearby park benches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conelike - shaped like a cone
  • antonym: cylindrical - having the form of a cylinder
