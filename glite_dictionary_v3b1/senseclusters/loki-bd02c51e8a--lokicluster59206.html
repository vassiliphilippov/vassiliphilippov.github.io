Loki

proper noun
Norse god of mischief and discord
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.kiː/ (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the trickster god of mischief and discord, known for causing the death of Balder and being opposed by Thor.
