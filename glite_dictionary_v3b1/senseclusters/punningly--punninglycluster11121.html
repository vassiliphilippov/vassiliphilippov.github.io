punningly

adverb
with wordplay
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpʌnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or makes puns, often using words with double meanings for humor

Examples

To speak punningly
The headline was punningly crafted to make readers smile.
She punningly called her cat 'Claws' because it always scratched the furniture.
He punningly described the math teacher as someone who could always count on success.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seriously - in a way that is not intended to be funny or playful
  • synonym: wittily - in a clever and amusing way
