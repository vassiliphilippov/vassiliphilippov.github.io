adhesive tape

noun
strip of sticky tape
1.5
Pronunciation: / ədˈhɪsɪv teɪp / (AmE), / ədˈhiːsɪv teɪp / (BrE)
A strip of material coated with adhesive on one side, used for sticking things together.

Examples

A roll of adhesive tape
Always keep some adhesive tape in your first aid kit for emergencies.
She wrapped the box with adhesive tape to keep it closed.
The nurse applied a strip of adhesive tape over the bandage.
