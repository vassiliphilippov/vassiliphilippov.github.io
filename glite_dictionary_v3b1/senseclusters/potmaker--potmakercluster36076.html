potmaker

noun
person who makes pots
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make pots, usually from clay

Examples

The potmaker makes pots
The village potmaker displayed her wares at the market.
As a skilled potmaker, he crafted beautiful vases and bowls by hand.
The potmaker taught children how to shape clay into useful objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: potter - a person who makes pottery
  • synonym: sculptor - an artist who makes sculptures
