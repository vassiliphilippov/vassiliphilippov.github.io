flex

noun
act of tensing or bending a muscle
Pronunciation: / flɛks / (AmE), / flɛks / (BrE)
an act of bending, tightening, or tensing a muscle, especially to show strength

Examples

A strong flex
During the contest, each competitor performed a signature flex on stage.
He did a quick flex for the camera after finishing his workout.
With a dramatic flex of her arm, she showed off her new muscle definition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexion - the act of bending a limb or joint
  • antonym: relax - to make or become less tense
