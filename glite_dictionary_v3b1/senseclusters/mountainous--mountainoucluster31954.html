mountainous

adjective
containing many mountains
1.7 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tən.əs /; / ˈmaʊntənəs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪ.nəs /; / ˈmaʊntɪnəs / (BrE)
(of land or a region) having many mountains or being very hilly or craggy

Examples

Mountainous region
Switzerland is known for its mountainous landscapes and scenic views.
The region is mountainous and difficult to traverse by car.
They settled in a mountainous area far from the city.
The mountainous terrain provided a breathtaking view of the valley below.
The region is very mountainous, making transportation difficult.
They enjoy hiking through mountainous landscapes during their vacations.
