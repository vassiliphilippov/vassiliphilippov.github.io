slut

noun
offensive term for sexually promiscuous woman
Pronunciation: / slʌt / (AmE), / slʌt / (BrE)
a woman who is considered to have many casual sexual partners or to behave in a sexually promiscuous way; this term is highly offensive and vulgar

Examples

Called a slut
He insulted her by calling her a slut, which was deeply hurtful and untrue.
It's important to recognize that labeling someone as a slut is both offensive and sexist.
Some activists are working to reclaim the word slut as a symbol of empowerment.
Many people criticize the media for labeling women as a slut based on their clothes or behavior.
She was unfairly called a slut by some classmates, though her personal life was nobody's business.
The word slut is often used offensively and can be deeply hurtful.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prude - person, especially a woman, regarded as excessively proper or modest in sexual matters
  • synonym: whore - offensive term for a prostitute or promiscuous woman
