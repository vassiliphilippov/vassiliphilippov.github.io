interrogatively

adverb
in a questioning or curious manner
4.8 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈrɑːɡətɪvli /; / ɪn.təˈrɑː.ɡə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈrɒɡətɪvli /; / ɪn.təˈrɒ.ɡə.tɪv.li / (BrE)
