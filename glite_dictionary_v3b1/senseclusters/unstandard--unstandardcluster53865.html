unstandard

adjective
not meeting standards
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstændərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstændəd / (BrE)
not conforming to an accepted or official standard

Examples

Unstandard parts
The engineer noticed that several unstandard components had been used in the construction.
Using unstandard procedures can lead to unexpected problems during production.
The report was rejected because it contained unstandard formatting and terminology.
