misprision

noun
concealment of a crime
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈprɪʒən / (AmE), / mɪsˈprɪʒən / (BrE)
the deliberate failure to report or the concealment of a known crime by someone who is aware of it

Examples

Misprision of a crime
