trilaterally

adverb
involving three parties
Pronunciation: / traɪˈlæt̬ɚəli / (AmE), / traɪˈlætərəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or relates to three sides, parties, or countries

Examples

To negotiate trilaterally
The trade deal was discussed trilaterally by the leaders of the three countries.
They agreed to cooperate trilaterally on environmental protection projects.
The issue was resolved trilaterally after several rounds of negotiation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bilaterally - in a way that involves two sides or parties
  • degree higher: multilaterally - in a way that involves more than two parties or countries
