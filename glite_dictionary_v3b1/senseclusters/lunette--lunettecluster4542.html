lunette

noun
oval or circular architectural opening
Pronunciation: / luˈnɛt /; / luːˈnɛt / (AmE), / luˈnɛt /; / luːˈnɛt / (BrE)
a crescent-shaped, semicircular, or oval window or architectural opening, especially above a door or in a vault
