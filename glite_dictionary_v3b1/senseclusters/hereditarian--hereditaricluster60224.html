hereditarian

adjective
focused on heredity
Pronunciation: / həˌrɛdəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / hɪˌrɛdɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
believing that heredity is the main factor in determining traits or behavior

Examples

Hereditarian theory
The hereditarian perspective argues that intelligence is mostly inherited from parents.
Some scientists criticize the hereditarian approach for ignoring environmental factors.
Debates between hereditarian and environmental explanations have shaped psychology for decades.

Related Terms

  • antonym: environmental - relating to the influence of environment rather than heredity
  • synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity
