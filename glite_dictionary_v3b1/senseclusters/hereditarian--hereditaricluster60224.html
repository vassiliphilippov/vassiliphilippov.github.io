hereditarian

adjective
focused on heredity
9.3
Pronunciation: /hə.rɪ.ˈdɪ.tər.iː.ən/ /; / /he.rɪ.ˈdɪ.tər.iː.ən/ (AmE), /hə.rɪ.ˈdɪ.tə.riː.ən/ /; / /he.rɪ.ˈdɪ.tə.riː.ən/ (BrE)
believing that heredity is the main factor in determining traits or behavior

Examples

Hereditarian theory
The hereditarian perspective argues that intelligence is mostly inherited from parents.
Some scientists criticize the hereditarian approach for ignoring environmental factors.
