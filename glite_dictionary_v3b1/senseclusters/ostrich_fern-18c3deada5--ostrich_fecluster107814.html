ostrich fern

noun
tall northern fern with ostrich-like fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstrɪtʃ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈɒstrɪtʃ fɜːn / (BrE)
a tall fern of northern temperate regions with large, graceful, arching fronds and spore-bearing structures that resemble ostrich plumes

Examples

The ostrich fern plant
Fiddleheads from the ostrich fern are sometimes harvested as a seasonal delicacy.
She planted an ostrich fern at the edge of her garden to add texture and height.
The ostrich fern thrives in moist, shaded woodlands across North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fern - any of numerous flowerless, seedless vascular plants with fronds that reproduce by spores
