Father's Day

noun
holiday honoring fathers
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑðɚz deɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðəz deɪ / (BrE)
A holiday honoring fathers, celebrated on the third Sunday in June in the United States and on varying dates in other countries.

Examples

Father's Day card
He received a handmade card from his children for Father's Day.
On Father's Day, many families gather to celebrate and show appreciation for their fathers.
Restaurants are often busy on Father's Day as people treat their dads to a special meal.
