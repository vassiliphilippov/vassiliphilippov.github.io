stintless

adjective
without restraint
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪntləs / (AmE), / ˈstɪntləs / (BrE)
without limit or restraint; not restricted or sparing in amount or degree

Examples

Stintless enthusiasm
Her stintless enthusiasm inspired everyone on the team to work harder.
The artist poured stintless creativity into every piece she made.
He gave stintless support to his friends during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundless - having no boundaries; vast or unlimited
  • antonym: restricted - limited in amount or range
  • synonym: unlimited - without any limit or end
