epigraphy

noun
study of ancient inscriptions
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪɡrəfi / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪɡrəfi / (BrE)
the study and interpretation of ancient inscriptions, especially those engraved on stone or other durable materials

Examples

Study of epigraphy
Advances in epigraphy have helped historians better understand ancient civilizations.
She decided to specialize in epigraphy, focusing on deciphering Roman stone carvings.
The museum's new exhibit highlights the importance of epigraphy in archaeological research.
