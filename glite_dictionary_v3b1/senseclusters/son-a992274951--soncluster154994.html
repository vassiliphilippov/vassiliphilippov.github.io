Son

noun
the second person of the Trinity in Christianity
Pronunciation: / sʌn / (AmE), / sʌn / (BrE)
(in Christian theology) the divine Word of God; the second person of the Trinity, incarnate as Jesus Christ.

Examples

The Son of God
Christians believe that the Son was sent into the world to offer salvation to humanity.
In the prayer, she addressed the Son as her redeemer and guide.
The doctrine of the Trinity speaks of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit as one God.

  • synonym: Jesus Christ - central figure of Christianity, believed to be the incarnate Son of God
