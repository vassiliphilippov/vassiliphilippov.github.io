plural form

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈplʊr.əl fɔːrm/ /; / /ˈplɜːr.əl fɔːrm/ /; / /ˈplʊr.əl foʊrm/ (AmE), /ˈplʊə.rəl fɔːm/ /; / /ˈpljʊə.rəl fɔːm/ /; / /ˈplɔː.rəl fɔːm/ (BrE)
(in localization and software) one of the translation variants or categories a language uses for pluralization, together with the rules that choose the correct variant for a given number.
