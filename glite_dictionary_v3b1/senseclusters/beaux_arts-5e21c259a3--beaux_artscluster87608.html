beaux arts

noun
fine arts or a style of art/architecture
3.3
Pronunciation: / boʊ ˈzɑr / (AmE), / boʊ ˈzɑː / (BrE)
(often Beaux Arts) The study and creation of visual works of art; also, a style of art, architecture, and design associated with the École des Beaux-Arts in Paris, especially characterized by classical forms, grand scale, and elaborate ornamentation.

Examples

Beaux arts style
Architects trained in the beaux arts tradition emphasized classical forms and elaborate ornamentation.
Her passion for beaux arts led her to study painting and sculpture in Paris.
The city's grand museum houses a collection that reflects the influence of the beaux arts movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fine arts - visual arts appreciated for their beauty and intellectual value
