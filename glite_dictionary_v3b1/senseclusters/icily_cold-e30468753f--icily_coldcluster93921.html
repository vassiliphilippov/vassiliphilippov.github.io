icily cold

adjective
very harshly and intensely cold
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.sɪ.li koʊld / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.sɪl.i kəʊld / (BrE)
extremely cold in an intense or harsh way, often with an added sense of severity or unfriendliness.

Examples

Icily cold stare
His stare was icily cold, sending shivers down my spine.
The mountain wind felt icily cold against her cheeks.
Water from the spring was icily cold, refreshingly so on a hot day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frigid - extremely cold in temperature; very cold
  • antonym: warm - having some heat, not cold
