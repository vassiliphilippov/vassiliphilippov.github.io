confinement

noun
state of being confined or imprisoned
6.7 4.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈfaɪn.mənt/ (AmE), /kən.ˈfaɪn.mənt/ (BrE)
the state or act of being kept in a limited space or being imprisoned; restriction of freedom

The scientist described the confinement of the experiment to a controlled environment.
