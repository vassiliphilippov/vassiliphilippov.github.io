Sikh

proper noun
follower of Sikhism
4.9
Pronunciation: / siːk / (AmE), / siːk / (BrE)
a member of a monotheistic religious group originating in the Punjab region of India, following Sikhism

Examples

Sikh community
Many Sikhs gather at the gurdwara to celebrate important religious festivals.
A Sikh is easily recognized by the turban and uncut hair, which are symbols of their faith.
The Sikh community is known for its tradition of serving free meals to all visitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Hindu - a follower of Hinduism, a major religion of India
  • antonym: Muslim - a follower of Islam
