synonymous with

adjective
closely associated in meaning or identity with
5.0
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɑːnɪməs wɪð / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪməs wɪð / (BrE)
so closely associated with something that the two are considered to have the same meaning or strongly connected identity

Examples

Synonymous with quality
For many people, the brand name has become synonymous with quality and reliability.
Innovation is synonymous with success in the tech industry.
To some, the town is synonymous with its annual music festival.
