fortuitously

adverb
by chance; luckily
5.4 7.5
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈtuːɪtəsli / (AmE), / fɔːˈtjuːɪtəsli / (BrE)
By good fortune; happening by chance in a way that is fortunate or lucky.

Examples

To fortuitously meet
Fortuitously, I ran into my old friend at the airport just as I was about to leave.
The team fortuitously discovered a shortcut that saved them hours on their journey.
We arrived at the restaurant without a reservation and fortuitously found an empty table.
