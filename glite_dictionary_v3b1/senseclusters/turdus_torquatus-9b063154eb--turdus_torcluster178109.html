Turdus torquatus

noun
European thrush with a white-neck band
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːdəs tɔrˈkwɑːtəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːdəs tɔːˈkwɑːtəs / (BrE)
A European thrush (ring ouzel) commonly found in rocky areas, with males displaying blackish plumage and a distinctive white band around the neck.
