chola

noun
Andean woman, informal
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃoʊ.lə/ /; / /ˈtʃoʊ.lɑː/ (AmE), /ˈtʃəʊ.lə/ /; / /ˈtʃəʊ.lɑː/ (BrE)
