mileage

noun
travel allowance per mile traveled
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
an amount of money paid or allowed for each mile traveled, especially as a travel expense allowance

Examples

Mileage allowance
Many organizations offer a standard mileage rate for use of personal vehicles.
She kept a log of her mileage to claim her travel expenses.
The company reimburses employees for business trips based on mileage driven.
