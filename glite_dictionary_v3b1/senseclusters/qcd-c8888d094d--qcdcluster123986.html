QCD

noun
manufacturing KPI (Quality/Cost/Delivery)
8.2
Pronunciation: /kjuː siː diː/ /; / /kuː siː diː/ (AmE), /kjuː siː diː/ /; / /kuː siː diː/ (BrE)
Abbreviation for Quality, Cost, Delivery; a manufacturing KPI used to measure and compare production performance across those three dimensions.

Examples

QCD score report
The plant's QCD score improved after lean reforms focusing on quality, cost, and delivery times.
Management reviews monthly QCD reports to identify bottlenecks and reduce production costs quickly.
Improving QCD requires cross-functional teams to balance product quality with on-time delivery and budget.
