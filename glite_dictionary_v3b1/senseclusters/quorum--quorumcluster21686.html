quorum

noun
minimum required members for a meeting
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔɹ.əm / (AmE), / ˈkwɔː.rəm / (BrE)
the minimum number of members required to be present at a meeting in order to conduct official business

Examples

The quorum required
According to the bylaws, a quorum is needed to pass any resolutions.
The committee could not vote because a quorum was not present.
We waited an hour to achieve a quorum before starting the meeting.
