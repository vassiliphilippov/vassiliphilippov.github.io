unpitched

adjective
without definite pitch
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪtʃt / (BrE)
not having a definite or specific musical pitch

Examples

An unpitched instrument
Many percussion instruments, such as the snare drum, are considered unpitched.
The composer used both pitched and unpitched sounds to create a unique musical texture.
Cymbals are typically classified as unpitched instruments in an orchestra.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atonal - lacking a clear musical key or pitch center
  • antonym: pitched - having a definite musical pitch
