semidouble

adjective
partly double petals
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈdʌbəl / (BrE)
having more petals than a single flower but fewer than a fully double flower

Examples

Semidouble flowers

Many gardeners prefer semidouble flowers for their balanced appearance.
This variety produces semidouble blooms that attract bees and butterflies.
