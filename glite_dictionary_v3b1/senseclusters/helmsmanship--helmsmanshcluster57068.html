helmsmanship

noun
skill in steering boats
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlmzmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmzmənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill or ability to steer and control a ship or boat effectively

Examples

Expert helmsmanship at sea
Her excellent helmsmanship helped the crew win the sailing competition.
The captain praised his first mate's helmsmanship during the rough weather.
Good helmsmanship is essential when navigating narrow or crowded waterways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigation - the process of planning and controlling the course of a ship
  • synonym: seamanship - the skill, techniques, and knowledge needed to operate a ship
