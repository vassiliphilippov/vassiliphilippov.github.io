four-poster

noun
bed with four corner posts, often with canopy
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr ˌpoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˌpəʊstə / (BrE)
A bed with four tall posts at the corners, often designed to support a canopy or curtains.

Examples

A four-poster bed
She always dreamed of sleeping in a grand four-poster with heavy velvet curtains.
The old manor house still has a Victorian four-poster in the master bedroom.
They bought an antique four-poster to give their room a classic look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canopy bed - bed with a frame for supporting curtains or drapes overhead
