physical condition

noun
state of the body or its functions
2.2 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the state of a person's body or how well its functions are operating, especially in terms of health and fitness

Examples

Good physical condition

Good physical condition is essential for athletes competing at a high level.
He trains regularly to improve his physical condition.
