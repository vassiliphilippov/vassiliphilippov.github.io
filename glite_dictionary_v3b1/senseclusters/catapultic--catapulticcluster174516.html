catapultic

adjective
relating to a catapult; sudden and forceful
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈpʌltɪk / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈpʌltɪk / (BrE)
having the qualities or motion of a catapult; sudden and forceful, like something thrown by a catapult

Examples

His career took a catapultic leap after his breakthrough performance.
The game ended with a catapultic shot that stunned everyone.
With a catapultic burst of energy, the gymnast launched herself into the air.
