Mother's Day

noun
annual holiday honoring mothers
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðərz deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəz deɪ / (BrE)
A holiday honoring mothers, celebrated on the second Sunday in May in many countries.

Examples

Mother's Day holiday
Don't forget to call your mother this Mother's Day!
On Mother's Day, many children give flowers and cards to their mothers.
We celebrated Mother's Day by taking our mom out for brunch.
