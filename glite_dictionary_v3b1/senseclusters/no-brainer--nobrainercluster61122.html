no-brainer

noun
something obvious or requiring little thought
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊˈbreɪnər / (AmE), / ˌnəʊˈbreɪnə / (BrE)
something that is very easy to decide or understand and requires little thought

Examples

A no-brainer choice
Choosing the cheaper and better-quality product was a no-brainer for me.
For most voters, supporting public education is a no-brainer.
It was a no-brainer to accept their generous offer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dilemma - a situation in which a difficult choice must be made
  • synonym: easy decision - a decision that is simple to make
