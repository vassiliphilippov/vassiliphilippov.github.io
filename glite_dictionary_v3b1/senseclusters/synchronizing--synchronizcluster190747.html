synchronizing

noun
the act of occurring at the same time
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making things happen at the same time or in unison
