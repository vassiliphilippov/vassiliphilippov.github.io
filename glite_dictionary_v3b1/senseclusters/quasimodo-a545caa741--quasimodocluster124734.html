Quasimodo

noun
offensive term for hunchback
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɑːziˈmoʊdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkwæzɪˈməʊdəʊ / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person with a severe humpback or noticeable physical deformity, referencing the fictional character from Victor Hugo's novel

Examples

A Quasimodo insult
The children cruelly called him Quasimodo because of his hunched back.
Using the word Quasimodo to describe someone is deeply hurtful and offensive.
He was unfairly nicknamed Quasimodo by classmates due to his physical appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunchback - a person with a severely curved back
