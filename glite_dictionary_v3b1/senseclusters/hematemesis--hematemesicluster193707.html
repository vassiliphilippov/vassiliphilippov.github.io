hematemesis

noun
vomiting of blood
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: /hiː.mə.ˈtem.ə.sɪs/ /; / /e.mə.ˈtem.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /hiː.mə.ˈtem.ə.sɪs/ /; / /e.mə.ˈtem.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
The vomiting of blood, often as a symptom of disease or injury, usually resulting from internal bleeding in the stomach or esophagus.
