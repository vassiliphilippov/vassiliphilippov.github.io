tomahawk

noun
light axe of Indigenous North Americans
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑː.məˌhɑk / (AmE), / ˈtɒ.məˌhɔːk / (BrE)
a light axe originally used as a tool or weapon by Indigenous peoples of North America.

Examples

A tomahawk axe
She saw a tomahawk displayed in the museum’s Native American exhibit.
The warrior carried a tomahawk as both a weapon and a tool.
Traders would sometimes exchange goods for a finely crafted tomahawk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatchet - a small, short-handled axe
