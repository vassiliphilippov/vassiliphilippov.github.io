human knee

noun
joint between thigh and lower leg in humans
2.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhjuÀêm…ôn niÀê / (AmE), / ÀàhjuÀêm…ôn niÀê / (BrE)
the joint in the human leg where the thigh bone (femur) meets the shin bones (tibia and fibula), protected in front by the patella (kneecap)

Examples

The human knee joint
An injury to the human knee can make it difficult to climb stairs.
The human knee is designed to support the weight of the body during standing and movement.
The doctor examined my human knee after I complained of pain while walking.
