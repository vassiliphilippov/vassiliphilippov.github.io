founder

verb
fail utterly; collapse
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndə / (BrE)
to fail or collapse completely (of a plan, project, institution, or structure)

Examples

Plans founder quickly
After months of hard work, the business foundered due to lack of funding.
The negotiations foundered when both sides refused to compromise.
Their plan to renovate the old house foundered because of constant disagreements.
The company began to founder after losing its main client.
The old bridge will founder if it is not repaired soon.
Their ambitious plan to expand overseas foundered due to a lack of funding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - to fall down or fail suddenly
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the desired aim or result
