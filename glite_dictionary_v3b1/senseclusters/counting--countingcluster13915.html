counting

noun
the act of enumerating or reciting numbers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
The action or process of determining the total number of something or reciting numbers in order.

Examples

Accurate counting of items
Accurate counting of the inventory was essential for the annual audit.
She practiced her counting by reciting numbers up to one hundred.
The counting continued for several hours after the polls closed.
