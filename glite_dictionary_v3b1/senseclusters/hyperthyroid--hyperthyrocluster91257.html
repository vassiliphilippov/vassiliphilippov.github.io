hyperthyroid

noun
person with overactive thyroid
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈθaɪrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈθaɪrɔɪd / (BrE)
a person or animal who has hyperthyroidism, meaning their thyroid gland is overactive

Examples

A hyperthyroid patient
The veterinarian prescribed medication for the hyperthyroid dog to help control its symptoms.
As a hyperthyroid, she needed regular blood tests to monitor her hormone levels.
Many older cats become hyperthyroid and require special diets or treatments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperthyroidism - the condition of having an overactive thyroid gland
  • antonym: hypothyroid - a person or animal with an underactive thyroid gland
