gridline

noun
line in a grid
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪdlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪdlaɪn / (BrE)
a line that is part of a grid, used to help align or reference elements in a layout or chart

Examples

A gridline on chart
The designer used the gridline to make sure all the images were perfectly aligned.
You can turn on the gridlines in your spreadsheet to help organize your data.
The map's gridlines make it easier to locate specific coordinates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guideline - a line used as a reference for alignment
