Home Secretary

noun
UK government minister; head of Home Office
4.6
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / həʊm ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
the British government minister who is the head of the Home Office, responsible for internal affairs such as security, law and order, and immigration

Examples

The Home Secretary role
Britain's Home Secretary is responsible for immigration policy and national security.
Following the attack, the Home Secretary faced questions in Parliament about police funding.
The Home Secretary announced new measures to combat cybercrime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interior minister - the government minister responsible for internal affairs in other countries
