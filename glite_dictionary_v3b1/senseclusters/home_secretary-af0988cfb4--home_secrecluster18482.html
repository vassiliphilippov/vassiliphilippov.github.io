Home Secretary

noun
UK government minister; head of Home Office
4.6
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / həʊm ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)

Britain's Home Secretary is responsible for immigration policy and national security.
