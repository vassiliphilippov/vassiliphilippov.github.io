practicability

noun
quality of being usable or feasible
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpræktɪkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌpræktɪkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being able to be used or put into practice successfully.

Examples

Economic practicability
Before launching the project, we should assess its economic practicability.
Engineers debated the practicability of constructing the bridge in that remote location.
The practicability of the new policy was questioned by several experts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feasibility - the state of being possible to do easily or conveniently
