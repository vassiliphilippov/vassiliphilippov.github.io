true blackberry

noun
European blackberry plant with edible berries
Pronunciation: / tru ˈblækbɛri / (AmE), / truː ˈblækbəri / (BrE)
A common European blackberry plant (Rubus fruticosus) and its varieties, bearing sweet, edible black or dark purple berries.

Examples

The true blackberry plant
Botanists study the differences between the true blackberry and its lookalikes in North America.
She made jam using only true blackberry varieties collected from the countryside.
The hedgerows were heavy with true blackberry bushes bursting with fruit in late summer.
