chopping block

noun
board for cutting food or wood
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑpɪŋ ˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpɪŋ ˌblɒk / (BrE)
a thick, sturdy wooden board or block on which food is cut or wood is chopped

Examples

A chopping block board
He used a sturdy chopping block outside to split logs for the fire.
She placed the carrots on the chopping block and began slicing them for the stew.
The old wooden chopping block was stained from years of use in the kitchen.
