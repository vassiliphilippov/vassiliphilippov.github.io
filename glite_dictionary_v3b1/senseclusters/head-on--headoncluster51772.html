head-on

adjective, adverb
directly, with confrontation
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛdˈɑn / (AmE), / ˌhɛdˈɒn / (BrE)
in a direct, forceful, or unhesitating way; addressing something without avoidance

She prefers to address problems head-on rather than ignore them.

You need to face your fears head-on to overcome them.
