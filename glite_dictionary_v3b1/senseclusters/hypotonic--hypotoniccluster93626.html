Examples

Hypotonic solution
A hypotonic fluid is often used to treat dehydration when cells need to absorb more water.
The laboratory compared the effects of hypotonic and hypertonic solutions on plant cells.
When placed in a hypotonic solution, red blood cells can swell and burst due to water intake.
