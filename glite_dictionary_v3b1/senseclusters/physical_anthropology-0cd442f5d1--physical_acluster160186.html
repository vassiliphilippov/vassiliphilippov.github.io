physical anthropology

noun
study of human biological and physical variation
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl ænˌθrɑːpəˈlɑːdʒi / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ænˌθrɒpəˈlɒdʒi / (BrE)
the branch of anthropology that studies the origins, biological evolution, and physical variation of human beings

Examples

Physical anthropology course
A course in physical anthropology typically covers genetics, evolution, and fossil records.
Recent discoveries in physical anthropology have changed our understanding of early human migration.
She decided to major in physical anthropology because she was fascinated by human evolution.
