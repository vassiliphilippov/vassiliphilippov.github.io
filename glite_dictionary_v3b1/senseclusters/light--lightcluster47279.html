Synonyms

  • light up
    2.5 3.19/m
    light up means to become bright or make something burn in everyday speech, while light means to make something bright or start a fire.
  • illuminate
    4.1 7.69/m
    illuminate means to shine light and make things visible in a clear, formal way, while light means to make something bright or start a fire.
  • ignite
    4.0 3.82/m
    ignite means to cause something to catch fire, often suddenly, while light means to make something bright or start a fire.
  • fire
    2.2 7.20/m
    fire means to start or feed a fire in a stove or engine, while light means to make something bright or start a fire.
