take a joke

verb
not be offended by a joke about oneself
3.5
Pronunciation: / teɪk ə dʒoʊk /; / teɪk ə ʤoʊk / (AmE), / teɪk ə dʒəʊk /; / teɪk ə ʤəʊk / (BrE)
