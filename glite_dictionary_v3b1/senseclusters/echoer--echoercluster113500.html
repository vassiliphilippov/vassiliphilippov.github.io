echoer

noun
one who repeats
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkoʊər / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊə / (BrE)
a person or thing that repeats or reflects sounds, words, or ideas made by others

Examples

An echoer repeats words
The child became an echoer of every word his older brother said.
In the mountains, the valley was a perfect echoer of distant thunder.
She was criticized for being an echoer of popular opinions rather than sharing her own ideas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: originator - a person who creates or starts something
  • synonym: repeater - a person or device that repeats something
