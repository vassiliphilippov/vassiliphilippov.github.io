Scheherazade

proper noun
orchestral suite by Rimsky-Korsakov
Pronunciation: / ʃəˌhɛrəˈzɑːd / (AmE), / ʃəˌhɛərəˈzɑːd / (BrE)
a four-movement orchestral suite composed by Nikolai Rimsky-Korsakov, inspired by the tales of One Thousand and One Nights
