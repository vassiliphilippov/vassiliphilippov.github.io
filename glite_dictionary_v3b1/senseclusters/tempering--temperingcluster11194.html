tempering

noun
heat treatment for hardening material
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of strengthening or hardening a material, especially metal or glass, by heating and then cooling it

Examples

Tempering steel
Glass used in car windows undergoes tempering to increase its strength and safety.
Proper tempering is essential to ensure the durability of metal tools.
The tempering of steel gives it the necessary toughness for construction use.
