American shrew mole

noun
small North American shrew-like mole
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ʃruː moʊl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ʃruː məʊl / (BrE)
a small, greyish-black shrew-like mole native to the United States and Canada, known for its slender body and long snout
