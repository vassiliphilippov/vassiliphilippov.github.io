foxface fish

noun
tropical reef fish with black and yellow markings
4.7 7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑksˌfeɪs fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌfeɪs fɪʃ / (BrE)
a tropical marine fish of the rabbitfish family (Siganidae), characterized by its elongated snout and bold black-and-yellow markings, native to the Indo-Pacific region
