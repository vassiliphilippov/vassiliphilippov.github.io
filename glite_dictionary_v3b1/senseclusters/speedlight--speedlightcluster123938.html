speedlight

noun
portable camera flash
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːdlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈspiːdlaɪt / (BrE)
a small, portable flash unit that attaches to a camera to provide additional lighting

Examples

A speedlight flash

She always carries a speedlight in her camera bag for unexpected low-light situations.
Adding a speedlight can greatly improve the quality of your photos in dim environments.
