push aside

verb
dismiss or ignore someone or something
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ əˈsaɪd / (AmE), / pʊʃ əˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to refuse to consider or pay attention to someone or something; to dismiss from one's thoughts or consideration

Examples

To push aside worries
He pushed aside the issue, preferring not to talk about it anymore.
She tried to push aside her doubts and focus on the task.
The manager quickly pushed aside any objections to the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismiss - to refuse to consider something
  • synonym: ignore - to pay no attention to
