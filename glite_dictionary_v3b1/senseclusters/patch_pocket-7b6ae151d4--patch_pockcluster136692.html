patch pocket

noun
flat pocket sewn on outside of clothing
1.0
Pronunciation: / pætʃ ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / pætʃ ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a flat pocket made from a separate piece of fabric and sewn onto the outside of a garment

Examples

A patch pocket on jacket
He slipped his phone into the patch pocket on his jacket.
Her coat is decorated with brightly colored patch pockets for a stylish touch.
The shirt features a small patch pocket on the left side of the chest.
