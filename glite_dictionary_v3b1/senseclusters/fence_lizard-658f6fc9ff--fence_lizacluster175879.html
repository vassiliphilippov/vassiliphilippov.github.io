fence lizard

noun
a North American spiny lizard
8.2
Pronunciation: / fɛns ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / fɛns ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a spiny lizard commonly found basking on fences or rocks in the United States and northern Mexico

Examples
