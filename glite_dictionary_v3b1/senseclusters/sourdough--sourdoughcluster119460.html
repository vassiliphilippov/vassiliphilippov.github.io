sourdough

noun
seasoned settler or prospector (western North America)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊrˌdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊədəʊ / (BrE)
a seasoned settler or prospector, especially in the western United States, northwest Canada, or Alaska, often one who participated in gold rushes

Examples

Old sourdough prospector
As a true sourdough, he knew every trail and shortcut through the mountains.
Many seasoned sourdoughs settled in Alaska after years of prospecting.
The old sourdough shared tales of the Yukon Gold Rush with the newcomers.
