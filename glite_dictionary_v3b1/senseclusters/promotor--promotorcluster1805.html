promotor

noun
company founder
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / prəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a person who takes the initiative to form a company, arranging its finances and legal setup

Examples

Company promotor
The promotor was responsible for securing funding and registering the new business.
Without a skilled promotor, the company struggled to get off the ground.
As a promotor, she handled all the legal paperwork for the startup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrepreneur - a person who starts and manages a business, taking on financial risks
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges and coordinates activities or events
