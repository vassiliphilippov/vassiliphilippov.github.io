proliferous

adjective
rapid cell growth
Pronunciation: / prəˈlɪfərəs / (AmE), / prəˈlɪfərəs / (BrE)
showing or characterized by rapid or abnormal increase in the number of cells or tissue elements

Examples

Proliferous cell increase
The doctor noted a proliferous growth in the tissue sample taken from the patient.
Researchers are studying how proliferous tumors develop and spread in the body.
A proliferous response in the cells can indicate an underlying medical condition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proliferative - relating to or characterized by cell multiplication
  • antonym: regressive - characterized by a return to a former or less developed state
