State of Qatar

proper noun
country in western Asia
3.9
Pronunciation: / steɪt əv kəˈtɑr / (AmE), / steɪt əv ˈkætɑː / (BrE)
a country in western Asia, occupying the Qatar Peninsula on the northeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, known for its oil wealth and capital city, Doha

Examples

The State of Qatar country
Natural gas reserves are a major source of wealth for the State of Qatar.
The State of Qatar invested heavily in infrastructure ahead of the World Cup.
The capital of the State of Qatar is Doha, a rapidly developing city.
