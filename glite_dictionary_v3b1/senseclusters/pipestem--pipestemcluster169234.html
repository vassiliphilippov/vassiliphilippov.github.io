pipestem

noun
dried plant tube
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpˌstɛm / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpˌstɛm / (BrE)
a long, thin plant stem, especially one used as a tube, whistle, or resembling the stem of a pipe
