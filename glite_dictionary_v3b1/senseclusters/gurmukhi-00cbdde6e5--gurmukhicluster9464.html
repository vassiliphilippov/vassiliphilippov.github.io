Gurmukhi

noun
Punjabi writing script
Pronunciation: / ɡʊrˈmuːki / (AmE), / ɡʊəˈmuːki / (BrE)
the script used for writing the Punjabi language, especially in Sikh religious texts

Examples

The Gurmukhi alphabet
Many Sikh scriptures are written in Gurmukhi and studied by followers around the world.
She took a class to learn how to read and write in Gurmukhi.
The Punjabi language is commonly written using the Gurmukhi script in India.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Devanagari - a script used for several Indian languages including Hindi and Sanskrit
  • synonym: Punjabi - the language primarily written in the Gurmukhi script
