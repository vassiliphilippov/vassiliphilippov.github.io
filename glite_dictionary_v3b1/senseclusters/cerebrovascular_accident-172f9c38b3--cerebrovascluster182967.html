cerebrovascular accident

noun
medical term for stroke
7.0
Pronunciation: / səˌriːbroʊˈvæskjulɚ ˈæksɪdənt /; / səˌriːbroʊˈvæskjələr ˈæksɪdənt / (AmE), / sɛˌriːbrəʊˈvæskjʊlə ˈæksɪdənt /; / səˌriːbrəʊˈvæskjʊlə ˈæksɪdənt / (BrE)
a sudden medical event where blood flow to the brain is interrupted, causing loss of brain function; a stroke

Related Terms

  • synonym: stroke - a sudden attack of weakness or paralysis affecting one side of the body, caused by an interruption of blood flow to the brain
