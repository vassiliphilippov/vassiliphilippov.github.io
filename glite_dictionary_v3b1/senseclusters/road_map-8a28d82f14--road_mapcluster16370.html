road map

noun
detailed plan to achieve a goal
Pronunciation: / roʊd mæp / (AmE), / rəʊd mæp / (BrE)
a detailed plan or set of instructions designed to guide progress towards a goal or solution

Examples

Road map for success
Her presentation provided a detailed road map for implementing the new system.
The company released a road map outlining its strategy for the next five years.
We need a clear road map if we want to achieve our targets efficiently.
