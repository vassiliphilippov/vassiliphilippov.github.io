hectometer

noun
unit of length (100 meters)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛk.təˌmiː.tɚ /; / ˈhɛktəˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈhɛk.təˌmiː.tə /; / ˈhɛktəˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to 100 meters

Examples

One hectometer
A hectometer is equivalent to one hundred meters in the metric system.
Engineers sometimes use the hectometer to measure large distances.

A hectometre is not commonly used in daily measurements, but it is useful for scientific purposes.
Satellite images can cover several hectometres of terrain in a single scan.
The race track was exactly one hectometre in length, making calculations easy.
