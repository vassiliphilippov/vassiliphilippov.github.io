cost overrun

noun
excess cost above budget
7.0 4.1
Pronunciation: /kɑːst ˈoʊ.və.rʌn/ /; / /kɔːst ˈoʊ.və.rʌn/ (AmE), /kɒst ˈəʊ.və.rʌn/ (BrE)
