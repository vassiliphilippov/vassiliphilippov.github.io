Mesonyx

proper noun
extinct mammal genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsəˌnɪks / (AmE), / ˈmiːsəˌnɪks / (BrE)
an extinct genus of carnivorous mammals that lived during the Eocene epoch

Examples

The Mesonyx fossil

The powerful jaws of Mesonyx suggest it was a top predator in its environment.
Fossils of Mesonyx have been found in both North America and Asia.
