Synonyms

  • ectothermic
    9.1 0.02/m
    ectothermic is a scientific term for animals that use environmental heat to control body temperature, while cold-blooded is the common everyday term.
  • poikilothermic
    9.9 0.00/m
    poikilothermic describes animals whose internal temperature varies with the environment, while cold-blooded is the broader everyday label.
  • heterothermic
    9.9 0.00/m
    heterothermic refers to animals that sometimes have changing body temperatures and may exclude birds and mammals, while cold-blooded means not internally regulated.
