fancy dress

noun
costume for a masquerade or party
Pronunciation: / ˈfænsi drɛs / (AmE), / ˈfænsi drɛs / (BrE)
a costume worn to disguise oneself, especially at a masquerade or costume party

Examples

A fancy dress costume
At the charity event, children competed for the best fancy dress award.
She arrived at the party in a striking fancy dress that looked like a 1920s flapper outfit.
The invitation said that fancy dress was optional, but most people wore creative costumes.
