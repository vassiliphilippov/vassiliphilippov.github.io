lepidopterous insect

noun
insect with scaly wings (butterflies, moths)
5.3 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˈdɑptərəs ˈɪnsɛkt / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərəs ˈɪnsɛkt / (BrE)
