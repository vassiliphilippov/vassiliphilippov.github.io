unrepairable

noun
item beyond repair
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈpɛrəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpeərəbəl / (BrE)
an object or thing that cannot be fixed or restored to working condition

Examples

An unrepairable machine
After the fire, many of the machines were classified as unrepairable and removed from the factory.
The technician sorted the unrepairable from the items that could still be fixed.
All unrepairable electronics must be disposed of according to local regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreparable - impossible to repair or fix
