too good to go to waste

adjective
so valuable or useful it should not be wasted
2.2
Pronunciation: / tu ɡʊd tə ɡoʊ tə weɪst / (AmE), / tuː ɡʊd tə ɡəʊ tə weɪst / (BrE)
so valuable, useful, or enjoyable that it should not be thrown away, ignored, or wasted

Examples

Too good to go to waste skills
His skills are too good to go to waste in a desk job; he should be out in the field.
The leftover paint was too good to go to waste, so she gave it to her neighbor.
The strawberries were still fresh and too good to go to waste, so I made a pie.
