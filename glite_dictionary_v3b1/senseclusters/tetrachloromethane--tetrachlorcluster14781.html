tetrachloromethane

noun
toxic chemical solvent (CCl₄)

Pronunciation: /te.trə.klɑːr.oʊ.ˈmeθ.eɪn/ /; / /te.trə.klɔːr.oʊ.ˈmeθ.eɪn/ (AmE), /te.trə.klɔː.rəʊ.ˈmeθ.eɪn/ (BrE)
