predestinarian

adjective
relating to belief in predestination
Pronunciation: / prɪˌdɛstəˈnɛriən / (AmE), / priˌdɛstɪˈneəriən / (BrE)
Relating to or believing in the doctrine of predestination, especially in theology.

Examples

Predestinarian doctrine
His predestinarian beliefs shaped his view of salvation and free will.
Some critics find predestinarian doctrines at odds with moral responsibility.
The church split due to disagreements over predestinarian theology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predestination - the doctrine that all events, especially salvation, are predetermined by God
