Examples

Mythopoetic style

Synonyms

  • mythopoeic
    9.9 0.05/m
    mythopoeic refers to inventing myths in stories, while mythopoetic describes an imaginative process actively creating new myths.
  • mythic
    6.2 2.92/m
    mythic evokes legendary figures and grand stories, while mythopoetic focuses on shaping or creating myths in art and literature.
