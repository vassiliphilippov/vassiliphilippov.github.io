apolune

noun
point farthest from the Moon in orbit
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæp.ə.luːn/ /; / /ə.ˈpoʊ.luːn/ (AmE), /ˈæp.ə.luːn/ /; / /ə.ˈpəʊ.luːn/ (BrE)
The point in the orbit of a spacecraft or object around the Moon that is farthest from the Moon.

Examples

The apolune point
At apolune, the probe is farthest from the Moon during its elliptical orbit.
Mission controllers adjusted the satellite's apolune to optimize scientific observations.
The spacecraft will reach its apolune before starting its descent to the lunar surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apoapsis - the point in an orbit farthest from the body being orbited
  • antonym: perilune - the point in the orbit of a spacecraft or object around the Moon that is nearest to the Moon
