concealer

noun
person or thing that hides
4.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈsiːlər / (AmE), / kənˈsiːlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that hides or covers something, especially to prevent it from being seen or known

Examples

A secret concealer
The makeup artist used a concealer to hide the dark circles under her eyes.
The police believed the landlord was a concealer who helped fugitives avoid capture.
A good concealer can make blemishes almost invisible on your skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hider - a person who hides something or themselves
  • antonym: revealer - a person or thing that makes something known or visible
