Jah

proper noun
Rastafarian name for God
Pronunciation: / dʒɑː / (AmE), / dʒɑː / (BrE)
a name for God, especially used in Rastafarianism and reggae music

Examples

Many reggae songs give thanks and praise to Jah for blessings and guidance.
Followers of Rastafarianism believe that Jah is always present in their lives.
