storytime

noun
occasion for sharing stories
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈstɔːritaɪm / (BrE)
an informal occasion when people share stories, anecdotes, or personal experiences, often for entertainment

Examples

Family storytime
After dinner, the friends gathered for storytime and shared memories from their travels.
The teacher organized a weekly storytime where everyone could tell a personal tale.
During the camping trip, storytime by the fire became everyone's favorite tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: storytelling - the activity of telling or writing stories
