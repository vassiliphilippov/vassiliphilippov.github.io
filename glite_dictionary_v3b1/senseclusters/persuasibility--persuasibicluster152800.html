persuasibility

noun
ability to persuade
7.5
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.suː.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /pɜːr.sweɪ.zə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /pə.suː.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /pə.sweɪ.zə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality or degree to which something is able to persuade or convince people

Examples

Persuasibility of a message
The persuasibility of the advertisement was tested on a group of potential customers.
Researchers studied the persuasibility of different political messages during the campaign.
Her speech lacked the persuasibility needed to change anyone's mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convincingness - the quality of being able to convince
  • synonym: influence - the power to affect or change someone or something
  • antonym: unpersuasiveness - the quality of not being able to persuade
