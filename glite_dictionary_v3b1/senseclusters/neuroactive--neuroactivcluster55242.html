neuroactive

noun
nervous system agent
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
a chemical or drug that affects the nervous system or brain function

Examples

A neuroactive compound
Researchers are studying a new neuroactive to treat memory loss in elderly patients.
The effects of the neuroactive were observed in the brain scans of volunteers.
Before approval, every neuroactive must undergo strict safety testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurotoxin - a substance that is poisonous to nerve tissue
  • synonym: stimulant - a substance that increases activity in the nervous system
