confessionary

noun
manual for confessions
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / kənˈfɛʃənəri / (BrE)
a book or manual containing model confessions, questions, or instructions for confessors and penitents, used in religious contexts
