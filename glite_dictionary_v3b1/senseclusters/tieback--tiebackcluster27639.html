tieback

noun
device for holding curtains back
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈtaɪˌbæk / (BrE)
A loop or band, often decorative, used to hold or pull back curtains or drapes at the sides of a window.

Examples

Curtain tieback loop
She chose elegant gold tiebacks for her living room curtains.
The velvet drapes were secured with matching tiebacks on each side.
Without tiebacks, the heavy curtains would block too much sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holdback - a device used to keep something held back; also used for curtains
