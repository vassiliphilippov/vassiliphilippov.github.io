pungy

noun
Chesapeake Bay sailboat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌndʒi / (AmE), / ˈpʌndʒi / (BrE)
a two-masted sailing boat traditionally used in the Chesapeake Bay for oyster dredging and cargo transport

Examples

Fishermen relied on the sturdy pungy for transporting oysters and other cargo.
