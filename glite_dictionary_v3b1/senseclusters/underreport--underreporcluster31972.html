underreport

verb
to report too little
6.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.də.rɪ.ˈpɔːrt/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.rɪ.ˈpɔːt/ (BrE)
to report an amount, value, or number as less than it actually is, often intentionally or by not reporting fully
