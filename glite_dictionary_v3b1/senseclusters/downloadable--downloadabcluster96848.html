downloadable

adjective
able to be saved
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌloʊdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnləʊdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be saved or transferred from the internet or another computer to your own device

Examples

Downloadable software
The software is downloadable from the official website after you create an account.
All the course materials are downloadable as PDF files for offline study.
Some music albums are only available as downloadable content rather than physical CDs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: streaming - able to be played directly from the internet without saving
  • synonym: transferable - able to be moved from one place or device to another
