agal

noun
cord worn to secure a kaffiyeh
9.9 9.1
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɑl / (AmE), / əˈɡɑːl / (BrE)
A cord, traditionally made of goat's hair, worn by Arab men, especially Bedouins, to secure the kaffiyeh on the head.

Examples

The black agal contrasted sharply with his white headscarf.
Traditional Bedouin attire includes both the kaffiyeh and the agal.
