pictographically

adverb
using pictographs
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪktəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpɪktəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses pictures, pictographs, or illustrations to convey meaning or information instead of words

Examples

To write pictographically
The instructions were presented pictographically so that people of any language could understand them.
Some ancient cultures recorded their history pictographically on stone tablets.
The safety procedures are explained pictographically on the airplane card for easy comprehension.
The instructions were presented pictographically to help people who do not speak the language.
The ancient story was recorded pictographically on the walls of the cave.
He summarized the data pictographically with charts and icons for easier understanding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: verbally - in a way that uses words or speech
  • synonym: visually - in a way that relates to seeing or sight
