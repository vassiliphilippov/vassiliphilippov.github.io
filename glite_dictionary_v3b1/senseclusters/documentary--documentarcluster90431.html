documentary

noun
nonfiction film or TV program
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri / (AmE), / ˌdɒkjʊˈmentəri / (BrE)
a film or television program that presents factual information about a person, event, or subject

Examples

A documentary movie
After watching the documentary, he felt inspired to learn more about the subject.
She spent three years filming a documentary on wildlife conservation in Africa.
The documentary about climate change was both informative and engaging.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: docudrama - a dramatized television program based on real events
  • antonym: feature film - a full-length film with a fictional narrative
