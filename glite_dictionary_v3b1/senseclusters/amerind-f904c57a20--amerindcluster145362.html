Amerind

noun
member/language of indigenous peoples of the Americas
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.ə.rɪnd / (AmE), / ˈæm.ər.ɪnd / (BrE)
a member of the indigenous peoples of the Americas, or any of the languages spoken by these peoples

Examples
