bistable

adjective
having two stable states
Pronunciation: / baɪˈsteɪbəl / (AmE), / baɪˈsteɪbəl / (BrE)
able to exist in two different stable states, especially in perception, physical systems, or electronics

Examples

The device is bistable, meaning it can remain in either state until triggered.
