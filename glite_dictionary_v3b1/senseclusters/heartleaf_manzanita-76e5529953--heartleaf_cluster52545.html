heartleaf manzanita

noun
a Californian shrub with heart-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌlif mænzəˈniːtə / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtlˌiːf mænzəˈniːtə / (BrE)
an upright shrub native to California, characterized by leaves with heart-shaped lobes at the base

Examples

The heartleaf manzanita shrub
Botanists studied the unique shape of the leaves on the heartleaf manzanita.
The heartleaf manzanita is commonly found growing on rocky slopes in northern California.
Wildlife depend on the berries produced by the heartleaf manzanita shrub.
