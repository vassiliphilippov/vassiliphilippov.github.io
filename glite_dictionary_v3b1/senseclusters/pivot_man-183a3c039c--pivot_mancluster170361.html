pivot man

noun
person around whom others turn in a formation
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvət mæn / (AmE), / ˈpɪvət mæn / (BrE)
the person in a formation around whom the others turn, especially in marching or military maneuvers.

Examples

The pivot man stands
During the drill, the pivot man remained stationary while the rest of the squad wheeled around him.
Each time the team changed direction, the pivot man set the position for the turn.
In marching band formations, the pivot man plays a crucial role in keeping lines straight.

  • synonym: pivot - the central point, pin, or shaft on which a mechanism turns or oscillates
