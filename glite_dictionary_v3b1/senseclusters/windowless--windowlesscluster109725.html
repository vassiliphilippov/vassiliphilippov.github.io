windowless

adjective
without windows
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊləs / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊləs / (BrE)
having no windows

Examples

A windowless room
He worked in a small, windowless office for most of the year.
The prisoners were kept in windowless cells deep underground.
They held the meeting in a windowless conference room with artificial lighting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airless - lacking fresh air or ventilation
  • antonym: bright - full of light; well-lit
