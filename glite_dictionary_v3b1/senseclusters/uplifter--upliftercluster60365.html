uplifter

noun
lifting device
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈlɪftər / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftə / (BrE)
a mechanical device designed to lift, raise, or elevate heavy objects or materials

Examples

A uplifter machine
The workers used an uplifter to move the heavy machinery onto the platform.
Safety guidelines require regular maintenance of every uplifter in the warehouse.
A new uplifter was installed to help staff handle large boxes more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crane - a large machine for lifting and moving heavy objects
  • synonym: hoist - a device used for lifting or raising heavy things
  • antonym: lowerer - a device used to move objects downward
