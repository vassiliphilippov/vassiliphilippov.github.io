logion

noun
saying attributed to Jesus, not in Gospels
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊɡiˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡiˌɒn / (BrE)
a saying attributed to Jesus, regarded as authentic by some scholars, but not recorded in the canonical Gospels

Examples

A logion of Jesus
Scholars have debated the authenticity of the logion found in the Gospel of Thomas.
Some researchers consider this statement to be an independent logion of Jesus.
This ancient manuscript contains a logion that is not found in the New Testament.
