Aedes albopictus

noun
species of mosquito known as Asian tiger mosquito
Pronunciation: / aɪˈiːdiːz ˌælboʊˈpɪktəs / (AmE), / aɪˈiːdiːz ˌalbəʊˈpɪktəs / (BrE)
a species of mosquito native to Asia, now widespread in other regions, known for its striped appearance and ability to transmit diseases such as dengue and Zika
