onboard

adjective
built-in feature
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌbɔːd / (BrE)
built into or included as part of a machine, device, or system rather than being separate or external

Examples

Onboard camera
The new laptop comes with an onboard graphics card for better performance.
Many cars now have onboard navigation systems to help drivers find their way.
The spacecraft's onboard computer controls all of its main functions automatically.

Related Terms

  • antonym: external - separate from the main device or system
  • synonym: integrated - combined into a single unit or system
