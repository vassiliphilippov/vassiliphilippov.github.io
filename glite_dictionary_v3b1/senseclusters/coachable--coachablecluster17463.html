coachable

adjective
open to feedback
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃəbəl / (BrE)
willing and able to learn and improve from feedback or instruction

Examples

Coachable player
A coachable player listens carefully to the coach and tries to improve every day.
Employers value coachable employees who are eager to learn new skills.
Her coachable attitude helped her adapt quickly to the new team's strategies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stubborn - unwilling to change or accept advice
  • synonym: teachable - willing to learn and accept instruction
