in someone's eyes

preposition
in someone's opinion or judgment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsʌmwənz aɪz / (BrE)
according to someone's opinion or perspective; as seen or judged by someone

Examples

In my eyes
In my eyes, she is the most talented artist in the group.
In the teacher's eyes, punctuality and effort are just as important as grades.
Their actions were justified in his eyes, even if others disagreed.
