deoxidize

verb
remove oxygen from (chemically)
Pronunciation: / diˈɑksɪˌdaɪz /; / diˈɑksɪdaɪz / (AmE), / diˈɒksɪdaɪz / (BrE)
to remove oxygen from a compound or substance, especially by a chemical process

Examples

To deoxidize metal
Hydrogen gas can be used to deoxidize certain chemical compounds in industrial processes.
Laboratory procedures frequently deoxidize solutions to prevent unwanted reactions with oxygen.
Metals are often treated to deoxidize them before use in construction.
Certain processes can deoxidise water to prevent corrosion.
Manufacturers often deoxidise steel to improve its quality.
The chemist used hydrogen to deoxidise the sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oxidise - to combine or become combined with oxygen
  • antonym: oxidize - to combine or react with oxygen
  • synonym: reduce - to cause a chemical reduction; to gain electrons or lose oxygen
