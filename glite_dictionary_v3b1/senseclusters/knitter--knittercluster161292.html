knitter

noun
person who knits fabrics or clothing
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪtər / (AmE), / ˈnɪtə / (BrE)
a person who makes clothing or fabric by forming yarn or thread into a series of connected loops with needles or a machine

Examples

A knitter at work
As a passionate knitter, she spends her weekends creating intricate patterns.
My grandmother is an avid knitter who makes beautiful scarves every winter.
The club welcomes both experienced knitters and complete beginners.
