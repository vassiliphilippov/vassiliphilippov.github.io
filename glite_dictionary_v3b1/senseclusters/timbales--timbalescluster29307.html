timbales

noun
small molded dishes
9.0
Pronunciation: / tɪmˈbɑːlz / (AmE), / tɪmˈbɑːlz / (BrE)
small savory or sweet dishes baked or molded in a round pan, often served individually as appetizers or desserts

Examples

Spinach timbales appetizer
The chef served elegant timbales filled with spinach and cheese as a starter.
For dessert, we enjoyed sweet timbales made with custard and berries.
She learned to make vegetable timbales during her cooking class in Paris.
