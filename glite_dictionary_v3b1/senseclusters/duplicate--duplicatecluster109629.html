duplicate

verb
increase twofold; double
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈduːplɪˌkeɪt, ˈdjuːplɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈdjuːplɪˌkeɪt / (BrE)
to increase to twice the original amount or number; to double.

Examples

To duplicate the amount
If you duplicate the ingredients, the recipe will yield twice as much food.
Our goal is to duplicate last year's sales figures within the next quarter.
The number of participants duplicated over the weekend due to the viral marketing campaign.
