work-clothes

noun
clothing for manual labor
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːk ˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk ˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
clothes designed to be worn while performing manual labor, typically durable and practical rather than fashionable.

Examples

Work-clothes for labor
He changed into his work-clothes before heading out to repair the fence.
Her work-clothes were stained with paint after a long day on the job.
The factory required all employees to wear protective work-clothes during their shifts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uniform - standardized clothing worn by members of an organization while participating in that organization's activity
