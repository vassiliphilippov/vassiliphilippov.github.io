Indian meal

noun
coarsely ground corn (as food)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən miːl / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪən miːl / (BrE)
Coarsely ground corn, especially as used for food.

Examples

Coarse Indian meal
Early settlers in America relied heavily on Indian meal as a basic ingredient for bread and porridge.
In some regions, Indian meal is still used to make traditional cornbread and pudding.
She baked a rustic loaf using Indian meal and whole wheat flour.
