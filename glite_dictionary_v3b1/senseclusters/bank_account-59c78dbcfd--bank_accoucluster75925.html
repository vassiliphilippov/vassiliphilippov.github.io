bank account

noun
an account with a bank for depositing and withdrawing money
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋk əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈbæŋk əˌkaʊnt /; / ˈbaŋk əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an arrangement with a bank that allows you to deposit, withdraw, and keep track of your money
