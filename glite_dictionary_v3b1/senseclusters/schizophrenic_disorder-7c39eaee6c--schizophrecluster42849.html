schizophrenic disorder

noun
severe mental disorder with distorted reality
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk dɪsˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a severe mental disorder characterized by distortions of reality, disordered thinking and language, and withdrawal from social interaction
