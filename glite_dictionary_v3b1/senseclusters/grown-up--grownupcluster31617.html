grown-up

noun
adult person
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊn ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊn ʌp / (BrE)
an adult person, especially in contrast to a child

Examples

Grown-up helps children
The grown-up helped the children cross the street safely.
At the party, the kids played games while the grown-ups talked in the kitchen.
You need to ask a grown-up before using the stove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adult - a fully grown person
  • antonym: child - a young person who is not yet an adult
