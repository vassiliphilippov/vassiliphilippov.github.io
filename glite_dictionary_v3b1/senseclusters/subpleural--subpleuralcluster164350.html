subpleural

adjective
beneath the pleura
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈplʊrəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈplʊərəl / (BrE)
located or occurring just beneath the pleura, the membrane covering the lungs

Examples

Subpleural lesion
The doctor identified a subpleural lesion on the patient's chest X-ray.
A subpleural nodule was found during the routine lung screening.
Researchers studied the effects of subpleural fibrosis in patients with chronic lung disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleural - relating to the pleura, the membrane covering the lungs
