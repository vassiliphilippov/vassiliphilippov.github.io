fazenda

noun
Brazilian country estate
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈzɛndə / (AmE), / fəˈzɛndə / (BrE)
a large rural estate in Brazil, including the main house and the surrounding farmland or plantation, often used for accommodation, leisure, or farming (such as coffee, sugar, or cattle)
