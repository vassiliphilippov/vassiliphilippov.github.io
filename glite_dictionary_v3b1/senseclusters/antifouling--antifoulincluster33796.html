antifouling

adjective
prevents marine growth
7.2
Pronunciation: /æn.ti.ˈfaʊ.lɪŋ/ /; / /æn.tə.ˈfaʊ.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /æn.ti.ˈfaʊ.lɪŋ/ /; / /æn.tə.ˈfaʊ.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
