antifouling

adjective
prevents marine growth
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈfaʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈfaʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
designed to prevent the accumulation of unwanted organisms, such as barnacles or algae, on underwater surfaces

Examples

Antifouling paint
The yacht was treated with an antifouling coating to keep its hull clean.
Regular application of antifouling paint helps prevent marine organisms from attaching to boats.
Ship owners invest in antifouling technology to improve fuel efficiency and reduce maintenance costs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fouled - covered with unwanted material or organisms
  • synonym: protective - serving to protect something from harm or damage
