star-shaped

adjective
shaped like a star
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑr ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈstɑː ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a star, with points radiating from a center.

Examples

A star-shaped crack
A star-shaped crack appeared in the car's windshield after the impact.
She wore a necklace with a star-shaped pendant.
The child drew a star-shaped figure in the sand.
