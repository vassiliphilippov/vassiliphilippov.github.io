radio source

noun
object emitting radio waves
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiˌoʊ sɔrs / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ sɔːs / (BrE)
an object, especially in space, that emits radio waves detectable by radio telescopes

Examples

A radio source in space
Astronomers discovered a new radio source in a distant galaxy cluster.
Every radio source in the catalog has its position precisely measured.
The intense radio source puzzled scientists for years before being identified as a pulsar.

  • synonym: radio galaxy - a galaxy that emits a large amount of radio waves
