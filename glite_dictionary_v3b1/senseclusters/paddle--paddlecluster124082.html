paddle

noun
short oar for canoes or small boats
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd(ə)l / (AmE), / ˈpad(ə)l / (BrE)
a short, light oar used without an oarlock to propel a canoe or small boat

Examples

Canoe paddle
Each person took a paddle and helped row across the lake.
He grabbed the paddle and pushed the canoe into the stream.
She leaned over the side to dip her paddle into the clear water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oar - a pole with a flat blade used to row or steer a boat
