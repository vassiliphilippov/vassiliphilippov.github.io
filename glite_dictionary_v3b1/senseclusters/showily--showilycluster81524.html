Examples

To showily display
He showily displayed his new watch at every opportunity.
She decorated her living room showily, using lots of gold and glitter everywhere.
The actor arrived showily in a bright red sports car.
He tended to act showily in public, always seeking everyone's attention.
She arrived at the party showily dressed in a glittering gold gown.
The mansion was decorated showily with bright lights and colorful banners.
