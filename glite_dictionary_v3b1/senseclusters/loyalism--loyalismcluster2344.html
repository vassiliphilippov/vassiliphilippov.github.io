loyalism

noun
support for political union
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔɪəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪəlɪzəm / (BrE)
support for maintaining political union with an established authority, especially the union of Northern Ireland with Great Britain.

Examples

Northern Ireland loyalism
The history class discussed the rise of loyalism in Northern Ireland during the twentieth century.
Many political debates in Belfast center around issues of loyalism and national identity.
Some communities express their loyalism through parades and public celebrations of British heritage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nationalism - advocacy for national independence or self-government
  • synonym: unionism - support for the political union between Northern Ireland and Great Britain
