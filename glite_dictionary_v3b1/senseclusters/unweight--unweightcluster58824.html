unweight

verb
to reduce load
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪt / (BrE)
to reduce the weight or load supported by a body part, especially during movement or exercise

Examples

To unweight the legs
The therapist used a harness to unweight the patient's legs during walking exercises.
Special treadmills can unweight the body to help people recover from injuries.
Athletes sometimes unweight their joints to reduce stress during rehabilitation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: support - to hold up or bear the weight of something
  • synonym: unload - to remove a load or burden from something
