demon

noun
evil supernatural being
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːmən / (AmE), / ˈdiːmən / (BrE)
A supernatural being, often considered evil, that is believed to possess or torment people or to bring about harm.

Examples

An evil demon
In many stories, a demon tries to tempt heroes into committing evil acts.
Some people claimed they saw a demon disappear into the shadows.
The villagers believed a demon was haunting the old church.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - benevolent supernatural being
  • synonym: devil - supreme evil spirit; Satan
