archivolt

noun
curved decorative molding
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrkɪˌvoʊlt / (AmE), / ˈɑːkɪˌvɒlt / (BrE)
a decorative molding or band that follows the curve along the face of an arch

Examples

Carved archivolt band
The ancient cathedral featured a beautifully carved archivolt above its main entrance.
Restorers carefully cleaned the damaged archivolt to reveal its original patterns.
Each archivolt in the building was decorated with floral designs and geometric shapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arch - a curved structure supporting the weight above it
  • synonym: molding - a decorative strip used for ornamentation
