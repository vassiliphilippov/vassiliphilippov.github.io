hiring hall

noun
union job placement office
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɹɪŋ hɔl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəɹɪŋ hɔːl / (BrE)
A union-run office where job assignments are given to workers, often based on seniority or a rotation system.

Examples

The hiring hall office
After waiting at the hiring hall for several hours, she finally received a job assignment.
Many construction workers depend on the hiring hall to find their next project.
The union operates a hiring hall to ensure jobs are distributed fairly among its members.

  • synonym: employment agency - an organization that finds jobs for people
