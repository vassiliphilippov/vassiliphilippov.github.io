Voltaic

noun
a group of West African languages
9.9
Pronunciation: / voʊlˈteɪɪk / (AmE), / vɒlˈteɪɪk / (BrE)
a group of closely related Niger-Congo languages spoken mainly in southeastern Mali and northern Ghana

Examples

Voltaic languages group
Many linguists study the Voltaic languages to better understand Niger-Congo language structures.
She is fluent in a Voltaic language widely used in southeastern Mali.
The Voltaic family includes languages spoken by several ethnic groups in northern Ghana.
