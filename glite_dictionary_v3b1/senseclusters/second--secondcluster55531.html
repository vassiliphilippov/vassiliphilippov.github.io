second

noun
second gear in a vehicle's transmission
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənd / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd / (BrE)
the gear in a vehicle's transmission that provides the second lowest forward gear ratio, used for moderate speed or climbing hills

Examples

Second gear shift
She learned to change from first to second smoothly during her driving lessons.
The engine sounded better once he put it in second.
You'll need to shift into second before taking that steep curve.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: first - the lowest forward gear in a vehicle's transmission
  • degree higher: third - the gear in a vehicle's transmission above second
