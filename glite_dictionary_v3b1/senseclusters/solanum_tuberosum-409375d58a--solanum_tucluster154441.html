Solanum tuberosum

noun
scientific name for potato
Pronunciation: / soʊˈleɪnəm tuːbəˈroʊsəm / (AmE), / səʊˈleɪnəm tjuːbəˈrəʊsəm / (BrE)
the scientific name for the potato, a South American plant cultivated widely for its edible starchy tubers
