bar

noun
horizontal rod for gymnastic exercises
1.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /bɑːr/ (AmE), /bɑː/ (BrE)
a long, rigid, and typically straight piece of metal, wood, or other material, or a block of solid material such as soap or chocolate

Examples

Iron bar
Children enjoy playing on the bar at the playground.
He injured his hand while practicing on the high bar.
The gymnast confidently swung around the bar during her routine.
He washed his hands with a new bar of soap.
She bought a bar of chocolate at the shop.
The candle was made by melting down a bar of wax.
She watched in disbelief as the ball clipped the bar and flew over.
The goalkeeper leaped but the ball struck the bar and was cleared.
The striker's shot bounced off the bar and stayed out of the goal.
