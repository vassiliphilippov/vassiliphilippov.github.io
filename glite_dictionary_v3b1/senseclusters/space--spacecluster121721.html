space

noun
area between musical staff lines
7.4
Pronunciation: / speɪs / (AmE), / speɪs / (BrE)
one of the areas between the lines of a musical staff, used to indicate specific musical notes

Examples

Musical space
Learning the names of the spaces helps you read sheet music more easily.
On the treble clef, each space represents a different note.
Please write the notes that correspond to each space on the staff.
