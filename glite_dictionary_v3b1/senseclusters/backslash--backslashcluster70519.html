backslash

noun
punctuation mark (\)
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌslæʃ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌslæʃ / (BrE)
the symbol (\) used in computing, programming, and mathematics, especially in file paths or to indicate special characters
