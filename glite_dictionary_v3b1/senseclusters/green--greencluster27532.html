green

adjective
looking pale and unhealthy
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
(of a person) looking pale and unhealthy, as if about to vomit or unwell.

Examples

Green face
After the long boat ride, she looked green and unsteady on her feet.
He turned green as soon as he smelled the spoiled milk.
You look a bit green—maybe you should sit down for a moment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pale - having little color in your face, usually because you are ill or frightened
  • synonym: sickly - having an unhealthy pale or yellowish complexion
