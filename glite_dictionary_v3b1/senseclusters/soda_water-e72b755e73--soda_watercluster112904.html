soda water

noun
sweet carbonated drink
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdə ˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
a clear, carbonated drink made by adding carbon dioxide to water, sometimes sweetened and flavored, sometimes plain (often used as a mixer or drink)

Examples

A glass of soda water
He ordered a glass of soda water to go with his meal.
Many cocktails use soda water as a mixer.
Would you like some soda water with your lunch?
He prefers soda water over sweetened soft drinks because it has no sugar.
Many cocktails call for a splash of soda water to add fizz.
She ordered a glass of soda water with lemon at the bar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: club soda - carbonated water to which minerals are added
  • synonym: soft drink - a non-alcoholic, carbonated beverage
  • synonym: sparkling water - carbonated water, usually without added minerals
