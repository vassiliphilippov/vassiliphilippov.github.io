tiebreaking

verb
to decide a tie
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
deciding a contest, vote, or competition by using a method to break a tie

Examples

To tiebreaking a match

In some games, a special rule is used for tiebreaking if the score is even.
