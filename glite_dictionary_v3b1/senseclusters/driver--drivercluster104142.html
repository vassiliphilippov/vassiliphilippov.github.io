driver

noun
golf club for long tee shots
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a golf club with a large, rounded head, used for hitting long-distance shots from the tee

Examples

Golf driver
He used his driver to hit the ball over 300 yards on the first hole.
Most golfers keep their driver in a special head cover to protect it.
The new titanium driver helps increase both distance and accuracy.
