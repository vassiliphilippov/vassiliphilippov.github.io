hurley

noun
hurling stick
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈhɜːli / (BrE)
a wooden stick with a flat end used to hit the ball in the Irish sport of hurling

Examples

A hurley stick
He bought a new hurley before the championship game.
The players lined up, each holding their hurley and helmet.
She learned how to swing a hurley at her local sports club.
