hiatal

adjective
relating to an opening
8.7
Pronunciation: / haɪˈeɪtəl / (AmE), / haɪˈeɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to a gap or opening, especially in the diaphragm

Examples

Hiatal hernia
The doctor explained that a hiatal hernia occurs when part of the stomach pushes through the diaphragm.
Surgery may be required to repair a hiatal defect in severe cases.
A hiatal opening can sometimes cause digestive problems if it becomes too large.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiatus - a gap or opening in a structure or process
