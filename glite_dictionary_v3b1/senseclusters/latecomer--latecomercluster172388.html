latecomer

noun
a person who arrives late
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtˌkʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪtˌkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who arrives after the expected or usual time

Examples

A latecomer arrives
As a latecomer, he missed the opening remarks of the presentation.
The meeting had already begun when the latecomer slipped quietly into the room.
They reserved seats in the back for any latecomer who might still arrive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: early bird - a person who arrives or gets up early; opposite of latecomer
