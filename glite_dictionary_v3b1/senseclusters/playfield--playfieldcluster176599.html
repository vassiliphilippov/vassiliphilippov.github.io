playfield

noun
game playing area
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌfiːld / (BrE)
the main surface or area where a game is played, especially in pinball or arcade machines

Examples

Pinball playfield
The ball shot across the playfield and hit the bumper with a loud sound.
Players must keep the puck within the playfield to continue the game.
The lights on the playfield made the pinball machine look exciting and colorful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: court - an area marked out for sports like tennis or basketball
  • synonym: playing field - an area where a sport or game is played
