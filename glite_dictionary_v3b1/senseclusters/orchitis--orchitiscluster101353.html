orchitis

noun
inflammation of the testicles
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈkaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɔːˈkaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of one or both testicles, typically causing pain and swelling

Examples

Orchitis causes pain
Mumps can sometimes lead to complications such as orchitis.
The doctor diagnosed him with orchitis after he complained of pain and swelling.
Treatment for orchitis often involves rest, ice packs, and medication to reduce pain.
