individuate

verb
to make distinct or individual
8.6 6.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdɪ.vɪ.dʒuː.eɪt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdɪ.vɪd.juː.eɪt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdɪ.vɪ.dʒuː.eɪt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdɪ.vɪd.juː.eɪt/ (BrE)
to give something a unique or individual character; to make it distinct from others

Examples
