pro-choice faction

noun
group supporting abortion rights
Pronunciation: / proʊ ˈtʃɔɪs ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / prəʊ ˈtʃɔɪs ˈfækʃən / (BrE)
a group of people who support the right of women to choose whether to have an abortion

Examples

The pro-choice faction
After the rally, members of the pro-choice faction were interviewed by reporters.
Political parties often consult with the pro-choice faction to shape their policies on reproductive rights.
The pro-choice faction was vocal during the debate over new abortion laws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pro-choice - supporting a woman's right to choose abortion
  • antonym: pro-life faction - a group opposing abortion and supporting the right to life of the unborn
