reactiveness

noun
quick response quality
Pronunciation: / riˈæktɪvnəs / (AmE), / riˈæktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of responding quickly or easily to stimuli, events, or situations

Examples

High reactiveness to change
Her reactiveness to sudden changes made her an excellent emergency responder.
The reactiveness of the new software allows it to adapt quickly to user input.
Too much reactiveness in a discussion can lead to misunderstandings and conflict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passivity - lack of response or action
  • synonym: responsiveness - the ability to react quickly and positively
