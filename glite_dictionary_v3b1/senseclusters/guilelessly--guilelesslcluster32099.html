guilelessly

adverb
innocently, without deceit
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪl.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪl.ləs.li / (BrE)
in a manner that shows innocence and a lack of deceit or cunning

Examples

To answer guilelessly
He answered the difficult question guilelessly, without trying to hide anything.
The child looked guilelessly at the teacher, not understanding the accusation.
They guilelessly accepted the offer, believing everyone had good intentions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deceitfully - in a way that is dishonest or intended to mislead
  • synonym: innocently - in a way that shows innocence or lack of guilt
  • synonym: sincerely - in a genuine and honest way
