brick-making machine

noun
industrial machine for making bricks
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪk ˌmeɪkɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈbrɪk ˌmeɪkɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine designed for manufacturing bricks from clay or other materials, typically used in industrial production.
