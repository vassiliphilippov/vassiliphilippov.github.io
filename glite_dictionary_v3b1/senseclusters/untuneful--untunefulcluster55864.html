untuneful

adjective
not having a pleasant musical sound
5.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàtunf…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàtjuÀênf äl / (BrE)
not having a pleasant or musical sound; lacking a pleasing tune or melody.

Examples

An untuneful sound
He found the clattering of the old machine harsh and untuneful.
Her attempt at the piano was awkward and distinctly untuneful.
The choir's untuneful singing disappointed the audience.
