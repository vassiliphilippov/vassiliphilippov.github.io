own it

verb
accept and take responsibility (idiom)
2.9
Pronunciation: / oʊn ɪt / (AmE), / əʊn ɪt / (BrE)
to accept and take full responsibility for oneself, one's actions, or one's characteristics, often with confidence or pride

Examples

To own it confidently
If you make a mistake, the best thing you can do is to own it and learn from it.
She decided to own it instead of hiding her quirky sense of humor.
When faced with criticism, he chose to own it and grow as a person.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deny - to refuse to accept responsibility or admit something
  • synonym: take responsibility - to accept duty or blame for something
