restatement

noun
expression of something in a different way
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
the act of expressing something again in a different or revised way

Examples

A restatement of position
After feedback, the lawyer submitted a restatement of her position.
His restatement of the argument made it easier to understand.
The professor provided a restatement of the theory to clarify its meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paraphrase - a rewording of something written or spoken by someone else
