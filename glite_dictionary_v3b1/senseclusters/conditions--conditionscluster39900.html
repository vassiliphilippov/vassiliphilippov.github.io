Synonyms

  • weather
    1.0 70/m
    weather describes the actual state of the atmosphere now or today, while conditions refers generally to those atmospheric states.
  • weather condition
    1.9 1/m
    weather condition refers to one specific moment's weather, while conditions names the general atmospheric state used in reports.
  • atmospheric condition
    3.4 0.2/m
    atmospheric condition is a technical phrase about air variables at a place and time, while conditions is the broader everyday term.
  • meteorological conditions
    5.2 0.02/m
    meteorological conditions is a formal term used in forecasts and studies, while conditions is more common in everyday weather talk.
  • atmospheric state
    5.8 0.00/m
