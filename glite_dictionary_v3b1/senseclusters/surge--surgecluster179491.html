surge

noun
a sudden, powerful increase
4.2 8.18/m
Pronunciation: / s…ùÀêd í / (AmE), / s…úÀêd í / (BrE)
a sudden and powerful increase in amount, number, or intensity

Examples

A surge in cases
Hospitals are preparing for a possible surge in flu cases during the winter months.
The team experienced a surge of excitement after scoring the winning goal.
There has been a surge in demand for electric vehicles this year.
