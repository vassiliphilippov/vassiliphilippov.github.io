living room suite

noun
set of living room furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ruːm swit / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ruːm swiːt / (BrE)
a set of matching furniture intended for use in a living room, such as sofas and chairs

Examples

Their living room suite includes a comfortable couch and matching coffee table.
We just bought a new living room suite with a sofa and two armchairs.
