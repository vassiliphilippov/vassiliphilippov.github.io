apt

noun
apartment abbreviation
Pronunciation: / æpt / (AmE), / æpt / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for apartment, used especially in addresses or listings.

Examples

Apt 2B
The listing says there are three apt units available on the top floor.
Please send your application to 45 Main St., apt 2B.
They moved into a new apt downtown last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apartment - a set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor of a building
