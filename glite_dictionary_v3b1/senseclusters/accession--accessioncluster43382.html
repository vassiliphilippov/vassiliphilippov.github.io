accession

noun
attaining a position, especially the throne
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / əkˈsɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
the act of attaining a position of power or office, especially the throne

Examples

Royal accession
Queen Victoria's accession was met with public celebration across Britain.
The treaty detailed the terms for the country's accession to the international alliance.
Upon his father's death, the prince's accession to the throne was announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abdication - the act of formally relinquishing a position or office
  • synonym: succession - the sequence or process of inheriting a position, especially the throne
