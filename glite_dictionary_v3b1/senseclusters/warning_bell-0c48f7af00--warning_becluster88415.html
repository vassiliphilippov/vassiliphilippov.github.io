warning bell

noun
a bell used to signal danger or warning
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrnɪŋ bɛl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ bɛl / (BrE)
a bell that is rung to give an alarm of danger or to signal a warning

Examples

Fire warning bell
A warning bell is installed in the tower to alert the village of a fire.
The sudden blare of the warning bell sent everyone rushing to the emergency exit.
When the warning bell sounded, workers evacuated the building immediately.
