vehicle

noun
substance that serves as a medium for another
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A substance used as a medium to convey or distribute a drug, pigment, or other material with which it is mixed.

Examples

Oil vehicle in cream
In this ointment, an oil-based vehicle is used to carry the medication into the skin.
The choice of vehicle can affect how evenly the pigment spreads on the surface.
Water often serves as the vehicle for dyes in solutions used for scientific experiments.
