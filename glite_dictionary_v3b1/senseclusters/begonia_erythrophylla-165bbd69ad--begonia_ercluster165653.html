Begonia erythrophylla

noun
species of begonia with red-tinged leaves
8.7
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡoʊniə ˌɛrɪθroʊˈfɪlə / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə ˌɛrɪθrəˈfɪlə / (BrE)
A species of rhizomatous begonia with round, fleshy leaves that are reddish underneath, often grown as a houseplant.
