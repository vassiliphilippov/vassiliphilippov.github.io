multivitamin

noun
a supplement containing multiple vitamins
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈvaɪtəmɪn / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈvɪtəmɪn / (BrE)
a pill, tablet, or supplement containing several different vitamins, often taken to improve general health or fill dietary gaps

Examples

A multivitamin pill
I take a multivitamin every morning to help ensure I get enough nutrients.
Some people prefer to get their vitamins from food rather than a multivitamin.
The doctor recommended adding a daily multivitamin to her diet.
