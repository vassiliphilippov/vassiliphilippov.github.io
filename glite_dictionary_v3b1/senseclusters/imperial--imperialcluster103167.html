imperial

adjective
relating to the British system of measurements
2.7 4.9
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈpɪr.iː.əl/ /; / /ɪm.ˈpɪr.ɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈpɪə.riː.əl/ (BrE)
relating to or using the British system of weights and measures, as opposed to metric.

Examples

Imperial measurements
British road signs often use imperial distances such as miles and yards.
She is more familiar with imperial weights, like pounds and ounces, than with kilograms.
The recipe calls for measurements in imperial units rather than metric.
