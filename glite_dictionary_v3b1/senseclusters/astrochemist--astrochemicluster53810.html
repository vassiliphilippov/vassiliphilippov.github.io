astrochemist

noun
space chemistry scientist
Pronunciation: / ˈæstroʊˌkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ˈæstrəʊˌkɛmɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the chemical composition and processes of space and celestial bodies

Examples

An astrochemist studies space
The astrochemist used advanced telescopes to detect molecules in distant galaxies.
As an astrochemist, she investigates how organic compounds form in space.
A team led by an astrochemist discovered new evidence of water on a comet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronomer - a scientist who studies stars, planets, and other celestial bodies
  • synonym: chemist - a scientist who studies the properties and reactions of substances
