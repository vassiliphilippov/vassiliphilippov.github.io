SPL

noun
sound loudness measure
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɛs piː ɛl / (AmE), / ɛs piː ɛl / (BrE)
an abbreviation for sound pressure level, a measurement of the loudness of a sound in decibels (dB)

Examples

SPL reading in decibels
The technician measured the SPL in the concert hall to ensure it was within safe limits.
High SPL levels can cause hearing damage if you are exposed for too long.
The new headphones can handle a higher SPL without distorting the sound.
