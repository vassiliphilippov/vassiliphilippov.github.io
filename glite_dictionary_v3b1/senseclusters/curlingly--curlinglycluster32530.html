curlingly

adverb
in a curling manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːrlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkɜːlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that curls, twists, or forms into curves or spirals

Examples

Flows curlingly through
The river flowed curlingly through the valley, creating beautiful bends along its path.
Her hair fell curlingly over her shoulders after she removed the braids.
Mist drifted curlingly across the field in the early morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirally - in a spiral or winding manner
  • antonym: straight - in a direct or uncurved manner
