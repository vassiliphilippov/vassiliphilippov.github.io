speakership

noun
the role of Speaker in a legislative body
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːkərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈspiːkəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or office of Speaker, especially of a legislative assembly

Examples

The speakership of House
After the election, he contested for the speakership of the House of Representatives.
Her speakership was marked by procedural reforms and greater transparency.
The speakership changed hands after a heated political debate.
