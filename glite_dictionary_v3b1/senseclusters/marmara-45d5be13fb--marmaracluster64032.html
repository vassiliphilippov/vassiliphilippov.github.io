Marmara

proper noun
inland sea in northwestern Turkey
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrmərə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːmərə / (BrE)
An inland sea in northwestern Turkey, connecting the Black Sea to the Aegean Sea via the Bosporus and Dardanelles.

Examples

The Marmara Sea
An oil spill in the Marmara threatened the region's fisheries earlier this year.
Ferries cross the Marmara daily, linking Istanbul to towns on its southern shores.
The Sea of Marmara plays a crucial role in Turkey's maritime trade routes.
