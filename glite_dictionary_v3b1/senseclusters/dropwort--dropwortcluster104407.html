dropwort

noun
meadow-flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Filipendula or related genera, with small white or pink flowers, often found in meadows

Examples

Meadow dropwort
The dropwort added a delicate touch of white to the summer meadow.
Botanists studied the dropwort to learn more about its medicinal properties.
She picked a bouquet of dropwort and wildflowers for the kitchen table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meadowsweet - a flowering plant of the genus Filipendula, related to dropwort
