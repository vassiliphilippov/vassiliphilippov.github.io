envoi

noun
concluding stanza in some poems
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːn.vwɑː / (AmE), / ˈɒn.vwɑː / (BrE)
A short stanza at the end of a poem, especially in certain fixed poetic forms, used as a conclusion or dedication.

Examples

A short envoi
Her poem featured a brief envoi that summarized its central theme.
In traditional French verse forms, the envoi serves as both a conclusion and a dedication.
The poet ended the ballad with a delicate envoi addressed to the reader.
