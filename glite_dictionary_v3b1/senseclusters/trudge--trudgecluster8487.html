trudge

noun
a long, weary or difficult walk
9.9 6.3
Pronunciation: / trʌdʒ / (AmE), / trʌdʒ / (BrE)
A long and laborious walk, typically made with difficulty or weariness.

Examples

A long trudge
After several hours of their uphill trudge, the hikers finally reached the summit.
She was not looking forward to the long trudge through the snow to get home.
The daily trudge to the bus stop in the rain left him exhausted.

Related Terms
