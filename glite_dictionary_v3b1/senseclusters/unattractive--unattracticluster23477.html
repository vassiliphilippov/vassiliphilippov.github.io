unattractive

adjective
not pleasing in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (BrE)
not pleasing in appearance or to the senses; lacking beauty or charm

Examples

An unattractive face
She felt unattractive in the old dress, even though it was comfortable.
The new office building is quite unattractive, with its dull concrete walls and lack of windows.
The proposal was rejected because it seemed financially unattractive to potential investors.
Many people find processed foods unattractive because of their artificial appearance.
That grey dress is rather unattractive compared to her usual bright colors.
The house looked unattractive after years of neglect and needed repairs.

  • antonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • synonym: plain - not beautiful or attractive in appearance
