black rhinoceros

noun
an endangered African rhinoceros species
5.3
Pronunciation: / blæk raɪˈnɑːsərəs / (AmE), / blæk raɪˈnɒsərəs / (BrE)
a large African rhinoceros (Diceros bicornis), characterized by two horns on the snout and a pointed upper lip, now critically endangered

Examples

The black rhinoceros animal
Researchers are working to protect the habitat of the black rhinoceros across southern Africa.
The number of black rhinoceros in the wild has declined dramatically due to poaching.
Visiting the reserve, we were thrilled to catch a glimpse of a black rhinoceros grazing near a waterhole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: white rhinoceros - another species of African rhinoceros with a wide mouth
