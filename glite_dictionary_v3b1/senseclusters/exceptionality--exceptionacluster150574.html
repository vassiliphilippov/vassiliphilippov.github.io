exceptionality

noun
being outstanding or unusual
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌsɛpʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ɪkˌsɛpʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)

Her exceptionality was evident in the way she solved complex problems with ease.
