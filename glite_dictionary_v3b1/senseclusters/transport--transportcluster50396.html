transport

noun
mechanism moving tape in audio devices
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtrænspɔːt / (BrE)
the mechanism in a tape recorder or playback device that moves the magnetic tape across the read/write heads

Examples

Tape transport mechanism
A faulty transport can cause uneven playback or damage to the magnetic tape.
Some high-end recorders have a precision transport system for superior sound quality.
The technician adjusted the transport to ensure the tape moved smoothly across the heads.
