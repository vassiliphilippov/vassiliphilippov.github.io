familiarity

noun
casual or informal manner
Pronunciation: / fəˌmɪlˈjæɾɪti /; / fəˌmɪl.jəˈrɛt.i / (AmE), / fəˌmɪlˈjærɪti /; / fəˌmɪl.iˈær.ə.ti / (BrE)
a relaxed, informal, or close way of behaving with others; a warm friendship or intimacy

Examples

Friendly familiarity
His familiarity with the staff sometimes made new employees uncomfortable.
She approached the meeting with surprising familiarity, greeting everyone by their first names.
There's a danger in too much familiarity when dealing with clients.
Despite the years apart, their familiarity was instantly rekindled when they met again.
The familiarity between the old friends was evident in their warm laughter and shared stories.
Their longstanding familiarity allowed them to speak openly and honestly about anything.

Related Terms

  • antonym: estrangement - the fact of no longer being on friendly terms or part of a social group
  • antonym: formality - strict adherence to established rules or conventions
  • synonym: informality - the quality of being relaxed and friendly without strict rules of behavior
  • synonym: intimacy - close familiarity or friendship; closeness
