immature

adjective
showing lack of maturity
Pronunciation: / ˌɪməˈtʃʊr / (AmE), / ˌɪməˈtʃʊə / (BrE)
behaving in a way that shows a lack of emotional or mental maturity; not fully developed in attitude or behavior

Examples

Immature attitude
His immature behavior embarrassed everyone at the meeting.
Many immature comments were made during the heated discussion.
She realizes now that her actions were immature and regrets them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childish - typical of a child; immature
  • antonym: mature - showing the mental, emotional, or physical qualities of an adult
