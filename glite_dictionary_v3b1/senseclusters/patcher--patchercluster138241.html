patcher

noun
signal routing technician
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃər / (AmE), / ˈpætʃə / (BrE)
a person who connects or routes electronic or audio signals using patch cables or a patch bay, especially in studios or technical settings

Examples

Studio patcher person
The patcher made sure all the microphones were connected before the concert started.
During the live broadcast, the patcher quickly fixed a problem with the audio feed.
In the recording studio, the patcher is responsible for organizing and connecting all the cables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs or manages technical systems
  • synonym: technician - a person skilled in the technical details of a subject
