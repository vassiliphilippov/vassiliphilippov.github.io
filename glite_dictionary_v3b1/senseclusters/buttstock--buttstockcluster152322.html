buttstock

noun
rear part of gun
8.7 0.06/m

the rear part of a firearm that rests against the shooter's shoulder to provide support and stability

Examples

The buttstock of rifle
He replaced the buttstock on his rifle for better comfort.
The wooden buttstock was polished and fit snugly against her shoulder.
A damaged buttstock can make aiming a firearm much more difficult.

Synonyms

  • rifle butt
    6.8 0.11/m
