average

verb
calculate the average value
Pronunciation: / ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
to calculate or have an average value over a set of numbers or a period of time

Examples

To average the results
If you average all your test scores, you'll see you are doing quite well.
Scientists averaged the temperatures recorded across the region to estimate the overall climate change.
The company averaged the monthly sales data to determine the yearly growth.
Her test scores averaged higher than anyone else's in the class.
On busy weeks, my hours average nearly fifty.
The costs average about $20 per person each month.
Despite some high and low scores, he averaged a B in his math class.
She averages seven hours of sleep each night.
The team averaged twenty points per game last season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calculate - to determine something mathematically
  • synonym: mean - a number that is the average value of a set of numbers
