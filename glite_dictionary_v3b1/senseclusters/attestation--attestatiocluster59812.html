attestation

noun
the act of bearing witness
5.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /æt.ɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.tɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /æt.ɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.tɪ.ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of formally providing evidence or proof that something is true or authentic

Examples

Official attestation
His signature provided official attestation to the accuracy of the report.
Historical documents often include an attestation to confirm their authenticity.
The contract required the attestation of two witnesses before it could be finalized.
His signature on the document is an attestation to its authenticity.
The certificate serves as an attestation of your membership in the organization.
The court required an attestation from a third-party witness.

Related Terms
