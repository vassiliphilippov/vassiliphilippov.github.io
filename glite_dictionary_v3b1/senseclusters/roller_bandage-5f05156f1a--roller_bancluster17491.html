roller bandage

noun
a strip of rolled fabric used as a bandage
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
a bandage made of a strip of sterile fabric rolled into a cylinder, used to cover wounds or support injured body parts

Examples

A roller bandage wrap
A roller bandage is ideal for wrapping sprained ankles because it provides uniform pressure.
He learned how to apply a roller bandage during his first aid training course.
The nurse secured the wound with a roller bandage to ensure it stayed clean and protected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to bind a wound or protect an injured part of the body
