roller bandage

noun
a strip of rolled fabric used as a bandage
3.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.lər ˈbæn.dɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.lə ˈbæn.dɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a bandage made of a strip of sterile fabric rolled into a cylinder, used to cover wounds or support injured body parts
