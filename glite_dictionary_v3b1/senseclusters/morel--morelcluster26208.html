morel

noun
an edible fungus with a honeycomb-like cap
Pronunciation: / məˈrɛl / (AmE), / məˈrɛl / (BrE)
an edible mushroom of the genus Morchella, having a brown, honeycomb-like spongy cap

Examples

A morel mushroom
Because of their rich flavor, morels are highly prized by gourmet cooks.
Hiking in the spring, they were excited to spot a cluster of morels under an old tree.
The chef added sliced morel mushrooms to the creamy risotto.
