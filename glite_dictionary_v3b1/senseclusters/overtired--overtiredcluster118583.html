overtired

verb
to exhaust completely
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtaɪərd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
to have caused someone to become extremely tired or exhausted by too much activity or effort.

Examples

To overtire yourself
She had overtired herself by working two full shifts without a break.
The coach warned that the players might become overtired if they trained too hard before the match.
After a long day of travel, he realized he had overtired his children with too many activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhaust - to make someone very tired
  • antonym: refresh - to make someone feel less tired
