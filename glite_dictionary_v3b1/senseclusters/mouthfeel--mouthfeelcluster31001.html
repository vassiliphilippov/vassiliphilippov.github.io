mouthfeel

noun
texture sensation
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊθˌfiːl / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθˌfiːl / (BrE)
the way food or drink feels in your mouth when you eat or drink it

Examples

Smooth mouthfeel
The chocolate's smooth mouthfeel made it especially enjoyable to eat.
Some people prefer a crunchy mouthfeel in their peanut butter.
The wine's silky mouthfeel impressed the judges at the tasting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: taste - the flavor of something as perceived in the mouth
  • synonym: texture - the feel or appearance of a surface or substance
