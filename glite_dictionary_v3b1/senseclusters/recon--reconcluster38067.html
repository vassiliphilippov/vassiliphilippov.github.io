recon

verb
to scout an area
7.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêk…ën / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêk…ín / (BrE)
to inspect or survey an area in order to gather information, especially for military or strategic purposes

Examples

To recon an area
The team will recon the abandoned building before the operation begins.
We need to recon the trail ahead to avoid any surprises.
Before sunrise, the soldiers quietly reconned the enemy's position.

Synonyms

    9.2 0.33/m
