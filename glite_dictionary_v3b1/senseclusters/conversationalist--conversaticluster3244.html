conversationalist

noun
a person skilled at conversation
4.5 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.vərˈseɪ.ʃən.əlɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən.əlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in holding conversations, especially one who talks in an interesting and engaging way

Examples

A skilled conversationalist

Despite being a brilliant scientist, he was not much of a conversationalist.
He is such a gifted conversationalist that parties are never dull when he’s around.
