simplex machine

noun
textile machine for making roving
8.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…™m.pl…õks m…ôÀà Éin / (AmE), / Ààs…™m.pleks m…ôÀà ÉiÀên / (BrE)
a textile machine that converts drawn sliver into roving by giving it a slight twist to provide strength before spinning

Examples

The simplex machine twists fibers
After passing through the simplex machine, the fibers acquire extra strength from the added twist.
Modern textile mills still use the simplex machine in the roving process before the yarn is spun.
The simplex machine prepares fibers for spinning by twisting and thinning the material.
