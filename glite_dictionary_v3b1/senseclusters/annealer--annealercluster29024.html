annealer

noun
simulated annealing tool
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈniːlər / (AmE), / əˈniːlə / (BrE)
a program or device that uses simulated annealing to find approximate solutions to complex optimization problems

Examples

Quantum annealer
The engineers used an annealer to optimize the layout of the computer chip.
A quantum annealer can solve certain problems faster than classical computers.
The software package includes a powerful annealer for complex scheduling tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimizer - a tool or algorithm that improves a solution to a problem
  • synonym: solver - a program or algorithm that finds solutions to mathematical problems
