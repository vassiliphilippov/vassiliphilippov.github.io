conversable

adjective
knowledgeable about topic
8.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrsəbəl / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːsəbəl / (BrE)
familiar with or knowledgeable about a particular subject, especially enough to discuss it intelligently

Examples

Conversable with history
She is conversable with modern art and can discuss many famous painters.
Although not an expert, he is quite conversable in the basics of computer programming.
The professor expects students to be conversable with the main theories before the seminar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about a specific subject
  • synonym: versed - experienced or skilled in a particular area
