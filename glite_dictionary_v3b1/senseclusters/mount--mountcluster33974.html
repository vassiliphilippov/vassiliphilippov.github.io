mount

verb
organize and begin (e.g. a campaign)
Pronunciation: / maʊnt / (AmE), / maʊnt / (BrE)
to organize and begin something, such as a campaign, event, or attack

Examples

To mount a campaign
Activists are working together to mount protests against pollution in the city.
After months of planning, the team finally mounted their ambitious fundraising event.
The government decided to mount a massive vaccination campaign to prevent the outbreak.
After months of planning, the team managed to mount a successful exhibition.
The company decided to mount an ambitious advertising campaign to launch its new product.
They are hoping to mount a charity concert next spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: initiate - to cause (a process or action) to begin
  • synonym: launch - to start or set in motion (an activity or enterprise)
  • synonym: organize - to arrange or coordinate the activities involved in something
