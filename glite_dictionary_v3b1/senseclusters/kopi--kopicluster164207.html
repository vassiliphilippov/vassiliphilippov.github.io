kopi

noun
Malay coffee drink
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊpi / (AmE), / ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
a traditional coffee drink commonly served in Southeast Asia (especially Malaysia, Indonesia, and Singapore), often sweetened and served with condensed milk
