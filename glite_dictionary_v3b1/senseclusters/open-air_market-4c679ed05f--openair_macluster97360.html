open-air market

noun
outdoor public market for goods and food
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpən ˈɛr ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈeə ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
a market held outdoors, where vendors sell food and other goods to the public

Examples
