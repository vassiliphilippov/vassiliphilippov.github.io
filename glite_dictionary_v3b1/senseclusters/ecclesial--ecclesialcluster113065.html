ecclesial

adjective
of the church
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkliːziəl / (AmE), / ɪˈkliːzɪəl / (BrE)
relating to a church or its functions, organization, or activities

Examples

An ecclesial meeting
The new ecclesial building was designed to host community gatherings and worship services.
She studied the ecclesial traditions that shaped the church's annual celebrations.
Many ecclesial leaders attended the conference to discuss reforms in church governance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecclesiastical - relating to the Christian Church or its clergy
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or church matters
