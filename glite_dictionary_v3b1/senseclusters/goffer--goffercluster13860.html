goffer

noun
ornamental pleated frill or edging
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɒfər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒfə / (BrE)
an ornamental frill or border made by pressing fabric into pleats or ridges, often for decorative purposes

Examples

A lace goffer
Her Victorian blouse was adorned with a delicate white goffer around the neckline.
Old-fashioned bonnets often featured a wide goffer as decoration.
The maid carefully ironed each goffer on the dress’s collar to make it crisp.
