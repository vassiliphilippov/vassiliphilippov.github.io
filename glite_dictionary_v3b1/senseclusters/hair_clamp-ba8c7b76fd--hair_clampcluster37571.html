hair clamp

noun
spring-loaded hair clip with interlocking teeth
Pronunciation: / hɛr klæmp / (AmE), / heə klamp / (BrE)
a type of hair clip featuring interlocking teeth and a spring mechanism, used to hold hair securely in place
