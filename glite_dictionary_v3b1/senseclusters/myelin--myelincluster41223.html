myelin

noun
fatty sheath around nerve fibers
6.2 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.ə.lɪn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.əl.ɪn/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.ə.lɪn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.əl.ɪn/ (BrE)
a white, fatty substance that forms a protective sheath around the axons of some nerve fibers, aiding in the transmission of nerve impulses

Examples
