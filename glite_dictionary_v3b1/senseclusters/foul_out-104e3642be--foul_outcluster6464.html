foul out

verb
baseball: be put out by foul ball catch
Pronunciation: / faʊl aʊt / (AmE), / faʊl aʊt / (BrE)
(Baseball) to be put out by hitting a ball that is caught by a fielder while still in foul territory.

Examples

To foul out in baseball
He was disappointed to foul out after a long at-bat.
Several players have fouled out during this tight game.
The batter fouled out to first base in the sixth inning.
