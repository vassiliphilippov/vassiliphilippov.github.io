posthypnotic

noun
suggestion after hypnosis
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊsthɪpˈnɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpəʊsthɪpˈnɒtɪk / (BrE)
a suggestion or instruction given during hypnosis that is intended to affect behavior after the hypnotic state ends

Examples

Posthypnotic suggestion
The therapist gave a posthypnotic to help the patient quit smoking after the session.
Researchers studied how effective a posthypnotic could be in changing eating habits.
After waking, she followed the posthypnotic without remembering the original suggestion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypnosis - a trance-like state of focused attention and increased suggestibility
  • synonym: suggestion - an idea or plan put forward for consideration
