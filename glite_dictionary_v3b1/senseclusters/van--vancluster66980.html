van

noun
vehicle with living quarters; camper
Pronunciation: / væn / (AmE), / væn / (BrE)
A vehicle, often modified or specially equipped, that contains living quarters and is used for camping or traveling.

Examples

Camper van
The couple traveled across Europe in their comfortable van, stopping wherever they pleased.
They converted their old van into a cozy camper for summer road trips.
This van sleeps four and comes with a small kitchenette and shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campervan - a van equipped as a mobile home
  • synonym: motorhome - a large vehicle designed for living in while traveling
