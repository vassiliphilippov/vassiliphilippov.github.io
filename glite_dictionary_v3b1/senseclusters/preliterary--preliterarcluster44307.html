preliterary

adjective
before written literature
9.1
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈlɪt.ər.iː/ /; / /prɪ.ˈlɪt.ər.iː/ (AmE), /priː.ˈlɪt.ə.riː/ /; / /prɪ.ˈlɪt.ə.riː/ (BrE)
existing or occurring before the development of written literature or writing systems

Examples

Preliterary oral traditions
Many ancient stories were preserved through preliterary oral traditions before writing was invented.
Archaeologists study preliterary societies to understand how culture was transmitted without written records.
The epic was first told in a preliterary age, long before it was written down.
