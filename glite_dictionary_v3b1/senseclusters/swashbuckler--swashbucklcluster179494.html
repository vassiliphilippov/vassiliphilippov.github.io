swashbuckler

noun
adventurous, daring person
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑʃˌbʌk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈswɒʃˌbʌk.lə / (BrE)
a person who is adventurous, daring, and sometimes reckless, especially one who engages in daring exploits

Examples

A swashbuckler hero
As a child, he dreamed of sailing the seas as a swashbuckler fighting for justice.
Stories of pirates and swashbucklers have always fascinated adventure lovers.
The old film features a fearless swashbuckler who seeks out trouble wherever he goes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurer - a person who seeks excitement and adventure
  • antonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • degree higher: hero - a person admired for brave acts or noble character
