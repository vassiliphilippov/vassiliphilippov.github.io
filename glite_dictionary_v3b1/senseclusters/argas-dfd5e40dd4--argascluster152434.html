Argas

noun
genus of soft ticks
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡæs / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡæs / (BrE)
a genus of soft-bodied ticks that parasitize birds and mammals

Examples

Argas tick species
The scientist identified the specimen as an Argas collected from a bird's nest.
Some species of Argas can transmit diseases to poultry and other animals.
Veterinarians are trained to recognize infestations caused by Argas ticks in livestock.
