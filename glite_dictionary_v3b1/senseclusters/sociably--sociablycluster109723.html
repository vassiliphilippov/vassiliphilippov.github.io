sociably

adverb
in a sociable manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəbli /; / ˈsoʊ.ʃəb.li / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəbli /; / ˈsəʊ.ʃəb.li / (BrE)
in a friendly and sociable way; enjoying being with other people

Examples

To act sociably
He always behaves sociably at work events, joining group conversations and activities.
She greeted everyone sociably at the dinner party, making guests feel welcome.
The new neighbors sociably invited everyone over for coffee on their first weekend.
He always behaves sociably at parties, chatting with guests he barely knows.
She greeted everyone sociably, making newcomers feel at ease right away.
The group mingled sociably at the networking event, exchanging ideas and stories.

  • antonym: unsociably - in an unsociable manner; not wanting to interact with others
