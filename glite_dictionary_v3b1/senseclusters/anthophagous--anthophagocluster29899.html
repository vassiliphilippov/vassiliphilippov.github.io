anthophagous

adjective
feeding on flowers
6.1
Pronunciation: / ænˈθɑfəɡəs / (AmE), / anˈθɒfəɡəs / (BrE)
(of an organism) feeding on flowers.

Examples

Anthophagous beetles
Many beetles are anthophagous, causing damage to ornamental gardens.
Scientists studied the anthophagous larvae found on the rare wildflower.
The anthophagous habits of this moth make it a pest for sunflower farmers.
