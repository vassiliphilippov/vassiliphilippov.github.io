glistening

adjective
shining with reflected light
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪsənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪs.ən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
shining with reflected light, typically in a way that is bright and often wet or smooth

Examples

The glistening leaves
After the storm, the glistening leaves sparkled in the sunlight.
He admired the glistening surface of the freshly waxed car.
Snow covered the meadow in a glistening white blanket.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or reflectiveness
  • synonym: shiny - reflecting light; bright with a smooth surface
