legalistically

adverb
in a strict legal way
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌliːɡəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌliːɡəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that strictly follows the exact wording or rules of the law, often without considering the broader meaning or intent

Examples

Applied legalistically strictly
The policy was applied legalistically, leaving no room for exceptions or special cases.
She argued legalistically rather than considering the spirit of the agreement.
The manager handled the complaint legalistically, focusing only on the written rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formally - in a way that follows official rules or procedures
