designed

adjective
planned or intended for a purpose
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnd / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnd / (BrE)
planned or created for a particular purpose or intention; not accidental or random.

Examples

Carefully designed plan
His response seemed carefully designed to avoid answering the question directly.
The museum has a designed layout to guide visitors through each exhibit.
These chairs were designed for comfort as well as style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidental - happening by chance; not planned
  • synonym: intentional - done deliberately; planned or intended
