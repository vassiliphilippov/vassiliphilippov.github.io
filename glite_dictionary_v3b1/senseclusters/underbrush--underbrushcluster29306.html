underbrush

noun
vegetation beneath taller trees
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
Low-growing plants, bushes, and small trees that grow beneath taller trees in a forest or wood.

Examples

Dense underbrush in forest
Firefighters cleared the underbrush to prevent the spread of wildfires in the forest.
Many animals make their homes in the thick underbrush beneath the tall trees.
The hikers carefully made their way through the dense underbrush along the trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undergrowth - dense growth of plants under larger trees
