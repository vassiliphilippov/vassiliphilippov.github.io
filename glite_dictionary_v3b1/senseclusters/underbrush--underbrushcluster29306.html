Low-growing plants, bushes, and small trees that grow beneath taller trees in a forest or wood.

Examples

Dense underbrush in forest
Firefighters cleared the underbrush to prevent the spread of wildfires in the forest.
Many animals make their homes in the thick underbrush beneath the tall trees.
The hikers carefully made their way through the dense underbrush along the trail.

Synonyms

  • undergrowth
    3.9 1/m
    undergrowth is a dense, tangled growth of shrubs and small trees beneath taller trees, while underbrush names low plants below the canopy.
  • underwood
    8.0 1/m
    underwood describes the low trees and bushes growing beneath taller trees, while underbrush focuses on low shrubs and plants close to the ground.
  • understory
    5.1 0.7/m
    understory is the forest layer of small trees and shade plants beneath the canopy, while underbrush means the low shrubs and tangled plants on the forest floor.
  • brush
    2.2 8/m
    brush is a general dense growth of bushes or shrubs in many places, while underbrush specifically grows beneath taller forest trees.
