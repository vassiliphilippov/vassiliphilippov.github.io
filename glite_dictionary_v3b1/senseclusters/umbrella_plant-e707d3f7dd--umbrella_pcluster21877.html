umbrella plant

noun
ornamental water plant with umbrella-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɹɛl.ə plænt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɹɛl.ə plɑːnt / (BrE)
An African aquatic plant grown for its umbrellalike cluster of slender, grass-like leaves, commonly used as an ornamental in gardens and ponds.

Examples

Aquatic umbrella plant
Leaves of the umbrella plant fan out in a striking circular display above the stems.
She bought an umbrella plant to decorate the corner of her living room.
The umbrella plant is often used to add a tropical feel to water gardens.
