rice paper

noun
thin paper-like material from rice-paper tree
9.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /raɪs ˈpeɪ.pər/ (AmE), /raɪs ˈpeɪ.pə/ (BrE)
a thin, delicate material resembling paper, made from the pith of the rice-paper tree and used for writing, artwork, or as wrapping

Examples

Rice paper for painting
He used rice paper for his watercolor paintings to achieve a delicate effect.
The candies were wrapped in thin sheets of rice paper that could be eaten.
Traditional calligraphy often makes use of high-quality rice paper.
