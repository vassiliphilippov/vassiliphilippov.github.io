disconformity

noun
lack of conformity
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskənˈfɔːrməti / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈfɔːməti / (BrE)
the state of not conforming to accepted rules, practices, or standards

Examples

Disconformity with rules
The minister's disconformity with certain rituals led to heated debate within the congregation.
Her disconformity to the company's dress code made her stand out among her colleagues.
Social disconformity can sometimes inspire positive change in a community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conformity - compliance with accepted rules or standards
  • synonym: nonconformity - failure or refusal to follow established customs or rules
