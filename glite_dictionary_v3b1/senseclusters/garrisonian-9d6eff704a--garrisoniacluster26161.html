Garrisonian

noun
supporter of Garrison
Pronunciation: / ˌɡærɪˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌɡærɪˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the views and policies of William Lloyd Garrison, especially immediate abolition of slavery

Examples

A Garrisonian supporter
As a proud Garrisonian, he spoke out against slavery at every opportunity.
The meeting was attended by several well-known Garrisonians who advocated for immediate emancipation.
Her views aligned closely with those of the Garrisonian movement in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abolitionist - a person who supports ending slavery
