heat

noun
preliminary race or round in a competition
7.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /hiːt/ (AmE), /hiːt/ (BrE)
a preliminary race in which the winner qualifies for a later and usually more important round of a competition

Examples

A race heat
Each runner must compete in a preliminary heat to earn a place in the finals.
She qualified for the finals by winning her heat earlier in the day.
There are three heats before the main race to decide who advances.
