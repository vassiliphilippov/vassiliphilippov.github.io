electric shaver

noun
razor powered by electricity
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈʃeɪvər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈʃeɪvə / (BrE)
A device for shaving hair, especially from the face, that is powered by an electric motor rather than being operated manually.

Examples

An electric shaver device
An electric shaver is convenient for quick morning routines.
He bought a new electric shaver to replace his old razor.
My father prefers an electric shaver over a traditional blade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual razor - a traditional razor operated by hand without electricity
  • synonym: razor - an instrument with a sharp blade used for shaving hair
