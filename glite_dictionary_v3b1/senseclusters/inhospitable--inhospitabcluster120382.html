inhospitable

adjective
harsh and difficult to live in
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.hɑːˈspɪ.t̬ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.hɒˈspɪ.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not welcoming or difficult to live in or visit; harsh or unfriendly

Examples

An inhospitable environment
Few plants can survive the inhospitable environment of the Arctic tundra.
The abandoned house stood in an inhospitable stretch of land far from civilization.
The climbers faced inhospitable weather conditions at the summit.
His inhospitable manner made guests feel unwelcome at the party.
The Arctic tundra is one of the most inhospitable regions on Earth.
The abandoned house had an inhospitable atmosphere that kept people away.
