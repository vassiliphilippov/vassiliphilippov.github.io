talentlessness

noun
lack of talent
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtæləntləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtaləntləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no natural ability or talent in a particular area

Examples

His talentlessness in music
He worried that his talentlessness would hold him back in the competitive music industry.
Her talentlessness did not stop her from pursuing her passion for painting.
The harsh review criticized the movie for its overall talentlessness and lack of creativity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: talent - natural ability or skill
  • synonym: talentless - having no talent or natural ability
