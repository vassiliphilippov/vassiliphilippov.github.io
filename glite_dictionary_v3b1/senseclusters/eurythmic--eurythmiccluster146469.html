eurythmic

adjective
about musical movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / juˈrɪðmɪk / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɪðmɪk / (BrE)
relating to eurhythmics, a method of teaching musical rhythm and expression through coordinated bodily movement.

Examples

Eurythmic exercises
The children enjoyed the eurythmic exercises that helped them feel the beat of the music.
A eurythmic approach can make music lessons more engaging for young students.
She developed a eurythmic routine to teach rhythm through movement in her dance class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhythmic - having or relating to rhythm
