seats

noun
places where several people can sit
1.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / siÀêts / (AmE), / siÀêts / (BrE)
an area or set of places where several people can sit, especially in a particular venue or room

Examples

Theater seats
The concert hall has 500 seats available for guests.
There are enough seats in the auditorium for the entire class.
We arrived early to make sure we got good seats at the cinema.

Synonyms

  • seating area
    1.4 0.55/m
    seating area is a part of a room with grouped chairs for people, while seats mean the individual places to sit.
  • seating
    1.0 5.61/m
    seating is an arranged row of chairs in a venue, while seats are the specific places people use to sit.
  • seating room
    2.3 0.01/m
    seating room is a whole room set up with chairs for a group, while seats are the individual spots people occupy.
