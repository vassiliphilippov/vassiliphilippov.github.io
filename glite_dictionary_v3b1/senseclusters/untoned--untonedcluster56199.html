untoned

adjective
lacking firmness
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊnd / (BrE)
lacking firmness, definition, or strength, especially in muscles or voice

Examples

Untoned muscles
After months without exercise, his arms were noticeably untoned.
The singer's untoned voice made the performance sound weak and unsteady.
She wanted to improve her untoned legs by starting a regular workout routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flabby - soft and lacking firmness, especially in muscles
  • antonym: toned - having firmness and definition, especially in muscles
