gotcha

interjection
expression of understanding
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑtʃə / (AmE), / ˈɡɒtʃə / (BrE)
used to show that you understand what someone has said or that you have caught someone in the act

Examples

Gotcha! Understood
When you said the meeting was at noon, I replied, "gotcha!"
He tried to sneak past, but I caught him and said, "gotcha!"
"Gotcha, I understand what you mean now," she said after the explanation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aha - an exclamation used when you suddenly understand something
  • synonym: understood - used to show comprehension or agreement
