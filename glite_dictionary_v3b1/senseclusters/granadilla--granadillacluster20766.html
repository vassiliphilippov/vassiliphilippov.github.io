granadilla

noun
Brazilian passionflower with purple fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræn.əˈdɪl.ə /; / ˌɡrænəˈdɪlə /; / ˌɡrænəˈdɪliə / (AmE), / ˌɡræn.əˈdɪl.ə /; / ˌɡranəˈdɪlə /; / ˌɡranəˈdɪlɪə / (BrE)
a tropical climbing plant of the passionflower family, or its sweet, edible fruit
