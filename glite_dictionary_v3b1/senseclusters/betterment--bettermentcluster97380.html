betterment

noun
an improvement increasing property value
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛtərmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɛtəmənt / (BrE)
an improvement that increases the value of a property or facility, especially one that is permanent or structural

Examples

Property betterment
A new roof is considered a betterment rather than just a repair.
Owners must pay a portion of the costs for any betterment made to the public facilities near their property.
The city funded betterment projects to revitalize the downtown area.
