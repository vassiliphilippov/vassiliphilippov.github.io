criticizer

noun
person who criticizes
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪˌsaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who expresses disapproval of or finds fault with someone or something

Examples

A harsh criticizer
The harshest criticizer of the plan eventually admitted it had some benefits.
Every artist faces at least one criticizer who doubts their work.
Instead of being a criticizer, try offering constructive feedback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who judges or evaluates something, often professionally
  • antonym: supporter - a person who approves of or defends someone or something
