clear-cut

adjective
sharply defined; easily recognizable
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪr ˌkʌt /; / ˈklɪrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˌklɪə ˈkʌt /; / ˈklɪəˌkʌt /; / ˈklɪə ˌkʌt / (BrE)
definite, clear, and easy to perceive, recognize, or understand; unambiguous
