simp

noun
foolish or gullible person
Pronunciation: / sɪmp / (AmE), / sɪmp / (BrE)
a foolish or gullible person who is easily deceived or lacks common sense, especially in a naive way

Examples

A foolish simp
He was called a simp for believing every rumor that spread through the town.
In old stories, the simp is often the character who falls for obvious tricks.
They warned him not to act like a simp and to think before trusting strangers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense or intelligence
  • antonym: wise - having or showing experience, knowledge, and good judgment
