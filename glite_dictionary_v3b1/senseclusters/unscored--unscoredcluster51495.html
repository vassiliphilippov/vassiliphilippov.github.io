unscored

adjective
not given a score
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɔrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɔːd / (BrE)
not having received a score or not marked with a score

Examples

Unscored exam papers
The exam papers were still unscored when the students left for the weekend.
Please submit any unscored assignments to the teacher for grading.
Several entries in the competition remained unscored due to a lack of judges.
