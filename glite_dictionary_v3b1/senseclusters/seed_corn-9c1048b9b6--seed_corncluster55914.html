seed corn

noun
corn seeds kept for planting
2.5
Pronunciation: / sid kɔrn / (AmE), / siːd kɔːn / (BrE)
corn kernels or seeds of high quality reserved specifically for planting the next crop, rather than for consumption or other uses.

Examples

Planting seed corn
After a poor harvest, they had to use most of their seed corn just to eat.
Farmers carefully select the best seed corn to ensure a healthy crop next season.
Saving enough seed corn is crucial for sustainable farming from year to year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seed grain - grain reserved for sowing rather than consumption
