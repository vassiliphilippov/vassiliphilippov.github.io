corner

noun
difficult or inescapable situation
6.2 8.92/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îrn…ôr / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀên…ô / (BrE)
a difficult or awkward situation from which escape is very hard or impossible

Examples

In a tough corner
After the mistake was discovered, she found herself in a corner with no clear way out.
His gambling debts put him in a financial corner he couldn't escape.
The politician was painted into a corner by his contradictory statements.

Synonyms

    6.0 0.98/m
