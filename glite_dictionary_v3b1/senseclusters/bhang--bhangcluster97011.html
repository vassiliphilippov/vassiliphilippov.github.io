bhang

noun
Indian cannabis preparation
5.2
Pronunciation: / bɑŋ / (AmE), / bɑːŋ / (BrE)
an edible preparation made from the leaves and flowers of the cannabis plant, traditionally consumed in India, especially during festivals

Examples

Traditional bhang drink
During Holi, many people in northern India drink bhang as part of the celebrations.
She learned to make traditional sweets using bhang as an ingredient.
The effects of bhang can vary depending on the potency and amount consumed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cannabis - the plant from which bhang is made
  • synonym: marijuana - dried leaves and flowers of the cannabis plant for recreational or medicinal use
