velvet bean

noun
climbing vine with hairy pods, used for green manure
7.2 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvət biːn / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt biːn / (BrE)
an annual climbing vine native to Asia, with long clusters of purplish flowers and densely hairy pods, often cultivated as green manure or for animal grazing

Examples
