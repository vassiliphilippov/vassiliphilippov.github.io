brachistochrone

noun
curve of fastest descent under gravity
8.6
Pronunciation: / brəˈkɪstəˌkroʊn / (AmE), / brəˈkɪstəkrəʊn / (BrE)
A curve between two points, down which a body moves under gravity in the shortest possible time.

Examples

The brachistochrone curve

The brachistochrone is fundamental to understanding problems of quickest descent in physics.
