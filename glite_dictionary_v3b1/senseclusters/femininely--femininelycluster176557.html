femininely

adverb
in a feminine way
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪnɪnli / (AmE), / fəˈmɪnɪnli / (BrE)
in a way that is characteristic of women or girls, often showing qualities considered feminine

Examples

To walk femininely
She walked femininely, with light steps and a gentle posture.
The actress dressed femininely for the award ceremony, choosing a flowing gown.
He decorated the room femininely, using soft colors and delicate patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gracefully - in an elegant and smooth way
  • antonym: masculinely - in a way that is characteristic of men or boys
