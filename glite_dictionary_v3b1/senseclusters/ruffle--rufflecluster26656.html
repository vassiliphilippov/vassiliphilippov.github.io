ruffle

noun
a high, tight decorative collar
5.8 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌf(ə)l /; / ˈrʌfəl / (BrE)
a strip or collar of gathered or pleated fabric used as a decorative frill on clothing or other textiles

Examples
