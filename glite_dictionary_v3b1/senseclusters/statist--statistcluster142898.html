statist

adjective
supporting state control
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtɪst / (BrE)
favoring or relating to strong government control over public, economic, or social affairs

Examples

Statist policies
The new law was criticized as a statist approach to economic regulation.
Some voters prefer statist policies that increase government involvement in healthcare.
His statist views often clash with those who support free-market solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian - favoring strict obedience to authority
  • antonym: libertarian - favoring minimal government intervention
