bacteriuria

noun
bacteria in urine
8.3
Pronunciation: /bæk.tə.ri.ˈjʊr.iː.ə/ /; / /bæk.tə.ri.ˈjuːr.iː.ə/ (AmE), /bæk.tə.ri.ˈʊə.riː.ə/ /; / /bæk.tə.ri.ˈjʊə.riː.ə/ (BrE)
the presence of bacteria in the urine, which may indicate a urinary tract infection

Examples

Bacteriuria in urine
Routine screening revealed bacteriuria in several patients at the clinic.
The presence of bacteriuria can be a sign of a urinary tract infection.
Asymptomatic bacteriuria does not always require antibiotic treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urinary tract infection - an infection in any part of the urinary system
