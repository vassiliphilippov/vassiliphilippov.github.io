dethaw

verb
make or become unfrozen
8.6
Pronunciation: / diˈθɔ / (AmE), / diˈθɔː / (BrE)
to cause (something frozen) to become soft or liquid, especially by warming; to thaw or defrost

Examples

To dethaw frozen food
I forgot to dethaw the frozen vegetables last night.
Please dethaw the chicken before you start cooking.
The instructions say to dethaw the pie at room temperature.
