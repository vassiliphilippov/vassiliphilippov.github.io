atmospherics

noun
background mood effects
Pronunciation: / ˌætməsˈfɛrɪks / (AmE), / ˌætməsˈfɛərɪks / (BrE)
background sounds or effects used to create a particular mood or feeling in a place, event, or media

Examples

Movie atmospherics
The director used subtle atmospherics to make the horror movie more suspenseful.
Soft music and lighting provided the perfect atmospherics for the romantic dinner.
Radio producers often add atmospherics to create a sense of place in their stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambience - the character or mood of a place or situation
  • synonym: soundscape - the overall sound environment in a place or recording
