procreativity

noun
ability to reproduce
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊkriːəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌprəʊkriːəˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to produce offspring or reproduce

Examples

Animal procreativity
The scientist studied the procreativity of different animal species in the wild.
Concerns about declining procreativity have led to new research in reproductive health.
Cultural beliefs often influence how societies view procreativity and family life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infertility - the inability to produce offspring
