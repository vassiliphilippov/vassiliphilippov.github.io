preproduction

noun
planning before production
4.5 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåpriÀêpr…ôÀàd åk É…ôn / (AmE), / ÀåpriÀêpr…ôÀàd åk É…ôn / (BrE)
the planning and preparation phase before the actual production of a project, such as a film or show

Examples

Preproduction planning phase
The team spent several weeks in preproduction to finalize the script and casting.
Good preproduction can save time and money during the filming process.
They are currently in preproduction for their new television series.

Synonyms

  • preplanning
    3.5 0.09/m
    preplanning is a general act of making plans before an event, while preproduction is the focused preparation phase before film or show production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: planning - the process of making plans for something
  • antonym: postproduction - the work done on a project after the main production, such as editing
