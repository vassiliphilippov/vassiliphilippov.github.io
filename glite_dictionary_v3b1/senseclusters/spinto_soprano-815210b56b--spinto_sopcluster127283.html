spinto soprano

noun
type of operatic soprano voice
6.8
Pronunciation: / spiːn.toʊ səˈprɑː.noʊ / (AmE), / ˈspiːntəʊ səˈprɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
An operatic soprano voice that combines the clear, high notes of a lyric soprano with the ability to sing with dramatic intensity without strain.

Examples

A spinto soprano voice
As a spinto soprano, she excels in roles that demand both lyrical beauty and dramatic power.
Her voice developed into a true spinto soprano, allowing her to sing both lyric and dramatic parts.
The company cast a spinto soprano for the challenging role in the opera.
