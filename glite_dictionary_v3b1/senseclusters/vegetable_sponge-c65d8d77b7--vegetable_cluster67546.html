vegetable sponge

noun
plant yielding fibrous sponge-like fruit
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒtəbl spʌndʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl spʌndʒ / (BrE)
a climbing plant of the gourd family whose cylindrical fruit, when mature, yields a fibrous material used as a sponge (especially the loofah)

Examples

The vegetable sponge fruit
After harvest, the ripe vegetable sponge is dried and used for bathing and cleaning.
Botanists classify the vegetable sponge as a member of the gourd family.
The vegetable sponge is commonly grown in warm climates for its fibrous fruit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loofah - the fibrous fruit of the vegetable sponge, used as a sponge
