taunter

noun
person who mocks
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔntər / (AmE), / ˈtɔːntə / (BrE)
a person who mocks or provokes others in a mean or teasing way

Examples

The taunter mocks others
The taunter kept making rude jokes at the expense of his classmates.
Ignoring the taunter was the best way to avoid getting upset.
During the game, one taunter tried to distract the opposing team with constant remarks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comforter - a person who gives comfort or support
  • synonym: mockery - the act of making fun of someone
  • synonym: teaser - a person who makes fun of or annoys others playfully
