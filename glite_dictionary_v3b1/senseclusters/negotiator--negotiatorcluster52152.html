negotiator

noun
automated software agent that negotiates
5.9
Pronunciation: /nə.ˈgoʊ.ʃiː.eɪ.tər/ /; / /ɪ.ˈgoʊ.ʃiː.eɪ.tər/ (AmE), /nə.ˈgəʊ.ʃiː.eɪ.tə/ /; / /ɪ.ˈgəʊ.ʃiː.eɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a software agent or automated system that negotiates terms, prices, or agreements on behalf of a user or organization, often using algorithms or AI.

Examples

AI negotiator
An AI negotiator can autonomously negotiate terms with suppliers, adapting to company preferences and limits.
The online marketplace included a built-in negotiator that bargained prices on behalf of buyers automatically.
Developers programmed the negotiator to respect budgets while seeking the best delivery schedules for clients.
