skyfall

noun
dramatic fall from sky
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌfɔːl / (BrE)
a sudden or dramatic event in which something falls from the sky, such as rain, meteors, or debris

Examples

A skyfall event
The meteor shower was described as a spectacular skyfall by the crowd.
After the explosion, pieces of debris created a dangerous skyfall over the city.
The sudden skyfall of hailstones damaged several cars parked outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downpour - a heavy and sudden fall of rain
  • synonym: shower - a brief and usually light fall of rain or other things from the sky
