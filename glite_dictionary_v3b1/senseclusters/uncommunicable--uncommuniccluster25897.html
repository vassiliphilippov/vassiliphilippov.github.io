uncommunicable

adjective
unable to be shared
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈmjunɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmjuːnɪkəbl / (BrE)
not able to be shared, expressed, or transmitted to others

Examples

An uncommunicable feeling
Some feelings are deeply personal and uncommunicable.
The artist struggled to capture the uncommunicable beauty of the sunset in her painting.
He found his grief to be uncommunicable, no matter how hard he tried to explain it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communicable - able to be shared or transmitted to others
  • synonym: inexpressible - impossible to express in words
  • synonym: unspeakable - too bad or shocking to be described in words
