life mask

noun
cast of a living person's face
3.3
Pronunciation: / laɪf mæsk / (AmE), / laɪf mɑːsk / (BrE)
A moulded cast taken from the face of a living person, typically used in art or historical documentation.

Examples

A life mask cast
Artists sometimes use a life mask as a base for creating realistic sculptures.
He commissioned a life mask to capture his likeness for future generations.
The museum displayed a life mask of the famous composer, carefully preserved for visitors to see.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death mask - a cast taken from the face of a dead person
