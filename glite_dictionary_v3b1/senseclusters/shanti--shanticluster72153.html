shanti

interjection
call for peace
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːnti / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːnti / (BrE)
an exclamation used to call for peace, calm, or silence, especially in spiritual or informal contexts

Examples

Shanti for calm
The meditation leader softly said shanti at the end of the session to encourage calm.
When the children became restless, their teacher raised her hand and said shanti to quiet the room.
During the ceremony, everyone chanted shanti together to wish for peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peace - a state of calm and tranquility
  • synonym: silence - the absence of sound or noise
