stinking nightshade

noun
a poisonous smelly herbaceous plant
9.0 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪŋ.kɪŋ ˈnaɪt.ʃeɪd/ (AmE), /ˈstɪŋ.kɪŋ ˈnaɪt.ʃeɪd/ (BrE)
