Synonyms

  • expectancy
    5.1 7/m
    expectancy is a formal state of waiting for something to occur, while expectation is the usual belief about something likely to happen.
  • anticipation
    3.4 13/m
    anticipation is a feeling of eager looking forward to an event, while expectation is a more general belief that it will happen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticipation - expectancy or prediction, often with excitement
  • degree higher: certainty - something sure or inevitable
  • antonym: doubt - a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction
  • degree lower: hope - a feeling of desire for a particular outcome
  • antonym: surprise - a feeling resulting from something unexpected happening
