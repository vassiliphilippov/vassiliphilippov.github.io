lapidify

verb
turn into stone
Pronunciation: / ləˈpɪdəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ləˈpɪdɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to change or be changed into stone; to petrify

Examples

To lapidify into stone
Legends tell of a curse that can lapidify anyone who looks into the statue's eyes.
Over centuries, minerals lapidify organic material buried underground.
Scientists studied the rare process that slowly lapidified the fallen tree trunks.
