gear case

noun
metal casing housing gears
Pronunciation: / ɡɪr keɪs / (AmE), / ɡɪə keɪs / (BrE)
a metal casing that encloses and protects a set of gears in a machine or vehicle

Examples

A gear case leak
A leaking gear case can cause damage to the transmission system if not addressed promptly.
The engineer designed a new gear case to improve protection against dust and debris.
The technician removed the gear case to inspect the gears for any signs of wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gearbox - a metal box containing a set of gears for mechanical power transmission, especially in vehicles
