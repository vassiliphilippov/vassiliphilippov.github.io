digestively

adverb
relating to digestion
Pronunciation: / daɪˈdʒɛstɪvli / (AmE), / daɪˈdʒɛstɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to digestion or the digestive process

Examples

Digestively prepared food
The soup was prepared digestively to be gentle on sensitive stomachs.
He chose foods that were digestively easy to process after his illness.
Some herbs are believed to act digestively and support a healthy gut.
