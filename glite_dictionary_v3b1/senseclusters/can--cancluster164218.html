can

noun
a buoy with a conical top
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæn / (AmE), / kæn / (BrE)
a buoy with a round bottom and a conical top, used as a navigational marker at sea

Examples

A can buoy
During the night, the ship nearly collided with a floating can buoy.
Fishermen rely on the can to guide them safely through shallow waters.
The harbor entrance was clearly marked by a red can on the starboard side.
