unconnectedness

noun
lack of connection; being unconnected
Pronunciation: / ʌn.kəˈnɛkt.ɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ʌn.kəˈnɛk.tɪd.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being unconnected; lack of a connection or relationship between things

Examples

Sense of unconnectedness
She felt a sense of unconnectedness in the busy city, as if she didn't belong.
The unconnectedness between the paragraphs made the essay difficult to follow.
The unconnectedness of the two incidents became clear after the investigation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connectedness - the state of being connected
