shirtlessness

noun
not wearing a shirt
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜːrtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or condition of not wearing a shirt

Examples

His shirtlessness at beach
His shirtlessness at the beach was not unusual on a hot day.
The coach commented on the players' shirtlessness during the intense summer practice.
Some restaurants do not allow shirtlessness inside for health and safety reasons.
