vestal

adjective
relating to sexual purity or virginity
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvɛstl̩ / (BrE)
(literary) Relating to sexual purity or virginity; chaste.

Examples

Vestal purity
Her vestal reputation was admired by everyone in the small town.
The poet described the princess as a vestal figure, untouched by scandal.
They were expected to adhere to vestal standards of conduct at all times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: virginal - In a state of never having had sexual intercourse
