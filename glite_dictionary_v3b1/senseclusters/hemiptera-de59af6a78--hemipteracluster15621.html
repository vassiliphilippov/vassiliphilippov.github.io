Hemiptera

noun
order of insects (true bugs, aphids, cicadas)
Pronunciation: / hɛˈmɪp.tə.rə /; / hɛˈmɪptəɹə / (AmE), / hɛˈmɪp.tərə /; / hɛˈmɪptərə / (BrE)
a large order of insects (true bugs) such as aphids, bedbugs, and cicadas, with mouthparts for piercing and sucking
