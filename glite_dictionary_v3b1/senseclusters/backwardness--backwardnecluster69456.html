backwardness

noun
lack of normal development, especially intellectually
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk.wɚd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbæk.wəd.nəs / (BrE)
The state of lacking normal development, progress, or advancement, especially in intellectual or social aspects.

Examples

Educational backwardness
Educational backwardness remains a major challenge for rural communities in many countries.
His teachers noted some intellectual backwardness when compared to his peers.
The village struggled with economic backwardness due to a lack of infrastructure and resources.
