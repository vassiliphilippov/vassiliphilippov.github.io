ruminal

adjective
of the rumen
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈruːmɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to the rumen, the first stomach compartment in cattle and similar animals

Examples

Ruminal bloating
The veterinarian examined the cow for signs of ruminal bloating after it ate too quickly.
Proper ruminal function is essential for the digestion of fibrous plants in cattle.
Researchers study ruminal microbes to improve livestock nutrition and health.
