coachability

noun
willingness to learn
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊtʃəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌkəʊtʃəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being willing and able to learn from coaching or feedback

Examples

Coachability in sports
Her coachability made her a favorite among the trainers on the team.
Employers often look for coachability when hiring new staff members.
His lack of coachability made it difficult for him to improve his performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stubbornness - the quality of being unwilling to change or accept advice
  • synonym: teachability - the quality of being easy to teach or receptive to instruction
