combiner

noun
person who joins things
4.6
Pronunciation: / kəmˈbaɪnər / (AmE), / kəmˈbaɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings together or mixes separate items, materials, or pieces of information

Examples

Factory combiner
The combiner in the factory ensures all parts are joined before packaging.
As a data combiner, she merges information from several sources for analysis.
A skilled combiner can create new solutions by mixing different ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembler - a person or thing that puts things together
  • antonym: separator - a person or thing that divides or keeps things apart
