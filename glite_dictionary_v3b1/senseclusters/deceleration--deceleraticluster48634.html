Synonyms

  • slowing
    2.7 1.08/m
    slowing is a simple, common reduction in speed you notice, while deceleration is the more formal name for that process.
  • retardation
    7.7 0.35/m
    retardation is a formal word for slowing or delay in progress, while deceleration specifically means a reduction in physical speed.
  • decelerated motion
    7.2 0.00/m
    decelerated motion describes movement whose velocity falls over time, while deceleration names the act or process of slowing down.
