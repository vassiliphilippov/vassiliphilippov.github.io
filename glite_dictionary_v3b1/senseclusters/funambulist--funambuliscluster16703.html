funambulist

noun
tightrope walker; rope acrobat
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈnæmbjəlɪst / (AmE), / fjʊˈnæmbjʊlɪst / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs feats of balance on a tightrope or slack rope

Examples

A funambulist on rope
It takes immense concentration and balance to succeed as a funambulist.
She trained for years to become a professional funambulist and perform in circuses around the world.
The skilled funambulist amazed the audience as he crossed the high wire without a safety net.
