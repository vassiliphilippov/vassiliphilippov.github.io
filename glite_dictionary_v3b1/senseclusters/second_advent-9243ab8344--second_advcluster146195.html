Synonyms

  • Second Coming of Christ
    6.8 0.08/m

  • Second Coming
    7.4 0.55/m
    Second Coming refers to Christ's future return to judge the living and the dead, while Second Advent is the same promised return.
  • Parousia
    9.9 0.43/m
    Parousia is the technical theological term for Christ's return to judge the living and the dead, while Second Advent is the common English name.

    Advent refers to the anticipated coming of Jesus as judge, while Second Advent names the specific return when he judges humanity at the Last Judgment.
