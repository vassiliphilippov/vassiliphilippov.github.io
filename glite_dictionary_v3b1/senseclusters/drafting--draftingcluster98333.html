drafting

noun
drawing technical blueprints or plans
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdræf.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdræf.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the skill or process of making detailed technical drawings or plans, especially for engineering, architecture, or diagrams

Examples
