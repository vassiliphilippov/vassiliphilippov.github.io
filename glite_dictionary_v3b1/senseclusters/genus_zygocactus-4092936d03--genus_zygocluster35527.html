genus Zygocactus

noun
genus of Brazilian cacti with jointed stems and showy flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈziːɡoʊˌkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈzaɪɡəʊˌkæktəs / (BrE)
A small genus of Brazilian cacti characterized by flat, fleshy, usually branched joints and showy red or pink flowers, followed by red, fleshy fruits.
