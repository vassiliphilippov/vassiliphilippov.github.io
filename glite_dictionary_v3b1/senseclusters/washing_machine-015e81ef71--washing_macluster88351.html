washing machine

noun
machine for automatically washing clothes
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used in the home for washing clothes and linens automatically with water and detergent.

Examples

A washing machine cycle
After the cycle finished, he transferred his clothes from the washing machine to the dryer.
She loaded the dirty laundry into the washing machine before leaving for work.
The new washing machine is much quieter and more energy-efficient than the old one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dryer - a machine for drying clothes after they have been washed
