frisking

noun
searching a person for concealed items
9.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɪ.skɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfrɪ.skɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of searching someone for concealed weapons, drugs, or other illegal items by patting their outer clothing

Examples

Airport frisking
At the airport, frisking is often performed as a security measure.
The police officer conducted a brief frisking before allowing entry into the building.
The suspect complained about the thorough frisking during his arrest.
