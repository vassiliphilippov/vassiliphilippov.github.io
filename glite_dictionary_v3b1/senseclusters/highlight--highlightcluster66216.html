highlight

verb
emphasize or make prominent
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.laɪt / (BrE)
to emphasize or make something stand out as important or notable

Examples

To highlight key points
She used bold type to highlight the most important points in her presentation.
The documentary highlights the challenges faced by small businesses during the pandemic.
The report highlighted several areas where improvements could be made.
Can you highlight the main points of your proposal for the committee?
The report highlights the achievements of women in science over the past century.
This exhibition highlights the influence of modern art on contemporary culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emphasize - to give special importance or prominence to something in speaking or writing
  • antonym: understate - to present something as less important or significant than it really is
