unhydrated

adjective
without water
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhaɪdreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaɪdreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not containing or combined with water; not having undergone a chemical reaction with water (used for substances or materials)
