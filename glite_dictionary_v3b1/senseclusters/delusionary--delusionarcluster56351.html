delusionary

adjective
misleading or deceptive
8.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈluːʒəˌnɛri / (AmE), / dɪˈluːʒənəri / (BrE)
giving a false impression or relating to false beliefs; connected to delusions or misleading ideas
