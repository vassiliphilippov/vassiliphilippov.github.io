hummer

noun
person who sings by humming
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌmə / (BrE)
a person who sings a tune by humming, without opening the lips or forming words

Examples

A constant hummer
As a child, he was known as a constant hummer, inventing tunes wherever he went.
The gentle hummer filled the room with a soft, soothing melody.
Whenever she was nervous, she became a quiet hummer in the back of the classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hum - to make a continuous low sound or sing without words
