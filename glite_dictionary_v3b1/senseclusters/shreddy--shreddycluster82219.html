shreddy

adjective
fast, technical music
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɛdi / (AmE), / ˈʃrɛdi / (BrE)
informal, describing music that features very fast, highly technical, and virtuosic playing, especially on electric guitar

Examples

Shreddy guitar solo
The guitarist played a shreddy solo that impressed everyone at the concert.
Fans love the band's shreddy style, full of rapid and complex guitar riffs.
Her new album is less shreddy and focuses more on melody than speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: virtuosic - showing great technical skill in music
