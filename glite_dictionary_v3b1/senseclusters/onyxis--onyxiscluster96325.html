onyxis

noun
ingrown toenail condition
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈɪksɪs / (AmE), / ɒnˈɪksɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition in which the free edge or tip of a toenail grows into the surrounding skin.

Examples

An onyxis infection
People with diabetes should pay special attention to signs of onyxis to avoid complications.
The doctor diagnosed her painful toe as a case of onyxis.
Treatment for onyxis often involves trimming the nail and reducing inflammation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ingrown toenail - a toenail that has grown into the surrounding skin
