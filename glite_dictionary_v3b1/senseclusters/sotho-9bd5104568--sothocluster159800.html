Sotho

adjective
relating to Sotho languages or people
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊˌtuː / (AmE), / ˈsəʊˌtuː / (BrE)
of or relating to the group of Sotho languages or the Sotho people of southern Africa.

Examples

Sotho culture
Linguists study the structure of Sotho noun classifiers.
Many Sotho traditions are celebrated in Lesotho and South Africa.
The Sotho word for rain is 'pula'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Basotho - the Sotho people of southern Africa
  • synonym: Sotho - a group of closely related Bantu languages spoken in southern Africa
