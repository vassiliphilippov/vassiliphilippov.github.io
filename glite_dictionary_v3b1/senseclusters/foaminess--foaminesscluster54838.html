foaminess

noun
quality of being foamy
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.mi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.mi.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being foamy, characterized by the presence of many small bubbles

Examples

The foaminess of soap
He tested the foaminess of the soap by rubbing it between his hands.
The foaminess in the beer was perfect, forming a thick, creamy head.
The foaminess of the cappuccino made it especially enjoyable to drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foamy - covered with or resembling foam
