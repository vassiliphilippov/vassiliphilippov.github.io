autoscopy

noun
seeing one's double
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑːskəpi / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒskəpi / (BrE)
the experience of seeing or perceiving oneself from outside the body, as if observing your own body from an external viewpoint
