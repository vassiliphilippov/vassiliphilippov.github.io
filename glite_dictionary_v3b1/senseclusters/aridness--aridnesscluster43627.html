aridness

noun
extreme dryness, lack of rainfall
Pronunciation: / ˈær.ɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈær.ɪd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely dry, especially as a result of very little or no rainfall

Examples

Extreme aridness
Farmers struggle with aridness when there is a severe lack of rainfall.
In recent years, the aridness of the region has increased due to prolonged drought.
The aridness of the desert makes it difficult for most plants to grow.
