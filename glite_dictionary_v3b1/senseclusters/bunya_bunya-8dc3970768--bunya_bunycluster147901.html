bunya bunya

noun
large edible nut from the bunya pine tree
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌnjə ˌbʌnjə / (AmE), / ˈbʌnjə ˌbʌnjə / (BrE)
a large edible nut from the bunya pine, tasting like roasted chestnuts and traditionally eaten by Australian Aboriginal people
