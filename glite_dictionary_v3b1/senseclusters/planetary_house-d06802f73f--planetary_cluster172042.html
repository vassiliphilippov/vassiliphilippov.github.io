planetary house

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈplæn.ə.riː haʊs/ /; / /ˈplæn.ɪ.triː haʊs/ (AmE), /ˈplæn.ɪ.triː haʊs/ /; / /ˈplæn.ɪ.tə.riː haʊs/ (BrE)
In astrology, one of the twelve equal divisions of the zodiac, each representing different aspects of life.

Examples

Planetary house in astrology
Astrologers interpret events based on the placement of planets within each planetary house.
In her birth chart, the Sun is located in the fourth planetary house.
The influence of each planetary house is said to affect different areas of a person's life.
