kilo

noun
kilogram; unit of mass (1000 grams)
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkiːləʊ / (BrE)
A kilogram; a unit of mass equal to one thousand grams, used in the metric system.

Examples

A kilo of rice
He bought a kilo of rice at the market.
She lost three kilos after starting her new exercise routine.
The recipe calls for half a kilo of sugar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kilogram - the base unit of mass in the metric system, equal to 1,000 grams
