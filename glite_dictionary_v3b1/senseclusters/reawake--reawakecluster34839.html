reawake

verb
to wake again
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈweɪk / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈweɪk / (BrE)
to wake up again after having been asleep or inactive

Examples

To reawake after sleep
After a short nap, she reawoke feeling refreshed and ready to work.
The city reawoke to life as the sun rose over the skyline.
He reawoke his interest in painting after many years away from the hobby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awaken - to stop sleeping or make someone stop sleeping
  • antonym: sleep - to be in a state of rest in which you are not awake
