chili con carne

noun
spicy stew with beef and chili peppers
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɪli kɑn ˈkɑrni / (AmE), / ˌtʃɪli kɒn ˈkɑːni / (BrE)
A spicy stew made with ground beef, chili peppers or chili powder, often including tomatoes and kidney beans.

Examples

A bowl of chili con carne
On a chilly evening, nothing beats a bowl of chili con carne to warm you up.
She prepared a large pot of chili con carne for the family gathering.
The restaurant's signature dish is chili con carne served with rice and sour cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chili - spicy fruit of plants in the genus Capsicum; the pepper used in cooking
