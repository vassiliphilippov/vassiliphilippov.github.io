order Ephemerida

noun
former insect order including mayflies
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrdər ɪˌfɛmɚˈɪdə / (AmE), / ˌɔːdə ɪˌfiːməˈrɪdə / (BrE)
a former taxonomic order of insects comprising the mayflies or shadflies, now commonly classified under Ephemeroptera

Examples

Order Ephemerida insects
Entomologists often refer to the order Ephemerida when discussing fossil records of mayflies.
Historically, mayflies were classified under the order Ephemerida, though the term Ephemeroptera is now preferred.
The order Ephemerida is known for species with short adult lifespans.
