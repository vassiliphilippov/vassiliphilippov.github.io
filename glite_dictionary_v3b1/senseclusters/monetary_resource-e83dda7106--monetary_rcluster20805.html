monetary resource

noun
assets in the form of money
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌnəˌtɛri rɪˈsɔrs / (AmE), / ˈmʌnɪtəri rɪˈsɔːs / (BrE)
assets that exist in the form of money, especially available for spending or investment

Examples

Monetary resources available
Access to monetary resources can make a significant difference in educational opportunities.
Nonprofit organizations often rely on donations as their primary monetary resource.
The company lacked sufficient monetary resources to expand its operations this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capital - wealth in the form of money or other assets used for investment
  • synonym: financial resource - assets available to a person or organization, including but not limited to money
