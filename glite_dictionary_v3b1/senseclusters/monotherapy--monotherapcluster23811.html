monotherapy

noun
single-drug treatment
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
treatment of a medical condition using a single drug or therapy rather than a combination

Examples

Single monotherapy treatment
The doctor decided to use monotherapy to treat the patient's high blood pressure.
Some infections can be managed effectively with monotherapy instead of multiple medications.
Researchers are studying whether monotherapy is as effective as combination treatments for certain diseases.

Related Terms
