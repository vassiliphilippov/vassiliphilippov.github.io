rhapsodize

verb
speak with great enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˈræp.səˌdaɪz /; / ˈræpsəˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈræp.səˌdaɪz /; / ˈræp.sə.daɪz /; / ˈræpsədaɪz / (BrE)
