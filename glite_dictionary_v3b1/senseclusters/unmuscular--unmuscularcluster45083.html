unmuscular

adjective
lacking vigor
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmʌskjələr / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌskjʊlə / (BrE)
lacking force, vigor, or energy in style, manner, or execution; not powerful or intense (figurative)

Examples

Unmuscular writing style
The novel's plot was surprisingly unmuscular, lacking the excitement readers expected from the author.
Critics described the performance as unmuscular, saying it failed to deliver the needed energy.
Her argument was too unmuscular to persuade the audience during the debate.
