labor organizer

noun
person who recruits workers for a union
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bɚ ˌɔːr.ɡəˈnaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə ˌɔː.ɡəˈnaɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person who encourages and recruits workers to join a labor union, often coordinating union activities and advocacy

Examples

Labor organizer recruits workers
As a labor organizer, she spent years meeting with employees to explain their rights.
The labor organizer encouraged factory workers to join the union for better wages.
The company tried to prevent the labor organizer from talking with the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strikebreaker - a person who works or is employed in place of others who are on strike, thereby making the strike ineffective
  • synonym: union organizer - a person who organizes workers to form or join a union
