hand-down

adjective
passed from one person to another
5.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhænd.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈhænd.daʊn/ (BrE)
passed on from one person to another, especially previously owned and now given or sold to someone else

Examples

Most of the books in the library are hand-down copies from other schools.
She treasured the hand-down ring her grandmother gave her.
