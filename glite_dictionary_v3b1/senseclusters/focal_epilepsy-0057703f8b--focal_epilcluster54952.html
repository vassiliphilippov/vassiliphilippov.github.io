focal epilepsy

noun
epilepsy starting in one brain area

A form of epilepsy in which seizures originate in a specific area of the brain, often causing localized symptoms such as twitching or unusual sensations.

Examples

Focal epilepsy seizures
Patients with focal epilepsy may experience brief, unusual sensations or movements limited to one limb.
The neurologist diagnosed her with focal epilepsy after reviewing her brain scans and seizure symptoms.
Unlike generalized epilepsy, focal epilepsy originates in one specific part of the brain.
