Salesian

noun
member of Salesian order
8.6
Pronunciation: / səˈliːʒən / (AmE), / səˈliːzɪən / (BrE)
a member of the Salesians, a Roman Catholic religious congregation founded by St John Bosco, dedicated to education and youth work

Examples

A Salesian priest
The Salesian devoted his life to helping disadvantaged children in the city.
Many Salesians work in schools and youth centers around the world.
She was inspired by the teachings of a Salesian she met during her travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Franciscan - a member of the Franciscan order, a Roman Catholic religious group
  • synonym: Jesuit - a member of the Society of Jesus, a Roman Catholic religious order
