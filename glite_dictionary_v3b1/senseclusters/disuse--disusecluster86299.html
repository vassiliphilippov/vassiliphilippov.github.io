disuse

noun
the state of being unused or neglected
6.2
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈjuːs / (AmE), / dɪsˈjuːs / (BrE)
the state of being unused, especially for a long period, often leading to neglect or deterioration

Examples

Years of disuse
After years of disuse, the playground equipment was rusty and unsafe.
His muscles weakened due to disuse after the long hospital stay.
The old railway line has fallen into disuse since the new station opened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neglect - the state or fact of being uncared for
