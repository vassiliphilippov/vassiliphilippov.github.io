wolfskin

noun
wolf pelt
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfˌskɪn / (BrE)
the skin or pelt of a wolf, often used as material for clothing or decoration

Examples

A wolfskin coat
The ancient warrior draped a wolfskin over his shoulders as a sign of bravery.
She admired the soft texture of the wolfskin rug in the cabin.
Traders once valued wolfskin for its warmth and durability in harsh winters.
