amphibology

noun
grammatical ambiguity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæmfəˈbɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌamfɪˈbɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A phrase or sentence that is grammatically ambiguous, so it can be interpreted in more than one way.
