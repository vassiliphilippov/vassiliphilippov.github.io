Ag

noun
chemical symbol for silver
4.0
Pronunciation: / eɪ dʒi / (AmE), / eɪ dʒi / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for silver, a soft white precious metallic element used in coins, jewelry, tableware, and photography.

Examples

Ag metal
Coins made of Ag have been minted for thousands of years.
Photographic films traditionally used Ag compounds to capture images.
The conductivity of Ag makes it valuable in electronics and electrical contacts.
