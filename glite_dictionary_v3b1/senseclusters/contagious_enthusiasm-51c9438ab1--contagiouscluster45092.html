contagious enthusiasm

noun
enthusiasm that spreads to others
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪdʒəs ɪnˈθuziæzəm / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəs ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
enthusiasm that is easily transmitted to and inspires others

Examples

Contagious enthusiasm spreads quickly

It's his contagious enthusiasm that makes him such a great teacher.
