Capreolus capreolus

noun
small Eurasian deer, European roe deer
Pronunciation: / kæpˈriːələs kæpˈriːələs / (AmE), / kæpˈriːələs kæprɪˈəʊləs / (BrE)
A small, graceful deer native to Eurasian woodlands, known for its reddish coat and small forked antlers; also called the European roe deer.
