constituent

adjective
forming a component part; essential to structure
2.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈstɪtʃuənt / (AmE), / kənˈstɪtjʊənt / (BrE)
Being an essential part of the structure or composition of something, especially in regard to physical makeup.

Examples

Constituent element
Proteins are a constituent part of every living cell.
The wall's strength depends on each constituent material used in its construction.
Water is a constituent element in most beverages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: component - forming an essential or integral part
  • synonym: structural - relating to or forming part of the structure
