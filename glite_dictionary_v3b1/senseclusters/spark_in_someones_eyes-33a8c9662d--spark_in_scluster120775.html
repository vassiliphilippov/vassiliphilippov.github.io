spark in someone's eyes

noun
look of enthusiasm or excitement in one's eyes
1.1
Pronunciation: / spɑrk ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / spɑːk ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
a lively or enthusiastic expression in someone's eyes, often showing excitement or strong interest

Examples

A spark in his eyes
As he spoke about the new project, a spark in his eyes revealed his enthusiasm.
There was a noticeable spark in her eyes when her favorite band came on stage.
Whenever she talks about painting, you can see a spark in her eyes.
