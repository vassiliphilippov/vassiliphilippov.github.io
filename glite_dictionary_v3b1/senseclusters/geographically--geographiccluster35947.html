geographically

adverb
in relation to geography or location
1.0
Pronunciation: / dʒiˌɑɡrəˈfɪkli / (AmE), / dʒiˌɒɡrəˈfɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the physical features, location, or characteristics of a place or region.

Examples

The country is geographically central
Geographically, the country is located in the heart of Europe.
The population is geographically concentrated along the coastline.
The two cities are geographically separated by a wide river.
