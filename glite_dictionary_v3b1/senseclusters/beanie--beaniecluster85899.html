beanie

noun
small round cap; casual hat
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːni / (AmE), / ˈbiːni / (BrE)
A small, round close-fitting cap, often without a brim, formerly worn by schoolboys and college freshmen, and now worn as a casual hat.

Examples

A beanie hat
At orientation, every new student was required to wear a bright green beanie.
He pulled his beanie down to keep his ears warm during the chilly morning.
She knitted a red beanie as a gift for her younger brother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skullcap - a close-fitting cap covering the top of the head
