shadowy deal

noun
dubious or suspicious agreement
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊi diːl / (AmE), / ˈʃadəʊi diːl / (BrE)
an agreement or arrangement that is dubious, secretive, or possibly illegal

Examples

A shadowy deal
Investigators uncovered a shadowy deal that led to several arrests.
Rumors spread quickly about the shadowy deal negotiated behind closed doors.
The company was implicated in a shadowy deal with a foreign supplier.

Related Terms

  • antonym: legitimate deal - an agreement that is legal and proper
  • synonym: underhand deal - a secret or dishonest agreement
