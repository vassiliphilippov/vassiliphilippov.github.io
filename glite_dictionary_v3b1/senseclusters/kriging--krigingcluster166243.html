kriging

verb
to estimate spatial values
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkriː.gɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkrɪ.gɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkriː.gɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkrɪ.gɪŋ/ (BrE)
to estimate unknown values at specific locations by applying the kriging statistical method to spatial data

Examples

To krige rainfall data
Scientists are kriging rainfall data to predict precipitation in areas without weather stations.
The research team kriged the mineral concentrations to create a detailed resource map.
By kriging the temperature readings, they produced a smooth climate surface for the region.
