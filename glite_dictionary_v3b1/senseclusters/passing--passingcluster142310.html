passing

noun
overtaking another vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of going by another vehicle that is moving in order to get in front of it

Examples

Dangerous passing maneuver
Her reckless passing on the highway made everyone nervous.
The accident happened while he attempted a passing maneuver on a sharp curve.
Unsafe passing is a common cause of road collisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overtaking - the act of catching up with and moving in front of another vehicle
