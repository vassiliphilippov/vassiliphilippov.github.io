pigtail

noun
a plait or braid of hair
6.1 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡ.teɪl / (BrE)
A lock of hair that is tightly braided or plaited, often one of two worn on each side of the head.

Examples

A pigtail braid
After braiding her pigtail, she tied a ribbon at the end.
She wore her hair in a single pigtail down her back.
The little girl skipped into class with two neat pigtails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braid - a length of hair or other material interwoven into three or more strands
  • synonym: plait - a length of hair that has been twisted or woven together
  • antonym: ponytail - a hairstyle with hair pulled back and tied at the back
