iridectomy

noun
removal of part of the iris (eye surgery)
7.6 8.9
Pronunciation: /ɪr.ɪ.ˈdek.tə.miː/ (AmE), /ɪr.ɪ.ˈdek.tə.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which a portion of the iris is removed, usually to treat glaucoma or other eye conditions

Examples

Laser iridectomy surgery
A laser iridectomy is often recommended for certain types of glaucoma.
After the iridectomy, his vision gradually improved as the intraocular pressure decreased.
The patient underwent an emergency iridectomy to relieve the pressure in her eye.
