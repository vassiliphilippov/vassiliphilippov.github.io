priss

noun
overly proper person
8.7
Pronunciation: / prɪs / (AmE), / prɪs / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman or girl, who is overly concerned with being proper, neat, or sensitive

Examples

She is a priss
Don't be such a priss—it's okay to get a little dirt on your shoes.
Her friends teased her for acting like a priss at the picnic.
He was called a priss because he refused to eat with his hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prude - a person who is easily shocked by improper things
  • antonym: slob - a person who is messy or careless
  • synonym: snob - a person who thinks they are better than others
