ham-fisted

adjective
clumsy or lacking manual skill
Pronunciation: / ˈhæm ˌfɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈhæm ˌfɪstɪd / (BrE)
clumsy or lacking skill, especially in using the hands; awkward or inept

Examples

A ham-fisted attempt
He gave a ham-fisted attempt at fixing the leaky faucet, making the problem worse.
Her ham-fisted approach to negotiation offended everyone at the meeting.
The painting was ruined by several ham-fisted brush strokes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • antonym: deft - skillful and quick in movement
