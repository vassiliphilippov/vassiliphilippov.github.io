breathlessness

noun
difficulty or discomfort in breathing
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛθləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθlɪsnəs / (BrE)
the condition of having difficulty breathing or being unable to breathe easily; shortness of breath

Examples

Severe breathlessness
He complained of breathlessness after climbing only a short flight of stairs.
Prolonged breathlessness can be a symptom of underlying heart or lung problems.
The doctor noted signs of breathlessness during the patient's examination.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: asphyxia - condition caused by lack of oxygen; suffocation
  • synonym: dyspnea - medical term for difficulty breathing
