collimate

verb
align an optical instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.lə.meɪt /; / ˈkɑləˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.ɪ.meɪt /; / ˈkɒlɪmeɪt / (BrE)
