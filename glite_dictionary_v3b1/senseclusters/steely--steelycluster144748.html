steely

adjective
hard or tough like steel
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːli / (AmE), / ˈstiːli / (BrE)
having a hard, strong, or determined quality, like steel; tough or resolute

Examples

A steely glance
He gave her a steely glance that revealed his determination.
Her steely resolve helped her overcome all obstacles.
The officer's steely expression showed no sign of fear.
Despite the chaos, her voice remained steely and calm throughout the crisis.
She met his gaze with a steely determination that showed she would not back down.
The manager gave a steely look to silence the noisy team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resolute - admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering
  • antonym: soft - easy to mold, cut, compress, or fold; not hard or firm to the touch
