ununiformed

adjective
not consistent
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.juːˈnɪ.fɔːrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.juːˈnɪ.fɔːmd / (BrE)
not uniform; lacking consistency, regularity, or evenness in appearance, quality, or distribution

Examples

Ununiformed surface
The paint on the wall dried in an ununiformed pattern, leaving some areas darker than others.
Her handwriting was ununiformed, with letters of different sizes and shapes throughout the essay.
The distribution of resources across the regions was ununiformed, causing some communities to struggle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irregular - not following a pattern or rule
  • synonym: uneven - not level, equal, or regular
  • antonym: uniform - always the same; not varying
