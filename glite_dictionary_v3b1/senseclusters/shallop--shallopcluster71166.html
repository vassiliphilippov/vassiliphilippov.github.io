shallop

noun
small open boat
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæləp / (AmE), / ˈʃæləp / (BrE)
a small, light or open boat, often used in the past for travel on rivers or along the coast, sometimes with sails or oars

Related Terms

  • antonym: barge - a flat-bottomed boat for carrying goods on rivers and canals
  • synonym: canoe - a narrow, light boat pointed at both ends and moved by paddles
  • synonym: dinghy - a small open boat, often carried on a larger ship
  • synonym: skiff - a small, light boat for rowing or sailing
