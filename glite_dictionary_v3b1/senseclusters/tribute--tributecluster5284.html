tribute

noun
gift or act to show respect or admiration
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪb.juːt / (AmE), / ˈtrɪb.juːt / (BrE)
something given, said, or done to show respect or admiration for someone or something

Examples

A tribute speech
He wrote a heartfelt tribute in memory of his late mentor.
The concert was organized as a tribute to the legendary musician.
Their victory is a tribute to hard work and teamwork.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval based on perceived faults
  • synonym: homage - special honor or respect shown publicly
