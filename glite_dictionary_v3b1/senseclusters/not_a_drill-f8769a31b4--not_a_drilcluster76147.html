not a drill

x
real, not a practice (emergency warning)
6.3
Pronunciation: / nɑt ə drɪl / (AmE), / nɒt ə drɪl / (BrE)
used to emphasize that a situation is real and not a practice, especially in emergencies or urgent situations.

Examples

This is not a drill
He shouted that it was not a drill when he saw the floodwaters rising.
Listen carefully—this is not a drill; there is an actual fire in the building.
When the security sirens blared, we quickly realized it was not a drill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: false alarm - a warning or signal that turns out to be incorrect or not actual
  • synonym: real deal - a genuine or authentic situation or thing; not fake or practice
