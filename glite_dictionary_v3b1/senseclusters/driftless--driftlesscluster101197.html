driftless

adjective
without direction
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪftləs / (AmE), / ˈdrɪftləs / (BrE)
lacking purpose, direction, or clear goals; aimless or idle

Examples

A driftless person
After losing his job, he felt completely driftless and unsure of what to do next.
The driftless summer days passed slowly, with no plans or responsibilities.
She worried her son was becoming driftless after dropping out of college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aimless - without a clear purpose or direction
  • antonym: purposeful - having a clear aim or goal
