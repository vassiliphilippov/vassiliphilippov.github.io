bottom rot

noun
fungal disease of lettuce and similar crops
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəm rɑt / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm rɒt / (BrE)
a fungal disease of lettuce and other crops in which the lower leaves rot first before the infection spreads upward

Examples

Bottom rot in lettuce
Farmers often inspect plants for early signs of bottom rot to prevent its spread.
Proper soil drainage can help reduce the risk of bottom rot in vegetable gardens.
The lettuce crop was devastated by bottom rot after a week of heavy rain.
