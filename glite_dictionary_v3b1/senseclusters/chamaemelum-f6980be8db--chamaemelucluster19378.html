Chamaemelum

noun
genus of chamomile plants
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiːmɪləm /; / kəˈmiːmələm / (AmE), / kəˈmiːmɪləm / (BrE)
a small genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, commonly known as chamomile, sometimes considered part of the genus Anthemis
