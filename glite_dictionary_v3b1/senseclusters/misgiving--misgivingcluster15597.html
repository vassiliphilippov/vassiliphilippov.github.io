misgiving

noun
doubt or unease about correctness or ethics
8.8
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪsˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
a feeling of doubt, worry, or uneasiness about whether something is right or will have a good result
