sacramentality

noun
quality of being sacramental
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˌmɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˌmɛnˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being related to or having the nature of a sacrament, especially in a religious context

Examples

The sacramentality of ceremony
The sacramentality of the ceremony was evident in its solemn rituals and prayers.
Many theologians discuss the sacramentality of everyday life, seeing sacred meaning in ordinary actions.
The church emphasized the sacramentality of marriage as a central part of its teachings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sacramental - relating to or resembling a sacrament
