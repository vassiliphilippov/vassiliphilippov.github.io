Yugoslavia

proper noun
former country in southeastern Europe
Pronunciation: / juːɡəˈslɑːviə /; / juˌgoʊˈslɑːviə / (AmE), / juːɡəˈslɑːviə /; / juːˌɡɒˈslɑːviə / (BrE)
a former country in southeastern Europe (1918–2003) that later split into several independent nations, including Serbia, Croatia, Slovenia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro, and North Macedonia
