nonforest

noun
land without trees
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌfɔrɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌfɒrɪst / (BrE)
an area of land that is not covered by forest or trees

Examples

A nonforest area
The satellite image showed a clear boundary between the nonforest and the dense woodland.
Farmers often convert nonforest areas into fields for crops or grazing.
Wildlife populations can differ greatly between nonforest and forested regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth
  • synonym: grassland - an area where the dominant vegetation is grass
