rounded vowel

noun
vowel pronounced with rounded lips
7.1 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈraʊn.dɪd ˈvaʊ.əl/ /; / /ˈraʊn.dəd ˈvaʊ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈraʊn.dɪd ˈvaʊ.əl/ /; / /ˈraʊn.dəd ˈvaʊ.əl/ (BrE)
A vowel sound produced with the lips rounded or drawn together to form a circular opening.

Examples

Rounded vowel sound
French contains several rounded vowels that do not exist in English.
In some languages, rounded vowels and unrounded vowels contrast to help distinguish words.
The pronunciation of 'oo' in 'food' is an example of a rounded vowel.
