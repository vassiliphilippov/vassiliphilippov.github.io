bump

noun
raised swelling on the body
1.0
Pronunciation: / bʌmp / (AmE), / bʌmp / (BrE)
a raised swelling or lump on the body or a surface, especially caused by a blow or impact

Examples

A bump on the head
She had a small bump on her head after hitting the door frame.
The child cried when he noticed the bump on his forehead from falling.
You should put some ice on that bump to reduce the swelling.
He noticed a small bump on the surface of the road.
The child had a bump on her forehead after falling off her bike.
There was a bump in the carpet where it hadn't been laid flat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bruise - an injury that appears as a dark mark on the skin, caused by a blow
  • synonym: bulge - a rounded swelling or protrusion
  • synonym: hump - a rounded protuberance, especially on an animal or object
