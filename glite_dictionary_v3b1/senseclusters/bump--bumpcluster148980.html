bump

noun
raised swelling on the body
2.5 11/m
Pronunciation: / bÊŒmp / (AmE), / bÊŒmp / (BrE)
a raised swelling or lump on the body or a surface, especially caused by a blow or impact

Examples

A bump on the head
She had a small bump on her head after hitting the door frame.
The child cried when he noticed the bump on his forehead from falling.
You should put some ice on that bump to reduce the swelling.
