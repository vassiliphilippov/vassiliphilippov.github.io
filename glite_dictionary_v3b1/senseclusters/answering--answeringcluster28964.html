answering

adjective
given in response or reply
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a look, gesture, etc.) given in response or reply to something; responsive.

Examples

An answering cheer
An answering cheer rose from the crowd after the announcement.
His answering nod reassured her that he understood.
She gave him an answering smile as their eyes met.

Related Terms

  • synonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively; responsive
