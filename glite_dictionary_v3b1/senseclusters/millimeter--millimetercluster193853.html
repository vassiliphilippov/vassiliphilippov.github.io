a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter

Examples

One millimeter thick
A sheet of paper is about one-tenth of a millimeter thick.
Most screws used in electronics are only a few millimeters long.
The rainfall yesterday measured just six millimeters.
Rainfall increased by ten millimetres overnight in the city.
The insect measured just five millimetres long.
This ruler is marked in millimetres for precise measurements.
