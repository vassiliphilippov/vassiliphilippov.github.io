darner

noun
needle for mending
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrnər / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnə / (BrE)
a large needle used for mending holes in fabric, especially in socks or stockings

Examples

A darner needle
She used a darner to repair the hole in her sock.
A darner is especially useful for fixing worn-out heels in old stockings.
He bought a new darner after losing his old sewing kit.
She reached for a darner to repair the hole in her favorite wool socks.
A darner is especially useful when fixing thick fabrics like denim or canvas.
He learned to use a darner from his grandmother, who often mended clothes by hand.
