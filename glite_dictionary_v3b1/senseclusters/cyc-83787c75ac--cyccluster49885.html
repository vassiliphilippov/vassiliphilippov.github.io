Cyc

proper noun
AI knowledge project
Pronunciation: / saɪk / (AmE), / saɪk / (BrE)
a long-running artificial intelligence project that aims to encode general human knowledge for computers to use common sense reasoning

Examples

Cyc project
The Cyc project has been collecting facts and rules for decades to help computers understand the world.
Researchers often reference Cyc when discussing advances in artificial intelligence and knowledge representation.
Many AI systems have tried to use data from Cyc to improve their reasoning abilities.
