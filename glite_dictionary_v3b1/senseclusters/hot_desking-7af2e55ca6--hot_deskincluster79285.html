hot desking

noun
office arrangement with unassigned desks
9.9 3.2
Pronunciation: / hɒt ˈdɛskɪŋ / (AmE), / hɒt ˈdɛskɪŋ / (BrE)
a system in which employees do not have assigned desks but use any available workstation in a shared office

Examples

Hot desking system
Many startups use hot desking to create a collaborative environment.
Since the company adopted hot desking, employees can choose a new seat each day.
Some workers find hot desking disruptive to their workflow and prefer a permanent desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible working - working arrangement allowing variation in location or hours
  • synonym: hoteling - system of reserving office space instead of having permanently assigned desks
