adjoint

adjective
connected or adjoining
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / əˈdʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
joined to or directly connected with something else; adjoining or adjacent.

Examples

Adjoint room
The conference room is in the adjoint wing of the hospital.
The library and the study are adjoint rooms, making it easy to move between them.
They built an adjoint garage to provide extra storage space next to the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjacent - next to or very near something
  • synonym: adjoining - next to or joined with something
  • antonym: separate - not connected or joined
