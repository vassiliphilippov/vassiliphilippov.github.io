fluorination

noun
adding fluorine
Pronunciation: / ˌflʊrəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌflɔːrəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adding fluorine or a fluorine compound to another substance

Examples

The fluorination process
The fluorination of water helps prevent tooth decay in many communities.
Industrial fluorination is used to produce certain plastics and chemicals.
Strict safety measures are required during the fluorination of materials in laboratories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chlorination - the process of adding chlorine to a substance
