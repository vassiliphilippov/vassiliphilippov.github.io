starchiness

noun
stiffness of fabric
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːr.tʃɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.tʃɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being stiff or crisp, especially in fabric or clothing after being starched

Examples

Starchiness of shirts
The starchiness of the tablecloth made it look elegant but feel a bit rough.
He disliked the starchiness in his new dress shirt because it made movement uncomfortable.
After washing, the starchiness of the curtains disappeared and they hung more naturally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: softness - the quality of being soft or not stiff
  • synonym: stiffness - the quality of being firm and not easily bent
