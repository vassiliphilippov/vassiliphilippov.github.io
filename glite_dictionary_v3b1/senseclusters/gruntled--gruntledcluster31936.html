gruntled

adjective
sullen or grumpy
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌntəld / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌntəld / (BrE)
sullen, grumpy, or ill-tempered; in a dissatisfied or sulky mood (archaic)

Examples

A gruntled face
He sat in the corner looking gruntled after the argument with his friend.
The gruntled servant muttered complaints under his breath as he worked.
After losing the game, she was noticeably gruntled and refused to join the celebration.
