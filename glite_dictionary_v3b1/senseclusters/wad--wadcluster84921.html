wad

noun
large bundle, especially of money
8.3
Pronunciation: / wɑd / (AmE), / wɒd / (BrE)
a thick bundle or large amount of something, especially a pile of paper money

Examples

The thief made off with a wad of jewelry and expensive watches.
He pulled out wads of cash from his pocket at the auction.
She donated wads of clothes to charity every spring.
There were wads of paper scattered across her desk after the meeting.
