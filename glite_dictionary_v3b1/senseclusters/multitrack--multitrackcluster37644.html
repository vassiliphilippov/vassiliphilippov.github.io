multitrack

noun
separate audio recording
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.tɪ.træk/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.træk/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.tɪ.træk/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.træk/ (BrE)
a recording made up of several separate audio tracks that can be mixed or edited individually

Examples

A multitrack file
The engineer saved each instrument as a separate multitrack for easier editing later.
After recording the vocals, they added them to the existing multitrack of the song.
Listening to the multitrack allowed the band to adjust the balance of each instrument.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mixdown - the process of combining multiple tracks into a single audio file
  • antonym: stereo - a recording with two audio channels for left and right sound
