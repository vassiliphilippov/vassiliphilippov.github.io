esoteric

adjective
understood by a few with special knowledge
7.2 9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːsəˈtɛrɪk / (BrE)
intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with specialized knowledge or interest

Examples

An esoteric text
She enjoys reading esoteric texts that are outside the mainstream of popular literature.
The professor gave an esoteric lecture on quantum mechanics that left most students confused.
Their discussion became so esoteric that even experts struggled to follow.
