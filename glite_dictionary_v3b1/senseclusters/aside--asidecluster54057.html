aside

adverb
excluded from consideration
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪd / (AmE), / əˈsaɪd / (BrE)
(used after a noun or clause) excluded from consideration; not taken into account.

Examples

Aside from cost
Aside from the weather, the trip was a complete success.
All joking aside, we need to start making serious decisions.
All technical difficulties aside, the event went as planned.
