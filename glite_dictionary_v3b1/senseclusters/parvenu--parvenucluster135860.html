parvenu

adjective
of or relating to a newly rich person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑɹ.vəˌnu / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.və.njuː / (BrE)
describing someone or something as having the qualities of a parvenu (recently wealthy or of new social status, lacking acceptance or refinement)

Examples

Parvenu manners
High society often looks down on parvenu manners as lacking refinement and tradition.
Many critics dismissed the parvenu elegance of their parties as mere show.
The family's parvenu taste was evident in their lavish, yet tasteless, home decor.
Despite her wealth, she remained a parvenu in the eyes of high society.
His parvenu manners made him the subject of ridicule among the old aristocracy.
The parvenu family tried to impress their neighbors with lavish parties but failed to fit in.

Related Terms
