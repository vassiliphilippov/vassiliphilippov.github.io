pare down

verb
reduce gradually; make smaller or simpler
7.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /per daʊn/ (AmE), /peə daʊn/ (BrE)
to reduce something in size, amount, or extent gradually or bit by bit, often to only what is necessary or essential

Examples

To pare down expenses
She managed to pare down the report by removing unnecessary details.
The designer decided to pare down the collection to its most essential pieces.
We need to pare down our expenses if we want to save money this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reduce - to make something smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
  • synonym: trim - to make something neater or smaller by cutting away extra parts
