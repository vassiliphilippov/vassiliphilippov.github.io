penciler

noun
person making tentative plans
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsələr / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsələ /; / ˈpɛnsɪlə / (BrE)
a person who makes provisional entries or schedules by writing them in pencil, often to indicate plans that may change

Examples

As the office penciler, she tentatively scheduled interviews for next Monday.
The team's penciler updated the calendar with possible meeting dates in pencil.
Before confirming the event, the penciler made a note in the shared planner.
The penciller added my name to the list, but said it was not confirmed yet.
As the team's penciller, she often made changes to the meeting schedule at the last minute.
You should check with the penciller to see if your appointment is still tentative or finalized.
