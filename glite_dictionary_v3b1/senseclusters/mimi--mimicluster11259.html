mimi

noun
cute pet name
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːmi / (AmE), / ˈmiːmi / (BrE)
an affectionate term for a small or very cute person or animal, often used as a pet name

Examples

A mimi kitten
She called her kitten mimi because of its tiny paws and playful nature.
Whenever her little brother smiled, their mother affectionately called him mimi.
The puppy was so adorable that everyone in the family started calling it mimi.
