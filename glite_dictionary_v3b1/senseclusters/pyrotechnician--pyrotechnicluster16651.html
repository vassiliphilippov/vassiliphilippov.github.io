pyrotechnician

noun
fireworks expert
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪroʊtɛkˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərəʊtɛkˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is trained to design, prepare, and set off fireworks or explosives

Examples

A pyrotechnician sets fireworks
The city hired a pyrotechnician to organize the New Year's Eve fireworks show.
Each pyrotechnician must follow strict safety procedures when handling explosives.
After years of training, she became a certified pyrotechnician and worked at major events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firework maker - a person who makes or sets off fireworks
