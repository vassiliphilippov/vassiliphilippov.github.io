neurilemma

noun
membrane around a nerve fiber
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊrɪˈlɛmə / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərɪˈlɛmə / (BrE)
the thin outer membrane that surrounds a nerve fiber, particularly the axon of a peripheral nerve

Examples

The neurilemma membrane
Damage to the neurilemma can impair nerve healing after injury.
Each peripheral nerve is covered by a delicate neurilemma that protects the axon.
The neurilemma plays an important role in the regeneration of damaged nerve fibers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: myelin sheath - the insulating covering around many nerve fibers
