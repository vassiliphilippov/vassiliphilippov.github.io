iroko

noun
African hardwood tree
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈroʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a large tropical African tree valued for its hard, durable wood used in furniture and construction

Examples

Iroko wood furniture
The carpenter chose iroko for the outdoor bench because of its resistance to weather.
Many traditional African boats are built from iroko due to its strength and durability.
The new flooring was made of polished iroko, giving the room a warm, golden color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oak - a common hardwood tree used in furniture and construction
  • synonym: teak - a tropical hardwood tree known for its durable wood
