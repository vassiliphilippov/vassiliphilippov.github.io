suction cup

noun
cup-shaped device that sticks by vacuum
6.5 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkʃən kʌp / (AmE), / ˈsʌkʃən kʌp / (BrE)
A small, cup-shaped device made of rubber, glass, or plastic that attaches to surfaces by creating a partial vacuum.

Examples

A suction cup device
After cleaning the window, he pressed the suction cup firmly to ensure it wouldn't fall.
She used a suction cup to hang decorations on the glass door without leaving marks.
The mirror stayed on the wall thanks to a suction cup attached to its back.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adhesive - substance or device used to stick objects or materials together
  • synonym: plunger - tool with a rubber cup used to create suction, typically to clear drains
