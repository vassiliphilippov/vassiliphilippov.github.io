myogenesis

noun
muscle tissue formation
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which muscle tissue forms, develops, or regenerates in an organism (such as during growth, after injury, or in embryos)
