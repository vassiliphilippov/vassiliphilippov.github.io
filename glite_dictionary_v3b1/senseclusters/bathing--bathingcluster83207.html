bathing

noun
washing oneself or another
2.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.ðɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪ.ðɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of washing or immersing your body in water or exposing it to sunshine, usually for cleanliness, relaxation, or health
