lined

adjective
having lines or stripes
Pronunciation: / laɪnd / (AmE), / laɪnd / (BrE)
having lines, stripes, or rows on the surface, often as decoration or for organization

Examples

Lined paper
She wrote her notes on lined paper to keep her handwriting straight.
The artist drew a lined pattern across the canvas for a modern look.
He wore a lined shirt with thin blue stripes to the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - without any lines, stripes, or decoration
  • synonym: striped - having long, narrow bands of color or texture
