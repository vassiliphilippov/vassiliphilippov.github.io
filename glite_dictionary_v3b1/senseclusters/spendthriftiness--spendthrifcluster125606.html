spendthriftiness

noun
wasteful behavior
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛndˌθrɪftɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈspɛndˌθrɪftɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or habit of spending money or resources carelessly or wastefully

Examples

Spendthriftiness with money
His spendthriftiness with company funds eventually led to a financial crisis.

His spendthriftiness quickly depleted his savings.
The family's spendthriftiness led them into financial trouble within a few years.
She regretted her spendthriftiness when she realized how much money she had wasted.

  • antonym: thriftiness - the quality of using resources carefully and not wastefully
