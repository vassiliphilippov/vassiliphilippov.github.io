geriatric

adjective
relating to elderly people or their healthcare
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛriˈætrɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛriˈætrɪk /; / ˌdʒɪəriˈætrɪk / (BrE)
relating to old people, especially their health or medical care

Examples

Geriatric care
A geriatric psychiatrist helped evaluate his mental health.
She specializes in geriatric medicine and the care of elderly people.
The hospital has a special unit for geriatric patients.
After retiring, Dr. Lee became a geriatric specialist to help the aging population.
Many geriatric patients require assistance with daily living activities.
The hospital has a special geriatric unit that focuses on elderly patients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pediatric - relating to the medical care of children
  • synonym: senile - showing characteristics of old age, especially mental decline
