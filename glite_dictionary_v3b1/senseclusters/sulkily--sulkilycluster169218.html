In a way that shows annoyance or resentment, especially by being silent or withdrawn.

Examples

To leave sulkily
After losing the game, he left the field sulkily without shaking anyone's hand.
She folded her arms and replied sulkily when asked to help with chores.
The child sat sulkily in the corner, refusing to join the others.
