convenience

noun
useful device or control
Pronunciation: / kənˈvinjəns / (AmE), / kənˈviːniəns / (BrE)
a device or control that is very useful for a particular job

Examples

A kitchen convenience
A dishwasher is a kitchen convenience that saves a lot of time and effort.
Many cars come with added conveniences like heated seats and backup cameras.
The hotel room is equipped with every modern convenience for travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appliance - a device or piece of equipment designed for a particular function
