horizontal section

noun
mechanical drawing cut horizontally
Pronunciation: / ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntəl ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntəl ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
a drawing of an object that shows what would be seen if it were cut by a plane passing horizontally through it

Examples

A horizontal section drawing
A horizontal section can reveal details about structural walls and door placements.
For renovation, a horizontal section drawing helped the engineers plan cable installations.
The architect provided a horizontal section of the building to show the interior layout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vertical section - a drawing representing a cut made vertically through an object
