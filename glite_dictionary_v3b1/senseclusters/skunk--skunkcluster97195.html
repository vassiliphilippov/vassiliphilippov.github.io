skunk

noun
despicable or contemptible person (informal)
Pronunciation: / skʌŋk / (AmE), / skʌŋk / (BrE)
an informal term for a person regarded as despicable or contemptible

Examples

He is a skunk
After cheating his own friends, many started calling him a skunk.
Everyone agreed that only a real skunk would abandon their team like that.
I can't believe that skunk tried to steal your idea at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bum - a person regarded as worthless or contemptible
  • antonym: hero - a person admired for courage, the opposite of a despicable person
  • synonym: rat - a person regarded as despicable or a traitor
