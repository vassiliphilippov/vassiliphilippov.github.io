volutation

noun
rolling movement
Pronunciation: / ˌvoʊluˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɒljuˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an archaic term for a rolling or turning movement

Examples

Wave volutation
The ancient text described the volutation of the waves along the shore.
He marveled at the volutation of the wheels as the carriage sped by.
The volutation of the dancers added a graceful motion to the performance.
