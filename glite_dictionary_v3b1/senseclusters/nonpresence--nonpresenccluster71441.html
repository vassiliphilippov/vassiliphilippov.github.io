nonpresence

noun
offline status
8.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈprez.əns/ /; / /nən.ˈprez.əns/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈprez.əns/ /; / /nən.ˈprez.əns/ (BrE)
the state of a user or device being offline or not shown as available, especially in computing or online systems

Examples

User nonpresence online
The system automatically marks users as nonpresence if they have not interacted for ten minutes.
Her nonpresence during the video call made it difficult to reach a decision.
When the device is in nonpresence, it will not receive any notifications.
