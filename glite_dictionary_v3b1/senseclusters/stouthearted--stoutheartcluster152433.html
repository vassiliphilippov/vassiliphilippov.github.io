stouthearted

adjective
brave and determined
Pronunciation: / staʊtˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / staʊtˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
brave and determined; showing courage and resolve, especially in the face of difficulty

Examples

A stouthearted person
Despite the danger, her stouthearted attitude inspired everyone around her.
The stouthearted firefighter rushed into the burning building to save the family.
They admired the stouthearted volunteers who helped during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courageous - having or showing courage
  • antonym: timid - lacking in courage or confidence
