organ

noun
part of an organization or government
5.5 3.79/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îÀêr…°…ôn / (AmE), / Àà…îÀê…°…ôn / (BrE)
a department, agency, or part of an organization, especially of government, responsible for a particular function or activity

Examples

Government organ
The Central Intelligence Agency is an important organ of the United States government.
The courts serve as an organ for interpreting and enforcing the law.
The newspaper acted as the official organ of the party, communicating policies to the public.
