unoxygenated

adjective

Examples

Unoxygenated blood
After traveling through the tissues, the blood becomes unoxygenated and returns to the heart.
The pulmonary artery carries unoxygenated blood to the lungs for oxygenation.
The right side of the heart receives unoxygenated blood from the body.
