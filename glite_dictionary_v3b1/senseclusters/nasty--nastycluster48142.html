nasty

noun
unpleasant things or people
Pronunciation: / ˈnæsti / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsti / (BrE)
an unpleasant, mean, or harmful person or thing, often used in the plural to refer to insults or offensive remarks

Examples

He said nasties
He said some nasties during the argument, which hurt her feelings.
The teacher warned the students not to write any nasties in their notes to each other.
Online forums sometimes attract people who post nasties just to upset others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym: insult - a rude or offensive remark or action
  • synonym: offense - something that causes hurt or displeasure
