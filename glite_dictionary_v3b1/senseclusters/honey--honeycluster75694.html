honey

verb
to flatter sweetly
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌni / (AmE), / ˈhʌni / (BrE)
to speak to someone in a sweet, flattering, or overly affectionate way to gain favor or advantage

Examples

To honey the boss
She tried honeying her teacher to get a better grade on the assignment.
Stop honeying me—I know you just want something.
He was honeying his way into the manager's good graces at the office party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butter up - to praise or flatter someone to gain favor
  • synonym: flatter - to praise someone excessively, often insincerely
  • antonym: insult - to speak to someone in a rude or offensive way
