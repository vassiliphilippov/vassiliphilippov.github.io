eyewear

noun
glasses or contact lenses
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌwɛə / (BrE)
devices worn on the eyes, such as glasses or contact lenses, to improve vision or protect the eyes

Examples

New eyewear styles
She bought new eyewear to help her see the board clearly at school.
Protective eyewear is required when working in the laboratory.
Many people choose stylish eyewear to match their outfits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contact lens - a thin lens placed directly on the surface of the eye to correct vision
  • synonym: glasses - lenses set in a frame worn on the nose and ears to correct or protect vision
  • synonym: spectacles - another word for glasses, especially in British English
