total puker

noun
person prone to frequent vomiting
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈpjuː.kər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈpjuː.kə / (BrE)
a person who vomits frequently, especially in situations involving motion or discomfort

Unfortunately, I became known as the total puker during our school bus rides.
