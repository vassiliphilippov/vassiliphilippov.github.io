unthrifty

adjective
not healthy or thriving
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθrɪfti / (AmE), / ʌnˈθrɪfti / (BrE)
not thriving, healthy, or growing well; stunted, weak, or failing to develop properly, especially of plants or animals

Examples

Unthrifty plants
The unthrifty plants in the corner of the garden needed more sunlight and water.
Farmers often remove unthrifty animals from the herd to maintain overall health.
After the harsh winter, many trees appeared unthrifty and failed to produce new leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stunted - not growing or developing properly
  • antonym: thriving - growing or developing well
  • synonym: unhealthy - not in good health or condition
