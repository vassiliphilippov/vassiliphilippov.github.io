airbill

noun
air shipment document
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbɪl / (BrE)
a document that provides details and charges for goods shipped by air, serving as a receipt and contract of carriage

Examples

An airbill document
The airbill must be attached to every international air shipment.
Before sending the package, she carefully filled out the airbill with all the required information.
Customs officials checked the airbill to verify the contents and value of the cargo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invoice - a document showing the amount due for goods or services
  • synonym: waybill - a document listing details and instructions for the transport of goods
