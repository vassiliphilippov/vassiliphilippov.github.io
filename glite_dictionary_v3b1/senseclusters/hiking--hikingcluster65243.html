hiking

noun
long walk for exercise or pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of taking long walks in the countryside or wilderness for exercise or pleasure.

Examples

Hiking in mountains
I love hiking in the mountains every weekend.
Many people enjoy hiking trails in national parks during the summer.
She took up hiking to improve her fitness and see more of nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: walking - the activity of going for a walk, especially as a form of exercise
