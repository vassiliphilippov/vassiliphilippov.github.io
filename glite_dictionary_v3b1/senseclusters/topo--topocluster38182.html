topo

noun
climbing route diagram
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊpəʊ / (BrE)
a diagram or guide used by rock climbers that shows climbing routes and key features of a cliff or crag

Examples

Climbing topo guide
The climbers studied the topo before attempting the new route on the cliff.
She always brings a detailed topo to help navigate complex climbing areas.
A good topo can make a big difference when planning a safe ascent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guidebook - a book giving information about a place or activity
  • synonym: route map - a map showing the path or course of a route
