topo

noun
climbing route diagram
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊpəʊ / (BrE)
a diagram or guide used by rock climbers that shows climbing routes and key features of a cliff or crag

Examples

A good topo can make a big difference when planning a safe ascent.
