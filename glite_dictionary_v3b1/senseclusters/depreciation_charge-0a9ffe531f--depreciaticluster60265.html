Examples

Annual depreciation charge
Every year, a depreciation charge is included in the financial statements to reflect the machinery's wear and tear.
Ignoring the depreciation charge could significantly overstate the firm's net income.
The company recorded a depreciation charge of $5,000 for its equipment this year.
