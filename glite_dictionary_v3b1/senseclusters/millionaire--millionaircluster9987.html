millionaire

noun
person with at least a million in assets
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪljəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌmɪljəˈneə / (BrE)
a person whose assets are worth at least a million units of currency, especially dollars, pounds, or euros

Examples

A millionaire donor
Several local millionaires donated generously to the new hospital fund.
She dreamed of marrying a millionaire and traveling the world in luxury.
The young entrepreneur became a millionaire before the age of thirty.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: billionaire - a person whose assets are worth at least a billion units of currency
  • degree higher: multimillionaire - a person whose assets are worth many millions
  • antonym: pauper - a very poor person with little or no money
