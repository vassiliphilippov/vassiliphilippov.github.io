ablate

verb
to remove bodily tissue or organ
6.0 7.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbleɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbleɪt/ (BrE)
to remove an organ, tissue, or bodily structure, typically by surgery or other medical procedures

Examples

To ablate tissue
In some cases, doctors may ablate part of the thyroid gland to treat hyperthyroidism.
Radiofrequency energy is often used to ablate abnormal heart tissue in arrhythmia patients.
The surgeon decided to ablate the tumor to prevent further complications.
