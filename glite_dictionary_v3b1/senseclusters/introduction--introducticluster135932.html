Synonyms

  • lead-in
    4.9 0.4/m
    lead-in is a brief phrase or section that leads into a talk or show, while introduction is a fuller opening that sets the stage.
  • preface
    4.9 1.0/m
    preface is an author's short statement in a book about its purpose, while introduction is the general first section that prepares the reader.
  • prologue
