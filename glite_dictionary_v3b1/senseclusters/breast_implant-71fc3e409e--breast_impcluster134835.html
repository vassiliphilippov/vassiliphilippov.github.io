breast implant

noun
prosthetic device for breast replacement or enhancement
5.4
Pronunciation: / brɛst ˈɪmplænt / (AmE), / brɛst ˈɪmplɑːnt / (BrE)
a prosthetic device implanted to replace or augment a breast, typically after surgery or for cosmetic enhancement

Examples

A breast implant surgery
After her surgery, she decided to get a breast implant for reconstruction.
Some people choose a breast implant for purely cosmetic reasons.
The quality and safety of a breast implant are carefully regulated by health authorities.
