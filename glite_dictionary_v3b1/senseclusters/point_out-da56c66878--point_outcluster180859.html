point out

verb
indicate something clearly
1.0
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt aʊt / (AmE), / pɔɪnt aʊt / (BrE)
to draw attention to something by indicating it carefully and clearly

Examples

To point out a fact
He points out that the solution is not as simple as it seems.
She pointed out the error in his calculation during the meeting.
The guide pointed out several interesting buildings on our tour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to; disregard
  • synonym: indicate - to show or point to something
