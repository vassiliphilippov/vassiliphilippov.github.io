nonemergency

adjective
not urgent
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˈmɝːdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈmɜːdʒənsi / (BrE)
not urgent; relating to situations or services that do not require immediate attention or action

Examples

Nonemergency medical care
The clinic only accepts nonemergency patients during regular business hours.
For nonemergency repairs, please submit a maintenance request online.
She called the nonemergency police number to report a lost wallet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: routine - regular and not urgent or special
  • antonym: urgent - requiring immediate action or attention
