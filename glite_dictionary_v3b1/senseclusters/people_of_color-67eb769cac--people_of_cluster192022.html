people of color

noun
non-white people; racial minorities
2.4 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpipəl əv ˈkʌlər /; / ˈpipəl əv ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpiːpl əv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
People who are not classified as white, especially those who are Black, Asian, Indigenous, or from other non-white groups.
