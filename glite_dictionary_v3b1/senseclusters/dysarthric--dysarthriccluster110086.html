dysarthric

adjective
speech muscle difficulty
9.9
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ˈɑːr.θrɪk/ (AmE), /dɪs.ˈɑː.θrɪk/ (BrE)
having difficulty speaking clearly because of problems with the muscles used for speech

Examples

Dysarthric speech
After the accident, his speech became noticeably dysarthric and difficult to understand.
The therapist worked with the dysarthric child to improve her pronunciation and clarity.
Patients who are dysarthric may benefit from specialized speech therapy exercises.
