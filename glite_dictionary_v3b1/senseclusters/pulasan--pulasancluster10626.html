pulasan

noun
sweet tropical fruit similar to rambutan
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / puÀêÀàl…ëÀês…ën / (AmE), / p äÀàl…ëÀês…ôn / (BrE)
A tropical fruit native to Southeast Asia, similar in appearance to the rambutan but with sweeter flesh.

Examples

Sweet pulasan fruit
Locals enjoy eating pulasan fresh or adding it to desserts for its sweet flavor.
The market had a fresh batch of pulasan alongside mangosteens and rambutans.
The skin of the pulasan fruit is easily twisted open to reveal the juicy flesh.
