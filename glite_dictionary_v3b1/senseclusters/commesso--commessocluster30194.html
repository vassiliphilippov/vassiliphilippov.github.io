commesso

noun
stone inlay artisan
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmes.oʊ/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmes.əʊ/ (BrE)
an artisan who creates images by inlaying colored stones, especially in decorative panels or artworks

Examples

A skilled commesso
The commesso carefully selected each stone to create a lifelike floral pattern.
Florence is famous for its skilled commesso artists and their intricate inlaid panels.
A master commesso can transform simple stones into stunning works of art.
