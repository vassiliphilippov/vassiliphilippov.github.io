osteopathist

noun
practitioner of osteopathy
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstioʊˈpæθɪst / (AmE), / ˌɒstɪˈɒpəθɪst / (BrE)
A medical practitioner who treats diseases and injuries by manipulating bones, joints, and muscles, especially using osteopathy techniques.

Examples

An osteopathist treatment
After her accident, she regularly visited an osteopathist for spinal adjustments.
An experienced osteopathist can help with chronic back pain through manual therapy.
The osteopathist explained how muscle tension could affect posture.
