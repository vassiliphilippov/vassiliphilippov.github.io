punt

noun
former Irish currency unit
Pronunciation: / pʌnt / (AmE), / pʌnt / (BrE)
the former basic unit of money in Ireland, equal to 100 pence, replaced by the euro in 2002

Examples

Irish punt coin
Collectors can still find old punt notes and coins in Ireland.
He exchanged his punts for euros at a local bank.
The Irish punt was replaced by the euro in 2002.

Related Terms

  • antonym: euro - currency that replaced the punt in Ireland
