ensample

noun
old-fashioned example
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈsæmpəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɑːmpəl /; / ɛnˈsɑːmpəl / (BrE)
an example or model for others to follow, especially in moral, religious, or general contexts

Examples

A moral ensample
