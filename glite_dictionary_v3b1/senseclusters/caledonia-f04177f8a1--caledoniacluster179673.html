Caledonia

proper noun
poetic name for Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˌkælɪˈdoʊniə / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈdəʊniə / (BrE)
a poetic and historical name for Scotland, originally referring to the area north of the Antonine Wall in Roman times
