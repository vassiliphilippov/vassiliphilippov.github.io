yack

noun
noisy or trivial chatter
Pronunciation: / jæk / (AmE), / jæk / (BrE)
loud, incessant, or trivial talk; chatter, often considered annoying or unnecessary

Examples

Endless yack
His yack about office gossip quickly became tiresome.
Sometimes the endless yack during meetings makes it hard to focus.
They spent hours in the café surrounded by constant yack from the other tables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - foolish or meaningless talk
  • synonym: chatter - rapid, trivial, and continuous talk
