ascidian

noun
small marine invertebrate (sea squirt)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪdiən / (AmE), / əˈsɪdiən / (BrE)
a small, sedentary marine invertebrate of the class Ascidiacea, having a sac-like body with two siphons through which water enters and leaves

Examples

A ascidian larva
Scientists study ascidian larvae to understand early chordate development.
The diver carefully observed the ascidian attached to the underwater rock.
The rocky tide pools were home to several brightly colored ascidians.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea squirt - common name for various ascidian species
  • synonym: tunicate - member of the subphylum Tunicata, including ascidians
