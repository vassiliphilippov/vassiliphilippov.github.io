orotund

adjective
full and resonant (of sound)
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îÀêr…ôÀåt ånd / (AmE), / Àà…ír…ôt ånd / (BrE)
(of a sound or a voice) full, round, and imposing in quality.

Examples

Orotund voice
He practiced until his vowels were perfectly orotund for the performance.
Her orotund tones gave the speech a powerful and commanding presence.
The actor's orotund voice filled every corner of the theater.

Synonyms

  • rotund
