yogin

noun
yoga practitioner
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊɡɪn / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡɪn / (BrE)
a person who practices yoga, especially as a spiritual or religious discipline, sometimes attaining spiritual insight or powers

Examples

A yogin meditates
The yogin meditated in silence at dawn, seeking inner peace.
Many people traveled to India to learn from a renowned yogin in the mountains.
The teachings of the yogin inspired his followers to live more mindfully.
The yogin meditated in the mountains for years, seeking enlightenment.
Many followers traveled great distances to learn from the yogin and hear his teachings.
Legends say the yogin could heal the sick with his spiritual powers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sage - a wise person, often regarded as having deep spiritual insight
  • synonym: yogi - a person who practices yoga
