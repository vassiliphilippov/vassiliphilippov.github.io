gladiatrix

noun
fierce female fighter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlædiˈeɪtrɪks / (AmE), / ˌɡlædiˈætrɪks / (BrE)
a woman who fights fiercely or aggressively, especially in politics, business, or argument

Examples

A gladiatrix in business
She became known as a gladiatrix in the courtroom, never backing down from a challenge.
In the world of business, her reputation as a gladiatrix made competitors wary.
The debate was intense, but the gladiatrix held her ground with sharp arguments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gladiator - a person, especially a man, who fights in public contests
  • synonym: warrior - a person who fights in battles or is very brave
