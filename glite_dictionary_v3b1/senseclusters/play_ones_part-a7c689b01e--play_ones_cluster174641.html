play one's part

verb
fulfill one's role in a group effort
Pronunciation: / pleɪ wʌnz pɑrt / (AmE), / pleɪ wʌnz pɑːt / (BrE)
to do what one is responsible for in order to contribute to a group effort or shared goal

Examples

To play their part well
During the cleanup, everyone played their part and the park looked beautiful again.
Each team member must play one's part for the project to succeed.
If you play your part, the whole group benefits from your effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: do one's bit - make one's required contribution to a group or cause
