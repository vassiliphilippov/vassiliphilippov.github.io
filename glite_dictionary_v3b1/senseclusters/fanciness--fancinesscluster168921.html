fanciness

noun
decorative quality
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfænsi nəs / (AmE), / ˈfænsi nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being decorative, stylish, or luxurious

Examples

The fanciness of the hotel
The fanciness of the restaurant made it a popular spot for celebrations.
She admired the fanciness of the wedding invitations, which were decorated with gold foil.
He preferred comfort over fanciness when choosing his clothes for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish
  • antonym: plainness - the quality of being simple and not decorated
