cava

noun
major heart vein
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.və/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.və/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.və/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.və/ (BrE)
either of the two large veins, the superior or inferior vena cava, that carry deoxygenated blood to the heart

Examples
