Synonyms

  • train station
    1.0 3.65/m
    train station is the common everyday name used by people, while railway station sounds more British and formal in writing.
  • railroad station
    1.5 0.57/m
    railroad station is the usual American term for stops with platforms and ticket halls, while railway station is the British form.
  • train depot
    3.1 0.16/m
    train depot is a practical area for loading, repairs, or freight, while railway station focuses on passenger boarding and scheduled stops.
  • depot
    4.4 3.44/m
    depot is a general place for storing or servicing vehicles, while railway station is specifically where trains stop for passengers or goods.
