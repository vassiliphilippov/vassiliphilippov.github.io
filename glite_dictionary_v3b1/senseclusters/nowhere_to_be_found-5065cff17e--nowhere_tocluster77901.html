nowhere to be found

adjective
completely missing; cannot be found
4.7 1.41/m
Pronunciation: / Ààno äÀåw…õr tuÀê bi fa änd / (AmE), / Ààn…ô äwe…ô tuÀê bi fa änd / (BrE)
impossible to locate; completely missing or unavailable

Examples

Nowhere to be found anywhere
After searching the entire house, her passport was still nowhere to be found.
His glasses are nowhere to be found since yesterday's meeting.
The book you lent me is nowhere to be found on my shelves.

Synonyms

  • missing
    1.0 30/m
    missing is used for things expected but not present where needed, while nowhere to be found sounds stronger and means impossible to locate.
  • unfound
    6.4 0.03/m
    unfound describes something not yet discovered or located, while nowhere to be found suggests it cannot be located at all.
  • unavailable
    3.4 5.86/m
    unavailable means not ready to use or reach now, while nowhere to be found focuses on being impossible to locate physically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: missing - not present or lost
