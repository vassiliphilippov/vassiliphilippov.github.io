peptidase

noun
enzyme that breaks down proteins
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛp.tɪˌdeɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛp.tɪˌdeɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that catalyzes the breakdown of proteins into peptides and amino acids by proteolysis
