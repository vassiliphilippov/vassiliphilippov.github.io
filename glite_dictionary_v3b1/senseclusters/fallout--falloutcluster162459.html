fallout

noun
radioactive particles after a nuclear explosion
7.5 1.50/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…ëlÀåa ät / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀêla ät / (BrE)

Examples

Nuclear fallout
After the nuclear test, concerns grew about the fallout affecting nearby communities.
Residents were advised to stay indoors to avoid exposure to radioactive fallout.
The long-term environmental impact of nuclear fallout remains a topic of research.
