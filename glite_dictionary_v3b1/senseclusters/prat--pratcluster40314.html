prat

noun
slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / præt / (AmE), / præt / (BrE)
a foolish or annoying person (British slang, informal insult)

Examples

He is a prat
He made a scene at the party and looked like a total prat.
Don't be such a prat—just listen to what she's saying.
Everyone laughed when she called her brother a prat for forgetting his keys again.
Stop acting like a prat and listen to what I am saying.
He called his brother a prat after the silly mistake.
Only a real prat would forget their own birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who behaves in a silly or unwise way
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a very stupid way
