respirometer

noun
device for measuring respiration
7.4
Pronunciation: /re.pɪ.ˈrɑːm.ɪ.tər/ /; / /re.pɪ.ˈrɔːm.ɪ.tər/ (AmE), /re.pɪ.ˈrɒm.ɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a device used to measure respiration or breathing, especially in living organisms or in medical tests

Examples
