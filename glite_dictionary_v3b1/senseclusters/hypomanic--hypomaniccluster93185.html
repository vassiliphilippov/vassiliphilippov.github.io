hypomanic

adjective
mildly manic state
8.7
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pə.ˈmæn.ɪk/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.ˈmæn.ɪk/ (BrE)
feeling or showing unusually high energy, activity, or happiness, but not as extreme as in full mania

Examples

Hypomanic mood swings
She felt hypomanic for several days, working late into the night with endless ideas.
During a hypomanic phase, he became unusually talkative and energetic.
Doctors monitor patients for hypomanic symptoms to prevent a shift into full mania.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depressed - feeling very sad or lacking energy
  • degree higher: manic - showing extremely high energy or excitement, often dangerously so
