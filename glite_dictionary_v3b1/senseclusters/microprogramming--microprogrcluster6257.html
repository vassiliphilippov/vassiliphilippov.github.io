microprogramming

noun
writing processor microcode
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.proʊ.ɡræm.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.prəʊ.ɡræm.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of writing or designing microcode that controls the operations of a computer's processor

Examples

Microprogramming design process
