hollowness

noun
a sunken or depressed quality
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑloʊnəs /; / ˈhɑl.oʊ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊnəs /; / ˈhɒl.ə.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being hollow or having an empty space or sunken area

Examples

After years of neglect, the statue had developed a noticeable hollowness inside.
His voice echoed with a certain hollowness that betrayed his sadness.
The hollowness of the tree trunk made it an ideal home for squirrels.
