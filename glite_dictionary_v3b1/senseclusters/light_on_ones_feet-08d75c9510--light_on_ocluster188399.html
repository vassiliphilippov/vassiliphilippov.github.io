light on one's feet

adjective
nimble and agile in movement
4.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / la…™t …ën w ånz fit / (AmE), / la…™t …ín w ånz fiÀêt / (BrE)
able to move quickly and easily, especially with grace or agility

Examples

Light on his feet
Even in his sixties, he remains remarkably light on his feet while playing tennis.
Our new puppy is incredibly light on his feet, darting around the house with ease.
The ballet dancer is so light on her feet that she seems to float across the stage.
