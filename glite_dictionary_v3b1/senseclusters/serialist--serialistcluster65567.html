serialist

noun
writer of serials
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəlɪst / (BrE)
a writer who creates stories or works published in successive parts, often in magazines or newspapers

Examples

Magazine serialist writer
The famous serialist kept readers eagerly awaiting each new chapter in the magazine.
As a serialist, she enjoyed developing her stories over many weekly installments.
The publisher hired a skilled serialist to boost the newspaper's readership with an ongoing mystery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers or magazines
  • synonym: novelist - a person who writes novels
