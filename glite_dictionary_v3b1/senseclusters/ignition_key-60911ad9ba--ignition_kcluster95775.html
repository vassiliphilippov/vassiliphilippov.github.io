ignition key

noun
key for starting a vehicle's engine
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɪʃən ki / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɪʃən kiː / (BrE)
a key used to operate the ignition switch of a motor vehicle, typically to start the engine

Examples

A ignition key in car
Many newer cars have replaced the traditional ignition key with a push-button start system.
She realized she had left her ignition key at home and couldn't start the car.
The mechanic asked if I had a spare ignition key in case the original was lost.
