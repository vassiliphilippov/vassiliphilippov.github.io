Adenium

noun
a genus of succulent shrubs or trees
6.6
Pronunciation: / əˈdiːniəm / (AmE), / əˈdiːniəm / (BrE)
a genus of succulent shrubs or small trees from tropical Africa and Arabia, with thick stems and showy flowers (also called desert rose)
