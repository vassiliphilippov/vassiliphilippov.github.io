nunchaku

noun
martial arts weapon
Pronunciation: / nʌnˈtʃɑːku /; / ˈnʌn.tʃɑː.kuː / (AmE), / nʌnˈtʃaku /; / ˈnʌn.tʃæ.kuː / (BrE)
a martial arts weapon made of two sticks connected by a short chain or rope
