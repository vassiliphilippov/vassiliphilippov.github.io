bashfully

adverb
in a shy or timid way
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæʃ.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈbæʃ.fʊl.i / (BrE)
In a shy or timid manner; showing embarrassment or self-consciousness.

Examples

To bashfully smile
He bashfully admitted that he had forgotten her birthday.
She spoke bashfully to the audience, hardly meeting anyone's gaze.
The boy smiled bashfully when praised by his teacher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shyly - in a shy manner; lacking confidence or boldness
