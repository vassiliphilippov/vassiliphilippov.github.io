sigil

noun
official seal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪdʒɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪdʒɪl / (BrE)
an official seal or signet used to authenticate a document or indicate authority

Examples

Royal sigil
The king's sigil was pressed into the wax to seal the royal decree.
Each noble family had a unique sigil to mark their official documents.
The ancient scroll bore a faded sigil indicating its authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seal - an official stamp or emblem used to authenticate documents
  • synonym: signet - a small seal, especially one set in a ring, used for stamping documents
