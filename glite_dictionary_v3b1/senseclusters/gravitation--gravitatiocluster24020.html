gravitation

noun
movement toward an attraction (figurative)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrævəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡravɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a movement or tendency toward something attractive or desirable

Examples

Gravitation toward success
Over time, his gravitation toward minimalist design became increasingly evident in his work.
The gravitation of artists to cosmopolitan cities is a well-known phenomenon.
There is a natural gravitation toward urban centers among young professionals.
