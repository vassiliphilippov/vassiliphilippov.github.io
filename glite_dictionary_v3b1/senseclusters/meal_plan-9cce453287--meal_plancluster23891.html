meal plan

noun
hotel room rate with meals included
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːl ˌplæn / (AmE), / ˈmiːl ˌplæn / (BrE)
an arrangement offered by hotels or resorts that includes a certain number of meals each day along with the room rate

Examples

A meal plan at hotel
Choosing a meal plan saved us money on dining during our stay.
Many hotels give guests the option to add a meal plan to their booking.
The resort offered a meal plan that included breakfast and dinner every day.
