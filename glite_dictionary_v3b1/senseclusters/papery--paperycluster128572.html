papery

adjective
thin and paperlike
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpəri /; / ˈpeɪ.pɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəri /; / ˈpeɪ.pəri / (BrE)
thin and having a texture or appearance like paper

Examples

Thin papery petals
After weeks without rain, the petals of the flowers became thin and papery.
He handled the papery skin of the onion with care, trying not to tear it.
The old letters were stored in papery envelopes that tore easily.
After months without rain, the flower petals became thin and papery.
She wore a dress made of a papery material that rustled when she moved.
The dried leaves had a papery feel in my hands.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cardboardy - having the texture or quality of cardboard
  • synonym: paper-like - resembling or similar to paper in texture or appearance
