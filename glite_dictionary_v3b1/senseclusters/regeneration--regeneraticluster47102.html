regeneration

noun
the act of renewing or restoring
5.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of renewal, restoration, or being formed again, including spiritual or physical improvement

Examples

Urban regeneration
After the fire, the forest showed signs of regeneration within a few months.
The city's economic regeneration has attracted new businesses and created jobs.
The lizard's ability for limb regeneration impressed the biologists.
Advances in medicine may one day allow for the regeneration of damaged tissues and organs.
Many religions teach about the regeneration of the soul through faith and repentance.
The forest entered a period of regeneration after the devastating wildfire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decay - the process of declining or rotting
  • synonym: renewal - the act of renewing or being renewed
  • synonym: restoration - the act of restoring something to its former condition
