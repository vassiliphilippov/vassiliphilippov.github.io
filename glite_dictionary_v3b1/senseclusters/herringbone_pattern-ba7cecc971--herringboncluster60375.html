herringbone pattern

noun
distinctive V-shaped zigzag pattern
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrɪŋˌboʊn ˈpætɚn / (AmE), / ˈhɛrɪŋbəʊn ˈpætn̩ / (BrE)
a pattern consisting of columns of short, parallel lines, with the lines in each column sloping in opposite directions to those in adjacent columns, commonly used in weaving, masonry, parquetry, and embroidery

Examples

A herringbone pattern design
He chose a suit with a subtle herringbone pattern woven into the fabric.
The brick wall featured a striking herringbone pattern, adding texture to the design.
The floor was laid in an elegant herringbone pattern that caught the light beautifully.
