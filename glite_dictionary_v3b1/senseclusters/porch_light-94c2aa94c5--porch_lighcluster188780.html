porch light

noun
light fixture on or near a porch
Pronunciation: / pɔrtʃ laɪt / (AmE), / pɔːtʃ laɪt / (BrE)
A light fixture installed on or near a porch, usually at the entrance of a house, for illumination and security.

Examples

The porch light
During the storm, the porch light flickered but didn't go out.
Every summer evening, insects gather around the porch light at dusk.
She left the porch light on so her guests would find the house easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outdoor light - light fixture designed for use outside a building
