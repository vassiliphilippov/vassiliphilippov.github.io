caterpillar tread

noun
continuous metal track on a vehicle
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬ɚˌpɪlɚ trɛd / (AmE), / ˈkatəˌpɪlə trɛd / (BrE)
a continuous metal belt or track used on vehicles such as tanks and bulldozers to allow movement over rough ground

Examples

A caterpillar tread belt
Engineers replaced a damaged caterpillar tread on the construction vehicle yesterday.
Snowmobiles and some rescue vehicles use a caterpillar tread system to operate on difficult terrain.
The tank's caterpillar tread allowed it to navigate the muddy battlefield with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: track - the continuous band of treads used on tracked vehicles
