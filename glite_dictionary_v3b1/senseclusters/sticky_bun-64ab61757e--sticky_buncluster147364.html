sticky bun

noun
sweet pastry with syrup, nuts, and butter
1.3 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆstÉªki bÊŒn / (AmE), / ËˆstÉªki bÊŒn / (BrE)
A sweet pastry made of rolled dough spread with sugar and nuts, sliced, and baked with honey or syrup and butter so that it becomes sticky and gooey.

Examples

A sticky bun with nuts
I ordered a fresh sticky bun and a coffee for breakfast.
She learned to bake sticky buns from her grandmother's old recipe.
The bakery is famous for its gooey sticky buns topped with pecans.

Synonyms

  • honey bun
    1.4 0.05/m
    honey bun is a spiral, sweet pastry with honey and cinnamon, while sticky bun is a gooey roll often made with nuts and syrup.
  • caramel bun
    2.3 0.00/m
    caramel bun is baked in a muffin tin with sugar or butter under each roll, while sticky bun is baked with syrup to become gooey.
