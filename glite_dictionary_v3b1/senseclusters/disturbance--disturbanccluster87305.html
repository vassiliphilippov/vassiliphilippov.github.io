disturbance

noun
psychological disorder (psychiatry)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɝbəns /; / dɪˈstɝːbəns / (AmE), / dɪˈstɜːbəns / (BrE)
a state of mental or emotional trouble, worry, or disorder

Examples

Emotional disturbance
Early treatment can help manage emotional disturbance in adolescents.
Sleep disturbance is often linked to underlying psychological conditions.
The patient was suffering from a severe disturbance of mood and perception.
After hearing the bad news, he experienced a deep disturbance that kept him awake all night.
Meditation can help ease the disturbance of a troubled mind.
The unexpected criticism caused a noticeable disturbance in her usual confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of mental peace or tranquility
  • synonym: disorder - a disturbance in normal functioning, especially of the mind
  • synonym: mental illness - a condition involving severe disturbance in thought or behavior
  • synonym: upset - a state of emotional distress or agitation
