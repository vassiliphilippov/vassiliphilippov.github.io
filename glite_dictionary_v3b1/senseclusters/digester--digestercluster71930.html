digester

noun
vessel for digesting materials
5.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˈdʒɛstər / (AmE), / daɪˈdʒɛstə / (BrE)
a strong container, often a pressure vessel, used for digesting plant or animal material through chemical or biological processes

Examples

A digester tank
