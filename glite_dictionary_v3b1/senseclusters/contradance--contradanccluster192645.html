contradance

verb
to perform a contradance
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːntrəˌdæns /; / ˌkɑːn.trəˈdæns / (AmE), / ˈkɒntrəˌdɑːns /; / ˈkɒn.trə.dɑːns / (BrE)
to take part in or perform a contradance, a type of lively partnered country/folk dance with couples in two facing lines, originally from France.
