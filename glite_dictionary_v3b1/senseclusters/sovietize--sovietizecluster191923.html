sovietize

verb
bring under Soviet control
9.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.vɪ.ə.taɪz/ (AmE), /ˈsɒ.vɪ.ə.taɪz/ (BrE)
to make a country, area, or its institutions more like the Soviet Union, especially by adopting its government, policies, or culture

Policies designed to sovietise agriculture led to widespread resistance.
