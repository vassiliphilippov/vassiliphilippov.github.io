incommutable

adjective
not capable of being changed or altered
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəˈmjutəbəl /; / ˌɪn.kəˈmjuː.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəˈmjuːtəbl̩ /; / ˌɪn.kəˈmjuː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be changed, substituted, or replaced; unchangeable

Examples
