wildcatting

noun
unauthorized strike action
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪld.kæt.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪld.kæt.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of participating in an unauthorized or unofficial strike by employees, often without union approval

Examples

Wildcatting by workers
The sudden wildcatting at the factory disrupted production for several days.
Union leaders were not informed about the wildcatting that began early Monday morning.
Management responded quickly to the wildcatting by opening talks with the workers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negotiation - discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
  • synonym: strike - a work stoppage by employees as a protest
  • synonym: walkout - a sudden departure from work as a form of protest
