screamer

noun
online jump‑scare clip
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskriː.mər/ /; / /ˈskrɪ.mər/ (AmE), /ˈskriː.mə/ (BrE)
An internet or media clip (video or image) intentionally designed to startle viewers by including a sudden loud noise or shocking image (a jump scare).

Examples

Online screamer video
The late-night stream unexpectedly included a short screamer that made half the chat drop their drinks.
I warned her about the video's thumbnail but she clicked and screamed at the screamer.
Creators often label short screamers as pranks, prompting content warnings on some platforms.
