murder charge

noun
an accusation of committing murder
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdɚ tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an official accusation that a person has committed murder, usually brought in a court of law

Examples

The murder charge filed
After a lengthy trial, the jury found him guilty of the murder charge.
She was acquitted of the murder charge due to lack of evidence.
The suspect was arrested last night on a murder charge after new evidence was found.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acquittal - a judgment that a person is not guilty of the crime charged
  • synonym: indictment - a formal accusation that a person has committed a crime
