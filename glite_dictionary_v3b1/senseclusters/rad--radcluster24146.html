rad

adjective
excellent or cool
Pronunciation: / ræd / (AmE), / ræd / (BrE)
very good, excellent, or impressive in a cool or exciting way, especially in informal speech

Examples

A rad skateboard
That new video game is absolutely rad and everyone wants to play it.
He wore a rad jacket that made him stand out at the party.
The concert last night was so rad that I can't stop talking about it.
