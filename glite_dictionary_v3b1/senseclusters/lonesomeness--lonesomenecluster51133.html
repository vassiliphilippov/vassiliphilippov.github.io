lonesomeness

noun
preference for being alone
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnsəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊnsəmnəs / (BrE)
a tendency or preference for being alone; the quality of enjoying solitude

Examples

Enjoying lonesomeness
Despite his lonesomeness, he found fulfillment in his quiet routine.
His lonesomeness made him content to spend weekends hiking by himself in the mountains.
There is a certain lonesomeness in her nature that draws her away from large gatherings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loneliness - sadness because one has no friends or company
  • synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, often by choice
