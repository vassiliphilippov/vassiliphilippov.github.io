chapeau

noun
hat with a brim for weather protection
Pronunciation: / ʃæˈpoʊ / (AmE), / ʃaˈpəʊ / (BrE)
A hat, especially one with a brim, designed to protect the head from bad weather.

Examples

An elegant chapeau
At the racecourse, many guests arrived sporting elaborate chapeaux.
She wore a wide-brimmed chapeau to shield herself from the sun.
The elegant chapeau completed his outfit perfectly.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cap - a soft, close-fitting head covering, often without a brim
  • synonym: hat - a covering for the head, often with a brim
