police car

noun
car used by the police for patrols
1.0 5.04/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôÀàlis k…ër / (AmE), / p…ôÀàliÀês k…ëÀê / (BrE)
A car used by the police, typically equipped with communication systems and used to patrol streets and respond to incidents.

Examples

A police car patrol
After the call came in, a police car arrived at the scene within minutes.
The police car sped down the street with its siren blaring.
Two officers got out of the police car and approached the building.

Synonyms

  • patrol car
    2.8 1.78/m
    patrol car is a police vehicle used mainly for regular street patrolling, while police car is the general term for such vehicles.
  • police cruiser
    3.0 0.68/m
    police cruiser is a marked vehicle often with extra emergency gear for fast response, while police car is any vehicle used by police.
  • cruiser
    3.2 2.21/m
    cruiser is an informal short name for a police car used on patrols, while police car is the full, neutral term for such vehicles.
  • squad car
    2.7 1.71/m
    squad car is a police vehicle used by a team for patrols and quick response, while police car is the general name for such vehicles.
  • prowl car
    7.4 0.00/m
    prowl car is an older or regional term for a police patrol vehicle, while police car is the common, modern name for those vehicles.
