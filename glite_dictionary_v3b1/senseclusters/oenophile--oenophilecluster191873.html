oenophile

noun
person who appreciates wine
Pronunciation: / ˈinoʊˌfaɪl /; / ˈɛn.əˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːnəʊfaɪl /; / ˈiː.nəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
