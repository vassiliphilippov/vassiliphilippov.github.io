oenophile

noun
person who appreciates wine
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈinoʊˌfaɪl /; / ˈɛn.əˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːnəʊfaɪl /; / ˈiː.nəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
A person who enjoys or has a deep appreciation of wine, especially as a connoisseur.
