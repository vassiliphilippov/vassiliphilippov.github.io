divan

noun
backless sofa with pillows
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvæn / (AmE), / dɪˈvan / (BrE)
a long, low backless sofa, typically placed against a wall and often furnished with pillows

Examples

A velvet divan
In the hotel lobby, a velvet divan provided the perfect place to relax and wait.
She reclined on the divan, reading her favorite book in the afternoon sun.
They arranged colorful cushions on the divan to make it more comfortable for guests.
