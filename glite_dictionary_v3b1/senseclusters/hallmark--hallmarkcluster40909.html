hallmark

noun
official mark certifying origin or authenticity
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔlˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an official mark stamped on articles of gold, silver, or platinum to certify their origin and purity; more generally, a mark indicating authenticity or origin

Examples

A hallmark stamp
Every genuine product from the company has a unique hallmark stamped on it.
She checked the jewelry for a hallmark before agreeing to buy it.
The silver teapot bears a hallmark that guarantees its authenticity.
