arc sine

noun
inverse sine function (math)
Pronunciation: / ɑrk saɪn / (AmE), / ɑːk saɪn / (BrE)
the angle whose sine is a given number; the inverse function of the sine

Examples

The arc sine function
Plotting the arc sine curve helps students understand inverse trigonometric functions.
The arc sine of 0.5 is 30 degrees or π/6 radians.
You can calculate the angle using the arc sine function on your calculator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arcsin - abbreviation for arc sine; the inverse sine function
