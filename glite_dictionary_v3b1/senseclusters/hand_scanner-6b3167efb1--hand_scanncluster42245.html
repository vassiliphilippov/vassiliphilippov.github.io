hand scanner

noun
biometric device for hand-based ID
3.7
Pronunciation: / hænd ˈskænər / (AmE), / hænd ˈskanə / (BrE)
a biometric device that reads the shape of a hand, palm prints, or vein patterns to identify or verify a person's identity.

Examples

Security hand scanner
The facility installed a hand scanner at the door to verify employees' identities.
Visitors must place their palm under the hand scanner before entering the secure laboratory.
When the system failed, security manually checked IDs while technicians repaired the hand scanner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biometric scanner - a device that uses biological characteristics to identify or verify people
  • synonym: hand geometry recognition - a biometric method that measures the shape and size of the hand for identification
