pulsative

adjective
rhythmically pulsing
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlsətɪv / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsətɪv / (BrE)
having the quality of pulsing or beating in a rhythmic manner

Examples

A pulsative movement
The doctor could feel the pulsative movement of blood in the patient's artery.
A pulsative light flashed on the machine, indicating it was working properly.
The music had a pulsative beat that made everyone want to dance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulsating - expanding and contracting with a regular rhythm
  • antonym: steady - not changing or moving; constant
  • synonym: throbbing - beating with a strong, regular rhythm
