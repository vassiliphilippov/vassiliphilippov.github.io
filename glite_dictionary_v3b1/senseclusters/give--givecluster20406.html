give

verb
estimate or state an amount or chance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv / (AmE), / ɡɪv / (BrE)
to estimate, state, or allow a particular amount, chance, or permission

Examples

Give a chance
I give her a good chance of winning the competition this year.
The coach gave us a 50-50 shot at making the finals.
The doctors gave him only six months to live after the diagnosis.
Her parents finally agreed to give her permission to go to the concert.
I can't give you any more chances to complete the work.
The restaurant doesn't give refunds on special orders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grant - to give or allow someone something, often officially
  • antonym: withhold - to refuse to give something that is due or desired
