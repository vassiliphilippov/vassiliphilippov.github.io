Arcadian

proper noun
person from Arcadia
8.3
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkeɪdiən / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪdiən / (BrE)
a person from Arcadia, or someone associated with an idealized, peaceful rural life

Examples

The Arcadian poet
The poet was inspired by the simple life of the Arcadian villagers.
Many artists painted scenes of Arcadian landscapes filled with peace and beauty.
He dreamed of escaping the city to live an Arcadian existence in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pastoral - relating to the countryside or rural life
  • antonym: urbanite - a person who lives in a city
