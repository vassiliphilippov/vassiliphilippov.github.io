renderer

noun
plastering worker
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛndərər / (AmE), / ˈrɛndərə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply plaster, cement, or similar material to walls or surfaces

Examples

Wall renderer
The construction company hired a skilled renderer to finish the exterior of the new house.
After the walls were built, a renderer applied a smooth layer of cement to protect them.
The old building needed a professional renderer to repair the damaged plaster on its facade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: builder - a person who constructs or repairs buildings
  • synonym: plasterer - a person who applies plaster to walls and ceilings
