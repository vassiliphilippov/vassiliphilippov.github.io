Rattus

proper noun
rat genus
Pronunciation: / ˈrætəs / (AmE), / ˈrætəs / (BrE)
a genus of rodents that includes the common rat and related species

Examples

The genus Rattus
The black rat, known scientifically as Rattus rattus, has spread to many continents.
Researchers studied several species within the genus Rattus to understand their behavior.
Urban environments often provide ideal habitats for Rattus populations to thrive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Mus - a genus of rodents that includes the common house mouse
  • synonym: rat - a small rodent with a long tail, often found in cities
