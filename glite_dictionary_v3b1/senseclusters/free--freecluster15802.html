free

verb
release from confinement or restriction
1.0
Pronunciation: / friː / (AmE), / friː / (BrE)
to release someone or something from confinement, captivity, restraint, or being stuck; to set loose or make available

Examples

He carefully freed her foot from beneath the heavy branch.
It took several tries to free the bicycle chain from the spokes.
The rescue team worked quickly to free the trapped miners after the cave-in.
