vinelike

adjective
resembling a vine
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈvaɪnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a vine, especially in the way it grows or appears

Examples

Vinelike branches

Some houseplants have vinelike stems that trail down from hanging baskets.
The artist painted vinelike patterns along the border of the mural.
