not long for this world

adjective
unlikely to last much longer
1.8
Pronunciation: / nɑt lɔŋ fər ðɪs wɝld / (AmE), / nɒt lɒŋ fə ðɪs wɜːld / (BrE)
unlikely to live or exist for much longer; near the end of life or existence.

Examples
