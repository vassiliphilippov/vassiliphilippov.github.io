snake oil

noun
fraudulent or useless medicine
6.7
Pronunciation: / sneɪk ɔɪl /; / ˈsneɪk ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / sneɪk ɔɪl /; / ˈsneɪk ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
a product, idea, or claim that is presented as valuable or effective but is actually false, worthless, or intended to deceive

Examples

He gave the investors a presentation full of snake oil and empty promises.
Many health websites are full of snake oil that promises miraculous results but delivers nothing.
