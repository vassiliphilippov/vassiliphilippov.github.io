needy

adjective
poor; in need of help
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdi /; / ˈnidi / (AmE), / ˈniːdi / (BrE)
lacking the basic necessities of life and needing help or support; poor or impoverished

Examples

Needy families
Local organizations are raising funds to help needy residents affected by the storm.
Many needy children rely on donations for school supplies and clothing.
The charity provides meals to needy families in the city.
Government programs are designed to provide assistance for the needy.
The charity distributed blankets and food to the needy during the winter.
Volunteers dedicated their time to helping needy families in the local area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destitute - extremely poor and lacking the means to provide for oneself
  • synonym: impoverished - lacking sufficient money or resources; very poor
  • synonym: poor - lacking sufficient money to live at a standard considered comfortable
  • antonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money, resources, or assets
