freddo

noun
cold coffee drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfredəʊ / (BrE)
a cold coffee drink, especially popular in Italy and Greece, typically served over ice

Examples

A freddo coffee
On a hot afternoon, she enjoyed a refreshing freddo at the outdoor café.
Many tourists in Greece try a freddo to cool down during the summer months.
He prefers a freddo to hot coffee when the weather is warm.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: espresso - a strong coffee made by forcing steam through ground coffee beans
  • synonym: iced coffee - coffee served cold, often over ice
