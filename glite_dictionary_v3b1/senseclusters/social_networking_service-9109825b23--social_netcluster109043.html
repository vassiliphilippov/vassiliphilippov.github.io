social networking service

noun
online platform for social connections
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊʃl ˈnɛtwɜrkɪŋ ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃl ˈnɛtwɜːkɪŋ ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a website or online platform that enables people to connect, communicate, and share content with others, typically based on shared interests or relationships
