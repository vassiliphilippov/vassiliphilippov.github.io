thesaurus

noun
reference book of synonyms
Pronunciation: / θəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / θɪˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
A reference book that lists words grouped together according to similarity of meaning, containing synonyms and sometimes antonyms.

Examples

A thesaurus book
He used a thesaurus to find synonyms for the word 'happy'.
The library's reference section includes a dictionary and a thesaurus.
Writers often consult a thesaurus when looking for more expressive language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dictionary - a book or electronic resource listing words and their meanings
