Examples

Synonyms

  • demineralization
    8.2 0.03/m
    demineralization describes an abnormal loss of mineral salts in bone or teeth, while decalcification names the loss of calcium.
  • halisteresis
    9.9 0.00/m
    halisteresis is the removal of mineral salts, especially calcium, from bone tissue, while decalcification is calcium loss from bones, teeth, or tissues.
  • deossification
    9.9 0.00/m
    deossification is the process where bone loses its mineral content and weakens, while decalcification specifically means losing calcium.
