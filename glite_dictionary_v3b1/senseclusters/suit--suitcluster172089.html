suit

verb
to enhance appearance, be flattering
1.4 6.10/m
Pronunciation: / sut / (AmE), / sjuÀêt / (BrE)
(of clothes, colors, styles, etc.) to make someone look more attractive or appropriate; to be flattering to a person's appearance or character.

Examples

Bright colors suit her
Bright colors don't suit everyone, but they look fantastic on him.
Short hair suits her much better than long hair ever did.
That dress really suits you; you should wear it more often.

Synonyms

  • become
    3.5 12/m
    become is a bit formal and is used to say clothes flatter someone, while suit is the more common word for clothes or styles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: become - to look good or suitable on someone
