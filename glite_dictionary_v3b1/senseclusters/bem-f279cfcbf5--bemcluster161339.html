BEM

proper noun
British Empire Medal honor
9.8
Pronunciation: / biː iː ɛm / (AmE), / biː iː ɛm / (BrE)
post-nominal letters indicating the recipient of the British Empire Medal, awarded for meritorious civil or military service in the UK.

Examples

BEM medal award
After years of community work, he was awarded the BEM in the Queen's Birthday Honours.
Her official title now includes the BEM after her name.
The ceremony recognized several volunteers who received the BEM for their dedication.
