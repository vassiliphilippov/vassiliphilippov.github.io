brachial

adjective
relating to the arm
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkiəl / (AmE), / ˈbrækɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the arm, especially the upper arm or the area between the shoulder and the elbow

Examples

Brachial artery
A brachial injury can affect movement and sensation in the entire arm.
The doctor located the patient's brachial artery to check the pulse.
They measured blood pressure using a cuff placed above the brachial artery.
