off-line

adjective
not on a main transportation route
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌlaɪn / (BrE)
Not situated on the regular or main route of a transportation system; located away from the main service line.

Examples

Off-line station
Several hotels offer shuttle service from off-line locations to the main train route.
The new terminal is an off-line facility serving remote communities.
We purchased our tickets at an off-line ticket office near the old station.
