naturism

noun
practice of social nudity
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.tʃəˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tʃə.rɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the practice of going without clothes, especially as part of a social movement advocating nudity in mixed company

Examples

Naturism movement
He embraced naturism as a way to feel more connected to nature.
Many resorts now cater to naturism and offer clothing-optional facilities.
The association promotes naturism as a healthy and respectful lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nudism - the practice of going without clothes, typically for health or comfort, often used interchangeably
