travailed

verb
to labor in pain
Pronunciation: / trəˈveɪld / (AmE), / trəˈveɪld / (BrE)
to experience great pain or effort, especially during childbirth or hard labor

Examples

Travailed through pain
She travailed through the night and finally gave birth to a healthy son at dawn.
The workers travailed for hours in the hot sun to finish the harvest.
He travailed over his studies, determined to pass the difficult exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: labor - to undergo the process of childbirth
  • antonym: rest - to relax or be free from effort or pain
  • synonym: struggle - to make a great effort or have difficulty
