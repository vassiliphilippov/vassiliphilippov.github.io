crystal gazing

noun
fortune-telling using a crystal ball
4.6 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪs.təl ˈgeɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪs.təl ˈgeɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
