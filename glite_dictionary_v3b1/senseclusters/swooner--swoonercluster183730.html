swooner

noun
person who excites others
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈswuːnər / (AmE), / ˈswuːnə / (BrE)
a person, especially an attractive or charming figure, who causes others to swoon or become intensely excited

Examples

A swooner idol
The young singer became a swooner overnight, with fans lining up to see him perform.
Many teenagers considered the movie star a real swooner because of his charming smile.
She was called a swooner after her romantic role in the popular television series.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heartthrob - a famous person who is very attractive and admired, especially by many people
  • synonym: idol - a person who is greatly admired or loved, often a celebrity
