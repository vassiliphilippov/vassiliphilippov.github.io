coupling

noun
act of pairing for reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌplɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pairing a male and female, especially for the purpose of reproduction or mating

Examples

Animal coupling
Observing the coupling behavior of the birds provided useful data for researchers.
Selective coupling of horses can improve desirable traits in the offspring.
The wildlife reserve monitors the coupling of endangered animals during breeding season.
