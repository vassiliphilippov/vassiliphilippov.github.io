nonpracticing

adjective
not currently active
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpræktɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpræktɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
not currently engaged in a profession, activity, or faith, though previously qualified or associated

Examples

A nonpracticing doctor
She is a nonpracticing doctor who now teaches at the university.
Although raised Catholic, he is now a nonpracticing member of the church.
After leaving the law firm, he became a nonpracticing attorney and started a new business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inactive - not currently participating or involved
  • antonym: practicing - actively engaged in a profession, activity, or faith
  • synonym: retired - having stopped working in a profession, usually due to age
