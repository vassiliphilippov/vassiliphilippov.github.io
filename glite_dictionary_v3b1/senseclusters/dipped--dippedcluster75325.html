dipped

adjective
abnormally sagging spine (esp. in horses)
Pronunciation: / dɪpt / (AmE), / dɪpt / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a horse) having abnormal sagging of the spine.

Examples

A dipped back
A dipped back can indicate poor health or improper care in horses.

The veterinarian noted that the horse was dipped, a condition often seen in older animals.
