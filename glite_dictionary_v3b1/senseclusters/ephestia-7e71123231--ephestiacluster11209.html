Ephestia

noun
genus of moths that infest stored food
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛstiə / (AmE), / ɪˈfiːstiə /; / ɛˈfɛstɪə / (BrE)
a genus of small moths whose larvae spin silky tunnels and feed on stored food products, often considered pests
