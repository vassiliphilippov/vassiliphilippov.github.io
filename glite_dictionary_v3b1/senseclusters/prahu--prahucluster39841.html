prahu

noun
Southeast Asian boat
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈprɑː.huː/ (AmE), /ˈprɑː.huː/ (BrE)
a small canoe-like boat, usually propelled by paddles or oars, used for transport or fishing in Southeast Asia

Examples

Fishing prahu
Fishermen set out at dawn in a wooden prahu to catch fish along the coast.
The villagers loaded supplies into a narrow prahu before paddling down the river.
Tourists enjoyed a peaceful ride in a traditional prahu on the calm lake.

  • synonym: dinghy - a small open boat, often used for fishing or as a tender
