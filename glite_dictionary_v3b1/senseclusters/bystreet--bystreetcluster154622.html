bystreet

noun
minor street or topic
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌstriːt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌstriːt / (BrE)
a minor or less important street, not a main road (can also be used figuratively for something less prominent)

Examples

A quiet bystreet
The old bookstore is hidden down a quiet bystreet away from the busy city center.
She prefers investigating the bystreet stories that national papers usually overlook.
Many interesting cafes can be found on a bystreet rather than the main avenue.
They wandered down a quiet bystreet away from the city center.
The restaurant is hidden on a narrow bystreet that most tourists never find.
Children played soccer in the empty bystreet behind their apartment building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: main street - the principal street of a town or city
  • synonym: side street - a smaller street off a main road
