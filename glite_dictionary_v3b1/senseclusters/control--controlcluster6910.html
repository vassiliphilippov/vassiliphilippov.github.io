control

noun
device for operating a machine
4.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
a device or mechanism used to operate, regulate, or adjust the function of a machine or system

Examples

TV control
Newer cars have digital controls instead of traditional knobs and buttons.
She adjusted the control to increase the volume on the speaker.
The control on the air conditioner was stuck and wouldn't turn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regulator - a device that controls or maintains the level or speed of a machine
  • synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit
