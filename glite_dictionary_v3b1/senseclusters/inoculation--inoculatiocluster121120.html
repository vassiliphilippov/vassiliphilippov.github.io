inoculation

noun
introduction of a vaccine to prevent disease
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɑkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌnɒkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of introducing a vaccine or antigen into the body to produce immunity against a specific disease

Examples

Flu inoculation
After receiving his inoculation against the flu, he felt more at ease traveling overseas.
The campaign promoted inoculation as the most effective way to prevent the spread of the disease.
The widespread inoculation of children has greatly reduced the incidence of measles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vaccination - the act of administering a vaccine to produce immunity
