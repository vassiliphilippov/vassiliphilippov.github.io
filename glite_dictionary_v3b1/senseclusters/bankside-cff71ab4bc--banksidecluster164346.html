Bankside

noun
land beside river
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋksaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbæŋksaɪd / (BrE)
the land or area along the side of a river; the riverside

Examples

A Bankside picnic
The old mill stood on the Bankside, overlooking the slow-moving river.
Families enjoyed picnics along the Bankside during the warm summer afternoons.
Artists often set up their easels on the Bankside to paint the scenic view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riverside - the land next to a river
  • synonym: waterside - the land beside a body of water
