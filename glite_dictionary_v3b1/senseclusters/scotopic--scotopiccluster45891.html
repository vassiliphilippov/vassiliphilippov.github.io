scotopic

adjective
low-light vision
Pronunciation: / skoʊˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / skəʊˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to vision or visual perception in low light conditions, such as at night

Examples

Scotopic vision
Cats have highly developed scotopic vision, which helps them hunt at night.
The experiment measured the subject's scotopic response to dim light stimuli.
Some animals rely on scotopic vision to navigate in darkness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: night vision - the ability to see in low light or darkness
  • antonym: photopic - relating to vision in bright light conditions
