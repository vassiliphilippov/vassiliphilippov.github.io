millime

noun
Tunisian monetary unit
Pronunciation: / mɪˈliːm / (AmE), / mɪˈliːm / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Tunisia, equal to one thousandth of a dinar

Examples

200 millimes
A loaf of bread costs around 200 millimes at the local bakery.
The price was listed as 500 millimes, which is half a dinar.
Tunisian coins are often denominated in millimes rather than dinars.
