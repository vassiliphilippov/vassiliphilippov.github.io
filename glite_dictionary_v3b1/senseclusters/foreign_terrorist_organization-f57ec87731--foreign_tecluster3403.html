foreign terrorist organization

noun
group using terror for political aims, foreign-based
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrən ˈtɛrərɪst ˌɔːrgənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ˈtɛrərɪst ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a group based outside a country that is designated as using terror or violence to pursue political objectives, often officially classified as terrorist by a government

Examples

A foreign terrorist organization
A foreign terrorist organization is subject to strict sanctions under national law.
Journalists discussed the links between a foreign terrorist organization and recent attacks.
The government added a new group to its list of foreign terrorist organizations today.
