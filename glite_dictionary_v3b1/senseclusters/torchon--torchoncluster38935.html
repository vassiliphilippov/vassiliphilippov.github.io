torchon

noun
cleaning cloth or food dish
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈʃoʊn / (AmE), / tɔːˈʃɒ̃ / (BrE)
a coarse linen or cotton cloth used for cleaning, or a dish prepared by wrapping and poaching, especially foie gras

Examples

Torchon of foie gras
The chef prepared a torchon of foie gras for the appetizer.
She wiped the counter with a clean torchon after cooking dinner.
In French cuisine, a torchon can refer to both a kitchen cloth and a style of food preparation.
