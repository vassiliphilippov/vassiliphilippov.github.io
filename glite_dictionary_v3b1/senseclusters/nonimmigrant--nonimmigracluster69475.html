nonimmigrant

adjective
temporary entry status
6.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈɪm.ɪ.grənt/ /; / /nə.ˈɪm.ɪ.grənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈɪm.ɪ.grənt/ /; / /nə.ˈɪm.ɪ.grənt/ (BrE)
relating to a person, visa, or status that allows temporary entry into a country without the intention to settle permanently

Examples

Nonimmigrant visa
A nonimmigrant visa allows you to visit the United States for business or tourism.
The embassy requires proof of nonimmigrant intent before issuing a student visa.
Certain nonimmigrant categories permit temporary work in the country for a limited time.
