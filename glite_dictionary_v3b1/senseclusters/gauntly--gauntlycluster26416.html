gauntly

adverb
in a thin, haggard way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔntli / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows extreme thinness and a bony, haggard appearance

Examples

Looked gauntly thin
The old man looked gauntly at his reflection in the cracked mirror.
After weeks of illness, she moved gauntly through the hospital corridors.
The stray dog wandered gauntly down the deserted street in search of food.

Related Terms

  • antonym: robustly - in a strong and healthy way
  • synonym: skeletally - in a way that resembles a skeleton or extreme thinness
