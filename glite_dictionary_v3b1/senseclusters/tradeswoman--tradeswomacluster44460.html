tradeswoman

noun
female shop owner or merchant
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who owns or manages a shop or works in a trade, especially as a merchant

Examples

A tradeswoman runs a shop
As a successful tradeswoman, she ran her own bakery for over twenty years.
The council provided grants to help young tradeswomen start their businesses.
The local tradeswoman is known for her honesty and fair prices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shopkeeper - a person who owns or manages a shop
  • antonym: tradesman - a man who works in a particular trade; male merchant or craftsman
