reaper

noun
person who gathers crops at harvest
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈriːpər / (AmE), / ˈriːpə / (BrE)
a person who gathers crops at harvest, especially by hand or with a simple tool.

Examples

A reaper in the field
During the wheat harvest, every reaper in the village worked from dawn until dusk.
In traditional farming communities, a skilled reaper was highly valued for a successful harvest.
The reaper moved steadily through the golden field, bundling sheaves of grain.
