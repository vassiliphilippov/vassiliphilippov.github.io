clubroot fungus

noun
plant pathogen causing clubroot in cabbages
8.3 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈklʌ.bruːt ˈfʌŋ.gəs/ (AmE), /ˈklʌ.bruːt ˈfʌŋ.gəs/ (BrE)
a soil-borne fungus-like organism that causes swelling and distortion of the roots of cabbages and other cruciferous plants, leading to the plant disease known as clubroot

Examples

Clubroot fungus infection
Many farmers lost their crops to clubroot fungus infestations last year.
The presence of clubroot fungus can devastate fields of cabbage and broccoli.
To prevent clubroot fungus, crop rotation is often recommended by agricultural experts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Plasmodiophora brassicae - the scientific name of the clubroot pathogen
