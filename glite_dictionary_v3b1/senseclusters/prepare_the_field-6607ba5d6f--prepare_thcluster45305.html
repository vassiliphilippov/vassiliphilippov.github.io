prepare the field

verb
make land ready for agriculture
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpɛr ðə fiːld / (AmE), / prɪˈpeə ðə fiːld / (BrE)
to make land ready for planting or cultivation by performing necessary tasks such as ploughing and fertilizing

Examples

To prepare the field for planting
A tractor is often used to prepare the field for corn or wheat.
Every spring, the farmers prepare the field before sowing seeds.
It takes several days to fully prepare the field after the last frost.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultivate - to prepare and use land for crops or gardening
  • synonym: plough - to turn over the soil to prepare land for planting
