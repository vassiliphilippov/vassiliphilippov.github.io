gladius

noun
internal squid shell
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈglæd.ɪ.əs/ /; / /ˈgleɪd.ɪ.əs/ (AmE), /ˈglæd.ɪ.əs/ /; / /ˈgleɪd.ɪ.əs/ (BrE)
a rigid, feather-shaped internal shell made of chitin that supports the mantle in certain squids and other cephalopods

Examples
