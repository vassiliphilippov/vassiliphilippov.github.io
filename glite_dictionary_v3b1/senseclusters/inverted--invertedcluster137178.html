Synonyms

  • upside-down
    1.8 1.44/m
    upside-down is a plain, common way to say the top and bottom are switched, while inverted is more formal.
  • overturned
    3.6 0.00/m
    overturned means physically flipped so the upper side faces down, while inverted focuses on position reversal, often in formal contexts.
  • upturned
    4.5 0.05/m
    upturned describes something whose bottom now points up, often slightly tipped, while inverted implies a complete top-bottom reversal.
  • upended
    4.4 0.00/m
    upended often means set or knocked onto its end with the bottom upward, while inverted stresses full top-bottom reversal.
  • reversed
    3.4 0.00/m
    reversed means changed to the opposite direction or order, while inverted specifically means the top and bottom are turned the other way.
