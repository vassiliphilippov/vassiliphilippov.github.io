fansign

noun
sign made by a fan for a celebrity
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.saɪn / (AmE), / ˈfæn.saɪn / (BrE)
a sign created by a fan, often displaying the name or image of a celebrity, shared at concerts or online to show support

Examples

A fansign design
He spent hours designing a colorful fansign to bring to the celebrity meet and greet.
Many K-pop idols respond to personalized fansigns during online events.
She held up her fansign at the concert, hoping her favorite singer would notice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banner - a large strip of cloth with a slogan or design, often carried or displayed to show support
