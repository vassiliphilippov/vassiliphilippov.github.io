sideman

noun
assistant or supporter
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdmæn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdmæn / (BrE)
a person who supports or assists another, especially in a secondary or subordinate role outside of music

Examples

Trusted sideman
During the campaign, he served as the candidate's trusted sideman at every event.
She preferred working as a sideman rather than being in the spotlight herself.
The manager relied on his sideman to handle logistics and communication with the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
