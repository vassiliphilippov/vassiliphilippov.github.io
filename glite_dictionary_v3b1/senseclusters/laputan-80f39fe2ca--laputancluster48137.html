Laputan

adjective
of or relating to Laputa; fanciful or impractical
9.9
Pronunciation: / ləˈpjutən /; / ləˈpjuːtən / (AmE), / ləˈpjuːtən / (BrE)
relating to fanciful, visionary, or absurdly impractical ideas, like those of Laputa in 'Gulliver's Travels'
