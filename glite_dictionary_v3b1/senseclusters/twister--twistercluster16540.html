twister

noun
a destructive windstorm; a tornado
4.6 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstə / (BrE)
A violently destructive windstorm occurring over land, typically characterized by a funnel-shaped cloud extending toward the ground; a tornado.

Examples

A violent twister
Residents took shelter in their basements as the twister approached.
Storm chasers drove hundreds of miles to see a twister in action.
The small town was devastated when a twister touched down last spring.
