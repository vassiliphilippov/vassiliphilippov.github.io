surma

noun
folk wind instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈsʊrmə / (AmE), / ˈsʊːmə / (BrE)
a traditional Eastern European double-reed wind instrument, similar to a shawm, used in folk music

Examples

Play the surma
The musician captivated the audience with a lively tune on his surma during the folk festival.
In traditional celebrations, the sound of the surma often fills the air with energy and excitement.
She learned to play the surma from her grandfather, who was a respected folk musician.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oboe - a modern double-reed woodwind instrument
  • synonym: shawm - a medieval double-reed woodwind instrument
