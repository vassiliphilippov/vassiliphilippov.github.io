download

verb
transfer data from the Internet or server
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌloʊd / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌləʊd / (BrE)
to transfer data or a file from a central system, such as the Internet or a server, to a local computer or device.

Examples

To download a file
I need to download the latest software update to my laptop.
She downloaded several songs to her phone before the trip.
We're downloading the report from the company's website.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upload - to transfer data from a local device to a central server or the Internet
