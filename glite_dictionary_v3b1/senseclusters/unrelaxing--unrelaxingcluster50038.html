unrelaxing

adjective
causing tension
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈlæksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈlæksɪŋ / (BrE)
causing tension or not making you feel calm or comfortable; making it hard to relax

Examples

Unrelaxing noise
The loud construction noise made the afternoon especially unrelaxing for everyone in the office.
She found the stiff hotel bed to be quite unrelaxing after a long day of travel.
His unrelaxing attitude during meetings made it hard for the team to feel comfortable.
The constant noise from the street made the hotel room feel very unrelaxing.
She found the bright lights and busy atmosphere of the restaurant quite unrelaxing.
After a long day, he wanted a peaceful evening, but the party was too unrelaxing for him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relaxing - making you feel calm and free from stress
  • synonym: stressful - causing stress or tension
  • synonym: tense - causing or feeling nervousness or stress
