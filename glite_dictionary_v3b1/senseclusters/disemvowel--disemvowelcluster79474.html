disemvowel

verb
remove vowels from words or text
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɛmˈvaʊəl / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɛmˈvaʊəl / (BrE)
to remove the vowels from a word or text, especially as a form of censorship or play

Examples

To disemvowel text
He decided to disemvowel his username as a joke.
Many internet forums disemvowel inappropriate words automatically.
Online moderators sometimes disemvowel offensive comments to reduce their impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vowel - a speech sound pronounced without any obstruction in the vocal tract
