naked mole rat

noun
hairless East African burrowing rodent
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkɪd ˈmoʊl ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd ˈməʊl ˌræt / (BrE)
a small, nearly hairless burrowing rodent of East Africa (Heterocephalus glaber), known for its eusocial behavior and wrinkled, fetal-like appearance
