uncensured

adjective
not censored
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛnʃərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛnʃəd / (BrE)
not censored; not subjected to official suppression, editing, or removal of content.

Examples

Uncensured news report
The journalist published an uncensured report that included all the controversial details.
Viewers appreciated the uncensured version of the documentary for its honesty.
The author fought to keep her novel uncensured despite pressure from the authorities.
