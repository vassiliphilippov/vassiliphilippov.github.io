incompetent

adjective
lacking skill or ability
4.9 6.92/m

not having the necessary skill, ability, or qualities to do something well; not able to do a job or task successfully

Examples

Incompetent worker
He was fired for being consistently incompetent in his job duties.
Her incompetent handling of the situation made things worse for everyone.
The mechanic proved to be incompetent at fixing basic engine problems.
Due to his incompetent driving, he failed the test again.
Hiring an incompetent contractor led to countless delays and mistakes.
The manager was widely regarded as incompetent by both staff and clients.

The surgeon was found to be incompetent and was dismissed from the hospital.

  • inexpert

  • incapable

  • unqualified

  • unskillful
