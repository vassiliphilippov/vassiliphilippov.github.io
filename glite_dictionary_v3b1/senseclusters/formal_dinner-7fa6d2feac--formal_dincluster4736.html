formal dinner

noun
dinner event with formal dress code
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrməl ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməl ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
a dinner event at which guests are expected to dress and behave according to formal rules of etiquette

Examples

A formal dinner event
She wore a long evening gown to the formal dinner.
The formal dinner required all guests to wear black tie.
They received an invitation to the formal dinner at the embassy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a large formal meal for many people
