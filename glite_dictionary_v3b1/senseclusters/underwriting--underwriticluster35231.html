underwriting

noun
financial guarantee
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
financial support or guarantee provided for an event, organization, or project, often ensuring funding or covering potential losses

Examples

Underwriting support
The local radio station relies on underwriting from community businesses to stay on the air.
Without underwriting, the annual science fair would not be possible.
The museum secured underwriting to cover the costs of its new exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: funding - providing money for a particular purpose
  • synonym: sponsorship - financial support given to an event or activity
  • antonym: withdrawal - removal of financial support
