toxaphene

noun
banned insecticide
Pronunciation: / tɑkˈsæfiːn / (AmE), / tɒkˈsæfiːn / (BrE)
a toxic chemical mixture formerly used as a pesticide, now banned or restricted in many countries because it is harmful to the environment
