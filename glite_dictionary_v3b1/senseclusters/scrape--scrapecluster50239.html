Synonyms

  • scratch
    1.1 7.86/m
    scratch is to cut or mark a surface with something sharp, while scrape is to rub and remove material more deeply.
  • abrade
    8.1 0.46/m
    abrade is more formal and means slowly wearing a surface by rubbing, while scrape means rubbing off material, often with a tool.
