going

verb
to move or travel
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
to move or travel from one place to another, or to leave a place.

Examples

They are going home
They are going to the park after lunch.
She was going home when it started to rain.
We are going on vacation next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leaving - to go away from a place
  • antonym: staying - to remain in the same place
