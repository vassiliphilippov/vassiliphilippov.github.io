bury one's head in the sand

verb
ignore or avoid facing problems
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛri wʌnz hɛd ɪn ðə sænd / (AmE), / ˈbɛri wʌnz hɛd ɪn ðə sand / (BrE)
to deliberately ignore or avoid facing a difficult or unpleasant situation

Examples

To bury one's head in the sand always
He always buries his head in the sand when it comes to financial problems.
Instead of burying your head in the sand, try to address the issue directly.
Too many people bury their heads in the sand and hope difficulties will go away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turn a blind eye - to deliberately ignore something bad or wrong
