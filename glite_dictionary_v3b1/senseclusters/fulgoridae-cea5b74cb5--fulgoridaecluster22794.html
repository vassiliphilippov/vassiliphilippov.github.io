Fulgoridae

noun
family of lantern fly insects
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈɡɔrɪˌdi / (AmE), / fʊlˈɡɔːrɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of large planthopper insects, commonly known as lantern flies, found especially in tropical regions.

Examples

The Fulgoridae family
Many Fulgoridae species are known for their elongated heads and vivid coloration.
Scientists discovered a new species belonging to the Fulgoridae family in the Amazon rainforest.
The Fulgoridae are often studied because of their unique appearance and ecological roles.
