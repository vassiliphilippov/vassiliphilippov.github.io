con man

noun
man who cheats by gaining trust
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn mæn / (AmE), / ˈkɒn mæn / (BrE)
a man who deceives people by gaining their trust in order to cheat or defraud them

Examples

A con man tricks
It takes a skilled con man to win the trust of so many people.
Police finally caught the con man who had been running scams in several cities.
The con man tricked the elderly couple into giving him their savings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: con artist - a person who deceives others by gaining their trust for cheating purposes
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats or defrauds others
  • antonym: victim - a person who is cheated or otherwise harmed
