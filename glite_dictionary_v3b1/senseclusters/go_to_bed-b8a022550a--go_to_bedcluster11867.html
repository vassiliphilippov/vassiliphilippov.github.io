go to bed

verb
get into bed to sleep
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə bɛd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə bɛd / (BrE)
to get into bed in order to sleep

Examples

To go to bed early
After watching the late movie, I finally went to bed around 1 a.m.
She felt tired and decided to go to bed early for once.
The children usually go to bed at eight o'clock on school nights.
Children should go to bed at the same time every night for better sleep.
I'm tired, so I'm going to go to bed early tonight.
She usually drinks some tea before she goes to bed.
