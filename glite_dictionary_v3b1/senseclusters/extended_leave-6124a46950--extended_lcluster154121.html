extended leave

noun
prolonged absence from work
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛndɪd liv / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndɪd liːv / (BrE)
a period of time away from work that is longer than usual, typically for personal, medical, or family reasons

Examples

Extended leave period
After her surgery, she requested extended leave to recover at home.
Many employees consider taking extended leave to travel or care for family members.
The company has a clear policy for handling applications for extended leave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leave of absence - official permission to be away from work
