toast

verb
make food brown and crisp by heating
Pronunciation: / toʊst / (AmE), / təʊst / (BrE)
to make (bread or other food) brown and crisp by heating it, especially in a toaster or under a grill

Examples

To toast bread
Please toast the bread until it is golden brown and crispy.
She likes to toast her bagels before spreading cream cheese on them.
You can toast marshmallows over the campfire for a sweet treat.
