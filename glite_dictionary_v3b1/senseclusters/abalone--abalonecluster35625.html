abalone

noun
edible marine mollusk with pearly shell
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈloʊni / (AmE), / ˌabəˈləʊni / (BrE)
a large edible marine mollusk of the genus Haliotis, having a flattened, ear-shaped shell lined with mother-of-pearl

Examples

Fresh abalone shell
Divers risk their lives to harvest abalone from rocky coastal waters.
She found a beautiful abalone shell washed up on the beach.
The restaurant serves fresh abalone prepared with garlic butter and herbs.

Related Terms
