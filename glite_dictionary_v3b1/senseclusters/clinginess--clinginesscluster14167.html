clinginess

noun
excessive attachment
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪŋinəs / (AmE), / ˈklɪŋinəs / (BrE)
the quality of wanting to stay very close to someone or needing constant attention or reassurance from them

Examples

Emotional clinginess
His clinginess made it difficult for his partner to have time alone with friends.
Parents sometimes worry that too much clinginess can prevent children from becoming independent.
The puppy's clinginess was endearing at first but became challenging over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependence - the state of relying on or needing someone for support
  • antonym: independence - the state of not relying on others
