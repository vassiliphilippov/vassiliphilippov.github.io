granularly

adverb
in detailed manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræn.jə.lɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈɡræn.jʊ.lə.li / (BrE)
in a detailed or finely divided manner, considering small or specific parts separately

Examples

Analyze granularly
The report examined each issue granularly to ensure nothing was overlooked.
We need to review the budget granularly before making any final decisions.
By analyzing the results granularly, the team discovered important patterns in the data.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generally - in a broad or overall way, not detailed
  • synonym: specifically - in a detailed and exact way
