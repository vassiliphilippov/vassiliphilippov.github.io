whomp on

verb
defeat soundly or work hard on
9.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / wÉ‘mp É‘n / (AmE), / wÉ’mp É’n / (BrE)
to defeat someone or something decisively, or to exert a lot of effort or force on something

Examples

To whomp on the drums
He likes to whomp on the drums when he plays in the band.
She decided to whomp on her essay the night before it was due.
The team really whomped on their rivals in the final game.

Synonyms

    8.8 0.02/m

  • whup

    whup is an informal word for beating someone decisively, while whomp on is similar but also stresses hitting with strong force.
  • wallop
    6.7 0.01/m

  • whoop
    4.2 0.75/m

  • mop up

  • overwhelm
    4.0 2.99/m
