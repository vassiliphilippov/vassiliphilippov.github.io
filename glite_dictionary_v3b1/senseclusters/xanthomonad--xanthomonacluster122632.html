xanthomonad

noun
bacterium producing yellow pigment, some plant pathogens
9.9
Pronunciation: / zæn.θəˈmoʊ.næd / (AmE), / zæn.θəˈməʊ.næd / (BrE)
A bacterium of a group that produces yellow, non-water-soluble pigments, some species of which are pathogenic to plants.
