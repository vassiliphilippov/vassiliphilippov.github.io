freshen

verb
make or become fresh
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛʃən /; / ˈfrɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃən /; / ˈfrɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
to make something or yourself feel, look, or become fresh, clean, or refreshed

Examples

A quick shower in the morning helps to freshen me before work.
Opening the windows will freshen the air in this room.
