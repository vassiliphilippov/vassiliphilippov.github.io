gantline

noun
ship hoisting rope
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæntˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡæntˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a rope on a ship used for hoisting or lowering objects, especially to or from the mast

Examples

The gantline rope
The crew attached the cargo to the gantline before lifting it onto the deck.
He climbed the mast using a gantline secured by his fellow sailors.
During the storm, the gantline was essential for lowering the sails quickly.
