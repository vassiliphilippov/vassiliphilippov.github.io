vaporize

verb
to turn into vapor or gas
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪpəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəraɪz /; / ˈveɪpəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to turn or be turned into vapor or gas, especially by heating or removing water
