wakeboarding

noun
water sport with board
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪkˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪkˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport in which a person rides a board over water while being towed by a boat

Examples

A wakeboarding competition
He tried wakeboarding for the first time and quickly learned how to balance on the board.
Many lakes offer wakeboarding lessons for beginners during the summer months.
She enjoys wakeboarding because it combines speed, balance, and excitement on the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surfing - riding on ocean waves using a board
  • synonym: waterskiing - a sport where a person is pulled over water on skis by a boat
