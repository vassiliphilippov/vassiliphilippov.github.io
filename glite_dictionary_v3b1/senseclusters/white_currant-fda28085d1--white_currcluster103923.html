white currant

noun
type of currant with white berries
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈkɜrənt / (AmE), / waɪt ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a plant that produces small, translucent white berries, or the berries themselves, which are sweet and edible

Examples

White currant berries
White currant jam is less common than red, but has a delicate flavor.
She planted white currants alongside red and black varieties in her garden.
The dessert was garnished with a handful of white currants for a fresh, tangy touch.
She made a delicious tart with fresh white currants from the garden.
The market was selling both red and white currants in small baskets.
Unlike red currants, white currants have a milder, sweeter flavor ideal for desserts.
