kinkiness

noun
irregularity or fault
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋkinəs / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋkinɪs /; / ˈkɪŋkinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having kinks, bends, twists, or irregularities in something (such as a wire, rope, or system)

Examples

Kinkiness of the wire
The kinkiness of the wire made it difficult to install in a straight line.
Engineers must address any kinkiness in the system to ensure smooth operation.
Unexpected kinkiness in the conveyor belt caused delays in the production process.
The kinkiness of the old extension cord made it difficult to lay flat on the ground.
She tried to smooth out the kinkiness in her hair after removing the braids.
The kinkiness of the garden hose caused the water flow to stop suddenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irregularity - the state of not being regular or smooth
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even and without irregularities
  • antonym: straightness - the quality of being straight, without bends or curves
  • synonym: twist - a bend or curve in something that is usually straight
