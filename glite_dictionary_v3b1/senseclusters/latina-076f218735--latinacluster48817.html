Latina

noun
Latin American woman
Pronunciation: / ləˈtinə / (AmE), / ləˈtiːnə /; / laˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a woman from Latin America or of Latin American descent or heritage, especially in the United States

Examples

A proud Latina
She is proud to be a Latina and celebrates her heritage every year.
Many Latina artists have influenced the music industry worldwide.
The organization supports young Latina students in pursuing higher education.
Maria is a proud Latina who shares her culture with her friends.
The organization supports young Latinas pursuing careers in science and technology.
As a Latina, she brings a unique perspective to the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hispanic - a person from a Spanish-speaking country or of Spanish-speaking descent
  • antonym: Latino - a man from Latin America or of Latin American descent
