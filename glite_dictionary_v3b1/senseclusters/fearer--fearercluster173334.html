fearer

noun
person who is afraid
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪrər / (AmE), / ˈfɪərə / (BrE)
a person who feels fear or is afraid of something

Examples

A fearer of spiders
As a lifelong fearer of heights, she avoided tall buildings.
The fearer of public speaking found it difficult to present in front of a crowd.
He admitted he was a fearer of the unknown, but wanted to overcome it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - a person who faces danger or fear without being afraid
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage and is easily frightened
