breadline

noun
queue for free food
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a line of people waiting to receive free food, typically during a time of poverty or unemployment

Examples

Waiting in the breadline
By dusk, the breadline outside the shelter stretched down the block.
During the Great Depression, many families waited in the breadline for their next meal.
Photographs from the era show people standing in a breadline in the freezing cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soup kitchen - a place where free food is served to those in need
