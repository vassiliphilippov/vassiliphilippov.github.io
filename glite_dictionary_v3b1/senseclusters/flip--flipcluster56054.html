flip

adjective
disrespectful or flippant in attitude
Pronunciation: / flɪp / (AmE), / flɪp / (BrE)
behaving in a casual or disrespectful way; lacking seriousness, often in a manner that shows little regard for others' feelings or the situation

Examples

A flip answer
Don’t be so flip when answering important questions in class.
His flip remarks during the meeting annoyed everyone who expected a more serious discussion.
She gave a flip response that made it clear she didn't care about the rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flippant - not showing a serious or respectful attitude
  • antonym: respectful - showing respect or esteem; courteous
