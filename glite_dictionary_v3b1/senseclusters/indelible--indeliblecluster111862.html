Examples

Synonyms

  • ineffaceable
    9.9 0.01/m
    ineffaceable is impossible to erase and sounds formal or literary, while indelible is common and used for lasting marks or memories.
  • unerasable
    7.1 0.01/m
    unerasable is a plain, literal word for marks that cannot be wiped out, while indelible is more usual for memories and prints.
  • inexpungible
    9.9 0.00/m
    inexpungible is a rare, legal-sounding term for things you cannot remove, while indelible is the common, everyday word for lasting marks.
