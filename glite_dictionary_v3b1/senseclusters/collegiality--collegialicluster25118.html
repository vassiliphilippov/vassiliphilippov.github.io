collegiality

noun
cooperation among colleagues
6.5
Pronunciation: /kə.liː.ˈdʒæ.lɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /kə.liː.ˈdʒæ.lɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
a cooperative and respectful way of working together, where colleagues share responsibility and decision-making

Examples

Work collegiality together
The university encourages collegiality among faculty members to create a positive work environment.
Strong collegiality can lead to better collaboration and more innovative ideas in the workplace.
Despite their differences, the team maintained a sense of collegiality throughout the project.
The university values collegiality and expects faculty members to participate in shared governance.
A spirit of collegiality made the team more effective and improved workplace morale.
Without collegiality, important decisions might be made without input from all members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperation - the act of working together for a common purpose
  • antonym: hostility - unfriendly or antagonistic behavior
  • synonym: teamwork - the combined effort of a group to achieve a goal
