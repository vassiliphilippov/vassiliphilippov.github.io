assignable

adjective
able to be legally transferred
2.8
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪn.ə.bəl / (AmE), / əˈsaɪn.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a right or property) able to be legally transferred to the ownership of another.

Examples

Assignable rights
Only assignable rights can be transferred in this contract.
The company holds several assignable patents valuable to potential buyers.
The lease is assignable, subject to the landlord's written consent.
