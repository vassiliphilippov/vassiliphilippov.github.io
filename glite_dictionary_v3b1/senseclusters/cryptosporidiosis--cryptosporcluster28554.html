cryptosporidiosis

noun
parasitic infection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊˌspɔːrɪdiˈoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəʊˌspɒrɪdiˈəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an illness caused by infection with Cryptosporidium parasites, often resulting in diarrhea and spread through contaminated water

Examples

Cryptosporidiosis infection
After swimming in a contaminated pool, several children developed cryptosporidiosis and needed medical attention.
Doctors warned that untreated drinking water could be a source of cryptosporidiosis outbreaks in the community.
Symptoms of cryptosporidiosis include stomach cramps, diarrhea, and dehydration, especially in young children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diarrhea - frequent, watery bowel movements
  • synonym: giardiasis - an intestinal infection caused by Giardia parasites
