La Plata

proper noun
estuary between Argentina and Uruguay
Pronunciation: / lɑ ˈplɑtə / (AmE), / lɑ ˈplɑːtə / (BrE)
an estuary in southeastern South America, forming part of the border between Argentina and Uruguay, where the Paraná and Uruguay rivers meet the Atlantic Ocean.

Examples

La Plata estuary
Many migratory birds stop at La Plata during their seasonal journeys.
The ship sailed up the La Plata estuary towards Buenos Aires.
The waters of La Plata are influenced by both river and ocean currents.
