tunefully

adverb
in a melodious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːn.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈtjuːn.fə.li / (BrE)
In a melodious or pleasing musical manner.

Examples

To whistle tunefully
He whistled tunefully as he walked down the street.
She played the piano tunefully, captivating the entire audience.
The birds sang tunefully outside my window every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melodiously - in a musical, pleasant-sounding way
