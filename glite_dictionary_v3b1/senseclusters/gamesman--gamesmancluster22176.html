gamesman

noun
shrewd manipulator
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmzˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who seeks advantage by using clever, strategic, or sometimes unethical tactics, especially in politics or business

Examples

Political gamesman
The experienced gamesman always knew how to turn negotiations in his favor.
In politics, a skilled gamesman can outmaneuver opponents with clever tactics.
She was wary of the office gamesman who manipulated situations to his advantage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: operator - a person skilled at getting what they want, often by manipulation
  • synonym: schemer - someone who makes secret plans to achieve their own ends
  • antonym: straight-shooter - a person who is honest and direct
