stunt

noun
difficult or dangerous feat for attention
Pronunciation: / stʌnt / (AmE), / stʌnt / (BrE)
a difficult, unusual, or dangerous action, usually performed to attract attention or entertain people

Examples

A dangerous stunt
He became famous for his daring motorcycle stunts.
The actor performed a dangerous stunt without using a double.
The magician wowed the crowd with an impressive stunt involving fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trick - an action intended to deceive or impress, often used in context of performance
