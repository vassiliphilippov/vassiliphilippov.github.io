garlic sauce

noun
sauce flavored with garlic
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk sɔs / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk sɔːs / (BrE)
A sauce made with garlic as the main flavoring ingredient, often referring to garlic mayonnaise or similar sauces.

Examples

A side of garlic sauce
Many Mediterranean dishes are served with a side of garlic sauce.
She added a generous amount of garlic sauce to her kebab for extra flavor.
The fries tasted much better after being dipped in garlic sauce.
