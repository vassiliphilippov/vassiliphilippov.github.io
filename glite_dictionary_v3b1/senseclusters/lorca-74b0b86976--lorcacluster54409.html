Lorca

proper noun
Spanish poet and dramatist (1898–1936)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrkə / (AmE), / ˈlɔːkə / (BrE)
Federico García Lorca (1898–1936), a prominent Spanish poet and dramatist, known for his influential works and tragic death during the Spanish Civil War.
