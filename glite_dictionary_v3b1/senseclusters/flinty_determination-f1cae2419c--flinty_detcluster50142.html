flinty determination

noun
unyielding determination; resolute attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪnti ˌdɛtərˈmɪneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈflɪnti ˌdɛtəˈmɪneɪʃən / (BrE)
A resolute and unyielding attitude or determination, likened to the hardness of flint.

Examples

Flinty determination never wavers
Despite countless setbacks, her flinty determination never wavered.
He faced criticism with flinty determination, refusing to back down.
It was only his flinty determination that carried him through the arduous training.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grim determination - a similarly unwavering resolve
