villous

adjective
hairy or fuzzy
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪloʊs / (AmE), / ˈvɪləs / (BrE)
covered with or resembling soft, long hairs or tiny projections

Examples

Villous plant stem

Some mushrooms have a villous surface that feels fuzzy to the touch.
The caterpillar's villous body helps protect it from predators.
