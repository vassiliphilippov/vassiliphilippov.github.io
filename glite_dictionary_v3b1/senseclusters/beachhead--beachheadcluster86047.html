beachhead

noun
initial achievement enabling further advances
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌhɛd / (BrE)
an initial achievement that paves the way for further progress in a particular activity or field

Examples

Business beachhead
Securing young talent is seen as a beachhead in strengthening the company's research capacity.
The new store serves as a beachhead for the brand's expansion across the country.
This partnership with a local company will give us a beachhead in the Asian market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foothold - a secure position from which further progress can be made
