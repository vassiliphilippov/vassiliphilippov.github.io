beachhead

noun
initial achievement enabling further advances
8.2 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbiÀêt ÉÀåh…õd / (AmE), / ÀàbiÀêt ÉÀåh…õd / (BrE)

Examples

Business beachhead
