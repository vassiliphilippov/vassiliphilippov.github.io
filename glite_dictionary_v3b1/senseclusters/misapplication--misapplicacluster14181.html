misapplication

noun
fraudulent use of entrusted funds/property
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the dishonest or improper use of funds or property entrusted to one's care, especially for personal gain

Examples

Misapplication of funds
His misapplication of client assets led to a lengthy investigation.
The accountant was charged with the misapplication of company funds.
The trust sued the fiduciary for the misapplication of property entrusted to him.
