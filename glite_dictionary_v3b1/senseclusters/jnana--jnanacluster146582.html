jnana

noun
spiritual wisdom
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɑːnə / (BrE)
spiritual knowledge or wisdom, especially as emphasized in Hindu philosophy

Examples

Jnana yoga practice
The path of jnana is one of self-inquiry and deep understanding in Hindu philosophy.
She devoted her life to seeking jnana through meditation and study of sacred texts.
Many spiritual teachers emphasize the importance of jnana for achieving enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • synonym: wisdom - the ability to use knowledge and experience to make good decisions
