woof

verb
to bark like a dog
Pronunciation: / wʊf / (AmE), / wʊf / (BrE)
to make a barking sound like a dog

Examples

To woof loudly
The puppy started to woof excitedly when it saw its owner.
At night, you could hear the neighbor's dog woofing at passing cars.
She laughed as her little brother tried to woof like their pet dog.
The puppy woofed excitedly when it saw its owner come home.
Every time someone passed the gate, the guard dog woofed to warn them away.
She laughed when her little dog woofed at its own reflection in the mirror.
