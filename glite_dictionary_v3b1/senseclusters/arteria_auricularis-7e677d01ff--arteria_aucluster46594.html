arteria auricularis

noun
artery supplying blood to the ear
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪəriə ɔːˌrɪkjəˈlɛərɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ɔːˌrɪkjʊˈleərɪs / (BrE)
An artery that supplies blood to the ear.

Examples

Arteria auricularis artery
Damage to the arteria auricularis can lead to inadequate blood supply in the ear region.
In anatomy class, we traced the path of the arteria auricularis in the human head.
The arteria auricularis branches off to deliver blood directly to the outer ear.
