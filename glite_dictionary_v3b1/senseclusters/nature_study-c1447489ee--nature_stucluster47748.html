nature study

noun
the study of animals and plants in the wild
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪtʃɚ ˌstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈneɪtʃə ˌstʌdi / (BrE)
the observation and study of animals and plants in their natural environment, typically as part of elementary education

Examples

Nature study in school
He developed a love for science through nature study during his childhood.
Our class went on a nature study trip to learn about local wildlife.
The school offers weekly nature study sessions in the nearby woods.
