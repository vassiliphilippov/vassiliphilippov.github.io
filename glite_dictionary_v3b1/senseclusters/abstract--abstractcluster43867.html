abstract

verb
make a summary of a work
3.1
Pronunciation: / æbˈstrækt / (AmE), / æbˈstrækt / (BrE)
to make a summary of a written work, research, or speech; to produce an abstract of something.

Examples

To abstract a paper
Before publication, authors are asked to abstract their thesis in no more than 300 words.
Researchers must abstract the key findings from each article they review.
The librarian will abstract the report so that readers can quickly grasp its contents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points of something
