Roccellaceae

noun
family of lichen-forming fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: /rɑːk.sə.ˈleɪ.siː/ (AmE), /rɒk.sə.ˈleɪ.siː/ (BrE)
a family of fungi in the division Lichenes, most of which form lichens with green algae, though some species grow on other lichens
