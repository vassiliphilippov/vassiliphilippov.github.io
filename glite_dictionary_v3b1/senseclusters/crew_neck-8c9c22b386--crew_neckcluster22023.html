crew neck

noun
round, plain neckline style
6.3
Pronunciation: / kru nɛk / (AmE), / kruː nɛk / (BrE)
a round, plain neckline that sits close to the base of the neck, typically found on sweaters and t-shirts

Examples

A crew neck sweater
He bought a simple black crew neck sweater for the chilly evening.
Most school uniforms require students to wear white crew neck shirts.
This t-shirt has a classic crew neck that goes well with most jackets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: V-neck - a neckline with a V-shaped cut in the front
  • degree lower: scoop neck - a neckline with a wide, low, rounded shape
