Synonyms

  • refusal
    1.4 11/m
    refusal is an act of saying no to an offer or request, while rejection is the broader state of being turned down.
  • nonacceptance
    6.5 0.03/m
    nonacceptance is a formal term for not accepting an offer or idea, while rejection is the common word for being refused.
  • turndown
    4.6 0.12/m
    turndown is a casual word for refusing an offer, while rejection covers many kinds of refusing or dismissal in different situations.
  • rebuff
    7.7 0.34/m
    rebuff is an abrupt, often rude refusal of a request or advance, while rejection is a general refusal that may be less sharp.
  • denial
    2.8 6.21/m
    denial is refusing to accept a fact or claim as true, while rejection is refusing an offer, person, or idea.
  • resistance
    4.0 18/m
    resistance is active opposition or effort to stop something, while rejection is a decision not to accept or approve it.
