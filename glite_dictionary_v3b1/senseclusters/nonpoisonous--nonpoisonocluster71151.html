nonpoisonous

adjective
not poisonous; safe to eat or touch
1.4 2.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈpɔɪ.zən.əs/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈpɔɪ.zən.əs/ (BrE)
not containing or producing poison; not harmful or toxic if touched, eaten, or used
