Steinway

proper noun
piano brand
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪnˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈstaɪnˌweɪ / (BrE)
a famous brand of high-quality pianos made by Steinway & Sons, known for their craftsmanship and sound

Examples

A Steinway piano
The world-renowned pianist insisted on playing a Steinway for her recital.
Many music schools choose a Steinway for their concert halls because of its rich sound.
He saved for years to buy a grand Steinway for his living room.
