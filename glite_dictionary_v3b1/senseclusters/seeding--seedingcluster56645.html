seeding

noun
sharing files online
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of making a complete file available for others to download, especially in peer-to-peer networks or file sharing systems

Examples

Seeding a file online
After finishing the download, he left the client running to continue seeding the file.
Good seeding helps other users download large files more quickly in peer-to-peer networks.
The community encourages seeding to keep popular files available for everyone.
