raptus hemorrhagicus

noun
seizure from sudden heavy bleeding
Pronunciation: / ˈræptəs hɛməˈrædʒɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈraptəs hɛməˈraedʒɪkəs / (BrE)
A sudden seizure caused by a sudden, heavy loss of blood.

Examples

A raptus hemorrhagicus seizure
A raptus hemorrhagicus can be life-threatening without immediate medical intervention.
Doctors rushed to control the bleeding to prevent another raptus hemorrhagicus.
The patient experienced a raptus hemorrhagicus following the traumatic injury.
