endovenous

adjective
within or by means of a vein
Pronunciation: / ˌendoʊˈvinəs / (AmE), / ˌendə(ʊ)ˈviːnəs / (BrE)
(of a procedure or occurrence) taking place within or by means of a vein.

Examples

Endovenous treatment
Endovenous treatment is often less invasive than traditional open surgery.
Doctors may recommend endovenous therapy as an alternative to vein stripping.
The surgeon performed an endovenous ablation to treat the patient's varicose veins.
