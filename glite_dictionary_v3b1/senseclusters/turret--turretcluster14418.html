turret

noun
rotating weapons platform
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.ət / (AmE), / ˈtʌr.ɪt / (BrE)
A self-contained, usually rotating structure on a military vehicle, ship, or aircraft, housing guns or other weapons.

Examples

Tank turret
Most modern warships are equipped with at least one heavy turret for their main armament.
The gunner climbed into the turret and prepared the weapon for firing.
The tank's turret rotated swiftly to aim at the approaching enemy vehicle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casemate - an armored gun position or room on a warship
  • synonym: gun emplacement - a fixed position for mounting guns
