dourness

noun
stern or gloomy quality
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊrnəs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊənəs / (BrE)
the quality of being stern, gloomy, or unyielding in manner or appearance

Examples

The dourness of face
The dourness of the old teacher made students nervous in class.
Despite the celebration, his dourness remained unchanged throughout the evening.
Her dourness contrasted sharply with her friend's lively personality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being happy and optimistic
  • synonym: severity - the quality of being strict or harsh
