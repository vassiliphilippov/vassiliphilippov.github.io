antiglare

adjective
reducing glare
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɡlɛr / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈɡlɛə / (BrE)
designed to reduce or prevent glare from light, especially on surfaces like screens or glass

Examples

Antiglare screen protector
The antiglare screen protector made it easier to use my phone outdoors.
Many modern cars have antiglare mirrors to improve nighttime driving safety.
He bought antiglare glasses to reduce eye strain while working at his computer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glare - strong, harsh light that makes it difficult to see
  • synonym: nonreflective - not reflecting light; reducing glare
