puggy

adjective
short and chubby
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡi / (BrE)
informal term describing someone or something as short and chubby in appearance

Examples

A puggy kitten
The kitten looked especially puggy after eating a big meal.
His puggy cheeks made everyone smile at the family reunion.
She picked out the most puggy teddy bear from the shelf.
