Asante

proper noun
West African kingdom
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈsɑːnteɪ / (AmE), / əˈsɑːnteɪ / (BrE)
a historical West African kingdom and empire located in present-day Ghana, known for its power and influence from the 17th to 19th centuries

Examples

Asante kingdom in Ghana
The Asante were renowned for their advanced political system and military strength.
British forces eventually defeated the Asante kingdom in the late 19th century.
Many cultural traditions in Ghana today trace their roots to the Asante empire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ashanti - another name for the Asante people or kingdom
  • synonym: Ghana - the modern country where the Asante kingdom was located
