cabriole

noun
ballet leap or curved leg
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkæbriˈoʊl /; / ˈkæbriˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæbrɪəʊl /; / ˈkæbriəʊl / (BrE)
a leap in ballet where one leg is extended and the other beats against it in the air
