scale up

verb
increase proportionally in size or amount
Pronunciation: / skeɪl ʌp / (AmE), / skeɪl ʌp / (BrE)
to increase something in size or amount, especially in a way that maintains its proportions or effectiveness

Examples

To scale up production
It can be difficult to scale up a project without additional resources.
Researchers hope to scale up the experiment for industrial applications.
The company plans to scale up production to meet increasing demand.
