low-powered

adjective
having little power or energy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊˈpaʊɚd / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
Having or using little power or energy; not powerful.

Examples

A low-powered engine
He drove a low-powered scooter that struggled to climb steep hills.
The factory replaced its low-powered machines with more efficient ones.
This low-powered signal won't reach the entire city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: powerful - having great power or strength
