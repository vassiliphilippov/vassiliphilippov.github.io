ethnohistoric

adjective
about cultural history
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊhɪˈstɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊhɪˈstɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of cultures by combining historical records with ethnographic methods

Examples

An ethnohistoric study
The ethnohistoric approach helped the team understand how traditions changed over time.
She published an ethnohistoric analysis of the region's indigenous communities.
Many ethnohistoric studies rely on both oral histories and written documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethnographic - relating to the scientific description of peoples and cultures
  • synonym: historical - relating to history or past events
