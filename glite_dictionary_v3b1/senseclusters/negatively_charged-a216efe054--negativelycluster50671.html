negatively charged

adjective
having a negative electric charge
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪvli tʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪvli tʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
Having an excess of electrons, resulting in a negative electric charge.

Examples

Negatively charged ion
A negatively charged ion is called an anion in chemistry.
Lightning occurs when negatively charged particles move from the cloud to the ground.
The atom became negatively charged after gaining an extra electron.
