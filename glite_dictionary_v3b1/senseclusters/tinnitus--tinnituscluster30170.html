tinnitus

noun
ringing or buzzing in the ears
3.8
Pronunciation: / tɪˈnaɪtəs / (AmE), / tɪˈnɪtəs / (BrE)
A medical condition in which a person hears ringing, buzzing, or similar noises in one or both ears when no external sound is present.

Examples

Chronic tinnitus symptoms
After the rock concert, she experienced tinnitus that lasted for several hours.
Many people with hearing loss also suffer from tinnitus.
There is currently no cure for chronic tinnitus, but certain treatments can help manage the symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ringing in the ears - perception of sound when no external sound is present, especially ringing
