equalist

adjective
supporting equality
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkwəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəlɪst / (BrE)
believing in, promoting, or relating to the principle that all people should have equal rights and opportunities

Examples

Equalist policies
The organization follows an equalist approach to hiring, ensuring everyone has the same chance.
Her equalist beliefs inspired her to campaign for fair education policies.
Many young people today hold equalist views about gender and opportunity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - believing in or based on the principle that all people are equal
  • antonym: inegalitarian - not supporting or characterized by social equality
