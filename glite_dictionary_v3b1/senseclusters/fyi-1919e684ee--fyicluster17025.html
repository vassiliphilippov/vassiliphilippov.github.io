FYI

noun
informational message
5.9
Pronunciation: /ef.waɪ.ˈaɪ/ (AmE), /ef.waɪ.ˈaɪ/ (BrE)
a message or note sent to provide information, usually not requiring a response or action

Examples

An FYI email
He forwarded an FYI about the upcoming meeting to the whole team.
Just sending this FYI so you are aware of the new policy changes.
You do not need to reply to this FYI unless you have questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: notification - a message that gives information or a warning
