competence

noun
ability to do something well
4.1 14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêmp…ôt…ôns / (AmE), / Ààk…ímp…™t…ôns / (BrE)
the ability to do something successfully or efficiently; the state of being adequately or well qualified in knowledge and skill

Examples

Competence in skills
Demonstrating linguistic competence is essential when applying for the translator position.
Her competence as an engineer was quickly recognized by her colleagues.
The company hired him because of his proven competence in project management.

Synonyms

  • competency
    4.1 17/m
    competency is the same practical ability to do a job well, while competence emphasizes being qualified and effective in a role.
  • proficiency
    4.7 14/m
    proficiency shows a high level of practiced skill, while competence means being able and adequately qualified to do tasks.
  • skill
    1.0 221/m
    skill is a learned ability to do something well, while competence covers broader qualification and consistent effective performance.
  • skillfulness
    4.1 0.03/m
    skillfulness is the visible display of practiced skill, while competence refers to overall ability and being properly qualified.
  • ability
    1.0 195/m
    ability is a general power to do things, while competence implies trained, tested skill and reliable performance in tasks.
  • facility
    4.2 2.37/m
    facility means doing something easily and smoothly, while competence points to being qualified and effective at the required tasks.
  • know-how
    4.1 7.32/m
    know-how is practical technical knowledge for getting tasks done, while competence combines knowledge, skill, and proper qualification.
  • capability
    3.6 35/m
    capability is the potential or capacity to do something, whereas competence is the proven ability to do it well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ability - possession of the means or skills to do something
  • antonym: incompetence - lack of ability or qualification for a task
  • synonym: proficiency - advanced skill or expertise in a subject or activity
