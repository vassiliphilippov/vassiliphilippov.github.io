reflash

noun
firmware update process
Pronunciation: / ˈriːflæʃ / (AmE), / ˈriːflæʃ / (BrE)
the act or process of rewriting or updating the software stored in a device's memory, especially firmware

Examples

A reflash of firmware
A reflash of the smartphone's firmware fixed the persistent software bugs.
The mechanic recommended a reflash to update the car's engine control unit.
Before selling the device, he performed a reflash to ensure it had the latest software.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firmware update - the process of installing new software on a device's firmware
  • antonym: rollback - the process of restoring software to a previous version
