clipboard

noun
temporary computer storage for copied data
2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈklɪp.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈklɪp.boʊrd/ (AmE), /ˈklɪp.bɔːd/ (BrE)
a temporary area in a computer's memory where data that has been copied or cut is held until it is pasted elsewhere.

Examples

Copy to clipboard
She copied the quote to the clipboard and pasted it into her report.
When you cut the image, it replaces the previous item on the clipboard immediately.
Multiple apps can access the system clipboard to share copied text or images between programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffer - a region of memory used to hold data temporarily (clipboard is a specific kind of buffer)
  • synonym: pasteboard - the term used on some systems (notably macOS) for the clipboard — a temporary area holding copied items
