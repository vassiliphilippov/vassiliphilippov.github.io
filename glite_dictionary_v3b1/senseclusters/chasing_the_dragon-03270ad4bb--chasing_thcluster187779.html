chasing the dragon

verb
drug addiction or futile pursuit
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ ðə ˈdræɡən / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ ðə ˈdræɡən / (BrE)
Using drugs, especially by inhaling the vapor from heated opiates; also, persistently pursuing an unattainable or elusive goal, often to one's detriment.

Examples

To chase the dragon habit
After years of chasing the dragon, he finally sought help to overcome his addiction.
For some, trying to relive past glory is like chasing the dragon—it never quite feels the same.
The documentary revealed the dangers of chasing the dragon and its toll on communities.
