bond

verb
stick or hold together firmly
3.2 1.93/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘nd / (AmE), / bÉ’nd / (BrE)
to stick or hold together firmly, especially by means of an adhesive or other force; to form a strong connection or attachment.

Examples

To bond two surfaces
Once the paint dries, it bonded strongly to the metal surface.
The glue will help the pieces bond together overnight.
These two substances bond on a molecular level to create a durable material.

  • stick

  • glue

  • attach
    2.5 39/m

  • fasten
    1.6 6.55/m

  • affix
    5.0 2.32/m

  • hold together
    3.4 5.02/m

  • connect
