paddle one's own canoe

verb
be independent or self-reliant
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəl wʌnz oʊn kəˈnu / (AmE), / ˈpædəl wʌnz əʊn kəˈnuː / (BrE)

You won't learn responsibility until you paddle your own canoe.
