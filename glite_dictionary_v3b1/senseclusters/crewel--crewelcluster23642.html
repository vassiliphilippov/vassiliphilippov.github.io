crewel

noun
wool embroidery style
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːəl / (AmE), / ˈkruːəl / (BrE)
a type of embroidery made with loosely twisted wool yarn, or a textile decorated in this style

Examples

Crewel tapestry art
The museum displayed a tapestry featuring intricate crewel designs from the 18th century.
She learned how to do crewel as part of her textile arts class.
The pillow was decorated with colorful crewel depicting flowers and birds.
