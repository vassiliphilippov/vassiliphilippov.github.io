unavailingly

adverb
without success
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈveɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈveɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not achieve the intended result; without success

Examples

Argue unavailingly
He argued unavailingly for a change in the policy, but nothing was done.
They searched unavailingly for the missing keys all afternoon.
She waited unavailingly for a reply to her letter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: successfully - in a way that achieves the intended result
  • synonym: unsuccessfully - in a way that does not succeed
