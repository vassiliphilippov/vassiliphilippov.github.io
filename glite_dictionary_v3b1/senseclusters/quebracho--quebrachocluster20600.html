quebracho

noun
tannin-rich wood extract
9.9
Pronunciation: / keɪˈbrɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / keɪˈbrætʃəʊ / (BrE)
a strong, tannin-rich extract from the wood of quebracho trees, used in tanning leather and making adhesives

Examples

Quebracho extract
The tannery treated the leather with concentrated quebracho to ensure a deep, durable finish.
Many adhesives are made using extracts from quebracho because of its strong binding properties.
The export of quebracho has been important for the leather industry in South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tannin - a natural substance used in tanning leather
