potentially

adverb
possibly, with the capacity to happen
1.8 2.8
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈten.ʃə.liː/ /; / /pə.ˈten.ʃə.lɪ/ (AmE), /pə.ˈten.ʃə.lɪ/ /; / /pə.ˈten.ʃə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows the possibility of becoming actual or real; possibly, but not yet achieved or existing

Examples
