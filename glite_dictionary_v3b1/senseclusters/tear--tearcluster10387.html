Synonyms

  • rip
    1.9 1.11/m
    rip is a sudden, forceful pulling that makes a hole in cloth or paper, while tear is a general word for such a split.
  • rent
    8.4 0.78/m
    rent is a formal or older word for an opening made by pulling fabric apart, while tear is the common modern term.
  • split
    1.1 1.11/m
    split is a break that can be a crack or division in many things, while tear refers to a rip made by force in material.
