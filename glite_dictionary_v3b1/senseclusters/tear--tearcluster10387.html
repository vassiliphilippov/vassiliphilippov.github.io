tear

noun
an act of tearing
4.3 1.0
Pronunciation: / tɛr / (AmE), / tɪə / (BrE)
a rip, split, or the act of tearing something apart by force

Examples

There was a large tear down the side of the paper.
Can you fix the tear in my jeans before the party tonight?
She noticed a small tear in the sleeve of her jacket after brushing against the fence.
The curtains had a tear near the bottom where the cat scratched them.
