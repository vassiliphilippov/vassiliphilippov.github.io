nut

noun
metal block with screw thread for bolts
Pronunciation: / nʌt / (AmE), / nʌt / (BrE)
a small, usually square or hexagonal, metal block with an internal screw thread, used for securing a bolt

Examples

A metal nut
Make sure the nut fits the threaded end of the bolt properly.
The nut became loose, so the wheel started rattling.
Use a wrench to tighten the nut onto the bolt securely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bolt - a metal fastener with a threaded shaft, used with a nut
