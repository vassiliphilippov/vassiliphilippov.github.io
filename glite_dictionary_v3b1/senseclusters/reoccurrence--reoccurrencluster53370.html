reoccurrence

noun
happening again
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈkɜːrəns / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈkʌrəns / (BrE)
the act or instance of something happening again or being repeated

Examples

A reoccurrence of problems
The reoccurrence of the symptoms worried the doctors.
After months of peace, the reoccurrence of conflict surprised everyone.
They took extra precautions to prevent the reoccurrence of the issue.
The reoccurrence of the error caused delays in the project.
Doctors are concerned about the reoccurrence of symptoms after treatment.
Regular maintenance can help prevent the reoccurrence of similar issues in the future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cessation - the process of ending or being brought to an end
  • antonym: continuation - the process of continuing without stopping
  • synonym: recurrence - the act of something happening again
  • synonym: return - the act of coming back or happening again
