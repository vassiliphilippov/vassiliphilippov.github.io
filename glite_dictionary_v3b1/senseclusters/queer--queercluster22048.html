queer

verb
to spoil plans
9.1
Pronunciation: / kwɪr / (AmE), / kwɪə / (BrE)
to spoil, ruin, or put in jeopardy, especially a plan, arrangement, or situation

Examples

To queer a deal
His careless remark queered the deal for everyone.
She was afraid that one mistake might queer her chances of getting the job.
The sudden rainstorm queered our plans for a picnic in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ruin - to destroy or spoil something completely
  • synonym: sabotage - to deliberately damage or destroy something
  • antonym: save - to prevent something from being ruined or lost
