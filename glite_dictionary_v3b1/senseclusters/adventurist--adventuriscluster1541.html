adventurist

noun
risk-seeking person
6.8
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃərɪst / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person who seeks excitement and new experiences, often by taking risks

Examples

An adventurist traveler
As an adventurist, she traveled to remote places few people had ever seen.
The adventurist in him could not resist the challenge of climbing the mountain.
Many adventurists are drawn to extreme sports for the thrill and excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurer - a person who enjoys or seeks adventure
  • antonym: cautious person - someone who avoids risks and danger
  • synonym: risk-taker - someone who is willing to take risks
