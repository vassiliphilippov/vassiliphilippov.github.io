Hispanism

noun
promotion of Spanish culture
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪspəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪspənɪzəm / (BrE)
a political or cultural movement or attitude that promotes the language, culture, or influence of Spain

Examples

Hispanism movement
The rise of Hispanism in the region led to more Spanish language classes in schools.
Many cultural festivals reflect the influence of Hispanism through music, dance, and food.
His interest in Hispanism inspired him to study Spanish history and literature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Anglicism - a movement or tendency promoting English language or culture
  • synonym: pan-Hispanism - the advocacy of unity among Spanish-speaking peoples
