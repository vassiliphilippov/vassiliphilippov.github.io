Hispanism

noun
promotion of Spanish culture
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪspəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪspənɪzəm / (BrE)
a political or cultural movement or attitude that promotes the language, culture, or influence of Spain
