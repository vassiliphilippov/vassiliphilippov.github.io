dislikable

adjective
causing or deserving dislike
3.7
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈlaɪk.ə.bəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈlaɪk.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Likely to be disliked; causing or deserving dislike.

Examples

Dislikable character

His rude behavior made him a very dislikable person at the office.
The character in the novel was so dislikable that readers felt little sympathy for her.
