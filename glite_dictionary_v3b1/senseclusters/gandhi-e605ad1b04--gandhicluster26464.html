Gandhi

proper noun
Indian Prime Minister (Indira Gandhi, 1917-1984)
4.3 1.49/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…°…ëÀêndi / (AmE), / Àà…°andi / (BrE)
(Indira Gandhi) The first female Prime Minister of India, daughter of Jawaharlal Nehru, who held office from 1966 to 1977 and again from 1980 until her assassination in 1984.

Examples

Prime Minister Gandhi
Gandhi was India's first and, to date, only female Prime Minister.
Many people admired Gandhi for her determination and strong leadership.
The assassination of Gandhi in 1984 shocked the entire nation.
