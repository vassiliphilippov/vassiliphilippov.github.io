poison ivy

noun
skin rash from poison ivy plant
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzən ˌaɪvi / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən ˌaɪvi / (BrE)
A form of skin inflammation caused by contact with the poison ivy plant, typically resulting in an itchy rash.

Examples

Poison ivy rash
After hiking through the woods, he realized he had developed poison ivy on his arms and legs.
She was absent from work because her poison ivy had become severely inflamed.
The doctor prescribed a cream to soothe her poison ivy reaction.
