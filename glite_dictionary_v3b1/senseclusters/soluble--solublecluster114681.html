soluble

adjective
able to be dissolved in a liquid

(of a substance) able to be dissolved in a liquid, especially water.

Examples

Soluble in water
Not all vitamins are soluble in water; some require fat to be absorbed.
Salt is soluble in water and dissolves quickly when stirred.
Sugar is highly soluble, which is why it disappears so easily in tea.
