soluble

adjective
able to be dissolved in a liquid
3.7 1.94/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀêlj…ôb…ôl / (AmE), / Ààs…ílj äb…ôl / (BrE)
(of a substance) able to be dissolved in a liquid, especially water.

Examples

Soluble in water
Not all vitamins are soluble in water; some require fat to be absorbed.
Salt is soluble in water and dissolves quickly when stirred.
Sugar is highly soluble, which is why it disappears so easily in tea.

Synonyms

  • water-soluble
    3.9 0.78/m
    water-soluble is able to dissolve in water specifically, while soluble can mean able to dissolve in liquids, especially water.
  • dissolvable
    3.5 0.22/m
    dissolvable is capable of being broken down in a liquid, while soluble usually refers to substances that dissolve, often in water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insoluble - unable to be dissolved in a liquid
