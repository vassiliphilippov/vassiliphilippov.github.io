false pimpernel

noun
a small weedy plant with dry leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈpɪmpərˌnɛl / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈpɪmpənɛl / (BrE)
a small weedy plant with short, dry, chafflike leaves, not actually a true pimpernel

Examples

The false pimpernel plant
Gardeners sometimes confuse false pimpernel with the true pimpernel due to their similar appearance.
The false pimpernel thrives in dry, sandy soils where few other plants can grow.
We found a patch of false pimpernel growing beside the old stone wall.
