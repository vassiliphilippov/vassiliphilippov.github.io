phase

verb
adjust into synchronization
7.6
Pronunciation: / feɪz / (AmE), / feɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or carry out something in stages or in synchrony with something else

Examples

To phase a project
Engineers had to phase the new software update with the existing system to avoid downtime.
The lighting effects are carefully phased to match the rhythm of the music.
We need to phase our deliveries so that they arrive just as the shelves are empty.
The company decided to phase the introduction of new technology over six months.
The government plans to phase their withdrawal from the region in three stages.
We will phase the project to minimize disruption for our staff.

  • synonym: implement - to put into effect or carry out
