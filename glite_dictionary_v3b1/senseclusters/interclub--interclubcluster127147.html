interclub

adjective
between clubs
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈklʌb / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈklʌb / (BrE)
relating to or taking place between two or more clubs

Examples

Interclub match
The annual interclub chess competition attracted teams from across the city.
She enjoys participating in interclub debates to meet students from other schools.
An interclub soccer match was scheduled for Saturday afternoon.
