froggish

adjective
hoarse or croaky voice
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡɪʃ / (BrE)
having a hoarse, rough, or croaky-sounding voice, like that of a frog

Examples

Froggish voice
After cheering at the game, his voice became froggish and he could barely speak.
She woke up with a froggish tone to her voice after catching a cold.
The teacher's froggish voice made it hard for the students to hear her instructions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free from roughness or hoarseness in sound
  • synonym: hoarse - having a rough or harsh-sounding voice
  • synonym: raspy - having a harsh, grating sound
