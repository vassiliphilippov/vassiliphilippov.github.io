circle

noun
road junction with circular traffic flow

A road junction at which traffic moves in one direction around a central island.

Examples

Traffic circle junction
Drivers often slow down too much when approaching a circle for the first time.
The city recently installed a new circle to improve the traffic flow at the intersection.
There were clear signs directing vehicles through the circle outside the train station.

Synonyms

  • roundabout
    3.4 0.46/m
    roundabout directs traffic smoothly with yield rules and short entries, while circle is a general term for a circular junction.
  • traffic circle
    2.8 0.17/m
    traffic circle is often larger and has different entering rules, while circle names any circular road junction.
  • rotary
    4.3 0.16/m
    rotary is a regional US term for older, often bigger circular junctions, while circle is the neutral general name.
