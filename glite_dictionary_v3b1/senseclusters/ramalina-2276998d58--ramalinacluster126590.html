Ramalina

noun
genus of shrubby lichens
Pronunciation: / ræməˈlaɪnə /; / ˌræməˈlaɪnə / (AmE), / ræməˈliːnə /; / ˌraməˈlʌɪnə / (BrE)
a genus of shrubby lichens in the family Usneaceae, characterized by a flattened, often branching thallus
