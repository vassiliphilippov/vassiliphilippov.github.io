arctangent

noun
inverse function of the tangent
Pronunciation: / ɑrktændʒənt / (AmE), / ɑːktændʒənt / (BrE)
the inverse function of the tangent; the angle whose tangent is a given number

Examples

The arctangent function
He asked his students to plot the graph of the arctangent function.
In trigonometry, the arctangent function is used to find angles from given ratios.
The calculator can determine the arctangent of any number you enter.
