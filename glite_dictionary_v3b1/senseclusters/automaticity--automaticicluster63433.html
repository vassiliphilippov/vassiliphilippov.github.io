automaticity

noun
performing without thinking
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˌməˈtɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˌməˈtɪsəti / (BrE)
the ability to perform an action or task automatically, without conscious thought, usually as a result of repeated practice

Examples

Automaticity in reading
After months of practice, typing became an act of automaticity for her.
Musicians rely on automaticity to play complex pieces without thinking about every note.
With enough repetition, reading can be done with automaticity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consciousness - the state of being aware of and able to think about one's actions
  • synonym: habit - a regular practice or routine, often done unconsciously
