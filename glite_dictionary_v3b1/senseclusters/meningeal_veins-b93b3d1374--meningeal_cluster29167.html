meningeal veins

noun
veins in the membranes around the brain and spinal cord
7.2
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪn.dʒi.əl veɪnz / (AmE), / məˈnɪn.dʒi.əl veɪnz / (BrE)
veins that accompany the meningeal arteries in the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord.

Examples

Meningeal veins near brain
Injury to the meningeal veins can contribute to the formation of epidural hematomas.
Surgeons must take care not to damage the meningeal veins during brain surgery.
The meningeal veins play an important role in draining blood from the dura mater.
