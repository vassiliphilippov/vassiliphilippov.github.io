cheerio

noun
friendly expression of farewell
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɪəˈrioʊ / (AmE), / ˌtʃɪəˈriəʊ / (BrE)
used as a friendly expression of farewell; goodbye.

Examples

Say cheerio goodbye
At the end of the call, she cheerfully ended with a quick cheerio.
They all shouted cheerio as the train pulled away from the station.
When he left the office, he waved and said cheerio to everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodbye - an expression used to bid someone farewell
  • antonym: hello - an expression used to greet someone
