SMO

noun
social media manager
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈəʊ / (BrE)

Examples

SMO content creator

As the SMO, she schedules posts and responds to comments on all social platforms.
Many businesses now rely on an SMO to handle their digital marketing strategies.
