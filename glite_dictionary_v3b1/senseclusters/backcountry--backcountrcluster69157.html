backcountry

adjective
remote rural areas
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌkʌntri / (AmE), / ˈbækˌkʌntri / (BrE)
relating to remote, undeveloped rural areas far from towns or cities, often used before a noun

Examples

Backcountry trail
They planned a backcountry hike that would take them far from any roads or towns.
The guide specializes in backcountry skiing trips for experienced adventurers.
You need special equipment for a safe backcountry camping experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
  • synonym: wilderness - a natural area that is not developed or inhabited
