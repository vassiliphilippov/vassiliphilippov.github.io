Empire

noun
a variety of apple
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.paɪr / (AmE), / ˈɛm.paɪə / (BrE)
A variety of eating apple, developed as a cross between McIntosh and Red Delicious, used both for eating fresh and for cooking.

Examples

The Empire apple
In the orchard, Empire apples were ready to be picked by late September.
The Empire apple is prized for its crisp texture and sweet-tart flavor.
We used Empire apples to bake the pie for a more refreshing taste.
