unspool

verb
to unwind from reel
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspuːl / (AmE), / ʌnˈspuːl / (BrE)
to unwind or release something, such as thread or film, from a spool or reel

Examples

To unspool thread
She carefully unspooled the film to avoid damaging it.
The fisherman began to unspool the line as he prepared his rod.
As the tape unspooled, the old recording played through the speakers.
She carefully unspooled the old film to examine each frame for damage.
The cable unspooled quickly as the technician lowered the equipment into the well.
He watched the thread unspool from the bobbin as the sewing machine ran.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unwind - to undo or loosen something that is wound
  • antonym: wind - to wrap or coil something around a spool or reel
