tebow

noun
kneeling in prayer
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːboʊ / (AmE), / ˈtiːbəʊ / (BrE)
a kneeling position on one knee, often in prayer or as a public gesture of thanks or humility

Examples

A quiet tebow
After scoring the winning goal, he paused for a moment in a quiet tebow on the field.
The photograph captured her in a tebow, showing gratitude after the announcement.
Many fans imitated the famous tebow gesture during the celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genuflection - the act of bending one knee to the ground as a sign of respect or worship
  • antonym: stand - to be in an upright position on the feet
