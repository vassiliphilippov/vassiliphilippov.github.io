sublimate

verb
make more subtle or refined
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbləˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌblɪmeɪt / (BrE)
to make something more subtle, refined, or elevated, especially in quality or character

Examples

To sublimate experience
Artists often sublimate personal experiences into their creative work.
She sought to sublimate her anger into careful, constructive criticism.
The teacher tried to sublimate the students' crude jokes into thoughtful humor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refine - to improve something by making small changes, especially to make it more subtle or precise
