Synonyms

    6.3 0.8/m
    enslavement describes being made into a slave or kept as one, while slavery is the wider system of owning and forcing people to work.
  • servitude
    8.9 1/m
    servitude is forced or low-paid work often used as punishment, while slavery is the system of owning people and forcing them to work.
  • slaveholding
    7.9 0.02/m
