metabolically

adverb
in a way relating to metabolism
Pronunciation: / məˌtæbəˈlɪkli / (AmE), / mɛtəˈbɒlɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to metabolism or the chemical processes within a living organism.

Examples

To be metabolically active
Some species are metabolically adapted to survive in extremely cold environments.

This compound is metabolically active in the liver.
