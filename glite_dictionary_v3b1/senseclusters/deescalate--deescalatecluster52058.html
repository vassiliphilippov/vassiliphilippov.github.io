deescalate

Examples

To deescalate a conflict
The police tried to deescalate the situation before it turned violent.
Tensions between the two countries began to deescalate after the peace talks.
She used calm words to deescalate the argument with her friend.
The police officer managed to deescalate the confrontation before it became violent.
