pommes

noun
French fries
4.1
Pronunciation: / pɑmz / (AmE), / pɒmz / (BrE)
thin slices of potato that are deep-fried and served hot, also known as French fries

Examples

Pommes frites
She ordered a plate of pommes to go with her burger.
The restaurant serves crispy pommes with a variety of sauces.
He prefers pommes over mashed potatoes as a side dish.
