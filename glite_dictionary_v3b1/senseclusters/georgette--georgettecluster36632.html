georgette

noun
thin crinkled dress fabric
Pronunciation: / dʒɔrˈʒɛt / (AmE), / dʒɔːˈʒɛt / (BrE)
A thin, lightweight, crinkled fabric, typically made from silk or polyester, used for dresses and other garments.

Examples

A georgette dress
She chose a beautiful blue georgette for her bridesmaid dress.
Summer blouses made of georgette are comfortable and stylish.
The lightweight georgette draped elegantly over her shoulders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chiffon - a light, sheer fabric similar to georgette
  • synonym: organza - a thin, stiff, sheer fabric used for dresses
