biosystematic

adjective
relating to biosystematics
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˌsɪs.təˈmæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˌsɪs.tɪˈmat.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study and classification of organisms by biosystematics, which integrates taxonomy with biological data, especially genetics and ecology.

Examples

Biosystematic study methods
Many recent studies employ biosystematic approaches to integrate molecular and morphological data.
Our laboratory specializes in biosystematic research to modernize plant classification systems.
The biosystematic analysis revealed new insights into the evolutionary relationships among bird species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biosystematics - the study of the classification of organisms using a range of biological data
