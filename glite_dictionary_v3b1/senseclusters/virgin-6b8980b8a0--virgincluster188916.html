Virgin

noun
person born under Virgo (astrology)
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
(astrology) A person born while the sun is in the astrological sign Virgo, typically considered to fall between August 23 and September 22.

Many Virgins are said to be practical and detail-oriented according to astrology.
