purl stitch

verb
to knit with a purl stitch
8.5 6.9
Pronunciation: /pɜːrl stɪtʃ/ (AmE), /pɜːl stɪtʃ/ (BrE)
to knit using the purl stitch, in which the yarn is brought to the front of the work before the needle is inserted.

Examples

To purl stitch a scarf
After learning to knit, he practiced how to purl stitch for his scarf.
She skillfully purl stitched the next row to create a textured pattern.
You should purl stitch every alternate row to achieve the ribbed effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: knit stitch - to knit using the basic knit stitch, the opposite technique to purl stitch
