ingratiatingly

adverb
in a flattering or sycophantic manner
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡreɪʃi.eɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪʃi.eɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner intended to gain favor or approval, often by using flattery or being overly agreeable.

Examples

To laugh ingratiatingly
He laughed ingratiatingly at every one of her jokes, even when they weren't funny.
She smiled ingratiatingly at the guests, hoping to win their approval.
The salesman spoke ingratiatingly, trying to charm the skeptical customer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatteringly - in a manner showing excessive or insincere praise
  • synonym: obsequiously - in an excessively eager way to please or obey
