barhop

verb
to visit multiple bars
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrˌhɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɑːˌhɒp / (BrE)
to visit several bars in one night, usually to have drinks at each place

Examples

To barhop all night
After the concert, they decided to barhop through the city center.
We used to barhop every Friday night when we were in college.
Instead of staying in one place, she prefers to barhop with friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pub crawl - an event where people visit several pubs or bars in succession
