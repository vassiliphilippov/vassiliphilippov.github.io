cantorial

adjective
of a cantor
Pronunciation: / kænˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / kænˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to a cantor, especially in the context of Jewish or Christian liturgical music

Examples

Cantorial music
She studied cantorial music to prepare for her role in the synagogue.
The choir performed a piece with a distinct cantorial style during the service.
His cantorial duties included leading prayers and singing traditional melodies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liturgical - relating to public worship or liturgies
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
