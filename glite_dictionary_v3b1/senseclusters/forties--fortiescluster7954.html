forties

noun
the decade 1940–1949
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.tiz / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1940 to 1949 in a particular century, especially the 20th century

Examples

The forties style
Fashion trends in the forties were influenced by wartime restrictions.
Many influential jazz musicians rose to fame during the forties.
The forties were marked by World War II and significant social change.
