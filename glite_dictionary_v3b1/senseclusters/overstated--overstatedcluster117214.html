overstated

adjective
exaggerated beyond accuracy
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsteɪtɪd / (BrE)
represented as greater than is true or reasonable; exaggerated beyond what is accurate

Examples

Clearly overstated claims
His claims about his achievements were clearly overstated.
The importance of this result should not be overstated.
The risks of the procedure were overstated in the media reports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggerated - represented as greater than is true or reasonable
  • antonym: understated - represented as less than is true or reasonable
