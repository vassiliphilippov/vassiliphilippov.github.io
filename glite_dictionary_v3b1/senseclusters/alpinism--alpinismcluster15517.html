alpinism

noun
the sport of mountain climbing
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.pɪˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈæl.pɪ.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
the sport or practice of climbing high mountains, especially by difficult routes, not limited to the Alps

Examples

Alpinism sport
He dedicated his life to alpinism and summited many of the world's highest peaks.
Modern alpinism emphasizes speed and technical skill on challenging routes.
The history of alpinism in the Himalayas is filled with stories of both triumph and tragedy.
