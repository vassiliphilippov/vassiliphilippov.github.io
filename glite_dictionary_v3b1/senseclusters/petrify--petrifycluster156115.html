petrify

verb
to paralyze with fear or shock
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make someone so frightened or shocked that they cannot move or think

Examples

To petrify with fear
His loud scream petrified everyone in the room.
She was so petrified during the thunderstorm that she couldn't move.
The sudden appearance of the snake petrified him with fear.
He was petrified with fear when he saw the snake in his bed.
The ghost story so petrified her that she could barely speak.
Thunder petrified the children, making them cling to their mother.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - to make someone peaceful and relaxed
  • synonym: paralyze - to make someone unable to move or act, especially from fear or shock
  • synonym: stun - to shock or surprise someone so that they cannot react immediately
  • synonym: terrify - to frighten someone greatly
