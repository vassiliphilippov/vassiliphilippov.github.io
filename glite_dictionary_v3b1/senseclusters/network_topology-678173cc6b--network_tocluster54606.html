network topology

noun
architecture of an artificial neural network
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈnet.wɜːrk təp.ˈɔː.lə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈnet.wɜːk təp.ˈɒ.lə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
The arrangement or architecture of an artificial neural network, especially the pattern of layers, units (neurons) and connections used for computation.

Examples

Neural network topology
Researchers compared how different network topology choices affected learning speed and generalization in the model.
She designed a sparse network topology with skip connections to improve gradient flow during training.
The conference paper analyzed how convolutional network topology variations influence accuracy on image recognition tasks.
