progressiveness

noun
support of social or political reform
3.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of favoring or promoting change, improvement, or reform, especially towards better conditions or policies

Examples

The progressiveness of ideas
Our company values progressiveness and encourages employees to propose innovative solutions.
She was praised for her progressiveness in supporting equal rights for all citizens.
The progressiveness of his ideas helped drive major educational reforms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservatism - opposition to rapid change or preference for tradition in politics or society
  • synonym: progressivism - support for or advocacy of social reform and progressive policies
