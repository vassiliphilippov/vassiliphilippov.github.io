henpecked

adjective
dominated or nagged by wife/partner
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnˌpɛkt / (AmE), / ˈhɛnˌpɛkt / (BrE)
(of a man) continually harassed, criticized, or controlled by his wife or female partner.

Examples

A henpecked husband

He grew tired of being henpecked and finally spoke up for himself.
The henpecked husband followed his wife's instructions without complaint.
