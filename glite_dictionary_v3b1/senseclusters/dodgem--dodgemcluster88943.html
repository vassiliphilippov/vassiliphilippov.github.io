dodgem

noun
small amusement park bumper car
9.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːdʒ.əm/ (AmE), /ˈdɒdʒ.əm/ (BrE)
A small, low-powered electric car, driven for fun on a special platform at amusement parks, where the aim is to avoid or bump into other cars.

Examples

A dodgem car
At the fair, we spent hours riding the dodgems and chasing each other in circles.
She tried to avoid a collision, but her dodgem was struck from behind.
The children laughed as they steered their dodgem around the crowded arena.
