creaseless

adjective
smooth, free from creases
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːsləs / (AmE), / ˈkriːslɪs / (BrE)
(of a fabric or surface) smooth and free from creases or wrinkles.

Examples

Smooth creaseless fabric
He preferred creaseless pants for a sharp, professional appearance.
Her creaseless blouse looked freshly ironed throughout the long meeting.
The new fabric promises to remain creaseless even after washing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wrinkled - having small lines or folds; creased or crumpled
