planisher

noun
metalworker who smooths
Pronunciation: / ˈplænɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈplænɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who smooths and polishes metal surfaces by hammering or pressing them

Examples

A skilled planisher
The planisher carefully hammered the silver tray until it gleamed.
As a skilled planisher, she restored old metal artifacts to their original shine.
Every planisher needs patience and a steady hand to achieve a flawless finish.

  • synonym: metalworker - a person who works with metal
