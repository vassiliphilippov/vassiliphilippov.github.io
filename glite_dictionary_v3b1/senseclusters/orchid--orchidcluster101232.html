orchid

noun
a plant with showy, unusual flowers
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
A plant of the large family Orchidaceae, typically having complex and often showy flowers with unusual shapes and vivid colors.

Examples

A orchid flower
He bought an orchid as a gift because of its unique appearance and long-lasting flowers.
Many collectors are fascinated by the rare and delicate beauty of the orchid.
The orchid on her windowsill bloomed in a dazzling shade of purple.
