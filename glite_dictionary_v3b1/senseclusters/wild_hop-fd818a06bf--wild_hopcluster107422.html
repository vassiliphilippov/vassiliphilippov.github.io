wild hop

noun
a climbing plant with red berries (Bryonia dioica)
7.9 7.3
Pronunciation: /waɪld hɑːp/ (AmE), /waɪld hɒp/ (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant (Bryonia dioica) with fleshy roots, pale green flowers, and small red berries, found in Europe, North Africa, and western Asia.

Examples

The wild hop plant
Gardeners often mistake wild hop for the true hop plant used in brewing.
In some regions, wild hop is valued for its ornamental vines and unique flowers.
The hedgerows were covered in wild hop, its red berries gleaming among the leaves.
