Mycenaean

noun
person from ancient Mycenae
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪsɪˈniːən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪsɪˈniːən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of ancient Mycenae, an important center of Greek civilization in the late Bronze Age.

Examples

A Mycenaean king
