pizzicato

adjective, adverb
played by plucking the strings
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /pɪt.sɪ.ˈkɑː.toʊ/ /; / /pɪt.sə.ˈkɑː.toʊ/ (AmE), /pɪt.sɪ.ˈkɑː.təʊ/ /; / /pɪt.sə.ˈkɑː.təʊ/ (BrE)
in a style of playing stringed instruments by plucking the strings with the fingers instead of using the bow
