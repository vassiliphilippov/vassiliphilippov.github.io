Syringa josikea

noun
Hungarian lilac shrub
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈrɪŋɡə joʊˈsɪkiə / (AmE), / sɪˈrɪŋɡə jɒˈsiːkiə / (BrE)
A central European shrub with upright clusters of lilac or deep violet flowers and elliptic leaves, commonly known as the Hungarian lilac.
