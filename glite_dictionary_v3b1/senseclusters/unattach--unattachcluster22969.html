unattach

verb
to disconnect emotionally
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈtætʃ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈtætʃ / (BrE)
to remove an emotional or physical attachment to someone or something

Examples

To unattach from feelings
After the breakup, he worked hard to unattach himself from old habits and routines.
It can be difficult to unattach from memories that have shaped who you are.
She needed time to unattach emotionally before starting a new relationship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attach - to connect or join to something
  • synonym: detach - to separate or remove from something
  • synonym: separate - to set or keep apart
