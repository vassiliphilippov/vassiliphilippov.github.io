sprayer

noun
worker who applies liquids by spraying
Pronunciation: / ˈspreɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈspreɪ.ə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to apply liquid in the form of a spray to a surface, such as with paint or pesticides

Examples

Field sprayer
All workers must wear protective gear while working as a sprayer in the field.
The sprayer carefully coated the car with a flawless layer of paint.
The company is hiring experienced sprayers for its agricultural division.

Related Terms

  • synonym: painter - a worker whose job is to paint surfaces
