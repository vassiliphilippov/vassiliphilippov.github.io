brakes

noun
device for slowing or stopping a vehicle
Pronunciation: / breɪks / (AmE), / breɪks / (BrE)
A device consisting of interconnected parts that is used to slow down or stop a vehicle by applying resistance to its motion.

Examples

Car brakes
She pressed hard on the brakes when she saw the child run into the street.
The car's brakes failed, causing it to roll down the hill uncontrollably.
You should check your brakes regularly to ensure your vehicle is safe to drive.
