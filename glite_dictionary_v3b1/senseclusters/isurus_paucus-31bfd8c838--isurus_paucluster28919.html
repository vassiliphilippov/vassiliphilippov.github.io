Isurus paucus

noun

A species of mackerel shark, also known as the longfin mako, characterized by its long pectoral fins and dark blue color, similar to the shortfin mako.

The Isurus paucus is known for its unusually long pectoral fins.
Unlike its close relative, the shortfin mako, Isurus paucus has a noticeably darker blue coloration.
