overcrowding

noun
dental crowding
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.kraʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.kraʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
a dental condition in which there is not enough space in the mouth for all the teeth, causing them to be crowded or misaligned

Examples

Dental overcrowding
The dentist said that overcrowding was the main reason for her crooked teeth.
Braces are often used to correct overcrowding in teenagers' mouths.
Severe overcrowding can make it difficult to clean between teeth properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malocclusion - improper alignment of the teeth and jaws
  • antonym: spacing - a dental condition where there are gaps between teeth
