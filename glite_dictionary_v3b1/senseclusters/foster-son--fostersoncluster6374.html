foster-son

noun
boy/man raised as a son though not by birth
7.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːs.tər.sʌn/ /; / /ˈfɔːs.tər.sʌn/ (AmE), /ˈfɒs.tə.sʌn/ (BrE)
A boy or man who is raised as a son by someone not his biological parent, usually as part of a foster care arrangement.

Examples

A foster-son in home
After years in the system, Marcus finally found a loving home as their foster-son.
She treats her foster-son with the same care as her biological children.
The ceremony honored both his achievements and his journey as a foster-son.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adopted son - a son legally adopted by someone
  • antonym: biological son - a son related by birth
