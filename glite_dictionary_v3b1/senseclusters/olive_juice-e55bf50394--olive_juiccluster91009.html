olive juice

noun
juice extracted from olives
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv dʒuːs / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv dʒuːs / (BrE)
the liquid obtained by extracting or pressing olives, often used in cooking or as an ingredient in cocktails

Examples

Olive juice from jar
She used leftover olive juice from the jar to marinate the vegetables.
The recipe calls for a tablespoon of olive juice mixed with herbs and spices.
When making a martini, some bartenders add a splash of olive juice for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: olive oil - oil extracted from olives
