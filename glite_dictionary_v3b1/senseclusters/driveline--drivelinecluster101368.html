driveline

noun
vehicle power system
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪvˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the system of parts in a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels

Examples

Vehicle driveline system
A faulty driveline can lead to unusual noises and vibrations while driving.
The mechanic inspected the driveline to find the source of the power loss.
Upgrading the driveline can improve a vehicle's performance and efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powertrain - the system in a vehicle that generates and transmits power to the road
  • synonym: transmission - the part of a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels
