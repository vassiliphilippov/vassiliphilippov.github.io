unafflicted

adjective
not affected
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈflɪktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈflɪktɪd / (BrE)
not affected by pain, trouble, or disease

Examples

Remain unafflicted by disease
During the outbreak, only a few people remained unafflicted by the virus.
He felt grateful to be unafflicted while many around him suffered.
The region was largely unafflicted by the recent storms that caused damage elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afflicted - suffering from pain, trouble, or disease
  • synonym: unharmed - not injured or damaged
