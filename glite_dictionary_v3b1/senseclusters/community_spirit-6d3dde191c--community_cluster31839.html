community spirit

noun
a sense of belonging and support in a community
3.9 2.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmjuː.nə.tɪ ˈspɪr.ɪt/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmjuː.nɪ.tɪ ˈspɪr.ɪt/ (BrE)
a feeling of belonging, mutual support, and fellowship among members of a community

Examples

Strong community spirit
A strong community spirit helps people support each other during difficult times.
The annual clean-up day fosters community spirit among residents.
Volunteers worked together with great community spirit to repair the local playground.
