nonurban

adjective
outside cities
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɜrbən / (AmE), / nɒnˈɜːbən / (BrE)
located outside of cities or not characteristic of urban areas

Examples

Nonurban area
They decided to buy a house in a nonurban area to enjoy more open space.
Access to public transportation is often limited in nonurban regions.
The study compared pollution levels in urban and nonurban environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than the city
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
