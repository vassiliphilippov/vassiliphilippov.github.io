tariff

verb
impose a tariff or duty
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəf / (AmE), / ˈtærɪf / (BrE)
to impose a tariff or duty on goods, especially imports

Examples

To tariff goods
If the country were to tariff steel imports, prices would likely rise.
The government decided to tariff luxury vehicles to protect local manufacturers.
They have tariffed certain agricultural products to support domestic farmers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax - to impose a tax; to make someone pay a tax
