Examples

Synonyms

  • foresighted
    6.5 0.07/m
    foresighted describes someone who plans carefully ahead in formal contexts, while foresightful sounds more general and less formal.
  • farsighted
    4.6 0.3/m
    farsighted means planning with long-term goals in mind for people or groups, while foresightful focuses on practical near-future needs.
  • longsighted
    6.7 0.00/m
    longsighted suggests thinking ahead over many years in strategy or policy, while foresightful describes careful planning for upcoming needs.
  • farseeing
    7.5 0.00/m
    farseeing often sounds literary and shows wide future vision, while foresightful is more direct about practical planning.
