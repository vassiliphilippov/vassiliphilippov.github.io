foresightful

adjective
able to plan for the future
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌsaɪtfəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.saɪt.fʊl / (BrE)
Having or showing the ability to anticipate and plan for future needs or events.

Examples

A foresightful plan
His foresightful decisions saved the company from financial trouble.
She is a foresightful manager who always considers potential risks.
They praised his foresightful approach to environmental planning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farsighted - able to judge what will happen in the future and plan accordingly
  • antonym: shortsighted - lacking consideration for future consequences
