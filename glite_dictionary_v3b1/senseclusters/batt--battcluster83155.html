batt

noun
insulation sheet
8.0
Pronunciation: /bæt/ (AmE), /bæt/ (BrE)
a sheet or roll of material, such as cotton or fiberglass, used for insulation or padding

Examples

A batt of cotton
The contractor placed a batt of fiberglass between the wall studs for better insulation.
She bought a cotton batt to use as padding in her homemade quilt.
Before finishing the attic, they installed several batts to keep the house warm.
