unpolled

adjective
having horns
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊld / (BrE)
having horns; not naturally hornless or not having had the horns removed.

Examples

Unpolled cattle breed
The farmer preferred unpolled cattle for traditional breeding purposes.
Some breeds are naturally unpolled, while others are bred to be hornless.
The unpolled ram stood out among the rest of the flock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horned - having horns
  • antonym: polled - naturally hornless or having had the horns removed
