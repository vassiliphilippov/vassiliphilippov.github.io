crowdfund

noun
online fundraising campaign
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊdˌfʌnd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊdˌfʌnd / (BrE)
an online fundraising campaign that collects small contributions from many people to support a project or cause

Examples

A crowdfund campaign
The community started a crowdfund to help rebuild the local playground after the storm.
She contributed to a crowdfund supporting independent artists from her city.
A successful crowdfund made it possible for the inventor to launch his new product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donation drive - an organized effort to collect donations for a cause
  • synonym: fundraiser - an event or campaign to collect money for a cause
