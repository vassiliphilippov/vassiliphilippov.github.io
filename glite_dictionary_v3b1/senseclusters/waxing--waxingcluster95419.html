waxing

adjective
increasing phase of the moon
6.2 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwæksɪŋ / (BrE)
(of the moon) describing the period when the visible part of the moon is increasing.

Examples

Waxing moon
During the waxing phase, the moon appears larger each night.
Farmers once relied on the waxing moon to time their plantings.
The waxing moon shone brightly in the early evening sky.
