titular

adjective
relating to the title of a work
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪtʃələr / (AmE), / ˈtɪtjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the title of a book, play, film, or other work of art; appearing in or taken from the title itself.

Examples

Titular character
Her analysis focused on the titular theme present in the film's opening scenes.
The actor was perfectly cast in the titular role of the new stage production.
The novel's titular character faces moral dilemmas throughout the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: title role - the role named in the title of a play or film
