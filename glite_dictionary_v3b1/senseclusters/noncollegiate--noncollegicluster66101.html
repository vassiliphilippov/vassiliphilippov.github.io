noncollegiate

noun
person not in college
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈlidʒiət / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈlɪdʒiət / (BrE)
a person who is not enrolled in or affiliated with a college or university as a student.

Examples

A noncollegiate joins
The program is open to both noncollegiates and current university students.
Many noncollegiates attended the seminar to gain new skills for their careers.
Scholarships are not limited to college students; noncollegiates may also apply.

Related Terms

  • antonym: student - a person who is enrolled in a school or college
  • antonym: undergraduate - a student at a college or university who has not yet earned a degree
