dedicant

noun
person making dedication
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkənt / (BrE)
a person who formally dedicates something, such as a building, object, or work, often in a ceremony

Examples

Dedicant unveils statue
The dedicant gave a heartfelt speech before unveiling the new statue in the park.
At the ceremony, the dedicant placed a plaque on the building to mark its opening.
Each year, a different dedicant is chosen to lead the festival's opening ritual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dedicator - a person who dedicates something to someone or for a purpose
