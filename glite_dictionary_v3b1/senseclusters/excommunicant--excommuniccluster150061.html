excommunicant

adjective
excluded from church
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkskəˈmjunɪkənt / (AmE), / ˌɛkskəˈmjuːnɪkənt / (BrE)
having been officially excluded from participation in a religious community, especially by excommunication.

Examples

Excommunicant member
The excommunicant nun struggled to find acceptance outside her former community.
As an excommunicant member, he was no longer allowed to attend church services.
The council debated whether to readmit the excommunicant after many years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excommunicated - formally excluded from a religious community
  • antonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or organization
