endowment

noun
funds or property supporting an institution
7.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /en.ˈdaʊ.mənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdaʊ.mənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdaʊ.mənt/ /; / /en.ˈdaʊ.mənt/ (BrE)
money or property given to an institution, person, or group to provide a permanent source of income, or the act of giving it

Examples
