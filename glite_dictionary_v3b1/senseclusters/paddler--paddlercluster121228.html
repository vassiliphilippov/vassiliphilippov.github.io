paddler

noun
person who paddles a canoe or kayak
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæd.lə / (BrE)
a person who propels a canoe, kayak, or similar small boat using a paddle

Examples

A paddler in a canoe
As a competitive paddler, she trains year-round for races.
Each paddler wore a life vest for safety during the river trip.
The experienced paddler navigated the rapids with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoeist - a person who paddles or travels in a canoe
