twentysomething

adjective
in their twenties
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɛntiˌsʌmθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtwɛntiˌsʌmθɪŋ / (BrE)
aged between 20 and 29 years old.

Examples

Twentysomething manager
The new manager is a talented twentysomething with fresh ideas for the company.
Many twentysomething professionals are moving to big cities for better job opportunities.
She started her own business as a twentysomething entrepreneur.
