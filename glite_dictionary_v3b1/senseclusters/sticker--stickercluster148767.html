sticker

noun
a short pointed knife
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstɪkə / (BrE)
a short knife with a pointed blade designed for piercing or stabbing

Examples

A sharp sticker
In the old days, a sticker was often used in close combat situations.

The museum displayed a collection of antique stickers from the 19th century.
