cumuliform

adjective
shaped like a cumulus cloud
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːmjələˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈkjuːmjʊlɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
(of a cloud) having a rounded, fluffy shape resembling a cumulus cloud.

Examples

Cumuliform cloud shape
Meteorologists often refer to cumuliform structures when predicting thunderstorm development.
The artist painted a landscape filled with cumuliform clouds rolling across the blue sky.
The sky was dotted with cumuliform clouds, promising fair weather for the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cumulus - a type of cloud with a puffy, white appearance
