comforter

noun
an infant's device to suck or bite on
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərtɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətə / (BrE)
a device, usually made of rubber or plastic, given to an infant to suck or bite on for comfort; a pacifier.

Examples

Baby comforter
Please make sure the comforter is clean before handing it to the infant.
Some parents prefer not to use a comforter to avoid dependency.
The baby calmed down as soon as she was given her comforter.

  • synonym: pacifier - an artificial nipple for babies
