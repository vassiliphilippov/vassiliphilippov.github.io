decadently

adverb
in a self-indulgent way
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkədəntli / (AmE), / ˈdɛkədəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is rich, luxurious, or self-indulgent

Examples

To decadently enjoy food
The guests dined decadently on rich foods and expensive wines all evening.
He spent his fortune decadently, buying luxury cars and designer clothes without restraint.
The palace was decorated decadently, with gold accents and lavish furnishings in every room.
She decorated the room decadently, with velvet curtains and gold accents everywhere.
The chef prepared a meal so decadently rich that everyone asked for seconds.
They spent the weekend decadently relaxing at a five-star spa resort.
