bang

noun
a conspicuous success
Pronunciation: / bæŋ / (AmE), / bæŋ / (BrE)
an event or performance that is very successful or impressive

Examples

A show with a bang
Her latest book went off with a bang, outselling all her previous works.
The opening night of the show was a bang and everyone talked about it for weeks.
We want this year's festival to start with a bang and attract as many people as possible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flop - a complete failure, especially of an event or performance
  • synonym: hit - a successful venture or performance
