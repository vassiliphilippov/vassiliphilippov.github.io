social interaction

noun
exchanges between people
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˌɪntərˈækʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˌɪntərˈakʃən / (BrE)
the process by which people act and react in relation to others

Examples

Social interaction at school
Children learn important life skills through social interaction at school.
Effective social interaction is important for building relationships and understanding others.
The pandemic limited opportunities for social interaction among colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communication - the act of exchanging information between people
