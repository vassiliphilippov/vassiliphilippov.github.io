tolerably

adverb
in an acceptable but not outstanding manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːlərəbli / (AmE), / ˈtɒlərəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is acceptable, but not remarkable or outstanding; moderately well.

Examples

To tolerably manage
He managed to fix the sink tolerably, though it still leaks a little.
She speaks French tolerably but still struggles with complex conversations.
The movie was tolerably entertaining, but nothing truly memorable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adequately - to a satisfactory or acceptable extent
  • antonym: poorly - in a way that is not adequate or satisfactory
