castoff

noun
discarded item
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstˌɒf / (BrE)
an item that is no longer wanted or used by its previous owner and is given away or discarded

Examples

A castoff item
The thrift store was filled with castoffs from local families.
He wore his brother's castoff jacket to school every day.
Many people find treasures among the castoffs at garage sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discard - something thrown away or rejected
  • synonym: hand-me-down - an item, especially clothing, passed on from one person to another
  • antonym: possession - something owned or possessed
