maki

noun
Japanese sushi roll
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːki / (AmE), / ˈmɑːki / (BrE)
a type of Japanese sushi roll wrapped in seaweed and filled with rice and various ingredients

Examples

Maki sushi roll
We shared a platter of maki at the new Japanese restaurant downtown.
He learned how to make maki during his cooking class in Tokyo.
Vegetarian maki rolls are a popular choice for those who do not eat fish.
