local city hall

noun
municipal building for civil functions
1.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààlo ä.k…ôl Àås…™.ti h…îl / (AmE), / Ààl…ô ä.k…ôl Àås…™.ti h…îÀêl / (BrE)

Examples

The local city hall

The mayor announced the new policy at the local city hall during a public meeting.
