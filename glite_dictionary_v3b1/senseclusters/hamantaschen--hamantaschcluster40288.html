hamantaschen

noun
Purim pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːmənˌtɑːʃən / (AmE), / ˈhæmənˌtæʃən / (BrE)
a triangular pastry with a sweet filling, traditionally eaten during the Jewish festival of Purim

Examples

Sweet hamantaschen pastry
Every year, we make hamantaschen with different fruit fillings for Purim.
She brought a plate of homemade hamantaschen to share with her classmates.
Chocolate and poppy seed are popular flavors for hamantaschen during the holiday.
