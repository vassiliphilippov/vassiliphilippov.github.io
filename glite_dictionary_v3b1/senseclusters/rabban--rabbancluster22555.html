rabban

noun
Syriac religious title
Pronunciation: / rɑːˈbɑːn / (AmE), / rəˈbɑːn / (BrE)
a historical title for Christian monks or religious leaders in the Syriac (East Syriac) tradition

Examples

Rabban Bar Sauma
The ancient manuscript was signed by rabban Bar Sauma, a respected religious leader.
Many pilgrims visited the monastery to seek the wisdom of the rabban who lived there.
The title rabban was given to monks of high standing in the Syriac Christian tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abbot - the head of a monastery
  • synonym: monk - a member of a religious community of men
