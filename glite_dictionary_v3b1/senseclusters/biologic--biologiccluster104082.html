biologic

noun
biotech medicine, therapeutic product
5.5
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪk/ (AmE), /baɪ.ə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪk/ (BrE)
a medical drug or therapeutic product derived from living organisms (for example proteins, antibodies, or cells) used to treat disease.

Examples

Biologic drug

Hospitals often reserve special storage for expensive biologics that require refrigeration.
After reviewing options, her doctor recommended switching to a different biologic.
