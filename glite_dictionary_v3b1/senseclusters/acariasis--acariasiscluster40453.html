acariasis

noun
infestation with mites
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌækəˈraɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌækəˈraɪəsɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition caused by infestation with mites, particularly itch mites

Examples

Acariasis skin irritation
In some regions, acariasis is a frequent cause of skin irritation in livestock.
The veterinarian diagnosed the dog with acariasis after observing intense scratching and hair loss.
Treatment for acariasis often includes topical or oral medications to eliminate the mites.
