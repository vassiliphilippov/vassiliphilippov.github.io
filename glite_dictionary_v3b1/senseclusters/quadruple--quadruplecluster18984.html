quadruple

noun
four times the quantity
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdruːpəl /; / kwɑːˈdrupəl / (AmE), / kwɒˈdruːpəl /; / kwɒˈdrʊpl / (BrE)
a group or amount of four, or something that is four times as much as another thing

Examples

A quadruple of tasks
Her workload became a quadruple after taking on several new projects.
The company reported a quadruple in profits compared to last year's earnings.
The investment yielded a quadruple of the initial amount in just two years.
Each table was set with a quadruple of identical glasses.
She collected a quadruple of rare stamps from the same series.
The relay team won gold in the quadruple sculls at the Olympics.
