allogenic

adjective
from an external source
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌaləˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
originating from a source outside the organism, area, or system; not produced internally

Examples

Scientists studied the allogenic sediments deposited by wind in the desert basin.
