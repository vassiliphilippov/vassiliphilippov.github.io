starches

noun
foods high in starch (e.g. potatoes, rice)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtʃɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtʃɪz / (BrE)
Foods that are rich in natural starch, such as potatoes, rice, and bread.

Examples

Natural starches foods
At dinner, the restaurant offers several starches including potatoes, pasta, and bread.
He tries to limit his intake of starches and eat more vegetables instead.
Nutritionists recommend choosing whole grain starches like brown rice and oatmeal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carbohydrates - nutrients including sugars, starches, and fiber
  • antonym: proteins - foods high in protein content
  • antonym: vegetables - edible plant parts often lower in starch than 'starches'
