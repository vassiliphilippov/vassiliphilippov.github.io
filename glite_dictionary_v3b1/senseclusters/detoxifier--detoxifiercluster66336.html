detoxifier

noun
remover of toxins
6.3
Pronunciation: / diˈtɑksɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / diˈtɒksɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person, clinic, or program that helps someone remove harmful substances or toxins from their body, especially during treatment for addiction or poisoning

Examples

Detoxifier clinic
The hospital opened a new detoxifier program to help patients safely withdraw from alcohol.
A skilled detoxifier can make the withdrawal process safer and more comfortable for patients.
Many people seek out a detoxifier after struggling with substance abuse for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detox center - a facility where people are treated to remove toxins or drugs from their bodies
  • synonym: rehabilitation - the process of helping someone recover from addiction or illness
