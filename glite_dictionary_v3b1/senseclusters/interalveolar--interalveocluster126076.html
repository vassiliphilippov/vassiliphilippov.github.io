interalveolar

adjective
between alveoli
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərælˈviːələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌælviˈəʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring between alveoli, especially in anatomical structures such as the lungs

Examples

The interalveolar septum
The interalveolar septum separates the air sacs in the lung.
Damage to the interalveolar walls can affect breathing efficiency.
Researchers studied the thickness of the interalveolar tissue in different lung diseases.
