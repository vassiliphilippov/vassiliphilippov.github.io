arc cosine

noun
inverse cosine function
8.5 7.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrk ˈkoʊ.saɪn / (AmE), / ɑːk ˈkəʊ.saɪn / (BrE)
the inverse function of the cosine, giving the angle whose cosine is a given number

Examples

The arc cosine function
In mathematics, the arc cosine function is often written as arccos(x).
The arc cosine of 0.5 is 60 degrees.
To find the angle, you need to calculate the arc cosine of the ratio.

Related Terms
