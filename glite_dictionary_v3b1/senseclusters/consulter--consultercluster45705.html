consulter

noun
person seeking advice
6.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈsʌltər / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltə / (BrE)
a person who seeks advice or information from someone, especially an expert

Examples

Client consulter
The consulter prepared a list of questions before meeting the financial advisor.
As a frequent consulter of medical professionals, she always valued expert opinions.
Each consulter received a detailed response from the help desk within 24 hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consultant - a person who gives expert advice
  • synonym: inquirer - a person who asks for information
