hill

noun
artificial mound of earth or stones
Pronunciation: / hɪl / (AmE), / hɪl / (BrE)
An artificial heap or bank of earth or stones, especially one built as a structure or barrier.

Examples

A burial hill
A small hill marked the burial site in the old battlefield.
Archaeologists discovered an ancient ceremonial hill constructed from carefully placed stones.
The soldiers took cover behind a hill built to shield them from enemy fire.
