arteria digitalis

noun

Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪ.rɪə dɪ.dʒə.ˈtæl.əs/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪ.rɪə dɪ.dʒɪ.ˈtæl.ɪs/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪ.rɪə dɪ.dʒɪ.ˈteɪ.lɪs/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪ.rɪə dɪ.dʒɪ.ˈtɑː.lɪs/ /; / /ɑː.ˈtɪ.rɪə dɪ.dʒɪ.ˈtæl.ɪs/ (BrE)
Any of the arteries in the hand or foot that supply blood to the fingers or toes.

Examples
