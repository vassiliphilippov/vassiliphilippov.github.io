blocker

noun
software that blocks ads/trackers
Pronunciation: / c8bl51k59r / (AmE), / c8bl52k59 / (BrE)
software (often a browser extension) that prevents advertisements, tracking scripts, or other unwanted content from loading on web pages.

Examples

An ad blocker

Many news sites detect an blocker and ask readers to disable it to view content.
