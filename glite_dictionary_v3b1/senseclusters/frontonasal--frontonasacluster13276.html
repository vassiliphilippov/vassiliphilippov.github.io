frontonasal

adjective
frontal and nasal bones
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfrʌntoʊˈneɪzəl / (AmE), / ˌfrʌntəʊˈneɪzəl / (BrE)
relating to both the frontal bone and the nasal bones of the skull

Examples

The frontonasal bone
