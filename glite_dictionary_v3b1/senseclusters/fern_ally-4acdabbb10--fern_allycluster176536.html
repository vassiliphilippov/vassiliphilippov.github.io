fern ally

noun
vascular plant related to ferns
8.5 6.8
Pronunciation: /fɜːrn ˈæl.aɪ/ /; / /fɜːrn ˈæl.ɪ/ (AmE), /fɜːn ˈæl.aɪ/ /; / /fɜːn ˈæl.ɪ/ (BrE)
any of various vascular plants related to, but not true, ferns, such as clubmosses, horsetails, and whisk ferns

Examples

Fern ally plants
Botanists classify clubmosses and horsetails as fern allies due to their similarities with true ferns.
Many fern allies played an important ecological role during the Carboniferous period.
Whisk ferns are a lesser-known group among the fern allies found in tropical regions.
