Fomalhaut

proper noun
bright star
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊməlˌhoʊt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊməlˌhaʊt / (BrE)
a bright star in the constellation Piscis Austrinus, one of the brightest stars visible from Earth

Examples

Bright Fomalhaut star
The astronomer pointed out Fomalhaut in the southern sky during the stargazing event.
Many cultures have stories about Fomalhaut because of its brightness and position.
Through the telescope, Fomalhaut appeared even more brilliant than with the naked eye.
