garden plant

noun
plant commonly grown in gardens
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that is commonly grown in a garden, especially for decorative or culinary purposes

Examples

Garden plants grow well
Regular watering is essential for healthy garden plants during hot, dry months.
The nursery offers a wide selection of garden plants, from roses to lavender.
We bought several garden plants to brighten up our backyard this summer.
