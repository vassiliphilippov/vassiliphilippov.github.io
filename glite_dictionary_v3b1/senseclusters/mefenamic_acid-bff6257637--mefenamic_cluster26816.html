mefenamic acid

noun
NSAID pain relief drug
Pronunciation: / məˌfɛnəˈmɪk ˌæsɪd / (AmE), / məˌfɛnəˈmɪk ˌæsɪd / (BrE)
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve mild to moderate pain, especially menstrual cramps

Examples

Mefenamic acid pain relief
Patients who are allergic to other NSAIDs should avoid taking mefenamic acid.
The doctor prescribed mefenamic acid to help manage her menstrual pain.
You should take mefenamic acid with food to reduce stomach irritation.
