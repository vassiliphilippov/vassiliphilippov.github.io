Rosellinia

noun
genus of fungi with dark ascospores

A genus of fungi characterized by smooth perithecia and dark, one-celled ascospores.

Examples

Rosellinia fungi genus
Identification of Rosellinia requires microscopic examination of their distinctive dark ascospores.
Some Rosellinia fungi are significant plant pathogens, causing diseases in crops and trees.
Species of Rosellinia are often found growing on decaying wood in forests.
