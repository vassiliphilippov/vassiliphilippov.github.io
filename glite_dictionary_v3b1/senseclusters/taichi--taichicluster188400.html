taichi

noun
Chinese system of slow meditative exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.tʃiː / (BrE)
a Chinese system of slow, meditative physical exercise designed to promote relaxation, balance, and good health

Examples

Practice taichi exercises
Every morning, the park is filled with people practicing taichi to improve their well-being.
I enrolled in a taichi class to help reduce my stress levels.
Many elderly people find taichi beneficial for maintaining balance and flexibility.
