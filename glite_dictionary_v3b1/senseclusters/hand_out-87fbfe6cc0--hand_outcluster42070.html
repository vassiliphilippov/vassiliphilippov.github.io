Synonyms

  • give out
    2.3 7/m
    give out is used for giving or distributing things to people, while hand out is a casual way to pass items to a group.
  • distribute
    2.0 4/m
    distribute sounds more formal and is used for sharing or spreading items widely, while hand out is everyday and direct.
  • pass out
    2.7 3/m
    pass out means to give something to people and can also mean fainting, while hand out only means to distribute to a group.
  • pass around
    2.1 2/m
    pass around means people pass items from one to another across a group, while hand out means giving items directly to each person.
  • dispense
    3.7 0.9/m
    dispense is used for giving controlled items like medicine or advice, often by a machine or professional, while hand out is general.
  • dish out
    3.4 1/m
    dish out means to give things or harsh comments freely, often informally, while hand out is neutral and simply means distribute.
  • dole out
    4.0 2/m
    dole out describes giving small amounts or official help over time, while hand out is a neutral act of distributing to people.
  • deal out
    3.2 0.2/m
    deal out often refers to giving cards or rough treatment to people, while hand out focuses on distributing items to a group.
  • parcel out
    4.8 0.1/m
    parcel out means to divide something into parts and assign them to groups, while hand out means to give items directly to people.
