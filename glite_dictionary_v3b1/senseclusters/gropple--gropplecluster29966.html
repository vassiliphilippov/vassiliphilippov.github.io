gropple

noun
confused search by touch
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑpəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒpəl / (BrE)
a brief, confused struggle or act of searching by touch, especially in the dark or when unable to see clearly

Examples

A quick gropple
During the power outage, there was a quick gropple as everyone tried to find their phones.
He had a brief gropple in his bag before finally finding his keys.
After the lights went out, a gropple ensued as people searched for the exit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fumble - to use the hands clumsily while searching or handling something
  • synonym: grope - to search for something by feeling with the hands
