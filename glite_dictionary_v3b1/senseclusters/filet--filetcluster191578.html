filet

verb
cut meat/fish into filets
6.1
Pronunciation: / fɪˈleɪ /; / ˈfɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪt / (BrE)
to cut meat or fish into boneless strips or pieces called filets

Examples

To filet a fish
He skillfully fileted the trout before cooking it.
It takes practice to filet a fish cleanly without wasting any meat.
She watched the chef filet the salmon into perfect strips.
It takes some practice to fillet a fish without leaving any bones behind.
She watched a video to learn how to fillet chicken breasts properly.
The chef skillfully filleted the salmon before cooking it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bone - to remove the bones from meat or fish
