multi-

x
prefix: many
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌlti / (AmE), / ˈmʌlti / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'many' or 'more than one', used to form words indicating several or multiple parts, people, or things

Examples

Multi- national company
The company became a multi-national organization with offices in several countries.
She is a multi-talented artist who sings, dances, and paints.
The project required a multi-disciplinary team of experts from different fields.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mono- - prefix meaning 'one' or 'single'
  • synonym: poly- - prefix meaning 'many' or 'much'
