networking

noun
making professional contacts
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɝːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of meeting and interacting with people to exchange information and develop professional or social contacts

Examples

Business networking event
Attending industry events is a great way to do networking and meet potential employers.
She improved her networking skills by joining several professional organizations.
Effective networking can open doors to new business opportunities and partnerships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborating - working together with others to achieve a goal
  • antonym: isolating - avoiding contact with others
  • synonym: socializing - spending time with others in a friendly way
