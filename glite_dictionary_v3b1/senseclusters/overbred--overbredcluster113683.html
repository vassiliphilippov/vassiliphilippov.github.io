overbred

adjective
excessively refined
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbrɛd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbrɛd / (BrE)
showing excessive refinement or affectedly genteel manners, often to the point of being snobbish or artificial

Examples

Overbred manners
Her overbred accent and manners made her seem out of place at the casual gathering.
The overbred decor of the mansion felt cold and unwelcoming to most visitors.
He was criticized for his overbred tastes, which some found pretentious and artificial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or refined than is actually the case
  • synonym: snobbish - behaving as though one is superior to others
  • antonym: unrefined - lacking in sophistication or polish
