bobstay

noun
bowsprit support rope
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbˌsteɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌsteɪ / (BrE)
a strong rope or chain used to hold down the bowsprit of a ship and keep it steady
