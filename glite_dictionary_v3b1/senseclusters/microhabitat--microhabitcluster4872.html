microhabitat

noun
small habitat area
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈhæbɪtæt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈhæbɪtæt / (BrE)
a small, specific area within a larger habitat where particular organisms live or interact

Examples

A microhabitat for insects
A fallen log in the forest can serve as a microhabitat for insects and fungi.
Each rock pool on the beach forms a unique microhabitat for small marine creatures.
Scientists studied the microhabitat under the leaf litter to understand the diversity of soil organisms.

  • synonym: niche - the role or position of an organism within its environment
