short and sweet

adjective
brief and to the point
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt ənd swiːt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ənd swiːt / (BrE)
(of something) brief but pleasant or effective; to the point without unnecessary detail.

Examples

Short and sweet message
Her resignation letter was short and sweet, with just two sentences.
Let's keep this meeting short and sweet so everyone can get home early.
The ceremony was short and sweet, lasting less than fifteen minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concise - expressing much in few words
  • antonym: long-winded - lasting too long and not interesting
  • synonym: succinct - clearly and briefly expressed
