tadpole shrimp

noun
freshwater crustacean resembling a tadpole
Pronunciation: / ˈtædˌpoʊl ˌʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ˈtad.pəʊl ˌʃrɪmp / (BrE)
a small freshwater crustacean with a broad, shield-like carapace, resembling a tadpole, belonging to the order Notostraca

Examples

A tadpole shrimp animal
A tadpole shrimp can survive in harsh conditions where few other creatures live.
Scientists studied the behavior of tadpole shrimp in temporary pools.
The pond was full of tadpole shrimp after the spring rains.
