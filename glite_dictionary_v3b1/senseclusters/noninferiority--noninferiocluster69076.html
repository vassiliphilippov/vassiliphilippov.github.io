noninferiority

noun
not worse in studies
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪnfɪriˈɔrɪti /; / ˌnɑnˌɪnˌfɪriˈɔrəti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪnfɪəriˈɒrɪti /; / ˌnɒnˌɪnˌfɪəriˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being worse than something else, especially in clinical or scientific comparisons

Examples
