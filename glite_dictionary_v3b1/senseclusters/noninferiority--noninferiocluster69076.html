noninferiority

noun
not worse in studies
9.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ɪn.fə.ˈrɪr.ɪ.ti/ (AmE), /nɒn.ɪn.fə.ˈrɪə.rɪ.ti/ (BrE)
the state or quality of not being worse than something else, especially in clinical or scientific comparisons

Examples

The noninferiority trial
The clinical trial aimed to prove the noninferiority of the generic medication compared to the original drug.
Researchers concluded that the new therapy demonstrated noninferiority in treating the disease.
