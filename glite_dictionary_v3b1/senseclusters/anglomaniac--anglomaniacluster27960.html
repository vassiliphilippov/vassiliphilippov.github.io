anglomaniac

noun
fan of British culture
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡloʊˈmeɪniæk / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləʊˈmeɪniæk / (BrE)
a person who is extremely enthusiastic about English or British culture, customs, or people

Examples

An anglomaniac collector
As an anglomaniac, she collected British novels and watched every royal event on television.
His friends teased him for being an anglomaniac because he only drank English tea and wore tweed jackets.
The anglomaniac decorated his apartment with Union Jack flags and posters of London landmarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anglophile - a person who admires England or English things
  • antonym: francophile - a person who admires France or French things
