gravitationally

adverb
in relation to gravity
2.1
Pronunciation: / ɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ɡravɪˈteɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In a manner related to or caused by gravitation.

Examples

To be gravitationally pulled
Objects with greater mass are gravitationally attracted to each other more strongly.
The planets are gravitationally bound to the Sun.
The satellite is gravitationally influenced by both Earth and the Moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gravitational - relating to or caused by gravity
