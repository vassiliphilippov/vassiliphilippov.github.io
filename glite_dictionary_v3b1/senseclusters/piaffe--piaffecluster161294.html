piaffe

noun
cadenced trot performed in place by a horse
Pronunciation: / piˈɑf / (AmE), / piˈaf / (BrE)
A highly controlled, cadenced trot in which a horse moves in place, commonly used in dressage.

Examples

A piaffe trot
Her horse maintained perfect balance throughout the piaffe segment of their routine.
Learning the piaffe requires both skillful riding and a highly trained horse.
The stallion performed a flawless piaffe during the dressage competition.
