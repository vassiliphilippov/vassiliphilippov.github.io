salina

noun
salt evaporation pond
Pronunciation: / səˈliːnə / (AmE), / səˈliːnə / (BrE)
a man-made shallow area or pond where salt is produced by evaporating seawater or brine

Examples

A coastal salina
The workers harvested salt from the large salina near the coast every summer.
Birds gathered around the shallow salina as the water slowly evaporated in the sun.
Tourists visited the historic salina to learn about traditional salt production methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salt pan - a natural or artificial flat area where salt collects after water evaporates
  • synonym: saltworks - a place where salt is produced from seawater or brine
