char

noun
blackened substance from burning
7.0
Pronunciation: / tʃɑr / (AmE), / tʃɑː / (BrE)
A blackened substance produced by burning or charring; partially burnt material.

Examples

A layer of char
After the fire, only a layer of char remained on the ground.
The scientist examined the char left after burning the sample.
You could still smell the char from yesterday’s barbecue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ash - the powdery residue left after burning
  • synonym: soot - a black powdery substance produced by combustion
