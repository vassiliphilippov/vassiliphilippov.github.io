Pongamia glabra

noun
evergreen Asiatic shade tree with scented flowers
Pronunciation: / poʊnˈɡɑːmiə ˈɡleɪbrə / (AmE), / pɒnˈɡɑːmiə ˈɡleɪbrə / (BrE)
an evergreen tree native to Asia, with glossy pinnate leaves and clusters of creamy-white scented flowers, used as a shade tree

Examples

Pongamia glabra tree
Farmers often plant Pongamia glabra along roadsides because of its beautiful flowers and large leaves.
The leaves of Pongamia glabra are used in some traditional medicines.
The shady spot under the Pongamia glabra became a popular place for students to gather.
