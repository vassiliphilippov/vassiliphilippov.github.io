overeducated

adjective
excessively educated
5.1
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈed.juː.keɪ.tɪd/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈed.dʒuː.keɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈed.jʊ.keɪ.tɪd/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈed.dʒʊ.keɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
having more education than is necessary or required for a particular job or situation

Examples

An overeducated worker
Many recent graduates worry about being overeducated for the jobs available in their field.
She sometimes felt overeducated when her advanced degree was not needed for her daily tasks.
The company hesitated to hire someone they considered overeducated for the entry-level position.
