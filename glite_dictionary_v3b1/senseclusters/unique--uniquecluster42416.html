unique

adjective
the only one of its kind
1.4
Pronunciation: / juˈnik / (AmE), / juːˈniːk / (BrE)
being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else; found only in a particular place, group, or situation

Examples

A unique design
Each snowflake is considered unique because no two are exactly alike.
Her approach to solving the problem was truly unique and impressed everyone.
The museum displayed a unique artifact from ancient Egypt.
His style of painting is truly unique among contemporary artists.
She spoke with a unique accent that was hard to place.
We enjoyed a unique dining experience at the new restaurant.
Several customs are unique to the region and are not practiced elsewhere.
The dialect spoken here is unique to this remote mountain village.
This type of bird is unique to the Galapagos Islands and lives nowhere else in the world.
