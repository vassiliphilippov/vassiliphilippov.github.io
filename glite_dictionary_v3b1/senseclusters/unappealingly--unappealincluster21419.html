unappealingly

adverb
in an unattractive or displeasing manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈpilɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpiːlɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not attractive or pleasing.

Examples

Designed unappealingly
She found the advertisement unappealingly designed, making her lose interest.
The food was unappealingly presented on the plate and looked bland.
The room was unappealingly decorated, with drab colors and old furniture.
