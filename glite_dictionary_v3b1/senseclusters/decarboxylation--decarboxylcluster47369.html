decarboxylation

noun
removal of a carboxyl group (chemistry)
8.7
Pronunciation: /diː.kɑːr.bɑk.sɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /də.kɑːr.bɑk.sɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.kɑː.bɒk.sɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /də.kɑː.bɒk.sɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
