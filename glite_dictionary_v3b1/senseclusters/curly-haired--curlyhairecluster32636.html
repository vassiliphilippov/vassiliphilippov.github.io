curly-haired

adjective
having curly hair
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːli hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli heəd / (BrE)
Having hair that forms curls or waves.

Examples

Curly-haired person
Many people with curly-haired genes struggle to find the right hair products.
She has been curly-haired since she was a child.
The new dog is a curly-haired poodle with a playful temperament.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight-haired - having straight hair, not curly
  • degree lower: wavy-haired - having hair forming gentle waves
