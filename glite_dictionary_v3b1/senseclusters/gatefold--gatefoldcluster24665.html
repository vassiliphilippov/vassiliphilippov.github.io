gatefold

noun
an oversize folded page in books or magazines
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪt.fəʊld / (BrE)
an oversize page in a book, magazine, or record sleeve that is folded so it can be opened out for viewing

Examples

A gatefold page
Collectors value vinyl releases that have a gatefold design for displaying additional information.
The album came with a stunning gatefold cover featuring exclusive artwork.
The magazine included a special gatefold page with a panoramic photograph of the city.
