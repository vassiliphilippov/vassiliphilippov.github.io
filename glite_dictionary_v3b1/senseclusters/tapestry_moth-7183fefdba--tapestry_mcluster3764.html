tapestry moth

noun
moth whose larvae eat woolen materials
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtæp.əstri mɑθ / (AmE), / ˈtæp.əstri mɒθ / (BrE)
a small moth whose larvae feed on carpets, tapestries, and other woolen materials, often causing damage to household fabrics

Examples

Tapestry moth larvae
A professional exterminator was called to deal with the tapestry moth problem.
Proper storage can prevent a tapestry moth infestation in your home.
We discovered several tapestry moth larvae in the old carpet.
