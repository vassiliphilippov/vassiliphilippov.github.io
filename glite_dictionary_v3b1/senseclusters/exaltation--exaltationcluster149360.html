exaltation

noun
group of larks (collective noun)
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˌzɑlˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzɑːlˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a collective noun for a group of larks, especially when they are flying together overhead.

Examples

An exaltation of larks
As dawn broke, an exaltation of larks rose above the meadow, their song filling the air.
In her poem, she beautifully described the sky alive with an exaltation of larks.
We paused on our walk to admire an exaltation of larks soaring in perfect harmony overhead.
