north-polar

adjective
relating to the Arctic/North Pole
2.1
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ ˈpoʊlɚ / (AmE), / nɔːθ ˈpəʊlə / (BrE)
of or relating to the Arctic or the region around the North Pole.

Examples

North-polar region
Polar bears are native to north-polar regions where sea ice is abundant.
Satellite images revealed changes in the north-polar ice cap over the past decade.
The team conducted research in north-polar ice fields for several months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: south-polar - of or relating to the Antarctic or the region around the South Pole
