hard rush

noun
a tall rush plant of temperate regions
5.9
Pronunciation: / hɑrd rʌʃ / (AmE), / hɑːd rʌʃ / (BrE)
a tall perennial rush (Juncus inflexus) with stiff, cylindrical stems, found in temperate regions

Examples

The hard rush plant
During the summer, the wet meadows are dominated by hard rush and other water-loving plants.
Gardeners often use hard rush as an ornamental plant because of its distinctive, upright stems.
The riverbank was lined with dense clusters of hard rush swaying in the breeze.
