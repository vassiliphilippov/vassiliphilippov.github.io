sciolist

noun
person who feigns knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.ə.lɪst / (BrE)
A person who pretends to be knowledgeable or learned, especially without real understanding or expertise.

Examples

A sciolist pretends knowledge
He was dismissed as a sciolist, impressive in speech but shallow in understanding.
The debate was undermined by a sciolist who contributed baseless opinions.
True scholars distinguish themselves from the sciolists who only pretend to know.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dilettante - person who dabbles in a field without real commitment or knowledge
  • antonym: expert - person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area
