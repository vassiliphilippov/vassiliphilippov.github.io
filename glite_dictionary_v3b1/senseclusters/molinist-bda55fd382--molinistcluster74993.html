Molinist

adjective
of Molinism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊlɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒlɪnɪst / (BrE)
relating to Molinism or the theological views of Luis de Molina, especially concerning divine providence and human free will
