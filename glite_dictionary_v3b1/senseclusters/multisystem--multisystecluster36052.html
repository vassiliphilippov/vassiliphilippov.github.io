multisystem

adjective
involving multiple systems
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
involving or affecting more than one system, especially within the body or in technology

Examples

Multisystem disease
The disease can cause multisystem complications, affecting the heart, lungs, and kidneys.
Engineers designed a multisystem network to improve communication between different departments.
Doctors monitored the patient for signs of multisystem involvement after the infection spread.

Related Terms

  • antonym: localized - limited to a specific area or system
  • synonym: systemic - affecting the whole system or body
