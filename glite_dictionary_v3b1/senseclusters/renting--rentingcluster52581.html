renting

noun
the act of paying to use something temporarily
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of paying money to use something, such as a house, apartment, or car, for a period of time

Examples

She is considering renting a car for her trip to the coast.
With renting, you have the flexibility to move without selling property.
