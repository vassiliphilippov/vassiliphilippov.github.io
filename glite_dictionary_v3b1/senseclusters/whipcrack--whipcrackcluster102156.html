whipcrack

noun
sharp whip sound
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpkræk / (AmE), / ˈwɪpkræk / (BrE)
a sharp, loud sound produced by the sudden movement or snapping of a whip

Examples

A loud whipcrack
The sudden whipcrack startled the horses in the stable.
With a loud whipcrack, the trainer signaled the start of the show.
The whipcrack echoed through the empty canyon, breaking the silence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crack - a sudden sharp noise
  • synonym: snap - a sudden, sharp, cracking sound
