peripheral

noun
external computer device
5.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɪfɚəl / (AmE), / pəˈrɪfərəl / (BrE)
a device, such as a keyboard, printer, or external drive, that is connected to a computer and works with it but is not an essential part of the central processing unit

Examples

Computer peripheral
A keyboard is considered a basic peripheral for any desktop computer setup.
Installing the correct drivers is crucial for your peripheral devices to function properly.
Our office recently upgraded several peripherals, including new printers and external hard drives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accessory - an extra piece of equipment that can be added for a particular purpose
  • synonym: component - a part or element of a larger whole, especially in technology
