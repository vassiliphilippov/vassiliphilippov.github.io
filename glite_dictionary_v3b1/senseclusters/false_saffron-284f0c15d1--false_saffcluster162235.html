false saffron

noun
plant with saffron-like flowers; yields oil
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: /fɔːls ˈsæf.rən/ /; / /fɑːls ˈsæf.rən/ (AmE), /fɔːls ˈsæf.rən/ /; / /fɒls ˈsæf.rən/ (BrE)
a thistlelike Eurasian plant with red or orange flower heads and seeds that produce a valuable oil, often used as a substitute for saffron
