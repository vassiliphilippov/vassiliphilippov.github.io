scooper

noun
journalist with exclusive
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːpər / (AmE), / ˈskuːpə / (BrE)
a journalist who obtains and publishes exclusive news or information before anyone else

Examples

Star scooper reporter
The young scooper secured the exclusive interview and published the story before rival papers learned of it.
As a skilled scooper, she was always the first to break important news.
Every newsroom wants a scooper who can deliver big stories ahead of the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitor - a person or organization that tries to win or do better than others
  • synonym: journalist - a person who collects, writes, and reports news
  • synonym: reporter - a person who writes news stories for a newspaper or broadcast
