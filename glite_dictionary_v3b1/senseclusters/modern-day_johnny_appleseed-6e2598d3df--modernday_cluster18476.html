modern-day Johnny Appleseed

noun
person who promotes tree planting or environmentalism
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdərn deɪ ˌdʒɑni ˈæpəlˌsid / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən deɪ ˌdʒɒni ˈæp(ə)lsiːd / (BrE)
a person who actively promotes environmentalism or the planting of trees, especially by encouraging others or planting many trees themselves

Examples

A modern-day Johnny Appleseed plants trees
After retiring, he became a modern-day Johnny Appleseed by planting orchards in every nearby town.
Local students praised their teacher as a modern-day Johnny Appleseed for starting a tree-planting initiative.
The city's modern-day Johnny Appleseed has organized dozens of reforestation projects over the past decade.
