tienda

noun
Spanish tent
Pronunciation: / ˈtjɛndə / (AmE), / ˈtjɛndə / (BrE)
a tent, especially one used for camping or as a temporary shelter, in Spanish-speaking contexts

Examples

Camping tienda
They set up a tienda in the forest to spend the night safely.
During the festival, many families stayed in a large tienda near the lake.
The hikers carried a lightweight tienda for their journey through the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth, supported by poles and ropes
