caritas

noun
Christian charity
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪtɑs / (AmE), / ˈkærɪtæs / (BrE)
Christian love for others, especially shown through charity and selfless actions.

Examples

Caritas for the poor
The nun devoted her life to caritas and service to the poor.
Acts of caritas are central to many religious communities around the world.
He admired her spirit of caritas and willingness to help anyone in need.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruism - selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • synonym: charity - generous actions or donations to help others
  • antonym: selfishness - concern only for one's own interests
