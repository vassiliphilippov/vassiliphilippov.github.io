encephalomyocarditis

noun
viral brain and heart inflammation
Pronunciation: / ɛnˌsɛfəloʊˌmaɪoʊkɑrˈdaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɛnˌsɛfələʊˌmaɪəʊkɑːˈdaɪtɪs / (BrE)
