semiconducting

adjective
having properties of a semiconductor
4.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /sə.mɪ.kən.ˈdʌk.tɪŋ/ /; / /se.mɪ.kən.ˈdʌk.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /sə.mɪ.kən.ˈdʌk.tɪŋ/ /; / /se.mɪ.kən.ˈdʌk.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
