semiconducting

adjective
having properties of a semiconductor
Pronunciation: / ˌsem.i.kənˈdʌk.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛm.i.kənˈdʌk.tɪŋ / (BrE)
having the electrical properties of a semiconductor, with conductivity intermediate between that of an insulator and a conductor

Examples

Semiconducting material
Researchers discovered a new semiconducting polymer that could improve solar cell efficiency.
Silicon is the most commonly used semiconducting element in the electronics industry.
The device uses a semiconducting material to control the flow of electricity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: semiconductor - a substance with conductivity between a conductor and an insulator
