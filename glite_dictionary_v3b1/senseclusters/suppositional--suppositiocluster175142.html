suppositional

adjective
based on supposition or conjecture
Pronunciation: / səˌpoʊˈzɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / səˌpəʊˈzɪʃənl / (BrE)
based primarily on assumption, conjecture, or surmise rather than solid evidence

Examples

A suppositional idea
Her explanation for the missing money was entirely suppositional and lacked proof.
Many stories about the ancient world are largely suppositional, built on fragmentary evidence.
The scientist warned that the results were suppositional and not confirmed by direct observation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conjectural - based on guesswork or conjecture
  • synonym: hypothetical - based on suggested idea or theory
