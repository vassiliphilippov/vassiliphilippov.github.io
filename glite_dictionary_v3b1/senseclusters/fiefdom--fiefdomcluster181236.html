fiefdom

noun
organization dominated by one person or group

Pronunciation: / ÀàfiÀêf.d…ôm / (AmE), / ÀàfiÀêf.d…ôm / (BrE)
an area, organization, or domain controlled or dominated by one person or group, originally referring to land held by a feudal lord
