gastritis

noun
inflammation of the stomach lining
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /gæs.ˈtraɪ.təs/ /; / /gæs.ˈtraɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /gæs.ˈtraɪ.tɪs/ /; / /gæs.ˈtraɪ.təs/ (BrE)
Inflammation of the lining of the stomach, often causing nausea, loss of appetite, and discomfort after eating.

Examples

Gastritis symptoms
Symptoms of gastritis can include a burning sensation in the upper abdomen and loss of appetite.
The doctor diagnosed her with gastritis after she complained of persistent stomach pain and nausea.
Untreated gastritis may lead to more serious health problems if the inflammation persists.
