shiveringly

adverb
with visible shivering
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪvərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪvərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or shows shivering, usually because of cold or fear

Examples

To shiveringly step in cold
He stepped shiveringly into the cold water, his teeth chattering from the chill.
The child clung shiveringly to her mother as the thunderstorm raged outside.
They waited shiveringly for the bus, bundled in their thin jackets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steadily - in a way that is firm, stable, or without shaking
  • synonym: tremblingly - in a way that shows trembling or shaking
