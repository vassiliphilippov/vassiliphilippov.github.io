Synonyms

  • cyclist
    1.6 9.51/m
    cyclist is the common, neutral word for someone who rides a bicycle in sport or transport, while bicyclist sounds less common.
  • biker
    1.1 1.02/m
    biker often describes someone who rides for fun or sport and is used in casual speech, while bicyclist sounds more formal.
  • bicycler
    1.3 0.01/m
    bicycler is a rare variant that simply names a bicycle rider, while bicyclist is the common, widely used term.
  • pedaler
    3.0 0.04/m
    pedaler can mean someone who pedals any vehicle, like a trike, while bicyclist only refers to a bicycle rider.
  • wheeler
    5.8 0.16/m
    wheeler sometimes names any rider on a wheeled vehicle and sounds vague, while bicyclist clearly means a bicycle rider.
