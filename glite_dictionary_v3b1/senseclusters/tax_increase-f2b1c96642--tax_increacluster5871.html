tax increase

noun
increase in the level of taxes
Pronunciation: / tæks ɪnˈkriːs / (AmE), / tæks ɪnˈkriːs / (BrE)
an increase in the amount or rate of taxes charged by a government

Examples

A tax increase
A tax increase often faces opposition from both politicians and citizens.
Many businesses are concerned about the impact of the proposed tax increase.
The government announced a tax increase to help fund public services.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tax cut - a reduction in the amount or rate of taxes
