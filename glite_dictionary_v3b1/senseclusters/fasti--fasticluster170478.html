fasti

noun
Roman official lists
Pronunciation: / ˈfæstaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːsti / (BrE)
official lists or calendars, especially those recording public events or magistrates in ancient Rome

Examples

Ancient fasti lists
The ancient fasti provide historians with valuable information about Roman officials.
Scholars studied the fasti to trace the sequence of Roman consuls.
A copy of the fasti was displayed in the forum for public reference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annals - historical records arranged year by year
  • synonym: chronicle - a factual written account of important events in order
