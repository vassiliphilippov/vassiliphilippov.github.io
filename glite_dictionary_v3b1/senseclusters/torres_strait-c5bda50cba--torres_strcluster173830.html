Torres Strait

proper noun
strait between Australia and New Guinea
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːrəz streɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒrɪz streɪt / (BrE)
a strait between northeastern Australia and southern New Guinea, connecting the Coral Sea with the Arafura Sea
