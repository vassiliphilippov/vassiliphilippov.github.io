Vincentian

noun
member of Catholic order
Pronunciation: / vɪnˈsɛnʃən / (AmE), / vɪnˈsɛnʃən / (BrE)
a member of the Congregation of the Mission, a Catholic religious order founded by Saint Vincent de Paul

Examples

Vincentian priest
The Vincentian dedicated his life to serving the poor in the community.
Several Vincentians organized a food drive for families in need.
She was inspired by the compassion shown by the Vincentian during her visit to the mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Franciscan - a member of the Franciscan order, a Catholic religious group
  • synonym: Jesuit - a member of the Society of Jesus, a Catholic religious order
