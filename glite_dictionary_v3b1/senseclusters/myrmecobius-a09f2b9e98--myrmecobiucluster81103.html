Myrmecobius

noun
Australian marsupial, the numbat
Pronunciation: / mɜːrˌmiːˈkoʊbiəs /; / mɜrˌmiːˈkoʊbi.əs / (AmE), / mɜːˌmiːˈkəʊbiəs /; / mɜːˌmiːˈkəʊbi.əs / (BrE)
a small Australian marsupial, also called the numbat or banded anteater, that feeds mainly on termites; also the genus containing this animal
