claymation

adjective
stop-motion with clay
6.3
Pronunciation: /kleɪ.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kleɪ.ˈmeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
made using the stop-motion animation technique in which clay figures are moved and filmed frame by frame

Examples

Claymation figures
The studio released a claymation film that delighted audiences of all ages.
She prefers claymation movies because of their unique handmade look.
The claymation characters moved in a charming, lifelike way throughout the commercial.

Related Terms

  • antonym: computer-generated - created using computer graphics rather than physical models
  • synonym: stop-motion - animation created by photographing objects in small movements frame by frame
