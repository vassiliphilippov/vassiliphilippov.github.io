fragging

noun
video game kill
Pronunciation: / ˈfræɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfræɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of killing or defeating an opponent in a video game, especially in a shooting game

Examples

Online fragging
He kept track of every fragging during the intense online match.
Her impressive fragging made her one of the top players in the tournament.
After each fragging, the scoreboard updated with his new total.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kill - an instance of defeating or eliminating an opponent in a game
