crystallographic

adjective
about crystal structures
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪstələˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstələˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or description of the structure and properties of crystals

Examples

A crystallographic study
The crystallographic analysis revealed the mineral's internal structure.
Researchers used crystallographic techniques to determine the arrangement of atoms in the sample.
Advances in crystallographic methods have improved our understanding of protein structures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crystalline - having the structure or form of a crystal
