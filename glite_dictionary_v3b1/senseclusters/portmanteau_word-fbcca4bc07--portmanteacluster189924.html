portmanteau word

noun
word formed by blending two words
Pronunciation: / pɔrtˈmæntoʊ wɝːd / (AmE), / pɔːtˈmæntəʊ wɜːd / (BrE)
A word formed by blending parts of two or more other words and combining their meanings.

Examples

A portmanteau word example
Many modern portmanteau words like 'smog' and 'motel' have become common in English.
She explained that 'infomercial' is a portmanteau word blending 'information' and 'commercial'.
The word brunch is a classic portmanteau word combining 'breakfast' and 'lunch'.
