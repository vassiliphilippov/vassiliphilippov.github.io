fall

noun
wrestling move—opponent pinned on mat
Pronunciation: / fɑl / (AmE), / fɔːl / (BrE)
(in wrestling) an act of holding an opponent's shoulders on the mat for a set period, resulting in a win.

Examples

A fall in wrestling
He won by fall, pinning his opponent in under two minutes.
Securing a fall is often the fastest route to victory in wrestling.
The match ended quickly with a surprise fall in the first round.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pin - wrestling hold immobilizing opponent's shoulders to the mat
