A medical condition in which the ability to hear is reduced or impaired.

Examples

A hearing disorder condition
A hearing disorder can affect a person's ability to communicate effectively.
Children with a hearing disorder may benefit from speech therapy and assistive devices.
Early identification of a hearing disorder improves the chances for better outcomes.

Synonyms

  • hearing impairment
    3.9 0.39/m
    hearing impairment names a medical, clinical reduction in hearing, while hearing disorder is a general medical label for such reduced hearing.
  • hearing loss
    2.7 2.17/m
    hearing loss means a partial or total reduction in the ability to hear, while hearing disorder is a clinical term for that condition.
  • hypoacusis
    9.9 0.00/m
    hypoacusis is the medical, technical term for partial hearing loss, while hearing disorder is a broader, less technical medical phrase.
  • deafness
    2.7 1.06/m
    deafness means a very limited or complete loss of hearing, while hearing disorder covers any reduced hearing, mild to severe.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: deafness - complete or severe loss of hearing
  • synonym: hearing loss - partial or total inability to hear
