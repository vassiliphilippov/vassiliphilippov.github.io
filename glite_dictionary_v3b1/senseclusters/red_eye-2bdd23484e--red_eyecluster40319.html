red eye

noun
photo flash effect
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd aɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd aɪ / (BrE)
the appearance of bright red pupils in a photograph caused by camera flash reflecting off the retina

Examples

Red eye in a photo
The group photo had noticeable red eye in several people's faces.
Many cameras have a special mode to prevent red eye in pictures taken with flash.
She used software to remove the red eye from her vacation photos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glare - an unwanted bright spot or reflection in a photo
  • synonym: red-eye reduction - a camera feature that reduces red eye in photos
