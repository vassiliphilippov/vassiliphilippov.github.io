rock sunfish

noun
North American freshwater sunfish
8.7
Pronunciation: / rɑk ˈsʌnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / rɒk ˈsʌnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a North American freshwater fish of the sunfish family, found in the upper Mississippi River and Great Lakes regions, valued as a game and food fish

Examples

A large rock sunfish
After catching a large rock sunfish, he cooked it for dinner.
The rock sunfish is often sought after by anglers in the Great Lakes region.
We spotted a rock sunfish swimming among the rocks near the riverbank.
