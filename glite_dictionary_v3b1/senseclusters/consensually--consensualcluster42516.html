consensually

adverb
with mutual agreement
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛnʃuəli / (AmE), / kənˈsɛnʃuəli / (BrE)
in a way that is based on the agreement or consent of all parties involved

Examples

To act consensually
The two companies merged consensually after months of negotiations.
All members of the team consensually agreed to the new schedule.
The decision was made consensually, ensuring everyone felt included.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coercively - in a way that is forced or pressured
  • synonym: voluntarily - in a way that is done by choice or free will
