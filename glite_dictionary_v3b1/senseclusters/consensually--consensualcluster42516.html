consensually

adverb
with mutual agreement
5.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsen.ʃə.lɪ/ /; / /kən.ˈsen.ʃuː.ə.lɪ/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsen.ʃə.lɪ/ /; / /kən.ˈsen.ʃuː.ə.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

To act consensually

The decision was made consensually, ensuring everyone felt included.
