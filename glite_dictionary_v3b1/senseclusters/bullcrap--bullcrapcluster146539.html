bullcrap

interjection
disbelief; nonsense
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌkræp / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌkræp / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express disbelief or to say that something is nonsense or untrue

Examples

Bullcrap! Really
When I heard his explanation, I immediately thought it was bullcrap.
She shouted 'bullcrap!' when she realized the story was made up.
That's just bullcrap—there's no way he finished the project in one day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are not true or make no sense
  • synonym: rubbish - something that is not true or is foolish
  • antonym: truth - something that is true or factual
