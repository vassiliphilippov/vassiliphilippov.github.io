antifoam

adjective
prevents foam
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈfoʊm / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈfəʊm / (BrE)
preventing or reducing the formation of foam, especially in industrial or chemical processes

Examples

Antifoam properties
The technician added an antifoam agent to the solution to prevent bubbles from forming.
Using an antifoam coating helps keep the machinery running smoothly during production.
The laboratory uses antifoam chemicals to control excessive foaming in their experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defoaming - removing or reducing foam
