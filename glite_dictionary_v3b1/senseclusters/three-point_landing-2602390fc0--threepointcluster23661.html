three-point landing

noun
aircraft landing where all wheels touch at once
Pronunciation: / ˌθri pɔɪnt ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌθriː pɔɪnt ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
A landing in which all three wheels of an aircraft touch the ground simultaneously.

Examples

A smooth three-point landing
He was praised for making a smooth three-point landing in the vintage aircraft.
Learning to perform a three-point landing is essential for any student pilot.
The pilot executed a perfect three-point landing despite the crosswind.
