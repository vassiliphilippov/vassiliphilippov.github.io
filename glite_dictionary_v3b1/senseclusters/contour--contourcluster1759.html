contour

noun
outline or arrangement of features
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.tʊr / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tʊə / (BrE)
the outline, shape, or arrangement of features that defines something, either physical or abstract

Examples

The contour of a hill
She described the contour of his argument with compelling clarity.
The contours of the city's economic policy shifted after the election.
The new novel traces the contour of a changing society.
Architects studied the contour of the land before designing the building.
In the darkness, the contour of the mountain was just visible against the sky.
She traced the contour of the vase with her fingers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outline - the general shape or edge of something without the detail
  • synonym: profile - the outline of something, especially a face, as seen from one side
