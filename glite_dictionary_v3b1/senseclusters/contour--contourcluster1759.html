noun
outline or arrangement of features

Synonyms

  • outline
    2.7 7/m
    an outline is a line or shape that marks an object's outer edge, while a contour gives the overall edge defining its form.
  • shape
    1.0 40/m
    shape is the visible form or appearance of something, while a contour focuses on the line or arrangement that defines it.
  • form
    1.3 80/m
    form is the general visible structure or style of something, while a contour describes the specific outline or feature arrangement.
  • conformation
    7.0 0.5/m
    conformation is the internal shape set by parts and their arrangement, while a contour is the outer outline or visible edge.
  • profile
    3.4 8/m
    profile is a side view or cross-section showing one angle, while a contour is the full outline or shape from any view.
