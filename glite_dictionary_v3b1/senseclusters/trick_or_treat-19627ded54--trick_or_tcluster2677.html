trick or treat

noun
children's Halloween request for treats
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪk ər ˈtriːt / (AmE), / ˈtrɪk ɔː ˈtriːt / (BrE)
a customary request made by children on Halloween as they go door to door asking for sweets, typically accompanied by the playful threat of a trick if no treat is given

Examples

Children say trick or treat
Every Halloween, children shout trick or treat as they knock on doors in the neighborhood.
Please have some candy ready in case any trick or treat visitors arrive tonight.
The kids dressed up as ghosts and witches for trick or treat this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trick - a cunning act; a mischievous deed
