Lysenkoism

noun
suppression of scientific research
Pronunciation: / laɪˈsɛŋkoʊˌɪzəm / (AmE), / laɪˈsɛŋkəʊˌɪzəm / (BrE)
the political policy of suppressing independent scientific research and enforcing ideological conformity in science, especially as practiced in the Soviet Union under Trofim Lysenko

Examples

Lysenkoism in science policy
The rise of Lysenkoism in the Soviet Union led to the persecution of many respected scientists.
Historians often cite Lysenkoism as a warning against political interference in scientific research.
Many scientific advancements were delayed due to the influence of Lysenkoism on research policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic freedom - the right of scholars to pursue research and express ideas without interference
  • synonym: scientific censorship - the suppression or control of scientific information or research
