hoose

noun
lungworm disease in livestock
Pronunciation: / huːs / (AmE), / huːs / (BrE)
a parasitic disease of the lungs in cattle and sheep caused by lungworms, leading to coughing and breathing difficulties

Examples

Sheep with hoose
The veterinarian diagnosed the coughing sheep with hoose after a thorough examination.
Farmers often vaccinate their livestock to prevent outbreaks of hoose in the herd.
Persistent coughing in young calves can be an early sign of hoose infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lungworm - a parasitic worm that infects the lungs of animals
  • antonym: pneumonia - a serious lung infection causing inflammation
