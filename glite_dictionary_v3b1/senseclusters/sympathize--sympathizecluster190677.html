Synonyms

  • empathize
    4.9 2.91/m
    empathize means understanding and sharing another person's feelings deeply, while sympathize is more about feeling pity or concern.
  • commiserate
    7.0 0.76/m
    commiserate means expressing sympathy for someone's suffering, while sympathize can be more general and include understanding without action.
  • feel for
    3.0 2.49/m
    feel for is an informal phrase showing quick sympathy, while sympathize is more general and suits formal or written contexts.
