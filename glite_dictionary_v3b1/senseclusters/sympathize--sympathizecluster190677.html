sympathize

verb
share or understand another's feelings
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəˌθaɪz /; / ˈsɪm.pə.ˌθaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəθaɪz /; / ˈsɪm.pə.θaɪz / (BrE)
to share, understand, or express compassion for another person's feelings or situation

  • synonym: empathise - to understand and share the feelings of another
  • synonym: empathize - to understand and share the feelings of another
