pop tart

noun
sweet, filled toaster pastry
2.7
Pronunciation: / pɑp tɑrt / (AmE), / pɒp tɑːt / (BrE)
A brand of thin, sweet pastry with a filling, often fruit-flavored, that is typically heated in a toaster.

Examples

A pop tart snack
He likes to eat a chocolate pop tart as an afternoon snack.
I toasted a pop tart for a quick breakfast before work.
She keeps a box of pop tarts in the pantry for her children.
