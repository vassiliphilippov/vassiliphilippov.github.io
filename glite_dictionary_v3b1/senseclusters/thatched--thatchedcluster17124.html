thatched

verb
to roof with straw
Pronunciation: / θætʃt / (AmE), / θætʃt / (BrE)
covered a roof or structure with straw, reeds, or similar material as a traditional roofing method

Examples

To thatched a roof
The cottage was thatched by skilled craftsmen using bundles of straw.
Many old English houses were originally thatched to keep them warm and dry.
They thatched the barn last summer using local reeds and traditional techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roofed - covered with a roof
  • antonym: tiled - covered with tiles
