revoke

noun
card game infraction: failing to follow suit
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvoʊk / (AmE), / rɪˈvəʊk / (BrE)
(in card games) the violation of the rules caused by a player failing to follow suit when able to do so.

Examples

A revoke penalty
Debate broke out at the table after a controversial revoke late in the match.
His careless revoke cost their team the chance to win the round.
If you make a revoke during the game, you could be penalized by losing points.
