outgoingness

noun
sociable quality
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌɡoʊɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌɡəʊɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, sociable, and willing to talk to new people

Examples

Friendly outgoingness
Her outgoingness helped her make friends quickly at her new job.
People often admire his outgoingness at social gatherings.
Despite her natural outgoingness, she sometimes enjoys quiet evenings alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extroversion - the tendency to be outgoing and socially confident
  • antonym: shyness - the quality of being reserved or having difficulty talking to others
  • synonym: sociability - the quality of enjoying the company of others
