straw

adjective
pale yellow (like dry straw)
7.7
Pronunciation: / strɔ / (AmE), / strɔː / (BrE)
Of a pale yellow color, resembling that of dry straw.

Examples

Straw blonde hair
His hair turned straw blonde after weeks in the sun.
She wore a straw dress that complemented her fair skin.
The kitten's straw fur made it easy to spot in the tall grass.
