outseam

noun
outer pants seam
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌsiːm / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌsiːm / (BrE)
the outer seam running down the side of a pair of pants or trousers from the waistband to the hem

Examples

The outseam of pants
The tailor measured the outseam to ensure the pants would fit properly.
If the outseam is too short, the trousers may look awkward when worn.
She requested that the outseam be let out for a more comfortable fit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inseam - the seam running along the inside of the leg of pants or trousers
