scratch by

verb
manage to survive with little
8.4
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ baɪ / (AmE), / skrætʃ baɪ / (BrE)
to manage to survive or get by with very limited resources or money

Examples

To scratch by on savings
After losing his job, he had to scratch by on his savings for months.
Even as a student, she managed to scratch by with careful budgeting.
Many families scratch by on minimum wage incomes during tough financial times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: get by - to manage to survive or cope, especially with difficulty
  • synonym: scrape by - to just manage to succeed, especially with very little money
  • antonym: thrive - to grow or develop successfully
