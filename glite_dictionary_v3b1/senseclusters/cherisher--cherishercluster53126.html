cherisher

noun
one who cares deeply
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛrɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who deeply values, cares for, or protects someone or something

Examples

A cherisher of memories
As a cherisher of rare books, he spent hours preserving his collection.
The dog became a loyal cherisher of its new family.
She was remembered as a cherisher of community traditions and values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guardian - someone who looks after or is responsible for someone or something
  • antonym: neglector - a person who fails to care for someone or something
  • synonym: protector - a person who defends or guards someone or something
