ain't that the truth

interjection
expression of strong agreement
1.4
Pronunciation: / eɪnt ðæt ðə truθ / (AmE), / eɪnt ðæt ðə truːθ / (BrE)
used to express strong agreement with something that has just been said, especially when it is obviously or undeniably true

Examples

Ain't that the truth phrase
"Parenting is a full-time job." "Ain't that the truth, especially with toddlers."
"Prices are always rising these days." "Ain't that the truth."
When he said everyone makes mistakes, all I could say was, "Ain't that the truth."

Related Terms

  • synonym: couldn't agree more - used to express maximum agreement
  • synonym: you can say that again - used to show strong agreement with what someone has said
