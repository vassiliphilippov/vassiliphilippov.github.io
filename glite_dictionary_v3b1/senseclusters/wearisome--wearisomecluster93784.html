wearisome

adjective
causing tiredness or boredom
7.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪr.ɪ.səm/ /; / /ˈwɪr.ə.səm/ (AmE), /ˈwɪə.rɪ.səm/ /; / /ˈwɪə.rə.səm/ (BrE)
So uninteresting or repetitive as to cause tiredness or boredom.

Examples

A wearisome task
Filling out endless forms can be a wearisome task for anyone.
He found the novel wearisome and struggled to finish it.
The lecture became increasingly wearisome as the professor repeated the same points.
