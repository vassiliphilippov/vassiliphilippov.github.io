Japanese maple

noun
tree with deeply cut red leaves in autumn
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ˈmeɪpəl /; / dʒəˈpɑːniːz ˈmeɪpəl / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub from Japan (and Korea), grown for its decorative, deeply cut leaves that turn bright colors in autumn
