hug drug

noun
slang for MDMA/ecstasy
Pronunciation: / hʌɡ drʌɡ / (AmE), / hʌɡ drʌɡ / (BrE)
A slang term for the synthetic drug MDMA (ecstasy), known for its effects of increased feelings of empathy and affection.
