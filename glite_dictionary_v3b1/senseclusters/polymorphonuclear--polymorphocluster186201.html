polymorphonuclear

noun
white blood cell type
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.mɔːr.foʊ.nuː.ˈkliː.ər/ /; / /pɑː.lɪ.mɔːr.fə.nuː.ˈkliː.ər/ (AmE), /pɒl.ɪ.mɔː.fə.njuː.ˈkliː.ə/ /; / /pə.lɪ.mɔː.fə.njuː.ˈkliː.ə/ (BrE)
a type of white blood cell with a nucleus divided into several lobes, especially a granulocyte such as a neutrophil

Examples

A polymorphonuclear cell
The doctor noted an increase in polymorphonuclear cells in the patient's blood test.
A high count of polymorphonuclear is often seen in bacterial infections.
Laboratory analysis revealed that most of the white blood cells were polymorphonuclear.

  • synonym: neutrophil - a type of white blood cell that is a granulocyte
