Trojan War

proper noun
legendary war between Greece and Troy
7.2 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtro äd í…ôn w…îr / (AmE), / Ààtr…ô ä.d í…ôn w…îÀê / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the legendary war fought between Greece and Troy, sparked by the abduction of Helen, culminating after ten years with the Greek victory through the ruse of the Trojan Horse.

Examples

The Trojan War story

The concept of the 'Trojan Horse' comes from the final stages of the Trojan War.
The story of the Trojan War is central to many works of ancient Greek literature.
