overexercise

verb
to use excessively
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɛksərsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
to use power, authority, or a right to an excessive or harmful degree

Examples

To overexercise authority
The manager tended to overexercise his authority, making decisions without consulting his team.
If you overexercise your rights, people may see you as unfair or controlling.
Some leaders overexercise their power, which can lead to resentment among employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abuse - to use something in a way that is harmful or wrong
  • antonym: restrain - to limit or control the use of something
