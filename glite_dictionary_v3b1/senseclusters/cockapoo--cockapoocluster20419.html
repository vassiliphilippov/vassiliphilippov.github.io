cockapoo

noun
Cocker Spaniel–Poodle mix
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkəˌpu / (AmE), / ˈkɒkəˌpuː / (BrE)
a dog that is a crossbreed between a Cocker Spaniel and a Poodle, known for being friendly and intelligent

Examples

A cockapoo dog
Their family adopted a playful cockapoo from the animal shelter last year.
A cockapoo usually has a curly coat and loves to be around people.
Many people choose a cockapoo because it is friendly and easy to train.
Their cockapoo is friendly and loves to play fetch in the park.
Many families choose a cockapoo because of its gentle temperament and low-shedding coat.
The cockapoo quickly learned new tricks and became a favorite at the dog training class.
