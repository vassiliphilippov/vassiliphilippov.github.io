silverness

noun
bright silvery quality
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvənəs / (BrE)
a bright, clear, or silvery quality, especially in sound, tone, or appearance.

Examples

Silverness of her voice
The silverness of her voice made the song especially beautiful to the audience.
He admired the silverness of the moonlight reflecting on the calm lake.
The flute's silverness added a sparkling quality to the orchestra's performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brightness - the quality of being full of light or shining
  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear or easy to hear
  • antonym: dullness - the quality of lacking brightness or clarity
