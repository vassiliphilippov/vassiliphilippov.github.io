idolatry

noun
excessive admiration
7.6
Pronunciation: /aɪ.ˈdɑː.lə.triː/ (AmE), /aɪ.ˈdɒ.lə.triː/ (BrE)
very strong admiration or love for someone or something, often to an excessive or unreasonable degree

Examples

Idolatry of celebrities
Some fans' idolatry of celebrities can lead them to imitate everything their idols do.
The leader was uncomfortable with the idolatry shown by his most devoted followers.
Her idolatry of the artist inspired her to pursue a career in painting.
