torta

noun
Spanish slap
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtə / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtə / (BrE)
an informal Spanish term for a quick blow delivered with the open hand; a slap

Examples

A quick torta slap
When he insulted her, she gave him a sharp torta across the cheek.
The argument ended with a loud torta that surprised everyone in the room.
He joked too much and received a playful torta from his friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punch - a quick hit with a closed fist
  • synonym: slap - a quick hit with the open hand
