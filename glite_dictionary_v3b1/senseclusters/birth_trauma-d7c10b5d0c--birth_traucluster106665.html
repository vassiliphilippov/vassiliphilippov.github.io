birth trauma

noun
physical injury to a newborn during birth
Pronunciation: / bɝːθ ˈtrɔmə / (AmE), / bɜːθ ˈtrɔːmə / (BrE)
physical injury sustained by a newborn during the process of birth.

Examples

Newborn birth trauma
Many cases of birth trauma are mild and resolve without long-term effects.
Proper obstetric techniques can significantly reduce the risk of birth trauma.
The doctors took extra precautions to prevent birth trauma during the delivery.
