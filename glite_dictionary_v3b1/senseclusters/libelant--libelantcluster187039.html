libelant

noun
person who libels
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪbələnt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪbələnt / (BrE)
a person who makes, composes, or publishes a libel or defamatory statement about another

Examples

Libelant spreads rumors
The court found the libelant responsible for spreading false rumors about the politician.
After the article was published, the company sued the libelant for defamation.
A libelant can face serious legal consequences for making untrue statements in print.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defamer - a person who damages another's reputation by making false statements
  • antonym: libelee - a person about whom a libel is written
