attune to

verb
adapt or adjust to something
6.5 1.15/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàtun tu / (AmE), / …ôÀàtjuÀên t ä / (BrE)
to adjust or adapt to something, so as to become more in harmony or alignment with it

Examples

To attune to culture
After moving abroad, it can be challenging to attune to a different culture.
It took her a while to attune to the new work environment.
Musicians often need time to attune to each other's playing style.
