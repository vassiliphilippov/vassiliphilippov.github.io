Amanita

proper noun
mushroom genus
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈniːtə / (AmE), / ˌæməˈniːtə / (BrE)
a genus of mushrooms, some species of which are highly poisonous and include well-known deadly varieties

Examples

Deadly Amanita
The deadly Amanita phalloides is also known as the death cap mushroom.
Many foragers avoid picking any Amanita species because of the risk of poisoning.
Some Amanita mushrooms are beautiful but extremely dangerous if eaten.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mushroom - a fungus with a stem and cap, some edible, some poisonous
  • synonym: toadstool - a poisonous or inedible mushroom
