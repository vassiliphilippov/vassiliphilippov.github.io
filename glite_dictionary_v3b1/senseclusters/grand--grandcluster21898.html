grand

adjective
elevated in moral or intellectual value
1.1
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd / (AmE), / ɡrænd / (BrE)
of high moral or intellectual value; elevated in character, style, or purpose

Examples

Grand ideas
He spoke of his grand vision for a more just society.
She devoted her life to the grand cause of educating the poor.
Their grand ideals inspired generations to strive for progress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: base - without moral principles; ignoble
  • synonym: lofty - of imposing height or elevated in style or character
  • synonym: noble - having or showing fine personal qualities or high moral principles
