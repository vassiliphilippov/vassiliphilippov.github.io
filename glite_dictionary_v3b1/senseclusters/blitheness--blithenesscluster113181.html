blitheness

noun
cheerful, carefree happiness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪðnəs / (AmE), / ˈblaɪðnɪs / (BrE)
a feeling or quality of cheerful and carefree happiness

Examples

A blitheness of spirit
Despite the challenges, she maintained a sense of blitheness that inspired everyone around her.
Her blitheness was contagious, brightening up even the dullest meetings.
I admired the blitheness with which he handled setbacks in his life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfulness - the state of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • antonym: gloom - a state of partial or total darkness; metaphorically, sadness or depression
