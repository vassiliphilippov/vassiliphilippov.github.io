technicism

noun
specialized technical term
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛknɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
a technical term or expression used in a particular field, often difficult for non-specialists to understand

Examples

Complex technicism
The contract was filled with technicisms that made it hard for ordinary people to follow.
She tried to avoid using technicisms when explaining the process to new employees.
Many scientific papers contain technicisms that require background knowledge to understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jargon - special words or expressions used by a particular profession or group
  • antonym: plain language - language that is clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: terminology - the set of technical words or expressions used in a particular subject
