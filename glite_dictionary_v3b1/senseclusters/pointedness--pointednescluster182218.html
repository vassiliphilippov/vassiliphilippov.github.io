pointedness

noun
quality of being intentionally directed or pointed
4.5 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpɔɪn.tɪd.nəs/ /; / /ˈpɔɪn.təd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈpɔɪn.tɪd.nəs/ /; / /ˈpɔɪn.təd.nəs/ (BrE)
