heartwarming

adjective
emotionally uplifting; causing happiness or pleasure
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing feelings of happiness, pleasure, or tenderness, especially by being emotionally uplifting.

Examples

A heartwarming story
Her heartwarming smile made everyone feel welcome.
It was a heartwarming moment when the lost dog was reunited with its owner.
The film's heartwarming story brought tears of joy to the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heartbreaking - causing great sadness or distress
  • synonym: moving - having a strong emotional effect
  • synonym: touching - evoking strong feelings of tenderness or sympathy
