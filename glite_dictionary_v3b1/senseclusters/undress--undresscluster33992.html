undress

verb
remove clothing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɛs / (BrE)
to take off some or all of the clothes from yourself or another person

Examples

To undress before bed
After a long day, he was eager to undress and relax.
She helped her grandmother undress before getting into bed.
The doctor asked the child to undress for the examination.
He quickly undressed and put on his pajamas before bed.
Please don't undress in front of everybody!
She began undressing for her shower while talking on the phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disrobe - to remove one's clothes
  • antonym: dress - to put clothes on oneself or another
  • synonym: strip - to remove all or most of one's clothes
