caress

noun
gentle, affectionate touch or stroke
2.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈres/ (AmE), /kə.ˈres/ (BrE)
a gentle and loving touch or stroke; an act of showing affection through gentle contact, sometimes used metaphorically.

Examples

A gentle caress
His words were a caress to her troubled soul.
She welcomed him with a gentle caress on his cheek.
The soft caress of the breeze made her feel calm and peaceful.
