midstory

noun
middle building level
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌstɔːri / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌstɔːri / (BrE)
the middle floor or level of a building, especially the one between the ground floor and the top floor

Examples

Midstory office
Their office is located on the midstory, making it easy to access from both the lobby and the rooftop.
The midstory of the hotel offers a perfect balance between city views and quiet surroundings.
She decorated her midstory apartment with plants to create a cozy atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ground floor - the floor of a building at ground level
  • synonym: mezzanine - a low story between two main stories of a building
  • antonym: top floor - the highest floor of a building
