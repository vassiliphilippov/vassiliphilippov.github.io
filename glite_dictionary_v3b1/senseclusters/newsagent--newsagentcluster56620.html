newsagent

noun
a person or shop selling newspapers and magazines
2.5 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀànuÀêzÀåe…™d í…ônt / (AmE), / ÀànjuÀêzÀåe…™d í…ônt / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells newspapers, magazines, and often other items such as sweets and tobacco

Examples

The newsagent shop
