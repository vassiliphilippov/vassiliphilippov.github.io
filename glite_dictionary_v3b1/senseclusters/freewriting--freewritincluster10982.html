freewriting

noun
unstructured writing exercise
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfriː.raɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /friː.ˈraɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfriː.raɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /friː.ˈraɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
