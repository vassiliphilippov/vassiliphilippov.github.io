freewriting

noun
unstructured writing exercise
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a writing activity where you write continuously without worrying about grammar, spelling, or topic, to generate ideas or creativity
