malignantly

adverb
in a malignant manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪɡnəntli / (AmE), / məˈlɪɡnəntli / (BrE)
In a malignant manner; with a tendency to cause harm, especially describing tumors that spread aggressively.

Examples

Grow malignantly fast
Cancer cells may grow malignantly, invading nearby tissues rapidly.
Some viruses behave malignantly within the host, causing extensive damage.
The doctor explained that the tumor was spreading malignantly and required immediate treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benignly - in a benign manner; without harm or threat
  • synonym: malignant - actively harmful; tending to spread and worsen (especially of tumors)
