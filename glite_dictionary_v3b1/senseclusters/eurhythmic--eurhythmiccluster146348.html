eurhythmic

adjective
musical movement method
Pronunciation: / jʊˈrɪðmɪk / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɪðmɪk / (BrE)
relating to Dalcroze eurhythmics, a method of teaching musical rhythm and expression through coordinated movement.

Examples

Eurhythmic exercises
The dance class incorporated eurhythmic exercises to help students internalize musical timing.
Children enjoyed the eurhythmic activities that combined movement with learning about rhythm.
Her eurhythmic training improved her sense of musical phrasing and coordination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhythmic - having a regular repeated pattern of sounds or movements
