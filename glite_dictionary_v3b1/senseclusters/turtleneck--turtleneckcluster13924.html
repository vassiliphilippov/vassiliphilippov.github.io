turtleneck

noun
sweater with a high close-fitting collar
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːt̬lˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːt(ə)lˌnɛk / (BrE)
A sweater or jersey with a high, close-fitting, turned-over collar that covers most of the neck.

Examples

A turtleneck sweater
A turtleneck sweater is perfect for keeping warm in winter.
His collection of vintage turtlenecks is the envy of his friends.
She wore a black turtleneck under her jacket on the chilly evening.
