kiwano

noun
spiny orange fruit with green jelly-like flesh
9.6 8.6
Pronunciation: /kiː.ˈwɑː.noʊ/ /; / /kɪ.ˈwɑː.noʊ/ (AmE), /kiː.ˈwɑː.nəʊ/ /; / /kɪ.ˈwɑː.nəʊ/ (BrE)
A spiny orange fruit with lime-green, jelly-like flesh and a refreshingly fruity taste, produced by the African horned cucumber (Cucumis metuliferus).
