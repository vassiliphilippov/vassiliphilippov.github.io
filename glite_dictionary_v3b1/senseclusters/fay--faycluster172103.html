fay

noun
a small magical being; a fairy
Pronunciation: / feɪ / (AmE), / feɪ / (BrE)
A small mythical being in human form, typically playful and possessing magical powers; a fairy.

Examples

A fay with magic
As a child, she believed that a fay lived at the bottom of her garden.
He claimed that he once saw a fay dancing among the wildflowers at dusk.
The story told of a mischievous fay who granted wishes to kind-hearted villagers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy - small imaginary being with magical powers
  • synonym: sprite - mythical being of small size with magical properties
