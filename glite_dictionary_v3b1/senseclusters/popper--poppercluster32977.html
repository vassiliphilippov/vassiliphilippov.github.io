popper

noun
fishing lure
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpər / (AmE), / ˈpɒpə / (BrE)
a floating fishing lure designed to make a popping sound on the water surface to attract fish

Examples

A fishing popper
He tied a bright popper to his line, hoping to catch a big bass near the reeds.
The surface of the lake rippled as the popper made a loud noise to attract fish.
Many anglers prefer using a popper when fishing for aggressive species in shallow water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bait - food or substance used to attract and catch fish
  • synonym: lure - an object used to attract fish so they can be caught
