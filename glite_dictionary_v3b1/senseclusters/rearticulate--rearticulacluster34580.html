rearticulate

verb
to join again
8.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ɑːr.ˈtɪk.jə.leɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ɑːr.ˈtɪk.jə.leɪt/ (AmE), /riː.ɑː.ˈtɪk.jə.leɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ɑː.ˈtɪk.jə.leɪt/ (BrE)
