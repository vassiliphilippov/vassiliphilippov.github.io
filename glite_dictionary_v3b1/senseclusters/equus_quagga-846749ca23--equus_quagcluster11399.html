Equus quagga

noun
extinct zebra-like mammal of South Africa
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwəs ˈkwɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəs ˈkwæɡə / (BrE)
an extinct South African mammal, related to the zebra, that disappeared in the late 19th century

Examples

The Equus quagga species
Scientists study preserved skins of the Equus quagga to understand its coloration patterns.
The Equus quagga once roamed the plains of South Africa in large herds.
The extinction of Equus quagga has become a symbol of lost biodiversity.
