hot air

noun
boastful or empty talk
5.5 4.3
Pronunciation: /hɑːt er/ /; / /hɑːt eər/ (AmE), /hɒt eə/ (BrE)
statements or talk that sound impressive but have no real meaning or value; empty or insincere talk

Examples

  • synonym: nonsense - language or ideas that are foolish or untrue
  • synonym: rhetoric - speech or writing intended to be effective but regarded as lacking in sincerity or meaningful content
