Oncorhynchus tshawytscha

noun
species of large Pacific salmon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑŋkoʊˈraɪŋkəs tʃəˈwɪtʃə / (AmE), / ɒŋkəˈrɪŋkəs tʃəˈwɪtʃə / (BrE)
a large Pacific salmon, also known as king salmon or Chinook salmon, valued as food; adults die after spawning

The Oncorhynchus tshawytscha is prized by anglers for its impressive size and strength.
