thermalize

verb
to equalize temperature
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːrməˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈθɜːməlaɪz / (BrE)
to make a system or its parts reach the same temperature by distributing heat or converting energy into heat
