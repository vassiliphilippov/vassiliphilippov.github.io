autosuggest

verb
to suggest automatically
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtoʊsəˈdʒɛst / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛst / (BrE)
to automatically provide suggestions to a user as they type text, especially in a search or input field

Examples

To autosuggest words
Many modern search engines autosuggest possible queries as you type in the search box.
The app will autosuggest contacts when you start entering a name or email address.
If you enable the feature, your phone can autosuggest words to speed up your typing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocomplete - to automatically complete a word or phrase as a user types
  • synonym: recommend - to suggest something as suitable or beneficial
