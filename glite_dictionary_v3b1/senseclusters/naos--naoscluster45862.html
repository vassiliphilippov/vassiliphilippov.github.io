naos

noun
temple inner chamber
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.ɒs /; / ˈneɪ.ɑs / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.ɒs / (BrE)
the inner chamber of an ancient Greek or Roman temple, usually housing the statue of the deity
