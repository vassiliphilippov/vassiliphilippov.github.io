keener

noun
overly eager person
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːnər / (AmE), / ˈkiːnə / (BrE)
a person who is very eager or enthusiastic, especially one who is keen to participate or impress, sometimes excessively so

Examples

A keener in class
In class, the keener always answers every question first.
Some students call her a keener because she volunteers for every project.
Being a keener can sometimes annoy classmates who prefer to stay quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
  • synonym: overachiever - a person who does more than is expected or required
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
