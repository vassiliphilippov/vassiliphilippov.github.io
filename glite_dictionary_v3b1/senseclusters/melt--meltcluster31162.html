melt

verb
change from solid to liquid by heat
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /melt/ (AmE), /melt/ (BrE)
to change or make something change from a solid to a liquid, usually by heating

Examples

To melt chocolate

If you leave the snowman in the sun, he will quickly melt.
She watched the butter melt in the hot frying pan.
