intercity

adjective
between cities
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɪti / (BrE)
relating to or connecting different cities, especially by means of transportation

Examples

Intercity train route
The intercity bus service makes it easy to travel between the two capitals.
She prefers taking intercity trains for long-distance journeys across the country.
An intercity highway connects several major urban centers in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracity - existing or occurring within a single city
