bump

noun
light blow or jolt from a collision
2.1
Pronunciation: / bʌmp / (AmE), / bʌmp / (BrE)
a light blow or jolt, especially one caused by a collision or impact

Examples

A car bump
After the minor bump in the parking lot, there was only a small dent.
She felt a sudden bump as the bus went over a pothole.
The car hit a bump in the road and shook violently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from an impact
  • synonym: knock - a blow, especially one that makes a noise
