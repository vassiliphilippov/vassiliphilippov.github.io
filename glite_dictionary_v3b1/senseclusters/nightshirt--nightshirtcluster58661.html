nightshirt

noun
loose sleeping garment for men
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A loose shirt-like garment worn in bed, typically by men, as nightwear.

Examples

A nightshirt for bed
After his bath, he changed into a clean nightshirt before going to bed.
In winter, he preferred a thick flannel nightshirt to stay warm at night.
The old photograph showed him standing in a long, striped nightshirt.
