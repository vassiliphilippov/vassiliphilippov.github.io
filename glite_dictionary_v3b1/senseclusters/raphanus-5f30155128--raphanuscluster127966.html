Raphanus

proper noun
plant genus; radish
Pronunciation: / ˈræfənəs / (AmE), / ˈræfənəs / (BrE)
a genus of plants in the mustard family, including the radish

Examples

Genus Raphanus
The common radish is classified under the genus Raphanus.
Botanists study Raphanus to understand the diversity of root vegetables.
Several wild species belong to the Raphanus genus in the mustard family.
