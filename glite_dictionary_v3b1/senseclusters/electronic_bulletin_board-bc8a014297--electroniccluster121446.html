electronic bulletin board

noun
system for posting and accessing messages electronically
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈbʊlɪtɪn bɔrd / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈbʊlɪtɪn bɔːd / (BrE)
a computer system running software that allows users to post messages and access shared information, often used for community or interest group communication

Examples

An electronic bulletin board system
Before social media, many people relied on an electronic bulletin board to find news and events online.
The club members used an electronic bulletin board to share updates and plan activities.
You must register before you can post messages on the electronic bulletin board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulletin board - a board for posting public messages or notices
