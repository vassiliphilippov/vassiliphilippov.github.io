express

verb
to obtain by pressing or squeezing
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs / (BrE)
to obtain a liquid or substance from something by squeezing or pressing it, especially mechanically

Examples

To express juice
In cheese-making, workers express whey from curds by applying gentle pressure.
She used a juicer to express fresh orange juice for breakfast.
The machine is designed to express oil from sunflower seeds efficiently.

Related Terms
