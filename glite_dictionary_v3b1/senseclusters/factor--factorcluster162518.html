factor

noun
element contributing to a result
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfæk.tər/ (AmE), /ˈfæk.tə/ (BrE)
something that contributes to or influences a result, situation, or outcome; an element or circumstance that helps produce an effect (including in statistics)

Examples

A key factor
A lack of preparation was a major factor in their defeat.
Cost was the most important factor in their decision to move.
Genetics is one factor that influences your risk for certain diseases.
High humidity is a significant factor in the development of mold.
Many different factors can affect your decision-making process.
Their success was due to a number of important factors working together.
'Gender' and 'treatment group' were considered as factors in the analysis.
Selecting the right factor is crucial for accurate statistical testing.
The experiment measured how each factor influenced the final result.
