hoghead

noun
large liquid cask
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌhɛd / (BrE)
a large cask or barrel, especially used for storing or measuring liquids

Examples

The old distillery still had a hoghead filled with aging whiskey in the cellar.
Workers carefully rolled the heavy hoghead across the warehouse floor.
A single hoghead could hold enough wine for an entire season's celebrations.
