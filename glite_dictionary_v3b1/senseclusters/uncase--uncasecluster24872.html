uncase

verb
to make lowercase
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkeɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkeɪs / (BrE)
to convert text from uppercase to lowercase, especially in computing contexts

Examples

To uncase text
The program will automatically uncase all user input before saving it to the database.
If you uncase the text, it will be easier to compare with other entries.
Developers often need to uncase strings to ensure consistent data processing.

Related Terms
