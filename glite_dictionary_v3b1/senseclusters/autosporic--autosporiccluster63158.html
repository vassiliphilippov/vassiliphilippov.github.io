autosporic

adjective
producing single spores
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˈspɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈspɒrɪk / (BrE)
producing spores independently, not in groups or clusters

Examples

Autosporic green algae
Some green algae are autosporic, releasing individual spores into their environment.
The autosporic nature of this microorganism allows it to reproduce without forming spore clusters.
Researchers observed that the autosporic species produced single spores rather than groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autosporous - producing spores independently
  • antonym: synzoosporic - producing spores in groups or clusters
