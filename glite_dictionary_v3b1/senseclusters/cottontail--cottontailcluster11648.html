cottontail

noun
a North American rabbit with a white-tailed underside
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtənˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒtənteɪl / (BrE)
a small North American rabbit with greyish or brownish fur and a tail that has a white underside

Examples

A cottontail rabbit
A cottontail's white tail is easy to spot when it runs away.
Several cottontails can often be seen feeding in the fields at dusk.
The cottontail darted into the bushes as soon as it heard our footsteps.
