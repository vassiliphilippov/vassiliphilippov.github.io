dovelike

adjective
peace-seeking attitude
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊvˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdəʊvˌlaɪk / (BrE)
favoring peace, negotiation, or nonmilitary solutions rather than aggressive or confrontational actions

Examples

Dovelike policy
The president's dovelike approach to international disputes earned him a reputation as a peacemaker.
Her dovelike views stood in contrast to her opponent's more aggressive policies.
Many citizens appreciated the mayor's dovelike response to the escalating tensions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hawkish - favoring aggressive or military solutions
