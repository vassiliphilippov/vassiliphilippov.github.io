soft spot

noun
point of vulnerability
Pronunciation: / sɔft spɑt / (AmE), / sɒft spɒt / (BrE)
a particular place or aspect where someone or something is most vulnerable or easily hurt

Examples

Find the soft spot in armor
Cybersecurity is a soft spot for many companies these days.
Her trust in people is her soft spot, which others sometimes take advantage of.
That old knee injury is still a soft spot for him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strong point - an aspect in which someone or something is especially strong or successful
  • synonym: weakness - a weak point or disadvantage
