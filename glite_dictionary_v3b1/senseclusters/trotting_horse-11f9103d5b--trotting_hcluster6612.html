trotting horse

noun
horse trained to trot, esp. in racing
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑtɪŋ hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈtrɒtɪŋ hɔːs / (BrE)
a horse trained to move at a trot, especially one used in harness racing

Examples

A trotting horse race
He owns a champion trotting horse that has won several harness races.
The trotting horse sped around the racetrack with remarkable stamina.
Training a trotting horse requires patience and a deep understanding of animal behavior.
