Synonyms

  • link
    1.4 65/m
    link is a direct, practical bond between things or places, while connection is a broader term for any relationship.
  • relation
    2.8 78/m
    relation is the way two or more people or things interact, while connection refers to a link or bond between them.
  • tie
    2.4 24/m
    tie is an often emotional or business bond between people or groups, while connection is a neutral link between things or ideas.
  • linkage
    3.9 5.94/m
    linkage is the act or system of joining parts together, while connection names a single relationship or link between parts.
  • nexus
    7.9 6.94/m
    nexus is a central point where many things meet or join, while connection is any simple link between two items.
