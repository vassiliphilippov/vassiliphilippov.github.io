prostration

noun
complete physical or mental exhaustion
Pronunciation: / prɑːˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / prɒˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a sudden collapse or complete physical or mental exhaustion, often resulting in inability to function

Examples

Heat prostration
After hours of intense labor in the heat, he collapsed from sheer prostration.
The news of his failure left him in a state of emotional prostration.
The runner’s prostration at the finish line showed how grueling the marathon was.
