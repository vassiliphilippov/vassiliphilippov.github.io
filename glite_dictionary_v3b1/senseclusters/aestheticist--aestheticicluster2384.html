aestheticist

noun
advocate of aestheticism
8.3
Pronunciation: /iːs.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ /; / /e.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /iːs.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ /; / /e.ˈθet.ɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports or practices the philosophy that art and beauty are important for their own sake

Examples

Aestheticist philosophy supporter
As an aestheticist, she valued the beauty of art above its practical use.
The aestheticist movement influenced many artists in the late nineteenth century.
He was called an aestheticist because he believed art should be appreciated for its own sake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aesthete - a person who has a deep appreciation for art and beauty
  • antonym: philistine - a person who is indifferent or hostile to art and culture
