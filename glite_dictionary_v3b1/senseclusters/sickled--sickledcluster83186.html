sickled

adjective
crescent-shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkəld / (AmE), / ˈsɪkəld / (BrE)
curved or shaped like a sickle or crescent, often describing cells or objects

Examples

Sickled red cells
The red blood cells appeared sickled under the microscope.
The plant's leaves were sickled, curving gracefully toward the ground.
Doctors observed sickled cells in the patient's blood sample, indicating a possible disorder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crescent - shaped like a segment of a ring tapering to points at the ends
  • antonym: straight - not curved or bent; having no bends or angles
