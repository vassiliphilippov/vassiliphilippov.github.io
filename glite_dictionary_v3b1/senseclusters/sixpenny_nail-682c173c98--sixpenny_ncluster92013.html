sixpenny nail

noun
a nail 2 inches long
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪksˌpɛni neɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪkspəni neɪl / (BrE)
A nail that is 2 inches (about 5 cm) long, traditionally denoted by 'sixpenny'.

Examples

A sixpenny nail
She accidentally bent a sixpenny nail while trying to drive it into the wood.
The carpenter reached for a sixpenny nail to secure the frame.
You will need a hammer and a box of sixpenny nails for this project.
