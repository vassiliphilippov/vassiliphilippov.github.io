offish

adjective
distant or unfriendly in manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔː-fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒ-fɪʃ / (BrE)
not friendly or willing to mix with others; distant and reserved in manner

Examples

An offish person
Although he was usually warm, today he was surprisingly offish.
He seemed rather offish at the party and kept to himself.
Her offish attitude made it hard for new colleagues to approach her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: friendly - kind and pleasant; easy to approach
  • synonym: standoffish - unfriendly or reluctant to mix with others
