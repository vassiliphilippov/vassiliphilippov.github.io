offish

adjective
distant or unfriendly in manner
8.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îÀê-f…™ É / (AmE), / Àà…í-f…™ É / (BrE)
not friendly or willing to mix with others; distant and reserved in manner

Examples

An offish person
Although he was usually warm, today he was surprisingly offish.
He seemed rather offish at the party and kept to himself.
Her offish attitude made it hard for new colleagues to approach her.

Synonyms

  • standoffish
    7.1 0.43/m
    standoffish is unwilling to engage and keeps clear distance in social situations, while offish describes a reserved, distant manner.
  • aloof
    6.3 2.54/m
    aloof is quietly distant and not very friendly in groups, while offish feels a bit cold and reluctant to mix.
  • distant
    4.9 3.77/m
    distant sounds remote and emotionally far in many contexts, while offish suggests a reserved, slightly unfriendly manner.
  • unapproachable
    6.6 0.25/m
    unapproachable looks hard to talk to and may stop people from trying, while offish simply acts reserved and avoids mixing.
