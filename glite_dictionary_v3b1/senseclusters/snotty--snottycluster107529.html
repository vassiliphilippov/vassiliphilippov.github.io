snotty

adjective
arrogant or conceited (informal)
8.0 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑti / (AmE), / ˈsnɒti / (BrE)
(informal) behaving as if you are more important or better than other people; conceited or arrogant.

Examples

Snotty attitude
Don't be so snotty just because you got a better grade.
She found his snotty attitude unbearable during the meeting.
The snotty receptionist barely acknowledged us when we walked in.
