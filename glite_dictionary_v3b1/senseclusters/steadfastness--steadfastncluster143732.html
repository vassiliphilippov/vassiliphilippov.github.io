steadfastness

noun
firm and unwavering determination
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛdˌfæstnəs /; / ˈstɛdˌfæst.nəs / (AmE), / ˈstɛdˌfɑːstnəs /; / ˈstɛd.fɑːst.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very firm, loyal, and unwavering, especially in difficult situations
