sanzen

verb
to consult Zen teacher
Pronunciation: / sɑnˈzɛn / (AmE), / sænˈzɛn / (BrE)
to meet privately with a Zen teacher for instruction, advice, or kōan discussion

Examples

To sanzen with a teacher
Each morning, the monks would sanzen with their teacher to deepen their understanding of Zen practice.
She hoped to sanzen and receive guidance on her meditation technique.
After months of study, he finally felt ready to sanzen and discuss his first kōan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dokusan - a private interview with a Zen master
