tansy mustard

noun
North American plant with bitter, tansy-like leaves
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtænzi ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈtænzi ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
A North American herb with pinnate, bitter-tasting leaves that resemble those of tansy, commonly found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Examples

The tansy mustard plant
Many wild animals avoid tansy mustard because of its bitter taste.
She recognized the sharp scent of tansy mustard growing by the roadside.
The fields were full of tansy mustard blooming in early spring.
