hacktivist

noun
political computer hacker
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhæktɪvɪst / (AmE), / ˈhæktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses computer hacking skills to promote a political or social cause, often by disrupting websites or systems

Examples

A hacktivist attack
A group of hacktivists launched a cyberattack to protest government censorship.
The hacktivist was arrested after exposing confidential documents online.
Many hacktivists believe their actions help raise awareness about important issues.
A well-known hacktivist exposed confidential documents to raise awareness about government surveillance.
The group recruited a skilled hacktivist to help with their online protest campaign.
Many hacktivists believe their actions are justified by the causes they support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social or political change
  • antonym: cybercriminal - a person who commits crimes using computers or the internet
  • synonym: hacker - a person skilled in computer programming and hacking
