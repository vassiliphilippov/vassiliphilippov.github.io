target

verb
direct toward a goal or person
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑrɡɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt / (BrE)
to direct something, such as an action, effort, or object, towards a particular goal or person; to aim at

Examples

To target a group
In his speech, he targets people’s growing concerns about safety.
She targeted her criticism at the policy makers.
The company will target its new products at younger consumers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aim - to point or direct something at a target
  • synonym: direct - to focus attention, action, or emotion toward someone or something
