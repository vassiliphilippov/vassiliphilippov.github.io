pineapple weed

noun
aromatic annual weed with yellow, rayless flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnˌæpəl wid / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnˌæpəl wiːd / (BrE)
a small, aromatic annual plant (Matricaria discoidea) of Pacific coastal regions, with feathery leaves and yellow, rayless flower heads, often found along roadsides and paths

Examples

Dried pineapple weed flowers
Some people use dried pineapple weed flowers to make a caffeine-free herbal tea.
The scent of pineapple weed is reminiscent of pineapple when the leaves are crushed.
You can often spot pineapple weed growing between cracks in the pavement during the summer.
