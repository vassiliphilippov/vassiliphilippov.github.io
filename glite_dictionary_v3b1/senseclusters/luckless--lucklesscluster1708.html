luckless

adjective
unlucky; having bad luck
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌk.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlʌk.ləs / (BrE)
having or experiencing bad luck; marked by misfortune.

Examples

The luckless hero
In the novel, the luckless hero faces one setback after another.
She always seemed to pick the luckless numbers in every lottery.
The luckless traveler missed his train due to a sudden strike.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fortunate - having good luck; favored by fortune
  • synonym: unlucky - having or experiencing bad luck
