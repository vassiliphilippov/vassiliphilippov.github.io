nephropathy

noun
kidney disease or disorder
7.2
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfrɑːpəθi / (AmE), / nɛˈfrɒpəθi / (BrE)
A disease or disorder affecting the kidneys.

Examples

Diabetic nephropathy
Diabetic nephropathy is a common complication in people with long-standing diabetes.
Doctors are researching new treatments for chronic nephropathy to improve patient outcomes.
The patient was diagnosed with nephropathy after showing signs of decreased kidney function.
