tzimmes

noun
sweet Jewish stew
Pronunciation: / ˈtsɪmɪs / (AmE), / ˈtsɪmɪs / (BrE)
a sweet Jewish stew made with carrots, sweet potatoes, and sometimes meat or dried fruit, often served on holidays

Examples

A sweet tzimmes dish
For Rosh Hashanah, her grandmother always makes a traditional tzimmes with carrots and dried prunes.
The sweet aroma of tzimmes filled the kitchen as the family gathered for dinner.
He learned to cook tzimmes from a recipe passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
