ovenware

noun
heat-resistant cooking and serving dishes
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʌvənˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈʌv.ən.weə / (BrE)
Dishes or containers made of heat-resistant material, used for cooking food in an oven and often for serving it as well.

Examples

Heat-resistant ovenware
Good ovenware is essential for even cooking and easy cleaning.
She bought a new set of ovenware for baking casseroles and lasagnas.
The recipe suggests serving the dish straight from the ovenware to the table.
