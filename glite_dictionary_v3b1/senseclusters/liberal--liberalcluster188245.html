liberal

adjective
generous; given or giving freely
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
(of a person or their giving) characterized by generosity; given or giving freely and in large amounts.

Examples

Liberal amounts
His liberal use of compliments made everyone at the party feel appreciated.
She made a liberal donation to the local food bank, helping hundreds in need.
The chef was liberal with spices, making the dish exceptionally flavorful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary or expected
  • antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
