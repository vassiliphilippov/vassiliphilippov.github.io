vagrant

noun
person who wanders without a home or job
6.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈveɪ.grənt/ /; / /ˈvæ.grənt/ (AmE), /ˈveɪ.grənt/ /; / /ˈvæ.grənt/ (BrE)
a person without a settled home or regular work who wanders from place to place, typically living by begging

Examples

A homeless vagrant
A group of vagrants gathered under the bridge seeking shelter from the rain.
She offered some food to a vagrant standing at the corner, hoping to help.
The police arrested the vagrant who had been sleeping in the park for several nights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tramp - person who travels from place to place, especially without a home or job
  • synonym: vagabond - person who wanders from place to place without a permanent home
