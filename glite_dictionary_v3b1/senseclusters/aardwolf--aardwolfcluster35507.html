aardwolf

noun
insect-eating striped mammal of Africa
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.dwʊlf/ /; / /ˈɑːrd.wʊlf/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.dwʊlf/ (BrE)
A nocturnal mammal of southern and eastern Africa, related to the hyena but feeding mainly on termites and other insects, with distinctive black stripes on its body.

Examples

The aardwolf animal
The aardwolf spends much of the night foraging for termites in the savannah.
Unlike its more aggressive relatives, the aardwolf has a diet based almost entirely on insects.
