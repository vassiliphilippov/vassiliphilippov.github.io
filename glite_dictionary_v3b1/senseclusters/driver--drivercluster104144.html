driver

noun
program for controlling computer hardware
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
(Computing) A program that controls how a computer interacts with a peripheral device, such as a printer or graphics card.

Examples

Printer driver
Each device connected to the system requires its own driver for communication.
The printer would not work until I installed the correct driver on my computer.
Updating your graphics driver can often fix visual glitches in games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: device driver - software that controls a particular type of device attached to a computer
