superannuation fund

noun
pension fund for retired workers
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˌænjuˈeɪʃən fʌnd / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərˌanjʊˈeɪʃən fʌnd / (BrE)
A fund set aside by an employer or by employees to provide retirement income or pensions to workers when they retire.

Examples

A superannuation fund plan
In Australia, a superannuation fund is a common way to save for retirement.
She checked her superannuation fund balance before retiring.
The company contributes regularly to each employee's superannuation fund.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pension fund - A fund from which pensions are paid
  • synonym: superannuation - The act of retiring on a pension
