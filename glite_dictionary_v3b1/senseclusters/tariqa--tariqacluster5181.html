tariqa

noun
method or procedure
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈriːkə / (AmE), / tɑːˈriːkə / (BrE)
a method, way, or procedure for doing something, especially in a systematic or traditional manner

Examples

A tariqa method
She explained a simple tariqa for organizing her research notes that everyone could follow.
The teacher introduced a new tariqa to help students remember vocabulary more effectively.
Each master has their own tariqa for teaching the art to beginners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procedure - an established or official way of doing something
