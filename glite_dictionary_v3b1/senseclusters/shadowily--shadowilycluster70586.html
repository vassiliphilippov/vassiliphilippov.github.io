shadowily

adverb
secretively or mysteriously
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊɪli / (BrE)
in a secretive, mysterious, or concealed manner, often suggesting something hidden or suspicious

Examples

Shadowily moving
He moved shadowily through the alley, careful not to be seen by anyone.
Rumors spread shadowily among the staff, creating an air of suspicion in the office.
The deal was arranged shadowily, with few details made public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: furtively - in a way that attempts to avoid notice or attention
  • antonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden
