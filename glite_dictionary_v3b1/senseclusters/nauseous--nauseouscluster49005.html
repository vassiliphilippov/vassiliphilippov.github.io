nauseous

adjective
feeling sick to the stomach
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːʃəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːziəs / (BrE)
feeling as if you are about to vomit; affected by nausea.

Examples

Feel nauseous after eating
Many people feel nauseous after riding a rollercoaster.
She became nauseous during the long, bumpy car ride to the mountains.
The strong smell of paint made me feel nauseous all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: queasy - feeling sick to the stomach
  • synonym: sick - feeling ill, especially wanting to vomit
