fishing boat

noun
boat used for fishing
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ boʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ bəʊt / (BrE)
a boat equipped and used for catching fish, often with a compartment to keep the catch alive

Examples

A fishing boat deck
Fishermen prepared their fishing boat for another day on the water.
Seagulls circled above the fishing boat as the crew sorted the catch.
The fishing boat returned at dawn with its nets full of fresh fish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rowboat - a small boat propelled by oars
  • synonym: trawler - a large fishing boat equipped for trawling
