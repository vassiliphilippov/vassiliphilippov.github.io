nonrespondent

noun
party not replying
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
a person or party who does not reply or file an answer to a legal claim, motion, or petition

Examples

Court nonrespondent
The court issued a default judgment against the nonrespondent for failing to answer the complaint.
If a nonrespondent does not appear in court, the case may proceed without their input.
The lawyer explained the consequences of being labeled a nonrespondent in the legal process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defendant - a person against whom a legal claim is brought
  • antonym: respondent - a person who replies to a legal claim or petition
