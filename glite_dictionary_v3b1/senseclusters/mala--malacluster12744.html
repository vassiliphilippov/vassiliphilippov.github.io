mala

noun
spicy numbing seasoning
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːlɑː / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlɑː / (BrE)
a spicy and numbing seasoning or dish, especially in Sichuan cuisine, made with chili peppers and Sichuan peppercorns

Examples

Mala hot pot
The chef prepared a traditional mala hot pot filled with beef, tofu, and vegetables.
I love the unique numbing sensation that mala brings to spicy dishes.
Many people enjoy adding extra mala to their noodles for a stronger flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sichuan peppercorn - a spice that produces a numbing sensation, used in Chinese cooking
  • synonym: hot pot - a Chinese dish where ingredients are cooked in a simmering pot of broth at the table
