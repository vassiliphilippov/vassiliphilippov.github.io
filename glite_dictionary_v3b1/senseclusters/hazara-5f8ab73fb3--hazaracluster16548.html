Hazara

noun
Persian dialect of Hazaras
Pronunciation: / həˈzɑːrə / (AmE), / həˈzɑːrə / (BrE)
the variety of Persian, also known as Hazaragi, spoken by the Hazara people of central Afghanistan

Examples

Hazara language
Many people in central Afghanistan speak Hazara as their first language.
She studied the Hazara dialect to better understand local culture.
The Hazara language has unique words not found in standard Persian.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Dari - the variety of Persian spoken in Afghanistan
  • synonym: Hazaragi - the dialect of Persian spoken by the Hazara people
