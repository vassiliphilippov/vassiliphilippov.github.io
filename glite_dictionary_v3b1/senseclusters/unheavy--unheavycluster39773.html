unheavy

adjective
not heavy
6.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛvi / (BrE)
not heavy; light in weight or easy to lift or carry

Examples

An unheavy box
The package was surprisingly unheavy despite its large size.
She found the suitcase unheavy enough to carry up the stairs by herself.
After removing some books, the backpack became much more unheavy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heavy - having great weight; difficult to lift or move
  • synonym: light - not heavy; easy to lift or carry
