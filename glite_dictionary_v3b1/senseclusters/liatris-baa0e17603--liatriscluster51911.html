Liatris

noun
a genus of North American flowering herbs
Pronunciation: / laɪˈætrɪs /; / liˈætrɪs / (AmE), / laɪˈætrɪs /; / liˈætrɪs / (BrE)
a genus of perennial North American herbs with aromatic, cormous roots and spikes of purple or white flowers, commonly called blazing star or gayfeather
