petroleum jelly

noun
semi-solid ointment and lubricant
2.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtroʊ.liː.əm ˈdʒe.li/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtrəʊ.liː.əm ˈdʒe.li/ (BrE)
A soft, semisolid mixture derived from petroleum, used in ointments, skin care, and as a lubricant.

Examples
