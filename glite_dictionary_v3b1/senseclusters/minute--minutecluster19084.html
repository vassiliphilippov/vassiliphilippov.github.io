minute

adjective
extremely small; tiny
Pronunciation: / maɪˈnut / (AmE), / maɪˈnjuːt / (BrE)
extremely small; tiny or insignificant in size, amount, or degree.

Examples

Minute particles
Dust mites are so minute that they cannot be seen with the naked eye.
The scientist examined the minute details through a powerful microscope.
There was only a minute difference between the two samples, but it was significant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: huge - very large in size or amount
  • synonym: microscopic - so small as to be visible only with a microscope
  • synonym: tiny - very small in size
