stodginess

noun
quality of being dull or overly solemn

Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːdʒ.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈstɑːdʒ.iː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈstɒdʒ.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈstɒdʒ.iː.nəs/ (BrE)
