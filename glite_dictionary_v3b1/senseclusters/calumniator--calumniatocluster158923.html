calumniator

noun
person who slanders
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈlʌmniˌeɪtər / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmnɪeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who makes false and damaging statements about someone in order to harm their reputation

Examples

The calumniator spreads lies
The newspaper article identified the anonymous calumniator who had spread rumors about the mayor.
She refused to let the words of a calumniator damage her confidence.
Lawsuits were filed against the calumniator for making false accusations online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defamer - a person who damages the reputation of another by false statements
  • synonym: slanderer - a person who makes false spoken statements that damage someone's reputation
  • antonym: supporter - a person who helps or defends someone
