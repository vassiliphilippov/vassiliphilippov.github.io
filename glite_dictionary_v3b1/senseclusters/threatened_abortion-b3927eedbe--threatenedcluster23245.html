threatened abortion

noun
early pregnancy at risk of miscarriage
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɛtənd əˈbɔrʃən / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənd əˈbɔːʃən / (BrE)
a condition during early pregnancy in which symptoms such as vaginal bleeding suggest a possible impending miscarriage, but the pregnancy may still continue

Examples

A threatened abortion diagnosis
Because she experienced cramping and spotting, the obstetrician diagnosed her with a threatened abortion.
Patients with signs of a threatened abortion are usually advised to rest and avoid strenuous activity.
The doctor explained that her bleeding could indicate a threatened abortion, but the pregnancy might still proceed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miscarriage - the spontaneous loss of a pregnancy before the fetus can survive
  • synonym: spontaneous abortion - medical term for naturally occurring miscarriage
