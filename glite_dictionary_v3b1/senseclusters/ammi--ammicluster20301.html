ammi

noun
South Asian mother
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
an informal word for 'mother' used in Urdu, Hindi, and some South Asian communities, often as a form of address

Examples

My ammi cooks dinner
Every Sunday, ammi cooked her special biryani for the whole family.
He called out to his ammi when he came home from school.
During the festival, everyone thanked their ammi for her love and care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mom - an informal word for mother in English
  • synonym: mother - a female parent
