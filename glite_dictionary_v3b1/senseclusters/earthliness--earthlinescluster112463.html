earthliness

noun
focus on material things
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːrθ.lɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈɜːrθ.lɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being concerned with or connected to the physical, material, or worldly aspects of life, rather than spiritual matters

Examples

The earthliness of life
Her earthliness was clear in her love for fine food and beautiful clothes.
The novel explores the tension between earthliness and spiritual longing in its main character.
He admired her earthliness, finding her practical and grounded in everyday life.
Her earthliness showed in the way she appreciated simple pleasures like walking in the woods.
The artist's work is admired for its earthliness and honest depiction of rural life.
He balanced his earthliness with a deep interest in philosophy and abstract ideas.
The novel explores the earthliness of daily struggles and simple pleasures.
He admired the earthliness in her art, which celebrated ordinary life.
Despite his fame, the actor's earthliness made him approachable to everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spirituality - the quality of being concerned with the human spirit or soul rather than material things
  • synonym: worldliness - concern with material values or ordinary life rather than spiritual matters
