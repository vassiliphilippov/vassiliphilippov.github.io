act up

verb
behave badly or mischievously
4.4
Pronunciation: / ækt ʌp / (AmE), / ækt ʌp / (BrE)
to behave badly, disruptively, or to not work properly (of people or things)

Examples

Kids act up in class
His old knee injury tends to act up in cold weather.
If your dog starts to act up, put it back on its leash.
The children began to act up as soon as the teacher left the room.
If your knee starts to act up again, you should see a doctor.
My printer tends to act up whenever I need it most.
The children began to act up as soon as their parents left the room.

  • synonym: malfunction - to fail to function properly
  • synonym: misbehave - to behave badly or improperly
