go to work

verb
travel to one's place of employment
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə wɜrk / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə wɜːk / (BrE)
to leave home or another place to travel to one's place of employment; to begin the journey or commute to work

Examples

To go to work in the morning
After breakfast, she quickly went to work so she wouldn't be late.
He's going to work by train this week while his car is being repaired.
I usually go to work at eight o'clock every morning.
