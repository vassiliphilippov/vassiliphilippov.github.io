Thalidone

noun
trade name for chlorthalidone diuretic
Pronunciation: / θəˈlɪdoʊn / (AmE), / θəˈlɪdəʊn / (BrE)
A proprietary name for the drug chlorthalidone, a diuretic used to treat high blood pressure and edema.

Examples

Thalidone medicine
Thalidone is often chosen for its effectiveness in reducing fluid retention.
Patients taking Thalidone should have their blood electrolytes checked regularly.
The doctor prescribed Thalidone to help manage her high blood pressure.
