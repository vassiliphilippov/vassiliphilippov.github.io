ironbound determination

noun
unbreakable, firm determination
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnbaʊnd ˌdɛtərməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈaɪəbaʊnd ˌdɛtəmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
An unwavering and unbreakable determination; a very strong will to achieve something despite obstacles.

Examples

With ironbound determination, he overcame every setback and reached his goal.
