theological system

noun
organized set of religious beliefs
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑlədʒɪkəl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / θɪˈɒlədʒɪkəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a particular set of religious beliefs and doctrines organized into a systematic structure

Examples

A theological system of beliefs
Calvinism is an influential theological system within Protestant Christianity.
Different faith traditions each have a distinct theological system for interpreting scripture.
The Roman Catholic Church developed its own theological system over centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doctrine - a belief or set of beliefs held and taught by a Church or other group
  • synonym: theology - the study of the nature of God and religious belief
