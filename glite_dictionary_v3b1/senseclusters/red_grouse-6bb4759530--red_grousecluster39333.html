red grouse

noun
a moorland game bird of Great Britain
8.2
Pronunciation: / rɛd ɡraʊs / (AmE), / rɛd ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a reddish-brown grouse found on upland moors in Great Britain, known for its distinctive coloring and importance in game shooting
