bowling

noun
game of rolling balls to knock over pins
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
a game in which players roll a heavy ball toward pins, aiming to knock them over

Examples

Go bowling with friends
She scored a perfect 300 in her last bowling game.
The local bowling alley offers league games every Tuesday night.
We went bowling with friends last weekend and had a great time.
She took up bowling as a fun way to stay active and social.
The local league holds bowling tournaments every Saturday afternoon.
We went bowling last night and had a great time at the alley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowls - lawn game using biased balls to approach a target ball
  • synonym: duckpin bowling - variation of bowling with smaller pins and balls
  • synonym: tenpin bowling - bowling played with ten pins at the end of the lane
