basic requirement

noun
minimum necessary standard
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsɪk rɪˈkwaɪərmənt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪk rɪˈkwaɪəmənt / (BrE)
a necessary condition or minimum standard that must be met

Examples

A basic requirement
Access to clean water is a basic requirement for good health.
Her application was rejected because she did not meet the basic requirement for language proficiency.
The job listing stated a college degree as a basic requirement for applying.

Related Terms

  • antonym: luxury - something desirable but not necessary
  • synonym: prerequisite - something required as a prior condition
  • synonym: requirement - something that is needed or demanded
