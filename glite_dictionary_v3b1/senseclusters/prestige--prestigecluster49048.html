prestige

noun
video‑game rank or tier
7.3
Pronunciation: /pre.ˈstiːdʒ/ /; / /prə.ˈstiːdʒ/ (AmE), /pre.ˈstiːdʒ/ /; / /prə.ˈstiːdʒ/ (BrE)
(in video games) a special rank, tier, or status (often earned by reaching milestones or by resetting progress) that indicates achievement and may unlock rewards or exclusive items.

Examples

Prestige level unlocked
Many players reach prestige after completing all seasonal challenges and resetting their rank.
The tournament awarded exclusive skins to those with high prestige from previous seasons.
She debated whether to enter prestige mode and lose her unlocked weapons for a new challenge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rank - a player's position or level in a game or system
  • synonym: tier - a level in a ranking system, often used to sort players or rewards
