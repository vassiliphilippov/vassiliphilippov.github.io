flea infestation

noun
invasion of fleas on a host
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfli ɪnˌfɛsˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfli ɪnˌfɛsˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an invasion of fleas on a host animal, especially causing irritation or disease

Examples

A flea infestation on pets
After adopting the stray cat, they discovered it had a flea infestation that required professional care.
If left untreated, a flea infestation can cause serious health problems for your pet.
There was a severe flea infestation in the old carpet that needed to be treated.
