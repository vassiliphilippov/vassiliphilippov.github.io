directionality

noun
property of sensitivity in specific directions
7.0 6.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.rek.ʃə.ˈnæl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /dɪ.rek.ʃə.ˈnæl.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /dɪ.rek.ʃə.ˈnæl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /dɪ.rek.ʃə.ˈnæl.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)
