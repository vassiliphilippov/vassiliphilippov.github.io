fore-wing

noun
front wing of a four-winged insect
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
either of the front pair of wings on an insect that has four wings, such as beetles or butterflies

Examples

An insect's fore-wing
Damage to an insect's fore-wing can greatly affect its ability to fly.
In some beetles, the fore-wing hardens into a protective cover called an elytron.
The butterfly's fore-wing displays a striking pattern of colors.

Related Terms
