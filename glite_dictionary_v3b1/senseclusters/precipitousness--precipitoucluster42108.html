precipitousness

noun
quality of suddenness or haste
6.3
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪp.ɪ.tʃuːs.nəs / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪp.ɪ.tjuːs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of happening suddenly or with great haste, often without warning or preparation.

Examples

Precipitousness of a decision
Business leaders criticized the precipitousness of the government's sudden policy change.
She was alarmed by the precipitousness with which the storm arrived.
The precipitousness of his resignation left everyone shocked and unprepared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abruptness - The quality of being sudden or unexpected
  • synonym: suddenness - The quality of occurring quickly or unexpectedly
