nonmotile

adjective
unable to move (of cells or microbes)
8.6
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmoʊtɪl / (AmE), / nɒnˈməʊtaɪl / (BrE)
(of cells, spores, or microorganisms) not capable of movement.

Examples

Nonmotile cells
Certain algae produce nonmotile spores that rely on water currents for dispersal.
The bacteria observed under the microscope were nonmotile and remained stationary.
The test results showed a mix of motile and nonmotile cells in the culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: motile - (of cells, spores, or microorganisms) capable of movement
