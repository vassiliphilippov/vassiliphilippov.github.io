liquefiable

adjective
able to be made into a liquid
2.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /lɪ.ˈkwɪ.fə.bəl/ /; / /lɪ.ˈkwiː.fə.bəl/ (AmE), /lɪ.ˈkwɪ.fə.bəl/ /; / /lɪ.ˈkwiː.fə.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

A liquefiable metal

Not all solids are liquefiable with common laboratory methods.
The gas is liquefiable under high pressure and low temperature.
