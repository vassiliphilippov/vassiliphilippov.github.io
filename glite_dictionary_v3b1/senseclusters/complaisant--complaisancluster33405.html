complaisant

adjective
willing to please; obliging
9.6 6.6
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpleɪ.sənt/ /; / /kəm.ˈpleɪ.zənt/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpleɪ.sənt/ /; / /kəm.ˈpleɪ.zənt/ (BrE)
willing to please others by being polite and accommodating; obliging

Examples

A complaisant person
His complaisant manner made him popular among friends, but easy to take advantage of.
She was always complaisant, eager to fulfill every request from her colleagues.
The complaisant concierge arranged tickets to the sold-out show without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accommodating - helpful and eager to make others comfortable
  • synonym: obliging - helpful and willing to do favors for others
  • antonym: uncooperative - not willing to help or do what is asked
