ossiculum

noun
a small bone, esp. in the ear
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪkjələm / (AmE), / ɒˈsɪkjʊləm / (BrE)
A small bone, especially one of the three tiny bones in the middle ear.

Examples

The ossiculum bone
A fracture of the ossiculum can result in impaired hearing.
Ossicula are essential for the transmission of sound in the human ear.
The surgeon carefully examined the ossiculum for signs of damage.
