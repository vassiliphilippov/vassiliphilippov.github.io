verdantly

adverb
naively; unsophisticatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdəntli / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that is inexperienced, naive, or unsophisticated.

Examples

Verdantly answers a question
He answered the difficult question verdantly, not realizing the complexity involved.
The new employee approached her first project verdantly, eager but lacking experience.
They verdantly believed that success would come easily without any setbacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innocently - in a way that shows lack of guilt or experience
  • synonym: naively - in a way that shows a lack of experience or sophistication
  • antonym: worldly - in a way that shows experience and sophistication
