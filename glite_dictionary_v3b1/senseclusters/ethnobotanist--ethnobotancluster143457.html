ethnobotanist

noun
plant culture scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊˈbɑːtənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊˈbɒtənɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies how people in different cultures use and understand plants

Examples

An ethnobotanist studies plants
The ethnobotanist traveled to remote regions to learn about traditional plant medicines.
As an ethnobotanist, she worked closely with local communities to document their agricultural practices.
A skilled ethnobotanist can help preserve valuable knowledge about edible and medicinal plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropologist - a scientist who studies human societies and cultures
  • synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants
