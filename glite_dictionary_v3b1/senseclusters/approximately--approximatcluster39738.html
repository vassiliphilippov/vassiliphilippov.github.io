approximately

adverb
not exactly but nearly; roughly
5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈprɑksəmətli / (AmE), / əˈprɒksɪmətli / (BrE)
used to show that a number or amount is not exact but close to the correct value

Examples

About approximately fifty
She earns approximately fifty thousand dollars a year.
The movie lasted approximately two hours and fifteen minutes.
There were approximately ten people waiting in line outside the store.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exactly - in a precise, accurate way
  • synonym: roughly - not exactly but approximately; about
