wobbler

noun
fishing lure
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑblər / (AmE), / ˈwɒblə / (BrE)
a fishing lure designed to move with a side-to-side wobbling motion to attract fish

Examples

Fishing wobbler
He attached a wobbler to his fishing line to attract more fish.
Many anglers prefer using a wobbler when fishing for pike in the lake.
The shiny wobbler moved through the water, catching the attention of several trout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bait - something used to attract and catch fish
  • synonym: spinner - a fishing lure that spins to attract fish
