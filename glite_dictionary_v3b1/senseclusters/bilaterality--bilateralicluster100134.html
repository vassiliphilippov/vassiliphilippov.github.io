bilaterality

noun
quality of bilateral symmetry
4.0
Pronunciation: / baɪˌlætəˈrælɪti / (AmE), / baɪˌlatəˈralɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of having symmetry on both sides of a central vertical plane; bilateral symmetry.

Examples

Bilateral bilaterality in animals
In biology class, we learned about the evolutionary significance of bilaterality in complex organisms.
Most animals exhibit bilaterality, making movement and sensory perception more efficient.
The examination of the fossil showed clear bilaterality in its structure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asymmetry - lack of equality or equivalence between parts or aspects
  • synonym: symmetry - balance of proportions on opposite sides of a dividing line
