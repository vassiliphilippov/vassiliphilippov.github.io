actualize

verb
represent or describe realistically
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tʃu.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaktjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to represent or describe something in a realistic or true-to-life way

Examples

Actualize feelings in art
Her paintings actualize the emotions that words cannot express.
The director sought to actualize the lives of ordinary people in the film.
Writers often actualize their experiences through their fictional characters.
His paintings powerfully actualise the harsh realities of urban life.
The documentary aims to actualise the experiences of refugees for the public.
The novelist sought to actualise the struggles of everyday people in her latest book.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actualize - to make actual or real; to realize
  • synonym: realize - to make real or give reality to
