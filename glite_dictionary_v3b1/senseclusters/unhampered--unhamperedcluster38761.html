unhampered

adjective
not restricted or interfered with
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhæmpərd /; / ʌnˈhæmpɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhæmpəd / (BrE)
not restricted, blocked, or interfered with; free to act or progress

Examples

Unhampered progress
He worked unhampered by unnecessary bureaucracy in the new office.
She moved through the crowd unhampered by doubt or hesitation.
The river flowed unhampered after the debris was cleared away.
Economic growth was unhampered by government regulations.
He spoke with an unhampered honesty that surprised the audience.
The artist enjoyed an unhampered creative process in her new studio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free - not under the control or in the power of another; able to act or be as one wishes
  • antonym: hampered - impeded or restricted in movement or progress
  • antonym: restricted - limited in movement, action, or progress
  • synonym: unrestricted - not restrained or limited
