hypoplastic

adjective
underdeveloped body part
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊˈplastɪk / (BrE)
having underdeveloped or abnormally small organs or body parts

Examples

A hypoplastic organ
The doctor explained that the child had a hypoplastic left heart.
A hypoplastic thumb may require surgical intervention to improve function.
The X-ray revealed a hypoplastic jawbone in the patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atrophic - relating to the wasting away or decrease in size of a body part
  • antonym: hyperplastic - having an abnormal increase in the number of cells, leading to enlargement
