Moluccan

noun
person from the Moluccas
9.2
Pronunciation: / məˈlʌkən / (AmE), / məˈlʌkən / (BrE)
a person from the Moluccas islands of eastern Indonesia, or a descendant of such a person

Examples

A Moluccan person
A Moluccan elder shared stories about life on the islands with the visiting students.
Many Moluccans maintain strong cultural traditions from their homeland.
The museum features artifacts donated by the local Moluccan community.
