Ascaphidae

noun
family of frogs (tailed frog)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈskæfəˌdiː / (AmE), / əˈskæfɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of frogs comprising the tailed frog, native to fast-flowing streams in North America.

Examples

Ascaphidae frog family
Members of the Ascaphidae family are found mainly in the streams of western North America.
Scientists study Ascaphidae to learn more about amphibian evolution.
The Ascaphidae are considered one of the most primitive groups of frogs.
