cremator

noun
person who cremates
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːmeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkriːmeɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to perform cremation or operate a crematory, reducing bodies to ashes

Examples

A skilled cremator
The cremator carefully explained the process to the grieving family before beginning his work.
After years as a cremator, she approached her duties with professionalism and compassion.
The local cremator is responsible for operating the furnace and handling the remains respectfully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crematory - a place where bodies are cremated
  • synonym: undertaker - a person whose job is to arrange funerals
