meteorologist

noun
weather scientist
6.6 2.91/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåmiÀêti…ôÀàr…ël…ôd í…™st / (AmE), / ÀåmiÀêti…ôÀàr…íl…ôd í…™st / (BrE)
A scientist who studies and forecasts the weather by examining processes in the earth's atmosphere.

Examples

A meteorologist forecasts weather
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a meteorologist and studying hurricanes.
During the broadcast, the meteorologist explained the formation of the approaching storm.
The meteorologist predicted heavy rainfall for the weekend.

Synonyms

  • aerologist
    9.8 0.00/m
    aerologist studies broader atmospheric phenomena and climate patterns, while meteorologist focuses on weather systems and short-term forecasts.
  • weather forecaster
    2.2 0.18/m
    a weather forecaster predicts and reports short-term conditions to the public, while meteorologist studies causes and models to make forecasts.

Related Terms
