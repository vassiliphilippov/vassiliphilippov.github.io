Vibramycin

noun
trademarked trade name for doxycycline
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪbrəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˌvɪbrəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked trade name for doxycycline, an antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.
