simonist

noun
follower of Simon Magus
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪmənɪst / (BrE)
a follower of Simon Magus or a member of the early Simonian sect, an ancient Gnostic movement

Examples

Ancient simonist
The historian explained that a simonist was part of a unique early Christian sect.
Ancient writings mention several simonists who spread the teachings of Simon Magus.
Few people today know about the beliefs held by a simonist in the first centuries AD.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gnostic - a member of an early Christian movement emphasizing esoteric knowledge
