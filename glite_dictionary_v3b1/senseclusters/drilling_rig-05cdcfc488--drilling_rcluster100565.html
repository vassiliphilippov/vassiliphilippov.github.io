drilling rig

noun
equipment for drilling oil or gas wells
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪlɪŋ rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪlɪŋ rɪɡ / (BrE)
A structure and equipment used to drill wells for oil or gas extraction.

Examples

An oil drilling rig
A new drilling rig was assembled at the edge of the oil field.
The company invested millions in a state-of-the-art offshore drilling rig.
The workers operated the drilling rig in harsh weather conditions.
