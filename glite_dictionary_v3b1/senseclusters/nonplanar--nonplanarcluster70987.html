nonplanar

adjective
not flat
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpleɪnər / (AmE), / nɒnˈpleɪnə / (BrE)
not lying in a single flat surface or plane; having a shape that is not flat

Examples

Nonplanar roof design
The architect designed a nonplanar roof that curves in several directions.
Some molecules are nonplanar due to the arrangement of their atoms.
The sculpture's nonplanar surface made it interesting to touch and observe.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: flat - having a level or even surface
  • antonym: planar - lying in a single flat surface or plane
