receipting

noun
issuing receipts
3.7
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈsiː.tɪŋ/ /; / /rə.ˈsiː.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈsiː.tɪŋ/ /; / /rə.ˈsiː.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or process of officially acknowledging and recording that something (such as a payment or item) has been received, usually by making a receipt or entering it into a system

Examples

The receipting process
The finance department is responsible for receipting all donations received by the organization.
Proper receipting ensures that every transaction is recorded and acknowledged.
After receipting the payment, the clerk handed the customer a printed slip.
She is responsible for receipting all incoming payments at the end of each business day.
Accurate receipting is essential to maintain proper financial records in any organization.
The new software has automated much of the receipting process for the accounting team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acknowledgment - the act of accepting or recognizing receipt of something
  • synonym: auditing - the process of examining financial records for accuracy
  • antonym: payment - the act of giving money for goods or services
  • synonym: recording - the act of keeping a written or digital account of something
