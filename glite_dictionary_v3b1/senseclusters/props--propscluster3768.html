props

noun
(informal) respect or acknowledgment
8.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / prÉ‘ps / (AmE), / prÉ’ps / (BrE)
(informal) Respect or proper acknowledgment, often used to express approval or admiration for someone's actions or achievements.

Examples

Give props to someone
Much props to the organizers for making the event a success.
She always gives props to her team for their hard work.
You have to give him props for coming up with such a creative idea.
