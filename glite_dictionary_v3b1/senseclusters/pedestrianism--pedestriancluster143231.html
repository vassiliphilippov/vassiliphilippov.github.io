pedestrianism

noun
walking as activity
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɛstriəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / pɪˈdɛstriənɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or activity of walking, especially as a regular means of travel or exercise

Examples

Urban pedestrianism
Many cities promote pedestrianism to reduce traffic congestion and improve public health.
The rise of pedestrianism has led to more walking paths and safer crosswalks.
He advocates for pedestrianism as a simple way to stay active every day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cycling - the activity of riding a bicycle
  • synonym: walking - the act of moving on foot at a regular pace
