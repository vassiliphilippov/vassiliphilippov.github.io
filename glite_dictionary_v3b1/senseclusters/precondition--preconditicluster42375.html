precondition

Examples

Synonyms

  • prerequisite
    6.6 0.38/m
    prerequisite is a common single-word term for something required before a course or task, while precondition is more formal and technical.
  • essential condition
    3.5 0.15/m
    essential condition is a phrase that stresses the need for something, while precondition names a prior requirement before an action.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consequence - something that follows as a result
  • synonym: prerequisite - a thing that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen
  • synonym: requirement - something that is needed or demanded
