labeling

noun
product information
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the words, symbols, or information printed on a label or package to describe or give instructions about a product

Examples

Labeling on medicine
The labeling on the medication clearly explained the dosage and possible side effects.
Food labeling regulations require companies to list all ingredients and nutritional information.
She checked the labeling on the cleaning product to make sure it was safe for children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: label - a piece of paper or material attached to something that gives information about it
  • synonym: packaging - materials used to wrap or protect goods, often including information
