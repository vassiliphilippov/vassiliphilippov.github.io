unmappable

adjective
cannot be mapped
8.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæpəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmæpəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to represent or locate accurately on a map or diagram

Examples

Unmappable jungle area
The dense jungle was so overgrown it became unmappable for explorers.
Due to frequent flooding, the river delta remained largely unmappable for decades.
Some digital data is unmappable because it does not fit any known format.

Related Terms

  • antonym: map - to represent an area or object on a map
  • synonym: untraceable - impossible to find or track
