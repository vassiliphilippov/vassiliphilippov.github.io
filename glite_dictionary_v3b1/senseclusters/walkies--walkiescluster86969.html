walkies

interjection
pet walk exclamation
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkiːz / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkiːz / (BrE)
an exclamation used to tell or encourage a pet, especially a dog, that it is time to go for a walk

Examples

Walkies time!
When she hears walkies, the dog immediately grabs her leash and waits by the door.
He shouted walkies and the puppy started wagging its tail excitedly.
Just say walkies and you'll see how quickly the dog gets ready to go outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: walk - to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
