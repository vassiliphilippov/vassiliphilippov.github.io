loud pedal

noun
right piano pedal for sustaining sound
Pronunciation: / laʊd ˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / laʊd ˈpɛdəl / (BrE)
the right pedal on a piano that lifts the dampers from the strings, allowing them to continue vibrating and thus sustaining the sound
