dies Veneris

noun
Latin for Friday
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdiː.eɪs ˈwɛn.ɛr.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈdiː.eɪs ˈvɛn.ɛr.ɪs / (BrE)
the Latin term for Friday, literally meaning 'day of Venus'

Examples

dies Veneris day
In ancient Roman calendars, dies Veneris was the name given to Friday.
The phrase dies Veneris appears in many medieval manuscripts to indicate the day of the week.
Some languages still reflect the influence of dies Veneris in their word for Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Friday - the sixth day of the week in many calendars
