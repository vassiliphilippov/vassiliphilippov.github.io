skinsuit

noun
athletic tight garment
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪnˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈskɪnˌsuːt / (BrE)
a tight-fitting garment worn by athletes to reduce air resistance and improve performance

Examples

Racing skinsuit
The speed skater wore a special skinsuit to minimize drag during the race.
Cyclists often choose a skinsuit for time trials to gain a competitive edge.
Her new skinsuit fit perfectly and helped her swim faster in the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodysuit - a one-piece, close-fitting stretch garment covering the torso and sometimes limbs
  • antonym: tracksuit - a loose-fitting suit worn by athletes for training or warm-up
