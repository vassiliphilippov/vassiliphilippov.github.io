foamlike

adjective
resembling foam
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfəʊmlaɪk / (BrE)
having a structure, texture, or appearance similar to foam, often with many small holes or a spongy quality

Examples

Foamlike material
The new insulation material is foamlike and helps keep the building warm.
Scientists developed a foamlike metal that is both lightweight and strong.
The cake had a foamlike texture, making it very light and airy.
The chef created a foamlike sauce that added a light texture to the dish.
Her dress was made of a foamlike material that felt soft and airy.
Clouds drifted across the sky in foamlike shapes after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frothy - full of or covered with small bubbles; foamy
  • antonym: solid - firm and without holes or spaces inside
  • synonym: spongy - soft and full of small holes like a sponge
