ballpen

noun
pen with a ball-shaped tip
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlˌpɛn / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlpɛn / (BrE)
A pen that dispenses ink over a metal ball at its point, allowing for smooth writing.

Examples

A ballpen for writing
I always carry a ballpen in my bag for taking notes during meetings.
She wrote her name neatly with a blue ballpen on the registration form.
The ballpen ran out of ink just as I was about to finish the letter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballpoint pen - a pen with a small rotating ball that dispenses ink
