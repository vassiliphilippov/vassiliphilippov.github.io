knock off

verb
stop doing something; cease an activity
4.1 7.4
Pronunciation: / nɑk ɑf / (AmE), / nɒk ɒf / (BrE)
(informal) to stop doing something or to cease an activity, especially one that is annoying or inappropriate.

Examples

Knock off the noise
Hey, knock off with the complaining and do your work.
If you don't knock off that noise, I'll call security.
The teacher told the students to knock off the chatter and focus on their assignments.

Related Terms
