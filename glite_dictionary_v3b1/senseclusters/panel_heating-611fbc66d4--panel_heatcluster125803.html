panel heating

noun
a heating system using panels with heating elements
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəl ˈhitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpænəl ˈhiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a heating system in which electric conductors or heating pipes are installed within wall, floor, baseboard, or ceiling panels to provide warmth

Examples

Panel heating system
During winter, the panel heating system keeps the entire floor comfortably warm.
Modern homes are increasingly adopting panel heating for its energy efficiency and even heat distribution.
The contractors installed panel heating in the new office building to eliminate the need for radiators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiant heating - heating system using radiant energy, often through floors or panels
