thereabouts

adverb
near that place or area
Pronunciation: / ˌðɛr.əˈbaʊts / (AmE), / ˌðeər.əˈbaʊts / (BrE)
In or near that place or area.

Examples

London or thereabouts
He stayed in London or thereabouts for several weeks.
I'm not sure of her exact age, but she's thirty or thereabouts.
You'll find the museum on Baker Street or thereabouts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nearby - close in distance; not far away
