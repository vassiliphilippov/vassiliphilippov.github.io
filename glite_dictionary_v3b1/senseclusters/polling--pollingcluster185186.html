polling

noun
collecting votes or opinions
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of collecting votes or opinions from people, especially during an election or survey

Examples

Polling station opens
Polling stations opened early to accommodate all voters.
Recent polling shows a shift in public opinion about the new policy.
The organization is conducting polling to understand community needs before making decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surveying - the process of asking people questions to collect information
  • synonym: voting - the act of casting a ballot in an election
