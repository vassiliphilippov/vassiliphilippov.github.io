Xenicus

noun
genus of New Zealand wrens
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛnɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈzɛnɪkəs / (BrE)
A genus of small insectivorous birds native to New Zealand, constituting the type genus of the family Xenicidae.
