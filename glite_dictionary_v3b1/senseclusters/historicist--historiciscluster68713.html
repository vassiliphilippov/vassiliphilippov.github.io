historicist

adjective
historical context-based
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪsɪst / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪsɪst / (BrE)
relating to the theory that cultural, intellectual, or social phenomena should be understood in their historical context

Examples

Historicist theory approach
The historicist approach to literature examines how texts reflect the time in which they were written.
Many architects adopted a historicist style, drawing inspiration from earlier periods.
Her historicist analysis of the law emphasized the importance of social and economic background.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ahistorical - not concerned with or related to history
  • synonym: historical - connected with history or past events
