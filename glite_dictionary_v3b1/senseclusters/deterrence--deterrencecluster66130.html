Synonyms

  • determent
    8.5 0.00/m
    determent is a warning action that tries to stop someone directly, while deterrence is the broader process of creating fear to prevent actions.
  • disincentive
    7.2 1.61/m
    disincentive is a factor like a cost or rule that reduces people's desire to act, while deterrence uses fear of consequences to stop behaviour.
