faggotry

noun
making or selling faggots
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡətri / (AmE), / ˈfæɡətri / (BrE)
the making, collecting, or selling of faggots (bundles of sticks used as fuel), especially as an occupation in the past

Examples

Old faggotry trade
In the 18th century, faggotry was a common trade in rural English villages.
The decline of faggotry came with the rise of coal as a primary fuel source.
Historical records mention families who relied on faggotry for their livelihood during harsh winters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charcoal burning - the process of making charcoal from wood
  • synonym: woodcutting - the activity of cutting wood for fuel or other uses
