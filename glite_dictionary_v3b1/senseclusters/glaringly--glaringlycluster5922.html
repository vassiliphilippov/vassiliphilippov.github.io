glaringly

adverb
extremely obviously or conspicuously
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡlɛːrɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely obvious or conspicuous, often unpleasantly so

Examples

A glaringly obvious mistake
Her report was glaringly inconsistent with the facts.
It became glaringly clear that he had never read the instructions.
The mistake was glaringly apparent to everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood
