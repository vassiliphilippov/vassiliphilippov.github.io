tulasi

noun
Indian female name
9.9
Pronunciation: / tuːˈlɑːsi / (AmE), / tuːˈlɑːsi / (BrE)
a female given name in India, derived from the sacred basil plant revered in Hindu culture

Examples

Tulasi Sharma
Her parents chose the name tulasi to honor their cultural heritage.
In many Indian families, tulasi is a popular name for girls.
The meaning of tulasi is closely connected to the sacred basil plant.
