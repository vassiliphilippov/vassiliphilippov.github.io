loggerhead

noun
large sea turtle with a broad head
8.2 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…ëÀê…°…ôrÀåh…õd / (AmE), / Ààl…í…°…ôh…õd / (BrE)
A very large marine turtle with a broad head, found in warm seas, known for its powerful jaws and carnivorous diet.

Examples

Loggerhead turtle
Many loggerhead turtles come ashore each year to lay their eggs on sandy beaches.
Scientists tagged a young loggerhead to study its migration patterns across the Atlantic.
The loggerhead is easily recognized by its massive head and reddish-brown shell.
