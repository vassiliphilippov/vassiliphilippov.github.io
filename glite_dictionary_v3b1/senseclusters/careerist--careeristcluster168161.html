careerist

noun
person focused on advancing their career
6.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪrɪst / (AmE), / kəˈrɪərɪst / (BrE)
A person who is primarily concerned with furthering their own career, often without regard for ethics or principles.

Examples

A careerist attitude
She disliked the careerist attitude that prioritized personal advancement over teamwork.
Some people view him as a careerist who will do anything to get ahead.
The company suffered when its top leadership consisted mainly of careerists rather than true visionaries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climber - an ambitious person who tries to advance socially or professionally
  • antonym: idealist - a person who is guided more by ideals than by practical considerations
