negativistic

adjective
pessimistic attitude
8.4
Pronunciation: /neɡ.ə.tɪ.ˈvɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /neɡ.ə.tɪ.ˈvɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
characterized by a tendency to focus on problems, faults, or negative outcomes rather than positive aspects
