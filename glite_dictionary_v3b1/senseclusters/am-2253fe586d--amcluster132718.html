AM

noun
time from midnight to noon
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
The period of time between midnight and noon, used in stating the time of day.

Examples

6:00 AM
He usually finishes his morning run by 6:00 AM.
Please arrive before 10:00 AM for registration.
The train leaves at 7:30 AM every weekday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: PM - the period between noon and midnight
