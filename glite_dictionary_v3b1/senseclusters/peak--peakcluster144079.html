peak

noun
front brim of a cap or hat
4.8
Pronunciation: / pik / (AmE), / piːk / (BrE)
the stiff, projecting brim at the front of a cap or hat that shades the eyes

Examples

Peak of a cap
He adjusted the peak of his cap to block out the sun.
Rain dripped off the peak as he stood waiting at the bus stop.
She wore her baseball cap with the peak facing backward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visor - the part of a cap that sticks out in front to protect the eyes from sun
