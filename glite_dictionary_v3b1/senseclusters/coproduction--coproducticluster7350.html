coproduction

noun
joint production
3.7
Pronunciation: /koʊ.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ /; / /kə.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəʊ.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ /; / /kə.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

A film coproduction

International coproduction allows studios to share costs and creative ideas.
The two artists' coproduction resulted in a unique and popular album.
