coproduction

noun
joint production
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act of producing something together by two or more people, companies, or organizations

Examples

A film coproduction
The movie was a coproduction between a French and a Canadian company.
International coproduction allows studios to share costs and creative ideas.
The two artists' coproduction resulted in a unique and popular album.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others to achieve something
  • synonym: partnership - an arrangement where parties agree to cooperate to advance their interests
  • antonym: solo production - the act of producing something by a single person or entity
