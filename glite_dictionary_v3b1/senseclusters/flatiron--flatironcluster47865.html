flatiron

noun
old-fashioned iron heated on a stove
8.1 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈflæt.aɪ.ərn/ /; / /ˈflæt.aɪrn/ (AmE), /ˈflæt.aɪ.ən/ (BrE)
