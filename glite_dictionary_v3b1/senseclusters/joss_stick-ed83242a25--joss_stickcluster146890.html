joss stick

noun
stick of incense, used in rituals
5.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɑs stɪk / (AmE), / dʒɒs stɪk / (BrE)
A slender stick of incense that is burned, especially during religious ceremonies, particularly in Chinese traditions.

Examples

Light a joss stick
She lit a joss stick and placed it before the shrine.
The temple was filled with the fragrant smoke of burning joss sticks.
Vendors sell joss sticks outside during festival days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incense - a substance that is burned for its fragrant smoke
