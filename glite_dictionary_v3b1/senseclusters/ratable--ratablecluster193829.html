ratable

adjective
liable to local property taxes
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪtəbəl /; / ˈreɪtəbl / (BrE)
(of property) liable to payment of locally assessed property taxes.

Examples

A ratable property
Some properties are not ratable if they belong to charitable organizations.

The government updated the list of ratable properties for the coming tax year.
Only rateable properties are included in the local tax assessments.
Owners must ensure their rateable value is correctly registered with the authorities.
The council determined that the new building is rateable under current law.
