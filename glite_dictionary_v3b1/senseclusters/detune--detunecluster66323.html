detune

verb
to adjust off frequency
Pronunciation: / diˈtuːn / (AmE), / diˈtjuːn / (BrE)
to adjust a device, such as a radio or antenna, so it is not tuned to a specific frequency.

Examples

To detune a radio
The engineer needed to detune the transmitter to prevent signal overlap.
If you detune the guitar string, it will sound lower than intended.
To avoid interference, the technician decided to detune the receiver slightly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retune - to adjust again to a different frequency
  • antonym: tune - to adjust a device to a particular frequency or setting
