cat

noun
spiteful or gossipy woman (informal)
Pronunciation: / kæt / (AmE), / kæt / (BrE)
A spiteful or malicious woman, especially one who gossips.

Examples

Gossiping cat
Don't be such a cat; there's no need to spread those rumors.
Everyone avoids her at parties because she's such a cat behind people's backs.
She was known as the office cat for her biting comments and gossip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catty - spiteful or malicious (typically of remarks or behavior)
