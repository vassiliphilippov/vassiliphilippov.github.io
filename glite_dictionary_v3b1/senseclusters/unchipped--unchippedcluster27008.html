unchipped

adjective
without a microchip
7.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪpt / (BrE)
not fitted with or containing a microchip or electronic identification chip, especially referring to pets or devices

Examples

Unchipped pets
The animal shelter will not release any unchipped cats for adoption.
Many older phones remain unchipped and cannot be tracked electronically.
It is safer to avoid buying unchipped devices from unofficial sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: microchipped - fitted with a microchip for identification
