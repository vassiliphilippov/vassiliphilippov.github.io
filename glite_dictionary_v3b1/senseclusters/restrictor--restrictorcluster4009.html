restrictor

noun
flow-limiting device
7.0
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈstrɪk.tər/ /; / /rə.ˈstrɪk.tər/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈstrɪk.tə/ /; / /rə.ˈstrɪk.tə/ (BrE)
a device or part that limits the movement, flow, or amount of something

Examples

Fuel restrictor
The mechanic installed a restrictor to control the fuel flow in the engine.
A water restrictor helps save water by limiting how much comes out of the tap.
Some sports cars use a restrictor to reduce engine power for safety reasons.
