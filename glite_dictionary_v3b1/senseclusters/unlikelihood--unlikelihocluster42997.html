unlikelihood

noun
improbability; unlikely nature
8.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈlaɪ.klɪ.hʊd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈlaɪ.kliː.həd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈlaɪ.klɪ.hʊd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈlaɪ.kliː.həd/ (BrE)

Examples

The unlikelihood of success
