grillework

noun
decorative metal lattice
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪlˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a decorative or functional structure of bars, lattice, or openwork panels (often metal, stone, or wood) used in architecture for security, ventilation, or ornamentation
