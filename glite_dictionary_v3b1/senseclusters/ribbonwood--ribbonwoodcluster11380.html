ribbonwood

noun
deciduous New Zealand tree with strong fiber bark
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.ən.wʊd / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.ən.wʊd / (BrE)
a tree or shrub native to New Zealand, known for its strong fiber and clusters of fragrant white flowers
