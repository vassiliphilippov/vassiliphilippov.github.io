sphincteric

adjective
of a sphincter muscle
9.1
Pronunciation: / sfɪŋkˈtɛrɪk / (AmE), / sfɪŋkˈtɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affecting a sphincter muscle, which controls the opening and closing of a bodily passage

Examples

Sphincteric muscle control
The patient experienced sphincteric weakness after the surgery.
Proper sphincteric function is essential for bladder control.
Doctors monitor sphincteric activity during certain neurological exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: involuntary - not under conscious control
  • synonym: muscular - relating to muscles
