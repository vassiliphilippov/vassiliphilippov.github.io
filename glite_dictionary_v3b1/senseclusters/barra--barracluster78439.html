barra

noun
Australian fish slang
Australian slang for a barramundi, a large freshwater fish found in northern Australia.

Examples

A big barra fish
He was thrilled when he caught a massive barra during his trip to the Northern Territory.
Locals say the best time to fish for barra is just after the wet season.
The restaurant is famous for its grilled barra served with lemon and herbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barramundi - a large freshwater fish native to Australia and Southeast Asia
