Frey

proper noun
Norse god of fertility and peace
Pronunciation: / freɪ / (AmE), / freɪ / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) a god associated with fertility, peace, and prosperity; son of Njorth and brother of Freya, and originally one of the Vanir.

Examples

Frey god of fertility
Frey was often depicted with his golden boar and was beloved by the common folk.
In mythology, Frey is revered as the bringer of peace and good harvests.
Many ancient Scandinavian festivals were dedicated to honoring Frey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Freya - Norse goddess, sister of Frey, associated with love and fertility
  • synonym: Njorth - Norse god of the sea, father of Frey
