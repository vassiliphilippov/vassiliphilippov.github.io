scripturally

adverb
in a scriptural way
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪptʃɚəli / (AmE), / ˈskrɪptʃərəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or based on sacred writings or scripture

Examples

Interpreted scripturally
The rules were interpreted scripturally to ensure they matched the teachings of the holy texts.
He argued scripturally, citing several passages to support his point of view.
The holiday is observed scripturally by following ancient religious customs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biblically - in a manner relating to the Bible or biblical teachings
  • antonym: secularly - in a manner not connected with religion or sacred writings
