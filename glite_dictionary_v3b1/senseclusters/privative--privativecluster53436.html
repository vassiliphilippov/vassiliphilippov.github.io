privative

adjective
indicating absence
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvətɪv / (AmE), / ˈprɪvətɪv / (BrE)
indicating the absence or lack of a quality or attribute

Examples

Privative prefix
The prefix 'a-' in 'amoral' is a privative element that shows the lack of morality.
In linguistics, a privative affix removes or negates the meaning of the root word.
The word 'colorless' contains a privative suffix that indicates the absence of color.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affirmative - expressing agreement or presence
  • synonym: negative - expressing denial or absence
