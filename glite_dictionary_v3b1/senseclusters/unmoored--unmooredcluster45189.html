unmoored

adjective
not anchored
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɔrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɔːd / (BrE)
not fixed, anchored, or connected; free to move or lacking stability or support

Examples

Feel unmoored in life
After the move, she felt unmoored and uncertain about her future.
The boat was unmoored and began to drift away from the dock.
He became unmoored from his routine after losing his job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adrift - floating without being anchored or guided
  • antonym: anchored - firmly fixed or secured in place
