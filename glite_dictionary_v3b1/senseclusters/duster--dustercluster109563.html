duster

noun
windstorm lifting dust or sand
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstər / (AmE), / ˈdʌstə / (BrE)
a windstorm, especially in dry regions, that lifts up and carries clouds of dust or sand.

Examples

A strong duster wind
Farmers rushed to protect their crops when a duster swept across the plains.
Last night's duster left a thick layer of sand covering the entire town.
The sudden duster reduced visibility to almost nothing on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dust storm - a strong, violent wind that carries clouds of dust, sand, and dirt over an extensive area
