able to be kept out or denied access, especially referring to goods or services that can be restricted to certain people

Examples

Excludable resource
A private swimming pool is considered an excludable resource because access can be restricted.
Public parks are not excludable since anyone can enter and use them.
Cable television is an excludable service because only paying customers can watch it.
