baptizer

proper noun
title for John the Baptist
Pronunciation: / ˈbæptaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈbæptaɪzə / (BrE)
a title for John the Baptist, who baptized Jesus and others in Christian tradition

Examples

In many Orthodox churches, John is referred to as the baptizer in religious texts.
