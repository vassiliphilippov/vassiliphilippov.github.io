last

adjective
immediately before; most recent
1.1
Pronunciation: / læst / (AmE), / lɑːst / (BrE)
happening or coming immediately before the present; most recent in a sequence or list.

Examples

Last week's news
I remember what we discussed in last week's meeting.
The last time I saw her was at the conference.
We finished reading the last chapter before the exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: next - immediately following in time or order
  • synonym: previous - coming before in order or time; preceding
