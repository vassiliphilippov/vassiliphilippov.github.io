heel

noun
back part of the human foot
Pronunciation: / hil / (AmE), / hiːl / (BrE)
the back part of the human foot, below the ankle and behind the arch

Examples

Sore heel
He stood on tiptoe, raising his heel off the ground.
She felt a sharp pain in her heel after running the marathon.
The shoes rubbed against his heel, causing a blister.
