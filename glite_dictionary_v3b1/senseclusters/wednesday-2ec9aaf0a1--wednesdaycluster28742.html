Wednesday

proper noun
day between Tuesday and Thursday
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛnzdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɛnzdeɪ / (BrE)
the day of the week between Tuesday and Thursday

Examples

The new movie will be released next Wednesday in theaters nationwide.
Our class always meets on Wednesday afternoons for group discussions.
She has piano lessons every Wednesday after school.
The library stays open late on Wednesday evenings for students.
