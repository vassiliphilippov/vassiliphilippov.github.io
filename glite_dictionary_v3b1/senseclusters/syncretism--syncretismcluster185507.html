syncretism

noun

Examples

Grammatical syncretism
In some languages, syncretism occurs when different case endings merge into a single form.
The phenomenon of syncretism simplifies the grammatical structure by reducing the number of inflections used.
The study focused on the syncretism of subject and object pronouns in colloquial speech.
