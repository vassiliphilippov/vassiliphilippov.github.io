top

adjective
at the highest position
1.0 174/m
Pronunciation: / tÉ‘p / (AmE), / tÉ’p / (BrE)
situated at the highest point or position; highest in rank or place.

Examples

Top row seats
He always sits in the top row of the stadium for the best view.
Look for your name at the top of the list.
The cookies are stored on the top shelf, out of reach of children.

Synonyms

  • uppermost
    4.7 0.90/m
    uppermost means at or toward the very top of something, while top is the common word for the highest position.
