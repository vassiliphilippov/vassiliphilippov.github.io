unfitly

adverb
inappropriately
8.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtli / (BrE)
in an unsuitable or inappropriate manner

Examples

Behave unfitly at meeting
He behaved unfitly at the important business meeting, drawing criticism from his colleagues.
The document was unfitly formatted, making it hard to read and understand.
She spoke unfitly for such a formal occasion, surprising everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appropriately - in a suitable or proper manner
  • synonym: inappropriately - in a way that is not suitable or proper
  • synonym: unsuitably - in a manner that is not suitable
