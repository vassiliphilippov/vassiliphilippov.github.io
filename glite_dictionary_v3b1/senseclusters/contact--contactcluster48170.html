contact

noun
a connecting point in electronics
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌtækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt / (BrE)
a junction where two electrical conductors meet or touch, allowing current to pass

Examples

Battery contact point
If the battery contacts are corroded, your device might not work properly.
Make sure all the contacts are securely fastened before turning on the power.
The technician cleaned the contacts on the circuit board for better conductivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connector - a device for joining electrical circuits together
