hedge trimmer

noun
garden tool for trimming hedges
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdʒ ˌtrɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʒ ˌtrɪmə / (BrE)
a tool with blades, powered manually or by electricity, used to trim or shape hedges and shrubs in gardens.

Examples

Electric hedge trimmer
Before winter, she cleaned and stored the hedge trimmer in the shed.
Electric hedge trimmers are faster and easier to use than manual shears.
He used the hedge trimmer to shape the overgrown bushes in his backyard.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lawnmower - machine for cutting grass in lawns
  • synonym: pruning shears - hand tool for cutting small branches
