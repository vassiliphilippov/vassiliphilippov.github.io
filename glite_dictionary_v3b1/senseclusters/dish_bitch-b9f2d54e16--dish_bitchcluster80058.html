dish bitch

noun
self-mocking term for person who washes dishes
Pronunciation: / dɪʃ bɪtʃ / (AmE), / dɪʃ bɪtʃ / (BrE)
a humorous, self-deprecating slang term for a person, usually oneself, who is responsible for washing dishes, especially in a group or workplace

Examples

The dish bitch role
I’ve been the dish bitch at every family gathering since I was twelve.
Nobody wanted to be the dish bitch after the party ended.
She laughed about being the unofficial dish bitch at her house every night.
