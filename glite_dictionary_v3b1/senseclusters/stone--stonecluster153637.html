stone

verb
kill by throwing stones
6.5
Pronunciation: / stoʊn / (AmE), / stəʊn / (BrE)
to kill (someone) by throwing stones at them as a form of punishment

Examples

To stone a criminal
In some ancient societies, it was common to stone those found guilty of certain crimes.
She pleaded for mercy as the angry crowd prepared to stone her.
The villagers gathered in the square to stone the accused thief.
