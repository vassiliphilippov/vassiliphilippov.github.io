chairperson

noun
person presiding over a meeting
3.5 2.81/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt É…õrÀåp…ùÀês…ôn / (AmE), / Ààt Ée…ôÀåp…úÀês…ôn / (BrE)
the person who presides over a meeting, committee, or organization

Examples

The chairperson leads
All questions should be directed to the chairperson during the discussion.
As the chairperson, she opened the annual meeting with a short welcome speech.
The chairperson called for a vote on the proposal.

Synonyms

  • chair
    3.1 19/m
    a chair is the person who leads meetings in everyday speech, while a chairperson is the formal title used in official contexts.
  • presiding officer
    6.1 0.26/m
    a presiding officer enforces rules and order in formal assemblies, while a chairperson more broadly runs meetings or committees.
  • presider
    7.3 0.10/m
    a presider often leads ceremonies or religious events, while a chairperson runs meetings or committees in organizations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairman - male or gender-specific presiding officer
  • synonym: chairwoman - female presiding officer
  • antonym: secretary - officer responsible for administrative work in meetings
