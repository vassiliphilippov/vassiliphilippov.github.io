Mideast

noun
region around eastern Mediterranean
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːˌdɪst / (AmE), / ˈmiːˌdɪːst / (BrE)
a region around the eastern Mediterranean, typically including countries from Turkey and Egypt to Iran; the site of ancient civilizations and continuous modern significance
