Eriosoma

noun
genus of woolly aphids
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪriˈoʊsəmə / (AmE), / ˌɪəriˈəʊsəmə / (BrE)
A genus of woolly aphids, insects that are plant pests and produce a white waxy covering.

Examples

Eriosoma woolly aphids
Eriosoma can be found on apple trees, where they use their piercing mouthparts to feed on sap.
Orchard owners are often on the lookout for infestations of Eriosoma during the growing season.
The damage caused by Eriosoma can result in distorted leaves and stunted plant growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphid - a small sap-sucking insect
