laydown

noun
act of lying down
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈleɪˌdaʊn / (BrE)
an act or instance of placing something down or lying in a position of rest

Examples

A quick laydown
After a long run, the athlete enjoyed a quick laydown on the grass.
The yoga instructor recommended a laydown at the end of the session to relax the body.
He took a brief laydown before starting his homework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repose - a state of rest or lying down
  • synonym: rest - a period or state of relaxing or ceasing activity
