crush

noun
brief romantic attraction, mostly by youths
Pronunciation: / krʌʃ / (AmE), / krʌʃ / (BrE)
a brief feeling of romantic attraction, especially by a young person towards someone they find appealing

Examples

A secret crush
He confessed his childhood crush during the reunion dinner.
Many teenagers experience their first crush while still in school.
Sarah had a crush on her older brother's best friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infatuation - an intense but short-lived passion or admiration for someone
  • degree higher: love - a strong feeling of affection
