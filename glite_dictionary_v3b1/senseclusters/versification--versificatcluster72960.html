versification

noun
metrical composition of poetry
4.3 7.2
Pronunciation: /vɜːr.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /vɜː.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the art or practice of composing poetry, especially with attention to meter and poetic technique

Examples

Classical versification style

  • antonym: prose - written or spoken language in its ordinary form, without metrical structure
