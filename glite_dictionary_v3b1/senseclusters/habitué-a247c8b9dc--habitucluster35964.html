habitué

noun
regular visitor or patron
5.2 8.2
Pronunciation: / hæˌbɪtʃuˈeɪ / (AmE), / hæbɪtjuˈeɪ / (BrE)
A person who regularly visits or frequents a particular place.

Examples

A habitué of the cafe
As an old habitué of the library, he could be found in the reading room almost daily.
She was a well-known habitué of the city's jazz clubs.
The café had its share of habitués who gathered every morning for coffee and conversation.
