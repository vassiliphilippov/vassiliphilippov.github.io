sweet cherry

noun
tree or fruit producing edible sweet cherries
Pronunciation: / swit ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / swiːt ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a tree (Prunus avium) that produces sweet, edible cherries, or the fruit itself

Examples

Sweet cherry tree
Sweet cherry blossoms cover the hillside every spring.
She picked a handful of ripe sweet cherry fruits from the branch.
The farmer planted several sweet cherry trees in his orchard.
Sweet cherries are perfect for eating raw or adding to fruit salads.
She bought a basket of fresh sweet cherries at the farmers' market.
Unlike sour cherries, sweet cherries are usually eaten fresh rather than used for baking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherry - the general fruit and tree of the genus Prunus
  • antonym: sour cherry - a different cherry species with tart fruit
