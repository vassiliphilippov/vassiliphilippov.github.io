stratocracy

noun
government by the military
8.3
Pronunciation: / strəˈtɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / strəˈtɒkrəsi / (BrE)
A system of government in which the military has complete control and authority over the state.

Examples

A stratocracy government
A stratocracy abolishes the separation between the army and the state.
Historians often debate whether ancient Sparta was a true stratocracy or simply a militaristic oligarchy.
Some critics argue that prolonged military rule leads toward a de facto stratocracy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democracy - a system of government by the whole population, usually through elected representatives
  • synonym: military junta - a government led by a committee of military leaders
