organ pipe

noun
tube producing sound in a pipe organ
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɡən paɪp / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡən paɪp / (BrE)
a tube that produces musical sound as part of a pipe organ when air is driven through it

Examples

The largest organ pipe
During the concert, the sound from the largest organ pipe resonated throughout the hall.
Each organ pipe has a unique length to create different musical notes.
The cathedral's organ pipes gleamed in the sunlight above the choir.
