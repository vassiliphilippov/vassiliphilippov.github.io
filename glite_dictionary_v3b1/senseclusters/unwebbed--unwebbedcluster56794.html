unwebbed

adjective
not having webbed feet or toes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛbd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛbd / (BrE)
(of the feet or toes of some animals) not having membranes of skin connecting the digits.

Examples

Unwebbed animal toes
Scientists observed that the primitive frog’s unwebbed toes limited its movement in water.
Some duck species have unwebbed toes, which affects their swimming ability.
The lizard had unwebbed feet, unlike the frogs in the same pond.

Related Terms

  • antonym: webbed - having the toes or fingers connected by a web of skin
