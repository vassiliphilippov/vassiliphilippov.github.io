submerge

verb
to cover or hide completely
1.8
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɝrdʒ / (AmE), / səbˈmɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to cover or hide completely, especially by being under a surface or by making something imperceptible

Examples

To submerge feelings
Her doubts were quickly submerged by a wave of excitement.
The announcement was submerged in the noise of the cheering crowd.
The entire village was submerged by floodwaters after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immerse - to plunge or dip completely under a liquid
