constellation

noun
arrangement of related elements or factors
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A group or arrangement of related things or people considered as a unit or system, especially when thought of as influencing an outcome.

Examples

Constellation of factors
A unique constellation of factors led to the company's rapid success.
In his speech, he addressed the constellation of problems facing urban communities.
The festival resulted from a fortunate constellation of creative minds working together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combination - a joining or grouping of different elements
  • synonym: configuration - the arrangement of parts or elements; shape or form
