fireguard

noun
metal screen before a fire for protection
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌɡɑːd / (BrE)
A metal screen placed in front of an open fire to protect the room from sparks or embers.

Examples

Metal fireguard
Please make sure the fireguard is in place before lighting the fire.
She bought a sturdy fireguard to protect the children from the fireplace.
The antique fireguard added both safety and elegance to the room.
