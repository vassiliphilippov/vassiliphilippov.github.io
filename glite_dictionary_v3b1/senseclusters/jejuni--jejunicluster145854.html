jejuni

noun
bacterium causing illness
9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒə.ˈdʒuː.niː/ /; / /dʒɪ.ˈdʒuː.niː/ (AmE), /dʒə.ˈdʒuː.niː/ /; / /dʒɪ.ˈdʒuː.niː/ (BrE)
an informal name for Campylobacter jejuni, a bacterium that commonly causes foodborne illness in humans

Examples

Jejuni infection
The laboratory confirmed that jejuni was responsible for the recent food poisoning cases.
Doctors often test for jejuni when patients present with severe stomach cramps and diarrhea.
After eating undercooked chicken, several people became ill with a jejuni infection.
