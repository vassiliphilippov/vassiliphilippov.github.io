noumenal

adjective
beyond perception
9.9
Pronunciation: /nuː.ˈmiː.nəl/ /; / /njuː.ˈmiː.nəl/ (AmE), /njuː.ˈmiː.nəl/ /; / /nəʊ.ˈmiː.nəl/ (BrE)
relating to things as they are in themselves, independent of human perception or experience

Examples

The noumenal world
Philosophers debate whether the noumenal world can ever be truly known.
Kant argued that our knowledge is limited to the phenomenal, not the noumenal realm.
The concept of noumenal reality challenges our understanding of existence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: phenomenal - relating to things as they appear to human senses
