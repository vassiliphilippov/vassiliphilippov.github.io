exhaust

noun
system for discharging engine gases
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔst / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst / (BrE)
the system of pipes and other parts in a car or engine through which waste gases or steam are released

Examples

Car exhaust system
A faulty exhaust can make a car much noisier than usual.
After years of use, the car's exhaust began to rust and needed replacement.
The mechanic checked the exhaust for any signs of damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhaust pipe - a pipe in an engine through which exhaust gases are expelled
