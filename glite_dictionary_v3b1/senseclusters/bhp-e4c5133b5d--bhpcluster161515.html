BHP

sym
boiler horsepower unit
Pronunciation: / biː eɪtʃ piː / (AmE), / biː eɪtʃ piː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for boiler horsepower, a historical unit used to measure the power output of steam boilers

Examples

BHP of a steam boiler
The old factory's steam generator was rated at 100 BHP.
Engineers used BHP to compare the power of different steam boilers.
The technical manual listed the maximum BHP for safe operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horsepower - a unit of power used to measure engines
  • synonym: kilowatt - a metric unit of power equal to 1,000 watts
