ribboning

noun
decorating with ribbons
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of forming, decorating, or adorning with ribbons

Examples

Cake ribboning
The baker's careful ribboning gave the cake an elegant appearance for the celebration.
She spent hours on the ribboning of the gift boxes to make them look special.
The festive ribboning along the parade route delighted the children.
