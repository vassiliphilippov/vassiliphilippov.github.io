stomach flu

noun
illness causing vomiting and diarrhea
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək fluː / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək fluː / (BrE)
an illness causing inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting in vomiting and diarrhea; gastroenteritis
