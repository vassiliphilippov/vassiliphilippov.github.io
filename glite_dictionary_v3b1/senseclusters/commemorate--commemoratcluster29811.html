Synonyms

  • memorialize
    7.2 1.08/m
    memorialize creates a lasting tribute like a plaque or monument, while commemorate usually means holding a ceremony or special event.
  • mark
    3.3 14/m
    mark observes an important date or event with a simple ceremony or activity, while commemorate often implies formal remembrance.
  • honor
    2.3 31/m
    honor shows respect or praise for someone publicly, while commemorate stresses remembering them through ceremonies or memorial acts.
  • pay homage to
    5.5 1.51/m
    pay homage to expresses deep public respect often in a formal act, while commemorate centers on remembering by ceremonies or memorials.
  • remember
    1.0 19/m
    remember simply means keep someone in mind or memory, while commemorate involves public ceremonies or special acts to honor them.

Related Terms
