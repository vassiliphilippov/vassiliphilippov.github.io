bog hemp

noun
non-stinging Boehmeria plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑɡ hɛmp / (AmE), / bɒɡ hɛmp / (BrE)
Any of several flowering weeds of the genus Boehmeria, characterized by the absence of stinging hairs.

Examples

Tall bog hemp plants
Local wildlife often shelters among the tall stands of bog hemp near the river.
Researchers studied the bog hemp for its unique properties compared to other nettles.
The wetland was full of bog hemp thriving alongside the reeds and sedges.
