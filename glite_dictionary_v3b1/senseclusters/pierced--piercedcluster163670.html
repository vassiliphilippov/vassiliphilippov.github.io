pierced

adjective
having a hole made through something
4.4 2.8
Pronunciation: /pɪrst/ /; / /pɪərst/ (AmE), /pɪəst/ (BrE)
(of a material or body part) having a hole made through it, especially for attaching an ornament or for medical reasons.

Examples

She wears delicate earrings in her pierced ears every day.
The bakery sold pierced doughnuts covered in sugar.
