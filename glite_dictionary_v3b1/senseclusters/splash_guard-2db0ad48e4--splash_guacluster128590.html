splash guard

noun
cover over wheel stopping splashes
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃ ˌɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃ ˌɡɑːd / (BrE)
a curved protective covering positioned above the wheel of a bicycle or motorcycle to stop water or mud from being thrown onto the rider

Examples

A splash guard on bicycle
She installed a new splash guard on her bicycle to keep her clothes clean on rainy days.
The motorcycle's splash guard was dented after riding through rough terrain.
Without a splash guard, mud and water sprayed onto his legs every time he rode through a puddle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fender - a guard over a vehicle wheel to keep off mud
  • synonym: mudguard - a cover that protects against mud thrown by wheels, especially on bicycles and motorcycles
