pestiferous

adjective
morally corrupting
5.7
Pronunciation: / pɛˈstɪfərəs / (AmE), / pɛˈstɪfərəs / (BrE)
Causing moral corruption or perversion; morally harmful or corrupting.

Examples

Pestiferous influence
Certain pestiferous influences in the city led many astray from virtuous living.
Some viewed the new novel as so pestiferous that it threatened the morals of young readers.
The teacher warned about the pestiferous effects of unchecked gossip and slander.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrupting - causing someone to become morally depraved
  • antonym: edifying - providing moral or intellectual improvement
