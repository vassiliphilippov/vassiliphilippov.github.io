busing

verb
to transport by bus
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌsɪŋ / (BrE)
to transport people, especially groups such as students, by bus from one place to another

Examples

To bus students
The school district is busing students from the suburbs to the city schools each morning.
During the event, organizers were busing attendees from the parking lot to the stadium.
The company started busing employees to the new office to make commuting easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drive - to operate a vehicle to transport people
  • synonym: shuttle - to transport people regularly between two places
