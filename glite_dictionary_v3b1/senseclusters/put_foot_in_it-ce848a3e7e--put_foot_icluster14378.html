put foot in it

verb
say or do something embarrassing
6.9
Pronunciation: / pʊt fʊt ɪn ɪt / (AmE), / pʊt fʊt ɪn ɪt / (BrE)
to say or do something embarrassing or tactless, usually accidentally

Examples

To put foot in it accidentally

She didn't mean to put foot in it, but her comment made everyone uncomfortable.
Whenever he tries to be funny, he tends to put foot in it instead.
