whipping cream

noun
cream that can be whipped
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpɪŋ ˌkriːm / (AmE), / ˈwɪpɪŋ ˌkriːm / (BrE)
cream with a high fat content (30% to 36%) that can be whipped to form a light, thick foam

Examples

Whipping cream for dessert
She added whipping cream to the soup to make it richer and smoother.
The recipe calls for one cup of whipping cream, chilled before use.
You need whipping cream if you want to make homemade whipped cream.
