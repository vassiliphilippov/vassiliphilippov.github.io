unculturable

adjective
not suitable for farming
8.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkʌl.tʃər.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkʌl.tʃə.rə.bəl/ (BrE)
not suitable for cultivation, farming, or growing crops due to poor conditions or other limitations

Examples

Unculturable land
The soil in this region is so rocky that it remains unculturable despite modern techniques.
Large areas of desert are considered unculturable and are left untouched by farmers.
After years of drought, the land became almost completely unculturable for agriculture.
