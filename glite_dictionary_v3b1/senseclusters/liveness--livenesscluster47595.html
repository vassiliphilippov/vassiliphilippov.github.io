liveness

noun
proof a biometric sample is from a live person
8.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / c8la6avn59s / (AmE), / c8la6avn6as / (BrE)
(in biometric security) the property or evidence that a presented biometric sample (for example, a face image or fingerprint) comes from a live person rather than from a photo, mask, replay, or other spoof.

Examples

Biometric liveness check
The smartphone's security app checks liveness to block logins using photos or videos.
For high-security access, systems require proof of liveness before granting biometric authentication.
Researchers improved algorithmic liveness measures to detect life-like masks in face recognition tests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liveness detection - automated methods and tests used to determine whether a biometric sample is from a live person
  • antonym: spoofing - attempts to bypass biometric systems by presenting fake or copied biometric samples
