gamble

noun
risky act or undertaking
2.9 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgæm.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈgæm.bəl/ (BrE)
a risky act or undertaking in which the outcome is uncertain and success depends largely on chance

Examples

A risky gamble
Investing in that startup was a huge gamble, but it paid off.
Starting your own business can be a big gamble if you don't have a clear plan.
Taking the mountain road at night was a dangerous gamble given the weather conditions.
