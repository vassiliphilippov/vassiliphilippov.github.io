International Wanted Notice

noun
Interpol notice seeking arrest and extradition
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənl ˈwɑːntɪd ˈnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃ(ə)n(ə)l ˈwɒntɪd ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
an official Interpol notice describing a person sought for arrest and extradition, issued at the request of a member country and distributed globally

Examples

An International Wanted Notice
Police issued an International Wanted Notice after the suspect fled abroad.
Thanks to the International Wanted Notice, border authorities were able to detain the individual at the airport.
The fugitive was named in an International Wanted Notice circulated to all Interpol member countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Red Notice - Interpol's formal request to locate and provisionally arrest a person pending extradition
