heynim

noun
respectful address for elder
9.9
Pronunciation: / heɪ.nɪm / (AmE), / heɪ.nɪm / (BrE)
a respectful term used to address an elder or senior, especially in Korean culture

Examples

Respectful heynim greeting
When entering the room, he respectfully greeted his grandmother with 'heynim'.
Children are taught to use 'heynim' when speaking to elders in their community.
She addressed the village elder as 'heynim' to show her respect.
