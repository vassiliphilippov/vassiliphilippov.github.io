possessible

adjective
able to be owned
7.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛsəbəl / (AmE), / pəˈzɛsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be owned, held, or possessed by someone

Examples

Possessible property
Only possessible goods can be transferred by sale.
Land is considered possessible property in most legal systems.
Some ideas are not possessible and cannot be owned by anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ownable - able to be owned
  • antonym: unpossessable - not able to be possessed or owned
