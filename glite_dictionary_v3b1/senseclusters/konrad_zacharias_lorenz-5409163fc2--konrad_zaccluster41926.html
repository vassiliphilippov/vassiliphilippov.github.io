Konrad Zacharias Lorenz

proper noun
Austrian zoologist, pioneer of animal behavior research
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.rɑd ˌzɑː.kəˈraɪ.i.əs ləˈrɛnts / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.rad ˌzæ.kəˈraɪ.i.əs lɒˈrɛnts / (BrE)
Austrian zoologist (1903–1989) known for pioneering studies of animal behavior, particularly imprinting in birds, and his emphasis on innate behavioral patterns.
