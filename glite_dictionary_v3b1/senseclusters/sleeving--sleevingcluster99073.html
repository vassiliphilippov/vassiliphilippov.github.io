sleeving

noun
protective tube
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
a protective tube or covering placed around wires, cables, or other objects to insulate or shield them

Examples

Cable sleeving
The technician added sleeving to the cables to prevent electrical shorts.
Colored sleeving helps organize and protect computer wires inside the case.
Before installation, all exposed wires were covered with sleeving for safety.
