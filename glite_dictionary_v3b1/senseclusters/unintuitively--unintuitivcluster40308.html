unintuitively

adverb
not in an obvious way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈtuːɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtjuːɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is not easy to understand or does not follow what one would expect naturally

Examples

Written unintuitively
The instructions were written so unintuitively that even experienced users struggled to follow them.
He found the controls on the new phone worked unintuitively compared to his old device.
Some websites are designed unintuitively, making it hard for visitors to find important information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterintuitively - in a way that is contrary to what one would expect
  • antonym: intuitively - in a way that is easy to understand or feels natural
