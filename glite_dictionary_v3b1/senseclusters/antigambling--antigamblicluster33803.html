antigambling

noun
opposition to gambling
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈɡæmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈɡæmblɪŋ / (BrE)
opposition to gambling, especially as a policy, movement, or public sentiment seeking to restrict or prevent gambling

Examples

Antigambling movement
The city council passed new antigambling laws to limit the number of casinos.
His strong antigambling stance made him popular among conservative voters.
A national antigambling movement gained momentum after several scandals.
