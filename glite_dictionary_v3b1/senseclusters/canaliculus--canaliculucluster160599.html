canaliculus

noun
small duct or canal in bone or plants
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəˈlɪkjələs / (AmE), / ˌkænəˈlɪkjʊləs / (BrE)
a small canal or duct, especially in bone or plant tissue

Examples

A canaliculus in bone
Bile passes through a canaliculus before reaching the gall bladder.
The osteocyte communicates with others through the canaliculus network in bone tissue.
The root hairs absorbed water that traveled along a narrow canaliculus in the plant stem.
