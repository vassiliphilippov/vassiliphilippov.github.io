flash lamp

noun
photography lamp for momentary light
Pronunciation: / flæʃ læmp / (AmE), / flæʃ læmp / (BrE)
a lamp that produces a brief, intense burst of light used for taking photographs

Examples

A flash lamp for photos
Early cameras often required a flash lamp to capture clear indoor photos.
She replaced the old bulb in her flash lamp before the photo shoot.
The photographer used a flash lamp to illuminate the dark room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flashbulb - an electric bulb that produces a brief bright flash for photography
  • synonym: flashgun - a device for producing a flash of artificial light in photography
