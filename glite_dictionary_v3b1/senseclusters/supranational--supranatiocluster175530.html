supranational

adjective
beyond or above national boundaries
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈnæʃənl / (AmE), / ˌsjuːprəˈnæʃənl / (BrE)
extending beyond or transcending established national boundaries or spheres of interest, especially in reference to organizations or activities involving multiple countries

Examples

Supranational organization
Global problems like climate change require supranational cooperation to solve.
Many treaties are decided by supranational bodies rather than local governments.
The European Union is considered a leading example of a supranational organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: international - existing, occurring, or carried on between nations
