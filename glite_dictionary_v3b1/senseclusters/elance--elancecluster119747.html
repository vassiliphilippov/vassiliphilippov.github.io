elance

verb
to dart forward
9.9
Pronunciation: / iˈlæns / (AmE), / iˈlɑːns / (BrE)
to dart or rush forward suddenly and quickly in a particular direction

Examples

To elance across the field
The rabbit would elance across the field whenever it sensed danger nearby.
At the sound of the starting gun, the runners elanced from the line with incredible speed.
She watched the children elance toward the playground as soon as the bell rang.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bolt - to move suddenly and quickly, often to escape
  • synonym: dash - to move quickly and suddenly
  • antonym: linger - to stay in a place longer than necessary
