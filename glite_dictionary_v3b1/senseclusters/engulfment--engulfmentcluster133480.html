engulfment

noun
being surrounded completely
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of being completely surrounded, covered, or overwhelmed by something

Examples

Engulfment by lava
The sudden engulfment of the village by lava left little time for evacuation.
During the storm, the engulfment of the highway by water made travel impossible.
The engulfment of the city in darkness was caused by a widespread power outage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exposure - the state of being uncovered or unprotected
  • synonym: immersion - the state of being deeply covered or involved in something
