trumpet one's achievements

verb
boast about one's successes
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpɪt wʌnz əˈtʃivmənts / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt wʌnz əˈtʃiːvmənts / (BrE)

Examples

To trumpet one's achievements

Some people prefer to stay humble instead of trumpeting their achievements in public.
