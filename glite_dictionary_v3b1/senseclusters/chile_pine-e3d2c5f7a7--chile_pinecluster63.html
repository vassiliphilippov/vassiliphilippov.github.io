chile pine

noun
a Chilean evergreen conifer with edible nuts
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːleɪ paɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli paɪn / (BrE)
a large evergreen conifer native to Chile, with intertwined branches and edible nuts, also known as monkey puzzle tree

Examples

A chile pine tree
Gardeners sometimes plant a chile pine for its striking, unusual appearance.
In Chilean forests, the chile pine produces nutritious nuts eaten by wildlife and humans alike.
The chile pine stands tall and majestic, its unique branches twisting skyward.
