regurgitate

verb
repeat memorized information
9.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡɜrdʒəˌteɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɜːdʒɪteɪt / (BrE)
to repeat information from memory, especially without understanding or originality

Examples

Regurgitate facts in class
During the discussion, she regurgitated arguments she had read online without adding any personal insight.
He could regurgitate the entire speech word for word from memory.
Some students simply regurgitate facts for the test without truly grasping the concepts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parrot - to repeat mechanically without understanding
