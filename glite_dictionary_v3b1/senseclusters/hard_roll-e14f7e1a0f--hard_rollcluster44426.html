hard roll

noun
bread roll with a hard crust
Pronunciation: / hɑrd roʊl / (AmE), / hɑːd rəʊl / (BrE)
a small, crusty bread roll raised with yeast and baked to have a hard outer crust

Examples

A hard roll with butter
I love the crisp crust of a hard roll with butter in the morning.
She ordered a coffee and a hard roll for breakfast at the café.
The deli served sandwiches made with fresh hard rolls and a variety of fillings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kaiser roll - a type of hard roll, typically round with a fivefold pattern on top
