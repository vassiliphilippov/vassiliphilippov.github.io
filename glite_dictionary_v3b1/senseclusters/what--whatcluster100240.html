what

pronoun
question word
Pronunciation: / wʌt / (AmE), / wɒt / (BrE)
used to ask for information about something or to clarify details in a question

Examples

What happened next
What time does the train leave this evening?
Can you tell me what happened at the meeting yesterday?
What are you planning to do for your birthday this year?

Related Terms
