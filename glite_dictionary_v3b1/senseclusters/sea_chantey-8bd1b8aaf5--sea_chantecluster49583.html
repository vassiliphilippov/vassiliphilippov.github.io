sea chantey

noun
sailors' work song
6.8
Pronunciation: / si ˈʃæn.ti / (AmE), / si ˈʃæn.ti / (BrE)
a rhythmical work song originally sung by sailors, typically to coordinate labor on a ship

Examples

A sea chantey song
During the festival, a group performed a lively sea chantey from the 19th century.
He taught the children to sing an old sea chantey about a whaling voyage.
The crew sang a sea chantey as they hoisted the heavy anchor.
