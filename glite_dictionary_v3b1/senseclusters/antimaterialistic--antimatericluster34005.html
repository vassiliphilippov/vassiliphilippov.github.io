antimaterialistic

adjective
opposed to materialism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntimaˌtɪriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiməˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
not valuing material possessions or wealth highly; opposed to materialism

Examples

Antimaterialistic lifestyle choice
He chose an antimaterialistic lifestyle, focusing on experiences rather than possessions.
Their antimaterialistic philosophy encouraged living simply and helping others.
The movement promotes antimaterialistic values in a consumer-driven society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetic - practicing strict self-denial and avoiding material comforts
  • antonym: materialistic - valuing material possessions and wealth highly
