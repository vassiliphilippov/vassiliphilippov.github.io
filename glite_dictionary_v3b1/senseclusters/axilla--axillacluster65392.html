axilla

noun
the armpit; hollow under the shoulder
Pronunciation: / æɡˈzɪlə / (AmE), / ækˈsɪlə / (BrE)
the hollow area beneath the junction of the arm and shoulder; the armpit, especially in medical or anatomical use

Examples

The axilla area
A rash appeared in the axilla after using a new deodorant.
Enlarged lymph nodes were detected in her left axilla.
The doctor examined the patient's axilla for signs of swelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armpit - the underarm area where the arm joins the shoulder
