lurchingly

adverb
abruptly and unpredictably
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɜːr.tʃɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈlɜː.tʃɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that moves or changes suddenly, unpredictably, or in an unsteady manner, often figuratively.

Examples

Lurchingly change topics
The conversation moved lurchingly from one topic to another, making it hard to follow.
After the announcement, the stock market reacted lurchingly for several days.
His career advanced lurchingly, with sudden changes and unexpected setbacks.
