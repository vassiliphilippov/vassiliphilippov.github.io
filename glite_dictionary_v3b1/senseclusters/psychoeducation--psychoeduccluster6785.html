psychoeducation

noun
mental health instruction
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or program of teaching people about mental health conditions, treatments, and ways to cope

Examples

psychoeducation sessions
The hospital offers psychoeducation sessions to help patients understand their diagnoses.
Family members attended a psychoeducation workshop to learn how to support their loved ones.
Effective psychoeducation can improve treatment outcomes for people with mental health conditions.
The group session included psychoeducation about recognizing early signs of depression.
Parents received psychoeducation to better support their child's recovery from anxiety.
Effective psychoeducation can empower patients to manage their mental health more confidently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counseling - professional guidance to help people resolve personal or psychological problems
  • synonym: therapy - treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder
