overseas cap

noun
wedge-shaped uniform cap
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsiz kæp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsiːz kæp / (BrE)
A wedge-shaped wool or cotton cap, often without a brim, worn as part of a military or uniformed organization's attire.

Examples

An overseas cap worn
Each soldier was issued an overseas cap along with their uniform.
He adjusted his overseas cap before marching in the parade.
The overseas cap fits easily into a pocket when it is not being worn.
