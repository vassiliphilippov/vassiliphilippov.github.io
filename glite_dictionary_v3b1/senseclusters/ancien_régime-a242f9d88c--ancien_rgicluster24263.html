ancien régime

noun
former political/social system (esp. pre-Revolutionary France)
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑn.si.æ̃ ʁeɪˈʒiːm /; / ɑ̃.si.ɛ̃ ɹeɪˈʒiːm / (AmE), / ˌɒ̃.si.ɑ̃ ʁeɪˈʒiːm /; / ɒ̃.sjɛ̃ ʁeɪˈʒiːm / (BrE)
the political and social system in France before the French Revolution, or any similar old system of government that has been replaced

Examples

The ancien régime system

Some historians compare the company’s rigid hierarchy to an ancien régime.
The collapse of the ancien régime paved the way for modern democracy in France.
