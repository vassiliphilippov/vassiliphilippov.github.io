ticky

adjective
infested with ticks

covered with or infested by ticks, which are small parasitic insects.

Examples

Ticky socks
After hiking in the woods, her socks were ticky and needed to be washed carefully.
The veterinarian warned that the old barn was especially ticky in the summer months.
Farmers check their animals regularly to make sure they are not ticky after grazing.
