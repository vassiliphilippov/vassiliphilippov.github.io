monkeypox

noun
viral disease with lesions
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkipɑks /; / ˈmʌŋkiˌpɑks / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkipɒks /; / ˈmʌŋkiˌpɒks / (BrE)
a contagious viral disease that causes fever, rash, and skin lesions, mainly affecting monkeys, rodents, and sometimes humans
