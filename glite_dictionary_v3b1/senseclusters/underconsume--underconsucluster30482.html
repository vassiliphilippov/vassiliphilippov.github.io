underconsume

verb
to consume too little
6.7
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.kən.ˈsuːm/ /; / /ʌn.dər.kən.ˈsjuːm/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.kən.ˈsjuːm/ /; / /ʌn.də.kən.ˈsuːm/ (BrE)
to consume less than the usual, recommended, or necessary amount of something

Examples

To underconsume calories
If you underconsume protein, your muscles may not recover properly after exercise.
Many people unintentionally underconsume vegetables despite knowing their health benefits.
Children who underconsume calories may have trouble concentrating in school.
