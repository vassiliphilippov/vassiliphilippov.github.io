puy

noun
volcanic hill
Pronunciation: / pwi / (AmE), / pwi / (BrE)
a small, isolated volcanic hill, especially found in central France

Examples

A volcanic puy
The region is famous for its many puys rising above the surrounding fields.
A single puy dominated the otherwise flat landscape of central France.
Geologists studied the unique formation of the ancient puy near the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hill - a naturally raised area of land, smaller than a mountain
  • synonym: volcano - a mountain or hill with a crater through which lava erupts
