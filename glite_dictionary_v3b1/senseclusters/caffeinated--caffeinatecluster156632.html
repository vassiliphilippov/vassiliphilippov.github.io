caffeinated

verb
to add caffeine
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæfɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
gave someone or something caffeine, or made energetic or stimulated, especially by adding caffeine

Examples

To caffeinate the team
The coach caffeinated the team with strong coffee before the early morning game.
She caffeinated herself with a double espresso to stay awake during the long meeting.
The students caffeinated their study session with energy drinks and lively music.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depress - to make less active or energetic
  • synonym: energize - to give energy or vitality to
  • synonym: stimulate - to make more active or alert
