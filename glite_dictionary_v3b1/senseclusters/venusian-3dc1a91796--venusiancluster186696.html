Venusian

noun
being from Venus
9.5
Pronunciation: / vɪˈnuːʒən / (AmE), / vɪˈnjuːʒən / (BrE)
a hypothetical or fictional being from the planet Venus, often found in science fiction

Examples

A Venusian alien
The science fiction novel described a peaceful society of Venusians living beneath thick clouds.
In the movie, the Venusian ambassador arrived on Earth to negotiate an alliance.
Children imagined what a Venusian might look like while drawing pictures in class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Earthling - an inhabitant of Earth
  • synonym: Martian - a being from the planet Mars
