acute

adjective
keenly perceptive; insightful
Pronunciation: / əˈkjut / (AmE), / əˈkjuːt / (BrE)
having or showing a perceptive, insightful, or keen mind; able to notice and understand things quickly and clearly

Examples

Acute mind
He offered an acute analysis of the economic situation that impressed everyone.
Her acute mind quickly grasped the underlying problem in the discussion.
Only an acute observer would notice the subtle changes in his behavior.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking sharpness of mind or perception
  • synonym: incisive - showing clear and direct understanding
  • synonym: perceptive - quick to notice and understand things
