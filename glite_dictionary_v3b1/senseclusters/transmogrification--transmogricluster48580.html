transmogrification

noun
cosmetic change of game equipment
9.9
Pronunciation: /træns.mə.ɡrɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /trænz.mə.ɡrɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /træns.mə.ɡrɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /trænz.mə.ɡrɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
(in video games) the action of changing the appearance of a weapon, armor, or other item without altering its gameplay statistics or functionality.

Examples

Armor transmogrification
Many MMORPGs let players use transmogrification to make their armor look unique without changing stats.
She applied a rare skin in the game as a transmogrification to match her character's style.
The patch added a menu option for transmogrification, letting users swap appearances while keeping equipment power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmetic change - a visual alteration that does not affect underlying function or performance
  • synonym: reskin - to change the visible appearance of a game item or character without changing how it works
