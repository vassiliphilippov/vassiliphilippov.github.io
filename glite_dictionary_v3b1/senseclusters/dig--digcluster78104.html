dig

verb
to poke or thrust abruptly
Pronunciation: / dɪɡ / (AmE), / dɪɡ / (BrE)
to poke or thrust something abruptly into or at something or someone

Examples

To dig an elbow
He dug his elbow sharply into my side to get my attention.
She dug her fingers into the soft cake to test its texture.
The nurse gently dug the needle into his arm to draw blood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jab - to poke quickly and forcefully
  • synonym: poke - to prod or push with something pointed
