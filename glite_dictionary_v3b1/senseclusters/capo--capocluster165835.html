capo

noun
guitar pitch clamp
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkæpəʊ / (BrE)
a device that clamps onto the neck of a guitar or similar instrument to raise the pitch of the strings

Examples

Guitar capo
Before playing the song, she placed a capo on the third fret of her guitar.
Many folk musicians use a capo to easily change the key of a song.
He forgot his capo at home and had to borrow one for the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clamp - a device used to hold things tightly together
