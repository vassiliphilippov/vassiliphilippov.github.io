monstrous

adjective
abnormally large or huge
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnstrəs / (AmE), / ˈmɒnstrəs / (BrE)
Extremely and abnormally large in size, amount, or degree.

Examples

Monstrous wave
