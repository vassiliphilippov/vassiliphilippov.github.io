mid-off

noun
cricket fielding position near bowler on off side
6.8
Pronunciation: / mɪd ˈɔf / (AmE), / mɪd ˈɒf / (BrE)
the fielding position in cricket near the bowler on the off side, typically positioned between extra cover and the bowler

Examples

Mid-off fielder
A sharp drive went straight to mid-off, resulting in an easy wicket.
He took a brilliant catch at mid-off during the final over.
The captain positioned the best fielder at mid-off to stop any quick singles.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cover - a fielding position on the off side further from the bowler than mid-off
  • antonym: mid-on - the corresponding fielding position on the leg side near the bowler in cricket
