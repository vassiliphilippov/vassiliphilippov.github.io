caterpillar

noun
larval stage of butterfly or moth
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬ɚˌpɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkætəˌpɪlə / (BrE)
the wormlike larval stage of a butterfly or moth, often brightly colored and sometimes covered in hairs or spines

Examples

A green caterpillar
Monarch butterflies begin life as a caterpillar before transforming into their adult form.
The caterpillar inched its way along the leaf, eating as it went.
We found a brightly colored caterpillar crawling on the garden fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: larva - immature form of an animal that undergoes transformation
