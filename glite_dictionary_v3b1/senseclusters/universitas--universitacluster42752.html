universitas

noun
historical corporate body
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪvərˈsiːtɑːs / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪvɜːˈsiːtɑːs / (BrE)
a corporate body, guild, or association; a legal community or corporation, especially in historical contexts

Examples

Medieval universitas guild
In medieval Europe, a universitas could refer to a guild of craftsmen or merchants.
The universitas of scholars played a key role in the development of early universities.
Membership in a universitas provided legal protection and collective bargaining power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporation - a legal entity recognized as a single body
  • synonym: guild - an association of people with similar interests or professions
