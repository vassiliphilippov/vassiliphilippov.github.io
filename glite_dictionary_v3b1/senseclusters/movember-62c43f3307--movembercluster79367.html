Movember

proper noun
men's health campaign
6.5
Pronunciation: /moʊ.ˈvem.bər/ (AmE), /məʊ.ˈvem.bə/ (BrE)
an annual campaign and charity in November where men grow mustaches to raise awareness and funds for men's health issues

Examples

Movember campaign
Every Movember, thousands of men grow mustaches to support awareness of prostate cancer.
He participated in Movember by raising money for men's mental health charities.
The office held a Movember contest to see who could grow the best mustache.
Every year, Movember encourages men to grow moustaches to support health causes.
He participated in Movember to help raise money for prostate cancer research.
The Movember campaign has become a global movement for men's health awareness.
