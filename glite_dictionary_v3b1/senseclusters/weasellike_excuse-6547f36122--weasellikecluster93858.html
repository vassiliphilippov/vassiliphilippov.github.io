weasellike excuse

noun
deceitful or evasive excuse
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwizəlˌlaɪk ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ˈwiːzəllaɪk ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
an excuse that is deceitful or evasive, intended to avoid blame or responsibility

Examples

Weasellike excuse to avoid blame
His weasellike excuses did nothing to convince the committee of his innocence.
She offered a weasellike excuse when asked why she forgot their anniversary.
The manager was tired of hearing yet another weasellike excuse for missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alibi - an excuse, especially one used to avoid blame
  • synonym: pretext - a false reason given to hide the real motivation
