lyrical

adjective
expressing deep emotion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪkəl /; / ˈlɪr.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪrɪkəl /; / ˈlɪr.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
