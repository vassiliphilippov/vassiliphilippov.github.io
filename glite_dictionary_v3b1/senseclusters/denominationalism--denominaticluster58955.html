denominationalism

noun
adherence to a religious denomination
4.6 9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm /; / dɪˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm / (BrE)
strong loyalty to a particular religious denomination, or the tendency to form separate religious groups based on differences in belief
