one-star review

noun
a very poor review or rating
1.5
Pronunciation: / wʌn stɑɹ rɪˈvju / (AmE), / wʌn stɑː rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a review or rating indicating the lowest level of quality, typically using a one-star system

Examples

A one-star review
A single one-star review can seriously impact a restaurant's reputation.
After experiencing terrible customer service, she left a one-star review on the business's website.
The hotel manager responded to the one-star review and promised to make improvements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: five-star review - a review or rating indicating the highest level of quality
  • synonym: negative review - a review expressing disapproval or dissatisfaction
