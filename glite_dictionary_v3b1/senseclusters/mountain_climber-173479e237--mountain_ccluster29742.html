mountain climber

noun
person who climbs mountains
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən ˌklaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˌklaɪmə / (BrE)

Examples

Many mountain climbers dream of conquering Mount Everest.
The mountain climber scaled the peak despite the harsh weather conditions.
