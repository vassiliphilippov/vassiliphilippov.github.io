idyll

noun
short poem about rural life
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.dəl /; / ˈaɪ.dl̩ /; / ˈaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˈɪd.ɪl /; / ˈɪ.dɪl /; / ˈɪdɪl / (BrE)
