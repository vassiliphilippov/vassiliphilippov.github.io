pulselessness

noun
lack of vitality
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsləsnəs / (BrE)
a state of lacking vitality, energy, or liveliness; dullness or lifelessness, especially in a figurative sense.

Examples

Pulselessness of the party
The pulselessness of the event left the audience feeling bored and uninterested.
Critics noted the pulselessness in the team's performance during the final match.
Her speech was criticized for its pulselessness and lack of inspiration.
