shingle tree

noun
East Indian timber tree for tea boxes
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪŋɡəl ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈʃɪŋɡəl ˌtriː / (BrE)
An East Indian timber tree whose hard and durable wood is used especially for making tea boxes.

Examples

The shingle tree wood
In some regions, the shingle tree is also used in furniture and construction.
The wood from the shingle tree is prized for its strength and durability.
Workers carefully selected shingle tree timber for the production of export-quality tea boxes.
