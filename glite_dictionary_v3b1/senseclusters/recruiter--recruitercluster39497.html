recruiter

noun
person who finds and hires members or employees
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkruːtər / (AmE), / rɪˈkruːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to find and enlist new employees, members, or participants for an organization or group

Examples

Company recruiter
During the job fair, I spoke with a recruiter from a major tech company.
Many military recruiters visit high schools to discuss career opportunities.
The recruiter contacted several candidates for the new engineering positions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: applicant - a person who applies for a job
  • degree higher: employer - a person or organization that hires employees
  • synonym: headhunter - a person who recruits candidates for high-level jobs
