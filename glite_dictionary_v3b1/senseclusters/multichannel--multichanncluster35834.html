multichannel

adjective
using multiple channels
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈtʃænəl / (BrE)
involving or using several different methods or channels at the same time

Examples

Multichannel marketing approach
The retailer adopted a multichannel strategy to improve customer engagement both online and in stores.
A multichannel marketing campaign can reach a wider audience than a single-channel approach.
Many companies now use multichannel communication to provide better customer service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: omnichannel - using all available channels in a seamless way
  • antonym: single-channel - using only one method or channel
