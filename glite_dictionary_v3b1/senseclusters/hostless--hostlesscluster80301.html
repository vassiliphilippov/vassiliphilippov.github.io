hostless

adjective
without a central host
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstləs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstləs / (BrE)
not requiring or depending on a host, especially referring to systems or software that operate without a central server or controller

Examples

Hostless system
The new hostless platform allows users to connect directly without a central server.
Many modern applications are designed to be hostless for greater flexibility and security.
A hostless network can reduce costs by eliminating the need for dedicated infrastructure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decentralized - not controlled by a single central authority
  • antonym: hosted - requiring or depending on a host or central server
  • synonym: serverless - operating without a dedicated server
