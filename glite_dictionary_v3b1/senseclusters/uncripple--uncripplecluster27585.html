uncripple

verb
to restore function
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɪpəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪpəl / (BrE)
to restore something or someone to normal function after being crippled or damaged; to remove disabling limitations.

Examples

To uncripple the network
The engineers worked overnight to uncripple the network after the cyberattack.
It took several updates to uncripple the software and restore its full features.
After the repairs, the team managed to uncripple the damaged equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cripple - to cause severe damage or disable
  • synonym: repair - to fix something that is broken or damaged
