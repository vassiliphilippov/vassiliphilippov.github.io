programmable

adjective
can be set by computer
3.8
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡræməbəl / (AmE), / prəˈɡræməbəl / (BrE)
able to be set or controlled by a computer or according to instructions

Examples

A programmable device
The new coffee maker is programmable, so you can set it to brew before you wake up.
Many modern washing machines are programmable for different types of laundry cycles.
A programmable robot can perform a variety of tasks based on your instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • antonym: manual - operated or done by hand rather than automatically
