plyometric

adjective
explosive exercise-related
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌplaɪ.oʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌplaɪ.əʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to exercises that use rapid, explosive movements to increase muscular power

Examples

Plyometric exercises
The coach designed a plyometric workout to help the team improve their speed and agility.
Many fitness programs include plyometric drills such as jump squats and box jumps.
Athletes use plyometric exercises to develop explosive power for sports like basketball and volleyball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explosive - involving sudden or rapid force
  • antonym: isometric - relating to exercises involving static muscle contraction without movement
