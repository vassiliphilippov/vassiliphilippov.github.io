buy into

verb
purchase shares or a stake in a company
Pronunciation: / baɪ ˈɪntuː / (AmE), / baɪ ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to purchase stocks, shares, or a stake in a company or business

Examples

To buy into a company
He was hesitant to buy into the startup until he saw its financial reports.
Many employees were given the opportunity to buy into the firm during its expansion.
Several investors decided to buy into the software company last year.
