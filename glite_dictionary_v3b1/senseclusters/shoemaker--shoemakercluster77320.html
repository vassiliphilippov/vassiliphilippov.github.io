shoemaker

noun
person who makes or repairs shoes
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or repair shoes

Examples

A shoemaker repairs shoes
In the fairy tale, elves helped the shoemaker finish his work overnight.
She brought her old boots to the shoemaker for repair.
The shoemaker carefully stitched the leather before attaching the sole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cobbler - a person who mends shoes as a profession
