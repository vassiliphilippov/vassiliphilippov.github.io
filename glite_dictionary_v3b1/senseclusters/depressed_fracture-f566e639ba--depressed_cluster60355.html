depressed fracture

noun
skull fracture with bone pushed inward
Pronunciation: / dɪˈprɛst ˈfræk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈprɛst ˈfræk.tʃə / (BrE)
a fracture of the skull in which a bone fragment is pushed inward toward the brain

Examples

A depressed fracture of skull
A CT scan revealed a depressed fracture causing pressure on the brain tissue.
Immediate surgery was required to treat the depressed fracture of her skull.
The patient suffered a depressed fracture after a heavy object fell on his head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skull fracture - a break in one of the bones of the skull
