stalwart

adjective
loyal and dependable person
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔlwɚt /; / ˈstɔlˌwɑrt / (AmE), / ˈstɔːlwət /; / ˈstɔːl.wət / (BrE)
loyal, reliable, and hardworking; strongly dependable in character or support

Examples

Stalwart supporter
As a stalwart defender, she rarely missed a game.
Despite many challenges, the team remained stalwart in their commitment to success.
Her father was a stalwart supporter of the local community.
She has always been a stalwart supporter of human rights causes.
The team relied on his stalwart commitment during difficult times.
They praised the stalwart volunteers who helped rebuild the town after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faithful - remaining loyal and steadfast
  • synonym: loyal - showing firm support or allegiance
  • synonym: reliable - able to be depended on; trustworthy
  • antonym: unreliable - not able to be trusted or depended on
