sanctuary

noun
jurisdiction protecting undocumented immigrants
6.1 2/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8s\u00e6\u014bkt\u0283u\u02cc\u025bri / (AmE), / \u02c8sa\u014bk.tju.\u0259.ri / (BrE)
a city, state, or other political jurisdiction that adopts policies limiting cooperation with federal immigration enforcement and that provides protections for undocumented immigrants.

Examples

Sanctuary city
Several cities declared themselves sanctuary jurisdictions to protect undocumented residents from deportation efforts.
Local leaders argued that becoming a sanctuary would build trust between immigrant communities and city services.
The state passed a law forbidding municipalities from adopting sanctuary policies that limit cooperation with federal agents.
