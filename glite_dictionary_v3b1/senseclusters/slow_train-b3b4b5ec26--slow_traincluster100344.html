slow train

noun
train that moves slowly and has many stops
1.8 2.2
Pronunciation: /sloʊ treɪn/ /; / /sloʊ tʃreɪn/ (AmE), /sləʊ treɪn/ /; / /sləʊ tʃreɪn/ (BrE)
a train that travels at a slow speed, often stopping at most or all stations along its route

Examples

A slow train stops often
After missing the express, he reluctantly boarded the slow train home.
The slow train stopped at every small station along the countryside route.
Tourists preferred the slow train so they could enjoy the scenic views.
