pontificator

noun
pompous speaker
8.9
Pronunciation: / pɒnˈtɪfɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / pɒnˈtɪfɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who speaks or acts in a pompous, dogmatic, or self-important manner

Examples

Annoying pontificator
Everyone grew tired of the pontificator who dominated every meeting with his opinions.
She avoided sitting near the office pontificator because he never let anyone else speak.
The debate was disrupted by a pontificator who refused to consider other viewpoints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowhard - a person who boasts or brags loudly
  • synonym: braggart - someone who boasts a lot
  • antonym: listener - a person who listens to others
