petalous

adjective
having petals (in botany)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtələs / (AmE), / ˈpɛtələs / (BrE)
(of a flower) having petals.

Examples

Petalous flower
Some species in this genus are petalous, while others are completely without petals.
The botanist described the specimen as highly petalous, with five broad petals.
The magnolia is a petalous plant with large, showy flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petal - a modified leaf that is typically colored and forms part of a flower
