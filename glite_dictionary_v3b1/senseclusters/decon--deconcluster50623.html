decon

verb
to remove contaminants
8.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêk…ën / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêk…ín / (BrE)
to clean or remove hazardous substances, such as chemicals or biological agents, from something thoroughly

Examples

To decon equipment

The emergency team quickly deconned the equipment to prevent the spread of chemicals.
