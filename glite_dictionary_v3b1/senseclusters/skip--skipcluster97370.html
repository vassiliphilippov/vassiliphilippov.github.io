skip

noun
a light gait of alternating steps and hops
Pronunciation: / skɪp / (AmE), / skɪp / (BrE)
A light, bouncing movement in which steps and hops alternate, typically used by children.

Examples

A happy skip
His skip showed his excitement as he approached the stage.
She moves with a light skip when she's happy.
The little girl crossed the playground with a joyful skip.
