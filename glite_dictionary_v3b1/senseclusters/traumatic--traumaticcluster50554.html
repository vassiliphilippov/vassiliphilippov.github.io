traumatic

adjective
relating to physical injury
Pronunciation: / trəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / trɔːˈmatɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by physical injury or a wound to the body.

Examples

Traumatic injury
A traumatic fracture can take months to heal completely.
Doctors are trained to respond quickly to traumatic wounds in emergency situations.
The accident resulted in a traumatic brain injury that required immediate surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injurious - causing or likely to cause damage or injury
