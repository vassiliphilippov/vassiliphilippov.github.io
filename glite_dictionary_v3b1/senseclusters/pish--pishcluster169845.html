pish

verb
to attract birds by sound
Pronunciation: / pɪʃ / (AmE), / pɪʃ / (BrE)
to make a short, sharp chattering or hissing sound, especially to attract small birds

Examples

To pish for birds
Birdwatchers often pish softly to encourage warblers to come closer.
He tried to pish behind the bushes, hoping to spot a rare finch.
If you pish near the trees, curious birds may appear to investigate the sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call - to make a sound to attract attention
  • synonym: hiss - to make a sharp sibilant sound like a snake
