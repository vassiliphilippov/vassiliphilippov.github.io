euphoria

noun
intense feeling of happiness
Pronunciation: / juˈfɔriə / (AmE), / juːˈfɔːriə / (BrE)
A feeling of intense happiness or excitement, sometimes exaggerated or irrational.

Examples

A euphoria feeling
Many people experience euphoria after receiving good news or achieving a major goal.
The medication can cause brief periods of euphoria followed by fatigue.
Winning the championship filled her with euphoria that lasted for days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depression - a state of unhappiness or low mood
  • synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • synonym: elation - a feeling of great joy or pride
