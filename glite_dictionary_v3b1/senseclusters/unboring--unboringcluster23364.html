unboring

adjective
interesting or lively
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
not boring; interesting, lively, or engaging

Examples

An unboring lesson
The teacher used games to make the lesson truly unboring for her students.
His stories were so unboring that everyone listened closely until the end.
They wanted their party to be as unboring as possible, so they planned lots of activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boring - not interesting; dull or tedious
  • synonym: engaging - attracting or holding interest
  • synonym: interesting - holding attention or arousing curiosity
