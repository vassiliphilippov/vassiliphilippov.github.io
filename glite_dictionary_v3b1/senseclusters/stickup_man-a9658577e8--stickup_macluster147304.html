stickup man

noun
male armed robber
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkˌʌp mæn / (AmE), / ˈstɪkʌp man / (BrE)
a man who commits armed robbery, especially by threatening people to steal from them

Examples

The neighborhood grew anxious after a stickup man was reported in the area.
