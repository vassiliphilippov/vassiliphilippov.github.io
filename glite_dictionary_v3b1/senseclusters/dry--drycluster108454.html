Synonyms

  • dry out
    2.4 1.26/m
    dry out means to become or make something dry after being wet, while dry is the general verb for removing moisture.
  • dehydrate
    4.1 0.35/m
    dehydrate means to lose water or moisture, often in bodies or food, while dry is the general verb for removing moisture.
  • desiccate
    9.0 0.81/m
    desiccate means to remove moisture completely, often to preserve food or samples, while dry is the more general everyday verb.
  • dry up
    2.7 1.56/m
    dry up means to lose all water and become shriveled or empty, while dry simply means to remove moisture.
