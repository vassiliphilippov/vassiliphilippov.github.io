unsympathetic

adjective
not likable; not evoking sympathy
6.4 5.3
Pronunciation: /ən.sɪm.pə.ˈθe.tɪk/ /; / /ʌn.sɪm.pə.ˈθe.tɪk/ (AmE), /ʌn.sɪm.pə.ˈθe.tɪk/ /; / /ən.sɪm.pə.ˈθe.tɪk/ (BrE)
Not evoking feelings of sympathy or approval; lacking in likable or appealing qualities, especially when referring to characters in literature or drama.

Examples
