anchorless

adjective
without stability
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkərləs / (AmE), / ˈæŋkələs / (BrE)
lacking a firm basis, support, or sense of stability; ungrounded or without roots.

Examples

Feeling anchorless
After moving to a new city, he felt anchorless and struggled to find a sense of belonging.
The sudden changes left the organization anchorless and unsure of its direction.
Without close friends or family nearby, she sometimes felt completely anchorless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grounded - having a firm basis or sense of stability
  • synonym: rootless - having no roots or permanent ties
  • synonym: unstable - not steady or secure
