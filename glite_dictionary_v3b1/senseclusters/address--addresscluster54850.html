address

noun
skill in appropriate behavior or speech
Pronunciation: / əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
the manner or style in which someone speaks or acts, especially with skill or self-possession

His condescending address made everyone uncomfortable.
She corrected the student's address when speaking to the professor.
The butler's formal address impressed the guests.
