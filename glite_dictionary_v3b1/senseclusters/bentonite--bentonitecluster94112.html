bentonite

noun
absorbent clay formed from volcanic ash
7.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈben.tə.naɪt/ (AmE), /ˈben.tə.naɪt/ (BrE)
a soft, absorbent type of clay composed mainly of montmorillonite, formed from the weathering of volcanic ash

Examples
