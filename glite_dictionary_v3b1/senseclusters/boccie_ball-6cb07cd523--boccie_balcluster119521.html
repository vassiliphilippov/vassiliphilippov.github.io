boccie ball

noun
wooden ball used in bocce
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtʃi bɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃi bɔːl / (BrE)
A wooden ball used in the Italian game of bocce, which players bowl toward a smaller target ball.
