Myotis

noun
genus of mouse-eared bats
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈoʊtɪs / (AmE), / maɪˈəʊtɪs / (BrE)
A genus of bats known as mouse-eared bats, comprising the largest and most widely distributed group of bats worldwide.

Examples

Myotis bat species
Many members of Myotis are important for controlling insect populations in their habitats.
Researchers discovered a new species belonging to the Myotis genus in Southeast Asia.
The Myotis genus includes nearly one hundred species of mouse-eared bats.
