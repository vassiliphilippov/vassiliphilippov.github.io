deadening

adjective
causing mental fatigue or boredom
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɛd(ə)nɪŋ / (BrE)
making something so dull, repetitive, or uninteresting as to cause mental weariness or boredom.

Examples

Deadening routine
She tried to escape the deadening boredom of the small town.
That meeting had a truly deadening effect on the whole team.
The deadening routine of his job made him long for a change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boring - not interesting; dull
  • antonym: stimulating - encouraging interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: tedious - too long, slow, or dull; tiresome or monotonous
