Yoruba

noun
member of a West African ethnic group
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔr ə bə / (AmE), / jəˈruːbə / (BrE)
A member of a West African ethnic group living mainly in southwestern Nigeria and neighboring regions.

Examples

Yoruba people
He is proud to be a descendant of the Yoruba people.
Many Yoruba have migrated to cities in Nigeria and abroad.
The Yoruba are known for their rich traditions in art and music.
