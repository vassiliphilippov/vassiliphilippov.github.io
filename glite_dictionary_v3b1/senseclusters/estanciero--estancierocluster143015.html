estanciero

adjective
of South American ranches
9.9
Pronunciation: / esˌtɑnˈsjɛəroʊ / (AmE), / esˌtanˈsjɛərəʊ / (BrE)
relating to an estancia, a large South American ranch, or to the lifestyle and traditions of its owners

Examples

Estanciero lifestyle
The estanciero architecture features wide verandas and high ceilings to keep the house cool.
They decorated the room in an estanciero style, using leather furniture and traditional patterns.
Many estanciero customs are still celebrated in rural Argentina today.
