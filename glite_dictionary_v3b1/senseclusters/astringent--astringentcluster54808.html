astringent

adjective
tasting sharp, sour, or bitter
2.7
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɪndʒənt / (AmE), / əˈstrɪndʒənt / (BrE)
Having a sharp, slightly sour or bitter taste that causes a puckering sensation in the mouth.

Examples

Astringent lemon taste
He grimaced at the astringent taste of the grapefruit.
Some unripe fruits are too astringent to be eaten raw.
The wine has an astringent flavor that lingers on the palate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitter - having a sharp, pungent taste
  • antonym: sweet - having a taste similar to that of sugar or honey
