normie crowd

noun
group conforming to mainstream trends
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrmi kraʊd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːmi kraʊd / (BrE)
A group of people who conform to mainstream trends and popular culture, often seen as unoriginal or conventional.

Examples

The normie crowd
Many online forums are created specifically to avoid the influence of the normie crowd.
She never felt comfortable with the normie crowd at school, preferring more alternative friends.
The normie crowd filled the stadium at the pop concert, singing along to every hit song.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hipster - a person who rejects mainstream trends, often in opposition to 'normies'
  • synonym: normie - a person who conforms to mainstream tastes and trends
