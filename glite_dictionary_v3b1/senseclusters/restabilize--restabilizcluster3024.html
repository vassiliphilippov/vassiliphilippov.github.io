restabilize

verb
to become stable again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsteɪbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsteɪbəlaɪz / (BrE)
to become stable again after having been unstable or disrupted

Examples

To restabilize after shaking
After the earthquake, engineers worked quickly to restabilize the damaged bridge.
The economy began to restabilize following several months of uncertainty.
It took a few minutes for the computer system to restabilize after the unexpected shutdown.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destabilize - to make unstable or less steady
  • synonym: stabilize - to make or become stable
