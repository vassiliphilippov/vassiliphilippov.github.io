insectivorous

adjective
feeding on insects (animals/plants)
4.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.sek.ˈtɪ.vər.əs/ (AmE), /ɪn.sek.ˈtɪ.vər.əs/ (BrE)
(of animals or plants) feeding on insects.

Examples

Insectivorous animals
Bats are often insectivorous, consuming large quantities of mosquitoes each night.
Certain birds, such as swifts and swallows, are highly insectivorous in their feeding habits.
The Venus flytrap is a remarkable insectivorous plant that catches and digests insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnivorous - feeding on other animals; meat-eating
  • antonym: herbivorous - feeding on plants
  • antonym: omnivorous - feeding on both plants and animals
