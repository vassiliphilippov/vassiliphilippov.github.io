Corpus Christi

noun
Christian feast day after Trinity Sunday
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrpəs ˈkrɪsti / (AmE), / ˌkɔːpəs ˈkrɪsti / (BrE)
A Christian feast observed on the Thursday after Trinity Sunday, celebrating the Eucharist; first established in 1246.

Examples

Corpus Christi holiday
Schools and offices may close in some regions during Corpus Christi.
She attended a special mass for Corpus Christi at her local church.
The festival of Corpus Christi is marked by processions in many countries.
