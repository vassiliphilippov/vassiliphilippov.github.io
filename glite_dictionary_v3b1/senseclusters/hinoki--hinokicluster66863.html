hinoki

noun
Japanese cypress wood
Pronunciation: / hɪˈnoʊki / (AmE), / hɪˈnəʊki / (BrE)
the fragrant, durable wood of the Japanese cypress tree, often used in construction, carpentry, and baths

Examples

Hinoki wood bath
The traditional bathhouse was built entirely from hinoki to create a relaxing atmosphere.
Carpenters value hinoki for its pleasant scent and resistance to rot.
A hinoki bench in the garden added a touch of Japanese elegance to the space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cypress - a type of coniferous tree with aromatic wood
