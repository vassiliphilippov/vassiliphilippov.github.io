Saint Matthew

proper noun
apostle and Gospel author
4.7
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈmæθ.ju / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈmæθ.juː / (BrE)
An apostle of Jesus in the New Testament, traditionally regarded as the author of the first Gospel.

Examples
