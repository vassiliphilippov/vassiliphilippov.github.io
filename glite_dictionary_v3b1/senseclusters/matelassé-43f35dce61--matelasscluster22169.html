matelassé

adjective
quilted-texture fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmætəˈlɑːseɪ / (AmE), / ˌmatləˈseɪ / (BrE)
having a raised, quilted pattern that gives a padded or textured appearance, especially in fabric.

Examples

Matelassé pattern fabric
The designer chose a matelassé fabric to add texture to the elegant evening gown.
Our new bedspread features a soft matelassé pattern that feels luxurious and looks stylish.
She admired the matelassé upholstery on the antique armchair in the living room.
