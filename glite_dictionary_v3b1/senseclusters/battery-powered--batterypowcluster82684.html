battery-powered

adjective
powered by batteries
5.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.ər.iː ˈpaʊ.ərd/ /; / /ˈbæt.riː ˈpaʊ.ərd/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.ər.iː ˈpaʊ.əd/ /; / /ˈbæt.riː ˈpaʊ.əd/ (BrE)
(of a device) operated using one or more electric batteries as a source of power.

Examples

Battery-powered device
The battery-powered lantern was a lifesaver during the camping trip.
This toy robot is fully battery-powered and does not require any cables.
We bought a battery-powered fan for use during power outages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rechargeable - able to be recharged for repeated use, especially of a battery or device
