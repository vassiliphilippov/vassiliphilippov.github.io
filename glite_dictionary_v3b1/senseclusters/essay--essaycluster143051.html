essay

noun
a tentative or experimental attempt
Pronunciation: / ˈɛseɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛseɪ / (BrE)
a tentative or experimental attempt to do or achieve something, especially in a formal or literary context

Examples

An essay at painting
His first essay at painting was surprisingly successful.
In her essay to resolve the dispute, she learned a lot about mediation.
The invention, though primitive, was an early essay in flight technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attempt - an act of trying to do something, especially something difficult
  • synonym: trial - a test of the performance, qualities, or suitability of someone or something
