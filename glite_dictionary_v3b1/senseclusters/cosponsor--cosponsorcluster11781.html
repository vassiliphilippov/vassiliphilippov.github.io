cosponsor

verb
jointly sponsor (esp. legislation or events)
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈspɑnsər / (AmE), / kəʊˈspɒnsə / (BrE)
to sponsor something jointly with one or more other sponsors, especially in legislation or events

Examples

To cosponsor a bill
Local businesses decided to cosponsor the charity marathon this year.
Several senators agreed to cosponsor the bill to increase healthcare funding.
She will cosponsor the event with her longtime community partners.
