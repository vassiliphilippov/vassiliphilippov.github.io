lug wrench

noun
tool for loosening or tightening lug nuts
8.2 3.9
Pronunciation: /lʌg rentʃ/ (AmE), /lʌg rentʃ/ (BrE)
a tool with projecting jaws, used to loosen or tighten lug nuts, typically on the wheels of vehicles

Examples

A lug wrench tool
He grabbed the lug wrench from the trunk to remove the flat tire.
The mechanic recommended keeping a lug wrench in the car for emergencies.
Without a lug wrench, it was impossible to loosen the tight lug nuts on the wheel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socket wrench - a tool with a socket to fit over a nut or bolt head and a handle for turning
  • synonym: spanner - a tool with jaws or a hole for gripping and turning nuts or bolts
