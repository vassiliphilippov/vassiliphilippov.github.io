frightfully

adverb
very; extremely (emphasizer)
3.8 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfraɪt.fə.liː/ /; / /ˈfraɪt.fliː/ (AmE), /ˈfraɪt.fə.liː/ /; / /ˈfraɪt.fliː/ (BrE)
(especially British English) used to emphasize the degree of something, similar to 'very' or 'extremely'.
