disability insurance

noun
insurance for inability to work due to disability
5.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsæbəlɪti ɪnˈʃʊrəns / (AmE), / dɪˈsæbɪlɪti ɪnˈʃʊərəns / (BrE)
insurance that provides income or benefits to individuals who are unable to work due to a disability.

Examples

Disability insurance benefits
After her injury at work, her disability insurance helped cover her living expenses.
Many employers offer disability insurance as part of their benefits package.
Without disability insurance, he worried about supporting his family if he ever became unable to work.
