unshipping

noun
removal of cargo
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing cargo or goods from a ship or vehicle

Examples

The unshipping of cargo
The unshipping of the cargo was delayed due to heavy rain at the dock.
After a long journey, the crew began the unshipping of supplies from the vessel.
Efficient unshipping is essential to keep the port operations running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loading - the act of putting goods onto a ship or vehicle
  • synonym: unloading - the process of removing goods from a vehicle or container
