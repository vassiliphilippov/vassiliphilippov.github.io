knacker

noun
person who slaughters old horses
Pronunciation: / ˈnækər / (AmE), / ˈnækə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to buy and slaughter old or injured horses, usually for their by-products such as meat, hides, or glue

Examples

Horse knacker
After years of faithful service, the horse met its end at the hands of a knacker.
Many rural communities had a local knacker who dealt with unfit animals.
The old mare was sold to the knacker after she could no longer work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slaughterer - a person who kills animals for food or other products
