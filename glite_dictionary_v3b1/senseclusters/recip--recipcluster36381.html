recip

noun
reciprocating saw
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛsɪp / (AmE), / ˈrɛsɪp / (BrE)
an informal term for a reciprocating saw, a handheld power tool with a push-and-pull blade used for cutting and demolition

Examples

Cutting with a recip
The plumber used a recip to quickly cut through the old pipes under the sink.
Before starting the demolition, she made sure her recip was fully charged and ready.
He prefers using a recip for tough jobs that require cutting through metal or wood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jigsaw - a power tool with a straight, narrow blade for cutting curves
  • synonym: reciprocating saw - a power tool with a blade that moves back and forth for cutting
