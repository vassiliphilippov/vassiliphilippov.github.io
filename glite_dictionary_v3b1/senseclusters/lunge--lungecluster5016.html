lunge

noun
exercise movement
3.2
Pronunciation: / lʌndʒ / (AmE), / lʌndʒ / (BrE)
an exercise movement in which you step forward and bend your front knee while keeping your back leg straight or bent

Examples

A lunge exercise
The trainer demonstrated how to do a proper lunge to strengthen your legs.
Adding lunges to your routine can help improve your balance and flexibility.
He felt his muscles working after just a few lunges during the warm-up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumping jack - an exercise involving jumping to a position with legs spread and arms overhead
  • synonym: squat - an exercise where you bend your knees and lower your body
