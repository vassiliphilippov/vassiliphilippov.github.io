sun

noun
star with a planetary system
Pronunciation: / sʌn / (AmE), / sʌn / (BrE)
the star at the center of our solar system that gives us light and heat; also, any similar star in other planetary systems

Examples

The sun rises
Each sun in the universe might have its own system of worlds.
Scientists have discovered planets orbiting distant suns in our galaxy.
They studied how light from other suns affects surrounding planets.
Astronomers study the sun to learn more about other stars in the galaxy.
The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.
Without the sun, life on Earth would not be possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star - a luminous celestial body of gas, such as the sun
