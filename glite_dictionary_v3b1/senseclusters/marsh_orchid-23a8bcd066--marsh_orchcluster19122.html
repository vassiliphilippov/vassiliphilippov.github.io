marsh orchid

noun
orchid of genus Dactylorhiza found in marshes
4.1
Pronunciation: / mɑrʃ ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / mɑːʃ ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
any of several orchids of the genus Dactylorhiza, native to Europe and Mediterranean regions, with fingerlike tubers and purple or pink flowers found in wet habitats.

Examples

Wild marsh orchids
Botanists spent the morning surveying the population of marsh orchids in the reserve.
The marsh orchid thrives in wet meadows throughout northern Europe.
We spotted a rare marsh orchid during our walk along the riverbank.
