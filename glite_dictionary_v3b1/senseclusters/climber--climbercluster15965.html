climber

noun
person seeking social advancement
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmə / (BrE)
a person who seeks to advance socially, often by ingratiating themselves with influential people

Examples

Social climber
She was seen as a ruthless climber in the corporate world, always looking for connections.
The party was full of climbers trying to impress the wealthy guests.
Unlike some climbers, he preferred to earn his position through hard work rather than flattery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society
