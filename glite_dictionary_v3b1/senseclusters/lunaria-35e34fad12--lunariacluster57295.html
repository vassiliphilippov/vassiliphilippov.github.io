Lunaria

noun
genus of European flowering plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / luːˈnɛəriə /; / luˈnɛəriə / (AmE), / ljuːˈnɛːrɪə /; / lʊˈnɑːrɪə / (BrE)
a small genus of European flowering plants in the mustard (cabbage) family, including the plant 'honesty', known for its decorative, coin-shaped seed pods and purple flowers
