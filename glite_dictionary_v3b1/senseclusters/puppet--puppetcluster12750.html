puppet

noun
doll worn on hand and manipulated by fingers
1.5 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌ.pɪt/ /; / /ˈpʌ.pət/ (AmE), /ˈpʌ.pɪt/ /; / /ˈpʌ.pət/ (BrE)
a doll or small figure of a person or animal that is moved by hand or strings, especially in shows or entertainment
