bead

verb
form into small drops
4.9
Pronunciation: / bid / (AmE), / biːd / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to form small, rounded drops on a surface.

Examples

Sweat beads on skin
Sweat began to bead on his forehead as he ran up the hill.
Tiny droplets of dew beaded on the blades of grass in the early morning.
Water will bead on a waxed car, repelling moisture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coalesce - come together to form one mass or whole
  • degree higher: drip - to fall in drops
