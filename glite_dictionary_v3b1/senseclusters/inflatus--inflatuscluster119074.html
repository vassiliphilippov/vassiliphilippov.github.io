inflatus

noun
swelling with gas
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪtəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtəs / (BrE)
a state of being inflated or swollen, especially due to the presence of gas in a body part

Examples

Abdominal inflatus
The doctor noted an inflatus in the patient's abdomen during the examination.
After the meal, he complained of discomfort caused by inflatus.
The radiograph showed an inflatus of the stomach.

  • synonym: flatulence - the accumulation of gas in the digestive system
