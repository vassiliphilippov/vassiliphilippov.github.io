maiden

noun
cricket: over with no runs scored
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn / (BrE)
(in cricket) an over in which no runs are scored by the batting team.

Examples

Maiden over bowled
He celebrated after bowling his first maiden in international cricket.
Spin bowlers often aim to bowl a maiden to frustrate the opposition.
The bowler managed to deliver a maiden in his first over, increasing pressure on the batsmen.
