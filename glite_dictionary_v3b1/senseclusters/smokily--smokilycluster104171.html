smokily

adverb
in a smoky manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪli / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is filled with or resembles smoke

Examples

Candles burn smokily
The candles burned smokily, casting soft shadows on the walls.
She sang smokily, her voice low and rough like a jazz singer.
The sun shone smokily through the morning mist after the wildfire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • synonym: hazy - unclear or filled with fine particles in the air
