pareu

noun
Polynesian wrap skirt
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈreɪuː / (AmE), / pæˈreɪuː / (BrE)
a wraparound skirt or cloth, often brightly colored, traditionally worn in Polynesia, especially Tahiti

Examples

A colorful pareu
She tied a colorful pareu around her waist before heading to the beach.
During the festival, many dancers wore a traditional pareu as part of their costume.
He bought a bright pareu as a souvenir from his trip to Tahiti.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sarong - a large piece of cloth wrapped around the waist, worn in Southeast Asia
