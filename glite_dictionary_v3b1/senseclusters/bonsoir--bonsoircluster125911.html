bonsoir

interjection
evening greeting
Pronunciation: / bɔ̃ˈswaʁ / (AmE), / bɔ̃ˈswaʁ / (BrE)
a French greeting or farewell used in the evening, meaning 'good evening'

Examples

Say bonsoir in the evening
As she entered the restaurant, she smiled and said bonsoir to the staff.
He waved and called out bonsoir as he left the evening gathering.
The host greeted each guest with a warm bonsoir at the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good evening - a polite greeting used in the evening
  • antonym: good night - a farewell used at night or before going to bed
