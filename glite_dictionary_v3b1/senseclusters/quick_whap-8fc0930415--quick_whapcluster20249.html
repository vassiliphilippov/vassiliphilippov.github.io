quick whap

noun
swift, sharp hit or sound
6.9
Pronunciation: / kwɪk wæp / (AmE), / kwɪk wæp / (BrE)
A swift, sharp hit or sound occurring suddenly.

Examples

A quick whap of hand
He knocked the dust off the rug with a quick whap of his hand.
The fly met its end with a quick whap from the newspaper.
With a quick whap, she closed the book.
