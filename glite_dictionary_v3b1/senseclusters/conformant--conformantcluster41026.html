conformant

adjective
meeting standards
6.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɔrmənt / (AmE), / kənˈfɔːmənt / (BrE)
meeting the required standards, rules, or specifications

Examples

Conformant with standards
The new building is fully conformant with local safety regulations.
To be conformant, all devices must pass the official certification tests.
Our procedures are conformant with international quality standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noncompliant - not following rules or standards
