classist

adjective
discriminating by class
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsɪst / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪst / (BrE)
showing prejudice or discrimination based on social class

Examples

Classist policy
The hiring policy was criticized as classist because it favored applicants from wealthy backgrounds.
Many activists fight against classist attitudes in education and employment.
Her classist remarks offended several people at the meeting.
Many people found the advertisement to be classist and offensive.
The hiring process was criticized for being classist and favoring wealthy applicants.
She spoke out against the classist attitudes present in the organization.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - believing in or promoting equal rights for all people
  • synonym: elitist - believing that certain people or groups deserve more influence or privileges
  • synonym: prejudiced - having an unreasonable dislike of people based on their group
