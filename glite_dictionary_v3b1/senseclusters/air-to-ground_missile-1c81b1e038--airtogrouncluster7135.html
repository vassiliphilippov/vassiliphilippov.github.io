air-to-ground missile

noun
missile launched from aircraft to ground target
Pronunciation: / ˌɛr tə ˈɡraʊnd ˌmɪsəl / (AmE), / ˌɛə tə ˈɡraʊnd ˌmɪsaɪl / (BrE)
a missile designed to be launched from an aircraft and strike targets on the ground

Examples

Advanced air-to-ground missiles
Advanced air-to-ground missiles are crucial for modern military aircraft.
The fighter jet launched an air-to-ground missile at the enemy bunker.
The pilot was trained to deploy an air-to-ground missile with precision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: surface-to-air missile - a missile designed to be launched from the ground to destroy airborne targets
