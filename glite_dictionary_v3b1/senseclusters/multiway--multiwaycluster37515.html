multiway

noun
speaker with multiple drivers
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌweɪ / (BrE)
a loudspeaker or speaker system that uses two or more separate drivers to reproduce different frequency ranges

Examples

The new home theater system features a powerful multiway for enhanced sound quality.
Audio enthusiasts often prefer a multiway to achieve clearer highs and deeper bass.
