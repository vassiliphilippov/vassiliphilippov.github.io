popsy

noun
slang for attractive woman
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpsi / (AmE), / ˈpɒpsi / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for an attractive young woman, sometimes used to refer to one's father in a playful way

Examples

Pretty popsy

The old man smiled and said, 'Come here, popsy,' to his daughter.
Some people find the word popsy outdated or a bit cheeky.
