messing

verb
to act playfully
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
acting in a careless, playful, or time-wasting way, often not taking things seriously

Examples

Kids messing around
The children were messing around in the backyard instead of cleaning up.
Stop messing and help me set the table for dinner.
He spent the afternoon messing with his new phone, trying out different apps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fooling - acting in a playful or silly way
  • antonym: working - doing tasks seriously or with effort
