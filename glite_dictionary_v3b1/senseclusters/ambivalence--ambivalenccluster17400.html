ambivalence

noun
the state of mixed feelings or attitudes
Pronunciation: / æmˈbɪvələns / (AmE), / æmˈbɪvələns / (BrE)
The state of having mixed or contradictory feelings or attitudes towards someone or something.

Examples

Strong ambivalence feelings
His ambivalence towards the project made it difficult for the team to move forward.
She felt a deep ambivalence about leaving her hometown for a new job.
The novel explores the ambivalence of love and hate within family relationships.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certainty - the state of being completely sure or confident
  • synonym: uncertainty - the state of being unsure or having doubts
