adenoidal

adjective
relating to adenoids; nasal in speech
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.əˈnɔɪ.dl̩ /; / ˌædəˈnɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌæd.ɪˈnɔɪ.dl̩ /; / ˌædɪˈnɔɪdəl / (BrE)
having a nasal-sounding voice, as if the nose is blocked (often due to enlarged adenoids)

Examples

Adenoidal voice
He underwent surgery to remove his adenoidal tissue last year.
Some singers struggle with an adenoidal tone that affects their vocal clarity.
The child's adenoidal voice made her speech sound unusually nasal.
He spoke in such an adenoidal tone that it sounded like he had a constant cold.
Her adenoidal voice made her easy to recognize on the phone.
The comedian exaggerated an adenoidal accent for comedic effect.
