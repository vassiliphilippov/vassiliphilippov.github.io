storm

verb
attack and take by force
4.4 4.6
Pronunciation: / stɔrm / (AmE), / stɔːm / (BrE)
to attack and capture a place suddenly and with force, especially in a military or protest context

Examples

The castle was heavily fortified, but the army managed to storm it after hours of fighting.
The soldiers prepared to storm the gates at dawn.
Protesters tried to storm the government building during the demonstration.
The soldiers prepared to storm the enemy fortress at dawn.
They stormed the castle gates before the sun had risen.
