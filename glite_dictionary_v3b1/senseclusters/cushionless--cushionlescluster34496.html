cushionless

adjective
without a cushion
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃənləs / (BrE)
having no cushion or padding for comfort or support

Examples

Cushionless chair
After hours on the cushionless chair, his back began to ache.
They replaced the old cushionless sofa with a more comfortable one.
The park bench was cushionless, making it hard to sit for long.

Related Terms

  • antonym: padded - provided with padding or cushions
  • synonym: unpadded - not provided with padding
