sawed-off

adjective
shortened by sawing
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔdˌɑf / (AmE), / ˈsɔːd ɒf / (BrE)
shortened by sawing, especially referring to an object that has had part of its length cut off

Examples

Sawed-off shotgun
A sawed-off piece of pipe was lying on the ground.
He used a sawed-off broomstick to reach the ceiling.
The police confiscated a sawed-off shotgun from the vehicle.
