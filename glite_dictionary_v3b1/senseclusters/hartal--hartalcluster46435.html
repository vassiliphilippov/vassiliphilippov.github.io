hartal

noun
general protest strike
Pronunciation: / hɑːrˈtɑːl / (AmE), / hɑːˈtɑːl / (BrE)
a general strike, especially as a form of protest, commonly used in South Asia

Examples

A hartal protest
Shops and offices remained closed throughout the hartal organized by the trade unions.
The government urged citizens to stay calm during the nationwide hartal.
Many commuters were stranded because of the hartal that brought public transport to a halt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lockout - a situation where employers prevent workers from entering the workplace
  • synonym: strike - a period when workers stop working to protest or demand something
