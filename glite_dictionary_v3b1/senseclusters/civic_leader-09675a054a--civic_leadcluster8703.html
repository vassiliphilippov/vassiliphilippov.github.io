civic leader

noun
prominent person in city or town affairs
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪk ˈlidər / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪk ˈliːdə / (BrE)
A person who plays a prominent role in the public or municipal affairs of a city or town.

Examples

A civic leader speaks
As a civic leader, she advocated for better public transportation in the community.
Many civic leaders attended the meeting to discuss new policies for urban development.
The civic leader was instrumental in organizing the city's annual festival.
