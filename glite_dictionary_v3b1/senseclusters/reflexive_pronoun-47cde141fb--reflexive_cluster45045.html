reflexive pronoun

noun
pronoun referring back to the subject
7.6 2.6
Pronunciation: /rə.ˈflek.sɪv ˈproʊ.naʊn/ /; / /rɪ.ˈflek.sɪv ˈproʊ.naʊn/ (AmE), /rə.ˈflek.sɪv ˈprəʊ.naʊn/ /; / /rɪ.ˈflek.sɪv ˈprəʊ.naʊn/ (BrE)
a pronoun like 'myself', 'yourself', or 'themselves' that refers back to the subject of the same clause or sentence

Examples

The reflexive pronoun myself
Please help yourself to some coffee if you like.
She blamed herself for the mistake she made.
The cat cleaned itself after eating.
In the sentence 'She taught herself French,' the word 'herself' is a reflexive pronoun.
Please check your work yourself before submitting it to the teacher.
They blamed themselves for the accident.
