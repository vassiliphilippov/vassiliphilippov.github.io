nonachievement

noun
failure to achieve a goal
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈtʃiv.mənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈtʃiːv.mənt / (BrE)
The act or state of failing to achieve a desired or intended goal.

Examples

The nonachievement of goals
Critics pointed out the nonachievement of the promised reforms.
Despite several attempts, his efforts resulted in nonachievement rather than success.
The lack of improved test scores was seen as a nonachievement by the school board.

Related Terms

  • antonym: achievement - something accomplished successfully
  • synonym: failure - lack of success in achieving a goal
