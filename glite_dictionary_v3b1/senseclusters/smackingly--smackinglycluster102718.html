smackingly

adverb
with a sharp smack
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsmækɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsmækɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or resembles a sharp smack or slap, often producing a loud sound

Examples

Smackingly slap the table
He smackingly slapped the table to get everyone's attention at the meeting.
The child was smackingly kissed on the cheek by her grandmother.
She closed the book smackingly, startling everyone in the quiet room.
