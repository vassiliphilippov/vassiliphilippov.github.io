Illyrian

adjective
of ancient Illyria
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪriən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the ancient region of Illyria or its people, located in the western Balkans

Examples

Illyrian artifacts
The museum displayed several Illyrian artifacts dating back to the Bronze Age.
She wrote her thesis on Illyrian tribes and their influence on Balkan history.
Archaeologists discovered an Illyrian settlement near the Adriatic coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancient - belonging to the distant past
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
