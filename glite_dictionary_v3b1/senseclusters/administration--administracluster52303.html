administration

noun
enforcement or application of law
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of carrying out or applying justice, laws, or rules, especially by a legal authority

Examples

Administration of justice
Delays in the administration of the court’s decisions can undermine public trust.
She worked on improving the administration of civil laws in the region.
The administration of justice must be fair and impartial to all citizens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enforcement - the act of compelling observance of or compliance with a law, rule, or obligation
