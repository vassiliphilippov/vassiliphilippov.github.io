misrecognize

verb
to identify incorrectly
8.1
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈrek.əg.naɪz/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈrek.əg.naɪz/ (BrE)
to identify someone or something incorrectly or mistake them for someone or something else

Examples

To misrecognize a person
He often misrecognizes people he has only met once before.
Because of the fog, she misrecognized the building and walked past her destination.
Children sometimes misrecognize animals in the dark and become frightened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mistake - to identify incorrectly or confuse with something else
  • antonym: recognize - to identify someone or something correctly
