hob

noun
cooking surface
5.1 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / hÉ‘b / (AmE), / hÉ’b / (BrE)
a flat cooking surface on a stove or cooker, used for heating pots and pans

Examples

Cooking hob
She placed the kettle on the hob to boil water.
The soup simmered gently on the hob while he prepared the bread.
Please turn off the hob after you finish cooking.

Synonyms

  • cooktop
    3.6 0.55/m
    a cooktop is a flat surface with built-in burners in a counter, while a hob is the flat cooking surface on a stove.
  • stovetop
    3.5 0.00/m
    a stovetop is the area used for cooking on top of a stove, while a hob is the specific flat surface for heating pans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for baking or roasting food
  • synonym: stovetop - the top surface of a stove used for cooking
