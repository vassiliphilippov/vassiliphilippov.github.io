unrejuvenated

adjective
not restored
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈdʒuːvəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈdʒuːvəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not restored to a former, better, or fresher condition; still worn, aged, or unchanged after a process meant to improve it

Examples

Unrejuvenated old building
The building remained unrejuvenated despite the city's plans for renovation.
After years of neglect, the park looked old and unrejuvenated.
The unrejuvenated equipment caused frequent delays in the factory's production line.
