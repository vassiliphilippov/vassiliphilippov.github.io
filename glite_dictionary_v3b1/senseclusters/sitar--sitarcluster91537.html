sitar

noun
Indian long-necked string instrument
6.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈtɑr / (AmE), / sɪˈtɑː / (BrE)
A large, long-necked stringed instrument from India with curved movable frets, typically having six or seven main strings for playing and several resonating strings.

Examples

To play the sitar
He learned to play the sitar from a renowned Indian musician.
She bought a handmade sitar during her trip to Delhi.
The soulful sound of the sitar filled the concert hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guitar - a Western fretted string instrument
  • synonym: veena - an ancient Indian plucked string instrument
