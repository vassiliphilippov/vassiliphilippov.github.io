therapeutic abortion

noun
legally induced abortion for medical reasons
4.3 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk əˈbɔrʃən / (AmE), / ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk əˈbɔːʃən / (BrE)
an abortion that is induced legally for medical reasons, such as when the mother's life or health is at risk
