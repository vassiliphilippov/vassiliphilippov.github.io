fideistic

adjective
faith over reason
9.9
Pronunciation: /faɪ.dɪ.ˈɪs.tɪk/ /; / /faɪ.diː.ˈɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /faɪ.dɪ.ˈɪs.tɪk/ /; / /faɪ.diː.ˈɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to the belief that faith is independent of or more important than reason

Examples

Fideistic belief
Her fideistic perspective led her to trust religious teachings over scientific explanations.
The debate contrasted fideistic arguments with those grounded in logic and evidence.
Some theologians adopt a fideistic stance, emphasizing faith as the only path to truth.
