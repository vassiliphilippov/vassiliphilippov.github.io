Cottonian

adjective
of John Cotton
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / kɒˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to John Cotton, the Puritan minister, or to his religious teachings and influence

Examples

Cottonian beliefs
The church displayed several Cottonian manuscripts from the early colonial period.
Her research focused on the Cottonian influence in New England's religious development.
Many Cottonian ideas shaped the beliefs of the first settlers in Massachusetts.
