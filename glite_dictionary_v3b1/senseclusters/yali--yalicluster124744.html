yali

noun
Bosphorus mansion
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈli / (AmE), / jɑːˈli / (BrE)
a traditional wooden mansion built along the Bosphorus in Istanbul, often used as a summer residence

Examples

A Bosphorus yali
The wealthy family owned a beautiful yali overlooking the water.
Many tourists admire the historic yali houses lining the Bosphorus during boat tours.
He dreamed of living in a restored yali with views of the Istanbul skyline.
