unshod

adjective
barefoot; not wearing shoes (esp. religious)
8.6 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÊŒnËˆÊƒÉ‘d / (AmE), / ÊŒnËˆÊƒÉ’d / (BrE)
not wearing shoes (for people) or not having horseshoes (for animals); barefoot

Examples

Unshod feet
Members of the order took a vow to remain unshod as a sign of humility and poverty.
She noticed the children's unshod feet, covered in dust from playing in the field.
