stylopodium

noun
enlargement at base of style in some plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / staɪləˈpoʊdiəm / (AmE), / staɪləˈpəʊdiəm / (BrE)
An enlargement at the base of the style in some plants, especially in the family Umbelliferae (Apiaceae).
