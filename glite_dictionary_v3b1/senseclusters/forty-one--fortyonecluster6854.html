forty-one

adjective
the number 41; one more than forty
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrtiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈwʌn / (BrE)
being one more than forty; the cardinal number following forty and preceding forty-two.

Examples

Forty-one students
Out of forty-one applicants, three were selected for further interviews.
She received forty-one votes, just enough to win the election.
This is the forty-onest edition of the festival, making it one of the oldest in the country.
