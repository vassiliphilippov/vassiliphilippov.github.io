daishiki

noun
loose, colorful African shirt
Pronunciation: / dɑːˈʃiːki / (AmE), / dæˈʃiːki / (BrE)
a loose, brightly colored garment, often worn by men, originating from West Africa and featuring decorative patterns

Examples

A daishiki garment
He wore a bright daishiki to the cultural festival.
Many performers on stage were dressed in traditional daishikis.
The daishiki has become a symbol of African heritage and pride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dashiki - alternative spelling referring to the same African garment
