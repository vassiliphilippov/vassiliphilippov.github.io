lysimeter

noun
soil water analyzer
Pronunciation: / laɪˈsɪmətər / (AmE), / laɪˈsɪmɪtə / (BrE)
a scientific device used to measure how much water moves through soil, including water draining, leaching, or lost by evaporation and transpiration
