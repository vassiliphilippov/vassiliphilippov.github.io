sheer

adjective
very steep; almost vertical
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
(of a cliff, drop, or surface) very steep; almost vertical.

Examples

Sheer cliff face
From the top, she looked down the sheer face of the mountain and felt a rush of excitement.
The castle stood on a sheer drop above the roaring sea.
The hikers were faced with a sheer cliff that was impossible to climb without proper equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steep - rising or falling sharply; almost perpendicular
  • degree higher: vertical - standing or pointing straight up; at a right angle to a horizontal surface
