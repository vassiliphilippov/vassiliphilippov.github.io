cytogeneticist

noun
specialist in chromosomes and heredity
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪtə(ʊ)dʒɪˈnɛtɪsɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of chromosomes and cell structures related to heredity

Examples

A cytogeneticist studies chromosomes
As a cytogeneticist, she often collaborated with oncologists to study cancer cells.
