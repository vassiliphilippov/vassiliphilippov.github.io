wirelessly

adverb
without wires
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərləsli /; / ˈwaɪɚləsli / (AmE), / ˈwaɪələsli / (BrE)
in a way that does not use wires or cables, usually by using radio signals or other contactless technology

Examples

To connect wirelessly
You can print documents wirelessly from your laptop to the office printer.
The headphones connect wirelessly to your phone using Bluetooth technology.
Many modern devices can now be charged wirelessly for added convenience.
You can connect your headphones wirelessly to your phone using Bluetooth.
The security cameras transmit video wirelessly to the control room.
Many modern devices can share files wirelessly without needing any cables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remotely - from a distance, without direct physical connection
  • antonym: wired - using physical wires or cables
