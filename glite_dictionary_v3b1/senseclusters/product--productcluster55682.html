product

noun
result of multiplication
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.dʌkt / (AmE), / ˈprɒd.ʌkt / (BrE)
the number or quantity resulting from multiplying two or more numbers or amounts together

Examples

The product of numbers
In algebra, you often need to find the product of variables.
The product of 4 and 5 is 20.
The teacher asked them to write down the product for each pair of numbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: factor - a number that is multiplied to obtain a product
  • antonym: sum - the result of addition
