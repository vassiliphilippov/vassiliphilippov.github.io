furless

adjective
without fur
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrləs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːləs / (BrE)
having no fur or hair on the body

Examples

Furless cat
The Sphynx is a breed of furless cat that requires special care.
After the treatment, the once fluffy dog became almost completely furless.
Some animals are naturally furless and need protection from cold weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: furry - covered with fur
  • synonym: hairless - having no hair on the body
