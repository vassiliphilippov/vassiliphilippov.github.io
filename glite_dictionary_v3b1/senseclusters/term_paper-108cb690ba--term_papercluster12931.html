term paper

noun
student's written assignment for a course
Pronunciation: / tɝm ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / tɜːm ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
A long essay or research paper written by a student on a subject covered in a course, typically due at the end of an academic term, to demonstrate their understanding and progress.

Examples

A term paper assignment
Each student must submit a term paper by the end of the semester.
I spent the entire weekend working on my term paper for history class.
The professor returned our graded term papers with detailed feedback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: research paper - a detailed written report based on original research
