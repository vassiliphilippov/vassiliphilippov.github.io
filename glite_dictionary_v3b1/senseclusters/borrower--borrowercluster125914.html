borrower

noun
someone who borrows something
2.3 9/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêr.o ä.…ö / (AmE), / Ààb…ír.…ô ä.…ô / (BrE)
a person who receives something, especially money or property, with the intention of returning it or its equivalent

Examples

A borrower of money

She was approved as a borrower for the home loan after a lengthy application process.
The library allows each borrower to check out up to ten books at a time.
