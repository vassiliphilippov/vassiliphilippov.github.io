borrower

noun
someone who borrows something
2.3 6.45/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêr.o ä.…ö / (AmE), / Ààb…ír.…ô ä.…ô / (BrE)
a person who receives something, especially money or property, with the intention of returning it or its equivalent

Examples

A borrower of money
As a responsible borrower, he always returned the money he borrowed on time.
She was approved as a borrower for the home loan after a lengthy application process.
The library allows each borrower to check out up to ten books at a time.

Synonyms

  • debtor
    4.1 4.93/m
    a debtor owes money to another, while a borrower receives something and intends to give it back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debtor - a person who owes a sum of money
  • antonym: lender - a person or organization that lends something, especially money
