sparing

adjective
avoiding waste; economical
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspeərɪŋ / (BrE)
careful in the use or consumption of resources; avoiding waste or extravagance

Examples

Be sparing with salt
Be sparing with the salt when cooking this dish for health reasons.
Due to limited resources, the team was sparing in their use of equipment.
She led a sparing lifestyle, avoiding unnecessary expenses whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economical - careful not to waste resources or money
  • antonym: extravagant - spending or using more than necessary
  • synonym: frugal - careful with money or resources; avoiding waste
