feeling dizzy

adjective
sensation of spinning or unsteadiness

experiencing a sensation in which you feel as if you or your surroundings are spinning or moving and you may be about to lose your balance.

Examples

To feel dizzy suddenly
After standing up too quickly, I suddenly started feeling dizzy and had to sit down.
He kept feeling dizzy throughout the day, so he decided to visit his doctor.
She told the nurse she was feeling dizzy after taking her medication.

Synonyms

  • dizziness
    2.9 5.99/m
    dizziness feels like unsteadiness or lightheaded spinning, while feeling dizzy describes the same spinning sensation and loss of balance.
  • vertigo
    7.8 2.02/m
    vertigo is a strong spinning sensation that makes you feel the world move, while feeling dizzy can be milder or mixed.
  • wooziness
    5.4 0.04/m
    wooziness is a slightly confused, unsteady or lightheaded feeling, while feeling dizzy highlights a clear spinning or balance loss.
  • headedness
    7.8 0.26/m
    headedness is a less common term for feeling lightheaded or faint, while feeling dizzy focuses on spinning and losing balance.
  • giddiness
    4.6 0.16/m
    giddiness is lightheadedness often mixed with excitement or happiness, while feeling dizzy usually means a physical spinning or unsteady sensation.
