spendthrift

adjective
recklessly wasteful with money
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛndˌθrɪft / (AmE), / ˈspɛndˌθrɪft / (BrE)
spending money in a reckless or wasteful manner; given to extravagant expenditures

Examples

Spendthrift habits
He regretted his spendthrift ways when he found himself deep in debt.
Her spendthrift habits quickly drained her savings account.
The spendthrift lifestyle of the celebrity attracted both admiration and criticism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frugal - being careful about spending money
  • synonym: prodigal - wastefully extravagant
