uncrossable

adjective
impossible to cross
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɔsəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɒsəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to cross or pass over

Examples

An uncrossable bridge
After the storm, the bridge was declared uncrossable due to severe damage.
The mountain pass became uncrossable in winter because of heavy snow.
The swollen river was completely uncrossable for several days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crossable - able to be crossed or passed over
  • synonym: impassable - impossible to travel through or across
