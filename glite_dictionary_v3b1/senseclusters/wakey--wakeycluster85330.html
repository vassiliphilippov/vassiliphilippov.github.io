wakey

interjection
wake-up exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪki / (AmE), / ˈweɪki / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to tell someone to wake up or become alert

Examples

Wakey, time for school
She called out 'wakey, it's time for breakfast!'
He tapped on the door and said 'wakey, you have to get ready for school.'
Every morning, her mother would cheerfully shout 'wakey!' to start the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rise and shine - an exclamation used to tell someone to get out of bed and start the day
  • synonym: wake up - to stop sleeping and become awake
