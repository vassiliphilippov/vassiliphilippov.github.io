French toast

noun
bread fried in egg and milk
3.3 1.3
Pronunciation: /frentʃ toʊst/ (AmE), /frentʃ təʊst/ (BrE)
A dish made from slices of bread soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk, then fried and typically served with sugar, fruit, or syrup.

Examples

French toast with syrup
He learned how to make French toast in his cooking class.
She likes her French toast topped with maple syrup and powdered sugar.
We had French toast with strawberries for breakfast this morning.
