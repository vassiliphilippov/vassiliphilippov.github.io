hockshop

noun
pawn shop
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈhɒkˌʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop where people can pawn or sell items for cash, typically at a low price

Examples

A hockshop loan
He took his watch to the hockshop when he needed some extra cash.
The old hockshop on Main Street is filled with all kinds of used goods.
She found a vintage camera at the local hockshop for a bargain price.

Related Terms
