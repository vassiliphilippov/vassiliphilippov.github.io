hypopharyngeal

adjective
of lower pharynx
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəfəˈrɪndʒɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the lower part of the pharynx, the area of the throat below the oropharynx

Examples

Hypopharyngeal area
The surgeon found a tumor in the hypopharyngeal area during the examination.
Difficulty swallowing can be a symptom of hypopharyngeal cancer.
A detailed scan revealed inflammation in the patient's hypopharyngeal tissues.
