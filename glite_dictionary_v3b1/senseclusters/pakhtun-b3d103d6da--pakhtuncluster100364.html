Pakhtun

proper noun
Pashto-speaking ethnic group
Pronunciation: / pʌkˈtuːn / (AmE), / pʌkˈtuːn / (BrE)
a member of a Pashto-speaking ethnic group native to Afghanistan and Pakistan

Examples

A Pakhtun person
The Pakhtun have a long tradition of hospitality and honor.
Many Pakhtun communities live along the border between Afghanistan and Pakistan.
He is proud of his Pakhtun heritage and speaks Pashto fluently.
