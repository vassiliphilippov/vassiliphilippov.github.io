three-party

adjective
involving three parties
Pronunciation: / ˈθriː ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈθriː ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
involving three separate parties or participants

Examples

A three-party negotiation
A three-party negotiation can often take longer to resolve than one between two sides.
The agreement was a three-party contract between the company, the supplier, and the distributor.
The merger required approval from all entities in the three-party alliance.
