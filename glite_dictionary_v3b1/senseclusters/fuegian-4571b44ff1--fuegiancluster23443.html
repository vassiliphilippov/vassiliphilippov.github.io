Fuegian

proper noun
person from Tierra del Fuego
Pronunciation: / fjuˈiːdʒiən / (AmE), / fjʊˈiːdʒiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Tierra del Fuego, an archipelago at the southern tip of South America

Examples

Fuegian people
The Fuegian people have a rich cultural heritage shaped by their harsh environment.
Researchers documented the languages spoken by different Fuegian groups.
A Fuegian guide led the expedition through the remote islands.
