waterlogging

noun
soil saturated with water
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑː.tər.lɑː.gɪŋ/ /; / /ˈwɔː.tər.lɑː.gɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə.lɒ.gɪŋ/ (BrE)
a condition where soil or land becomes saturated with water, making it unsuitable for normal use or plant growth

Examples

Soil waterlogging
