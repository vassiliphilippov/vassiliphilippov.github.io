excite

verb
to stimulate or arouse activity or interest
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsaɪt / (AmE), / ɪkˈsaɪt / (BrE)
to increase the activity, energy, or responsiveness of something; to stimulate or arouse interest or enthusiasm

Examples

To excite interest
Caffeine can excite the nervous system and make you feel more alert.
The bright colors in the painting excite the viewer's imagination.
The teacher used interesting activities to excite the students' curiosity.
