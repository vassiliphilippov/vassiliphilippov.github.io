Zumba

verb
to do Zumba exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈzʊmbə / (AmE), / ˈzʊmbə / (BrE)
to take part in or teach a fitness class that combines dance and aerobic movements to Latin music, known as Zumba

Examples

To Zumba at the gym
Every Tuesday evening, a group of friends Zumba together at the local gym.
She loves to Zumba because it makes exercising feel like a dance party.
After work, he often Zumbas to relieve stress and stay active.
