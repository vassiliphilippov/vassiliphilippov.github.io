roadable

adjective
suitable for roads
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be driven or used on public roads

Examples

A roadable aircraft
The company unveiled a roadable aircraft that can be driven on highways and flown in the sky.
Engineers are working to make the prototype fully roadable for everyday use.
A roadable vehicle must meet strict safety standards before it can be sold to the public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off-road - designed for use away from public roads
  • synonym: street-legal - meeting legal requirements to be used on public roads
