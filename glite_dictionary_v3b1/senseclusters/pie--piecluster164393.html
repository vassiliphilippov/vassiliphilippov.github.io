pie

noun
baked dish with pastry and filling
Pronunciation: / paɪ / (AmE), / paɪ / (BrE)
a dish consisting of pastry filled with sweet or savory ingredients, baked in a pastry-lined pan, often with a pastry top

Examples

Apple pie
She served a homemade apple pie for dessert at the family dinner.
The bakery on Main Street is famous for its chicken pie.
Would you like a slice of blueberry pie with your coffee?

Related Terms

  • synonym: tart - an open pastry case containing a filling
