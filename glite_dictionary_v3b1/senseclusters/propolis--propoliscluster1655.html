propolis

noun
bee-made resin
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊpəlɪs / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəlɪs / (BrE)
a sticky substance made by bees from tree resin, used to seal and protect their hives

Examples

Propolis from bees
Bees collect tree resin to make propolis, which helps keep their hive safe from bacteria.
Some health products contain propolis because of its natural antibacterial properties.
The beekeeper carefully scraped propolis from the edges of the hive frames.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bee glue - another name for propolis, a resinous substance made by bees
  • synonym: wax - a natural substance produced by bees to build honeycombs
