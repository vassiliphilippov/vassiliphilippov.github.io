unswallowable

adjective
impossible to accept
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswɑːloʊəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈswɒləbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to accept, believe, or tolerate; unacceptable, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Unswallowable truth
The new policy was so strict that many employees found it unswallowable.
His excuse was simply unswallowable to anyone who knew the facts.
For some, the idea of moving abroad was unswallowable despite the opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptable - able to be agreed to or tolerated
  • synonym: intolerable - impossible to endure or accept
  • synonym: unacceptable - not satisfactory or allowable
