enteroviral

adjective
caused by enteroviruses
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəroʊˈvaɪrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛntərəʊˈvaɪrəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by enteroviruses, a group of viruses that primarily affect the intestines
