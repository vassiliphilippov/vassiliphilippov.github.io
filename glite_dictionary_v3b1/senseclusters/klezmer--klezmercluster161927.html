klezmer

noun
Jewish folk musician
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛzmər / (AmE), / ˈklɛzmə / (BrE)
a musician who plays traditional Jewish klezmer music, especially as an instrumentalist

Examples

A skilled klezmer
The talented klezmer played lively tunes at the wedding celebration.
Many people gathered to listen to the famous klezmer perform traditional melodies.
As a skilled klezmer, he traveled across Europe sharing his music.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cantor - a person who leads singing in Jewish religious services
  • synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented
