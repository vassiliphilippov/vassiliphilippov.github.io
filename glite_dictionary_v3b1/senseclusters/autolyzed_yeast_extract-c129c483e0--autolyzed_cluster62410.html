autolyzed yeast extract

noun
flavor enhancer made from yeast
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtəˌlaɪzd jist ɪkˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəˌlaɪzd jiːst ɪkˌstrakt / (BrE)
a flavor enhancer made by breaking down yeast cells with their own enzymes, used in processed foods to add savory taste
