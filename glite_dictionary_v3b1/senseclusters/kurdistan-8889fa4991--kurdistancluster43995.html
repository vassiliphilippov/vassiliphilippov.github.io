Kurdistan

proper noun
region of the Middle East inhabited by Kurds
Pronunciation: / ˌkɜːrdɪˈstɑːn / (AmE), / ˌkɜːdɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
a large region in the Middle East, traditionally inhabited by Kurds, spanning parts of Turkey, Iraq, Iran, and Syria

Examples

The region of Kurdistan
Many Kurds dream of an independent Kurdistan where they have their own government and rights.
The mountainous terrain of Kurdistan has shaped the culture and history of its people.
Tourists are increasingly drawn to the scenic landscapes of Kurdistan despite regional challenges.
