fideles

noun
religious congregation members
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪˈdeɪleɪz / (AmE), / fɪˈdeɪleɪz / (BrE)

Examples

The fideles gathered

During the ceremony, the priest offered blessings to all the fideles present.
The fideles sang hymns together as part of the religious service.
