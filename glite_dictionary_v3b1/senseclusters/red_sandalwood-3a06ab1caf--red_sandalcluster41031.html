red sandalwood

noun
wood from the Pterocarpus santalinus tree
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd ˈsændl̩ˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈsændl̩ˌwʊd / (BrE)
the hard, durable wood of the red sandalwood tree (Pterocarpus santalinus), valued for furniture and cabinetwork

Examples

Red sandalwood furniture
Artisans in India craft traditional jewellery boxes using red sandalwood.
Because of its durability, red sandalwood is often chosen for high-quality cabinetwork.
The intricate table was made from red sandalwood, renowned for its rich hue and strength.
