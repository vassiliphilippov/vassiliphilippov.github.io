factor of proportionality

noun
constant in proportional relationship
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ əv prəˌpɔr.ʃəˈnæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tər əv prəˌpɔː.ʃəˈnæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a constant value by which one quantity is multiplied to obtain a proportional quantity; commonly called the constant k in equations like y = kx
