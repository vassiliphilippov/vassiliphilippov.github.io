Henry Cavendish

proper noun
British chemist and physicist (1731–1810)
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnri ˈkævəndɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɛnri ˈkævəndɪʃ / (BrE)
(1731–1810) British chemist and physicist known for discovering that water is a compound of hydrogen and oxygen and for calculating the density of the Earth.
