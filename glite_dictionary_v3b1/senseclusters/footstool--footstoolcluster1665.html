footstool

noun
a low stool for resting one's feet
2.7 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf ätÀåstuÀêl / (AmE), / Ààf ät.stuÀêl / (BrE)
A low stool or support for resting the feet of a person while seated.

Examples

A footstool for resting
He bought a small footstool to go with his office chair for extra comfort.
She placed her tired feet on the footstool after a long day at work.
The old leather footstool matched the armchair perfectly in the living room.

Synonyms

  • footrest
    2.2 0.44/m
    a footrest is a simple, often built-in support for sitting people's feet, while a footstool is a separate low stool for resting feet.
  • ottoman
    4.7 0.75/m
    an ottoman is a padded, often upholstered piece used as a seat or storage, while a footstool is a low stool mainly for resting feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ottoman - a padded seat or footstool without a back or arms
