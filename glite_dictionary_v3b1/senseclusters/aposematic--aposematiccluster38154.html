aposematic

adjective
warning coloration
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˌsəˈmætɪk /; / ˌæpoʊsəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəʊsɪˈmatɪk / (BrE)
having bright colors or markings that warn predators an animal is dangerous or not good to eat
