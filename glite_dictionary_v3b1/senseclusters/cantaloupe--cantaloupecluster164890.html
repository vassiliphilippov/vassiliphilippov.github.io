cantaloupe

noun
pale orange colour (named after the melon)
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.tə.loʊp / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tə.luːp / (BrE)
A pale orange color resembling the flesh of a cantaloupe melon, used as a named shade in fashion, decoration, and product descriptions.
