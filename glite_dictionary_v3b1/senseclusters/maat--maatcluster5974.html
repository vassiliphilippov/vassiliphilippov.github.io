maat

noun
Egyptian truth and order
Pronunciation: / mɑːt / (AmE), / mɑːt / (BrE)
the ancient Egyptian concept of truth, balance, order, and justice, often personified as a goddess

Examples

Ancient maat goddess
The pharaoh was expected to uphold maat throughout his reign.
Ancient Egyptian laws were based on the principles of maat.
Priests performed rituals to maintain maat in society and the universe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaos - complete disorder and confusion
  • synonym: justice - the quality of being fair and reasonable
