Clintonian

adjective
of Bill Clinton
Pronunciation: / klɪnˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / klɪnˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the policies, style, or characteristics of Bill Clinton, former U.S. president

Examples

Clintonian welfare reform
The senator's Clintonian approach to welfare reform focused on balancing economic growth with social support.
Many analysts described the administration's foreign policy as distinctly Clintonian in its emphasis on diplomacy.
Her Clintonian optimism appealed to voters who remembered the economic boom of the 1990s.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Reaganesque - resembling the style or policies of Ronald Reagan
  • synonym: presidential - relating to a president or presidency
