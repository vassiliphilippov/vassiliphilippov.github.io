acentric

adjective
lacking a centromere (genetics)
Pronunciation: / eɪˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
(of a chromosome or chromosome fragment) lacking a centromere.

Examples

Acentric chromosome fragment
An acentric chromosome cannot attach to the spindle and is often lost during mitosis.
