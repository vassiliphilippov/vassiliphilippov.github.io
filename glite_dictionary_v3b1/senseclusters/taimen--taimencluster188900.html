taimen

noun
large Asian river fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmən / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmən / (BrE)
a large predatory fish native to rivers of northern Asia, especially Siberia and Mongolia

Examples

A large taimen fish
The fisherman traveled to Siberia hoping to catch a giant taimen in the wild river.
A taimen can grow to impressive sizes, making it a prized catch among anglers.
Researchers are studying the habitat of the taimen to help protect this rare species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salmon - a large fish that migrates from the sea to rivers to spawn
  • synonym: trout - a freshwater fish related to salmon, found in rivers and lakes
