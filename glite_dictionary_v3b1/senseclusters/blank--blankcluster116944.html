blank

noun
unfinished prepared material piece
Pronunciation: / blæŋk / (AmE), / blaŋk / (BrE)
a piece of material that has been prepared but not yet finished, ready to be made into something else

Examples

Wooden blank
He started the project with a wooden blank and shaped it into a bowl.
The factory cut each metal blank before stamping it into a coin.
The knife-maker selected a steel blank to forge into a blade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workpiece - an item being worked on and shaped in manufacturing
