cow college

noun
less prestigious or agricultural college (derogatory)
9.1
Pronunciation: / kaʊ ˈkɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / kaʊ ˈkɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
a college, especially one specializing in agricultural studies, regarded as less prestigious or of lower academic standing (often used derogatorily or humorously)

Examples

A cow college campus
Although it started as a cow college, the university has since expanded its programs and reputation.
He was defensive when classmates mocked his degree from a so-called cow college.
Some people still unfairly refer to the state university as a cow college.
