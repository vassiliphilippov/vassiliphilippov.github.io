psychophysicist

noun
psychologist specializing in psychophysics
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊfəˈzɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊfɪˈzɪsɪst / (BrE)
a psychologist who specializes in psychophysics, studying the relationship between physical stimuli and mental processes

Examples

A psychophysicist studies perception
As a psychophysicist, she designed experiments to measure how people perceive differences in light intensity.
Many psychophysicists work in universities, exploring how physical stimuli affect mental processes.
The conference featured a keynote speaker who is a renowned psychophysicist in the field of sensory research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychologist - a professional who studies mental processes and behavior
