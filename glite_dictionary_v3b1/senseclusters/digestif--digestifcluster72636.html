digestif

noun
post-meal digestive aid
7.6
Pronunciation: / diˌʒɛsˈtif / (AmE), / diˌʒɛsˈtiːf / (BrE)
an alcoholic drink served after a meal to help with digestion

Examples

A digestif drink
After dinner, he enjoyed a small glass of digestif to settle his stomach.
The restaurant offered a herbal digestif to help guests with digestion after their meal.
Some people prefer a nonalcoholic digestif like peppermint tea after eating.
After the meal, the waiter recommended a digestif to complete the dining experience.
He enjoyed a small glass of digestif while chatting with friends at the restaurant.
Some people believe that a digestif helps settle the stomach after a large dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aperitif - an alcoholic drink taken before a meal to stimulate the appetite
  • antonym: apéritif - an alcoholic drink served before a meal to stimulate the appetite
  • synonym: liqueur - a sweet, flavored alcoholic drink often served after meals
