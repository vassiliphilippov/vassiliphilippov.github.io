intratracheal

adjective
within the trachea
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈtreɪkiəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈtreɪkɪəl / (BrE)
administered or occurring within the trachea

Examples

An intratracheal tube
The medication was given through an intratracheal tube during the emergency procedure.
Veterinarians sometimes use intratracheal administration for certain drugs in animals.
An intratracheal injection can be life-saving in cases of severe allergic reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endotracheal - placed or occurring inside the trachea
  • antonym: intravenous - administered within a vein
