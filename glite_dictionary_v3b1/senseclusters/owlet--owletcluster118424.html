owlet

noun
young owl
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊl.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈaʊl.ɪt / (BrE)
A young or juvenile owl.

Examples

A owlet chick
A mother owl fiercely protects her tiny owlet from predators.
The rescued owlet was carefully released back into the forest.
We spotted an owlet peeking out from its nest at dusk.
