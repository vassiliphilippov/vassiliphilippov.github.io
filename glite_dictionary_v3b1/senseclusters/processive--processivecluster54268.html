processive

adjective
involving a process
8.4
Pronunciation: / prəˈsɛsɪv / (AmE), / prəˈsɛsɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving a process or a sequence of actions, especially in a step-by-step manner.

Examples

Processive approach

A processive method can help break down complex tasks into manageable steps.
Her processive thinking allowed her to solve problems by following a clear sequence of actions.
