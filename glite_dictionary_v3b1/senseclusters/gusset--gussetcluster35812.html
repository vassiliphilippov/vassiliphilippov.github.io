gusset

noun
chain mail covering unprotected armor areas
7.8 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌs.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈgʌs.ɪt/ (BrE)
a piece of material sewn into clothing, bags, or armor to strengthen, enlarge, or protect a part

Examples
