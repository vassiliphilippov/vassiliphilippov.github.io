coziness

noun
state of being warm and comfortable
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊzi.nəs /; / ˈkoʊzinəs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi.nəs /; / ˈkəʊzinəs / (BrE)
A feeling or quality of being warm, comfortable, and protected, often in a small or familiar space.

Examples

The coziness of home
She enjoyed the coziness of a blanket and a good book on a rainy day.
The coziness of the little cabin made us feel instantly at home.
Their living room radiated coziness with its soft lighting and plush sofas.
I love the cosiness of a small, candle-lit room on a winter night.
She added blankets and soft pillows to increase the cosiness of the living room.
The cosiness of the café made it a perfect place to relax with friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coldness - a state or feeling of being cold or lacking warmth
  • synonym: comfort - a state of physical ease and freedom from pain or constraint
  • antonym: restlessness - the inability to rest or relax; lack of calm or peace
  • synonym: snugness - the quality of being comfortably warm and protected
  • synonym: warmth - the quality, state, or sensation of being warm; moderate and comfortable heat
