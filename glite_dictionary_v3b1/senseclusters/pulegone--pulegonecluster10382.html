pulegone

noun
natural insect repellent
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːlɪˌɡoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpjuːlɪɡəʊn / (BrE)
a natural compound found in mint-family plants and essential oils, used in insect repellents, flavoring, perfumes, and can be toxic to humans and animals
