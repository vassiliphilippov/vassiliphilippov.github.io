baraza

noun
East African council
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɑːzə / (AmE), / bəˈrɑːzə / (BrE)
a public meeting place or council, especially used for community discussions in East Africa

Examples

Village baraza meeting
The elders met at the baraza to resolve disputes within the village.
Community members gathered at the baraza to discuss plans for the new school.
During the baraza, everyone had a chance to share their opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembly - a group of people gathered together for a common purpose
  • synonym: council - a group of people who meet to make decisions or give advice
