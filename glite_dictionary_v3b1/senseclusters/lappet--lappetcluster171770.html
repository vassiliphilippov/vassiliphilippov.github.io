lappet

noun
a type of hairy moth
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlæp.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈlap.ɪt / (BrE)
A medium-sized moth with hairy larvae, known as lappet caterpillars, belonging to the family Lasiocampidae.

Examples

Lappet moth
Scientists collected several lappet specimens for their study of moth diversity.
The lappet is most active at dusk during the summer months.
The hairy caterpillar of a lappet can be found feeding on leafy shrubs.
