Sagittarius

noun
the ninth sign of the zodiac
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsædʒɪˈtɛriəs / (AmE), / ˌsædʒɪˈtɪəriəs / (BrE)
the ninth astrological sign in the zodiac, covering those born approximately between November 22 and December 21

Examples

Sagittarius zodiac sign
If you were born in early December, your star sign might be Sagittarius.
Many people believe those born under Sagittarius are adventurous and optimistic.
The sun moves into Sagittarius in late November each year.
