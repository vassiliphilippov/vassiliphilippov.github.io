mechanics

noun
technical details or processes involved in something
6.0
Pronunciation: / məˈkænɪks / (AmE), / mɪˈkænɪks / (BrE)
the technical or practical details and procedures involved in doing or understanding how something works, especially in a particular field or activity

Examples

Mechanics of writing
Before you can play the piece well, you need to master the mechanics of the piano technique.
Many students struggle with the mechanics of solving complex math problems.
The author explains the mechanics of creating believable dialogue in fiction writing.

  • synonym: technique - a particular way of doing something, especially one involving skill or expertise
