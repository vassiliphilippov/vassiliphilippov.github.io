Frelimo

proper noun
Mozambican political movement
Pronunciation: / freˈliːmoʊ / (AmE), / freˈliːməʊ / (BrE)
the Mozambique Liberation Front (Frelimo), a political party and former nationalist movement in Mozambique

Examples
