circumnavigator

noun
one who travels around
8.6
Pronunciation: /sɜːr.kəm.ˈnæv.ɪ.ɡeɪ.tər/ /; / /sɜːr.kəm.ˈnæv.ə.ɡeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /sɜː.kəm.ˈnæv.ɪ.ɡeɪ.tə/ /; / /sɜː.kəm.ˈnæv.ə.ɡeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person or vehicle that travels all the way around the world, an island, or another large area, especially by sea or air
