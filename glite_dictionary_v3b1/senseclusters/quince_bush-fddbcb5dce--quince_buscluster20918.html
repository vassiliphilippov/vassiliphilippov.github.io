quince bush

noun
small Asian tree or shrub with pinkish flowers and edible fruit
Pronunciation: / kwɪns bʊʃ / (AmE), / kwɪns bʊʃ / (BrE)
A small Asian tree or shrub with pinkish flowers and pear-shaped fruit, often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Examples

The quince bush flowers
Many gardeners appreciate the quince bush for its resilience and attractive flowers.
She planted a quince bush beside the fence to enjoy its spring blossoms.
The quince bush in our garden produced a lot of fragrant fruit this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quince - the fruit or tree Cydonia oblonga, related to apples and pears
