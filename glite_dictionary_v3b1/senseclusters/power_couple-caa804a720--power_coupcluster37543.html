power couple

noun
influential or successful couple
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊɚ ˌkʌpəl / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌkʌpəl / (BrE)
A couple, often in a romantic relationship, in which both people are highly successful or influential, especially in their careers or public life.

Examples

A power couple in business
As a power couple, their influence extended far beyond business into politics and social causes.
Many magazines consider Beyoncé and Jay-Z to be the ultimate power couple of the music industry.
The city's newest power couple attended the charity gala together, drawing a lot of media attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrity couple - a couple both of whom are famous
