civil time

noun
official standard time used in daily life
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl taɪm / (BrE)
the officially adopted time for a particular region, used for all civil purposes and often established by law or custom
