marsh horsetail

noun
species of horsetail in wet areas
Pronunciation: / mɑrʃ ˈhɔrˌstɛɪl / (AmE), / mɑːʃ ˈhɔːsteɪl / (BrE)
a species of horsetail (Equisetum palustre) commonly found in wet or boggy areas of the Northern Hemisphere
