monochromatic

adjective
having only one color or wavelength
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊkrəˈmætɪk /; / ˌmoʊnəkrəˈmætɪk /; / ˌmɑnəˌkroʊˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪk /; / ˌmɒnəˌkrəʊˈmatɪk / (BrE)
having or showing only one color or (in science) only one wavelength of light
