velvet bent grass

noun
a type of fine-leaved grass
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvət bɛnt ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt bɛnt ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a grass species with slender stems and narrow leaves, often used for lawns and golf courses

Examples

Velvet bent grass lawn
Groundkeepers often choose velvet bent grass for areas needing dense, low-growing turf.
Many ornamental lawns use velvet bent grass because of its fine texture and rich color.
The golf course was covered with velvet bent grass, which provided a smooth playing surface.
