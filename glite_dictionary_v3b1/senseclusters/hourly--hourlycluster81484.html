hourly

adjective
occurring or paid every hour
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊɚli / (AmE), / ˈaʊəli / (BrE)
happening every hour or paid/calculated at a rate per hour

Examples

Hourly wage
She is paid on an hourly basis for her work as a tutor.
The hourly weather report forecasted rain throughout the afternoon.
The factory's hourly chime could be heard across the neighborhood.
