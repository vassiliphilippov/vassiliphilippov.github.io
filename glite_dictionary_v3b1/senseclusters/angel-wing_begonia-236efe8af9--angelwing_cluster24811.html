angel-wing begonia

noun
type of begonia with wing-shaped leaves
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl wɪŋ bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl wɪŋ bɪˈɡəʊnɪə / (BrE)
A South American begonia with fibrous roots, distinctive wing-shaped leaves, and clusters of coral-red flowers.

Examples

Angel-wing begonia plant
Gardeners often choose angel-wing begonia for its striking, wing-shaped foliage and ease of care.
He displayed his prize-winning angel-wing begonia at the annual flower show.
The angel-wing begonia in her living room blooms with vibrant coral-red flowers every spring.
