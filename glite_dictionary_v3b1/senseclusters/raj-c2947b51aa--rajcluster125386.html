Raj

proper noun
British rule in India
Pronunciation: / rɑdʒ / (AmE), / rɑːdʒ / (BrE)
the period of British rule in India from 1858 to 1947

Examples

The Raj period
Many social and political reforms were introduced during the Raj.
The end of the Raj marked the beginning of India's independence.
Historians often debate the long-term effects of the Raj on Indian society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: British Empire - the former empire of the United Kingdom including colonies worldwide
  • antonym: independence - the state of being free from outside control
