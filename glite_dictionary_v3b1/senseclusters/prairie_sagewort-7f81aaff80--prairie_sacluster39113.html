prairie sagewort

noun
aromatic perennial plant with tawny florets
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr.i ˈseɪdʒˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈpreəri ˈseɪdʒwɜːt / (BrE)
a silky-leaved, aromatic perennial plant native to dry northern regions of the Northern Hemisphere, bearing tawny florets

Examples

The prairie sagewort plant
Botanists studied the prairie sagewort for its unique aromatic leaves.
In the northern plains, prairie sagewort is a common sight among wild grasses.
The field was dotted with prairie sagewort swaying in the summer breeze.
