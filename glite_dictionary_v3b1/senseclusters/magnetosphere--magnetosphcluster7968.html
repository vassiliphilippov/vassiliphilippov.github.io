magnetosphere

noun
region dominated by a planet's magnetic field

The region surrounding a planet in which its magnetic field dominates and controls the movement of charged particles.

Examples

Earth's magnetosphere
Earth's magnetosphere protects us from the harmful effects of solar wind.
Jupiter's magnetosphere is much larger than Earth's, extending millions of kilometers into space.
Satellites often study the magnetosphere to better understand space weather.

Related Terms
