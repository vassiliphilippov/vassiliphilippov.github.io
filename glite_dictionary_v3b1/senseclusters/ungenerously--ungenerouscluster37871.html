ungenerously

adverb
in a stingy way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛnərəsli / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛnərəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not generous or kind; giving little or less than expected

Examples

Ungenerously portioned food
The food was ungenerously portioned, leaving many guests still hungry after the meal.
He ungenerously offered only a few coins to the charity collection.
The teacher ungenerously graded the assignments, giving lower marks than usual.
She ungenerously kept all the snacks for herself during the meeting.
The manager ungenerously denied the team any extra time off after their hard work.
He ungenerously criticized his colleague's efforts instead of offering help.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generously - in a way that shows willingness to give more than expected
  • synonym: selfishly - in a way that only cares about oneself
  • synonym: stingily - in a way that is unwilling to give or spend
