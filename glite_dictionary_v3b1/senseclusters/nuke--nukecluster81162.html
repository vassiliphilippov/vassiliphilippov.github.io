nuke

verb
cook or heat in a microwave oven
5.8 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / nuÀêk /; / nuk / (AmE), / njuÀêk / (BrE)
to cook or heat food in a microwave oven

Examples

To nuke leftovers
I'll just nuke my coffee before we leave.
Instead of cooking from scratch, he often nukes his meals.
She nuked the frozen dinner for three minutes.
He often nukes popcorn for movie nights with friends.
If you're in a hurry, just nuke your meal for a couple of minutes.
She nuked a burrito for lunch because she didn't have time to cook.

Synonyms

  • microwave
    2.1 1.15/m
    microwave is the neutral verb for heating food in a microwave oven, while nuke is informal and slangy.
  • zap
    4.1 0.09/m
    zap is a casual verb meaning to heat food quickly in a microwave, while nuke is informal and sometimes suggests stronger heating.
