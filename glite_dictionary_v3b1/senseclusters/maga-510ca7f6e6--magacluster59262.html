MAGA

noun
Trump political slogan
Pronunciation: / ˈmægə / (AmE), / ˈmægə / (BrE)
a political slogan and movement meaning 'Make America Great Again', associated with supporters of Donald Trump in the United States

Examples

The MAGA movement
He wore a red MAGA hat to show his support at the rally.
The MAGA movement gained national attention during the 2016 election.
Many people associate the slogan MAGA with conservative American politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Trumpism - the political ideology and movement associated with Donald Trump
  • antonym: progressive - supporting or favoring social reform and liberal ideas
