slugging

noun
forceful hitting
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hitting someone or something with great force, especially with a fist.

Examples

Boxer slugging hard
The boxer was disqualified for excessive slugging during the final round.
After the heated argument, the two men resorted to slugging in the parking lot.
Police arrived quickly to break up the slugging that erupted outside the bar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caressing - to touch or stroke gently
  • synonym: punching - the act of striking with a closed fist
  • synonym: striking - the act of hitting forcefully
