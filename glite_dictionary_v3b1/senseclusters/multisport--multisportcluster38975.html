multisport

adjective
involving several sports
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌspɔːt / (BrE)

Examples

Multisport club activities

The school organized a multisport event to encourage students to try different athletic activities.
His multisport background helped him adapt quickly to the new training program.
