thematics

noun
study of themes
Pronunciation: / θəˈmætɪks / (AmE), / θɪˈmætɪks / (BrE)
the study or analysis of themes, especially in literature, art, or other fields

Examples

Thematics in literature
The course in literary thematics helped students understand recurring ideas in classic novels.
Her research in thematics explores how different cultures express similar concepts through art.
Modern film critics often use thematics to analyze the underlying messages in movies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motif - a recurring element or idea in a work of art or literature
  • synonym: theme - the main subject or idea in a work of art or literature
