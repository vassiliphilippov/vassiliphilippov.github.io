blue-violet

adjective
violet tinted with blue; a color
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈvaɪ.ələt / (AmE), / bluː ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
of a violet color that is tinted with blue; situated between blue and violet in the color spectrum.

Examples

A blue-violet color
After mixing the paints, the result was a rich blue-violet color perfect for the project.
She wore a blue-violet dress that made her eyes stand out.
The artist chose a blue-violet hue to capture the evening sky in her painting.
