fourteenth salary

noun
an extra month's salary after the thirteenth
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrˈtinθ ˈsæləri / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈtiːnθ ˈsæləri / (BrE)
an additional month's pay given to employees, separate from and following a thirteenth salary, as mandated by certain laws or employment contracts

Examples

A fourteenth salary bonus
Employees in some countries receive a fourteenth salary as a bonus during the summer.
Her employment contract specifically mentioned the entitlement to a fourteenth salary each year.
The company revised its policy on fourteenth salary payments to comply with new regulations.
