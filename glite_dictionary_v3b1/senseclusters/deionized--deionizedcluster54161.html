deionized

adjective
without ions; very pure
6.7
Pronunciation: / diˈaɪəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / diˈaɪənaɪzd / (BrE)
having had ions removed, making it very pure, especially referring to water used in scientific or technical settings

Examples

Deionized water
The laboratory uses only deionized water for experiments.
To avoid contamination, all glassware was rinsed with deionized water before use.
Batteries often require deionized water to function properly and last longer.
