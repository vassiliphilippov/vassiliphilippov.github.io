Allahu

interjection
exclamation invoking God
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈlɑːhuː / (AmE), / əˈlɑːhuː / (BrE)
an exclamation invoking God, used to express strong emotion such as surprise, fear, or distress

Examples

Allahu in shock
When the earthquake struck, she cried out Allahu in fear.
He whispered Allahu as he received the unexpected news.
Startled by the loud noise, the old man exclaimed Allahu and looked around anxiously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alas - an exclamation expressing sorrow or regret
  • synonym: oh God - an exclamation expressing surprise or emotion by invoking God
