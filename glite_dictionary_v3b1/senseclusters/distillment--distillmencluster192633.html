distillment

noun
concentrated extract
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪlmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪlmənt / (BrE)
a concentrated aromatic extract obtained by distillation, used for flavoring, perfume, or medicinal purposes

Examples

Aromatic distillment
The chef used a drop of distilment to enhance the flavor of the dessert.
Ancient healers often relied on herbal distilment for their medicinal remedies.
A single bottle of rose distilment can scent hundreds of perfumes.

  • synonym: extract - a concentrated substance obtained from a plant or other material
