signal flag

noun
flag used for signaling messages
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nəl flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nəl flaɡ / (BrE)
A rectangular piece of fabric used to convey messages or signals, especially by ships or at sea.

Examples

A signal flag set
Each signal flag in the set represents a different letter of the alphabet.
The crew practiced using a signal flag to communicate with other ships.
The sailor raised the signal flag to indicate that help was needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flag - a piece of cloth, usually rectangular, attached by one edge to a pole used as a symbol or signaling device
