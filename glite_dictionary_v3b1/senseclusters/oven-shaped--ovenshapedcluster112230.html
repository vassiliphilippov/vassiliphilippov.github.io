oven-shaped

adjective
shaped like an oven
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʌvən ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈʌvən ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of an oven, typically rounded or arched like a traditional oven.

Examples

Oven-shaped huts
Archaeologists discovered several oven-shaped huts in the ancient village site.
The artist designed an oven-shaped fireplace as the room’s centerpiece.
The old bakery had an oven-shaped stone entrance that welcomed customers inside.
