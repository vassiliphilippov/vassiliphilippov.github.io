lunar month

noun
period between successive new moons
3.6 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀên…ö m ånŒ∏ / (AmE), / ÀàluÀên…ô m ånŒ∏ / (BrE)
the period of about 29.5 days between successive new moons in the lunar cycle

Examples

A lunar month period
A lunar month is slightly longer than four weeks.
During a lunar month, the moon completes all its phases from new to full and back again.
Many traditional calendars are based on the lunar month rather than the solar year.

Synonyms

  • synodic month
    9.3 0.02/m
    a synodic month is the technical astronomy name for the new-moon cycle, while a lunar month is the general term used in calendars.
  • lunation
    9.9 0.03/m
    a lunation is the older astronomy word for a full new-moon cycle, while a lunar month is the modern calendar term for that period.
  • moon
    3.7 3.37/m
    a moon is a calendar period people use in some cultures, while a lunar month is the specific astronomical new-moon cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: synodic month - the period between identical lunar phases, especially new moons; synonym of lunar month
