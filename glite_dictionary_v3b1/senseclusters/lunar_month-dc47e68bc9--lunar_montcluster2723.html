lunar month

noun
period between successive new moons
3.6 0.1/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀên…ö m ånŒ∏ / (AmE), / ÀàluÀên…ô m ånŒ∏ / (BrE)
the period of about 29.5 days between successive new moons in the lunar cycle

Examples

A lunar month period
A lunar month is slightly longer than four weeks.
During a lunar month, the moon completes all its phases from new to full and back again.
Many traditional calendars are based on the lunar month rather than the solar year.
