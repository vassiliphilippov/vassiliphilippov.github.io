telekinetic

adjective
moving objects mentally
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
able to move or control objects with the mind, without physical contact

Examples

Telekinetic powers
The movie features a telekinetic hero who can bend metal with his mind.
Scientists have never proven that telekinetic abilities exist in real life.
She claimed her telekinetic powers allowed her to move the glass across the table.
