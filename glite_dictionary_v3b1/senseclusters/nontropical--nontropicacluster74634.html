nontropical

adjective
outside the tropics
4.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtrɑpɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtrɒpɪkəl / (BrE)
not relating to, characteristic of, or located in the tropics

Examples

Nontropical storm
Meteorologists tracked a nontropical storm developing over the North Atlantic.
The hurricane weakened and became a nontropical system as it moved northward.
Unlike hurricanes, nontropical cyclones often form in cooler regions far from the equator.
Many nontropical plants cannot survive in hot, humid climates.
The scientist studied nontropical diseases that affect people in temperate regions.
Some birds migrate from nontropical areas to warmer places during winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extratropical - occurring outside the tropics, especially in meteorology
  • antonym: tropical - originating in or relating to the tropics
