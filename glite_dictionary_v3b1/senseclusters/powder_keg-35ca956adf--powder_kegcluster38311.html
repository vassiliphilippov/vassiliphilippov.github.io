powder keg

noun
tense or explosive situation
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ kɛɡ /; / ˈpaʊdər kɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə kɛɡ / (BrE)
a situation or place that is very tense and could suddenly become violent or chaotic

Examples

A political powder keg
The city became a powder keg after weeks of demonstrations and mounting tension.
Their heated argument turned the atmosphere at work into a powder keg.
With growing political unrest, the country was seen as a powder keg ready to explode.
If no compromise is reached soon, the boardroom could become a powder keg of anger and frustration.
The unresolved conflict made the city a true powder keg over the summer.
Years of economic hardship have turned the country into a powder keg.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - free from disturbance or violent activity
  • synonym: flashpoint - a critical moment or place where violence or conflict might suddenly break out
  • synonym: tinderbox - a situation that is highly volatile or likely to erupt in conflict
