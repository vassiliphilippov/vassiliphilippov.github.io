advocation

noun
support for a cause
Pronunciation: / ˌædvəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌædvəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of supporting or recommending a particular cause or policy

Examples

Advocation of change
Her advocation of environmental protection inspired many people to take action.
The senator's advocation for healthcare reform was widely discussed in the media.
Community leaders praised his advocation of equal rights for all citizens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocacy - public support for or recommendation of a cause or policy
  • antonym: opposition - active resistance or disagreement with a cause or policy
