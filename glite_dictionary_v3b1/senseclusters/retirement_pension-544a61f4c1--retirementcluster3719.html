Examples

Synonyms

  • pension
    3.2 39/m
    pension is the common one-word term for regular retired income, while retirement pension is a slightly more formal phrase.
  • old-age pension
    3.2 0.25/m
    old-age pension is paid mainly because of age by the state, while retirement pension names the payment after leaving work.
  • retirement benefit
    3.4 1.24/m
    retirement benefit can include lump sums, health cover, or services for older people, while retirement pension is a regular cash payment.
  • superannuation
    9.7 0.02/m
    superannuation is the technical fund system used in some countries, while retirement pension is the general regular payment for retired people.
  • retirement check
    3.1 0.10/m
    retirement check is an informal phrase for the monthly paper payment, while retirement pension is the standard term for that income.
