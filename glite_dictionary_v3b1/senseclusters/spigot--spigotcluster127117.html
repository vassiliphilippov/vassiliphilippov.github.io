spigot

noun
device for controlling flow of liquid
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪɡət / (AmE), / ˈspɪɡət / (BrE)
a device used to control the flow of liquid from a container, such as a tap or faucet

Examples

Water spigot
Before refilling the tank, make sure the spigot is tightly closed.
He turned the spigot and fresh juice flowed into his glass.
The rain barrel has a spigot at the bottom for easy water access.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faucet - a device for drawing liquid, especially in American English
  • synonym: tap - a device for controlling liquid flow, especially in British English
