babirussa

noun
Indonesian wild pig with large curved teeth
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑː.biˈruː.sə / (AmE), / ˌbæ.bɪˈruː.sə / (BrE)
A wild pig native to Indonesia, noted for its enormous curved canine teeth.

Examples

The babirussa pig
Researchers traveled to Sulawesi to observe the habitat of the babirussa.

The babirussas in the sanctuary are part of a conservation effort.
