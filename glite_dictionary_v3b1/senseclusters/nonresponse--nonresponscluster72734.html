nonresponse

noun
lack of reaction
7.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈspɑːns/ /; / /nɑːn.rə.ˈspɑːns/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈspɒns/ /; / /nɒn.rə.ˈspɒns/ (BrE)
the failure to show an expected improvement or reaction to a medical treatment, therapy, or drug

Examples

Patient nonresponse
