nonresponse

noun
lack of reaction
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
the failure to show an expected improvement or reaction to a medical treatment, therapy, or drug

Examples

Patient nonresponse
The doctor was concerned about the patient's nonresponse to the new medication.
Researchers are studying why some individuals show nonresponse to standard therapies.
After several weeks of treatment, the nonresponse became evident in the test results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resistance - the ability of a disease or condition to withstand treatment
  • antonym: response - a positive or expected reaction to a treatment or stimulus
