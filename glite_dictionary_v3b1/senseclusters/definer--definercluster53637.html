definer

noun
deciding factor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that determines or decides the nature, character, or outcome of something

Examples

Key definer of success
Access to education is often the main definer of future economic success for many families.
For many athletes, discipline is the true definer of long-term achievement.
The turning point in the story became the definer of the main character's journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decider - a person or thing that makes a final decision
  • synonym: determinant - a factor that decisively affects the outcome of something
