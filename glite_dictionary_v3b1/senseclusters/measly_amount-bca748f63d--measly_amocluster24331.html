measly amount

noun
very small and insufficient amount
5.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààmizli …ôÀàma änt / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêzli …ôÀàma änt / (BrE)
an amount that is very small and considered insufficient or disappointing

Examples

A measly amount of money
After taxes, his bonus was reduced to a measly amount.
The charity received a measly amount of support from the local businesses.
They only paid us a measly amount for all that work we did.

Synonyms

  • pittance
    7.9 0.85/m
