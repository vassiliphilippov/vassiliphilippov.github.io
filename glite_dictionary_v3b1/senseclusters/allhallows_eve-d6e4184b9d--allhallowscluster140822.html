Allhallows Eve

noun
evening before All Saints' Day; Halloween
9.9 4.4
Pronunciation: /ɑːl ˈhæl.oʊz iːv/ /; / /ɔːl ˈhæl.oʊz iːv/ (AmE), /ɔːl ˈhæl.əʊz iːv/ (BrE)
