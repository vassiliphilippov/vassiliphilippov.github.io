leave

noun
formal or polite departure
4.2 14/m
Pronunciation: / liv / (AmE), / liÀêv / (BrE)
formal permission or instance of departing politely; the act of taking one's departure.

Examples

Take leave politely
He quietly took his leave after the dinner party was over.
The ambassador requested permission to take his leave from the king.
They exchanged polite words before she made her leave.

Synonyms

    7.0 0.10/m
    leave-taking is a formal act of saying goodbye at a meeting or event, while leave is formal permission or the act of departing.
  • parting
    3.2 1.25/m

  • farewell
    1.9 9.04/m
