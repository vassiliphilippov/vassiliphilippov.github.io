unrevealing

adjective
modest clothing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈviːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈviːlɪŋ / (BrE)
describing clothing or appearance that does not show much of the body and is modest

Examples

Unrevealing dress
She wore an unrevealing blouse to the formal dinner.
The school's dress code requires students to choose unrevealing outfits.
He appreciated her preference for unrevealing clothing in professional settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modest - not showing much of the body; reserved in dress
  • antonym: revealing - showing a lot of the body; not modest
