injure

verb
to cause bodily harm or damage
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈɪndʒə / (BrE)
to harm or damage someone's body or part of it, often as a result of an accident or attack

Examples

To injure your arm
Improper lifting techniques can injure your back over time.
She accidentally injured her finger while chopping vegetables.
The soccer player was careful not to injure his ankle again before the big game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heal - to become healthy again; to make something healthy again after injury or damage
  • synonym: wound - to inflict an injury, especially one involving a break in the skin
