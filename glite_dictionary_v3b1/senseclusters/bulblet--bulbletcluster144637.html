bulblet

noun
small bulb-shaped plant growth

A small bulb or bulb-shaped growth that develops from the leaf axil or in place of flowers, especially in certain plants such as lilies and onions.

Examples

Small bulblets on plants

Some species form bulblets instead of flowers, aiding in their propagation.
The gardener separated the bulblets from the parent plant to grow new lilies.
