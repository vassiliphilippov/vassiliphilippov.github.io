brushing machine

noun
textile machine for fabric finishing
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used in the textile industry during the finishing process to improve the surface appearance of fabric by brushing its surface.

Examples

A brushing machine for fabric
After weaving, the fabric is sent through a brushing machine to improve its texture.
Technicians regularly maintain the brushing machine to ensure optimal performance during the finishing process.
The textile factory installed a new brushing machine to enhance the softness of their fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finishing machine - a machine used in the final stage of textile manufacturing
