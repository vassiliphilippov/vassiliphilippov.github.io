brushing machine

noun
textile machine for fabric finishing
5.5 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbrʌ.ʃɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈbrʌ.ʃɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
