individually

adverb
separately; one at a time
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəli / (BrE)
Separately; considered one at a time or apart from others.

Examples

Work individually
Although the students worked individually, they shared their results with the class.
Each member of the team was evaluated individually during the interview process.
The artworks are priced individually, not as a set.
