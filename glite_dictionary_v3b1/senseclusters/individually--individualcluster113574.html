Synonyms

    separately means apart or not together in action or space, while individually means considering each person or thing alone.
  • on an individual basis
    4.4 0.3/m
    on an individual basis treats each case in a formal or planned way, while individually simply means one by one.
  • one at a time
    1.9 5/m
    one at a time shows a clear sequence with items following each other, while individually can mean separate or alone.
  • one by one
    1.4 9/m
    one by one shows actions happening one after another in order, while individually focuses on being separate or single.
  • singly
    4.6 0.2/m
    singly means one at a time or alone, often used for single units, while individually stresses each item considered alone.
  • apiece
    2.9 1/m
    apiece means a shared amount for each person or thing, while individually means considering each one separately rather than together.

  • severally
