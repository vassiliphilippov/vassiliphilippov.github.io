one-humped

adjective
having a single hump
1.0
Pronunciation: / wʌn hʌmpt / (AmE), / wʌn hʌmpt / (BrE)
Having a single hump, especially referring to animals such as camels.

Examples

The one-humped camel
The one-humped camel is commonly known as the dromedary.
Unlike its two-humped cousin, the one-humped camel is native to the Middle East and Africa.
We saw a one-humped camel carrying supplies across the desert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: two-humped - having two humps, especially referring to Bactrian camels
