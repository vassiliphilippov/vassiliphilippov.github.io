empress

noun
female emperor or wife of an emperor
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.prəs / (AmE), / ˈɛm.prəs / (BrE)
A woman who is an emperor or the wife of an emperor.

Examples

The empress wore a crown
During the coronation, the empress wore an elaborate crown adorned with jewels.
Historians are fascinated by the powerful empresses who shaped ancient empires.
The empress ruled the nation with wisdom and grace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emperor - male sovereign ruler of an empire
