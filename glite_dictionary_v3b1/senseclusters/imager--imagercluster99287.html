imager

noun
device for copying data
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒə / (BrE)
a device or software program that creates an exact digital copy of a storage medium for backup or analysis

Examples

Disk imager
The forensic team used an imager to create a copy of the computer's hard drive.
Before upgrading the system, the technician ran an imager to back up all important files.
Specialized imagers are essential tools in digital forensics and data recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backup - a copy of data kept for safety or recovery
  • synonym: cloner - a device or program that makes an exact copy of data
