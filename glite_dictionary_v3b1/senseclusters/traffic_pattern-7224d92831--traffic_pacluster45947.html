traffic pattern

noun
flight path for landing aircraft
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtræfɪk ˌpætɚn / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk ˌpætn̩ / (BrE)
the prescribed path that aircraft follow in preparation for landing at an airport

Examples

Airport traffic pattern
Air traffic controllers monitored the traffic pattern closely to ensure safety during peak hours.
Fog forced several planes to circle in the traffic pattern until visibility improved.
Pilots must familiarize themselves with the traffic pattern before landing at a new airport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flight path - the route a flight follows from origin to destination
