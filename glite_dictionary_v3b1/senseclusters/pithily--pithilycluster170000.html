pithily

adverb
in a concise and forceful way
6.6 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪðɪli / (AmE), / ˈpɪθɪli / (BrE)
In a concise and forcefully expressive manner; using few words to convey much meaning.

Examples

To speak pithily
She responded pithily, making her point in just a few sharp words.
The editor pithily summed up the article's main findings in one sentence.
When asked about the problem, he pithily replied, "Less talk, more action."
