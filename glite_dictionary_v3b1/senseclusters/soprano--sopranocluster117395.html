soprano

adjective
having a high musical range
Pronunciation: / səˈprɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / səˈprɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
(of a voice or instrument) having a high range or pitch.

Examples

Soprano instrument
She was cast in the leading role because of her powerful soprano voice.
The group preferred a soprano instrument to achieve a lighter, more ethereal tone.
The new piece was written for a soprano saxophone, which gave the melody a bright, clear sound.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: alto - having a moderately high vocal or instrumental range
  • synonym: treble - having a very high-pitched, light, or shrill sound
