trash

noun
waste material to be thrown away
1.0
Pronunciation: / træʃ / (AmE), / træʃ / (BrE)
waste material or things that are no longer wanted and are thrown away; rubbish or refuse.

Examples

It’s important to sort the trash for recycling properly.
Please take out the trash before the truck comes in the morning.
