compass flower

noun
plant with leaves aligned to compass points
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm.pəs ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pəs ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
any of several plants with leaves arranged to indicate the cardinal points of the compass, especially certain prairie plants of North America.

Examples

The compass flower plant
Botanists studied the compass flower to learn more about its alignment with the cardinal directions.
Many hikers recognize the compass flower by the way its leaves point north and south.
The compass flower is often used in prairie restoration projects due to its unique leaf orientation.
