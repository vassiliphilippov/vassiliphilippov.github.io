noncharismatic

adjective
without charm or appeal
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkærɪzˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkærɪzˈmætɪk / (BrE)
lacking charm, appeal, or the ability to attract and inspire others

Examples

A noncharismatic leader
Despite his experience, the candidate was considered noncharismatic by many voters.
Her noncharismatic approach made it difficult to energize the team.
The speaker's noncharismatic delivery failed to capture the audience's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charismatic - having a special charm or appeal that inspires others
  • synonym: unimpressive - not able to attract attention or admiration
