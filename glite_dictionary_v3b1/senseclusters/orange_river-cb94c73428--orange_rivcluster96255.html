Orange River

proper noun
river in South Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
the main river in southern Africa, flowing west across South Africa to the Atlantic Ocean and forming part of the border with Namibia
