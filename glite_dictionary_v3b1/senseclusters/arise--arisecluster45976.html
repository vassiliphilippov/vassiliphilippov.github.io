arise

verb
rebel; renounce allegiance
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪz / (AmE), / əˈraɪz / (BrE)
to take part in a rebellion or revolt against authority; to renounce former allegiance

Examples

People arise in revolt
He urged his followers to arise and reclaim their independence.
The villagers decided to arise against the oppressive regime.
When injustice became unbearable, many citizens arose and fought for their freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebel - to rise in opposition or armed resistance to an established government or leader
  • antonym: submit - to accept or yield to a superior force or to the authority of another person
