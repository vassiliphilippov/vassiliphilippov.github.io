filthily

adverb
in a filthy or dirty manner
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlθɪli / (AmE), / ˈfɪlθɪli / (BrE)
In a filthy or unclean manner.

Examples

Boots filthily covered
His boots were filthily caked with dirt after the long hike through the woods.
The child emerged from the yard filthily covered in mud after playing for hours.
The kitchen had been filthily neglected, with dishes piled high and grime everywhere.
