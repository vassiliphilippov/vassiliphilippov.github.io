interprovincial

adjective
between provinces
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərprəˈvɪnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəprəˈvɪnʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two or more provinces

Examples

Interprovincial trade agreements
The government announced new interprovincial trade agreements to boost the economy.
Strict interprovincial travel restrictions were put in place during the health crisis.
An interprovincial highway connects the major cities across several provinces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: international - between or involving two or more countries
  • antonym: intraprovincial - within a single province
