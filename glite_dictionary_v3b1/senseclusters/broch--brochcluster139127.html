broch

noun
ancient Scottish tower
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɑk / (AmE), / brɒx / (BrE)
a circular stone tower built in ancient Scotland, typically used for defense or as a dwelling

Examples

An ancient broch
The ancient broch stood on a hill overlooking the valley.
Archaeologists discovered pottery inside the remains of a broch in northern Scotland.
Tourists often visit the well-preserved broch to learn about Scottish history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fort - a strong building or group of buildings used for defense
