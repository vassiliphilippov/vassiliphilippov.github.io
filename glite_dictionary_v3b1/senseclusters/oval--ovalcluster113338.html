oval

adjective
egg-shaped; elliptical
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.vəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vəl / (BrE)
having a rounded and slightly elongated shape, like that of an egg or ellipse.

Examples

Oval glasses frame
