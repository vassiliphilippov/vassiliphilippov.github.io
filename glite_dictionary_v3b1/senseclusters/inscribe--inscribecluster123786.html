inscribe

verb
formally register as a member
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈskraɪb / (AmE), / ɪnˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to register formally as a participant or member of a group or institution

Examples

To inscribe on a list
He inscribed himself on the list of volunteers for the charity event.
New students must inscribe in the university register before attending classes.
She was inscribed as a member of the association at the annual meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enroll - to officially join or sign up for membership
  • antonym: expel - to remove from membership or participation
  • synonym: register - to enter or enroll formally, especially as a participant
