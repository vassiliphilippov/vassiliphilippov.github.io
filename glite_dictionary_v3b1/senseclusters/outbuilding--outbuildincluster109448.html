outbuilding

noun
a separate secondary building
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
a smaller building separate from but located on the same property as a main building, typically used for storage or other secondary purposes

Examples

A outbuilding on property
Firefighters managed to save the main house, but the outbuilding was destroyed.
The old stone outbuilding was used as a workshop by the previous owner.
They kept their gardening tools in a small outbuilding behind the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shed - a simple roofed outbuilding, typically used for storage
