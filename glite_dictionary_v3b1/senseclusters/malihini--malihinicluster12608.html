malihini

noun
newcomer to Hawaii
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːliˈhini / (AmE), / ˌmɑːliˈhini / (BrE)
a newcomer or stranger, especially someone new to Hawaii or unfamiliar with local customs

Examples

A malihini visitor
As a malihini, she was eager to learn about local customs.
The tour guide welcomed every malihini with a traditional lei at the airport.
It can take time for a malihini to feel at home in a new community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kamaaina - a long-term resident of Hawaii
  • synonym: newcomer - a person who has recently arrived in a place
