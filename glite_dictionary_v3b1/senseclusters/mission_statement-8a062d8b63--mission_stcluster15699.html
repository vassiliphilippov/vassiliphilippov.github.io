mission statement

noun
summary of an organization's aims and values
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃən ˌsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃən ˌsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
a formal written summary of the aims, values, and overall philosophy of an organization, especially a company

Examples

A mission statement of company
Every new employee is required to read the company's mission statement during orientation.
The school's mission statement focuses on fostering creativity and inclusion among students.
Their updated mission statement reflects a commitment to environmental responsibility.
