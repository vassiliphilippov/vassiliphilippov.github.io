thematics

noun
themes or topics
Pronunciation: / θəˈmætɪks / (AmE), / θɪˈmætɪks / (BrE)
the themes, topics, or subject matter that are present in a text, work, event, or discussion

Examples

Thematics of a story
The thematics of the novel include love, betrayal, and the search for identity.
In her lecture, she explored the thematics of migration and cultural change.
The film's thematics revolve around family relationships and personal growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subject matter - the content or topics covered in a work or discussion
  • synonym: theme - the main subject or idea in a work or discussion
