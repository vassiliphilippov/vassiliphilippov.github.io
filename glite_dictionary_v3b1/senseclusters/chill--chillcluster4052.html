chill

adjective
relaxed and calm
3.7
Pronunciation: / tʃɪl / (AmE), / tʃɪl / (BrE)
relaxed, calm, and not easily upset or stressed

Examples

A chill person

He stayed chill even though everyone else was panicking about the deadline.
People say our teacher is the chillest because she never gets angry in class.
