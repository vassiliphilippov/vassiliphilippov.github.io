chasmic

adjective
very great difference
Pronunciation: / ˈkæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˈkazmɪk / (BrE)
showing or involving a very great difference, separation, or division between people, groups, or ideas

Examples

Chasmic gap
There is a chasmic gap in understanding between the two generations.
The report highlighted the chasmic differences in access to education across regions.
Negotiations failed due to the chasmic divide between the parties' positions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divisive - causing disagreement or hostility between people
  • synonym: gulf - a wide difference or separation between people or groups
  • antonym: united - joined together for a common purpose or by common feelings
