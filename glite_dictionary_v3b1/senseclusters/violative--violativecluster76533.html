violative

adjective
tending to violate rules or principles
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪəˌleɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.ə.lə.tɪv / (BrE)
tending to violate rules, laws, or principles; constituting a violation.

Examples

Violative behavior
Such behavior is considered violative of company policy and may result in dismissal.
The court found the law to be violative of the constitution.
The proposed regulations are not violative of any existing environmental standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - acting in accordance with rules or principles
  • synonym: violating - actively breaking a rule, law, or principle
