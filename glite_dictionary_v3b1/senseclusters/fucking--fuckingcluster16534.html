fucking

adjective, adverb
intensifier (very; vulgar slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) used to strongly emphasize something, often to show anger, surprise, or intensity

Examples

So fucking annoying
I can't believe he was fucking late again to the meeting.
It was so fucking cold last night that my car wouldn't start.
This is fucking amazing news—you got the job!
He was fucking furious when he found out what happened.
I can't believe how fucking cold it is outside today.
That was a fucking amazing concert last night!
