starved

adjective
suffering from lack of food
1.5
Pronunciation: / stɑrvd / (AmE), / stɑːvd / (BrE)
extremely hungry or suffering from a lack of food

Examples

The starved child
After days lost at sea, the survivors were starved and weak.
In times of famine, many people become starved for nourishment.
The abandoned dog was starved and needed immediate care.
After hiking all day, we were absolutely starved and couldn't wait to eat.
She was so starved that even a simple sandwich felt like a feast.
The starved refugees received food and water upon arrival at the camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: famished - extremely hungry, starving
  • degree lower: hungry - feeling the need or desire for food
  • antonym: satiated - satisfied to the full; no longer hungry
