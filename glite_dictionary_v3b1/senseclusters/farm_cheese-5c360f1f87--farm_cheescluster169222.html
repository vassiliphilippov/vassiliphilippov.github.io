farm cheese

noun
mild white cheese from skim milk curds
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / fɑrm tʃiːz / (AmE), / fɑːm tʃiːz / (BrE)
A mild white cheese made from the curds of soured skim milk, often produced on small farms.

Examples
