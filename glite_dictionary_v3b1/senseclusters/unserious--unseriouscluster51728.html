unserious

adjective
not severe or important
3.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪriəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪəriəs / (BrE)
not severe, important, or dangerous; of little seriousness or consequence

Examples

Unserious injury
The doctor said the injury was unserious and would heal quickly.
They laughed off his unserious comment during the meeting.
Most of the problems we faced were unserious and easily solved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious - important, severe, or dangerous
  • synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
