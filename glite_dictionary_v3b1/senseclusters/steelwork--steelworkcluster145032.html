steelwork

noun
steel production plant
3.4 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstiÀêlÀåw…úrk / (AmE), / ÀàstiÀêlÀåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
a factory or industrial plant where steel is produced and processed, often referred to as steelworks.

Examples

A steelwork factory
The old steelwork was once the heart of the town's industry.
Many families in the area have worked at the steelwork for generations.
After the steelwork closed, the community faced significant economic challenges.

Synonyms

  • steelworks
    3.9 0.01/m
    steelworks are a group of factories that make steel, while steelwork refers to the same industrial site in singular or collective use.
  • steel mill
    3.3 1.24/m
    a steel mill melts and shapes raw iron into steel, while steelwork names the whole industrial plant where steel is produced and processed.
  • steel plant
    3.7 0.20/m
    steel plant describes a large industrial site that runs many steel processes, while steelwork refers more to the factory or complex itself.
  • steel factory
    2.9 0.06/m
    steel factory is a plain word for a place that makes steel, while steelwork sounds industrial and can mean the whole complex.
