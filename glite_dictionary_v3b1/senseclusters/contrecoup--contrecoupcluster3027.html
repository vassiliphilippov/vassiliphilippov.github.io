contrecoup

noun
injury opposite impact
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈkuː / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈkuː / (BrE)
an injury to the brain or body that occurs on the side opposite to where the impact happened

Examples

A contrecoup injury
The doctor explained that a contrecoup injury can occur when the brain strikes the opposite side of the skull.
After the car accident, the patient was diagnosed with a contrecoup brain injury.
A contrecoup injury is often seen in cases of severe head trauma.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coup - an injury at the site of impact
