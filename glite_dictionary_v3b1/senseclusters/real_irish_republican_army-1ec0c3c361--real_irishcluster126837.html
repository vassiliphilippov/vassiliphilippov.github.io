Real Irish Republican Army

proper noun
Irish republican dissident paramilitary group (formed 1997)
Pronunciation: / ɹiːl ˌaɪɹɪʃ ɹɪˌpʌblɪkən ˈɑːɹmi / (AmE), / ɹiːl ˌaɪɹɪʃ ɹɪˌpʌblɪkən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a dissident Irish republican paramilitary group, formed in 1997 as a breakaway from the Provisional IRA, known for opposing peace agreements and carrying out terrorist attacks
