angle

verb
present something with a biased slant
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡəl / (BrE)
to present or express something from a particular point of view, often in order to influence or persuade

Examples

To angle a story
Politicians often angle their speeches to favor certain interest groups.
She angled her report to highlight the positive aspects of the project.
The article was clearly angled to appeal to young professionals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bias - to influence or present with a particular preference or prejudice
  • antonym: neutralize - to make something neutral or unbiased
