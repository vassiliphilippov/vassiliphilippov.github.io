Aspleniaceae

noun
family of ferns including Asplenium
Pronunciation: / æˌspliːniˈeɪsiːˌi /; / æˌspliːniˈeɪsiːˌiː / (AmE), / æˌspliːniˈeɪsiːˌi /; / æˌspliːniˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of ferns that includes the genera Asplenium, Pleurosorus, and Schaffneria, sometimes considered a subdivision of Polypodiaceae or a separate family in botanical classification
