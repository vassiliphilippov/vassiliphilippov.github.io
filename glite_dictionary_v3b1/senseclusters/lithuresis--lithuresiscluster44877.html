lithuresis

noun
passing small stones in urine
Pronunciation: / lɪθjʊˈriːsɪs / (AmE), / lɪθjʊˈriːsɪs / (BrE)
the passing of small stones in the urine, usually from the urinary tract

Examples

Lithuresis of stones
Doctors monitor lithuresis in patients with a history of urinary tract stones.
Frequent lithuresis can indicate an underlying kidney disorder.
The patient experienced lithuresis after treatment for kidney stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urination - the process of passing urine from the body
