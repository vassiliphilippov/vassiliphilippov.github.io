ama

noun
Japanese female diver
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmɑː / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese female free-diver who collects pearls, seaweed, or shellfish by diving without breathing equipment

Examples

Japanese ama diver
The ama spent hours in the cold sea searching for valuable pearls.
Tourists watched in awe as an ama surfaced with a handful of shellfish.
For centuries, the tradition of the ama has been passed down from mother to daughter.
