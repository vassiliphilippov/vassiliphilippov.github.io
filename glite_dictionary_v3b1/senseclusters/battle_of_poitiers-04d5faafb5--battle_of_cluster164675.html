Battle of Poitiers

proper noun
battle in 1356 where English defeated the French
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv pwɑːˈtjeɪz / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈpwɑːtjeɪ / (BrE)
the battle fought in 1356 during the Hundred Years' War, in which the English, led by the Black Prince, defeated the French forces

Examples

The Battle of Poitiers 1356

The Battle of Poitiers marked a turning point in the Hundred Years' War between England and France.
