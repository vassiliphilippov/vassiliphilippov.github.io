bank

verb
tilt sideways when turning, esp. aircraft
Pronunciation: / bæŋk / (AmE), / bæŋk / (BrE)
to tilt or cause to tilt sideways, especially while turning (often used of aircraft or vehicles)

Examples

To bank the plane
In strong winds, the small sailboat banked dangerously to one side.
The cyclist banked hard into the curve to maintain speed.
The pilot began to bank the plane as it prepared to turn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: level - to make or become horizontal, not inclined
  • synonym: tilt - to cause to slope or incline
