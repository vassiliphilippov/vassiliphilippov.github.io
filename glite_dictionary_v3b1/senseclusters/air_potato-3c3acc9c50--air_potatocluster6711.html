air potato

noun
climbing tropical vine with edible tubers
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr pəˌteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛə pəˌteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
a climbing tropical vine of Africa and Asia, cultivated for its large, edible tubers that grow above ground

Examples

The air potato vine
In some regions, the air potato is considered an invasive species due to its rapid growth.
The air potato vine quickly covered the fence with its broad leaves and bulbous tubers.
The farmers harvested several large air potatoes to sell at the local market.
