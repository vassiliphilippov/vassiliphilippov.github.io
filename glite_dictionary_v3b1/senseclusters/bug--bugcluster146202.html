bug

noun
informal term for a microorganism, especially a germ
Pronunciation: / bʌɡ / (AmE), / bʌɡ / (BrE)
A microorganism, especially one that causes illness, used informally rather than scientifically.

Examples

Catch a bug
Doctors think it was just a common bug and nothing serious.
My son caught a nasty bug at school and had to stay home for a week.
There's a bug going around the office that is making everyone sick.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacterium - a unicellular microorganism
  • synonym: germ - a microorganism, especially one that causes disease
