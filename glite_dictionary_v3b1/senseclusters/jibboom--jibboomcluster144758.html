jibboom

noun
nautical spar extending the bowsprit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪbˌbuːm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪb.buːm / (BrE)
a spar that extends forward from the end of a ship's bowsprit, used to secure the outer edge of a jib sail.

Examples

The jibboom spar
A sailor climbed out onto the jibboom to adjust the rigging.
Stormy weather made it dangerous to stand on the jibboom while the ship was underway.
The new ship was fitted with a longer jibboom to carry an additional sail.
