metacarpal bone

noun
bone between wrist and fingers
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈkɑrpəl boʊn / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈkɑːpəl bəʊn / (BrE)
any of the five long bones in the hand, located between the wrist and the fingers.

Examples

The metacarpal bone break
An X-ray revealed a break in the third metacarpal bone of his right hand.
Each metacarpal bone connects to a different finger, allowing movement and grip.
The doctor said her fractured metacarpal bone would heal with proper splinting.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: phalange - one of the bones in the fingers or toes
