tenselessness

noun
absence of tense sounds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛnsləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking tense articulation in speech sounds, resulting in a more relaxed or lax pronunciation

Examples

Tenselessness of vowels
Linguists noted the tenselessness of certain vowels in the regional accent.
The tenselessness of her speech made her pronunciation sound softer than usual.
Some languages are characterized by the tenselessness of their vowel systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laxness - the state of being relaxed or not tense in pronunciation
  • antonym: tenseness - the quality of being tense or tightly articulated in speech sounds
