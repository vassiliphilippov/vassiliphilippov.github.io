familiar

adjective
well informed about; acquainted with
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪljɚ / (AmE), / fəˈmɪliə / (BrE)
well informed about or thoroughly acquainted with someone or something, especially through experience or close association (usually followed by 'with').

Examples

Familiar with computers
After working here for years, he became very familiar with every shortcut in the building.
Most tourists are not familiar with the local customs and traditions.
She is familiar with the latest scientific research in her field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acquainted - having personal knowledge or experience of something
  • antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about something specific
