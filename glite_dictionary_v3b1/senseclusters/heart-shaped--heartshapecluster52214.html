heart-shaped

adjective
shaped like a heart
1.8 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrt ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a heart; resembling the outline of a classic heart symbol, especially as applied to leaves or objects.

Examples

Heart-shaped leaves

Some leaves on the plant were distinctly heart-shaped and vibrant green.
The necklace featured a heart-shaped pendant crafted from silver.
