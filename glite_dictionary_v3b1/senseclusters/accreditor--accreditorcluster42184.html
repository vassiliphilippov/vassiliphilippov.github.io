accreditor

noun
official certifier
6.8
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɛdɪtər / (AmE), / əˈkrɛdɪtə / (BrE)
an organization or person that officially recognizes or certifies that something meets required standards

Examples

An accreditor organization
The college was reviewed by an accreditor to ensure it met educational standards.
Before launching the program, the organization sought approval from a recognized accreditor.
Universities often display the seal of their accreditor on their official documents.

  • synonym: inspector - a person who examines something to ensure it meets standards
