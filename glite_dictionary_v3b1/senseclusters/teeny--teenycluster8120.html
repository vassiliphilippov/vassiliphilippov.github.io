teeny

adjective
very small; tiny (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːni / (AmE), / ˈtiːni / (BrE)
Very small; tiny. Used informally to describe something of small size.

Examples

A teeny tiny crack
A teeny crack had formed in the window over the years.
She gave me a teeny piece of cake just to taste.
The kitten was so teeny that it fit in the palm of my hand.
