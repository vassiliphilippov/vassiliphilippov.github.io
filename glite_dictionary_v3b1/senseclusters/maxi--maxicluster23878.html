maxi

adjective
ankle-length (of clothing)
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksi / (AmE), / ˈmæksi / (BrE)
(of a woman's garment) having a hemline at or reaching the ankle.

Examples

Maxi dress style
She chose a beautiful maxi dress for the summer wedding.
The designer's new collection features several maxi skirts in bold prints.
Wearing a maxi coat kept her warm during the chilly evening.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: midi - (of clothing) having a hemline at mid-calf length
  • antonym: mini - (of clothing) having a very short hemline, above the knee
