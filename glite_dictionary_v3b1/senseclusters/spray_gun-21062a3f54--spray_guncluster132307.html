spray gun

noun
device for spraying liquids
Pronunciation: / spreɪ ɡʌn / (AmE), / spreɪ ɡʌn / (BrE)
a device resembling a gun that is used to apply liquid substances, such as paint, in the form of a fine mist or spray

Examples

A spray gun tool
Before you use the spray gun, make sure it is filled with the correct paint.
He used a spray gun to paint the fence quickly and evenly.
The technician cleaned the car parts using a specialized spray gun.
