posterboard

noun
stiff cardboard for posters or signs
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstəbɔːd / (BrE)
a stiff, smooth cardboard used for making posters, signs, and displays

Examples

Bright posterboard
Brightly colored posterboard was used to create the stage decorations.
He bought a sheet of posterboard for his science project display.
The teacher asked students to bring posterboard for their presentations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardboard - a stiff, heavy paper used for boxes and signs
