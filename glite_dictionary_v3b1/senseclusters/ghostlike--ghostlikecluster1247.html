ghostlike

adjective
resembling a ghost or phantom
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a ghost; having an appearance or quality suggesting a ghost or phantom.

Examples

A ghostlike figure
Mist drifted through the cemetery, giving everything a ghostlike quality.
She appeared in the doorway, her face pale and ghostlike in the moonlight.
The old house had a ghostlike atmosphere that made everyone uneasy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghostly - resembling or characteristic of a ghost
  • synonym: spectral - of or like a ghost
