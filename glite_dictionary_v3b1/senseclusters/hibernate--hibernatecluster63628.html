hibernate

verb
be in a dormant or inactive state
2.7 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪbɚˌneɪt /; / ˈhaɪbərˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪbəneɪt /; / ˈhaɪbəneɪt / (BrE)
for an animal to spend the winter in a state like deep sleep or inactivity, usually to survive cold and lack of food

Examples

Many animals, such as bats and groundhogs, hibernate to survive the cold winter months.
