prohibition

noun
the act of forbidding something
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.hɪˈbɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.ɪˈbɪʃ.ən /; / ˌprəʊ.hɪˈbɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the act of officially forbidding or banning something by law, rule, or authority

Examples

There was a prohibition on building new structures in the historic area.
