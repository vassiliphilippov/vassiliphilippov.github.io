techbro lifestyle

noun
wealthy tech industry men's subculture
9.6 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkˌbroʊ ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛkˌbrəʊ ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living associated with affluent young men in the technology sector, often characterized by startup culture, conspicuous wealth, and a focus on technology, work, and sometimes social status or exclusivity.

Examples

The techbro lifestyle
Many aspire to the techbro lifestyle, drawn by stories of rapid success and lucrative investments.
She noticed the influence of the techbro lifestyle on office culture after the startup was acquired.
The techbro lifestyle is often criticized for promoting long hours and an unsustainable work ethic.
