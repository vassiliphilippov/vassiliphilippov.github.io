procreant

noun
parent or begetter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊkriənt / (AmE), / ˈprəʊkriənt / (BrE)
a person or animal that produces offspring; a parent or begetter (archaic or literary)

Examples

A procreant parent
In ancient texts, a procreant was often praised for continuing the family line.
The poet described the king as a noble procreant of his dynasty.
Legends spoke of a great procreant whose children founded many villages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offspring - a child or animal born to a parent
  • synonym: parent - a mother or father
