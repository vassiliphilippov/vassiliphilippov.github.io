cashew tree

noun
tropical evergreen tree yielding edible cashew nuts
4.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈʃu ˌtri / (AmE), / kəˈʃuː ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tropical American evergreen tree (Anacardium occidentale) that produces kidney-shaped edible nuts, which are safe to eat only when roasted

Examples

The cashew tree fruit
Every summer, the cashew tree in our backyard produces masses of fruit and nuts.
Harvesting the nuts from a cashew tree requires careful processing to remove toxins.
The cashew tree thrives in tropical climates and is widely grown in Brazil and India.
