handloader

noun
manual cargo loader
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhænd.loʊ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈhænd.ləʊ.də/ (BrE)
a person who loads goods, cargo, or materials by hand instead of using machinery

Examples

Warehouse handloader
The company hired a handloader to move boxes in areas where forklifts could not reach.
As a handloader, he spent hours lifting heavy crates onto the truck each day.
During the strike, only one handloader was available to unload the shipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: machine operator - a person who uses machinery to load or move goods
  • synonym: stevedore - a person employed at a dock to load and unload ships
