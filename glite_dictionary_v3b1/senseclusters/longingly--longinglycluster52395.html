longingly

adverb
with yearning; with desire
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a strong desire or yearning for something that seems unattainable or far away.

Examples

Look longingly at toys
Children pressed their faces against the toy shop window, staring longingly at the new trains.
He spoke longingly of his childhood home, which he had not seen in years.
She looked longingly at the dessert menu, wishing she could order everything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yearningly - in a manner full of yearning; with intense longing
