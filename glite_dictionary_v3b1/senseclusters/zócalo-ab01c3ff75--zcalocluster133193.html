zócalo

noun
base or plinth
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.kə.loʊ/ /; / /ˈzoʊ.kə.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.kə.ləʊ/ /; / /ˈzəʊ.kə.ləʊ/ (BrE)
a low base or plinth that supports a statue, column, or similar structure, or a skirting board at the bottom of a wall
