nonreconciliation

noun
lack of agreement
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the state or condition of not being reconciled or brought into agreement

Examples

The nonreconciliation of accounts
The ongoing nonreconciliation of the accounts caused concern among auditors.
Years of nonreconciliation between the two countries led to continued tension.
The report highlighted the nonreconciliation of conflicting data from different sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reconciliation - the process of making two things compatible or bringing them into agreement
