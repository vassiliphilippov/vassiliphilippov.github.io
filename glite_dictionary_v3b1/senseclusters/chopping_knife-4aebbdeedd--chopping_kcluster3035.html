chopping knife

noun
large knife for chopping food
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːpɪŋ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpɪŋ naɪf / (BrE)
a large, heavy knife designed for chopping or mincing meat, vegetables, or other food items

Examples

A chopping knife blade
Always be careful when using a chopping knife to avoid cutting yourself.
She bought a new chopping knife because her old one had become too dull.
The chef grabbed a chopping knife to prepare the ingredients for the stew.
