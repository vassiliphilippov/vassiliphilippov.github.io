Extremely bad in a way that is very noticeable or shocking; remarkably bad or flagrant.

Examples

An egregious mistake
Calling her late to the meeting was a minor issue, but lying to the team was truly egregious.
His disregard for safety regulations was an egregious oversight that endangered everyone on site.
The report detailed several egregious violations of ethical standards.
