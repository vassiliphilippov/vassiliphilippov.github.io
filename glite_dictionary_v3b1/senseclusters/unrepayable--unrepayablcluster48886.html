unrepayable

adjective
impossible to repay
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈpeɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpeɪəbl / (BrE)

Examples

The damage caused by the disaster was considered unrepayable by the local community.
