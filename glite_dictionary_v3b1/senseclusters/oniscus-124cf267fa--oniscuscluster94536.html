Oniscus

noun
genus of woodlice
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnɪskəs /; / oʊˈnɪskəs / (AmE), / əˈnɪskəs /; / əʊˈnɪskəs / (BrE)
a genus of woodlice (small land crustaceans) that cannot roll into a ball, and is the type genus of the family Oniscidae
