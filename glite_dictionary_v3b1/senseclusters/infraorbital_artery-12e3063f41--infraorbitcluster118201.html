infraorbital artery

noun
artery supplying area below the eye
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.frəˈɔːr.bɪ.t̬əl ˈɑːr.t̬ə.ri / (AmE), / ˌɪn.frəˈɔː.bɪ.təl ˈɑː.təri / (BrE)
an artery that branches from the maxillary artery and supplies blood to structures below the orbit of the eye, including the lower eyelid, cheek, and upper lip

Examples

The infraorbital artery branch
An injury to the infraorbital artery can cause significant bleeding in the upper cheek area.
The infraorbital artery supplies blood to the lower eyelid and adjacent facial structures.
The surgeon carefully avoided the infraorbital artery during the facial procedure.
