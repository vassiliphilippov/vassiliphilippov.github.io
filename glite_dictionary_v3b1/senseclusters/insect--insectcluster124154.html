insect

noun
small air-breathing arthropod
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsɛkt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sekt / (BrE)
a small air-breathing arthropod that typically has a segmented body, three pairs of legs, and, usually, two pairs of wings

Examples

A flying insect
A butterfly is a beautiful type of insect found in gardens.
Many farmers use pesticides to control harmful insects in their crops.
The insect landed on the window sill and buzzed softly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arachnid - a related arthropod group including spiders and scorpions, but not insects
  • synonym: bug - an informal term for an insect
