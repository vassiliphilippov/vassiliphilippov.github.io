instant

adjective
requiring immediate attention
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstənt /; / ˈɪn.stənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.stənt / (BrE)
happening or needing to be done immediately, with no delay

Examples

Instant reply
Tackling climate change is an instant priority for our generation.
The committee recognized an instant need for new safety measures.
There is an instant demand for emergency supplies after the earthquake.
She responded with instant enthusiasm when asked to join the team.
The medicine provided instant relief from my headache.
The new messaging app allows instant communication between friends around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delayed - Happening later than expected or desired; not instant.
  • synonym: immediate - Occurring or done at once; instant.
  • synonym: pressing - requiring quick or immediate attention
  • synonym: urgent - requiring immediate action or attention
