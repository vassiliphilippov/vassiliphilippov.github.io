Synonyms

  • cry out
    1.8 9/m
    cry out is a sudden loud sound people make from pain or fear, while cry is a general loud vocal expression of feeling.
  • yell
    1.0 50/m
    yell is a very loud, sharp call used in pain or anger, while cry is a broader loud sound to show feeling or call.
  • shout
    1.0 40/m
    shout is speaking loudly to be heard or to attract attention, while cry often shows strong feeling or calls for help.
  • shout out
    4.2 1.0/m
    shout out is a sudden loud call, often in public or informal speech, while cry is any loud vocal expression of feeling.
  • call out
    3.5 14/m
    call out is to announce or name someone loudly, often to get attention, while cry more strongly shows emotion or alarm.
  • call
    1.0 110/m
    call can mean a loud request or any way to get attention and is broader, while cry usually shows strong feeling or alarm.
