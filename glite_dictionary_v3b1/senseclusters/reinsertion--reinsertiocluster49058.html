reinsertion

noun
return to society
6.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.ˈsɜːr.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.ˈsɜː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of helping someone return to normal social life, work, or community after being excluded, imprisoned, or ill

Examples

Social reinsertion
The organization focuses on the reinsertion of former prisoners into the workforce.
Successful reinsertion programs help people rebuild their lives after long-term illness.
Community support is essential for the reinsertion of individuals who have faced social exclusion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusion - the act of preventing someone from entering or being part of a group
  • synonym: integration - the act of becoming part of a group or society again
  • synonym: rehabilitation - the process of restoring someone to health or normal life
