bast

noun
woody plant fiber from phloem, used in rope or mats
9.2 8.5
Pronunciation: /bæst/ (AmE), /bɑːst/ (BrE)
Strong woody fibers obtained especially from the phloem of certain plants, used for making rope, mats, and similar products.

Examples

Bast fibers for rope
The craftsmen separated the bast from the stems before weaving baskets.
The hemp plant is cultivated for its bast, which is processed into strong ropes.
Traditionally, sandals were made from bast obtained from the inner bark of trees.
