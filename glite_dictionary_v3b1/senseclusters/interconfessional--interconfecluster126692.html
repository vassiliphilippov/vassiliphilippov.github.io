interconfessional

adjective
between faith groups
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkənˈfɛʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkɒnˈfɛʃənəl / (BrE)
involving or relating to more than one religious denomination or faith group

Examples

An interconfessional service
The interconfessional service welcomed members from several different churches in the community.
An interconfessional committee was formed to address shared concerns among various faiths.
The school encourages interconfessional understanding through joint religious studies classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecumenical - promoting unity among different Christian churches
  • antonym: sectarian - relating to or characteristic of a particular religious sect
