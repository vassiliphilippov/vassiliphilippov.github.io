pierhead

noun
top of bridge pier
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪrˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪəˌhɛd / (BrE)
the uppermost part or cap of a bridge pier or supporting column that supports the beams or deck

Examples

Bridge pierhead support
The engineers reinforced the pierhead to ensure the bridge could handle heavier traffic.
Cracks were discovered in the concrete pierhead during the routine inspection.
Workers installed new beams resting on the pierhead to complete the bridge repairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abutment - the end support of a bridge or arch
  • degree lower: foundation - the lowest load-bearing part of a structure
