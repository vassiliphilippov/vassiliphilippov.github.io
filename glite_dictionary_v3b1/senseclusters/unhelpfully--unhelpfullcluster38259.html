unhelpfully

adverb
in an unhelpful way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛlpfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlpfəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not helpful or does not provide assistance.

Examples

He unhelpfully shrugged
He unhelpfully shrugged his shoulders when I asked for directions.
She unhelpfully pointed out the mistakes without offering any solutions.
The instructions were unhelpfully vague and difficult to follow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: helpfully - in a helpful manner
