live

adverb
not recorded; happening in real time
3.2 10/m
Pronunciation: / laÉªv / (AmE), / laÉªv / (BrE)
(of a broadcast, performance, etc.) happening at the same time as it is seen or heard, not recorded in advance.

Examples

Broadcast live
The concert will be broadcast live at 8 p.m. tonight.
The reporter covered the breaking news live from the scene.
They watched the championship game live on television from their living room.

Synonyms
