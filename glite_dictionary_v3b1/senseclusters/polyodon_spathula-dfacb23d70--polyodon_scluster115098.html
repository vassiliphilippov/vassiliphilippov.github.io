Polyodon spathula

noun
primitive freshwater fish with paddle-shaped snout
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑlioʊˈdɒn ˈspæʧələ / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈəʊdɒn ˈspætjʊlə / (BrE)
a large primitive freshwater fish of the Mississippi Valley, also known as the American paddlefish, noted for its long paddle-shaped snout
