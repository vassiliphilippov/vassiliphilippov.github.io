spiff

verb
to make neater
8.0
Pronunciation: / spɪf / (AmE), / spɪf / (BrE)
to make something look cleaner, neater, or more attractive, often by adding small improvements

Examples

To spiff up a room
She will spiff the living room before her parents arrive this evening.
He likes to spiff up his outfit with a colorful tie.
They decided to spiff the office by adding some plants and new artwork.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mess up - to make something untidy or disorganized
  • synonym: spruce - to make something tidy or smart in appearance
  • synonym: tidy - to make something neat and orderly
