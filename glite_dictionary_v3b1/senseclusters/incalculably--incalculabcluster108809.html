incalculably

adverb
beyond calculation
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkælkjələbli /; / ɪnˈkælkjʊləbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkælkjʊləbli / (BrE)
to an extent that cannot be measured or calculated; extremely or immeasurably

Examples

An incalculably large amount
The value of her contribution to the project was incalculably great.
The landscape had changed incalculably after the earthquake struck the region.
His kindness helped me incalculably during a very difficult time.
The museum's collection is incalculably valuable to historians around the world.
Her kindness has incalculably improved the lives of everyone she meets.
The damage caused by the fire was incalculably greater than anyone expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immeasurably - to an extent that cannot be measured
  • synonym: infinitely - to an unlimited or endless degree
  • antonym: predictably - in a way that can be predicted
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree
