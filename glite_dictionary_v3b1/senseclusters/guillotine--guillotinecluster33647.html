guillotine

verb
to cut with guillotine
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪləˌtin / (AmE), / ˈɡɪətiːn / (BrE)
to cut something using a guillotine or a similar cutting device

Examples

To guillotine paper
The workers will guillotine the paper into neat stacks before packaging.
To ensure a clean edge, the technician carefully guillotined each sheet of metal.
In the print shop, they often guillotine large posters to the correct size.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sever - to cut through something completely
  • synonym: slice - to cut something into thin pieces
