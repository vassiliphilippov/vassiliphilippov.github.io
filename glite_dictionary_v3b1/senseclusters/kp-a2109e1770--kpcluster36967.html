KP

noun
enlisted person assigned to kitchen duties
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪ ˈpi / (AmE), / ˌkeɪ ˈpiː / (BrE)
an enlisted military person assigned to help with kitchen duties, such as preparing or serving food

Examples

A KP duty
After breaking the rules, she spent several days as a KP in the mess hall.
He was assigned as a KP for the week and had to help prepare meals.
The new recruits took turns doing KP duty in the kitchen.
