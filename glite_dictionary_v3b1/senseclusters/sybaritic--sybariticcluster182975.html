sybaritic

adjective
luxurious; pleasure-seeking
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪbəˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪbəˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
fond of or characterized by luxury and pleasure-seeking; providing or displaying sensuous luxury

Examples

Sybaritic lifestyle
His sybaritic tastes were evident in his decadent home furnishings.
She indulged in a sybaritic day at the spa, enjoying every possible luxury.
The couple hosted a sybaritic dinner party with gourmet food and fine wines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: austere - having a plain and simple style; not luxurious
  • synonym: luxurious - characterized by luxury, elegance, and comfort
