stocks

noun
frame supporting a boat during construction
4.4
Pronunciation: / stɑks / (AmE), / stɒks / (BrE)
a framework that supports a boat or ship while it is being built or repaired

Examples

Ship on the stocks
Before launching, the vessel rested safely on the stocks for several months.
The old stocks were worn from years of supporting fishing boats in the shipyard.
The workers positioned the keel on the stocks before beginning the hull assembly.
