high-spiritedness

noun
exuberant liveliness or enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈspɪrɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / haɪ ˈspɪərɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
lively and cheerful behavior; exuberant enthusiasm or energy

Examples

The high-spiritedness of players
Despite the setbacks, the team's high-spiritedness never faltered.
Her high-spiritedness was contagious, lifting everyone's mood.
The high-spiritedness of the children made the party lively and enjoyable.
