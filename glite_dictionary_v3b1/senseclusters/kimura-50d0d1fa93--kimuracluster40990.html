Kimura

noun
martial arts arm lock
Pronunciation: / kɪˈmʊrə / (AmE), / kɪˈmʊərə / (BrE)
a grappling technique in martial arts that locks an opponent's arm by twisting it behind their back

Examples

A Kimura lock
The fighter used a Kimura to quickly end the match in the first round.
During training, she practiced escaping from a Kimura hold.
Applying a Kimura requires precise control of your opponent's arm and shoulder.
