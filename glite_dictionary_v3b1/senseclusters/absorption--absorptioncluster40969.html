the process by which energy or a substance is taken up and incorporated by another material (such as in physics or chemistry)

Examples

Light absorption
The absorption of sunlight by Earth's atmosphere warms the planet's surface.
The experiment measured the absorption of infrared radiation by water vapor.
X-rays have high absorption rates in dense materials like bone.
Activated charcoal is known for its absorption of toxins in the digestive system.
The absorption of water by the sponge occurs rapidly due to its porous structure.
The experiment showed that the absorption of the gas increased as the temperature rose.

Synonyms

  • antonym: transmission - the process by which energy passes through a medium instead of being absorbed
