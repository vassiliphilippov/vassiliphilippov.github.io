carbonate

verb
add carbon dioxide to a liquid
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.bə.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.bə.neɪt / (BrE)
to add carbon dioxide to a liquid, especially to make it fizzy or sparkling

Examples

To carbonate water
If you carbonate the beverage for too long, it can become overly fizzy.
Most soft drink manufacturers carbonate water to create the signature fizz in their beverages.
The bartender offered to carbonate my juice with a soda maker.
