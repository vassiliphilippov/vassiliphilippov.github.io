unchristlike

adjective
not resembling Christ or Christian ideals
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɪst.laɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪst.laɪk / (BrE)
Not behaving in a manner consistent with Christian values or ideals; not resembling the character or actions attributed to Christ.

Examples

An unchristlike act
His rude and selfish actions were completely unchristlike and shocked the community.
Many strive to be compassionate and forgiving, not unchristlike in their attitudes.
The teacher reminded the students that bullying was unchristlike behavior.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Christlike - resembling or embodying the qualities attributed to Christ
