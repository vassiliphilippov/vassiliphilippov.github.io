sticky

adjective
difficult or embarrassing (situation)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪki / (AmE), / ˈstɪki / (BrE)
(of a situation or problem) difficult, awkward, or causing embarrassment.

Examples

Sticky problem
It's a sticky situation, so we need to be careful what we say.
She found herself in a sticky spot after forgetting her friend's birthday.
The negotiations reached a sticky point when neither side would compromise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tricky - difficult to deal with or handle
