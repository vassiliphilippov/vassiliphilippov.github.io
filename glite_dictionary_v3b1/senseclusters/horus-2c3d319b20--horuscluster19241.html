Horus

proper noun
Egyptian sun god with falcon head
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrəs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːrəs / (BrE)
An Egyptian solar god, often depicted with the head of a falcon, revered as the son of Osiris and Isis.

Examples

Horus the falcon god
Ancient Egyptians believed that Horus watched over the pharaohs as their protector.
In mythology, Horus avenged his father Osiris by defeating Set in battle.
The temple was adorned with carvings of Horus, easily recognizable by his falcon head.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Osiris - Egyptian god, father of Horus
  • synonym: Ra - chief Egyptian sun god, also associated with the sun
