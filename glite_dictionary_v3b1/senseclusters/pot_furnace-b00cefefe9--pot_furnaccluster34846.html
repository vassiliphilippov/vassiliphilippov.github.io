pot furnace

noun
glass-melting furnace with pots
Pronunciation: / pɑt ˈfɝnɪs / (AmE), / pɒt ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
a type of furnace containing several pots in which glass is melted, used in glass-making processes.

Examples

A pot furnace for glass
Each pot furnace in the workshop can hold several different colors of molten glass.
Improvements to the pot furnace have made the glass-melting process more efficient.
The old glass factory still uses a traditional pot furnace to produce its handmade vases.
