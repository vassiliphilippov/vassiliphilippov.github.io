plank

noun
long, flat piece of sawn timber
1.3
Pronunciation: / plæŋk / (AmE), / plæŋk / (BrE)
a long, flat, and sturdy piece of sawn timber, typically wider than a board, used in building and construction

Examples

Wooden plank floor
He used a plank to replace the broken step on the porch.
Several planks were stacked in the workshop, ready for use in the new deck.
The gymnast balanced delicately on a narrow plank during her routine.
