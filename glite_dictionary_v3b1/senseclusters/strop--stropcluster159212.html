strop

verb
sharpen with a strop
Pronunciation: / strɑp / (AmE), / strɒp / (BrE)
to sharpen (a razor or blade) by rubbing it on a strop

Examples

To strop a razor
Barbers used to strop their blades on a leather strap between customers.
He carefully stropped his razor before every shave to keep it sharp.
You should strop the knife regularly to maintain its fine edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hone - to sharpen by rubbing on a stone or with precision
