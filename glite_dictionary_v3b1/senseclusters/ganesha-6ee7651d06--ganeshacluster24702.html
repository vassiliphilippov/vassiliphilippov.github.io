Ganesha

proper noun
Hindu god of wisdom and remover of obstacles
7.9
Pronunciation: / gəˈneɪʃə / (AmE), / gəˈneɪʃə / (BrE)
A Hindu god of wisdom, success, and remover of obstacles, often depicted with the head of an elephant.

Examples

Ganesha festival
During the festival of Ganesh Chaturthi, devotees celebrate Ganesha with music, dance, and offerings.
Many Hindus pray to Ganesha before starting a new venture to seek his blessings.
The image of Ganesha is commonly found at the entrance of homes and temples in India.
