unloader

noun
device for removing ammunition
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈloʊdər / (AmE), / ʌnˈləʊdə / (BrE)
a device used to remove cartridges or ammunition from a firearm or magazine

Examples

Unloader for magazines
After shooting at the range, he used an unloader to empty the magazine safely.
The technician demonstrated how the unloader works with different types of firearms.
Always use an unloader to remove ammunition before cleaning your weapon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loader - a device used to insert cartridges or ammunition into a firearm or magazine
