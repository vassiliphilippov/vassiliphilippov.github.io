thin blue line

noun
phrase symbolizing police as protectors of society
8.1
Pronunciation: / θɪn bluː laɪn / (AmE), / θɪn bluː laɪn / (BrE)
a phrase symbolizing the role of police officers as the maintainers of order and protectors between society and chaos; also, a symbol of police solidarity

Examples

The thin blue line symbol
Debates about the thin blue line often arise during discussions about police funding.
He believes that the thin blue line keeps communities safe from disorder.
The community painted a thin blue line outside the police station as a show of support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: law enforcement - the system by which police maintain public order and enforce laws
  • synonym: police solidarity - unity or a sense of support among police officers
