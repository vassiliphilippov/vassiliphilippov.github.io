fall girl

noun
gullible woman; scapegoat
5.6 7.3
Pronunciation: /fɔːl gɜːrl/ /; / /fɑːl gɜːrl/ (AmE), /fɔːl gɜːl/ /; / /fɔːl ˈgɪ.əl/ (BrE)
A woman who is easily deceived or taken advantage of; a gullible female, sometimes used for someone who is made to take the blame for others' misdeeds.
