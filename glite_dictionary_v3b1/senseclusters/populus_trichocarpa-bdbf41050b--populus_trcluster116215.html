Populus trichocarpa

noun
western North American cottonwood tree species
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələs traɪkoʊˈkɑːrpə / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊləs trɪkəʊˈkɑːpə / (BrE)
A species of cottonwood tree native to western North America, with dark green glossy leaves above and rusty or silvery undersides.
