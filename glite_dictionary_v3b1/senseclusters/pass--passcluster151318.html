pass

noun
low point in a mountain range used for crossing
Pronunciation: / pæs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
a low point in a range of mountains, often used as a route for crossing from one side to the other

Examples

A mountain pass
Snow often blocks the pass in winter, making travel difficult.
The old trade route crosses the pass connecting the two valleys.
We hiked all morning to reach the mountain pass before noon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: col - the lowest point on a mountain ridge between two peaks
  • synonym: gap - a narrow opening or pass, especially one through mountains
  • antonym: summit - the highest point of a mountain
