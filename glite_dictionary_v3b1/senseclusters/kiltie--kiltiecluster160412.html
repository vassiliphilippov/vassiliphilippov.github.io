kiltie

noun
shoe fringe or band member
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlti / (AmE), / ˈkɪlti / (BrE)
a decorative fringed leather tongue on a shoe, or a member of a Scottish band who wears a kilt

Examples

Shoe kiltie fringe
The golfer wore shoes with a kiltie to protect the laces from dirt.
Each member of the Scottish band, known as a kiltie, marched proudly in the parade.
He bought a new pair of loafers featuring a stylish kiltie over the instep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kilt - a traditional Scottish skirt with pleats
  • synonym: tongue - the part of a shoe that sits under the laces
