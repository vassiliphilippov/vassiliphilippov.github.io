prospector

noun
person seeking new customers or business

A person who searches for and pursues new business opportunities or potential customers, especially in sales, marketing, or business development.

Examples

Sales prospector
The company hired a prospector to identify potential enterprise customers in new markets.
Startups often rely on experienced prospectors to build their first pipeline of qualified leads.
As a freelance prospector, she cold-called dozens of firms each week to find clients.
