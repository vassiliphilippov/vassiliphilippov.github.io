composite

verb
to combine images
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑːzɪt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
to combine images or elements to create a single picture or whole, especially in film, photography, or digital graphics

Examples

To composite photos
The designer composited several layers to create a striking magazine cover.
In film production, editors often composite actors onto digital backgrounds.
She composited different textures to achieve a unique visual effect in her artwork.
The artist spent hours compositing different backgrounds to create a realistic scene.
Advanced software makes compositing special effects into movies much easier than before.
She learned the basics of compositing in her digital art class last semester.
