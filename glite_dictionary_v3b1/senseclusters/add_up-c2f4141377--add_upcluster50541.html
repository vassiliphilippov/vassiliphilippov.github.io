add up

verb
determine the sum of several amounts
Pronunciation: / æd ʌp / (AmE), / æd ʌp / (BrE)
to calculate or reach a total by combining numbers or amounts

Examples

To add up numbers
After adding up all her expenses, she realized she had overspent.
Please add up these numbers and write the total in the box.
The accountant added up the receipts at the end of the day.
All his debts add up to a considerable sum.
At the end of the month, all my expenses add up to more than I expected.
The numbers just don't add up—we must have made a mistake somewhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amount to - to be a total; to add up to a particular amount
  • antonym: subtract - to take one number or amount away from another
  • synonym: sum - find the total amount by addition
