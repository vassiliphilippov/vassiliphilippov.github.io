mongrelization

noun
offensive mixing term
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌŋɡrələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmʌŋɡrɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an offensive term for the mixing of different groups, especially used to express disapproval of racial or cultural mixing

Examples

Racial mongrelization
The politician's speech was criticized for its use of the word mongrelization to describe cultural diversity.
Many people find the term mongrelization deeply offensive and rooted in racist ideology.
The book discusses how the concept of mongrelization has been used to justify discrimination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybridization - the process of combining different varieties or groups
  • antonym: purity - the state of being pure or unmixed
