crib death

noun
sudden unexplained infant death during sleep
3.1
Pronunciation: / krɪb dɛθ / (AmE), / krɪb dɛθ / (BrE)
the sudden and unexpected death of a seemingly healthy infant, typically during sleep; also known as sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS)

Examples

Crib death syndrome
