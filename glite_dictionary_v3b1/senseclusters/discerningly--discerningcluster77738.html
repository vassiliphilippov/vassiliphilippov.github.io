discerningly

adverb
with good judgment
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɜrnɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈsɜːnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows good judgment, insight, or the ability to distinguish well

Examples

To choose discerningly
She selected the candidates discerningly, focusing on those with the most relevant experience.
The critic discerningly reviewed each film, highlighting both strengths and weaknesses.
He discerningly invested his money, choosing only reputable companies with strong growth potential.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carelessly - in a way that shows a lack of attention or thought
  • synonym: wisely - in a way that shows good sense or judgment
