overdevelop

verb
to process film too long

to process photographic film or paper for too long or with too strong chemicals, resulting in images that are too dark or high in contrast

Examples

To overdevelop film
If you overdevelop the film, the negatives become too dark and lose detail in the shadows.
Photographers must be careful not to overdevelop prints, or the images will appear harsh and unnatural.
She accidentally overdeveloped the photo, making the contrast much too strong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overexpose - to expose film or a photo to too much light
