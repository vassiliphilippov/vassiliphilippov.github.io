tortiously

adverb
in a wrongful manner
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtɔːr.ʃəs.liː/ /; / /ˈtɑːr.ʃəs.liː/ (AmE), /ˈtɔː.ʃəs.liː/ /; / /ˈtɔː.ʃə.liː/ (BrE)

Examples

If someone behaves tortiously, they may be required to pay damages to the injured party.
The lawyer argued that her client did not act tortiously in the matter.
