scarring

verb
to cause lasting damage
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɑːrɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a mark or lasting damage on the skin or to someone's emotions

Examples

Scarring his mind
The accident is scarring him both physically and emotionally.
Repeated scratching can end up scarring your skin permanently.
The harsh criticism was scarring for her confidence as a young artist.
