Baka

proper noun
Central African people
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkə / (BrE)
an indigenous people of Central Africa who live in rainforest regions, known for their traditional forest-based lifestyle

Examples

The Baka people
The Baka have a deep knowledge of the plants and animals in their rainforest home.
Researchers visited the Baka to learn about their traditional music and storytelling.
Many Baka communities are working to preserve their language and culture.
