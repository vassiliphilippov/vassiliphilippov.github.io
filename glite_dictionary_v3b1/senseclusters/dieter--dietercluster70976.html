dieter

noun
person following a diet
7.5 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪətə / (BrE)
a person who restricts or regulates their food intake, often for health or weight-loss reasons.

Examples

A dieter tracks meals
As a dieter, she kept a detailed log of her daily meals.
He joined a support group for dieters to share tips and stay motivated.
Many dieters struggle with cravings during their first few weeks of dieting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diet - a regulated selection of foods; a special food regimen
  • degree higher: faster - a person who voluntarily abstains from all or some kinds of food or drink
  • antonym: overeater - a person who eats to excess
