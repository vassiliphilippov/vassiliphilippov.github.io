hooky player

noun
person who skips school without permission
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊki ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈhʊki ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who is absent from school without permission; a truant

Examples

A hooky player at school
Back in high school, he had a reputation as the class hooky player.
Teachers worked hard to keep every hooky player in class and focused on their studies.
The principal warned the hooky player that repeated absences would lead to consequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truant - a student who stays away from school without leave or explanation
