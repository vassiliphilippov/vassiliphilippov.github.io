determined

adjective
having a strong resolve
1.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɝːmɪnd /; / dɪˈtɚˌmɪnd /; / dɪˈtɝː.mɪnd / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnd /; / dɪˈtɜː.mɪnd / (BrE)
showing a firm decision, strong resolve, or unwavering commitment to do something

Examples
