leatherjack

noun
tropical fish with tiny embedded scales
7.3 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈleð.ər.dʒæk/ (AmE), /ˈleð.ə.dʒæk/ (BrE)
Any of several tropical fish species of the New World, noted for their very small embedded scales and leather-like skin.
