poor boy

noun
large sandwich on a crusty roll, US regional
Pronunciation: / pɔr bɔɪ / (AmE), / pɔː bɔɪ / (BrE)
a large sandwich made with a long crusty roll, typically filled with meats, cheese, and other ingredients, known in some regions of the United States
