discoloration

noun
change of color to a duller or faded state
Pronunciation: / dɪsˌkʌləˈreɪʃən /; / ˌdɪsˌkʌlɚˈeɪʃən /; / dɪsˌkʌlərˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪsˌkʌləˈreɪʃən /; / ˌdɪsˌkʌləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a change in color (usually making something faded, dull, or less attractive), or a mark where this has happened
