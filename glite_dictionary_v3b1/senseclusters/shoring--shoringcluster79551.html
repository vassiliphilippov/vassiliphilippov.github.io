shoring

noun
act of supporting with props
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔr.ɪŋ /; / ˈʃɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɔː.rɪŋ /; / ˈʃɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
