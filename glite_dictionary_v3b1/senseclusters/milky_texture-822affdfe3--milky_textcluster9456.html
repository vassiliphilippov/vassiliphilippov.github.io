milky texture

noun
smooth and creamy quality
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlki ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlki ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
a smooth and creamy quality, often resembling that of milk, especially in appearance or mouthfeel

Examples

A milky texture paint
Artists often choose paints with a milky texture for creating subtle, translucent effects.
She prefers her foundation to have a milky texture for a smooth application.
The yogurt has a milky texture that makes it especially pleasant to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creamy - smooth and rich in texture, like cream
  • antonym: watery - thin or diluted in texture, lacking creaminess
