Lycosidae

noun
family of wolf spiders
9.9
Pronunciation: / laɪˈkɑːsɪˌdi / (AmE), / lɪˈkɒsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of spiders commonly known as wolf spiders, characterized by their excellent eyesight and hunting behavior.

Examples

Lycosidae wolf spiders
Lycosidae are commonly found in grasslands and forests around the world.
Scientists discovered a new species within the Lycosidae family during their expedition.
