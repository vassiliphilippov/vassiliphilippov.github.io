yup

interjection
informal yes
8.2
Pronunciation: / jʌp / (AmE), / jʌp / (BrE)
an informal way of saying 'yes'

Examples

Yup, I agree
"Do you want some coffee?" "Yup, that would be great!"
He asked if I had finished my homework and I replied, "yup."
Whenever someone calls her name, she always answers with a cheerful yup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yes - used to express agreement or affirmation
