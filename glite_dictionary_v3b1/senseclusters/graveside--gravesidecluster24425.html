graveside

noun
area beside a grave
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪvˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvsaɪd / (BrE)
the area beside a grave, especially where a funeral or ceremony is held

Examples

Graveside gathering
Family and friends gathered at the graveside to say their final goodbyes.
The priest spoke a few comforting words during the graveside service.
Fresh flowers were placed at the graveside after the ceremony ended.
The family stood in silence at the graveside as the ceremony began.
Flowers were placed gently at the graveside after the funeral service.
Many friends gathered at the graveside to say their final goodbyes.
