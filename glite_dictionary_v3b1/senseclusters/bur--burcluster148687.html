bur

noun
surgical or dental cutting bit
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɝː / (AmE), / bɜː / (BrE)
A small cutting or grinding tool, typically a rotating bit, used in dentistry or surgery for cutting hard tissue or bone.

Examples

Dental bur tool
He used a high-speed bur to remove the decayed portion of the tooth.
Surgical burs must be sterilized after every procedure to prevent infection.
The dentist selected a diamond bur to shape the filling precisely.
