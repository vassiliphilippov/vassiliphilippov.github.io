catsuit

noun
tight one-piece garment
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkæt.suːt/ /; / /ˈkæt.sjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈkæt.sjuːt/ /; / /ˈkæt.suːt/ (BrE)
