catsuit

noun
tight one-piece garment
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkætsuːt / (AmE), / ˈkætsuːt / (BrE)
a tight-fitting, one-piece garment that covers the body from the neck to the ankles

Examples

A tight catsuit
The singer dazzled the audience in a sparkling catsuit during her performance.
She bought a black catsuit for the costume party next weekend.
Many dancers prefer wearing a catsuit because it allows for easy movement on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodysuit - a close-fitting, one-piece garment covering the torso and sometimes the arms
  • synonym: jumpsuit - a one-piece garment with sleeves and legs, often looser than a catsuit
