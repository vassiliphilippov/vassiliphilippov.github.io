arc secant

noun
inverse function of the secant
Pronunciation: / ɑrk ˈsiːkənt / (AmE), / ɑːk ˈsiːkənt / (BrE)
the inverse function of the secant; the angle whose secant is a given number

Examples

The arc secant function
Calculators often have a special function key for arc secant.
The arc secant of 2 gives an angle whose secant is 2.
To solve the equation, find the arc secant of the value on the right side.
