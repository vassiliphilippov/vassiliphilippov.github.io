stone-broke

adjective
completely without money
5.2
Pronunciation: / stoʊn ˈbroʊk / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈbrəʊk / (BrE)
Completely without money; having no funds at all.

Examples

Completely stone-broke
After losing his job, he was stone-broke and had to move back home.
By the end of the month, many students find themselves stone-broke.
I can't go on holiday this year because I'm stone-broke.
