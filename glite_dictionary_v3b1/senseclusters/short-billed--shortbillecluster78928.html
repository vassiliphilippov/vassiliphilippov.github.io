short-billed

adjective
having a short beak (bird)
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt bɪld / (AmE), / ʃɔːt bɪld / (BrE)
(of a bird) having a short beak or bill.

Examples

Short-billed bird
Many coastal birds with short-billed features hunt for insects along muddy shores.
Scientists observed that short-billed species often feed differently than their long-billed relatives.
The short-billed wren is easily identified by its unusually stubby beak.
