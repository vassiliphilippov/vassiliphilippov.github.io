aberrantly

adverb
unusually or abnormally
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈbɛrəntli / (AmE), / əˈbɛrəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is unusual, abnormal, or not typical

Examples

Cells divide aberrantly
The scientist noticed that the cells were dividing aberrantly under the microscope.
During the experiment, the machine behaved aberrantly and produced unexpected results.
His heart rate increased aberrantly after taking the new medication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normally - in a usual or expected way
  • synonym: unusually - in a way that is not usual or common
