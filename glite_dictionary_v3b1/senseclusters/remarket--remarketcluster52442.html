remarket

verb
to resell used goods
5.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈmɑːr.kət/ /; / /rɪ.ˈmɑːr.kət/ (AmE), /riː.ˈmɑː.kɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈmɑː.kɪt/ (BrE)
to sell returned, used, or off-lease goods again, often through specialized channels such as auctions or dealer networks

Examples

To remarket used cars
After the lease ended, the dealer will remarket the car through an online auction for dealers.
Companies often remarket refurbished electronics to budget-conscious customers.
The bank decided to remarket the repossessed homes through a specialized real estate agency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispose - to get rid of something, often by selling or discarding
  • synonym: resell - to sell something again after buying or using it
