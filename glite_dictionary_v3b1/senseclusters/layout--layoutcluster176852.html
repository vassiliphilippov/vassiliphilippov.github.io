layout

noun
arrangement or planning of parts
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪˌaʊt /; / ˈleɪaʊt / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.aʊt /; / ˈleɪaʊt / (BrE)
the way in which the parts of something are arranged or designed, or the process of planning this arrangement (such as for a page, space, or object)

Examples

Page layout
She spent hours working on the layout for the magazine's cover page.
The architect showed us the layout of the new building.
We need to finalize the layout before printing the invitations.
Before building the garden, he sketched a layout of where each plant would go.
She admired the magazine's new layout, which made it easier to read.
The open office layout encourages collaboration among team members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrangement - the way in which things are arranged
  • synonym: design - a plan or drawing produced to show the look and function of an object before it is built or made
