Synonyms

  • set-out
    3.9 0.00/m
    set-out is the arrangement of text and images on a page, while layout is the planned design and spacing of that page.
  • setup
    3.0 3/m
    setup is how parts are organized or planned for use, while layout focuses on the visual placement and design of those parts.
  • arrangement
    2.7 6/m
    arrangement is the order or position of things in a space, while layout is the designed plan that sets their exact look.
  • configuration
    4.8 5/m
    configuration is the technical pattern of parts or settings, while layout is the visual plan of how things appear and fit.
