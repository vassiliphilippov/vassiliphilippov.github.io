elemental

noun

9.9 0.2/m

A supernatural being or spirit that embodies or represents one of the classical elements (fire, water, earth, air), often appearing in fantasy fiction, games, or folklore.

Examples

A fire elemental

Legends say the forest is watched by invisible elementals that protect the trees and streams.
