nonemergency

noun
not urgent situation
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˈmɝːdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈmɜːdʒənsi / (BrE)
a situation or case that does not require immediate action or urgent attention

Examples

A nonemergency visit
For a nonemergency, you should visit your regular doctor instead of the emergency room.
The city set up a separate phone line for nonemergency calls to reduce pressure on emergency services.
Please use this form to report any nonemergency maintenance issues in your apartment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emergency - a serious situation needing immediate action
  • synonym: routine - a regular or normal situation, not urgent
