biological clock

noun
innate mechanism regulating physiological cycles
Pronunciation: / baɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl klɑk /; / ˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl klɑk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl klɒk / (BrE)
an internal, natural mechanism in living things that controls the timing of processes like sleep, aging, or hormone production
