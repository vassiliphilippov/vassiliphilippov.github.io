apostle

noun
early Christian teacher or missionary
4.7 6.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpɔː.səl/ /; / /ə.ˈpɑː.səl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpɒ.səl/ (BrE)
an important early teacher of Christianity, especially one of the twelve disciples chosen by Jesus, or a Christian missionary to a people

Examples

The twelve apostles spread the teachings of Jesus throughout the Roman Empire.
