envenomation

noun
injection of venom
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvɛnəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌvɛnəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or condition of venom being injected into an organism, usually by a bite or sting

Examples
