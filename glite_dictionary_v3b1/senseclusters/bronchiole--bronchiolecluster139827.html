bronchiole

noun
smallest air passages in the lungs
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːŋ.ki.oʊl / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋ.kɪ.əʊl / (BrE)
any of the small branches into which a bronchus divides, ending in alveoli in the lungs.

Examples

Small bronchiole branch
Air passes through the bronchiole before reaching the alveoli.
Inflammation of a bronchiole can cause difficulty in breathing, especially in young children.
The doctor explained how each bronchiole plays a vital role in oxygen exchange.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bronchus - a larger airway in the lungs that divides into bronchioles
