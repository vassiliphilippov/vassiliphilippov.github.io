referral

noun
marketing program using referral links or codes
3.9
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈfɜː.rəl/ /; / /rə.ˈfɜː.rəl/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈfɜː.rəl/ /; / /rə.ˈfɜː.rəl/ (BrE)
a marketing mechanism or program (often using referral links or codes) that tracks and rewards customers or users who recommend a product or service to others.

Examples

Referral program rewards
The startup grew quickly after launching a referral program that rewarded customers for inviting friends.
He sent his friend a discount using his personal referral code before the sale ended.
Many apps offer bonuses through referral links to encourage users to invite new customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affiliate program - a commercial arrangement where partners earn rewards or commissions for directing customers via tracked links.
  • synonym: referral program - a specific promotional scheme that gives rewards to customers who refer new users.
