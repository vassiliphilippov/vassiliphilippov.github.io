covert

adjective
secret; not openly shown

Examples

Covert operation
The agency was accused of conducting covert surveillance on its citizens.
They held a covert meeting late at night to avoid detection.
Through a covert operation, the documents were recovered without anyone noticing.

Synonyms

  • secret
    2.0 41/m
    secret is kept from others on purpose and in many contexts, while covert is a neutral word for hidden actions.
  • surreptitious
    8.5 0.59/m
    surreptitious is done quietly and by stealth to avoid notice, while covert describes actions simply kept secret or hidden.
  • clandestine
    8.2 2.95/m
    clandestine is secret and often illegal or forbidden, while covert is more neutral and used for hidden or private actions.
  • undercover
    6.3 7.20/m
    undercover describes people who work in secret to gather information, while covert describes actions or things kept hidden generally.
  • hidden
    1.1 7.26/m
    hidden means not easily seen or found, often physically, while covert often implies secret plans or actions rather than only physical hiding.
  • concealed
    3.7 0.27/m
    concealed is mainly about keeping something out of sight or covered, while covert refers to secret or not openly shown actions.
  • undisclosed
    5.8 1.65/m
    undisclosed means not revealed or told, usually formally or in documents, while covert is broader and can describe secret behavior.
  • veiled
    6.2 2.00/m
    veiled is partially hidden or hinted at, often in speech or writing, while covert means fully secret or not openly acknowledged.
  • disguised
    4.2 0.45/m
    disguised changes how something appears to hide identity, often to deceive, while covert simply means kept secret or not shown.
  • masked
    5.8 0.44/m
    masked hides true nature or intentions, often using a false appearance, while covert describes secret actions without necessarily using a disguise.
  • underground
    5.7 6.93/m
    underground is used for secret groups or movements that oppose authorities, while covert is broader and applies to any hidden action.
