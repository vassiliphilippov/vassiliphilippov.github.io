supracondylar

adjective
above a condyle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈkɑndɪlər / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈkɒndɪlə / (BrE)
located above a condyle, especially referring to a part of a bone or a type of fracture

Examples

A supracondylar fracture

The surgeon explained that the break was supracondylar, occurring just above the elbow joint.
X-rays revealed a supracondylar region affected by the trauma after the fall.
