olive

noun
evergreen Mediterranean tree with edible fruit
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region, cultivated for its edible drupes (olives) and oil.

Examples

Olive tree
An olive tree can live for hundreds of years in the right conditions.
The olive is a symbol of peace in many cultures.
The ancient Greeks prized the olive for its fruit and oil.
