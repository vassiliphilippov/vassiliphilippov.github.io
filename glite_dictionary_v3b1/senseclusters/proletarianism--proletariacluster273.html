proletarianism

noun
condition or ideology of the working class

Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.ləˈtɛr.i.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.lɪˈtɛə.ri.ə.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
