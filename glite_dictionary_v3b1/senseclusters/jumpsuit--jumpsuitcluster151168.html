jumpsuit

noun
one-piece outer garment
5.2 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌmp.suːt/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌmp.suːt/ (BrE)
a one-piece garment that covers the body and legs, originally worn by parachutists but now also used for work or fashion
