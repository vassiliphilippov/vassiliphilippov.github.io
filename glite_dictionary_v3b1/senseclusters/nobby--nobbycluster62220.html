nobby

noun
fashionable person
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑbi / (AmE), / ˈnɒbi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is fashionable or dresses in a showy, upper-class way

Examples

A real nobby at parties
He always looked like a nobby at parties, wearing the latest designer clothes.
The group of nobbies gathered near the entrance, chatting about exclusive brands.
She was called a nobby because of her elegant style and expensive jewelry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dandy - a man who pays great attention to his appearance and clothes
  • synonym: snob - a person who admires and seeks to associate with those of higher social status
