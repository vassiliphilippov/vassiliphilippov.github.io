wrong side out

adverb
with the inside of a garment facing outward
1.3 4.0
Pronunciation: /rɔːŋ saɪd aʊt/ /; / /rɑːŋ saɪd aʊt/ (AmE), /rɒŋ saɪd aʊt/ (BrE)
with the inside surface of a garment or fabric facing outward, instead of the intended outer side

Examples

Wrong side out fabric
Before sewing, check that the fabric isn't wrong side out.
He accidentally put his T-shirt on wrong side out after rushing out in the morning.
She wore her shirt wrong side out without realizing it until lunchtime.
