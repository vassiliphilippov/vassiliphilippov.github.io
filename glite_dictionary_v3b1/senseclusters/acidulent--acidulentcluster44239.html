acidulent

adjective
slightly sour in taste
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪdʒələnt / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjʊlənt / (BrE)
slightly sour or somewhat acid to the taste

Examples

An acidulent taste
She detected an acidulent note in the sauce, complementing the sweetness of the tomatoes.
Some apples are prized for their acidulent taste, which balances their natural sugars.
The lemonade had an acidulent flavor that was both refreshing and tangy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acidic - having the properties of an acid; tasting sharp or sour
  • antonym: sweet - having a sugary or pleasant taste, opposite of sour
