gentle

verb
to stroke soothingly
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntəl / (BrE)
to stroke or handle soothingly to calm or comfort

Examples

To gentle a horse
He gentled the baby with a soft touch until she stopped crying.
She tried to gentle the frightened horse by stroking its neck.
The trainer carefully gentled the animal before approaching its cage.
