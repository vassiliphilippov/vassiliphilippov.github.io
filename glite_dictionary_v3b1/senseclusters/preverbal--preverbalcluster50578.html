preverbal

adjective
before the verb
7.8
Pronunciation: / priˈvɜrbəl / (AmE), / priˈvɜːbəl / (BrE)
occurring or placed before the verb in a sentence, especially in reference to word order or syntax

Examples

Preverbal position
In French, object pronouns often appear in the preverbal position.
The adverb was placed preverbal to emphasize the action in the sentence.
Some languages require subjects to be in a preverbal slot for correct grammar.

Related Terms
