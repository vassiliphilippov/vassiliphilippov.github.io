elder

noun
an older person
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛldɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛldə / (BrE)
a person who is older, especially someone who has more experience or authority due to their age

Examples

A wise elder
At the meeting, the community's elders shared stories from the past.
She asked her elder for guidance before making a big decision.
You should always respect your elders and listen to their advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: senior - an older or more experienced person
  • antonym: younger - a person who is younger than another
