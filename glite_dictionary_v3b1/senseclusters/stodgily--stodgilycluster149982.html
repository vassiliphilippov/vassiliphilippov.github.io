stodgily

adverb
in a dull or stuffy way
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑdʒɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɒdʒɪli / (BrE)
In a dull, stuffy, or unimaginative manner.

Examples

To write stodgily
Critics described the novel as stodgily written, lacking in excitement or originality.
He stodgily refused to consider any new ideas at the meeting.
The dinner conversation proceeded stodgily, with little enthusiasm or wit.
