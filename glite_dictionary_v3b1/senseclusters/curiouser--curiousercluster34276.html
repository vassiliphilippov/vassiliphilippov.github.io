curiouser

interjection
expression of surprise
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊriəsər / (AmE), / ˈkjʊəriəsə / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise or puzzlement at something increasingly strange or unexpected

Examples

Curiouser! What now
When the cat started talking, she whispered curiouser in disbelief.
He found a second hidden door and muttered curiouser as he examined it.
The events of the day kept getting curiouser, leaving everyone confused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: odd - different from what is usual or expected
  • synonym: strange - unusual or surprising in a way that is unsettling
