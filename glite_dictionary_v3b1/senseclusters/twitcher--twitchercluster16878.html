twitcher

noun
person with muscle twitches
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtʃə / (BrE)
a person who has frequent, small, involuntary muscle movements or twitches

Examples

A twitcher with shaky hands
The twitcher found it difficult to keep his hands still during the meeting.
Doctors examined the twitcher to determine the cause of his involuntary movements.
Despite being a twitcher, she managed to play the piano beautifully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spasmodic - having sudden, involuntary muscle movements
  • antonym: steady - not moving or shaking; stable
