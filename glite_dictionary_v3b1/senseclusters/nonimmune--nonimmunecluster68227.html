nonimmune

adjective
susceptible; lacking immunity
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnɪˈmjun / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːn / (BrE)
Not possessing immunity; susceptible to or likely to be affected by a particular disease or agent.

Examples

Nonimmune pregnant women
Pregnant women who are nonimmune to rubella are at increased risk during outbreaks.
Some patients are nonimmune to measles and must receive vaccination.
Travelers who are nonimmune should take extra precautions when visiting endemic areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immune - protected against a particular disease
