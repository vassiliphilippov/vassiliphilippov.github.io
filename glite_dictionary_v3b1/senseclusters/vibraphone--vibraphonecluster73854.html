vibraphone

noun
a percussion instrument with metal bars and vibrato effect
6.2 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈvaɪ.brə.foʊn/ (AmE), /ˈvaɪ.brə.fəʊn/ (BrE)
a percussion instrument with metal bars and rotating disks in the resonators that produce a vibrato effect, similar to a xylophone

Examples

The vibraphone instrument
She decided to learn the vibraphone because she loved its mellow, ringing tones.
The jazz musician played an intricate solo on the vibraphone during the concert.
You can hear the shimmering sound of the vibraphone in many modern orchestral pieces.
