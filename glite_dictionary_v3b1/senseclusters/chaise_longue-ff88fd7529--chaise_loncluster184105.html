chaise longue

noun
long reclining chair
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪz ˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / ʃeɪz ˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
a long chair designed for reclining, typically with an extended seat to support the legs

Examples

A chaise longue chair
He relaxed on the chaise longue with a good book and a cold drink.
She bought a beautiful chaise longue for the sunroom.
The hotel pool area was lined with elegant chaise longues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lounge chair - an armchair with an extended seat for reclining
