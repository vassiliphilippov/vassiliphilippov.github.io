subdepartment

noun
division of a department
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbdɪˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌsʌbdɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a smaller division within a larger department in an organization

Examples

A subdepartment team
The finance subdepartment handles all internal audits for the company.
Each subdepartment is responsible for a specific set of tasks within the main department.
After the reorganization, a new subdepartment was created to manage digital marketing.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: department - a main division within an organization
  • synonym: section - a part or subdivision of a larger group or organization
