oddment

noun
a leftover piece of cloth or material
Pronunciation: / ˈɑdmənt / (AmE), / ˈɒdmənt / (BrE)
a piece or remnant of material, especially a leftover part of cloth after the main portion has been used or sold

Examples

Fabric oddment
Craft enthusiasts often collect oddments for small sewing projects.
She used a colorful oddment to patch the torn sleeve of her jacket.
The tailor sold several oddments of cloth at a discount.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remnant - a small remaining quantity of something, especially fabric
