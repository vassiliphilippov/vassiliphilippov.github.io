hatemongering

adjective
promoting hatred
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪtˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheɪtˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
encouraging or promoting hatred, especially through speech, writing, or actions that stir up hostility toward a person or group

Examples
