sporophytic

adjective
of the sporophyte
9.9
Pronunciation: /spɑːr.ə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ /; / /spɔːr.ə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ (AmE), /spɒr.ə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ /; / /spɔːr.ə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the sporophyte generation (the diploid stage) in the life cycle of plants and algae, or determined by the sporophyte

Examples
