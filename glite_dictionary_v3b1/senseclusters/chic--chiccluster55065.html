chic

noun
stylish elegance; fashionable style
4.9 2.06/m
Pronunciation: /  Éik / (AmE), /  ÉiÀêk / (BrE)
Stylish elegance and sophistication, especially as shown in fashionable dress or lifestyle.

Examples

Parisian chic
Her effortless chic made her a fashion icon in the city.
The interior's modern chic set it apart from other cafes.
The restaurant exuded an air of chic that attracted a trendy crowd.

Synonyms

  • chicness
    6.5 0.02/m
    chicness names the quality of elegant, fashionable style, while chic describes someone or something that shows refined, fashionable taste.
  • stylishness
    3.6 0.07/m
    stylishness means attractiveness from following current fashions, while chic implies refined, timeless elegance rather than just being trendy.
  • smartness
    3.6 0.00/m
    smartness shows a neat, well-dressed appearance and sometimes cleverness, while chic focuses on sophisticated fashion and an elegant lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: style - a manner of doing or presenting things, especially in fashion
  • antonym: unfashionable - not in keeping with current fashion; lacking style or chic
