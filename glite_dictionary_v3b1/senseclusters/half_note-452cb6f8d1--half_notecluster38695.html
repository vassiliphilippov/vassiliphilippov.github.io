half note

noun
musical note worth half a whole note
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhæf ˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf ˌnəʊt / (BrE)
A musical note with a duration equal to half that of a whole note.

Examples

A half note in music
In 4/4 time, a half note receives two counts.
She held the half note for two beats as instructed in the score.
The melody begins with a half note followed by two quarter notes.
