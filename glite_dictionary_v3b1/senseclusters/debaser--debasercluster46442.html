debaser

noun
one who lowers value or quality
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbeɪsər / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪsə / (BrE)
a person who lowers the quality, value, or character of something, especially through corrupting influence or adulteration

Examples

A debaser of standards
Critics accused the filmmaker of being a debaser of artistic standards.
The dishonest mint official was labeled a debaser of currency.
Throughout history, every debaser of morals has ultimately damaged society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corruptor - a person who corrupts the integrity or purity of something
  • antonym: purifier - a person who makes something pure or improves its quality
