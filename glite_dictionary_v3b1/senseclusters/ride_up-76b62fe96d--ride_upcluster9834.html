ride up

verb
(of clothing) move up on the body
8.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /raɪd ʌp/ /; / /raɪd əp/ (AmE), /raɪd ʌp/ /; / /raɪd əp/ (BrE)
(of a piece of clothing) to move up from its intended position on the body, often causing discomfort or awkwardness.
