bushwalk

verb
to hike in bushland
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃwɔːk / (BrE)
to walk for pleasure through the countryside or natural bushland, especially in Australia.

Examples

To bushwalk in nature
They spent the weekend bushwalking in the Blue Mountains National Park.
Many tourists enjoy bushwalking to experience Australia's unique wildlife and scenery.
Before bushwalking, make sure you have enough water and a map of the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hike - to take a long walk in the countryside or wilderness
  • degree lower: stroll - to walk in a slow, relaxed way
