journey to the afterlife

noun
the path or process to life after death
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːni tə ði ˈæftɚˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːni tə ði ˈɑːftəlaɪf / (BrE)
the process or path believed to lead from earthly life to existence after death, especially as described in religious or mythological contexts

Examples

The journey to the afterlife
For some, rituals at funerals are meant to help the deceased on their journey to the afterlife.
In Egyptian mythology, the journey to the afterlife involves passing through various trials before reaching eternal peace.
Many ancient cultures depicted the journey to the afterlife as a perilous adventure guarded by mystical creatures.
