psychiatric

adjective
relating to psychiatry or mental illness
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkjaɪətrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkɪˈatrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study, diagnosis, or treatment of mental illness and disorders.

Examples

Psychiatric treatment

He spent several weeks in a psychiatric hospital for treatment.
The patient was diagnosed with a rare psychiatric disorder.
