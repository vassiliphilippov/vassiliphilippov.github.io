Ghibelline

adjective
supporting imperial faction
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪbəliːn / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbəliːn / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the medieval Italian Ghibelline faction that backed the Holy Roman Emperor against the Papal party.

Examples

Ghibelline city
The city was known for its strong Ghibelline sympathies during the medieval power struggles.
Many noble families declared themselves Ghibelline to align with the emperor's interests.
The Ghibelline towers still stand as a reminder of the city's historical allegiance.
