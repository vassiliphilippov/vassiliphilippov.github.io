mononitrate

adjective
one nitrate group
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊˈnaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˈnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
containing exactly one nitrate group in its molecular structure

Examples

Mononitrate compound
The scientist synthesized a mononitrate compound for the experiment.
Isosorbide mononitrate is commonly used to treat chest pain caused by heart disease.
Unlike dinitrates, a mononitrate contains only one nitrate group in its structure.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: dinitrate - containing two nitrate groups
  • synonym: nitrate - a compound containing the nitrate group
