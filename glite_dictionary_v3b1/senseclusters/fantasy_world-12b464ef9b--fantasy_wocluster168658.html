fantasy world

noun
imaginary world; made-up universe
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.tə.si wɝːld / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tə.si wɜːld / (BrE)
An imagined place or universe, existing only in the imagination and not in reality.

Examples

Escape to fantasy world
As a child, she often escaped into her fantasy world to avoid the difficulties at home.
He sometimes confuses his fantasy world with reality, especially when under stress.
Many novels are set in an elaborate fantasy world full of magic and mythical creatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imaginary world - a world existing only in the imagination
  • antonym: reality - the world or state of things as they actually exist
