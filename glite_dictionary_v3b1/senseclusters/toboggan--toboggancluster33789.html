toboggan

verb
slide downhill on a toboggan
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈbɑːɡən / (AmE), / təˈbɒɡən / (BrE)
to travel or slide downhill over snow or ice using a toboggan or similar sled

Examples

To toboggan down hills
Every winter, children gather at the hill to toboggan down the snowy slopes.
She loves tobogganing with her friends after a fresh snowfall.
We tobogganed all afternoon until the sun began to set.
