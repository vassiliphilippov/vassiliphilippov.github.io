Dáil

noun
Gaelic assembly
9.9
Pronunciation: /deɪl/ (AmE), /deɪl/ (BrE)
an assembly or council in Gaelic Ireland, historically used for decision-making and settling disputes

Examples

Ancient Dáil council

In ancient Ireland, important laws were discussed and agreed upon at the Dáil.
Each year, the Dáil would meet to address disputes and form new alliances among the tribes.
