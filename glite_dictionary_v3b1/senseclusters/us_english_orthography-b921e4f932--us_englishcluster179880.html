US English orthography

noun
American English spelling system
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː ɛs ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔrˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛs ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the system of spelling conventions used in American English, often distinguished by words like 'analyze', 'center', and 'usable'

Examples

US English orthography rules
Her essay was marked down because she mixed US English orthography with British conventions.
Many textbooks printed in the United States use US English orthography rather than British spellings.
Spellcheckers can usually be set to recognize either US English orthography or British English orthography.
