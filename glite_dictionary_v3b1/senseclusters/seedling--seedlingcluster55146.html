seedling

noun
young plant grown from a seed
6.6 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsiːd.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsiːd.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
A young plant that has grown from a seed, especially before it develops adult leaves or is transplanted.

Examples

A seedling plant
After a week, the tiny seedling began to sprout its first true leaves.
Farmers often protect every new seedling from insects and harsh weather.
The gardener carefully transplanted each seedling into a larger pot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sapling - a young tree, especially one with a slender trunk
