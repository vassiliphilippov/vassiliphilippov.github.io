giga-

x
prefix for billion
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡə / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡə / (BrE)
a prefix meaning one billion (1,000,000,000), used in units of measurement such as gigabyte or gigawatt

Examples

Giga- prefix in gigabyte
A giga- prefix is used in words like gigabyte and gigawatt to indicate a billion units.
The new hard drive can store up to two giga-bytes of data.
Scientists measured the energy output in giga-watts during the experiment.
