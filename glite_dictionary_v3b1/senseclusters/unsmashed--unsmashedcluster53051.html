unsmashed

adjective
not drunk
8.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsmæʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmæʃt / (BrE)
not drunk; sober and unaffected by alcohol.

Examples

Stayed unsmashed
Despite the party's wild atmosphere, she stayed unsmashed all night.
He was proud to be the only unsmashed person at the end of the wedding.
After the concert, most of the group was tipsy, but Mark remained unsmashed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drunk - affected by alcohol; intoxicated
  • synonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
