social evil

noun
harmful societal problem or issue
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈiːvəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈiːvəl / (BrE)
A problem or issue within a society that causes harm or has a negative impact on individuals or the community as a whole.

Examples

Campaigners are working tirelessly to eliminate child labor, which they regard as a persistent social evil.
Racism has long been considered a major social evil in modern societies.
The government launched new policies to combat the social evil of corruption.
