everlasting pea

noun
perennial climbing plant of the Lathyrus genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ pi / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ piː / (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant of the genus Lathyrus, especially Lathyrus latifolius, having clusters of pink or white flowers and cultivated as an ornamental.

Examples

The everlasting pea plant
She planted an everlasting pea near the fence to create a natural screen.
The everlasting pea adds vibrant color to the garden throughout the summer.
Unlike annuals, the everlasting pea returns year after year with little care.
