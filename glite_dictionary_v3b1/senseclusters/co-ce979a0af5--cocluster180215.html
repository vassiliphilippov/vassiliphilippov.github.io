CO

noun
carbon monoxide gas
4.7
Pronunciation: / siː oʊ / (AmE), / siː əʊ / (BrE)
An odorless, very poisonous gas formed by incomplete combustion of carbon; chemical formula CO.

Examples

CO alarm
Firefighters checked the house for dangerous levels of CO after the alarm went off.
Incomplete combustion in car engines can release CO into the atmosphere.
It's important to install CO detectors in your home to prevent poisoning.
