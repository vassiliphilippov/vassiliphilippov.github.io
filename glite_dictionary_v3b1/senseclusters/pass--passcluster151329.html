pass

verb
throw a ball to a teammate
Pronunciation: / pæs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
to throw or send a ball to another player during a game

Examples

To pass the ball
He quickly passed the ball to his teammate, who scored a goal.
The coach told them to pass more often to keep possession.
When pressured by the defense, she passed instead of shooting herself.
