lacking

adjective
not having enough; inadequate
Pronunciation: / ˈlækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlakɪŋ / (BrE)
not having enough of something; inadequate in amount or degree

Examples

Lacking experience
He felt that his cooking skills were still lacking compared to the chef's.
Her answer was lacking in conviction and failed to persuade the audience.
The report was lacking in detail, making it hard to understand the results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adequate - sufficient or satisfactory in quantity or quality
  • synonym: deficient - not having enough of something essential
  • synonym: wanting - lacking or missing a desired quality
