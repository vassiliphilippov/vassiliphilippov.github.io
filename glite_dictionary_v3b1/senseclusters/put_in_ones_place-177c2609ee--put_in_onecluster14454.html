put in one's place

verb
to correct or humiliate someone
1.0 7.2
Pronunciation: / pʊt ɪn wʌnz pleɪs / (AmE), / pʊt ɪn wʌnz pleɪs / (BrE)

Examples

When the student acted out, the teacher calmly put him in his place.
