interdictory

adjective
prohibiting or preventing
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdɪktəri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdɪktəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving prohibition or prevention, especially by an authority

Examples

An interdictory order
The government imposed interdictory measures to stop the illegal trade.
An interdictory order was issued to prevent the construction of the building.
The police took interdictory action to block access to the restricted area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permissive - allowing or permitting something
  • synonym: prohibitive - serving to forbid or prevent something
