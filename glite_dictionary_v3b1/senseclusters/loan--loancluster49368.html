loan

verb
give temporarily with expectation of return
4.9
Pronunciation: / loʊn / (AmE), / ləʊn / (BrE)
to give something, especially money, to someone for a limited period of time, expecting it to be returned

Examples

To loan money
Could you loan me twenty dollars until tomorrow?
She agreed to loan him her textbook for the week.
The museum loaned several paintings to a gallery for a special exhibition.

Related Terms
