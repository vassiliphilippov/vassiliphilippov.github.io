hacker

noun
enthusiastic computer programmer, not malicious
2.8 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhæk.ər/ (AmE), /ˈhæk.ə/ (BrE)
A person who enjoys exploring and writing computer programs, often finding creative or unconventional solutions and sometimes engaging in challenging activities like testing the limits of computer systems, usually without malicious intent.

Examples

Computer hacker skills
At its core, being a hacker is about curiosity and creativity, not causing harm.
Many hackers enjoy learning new programming languages for the mental challenge.
The hacker spent all night developing an elegant solution to the problem.
