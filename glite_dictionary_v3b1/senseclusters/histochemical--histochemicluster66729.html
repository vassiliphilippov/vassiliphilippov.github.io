histochemical

adjective
cell chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstoʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the chemical composition or chemical processes of cells and tissues

Examples

Histochemical analysis
The scientist performed a histochemical analysis to study the distribution of enzymes in the tissue.
A histochemical technique can reveal differences in cell structure that are not visible with standard stains.
Researchers used histochemical methods to detect specific proteins in the brain samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biochemical - relating to the chemical processes in living organisms
