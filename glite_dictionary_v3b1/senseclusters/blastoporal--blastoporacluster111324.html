blastoporal

adjective
relating to a blastopore (embryology)
9.9
Pronunciation: / blæˈstɑːpərəl / (AmE), / blɑːˈstɒpərəl / (BrE)
relating to or associated with a blastopore, the opening that forms during early embryonic development in some animals.
