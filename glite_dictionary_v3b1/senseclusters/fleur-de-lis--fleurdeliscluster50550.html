fleur-de-lis

noun
type of iris plant with showy flowers
9.0
Pronunciation: / flɝː də ˈliː / (AmE), / flɜː də ˈliː / (BrE)
A plant of the iris family with sword-shaped leaves and showy flowers typically composed of three petals and three drooping sepals.

Examples

Fleur-de-lis flower
Botanists often study the unique structure of the fleur-de-lis flower.
She painted a delicate fleur-de-lis next to the roses on the vase.
The garden was filled with colorful fleur-de-lis in full bloom.
