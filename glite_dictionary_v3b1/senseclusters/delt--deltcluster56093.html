delt

noun
shoulder muscle slang
9.1
Pronunciation: / dɛlt / (AmE), / dɛlt / (BrE)
a slang term for the deltoid muscle in the shoulder, often used in fitness contexts

Examples

Strong delt muscle
He injured his delt while lifting heavy weights at the gym.
Strong delts are important for many upper body exercises.
She focused on building her delts to improve her posture and strength.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biceps - the muscle at the front of the upper arm
  • synonym: deltoid - the large, triangular muscle covering the shoulder joint
