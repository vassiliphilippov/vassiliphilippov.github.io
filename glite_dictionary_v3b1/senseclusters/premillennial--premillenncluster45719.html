premillennial

adjective
before thousand-year reign
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəl / (BrE)
relating to the Christian belief that Christ will return before a thousand-year reign of peace on earth

Examples

Premillennial belief
The church's teachings are strongly premillennial, expecting Christ's return before the millennium.
He wrote a book explaining the premillennial interpretation of biblical prophecy.
Many religious conferences discuss premillennial views about the end times.
Some Christian groups hold a premillennial view of the end times.
The book discusses premillennial beliefs and their influence on religious movements.
Debates about premillennial doctrine have shaped church history for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millennial - relating to a period of a thousand years, especially the prophesied reign of Christ
  • antonym: postmillennial - relating to the belief that Christ will return after the thousand-year reign
