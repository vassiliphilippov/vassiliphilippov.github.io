to give someone false or inaccurate information, especially unintentionally

Examples

To misinform people
False rumors can easily misinform large groups of people on social media.
She was misinformed about the meeting's start time and arrived late.
The journalist did not intend to misinform the public with the incorrect data.

Synonyms

  • misstate
    6.2 0.74/m

  • mislead
    3.9 4.51/m

  • misrepresent
    6.9 4.38/m
