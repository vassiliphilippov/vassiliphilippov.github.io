stargaze

verb
to indulge in daydreams or fantasies
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌɡeɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌɡeɪz / (BrE)
to indulge in daydreams or fantasies rather than paying attention to reality

Examples

To stargaze and dream
During boring meetings, Tom often stargazes about traveling the world.
If you stargaze all day, you'll miss what's happening around you.
She tends to stargaze instead of focusing on her studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daydream - to indulge in pleasant thoughts that distract from the present
  • synonym: fantasize - to imagine things, especially things that are unlikely or impossible
