stargaze

verb
to indulge in daydreams or fantasies
5.8 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːr.geɪz/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.geɪz/ (BrE)

Examples

If you stargaze all day, you'll miss what's happening around you.
She tends to stargaze instead of focusing on her studies.
