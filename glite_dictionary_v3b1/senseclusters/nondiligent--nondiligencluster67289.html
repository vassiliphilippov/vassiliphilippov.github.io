nondiligent

adjective
lacking proper care
7.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈdɪlədʒənt / (AmE), / nɒnˈdɪlɪdʒənt / (BrE)
not showing care, attention, or effort in work or duties; not diligent

Examples

A nondiligent worker
The company was criticized for its nondiligent approach to safety inspections.
A nondiligent review of the documents led to several important errors being missed.
Lawyers must avoid being nondiligent when handling sensitive client information.
A nondiligent employee may miss important deadlines and details.
Teachers often notice when a student is being nondiligent with their homework.
The project failed because the team was too nondiligent in their preparations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • antonym: diligent - showing careful and persistent effort or work
  • antonym: industrious - hardworking and persistent
  • synonym: negligent - failing to take proper care in doing something
