neat

adjective
clean and well organized

Synonyms

  • tidy
    1.0 3.41/m
    tidy describes things that are arranged and uncluttered at home, while neat also suggests careful, skilled work.
  • orderly
    2.4 4.59/m
    orderly shows a methodical, planned arrangement like files or desks, while neat can mean both tidy appearance and skilled finish.
  • shipshape
    4.9 0.00/m
    shipshape means very tidy and in good working order on a boat or home, while neat also covers careful skill.
  • well-kept
    1.8 0.82/m
    well-kept describes something kept in good condition over time like gardens or houses, while neat focuses on current tidiness.
  • clean
    3.6 1.69/m
    clean can mean free of dirt or done with smooth, precise skill in action, while neat blends tidiness and careful workmanship.
