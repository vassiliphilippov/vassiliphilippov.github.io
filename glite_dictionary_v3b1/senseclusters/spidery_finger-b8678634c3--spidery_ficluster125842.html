spidery finger

noun
thin, delicate finger
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪ.dɚ.i ˈfɪŋ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪ.dəri ˈfɪŋ.ɡə / (BrE)
a thin and delicate finger, resembling the long legs of a spider

Examples

Spidery fingers touch gently
He offered a handshake with his spidery fingers, cool and gentle to the touch.
Her spidery finger reached out to adjust the delicate chain around her neck.
The artist's spidery finger traced graceful lines across the paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slender finger - a finger that is thin and gracefully narrow
  • antonym: stubby finger - a finger that is short and thick
