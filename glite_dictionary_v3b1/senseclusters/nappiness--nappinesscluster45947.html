nappiness

noun
soft or fuzzy texture
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈnæpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a soft, fuzzy, or slightly rough texture, like that of certain fabrics

Examples

Nappiness of the fabric
The nappiness of the new towel made it perfect for drying off quickly.
She loved the nappiness of her favorite sweater, especially on cold days.
After washing, the nappiness of the blanket became even more noticeable.
