CPM

noun
typing speed measure
Pronunciation: / si pi ɛm / (AmE), / siː piː ɛm / (BrE)
a measure of typing or text-entry speed equal to the number of characters entered in one minute

Examples

Typing CPM score
The typing software displays your CPM after each practice session.
He improved his CPM by practicing daily on a new keyboard.
A high CPM is important for data entry jobs that require fast typing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: WPM - a measure of typing speed in words per minute
