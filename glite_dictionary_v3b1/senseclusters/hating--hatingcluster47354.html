hating

noun
public criticism
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of expressing hatred or strong negative criticism, especially in public or on social media

Examples

Online hating
He was tired of all the hating he saw in the comments section.
Online hating can seriously affect a person's confidence and well-being.
Instead of hating, try to offer constructive feedback and support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criticism - the act of expressing disapproval or negative judgment
  • antonym: praise - the act of expressing approval or admiration
