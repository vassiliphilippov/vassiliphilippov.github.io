carouser

noun
person who enjoys noisy drinking parties
Pronunciation: / kəˈraʊzər / (AmE), / kəˈraʊzə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys lively, noisy drinking parties

Examples

A carouser at parties
His reputation as a carouser often overshadowed his achievements in business.
The old tavern was filled with carousers celebrating late into the night.
Though he was a notorious carouser, he never let his drinking affect his work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reveler - a person who is enjoying themselves in a lively way, especially with drinking
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
