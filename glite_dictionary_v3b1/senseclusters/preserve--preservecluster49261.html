preserve

noun
fruit cooked with sugar to make a sweet spread
6.9
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝv / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
A type of food made by cooking fruit with sugar to prevent it from spoiling, often served as a spread or dessert.

Examples

Strawberry preserve
Apricot preserve goes well with both bread and cheese.
She spread homemade preserve on her toast for breakfast.
The shop sells a wide range of fruit preserve in decorative jars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jam - sweet spread made from fruit and sugar, often less firm than preserve
  • synonym: marmalade - a preserve made from citrus fruit, especially oranges, usually with peel
