standout

adjective
exceptionally impressive
Pronunciation: / ˈstændˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈstændˌaʊt / (BrE)
especially impressive or noticeable compared to others in the same group

Examples

Standout performance
Her standout performance earned her the top prize in the competition.
The standout player on the team scored three goals in the final match.
Among all the candidates, his resume was the clear standout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinary - not special or distinctive; normal
  • synonym: outstanding - extremely good or impressive
  • synonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
