Solvay process

noun
industrial method for making sodium carbonate
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːl.veɪ ˌprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.veɪ ˌprəʊ.sɛs / (BrE)
an industrial method for producing sodium carbonate (soda ash) by reacting sodium chloride with ammonia and carbon dioxide

Examples
