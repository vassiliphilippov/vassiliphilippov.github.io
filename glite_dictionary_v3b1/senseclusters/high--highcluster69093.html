high

noun
state of euphoria from drugs or alcohol
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
A state of euphoria or altered consciousness produced by alcohol, drugs, or another intoxicant.

Examples

Drug-induced high
After the effects wore off, she realized the high was not worth the consequences.
He admitted that he was chasing the same high he felt after his first experience with heroin.
They talked about the dangers of seeking a high through drug use.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: buzz - a mildly euphoric state; slight intoxication
  • degree higher: rush - a sudden strong feeling of euphoria, especially from drugs
