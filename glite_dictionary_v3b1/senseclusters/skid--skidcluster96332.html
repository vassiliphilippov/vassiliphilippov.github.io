skid

noun
sliding tire mark
3.0
Pronunciation: / skɪd / (AmE), / skɪd / (BrE)
a mark or track left on a surface by something sliding, especially the tire of a vehicle that has slipped or lost control

Examples

Skid marks
The police measured the skid left by the car to determine how fast it was going.
After braking suddenly, the truck left a long skid on the highway.
Rain made the road slippery, causing several vehicles to leave skids at the intersection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scratch - a thin mark or line made by scraping or rubbing
  • synonym: tire mark - a mark left on a road by a vehicle's tire
