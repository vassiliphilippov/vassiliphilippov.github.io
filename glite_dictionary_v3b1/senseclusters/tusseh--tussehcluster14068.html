tusseh

noun
oriental moth producing brownish silk
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊs ə / (AmE), / ˈtʌs ə / (BrE)
An oriental moth that produces brownish silk, often used for making tussah silk fabric.

Examples

The tusseh moth
Artisans value the brownish silk spun by the tusseh for its natural texture.
Some luxury fabrics are woven from tusseh silk, prized for its strength.
The tusseh moth can be found in forests throughout Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tussah - type of silk produced from wild silkworms, especially Antheraea moths
