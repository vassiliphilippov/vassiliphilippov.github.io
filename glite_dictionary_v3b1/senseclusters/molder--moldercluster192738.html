Examples

To molder away
Books left in the damp basement began to molder and fall apart.
If neglected, the old wooden fence will molder in the rain.
The abandoned house had started to molder after years of disuse.

Synonyms

  • decay
    4.4 3/m
    decay is a general process of gradual breakdown or ruin over time, while molder stresses slow disintegration from dampness or neglect.
  • rot
    1.4 6/m
    rot is organic breakdown by bacteria or fungi, while molder suggests slow crumbling from moisture, time, or neglect.
  • decompose
    4.4 2/m
    decompose refers to biological breakdown into simpler substances, while molder means slow disintegration often caused by dampness or neglect.
  • mold
    4.4 0.1/m
    mold means become covered in fungus and spoil from dampness, while molder means to decay slowly through damp or neglect.
