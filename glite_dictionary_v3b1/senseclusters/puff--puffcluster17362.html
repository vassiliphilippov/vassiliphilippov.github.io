puff

noun
light hollow pastry
Pronunciation: / pʌf / (AmE), / pʌf / (BrE)
a light, hollow pastry made from choux or puff pastry, often filled with cream or other ingredients

Examples

A cream puff
I tried to make cream puffs for dessert, but the pastry collapsed.
She enjoyed a chocolate puff with her afternoon tea.
The bakery displayed a tray of golden puffs filled with custard.

  • synonym: pastry - baked food made of dough, often light and flaky
