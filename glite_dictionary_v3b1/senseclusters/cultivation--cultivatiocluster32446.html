cultivation

noun
the process of fostering growth or development
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.təˈveɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkʌl.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of encouraging or supporting the growth or development of something, especially plants, crops, or skills

Examples

Cultivation of rice
Modern farming methods have improved the cultivation of rice in many countries.
She dedicated her time to the cultivation of young artists' talents.
The cultivation of bees for honey requires careful management of their hives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fostering - the process of encouraging the development of something
  • antonym: neglect - the failure to care for or foster growth
