hyperchromic anemia

noun
anemia with excess hemoglobin in red cells
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈkroʊmɪk əˈniːmiə /; / ˌhaɪpərˌkroʊmɪk əˈniːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈkrəʊmɪk əˈniːmɪə /; / ˌhaɪpəkˌrəʊmɪk əˈniːmiə / (BrE)
a type of anemia in which red blood cells contain more hemoglobin than normal
