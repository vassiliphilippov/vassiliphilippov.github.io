multisite

adjective
at multiple locations
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt / (BrE)
involving or existing at more than one location or site

Examples

Multisite project
The hospital conducted a multisite clinical trial to gather more comprehensive data.
A multisite project requires careful coordination between different teams.
They implemented a multisite approach to expand their business internationally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-site - involving only one location or site
