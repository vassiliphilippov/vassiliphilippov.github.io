nofollow

verb
to mark links for SEO
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnoʊ.fɑː.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈnəʊ.fɒl.əʊ/ (BrE)
to mark a hyperlink so that search engines do not follow it or give it ranking credit

Examples

To nofollow a link
Webmasters often nofollow links in the comments section to reduce spam.
You should nofollow paid links to comply with search engine guidelines.
The blog automatically nofollowed all external links to protect its ranking.
