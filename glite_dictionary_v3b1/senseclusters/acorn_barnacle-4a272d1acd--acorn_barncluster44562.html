acorn barnacle

noun
a type of cone-shaped barnacle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪkɔrn ˈbɑrnəkəl / (AmE), / ˈeɪkɔːn ˈbɑːnəkl / (BrE)
a small, cone-shaped barnacle that attaches to rocks, especially in intertidal zones

Examples

Acorn barnacles on rocks

The acorn barnacle is commonly found along rocky coasts worldwide.
