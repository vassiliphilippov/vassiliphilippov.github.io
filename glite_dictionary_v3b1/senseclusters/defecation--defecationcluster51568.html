defecation

noun
elimination of solid bodily waste
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of eliminating solid waste from the body through the anus

Examples

The defecation process
Infants may have irregular patterns of defecation in the first months.
Regular defecation is important for healthy digestion.
Some medications can affect the frequency of defecation.
