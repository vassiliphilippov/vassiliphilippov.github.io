Agrostemma githago

noun
a weed with reddish-purple flowers, also called corncockle
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌɡrɑːˈstɛm.ə ɡɪˈθɑː.ɡoʊ / (AmE), / əˌɡrɒˈstɛm.ə ɡɪˈθɑː.ɡəʊ / (BrE)
A European annual plant with large, trumpet-shaped reddish-purple flowers and poisonous seeds, commonly found as a weed in grainfields and roadsides; also known as corncockle.
