cosmopolis

noun
multicultural city
8.7
Pronunciation: / kɑzˈmɑpəlɪs / (AmE), / kɒzˈmɒpəlɪs / (BrE)
a city or society where people from many different countries and cultures live together

Examples

A cosmopolis city
New York is often described as a cosmopolis because of its diverse population.
The city has become a true cosmopolis, attracting people from every continent.
Living in a cosmopolis exposes you to many different languages and traditions.
The author described New York as a modern cosmopolis where cultures from every continent mix daily.
In his speech, the leader envisioned a future cosmopolis united by shared values and cooperation.
Many people dream of a peaceful cosmopolis where everyone is treated equally regardless of origin.
