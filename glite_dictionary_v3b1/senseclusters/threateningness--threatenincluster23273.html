threateningness

noun
quality of being menacing
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɛtənɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of appearing likely to cause harm, danger, or fear

Examples

The threateningness of clouds
The threateningness of the dark clouds made people hurry indoors.
She was startled by the threateningness in his voice during the argument.
The movie's atmosphere was filled with a constant sense of threateningness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmlessness - the quality of not causing harm or danger
  • synonym: menace - a quality or feeling of being dangerous or threatening
