Berean

noun
careful scripture examiner
Pronunciation: / bəˈriːən / (AmE), / bəˈriːən / (BrE)
a member of a group in the Bible known for carefully examining and studying the scriptures

Examples

A Berean reader
The pastor encouraged everyone to adopt a Berean attitude when reading the Bible.
As a Berean, he always checked the scriptures before accepting any teaching.
Many admire the Berean commitment to thoughtful and independent study of religious texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scholar - a person who studies a subject in detail
