Gemini

noun
person born under Gemini zodiac sign
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmɪˌnaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmɪni / (BrE)
A person born between May 21 and June 20, when the sun is in the astrological sign Gemini.

Examples

A Gemini person
Many Geminis are said to be adaptable and outgoing.
My brother is a Gemini and loves meeting new people.
She was born in early June, making her a Gemini by zodiac sign.
