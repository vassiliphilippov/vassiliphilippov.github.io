cappuccino

noun
coffee drink with espresso and steamed milk
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /kæp.ə.ˈtʃiː.noʊ/ /; / /kæp.uː.ˈtʃiː.noʊ/ (AmE), /kæp.ə.ˈtʃiː.nəʊ/ /; / /kæp.uː.ˈtʃiː.nəʊ/ (BrE)
a coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and foamed milk, sometimes topped with cinnamon, cocoa, or whipped cream

Examples

A cappuccino coffee

The barista skillfully poured the steamed milk to create the perfect cappuccino.
