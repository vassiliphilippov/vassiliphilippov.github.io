preventer

noun
asthma control medicine
4.8
Pronunciation: / prɪˈvɛntər / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛntə / (BrE)
a medicine or device taken regularly to prevent or reduce symptoms of a chronic condition, especially asthma

Examples

Asthma preventer medicine
The doctor recommended using a preventer every morning to help control her asthma.
He forgot to take his preventer and noticed his symptoms getting worse.
Many people with chronic asthma rely on a preventer as part of their daily routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - a medicine used to manage chronic symptoms, especially in asthma
  • antonym: reliever - a medicine used to quickly relieve symptoms, especially in asthma
