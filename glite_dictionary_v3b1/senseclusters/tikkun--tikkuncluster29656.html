tikkun

noun
spiritual repair
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪˈkuːn / (AmE), / tɪˈkuːn / (BrE)
a Jewish concept or ceremony focused on spiritual repair, improvement, or rectification

Examples

Tikkun for the world
The rabbi spoke about the importance of tikkun in personal and communal life.
During the festival, participants engaged in a night of study called tikkun to seek spiritual growth.
Many believe that acts of kindness contribute to the ongoing process of tikkun in the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atonement - the act of making amends for wrongdoing or sin
  • antonym: sin - an act considered to be a transgression against divine law
