islanded

adjective
independent from grid
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪləndɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪləndɪd / (BrE)
operating independently from the main electrical grid after being disconnected

Examples

An islanded power system
During the storm, the hospital's power system became islanded to ensure continuous electricity.
Engineers tested the facility in an islanded mode to simulate a grid failure.
Some microgrids are designed to operate islanded when the main grid is down.
