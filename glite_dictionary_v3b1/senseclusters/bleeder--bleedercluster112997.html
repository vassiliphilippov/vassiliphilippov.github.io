bleeder

noun
device for releasing fluid
6.7 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbliÀêd…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàbliÀêd…ô / (BrE)
a person or thing that bleeds, or a device that allows fluid or air to escape from a system

Examples

Brake bleeder valve
The mechanic opened the bleeder to let air out of the brake system.
A faulty bleeder can cause problems in hydraulic equipment.
He was known as a heavy bleeder after even minor injuries.

  • discharger
    6.9 0.22/m
    a discharger releases fluid, gas, or energy from a device, while a bleeder specifically lets fluid or air escape to relieve pressure.
