fertilize

verb
to make (a female) conceive by introducing semen
3.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfɜːr.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈfɜːr.tɪ.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈfɜː.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈfɜː.tɪ.laɪz/ (BrE)
