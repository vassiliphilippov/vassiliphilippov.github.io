heptachlor

noun
toxic insecticide
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛptəˌklɔr / (AmE), / ˈhɛptəˌklɔː / (BrE)
a toxic chemical compound formerly used as an insecticide, now banned in many countries due to health risks
