Dietrich Bonhoeffer

proper noun
German theologian and anti-Nazi dissident
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.trɪk ˈbɑːn.hə.fər/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.trɪk ˈbɑːn.hə.fər/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.trɪk ˈbɒn.hə.fə/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.trɪk ˈbɒn.hə.fə/ (BrE)
