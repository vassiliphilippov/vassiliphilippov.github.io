Dietrich Bonhoeffer

proper noun
German theologian and anti-Nazi dissident
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːtrɪx bɔnˌhœfər / (AmE), / ˈdiːtrɪç ˈbɒnhɜːfə / (BrE)
(1906–1945) A German Lutheran theologian and pastor known for his influential writings on Christianity and his staunch opposition to Nazism, for which he was executed.
