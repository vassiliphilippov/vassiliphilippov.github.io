miss a deadline

verb
fail to meet a due date
Pronunciation: / mɪs ə ˈdɛdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / mɪs ə ˈdɛdˌlaɪn / (BrE)
to fail to submit or complete something by the agreed or required time

Examples

To miss a deadline badly
If you miss a deadline, the application may be automatically rejected.
Our team cannot afford to miss a deadline during this critical project phase.
She panicked when she realized she was about to miss a deadline for her report.
