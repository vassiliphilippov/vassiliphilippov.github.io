lacrimal bone

noun
small bone in the inner eye socket
Pronunciation: / ˈlækrɪməl boʊn / (AmE), / ˈlækrɪməl bəʊn / (BrE)
a small, thin bone forming part of the inner wall of the eye socket, through which the lacrimal ducts pass

Examples

The lacrimal bone socket
Damage to the lacrimal bone can affect tear production and drainage.
During the surgery, the doctor examined the patient's lacrimal bone for fractures.
The lacrimal bone is one of the smallest bones in the human skull.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nasal bone - a bone forming the bridge of the nose
  • synonym: zygomatic bone - the bone that forms the prominence of the cheek
