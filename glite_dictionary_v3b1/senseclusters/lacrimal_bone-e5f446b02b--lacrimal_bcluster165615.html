lacrimal bone

noun
small bone in the inner eye socket
5.7 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlæk.rɪ.məl boʊn/ (AmE), /ˈlæk.rɪ.məl bəʊn/ (BrE)
a small, thin bone forming part of the inner wall of the eye socket, through which the lacrimal ducts pass

Examples

The lacrimal bone is one of the smallest bones in the human skull.
