womanless

adjective
lacking femininity
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənləs / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənləs / (BrE)
lacking qualities, characteristics, or presence traditionally associated with women or femininity

Examples

Womanless home
The old house felt strangely womanless after her departure.
His writing style was described as deliberately womanless by some literary critics.
The club remained womanless for decades before finally admitting female members.
