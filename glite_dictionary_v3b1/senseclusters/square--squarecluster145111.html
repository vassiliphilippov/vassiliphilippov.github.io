square

verb
turn the oar in rowing
Pronunciation: / skwɛr / (AmE), / skweə / (BrE)
(rowing) to turn the oar so that its blade is perpendicular to the water, usually in preparation for the next stroke.

Examples

To square the oar
As the boat approached the catch, the rowers squared their oars in perfect synchrony.
She learned how to square the blade to maximize efficiency on each stroke.
You must remember to square before feathering the oar during drills.
