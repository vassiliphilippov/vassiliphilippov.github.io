CCW

noun
concealed weapon permit
9.9
Pronunciation: / si si ˈdʌbəl.ju / (AmE), / si si ˈdʌbəl.ju / (BrE)

Examples

CCW permit

Many states require a training course before issuing a CCW to applicants.
She always carries her CCW permit when traveling across state lines.
