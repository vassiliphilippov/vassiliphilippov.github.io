Linnaeus

proper noun
Swedish botanist, pioneer of biological classification
8.7
Pronunciation: / lɪˈniːəs / (AmE), / lɪˈniːəs / (BrE)
Carl Linnaeus (1707–1778), Swedish botanist who developed the modern system of naming and classifying organisms.

Examples

Linnaeus system
Linnaeus is often called the father of modern taxonomy.
Many species were first classified by Linnaeus in his influential writings.
The naming system introduced by Linnaeus is still used by scientists today.
