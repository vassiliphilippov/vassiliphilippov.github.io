MOT

noun
UK: annual vehicle safety test
8.1 7.2
Pronunciation: /em.ˈoʊ.tiː/ (AmE), /em.ˈəʊ.tiː/ /; / /mɒt/ (BrE)
(in the UK) a compulsory annual test of an older motor vehicle to check its safety, roadworthiness, and exhaust emissions.

Examples

The MOT test
He sold his old van just before the MOT was due to avoid expensive repairs.
My car is due for its MOT next week, so I hope it passes without any problems.
You can't legally drive your vehicle on the road if the MOT has expired.
