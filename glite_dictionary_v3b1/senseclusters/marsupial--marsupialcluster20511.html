marsupial

adjective
relating to marsupials (pouched mammals)
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈsuːpiəl / (AmE), / mɑːˈsuːpiəl / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to marsupials, a group of mammals that carry and nurture their young in a pouch.

Examples

Marsupial animals
A kangaroo is a well-known marsupial animal.
Scientists studied the marsupial reproductive system in detail.
The koala is a marsupial species native to Australia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marsupial - a mammal of the group Marsupialia, such as a kangaroo or opossum
