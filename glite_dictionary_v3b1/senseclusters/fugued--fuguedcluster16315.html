fugued

adjective
in dissociative trance
Pronunciation: / fjuːgd / (AmE), / fjuːgd / (BrE)
in a state resembling a fugue, often marked by dissociation, trance, or loss of awareness of one's surroundings

Examples

Fugued state
After the accident, she seemed fugued and unable to recall what had happened.
He walked through the city in a fugued state, unaware of his surroundings.
The witness described the suspect as appearing fugued and unresponsive during questioning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - fully aware and attentive
  • synonym: dissociative - experiencing a disconnection from thoughts, identity, or surroundings
