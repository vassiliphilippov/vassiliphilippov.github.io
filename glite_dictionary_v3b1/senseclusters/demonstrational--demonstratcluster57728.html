demonstrational

adjective
of protests
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or intended for a public protest or political demonstration.

Examples

Demonstrational protest march
The city issued permits for the demonstrational march planned for next weekend.
Several demonstrational events were organized to raise awareness about climate change.
Police monitored the demonstrational activities to ensure public safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protest - a public demonstration expressing strong objection
