switchboard

noun
telephone exchange connecting panel
1.6 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪtʃˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a panel with connected circuits used for making manual connections in a telephone exchange or electrical system, especially historically

Examples

Old manual switchboard
Modern telecommunications have replaced the old manual switchboard with advanced digital systems.
She worked as a switchboard operator during the 1940s.
The hotel's switchboard was busy connecting calls all morning.
