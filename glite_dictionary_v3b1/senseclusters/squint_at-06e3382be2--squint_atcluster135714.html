squint at

verb
look with narrowed eyes

Pronunciation: /skwɪnt æt/ /; / /skwɪnt ət/ (AmE), /skwɪnt æt/ /; / /skwɪnt ət/ (BrE)

Examples

I often squint at my computer screen when the sun is shining.
She had to squint at the tiny writing on the label.
