Chicago

noun
a gambling card game
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈkɑɡoʊ / (AmE), / ʃɪˈkɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A gambling card game in which chips are placed on the ace, king, queen, and jack of separate suits (from a separate deck), and players try to win chips by playing cards that match those in the layout.

Examples

Chicago card game
In Chicago, you try to play cards that match those on the table to win chips.
She taught her friends the rules of Chicago during their weekly game night.
We decided to play a round of Chicago at the casino last night.
