biliousness

noun
gastric distress from liver or gall bladder disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪliəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəsnəs / (BrE)
a condition characterized by gastric distress, such as nausea or queasiness, resulting from a disorder of the liver or gall bladder

Examples

Biliousness after eating
He complained of biliousness after eating a heavy meal.
Persistent biliousness should be evaluated by a healthcare professional.
Symptoms such as nausea and biliousness can indicate a liver issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indigestion - difficulty in digesting food, often with discomfort
  • synonym: nausea - feeling of wanting to vomit
