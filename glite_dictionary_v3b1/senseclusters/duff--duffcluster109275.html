duff

noun
slang: insulting label for least attractive friend
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʌf / (AmE), / dʌf / (BrE)
slang (offensive) an insulting term for a person considered the least attractive or least desirable member of a friend group; originally from the acronym 'Designated Ugly Fat Friend'.

Examples

The group duff
She dreaded being the duff in her circle after the makeover.
After the makeover, they stopped teasing him as a duff and he felt much happier.
Teen movies often portray one character as the duff who later becomes confident and popular.

Related Terms

  • antonym: popular - widely liked, admired, or accepted by many people; opposite of someone labelled a DUFF in social contexts
