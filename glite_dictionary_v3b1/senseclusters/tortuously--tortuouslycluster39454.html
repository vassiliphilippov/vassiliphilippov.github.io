tortuously

adverb
in a winding or complex manner
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtɔːr.tʃuː.ə.sliː/ /; / /ˈtɔːr.tʃjuː.ə.sliː/ (AmE), /ˈtɔː.tʃuː.ə.sliː/ /; / /ˈtɔː.tʃjuː.ə.sliː/ (BrE)
