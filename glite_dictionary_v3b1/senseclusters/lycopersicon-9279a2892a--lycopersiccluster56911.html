Lycopersicon

noun
former botanical genus for tomatoes
Pronunciation: / laɪ.kəˈpɜr.sɪ.kɒn / (AmE), / lɪ.kəˈpɜː.sɪ.kɒn / (BrE)
a former genus of flowering plants that included the tomato; now usually classified under the genus Solanum

Examples

The Lycopersicon genus
Botanists now refer to the tomato as Solanum lycopersicum, but it was formerly placed in Lycopersicon.
Several wild species once classified as Lycopersicon are native to western South America.
The name Lycopersicon is often encountered in older botanical literature.
