sexism

noun
discrimination based on sex or gender
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsɛksɪzəm / (BrE)
prejudice, stereotyping, or discrimination, typically against women, on the basis of sex or gender

Examples

Sexism against women
Many laws have been enacted to combat sexism in education and employment.
She spoke out against sexism in the workplace during the meeting.
The company has been criticized for promoting sexism in its hiring practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarianism - belief in the equality of all people, especially in political, social, or economic life
  • synonym: misogyny - dislike of, contempt for, or ingrained prejudice against women
  • synonym: sexist - a person who believes or acts in a sexist way
