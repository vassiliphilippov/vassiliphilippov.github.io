Balearic

noun
person from Balearic Islands
Pronunciation: / ˌbæliˈærɪk / (AmE), / ˌbæliˈærɪk / (BrE)
a person who is from or lives in the Balearic Islands, an archipelago in the Mediterranean Sea belonging to Spain

Examples

A Balearic person
Many Balearics celebrate traditional festivals unique to their islands.
The Balearic welcomed us warmly when we visited Menorca last summer.
As a proud Balearic, she often shares stories about life on Mallorca.
