foot

verb
add up a column of numbers
Pronunciation: / fʊt / (AmE), / fʊt / (BrE)
to add up and total a column of numbers, especially in accounting

Examples

To foot the column
After entering all the expenses, please foot the final column to check the total.
It is easy to make a mistake if you foot the numbers in a hurry.
The accountant footed the figures and found a discrepancy.
