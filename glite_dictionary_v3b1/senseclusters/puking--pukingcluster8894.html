puking

noun
vomiting; ejecting stomach contents
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpjuːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of ejecting the contents of the stomach through the mouth; vomiting

Examples

To puke violently
She couldn't stop puking after the rollercoaster ride.
The nurse quickly assisted the child who was puking in the hallway.
The sight of the spoiled food almost had me puking at the table.
