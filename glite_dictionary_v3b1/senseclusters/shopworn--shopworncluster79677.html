shopworn

adjective
worn or faded from store display
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌwɔrn / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌwɔːn / (BrE)
(of goods) worn, faded, or slightly damaged from being displayed or handled in a shop, but not used.

Examples

Shopworn jacket
Although the jacket was shopworn, it was still in wearable condition.
Customers can sometimes find shopworn items marked down during clearance sales.
The bookstore offered several shopworn copies at a reduced price.

Related Terms

  • antonym: new - not previously used or worn
  • synonym: used - having been used before
