transliterator

noun
script converter
7.9
Pronunciation: / trænzˈlɪtəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / trænzˈlɪtəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or tool that converts text from one writing system or script to another without translating the meaning

Examples

The transliterator converts text
The transliterator converted the Japanese characters into the Roman alphabet for easier reading.
A skilled transliterator is needed to accurately represent names from Cyrillic to Latin script.
The software acts as a transliterator for users typing in different writing systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transcriber - a person who writes down spoken words or converts text into another form
  • antonym: translator - a person who converts text from one language to another
