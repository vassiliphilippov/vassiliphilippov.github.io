mump

verb
to sulk silently
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌmp / (AmE), / mʌmp / (BrE)
to sulk, pout, or remain silent in a sullen or moody way

Examples

To mump in silence
Whenever he lost a game, he would mump in the corner for hours.
She tried not to mump after being told she could not go to the party.
Instead of joining the conversation, he chose to mump quietly at the table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheer - to show happiness or encouragement
  • synonym: pout - to push out the lips in annoyance or sulkiness
  • synonym: sulk - to be silent and bad-tempered out of annoyance or disappointment
