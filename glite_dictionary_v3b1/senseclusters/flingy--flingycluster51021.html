flingy

adjective
easily thrown or scattered
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈflɪŋi / (BrE)
easily thrown, scattered, or likely to slip off when handled or worn

Examples

Flingy clothes fall
The flingy scarf kept slipping off her shoulders during the windy afternoon.
He avoided wearing flingy clothes that might fall off easily while dancing.
Because the fabric was so flingy, it never stayed neatly folded in the drawer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secure - firmly fixed or held in place
  • synonym: slippery - difficult to hold or keep in place
