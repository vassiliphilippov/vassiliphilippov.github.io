nonobvious

adjective
not easily noticed
7.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɑbviəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈɒbviəs / (BrE)
not immediately clear or easily noticed; requiring careful thought to understand or recognize

Examples

A nonobvious answer
The answer to the puzzle was nonobvious and took everyone by surprise.
She made a nonobvious connection between the two seemingly unrelated topics.
Sometimes the best solutions are nonobvious and require creative thinking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obvious - easily seen, recognized, or understood
  • synonym: subtle - not immediately obvious or easy to notice
