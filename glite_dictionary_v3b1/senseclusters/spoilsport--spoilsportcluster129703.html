spoilsport

noun
person who spoils others' fun
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔɪlˌspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈspɔɪlˌspɔːt / (BrE)
A person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment or fun of others.

Examples

A spoilsport at parties
Don't be such a spoilsport; join in the game and have some fun!
He was labeled the spoilsport for refusing to approve the party decorations.
No one likes a spoilsport who ruins the excitement for everyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: killjoy - a person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment of others
