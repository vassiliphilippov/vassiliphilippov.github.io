feast day

noun
day for religious or cultural celebration
Pronunciation: / fiːst deɪ / (AmE), / fiːst deɪ / (BrE)
A day set aside for celebration and feasting, often for religious or cultural reasons.

Examples

The feast day celebration
Many Christians attend church on the feast day of their patron saint.
Special meals are prepared for every feast day in their religious calendar.
The village held a grand festival to mark the feast day each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday - a special day of celebration or commemoration
