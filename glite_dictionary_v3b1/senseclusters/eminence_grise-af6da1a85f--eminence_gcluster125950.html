eminence grise

noun
person with unofficial influence or power
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmɪˈnɑns ˈɡriz / (AmE), / ˌɛ.mɪ.nəns ˈɡriːz / (BrE)
A person who has great influence or power, especially unofficially or behind the scenes, in a particular sphere.
