red-shouldered hawk

noun
North American hawk species with reddish shoulders
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈʃʊldɚd hɑk / (AmE), / rɛd ˈʃəʊldəd hɔːk / (BrE)
A North American hawk (Buteo lineatus) with reddish-brown shoulders, often found in woodlands and near water.

Examples

A red-shouldered hawk soars
A red-shouldered hawk soared overhead, its rufous shoulders catching the afternoon light.
The distinct call of the red-shouldered hawk is often heard in wooded areas of North America.
We spotted a red-shouldered hawk perched high in the maple tree near the river.
