reidentification

noun
identifying again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːaɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːaɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the action or process of identifying someone or something again, often to confirm or correct a previous identification

Examples

Sample reidentification

Due to a labeling error, the museum required reidentification of several artifacts in its collection.
Advances in technology have made reidentification of old photographs much more accurate.
