ghostwrite

verb
write on behalf of another person
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstˌraɪt / (BrE)
to write a book, article, speech, or other text for another person, who is named as the author.

Examples

To ghostwrite a book
He makes a living by ghostwriting novels for other authors.
Many politicians have their speeches ghostwritten by professional writers.
She was hired to ghostwrite the celebrity's autobiography.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coauthor - to write (something) together with another person
