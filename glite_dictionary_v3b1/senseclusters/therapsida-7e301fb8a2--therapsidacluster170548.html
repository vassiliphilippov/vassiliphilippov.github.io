Therapsida

noun
extinct order of mammal-like reptiles
9.9
Pronunciation: /θə.ˈræp.sɪ.də/ (AmE), /θə.ˈræp.sɪ.də/ (BrE)
an extinct order of mammal-like reptiles that lived from the mid Permian to late Triassic, ancestors of mammals

Examples

Fossils of Therapsida
Fossils of Therapsida have been discovered on every continent, indicating their wide distribution.
Paleontologists believe that mammals evolved from certain groups within the Therapsida.
The extinction of most Therapsida coincided with major climate changes at the end of the Triassic period.
