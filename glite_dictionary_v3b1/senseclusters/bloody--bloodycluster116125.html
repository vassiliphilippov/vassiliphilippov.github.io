bloody

adjective
covered with or involving blood
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdi / (AmE), / ˈblʌdi / (BrE)
covered with, stained by, or accompanied by blood; involving or resulting in bloodshed.

Examples

Bloody hands
He walked in with a bloody nose after the game.
It was a bloody battle, leaving injuries on both sides.
The towel became bloody as she wiped her cut.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bloodless - without blood or not involving bloodshed
  • synonym: gory - involving or showing a lot of bloodshed
