mae

verb
Welsh 'is' form
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪ / (AmE), / maɪ / (BrE)
the Welsh third-person singular present form of the verb 'to be', meaning 'is'

Examples

Mae hi'n braf
In the sentence 'Mae hi'n braf heddiw', the word mae means 'it is'.
