base

noun
bottom side of a geometric figure
3.7
Pronunciation: / beɪs / (AmE), / beɪs / (BrE)
the side of a geometric figure, usually a polygon, from which the altitude is measured or constructed

Examples

Base of a triangle
He labeled the base and height on the rectangle before solving the problem.
In a right-angled triangle, the base is often the side lying on the horizontal axis.
The area of a parallelogram is calculated as base times height.

Related Terms

  • synonym: side - a line segment joining two adjacent vertices in a polygon
