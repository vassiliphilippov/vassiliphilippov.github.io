CFO

noun
executive responsible for finances
4.6 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / si …õf o ä / (AmE), / siÀê …õf …ô ä / (BrE)
the executive in a company or organization responsible for managing its financial actions, planning, and reporting

Examples

Company CFO
After a merger, the new CFO implemented stricter budget controls across all departments.
She was promoted to CFO after ten years of experience in finance and accounting.
The CFO presented the quarterly financial report at the shareholders' meeting.

Synonyms

  • chief financial officer
    3.4 0.90/m
    chief financial officer is the formal full job title used in reports and meetings, while CFO is the common abbreviation used in speech and notes.
