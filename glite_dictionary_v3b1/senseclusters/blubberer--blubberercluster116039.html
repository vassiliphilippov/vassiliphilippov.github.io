blubberer

noun
someone who sobs loudly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌbɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈblʌb(ə)rə / (BrE)
a person who weeps and sobs noisily or in an exaggerated way

Examples

A blubberer crying
Instead of being brave, he became a blubberer at the slightest criticism.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the blubberer after the sad movie.
The child was such a blubberer that every small setback brought on loud sobs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crybaby - a person who cries or complains easily or often
  • antonym: stoic - a person who endures pain or hardship without showing their feelings or complaining
