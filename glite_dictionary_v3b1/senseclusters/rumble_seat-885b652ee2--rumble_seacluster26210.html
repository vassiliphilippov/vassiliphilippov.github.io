rumble seat

noun
folding rear seat in old cars
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌm.bəl siːt /; / ˈrʌmbəl siːt / (AmE), / ˈrʌm.bəl siːt /; / ˈrʌmbəl siːt / (BrE)
a folding or externally mounted rear seat in an old car, usually open to the weather, used for extra passengers
