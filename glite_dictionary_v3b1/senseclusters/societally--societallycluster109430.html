societally

adverb
in relation to society
Pronunciation: / səˈsaɪətəli / (AmE), / səˈsaɪətəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to society or social structures as a whole

Examples

A societally important issue
The new law is societally important because it affects everyone in the country.
Certain behaviors are considered unacceptable societally, even if they are not illegal.
The project was designed to be societally beneficial by improving access to education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socially - in a way that relates to society or social interaction
