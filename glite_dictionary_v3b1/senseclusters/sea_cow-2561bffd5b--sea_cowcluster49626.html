sea cow

noun
large aquatic mammal (manatee or dugong)
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌkaʊ / (BrE)
A large herbivorous aquatic mammal with paddle-shaped tail and flipper-like forelimbs, belonging to either of two families: manatees or dugongs.

Examples

The sea cow swims
Early explorers mistook the sea cow for a kind of mermaid due to its unusual appearance.
The sea cow is a gentle creature that grazes on underwater vegetation in shallow coastal waters.
Unlike whales and dolphins, the sea cow is strictly herbivorous and moves slowly through the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dugong - marine mammal closely related to the manatee
  • synonym: manatee - large aquatic mammal, part of the sea cow family
