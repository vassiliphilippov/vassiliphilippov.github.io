brunch

verb
eat a late morning meal
Pronunciation: / brʌntʃ / (AmE), / brʌntʃ / (BrE)
to eat a meal that combines breakfast and lunch, typically in the late morning or early afternoon

Examples

To brunch together
Are you brunching with us after the meeting?
They brunched at a new café by the river last weekend.
We usually brunch with friends on Sundays.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: breakfast - the first meal of the day, eaten in the morning
  • degree higher: lunch - a meal eaten in the middle of the day
