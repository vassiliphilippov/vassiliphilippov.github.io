where

conjunction
introducing place clause
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɛr / (AmE), / weə / (BrE)
used to introduce a clause that gives more information about the place or situation in which something happens

Examples

The house where I lived
This is the house where I grew up and spent my childhood.
They moved to a city where the weather is warm all year round.
She found a café where she could study quietly every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in which - used to refer to the place or situation previously mentioned
