fulfilled

adjective
happy and satisfied
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈfɪld / (AmE), / fʊlˈfɪld / (BrE)
feeling happy and satisfied because your wishes, needs, or goals have been met

Examples

Feel fulfilled at work
After years of hard work, he finally felt fulfilled in his career.
Many people search for ways to feel more fulfilled in their personal lives.
Volunteering at the shelter made her feel truly fulfilled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: content - feeling happy and at ease with what you have
  • antonym: disappointed - unhappy because something you hoped for did not happen
  • synonym: satisfied - pleased because you have what you want or need
