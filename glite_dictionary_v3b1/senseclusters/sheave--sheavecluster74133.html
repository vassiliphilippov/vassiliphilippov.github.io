sheave

verb
to bundle grain stalks
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʃiːv/ (AmE), /ʃiːv/ (BrE)
to bind cut stalks of grain together into a sheaf or bundle

Examples

To sheave the barley
Farmers used to sheave the barley by hand before modern machinery was invented.
After harvesting, the workers sheaved the wheat into neat bundles for drying.
It was common to sheave the grain in the field before transporting it to the barn.
