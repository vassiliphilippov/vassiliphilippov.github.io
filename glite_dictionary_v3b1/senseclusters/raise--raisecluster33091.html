raise

verb
make dough puff up with leaven
7.9
Pronunciation: / reɪz / (AmE), / reɪz / (BrE)
to cause dough or bread to become light and puffed up through fermentation or the use of a leavening agent

Examples

To raise bread
If you forget to add yeast, the bread will not raise properly.
The baker left the dough overnight to raise before baking it in the morning.
Warm temperatures help the yeast raise the dough more quickly.
