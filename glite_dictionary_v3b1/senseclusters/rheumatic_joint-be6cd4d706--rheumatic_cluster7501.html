rheumatic joint

noun
joint affected by rheumatism
Pronunciation: / ruˈmætɪk dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ruːˈmatɪk dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
A joint that is inflamed or affected by rheumatism, often causing pain and stiffness.

Examples

A rheumatic joint pain
Physical therapy brought some relief to her most affected rheumatic joint.
She experiences severe pain in every rheumatic joint during cold weather.
The doctor examined his rheumatic joint for signs of inflammation and swelling.
