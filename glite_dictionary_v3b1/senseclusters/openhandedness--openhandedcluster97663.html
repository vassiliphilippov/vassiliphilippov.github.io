openhandedness

noun
generosity; willingness to give freely
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpənˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊpənˈhændɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very generous and willing to give freely

Examples

Her openhandedness showed
Her openhandedness was evident in the way she always helped those in need.
His openhandedness made him a beloved figure in the community.
The foundation owes its success to the openhandedness of its donors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generosity - the quality of being kind and generous
  • antonym: stinginess - unwillingness to give or spend; meanness
