on the spur of the moment

adverb
without prior planning; suddenly
7.3 3.6
Pronunciation: /ɑːn ðə spɜːr əv ˈmoʊ.mənt/ (AmE), /ɒn ðə spɜː əv ˈməʊ.mənt/ (BrE)
If you do something on the spur of the moment, you do it suddenly, without planning or thinking about it beforehand.

Examples

Act on the spur of the moment
He proposed on the spur of the moment, surprising everyone at the party.
I bought the tickets on the spur of the moment and didn’t tell anyone.
We drove to the beach on the spur of the moment after work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberately - done consciously and intentionally, with planning
  • synonym: impulsively - in a way that is done suddenly without thinking
