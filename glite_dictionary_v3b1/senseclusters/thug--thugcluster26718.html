thug

verb
to act aggressively
8.1
Pronunciation: / θʌɡ / (AmE), / θʌɡ / (BrE)
to act in a violent, threatening, or criminal way, often to intimidate others

Examples

To thug around
Some teenagers were thugging around the park, scaring the younger kids.
He was arrested after thugging his way through the neighborhood last night.
The group was known for thugging and causing trouble wherever they went.
The gang tried to thug their way into the club after being denied entry.
He was accused of thugging his classmates for their lunch money.
Some people thug around the neighborhood, causing fear among residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - to use force against someone or something
  • synonym: bully - to use strength or power to harm or intimidate those who are weaker
  • synonym: intimidate - to frighten or threaten someone, often to make them do what you want
  • antonym: protect - to keep someone or something safe from harm
