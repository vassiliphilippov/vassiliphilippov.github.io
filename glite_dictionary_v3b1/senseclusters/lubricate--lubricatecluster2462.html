Synonyms

  • lube
    6.4 0.4/m
    lube is an informal verb people use for quickly applying lubricant to parts, while lubricate is the formal technical word.
  • grease
    2.8 3/m
    grease means putting thick grease on moving parts to keep them smooth, while lubricate covers any substance used to reduce friction.
  • oil
    4.1 2/m
    oil means putting liquid oil on objects for treatment, cooking, or ritual, while lubricate focuses on reducing friction.
