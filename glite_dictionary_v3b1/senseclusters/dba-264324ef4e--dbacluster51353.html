DBA

noun
an assumed business name (legal)
8.3 0.76/m
Pronunciation: / di.bi.e…™ / (AmE), / diÀê.biÀê.e…™ / (BrE)
a name used by a corporation to conduct business, which is different from its legal registered name

Examples

A DBA name
Many small businesses operate under a DBA instead of their official company name.
The company registered the DBA 'Sunny Landscapes' for its gardening services.
When signing contracts, remember to include both the corporation's legal name and its DBA.

Synonyms

  • doing business as
    5.6 0.03/m
    doing business as is the full spoken phrase for a trade name, while DBA is the common abbreviated form used on forms.
  • assumed name
    6.9 0.04/m
    an assumed name is a legal term used in some jurisdictions for a trade name, while DBA is the common abbreviation on filings.
  • fictitious name
    7.8 0.04/m
    a fictitious name is the legal label saying the business name is not the registered name, while DBA is the familiar abbreviation used.
