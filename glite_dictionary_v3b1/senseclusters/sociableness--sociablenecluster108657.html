sociableness

noun
quality of being sociable
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəbəlnɪs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sociable; the tendency or disposition to seek or enjoy the company of others

Examples

Natural sociableness
Despite his natural sociableness, he still enjoys quiet evenings alone.
Her sociableness made her popular at every event she attended.
The sociableness of the staff creates a welcoming atmosphere in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociability - the quality of being sociable or disposed to seek companions
  • antonym: unsociableness - the quality of not being sociable; unsociability
