craniofacial

adjective
of skull and face
Pronunciation: / ˌkreɪniəˈfeɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌkreɪniəˈfeɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to both the skull and the face

Examples

Craniofacial surgery
The surgeon specializes in craniofacial reconstruction for accident victims.
Researchers are studying craniofacial development in children with genetic disorders.
A craniofacial anomaly can affect both appearance and function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facial - relating to the face
