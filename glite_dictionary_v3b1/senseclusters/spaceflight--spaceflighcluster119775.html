spaceflight

noun
flight into outer space
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsˌflaɪt / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌflaɪt / (BrE)
the act of traveling into or through outer space, especially beyond the Earth's atmosphere

Examples

Commercial spaceflight
Advancements in technology have made commercial spaceflight a real possibility for civilians.
The first manned spaceflight was launched by the Soviet Union in 1961.
The risks associated with spaceflight require extensive training and preparation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: space travel - the act of traveling outside the Earth's atmosphere
