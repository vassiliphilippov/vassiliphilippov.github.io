hemangioma simplex

A soft, red, benign birthmark caused by a cluster of blood vessels near the skin's surface.

Examples

A hemangioma simplex birthmark
A hemangioma simplex often appears at birth and is usually harmless.
Doctors reassured the parents that the hemangioma simplex did not require immediate treatment.
The pediatrician diagnosed the infant's red mark as a hemangioma simplex that would likely fade with time.
