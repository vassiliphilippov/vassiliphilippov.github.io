top

verb
surpass; exceed in quality or amount
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
to be better than, surpass, or rank first

Examples

To top the list
He always tries to top his previous achievements.
Her new novel topped the bestseller charts for weeks.
Their latest score topped our team's record by ten points.
Despite fierce competition, our team topped the national rankings this year.
She worked hard to top her class every semester.
The movie topped the box office charts for three weeks in a row.

  • synonym: lead - to be in charge or ahead of others
  • synonym: surpass - to be greater than; exceed
  • antonym: trail - to fall behind or be outperformed by others
