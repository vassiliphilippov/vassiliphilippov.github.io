handsome devil

noun
charming or attractive man (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈhænsəm ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈhansəm ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
an informal term for a man who is especially attractive or charming, often with a playful or teasing sense

Examples

The handsome devil smiles
Everyone at the office adores that handsome devil from accounting.
When he smiled, he looked like a real handsome devil.
You can't trust a handsome devil like Ben with your secrets!
