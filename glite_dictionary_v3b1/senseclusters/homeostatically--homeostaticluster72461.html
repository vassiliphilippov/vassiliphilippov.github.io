homeostatically

adverb
in a homeostatic manner
2.6 9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmi.oʊˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiəʊˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that maintains or relates to homeostasis, especially with reference to the regulation of internal conditions in living organisms.

Examples

To homeostatically regulate
Sleep patterns are homeostatically regulated to meet physiological needs.
Some drugs act homeostatically to balance blood glucose levels.
The body responds homeostatically to changes in temperature by sweating or shivering.
