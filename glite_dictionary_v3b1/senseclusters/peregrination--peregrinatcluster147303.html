peregrination

noun
traveling or wandering, especially long or deliberate
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrəɡrɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪɡrɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of traveling or wandering from place to place, especially over a long distance or in a deliberate way

Examples

Long peregrination
After a long peregrination, she finally returned home with many stories to tell.
His peregrination across Europe lasted over a year and took him to dozens of cities.
The novel recounts the hero’s peregrination through strange and distant lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journey - an act of traveling from one place to another
  • synonym: wander - to move about aimlessly or without a fixed course or destination
