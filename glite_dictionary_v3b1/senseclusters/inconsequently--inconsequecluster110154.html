inconsequently

adverb
in an illogical or irrelevant manner
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑnsəkwəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that lacks logical connection or significance; without consequence or relevance to the matter at hand

Examples

Answer inconsequently
He answered the question inconsequently, ignoring its relevance to the meeting.
She switched topics inconsequently, leaving everyone puzzled by her sudden change of subject.
The discussion drifted inconsequently without reaching any conclusion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consequently - as a result; with logical consequence
  • synonym: irrelevantly - in a manner that is not connected to the subject
