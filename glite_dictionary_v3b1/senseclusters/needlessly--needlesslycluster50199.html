without necessity; in a way that is not needed or justified

Examples

To needlessly complicate
He needlessly complicated the process by insisting on more paperwork.
She became upset after he needlessly brought up an old argument.
The company was criticized for needlessly wasting valuable resources.

Synonyms

  • unnecessarily
    3.7 3.66/m
    unnecessarily describes doing something that is not needed or required, while needlessly highlights lack of necessity or justification.
  • gratuitously
    7.9 0.72/m
    gratuitously describes doing something without good reason and often with criticism, while needlessly stresses that it is not needed.
  • superfluously
    8.1 0.03/m
    superfluously means doing more than is needed or extra, while needlessly focuses on actions that are not justified.
  • unwarrantably
    9.9 0.00/m
    unwarrantably describes actions done without reasonable grounds and sounds formal, while needlessly is the everyday word for not needed.
