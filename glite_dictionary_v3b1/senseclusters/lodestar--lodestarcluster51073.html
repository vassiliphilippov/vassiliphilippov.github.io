lodestar

noun
model or guiding principle
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊd.stɑr / (AmE), / ˈləʊd.stɑː / (BrE)
a person or thing that serves as an inspiration or guide; a guiding principle

Examples

A moral lodestar
His integrity has long been the lodestar for our company values.
Honesty remains the lodestar of their organization.
She used her mother's advice as a lodestar throughout her career.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antithesis - the direct or exact opposite
  • synonym: guiding light - a person who influences or inspires others
  • synonym: model - something to be imitated or followed
