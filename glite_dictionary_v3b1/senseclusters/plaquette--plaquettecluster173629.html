plaquette

noun
small decorative plaque
Pronunciation: / plæˈkɛt / (AmE), / plæˈkɛt / (BrE)
a small, flat decorative plaque, usually made of metal or ceramic, often used as an ornament or collectible

Examples

Bronze plaquette
The collector proudly displayed a rare bronze plaquette from the Renaissance period.
Each plaquette in the series was crafted with intricate designs and fine details.
She received a ceramic plaquette as a souvenir from her trip to Italy.

  • synonym: plaque - a flat, typically ornamental tablet or plate
