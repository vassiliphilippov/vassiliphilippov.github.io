epitaxial

adjective
crystal layer growth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈtæksiəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈtæksiəl / (BrE)
describing a thin crystal layer grown on a substrate so that its structure matches the underlying material

Examples

An epitaxial layer
The epitaxial layer ensures that the electronic properties of the device are consistent.
Researchers studied the epitaxial growth of thin films on various substrates.
High-quality epitaxial wafers are essential for advanced semiconductor manufacturing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crystalline - having the structure and form of a crystal
