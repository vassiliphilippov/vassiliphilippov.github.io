proc

verb
to trigger effect
Pronunciation: / prɑk / (AmE), / prɒk / (BrE)
to trigger a special effect, ability, or event in a video game, often automatically or by chance.

Examples

To proc a bonus
If you use this weapon, its bonus damage can proc randomly during a fight.
The healing effect will proc when your health drops below 20 percent.
Some players build their characters to increase the chance that special abilities proc more often.

Related Terms

  • synonym: activate - to make something start working or happen
  • antonym: deactivate - to make something stop working or turn off
