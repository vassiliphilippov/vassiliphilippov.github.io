storm window

noun
external protective window
9.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /stɔːrm ˈwɪn.doʊ/ (AmE), /stɔːm ˈwɪn.dəʊ/ (BrE)
a second window installed on the outside of an existing window to protect against bad weather or cold temperatures

Examples

A storm window outside

Strong winds rattled the storm window during last night's thunderstorm.
We decided to install a storm window to keep the house warmer in winter.
