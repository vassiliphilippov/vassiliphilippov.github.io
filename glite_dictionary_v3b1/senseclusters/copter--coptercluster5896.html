copter

noun
rotorcraft or drone
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑptər / (AmE), / ˈkɒptə / (BrE)
an informal word for a helicopter, an aircraft that flies using rotating blades

Examples

A copter flies
He piloted his new copter over the park to capture aerial photos.
The children watched the small copter hover above their backyard.
She learned to control a copter for her photography hobby.
The rescue copter arrived quickly to help the stranded hikers.
We watched a police copter circle above the highway after the accident.
Several copters hovered above the city during the parade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drone - an unmanned aerial vehicle controlled remotely
  • synonym: helicopter - an aircraft with rotating blades that can take off and land vertically
