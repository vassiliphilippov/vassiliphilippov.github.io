uncirculated

adjective
never used currency
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈsɜːrkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsɜːkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
describes a coin or banknote that has never been used in transactions and remains in perfect condition

Examples

Uncirculated coin
The rare uncirculated coin sold for a much higher price than its face value.
Collectors often seek uncirculated banknotes because they are free from any signs of wear.
He proudly displayed his uncirculated coins in a protective case to keep them pristine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: circulated - used in transactions and no longer in perfect condition
  • synonym: mint - in perfect, unused condition
