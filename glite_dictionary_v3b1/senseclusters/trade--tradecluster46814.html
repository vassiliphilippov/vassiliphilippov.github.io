trade

noun
people working in a skilled occupation
Pronunciation: / treɪd / (AmE), / treɪd / (BrE)
the people who work in a particular skilled occupation or business, especially manual or specialized trades

Examples

The trade community
Many in the trade believe these regulations are necessary to uphold professional standards.
She has earned the respect of everyone in her trade.
The new tools have been widely adopted by the carpentry trade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craft - the people engaged in a skilled trade or occupation
  • antonym: profession - an occupation, especially one requiring advanced education or training
