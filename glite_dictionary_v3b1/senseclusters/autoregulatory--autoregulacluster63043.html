autoregulatory

adjective
self-regulating process
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtoʊˈrɛɡjələtɔri / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˈrɛɡjʊlətəri / (BrE)
describing a process or mechanism that regulates itself automatically without external control

Examples

Autoregulatory process
The kidney's autoregulatory response helps maintain stable blood flow despite changes in blood pressure.
Many biological systems rely on autoregulatory mechanisms to keep conditions within a healthy range.
Researchers studied the autoregulatory properties of the heart to understand how it adapts to stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • synonym: self-regulating - able to control or adjust itself without outside help
