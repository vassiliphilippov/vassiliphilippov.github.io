munchkin

noun
rule-exploiting gamer
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌntʃkɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃkɪn / (BrE)
a player in role-playing games who tries to gain maximum power or rewards by exploiting the rules, often ignoring teamwork or story

Examples

A munchkin player
In our group, a munchkin always tries to find loopholes in the rules to get stronger.
The game master warned that being a munchkin could ruin the fun for everyone else.
Instead of working with the team, the munchkin focused only on collecting the best weapons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powergamer - a player who focuses on maximizing their character's power
  • antonym: roleplayer - a player who focuses on story and character development
