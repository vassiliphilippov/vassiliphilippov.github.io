blepharospasm

noun
involuntary eyelid muscle spasm
9.9
Pronunciation: / blɛf.ə.roʊˌspæz.əm / (AmE), / blɛf.ə.rəʊˌspaz.əm / (BrE)
A condition in which the muscles of the eyelid involuntarily spasm, causing obstruction or closure of the eye.

Examples

Severe blepharospasm condition
Severe blepharospasm can interfere with daily activities such as reading or driving.
She was diagnosed with blepharospasm after experiencing frequent involuntary eyelid closures.
Treatment for blepharospasm may include medication or Botox injections.
