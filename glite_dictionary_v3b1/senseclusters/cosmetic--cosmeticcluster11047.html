cosmetic

noun
beauty product for the body
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /kɑːz.ˈme.tɪk/ /; / /kəz.ˈme.tɪk/ (AmE), /kɒz.ˈme.tɪk/ /; / /kəz.ˈme.tɪk/ (BrE)
A substance or preparation applied to the body, especially the face, to improve or change its appearance.

Examples

A cosmetic product
Many people use at least one cosmetic daily, such as lotion or lipstick.
She always keeps her cosmetic bag with her for quick touch-ups.
The new law requires all cosmetic products to be tested for safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: makeup - cosmetics applied to the face to enhance appearance
  • antonym: medicine - a substance used for treating illness, antonym in the context of function (health vs. appearance)
  • synonym: toiletry - an article used in washing and taking care of one's body
