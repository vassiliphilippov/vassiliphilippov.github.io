makeover

noun
complete transformation or renovation
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkˌəʊvə / (BrE)
A complete transformation or renovation of something, especially to improve its appearance or function.

Examples

A room makeover
After her room's makeover, she barely recognized it.
The company is planning a makeover of its brand image next year.
The old hotel received a stunning makeover and now looks completely modern.
