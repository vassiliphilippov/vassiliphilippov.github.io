roustabout

noun
manual laborer on a ship
8.2 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈraʊ.stə.baʊt/ (AmE), /ˈraʊ.stə.baʊt/ (BrE)
a person who does hard, unskilled physical work, especially in places like ships, circuses, or oil fields
