roustabout

noun
manual laborer on a ship
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊstəˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈraʊstəˌbaʊt / (BrE)
a person who does hard, unskilled physical work, especially in places like ships, circuses, or oil fields

The traveling circus hired several roustabouts to help set up the tents and equipment.
He worked as a roustabout on an oil rig before starting his own business.

He worked as a roustabout on the oil rig, handling heavy equipment every day.
