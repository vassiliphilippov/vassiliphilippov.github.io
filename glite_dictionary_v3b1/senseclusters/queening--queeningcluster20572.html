queening

noun
raising a queen bee
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of introducing or raising a queen bee in a beehive, especially in beekeeping

Examples

Queening a hive
The beekeeper carefully managed the queening process to ensure a healthy hive.
Successful queening can improve the productivity and temperament of a bee colony.
After queening, the new queen began laying eggs within a few days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: requeening - replacing an old queen bee with a new one in a hive
