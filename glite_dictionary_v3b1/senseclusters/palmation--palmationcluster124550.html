palmation

noun
hand-like spreading
9.9
Pronunciation: / pælˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / pælˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the condition of having parts, such as fingers or lobes, spread out like the fingers of a hand

Examples

Palmation of fingers
The palmation of a duck's feet helps it swim efficiently in water.

Frogs often have strong palmation between their toes to aid in swimming.
