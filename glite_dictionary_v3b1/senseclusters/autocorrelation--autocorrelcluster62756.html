autocorrelation

noun
statistical self-similarity
8.6
Pronunciation: /ɑː.toʊ.kɑːr.ə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɔː.toʊ.kɔːr.ə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɔː.təʊ.kɒ.rə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a statistical measure of how much a variable or set of data is related to itself at different times or positions
