forward-leaning

adjective
proactive or innovative in approach
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərd ˌliːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd ˌliːnɪŋ / (BrE)
adopting or showing a proactive, innovative, or assertive attitude or approach, especially in strategy or policy

Examples

A forward-leaning mindset
A forward-leaning mindset is crucial for staying competitive in today's fast-paced world.
Her forward-leaning leadership style fosters creativity and rapid decision-making.
The company adopted a forward-leaning strategy to address technological changes in the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proactive - taking the initiative to act in advance rather than reacting later
  • antonym: reactive - responding to events after they have happened
