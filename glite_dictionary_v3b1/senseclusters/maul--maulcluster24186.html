maul

verb
injure badly by beating or tearing
6.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /mɔːl/ (AmE), /mɔːl/ (BrE)
to injure, damage, or spoil something or someone by violent or rough handling (such as an animal attack or rough treatment)

Examples
