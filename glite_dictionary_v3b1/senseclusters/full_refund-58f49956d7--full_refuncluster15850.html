full refund

noun
complete repayment of purchase amount
Pronunciation: / fʊl rɪˈfənd / (AmE), / fʊl rɪˈfʌnd / (BrE)
the complete return of money paid for a product or service, usually after a return or complaint

Examples

A full refund
After discovering the defect, she demanded a full refund from the company.
If you're not satisfied, you can return the item for a full refund.
The airline offered a full refund when the flight was cancelled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: partial refund - a refund of only part of the purchase amount
