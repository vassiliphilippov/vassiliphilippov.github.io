maquila

noun
processing fee or share
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈkiːlə / (AmE), / məˈkiːlə / (BrE)
a portion of goods or a fee taken as payment for processing agricultural products, especially grain milling

Examples

Maquila of flour
The miller received his maquila in flour after grinding the villagers' wheat.
Farmers agreed to pay a small maquila for the processing of their corn.
Traditionally, the maquila was taken as a share of the finished product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commission - a fee paid to someone for a service or transaction
  • synonym: toll - a fee paid for a service, especially for using a facility
