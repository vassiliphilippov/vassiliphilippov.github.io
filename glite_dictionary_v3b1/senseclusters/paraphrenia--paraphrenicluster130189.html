paraphrenia

noun
form of schizophrenia with delusions
8.6 9.9
Pronunciation: /pær.ə.ˈfriː.ni.ə/ (AmE), /pær.ə.ˈfriː.ni.ə/ (BrE)
a form of schizophrenia marked by persistent delusions, often with relatively preserved personality and social functioning

Examples

Persistent paraphrenia delusions
People with paraphrenia may appear socially adept despite having persistent delusions.
Recent studies suggest that paraphrenia often emerges in later life compared to other forms of schizophrenia.
The psychiatrist diagnosed the elderly patient with paraphrenia due to her elaborate delusional system.
