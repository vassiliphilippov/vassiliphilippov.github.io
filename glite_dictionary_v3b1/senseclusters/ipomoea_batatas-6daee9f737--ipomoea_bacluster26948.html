Ipomoea batatas

noun
species of sweet potato vine
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪpəˈmoʊ.i.ə bəˈteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌɪpəˈməʊ.i.ə bəˈtɑːtəs / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the morning glory family widely cultivated for its large, sweet, orange-fleshed tuberous root, commonly known as the sweet potato.
