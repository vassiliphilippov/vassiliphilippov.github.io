cheese slicer

noun
tool for slicing cheese thinly
Pronunciation: / tʃiz ˈslaɪsɚ / (AmE), / tʃiːz ˈslaɪsə / (BrE)
a tool with a flat, sharp-edged blade used to cut thin, even slices of semi-hard or hard cheeses

Examples

A cheese slicer tool
Every kitchen drawer should have a cheese slicer for quick and easy cheese preparation.
She used a cheese slicer to cut perfect, even slices for the sandwich.
The cheese slicer is ideal for slicing blocks of cheddar or gouda.
