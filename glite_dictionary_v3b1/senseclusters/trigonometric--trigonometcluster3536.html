trigonometric

adjective
relating to trigonometry
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪɡ.ə.nəˈmɛ.trɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrɪɡ.ən.əˈmɛ.trɪk / (BrE)
relating to or based on the principles of trigonometry, the branch of mathematics dealing with the relationships between the angles and sides of triangles

Examples

A trigonometric function
A trigonometric function such as sine or cosine is fundamental to studying triangles.
He used trigonometric equations to solve the geometrical problem.
In calculus, students often work with trigonometric identities during integration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trigonometry - the branch of mathematics dealing with triangles
