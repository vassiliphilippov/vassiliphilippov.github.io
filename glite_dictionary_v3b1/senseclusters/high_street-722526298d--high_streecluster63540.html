high street

noun
main street with shops and businesses (Britain)
Pronunciation: / haɪ strit / (AmE), / haɪ striːt / (BrE)
the main street in a town or city where most of the shops, banks, and other businesses are located, especially in Britain

Examples

The high street shops
Many independent shops still thrive on the high street despite competition from big supermarkets.
She works at a clothing store on the high street in the town centre.
The annual parade will march down the high street this Saturday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: main street - the principal street of a town, especially in the US
