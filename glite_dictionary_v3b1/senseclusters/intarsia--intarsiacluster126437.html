intarsia

noun
knitting color technique
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɑrsiə / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɑːsiə / (BrE)
a knitting technique for creating patterns by using separate blocks of color, each with its own yarn

Examples

Intarsia sweater pattern

Learning intarsia allows knitters to create pictures and patterns in their projects.
She admired the intricate intarsia work on the handmade scarf at the craft fair.
