Adolf Hitler

proper noun
German Nazi dictator, World War II
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈædɑlf ˈhɪtlɚ / (AmE), / ˈadɒlf ˈhɪtlə / (BrE)
(1889–1945) The German Nazi dictator who led Germany during World War II and was responsible for the Holocaust.
