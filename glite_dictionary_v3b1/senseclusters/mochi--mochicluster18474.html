mochi

noun
South Asian shoemaker caste
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtʃi / (AmE), / ˈməʊtʃi / (BrE)
a member of a South Asian caste, especially in India and Pakistan, traditionally engaged in shoemaking or leatherworking

Examples

Mochi caste member
The mochi community has preserved its traditional skills for generations.
Many mochi in the region are known for their high-quality leather goods.
He learned the art of shoemaking from his father, who is a respected mochi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cobbler - a person who makes or repairs shoes
  • synonym: leatherworker - a person who works with leather
