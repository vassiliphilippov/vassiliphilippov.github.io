sizzler

noun
hot dish or event
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪzlə / (BrE)
a dish served on a hot, sizzling plate, or something that is very hot or exciting

Examples

Chicken sizzler plate
The restaurant is famous for its chicken sizzler served on a steaming hot plate.
Yesterday was a real sizzler, with temperatures reaching over 100 degrees.
The new movie is a sizzler that kept the audience on the edge of their seats.
