trained

adjective
having been taught or disciplined
1.9
Pronunciation: / treɪnd / (AmE), / treɪnd / (BrE)
having developed skill, behavior, or discipline through training or practice

Examples

A trained service animal
After weeks of practice, the dog became a fully trained service animal.
Only trained professionals are allowed to operate the laboratory equipment.
The trained chef prepared dishes with remarkable efficiency and creativity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skilled - having skill, especially as a result of training or experience
  • antonym: untrained - not having been trained or disciplined, lacking experience
