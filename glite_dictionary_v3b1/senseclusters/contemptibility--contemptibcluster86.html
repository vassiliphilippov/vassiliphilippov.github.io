contemptibility

noun
quality of being contemptible
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˌtɛmptəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / kənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪləti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being deserving of contempt or considered worthless and lacking in higher values.

Examples

The contemptibility of actions
Few were surprised by the contemptibility displayed by the corrupt officials.
His blatant dishonesty only added to the contemptibility of his actions.
The film explores the contemptibility of greed and selfishness in modern society.
