shake on

verb
finalize by shaking hands
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk ɑn / (AmE), / ʃeɪk ɒn / (BrE)
to confirm an agreement or finalize a deal by shaking hands

Examples

To shake on a deal
After much negotiation, the two companies decided to shake on the partnership.
If you accept my offer, we can shake on it right now.
They shook on the agreement with big smiles and firm handshakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agree - to have the same opinion or accept a proposal
  • antonym: disagree - to have a different opinion or refuse an agreement
