ironless

adjective
does not need ironing
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnləs / (AmE), / ˈaɪənləs / (BrE)
not requiring ironing; designed to remain smooth and unwrinkled without the use of a clothes iron

Examples

Ironless shirt
He prefers ironless trousers because they save time on laundry days.
The hotel provided ironless pillowcases that always looked neat and fresh.
Many travelers pack ironless clothing to avoid wrinkles during long trips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crease-resistant - resistant to forming creases or wrinkles
  • synonym: wrinkle-free - not likely to get creased or wrinkled
