cultural chauvinism

noun
belief in cultural superiority
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃərəl ʃoʊˈvɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈʃəʊvɪnɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
