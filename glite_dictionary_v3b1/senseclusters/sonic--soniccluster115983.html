sonic

adjective
relating to audible sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to audible sound or the frequencies that can be heard by the human ear.

Examples

Sonic waves
Bats use sonic frequencies to navigate and locate their prey at night.
The concert featured a light show synchronized with sonic effects.
The scientists measured the sonic vibrations produced by the machinery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acoustic - relating to sound or the science of sound
  • degree lower: subsonic - relating to sound waves or motion below the speed of sound or the audible range
  • degree higher: ultrasonic - relating to sound waves with frequencies above the audible range
