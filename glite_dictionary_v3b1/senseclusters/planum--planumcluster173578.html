planum

noun
flat anatomical surface
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnəm / (BrE)
a flat or level surface, especially used to describe anatomical structures

Examples

Brain planum
The planum temporale is important for processing language in the brain.
Surgeons must carefully identify the planum sphenoidale during certain procedures.
A fracture was found along the planum of the bone in the scan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plane - a flat surface extending in all directions
  • antonym: ridge - a raised or elevated anatomical structure
