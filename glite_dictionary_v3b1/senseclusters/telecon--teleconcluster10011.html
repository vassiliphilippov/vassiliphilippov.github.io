telecon

verb
to hold remote meeting
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
to take part in or hold a meeting using telephone or video conferencing technology

Examples

To telecon with clients
We will telecon with the overseas team tomorrow morning to finalize the project details.
Due to travel restrictions, the managers decided to telecon instead of meeting at the office.
Can you telecon with the client this afternoon to review the contract changes?

Related Terms

  • antonym: meet in person - to gather physically for a meeting
  • synonym: teleconference - to hold a meeting by telephone or video link
