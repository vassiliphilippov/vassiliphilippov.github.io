overexert

verb
exert oneself excessively
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜrt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪɡˈzɜːt / (BrE)
to make too much physical or mental effort, going beyond one's strength or limits

Examples

To overexert yourself
Doctors advise patients not to overexert themselves during recovery.
He tends to overexert himself when he is anxious to impress others.
If you overexert yourself at the gym, you might injure your muscles.
