contaminate

verb
make radioactive by adding radioactive material
5.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈtæməˌneɪt / (AmE), / kənˈtæmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to make something impure or harmful by adding unwanted or dangerous substances

Examples

To contaminate water
Authorities are monitoring the site to ensure the soil does not become contaminated by leaked radioactive waste.
It is dangerous if water supplies become contaminated with radioactive material after an accident.
Nuclear tests can contaminate large areas with harmful radiation.
Industrial chemicals can contaminate the water supply if not handled properly.
It is important to avoid contaminating clean surfaces when cooking.
The food was found to be contaminated with bacteria after being left out overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pollute - to make impure or harmful by adding contaminants
  • antonym: purify - remove contaminants from; make clean or pure
