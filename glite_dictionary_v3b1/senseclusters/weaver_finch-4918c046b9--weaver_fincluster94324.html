weaver finch

noun
African or Asian bird known for woven nests
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwiː.vər fɪntʃ/ (AmE), /ˈwiː.və fɪntʃ/ (BrE)
A finch-like African or Asian bird that builds intricately woven nests and often lives in colonies.

Examples

The weaver finch constructs an intricate nest using blades of grass and twigs.
