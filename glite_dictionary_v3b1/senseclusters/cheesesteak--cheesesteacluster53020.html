cheesesteak

noun
beef and cheese sandwich
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌsteɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌsteɪk / (BrE)
a sandwich made with thinly sliced beef and melted cheese served in a long roll

Examples

A cheesesteak sandwich
We stopped at a famous shop in Philadelphia to try their classic cheesesteak.
She made homemade cheesesteaks for the family using fresh rolls and provolone cheese.
Many people debate whether onions belong on a cheesesteak or not.
He ordered a classic cheesesteak with extra onions and peppers for dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its delicious cheesesteak sandwiches.
I tried my first cheesesteak when I visited Philadelphia last summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hamburger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground beef in a bun
  • synonym: submarine sandwich - a long sandwich filled with meat, cheese, and vegetables
