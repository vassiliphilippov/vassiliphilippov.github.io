stollen

noun
horizontal mine tunnel
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊlən / (AmE), / ˈstɒlən / (BrE)
a nearly horizontal passage or tunnel driven into a hillside or mine for access, drainage, or working of ore

Examples

Mine stollen
The miners entered the stollen to begin their shift deep inside the mountain.
Water was drained from the old mine through a long stollen built centuries ago.
Engineers inspected the stollen for signs of collapse after the recent earthquake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adit - a horizontal passage leading into a mine for access or drainage
  • antonym: shaft - a vertical or inclined passage in a mine
