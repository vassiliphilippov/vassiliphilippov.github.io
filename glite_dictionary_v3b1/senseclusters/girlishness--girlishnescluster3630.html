girlishness

noun
the quality of being like a girl
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrlɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːlɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being characteristic of a girl, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

The girlishness of style
Despite her age, there was a sense of girlishness about her demeanor.
Her girlishness was evident in the way she laughed and played.
The dress added a touch of girlishness to her usually professional style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femininity - qualities associated with being female
  • synonym: girlish - like or characteristic of a girl
