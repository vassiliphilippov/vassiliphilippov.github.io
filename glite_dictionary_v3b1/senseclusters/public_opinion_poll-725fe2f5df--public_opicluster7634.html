public opinion poll

noun
survey of public views on issues
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk əˈpɪnjən poʊl / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk əˈpɪnjən pəʊl / (BrE)
a survey of a representative sample of people to gather information about the views or preferences of the public on specific issues

Examples

A public opinion poll
According to a recent public opinion poll, most citizens support stricter environmental regulations.
He cited results from a public opinion poll to back his arguments during the debate.
The latest public opinion poll shows a significant shift in voter attitudes toward the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poll - an inquiry into public opinion by interviewing people
  • synonym: survey - an investigation of the opinions or experience of a group of people
