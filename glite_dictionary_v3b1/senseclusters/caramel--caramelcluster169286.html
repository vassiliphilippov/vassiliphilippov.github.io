caramel

noun
chewy candy made from caramelized sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌmɛl, ˈkɑrməl / (AmE), / ˈkærəməl, ˈkærəmɛl / (BrE)
a firm, chewy candy made from caramelized sugar, butter, and milk

Examples

A chewy caramel candy
He bought a box of assorted caramels as a gift.
She offered me a caramel after dinner.
The caramel stuck to my teeth as I chewed it slowly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toffee - a candy made by boiling sugar and butter together
