duck

verb
to dip quickly into liquid
6.3
Pronunciation: / dʌk / (AmE), / dʌk / (BrE)
to dip or push quickly into liquid and take out again

Examples

To duck your head
He quickly ducked his head under the faucet to cool off.
She ducked the cloth into the bucket of water before wiping the table.
The coach ducked the basketball in paint as part of the training drill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dip - to put something briefly into a liquid and remove it
