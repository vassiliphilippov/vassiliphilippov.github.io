liquescent

adjective
becoming liquid
7.6
Pronunciation: / lɪˈkwɛsənt / (AmE), / lɪˈkwɛsənt / (BrE)
becoming liquid; in the process of turning from solid into liquid.

Examples

Liquescent substance
A liquescent substance may be unstable at room temperature.
Certain metals become liquescent under intense heat.
The ice was liquescent as the temperature rose above freezing.
