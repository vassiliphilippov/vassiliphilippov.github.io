folk dancing

noun
traditional style of dance by ordinary people
3.5
Pronunciation: / foʊk ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / fəʊk ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dancing that originated among ordinary people, often passed down through generations and typical of a specific community or region.

Examples

Folk dancing classes
Every Saturday, the local community center hosts folk dancing classes for people of all ages.
Many cultures preserve their history and traditions through folk dancing events.
She became interested in folk dancing after watching a colorful performance during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folk dance - a traditional dance from a particular region or community
