Blackwall hitch

noun
knot for attaching a line to a hook
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌwɔl hɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈblækˌwɔːl hɪtʃ / (BrE)
A simple knot used for temporarily attaching a rope or line to a hook, commonly in nautical contexts.

Examples

Blackwall hitch knot
