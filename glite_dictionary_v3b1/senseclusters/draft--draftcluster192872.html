draft

verb
order into the armed forces
Pronunciation: / dræft / (AmE), / drɑːft / (BrE)
to select or order someone to join the armed forces, especially during a time of war or national emergency

Examples

To draft soldiers
He was drafted into the army right after finishing college.
If you refuse to draft eligible citizens, you can face legal penalties.
The government may draft young men in times of national crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discharge - officially release someone from military service
