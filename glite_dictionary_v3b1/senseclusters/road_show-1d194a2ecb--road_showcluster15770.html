road show

noun
series of investor presentations
4.8
Pronunciation: / ro\u028ad \u0283o\u028a / (AmE), / r\u0259\u028ad \u0283\u0259\u028a / (BrE)
(in business, especially finance) a series of presentations given in different locations to potential investors to promote a company or a securities offering.

Examples

An investor road show
Before the IPO, the executives toured Europe on a road show to meet institutional investors.
The investment bank organized a series of road show presentations to drum up interest in the offering.
After the scandal, several investors pulled out despite the company's well-prepared road show schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investor presentation - a formal presentation made to potential or current investors about a company's business and prospects
  • synonym: pitch - a presentation or talk to persuade people to invest in or support something
