mud stain

noun
stain made by mud
Pronunciation: / mʌd steɪn / (AmE), / mʌd steɪn / (BrE)
a mark or discoloration on a surface caused by mud

Examples

A mud stain on carpet
He tried to scrub the mud stain from the carpet but it wouldn't come out.
The car's upholstery was ruined by a large mud stain left by dirty boots.
There was a mud stain on the bottom of her jeans after the hike.
