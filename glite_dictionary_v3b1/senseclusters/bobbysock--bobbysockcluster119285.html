bobbysock

noun
short ankle-length sock
7.2 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbiˌsɑk / (AmE), / ˈbɒbiˌsɒk / (BrE)
A sock that reaches just above the ankle, typically worn with skirts or dresses, especially fashionable among teenage girls in the 1940s and 1950s.
