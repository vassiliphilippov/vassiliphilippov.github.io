shrivel up

verb
become shrunken or withered
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪvəl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪvəl ʌp / (BrE)
to become wrinkled, shrunken, or withered, especially due to loss of moisture

Examples

To shrivel up leaves
If you don't water the plant, it will shrivel up and die.
My skin tends to shrivel up after spending too long in the sun.
The old apple had shrivelled up into a hard, dry shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry up - to lose moisture and become dry
  • synonym: wither - to become dry and shriveled
