Synonyms

  • wither
    4.4 6.80/m
    wither is used for plants that dry and lose strength, while shrivel up describes physical wrinkling and shrinkage from losing moisture.
  • dry up
    2.7 1.56/m
    dry up means lose all moisture until something becomes very dry, while shrivel up focuses on wrinkling and shrinking from that moisture loss.
  • shrink
    1.8 5.84/m
    shrink means become smaller in size, often from heat or pressure, while shrivel up means shrink with visible wrinkling due to drying.
