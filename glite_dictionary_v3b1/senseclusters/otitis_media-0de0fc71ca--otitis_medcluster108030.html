otitis media

noun
inflammation of the middle ear
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtaɪtɪs ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / əʊˈtaɪtɪs ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
inflammation of the middle ear, often found in children

Examples

Otitis media infection
Children who have otitis media may experience ear pain and trouble hearing.
If left untreated, otitis media can sometimes lead to more serious ear problems.
The doctor diagnosed the child with otitis media and prescribed antibiotics.
