crossword puzzle

noun
a word puzzle with clues and a grid
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑːs.wɝd ˈpʌz.əl / (AmE), / ˈkrɒs.wɜːd ˈpʌz.əl / (BrE)
A puzzle consisting of a grid of squares and blanks into which words are written according to clues given for each word.

Examples

A crossword puzzle book
He bought a book filled with crossword puzzles to improve his vocabulary.
She always solves the crossword puzzle in the Sunday newspaper over her morning coffee.
The clues in today's crossword puzzle were surprisingly tricky and required some creative thinking.
