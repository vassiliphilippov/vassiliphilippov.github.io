calcaneal

adjective
relating to the heel bone
Pronunciation: / kælˈkeɪniəl / (AmE), / kælˈkeɪnɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the heel bone (calcaneus) or the heel area of the foot

Examples

Calcaneal bone pain
Improper footwear can contribute to calcaneal pain in athletes.
The patient suffered a fracture of the calcaneal bone after falling off a ladder.
The surgeon examined the calcaneal tendon during the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calcaneus - the large bone forming the heel of the foot
