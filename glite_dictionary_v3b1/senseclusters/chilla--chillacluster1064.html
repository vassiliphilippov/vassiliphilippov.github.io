chilla

noun
South American fox
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːjə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlə / (BrE)
a small South American fox (Lycalopex griseus) found in open and arid regions of Chile and Argentina

Examples

A wild chilla
The chilla is well adapted to the dry grasslands of southern South America.
Farmers sometimes spot a chilla hunting rodents near their fields at dusk.
The thick fur of the chilla helps it survive cold desert nights.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: culpeo - a larger South American fox species
  • synonym: fox - a small wild animal with a bushy tail and pointed ears
