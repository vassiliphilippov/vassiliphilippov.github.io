curbstoner

noun
informal street seller
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːbˌstoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈkɜːbˌstəʊnə / (BrE)
a person who sells goods informally on the sidewalk or street curb, often without a fixed shop

Examples

Sidewalk curbstoner
The city passed new regulations to limit the activities of curbstoners selling electronics on busy corners.
After losing his job, he became a curbstoner to support his family by selling handmade crafts.
Many curbstoners set up their tables early in the morning to catch the rush of commuters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shopkeeper - a person who owns or manages a shop
  • synonym: street vendor - a person who sells goods in public places, especially on the street
