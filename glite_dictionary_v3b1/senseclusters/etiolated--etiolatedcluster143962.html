etiolated

adjective
made pale/weak by lack of light (esp. plants)
6.7 8.3
Pronunciation: /iː.ˈtɪə.leɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ɪ.ˈtaɪ.ə.leɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /iː.ˈtɪə.leɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ɪ.ˈtaɪ.ə.leɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
(especially of plants) pale and weak because of lack of light, often resulting in a yellowish or whitish color due to the absence of chlorophyll.

Examples

An etiolated plant
After weeks in the cupboard, the potatoes became pale and etiolated.
She noticed that the etiolated leaves were a sign the plant needed more exposure to light.
The etiolated seedlings struggled to grow without enough sunlight.
