hypognathous

adjective
having a projecting lower jaw
6.6
Pronunciation: / haɪˈpɑɡ.nə.θəs / (AmE), / haɪˈpɒɡ.nə.θəs / (BrE)
Having a lower jaw that projects beyond the upper jaw, typically used to describe certain animals.

Examples

Hypognathous mouthparts
Insects with hypognathous mouthparts often feed on surfaces below their heads.
Scientists identified the species by its hypognathous jaw structure.
The fish is hypognathous, with its lower jaw sticking out noticeably farther than the upper.
