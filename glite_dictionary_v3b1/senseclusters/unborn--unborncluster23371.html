unborn

adjective
not yet born or existing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɔrn / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɔːn / (BrE)
not yet born; not yet brought into existence

Examples

The unborn child
Advances in science may benefit many people yet unborn.
She wrote a letter to her unborn child.
The artist hoped her work would inspire unborn generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: born - brought into existence; having entered the world at birth
