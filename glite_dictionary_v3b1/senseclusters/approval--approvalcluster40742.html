approval

noun
favorable statement or message
1.6
Pronunciation: / əˈpruːvəl /; / əˈpruvəl / (AmE), / əˈpruːvəl / (BrE)
a feeling, expression, or sign that you think someone or something is good or acceptable

Examples

A smile of approval
A word of approval from her mentor meant a great deal to her.
His boss rarely offers approval, making positive feedback especially valuable.
She smiled at his comment, taking it as a quiet sign of approval.
He smiled in approval when she finished her presentation.
She always looked for signs of her father's approval before making decisions.
The project received universal approval from the committee members.
