defection

noun
the act of abandoning allegiance
5.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfek.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfek.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of leaving or abandoning a country, group, or cause, often to join an opposing one
