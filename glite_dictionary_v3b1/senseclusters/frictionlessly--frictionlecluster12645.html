frictionlessly

adverb
smoothly and easily
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪkʃənləsli / (AmE), / ˈfrɪkʃənləsli / (BrE)
in a way that happens smoothly and easily, without resistance or difficulty (physically or metaphorically)

Examples

To work frictionlessly
The new software allows users to transfer files frictionlessly between devices.
Negotiations proceeded frictionlessly, with both sides quickly reaching an agreement.
Her ideas blended frictionlessly into the team's overall strategy.
The gears turned frictionlessly after they were properly lubricated.
Her ideas flowed frictionlessly from her mind onto the paper.
The new software allows data to transfer frictionlessly between devices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or difficult way
  • synonym: effortlessly - in a way that requires little or no effort
  • antonym: roughly - in a way that is not smooth or has resistance
  • synonym: smoothly - in an even, easy, or uninterrupted way
