bolo

noun
a heavy Philippine knife
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a long, heavy knife with a single edge; originally from the Philippines, used as a tool and weapon

Examples

A bolo knife
