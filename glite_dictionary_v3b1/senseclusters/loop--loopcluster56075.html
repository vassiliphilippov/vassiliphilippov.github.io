loop

noun
aerial maneuver: vertical circle
7.2 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / luÀêp / (AmE), / luÀêp / (BrE)
A flight maneuver in which an aircraft flies a complete circle in a vertical plane.

Examples

An airplane loop
During the airshow, her daring loop drew loud applause from the audience.
It takes great skill for a fighter jet to execute a loop at high speed.
The pilot performed a perfect loop and thrilled the crowd.
