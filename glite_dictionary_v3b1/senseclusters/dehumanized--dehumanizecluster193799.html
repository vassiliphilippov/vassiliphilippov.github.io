dehumanized

adjective
lacking human qualities or dignity
Pronunciation: / diˈhjuːmənˌaɪzd / (AmE), / diˈhjuːmənˌaɪzd /; / diːˈhjuːmənʌɪzd / (BrE)
Deprived of human qualities, personality, or dignity.

Examples

Dehumanized treatment
After years in prison, he felt completely dehumanized by the experience.
Some critics argue that modern warfare has dehumanized soldiers, turning them into mere cogs in a machine.
The film tells the story of people who are dehumanized by a totalitarian regime.
Many prisoners described feeling dehumanised by the harsh conditions and lack of respect.
She spoke out against policies that left vulnerable groups feeling dehumanised and powerless.
The film portrayed a society in which people were increasingly dehumanised by technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dehumanized - Made to lose human qualities or treated as less than human (American spelling)
