confidence

noun
belief in oneself or abilities
2.3 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.fɪ.dəns/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.fɪ.dəns/ (BrE)
A feeling or belief that you can do something well or succeed at something; freedom from doubt about your abilities or qualities.

Examples

He answered the difficult question with confidence, impressing everyone in the room.
Her confidence grew with every successful presentation she gave at work.
The training helped restore her confidence after months of uncertainty.
