Kegel

noun
pelvic floor exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈkiːɡəl / (BrE)
an exercise designed to strengthen the pelvic floor muscles, often recommended for improving bladder control or after childbirth

Examples

Kegel exercises daily
After her surgery, the doctor recommended daily Kegel exercises to help with recovery.
Many people do Kegel exercises to improve bladder control and core strength.
He learned how to perform a proper Kegel by following instructions from a physical therapist.
