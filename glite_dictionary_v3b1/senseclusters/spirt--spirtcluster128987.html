spirt

noun
sudden jet or discharge of liquid
Pronunciation: / spɝt / (AmE), / spɜːt / (BrE)
A sudden brief burst or jet of liquid or other substance.

Examples

A spirt of liquid
A sudden spirt of water shot out of the broken pipe.
He squeezed the bottle, and a spirt of ketchup landed on his plate.
The whale surfaced with a spirt of mist from its blowhole.
