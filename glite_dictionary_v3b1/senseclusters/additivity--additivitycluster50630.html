additivity

noun
sum of effects
Pronunciation: / ˌædɪtəˈvɪɾəti /; / ˌædɪˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌædɪtəˈvɪtɪti /; / ˌædɪˈtɪvɪti /; / ˌædɪˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the property that the total effect, value, or result is equal to the sum of the individual parts
