adenosis

noun
disorder affecting the glands
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.əˈnoʊ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌad.ɪˈnəʊ.sɪs / (BrE)
a disease or abnormal condition affecting the glands, often involving excessive growth or proliferation of glandular tissue

Examples

Adenosis gland disease
Adenosis can affect various organs, depending on which glands are involved.
Researchers are studying new treatments for mammary adenosis in young women.
The patient was diagnosed with adenosis after symptoms of swelling and pain developed in her glands.
