  • aperiodic
    8.2 0.01/m

  • noncyclical
    7.2 0.00/m
    noncyclical does not happen in repeating cycles and fits technical talk, while irregular covers many kinds of uneven patterns.
  • episodic
    7.1 0.1/m
    episodic appears as separate events over time, often briefly, while irregular simply means lacking any fixed rhythm or pattern.
  • sporadic
    7.0 2/m

  • arrhythmic
