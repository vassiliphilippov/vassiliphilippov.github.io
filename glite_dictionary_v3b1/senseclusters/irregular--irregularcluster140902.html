irregular

adjective
not at regular intervals; uneven
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈɹɛgjələr /; / ɪˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ɪˈɹɛgjʊlə /; / ɪˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
not occurring at regular intervals; lacking a fixed pattern or rhythm

Examples

Irregular heartbeat
His doctor was concerned by his irregular heartbeat during the examination.
Rainfall in this region is irregular, making agriculture unpredictable.
The meeting schedule was rather irregular, with no set day or time each week.
As an irregular visitor, she only drops by the office every few weeks.
He found irregular work as a substitute teacher.
The bus service has been irregular since the new schedule was introduced.

  • antonym: regular - Occurring at fixed intervals or following a set pattern
