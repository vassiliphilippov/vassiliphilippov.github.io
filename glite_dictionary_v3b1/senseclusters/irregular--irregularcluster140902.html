irregular

adjective
not at regular intervals; uneven
3.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈre.gjə.lər/ /; / /ɪ.ˈre.gjʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈre.gjʊ.lə/ /; / /ɪ.ˈre.gjə.lə/ (BrE)
not occurring at regular intervals; lacking a fixed pattern or rhythm

Examples
