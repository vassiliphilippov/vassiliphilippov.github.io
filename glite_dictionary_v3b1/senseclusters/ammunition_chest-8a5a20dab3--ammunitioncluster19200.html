ammunition chest

noun
container for storing ammunition
Pronunciation: / ˌæmjəˈnɪʃən tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˌamjʊˈnɪʃən tʃɛst / (BrE)
a strong container used to store and transport ammunition, especially for military use
