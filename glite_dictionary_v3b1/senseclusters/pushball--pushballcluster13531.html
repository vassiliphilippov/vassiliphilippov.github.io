pushball

noun
game using a giant leather ball pushed by teams
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game in which two teams try to push a large leather ball, typically six feet in diameter, across each other's goal.

Examples

A pushball game
Despite its size, the pushball can be tricky to control in wet conditions.
Teams strategize together to move the huge pushball toward the goal line.
The college hosted an exhibition pushball match to celebrate its sports day.
