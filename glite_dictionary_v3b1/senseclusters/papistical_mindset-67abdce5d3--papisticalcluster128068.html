papistical mindset

noun
dogmatic or hierarchical way of thinking
9.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈpɪstɪkəl ˈmaɪndˌsɛt / (AmE), / pəˈpɪstɪkəl ˈmaɪndsɛt / (BrE)
A dogmatic or rigidly hierarchical way of thinking, especially one resembling or attributed to the practices or attitudes associated with the Roman Catholic Church.

Examples

A papistical mindset
Her papistical mindset left no room for discussion or compromise in the meeting.
Many reformers of the era rebelled against what they saw as a papistical mindset dominating religious authority.
The report condemned the university administration for adopting a papistical mindset toward student protests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dogmatic - inclined to lay down principles as undeniably true
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas; unprejudiced
