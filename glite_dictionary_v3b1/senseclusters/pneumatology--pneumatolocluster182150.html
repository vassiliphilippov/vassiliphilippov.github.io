pneumatology

noun
study of spirits
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of spirits or spiritual phenomena, especially in relation to spiritualism or parapsychology
