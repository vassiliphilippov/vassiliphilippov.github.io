subzero

adjective
below zero temperature
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈzɪəroʊ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
colder than zero degrees, especially on the Celsius or Fahrenheit scale

Examples

Subzero temperatures
The hikers braved subzero temperatures during their winter expedition.
Water pipes can burst if exposed to subzero weather for too long.
The forecast predicts several days of subzero conditions this week.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cold - having a low temperature, especially when compared to the temperature of the human body
  • synonym: freezing - at or below the temperature at which water turns to ice
  • degree higher: frigid - extremely cold
