integrin

noun
cell-attachment protein
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.tɪ.grɪn/ /; / /ˈɪn.tə.grɪn/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.tɪ.grɪn/ /; / /ˈɪn.tə.grɪn/ (BrE)
