stimulator

noun
activity-increasing device
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪmjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈstɪmjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a device or substance that increases activity or excitement in a part of the body or a system

Examples

Nerve stimulator
The doctor recommended a stimulator to help with nerve function.
A cardiac stimulator can help regulate a patient's heartbeat.
Researchers tested a new brain stimulator to treat certain neurological conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: activator - something that starts or increases activity
  • antonym: inhibitor - something that slows down or prevents activity
