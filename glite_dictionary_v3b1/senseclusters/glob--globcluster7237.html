glob

noun
small compact mass, especially of something thick or sticky
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑb / (AmE), / ɡlɒb / (BrE)
a small, round or irregularly shaped mass of a substance, especially a thick or sticky one.

Examples

A glob of honey
A glob of honey dripped from the spoon onto the table.
She wiped a glob of paint off her jeans.
There was a glob of toothpaste left in the sink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clump - a compact, clustered mass
  • synonym: drop - a small quantity of liquid shaped like a sphere
