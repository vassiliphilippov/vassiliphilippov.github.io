A type of sweet spread made by cooking fruit with sugar, often with fruit pieces remaining, and used as a condiment or spread.

Examples

Homemade preserves

Synonyms

  • preserve
    2.6 1.28/m
    preserve is a general term for fruit cooked with sugar to last longer, while preserves refers to the spread with fruit pieces.
  • conserve
    5.1 0.04/m
    conserve is made with whole fruit pieces cooked in sugar, while preserves is a spread that often keeps visible fruit.
  • jam
    1.0 4.65/m
    jam is made by boiling crushed fruit with sugar into a soft spread, while preserves keep larger pieces or whole fruit.

Related Terms
