scratchingly

adverb
in an irritating way
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes a scratching sensation or irritation on the skin.

Examples

Scratchingly rough fabric
The sweater felt scratchingly rough against his arms all day.
She complained that the label on her shirt rubbed scratchingly on her neck.
After hiking through the bushes, his legs itched scratchingly from the contact with the plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritatingly - in a way that causes annoyance or discomfort
  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without roughness
