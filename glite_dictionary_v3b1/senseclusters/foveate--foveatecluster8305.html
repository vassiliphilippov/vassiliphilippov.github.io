foveate

verb
to focus precisely
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.viː.eɪt/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.viː.ət/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.viː.eɪt/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.viː.ət/ (BrE)
