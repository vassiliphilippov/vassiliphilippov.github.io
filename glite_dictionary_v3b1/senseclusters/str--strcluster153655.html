str

noun
game stat for strength
Pronunciation: / ɛs ti ɑr / (AmE), / ɛs ti ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'strength', used as a statistic representing physical power in role-playing games

Examples

High str stat
Her character's str was high, so she could lift heavy objects and deal more damage.
If your str is too low, you might not be able to open the heavy door.
Players often increase their str to improve their combat abilities in the game.
