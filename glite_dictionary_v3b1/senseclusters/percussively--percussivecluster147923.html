percussively

adverb
by tapping in examination
Pronunciation: / pərˈkʌsɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈkʌsɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner involving tapping, especially as part of a medical examination to assess underlying conditions

Examples

Tap percussively for diagnosis
The doctor listened carefully as he tapped percussively on the patient's abdomen.
During the examination, the nurse percussively checked the patient's back for any unusual sounds.
Fluid in the lungs can sometimes be detected when a physician examines the chest percussively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tapping - striking lightly and repeatedly
