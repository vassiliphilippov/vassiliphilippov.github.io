Bowlegs

proper noun
Seminole chief
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌlɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌlɛɡz / (BrE)
the name of a 19th-century Seminole chief, also known as Billy Bowlegs, who resisted U.S. forces during the Seminole Wars in Florida

Examples

Chief Bowlegs
During the Seminole Wars, Bowlegs became a symbol of resistance against U.S. expansion.
Many history books mention Bowlegs as a prominent leader of the Seminole people.
The legacy of Bowlegs is still remembered in Florida today.
