kick turn

noun
skiing maneuver for turning while stationary
9.3
Pronunciation: / kɪk tɝn / (AmE), / kɪk tɜːn / (BrE)
a skiing maneuver in which one ski is swung vertically and pivoted backward to allow the skier to change direction while standing

Examples

A kick turn maneuver
He demonstrated a perfect kick turn at the edge of the piste.
Mastering the kick turn is essential for beginners navigating tight trails.
The steep slope forced her to use a kick turn to reverse direction safely.
