kick turn

noun
skiing maneuver for turning while stationary
9.3 8.2
Pronunciation: / kɪk tɝn / (AmE), / kɪk tɜːn / (BrE)
a skiing maneuver in which one ski is swung vertically and pivoted backward to allow the skier to change direction while standing
