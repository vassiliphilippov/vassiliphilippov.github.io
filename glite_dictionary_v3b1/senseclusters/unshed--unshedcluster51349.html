unshed

adjective
not yet released
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɛd / (BrE)
not yet released, fallen, or let go, especially referring to tears, fur, or leaves

Examples

Unshed tears
She blinked away her unshed tears during the sad movie.
The dog’s unshed fur collected on the brush after grooming.
Autumn leaves remained unshed on the tree despite the strong wind.
