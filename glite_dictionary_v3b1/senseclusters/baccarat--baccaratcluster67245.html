baccarat

noun
casino card game
Pronunciation: / ˌbækərˈɑ / (AmE), / ˈbækərɑː / (BrE)
a card game played in casinos in which players bet on whose hand, the player's or the banker's, will have a total closest to nine

Examples

Baccarat card game
He won a large sum of money by betting on the banker in baccarat.
Many high rollers enjoy playing baccarat at exclusive casino tables.
The elegant atmosphere of the casino made baccarat especially exciting for the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackjack - another popular casino card game, also known as twenty-one
