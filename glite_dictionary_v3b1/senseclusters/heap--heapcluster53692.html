heap

noun
old, unreliable car (informal)
9.8
Pronunciation: / hip / (AmE), / hiːp / (BrE)
An old and unreliable car, especially one in poor condition.

Examples

An old heap
After years of neglect, her car had become a total heap.
He still drives that old heap to work every morning, even though it barely runs.
They bought the heap for almost nothing, planning to fix it up themselves.
