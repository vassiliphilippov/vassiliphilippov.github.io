bedpost

noun
vertical corner support of a bed
7.8 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌpəʊst / (BrE)
one of the four upright supports at the corners of a bedstead, often extending above the mattress

Examples
