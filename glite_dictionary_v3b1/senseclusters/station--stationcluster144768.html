station

noun
facility for specific activity or service
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place or building with special equipment and staff, designed for a specific purpose such as transportation, work, or services.

Examples

Train station building
She works at a fire station in the city center.
The train arrived at the station ten minutes late due to heavy traffic on the line.
We stopped at a gas station to fill up the car before continuing our journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depot - a place for storing large quantities of equipment, food, or goods
  • synonym: terminal - a building where passengers board or leave vehicles, especially trains or buses
