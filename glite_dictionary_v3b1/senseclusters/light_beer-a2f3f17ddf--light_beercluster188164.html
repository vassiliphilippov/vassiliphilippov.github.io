light beer

noun
lager with lower alcohol content
Pronunciation: / laɪt bɪr / (AmE), / laɪt bɪə / (BrE)
a type of lager with reduced alcohol content, often lower in calories than regular beer

Examples

A glass of light beer
Many people choose light beer for its lower calorie content.
She ordered a light beer because she wanted something with less alcohol.
The bar offers several brands of light beer on tap.
