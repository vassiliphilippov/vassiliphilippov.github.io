pungence

noun
sharp expressiveness
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌndʒəns / (AmE), / ˈpʌndʒəns / (BrE)
the quality of being sharply expressive, caustic, or forceful in style or language

Examples

Pungence of his words
The pungence of his remarks left a lasting impression on the audience.
Her writing is known for its pungence and ability to challenge conventional ideas.
Critics praised the pungence of the editorial for exposing hidden truths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluntness - the quality of being direct and sometimes harsh in speech
  • synonym: incisiveness - the quality of being clear and direct in expression
  • antonym: mildness - the quality of being gentle or not strong in expression
