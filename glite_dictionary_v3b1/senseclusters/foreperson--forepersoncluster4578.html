foreperson

noun
group supervisor
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
the leader or supervisor of a group, especially in a workplace or as the spokesperson of a jury

Examples

Site foreperson
The foreperson assigned tasks to each member of the construction crew.
After hours of discussion, the foreperson delivered the jury's decision to the judge.
As the new foreperson, she is responsible for ensuring safety on the factory floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees others at work
  • antonym: worker - a person who does a job but does not supervise others
