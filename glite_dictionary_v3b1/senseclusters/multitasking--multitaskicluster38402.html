multitasking

adjective
doing several tasks
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌtæskɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌtɑːskɪŋ / (BrE)
able to perform more than one task or job at the same time

Examples

Multitasking worker
A multitasking employee can answer emails while talking on the phone and organizing files.
The multitasking software allows users to run several applications at once.
Her multitasking skills impressed everyone during the busy holiday season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-minded - focused on only one task or goal
  • synonym: versatile - able to do many different things well
