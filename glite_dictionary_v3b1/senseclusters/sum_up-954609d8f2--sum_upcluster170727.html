sum up

verb
state the main points concisely
1.3
Pronunciation: / sʌm ʌp / (AmE), / sʌm ʌp / (BrE)
to express or state the main points or essential facts of something in a concise way; to summarize

Examples

To sum up the story
One sentence cannot sum up everything she has achieved in her lifetime.
The final paragraph should sum up the main arguments of the essay.
To sum up, the project's success relied on teamwork and dedication.
In his closing remarks, the manager summed up the team's achievements.
She tried to sum up the movie in a single sentence for her friends.
To sum up, the committee approved five new projects this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recap - to repeat the main points of something
  • synonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points
