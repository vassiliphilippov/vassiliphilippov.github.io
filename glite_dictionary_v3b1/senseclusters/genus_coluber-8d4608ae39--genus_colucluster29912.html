genus Coluber

noun
a genus of snakes (racers)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs kəˈluːbər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs kəˈluːbə / (BrE)
A genus of fast-moving, slender snakes commonly known as racers, belonging to the family Colubridae.

Examples

The genus Coluber snakes
Many harmless snakes in North America belong to the genus Coluber.
Scientists studying the genus Coluber have catalogued their swift movements and slender bodies.
The genus Coluber includes several species of nonvenomous racers found throughout the world.
