overdelicate

adjective
excessively delicate or sensitive
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdɛlɪkət / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdɛlɪkət / (BrE)
Excessively delicate; too refined, sensitive, or fragile.

Examples

An overdelicate taste
After her illness, her appetite became overdelicate and she could only eat bland food.
He criticized the overdelicate workmanship, saying it wouldn't endure daily use.
Some critics are overdelicate about language and take offense too easily.

  • antonym: robust - strong and healthy; not delicate
