gray lemming

noun
Old World lemming species
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈlɛmɪŋ /; / ˈɡreɪ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈlɛmɪŋ /; / ˈɡreɪ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
