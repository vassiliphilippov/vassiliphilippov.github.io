quacky

adjective
fraudulent or unscientific
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkwæki / (AmE), / ˈkwæki / (BrE)
fraudulent, unscientific, or resembling a quack, especially in reference to medical treatments or claims

Examples

Quacky medicine
Many people were skeptical of the quacky remedies advertised on late-night television.
The doctor warned his patients to avoid quacky treatments that had no scientific support.
She realized the website was selling quacky products with exaggerated health claims.
