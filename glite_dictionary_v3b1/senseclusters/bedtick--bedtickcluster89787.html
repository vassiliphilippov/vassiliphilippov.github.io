bedtick

noun
mattress covering fabric
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌtɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌtɪk / (BrE)
a strong, tightly woven fabric used to cover a mattress or pillow

Examples

Striped bedtick fabric
The old mattress was covered with a striped bedtick.
She bought new bedtick to reupholster the worn-out pillows.
Traditional bedtick is often made from cotton or linen for durability.

Related Terms
