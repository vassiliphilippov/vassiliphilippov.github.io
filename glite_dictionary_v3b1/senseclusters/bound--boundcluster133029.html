bound

adjective
heading or intending to go somewhere
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / baʊnd / (AmE), / baʊnd / (BrE)
heading or intending to go in a particular direction or toward a specific place, often used after a noun or in compound forms (e.g., eastbound, college-bound)

Examples

College-bound students

The train is bound for Boston and will leave in ten minutes.
