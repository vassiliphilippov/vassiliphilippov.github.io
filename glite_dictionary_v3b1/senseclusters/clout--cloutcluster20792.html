clout

noun
power or influence
5.3 4.35/m
Pronunciation: / klaÊŠt / (AmE), / klaÊŠt / (BrE)
power or influence, especially in politics or business.

Examples

Political clout
As a successful investor, he wields a lot of clout on Wall Street.
Her family has considerable clout in local politics.
The manager used her clout to get the project approved quickly.
