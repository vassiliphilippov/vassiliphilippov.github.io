postcolonialism

noun
challenge to colonial legacy
8.8
Pronunciation: /poʊst.kə.ˈloʊ.ni.əl.ɪz.əm/ (AmE), /pəʊst.kə.ˈləʊ.ni.əl.ɪz.əm/ (BrE)
a movement, theory, or field of study that examines and challenges the effects and legacy of colonial rule on societies and cultures after independence
