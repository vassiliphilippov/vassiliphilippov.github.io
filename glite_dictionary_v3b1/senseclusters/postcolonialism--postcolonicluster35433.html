postcolonialism

noun
challenge to colonial legacy
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstkəˈloʊniəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstkəˈləʊniəlɪzəm /; / ˌpəʊstkəˈləʊniəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
a movement, theory, or field of study that examines and challenges the effects and legacy of colonial rule on societies and cultures after independence

Related Terms

  • antonym: colonialism - the practice of acquiring and maintaining colonies
  • synonym: decolonization - the process of ending colonial rule and gaining independence
  • synonym: postcolonial - relating to the period after colonial rule
