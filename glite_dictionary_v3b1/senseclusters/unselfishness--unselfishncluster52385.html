unselfishness

noun
quality of putting others first
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛlfɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛlfɪʃnɪs /; / ʌnˈsɛlfɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of caring for others and putting their needs before your own; generosity or selflessness

Examples

Show unselfishness in actions
He demonstrated great unselfishness by volunteering his time to help others instead of relaxing on weekends.
Her unselfishness inspired everyone in the community to contribute to the cause.
Parents often show remarkable unselfishness in the sacrifices they make for their children.
Her unselfishness impressed everyone during the charity fundraiser.
The team's victory was a result of their hard work and unselfishness on the court.
True unselfishness means helping others without expecting anything in return.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruism - selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • antonym: selfishness - the quality of caring only about yourself
