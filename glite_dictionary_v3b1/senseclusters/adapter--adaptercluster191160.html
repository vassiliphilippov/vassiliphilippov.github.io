adapter

noun
musician who arranges compositions
Pronunciation: / əˈdæptər / (AmE), / əˈdæptə / (BrE)
a musician who adapts a composition for particular voices or instruments or for another style of performance

Examples

Music adapter
As an adapter, she skillfully transformed classical songs for modern instruments.
Each Broadway revival relies on a talented adapter to update the score.
The adapter turned the symphony into a piece for jazz trio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arranger - a person who arranges music for performance
