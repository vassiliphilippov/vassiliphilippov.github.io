wildcatter

noun
person drilling for oil in unproven areas
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌkætər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌkatə / (BrE)
a person who drills for oil in areas not known to contain oil

Examples

A wildcatter drills oil
His grandfather was a legendary wildcatter who struck oil in Oklahoma.
Many early wildcatters were instrumental in discovering major oil fields across Texas.
The wildcatter took a huge risk by drilling where no one else had tried before.

  • synonym: oilman - a person involved in the oil industry
