sunscreening

noun
applying sunscreen
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌskriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌskriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying sunscreen to protect the skin from the sun's harmful rays

Examples

Proper sunscreening before swimming
Proper sunscreening is important before spending time outdoors.
Daily sunscreening can help prevent sunburn and long-term skin damage.
Parents should teach children about the importance of sunscreening during summer activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sun protection - measures taken to protect the skin from sun exposure
