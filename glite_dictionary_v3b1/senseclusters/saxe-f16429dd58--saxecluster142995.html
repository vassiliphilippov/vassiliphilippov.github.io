Saxe

proper noun
historical region in Germany; homeland of the Saxons
9.9
Pronunciation: / sæks / (AmE), / sæks / (BrE)
a historical region in Germany around the upper Elbe River; regarded as the original homeland of the Saxons

Examples

Saxe region
Historians believe that the Saxons migrated from Saxe to other parts of Europe in the early Middle Ages.
Many cultural traditions in Saxe influenced the surrounding Germanic regions.
The royal family traced its origins back to Saxe, the ancient homeland of the Saxons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Saxony - region and state in modern eastern Germany, historically associated with the Saxons
