buzz saw

noun
circular power saw with toothed edge
5.7
Pronunciation: / bʌz sɑ / (AmE), / bʌz sɔː / (BrE)
A power-driven saw with a circular steel disk having cutting teeth around its edge, which rotates rapidly.

Examples

A buzz saw blade
He used the buzz saw to cut through the thick wooden beams.
Safety goggles are essential when operating a buzz saw.
The sharp whirring sound of the buzz saw filled the workshop.
