grittiness

noun
courage and determination
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪtinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing courage, determination, or toughness in difficult situations

Examples

Grittiness in a leader
Her grittiness inspired everyone on the team to keep pushing forward despite the setbacks.
The grittiness he showed during the long recovery impressed his doctors and family.
Success in that industry often depends on a person's grittiness and willingness to face challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resilience - the ability to recover quickly from difficulties
  • synonym: tenacity - the quality of being very determined
  • antonym: weakness - lack of strength or determination
