torch singer

noun
singer of sentimental love songs
6.5
Pronunciation: / tɔrtʃ ˈsɪŋər / (AmE), / tɔːtʃ ˈsɪŋə / (BrE)
A singer, typically a woman, who specializes in performing torch songs, which are sentimental love songs, often about unrequited or lost love.

Examples

A torch singer performs
During the 1940s, many torch singers became famous for their heartfelt performances.
She dreamed of becoming a torch singer, pouring her soul into every melancholic tune.
The club featured a renowned torch singer whose emotional ballads captivated the audience.
