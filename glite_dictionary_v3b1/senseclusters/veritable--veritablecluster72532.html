veritable

adjective
real or genuine; used for emphasis
4.6 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛrɪtəbl /; / ˈvɛrɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛrɪtəbl /; / ˈvɛrɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is truly or really what it is said to be

Examples

She is a veritable genius when it comes to solving complex problems.
The museum is a veritable treasure trove of ancient artifacts.
