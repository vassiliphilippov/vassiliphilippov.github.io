Synonyms

  • authentic
    3.8 20/m
    authentic means real and trustworthy in a simple, direct way, while veritable is a stronger, often figurative intensifier.
  • bona fide
    5.9 1.29/m
    bona fide describes someone or something genuinely real and approved, while veritable is mainly an emphatic word used for effect.
  • unquestionable
    4.4 0.02/m
    unquestionable means cannot be doubted or denied, while veritable emphasizes that something is truly or really what it is said to be.
