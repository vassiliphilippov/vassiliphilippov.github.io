toddlerhood

noun
early childhood stage
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑdlɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈtɒdləhʊd / (BrE)
the period in a child's life when they are a toddler, typically between ages one and three

Examples

During toddlerhood years
Many children learn to speak in full sentences during toddlerhood.
Parents often find toddlerhood both challenging and rewarding as their child becomes more independent.
The rapid growth and development in toddlerhood can surprise many first-time parents.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: childhood - the period of being a child after toddlerhood
  • degree lower: infancy - the earliest stage of childhood, before toddlerhood
