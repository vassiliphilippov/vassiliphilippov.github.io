meat pie

noun
pie filled with meat or poultry
2.7
Pronunciation: / mit paɪ / (AmE), / miːt paɪ / (BrE)
A baked dish consisting of meat or poultry enclosed in pastry or covered with a pastry or biscuit dough crust.

Examples

A meat pie dish
I ordered a chicken meat pie for lunch because it looked delicious.
She served homemade meat pies at the family gathering.
Whenever I visit Australia, I always buy a hot meat pie from the bakery.
