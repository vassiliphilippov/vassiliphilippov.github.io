meat pie

noun
pie filled with meat or poultry
2.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /miːt paɪ/ (AmE), /miːt paɪ/ (BrE)
A baked dish consisting of meat or poultry enclosed in pastry or covered with a pastry or biscuit dough crust.
