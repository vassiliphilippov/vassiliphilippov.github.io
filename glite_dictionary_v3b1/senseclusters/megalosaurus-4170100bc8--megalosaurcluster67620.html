Megalosaurus

noun
genus of large Jurassic carnivorous dinosaurs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəloʊˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡələˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of large carnivorous dinosaurs that lived during the Middle Jurassic period, known as the type genus of the Megalosauridae family.

Examples

Megalosaurus dinosaur fossils
Fossils of Megalosaurus have been found in Jurassic rock formations in Europe.
Many paleontologists believe that Megalosaurus was an apex predator of its time.
The Megalosaurus was one of the first dinosaurs ever described by scientists.
