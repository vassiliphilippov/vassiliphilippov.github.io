balloonfish

noun
a small inflating marine fish
3.1 6.7
Pronunciation: / bəˈluːnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / bəˈluːnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small marine fish closely related to the porcupinefish, known for its ability to inflate its body when threatened

Examples

A balloonfish inflates body
A balloonfish has short spines and a rounded, balloon-like body.
Divers were delighted to spot a balloonfish hiding among the coral reefs.
The balloonfish inflated its body when it sensed a predator nearby.
