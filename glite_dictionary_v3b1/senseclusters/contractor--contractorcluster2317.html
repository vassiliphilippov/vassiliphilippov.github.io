contractor

noun
party to a contract
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtræk.tər/ /; / /ˈkɑːn.træk.tər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtræk.tə/ /; / /ˈkɒn.træk.tə/ (BrE)
a person or company hired to do work or provide services under a contract, especially in construction or business

Examples

That company is a well-known contractor specializing in commercial construction projects.
