wood engraving

noun
print made from an engraved wood block
Pronunciation: / wʊd ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ /; / wʊd ɛnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / wʊd ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
a print made by carving a design into a block of wood and pressing paper onto it, or the technique of making such prints
