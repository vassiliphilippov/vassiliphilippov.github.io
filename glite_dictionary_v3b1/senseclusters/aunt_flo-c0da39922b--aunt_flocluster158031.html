Aunt Flo

noun
euphemism for menstruation
Pronunciation: / ɑnt floʊ / (AmE), / ɑːnt fləʊ / (BrE)
A euphemistic, informal term for menstruation (a woman's monthly period).

Examples

Aunt Flo arrives
She always carries extra supplies in her bag in case Aunt Flo arrives unexpectedly.
Some commercials humorously refer to tampons as products for when Aunt Flo comes to town.
Whenever Aunt Flo visits, she prefers to stay home and rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: period - the monthly flow of blood from the uterus
