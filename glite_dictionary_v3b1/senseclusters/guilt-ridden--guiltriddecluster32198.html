guilt-ridden

adjective
filled with guilt
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪlt ˌrɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlt ˌrɪdn̩ / (BrE)
Feeling very guilty about something; overwhelmed by a sense of guilt.

Examples

Guilt-ridden feelings
Living in a guilt-ridden state can be emotionally exhausting.
She was guilt-ridden after lying to her best friend.
The guilt-ridden employee confessed his mistake to his manager.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remorseful - filled with deep regret for wrongdoing
