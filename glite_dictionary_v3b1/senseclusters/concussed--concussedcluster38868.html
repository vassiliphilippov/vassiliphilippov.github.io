concussed

adjective
having a concussion
7.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈkʌst / (AmE), / kənˈkʌst / (BrE)
suffering from a concussion, typically after a blow to the head

Examples

Concussed player rests
The concussed athlete was advised to rest and avoid bright lights.
After the accident, she felt dizzy and was later diagnosed as concussed.
Doctors monitored the concussed patient closely for any signs of worsening symptoms.
