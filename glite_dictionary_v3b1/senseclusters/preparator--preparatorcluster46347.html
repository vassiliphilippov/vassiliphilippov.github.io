preparator

noun
lab materials preparer
7.4
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpærətɔr / (AmE), / prɪˈpærətə / (BrE)
a person who prepares materials, specimens, or items for scientific, educational, or museum work

Examples

Museum preparator
The preparator carefully arranged the chemicals before the biology class began.
Each museum relies on a skilled preparator to handle delicate fossils and artifacts.
Before the experiment, the preparator ensured all equipment was clean and ready for use.
The preparator carefully arranged the fossils for the museum exhibit.
A skilled preparator ensures that scientific samples are ready for analysis.
Before the art show, the preparator checked each painting for damage.
