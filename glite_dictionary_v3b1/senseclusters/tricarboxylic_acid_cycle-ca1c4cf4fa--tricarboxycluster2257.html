tricarboxylic acid cycle

noun
cellular process for energy production
5.2
Pronunciation: /traɪ.kɑːr.bɑːk.ˈsɪ.lɪk ˈæs.əd ˈsaɪ.kəl/ /; / /trɪ.kɑːr.bɑːk.ˈsɪ.lɪk ˈæs.ɪd ˈsaɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /traɪ.kɑː.bɒk.ˈsɪ.lɪk ˈæs.ɪd ˈsaɪ.kəl/ /; / /trɪ.kɑː.bɒk.ˈsɪ.lɪk ˈæs.ɪd ˈsaɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
