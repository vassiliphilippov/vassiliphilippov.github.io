chuckwalla

noun
large herbivorous desert lizard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkˌwɑlə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkˌwɒlə / (BrE)
A large, herbivorous lizard native to rocky, arid regions of the southwestern United States and Mexico.

Examples

The chuckwalla lizard
Naturalists observed that the chuckwalla feeds mainly on leaves, fruits, and flowers.
The chuckwalla basked motionless on a sunlit boulder in the desert.
When threatened, a chuckwalla will wedge itself tightly into rock crevices.
