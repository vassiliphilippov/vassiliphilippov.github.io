nodular

adjective
having nodules or lumps
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːdʒələr / (AmE), / ˈnɒdjʊlə / (BrE)
having or relating to nodules; lumpy or knotty in appearance

Examples

A nodular rash
A nodular rash can indicate certain underlying conditions.
Chronic infection led to nodular changes in the tissue.
The doctor noted that the patient's liver was nodular on palpation.
Farmers noticed nodular lesions on the roots of the crops.
The doctor observed a nodular swelling in the patient's lung.
This mineral sample has a distinctly nodular structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noduled - having small knots or lumps (nodules)
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface; not lumpy or nodular
