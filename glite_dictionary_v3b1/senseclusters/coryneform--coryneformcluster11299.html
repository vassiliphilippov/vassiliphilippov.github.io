coryneform

noun
club-shaped bacterium
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈraɪnɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / kəˈraɪnɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
a bacterium that is club-shaped and resembles those of the genus Corynebacterium, often identified by its shape in laboratory settings

Examples

A coryneform bacterium
The laboratory detected a coryneform in the patient's blood culture.
Some coryneform bacteria are harmless, while others can cause disease.
Identification of a coryneform often requires further biochemical testing.
