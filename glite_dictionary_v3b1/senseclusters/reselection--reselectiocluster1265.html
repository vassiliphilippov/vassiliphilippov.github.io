reselection

noun
choosing again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːsəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːsɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of choosing or selecting someone or something again

Examples

If you make a mistake, the software allows easy reselection of your preferred language.
The reselection of candidates was necessary after the initial vote was declared invalid.
After accidentally deleting the file, he used the reselection feature to choose it again.
