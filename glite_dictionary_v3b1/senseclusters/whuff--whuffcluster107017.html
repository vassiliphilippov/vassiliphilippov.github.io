whuff

verb
to make soft puffing sound
9.5
Pronunciation: / wʌf / (AmE), / wʌf / (BrE)
to make a soft, muffled puffing or blowing sound, often repeatedly and gently.

Examples

To whuff like steam
The old engine began to whuff as steam escaped from the pipe.
The dog whuffed softly when it saw a stranger at the door.
As the wind picked up, the chimney started to whuff quietly in the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiss - to make a long, sharp sibilant sound
  • synonym: puff - to blow out air in a short burst
