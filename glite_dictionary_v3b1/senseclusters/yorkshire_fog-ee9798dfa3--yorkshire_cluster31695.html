Yorkshire fog

noun
a species of tall grass used for forage
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔrkʃɪr fɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈjɔːkʃə fɒɡ / (BrE)
a tall European perennial grass (Holcus lanatus) with velvety stems, naturalized in North America and used for forage

Examples

Yorkshire fog grass
Farmers often plant Yorkshire fog because it provides reliable forage for livestock.
