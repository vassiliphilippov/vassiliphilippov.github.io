troll

verb
provoke or annoy others, especially online
2.2
Pronunciation: / troʊl / (AmE), / trəʊl / (BrE)
to deliberately provoke or annoy others online or in a social setting, often by posting inflammatory or off-topic messages

Examples

To troll online
Online forums have strict rules against anyone who tries to troll the community.
She realized he was only trying to troll her with that outrageous comment.
Some users like to troll others by posting absurd arguments just to get a reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bait - deliberately provoke a reaction from someone
