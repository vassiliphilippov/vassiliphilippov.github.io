Examples

A moldy smell
A moldy smell drifted from the damp basement.
She threw away the moldy bread before anyone could eat it.
The cheese in the fridge turned moldy after being left out for weeks.
After weeks of rain, the cellar smelled terribly mouldy.

Synonyms

  • mildewy
    4.5 0.06/m
    mildewy is smelling or looking like damp mildew on fabric or walls, while moldy is covered with visible mold and decay.
  • musty
    3.9 2.41/m
    musty describes a stale, damp smell in closed rooms or old boxes, while moldy means actually covered in mold and decay.
