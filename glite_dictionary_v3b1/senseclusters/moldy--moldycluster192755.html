moldy

adjective
covered with or smelling of mold
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊl.di /; / ˈmoʊldi / (AmE), / ˈməʊl.di /; / ˈməʊldi / (BrE)
covered with or smelling of mold, typically showing signs of decay or mildew

Examples

A moldy smell
A moldy smell drifted from the damp basement.
She threw away the moldy bread before anyone could eat it.
The cheese in the fridge turned moldy after being left out for weeks.
After weeks of rain, the cellar smelled terribly mouldy.
She threw away the mouldy loaf she found in the cupboard.
The cheese had become mouldy and was no longer safe to eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - recently made or obtained; not decayed or spoiled
  • synonym: moldy - covered with or smelling of mold (American spelling)
  • synonym: musty - having a stale, moldy, or damp smell
