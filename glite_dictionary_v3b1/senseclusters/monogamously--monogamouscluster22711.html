monogamously

adverb
with one partner
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːɡəməsli / (AmE), / məˈnɒɡəməsli / (BrE)
in a way that involves having only one partner at a time, especially in a romantic or sexual relationship

Examples

Pair monogamously
Many birds pair monogamously and raise their young together each season.
Some people choose to live monogamously throughout their entire lives.
He believes in loving monogamously rather than dating several people at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faithfully - in a loyal and devoted way to one partner
  • antonym: polygamously - in a way that involves having more than one partner at the same time
