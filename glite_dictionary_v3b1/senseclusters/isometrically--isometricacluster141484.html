isometrically

adverb
with equal length
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that maintains equal dimensions or length, especially during movement or contraction.

Examples

Hold isometrically
The athlete held the position isometrically to strengthen her core muscles.
During the test, the device measured how long the patient could contract the muscle isometrically.
Some exercises require you to push against resistance isometrically without changing the length of the muscle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anisometrically - in a way that does not maintain equal dimensions or length
  • synonym: isometrical - having equal dimensions or measurements
