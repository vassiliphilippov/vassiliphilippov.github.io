professorial

adjective
like a professor; scholarly in manner
Pronunciation: / prəˌfɛˈsɔriəl / (AmE), / prəˌfɛˈsɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a professor, especially in manner or behavior.

Examples

Professorial manner
He wore tweed jackets and glasses, giving him a very professorial appearance.
Her professorial approach to the topic impressed her students.
The dean's professorial tone made everyone listen intently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academic - relating to education or scholarship
