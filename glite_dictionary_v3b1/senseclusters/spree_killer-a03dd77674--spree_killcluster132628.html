spree killer

noun
person who kills several people in a short span
7.0 7.3
Pronunciation: / spri ˈkɪlər / (AmE), / spriː ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
A person who kills multiple people in a short period of time, often at different locations, without a cooling-off period between the murders.

Examples

The spree killer attacks

Unlike a serial killer, a spree killer does not pause between attacks.
