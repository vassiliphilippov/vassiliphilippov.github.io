spree killer

noun
person who kills several people in a short span
7.0
Pronunciation: / spri ˈkɪlər / (AmE), / spriː ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
A person who kills multiple people in a short period of time, often at different locations, without a cooling-off period between the murders.

Examples

The spree killer attacks
Police struggled to keep up with the spree killer's unpredictable rampage across the country.
The spree killer moved from city to city, leaving devastation in his wake.
Unlike a serial killer, a spree killer does not pause between attacks.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: mass murderer - person who kills multiple victims at the same time or location
  • degree lower: serial killer - a person who murders several people, typically over a period of time with a cooling-off period
