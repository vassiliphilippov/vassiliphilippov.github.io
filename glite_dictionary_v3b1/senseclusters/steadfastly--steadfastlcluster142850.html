steadfastly

adverb
with firm determination
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛdˌfæstli / (AmE), / ˈstɛdˌfɑːstli / (BrE)
in a firm and unwavering manner; with resolute determination

Examples

To steadfastly pursue goals
He steadfastly pursued his goals throughout his career.
She steadfastly refused to give up despite the difficulties.
They steadfastly supported each other during challenging times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firmly - in a strong and steady manner
  • synonym: resolutely - in an admirably determined and unwavering manner
