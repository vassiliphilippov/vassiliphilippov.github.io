réseau

noun
contact network
9.9
Pronunciation: / reɪˈzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəʊ / (BrE)

Examples

Building a strong réseau can help you find new job opportunities.
Her réseau provided valuable support during her career transition.
