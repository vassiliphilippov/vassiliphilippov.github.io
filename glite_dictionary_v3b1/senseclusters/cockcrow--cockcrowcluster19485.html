cockcrow

noun
the first light of morning
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkˌkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌkrəʊ / (BrE)
the time when the first light appears in the morning; daybreak or dawn

Examples

At cockcrow
At cockcrow, the village slowly came to life.
She finished her letter just as cockcrow broke through the window.
We set out at cockcrow to reach the summit by midday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawn - the time at which day begins; first appearance of light in the morning
  • antonym: night - the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise
  • degree higher: sunrise - the time at which the sun appears above the horizon in the morning
