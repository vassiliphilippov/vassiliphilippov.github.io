nuh

particle
confirmation particle
9.9
Pronunciation: / nə / (AmE), / nə / (BrE)
a particle used at the end of a statement to seek confirmation or agreement, especially in Caribbean and informal English

Examples

Finished, nuh?
You finished your homework already, nuh?
It's really hot today, nuh, so let's get some ice cream.
We should leave early to avoid traffic, nuh?

Related Terms

  • synonym: eh - a particle used to ask for agreement or confirmation
  • synonym: right - a word used at the end of a sentence to seek agreement
