felicitously

adverb
in an apt or pleasing way
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪs.ɪ.t̬əs.li / (AmE), / fəˈlɪs.ɪ.təs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is well-chosen, apt, or pleasing; with appropriateness and effectiveness.

Examples

To speak felicitously
He always chooses his words felicitously, making people feel at ease.
Her remarks were felicitously phrased, leaving a strong impression on the audience.
The poem is felicitously constructed, blending imagery and emotion perfectly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aptly - in a suitable or appropriate manner
  • antonym: awkwardly - in a manner that is clumsy or lacking ease
