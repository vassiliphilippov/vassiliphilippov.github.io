nonpaying

adjective
not paying
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpeɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpeɪɪŋ / (BrE)
not required or not choosing to pay for something; not paying money for a service or product

Examples

Nonpaying visitor
The museum offers free admission for nonpaying visitors.
Only nonpaying members are excluded from the premium features of the website.
The event was open to both nonpaying and paying guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free - not costing any money
  • antonym: paying - required to pay or actually paying for something
