tug-of-war

noun
team sport using a rope
6.1 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtʌg.əv.wɔːr/ /; / /tʌg.ə.ˈwɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈtʌg.əv.wɔː/ /; / /tʌg.ə.ˈwɔː/ (BrE)
