Ryukyuan

noun
Okinawan and related languages of the Ryukyu Islands
Pronunciation: / raɪˈjuːkjuən / (AmE), / raɪˈjuːkjʊən / (BrE)
the group of languages related to Japanese, spoken by the people of the Ryukyu Islands in Japan

Examples

Ryukyuan languages
Efforts are being made to preserve the endangered Ryukyuan languages.
Linguists study Ryukyuan to understand its relationship with Japanese.
The Ryukyuan language is still spoken by some residents of Okinawa.
