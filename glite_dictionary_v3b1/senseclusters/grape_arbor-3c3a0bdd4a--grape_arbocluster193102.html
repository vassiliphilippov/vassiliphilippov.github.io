grape arbor

noun
structure for growing grapevines
8.3 4.6
Pronunciation: /greɪp ˈɑːr.bər/ (AmE), /greɪp ˈɑː.bə/ (BrE)
A wooden framework or shelter covered with climbing grapevines, often in a garden, used to support their growth and provide shade.

Examples

A grape arbor shelter
Clusters of ripe fruit hung from the grape arbor near the back porch.
He built a wooden grape arbor to create a pleasant space for outdoor dinners.
The old grape arbor in my grandmother's garden provided a cool, shady retreat in the summer.
He built a new grape arbour to provide both shade and fresh grapes for his family.
The old grape arbour was heavy with clusters of ripe fruit by late summer.
We enjoyed a picnic lunch under the shady grape arbour in her backyard.
