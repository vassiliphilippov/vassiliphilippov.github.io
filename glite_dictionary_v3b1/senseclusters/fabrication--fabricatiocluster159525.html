fabrication

noun
making or constructing something
Pronunciation: / ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making or constructing something, especially by manufacturing or building

Examples

Fabrication of parts
Advances in technology have revolutionized the fabrication of medical devices.
Local businesses specialize in the fabrication of custom metalwork.
The fabrication of aircraft parts requires precision and skill.
He oversees the fabrication of intricate electronic devices in his workshop.
Modern aircraft rely on the precise fabrication of lightweight components.
The fabrication of the steel bridge took more than a year to complete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: construction - the act of building something
  • synonym: manufacture - the process of making goods on a large scale
