shellacking

noun
application of shellac
8.0
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈlækɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃəˈlækɪŋ / (BrE)
a coating of shellac or the process of applying shellac as a varnish to a surface

Examples

A shellacking on wood
The craftsman gave the antique table a careful shellacking to protect its wood.
After a fresh shellacking, the floorboards shone with a deep, glossy finish.
Proper shellacking can make old furniture look almost new again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: painting - the act of applying paint to a surface
  • synonym: varnishing - the act of applying varnish to a surface
