bubba

noun
informal address for man
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbə / (AmE), / ˈbʌbə / (BrE)
an informal term used to address a man, often in a friendly, dismissive, or confrontational way depending on context

Examples

Hey bubba
Hey bubba, can you help me move this box to the truck?
Listen here, bubba, you need to calm down before things get out of hand.
Some people use bubba as a friendly nickname for their brother or close friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buddy - an informal way to address a man or friend
  • synonym: pal - a casual term for a friend or man
