Synonyms

  • takeout
    1.5 3/m
    takeout is the common short word for food sold to eat elsewhere, while takeout food names the same food more fully.
  • takeaway
    1.5 0.3/m
    takeaway is the British word for prepared food bought to eat elsewhere, while takeout food is the more American term.
  • carryout
    2.7 0.1/m
    carryout refers to food bought to eat elsewhere or the shop that sells it, while takeout food names the food itself.
