Dipsacus

noun
genus of teasel plants
Pronunciation: / dɪpˈseɪkəs / (AmE), / dɪpˈseɪkəs /; / dɪpˈsɑːkəs / (BrE)
a genus of tall, prickly herbs known as teasels, native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, with spiny flower heads
