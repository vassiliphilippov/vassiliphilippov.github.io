clandestinity

noun
state of secrecy
Pronunciation: / ˌklændɛsˈtɪnəti / (AmE), / ˌklændɛsˈtɪnəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being secret or concealed, especially to avoid detection

Examples

The clandestinity of operation
The clandestinity of the operation ensured that no one outside the group knew about it.
He admired the clandestinity with which the documents were exchanged.
Their relationship was marked by clandestinity, as they met only in secret places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secrecy - the condition of being kept hidden or unknown
