reflex response

noun
instinctive automatic response to stimulus
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.fleks rɪ.ˈspɑːns/ /; / /ˈre.fleks rɪ.ˈspɑːns/ (AmE), /ˈriː.fleks rɪ.ˈspɒns/ /; / /ˈre.fleks rɪ.ˈspɒns/ (BrE)

Examples
