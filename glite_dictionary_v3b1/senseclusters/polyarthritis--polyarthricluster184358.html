polyarthritis

noun
inflammation of many joints
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliɑrˈθaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɒliɑːˈθraɪtɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition in which several joints are inflamed at the same time

Examples

Polyarthritis joint pain
The doctor diagnosed her with polyarthritis after she reported pain in several joints.
Treatment for polyarthritis often includes medication to reduce inflammation and pain.
People with polyarthritis may experience stiffness and swelling in multiple areas of the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthritis - inflammation of one or more joints
  • antonym: monoarthritis - inflammation of a single joint
