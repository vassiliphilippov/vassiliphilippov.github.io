twistable

adjective
can be twisted
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪstəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstəbəl / (BrE)
able to be twisted or rotated by hand, especially to open, close, or adjust something

Examples

Twistable lid
The jar comes with a twistable lid that makes it easy to open and close.
She bought a lamp with a twistable knob to adjust the brightness.
The twistable cap prevents spills when you carry the bottle in your bag.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - not able to be moved or changed
  • synonym: rotatable - able to be turned around a central point
