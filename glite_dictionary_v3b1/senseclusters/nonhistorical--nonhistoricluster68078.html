nonhistorical

adjective
not historical
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnhɪˈstɔrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnhɪˈstɒrɪkəl / (BrE)
not related to, based on, or concerned with history or historical events

Examples

A nonhistorical setting
The movie takes place in a completely nonhistorical setting with fictional characters.
Her research focused on nonhistorical factors influencing modern society.
The exhibition features nonhistorical artworks that do not reference any real events.
