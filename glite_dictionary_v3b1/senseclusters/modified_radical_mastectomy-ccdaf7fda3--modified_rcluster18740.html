modified radical mastectomy

noun
surgical removal of breast, minor muscle, and lymph nodes
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdɪˌfaɪd ˈrædɪkəl məˈstɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˈmɒdɪfaɪd ˈrædɪkəl mæˈstɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation involving removal of a breast along with the pectoralis minor muscle and some lymph nodes in the adjacent armpit

Examples

A modified radical mastectomy operation
After her diagnosis, she chose to undergo a modified radical mastectomy to reduce the risk of recurrence.
Recovery from a modified radical mastectomy often includes physical therapy for the affected arm.
The surgeon explained the steps involved in a modified radical mastectomy before the procedure.
