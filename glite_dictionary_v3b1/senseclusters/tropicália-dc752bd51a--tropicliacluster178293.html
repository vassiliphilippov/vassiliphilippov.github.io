Tropicália

noun
Brazilian cultural movement
Pronunciation: / ˌtroʊpiˈkɑːliə / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpɪˈkɑːliə / (BrE)
a person, especially a musician or artist, associated with the Brazilian Tropicália cultural movement of the late 1960s

Examples

Tropicália artist
Many musicians identified as part of Tropicália challenged traditional Brazilian music styles.
The influence of Tropicália can still be heard in contemporary Brazilian art and music.
She became a celebrated Tropicália artist, mixing traditional rhythms with avant-garde sounds in her songs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avant-garde - artists or works that are experimental or innovative
  • synonym: tropicalismo - another name for the Tropicália movement in Brazil
