pudding-face

noun
large, plump human face
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊdɪŋ feɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʊdɪŋ feɪs / (BrE)
A face that is large and plump, often appearing soft or fleshy.

Examples

A pudding-face smile
Everyone noticed the politician's pudding-face as he smiled for the photo.
He was often teased at school because of his round, pudding-face.
The child's pudding-face was flushed with excitement after the party.
