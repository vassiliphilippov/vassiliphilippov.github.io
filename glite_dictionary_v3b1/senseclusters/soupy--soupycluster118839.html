soupy

adjective
thick or dense like soup
8.1 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpi / (AmE), / ˈsuːpi / (BrE)
thick or dense in consistency or appearance, like that of soup; resembling soup in texture.

Examples

Soupy fog
A soupy fog descended on the city, obscuring everything from sight.
After hours of rain, the field became soupy and impossible to walk through.
The air was so humid and dense that it felt almost soupy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thick - having a relatively large distance between opposite sides; not thin
  • antonym: watery - containing too much water; thin in consistency
