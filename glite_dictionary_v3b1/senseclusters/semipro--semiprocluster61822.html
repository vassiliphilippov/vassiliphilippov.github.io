semipro

adjective
partly professional
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈproʊ / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈprəʊ / (BrE)
partly professional, especially in sports or activities where participants are paid but not at a full professional level

Examples

Semipro soccer league
He joined a semipro soccer league to gain more experience before turning professional.
Many semipro musicians perform at local venues while working other jobs.
The city supports several semipro baseball teams that attract enthusiastic fans.
