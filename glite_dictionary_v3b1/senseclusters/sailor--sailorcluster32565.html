sailor

noun
stiff straw hat with flat crown
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlər / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə / (BrE)
a stiff straw hat with a flat crown, traditionally worn in summer and sometimes as part of a uniform

Examples

A sailor hat
She wore a white dress and a sailor to the garden party.
The school uniform included a navy sailor for special occasions.
Vintage shops sometimes sell the classic sailor alongside other hats from the 1920s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boater - a stiff straw hat with a flat crown and straight brim
