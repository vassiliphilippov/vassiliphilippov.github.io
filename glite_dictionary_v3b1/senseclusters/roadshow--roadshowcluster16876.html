roadshow

noun
traveling performance
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊd.ʃoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊd.ʃəʊ/ (BrE)
a traveling performance or event, such as a play, concert, or film, that visits different locations or towns

Examples

Theater roadshow
The theater company launched a roadshow to bring their new play to small towns across the region.
Fans eagerly awaited the arrival of the music roadshow in their city.
The film's roadshow format allowed audiences in remote areas to see the premiere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: residency - a period of regular performances in one location
  • synonym: tour - a series of performances or events in different places
