cold

adjective
offline, air-gapped (crypto/security)
8.0 2/m
Pronunciation: / ko8ald / (AmE), / k598ald / (BrE)
(in computing and cryptocurrency) kept offline or physically isolated (often air-gapped) so it cannot be accessed remotely, especially to protect private keys or sensitive data.

Examples

Cold storage wallet
He moved most of his bitcoin holdings to cold storage on a disconnected hardware device for safety.
Security teams prefer cold backups stored offsite to protect sensitive keys from online attacks or malware.
Don't use a cold wallet for daily purchases because it requires manual connection to sign transactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air-gapped - physically isolated from other networks to prevent electronic communication
  • antonym: hot - connected to the internet and therefore accessible online (opposite of cold in crypto/security contexts)
  • synonym: offline - not connected to or accessible via a network; not online
