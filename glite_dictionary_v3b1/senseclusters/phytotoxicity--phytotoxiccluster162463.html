phytotoxicity

noun
plant chemical damage
9.9
Pronunciation: /faɪ.toʊ.tɑːkˈsɪs.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /faɪ.təʊ.tɑːkˈsɪs.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /faɪ.təʊ.tɒkˈsɪs.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples
