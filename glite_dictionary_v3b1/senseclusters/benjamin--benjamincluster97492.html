benjamin

noun
youngest child
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn / (BrE)
the youngest or last-born child in a family or group

Examples

The benjamin of the family
As the benjamin of the family, he was often spoiled by his siblings.
The team treated her like the benjamin because she was the newest and youngest member.
Being the benjamin sometimes meant getting extra attention from everyone else.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eldest - the person who is the oldest in a group
  • synonym: youngest - the person who is the most junior in age in a group
