arrangement

noun
plan or agreement
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / əˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
a plan or agreement made with someone to do something at a particular time or place

Examples

Travel arrangement
Due to a last-minute change, our arrangement had to be canceled.
The travel arrangement includes hotel and transportation.
We made an arrangement to meet for lunch on Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement between parties
  • synonym: plan - a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something
