inspection

noun
formal official examination
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a formal or official examination of something, especially to ensure that it is satisfactory or complies with regulations

Examples

A inspection of safety
After a thorough inspection, the officer declared the barracks ready for use.
Health officials conducted an inspection of the restaurant following a customer complaint.
The building must pass an annual inspection to ensure it meets safety standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: examination - careful inspection or study of something
  • synonym: review - a formal assessment or examination of something
