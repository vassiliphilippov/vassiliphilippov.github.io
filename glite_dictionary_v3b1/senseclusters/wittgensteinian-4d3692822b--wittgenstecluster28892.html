Wittgensteinian

adjective
of or relating to Wittgenstein's philosophy
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪtɡənˈstaɪniən / (AmE), / ˌvɪtɡənˈstaɪniən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the philosophy or style of Ludwig Wittgenstein

Examples

Wittgensteinian philosophy
Her arguments were distinctly Wittgensteinian, emphasizing the importance of language in philosophy.
Many contemporary philosophers have adopted a Wittgensteinian approach to analyzing linguistic problems.
The seminar focused on Wittgensteinian themes, such as the use of ordinary language in philosophical investigation.
