Alsophila

noun
genus of moths with dimorphic females
Pronunciation: / ælˈsɑːfɪlə /; / ˌælˈsɒfɪlə / (AmE), / ælˈsɒfɪlə /; / ˌalˈsɒfɪlə / (BrE)
a genus of geometrid moths characterized by pronounced sexual dimorphism, with females having greatly reduced wings
