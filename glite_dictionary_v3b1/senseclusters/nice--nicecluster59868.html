nice

adjective
excessively fastidious; easily disgusted
5.8
Pronunciation: / naɪs / (AmE), / naɪs / (BrE)
very particular and easily disgusted about cleanliness, propriety, or minor details; excessively fastidious or squeamish

Examples

Nice about food
He was too nice to eat anything that wasn't perfectly clean and well-prepared.
She is so nice about tidiness that even a smudge will bother her all day.
Some critics are nice to the point of rejecting any artwork that isn't meticulously executed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squeamish - easily made to feel sick, faint, or disgusted
