gusty

adjective
blowing in sudden, brief bursts (wind)
1.3 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌs.tiː/ /; / /ˈgʌs.tɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgʌs.tɪ/ /; / /ˈgʌs.tiː/ (BrE)
(of wind or weather) blowing in sudden, brief bursts or with strong, intermittent gusts.

Examples

A gusty wind
Sailboats darted across the lake in the gusty afternoon breeze.
The gusty wind knocked over the patio chairs during the storm.
We cancelled the picnic because it was too gusty at the park.

Related Terms
