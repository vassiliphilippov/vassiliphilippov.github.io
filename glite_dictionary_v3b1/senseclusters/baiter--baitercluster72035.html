baiter

noun
person who provokes
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who deliberately tries to annoy, provoke, or upset others, especially online or in conversation

Examples

Internet baiter
The online baiter posted controversial comments just to get a reaction from others.
Ignoring the baiter is often the best way to avoid unnecessary arguments.
She recognized the baiter in the group and chose not to respond to his remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instigator - a person who initiates trouble or provokes others
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to stop arguments or fights
  • synonym: provocateur - a person who provokes others, often deliberately
