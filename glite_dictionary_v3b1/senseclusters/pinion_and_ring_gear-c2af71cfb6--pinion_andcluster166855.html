pinion and ring gear

noun
set of angled meshing gears
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnjən ənd rɪŋ ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈpɪnjən ənd rɪŋ ɡɪə / (BrE)
a set of gears that mesh at an angle, commonly used to transmit power between rotating shafts at right angles, especially in automotive differentials

Examples

The pinion and ring gear
Proper alignment of the pinion and ring gear is essential for smooth transmission of power.
Replacing a damaged pinion and ring gear can restore functionality to a car's rear axle.
The mechanic checked the pinion and ring gear for signs of wear in the differential.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bevel gear - a gear working on an intersecting axis, often at right angles
