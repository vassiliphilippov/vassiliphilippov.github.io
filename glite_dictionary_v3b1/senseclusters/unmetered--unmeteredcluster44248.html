unmetered

adjective
without fixed meter
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmiːtərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmiːtəd / (BrE)
not written in a regular metrical pattern; lacking a fixed poetic meter, as in free verse

Examples

Unmetered poetry lines
The poet preferred to write in unmetered lines to express ideas more freely.
Many modern poems are unmetered, giving them a more natural and conversational feel.
Her collection features both traditional and unmetered forms, showcasing her versatility.
