applicative

adjective
readily applicable or practical
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæplɪkətɪv / (AmE), / əˈplɪkətɪv / (BrE)
(of a method, idea, or technique) readily applicable or practical; able to be used effectively in practice.

Examples

Applicative method
He always prefers theories with applicative value over abstract speculation.
The applicative nature of her research has led to many technological innovations.
This technique is highly applicative in solving real-world engineering problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practical - concerned with the actual doing or use of something rather than theory
