malnourishment

noun
lack of proper nutrition
4.8 3.9
Pronunciation: /mæl.ˈnʌ.rɪʃ.mənt/ (AmE), /mæl.ˈnʌ.rɪʃ.mənt/ (BrE)
A condition in which a person does not have enough food or nutrients to be healthy or to grow and function properly.

Examples

Severe malnourishment
Efforts to combat malnourishment focus on providing both sufficient calories and essential nutrients.
Long-term malnourishment can lead to serious and sometimes irreversible health problems.
The doctor diagnosed the child with malnourishment after seeing clear signs of stunted growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnutrition - a condition of poor nutrition due to insufficient or unbalanced diet
