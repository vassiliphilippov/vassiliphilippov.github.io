monolayer

adjective
single-layered
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.nə.leɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈmɒn.ə.leɪ.ə/ (BrE)
consisting of a single layer, especially one atom or molecule thick, often describing films or coatings

Examples

Monolayer film
The researchers created a monolayer graphene sheet for their experiment.
A monolayer coating can significantly improve the surface properties of a material.
Scientists observed the behavior of cells grown on a monolayer substrate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: multilayer - consisting of multiple layers
  • synonym: single-layer - having only one layer
