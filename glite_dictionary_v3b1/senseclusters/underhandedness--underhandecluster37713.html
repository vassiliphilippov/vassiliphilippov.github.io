underhandedness

noun
use of underarm motion
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌndəhˈhændɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of using an underhand method or motion, especially a low or underarm technique in throwing or serving

Examples

Tennis underhandedness
The coach praised her underhandedness for making her serves difficult to predict.
His underhandedness in bowling gave him a unique advantage over his opponents.
Using underhandedness in passing the ball surprised the other team during the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underarm - a motion or throw made with the arm moving below shoulder level
