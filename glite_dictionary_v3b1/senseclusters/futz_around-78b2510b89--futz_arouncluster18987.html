futz around

verb
waste time; be idle
7.6
Pronunciation: / fʌts əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / fʌts əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to waste time or behave in an idle or aimless way, often avoiding productive work

Examples

To futz around at home
He spent the afternoon futzing around in his garage instead of finishing his project.
Stop futzing around and get to work.
They futzed around for hours before finally making a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mess around - to behave in a careless or idle way
  • antonym: work - to engage in productive activity
