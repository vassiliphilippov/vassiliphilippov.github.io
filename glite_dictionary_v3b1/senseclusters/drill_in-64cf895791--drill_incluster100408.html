drill in

verb
teach by repetition and practice
3.2
Pronunciation: / drɪl ɪn / (AmE), / drɪl ɪn / (BrE)
to teach or instill something through repetitive practice or instruction

Examples

To drill in skills
Coaches often drill in the basic moves to ensure players remember them during games.
The safety procedures were drilled in until everyone could recite them by heart.
The teacher tried to drill in the importance of punctuality to her students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instill - to gradually but firmly establish an idea or attitude
