agglomerate

noun
coarse volcanic rock of fused fragments
Pronunciation: / əˈɡlɑːmɚˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈɡlɒməˌreɪt / (BrE)
A coarse volcanic rock composed of large fragments of material fused together.

Examples

Volcanic agglomerate
Layers of agglomerate often indicate a history of explosive volcanic eruptions in the region.
The geologist collected a sample of agglomerate from the base of the volcano.
The mountain's summit was capped with a thick deposit of agglomerate and ash.
