Resurrection of Christ

noun
Christian event: Christ's rising from the dead
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən əv kraɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən əv krʌɪst / (BrE)
