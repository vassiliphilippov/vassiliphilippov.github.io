costing

noun
calculation of costs (business/finance)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒstɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of estimating or determining the cost of a product, project, or service, especially as part of cost accounting.

Examples

Accurate costing process
Accurate costing is essential for setting appropriate prices and managing budgets.
The finance team presented a detailed costing for the new product launch.
We need to review the costing before approving the construction project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cost accounting - the recording and analysis of costs for products or services
