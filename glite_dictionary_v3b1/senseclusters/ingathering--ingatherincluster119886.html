ingathering

noun
collection of things
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌɡæðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of collecting or gathering things, especially crops or resources, into one place

Examples

Ingathering of crops
The annual ingathering of the harvest was a time of celebration for the entire village.
Volunteers helped with the ingathering of donated supplies after the storm.
The museum organized an ingathering of rare artifacts from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collection - the act of bringing things together
  • antonym: distribution - the act of giving out or spreading things
  • synonym: harvest - the process of gathering crops
