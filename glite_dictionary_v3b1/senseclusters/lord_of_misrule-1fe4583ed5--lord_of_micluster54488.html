Lord of Misrule

noun
master of revels during Christmas
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɔrd əv mɪsˈruːl / (AmE), / lɔːd əv mɪsˈruːl / (BrE)
a person appointed to preside over Christmas festivities, especially with a role of orchestrating revelry and disorder; a master of revels in traditional English celebrations

Examples

The Lord of Misrule leads
Historical accounts often mention the Lord of Misrule leading games and playful chaos during Christmas.
In the old country manor, a Lord of Misrule was chosen to oversee the Twelfth Night festivities.
The tradition of appointing a Lord of Misrule faded as holiday customs became more restrained.
