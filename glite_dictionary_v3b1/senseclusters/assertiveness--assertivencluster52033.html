assertiveness

noun
confident self-expression
Pronunciation: / əˈsɝː.t̬ɪv.nəs / (AmE), / əˈsɜː.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being confident and direct in expressing opinions or desires

Examples

Show assertiveness in meetings
Her assertiveness in meetings helped her gain respect from her colleagues.
Some people mistake assertiveness for aggression, but they are not the same.
Teaching children assertiveness can help them stand up for themselves in difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or self-confidence
