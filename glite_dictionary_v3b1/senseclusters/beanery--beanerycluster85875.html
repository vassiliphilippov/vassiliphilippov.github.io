beanery

noun
cheap restaurant
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːnəri / (AmE), / ˈbiːnəri / (BrE)
a cheap or informal restaurant, especially one serving simple or inexpensive food

Examples

A beanery restaurant
We stopped at a small beanery for a quick lunch on our road trip.
The old beanery downtown is famous for its homemade pies and friendly staff.
After the concert, they grabbed burgers at a local beanery that stayed open late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: café - a small restaurant where drinks and light meals are served
  • synonym: diner - a small, informal restaurant serving simple meals
  • degree higher: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
