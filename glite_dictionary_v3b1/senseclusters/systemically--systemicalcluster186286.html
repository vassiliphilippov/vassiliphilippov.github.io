systemically

adverb
affecting whole system
6.5
Pronunciation: / sɪˈstɛmɪkli / (AmE), / sɪˈstɛmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that affects or relates to an entire system rather than just individual parts

Examples

To work systemically
The medication works systemically, treating the entire body rather than just one area.
Pollution can spread systemically through the water supply, affecting many communities.
The company decided to address the issue systemically instead of making small, isolated changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locally - in a way that affects only a specific part or area
