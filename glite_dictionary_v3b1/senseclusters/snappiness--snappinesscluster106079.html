snappiness

noun
tendency to break
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsnæp.ɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsnæp.ɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of breaking or snapping easily, especially in a material or object

Examples

After exposure to cold temperatures, the material's snappiness became a concern for the manufacturer.
