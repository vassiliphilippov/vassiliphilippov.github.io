panopticon

noun
building or system for total observation
9.7
Pronunciation: / pænˈɑptɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / pænˈɒptɪkən /; / pænˈɒptɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
a circular building or system, especially a prison, designed so that everything can be observed from a central point
