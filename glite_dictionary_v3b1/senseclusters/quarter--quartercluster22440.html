quarter

noun
distinct district within a city
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
a district of a city that is characterized by a particular feature, community, or origin

Examples

The French quarter
Many tourists visit the French quarter to experience its lively music and cuisine.
The old Jewish quarter is famous for its winding streets and vibrant markets.
They lived for a year in the artists' quarter of the city, surrounded by galleries and cafés.

Related Terms

  • synonym: district - an area of a city or country, often defined for a particular purpose
  • synonym: neighborhood - a local area within a city or town
