hot sauce

noun
spicy condiment made with chili peppers
1.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /hɑːt sɔːs/ (AmE), /hɒt sɔːs/ (BrE)
A spicy condiment made with chili peppers and other ingredients, used to add heat and flavor to food.

Examples

A bottle of hot sauce

She loves adding hot sauce to her scrambled eggs every morning.
The restaurant makes its own signature hot sauce using local peppers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chili sauce - a sauce made with chili peppers, often milder than hot sauce
