uncalled

adjective
not appropriate
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːld / (BrE)
not necessary or not appropriate in a particular situation

Examples

Uncalled comments
Her criticism of the project was completely uncalled and upset the whole team.
Making such a comment during the meeting was totally uncalled.
The punishment seemed uncalled given the minor mistake he made.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in a situation
  • antonym: justified - having a good reason or being appropriate
  • synonym: unnecessary - not needed or required
