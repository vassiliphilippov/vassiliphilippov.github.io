uncalled

adjective
not appropriate
5.6 0.87/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàk…îld / (AmE), /  ånÀàk…îÀêld / (BrE)
not necessary or not appropriate in a particular situation

Examples

Uncalled comments
Her criticism of the project was completely uncalled and upset the whole team.
Making such a comment during the meeting was totally uncalled.
The punishment seemed uncalled given the minor mistake he made.

Synonyms

  • uncalled for
    5.1 0.72/m
    uncalled for labels an action as unjustified or excessive, while uncalled describes something simply not necessary or appropriate.
  • inappropriate
    3.7 21/m
    inappropriate describes something not suitable in a situation, while uncalled stresses being unnecessary or out of place.
  • unbefitting
    7.2 0.00/m
    unbefitting sounds formal and often refers to behavior not fitting a person or role, while uncalled means unnecessary or inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in a situation
  • antonym: justified - having a good reason or being appropriate
  • synonym: unnecessary - not needed or required
