Synonyms

  • augment
    5.3 7.20/m
    augment increases or makes something greater, often in a formal way, while supplement adds extra to improve or fill a lack.
  • add
    1.0 211/m
    add means joining something to make more or better, while supplement suggests adding extra to improve or make up for a lack.
  • append
    4.6 1.40/m
    append means attach something at the end of a document or list, while supplement means add extra to improve or fill a gap.
  • add on
    3.2 0.44/m
    add on is an informal phrasal verb for attaching extras, while supplement is a more formal way to add to improve or fill a lack.
