Asiatic shrew mole

noun
small insectivorous mammal of eastern Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk ʃruː moʊl / (AmE), / ˌeɪʃiˈætɪk ʃruː məʊl / (BrE)
A small, burrowing insectivorous mammal found in eastern Asia, resembling both shrews and moles.
