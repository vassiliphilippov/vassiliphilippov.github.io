Katipunan

proper noun
Filipino revolutionary group
Pronunciation: / kɑːtiˈpunɑːn / (AmE), / kætiˈpuːnæn / (BrE)
a Filipino revolutionary society founded in 1892 that fought for independence from Spanish colonial rule

Examples

The Katipunan revolution
The Katipunan was instrumental in organizing the Philippine Revolution against Spanish rule.
Many members of the Katipunan risked their lives to fight for independence.
The history teacher explained how the Katipunan inspired later movements for freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurgent - a person who rises in revolt against authority
  • synonym: revolutionary - a person or group involved in a revolution
