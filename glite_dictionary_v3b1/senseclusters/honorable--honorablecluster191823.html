honorable

adjective
having high moral standards
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnərəbəl /; / ˈɑn(ə)rəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɒnərəbəl /; / ˈɒnərəbl /; / ˈɒn(ə)rəbəl / (BrE)
having or showing high moral standards and deserving respect or honor

Examples

It is honorable to admit your mistakes and take responsibility.
