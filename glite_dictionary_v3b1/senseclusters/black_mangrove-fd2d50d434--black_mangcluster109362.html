black mangrove

noun
a coastal tropical mangrove tree
1.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /blæk ˈmæŋ.groʊ/ /; / /blæk ˈmæn.groʊ/ (AmE), /blæk ˈmæŋ.grəʊ/ /; / /blæk ˈmæn.grəʊ/ (BrE)
a tropical tree or shrub (genus Avicennia) found in coastal areas, with dark bark and roots that stick up from the ground
