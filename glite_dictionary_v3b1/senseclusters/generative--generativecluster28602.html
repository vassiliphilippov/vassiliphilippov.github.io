(in computing and artificial intelligence) relating to models or systems that produce new data, content, or outputs (for example images, text, audio, or code) often by learning patterns from training data.

Examples

Generative AI model
The startup uses generative models to create photorealistic images from text prompts.
Researchers developed a generative system that composes realistic music from short user-provided melodies.
Universities teach students to evaluate biases in generative AI outputs and their societal impacts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discriminative model - a model that predicts labels or boundaries rather than generating new data; often contrasted with generative models
  • synonym: generative model - a statistical or neural model that can generate new data or content resembling its training data
