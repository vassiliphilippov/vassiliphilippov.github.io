pump out

verb
produce in quantity or remove liquid with a pump
1.0
Pronunciation: / pʌmp aʊt / (AmE), / pʌmp aʊt / (BrE)
to produce something quickly or in large quantities, or to remove liquid from something using a pump

Examples

To pump out water
After the heavy rain, workers rushed in to pump out the flooded basement.
The crew worked overnight to pump out the water from the sinking boat.
This team can pump out three new reports in a single day.
