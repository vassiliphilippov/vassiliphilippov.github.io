disassociation

noun
termination of Wi‑Fi association
Pronunciation: / ccd6as59so8asic8e6a8359n / (AmE), / ccd6as598asic8e6a8359n / (BrE)
(in computer networking) the termination of a device's association with a wireless network or access point, typically indicated by a management frame and causing the device to disconnect from the network.

Examples

Wi-Fi disassociation
During troubleshooting, the router log showed a sudden disassociation of several clients at midnight.
The smartphone experienced repeated disassociation from the access point, causing slow reconnections.
Engineers implemented a firmware fix to prevent unexpected disassociation during heavy network traffic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: association - the process of a device connecting and establishing a relationship with a wireless access point; opposite of disassociation
  • synonym: deauthentication - a related network action that removes a device's authentication, often preceding or accompanying disassociation
  • synonym: disconnect - to end a connection so that communication over a network is stopped
