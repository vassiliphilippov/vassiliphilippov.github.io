unprepared

adjective
not made ready in advance
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prɪˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈpeəd / (BrE)
not made ready or equipped in advance; lacking preparation for something.

Examples

An unprepared speech
His unprepared speech impressed everyone with its honesty.
She was completely unprepared for the difficulty of the exam.
The hikers found themselves unprepared for the sudden change in weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prepared - having made ready or equipped in advance
  • synonym: ready - fully prepared for something
