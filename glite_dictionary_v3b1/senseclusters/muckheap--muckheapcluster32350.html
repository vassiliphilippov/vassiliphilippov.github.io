muckheap

noun
heap of manure or refuse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌkˌhip / (AmE), / ˈmʌkˌhiːp / (BrE)
A pile of dung or refuse, typically found on a farm or near stables.

Examples

A muckheap pile
Flies buzzed around the old muckheap that had grown over the summer.
The farmer shoveled fresh straw onto the muckheap behind the barn.
They moved the muckheap to the far side of the field to keep the smell away from the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manure heap - a pile of manure
