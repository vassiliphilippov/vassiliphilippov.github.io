orchestrator

noun
organizer or coordinator
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.kə.streɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.kə.streɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that organizes and coordinates people, resources, or complex activities to achieve a goal

Examples

Event orchestrator
As the orchestrator of the event, he made sure everything ran smoothly.
The software acts as an orchestrator to manage different services automatically.
She was praised for being the orchestrator behind the successful campaign.
As the main orchestrator, he ensured every detail of the conference was perfect.
The orchestrator of the project brought together experts from many different fields.
Without a skilled orchestrator, the event would not have run so smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or manages activities or people
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in something but does not organize it
