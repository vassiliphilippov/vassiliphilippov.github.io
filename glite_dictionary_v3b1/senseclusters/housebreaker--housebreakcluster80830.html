housebreaker

noun
someone who breaks into a house to steal
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
A person who illegally enters another person's house with intent to steal; a burglar.

Examples

A housebreaker at night
A skilled housebreaker can often bypass basic security systems unnoticed.
She was frightened to discover that a housebreaker had stolen several valuables during the night.
The police arrested the housebreaker after he was caught leaving the premises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burglar - someone who enters a building illegally to steal
  • synonym: robber - person who takes property illegally by force or threat
