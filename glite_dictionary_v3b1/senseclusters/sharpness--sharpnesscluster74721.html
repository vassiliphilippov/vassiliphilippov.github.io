sharpness

noun
quality of having a fine edge or point
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a thin edge or a fine point that can cut or pierce easily

Examples

Sharpness of a knife
Over time, the sharpness of the pencil wore down and needed to be restored.
The sharpness of the knife allowed her to cut the vegetables with ease.
The jeweler admired the sharpness of the diamond's point under the magnifying glass.
