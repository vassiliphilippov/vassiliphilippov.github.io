imposition

noun
the act of imposing something
Pronunciation: / ˌɪm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɪm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the act of establishing or applying something such as a rule, tax, or restriction

Examples

Imposition of a tax
Many citizens opposed the imposition of new taxes by the government.
The imposition of strict regulations made business more difficult for small companies.
The sudden imposition of a curfew took the residents by surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enforcement - the act of making people obey a law or rule
  • antonym: removal - the act of taking something away
