Pandora

proper noun
fictional moon in the film Avatar
7.4
Pronunciation: / pænˈdɔrə / (AmE), / pænˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
the fictional moon in the film Avatar (2009), an alien world known for its bioluminescent forests, floating mountains, and diverse native life.
