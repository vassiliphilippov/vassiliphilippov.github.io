TCE

noun
toxic solvent chemical (trichloroethylene)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ti si i / (AmE), / tiː siː iː / (BrE)
a heavy, colorless, highly toxic liquid (trichloroethylene) used as a solvent in cleaning electronic components, in dry cleaning, and as a fumigant; known to cause cancer and liver and lung damage

Examples

TCE solvent use
