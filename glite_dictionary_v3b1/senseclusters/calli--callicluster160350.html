calli

noun
plural of callus
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkælaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkælaɪ / (BrE)
the plural form of callus, meaning thickened or hardened areas of skin caused by repeated pressure or friction

Examples

Foot calli
After the long hike, she noticed several small calli forming on the soles of her feet.
The guitarist's fingers were covered in calli from hours of practice.
Wearing tight shoes for days can result in painful calli on your toes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blister - a small bubble on the skin filled with fluid
  • synonym: callus - a thickened or hardened area of skin
