tinglingly

adverb
with excitement or thrill
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋɡlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋɡlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes a strong feeling of excitement, thrill, or pleasurable shiver, often figuratively

Examples

Tinglingly exciting news
The news was tinglingly unexpected and filled everyone with anticipation.
She described the experience as tinglingly magical, unlike anything she had felt before.
The cold wind blew tinglingly across his face as he reached the mountain peak.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks excitement or interest
  • synonym: excitingly - in a way that causes excitement
