eyelash

noun
short curved hair on eyelid edge
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌlæʃ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.læʃ / (BrE)
Any of the short, curved hairs that grow from the edge of the eyelids.

Examples

A eyelash hair
He blinked quickly when a eyelash got into his eye.
Mascara is applied to the eyelash to make it look longer and thicker.
