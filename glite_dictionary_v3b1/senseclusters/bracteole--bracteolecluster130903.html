bracteole

noun
a small bract in botany
Pronunciation: / ˈbræk.ti.oʊl / (AmE), / ˈbræk.tɪəʊl / (BrE)
a small bract, especially one situated on the pedicel of a flower

The botanist pointed out the difference between a bracteole and a full-sized bract.
