constraint

noun
limiting condition
Pronunciation: / kənˈstreɪnt / (AmE), / kənˈstreɪnt / (BrE)
a rule, condition, or limitation that restricts what can be done or how something can be done

Examples

Budget constraint
The project faced several constraints due to limited funding and tight deadlines.
Legal constraints prevent the company from sharing customer data without permission.
Time constraints made it difficult for the team to finish the work on schedule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freedom - the state of being able to act without restrictions
  • synonym: limitation - a condition that controls or reduces what is possible
  • synonym: restriction - a rule or condition that limits what is possible
