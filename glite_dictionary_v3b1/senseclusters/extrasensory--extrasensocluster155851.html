extrasensory

adjective
beyond normal sensory perception
5.7 7.3
Pronunciation: /ek.strə.ˈsen.sər.iː/ (AmE), /ek.strə.ˈsen.sə.riː/ (BrE)
Relating to or denoting faculties or perceptions that appear to operate outside the known senses, such as telepathy or precognition.

Examples

Extrasensory perception
He believes that animals sometimes possess extrasensory awareness of approaching danger.
Some people claim to have extrasensory abilities such as telepathy or clairvoyance.
The experiment was designed to test for any extrasensory perception in the participants.
