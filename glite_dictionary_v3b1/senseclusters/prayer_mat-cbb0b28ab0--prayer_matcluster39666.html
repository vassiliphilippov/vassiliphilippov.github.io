prayer mat

noun
small rug for Muslim prayers
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr ˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈpreə ˌmæt / (BrE)
A small rug used by Muslims to kneel on while performing prayers.

Examples

A prayer mat for Muslims
Before starting his prayers, Ahmed laid out his prayer mat facing Mecca.
She always carried a clean prayer mat when traveling.
The prayer mat was beautifully decorated with intricate patterns.
