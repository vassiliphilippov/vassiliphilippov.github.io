pharmacol

noun
pharmacology abbreviation
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrməkɒl / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməkɒl / (BrE)
an abbreviation used in bibliographies and citations for journal titles related to pharmacology

Examples

Pharmacol citation
The journal title was shortened to pharmacol in the bibliography according to the citation rules.
When citing scientific articles, she used pharmacol as the standard abbreviation for pharmacology.
Many medical journals include pharmacol in their abbreviated titles for reference lists.
