rakishness

noun
charming unconventional quality
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.kɪʃ.nəs /; / ˈreɪkɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.kɪʃ.nɪs /; / ˈreɪkɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being confidently stylish, lively, and a bit unconventional or bold, often in a charming or attractive way
