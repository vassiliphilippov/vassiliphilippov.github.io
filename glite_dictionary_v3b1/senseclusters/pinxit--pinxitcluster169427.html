Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋksɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋksɪt / (BrE)
a Latin word meaning 'painted (it)', used after an artist's name to indicate authorship of a painting

Examples

Rembrandt pinxit on painting
The painting was signed 'Rembrandt pinxit' to show he was the artist.
Many Renaissance works include the word pinxit after the painter's name.
The label 'pinxit' is often found on classical European paintings.
