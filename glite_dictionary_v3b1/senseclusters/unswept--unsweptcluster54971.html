unswept

adjective
not swept; lacking an angled form
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswɛpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈswɛpt / (BrE)
not swept; not having a backward slant or angle (especially describing aircraft wings)

Examples

Unswept wings
Dust collected in the corners of the unswept hallway.
The living room remained unswept after the children played inside all day.
The museum displayed a World War II fighter with unswept wings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: swept - having a backward slant or angle (for wings); cleaned by sweeping
