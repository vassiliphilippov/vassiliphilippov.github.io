dork

noun
informal, socially awkward or dull person
2.5 7.7
Pronunciation: / dɔrk / (AmE), / dɔːk / (BrE)
an informal term for a dull, awkward, or socially inept person, often used as a mild insult (sometimes with obsessive interests)

Examples

She called me a dork for wearing mismatched socks, but I didn't mind.
