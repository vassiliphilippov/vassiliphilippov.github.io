Venetian blind

noun
window blind of adjustable horizontal slats
4.9 3.9
Pronunciation: / vəˈniʃən blaɪnd / (AmE), / vəˈniːʃən blaɪnd / (BrE)
A type of window blind made of horizontal slats that overlap when closed and can be adjusted to control light and privacy.

Examples

A Venetian blind slat
Dust collects easily on the slats of a Venetian blind.
He adjusted the Venetian blind to let in a little more sunlight.
The office was equipped with Venetian blinds on every window.
