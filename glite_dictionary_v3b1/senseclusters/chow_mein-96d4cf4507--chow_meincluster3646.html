chow mein

noun
Chinese stir-fried noodles dish
6.5
Pronunciation: / tʃaʊ ˈmeɪn / (AmE), / tʃaʊ ˈmeɪn / (BrE)
A Chinese dish consisting of fried noodles mixed with vegetables, meat, or seafood, often served with a savory sauce.

Examples

Vegetable chow mein
Her favorite takeout is vegetable chow mein from the local Chinese restaurant.
The chef prepared a spicy shrimp chow mein with plenty of fresh vegetables.
We ordered chow mein and sweet and sour chicken for dinner.
