safety device

noun
equipment that protects from harm
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a piece of equipment or mechanism designed to protect people from injury or accidents

Examples

A safety device in cars
All cars are required to have at least one safety device such as an airbag or seatbelt.
The factory upgraded its old machines with a new safety device to prevent accidents.
Wearing a helmet is a simple safety device that can save lives in sports like cycling.
