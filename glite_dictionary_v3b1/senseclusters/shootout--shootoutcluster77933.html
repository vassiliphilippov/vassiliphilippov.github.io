shootout

noun
gunfight between opposing groups
5.6 2.25/m
Pronunciation: / Àà ÉuÀêtÀåa ät / (AmE), / Àà ÉuÀêtÀåa ät / (BrE)
a violent fight between people or groups in which guns are fired

Examples

A shootout between gangs
Police responded quickly when a shootout broke out in the downtown area.
Several people were injured in the shootout outside the bank.
The movie ended with a dramatic shootout between the hero and the villain.

Synonyms

  • gunfight
    3.2 1.17/m
    a gunfight is a short fight where people shoot at each other, while a shootout is any violent fight involving gunfire.
  • firefight
    6.5 1.86/m
    a firefight is an exchange of gunfire often between armed groups, while a shootout is a violent gun fight between people or groups.
  • gunplay
    7.3 0.35/m
    gunplay is the use of guns to threaten or harm in a dangerous way, while a shootout is an actual violent gun fight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunfight - a battle involving an exchange of gunfire
  • antonym: melee - a confused fight involving many people, usually without firearms
