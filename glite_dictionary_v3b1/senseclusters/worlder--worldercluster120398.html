worlder

noun
global traveler
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldə / (BrE)
a person who travels widely or has lived in many different places around the world

Examples

A well-traveled worlder
Many worlders share their experiences and photos on social media.
As a true worlder, she has lived on four different continents.
The magazine featured interviews with several worlders about their favorite destinations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: globetrotter - a person who travels to many different countries
  • antonym: homebody - a person who prefers to stay at home
