crispness

noun
directness and clarity of style
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsp.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsp.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being direct, clear, and to the point in expression or style

Examples

Crispness in writing
Editors look for crispness and clarity in submitted articles.
I admired the crispness of her presentation at the meeting.
The crispness of his reply left no room for misunderstanding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conciseness - the quality of being brief and to the point
  • antonym: verbosity - the quality of using more words than necessary
