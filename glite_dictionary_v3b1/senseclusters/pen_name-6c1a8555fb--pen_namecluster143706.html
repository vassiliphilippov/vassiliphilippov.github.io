pen name

noun
an author's pseudonym
Pronunciation: / pɛn neɪm / (AmE), / pɛn neɪm / (BrE)
a name used by an author instead of their real name when publishing works; a pseudonym

Examples

Use a pen name
His real identity was a mystery for years because he published all his works using a pen name.
It's common for authors to choose a pen name to maintain their privacy.
Mary Ann Evans wrote her most famous novel under the pen name George Eliot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudonym - a fictitious name used by someone, especially an author
