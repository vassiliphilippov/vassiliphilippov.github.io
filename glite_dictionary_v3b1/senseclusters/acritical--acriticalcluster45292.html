acritical

adjective
not involving a crisis (medical)
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈkrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
(of a disease or condition) not involving or passing through a crisis phase.

Examples

An acritical disease
Doctors noted that the disease progressed in an acritical fashion.
The patient's fever remained acritical throughout the illness.
Unlike malaria, this infection tends to be acritical and lacks a sudden crisis stage.
