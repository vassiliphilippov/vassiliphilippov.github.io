creance

noun
falconry training cord
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkriː.əns/ (AmE), /ˈkriː.əns/ (BrE)
a long, lightweight cord used in falconry to train birds of prey during their early flights

Examples

Falcon creance
The falconer secured a creance to the young hawk before its first training flight.
Using a creance allows trainers to control the bird's movements while it learns to fly.
He carefully coiled the creance after the training session to prevent tangles.
