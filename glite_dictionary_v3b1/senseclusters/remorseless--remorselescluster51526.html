remorseless

adjective
having no pity or compassion
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɔːrsləs / (AmE), / rɪˈmɔːslɪs / (BrE)
Having or showing no pity or compassion; cruel and unrelenting.

Examples

A remorseless storm
A remorseless storm battered the coastline for days, leaving destruction in its wake.
Her remorseless criticism left him feeling defeated and hopeless.
The villain pursued his goals with a remorseless determination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • synonym: merciless - showing no mercy or compassion
  • synonym: pitiless - devoid of pity; cruel
