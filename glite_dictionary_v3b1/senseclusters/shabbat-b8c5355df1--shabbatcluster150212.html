Shabbat

noun
period of rest
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈbɑt / (AmE), / ʃəˈbæt / (BrE)
a period of rest or pause from work or regular activity, often used metaphorically to mean a personal break.

Examples

A personal Shabbat
After a stressful week, he decided to take a Shabbat and spend time with his family.
Many people find it helpful to schedule a regular Shabbat to recharge and reflect.
She declared a digital Shabbat, turning off her phone and computer for the entire weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sabbath - a day of religious observance and abstinence from work
  • synonym: break - a short period of rest from work or activity
