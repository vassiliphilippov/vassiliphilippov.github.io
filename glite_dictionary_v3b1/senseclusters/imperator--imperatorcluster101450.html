imperator

noun
absolute ruler
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpəˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who rules or acts like an emperor, often with absolute or domineering authority

Examples

A strict imperator
The general seized power and declared himself imperator of the nation.
Many feared the new leader would become an imperator and ignore the people's wishes.
In ancient Rome, the title imperator was given to victorious military commanders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power over a country
  • synonym: emperor - the male ruler of an empire
  • antonym: subordinate - a person under the authority of another
