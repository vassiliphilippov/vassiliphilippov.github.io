hustler

noun
shrewd or unscrupulous person
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌslɚ /; / ˈhʌslər / (AmE), / ˈhʌslə / (BrE)
a person who is energetic, clever, and determined to succeed, often by working hard and using resourceful or sometimes dishonest methods

  • antonym: dupe - someone easily deceived or tricked
  • synonym: go-getter - an energetic person who is determined to succeed
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats or defrauds others
