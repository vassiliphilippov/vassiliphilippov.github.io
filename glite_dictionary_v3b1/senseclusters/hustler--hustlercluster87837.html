hustler

noun
shrewd or unscrupulous person
2.6 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌslɚ /; / ˈhʌslər / (AmE), / ˈhʌslə / (BrE)
a person who is energetic, clever, and determined to succeed, often by working hard and using resourceful or sometimes dishonest methods
