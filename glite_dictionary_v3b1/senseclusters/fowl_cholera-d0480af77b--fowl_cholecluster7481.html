fowl cholera

noun
contagious bacterial disease of birds
7.2 6.5
Pronunciation: /faʊl ˈkɑː.lə.rə/ (AmE), /faʊl ˈkɒl.ə.rə/ (BrE)
a contagious and often fatal bacterial disease of birds, especially chickens, caused by Pasteurella multocida, characterized by acute diarrhea and septicemia
