Saipan

proper noun
island in the Western Pacific; largest of the Northern Mariana Islands
Pronunciation: / saɪˈpæn / (AmE), / saɪˈpæn / (BrE)
the largest island of the Northern Mariana Islands in the western Pacific Ocean, known as the administrative center and for its historical importance in World War II
