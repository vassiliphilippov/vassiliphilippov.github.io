leatherhead

noun
informal insult
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəhɛd / (BrE)
an unintelligent or foolish person, used informally and sometimes as an insult

Examples

He is a leatherhead
He called his friend a leatherhead after he forgot his keys for the third time.
Don't be a leatherhead—read the instructions before you start assembling the furniture.
She laughed and said, 'Only a leatherhead would try to fix that without turning off the power.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockhead - a stupid or foolish person
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
