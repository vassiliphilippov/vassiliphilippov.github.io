mat

noun
border or background for a picture
Pronunciation: / mæt / (AmE), / mæt / (BrE)
a piece of material used as a border or background behind a picture, especially within a frame, to enhance its appearance

Examples

A mat for pictures
Before framing the drawing, he carefully cut a custom mat to fit around it.
She chose a blue mat to complement the colors in her painting.
The gallery displayed the photograph with a white mat and a simple black frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mount - an object used to support or display something, especially in a frame
