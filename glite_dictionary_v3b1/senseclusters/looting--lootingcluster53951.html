looting

noun
theft during war or riot
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈluːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of stealing goods, especially during a war or riot

Examples

The looting after riot
Many shops were left empty following the looting that erupted after the riot.
Police attempted to control the crowds and stop the looting during the demonstration.
The city experienced widespread looting after the power outage caused chaos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pillaging - robbing a place using violence, especially in wartime
  • synonym: plundering - violently stealing goods, often in a time of disorder
  • synonym: robbery - the act of taking property unlawfully from a person or place by force or threat
