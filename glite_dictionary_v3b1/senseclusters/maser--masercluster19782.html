maser

noun
device that emits coherent microwaves
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.zə / (BrE)
A device that amplifies or generates coherent microwave radiation by stimulated emission, operating on principles similar to a laser.

Examples

A maser device
Modern applications of the maser include highly sensitive receivers in radio astronomy.
Scientists developed a maser to amplify faint microwave signals from space.
The maser was a crucial precursor to the invention of the laser.

  • synonym: laser - device that emits coherent light by stimulated emission
