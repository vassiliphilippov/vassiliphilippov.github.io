floor show

noun
live entertainment series at a nightclub
Pronunciation: / flɔɚ ʃoʊ / (AmE), / flɔː ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a series of live entertainment acts performed in a nightclub or restaurant, usually featuring music, dance, or comedy

Examples

A floor show performance
After dinner, we enjoyed a lively floor show featuring jazz musicians and dancers.
The club is famous for its spectacular floor show every Saturday night.
The new lounge will offer a different floor show every weekend.
