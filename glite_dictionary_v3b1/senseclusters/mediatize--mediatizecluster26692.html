mediatize

verb
to reduce sovereignty
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêdi…ôÀåta…™z / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêdi…ôta…™z / (BrE)
to deprive a sovereign prince, state, or territory of its direct authority and make it subject to another power, especially in historical contexts

Examples

To mediatize a state
Several small German states were mediatized during the early 19th century and lost their independence.
The process to mediatize a principality often involved complex negotiations among European powers.
After being mediatized, the territory was governed by a larger neighboring kingdom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annex - to take control of a territory and add it to another state
  • antonym: sovereign - having full, independent authority
