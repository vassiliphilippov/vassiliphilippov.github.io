pikelet

noun
small thick pancake
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪklɪt / (BrE)
a small, thick, round pancake, often served with butter or jam, popular in Britain and Australia

Examples

A pikelet with jam
For afternoon tea, she made a plate of warm pikelets topped with strawberry jam.
Children often enjoy eating pikelets as a snack after school in Australia.
He learned to cook pikelets from his grandmother's traditional recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crumpet - a thick, soft bread cooked on a griddle, similar to a pikelet
  • synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on a pan
