corporatize

verb
to make a corporation
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrpərəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpərətaɪz / (BrE)
to change an organization so that it becomes or operates like a corporation, often with a focus on efficiency and profit

Examples

To corporatize a company
The government decided to corporatize the postal service to improve efficiency.
Many universities have begun to corporatize their operations in recent years.
The board voted unanimously to corporatize the nonprofit organization.
The government decided to corporatize the postal service to improve its efficiency.
Many universities have corporatized their operations, focusing more on revenue generation.
Some critics argue that to corporatize healthcare reduces its focus on patient care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incorporate - to form a legal corporation
  • antonym: nationalize - to transfer from private to state ownership
  • synonym: privatize - to transfer from public to private ownership
