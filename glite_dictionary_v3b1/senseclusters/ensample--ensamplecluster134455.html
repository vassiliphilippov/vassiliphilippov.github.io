ensample

verb
to set an example
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈsæmpəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɑːmpəl / (BrE)
to serve as or set forth as an example or model for others to follow (archaic)

Examples

To ensample good behavior
The wise king hoped his actions would ensample his subjects for generations.
Teachers in the past would ensample good behavior through their own conduct.
Her courage in adversity would long ensample those who heard her story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exemplify - to serve as a typical example of something
  • antonym: imitate - to copy or follow as a model
