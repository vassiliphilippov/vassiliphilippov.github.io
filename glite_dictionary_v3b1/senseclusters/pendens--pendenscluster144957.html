pendens

adjective
hanging downward
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpen.denz/ /; / /ˈpen.dens/ (AmE), /ˈpen.denz/ /; / /ˈpen.dens/ (BrE)
used in scientific names to describe something that is hanging or drooping downward, especially in plants or animals

Examples

Pendens leaves
The species name pendens refers to the way the plant's leaves hang down from the stem.
In zoology, the term pendens is used to describe animals with body parts that droop or hang.
The orchid was named pendens because its flowers dangle gracefully from the stem.
