envoy extraordinary

noun
diplomatic official below ambassador
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.vɔɪ ɪkˈstrɔːrdəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛn.vɔɪ ɪkˈstrɔːdnəri / (BrE)
a diplomatic official of a rank below ambassador, sent on a special mission or to represent a government

Examples

An envoy extraordinary mission
An envoy extraordinary traveled to the capital with instructions to improve diplomatic relations.
Historically, countries would send an envoy extraordinary rather than a full ambassador for certain missions.
The president appointed an envoy extraordinary to negotiate the trade agreement.
