diligence

noun
persistent, careful effort
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪ.lɪ.dʒəns/ /; / /ˈdɪ.lə.dʒəns/ (AmE), /ˈdɪ.lɪ.dʒəns/ /; / /ˈdɪ.lə.dʒəns/ (BrE)
careful and persistent effort or work; the quality of being diligent

Examples
