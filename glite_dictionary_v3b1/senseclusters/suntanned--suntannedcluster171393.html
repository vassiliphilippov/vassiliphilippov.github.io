suntanned

adjective
brown from exposure to sun
1.1 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌtænd / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌtænd / (BrE)
(of skin) having a brown or golden color as a result of exposure to the sun.

Examples

Suntanned skin
After two weeks at the beach, his suntanned skin was noticeably darker.
Her suntanned arms contrasted with the pale walls of the office.
The model walked down the runway with glowing, suntanned legs.
