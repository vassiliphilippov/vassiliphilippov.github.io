nonmainstream

adjective
not widely accepted
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmeɪnˌstriːm / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmeɪnstriːm / (BrE)
not belonging to or characteristic of the dominant or widely accepted group, style, or trend

Examples

Nonmainstream music scene
He enjoys listening to nonmainstream music that is rarely played on the radio.
The artist became famous for her nonmainstream approach to painting and sculpture.
Many young people are drawn to nonmainstream fashion trends that set them apart.

Related Terms
