subversive

adjective
seeking to undermine authority or government
7.5
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɝː.sɪv / (AmE), / səbˈvɜː.sɪv / (BrE)
Intended to undermine or overthrow an established government or authority.

Examples

Subversive ideas
He was arrested for distributing subversive leaflets that criticized the regime.
Some view her subversive ideas as dangerous to national stability.
The government banned the book, labeling it as subversive propaganda.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loyal - faithful to a government, cause, or person
  • synonym: revolutionary - actively supporting complete political or social change
