screak

verb

to make a harsh, high-pitched, screeching or shrill noise

Examples

An owl screaked

She screaked in surprise when the cold water splashed on her back.
The rusty gate began to screak every time the wind blew against it.
Her old bicycle would screak loudly every time she hit the brakes.

Synonyms

  • squeak

    squeak is a short, high-pitched sound from small things, while screak is harsher, louder, and more shrill.
  • creak
    2.9 5.08/m

  • shriek
    2.9 7.17/m
    shriek is a loud, high-pitched cry of fear or pain, while screak is a harsh shrill noise often not emotional.
