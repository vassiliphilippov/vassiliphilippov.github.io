antipersonnel

adjective
designed to harm people, not things
5.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /æn.taɪ.pɜːr.sən.ˈel/ /; / /æn.tɪ.pɜːr.sən.ˈel/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.pɜː.sə.ˈnel/ /; / /æn.taɪ.pɜː.sə.ˈnel/ (BrE)
(of a weapon or device) designed to injure or kill people rather than destroy property or material.

Examples

Antipersonnel weapons
He explained that antipersonnel weapons are specifically designed to target individuals rather than equipment.
International treaties aim to ban the use of antipersonnel landmines in conflict zones.
The military buried antipersonnel mines along the border to prevent unauthorized crossings.
