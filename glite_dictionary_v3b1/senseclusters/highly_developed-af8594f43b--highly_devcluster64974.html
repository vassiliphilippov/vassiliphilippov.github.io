highly developed

adjective
(of society) advanced in industry and technology
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli dɪˈvɛləpt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli dɪˈvɛləpt / (BrE)
(of a society or country) possessing advanced industrial, economic, and technological development.

Examples

Highly developed society
Access to education is usually better in highly developed societies.
Only a few highly developed countries have achieved such low levels of infant mortality.
The region is one of the most highly developed in terms of technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: developed - having a relatively high level of economic or industrial progress
  • degree lower: developing - undergoing industrialization and economic growth
  • antonym: undeveloped - lacking industrial or economic advancement
