semiprofessional

noun
athlete paid part-time
3.8 3.8
Pronunciation: /sɛm.ɪ.prə.ˈfeʃ.ən.əl/ /; / /se.maɪ.prə.ˈfeʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /sɛm.ɪ.prə.ˈfeʃ.ən.əl/ /; / /se.maɪ.prə.ˈfeʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)
An athlete who plays a sport for pay on a part-time basis, not as a full-time professional.

Examples

A semiprofessional athlete
As a semiprofessional, she balanced her soccer career with a full-time job in education.
Many former college athletes choose to compete as semiprofessionals before turning fully professional.
The baseball team is composed mainly of semiprofessionals who practice in the evenings.
