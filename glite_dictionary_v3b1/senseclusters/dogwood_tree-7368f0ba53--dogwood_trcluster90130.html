dogwood tree

noun
tree or shrub of the genus Cornus
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡ.wʊd ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡ.wʊd ˌtriː / (BrE)
A small tree or shrub of the genus Cornus, often bearing clusters of small flowers with showy, petal-like bracts.

Examples

A dogwood tree flower
A dogwood tree is known for its beautiful white or pink bracts.
The garden was full of blooming dogwood trees in the spring.
We planted a dogwood tree near the patio for some shade and color.
