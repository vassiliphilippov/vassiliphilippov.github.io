chirk

verb
make a shrill creaking or squeaking sound
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / t É…úrk / (AmE), / t É…úÀêk / (BrE)
to make a shrill creaking or squeaking sound, like that of a door, mouse, or bird

Examples

A mouse chirks loudly
A mouse chirks in the corner whenever it is startled by a sudden movement.
Birds chirking outside the window woke me up early this morning.
The old wooden steps chirked under his feet as he climbed to the attic.
