intake manifold

noun
engine pipe for distributing air or fuel mixture
8.1 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.teɪk ˈmæn.ə.foʊld/ /; / /ˈɪn.teɪk ˈmæn.ɪ.foʊld/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.teɪk ˈmæn.ɪ.fəʊld/ /; / /ˈɪn.teɪk ˈmæn.ə.fəʊld/ (BrE)
a pipe or chamber in an internal combustion engine that distributes the air or air-fuel mixture to the cylinders
