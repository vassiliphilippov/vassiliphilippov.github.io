submandibular

noun
salivary gland under jaw
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbmænˈdɪbjələr / (AmE), / ˌsʌbmænˈdɪbjʊlə / (BrE)
a salivary gland located beneath the lower jaw (mandible)

Examples

Submandibular gland
The doctor examined the patient's submandibular for signs of swelling or tenderness.
An infection in the submandibular can cause pain and difficulty swallowing.
Surgical removal of the submandibular may be necessary if a tumor is found.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parotid gland - the largest salivary gland located near the ear
  • synonym: sublingual gland - a salivary gland located under the tongue
