attractant

noun
thing that attracts
5.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈtræk.tənt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈtræk.tənt/ (BrE)
something that draws people, animals, or attention by being appealing or interesting

Examples

Tourist attractant
The new art exhibition served as a major attractant for tourists visiting the city.
Brightly colored flowers act as an attractant for bees and butterflies in the garden.
The festival's live music was the main attractant for young people in the area.
