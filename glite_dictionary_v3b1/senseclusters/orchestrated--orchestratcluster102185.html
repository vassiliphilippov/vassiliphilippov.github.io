orchestrated

adjective
coordinated by orchestration software
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.kɪ.streɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ˈɔːr.kə.streɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.kə.streɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ˈɔː.kɪ.streɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
(in computing) coordinated and managed automatically by orchestration software or a workflow system, especially across multiple services, containers, or deployment steps.

Examples

Orchestrated cloud deployment
The cloud deployment was orchestrated by an automated pipeline coordinating several microservices.
During the incident, dozens of containers were orchestrated to restart in a specific order automatically.
Their CI/CD system orchestrated database migrations, service updates, and rollbacks without manual steps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automate - to make a process operate automatically without human intervention
  • synonym: coordinate - to bring different elements into a harmonious or efficient relationship
