genus Cajanus

noun
genus of tropical shrubby plants
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs kəˈdʒeɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs kəˈdʒɑːnəs / (BrE)
A genus of erect, densely branched, shrubby perennial plants native to the Old World tropics, including species like the pigeon pea.
