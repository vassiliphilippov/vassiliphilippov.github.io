backseat

noun
seat at rear of a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌsit / (AmE), / ˈbækˌsiːt / (BrE)
a seat behind the front seats in a vehicle, especially an automobile

Examples

Car backseat
He found the missing book lying under the backseat.
She threw her jacket onto the backseat before starting the car.
The children sat quietly in the backseat during the long drive.
