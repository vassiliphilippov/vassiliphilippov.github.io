photoactive

adjective
reacting to light
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
describing a substance or material that reacts or changes when exposed to light

Examples

A photoactive material
The photoactive layer in solar panels converts sunlight into electricity.
Scientists developed a photoactive drug that activates only when exposed to light.
This paint contains photoactive compounds that help reduce air pollution in sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photosensitive - reacting to or affected by light
