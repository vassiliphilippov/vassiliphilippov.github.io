unhandsome

adjective
not attractive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhænsəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈhansəm / (BrE)
not attractive in appearance; lacking beauty or charm

Examples

An unhandsome face
Despite his unhandsome features, he was admired for his intelligence and kindness.
The building was rather unhandsome, but it served its purpose well.
She found the statue unhandsome, yet somehow interesting to look at.
