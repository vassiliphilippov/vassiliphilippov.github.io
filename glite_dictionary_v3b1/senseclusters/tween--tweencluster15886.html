tween

noun
pre-teen child
2.9 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / twiÀên / (AmE), / twiÀên / (BrE)
a child between childhood and the teenage years, usually aged 9 to 12

Examples

A tween girl
Many tweens are interested in both toys and more grown-up activities.
The new clothing line is designed especially for tweens who want to express their style.
Parents often find it challenging to communicate with their tween as they become more independent.

The store has a special clothing section just for tweens.
As a tween, she enjoyed both cartoons and more grown-up shows.
