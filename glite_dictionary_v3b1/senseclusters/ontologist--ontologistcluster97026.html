ontologist

noun
expert in ontology
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in ontology, the philosophical study of the nature of being and existence

Examples

A philosophical ontologist
The ontologist presented a new theory about the nature of existence at the conference.
As an ontologist, she often debates questions about what is truly real.
Many philosophers consult an ontologist when discussing the foundations of reality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metaphysician - a person who studies metaphysics, the branch of philosophy dealing with the nature of reality
  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged in the study of fundamental questions about existence and knowledge
