enframing

noun
setting image boundaries
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfreɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfreɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of composing or setting the boundaries of a visual image or camera shot in photography or film

Examples

Enframing a scene
The enframing of the subject in the photograph draws attention to her expression.
Good enframing can turn an ordinary scene into a powerful visual statement.
The director discussed the importance of enframing in creating the film's unique atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composition - the arrangement of elements in a visual work
  • synonym: framing - the way a visual image or scene is composed within its borders
