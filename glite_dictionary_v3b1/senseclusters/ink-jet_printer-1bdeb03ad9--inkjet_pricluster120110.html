ink-jet printer

noun
printer that sprays ink onto paper
7.1 2.4
Pronunciation: /ɪŋk dʒet ˈprɪn.tər/ (AmE), /ɪŋk dʒet ˈprɪn.tə/ (BrE)
A printer that forms images and text by spraying tiny droplets of ink onto paper.

Examples

An ink-jet printer sprays ink
Because of its low upfront cost, an ink-jet printer is popular with students.
She replaced her old ink-jet printer after it started leaving streaks on every page.
The office recently invested in a new ink-jet printer for high-quality color documents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dot matrix printer - a printer that forms characters using a matrix of dots
  • antonym: laser printer - a printer that uses laser technology to produce images
