hilariousness

noun
extreme funniness
Pronunciation: / hɪˈlɛriəsnəs / (AmE), / hɪˈleəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely funny or causing great amusement

Examples

The hilariousness of jokes
The hilariousness of his jokes made the whole audience burst into laughter.
She could not stop talking about the hilariousness of the comedy show she saw last night.
Their prank was remembered for its sheer hilariousness at every family gathering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humor - the quality of being amusing or entertaining
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious or not funny
