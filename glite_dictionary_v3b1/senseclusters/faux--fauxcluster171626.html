faux

adjective
artificial or imitation; not real
Pronunciation: / foʊ / (AmE), / fəʊ / (BrE)
not genuine; made to look like something else in order to deceive or imitate

Examples

Faux fur coat
Many people prefer faux fur coats for animal welfare reasons.
She wore a faux leather jacket that looked almost real.
The living room was decorated with faux plants to avoid maintenance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fake - not genuine; made to look like something else
  • antonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
