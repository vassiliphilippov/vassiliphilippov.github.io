mint

verb
make coins or medals by stamping
Pronunciation: / mɪnt / (AmE), / mɪnt / (BrE)
to make coins or medals by stamping, punching, or printing metal

Examples

To mint coins
Millions of coins were minted to celebrate the national anniversary.
The government plans to mint a new series of commemorative coins next year.
The medal was carefully minted to honor the achievements of the scientist.
