terry robe

noun
bathrobe made of terrycloth
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛri roʊb / (AmE), / ˈtɛri rəʊb / (BrE)
a bathrobe made of absorbent terrycloth, typically worn after bathing or showering

Examples

A soft terry robe
After swimming, she wrapped herself in a soft terry robe to dry off.
Guests at the hotel found fresh terry robes provided in every room.
He received a luxurious terry robe as a birthday gift from his sister.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathrobe - a loose-fitting robe worn before and after bathing
