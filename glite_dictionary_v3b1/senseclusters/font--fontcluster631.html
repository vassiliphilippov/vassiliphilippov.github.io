font

noun
bowl for baptismal water in a church
8.5 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / fÉ‘nt / (AmE), / fÉ’nt / (BrE)
a large bowl, typically of stone, in a church, used for holding the water for baptisms

Examples

Church font water
The baby was christened at the font in the village church.
The ornate marble font dates back to the twelfth century.
Visitors admired the ancient font carved with religious symbols.
