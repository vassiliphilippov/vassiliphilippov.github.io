sprout

noun
young plant shoot or bud
1.8 6.38/m
Pronunciation: / spraÊŠt / (AmE), / spraÊŠt / (BrE)
a young shoot or new growth on a plant, especially from a seed

Examples

A green sprout
After just a week in the soil, the seeds began to send up sprouts.
She carefully pruned the sprouts to help the plant grow stronger.
Tiny sprouts appeared on the branches in early spring.
Each seed eventually produces a sprout before growing into a mature plant.
The gardener pointed to the green sprout emerging from the soil.
Tiny sprouts appeared on the branches after the first spring rain.

Synonyms

  • shoot
    3.0 3.06/m
    shoot is a young plant branch or stem that grows from the main plant, while sprout is the first growth from a seed.

  • synonym: shoot - a young branch or growth from a plant
