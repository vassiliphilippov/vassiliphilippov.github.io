missal

noun
book of prayers for celebrating Mass
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪs.əl / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.əl / (BrE)
a book containing all the prayers, readings, and responses necessary for the celebration of the Mass throughout the liturgical year, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.

Examples

The missal book
A new translation of the Roman missal was introduced to the parish last year.
Her grandmother’s treasured missal was passed down as a family heirloom.
The priest opened the missal and began to recite the opening prayers for the congregation.
