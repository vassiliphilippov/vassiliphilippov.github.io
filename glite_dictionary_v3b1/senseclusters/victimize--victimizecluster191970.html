Synonyms

  • mistreat
    2.9 2.91/m
    mistreat means treating a person or animal badly or unfairly, while victimize emphasizes causing someone to suffer as a victim.
  • ill-treat
    5.2 0.01/m
    ill-treat describes unkind or unfair handling of someone, while victimize stresses making them a victim or target.
  • abuse
    3.7 14/m
    abuse often means using force, power, or cruel words to hurt someone, while victimize focuses on making someone a victim.
  • brutalize
    7.7 1.61/m
