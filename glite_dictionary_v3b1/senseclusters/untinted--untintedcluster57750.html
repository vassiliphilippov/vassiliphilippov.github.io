untinted

adjective
unaffected; unbiased
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪntɪd / (BrE)
not influenced, affected, or colored by a particular quality, feeling, or bias; pure or neutral.

Examples

Untinted opinion
Her report was praised for its untinted analysis of the situation.
He tried to keep his opinion untinted by personal feelings.
The article offered an untinted perspective on the controversial issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biased - showing an unreasonable preference or dislike
  • synonym: neutral - not supporting or favoring either side
  • synonym: unbiased - not influenced by personal feelings or opinions
