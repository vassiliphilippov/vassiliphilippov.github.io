hingeless

adjective
without hinges
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪndʒləs / (AmE), / ˈhɪndʒləs / (BrE)
not having a hinge or hinges; lacking a jointed mechanism for opening or closing.

Examples

A hingeless door

Many modern phone cases feature a hingeless design for a sleeker appearance.
The storage box has a hingeless lid that simply lifts off.
