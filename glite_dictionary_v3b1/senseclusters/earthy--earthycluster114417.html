earthy

adjective
resembling or pertaining to earth or soil
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθi /; / ˈɝːθi / (AmE), / ˈɜːθi / (BrE)
having qualities of earth or nature, such as a natural or soil-like appearance, smell, or taste

Examples

Earthy smell
He built a shelter using earthy materials found nearby.
The artist used earthy tones to give the painting a natural feel.
The wine had an earthy aroma that reminded her of a forest floor.
She preferred the earthy flavors of whole grain bread to white bread.
The newly dug vegetables had an earthy smell that filled the kitchen.
The wine had a distinct, earthy aroma reminiscent of damp forest soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthlike - having qualities resembling earth; similar to earth
  • synonym: natural - not artificial or man-made; existing in nature
  • synonym: soil-like - having qualities or the appearance of soil
