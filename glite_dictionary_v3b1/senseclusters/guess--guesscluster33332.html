guess

verb
suppose or believe without certainty
Pronunciation: / ɡɛs / (AmE), / ɡɛs / (BrE)
to form an opinion or belief without certain knowledge; to suppose or believe something to be true or likely

Examples

To guess so

I guessed that it would rain, so I brought my umbrella.
You can guess what happened next, can't you?
