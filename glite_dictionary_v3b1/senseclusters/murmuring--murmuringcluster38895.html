murmuring

noun

a low, continuous, and indistinct sound, often produced by people speaking softly or by natural phenomena

Examples

Soft murmuring sound
A gentle murmuring could be heard from the audience as they waited for the show to begin.
After the announcement, there was a murmuring of disappointment among the crowd.
The murmuring of the stream created a peaceful atmosphere in the forest.
