Synonyms

  • bush
    3.7 10/m
    bush is the everyday, informal name for wild wooded areas people use in speech, while bushland appears on maps and reports.
  • shrubland
    4.6 0.13/m
    shrubland has mostly low shrubs and few trees, while bushland often includes both shrubs and many trees in Australian areas.
  • brushland
    4.6 0.04/m
    brushland is land with bushes and few or no trees, while bushland can also have more trees and denser growth.
  • scrubland
    6.6 0.20/m
    scrubland has low, stunted shrubs in poor soils and is often uncultivable, while bushland describes broader wild areas with trees.
