take the low road

verb
choose a less moral option
4.1 6.6
Pronunciation: /teɪk ðə loʊ roʊd/ /; / /teɪk ðiː loʊ roʊd/ (AmE), /teɪk ðə ləʊ rəʊd/ /; / /teɪk ðiː ləʊ rəʊd/ (BrE)
