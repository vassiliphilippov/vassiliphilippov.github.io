fastidious

adjective
very attentive to detail, hard to please
5.9
Pronunciation: / fæˈstɪdiəs / (AmE), / fæˈstɪdiəs / (BrE)
very attentive to detail, hard to please, or excessively concerned with cleanliness or accuracy

Examples

Fastidious about cleanliness
His fastidious attention to detail impressed everyone on the team.
She is fastidious about keeping her desk organized and spotless.
Some editors are so fastidious that they will spend hours correcting minor errors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
  • antonym: sloppy - careless and lacking attention to detail
