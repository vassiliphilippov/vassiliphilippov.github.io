Sephiroth

proper noun
Kabbalistic attributes

Pronunciation: / sɛˈfɪrəθ / (AmE), / sɛˈfɪərəθ / (BrE)

Scholars study the Sephiroth to better understand the structure of the universe in Kabbalah.
