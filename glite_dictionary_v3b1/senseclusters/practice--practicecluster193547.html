Synonyms

  • exercise
    1.4 2.82/m
    exercise involves doing an activity regularly to improve a skill, while practice focuses on repeating actions to learn and master it.
  • drill
    2.9 2.97/m
    drill repeats short, structured exercises many times to build habit, while practice is broader repeated work to improve overall skill.
  • train
    1.0 21/m
    train prepares someone over time, often with a coach or plan, while practice is doing tasks repeatedly to improve skill.
