prompt

verb
assist by suggesting forgotten words
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /prɑːmpt/ (AmE), /prɒmpt/ (BrE)
to help an actor or speaker by suggesting the next words of something they have forgotten or not fully learned

Examples

To prompt an actor
She needed someone to prompt her during the rehearsal when she missed a few words.
The teacher discreetly prompted the student when he struggled to remember his part in the play.
Whenever the actor forgot his lines, the stage manager would prompt him from the wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cue - give an actor a signal to begin or continue speaking
