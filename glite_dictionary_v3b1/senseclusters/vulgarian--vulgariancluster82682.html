vulgarian

noun
person lacking refinement, especially ostentatious with wealth
Pronunciation: / vʌlˈɡɛriən / (AmE), / vʌlˈɡɛːriən / (BrE)
a person who is considered vulgar, especially someone who makes an ostentatious display of wealth or lacks refinement in manners or taste.

Examples

A vulgarian person
Despite his wealth, the press often described him as a vulgarian lacking social grace.
He was regarded as a vulgarian because of his flashy cars and tasteless parties.
She thought the guests were mostly vulgarians who enjoyed showing off their riches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nouveau riche - a person who has recently acquired wealth, typically in a showy or vulgar way
