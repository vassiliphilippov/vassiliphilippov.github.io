eventless

adjective
without notable events
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɛntləs / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛntləs / (BrE)
having no significant or notable events; uneventful or lacking in activity

Examples

An eventless weekend
The weekend was completely eventless, and I spent most of it reading at home.
After an eventless journey, they arrived safely at their destination.
His days at the new job were so eventless that he started looking for new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eventful - full of interesting or important events
  • synonym: uneventful - not marked by interesting or important events
