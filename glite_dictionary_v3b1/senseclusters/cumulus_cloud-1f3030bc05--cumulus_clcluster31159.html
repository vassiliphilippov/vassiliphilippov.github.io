cumulus cloud

noun
rounded, fluffy fair-weather cloud
3.9 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkjumj…ôl…ôs kla äd / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêmj äl…ôs kla äd / (BrE)

Examples

A large cumulus cloud
