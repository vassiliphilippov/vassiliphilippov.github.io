lyricist

noun
a person who writes song lyrics
4.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪ.rɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /ˈlɪ.rɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who writes the words for songs, especially popular songs or musicals.

Examples

A lyricist writes songs

She is a talented lyricist who has written songs for many famous artists.
The musical's lyricist worked closely with the composer to create memorable tunes.
