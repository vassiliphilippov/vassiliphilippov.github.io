hence the

adverb
therefore; for this reason (phrase)
4.7
Pronunciation: / hɛns ðə / (AmE), / hɛns ðə / (BrE)
used to indicate that what follows is a direct consequence of what has just been mentioned

Examples

Hence the low grade
He didn't study, hence the low grade.
She was exhausted, hence the early bedtime.
The roads were icy, hence the need for extra caution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: therefore - for that reason; consequently
  • synonym: thus - as a result or consequence of this; therefore
