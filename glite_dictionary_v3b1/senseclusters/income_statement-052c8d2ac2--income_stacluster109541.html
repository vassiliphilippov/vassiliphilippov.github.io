income statement

noun
financial record of company profit and loss
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.kəm ˈsteɪt.mənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.kʌm ˈsteɪt.mənt / (BrE)
A financial document that shows a company's revenues, expenses, and profits or losses over a specific period of time.

Examples

The income statement report
Before making an investment, you should carefully review the income statement for any signs of financial instability.
The accountant prepares the income statement at the end of every fiscal year.
The company's quarterly income statement revealed a significant increase in profits compared to last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profit and loss account - British term for income statement, a statement showing revenues and expenses
