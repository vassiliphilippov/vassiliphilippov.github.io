rebarbative

adjective
tending to repel or be unpleasant
9.7
Pronunciation: / riˈbɑrbətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈbɑːbətɪv / (BrE)
unattractive and objectionable; tending to repel or be unpleasant

Examples

A rebarbative manner
Despite his intelligence, his rebarbative manner made it difficult for colleagues to work with him.
Her rebarbative comments during the meeting discouraged further discussion.
The museum's new exhibit was criticized as unnecessarily rebarbative and alienating to visitors.
