microsurgeon

noun
delicate surgery doctor
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɜrdʒən / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
a doctor who performs surgery using a microscope and specialized fine instruments

Examples

A microsurgeon operates
The skilled microsurgeon reattached the patient's severed finger using advanced techniques.
After years of training, she became a leading microsurgeon in reconstructive hand surgery.
A microsurgeon must have steady hands and excellent vision to operate on tiny blood vessels.
The skilled microsurgeon was able to reconnect the tiny blood vessels in the patient's hand.
After years of training, she became a leading microsurgeon in the hospital's reconstructive surgery department.
A microsurgeon often works with powerful microscopes to perform delicate procedures on nerves and tissues.
