Gasherbrum

proper noun
mountain massif in the Karakoram, Kashmir
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæʃərˌbrum / (AmE), / ˈɡæʃəbruːm / (BrE)
a mountain massif in the Karakoram range of northern Kashmir, known for several high peaks including Gasherbrum I and II, with the highest reaching 8,068 meters (26,470 feet)
