break

noun
a short pause from activity
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /breɪk/ (AmE), /breɪk/ (BrE)
a short period of rest or pause from work or activity; an interruption in continuity

Examples

A lunch break
After working for hours, he needed a short break to rest his eyes.
Let's take a break and grab some coffee before we continue.
