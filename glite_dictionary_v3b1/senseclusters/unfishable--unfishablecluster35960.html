unfishable

adjective
not suitable for fishing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪʃəbl / (BrE)
not suitable or possible to fish in, often due to pollution or other adverse conditions

Examples

An unfishable lake
After the oil spill, the lake was declared unfishable by local authorities.
Heavy pollution made the once-popular river completely unfishable for years.
The drought left many streams unfishable due to low water levels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fishable - suitable or possible for fishing
  • synonym: unusable - not able to be used
