pterygoid process

noun
bony projections of the sphenoid bone in the skull
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛr.ɪˌɡɔɪd ˈprɑː.ses / (AmE), / ˈtɛr.ɪˌɡɔɪd ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
either of two bony projections descending from the sphenoid bone in the skull, forming part of the nasal and oral cavities
