overlock

noun
sewing machine type
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərlɑk / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlɒk / (BrE)
a sewing machine that trims and sews over the edge of fabric to prevent fraying, also known as a serger

Examples

An overlock machine
She finished the raw edges of the fabric using her overlock.
An overlock is essential for creating professional-looking seams in garment making.
The tailor recommended buying an overlock to speed up the sewing process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serger - a sewing machine that makes overlock stitches
  • synonym: sewing machine - a machine used to stitch fabric together
