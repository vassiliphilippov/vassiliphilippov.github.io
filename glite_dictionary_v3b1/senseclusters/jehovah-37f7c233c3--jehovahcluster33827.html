Jehovah

proper noun
a name for God (YHVH) in the Old Testament
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈhoʊvə / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhəʊvə / (BrE)
a name for God, especially in the Old Testament, used as a transliteration of the Hebrew name YHVH in Judaism and Christianity
