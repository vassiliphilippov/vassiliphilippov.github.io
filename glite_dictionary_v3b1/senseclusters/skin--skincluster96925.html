skin

noun
animal-hide liquid container
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
a container for liquids made from the hide of an animal, traditionally used to store and carry water, wine, or oil

Examples

A water skin
He offered us a drink from his skin as we rested by the fire.
The shepherd filled his skin with fresh water before the long journey.
Wine was kept cool in a skin slung over the donkey's back.
