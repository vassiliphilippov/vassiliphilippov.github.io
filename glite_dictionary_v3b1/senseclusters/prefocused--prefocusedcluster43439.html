prefocused

adjective
set in focus beforehand
5.8
Pronunciation: / priːˈfoʊkəst / (AmE), / priːˈfəʊkəst / (BrE)
adjusted to be in focus in advance, before use or before an event occurs

Examples

The microscope came prefocused for easy use by students in the lab.
