Svalbard

proper noun
Norwegian Arctic archipelago
Pronunciation: / ˈsvɑlˌbɑrd / (AmE), / ˈsvalgˌbɑːd / (BrE)
a Norwegian archipelago located in the Arctic Ocean, known for its remote terrain, glaciers, and polar wildlife

Examples

Svalbard islands
Few people live permanently on Svalbard, but researchers visit the islands year-round.
The unique wildlife of Svalbard includes polar bears, reindeer, and Arctic foxes.
Tourists visit Svalbard to experience the midnight sun and polar night.
