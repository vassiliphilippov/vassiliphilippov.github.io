pigeon guillemot

noun
northern Pacific seabird (Cepphus columba)
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒən ˈɡɪlɪˌmɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn ˈɡɪlɪmɒt / (BrE)
