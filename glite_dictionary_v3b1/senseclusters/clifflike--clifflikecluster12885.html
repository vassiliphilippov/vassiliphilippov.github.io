clifflike

adjective
resembling a cliff
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the steep, high characteristics of a cliff

Examples

Clifflike steep edges
The clifflike edges of the mountain made climbing dangerous for inexperienced hikers.
A clifflike drop separated the two plateaus, making travel between them difficult.
The building's clifflike facade towered over the narrow street below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precipitous - very steep or high, like a precipice
  • synonym: sheer - extremely steep or almost vertical
