modern ballet

noun
contemporary style of ballet dance
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdɚn bæˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɒdn̩ bæˈleɪ / (BrE)
a form of ballet that incorporates contemporary techniques and a wider variety of movements than traditional classical ballet

Examples

Modern ballet dance
She has been training in modern ballet since she was a teenager.
The new production blends modern ballet with elements of jazz dance.
Unlike classical ballet, modern ballet allows for greater freedom of expression.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classical ballet - the traditional, formal style of ballet
