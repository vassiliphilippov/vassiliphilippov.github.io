maquette

noun
preliminary scale model
9.0
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈket/ /; / /mæ.ˈket/ (AmE), /mə.ˈket/ /; / /mæ.ˈket/ (BrE)
a small model or rough draft made to plan or test the design of a larger artwork, sculpture, building, or set
