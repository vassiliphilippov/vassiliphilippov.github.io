Examples

To poll a tree
Every spring, the gardener polls the willow trees along the riverbank.
The farmer decided to poll the old oaks to encourage new growth.

Synonyms

  • top
    4.1 1.48/m
    top means cutting the top part off a tree or bush, while poll means trimming a projecting top like branches or horns.
  • pollard
    9.8 0.02/m
    pollard means cutting a tree's upper branches to encourage dense new growth, while poll means cutting a projecting top more generally.
