Todea superba

noun
New Zealand fern with woolly stalks
Pronunciation: / toʊˈdiːə suːˈpɜrbə / (AmE), / təʊˈdiːə suːˈpɜːbə / (BrE)
A large fern native to New Zealand, distinguished by its pinnate fronds and densely woolly stalks; occasionally included in the genus Todea.
