guttering

noun
roof rainwater channels
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌ.tər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgʌ.tə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
a system of channels or pipes attached to the edge of a roof to collect and carry away rainwater

Examples

Roof guttering system
The old guttering on the house needed to be replaced before the rainy season.
Leaves and debris can easily block guttering, causing water to overflow.
After the storm, workers inspected the guttering for any signs of damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drainpipe - a pipe that carries rainwater from the gutter to the ground
  • synonym: gutter - a channel at the edge of a street or roof for carrying off water
