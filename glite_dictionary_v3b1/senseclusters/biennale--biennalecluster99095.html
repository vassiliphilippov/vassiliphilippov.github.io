biennale

noun
two-year art event
Pronunciation: / biːəˈnɑːleɪ / (AmE), / biːəˈnɑːleɪ / (BrE)
a large art exhibition or event that is held every two years

Examples

The biennale exhibition
The Venice biennale attracts artists and visitors from around the world.
She was thrilled when her sculpture was selected for the international biennale.
Many cities now host a biennale to showcase contemporary art and culture.
The city hosted its first biennale to showcase contemporary art from local and international artists.
Many artists dream of being invited to participate in the prestigious biennale held in Venice.
The biennale attracted thousands of visitors eager to see the latest trends in modern art.

Related Terms

  • antonym: annual - happening once every year
  • synonym: exhibition - a public display of works of art or items of interest
  • synonym: festival - a series of performances or events, often cultural or artistic
