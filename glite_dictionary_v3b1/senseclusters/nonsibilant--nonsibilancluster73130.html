nonsibilant

adjective
not hissing sound
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɪbɪlənt / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɪbɪlənt / (BrE)
not producing a hissing sound, especially when describing certain speech sounds or consonants

Examples

Nonsibilant consonant sound
The letter 'b' is a nonsibilant consonant in English.
Unlike 's' or 'z', 'm' is classified as a nonsibilant sound.
Linguists often compare sibilant and nonsibilant consonants when analyzing speech patterns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sibilant - producing a hissing sound, like 's' or 'sh'
