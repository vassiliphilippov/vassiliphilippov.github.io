circle

noun
informal group of people
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkl̩ / (BrE)
a group of people with a shared interest or association, often unofficial or informal

Examples

A social circle
He moved in influential circles throughout his career.
Rumors often spread quickly within a small social circle.
She was welcomed into the literary circle of her university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clique - a small, exclusive group; a synonym in social contexts
  • synonym: set - a group of people with shared interests, especially in society
