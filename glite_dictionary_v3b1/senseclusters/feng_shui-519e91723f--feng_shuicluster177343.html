feng shui

noun
Chinese system for arranging space harmoniously
Pronunciation: / fʌŋ ˈʃweɪ, ˈfɛŋ ˈʃuːi /; / fʌŋ ˈʃweɪ / (AmE), / fʌŋ ˈʃweɪ, ˈfɛŋ ˈʃuːi /; / fʌŋ ˈʃweɪ / (BrE)
a traditional Chinese system for arranging buildings and objects to create harmony with the environment
