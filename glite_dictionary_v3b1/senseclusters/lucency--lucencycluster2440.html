lucency

noun
transparency or translucence
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈluː.sən.siː/ /; / /ˈluː.sen.siː/ (AmE), /ˈluː.sən.siː/ /; / /ˈluː.sen.siː/ (BrE)
the quality of allowing light to pass through, making something translucent or transparent

Examples

Glass lucency
The X-ray revealed a region of lucency in the patient's bone.
The artist admired the lucency of the stained glass window as sunlight streamed through.
Modern smartphone screens are designed for maximum lucency and clarity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opacity - the quality of not allowing light to pass through
  • synonym: translucency - the quality of allowing some light to pass through but not detailed shapes
  • synonym: transparency - the quality of being able to see through something
