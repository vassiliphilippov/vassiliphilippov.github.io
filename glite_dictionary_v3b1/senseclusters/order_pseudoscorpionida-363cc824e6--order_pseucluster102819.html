order Pseudoscorpionida

noun
an order of arachnids; false scorpions
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ ˌsjuːdoʊˌskɔːrpiˈoʊnɪdə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌsjuːdəʊˌskɔːpiˈɒnɪdə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of small arachnids known as false scorpions, characterized by pincer-like pedipalps and the absence of a tail or stinger.

Examples

The order Pseudoscorpionida
Scientists discovered a new species within the order Pseudoscorpionida in the rainforest.
The order Pseudoscorpionida includes many tiny, harmless arachnids found worldwide.
Unlike true scorpions, members of the order Pseudoscorpionida do not have a stinger.
