calorimetry

noun
measurement of heat quantities
7.4
Pronunciation: / kæləˈrɪmətɹi / (AmE), / kæləˈrɪmətɹi / (BrE)
the measurement of the amount of heat released or absorbed during chemical reactions or physical changes

Examples

Calorimetry measurement
Advancements in calorimetry have improved the accuracy of measuring calories in foods.
She wrote her thesis on innovative techniques in calorimetry for chemical processes.
The laboratory course included an experiment on calorimetry to determine the heat of reaction.
