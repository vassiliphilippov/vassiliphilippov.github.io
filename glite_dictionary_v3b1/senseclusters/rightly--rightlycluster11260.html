rightly

adverb
in a fair or deserving way
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtli / (AmE), / ˈraɪtli / (BrE)
in a fair, just, or morally correct way; as is deserved or appropriate

Examples

To be rightly proud
Many believe the decision was rightly made in light of the evidence.
She was rightly praised for her dedication to the project.
The award was rightly given to someone who worked tirelessly for the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: justly - in a fair or morally right way
  • antonym: wrongly - in a way that is not correct or fair
