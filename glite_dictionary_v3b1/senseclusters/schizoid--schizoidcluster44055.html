schizoid

noun
person with symptoms less severe than schizophrenia
7.5 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪ.zɔɪd/ /; / /ˈskɪd.zɔɪd/ /; / /ˈskɪt.sɔɪd/ (AmE), /ˈskɪ.zɔɪd/ /; / /ˈskɪd.zɔɪd/ /; / /ˈskɪt.sɔɪd/ (BrE)
