avoidant

adjective
reluctant with intimacy
7.7
Pronunciation: / əˈvɔɪdənt / (AmE), / əˈvɔɪdənt / (BrE)
reluctant to form close relationships or to face uncomfortable situations; tending to avoid intimacy or things that cause anxiety

Examples

Avoidant personality type
People with an avoidant attachment style often struggle to express their emotions in relationships.
His avoidant behavior made it difficult for him to connect deeply with friends and family.
Therapists may help clients recognize avoidant patterns that prevent them from forming close bonds.
People with an avoidant personality may find it difficult to form close relationships.
Her avoidant behavior made it hard for her to participate in group activities.
He became increasingly avoidant after experiencing several stressful events at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distant - emotionally detached or reserved
  • antonym: open - willing to share feelings and form close relationships
  • antonym: sociable - enjoying the company of others; outgoing
  • synonym: withdrawn - not wanting to communicate or be involved with others
