rove beetle

noun
a type of predatory or scavenging beetle
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊv ˌbiːt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊv ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
A small, active beetle of the family Staphylinidae, typically with predatory or scavenging habits and very short wing covers.

Examples

A rove beetle insect
Gardeners appreciate the presence of rove beetles because they feed on pests.

The rove beetle is known for its swift movements and predatory behavior.
