John the Evangelist

(In the New Testament) one of the disciples of Jesus, traditionally regarded as the author of the Fourth Gospel, three epistles, and the Book of Revelation.

Examples

John the Evangelist disciple
In Christian art, John the Evangelist is often depicted with an eagle.
Many churches are dedicated to John the Evangelist in honor of his contributions to Christianity.
Scholars debate whether John the Evangelist wrote all the texts attributed to him.
