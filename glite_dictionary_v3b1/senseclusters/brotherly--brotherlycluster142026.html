brotherly

adjective, adverb
like a brother; affectionate
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌð.ɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈbrʌð.ə.li / (BrE)
showing or acting with affection, loyalty, or kindness like a brother

Examples

Brotherly love
He gave his younger sister a brotherly hug before leaving for college.
She appreciated his brotherly concern whenever she was feeling down.
Their brotherly bond remained strong even after years apart.
He welcomed his friend brotherly and with open arms.
She treated the orphan brotherly, offering kindness and support.
The king spoke brotherly to his subjects, winning their affection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraternal - relating to brothers; characteristic of brothers
