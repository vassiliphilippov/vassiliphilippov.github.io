Thomistic

adjective
of Thomas Aquinas
9.9
Pronunciation: / θoʊˈmɪstɪk / (AmE), / θəˈmɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the philosophy or teachings of Thomas Aquinas, especially his theological and philosophical ideas

Examples

Thomistic philosophy
Her research focused on Thomistic arguments for the existence of God.
The professor explained how Thomistic thought influenced later Christian philosophy.
Many universities offer courses in Thomistic theology and ethics.
