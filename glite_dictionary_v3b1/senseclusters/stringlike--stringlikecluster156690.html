stringlike

adjective
resembling a string
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstrɪŋˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a string, especially in shape or appearance

Examples

Stringlike roots
The plant's stringlike roots spread out in all directions beneath the soil.
She noticed a stringlike pattern running through the fabric of the old curtain.
Some types of cheese develop a stringlike texture when melted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ropey - resembling rope, especially in being long and thin
  • synonym: threadlike - resembling a thread in form or appearance
