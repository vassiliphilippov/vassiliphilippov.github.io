soluble

adjective
able to be solved or explained
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːljəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɒljʊbəl / (BrE)
(of a problem or mystery) capable of being solved or explained.

Examples

Soluble problem
Many scientific mysteries are difficult but ultimately soluble.
She found the riddle surprisingly soluble once she had all the facts.
The committee believes the issue is soluble with more discussion and planning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insoluble - impossible to solve or explain
