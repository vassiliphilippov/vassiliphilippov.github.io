Synonyms

  • devote
    3.7 20/m
    devote means giving long, strong dedication to an activity, while put into means putting time, effort, or emotion into it.
  • commit
    3.4 30/m
    commit means deciding to give yourself or your resources to something long-term, while put into is a more neutral phrase for investing effort.
  • invest
    3.5 30/m
    invest means putting time, money, or effort in hope of gain or benefit, while put into just means spending resources.
  • expend
    5.0 2/m
    expend means using or spending resources like time or energy in one act, while put into suggests broader ongoing effort.
