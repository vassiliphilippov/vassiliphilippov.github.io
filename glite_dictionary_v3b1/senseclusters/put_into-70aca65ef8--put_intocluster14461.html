put into

verb
invest effort, time, or emotion
Pronunciation: / pʊt ˈɪntu / (AmE), / pʊt ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to invest effort, time, emotion, or another resource into an activity or project

Examples

To put into effort

You can always tell when someone has put into their performance.
