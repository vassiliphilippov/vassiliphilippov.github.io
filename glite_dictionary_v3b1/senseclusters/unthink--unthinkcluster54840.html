unthink

verb
to erase a thought
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɪŋk / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋk / (BrE)
to deliberately try to forget or remove a thought or idea from your mind

Examples

To unthink a memory
After hearing the bad news, he wished he could unthink what he had just learned.
It is difficult to unthink an idea once it has taken root in your mind.
She tried to unthink the embarrassing moment from yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forget - to fail to remember something
  • antonym: remember - to keep something in your memory
