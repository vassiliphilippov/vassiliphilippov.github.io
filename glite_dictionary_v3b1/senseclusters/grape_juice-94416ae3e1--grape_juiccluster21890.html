grape juice

noun
juice made from grapes
2.3
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪp d͡ʒus /; / ˈɡreɪp ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ɡreɪp d͡ʒuːs /; / ˈɡreɪp ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
the sweet, non-alcoholic juice obtained from grapes, usually consumed as a drink or used as an ingredient
