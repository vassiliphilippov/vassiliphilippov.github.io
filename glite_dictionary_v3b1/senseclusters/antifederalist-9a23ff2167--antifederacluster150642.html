Antifederalist

noun
opponent of US Constitution
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈfɛdərəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈfɛdərəlɪst / (BrE)
a person in late 18th-century America who opposed a strong central government and the ratification of the US Constitution
