off one's own bat

adverb
independently; on one's own initiative
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɑf wʌnz oʊn bæt / (AmE), / ɒf wʌnz əʊn bæt / (BrE)
acting independently or without being prompted or assisted by others

Examples

Off his own bat
He volunteered for the committee off his own bat, without being asked.
No one told her what to do; she finished the task off her own bat.
They organized the fundraiser entirely off their own bat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: off one's own initiative - acting based on one's own decision
  • synonym: on one's own - by oneself; without others' help
