bridgeway

noun
figurative connector
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪdʒˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdʒˌweɪ / (BrE)
a means or method of connecting people, groups, ideas, or systems, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Bridgeway for ideas
The new program served as a bridgeway between local businesses and young entrepreneurs.
Education can be a powerful bridgeway to greater understanding among different cultures.
The festival acted as a bridgeway connecting artists from around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrier - something that blocks or separates
  • synonym: link - something that connects or joins things together
