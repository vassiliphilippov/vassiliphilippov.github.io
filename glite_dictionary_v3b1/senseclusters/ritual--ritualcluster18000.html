ritual

noun
customary practice or ceremony
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃu.əl /; / ˈrɪtjʊəl / (BrE)
a set of fixed actions or ceremonies, especially as part of a religious or traditional practice

Examples

Religious ritual
Every culture has its own unique funeral rituals.
He followed the same bedtime ritual every night to help him sleep.
The morning coffee ritual helped her start the day on the right foot.
Attendees were required to follow the traditional ritual before entering the temple.
Every morning, the monks performed an ancient ritual at sunrise.
The wedding ritual included exchanging vows and lighting a candle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremony - a formal event performed on important occasions
  • degree lower: custom - a traditional and accepted way of behaving or doing something
  • degree lower: habit - a regular practice or routine, often one that is hard to give up
