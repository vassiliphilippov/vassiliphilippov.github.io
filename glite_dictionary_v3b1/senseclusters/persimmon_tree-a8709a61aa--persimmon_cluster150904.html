persimmon tree

noun
tree producing edible persimmons
7.4
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪm.ən ˌtriː / (AmE), / pəˈsɪm.ən ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tropical or subtropical tree of the genus Diospyros, cultivated for its edible orange fruit known as persimmon.

Examples

A persimmon tree fruit
A mature persimmon tree can live and bear fruit for many decades.
Farmers planted several persimmon trees along the hillside to diversify their crops.
The persimmon tree in our backyard produces sweet fruit each autumn.
