chest

noun
front part of the torso
Pronunciation: / tʃɛst / (AmE), / tʃɛst / (BrE)
the front part of the human or animal body between the neck and the abdomen, containing the heart and lungs

Examples

Pain in the chest
He suffered a pain in his chest after running up the stairs.
She put her hand on her chest to feel her heartbeat.
The doctor listened to his chest with a stethoscope.
He felt pain in his chest after running up the stairs.
She crossed her arms over her chest when she felt cold.

  • degree higher: neck - body part above the chest
