popup

noun
3D paper structure
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpɒpˌʌp / (BrE)
a three-dimensional paper or card structure that folds flat and springs up when opened, especially in books or cards

Examples

Popup book
The children were delighted by the popup in the center of the storybook.
She designed a birthday card with a flower popup that surprised everyone.
Each page of the art book featured a different popup scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greeting card - a decorative card sent to express good wishes, sometimes with 3D elements
  • synonym: pop-up book - a book containing pages with 3D paper structures that appear when opened
