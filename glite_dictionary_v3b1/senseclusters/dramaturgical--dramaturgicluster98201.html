dramaturgical

adjective
relating to drama or theatre techniques
Pronunciation: / ˌdræməˈtɝːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdræməˈtɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the technical or artistic aspects of drama and theatrical production

Examples

Dramaturgical techniques
A sound understanding of dramaturgical techniques is essential for creating effective stage performances.
Her dramaturgical skills helped to clarify the motivations of each character in the production.
The director's dramaturgical choices gave the play a modern and edgy feel.
