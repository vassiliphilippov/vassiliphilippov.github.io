reinitialize

verb
to reset to original state
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪˈnɪʃəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˈnɪʃəlaɪz / (BrE)
to set something, especially a computer program or device, back to its original or default state
