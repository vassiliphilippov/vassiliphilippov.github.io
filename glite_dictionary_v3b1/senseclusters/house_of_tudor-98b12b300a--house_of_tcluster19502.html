House of Tudor

proper noun
English royal dynasty (1485–1603)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊs əv ˈtuːdər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ˈtjuːdə / (BrE)
the English royal dynasty that ruled from 1485 to 1603, beginning with Henry VII and ending with Elizabeth I

Examples

The House of Tudor dynasty
Elizabeth I was the last monarch from the House of Tudor.
The House of Tudor played a crucial role in shaping the history of England.
The reign of the House of Tudor marked the end of the medieval period in England.
