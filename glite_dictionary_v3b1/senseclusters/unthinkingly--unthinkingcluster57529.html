unthinkingly

adverb
without thinking or consideration
5.5 4.7
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈθɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ˈθɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ən.ˈθɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ˈθɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
