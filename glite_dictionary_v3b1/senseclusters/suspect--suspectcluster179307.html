suspect

noun
person under suspicion
1.0
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛkt / (AmE), / səˈspɛkt / (BrE)
a person thought to be guilty of a crime or offense

Examples

A murder suspect
After reviewing the evidence, the detective identified a new suspect.
Each suspect was asked to provide an alibi for the night of the incident.
The police questioned the suspect for several hours about the robbery.
