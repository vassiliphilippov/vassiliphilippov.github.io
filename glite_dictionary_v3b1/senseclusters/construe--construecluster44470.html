construe

verb
interpret or assign meaning to something
8.1 6.25/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàstru / (AmE), / k…ônÀàstruÀê / (BrE)
to interpret or explain the meaning of words, actions, or events; to assign a meaning to something

Examples

To construe a sentence
He tends to construe every comment as a personal attack.
Her words could easily be construed as an insult if taken out of context.
The judge will construe the contract in accordance with the law.

Synonyms

  • interpret
    3.3 37/m
    interpret explains meaning clearly for others, while construe is a more formal act of assigning that meaning.
  • see
    1.0 654/m
    see means to understand or notice something quickly, while construe means to interpret words or actions carefully.

  • synonym: interpret - to explain the meaning of something
