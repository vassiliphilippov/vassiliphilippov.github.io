antilock

verb
to prevent wheel locking
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.ti.lɑːk/ /; / /ˈæn.taɪ.lɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tɪ.lɒk/ /; / /ˈæn.taɪ.lɒk/ (BrE)
to prevent a wheel or brakes from locking, typically by activating an antilock system during braking.

Examples

To antilock the brakes
Modern vehicles automatically antilock the brakes to help drivers maintain control on slippery roads.
If you brake suddenly on ice, the system will antilock to prevent skidding.
The car's advanced safety features can antilock the wheels during emergency stops.
