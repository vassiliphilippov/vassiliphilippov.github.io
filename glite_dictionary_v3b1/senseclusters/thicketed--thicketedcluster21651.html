thicketed

adjective
covered with bushes
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪkɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈθɪkɪtɪd / (BrE)
covered with or full of dense bushes or small trees

Examples

Thicketed hillside
The thicketed hillside made it hard to see the river below.
We followed a narrow trail through the thicketed forest.
Birds nested in the thicketed area behind the old farmhouse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - not covered with plants or trees
  • synonym: overgrown - covered with plants that have grown wild and thick
