neo-Marxism

noun
updated form of Marxist theory
9.8 9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊ ˈmɑrkˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊ ˈmɑːkˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
A social, political, and economic theory that builds upon and modifies traditional Marxist thought by incorporating elements from other intellectual traditions.

Examples

Neo-Marxism theory
Many contemporary social movements are influenced by neo-Marxism in their analysis of class and power.
Some critics argue that neo-Marxism diverges significantly from classical Marxist principles.
The professor taught a course on neo-Marxism and its impact on modern sociology.
