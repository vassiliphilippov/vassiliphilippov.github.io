excrescent

adjective
growing abnormally as an outgrowth
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskrɛsənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈskrɛsənt / (BrE)
(of a part or feature) growing abnormally or excessively as an outgrowth, especially one that is superfluous or abnormal.

Examples

An excrescent growth
In some languages, an excrescent sound appears at the end of certain words.
The botanist pointed out an excrescent growth on the tree branch.
The doctor was concerned about the excrescent tissue developing on the patient's skin.
