tallish

adjective
somewhat tall
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat tall; fairly tall but not very tall

Examples

A tallish man
He was a tallish man, easy to spot in a crowd.
Her son is tallish for his age, often mistaken for someone older.
The plant grew to a tallish height, towering above the others in the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: short - of less than average height
  • degree higher: tall - of greater than average height
