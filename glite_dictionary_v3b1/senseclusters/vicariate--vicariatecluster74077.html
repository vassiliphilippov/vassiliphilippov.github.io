vicariate

noun
district under authority of a vicar
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɪˈkɛriət / (AmE), / vɪˈkɛəriət / (BrE)
A district or office in a Christian church under the authority and jurisdiction of a vicar.

Examples

The vicariate office
After his appointment, the new vicar oversaw reforms throughout the vicariate.
Several churches in the vicariate participated in the community outreach program.
The local parish falls within the vicariate established by the bishop.
