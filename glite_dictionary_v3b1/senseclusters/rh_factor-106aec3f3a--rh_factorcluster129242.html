Rh factor

noun
a protein determining blood type compatibility
5.5 3.6
Pronunciation: /ɑːr eɪtʃ ˈfæk.tər/ /; / /ˈriː.səs ˈfæk.tər/ (AmE), /ɑː eɪtʃ ˈfæk.tə/ /; / /ˈriː.səs ˈfæk.tə/ (BrE)
a protein present on the surface of red blood cells in most people, used to determine blood type compatibility

Examples
