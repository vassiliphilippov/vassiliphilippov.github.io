cultural model

noun
framework of shared societal beliefs and behaviors
1.0 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl ˈmɑː.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl ˈmɒd.əl/ (BrE)
a mental framework of shared ideas, attitudes, and behaviors common to a particular society or group

Examples

A cultural model of society
Anthropologists often analyze a society's cultural model to understand its collective beliefs.
Educators must consider the prevailing cultural model when designing inclusive curricula.
The cultural model of health varies widely between Eastern and Western societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: social norm - an accepted standard of behavior within a group or society
