Acrocomia aculeata

noun
tropical palm tree with spiny trunk and edible nuts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæk.roʊˈkoʊ.mi.ə ˌæk.jʊˈleɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˌæ.krəʊˈkəʊ.mi.ə ˌæk.jʊˈleɪ.tə / (BrE)
