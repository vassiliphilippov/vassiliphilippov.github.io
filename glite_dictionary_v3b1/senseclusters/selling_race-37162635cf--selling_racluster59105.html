selling race

noun
horse race where winner is auctioned
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlɪŋ reɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪŋ reɪs / (BrE)
a horse race in which the winner must be offered for sale by auction immediately after the race

Examples

A selling race event
After winning the selling race, the horse was immediately put up for auction.
Many owners prefer entering their horses in a selling race to reduce ownership costs.
The trainer decided to enter his least promising horse in a selling race at the local track.

Related Terms

  • synonym: claiming race - a horse race in which every horse entered is for sale at a specified price
