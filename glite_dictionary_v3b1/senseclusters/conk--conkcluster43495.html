conk

verb
hit or strike on the head
Pronunciation: / kɑŋk / (AmE), / kɒŋk / (BrE)
to hit or strike someone, especially on the head, usually with some force

Examples

To conk your head
He accidentally conked himself with the heavy toolbox.
If you conk your brother like that again, you'll be in trouble.
The thief tried to conk the guard on the head before escaping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bash - to strike heavily
  • synonym: smack - to hit with a sharp slap or blow
