nondrug

adjective
without drugs
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
not involving or relating to drugs, especially in medical treatment or therapy

Examples

Nondrug therapy
The hospital encourages nondrug therapies such as physical exercise and counseling for chronic pain.
Some patients prefer nondrug treatments to avoid possible side effects of medication.
Meditation and acupuncture are popular nondrug approaches for stress relief.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug-free - not using or involving drugs
  • antonym: pharmacological - relating to or involving drugs or medication
