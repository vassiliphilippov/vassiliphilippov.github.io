puxy

adjective
soft and spongy
soft and spongy in texture, especially describing decaying wood that easily crumbles

Examples

Puxy wood
The fallen branch was so puxy that it broke apart in my hands.
After years in the damp forest, the fence posts became puxy and unstable.
We avoided stepping on the puxy logs because they would collapse under our weight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firm - solid and not easily pressed or crumbled
  • synonym: rotten - decayed and decomposed, especially wood or food
