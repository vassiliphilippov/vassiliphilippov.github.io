elitist policy

noun
policy favoring a privileged group
3.7 5.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈliːtɪst ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / ɪˈliːtɪst ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
a rule or set of rules that gives preferential treatment to a select, privileged group rather than the general population

Examples

An elitist policy favors few
Activists are demanding the school board reconsider its elitist policy on student scholarships.
Many believe that an elitist policy entrenches social divisions within the organization.
The new admissions guidelines have been criticized as an elitist policy that excludes many qualified applicants.
