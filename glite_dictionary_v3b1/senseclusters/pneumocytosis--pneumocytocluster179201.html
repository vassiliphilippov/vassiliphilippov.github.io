pneumocytosis

noun
pneumonia caused by Pneumocystis in immune-compromised patients
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːmoʊsaɪˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməʊsaɪˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A type of pneumonia, especially affecting infants or people with weakened immune systems, caused by infection with Pneumocystis fungus.

Examples

pneumocytosis infection
Infants with HIV are at higher risk for developing pneumocytosis if not treated early.
Researchers are working on better ways to prevent pneumocytosis in organ transplant patients.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with pneumocytosis due to their compromised immune system.

  • synonym: pneumonia - lung infection causing inflammation and fluid in the alveoli
