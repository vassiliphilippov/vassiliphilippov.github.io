pneumocytosis

noun
pneumonia caused by Pneumocystis in immune-compromised patients
5.8 9.8
Pronunciation: /nuː.moʊ.saɪ.ˈtoʊ.sɪs/ /; / /njuː.moʊ.saɪ.ˈtoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /njuː.mə.saɪ.ˈtəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /nuː.mə.saɪ.ˈtəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
A type of pneumonia, especially affecting infants or people with weakened immune systems, caused by infection with Pneumocystis fungus.
