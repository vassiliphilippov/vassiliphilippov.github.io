fartsy

adjective
pretentiously artistic
9.9 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…ëÀêrtsi / (AmE), / Ààf…ëÀêtsi / (BrE)
pretentiously artistic or affected in a showy or exaggerated way

Examples

A fartsy film
The film was too fartsy for most audiences, with its strange camera angles and abstract dialogue.
He decorated his apartment in a fartsy style, full of odd sculptures and bold colors.
Some critics dismissed the exhibition as just another fartsy attempt to shock viewers.

Synonyms

  • arty-farty
    8.9 0.01/m
    arty-farty is loudly pretentious about art and style, while fartsy means being affected in an exaggerated, showy artistic way.
  • artsy
    7.5 0.11/m
    artsy can mean casually creative or trying to look artistic, while fartsy stresses a more exaggerated, pretentious and showy art style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arty - showing a pretentious interest in art or artistic style
  • synonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or artistic than is actually the case
  • antonym: unaffected - not trying to impress others; natural and genuine
