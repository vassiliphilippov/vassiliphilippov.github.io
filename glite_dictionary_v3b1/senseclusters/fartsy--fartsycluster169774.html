fartsy

adjective
pretentiously artistic
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːrtsi / (AmE), / ˈfɑːtsi / (BrE)
pretentiously artistic or affected in a showy or exaggerated way

Examples

A fartsy film
The film was too fartsy for most audiences, with its strange camera angles and abstract dialogue.
He decorated his apartment in a fartsy style, full of odd sculptures and bold colors.
Some critics dismissed the exhibition as just another fartsy attempt to shock viewers.
