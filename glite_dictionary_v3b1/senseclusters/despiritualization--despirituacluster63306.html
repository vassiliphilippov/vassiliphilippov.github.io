despiritualization

noun
loss of spirituality
Pronunciation: / diˌspɪrɪtʃuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌspɪrɪtjʊəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of removing or losing spiritual qualities or significance

Examples

The despiritualization of rituals
The despiritualization of traditional rituals has changed how people celebrate important events.
Many worry that rapid technological progress leads to the despiritualization of daily life.
Some authors discuss the despiritualization of art in a materialistic society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secularization - the process of making something less religious or spiritual
  • antonym: spiritualization - the process of making something more spiritual
