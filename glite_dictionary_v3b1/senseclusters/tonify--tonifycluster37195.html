tonify

verb
to strengthen health
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊnɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊnɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to make stronger or more toned, especially in relation to health or the body

Examples

To tonify the body
Many traditional medicines claim to tonify the body's natural defenses.
Regular exercise can help tonify your muscles and improve overall health.
The herbal tea is said to tonify the digestive system when consumed daily.
