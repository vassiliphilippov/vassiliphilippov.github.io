enthralling

adjective
captivating; holding attention
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈθrɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈθrɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
captivating or holding one's attention completely; fascinating

Examples

An enthralling story
She found the novel so enthralling that she stayed up all night to finish it.
The documentary offered an enthralling look at life in the Amazon rainforest.
The magician's performance was enthralling and kept the audience mesmerized until the very end.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boring - not interesting; tedious
  • synonym: captivating - attracting and holding interest; charming
  • synonym: fascinating - extremely interesting or charming
