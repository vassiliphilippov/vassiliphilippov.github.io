lexicology

noun
study of words in a language
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛksɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌlɛksɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of linguistics that studies the structure, development, and use of words in a language

Examples

Lexicology study
He specializes in lexicology and has written several papers on the evolution of English words.
The professor introduced us to basic concepts in lexicology during our first linguistics lecture.
Understanding lexicology is essential for anyone studying comparative linguistics.
