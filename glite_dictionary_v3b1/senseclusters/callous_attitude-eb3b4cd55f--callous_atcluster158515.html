callous attitude

noun
insensitive or unfeeling manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləs ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈkæləs ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an insensitive or emotionally unresponsive manner or approach toward others

Examples

Callous attitude toward victims
Critics condemned the politician's callous attitude toward the victims of the disaster.
Her callous attitude made it difficult for others to trust her in difficult situations.
The supervisor showed a callous attitude by ignoring the employees' concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: callous - showing insensitive or cruel disregard for others
  • antonym: compassionate attitude - a sympathetic and caring manner or approach
