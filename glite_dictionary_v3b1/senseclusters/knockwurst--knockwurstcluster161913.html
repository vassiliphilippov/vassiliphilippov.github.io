knockwurst

noun
short, thick, seasoned German sausage
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑkˌwɜrst / (AmE), / ˈnɒkˌwɜːst / (BrE)
a short, thick, highly seasoned sausage of German origin, typically made from pork and beef

Examples

Grilled knockwurst sausage
At Oktoberfest, guests enjoyed grilled knockwurst alongside potato salad and pretzels.
He ordered a knockwurst with sauerkraut and mustard for lunch.
The deli was famous for its homemade knockwurst and rye bread sandwiches.
