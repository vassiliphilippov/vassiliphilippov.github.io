fatherly

adjective
like or characteristic of a father
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːðərli / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðəli / (BrE)
kind and protective in a manner typical of a father; resembling or befitting a father.

Examples

Fatherly advice
He offered some fatherly advice as I prepared for my first job interview.
Her uncle gave her a fatherly hug when she was feeling down.
The teacher watched over his students with a fatherly sense of responsibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maternal - having the qualities of a mother; motherly
  • antonym: motherly - having the qualities of a mother; kind and caring in a way typical of a mother
  • synonym: paternal - relating to a father
