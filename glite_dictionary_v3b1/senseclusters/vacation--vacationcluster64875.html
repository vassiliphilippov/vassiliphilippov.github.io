vacation

noun
the act of legally voiding something
Pronunciation: / vəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act of making something legally void, especially in a legal context

Examples

Vacation of judgment
His lawyer filed a motion for the vacation of the conviction.
The vacation of the contract restored the parties to their original positions.
The court ordered the vacation of the previous judgment due to new evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annulment - the act of declaring something invalid, especially a marriage or legal contract
  • synonym: vacate - to cancel or annul a legal decision or order
