distributor

noun
person or organization that allots or distributes
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪb.ju.tɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪb.jʊ.tə / (BrE)
a person or organization that allocates, distributes, or supplies something to others

Examples

Distributor of funds
As the distributor of the estate, she ensured that everyone received their fair share.
Each distributor was responsible for delivering resources to different departments.
The company hired a new distributor to manage the allocation of goods across the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collector - a person who gathers or collects items
  • synonym: supplier - a person or organization that provides something needed
