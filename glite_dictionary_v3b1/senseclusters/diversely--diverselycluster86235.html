diversely

adverb
in diverse ways; differently
3.2
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɜrsli / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
in various or different ways; in a manner showing diversity

Examples

Think diversely
People from around the world think diversely about important issues.
Plants in the rainforest grow diversely, making the ecosystem very rich.
The committee is composed of members who are diversely skilled.
