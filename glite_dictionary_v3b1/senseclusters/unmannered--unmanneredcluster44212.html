unmannered

adjective
lacking good manners
3.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænəd / (BrE)
(of a person or behavior) lacking good manners; socially incorrect or rude.

Examples

Unmannered behavior
His unmannered remarks offended many people at the dinner table.
She found his unmannered tone quite disrespectful.
The guests were shocked by the unmannered behavior of their host.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ill-mannered - rude or impolite; having bad manners
  • antonym: polite - showing good manners and respect for others
