curiously

adverb
in a curious or inquisitive manner
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊriəsli / (AmE), / ˈkjʊəriəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows curiosity or interest in learning or knowing something.

Examples

Looked curiously at him
She asked curiously about the new student in her class.
The kitten sniffed the strange box curiously before climbing inside.
Tourists wandered curiously through the old castle, eager to discover its secrets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casually - in a manner lacking interest or curiosity
  • synonym: inquisitively - in a manner showing an eagerness to know or learn something
