In a way that shows curiosity or interest in learning or knowing something.

Examples

Looked curiously at him
She asked curiously about the new student in her class.
The kitten sniffed the strange box curiously before climbing inside.
Tourists wandered curiously through the old castle, eager to discover its secrets.

Synonyms

  • inquisitively
    4.5 0.15/m
    inquisitively is used when someone looks or asks closely about things, while curiously shows a general eager interest.
  • inquiringly
    4.7 0.25/m
    inquiringly sounds polite and often comes with a question, while curiously simply shows general interest in learning.
  • interestedly
    3.4 0.05/m
    interestedly shows quiet attention or involvement, while curiously suggests active wanting to know or explore more closely.
  • questioningly
    3.3 0.65/m
    questioningly often shows doubt or a desire to ask for proof, while curiously shows interest in learning.
