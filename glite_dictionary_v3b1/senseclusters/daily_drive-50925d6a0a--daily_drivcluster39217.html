daily drive

noun
regular car commute to work
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪli ˌdraɪv / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ˌdraɪv / (BrE)
a person's regular journey by car to and from work, usually occurring each day

Examples

My daily drive
My daily drive usually starts at 7 a.m. to avoid the morning traffic.
Podcasts help me relax during my daily drive.
The weather can really affect how pleasant my daily drive is.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commute - the regular journey to and from work
