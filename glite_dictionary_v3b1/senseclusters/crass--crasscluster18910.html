crass

adjective
lacking sensitivity or refinement
8.1
Pronunciation: / kræs / (AmE), / krɑːs / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) showing a lack of sensitivity, refinement, or intelligence.

Examples

A crass comment
His crass comments offended everyone at the table.
It would be crass to ask how much money he makes.
The comedian's jokes were considered too crass for the family audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insensitive - showing a lack of concern for others' feelings
  • antonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance, manner, or taste
