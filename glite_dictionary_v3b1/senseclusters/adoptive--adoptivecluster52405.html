adoptive

adjective
acquired by choice, not birth
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑptɪv / (AmE), / əˈdɒptɪv / (BrE)
Acquired or chosen as one's own, not by birth but by free choice or legal means.

Examples

Adoptive country
After moving to France, Paris became her adoptive city.
He felt a deep loyalty to his adoptive country.
She considers her adoptive family as close as any biological relatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adopted - legally taken as one's own (child, homeland, etc.)
