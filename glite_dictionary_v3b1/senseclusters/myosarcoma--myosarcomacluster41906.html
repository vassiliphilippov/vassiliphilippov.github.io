myosarcoma

noun
malignant tumor of muscle tissue
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.oʊ.sɑːrˈkoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.əʊ.sɑːˈkəʊ.mə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor (sarcoma) arising from muscle tissue.

Examples

A myosarcoma tumor
Because myosarcoma is rare, early diagnosis can be challenging for clinicians.
The biopsy confirmed that the mass was a myosarcoma in the patient's thigh.
Treatment for myosarcoma often involves surgery followed by chemotherapy or radiation.
