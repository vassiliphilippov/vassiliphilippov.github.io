cogeneration

noun
combined energy production
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the simultaneous production of electricity and useful heat from the same energy source

Examples

Cogeneration power plant
The new plant uses cogeneration to supply both electricity and heating to the local community.
By adopting cogeneration, the company reduced its energy waste and operating costs.
Many universities have installed cogeneration systems to improve their energy efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combined heat and power - a system that produces both electricity and useful heat together
  • degree lower: power generation - the process of producing electricity
