dividedness

noun
inner conflict
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪdɪdnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdɪdnəs / (BrE)
a state of having conflicting feelings, loyalties, or opinions within oneself or among a group

Examples

Personal dividedness
He felt a deep dividedness about whether to follow his dreams or support his family.
The team's dividedness over the new strategy led to several heated discussions.
Her dividedness was clear as she struggled to make a final decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conflict - a struggle between opposing feelings or ideas
  • antonym: unity - the state of being united or joined as a whole
