Synonyms

  • proselytization
    9.1 0.30/m
    proselytization is the act of trying to persuade someone to change their religion or beliefs, while proselytism is the broader practice of converting others.
  • conversionism
    9.5 0.01/m
    conversionism involves an organized policy of actively seeking to convert people, while proselytism is the general practice of trying to persuade others.
