lymphangiectasis

noun
abnormal widening of a lymph vessel
Pronunciation: / lɪmˌfændʒiˈɛktəsɪs / (AmE), / lɪmˌfændʒiˈɛktəsɪs / (BrE)
the dilatation or abnormal enlargement of a lymph vessel

Examples

Lymphangiectasis of vessels
Lymphangiectasis can lead to swelling due to impaired lymphatic drainage.
Pediatric cases of lymphangiectasis are often associated with other congenital abnormalities.
The doctor identified lymphangiectasis on the ultrasound of the patient's abdomen.
