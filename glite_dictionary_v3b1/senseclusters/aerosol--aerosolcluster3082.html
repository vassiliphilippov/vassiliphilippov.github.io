aerosol

noun
suspension of particles in a gas
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.əˌsɑl / (AmE), / ˈeə.rə.sɒl / (BrE)
A suspension of fine solid particles or liquid droplets in a gas, such as air.

Examples

Airborne aerosol particles
Airborne viruses can travel in tiny aerosols produced when a person coughs or sneezes.
Household sprays release chemical aerosols that may contribute to air pollution.
The laboratory measured the concentration of dust aerosols in the atmosphere following the eruption.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mist - tiny droplets of water suspended in air
  • synonym: smoke - solid particles suspended in air due to burning
