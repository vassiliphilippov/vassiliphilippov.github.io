windiness

noun
the state of being windy
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndiˌnɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɪndɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being windy; the presence of wind, especially to a mild or moderate extent

Examples

The windiness outside
She checked the forecast for windiness before planning her outdoor picnic.
The windiness at the top of the hill surprised the hikers.
The windiness of the afternoon made it perfect for flying kites in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeziness - the quality of being breezy, pleasantly windy
  • antonym: calmness - absence of wind, stillness of the air
