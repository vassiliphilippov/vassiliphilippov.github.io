4WD

noun
vehicle with four-wheel drive system
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔr ˌˈdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː /; / fɔr ˌwiː diː / (AmE), / ˌfɔː ˌdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː /; / fɔː ˌwiː diː / (BrE)
a vehicle or system that provides power to all four wheels, used for better traction on rough or slippery surfaces
