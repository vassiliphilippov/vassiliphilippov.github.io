supersensual

adjective
beyond physical senses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈsɛnʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈsɛnʃuəl / (BrE)
existing or perceived beyond the physical senses, often relating to spiritual or mystical experience

Examples

Supersensual experience
The philosopher spoke of a supersensual reality that cannot be detected by our five senses.
Meditation helped her reach a supersensual state of awareness beyond ordinary experience.
Some artists try to express supersensual ideas through abstract forms and colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mystical - having a spiritual meaning or reality that is not apparent to the senses
  • antonym: physical - relating to the body or material things, not spiritual
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the soul or spirit rather than physical things
