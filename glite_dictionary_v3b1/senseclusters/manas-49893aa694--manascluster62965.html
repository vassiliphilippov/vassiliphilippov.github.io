Manas

proper noun
Himalayan river
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnəs / (AmE), / ˈmænəs /; / ˈmɑːnəs / (BrE)
a major river in the eastern Himalayas and the national park in Assam, India, named after it, both known for rich biodiversity

Examples

Manas River park
The Manas River is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.
Many villages depend on the Manas for irrigation and daily water needs.
During the monsoon, the Manas often floods the surrounding plains.
Many tourists travel to Manas to see endangered animals in their natural habitat.
The Manas National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in northeastern India.
Conservationists work hard to protect the unique wildlife found in Manas.
