top secret

adjective
classified at the highest secrecy level
1.3
Pronunciation: / tɑp ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / tɒp ˈsiːkrət / (BrE)
(of information) highly confidential; classified at the highest level of secrecy, especially for security reasons.

Examples

Top secret information
Discussing top secret information outside authorized areas is strictly prohibited.
She was assigned to a top secret government mission overseas.
The agency stored all top secret files in a secure vault.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classified - officially designated as restricted or secret
  • synonym: confidential - meant to be kept secret or private
  • antonym: public - not secret, open to everyone
