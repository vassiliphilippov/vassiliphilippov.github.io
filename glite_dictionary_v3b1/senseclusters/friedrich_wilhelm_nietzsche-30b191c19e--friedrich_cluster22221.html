Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche

proper noun
German philosopher and existentialist thinker (1844–1900)
Pronunciation: / ˈniːtʃə / (AmE), / ˈniːtʃə / (BrE)
(1844–1900) An influential German philosopher known for his critique of traditional moral values and Christianity, and for introducing the concept of the 'superman'; regarded as a founder of existentialism.

Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche philosopher
