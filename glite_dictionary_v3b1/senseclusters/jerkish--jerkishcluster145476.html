jerkish

adjective
rude or inconsiderate
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜrkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːkɪʃ / (BrE)
behaving in a rude, mean, or inconsiderate way, similar to a jerk

Examples

Jerkish behavior
It was really jerkish of him to ignore her apology.
Her jerkish attitude made it hard for anyone to work with her.
Don't be so jerkish when people make honest mistakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: considerate - thoughtful of others' feelings
  • synonym: mean - unkind or spiteful
  • synonym: rude - impolite or disrespectful
