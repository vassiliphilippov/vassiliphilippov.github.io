spotted flycatcher

noun
European songbird that catches insects in flight
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑt̬ɪd ˈflaɪˌkætʃər / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈflaɪˌkætʃə / (BrE)
a small European songbird (Muscicapa striata) with greyish-brown plumage, noted for catching insects in flight
