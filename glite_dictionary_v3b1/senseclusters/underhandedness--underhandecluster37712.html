underhandedness

noun
dishonest behavior
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈhændɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or practice of being secretive or dishonest to achieve one's goals

Examples

Political underhandedness
The politician's underhandedness eventually led to a public scandal.
She was disappointed by the underhandedness of her colleague during the negotiations.
Rumors of underhandedness damaged the company's reputation in the industry.
