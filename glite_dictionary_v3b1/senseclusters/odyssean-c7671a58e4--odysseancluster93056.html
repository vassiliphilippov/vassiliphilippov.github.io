Odyssean

noun
adventurous traveler
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊdɪˈsiːən / (AmE), / ˌɒdɪˈsiːən / (BrE)
a person who undertakes a long, adventurous, and challenging journey, often facing many difficulties

Examples

An Odyssean explorer
After years in the corporate world, she became an Odyssean and set out to travel across Asia.
The documentary followed an Odyssean on his quest to cross the Sahara Desert alone.
Many admired the Odyssean spirit he showed during his months-long journey by bicycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurer - a person who seeks exciting or risky experiences
  • synonym: explorer - someone who travels to new or unfamiliar places
