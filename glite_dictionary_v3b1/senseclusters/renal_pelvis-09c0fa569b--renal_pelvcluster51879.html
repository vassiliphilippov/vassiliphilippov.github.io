renal pelvis

noun
funnel-shaped part of the kidney
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈriːnəl ˈpɛlvəs / (AmE), / ˈriːnəl ˈpɛlvɪs / (BrE)
the funnel-shaped structure in the kidney that collects urine before it passes into the ureter

Examples

The renal pelvis collects urine
A blockage in the renal pelvis can lead to kidney swelling and decreased urine flow.
Inflammation of the renal pelvis, known as pyelitis, can cause severe abdominal pain.
The doctor explained that the renal pelvis plays a crucial role in collecting urine from the kidney.
