epaulet

noun
decorative shoulder pad
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpəˌlɛt /; / ˌɛpəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˈɛpɔːlɛt /; / ˈɛpəlɛt / (BrE)
an ornamental cloth pad worn on the shoulder, often as part of a military or ceremonial uniform

Examples

Silk epaulets on jacket
His collection included an antique jacket featuring elaborate silk epaulets.
The epaulet on her costume sparkled under the stage lights.
The officer's uniform was decorated with gold epaulets on each shoulder.
He adjusted his epaulette before the ceremony commenced.
Many formal military jackets feature ornate epaulettes on the shoulders.
The general's uniform was adorned with gold epaulettes that gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shoulder strap - a strap worn over the shoulder, sometimes decorative
