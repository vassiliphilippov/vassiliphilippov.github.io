plethysmography

noun
measurement of body volume
9.9
Pronunciation: / plɛθˌɪzˈmɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / plɛθˌɪzˈmɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a medical test or method that measures changes in volume within an organ or body part, often to assess blood flow, air capacity, or arousal
