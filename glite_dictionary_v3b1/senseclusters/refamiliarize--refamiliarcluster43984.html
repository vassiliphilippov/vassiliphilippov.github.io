refamiliarize

verb
to reacquaint
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːfəˈmɪljəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːfəˈmɪliəraɪz / (BrE)
to become or make someone familiar with something again after a period of not using or experiencing it

Examples

After a long vacation, she needed to refamiliarize herself with her daily routine.

After a long vacation, he had to refamiliarize himself with the office procedures.
Teachers often refamiliarize students with classroom rules at the start of a new term.
It took her some time to refamiliarize herself with the controls of the machine.
