Allium ascalonicum

noun
species of onion producing mild, clustered bulbs
Pronunciation: / əˈliːəm ˌæskəˈlɒnɪkəm / (AmE), / əˈliːəm ˌaskəˈlɒnɪkəm / (BrE)
A type of onion plant that produces small, clustered, mild-flavored bulbs, commonly used as a seasoning in cooking; also known as the shallot.
