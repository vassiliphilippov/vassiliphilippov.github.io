A sharp, triangular bony projection, especially found on the mandible or the ulna in vertebrates.

Examples

The processus coronoideus bone
In some mammals, the processus coronoideus is larger to allow stronger jaw closure.

The surgeon carefully avoided the processus coronoideus during the elbow operation.
