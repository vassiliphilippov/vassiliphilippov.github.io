sauerkraut

noun
fermented shredded cabbage
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊ.ərˌkraʊt / (AmE), / ˈsaʊ.əˌkraʊt / (BrE)
Shredded cabbage that has been fermented in brine, typically used as a condiment or side dish.

Examples

A bowl of sauerkraut
After fermenting for several weeks, the sauerkraut developed a tangy flavor.
He topped his hot dog with plenty of sauerkraut and mustard.
Traditional German meals often include sauerkraut as a side dish.
