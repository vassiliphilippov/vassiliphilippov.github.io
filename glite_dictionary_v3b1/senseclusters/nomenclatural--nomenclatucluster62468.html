nomenclatural

adjective
naming rules
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊmənˈkleɪtʃərəl / (AmE), / ˌnəʊmɛnˈklætʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to the system or rules of naming things, especially in scientific fields

Examples

Strict nomenclatural rules
The scientist followed strict nomenclatural guidelines when naming the new species.
A nomenclatural error in the report led to confusion among researchers.
International committees often resolve nomenclatural disputes in biology and chemistry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: descriptive - serving to describe or classify
  • synonym: taxonomic - relating to the classification of things, especially organisms
