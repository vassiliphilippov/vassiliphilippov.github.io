motorbike

noun
a small motorcycle
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.tər.baɪk/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.tə.baɪk/ (BrE)
a two-wheeled vehicle powered by a motor, usually for one or two people

Examples

A motorbike ride
He repaired his old motorbike in the garage over the weekend.
She learned to ride a motorbike on quiet country roads when she was sixteen.
The gang zipped through the city on their noisy motorbikes.
He bought a new motorbike to commute to work more quickly.
Wearing a helmet is essential when riding a motorbike on busy roads.
They traveled across the country on a powerful motorbike last summer.
