tipper truck

noun
truck with tilting platform for unloading
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpər trʌk / (AmE), / ˈtɪpə trʌk / (BrE)
A truck with a platform that can be tilted to unload its contents by gravity, typically used for transporting loose materials like sand, gravel, or demolition waste.

Examples

A tipper truck platform
After unloading the soil with a tipper truck, the driver lowered the platform and drove away.
Several tipper trucks lined up at the quarry, waiting to be filled with sand.
The construction crew used a tipper truck to deliver gravel to the building site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dump truck - American term for a truck with a tilting platform used to unload materials
