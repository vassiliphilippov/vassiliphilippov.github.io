vizard

noun
mask or disguise
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈvɪzəd / (BrE)
an outward appearance or mask that conceals a person's true feelings or character

Examples

A polite vizard
He wore a polite smile as a vizard to hide his disappointment.
Behind her cheerful vizard, she was struggling with sadness.
The politician's charm was merely a vizard concealing his real ambitions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guise - an external form or appearance concealing the true nature of something
  • synonym: mask - a covering for the face used to hide identity or expression
  • antonym: reveal - to make known or show something that was hidden
