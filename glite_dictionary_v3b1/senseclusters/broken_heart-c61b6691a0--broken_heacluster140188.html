broken heart

noun
intense sorrow from lost love
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊ.kən hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kən hɑːt / (BrE)
a feeling or state of deep emotional pain or grief, especially caused by the loss or rejection of a loved one
