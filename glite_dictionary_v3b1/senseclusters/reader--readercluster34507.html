reader

noun
person who enjoys reading
1.0 83/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêd…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêd…ô / (BrE)
a person who enjoys reading books, magazines, or other written material regularly

Examples

Avid reader
As a child, he was already a passionate reader of adventure stories.
My sister is an avid reader who finishes several novels every month.
The club welcomes every reader eager to discuss literature.
