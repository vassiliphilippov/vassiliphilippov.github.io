unconformable

adjective
not corresponding; not conforming
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈfɔrməbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfɔːməbəl / (BrE)
not corresponding or not in agreement with something else; not conforming.

Examples

Unconformable to rules
Her theory was considered unconformable to established scientific principles.
The architect's proposal was unconformable to the historic style of the neighborhood.
The new policy is unconformable with current regulations and may require revision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compatible - able to exist or be used together without conflict
  • synonym: incongruent - not in agreement or harmony; inconsistent
