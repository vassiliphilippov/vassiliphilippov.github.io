Ocypete

proper noun
mythological Harpy
Pronunciation: / oʊˈsɪpɪti / (AmE), / əʊˈsɪpɪti / (BrE)
a Harpy in Greek mythology known for her swift flight

Examples

Ocypete the Harpy

The poet described Ocypete as flying across the sea faster than the wind.
Legends say that Ocypete and her sisters would snatch food from unwary travelers.
