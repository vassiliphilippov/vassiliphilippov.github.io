Light

noun
divine guidance in Quaker belief
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
(in Quaker doctrine) a divine presence believed to enlighten and guide the soul.

Examples

The Light within
Early Friends emphasized the importance of following the Light as revealed to each person.
Many Quakers speak about the Light within as a source of personal guidance.
She sought the Light during times of spiritual uncertainty.
