unmixedly

adverb
purely; without mixture
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪkstli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪkstli / (BrE)
in a way that is pure or not combined with anything else

Examples

To be unmixedly happy
She was unmixedly delighted by the news of her promotion.
His motives were unmixedly generous, with no hidden agenda.
The team celebrated their victory unmixedly, without any regrets or doubts.
