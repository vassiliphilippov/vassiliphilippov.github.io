indeed

adverb
expression of surprise or irony
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdid / (AmE), / ɪnˈdiːd / (BrE)
used as an interjection to express surprise, skepticism, or irony

Examples

Indeed! Surprising
He said he would pay for dinner? Indeed! I’ll believe it when I see it.
You think you can beat me at chess, indeed!
‘You’ve finished all the work already?’ ‘Indeed!’ she replied with a grin.
