airpark

noun
aircraft parking area
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpɑːk / (BrE)
an area at an airport or airbase where aircraft are parked, stored, and serviced

Examples

Airport airpark
The new airpark can accommodate up to fifty small planes at once.
After landing, the pilot taxied the aircraft to the airpark for maintenance.
Security checks are performed regularly throughout the airpark to ensure safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hangar - a large building for housing aircraft
  • antonym: runway - a strip of land at an airport where aircraft take off and land
