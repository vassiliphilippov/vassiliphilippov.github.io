rehearsal

noun
practice session before a performance
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈhɜrsəl / (AmE), / rɪˈhɜːsəl / (BrE)
a session in which a play, piece of music, or other performance is practiced before being performed in front of an audience

Examples

A rehearsal before the show
Her anxiety eased after several rehearsals with the cast.
The choir met every week for rehearsal at the community center.
We have a final rehearsal tonight before the opening show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practice - repeated exercise in or performance of an activity for improvement or preparation
