vertebrobasilar

adjective
of vertebrae and basilar artery
Pronunciation: / vərˌtibrəʊˈbeɪsɪlər / (AmE), / vɜːˌtiːbrəʊˈbæsɪlə / (BrE)
relating to both the vertebrae and the basilar artery, especially in the context of the brain or blood supply

Examples

Vertebrobasilar stroke
A vertebrobasilar stroke can cause dizziness, double vision, and difficulty speaking.
Doctors examined the patient's vertebrobasilar system for signs of restricted blood flow.
Imaging revealed a blockage in the vertebrobasilar arteries supplying the brainstem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cerebrovascular - relating to the blood vessels of the brain
