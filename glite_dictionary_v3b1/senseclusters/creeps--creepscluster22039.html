creeps

noun
a feeling of fear and revulsion
Pronunciation: / krips / (AmE), / kriːps / (BrE)
a feeling of fear and revulsion, often in reaction to something unpleasant, disturbing, or unnerving

Examples

Get the creeps
Spiders really give my sister the creeps and she refuses to go near them.
That abandoned house gives me the creeps whenever I walk by.
The way he stared at me all night gave me the creeps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heebie-jeebies - a state of nervous fear or anxiety
  • synonym: shivers - a sudden feeling of fear or chill
