Mecca

noun
central place for enthusiasts
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛkə / (AmE), / ˈmɛkə / (BrE)
A place that attracts people who share a particular interest, regarded as the most significant or central location for that field or activity.

Examples

A Mecca for tourists
Comic-Con is considered the Mecca for fans of comics and pop culture.
During the 1990s, Silicon Valley became the Mecca for technology startups.
The Louvre is a Mecca for art lovers around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hub - the effective center of an activity, region, or network
