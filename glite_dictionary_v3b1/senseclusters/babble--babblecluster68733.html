babble

noun
incoherent, baby-like speech
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbæbəl / (AmE), / ˈbæbəl / (BrE)
incoherent or meaningless speech that resembles the sounds made by a baby

Examples

Baby babble
Amid the adults' laughter, the toddler's babble provided a sweet background noise.
He listened to the joyful babble coming from the playpen and smiled.
The quiet babble of the infant filled the room with gentle sounds.
