mountain fern

noun
European fern with fragrant fronds
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn fɜːn / (BrE)
a common European fern found in mountainous areas, known for its fragrant lemon- or balsam-scented fronds

Examples

A mountain fern plant
A cluster of mountain ferns grew beside the hiking trail, filling the air with a fresh citrus scent.
She collected samples of mountain fern for her botanical study of alpine plants.
The mountain fern is prized by gardeners for its attractive and fragrant foliage.
