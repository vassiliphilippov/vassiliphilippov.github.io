goalkeeper

noun
player who guards the goal in sport
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊlˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊlˌkiːpə / (BrE)
the player in sports such as soccer, hockey, or lacrosse whose main job is to stop the other team from scoring by guarding the goal

Examples

The goalkeeper saves shots
After injuring his hand, the goalkeeper had to be substituted.
Each team relies on its goalkeeper to stop shots and organize the defense.
The goalkeeper made an impressive save in the final minutes of the game.
During the penalty shootout, the team's goalkeeper was the hero of the match.
Hockey fans cheered as their goalkeeper blocked another shot.
The goalkeeper made an amazing save at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: defender - player positioned to defend against opponent attacks
  • synonym: goalie - informal short form for goalkeeper
