permutation

noun
substitution; replacement of one thing by another
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝː.mjuˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.mjuˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of one thing being substituted for another; substitution or replacement

Examples

Permutation of tissue
In medical procedures, the permutation of tissues can be a complex process.
Modern supply chains require the frequent permutation of materials to meet demand.
The permutation of staff members meant new roles for everyone involved.
