family Boidae

noun
snake family: boas and pythons
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˈboʊɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˈbəʊɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of snakes that includes boas and pythons, known for their constricting method of subduing prey.

The family Boidae is characterized by non-venomous snakes that kill their prey by constriction.
