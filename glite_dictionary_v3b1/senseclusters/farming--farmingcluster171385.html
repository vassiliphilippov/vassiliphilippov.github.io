farming

adjective
relating to agriculture
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the activity or business of growing crops and raising animals for food or other products

Examples

Farming community
Many farming communities depend on irrigation to grow their crops.
She grew up in a farming region known for its dairy production.
The government provides subsidies for farming equipment and machinery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agricultural - relating to agriculture
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town, not rural
