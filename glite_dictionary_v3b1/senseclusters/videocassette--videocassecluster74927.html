videocassette

noun
cassette for videotape recordings
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /vɪ.dɪ.oʊ.kə.ˈset/ (AmE), /vɪ.dɪ.əʊ.kə.ˈset/ (BrE)
A plastic case containing a length of videotape for recording and playing back video and sound, used in video recorders or camcorders.

Examples

An old videocassette
She found an old videocassette of her childhood birthday party in the attic.
The documentary is available on videocassette as well as DVD.
We had to rewind the videocassette before returning it to the rental store.

Related Terms

  • antonym: audiocassette - cassette for magnetic tape used to record and play sound only
  • synonym: videotape - magnetic tape used for recording video and audio signals
