piragua

noun
Puerto Rican ice dessert
9.5
Pronunciation: / piˈrɑːɡwə / (AmE), / piˈrɑːɡwə / (BrE)
a Puerto Rican shaved ice dessert topped with flavored syrup, often sold by street vendors

Examples

A sweet piragua
On a hot afternoon, children lined up to buy a piragua from the colorful cart.
The vendor carefully shaped the ice before pouring cherry syrup over the piragua.
During the festival, everyone enjoyed a cold piragua to beat the heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snow cone - a sweet treat of crushed ice with flavored syrup
