slipper plant

noun
tropical succulent plant with foot-shaped bracts
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpɚ plænt / (AmE), / ˈslɪpə plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any of several tropical American shrubby succulent plants that resemble cacti and have distinctive foot-shaped bracts.

Examples

The slipper plant flower
Gardeners often choose the slipper plant to add unique texture to desert landscapes.
The slipper plant is prized for its unusual, shoe-shaped bracts and drought resistance.
Unlike true cacti, the slipper plant produces bright yellow, slipper-shaped blooms in summer.
