question

noun
formal proposal for decision
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
a formal proposal put forward in a deliberative assembly to be discussed and voted on

Examples

Put the question to vote

Anyone wishing to oppose the question should indicate now.
The senator called for the question to be put to a vote.
