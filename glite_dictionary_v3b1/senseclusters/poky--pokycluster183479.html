poky

noun
(informal) prison or jail
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊki / (AmE), / ˈpəʊki / (BrE)
(informal) A prison or jail, especially one that is small, unpleasant, or poorly maintained.

Examples

A night in the poky
After the fight, he spent the night in the poky for disturbing the peace.
She dreaded the thought of ending up in a dirty poky like that.
The suspects were held in the local poky until their court appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • synonym: prison - an institution for the confinement of persons convicted of serious crimes
