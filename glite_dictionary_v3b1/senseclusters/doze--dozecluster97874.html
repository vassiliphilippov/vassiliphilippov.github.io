doze

noun
a brief light sleep
Pronunciation: / doʊz / (AmE), / dəʊz / (BrE)
a short period of light or fitful sleep, often taken unintentionally

Examples

A quick doze
A brief doze is sometimes all you need to feel refreshed.
After lunch, she enjoyed a quiet doze in her armchair.
He awoke from his doze to the sound of birds outside the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nap - a short sleep, especially during the day
