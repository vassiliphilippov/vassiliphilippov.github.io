blading

noun
inline skating activity
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbleɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of skating on inline skates or rollerblades for exercise or recreation

Examples

Go blading in the park
He spends his afternoons blading around the city with his friends.
Many people enjoy blading along the beach path for fun and exercise.
She bought new protective gear before going blading in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rollerblading - skating using rollerblades
  • synonym: skating - moving on skates over a surface
