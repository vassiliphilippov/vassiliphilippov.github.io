pin up

verb
fasten with a pin
Pronunciation: / pɪn ʌp / (AmE), / pɪn ʌp / (BrE)
to fasten or attach something to a surface using a pin or something similar

Examples

To pin up notes
I will pin up the notes so everyone can see them.
She decided to pin up her favorite photograph on the wall.
The teacher asked the students to pin up their artwork for the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attach - to fasten, join, or connect something
  • antonym: take down - to remove something that has been attached or put up
