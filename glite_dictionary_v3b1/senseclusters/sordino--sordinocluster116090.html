sordino

noun
mute for a violin or stringed instrument
Pronunciation: / sɔrˈdiːnoʊ / (AmE), / sɔːˈdiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a device placed on the bridge of a violin or other stringed instrument to muffle or soften the sound

Examples

A sordino on violin
Please remember to bring your sordino to the orchestra rehearsal tonight.
The violinist fitted a sordino before performing the softer section of the piece.
Using a sordino gives the instrument a much gentler and muted tone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mute - a device used to soften or muffle the sound of a musical instrument
