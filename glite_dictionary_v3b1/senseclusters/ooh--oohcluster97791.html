ooh

interjection
expressing surprise or admiration
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
used to express surprise, admiration, or excitement

Examples

Ooh of surprise
When she saw the sparkling necklace, she whispered ooh in amazement.
The children shouted ooh as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
He let out a soft ooh when he tasted the delicious dessert.
