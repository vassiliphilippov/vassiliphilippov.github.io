biyearly

adjective, adverb
twice a year
Pronunciation: / baɪˈjɪrli / (AmE), / baɪˈjɪəli / (BrE)
happening or done twice a year (every six months); semiannual (used as adjective or adverb)

Examples

Biyearly payment plan
Insurance premiums are sometimes billed on a biyearly basis to make payments easier.
The committee meets biyearly to review their progress and set new goals.
We have biyearly maintenance checks every June and December.
Our garden club organizes a biyearly plant sale every spring and fall.
School fees are due on a biyearly basis, in January and July.
The company holds biyearly meetings to review its progress and plans.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: annual - occurring once a year
  • synonym: biannual - occurring twice a year
  • degree higher: quarterly - occurring four times a year
