Oedipal

adjective
parental attachment rivalry
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈiːdɪpəl / (BrE)
relating to a strong emotional attachment or rivalry involving a parent, especially of the opposite sex, as described in psychoanalytic theory
