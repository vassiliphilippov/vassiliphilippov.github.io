Oedipal

adjective
parental attachment rivalry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈiːdɪpəl / (BrE)
relating to a strong emotional attachment or rivalry involving a parent, especially of the opposite sex, as described in psychoanalytic theory

Examples

Oedipal conflict
The psychologist discussed the patient's Oedipal conflicts during therapy.
Many literary works analyze characters with Oedipal themes and struggles.
His Oedipal feelings influenced his relationship with his parents.
