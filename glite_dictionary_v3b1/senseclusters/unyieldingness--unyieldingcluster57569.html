unyieldingness

noun
state of being firm or resolute
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjiːldɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈjiːldɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being firm, resolute, or unwilling to bend or give way

Examples

His unyieldingness in talks
His unyieldingness in negotiations made compromise difficult.
The unyieldingness of her stance impressed everyone in the room.
The unyieldingness of the material ensures the structure's stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firmness - the quality of being strong and unyielding
  • antonym: flexibility - the quality of bending easily without breaking
