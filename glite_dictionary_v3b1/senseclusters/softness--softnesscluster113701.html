softness

noun
gentle or mild quality in manner or behavior
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɔːft.nəs/ /; / /ˈsɑːft.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsɒft.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being gentle, mild, or forgiving in behavior or manner

Examples

Softness in her manner
Despite her tough appearance, softness was evident in the way she cared for her family.
She spoke to her students with remarkable softness, never raising her voice even when they made mistakes.
There was a softness in his eyes as he looked at the stray kitten.
He was criticized for his softness when dealing with those who broke the rules.
Her softness toward her employees made her a beloved manager.
Some people see softness as a weakness, but others value compassion in leaders.
