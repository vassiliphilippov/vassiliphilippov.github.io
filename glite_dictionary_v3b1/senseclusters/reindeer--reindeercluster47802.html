reindeer

noun
Arctic deer with large antlers
6.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪn.dɪr/ /; / /ˈreɪn.dər/ (AmE), /ˈreɪn.dɪə/ /; / /ˈreɪn.də/ (BrE)
A large Arctic deer of northern regions, with large antlers in both males and females; known as 'reindeer' in Eurasia and 'caribou' in North America.

Examples

A reindeer herd
A herd of reindeer grazed peacefully on the snowy tundra.
In North America, the reindeer is usually called a caribou.
Santa Claus is said to travel in a sleigh pulled by reindeer.
