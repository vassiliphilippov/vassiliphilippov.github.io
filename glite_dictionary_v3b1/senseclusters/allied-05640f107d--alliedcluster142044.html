Allied

adjective
relating to the Allies in World War II
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈælaɪd / (AmE), / ˈælaɪd / (BrE)
of or relating to the countries that fought together against the Axis powers in World War II; denoting their armed forces or actions.

Examples

Allied victory in WWII
An Allied victory in Europe marked the end of the war there.
He wrote a book on Allied military strategy during World War II.
The Allied powers launched a major offensive in 1944.
