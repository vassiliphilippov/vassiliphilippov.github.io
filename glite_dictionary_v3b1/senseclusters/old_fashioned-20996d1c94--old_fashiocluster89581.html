old fashioned

noun
whiskey-based cocktail
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈfæʃənd / (AmE), / əʊld ˈfæʃənd / (BrE)
a cocktail made with whiskey, bitters, sugar, and fruit, typically garnished with an orange slice and a cherry

Examples

An old fashioned cocktail
Bartenders often add a twist of orange peel to an old fashioned.
He ordered an old fashioned at the bar before dinner.
The old fashioned is my father's favorite cocktail.
