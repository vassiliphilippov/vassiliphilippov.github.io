misericordia

noun
choir seat support
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪzərɪˈkɔrdiə / (AmE), / ˌmɪzərɪˈkɔːdiə / (BrE)
a small wooden ledge or bracket under a folding church choir seat, used to support someone standing during long services

Examples

Church misericordia seat
The ancient church still has its original misericordia beneath each choir seat.
Carved figures decorate the underside of every misericordia in the cathedral.
During the lengthy service, the singers rested on the misericordia for support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choir stall - a seat in the choir area of a church
