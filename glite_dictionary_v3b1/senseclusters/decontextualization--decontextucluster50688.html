decontextualization

noun
removal from context
9.9
Pronunciation: /diː.kən.tek.tʃʊə.lə.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.kən.tek.tʃʊə.lə.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.kən.tek.tʃʊə.lə.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.kən.tek.tʃʊə.lə.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of removing something from its original context, so it can be considered or used independently of its original situation
