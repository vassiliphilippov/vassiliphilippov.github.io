toastiness

noun
toasted flavor or aroma
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.stɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.stɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of having a toasted, roasted, or browned flavor, aroma, or texture, especially in food or drink

Examples

Bread toastiness
The toastiness of the bread added a delicious depth to the sandwich.
I enjoy the subtle toastiness in this dark chocolate bar.
The wine's toastiness comes from being aged in oak barrels.
The toastiness of the bread made it perfect for spreading butter.
She enjoyed the subtle toastiness in the flavor of the roasted nuts.
The chef aimed for just the right toastiness on the marshmallows for dessert.

  • synonym: smokiness - the quality of having a smoky flavor or aroma
  • antonym: softness - the quality of being soft or not crisp
