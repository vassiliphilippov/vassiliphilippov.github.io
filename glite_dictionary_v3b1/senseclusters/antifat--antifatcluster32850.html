antifat

noun
person hostile to fatness
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌfæt / (BrE)
a person who is opposed to or expresses hostility toward overweight people or fatness

Examples

An antifat person
The online community worked to challenge antifat attitudes and promote acceptance of all body types.
Some antifat comments in the media sparked a debate about discrimination and respect.
Activists spoke out against antifat behavior in schools and workplaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: body-positive - supportive of acceptance of all body types
  • synonym: fatphobe - a person with an irrational fear or dislike of fat people
