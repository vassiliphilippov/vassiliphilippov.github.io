decorative

adjective
intended for beauty, not use
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkɚətɪv / (AmE), / ˈdɛk(ə)rətɪv / (BrE)
Serving to make something look more attractive rather than having a practical use.

Examples

Decorative plants
Some plants are grown primarily for their decorative value rather than as food.
The living room was filled with decorative pillows and colorful rugs.
Those decorative plates on the wall are not meant to be used for eating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ornamental - serving as or relating to decoration
  • antonym: utilitarian - designed to be useful or practical
