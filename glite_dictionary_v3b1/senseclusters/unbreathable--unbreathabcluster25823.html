unbreathable

adjective
not fit to breathe
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbriːðəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbriːðəbl̩ / (BrE)
not suitable or safe for breathing; unable to be breathed in

Examples

Unbreathable air
The smoke from the fire made the air unbreathable for everyone inside the building.
After the chemical spill, the factory was filled with unbreathable fumes.
Astronauts need special suits to survive in the unbreathable atmosphere of space.
