pignon

noun
pine nut; bicycle gear
Pronunciation: / piːnˈjoʊn / (AmE), / piːnˈjɒn / (BrE)
a pine nut used in cooking, or in cycling, a small gear on a bicycle

Examples

Toasted pignon on salad
The chef sprinkled pignon on the salad for extra flavor.
He replaced the smallest pignon on his bicycle to improve his speed.
Traditional recipes often call for roasted pignon to add a nutty taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gear - a toothed wheel in a machine or vehicle
  • synonym: pine nut - an edible seed from a pine tree
