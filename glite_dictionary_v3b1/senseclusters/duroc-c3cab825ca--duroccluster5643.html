Duroc

proper noun
pig breed
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrɑk / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərɒk / (BrE)
a breed of domestic pig recognized for its reddish color and high-quality meat

Examples

Duroc pig breed
The Duroc is prized by farmers for its rapid growth and excellent pork quality.
At the agricultural fair, the champion pig was a large Duroc with a deep red coat.
Many commercial farms choose Duroc pigs because they are hardy and easy to raise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Yorkshire - a popular breed of domestic pig known for its white color
