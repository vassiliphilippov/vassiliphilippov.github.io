astronomically

adverb
to an extremely large degree
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæstrəˈnɑmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)

Examples

Fuel costs have risen astronomically over the past year, affecting everyone's budget.
The population of the city has grown astronomically in the past two decades.
The prices in that exclusive boutique are astronomically high compared to regular stores.
