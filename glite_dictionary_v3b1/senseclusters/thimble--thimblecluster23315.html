thimble

verb
to put on a thimble
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˈθɪmbəl / (BrE)
to fit or put a thimble on a finger, or to cover or protect with a thimble

Examples

To thimble a finger
Before starting her embroidery, she thimbled her finger to avoid getting pricked.
He carefully thimbled his thumb before working with the thick fabric.
The tailor always thimbles his finger when sewing heavy materials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover - to place something over to protect
  • antonym: expose - to leave unprotected or uncovered
