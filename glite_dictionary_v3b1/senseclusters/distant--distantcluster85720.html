Synonyms

  • far
    1.0 23/m
    far describes something a great distance away in simple, common speech, while distant sounds more formal and neutral.
  • far-off
    4.2 2.77/m
    far-off describes places or times very far away or imagined, while distant is a general word for being far away.
  • faraway
    2.8 2.73/m
    faraway paints a picture of places or memories that feel very distant and dreamy, while distant is plain and neutral.
  • remote
    3.6 20/m
    remote refers to places far from people and services, often isolated, while distant simply means far away in space.
  • far-flung
    6.3 1.91/m
    far-flung describes things spread over a very wide area, while distant focuses on being far away in one place.
