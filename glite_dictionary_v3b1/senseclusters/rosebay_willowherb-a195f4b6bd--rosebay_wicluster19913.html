rosebay willowherb

noun
tall perennial with pinkish-purple flowers, also called fireweed
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzˌbeɪ ˈwɪloʊˌhɜrb / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzbeɪ ˈwɪləhɜːb / (BrE)
a tall perennial plant with narrow leaves and spikes of pinkish-purple flowers, often found in abundance in burned or cleared areas; also valued as a honey plant
