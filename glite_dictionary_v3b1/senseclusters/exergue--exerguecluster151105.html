exergue

noun
inscription area on coin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌzɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈɛɡzɜːɡ / (BrE)
a small space on a coin, medal, or monument, usually at the bottom, where an inscription, motto, or date is placed
