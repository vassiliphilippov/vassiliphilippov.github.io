home life

noun
domestic life
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊm laɪf / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm laɪf / (BrE)
the way a person lives and experiences things in their home environment

Examples

Private home life
She prefers to keep her home life private and rarely discusses it at work.
A stable home life can have a positive effect on children's development.
After moving to a new city, his home life changed significantly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: work life - the part of a person's life spent at work or related to their job
