devolutionary

adjective
relating to transfer by law
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to the transfer or passing on of property, rights, or titles, especially by inheritance or legal process

Examples

Devolutionary transfer of property
The devolutionary process determined how the estate would be divided among the heirs.
A devolutionary clause in the contract specified what would happen if the owner died.
Legal experts discussed the devolutionary aspects of property transfer in court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hereditary - passed from parent to child by inheritance
