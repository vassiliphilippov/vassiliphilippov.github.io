workaholic-zilla

noun
someone obsessively devoted to work
Pronunciation: / ˌwɝkəˈhɑlɪkˈzɪlə / (AmE), / ˌwɜːkəˈhɒlɪkˈzɪlə / (BrE)
A person who is extremely obsessed with work, to an excessive or overbearing degree.

Examples

A workaholic-zilla person
Being a workaholic-zilla might impress your boss, but it can ruin your social life.
Everyone knows Sam is a total workaholic-zilla who never leaves the office before midnight.
Jenna's friends worry that her workaholic-zilla tendencies are making her miss out on important events.
