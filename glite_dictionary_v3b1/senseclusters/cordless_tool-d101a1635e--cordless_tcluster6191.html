cordless tool

noun
tool powered by battery (not corded)
7.6 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdləs tuːl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdləs tuːl / (BrE)
A tool powered by a battery, not requiring an electrical cord for operation.

Examples

When working outdoors, having a cordless tool makes the job much more convenient.
