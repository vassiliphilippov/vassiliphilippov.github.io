dialogize

verb
to hold a dialogue
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌlɑːɡaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdaɪələɡaɪz / (BrE)
to engage in or promote open dialogue or discussion between people or groups

Examples

To dialogize with others
The teacher encouraged students to dialogize about the topic in small groups.
After the conflict, both sides agreed to dialogize in hopes of finding common ground.
Community leaders met to dialogize and resolve misunderstandings between residents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: argue - to express opposite views in a discussion
  • synonym: converse - to talk informally with someone
  • synonym: discuss - to talk about something with others
