dialogize

verb
to hold a dialogue
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdaɪ.ə.lɑː.gaɪz/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.ə.lɔː.gaɪz/ (AmE), /ˈdaɪ.ə.lɒ.gaɪz/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.ə.lə.gaɪz/ (BrE)

Examples
