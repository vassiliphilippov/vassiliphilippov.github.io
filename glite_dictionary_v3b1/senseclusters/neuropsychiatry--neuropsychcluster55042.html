neuropsychiatry

noun
medicine dealing with mental disorders from nervous system diseases
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊsaɪˈkaɪətri / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊsaɪˈkaɪətri / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with mental disorders resulting from diseases of the nervous system

Examples

Neuropsychiatry medicine branch
He is pursuing a fellowship in neuropsychiatry to better understand the connection between neurology and mental health.
Many recent advances in neuropsychiatry have improved the treatment of patients with brain injuries.
The journal publishes groundbreaking research in the field of neuropsychiatry.
