white monkey

noun
slang: derogatory term or odd person
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / waɪt ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
A derogatory term for an Asian person perceived as adopting Western customs or a slang expression for someone displaying odd or eccentric behavior.

Examples

A white monkey label
He was labelled a white monkey after moving abroad and changing his style completely.
Some classmates called her a white monkey just because she liked strange hobbies.
The gossip column referred to his public outburst as a typical white monkey moment.
