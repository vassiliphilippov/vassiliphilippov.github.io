probenecid

noun
a drug for reducing uric acid, used for gout
8.3
Pronunciation: / proʊˈbɛnəsɪd / (AmE), / prəˈbɛnəsɪd / (BrE)
a uricosuric drug used to lower uric acid levels in the blood, especially in the treatment of gout

Examples

Probenecid drug
After starting probenecid, her uric acid levels dropped significantly.
Patients on probenecid should be monitored regularly for kidney function.
The doctor prescribed probenecid to help manage his gout symptoms.
