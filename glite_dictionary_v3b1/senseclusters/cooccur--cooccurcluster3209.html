cooccur

verb
occur together; happen simultaneously
7.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ko ä…ôÀàk…ùÀê / (AmE), / k…ô ä…ôÀàk…úÀê / (BrE)
to happen at the same time or together with something else, especially by coincidence or in relation to phenomena

Examples

To cooccur together
Certain bird migrations cooccur with changes in seasonal temperature.
Economic crises often cooccur with political instability in some regions.
The symptoms may cooccur, making diagnosis more challenging for doctors.

Synonyms

  • coincide
    4.4 9.02/m
    coincide describes two things happening at the same time or matching closely, while cooccur focuses on things happening together in studies.
  • concur
    6.8 0.11/m
    concur is often used in formal writing for events happening at the same time, while cooccur is common in science for things happening together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coincide - to occur at the same time as something else
  • degree lower: occur - to happen, especially unexpectedly
