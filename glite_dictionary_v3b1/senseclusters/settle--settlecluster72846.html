Synonyms

  • resolve
    3.3 30/m
    resolve means to solve a problem or end a dispute firmly, while settle stresses making a decision or agreement to finish it.
  • reach an agreement
    3.0 2/m
    reach an agreement happens when both sides accept terms after discussion, while settle can also mean a single decision ends the dispute.
  • decide
    1.0 60/m
    decide means one person or authority makes a final choice, while settle can mean agreement or decision to end the dispute.
  • compromise
    3.7 10/m
    compromise is when each side gives up something to reach agreement, while settle covers any decision or way to end a dispute.
  • reconcile
    5.1 2/m
    reconcile restores friendly relations after a disagreement, while settle focuses on ending the dispute by decision or agreement.
  • iron out
    3.8 1/m
    iron out smooths or removes small problems by discussion, while settle often means a final decision or formal agreement to end it.
