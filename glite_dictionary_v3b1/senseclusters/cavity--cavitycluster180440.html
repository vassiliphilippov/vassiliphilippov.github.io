cavity

noun
natural hollow in the body
3.9 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈkæv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(anatomy) A natural hollow or sinus within the body, such as the mouth or chest.

Examples

Chest cavity

The chest cavity protects vital organs such as the heart and lungs.
The human skull contains several air-filled cavities known as sinuses.
