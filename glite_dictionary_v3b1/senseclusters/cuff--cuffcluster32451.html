cuff

verb
to strike with an open hand
Pronunciation: / kʌf / (AmE), / kʌf / (BrE)
to hit someone with an open hand

Examples

To cuff a friend
He playfully cuffed his brother on the shoulder as a greeting.
She cuffed him across the ear for being cheeky.
The coach cuffed the boy gently for not paying attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punch - to strike with a closed fist
  • synonym: slap - to hit with the palm or fingers of the hand
