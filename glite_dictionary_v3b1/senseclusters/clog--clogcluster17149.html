clog

noun
something that obstructs or hinders
5.6
Pronunciation: / klɑɡ / (AmE), / klɒɡ / (BrE)
an object or mass that blocks or obstructs a pipe, passage, or process

Examples

A clog in pipes
A buildup of leaves acted as a clog in the gutter, preventing water from flowing freely.
Bureaucratic procedures can become a clog that slows down important projects.
The filter must be changed regularly to avoid a clog in the system.
A massive clog in the bathroom pipe led to water overflowing onto the floor.
Pouring grease down the sink can cause a stubborn clog.
The plumber discovered a clog deep inside the kitchen drain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aid - something that helps or facilitates
  • synonym: barrier - something that prevents movement or access
  • synonym: blockage - an obstruction that makes movement or flow difficult or impossible
  • synonym: jam - a situation where something, such as a mechanism, becomes stuck
  • synonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress or makes it difficult
  • synonym: obstruction - something that blocks a passage or hinders progress
