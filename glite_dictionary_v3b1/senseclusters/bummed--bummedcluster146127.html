bummed

adjective
disappointed or upset
7.5
Pronunciation: / bʌmd / (AmE), / bʌmd / (BrE)
feeling sad, disappointed, or upset about something

Examples

To feel bummed out
He was really bummed when the concert was canceled at the last minute.
I felt bummed after failing my driving test for the second time.
They were bummed that their vacation plans had to change unexpectedly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - feeling happy and positive
  • synonym: disappointed - feeling unhappy because something was not as good as expected
  • synonym: upset - feeling unhappy or worried
