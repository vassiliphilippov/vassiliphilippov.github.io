Synonyms

  • counterargument
    5.1 0.9/m
    a counterargument is a reasoned set of reasons that opposes an idea, while a rebuttal is an immediate response to challenge it.
  • refutation
    6.8 1/m
    a refutation proves that an idea or claim is false with evidence, while a rebuttal is an argument offered to oppose it.
  • counterstatement
    7.3 0.02/m
    a counterstatement is a formal reply often used in documents or court, while a rebuttal is a direct argument to disprove a claim.
