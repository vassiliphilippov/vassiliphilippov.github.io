sulker

noun
person who sulks
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlkər / (AmE), / ˈsʌlkə / (BrE)
a person who is often silent and withdrawn because they are upset or annoyed

Examples

A sulker after argument
As a child, he was a notorious sulker who would avoid talking for hours after an argument.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the sulker during the long bus ride.
She stopped being a sulker and started expressing her feelings more openly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful person - a person who is happy and positive
  • synonym: grump - a person who is often irritable or in a bad mood
