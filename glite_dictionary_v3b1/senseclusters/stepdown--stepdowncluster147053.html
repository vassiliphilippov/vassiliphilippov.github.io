stepdown

noun
voltage-reducing device
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a device or circuit that reduces electrical voltage or power to a lower level

Examples

Stepdown transformer
The engineer installed a stepdown to safely power the low-voltage equipment.
A stepdown is necessary when connecting sensitive electronics to a high-voltage source.
They replaced the faulty stepdown to prevent damage to the circuit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: converter - a device that changes electrical energy from one form to another
  • antonym: stepup - a device or circuit that increases electrical voltage or power
  • synonym: transformer - a device that changes the voltage of an electric current
