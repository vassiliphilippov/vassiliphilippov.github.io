unhoused

noun
homeless people
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhaʊzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaʊzd / (BrE)
people who do not have a permanent home or shelter, often referred to as homeless individuals

Examples

Support the unhoused
The city council is working on new programs to support the unhoused during the winter months.
Volunteers distributed food and blankets to the unhoused living in the downtown area.
Many organizations advocate for better services for the unhoused in urban communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeless - people without a permanent home
  • antonym: housed - people who have a permanent home
