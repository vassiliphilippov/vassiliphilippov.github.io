maître

noun
head waiter
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪtrə / (AmE), / ˈmeɪtrə / (BrE)
a master or head waiter, especially in a restaurant, responsible for welcoming guests and overseeing service

Examples

The maître welcomes guests
The maître greeted us warmly and showed us to our table.
If you have a reservation, please speak to the maître at the entrance.
The maître ensured that every guest received excellent service throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maître d' - the head waiter in a restaurant
  • degree lower: waiter - a person who serves food and drinks in a restaurant
