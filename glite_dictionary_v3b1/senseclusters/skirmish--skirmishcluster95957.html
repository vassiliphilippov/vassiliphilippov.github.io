skirmish

verb
take part in a brief or minor fight
7.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskɜːr.mɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈskɜː.mɪʃ/ (BrE)
to take part in a brief or minor fight or conflict, especially as an outpost or preliminary action before a larger battle

Examples

To skirmish before battle
The teams skirmished over tactics before settling into serious negotiations.
The two armies skirmished briefly at dawn before the main battle began.
Wildlife photographers often skirmish with each other over the best locations.
