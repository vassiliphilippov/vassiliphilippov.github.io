cobb

noun
sturdy riding horse
Pronunciation: / kɑb / (AmE), / kɒb / (BrE)
a small, strong horse, typically used for riding or light work

Examples

A sturdy cobb
The farmer bought a reliable cobb to help with daily chores around the stable.
Children often learn to ride on a gentle cobb because of its calm nature.
Her favorite horse was a sturdy cobb that never tired on long country rides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pony - a small horse, usually under 14.2 hands high
