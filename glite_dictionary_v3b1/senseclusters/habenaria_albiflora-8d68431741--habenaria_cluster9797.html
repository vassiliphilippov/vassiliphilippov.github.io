Habenaria albiflora

noun
a bog orchid with white fringed flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhæbəˈnɛəriə ˌæl.bɪˈflɔrə / (AmE), / ˌhabəˈnɛːrɪə ˌal.bɪˈflɔːrə / (BrE)
A species of bog orchid native to eastern North America, characterized by a spike of pure white fringed flowers.
