plunder

noun
stolen goods; loot
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈplʌndə / (BrE)
goods or money taken illegally, especially by force during war or robbery.

Examples

War plunder
He was arrested in possession of plunder stolen from the museum.
The pirates divided the plunder among themselves after the raid.
The victors returned home, their carts filled with plunder.

  • synonym: loot - stolen goods, especially obtained by force
