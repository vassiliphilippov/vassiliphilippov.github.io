midheaven

noun
astrological career point
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪd.hev.ən/ (AmE), /ˈmɪd.hev.ən/ (BrE)
the point in the sky directly above the southern horizon, used in astrology to represent career or public life, or in astronomy as the highest position of a celestial body above the horizon

Examples

Midheaven in astrology
In her birth chart, her midheaven is in Leo, suggesting a strong public presence.
Astrologers often interpret the midheaven to understand a person's career path.
The midheaven can reveal how someone is seen by society or in their professional life.
At noon, the sun reached its midheaven and shone directly above the city.
Astronomers use the midheaven to determine when a star is at its highest point in the sky.
The planet was visible at midheaven just before it began to descend toward the western horizon.
