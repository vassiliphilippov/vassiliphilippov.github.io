sayyid

noun
Arabic honorific title
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪjɪd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪjɪd / (BrE)
a respectful form of address in Arabic meaning 'master' or 'sir', used to politely refer to a man

Examples

In some communities, people use sayyid as a sign of respect for leaders.
