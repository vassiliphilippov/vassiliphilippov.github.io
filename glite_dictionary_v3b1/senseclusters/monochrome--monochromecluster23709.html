monochrome

adjective
using one color or shades of one color
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnəˌkroʊm / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌkrəʊm / (BrE)
having or appearing to have only one color; made up of shades of a single color

Examples

Monochrome design
She wore a striking monochrome outfit from head to toe.
The monochrome photograph gave the city a nostalgic and timeless feel.
The designer chose a monochrome palette for the living room, using only shades of blue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polychrome - having many colors or being decorated in several colors
