counterirritant

noun
topical medicine to relieve deeper pain
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈɪrɪtənt / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərˈɪrɪtənt / (BrE)
a medicine applied to the skin to cause mild inflammation and relieve pain or irritation in deeper tissues or joints

Examples

A counterirritant cream
After applying the counterirritant, she felt a warming sensation that distracted her from the deep muscle ache.
Capsaicin cream is commonly used as a counterirritant for minor joint pain.
The doctor recommended a counterirritant ointment to help ease the pain in his knee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analgesic - a medicine that relieves pain
  • synonym: liniment - a liquid or lotion for rubbing on the body to relieve pain
