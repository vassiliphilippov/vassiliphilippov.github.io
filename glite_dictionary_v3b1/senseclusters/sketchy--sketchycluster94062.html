sketchy

adjective
suspicious; unsafe (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛtʃi / (AmE), / ˈskɛtʃi / (BrE)
informal: appearing suspicious, unreliable, or potentially unsafe; giving reason to feel uneasy or distrustful.

Examples

Sketchy neighborhood
That alley looks sketchy at night, so let's walk the other way.
I wouldn't trust that dealer; his offer sounds sketchy and probably too good to be true.
Since the renovation, the neighborhood has become noticeably sketchier, and people avoid walking alone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: safe - not dangerous; free from risk or harm
  • synonym: suspicious - causing a feeling that something is wrong or that someone cannot be trusted
