television

noun
an electronic device for viewing TV programs
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən / (BrE)
an electronic device for receiving television broadcasts and displaying them on a screen

Examples

A television set
She spends her evenings watching documentaries on television.
The children gathered around the television to watch their favorite cartoon.
We bought a new television for the living room last weekend.
