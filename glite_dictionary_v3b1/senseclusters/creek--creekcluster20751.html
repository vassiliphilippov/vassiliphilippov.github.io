creek

noun
small natural stream of water
7.2
Pronunciation: / krik, krik̚ / (AmE), / kriːk / (BrE)
a natural stream of water that is smaller than a river and often flows into a river

Examples

A small creek
A wooden bridge crosses the creek at the edge of the park.
During summer, the creek near our house often dries up completely.
The children asked if they could play by the creek after lunch.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: river - a large natural stream of water flowing in a channel
  • synonym: stream - a small, narrow river
