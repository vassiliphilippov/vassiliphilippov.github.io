untouchability

noun
state of immunity
9.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.tʌtʃ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /ən.tʌtʃ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /ʌn.tʌtʃ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /ən.tʌtʃ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)
the state of being immune from attack, interference, punishment, or physical contact, either literally or metaphorically

The fortress was built to give its inhabitants a feeling of untouchability from outside threats.
