nonnormality

noun
not following normal pattern
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌnɔrˈmæləti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌnɔːˈmæləti / (BrE)
the state or condition of not following a normal distribution or standard pattern, especially in statistics

Examples

Nonnormality in data
The scientist noted the nonnormality of the results and adjusted the statistical tests accordingly.
Because of the nonnormality in the data, a nonparametric method was chosen for analysis.
Detecting nonnormality early can help researchers avoid incorrect conclusions in their studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anomaly - something that deviates from what is standard or expected
  • antonym: normality - the state of following a normal distribution or standard pattern
