stock up

verb
buy or gather a large supply
Pronunciation: / stɑk ʌp / (AmE), / stɒk ʌp / (BrE)
to buy or collect a large supply of something to use in the future

Examples

To stock up supplies
Many people stocked up during the holiday sales.
She likes to stock up on supplies when there are discounts.
We should stock up on canned food before the storm arrives.

  • synonym: hoard - to collect and keep large amounts of something secretly
