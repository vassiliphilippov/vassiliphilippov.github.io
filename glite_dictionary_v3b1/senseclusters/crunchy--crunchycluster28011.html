crunchy

adjective
firm and crisp
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃi / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃi / (BrE)
firm and making a loud, crisp sound when chewed or pressed

Examples

Crunchy chips

She prefers her apples crunchy rather than soft and mushy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soft - not hard or firm; easy to press or chew
