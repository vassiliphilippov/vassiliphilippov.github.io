decor

noun
style and furnishings of an interior
1.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.kɔːr/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.kər/ /; / /ˈdɪ.kɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.kɔː/ /; / /ˈdɪ.kɔː/ (BrE)
