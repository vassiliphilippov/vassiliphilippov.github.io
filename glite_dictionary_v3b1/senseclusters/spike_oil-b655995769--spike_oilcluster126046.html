spike oil

noun
essential oil from spike lavender
Pronunciation: / spaɪk ɔɪl / (AmE), / spaɪk ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow essential oil obtained from spike lavender, used in scenting soaps and cosmetics

Examples

Spike oil scent
Art restorers sometimes clean old paintings with a solution containing spike oil.
She prefers to use spike oil for making handcrafted soaps because of its fresh aroma.
The perfume is delicately scented with spike oil, giving it a floral note.
