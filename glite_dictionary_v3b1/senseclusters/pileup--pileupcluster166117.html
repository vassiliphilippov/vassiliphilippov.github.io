a situation in which a large number of things or vehicles accumulate in one place, often causing a blockage or delay

Examples

Traffic pileup
A massive pileup on the highway caused traffic to back up for miles.
There was a pileup of paperwork on her desk after the long weekend.
Winter storms often lead to a pileup of cars on icy roads.
