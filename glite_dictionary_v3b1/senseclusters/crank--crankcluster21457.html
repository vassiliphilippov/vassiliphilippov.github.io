crank

noun
rotating handle for turning a shaft
Pronunciation: / kræŋk / (AmE), / kræŋk / (BrE)
a handle attached at right angles to a rotating shaft, used to transmit motion by turning by hand

Examples

A metal crank
He turned the crank to start the old-fashioned engine.
The window wouldn’t open until she used the crank to raise it.
You need to rotate the crank to wind up the jack.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handle - part of a tool designed to be held in the hand
