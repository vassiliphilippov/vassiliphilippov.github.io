windflower

noun
woodland plant of the genus Anemone
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌflʌwə / (BrE)
any woodland plant of the genus Anemone, grown for its attractive flowers and finely divided leaves

Examples

The windflower plant
In early April, the pale blue windflower is one of the first wildflowers to appear in the forest.
She planted white windflowers along the garden path for a touch of spring color.
The woodland was dotted with delicate windflower blooms swaying in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anemone - a plant of the buttercup family with showy flowers
