spacer

noun
inhaler attachment
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsə / (BrE)
a plastic tube or chamber used with an inhaler to help deliver medication more effectively to the lungs

Examples

Inhaler spacer tube
The doctor recommended using a spacer with the inhaler to improve medication delivery.
Children often find it easier to use their inhaler with a spacer attached.
After adding the spacer, she noticed her breathing improved during asthma attacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhaler - a device for administering medication by inhalation
