get worse

verb
to deteriorate; become more serious or unhealthy
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt wɜrs / (AmE), / ɡɛt wɜːs / (BrE)
to become more serious, severe, or unhealthy; to deteriorate in condition, especially with regard to health or a situation

Examples

To get worse quickly
Her symptoms began to get worse after the medication was stopped.
His cold has only gotten worse since last week.
If the pain continues to get worse, you should contact your doctor immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deteriorate - to worsen in condition or quality
  • antonym: improve - to become better in condition or health
