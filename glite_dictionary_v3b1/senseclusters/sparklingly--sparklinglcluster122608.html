sparklingly

adverb
in a brilliant manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrklɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shines brightly or with brilliance, often reflecting light in many directions

Examples

Sparklingly bright water
The lake reflected the sunlight sparklingly on a clear summer morning.
She smiled sparklingly as she accepted the award on stage.
The jewels on her dress glittered sparklingly under the bright lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliantly - in a very bright or impressive way
  • antonym: dully - in a way that is not bright or shiny
