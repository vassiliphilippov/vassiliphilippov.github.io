bad terms

noun
hostile relationship; not on good terms
1.5
Pronunciation: / bæd tɝmz / (AmE), / bad tɜːmz / (BrE)
a state in which people have a poor or hostile relationship with each other

Examples

Part on bad terms
After the heated disagreement, they parted on bad terms and didn't speak for weeks.
It's never wise to leave a job on bad terms with your employer.
She and her former friend are on bad terms after that misunderstanding.

Related Terms
