shelving

noun
postponement of plans
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈʃel.vɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈʃel.vɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of postponing or setting aside a plan, project, or proposal, often temporarily or indefinitely

Examples

Shelving a plan
The sudden shelving of the project surprised everyone in the department.
After much debate, the committee decided on the shelving of the new policy.
Budget constraints led to the shelving of several important initiatives this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cancellation - the act of deciding that something will not happen
  • synonym: postponement - the action of delaying something
