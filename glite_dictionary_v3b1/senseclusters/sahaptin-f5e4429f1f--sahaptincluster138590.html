Sahaptin

noun
member of a Native American people of the Pacific Northwest
Pronunciation: / səˈhæptɪn / (AmE), / səˈhæptɪn / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people originally inhabiting regions along the Columbia River in Oregon, Washington, and northern Idaho.

Examples

Sahaptin people
Anthropologists have studied the traditions of the Sahaptin for over a century.
Several Sahaptin families attended the annual tribal gathering along the river.
The Sahaptin were skilled fishers and traders in the Pacific Northwest.

Related Terms
