unfreeze

verb
make assets or funds available again
4.3 0.06/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàfriÀêz / (AmE), /  ånÀàfriÀêz / (BrE)
to make available or allow the use of something, especially assets or funds, that were previously restricted or blocked

Examples

To unfreeze bank accounts
After several years of restrictions, the court ordered the bank to unfreeze the client's funds.
International agencies urged the authorities to unfreeze humanitarian aid accounts immediately.
The government decided to unfreeze the company's assets after the investigation ended.

Synonyms

  • unblock
    3.5 0.23/m
    unblock removes a block so assets or resources can be used again, while unfreeze makes previously frozen funds available.
  • free
    2.8 5.16/m
    free makes assets, information, or resources available after restriction, while unfreeze specifically allows previously frozen funds to be used.
  • release
    1.8 59/m
    release makes something public, like a product or statement, while unfreeze lets blocked funds or assets be used again.
