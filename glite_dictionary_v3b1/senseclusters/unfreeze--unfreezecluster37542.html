unfreeze

verb
make assets or funds available again
2.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfriːz / (AmE), / ʌnˈfriːz / (BrE)
to make available or allow the use of something, especially assets or funds, that were previously restricted or blocked

Examples

To unfreeze bank accounts
After several years of restrictions, the court ordered the bank to unfreeze the client's funds.
International agencies urged the authorities to unfreeze humanitarian aid accounts immediately.
The government decided to unfreeze the company's assets after the investigation ended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freeze - to make assets or funds unavailable or inaccessible
