unpopular

adjective
not liked or approved by many people
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɑːpjələr / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɒpjʊlə / (BrE)
Not liked or approved by most people; regarded with dislike or lack of approval.

Examples

An unpopular opinion
Her unpopular opinion sparked a heated debate at the meeting.
The mayor faced criticism for making unpopular decisions during his term.
The new policy was extremely unpopular among employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: popular - liked or admired by many people
