stylar

adjective
of a flower's style
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a style, especially the style part of a flower in plants

Examples

The stylar region
The stylar region plays a crucial role in the fertilization process of many flowering plants.
Researchers examined the stylar length to understand differences between plant species.
Damage to the stylar tissue can prevent successful pollination in some flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: style - the slender stalk in a flower connecting the stigma and ovary
