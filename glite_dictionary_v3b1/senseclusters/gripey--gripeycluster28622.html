gripey

adjective
causing stomach pain
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪpi / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪpi / (BrE)
causing or characterized by stomach pain or digestive discomfort, especially with cramping or colicky sensations

Examples

Gripey stomach pain
After eating the undercooked beans, he complained of a gripey feeling in his stomach.
Some medicines can have gripey side effects, especially in sensitive individuals.
The doctor advised her to avoid foods that made her feel gripey or uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colicky - relating to or suffering from severe abdominal pain
  • antonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect
