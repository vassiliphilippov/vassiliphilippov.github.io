noncommitted

adjective
undecided or neutral
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not showing or having a firm decision, opinion, or dedication to a particular course or idea

Examples

Noncommitted attitude
She remained noncommitted during the meeting, refusing to take sides in the debate.
His noncommitted response made it hard to know what he really thought about the plan.
Voters who are still noncommitted can influence the outcome of the election.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decisive - able to make decisions quickly and firmly
  • synonym: neutral - not supporting either side in a conflict or dispute
  • synonym: uncommitted - not pledged or bound to a specific course or action
