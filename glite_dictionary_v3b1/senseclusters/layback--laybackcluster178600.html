layback

verb
to lean backward
7.4
Pronunciation: /leɪ.ˈbæk/ /; / /ˈleɪ.bæk/ (AmE), /leɪ.ˈbæk/ /; / /ˈleɪ.bæk/ (BrE)
to lean the body backward or away from something, especially as a deliberate movement in climbing or board sports

Examples

To layback on the wall
The climber had to layback against the crack to keep her balance on the steep wall.
In skateboarding, he performed a smooth layback as he turned around the corner.
She learned how to layback properly to reach higher holds during her training session.
