pendulum watch

noun
18th-century watch with fake pendulum
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.dʒə.ləm wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.djʊ.ləm wɒtʃ / (BrE)
an 18th-century watch featuring a balance wheel with a decorative, non-functioning pendulum attached

Examples

An pendulum watch design
Collectors highly value a well-preserved pendulum watch from the 18th century.
The pendulum watch on display had an ornate, swinging fake pendulum under its glass.
Though the pendulum watch looked like it had a real pendulum, timekeeping was controlled by the balance wheel.
