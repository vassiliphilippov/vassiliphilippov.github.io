floral envelope

noun
the calyx and corolla of a flower
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrəl ˈɛnvəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrəl ˈɛnvələʊp / (BrE)
the collective outer parts of a flower, comprising the calyx and corolla, that enclose the stamens and pistils
