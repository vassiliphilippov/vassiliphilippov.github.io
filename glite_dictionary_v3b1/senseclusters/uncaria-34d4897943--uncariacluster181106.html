Uncaria

proper noun
plant genus
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɛəriə / (AmE), / ʌŋˈkeəriə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical climbing plants in the coffee family, some species of which are used in traditional medicine

Examples

Uncaria climbing plants
Uncaria is known for its hook-shaped thorns that help it climb trees in the rainforest.
Traditional healers often use extracts from Uncaria species for medicinal purposes.
Scientists are studying the potential health benefits of compounds found in Uncaria plants.
