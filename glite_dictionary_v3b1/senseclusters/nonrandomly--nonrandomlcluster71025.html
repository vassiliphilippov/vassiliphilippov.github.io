nonrandomly

adverb
not by chance
5.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈrændəmli / (AmE), / nɒnˈrændəmli / (BrE)
in a way that is not random or by chance, but according to a specific plan or pattern

Examples

Assigned nonrandomly by plan
The participants were assigned to groups nonrandomly based on their previous experience.
Researchers selected the test sites nonrandomly to control for environmental factors.
The data was collected nonrandomly to focus on a specific demographic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: randomly - in a way that happens by chance, without a plan
  • synonym: systematically - in an organized and methodical way
