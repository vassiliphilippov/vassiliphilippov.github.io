sequestrum

noun
dead bone fragment
9.9
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈkwes.trəm/ /; / /sə.ˈkwes.trəm/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈkwes.trəm/ /; / /sə.ˈkwes.trəm/ (BrE)
a piece of dead bone that has separated from healthy bone due to disease or injury

Examples

Bone sequestrum
The X-ray revealed a sequestrum that needed to be surgically removed.
After the infection, a sequestrum developed in the patient's leg bone.
Doctors monitored the healing process to ensure no sequestrum remained.
