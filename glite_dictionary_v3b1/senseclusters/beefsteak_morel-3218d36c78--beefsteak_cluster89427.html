beefsteak morel

noun
a poisonous Gyromitra mushroom
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːfˌsteɪk məˈrɛl / (AmE), / ˈbiːfsteɪk məˈrɛl / (BrE)
a poisonous mushroom of the genus Gyromitra, with a smooth, later wrinkled surface, ranging in color from dull yellow to brown

Examples

A beefsteak morel mushroom
Experts warn that beefsteak morels can be lethal if consumed raw or improperly cooked.
The forager mistook a beefsteak morel for an edible mushroom, unaware of its poisonous nature.
The surface of the beefsteak morel becomes increasingly wrinkled as it matures.
