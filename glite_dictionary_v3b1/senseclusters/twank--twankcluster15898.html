twank

noun
sharp plucking sound
Pronunciation: / twæŋk / (AmE), / twæŋk / (BrE)
a sharp, resonant plucking or twanging sound, especially produced by a stringed instrument or stretched material

Examples

A guitar twank
The guitar made a loud twank as she strummed the open strings.
He heard a sudden twank when the rubber band snapped back into place.
Each time the harpist plucked a string, a clear twank echoed through the hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pluck - a sound made by pulling and releasing a string
  • synonym: twang - a sharp, vibrating sound made by plucking a string
