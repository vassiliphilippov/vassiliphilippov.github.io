countermark

noun
mark for validation
9.3 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààka änt…ôrÀåm…ërk / (AmE), / Ààka änt…ôm…ëÀêk / (BrE)
an official or identifying mark stamped on a coin, document, or goods to show validation, reissue, origin, or ownership

Examples
