mumsy

noun
affectionate term for mother
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmzi / (AmE), / ˈmʌmzi / (BrE)
an affectionate or informal term for one's mother, often used by children or in a playful context

Examples

Whenever he felt sad, he would call out for mumsy to comfort him.
At the family picnic, everyone laughed when she affectionately called her mother mumsy.
Little Timmy made a card for his mumsy on Mother's Day.
