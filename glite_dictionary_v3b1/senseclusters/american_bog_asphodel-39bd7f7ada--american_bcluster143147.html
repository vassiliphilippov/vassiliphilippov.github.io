American bog asphodel

noun
wetland plant of eastern US
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən bɑɡ ˈæsfəˌdɛl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən bɒɡ ˈæs.fə.dɛl / (BrE)
