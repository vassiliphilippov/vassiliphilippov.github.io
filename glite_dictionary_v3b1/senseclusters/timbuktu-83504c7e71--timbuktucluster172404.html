Timbuktu

proper noun
city in Mali, famed historic trade center
Pronunciation: / tɪmˌbʌkˈtu / (AmE), / tɪmˌbʌkˈtuː / (BrE)
A city in central Mali, near the Niger River, historically renowned as a center of trade and Islamic learning, and famous for its role in the trans-Saharan gold trade.

Examples

Timbuktu city
Many explorers dreamed of reaching Timbuktu, which was long thought to be an unreachable city of gold.
Scholars once traveled great distances to study at Timbuktu's legendary libraries and universities.
The ancient manuscripts preserved in Timbuktu are essential to understanding West African history.
