corkscrew

noun
bottle opener with a metal spiral
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrkˌskru / (AmE), / ˈkɔːk.skruː / (BrE)
a device for pulling corks from bottles, typically consisting of a pointed metal spiral attached to a handle

Examples

A corkscrew opener
He reached for the corkscrew to open the bottle of wine.
Make sure to pack a corkscrew for the picnic or we won't be able to open anything.
The elegant corkscrew had a wooden handle and a shiny metal spiral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottle opener - a device for removing caps from bottles
