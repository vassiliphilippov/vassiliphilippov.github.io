panoramic view

noun
broad, comprehensive view
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpænəˈræmɪk vju / (AmE), / ˌpænəˈræmɪk vjuː / (BrE)
an extensive and comprehensive view of a place, situation, or topic, often as if seen from a broad vantage point

Examples

A panoramic view of city
From the top floor, you get a panoramic view of the entire city.
Standing on the hill, she admired the panoramic view stretching out below her.
The report provides a panoramic view of the challenges facing the industry.
