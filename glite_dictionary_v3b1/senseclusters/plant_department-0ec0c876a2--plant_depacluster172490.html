plant department

noun
division in charge of physical facilities in a business
Pronunciation: / plænt dɪˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / plɑːnt dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division within a company responsible for building, maintaining, and overseeing the physical facilities and equipment.

Examples

The plant department manager
Any issues with machinery should be reported to the plant department for prompt repair.
The plant department oversaw the renovation of the factory last spring.
The company expanded its plant department to handle new equipment installations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facilities management - the process of managing and maintaining buildings and equipment
