teaser

noun
narrow stage flat blocking view into wings
8.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtiÀêz…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàtiÀêz…ô / (BrE)
A narrow flat or curtain placed at each side and/or above the stage to prevent the audience from seeing into the wings or the backstage area.

Examples

Stage teaser curtain
Adjusting the teaser helped conceal the actors waiting in the wings.
The stage crew moved the teaser into position before the show began.
We repainted the left teaser to match the new set design.

Synonyms

  • tormentor
    9.9 0.04/m

  • masking piece
    9.5 0.00/m
    a masking piece is any scenery that hides stage areas from the audience, while a teaser is a narrow top or side curtain.
