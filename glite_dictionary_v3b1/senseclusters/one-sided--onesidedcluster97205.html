Synonyms

  • unilateral
    7.2 2/m
    unilateral describes an action taken by one side alone, while one-sided suggests something not fair or balanced.
  • biased
    4.0 4/m
    biased shows an unfair preference for one person or group, while one-sided emphasizes lack of balance or fairness.
  • slanted
    6.2 0.03/m
    slanted presents information in a way that favors one side, while one-sided can mean any unfair or unbalanced situation.
