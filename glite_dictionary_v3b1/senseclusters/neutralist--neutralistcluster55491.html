neutralist

noun
advocate of neutrality in international affairs
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.trə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.trə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates a policy of neutrality in international affairs or conflicts

Examples

A neutralist in politics
As a committed neutralist, she argued against joining any military alliances.
He was criticized by both sides for his neutralist position during the conflict.
The country's new government was led by neutralists intent on avoiding Cold War entanglements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutral - not supporting any side in a conflict
