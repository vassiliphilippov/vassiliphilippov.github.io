observability

noun
system state visibility
7.9
Pronunciation: / əbˌzɜːrvəˈbɪləti /; / əbˌzɝːvəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / əbˌzɜːvəˈbɪləti /; / əbˌzɜːvəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the degree to which the internal state of a system can be determined or measured from its external outputs, especially in engineering or technical contexts
