medical practitioner

noun
someone qualified to practise medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl prækˈtɪʃənər / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl prækˈtɪʃənə / (BrE)
a person who is qualified to practise medicine, such as a doctor or physician

Examples

A medical practitioner treats patients
As a medical practitioner, she puts her patients' well-being first.
Many rural areas are experiencing a shortage of medical practitioners.
The medical practitioner prescribed antibiotics to treat the infection.
