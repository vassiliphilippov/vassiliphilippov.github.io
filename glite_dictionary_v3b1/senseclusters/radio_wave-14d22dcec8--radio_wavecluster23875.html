Synonyms

  • radio radiation
    4.0 0.00/m
    radio radiation is the scientific name for electromagnetic waves in the radio band, while radio wave is the common term for those communication waves.
  • radio emission
    5.2 0.02/m
    radio emission is the act or energy released as radio waves from a source, while radio wave names the physical wave used for communication.
  • radio signal
    2.7 1/m
    radio signal is a radio wave carrying information like voice or data, while radio wave is the underlying physical electromagnetic wave.
