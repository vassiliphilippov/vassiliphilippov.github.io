twist

verb
to injure by turning suddenly
5.4 2.2
Pronunciation: / twɪst / (AmE), / twɪst / (BrE)
to turn or strain a part of the body, especially a joint, so that it is injured or sprained

Examples

To twist an ankle
He twisted his knee on the uneven pavement and had to limp home.
If you twist your wrist during a fall, it may swell up quickly.
She twisted her ankle while jogging in the park.
