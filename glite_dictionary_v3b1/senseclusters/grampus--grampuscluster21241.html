grampus

noun
predatory toothed whale (orca or Risso's dolphin)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræm.pəs /; / ˈɡræmpəs / (AmE), / ˈɡræm.pəs /; / ˈɡræmpəs / (BrE)
a large predatory whale or dolphin, such as the orca or Risso's dolphin, found in cold seas
