maria

noun
dark plains on the moon's surface
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛəriə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːrɪə / (BrE)
dark, extensive regions on the surface of the moon, formed by ancient volcanic eruptions, and appearing as large, flat plains

Examples

Lunar maria plains
Astronomers have mapped the maria on the lunar surface in detail.
The Apollo missions landed near some of the largest maria on the Moon.
Unlike the brighter highlands, maria are rich in basalt and appear darker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mare - a large, dark plain on the surface of the moon
