eep

interjection
cry of alarm
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / iÀêp / (AmE), / iÀêp / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express a sudden short cry of fear, surprise, or embarrassment

Examples

Eep! in the dark
When the lights suddenly went out, she let out a loud eep.
He gave a quick eep when the cold water splashed on him.
I heard an eep from the other room and rushed to see what happened.
