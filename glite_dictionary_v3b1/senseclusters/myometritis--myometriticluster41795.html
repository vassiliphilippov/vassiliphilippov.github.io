myometritis

noun
inflammation of the uterine muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˌoʊməˈtraɪtɪs / (AmE), / maɪəʊmɪˈtraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the muscular layer of the uterus (myometrium)

Examples

Myometritis after childbirth
Antibiotics are commonly used to treat myometritis in women after childbirth.
Symptoms of myometritis may include pelvic pain and fever.
The patient was diagnosed with myometritis following a postpartum infection.

Related Terms
