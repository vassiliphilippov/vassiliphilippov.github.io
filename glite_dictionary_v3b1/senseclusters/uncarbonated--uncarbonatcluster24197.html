uncarbonated

adjective
not containing carbonation (of a drink)
4.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkɑːr.bə.neɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈkɑːr.bə.neɪ.təd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkɑː.bə.neɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈkɑː.bə.neɪ.təd/ (BrE)
(of a drink) not containing dissolved carbon dioxide; not fizzy or sparkling.

Examples

Uncarbonated soft drink
Many soft drinks are uncarbonated to appeal to people who dislike fizzy beverages.
She prefers uncarbonated water with her lunch instead of sparkling varieties.
The juice was uncarbonated and tasted refreshingly natural.
