Bismarck Sea

proper noun
sea near Bismarck Archipelago in Pacific Ocean
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪzmɑrk si / (AmE), / ˈbɪzˌmɑːk siː / (BrE)
an arm of the southwestern Pacific Ocean, located to the southwest of the Bismarck Archipelago, north of Papua New Guinea

Examples

The Bismarck Sea coast
Fishermen from Papua New Guinea rely on the rich marine life of the Bismarck Sea.
Several rivers from New Britain island flow into the Bismarck Sea.
The Bismarck Sea is known for the important World War II battle fought in its waters.
