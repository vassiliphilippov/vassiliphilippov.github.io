feloniously

adverb
in a criminal way
9.4
Pronunciation: / fəˈloʊniəsli / (AmE), / fɪˈləʊniəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or involves committing a serious crime or felony

Examples

Act feloniously
The suspect was charged after acting feloniously during the break-in.
He was found guilty of behaving feloniously in connection with the theft.
The court determined that she had acted feloniously by forging the documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminally - in a way that involves a crime
  • antonym: lawfully - in a way that is allowed by law
