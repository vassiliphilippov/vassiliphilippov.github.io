slug

verb
to strike hard with a fist
Pronunciation: / slʌɡ / (AmE), / slʌɡ / (BrE)
to hit someone or something very hard, especially with the fist

Examples

To slug an opponent
After the argument, he slugged his opponent in the jaw.
Don't threaten to slug someone just because you disagree.
The batter slugged the ball out of the park with one powerful swing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hit - to strike someone or something
  • synonym: punch - to hit with a closed fist
