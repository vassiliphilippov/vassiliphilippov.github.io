puny

adjective
small or weak; insignificant
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuni /; / ˈpjuːni / (AmE), / ˈpjuːni / (BrE)
small and weak; not impressive or significant in size, strength, or importance

Examples

A puny effort
His puny efforts did little to solve the problem.

The wrestler looked down at his puny opponent and laughed.
Despite his puny frame, he was determined to compete in the wrestling match.
She dismissed his puny efforts, insisting he could do much better.
The seedlings looked puny compared to the sturdy young trees around them.
