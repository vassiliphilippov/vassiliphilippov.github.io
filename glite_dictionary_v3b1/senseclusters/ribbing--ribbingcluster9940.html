ribbing

noun
framework of ribs in a structure
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbɪŋ / (BrE)
a framework or arrangement of ribs, especially as part of a structure such as a building or ship

Examples

Wooden ribbing
During restoration, workers discovered the original wooden ribbing beneath the plaster.
The architect designed the bridge with reinforced steel ribbing for greater stability.
The vaulted ceiling was supported by a complex ribbing system.
