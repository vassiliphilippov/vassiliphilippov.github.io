trash heap of history

noun
obsolete or discarded by history
Pronunciation: / træʃ hiːp əv ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / træʃ hiːp ɒv ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
used to refer to ideas, practices, or things that have become obsolete or irrelevant and are considered discarded by history

Examples

Many failed policies have ended up on the trash heap of history.
That outdated technology belongs in the trash heap of history.
Their once-popular movement is now just a trash heap of history.
