longhorn

noun
breed of cattle with long horns
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a breed of cattle with long, curved horns, originally from the southwestern United States

Examples

A longhorn cow

Ranchers prize the longhorn for its hardiness and historical significance.
The Texas longhorn is famed for its distinctive curving horns.
