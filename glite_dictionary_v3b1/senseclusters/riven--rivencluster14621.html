riven

adjective
split or torn apart
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvən / (AmE), / ˈrɪvən / (BrE)
split or torn apart, often used to describe something divided by conflict or force

Examples

Riven by conflict
The community was riven by disagreements over the new development project.
After the scandal, the organization remained riven for years.
The ancient rock was riven by deep cracks from centuries of erosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divided - separated into parts or groups
  • antonym: united - joined together as a whole
