calligrapher

noun
a person skilled in calligraphy
1.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪɡrəfər / (AmE), / kəˈlɪɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person skilled in the art of beautiful handwriting, especially one who creates decorative or artistic lettering

Examples

A calligrapher writes beautifully
Many artists admire the precision required to become a skilled calligrapher.
She studied under a master calligrapher for several years.
The wedding invitations were addressed by a talented calligrapher.
