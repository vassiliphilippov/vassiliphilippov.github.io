rejustify

verb
to justify again
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to justify again, especially to adjust the alignment or reasoning of text or arguments a second time

Examples

To rejustify text
After editing the document, she needed to rejustify the text to fit the new margins.
The lawyer decided to rejustify his argument in light of the new evidence.
If you change the font size, you may have to rejustify the entire page.
