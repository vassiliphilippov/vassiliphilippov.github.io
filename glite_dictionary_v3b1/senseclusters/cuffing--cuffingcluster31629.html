cuffing

noun
folding clothing edge
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌf.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkʌf.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of folding back or turning up the edge of a sleeve or trouser to create a cuff, or attaching a cuff to clothing

Examples

Cuffing the jeans
She spent the morning cuffing her jeans to keep them above her boots.
The tailor demonstrated proper cuffing techniques for dress shirt sleeves.
After cuffing the pants, he checked that both legs were even.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemming - the act of sewing or folding the edge of fabric to prevent fraying
  • antonym: unrolling - the act of undoing a fold or roll in fabric
