cap

noun
a soft, close-fitting hat
Pronunciation: / kæp / (AmE), / kap / (BrE)
a type of soft, often close-fitting covering for the head, typically with a visor or brim

Examples

Baseball cap
A tight-fitting cap is required in the swimming pool.
He wore a blue cap to keep his head warm.
The baseball player adjusted his cap before stepping up to bat.
