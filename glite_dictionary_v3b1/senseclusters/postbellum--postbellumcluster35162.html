postbellum

adjective
after a war
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈbɛləm / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈbɛləm / (BrE)
relating to the period after a war, especially after the American Civil War

Examples

Postbellum South
The postbellum era brought significant changes to the Southern economy.
Many postbellum writers focused on themes of reconstruction and recovery.
Historians often compare antebellum and postbellum social structures in the United States.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antebellum - relating to the period before a war, especially the American Civil War
