habitual lateness

noun
a regular pattern of being late
Pronunciation: / həˈbɪtʃuəl ˈleɪtnəs / (AmE), / həˈbɪtjʊəl ˈleɪtnəs / (BrE)
the consistent or frequent tendency to be late

Examples

Habitual lateness at work
Habitual lateness can harm your professional reputation and affect your relationships at work.
Despite many warnings, his habitual lateness resulted in a formal complaint from his manager.
The teacher addressed the student's habitual lateness during the parent-teacher meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punctuality - the quality of being on time
  • synonym: tardiness - the quality or fact of being late
