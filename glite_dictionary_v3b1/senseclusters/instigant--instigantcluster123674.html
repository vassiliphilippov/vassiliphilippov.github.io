instigant

noun
someone who incites or stirs up trouble
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstɪɡənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnstɪɡənt / (BrE)
a person who deliberately incites or stirs up trouble or conflict

Examples

An instigant of trouble
During the heated debate, he was labeled an instigant by his opponents.
The journalist accused the protest leader of being an instigant in the recent unrest.
The report concluded that several instigants had encouraged the crowd's violent actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instigator - person who initiates or provokes something, especially trouble
