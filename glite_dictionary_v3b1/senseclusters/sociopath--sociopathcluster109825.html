sociopath

noun
person with antisocial personality disorder
2.5 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.siː.oʊ.pæθ/ /; / /ˈsoʊ.siː.ə.pæθ/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.siː.ə.pæθ/ /; / /ˈsəʊ.siː.əʊ.pæθ/ (BrE)
