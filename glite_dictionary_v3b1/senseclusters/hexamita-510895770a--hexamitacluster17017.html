Hexamita

noun
a genus of flagellated protozoan parasites
Pronunciation: / hɛkˈsæmɪtə / (AmE), / hɛkˈsamɪtə /; / hɛkˈsæmɪtə / (BrE)
a genus of flagellated protozoa, some species of which are free-living or parasitic in the intestines of birds and other animals
