entranceway

noun
a way in; entry passage or door
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrənsˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛntrənsˌweɪ / (BrE)
a passage, door, or area that provides access to a building, room, or enclosure

Examples

The entranceway door
A sudden gust of wind blew through the open entranceway, scattering papers across the floor.
She placed a potted plant in the entranceway to welcome guests as they arrived.
The children left their shoes in the entranceway before rushing inside to play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entryway - a passage or hall at the entrance to a building
  • antonym: exit - a way out; a means of leaving a place
