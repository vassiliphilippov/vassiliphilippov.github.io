knightliness

noun
qualities or principles of knighthood
9.5 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈnaɪt.lɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈnaɪt.liː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈnaɪt.lɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈnaɪt.liː.nəs/ (BrE)
the qualities or principles associated with knighthood, especially the ideals of honor, courage, and courteous conduct typical of medieval knights

Examples

Knightliness and honor
He was admired for his knightliness, always showing respect, bravery, and loyalty.
Stories of old often praise the knightliness of those who lived by a strict code of honor.
The hero demonstrated remarkable knightliness in defending the weak and upholding justice.
