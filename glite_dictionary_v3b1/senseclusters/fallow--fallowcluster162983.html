fallow

verb
to leave land unplanted
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfæ.loʊ/ /; / /ˈfæl.oʊ/ (AmE), /ˈfæ.ləʊ/ /; / /ˈfæl.əʊ/ (BrE)
