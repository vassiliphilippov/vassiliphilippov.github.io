respell

verb
to spell again
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈspɛl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈspɛl / (BrE)
to spell a word again or in a different way, often to show its pronunciation more clearly

Examples

To respell a word
If you make a mistake, you can respell the word on the next line.
The teacher asked him to respell the answer to make sure it was correct.
Some names are often respelled to match their pronunciation.
The teacher asked the student to respell the word to make its pronunciation clearer.
Some dictionaries respell foreign names to help readers say them correctly.
You may need to respell technical terms for a general audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: misspell - to spell a word incorrectly
  • synonym: rewrite - to write something again in a different way
  • synonym: transcribe - to write out spoken words or sounds using written symbols
