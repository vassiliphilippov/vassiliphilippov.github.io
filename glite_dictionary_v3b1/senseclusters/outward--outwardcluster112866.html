outward

adverb
towards the outside
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊt.wɚd / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.wəd / (BrE)
Towards the outside; away from the centre or interior.

Examples

Move outward from center
He moved the box outward to make more space in the closet.
Push the lever outward to open the panel.
The petals of the flower spread outward as it bloomed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inward - towards the inside; towards the centre
