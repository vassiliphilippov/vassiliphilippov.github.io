sapodilla

noun
tropical fruit tree; edible fruit; source of chicle
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpəˈdɪlə / (AmE), / ˌsapəˈdɪlə / (BrE)
A large tropical American evergreen tree (Manilkara zapota), cultivated for its sweet edible fruit and as a source of chicle gum.

Examples

Sapodilla tree gum
Chicle, used in making chewing gum, is extracted from the sapodilla tree.
I tried sapodilla for the first time while traveling in Mexico.
The farmer harvested a basket of ripe sapodilla from the orchard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sapote - Edible tropical fruit from several species, often confused with sapodilla
