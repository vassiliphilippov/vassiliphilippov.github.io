loa

noun
Vodou spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə / (BrE)
a spirit or deity in Haitian Vodou and related Afro-Caribbean religions, believed to act as an intermediary between humans and the divine
