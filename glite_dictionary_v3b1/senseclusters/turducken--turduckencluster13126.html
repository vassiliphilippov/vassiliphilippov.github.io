turducken

noun
nested combination
Pronunciation: / tɚˈdʌkən / (AmE), / təˈdʌkən / (BrE)
a combination of several different things, especially when elements are nested or layered together, often used figuratively

Examples

A tech turducken
The new smartphone is a technological turducken with features from tablets, cameras, and laptops.
Her novel is a literary turducken, blending mystery, romance, and science fiction in one story.
The festival was a cultural turducken with music, food, and art from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybrid - something made by combining two or more different things
  • synonym: mashup - a mixture or combination of different elements
