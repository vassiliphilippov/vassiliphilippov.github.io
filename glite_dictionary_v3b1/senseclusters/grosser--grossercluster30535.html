grosser

noun
high earner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊsər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that earns a specified amount of money, especially before expenses are deducted

Examples

Top grosser movie
The film became the highest grosser of the year, breaking several box office records.
Among all the products, the new smartphone was the top grosser for the company.
Summer blockbusters are often the biggest grossers at the box office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earner - a person or thing that earns money
  • antonym: spender - a person who spends money
