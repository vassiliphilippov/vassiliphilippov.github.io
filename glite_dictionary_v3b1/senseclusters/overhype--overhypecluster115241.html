overhype

verb
to exaggerate promotion
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhaɪp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhaɪp / (BrE)
to promote or praise something or someone more than is reasonable or deserved, often exaggerating its qualities or importance

Examples

To overhype a product
Critics argued that the company overhyped its new product, leading to disappointment among customers.
The movie was overhyped before its release, but many viewers found it average.
Some fans felt the concert was overhyped and did not live up to expectations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggerate - to make something seem larger, better, or more important than it really is
  • antonym: understate - to describe something as less important or smaller than it really is
