intermodule

noun
module connector
8.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈmɑːdʒ.uːl/ /; / /ɪn.tər.ˈmɑː.djuːl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈmɒd.juːl/ /; / /ɪn.tə.ˈmɒdʒ.uːl/ (BrE)
a physical component, interface, or unit that connects two or more modules in a system or structure

Examples

An intermodule unit
The engineers installed an intermodule to link the laboratory and living quarters.
A damaged intermodule can disrupt communication between different parts of the spacecraft.
Each intermodule must be carefully tested to ensure a secure connection between modules.
