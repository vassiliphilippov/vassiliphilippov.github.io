addict

noun
person overly devoted to an activity
Pronunciation: / ˈædɪkt / (AmE), / ˈædɪkt / (BrE)
a person who is extremely enthusiastic about a particular activity or interest, often in a way that is compared to addiction

Examples

A news addict
As a news addict, he checks his phone for updates every few minutes.
My brother is a real addict when it comes to video games; he can play for hours on end.
She's become a fitness addict since joining the new gym downtown.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: casual - a person with a mild interest, not passionate
  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is highly interested in a particular activity or subject
  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive and single-minded zeal
