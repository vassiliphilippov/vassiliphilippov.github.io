sab

noun
person who sabotages
Pronunciation: / sæb / (AmE), / sæb / (BrE)
a person who deliberately damages or interferes with something, especially as a form of protest; a saboteur

Examples

A sab at a protest
The group accused him of being a sab after the protest equipment was found broken.
During the demonstration, a sab tried to disrupt the event by cutting the power.
Rumors spread that a sab was responsible for the sudden malfunction of the machines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saboteur - a person who deliberately destroys or damages something
  • synonym: vandal - a person who deliberately damages property
