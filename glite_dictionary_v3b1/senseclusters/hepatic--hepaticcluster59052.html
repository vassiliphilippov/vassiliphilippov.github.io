hepatic

adjective
relating to the liver
Pronunciation: / hɪˈpætɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈpætɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting the liver.

Examples

Hepatic disease
Certain viruses can lead to hepatic inflammation and damage.
He developed hepatic complications after prolonged use of certain medications.
The doctor ordered a hepatic function test to assess the patient's liver health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liver - a large organ in the body that processes blood and produces bile
