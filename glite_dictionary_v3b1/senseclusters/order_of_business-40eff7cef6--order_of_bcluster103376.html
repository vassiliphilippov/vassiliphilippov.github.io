Synonyms

  • agenda
    2.3 64/m
    agenda is a short plan of items to discuss at meetings, while order of business names the formal sequence to follow.
  • order of the day
    4.9 0.01/m
    order of the day lists matters for a particular meeting day, while order of business refers to the overall sequence to be handled.
  • order book
    4.1 0.01/m
    order book records the planned sequence of activities for a day, while order of business is the meeting's list to be dealt with.
  • order paper
    7.6 0.00/m
