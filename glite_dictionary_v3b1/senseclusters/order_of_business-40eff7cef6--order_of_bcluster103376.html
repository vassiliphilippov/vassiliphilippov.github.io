order of business

noun
agenda for a meeting
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər əv ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əv ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
a list or sequence of items to be dealt with in a meeting or an assembly

Examples

The order of business today
Before the discussion began, the chairman reviewed the order of business for the day.
Changing the order of business requires a motion and a vote from the committee.
Let's stick to the order of business to make sure we cover all topics efficiently.
