hot up

verb
become hot or hotter
Pronunciation: / hɑt ʌp / (AmE), / hɒt ʌp / (BrE)
(of a place, object, or situation) to become hot or hotter; to gain heat.

Examples

The weather hots up
As summer approached, the weather began to hot up considerably.
The debate will hot up once the results are announced.
Things really hotted up in the kitchen when the oven was turned on.
