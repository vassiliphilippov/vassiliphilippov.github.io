ovarian artery

noun
artery supplying blood to the ovaries
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvɛri.ən ˈɑːr.t̬ɚ.i / (AmE), / əʊˈvɛəri.ən ˈɑː.tər.i / (BrE)
the artery that branches from the aorta to supply blood to the ovaries

Examples

The ovarian artery branch
An ultrasound can sometimes reveal abnormalities in the ovarian artery.
Blood supply to the ovaries primarily comes from the ovarian artery.
The surgeon carefully isolated the ovarian artery before proceeding with the operation.
