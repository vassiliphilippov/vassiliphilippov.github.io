twosome

noun
a pair of people together
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːsəm / (AmE), / ˈtuːsəm / (BrE)
a pair of people or things, especially people who do things together

Examples

We often saw the twosome walking hand in hand along the beach.
Each card in the deck comes in a twosome for matching games.
The restaurant offered a special dessert for a twosome to share.
We spotted a twosome of swans gliding across the lake.
