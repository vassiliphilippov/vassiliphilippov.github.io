fourth-rate

adjective
of very poor quality
1.4
Pronunciation: / fɔrθ ˌreɪt / (AmE), / fɔːθ ˌreɪt / (BrE)
of very poor quality; inferior or substandard

Examples

A fourth-rate product
Despite its budget, the product was dismissed as fourth-rate by most consumers.
Many critics considered the play a fourth-rate attempt at comedy with little originality.
The restaurant served fourth-rate food that failed to impress any diners.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: third-rate - of poor or inferior quality
