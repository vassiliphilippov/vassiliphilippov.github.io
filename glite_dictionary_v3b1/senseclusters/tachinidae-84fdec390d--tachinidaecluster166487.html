Tachinidae

noun
family of parasitic flies
Pronunciation: / təˈkɪnɪdiː /; / təˈkɪnəˌdiː / (AmE), / təˈkɪnɪdiː /; / takɪˈnɪːdiː / (BrE)
a large family of parasitic flies whose larvae develop inside other insects; often used to control pest insects
