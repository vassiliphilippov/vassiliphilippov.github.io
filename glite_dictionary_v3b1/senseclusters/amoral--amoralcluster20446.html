amoral

adjective
lacking morals
6.3
Pronunciation: /eɪ.ˈmɔː.rəl/ (AmE), /eɪ.ˈmɒ.rəl/ (BrE)
not concerned with or not related to questions of right and wrong; morally neutral or indifferent

Examples

An amoral decision
The company made an amoral decision based only on profit.
He is often described as amoral because he does not consider the consequences of his actions.
Some people believe that nature is amoral, neither good nor bad.
The scientist insisted that his research was amoral and did not address ethical questions.
Some people view nature as amoral, operating without any sense of right or wrong.
Her decision was based on practical reasons and was completely amoral.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immoral - not following accepted standards of right and wrong
  • antonym: moral - concerned with principles of right and wrong
  • synonym: neutral - not taking sides or showing no strong opinion
  • synonym: unethical - not morally correct
