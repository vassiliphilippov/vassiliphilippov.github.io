warm brown

noun
a cozy, reddish or yellowish shade of brown
Pronunciation: / wɔrm braʊn / (AmE), / wɔːm braʊn / (BrE)
a shade of brown with reddish or yellowish undertones, creating a cozy and inviting appearance

Examples

A warm brown color
A warm brown rug gave the office a sense of comfort and style.
Her coat was a deep, warm brown that complemented her complexion.
The living room was decorated in shades of warm brown to create a welcoming atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: brown - a color resembling that of earth or wood
  • degree higher: reddish brown - a shade of brown with a stronger red tone
