bathroom singer

noun
person who sings privately, especially in the bathroom
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθˌruːm ˈsɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθruːm ˈsɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys singing informally and privately, typically while in the bathroom
