Murinae

noun
rodent subfamily
Pronunciation: / ˈmjʊrəˌniː / (AmE), / ˈmjʊərɪniː / (BrE)
a subfamily of rodents that includes true mice and rats

Examples

The Murinae subfamily
The Murinae are one of the most widespread subfamilies of mammals in the world.
Scientists study the Murinae to understand the evolution of rodents.
Many common house mice and rats belong to the Murinae subfamily.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Muridae - the family of rodents that includes Murinae and other subfamilies
  • synonym: rodent - a mammal of the order Rodentia, such as a mouse or rat
