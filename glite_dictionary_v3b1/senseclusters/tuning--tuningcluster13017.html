tuning

noun
optimizing system or model settings

(in computing and data science) the process of selecting and adjusting parameters or settings of a system, algorithm, or model (for example, hyperparameters) to improve performance.

Examples

Model tuning
The data scientist spent weeks on tuning the neural network to improve its validation accuracy.
Automated tuning tools can search large parameter spaces faster than manual experimentation.
Effective tuning balances model complexity and generalization to avoid overfitting on the training data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperparameter tuning - the specific process of choosing hyperparameters for a machine learning model
  • synonym: optimization - the process of making something as effective or functional as possible
