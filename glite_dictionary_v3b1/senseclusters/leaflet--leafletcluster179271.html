leaflet

noun
small informational booklet or pamphlet
2.7 1.93/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàliÀêfl…ôt / (AmE), / ÀàliÀêfl…™t / (BrE)
a small printed sheet, usually folded, used for giving information or advertising, often having a paper cover

Examples

A leaflet in the mail
A leaflet was included in the mail to advertise the new restaurant.
Please read the leaflet before taking any medication.
The charity distributed thousands of leaflets to raise awareness of the campaign.

  • handbill
    5.4 0.32/m
    handbill is a short printed notice handed out on streets, while leaflet is a folded or flat sheet for information or advertising.
  • circular
    4.4 1.54/m
    circular is an official or commercial notice sent widely, while leaflet is a simple folded sheet often given by hand.
  • brochure
    2.7 7.42/m

  • pamphlet
    3.1 5.62/m

  • booklet
    1.9 4.16/m

  • handout
    2.5 1.59/m
    handout is a sheet given in class to help learning, while leaflet is for public information or advertising and mass distribution.
