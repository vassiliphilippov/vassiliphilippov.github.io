cylindrical lining

noun
cylindrical metal sleeve reducing friction
6.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlɪndrɪkəl ˈlaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪndrɪkəl ˈlaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
a cylindrical metal sleeve or layer used in machinery to reduce friction between moving parts
