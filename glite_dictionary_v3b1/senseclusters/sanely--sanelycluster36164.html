sanely

adverb
in a rational way
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪnli / (AmE), / ˈseɪnli / (BrE)
in a sane, reasonable, or rational way

Examples

To think sanely
After some rest, he was able to think sanely about the situation.
It is important to respond sanely even when faced with difficult challenges.
She spoke sanely about the options during the meeting, despite the stress involved.
He addressed the concerns sanely, refusing to let panic dictate his actions.
It is important to respond sanely when faced with unexpected challenges.
She calmly and sanely explained her side of the story during the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrationally - in an illogical or unreasonable manner
  • synonym: sensibly - in a sensible, practical, or reasonable manner
