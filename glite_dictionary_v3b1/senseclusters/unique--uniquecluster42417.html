unique

noun
one of a kind
Pronunciation: / juˈnik / (AmE), / juːˈniːk / (BrE)
a person or thing that is the only one of its kind; someone or something unlike anything else.

Examples

A true unique
He was considered a unique in his field, admired for his innovative ideas.
The museum displayed a unique that attracted visitors from around the world.
Among all her classmates, she stood out as a true unique.

Related Terms

  • antonym: copy - a thing made to be exactly like another
  • synonym: original - a person or thing that is new and different from others
