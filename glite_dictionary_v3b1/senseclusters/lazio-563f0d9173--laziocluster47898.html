Lazio

proper noun
region of west central Italy
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːtsi.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈlætsioʊ / (BrE)
an area in west central Italy on the Tyrrhenian Sea, historically an ancient region and now a modern administrative region that includes Rome

Examples

Lazio region Italy
Many tourists visit Lazio to explore its rich Roman history and beautiful countryside.
The city of Rome is located within the region of Lazio.
Wines produced in Lazio are becoming increasingly popular internationally.
