carpetless

adjective
without a carpet
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrpɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪtləs / (BrE)
not covered by a carpet; lacking a carpet on the surface

Examples

Carpetless floor
The children played on the carpetless floor, which was cool and smooth.
After removing the old rug, the room looked strangely carpetless and empty.
She preferred a carpetless hallway because it was easier to clean.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carpeted - covered with a carpet
