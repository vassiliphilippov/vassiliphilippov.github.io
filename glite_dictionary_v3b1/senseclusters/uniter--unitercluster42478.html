uniter

noun
machine that joins parts
Pronunciation: / juˈnaɪtər / (AmE), / juˈnaɪtə / (BrE)
a machine or device that joins or combines separate parts or materials into a single unit

Examples

Industrial uniter machine
The new uniter in the assembly line increased production speed by joining metal sheets efficiently.
Technicians monitored the uniter to ensure all parts were combined correctly during the process.
After maintenance, the uniter was able to join plastic components without any errors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joiner - a machine or person that joins things together
  • antonym: separator - a machine that divides or separates materials
