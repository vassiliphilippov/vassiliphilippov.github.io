crofting

noun
Scottish small-scale farming
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒftɪŋ / (BrE)
a system of small-scale farming, especially in Scotland, where land is rented and worked by individual families

Examples

Small-scale crofting farms
Many families in the Highlands have relied on crofting for generations.
The tradition of crofting continues to influence rural communities in Scotland today.
Government policies have aimed to support crofting as a way to preserve local culture and landscapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subsistence farming - farming that provides only enough for the farmer's family
  • synonym: tenant farming - farming land rented from a landlord
