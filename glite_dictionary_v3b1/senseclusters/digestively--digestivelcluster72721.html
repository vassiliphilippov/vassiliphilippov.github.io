digestively

adverb
clearly and simply
Pronunciation: / daɪˈdʒɛstɪvli / (AmE), / daɪˈdʒɛstɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that makes information or ideas easy to understand or absorb

Examples

Digestively explained
The teacher presented the lesson digestively, making even the hardest concepts easy to follow.
She summarized the report digestively so everyone could understand the main points quickly.
The article explains the science digestively, which helps readers without a technical background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to understand
  • antonym: confusingly - in a way that is difficult to understand
