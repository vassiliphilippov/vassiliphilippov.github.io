Ah Mm

noun
expressive older woman (Singapore/Malaysia)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑː ˈməm / (AmE), / ɑː ˈməm / (BrE)
(especially in Singapore and Malaysia) a term referring to an expressive, sociable, or talkative older woman, often of Chinese descent.

Examples

The Ah Mm woman
My neighbor is quite the Ah Mm, always chatting with everyone in the block.
People often rely on the Ah Mm in our neighborhood for local news and gossip.
The community event was organized by a group of enthusiastic Ah Mms.
