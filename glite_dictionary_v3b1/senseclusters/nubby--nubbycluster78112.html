nubby

adjective
rough or knobbly-textured (especially fabric)
9.3 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌ.bi/ /; / /ˈnʌ.bɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnʌ.bi/ /; / /ˈnʌ.bɪ/ (BrE)
