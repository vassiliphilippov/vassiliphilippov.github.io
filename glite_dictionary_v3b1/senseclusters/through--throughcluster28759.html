through

adjective
finished; completed; done
6.4
Pronunciation: / θru / (AmE), / θruː / (BrE)
(predicative or after verb) Having finished or completed something; no longer involved with or affected by it.

Examples

He told his boss that he was through working overtime every weekend.
Once the repairs are through, the house will be as good as new.
She was finally through with her exams and could relax.
