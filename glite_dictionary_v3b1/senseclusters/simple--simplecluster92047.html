simple

adjective
mere; without additions or modifications
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl / (BrE)
used to emphasize that nothing more than what is mentioned is involved; mere, without additions or modifications

Examples

The simple truth
He told her the simple truth about what had happened.
Sometimes, the simple presence of a friend makes all the difference.
The simple fact is that we just don't have enough time.

  • synonym: mere - nothing more than; only
