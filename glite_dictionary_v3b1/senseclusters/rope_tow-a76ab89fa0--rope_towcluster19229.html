rope tow

noun
ski lift of a moving rope for towing
Pronunciation: / roʊp toʊ / (AmE), / rəʊp təʊ / (BrE)
a ski lift consisting of a continuously moving rope that skiers hold onto to be pulled uphill

Examples

A rope tow lift
After falling, she quickly got back in line for the rope tow to try again.
Holding on to the rope tow can be tiring, especially if the ride is long and steep.
The beginner slope has a rope tow that makes it easier for new skiers to reach the top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ski lift - a mechanical means of transporting skiers uphill
