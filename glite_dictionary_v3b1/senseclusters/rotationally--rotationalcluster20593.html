rotationally

adverb
in a rotational manner
Pronunciation: / roʊˈteɪʃənəli / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves rotation.

Examples

Move rotationally around
The object can move both linearly and rotationally under the applied forces.
The platform spins rotationally at a constant speed during the experiment.
The wheels must be aligned rotationally to ensure proper function.
