chooser

noun
person who chooses or selects
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃuzər / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːzə / (BrE)
a person who chooses or selects something from a group or range of options

Examples

Award chooser
As the appointed chooser, she picked the nominees for the award.
Every chooser in the survey had different criteria for their selections.
In the game, the chooser must decide between two difficult options.
