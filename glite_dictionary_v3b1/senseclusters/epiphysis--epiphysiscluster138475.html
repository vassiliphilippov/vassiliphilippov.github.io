epiphysis

noun
pineal gland in the brain
6.4 9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪfɪsɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪfɪsɪs / (BrE)
a small endocrine gland located in the brain beneath the back part of the corpus callosum that secretes melatonin; also known as the pineal gland
