Examples

One tonne-kilometer unit
A short journey of heavy cargo can amount to the same number of tonne-kilometers as a long haul of lighter goods.
By combining weight and distance, the tonne-kilometer offers a clear measure of transport efficiency.
The total freight transported last year was reported as 500 million tonne-kilometers.
