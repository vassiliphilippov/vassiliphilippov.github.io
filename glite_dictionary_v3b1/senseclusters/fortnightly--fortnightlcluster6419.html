fortnightly

adjective, adverb
every two weeks
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtˌnaɪtli /; / ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtˌnaɪtli /; / ˈfɔːtnaɪtli / (BrE)
happening or done every two weeks (can be used as an adjective or adverb)

Examples

A fortnightly paycheck
He receives a fortnightly paycheck from his employer.
Our team has fortnightly meetings to review the project’s progress.
The magazine is published fortnightly throughout the year.
He called his parents fortnightly during his university years.
Our club meetings are held fortnightly to keep everyone updated.
The magazine is published fortnightly and covers current events.
