yellow rocket

noun
noxious weed with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈrɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
A noxious weed with yellow flowers, often found in fields and roadsides; sometimes classified in the genus Sisymbrium.

Examples

The yellow rocket weed
Gardeners often remove yellow rocket to protect crops from its invasive growth.
The field was covered with yellow rocket after a rainy spring.
You can identify yellow rocket by its bright yellow blossoms and tall, slender stem.
