bill of goods

noun
a shipment of merchandise
5.2
Pronunciation: / bɪl əv ɡʊdz / (AmE), / bɪl əv ɡʊdz / (BrE)
a consignment or shipment of merchandise, often listed on an invoice or receipt

Examples

A bill of goods shipment

They discovered that some items were missing from the bill of goods when unpacking the shipment.
