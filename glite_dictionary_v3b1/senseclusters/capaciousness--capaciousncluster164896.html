capaciousness

noun
spaciousness; roominess
7.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈpeɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / kəˈpeɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Capaciousness of a room

We admired the capaciousness of the hotel lobby when we first arrived.
