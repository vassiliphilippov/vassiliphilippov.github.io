boid

noun
humorous 'bird'
Pronunciation: / bɔɪd / (AmE), / bɔɪd / (BrE)
a humorous or dialectal pronunciation of 'bird', often used to imitate certain accents in English.

Examples

Funny boid accent
He pointed to the sky and said, 'Look at dat boid!'
In old cartoons, characters would often say boid instead of 'bird' for comic effect.
She laughed when her uncle called the parrot a colorful boid.
