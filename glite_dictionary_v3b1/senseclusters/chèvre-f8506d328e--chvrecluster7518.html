chèvre

noun
goat's milk cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛvrə / (AmE), / ˈʃɛvrə / (BrE)
a type of cheese made from goat's milk, usually soft and tangy in flavor

Creamy chèvre cheese

The chef recommended pairing the fresh chèvre with a crisp white wine.
At the market, I bought a log of herbed chèvre for the appetizer platter.
