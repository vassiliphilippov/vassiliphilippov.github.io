Orphean

adjective
of Orphic tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrfiən / (AmE), / ˈɔːfiən / (BrE)
relating to Orphism or the Orphic tradition, especially its religious beliefs, rites, or literature from ancient Greece

Orphean rituals
