book

noun
financial record or account book
3.7 23/m
Pronunciation: / bÊŠk / (AmE), / bÊŠk / (BrE)
a set of records in which a business's financial transactions are systematically written or stored; an account book

Examples

Company books
According to the books, the company made a significant profit last year.
She handles the books for several small businesses in town.
The auditors carefully reviewed every book before preparing their report.

Synonyms

  • account book
    2.3 0.32/m
    an account book records and summarizes business transactions, while book is the general term for that type of financial record.
  • ledger
    5.0 2.91/m
    a ledger is the main formal record businesses use to track accounts, while book is a broader, less technical name.
  • book of account
    5.1 0.03/m
    a book of account is a formal or older phrase for business records, while book is the simple modern term used in everyday contexts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journal - a book in which transactions are recorded before being transferred to a ledger
  • synonym: ledger - a book or other collection of financial accounts
