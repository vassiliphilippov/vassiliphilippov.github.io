unconstraining

adjective
not restrictive
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
not limiting or restricting; allowing freedom or flexibility

Examples

Unconstraining environment
The teacher created an unconstraining environment that encouraged students to express their ideas freely.
Her unconstraining parenting style allowed the children to develop independence and creativity.
The company adopted unconstraining policies to foster innovation among its employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberating - making someone feel free and able to behave as they like
  • antonym: restrictive - limiting the freedom or movement of someone or something
