potentiator

noun
effect enhancer
8.0
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈten.ʃiː.eɪ.tər/ /; / /pə.ˈten.ʃɪ.eɪ.tər/ (AmE), /pə.ˈten.ʃiː.eɪ.tə/ /; / /pə.ˈten.ʃɪ.eɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a substance that increases or enhances the effect of another substance, especially in medicine or pharmacology

Examples

Drug potentiator
Caffeine can act as a potentiator for certain pain medications.
The doctor explained that grapefruit juice is a potentiator for some prescription drugs.
Researchers are studying new potentiators to improve the effectiveness of antibiotics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: synergist - a substance that works together with another to produce a greater effect
