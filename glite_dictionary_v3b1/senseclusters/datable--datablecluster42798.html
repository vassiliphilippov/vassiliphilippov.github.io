datable

adjective
able to be dated
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.tə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be assigned a date or determined as to its date of origin

Examples

Easily datable bones
Only well-preserved bones are easily datable using current scientific techniques.
The pottery shards were datable to the 3rd century BCE.
This document is one of the few datable artifacts from the period.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undatable - unable to be assigned a date
