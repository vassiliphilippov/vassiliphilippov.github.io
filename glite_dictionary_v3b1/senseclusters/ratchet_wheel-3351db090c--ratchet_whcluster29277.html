ratchet wheel

noun
toothed wheel used with a pawl
7.1 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrætʃət wiːl / (AmE), / ˈratʃɪt wiːl / (BrE)
a wheel with teeth that is held in place by a pawl or detent and is turned in one direction by a lever
