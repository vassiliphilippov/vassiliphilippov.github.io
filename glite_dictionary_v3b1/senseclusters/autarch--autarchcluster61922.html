autarch

noun
advocate of autarky
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɔːtɑːk / (BrE)
a person who supports or practices autarky, advocating for economic or national self-sufficiency.

Examples

Autarch of a nation
The new leader was an outspoken autarch who promoted national self-reliance.
As an autarch, she opposed all foreign trade agreements.
The party's autarch members argued for strict economic independence from other countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autarkist - a person who supports autarky
  • antonym: globalist - a person who supports global economic integration
