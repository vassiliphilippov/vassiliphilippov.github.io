blush wine

noun
pinkish table wine from red grapes
9.9
Pronunciation: / blʌʃ waɪn / (AmE), / blʌʃ waɪn / (BrE)
a light pink table wine made from red grapes, with the skins removed after fermentation has begun

Examples

A glass of blush wine
She ordered a glass of blush wine to go with her salad.
Some people prefer blush wine over red or white for its light, fruity flavor.
The vineyard is famous for its award-winning blush wine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rosé - a type of pink wine made with limited skin contact
