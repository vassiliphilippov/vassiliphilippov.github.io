service

noun
religious ceremony or act of public worship
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)

Examples

A church service
A special memorial service was held to honor the victims.
The choir sang beautifully during the Christmas Eve service.
The church holds a service every Sunday morning attended by dozens of parishioners.
