Synonyms

  • religious service
    2.8 0.75/m
    religious service is a public act of worship held in a church or temple, while service is the general word for such ceremonies.
  • divine service
    7.4 0.14/m
    divine service is a formal worship ceremony often led by clergy, while service is the broader term for public religious gatherings.
  • liturgy
    8.9 5.95/m
    liturgy is the fixed set of words and actions used in worship, while service is the full ceremony where that form is performed.
  • religious ceremony
    2.9 0.66/m
    religious ceremony is a formal rite like a wedding or funeral, while service usually refers to public worship under set rules.
