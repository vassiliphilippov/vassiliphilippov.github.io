Franco-American

noun
an American of French origin
Pronunciation: / ˌfræŋkoʊ əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌfræŋkəʊ əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
an American who was born in France or whose ancestors were French

Examples

Franco-American community
Many Franco-Americans in Louisiana have preserved their French language and traditions.
She is a proud Franco-American, tracing her roots back to Normandy.
The museum exhibit highlighted the contributions of Franco-Americans to American history.
