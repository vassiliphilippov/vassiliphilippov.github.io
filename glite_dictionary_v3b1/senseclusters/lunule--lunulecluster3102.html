lunule

noun
crescent-shaped area at fingernail base
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnjuːl / (AmE), / ˈljuːnjuːl / (BrE)
the small, crescent-shaped whitish area at the base of the human fingernail

Examples

The lunule of nail
Each nail typically has a lunule, although it can be more prominent on the thumbs.
He noticed that his lunule had changed color and consulted a doctor.
The white lunule at the base of her fingernail was clearly visible under the light.
