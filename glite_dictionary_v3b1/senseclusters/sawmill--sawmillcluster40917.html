sawmill

noun
factory where logs are cut into lumber
8.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɔː.mɪl/ (AmE), /ˈsɔː.mɪl/ (BrE)
a factory or building where logs or wood are cut into lumber using large saws

Examples

A sawmill factory
A new sawmill opened in town, creating dozens of new jobs for residents.
The old sawmill by the river has been running for over a century.
Workers loaded the logs onto trucks and drove them to the sawmill for processing.
After the old sawmill closed, the town lost many jobs.
Noise from the sawmill echoed throughout the valley every morning.
The logs were transported to the sawmill for processing into lumber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumber mill - a mill where logs are processed into lumber
  • synonym: mill - a building or machine where raw materials are processed, especially by grinding or cutting
  • degree higher: planing mill - a mill for smoothing lumber after it is sawn
