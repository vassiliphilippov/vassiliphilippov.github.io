mixed

adjective
consisting of various kinds; assorted
Pronunciation: / mɪkst / (AmE), / mɪkst / (BrE)
consisting of different kinds or elements, especially arranged in a random or varied way; composed of an assortment of types.

Examples

Mixed salad
She prepared a mixed bouquet containing roses, lilies, and daisies for the centerpiece.
The festival offered a mixed program of music, featuring jazz, rock, and classical performances.
The team is composed of a mixed group of experts from several different fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miscellaneous - consisting of many different kinds; mixed
  • antonym: uniform - all the same; not mixed or varied
