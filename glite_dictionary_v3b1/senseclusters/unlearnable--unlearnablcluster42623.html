unlearnable

adjective
impossible to forget
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɜːnəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to forget or remove from one's memory or knowledge

Examples

Unlearnable memory
The traumatic event left an unlearnable impression on his mind.
Some childhood lessons are so deeply rooted that they become unlearnable.
The melody was so hauntingly beautiful it felt almost unlearnable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgettable - easy to forget
  • synonym: unforgettable - impossible to forget
