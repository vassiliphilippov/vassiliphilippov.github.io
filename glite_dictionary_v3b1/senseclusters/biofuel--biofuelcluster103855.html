biofuel

adjective
from biological fuel
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌfjuːəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌfjʊəl / (BrE)
relating to, produced from, or using fuel derived from biological sources such as plants or waste.

Examples

Biofuel technology
The company invested in biofuel vehicles to reduce its carbon footprint.
Many airlines are testing biofuel alternatives to traditional jet fuel.
A biofuel plant was built near the city to process agricultural waste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fossil-fuel - relating to energy from coal, oil, or natural gas
  • synonym: renewable - relating to energy sources that can be replenished naturally
