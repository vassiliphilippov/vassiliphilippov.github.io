distil complex ideas

verb
simplify or extract main points from complex concepts
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪl kəmˈplɛks aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪl kəmˈplɛks aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
to simplify complicated concepts or extract the main idea or meaning from them

Examples

To distil complex ideas clearly
Good teachers know how to distil complex ideas into language their students can grasp.

In academic writing, it's crucial to distil complex ideas into concise arguments.
