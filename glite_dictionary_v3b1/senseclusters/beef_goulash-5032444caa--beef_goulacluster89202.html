beef goulash

noun
a paprika-flavored beef stew
4.0
Pronunciation: / bif ɡuˈlɑʃ / (AmE), / biːf ˈɡuːlæʃ / (BrE)
A stew made with beef that is browned before being slowly cooked, typically flavored with paprika and other seasonings, often associated with Central European cuisine.

Examples

A beef goulash stew
After a long hike, we were served a hearty beef goulash with dumplings at the mountain inn.
My grandmother’s recipe for beef goulash always includes plenty of paprika and fresh herbs.
You can prepare beef goulash in advance and reheat it for an even richer flavor the next day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goulash - a rich stew of meat and vegetables, seasoned with paprika
