bounty

noun
reward or payment, often by a government
3.0 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnti / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnti / (BrE)
a payment or reward given for doing something, such as capturing a criminal or reporting a problem

Examples

Bounty on a criminal

The company offered a generous bounty to anyone who found and reported critical security bugs.
Independent researchers earned a small bounty after responsible disclosure of the website vulnerability.
Many startups run public bounty programs to encourage reporting before attackers exploit flaws.
