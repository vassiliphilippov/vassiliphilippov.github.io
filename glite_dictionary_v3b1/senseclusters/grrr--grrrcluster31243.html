grrr

noun
animal growl sound
Pronunciation: / ɡɜr / (AmE), / ɡɜː / (BrE)
a low, guttural growling sound, especially made by an animal, or the written representation of that sound

Examples

Dog's grrr sound
The dog let out a warning grrr when the stranger approached the gate.
She ended her message with a loud grrr to show her frustration.
A low grrr came from the cat hiding under the couch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: growl - a low, threatening sound made by animals or people
  • degree higher: roar - a loud, deep sound made by a wild animal
