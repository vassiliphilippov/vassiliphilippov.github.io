rinser

noun
device or person rinsing
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪnsər / (AmE), / ˈrɪnsə / (BrE)
a device or person that rinses something with water or another liquid to remove dirt or residue

Examples

A rinser device
The bartender used a rinser to quickly clean the glasses between customers.
She bought a new rinser for her kitchen to make washing vegetables easier.
At the car wash, a powerful rinser sprayed water over the vehicles before the soap was applied.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleaner - a person or device that cleans
  • synonym: washer - a device or person that washes something
