contrecoup

adjective
opposite-side injury
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.trə.kuː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.trə.kuː/ (BrE)
located on or relating to the side of an organ, especially the brain, opposite to where a blow or injury occurred

The MRI revealed a contrecoup lesion on the right side of the brain.
