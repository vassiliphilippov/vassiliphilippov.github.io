Ga

noun
chemical symbol for gallium (element)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæliəm / (AmE), / ˈɡalɪəm / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for gallium, a rare silvery metallic element that is brittle at low temperatures but liquid above room temperature; occurs in trace amounts in bauxite and zinc ores
