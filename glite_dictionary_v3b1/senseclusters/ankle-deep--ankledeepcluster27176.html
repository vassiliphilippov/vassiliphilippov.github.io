ankle-deep

adjective
reaching the height of the ankles
8.4 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæŋ.kəl.diːp/ (AmE), /ˈæŋ.kəl.diːp/ (BrE)
reaching up to the ankles in depth or height, especially of water, mud, or similar substances

Examples

Ankle-deep water
Mud was ankle-deep on the path after last night's storm.
She trudged through ankle-deep snow to reach her front door.
The children splashed in the ankle-deep water at the edge of the lake.
