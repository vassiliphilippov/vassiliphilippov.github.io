langoustine

noun
edible shellfish similar to a small lobster
7.0
Pronunciation: / læŋˈɡuːstiːn / (AmE), / læŋˈɡuːstiːn / (BrE)
A large edible prawn or shellfish resembling a small lobster, found in European waters and valued as seafood.
