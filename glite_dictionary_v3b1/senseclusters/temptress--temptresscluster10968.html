temptress

noun
seductively alluring woman
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛm.trɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɛmp.trɪs / (BrE)
a woman who is considered dangerously seductive, often using her charm to entice or manipulate others

Examples

A temptress charms men
He warned his friend about the temptress who had charmed half the town.
Her portrayal of the temptress was both intriguing and unsettling to the audience.
The novel's main antagonist is a mysterious temptress who manipulates wealthy men.
