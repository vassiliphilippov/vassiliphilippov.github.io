syndrome

noun
group of symptoms indicating a disease
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.droʊm / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.drəʊm / (BrE)
a group or pattern of symptoms that consistently occur together, indicating a particular disease or condition

Examples

Down syndrome
Down syndrome is one of the most well-known genetic disorders affecting humans.
The doctor diagnosed her with a rare syndrome after observing a combination of unusual symptoms.
The new illness shares many symptoms with an existing syndrome but also displays unique characteristics.
