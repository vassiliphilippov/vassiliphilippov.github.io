AFB

noun
acid-fast bacterium
8.3
Pronunciation: /eɪ ef biː/ (AmE), /eɪ ef biː/ (BrE)
an abbreviation for acid-fast bacillus or acid-fast bacilli, a type of bacterium used in diagnosing tuberculosis and similar infections

Examples

AFB test result
The doctor ordered an AFB test to check for tuberculosis in the patient's sputum.
A positive AFB smear indicates the presence of acid-fast bacteria in the sample.
Laboratories often use special stains to identify AFB in clinical specimens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mycobacterium tuberculosis - the bacterium that causes tuberculosis
  • synonym: bacterium - a microscopic single-celled organism
