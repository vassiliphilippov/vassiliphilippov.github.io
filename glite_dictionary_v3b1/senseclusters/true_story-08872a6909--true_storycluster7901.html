true story

noun
an account of real events
1.0
Pronunciation: / tru ˈstɔri / (AmE), / truː ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an account of events that actually happened, not fictional or made up

Examples

A true story told
She swears her ghost encounter is a true story.
The book tells a true story about survival in the wilderness.
The documentary features a true story of bravery during the war.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fiction - a literary work based on imagination rather than fact
