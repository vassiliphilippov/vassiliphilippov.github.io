nonpredatory

adjective
not exploitative
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈprɛdəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈprɛdətri / (BrE)
not intended to exploit, harm, or take unfair advantage of others; not aggressive or exploitative

Examples

Nonpredatory business practices
The company is known for its nonpredatory business practices and transparent pricing.
They chose a nonpredatory lender who offered clear terms and reasonable interest rates.
A nonpredatory approach helps build trust with customers and long-term relationships.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exploitative - taking unfair advantage of others for personal gain
  • synonym: fair - treating people equally and not taking advantage
  • antonym: predatory - seeking to exploit or harm others for personal gain
