Moghul

proper noun
Indian Muslim dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊɡʊl / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡʊl / (BrE)
a member of the Muslim dynasty that ruled large parts of India from the 16th to 18th centuries

Examples

Moghul Empire
The Moghul Empire was known for its impressive architecture and cultural achievements.
Akbar was one of the most famous Moghul rulers in Indian history.
Many palaces and gardens built by the Moghul dynasty still attract tourists today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mughal - alternative spelling for the dynasty that ruled much of India
  • synonym: emperor - a sovereign ruler of great power and rank
