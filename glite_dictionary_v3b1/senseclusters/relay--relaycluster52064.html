Synonyms

  • pass along
    2.5 3/m
    pass along is used when people pass information through several others, while relay means receive then pass it on directly.
  • send on
    3.2 0.6/m
    send on means forwarding something from an intermediate point to its destination, while relay stresses receiving then passing it on.
  • pass on
    2.7 6/m
    pass on means give or communicate something to another person generally, while relay specifically involves receiving first and then passing it on.
  • convey
    3.4 20/m
    convey means communicate or express an idea or feeling clearly, while relay focuses on receiving a message then passing it on.
  • pass
    1.0 30/m
    pass means hand or transfer something directly between people, while relay implies receiving and then passing the information along through others.
