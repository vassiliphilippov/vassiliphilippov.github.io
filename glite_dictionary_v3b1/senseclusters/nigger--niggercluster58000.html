nigger

noun
extremely offensive racial slur for a Black person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory term for a Black person. It is a racial slur that should never be used.

Examples

The nigger slur
He was deeply hurt by being called a nigger at school.
Literature from the past sometimes contains the word nigger, reflecting historical racism.
The use of the word nigger is universally condemned as hate speech.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Black person - neutral, respectful term for someone of African descent
  • synonym: nigga - variant form of the same racial slur, used in African American Vernacular English with different connotations
