send away

verb
dispatch or mail something
4.7
Pronunciation: / sɛnd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / sɛnd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to dispatch or mail something to a particular destination

Examples

To send away a package
After sorting the books, we sent away several old editions to charity.
She quickly sends away her packages every Monday morning.
You can send away the application form by post if you prefer.
