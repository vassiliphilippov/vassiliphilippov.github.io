bitter

verb
make or become bitter
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪt̬ɚ /; / ˈbɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə / (BrE)
to make or become bitter in taste or feeling

Examples

To bitter the taste
The addition of lemon juice bittered the entire dish.
Too much hops can bitter the flavor of the beer.
Years of disappointment had bittered his personality.
They were bittering the pickles with crushed walnut leaves before sealing the jars.
The harsh criticism ended up bittering her against her former colleagues.
Some brewers use hops for bittering the beer and balancing its flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embitter - to make someone feel bitter or resentful
  • antonym: sweeten - to make sweet or sweeter
