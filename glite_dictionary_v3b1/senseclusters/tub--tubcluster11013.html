tub

noun
large open container for liquids
Pronunciation: / tʌb / (AmE), / tʌb / (BrE)
a large, open, usually cylindrical container used for holding or storing liquids or other substances

Examples

A metal tub
A metal tub was placed under the leak to catch the drips.
He filled the tub with water to soak the towels.
They stored the syrup in a large wooden tub in the cellar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrel - a large round container, usually bulging out in the middle, traditionally made of wood
  • degree lower: bucket - an open container with a handle, used for carrying liquids
