wiring

noun
neural connections in the brain
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪ.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪ.ə.rɪŋ/ /; / /ˈwaɪ.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the network of neural connections (neurons and synapses) in the brain or nervous system that underlies brain function and behaviour.

Examples

Brain wiring
Researchers mapped the wiring of the hippocampus to understand memory formation.
Early childhood experiences can reshape the brain's wiring, affecting later learning and emotion.
The MRI study revealed abnormal wiring between attention-related regions in several patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brain circuitry - the arrangement and connections of neural circuits within the brain.
  • synonym: neuronal connectivity - the patterns of connections between neurons that determine how brain regions communicate.
