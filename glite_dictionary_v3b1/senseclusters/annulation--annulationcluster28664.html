annulation

noun
formal cancellation
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.jəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæn.jʊˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of annulling or making something legally or formally void; an annulment or cancellation

Examples

Legal annulation
The court ordered the annulation of the contract due to fraud.
After the discovery of errors, the committee discussed the possible annulation of the results.
The annulation of the agreement left both parties free from their obligations.
