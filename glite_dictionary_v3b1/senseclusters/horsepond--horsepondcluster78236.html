horsepond

noun
pond for watering horses
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌpɑnd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːspɒnd / (BrE)
a pond constructed or used for watering horses, typically found on farms or near stables

Examples

A horsepond on farm
A willow tree stood beside the horsepond, offering shade to the animals.
During the summer, horses would cool off at the horsepond.
The farm still has an old horsepond near the stables.
