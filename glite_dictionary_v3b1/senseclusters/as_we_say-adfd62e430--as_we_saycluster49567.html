as we say

adverb
used to introduce a conventional or figurative phrase
5.4 4.6
Pronunciation: /əz wiː seɪ/ /; / /æz wiː seɪ/ (AmE), /əz wiː seɪ/ /; / /æz wiː seɪ/ (BrE)
used to indicate that a word or expression is being used in a conventional or figurative way; in a manner of speaking.

Examples
