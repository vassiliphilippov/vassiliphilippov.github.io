rooty

adjective
full of roots
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈruːti / (AmE), / ˈruːti / (BrE)

Examples

Rooty ground
The forest floor was so rooty that walking became difficult.
Gardeners often struggle with rooty soil when planting new flowers.
We tripped several times on the rooty path through the woods.
