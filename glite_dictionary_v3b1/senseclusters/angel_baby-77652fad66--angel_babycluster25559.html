angel baby

noun
deceased infant (memorial term)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl ˈbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl ˈbeɪbi / (BrE)
A baby who has died; a gentle, consoling term used in memorials or when speaking about an infant’s death.

Examples

Remembering an angel baby
At the memorial, friends placed flowers beside a tiny photograph of their angel baby.
She kept a small blanket that once wrapped her angel baby on the shelf.
Each year they light a candle on the day they lost their angel baby.
