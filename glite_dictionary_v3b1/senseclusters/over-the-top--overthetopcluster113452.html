Synonyms

  • excessive
    3.6 20/m
    excessive means more than is reasonable in amount or degree, while over-the-top suggests extreme, dramatic exaggeration.
  • overblown
    5.7 2.05/m
    overblown feels pompous and exaggerated, while over-the-top is more everyday and openly extreme in style or behavior.
  • overdone
    3.9 0.00/m
    overdone is exaggerated from trying too hard, while over-the-top is broadly extreme and often showy in a clear way.
  • outrageous
    4.2 4.46/m
    outrageous shocks or offends because it breaks rules, while over-the-top simply means extreme or excessively dramatic.
