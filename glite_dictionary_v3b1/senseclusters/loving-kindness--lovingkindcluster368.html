loving-kindness

noun
tender kindness motivated by affection
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvɪŋ ˌkaɪndnəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌvɪŋ ˌkaɪndnɪs / (BrE)
tender kindness or goodwill motivated by affection or love for others

Examples

Acts of loving-kindness
Acts of loving-kindness can greatly impact those who are suffering or in need.
She showed loving-kindness to the injured animal, caring for it until it recovered.
The monk embodied loving-kindness toward everyone he met, regardless of their background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings of others
  • antonym: cruelty - callous indifference to or pleasure in causing pain or suffering
