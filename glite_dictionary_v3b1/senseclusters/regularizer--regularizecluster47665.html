regularizer

noun
tool to prevent overfitting
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊləˌaɪzə / (BrE)
a method or tool used to prevent overfitting in mathematical or machine learning models by adding constraints or penalties

Examples

L2 regularizer
The regularizer improved the accuracy of the machine learning algorithm.
Adding a regularizer can help your model generalize better to new data.
Different types of regularizer are used depending on the problem and data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: penalty - a term added to a model's loss function to discourage complexity
  • synonym: regularization - the process of adding constraints to a model to prevent overfitting
