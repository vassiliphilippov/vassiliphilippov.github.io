Odyssean

adjective
epic or adventurous
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊdɪˈsiːən / (AmE), / ˌɒdɪˈsiːən / (BrE)
relating to long, adventurous journeys or to Odysseus from Greek mythology

Examples

Odyssean journey
Their Odyssean quest for knowledge led them to remote corners of the world.
The novel tells an Odyssean tale of survival and discovery.
He faced Odyssean challenges on his journey home after the war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try out new experiences
  • synonym: epic - grand or heroic in scale or character
