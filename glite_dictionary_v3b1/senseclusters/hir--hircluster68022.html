hir

pronoun
gender-neutral possessive
Pronunciation: / hɪr / (AmE), / hɪə / (BrE)
a gender-neutral possessive pronoun used without a following noun to indicate that something belongs to someone, instead of 'his' or 'hers'

Examples

The book is hir
That book is hir, so please return it to the correct person.
I borrowed a pen from Alex, and now I need to give it back to hir.
The teacher handed each student hir graded assignment at the end of class.
