murrine

noun
glass pattern technique
Pronunciation: / muˈriːneɪ /; / muˈriːni / (AmE), / mʊˈriːneɪ /; / mjuˈriːneɪ / (BrE)
a small, patterned piece of colored glass made by fusing, stretching, and slicing glass rods; also, the technique for making such pieces, used in decorative glasswork
