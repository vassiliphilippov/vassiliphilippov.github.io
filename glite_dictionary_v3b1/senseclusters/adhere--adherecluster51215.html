adhere

verb
stick firmly to a surface
6.7 4.2
Pronunciation: /əd.ˈhɪr/ /; / /æd.ˈhɪr/ (AmE), /əd.ˈhɪə/ /; / /æd.ˈhɪə/ (BrE)
to stick firmly to a surface or substance

Examples

Adhere to the surface
Make sure the label will adhere to the glass jar before storing it.
The tape didn't adhere properly and started to peel off.
These decals are designed to adhere to smooth surfaces such as metal or plastic.
Glue helps the wallpaper adhere to the wall for many years.
The paint will only last if it adheres properly to the surface.
The sticker adhered so well that it was hard to remove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attach - to fasten or join one thing to another
  • synonym: cling - stick tightly or firmly to something
  • synonym: cohere - stick together, especially as parts of the same substance
  • antonym: detach - to separate or unfasten something from something else
  • antonym: loosen - to make less firmly attached; to release from being tight or stuck
