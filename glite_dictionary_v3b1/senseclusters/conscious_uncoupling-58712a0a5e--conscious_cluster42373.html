conscious uncoupling

noun
amicable, deliberate end to a relationship
7.1 8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.ʃəs ʌnˈkʌp.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəs ʌnˈkʌp.lɪŋ / (BrE)
