inner

noun
inside lining
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnər / (AmE), / ˈɪnə / (BrE)
the inside part or lining of something, especially the inner layer of clothing or a tire

Examples

Jacket inner
The tailor replaced the worn inner of the jacket to make it more comfortable.
After the puncture, the cyclist had to fix the inner of his tire before continuing.
She checked the inner of her coat for any hidden pockets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lining - a layer of material covering the inside surface of something
  • antonym: outer - the outside part or surface of something
