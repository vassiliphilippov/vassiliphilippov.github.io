uncallused

adjective
without calluses
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæl.əst / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæl.juːst / (BrE)
not having calluses; smooth and free from areas of hardened skin

Examples

Uncallused hands
Her uncallused feet made it difficult to walk long distances without pain.
The uncallused hands of the new gardener showed he was not used to hard work.
After months of playing guitar, his fingers were no longer uncallused.

Related Terms

  • antonym: callused - having areas of hardened or thickened skin
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough
