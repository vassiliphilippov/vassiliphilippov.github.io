blood pudding

noun
sausage made from pig's blood
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a type of dark sausage typically made from pig's blood, fat, and oatmeal or other grain, often served cooked or fried

Examples

A blood pudding sausage
