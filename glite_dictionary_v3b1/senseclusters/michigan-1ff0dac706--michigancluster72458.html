Michigan

noun
gambling card game using sequences and chips
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃɪɡən / (BrE)
A gambling card game in which chips are placed on certain cards, and players seek to play in sequence to win chips placed on corresponding cards.

Examples

Michigan card game
Every Friday night, the family would gather to play Michigan around the dining room table.
She learned the rules of Michigan from her grandfather, who had played the game since childhood.
With a lucky play, he managed to win all the chips on the king during their game of Michigan.
