shopfront

noun
front of a shop with display windows
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
the front of a shop or store, typically containing windows to display goods to people passing by in the street

Examples

The shopfront window
After the renovations, the shopfront looked modern and inviting to customers.
The new bakery's shopfront is decorated with colorful pastries and elegant lettering.
They replaced the old wooden panels on their shopfront with large glass windows.
