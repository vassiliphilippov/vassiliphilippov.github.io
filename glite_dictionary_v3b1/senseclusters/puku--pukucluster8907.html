puku

noun
a medium-sized African antelope
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːkuː / (AmE), / ˈpuːkuː / (BrE)
a medium-sized African antelope (Kobus vardonii) found in wet grasslands, closely related to the waterbuck

Examples

The puku antelope
The puku is often seen grazing near rivers and wetlands in southern Africa.
Unlike the waterbuck, the puku prefers open floodplains and grassy marshes.
We spotted a herd of pukus during our safari in Zambia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterbuck - a large antelope found in sub-Saharan Africa
