dauntlessness

noun
fearless courage; resolve
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔnt.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdɔːnt.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
fearless determination and courage, especially in the face of difficulty or danger

Examples

The dauntlessness of leaders
Historians often write about the dauntlessness displayed by leaders during times of war.
In the face of overwhelming odds, her dauntlessness never wavered.
The dauntlessness of the mountain climbers inspired everyone who heard their story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courage - the ability to confront fear or adversity
  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence
