cayo

noun
small Caribbean island
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈkaɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
a small, low island, especially found in the Caribbean Sea, often formed on coral reefs.

Examples

A tropical cayo
The boat anchored near a remote cayo where we spent the afternoon swimming.
Many tourists visit the cayos of Cuba for their clear waters and sandy beaches.
Fishermen often stop at a cayo to rest before returning to the mainland.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: island - a piece of land surrounded by water
  • synonym: islet - a very small island
  • synonym: key - a small, low island, especially in the Caribbean or Florida
