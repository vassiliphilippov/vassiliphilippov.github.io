time-switch

noun
switch that operates at a set time
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmˌswɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌswɪtʃ / (BrE)
an electrical switch programmed to turn a device on or off at a set time

Examples

A time-switch device
After installing a time-switch, the heating system turns on before we get home.
The lights in the hallway are controlled by a time-switch for energy efficiency.
We set the time-switch to water the garden every morning at 6 a.m.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timer - a device that automatically measures time or controls a process for a set period
