Captain Bligh

proper noun
British naval captain of the Bounty mutiny
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.tən blaɪ/ /; / /ˈkæp.tɪn blaɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.tɪn blaɪ/ /; / /ˈkæp.tən blaɪ/ (BrE)
