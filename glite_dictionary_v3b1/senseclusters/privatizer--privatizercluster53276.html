privatizer

noun
supporter of privatization
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpraɪ.və.taɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈpraɪ.və.taɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person or organization that supports or carries out the transfer of public assets or services to private ownership or control

Examples
