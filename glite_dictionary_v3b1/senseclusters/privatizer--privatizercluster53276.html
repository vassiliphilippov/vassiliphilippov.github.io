privatizer

noun
supporter of privatization
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvəˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈprɪvətaɪzə /; / ˈpraɪvətaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or organization that supports or carries out the transfer of public assets or services to private ownership or control

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberalizer - a person who supports making laws or rules less strict, especially in economics
  • antonym: nationalizer - a person who supports transferring private assets to state ownership
  • synonym: privatize - to make something private or transfer from public to private control
