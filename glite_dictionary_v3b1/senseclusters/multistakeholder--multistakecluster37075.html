multistakeholder

noun
group with many stakeholders
7.5
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.ˈsteɪk.hoʊl.dər/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tɪ.steɪk.hoʊl.dər/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.ˈsteɪk.həʊl.də/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tɪ.steɪk.həʊl.də/ (BrE)
a group, forum, or process involving representatives from multiple stakeholder groups who jointly discuss or make decisions

Examples

Multistakeholder forum
The new policy was developed by a multistakeholder including government, business, and civil society representatives.
A multistakeholder met to address the challenges of internet governance.
Decisions made by a multistakeholder are often more widely accepted by different groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
  • synonym: panel - a group of people gathered to discuss or decide on a topic
