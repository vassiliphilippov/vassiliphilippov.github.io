boogum

noun
imaginary monster
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːɡəm / (AmE), / ˈbuːɡəm / (BrE)
a playful or nonsense word for a ghost, monster, or imaginary creature, often used with children

Examples

A scary boogum
The children screamed and ran when they thought a boogum was hiding in the closet.
At bedtime, her father joked that a friendly boogum lived under the stairs.
He made a silly face and pretended to be a boogum to make his little brother laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boogeyman - an imaginary evil creature used to frighten children
  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often imagined as frightening
  • synonym: monster - a large, frightening imaginary creature
