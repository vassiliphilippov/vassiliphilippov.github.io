downstate

adverb
toward southern region
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪt / (BrE)
toward or into the southern or less urban part of a state

Examples

Drive downstate
After finishing their meeting, they decided to drive downstate for the weekend.
Many students travel downstate to visit family during the holidays.
The company is opening a new branch downstate to reach more rural customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upstate - toward or in the northern or more rural part of a state
