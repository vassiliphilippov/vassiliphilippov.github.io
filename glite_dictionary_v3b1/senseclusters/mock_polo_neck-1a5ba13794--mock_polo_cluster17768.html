mock polo neck

noun
sweater with a short, unfolder turtleneck collar
Pronunciation: / mɑk ˈpoʊloʊ nɛk / (AmE), / mɒk ˈpəʊləʊ nɛk / (BrE)
A sweater or shirt with a short, close-fitting collar that stands up but does not fold over like a traditional polo neck or turtleneck.

Examples

A mock polo neck sweater
A mock polo neck is perfect if you want the turtleneck style without the bulk of a fold-over collar.
Many fashion brands offer sweaters with a mock polo neck for a stylish look that's not too warm.
She wore a comfortable mock polo neck under her jacket on the chilly evening.
