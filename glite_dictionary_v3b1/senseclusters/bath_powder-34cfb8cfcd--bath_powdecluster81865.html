bath powder

noun
powder for use after bathing
3.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /bæθ ˈpaʊ.dər/ (AmE), /bɑːθ ˈpaʊ.də/ (BrE)
a fine powder applied to the body, especially after bathing, to absorb moisture and impart fragrance

Examples

Bath powder on skin
After her shower, she sprinkled bath powder on her skin to stay fresh.
Some people use bath powder to prevent chafing in hot weather.
The fragrance of lavender from his mother's bath powder filled the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body powder - powder applied to the body for dryness and fragrance
  • synonym: talcum powder - powder made from talc, often used as bath powder
