nondiscretionary

adjective
required; automatic
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːndɪˈskrɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈskrɛʃənəri / (BrE)
not subject to personal choice or judgment; required or automatic by rule or law

Examples

Nondiscretionary government spending
Social Security payments are considered nondiscretionary government spending.
The company has several nondiscretionary expenses that must be paid every month.
Rent is a nondiscretionary cost that cannot be avoided.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsory - required; not optional
  • antonym: discretionary - left to personal choice or judgment
  • synonym: mandatory - required by law or rules
