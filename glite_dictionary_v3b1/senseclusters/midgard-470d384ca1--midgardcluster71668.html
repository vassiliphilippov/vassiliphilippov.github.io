Midgard

noun
the human world in Norse mythology
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌɡɑːd / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the world inhabited by humans, regarded as the middle realm among the nine worlds.

Examples

Midgard world
According to the legends, humans dwell in Midgard, while gods inhabit other realms.
In Norse mythology, Midgard is connected to Asgard by the rainbow bridge Bifrost.
The ancient Vikings believed that Midgard was surrounded by a vast ocean.
