bail

verb
remove water from a boat with a container
Pronunciation: / beɪl / (AmE), / beɪl / (BrE)

Examples

Every sailor took turns bailing during the heavy rain to keep the boat afloat.
She bailed the dinghy all morning after last night's storm.
When water started leaking into the canoe, we had to bail as quickly as possible.
