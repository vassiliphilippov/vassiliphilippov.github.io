goggly

noun
cricket spin delivery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑɡli / (AmE), / ˈɡɒɡli / (BrE)
in cricket, a ball bowled by a leg-spinner that spins in the opposite direction to a normal leg break, deceiving the batsman

Examples

A goggly ball
The bowler delivered a perfect goggly that completely fooled the batsman.
It takes years of practice to master the art of bowling a goggly in cricket.
The unexpected goggly led to the batsman's dismissal in the final over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: googly - alternative spelling of goggly; a deceptive cricket delivery
  • antonym: leg break - a type of spin bowling in cricket that turns from leg to off side
