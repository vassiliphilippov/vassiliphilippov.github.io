metapolitics

noun
political cultural strategy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈpɑlətɪks / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈpɒlɪtɪks / (BrE)
the study or practice of influencing culture, values, or ideas to achieve long-term political change outside formal institutions or policies

Examples
