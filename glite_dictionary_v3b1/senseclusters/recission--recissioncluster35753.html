recission

noun
legal cancellation of a contract
6.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
(law) the act of rescinding; cancellation of a contract, restoring parties to their pre-contract positions.

Examples

Mutual recission of contract
A mutual recission allowed both parties to walk away without penalty.
The company sought recission of the agreement after discovering fraud.
The court granted recission because the contract was entered into under duress.
