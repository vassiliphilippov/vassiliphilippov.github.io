maple-leaved bayur

noun
Indian ornamental tree with fragrant white flowers
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪpəl liːvd bəˈjʊr / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəl liːvd bəˈjʊə / (BrE)
An Indian tree with fragrant white flowers that bloom at night and reddish wood used for planking; often cultivated as an ornamental or shade tree.
