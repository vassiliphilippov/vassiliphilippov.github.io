ceiling

noun
maximum permitted limit
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an upper limit, especially on an amount or a number that is allowed

Examples

Ceiling on prices
A ceiling on credit card interest rates was introduced to protect consumers.
The company imposed a ceiling on pay raises this year.
There is a strict ceiling on the number of students admitted each term.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cap - an upper limit on an amount or value
  • antonym: floor - a lower limit, especially on an amount or number
