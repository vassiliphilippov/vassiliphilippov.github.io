improver

noun
additive or component that improves something
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpruvər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpruːvə / (BrE)
an ingredient or additive introduced to enhance the quality or performance of a product or process

Examples

Bread improver
Many commercial breads contain a flour improver to create a lighter texture.
The bakery switched to a natural dough improver to enhance the bread's flavor.
They added a fuel improver to increase the engine's efficiency.
