prudishness

noun
excessive or affected modesty
8.8 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpruː.dɪʃ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈpruː.dɪʃ.nəs/ (BrE)
The quality of being easily shocked by matters relating to sex or nudity; excessive or affected modesty.

Examples

Excessive prudishness
Despite accusations of prudishness, she insisted on maintaining certain boundaries in conversation.
He criticized the school's atmosphere of prudishness when it came to discussing relationships.
Her prudishness made her blush at even the mildest romantic scenes on television.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liberality - an openness of mind and absence of strictness about morals
  • degree lower: modesty - the quality of being modest; not being boastful or showing off, especially about one's body or abilities
  • synonym: prudery - the behavior or attitude of a prude, especially excessive propriety or modesty
