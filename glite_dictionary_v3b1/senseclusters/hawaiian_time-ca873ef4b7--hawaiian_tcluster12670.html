Hawaiian time

noun
relaxed attitude toward punctuality or time
Pronunciation: / həˈwaɪ.ən taɪm / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.ən taɪm / (BrE)
an informal, relaxed attitude toward punctuality and scheduling, characterized by a slower pace of life, especially as associated with Hawaiian culture

Examples

Relaxed Hawaiian time
Don't worry if the meeting starts late; it's just Hawaiian time.
Since moving to Maui, I've learned to appreciate Hawaiian time and enjoy a slower pace of life.
When you visit the islands, you'll notice that everyone seems to operate on Hawaiian time.
