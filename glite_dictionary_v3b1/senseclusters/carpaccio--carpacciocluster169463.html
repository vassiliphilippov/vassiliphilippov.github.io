carpaccio

noun
thin raw meat dish
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈpɑtʃioʊ / (AmE), / kɑːˈpatʃiəʊ / (BrE)
a dish of thinly sliced raw meat or fish, usually served as an appetizer with a dressing or garnish

Examples

Thin carpaccio slices
For the appetizer, we ordered carpaccio topped with arugula and parmesan cheese.
The chef prepared a salmon carpaccio with a light lemon dressing.
Many Italian restaurants serve beef carpaccio as a classic starter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sashimi - a Japanese dish of thinly sliced raw fish
  • synonym: tartare - a dish of raw ground meat or fish, often seasoned
