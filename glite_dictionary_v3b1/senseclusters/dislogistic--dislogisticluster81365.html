dislogistic

adjective
expressing disapproval; derogatory
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈlɑːdʒɪstɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈlɒdʒɪstɪk / (BrE)
(of a word or expression) expressing disapproval or disparagement; derogatory.

Examples

A dislogistic word
