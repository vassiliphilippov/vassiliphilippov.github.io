flopper

noun
complete failure
8.2 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfl…ëÀêp…ôr / (AmE), / Ààfl…íp…ô / (BrE)
an informal term for a person, event, or thing that is a complete failure

Examples

A movie flopper

Despite high expectations, the new product turned out to be a flopper in the market.
He was labeled a flopper after his business closed just months after opening.
