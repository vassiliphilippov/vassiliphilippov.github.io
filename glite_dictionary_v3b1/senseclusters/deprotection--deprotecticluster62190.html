deprotection

noun
removal of protection
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːprəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːprəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of removing a protective group or restriction, especially in chemical synthesis

Examples

Chemical deprotection step
The final step in the synthesis required deprotection of the amine group.
Careful deprotection is essential to avoid damaging the target molecule.
After deprotection, the chemist analyzed the purity of the compound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protection - the process of adding a protective group or restriction
  • synonym: removal - the act of taking something away
