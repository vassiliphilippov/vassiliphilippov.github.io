zucchetto

noun
clergy skullcap
9.9
Pronunciation: / zuˈkɛtoʊ / (AmE), / tsuˈkɛtto / (BrE)
a small, round skullcap worn by Catholic clergy, especially bishops and the pope

Examples

A zucchetto cap
The cardinal placed his red zucchetto on his head before entering the cathedral.
During the ceremony, the pope's white zucchetto was clearly visible to the crowd.
He explained the meaning of the zucchetto to the visiting students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kippah - a brimless cap worn by Jewish men
  • synonym: skullcap - a small, close-fitting cap covering the top of the head
