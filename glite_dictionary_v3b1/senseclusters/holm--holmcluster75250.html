holm

noun
small river island
Pronunciation: / hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
a small island, especially one found in a river or near a larger island

Examples

A holm island
The ancient church stands on a holm in the middle of the river.
During the flood, the cattle were moved to the holm for safety.
Birds nest every spring on the grassy holm near the main island.

Related Terms

  • synonym: islet - a very small island
