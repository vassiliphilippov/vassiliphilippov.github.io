regathering

noun
coming together again
Pronunciation: / riˈɡæðərɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of coming together again after being separated or apart

Examples

Family regathering
After months apart, the regathering of the family was a joyful occasion.
The regathering of the community took place in the town square after the storm.
Everyone looked forward to the annual regathering of old friends at the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reunion - a meeting of people who have been apart
  • antonym: separation - the state of being apart from others
