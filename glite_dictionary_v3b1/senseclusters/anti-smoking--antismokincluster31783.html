anti-smoking

adjective
against smoking
3.6
Pronunciation: /æn.tiː.ˈsmoʊ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.tiː ˈsmoʊ.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /æn.tiː.ˈsməʊ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.tiː ˈsməʊ.kɪŋ/ (BrE)

Examples
