anti-smoking

adjective
against smoking
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsmoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsməʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
opposed to or intended to prevent the use of tobacco products, especially smoking

Examples

Anti-smoking campaign
The government launched an anti-smoking campaign to reduce tobacco use among teenagers.
Many restaurants have adopted anti-smoking policies to create a healthier environment.
She supports anti-smoking laws to protect people from secondhand smoke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-smoking - supporting or promoting the use of tobacco products
  • synonym: smoke-free - not allowing smoking; free from tobacco smoke
