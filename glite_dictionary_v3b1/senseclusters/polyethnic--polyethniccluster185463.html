polyethnic

adjective
involving many ethnic groups
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈɛθnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɛθnɪk / (BrE)
involving or relating to several different ethnic groups

Examples

Polyethnic community
The city is famous for its polyethnic neighborhoods, where many cultures live side by side.
A polyethnic society often celebrates a wide variety of cultural festivals throughout the year.
The school promotes a polyethnic environment by encouraging students from different backgrounds to share their traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monoethnic - involving or relating to only one ethnic group
  • synonym: multiethnic - involving or relating to several ethnic groups
