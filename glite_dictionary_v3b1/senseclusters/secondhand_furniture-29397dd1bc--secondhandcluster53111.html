secondhand furniture

noun
used furniture; not new
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkəndˌhænd ˈfɝnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌsɛkəndˌhænd ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
furniture that has been owned and used by someone else before being acquired by its current owner

Examples

Buy secondhand furniture
Buying secondhand furniture is an affordable way to furnish your home sustainably.
She decorated her apartment with secondhand furniture she found at local thrift stores.
The couple restored the old, worn secondhand furniture to give it a modern look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: new furniture - furniture that has not been owned or used before
  • synonym: used furniture - furniture that has been previously owned
