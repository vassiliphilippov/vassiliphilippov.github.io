partygoer

noun
person who attends parties
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtiˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtiˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who attends parties, especially frequently or with enthusiasm

Examples

A partygoer at events
As a frequent partygoer, she always knows where the best events are.
Some partygoers stayed until the early hours of the morning.
The living room was filled with excited partygoers laughing and dancing to the music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reveller - a person who is enjoying themselves in a lively and noisy way at a party or celebration
