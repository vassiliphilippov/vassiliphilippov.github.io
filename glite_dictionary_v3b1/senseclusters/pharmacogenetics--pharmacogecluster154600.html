pharmacogenetics

noun
genetic effects on drug responses
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Àåf…ëÀêr.m…ô.ko ä.d í…ôÀàn…õ.t…™ks / (AmE), / Àåf…ëÀê.m…ô.k…ô ä.d í…ôÀàn…õ.t…™ks / (BrE)

Examples

Pharmacogenetics study
