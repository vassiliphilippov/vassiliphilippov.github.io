profession

noun
open declaration of belief or opinion
4.2 3.8
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈfeʃ.ən/ (AmE), /prə.ˈfeʃ.ən/ (BrE)
an open or formal declaration of a belief, opinion, or feeling (including religious faith)

Examples

Profession of faith

He made a public profession of his beliefs before the congregation.
Her profession of Christianity was met with both admiration and skepticism in her community.
The ceremony included the profession of faith by all new members.
