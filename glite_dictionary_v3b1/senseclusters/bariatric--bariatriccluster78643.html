bariatric

adjective
for obese patients
Pronunciation: / ˌbæriˈætrɪk / (AmE), / ˌbæriˈætrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the treatment or support of obesity, especially in medical contexts or equipment

Examples

Bariatric surgery
The hospital installed bariatric chairs in the waiting area to better serve all patients.
A bariatric surgery center specializes in weight-loss procedures for people with severe obesity.
They ordered a bariatric wheelchair to ensure it could safely support heavier individuals.
The hospital opened a new bariatric unit to help patients struggling with severe obesity.
She decided to consult a bariatric surgeon about possible weight loss procedures.
Many bariatric programs include nutritional counseling and support groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obesity - the condition of being very overweight
  • synonym: weight loss - the process of losing body weight
