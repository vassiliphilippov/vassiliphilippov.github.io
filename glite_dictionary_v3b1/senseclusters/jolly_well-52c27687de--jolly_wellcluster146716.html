jolly well

adverb
emphasizing certainty or determination
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑli wɛl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒli wɛl / (BrE)
used for emphasis to express strong certainty or determination when saying that someone does something or should do something

Examples

You jolly well must
If you want something done right, you had jolly well do it yourself.
She jolly well told him what she thought of his idea.
They jolly well should apologize for their mistake.
