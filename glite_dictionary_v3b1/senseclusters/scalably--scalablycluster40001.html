scalably

adverb
in an expandable way
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskeɪ.lə.blɪ/ (AmE), /ˈskeɪ.lə.blɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that allows for efficient expansion or increase in size, capacity, or performance

Examples

To work scalably
The new software was built scalably to handle millions of users as the company grows.
By designing the network scalably, future upgrades will be much easier and less expensive.
Cloud services allow businesses to expand scalably without investing in new hardware.

Related Terms

  • synonym: efficiently - in a way that achieves maximum productivity with minimum wasted effort
  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that can adapt to change easily
