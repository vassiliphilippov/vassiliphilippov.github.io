steelworks

noun
factory where steel is produced
3.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstiÀêlÀåw…úrks / (AmE), / ÀàstiÀêlÀåw…úÀêks / (BrE)

Examples

Synonyms

    3.4 0.08/m

  • steel mill
    3.3 1.24/m
    steel mill is a facility that turns raw iron into steel, while steelworks names a factory or group where steel is made.
  • steel plant
    3.7 0.20/m
    steel plant is a large industrial site that may include many processes, while steelworks is any factory or group making steel.
  • steel factory
    2.9 0.06/m
    steel factory is the general, everyday word for a place making steel, while steelworks suggests a larger industrial complex.
