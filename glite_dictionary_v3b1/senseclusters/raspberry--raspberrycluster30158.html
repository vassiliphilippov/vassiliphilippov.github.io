raspberry

noun
a small edible red or black berry
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈrɑːzˌbəri /; / ˈrɑːzbəri / (BrE)
a small, soft, red, black, or yellow edible fruit, or the bush that produces such fruits
