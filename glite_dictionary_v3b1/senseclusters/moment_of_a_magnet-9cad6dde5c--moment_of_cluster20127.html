moment of a magnet

noun
magnetic property indicating torque in a field
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊmənt əv ə ˈmægnət / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt əv ə ˈmægnɪt / (BrE)
the torque that a magnet or magnetic dipole experiences in a magnetic field; a measure of the strength and orientation of a magnet's ability to produce a force or torque.
