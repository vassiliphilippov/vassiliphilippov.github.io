crow pheasant

noun
large South Asian cuckoo species
1.8
Pronunciation: / kroʊ ˈfɛzənt / (AmE), / krəʊ ˈfɛzənt / (BrE)
a large, ground-dwelling cuckoo (Centropus sinensis) of India and China, with glossy black plumage and a long tail

Examples

Farmers sometimes spot a crow pheasant gliding low over their rice fields at dawn.

With its deep booming call, the crow pheasant can be heard from a surprising distance.
