olive

noun
yellow-green color
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.lɪv / (AmE), / ˈɒl.ɪv / (BrE)
A yellow-green color of low brightness and saturation, resembling the color of unripe olives.

Examples

Olive color
She chose an olive dress to wear to the party.
The army uniform was a dull olive that blended into the landscape.
You can redecorate your living room with olive walls for a modern look.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: jade - a bright green color, similar to the gemstone
  • synonym: khaki - light yellow-brown color, often used for uniforms
