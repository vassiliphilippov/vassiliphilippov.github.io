depreciation

noun
belittling or disparaging remark
5.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˌpriʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of belittling or demeaning someone or something in speech or writing; disparagement.

Examples

Harsh depreciation of work
Her constant depreciation of his achievements began to affect his confidence.
Such depreciation can damage a person's self-esteem over time.
The review was laced with subtle depreciation of the author's work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disparagement - the act of speaking about someone in a derogatory manner
  • antonym: praise - expression of approval; the opposite of belittling
