allude

verb
refer to something indirectly
4.9
Pronunciation: / əˈluːd / (AmE), / əˈluːd / (BrE)
to mention something in an indirect way; to refer to something without mentioning it explicitly

Examples

To allude to literature
He often alludes to classical literature in his lectures.
She alluded to her upcoming project during the interview.
The article alluded to rising tensions without giving any details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hint - suggest or indicate indirectly
  • degree lower: mention - to refer to something briefly or incidentally
  • synonym: refer - to mention or direct attention to something
