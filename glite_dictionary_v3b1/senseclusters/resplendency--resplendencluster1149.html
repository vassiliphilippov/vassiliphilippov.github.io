resplendency

noun
brilliant radiant beauty
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsplɛndənsi / (AmE), / rɪˈsplɛndənsi / (BrE)
The quality of being brilliantly radiant or splendid in appearance.

Examples

Bright resplendency
She dressed in gold and silver, her gown shining with remarkable resplendency under the lights.
The resplendency of the palace impressed every visitor who walked through its grand hallways.
The garden was noted for the resplendency of its flowers in summer, radiating vivid colors across the landscape.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the quality of lacking brightness or vividness
  • synonym: splendor - magnificent and splendid appearance; grandeur
