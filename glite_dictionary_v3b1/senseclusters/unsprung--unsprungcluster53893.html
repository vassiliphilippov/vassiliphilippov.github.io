unsprung

adjective
without springs
8.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsprʌŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsprʌŋ / (BrE)
not equipped with springs or lacking a spring mechanism, especially in vehicles or machinery

Examples

Unsprung axle
The old carriage had an unsprung axle, making the ride very rough.
Bicycles with unsprung frames can be uncomfortable on bumpy roads.
The mechanic explained that the unsprung parts of the car affect how it handles shocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: springless - not having springs
  • antonym: sprung - equipped with springs
