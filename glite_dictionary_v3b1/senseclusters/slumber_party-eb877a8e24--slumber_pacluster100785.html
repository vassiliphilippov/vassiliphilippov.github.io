slumber party

noun
overnight social gathering (often for children/teens)
5.4 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈslʌm.bər ˈpɑːr.tiː/ /; / /ˈslʌm.bər ˈpɑːr.tɪ/ (AmE), /ˈslʌm.bə ˈpɑː.tiː/ /; / /ˈslʌm.bə ˈpɑː.tɪ/ (BrE)
a social gathering, typically for children or teenagers, where guests stay overnight and usually wear nightclothes, talk, and play games
