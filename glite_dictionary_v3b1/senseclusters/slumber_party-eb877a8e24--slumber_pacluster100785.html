slumber party

noun
overnight social gathering (often for children/teens)
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌmbɚ ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈslʌmbə ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering, typically for children or teenagers, where guests stay overnight and usually wear nightclothes, talk, and play games

Examples

A slumber party night
At her first slumber party, she ate popcorn and watched movies with her classmates.
Maria invited all her friends to her slumber party this weekend.
They stayed up late telling stories at the slumber party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sleepover - an overnight stay at someone else's house, usually for children or teenagers
