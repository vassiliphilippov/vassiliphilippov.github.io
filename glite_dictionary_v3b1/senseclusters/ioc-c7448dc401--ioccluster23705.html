IOC

noun
International Olympic Committee (Olympic Games organization)
Pronunciation: / aɪ oʊ ˈsi / (AmE), / aɪ əʊ ˈsiː / (BrE)
the International Olympic Committee, the organization responsible for organizing and overseeing the modern Olympic Games

Examples

IOC decision on Olympics
Many countries submit bids to the IOC to host the Olympic Games.
The IOC announced new protocols to ensure athlete safety.
The IOC was established in 1894 to revive the tradition of the Olympics.
