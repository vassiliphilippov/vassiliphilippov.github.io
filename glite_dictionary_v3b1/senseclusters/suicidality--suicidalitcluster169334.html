suicidality

noun
risk of suicide
9.6
Pronunciation: /suː.ɪ.sɪ.ˈdæl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /suː.ɪ.saɪ.ˈdæl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /suː.ɪ.sɪ.ˈdæl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /suː.ɪ.saɪ.ˈdæl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
