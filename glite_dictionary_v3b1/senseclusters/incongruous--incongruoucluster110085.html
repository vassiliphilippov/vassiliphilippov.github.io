incongruous

adjective
not in harmony; out of place
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑːŋɡruəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒŋɡruːəs / (BrE)
not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something; unsuitable or out of place.

Examples

An incongruous dress
Her brightly colored dress looked incongruous at the somber funeral.
It seemed incongruous to see a cat sitting next to the barking dog.
The modern sculpture was incongruous amidst the ancient ruins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: congruous - in harmony or keeping with other aspects of something
