tinhorn

adjective
pretentious and unimportant
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪnˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈtɪnˌhɔːn / (BrE)
pretending to be important or impressive but actually cheap, insignificant, or lacking real value

Examples

A tinhorn politician
Everyone saw through the tinhorn businessman who bragged about his minor achievements.
The town was run by a tinhorn official who loved to show off but did little of value.
Critics dismissed the movie as a tinhorn attempt at serious drama, lacking real substance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • synonym: insignificant - not important or worthy of attention
  • synonym: pretentious - trying to appear more important or impressive than is actually the case
