butterfly fish

noun
small tropical marine fish with broad fins
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪ fɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored tropical marine fish with a narrow, deep body and large, broad fins, found worldwide

Examples

Bright butterfly fish
Aquarists often keep butterfly fish because of their beauty and peaceful nature.
The butterfly fish is known for its striking colors and patterns in coral reefs.
We saw a school of butterfly fish swimming gracefully among the sea anemones.
