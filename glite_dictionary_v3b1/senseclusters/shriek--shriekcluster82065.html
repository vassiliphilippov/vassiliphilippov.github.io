shriek

verb
utter a high-pitched cry
6.1 2.5
Pronunciation: / ʃrik / (AmE), / ʃriːk / (BrE)
to utter a loud, high-pitched cry, often due to fear, pain, or excitement

Examples

To shriek in fear
He shrieked in pain when he accidentally stepped on the sharp stone.
She was so startled she let out a loud shriek of terror.
The children began to shriek with laughter as they played in the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scream - to make a loud, piercing sound, usually from fear or pain
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very softly, using one's breath rather than one's throat
