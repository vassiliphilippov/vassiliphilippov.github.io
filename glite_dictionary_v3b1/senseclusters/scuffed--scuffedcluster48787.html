scuffed

adjective
damaged by scraping
Pronunciation: / skʌft / (AmE), / skʌft / (BrE)
damaged or marked by scraping, rubbing, or friction, especially on the surface of an object

Examples

Scuffed shoes
After walking through the gravel, his shoes looked scuffed and worn.
The table had several scuffed spots from years of use.
She tried to polish the scuffed leather bag to make it look new again.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pristine - in perfect, undamaged condition
  • synonym: scratched - marked with shallow cuts or lines
