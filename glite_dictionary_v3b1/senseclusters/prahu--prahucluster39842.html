prahu

noun
Southeast Asian boat
9.9
Pronunciation: / prɑːˈhuː / (AmE), / prɑːˈhuː / (BrE)
a traditional sailing boat with one or two masts, commonly used in Southeast Asia

Examples

Sailing prahu
The fishermen sailed their prahu across the calm waters at sunrise.
Tourists admired the colorful prahu docked along the village shore.
A skilled crew can navigate a prahu through both rivers and open sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a narrow boat pointed at both ends, moved by paddles
  • synonym: junk - a Chinese sailing ship with a flat bottom and battened sails
