uncredited

adjective
not officially recognized
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (BrE)
not officially recognized with credit, especially in academic or professional contexts.

Examples

Uncredited course work
She completed an uncredited workshop that improved her skills but did not count toward graduation.
The actor made an uncredited appearance in the film, so his name was not listed in the credits.
Many internships are uncredited, meaning they do not fulfill any academic requirements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: credited - officially recognized or given credit
  • synonym: unacknowledged - not recognized or given attention
