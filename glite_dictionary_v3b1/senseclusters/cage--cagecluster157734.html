cage

noun
enclosure of bars for animals or birds
Pronunciation: / keɪdʒ / (AmE), / keɪdʒ / (BrE)
a structure of bars or wires in which birds or animals are kept, preventing them from escaping

Examples

A bird cage
Many zoos have spacious cages to keep animals safe yet visible to visitors.
She cleaned the rabbit's cage every morning before breakfast.
The parrot sang happily in its cage by the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pen - an enclosure for confining animals
