Gulf of Venice

proper noun
northern arm of the Adriatic Sea
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌlf əv vəˈnɛs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlf əv vɪˈniːs / (BrE)
an arm of the northern Adriatic Sea, lying between northeastern Italy, Slovenia, and Croatia
