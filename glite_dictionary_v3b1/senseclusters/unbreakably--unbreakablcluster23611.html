unbreakably

adverb
in an indestructible way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbreɪkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈbreɪkəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be broken, destroyed, or separated

Examples

To be unbreakably loyal
The two friends were unbreakably loyal to each other throughout their lives.
Their promise was unbreakably strong, no matter what challenges they faced.
The chain was forged unbreakably, making it impossible to separate the links.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragilely - in a way that is easily broken or damaged
  • synonym: indestructibly - in a way that cannot be destroyed
