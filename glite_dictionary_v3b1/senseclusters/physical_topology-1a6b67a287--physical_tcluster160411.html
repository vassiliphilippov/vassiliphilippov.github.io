physical topology

noun
actual layout of devices in a network
4.8 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl tə.ˈpɔː.lə.dʒiː/ /; / /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl tə.ˈpɑː.lə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl tə.ˈpɒl.ə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
the arrangement and layout of devices and cables in a computer network, as it appears physically

Examples

Network physical topology
Different physical topologies can affect network performance and reliability.
Network administrators must plan the physical topology before installing cables and devices.
The physical topology of the office network is a star configuration.
