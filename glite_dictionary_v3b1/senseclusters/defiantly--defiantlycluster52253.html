defiantly

adverb
in a boldly disobedient way
4.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfaɪ.ənt.liː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈfaɪ.jənt.liː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfaɪ.jənt.liː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈfaɪ.ənt.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner that shows open resistance or bold disobedience.

Examples

To act defiantly
He crossed his arms defiantly and refused to leave the room.
She stared defiantly at the teacher, refusing to apologize for her actions.
The child spoke defiantly when told to share his toys.
