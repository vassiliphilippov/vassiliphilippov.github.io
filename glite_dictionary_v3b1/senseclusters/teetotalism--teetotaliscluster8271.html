teetotalism

noun
practice of abstaining from alcohol
Pronunciation: / tiˈtoʊtəlɪzəm / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or principle of abstaining completely from alcoholic drinks

Examples

Teetotalism practice
He has practiced teetotalism since his early twenties and avoids all alcoholic beverages.
His strict teetotalism was a source of both admiration and curiosity among his friends.
The movement advocating teetotalism gained popularity during the temperance era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstinence - the act of refraining from something, especially alcohol
