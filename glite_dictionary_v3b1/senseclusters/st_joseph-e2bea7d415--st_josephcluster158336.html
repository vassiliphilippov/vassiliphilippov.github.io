St Joseph

proper noun
Christian feast day for St. Joseph
6.5
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈdʒoʊzəf / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒəʊzɪf / (BrE)
A Christian holy day commemorating St. Joseph, the husband of the Virgin Mary and foster father of Jesus, observed in various Christian traditions.

St Joseph day
