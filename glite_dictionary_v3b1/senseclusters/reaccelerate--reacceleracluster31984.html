reaccelerate

verb
to accelerate again
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəkˈsɛləˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːəkˈsɛləreɪt / (BrE)
to accelerate again after slowing down or stopping, or to make something accelerate again

After the brief stop, the driver had to reaccelerate the car to merge onto the highway.

The cyclist managed to reaccelerate quickly after slowing down for the sharp turn.
