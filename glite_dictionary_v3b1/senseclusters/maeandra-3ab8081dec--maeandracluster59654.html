Maeandra

noun
genus of brain corals
9.9
Pronunciation: / miˈændrə / (AmE), / miˈændrə / (BrE)
A genus of brain corals known for their meandering, maze-like grooves, found in warm ocean waters.

Examples

Maeandra brain coral
Marine biologists recently discovered a new member of the Maeandra genus in the Caribbean Sea.
The coral species in the genus Maeandra form intricate labyrinthine structures on the reef.
Unlike other corals, Maeandra corals have distinctive brain-like grooves.
