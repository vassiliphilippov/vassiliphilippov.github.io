myxamoeba

noun
slime mold cell stage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪksəˈmiːbə / (AmE), / ˌmɪksəˈmiːbə / (BrE)
an amoeboid cell that is a stage in the life cycle of slime molds, moving independently before aggregation

Examples

A myxamoeba cell
A myxamoeba can move independently in search of food before joining others.
Scientists observed the myxamoeba stage under the microscope during their research on slime molds.
When conditions change, each myxamoeba may aggregate with others to form a new structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amoeba - a single-celled organism that moves and feeds by extending pseudopods
  • degree higher: plasmodium - a multinucleate mass formed by the aggregation of myxamoebae in slime molds
