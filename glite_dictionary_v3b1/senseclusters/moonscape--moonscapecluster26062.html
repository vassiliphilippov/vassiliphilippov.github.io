moonscape

noun
lunar-like landscape
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmuːn.skeɪp/ (AmE), /ˈmuːn.skeɪp/ (BrE)
a landscape or surface that looks barren, rocky, and empty, like the surface of the moon
