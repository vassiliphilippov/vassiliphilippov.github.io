moonscape

noun
lunar-like landscape
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈmuːnskeɪp / (BrE)
a landscape or surface that looks barren, rocky, and empty, like the surface of the moon

Examples

A barren moonscape

Artists often paint a moonscape to capture the mysterious beauty of the lunar surface.

After the wildfire, the once lush valley was transformed into a moonscape of ash and charred trees.
The abandoned construction site looked like a moonscape under the pale morning light.
Years of drought left the farmland a cracked and dusty moonscape.

The abandoned construction site resembled a moonscape with its piles of rubble and dust.
From the hilltop, we gazed at the barren moonscape left by the mining operation.

  • antonym: oasis - a fertile or pleasant area in a desert or wasteland
