attorney

noun
a person qualified to practice law
4.6 88/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàt…ùÀêni / (AmE), / …ôÀàt…úÀêni / (BrE)
a person who is qualified and authorized to represent clients in legal matters, including giving legal advice and conducting lawsuits

Examples

A attorney in court
Her attorney presented new evidence in court yesterday.
The attorney advised us on how to proceed with the case.
You should consult an attorney before signing the contract.

Synonyms

  • lawyer
    1.0 152/m
    a lawyer is professionally trained and handles many legal tasks, while an attorney is the authorized representative who acts for clients in court.
  • counsel
    4.3 25/m
    counsel acts as a lawyer who advises in court and during cases, while an attorney is a general legal representative for clients.
  • advocate
    5.8 8.81/m
    an advocate argues the client's case in court, while an attorney also gives legal advice and handles broader legal work.
