disturbing

adjective
causing distress or anxiety
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɜrbɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈstɜːbɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing distress, worry, or anxiety.

Examples

Disturbing behavior
His disturbing behavior worried his teachers and parents alike.
She found the news about the accident extremely disturbing.
The documentary contained several disturbing scenes that left the audience unsettled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calming - making someone feel calm and relaxed
  • synonym: distressing - causing anxiety, sorrow, or pain
  • synonym: upsetting - making someone feel unhappy or worried
