overeat

verb
eat too much; eat excessively
3.1 1.10/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàit / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàriÀêt / (BrE)
to eat more food than your body needs or is healthy, especially at one time

Examples

To overeat at dinner
After the holiday feast, she realized she had overeaten and felt uncomfortably full.
It's easy to overeat when you're distracted by television or conversation.
Many people tend to overeat when they're feeling stressed or anxious.

Synonyms

  • gorge
    5.9 0.93/m
    gorge involves eating a very large amount quickly and greedily, while overeat simply means eating more than your body needs.
  • glut
    7.9 0.01/m
    glut means eating or consuming too much at once, while overeat means eating more food than your body needs.
  • binge
    4.5 0.63/m
    binge means doing something in large amounts over a short time, while overeat focuses on eating more than is healthy.
  • overindulge
    6.2 0.17/m
    overindulge means allowing too much food or drink for yourself or others, while overeat means directly eating more than is healthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binge - to eat or drink too much, especially in a short period
  • antonym: under-eat - to eat less food than needed
