overeat

verb
eat too much; eat excessively
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈit / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈriːt / (BrE)
to eat more food than your body needs or is healthy, especially at one time

Examples

To overeat at dinner
After the holiday feast, she realized she had overeaten and felt uncomfortably full.
It's easy to overeat when you're distracted by television or conversation.
Many people tend to overeat when they're feeling stressed or anxious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binge - to eat or drink too much, especially in a short period
  • antonym: under-eat - to eat less food than needed
