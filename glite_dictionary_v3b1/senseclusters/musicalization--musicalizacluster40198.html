musicalization

noun
adding music
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmjuːzɪkələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmjuːzɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something musical, especially by adding music or adapting it into a musical form
