unconcernedly

adverb
in an indifferent or unconcerned way
7.0 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Àå ån.k…ônÀàs…ùÀên.…™d.li / (AmE), / Àå ån.k…ônÀàs…úÀên.…™d.li / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a lack of worry or interest; indifferently or without concern

Examples

To unconcernedly shrug off
He unconcernedly shrugged off the criticism from his colleagues.
She continued to read her book unconcernedly as the children played loudly nearby.
The dog wandered unconcernedly into the busy street, oblivious to the traffic.
