expose oneself to the sun

verb
to spend time in direct sunlight
1.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊz wʌnˌsɛlf tu ðə sʌn / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊz wʌnˌsɛlf tuː ðə sʌn / (BrE)
to deliberately be in direct sunlight for a period of time, especially for tanning or warmth
