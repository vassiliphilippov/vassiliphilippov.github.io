scuba diving

noun
underwater swimming with scuba equipment
5.9 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈskuː.bə ˈdaɪ.vɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskuː.bə ˈdaɪ.vɪŋ/ (BrE)
The activity of swimming and exploring underwater while using a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (scuba).
