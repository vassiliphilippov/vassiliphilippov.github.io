crossruff

verb
alternately trump in two hands (bridge)
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌrʌf / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌrʌf / (BrE)
In card games, especially bridge, to alternately use trumps in each hand, typically to win tricks with both hands' trump cards.

Examples

To crossruff in bridge
She expertly crossruffed to defeat the contract.
They began crossruffing immediately after winning the lead.
To maximize their tricks, they decided to crossruff the remaining suits.
