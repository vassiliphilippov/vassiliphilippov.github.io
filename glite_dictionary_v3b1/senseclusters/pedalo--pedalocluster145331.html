pedalo

noun
pedal-powered boat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɛdəˌləʊ / (BrE)
a small recreational boat that is powered by pedals operated with the feet

Examples

A pedalo ride
We rented a pedalo and enjoyed an hour on the lake.
Children laughed as they pedaled their pedalo across the calm water.
It was her first time steering a pedalo on the park pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paddle boat - a small boat moved by pedals or paddles
  • antonym: rowboat - a small boat propelled by oars
