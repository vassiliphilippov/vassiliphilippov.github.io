councilor

noun
member of a council
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊnsələr / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsələ /; / ˈkaʊns(ɪ)lə / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a council or committee and helps make decisions for an organization or local government

Examples

City councilor
The city appointed a new councilor to oversee the development project.
As a councilor, she participated in important decisions about local education policies.
Each councilor is responsible for representing the interests of their community.
The councilor listened carefully to the concerns of the local residents at the meeting.
After being elected, she became the youngest councilor in the city's history.
Several councilors voted in favor of the new community park project.
Each councillor is responsible for representing a specific district or ward.
Sarah was elected as a councillor on the city council last year.
The councillor attended the community meeting to hear residents' concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advisor - a person who gives advice, especially in an official capacity
  • synonym: alderman - a member of a municipal council or local government
  • synonym: councillor - an alternative spelling, especially in British English, for a member of a council
  • synonym: councilman - a male member of a council, especially in American English
  • synonym: councilwoman - a female member of a council, especially in American English
  • antonym: mayor - the elected head of a city, town, or municipality
  • synonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or organization
