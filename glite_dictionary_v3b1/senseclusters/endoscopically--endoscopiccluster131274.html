endoscopically

adverb
using an endoscope
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈskɑpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛndəˈskɒpɪkli / (BrE)
by means of or using an endoscope, especially for internal medical examination or surgery

Examples

Endoscopically examine a stomach
The doctor examined the patient's stomach endoscopically to check for ulcers.
Polyps can often be removed endoscopically without the need for open surgery.
The tumor was successfully treated endoscopically, resulting in a shorter hospital stay.
