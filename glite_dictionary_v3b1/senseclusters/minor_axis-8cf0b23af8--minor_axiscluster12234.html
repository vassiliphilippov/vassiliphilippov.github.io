minor axis

noun
shortest diameter of an ellipse
8.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.nər ˈæk.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.nə ˈæk.sɪs/ (BrE)
the shorter or shortest diameter of an ellipse or ellipsoid, passing through its center and perpendicular to the major axis

Examples

The minor axis length
The astronomer measured both the major and minor axis of the planetary orbit.
The length of the minor axis is always less than that of the major axis in an ellipse.
When you rotate an ellipse around its minor axis, you create a prolate spheroid.
