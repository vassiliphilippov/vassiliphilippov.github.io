whatevs

interjection
informal indifference
9.9
Pronunciation: / wəˈtɛvz / (AmE), / wəˈtɛvz / (BrE)
an informal way of saying 'whatever', used to show indifference or a lack of concern

Examples

He said whatevs casually
When asked if he wanted pizza or burgers, he just replied, 'whatevs.'
She rolled her eyes and said 'whatevs' when her friend complained again.
If you don't care about the outcome, you might just say 'whatevs'.
