cold

adjective
completely unconscious
4.4 6.4
Pronunciation: / koʊld / (AmE), / kəʊld / (BrE)
completely unconscious or dead (especially as a result of a blow, shock, or accident)

Examples

Knocked cold

She drank so much that she passed out cold on the couch.
The fighter was hit so hard that he went cold before hitting the mat.
After the accident, he was found cold by the roadside before help arrived.
She lay cold and still, having passed away peacefully during the night.
The phrase 'cold in his grave' refers to someone who is already cold, or dead.
