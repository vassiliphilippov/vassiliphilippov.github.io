predigest

verb
partially digest in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪˈdʒɛst / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪˈdʒɛst / (BrE)
to partially digest food or make food easier to digest before it is eaten or absorbed

Examples

To predigest food
Certain bacteria help predigest complex carbohydrates in the stomachs of cows.
Enzymes are sometimes used to predigest proteins in baby formula.
Some animals predigest their food before feeding it to their young.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digest - to break down food in the digestive system
