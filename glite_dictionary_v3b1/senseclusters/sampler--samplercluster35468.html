sampler

noun
embroidery displaying various stitches
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmplər / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmplə / (BrE)
a piece of embroidery created as a demonstration or test of skill in various stitches, often as a decorative record

Examples

Embroidered sampler
My grandmother's sampler from 1920 hangs in the hallway as a cherished family heirloom.
She stitched alphabets and patterns onto the sampler to practice new embroidery techniques.
The museum's collection includes an 18th-century sampler made by a young girl learning needlework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidery - the art or process of decorating fabric with needlework
