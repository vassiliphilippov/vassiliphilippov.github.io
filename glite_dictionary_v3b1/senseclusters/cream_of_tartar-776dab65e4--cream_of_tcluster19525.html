cream of tartar

noun
acid salt used in baking
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkrim əv ˈtɑrtər / (AmE), / ˌkriːm əv ˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
a white, powdery acid salt (potassium bitartrate) used in baking powders and as a stabilizer in cooking

Examples

Add cream of tartar
Add a pinch of cream of tartar to the egg whites to help them whip up stiffly.
She bought a jar of cream of tartar for her baking projects.
The recipe calls for cream of tartar to prevent the sugar from crystallizing.
