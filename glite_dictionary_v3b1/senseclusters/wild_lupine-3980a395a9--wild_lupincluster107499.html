wild lupine

noun
a North American flowering plant
4.5
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈluːpɪn / (AmE), / waɪld ˈluːpɪn / (BrE)
a perennial plant (Lupinus perennis) native to eastern and central North America, with palmate leaves and showy racemes of blue flowers

The wild lupine blooms in brilliant blue clusters every spring in the eastern woodlands.
