sheng

noun
Chinese volume unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃʌŋ / (AmE), / ʃɛŋ / (BrE)
a traditional Chinese unit of volume, approximately equal to one liter

Examples

A sheng of rice
The recipe called for two sheng of rice, so she measured carefully.
Farmers often sold grain by the sheng in traditional Chinese markets.
He learned that a sheng is roughly the same as a liter when studying ancient measurements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liter - a metric unit of volume equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters
