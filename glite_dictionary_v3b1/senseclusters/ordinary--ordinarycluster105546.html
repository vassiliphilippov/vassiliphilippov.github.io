ordinary

noun
common person or thing
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈɔːdnəri / (BrE)
a person or thing that is not special, different, or remarkable in any way

Examples

Just an ordinary
Among all the talented artists, he felt like just an ordinary.
The museum displayed objects used by ordinaries in daily life centuries ago.
She never wanted to be an ordinary, always striving to stand out from the crowd.
