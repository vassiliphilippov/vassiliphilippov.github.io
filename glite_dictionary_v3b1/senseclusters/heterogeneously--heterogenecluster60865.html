heterogeneously

adverb
in a non-uniform way
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈdʒiːniəsli / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not uniform, consisting of different or diverse parts or elements

Examples

Mixed heterogeneously
The ingredients were mixed heterogeneously, resulting in uneven texture throughout the cake.
In the study, participants were heterogeneously distributed across different age groups.
Pollution levels are often heterogeneously spread in large urban areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diversely - in a varied or different way
  • antonym: uniformly - in a consistent or identical way
