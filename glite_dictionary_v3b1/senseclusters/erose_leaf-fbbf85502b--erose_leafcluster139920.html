erose leaf

noun
leaf with jagged, gnawed margins
Pronunciation: / ɪˈroʊs liːf / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊs liːf / (BrE)
A leaf with a jagged, irregular margin that appears as if it has been gnawed.

Examples

An erose leaf margin
A close examination revealed that each erose leaf had a distinctly jagged outline.
Botanists identified the plant by its characteristic erose leaf margins.
The erose leaf is easily recognized by its uneven, almost chewed-looking edges.
