coalesced

adjective
joined into a whole
5.9
Pronunciation: / koʊ.əˈlɛst / (AmE), / kəʊ.əˈlɛst / (BrE)
(of things) joined together to form a single whole; united or combined into one.

Examples

Two companies coalesced
After months of negotiations, the two companies finally coalesced into a single corporation.
Snowflakes on the window gradually coalesced into a beautiful, frosty pattern.
The committee's different viewpoints coalesced to form a unified policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amalgamated - joined together to form a single organization or entity
  • synonym: consolidated - combined into a single whole or group
  • antonym: separate - not joined or united; distinct
