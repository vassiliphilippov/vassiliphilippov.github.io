Orbignya spesiosa

noun
Brazilian palm yielding oil and vegetable ivory
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˈbɪnjə spɛˈsiːoʊsə / (AmE), / ɔːˈbɪnjə spɛˈsiːəʊsə / (BrE)
A tall feather palm native to northern Brazil, bearing hard-shelled nuts that produce valuable oil and a type of vegetable ivory.

Examples

Orbignya spesiosa palm
Artisans carve the hard nuts of Orbignya spesiosa to produce objects resembling ivory.
Many cosmetic products contain oil extracted from the seeds of Orbignya spesiosa.
The Orbignya spesiosa is an important source of oil in rural areas of Brazil.
