walking carpet

noun
person or dog with very fluffy fur or clothing
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkɪŋ ˈkɑrpɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ ˈkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
an informal term for a person wearing extremely fluffy or furry clothes, or a very furry dog that resembles a carpet when it moves

Examples

A walking carpet dog
After grooming, their old sheepdog still looked like a walking carpet.
The comedian described his outfit as a walking carpet during the show.
When she arrived in her new faux fur coat, her friends called her a walking carpet.
