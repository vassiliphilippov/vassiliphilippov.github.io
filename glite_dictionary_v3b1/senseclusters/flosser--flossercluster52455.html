flosser

noun
dental cleaning tool
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑːsər / (AmE), / ˈflɒsə / (BrE)

Examples

A plastic flosser

Using a flosser makes it easier to reach the back teeth.
The dentist recommended using a flosser daily to maintain healthy gums.
