Aquarian

noun
Age of Aquarius follower
Pronunciation: / əˈkwɛriən / (AmE), / əˈkwɛːrɪən / (BrE)
a person who identifies with or supports the ideas and lifestyle associated with the Age of Aquarius or New Age movements

Examples

Aquarian thinker
As an Aquarian, he believed in peace, creativity, and a new way of living.
Many Aquarians gathered at the festival to celebrate their shared ideals.
Her friends described her as a true Aquarian because of her open-minded and progressive views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: New Ager - a person involved in New Age spiritual or cultural movements
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who supports traditional ideas and values
