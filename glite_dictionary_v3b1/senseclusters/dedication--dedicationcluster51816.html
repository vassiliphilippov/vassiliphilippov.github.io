dedication

noun
inscription dedicating a work to someone
4.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ded.ə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ded.ɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ded.ɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a message at the beginning of a book, piece of music, or other work, expressing respect or devotion to someone or something

Examples

Book dedication
He composed a piece of music as a dedication to his late friend.
The author wrote a heartfelt dedication to her parents at the beginning of the novel.
The photograph included a handwritten dedication in the corner.
A bronze plaque bears a dedication to those who served in the war.
The dedication reads, 'To all who inspired me along the way.'
The author's dedication at the front of the book was addressed to his children.
