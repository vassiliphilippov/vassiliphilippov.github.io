research worker

noun
person conducting research
3.3 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / r…™Ààs…úrt É Ààw…úrk…ôr / (AmE), / r…™Ààs…úÀêt É Ààw…úÀêk…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to conduct systematic investigation in order to discover or establish facts, typically in a scientific or academic field.

Examples

A research worker at university
As a research worker at the university, she published numerous scientific papers.
The research worker spent several years studying the effects of the new medicine.
The company hired a research worker to develop advanced materials for renewable energy.
