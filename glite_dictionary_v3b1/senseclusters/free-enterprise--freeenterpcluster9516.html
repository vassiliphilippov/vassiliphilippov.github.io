free-enterprise

adjective
supporting a free-market economy
5.8 3.8
Pronunciation: /friː.ˈen.tər.praɪz/ /; / /friː ˈen.tər.praɪz/ (AmE), /friː.ˈen.tə.praɪz/ /; / /friː ˈen.tə.praɪz/ (BrE)
Favoring or supporting an economic system in which private businesses operate in competition and largely free of state control.

Examples

She is a strong advocate of free-enterprise solutions in the healthcare industry.
The country has adopted a free-enterprise approach to stimulate economic growth.
