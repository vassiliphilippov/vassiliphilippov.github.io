free-enterprise

adjective
supporting a free-market economy
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfriː ˈɛntərˌpraɪz / (AmE), / ˌfriː ˈɛntəˌprʌɪz / (BrE)
Favoring or supporting an economic system in which private businesses operate in competition and largely free of state control.

Examples

Free-enterprise system
Many voters prefer free-enterprise policies that favor minimal government intervention.
She is a strong advocate of free-enterprise solutions in the healthcare industry.
The country has adopted a free-enterprise approach to stimulate economic growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capitalistic - supporting the principles of capitalism
  • antonym: socialist - supporting a system in which production is owned by the state or the community
