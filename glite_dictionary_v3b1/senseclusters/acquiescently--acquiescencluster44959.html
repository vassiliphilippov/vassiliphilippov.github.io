acquiescently

adverb
without protest
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌækwiˈɛsəntli / (AmE), / ˌækwiˈɛsəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows acceptance or agreement without protest

Examples

To acquiescently accept
He acquiescently followed the instructions, even though he had his own doubts.
The team acquiescently accepted the manager's decision to change the schedule.
She acquiescently agreed to help, not wanting to cause any trouble.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reluctantly - in a way that shows unwillingness or hesitation
  • synonym: submissively - in a way that shows willingness to submit without resistance
