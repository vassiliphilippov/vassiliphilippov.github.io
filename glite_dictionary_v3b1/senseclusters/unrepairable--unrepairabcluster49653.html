unrepairable

adjective
cannot be fixed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈpɛrəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpeərəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to fix or restore to a usable or good condition

Examples

Unrepairable damage
The flood caused unrepairable damage to the wooden floors in the house.
After the accident, the car was declared unrepairable by the insurance company.
Years of neglect left the bridge in an unrepairable state.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreparable - impossible to repair or make right again
  • antonym: repairable - able to be fixed or restored
