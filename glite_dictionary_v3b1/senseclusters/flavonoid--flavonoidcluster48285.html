flavonoid

noun
class of plant pigments
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈflavənɔɪd / (BrE)
any of a large group of plant pigments with a structure similar to flavone, often contributing to the color and antioxidant properties of plants

Examples

Plant flavonoids
Many fruits and vegetables are rich in flavonoids that may offer health benefits.
Researchers are studying how flavonoids act as antioxidants in the human body.
The yellow color of some flowers is due to the presence of flavonoids.
