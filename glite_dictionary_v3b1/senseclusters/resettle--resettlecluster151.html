resettle

verb
move to a new place to live
2.3 3.5
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈset.əl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈset.əl/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈset.əl/ /; / /rə.ˈset.əl/ (BrE)
to move or be moved to a new place to live, especially after being forced to leave an old one

Examples

The government provided aid to help families resettle in safer areas.
Thousands of refugees were forced to resettle after the war destroyed their villages.
