sculpt

verb
carve or shape a material into art
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / skʌlpt / (AmE), / skʌlpt / (BrE)
to create a work of art by carving or shaping materials such as stone, wood, or clay

Examples

To sculpt a statue
He decided to sculpt a swan out of a block of ice for the party.
She learned how to sculpt delicate figures from marble during her studies in Italy.
The artist will sculpt a new statue for the city square.
He spends his weekends sculpting clay figures in his garage studio.
She patiently sculpted an intricate statue from a single piece of marble.
The artist loves to sculpt animals from driftwood found on the beach.
