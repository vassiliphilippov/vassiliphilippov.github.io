infinitesimal

adjective
extremely small; almost immeasurable
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌfɪnəˈtɛsəməl / (AmE), / ɪnˌfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl / (BrE)
extremely small; so small as to be almost impossible to measure or calculate

Examples

Infinitesimal chance
Her chances of winning were infinitesimal, but she decided to try anyway.
Scientists study infinitesimal particles that cannot be seen with the naked eye.
The difference between the two measurements was infinitesimal, almost too small to detect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enormous - very large; huge
  • synonym: minute - extremely small; very tiny
