DCI

noun
digital cinema standards body/specification
8.1
Pronunciation: / di si ˈaɪ / (AmE), / diː siː ˈaɪ / (BrE)
Digital Cinema Initiatives; an industry consortium and its technical specification that defines standards for the distribution, encryption, and projection of digital cinema content.

Examples

DCI movie standards
The distributor released the film in DCI formats compliant with theatre projection systems.
The festival required all films to meet DCI standards for color and image quality.
The lab checked the projector to ensure it matched the DCI specification before screening.
