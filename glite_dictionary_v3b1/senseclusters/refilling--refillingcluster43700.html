refilling

noun
the act of filling again
Pronunciation: / riˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of filling something again by supplying what has been used up

Examples

The refilling of printer
After every use, the refilling of the printer with paper is necessary.
She made a note to schedule the refilling of her prescription next week.
The refilling of the water cooler happens every Monday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replenishment - the act of making something full again; can be used interchangeably with 'refilling' in some contexts
