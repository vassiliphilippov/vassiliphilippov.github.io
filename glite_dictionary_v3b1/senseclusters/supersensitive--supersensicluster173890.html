supersensitive

adjective
abnormally or excessively sensitive
2.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pərˈsɛn.sə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈsɛn.sɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
having an unusually strong reaction or susceptibility, especially to particular substances or stimuli; abnormally sensitive or prone to allergies

Examples

Supersensitive skin
His supersensitive skin reacts badly to even mild detergents.
She is supersensitive to pollen, so springtime can be very difficult for her.
Some children are supersensitive and develop allergies to everyday substances.
