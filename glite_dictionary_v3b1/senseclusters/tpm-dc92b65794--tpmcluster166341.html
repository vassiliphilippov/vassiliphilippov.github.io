TPM

noun
third-party maintenance
8.4
Pronunciation: / ti pi ɛm / (AmE), / tiː piː em / (BrE)
maintenance services for equipment or systems provided by a third-party company instead of the original manufacturer

Examples

TPM service contract
The company chose TPM to save costs on maintaining their old computer systems.
Many organizations rely on TPM providers for flexible service contracts.
Switching to TPM allowed them to extend the life of their equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: OEM maintenance - maintenance provided by the original equipment manufacturer
  • synonym: outsourcing - the practice of hiring external companies to perform services
