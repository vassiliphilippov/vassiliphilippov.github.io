needfulness

noun
state of need
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈniːdfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the state of being in need or lacking basic necessities

Examples

Needfulness after disaster
The needfulness of the community became clear after the natural disaster struck.
Charities work to reduce needfulness by providing food and shelter to those in poverty.
During the harsh winter, many families faced great needfulness and relied on donations.

  • synonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • synonym: want - a lack of something necessary
