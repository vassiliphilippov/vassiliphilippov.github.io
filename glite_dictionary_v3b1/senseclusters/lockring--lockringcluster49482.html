lockring

noun
washer to prevent loosening of a nut
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑk.rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒk.rɪŋ / (BrE)
A washer designed to prevent a nut from loosening by holding it securely in place.

Examples

A lockring washer
A missing lockring can cause the entire assembly to come loose.
Always check that the lockring is properly in place before riding your bike.
The mechanic tightened the lockring to secure the crankset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lock washer - a washer used to prevent nuts and bolts from turning, slipping, and coming loose
