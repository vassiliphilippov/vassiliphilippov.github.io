lockring

noun
washer to prevent loosening of a nut
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑk.rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒk.rɪŋ / (BrE)
A washer designed to prevent a nut from loosening by holding it securely in place.

Examples

A lockring washer

The mechanic tightened the lockring to secure the crankset.
