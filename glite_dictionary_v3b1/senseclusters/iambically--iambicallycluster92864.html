iambically

adverb
in iambic manner
9.1
Pronunciation: / aɪˈæmbɪkli / (AmE), / aɪˈæmbɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or uses iambs, especially in poetry or rhythm.

Examples

To speak iambically
The actor delivered his lines iambically to match the traditional style of the play.
She recited the verse iambically, giving it a gentle, rhythmic flow.
The teacher asked the students to read the poem iambically for their literature assignment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metrically - in a way relating to poetic meter
