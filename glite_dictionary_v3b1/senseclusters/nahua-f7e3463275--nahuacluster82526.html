Nahua

noun
Nahuatl dialect
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːwɑː / (AmE), / ˈnɑːwɑː / (BrE)
a variety or dialect of the Nahuatl language spoken by Nahua communities in Mexico and Central America

Examples

Nahua language
Linguists are working to preserve the Nahua language in remote villages.
The Nahua spoken in this region differs from other Nahuatl dialects.
She learned a few words of Nahua to communicate with local elders.
