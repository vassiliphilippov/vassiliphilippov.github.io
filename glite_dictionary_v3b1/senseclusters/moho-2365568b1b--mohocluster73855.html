Moho

noun
boundary between crust and mantle
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊhəʊ / (BrE)
the boundary between the Earth's crust and the mantle, also known as the Mohorovičić discontinuity

Examples

The Moho boundary
Geologists often study the Moho to understand plate tectonics better.
Seismic waves travel faster below the Moho than above it.
The depth of the Moho varies significantly between continental and oceanic regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mohorovičić discontinuity - the formal name for the boundary between the Earth's crust and mantle
