Shawnee

noun
member of a Native American people
8.8
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈniː / (AmE), / ʃɔːˈniː / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people formerly living along the Tennessee River, now found in Oklahoma and other regions

Examples

A Shawnee person
A group of Shawnee settled in Ohio during the 18th century.
Many Shawnee traditions have been passed down through generations.
The Shawnee played a significant role in early American history.
