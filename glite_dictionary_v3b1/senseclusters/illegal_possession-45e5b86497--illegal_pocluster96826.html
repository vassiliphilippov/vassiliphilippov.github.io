illegal possession

noun
having something unlawfully, especially drugs
7.3 4.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈliɡəl pəˈzɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈliːɡəl pəˈzɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act of having something, such as controlled substances or prohibited items, when it is against the law to do so

Examples

Illegal possession of drugs
He was convicted of illegal possession after police found banned substances in his apartment.
Students are warned that illegal possession of drugs can lead to severe disciplinary action.
The suspect was arrested for illegal possession of firearms and narcotics.
