knock out

verb
impress or overwhelm greatly
Pronunciation: / nɑk aʊt / (AmE), / nɒk aʊt / (BrE)
to impress or overwhelm (someone) greatly, especially with admiration or attraction

Examples

To knock out the audience
Her performance will knock out everyone in the audience.
His charm and wit knocked out all the guests at the party.
The new restaurant's design really knocked out the critics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bore - to make someone feel uninterested or tired
  • synonym: impress - to evoke admiration or respect
