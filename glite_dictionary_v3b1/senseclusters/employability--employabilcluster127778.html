employability

noun
ability to get a job
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌplɔɪəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪmˌplɔɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the ability to get and keep a job, especially because of having the right skills and qualities

Examples

The program is designed to boost the employability of young people entering the workforce.
Internships can greatly improve a student's employability after graduation.
The course is designed to boost the employability of young professionals entering the job market.
Good communication skills are essential for increasing your employability in today's workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: employable - having the skills and qualities needed to get a job
  • synonym: hireability - the quality of being likely to be hired for a job
  • antonym: unemployability - the state of being unlikely to get or keep a job
  • antonym: unemployable - not able to get or keep a job
