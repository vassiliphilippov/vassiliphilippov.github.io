spherometer

noun
instrument for measuring surface curvature
Pronunciation: / sfɪˈrɑːmətər / (AmE), / sfɪəˈrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the curvature or radius of a spherical surface, such as lenses or curved glass

Examples

A spherometer tool
A spherometer is essential equipment in an optical laboratory.
Accurate readings from the spherometer ensured the glass surface met the required specifications.
The technician used a spherometer to determine the curvature of the lens.
