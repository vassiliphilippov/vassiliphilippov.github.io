nunlike

adjective
resembling a nun
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a nun, especially in appearance, behavior, or manner

Examples

A nunlike manner
Her modest dress and quiet manner were very nunlike.
He admired her nunlike dedication to helping others in the community.
The nunlike atmosphere of the convent made visitors feel calm and reflective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modest - showing simplicity or humility in appearance or behavior
  • antonym: worldly - concerned with material values or ordinary life rather than spiritual matters
