mochaccino

noun
espresso with chocolate
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊkəˈtʃiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌməʊkəˈtʃiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a coffee drink made with espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk (sometimes also used to describe the flavor in desserts or beverages)
