clotty

adjective
full of clots
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑti / (AmE), / ˈklɒti / (BrE)
containing or resembling clots, especially of thickened liquid or lumps

Examples

Clotty blood sample
The blood sample appeared clotty after being left out for several hours.
She noticed the soup had turned clotty and was no longer appetizing.
If the paint becomes clotty, it will not spread smoothly on the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumpy - full of lumps or small masses
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface; not lumpy or clotted
