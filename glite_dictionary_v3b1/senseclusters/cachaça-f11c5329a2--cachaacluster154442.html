cachaça

noun
Brazilian sugarcane spirit
Pronunciation: / kəˈʃɑsə / (AmE), / kəˈʃæsə / (BrE)
a Brazilian alcoholic spirit distilled from fermented sugarcane juice

Examples

A cachaca cocktail
He ordered a cachaça cocktail at the bar to try something new.
The traditional Brazilian drink caipirinha is made with cachaça, lime, and sugar.
Tourists often bring back bottles of cachaça as souvenirs from Brazil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rum - a distilled alcoholic drink made from sugarcane byproducts
