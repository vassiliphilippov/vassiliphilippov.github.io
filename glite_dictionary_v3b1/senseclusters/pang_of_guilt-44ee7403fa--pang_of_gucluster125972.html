pang of guilt

noun
sudden feeling of remorse
Pronunciation: / pæŋ əv ɡɪlt / (AmE), / pæŋ əv ɡɪlt / (BrE)
a sudden, sharp feeling of remorse or regret for something one has done or failed to do

Examples

A pang of guilt
As she walked away, a pang of guilt made her hesitate.
He felt a sudden pang of guilt when he remembered his harsh words.
Whenever I see that photo, I experience a pang of guilt for what happened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compunction - a feeling of guilt or moral scruple that follows the doing of something bad
  • synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
