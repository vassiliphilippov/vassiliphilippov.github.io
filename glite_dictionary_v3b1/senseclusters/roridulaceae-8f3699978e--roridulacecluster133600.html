Roridulaceae

noun
family of carnivorous South African shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɔːˌrɪdjuːˈleɪsiː / (AmE), / rɒˌrɪdjuːˈleɪsiː / (BrE)
a small family of carnivorous South African shrubs sometimes included in the family Droseraceae

Examples

The Roridulaceae family
Botanists sometimes debate whether Roridulaceae should be classified within Droseraceae.
Members of the Roridulaceae possess sticky leaves to trap insects.
The Roridulaceae are native to the western part of South Africa.
