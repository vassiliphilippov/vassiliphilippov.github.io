postlarval

noun
young after larva stage
8.7
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ˈlɑːr.vəl/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ˈlɑː.vəl/ (BrE)
a young animal that has recently completed the larval stage, especially in marine or aquatic species

Examples

A postlarval shrimp

After hatching, the fish enter the postlarval stage before becoming juveniles.
The survival rate of postlarval organisms is crucial for the success of aquaculture operations.
