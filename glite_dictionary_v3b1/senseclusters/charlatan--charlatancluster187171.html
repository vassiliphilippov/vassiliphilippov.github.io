charlatan

noun
person who pretends to have skills or knowledge
8.6 1.61/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É…ërl…ôt…ôn / (AmE), / Àà É…ëÀêl…ôt…ôn / (BrE)
a person who falsely claims to have special skills or knowledge, often in order to deceive others

Examples

A charlatan tricks people
In the story, the traveling charlatan wowed crowds with his elaborate tricks and tales.
Many investors lost money after trusting the promises of a financial charlatan.
The self-proclaimed healer was eventually exposed as a charlatan who took advantage of desperate people.
