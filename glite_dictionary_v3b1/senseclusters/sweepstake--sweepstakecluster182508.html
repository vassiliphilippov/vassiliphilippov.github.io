Synonyms

  • drawing
    3.8 0.1/m
    An event where winners are chosen at random from ticket buyers or entries
    vs sweepstake: an event in which winners are selected by chance, often from among people who have bought tickets or entries
  • raffle
    3.3 0.3/m
    Offers prizes by selling numbered tickets, often to raise funds
    vs sweepstake: to offer or dispose of something as a prize in a lottery, typically by selling numbered tickets
  • lottery
    2.3 9/m
    A method of raising money where numbered tickets are sold and winning numbers are drawn
    vs sweepstake: A game or method of raising money in which people buy numbered tickets and prizes are given to the holders of numbers drawn at random.
  • lotto
    4.4 2/m
    A number-based game where balls are drawn and players match numbers on cards
    vs sweepstake: a game of chance in which numbered balls are drawn at random and players mark the corresponding numbers on their cards, hoping to complete a winning pattern
