umami bomb

noun
dish with intense umami flavor
Pronunciation: / uˈmɑːmi bɑm / (AmE), / uˈmɑːmi bɒm / (BrE)
a dish or food with an exceptionally intense and rich umami (savory) flavor

Examples

An umami bomb dish
He called the ramen broth an umami bomb, thanks to the combination of soy and mushrooms.
That miso-glazed cod is an umami bomb with every bite packed full of savory flavor.
The mushroom risotto was an umami bomb that delighted everyone at the table.
