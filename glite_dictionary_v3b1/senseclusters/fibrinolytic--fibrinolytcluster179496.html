fibrinolytic

adjective
breaks down blood clots
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbrənoʊˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbrɪnəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the breakdown of blood clots in the body

Examples

Fibrinolytic therapy
The patient received a fibrinolytic injection to help dissolve the dangerous clot.
Doctors may use fibrinolytic therapy in cases of acute heart attack or stroke.
A fibrinolytic agent can speed up the natural process of breaking down blood clots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticoagulant - a substance that prevents blood clotting
  • antonym: procoagulant - a substance that promotes blood clotting
