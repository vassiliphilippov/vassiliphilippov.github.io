in situ

adjective, adverb
in the original place or position
7.8 5.9
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈsiːtu /; / ɪn ˈsiːtuː / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsɪtjuː / (BrE)
in the original or natural place or position; not moved or relocated
