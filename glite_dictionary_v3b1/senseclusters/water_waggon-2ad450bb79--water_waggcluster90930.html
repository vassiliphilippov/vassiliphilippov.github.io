water waggon

noun
wagon for carrying and distributing water
2.5 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər ˈwæ.gən/ /; / /ˈɑː.tər ˈwæ.gən/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə ˈwæ.gən/ (BrE)
a wagon or cart designed to carry and distribute water, especially for supplying work crews, troops, or dampening dusty roads

Examples

A water waggon supplies water
A water waggon supplied fresh water to the troops stationed in the camp.
The dusty road was sprayed by a water waggon each morning to keep the air clear.
Workers relied on the water waggon to provide drinking water during the hot summer.
