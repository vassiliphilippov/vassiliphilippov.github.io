water waggon

noun
wagon for carrying and distributing water
6.2 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ît…ö Àåw√¶…°…ôn / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêt…ô Àåw√¶…°…ôn / (BrE)
a wagon or cart designed to carry and distribute water, especially for supplying work crews, troops, or dampening dusty roads

Examples

A water waggon supplies water
A water waggon supplied fresh water to the troops stationed in the camp.
The dusty road was sprayed by a water waggon each morning to keep the air clear.
Workers relied on the water waggon to provide drinking water during the hot summer.

Synonyms

  • water wagon
    5.1 0.00/m
    a water wagon carries and spreads water for troops or workers, while a water waggon is the same term with a different spelling.
  • watering cart
    3.2 0.00/m
    a watering cart sprays water with a tank and sprinkler to control dust, while a water waggon supplies water to crews or troops.
  • water cart
    4.3 0.00/m
    a water cart carries tanked water often for sale to the public, while a water waggon mainly supplies workers, troops, or dampens roads.
