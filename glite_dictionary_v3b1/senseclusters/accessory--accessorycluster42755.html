accessory

noun
item worn to complement clothing
1.0
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛsəri / (AmE), / əkˈsɛsəri / (BrE)
An item, such as a piece of jewelry, scarf, or bag, that is worn or carried to complement or enhance an outfit.

Examples

A fashion accessory
A colorful scarf is my favorite winter accessory.
Hats can be both a practical and stylish accessory.
She chose a gold necklace as the perfect accessory to her evening gown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adornment - something that decorates or adds beauty, especially in clothing
