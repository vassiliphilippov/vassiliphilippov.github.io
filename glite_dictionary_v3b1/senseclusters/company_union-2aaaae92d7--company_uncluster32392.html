company union

noun
a union representing workers of one company
7.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk åm.p…ô.ni ÀàjuÀê.nj…ôn / (AmE), / Ààk åm.p…ô.ni ÀàjuÀê.nj…ôn / (BrE)
a labor union that represents the workers of a single company and is independent from national or international unions, often controlled or influenced by the employer

Examples

A company union
After the strike, a company union was established to handle minor grievances internally.
Laws in some countries prohibit the formation of a company union controlled by the employer.
The workers were skeptical of the company union, suspecting it was influenced by management.
