punch bowl

noun
large bowl for serving beverages
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃ ˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃ ˌbəʊl / (BrE)
A large, deep bowl used for serving drinks, especially punch, typically accompanied by a ladle.

Examples

A punch bowl with ladle
After the wedding toast, everyone gathered around the punch bowl for a celebratory drink.
She ladled the fruit punch from the punch bowl into small cups for the guests.
The sparkling lemonade was served in a crystal punch bowl at the center of the table.
