belief system

noun
set of guiding principles or beliefs

A set of principles or tenets that form the basis of a particular philosophy, religion, or perspective.

Examples

A belief system framework
Different cultures often have distinct belief systems shaping their values and customs.
He questioned every assumption within his own belief system.
Her political opinions are influenced by her belief system.

Synonyms

  • philosophy
    4.4 39/m
    philosophy is a set of guiding ideas about life used by a person or group, while belief system is a broader organized set of principles.
  • doctrine
    6.0 32/m
    doctrine is a formal set of taught beliefs used by a religious or political group, while belief system is a wider set of guiding principles.
  • ideology
    6.3 34/m
    ideology is a system of political or social ideas that guides groups, while belief system covers any set of principles, not just political ones.
  • school of thought
    3.9 2.09/m
    a school of thought is a shared academic way of thinking among experts, while belief system is a broader set of principles guiding people's views.
