manipulate

verb
handle or move by hand
3.2
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪpjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / məˈnɪpjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to handle or move something with the hands, especially with skill

Examples

To manipulate objects

Technicians manipulate tiny components under a microscope in the laboratory.
The surgeon carefully manipulated the delicate tissue during the procedure.
