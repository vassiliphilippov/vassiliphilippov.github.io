interrelated

adjective
mutually connected or related
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪntərɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having a mutual or reciprocal relationship; connected to one another in such a way that each affects or depends on the other.

Examples

Issues interrelated closely
Many of the issues in the report are interrelated and cannot be solved in isolation.
The body’s systems are highly interrelated, meaning changes in one affect the others.
Their research explores how economic and environmental factors are deeply interrelated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: related - connected by a relationship or association
  • antonym: unrelated - not connected or associated
