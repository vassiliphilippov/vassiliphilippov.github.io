Aruba

proper noun
Caribbean island and tourist destination
1.2
Pronunciation: / əˈruːbə / (AmE), / əˈruːbə / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean Sea, part of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, known as a popular tourist destination, with its capital at Oranjestad.

Examples
