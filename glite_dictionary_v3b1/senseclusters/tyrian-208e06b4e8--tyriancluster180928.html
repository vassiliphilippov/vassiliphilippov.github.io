Tyrian

adjective
of Tyre or its dye
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪriən / (AmE), / ˈtɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the ancient city of Tyre or to its famous purple dye

Examples

Tyrian purple cloth
The ancient kings wore Tyrian robes as a symbol of their wealth and power.
Archaeologists discovered fragments of Tyrian pottery near the old harbor.
The artist chose a Tyrian shade to capture the luxury of the ancient world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Phoenician - relating to the ancient civilization of Phoenicia
  • synonym: purple - a color associated with royalty and luxury, often linked to Tyrian dye
