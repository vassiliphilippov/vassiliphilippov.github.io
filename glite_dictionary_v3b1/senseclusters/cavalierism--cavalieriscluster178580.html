cavalierism

noun
haughty disregard
Pronunciation: / ˌkævəˈlɪrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌkævəˈlɪərɪzəm / (BrE)
an attitude or behavior showing a lack of concern or a haughty disregard for others

Examples

His cavalierism at work
His cavalierism during meetings often offended his coworkers.
The manager's cavalierism led to poor team morale and communication.
She was criticized for her cavalierism in handling customer complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - an attitude of superiority and self-importance
  • antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
