garrotte

noun
instrument for execution by strangulation
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɑt / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɒt / (BrE)
A device used for execution by strangulation, typically consisting of a cord or wire tightened around the neck.

Examples

An iron garrotte
A garrotte was once used in Spain as a method of capital punishment.
Historically, the garrotte was a feared instrument during public executions.
The executioner prepared the garrotte before the condemned man was brought in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guillotine - a device for execution by beheading
  • synonym: noose - a loop with a running knot, used for execution by hanging
