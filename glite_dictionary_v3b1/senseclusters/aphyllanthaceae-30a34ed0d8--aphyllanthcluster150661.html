Aphyllanthaceae

noun
a plant family in some classifications
Pronunciation: / əˌfɪlænˈθeɪsi.iː /; / əˌfɪlænˈθeɪsiː / (AmE), / əˌfɪlænˈθeɪsɪ.iː /; / əˌfɪlænˈθeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, sometimes considered separate from the Liliaceae family, that includes the genus Aphyllanthes

Examples
