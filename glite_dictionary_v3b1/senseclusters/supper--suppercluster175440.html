supper

noun
evening social meal event
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌpɚ /; / ˈsʌp.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌpə /; / ˈsʌp.ə / (BrE)
a light evening meal, sometimes as a social event

Examples

Light supper meal
At their weekend supper, guests enjoyed light dishes and lively conversation.
She held an elegant supper for her colleagues at her home on Friday night.
The annual charity supper helped raise funds for the community center.
Many people prefer a light supper instead of a heavy dinner in the evening.
She invited us over for supper after a long day at work.
We had a simple supper of soup and bread before going to bed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breakfast - the first meal of the day, eaten in the morning
  • synonym: dinner - main meal of the day, often social; similar event with a heavier meal
