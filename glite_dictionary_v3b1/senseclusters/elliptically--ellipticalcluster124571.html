elliptically

adverb
indirectly, concisely
7.8
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlɪp.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ɪ.ˈlɪp.tɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlɪp.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ɪ.ˈlɪp.tɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
in a way that is indirect or uses few words, often leaving things implied rather than stated directly

Examples

Answers elliptically
The politician responded elliptically to avoid giving a clear answer to the reporter's question.
He spoke elliptically, hinting at the truth without saying it outright.
Her instructions were given elliptically, so the team had to guess what she really meant.
