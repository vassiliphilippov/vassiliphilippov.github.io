boogan

noun
mythical prankster creature
Pronunciation: / ˈbuː.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈbuː.ɡən / (BrE)
In folklore, a mischievous or troublesome mythical creature often blamed for minor mishaps or pranks.

Examples

A boogan creature
Old stories warn travelers not to anger a boogan in the woods.
Some say that every creaking floorboard is the work of a boogan up to mischief.
The children insisted a boogan was responsible for the missing cookies.
