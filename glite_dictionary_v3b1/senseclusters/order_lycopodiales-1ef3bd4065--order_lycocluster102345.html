order Lycopodiales

noun
order of lower vascular plants
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrdər laɪˌkɒpəˈdaɪəliːz / (AmE), / ˌɔːdə laɪˌkɒpəˈdaɪəliːz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of lower vascular plants, including the family Lycopodiaceae; in some classifications, it also includes Selaginellaceae and Isoetaceae.

Examples

The order Lycopodiales plants
Clubmosses are classified within the order Lycopodiales in most plant taxonomies.
Some classification systems expand the order Lycopodiales to include the Selaginellaceae and Isoetaceae families.
The order Lycopodiales consists largely of small, spore-producing vascular plants.
