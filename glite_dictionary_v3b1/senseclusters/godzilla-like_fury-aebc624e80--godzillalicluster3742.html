Godzilla-like fury

noun
extremely intense, destructive rage
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɡɑdˈzɪl.ə laɪk ˈfjʊr.i / (AmE), / ɡɒdˈzɪl.ə laɪk ˈfjʊə.ri / (BrE)
