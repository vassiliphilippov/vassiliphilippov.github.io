manual dexterity

noun
skill in using the hands
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmænjuəl dɛkˈstɛrəti / (AmE), / ˈmanjuəl dɛkˈstɛrəti / (BrE)
skill and ease in using the hands to perform tasks accurately and efficiently

Examples

High manual dexterity
A surgeon needs exceptional manual dexterity to perform delicate procedures.
Children can improve their manual dexterity by playing with building blocks and puzzles.
The art of calligraphy requires great manual dexterity and patience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of skill in movement or handling objects
