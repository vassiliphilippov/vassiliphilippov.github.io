Cleistes rosea

noun
South American orchid with rose-colored flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkliː.stiːz ˈroʊ.zi.ə/ /; / /ˈklaɪ.stiːz ˈroʊ.zi.ə/ (AmE), /ˈkliː.stiːz ˈrəʊ.zi.ə/ /; / /ˈklaɪ.stiːz ˈrəʊ.zi.ə/ (BrE)
