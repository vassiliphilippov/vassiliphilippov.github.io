retainment

noun
act of keeping
Pronunciation: / rɪˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act of keeping or holding something or someone so that it is not lost or taken away

Examples

Retainment of staff
The retainment of skilled employees is crucial for the company's success.
Effective retainment of information helps students perform better on exams.
Policies were introduced to improve the retainment of customers over time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loss - the state of no longer having something
  • synonym: retention - the continued possession or control of something
