tenselessness

noun
absence of tension
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛnsləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of being free from physical or emotional tension; a relaxed condition

Examples

A feeling of tenselessness
After the yoga class, I felt a deep tenselessness wash over my shoulders and back.
The peaceful music brought a sense of tenselessness to the entire room.
Meditation can help you achieve tenselessness even during stressful times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relaxation - a state of being free from tension or anxiety
  • antonym: tension - the state of being stretched tight or feeling mental strain
