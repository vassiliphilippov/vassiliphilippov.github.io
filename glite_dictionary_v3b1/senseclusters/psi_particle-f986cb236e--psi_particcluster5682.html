psi particle

noun
type of heavy neutral meson
Pronunciation: / saɪ ˈpɑːɹtɪkəl / (AmE), / saɪ ˈpɑːtɪkəl / (BrE)
A type of neutral meson with a large mass, discovered in particle physics and identified as the J/psi particle.

Examples

The psi particle
Researchers used special detectors to identify the psi particle in high-energy collisions.
The discovery of the psi particle was a major breakthrough in particle physics.
The mass of the psi particle distinguishes it from many other mesons.
