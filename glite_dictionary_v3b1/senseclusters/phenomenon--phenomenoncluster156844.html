phenomenon

noun
observable event or fact
1.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɑːməˌnɑːn / (AmE), / fəˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
an event, occurrence, or fact that can be observed or experienced, especially one that is perceptible by the senses

Examples

A natural phenomenon
Economic growth is a complex phenomenon influenced by multiple factors.
The Northern Lights are a natural phenomenon that many travel far to witness.
This strange phenomenon was later explained by advances in physics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: event - something that happens or takes place
  • synonym: process - a series of actions or steps taken to achieve a result
