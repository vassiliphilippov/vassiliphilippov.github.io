blondie

noun
vanilla dessert bar
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑndi / (AmE), / ˈblɒndi / (BrE)
a small, dense, cake-like dessert bar similar to a brownie but made without cocoa, usually flavored with vanilla or brown sugar

Examples

A sweet blondie
She brought a tray of homemade blondies to the office party, and they disappeared quickly.
The blondie was chewy and sweet, with a hint of caramel from the brown sugar.
If you prefer vanilla over chocolate, you might enjoy a blondie instead of a brownie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brownie - a small, dense, chocolate-flavored dessert bar
  • synonym: cookie bar - a baked dessert bar made from cookie dough
