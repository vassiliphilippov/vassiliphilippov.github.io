Examples

Hard labor at work
Completing the bridge was a massive labor that required teamwork and persistence.
The scientist devoted years of labor to his groundbreaking research.
Their greatest labor was rebuilding the village after the storm.
After a day of intense labor, she was exhausted but satisfied.

Writing the manuscript was a true labour of love.

Synonyms

  • work
    1.0 463/m
    work is the general activity of doing a job, while labor often means physical or effortful work.
  • hard work
    1.6 18/m
    hard work means long, difficult effort to reach a goal, while labor refers to work or a physical task.
  • toil
    5.8 1.35/m
    toil is long, exhausting physical work that wears you out, while labor can mean work in general.
  • effort
    1.1 31/m
    effort is the energy or attempt someone uses to do something, while labor is the work or task they perform.
  • exertion
    5.0 3.66/m
    exertion is the physical or mental strain of using energy, while labor is the completed work or task that requires effort.
  • task
    1.0 156/m
    task is a single piece of work or duty assigned to someone, while labor means work or effort, often larger or physical.
