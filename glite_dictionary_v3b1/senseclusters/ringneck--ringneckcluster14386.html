ringneck

noun
parrot with neck ring
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (BrE)
a species of parrot, especially the Indian ringneck, known for the colored ring around its neck and often kept as a pet

Examples

Ringneck parrot
The bright green ringneck perched happily on its owner's shoulder.
Many people choose a ringneck for its ability to mimic human speech.
A colorful ringneck can make a lively addition to any household.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parakeet - a small to medium-sized parrot with a long tail
