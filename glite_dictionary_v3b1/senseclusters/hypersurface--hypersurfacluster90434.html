hypersurface

noun
surface in higher dimensions
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.pər.sɜːr.fɪs/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.pər.sɜːr.fəs/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.pə.sɜː.fɪs/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.pə.sɜː.fəs/ (BrE)

Examples

In advanced geometry, a hypersurface can exist in spaces with more than three dimensions.
