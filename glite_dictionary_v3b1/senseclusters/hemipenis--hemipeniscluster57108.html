hemipenis

noun
reptile reproductive organ
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmiˈpiːnɪs / (AmE), / ˌhɛmiˈpiːnɪs / (BrE)
one of a pair of male reproductive organs found in some reptiles, such as snakes and lizards

Examples

The hemipenis of lizards
The biologist carefully examined the hemipenis of the lizard during the study.
Male snakes possess a hemipenis on each side of their body for reproduction.
Researchers discovered differences in the structure of the hemipenis among various reptile species.
