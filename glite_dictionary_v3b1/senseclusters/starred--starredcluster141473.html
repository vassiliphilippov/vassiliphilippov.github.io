starred

adjective
saved/favourited online
3.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘rd / (AmE), / stÉ‘Ëd / (BrE)
(in online contexts) marked, saved, or favored by a user, typically by clicking a star icon to highlight or bookmark an item.

Examples

Starred messages online
The project was starred by thousands of developers on GitHub.
She moved the important message to her starred folder to read later.
He starred the recipe on the cooking site so he could find it quickly.
