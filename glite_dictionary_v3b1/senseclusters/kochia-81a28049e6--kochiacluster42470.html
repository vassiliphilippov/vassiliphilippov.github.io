Kochia

noun
a genus of ornamental plants (summer cypress)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊʃi.ə /; / ˈkoʊkiə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊʃɪə /; / ˈkəʊkiə / (BrE)
a genus of plants in the goosefoot family, often called summer cypress and grown for ornamental purposes

Examples
