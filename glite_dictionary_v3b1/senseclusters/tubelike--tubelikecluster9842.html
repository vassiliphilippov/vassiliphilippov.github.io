tubelike

adjective
resembling a tube in shape or function
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the form or qualities of a tube; hollow and cylindrical, often for the passage of fluids.

Examples

Tubelike structure
She examined the tubelike structures under the microscope.
Some sea creatures have tubelike bodies to allow water to flow through them.
The plant has tubelike stems that help it store water.
