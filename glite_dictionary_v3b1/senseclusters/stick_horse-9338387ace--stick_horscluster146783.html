stick horse

noun
toy horse's head on a stick
Pronunciation: / stɪk hɔrs / (AmE), / stɪk hɔːs / (BrE)
a child's toy consisting of a horse's head attached to the end of a stick, on which a child can pretend to ride

Examples

A stick horse toy
The old photograph showed her holding a homemade stick horse with a painted face.
We found a forgotten stick horse in the attic covered in dust.
When he was little, he used to gallop around the yard on his stick horse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hobby horse - a toy similar to a stick horse, often with a horse's head at one end of a stick ridden by children
