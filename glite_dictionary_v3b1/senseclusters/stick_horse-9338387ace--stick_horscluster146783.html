stick horse

noun
toy horse's head on a stick
3.9 2.0
Pronunciation: /stɪk hɔːrs/ (AmE), /stɪk hɔːs/ (BrE)
a child's toy consisting of a horse's head attached to the end of a stick, on which a child can pretend to ride

Examples

A stick horse toy

When he was little, he used to gallop around the yard on his stick horse.
