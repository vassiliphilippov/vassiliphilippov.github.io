aptness

noun
tendency or disposition
Pronunciation: / ˈæpt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈæpt.nəs / (BrE)
a natural tendency or disposition to behave in a particular way or for something to happen

Examples

An aptness for music
Her aptness for learning new languages made her an outstanding translator.
The aptness of children to imitate adults can surprise their parents.
There is a certain aptness in the way rumors spread quickly in small communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclination - a tendency to feel or behave in a particular way
  • synonym: propensity - a natural inclination or tendency
