modesty

noun
lack of pride or boastfulness
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdəsti / (AmE), / ˈmɒdəsti / (BrE)
the quality of not being too proud or boastful about oneself or one's abilities; freedom from vanity or conceit

Examples

His modesty in victory
Despite his achievements, he always spoke with modesty.
Her modesty prevented her from taking credit for the team's success.
People admired his modesty as much as his talent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrogance - the quality of being overly proud or having an exaggerated sense of self-importance
  • synonym: humility - the quality of having a modest or low view of one's importance
