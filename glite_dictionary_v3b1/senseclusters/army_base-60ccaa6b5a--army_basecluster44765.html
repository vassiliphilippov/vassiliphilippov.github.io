army base

noun
military installation for an army
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmi beɪs / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi beɪs / (BrE)
A large military installation where army personnel are stationed and operations are conducted.

Examples

The army base camp
Families live alongside soldiers on the army base in well-organized residential quarters.
Security at the army base was tightened following last week’s incident.
The new recruits arrived at the army base early in the morning for training.
