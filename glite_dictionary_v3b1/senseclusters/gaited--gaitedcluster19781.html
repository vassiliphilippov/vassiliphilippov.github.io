gaited

adjective
having a special gait
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a particular way of walking, especially referring to horses with a distinctive or smooth gait

Examples

Gaited horses
The farm specializes in gaited horses known for their smooth ride.
Many riders prefer gaited breeds because they are more comfortable over long distances.
She admired the gaited stallion as it moved gracefully across the field.
