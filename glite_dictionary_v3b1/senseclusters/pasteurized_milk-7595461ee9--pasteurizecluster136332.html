pasteurized milk

noun
milk treated by heating to kill germs
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpæst.ʃə.raɪzd mɪlk/ /; / /ˈpeɪst.ʃə.raɪzd mɪlk/ (AmE), /ˈpæst.ʃə.raɪzd mɪlk/ /; / /ˈpeɪst.ʃə.raɪzd mɪlk/ (BrE)

Examples

Pasteurized milk safety

Unlike raw milk, pasteurized milk has been treated to kill bacteria.
