nonroyal

adjective
not royal
5.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈrɔɪəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈrɔɪəl / (BrE)
not related to, belonging to, or associated with a king, queen, or royal family

Examples

Nonroyal tombs
The exhibition focused on nonroyal tombs to show how ordinary people lived in ancient Egypt.
Many nonroyal families contributed to the country's history through their inventions and discoveries.
Unlike the palace, the nonroyal residences were much simpler and less decorated.
