tent-fly

noun
canvas flap as tent entrance
8.4
Pronunciation: / tɛnt flaɪ / (AmE), / tɛnt flaɪ / (BrE)
A flap made of canvas or similar material that can be opened or closed to provide entrance to a tent.

Examples

The tent-fly flap
Please close the tent-fly before it starts to rain.
She peered out from behind the tent-fly to check if breakfast was ready.
The wind blew the tent-fly open in the middle of the night.
