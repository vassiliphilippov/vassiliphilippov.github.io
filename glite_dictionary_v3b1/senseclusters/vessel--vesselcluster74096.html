vessel

noun
tube for fluid circulation in the body
4.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈves.əl/ (AmE), /ˈves.əl/ (BrE)
a tube in the body through which a fluid such as blood or lymph circulates

Examples

Blood vessel
An artery is a type of vessel that carries blood away from the heart.
Blockages in a blood vessel can cause serious medical problems.
The surgeon repaired the damaged vessel to restore proper circulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artery - a blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart
  • synonym: capillary - a tiny blood vessel connecting arteries and veins
  • synonym: vein - a blood vessel that carries blood toward the heart
