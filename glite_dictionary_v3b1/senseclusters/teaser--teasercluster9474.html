teaser

noun
a challenging puzzle or problem
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːzɚ / (AmE), / ˈtiːzə / (BrE)
a difficult problem or puzzle, especially one intended to test someone's ingenuity or knowledge

Examples

A tricky teaser
Solving the latest mathematical teaser kept her occupied for hours.
That crossword is a real teaser—I can't figure out any of the clues.
The newspaper published a challenging teaser for its readers to solve over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conundrum - a confusing and difficult problem or question
  • synonym: puzzle - a game, problem, or toy that tests a person's ingenuity or knowledge
  • synonym: riddle - a question or statement intentionally phrased to require ingenuity in ascertaining its answer
