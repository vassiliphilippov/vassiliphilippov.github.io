positive

adjective
affirmative or approving in attitude or outlook
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːzɪtɪv /; / ˈpɑːzətɪv /; / ˈpɑzətɪv /; / ˈpɑzɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɒzɪtɪv /; / ˈpɒzətɪv / (BrE)
showing certainty, confidence, or an approving/constructive attitude

Examples

Positive attitude
All the feedback we received about the project was overwhelmingly positive.
His response was positive, assuring us that everything would be fine.
She always tries to maintain a positive outlook, even during difficult times.
He gave a positive answer that left no room for doubt.
Scientists require positive results to support their claims.
The police needed positive proof before making an arrest.
I'm positive I left my keys on the kitchen table.
She sounded positive that the meeting would be a success.
Tom was positive he had seen the movie before.
