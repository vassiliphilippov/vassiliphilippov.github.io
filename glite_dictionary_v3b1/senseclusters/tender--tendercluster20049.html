tender

noun
person who takes care of another
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to look after or take care of something or someone

Examples

A tender for children
Each child was assigned a personal tender during their hospital stay.
He worked as a bar tender in the busy downtown pub.
The animal tender made sure every enclosure was clean and the animals were healthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attendant - a person who provides a service or attends to others
  • synonym: caretaker - a person employed to look after people or animals
