go through

verb
examine or consider thoroughly
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈθru / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈθruː / (BrE)
to consider or examine something carefully and in detail; to work through thoroughly.

Examples

To go through the list
Let's go through the list one more time to be sure we didn't miss anything.
She asked her accountant to go through her tax returns carefully.
The teacher will go through the instructions step by step before we begin the test.
