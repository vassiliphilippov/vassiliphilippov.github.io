softball

noun
a team sport like baseball played with a larger ball
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game similar to baseball, played on a smaller field with a larger ball

Examples

Play softball
He prefers softball over baseball because the games are usually more relaxed.
She has played softball since she was a child and loves pitching for her local team.
The school organized a softball tournament for students of all ages.

Related Terms
