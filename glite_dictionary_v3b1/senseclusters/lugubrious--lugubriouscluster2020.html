lugubrious

adjective
excessively sad or gloomy
9.2 7.5
Pronunciation: /luː.ˈguː.brɪ.əs/ /; / /lə.ˈgjuː.brɪ.əs/ (AmE), /luː.ˈgjuː.brɪ.əs/ /; / /lʊ.ˈgjuː.brɪ.əs/ (BrE)
looking or sounding sad and dismal, especially in an exaggerated or affected manner

Examples

A lugubrious expression

She spoke in such a lugubrious manner that it dampened the mood of the whole room.
The movie’s pace and lugubrious tone left the audience feeling somewhat depressed.
