filer

noun
tool for smoothing
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪlə / (BrE)
a tool or device used to smooth or shape surfaces by rubbing, especially in woodworking or metalworking

Examples

Metal filer
She used a filer to smooth the edges of the wooden board.
The metalworker reached for his filer to finish the sharp corners on the bracket.
After cutting the plastic, he used a filer to remove any rough spots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: file - a hand tool with a rough surface for smoothing or shaping
  • synonym: sandpaper - paper coated with abrasive material for smoothing surfaces
