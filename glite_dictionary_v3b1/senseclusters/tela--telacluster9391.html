tela

noun
woven fabric or web
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlə / (AmE), / ˈtiːlə / (BrE)
a piece of woven cloth, fabric, or a web-like material; sometimes used in literary or archaic contexts to mean a web or net

Examples

A silk tela
The artisan draped a fine silk tela over the statue to protect it from dust.
In the poem, the moonlight shone through the delicate tela of the spider's web.
The ancient tapestry was woven from a golden tela that shimmered in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloth - a piece of fabric used for a particular purpose
  • synonym: fabric - a material made by weaving or knitting fibers
  • synonym: web - a fine net made by a spider or something resembling it
