tela

noun
woven fabric or web
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààte…™l…ô / (AmE), / ÀàtiÀêl…ô / (BrE)

Examples

The ancient tapestry was woven from a golden tela that shimmered in the sunlight.
