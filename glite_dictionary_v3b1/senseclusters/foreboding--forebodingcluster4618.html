foreboding

noun
a sense of impending misfortune
8.1 6.4
Pronunciation: /fɔːr.ˈboʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /fɔː.ˈbəʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
a strong feeling or sense that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen

Examples

A foreboding feeling

He had a strong foreboding that something terrible was about to happen.

  • synonym: presentiment - an intuitive feeling about the future, especially something bad
