cocaine addiction

noun
compulsive dependence on cocaine
3.8 5.2
Pronunciation: /kəʊ.ˈkeɪn ə.ˈdɪk.ʃən/ /; / /koʊ.ˈkeɪn ə.ˈdɪk.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈkeɪn ə.ˈdɪk.ʃən/ (BrE)
a condition in which a person is unable to stop using cocaine, characterized by compulsive drug-seeking and use despite harmful consequences

Examples

Cocaine addiction treatment
Healthcare professionals are trained to recognize the signs of cocaine addiction.
His struggle with cocaine addiction lasted for many years before he sought help.
Overcoming cocaine addiction often requires therapy and strong support systems.
