regent

adjective
temporarily ruling in place of a monarch
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənt / (BrE)
Acting as a regent; temporarily ruling in place of a monarch or leader.

Examples

Regent government
A regent authority managed all royal affairs until the rightful heir came of age.
During her son's minority, she served as the regent queen of the country.
The regent government implemented new policies during the king's absence.
