spreadable

adjective
able to be transmitted
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be transmitted or passed from one person, animal, or place to another; contagious.

Examples

Spreadable virus
The flu is highly spreadable in crowded places like schools and offices.
Doctors are concerned about how spreadable the new virus variant might be.
Washing your hands can help prevent spreadable diseases from infecting others.
