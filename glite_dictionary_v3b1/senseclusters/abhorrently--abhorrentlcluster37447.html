abhorrently

adverb
disgustingly
Pronunciation: / əbˈhɔrəntli / (AmE), / əbˈhɒrəntli / (BrE)
in a way that causes or shows strong dislike, disgust, or moral opposition

Examples

To abhorrently behave
The dictator ruled abhorrently, showing no mercy to his people.
Many found the comedian's remarks abhorrently insensitive and offensive.
The animals were treated abhorrently in the illegal facility, sparking outrage.
The community reacted abhorrently to the news of the environmental disaster.
She spoke abhorrently about the injustices she had witnessed.
Many people view such acts abhorrently and demand strict punishment.

  • synonym: disgustedly - in a way that shows strong dislike or revulsion
  • synonym: offensively - in a way that causes someone to feel deeply hurt or upset
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a way that is enjoyable or pleasing
  • synonym: repulsively - in a way that is extremely unpleasant or disgusting
