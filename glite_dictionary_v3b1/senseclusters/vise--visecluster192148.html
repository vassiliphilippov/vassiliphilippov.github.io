vise

verb
to approve a document
Pronunciation: / vaɪz / (AmE), / vaɪz / (BrE)
to give official approval or endorsement to a document, especially by signing or stamping it

Examples

To vise a document

All travel documents must be vised by the embassy before your trip.
She checked and vised the application to confirm it met all requirements.
