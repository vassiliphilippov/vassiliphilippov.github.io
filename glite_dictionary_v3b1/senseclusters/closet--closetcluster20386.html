closet

noun
furniture for hanging clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːzət / (AmE), / ˈklɒzɪt / (BrE)
a small room, space, or piece of furniture for storing clothes

Examples

A closet for clothes
After moving in, he assembled a new closet to organize his clothes.
She opened the closet and selected a dress for the evening.
There are extra blankets stored in the hallway closet.
After cleaning out the closet, she donated her old clothes to charity.
She hung her coat neatly in the closet by the door.
The apartment has a large closet perfect for storing linens and shoes.
