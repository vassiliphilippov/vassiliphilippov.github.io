chimney swift

noun
North American bird that nests in chimneys
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmni swɪft / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni swɪft / (BrE)
A small American bird of the swift family, commonly nesting in chimneys and similar structures.

Examples

A chimney swift bird
A flock of chimney swifts circled rapidly above the old warehouse at sunset.
Each spring, a pair of chimney swifts returns to nest in our unused fireplace.
The distinctive chittering of chimney swifts filled the air as they hunted for insects.

  • synonym: swift - a small, fast-flying bird
