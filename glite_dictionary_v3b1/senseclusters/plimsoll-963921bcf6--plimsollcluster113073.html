Examples

The Plimsoll line

Synonyms

  • Plimsoll mark
    9.9 0.00/m
    Plimsoll mark is the painted symbol on a ship's hull that shows safe loading, while Plimsoll is the common name sailors use.
  • Plimsoll line
    9.0 0.00/m
    Plimsoll line marks the maximum waterline when a ship is loaded, while Plimsoll is the familiar name for that mark.

    6.7 0.00/m
