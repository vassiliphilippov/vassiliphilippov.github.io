nil

verb
to score zero
Pronunciation: / nɪl / (AmE), / nɪl / (BrE)
to score zero points in a game or competition; to be marked with a value of zero.

Examples

Team nilled
The visiting team was nilled by the strong defense and failed to score any points.
Despite their efforts, the underdogs were nilled in the championship match.
It is rare for a top-ranked player to be nilled in a tournament round.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blank - to fail to score any points
  • antonym: score - to gain points in a game or competition
