seamer

noun
cricket bowler or machine
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmər / (AmE), / ˈsiːmə / (BrE)
a bowler in cricket who makes the ball move off the seam, or a machine that creates seams in materials

Examples

Cricket seamer
The experienced seamer exploited the damp pitch to trouble the batsmen.
A new seamer was installed in the factory to improve production speed.
He is known as a skillful seamer who can swing the ball both ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fast bowler - a cricket bowler who delivers the ball at high speed
  • antonym: spinner - a cricket bowler who spins the ball to make it turn
