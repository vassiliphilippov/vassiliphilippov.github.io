John Llewellyn Lewis

proper noun
prominent American labor leader (1880–1969)
8.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈluːəlɪn ˈluːɪs / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈluːəlɪn ˈluːɪs / (BrE)
(1880–1969) American labor leader who served as president of the United Mine Workers of America (1920–1960) and president of the Congress of Industrial Organizations (1935–1940).

John Llewellyn Lewis labor leader
