leucojum

noun
bulbous flowering plant
Pronunciation: / luːˈkoʊdʒəm / (AmE), / luːˈkəʊdʒəm / (BrE)
a genus of bulbous plants with white, bell-shaped flowers, commonly called snowflakes

Examples

The leucojum flower
The leucojum adds a delicate touch to the spring garden with its white, bell-shaped flowers.
Gardeners often plant leucojum bulbs in autumn to enjoy their early blooms.
Unlike snowdrops, leucojum flowers are larger and appear later in the season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snowdrop - a small white-flowered bulbous plant of the genus Galanthus
