calabar-bean vine

noun
woody African vine yielding calabar beans
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˌbɑrˈbin vaɪn / (AmE), / ˌkaləˌbɑːˈbiːn vaɪn / (BrE)
a tropical African woody vine (Physostigma venenosum) known for producing calabar beans, which are used in medicine and historically as an ordeal poison
