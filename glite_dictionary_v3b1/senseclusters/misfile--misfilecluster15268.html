misfile

noun
wrongly filed document
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a document or record that has been placed in the wrong file or folder by mistake

Examples

A misfile in records
The missing contract was found as a misfile in the wrong cabinet.
Auditors spent hours searching for a single misfile that delayed the report.
A simple misfile can cause confusion and slow down office work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: file - a collection of documents kept together
  • synonym: misplacement - the act of putting something in the wrong place
