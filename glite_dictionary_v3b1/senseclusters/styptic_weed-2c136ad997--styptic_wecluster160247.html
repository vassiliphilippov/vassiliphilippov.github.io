styptic weed

noun
tropical shrub; seeds used to adulterate coffee
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪptɪk wid / (AmE), / ˈstɪptɪk wiːd / (BrE)
a leafy, strong-smelling tropical shrub (Senna occidentalis) whose seeds have sometimes been used as a coffee adulterant; also called coffee senna

Examples

The styptic weed plant
Botanists sometimes classify styptic weed in the genus Cassia due to its characteristics.
She was surprised by the pungent odor of the styptic weed while hiking through the tropics.
The seeds of the styptic weed have been used to adulterate coffee in some regions.
