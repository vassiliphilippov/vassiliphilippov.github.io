streamliner

noun
streamlined train
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmˌlaɪnər /; / ˈstriːmlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈstriːmˌlaɪnə /; / ˈstriːmlaɪnə / (BrE)
