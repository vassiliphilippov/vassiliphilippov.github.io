portent

noun
a sign of something about to occur
8.7 0.87/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…îrt…õnt / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀêt…õnt / (BrE)
a sign or warning that something, especially something momentous or calamitous, is about to happen

Examples

A portent of disaster
In ancient times, comets were often interpreted as a portent of disaster.
Some people believe that a solar eclipse is a portent of significant change.
The sudden drop in temperature was seen as a portent of the coming storm.
