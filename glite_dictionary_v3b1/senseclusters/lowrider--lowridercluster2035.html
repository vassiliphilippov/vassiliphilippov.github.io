lowrider

noun
car culture enthusiast
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌraɪdə / (BrE)
a person who owns, drives, or participates in the lowrider car subculture, often customizing cars with lowered suspensions

Examples

Lowrider club member
The local lowrider club organized a parade featuring their beautifully customized cars.
As a proud lowrider, he spent hours perfecting the look of his vehicle.
Many young people in the city look up to the experienced lowrider for advice on car modifications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: car enthusiast - someone passionate about cars and car culture
  • synonym: customizer - a person who modifies cars for style or performance
