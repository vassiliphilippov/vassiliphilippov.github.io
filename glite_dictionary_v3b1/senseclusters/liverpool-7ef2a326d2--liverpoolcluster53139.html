Liverpool

proper noun
major city and port in northwestern England
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvərˌpul / (AmE), / ˈlɪvəpuːl / (BrE)
a large city in northwestern England known for its major port, cultural heritage, and history as a center for industrial exports.
