squirreltail

noun
bushy squirrel tail
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːrəlteɪl / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəlteɪl / (BrE)
the long, bushy tail of a squirrel, often noted for its fluffiness and movement.

Examples

A squirrel's squirreltail
The squirreltail twitched rapidly as the animal searched for acorns in the park.
Children laughed as they watched the fluffy squirreltail disappear up the tree.
A squirreltail helps the animal balance while leaping from branch to branch.
