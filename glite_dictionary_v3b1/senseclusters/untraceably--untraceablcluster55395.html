untraceably

adverb
cannot be tracked
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtreɪsəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be tracked, discovered, or followed back to the source

Examples

Move untraceably online
The hacker moved the files untraceably across several servers to avoid detection.
She donated the money untraceably so that no one would know her identity.
Sensitive information was sent untraceably using encrypted channels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymously - in a way that does not reveal identity
  • antonym: traceably - in a way that can be tracked or discovered
