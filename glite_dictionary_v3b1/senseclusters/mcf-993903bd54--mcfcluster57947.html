MCF

noun
thousand cubic feet unit
Pronunciation: / ɛm si ɛf / (AmE), / ɛm si ɛf / (BrE)
a unit of measurement equal to one thousand cubic feet, commonly used for natural gas volumes

Examples

One MCF unit
The utility company billed us for 10 MCF of natural gas this month.
Natural gas prices are often quoted per MCF in industry reports.
Last winter, our household used over 15 MCF of gas for heating.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: MMCF - a unit of measurement equal to one million cubic feet
  • degree lower: cubic foot - a unit of volume equal to a cube with sides of one foot
