Easter Day

noun
the Christian festival day of Easter
1.0 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆiËstÉ™r deÉª / (AmE), / ËˆiËstÉ™ deÉª / (BrE)
The day, usually in March or April, on which the Christian festival of Easter is celebrated.

Examples

Easter Day celebration
He always prepares a special meal for friends and relatives on Easter Day.
Many families attend church services on Easter Day to celebrate the resurrection of Christ.
On Easter Day, children often hunt for painted eggs hidden in the garden.

Synonyms

  • Easter Sunday
    1.0 0.76/m
    Easter Sunday is the Sunday that marks the resurrection and the festival, while Easter Day names that specific celebration day.
  • Easter
    1.0 0.33/m
    Easter is the whole festival and holiday period, while Easter Day refers to the one specific day of celebration.
  • Pascha
    9.6 0.01/m
    Pascha is the name used in Eastern Christian traditions for the Easter festival, while Easter Day is the English term for that day.
