lauan

noun
Southeast Asian hardwood
Pronunciation: / laʊˈɑːn / (AmE), / laʊˈɑːn / (BrE)
a tropical hardwood tree of Southeast Asia, especially of the genus Shorea, valued for its timber

Examples

Lauan tree timber
The furniture was made from high-quality lauan wood imported from the Philippines.
Builders often use lauan plywood for its strength and smooth finish.
Many rainforests in Southeast Asia are home to the valuable lauan tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a tropical hardwood tree valued for its reddish-brown timber
  • synonym: meranti - a group of tropical hardwood trees from Southeast Asia, including Shorea species
