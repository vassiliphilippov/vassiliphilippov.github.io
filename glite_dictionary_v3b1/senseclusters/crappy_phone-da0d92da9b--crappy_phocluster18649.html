crappy phone

noun
poor-quality mobile phone
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkræpi foʊn / (AmE), / ˈkræpi fəʊn / (BrE)
A mobile phone that is of poor quality or performs badly.

Examples

A crappy phone
He was frustrated when his crappy phone froze during an important call.
I regret choosing such a crappy phone because the battery barely lasts a day.
Many people complain about buying a crappy phone from unreliable brands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crappy - of poor quality or bad; used informally
