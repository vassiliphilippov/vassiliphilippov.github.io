kneel

verb
rest or move onto knees
2.6 1.4
Pronunciation: / niːl / (AmE), / niːl / (BrE)
to be in or move into a position where your body is resting on your knees, especially as a sign of respect, worship, or submission

Examples

To kneel down
Every evening, he would kneel by his bed to pray.
She was kneeling on the floor, searching for her lost earring.
The bride and groom knelt at the altar during the ceremony.
