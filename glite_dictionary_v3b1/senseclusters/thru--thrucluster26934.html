thru

preposition
informal 'through'
8.3
Pronunciation: / θru / (AmE), / θru / (BrE)
informal spelling of 'through', used to mean up to and including a stated time or day

Examples

Thru Friday hours
The store is open Monday thru Friday from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
You can submit your application thru the end of the month.
The festival runs thru Sunday, so there is still time to attend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: through - up to and including a particular point in time
