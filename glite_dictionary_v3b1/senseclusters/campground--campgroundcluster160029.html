campground

noun
site for camping; place to pitch tents
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmp.ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkæmp.ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
an area set aside for people to pitch tents or park recreational vehicles, especially when on holiday

Examples

A campground by lake
Every year, the family gathers at the same campground by the lake.
The campground had clean bathrooms and plenty of space for tents.
We reserved a spot at the campground for our summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campsite - a place used for camping, especially by tents
