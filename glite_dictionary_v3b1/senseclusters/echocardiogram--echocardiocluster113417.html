echocardiogram

noun
ultrasound image of the heart
2.8 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkoʊˈkɑrdiəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˌɛkəʊˈkɑːdiəˌɡram / (BrE)
A diagnostic image of the heart produced by ultrasound, used to assess heart structure and function.

Examples

A echocardiogram image
A clear echocardiogram can help diagnose various heart conditions accurately.
After experiencing chest pain, Maria was scheduled for an echocardiogram at the hospital.
The doctor reviewed the echocardiogram to check for any abnormalities in the heart’s valves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrocardiogram - a recording of the electrical activity of the heart
