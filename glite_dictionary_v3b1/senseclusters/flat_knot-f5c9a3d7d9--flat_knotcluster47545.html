flat knot

noun
a type of knot; square knot
4.7
Pronunciation: / flæt nɑt / (AmE), / flæt nɒt / (BrE)
A type of knot, also known as a square knot, commonly used in joining two ropes or in reef lines on sails.

Examples

A flat knot tie
Learning how to tie a flat knot is essential for beginners in sailing.
The sailor used a flat knot to secure the sail's reef line.
When tying packages, a flat knot ensures the strings stay tight without slipping.
