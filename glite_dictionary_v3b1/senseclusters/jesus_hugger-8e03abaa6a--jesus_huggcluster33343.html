Jesus hugger

noun
very religious or devout Christian person
8.1 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒiː.zəs ˈhʌg.ər/ /; / /ˈdʒiː.zʌs ˈhʌg.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdʒiː.zəs ˈhʌg.ə/ /; / /ˈdʒiː.zʌs ˈhʌg.ə/ (BrE)
An informal, sometimes slightly humorous term for a person who is very religious and affectionate about their Christian faith, especially showing strong devotion to Jesus.

Examples

A Jesus hugger person
As a lifelong Jesus hugger, she sees her faith as central to her identity.
He was teased at work for being a Jesus hugger, but he didn't mind.
Her grandmother is a proud Jesus hugger who never misses church on Sundays.
