sing soprano

verb
perform in the highest singing voice
Pronunciation: / sɪŋ səˈproʊnoʊ / (AmE), / sɪŋ səˈprəʊnəʊ / (BrE)
to perform the soprano vocal part, singing in the highest voice range in a choir or musical ensemble

Examples

They needed someone to sing soprano for the new choral arrangement.
