poblano

noun
mild Mexican chili
3.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈblɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / pəʊˈblɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a mild green chili pepper from Mexico, often used in cooking for its flavor and low heat

Examples

Poblano pepper
She roasted a poblano and added it to the soup for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for one large poblano pepper, chopped and seeded.
He prefers using poblano peppers because they are mild and not too spicy.
She stuffed a poblano with cheese and baked it for dinner.
The recipe calls for two roasted poblanos to add a mild heat.
He prefers using poblano peppers in his salsa for their rich flavor.
