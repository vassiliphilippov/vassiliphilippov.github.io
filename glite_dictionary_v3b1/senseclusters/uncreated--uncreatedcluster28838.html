uncreated

adjective
not made or caused
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkriˈeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkriˈeɪtɪd / (BrE)
existing without having been made, caused, or brought into being by anything else

Examples

Uncreated universe
Some philosophers argue that the universe is uncreated and eternal.
In certain religious beliefs, God is considered uncreated and without origin.
The concept of an uncreated being is central to many theological discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: created - brought into existence by someone or something
  • synonym: eternal - existing forever without beginning or end
