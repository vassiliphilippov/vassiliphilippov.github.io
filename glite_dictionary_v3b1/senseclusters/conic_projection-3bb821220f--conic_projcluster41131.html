conic projection

noun
map projection onto a cone

Examples

A conic projection map
Many road and weather maps are based on a conic projection to accurately represent larger areas.
The conic projection is often used for mapping mid-latitude regions because it balances area and shape distortion.
When creating her atlas, the cartographer chose a conic projection for optimal representation of the continent.
