hedonistically

adverb
in pursuit of pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is focused on seeking pleasure or self-indulgence

Examples

To live hedonistically
They traveled hedonistically, always seeking the finest food and entertainment in every city.
He spent his weekends hedonistically, indulging in parties and luxurious experiences.
Some people choose to live hedonistically, focusing on enjoyment rather than responsibilities.
They lived hedonistically for a summer, attending parties and spending money freely.
He approached every weekend hedonistically, always searching for new pleasures and experiences.
Some people travel hedonistically, focusing only on luxury and enjoyment rather than cultural learning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascetically - in a way that avoids pleasure and self-indulgence
  • synonym: indulgently - in a way that allows oneself to enjoy pleasures
  • synonym: pleasurably - in a way that gives pleasure or enjoyment
