bastardization

noun
act of debasing or corrupting
8.1 7.4
Pronunciation: /bæs.tər.dɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /bə.stɑːr.də.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /bæs.tə.dɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /bə.stɑː.də.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of lowering the quality, value, or purity of something by introducing inferior elements or by corrupting its original form
