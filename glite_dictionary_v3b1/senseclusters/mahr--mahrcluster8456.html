mahr

noun
Islamic marriage gift
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑːr / (AmE), / mɑː / (BrE)
a mandatory gift or payment given by a groom to his bride as part of an Islamic marriage contract

Examples

The mahr payment
The amount of mahr was discussed and agreed upon by both families before the wedding.
In Islamic tradition, the groom must offer a mahr to his bride as part of the marriage contract.
She received her mahr on the day of the wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bride price - money or property given to the bride's family by the groom
  • antonym: dowry - property or money brought by a bride to her husband on marriage
