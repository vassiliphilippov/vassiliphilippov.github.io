breechclout

noun
loincloth garment
7.9 9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbriːtʃ.klaʊt/ (AmE), /ˈbriːtʃ.klaʊt/ (BrE)
A cloth worn around the hips and loins, traditionally by some Indigenous peoples and in ancient cultures, serving as a minimal covering for the lower body.

Examples

A breechclout cloth
Breechclouts were common attire in several ancient cultures.
