breechclout

noun
loincloth garment
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːtʃˌklaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbriːtʃˌklaʊt / (BrE)
A cloth worn around the hips and loins, traditionally by some Indigenous peoples and in ancient cultures, serving as a minimal covering for the lower body.

Examples

A breechclout cloth
Breechclouts were common attire in several ancient cultures.
He wore only a breechclout as he worked in the midday sun.
The museum displayed a Native American breechclout alongside other traditional clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loincloth - a simple garment that covers the loins
