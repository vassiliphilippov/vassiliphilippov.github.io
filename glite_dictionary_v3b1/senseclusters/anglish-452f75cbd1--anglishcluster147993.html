Anglish

adjective
using native English words
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
relating to English that uses only or mostly words of Germanic origin, avoiding foreign borrowings.

Examples

Anglish writing style
The author wrote the novel in an Anglish style, avoiding words of Latin or French origin.
Some language enthusiasts promote Anglish to highlight the Germanic roots of English.
Her essay was praised for its clear and Anglish vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: English - the language spoken in England and many other countries
