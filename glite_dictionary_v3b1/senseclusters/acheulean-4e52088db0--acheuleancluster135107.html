Acheulean

noun
prehistoric tool industry
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkjuː.lɪ.ən/ /; / /ə.ˈtʃuː.lɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkjuː.lɪ.ən/ /; / /ə.ˈtʃuː.lɪ.ən/ (BrE)
