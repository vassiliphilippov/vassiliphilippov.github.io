aphotic

adjective
lacking light (especially sunlight)
Pronunciation: / eɪˈfoʊtɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈfəʊtɪk / (BrE)
lacking light; especially not reached by sunlight, such as deep ocean zones where sunlight does not penetrate.

Examples

The aphotic zone
Few plants can survive in the aphotic depths of a lake.
Scientists study organisms adapted to aphotic zones of the ocean.
The trenches are the most extreme aphotic environments on Earth.
