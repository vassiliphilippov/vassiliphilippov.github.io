brainish

adjective
acting impulsively or rashly
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪ.nɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪ.nɪʃ / (BrE)
Acting hastily or impulsively without careful thought; rash or impetuous.

Examples

A brainish action
His brainish reaction to criticism often landed him in trouble.
It's unwise to make such brainish investments without proper research.
The brainish prince charged into battle without a plan.

  • synonym: impulsive - acting or done without forethought
