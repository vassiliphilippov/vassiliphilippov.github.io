estrogenicity

noun
estrogen-like effects
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛstrədʒəˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌiːstrədʒəˈnɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or degree of producing effects similar to the hormone estrogen

Examples

The estrogenicity level
Researchers measured the estrogenicity of several plant extracts in the laboratory.
Concerns about the estrogenicity of certain plastics have led to new safety regulations.
The study compared the estrogenicity of natural and synthetic compounds.
