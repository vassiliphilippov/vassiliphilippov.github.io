rigorousness

noun
extreme severity or harshness
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡərəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being extremely harsh, severe, or difficult to endure

Examples

Rigorousness of the climate
Only the strongest plants can survive the rigorousness of an alpine winter.
She was taken aback by the rigorousness of the exam, which tested every detail of the syllabus.
The rigorousness of the military training pushed many recruits to their limits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleness - kindness or mildness rather than severity
  • synonym: rigor - severity or strictness
