swing

noun
seat for swinging, playground equipment
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ / (AmE), / swɪŋ / (BrE)
a seat suspended by ropes or chains, usually from a frame, for swinging back and forth, especially as a plaything for children

Examples

A playground swing
She loved to sit on the old tire swing hanging from the tree in her backyard.
The children took turns pushing each other on the swing at the playground.
The park installed a new set of swings next to the slide for local families to enjoy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: see-saw - board balanced on a central point, used as playground equipment
  • synonym: slide - smooth sloping structure for children to slide down
