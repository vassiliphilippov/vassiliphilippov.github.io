swing

noun
seat for swinging, playground equipment
4.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /swɪŋ/ (AmE), /swɪŋ/ (BrE)
a seat suspended by ropes or chains, usually from a frame, for swinging back and forth, especially as a plaything for children

Examples

The park installed a new set of swings next to the slide for local families to enjoy.
