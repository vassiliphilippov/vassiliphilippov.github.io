tableau vivant

noun
posed group representing a scene

Pronunciation: /tə.ˈbloʊ vɪ.ˈvænt/ /; / /ˈtæb.loʊ vɪ.ˈvænt/ (AmE), /ˈtæb.ləʊ vɪ.ˈvænt/ /; / /tə.ˈbləʊ vɪ.ˈvænt/ (BrE)
A group of people artistically posed and often motionless to represent a scene or painting.

Examples

A tableau vivant scene
During the festival, participants formed a tableau vivant depicting a famous historical scene.
