geochemical

adjective
Earth's chemical properties
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the chemical composition, properties, or processes of the Earth or its parts

Examples

Geochemical survey
Scientists conducted a geochemical survey to understand the mineral content of the region.
The geochemical properties of the rocks suggest they formed millions of years ago.
A geochemical study can reveal how pollution affects soil and water quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical - relating to chemistry or the properties of substances
  • synonym: geological - relating to the structure and substance of the Earth
