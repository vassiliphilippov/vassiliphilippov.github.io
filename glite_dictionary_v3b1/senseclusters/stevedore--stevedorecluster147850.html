stevedore

verb
to load or unload ships
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːvəˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈstiːvədɔː / (BrE)
to load or unload cargo from a ship, especially as part of dock work.

Examples

To stevedore cargo
The workers spent the morning stevedoring heavy crates onto the cargo ship.
After stevedoring all day, he was exhausted from lifting and moving containers.
Many people are employed stevedoring at the busy port during the shipping season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: load - to put goods onto a vehicle or ship
  • synonym: unload - to remove goods from a vehicle or ship
