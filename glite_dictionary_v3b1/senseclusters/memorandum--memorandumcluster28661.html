memorandum

noun
written message for communication or record
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛməˈrændəm / (AmE), / ˌmɛm.əˈræn.dəm / (BrE)
a written message, especially in business or diplomacy, used to communicate information, proposals, or reminders

Examples

Business memorandum
Please read the memorandum before attending the meeting tomorrow morning.
She wrote a memorandum to remind herself of the upcoming deadlines.
The manager sent a memorandum to the staff outlining the new policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: letter - a written or printed message addressed to a person or organization
  • synonym: note - a brief written record or reminder
