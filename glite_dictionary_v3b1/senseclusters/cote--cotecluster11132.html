cote

noun
animal shelter or shed
Pronunciation: / koʊt / (AmE), / kəʊt / (BrE)
a small shelter or shed for domesticated animals such as sheep or pigeons

Examples

A cote for pigeons
A weathered wooden cote stood beneath the willow tree, providing shelter for the pigeons.
Every evening, the shepherd guided the sheep back to their cote for the night.
The doves found safety in the old stone cote at the end of the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coop - a cage or small enclosure for poultry
