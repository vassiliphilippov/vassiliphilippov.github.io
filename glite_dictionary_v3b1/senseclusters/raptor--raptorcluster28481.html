raptor

noun
carnivorous bird of prey
5.8 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈræp.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈræp.tə / (BrE)
A carnivorous bird of prey that hunts and kills other animals for food, such as an eagle, hawk, or owl.

Examples

A raptor bird
An eagle is a powerful raptor capable of carrying off large prey.
Many conservation efforts focus on protecting endangered raptors such as hawks and falcons.
The raptor perched high in the tree, scanning the ground for movement.
