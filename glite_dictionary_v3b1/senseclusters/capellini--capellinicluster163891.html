capellini

noun
thin Italian pasta
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpəˈliːni / (AmE), / ˌkæpɛˈliːni / (BrE)
a type of very thin Italian pasta, similar to angel hair, often served with light sauces

Examples

Thin capellini pasta
For a quick meal, she tossed capellini with olive oil and fresh basil.
The restaurant's signature dish features capellini served with seafood and a light lemon sauce.
He prefers capellini over thicker pastas because it cooks in just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angel hair - a very thin variety of pasta
  • degree higher: spaghetti - a long, thin, cylindrical pasta
