maleo

noun
a large bird from Sulawesi, Indonesia
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪ.oʊ /; / məˈleɪoʊ / (AmE), / məˈleɪ.əʊ /; / məˈleɪəʊ / (BrE)
a large black-and-white bird (Macrocephalon maleo) native to Sulawesi, Indonesia, known for burying its eggs in sandy beaches and using natural heat to incubate them

Examples

The maleo bird

The maleo lays its eggs in geothermal sand on Sulawesi's beaches.
Unlike most birds, the maleo relies on the earth's heat to incubate its eggs.
