Synonyms

  • enjoyment
    1.0 7.75/m
    enjoyment is the feeling you get from doing an activity, while pleasure is the broader term for that feeling or its cause.
  • delight
    1.2 17/m
    delight is a strong, sudden feeling of joy at something nice, while pleasure is a more general, everyday feeling or treat.
  • happiness
    1.0 41/m
    happiness is a longer-lasting state of being glad and content, while pleasure is often a shorter, immediate feeling from something.
  • joy
    1.0 44/m
    joy is a deep, powerful feeling of great happiness, while pleasure is a lighter, often shorter feeling from an event or object.
  • relish
    4.1 0.90/m
    relish is an eager, energetic enjoyment you savor in the moment, while pleasure is a general feeling or thing that gives satisfaction.
  • satisfaction
    2.2 25/m
    satisfaction is the pleasant feeling after you reach a goal or need, while pleasure is any enjoyment or happy feeling from something.
