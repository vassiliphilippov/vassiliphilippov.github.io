ocher

noun
natural earth pigment used for coloring
4.3 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈəʊkə / (BrE)
A natural earth pigment containing silica, alumina, and ferric oxide, used as a yellow or reddish-brown coloring agent.
