slapshot

noun
powerful ice hockey shot
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslæpˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈslæpˌʃɒt / (BrE)

Examples

A slapshot in hockey
Coaches often teach young players how to improve their slapshot technique.
He scored from the blue line with a powerful slapshot that the goalie couldn't stop.
Her slapshot flew past the defenders and straight into the net.
