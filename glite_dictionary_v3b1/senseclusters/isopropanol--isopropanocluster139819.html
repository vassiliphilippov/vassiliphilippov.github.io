isopropanol

noun
a flammable alcohol used as a solvent and disinfectant
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.səˈproʊ.pəˌnɒl / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.səʊˈprɒ.pənɒl / (BrE)
a flammable alcohol used chiefly as a solvent and rubbing alcohol, and in antifreeze

Examples

Isopropanol rubbing alcohol
Isopropanol is commonly used in laboratories as a solvent for cleaning equipment.
Be sure to use isopropanol in a well-ventilated area due to the fumes.
The disinfectant contains 70% isopropanol for effective sanitation.
