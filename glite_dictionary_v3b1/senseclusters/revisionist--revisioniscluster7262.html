revisionist

noun
Communist who modifies Marxist doctrine
5.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈvɪ.ʒə.nɪst/ /; / /riː.ˈvɪ.ʒə.nɪst/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈvɪ.ʒə.nɪst/ (BrE)
