expansive

adjective
in a state of exaggerated euphoria or grandiosity
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (BrE)
(of a mood or state) marked by exaggerated feelings of euphoria and delusions of grandeur.

Examples

Expansive mood
During his manic episode, he exhibited an expansive mood, talking incessantly about his grand plans.
Patients in an expansive state may display excessive optimism and an inflated self-esteem.
The psychiatrist noted her expansive demeanor and unrealistic sense of her own abilities.
