succeeding

adjective
coming after or following
Pronunciation: / səkˈsiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / səkˈsiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
coming after or following someone or something in order, time, or sequence

Examples

In succeeding years, the event grew in size and reputation.
The succeeding chapters of the book build upon the foundation established in the introduction.
