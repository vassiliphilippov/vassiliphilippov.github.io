hostile takeover

noun
acquisition of a company against management's wishes
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑstəl ˈteɪkˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɒstaɪl ˈteɪkəʊvə / (BrE)
the acquisition of a company despite opposition from its management or board of directors

Examples

A hostile takeover bid
After months of negotiations failed, a hostile takeover became their only option.
Several shareholders benefited financially from the hostile takeover of the company.
The board was surprised by the hostile takeover attempt from their largest competitor.

  • synonym: takeover - the acquisition of one company by another
