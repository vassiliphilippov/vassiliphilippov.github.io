folic acid

noun
a B vitamin important for growth
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊlɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˈfəʊlɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a B vitamin essential for cell growth and reproduction, often taken by pregnant women to prevent certain birth defects

Examples

Folic acid supplement
A deficiency in folic acid can lead to anemia and other health problems.
Doctors often recommend taking folic acid during pregnancy to help prevent birth defects.
Leafy green vegetables are a good natural source of folic acid.
