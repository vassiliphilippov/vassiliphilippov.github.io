patron

noun
supporter or benefactor
8.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.trən/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.trən/ (BrE)
a person who gives financial or other support to a person, organization, cause, or activity

Examples

Patron of the arts
As a patron of young writers, he provided mentorship and funding for new literary projects.
She became a patron of several charities, donating both time and money.
The arts festival would not have been possible without the generous support of its main patron.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefactor - a person who gives money or other help to a person or cause
  • antonym: opponent - a person who actively opposes or is hostile to something
  • synonym: sponsor - one who finances or promotes a person, organization, or event
