nonpsychotic

adjective
not affected by psychosis
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsaɪˈkɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsaɪˈkɒtɪk / (BrE)
not relating to or affected by psychosis; not showing symptoms of psychosis

Examples

Nonpsychotic symptoms
The doctor confirmed that the symptoms were nonpsychotic and did not indicate a severe mental disorder.
She was diagnosed with a nonpsychotic mood disorder after several assessments.
Most patients in the study had nonpsychotic forms of depression.
