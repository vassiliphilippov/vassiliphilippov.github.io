brassiness

noun
loud boldness
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being loud, harsh, or overly bold in manner or appearance

Examples

Brassiness in her style
The brassiness of his voice made it impossible to ignore him in the meeting.
Some people admired her brassiness, while others found it overwhelming.
The brassiness of the decor gave the restaurant a flashy, energetic vibe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subtlety - the quality of being delicate or understated
