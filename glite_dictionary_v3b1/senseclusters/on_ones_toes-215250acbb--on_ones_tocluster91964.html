on (one's) toes

adjective
alert and ready for action
Pronunciation: / ɑn wʌnz toʊz / (AmE), / ɒn wʌnz təʊz / (BrE)
alert, attentive, and ready for anything that might happen

Examples

On her toes all day
As a nurse in the emergency room, she is always on her toes.
Managing three energetic children means staying on your toes at all times.
The sudden announcement kept everyone on their toes throughout the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alert - quick to notice and react to possible danger or problems
  • antonym: asleep at the wheel - not paying attention when attention is needed
