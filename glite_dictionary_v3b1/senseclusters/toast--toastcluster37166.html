toast

verb
raise a glass in honour of someone/something
Pronunciation: / toʊst / (AmE), / təʊst / (BrE)
to drink or raise a glass in honor of someone or something, typically with a short speech

Examples

To toast the couple
At the wedding, everyone stood to toast the happy couple.
Before dinner, she smiled and toasted their twenty years of friendship.
We all raised our glasses to toast his recent promotion at work.
At midnight, we all toasted the newlyweds with champagne.
He stood up to toast the achievements of his colleagues.
Let's toast to a successful year ahead!
