rogatory

adjective
legal request-related
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːɡəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈrɒɡətəri / (BrE)
relating to a formal request, especially one made by a court to another court for legal assistance

Examples

Rogatory letter
The judge sent a rogatory request to a foreign court to gather crucial evidence.
International cases often require rogatory letters to obtain testimony from witnesses abroad.
A rogatory commission was established to facilitate cooperation between the two legal systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: informal - not following official or formal procedures
  • synonym: judicial - relating to courts or the administration of justice
