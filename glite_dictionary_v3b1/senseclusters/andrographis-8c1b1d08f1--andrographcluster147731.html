Andrographis

noun
ornamental garden plant
Pronunciation: / ˌændrəˈɡræfɪs / (AmE), / ˌændrəˈɡræfɪs / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Andrographis, often grown in gardens for its attractive spikes of flowers

Examples

Andrographis plant
Gardeners often choose Andrographis for its striking flower spikes and easy maintenance.
The border of the park was lined with blooming Andrographis in the summer.
She admired the tall, purple flowers of the Andrographis in her neighbor's garden.
