Salamandra atra

noun
a black Alpine salamander species
9.9
Pronunciation: /sæ.lə.ˈmæn.drə ˈeɪ.trə/ (AmE), /sæ.lə.ˈmæn.drə ˈeɪ.trə/ (BrE)
A species of salamander native to the Alps, notable for being ovoviviparous and for its entirely black coloration.
