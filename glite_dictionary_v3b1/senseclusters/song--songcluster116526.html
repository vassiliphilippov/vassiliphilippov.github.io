song

noun
a very small sum of money
Pronunciation: / sɔŋ / (AmE), / sɒŋ / (BrE)
a very small amount of money, especially when something is bought or sold cheaply

Examples

Bought for a song
I managed to buy the antique vase for a song at the flea market.
She sold her collection of books for a song when she moved abroad.
Tickets for the concert went for a song because of the last-minute sale.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fortune - a large amount of money
  • synonym: pittance - a very small amount of money
