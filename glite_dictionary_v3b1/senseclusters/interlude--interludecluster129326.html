interlude

noun
an intervening period or episode
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntɚˌlud / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌluːd / (BrE)
an intervening period of time or episode, especially one that interrupts or contrasts with what comes before and after

Examples

A quiet interlude
The play was divided by a short interlude featuring music and dance.
The rainstorm was merely an unexpected interlude during an otherwise sunny afternoon.
There was a peaceful interlude in their argument before tempers flared again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interval - a period of time between events
