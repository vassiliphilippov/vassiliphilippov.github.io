heliologist

noun
sun scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌhiːliˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the sun and its physical properties and phenomena

Examples

Solar heliologist research
The heliologist spent years observing sunspots to understand solar activity.
A team led by a renowned heliologist discovered new patterns in solar wind behavior.
During the conference, the heliologist explained the impact of solar flares on Earth's climate.
