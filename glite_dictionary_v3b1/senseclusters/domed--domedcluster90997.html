domed

adjective
having a rounded or dome-shaped form
8.9
Pronunciation: / doʊmd / (AmE), / dəʊmd / (BrE)
Having a rounded or hemispherical vault or dome-shaped form.

Examples

Domed ceiling
Many cathedrals in Europe are recognized by their domed ceilings, which create a spacious interior.
The old observatory featured a large domed roof that could slide open for telescopic observations.
The turtle's domed shell provides protection from predators and the environment.
