what's up

x
informal greeting; asking how someone is or what is happening
Pronunciation: / wʌts ʌp / (AmE), / wɒts ʌp / (BrE)
(informal) Used as a greeting to ask what is happening or how someone is doing; an inquiry about current events or one's well-being.

Examples

What's up?
What's up? I haven't seen you in ages!
He walked into the room and said, "What's up?" to everyone.
If you don't reply, I'll text to see what's up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: how are you - greeting asking about someone's well-being
  • synonym: what's going on - phrase asking about current events or situation
