showboat

verb
to attract attention
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈʃoʊ.boʊt/ (AmE), /ˈʃəʊ.bəʊt/ (BrE)
to behave in a flashy or showy way to attract attention or admiration

Examples

To showboat for fans
He tends to showboat on the basketball court instead of playing as part of the team.

She tried not to showboat during the presentation, even though her project was the best.
