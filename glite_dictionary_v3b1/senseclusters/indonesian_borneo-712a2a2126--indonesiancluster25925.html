Indonesian Borneo

proper noun
Indonesian part of the island of Borneo
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌdoʊˈniʒən bɔːrˈnioʊ / (AmE), / ɪnˌdəʊˈniːziən bɔːˈniːəʊ / (BrE)
