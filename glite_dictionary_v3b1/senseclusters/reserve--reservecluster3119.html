reserve

noun
formality and restraint in manner
1.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɝːv / (AmE), / rɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
the quality of being restrained or not showing emotions or opinions easily

Examples

Emotional reserve
Despite his reserve, he was well-liked by his colleagues for his reliability.
He spoke politely, but there was a distinct reserve in his manner.
Her natural reserve made it difficult for her to express her feelings openly.
