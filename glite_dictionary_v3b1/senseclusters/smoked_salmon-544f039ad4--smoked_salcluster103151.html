smoked salmon

noun
salmon cured and flavored by smoking
Pronunciation: / smoʊkt ˈsæmən / (AmE), / sməʊkt ˈsæmən / (BrE)
Salmon that has been cured and flavored by smoking, often served cold in thin slices.

Examples

A bagel with smoked salmon
For breakfast, she enjoyed a bagel with cream cheese and smoked salmon.
He bought a package of smoked salmon to serve at the party.
The restaurant's signature appetizer is a platter of smoked salmon with capers and onions.
