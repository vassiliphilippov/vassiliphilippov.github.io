gig

verb
to perform temporarily
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɡɪɡ / (AmE), / ɡɪɡ / (BrE)
to perform music or entertainment at a live event, especially as a paid job

She started gigging in jazz clubs while still in college.
Many musicians gig on weekends to earn extra money.
