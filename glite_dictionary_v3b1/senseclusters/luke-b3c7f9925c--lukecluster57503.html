Luke

proper noun
New Testament Gospel recounting Jesus's life
Pronunciation: / luːk / (AmE), / luːk / (BrE)
the third of the four Gospels in the New Testament, describing the birth, life, and teachings of Jesus Christ, traditionally attributed to St Luke
