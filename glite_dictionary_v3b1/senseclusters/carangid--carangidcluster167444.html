carangid

adjective
of or relating to the Carangidae fish family
Pronunciation: / kəˈræŋɪd / (AmE), / kəˈræŋɪd / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to the family Carangidae, a group of marine fish that includes jacks, pompanos, and trevallies.
