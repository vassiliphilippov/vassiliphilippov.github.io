plié

noun
ballet knee bend
8.6
Pronunciation: / pliˈeɪ / (AmE), / pliˈeɪ / (BrE)
a movement in ballet in which the dancer bends the knees while keeping the back straight

Examples

A deep plié
The instructor asked the students to perform a deep plié at the start of class.
Her graceful plié showed years of ballet training and discipline.
He practiced his plié in front of the mirror to perfect his form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bend - to move a part of the body so that it is no longer straight
  • antonym: relevé - a ballet movement where the dancer rises onto the balls of the feet
