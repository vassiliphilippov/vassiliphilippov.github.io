dynamo

noun
electric generator producing direct current
4.9 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnəˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəməʊ / (BrE)
A machine that generates electricity by means of a rotating coil within a magnetic field, producing a direct current.

Examples

A dynamo machine
Advances in technology have made the traditional dynamo less common in modern power plants.
He repaired his bicycle's dynamo so the headlight would work again.
The old factory used a massive dynamo to power its machinery.

  • synonym: generator - machine that converts mechanical energy into electricity
