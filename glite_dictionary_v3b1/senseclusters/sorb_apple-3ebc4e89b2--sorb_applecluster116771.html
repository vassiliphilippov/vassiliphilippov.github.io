sorb apple

noun
European tree with edible fruit
5.9
Pronunciation: / sɔrb ˈæp.əl / (AmE), / sɔːb ˈæp.əl / (BrE)
A medium-sized European tree of the species Sorbus domestica, resembling the rowan and bearing edible fruit.
