nutball

noun
eccentric person
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtbɔl / (AmE), / ˈnʌtbɔːl / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a silly, eccentric, or crazy way, often in a humorous or harmless manner

Examples

A real nutball
Everyone thinks my uncle is a real nutball at family parties.
The new student acted like such a nutball that everyone noticed him immediately.
You do not have to be a nutball to enjoy dressing up in costumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional or odd behavior
  • antonym: normal - a person who behaves in a typical or expected way
  • synonym: weirdo - a person who behaves strangely
