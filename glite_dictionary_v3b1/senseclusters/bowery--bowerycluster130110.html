bowery

adjective
leafy and shady like a bower
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈbaʊəri / (BrE)
Having the characteristics of a bower; leafy, shady, and pleasantly covered with foliage.

Examples

Bowery garden path
A bowery path wound through the garden, creating a tranquil atmosphere.
The bowery lane was a welcome escape from the midday sun.
They enjoyed a picnic under the bowery canopy of old oak trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leafy - full of or covered with leaves
  • synonym: shady - providing or full of shade
