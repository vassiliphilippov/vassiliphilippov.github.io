apportionable

adjective
that can be apportioned or distributed
4.6
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔːrʃənəbl̩ / (AmE), / əˈpɔːʃənəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being divided and distributed according to a plan or proportion

Examples

An apportionable cost
Certain expenses are not apportionable and must be borne entirely by the company.
Profits from the joint venture are apportionable according to each partner's share.
The settlement funds are apportionable among the plaintiffs based on their individual losses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divisible - capable of being divided
  • antonym: indivisible - not capable of being divided
