clap

noun
a sudden loud noise
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /klæp/ (AmE), /klæp/ (BrE)
a sudden loud or sharp noise, especially from thunder, an explosion, or two things striking together

Examples

We jumped in surprise at the sudden clap outside the window.

She could hear the clap of the judge's gavel echo in the courtroom.
There was a loud clap as the two pieces of wood struck together.
