comatic

adjective
optical aberration
Pronunciation: / koʊˈmætɪk / (AmE), / kəʊˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by coma, an optical aberration that causes point images to appear distorted or comet-shaped.

Examples

Comatic aberration
The new telescope lens was designed to reduce comatic distortion at the edges of the image.
Photographers often notice comatic effects when shooting bright stars near the corners of a frame.
Engineers tested the camera for comatic aberrations before finalizing the design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astigmatic - relating to astigmatism, another optical aberration
  • synonym: spherical - relating to spherical aberration, a different optical distortion
