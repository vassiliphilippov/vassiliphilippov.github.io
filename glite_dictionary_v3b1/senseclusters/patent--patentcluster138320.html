patent

adjective
open and unobstructed (medical)
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpætnt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtənt / (BrE)
(of a bodily tube or passageway) open and unobstructed, allowing free passage.

Examples

Patent airway
A patent airway is essential for adequate breathing.
The surgeon confirmed the artery was patent during the operation.
The test showed that the patient's fallopian tubes remained patent after the procedure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occluded - (of a bodily passage) closed or blocked
