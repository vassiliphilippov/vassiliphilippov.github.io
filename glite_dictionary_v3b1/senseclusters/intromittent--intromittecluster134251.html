intromittent

adjective
inserted during reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntroʊˈmɪtənt / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈmɪtənt / (BrE)
describing an organ or structure that is inserted into another during reproduction

Intromittent organ in insects

In some animals, the intromittent organ is highly specialized for reproduction.
