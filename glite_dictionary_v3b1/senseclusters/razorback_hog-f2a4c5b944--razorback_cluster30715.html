razorback hog

noun
wild hog with a ridged back (US)
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzərˌbæk hɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəbæk hɒɡ / (BrE)
a wild or semi-wild hog with a thin body, long legs, and a ridged back, found mainly in the southeastern United States

Examples

A wild razorback hog
A solitary razorback hog crossed the country road just after midnight.
Farmers in the region have struggled to keep razorback hogs away from their crops.
The hunters spotted a large razorback hog rooting in the underbrush.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feral hog - a wild or feral pig, especially in North America
