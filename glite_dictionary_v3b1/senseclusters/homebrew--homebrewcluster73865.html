homebrew

verb
to make alcohol at home
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbruː / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbruː / (BrE)
to make beer or other alcoholic drinks at home, rather than buying them from a store or bar

Examples

To homebrew beer
Many people enjoy homebrewing as a hobby and experiment with different flavors.
He started homebrewing during the lockdown and now shares his creations with friends.
Learning the basics of homebrewing can help you appreciate the craft of beer making.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brew - to make beer or other drinks by fermentation
  • synonym: distill - to make a strong alcoholic drink by distillation
  • antonym: purchase - to buy something rather than make it
