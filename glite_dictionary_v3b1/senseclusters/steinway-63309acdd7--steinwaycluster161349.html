Steinway

proper noun
piano-making family name
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪnˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈstaɪnˌweɪ / (BrE)
a person with the family name Steinway, especially a member of the family that founded or managed the Steinway & Sons piano company

Examples

The Steinway family
Henry E. Steinway founded the famous piano company in the 19th century.
The Steinway family became known worldwide for their craftsmanship in piano making.
At the museum, a portrait of Steinway hangs above one of his earliest grand pianos.
