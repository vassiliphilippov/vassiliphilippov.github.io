corkboard

noun
heat-insulating material made from cork
9.1 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːrk.bɔːrd/ (AmE), /ˈkɔːk.bɔːd/ (BrE)
a heat-insulating building material made of cork granules compressed and baked into sheets

Examples

Corkboard insulation panel
A sheet of corkboard helps keep the building warm in the winter.
Because it resists moisture and heat, corkboard is valued in eco-friendly construction.
The builders used corkboard as insulation between the interior and exterior walls.
