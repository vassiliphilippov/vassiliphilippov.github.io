firing

noun
dismissal from a job
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of dismissing someone from their job; the termination of someone’s employment

Examples

Job firing
After his firing, he decided to start his own business.
The company announced the firing of several employees due to budget cuts.
The controversial firing of the manager sparked protests among staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissal - the act of removing someone from their job
  • antonym: hiring - the act of taking someone into employment
