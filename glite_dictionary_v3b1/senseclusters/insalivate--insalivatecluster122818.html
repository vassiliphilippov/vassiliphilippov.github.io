insalivate

verb
to moisten with saliva
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsælɪˌveɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsælɪveɪt / (BrE)
to mix or moisten with saliva, especially during chewing

Examples

To insalivate food
Chewing helps to insalivate food before swallowing.
The process of insalivating food begins as soon as it enters the mouth.
Properly insalivated food is easier to digest.
