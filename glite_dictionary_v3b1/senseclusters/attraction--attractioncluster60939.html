attraction

noun
quality of being interesting or appealing
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈtrækʃən / (AmE), / əˈtrækʃən / (BrE)
the quality or feature that makes something appealing or draws interest

Freshly baked bread has an irresistible attraction for many people.
Her kindness was an attraction that drew people to her.
The city’s biggest attraction is its beautiful riverfront park.
