friendly banter

noun
light-hearted informal conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛn(d)li ˈbæntər / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli ˈbæntə / (BrE)
Light-hearted and informal conversation, often joking or teasing in a friendly way.

Examples

Friendly banter at work
Her friendly banter often made new team members feel welcome and at ease.
The friendly banter between colleagues created a relaxed atmosphere in the office.
They engaged in friendly banter throughout the game, laughing and joking with each other.
