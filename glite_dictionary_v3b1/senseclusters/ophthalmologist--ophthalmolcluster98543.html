ophthalmologist

noun
eye specialist doctor
1.2 6.2
Pronunciation: /ɑːf.θæl.ˈmɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /ɒf.θæl.ˈmɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and disorders of the eye.

Examples

An ophthalmologist appointment
After experiencing blurry vision, she scheduled an appointment with an ophthalmologist.
Many ophthalmologists also perform delicate eye surgeries to restore or improve sight.
The ophthalmologist diagnosed her with glaucoma during a routine eye exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oculist - an old term for a medical practitioner specializing in eye disorders
  • synonym: optometrist - a person trained to examine eyes and prescribe glasses
