unweathered

adjective
not altered by weather
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛðərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛðəd / (BrE)
Not changed or worn by exposure to weather or natural elements.

Examples

An unweathered stone
Newly quarried stone is typically unweathered and has a bright appearance.
The geologists took samples of unweathered rock from deep below the surface.
The statue remained remarkably unweathered even after decades outside.
