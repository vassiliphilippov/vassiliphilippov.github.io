fingerspelling

noun
alphabet using hand signs in sign language
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
a system of representing the letters of the alphabet using specific hand shapes or movements, especially as used in sign language

Examples

Fingerspelling alphabet
Mastering fingerspelling can help bridge the gap between spoken and sign languages.
She is learning fingerspelling to communicate with her deaf friend.
The interpreter used fingerspelling for names that didn't have specific signs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manual alphabet - an alphabet for spelling words with hand signs
  • synonym: sign language - a language that uses visual-manual modality to convey meaning
