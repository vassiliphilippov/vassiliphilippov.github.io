otter hound

noun
large shaggy British dog breed
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑt̬ɚ haʊnd / (AmE), / ˈɒtə haʊnd / (BrE)
a large, hardy British breed of dog with long pendulous ears and a thick, coarse, shaggy coat, originally bred for hunting otters

Examples

The otter hound breed
After years of decline, the otter hound breed is now considered rare.
Despite his large size, our otter hound was gentle and affectionate with children.
The otter hound is known for its keen sense of smell and love of water.
