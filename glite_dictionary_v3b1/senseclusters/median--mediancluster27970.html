median

adjective
in or toward the middle
5.3 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêdi…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêdi…ôn / (BrE)
situated in or relating to the middle or center, especially in anatomy (such as the midline dividing the body into right and left halves)

Examples

Median nerve
The median nerve runs down the center of the arm.
The city built a park along the median strip of the highway.
The incision was made along the median line of the abdomen.
A median incision is often made along the midline of the abdomen during surgery.
In anatomical diagrams, structures close to the median line are called medial.
The median plane divides the body into equal right and left halves.
