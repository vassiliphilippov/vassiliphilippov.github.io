belligerently

adverb
in a hostile or aggressive way
9.2
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪdʒɚəntli / (AmE), / bəˈlɪdʒərəntli / (BrE)
In a hostile or aggressive manner.

Examples

To speak belligerently
He spoke belligerently to anyone who challenged his opinion.
She belligerently confronted her neighbor about the noise.
The customer belligerently demanded a refund at the counter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressively - in an aggressive or forceful manner
  • antonym: peacefully - in a calm or non-hostile way
