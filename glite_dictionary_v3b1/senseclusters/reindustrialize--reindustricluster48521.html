reindustrialize

verb
to restore industry
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪz / (BrE)
to bring industry or manufacturing back to a place after it has declined or disappeared

Examples

To reindustrialize a city
The government hopes to reindustrialize the city to boost employment and economic growth.
After years of decline, several policies were introduced to reindustrialize the region.
Efforts to reindustrialize have attracted new factories and investment to the area.
The government introduced new policies to help the city reindustrialize after decades of factory closures.
Many experts believe it is possible to reindustrialize rural areas by investing in modern technology.
After losing most of its manufacturing jobs, the region began to slowly reindustrialize in the 21st century.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deindustrialize - to reduce or remove industry from an area or country
  • synonym: industrialize - to develop industry in a region or country
