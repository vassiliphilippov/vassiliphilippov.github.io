hippocampal

adjective
of the hippocampus
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪpoʊˈkæmpəl / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəl / (BrE)
relating to the hippocampus, a part of the brain involved in memory and learning

Examples

Hippocampal activity
The study found increased hippocampal activity when participants tried to remember new information.
Damage to hippocampal tissue can result in severe memory loss.
Scientists are exploring how hippocampal changes affect learning in older adults.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amygdaloid - relating to the amygdala, a brain region involved in emotion
  • synonym: cerebral - relating to the brain
