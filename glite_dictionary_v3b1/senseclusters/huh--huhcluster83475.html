huh

interjection
expressing confusion
Pronunciation: / hʌ / (AmE), / hʌ / (BrE)
used to express confusion, surprise, or to ask someone to repeat what they said

Examples

Huh? What?
He looked at the strange device and said, "Huh? What does this button do?"
"Huh? I thought the meeting was tomorrow, not today," she replied in confusion.
If you don't understand, just say "huh" and I'll explain it again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eh - used to request repetition or show surprise
  • synonym: what - used to ask someone to repeat something
