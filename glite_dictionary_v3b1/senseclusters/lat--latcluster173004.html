lat

noun
abbreviation for latitude
Pronunciation: / læt / (AmE), / læt / (BrE)
an abbreviation for latitude, the angular distance north or south of the equator

Examples

Lat and long
The lat and long of each city are listed in the travel guide.
Please provide the lat and elevation for the research station.
On the map, the lat is shown along the side for easy reference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: latitude - the angular distance north or south of the equator
  • antonym: long - abbreviation for longitude, the angular distance east or west of the prime meridian
