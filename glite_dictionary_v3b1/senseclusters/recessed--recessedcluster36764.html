recessed

adjective
set back or sunken below surroundings
3.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛst / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛst / (BrE)
(of a surface or area) set back or sunken below the surrounding level.

Examples

Recessed eyes

She admired the recessed panels on the antique door.
