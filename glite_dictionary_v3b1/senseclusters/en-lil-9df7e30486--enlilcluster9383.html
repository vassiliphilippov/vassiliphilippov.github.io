En-lil

proper noun
king of Sumerian gods; god of the air
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.lɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛn.lɪl / (BrE)
the god of the air and king of the Sumerian gods in Mesopotamian mythology

Examples

En-lil god of air
En-lil was considered a supreme deity in ancient Sumerian religion.
Prayers and hymns were often dedicated to En-lil by the priests of Mesopotamia.
The temple of En-lil at Nippur was a major religious center in Sumer.
