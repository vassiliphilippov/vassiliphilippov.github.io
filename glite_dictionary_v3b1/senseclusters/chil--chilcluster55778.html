chil

noun
slight cold feeling
Pronunciation: / tʃɪl / (AmE), / tʃɪl / (BrE)
a slight feeling of cold or a shiver; a sensation of coldness (nonstandard spelling of 'chill')

Examples

The room was silent except for the chil that crept in from the open window.
