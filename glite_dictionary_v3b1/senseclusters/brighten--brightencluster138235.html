brighten

verb
become clear or less cloudy (weather/sky)
3.0 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbraɪ.tən/ (AmE), /ˈbraɪ.tən/ (BrE)

The weather is expected to brighten in the afternoon, with clear skies by evening.
