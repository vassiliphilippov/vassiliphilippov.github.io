clip-on

noun
object fastened by a clip
2.7 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪp ɑn / (AmE), / ˈklɪp ɒn / (BrE)
a small object designed to be fastened by a clip, such as an earring or accessory, instead of being attached permanently or by piercing.

Examples

Clip-on earrings
He keeps a set of clip-on sunglasses in his car for sunny days.
She wore a pair of sparkling clip-on earrings to the party.
The clip-on microphone made it easy for the speaker to move around the stage.
