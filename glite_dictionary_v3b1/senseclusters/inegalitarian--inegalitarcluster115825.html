inegalitarian

noun
supporter of inequality
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnɪˌɡælɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ɪnɪˌɡælɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person who supports or favors inequality rather than equal treatment for all people

Examples

An inegalitarian leader
The policy was criticized by many as the work of an inegalitarian unwilling to support social reforms.
Some historians argue that the laws were designed by inegalitarians to maintain existing power structures.
She refused to vote for any candidate she considered an inegalitarian in their views on education.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - a person who believes in equal rights and opportunities for all
