afraid of heights

adjective
fear of high places
Pronunciation: / əˈfreɪd əv haɪts / (AmE), / əˈfreɪd əv hɑɪts / (BrE)
having a fear of being in high places

Examples

Afraid of heights fear
Because she is afraid of heights, she avoided looking out from the observation deck.
He admitted he was afraid of heights before trying rock climbing.
Many people who are afraid of heights feel uneasy near tall buildings or mountains.
