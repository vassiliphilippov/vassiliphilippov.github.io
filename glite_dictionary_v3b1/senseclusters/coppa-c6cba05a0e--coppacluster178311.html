COPPA

proper noun
US privacy law
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpə / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, a law restricting online collection of personal information from children under 13

Examples

COPPA rules
Websites must follow COPPA rules when collecting information from children under 13.
The company updated its privacy policy to comply with COPPA regulations.
Many parents appreciate the protections that COPPA provides for their children's online privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: GDPR - General Data Protection Regulation, a European law on data privacy
  • synonym: privacy policy - a statement about how a company collects and uses personal data
