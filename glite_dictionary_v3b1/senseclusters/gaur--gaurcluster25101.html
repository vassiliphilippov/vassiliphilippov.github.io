gaur

noun
large wild ox of South Asia
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊr / (AmE), / ɡaʊə / (BrE)
a large wild ox (Bos gaurus) native to the forests and hills of South and Southeast Asia, especially eastern India

Examples

A gaur herd
A herd of gaur was grazing on the hillside at dawn.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered gaur in India's national parks.
The gaur is the largest wild bovine species in the world.
