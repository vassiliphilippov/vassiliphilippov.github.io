working agreement

noun
informal partnership or collaborative arrangement
1.7 2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkɪŋ əˌɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ əˌɡriːmənt / (BrE)
an informal arrangement in which two or more parties agree to work together, often without a formal contract

Examples

A working agreement between companies
Before beginning the project, both companies entered into a working agreement to collaborate on research.
Our working agreement is based on mutual respect and shared responsibility.
The team established a working agreement to outline how they would communicate and share tasks.
