fairylike

adjective
delicate or magical
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛriˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfeəriˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or an appearance similar to a fairy, especially being delicate, magical, or enchanting

Examples

A fairylike appearance
The children danced through the fairylike mist that covered the meadow at dawn.
Her dress had a fairylike quality, shimmering softly in the candlelight.
Tiny lights gave the old forest a fairylike atmosphere after sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethereal - extremely delicate and light in a way that seems not of this world
  • synonym: magical - having the qualities of magic; enchanting
  • antonym: ordinary - not unusual or special; normal
