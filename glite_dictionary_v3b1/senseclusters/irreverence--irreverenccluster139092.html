irreverence

noun
lack of respect
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛvərəns / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛvərəns / (BrE)
a lack of respect or a disrespectful act towards people or things that are usually respected

Examples

Irreverence for authority
His jokes are full of irreverence for traditional authority.
The comedian's irreverence delighted younger audiences but shocked some older viewers.
There is a certain irreverence in her approach to classic literature.
She was criticized for her irreverence during the solemn ceremony.
Some people appreciate a little irreverence in political cartoons.
The comedian's irreverence toward authority made his jokes especially daring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disrespect - lack of respect or courtesy
  • antonym: reverence - deep respect for someone or something
