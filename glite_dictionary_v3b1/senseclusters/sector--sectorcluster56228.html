sector

noun
part of a military position
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛktə / (BrE)
a distinct part or subdivision of a military area or position, often assigned for defense or operations

Examples

A military sector
Each battalion was responsible for defending its own sector of the frontline.
Reinforcements were quickly sent to the compromised sector to repel the attack.
The enemy launched an assault on the northern sector just before dawn.
