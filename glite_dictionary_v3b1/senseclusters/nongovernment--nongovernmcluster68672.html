nongovernment

noun
non-governmental entity
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈɡʌvənmənt / (BrE)
a person or organization that is not part of or controlled by the government, especially a nongovernmental organization (NGO).

Examples

A nongovernment group
A nongovernment provided medical supplies to the remote village after the earthquake.
Several nongovernments work to protect the environment in this region.
The conference included representatives from both government and nongovernment organizations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: NGO - a non-governmental organization
  • antonym: government - the group of people who control and make decisions for a country or organization
