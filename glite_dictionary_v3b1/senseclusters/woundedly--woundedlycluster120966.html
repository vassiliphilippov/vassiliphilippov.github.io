woundedly

adverb
with emotional pain
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːndɪdli / (AmE), / ˈwuːndɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows pain or hurt, especially emotional pain

Examples

She replied woundedly
She replied woundedly when her efforts were not appreciated by the team.
He looked woundedly at his friend after the harsh comment.
The actor spoke woundedly about the criticism he received for his performance.
He spoke woundedly after hearing the unfair criticism from his boss.
She glanced woundedly at her friend when she was left out of the conversation.
The child looked woundedly at his parents after being scolded.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy or positive way
  • synonym: defensively - in a way that shows self-protection from criticism
  • synonym: hurtfully - in a way that shows being hurt or offended
