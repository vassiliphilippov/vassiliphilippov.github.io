word-splitting

noun
NLP tokenization/segmentation
Pronunciation: / \u02c8w\u025d\u02d0d\u02ccspl\u026at\u026a\u014b / (AmE), / \u02c8w\u025c\u02d0d\u02ccspl\u026at\u026a\u014b / (BrE)
In natural language processing, the process of dividing text into words or tokens for computational analysis, especially important for languages that lack explicit word boundaries.

Examples

Word-splitting in text analysis
Before training the model, we applied word-splitting to all raw text in the corpus.
Languages like Chinese require careful word-splitting because there are no spaces between words.
The preprocessing pipeline included sentence segmentation, tokenization, and word-splitting by rule-based heuristics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: segmentation - the act of dividing text into meaningful units such as sentences or words
  • synonym: tokenization - the process of breaking text into tokens (words, punctuation, or subword units) for processing by NLP systems
