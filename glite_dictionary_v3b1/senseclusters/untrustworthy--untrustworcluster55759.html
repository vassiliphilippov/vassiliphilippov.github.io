untrustworthy

adjective
not deserving of trust; unreliable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrʌstˌwɝːði / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrʌstˌwɜːði / (BrE)
Not deserving of trust or confidence; unreliable.

Examples

Untrustworthy person
It’s risky to share secrets with someone as untrustworthy as him.
She found her former friend to be completely untrustworthy after learning about the lies.
The report warned investors that the source appeared untrustworthy and should not be relied upon.
