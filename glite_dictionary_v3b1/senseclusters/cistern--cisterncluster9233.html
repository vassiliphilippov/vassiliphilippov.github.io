cistern

noun
toilet water storage tank
8.5 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstɚn / (AmE), / ˈsɪstən / (BrE)
a tank that stores water, especially one used to supply water for flushing a toilet

Examples

Toilet cistern
Older houses often have a separate cistern in the bathroom for toilet flushing.
She pressed the handle, and water from the cistern flushed the bowl clean.
The plumber fixed a leak in the cistern above the toilet.
