artifactual

adjective
relating to artifacts
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːr.tɪˈfæk.tʃu.əl /; / ˌɑːrtəˈfæktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfæk.tʃu.əl /; / ˌɑːtɪˈfæktʃuəl / (BrE)
