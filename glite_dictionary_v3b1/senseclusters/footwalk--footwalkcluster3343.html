footwalk

noun
pedestrian walkway
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌwɔk / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌwɔːk / (BrE)
a path or walkway designed for people to walk on, especially in a park or along a street

Examples

Park footwalk
The city built a new footwalk along the river for pedestrians to enjoy the view.
Children rode their bikes carefully on the footwalk near the playground.
Benches were placed every few meters along the footwalk for people to rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pathway - a route or track for walking
  • synonym: sidewalk - a paved path for pedestrians along the side of a street
