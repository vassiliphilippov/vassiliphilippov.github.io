illumination

noun
clarification or intellectual enlightenment
Pronunciation: / ɪˌluːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌljuːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an explanation or clarification that makes something easier to understand; intellectual enlightenment

Examples

Illumination of ideas
Her insightful illumination of the novel's themes changed the way I read it.
Thanks to his clear illumination of the problems, the solutions became obvious.
The teacher's illumination of the theory made it accessible to all the students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarification - the act of making something clearer or easier to understand
  • antonym: obscuration - the act of making something less clear or more difficult to understand
