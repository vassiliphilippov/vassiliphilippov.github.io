Columba

proper noun
genus of typical pigeons
9.8
Pronunciation: / kəˈlʌmbə / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbə / (BrE)
The genus of birds that includes the typical pigeons, such as the rock dove, and is the type genus of the family Columbidae.
