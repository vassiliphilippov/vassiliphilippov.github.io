heave

noun
upward or rhythmic movement
7.1
Pronunciation: / hiv / (AmE), / hiːv / (BrE)
an upward or rhythmic movement, especially a rising and falling motion

Examples

The heave of the sea
The heave of the ship made it hard to walk across the deck.
The heave of the waves against the rocks became stronger during the storm.
With every breath, the heave of his chest was visible beneath the blanket.
