nightwalker

noun
person active at night
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtwɔːkər / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtwɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who is active or travels at night, often in a secretive or unusual way

Examples

A nightwalker in streets
The nightwalker slipped through the quiet city streets while everyone else was asleep.
As a security guard, he became a regular nightwalker on his late shifts.
Legends spoke of a mysterious nightwalker who appeared only under the full moon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: daywalker - a person who is active during the day
  • synonym: noctambulist - a person who walks at night, especially while asleep
  • synonym: sleepwalker - a person who walks around while asleep
