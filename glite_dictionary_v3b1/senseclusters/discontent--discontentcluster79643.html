discontent

adjective
feeling dissatisfied or unhappy
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt / (BrE)
feeling or showing dissatisfaction with one's situation; not content

Examples

Discontent workers
He grew increasingly discontent with his tedious daily routine.
Her discontent glance revealed how she felt about the decision.
Many employees felt discontent after the new policies were introduced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: content - feeling satisfied or pleased; opposite of discontent
  • synonym: dissatisfied - feeling an absence of satisfaction, similar to discontent
