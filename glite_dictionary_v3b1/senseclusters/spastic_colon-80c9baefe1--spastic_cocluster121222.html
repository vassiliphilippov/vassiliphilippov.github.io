spastic colon

noun
gastrointestinal disorder with recurring pain and irregular bowels
5.9 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈspæstɪk ˈkoʊlən / (AmE), / ˈspæstɪk ˈkəʊlən / (BrE)
A condition marked by recurrent abdominal pain and irregular bowel habits, such as alternating diarrhea and constipation; commonly associated with stress.

Examples

Irritable spastic colon
Managing stress can often help reduce the symptoms of spastic colon.
Many people with a spastic colon experience alternating periods of constipation and diarrhea.
My doctor diagnosed me with spastic colon after I described my recurring abdominal pain and bowel changes.
