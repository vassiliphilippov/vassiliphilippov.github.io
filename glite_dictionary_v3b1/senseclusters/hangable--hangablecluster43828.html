hangable

adjective
punishable by hanging
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhæŋəbəl / (BrE)
legally punishable by death by hanging; liable to be hanged as a penalty for a crime

Examples

Hangable crime
In the past, stealing a horse was considered a hangable offense in some countries.
The judge declared the crime hangable under the strict laws of that era.
Many old laws listed certain acts as hangable, though such punishments are now rare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capital - punishable by death
  • antonym: noncapital - not punishable by death
