hun

noun
affectionate address
Pronunciation: / hʌn / (AmE), / hʌn / (BrE)
an informal and affectionate term used to address someone you care about, such as a partner or close friend

Examples

Dear hun
Could you pass me the salt, hun?
Don't worry, hun, everything will be fine.
I love you, hun, and I'm always here for you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dear - a term of endearment for someone you care about
  • synonym: honey - an affectionate term for someone you love
