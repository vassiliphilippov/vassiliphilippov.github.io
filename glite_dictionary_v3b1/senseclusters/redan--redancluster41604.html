redan

noun
diagonal golf hole
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdæn / (AmE), / rɪˈdæn / (BrE)
a short par-3 golf hole or green set on a diagonal slope, often protected by bunkers, designed to challenge approach shots

Examples

Golf redan hole
The course features a famous redan that tests even experienced golfers.
He struggled to land his ball safely on the sloping redan green.
Many architects try to recreate the classic redan design in modern golf courses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bunker - a sand-filled hazard on a golf course
  • synonym: par-3 - a golf hole expected to be completed in three strokes
