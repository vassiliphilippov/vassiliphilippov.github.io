dustman

noun
male refuse collector
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstmæn / (AmE), / ˈdʌstmən / (BrE)
A man whose job is to collect and dispose of household rubbish.

Examples

The dustman collects rubbish
As a child, he wanted to be a dustman because he loved riding on the back of the truck.
The dustman comes early on Mondays to collect the rubbish from our street.
When the dustman arrived, he greeted Mrs. Potter with a friendly smile.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dustwoman - a female employee who collects and disposes of refuse
  • synonym: garbage collector - a person whose job is to collect household refuse
