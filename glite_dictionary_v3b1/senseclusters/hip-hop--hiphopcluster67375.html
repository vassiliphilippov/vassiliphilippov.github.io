hip-hop

noun
genre of rhythmic rap music and culture
3.0 4.44/m

A genre of popular music developed by African-American communities in the 1980s and 1990s, characterized by rhythmic spoken lyrics (rap) over a backing track; also refers to the broader cultural movement surrounding this music.

Examples

Hip-hop music
She grew up listening to classic hip-hop artists from the 1990s.
The festival featured live hip-hop performances by local and international stars.
The influence of hip-hop can be seen in fashion, language, and art worldwide.
