Synonyms

  • intimidate
    4.9 13/m
    intimidate means frightening someone or lowering their confidence to force them, while terrorize means causing severe fear through threats or violence.
  • terrify
    3.2 7.51/m
    terrify means causing extreme fear in someone, while terrorize means frightening to control people through repeated threats or violence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reassure - to say or do something to remove someone's doubts or fears
