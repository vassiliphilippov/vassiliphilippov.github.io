Ephippiorhynchus senegalensis

noun
African saddle-billed stork
Pronunciation: / ɛˌfɪpiəroʊˈrɪŋkəs sɛnəgəˈlɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ɛˌfɪpɪəˌrɪŋkəs sɛnɪgəˈlɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A large black-and-white stork native to tropical Africa, noted for its distinctive red bill with a black band around the middle; also known as the saddle-billed stork.
