southbound

adjective
going toward the south
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊθˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsaʊθˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of vehicles, routes, or movement) going or leading toward the south.

Examples

Southbound traffic
Heavy traffic was reported on the southbound lanes of the highway.
She boarded a southbound bus to visit her family in the next city.
The southbound train leaves at 10 a.m. every day.

  • antonym: northbound - going or leading toward the north
