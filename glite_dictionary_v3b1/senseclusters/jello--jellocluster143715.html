jello

noun
fruit-flavored gelatin dessert
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛləʊ / (BrE)
A sweet, fruit-flavored dessert made from flavored gelatin, usually served chilled and set.

Examples

A bowl of jello
After dinner, we had strawberry jello as a light dessert.
She made a bowl of jello for the children's party.
The hospital served lime jello as part of the lunch menu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gelatin - a colorless, flavorless ingredient from animal collagen, used in desserts like jello
