Synonyms

  • gelatin dessert
    2.8 0.01/m
    gelatin dessert is a general name for sweet dishes made by dissolving gelatin and chilling, while jello is the common fruit-flavored example.
  • Jell-O
    2.8 1.08/m
    Jell-O is a commercial powdered mix sold for quick fruit-flavored desserts, while jello is the general chilled, set flavored gelatin.
  • jelly
    1.0 1.55/m
    jelly is a softer, semifluid fruit food often used as a glaze or spread, while jello is a firmer chilled, set dessert.
