nonfungible

adjective
unique, not interchangeable
9.3
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈfʌn.dʒə.bəl/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈfʌn.dʒɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈfʌn.dʒə.bəl/ /; / /nɒn.ˈfʌn.dʒɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
describing something that is unique and cannot be exchanged or replaced by another item because of its distinct characteristics (often used for digital tokens or assets)

Examples

Nonfungible token
Each nonfungible token represents a specific digital artwork that cannot be duplicated.
The value of a nonfungible asset comes from its uniqueness and ownership record.
Collectors are interested in nonfungible items because they cannot be exchanged for identical goods.
A nonfungible token represents a unique digital asset that cannot be exchanged for another.
Each piece of original artwork is nonfungible because it cannot be exactly duplicated.
The certificate proves the item is nonfungible and one of a kind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fungible - able to be exchanged or replaced by another identical item
  • synonym: irreplaceable - impossible to replace because of being unique
  • synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind
