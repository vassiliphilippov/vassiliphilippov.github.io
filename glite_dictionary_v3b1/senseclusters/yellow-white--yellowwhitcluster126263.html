yellow-white

adjective
pale white color with a yellow tint
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ waɪt / (BrE)
of a white color tinged with yellow; having a pale yellowish white hue.

Examples

A yellow-white color
Her blouse had a delicate yellow-white hue, making it look vintage.
The yellow-white flowers stood out against the lush green leaves.
The walls were painted a soft yellow-white that brightened the entire room.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cream - a pale yellowish-white color
  • synonym: off-white - a color shade between white and cream, slightly less pure than white
