quiescent

adjective
not active or causing symptoms (medical)
7.2
Pronunciation: / kwiˈɛsənt / (AmE), / kwiˈɛsənt / (BrE)
(of a disease or medical condition) not currently active; causing no symptoms.

Examples

Quiescent tumor
Despite its size, the quiescent tumor produced no noticeable symptoms in the patient.
Some cancers may remain quiescent for years before becoming aggressive.
The doctor explained that the infection was currently quiescent and did not require immediate treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - (of a disease) producing symptoms, not dormant
