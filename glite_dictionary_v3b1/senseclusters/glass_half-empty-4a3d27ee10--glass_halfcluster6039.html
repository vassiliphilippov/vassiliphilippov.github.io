glass half-empty

adjective
pessimistic attitude or viewpoint
5.0 5.2
Pronunciation: /glæs hæf ˈemp.tiː/ /; / /glɑːs hɑːf ˈemp.tiː/ (AmE), /gɑːs hɑːf ˈemp.tɪ/ /; / /glæs hɑːf ˈemp.tɪ/ (BrE)
used to describe a pessimistic attitude or tendency to see the negative side of a situation, often contrasted with 'glass half-full'.

Examples

A glass half-empty attitude
Don't approach challenges with a glass half-empty mindset.
Even when we achieve great results, she still sees the glass half-empty.
His glass half-empty outlook often discourages others around him.
