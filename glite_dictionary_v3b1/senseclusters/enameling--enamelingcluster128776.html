enameling

noun
decorative metal coating
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnæməlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈnæməlɪŋ / (BrE)
the art or process of applying a glassy, decorative coating to metal surfaces

Examples

Enameling metal jewelry
She took a class in enameling to learn how to decorate metal jewelry.
The museum displayed beautiful bowls created through the art of enameling.
Traditional enameling techniques can produce vibrant colors and intricate designs on metal objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enamelwork - objects or art created using enamel on metal
  • synonym: gilding - the process of applying gold leaf or gold paint to a surface
