blue

noun
the color of a clear sky
1.0
Pronunciation: / bluː / (AmE), / bluː / (BrE)
a color like that of a clear sky on a sunny day; the color between green and violet in the spectrum

Examples

A blue dress

The artist mixed yellow and blue to create a vibrant green for his painting.
The sky turned a deep blue just before nightfall.
