rubbing alcohol

noun
antiseptic solution for external use
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbɪŋ ˌælkəˌhɑl / (AmE), / ˈrʌbɪŋ ˌælkəhɒl / (BrE)
a liquid preparation of isopropyl alcohol or denatured ethanol used externally as a disinfectant or antiseptic

Examples

Rubbing alcohol disinfectant
Rubbing alcohol evaporates quickly, making it useful for cleaning surfaces.
Always keep rubbing alcohol out of reach of children due to its toxicity.
He cleaned the cut on his arm with rubbing alcohol and a cotton pad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isopropyl alcohol - type of alcohol used in disinfectants
