future

adjective
pertaining to a time after the present
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃər /; / ˈfjuːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊːtʃə /; / ˈfjuːtʃə / (BrE)
relating to something that will happen or exist later; expected or anticipated

Many future events are impossible to predict with certainty.
She is saving money for her future career as a doctor.
The company is investing in new technology to ensure future growth.
