basal temperature

noun
lowest body temperature at complete rest
4.0 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsəl ˈtɛmpərətʃər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsəl ˈtɛmpərətjʊə / (BrE)
the body temperature measured in the morning before rising, moving, or eating, reflecting the body's lowest resting temperature

Examples

Morning basal temperature
A fever may not be evident if only the basal temperature is considered.
Changes in basal temperature can indicate the onset of ovulation.
To track her fertility, she measured her basal temperature every morning.
