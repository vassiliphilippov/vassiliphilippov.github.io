orchid cactus

noun
epiphytic cactus with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrkɪd ˌkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːkɪd ˌkæktəs / (BrE)
A cactus of the genus Epiphyllum, having flattened, jointed, irregularly branching stems and large, showy tubular flowers.

Examples

The orchid cactus flower
Gardeners prize the orchid cactus for its unique, flattened stems and striking blossoms.
She keeps an orchid cactus on her windowsill because of its beautiful flowers.
The orchid cactus produces stunning, brightly colored blooms each spring.
