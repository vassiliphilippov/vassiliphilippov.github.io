levelly

adverb
in a flat way
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəli / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəli / (BrE)
in a way that is flat, even, or at the same height throughout

Examples

Stack levelly
The books were stacked levelly on the shelf, creating a neat appearance.
He poured the concrete levelly to ensure the floor would be smooth.
The table must stand levelly so that nothing rolls off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evenly - in a smooth, equal, or regular way
  • antonym: unevenly - in a way that is not flat or regular
