make a pit stop

verb
take a brief break during a journey
Pronunciation: / meɪk ə pɪt stɑp / (AmE), / meɪk ə pɪt stɒp / (BrE)
to stop briefly during a journey, especially to use the restroom or get refreshments, before continuing on your way

Examples

To make a pit stop quickly
Can we make a pit stop before the next meeting? I need to stretch my legs.
It's a long drive; let's make a pit stop somewhere to freshen up.
We decided to make a pit stop at the gas station for snacks and coffee.
