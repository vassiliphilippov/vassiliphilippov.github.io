fiber optic microphone

noun
microphone using light in optical fibers to detect sound
3.2 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbɚ ɑptɪk ˈmaɪkrəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə ˈɒptɪk ˈmaɪkrəfəʊn / (BrE)
A microphone that detects sound by sensing changes in light intensity in an optical fiber, rather than using electrical capacitance or magnetism.

Examples

Fiber optic microphone technology
A fiber optic microphone can operate in environments with high levels of electromagnetic interference.
Engineers chose a fiber optic microphone for the infrasound monitoring project due to its immunity to electrical noise.
The laboratory installed a fiber optic microphone to measure subtle vibrations in the experimental chamber.
