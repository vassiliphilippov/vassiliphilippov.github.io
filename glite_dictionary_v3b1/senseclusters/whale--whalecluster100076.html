whale

noun
large marine mammal with a blowhole
1.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /weɪl/ /; / /hweɪl/ (AmE), /weɪl/ (BrE)
a very large marine mammal with a streamlined body and a blowhole on the head for breathing, belonging to the order Cetacea
