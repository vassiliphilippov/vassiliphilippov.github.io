bullishly

adverb
with optimism
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that shows confidence or optimism, especially about prices or markets rising

Examples

To invest bullishly
Traders reacted bullishly to the news of the merger, expecting stock prices to rise.
The CEO spoke bullishly about the company's future growth during the press conference.
Many investors behaved bullishly after the government announced new economic policies.

Related Terms
