Christella

noun
genus of tropical terrestrial ferns
9.9
Pronunciation: / kraɪˈstɛlə / (AmE), / krɪˈstɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of medium to large terrestrial ferns found in tropical forests across the Old World and the Americas.

Examples

A Christella fern
Botanists recently discovered a new Christella species inhabiting South American riverbanks.
Many species within the Christella genus thrive in the humid understory of Southeast Asian rainforests.
The delicate fronds of Christella ferns are distinguished by their finely divided leaves.
