windrower

noun
crop-cutting machine
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌroʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndrəʊə / (BrE)
a machine that cuts crops and arranges them in rows to dry before further processing

Examples

A windrower machine
The farmer operated the windrower to cut and arrange the wheat in neat rows.
After using the windrower, the crops dried faster in the sun.
A modern windrower can cover large fields efficiently during harvest season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combine harvester - a machine that harvests and processes crops
  • synonym: mower - a machine used to cut grass or crops
