furnished

adjective
supplied with furniture or equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝnɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪʃt / (BrE)
(of a room, house, or container) supplied with the necessary furniture or equipment.

Examples

A furnished room
She bought a furnished office space to avoid the hassle of buying desks and chairs.
The cottage comes fully furnished, so you only need to bring your clothes.
They moved into a furnished apartment near the city center.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unfurnished - not supplied with furniture or equipment
