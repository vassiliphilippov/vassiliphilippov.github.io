lay

verb
produce or deposit eggs (animals)
Pronunciation: / leɪ / (AmE), / leɪ / (BrE)
(of a bird, insect, reptile, amphibian, or fish) to produce and deposit eggs.

Examples

To lay eggs
Chickens usually lay eggs once a day during their peak season.
Not all hens lay every day; it depends on their breed and health.
The turtle came ashore to lay her eggs in the sand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: produce - to bring forth or yield, especially as a natural product
  • synonym: spawn - (of fish, amphibians, mollusks, etc.) to release or deposit eggs
