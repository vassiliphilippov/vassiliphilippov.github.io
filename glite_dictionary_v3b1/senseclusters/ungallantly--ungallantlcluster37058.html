ungallantly

adverb
rudely or unchivalrously
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡæl.ənt.li / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡæl.ənt.li / (BrE)
in a way that is not polite, brave, or chivalrous

Examples

To act ungallantly
He ungallantly refused to offer his seat to the elderly woman on the bus.
The knight ungallantly ignored the plea for help from the villagers.
She was disappointed when he ungallantly left her to carry the heavy bags alone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chivalrously - in a polite, brave, or gentlemanly manner
  • synonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful manner
