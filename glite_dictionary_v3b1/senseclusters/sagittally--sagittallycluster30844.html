sagittally

adverb
in left-right plane
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsædʒɪtəli / (AmE), / ˈsædʒɪtəli / (BrE)
in a direction or plane that divides the body into left and right parts

Examples

Images taken sagittally
The MRI images were taken sagittally to provide a clear view of the brain's structure.
The surgeon made an incision sagittally to access the spinal cord.
For this experiment, the animal's body was sectioned sagittally for detailed analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: axially - in a direction or plane dividing the body into upper and lower parts
  • antonym: coronally - in a direction or plane dividing the body into front and back parts
