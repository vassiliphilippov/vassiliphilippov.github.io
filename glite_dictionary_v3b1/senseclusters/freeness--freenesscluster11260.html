freeness

noun
state of being free
6.7 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfriÀên…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàfriÀên…ôs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being free, without restrictions or limitations

Examples

Freeness of movement
The freeness of the open-source license encourages innovation and collaboration.
She admired the freeness with which the artist expressed his ideas.
Many people value the freeness of being able to travel without restrictions.

Synonyms

  • liberty
    3.2 0.60/m

  • independence
    3.0 35/m
    independence is freedom from control or influence by others, often used for countries, while freeness is any general state of being free.

  • synonym: freedom - the power or right to act, speak, or think without hindrance
