woohoo

interjection
excitement exclamation
7.4
Pronunciation: / wuːˈhuː / (AmE), / wuːˈhuː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express excitement, joy, or triumph

Examples

Woohoo with excitement
When she saw her exam results, she shouted woohoo and jumped up in excitement.
The team scored the winning goal, and the crowd erupted with a loud woohoo.
After months of hard work, he finally finished the marathon and yelled woohoo at the finish line.

  • synonym: hooray - an exclamation of joy or approval
  • synonym: yay - an exclamation expressing happiness or excitement
