East Indian rosewood

noun
tree with valuable dark wood, native to India
5.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /iːst ˈɪn.diː.ən ˈroʊz.wʊd/ (AmE), /iːst ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈrəʊz.wʊd/ (BrE)
