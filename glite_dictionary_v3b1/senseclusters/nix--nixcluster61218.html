Examples

To nix a plan
If you don't like the plan, just nix it and suggest something else.

Synonyms

  • reject
    1.9 52/m
    reject is a formal action of refusing to accept or approve something, while nix is more casual and can also stop plans.
  • refuse
    1.7 41/m
    refuse is to say no to a request or offer directly, while nix is a more casual word often used to cancel plans.
  • veto
    4.0 5.93/m
    veto is an official rejection by a person or body with power, while nix is informal and used in everyday speech.
  • rule out
    3.5 11/m
    rule out means to decide an option is not possible and remove it from consideration, while nix means to cancel or stop it.
