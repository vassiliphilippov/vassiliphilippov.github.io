arthroplasty

noun
surgical joint reconstruction or replacement
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrθroʊˌplæsti / (AmE), / ˈɑːθrəʊˌplɑːsti / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to reconstruct or replace a damaged or deformed joint

Examples

Arthroplasty surgery
After years of pain, she underwent arthroplasty to replace her damaged knee joint.
Hip arthroplasty has become increasingly common among elderly patients.
The surgeon explained that arthroplasty might restore mobility in the affected area.
