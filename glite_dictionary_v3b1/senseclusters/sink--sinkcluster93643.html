sink

noun
basin fixture for washing with drain
Pronunciation: / sɪŋk / (AmE), / sɪŋk / (BrE)
a large open container in a kitchen, bathroom, etc. fixed to a wall or floor, used for washing things and equipped with a drain for water to escape

Examples

Kitchen sink
Please don't leave dirty cups in the sink.
She washed the dishes in the sink after dinner.
The bathroom sink was clogged with hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basin - a bowl-shaped container for liquids
