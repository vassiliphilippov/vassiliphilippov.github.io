Synonyms

  • mythify
    8.9 0.01/m
    mythify makes someone or something seem legendary by exaggerating qualities, while mythologize treats it as an idealized public myth.
  • mythicize
    9.9 0.00/m
    mythicize gives things a mythical quality or reads events as symbolic, while mythologize often builds a full legendary story.
