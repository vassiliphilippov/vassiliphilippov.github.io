murph

noun
slang for mistake
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɜrf / (AmE), / mɜːf / (BrE)
a slang term for a mistake or blunder, especially in sports or military contexts

Examples

A big murph in sports
He made a costly murph during the final play, which led to the team's defeat.
After that murph in the first half, the coach decided to substitute him.
In the military, a simple murph can have serious consequences for the whole unit.
