available

adjective
not busy; free to do something
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈveɪləbəl / (AmE), / əˈveɪləbəl / (BrE)
(of a person) not busy, free to do something or to see someone.

Examples

Available to meet
I'm sorry, but Jessica isn't available right now; can I take a message?
Is Dr. Smith available to meet with me on Friday afternoon?
Let me check if the manager is available for a quick call.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unavailable - not free or not able to do something
