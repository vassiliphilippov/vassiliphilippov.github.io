finagler

noun
person who uses clever or tricky means
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈneɪɡlər / (AmE), / fɪˈneɪɡlə / (BrE)
a person who obtains something or achieves a goal by using clever, tricky, or dishonest methods

Examples

A finagler tricks others
He's such a finagler that he managed to score free tickets using a fake contest.
No one trusted the finagler who always had a new scheme up his sleeve.
The company suspected a finagler was manipulating the accounting records to hide losses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheat - a person who acts dishonestly in order to gain an advantage
  • antonym: honest person - a person who tells the truth and does not cheat or lie
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats or defrauds others
