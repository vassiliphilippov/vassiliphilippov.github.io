sudarium

noun
liturgical cloth
9.9
Pronunciation: / suˈdɛəriəm / (AmE), / suˈdɛəriəm / (BrE)
a liturgical cloth used in Christian worship to cover sacred vessels or to wipe the priest's hands

Examples

Liturgical sudarium
The priest carefully unfolded the sudarium before beginning the Eucharistic prayer.
During the ceremony, the deacon used the sudarium to wipe the chalice.
A beautifully embroidered sudarium was placed over the sacred vessels on the altar.
