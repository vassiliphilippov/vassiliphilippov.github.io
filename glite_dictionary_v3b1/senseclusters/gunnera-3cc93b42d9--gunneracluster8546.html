Gunnera

proper noun
large-leaved plant genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnərə / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnərə / (BrE)
a genus of large-leaved perennial plants commonly found in wet or marshy areas

Examples

Gunnera plant leaves
The leaves of Gunnera can grow to over a meter across in the right conditions.
Gardeners often plant Gunnera near ponds to create a dramatic, tropical effect.
During the summer, the massive Gunnera leaves provide shade for smaller plants.
