flowering raspberry

noun
North American shrub with pink-purple flowers and red fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚɪŋ ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈræzˌbɛri / (BrE)
a shrub native to eastern North America, with showy pink to purple flowers and red or orange thimble-shaped fruit

Examples

The flowering raspberry shrub
The flowering raspberry provides vibrant color to gardens in the summer months.
The red fruit of the flowering raspberry is shaped like a thimble and attracts many birds.
We spotted a flowering raspberry growing wild along the hiking trail.
