enter

verb
record or write down officially
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tɚ /; / ˈɛntɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə /; / ˈɛntə / (BrE)
to record or put information into a system, form, or computer

Examples

To enter data

She carefully entered the data into the computer system.
The clerk entered the transaction into the company’s records.
Remember to enter your password before submitting the form.
She carefully entered the ingredients into the mixing bowl one at a time.
You can enter your measurements into the spreadsheet for automatic calculations.
