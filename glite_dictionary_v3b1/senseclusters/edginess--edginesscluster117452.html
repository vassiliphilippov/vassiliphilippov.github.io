edginess

noun
bold or unsettling quality
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈedʒ.iː.nəs/ /; / /ˈedʒ.ɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈedʒ.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈedʒ.iː.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being bold, unconventional, or slightly unsettling, often creating a sense of excitement or tension

Examples

Artistic edginess
The director's new film has a certain edginess that sets it apart from mainstream movies.
Her fashion choices always show a bit of edginess and creativity.
The edginess of the music made the concert feel exciting and unpredictable.
