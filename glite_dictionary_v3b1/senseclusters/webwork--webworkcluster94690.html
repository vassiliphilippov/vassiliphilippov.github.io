webwork

noun
web-like structure
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈwɛbˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a structure or pattern that resembles a web, often consisting of interconnected lines or threads

Examples

A webwork of cracks
The old ceiling was covered in a fine webwork of cracks.
Engineers admired the steel webwork supporting the bridge.
A delicate webwork of roots spread through the soil beneath the tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mesh - a material made of a network of wire or thread
  • synonym: network - a system of interconnected elements or lines
