Christian

noun
follower of Christianity
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.tʃən / (BrE)
A person who believes in Jesus Christ and follows the religion based on his teachings.

Examples

A Christian believer
Early Christians often faced persecution for their faith.
He became a Christian after studying the teachings of Jesus.
She was raised as a Christian and goes to church every Sunday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of God
  • synonym: believer - a person who has faith in a particular religion or idea
  • antonym: nonbeliever - a person who does not believe in a particular religion or idea
