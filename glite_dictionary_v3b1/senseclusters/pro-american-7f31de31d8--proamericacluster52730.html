pro-American

adjective
supporting the United States
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
supporting the United States and its policies or interests.

Examples

Pro-American views
His pro-American views influenced the company's decision to expand into the US market.
The film received both praise and backlash for its pro-American message.
The new government's pro-American stance has drawn criticism from neighboring countries.
