Nobel

proper noun
international prize
4.2
Pronunciation: / noʊˈbɛl / (AmE), / nəʊˈbɛl / (BrE)
relating to the international prizes awarded annually for outstanding achievements in fields such as peace, literature, and science

Examples

Nobel ceremony
She was honored at the Nobel ceremony in Stockholm.
Winning a Nobel is considered one of the highest achievements in science.
The Nobel committee announced the new laureates for literature and peace.
