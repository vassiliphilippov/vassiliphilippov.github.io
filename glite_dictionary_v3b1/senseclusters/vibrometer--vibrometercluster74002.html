vibrometer

noun
vibration measuring device
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈbrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / vaɪˈbrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the amount or frequency of vibration

Examples

A vibrometer device
The technician used a vibrometer to monitor the vibrations of the engine.
A vibrometer can help detect problems in rotating machinery before they become serious.
Researchers placed a vibrometer on the bridge to measure its response to traffic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accelerometer - a device that measures acceleration or vibration
  • synonym: seismograph - an instrument that measures and records details of earthquakes
