flooded gum

noun
Australian eucalyptus tree of moist areas
7.7 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌdɪd ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈflʌdɪd ɡʌm / (BrE)
Any of several Australian eucalyptus trees that grow on moist or alluvial soil, especially Eucalyptus grandis or related species.
