cyclamen

noun
Mediterranean flowering plant, houseplant
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkləmən / (AmE), / ˈsɪkləmən / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant widely grown as a houseplant, known for its attractive dark green leaves with silver markings and nodding flowers with reflexed petals in white, pink, or red.

Examples

A cyclamen plant
Many people choose cyclamen for indoor decoration because of its attractive foliage and flowers.
She placed a pot of cyclamen on the windowsill to brighten the room.
The cyclamen blooms each winter, adding vibrant color to the garden.
