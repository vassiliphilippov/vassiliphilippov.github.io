folkie

adjective
in folk music style
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊki / (AmE), / ˈfəʊki / (BrE)
having the style, sound, or qualities of folk music, especially in a simple and traditional manner

Examples

Folkie style music
The singer's folkie voice brought a warm, nostalgic feeling to the performance.
They decorated the café in a folkie style with wooden tables and handmade crafts.
Her new album has a distinctly folkie sound, featuring acoustic guitars and simple melodies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folk - relating to traditional music or culture
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times, not traditional
  • synonym: traditional - following customs or styles from the past
