rose quartz

noun
a pinkish variety of quartz gemstone
Pronunciation: / roʊz ˈkwɔrts / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈkwɔːts / (BrE)
a translucent pink to rose-red variety of quartz used as a gemstone and for ornamental purposes

Examples

A rose quartz crystal
A large piece of rose quartz was displayed in the museum’s gemstone section.
She collects various minerals, but rose quartz is her favorite because of its color.
The necklace featured a pendant made of rose quartz that shimmered in the light.
