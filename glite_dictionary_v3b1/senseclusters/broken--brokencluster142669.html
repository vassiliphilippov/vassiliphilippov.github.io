broken

adjective
overpowered; unbalanced (gaming)
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbroʊ.kən/ /; / /ˈbroʊ.kɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbrəʊ.kən/ (BrE)
(informal, gaming) (of a character, weapon, ability, or mechanic) excessively strong or unbalanced, making the game unfair or trivial; sometimes caused by a bug or exploit.

Examples

Broken game character
After the patch, the broken weapon dominated every match, forcing the developers to nerf it.
Players complained that the new hero was broken, winning duels without skill or counterplay.
Some argue a powerful ability isn't broken unless it removes thoughtful choices from gameplay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overpowered - too strong in relation to other options, reducing fair competition
  • synonym: unbalanced - not properly balanced, giving an unfair advantage
