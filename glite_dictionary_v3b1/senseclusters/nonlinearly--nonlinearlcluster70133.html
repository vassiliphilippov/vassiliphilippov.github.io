nonlinearly

adverb
not in straight proportion
7.5
Pronunciation: /nən.ˈlɪn.iː.ər.liː/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈlɪn.iː.ər.liː/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈlɪn.ɪə.liː/ /; / /nən.ˈlɪn.ɪə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not follow a straight, proportional relationship, often involving complex or unpredictable changes.

Examples

System reacts nonlinearly
The system responds nonlinearly to small changes in temperature, making predictions difficult.
When the input increases, the output sometimes grows nonlinearly rather than at a constant rate.
In complex models, variables often interact nonlinearly, leading to unexpected results.
