impacted

adjective
wedged or packed in; unable to move
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæktɪd / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæktɪd / (BrE)
(of a tooth or other structure) firmly wedged or packed in a position so that it cannot move normally, often causing obstruction.

Examples

An impacted tooth
An impacted colon can cause severe discomfort and requires medical treatment.
Her X-ray revealed an impacted molar that had not erupted properly.
The dentist said my wisdom tooth is impacted and needs to be removed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impacted tooth - a tooth that has failed to emerge fully into its expected position
