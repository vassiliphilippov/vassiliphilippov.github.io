protectorship

noun
the office or position of a protector
8.4
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktəʃɪp / (BrE)
the office or position of being a protector, especially one who exercises authority over a territory, country, or group

Examples

The protectorship of a country
During the nineteenth century, some regions fell under the protectorship of European powers.
His appointment to the protectorship was met with strong opposition from rival factions.
The ceremony marked the official beginning of her protectorship over the disputed territory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protectorate - a state or territory that is protected and partially controlled by a more powerful state
