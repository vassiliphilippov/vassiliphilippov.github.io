Synonyms

  • coastal
    2.4 18/m
    coastal names land or areas next to the sea and is the common adjective, while coastside is a less common, similar term.
  • shoreside
    4.1 0.02/m
    shoreside means near the edge of a shore, often at water's edge, while coastside refers more broadly to land along the coast.
  • nearshore
    3.5 0.55/m
    nearshore describes places very close to the shore, often in sea or water contexts, while coastside refers to areas along the coast.
  • inshore
    4.2 1.58/m
    inshore refers to activity or water close to land used at sea, while coastside describes land or places along the coast.
