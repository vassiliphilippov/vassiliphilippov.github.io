coastside

adjective
near the coast
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊstˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstˌsaɪd / (BrE)
located on or relating to the coast; near the sea

Examples

Coastside village
They bought a small coastside cottage to enjoy the ocean views on weekends.
The coastside climate is milder than that of the inland areas.
Many tourists are attracted to the coastside towns during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastal - located on or near a coast
  • antonym: inland - situated away from the coast
