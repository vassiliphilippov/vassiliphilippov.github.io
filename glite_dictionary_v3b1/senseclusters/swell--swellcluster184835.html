swell

noun
fashionable man concerned with appearance
9.9 7.8
Pronunciation: / swɛl / (AmE), / swɛl / (BrE)
a man who is very concerned with his dress and appearance, especially in a fashionable or showy way; a dandy

Examples

A nineteenth-century swell
Back in the nineteenth century, a swell would never be seen without a top hat and cane.
He regarded himself as quite the swell when he arrived at the party in his tailored suit.
The novel describes the extravagant lifestyle of a wealthy swell in Victorian London.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dandy - a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress
  • synonym: fop - a man excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance
