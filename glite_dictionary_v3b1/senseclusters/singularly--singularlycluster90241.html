singularly

adverb
in a remarkable or unusual manner
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡjɚli / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡjʊləli / (BrE)
In a way that is very remarkable, unusual, or distinctive.

Examples

A singularly unusual event
He was singularly unconcerned about the consequences of his actions.
Her performance was singularly impressive, earning her a standing ovation from the audience.
The house is singularly well designed, combining elegance with functionality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinarily - in a usual or normal manner
  • synonym: remarkably - in a way that is worthy of attention; very notably
