attractive

adjective
pleasing or appealing; charming
Pronunciation: / əˈtræktɪv / (AmE), / əˈtræktɪv / (BrE)
pleasing or appealing to the senses, mind, or because of qualities that arouse interest or make something desirable

Examples

An attractive person
He found her confidence even more attractive than her looks.
She wore an attractive red dress to the party.
The apartment is small but very attractive to young professionals.
Many people found the idea of working abroad attractive due to the opportunity for personal growth.
The company offered an attractive benefits package to draw in new employees.
This investment plan looks particularly attractive to young professionals starting their careers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appealing - arousing interest or admiration
  • synonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • synonym: handsome - good-looking, especially used for men
  • antonym: unattractive - not pleasing or appealing in appearance or character
