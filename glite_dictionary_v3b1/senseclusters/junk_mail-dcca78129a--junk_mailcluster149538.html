junk mail

noun
unwanted advertising mail
1.9
Pronunciation: / dʒʌŋk meɪl / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk meɪl / (BrE)
unwanted or unsolicited mail, especially advertisements sent in bulk to a large number of people

Examples

Receive junk mail
Every day, my mailbox is full of junk mail that I never asked for.
She threw all the junk mail straight into the recycling bin.
Signing up online can often result in receiving more junk mail at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spam - unsolicited or unwanted email, typically advertisements
