shower

noun
act of washing under sprayed water
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə / (BrE)
an act of washing your body under a spray of water, or the device that sprays the water

Examples

Take a shower
After jogging, he enjoyed a long, hot shower to relax his muscles.
Please remember to turn off the water after your shower.
The hotel room comes with a private bathroom and a walk-in shower.
She prefers taking a shower in the morning to help wake up.
The hotel room included a spacious walk-in shower.
We replaced our old bathtub with a modern shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathtub - a large container for holding water in which you wash your body
