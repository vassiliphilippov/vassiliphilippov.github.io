needlebush

noun
Australian shrub with needle-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈniː.dl̩.bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈniː.dl̩.bʊʃ / (BrE)
a shrub with stiff, needle-shaped leaves and white flowers, native to eastern Australia

Examples

The needlebush shrub
Gardeners value the needlebush for its hardiness and attractive white flowers.
The needlebush is commonly found in sandy soils along the Australian coastline.
Wildlife often seek shelter among the dense branches of the needlebush.
