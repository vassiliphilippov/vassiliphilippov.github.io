kidskin

noun
soft leather from a young goat
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪdˌskɪn / (BrE)
soft, smooth leather made from the hide of a young goat, often used for gloves and fine garments

Examples

A kidskin purse
A fine purse made of black kidskin lay on the table.
She wore elegant kidskin gloves to the party.
The boots were crafted from supple kidskin for maximum comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leather - material made from animal hide, processed and tanned
  • synonym: suede - leather with a napped finish, often softer
