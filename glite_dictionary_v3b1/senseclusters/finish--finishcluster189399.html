finish

verb
apply a final surface or treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
to put a final surface or treatment on (an object), so as to complete its appearance or usability

Examples

To finish the surface
After sanding, you should finish the tabletop with a protective oil.
The woodworker took great care to finish the cabinet with a glossy varnish.
They hired a professional to finish their hardwood floors before moving in.
