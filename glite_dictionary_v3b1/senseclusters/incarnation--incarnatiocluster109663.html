Examples

The incarnation of kindness
His generosity was seen as the incarnation of kindness among his peers.
Some fans saw her as the very incarnation of honesty and intelligence.

Synonyms

  • embodiment
    5.5 4.82/m
    embodiment is a clear, visible example of an idea in real life, while incarnation is a specific person or form that represents that idea.
  • personification
    6.5 0.69/m
    personification gives human qualities to an idea in speech or story, while incarnation names a real person or thing that embodies it.
  • epitome
    7.4 1.87/m
    epitome is the perfect or typical example of a quality, while incarnation is a person or thing that actually embodies that quality.
  • avatar
    6.8 0.59/m
    avatar is often a symbolic or new form representing an idea, while incarnation is a direct person or thing embodying that same quality.
