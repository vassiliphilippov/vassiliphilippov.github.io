Volta

proper noun
river in Ghana, West Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊltə / (AmE), / ˈvɒltə / (BrE)
a major river in Ghana that flows south to the Bight of Benin

Examples

Volta River
Many local communities depend on the Volta for fishing and transportation.
The Volta River basin plays a vital role in Ghana's agriculture and energy production.
The construction of Akosombo Dam has transformed the lower Volta into Lake Volta, one of the world's largest man-made lakes.
