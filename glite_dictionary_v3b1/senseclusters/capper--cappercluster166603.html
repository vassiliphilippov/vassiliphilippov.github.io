capper

noun
final touch
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpər / (AmE), / ˈkæpə / (BrE)
something that completes or finishes a series of events, or a person or thing that puts a cap on something.

Examples

The final capper
The surprise announcement was the perfect capper to an already exciting day.
After a long week, their victory served as a fitting capper to the tournament.
The chef added a sprinkle of herbs as a capper to the delicious meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climax - the most exciting or important part of something
  • synonym: finale - the last part of something, especially a performance or event
  • antonym: introduction - the beginning part of something
