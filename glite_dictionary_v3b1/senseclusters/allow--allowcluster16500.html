allow

verb
to allocate or set aside (resources)
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊ / (AmE), / əˈlaʊ / (BrE)
to give or assign a resource, such as money or time, to a particular person or cause; to set aside for a specific purpose

Examples

To allow extra time
Please allow enough time for questions at the end of your presentation.
The committee will allow additional funds to support the new project.
They allowed a portion of their budget for emergency expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allocate - to distribute resources for a particular purpose
  • synonym: earmark - to designate funds or resources for a specific use
