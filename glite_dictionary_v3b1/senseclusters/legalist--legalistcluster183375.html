legalist

noun
strict rule follower
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɡəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who strictly follows or interprets laws or rules, often focusing on legal details

Examples

A strict legalist
As a known legalist, she always made sure every contract was followed to the letter.
The committee's legalist approach sometimes slowed down decision-making.
He was criticized for being a legalist who valued rules over compassion.
As a legalist, he refused to bend the rules even for minor issues.
The committee was led by a known legalist who valued procedure above all else.
