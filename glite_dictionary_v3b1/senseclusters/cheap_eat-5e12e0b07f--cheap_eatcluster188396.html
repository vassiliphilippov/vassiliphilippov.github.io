cheap eat

noun
inexpensive meal or dish
2.8
Pronunciation: / tʃip it / (AmE), / tʃiːp iːt / (BrE)
An inexpensive meal or dish, typically offered by restaurants, cafes, or food vendors.

Examples

Delicious cheap eats
After a long day of sightseeing, they enjoyed some delicious cheap eats at the night market.
Many students look for cheap eats near the campus to save money on food.
The guidebook lists the city's best cheap eats, from food trucks to hole-in-the-wall diners.
