kokanee

noun
landlocked sockeye salmon
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkæni / (AmE), / kəˈkɑːni / (BrE)
a landlocked variety of sockeye salmon native to lakes in North America

Examples

A kokanee salmon
Anglers travel from far away to catch a kokanee in the mountain lakes.
The kokanee population has increased due to careful wildlife management.
We grilled the freshly caught kokanee for dinner at the campsite.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chinook salmon - a large species of Pacific salmon
  • synonym: sockeye salmon - a species of salmon found in the northern Pacific Ocean and rivers
