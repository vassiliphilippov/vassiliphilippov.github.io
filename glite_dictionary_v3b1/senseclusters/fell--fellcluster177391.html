fell

noun
dressed animal skin
Pronunciation: / fɛl / (AmE), / fɛl / (BrE)
the dressed skin of an animal, especially a large animal, prepared for use

Examples

A fell cloak
In medieval times, a fell was often used to make warm cloaks.
The leatherworker inspected the fell before crafting the saddle.
The market had a stall selling sheepskin fells for rugs and clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hide - the skin of an animal, especially when tanned or dressed
  • synonym: pelt - the skin of an animal with the fur or hair still on it
