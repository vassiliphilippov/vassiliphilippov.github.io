abridgeable

adjective
able to be limited

Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbrɪdʒ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbrɪdʒ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)

Some legal protections are abridgeable, while others must always be upheld.
