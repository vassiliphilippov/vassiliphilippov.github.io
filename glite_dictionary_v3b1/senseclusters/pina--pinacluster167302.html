pina

noun
pineapple fiber fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnə / (AmE), / ˈpiːnə / (BrE)
a fine textile fiber or fabric made from pineapple leaves, traditionally used in the Philippines for clothing and embroidery

Examples

Pina fabric dress
The traditional Filipino dress was made from delicate pina fabric, giving it a light and elegant appearance.
Artisans in the Philippines still weave pina by hand for special garments and table linens.
She admired the intricate embroidery on the pina handkerchief her grandmother gave her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abaca - a fiber from a banana plant used for making textiles in the Philippines
  • synonym: linen - a textile made from flax fibers, known for its smoothness and strength
