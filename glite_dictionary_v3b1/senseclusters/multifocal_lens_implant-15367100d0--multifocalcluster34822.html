multifocal lens implant

noun
artificial eye lens for near and far focus
6.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.ˈfoʊ.kəl lɛnz ˈɪm.plænt/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.ˈfəʊ.kəl lɛnz ˈɪm.plænt/ (BrE)
an artificial lens implanted in the eye that has multiple zones to provide clear vision at different distances, allowing simultaneous focus on near and far objects

Examples

A multifocal lens implant surgery
Advances in multifocal lens implants have greatly improved vision outcomes for many patients.
After cataract surgery, she opted for a multifocal lens implant to help her see clearly at all distances.
The multifocal lens implant allows patients to reduce their dependence on reading glasses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intraocular lens - a lens implanted in the eye, usually after cataract removal
  • degree lower: monofocal lens implant - an artificial lens implant providing focus at only one distance
