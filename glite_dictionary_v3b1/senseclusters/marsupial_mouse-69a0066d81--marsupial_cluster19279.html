marsupial mouse

noun
small mouse-like marsupial
9.4 8.4
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈsupiəl maʊs / (AmE), / mɑːˈsuːpɪəl maʊs / (BrE)
any of various small, sharp-nosed insectivorous marsupials of Australia and nearby regions that superficially resemble mice or rats
