departed

adjective
having died; no longer living
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑrtɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtɪd / (BrE)
having died; no longer living, especially referring to someone who is remembered or mourned.

Examples

Departed grandfather
She still speaks fondly of her departed grandfather.
The town gathered to honor the departed at the annual remembrance ceremony.
We held a memorial service for our departed colleague.
