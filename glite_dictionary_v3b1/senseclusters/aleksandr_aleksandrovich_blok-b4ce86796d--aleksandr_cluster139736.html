Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Blok

proper noun
Russian poet (1880–1921)
Pronunciation: / əˈlɛksɑːndər ˌɑːlɛkˈsandrəvɪtʃ ˈblɒk / (AmE), / əˈlɛksəndə ˌælɪkˈsandrəvɪtʃ ˈblɒk / (BrE)
(1880–1921) Russian poet, one of the most significant figures of Russian symbolism, known for works such as 'The Twelve' and 'Verses About the Beautiful Lady'.
