monocyclic

adjective
single-ringed
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˈsaɪklɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈsɪklɪk / (BrE)
having or involving a single ring, especially in the structure of a chemical compound

Examples

Monocyclic compound
Benzene is a classic example of a monocyclic compound.
The scientist explained that the molecule was monocyclic rather than polycyclic.
Many simple aromatic compounds are monocyclic in structure.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cyclic - having a ring or rings in the structure
  • antonym: polycyclic - having more than one ring in the structure
