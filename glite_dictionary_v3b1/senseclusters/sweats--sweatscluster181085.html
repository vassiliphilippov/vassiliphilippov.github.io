sweats

noun
very competitive gamers (slang)
8.9
Pronunciation: / swɛts / (AmE), / swɛts / (BrE)
informal, slang — (in online gaming) players who play extremely competitively or try very hard to win, often appearing intense or overly focused on performance.

Examples

Online sweats dominate
The ranked lobby was full of sweats who refused to back down.
She refused to team with sweats who complained about every small mistake during the match.
At the weekend tournament, the sweats always practiced for hours to perfect their plays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitive player - a player who focuses on winning and performs at a high level
  • synonym: try-hard - a player who makes an excessive effort to win, often used negatively
