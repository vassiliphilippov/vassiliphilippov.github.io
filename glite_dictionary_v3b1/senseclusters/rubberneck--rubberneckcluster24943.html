rubberneck

noun
person who gawks inquisitively
7.0 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbərˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrʌbənɛk / (BrE)
a person who stares or gawks with great curiosity, especially at accidents or unusual events

Examples

A rubberneck at accidents
A group of rubbernecks gathered at the scene of the accident, blocking traffic.
The police had to shoo away the rubbernecks so emergency workers could do their job.
Tourists are often seen as rubbernecks when they stare at New York's skyscrapers.
As a typical rubberneck, he snapped photos at every stop on the tour.
Local drivers complained that rubbernecks slowed down traffic near the accident.
The bus was filled with rubbernecks eager to see every famous landmark in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person present at an event but not involved
  • synonym: onlooker - a person who watches something without taking part
  • synonym: sightseer - a person who visits interesting places, especially as a tourist
