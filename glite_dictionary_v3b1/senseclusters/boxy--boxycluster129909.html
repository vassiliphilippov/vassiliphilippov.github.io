boxy

adjective
having a rectangular, box-like shape
3.2 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆbÉ‘ksi / (AmE), / ËˆbÉ’ksi / (BrE)
(of an object) having a shape that is similar to a box, especially being rectangular and having straight sides.

Examples

A boxy jacket
I don't like wearing boxy jackets because they make my shoulders look wide.
That boxy building dominates the skyline with its sharp angles.
The new car has a boxy design that reminds me of older models.

Synonyms

  • box-shaped
    3.0 0.06/m
    box-shaped means having an exact box form with straight sides, while boxy feels more general and blunt in shape.
  • boxlike
    3.7 0.11/m
    boxlike looks like a box in its general outline, while boxy suggests a squarer, blockier shape with blunt edges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curvy - having smooth, rounded curves
  • synonym: rectangular - having a shape like a rectangle
