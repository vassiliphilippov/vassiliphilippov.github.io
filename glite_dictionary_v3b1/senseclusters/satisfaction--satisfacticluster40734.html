satisfaction

noun
compensation for a wrong
Pronunciation: / ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən / (AmE), / ˌsætɪsˈfakʃən / (BrE)
compensation or redress for a wrong or grievance, especially after a complaint or injury

Examples

Customer satisfaction after complaint
After several complaints, the customer finally received satisfaction from the company.
Many customers left negative reviews when they failed to obtain satisfaction for their faulty products.
The plaintiff sought satisfaction for the damages caused by the breach of contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compensation - payment or reparation given for loss or injury
  • synonym: redress - remedy or compensation for a wrong or grievance
