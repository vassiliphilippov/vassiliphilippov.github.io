bookstand

noun
support for books
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkstænd / (AmE), / ˈbʊkstænd / (BrE)
a support or frame designed to hold a book upright and open for reading or display

Examples

A bookstand for reading
She placed her cookbook on the bookstand while preparing dinner.
The library provided a bookstand for people who wanted to read large reference books.
He bought a wooden bookstand to display his favorite novel on his desk.
