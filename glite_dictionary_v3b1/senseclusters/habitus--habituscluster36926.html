habitus

noun
physical constitution, esp. re: disease susceptibility
Pronunciation: / ˈhæbɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈhæbɪtəs /; / ˈhabɪtəs / (BrE)
the physical build or constitution of a person, especially as considered in medicine or biology
