stoichiometric

adjective
relating to chemical proportions (stoichiometry)
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌstoʊ.ɪ.ki.əˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌstɔɪ.kɪ.əˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the precise proportions of elements or compounds as determined by stoichiometry, especially in chemical reactions

Examples

A stoichiometric mixture
In a stoichiometric mixture, fuel and oxygen are present in exact proportions for full combustion.
Many industrial processes require stoichiometric control to maximize efficiency and minimize waste.
The reaction was performed with a stoichiometric amount of reagent to ensure complete conversion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stoichiometry - the calculation of reactants and products in chemical reactions
