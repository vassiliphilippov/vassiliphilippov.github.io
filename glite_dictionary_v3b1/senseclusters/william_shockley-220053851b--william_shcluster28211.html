William Shockley

proper noun
physicist; transistor co-inventor
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪl.jəm ˈʃɑk.li / (AmE), / ˈwɪl.jəm ˈʃɒk.li / (BrE)
(1910–1989) United States physicist, born in England, who contributed to the development of the electronic transistor.

Examples
