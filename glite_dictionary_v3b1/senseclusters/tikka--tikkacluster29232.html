tikka

noun
spiced grilled piece
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkə / (AmE), / ˈtɪkə / (BrE)
a piece of meat or vegetable marinated in spices and usually cooked on a skewer, common in South Asian cuisine

Examples

Chicken tikka dish
She ordered chicken tikka with naan for dinner.
Vegetable tikka is a popular choice for vegetarians at Indian restaurants.
The chef marinated the lamb before grilling it as tikka on skewers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curry - a dish of meat or vegetables cooked in a spiced sauce
  • synonym: kebab - pieces of meat or vegetables cooked on a skewer
