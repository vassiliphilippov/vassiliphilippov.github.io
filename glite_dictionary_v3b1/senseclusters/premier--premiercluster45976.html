premier

noun
head of government; prime minister
3.8
Pronunciation: / prəˈmɪr / (AmE), / ˈprɛmiə /; / ˈprɛm.i.ə / (BrE)
the head of government in some countries; a prime minister or chief minister

Examples

French premier
After the scandal, the premier faced mounting pressure to resign.
In many countries, the premier represents the government at international summits.
The new premier promised sweeping reforms during his first address to Parliament.
After the election, the country welcomed its first female premier.
The premier met with international leaders to discuss climate change.
The premier of Ontario announced a new education plan yesterday.
