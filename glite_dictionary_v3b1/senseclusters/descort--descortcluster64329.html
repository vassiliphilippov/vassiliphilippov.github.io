descort

noun
medieval discordant poem
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɛsˈkɔrt / (AmE), / dɛsˈkɔːt / (BrE)
a medieval lyric poem characterized by discordant structure or theme, often expressing conflict or disunity

Examples

A medieval descort poem

Unlike the harmonious songs, the descort stood out for its irregular structure.
Scholars study the descort to understand expressions of conflict in medieval poetry.
