unbecoming

adjective
not proper or appropriate; unsuitable
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of behavior, speech, or appearance) not appropriate or proper in accordance with accepted standards; unsuitable or inappropriate.

Examples

Unbecoming behavior
Her rude remarks were completely unbecoming of a guest at the dinner table.
It is unbecoming for a judge to show bias in court.
Such casual attire would be unbecoming at a formal event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: becoming - suitable or appropriate for a particular situation; proper
  • synonym: unseemly - not proper or appropriate for the situation; improper
