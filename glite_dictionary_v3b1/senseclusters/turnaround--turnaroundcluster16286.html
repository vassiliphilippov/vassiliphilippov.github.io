turnaround

noun
act of turning in the opposite direction
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnəˌraʊnd /; / ˈtɜrnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnəraʊnd / (BrE)
an act or place for turning or moving in the opposite direction, especially for vehicles

Examples

Highway turnaround
A designated turnaround allowed vehicles to reverse direction safely on the highway.
The bus made a swift turnaround and headed back toward the city.
We had to perform a turnaround because we missed the exit.
A gravel turnaround was provided at the end of the driveway for delivery trucks.
Please do not block the turnaround, as it is needed for emergency vehicles.
The school bus driver used the turnaround near the entrance to change direction.
