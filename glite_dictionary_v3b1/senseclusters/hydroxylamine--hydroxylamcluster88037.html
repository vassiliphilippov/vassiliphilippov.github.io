hydroxylamine

noun
chemical reducing agent
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdrɑksɪlˈæmin / (AmE), / haɪˌdrɒksɪlˈæmiːn / (BrE)
a chemical compound (NH2OH) used as a reducing agent in laboratories and industry

Examples

Hydroxylamine chemical compound
The laboratory ordered more hydroxylamine for use in their chemical experiments.
Hydroxylamine is often handled with care because it can be unstable under certain conditions.
In industry, hydroxylamine is used to produce certain pharmaceuticals and plastics.
