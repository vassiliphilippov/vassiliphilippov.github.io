telephone bill

noun
statement of charges for phone service
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌfoʊn bɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌfəʊn bɪl / (BrE)
a statement showing the amount of money owed for telephone service during a particular period

Examples
