genus Rosellinia

noun
a genus of fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊsɛˌlɪniə / (AmE), / ˌrɒsɛˈlɪnɪə / (BrE)
A genus of fungi characterized by smooth perithecia and dark, one-celled ascospores.
