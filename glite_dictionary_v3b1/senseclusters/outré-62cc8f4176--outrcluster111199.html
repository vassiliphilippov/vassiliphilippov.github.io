outré

adjective
strikingly unconventional; bizarre
Pronunciation: / uːˈtreɪ / (AmE), / uːˈtreɪ / (BrE)
Unconventional and strikingly unusual; bizarre or eccentric.

Examples

An outré style
Critics described the novel's plot as both outré and imaginative.
Her outré sense of fashion drew attention wherever she went.
The artist became famous for his outré installations in urban spaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - unusual in an appealing or interesting way
  • synonym: outlandish - very strange or unusual
