reed

verb
to thatch with reeds
Pronunciation: / riːd / (AmE), / riːd / (BrE)
to cover or thatch a roof or building with reeds.

Examples

To reed a roof
The villagers reeded the cottage roof to prepare for the rainy season.
Before winter arrived, they carefully reeded the barn to keep it dry.
Local craftsmen reeded several houses in the traditional style last year.
