dairywoman

noun
woman working in a dairy
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdeəriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who works in or manages a dairy, especially one who makes or sells milk products.

Examples

A dairywoman makes butter
As a skilled dairywoman, she made butter and cheese by hand.
The dairywoman delivered fresh milk to our door every morning.
The farm hired a new dairywoman to oversee their milk production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dairyman - a man who works in a dairy
