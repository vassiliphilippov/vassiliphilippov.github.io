paintbox

noun
box for holding paints
2.8 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪnt.bɑːks/ /; / /ˈpeɪnt.bɔːks/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪnt.bɒks/ (BrE)
a container, often with compartments, used to hold a set of paints for artists or children

Examples

An artist's paintbox
He opened the old paintbox and found tubes of paint in every shade imaginable.
She took her paintbox and brushes out to the garden to capture the scenery.
The children's paintbox was filled with bright colors, ready for their next art project.
