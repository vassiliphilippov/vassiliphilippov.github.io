finished

adjective
having a surface coating or treatment applied
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
(of an object, material, or product) having reached its final state, often with a surface treatment or ready for use or sale

Examples
