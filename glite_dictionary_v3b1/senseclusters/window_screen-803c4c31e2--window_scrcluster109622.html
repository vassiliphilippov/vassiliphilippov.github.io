window screen

noun
mesh covering used to keep insects out of windows
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ skriːn / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ skriːn / (BrE)
A mesh screen fitted over a window opening to prevent insects from entering while allowing air to pass through.

Examples

A window screen mesh
After cleaning the window screen, the view from the kitchen was much clearer.
She installed a window screen to let fresh air in without letting bugs enter.
The window screen was torn, so mosquitoes kept getting into the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fly screen - fine mesh used to keep insects out
  • synonym: mosquito net - netting used to prevent mosquito bites
