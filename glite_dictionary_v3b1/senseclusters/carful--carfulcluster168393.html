carful

noun
amount filling a car
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrfʊl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːfʊl / (BrE)
an amount or quantity that fills a car

Examples

A carful of goods
We packed a carful of camping gear for our weekend trip.
After the party, she drove home with a carful of friends.
He donated a carful of old clothes to the charity.
