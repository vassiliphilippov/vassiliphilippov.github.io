klatch

noun
informal gathering
9.5
Pronunciation: / klætʃ / (AmE), / klætʃ / (BrE)
an informal social gathering, especially for conversation and companionship, often with coffee

She looked forward to the weekly klatch with her friends at the park.
The book club's klatch lasted well into the evening as everyone shared stories.
