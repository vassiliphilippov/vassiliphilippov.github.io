shoplifting

noun
stealing from a shop
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌlɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌlɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of stealing goods from a shop or store, especially by concealing them while pretending to be a customer

Examples

Shoplifting goods
Losses due to shoplifting cost retailers millions each year.
She was caught shoplifting and had to appear in court.
The store installed cameras to reduce incidents of shoplifting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: burglary - illegal entry into a building, especially to commit a crime
  • synonym: theft - the act of stealing; taking someone's property without permission
