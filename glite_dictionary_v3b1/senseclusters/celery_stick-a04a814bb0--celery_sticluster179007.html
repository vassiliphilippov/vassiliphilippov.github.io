celery stick

noun
long thin piece of celery stalk
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.ə.ɹi stɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.əri stɪk / (BrE)
a piece of celery stalk cut into a long, thin stick, typically served raw as a snack or in salads

Examples

A celery stick snack
Children often enjoy eating celery sticks with peanut butter.
He dipped a celery stick into the hummus for a healthy snack.
The salad was garnished with fresh celery sticks and cherry tomatoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celery - a cultivated plant with crisp, pale green stalks eaten as a vegetable
