cooked to perfection

adjective
cooked exactly right
Pronunciation: / kʊkt tə pərˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / kʊkt tə pəˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
(of food) having been cooked exactly right, to the highest standard possible.

Examples

The steak was cooked to perfection, tender and juicy with every bite.
This salmon is cooked to perfection, with a delicate, flaky texture.
