order Mallophaga

noun
former insect order; biting lice
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.dər mæl.ə.ˈfeɪ.gə/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.də mæl.ə.ˈfeɪ.gə/ (BrE)
A former taxonomic order of insects comprising the biting lice, which are ectoparasites mainly of birds and some mammals.
