rocket scientist

noun
very clever or intelligent person (often ironic use)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪt ˌsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a person who is very intelligent or clever, especially in solving complex problems; often used ironically to suggest that something does not require much intelligence

Examples

Not a rocket scientist
It's not exactly rocket scientist stuff—anyone could do it with a bit of effort.
She may not be a rocket scientist, but she knows how to solve tough problems.
You don't have to be a rocket scientist to understand how this software works.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
  • antonym: idiot - a person of very low intelligence; used as an antonym in ironic phrases
