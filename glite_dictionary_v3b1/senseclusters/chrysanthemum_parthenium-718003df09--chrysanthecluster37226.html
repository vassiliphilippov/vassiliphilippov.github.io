Chrysanthemum parthenium

noun
herbaceous plant; also called feverfew
9.1
Pronunciation: / krɪˌsænθəˌmjum pɑːrˈθiːniəm / (AmE), / krɪˌsænθəˌmiːəm pɑːˈθiːniəm / (BrE)
