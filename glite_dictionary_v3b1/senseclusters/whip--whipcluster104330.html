whip

noun
light, sweet dessert of beaten eggs or cream
4.3
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
A dessert made of sugar and stiffly beaten egg whites or cream, usually flavored with fruit.

Examples

A fruit whip
After dinner, we enjoyed a refreshing fruit whip on the patio.
He prepared a lemon whip for the party.
The strawberry whip was the highlight of the dessert menu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mousse - a light, airy dessert made with whipped cream or egg whites
