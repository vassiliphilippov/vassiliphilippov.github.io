overdramatic

adjective
excessively emotional
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərdrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvədrəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
behaving in an exaggerated, overly emotional, or theatrical way

Examples

An overdramatic reaction
She tends to be overdramatic about even the smallest problems.
His overdramatic reaction made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
Don't be so overdramatic; it's just a minor delay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melodramatic - showing exaggerated emotions or drama
  • antonym: understated - presented in a subtle or restrained way
