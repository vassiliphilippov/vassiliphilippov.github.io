Lee's Birthday

noun
US holiday marking Robert E. Lee's birth
Pronunciation: / liz ˈbɜːrθ.deɪ / (AmE), / liːz ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
A holiday observed chiefly in the southern United States on January 19 to commemorate the birth of Confederate General Robert E. Lee.

Examples

Lee's Birthday holiday
Debate continues over the celebration of Lee's Birthday in some communities.
Several southern states used to observe Lee's Birthday as a public holiday in January.
The school was closed in honor of Lee's Birthday this year.
