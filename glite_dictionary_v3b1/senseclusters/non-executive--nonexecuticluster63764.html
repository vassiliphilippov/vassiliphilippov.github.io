non-executive

adjective
without management duties
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
not having management responsibilities or not involved in the daily operations of an organization

Examples

Non-executive director
She was appointed as a non-executive director to provide independent advice to the board.
Many companies include non-executive members to ensure balanced decision-making.
His non-executive role means he does not manage the company's daily activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: executive - having management or decision-making responsibilities
