suntan

verb
become tanned by sun exposure
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌtæn / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.tæn /; / ˈsʌnˌtæn / (BrE)
to make your skin brown or darker by being in the sun

Examples

To suntan at the beach
After a week at the beach, she managed to suntan evenly all over.
He prefers to suntan in the backyard rather than use artificial tanning beds.
Some people suntan quickly, while others burn easily.
They spent the afternoon suntanning on the hotel terrace to prepare for the wedding.
Many people enjoy suntanning at the beach during summer vacations.
She was careful to use sunscreen while suntanning to avoid getting sunburned.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bleach - to make something lighter in color
  • synonym: sunbathe - to sit or lie in the sun to get a tan
  • synonym: tan - to become brown or darkened by the sun
