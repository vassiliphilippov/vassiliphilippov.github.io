thunderclap

noun
a loud crash of thunder
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚˌklæp / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəˌklæp / (BrE)
a single sudden and loud crash of thunder

Examples

A loud thunderclap
A loud thunderclap echoed across the valley just as rain began to fall.
She woke with a jolt to the sound of a thunderclap outside her window.
The sudden thunderclap startled everyone at the picnic.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: rumble - a continuous deep sound, often of thunder
  • synonym: thunderbolt - a flash of lightning with a simultaneous crash of thunder
