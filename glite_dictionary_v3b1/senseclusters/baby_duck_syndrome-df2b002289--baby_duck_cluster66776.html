baby duck syndrome

noun
attachment to initial method or situation
8.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbi dʌk sɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbi dʌk sɪnˌdrəʊm / (BrE)
a tendency to become attached to the first system, method, or situation encountered, making it difficult to adapt to something new
