customer

noun
person who buys goods or services
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌs.tə.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌs.tə.mə / (BrE)
A person or organization that buys goods or services from a business.

Examples

A customer buys goods
A satisfied customer is more likely to return and recommend the service.
Every customer is required to show their receipt before leaving the store.
The restaurant always tries to make every customer feel welcome.
