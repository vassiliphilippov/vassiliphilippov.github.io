homebrew

noun
alcoholic drink made at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbru / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbruː / (BrE)
An alcoholic beverage, especially beer, that is brewed at home rather than in a commercial brewery.

Examples

A glass of homebrew
At the festival, you could sample different types of homebrew from local enthusiasts.
He takes pride in the homebrew he crafts in his basement.
She gave me a bottle of her latest homebrew as a holiday gift.
