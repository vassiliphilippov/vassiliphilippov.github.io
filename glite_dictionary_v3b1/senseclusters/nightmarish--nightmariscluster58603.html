nightmarish

adjective
extremely frightening or unpleasant
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌmɛrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌmeərɪʃ / (BrE)
extremely frightening or unpleasant, like a nightmare; terrifying or distressing in nature

Examples

The city was plunged into a nightmarish chaos after the storm hit without warning.
The movie's nightmarish scenes left the audience too frightened to sleep.
