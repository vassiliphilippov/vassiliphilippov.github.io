Synonyms

  • outdated
    5.0 4/m
    outdated is commonly used for things that are no longer useful or fashionable, while obsolete suggests they are completely out of use or production.
  • outmoded
    6.5 0.5/m
    outmoded refers to ideas or styles that have gone out of fashion, while obsolete suggests total loss of usefulness or relevance.
  • antiquated
    8.2 1/m
    antiquated feels very old and from a past era, while obsolete means it is no longer used or produced today.
  • outworn
    7.1 0.01/m

  • dated
    6.2 0.2/m

  • archaic
    9.9 2/m
    archaic refers to language or things from a much earlier time, while obsolete means they are no longer in everyday use.
  • disused
    4.5 0.07/m

  • defunct
    7.0 1/m
