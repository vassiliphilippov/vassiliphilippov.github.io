boatlift

noun
device for lifting boats
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊt.lɪft/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊt.lɪft/ (BrE)
a structure or mechanical device used to raise or lower boats out of the water or between different water levels

Examples

Marina boatlift
The marina installed a new boatlift to protect boats from water damage during the winter.
Using the boatlift, workers easily moved the yacht out of the water for maintenance.
A boatlift can help prevent algae growth on the hull by keeping the boat dry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry dock - a structure used to keep boats or ships out of water for repairs
  • synonym: lock - a device for raising and lowering boats between stretches of water of different levels
