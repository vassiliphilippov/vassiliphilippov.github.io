chestnut blight

noun
fungal disease of chestnut trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛs.nət blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt blaɪt / (BrE)
a fungal disease that affects American chestnut trees, causing cankers and often killing the tree

Examples

Chestnut blight disease
Many mature American chestnut trees have disappeared due to chestnut blight.
Researchers are working to develop chestnut blight-resistant trees to restore the population.
The spread of chestnut blight devastated forests in the eastern United States during the 20th century.
