freeze out

verb
exclude someone from a group or activity
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfriz aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfriːz aʊt / (BrE)
to deliberately exclude someone from a group or prevent them from participating, especially by being unfriendly or by using unfair methods

Examples

To freeze out new members
After missing several meetings, she realized they were trying to freeze out new members from the club.
Some students felt they were being frozen out of group projects by their classmates.
The management attempted to freeze out union representatives during the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: include - to make someone part of a group or activity
  • synonym: ostracize - to exclude from a group or society
