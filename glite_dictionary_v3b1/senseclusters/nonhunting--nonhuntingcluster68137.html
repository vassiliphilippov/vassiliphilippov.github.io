nonhunting

adjective
not involving hunting
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
not related to or involving the activity of hunting

Examples

A nonhunting zone
The reserve is a nonhunting zone to ensure the safety of endangered species.
During certain months, the forest is declared nonhunting to allow animal populations to recover.
They visited a nonhunting area where wildlife could roam freely without disturbance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hunting - related to the activity of pursuing and killing animals
