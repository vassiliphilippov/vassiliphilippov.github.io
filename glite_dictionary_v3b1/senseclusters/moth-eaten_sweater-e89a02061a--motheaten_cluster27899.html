moth-eaten sweater

noun
sweater damaged by moths
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːθˌitən ˈswɛtɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɒθˌiːtn ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
A sweater that is worn out or has holes caused by moths.

Examples

An old moth-eaten sweater
He wore a moth-eaten sweater to the gathering.
She found her grandfather's old moth-eaten sweater at the back of the closet.
The moth-eaten sweater had more holes than fabric after years in storage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moth-eaten - damaged or worn, especially by moths; shabby or tattered
