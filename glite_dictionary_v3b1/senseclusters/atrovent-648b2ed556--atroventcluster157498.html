Atrovent

proper noun
inhaled bronchodilator medication (brand name)
Pronunciation: / ˈætɹoʊˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈætrəʊˌvɛnt / (BrE)
a brand name for an inhaled bronchodilator medication used to relieve bronchospasm, particularly in conditions like asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
