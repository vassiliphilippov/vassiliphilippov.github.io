moderate gale

noun
wind of 32–38 knots (Beaufort force 7)
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdɚət ɡeɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɒdərət ɡeɪl / (BrE)
A wind with a speed of 32–38 knots (about 37–44 mph or 59–70 km/h), rated as force 7 on the Beaufort scale.

Examples

A moderate gale wind
During the crossing, our ship endured a moderate gale for several hours.
Sailing in a moderate gale requires considerable skill and caution.
The weather report warned that a moderate gale was expected to hit the coast overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gale - a very strong wind, especially between 34 and 40 knots
