fencesitter

noun
neutral or uncommitted person
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfen.sɪt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈfen.sɪt.ə/ (BrE)
a person who chooses not to support either side in a dispute or controversy, especially in politics

Examples

A fencesitter in politics
As a committed fencesitter, he refused to endorse either political candidate.
Corporate leaders are often seen as fencesitters during times of industrial action.
It's hard to know what the fencesitters will decide until the last minute.
