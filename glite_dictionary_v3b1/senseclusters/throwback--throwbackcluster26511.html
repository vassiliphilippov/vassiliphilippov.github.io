throwback

noun
an organism showing ancestral traits
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊˌbæk / (BrE)
a person or thing that shows features or characteristics of an earlier time or ancestor

Examples

A throwback ancestor
Genetic mutations can sometimes result in a throwback with characteristics absent for many generations.
Some consider the platypus a living throwback because of its primitive reptilian traits.
The bird's unusual features made researchers suspect it was a throwback to an earlier evolutionary stage.
Her fashion sense is a true throwback to the 1970s.
That band's music is a throwback to the classic rock era.
The rare animal is considered a genetic throwback to its ancient ancestors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atavism - the reappearance of ancestral characteristics
  • synonym: reversion - a return to a previous condition, state, belief, or practice
