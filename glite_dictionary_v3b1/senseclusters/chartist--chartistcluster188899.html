chartist

noun
data-visualization professional
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who designs and creates charts, graphs, and interactive visualizations for reports, dashboards, or presentations.

Examples

Report chartist
The chartist created an interactive dashboard to show monthly sales trends to the management team.
Our company hired two chartists to redesign performance reports and improve clarity for stakeholders.
As a freelance chartist, she specializes in turning complex datasets into clear, engaging visual stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data visualizer - a professional who creates visual representations of data to make information easier to understand
  • synonym: information designer - someone who organises and presents information so it is clear and useful
