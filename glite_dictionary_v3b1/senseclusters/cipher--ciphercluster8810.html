cipher

noun
person of no influence or importance
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪfə / (BrE)
a person who has no power or importance in a situation; a person of no influence

Examples

A political cipher
After the takeover, Sam became a cipher in the company, with no real power.
Although he was present at all the meetings, he remained a political cipher.
She was treated like a cipher, barely acknowledged by the rest of the group.
