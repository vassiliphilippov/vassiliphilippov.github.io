slotted

adjective
with narrow openings
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈslɒtɪd / (BrE)
having one or more long, narrow openings or holes cut into a surface or object

Examples

A slotted screwdriver
The mechanic used a slotted screwdriver to remove the old screws.
The new design features slotted vents to improve airflow inside the device.
She organized her mail in a slotted wooden rack by the door.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solid - without any openings or holes
