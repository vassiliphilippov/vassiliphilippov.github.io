bey

noun
title of respect in Turkey or Egypt
7.9 8.9
Pronunciation: /beɪ/ (AmE), /beɪ/ (BrE)
a historical title of respect or office for a man in Turkey, Egypt, or the Ottoman Empire, often used for officials or dignitaries
