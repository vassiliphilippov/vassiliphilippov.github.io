germination

noun
beginning of seed or spore growth
4.0 0.81/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd í…ùÀê.m…ôÀàne…™. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåd í…úÀê.m…™Ààne…™. É…ôn / (BrE)
