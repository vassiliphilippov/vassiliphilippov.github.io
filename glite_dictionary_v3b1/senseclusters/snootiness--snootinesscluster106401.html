noun

Examples

Snootiness and arrogance
Many people disliked the club for its reputation of snootiness and exclusion.
She tried to hide her snootiness during the meeting, but it was obvious to everyone.
The snootiness of the staff at the restaurant made us feel unwelcome.

Synonyms

    snobbery focuses on class and wealth to show superiority, while snootiness shows a general air of being better.
  • snobbishness
    7.4 0.08/m
    snobbishness treats people as lower because of their status, while snootiness is a broader attitude of arrogance or pretension.
  • snobbism
    8.4 0.05/m
    snobbism shows a belief in social rank and acting superior, while snootiness covers general arrogance or pretentious behavior.
  • snottiness
    8.1 0.06/m
    snottiness is rude and openly disdainful to others, while snootiness is more about acting superior or pretentious.
  • superciliousness
    9.8 0.03/m
    superciliousness is a calm, proud look or manner toward others, while snootiness often shows simple snobbery or pretension.
  • pretentiousness
    7.7 0.26/m
    pretentiousness tries to impress others by seeming more cultured or important, while snootiness often simply signals feeling superior.
  • condescension
    7.9 1.39/m
    condescension speaks or acts in a patronizing way to others, while snootiness is a general show of superiority or pretension.
  • arrogance
    4.8 8.47/m
    arrogance means feeling greatly superior about one's abilities, while snootiness focuses more on social show and pretentious manner.
  • uppishness
    9.2 0.00/m

  • elitist attitude
    7.5 0.10/m

  • self-importance
