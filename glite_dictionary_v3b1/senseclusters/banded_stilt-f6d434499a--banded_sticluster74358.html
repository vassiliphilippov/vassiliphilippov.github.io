banded stilt

noun
Australian wading bird with chest band
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbændɪd stɪlt / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd stɪlt / (BrE)
a species of web-footed Australian wading bird with reddish-brown markings across the chest

Examples

The banded stilt bird

The distinctive reddish chest band makes the banded stilt easy to identify among other waders.
