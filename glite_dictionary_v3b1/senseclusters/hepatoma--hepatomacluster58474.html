hepatoma

noun
malignant liver tumor
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛpəˈtoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌhɛpəˈtəʊmə / (BrE)
a malignant tumor of the liver, especially one originating in the liver cells

Examples

A hepatoma tumor
Researchers are developing new therapies to treat hepatoma more effectively.
Surgical removal is sometimes possible if the hepatoma is detected early.
The patient was diagnosed with a hepatoma after several weeks of testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carcinoma - a cancer arising in epithelial tissue
  • synonym: liver cancer - general term for cancer of the liver
