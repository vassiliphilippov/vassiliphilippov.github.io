dumpsite

noun
site for dumping waste materials
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmp.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʌmp.saɪt / (BrE)
a piece of land where waste materials are dumped, often in an uncontrolled or informal manner

Examples

The dumpsite area
Illegal dumping at the dumpsite has caused several health issues for local residents.
The city council decided to close the old dumpsite due to environmental concerns.
The villagers organized a cleanup to remove plastics from the abandoned dumpsite.
