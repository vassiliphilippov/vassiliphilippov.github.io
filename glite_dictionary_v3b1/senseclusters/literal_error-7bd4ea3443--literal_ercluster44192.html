literal error

noun
misprint or typographical mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərəl ˈɛrər / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəl ˈɛrər / (BrE)
A mistake in printed material caused by mechanical or typographical faults, such as a misprint or typo.

Examples
