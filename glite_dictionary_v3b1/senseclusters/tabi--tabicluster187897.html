tabi

noun
Japanese split-toe sock
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑː.bi /; / ˈtɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈtæb.i /; / ˈtæbi / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese sock with a separation between the big toe and the other toes, typically worn with thong sandals
