saucier

noun
sauce-making pan
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊsieɪ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊsieɪ / (BrE)
a wide, rounded cooking pan with sloping sides, designed for making sauces and reductions

Examples

A saucier pan
The chef used a saucier to prepare a delicate cream sauce for the pasta.
A saucier is ideal for reducing liquids evenly without burning.
She bought a new saucier to improve her homemade gravies and soups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saucepan - a deep pan with straight sides used for cooking sauces and other foods
  • antonym: skillet - a frying pan with low sides, used for frying or sautéing
