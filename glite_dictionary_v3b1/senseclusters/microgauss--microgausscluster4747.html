microgauss

noun
unit of magnetic flux density
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈɡaʊs / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈɡaʊs / (BrE)
a unit of magnetic flux density equal to one millionth of a gauss, used especially in scientific contexts

Examples

One microgauss unit
Sensitive instruments can detect magnetic fields as low as one microgauss.
The interstellar medium has a magnetic field strength of only a few microgauss.
The laboratory reported the background magnetic noise in microgausses.
