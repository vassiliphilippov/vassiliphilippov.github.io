pulse

noun
edible seed of pea, bean, or lentil family
8.9
Pronunciation: / pʌls / (AmE), / pʌls / (BrE)
an edible seed of a leguminous plant, such as a pea, bean, or lentil, commonly used as food

Examples

A pulse crop

The chef added a mix of pulse to make the soup more nutritious.
