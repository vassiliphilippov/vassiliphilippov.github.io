bobwhite

noun
North American quail species
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbˌwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌwaɪt / (BrE)
A small North American quail with a distinctive call, often hunted as a game bird.

Examples

A bobwhite quail

The farmer heard the familiar call of the bobwhite as dusk fell.
