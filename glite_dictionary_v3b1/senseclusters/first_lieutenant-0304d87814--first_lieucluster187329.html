first lieutenant

noun
military officer rank above 2nd lieutenant
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrst ˌluːˈtɛnənt / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst lɛfˈtɛnənt / (BrE)
A commissioned officer in the Army, Air Force, or Marines, ranking above a second lieutenant and below a captain.

Examples

A first lieutenant rank
As a first lieutenant, he was responsible for overseeing several junior officers.
She was promoted to first lieutenant after only two years of service.
The first lieutenant led his platoon through the training exercise with confidence.
