gemology

noun
study of gemstones
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈmɑlədʒi / (AmE), / dʒɛˈmɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of gemstones and precious stones, including their identification and classification

Examples

Gemology study
She decided to study gemology to learn how to identify valuable stones.
A background in gemology is essential for anyone working in the jewelry industry.
The museum's new exhibit was curated by an expert in gemology.
