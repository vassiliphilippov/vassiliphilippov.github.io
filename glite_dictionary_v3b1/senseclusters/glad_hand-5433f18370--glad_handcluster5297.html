glad hand

noun
an enthusiastic (sometimes insincere) welcome or greeting
Pronunciation: / ɡlæd hænd / (AmE), / ɡlæd hænd / (BrE)
A warm or enthusiastic welcome or greeting, sometimes given insincerely for effect or advantage.
