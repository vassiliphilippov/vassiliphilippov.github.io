admire

verb
regard with respect or approval
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈmaɪɚ /; / ədˈmaɪr / (AmE), / ədˈmaɪə / (BrE)
to feel or show respect, approval, or pleasure towards someone or something, often by looking at them

Examples

To admire a person
As a child, he admired his older brother and wanted to be just like him.
I really admire her dedication to her work.
Many people admire the architecture of the ancient temple.
He paused to admire the beautiful sunset over the mountains.
Many people admire her dedication to helping others.
The tourists stopped to admire the architecture of the old cathedral.

Related Terms

  • antonym: despise - to feel contempt or a strong dislike for someone or something
  • synonym: respect - to show regard or esteem for someone or something
