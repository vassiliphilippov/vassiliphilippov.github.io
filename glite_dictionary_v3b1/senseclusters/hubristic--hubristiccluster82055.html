hubristic

adjective
excessively proud
8.4
Pronunciation: / hjuːˈbrɪstɪk / (AmE), / hjuːˈbrɪstɪk / (BrE)
showing excessive pride, arrogance, or self-confidence

Examples

Hubristic attitude
The CEO's hubristic attitude led to risky decisions that harmed the company.
Critics described the artist's claims as hubristic and out of touch with reality.
Her hubristic refusal to accept advice eventually caused her project to fail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance
  • antonym: modest - having a humble opinion of oneself
