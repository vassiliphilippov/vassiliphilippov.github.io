machine

noun
mechanical or electrical device
1.0 116/m
Pronunciation: / m…ôÀà Éin / (AmE), / m…ôÀà ÉiÀên / (BrE)
a device or piece of equipment with moving parts that uses power to do a particular job

Examples

A machine runs
Automated machines have replaced many manual tasks in factories.
She designed a simple machine to help lift heavy objects.
The washing machine in the laundry room broke down last night.
By turning the handle, you operate the machine and move the platform upward.
Early inventors built simple machines like levers and pulleys to make work easier.
The machine was designed to lift heavy loads with minimal effort.

Synonyms

  • mechanical device
    3.2 0.35/m
    a mechanical device is an object that works by moving parts to perform a task, while a machine uses power to do a job.
  • mechanism
    4.4 6.55/m
    a mechanism is a set of moving parts that make something work, while a machine is the whole powered device that does a particular job.
