Mediterranean flour moth

noun
a moth pest of stored grain and flour
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən flɔr mɑθ / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən flɔː mɒθ / (BrE)
a small species of moth whose larvae damage stored grain and flour products

Examples

Mediterranean flour moth larvae
Infestations of Mediterranean flour moth larvae can ruin large quantities of stored grain products.
