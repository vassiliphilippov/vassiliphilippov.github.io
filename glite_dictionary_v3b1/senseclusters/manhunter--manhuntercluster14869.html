manhunter

noun
criminal pursuer
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person who searches for and tries to capture criminals or fugitives

Examples

A manhunter tracks criminals
The skilled manhunter tracked the suspect across several states.
As a famous manhunter, she was often called to solve difficult cases.
The movie follows a determined manhunter on the trail of a dangerous fugitive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounty hunter - a person who pursues fugitives for a reward
  • synonym: detective - a person whose job is to investigate crimes
  • antonym: fugitive - a person who is running away from the law
