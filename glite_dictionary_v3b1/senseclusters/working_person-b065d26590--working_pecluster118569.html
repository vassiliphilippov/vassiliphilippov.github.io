working person

noun
manual or industrial employee
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkɪŋ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is employed to perform manual or industrial work, often distinguished from managerial or professional employees

Examples

A working person job
As a working person, she values fair wages and good working conditions.
He grew up in a family where every adult was a working person with a blue-collar job.
The new policies are designed to help every working person in the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manager - someone who manages others or is in a supervisory position
  • synonym: worker - someone who works, especially in manual or industrial jobs
