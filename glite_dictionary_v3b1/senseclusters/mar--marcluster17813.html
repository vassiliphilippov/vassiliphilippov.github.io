mar

noun
blemish or flaw on something
8.6
Pronunciation: / mɑːr / (AmE), / mɑː / (BrE)
a mark, blemish, or flaw that spoils the appearance of something, especially on a person's body

Examples

A mar on skin
A single mar on her otherwise flawless complexion drew his attention.
Despite the mar on the painting, it was still considered a masterpiece.
The antique vase had a small mar on its surface that reduced its value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blemish - a small mark or flaw that spoils the appearance of something
  • synonym: scar - a mark left on the skin after an injury has healed
