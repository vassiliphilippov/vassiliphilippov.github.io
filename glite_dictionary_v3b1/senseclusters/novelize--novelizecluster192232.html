novelize

verb
adapt into a novel
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːvɪˌlaɪz /; / ˈnɑːvəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnɒvɪˌlaɪz /; / ˈnɒvəlaɪz / (BrE)
