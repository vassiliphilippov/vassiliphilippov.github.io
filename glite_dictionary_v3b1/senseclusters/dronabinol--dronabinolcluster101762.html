dronabinol

noun
medical marijuana compound for nausea
Pronunciation: / droʊˈnæbɪnɒl / (AmE), / drəʊˈnabɪnɒl / (BrE)
a psychoactive compound found in marijuana, used medically to treat nausea and vomiting associated with cancer therapy
