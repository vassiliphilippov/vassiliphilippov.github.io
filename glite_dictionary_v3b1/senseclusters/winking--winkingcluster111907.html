winking

noun
rapid closing and opening of one eye
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the rapid closing and opening of one eye, usually as a reflex or signal

Examples

A secret winking
He tried to hide his nervousness with repeated winking.
Her constant winking made it clear she was trying to send a secret message.
The doctor observed the patient's winking to check for nerve reflexes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blink - to shut and open the eyes quickly
