Edward I

proper noun
King of England, 1272–1307
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdwərd ðə ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˈɛdwəd ðə ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
(1239–1307) King of England from 1272 to 1307, noted for his conquest of Wales and legal reforms.

Examples

Edward I king of England
Edward I implemented numerous legal reforms that shaped English common law.
Many castles built in Wales are attributed to Edward I's campaign in the region.
The conquest of Wales was a major achievement for Edward I during his reign.
