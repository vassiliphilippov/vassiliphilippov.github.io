dedicator

noun
person who dedicates
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who performs an official act of dedication or consecration, especially in a religious ceremony

Examples

Dedicator at a ceremony
The dedicator led the ceremony to bless the new hospital wing.
As the dedicator, she spoke movingly about the building's purpose.
A respected elder was chosen as the dedicator for the community center opening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consecrator - a person who makes something sacred through a religious ceremony
  • synonym: officiant - a person who leads a religious or formal ceremony
