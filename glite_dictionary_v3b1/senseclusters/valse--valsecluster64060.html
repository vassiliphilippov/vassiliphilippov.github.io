valse

noun
ballroom dance in triple time
Pronunciation: / væls / (AmE), / vals / (BrE)
a ballroom dance in triple time, similar to the waltz, with a strong accent on the first beat

Examples

An elegant valse
At the competition, they impressed the judges with their elegant valse.
Her favorite piece to play on the piano is a valse by Chopin.
The orchestra played a beautiful valse as the couple began to dance.
