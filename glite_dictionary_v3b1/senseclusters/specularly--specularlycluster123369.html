specularly

adverb
by mirror reflection
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛkjələrli / (AmE), / ˈspɛkjʊləli / (BrE)
in a manner involving reflection from a smooth surface, like a mirror

Examples

Light specularly reflects
The laser beam bounced specularly off the polished metal surface.
When sunlight hits calm water, it is reflected specularly and creates a clear image.
Unlike rough surfaces, a mirror reflects light specularly in a single direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diffusely - in a way that scatters light in many directions
