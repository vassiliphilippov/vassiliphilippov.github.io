protest

verb
affirm or declare solemnly
5.2 2.4
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈtest/ (AmE), /prə.ˈtest/ (BrE)
to state something firmly or assert it formally, especially in the face of doubt or accusation

Examples

To protest innocence
Despite the accusations, he protested his innocence in court.
During the interview, the employee repeatedly protested that he was not involved in any wrongdoing.
She protested that she had never seen the missing documents before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affirm - to state something as true in a formal way
  • antonym: deny - to declare something to be untrue
