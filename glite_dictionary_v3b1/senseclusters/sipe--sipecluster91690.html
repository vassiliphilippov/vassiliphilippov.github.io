sipe

noun
tire groove for grip
8.1
Pronunciation: / saɪp / (AmE), / saɪp / (BrE)
a thin slit or groove cut into the surface of a tire to improve traction, especially on wet or icy roads

Examples

Tire sipe
The mechanic pointed out each sipe on the tire and explained its purpose.
Winter tires have more sipes to help prevent slipping on icy roads.
After heavy rain, the sipes in the tires helped the car maintain control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tread - the part of a tire that touches the road
