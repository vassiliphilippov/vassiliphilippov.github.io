dim-witted

adjective
not intelligent; slow to understand
Pronunciation: / dɪmˈwɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪmˈwɪtɪd / (BrE)
not intelligent; slow to understand things or lacking in mental sharpness.

Examples

A dim-witted person
His dim-witted response to the simple question surprised everyone in the room.
She sometimes acts dim-witted to make people underestimate her.
The dim-witted character added comic relief to the movie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clever - quick to understand or learn; intelligent
  • synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
