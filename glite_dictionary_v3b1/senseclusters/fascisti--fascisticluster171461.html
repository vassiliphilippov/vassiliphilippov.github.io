fascisti

noun
authoritarian people (derogatory)
Pronunciation: / fɑːˈʃiːsti / (AmE), / fæˈʃiːsti / (BrE)
a derogatory term for people regarded as extremely authoritarian, repressive, or intolerant of opposition

Examples

Call them fascisti
The activists called the government officials fascisti for suppressing free speech at the rally.
Some critics labeled the strict school administrators as fascisti because of their harsh rules.
During the heated debate, one participant accused the others of being fascisti for not allowing dissent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian - favoring strict obedience to authority
  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power, often ruling oppressively
  • antonym: liberal - open to new ideas and tolerant of different views
