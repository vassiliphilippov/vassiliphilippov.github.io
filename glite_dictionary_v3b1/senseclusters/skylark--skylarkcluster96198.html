skylark

noun
a European songbird known for high hovering song
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌlɑrk / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌlɑːk / (BrE)
a small brown-speckled European songbird (Alauda arvensis) known for singing while hovering high in the sky

Examples

A singing skylark
Poets have long been inspired by the skylark and its soaring music.
The skylark's cheerful melody is often heard on summer days in the countryside.
We watched a skylark rise above the fields, filling the morning with its song.
