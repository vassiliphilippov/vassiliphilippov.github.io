flexible

adjective
able to bend without breaking
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛksəbəl / (AmE), / ˈflɛksəbl̩ /; / ˈflɛksɪbəl / (BrE)
able to bend easily without breaking and return to its original shape

Examples

Flexible plastic ruler
The plastic ruler is flexible enough to bend without snapping.
This hose is made from a flexible material that returns to its shape after being twisted.
Yoga helps you become more flexible by stretching your muscles and joints.
The new garden hose is very flexible and easy to maneuver.
Use a flexible piece of wire to reach into tight spaces.
Young branches are usually more flexible than old, dry ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pliable - easily bent; flexible
  • synonym: pliant - easily bent or flexible
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed; stiff
