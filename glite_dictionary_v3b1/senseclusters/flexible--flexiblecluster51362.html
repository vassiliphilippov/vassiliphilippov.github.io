Synonyms

  • elastic
    2.6 3.39/m
    elastic is springy and quickly returns to its original shape after stretching, while flexible focuses on bending without breaking.
  • resilient
    5.0 0.99/m
    resilient springs back after bending or pressure and suggests toughness, while flexible highlights easy bending without breaking.
  • pliant
    5.6 0.27/m
    pliant bends or is shaped easily like soft metal, while flexible stresses bending without breaking and returning to its shape.
  • supple
    3.7 1.65/m
    supple bends and moves smoothly, often in the body or leather, while flexible simply means it can bend without breaking.
  • pliable
    4.2 0.73/m
    pliable is easily shaped and often used for materials or people who adapt, while flexible is mainly about bending.
