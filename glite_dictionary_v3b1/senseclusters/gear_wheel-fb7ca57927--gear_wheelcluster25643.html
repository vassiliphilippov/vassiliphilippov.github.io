gear wheel

noun
toothed wheel for transmitting motion
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɡɪr wil / (AmE), / ɡɪə wiːl / (BrE)
A wheel with teeth around its edge, designed to mesh with another toothed component to transmit motion or change speed or direction.

Examples

A gear wheel system
A bicycle uses a gear wheel system to change speed and make pedaling easier.
Inside a clock, every gear wheel must be precisely aligned for accurate timekeeping.
The mechanic replaced the damaged gear wheel in the engine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cogwheel - a wheel with projecting teeth that mesh with another wheel
  • synonym: gear - a toothed mechanical part for transmitting motion or force
