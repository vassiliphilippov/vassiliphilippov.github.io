photovoltaic

noun
solar-electric device
5.6
Pronunciation: /foʊ.tə.voʊl.ˈteɪ.ɪk/ (AmE), /fəʊ.tə.vɒl.ˈteɪ.ɪk/ /; / /fəʊ.tə.vəʊl.ˈteɪ.ɪk/ (BrE)
a device or technology (such as a solar cell or panel) that converts light into electricity

Examples

A photovoltaic panel
They installed a new photovoltaic on the roof to power the garden lights.
The company tested every photovoltaic for efficiency before shipping the modules to customers.
After the storm, the technician replaced the broken photovoltaic to restore the house system.
The house was equipped with a photovoltaic system to reduce its reliance on fossil fuels.
Advances in photovoltaic technology have made solar energy more affordable for homeowners.
Researchers are developing new materials to improve the efficiency of photovoltaic devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photovoltaic panel - a panel made of photovoltaic cells to generate electricity from sunlight
  • synonym: solar cell - a device that directly converts light into electricity
  • synonym: solar panel - an assembly of solar cells packaged together to generate electricity
