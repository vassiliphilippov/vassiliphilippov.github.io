pappy

adjective
overly sentimental
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæpi / (AmE), / ˈpæpi / (BrE)
overly sentimental or weakly emotional in feeling or style, often in a way that seems insincere or excessive

Examples

Pappy story
The movie was too pappy for my taste, with every scene trying to make the audience cry.
Critics described the new song as pappy and lacking genuine emotion.
He prefers stories with depth, not pappy tales full of clichés.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mawkish - excessively sentimental or emotional
  • synonym: sentimental - showing or causing a lot of tender emotions
  • antonym: unsentimental - not emotional or sentimental
