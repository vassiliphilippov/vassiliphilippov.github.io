flouncy dress

noun
dress with ruffled, lively layers
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊnsi drɛs / (AmE), / ˈflaʊnsi drɛs / (BrE)
A dress with lively, bouncy, or ruffled layers that create a sense of movement.

Examples

A flouncy dress style
Designers often use layers of tulle to achieve the classic look of a flouncy dress.
She wore a flouncy dress to the summer garden party, drawing everyone's attention.
The little girl's flouncy dress bounced with every step she took.
