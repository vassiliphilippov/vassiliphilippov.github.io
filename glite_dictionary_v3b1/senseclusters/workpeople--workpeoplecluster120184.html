workpeople

noun
employees or staff
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌpipəl /; / ˈwɜrkˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌpiːpəl / (BrE)
people who are employed to do work, especially manual or industrial work

Examples

The new policy aims to improve conditions for all workpeople regardless of their roles.
The workpeople arrived early to prepare the factory for the day's production.
During the strike, all workpeople refused to enter the building until their demands were met.

The workpeople gathered in the morning to receive instructions for the day's tasks.
During the renovation, the workpeople made sure to keep the site clean and organized.
The factory owner praised the workpeople for their dedication and teamwork.
