workpeople

noun
employees or staff
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌpipəl /; / ˈwɜrkˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌpiːpəl / (BrE)
people who are employed to do work, especially manual or industrial work
