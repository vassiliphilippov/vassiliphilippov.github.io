depiction

noun
graphic or vivid description or representation
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɪkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪkʃən / (BrE)
a graphic or vivid representation or description, especially in words

Examples

A depiction in writing
Her novel offers a nuanced depiction of family relationships.
The author's depiction of rural life captured the hardships and joys of the community.
The film's depiction of the battle was both realistic and moving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: description - an account of something in words
  • synonym: portrayal - a representation or description in art or literature
  • synonym: representation - the action of depicting or showing something
