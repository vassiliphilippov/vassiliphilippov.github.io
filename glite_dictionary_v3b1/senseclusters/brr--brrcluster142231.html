brr

interjection
exclamation of cold
9.5
Pronunciation: / bɜr / (AmE), / bɜː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to show that you feel cold or are shivering

Examples

Brr outside
When he stepped outside into the snow, he shivered and said, "brr, it's freezing!"
She wrapped her scarf tighter and whispered, "brr, I can't feel my fingers."
After jumping into the cold lake, he gasped, "brr, that water is icy!"
He wrapped his arms around himself and said, "brr, I should have worn a warmer coat."
As the icy wind blew through the window, she whispered, "brr, it's so chilly in here."
After stepping outside into the snow, he exclaimed, "brr, winter has really arrived!"
