PFD

noun
life jacket
4.9
Pronunciation: / pi ɛf di / (AmE), / piː ɛf diː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'personal flotation device', a life jacket or vest worn for safety in water

Examples

Wear a PFD
Before getting into the kayak, make sure your PFD is fastened securely.
Children are required to wear a PFD at all times while on the dock.
The instructor checked that everyone had a PFD before starting the sailing lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: life jacket - a vest or jacket designed to keep a person afloat in water
  • synonym: life vest - a sleeveless jacket that helps keep you afloat in water
