rubby

adjective
slang for heavy drinker
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌ.bɪ/ /; / /ˈrʌ.biː/ (AmE), /ˈrʌ.bɪ/ /; / /ˈrʌ.biː/ (BrE)
slang describing someone, especially a homeless person, who drinks a lot of cheap alcohol

Examples

A rubby person
In some cities, the term rubby is used to refer to people who drink on the streets.
He was unfairly labeled a rubby because of his appearance and the bottle in his hand.
The shelter offers support to anyone, whether they are a rubby or just down on their luck.
