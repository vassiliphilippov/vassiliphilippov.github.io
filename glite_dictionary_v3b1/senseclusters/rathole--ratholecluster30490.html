rathole

noun
a hole made by rats
Pronunciation: / ˈrætˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈræt.həʊl / (BrE)
a hole in a wall or floor made by rats; also, any small hole made by rodents.

Examples

A rathole in the wall
The inspector pointed out several ratholes in the storeroom wall.
The old basement was full of ratholes and needed urgent repairs.
We found a rathole under the kitchen sink, so we set a trap nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burrow - a hole or tunnel dug by an animal, especially a small mammal
