pennywort

noun
Asian herb, salad green
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛniˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈpɛniˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a tropical Asian herb with rounded leaves, used as a salad green and in traditional medicine

Examples

Fresh pennywort leaves
She added chopped pennywort to the salad for a fresh, herbal flavor.
In traditional medicine, pennywort is believed to have healing properties.
Farmers in Southeast Asia often grow pennywort for use in local dishes and drinks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gotu kola - another name for pennywort, especially in South Asia
  • synonym: water pennywort - a related aquatic plant with similar rounded leaves
