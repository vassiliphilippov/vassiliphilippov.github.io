thrown

adjective
knocked to the ground
Pronunciation: / θroʊn / (AmE), / θrəʊn / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) knocked or caused to fall to the ground, especially by being pushed, tackled, or forced down.

Examples

Thrown to the ground
A thrown football player struggled to regain his footing on the field.
Several thrown wrestlers lay on the mat after the intense match.
The thrown rider quickly got back on his horse after the accident.
