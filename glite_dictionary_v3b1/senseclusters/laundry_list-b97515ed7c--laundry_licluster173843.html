laundry list

noun
long, detailed list of items or reasons
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔndɹi lɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndɹi lɪst / (BrE)
a long or detailed list of items, tasks, or reasons, often implying the list is extensive or tedious

Examples

A laundry list of tasks
Environmental activists compiled a laundry list of urgent actions for lawmakers.
Her doctor gave her a laundry list of things to avoid during recovery.
The manager presented a laundry list of proposed changes to the staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inventory - a complete list of items, often in stock or possessions
  • synonym: shopping list - a list of items to be bought at a store
