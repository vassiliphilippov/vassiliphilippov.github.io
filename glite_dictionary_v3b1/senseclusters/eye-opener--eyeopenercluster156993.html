eye-opener

noun
revealing or enlightening experience
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌoʊpənər / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌəʊpənə / (BrE)
an experience or event that makes you realize something new or surprising, often changing your perspective

Examples

A real eye-opener
Her first week at university was a real eye-opener about independent living.
The report was an eye-opener on the effects of pollution.
Traveling to a developing country can be an eye-opener for many people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revelation - a surprising and previously unknown fact, especially one made known in a dramatic way
  • synonym: wake-up call - an event that makes people realize that something needs to change
