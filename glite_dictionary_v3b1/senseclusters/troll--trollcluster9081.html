troll

verb
fish by drawing a line or lure through water
8.7 5.4
Pronunciation: /troʊl/ (AmE), /trəʊl/ /; / /trɒl/ (BrE)
to fish by dragging a baited line or lure behind a moving boat or through the water

Examples

To troll for fish
He prefers trolling near the shore where the big fish are often found.
The fishermen trolled the lake for hours without a single bite.
We decided to troll for trout in the early morning when the water was calm.
