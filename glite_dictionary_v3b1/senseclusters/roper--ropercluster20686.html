roper

noun
cowboy using a lasso
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊpər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊpə / (BrE)
a cowboy or ranch worker who uses a lasso to catch cattle or horses

Examples

Skilled roper at rodeo
Every roper in the region hoped to win the prestigious championship.
She admired the artistry of the roper during the rodeo competition.
The skilled roper caught the stray calf with a single toss of his lasso.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who herds and tends cattle, especially in the western US
