recalibrate

verb
to adjust again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkælɪˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkælɪbreɪt / (BrE)
to adjust or reset a device, instrument, or system so it works correctly or more accurately

Examples
