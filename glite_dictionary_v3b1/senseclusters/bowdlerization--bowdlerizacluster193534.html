bowdlerization

noun
removal of offensive material from texts
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /boʊd.lə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /baʊd.lə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /bəʊd.lə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /baʊd.lə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or result of removing or changing parts of a text that are considered offensive or inappropriate, often making it less authentic or effective
