phonographic

adjective
sound-based writing
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊnəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊnəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to a writing system or method that represents the sounds of speech.

Examples

Phonographic writing system
The Korean Hangul script is considered a highly phonographic writing system.
Linguists study how phonographic systems represent spoken language through symbols.
Unlike logographic scripts, phonographic alphabets focus on the sounds of words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alphabetic - relating to a writing system based on letters representing sounds
  • antonym: logographic - relating to a writing system that uses symbols for words or morphemes
