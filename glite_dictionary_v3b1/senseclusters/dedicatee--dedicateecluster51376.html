dedicatee

noun
recipient of dedication
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛdəˌkeɪˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌdɛdɪkeɪˈtiː / (BrE)
a person or organization to whom something, such as land or property, is formally dedicated, especially for public use.

Examples

Dedicatee of the land
The city became the official dedicatee of the new public park after the land was donated.
In the legal document, the school district was named as the dedicatee of the property.
The foundation acted as the dedicatee for several parcels of land intended for community use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beneficiary - a person or group who receives something, especially a benefit or gift
  • antonym: donor - a person who gives or donates something
