word-blind

adjective
unable to recognize written words; relating to alexia
5.2 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrd.blaɪnd/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːd.blaɪnd/ (BrE)
Relating to or symptomatic of alexia; unable to recognize or read written words due to brain injury or disorder.

Examples

Word-blind patient
