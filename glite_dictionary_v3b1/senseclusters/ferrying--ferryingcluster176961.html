ferrying

noun
act of transport by boat or aircraft
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɛriɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of transporting people or goods by boat or aircraft, especially as a routine or organized service

Examples

The ferrying of aircraft
His company specializes in the ferrying of aircraft between international airports.
The ferrying of supplies to the island was delayed by the storm.
The military coordinated the ferrying of troops across the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transportation - the movement of people or goods from one place to another
