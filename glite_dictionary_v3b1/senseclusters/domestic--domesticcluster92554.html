domestic

adjective
produced in a particular country
1.0
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)
produced in or relating to one's own country; not foreign

Examples

Domestic products
The government imposed tariffs on domestic steel to protect local producers.
The restaurant only serves domestic wines from local vineyards.
This company specializes in both imported and domestic furniture.
Efforts to boost the domestic economy have become a priority for the government.
Rising inflation is one of the key domestic issues facing the country.
The president's speech focused mainly on domestic policy and national security.
