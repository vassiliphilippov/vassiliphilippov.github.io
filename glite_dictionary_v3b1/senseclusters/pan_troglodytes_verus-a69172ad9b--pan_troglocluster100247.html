Pan troglodytes verus

noun
western African chimpanzee subspecies
9.9
Pronunciation: / pæn trɒɡləˈdaɪtiːz ˈvɛrəs / (AmE), / pan trɒɡlɒˈdɪːtiːz ˈvɛərʌs / (BrE)
A subspecies of the common chimpanzee native to western Africa, characterized by a masked or pale face, and possibly distinct from eastern and central chimpanzees.

Examples

Pan troglodytes verus chimpanzee
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the remaining Pan troglodytes verus in their natural habitat.

The Pan troglodytes verus population is critically endangered due to habitat loss.
