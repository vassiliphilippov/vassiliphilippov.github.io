word processor

noun
software for editing and formatting text documents
Pronunciation: / wɝːd ˈprɑː.ses.ɚ / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈprəʊ.ses.ə / (BrE)
a computer program that enables users to write, edit, format, and print text documents

Examples

A word processor program
Before submitting the report, she checked the formatting on her word processor.
I used a word processor to write my entire thesis.
Many people prefer a simple word processor for writing letters and essays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: text editor - a software application for editing plain text
