viperous

adjective
spiteful or snake-like
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪpərəs / (AmE), / ˈvaɪpərəs / (BrE)
resembling a viper, or showing spiteful or malicious behavior

Examples

Her viperous remarks

He avoided his viperous coworker after their heated disagreement.
The novel's villain is known for his viperous schemes and cruel words.
