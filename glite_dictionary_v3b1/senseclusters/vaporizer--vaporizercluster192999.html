vaporizer

noun
device releasing vapor, esp. for inhalation
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪpəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəraɪzə / (BrE)
a device that releases a substance in the form of a vapor, especially for medicinal inhalation

Examples

Medical vaporizer
Modern vaporizers can be used to deliver essential oils or medicinal compounds.
She uses a vaporizer in her room to help with her asthma symptoms.
The doctor recommended buying a vaporizer for inhalation therapy during the winter.
He filled the vaporiser with eucalyptus oil before turning it on.
Many people use a vaporiser to add moisture to dry indoor air.
The doctor recommended using a vaporiser to ease her breathing during the night.

  • synonym: vaporizer - device that emits a substance in vapor form, especially for medicinal use (American spelling)
