nubuck

noun
velvety leather
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnuˌbʌk / (AmE), / ˈnjuːˌbʌk / (BrE)
a soft, velvety leather made by buffing the outer side of animal hide

Examples

Nubuck shoes
He bought a pair of nubuck shoes because they felt soft and looked elegant.
The nubuck jacket was comfortable to wear and had a luxurious texture.
Care instructions for nubuck are different from those for regular leather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leather - material made from the skin of animals, used for clothing and goods
  • synonym: suede - a soft leather with a napped finish, made from the inner side of animal hide
