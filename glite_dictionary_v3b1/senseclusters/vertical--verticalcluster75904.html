vertical

noun
business sector
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkəl / (BrE)

Examples

Tech vertical
The startup decided to focus on the healthcare vertical to specialize its services.
Expanding into a new vertical allowed the company to reach a different group of customers.
Many software firms target specific verticals such as education or finance for their products.
