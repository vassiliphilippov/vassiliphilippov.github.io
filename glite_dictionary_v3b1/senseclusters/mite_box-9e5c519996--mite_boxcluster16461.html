mite box

noun
box for collecting alms, especially in a church
Pronunciation: / maɪt bɑks / (AmE), / maɪt bɒks / (BrE)
a box, often in a church, used for collecting alms or small donations

Examples

A mite box in church
Each Sunday, children would drop coins into the mite box at the front of the church.
She donated her allowance to the mite box as a gesture of charity.
The old mite box was painted blue and decorated with a golden cross.
