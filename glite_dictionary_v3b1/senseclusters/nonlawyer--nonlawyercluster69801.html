nonlawyer

noun
person not a lawyer
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈlɔjər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɔːjə / (BrE)
a person who is not trained, qualified, or licensed as a lawyer

Examples

A nonlawyer on the jury
Only a nonlawyer can serve on this jury to ensure impartiality.
The firm hired a nonlawyer to manage its administrative operations.
As a nonlawyer, she could not give legal advice to clients.
The judge explained the court procedures so that nonlawyers could understand them.
Many legal documents are difficult for nonlawyers to interpret without help.
Workshops are available to teach nonlawyers about their basic legal rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lawyer - a person qualified to practice law
  • synonym: layperson - someone who is not a professional in a particular field
