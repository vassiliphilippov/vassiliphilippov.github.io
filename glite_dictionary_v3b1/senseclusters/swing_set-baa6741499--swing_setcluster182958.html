swing set

noun
children's play equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋ ˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋ ˌsɛt / (BrE)
a structure with one or more seats suspended by ropes or chains, designed for children to swing back and forth

Examples

Playground swing set
The children laughed as they played on the swing set in the park.
We installed a new swing set in our backyard for the kids.
After school, the neighborhood kids gathered around the old swing set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playground - an outdoor area for children to play
  • synonym: slide - a sloping structure for children to slide down
