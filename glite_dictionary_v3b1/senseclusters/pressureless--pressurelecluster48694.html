pressureless

adjective
without pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃələs / (BrE)
having no internal or external pressure applied

Examples

Pressureless chamber
The experiment required a pressureless environment to ensure accurate results.
A pressureless tennis ball does not rely on internal air pressure to bounce.
Scientists studied the behavior of gases in a pressureless chamber.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pressurized - containing or having pressure applied
  • synonym: vacuum - a space entirely devoid of matter and pressure
