scauper

noun
engraving tool for scooping broad areas
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊpər / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpə / (BrE)
a graver with a curved edge used for scooping out broad areas of wood or metal in engraving

Examples

A scauper tool
He bought a new set of tools, including a fine scauper for broad cuts.
She learned to handle the scauper delicately when working on detailed metal engravings.
The engraver selected his scauper to remove large sections of wood quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graver - a hand tool for engraving, especially in metal or wood
