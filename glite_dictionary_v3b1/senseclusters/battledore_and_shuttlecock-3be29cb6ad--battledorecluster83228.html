battledore and shuttlecock

noun
traditional racket game with shuttlecock
7.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l̩ˌdɔːr ənd ˈʃʌt̬l̩ˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl.dɔː ənd ˈʃʌt.əl.kɒk / (BrE)
an old game in which players use small rackets (battledores) to keep a shuttlecock in the air as long as possible without letting it touch the ground
