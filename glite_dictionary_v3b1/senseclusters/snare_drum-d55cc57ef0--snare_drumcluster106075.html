snare drum

noun
small drum with snares across lower head
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɛr drʌm /; / snɛr drʌm / (AmE), / ˈsnɛə drʌm /; / snɛə drʌm / (BrE)
a small drum with metal wires (snares) stretched across one head, producing a sharp, crisp sound, used in orchestras and bands
