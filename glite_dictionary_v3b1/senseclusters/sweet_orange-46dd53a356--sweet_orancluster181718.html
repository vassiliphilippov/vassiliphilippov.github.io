sweet orange

noun
a variety of orange with sweet edible pulp
1.0
Pronunciation: / swit ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
a round, sweet, orange-colored fruit (Citrus × sinensis) that is commonly eaten fresh or used for juice
