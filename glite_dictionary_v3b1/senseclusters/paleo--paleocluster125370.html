paleo

x
ancient prefix
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪlioʊ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪlɪəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'ancient' or 'prehistoric', used in scientific and technical compound words

Examples

Paleoanthropology studies
The term paleoanthropology refers to the study of ancient human ancestors.
Scientists analyze paleoclimates to learn about Earth's environmental history.
The paleodiet is inspired by the presumed eating habits of prehistoric humans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neo - a combining form meaning 'new', used in compound words
