algor

noun
postmortem coldness
Pronunciation: / ˈælɡɔr / (AmE), / ˈælɡɔː / (BrE)
a medical term for coldness, especially the cooling of the body after death

Examples

Algor after death
The forensic pathologist measured the algor to estimate the time of death.
After several hours, algor was clearly present in the deceased patient.
The cooling process known as algor is an important clue in forensic investigations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: livor mortis - the pooling of blood in the body after death
  • synonym: rigor mortis - the stiffening of the body after death
