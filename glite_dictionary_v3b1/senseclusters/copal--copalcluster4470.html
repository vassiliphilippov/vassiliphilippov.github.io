copal

noun
brittle resin used in varnishes
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.pəl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.pəl / (BrE)
a hard, brittle, and aromatic resin from various tropical trees, used chiefly in making varnishes and lacquers

Examples
