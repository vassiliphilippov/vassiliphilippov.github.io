push off

verb
to leave; to begin a journey (informal)
2.5
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ ɔf / (AmE), / pʊʃ ɒf / (BrE)
(informal) to leave or to start a journey; to go away.

Examples

To push off for journey
After finishing their coffee, the hikers decided to push off for the mountain trail.
He told the annoying salesman to push off and leave him alone.
It's getting late—we should push off if we want to arrive before dark.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrive - to reach a place at the end of a journey
  • synonym: depart - to leave, especially to start a journey
  • synonym: set off - to begin a journey
