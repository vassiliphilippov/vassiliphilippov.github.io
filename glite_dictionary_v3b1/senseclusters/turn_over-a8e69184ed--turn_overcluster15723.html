turn over

verb
dig or loosen earth (soil)
Pronunciation: / tɝn ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
To dig up, loosen, or remove earth, especially to prepare soil for planting.

Examples

To turn over the soil
Every spring, the gardeners turn over the soil to prepare for planting.
Farmers often turn over their fields to keep the soil healthy.
He used a spade to turn over the earth in the flower bed.
