attention deficit hyperactivity disorder

noun
condition involving hyperactivity and attention difficulties
4.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈten.ʃən ˈdef.ɪ.sɪt haɪ.pər.æk.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti dɪs.ˈɔːr.dər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈten.ʃən ˈdef.ɪ.sɪt haɪ.pə.æk.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti dɪs.ˈɔː.də/ (BrE)
