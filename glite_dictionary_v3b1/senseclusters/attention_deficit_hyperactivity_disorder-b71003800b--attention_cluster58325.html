Synonyms

  • ADHD
    4.0 11/m
    ADHD is the common short medical name used by doctors and researchers, while attention deficit hyperactivity disorder is the full formal term.
  • attention deficit disorder
    3.6 0.95/m
    attention deficit disorder names a neurodevelopmental condition that often highlights inattention and may not stress hyperactivity, while attention deficit hyperactivity disorder includes clear hyperactivity and impulsivity.
  • ADD
    4.0 3.96/m
    ADD is an older label once used mainly for children and is less precise, while attention deficit hyperactivity disorder is the current clinical name.
