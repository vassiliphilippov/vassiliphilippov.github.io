attention deficit hyperactivity disorder

noun
condition involving hyperactivity and attention difficulties
4.9
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛnʃən ˈdɛfəsɪt ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvəti dɪsˌɔrdər / (AmE), / əˈtɛnʃən ˈdɛfɪsɪt ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvɪti dɪsˌɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition, especially in children, characterized by difficulty in maintaining attention, hyperactivity, and impulsive behavior.
