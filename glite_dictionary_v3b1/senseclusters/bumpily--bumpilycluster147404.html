Synonyms

  • jerkily
    5.2 0.21/m
    jerkily shows quick, sharp, sudden movements, while bumpily describes rough motion from many small bumps on an uneven surface.
  • choppily
    6.4 0.01/m
    choppily means movement or flow that stops and starts, while bumpily means rough movement caused by bumps under the wheels.
  • lurchingly
    8.9 0.00/m
    lurchingly is a sudden heavy movement like a shove forward or sideways, while bumpily is repeated small jolts from uneven ground.
  • unsteadily
    3.9 1.19/m
    unsteadily means moving without balance and likely to fall, while bumpily means moving roughly because of bumps on the surface.
  • unevenly
    3.5 1.16/m
