NICU

noun
newborn intensive care unit
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈen.aɪ.siː.juː/ /; / /ˈnɪk.juː/ (AmE), /ˈen.aɪ.siː.juː/ /; / /ˈnɪk.juː/ (BrE)
