troll

noun
fishing lure used in trolling
3.6 6.9
Pronunciation: / troʊl / (AmE), / trəʊl / (BrE)
a fishing lure or the method of fishing by dragging a line or lure behind a moving boat

Examples

A fishing troll

Several different trolls can be used to increase the chances of catching large fish.

He prefers to troll for bass in the early morning when the lake is calm.
