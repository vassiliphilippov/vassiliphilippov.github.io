hairy wood mint

noun
a North American herb with spotted flowers
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛri wʊd mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈheəri wʊd mɪnt / (BrE)
a North American perennial herb (Blephilia hirsuta) with square stems and clusters of light blue, pale purple, or white flowers spotted with purple
