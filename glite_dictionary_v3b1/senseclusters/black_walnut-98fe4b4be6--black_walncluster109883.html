black walnut

noun
North American walnut tree and its nut
5.1 3.5
Pronunciation: /blæk ˈwɔːl.nʌt/ /; / /blæk ˈwɔːl.nət/ (AmE), /blæk ˈwɔːl.nʌt/ /; / /blæk ˈwɔːl.nət/ (BrE)
a North American walnut tree (Juglans nigra) valued for its hard, dark wood and edible nuts
