frappe

noun
liqueur over shaved ice
1.7
Pronunciation: / fræˈpeɪ / (AmE), / fræˈpeɪ / (BrE)
A drink made by pouring liqueur over shaved ice, sometimes with added flavorings.

Examples

Coffee liqueur frappe
After dinner, she enjoyed a frappe with a splash of coffee liqueur.
The bartender served a refreshing frappe topped with orange zest.
They ordered a chocolate frappe as a cool treat on a summer evening.
