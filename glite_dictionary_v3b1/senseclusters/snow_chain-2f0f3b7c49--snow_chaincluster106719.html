snow chain

noun
chain fitted to tire for snow/ice traction
2.0
Pronunciation: / snoʊ tʃeɪn / (AmE), / snəʊ tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a chain fitted around a vehicle tire to improve traction on snow or ice

Examples

Snow chains on tires
It's mandatory to carry snow chains in some regions during the winter months.
Our car couldn't make it up the hill without snow chains on all four wheels.
The driver fitted snow chains to the tires before driving up the icy mountain road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tire chain - chain attached to a vehicle's tire to increase grip on slippery surfaces
