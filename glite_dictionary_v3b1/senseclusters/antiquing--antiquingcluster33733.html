antiquing

noun
search for antiques
Pronunciation: / ænˈtiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ænˈtiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of searching for and buying old, rare, or valuable items, especially antiques

Examples

To antique old items
They spent the weekend antiquing in several small towns across the countryside.
Many people enjoy antiquing as a way to find unique furniture and decorations.
On their vacation, the couple went antiquing and discovered a rare vintage clock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collecting - the activity of gathering items of a particular type as a hobby
  • synonym: shopping - the activity of buying goods from stores
