rehypothecation

noun
reuse of pledged collateral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːhaɪˌpɑːθəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːhɪˌpɒθɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the practice by which financial institutions use collateral pledged by clients to secure their own borrowing or loans

Examples

Rehypothecation of collateral
Investment banks may use rehypothecation to maximize their available funds for trading activities.
Clients should be aware of the risks involved when their collateral is subject to rehypothecation.
Regulations in some countries limit the extent of rehypothecation to protect investors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypothecation - the pledging of assets as collateral for a loan
  • synonym: securitization - the process of turning assets into securities for sale to investors
