hiya

interjection
informal greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪjə / (AmE), / ˈhaɪjə / (BrE)
an informal and friendly greeting used to say hello

Examples

Hiya there
Hiya, are you coming to the party tonight?
She waved and said hiya as she entered the room.
When I called him, he answered with a cheerful hiya.
