carbonated water

noun
fizzy water containing dissolved carbon dioxide
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.bə.neɪ.tɪd ˈwɔː.tər/ /; / /ˈkɑːr.bə.neɪ.tɪd ˈwɑː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.bə.neɪ.tɪd ˈwɔː.tə/ /; / /ˈkɑː.bə.neɪ.təd ˈwɔː.tə/ (BrE)
