carbonated water

noun
fizzy water containing dissolved carbon dioxide
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbəˌneɪtɪd ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbənətɪd ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
Water into which carbon dioxide gas has been dissolved, making it fizzy; used as a beverage or mixer.

Examples

Carbonated water drink
Many cocktail recipes use carbonated water as a mixer to add fizziness.
She prefers to drink carbonated water with lemon during lunch.
The waiter asked if we wanted still or carbonated water with our meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sparkling water - water with dissolved carbon dioxide, naturally or artificially carbonated
  • antonym: still water - plain water with no carbonation
