French chalk

noun
soft talc used in marking cloth or removing stains
4.1
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ tʃɔk / (AmE), / frentʃ tʃɔːk / (BrE)
a soft, white, compact form of talc used particularly for marking cloth in tailoring or for removing grease stains

Examples

A piece of French chalk
A little French chalk can help remove stubborn grease stains from clothing.
She always keeps a stick of French chalk in her sewing kit.
The tailor drew lines on the fabric with French chalk before cutting it.

  • synonym: talc - a soft mineral used in powder and industrial applications
