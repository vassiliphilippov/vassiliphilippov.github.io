historicist

adjective
imitating historical styles
8.6
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪsɪst / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪsɪst / (BrE)
characterized by the use or imitation of historical styles, especially in art, architecture, or design

Examples

Historicist architecture style

Critics argued that the building's historicist elements clashed with the surrounding modern structures.
She prefers historicist interiors that echo the elegance of past centuries.
