soft tree fern

noun
Australian and Tasmanian fern species
Pronunciation: / sɔft tri fɜrn / (AmE), / sɒft triː fɜːn / (BrE)
a species of fern native to Australia and Tasmania, often cultivated for its tall trunk and feathery fronds, and known for its hardiness in cool climates
