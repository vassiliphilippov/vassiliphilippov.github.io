canker sore

noun
painful mouth ulcer
Pronunciation: / ˈkæŋkɚ sɔr / (AmE), / ˈkæŋkə sɔː / (BrE)
A small painful ulcer that develops on the inside of the mouth or on the lips.

Examples

A canker sore inside
He was annoyed by the canker sore that appeared on his inner lip.
My doctor suggested a special rinse to treat my canker sore.
She couldn't enjoy her meal because a canker sore made it painful to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphthous ulcer - technical term for a canker sore
  • synonym: mouth ulcer - a sore in the lining of the mouth
