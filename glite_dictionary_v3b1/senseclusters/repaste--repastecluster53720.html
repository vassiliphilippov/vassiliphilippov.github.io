repaste

verb
to paste again
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpeɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpeɪst / (BrE)
to insert copied digital content into a document or field again using the paste command

Examples

To repaste copied text
If you make a mistake, you can always repaste the text from your clipboard.
She had to repaste the image after accidentally deleting it from the document.
Before submitting the form, he decided to repaste the updated address into the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paste - to insert copied content into a document or field
  • antonym: remove - to take something out of a document or field
