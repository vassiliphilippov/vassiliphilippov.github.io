Condylura cristata

noun
species of mole with star-shaped nose
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.dɪˈlʊr.ə krɪˈsteɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dɪˈljʊə.rə krɪˈsteɪ.tə / (BrE)
a species of mole native to eastern North America, notable for the pink, fleshy tentacles around its nose; also known as the star-nosed mole
