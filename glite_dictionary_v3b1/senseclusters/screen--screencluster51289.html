separate grain from chaff with a sieve

Pronunciation: / skriÀên / (AmE), / skriÀên / (BrE)
to separate grain from chaff or other unwanted material by using a sieve or riddle

Examples

To screen wheat

Old mills often have machines designed to screen barley from its hulls.
