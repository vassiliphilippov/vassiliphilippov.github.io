checkered

adjective
patterned with squares of color
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkɚd /; / ˈtʃɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəd / (BrE)
having a pattern of alternating squares of different colors, usually in a regular arrangement

Examples

Checkered shirt

The racing flag is famously checkered with black and white squares.
The tablecloth had a checkered pattern of red and white.
He wore a chequered shirt to the party, making him stand out in the crowd.
The picnic blanket was bright and chequered with red and white squares.
They laid a chequered tablecloth on the table before setting out the dishes.
