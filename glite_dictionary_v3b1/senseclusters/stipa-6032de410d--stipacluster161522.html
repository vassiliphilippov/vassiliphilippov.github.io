Stipa

noun
ornamental feather grass
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪpə / (AmE), / ˈstiːpə / (BrE)
a genus of ornamental grasses with narrow leaves and feathery flower heads, commonly known as feather grass

Examples
