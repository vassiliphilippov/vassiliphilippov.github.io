at hand

adjective
imminent; about to happen
Pronunciation: / ət hænd / (AmE), / ət hand / (BrE)
likely to happen soon; imminent or about to occur.

Examples

A change at hand
She sensed that a major change in her life was at hand.
The start of the festival is at hand, and excitement is building in the city.
Winter is at hand, so it's time to prepare the house for colder weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imminent - about to happen; impending
  • antonym: remote - distant in time; unlikely to happen soon
