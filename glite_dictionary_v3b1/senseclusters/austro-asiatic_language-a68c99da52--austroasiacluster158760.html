Austro-Asiatic language

noun
language family in southern and southeastern Asia
8.2 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔː.stroʊ eɪ.ʒiː.ˈæt.ɪk ˈlæŋ.gwɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.strəʊ eɪ.ʒiː.ˈæt.ɪk ˈlæŋ.gwɪdʒ/ (BrE)
