animatronic

adjective
robotic lifelike movement
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌænɪməˈtrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪk / (BrE)
using robotic technology to create lifelike movements in models or figures, especially for entertainment or display

Examples

Animatronic animals move
The theme park installed several animatronic animals that move and make sounds for visitors.
An animatronic dragon greeted guests at the entrance, blinking and breathing smoke.
The movie used animatronic creatures to create realistic special effects without computer graphics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mechanical - operated by a machine or mechanism
  • synonym: robotic - relating to or resembling a robot
