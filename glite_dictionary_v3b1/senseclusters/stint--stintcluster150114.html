stint

noun
limited time spent on a task or job
9.1
Pronunciation: / stɪnt / (AmE), / stɪnt / (BrE)
a period of time spent doing a particular job or activity, especially one that is limited or fixed in duration

Examples

A stint at work
After his stint in the army, he returned to civilian life with new skills.
He did a brief stint working at a law firm before starting his own business.
She completed a six-month stint as a volunteer in South America.
After a two-year stint in the military, he returned to university.
During his stint at the factory, he learned valuable skills.
She completed a brief stint as an assistant in a law firm last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shift - a period of work during which someone has specific duties
  • synonym: spell - a period of time spent doing an activity
  • synonym: stretch - a period of time spent engaged in a particular activity, especially in prison
