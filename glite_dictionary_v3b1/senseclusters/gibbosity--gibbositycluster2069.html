gibbosity

noun
a bulge or rounded projection
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈbɑsəti / (AmE), / dʒɪˈbɒsəti / (BrE)
a swelling, bulge, or rounded protuberance projecting from the surrounding surface

Examples

A gibbosity on skull
A slight gibbosity on the skull can sometimes be felt near the back of the head.
The old tree's trunk was covered with a large gibbosity where a branch had once grown.
The statue's intricate design features a curious gibbosity on its shoulder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulge - a rounded swelling or protuberance
  • antonym: depression - a sunken or lowered part on a surface; the opposite of a bulge
  • synonym: protuberance - something that protrudes from a surface
