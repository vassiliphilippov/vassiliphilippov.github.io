wonderstruck

adjective
amazed and admiring
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌndərˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəstrʌk / (BrE)
filled with great surprise and admiration, often because something is beautiful or amazing

Examples

Children wonderstruck by magic
The children were wonderstruck by the magician's performance.
Standing at the edge of the Grand Canyon, she felt completely wonderstruck by the view.
He listened, wonderstruck, as the orchestra played the beautiful symphony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awestruck - filled with awe and admiration
  • antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or interest
