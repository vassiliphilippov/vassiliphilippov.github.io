telescope sight

noun
optical sight for firearms
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌskoʊp saɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪskəʊp sʌɪt / (BrE)
an optical gunsight consisting of a telescope mounted on a firearm to improve accuracy when aiming

Examples

A telescope sight on a rifle
Many rifles come equipped with a telescope sight for better long-distance targeting.
She carefully cleaned the telescope sight after returning from the shooting range.
The hunter adjusted the telescope sight to improve his accuracy in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scope - an optical device used to magnify a distant object, especially on firearms
