daimonion

noun
evil spirit or demon
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˈmoʊniən / (AmE), / daɪˈməʊniən / (BrE)
an evil spirit or demon, especially as described in early Christian and New Testament texts

Examples

An evil daimonion
In the ancient text, a daimonion was believed to possess those who strayed from the faith.
The villagers feared the presence of a daimonion after several unexplained events occurred.
Scholars debate whether the term daimonion refers to a specific type of spirit or a general evil force.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - a benevolent spiritual being
  • synonym: demon - an evil supernatural being or spirit
