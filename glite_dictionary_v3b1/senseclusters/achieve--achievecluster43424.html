achieve

verb
accomplish with effort
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈtʃiv / (AmE), / əˈtʃiːv / (BrE)
to successfully accomplish something, especially by effort, skill, or courage

Examples

To achieve success
After years of hard work, she finally achieved her dream of becoming a doctor.
It's important to set realistic goals if you want to achieve them.
The team was able to achieve success through determination and collaboration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fail - to not succeed in achieving something
