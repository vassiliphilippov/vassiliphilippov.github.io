Clorox

verb
clean or disinfect with Clorox
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈklɔː.rɑːks/ /; / /ˈklɔː.rəks/ (AmE), /ˈklɔː.rɒks/ (BrE)
(informal) to clean or disinfect something using Clorox-brand bleach or a similar bleach product.

Examples

To Clorox the floor
She decided to Clorox the countertops after the spill to sanitize them for dinner.
We usually Clorox the bathroom once a week to keep mold from forming on the tiles.
After the kids' art project, he Cloroxed the table to remove paint and sticky residue.
