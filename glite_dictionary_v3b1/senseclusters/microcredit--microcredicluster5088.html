microcredit

noun
small loans for the poor
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.kre.dɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.kre.dɪt/ (BrE)
a system or loan that provides very small amounts of money to poor or low-income people, often to help them start small businesses
