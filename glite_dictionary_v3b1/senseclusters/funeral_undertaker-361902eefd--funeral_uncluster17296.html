funeral undertaker

noun
person who arranges funerals
Pronunciation: / ˈfjunɚəl ˌʌndɚˈteɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəl ˌʌndəˈteɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange and manage funerals

Examples

A funeral undertaker arranges funerals
After his grandfather died, the family contacted a local funeral undertaker to organize the service.
As a funeral undertaker, she often comforts grieving families during difficult times.
The funeral undertaker handled all the arrangements with great sensitivity and professionalism.
