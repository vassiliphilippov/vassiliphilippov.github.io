Orasone

noun
trademarked name for prednisone, an anti-inflammatory drug
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːr.əˌsoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɔː.rə.səʊn / (BrE)
A proprietary name for prednisone, a synthetic corticosteroid drug used as an anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressant, especially in the treatment of arthritis and other conditions.

Examples

Orasone medicine
After taking Orasone for several weeks, she noticed a significant improvement in her symptoms.
My doctor prescribed Orasone to help reduce the inflammation in my joints.
The pharmacist explained the correct way to take Orasone with food to avoid stomach upset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prednisone - synthetic corticosteroid drug, the active ingredient in Orasone
