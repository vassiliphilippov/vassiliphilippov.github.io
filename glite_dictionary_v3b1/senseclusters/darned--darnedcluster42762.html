darned

adjective
repaired with thread
Pronunciation: / dɑrnd / (AmE), / dɑːnd / (BrE)
repaired by sewing over a hole or worn area with thread

Examples

Darned socks
She wore a darned sweater that her grandmother had fixed for her.
The darned socks were almost as good as new after the repair.
He proudly showed off his darned jeans, which had been patched up by his mother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mended - repaired or fixed something that was damaged
  • antonym: torn - damaged by being ripped or split
