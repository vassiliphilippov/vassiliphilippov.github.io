Synonyms

  • level off
    3.9 1/m
    level off becomes steady after rising or falling, while plateau means to stop increasing and stay at the same level for a time.
  • flatline
    6.1 0.10/m
    flatline stays at a constant, unchanging level and often suggests a sudden stop or worry, while plateau simply stops rising and holds steady.
  • stagnate
    6.8 2/m
    stagnate means to stop developing and become inactive or dull, while plateau implies a stable level after a period of growth.
