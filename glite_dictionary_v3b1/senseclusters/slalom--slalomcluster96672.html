slalom

noun
downhill race with winding course marked by poles
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːləm / (AmE), / ˈslɑːləm / (BrE)
a downhill race in which competitors navigate a winding course marked by upright poles, typically on snow skis or similar vehicles

Examples

A slalom race
Learning to control your speed is crucial for a beginner in slalom skiing.
She completed the slalom course in record time at the Olympics.
The skier zigzagged through the poles during the challenging slalom.
