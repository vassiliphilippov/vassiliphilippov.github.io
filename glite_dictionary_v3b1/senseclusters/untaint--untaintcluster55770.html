untaint

verb
to clear from blame
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪnt / (BrE)
to remove blame, suspicion, or corruption from someone or something, restoring their good reputation or purity

Examples

To untaint a reputation
The new evidence helped untaint his reputation after years of suspicion.
She worked hard to untaint the company’s image following the scandal.
The apology was meant to untaint their name in the eyes of the public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exonerate - to officially clear someone from blame or fault
  • synonym: purify - to make something clean or free from impurities
  • antonym: taint - to damage or spoil the reputation or quality of something
