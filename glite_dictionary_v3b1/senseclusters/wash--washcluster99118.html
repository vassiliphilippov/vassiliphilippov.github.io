wash

noun
act of cleaning with water and soap
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ / (AmE), / wɒʃ / (BrE)
an act or process of cleaning something with water and usually soap or detergent

Examples

My dog hates taking a wash but loves rolling in the dirt.
