pisco

noun
South American grape brandy
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpiskoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpiːskəʊ / (BrE)
a clear brandy made from distilled grapes, especially produced in Peru and Chile

Examples

A glass of pisco
The bartender recommended a pisco sour as a refreshing cocktail.
She brought back a bottle of pisco from her trip to Peru.
Many Chilean celebrations include a toast with pisco.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brandy - a strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice
  • antonym: whisky - a distilled alcoholic beverage made from fermented grain mash
