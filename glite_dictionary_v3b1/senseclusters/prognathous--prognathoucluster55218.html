prognathous

adjective
having a projecting lower jaw
4.5 9.0
Pronunciation: /prɑːɡ.ˈneɪ.θəs/ /; / /prɑːɡ.ˈnə.θəs/ (AmE), /prɒɡ.ˈneɪ.θəs/ /; / /prɒɡ.ˈnə.θəs/ (BrE)
(of the jaw or face) having the lower jaw projecting forward beyond the upper part of the face.

Examples

Prognathous jaw shape
Some primate species are naturally prognathous compared to humans.
The fossil skull is prognathous, indicating a strong forward projection of the lower jaw.
The patient presented with a distinctly prognathous profile due to an extended jawbone.
