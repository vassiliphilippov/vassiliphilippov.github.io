holly

noun
evergreen tree or shrub with prickly leaves and red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑli / (AmE), / ˈhɒli / (BrE)
a tree or shrub of the genus Ilex, with shiny evergreen leaves, typically with prickly edges, and red berries

Examples

Red berries on holly
Every December, people decorate their homes with holly and other festive greenery.
The bright red berries on the holly tree attract many birds during the winter months.
The sharp leaves of the holly bush can make it difficult to prune without gloves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mistletoe - plant associated with Christmas, has white berries
