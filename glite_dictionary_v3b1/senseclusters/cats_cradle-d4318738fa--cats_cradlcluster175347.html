cat's cradle

noun
string game using fingers to make shapes
Pronunciation: / kæts ˈkreɪdəl / (AmE), / kæts ˈkreɪdəl / (BrE)
a children's game where a loop of string is twisted and passed between players' fingers to make shapes; also, a complicated or tangled situation (metaphorically)
