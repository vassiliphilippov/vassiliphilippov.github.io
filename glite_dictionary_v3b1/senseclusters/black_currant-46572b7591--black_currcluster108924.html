black currant

noun
edible aromatic black berry
7.4
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈkɝː.ənt / (AmE), / blæk ˈkʌr.ənt / (BrE)
a small, dark purple to black berry from the Ribes nigrum shrub, known for its aromatic flavor and commonly used in jams, desserts, and drinks

Examples

A tart filled with black currant is a popular dessert in many European countries.

The flavor of black currant is both sweet and slightly tart.
