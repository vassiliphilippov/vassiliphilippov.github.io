for Pete's sake

interjection
expression of frustration or urgency
Pronunciation: / fər pits seɪk / (AmE), / fə piːts seɪk / (BrE)
used to express annoyance, impatience, or urgency.

Examples

For Pete's sake stop
For Pete's sake, don't leave the door open all night!
For Pete's sake, will you answer your phone?
Could you please be quiet, for Pete's sake?

Related Terms

  • synonym: for God's sake - expression of pleading or frustration, more direct/blunt than "for Pete's sake"
  • synonym: for heaven's sake - expression of exasperation or urgency
