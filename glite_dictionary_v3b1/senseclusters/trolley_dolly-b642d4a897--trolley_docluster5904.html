trolley dolly

noun
informal: flight attendant
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑli ˈdɑli / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli ˈdɒli / (BrE)
an informal term for a flight attendant, especially one who serves food and drinks on an aircraft
