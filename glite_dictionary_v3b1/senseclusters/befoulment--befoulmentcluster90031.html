befoulment

noun
state of being polluted or dirty
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈfaʊlmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈfaʊlmənt / (BrE)
The state or condition of being made dirty or polluted.

Examples

The befoulment of water
Decades of industrial waste led to the befoulment of the lake's once-clear waters.
The befoulment of the river alarmed both locals and environmentalists.
They began the difficult task of reversing years of befoulment caused by neglect and pollution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filth - extreme dirt or foulness
  • synonym: pollution - the introduction of harmful substances or products into the environment
  • antonym: purification - the process of making something pure
