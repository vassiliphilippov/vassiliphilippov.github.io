  • downplay

    downplay makes an event sound less serious, while deemphasize can mean simply to give it less prominence.
  • play down
    4.3 0.83/m
    play down uses informal phrasing to make something seem less important, while deemphasize is more neutral or formal.
  • minimize

  • trivialize
