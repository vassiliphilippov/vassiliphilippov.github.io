evangelistically

adverb
in an evangelistic way
8.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.væn.dʒə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /ɪ.væn.dʒə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /iː.væn.dʒə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a manner relating to Christian evangelism, especially with the aim of converting others to the Christian faith

Examples

To preach evangelistically
She spoke evangelistically about her faith, hoping to inspire others to join her church.
The group acted evangelistically, organizing events to share their beliefs with the community.
He wrote evangelistically in his blog, encouraging readers to explore Christianity.
