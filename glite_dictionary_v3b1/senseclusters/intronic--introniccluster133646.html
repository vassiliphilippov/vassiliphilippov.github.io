intronic

adjective
within a gene intron
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ɪnˈtrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or located within an intron of a gene

Examples

Intronic mutation
The scientist identified an intronic mutation that affected gene expression.
Some genetic disorders are caused by changes in intronic regions rather than exons.
Researchers studied the intronic sequences to understand their role in gene regulation.
