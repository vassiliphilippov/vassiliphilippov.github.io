clothesless

adjective
not wearing any clothes
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊðzləs / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðzləs / (BrE)
not wearing any clothes; without clothing.

Examples

Clothesless person
He found himself accidentally clothesless when the wind blew his towel away.
In the dream, she was completely clothesless in front of a crowd.
The artist painted a clothesless figure sitting by the window.
