groundnut

noun
nutlike tuber; edible plant root
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd.nʌt / (BrE)
a North American plant (Apios americana) or its edible tuber, traditionally used as food by Native Americans
