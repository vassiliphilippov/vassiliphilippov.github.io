diverter

noun
something that distracts
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɜrtər / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes attention or interest to move away from something else

Examples

Event diverter
The comedian acted as a diverter to lighten the mood during the serious event.
A sudden loud noise served as a diverter from the awkward silence in the room.
In some machines, a diverter redirects the flow of materials to different paths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distraction - something that draws attention away
  • antonym: focus - the center of interest or activity
