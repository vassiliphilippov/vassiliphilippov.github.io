gothicism

noun
Gothic style or influence
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the style, characteristics, or influence of Gothic art, architecture, or literature, especially marked by mystery or the supernatural

Examples

Gothic gothicism style
The old castle's architecture is a perfect example of gothicism with its pointed arches and dark atmosphere.
Many horror novels draw on gothicism to create a sense of mystery and suspense.
The film's visual style is heavily influenced by gothicism, featuring gloomy settings and dramatic shadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gothic - relating to the Gothic style in art, architecture, or literature
  • degree lower: romanticism - an artistic and literary movement emphasizing emotion and imagination
