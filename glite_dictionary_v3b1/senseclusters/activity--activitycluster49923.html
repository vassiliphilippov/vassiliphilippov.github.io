(computing, online services) a recorded list or log of a user’s actions on a device, website, or application, kept for analytics, auditing, or security.

Examples

Login activity
The security team reviewed the user's login activity to identify suspicious patterns.
Our dashboard shows recent activity so administrators can spot failed logins or unusual behavior.
After the breach they examined server activity and traced the attacker’s steps through timestamps.
