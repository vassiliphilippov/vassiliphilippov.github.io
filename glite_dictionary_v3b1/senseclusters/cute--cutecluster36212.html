cute

adjective
artificially charming or affected
Pronunciation: / kjut / (AmE), / kjuːt / (BrE)
(of behaviour or style) deliberately or insincerely designed to be charming or appealing, often in a way that seems artificial or affected.

Examples

Cute remarks
Some critics found the film's dialogue cute rather than genuinely funny.
The ad's cute tone put off viewers who preferred something more sincere.
The singer's cute gestures on stage felt forced to many in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precious - affectedly or overly charming in a way that seems contrived
