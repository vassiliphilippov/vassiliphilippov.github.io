fractional

adjective
constituting a part or fraction of a whole
4.7 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfræk.ʃə.nəl/ (AmE), /ˈfræk.ʃə.nəl/ (BrE)
Constituting or comprising only a part or fraction of a whole; relating to or expressed as a fraction.

Examples

Fractional share
He received only a fractional share of the inheritance compared to his siblings.
The patient experienced only a fractional improvement in her symptoms after the medication.
There was a fractional difference in the prices between the two stores.
