dream

noun
mental images during sleep
Pronunciation: / dɹim / (AmE), / dɹiːm / (BrE)
a series of thoughts, images, or emotions occurring in a person's mind during sleep

Examples

A strange dream
He often remembers his dreams in vivid detail.
Last night, I had a strange dream about flying over the ocean.
Suddenly, she woke up from a terrifying dream and couldn't fall back asleep.
