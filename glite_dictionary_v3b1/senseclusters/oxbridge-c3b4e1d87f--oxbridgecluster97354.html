Oxbridge

noun
collective term for Oxford and Cambridge Universities
Pronunciation: / ɑksˈbrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒksˈbrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a general term for the universities of Oxford and Cambridge, regarded collectively as ancient, prestigious, and privileged institutions

Examples

Oxbridge universities
Her dream was to study at Oxbridge after finishing high school.
Many Oxbridge graduates have gone on to become influential leaders.
The traditions at Oxbridge are centuries old and highly respected.
