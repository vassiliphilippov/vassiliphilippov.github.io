vegetarianism

noun
diet excluding meat and fish
2.9 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛdʒəˈtɛriəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌvɛdʒɪˈteəriənɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice of not eating meat or fish, especially for moral, religious, or health reasons

Examples

Vegetarianism practice
His commitment to vegetarianism began after he learned more about animal welfare.
She chose vegetarianism as a way to improve her health and reduce her environmental impact.
There are many different reasons people practice vegetarianism, including ethics and religion.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: veganism - the practice of abstaining from all animal products
