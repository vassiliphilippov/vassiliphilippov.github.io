joyousness

noun
the state of being very joyful
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪəsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being full of joy; great happiness

Examples

Pure joyousness
Children often express pure joyousness during the holiday season.
Her face lit up with joyousness when she heard the good news.
The joyousness of the wedding celebration was felt by everyone in attendance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gladness - the state of being glad; pleasure or happiness
  • synonym: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • antonym: sorrow - a feeling of deep distress caused by loss or disappointment
