forefoot

noun
front part of foot
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌfʊt / (BrE)
the front part of the foot, including the toes and the ball of the foot

Examples

Blisters on the forefoot
The runner developed blisters on her forefoot after the marathon.
Proper shoes can help support the forefoot during long walks.
He injured his forefoot while playing soccer on the hard field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hindfoot - the back part of the foot, including the heel
  • synonym: toes - the digits at the front of the foot
