fall back

verb
return to previous bad behavior
1.0
Pronunciation: / fɔl ˈbæk / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈbæk / (BrE)
to return to previous bad habits or behavior, especially after an attempt to change or improve

Examples

Fall back into habits
After months of sobriety, he began to fall back into old drinking habits.
It's easy to fall back into unhealthy routines during stressful times.
She promised to change but eventually fell back into lying to her friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relapse - to fall back into a former state, especially a bad one
