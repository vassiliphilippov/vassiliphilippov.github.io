lightspeed

noun
extremely fast speed
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtspiːd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtspiːd / (BrE)
an extremely fast rate of movement or progress, often used informally to mean very quickly

Examples

The new software update made the computer run at lightspeed compared to before.
She finished her homework at lightspeed so she could go out with her friends.
Rumors spread through the office at lightspeed after the announcement was made.
