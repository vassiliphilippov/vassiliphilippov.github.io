cringing

adjective
excessively submissive
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
showing excessive or abject submission; lacking self-respect in fear or in trying to please others.

Examples

Cringing fear
Her cringing manner made it clear she feared her boss's anger.
I can't stand his cringing attitude toward anyone in power.
The cringing apology came across as insincere and desperate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assertive - having or showing a confident and forceful personality
  • synonym: submissive - ready to conform to the authority or will of others; meekly obedient
