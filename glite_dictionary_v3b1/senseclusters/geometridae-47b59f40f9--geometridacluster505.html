Geometridae

noun
family of moths with larvae called measuring worms
Pronunciation: / dʒiːˈɑːmɪtrɪˌdiː / (AmE), / dʒiːˈɒmɪtrɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of moths whose larvae are known as measuring worms due to their characteristic looping movement

Examples

Geometridae moth family
Many members of the Geometridae family display effective camouflage on tree bark.

The larvae of the Geometridae are commonly known as inchworms or measuring worms.
