metaphysically

adverb
in a metaphysical manner
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəli / (BrE)
In a metaphysical manner; relating to the fundamental nature of reality or existence.

Examples

Think metaphysically
He tends to approach every question metaphysically, always seeking the underlying causes.
She argued metaphysically that consciousness cannot be reduced to neural activity alone.
The poet wrote about reality metaphysically, exploring themes beyond the physical world.
