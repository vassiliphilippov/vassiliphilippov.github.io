limicoline bird

noun
wading bird of seashores and estuaries
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪmɪˈkoʊlin bɝd / (AmE), / ˌlɪmɪˈkəʊliːn bɜːd / (BrE)
any of numerous wading birds, such as sandpipers and plovers, that frequent mostly seashores and estuaries

Examples

Many limicoline birds migrate
Many limicoline birds migrate thousands of miles each year to breed along northern seashores.
Researchers observed a variety of limicoline birds probing the mudflats for insects.
The estuary provides a vital habitat for several species of limicoline birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shorebird - bird that frequents shores and mudflats; synonym for limicoline bird
  • synonym: wader - another term for a bird that wades in water, especially in British English
