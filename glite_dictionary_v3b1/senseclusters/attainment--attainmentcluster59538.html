attainment

noun
an ability gained by training or effort
Pronunciation: / əˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / əˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
A skill or ability that someone has developed through training or effort; an achievement.

Examples

A language attainment
Her attainment of fluency in three languages impressed the interview panel.
His professional attainment opened up many new career opportunities.
Musical attainment is often the result of years of dedicated practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accomplishment - something that has been achieved successfully
  • synonym: achievement - a thing done successfully, typically by effort, courage, or skill
