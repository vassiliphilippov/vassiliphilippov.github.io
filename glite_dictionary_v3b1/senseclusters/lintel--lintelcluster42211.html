lintel

noun
horizontal beam above door or window
6.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪn.təl/ (AmE), /ˈlɪn.təl/ (BrE)
A horizontal supporting beam placed above a door or window opening in a building.

Examples

Steel lintels
Modern houses often use steel lintels instead of traditional stone or timber.
The stone lintel above the doorway was intricately carved.
They inspected the wooden lintel for signs of rot before renovating the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: architrave - the main beam resting across the tops of columns, especially in classical architecture
