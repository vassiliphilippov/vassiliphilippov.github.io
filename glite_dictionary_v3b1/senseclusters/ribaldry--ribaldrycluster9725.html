ribaldry

noun
rude or irreverent humor
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.əld.ri /; / ˈrɪbəldri / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.əld.ri /; / ˈrɪbəldri / (BrE)
rude or vulgar talk or behavior that is meant to be funny, especially about sex

Examples

Funny ribaldry stories
His stories were full of ribaldry that shocked some listeners.
Medieval festivals were often occasions for much laughter and ribaldry.
The comedian's act was criticized for relying too heavily on ribaldry.
Despite the ribaldry at the party, everyone seemed to be having a good time.
His stand-up comedy routine was full of ribaldry that some found offensive.
The novel's dialogue contained a surprising amount of ribaldry for its time period.
