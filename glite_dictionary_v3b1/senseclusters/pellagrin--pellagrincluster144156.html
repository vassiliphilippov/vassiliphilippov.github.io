pellagrin

adjective
affected by pellagra
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈlæɡrɪn / (AmE), / pəˈlæɡrɪn / (BrE)
relating to or affected by pellagra, a disease caused by niacin deficiency, often showing symptoms like dermatitis and diarrhea
