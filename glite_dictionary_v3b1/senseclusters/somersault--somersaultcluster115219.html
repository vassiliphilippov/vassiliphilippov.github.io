somersault

noun
acrobatic head-over-heels movement
7.7 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌm.ər.sɔːlt/ /; / /ˈsʌm.ər.sɑːlt/ (AmE), /ˈsʌm.ə.sɔːlt/ /; / /ˈsʌm.ə.sɒlt/ (BrE)
an acrobatic movement in which a person turns head over heels in the air or on the ground, bringing the feet over the head, either forwards or backwards

Examples

A forward somersault
As a child, I loved doing forward somersaults on the lawn.
He lost his balance and nearly did a somersault down the stairs.
The gymnast performed a flawless somersault during her floor routine.
