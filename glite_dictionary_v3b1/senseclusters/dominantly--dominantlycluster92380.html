dominantly

adverb
with most influence
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːmɪnəntli / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows the most power, influence, or is most common or prevalent

Examples

Played dominantly
The team played dominantly throughout the entire match, leaving their opponents little chance to score.
In this region, agriculture is dominantly based on rice cultivation.
The discussion was dominantly led by the most experienced members of the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marginally - to only a small extent; slightly
  • synonym: predominantly - mainly or mostly
