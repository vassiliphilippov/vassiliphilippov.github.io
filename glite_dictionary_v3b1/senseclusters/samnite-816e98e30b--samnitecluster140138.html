Samnite

noun
ancient people of southern Italy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæm.naɪt / (AmE), / ˈsam.naɪt / (BrE)
a member of an ancient Oscan-speaking people from Campania in southern Italy, known for their conflicts with early Rome

Examples

The Samnite people
Archaeologists discovered artifacts that once belonged to the Samnite civilization.
In classical history, the Samnite wars were significant conflicts in early Roman development.
The ancient Samnite warriors are remembered for their resistance against Roman expansion.
