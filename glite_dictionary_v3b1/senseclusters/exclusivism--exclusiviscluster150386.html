exclusivism

noun
policy of exclusion
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskluːsɪˌvɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪkˈskluːsɪˌvɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or policy of deliberately excluding certain people or groups from membership, participation, or access

Examples

Club exclusivism
The club's exclusivism made it difficult for new members to join.
Many criticized the organization for its history of exclusivism toward minority groups.
Efforts to reduce exclusivism have led to more open and diverse communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discrimination - unfair treatment of people based on group membership
  • antonym: inclusivism - the practice or policy of including all people or groups
