experiencer

noun
one who feels or undergoes
7.2
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈspɪr.ɪ.ən.sər/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈspɪə.rɪ.ən.sə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that has or undergoes an experience or is affected by something

Examples

The experiencer of feelings
In the sentence 'The child heard the music,' the experiencer is 'the child.'
Linguists often analyze who the experiencer is in different sentence structures.
The experiencer of pain in this context is the patient, not the doctor.
As an experiencer of the festival, she shared her thoughts with the organizers.
The experiencer of the accident described the events in detail to the police.
Every experiencer brings a unique perspective to the discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
  • synonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity or event
  • synonym: subject - the person or thing performing the action in a sentence
