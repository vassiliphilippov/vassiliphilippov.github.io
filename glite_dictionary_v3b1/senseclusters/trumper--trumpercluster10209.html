trumper

noun
boastful person
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who makes loud or showy boasts

Examples

A loud trumper
Nobody took the trumper at the party seriously.
The office trumper was always exaggerating his accomplishments.
People grew tired of the trumper constantly seeking attention with his stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boaster - someone who talks with excessive pride about themselves
  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast or show off
