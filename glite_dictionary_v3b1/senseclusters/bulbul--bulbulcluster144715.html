bulbul

noun
songbird in Asia; nightingale of Persian poetry
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌbʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʊlbʊl / (BrE)
a songbird native to Asia and Africa, especially one celebrated as a nightingale in Persian poetry for its melodious song

Examples

A bulbul bird
At dawn, a bulbul perched on the balcony and filled the air with song.
Bulbuls are frequently celebrated in Persian literature for their sweet melodies.
The poet compared her voice to that of a bulbul in full song.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightingale - a small European songbird known for its melodious singing; often referenced in poetry
