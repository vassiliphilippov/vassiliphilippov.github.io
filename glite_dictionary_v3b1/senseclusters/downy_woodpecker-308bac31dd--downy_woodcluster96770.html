downy woodpecker

noun
small North American woodpecker species
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊni ˈwʊdˌpɛkər / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni ˈwʊdˌpɛkə / (BrE)
A small North American woodpecker (Picoides pubescens) with black and white plumage and a short bill.

Examples

A downy woodpecker on tree
A downy woodpecker clung to the tree as it pecked for insects.
The downy woodpecker is easily recognized by its small size and white back.
We often see downy woodpeckers visiting the bird feeders in our backyard.
