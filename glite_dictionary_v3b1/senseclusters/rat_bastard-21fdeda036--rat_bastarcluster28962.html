rat bastard

noun
contemptible or despicable person (insult)
Pronunciation: / ræt ˈbæstərd / (AmE), / ræt ˈbɑːstəd / (BrE)
(offensive, vulgar) An insulting term for a person considered to be contemptible or despicable.

Examples

A rat bastard person
After stealing from his own friend, everyone started calling him a rat bastard.
She glared at him, calling him a rat bastard for lying to her.
The coach was furious, yelling that only a rat bastard would cheat during the game.
