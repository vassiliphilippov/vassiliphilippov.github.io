unaffectedly

adverb
naturally, sincerely
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪdli / (BrE)
in a natural and sincere way, without pretending or showing off

Examples

Speak unaffectedly
He greeted everyone unaffectedly, making each guest feel comfortable and welcome.
The author writes unaffectedly about her childhood, sharing honest memories.
Despite her fame, she behaves unaffectedly with everyone she meets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificially - in a way that is not natural or real
  • synonym: genuinely - in a real and sincere way
