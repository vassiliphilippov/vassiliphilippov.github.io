ratty old sweater

noun
worn-out or shabby sweater
3.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈræ.tɪ oʊld ˈswet.ər/ /; / /ˈræ.tɪ əʊld ˈswet.ər/ (AmE), /ˈræ.tɪ əʊld ˈswet.ə/ (BrE)
A sweater that is worn-out and shabby in appearance, often due to age or heavy use.

Examples

A ratty old sweater worn
