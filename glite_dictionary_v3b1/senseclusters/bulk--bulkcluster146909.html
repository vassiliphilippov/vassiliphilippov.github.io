bulk

noun
the main or larger part
Pronunciation: / bʌlk / (AmE), / bʌlk / (BrE)
the larger or main part of something; the majority of something

Examples

The bulk of work
She handled the bulk of the responsibilities during the relocation.
The bulk of the report focuses on financial performance.
The bulk of the students completed the project ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: majority - the greater number or part
