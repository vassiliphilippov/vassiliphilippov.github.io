grump

verb
to complain sulkily
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌmp / (AmE), / ɡrʌmp / (BrE)
to complain or act in a bad-tempered or sulky way

Examples

To grump about chores
He grumped about the cold weather all morning.
She often grumps when things do not go her way.
Instead of helping, he just grumped in the corner and refused to join in.
He started to grump about the long wait at the restaurant.
Instead of helping, she just grumped about the mess in the kitchen.
Stop grumping and try to enjoy the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grouse - to complain or grumble about something
  • synonym: moan - to complain in an unhappy way
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
