frameless

adjective
without a frame
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈfreɪmləs / (BrE)
having no surrounding frame or border

Examples

A frameless shower door
They installed a frameless shower door in the bathroom.
The artist preferred a frameless canvas to give the painting a modern look.
We admired the sleek design of the frameless mirror in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: borderless - without a border or edge
  • antonym: framed - having a surrounding frame or border
