lunge

verb
make a sudden forward movement
Pronunciation: / lʌndʒ / (AmE), / lʌndʒ / (BrE)
to make a sudden forward movement, especially to attack, reach, or seize something

Examples

To lunge forward
During the workout, she lunged across the gym floor to build up her leg strength.
He suddenly lunged at the door when he heard someone screaming outside.
The fencer lunged forward, aiming directly at her opponent's torso.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrust - to push suddenly or violently in a specified direction
