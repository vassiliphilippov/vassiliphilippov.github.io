novellae

noun
plural of novella
Pronunciation: / noʊˈvɛlaɪ / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɛlaɪ / (BrE)
the plural form of novella, referring to short novels or long stories.

Examples

Several novellae published
The author's collection includes several novellae that explore different aspects of human nature.
In her literature class, students were assigned to read two novellae and compare their themes.
Many classic novellae have been adapted into films and stage plays.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: novel - a long work of fiction
  • synonym: novellas - alternative plural form of novella
  • degree lower: short story - a brief work of fiction
