hidden pouch

noun
a concealed compartment or pocket
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdən paʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɪdn paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a concealed compartment or pocket, typically used to securely store valuable or secret items

Examples

A hidden pouch in jacket
She sewed a hidden pouch into her jacket for extra security.
The old bookcase had a hidden pouch behind one of the shelves.
Travelers often use a hidden pouch to keep their passports and money safe.
