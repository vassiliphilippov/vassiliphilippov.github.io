nonadoption

noun
failure to adopt
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈdɑpʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈdɒpʃən / (BrE)
the state or act of not accepting, choosing, or implementing something, such as a policy or idea

Examples

Nonadoption of new rules
The nonadoption of the safety guidelines resulted in several workplace accidents.
Due to the nonadoption of the new technology, the company fell behind its competitors.
The committee discussed the reasons for the nonadoption of the proposed changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adoption - the act of accepting or starting to use something new
  • synonym: rejection - the act of refusing to accept or approve something
