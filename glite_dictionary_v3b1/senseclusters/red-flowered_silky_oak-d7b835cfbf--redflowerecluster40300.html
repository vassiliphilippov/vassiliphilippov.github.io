red-flowered silky oak

noun
Australian shrub with red flowers and silky leaves
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd ˈflaʊɚd ˈsɪlki oʊk / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈflaʊəd ˈsɪlki əʊk / (BrE)
a tall shrub of eastern Australia, with cylindrical clusters of red flowers and pinnatifid leaves that are silky and grey beneath
