Abkhazia

proper noun
region/republic in the Caucasus
9.6
Pronunciation: / æbˈkɑziə / (AmE), / æbˈkɑːziə / (BrE)
a region in the Caucasus recognized by some states as an independent republic but considered by most of the international community as part of Georgia
