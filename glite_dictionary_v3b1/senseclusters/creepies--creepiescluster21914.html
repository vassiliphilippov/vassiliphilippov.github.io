creepies

noun
feeling of unease
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːpiz / (AmE), / ˈkriːpiz / (BrE)
a feeling of unease, fear, or nervous discomfort, often caused by something spooky or unsettling

Examples

The strange noises in the empty building gave everyone the creepies.
I got the creepies walking through the dark forest alone.
