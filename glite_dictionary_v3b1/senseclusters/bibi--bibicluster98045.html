bibi

noun
grandmother (affectionate)
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːbi / (AmE), / ˈbiːbi / (BrE)
an affectionate term for grandmother, especially used in some South Asian families

Examples

Bibi cooks
Every Sunday, we visit my bibi and enjoy her delicious cooking.
My bibi tells the best stories from her childhood in India.
During the festival, all the grandchildren gathered around their bibi for blessings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's father or mother
