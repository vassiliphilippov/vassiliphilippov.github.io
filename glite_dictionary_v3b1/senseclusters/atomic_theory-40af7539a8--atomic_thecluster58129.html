atomic theory

noun
theory that matter is made of atoms
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑmɪk ˈθiəri /; / əˈtɑm.ɪk ˈθɪr.i / (AmE), / əˈtɒmɪk ˈθɪəri /; / əˈtɒm.ɪk ˈθɪə.ri / (BrE)
