busily

adverb
in a busy and active way
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪzɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɪzɪli / (BrE)
in a busy and active way; with a lot of energy or activity

Examples

Children busily building
Children were busily building sandcastles along the beach.
He typed busily on his laptop at the café.
She worked busily at her desk, barely looking up from her papers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actively - in a way that involves energetic or busy activity
  • antonym: idly - in a lazy or inactive way
