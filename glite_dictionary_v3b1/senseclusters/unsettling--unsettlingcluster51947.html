unsettling

adjective
causing instability
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛtlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtlɪŋ / (BrE)
causing something to become physically unstable, dislodged, or out of place

Examples

Unsettling movement
The earthquake had an unsettling effect on the old building's foundation.
After the repairs, there was still an unsettling wobble in the staircase.
The strong winds created an unsettling movement in the tall trees outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dislodging - causing something to be removed from its place
  • antonym: stabilizing - making something stable or steady
