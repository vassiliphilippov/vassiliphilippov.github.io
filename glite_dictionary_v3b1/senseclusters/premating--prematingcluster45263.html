premating

adjective
before mating
Pronunciation: / priːˈmeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / priːˈmeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
occurring or existing before mating takes place, especially in animals or plants

Examples

Premating behavior
Scientists studied the premating rituals of the butterfly species in their natural habitat.
The premating period is crucial for establishing pair bonds among certain birds.
Environmental factors can influence premating behaviors in many animal populations.
