CD

noun
ability cooldown (gaming)
3.2
Pronunciation: /siː diː/ (AmE), /siː diː/ (BrE)
(in video games) a short for 'cooldown': a fixed period after using an ability during which that ability cannot be used again.

Examples

Ability CD
After casting the fireball, she had to wait for the CD before using it again.
In the raid guide, the developer warned about long CDs on the boss's strongest abilities.
Use your defensive spell during the CD to avoid taking heavy damage from the dragon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooldown - a waiting period before an ability or action can be used again
  • synonym: recharge time - the time required for an ability, weapon, or device to become usable again
