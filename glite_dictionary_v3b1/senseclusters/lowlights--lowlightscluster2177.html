lowlights

noun
worst moments
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlaɪts / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlaɪts / (BrE)
the most disappointing or unpleasant parts of an event, period, or experience

Examples

Trip lowlights
The lowlights of the trip included missing our flight and losing our luggage.
Despite some lowlights, the overall experience at the festival was positive.
He focused on the lowlights of the season during his end-of-year review.

Related Terms

  • antonym: highlights - the best or most exciting parts of something
  • synonym: lows - the lowest or most disappointing points in a situation
