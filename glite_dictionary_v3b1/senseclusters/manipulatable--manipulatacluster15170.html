manipulatable

adjective
able to be controlled
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪpjəˌleɪtəbəl / (AmE), / məˈnɪpjʊlətəbl / (BrE)

Examples

Highly manipulatable software

Children can be especially manipulatable if they trust the person influencing them.
The experiment required a manipulatable environment to test different variables.
