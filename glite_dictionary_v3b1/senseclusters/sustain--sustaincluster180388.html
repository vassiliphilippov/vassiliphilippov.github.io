Synonyms

  • maintain
    3.4 14/m
    maintain means keeping something working and supplied over time, while sustain means providing broader support to keep it alive.
  • keep
    1.0 58/m
    keep is caring for someone or an animal with food and shelter, while sustain is more formal and means long-term support.
