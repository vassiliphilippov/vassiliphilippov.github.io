fateful

adjective
having very serious or disastrous consequences
4.8 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfeɪt.fəl/ (AmE), /ˈfeɪt.fəl/ (BrE)
having a very important, often negative or disastrous, effect on the future; decisive or determined by fate
