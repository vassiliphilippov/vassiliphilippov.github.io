frank

noun
type of smoked sausage
7.4
Pronunciation: / fræŋk / (AmE), / fræŋk / (BrE)
A smooth-textured sausage made of minced beef or pork, usually smoked and often served in a bread roll.

Examples

A grilled frank
He grilled several franks for the backyard barbecue.
Would you like a frank with mustard and relish for lunch?
You can buy a frank at the stadium during the game.
