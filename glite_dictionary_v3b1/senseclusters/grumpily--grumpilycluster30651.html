grumpily

adverb
in a bad-tempered or sulky way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌmpɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌmpɪli / (BrE)
In a bad-tempered or sulky manner.

Examples

To answer grumpily
He answered grumpily, clearly not in the mood for a conversation.
She grumpily agreed to come along, although it was clear she didn't want to.
The children grumpily put away their toys when it was time for bed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive manner
  • synonym: crossly - in an angry or annoyed manner
