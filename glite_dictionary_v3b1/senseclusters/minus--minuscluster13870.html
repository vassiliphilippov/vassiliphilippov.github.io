minus

adjective
negative, below standard or zero
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.nəs / (BrE)
on the negative side or below a defined standard or scale, often used with numbers or grades.

Examples

Minus ten degrees
His bank account balance is now minus fifty dollars.
She received a grade of C minus on her essay.
The temperature dropped to minus ten degrees overnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plus - on the positive side or above a defined standard or scale
