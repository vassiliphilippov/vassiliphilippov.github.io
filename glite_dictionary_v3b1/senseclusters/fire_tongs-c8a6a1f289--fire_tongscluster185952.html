fire tongs

noun
tool for handling burning coals
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ tɑŋz / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə tɒŋz / (BrE)
a metal tool with two arms, used for grasping and transferring burning coals in a fireplace or stove

Examples

Fire tongs for coals
Please hand me the fire tongs so I can add another log to the stove.
She used the fire tongs to carefully move the glowing embers in the fireplace.
The antique fire tongs on the hearth were forged in the nineteenth century.
