staidness

noun
serious, dignified quality
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being serious, calm, and dignified

Examples

The staidness of character
Her staidness was well-suited to the formality of the occasion.
His staidness can make him seem unapproachable, but it's just his nature.
The staidness of the ceremony impressed the visiting dignitaries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liveliness - the quality of being full of energy or excitement
  • synonym: sobriety - seriousness and lack of excess
