tonneau

noun
open car compartment
Pronunciation: / təˈnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɒnəʊ / (BrE)
an open rear passenger compartment of an automobile, especially in older or vintage cars

Examples

Vintage car tonneau
The couple enjoyed the parade from the tonneau of a beautifully restored antique car.
He carefully polished the wooden trim around the tonneau before the car show.
Vintage cars with an open tonneau are popular among classic automobile enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convertible - a car with a roof that can be folded down or removed
  • antonym: sedan - a car with a closed body and a separate trunk
