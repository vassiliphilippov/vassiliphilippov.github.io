untappable

adjective
not touch-responsive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæpəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæpəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be activated, selected, or responded to by tapping on a touchscreen or touch interface

Examples

Untappable button
After the update, the submit button was untappable and users could not complete the form.
If a menu item is untappable, it usually means the feature is not available yet.
The icon appeared on the screen but remained untappable no matter how many times I tried.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disabled - turned off or not functioning in a system
  • synonym: inactive - not currently working or able to be used
  • antonym: tappable - able to be activated or selected by tapping on a touchscreen
