grippingly

adverb
in a captivating way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that holds your attention very strongly or is extremely exciting

Examples

Grippingly told story
The novel was grippingly written, making it impossible to put down.
She described the accident grippingly, and everyone listened in silence.
The movie unfolds grippingly from the very first scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boringly - in a way that causes boredom or lack of interest
  • synonym: captivatingly - in a way that attracts and holds interest
