frontoparietal

noun
brain region
Pronunciation: / ˌfrʌntoʊpəˈraɪətəl / (AmE), / ˌfrʌntəʊpəˈraɪətəl / (BrE)
the region of the brain that includes parts of both the frontal and parietal lobes

Examples

Frontoparietal region injury
Damage to the frontoparietal can cause difficulties with attention and spatial awareness.
Researchers studied the frontoparietal to understand its role in cognitive control.
The MRI scan revealed a lesion in the patient's frontoparietal region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontal lobe - the front part of the brain involved in decision making and movement
  • synonym: parietal lobe - the part of the brain involved in processing sensory information
