toepiece

noun
front ski binding part
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌpis / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌpiːs / (BrE)
the front part of a shoe or ski binding that covers or secures the toe

Examples

Ski toepiece adjustment
Before stepping into your skis, check that the toepiece is free of snow and ice.
The technician adjusted the toepiece to fit the new ski boots properly.
A damaged toepiece can make it unsafe to ski down the mountain.
Before putting on your skis, check that the toepiece is clean and free of snow.
The new hiking boots have a reinforced toepiece for extra protection.
If the toepiece is damaged, your ski binding may not hold your foot securely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heelpiece - the rear part of a ski binding that holds the heel of the boot
  • synonym: toe cap - a protective covering for the front part of a shoe
