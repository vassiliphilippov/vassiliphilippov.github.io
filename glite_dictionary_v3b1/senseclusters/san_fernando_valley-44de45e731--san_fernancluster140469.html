San Fernando Valley

proper noun
valley and urban area north of Los Angeles
Pronunciation: / sæn fərˈnændoʊ ˈvæli / (AmE), / san fəːˈnandəʊ ˈvali / (BrE)
a fertile valley and metropolitan region in southern California, north of Los Angeles, known for its residential communities and film industry presence

Examples

The San Fernando Valley region
Many celebrities choose to live in the San Fernando Valley for its privacy and proximity to studios.
Our family moved from downtown L.A. to the San Fernando Valley when I was a child.
The summer temperatures in the San Fernando Valley can be much higher than in coastal Los Angeles.
