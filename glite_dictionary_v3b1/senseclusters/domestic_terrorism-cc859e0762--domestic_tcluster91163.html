domestic terrorism

noun
terrorism within one's own country
4.7 5.7
Pronunciation: /də.ˈmes.tɪk ˈter.ə.rɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /də.ˈmes.tɪk ˈter.ə.rɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the use of violent acts to intimidate or coerce a population or government within one's own country, typically for political or ideological reasons

Examples

Domestic terrorism acts
He was charged with acts of domestic terrorism under the new national laws.
Public awareness campaigns aimed to educate citizens about the warning signs of domestic terrorism.
The government increased security measures to prevent domestic terrorism after the recent attacks.

Related Terms
