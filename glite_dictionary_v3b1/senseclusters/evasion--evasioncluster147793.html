evasion

noun
bypassing digital security/detection

(in computing, especially cybersecurity) the act or techniques of bypassing or defeating digital security or detection systems so malicious activity is not discovered.

Examples

Security evasion
Advanced malware uses evasion techniques to bypass detection and remain hidden from scanners.
The incident response team analysed the sample's evasion methods to improve their rules.
Researchers published a report on machine-learning approaches to detect malware evasion in cloud systems.

Synonyms

    8.2 0.18/m

  • antonym: detection - the act of discovering or identifying malicious activity, threats, or security breaches
  • synonym: obfuscation - making code or behaviour unclear to hide malicious intent; often used to evade analysis
