apocopate

verb
to shorten by dropping ending
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːkəˌpeɪt / (AmE), / əˈpɒkəˌpeɪt / (BrE)
to shorten a word by removing its final sound or syllable

Examples

To apocopate a word
Some English dialects often apocopate words, such as saying 'goin'' instead of 'going'.
Linguists study how speakers apocopate words in casual conversation.
The word 'photo' is an example of a term that has been apocopated from 'photograph'.
