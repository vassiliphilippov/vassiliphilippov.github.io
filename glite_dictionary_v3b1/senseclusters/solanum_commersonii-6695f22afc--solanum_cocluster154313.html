Solanum commersonii

noun
South American wild potato species
Pronunciation: / soʊˈlænəm ˌkoʊmərˈsoʊniː / (AmE), / səˈleɪnəm ˌkɒməsəʊˈnaɪ / (BrE)
A species of wild potato native to South America, often used in breeding programs for cultivated potatoes due to its disease resistance.
