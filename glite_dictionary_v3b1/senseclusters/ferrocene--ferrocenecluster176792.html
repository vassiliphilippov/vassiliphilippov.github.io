ferrocene

noun
iron sandwich compound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrəˌsiːn / (AmE), / ˈfɛrəˌsiːn / (BrE)
a chemical compound consisting of an iron atom sandwiched between two cyclopentadienyl rings
