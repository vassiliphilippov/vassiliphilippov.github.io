rerack

verb
to operate firearm slide again
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈræk / (AmE), / ˌriːˈræk / (BrE)
to pull back and release the slide or bolt of a firearm again, especially to chamber a round or clear a malfunction

Examples

To rerack a pistol
After the misfire, she had to rerack the pistol's slide before firing again.
If your rifle does not fire, you may need to rerack it to clear the chamber.
He quickly reracked the shotgun to prepare for the next shot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jam - a malfunction that prevents a firearm from operating
  • synonym: reload - to load a firearm with ammunition again
