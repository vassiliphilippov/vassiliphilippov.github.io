hophead

noun
beer enthusiast
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑpˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒpˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is very enthusiastic about beers with a strong hop flavor, especially craft beers like IPAs

Examples

Craft hophead
As a proud hophead, she always seeks out the newest and most bitter IPAs at local breweries.
The festival attracted many hopheads eager to sample rare, hoppy beers from around the world.
If you are not a hophead, you might find these beers too bitter for your taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
