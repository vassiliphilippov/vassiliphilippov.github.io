paginate

verb
number the pages (a book, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈpædʒəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈpædʒɪneɪt / (BrE)
to number the pages of a book or manuscript

Examples

To paginate a book
After formatting the text, she carefully paginated each chapter.
The editor asked the assistant to paginate the final manuscript before printing.
You need to paginate these documents so readers can reference specific sections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pagination - the act or process of numbering pages
