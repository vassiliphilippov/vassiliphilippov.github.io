cast-off

adjective
no longer wanted; thrown away
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæst ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɑːst ɒf / (BrE)
no longer wanted or needed and therefore discarded; thrown away

Examples

Cast-off clothes
Many people in the village wear cast-off clothing from their relatives.
She donated her son's cast-off toys to charity.
The artist created sculptures from cast-off pieces of metal and wood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discarded - thrown away, no longer wanted
  • antonym: retained - kept, held onto, not thrown away
