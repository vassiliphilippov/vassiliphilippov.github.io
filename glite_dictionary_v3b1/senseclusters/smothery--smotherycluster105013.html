smothery

adjective
stifling or suffocating
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsmʌð.ər.ɪ/ /; / /ˈsmʌð.rɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsmʌð.ə.rɪ/ /; / /ˈsmʌð.rɪ/ (BrE)
having a quality that causes or feels like being smothered, often making breathing difficult or uncomfortable

Examples

She opened the window to escape the smothery feeling in the small room.
After hours in the smothery tent, they were relieved to step outside into the fresh air.
