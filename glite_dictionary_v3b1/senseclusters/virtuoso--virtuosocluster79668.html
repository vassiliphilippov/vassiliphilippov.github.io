virtuoso

noun
exceptionally skilled musician
Pronunciation: / ˌvɝː.tʃuˈoʊ.soʊ / (AmE), / ˌvɜː.tjuˈəʊ.səʊ / (BrE)
a person with exceptional skill or mastery, especially in music or the arts

Examples

A piano virtuoso
Critics called her the greatest piano virtuoso of her generation.
Many aspiring musicians dream of becoming a virtuoso in their instrument.
The virtuoso captivated the audience with a breathtaking violin solo.
As a culinary virtuoso, she could transform simple ingredients into gourmet dishes.
His reputation as a virtuoso extended well beyond the world of classical guitar.
The young pianist was quickly recognized as a virtuoso by critics around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maestro - a distinguished conductor or musician
  • synonym: master - a person who has complete control or skill in a subject
  • antonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a field
