gallery-goer

noun
art gallery visitor
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləri ˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈɡæləri ˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly visits or attends art galleries

Examples

Art gallery-goer
As a passionate gallery-goer, she never missed a new exhibition in the city.
The gallery-goer spent hours admiring the contemporary sculptures on display.
Many gallery-goers attended the opening night to see the artist's latest work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: museum-goer - a person who regularly visits museums
