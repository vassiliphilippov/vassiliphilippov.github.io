nonrealistic

adjective
impractical or unlikely
6.3
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /nɑːn.rɪə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /nɒn.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)

Examples

Nonrealistic expectations

Setting nonrealistic goals can lead to disappointment and frustration.
