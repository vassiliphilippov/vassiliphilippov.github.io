instrument

verb
equip with measuring or control devices
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
to equip (a device, machine, or environment) with instruments for measuring, recording, or controlling.

Examples

To instrument a bridge
Several bridges have been instrumented to detect stress changes in real time.
The laboratory team plans to instrument the reactor to collect continuous safety data.
The researchers instrumented the vehicle with sensors to monitor performance during the test.
