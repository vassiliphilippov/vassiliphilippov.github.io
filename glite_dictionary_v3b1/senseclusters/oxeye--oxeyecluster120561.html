oxeye

noun
shrubby herb with yellow daisy-like flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkˌsaɪ /; / ˈɑkˌsaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒkˌsaɪ /; / ˈɒksaɪ / (BrE)
a plant with large yellow or white daisy-like flowers, especially of the genera Heliopsis or Leucanthemum
