shrug

verb
raise shoulders to show indifference or uncertainty
2.4 1.9
Pronunciation: /ʃrʌg/ (AmE), /ʃrʌg/ (BrE)
to raise and lower one's shoulders slightly to show that one does not know something or does not care

Examples

To shrug your shoulders

She shrugged her shoulders, unsure of how to respond to the question.
When asked about the loss, he simply shrugged and walked away.
