goosebumps

noun
skin reaction to cold or fear
Pronunciation: / ˈɡusˌbʌmps / (AmE), / ˈɡuːsbʌmps / (BrE)
small raised bumps on the skin that appear when you are cold, afraid, or excited

Examples

Goosebumps on skin
The horror movie gave me goosebumps from start to finish.
She felt goosebumps on her arms as the cold wind blew through the window.
His powerful singing sent goosebumps down my spine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gooseflesh - the same skin reaction as goosebumps
