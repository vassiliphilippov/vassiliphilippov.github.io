hydrofrack

verb
to extract oil with water
9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.drə.fræk/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.droʊ.fræk/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.drəʊ.fræk/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.drə.fræk/ (BrE)
to extract oil or natural gas from underground rock by injecting high-pressure water and chemicals to fracture the rock.

Examples

To hydrofrack rock
The company plans to hydrofrack several new sites in the coming year.
Many environmental groups oppose the decision to hydrofrack near protected water sources.
After they hydrofracked the well, natural gas production increased significantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - to make a hole in the ground to extract oil or gas
  • synonym: frack - to extract oil or gas by fracturing rock with high-pressure fluid
