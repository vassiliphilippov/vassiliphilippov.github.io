comedogenic substance

noun
substance likely to cause acne
8.8 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊməˈdoʊdʒənɪk ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˌkəʊməˈdəʊdʒənɪk ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
A substance that is likely to cause the formation of acne by clogging pores in the skin.

Examples

A comedogenic substance in cream
Always check product labels to see if they contain a comedogenic substance before use.
Dermatologists warn that using a comedogenic substance can worsen acne-prone skin.
She avoided any comedogenic substance in her skincare routine to prevent breakouts.
