streakiness

noun
inconsistent performance
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstriː.kɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈstriː.ki.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈstriː.kɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈstriː.ki.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of showing inconsistent performance, with periods of success or failure occurring in short runs.

Examples

Team's streakiness in games
The basketball team's streakiness made it hard for fans to know what to expect each game.
Her streakiness at work meant she sometimes excelled but often missed important deadlines.
Analysts discussed the streakiness of the stock market during periods of rapid change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consistency - the quality of always behaving or performing in a similar way
  • synonym: unpredictability - the state of being difficult to predict or foresee
