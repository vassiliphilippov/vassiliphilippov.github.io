periodic breathing

noun
alternating shallow and deep breathing
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪriˈɑdɪk ˈbriðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪk ˈbriːðɪŋ / (BrE)
An abnormal respiratory pattern characterized by alternating periods of shallow and deep breathing.

Examples

Periodic breathing pattern
Periodic breathing is commonly observed in premature infants in neonatal care.
Doctors noticed periodic breathing in the patient during overnight monitoring.
The nurse became concerned when she detected a pattern of periodic breathing during the exam.
