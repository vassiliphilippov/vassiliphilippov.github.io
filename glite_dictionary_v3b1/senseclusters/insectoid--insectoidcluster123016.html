insectoid

noun
insect-like creature
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛk.tɔɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛk.tɔɪd / (BrE)
a creature or being that resembles an insect, especially in science fiction or fantasy contexts

Examples

A giant insectoid
The explorers were startled by a giant insectoid lurking in the shadows of the cave.
In the movie, the alien race was depicted as highly intelligent insectoids with complex societies.
The scientist studied the anatomy of the mysterious insectoid found deep in the rainforest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alien - a being from another planet, often imagined in science fiction
  • synonym: arthropod - an invertebrate animal with an exoskeleton and jointed legs
