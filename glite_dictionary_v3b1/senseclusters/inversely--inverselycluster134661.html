inversely

adverb
in an opposite or reverse way
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɜrsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
In an inverse or opposite manner; so that as one amount increases, another decreases, or vice versa.

Examples

Move inversely related
If demand increases, price tends to move inversely.
In physics, pressure and volume are often related inversely.
The speed of the car is inversely proportional to the travel time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reciprocally - in a mutual or inverse manner
