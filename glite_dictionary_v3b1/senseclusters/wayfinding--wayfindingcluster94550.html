wayfinding

adjective
navigation-related design
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪˌfaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪˌfaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
describing signs, systems, or design features that help people navigate or find their way in a place

Examples

Wayfinding signs
The airport installed new wayfinding signs to help travelers reach their gates more easily.
Good wayfinding design in a museum ensures that visitors do not get lost.
Hospitals often use color-coded wayfinding systems to guide patients to different departments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confusing - difficult to understand or follow
  • synonym: directional - providing guidance or indicating direction
