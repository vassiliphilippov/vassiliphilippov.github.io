canopying

noun
treetop adventure activity
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkæn.ə.pɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkæn.ə.pɪŋ/ (BrE)
the activity of moving through the treetops using platforms, ropes, or zip-lines, often as part of a canopy tour

Examples

Canopying on zip-lines

Many tourists try canopying for the thrill of gliding between platforms high in the forest.
Safety equipment is essential when canopying to ensure a fun and secure experience.
