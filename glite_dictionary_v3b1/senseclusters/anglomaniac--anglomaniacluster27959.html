anglomaniac

adjective
excessively admiring England
9.9
Pronunciation: /æŋ.gloʊ.ˈmeɪ.nɪ.æk/ /; / /æŋ.gloʊ.mə.ˈmeɪ.nɪ.æk/ (AmE), /æŋ.gləʊ.ˈmeɪ.nɪ.æk/ /; / /æŋ.gləʊ.mə.ˈmeɪ.nɪ.æk/ (BrE)
showing an excessive admiration for England, English people, or English culture and customs

Examples

Anglomaniac passion
His anglomaniac enthusiasm was clear from his collection of British novels and music.
The decor in her apartment reflected her anglomaniac taste, with London posters and English teapots everywhere.
Some considered his anglomaniac attitude excessive, but he simply loved all things British.
