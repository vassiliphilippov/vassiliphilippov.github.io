unmuffled

adjective
not softened or restrained
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmʌfəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌfəld / (BrE)
not made quieter, softer, or less intense; not muffled or muted (especially of sound or expression)

Examples

The unmuffled roar of the motorcycle startled everyone nearby.
He spoke in an unmuffled voice that carried across the room.
The unmuffled alarm could be heard from several blocks away.
