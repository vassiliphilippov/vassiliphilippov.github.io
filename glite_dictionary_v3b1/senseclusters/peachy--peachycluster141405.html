peachy

adjective
resembling a peach in color
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃi / (BrE)
Having a color similar to that of a ripe peach; soft pinkish-orange.

Examples

Peachy color dress
She chose a dress in a soft peachy hue for the summer wedding.
The morning sky glowed with peachy tones as the sun began to rise.
The walls were painted a lovely peachy shade that brightened the room.
