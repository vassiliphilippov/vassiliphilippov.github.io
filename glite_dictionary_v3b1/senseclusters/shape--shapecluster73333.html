shape

noun
form representing a concept
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a concrete or visible form that represents an abstract idea or concept

Examples

Shape of an idea
In her novel, the recurring shape of a key represented freedom for the main character.
The artist used the triangle as a shape to convey strength and stability.
The logo's circular shape serves as a symbol of unity and wholeness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: symbol - a thing that represents or stands for something else, especially an abstract concept
