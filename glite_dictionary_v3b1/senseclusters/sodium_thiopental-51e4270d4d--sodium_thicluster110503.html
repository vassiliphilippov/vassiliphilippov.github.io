sodium thiopental

noun
barbiturate sedative and anesthetic
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.dɪ.əm θaɪ.ə.ˈpen.təl/ /; / /ˈsoʊ.dɪ.əm θaɪ.oʊ.ˈpen.təl/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.dɪ.əm θaɪ.əʊ.ˈpen.təl/ (BrE)

Examples

Sodium thiopental injection
