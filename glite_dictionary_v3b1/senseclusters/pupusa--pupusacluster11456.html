pupusa

noun
Salvadoran stuffed tortilla
Pronunciation: / puˈpusə / (AmE), / puˈpuːsə / (BrE)
a thick, handmade corn tortilla from El Salvador, usually filled with beans, cheese, or meat

Examples

A pupusa with cheese
We enjoyed delicious pupusas filled with cheese and beans at the local Salvadoran restaurant.
She learned how to make traditional pupusas from her grandmother during the holidays.
The food festival featured a stand selling freshly made pupusas with a variety of fillings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arepa - a round, flat cornmeal cake from South America, often split and filled
  • synonym: taco - a Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various mixtures
