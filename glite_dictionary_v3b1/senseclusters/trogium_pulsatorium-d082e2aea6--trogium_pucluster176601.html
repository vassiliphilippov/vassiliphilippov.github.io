Trogium pulsatorium

noun
species of booklouse insect
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊdʒiəm ˌpʌlˈsætɔːriəm / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊdʒiəm ˌpʌlˈsətɔːriəm / (BrE)
A species of small wingless insect commonly known as the booklouse, found in old books, stored products, and damp environments.
