denominator

noun
the divisor in a fraction
3.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnɑm.əˌneɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈnɒm.ɪ.neɪ.tər / (BrE)
the number below the line in a fraction, which shows how many equal parts the whole is divided into

Examples

The denominator in a fraction
