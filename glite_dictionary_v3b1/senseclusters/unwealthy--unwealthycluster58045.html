unwealthy

noun
people without wealth
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛlθi / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlθi / (BrE)
people who do not have much money or wealth; the poor.

Examples

Help the unwealthy
Charities provide support for the unwealthy in the community during winter months.
The government introduced new programs to help the unwealthy find affordable housing.
Many organizations focus on improving education opportunities for the unwealthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poor - people who have little money or resources
  • antonym: wealthy - people who have a lot of money or resources
