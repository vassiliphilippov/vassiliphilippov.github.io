acrocephaly

noun
cone-shaped abnormality of the skull
Pronunciation: / ˌækrəˈsɛfəli / (AmE), / ˌakrəˈsɛfəli / (BrE)
A congenital condition in which the top of the skull is abnormally cone-shaped.

Examples

Abnormal acrocephaly shape
Research has linked certain genetic mutations to the development of acrocephaly.
The pediatrician diagnosed the infant with acrocephaly after observing the unusual shape of his skull.
Treatment options for acrocephaly may include surgery to correct the shape of the skull.
