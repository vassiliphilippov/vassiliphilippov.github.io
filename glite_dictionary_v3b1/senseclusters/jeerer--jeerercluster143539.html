jeerer

noun
someone who mocks or ridicules others
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪrər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪərə / (BrE)
a person who mocks or ridicules others, often in a contemptuous or derisive way

The jeerer stood at the edge of the crowd, shouting insults at the performers.
