emotional tampon

noun
person who absorbs others' emotional burdens
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənl ˈtæmˌpɑn / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl ˈtæm.pɒn / (BrE)
(informal, slang) A person who regularly absorbs the emotional burdens or problems of others, often without reciprocation or concern for their own well-being.

Examples

An emotional tampon person
After a while, she realized she was just his emotional tampon and nothing more.
Being an emotional tampon for friends can leave you drained if you're not careful.
He was tired of being her emotional tampon every time she had a bad day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotional labor - the effort of managing emotions for others, often at personal cost
  • synonym: sounding board - someone who listens sympathetically to others' problems or ideas
