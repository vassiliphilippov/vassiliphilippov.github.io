stopper

noun
object that tightly blocks a hole
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpər / (AmE), / ˈstɒpə / (BrE)
an object used to block or seal a hole, especially in a bottle or container, to prevent liquid or gas from escaping

Examples

A bottle stopper
Make sure the stopper fits tightly to prevent any leaks.
She pulled out the stopper and poured herself a glass of water.
The bottle had a cork stopper to keep the wine fresh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cap - a cover to close the top of a container
  • synonym: plug - an object used to close off a hole or opening, especially for preventing passage of fluid
