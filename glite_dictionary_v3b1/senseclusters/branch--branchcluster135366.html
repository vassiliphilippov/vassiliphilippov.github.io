branch

noun
a part growing from the main stem of a plant or tree
1.0
Pronunciation: / bræntʃ / (AmE), / brɑːntʃ / (BrE)
a part of a tree or plant that grows out from the main stem or trunk

Examples

Tree branch
A bird built its nest at the end of a branch high in the oak tree.
After the storm, several branches were scattered across the lawn.
The children climbed out onto a branch to pick some apples.
A bird landed on a low branch and began to sing.
She sketched a tree with every branch carefully detailed.
The wind snapped the branch off the old oak tree.
