overtip

verb
to tip over
5.8
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈtɪp/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈtɪp/ (BrE)
to tilt or cause something to tilt so far that it falls over or loses balance

Examples

To overtip a chair
Be careful not to overtip the ladder when reaching for the top shelf.
A sudden push from the wind could easily overtip the empty trash bin.
If you stack the boxes too high, they might overtip and fall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overturn - to turn something upside down or cause it to fall over
  • antonym: upright - to set or keep something in a vertical position
