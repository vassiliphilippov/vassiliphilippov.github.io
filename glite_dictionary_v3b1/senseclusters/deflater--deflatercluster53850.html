deflater

noun
one who discourages
7.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfleɪ.tər/ /; / /də.ˈfleɪ.tər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfleɪ.tə/ /; / /də.ˈfleɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that reduces the enthusiasm, pride, or confidence of others

Examples

A deflater of pride
Her negative comments made her the deflater of the team's excitement before the big game.
Every group has a deflater who always points out what could go wrong.
Instead of being a deflater, try to support your friends' ambitions.
