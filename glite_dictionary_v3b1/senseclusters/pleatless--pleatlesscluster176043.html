pleatless

adjective
without pleats
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈplitləs / (AmE), / ˈpliːtləs / (BrE)
having no pleats; smooth and without any sewn folds

Examples

pleatless skirt
She preferred pleatless skirts because they looked more modern and simple.
The designer showcased a pleatless dress that flowed smoothly to the floor.
Many business suits now feature pleatless trousers for a cleaner silhouette.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - simple and without decoration or folds
  • antonym: pleated - having pleats or sewn folds
