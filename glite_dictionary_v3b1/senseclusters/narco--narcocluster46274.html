narco

adjective
drug trade-related
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːrkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːkəʊ / (BrE)
relating to illegal drug trafficking, the drug trade, or the criminals and violence associated with it

Examples

Narco violence
The documentary explores the impact of narco violence on local communities.
Several politicians were accused of having ties to narco organizations.
The region has suffered from years of narco conflicts and instability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - involving crime or illegal acts
  • synonym: drug-related - connected to illegal drugs or drug activity
  • antonym: legal - permitted by law; not criminal
