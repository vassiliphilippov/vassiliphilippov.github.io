Gomer

proper noun
Biblical personal name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊmər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊmə / (BrE)
a personal name in the Bible, referring to both a son of Japheth and the wife of the prophet Hosea

Examples

Gomer in the Bible
In the Book of Genesis, Gomer is listed as one of Japheth's sons.
The prophet Hosea was commanded to marry Gomer as a symbol of Israel's unfaithfulness.
Biblical scholars often discuss the significance of the name Gomer in Old Testament narratives.
