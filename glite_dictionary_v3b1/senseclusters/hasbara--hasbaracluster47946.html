hasbara

noun
Israeli public relations
Pronunciation: / hɑsˈbɑrə / (AmE), / hɑːsˈbɑːrə / (BrE)
public relations activities or explanations intended to promote Israel's image or justify its policies, especially abroad

Examples

Hasbara campaign
The government launched a new hasbara campaign to address international criticism.
She works in hasbara, focusing on explaining Israel's policies to foreign audiences.
Many volunteers participate in hasbara efforts during times of conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propaganda - information used to promote a particular political cause or point of view
  • synonym: public relations - the practice of managing the spread of information between an organization and the public
