insolent

adjective
rude and disrespectful
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.səl.ənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.səl.ənt / (BrE)
showing a rude and arrogant lack of respect for others or for accepted standards

Examples

His insolent attitude eventually cost him the job.
She gave an insolent glare to the manager when reprimanded.
The student's insolent comment surprised the entire class.
