Examples

Synonyms

  • alleviate
    5.3 8.12/m
    alleviate reduces pain or problems directly, while palliate often eases effects without removing the root cause.
  • mitigate
    5.8 8.47/m
    mitigate makes harm or loss less serious through action, while palliate often treats only symptoms or seems to lessen severity.
  • extenuate
    8.8 0.23/m
    extenuate presents an offense as less serious by giving excuses, while palliate often eases effects without fixing the cause.
  • soften the blow
    4.7 0.39/m
