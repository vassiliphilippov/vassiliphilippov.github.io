rodding

noun
clearing pipes with rod
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of using a rod to clear blockages from pipes or drains

Examples

Rodding a drain
The plumber used rodding to clear the blocked drain in the bathroom.
After several attempts at rodding, the kitchen sink finally drained properly.
Professional rodding is often needed when household cleaners cannot remove a stubborn clog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snaking - the process of using a flexible tool to clear pipe blockages
  • synonym: unclogging - the act of removing a blockage from a pipe or drain
