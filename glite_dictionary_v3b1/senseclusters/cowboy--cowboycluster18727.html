cowboy

noun
male performer skilled in rodeo riding and roping
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a man who performs in exhibitions of riding, roping, and similar skills, especially in rodeos or wild west shows

Examples

The audience cheered as the cowboy expertly roped the charging steer.
