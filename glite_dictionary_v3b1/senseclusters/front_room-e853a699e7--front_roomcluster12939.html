front room

noun
room at the front of a house for relaxing
1.2 1.19/m
Pronunciation: / fr ånt ruÀêm / (AmE), / fr ånt ruÀêm / (BrE)
a room in a house, typically at the front, where people sit, talk, and relax, often used as a living room

Examples

The front room
Children were playing quietly in the front room while dinner was prepared.
Their old house still has a traditional front room rarely used except on special occasions.
We entertained our guests in the front room overlooking the garden.
