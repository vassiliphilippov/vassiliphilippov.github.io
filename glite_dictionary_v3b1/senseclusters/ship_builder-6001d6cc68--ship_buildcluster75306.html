ship builder

noun
someone who constructs ships
2.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɪp ˈbɪldər / (AmE), / ʃɪp ˈbɪldə / (BrE)
A person who constructs and launches ships, especially those made of wood.

Examples

A ship builder apprentice
Becoming a skilled ship builder requires years of apprenticeship and practice.
Many generations of ship builders worked in the old port town.
The ship builder carefully shaped the wooden hull before launching the new vessel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shipwright - a person skilled in the construction and repair of ships
