rebuker

noun
someone who rebukes or criticizes
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈbjukər / (AmE), / rɪˈbjuːkə / (BrE)
a person who expresses sharp disapproval or criticism to someone, especially because of their behavior or actions

Examples

A rebuker of mistakes
Despite the awkward silence, the rebuker did not hold back her criticism of the team's performance.
In literature, a rebuker often serves to challenge the protagonist's actions or beliefs.
The child was startled by the stern words of his rebuker after breaking the vase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval of someone or something
