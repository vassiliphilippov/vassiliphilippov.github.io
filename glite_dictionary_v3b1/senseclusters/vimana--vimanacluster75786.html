vimana

noun
temple tower; flying palace
Pronunciation: / viˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / vɪˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
a temple tower or a mythical flying palace described in ancient Indian texts

Examples

Ancient vimana tower
The ancient temple features a tall vimana above its main shrine.
Legends speak of a vimana that could soar through the skies like a chariot.
Architects studied the intricate carvings on the vimana to learn about historical temple design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pagoda - a tiered tower with multiple eaves, common in Asia
  • synonym: shikhara - the rising tower in North Indian temple architecture
