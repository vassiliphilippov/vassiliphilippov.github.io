unknowledgeable

adjective
not knowledgeable; lacking information
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈnɑlɪdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈnɒlɪdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
lacking knowledge or information; not well informed.

Examples

An unknowledgeable person
His advice was unknowledgeable and did more harm than good.
Many tourists are unknowledgeable about local customs when they first arrive in a new country.
The new intern was unknowledgeable about the company's procedures and needed extra guidance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about a particular thing
  • antonym: knowledgeable - well informed; having knowledge
