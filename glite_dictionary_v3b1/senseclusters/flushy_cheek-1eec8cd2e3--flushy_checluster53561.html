flushy cheek

noun
cheek that is red or rosy
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌʃi tʃik / (AmE), / ˈflʌʃi tʃiːk / (BrE)
A cheek that appears red or rosy, typically due to embarrassment, exertion, or emotion.

Examples

A flushy cheek
Her flushy cheek was noticeable as she walked into the crowded room.
The cold air gave him a flushy cheek that contrasted with his usual complexion.
Whenever he received a compliment, a flushy cheek would give away his feelings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blushing - showing a red color in the face due to embarrassment or shame
