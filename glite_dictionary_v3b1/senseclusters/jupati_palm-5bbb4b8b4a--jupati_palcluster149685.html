jupati palm

noun
a tall Brazilian palm with large divided leaves
9.9 9.3
Pronunciation: /dʒuː.ˈpɑː.ti pɑːm/ /; / /ˈdʒuː.pə.ti pɑːm/ (AmE), /dʒuː.ˈpɑː.ti pɑːm/ /; / /ˈdʒuː.pə.ti pɑːm/ (BrE)
