naturally occurring

adjective
found in nature, not artificial
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃərəli əˈkɝːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnætʃrəli əˈkɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
existing in nature without human intervention or artificial aid.

Examples

Naturally occurring minerals
Diamonds are naturally occurring minerals formed deep within the Earth.
Gold is a naturally occurring element found in the Earth's crust.
Some toxins are naturally occurring in certain plants and animals.
