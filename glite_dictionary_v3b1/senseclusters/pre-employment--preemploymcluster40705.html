pre-employment

noun
hiring checks
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː ɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriː ɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (BrE)
the checks, tests, or procedures carried out before someone is hired for a job

Examples

Pre-employment check
All candidates must pass a pre-employment background check before starting work.
The company offers free pre-employment health screenings to ensure workplace safety.
Many organizations include drug testing as part of their pre-employment process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: onboarding - the process of integrating a new employee into an organization
  • synonym: screening - the process of evaluating or investigating someone before a decision
