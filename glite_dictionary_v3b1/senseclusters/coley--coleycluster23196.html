coley

noun
edible northern fish
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊli / (AmE), / ˈkəʊli / (BrE)
a type of edible fish, also called saithe or pollock, found in northern Atlantic waters

Examples

Fresh coley fish
The chef prepared a delicious dish using fresh coley from the market.
Many people in Scotland enjoy eating coley as an affordable alternative to cod.
Fishermen often catch coley in the cold waters of the North Atlantic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cod - a large edible fish found in cold northern seas
  • synonym: pollock - an edible fish found in northern seas, related to coley
