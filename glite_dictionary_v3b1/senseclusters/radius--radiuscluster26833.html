radius

noun
line from center to edge of circle or sphere
3.3 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪ.dɪ.əs/ (AmE), /ˈreɪ.dɪ.əs/ (BrE)
the straight line or distance from the center of a circle or sphere to its edge or surface

Examples

Radius of a circle
Draw a line from the center to any point on the edge; that's the radius.
The radius of the circle is 5 centimeters.
The planet's radius makes it twice as big as Earth.

You can calculate the area of a circle if you know its radius.
