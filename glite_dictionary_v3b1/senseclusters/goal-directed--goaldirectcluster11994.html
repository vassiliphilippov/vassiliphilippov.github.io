goal-directed

adjective
having a specific aim or purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊl dɪˌrɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl dɪˌrɛktɪd / (BrE)
(of an action, behavior, or process) having a specific aim or purpose; driven by objectives.

Examples

Goal-directed behavior
Her goal-directed approach to studying helped her achieve excellent grades.
Most goal-directed behavior involves careful planning and motivation.
The project team remained goal-directed despite numerous setbacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aimless - without purpose or direction
  • synonym: purposive - having or showing purpose or design
