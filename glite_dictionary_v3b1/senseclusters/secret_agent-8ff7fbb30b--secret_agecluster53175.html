secret agent

noun
a person employed to gather secret information
3.4 0.81/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀê.kr…ôt Ààe…™.d í…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀê.kr…™t Ààe…™.d í…ônt / (BrE)

Examples
