secret agent

noun
a person employed to gather secret information
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.krət ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˈsiː.krɪt ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (BrE)
a person who is employed by a government or other organization to gather secret information, especially about enemies or rivals

Examples

A secret agent mission
He lived a double life as a secret agent, hiding his true identity even from his family.
In many films, the secret agent uses clever gadgets to complete dangerous missions.
The government sent a secret agent to infiltrate the foreign embassy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spy - a person who secretly collects and reports information on the activities, movements, and plans of an enemy or competitor
