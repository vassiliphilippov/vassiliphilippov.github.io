demilune

noun
semicircular table
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛmiˌluːn / (AmE), / ˈdɛmiˌluːn / (BrE)
a semicircular or half-moon–shaped table, often placed against a wall as a decorative piece

Examples

Demilune table
The antique demilune in the entryway held a small lamp and a bowl for keys.
She admired the elegant demilune table, which fit perfectly against the living room wall.
A vase of fresh flowers sat atop the polished demilune beneath the window.
