afterworld

noun
existence or realm after death
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌwɝld / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌwɜːld / (BrE)
the world or existence believed to follow death, often as conceived in religion or mythology

Examples

The afterworld belief
Many cultures have myths describing what happens in the afterworld.
