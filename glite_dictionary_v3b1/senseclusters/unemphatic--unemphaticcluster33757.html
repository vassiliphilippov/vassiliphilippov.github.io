unemphatic

adjective
not emphasized; lacking emphasis
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɛmˈfætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɛmˈfætɪk / (BrE)
Not expressed or delivered with emphasis; lacking emphasis.

Examples

An unemphatic apology
He offered an unemphatic apology that seemed insincere.
Her response was deliberately unemphatic, as if she wanted to avoid confrontation.
The speaker's unemphatic tone failed to capture the audience's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emphatic - stressed or delivered with emphasis
