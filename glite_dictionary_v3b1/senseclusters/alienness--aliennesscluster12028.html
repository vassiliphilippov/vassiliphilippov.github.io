alienness

noun
feeling of not belonging
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliənnəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪliənnɪs /; / ˈeɪliənnəs / (BrE)
the feeling or quality of being strange, unfamiliar, or not belonging

Examples

A sense of alienness
He felt a strong sense of alienness when he moved to a new country.
The alienness of the city made it difficult for her to settle in at first.
After years away, the familiar streets now filled him with unexpected alienness.
The alienness of the customs made her feel out of place in the new country.
Science fiction often explores the alienness of life on other planets.
He was fascinated by the alienness of the ancient language he was studying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estrangement - the state of being separated or feeling distant from others
  • antonym: familiarity - the quality of being well known or easily recognized
  • synonym: strangeness - the quality of being unfamiliar or odd
