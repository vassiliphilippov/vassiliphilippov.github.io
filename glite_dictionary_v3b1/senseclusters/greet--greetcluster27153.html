greet

1.0 5/m
Pronunciation: / …°rit / (AmE), / …°riÀêt / (BrE)
to react or respond to someone or something in a particular way when you meet or encounter them

Examples

Greeted with silence
His proposal was greeted by silence, making him feel uneasy.
The arrival of the new policy was greeted with skepticism by many employees.
The audience greeted the performer with thunderous applause as she walked on stage.
