albatross

noun
a troublesome burden or handicap
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.bəˌtrɑs / (AmE), / ˈæl.bə.trɒs / (BrE)
(figurative) a constant burden or obstacle that causes ongoing trouble or anxiety.

Examples

An albatross around his neck
His debt proved to be an albatross around his neck for many years.
She feared the scandal would be an albatross that would haunt her career.
The failed project became an albatross for the company, draining resources and lowering morale.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blessing - something that brings happiness or relief
  • synonym: burden - a load or responsibility that is difficult to carry
  • synonym: millstone - a heavy or inescapable responsibility
