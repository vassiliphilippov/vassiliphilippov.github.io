Synonyms

  • argue
    2.8 123/m
    argue often means giving reasons or fighting for an idea, while debate is a formal, structured discussion between opposing sides.
  • contend
    4.6 11/m
    contend is to state or claim a position strongly in an argument, while debate is a back-and-forth formal discussion among people.
  • dispute
    3.5 11/m
    dispute means to question or deny a fact and often to fight over truth, while debate is a formal, rule-led discussion of ideas.
