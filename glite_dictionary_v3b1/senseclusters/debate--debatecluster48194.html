debate

verb
to discuss or argue about something
5.1 2.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
to discuss or argue about a topic, especially in a formal or structured way

Examples

To debate an issue
The two candidates debated the issues on television last night.
They debated for hours without reaching an agreement.
We are still debating whether to accept the offer.
In class tomorrow, students will debate the benefits and drawbacks of online learning.
The committee will debate the proposed changes before making a decision.
They debated for hours about whether to invest in the new project.
Historians still debate the causes of the empire's collapse.
The candidates debated healthcare reform for over an hour on live television.
We debated whether to take the job offer or stay at our current positions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argue - give reasons for or against something, sometimes in a heated way
  • synonym: discuss - talk about something with others, especially in order to exchange ideas or reach a decision
