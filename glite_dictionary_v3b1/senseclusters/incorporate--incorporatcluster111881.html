incorporate

verb
include as part of a whole
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɔrpəˌreɪt /; / ɪnˈkɔːrpəˌreɪt /; / ɪnˈkɔːr.pəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt /; / ɪnˈkɔː.pə.reɪt / (BrE)
to include, combine, or unite something as part of a whole

Examples

Her novel incorporates elements of mystery and romance to create a unique story.
