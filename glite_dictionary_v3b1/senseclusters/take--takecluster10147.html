Synonyms

  • capture
    2.8 31/m
    capture often means taking someone or something by force after a chase, while take means to gain control generally.
  • take control
    3.5 8.23/m
    take control means to begin having power and making decisions, while take is a general verb for claiming or gaining control.
  • take over
    3.8 9.27/m
    take over means to gain ownership or control of a business or group, while take can mean gaining control more broadly.
  • seize
    4.6 8.02/m
    seize means to take possession quickly, often by legal force or order, while take covers many ways of gaining control.
  • usurp
    8.6 2.82/m
    usurp means to take power or a position without right, often illegally, while take is a general word for gaining control.

  • antonym: lose - to fail to keep control of something once held
