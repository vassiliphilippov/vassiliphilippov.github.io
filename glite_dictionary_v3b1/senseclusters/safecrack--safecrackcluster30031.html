safecrack

verb
to open a safe illegally
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfˌkræk / (AmE), / ˈseɪfˌkræk / (BrE)
to open or attempt to open a locked safe without the key or combination, usually by illegal or covert methods

Examples

To safecrack a vault
The thieves planned to safecrack the vault after midnight when the building was empty.
He learned how to safecrack by studying old security manuals and practicing on discarded locks.
Police arrested the suspect as he tried to safecrack the jewelry store's safe.
