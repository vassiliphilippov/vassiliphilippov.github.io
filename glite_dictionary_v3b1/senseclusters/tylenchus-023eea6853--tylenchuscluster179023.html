Tylenchus

noun
genus of nematode worms (plant parasites)
Pronunciation: / taɪˈlɛŋkəs / (AmE), / tɪˈlɛŋkəs / (BrE)
A genus of nematode worms, constituting the type genus of the family Tylenchidae, many species of which are plant parasites.

Examples

Tylenchus plant parasites
Researchers discovered a new species within the genus Tylenchus affecting potatoes.
Some plant diseases are associated with infestations of Tylenchus nematodes.
The microscopic Tylenchus worms can cause significant damage to crop roots.
