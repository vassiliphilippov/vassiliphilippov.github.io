straddler

noun
person sitting astride
Pronunciation: / ˈstrædlər / (AmE), / ˈstrædlə / (BrE)
a person who sits or stands with a leg on each side of something, such as a horse, motorcycle, or fence

Examples

Fence straddler
The straddler balanced carefully on the narrow fence, looking down at the garden below.
As a skilled straddler, he could mount his motorcycle quickly and ride off in seconds.
The children took turns being the straddler on the playground beam during recess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rider - a person who sits on and controls a horse, bicycle, or motorcycle
