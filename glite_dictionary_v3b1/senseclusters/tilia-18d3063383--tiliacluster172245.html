Tilia

noun
genus of linden or lime trees
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪliə / (AmE), / ˈtɪlɪə / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous trees with smooth silver-grey bark, native to North America, Europe, and Asia, commonly known as lindens or lime trees.

Examples

A Tilia tree
Many city parks are lined with Tilia trees due to their pleasant shade.
Several species of Tilia are valued for their soft, workable wood.
The fragrant flowers of Tilia are used to make herbal teas in Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lime tree - common English name for Tilia species; not related to citrus lime
  • synonym: linden - deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves, also called lime tree
