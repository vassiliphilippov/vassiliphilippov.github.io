threadless

adjective
disjointed or unconnected
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈθred.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈθred.ləs/ (BrE)
lacking a continuous theme, connection, or unifying idea; disjointed or unconnected

Examples

Threadless story
The film felt threadless, with scenes that seemed unrelated to each other.
Her argument was so threadless that the audience struggled to follow her point.
Critics described the anthology as threadless, lacking any central theme or connection.
