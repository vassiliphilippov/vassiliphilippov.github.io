pick up

verb
arrest or take into custody
Pronunciation: / pɪk ʌp / (AmE), / pɪk ʌp / (BrE)
(of the police) to take someone into custody; to arrest.

Examples

Police pick up a suspect

Authorities plan to pick up anyone involved in the illegal scheme.
The police decided to pick up the suspect early in the morning.
