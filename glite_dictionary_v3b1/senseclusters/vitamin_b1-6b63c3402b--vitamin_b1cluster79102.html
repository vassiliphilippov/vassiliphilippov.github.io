vitamin B1

noun
a B vitamin also known as thiamine
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪt̬əmɪn ˈbi wʌn / (AmE), / ˌvɪtəmɪn ˈbiː wʌn / (BrE)
a water-soluble B vitamin, also called thiamine, that is essential for energy metabolism and the prevention of beriberi

Examples

Vitamin B1 deficiency
A deficiency of vitamin B1 can cause serious neurological disorders such as beriberi.
Many breakfast cereals are fortified with vitamin B1 to support healthy growth.
The doctor recommended supplements containing vitamin B1 for improved energy metabolism.
