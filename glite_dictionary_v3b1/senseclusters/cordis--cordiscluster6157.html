cordis

noun
of the heart
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɪs / (BrE)
a medical term meaning 'of the heart', often used in Latin anatomical or medical phrases

Examples

Vena cordis
The surgeon inserted the catheter through the vena cordis to reach the heart.
In medical terminology, cordis is often used in Latin phrases describing heart anatomy.
The phrase 'valvulae cordis' refers to the valves of the heart in anatomical texts.

Related Terms
