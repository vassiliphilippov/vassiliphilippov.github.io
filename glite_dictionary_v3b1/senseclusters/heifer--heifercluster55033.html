heifer

noun
young female cow
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛfər / (AmE), / ˈhɛfə / (BrE)
a young female cow that has not borne a calf

Examples

A young heifer
After a year, the heifer will mature and be able to produce milk.
Each heifer was tagged for identification at the agricultural fair.
The farmer bought a new heifer to add to his herd.
