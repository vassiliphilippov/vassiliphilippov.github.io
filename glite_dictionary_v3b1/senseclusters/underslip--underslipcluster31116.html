underslip

noun
thin undergarment
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌslɪp / (AmE), / ˈʌndəslɪp / (BrE)
a thin undergarment worn beneath a dress or skirt to provide coverage or prevent transparency

Examples

A underslip garment
She always wears a underslip under her summer dresses for extra comfort.
The delicate fabric of the dress required a matching underslip to avoid being see-through.
After buying a new skirt, she realized she needed a underslip for proper coverage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petticoat - a light loose undergarment hanging from the shoulders or waist, worn under a skirt or dress
  • synonym: slip - a woman's undergarment worn beneath a dress or skirt
