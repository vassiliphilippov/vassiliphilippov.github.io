skin-dive

noun
underwater swimming with minimal equipment
4.5 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪn daɪv/ /; / /ˈskɪn diː.veɪ/ (AmE), /ˈskɪn daɪv/ /; / /ˈskɪn diː.veɪ/ (BrE)
