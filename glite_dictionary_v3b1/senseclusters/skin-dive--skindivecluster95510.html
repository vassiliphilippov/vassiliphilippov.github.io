skin-dive

noun
underwater swimming with minimal equipment
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪnˌdaɪv / (AmE), / ˈskɪn.daɪv / (BrE)
the activity of swimming underwater without the use of any breathing equipment other than a snorkel

Examples

A skin-dive adventure
He learned to skin-dive before trying more advanced scuba diving techniques.
Many tourists love to skin-dive in the clear, tropical waters of the Caribbean.
On weekends, she often goes to the lake to skin-dive with her friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scuba diving - the activity of swimming underwater using special breathing equipment
  • synonym: snorkeling - the practice of swimming on or through water while equipped with a diving mask and snorkel
