Toona calantas

noun
Philippine timber tree with fragrant red wood
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːnə kəˈlæntəs / (AmE), / ˈtuːnə kəˈlæntəs / (BrE)
A Philippine timber tree with hard, red, fragrant wood, valued for construction and furniture.

Examples

Toona calantas wood
Carpenters often prefer Toona calantas for its pleasant scent and reddish hue.
Many forests in Luzon once flourished with native Toona calantas before logging reduced its numbers.
The durable wood of the Toona calantas is highly prized for making fine furniture in the Philippines.
