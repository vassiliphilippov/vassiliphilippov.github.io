microbrew

adjective
small-batch beer
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruː / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌbruː / (BrE)
produced by a small, independent brewery, typically in limited quantities.

Examples

Microbrew beer selection
The restaurant offers a wide selection of microbrew beers from local producers.
She prefers microbrew ales because of their unique flavors and quality.
At the festival, every microbrew lager had its own distinct taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craft - made with skill and care, often in small quantities
  • antonym: mass-produced - made in large quantities by machines
