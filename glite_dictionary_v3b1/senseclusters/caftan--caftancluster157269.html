caftan

noun
long, loose robe
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkæf.tæn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːf.tæn /; / ˈkæf.tæn / (BrE)
a long, loose-fitting robe or dress with full sleeves, traditionally worn in the Middle East and also used as a women's dress

My grandmother brought a beautiful Moroccan caftan back from her travels.
