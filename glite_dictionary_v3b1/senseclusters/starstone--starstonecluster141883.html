starstone

noun
magical gemstone
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌstəʊn / (BrE)
in fiction, a magical gemstone believed to possess special powers or great significance

Examples

Magic starstone
The ancient legend spoke of a starstone hidden deep within the mountain.
Only those pure of heart could unlock the true power of the starstone.
In many fantasy stories, a starstone is the key to saving the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amulet - an object believed to have magical powers and protect its owner
  • synonym: philosopher's stone - a legendary alchemical substance said to grant immortality or turn metals into gold
