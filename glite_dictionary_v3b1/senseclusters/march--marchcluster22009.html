march

noun
an act of walking in procession
5.1
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
an act of walking with regular, measured steps, often as part of a procession or demonstration

Examples

A long march
After hours on the march, the soldiers finally reached their resting place.
The protestors began their march through the city streets at noon.
We watched the annual march honoring veterans on television.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amble - to walk at a slow, relaxed pace
  • synonym: parade - a public procession, especially to celebrate a special day or event
