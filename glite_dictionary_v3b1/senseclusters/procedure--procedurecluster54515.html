procedure

noun
series of actions or steps

Examples

Safety procedure
He followed the maintenance procedure step by step to repair the equipment.
Learning the correct procedure is essential when assembling the machine.
The company updated its safety procedure for handling hazardous materials.

She carefully explained each step of the safety procedure before we began.
The application procedure for the grant is quite straightforward.

Synonyms

  • process
    2.7 377/m
    process is a series of actions that develop over time, while procedure is a set of planned steps done in a fixed order.
  • method
    2.2 173/m
    method is a particular, often systematic way to do something, while procedure is a specific ordered set of steps for that task.
  • operation
    3.4 34/m
    operation is how something works or is used in action, while procedure is the planned series of ordered steps to complete it.
