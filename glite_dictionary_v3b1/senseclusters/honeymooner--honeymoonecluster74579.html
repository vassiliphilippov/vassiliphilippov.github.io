honeymooner

noun
recently married person on honeymoon
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌmunər / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌmuːnə / (BrE)
a person who has recently married, especially while on their honeymoon

Examples

Honeymooners on holiday
As honeymooners, they spent two weeks exploring the coast of Italy together.
The hotel offered a special package for honeymooners visiting the island.
The restaurant reserved a secluded table for the honeymooner and her spouse.
