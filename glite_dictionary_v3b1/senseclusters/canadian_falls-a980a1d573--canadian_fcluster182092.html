Canadian Falls

proper noun
the Canadian section of Niagara Falls
3.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪdiən fɔlz / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən fɔːlz / (BrE)
the larger section of Niagara Falls, located on the Canadian side, also known as Horseshoe Falls.

Examples

From the observation deck, you could see the mist rising above the Canadian Falls.
Many tourists visit the Canadian Falls every year for its breathtaking views and thunderous roar.
The best photos of Niagara often feature the majestic Canadian Falls.
