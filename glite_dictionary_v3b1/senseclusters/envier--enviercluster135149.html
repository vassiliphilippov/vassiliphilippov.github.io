envier

noun
person who envies
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnviər / (AmE), / ˈɛnviə / (BrE)
a person who feels or shows envy toward someone else

Examples

The envier feels jealousy
The envier could not hide his resentment when his colleague received the promotion.
She tried not to be an envier of her friend's good fortune.
Among the group, there was always one envier who wished for what others had.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admirer - a person who regards someone with respect or warm approval
  • synonym: jealous - feeling or showing envy of someone
