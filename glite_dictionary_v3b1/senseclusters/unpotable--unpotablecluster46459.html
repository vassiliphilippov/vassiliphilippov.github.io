unpotable

adjective
not drinkable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpoʊtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not safe or suitable for drinking

Examples

Unpotable water
The tap water in that remote village is unpotable and must be boiled before use.
After the chemical spill, the lake became unpotable for several months.
Travelers were warned that the well water was unpotable and should not be consumed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: potable - safe to drink
  • synonym: undrinkable - not fit to be drunk
