Adriatic Sea

proper noun
sea between Italy and the Balkans
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪdriˈætɪk ˈsi / (AmE), / ˌædriˈætɪk ˈsiː / (BrE)
an arm of the Mediterranean Sea located between the eastern coast of Italy and the western coasts of Slovenia, Croatia, Montenegro, and Albania.

Examples

The Adriatic Sea coast
Ferries regularly cross the Adriatic Sea between Italy and Croatia.
Storms on the Adriatic Sea can be sudden and fierce, making sailing challenging.
The Adriatic Sea is known for its clear waters and beautiful coastline.
