area

noun
extent of a surface within boundaries
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈeəriə / (BrE)
the amount of surface within a set of boundaries, measured in square units

Examples

Area of a rectangle
She calculated the area of the rug to make sure it would fit the room.
The area of the new park is almost ten acres.
To paint the wall, you need to know its total area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perimeter - the continuous line forming the boundary of a closed geometric figure
  • antonym: volume - the amount of space occupied by a 3-dimensional object
