epidemic parotitis

noun
acute contagious viral disease; mumps
9.9 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk pəˈrɑtətɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk pəˈrɒtɪtɪs / (BrE)
