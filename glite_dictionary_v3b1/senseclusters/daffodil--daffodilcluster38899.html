daffodil

noun
spring-flowering plant with trumpet-shaped flowers
3.4 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdæf.ə.dɪl/ (AmE), /ˈdæf.ə.dɪl/ (BrE)
a bulbous plant of the genus Narcissus with long, narrow leaves and bright yellow or white flowers with a trumpet-shaped corona

Examples

A field of daffodils
A field of daffodils stretched as far as the eye could see.
In early spring, the daffodil is one of the first flowers to bloom in the garden.
She picked a bright yellow daffodil and placed it in a vase on the table.
