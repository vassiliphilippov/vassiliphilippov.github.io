utter

verb
to put into circulation (forged money/documents)
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌtə / (BrE)
to put (especially counterfeit money or a forged document) into circulation as if it were genuine

Examples

To utter fake money
He was arrested for attempting to utter counterfeit bills at the store.
It is a crime to utter a forged check, even if you did not make it yourself.
The suspect was found guilty of conspiring to utter fake identification documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circulate - to cause something to pass around or be distributed
  • synonym: issue - to officially send out or distribute something
