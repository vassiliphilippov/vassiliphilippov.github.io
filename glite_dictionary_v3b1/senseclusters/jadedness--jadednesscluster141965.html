jadedness

noun
weariness or boredom
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒeɪ.dɪd.nəs/ /; / /ˈdʒeɪ.dəd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈdʒeɪ.dɪd.nəs/ /; / /ˈdʒeɪ.dəd.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of feeling tired, bored, or lacking enthusiasm, especially after having too much of something

Examples

Feeling jadedness after work
After years of repetitive work, she felt a growing jadedness toward her daily routine.
The traveler's jadedness was clear after visiting so many similar cities in a short time.
His jadedness made it hard for him to get excited about new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enthusiasm - a strong feeling of excitement or interest
  • synonym: weariness - a feeling of being very tired or bored
