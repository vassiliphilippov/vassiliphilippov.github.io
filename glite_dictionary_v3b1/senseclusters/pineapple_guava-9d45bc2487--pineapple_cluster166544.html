pineapple guava

noun
tropical fruit with sweet white flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌnæpəl ˈɡwɑvə / (AmE), / ˈpaɪˌnæpl ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
A small, oval, dark-green tropical fruit with sweet, aromatic white flesh, chiefly used for jellies and preserves.
