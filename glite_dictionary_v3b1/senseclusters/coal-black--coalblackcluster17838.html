coal-black

adjective
very deep black color
2.9
Pronunciation: / koʊl ˈblæk / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈblæk / (BrE)
of a very deep, intense black color, like that of coal or jet

Examples

Coal-black color
He wore a coal-black suit to the formal dinner.
Her coal-black hair shone in the sunlight.
The night sky was coal-black with no stars in sight.
