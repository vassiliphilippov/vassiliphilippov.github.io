hydropower

noun
electricity from water
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌpaʊə / (BrE)
electricity or energy generated by the movement of water, especially using turbines and dams
