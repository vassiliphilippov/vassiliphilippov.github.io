soshnik

noun
iron plowshare part of a sokha
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɒʃ.nɪk / (BrE)
An iron plowshare that is a part of a traditional Russian sokha plow.

Examples

An iron soshnik
In the museum, you can find a soshnik from the 19th century used in Russian agriculture.
The blacksmith expertly forged a new soshnik for the village plow.
Without a sturdy soshnik, the sokha cannot effectively cut through the soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plowshare - the cutting blade of a plow
