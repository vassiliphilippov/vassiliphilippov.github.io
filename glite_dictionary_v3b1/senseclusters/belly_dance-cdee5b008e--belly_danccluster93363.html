belly dance

noun
Middle Eastern dance with hip and abdominal movements
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli dæns / (AmE), / ˈbɛli dɑːns / (BrE)
a form of Middle Eastern dance characterized by sensual movements of the hips and abdomen

Examples

A belly dance show
Many people take belly dance classes for exercise and cultural appreciation.
She has been learning to belly dance for three years and now performs in local shows.
The festival featured a stunning belly dance performed by international artists.
