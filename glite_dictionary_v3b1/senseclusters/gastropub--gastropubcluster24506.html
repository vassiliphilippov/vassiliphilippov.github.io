gastropub

noun
pub with quality food
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæstroʊˌpʌb / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrəʊˌpʌb / (BrE)
a pub that serves high-quality food as well as drinks, often in a stylish setting

Examples

A gastropub restaurant
We celebrated her birthday at a trendy gastropub known for its creative menu.
The new gastropub downtown offers both craft beers and gourmet burgers.
He prefers a gastropub because he enjoys good food with his drinks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bistro - a small restaurant serving moderately priced meals
  • synonym: pub - a place where alcoholic drinks are served and people gather
