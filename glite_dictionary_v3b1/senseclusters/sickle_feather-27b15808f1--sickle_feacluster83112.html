sickle feather

noun
long curved tail feather of a rooster
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪk.əl ˌfɛð.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.əl ˌfeð.ə / (BrE)
one of the long, curved tail feathers of a rooster, often more prominent and decorative than other tail feathers

Examples

Long sickle feathers
He collected sickle feathers from the barn to use in his crafts.
Poultry judges pay special attention to the curve and color of a bird's sickle feathers.
The rooster's sickle feathers gleamed in the sunlight as he strutted across the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hackle - long, narrow feathers on the neck of a rooster or other birds
