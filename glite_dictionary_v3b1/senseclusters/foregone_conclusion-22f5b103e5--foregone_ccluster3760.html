foregone conclusion

noun
an inevitable or certain outcome
6.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /fɔːr.ˈɡɑːn kən.ˈkluː.ʒən/ /; / /fɔːr.ˈɡɔːn kən.ˈkluː.ʒən/ /; / /fə.ˈɡɑːn kən.ˈkluː.ʒən/ (AmE), /fɔː.ˈɡɒn kən.ˈkluː.ʒən/ /; / /fə.ˈɡɒn kən.ˈkluː.ʒən/ (BrE)
a result or outcome that is certain to happen and cannot be avoided

Examples

The outcome of the match was a foregone conclusion after the first half.
With such a strong lead, their victory became a foregone conclusion long before the final results.
