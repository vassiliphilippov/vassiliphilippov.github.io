mythic

adjective
game difficulty: highest level
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪk / (BrE)
(in gaming) of or relating to the highest/most difficult level or mode in a game, especially a raid or dungeon that requires coordinated, skilled play.

Examples

Mythic raid
The guild attempted the mythic raid tonight, the game's hardest mode.
They organized a group of twelve players to tackle a new mythic dungeon on Saturday.
After weeks of practice they finally cleared the mythic difficulty and earned unique loot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hard mode - a game's higher or most difficult setting or mode
