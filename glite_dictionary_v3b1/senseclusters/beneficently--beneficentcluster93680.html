beneficently

adverb
kindly, generously
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnɛfəsəntli / (AmE), / bɪˈnɛfɪsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows kindness and generosity

Examples

To beneficently help
The teacher spoke beneficently to the nervous student, helping him feel at ease.
He donated beneficently to several charities every year.
The queen ruled beneficently, always considering the needs of her people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cruelly - in a way that causes pain or suffering
  • synonym: generously - in a way that shows willingness to give more than expected
  • synonym: kindly - in a kind or gentle manner
