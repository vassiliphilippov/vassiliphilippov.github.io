windproof

adjective
blocks wind
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed or made to prevent wind from passing through

Examples

Windproof jacket
She bought a windproof coat to stay warm during her winter walks.
The tent is made from windproof material to protect campers in harsh weather.
Cyclists often wear windproof gloves to keep their hands warm on cold days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breathable - allowing air or moisture to pass through
  • synonym: waterproof - designed to prevent water from passing through
