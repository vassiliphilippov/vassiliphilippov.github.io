airbrush

verb
to paint or spray with an airbrush
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
to paint or spray a surface using an airbrush, a device that uses compressed air to apply paint evenly

Examples

To airbrush a model
After the repairs, the mechanic airbrushed the fender to match the car’s original color.
She spent hours airbrushing the portrait to remove any visible blemishes.
The artist decided to airbrush a mural on the gallery wall for a smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spray-paint - to paint with spray paint (aerosol can)
