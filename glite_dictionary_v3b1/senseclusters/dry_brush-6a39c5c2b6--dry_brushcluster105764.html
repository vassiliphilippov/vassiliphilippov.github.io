dry brush

noun
painting technique
5.1
Pronunciation: /draɪ brʌʃ/ /; / /dʒraɪ brʌʃ/ (AmE), /draɪ brʌʃ/ /; / /dʒraɪ brʌʃ/ (BrE)
a painting technique in which a dry brush is used to apply paint, creating a scratchy or textured effect

Examples

Dry brush technique
The artist used dry brush to add rough texture to the rocks in the painting.
With the dry brush technique, she created the look of windblown grass on the canvas.
Learning to control dry brush strokes can help beginners achieve more interesting effects in their artwork.
