neck exercise

noun
exercise for neck muscles
Pronunciation: / nɛk ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / nɛk ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
an exercise intended to strengthen or improve the flexibility of the neck muscles

Examples

Daily neck exercises
Daily neck exercises can improve your posture and reduce stiffness.
He followed a routine of neck exercises after his whiplash injury.
My physical therapist recommended some neck exercises to help relieve my pain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neck strain - injury to the muscles in the neck
  • synonym: stretching exercise - exercise aimed at increasing muscle flexibility
