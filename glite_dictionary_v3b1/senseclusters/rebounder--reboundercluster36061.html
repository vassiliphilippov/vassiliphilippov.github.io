rebounder

noun
player retrieving missed shots
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌbaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbaʊndə / (BrE)
a player who retrieves the ball after a missed shot, especially in basketball or similar sports

Examples

Top rebounder in basketball
The team's best rebounder dominated under the basket throughout the game.
As a skilled rebounder, she often gave her team extra chances to score.
Coaches value a strong rebounder for their ability to change the momentum of a match.
