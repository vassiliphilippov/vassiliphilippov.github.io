vertical section

noun
diagram of an interior cut by a vertical plane
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːtɪkəl ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkəl ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
a drawing or diagram showing the interior of an object or structure as if cut by a vertical plane

Examples

A vertical section drawing
A vertical section reveals how the different layers of soil are arranged in the earth.
In the engineering manual, each component is explained using a labeled vertical section diagram.
The architect presented a vertical section of the building to illustrate the floor levels and interior details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross section - a section made by a plane cutting across something at right angles to its length
