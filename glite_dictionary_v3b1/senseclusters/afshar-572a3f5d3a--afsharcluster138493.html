Afshar

proper noun
Turkic ethnic group
Pronunciation: / æfˈʃɑr / (AmE), / æfˈʃɑː / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic ethnic group primarily living in Iran and nearby regions

Examples

Afshar people
The Afshar have a rich tradition of weaving colorful carpets and textiles.
Many Afshar communities can be found in the northwestern regions of Iran.
Historians study the migration patterns of the Afshar people across Central Asia.
