accentless

adjective
without stress
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæksəntləs / (AmE), / ˈæksəntləs / (BrE)
having no prosodic accent or stress; not pronounced with emphasis or stress

Examples

Accentless syllable
In the poem, several syllables are intentionally accentless to create a soft rhythm.
The teacher explained that the final vowel in the word is accentless and should be pronounced softly.
Some languages have accentless syllables that are spoken more quietly than the stressed ones.
