roading

noun
traveling on roads
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of traveling or moving along roads, especially for leisure or work

Examples

Roading across America
They spent the summer roading across the country in their van.
After retiring, he enjoyed roading through scenic countryside with his friends.
Many people dream of roading from coast to coast to see new places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commuting - regular travel between home and work
  • synonym: driving - the act of operating a vehicle on roads
