Andrenidae

noun
family of solitary, ground-burrowing bees
9.9
Pronunciation: / ænˈdrɛnəˌdaɪ /; / ænˈdrɛnɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ænˈdrɛnɪdiː /; / ænˈdriːnɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a large family of solitary, short-tongued bees, most of which burrow in the ground
