Sium latifolium

noun
European aquatic perennial plant
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪəm læˈtɪˌfoʊliəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪʊm latɪˈfɔːliəm / (BrE)
A large, robust perennial plant with white flowers, found wild in shallow freshwater areas of Europe; also known as greater water-parsnip.
