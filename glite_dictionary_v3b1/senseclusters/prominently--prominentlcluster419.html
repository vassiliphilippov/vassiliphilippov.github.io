prominently

adverb
in a very noticeable or important way
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.mə.nənt.li / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.mɪ.nənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that is very easy to see or notice; so as to be important or attract attention.

Examples

Name prominently displayed
Her name appears prominently on the list of contributors to the project.
The painting hung prominently in the entrance hall, visible to everyone who entered.
The sponsor’s logo was prominently displayed on the banner above the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspicuously - in a way that is easily seen or attracts attention
