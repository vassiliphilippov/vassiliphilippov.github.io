partridge pea

noun
tropical American plant with sensitive leaflets
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑr.trɪdʒ pi / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.trɪdʒ piː / (BrE)
A tropical American plant (Chamaecrista fasciculata), related to the genus Cassia, with sensitive leaflets and yellow flowers, often found in prairies and open woods.

Examples

The partridge pea plant
