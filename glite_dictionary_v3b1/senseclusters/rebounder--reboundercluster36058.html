rebounder

noun
person who recovers quickly
5.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈbaʊndər / (AmE), / rɪˈbaʊndə / (BrE)
a person who quickly recovers from setbacks, difficulties, or disappointments

Examples

A strong rebounder
Even after several failures, she remained a determined rebounder and kept trying new approaches.
His friends admired him for being such a rebounder after his business closed unexpectedly.
As a natural rebounder, he always finds a way to move forward after setbacks.
