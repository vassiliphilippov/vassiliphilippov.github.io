forgive and forget

verb
let go of resentment and move on
1.0
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɪv ənd fərˈɡɛt / (AmE), / fəˈɡɪv ənd fəˈɡɛt / (BrE)
to stop feeling angry or resentful about a past event and allow relationships to return to normal

Examples

To forgive and forget mistakes
After a heated argument, they agreed it was time to forgive and forget for the sake of their friendship.
It's not always easy to forgive and forget, but it's often necessary for personal happiness.
Sometimes it's healthiest to simply forgive and forget, even if an apology was never given.

  • synonym: pardon - to forgive someone for a mistake or wrongdoing
