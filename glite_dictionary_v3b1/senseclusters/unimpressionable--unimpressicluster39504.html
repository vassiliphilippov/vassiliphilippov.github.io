unimpressionable

adjective
not easily influenced or affected
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛʃ.ən.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈprɛʃ.ən.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not easily influenced or affected; not sensitive or susceptible to external impressions or influences.

Examples

An unimpressionable child
Children are often more impressionable than adults, but some kids are remarkably unimpressionable.
His unimpressionable nature made him a reliable judge in difficult situations.
She remained unimpressionable despite all their attempts to sway her opinion.
