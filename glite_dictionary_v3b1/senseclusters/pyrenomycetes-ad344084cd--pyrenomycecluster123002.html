Pyrenomycetes

noun
class of fungi with perithecia
Pronunciation: / paɪˌrɛnəmaɪˈsiːtiːz /; / paɪˌrɛnoʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz / (AmE), / paɪˌrɛnəʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz /; / paɪˌriːnəʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz / (BrE)
a class of fungi with fruiting bodies called perithecia, including powdery mildews, ergot, and Neurospora
