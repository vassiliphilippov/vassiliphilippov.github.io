untolled

adjective
not announced or counted
9.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊld / (BrE)
not tolled, counted, or announced, especially referring to a bell or a number that has not been sounded or declared

Examples

Untolled bell
The church bell remained untolled during the quiet afternoon.
In memory of the lost sailors, the bell was left untolled that evening.
The hour passed untolled, unnoticed by anyone in the empty hall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tolled - announced or counted, especially by ringing a bell
  • synonym: unannounced - not made known or declared
