prereferral

noun
preliminary interventions
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːrɪˈfɜːrəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːrɪˈfɜːrəl / (BrE)
the process of informal interventions and assessments conducted before making a formal referral, especially in educational settings

Examples

Prereferral process steps
Teachers often use prereferral strategies to support students before seeking special education services.
The school implemented a new prereferral process to identify students who need extra help early.
Documentation from the prereferral stage is required before a formal evaluation can begin.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: referral - the act of directing someone to a specialist or service
  • synonym: screening - the process of evaluating for possible issues before further action
