quick on one's feet

adjective
able to think and act quickly
4.5 4.2
Pronunciation: /kwɪk ən wʌnz fiːt/ /; / /kwɪk ɑːn wʌnz fiːt/ (AmE), /kwɪk ən wʌnz fiːt/ /; / /kwɪk ɒn wʌnz fiːt/ (BrE)
