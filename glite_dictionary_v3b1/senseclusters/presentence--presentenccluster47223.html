presentence

noun
pre-sentencing report
8.1
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈsen.təns/ /; / /prɪ.ˈsen.təns/ (AmE), /priː.ˈsen.təns/ /; / /prɪ.ˈsen.təns/ (BrE)
a report or investigation prepared before a court imposes a sentence, usually to provide information to the judge

Examples

A presentence report
The judge reviewed the presentence before deciding on the appropriate punishment.
A detailed presentence can help the court understand the defendant's background.
The lawyer requested a copy of the presentence to prepare for the sentencing hearing.

Related Terms
