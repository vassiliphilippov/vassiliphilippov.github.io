clementine tree

noun
tree producing clementine oranges
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛm.ənˌtaɪn ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈklɛm.ənˌtiːn ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree that produces clementines, a type of small, sweet mandarin orange grown mainly around the Mediterranean and in South Africa.

Examples

A clementine tree fruit
A mature clementine tree can yield hundreds of oranges in a single season.
Farmers in Spain cultivate the clementine tree for export to European markets.
The clementine tree in her backyard produces sweet fruit each winter.

Related Terms
