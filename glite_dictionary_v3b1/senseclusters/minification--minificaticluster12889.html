minification

noun
making smaller
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the action or process of making something smaller in size or scale

Examples

Minification of a device
The minification of the device allowed it to fit easily into a pocket.
Advances in technology have led to the minification of many electronic components.
The architect discussed the minification of the model to better visualize the building's layout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enlargement - the act of making something larger
  • synonym: reduction - the act of making something smaller or less in amount
