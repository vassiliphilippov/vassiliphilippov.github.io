tragic massacre

noun
a violent, sorrowful mass killing event
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtrædʒɪk ˈmæsəkɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrædʒɪk ˈmæsəkə / (BrE)
an extremely violent and distressing event involving the killing of many people, often causing deep sorrow and shock

Examples

News reports detailed the aftermath of the tragic massacre with interviews from survivors.
The historian gave a talk on the tragic massacre that occurred in the village.
The memorial honors those lost in the tragic massacre a century ago.
