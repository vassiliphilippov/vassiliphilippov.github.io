pronunciation

noun
manner of uttering words
1.0
Pronunciation: / pɹəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən /; / prəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / pɹəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən /; / prəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the way in which a word or language is spoken, especially as considered correct or accepted
