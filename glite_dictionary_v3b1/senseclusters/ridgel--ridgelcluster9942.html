ridgel

noun
a colt with undescended testicles
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪdʒ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈrɪdʒ.əl/ (BrE)
A male horse (colt) whose testicles have not fully descended, often causing incomplete castration or persistent stallion-like behavior.

Examples

A ridgel horse
Because the ridgel was not fully castrated, he still showed signs of being a stallion.
Owners sometimes encounter difficulties when trying to geld a ridgel.
The vet discovered the colt was a ridgel, which explained his aggressive behavior.
