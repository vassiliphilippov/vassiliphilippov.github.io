sudorific

adjective
causing sweating
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdəˈrɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəˈrɪfɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Ginger tea is considered a natural sudorific in herbal medicine.
