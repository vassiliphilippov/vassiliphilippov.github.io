electrodiagnostic

adjective
using electrical diagnosis
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌdaɪəɡˈnɑstɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the use of electrical techniques to diagnose medical conditions or assess bodily functions

Examples

Electrodiagnostic tests
The neurologist ordered several electrodiagnostic procedures to evaluate the patient's nerve damage.
Advances in electrodiagnostic technology have improved the accuracy of muscle disorder diagnoses.
Many hospitals now offer electrodiagnostic testing as part of their standard neurological assessments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagnostic - relating to identifying a disease or condition
