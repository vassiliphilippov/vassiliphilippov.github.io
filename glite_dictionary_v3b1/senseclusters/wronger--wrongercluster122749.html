wronger

adjective
more incorrect (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔŋər / (AmE), / ˈrɒŋə / (BrE)
more incorrect or less accurate than something else, used informally as the comparative of 'wrong'.

Examples

An even wronger answer
If you think that answer is bad, the next one is even wronger.
She laughed and said his explanation was somehow wronger than before.
The first guess was off, but the second was much wronger.

  • antonym: right - correct or accurate
