keep one's options open

verb
not commit to one choice
4.2 0.46/m
Pronunciation: / kip w ånz Àà…ëp É…ônz Àào äp…ôn / (AmE), / kiÀêp w ånz Àà…íp É…ônz Àà…ô äp…ôn / (BrE)
to avoid committing to a particular course of action or decision so that other choices remain possible

Examples

To keep one's options open in business

It's best to keep your options open until all the offers are on the table.
