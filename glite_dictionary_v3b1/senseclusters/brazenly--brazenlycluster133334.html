brazenly

adverb
in a bold and shameless way
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪzənli / (AmE), / ˈbreɪzənli / (BrE)
In a bold and shameless manner, without embarrassment or hesitation.

Examples

To brazenly ignore rules
She walked brazenly into the meeting without an invitation.
The politician brazenly denied any involvement, despite clear evidence to the contrary.
The thief brazenly stole the wallet in front of several witnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shamelessly - without embarrassment or guilt; in a shameless fashion
  • antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking confidence manner; opposite of boldly
