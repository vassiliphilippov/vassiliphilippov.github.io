wood hoopoe

noun
African bird with metallic blackish plumage
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈhuːˌpoʊ / (AmE), / wʊd ˈhuːˌpuː / (BrE)
a tropical African bird with metallic blackish feathers and lacking a crest, belonging to the family Phoeniculidae.

Examples

The wood hoopoe bird
The wood hoopoe is easily recognized by its iridescent plumage and long tail.
Unlike the true hoopoe, the wood hoopoe does not have a crest.
