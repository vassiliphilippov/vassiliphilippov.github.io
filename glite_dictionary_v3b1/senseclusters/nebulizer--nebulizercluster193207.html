nebulizer

noun
device for spraying fine mist
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛbjəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a device that turns a liquid, such as medicine or perfume, into a fine mist for spraying or inhalation

Examples

A nebulizer machine
My allergies improved after using a nebulizer filled with saline solution.
She bought a small nebulizer for spraying her favorite perfume.
The nurse demonstrated how to use the nebulizer to deliver the medication.
Many asthma patients keep a nebuliser at home for emergencies.
She bought a portable nebuliser for her travels.
The nebuliser delivered the medicine directly into her lungs as a gentle mist.
