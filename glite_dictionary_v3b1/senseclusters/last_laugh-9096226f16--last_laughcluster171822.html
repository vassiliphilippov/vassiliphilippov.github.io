last laugh

noun
ultimate triumph after initial failure
5.3
Pronunciation: / læst læf / (AmE), / lɑːst lɑːf / (BrE)
the final success or triumph after initial failure or doubt, especially when it reverses others' expectations

Examples

Have the last laugh
After years of criticism, the scientist had the last laugh when his theories were finally proven correct.
Despite losing the early rounds, the underdog team had the last laugh by winning the championship.
She endured all the ridicule but ended up with a promotion and the last laugh.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defeat - failure to win or succeed
  • synonym: victory - success in a contest or struggle
