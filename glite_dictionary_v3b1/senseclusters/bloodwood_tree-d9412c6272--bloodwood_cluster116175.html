bloodwood tree

noun
South African tree with red sap and durable wood
9.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbl ådÀåw äd tri / (AmE), / Ààbl ådÀåw äd triÀê / (BrE)
A deciduous South African tree with large pinnate leaves, fragrant orange-yellow flowers, and red sap; valued for its heavy, strong, durable wood.

Examples

African bloodwood tree
Carpenters often seek out bloodwood trees for high-quality furniture making.
The bloodwood tree is known for its striking red sap and durable timber.
The fragrant flowers of the bloodwood tree attract bees and other pollinators.

Synonyms

    9.9 0.00/m

  • kiaat
    9.9 0.00/m
