bloodwood tree

noun
South African tree with red sap and durable wood
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌwʊd tri / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌwʊd triː / (BrE)
A deciduous South African tree with large pinnate leaves, fragrant orange-yellow flowers, and red sap; valued for its heavy, strong, durable wood.

Examples
