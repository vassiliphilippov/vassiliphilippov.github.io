commercial

adjective
of a quality suitable for commerce; often average or inferior
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝːʃəl / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl / (BrE)
(of goods or products) of a kind or quality suitable for use in commerce, often average or inferior rather than superior.

Examples

Commercial grade
He purchased a commercial variety of seed, which is less expensive but not as high-quality as specialty types.
The store only sells the commercial grade of cleaning products, not the ones for laboratory use.
They used commercial beef in the cafeteria, which some people considered to be of average flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: industrial - of or relating to goods manufactured in quantity for industry
  • antonym: premium - of higher than average quality
