pervertedly

adverb
in a contrary manner
Pronunciation: / pərˈvɜrtɪdli / (AmE), / pəˈvɜːtɪdli / (BrE)
in a deliberately contrary or unreasonable way, especially by doing the opposite of what is sensible or expected

Examples

Pervertedly ignores advice
He pervertedly ignored all the advice given to him, choosing the most difficult path instead.
She pervertedly argued against every suggestion, even when it was clearly in her best interest.
The child pervertedly did the opposite of what his parents asked, just to be difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrarily - in an opposite or stubborn manner
  • synonym: obstinately - in a stubborn or unyielding way
