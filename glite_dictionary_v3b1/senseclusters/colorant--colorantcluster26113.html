colorant

noun
substance for coloring
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlərənt / (BrE)
a substance that is added to something to give it color, such as in food, fabric, paint, or cosmetics

Examples

A colorant in paint
