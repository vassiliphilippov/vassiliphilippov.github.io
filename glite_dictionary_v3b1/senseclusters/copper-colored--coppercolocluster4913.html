copper-colored

adjective
having the color of copper
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpɚˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈkɒpəˌkʌləd / (BrE)

Examples

The bird's copper-colored feathers shimmered in the sunlight.
The statue had a copper-colored patina from years of exposure to the elements.
