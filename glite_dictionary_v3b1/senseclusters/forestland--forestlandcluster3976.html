forestland

noun
land with forests
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrɪstˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪstˌlænd / (BrE)
land that is covered with forests or set aside for the growth and management of trees

Examples

Protected forestland
The government is working to protect more forestland from illegal logging.
Many animals depend on forestland for their natural habitat and food sources.
Farmers sometimes clear forestland to create new fields for crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodland - land covered with trees and shrubs
