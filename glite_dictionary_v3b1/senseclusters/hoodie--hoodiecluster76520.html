hoodie

noun
informal young person
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdi / (AmE), / ˈhʊdi / (BrE)
an informal term for a young person, often male, who wears a hooded sweatshirt and may be viewed with suspicion

Examples

A teenage hoodie
The police questioned a hoodie who was seen near the entrance late at night.
Some people unfairly assume that anyone dressed as a hoodie is up to no good.
The local paper ran a story about a group of hoodies volunteering at the community center.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: delinquent - a young person who shows antisocial or criminal behavior
  • synonym: youth - a young person, especially a teenager
