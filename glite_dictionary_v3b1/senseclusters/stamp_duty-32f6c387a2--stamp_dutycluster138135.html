stamp duty

noun
tax on legal documents requiring a stamp
5.6 3.9
Pronunciation: /stæmp ˈduː.tiː/ /; / /stæmp ˈdjuː.tiː/ (AmE), /stæmp ˈdjuː.tiː/ /; / /stæmp ˈdʒuː.tiː/ /; / /stæmp ˈduː.tiː/ (BrE)
