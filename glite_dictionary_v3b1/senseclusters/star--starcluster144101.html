star

noun
network topology with components connected to a hub
Pronunciation: / stɑr / (AmE), / stɑː / (BrE)
a shape, symbol, or object with points radiating from a center, such as a star shape, an asterisk (*), or a central hub in a network

Examples

If the hub fails in a star topology, the entire network is affected.

He wore a gold star on his uniform as a badge of honor.
The American flag features a white star for each state.
Each footnote in the document is indicated by a star.
Please mark the correct answer with a star on the sheet.
She used a star to highlight the important terms in her notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asterisk - the typographical symbol (*) used in text
  • antonym: bus - a network topology where each node is connected to a single communication line
  • synonym: pentagram - a five-pointed star-shaped figure, often used as a symbol
  • antonym: ring - a network topology where each component is connected to two others, forming a closed loop
