Christian soldier

noun
devoted Christian serving faith or values
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstʃən ˈsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
A devoted follower of Christianity who actively serves or defends Christian values, often through acts of service or dedication.

Examples

A Christian soldier of faith
He strived to live every day as a Christian soldier, guided by his faith and convictions.
Many Christian soldiers volunteer their time to support the needy in their communities.
The congregation admired her as a true Christian soldier who dedicated her life to helping others.
