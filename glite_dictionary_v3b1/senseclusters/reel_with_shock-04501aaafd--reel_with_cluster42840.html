reel with shock

verb
to be stunned or overwhelmed by shock
3.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /riːl wɪð ʃɑːk/ /; / /riːl wɪθ ʃɑːk/ /; / /riːl wɪð ʃɔk/ (AmE), /riːl wɪð ʃɒk/ /; / /riːl wɪθ ʃɒk/ /; / /ˈriː.əl wɪð ʃɒk/ (BrE)
to be so shocked or surprised by something that you are temporarily unable to react or think clearly
