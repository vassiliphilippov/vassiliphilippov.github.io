dining-room table

noun
table used for dining
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
a table, usually placed in a dining room, on which meals are served and eaten

Examples

A dining-room table setting
She placed a fresh bouquet of flowers in the center of the dining-room table.
The family gathered around the dining-room table for their Sunday dinner.
We bought a new dining-room table to fit all our guests.
