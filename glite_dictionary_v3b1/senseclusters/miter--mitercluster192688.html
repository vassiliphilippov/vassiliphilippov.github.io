miter

verb
to fit pieces together at an angle (miter joint)
7.3 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈmɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈmɪ.tə/ (BrE)
to join or bevel two pieces (especially wood or metal) by cutting their ends at an angle so they fit together
