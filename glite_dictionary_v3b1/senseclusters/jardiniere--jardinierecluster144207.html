jardiniere

noun
vegetable side dish
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʒɑːrdnˈjɛr / (AmE), / ˌʒɑːdɪnˈjɛə / (BrE)
a mixture of small, cooked vegetables served as a garnish or side dish

Examples

Jardiniere vegetables
The chef prepared a colorful jardiniere to accompany the roasted lamb.

A traditional jardiniere often includes carrots, peas, and green beans.
