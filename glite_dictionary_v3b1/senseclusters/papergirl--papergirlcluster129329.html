papergirl

noun
artwork distributor
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a person, usually a girl or young woman, who participates in the Papergirl art project by collecting and distributing small artworks to the public, often by bicycle

Examples

Papergirl art project
As a papergirl, she cycled through the city handing out rolled-up pieces of art to strangers.
The papergirl smiled as she tossed colorful drawings into the crowd during the festival.
Many artists thanked the papergirl for sharing their work with the public in such a creative way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paperboy - a boy who delivers newspapers to people's homes
