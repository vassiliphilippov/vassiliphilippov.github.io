like a zombie

adverb
dazed or unresponsive manner
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə ˈzɑːmbi / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈzɒmbi / (BrE)
in a dazed, unresponsive, or automatic manner, as if lacking awareness or energy

Examples

Like a zombie walking
After staying up all night, she moved through her morning chores like a zombie.
He answered my questions like a zombie, barely aware of what was happening.
Jet lagged and exhausted, they wandered the airport like a zombie until their flight was called.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zombie - a person or character in a state of lifelessness or mindless obedience
