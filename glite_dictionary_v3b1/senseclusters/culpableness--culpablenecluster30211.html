culpableness

noun
state of being blameworthy
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlpəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌlpəblnəs / (BrE)
The state of being deserving of blame or responsibility for a wrongdoing; blameworthiness.
