unchurched

adjective
not attending church
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɜrtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɜːtʃt / (BrE)
not attending church or not belonging to a religious group or community

Examples

Unchurched adults
The survey found that a growing number of unchurched adults are interested in spiritual topics.
Programs were created to reach out to the unchurched members of the community.
He grew up in an unchurched family and learned about religion later in life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: religious - actively practicing or belonging to a religion
  • synonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
