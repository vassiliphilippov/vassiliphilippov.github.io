clusia

noun
tropical tree or shrub of the genus Clusia
Pronunciation: / ˈkluːʒə / (AmE), / ˈkluːzɪə / (BrE)
a tropical tree or shrub of the genus Clusia, known for its thick leaves and large white, yellow, or pink aromatic flowers

Examples

A clusia tree
A mature clusia can provide dense shade with its broad leaves.
The clusia is popular in landscaping for its tolerance to high heat and salt.
We admired the pink blossoms of the clusia during our walk in the botanical garden.
