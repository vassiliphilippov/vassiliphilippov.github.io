Cercospora

noun
genus of parasitic fungi affecting plants
Pronunciation: / sɚˌkɑːrˈspɔːrə /; / sɜrˌkɑːˈspɔrə / (AmE), / sɜːˌkɔːˈspɔːrə /; / sɜːˌkɒˈspɔːrə / (BrE)
a genus of imperfect fungi that are leaf parasites with long, slender spores, often causing leaf spot diseases in plants
