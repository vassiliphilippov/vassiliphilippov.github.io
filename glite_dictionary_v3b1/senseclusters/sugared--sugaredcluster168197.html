sugared

adjective
with sugar or sweetener added
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌɡɚd / (AmE), / ˈsʌɡəd / (BrE)
having had sugar or a sweetener added; sweetened with sugar.

Examples

Sugared doughnut
He reached for a sugared doughnut during the meeting.
She always prefers her coffee sugared, not black.
The sugared strawberries were perfect for topping the dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweetened - made sweet by adding sugar or another sweetener
  • antonym: unsweetened - without sugar or sweetener added
