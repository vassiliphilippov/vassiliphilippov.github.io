front

noun
organized group sharing a political aim
Pronunciation: / frʌnt / (AmE), / frʌnt / (BrE)
an organized group sharing a particular political or ideological aim, often acting as the public face of a movement or cause

Examples

A political front
A new political front emerged to challenge the government.
He founded the front that rallied for national independence.
Several organizations joined under the same front to promote environmental reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: movement - large group of people pursuing a common cause or ideology
