NSU

noun
inflammation of the urethra, unknown cause
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɛn ɛs ju / (AmE), / ɛn ɛs ju / (BrE)
inflammation of the urethra without a specific identified cause, especially not caused by gonorrhea

Examples

NSU infection
Cases of NSU are often treated with antibiotics even when the exact cause is unknown.
Patients with NSU may experience pain or discomfort while urinating.
The doctor diagnosed him with NSU after ruling out other common infections.
