gridded

adjective
covered with a grid
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪdɪd / (BrE)
fitted with or covered by a grid or lattice, often for protection or structural support

Examples

Maps often use a gridded layout to help users find locations easily.
