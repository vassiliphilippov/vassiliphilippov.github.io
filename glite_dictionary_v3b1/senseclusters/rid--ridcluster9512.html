rid

verb
make free from something unwanted
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪd / (AmE), / rɪd / (BrE)
To make someone or something free from an unwanted person or thing.

Examples

To rid bad habits
She struggled to rid herself of her bad habits.
The company hired professionals to rid the building of mold and pests.
We need to rid the office of unnecessary paperwork before the inspection.
