question

noun
doubt or uncertainty
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of doubt or uncertainty about the truth, validity, or existence of something

Examples

Question of trust
Her honesty was never in question among her colleagues.
The evidence removed any question of his guilt.
There is some question about whether the painting is genuine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certainty - something that is definitely true or is known to be true
  • synonym: doubt - a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction
