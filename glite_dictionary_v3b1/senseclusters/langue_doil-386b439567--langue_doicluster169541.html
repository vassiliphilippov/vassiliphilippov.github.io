langue d'oil

noun
medieval French dialects of northern France
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɑ̃ɡ də ˈwiːl / (AmE), / lɑ̃ɡ də ˈwiːl / (BrE)
a group of medieval dialects of French spoken in central and northern France, which later developed into standard French

Examples
