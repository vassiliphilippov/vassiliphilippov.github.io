Fleming

proper noun
Scottish discoverer of penicillin
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
(Sir Alexander Fleming) a Scottish bacteriologist who discovered penicillin, revolutionizing the treatment of bacterial infections.
