consumerist

noun
person focused on consumption
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmərɪst / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmərɪst / (BrE)
a person who strongly values buying and owning goods, often prioritizing material possessions

Examples

A consumerist buys
As a proud consumerist, he always wanted the newest phone on the market.
The documentary criticized the consumerist mindset that encourages constant shopping.
Many consumerists believe that buying more things leads to greater happiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: materialist - a person who values material possessions and physical comfort
  • antonym: minimalist - a person who prefers to own as few possessions as possible
