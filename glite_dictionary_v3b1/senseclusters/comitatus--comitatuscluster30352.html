comitatus

noun
group of attendants
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊməˈteɪtəs /; / ˌkoʊmɪˈtɑːtəs / (AmE), / ˌkɒmɪˈtɑːtəs / (BrE)
a group of loyal followers, attendants, or warriors who support and protect an important person, especially in historical or early Germanic contexts
