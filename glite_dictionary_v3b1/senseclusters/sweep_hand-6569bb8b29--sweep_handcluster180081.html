sweep hand

noun
second hand on a clock or watch
Pronunciation: / swip hænd / (AmE), / swiːp hænd / (BrE)
the long hand on a clock or watch that indicates seconds, usually moving smoothly around the dial

Examples

The sweep hand moves
He glanced at the sweep hand to start his stopwatch at exactly the right moment.
On his new wristwatch, the sweep hand moved smoothly instead of ticking.
The sweep hand makes it easy to time events to the second.
