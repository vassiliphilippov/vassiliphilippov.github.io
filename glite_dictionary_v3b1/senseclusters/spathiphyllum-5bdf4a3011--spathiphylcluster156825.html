Spathiphyllum

noun
genus of tropical perennials (peace lily)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌspæθɪˈfɪləm / (AmE), / ˌspæθɪˈfɪlɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen, rhizomatous perennial plants native to tropical regions of the Americas, the Philippines, and Indonesia, commonly grown as houseplants for their glossy leaves and white, hoodlike flowers.
