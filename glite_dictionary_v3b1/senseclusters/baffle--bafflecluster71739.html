baffle

verb
puzzle or confuse
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbæfəl / (AmE), / ˈbæfəl / (BrE)

Examples

To baffle the audience

The detective was baffled by the mysterious disappearance of the jewelry.
The tricky math problem completely baffled the students in the class.
