mammee

noun
tropical fruit with thick rind and yellow or reddish flesh
Pronunciation: / mæˈmi / (AmE), / mæˈmiː / (BrE)
A globular or ovoid tropical fruit with a thick, russet, leathery rind and juicy yellow or reddish flesh, native to the Caribbean and Central America.

Examples

A ripe mammee

The sweet, fragrant flesh of the mammee is often eaten fresh or made into preserves.
