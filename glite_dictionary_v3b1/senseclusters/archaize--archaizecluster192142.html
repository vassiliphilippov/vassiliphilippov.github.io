archaize

verb
make archaic in appearance or style
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrkeɪˌaɪz /; / ɑːrˈkeɪ.aɪz / (AmE), / ˈɑːkɪˌaɪz /; / ˈɑː.keɪ.aɪz / (BrE)
to give something an archaic or old-fashioned appearance or character

Examples

To archaize a design
The artist tried to archaize the building's design by using medieval motifs.
Their crafts deliberately archaize objects to make them appear centuries old.
