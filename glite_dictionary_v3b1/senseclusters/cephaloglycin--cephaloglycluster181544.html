cephaloglycin

noun
antibiotic related to cephalosporins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛf.ə.loʊˈɡlaɪ.sɪn / (AmE), / ˌsɛf.ə.ləʊˈɡlɪ.sɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic related to cephalosporins, formerly used to treat bacterial infections but now largely discontinued
