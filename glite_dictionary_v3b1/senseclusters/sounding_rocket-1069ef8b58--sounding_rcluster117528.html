sounding rocket

noun
research rocket for atmospheric data collection
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndɪŋ ˌrɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndɪŋ ˌrɒkɪt / (BrE)
a rocket designed to carry scientific instruments into the upper atmosphere for research purposes, typically to measure atmospheric properties at various altitudes before returning to Earth

Examples

A sounding rocket launch
A sounding rocket can reach altitudes that weather balloons cannot, providing valuable scientific information.
Several sounding rockets were used to gather data during the solar eclipse.
The researchers launched a sounding rocket to study the chemical composition of the upper atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: research rocket - rocket used for atmospheric or scientific research
