refi

noun
loan refinancing
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈriːfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈriːfaɪ / (BrE)
an act of refinancing a loan, or the new loan that replaces an existing one, usually to get better terms

Examples

A refi loan
They decided to get a refi when interest rates dropped significantly.
The bank approved her refi application within a week.
After three years, they did a refi to take advantage of lower interest rates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loan - money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back with interest
  • synonym: refinancing - the process of replacing an existing loan with a new one
