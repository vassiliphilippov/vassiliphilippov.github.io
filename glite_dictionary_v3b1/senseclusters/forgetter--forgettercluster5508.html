forgetter

noun
person who forgets
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛtər / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a person who forgets things, especially often or easily

Examples

A forgetter of names
My brother is a real forgetter and always misplaces his phone.
As a forgetter, she needs to write reminders for everything.
The teacher joked that every class has at least one forgetter who never brings their homework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absentminded person - someone who is often forgetful or distracted
  • antonym: rememberer - a person who remembers things well
