winter crookneck squash

noun
type of winter squash with curved neck
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈkrʊk.nɛk skwɑʃ /; / ˈwɪntər ˈkrʊknɛk skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈkrʊk.nɛk skwɒʃ /; / ˈwɪntə ˈkrʊknɛk skwɒʃ / (BrE)
a type of winter squash with a hard rind and a long, curved neck, typically grown for use in winter
