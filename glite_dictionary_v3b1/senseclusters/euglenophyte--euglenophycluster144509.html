euglenophyte

noun
aquatic flagellate organism of Euglenophyta
8.3
Pronunciation: / juːˈɡliːnəˌfaɪt / (AmE), / juːˈɡliːnəˌfaɪt / (BrE)
a single-celled, usually green or colorless aquatic organism with a flagellum, belonging to the group Euglenophyta

Examples

A euglenophyte cell
A euglenophyte can photosynthesize when light is available, but may also absorb nutrients directly.
Scientists observed the behavior of euglenophyte cells under a microscope.
Some species of euglenophyte are found in both freshwater and marine environments.
