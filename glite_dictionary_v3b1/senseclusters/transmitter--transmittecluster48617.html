transmitter

noun
device for sending radio or TV signals
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a device that sends out radio or television signals to be received by receivers at a distance

Examples

Radio transmitter
Engineers repaired the broken transmitter to restore television broadcasts in the area.
Every television station relies on a reliable transmitter to reach its viewers.
The radio transmitter is located on the top of the hill for better signal coverage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: receiver - device that receives radio or TV signals
