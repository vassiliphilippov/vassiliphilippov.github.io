epistemologically

adverb
relating to knowledge theory
Pronunciation: / ɪˌpɪstəməˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌpɪstɪməˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the theory of knowledge or the study of how we know things

Examples

Studied epistemologically
The theory was examined epistemologically to determine how knowledge is justified.
She approached the problem epistemologically, questioning the basis of all assumptions.
The debate was framed epistemologically to focus on the nature and limits of human understanding.
