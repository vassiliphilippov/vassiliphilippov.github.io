treat

noun
a special or sweet food enjoyed occasionally
Pronunciation: / trit /; / tɹit / (AmE), / triːt /; / tɹiːt / (BrE)
something special or enjoyable, such as a sweet food or a pleasant experience

Examples

A birthday treat
As a birthday treat, we went out for ice cream.
Fresh strawberries dipped in chocolate are always a delicious treat.
She brought home some chocolate treats for the kids.
For her birthday, dinner at the fancy restaurant was a real treat.
It was a treat to watch the sunrise over the mountains.
The children received a sweet treat after finishing their homework.
