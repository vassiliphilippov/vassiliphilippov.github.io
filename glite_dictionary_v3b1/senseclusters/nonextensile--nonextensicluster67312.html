nonextensile

adjective
not able to be stretched or extended
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.ɪkˈstɛn.saɪl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.ɪkˈstɛn.saɪl / (BrE)
Not able to be stretched or extended; not extensile.

Examples

Nonextensile fabric
Because the fabric is nonextensile, it does not deform under tension.
Some plant fibers are nonextensile and thus provide rigidity to the stem.
The nonextensile nature of the cable makes it ideal for secure installations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extensile - capable of being extended or stretched
