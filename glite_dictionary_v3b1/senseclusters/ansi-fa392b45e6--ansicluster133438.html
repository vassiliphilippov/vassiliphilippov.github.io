ANSI

adjective
meeting US standards
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈænsi / (AmE), / ˈænsi / (BrE)
conforming to standards set by the American National Standards Institute, especially in technical or industrial contexts

Examples

ANSI certified equipment
All safety equipment in the lab must be ANSI certified to ensure proper protection.
The new keyboard layout follows the ANSI standard used in the United States.
Before installation, check if the lighting fixtures are ANSI approved for this type of building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonstandard - not conforming to an accepted standard
  • synonym: standardized - made to conform to a standard
