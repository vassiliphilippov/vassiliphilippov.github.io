sunbath

noun
exposure to sunlight
2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌn.bæθ/ (AmE), /ˈsʌn.bɑːθ/ (BrE)
the act of sitting or lying in the sun, usually to get warm or to tan the skin

Examples

A relaxing sunbath
After swimming, he took a long sunbath on the warm sand.
Doctors recommend limiting the length of a sunbath to protect your skin.
She enjoys a quiet sunbath in her garden every morning.
