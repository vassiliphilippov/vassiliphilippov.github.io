conceptualist

adjective
focused on ideas
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst / (AmE), / kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to conceptualism or emphasizing ideas and concepts over visual or sensory qualities, especially in art.

Examples

Conceptualist art style
The conceptualist approach in her paintings challenges viewers to think about the meaning behind the work.
Many modern galleries feature conceptualist installations that prioritize ideas over traditional aesthetics.
His conceptualist style often leaves audiences debating the artist's intended message.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceptual - based on ideas or concepts rather than physical forms
  • antonym: realist - emphasizing the depiction of things as they actually are
