StairMaster

noun
stair-climbing machine
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈsteəˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a stair-climbing exercise machine, often used to refer to any similar stepper regardless of brand

Examples

A StairMaster workout
She spends thirty minutes on the StairMaster every morning to improve her fitness.
The gym's new StairMaster has digital displays and adjustable resistance levels.
After injuring his knee, he switched from the treadmill to the StairMaster for low-impact exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stepper - an exercise machine that simulates climbing stairs
  • synonym: treadmill - an exercise machine for walking or running in place
