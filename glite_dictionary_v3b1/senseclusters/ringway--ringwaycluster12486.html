ringway

noun
road encircling a city or town
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.weɪ / (BrE)
A road or highway that goes around the edge of a town or city to divert traffic from the city center; a ring road.

Examples

The ringway road
The ringway allows trucks to bypass residential neighborhoods entirely.
They built a new ringway to ease congestion in the city center.
Traffic on the ringway moves faster than through the town itself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ring road - a main road which goes around the edge of a town, used to reduce traffic in the center
