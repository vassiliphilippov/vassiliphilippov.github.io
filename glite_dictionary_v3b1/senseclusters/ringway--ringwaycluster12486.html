A road or highway that goes around the edge of a town or city to divert traffic from the city center; a ring road.

Examples

The ringway road
The ringway allows trucks to bypass residential neighborhoods entirely.
They built a new ringway to ease congestion in the city center.
Traffic on the ringway moves faster than through the town itself.

Synonyms

  • ring road
    3.2 0.1/m
    ring road is a main road that circles a town and carries traffic away from the center, while ringway names the same road.
  • beltway
    4.5 4/m
    beltway is an American term for a highway circling a city to let traffic avoid the center, while ringway is a general term for that road.
  • beltline
    4.3 0.4/m
    beltline often means a road or rail link around town that connects areas, while ringway means a ring road to divert traffic.
  • bypass
    3.8 0.6/m
    bypass is a road built to let traffic go around a town without entering it, while ringway is a full circular road around the city.
