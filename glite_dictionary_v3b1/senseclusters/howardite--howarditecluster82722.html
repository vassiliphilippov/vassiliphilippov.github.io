howardite

noun
stony mixed meteorite
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊərˌdaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊədaɪt /; / ˈhaʊədˌaɪt / (BrE)
a stony meteorite made of mixed rock fragments, often from different types of rocks or meteorites (especially from asteroid 4 Vesta)
