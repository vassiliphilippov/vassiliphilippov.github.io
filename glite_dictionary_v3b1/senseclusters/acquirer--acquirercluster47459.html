acquirer

noun
person or entity that acquires something
Pronunciation: / əˈkwaɪərər / (AmE), / əˈkwaɪərə / (BrE)
a person or company that acquires or gains possession of something, especially by purchase or investment

Examples

The acquirer of shares
In a merger, the acquirer often dictates the terms of the deal.
The acquirer of the company must adhere to regulatory requirements.
The museum became the new acquirer of the rare artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buyer - a person who purchases something; purchaser
  • antonym: seller - a person who sells something
