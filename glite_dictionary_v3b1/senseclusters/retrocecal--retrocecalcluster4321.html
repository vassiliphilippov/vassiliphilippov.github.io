retrocecal

adjective
behind the cecum
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊˈsiːkəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈsiːkəl / (BrE)
located behind the cecum, especially describing the position of the appendix in the abdomen

Examples

Retrocecal appendix
A retrocecal appendix can make diagnosis of appendicitis more difficult.
The surgeon noted that the patient's appendix was in a retrocecal position.
Pain from a retrocecal appendix may be less localized than usual.
