labyrinthine

adjective
resembling a labyrinth; very complex
9.0 7.3
Pronunciation: /læ.bər.ˈɪn.θiːn/ /; / /læ.bər.ˈɪn.θaɪn/ (AmE), /læ.bə.ˈrɪn.θaɪn/ /; / /læ.bə.ˈrɪn.θiːn/ (BrE)
