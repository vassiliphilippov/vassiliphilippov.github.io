retired

adjective
no longer working; stopped employment
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪərd / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Having stopped working in your job or profession, usually because of age.

Examples

Retired from work
After thirty years as a teacher, she finally became retired last summer.
My father is retired and spends most of his time gardening now.
The company offers special discounts to retired employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pensioner - person who has retired and receives a pension
  • antonym: working - still employed, not retired
