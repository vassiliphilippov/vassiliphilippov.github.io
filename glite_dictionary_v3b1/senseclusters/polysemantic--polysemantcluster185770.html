polysemantic

adjective
having multiple meanings (of a word)
4.1 7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːli səˈmænɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒli sɪˈmænɪk / (BrE)
(of a word) having several different meanings.

Examples

A polysemantic word
A polysemantic term can cause confusion if the context is not clear.
Some linguists study polysemantic words to understand language development.
The English word 'bank' is polysemantic, as it can refer to a financial institution or the side of a river.

Related Terms
