norethandrolone

noun
synthetic progestational hormone
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɔːˌrɛθænˈdroʊloʊn / (AmE), / nɔːˌrɛθænˈdrəʊləʊn / (BrE)
a synthetic progestational hormone used in oral contraceptives and in the treatment of endometriosis

Examples

Norethandrolone hormone treatment
Norethandrolone is sometimes prescribed to help manage symptoms of endometriosis.
Doctors evaluate the risks and benefits before recommending norethandrolone therapy.
The contraceptive pill contains norethandrolone as one of its active ingredients.
