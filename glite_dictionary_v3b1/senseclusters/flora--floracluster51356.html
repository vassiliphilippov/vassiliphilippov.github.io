flora

noun
plant life of a region or period
4.8 3.66/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfl…îr.…ô / (AmE), / Ààfl…îÀê.r…ô / (BrE)
all the plant life of a particular region, habitat, or geological period

Examples

Native flora
Human activity has had a significant impact on the native flora of many islands.
Researchers are cataloging the flora found in Arctic tundra regions.
The Amazon rainforest is known for its incredibly diverse flora.

Synonyms

  • plant life
    2.8 1.04/m
    plant life is the everyday phrase for all plants in an area, while flora is more formal and used by scientists.
  • vegetation
    4.1 14/m
    vegetation refers to plant cover and plant communities in an area, while flora names all plant life of a region.
