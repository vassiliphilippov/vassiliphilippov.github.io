cyclocross

noun
bicycle for cyclocross
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkləˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkləˌkrɒs / (BrE)
a type of bicycle designed for cyclocross racing, featuring a lightweight frame, wider tires, and clearance for mud

Examples

A cyclocross bicycle
He raced through the muddy course on his new cyclocross during the autumn competition.
A cyclocross is ideal for riders who want to tackle both paved and rough terrain.
She cleaned her cyclocross thoroughly after the challenging race in the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountain bike - a bicycle designed for off-road cycling
  • antonym: road bike - a lightweight bicycle designed for speed on paved roads
