showbiz

adjective
entertainment industry style
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌbɪz / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌbɪz / (BrE)
relating to the entertainment industry or having a flashy, commercial style typical of show business

Examples

Showbiz style
The awards ceremony had a showbiz atmosphere with bright lights and famous celebrities.
She prefers a more serious approach to acting, avoiding the showbiz lifestyle.
The new musical is full of showbiz glamour and energetic dance numbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entertainment - the business of providing performances or amusements
  • synonym: theatrical - relating to the theater or dramatic performance
