laksa

noun
spicy noodle soup
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːksə / (AmE), / ˈlæksə / (BrE)
a spicy noodle soup made with rice noodles, coconut milk, and various toppings, popular in Malaysia and Singapore

Examples

A bowl of laksa
She enjoyed a steaming bowl of laksa filled with shrimp and fresh herbs.
Many travelers seek out authentic laksa when visiting Singapore or Malaysia.
The restaurant's signature dish is their spicy coconut laksa with chicken and tofu.
