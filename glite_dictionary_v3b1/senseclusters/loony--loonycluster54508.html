loony

adjective
crazy; mentally irregular (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈluːni / (AmE), / ˈluːni / (BrE)
Crazy or mentally unsound; behaving in a way that is strange or eccentric, especially as an informal or slang term.

Examples

A loony idea
Everyone thought her plan was loony, but she proved them wrong.
He acted a bit loony after staying up all night.
That movie is full of loony characters and wild plot twists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - mentally deranged; insane or eccentric
  • antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill
