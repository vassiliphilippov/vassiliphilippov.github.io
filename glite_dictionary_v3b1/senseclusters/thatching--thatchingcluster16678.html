thatching

noun
roofing with straw
Pronunciation: / ˈθætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the material or process used to make a roof from straw, reeds, or similar plant material

Examples

Traditional thatching material
The old barn was repaired using traditional thatching techniques passed down through generations.
Experts in thatching are becoming rare as modern roofing materials replace older methods.
The golden color of the new thatching made the cottage stand out in the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roofing - the materials or process used to cover a roof
  • antonym: tiling - the process of covering a roof with tiles
