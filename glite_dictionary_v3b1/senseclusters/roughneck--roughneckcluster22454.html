roughneck

noun
a rough or brutal person
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrʌfnɛk / (BrE)
a rough, aggressive, or brutal person, especially one who is unruly or violent

Examples

A roughneck bully
Despite his roughneck exterior, he had a surprisingly kind heart.
He was regarded as a bit of a roughneck during his youth, always getting into trouble.
The town feared the arrival of the notorious roughneck who often started fights in the saloon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • antonym: gentleman - a man of good, courteous conduct
  • synonym: ruffian - a violent or lawless person
