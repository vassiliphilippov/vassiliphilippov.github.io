Vienna roll

noun
small crusty bread roll
3.4
Pronunciation: / viˈɛnə roʊl / (AmE), / viˈɛnə rəʊl / (BrE)
A small, yeast-raised bread roll with a crisp or hard crust, originally associated with Vienna.

Examples

A Vienna roll sandwich
He ordered a ham sandwich on a Vienna roll with mustard and pickles.
The bakery is known for its fresh Vienna rolls every morning.
Would you like your soup served with a Vienna roll or rye bread?
