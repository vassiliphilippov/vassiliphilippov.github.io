California roll

noun
sushi roll with avocado and crab
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪfərniə roʊl / (AmE), / kəˈlɪfəniə rəʊl / (BrE)
a type of sushi roll typically made with avocado, crab (or imitation crab), and cucumber, often with rice on the outside

Examples

A California roll sushi
He learned how to make a California roll by watching online videos.
Many people who are new to sushi start with a California roll because of its familiar flavors.
The restaurant's California rolls are topped with sesame seeds for extra flavor.
