transplanter

noun
a person who moves plants to new locations
Pronunciation: / trænsˈplæn.tɚ / (AmE), / trænsˈplæn.tə / (BrE)
a person who moves plants from one place and sets them in another, especially a gardener doing this as part of cultivation

Examples

Expert transplanter
Many homeowners hire a transplanter to relocate their mature shrubs without damaging them.
She worked as a transplanter during the busy spring season at the nursery.
The transplanter carefully dug up the seedlings and placed them in the new bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
