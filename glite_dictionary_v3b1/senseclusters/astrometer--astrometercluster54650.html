astrometer

noun
star position measurer
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɑːmətər / (AmE), / əˈstrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a person who measures the positions, movements, or brightness of stars, especially as a specialist in astrometry

Examples

Professional astrometer
The astrometer carefully recorded the positions of distant stars for the observatory's database.
As an experienced astrometer, she contributed valuable data to the international space project.
Early sailors relied on the work of the astrometer to navigate across the open sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronomer - a scientist who studies stars, planets, and other objects in space
  • synonym: astrophysicist - a scientist who studies the physical properties of celestial bodies
