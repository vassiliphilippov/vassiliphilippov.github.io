ill-fed

adjective
not getting enough food
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈfɛd / (AmE), / ɪlˈfɛd / (BrE)
not having enough food to eat; inadequately nourished

Examples

An ill-fed child
After months of drought, the villagers became visibly ill-fed and thin.
Many refugees in the camp appeared ill-fed and weak.
The veterinarian was concerned about the ill-fed condition of the stray animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnourished - suffering from poor nutrition
  • synonym: undernourished - not getting enough food or nutrients
  • antonym: well-fed - having enough food to eat
