tufa

noun
soft porous calcium carbonate rock
9.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.fə/ /; / /ˈtjuː.fə/ (AmE), /ˈtjuː.fə/ /; / /ˈtuː.fə/ (BrE)
A soft, porous rock composed of calcium carbonate, formed by precipitation from spring or groundwater rich in lime.
