initiation

noun
entry into a group or office
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɪʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌnɪʃiˈeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the formal act or process of being introduced or admitted into an organization, office, or group, often involving a special ceremony or rite

Examples

Initiation ceremony
After his initiation into the fraternity, David felt like he truly belonged.
Her initiation into the club required answering questions about its history.
The company's initiation ceremony for new employees included a welcome speech and team activities.
