devo

noun
informal for devolution
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdiːvəʊ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for devolution, referring to the transfer of powers from a central government to a regional authority

Examples

Devo of government powers
The new devo agreement will give more control to local councils.
Many politicians are debating the benefits of further devo for the region.
Supporters of devo believe it leads to better representation for local communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devolution - the transfer of power from a central government to regional governments
