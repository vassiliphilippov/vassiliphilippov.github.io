antidemocratic

adjective
opposed to democracy
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntidɛməˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntidɛməˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
opposed to democracy or the principles of democratic government

Examples

Antidemocratic law
The new law was criticized as antidemocratic by international observers.
Many people believe censorship is an antidemocratic practice that limits free speech.
The opposition accused the government of taking antidemocratic actions to stay in power.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democratic - supporting democracy or its principles
  • synonym: undemocratic - not in accordance with democratic principles
