tandem bicycle

noun
bicycle for two riders
Pronunciation: / ˈtændəm ˈbaɪsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtændəm ˈbaɪsɪkl / (BrE)
a bicycle designed for two riders, with two sets of pedals and two seats arranged one behind the other

Examples

A tandem bicycle ride
Renting a tandem bicycle can be a fun way for couples to explore the city.
The tandem bicycle requires good communication and teamwork between both riders.
They rode a tandem bicycle along the country trail, enjoying the scenery together.

  • synonym: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels powered by pedals
