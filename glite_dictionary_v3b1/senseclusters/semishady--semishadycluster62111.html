semishady

adjective
partly shaded
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈʃeɪdi / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈʃeɪdi / (BrE)
partly shaded; receiving some but not complete protection from direct sunlight

Examples

Semishady garden spot
The ferns grew best in the semishady area behind the house.
We set up our picnic table in a semishady spot to avoid the midday heat.
Some flowers prefer a semishady environment rather than full sun or deep shade.
