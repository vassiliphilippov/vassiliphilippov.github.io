spagyric

adjective
alchemical medicine
Pronunciation: / spəˈdʒɪrɪk / (AmE), / spəˈdʒɪrɪk / (BrE)
relating to alchemy, especially the preparation of medicines from plants using alchemical methods

Examples

Spagyric medicine
The ancient text described several spagyric processes for extracting healing properties from plants.
He studied spagyric remedies as part of his research into traditional alchemical practices.
A spagyric preparation often involves fermentation, distillation, and the use of mineral salts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alchemical - relating to alchemy or alchemists
  • synonym: herbal - relating to or made from herbs
