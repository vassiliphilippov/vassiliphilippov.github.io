foodborne

adjective
carried in food
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈfuːdbɔːn / (BrE)
caused by or carried in food, especially referring to illness or disease

Examples

Foodborne illness
The outbreak was traced to a foodborne pathogen found in undercooked meat.
Many foodborne illnesses can be prevented by proper food handling and storage.
Symptoms of foodborne diseases often include nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: airborne - carried or transmitted by the air
  • antonym: waterborne - carried or transmitted by water
