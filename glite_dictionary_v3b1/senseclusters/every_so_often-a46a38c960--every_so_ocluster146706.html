every so often

adverb
occasionally; at intervals
Pronunciation: / ˈɛv.ri soʊ ˈɑːf.ən / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ri səʊ ˈɒf.ən / (BrE)
from time to time; occasionally; at intervals rather than continuously or frequently

Examples

Every so often I walk
Every so often, I like to take a walk in the park to clear my mind.
They have a reunion every so often to catch up with old friends.
You should check your tire pressure every so often for better safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frequently - at short intervals; often
  • synonym: occasionally - at infrequent or irregular intervals
