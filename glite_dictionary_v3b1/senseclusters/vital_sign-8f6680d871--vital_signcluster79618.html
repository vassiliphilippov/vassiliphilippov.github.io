vital sign

noun
key metric of system health
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪtəl saɪn / (AmE), / ˈvaɪtəl saɪn / (BrE)
(in non-medical contexts) a key metric used to indicate the health, viability, or performance of a system, organization, or process.

Examples

A vital sign of success
Uptime is a vital sign of the software's overall performance and user experience.
Customer satisfaction scores are important vital signs for any subscription-based business.
Analysts monitor churn and revenue as vital signs of the company's market health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: health indicator - a measure used to assess the condition or functioning of a system or organization
  • synonym: key performance indicator - a measurable value used to evaluate how effectively an organization or system is achieving objectives
