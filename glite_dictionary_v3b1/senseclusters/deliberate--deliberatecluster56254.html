deliberate

verb
carefully consider or discuss
4.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈlɪ.bə.reɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈlɪ.br.eɪt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈlɪ.bə.reɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈlɪ.br.eɪt/ (BrE)
