profanation

noun
act of desecrating something sacred
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊfəˈneɪʃən /; / proʊfəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɒfəˈneɪʃən /; / prəʊfəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of treating something sacred or respected with disrespect or irreverence
