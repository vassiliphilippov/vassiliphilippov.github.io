Roman building

noun
structure built by ancient Romans
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.mən ˈbɪl.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən ˈbɪl.dɪŋ / (BrE)
a structure built by the ancient Romans, typically characterized by distinctive architectural features such as arches, domes, and vaults

Examples

Ancient Roman building
Archaeologists uncovered the remains of a Roman building near the ancient city walls.

The Pantheon is one of the best-preserved Roman buildings in the world.
