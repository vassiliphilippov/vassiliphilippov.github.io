sell down

verb
reduce holdings by selling assets
3.7
Pronunciation: / sɛl daʊn / (AmE), / sɛl daʊn / (BrE)
to sell a portion of one's holdings or assets, often in order to reduce risk or generate cash

Examples

To sell down assets
In response to new regulations, banks began to sell down their loan portfolios.
The fund manager may sell down positions that have become too large relative to the portfolio.
The investor decided to sell down his stock holdings after the market rally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sell off - to sell all or most of something, especially quickly
