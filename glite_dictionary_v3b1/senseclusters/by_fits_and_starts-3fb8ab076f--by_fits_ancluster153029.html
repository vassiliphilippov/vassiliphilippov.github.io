by fits and starts

adverb
intermittently; at irregular intervals
9.3
Pronunciation: / baɪ fɪts ənd stɑrts / (AmE), / baɪ fɪts ənd stɑːts / (BrE)
at irregular intervals; not continuously or steadily

Examples

By fits and starts all year
My attempts at exercise have been by fits and starts all year.
She studies by fits and starts, never maintaining a steady routine.
The construction progressed by fits and starts due to frequent weather delays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuously - without interruption
  • synonym: sporadically - happening at irregular intervals
