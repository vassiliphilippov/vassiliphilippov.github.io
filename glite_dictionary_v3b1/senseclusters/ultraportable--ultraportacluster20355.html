ultraportable

noun
compact lightweight device
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈpɔːrtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
a very small and lightweight portable electronic device, especially a laptop, designed for easy carrying

Examples

An ultraportable device
She bought an ultraportable for her business trips because it was easy to carry everywhere.
The new ultraportable offers long battery life and fits easily into a backpack.
Many students prefer an ultraportable over a traditional laptop for its convenience and light weight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desktop - a personal computer designed to fit on a desk and not easily moved
  • synonym: laptop - a portable computer suitable for use while traveling
  • synonym: notebook - a small, portable personal computer
