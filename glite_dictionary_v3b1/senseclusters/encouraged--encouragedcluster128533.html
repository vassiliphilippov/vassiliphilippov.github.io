encouraged

adjective
feeling confident or supported
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɜrɪdʒd / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒd / (BrE)
Feeling confident, hopeful, or supported, often as a result of positive feedback or motivation.

Examples

Feeling encouraged by praise
After seeing his improvement, he was encouraged to keep practicing.
She felt encouraged by the positive reviews of her book.
The coach's words left the players encouraged before the big match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confident - feeling self-assured or certain
  • antonym: discouraged - feeling less confident or less hopeful
