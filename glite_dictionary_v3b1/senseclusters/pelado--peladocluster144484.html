pelado

adjective
without skin or peel
Pronunciation: / peˈlaːdoʊ / (AmE), / peˈlaːdəʊ / (BrE)
having had the outer skin or covering removed, especially referring to fruit or vegetables that have been peeled

Examples

Pelado potatoes
The recipe calls for pelado potatoes to ensure a smooth texture in the soup.
She handed him a bowl of pelado grapes, ready to eat.
After washing the carrots, he made sure they were pelado before adding them to the salad.
