arati

noun
Hindu lamp ritual
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrəti / (AmE), / ˈɑːrəti / (BrE)
a Hindu religious ritual in which lighted lamps are waved before a deity as an offering

Examples

Temple arati ceremony
Every morning, the priest performed arati in front of the temple's main idol.
Visitors watched the evening arati as lamps were waved and bells rang.
During the festival, families gathered to participate in the arati ceremony together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puja - a Hindu act of worship or ritual offering to a deity
