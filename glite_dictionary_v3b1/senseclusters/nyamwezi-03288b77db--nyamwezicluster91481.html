Nyamwezi

proper noun
Tanzanian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / njɑːmˈwɛzi / (AmE), / njæmˈwɛzi / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu-speaking ethnic group native to Tanzania

Examples

Nyamwezi people
The Nyamwezi have a long history of trade and migration in central Tanzania.
Many Nyamwezi people continue to celebrate their traditional festivals each year.
Researchers studied the language and customs of the Nyamwezi during their fieldwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sukuma - a neighboring Bantu-speaking ethnic group in Tanzania
