hackberry

noun
tree of the genus Celtis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhækˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhækˌbɛri / (BrE)
Any of several trees of the genus Celtis, typically with inconspicuous flowers and small berrylike fruits.

Examples

A hackberry tree
Birds are attracted to hackberry because of its plentiful berrylike fruits.
Several hackberry trees shaded the edge of the old playground.
The hackberry is valued for its hardy wood and ability to grow in various soil types.
