overindulgence

noun
excessive indulgence
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒəns / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒəns / (BrE)
Excessive or immoderate indulgence, especially in pleasure or appetites.

Examples

His overindulgence in food
His overindulgence in rich foods eventually affected his health.
Parental overindulgence can lead to spoiled children.
The holiday season often leads to overindulgence in sweets and treats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restraint - moderation; self-control
