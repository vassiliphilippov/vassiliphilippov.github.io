ultra-basic

adjective
extremely simple
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈbeɪsɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈbeɪsɪk / (BrE)
extremely simple, lacking features, comfort, or sophistication

Examples

Ultra-basic rooms
The hostel offered only ultra-basic rooms with no private bathrooms or televisions.
We chose the ultra-basic package to save money on our trip.
Her phone is ultra-basic, with no internet or camera features.

Related Terms

  • antonym: luxurious - very comfortable and full of extra features
  • synonym: minimal - very simple or with the least amount possible
