Chagatai language

noun
historical Turkic literary language
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːɡətaɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːɡətaɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Turkic literary language once widely used in Central Asia, particularly from the 15th to early 20th centuries
