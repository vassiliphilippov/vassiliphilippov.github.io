subarctic

adjective
relating to areas just south of the Arctic Circle
9.3 5.8
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈbɑːrk.tɪk/ /; / /sʌb.ˈɑːrk.tɪk/ (AmE), /sə.ˈbɑːk.tɪk/ /; / /sʌb.ˈɑːk.tɪk/ (BrE)
