thoracoepigastric vein

noun
anatomical vein in the side of the trunk
9.9
Pronunciation: /θɔːr.ə.koʊ.ep.ɪ.ˈgæs.trɪk veɪn/ /; / /θə.rə.koʊ.ep.ɪ.ˈgæs.trɪk veɪn/ (AmE), /θɔː.rə.kəʊ.ep.ɪ.ˈgæs.trɪk veɪn/ /; / /θə.rə.kəʊ.ep.ɪ.ˈgæs.trɪk veɪn/ (BrE)
a vein that runs along the side of the trunk, connecting the superficial epigastric vein to the axillary vein or a thoracic vein

Examples

The thoracoepigastric vein connection
