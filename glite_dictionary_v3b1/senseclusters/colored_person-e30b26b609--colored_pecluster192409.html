colored person

noun
dated, offensive US term for a Black person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚd ˌpɚsən /; / ˈkʌlɚd ˌpɝsən / (AmE), / ˈkʌləd ˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
