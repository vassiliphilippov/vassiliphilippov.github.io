dollface

noun
sarcastic term of address
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑlˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɒlˌfeɪs / (BrE)
an informal and sometimes sarcastic term of address used to belittle someone as silly or superficial, especially a woman

Examples

Sarcastic dollface
He called her dollface in front of everyone, making her feel uncomfortable.
When the manager said 'Nice try, dollface,' it was clear he was not being kind.
She rolled her eyes when someone addressed her as dollface during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: idiot - a person considered to be silly or not intelligent
  • synonym: sweetheart - an affectionate term of address for someone you like
