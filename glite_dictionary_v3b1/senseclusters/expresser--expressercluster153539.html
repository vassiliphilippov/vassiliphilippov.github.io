expresser

noun
one who expresses
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛsər / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that expresses something, such as an idea, feeling, or gene

Examples

An expresser of feelings
The artist is an expresser of complex emotions through her paintings.
In genetics, a gene expresser determines which traits are visible in an organism.
As a natural expresser of ideas, she easily shared her thoughts with the group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communicator - a person who conveys information or ideas
  • antonym: suppressor - a person or thing that prevents something from being expressed
