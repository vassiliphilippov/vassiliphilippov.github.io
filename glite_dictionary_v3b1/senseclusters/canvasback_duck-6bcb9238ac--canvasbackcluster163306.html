canvasback duck

noun
species of North American wild duck
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.vəs.bæk dʌk / (AmE), / ˈkæn.vəs.bæk dʌk / (BrE)
A North American wild duck distinguished by its sloping profile and red head, valued for sport and as food.

Examples

A canvasback duck
During migration, large numbers of canvasback ducks gather on freshwater marshes and ponds.
The canvasback duck is prized by hunters for its distinctive flavor and game qualities.
