subnanosecond

adjective
less than a nanosecond
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbnænəˌsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˌsʌbnænəˌsɛkənd / (BrE)
describing a time interval that is shorter than one nanosecond

Examples

Subnanosecond laser pulses
The new laser emits pulses in the subnanosecond range for advanced scientific experiments.
Engineers measured subnanosecond delays in the high-speed communication system.
The device is capable of subnanosecond response times, making it ideal for fast data processing.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: nanosecond - a unit of time equal to one billionth of a second
  • degree lower: picosecond - a unit of time equal to one trillionth of a second
