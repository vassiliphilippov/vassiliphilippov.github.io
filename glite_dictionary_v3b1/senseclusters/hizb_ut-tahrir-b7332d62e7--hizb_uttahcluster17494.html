Hizb ut-Tahrir

proper noun
international Islamic political group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪzb ʊt tæˈhɪr / (AmE), / ˌhɪzb ʊt tæˈhɪə / (BrE)
an international Islamic political organization that advocates for a worldwide Islamic caliphate and promotes its interpretation of 'pure' Islam, particularly active in Central Asia and the Middle East.

Examples

Hizb ut-Tahrir political group
Hizb ut-Tahrir has been banned in several countries due to its political activities.
Authorities have accused Hizb ut-Tahrir of promoting radical ideologies in Central Asia.
The government monitors the activities of Hizb ut-Tahrir closely because of its stated aims.
