babby

noun
dialect word for baby
Pronunciation: / ˈbæbi / (BrE)
a dialect or playful form of 'baby', meaning a very young child or infant

Examples

A sleeping babby
She cradled the babby gently in her arms until he fell asleep.
In some regions, people affectionately call a small child a babby instead of a baby.
The proud parents introduced their newborn babby to the whole family.
