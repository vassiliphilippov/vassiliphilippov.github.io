maintain

verb
provide with necessities and support
1.0
Pronunciation: / meɪnˈteɪn / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪn / (BrE)
to supply someone or something with the necessities and support required to live or to function

Examples

To maintain a family
After his father's death, he had to maintain his entire family on a single salary.
She worked two jobs to maintain her elderly parents and younger siblings.
The charity helps maintain hundreds of orphans each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: support - give assistance or the necessities to someone; provide for
  • synonym: sustain - to provide the necessities for someone or something to continue
