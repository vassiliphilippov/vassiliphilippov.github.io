off-speed pitch

noun
a slower baseball pitch to deceive the batter
7.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îf spiÀêd p…™t É / (AmE), / Àà…íf spiÀêd p…™t É / (BrE)
a baseball pitch thrown at a slower speed than usual to deceive the batter, particularly when the batter is expecting a fastball

Examples

An off-speed pitch
Batters sometimes struggle when they can't anticipate an off-speed pitch.
He mixed in an off-speed pitch after two consecutive fastballs.
The pitcher threw an off-speed pitch to catch the batter off guard.

Synonyms

  • change-of-pace
    8.3 0.01/m
    change-of-pace is a slower pitch used to trick a batter who expects a fastball, while off-speed pitch is the general term for such slower, deceptive throws.
  • changeup
    5.6 0.22/m
    changeup is a specific slow pitch that mimics a fastball to fool the batter, while off-speed pitch names any slower, deceptive pitch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fastball - a baseball pitch thrown at high speed
