pedal

verb
operate pedals on a keyboard instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɛd.əl / (BrE)
to operate the pedals on a keyboard instrument (such as a piano or organ) in order to modify or sustain the sound

Examples

To pedal a piano

The music teacher demonstrated how to pedal softly for a gentle effect.
To sustain the chord, she carefully pedaled throughout the entire passage.
