copyism

noun
exact reproduction practice
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.pi.ɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈkɑː.pi.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈkɒp.i.ɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈkɒp.i.zəm/ (BrE)

Examples

Industrial copyism

Some critics argue that copyism stifles creativity in the design industry.
Strict copyism in publishing ensures that each edition matches the original exactly.
