japanning

noun
decorative glossy coating
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpænɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒəˈpænɪŋ / (BrE)
a hard, glossy black or colored finish applied to metal or wood, especially as an imitation of Asian lacquerwork.

Examples

Decorative japanning finish
The antique tray was decorated with beautiful japanning that had lasted for centuries.
Restorers carefully repaired the damaged japanning on the old metal box.
Collectors value items with original japanning for their historical authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lacquering - the process of applying a protective or decorative lacquer finish
  • synonym: varnishing - the act of applying varnish to a surface for protection or gloss
