overdry

adjective
excessively dry
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdraɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdraɪ / (BrE)
having lost too much moisture; excessively dry.

Examples

Overdry clothes
If you overdry your clothes in the dryer, they can become stiff and uncomfortable to wear.
The cake turned out tough because the oven caused it to become overdry.
Plants may wilt if the soil is allowed to get overdry for too long.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moist - slightly wet or damp
  • synonym: parched - very dry, especially due to heat
