noun
social media subscriber

a person who subscribes to another user's account on a social media platform in order to receive or view their posts and updates.

Examples

A social media follower
She gained thousands of followers after posting the viral travel video on her profile.
He checked his followers to reply to messages from fans before the livestream.
Many creators offer exclusive content to paying followers through subscription features on the platform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subscriber - a person who signs up to receive regular content or updates from a service or creator (often used interchangeably online).
  • synonym: supporter - a person who supports a cause or person; overlaps with 'follower' when following indicates approval or fandom.
