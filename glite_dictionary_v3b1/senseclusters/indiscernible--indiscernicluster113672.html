indiscernible

adjective
impossible or hard to perceive
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈsɜrnəbəl /; / ˌɪn.dɪˈsɝː.nə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈsɜːnəbəl /; / ˌɪn.dɪˈsɜː.nə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to see, hear, or notice clearly

Examples

An indiscernible voice
His voice was so soft it was indiscernible at the back of the room.
The difference between the two colors was almost indiscernible in the dim light.
Time seemed to pass with indiscernible changes in the quiet countryside.
His voice was so quiet it became almost indiscernible in the noisy room.
The path was covered by an indiscernible layer of dust after the storm.
There was an indiscernible shift in her attitude during the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imperceptible - impossible to perceive
  • antonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear
