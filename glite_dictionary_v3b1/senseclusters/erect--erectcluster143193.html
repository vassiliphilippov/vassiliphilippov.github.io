erect

adjective
(of a sexual organ) stiff and rigid
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
(of a sexual organ) stiff and rigid, especially as a result of sexual arousal.

Examples

Erect penis
Certain medications can make it difficult to become erect.
The flower's stamen stands erect as it releases pollen.
The male patient reported that his penis was erect during the examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flaccid - soft and hanging loosely or limply, especially so as to look or feel unpleasant
