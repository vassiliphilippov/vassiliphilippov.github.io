posted

adjective
publicly displayed or announced
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstɪd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪd / (BrE)
(of a notice, information, or rule) publicly displayed for others to see, especially on a sign or noticeboard.

Examples

The posted speed limits
Drivers must obey the posted speed limits at all times.
No trespassing signs were clearly posted along the edge of the property.
The posted schedule for the exams is now available on the noticeboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: announced - publicly or officially made known
  • antonym: hidden - not visible or made public
