roommate

noun
person who shares a room
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈruːm.meɪt / (BrE)
a person with whom you share a room, especially in a residence or dormitory

Examples

College roommate
After moving to college, she quickly became good friends with her roommate.
I asked my roommate to keep the noise down while I was studying.
My roommate and I split the rent evenly each month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatmate - person who shares a flat/apartment, not necessarily a room
