cosmopolitics

noun
global political relations
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməpəˈlɪtɪks / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməpəˈlɪtɪks / (BrE)
the study or practice of political relations and governance on a global or universal scale

Examples

Cosmopolitics in world governance
Her research explores how cosmopolitics shapes international cooperation and global governance.
The conference addressed the challenges of cosmopolitics in an increasingly interconnected world.
Many scholars debate the role of cosmopolitics in solving worldwide environmental issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geopolitics - the study of the effects of geography on politics and international relations
  • synonym: international relations - the study of relationships between countries
  • antonym: nationalism - political ideology focused on promoting the interests of a particular nation
