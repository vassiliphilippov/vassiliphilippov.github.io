rancorous

adjective
showing deep resentment or bitterness
Pronunciation: / ˈræŋkərəs / (AmE), / ˈraŋ.kər.əs / (BrE)
having or showing deep-seated resentment or bitterness

Examples

A rancorous argument
His rancorous remarks only deepened the hostility between the two groups.
The debate turned rancorous as both sides hurled accusations at each other.
Years of competition had left a rancorous atmosphere in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitter - feeling or showing anger and resentment
  • antonym: forgiving - willing to forgive, not holding resentment
  • synonym: resentful - feeling or expressing bitterness or indignation
