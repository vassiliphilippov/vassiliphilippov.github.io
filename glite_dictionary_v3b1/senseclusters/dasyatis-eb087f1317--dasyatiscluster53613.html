Dasyatis

proper noun
genus of whiptail stingrays
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdæsiˈætɪs / (AmE), / ˌdæsɪˈeɪtɪs / (BrE)
A genus of stingrays in the family Dasyatidae, commonly known as whiptail stingrays, found in coastal waters worldwide.

The genus Dasyatis includes several species commonly known as whiptail stingrays.
