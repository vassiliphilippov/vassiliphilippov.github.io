neuromarketing

noun
marketing based on neuroscientific consumer insights
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈmɑrkətɪŋ /; / ˌnuːroʊˈmɑrkətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the study and use of neuroscience to understand how consumers respond to marketing, in order to improve advertising and product strategies
