hydroforming

noun
metal shaping process
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌfɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌfɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
a manufacturing process that uses high-pressure fluid to shape metal into a specific form

Examples

Hydroforming metal parts
Many modern bicycles use frames made by hydroforming to achieve lightweight and strong designs.
Automotive engineers often choose hydroforming for creating complex metal parts with fewer welds.
The company invested in new hydroforming equipment to improve its manufacturing capabilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casting - a process of shaping material by pouring it into a mold
  • synonym: stamping - a process of shaping metal using a press
