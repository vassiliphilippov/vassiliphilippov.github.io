Arab-Berbers

noun
North African ethnic group of Berber and Arab descent
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈærəb ˈbɜrbərz / (AmE), / ˈærəb ˈbɜːbəz / (BrE)
A North African ethnic group descended from both Berbers and Arabs.

Examples

Arab-Berbers people
Arab-Berbers have played a significant role in the history of North Africa.
Many Arab-Berbers speak both Arabic and Berber languages fluently.
The cultural traditions of Arab-Berbers are reflected in their art and music.
