unpedigreed

adjective
without known ancestry
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɛdəˌɡrid / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɛdɪɡriːd / (BrE)
not having a recorded or officially recognized ancestry or lineage

Examples

Unpedigreed cat
The animal shelter had several unpedigreed cats looking for new homes.
She adopted an unpedigreed puppy because she cared more about personality than breed.
Many unpedigreed dogs can be just as healthy and loving as purebreds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mongrel - an animal of mixed or unknown breed
  • antonym: pedigreed - having a recorded or recognized ancestry
  • antonym: purebred - of known and unmixed ancestry
