beach flea

noun
hopping crustacean found on beaches
5.6 5.9
Pronunciation: /biːtʃ fliː/ (AmE), /biːtʃ fliː/ (BrE)
a small, hopping amphipod crustacean commonly found on ocean beaches, resembling a flea in its jumping movement

Examples

Small beach fleas
After lifting a piece of driftwood, we spotted a dozen beach fleas scurrying away.
Children love to watch the beach fleas hop around the shoreline at low tide.
Some anglers use beach fleas as bait when fishing near the coast.
