philoprogenitive

adjective
loving one's children
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪloʊproʊˈdʒɛnətɪv / (AmE), / ˌfɪləʊprəˈdʒɛnɪtɪv / (BrE)
having a strong love for one's own children or offspring

Examples

Philoprogenitive mother
Her philoprogenitive nature was clear in the way she cared for her children.
The philoprogenitive couple always put their family first in every decision.
He was described as philoprogenitive, always eager to spend time with his kids.
