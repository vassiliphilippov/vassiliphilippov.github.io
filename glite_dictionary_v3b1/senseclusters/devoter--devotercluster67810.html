devoter

noun
person who dedicates
8.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvoʊtər / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊtə / (BrE)
a person who is very dedicated or committed to a cause, activity, or person

Examples

He became known as a devoter of his time and energy to environmental causes.
As a lifelong devoter of environmental causes, he volunteers every weekend.
The devoter spent years supporting the local animal shelter through donations and advocacy.
Many people consider her a devoter to her family and friends.
