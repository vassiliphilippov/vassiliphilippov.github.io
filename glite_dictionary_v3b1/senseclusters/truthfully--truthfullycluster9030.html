truthfully

adverb
in an honest and accurate way
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtruθfəli / (AmE), / ˈtruːθfəli / (BrE)
In an honest and accurate way; without lying or hiding the facts.

Examples

Speak truthfully
He spoke truthfully about the challenges he had faced.
Please tell me truthfully, did you break the vase?
She answered all the questions truthfully during the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deceitfully - in a dishonest or misleading way
  • synonym: honestly - in a truthful or sincere way
