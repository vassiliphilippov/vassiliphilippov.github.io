mound

noun
an artificial heap of earth or stones
1.7 4.60/m
Pronunciation: / maÊŠnd / (AmE), / maÊŠnd / (BrE)
a small hill or raised area of earth or stones, natural or artificial

Examples

A grassy mound
A large mound of earth was built to fortify the campsite.
Archaeologists discovered a burial mound dating back thousands of years.
Children played on the grassy mound near the riverbank.
A small mound of earth marked the entrance to the fox's den.
From the top of the mound, we could see the entire valley.
The children rolled down the grassy mound behind the school.

    8.7 0.30/m
