abbreviated

adjective
shortened in duration or length
5.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbriː.vi.eɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / əˈbriː.vi.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
made shorter in length or duration than usual or expected; cut short.

Examples

Abbreviated meeting
Because of time constraints, the ceremony was abbreviated to just thirty minutes.
His vacation was abbreviated when he received an urgent call from work.
We attended an abbreviated meeting that covered only the most important topics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shortened - made or became shorter
  • synonym: truncated - shortened by cutting off
