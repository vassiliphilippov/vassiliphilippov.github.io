fishline

noun
cord for fishing
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a strong, thin cord or thread used for catching fish with a fishing rod or handline

Examples

A fishline for fishing
He carefully tied a new fishline to his hook before casting into the river.
The fishline snapped when the large trout pulled hard on the rod.
She bought a spool of strong fishline for her weekend fishing trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fishing line - a cord used for catching fish
  • degree higher: rope - a thick, strong cord made by twisting together strands of fiber
  • degree lower: thread - a thin strand of fiber used for sewing
