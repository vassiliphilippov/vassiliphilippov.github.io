tesseract

noun
four-dimensional analogue of a cube
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛs.əˌrækt / (AmE), / ˈtɛs.ər.ækt / (BrE)
A four-dimensional geometric figure which is the analogue of a cube in four dimensions.

Examples

A tesseract shape
In mathematics, a tesseract can be visualized as a cube extended into four dimensions.
The science fiction novel described a journey through a tesseract to reach distant worlds.
When projected into three dimensions, a tesseract appears as a cube within a cube.
