poor-spirited

adjective
lacking courage or determination
4.5 7.2
Pronunciation: /puːr.ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /pʊr.ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /pɔː.ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /pʊə.ˈspɪr.ɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
Lacking courage or determination; timid and easily discouraged.

Examples

A poor-spirited apology
Instead of standing up for his beliefs, he gave a poor-spirited apology and walked away.
She thought his poor-spirited response to the criticism showed a lack of leadership.
The team became poor-spirited after several crushing defeats.
