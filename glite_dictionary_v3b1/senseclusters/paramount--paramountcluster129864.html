paramount

adjective
most important; supreme
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpær.əˌmaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈpær.əˌmaʊnt / (BrE)
more important than anything else; having the highest authority or influence.

Examples

A paramount importance
In the negotiations, achieving a fair agreement was paramount for both sides.
Protecting public health should be of paramount importance in any policy decision.
The safety of our employees is paramount to the success of this project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preeminent - surpassing others; very distinguished
  • antonym: secondary - less important than something else
  • synonym: supreme - highest in rank or authority
