tellurian

adjective
relating to land (not sea or air)
Pronunciation: / təˈlʊriən / (AmE), / tɛˈljʊəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of land, as distinct from the sea or air

Examples

The tellurian environment supports a wide variety of animal and plant life.
Unlike aquatic organisms, tellurian species face challenges such as drought and soil erosion.
