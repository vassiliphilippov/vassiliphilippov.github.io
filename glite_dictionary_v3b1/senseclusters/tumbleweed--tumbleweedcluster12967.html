tumbleweed

noun
wind-blown rolling plant of arid regions
5.4 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtʌm.bəl.wiːd/ /; / /ˈtʌm.bəl.wɪd/ (AmE), /ˈtʌm.bəl.wiːd/ /; / /ˈtʌm.bəl.wɪd/ (BrE)
