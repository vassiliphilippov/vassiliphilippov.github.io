capuchin

noun
woman's hooded cloak
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.jə.tʃɪn / (AmE), / ˈkap.jʊ.tʃɪn / (BrE)
A woman's cloak with a hood, especially popular in the 18th century.

Examples
