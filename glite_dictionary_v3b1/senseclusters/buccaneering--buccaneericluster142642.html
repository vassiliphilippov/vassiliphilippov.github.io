buccaneering

noun
piracy or hijacking at sea or in similar contexts
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌbʌk.əˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌbʌk.əˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of engaging in piracy or hijacking on the high seas or in similar contexts, such as taking control of a ship or plane by force

Examples

Buccaneering at sea
He was convicted of buccaneering after hijacking a merchant vessel.
International law strictly prohibits acts of buccaneering and similar crimes.
There was a rise in buccaneering off the coast during the economic downturn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air piracy - the act of hijacking an aircraft; skyjacking
  • synonym: piracy - the act of attacking and robbing ships at sea
