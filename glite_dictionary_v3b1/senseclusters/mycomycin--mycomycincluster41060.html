mycomycin

noun
antibiotic acid from an actinomycete
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪkəˈmʌɪsɪn / (BrE)
a highly unsaturated antibiotic acid obtained from a species of actinomycete, used in certain medical applications
