Synonyms

  • show
    1.0 200/m
    show makes something visible or lets people see it, while present often adds explanation or formal introduction to an audience.
  • demonstrate
    3.1 20/m
    demonstrate shows how something works or is done to help people understand, while present can be more general and include introductions.
  • exhibit
    3.4 30/m
    exhibit displays objects or qualities publicly, often in a museum or show, while present can mean any formal showing or speech.
  • speak
    3.2 80/m
    speak means to give a speech or formal talk to an audience, while present covers giving talks but also showing or introducing things.
  • introduce
    1.8 40/m
    introduce means to make someone known by name in a social setting, while present can mean showing ideas, people, or performances formally.
