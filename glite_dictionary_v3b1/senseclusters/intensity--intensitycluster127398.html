intensity

noun
degree of chromatic purity in color
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnsəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪti / (BrE)
The degree of chromatic purity of a color, describing its vividness or freedom from dilution with white.

Examples

Intensity of a color
Artists often adjust the intensity of a color by mixing it with white or gray.
High intensity hues can create a vivid and lively effect in a painting.
The intensity of the red paint made the artwork especially striking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pastel - a color with low intensity; pale hue
  • synonym: saturation - the intensity or purity of a color
