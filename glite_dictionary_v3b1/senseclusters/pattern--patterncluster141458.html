pattern

noun
perceptual arrangement or structure
Pronunciation: / ˈpætɚn /; / ˈpætərn / (AmE), / ˈpætən /; / ˈpætn / (BrE)
a decorative or artistic arrangement, especially one that is repeated or used as a motif in design or art

Examples

A floral pattern
Her dress was decorated with a floral pattern in blue and white.
Our brains are naturally inclined to search for a pattern in random data.
The wallpaper features a geometric pattern that gives the room a modern look.
She wore a dress with a bold geometric pattern.
The ceramic tiles were arranged to create a colorful pattern on the floor.
The wallpaper featured an intricate pattern of flowers and vines.

  • synonym: form - structure or arrangement of something as detected by the senses
