forwarder

noun
message relay device
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərdər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwədə / (BrE)
a device, program, or service that receives messages, data, or requests and sends them to another destination

Examples

Email forwarder
The company uses a forwarder to automatically redirect customer emails to the support team.
A network forwarder can help manage traffic by sending requests to the correct server.
He configured the forwarder so that all messages would reach his new email address.

Related Terms

  • antonym: receiver - a device or program that only receives data
  • synonym: relay - a device or program that passes something on to another
