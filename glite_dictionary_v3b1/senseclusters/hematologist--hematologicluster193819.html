hematologist

noun
doctor specializing in blood diseases
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈtɑlədʒɪst /; / hiːˌmætəˈlɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪst /; / hiːˌmætəˈlɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs

Examples

Hematologist specialist
After several tests, the hematologist diagnosed her with a rare blood disorder.
As a hematologist, Dr. Smith often collaborates with oncologists for treatment plans.
The patient was referred to a hematologist for further evaluation of his anemia.
After being referred by her general practitioner, she scheduled an appointment with the haematologist.
Research by a haematologist led to new treatments for rare blood disorders.
The haematologist explained the blood test results in detail to the patient.
