Assamese language

noun
language of the people of Assam, India
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈsæˌmiz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌæsəˈmiːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Indo-Aryan language of the Assamese people, spoken primarily in the state of Assam in northeastern India.

Examples
