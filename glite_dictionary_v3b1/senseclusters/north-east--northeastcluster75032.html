north-east

adverb
in the direction of northeast
Pronunciation: / nɔrθˈist / (AmE), / nɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
To, toward, or in the direction of the northeast.

Examples

To north-east mountains

Storm clouds were gathering north-east of the city by late afternoon.
The hikers traveled north-east along the trail until they reached the lake.
