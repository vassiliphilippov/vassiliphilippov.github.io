anxiolytic

noun
drug relieving anxiety
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋziəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæŋziəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
A drug or substance that relieves anxiety and reduces tension or irritability.

Examples

Take an anxiolytic pill
He preferred natural remedies over pharmaceutical anxiolytics for his mild anxiety.
Some anxiolytic medications can cause drowsiness as a side effect.
The doctor prescribed an anxiolytic to help her manage her panic attacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tranquilizer - a drug used to reduce tension or anxiety
