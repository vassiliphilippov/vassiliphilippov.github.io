bell pepper

noun
a sweet, bell-shaped pepper
1.7 1.4
Pronunciation: /bel ˈpep.ər/ (AmE), /bel ˈpep.ə/ (BrE)
a large, sweet, bell-shaped pepper (green, red, yellow, orange, or sometimes black) eaten raw or cooked as a vegetable

Examples
