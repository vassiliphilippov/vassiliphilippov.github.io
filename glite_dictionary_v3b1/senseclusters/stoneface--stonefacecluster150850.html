stoneface

noun
a succulent plant resembling a stone
9.1 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstoʊn.feɪs/ (AmE), /ˈstəʊn.feɪs/ (BrE)
A succulent plant of the genus Lithops, native to Africa, with thick leaves that resemble stones and solitary yellow or white flowers.

Examples

A stoneface plant
Gardeners prize the stoneface for its unique, decorative appearance and low maintenance.
The stoneface blends in with the rocks, making it difficult to spot in the wild.
