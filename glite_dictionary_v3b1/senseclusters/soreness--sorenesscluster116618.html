soreness

noun
painful or tender bodily feeling
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔr.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.nəs / (BrE)
a painful or tender feeling in a part of the body, especially when touched

Examples

Muscle soreness
After the long run, I felt significant soreness in my legs the next day.
He complained of soreness in his throat whenever he swallowed.
The injection site can sometimes cause mild soreness or redness for a few hours.

  • synonym: tenderness - the quality of being easily hurt or sensitive to touch
