diesel-hydraulic locomotive

noun
locomotive with diesel engine and hydraulic transmission
1.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.zəl haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk ˈloʊ.kə.moʊ.tɪv/ /; / /ˈdɪz.əl haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk ˈloʊ.kə.moʊ.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.zəl haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lɪk ˈləʊ.kə.məʊ.tɪv/ /; / /ˈdiː.zəl haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk ˈləʊ.kə.məʊ.tɪv/ (BrE)
