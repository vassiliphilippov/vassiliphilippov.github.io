Nova Scotia

proper noun
Canadian province in eastern Canada
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊvə ˈskoʊʃə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊvə ˈskəʊʃə / (BrE)
a province in eastern Canada, located on a peninsula and including Cape Breton Island, known for its maritime history and as the former home of French settlers called Acadians
