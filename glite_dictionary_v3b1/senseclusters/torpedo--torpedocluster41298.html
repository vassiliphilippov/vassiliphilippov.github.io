torpedo

noun
professional killer (slang)
Pronunciation: / tɔːrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
(slang) A hired gunman; a professional killer, especially one employed by organized crime.

Examples

A mob torpedo
During the 1920s, every major crime boss kept a trusted torpedo on his payroll.
He lived in fear that a torpedo would be sent after him for talking to the police.
The mob hired a torpedo to take care of any threats to their operations.
