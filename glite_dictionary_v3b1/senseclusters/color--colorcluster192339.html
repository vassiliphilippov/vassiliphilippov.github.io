color

noun
timbre or character of musical sound
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
the quality of a musical sound that distinguishes different types of sound production, such as instruments or voices; timbre

Examples

Rich color in music
She admired the rich color in the choir's harmonies.
The composer used brass instruments to add more color to the orchestration.
The violin solo brought a distinctive color to the performance.
Critics praised the pianist for achieving a rich colour throughout her performance.
Each instrument brings its own colour to a musical composition.
The new violin added a warmer colour to the orchestra's overall sound.
