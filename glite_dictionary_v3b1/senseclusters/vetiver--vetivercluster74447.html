vetiver

noun
fragrant plant oil
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛtɪvər / (AmE), / ˈvɛtɪvə / (BrE)
a fragrant oil or scent obtained from the roots of the vetiver plant, used in perfumes and cosmetics

Examples

Vetiver perfume oil
The earthy aroma of vetiver is popular in many luxury perfumes.
She prefers lotions with a hint of vetiver for their calming scent.
Many colognes use vetiver as a base note to add depth and warmth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patchouli - a strong-scented oil from a tropical plant used in perfumes
  • synonym: sandalwood - a fragrant wood and oil used in perfumes
