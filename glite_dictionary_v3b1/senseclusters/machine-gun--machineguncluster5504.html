machine-gun

verb
to shoot with a machine gun
1.0
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin ɡʌn / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn ɡʌn / (BrE)

Examples

To machine-gun soldiers
Gunfire erupted as the troops machine-gunned across the field.
He machine-gunned the targets as quickly as he could.
The soldiers machine-gunned the abandoned building during the raid.
