lucifugous

adjective
shunning or avoiding light
Pronunciation: / luːˈsɪfjəɡəs / (AmE), / luːˈsɪfjʊɡəs / (BrE)
Avoiding or shunning light; tending to avoid exposure to light.

Examples

Lucifugous insects
Scientists studied the lucifugous tendencies of insects that hide under rocks during daylight hours.
Some fungi are lucifugous and thrive only in shaded, dark environments.
The lucifugous nature of certain cave-dwelling animals helps them survive in perpetual darkness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: photophilous - preferring or thriving in light
  • synonym: photophobic - having an aversion to light; light-avoiding
