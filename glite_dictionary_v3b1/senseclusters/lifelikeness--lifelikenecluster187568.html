lifelikeness

noun
realistic quality
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌlaɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌlaɪknəs / (BrE)
the quality of appearing real or true to life

Examples

The lifelikeness of figures
The lifelikeness of the wax figures made visitors do a double take.
Critics praised the lifelikeness of the animation in the new film.
She admired the lifelikeness of the painting, which seemed almost photographic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificiality - the quality of being unnatural or fake
  • synonym: realism - the quality of representing things in a way that is true to life
  • synonym: verisimilitude - the appearance of being true or real
