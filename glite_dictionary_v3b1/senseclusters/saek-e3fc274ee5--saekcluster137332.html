Saek

noun
a Tai language of Laos
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪk / (AmE), / ˈseɪk / (BrE)
a Tai language spoken in central Laos, particularly in Khammouan and Bolikhamxai provinces near the Vietnam border.

Examples

Saek language
Linguists are documenting vocabulary and grammar of Saek before it disappears.
