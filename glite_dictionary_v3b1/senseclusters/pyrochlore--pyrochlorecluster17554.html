pyrochlore

noun
niobium ore mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrəˌklɔr / (AmE), / ˈpʌɪrəˌklɔː /; / ˈpʌɪ.rə.klɔː / (BrE)
a mineral (or sometimes a synthetic compound) with a specific crystal structure and composition, important as an ore of niobium and studied for its scientific properties
