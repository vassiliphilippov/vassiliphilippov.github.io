chain tongs

noun
pipe wrench with chain for large pipes
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn tɑŋz / (AmE), / tʃeɪn tɒŋz / (BrE)
A type of pipe wrench consisting of a length of chain attached to a handle with serrated jaws, used for gripping and turning large pipes.

Examples

Chain tongs tool
For larger diameter pipes, chain tongs provide a strong, adjustable grip.
Make sure the chain tongs are properly secured before turning the pipe.
The plumber used chain tongs to tighten the heavy steel pipe.
