Synonyms

  • muscled

  • muscly
    3.7 0.03/m
    muscly feels informal and describes someone with visible arm and chest muscles, while muscular is more neutral and standard.
  • brawny
    4.7 0.67/m

  • sinewy
    7.0 0.51/m
