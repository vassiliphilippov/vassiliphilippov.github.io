muscular

adjective
having well-developed muscles
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.kjələr /; / ˈmʌskjələr / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə /; / ˈmʌskjʊlə / (BrE)
having well-developed muscles or a strong, powerful body

Examples

Muscular body
After months of training, his muscular physique impressed everyone at the gym.
She admired the muscular legs of the marathon runners as they sprinted to the finish line.
The muscular dog easily pulled the heavy sled across the snow.
She admired the muscular arms of the swimmer as he powered through the water.
The muscular boxer won the match with impressive strength and agility.
Years of training had given him a muscular physique.

  • antonym: slender - gracefully thin; not muscular or large
