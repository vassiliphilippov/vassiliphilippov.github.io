Swiss steak

noun
steak braised with tomato and onion
Pronunciation: / swɪs steɪk / (AmE), / swɪs steɪk / (BrE)
a dish consisting of steak braised in a tomato and onion sauce, often cooked until tender

Examples

A Swiss steak dish
He braised the meat slowly to create a classic Swiss steak full of flavor.
I ordered the Swiss steak with mashed potatoes at the diner.
My grandmother used to make Swiss steak every Sunday for our family dinners.
