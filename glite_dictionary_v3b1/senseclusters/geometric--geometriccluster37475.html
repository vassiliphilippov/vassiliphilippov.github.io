geometric

adjective
characterized by simple shapes in design
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒioʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
(of design or decoration) characterized by simple shapes such as squares, circles, or triangles, often arranged in a regular pattern.

Examples

Geometric patterns
Ancient pottery often displayed geometric designs using circles and straight lines.
She wore a dress with bold geometric prints that caught everyone's eye.
The rug features a beautiful geometric pattern of triangles and diamonds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstract - not representing external reality, but using shapes and colors
