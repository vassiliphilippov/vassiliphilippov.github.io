Lacanian

adjective
of Lacan's theories
9.9
Pronunciation: / ləˈkeɪniən / (AmE), / ləˈkɑːniən / (BrE)
relating to the theories or ideas of Jacques Lacan, a French psychoanalyst

Examples

Lacanian theory
The professor gave a Lacanian interpretation of the novel's symbolism.
Her research applies Lacanian concepts to modern social theory.
Many critics debate the usefulness of Lacanian analysis in contemporary psychology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Freudian - relating to the theories of Sigmund Freud
  • synonym: Jungian - relating to the theories of Carl Jung
