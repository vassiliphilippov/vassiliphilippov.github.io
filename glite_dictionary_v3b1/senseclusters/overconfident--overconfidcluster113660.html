overconfident

adjective
excessively confident
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnfɪdənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkɒnfɪdənt / (BrE)
having or showing excessive confidence in oneself or one's abilities

Examples

An overconfident person
Don't be overconfident—this test is more difficult than it looks.
His overconfident attitude led him to ignore the instructions, resulting in several mistakes.
She became overconfident after winning her first three matches and underestimated her next opponent.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • degree lower: confident - sure of oneself; having confidence
