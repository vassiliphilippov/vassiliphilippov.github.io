skate

noun
large flat marine ray with edible flesh
7.8
Pronunciation: / skeɪt / (AmE), / skeɪt / (BrE)
a large edible marine fish of the ray family with a long snout and thick tail, having broad pectoral fins that merge with the head and swimming by undulating the edges of these fins

Examples

Grilled skate
Skate is often served grilled or poached in seafood restaurants.
Scientists studied the swimming patterns of the skate to learn about its unique movement.
The fisherman caught a large skate off the coast and brought it to shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ray - any of various cartilaginous fishes with flattened bodies and enlarged pectoral fins
