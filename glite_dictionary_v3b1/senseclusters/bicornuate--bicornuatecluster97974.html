bicornuate

adjective
having two horns or horn-shaped parts
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkɔrn.ju.ɪt / (AmE), / baɪˈkɔː.njuː.eɪt / (BrE)
(of an organ or structure) having two horns or horn-shaped parts, especially applied to the uterus in some mammals.

Examples

A bicornuate uterus
A bicornuate structure is often seen in the uteri of certain mammals, such as cows.
The doctor explained that her uterus was bicornuate and might affect pregnancy outcomes.
The surgeon noted the presence of a bicornuate heart in this rare anatomical specimen.
