Washingtonian

adjective
of or relating to George Washington
Pronunciation: / ˌwɑːʃɪŋˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌwɒʃɪŋˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of George Washington or his principles.

Examples

Washingtonian ideals
Many admire the Washingtonian dedication to public service evident in his presidency.
The school adopted a Washingtonian approach to leadership education.
The senator gave a Washingtonian response, emphasizing honesty and integrity.
