flory

noun
heraldic floral motif
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːri / (AmE), / ˈflɔːri / (BrE)
a stylized flower or floral ornament used as a decorative motif, especially in heraldry

Examples

A flory ornament
The ancient shield was decorated with a golden flory at its center.
Artists often use a flory to add elegance to coats of arms.
The tapestry featured a repeating flory pattern along its border.
