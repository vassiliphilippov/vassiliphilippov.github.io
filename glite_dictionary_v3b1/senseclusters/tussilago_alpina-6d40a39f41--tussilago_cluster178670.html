Tussilago alpina

noun
a species of flowering alpine herb
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtʌsɪˈlɑːɡoʊ ælˈpiːnə / (AmE), / ˌtʌsɪˈlɑːɡəʊ ælˈpiːnə / (BrE)
A rhizomatous herb with purple-red flowers, native to alpine regions, sometimes classified within the genus Tussilago.

Examples

Tussilago alpina purple flowers
Tussilago alpina thrives in rocky soils at high altitudes in the Alps.
Gardeners sometimes use Tussilago alpina as a groundcover for shady areas.
The purple-red flowers of Tussilago alpina bloom early in the alpine spring.
