spur

noun
a sharply pointed projection
Pronunciation: / spɝː / (AmE), / spɜː / (BrE)
a sharply pointed projection from a surface or structure, often resembling a spike or prong

Examples

A plant spur
A sharp spur on the plant's stem protects it from animals.
Mountain ranges sometimes extend a long, narrow spur into the surrounding plains.
The rooster has a distinctive spur on each leg.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prong - a pointed projection, especially one of several on a tool or animal's body
  • synonym: protuberance - a thing that protrudes from something else
