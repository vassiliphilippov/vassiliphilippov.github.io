banker

noun
a person who directs or runs a bank
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈbaŋkə / (BrE)
a person who owns, manages, or is an executive in a bank

Examples

A successful banker
After years of hard work, she became a successful banker at a leading international firm.
As a banker, he is often required to travel to meet with investors and clients around the world.
The banker approved their mortgage application after carefully reviewing their finances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executive - a person with senior managerial responsibility
  • synonym: financier - a person skilled in large scale financial transactions
  • degree lower: teller - a bank employee who deals directly with customers
