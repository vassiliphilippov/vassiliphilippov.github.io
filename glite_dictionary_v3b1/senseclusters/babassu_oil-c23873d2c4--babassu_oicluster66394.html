babassu oil

noun
vegetable oil from babassu nuts
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈbɑːsu ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / bəˈbasu ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
a clear, light-yellow vegetable oil obtained from the kernels of babassu nuts, used in cooking, cosmetics, and soap-making

Examples

Babassu oil for cooking
In Brazil, babassu oil is commonly used as an alternative to coconut oil in cooking.
Many natural hair care products now include babassu oil for its moisturizing properties.
The soap was crafted from pure babassu oil, making it gentle and nourishing for the skin.
