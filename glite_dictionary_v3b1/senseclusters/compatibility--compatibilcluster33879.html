compatibility

noun
mutual understanding; ability to get along
1.7
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpætəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / kəmˌpatɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
a state in which two or more people feel mutual understanding and can exist together harmoniously

Examples

Compatibility in relationships
Relationship counselors often discuss the importance of compatibility between partners.
The team's compatibility made working together enjoyable and productive.
Their compatibility was evident from the way they finished each other's sentences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmony - a pleasing arrangement or agreement; agreement in feeling or opinion
  • antonym: incompatibility - the state of being unable to exist together without conflict
