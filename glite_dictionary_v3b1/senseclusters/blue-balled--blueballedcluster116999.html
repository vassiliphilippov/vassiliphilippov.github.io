blue-balled

adjective
frustrated due to unmet expectations
Pronunciation: / bluː bɑːld / (AmE), / bluː bɔːld / (BrE)
experiencing frustration or irritation because expectations, especially sexual, have not been fulfilled

Examples

Feeling blue-balled after waiting
After waiting two hours for the event to start, I felt completely blue-balled.
She was blue-balled by the cliffhanger ending of the new show.
The team's incredible comeback ended in a loss, leaving fans blue-balled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frustrated - feeling upset when prevented from achieving something
  • antonym: satisfied - pleased because expectations have been met or fulfilled
