cremini

noun
brown edible mushroom
5.5
Pronunciation: / krəˈmiːni / (AmE), / krəˈmiːni / (BrE)
a small, brown mushroom with a firm texture and mild flavor, often used in cooking

Examples

Fresh cremini mushrooms
She sautéed cremini mushrooms to add flavor to the risotto.
The chef prefers using cremini in pasta dishes for their rich taste.
You can substitute cremini for white mushrooms in most recipes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: button mushroom - a small, white, mild-flavored mushroom
  • synonym: portobello - a large, mature brown mushroom with a meaty texture
