bummer

noun
disappointing or frustrating experience
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmər / (AmE), / ˈbʌmə / (BrE)
An experience that causes disappointment, frustration, or irritation.

Examples

What a bummer
It's a bummer that the concert was cancelled due to rain.
Missing the train was a real bummer for everyone.
Sorry to hear about your lost keys—that's such a bummer.
