fumarole

noun
volcanic gas vent
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfjuː.mə.roʊl/ /; / /ˈfuː.mə.roʊl/ (AmE), /ˈfjuː.mə.rəʊl/ /; / /ˈfuː.mə.rəʊl/ (BrE)
a vent or opening in the Earth's surface, especially near volcanoes, that releases steam and gases
