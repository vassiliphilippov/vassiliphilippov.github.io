fumarole

noun
volcanic gas vent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːməˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmərəʊl / (BrE)
a vent or opening in the Earth's surface, especially near volcanoes, that releases steam and gases

Scientists measured the gases escaping from the fumarole to study volcanic activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geyser - a hot spring that intermittently sends up jets of water and steam
  • synonym: volcano - an opening in the Earth's crust that erupts lava, ash, and gases
