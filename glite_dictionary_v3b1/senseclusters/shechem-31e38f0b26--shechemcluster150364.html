Shechem

proper noun
biblical figure
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːkəm / (AmE), / ˈʃiːkəm / (BrE)
a man in the Hebrew Bible, son of Hamor, who appears in the Book of Genesis and is associated with the story of Dinah

Examples

Shechem son of Hamor
In the Book of Genesis, Shechem is described as the son of Hamor the Hivite.
The story of Dinah and Shechem has been discussed by many biblical scholars.
Shechem plays a significant role in the events involving Jacob's family.
