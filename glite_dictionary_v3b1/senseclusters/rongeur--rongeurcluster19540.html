rongeur

noun
gnawing animal
Pronunciation: / rɒnˈʒɜr / (AmE), / rɒ̃ˈʒɜː / (BrE)
an animal that gnaws, especially a rodent; now rare or archaic

Examples

A hungry rongeur
In the old tale a hungry rongeur gnawed the granary's wooden beams through the night.
The farmer set traps to keep the rongeur from destroying his crops.
Legends warned of a giant rongeur that haunted the village at night.
