delicateness

noun
sensitivity or tact
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪkətnəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkətnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sensitive, gentle, or careful in dealing with people or difficult situations

Examples

Delicateness in conversation
She handled the situation with great delicateness, making sure everyone felt respected.
The delicateness of his response helped to calm the tense meeting.
Diplomats must often show delicateness when discussing sensitive topics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of concern for others' feelings
  • synonym: tact - skill and sensitivity in dealing with others or difficult issues
