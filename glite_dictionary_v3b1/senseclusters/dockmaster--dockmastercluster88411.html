dockmaster

noun
harbor operations manager
5.6
Pronunciation: /dɑːk ˈmæ.stər/ (AmE), /dɒk ˈmɑː.stə/ (BrE)

Examples

When a storm was approaching, the dockmaster coordinated the safe mooring of every vessel.
