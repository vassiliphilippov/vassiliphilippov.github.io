dockmaster

noun
harbor operations manager
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑkˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈdɒkˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person in charge of managing and overseeing operations at a dock or harbor

Examples

The dockmaster manages ships
The dockmaster ensured that all ships followed the correct docking procedures.
After a busy morning, the dockmaster took a break in his office overlooking the harbor.
When a storm was approaching, the dockmaster coordinated the safe mooring of every vessel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harbormaster - an official responsible for the management of a harbor
  • degree lower: stevedore - a person employed at a dock to load and unload ships
