scale

noun
relative size or extent
Pronunciation: / skeɪl / (AmE), / skeɪl / (BrE)
the size, level, or extent of something, especially when compared to other things

Examples

A large scale
The company plans to expand its operations to a global scale next year.
The project grew in scale as more partners joined the effort.
They redecorated the house on a modest scale due to budget limitations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dimension - an aspect or feature of a situation, problem, or thing
  • synonym: magnitude - the great size or extent of something
  • synonym: scope - extent of the area or subject matter something deals with
