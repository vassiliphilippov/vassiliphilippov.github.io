hatchet man

noun
male professional killer or assassin
Pronunciation: / ˈhætʃɪt mæn / (AmE), / ˈhætʃɪt man / (BrE)
A man who is hired to kill someone, usually as a professional assassin.

Examples

Mob hatchet man
Detectives believe a hatchet man carried out the carefully planned murder.
He was rumored to work as a hatchet man for various criminal organizations.
The mob boss sent his trusted hatchet man to eliminate the witness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone for political or monetary reasons
  • synonym: hitman - a person hired to kill someone; professional killer or assassin
