friendly takeover

noun
welcomed company acquisition
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛndli ˈteɪkˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli ˈteɪkəʊvə / (BrE)
a takeover of a company that is approved and welcomed by the target company's management

Examples

A friendly takeover deal
A friendly takeover often results in a smoother transition for employees and customers.
Both firms announced the friendly takeover in a joint press release.
The company's board agreed to the friendly takeover from its larger competitor.
