Synonyms

  • draft
    6.1 13/m
    draft is a system that calls people to military service by law, while conscription is the legal practice of requiring such service.
  • levy
    8.0 0.99/m
    levy means the act of enlisting people into compulsory military service, while conscription names the broader legal system requiring service.
  • national service
    4.1 0.99/m
    national service is compulsory armed service for a set period, often in peacetime, while conscription is the legal requirement to serve.
