conscription

noun
compulsory enlistment for military service
9.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / kənˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
the practice of requiring people by law to serve in the armed forces

Examples

Military conscription
Many young men faced conscription during the war.
Opposition to conscription was widespread among certain groups.
The government introduced conscription to fill its military ranks.
