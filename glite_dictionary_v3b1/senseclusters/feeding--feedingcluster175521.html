feeding

noun
supplying data to a system
Pronunciation: / c8fid6ab / (AmE), / c8fid0d6ab / (BrE)
the act of supplying data or information to a computer, program, or system, especially continuously or in large amounts, to drive processing, updates, or outputs.

Examples

Data feeding
The streaming service relies on the constant feeding of user data into its recommendation model.
Regular feeding of updated sensor readings kept the factory monitoring dashboard accurate and timely.
They scheduled nightly feeding of the database to ensure analytics had fresh transaction records.
