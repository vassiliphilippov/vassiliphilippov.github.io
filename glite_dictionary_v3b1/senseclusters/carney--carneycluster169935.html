carney

noun
carnival worker
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrni / (AmE), / ˈkɑːni / (BrE)
a person who works at a carnival, especially someone operating games or rides

Examples

A carney runs games
The carney shouted to attract people to the ring toss game.
After years as a carney, he knew every trick to draw a crowd.
She spent her summer traveling with the fair as a carney running the bumper cars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnival worker - a person employed at a carnival
  • synonym: showman - a person who runs or presents shows, especially at fairs
