variolate

verb
to infect with smallpox
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛəriəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɛəriəˌleɪt / (BrE)
to infect or inoculate someone with smallpox, especially to induce immunity

Examples

Before vaccines, doctors would variolate patients to protect them from smallpox.
