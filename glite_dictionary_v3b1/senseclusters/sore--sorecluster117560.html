sore

adjective
painful or tender (body part)
Pronunciation: / sɔr / (AmE), / sɔː / (BrE)
(of a body part) painful or tender to touch; causing discomfort or pain.

Examples

Sore muscles
After the long hike, my legs felt sore for two days.
He pressed gently on the sore spot to see if it still hurt.
She avoided smiling because her gums were sore from dental work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: painful - causing physical pain; aching or sore
  • synonym: tender - sensitive to touch; painful
