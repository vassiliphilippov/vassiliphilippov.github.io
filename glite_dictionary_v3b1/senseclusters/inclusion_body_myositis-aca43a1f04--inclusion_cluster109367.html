inclusion body myositis

noun
progressive muscle disease in older adults
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluʒən ˈbɑdi ˌmaɪoʊˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːʒən ˈbɒdi ˌmaɪəʊˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A progressive muscle disorder characterized by weakness and inflammation of limb muscles, commonly affecting people over 50, often more in men.
