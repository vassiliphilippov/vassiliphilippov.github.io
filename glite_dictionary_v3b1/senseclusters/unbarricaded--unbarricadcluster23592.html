unbarricaded

adjective
not blocked by barrier
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈbærɪˌkeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈbærɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not blocked, closed, or protected by a barrier; open and vulnerable

Examples

An unbarricaded gate

The unbarricaded road allowed vehicles to pass through without delay.
The protesters marched through the unbarricaded streets, demanding justice from the authorities.
After the security system failed, the building was left unbarricaded and at risk of theft.
She felt emotionally unbarricaded after sharing her story with the public.
