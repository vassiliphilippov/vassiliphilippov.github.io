fast

noun
period of not eating (often religious)
8.3
Pronunciation: / fæst / (AmE), / fɑːst / (BrE)
A period during which a person abstains from all or some kinds of food, especially as a religious observance.

Examples

A Ramadan fast
During Ramadan, Muslims observe a fast from dawn until sunset.
Many cultures include a ritual fast as part of their religious practices.
The doctor ordered a twelve-hour fast before the medical tests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feast - a large meal, typically a celebratory one
