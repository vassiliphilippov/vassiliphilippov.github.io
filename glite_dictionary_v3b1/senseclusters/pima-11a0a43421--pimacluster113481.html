Pima

noun
member of a Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːmə / (AmE), / ˈpiːmə / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people living mainly in southern Arizona and northern Mexico.

Examples

A Pima farmer
Anthropologists have studied Pima agricultural techniques for decades.
Many Pima reside on reservations in southern Arizona.
The Pima have a rich tradition of basket weaving and pottery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Tohono O'odham - another Native American group of southern Arizona
