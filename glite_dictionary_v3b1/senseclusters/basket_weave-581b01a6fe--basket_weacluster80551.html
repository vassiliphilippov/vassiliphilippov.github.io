basket weave

noun
fabric weave resembling basket pattern
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskət ˌwiv / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt ˌwiːv / (BrE)
A method of weaving fabric in which two or more threads are interlaced to resemble the pattern of a woven basket.

Examples

A basket weave pattern
She admired the basket weave pattern in the designer's new clothing line.
The upholstery was made from durable basket weave fabric.
This rug features a subtle basket weave texture that adds depth to the room.

Related Terms
