Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam

proper noun
Sri Lankan militant separatist group
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən ˈtaɪɡərz əv tæˈmɪl iːləˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən ˈtaɪɡəz əv tæˈmɪl iːləˈɛm / (BrE)
a militant organization based in Sri Lanka, also known as the Tamil Tigers, which fought for the creation of an independent Tamil state and was known for its use of guerrilla warfare and suicide bombings.

Examples

Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam group
International organizations have studied the tactics of the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam for their use of guerilla warfare.
The Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam were involved in decades of conflict with the Sri Lankan government.
The United Nations condemned several attacks carried out by the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam.
