Hydrangeaceae

noun
a family of flowering plants (hydrangeas, etc.)
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdreɪndʒiˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / haɪˌdrændʒɪˈiːsiː /; / hʌɪˌdrandʒɪˈiːsiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants that includes hydrangeas and related plants, sometimes classified within the Saxifragaceae family
