link-up

verb
to connect systems
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
to connect two or more devices, systems, or organizations so they can communicate or work together

Examples

To link-up computers
The technicians will link-up the computers to create a shared network.
Several organizations decided to link-up for the charity event.
You need to link-up your phone with the new wireless speaker to play music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connect - to join or fasten together
  • antonym: separate - to keep or set apart
