insincerely

adverb
in a way that is not sincere
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪnˌsɪrli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪnsɪəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not genuine or honest; without sincerity.

Examples

Speak insincerely
He apologized insincerely, and his tone made it clear he did not mean it.
She insincerely praised her colleague's presentation despite thinking it was poorly done.
They insincerely smiled throughout the meeting, hiding their true feelings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sincerely - in a sincere or genuine way
