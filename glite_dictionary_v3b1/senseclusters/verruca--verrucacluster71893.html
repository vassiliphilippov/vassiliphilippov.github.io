verruca

noun
wart; raised viral skin growth
9.2
Pronunciation: / vəˈruːkə / (AmE), / vəˈruːkə / (BrE)
A firm, elevated growth on the skin caused by a viral infection, commonly known as a wart.

Examples

A verruca on foot
Children are often treated for a verruca after swimming in public pools.
Over-the-counter remedies can sometimes remove a verruca, but persistent cases may need medical attention.
The doctor identified the painful skin lesion as a verruca on her foot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wart - a small, hard, benign growth on the skin caused by a virus
