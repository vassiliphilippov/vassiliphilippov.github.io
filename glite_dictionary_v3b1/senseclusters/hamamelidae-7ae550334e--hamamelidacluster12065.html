Hamamelidae

noun
botanical group of primitive woody angiosperms
Pronunciation: / ˌhæməˌmiːlɪˈdiː /; / hæməˌmiːlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌhæməˌmiːlɪˈdiː /; / hæməˌmiːlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a former group of mostly woody, primitive flowering plants (angiosperms) with simple flowers, including families like oaks and birches
