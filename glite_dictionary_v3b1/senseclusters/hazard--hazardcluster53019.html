hazard

verb
venture a guess or suggestion
9.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhæ.zərd/ (AmE), /ˈhæ.zəd/ (BrE)

Examples

To hazard a guess

If I had to hazard a guess, I'd say the book was written in the 1980s.
She was reluctant to hazard an opinion on the controversial subject.
