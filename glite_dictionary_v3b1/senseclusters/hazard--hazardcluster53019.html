hazard

verb
venture a guess or suggestion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæzɚd / (AmE), / ˈhazəd / (BrE)
to venture or put forward a guess or suggestion despite the risk of being wrong or refuted

Examples

To hazard a guess
Can anyone hazard a suggestion for solving this problem?
If I had to hazard a guess, I'd say the book was written in the 1980s.
She was reluctant to hazard an opinion on the controversial subject.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suggest - to put forward an idea or plan for consideration
  • synonym: venture - to dare to do or say something that may be dangerous or unpleasant
