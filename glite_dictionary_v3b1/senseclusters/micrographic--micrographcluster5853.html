micrographic

adjective
microscopic imaging
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by microscopic photography or imaging

Examples

Micrographic image analysis
The scientist analyzed the micrographic details of the cell structure.
A micrographic examination can reveal features invisible to the naked eye.
The report included several micrographic images of the mineral samples.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macroscopic - large enough to be visible without a microscope
  • synonym: microscopic - so small as to be visible only with a microscope
