one

adjective
of the same kind or type
Pronunciation: / wʌn / (AmE), / wʌn / (BrE)
being a single unit, individual, or undivided; identical or united in type or purpose

Examples

There is one apple left on the table.
In times of crisis, the nation stands as one.
The committee spoke with one voice when announcing their decision.
They must act as one to achieve their common goal.
