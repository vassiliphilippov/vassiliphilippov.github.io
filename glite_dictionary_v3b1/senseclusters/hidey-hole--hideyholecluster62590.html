hidey-hole

noun
secret hiding place
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdi hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdi həʊl / (BrE)
a small, secret place where someone or something can be hidden or kept safe

Examples

A hidey-hole in bushes
The children built a hidey-hole in the bushes at the end of the garden.
He kept his old letters in a hidey-hole behind the bookshelf.
Whenever she felt overwhelmed, she retreated to her secret hidey-hole to relax.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiding place - a place where someone or something can be hidden
  • synonym: refuge - a safe place from danger or trouble
