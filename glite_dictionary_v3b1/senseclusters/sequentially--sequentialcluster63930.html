sequentially

adverb
in order; one after another
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkwɛnʃəlli / (AmE), / sɪˈkwɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that follows a particular order, one after the other.

Examples

Arranged sequentially by date
Each photo in the album is arranged sequentially according to the date it was taken.
Events in the novel are presented sequentially, making the plot easy to follow.
The files were named sequentially from 001 to 100.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consecutively - one after another in order, without interruption
