leaf-cutter

noun
bee that cuts leaves to build its nest
Pronunciation: / ˈlifˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈliːfˌkʌtə / (BrE)
A type of bee that cuts pieces from leaves or flowers to line its nest.

Examples

A leaf-cutter bee
A leaf-cutter can be seen carrying fragments back to its nest.
Gardeners sometimes notice damage caused by leaf-cutters around midsummer.
The leaf-cutter lines each brood cell with carefully cut leaves.
