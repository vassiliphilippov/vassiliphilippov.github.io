tendergreen

noun
Asiatic vegetable plant, edible leaves and root crown
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndərˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
An Asiatic plant (Brassica rapa) cultivated for its swollen root crown and edible leaves, used as a vegetable.

Examples

Chopped tendergreen leaves
She added chopped tendergreen to the soup for extra flavor and nutrition.
The farmer's market had fresh tendergreen for sale alongside other leafy vegetables.
They harvested the tendergreens early in the season for use in salads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mustard greens - the edible leaves of certain varieties of Brassica, similar to tendergreen
