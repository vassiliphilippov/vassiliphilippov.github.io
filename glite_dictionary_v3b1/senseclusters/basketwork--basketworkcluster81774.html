basketwork

noun
woven basket pattern
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbæs.kɪ.twɜːrk/ /; / /ˈbæs.kə.twɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈbæs.kɪ.twɜːk/ /; / /ˈbæs.kə.twɜːk/ (BrE)
