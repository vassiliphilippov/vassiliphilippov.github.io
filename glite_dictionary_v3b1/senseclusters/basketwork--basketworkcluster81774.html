basketwork

noun
woven basket pattern
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskətˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌwɜːk /; / ˈbɑːskɪtwɜːk / (BrE)
objects, patterns, or the craft of making things by weaving flexible materials together, like baskets or mats
