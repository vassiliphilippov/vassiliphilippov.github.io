educate

verb
to teach or train someone
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdʒəˌkeɪt /; / ˈɛdʒʊˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛdjʊkeɪt /; / ˈɛd.jʊ.keɪt / (BrE)
to give someone knowledge or skills through teaching or training, especially over a period of time
