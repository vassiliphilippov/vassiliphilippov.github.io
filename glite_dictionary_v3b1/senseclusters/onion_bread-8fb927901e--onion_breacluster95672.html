onion bread

noun
bread containing minced onions
Pronunciation: / ˈʌnjən brɛd / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən brɛd / (BrE)
bread made with finely minced onions mixed into the dough

Examples

A loaf of onion bread
I picked up a loaf of onion bread from the bakery for tonight's dinner.
She enjoyed toasted onion bread with a spread of butter in the morning.
The subtle flavor of onion bread makes it perfect for sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: onion roll - a roll baked with onions, often with onion pieces on top or in the dough
  • synonym: rye bread - bread made with rye flour, sometimes combined with other ingredients like onions
