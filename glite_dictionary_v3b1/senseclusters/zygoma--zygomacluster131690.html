zygoma

noun
bony arch of the cheek
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈɡoʊmə / (AmE), / zaɪˈɡəʊmə / (BrE)
the bony arch of the cheek formed by the union of the cheekbone and the temporal bone

Examples

The zygoma bone
A fracture to the zygoma can significantly affect the appearance of the face.
Surgeons repaired the patient's zygoma after the accident.
The zygoma creates the prominent curve of the human cheek.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zygomatic bone - the bone that forms the prominence of the cheek
