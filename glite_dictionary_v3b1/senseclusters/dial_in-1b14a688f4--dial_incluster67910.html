dial in

verb
connect to a call or adjust precisely
2.3
Pronunciation: / daɪəl ɪn / (AmE), / daɪəl ɪn / (BrE)
to connect to a telephone call or electronic meeting by entering a number or code; also, to adjust something precisely.

Examples

To dial in a meeting
After several tests, the engineer managed to dial in the correct settings for the equipment.
Please dial in tomorrow for the project briefing if you can't attend in person.
You need to dial in to the meeting ten minutes early so we can start on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call in - to make a telephone call to a place, especially a radio or TV program
