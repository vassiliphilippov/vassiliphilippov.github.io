tonsilla adenoidea

noun
adenoidal lymphatic tissue in the nasopharynx
8.0
Pronunciation: / tɑnˈsɪl.ə ˌæd.ɪˈnɔɪ.di.ə / (AmE), / tɒnˈsɪl.ə ˌæd.ɪˈnɔɪ.di.ə / (BrE)
A collection of lymphatic tissue located on the posterior wall and roof of the nasopharynx, commonly known as the adenoids.

Examples

Enlarged tonsilla adenoidea
Enlargement of the tonsilla adenoidea can cause breathing difficulties in children.
Inflammation of the tonsilla adenoidea is often referred to as adenoiditis.
The surgeon recommended removal of the tonsilla adenoidea to relieve chronic infections.
