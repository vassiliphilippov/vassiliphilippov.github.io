magnoliopsid

noun
flowering plant with two cotyledons
9.2
Pronunciation: / mægˌnoʊliˈɑːpsɪd / (AmE), / mæɡˌnəʊlɪˈɒpsɪd / (BrE)
A flowering plant of the group characterized by having two cotyledons (seed leaves) and stems that grow by depositing new tissue on the outside; a dicotyledon.
