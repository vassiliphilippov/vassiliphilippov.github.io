silver fox

noun
color morph of the red fox with silver-tipped fur
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ fɑks / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə fɒks / (BrE)
a red fox in a color phase where its fur is blackish with silvery-white tips, valued for its pelt

Examples

Wild silver fox
A silver fox has a striking coat with black fur and white-tipped hairs.
The fur of the silver fox was once highly prized by European royalty.
Today, most silver foxes are bred on farms for their pelts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: red fox - a species of fox with reddish fur, Vulpes vulpes
