bandmaster

noun
conductor of a band
Pronunciation: / ˈbændˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈbændˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the conductor or leader of a musical band, especially a military or marching band

Examples

The bandmaster leads
After the rehearsal, the bandmaster gave feedback to each section of musicians.
Every year, the bandmaster selects new pieces for the school band's performance.
The bandmaster raised his baton to signal the start of the parade march.
