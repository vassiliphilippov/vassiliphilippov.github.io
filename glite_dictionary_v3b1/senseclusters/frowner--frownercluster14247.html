frowner

noun
person who frowns
Pronunciation: / ˈfraʊnər / (AmE), / ˈfraʊnə / (BrE)
a person who frowns or often shows displeasure by contracting their brow

Examples

A strict frowner
The new manager was a notorious frowner who rarely showed any sign of happiness.
Children tried to avoid the frowner at the playground because he always looked upset.
Despite being a frowner most of the time, she occasionally surprised everyone with a warm smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scowler - a person who looks at others with an angry or displeased expression
  • antonym: smiler - a person who smiles frequently
