hymnary

noun
book of hymns
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪmˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈhɪm.nə.ri / (BrE)
a book containing a collection of hymns, typically used in religious services

Examples

The hymnary book

The church introduced a new hymnary during the Sunday service.
