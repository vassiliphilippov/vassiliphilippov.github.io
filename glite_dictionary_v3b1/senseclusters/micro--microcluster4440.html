micro

noun
small computer
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊ / (BrE)
a microcomputer, especially a small or personal computer used by individuals or in homes and offices

Examples

A micro computer
In the early 1980s, many schools introduced students to computers using a micro in the classroom.
He still keeps his first micro as a reminder of how technology has changed.
Many classic video games were originally played on a micro at home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mainframe - a large, powerful computer used mainly by large organizations
  • synonym: personal computer - a computer designed for use by one person at a time
