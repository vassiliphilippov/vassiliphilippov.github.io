WIP

noun
unfinished project
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
an unfinished project or piece of work that is still being developed or completed

Examples

An artist's WIP
The artist shared a WIP of her latest painting on social media.
Many writers keep several WIPs before deciding which story to finish first.
He asked for feedback on his WIP to improve the final version.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draft - a preliminary version of a written work
