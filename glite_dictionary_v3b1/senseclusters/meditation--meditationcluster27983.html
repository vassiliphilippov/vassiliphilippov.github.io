meditation

noun
contemplation on spiritual or philosophical matters
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
Deep and focused contemplation, especially on spiritual or philosophical matters, often practised as a religious or self-improvement activity.

Examples

Daily meditation practice
Buddhist monks are renowned for their long hours of meditation in search of enlightenment.
During the retreat, participants engaged in silent meditation on spiritual questions.
She practices meditation every morning to calm her mind and find inner peace.
