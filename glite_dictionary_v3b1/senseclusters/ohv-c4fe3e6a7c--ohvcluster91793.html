OHV

noun
off-road vehicle
Pronunciation: / oʊ eɪtʃ vi / (AmE), / əʊ eɪtʃ viː / (BrE)
a vehicle designed for use off public roads, such as on rough terrain or trails

Examples

OHV trail vehicle
The national forest has special trails for OHVs to protect the environment.
You need a permit to drive your OHV in this recreation area.
Many outdoor enthusiasts use their OHVs to explore remote mountain paths.

  • antonym: car - a road vehicle for regular public roads
