navigation

noun
menus and structure in websites/apps
3.6
Pronunciation: /næ.və.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /næ.vɪ.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /næ.vɪ.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /næ.və.ˈɡeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the design, arrangement, or system of menus, links, and controls in a website or app that lets users move between pages or sections.

Examples

Website navigation
The website's navigation menu helps users move quickly through product pages and information.
Good navigation makes it easy for visitors to find pricing, contact details, and support.
The app's minimalist navigation uses icons at the bottom of the screen for switching sections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menu - a list of options or pages in a website or app used to move between content.
  • synonym: sitemap - a structured list or map of a website's pages that helps users and search engines find content.
