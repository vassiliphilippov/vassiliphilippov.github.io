ungentlemanlike

adjective
not befitting a gentleman
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛntlmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛntlmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Not characteristic of or befitting a gentleman; lacking the qualities expected of a gentleman, such as courtesy or honor.

Examples

Ungentlemanlike conduct
His ungentlemanlike conduct at the meeting surprised everyone.
It was ungentlemanlike of him to insult the waiter in public.
She found his refusal to apologize rather ungentlemanlike.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentlemanly - having the qualities expected of a gentleman; courteous and honorable
