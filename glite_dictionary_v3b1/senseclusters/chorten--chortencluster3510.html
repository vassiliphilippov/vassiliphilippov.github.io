chorten

noun
Tibetan Buddhist stupa
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔːrtən / (AmE), / ˈtʃɔːtɛn / (BrE)
a Buddhist monument or stupa, especially found in Tibet and the Himalayan region, used as a place of meditation or worship

Examples

A chorten monument
The villagers gathered around the chorten to celebrate the annual festival.
Colorful prayer flags fluttered in the wind near the ancient chorten on the hillside.
Tourists admired the intricate carvings on the chorten during their trek through the Himalayas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stupa - a dome-shaped Buddhist shrine or monument
