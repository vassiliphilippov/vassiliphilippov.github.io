gather

verb
search for and collect food in nature
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæðɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡæðə / (BrE)
to search for and collect (food, such as fruits, nuts, or plants) in the wild

Examples

To gather wild berries
Every summer, the children would gather wild strawberries from the fields near their home.
In many cultures, people gather mushrooms and herbs from the forest to supplement their diets.
When food was scarce, early humans had to gather edible roots and berries to survive.
