funder

noun
financial supporter
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndər / (AmE), / ˈfʌndə / (BrE)
a person or organization that provides money to support a project, activity, or cause

Examples

A generous funder
The museum found a generous funder to help with its renovation project.
Many research studies rely on a single major funder for financial support.
Without a committed funder, the charity would not be able to continue its work.
The museum was able to expand its collection with the help of a generous funder.
Several funders contributed to the success of the community project.
Without a reliable funder, the research would not have been possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donor - a person who gives money or goods to help a cause
  • antonym: recipient - a person or organization that receives something, especially money
  • synonym: sponsor - a person or organization that supports something financially
