Synonyms

  • backer
    3.0 4.27/m
    a backer gives financial support to a project or business, while a funder is the neutral term for any money provider.
  • sponsor
    3.7 28/m
    a sponsor gives money and public support to events or teams, while a funder is a neutral source of financial backing.
  • contributor
    3.2 4.57/m
    a contributor gives or pledges money, often small amounts for a specific cause, while a funder may finance whole projects.
  • donor
    3.0 16/m
    a donor gives money or goods mainly to charities or people, while a funder pays to support projects, activities, or causes.
  • benefactor
    4.6 2.64/m
    a benefactor gives large gifts out of generosity and often gains no public credit, while a funder simply provides money.
  • patron
    3.5 6.14/m
    a patron supports artists or organizations often with regular payments and status, while a funder is a general money provider.
  • bankroller
    6.0 0.02/m
    a bankroller provides funds often informally or secretly for ventures, while a funder is the neutral, standard term for supporters.
