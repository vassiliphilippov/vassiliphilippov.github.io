cleanup brigade

noun
group organized to clean up an area
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklinʌp brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / ˈkliːnʌp brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
a group of people organized to clean up a particular area, often after an event or disaster

Examples

The cleanup brigade team
After the festival ended, a cleanup brigade arrived to pick up litter and restore the grounds.
The mayor praised the efforts of the cleanup brigade for their swift response after the storm.
Volunteers formed a cleanup brigade to help clear debris from the community park.
