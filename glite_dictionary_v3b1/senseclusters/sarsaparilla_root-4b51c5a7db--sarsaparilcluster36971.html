sarsaparilla root

noun
dried root used as a flavoring or remedy
Pronunciation: / ˌsæspəˈrɪlə ruːt / (AmE), / ˌsɑːspəˈrɪlə ruːt / (BrE)
the dried root of various plants of the genus Smilax, used especially as a flavoring agent or in herbal remedies
