internegative

adjective
photographic intermediate
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈnɛɡətɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnɛɡətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or used as an intermediate negative in photographic or motion-picture processing, especially for making release prints.

Examples

Internegative film process
The internegative process helps preserve the quality of the original film during duplication.
A technician must handle internegative materials carefully to avoid introducing defects.
Special equipment is required to produce internegative copies for mass distribution.
