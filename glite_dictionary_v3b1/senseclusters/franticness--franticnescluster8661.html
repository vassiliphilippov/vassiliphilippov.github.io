franticness

noun
state of frenzy
Pronunciation: / ˈfræntɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈfræntɪknəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being frantic, wildly excited, or anxious

Examples

The franticness in voice
The franticness in her voice made everyone pay attention to the emergency.
He struggled to stay focused despite the franticness of the busy office.
During the holidays, the franticness at the shopping mall can be overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the state of being calm and peaceful
  • synonym: frenzy - a state of wild excitement or agitation
