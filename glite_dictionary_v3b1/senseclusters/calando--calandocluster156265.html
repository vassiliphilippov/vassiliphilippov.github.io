calando

adjective
gradually decreasing in tempo and volume (music)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑndoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlɑːndəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) gradually decreasing in both tempo and volume.

Examples

To play calando
As the piece neared its end, the pianist played calando to create a gentle finish.
The conductor signaled for a calando passage, reducing both speed and dynamics.
The orchestra slowed and softened, following the calando marked in the score.
