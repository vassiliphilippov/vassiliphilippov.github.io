assign

verb
to place into a specific category or position
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪn / (AmE), / əˈsaɪn / (BrE)
to decide or specify a particular place, role, or category for something, especially as part of a system or classification

Examples

To assign a place
After much debate, the curator assigned the artifact to the Neolithic period.
She assigned the document to the 'confidential' file in the company records.
The committee will assign each project to a relevant category based on its scope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allocate - to assign or set apart for a specific purpose
  • synonym: categorize - to place into classes or groups; to classify
