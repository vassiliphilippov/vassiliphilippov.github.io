pastelike

adjective
resembling paste in pallor
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪst.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪst.laɪk / (BrE)
having a pale, pallid color or appearance resembling that of paste.

Examples

A pastelike color
Her face looked pastelike after days of illness.
His complexion became pastelike from lack of sunlight.
The actor's makeup gave him a pastelike appearance under the theater lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pasty - unhealthily pale or whitish in complexion
