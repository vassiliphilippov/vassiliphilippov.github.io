unfitness

noun
state of being unable to perform
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtnəs / (BrE)
the state of lacking the ability, strength, or qualification to perform a task or role

Examples

Unfitness for the task
Despite repeated training, her unfitness for the marathon was obvious.
His unfitness for the job became clear during the interview process.
The court ruled on his unfitness to stand trial due to medical reasons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fitness - the quality of being able to perform or be suitable
  • synonym: inability - lack of ability to do something
  • synonym: incapacity - lack of power, ability, or fitness
