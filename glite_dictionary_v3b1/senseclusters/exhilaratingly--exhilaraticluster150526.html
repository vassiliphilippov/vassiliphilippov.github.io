exhilaratingly

adverb
in an exciting way
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɪləˌreɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɪləreɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that makes you feel very happy, excited, or full of energy

Examples

Feels exhilaratingly fresh
The mountain air felt exhilaratingly fresh as we reached the summit.
She laughed exhilaratingly after hearing the good news.
The team played exhilaratingly in the final minutes, thrilling the entire crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excitingly - in a way that causes excitement
