inkstone

noun
tool for grinding ink
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɪŋk.stoʊn/ (AmE), /ˈɪŋk.stəʊn/ (BrE)
a flat stone used to grind solid ink with water for writing or painting, especially in East Asian calligraphy

Examples

A inkstone for grinding
The artist poured a little water onto the inkstone and began grinding the inkstick.
Traditional Chinese calligraphy often requires a brush, an inkstone, and special paper.
He inherited a beautifully carved inkstone from his grandfather, who was a calligrapher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calligraphy - the art of beautiful handwriting, often using ink and brush
  • synonym: inkstick - a solid stick of ink used with an inkstone
