madrone

noun
wood from madrone tree
Pronunciation: / məˈdroʊn / (AmE), / məˈdrəʊn / (BrE)
the wood or timber from the madrone tree, often used for firewood, smoking meat, or woodworking

Examples

Madrone firewood
They stacked seasoned madrone in the shed to use for slow-burning winter fires.
The craftsman carved a beautiful bowl from a piece of madrone.
Many people prefer madrone for smoking meat because of its pleasant aroma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbutus - wood from trees of the Arbutus genus, related to madrone
  • synonym: oak - hardwood commonly used for firewood and woodworking
