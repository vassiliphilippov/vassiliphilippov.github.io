El Greco

proper noun
Spanish painter of Greek origin, known for religious art
Pronunciation: / ɛl ˈɡreɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ɛl ˈɡrɛkəʊ / (BrE)
(1541–1614) A Spanish painter of Greek origin, renowned for his religious works featuring elongated figures and dramatic color contrasts.

Examples

El Greco painter
El Greco is celebrated for his distinctive style, which profoundly influenced later Western art.
During my visit to Toledo, I saw several masterpieces by El Greco in the cathedral.
The paintings of El Greco often feature expressive faces and vibrant colors.
