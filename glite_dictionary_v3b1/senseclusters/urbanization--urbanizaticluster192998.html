urbanization

noun
the growth of cities and urban life
Pronunciation: / ˌɝː.bən.əˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɜrbənəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɝː.bən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɝː.bə.nɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɜː.bən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɜːbənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which an area becomes more like a city, with more people living and working in towns and cities
