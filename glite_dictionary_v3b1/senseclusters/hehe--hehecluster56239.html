hehe

verb
to laugh quietly
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːˌhiː / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhiː / (BrE)
to make a short, quiet laugh or to write or say 'hehe' to show mild amusement or playfulness

Examples

To hehe quietly
She couldn't help but hehe when her friend made a silly face during the meeting.
He typed hehe at the end of his message to show he was joking.
Whenever he feels awkward, he tends to hehe softly instead of speaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chuckle - to laugh quietly or inwardly
  • synonym: giggle - to laugh in a light, silly way
  • antonym: sob - to cry noisily, taking short, sudden breaths
