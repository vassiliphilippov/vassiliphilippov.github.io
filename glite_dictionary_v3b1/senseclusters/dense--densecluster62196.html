dense

adjective
slow to understand; stupid (of a person)
4.7
Pronunciation: / dɛns / (AmE), / dɛns / (BrE)
(of a person) slow to understand; lacking intelligence or insight; stupid.

Examples

Dense student
I tried to explain it clearly, but he was too dense to get the point.
She called her brother dense after he missed another obvious joke.
Sometimes students who seem dense just need more time to learn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dim - (of a person) not smart or quick-witted
  • synonym: dull - slow to understand; not intelligent
  • antonym: intelligent - having or showing intelligence, especially of a high level
