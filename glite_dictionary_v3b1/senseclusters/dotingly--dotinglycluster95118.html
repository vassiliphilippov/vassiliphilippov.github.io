dotingly

adverb
with excessive affection
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdəʊtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows excessive love, affection, or fondness

Examples

Look dotingly at a child
He watched his granddaughter perform in the play dotingly, unable to hide his pride.
The old couple looked at each other dotingly after so many years together.
She dotingly prepared her son's favorite meal every weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fondly - in a loving or affectionate way
  • antonym: indifferently - in a way that shows no interest or concern
