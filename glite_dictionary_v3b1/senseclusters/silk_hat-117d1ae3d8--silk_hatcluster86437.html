silk hat

noun
tall formal man's hat, often covered with silk
Pronunciation: / sɪlk hæt / (AmE), / sɪlk hæt / (BrE)
A formal man's hat with a tall crown, traditionally covered with silk or sometimes beaver fur; also known as a top hat.

Examples

A silk hat style
Collectors value a vintage silk hat in pristine condition.
He tipped his silk hat politely as he entered the ballroom.
The conductor wore a silk hat and tails to the evening performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bowler hat - a man's hard felt hat with a rounded crown
  • synonym: top hat - a tall, flat-crowned, broad-brimmed formal hat for men
