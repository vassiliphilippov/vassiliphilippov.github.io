mantling

noun
heraldic drapery
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmæntlɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative drapery or cloth, especially the flowing cloth depicted around a helmet in heraldry

Examples

Heraldic mantling
The knight's shield was adorned with elaborate mantling in red and gold.
In medieval art, mantling often appears as flowing fabric around helmets.
The family crest featured blue mantling to symbolize loyalty and truth.
