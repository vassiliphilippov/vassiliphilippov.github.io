cross-pollinating

adjective
relating to cross-pollination
5.4
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˈpɑləˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / krɒs ˈpɒlɪneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the transfer of pollen from the flower of one plant to another, resulting in cross-pollination.

Examples

Cross-pollinating plants
Scientists are studying the effects of cross-pollinating species to improve crop yields.
Some fruit trees rely on insects for their cross-pollinating process.
The gardener was careful to keep the cross-pollinating plants separated from those that self-pollinate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: self-pollinating - relating to pollination within the same plant
