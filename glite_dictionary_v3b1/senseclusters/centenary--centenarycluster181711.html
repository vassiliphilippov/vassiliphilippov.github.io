centenary

adjective
relating to 100 years
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɛnəˌri / (AmE), / sɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
relating to or marking the completion of a period of 100 years

Examples

Centenary edition
The centenary edition of the novel was published on the author's 100th birthday.
The museum held a centenary exhibition to honor its 100 years of history.
They organized a centenary celebration to commemorate the founding of the school.
