common wart

noun
benign, rough skin growth
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən wɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən wɔːt / (BrE)
a small, benign growth on the skin, typically with a rough surface, caused by a viral infection

Examples

A common wart on hand
A common wart often appears on the hands and is usually painless.
He was worried when he noticed a common wart growing on his knuckle.
My doctor removed the common wart on my finger during a short procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wart - a small, hard, benign growth on the skin, caused by a virus
