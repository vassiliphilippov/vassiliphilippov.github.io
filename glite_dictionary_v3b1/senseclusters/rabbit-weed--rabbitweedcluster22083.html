rabbit-weed

noun
low-growing southwestern US subshrub with yellow flowers

Pronunciation: / ˈræb.ɪtˌwid / (AmE), / ˈræb.ɪtˌwiːd / (BrE)
A low-growing, sticky subshrub native to the southwestern United States, characterized by narrow linear leaves and numerous tiny yellow flower heads.
