maunder

verb
talk aimlessly or ramble
5.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔndər / (AmE), / ˈmɔːndə / (BrE)
to talk in a rambling, aimless, or indistinct way, often about unimportant things

Examples

To maunder about nothing
He tends to maunder when he's tired, drifting from topic to topic.

The old man would often maunder on about his childhood for hours.

The old man would maunder to himself as he walked down the street.
