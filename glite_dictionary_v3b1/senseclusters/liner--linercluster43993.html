liner

noun
a large commercial passenger ship
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a large commercial ship, especially one that carries passengers on a regular route or schedule

Examples

A cruise liner
Many immigrants crossed the Atlantic on a passenger liner in the early 20th century.
The luxury liner set sail from Southampton to New York last night.
The shipping company launched a new ocean liner designed for transatlantic travel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise ship - a large passenger ship used for pleasure voyages
  • antonym: freighter - a ship that carries cargo but not passengers
