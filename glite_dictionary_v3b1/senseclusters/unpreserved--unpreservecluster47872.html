unpreserved

adjective
not protected from decay
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprɪˈzɜrvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnprɪˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
not protected or treated to prevent decay, damage, or spoilage

Examples

Unpreserved wood
The unpreserved wooden fence began to rot after several rainy seasons.
Fresh, unpreserved foods should be eaten quickly to avoid spoilage.
Many artifacts were lost because they remained unpreserved for decades.

Related Terms

  • antonym: preserved - protected or treated to prevent decay or damage
  • synonym: untreated - not given special treatment or protection
