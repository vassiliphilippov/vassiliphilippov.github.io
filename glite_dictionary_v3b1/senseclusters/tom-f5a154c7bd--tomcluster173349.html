Tom

noun
offensive slur for a subservient Black man

Pronunciation: /tɑːm/ (AmE), /tɒm/ (BrE)
An offensive and derogatory term for a Black man considered to be excessively subservient or deferential to white people.

The article explained how the term Tom has been used to demean Black men seen as too deferential to white people.
