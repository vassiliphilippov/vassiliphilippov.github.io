cantaloup

noun
type of muskmelon with orange flesh
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.təˌluːp /; / ˈkæntəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tə.luːp /; / ˈkantələʊp / (BrE)
a type of melon with a rough, tan rind and sweet, orange or yellowish flesh, commonly eaten as a fruit
