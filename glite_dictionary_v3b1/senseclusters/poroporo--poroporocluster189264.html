poroporo

noun
Australian plant with purple flowers and yellow fruit
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔːroʊˈpɔːroʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɒrəˈpɒrəʊ / (BrE)
An Australian annual plant sometimes grown for its clusters of purple flowers and yellow, egg-shaped edible fruit.

Examples

The poroporo fruit
Aboriginal Australians have used the fruit of the poroporo in traditional cuisine.
Some gardeners cultivate poroporo for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
The poroporo produces attractive purple flowers followed by yellow berries.
