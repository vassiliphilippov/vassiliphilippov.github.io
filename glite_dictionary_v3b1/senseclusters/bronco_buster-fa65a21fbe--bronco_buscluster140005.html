bronco buster

noun
person who breaks wild horses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːŋkoʊ ˈbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋkəʊ ˈbʌstə / (BrE)
a person, especially in the western United States, who breaks wild or untrained horses for riding

Examples

A bronco buster rides
At the county fair, the young bronco buster demonstrated his remarkable skill with a spirited mustang.
She dreamed of becoming a bronco buster and traveling in rodeos across the West.
The legendary bronco buster could tame even the wildest of horses on the range.
