call on the carpet

verb
formally reprimand; censure
Pronunciation: / kɔl ɑn ðə ˈkɑr.pɪt / (AmE), / kɔːl ɒn ðə ˈkɑː.pɪt / (BrE)
to severely reprimand or censure someone, especially in a formal or authoritative manner.

Examples

To call on the carpet someone
After the error was discovered, the manager called Tom on the carpet during the morning meeting.
She was called on the carpet for missing several deadlines in a row.
The employee knew he would be called on the carpet after the client lodged a complaint.
