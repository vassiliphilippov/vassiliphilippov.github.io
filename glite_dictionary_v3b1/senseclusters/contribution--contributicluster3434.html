contribution

noun
voluntary gift of money, service, or ideas
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃən /; / ˌkɑːntrəˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
something (such as help, ideas, time, or effort) that you give to help achieve something or support a cause

Examples

Contribution to a project
He made a significant contribution to the project by offering his expertise.
Her generous contribution helped fund the new community center.
The museum is grateful for every contribution it receives from supporters.
Every contribution counts, no matter how small, when you're raising money for charity.
Her generous contribution to the scholarship fund helped several students attend college.
The community made a joint contribution to support families after the hurricane.
Every team member's contribution is essential for the project's overall success.
His contribution in the meeting helped clarify the main issues and led to productive solutions.
She was praised for her significant contribution to the charity's successful fundraising campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donation - something that is given to a charity or cause, usually money
  • synonym: effort - physical or mental energy used to achieve something
  • synonym: gift - something voluntarily given to another person or group
  • antonym: hindrance - something that impedes or makes achievement more difficult
  • synonym: input - something that is put in or contributed, often effort or ideas
  • antonym: withdrawal - the act of removing or taking something away
