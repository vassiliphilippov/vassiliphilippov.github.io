Bibos

noun
genus of wild oxen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪbɒs / (AmE), / ˈbiːbɒs / (BrE)
a genus (or former genus) of large wild oxen found in South and Southeast Asia, including species such as the gaur and banteng
