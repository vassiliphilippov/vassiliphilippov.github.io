clownery

noun
clown performance
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊnəri / (AmE), / ˈklaʊnəri / (BrE)
the art, practice, or performance of entertaining as a clown, often involving comic acts or antics

Examples

Circus clownery
The children's laughter filled the tent during the performer's hilarious clownery at the circus.
He studied clownery to perfect his comic timing and physical humor for stage shows.
Modern clownery often combines traditional slapstick with creative storytelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clowning - the act of performing as a clown
  • synonym: comedy - entertainment intended to make people laugh
