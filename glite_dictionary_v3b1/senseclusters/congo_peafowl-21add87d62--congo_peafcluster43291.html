Congo peafowl

noun
a brightly colored bird from central Africa
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡoʊ ˈpiˌfaʊl / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ ˈpiːˌfaʊl / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored bird native to the Congo Basin, known for its striking plumage and found only in central Africa.

Examples

The Congo peafowl bird
Researchers observed a pair of Congo peafowl near the rainforest edge.
The Congo peafowl is rarely seen in the wild due to its secretive habits.
Unlike its Asian relatives, the Congo peafowl has a unique coloration suited to its environment.
