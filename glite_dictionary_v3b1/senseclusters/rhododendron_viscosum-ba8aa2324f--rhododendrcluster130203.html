Rhododendron viscosum

noun
swamp shrub with fragrant white or pink flowers
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊdəˈdɛndrən vɪˈkɑsəm / (AmE), / ˌrəʊdəˈdɛndrən vɪˈkɒsəm / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to swamps of the eastern United States, with small white to pinkish, fragrant flowers resembling honeysuckle.

Examples
