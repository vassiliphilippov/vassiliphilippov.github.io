heart

verb
press the heart icon to like online
Pronunciation: / h51rt / (AmE), / h51d0t / (BrE)
(informal) To show approval, support, or affection for a post, image, or message on social media by clicking or tapping its heart icon.

Examples

To heart a post

She hearted several posts to show support for the campaign last night.
When users heart a tweet, the author receives a notification and feels appreciated.
