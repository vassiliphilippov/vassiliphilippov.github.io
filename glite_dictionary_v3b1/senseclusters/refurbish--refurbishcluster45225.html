refurbish

verb
to renovate or redecorate
1.0
Pronunciation: / riˈfɝː.bɪʃ / (AmE), / rɪˈfɜː.bɪʃ / (BrE)
to make (something, especially a building or furniture) clean, bright, or like new again by repairing or decorating it

Examples

The company spent millions to refurbish its headquarters after the fire.
They decided to refurbish the old cottage before moving in.
