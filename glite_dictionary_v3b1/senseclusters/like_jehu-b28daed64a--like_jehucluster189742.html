like Jehu

adverb
in a wild or energetic manner
Pronunciation: / laɪk ˈdʒiːhu / (AmE), / laɪk ˈdʒiːhjuː / (BrE)
with great energy or recklessness; in a wild or frenzied manner (especially when dancing, moving, or acting)

Examples

Dancing like Jehu
After hearing his favorite song, Tom started dancing like Jehu in the middle of the room.
The children ran like Jehu across the playground, laughing and shouting with excitement.
When the doors opened, shoppers rushed in like Jehu to grab the best deals.
