weight down

verb
make heavy or burdened
5.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /weɪt daʊn/ (AmE), /weɪt daʊn/ (BrE)
To make someone or something heavy or burdened, especially by adding a physical load.

Examples

The wet snow weighted down the branches until some of them snapped.
