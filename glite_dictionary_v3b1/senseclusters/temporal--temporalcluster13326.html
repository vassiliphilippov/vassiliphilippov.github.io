temporal

adjective
worldly rather than spiritual
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərəl /; / ˈtɛmpɚəl /; / ˈtɛmp(ə)rəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl /; / ˈtɛmp(ə)rəl / (BrE)
relating to the material or earthly world, as opposed to spiritual or eternal matters
