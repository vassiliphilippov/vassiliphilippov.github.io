saddle oyster

noun
type of thin-shelled bivalve mollusc
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl ˌɔɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈsæd(ə)l ˌɔɪstə / (BrE)
A thin-shelled bivalve mollusc with the right valve deeply notched, belonging to the family Anomiidae.

Examples

The saddle oyster shell
Marine biologists studied the habitat of the saddle oyster along the coastal reefs.
The shell of the saddle oyster is delicate and easily broken.
We found a saddle oyster clinging to the underside of the rock at low tide.
