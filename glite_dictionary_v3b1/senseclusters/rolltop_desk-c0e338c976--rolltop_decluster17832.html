rolltop desk

noun
desk with rolling cover
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlˌtɑp dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlˌtɒp dɛsk / (BrE)
a desk with a flexible curved cover that can be rolled down to close the working surface

Examples

A rolltop desk cover
He kept his important documents locked inside the rolltop desk in his study.
The antique rolltop desk was passed down through several generations of the family.
After finishing her work, she slid the cover of the rolltop desk closed for the night.
