calorie

noun
unit of energy (food/nutrition)
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləri / (AmE), / ˈkaləri /; / ˈkæləri / (BrE)
a unit of energy, especially used to measure the energy content of food

Examples

A calorie count
Athletes often use up thousands of calories during intense training sessions.
It is important to know how many calories you consume each day.
This snack contains only fifty calories per serving.
Nutrition labels often list the number of calories per serving.
One calorie is the amount of energy needed to raise 1 gram of water by 1°C.
Scientists accurately measure the calorie content of different foods in a laboratory.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: joule - basic SI unit of energy
  • synonym: kilojoule - SI unit of energy, used as alternative to calorie
