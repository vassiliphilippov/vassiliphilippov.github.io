ungentleness

noun
lack of gentleness
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛntlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛntlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being harsh, unkind, or lacking gentleness

Examples

Her ungentleness surprised
Her ungentleness surprised everyone who expected a softer response.
The ungentleness in his voice made the criticism feel even harsher.
Children can be hurt by the ungentleness of careless words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleness - the quality of being kind, mild, or tender
  • synonym: harshness - the quality of being severe or unkind
