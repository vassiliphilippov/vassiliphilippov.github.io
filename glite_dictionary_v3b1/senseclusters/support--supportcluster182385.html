support

noun
bearing weight or strengthening
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a structure or object that holds up or carries the weight of something else

Examples

A metal support
After her injury, she needed a walking stick for extra support.
He reached out and held onto the railing for support while climbing the stairs.
The bridge’s central pillar provides crucial support for the whole structure.
The support under the bridge needed to be reinforced after the storm.
The technician installed a new metal support to stabilize the equipment.
Without a proper support, the shelf could not hold the heavy books.
Each pillar acts as a support for the bridge above.
The heavy shelf was secured with a metal support underneath.
The sculpture required a sturdy support to remain upright.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brace - a device that supports or strengthens a structure
  • synonym: foundation - the lowest load-bearing part of a building, typically below ground level
  • synonym: prop - something used to support or keep something in position
  • synonym: reinforcement - the act of making something stronger
  • antonym: weakening - the process of making or becoming weaker
