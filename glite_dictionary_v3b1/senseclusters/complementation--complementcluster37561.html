Examples

Verb complementation patterns
English verbs show different patterns of complementation, such as taking direct objects or infinitives.
The complementation of the verb 'consider' often involves a clause, as in 'I consider him a friend.'
Understanding complementation helps in constructing complex sentences accurately.
