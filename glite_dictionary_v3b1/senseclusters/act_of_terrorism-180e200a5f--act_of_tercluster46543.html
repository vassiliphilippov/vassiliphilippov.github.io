A deliberate use of violence or the threat of violence, especially against civilians, intended to achieve political, religious, or ideological goals by instilling fear or coercion.

Examples

An act of terrorism
Security measures have been increased to prevent another act of terrorism in the city.
The government condemned the recent act of terrorism that targeted innocent civilians.
The trial focused on the suspects' involvement in an act of terrorism last year.
