respray

noun
repeat spray treatment
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈriːspreɪ / (AmE), / ˈriːspreɪ / (BrE)
an act of spraying something again, especially a new coat of paint or treatment applied by spraying

Examples

A car respray
After the accident, the car needed a complete respray to restore its original color.
The furniture looked brand new after a quick respray with protective coating.
He decided to give his old bicycle a fresh respray before selling it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repaint - to paint something again
  • degree lower: spray - to apply liquid in a fine mist
