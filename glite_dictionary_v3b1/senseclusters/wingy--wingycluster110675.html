wingy

adjective
having wings; quirky
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋi / (BrE)
having or resembling wings, or, informally, being odd or quirky in behavior or appearance

Examples

Wingy sleeves
The costume had large, wingy sleeves that fluttered as she danced.
His wingy ideas always make our meetings more interesting.
The bird looked especially wingy with its feathers sticking out everywhere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - usual or typical; not strange
  • synonym: quirky - unusual or eccentric in a charming way
  • synonym: winged - having wings
