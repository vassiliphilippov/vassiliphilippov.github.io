galvanized

adjective
zinc-coated metal
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɡælvənaɪzd / (BrE)
coated with a protective layer of zinc to prevent rusting

Examples

Galvanized steel fence
The fence was made from galvanized steel to withstand harsh weather conditions.
They used galvanized nails to prevent corrosion in the outdoor project.
A galvanized bucket is ideal for carrying water because it resists rust.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rusted - covered with rust due to oxidation
  • synonym: zinc-plated - covered with a thin layer of zinc
