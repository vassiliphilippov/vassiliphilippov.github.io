restuff

verb
to fill again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈstʌf / (AmE), / ˌriːˈstʌf / (BrE)
to fill or pack something again with stuffing or material to restore its shape or comfort

Examples

To restuff sofa cushions
After years of use, she decided to restuff the sagging sofa cushions.
The teddy bear looked brand new once they restuffed it with fresh cotton.
You can restuff your jacket to make it warmer for the winter.
After years of use, she decided to restuff her favorite armchair to make it comfortable again.
The toy maker offered to restuff old plush animals for customers at the store.
He learned how to restuff pillows to save money and reduce waste.
