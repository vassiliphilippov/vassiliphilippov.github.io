Lacking courage; having a tendency to avoid danger, difficulty, or pain out of fear.

Examples

A cowardly act
His boss accused him of being cowardly when he failed to admit his mistake.
It was a cowardly act to abandon his friends in their time of need.
The cowardly man refused to stand up to the bullies.

  • fearful
