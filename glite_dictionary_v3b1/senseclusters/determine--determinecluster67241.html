Synonyms

  • establish
    4.1 29/m
    establish means to prove or show that something is true, while determine focuses on finding or settling facts.
  • calculate
    2.8 37/m
    calculate means to work out numbers or amounts, while determine has a wider use including study, tests, or measurement.
  • deduce
    4.6 4.45/m
    deduce means to reach a conclusion by reasoning from facts, while determine covers finding facts or deciding their value.
  • find out
    2.1 131/m
    find out is to discover information by effort or asking, while determine suggests a more formal study or precise method.
  • discover
    1.0 120/m
    discover is to find something often unexpectedly, while determine implies a planned search or method to establish a fact.
  • figure out

  • find
