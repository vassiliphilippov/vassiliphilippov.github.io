washbowl

noun
basin for washing hands or face
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌbəʊl / (BrE)
a bowl or basin, either portable or fixed, used for washing hands and face

Examples

A washbowl on dresser
A porcelain washbowl stood on the wooden dresser in the old cottage.
He filled the washbowl with warm water and soap.
She rinsed her hands in the washbowl before dinner.
I splashed cold water from the washbowl onto my face to wake up.
She leaned over the washbowl to brush her teeth before going to sleep.
The hotel room's washbowl was spotless and had fresh soap by the side.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basin - a wide, open container used for holding liquids or for washing
  • synonym: sink - plumbing fixture for washing, kitchen or bathroom
