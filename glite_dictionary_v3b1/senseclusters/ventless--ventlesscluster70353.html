ventless

adjective
without a vent
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈvɛntləs / (BrE)
not having a vent or opening for air to pass through

Examples

A ventless dryer
The new apartment features a ventless dryer that does not require an external exhaust.
Many modern fireplaces are ventless, making installation easier in any room.
A ventless heater can be a good option where venting to the outside is not possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sealed - completely closed so that nothing can enter or leave
  • antonym: vented - having a vent or opening for air to pass through
