photonegative

noun
reversed photographic image
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈnɛɡətɪv / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈnɛɡətɪv / (BrE)
a photographic image in which the light and dark areas are reversed from those in the original scene

Examples

A photonegative strip
The photographer carefully stored each photonegative in a protective sleeve for future use.
You need a photonegative to make traditional prints in a darkroom.
She compared the photonegative to the final photograph to check for any flaws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negative - a photographic image with reversed light and dark areas
  • antonym: photopositive - a photographic image with the same light and dark areas as the original
