Synonyms

  • percolation
    6.4 0.01/m
    percolation is liquid slowly moving through a porous filter or ground, while infiltration means liquid enters and spreads into the material.
  • seepage
    5.7 0.65/m
    seepage is the slow leaking of liquid through small holes or soil, whereas infiltration describes liquid moving into and through a substance.
  • permeation
    7.0 0.08/m
