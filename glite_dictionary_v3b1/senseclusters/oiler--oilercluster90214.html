oiler

noun
worker who lubricates engines or machinery
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈɔɪlə / (BrE)
a worker responsible for lubricating engines or machinery to ensure smooth operation

Examples

Factory oiler
As an oiler, she ensured that all machines ran smoothly during her shift.
The oiler performed routine maintenance on the ship's engines every morning.
The factory hired another oiler to keep up with the increasing production demands.
