Stanislavsky

proper noun
Russian actor and theatre director
Pronunciation: / stəˈnɪslɑfski / (AmE), / stəˈnɪslɑfski / (BrE)
(1863–1938) Russian actor and theatre director known for developing a system of method acting emphasizing psychological motivation.
