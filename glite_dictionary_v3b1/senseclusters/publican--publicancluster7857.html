publican

noun
keeper of a pub (esp. British)
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪkən / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪkən / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a pub or public house, especially in Britain

Examples

A publican runs a pub
After decades as a publican, she knew all the regulars by name.
The publican greeted every customer with a warm smile as they entered the pub.
The village publican took pride in serving the best ale in the county.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar
  • synonym: innkeeper - a person who manages or owns an inn
