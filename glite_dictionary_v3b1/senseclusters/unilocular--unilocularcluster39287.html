unilocular

adjective
single-chambered
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈlɑːkjələr / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈlɒkjʊlə / (BrE)
having a single compartment or chamber, especially in a biological organ or structure

Examples

A unilocular cyst
The cyst was described as unilocular on the ultrasound scan.
A unilocular ovary contains only one chamber for developing eggs.
Botanists identified the fruit as unilocular, with a single cavity for seeds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: multilocular - having multiple compartments or chambers
