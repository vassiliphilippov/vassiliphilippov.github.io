I Esdra

noun
apocryphal biblical book (1 Esdras)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrst ˈɛzdrəz / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈɛzdrəz / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book, also known as 1 Esdras, consisting mainly of a compilation from 1 Chronicles, 2 Chronicles, Ezra, and Nehemiah.

Examples

I Esdra book
Scholars often refer to I Esdra when studying apocryphal Old Testament writings.
The church includes I Esdra in its list of sacred historical texts.
Translations of I Esdra differ widely depending on the tradition.
