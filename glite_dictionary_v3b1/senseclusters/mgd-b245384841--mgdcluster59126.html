MGD

noun
eye gland disorder
5.3
Pronunciation: /em dʒiː diː/ (AmE), /em dʒiː diː/ (BrE)
a common eye condition (meibomian gland dysfunction) in which the oil glands in the eyelids are blocked or do not work properly, causing dry or irritated eyes

Examples

MGD causes dry eyes
The doctor explained that MGD was the reason for her persistent dry eyes.
Regular warm compresses can help manage symptoms of MGD.
Many people with MGD experience discomfort when using digital devices for long periods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blepharitis - inflammation of the eyelids causing irritation
  • synonym: dry eye syndrome - a condition where the eyes do not produce enough tears or the right quality of tears
