backhandedly

adverb
indirectly or insincerely
Pronunciation: / ˌbækˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌbækˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
in an indirect, insincere, or ambiguous manner, often implying the opposite of what is said

Examples

Apologize backhandedly
He apologized backhandedly, making it sound more like a criticism than a real apology.
Her compliment was given backhandedly, so I was not sure if she meant it.
They thanked us backhandedly, leaving everyone confused about their true feelings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sarcastically - in a way that uses irony to mock or convey contempt
  • antonym: sincerely - in a genuine and honest manner
