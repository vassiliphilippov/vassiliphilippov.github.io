neighborhood

noun
approximate amount or value
7.1 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪ.bər.hʊd/ (AmE), /ˈneɪ.bə.hʊd/ (BrE)
an approximate amount or value; a figure close to a stated number or amount, especially used in the phrase 'in the neighborhood of'.

Examples

In the neighborhood of
The car is priced in the neighborhood of $30,000, give or take a few thousand.
The total number of employees is somewhere in the neighborhood of 500.
Their ages are all in the neighborhood of forty.
