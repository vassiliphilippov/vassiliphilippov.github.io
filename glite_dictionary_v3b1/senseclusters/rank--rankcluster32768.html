rank

adjective
very fertile; producing profuse growth
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: / ræŋk / (AmE), / ræŋk / (BrE)
(of land or vegetation) very fertile or growing profusely, often in a wild or uncontrolled way

Examples

Rank grass
After months of rain, the field had become so rank that it was difficult to walk through the thick growth.
Dense, rank grass grew unchecked in the abandoned lot.
The rank soil along the riverbank allowed wildflowers to flourish each spring.
Dense rank grass surrounded the abandoned house, making it hard to approach.
The rank weeds nearly covered the garden path after weeks of rain.
The jungle was filled with rank vegetation that thrived in the humid climate.
