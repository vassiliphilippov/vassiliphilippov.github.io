unsweetly

adverb
not pleasantly
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswiːtli / (AmE), / ʌnˈswiːtli / (BrE)
in a way that is not sweet, pleasant, or gentle

Examples

To speak unsweetly
He replied unsweetly, making it clear he was upset by the question.
The teacher spoke unsweetly to the class after repeated disruptions.
Her comments were delivered unsweetly, leaving everyone feeling uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant manner
  • antonym: sweetly - in a pleasant or gentle manner
