Geminid

adjective
of the Geminids
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmɪnɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmɪnɪd / (BrE)
relating to the Geminids meteor shower or to the constellation Gemini

Examples

Geminid meteor shower
Many astronomers gather each December to observe the spectacular Geminid meteor shower.
The Geminid display is known for its bright and frequent meteors.
She wrote an article about the origins of the Geminid meteors for the science magazine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geminids - an annual meteor shower appearing to come from the constellation Gemini
