benedict

noun
newly married man, formerly a bachelor
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛn.ə.dɪkt / (AmE), / ˈbɛn.ɪ.dɪkt / (BrE)
a man who has recently married, especially one who was a bachelor for a long time

Examples

A benedict groom
After years of insisting he would never settle down, Tom became a benedict at the age of forty.
No one expected the famous bachelor to become a benedict, but love surprised everyone.
The club celebrated the arrival of its latest benedict with good-natured teasing and gifts.
