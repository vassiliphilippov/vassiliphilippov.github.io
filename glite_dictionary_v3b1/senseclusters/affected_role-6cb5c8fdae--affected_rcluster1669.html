affected role

noun
semantic role affected by an action
9.1
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛktɪd roʊl / (AmE), / əˈfɛktɪd rəʊl / (BrE)
In linguistics, the semantic role of an entity that is directly involved in or impacted by an action, but is not the agent of that action.

Examples

The affected role in linguistics
A linguist may distinguish between the agent and the affected role to clarify meaning.
In the sentence 'The ball was kicked by John,' the ball has the affected role.
Understanding the affected role helps in analyzing sentence structure in linguistics.
