genus Erythroxylon

noun
genus of South American shrubs/small trees
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɪˌrɪθ.rɒkˈsaɪ.lɒn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɪˌrɪθ.rɒkˈsaɪ.lɒn / (BrE)
a large genus of South American shrubs and small trees in the family Erythroxylaceae, including species such as the coca plant
