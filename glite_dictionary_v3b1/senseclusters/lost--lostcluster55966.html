lost

adjective
confused; not knowing place or identity
4.4
Pronunciation: / lɔst / (AmE), / lɒst / (BrE)
confused or unable to understand what is happening or what to do

Examples

Feel lost in a city
After wandering the city for hours, she felt completely lost and unsure of where to go next.
During the blackout, many people were briefly lost, unsure of what was happening or where to find help.
He became lost in the large airport and couldn't remember which terminal he was in.
He looked lost when the instructions became too complicated to follow.
Many students feel lost at the beginning of university because everything is unfamiliar.
She felt completely lost during her first week at the new job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewildered - completely puzzled or confused
  • antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret
  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly; bewildered
  • synonym: disoriented - confused as to time or place; having lost one's sense of direction
