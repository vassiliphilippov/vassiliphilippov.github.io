Rynchops

noun
genus of skimmer birds
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋkɑps /; / ˈrɪn.kɑps / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋkɒps /; / ˈrɪŋ.kɒps / (BrE)
a genus of birds called skimmers, known for their unique beaks with a longer lower mandible used to skim water surfaces for food
