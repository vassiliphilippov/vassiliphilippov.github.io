cheapie

adjective
inexpensive; low-cost
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːpi / (BrE)
informal; costing very little money or being low in price

Examples

A cheapie umbrella
They bought a cheapie umbrella that broke after one use.
For the picnic, we just grabbed some cheapie snacks from the discount store.
He wore a cheapie watch that looked more expensive than it was.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheap - costing little money; low in price
  • antonym: expensive - costing a lot of money; high in price
