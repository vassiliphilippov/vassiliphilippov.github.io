left-eyed

adjective
having only the left eye
6.4
Pronunciation: / lɛft aɪd / (AmE), / lɛft aɪd / (BrE)
Having only the left eye or seeing primarily with the left eye.

Examples

Left-eyed flatfish
Some flatfish are naturally left-eyed, with both eyes on the left side of their bodies.
The artist depicted a left-eyed mythical creature in the mural.
The pirate was left-eyed after losing his right eye in battle.
