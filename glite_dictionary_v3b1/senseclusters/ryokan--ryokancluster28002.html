ryokan

noun
Japanese traditional inn
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːoʊˌkɑːn / (AmE), / ˈriːəʊˌkæn / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese inn with tatami-matted rooms, communal baths, and local cuisine

Examples

A ryokan inn
During our trip to Japan, we stayed at a beautiful ryokan overlooking the mountains.
Guests at a ryokan often wear yukata robes and enjoy traditional Japanese meals.
The peaceful atmosphere of the ryokan made our vacation truly relaxing.
