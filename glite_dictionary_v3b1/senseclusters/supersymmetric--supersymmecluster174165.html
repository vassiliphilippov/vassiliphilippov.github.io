supersymmetric

adjective
relating to supersymmetry
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərsɪˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəsɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or exhibiting supersymmetry, a theoretical concept in physics where each particle has a corresponding partner

Examples

Supersymmetric particle theory
The supersymmetric theory suggests that every particle has a heavier partner particle.
Physicists are searching for evidence of supersymmetric particles at the Large Hadron Collider.
A supersymmetric model could help explain some mysteries of dark matter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supersymmetry - a theory in physics proposing a symmetry between fundamental particles
