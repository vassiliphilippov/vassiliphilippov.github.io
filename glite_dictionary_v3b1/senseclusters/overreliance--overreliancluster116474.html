overreliance

noun
excessive dependence
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪˈlaɪəns / (BrE)
the state of depending too much on someone or something

Examples

An overreliance on imports
There is an overreliance on imported goods in many countries.
His overreliance on his friends made it hard for him to solve problems alone.
The report warns against overreliance on a single source of income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependence - the state of relying on someone or something
  • antonym: independence - the state of not relying on others
