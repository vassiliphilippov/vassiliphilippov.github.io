eatery

noun
restaurant or café
Pronunciation: / ˈiːtəri / (AmE), / ˈiːtəri / (BrE)
A place such as a restaurant or café where people go to eat meals.

Examples

A small eatery
After a long drive, we stopped at a roadside eatery for lunch.
The city is famous for its diverse range of eateries offering international cuisine.
We decided to try the new eatery that opened downtown last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: café - a small restaurant where drinks and light meals are served
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals that are cooked and served on the premises
