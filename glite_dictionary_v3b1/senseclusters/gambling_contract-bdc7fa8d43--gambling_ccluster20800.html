gambling contract

noun
contract dependent on gambling outcome
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæm.blɪŋ ˈkɑn.trækt / (AmE), / ˈɡæm.blɪŋ ˈkɒn.trækt / (BrE)
A contract in which the obligation for one party to perform is dependent on the outcome of a bet; usually legally unenforceable in most places.

Examples

A gambling contract
His lawyer explained that the gambling contract was void under the jurisdiction’s statutes.
In most countries, a gambling contract cannot be enforced in a court of law.
The two parties entered into a gambling contract regarding the outcome of the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betting agreement - an agreement based on the result of a bet
  • synonym: contract - a legally binding agreement between parties
  • synonym: wager - a bet or something staked on an uncertain outcome
