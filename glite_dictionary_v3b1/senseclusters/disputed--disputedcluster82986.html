subject to disagreement, argument, or debate; not accepted as true or valid by everyone

Examples

A disputed claim

Synonyms

    6.6 0.25/m

  • debatable
    5.0 2.49/m

  • contestable
    6.8 0.14/m

  • arguable
    6.0 1.02/m

  • controvertible
    8.3 0.01/m
    controvertible is a formal word for being open to dispute or doubt, while disputed describes something people already question.
  • challengeable

  • controversial
    5.0 27/m
