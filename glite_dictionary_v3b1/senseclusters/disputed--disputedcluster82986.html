disputed

adjective
subject to disagreement or debate
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspjutɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈspjuːtɪd / (BrE)
subject to disagreement, argument, or debate; not accepted as true or valid by everyone

Examples

A disputed claim
Her disputed claim to the throne led to years of conflict.
Historians have offered several disputed interpretations of the ancient text.
The ownership of the land remains disputed after decades of legal battles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controversial - causing public disagreement or discussion
  • antonym: undisputed - not questioned or doubted; accepted by everyone
