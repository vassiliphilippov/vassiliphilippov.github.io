wire wool

noun
fine steel fibers used as an abrasive
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ wʊl / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə wʊl / (BrE)
A mass of fine steel fibers used as an abrasive for cleaning, polishing, or smoothing surfaces.

Examples

Wire wool pad
Wire wool is effective for polishing metal surfaces until they shine.
Always wear gloves when handling wire wool to avoid injuring your hands.
She used wire wool to scrub the rust off the old bicycle frame.
