moderationist

noun
person with centrist political views
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who holds moderate or centrist political views, avoiding extremes

Examples

Moderationist politician
As a moderationist, she supported policies that balanced progressive and conservative values.
His moderationist stance made him a popular compromise candidate among undecided voters.
The party attracted many moderationists who were dissatisfied with the extremes on both sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrist - a person whose political opinions are not extreme, falling in the center
