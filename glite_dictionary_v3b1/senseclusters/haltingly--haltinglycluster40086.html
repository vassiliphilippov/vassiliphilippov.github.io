haltingly

adverb
in a hesitant or uncertain manner
8.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɔːl.tɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈhɑːl.tɪŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhɔːl.tɪŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a manner that is hesitant or marked by pauses, especially due to lack of confidence or certainty.

Examples

To speak haltingly
Despite practicing many times, the actor delivered his lines haltingly on opening night.
He read the letter haltingly, pausing often to gather his thoughts.
She answered haltingly, unsure of how to express her feelings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fluently - in a smooth and confident way, without hesitation
  • synonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant way
