nonentity

noun
an unimportant or insignificant person
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑˈnɛntəti /; / nɑːˈnɛn.t̬ə.ti / (AmE), / nɒˈnɛntəti /; / nɒˈnɛn.tɪ.ti / (BrE)
a person or thing that is unimportant, has no influence, or is regarded as not existing
