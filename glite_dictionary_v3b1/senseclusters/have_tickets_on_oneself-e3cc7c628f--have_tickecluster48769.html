have tickets on oneself

verb
to be conceited or self-important
Pronunciation: / hæv ˈtɪkɪts ɑn ˌwʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / hæv ˈtɪkɪts ɒn ˌwʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to consider oneself to be more important, talented, or attractive than is actually the case; to be conceited or arrogant
