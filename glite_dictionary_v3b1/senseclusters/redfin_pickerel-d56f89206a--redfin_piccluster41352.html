redfin pickerel

noun
small fish with red fins, US coastal species
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd.fɪn ˈpɪk.ər.əl / (AmE), / ˈrɛd.fɪn ˈpɪk.ər.əl / (BrE)
A small but lively species of pickerel found in the Atlantic coastal states of North America, known for its reddish fins.

Examples

The redfin pickerel fish
Anglers often target the redfin pickerel for its energetic fight despite its small size.
The redfin pickerel is native to slow-moving streams along the Atlantic coast.
We spotted a redfin pickerel darting beneath the lily pads on our fishing trip.
