underchin

noun
area below chin
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌtʃɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌtʃɪn / (BrE)
the area beneath the chin, or a strap or covering that goes under the chin

Examples

Soft cloth on underchin
The violinist placed a soft cloth on the underchin area for comfort while playing.
Make sure the underchin strap is fastened tightly before riding your bike.
Sweat collected on his underchin after running in the heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chinstrap - a strap that goes under the chin to secure a helmet or hat
