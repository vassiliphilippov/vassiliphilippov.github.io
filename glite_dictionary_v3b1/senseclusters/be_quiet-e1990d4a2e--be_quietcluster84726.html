be quiet

verb
stop talking; become silent
1.0
Pronunciation: / bi ˈkwaɪət / (AmE), / bi ˈkwaɪət / (BrE)
to stop talking or to remain silent; to fall silent

Examples

To be quiet now
I asked the children to be quiet so I could take the call.
Please be quiet while the exam is in progress.
When they saw the teacher enter, everyone tried to be quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keep quiet - to remain silent; not to talk
  • synonym: shut up - to stop talking, often abruptly or rudely
  • antonym: speak - to talk; to utter words
