joker

noun
person fond of telling jokes
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊkə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys telling or playing jokes; someone who frequently makes others laugh with jokes or playful behavior

Examples

Class joker
Even as a child, he was a natural joker, always pulling pranks on his friends.
Everyone at the office knows Tom as the joker who always has a funny story to share.
She's such a joker—her quick wit keeps everyone laughing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comedian - a professional entertainer who tells jokes or acts comically
  • synonym: jester - someone whose job is to entertain by telling jokes and acting playfully
  • antonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the fun of others
