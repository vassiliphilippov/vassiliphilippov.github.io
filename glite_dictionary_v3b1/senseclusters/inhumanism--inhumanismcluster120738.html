inhumanism

noun
extreme cruelty
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhjuːmənɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪnˈhjuːmənɪzəm / (BrE)
extreme cruelty or treating people as less than human; a lack of compassion or kindness

  • synonym: dehumanization - the process of depriving people of human qualities
  • antonym: humanism - a belief in the value and dignity of all people
