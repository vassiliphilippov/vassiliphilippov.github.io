pyroelectrical

adjective
relating to pyroelectricity
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪroʊɪˈlɛktrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərəʊɪˈlɛktrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or exhibiting pyroelectricity, the property of certain materials to generate an electrical charge in response to changes in temperature.

Examples

A pyroelectrical material
Her research focuses on new materials with enhanced pyroelectrical responses for thermal imaging.
Some crystals display pyroelectrical properties, generating voltage when their temperature changes.
The sensor relies on a pyroelectrical effect to detect infrared radiation from warm objects.
