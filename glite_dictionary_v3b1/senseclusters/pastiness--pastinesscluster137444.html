pastiness

noun
pale complexion
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪstinəs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstinəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a pale, dull, or whitish complexion or appearance

Examples

Pastiness of the skin
The pastiness of his skin suggested he had spent the winter indoors.
After being ill for several days, her pastiness was noticeable to everyone.
The makeup was used to cover the pastiness on his face before the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pallor - an unhealthy pale appearance
  • antonym: rosiness - a healthy pink or reddish color in the face
