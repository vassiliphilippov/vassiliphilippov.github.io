palm oil

noun
edible oil from oil palm fruit
Pronunciation: / pɑm ɔɪl / (AmE), / pɑːm ɔɪl / (BrE)
an edible and industrial oil obtained from the fruit or nuts of oil palm trees, especially the African oil palm.

Examples

Palm oil production
Global demand for palm oil has increased due to its use in processed foods and cosmetics.

The plantation specializes in harvesting and refining palm oil for export.
