possession

noun
control of the ball in sport
2.3
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
(in sport) the state of having physical control of the ball or puck during play.

Examples

Possession of the ball
After a turnover, the players scrambled to regain possession of the ball.
Maintaining possession is crucial if you want to control the pace of the game.
Our team kept possession for most of the second half, limiting the opposition's chances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: control - the power to direct or manage
  • antonym: loss of possession - losing control of the ball or puck
