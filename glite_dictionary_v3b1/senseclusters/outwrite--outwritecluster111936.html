outwrite

verb
to surpass in writing
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈraɪt / (BrE)
to write more or with greater skill than someone else

Examples

To outwrite the others
Despite his lack of experience, he managed to outwrite all the other contestants in the competition.
The journalist hoped to outwrite her rivals by producing more compelling stories.
With creativity and speed, she could easily outwrite her classmates during timed essays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excel - to be exceptionally good at an activity
  • synonym: outperform - to do better than someone else in a particular activity
  • antonym: underperform - to do worse than others in a particular activity
