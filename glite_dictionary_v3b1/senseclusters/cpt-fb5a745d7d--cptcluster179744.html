CPT

noun
trade term for shipping
8.0
Pronunciation: /siː.piː.tiː/ (AmE), /siː.piː.tiː/ (BrE)
an international trade term (Incoterm) meaning the seller pays the cost of transporting goods to a named destination

Examples

CPT Shanghai
The contract specified CPT Shanghai, so the seller arranged and paid for the main transport.
When using CPT, the risk transfers to the buyer once the goods are handed to the carrier.
Our company prefers CPT terms to simplify the shipping process for international orders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CIF - a trade term meaning Cost, Insurance and Freight, where the seller pays for transport, insurance, and freight
  • antonym: FOB - a trade term meaning Free On Board, where the buyer pays for transport after goods are loaded
