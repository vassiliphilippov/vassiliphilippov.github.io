born in a tent

adjective
lacking manners; uncivilized
9.6
Pronunciation: / bɔrn ɪn ə tɛnt / (AmE), / bɔːn ɪn ə tɛnt / (BrE)
used to criticize someone for behaving in a rude or uncivilized way, as if lacking proper upbringing or manners

Examples

He is born in a tent
He slammed the door so loudly, I asked if he was born in a tent.
Leaving muddy footprints through the house, he was teased for behaving as if he had been born in a tent.
Whenever she eats with her mouth open, her mother reminds her not to act like she was born in a tent.
