group participation

noun
involvement of all group members
2.7
Pronunciation: / ɡruːp pɑːɹˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɡruːp pɑːˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the involvement or participation of all members within a group in an activity or decision-making process

Examples

Active group participation
Active group participation is essential for the success of any team project.
Company meetings are more productive when there is equal group participation.
The teacher encouraged group participation to make the lesson more engaging for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the situation of working together to achieve something
  • antonym: individual participation - involvement by a single person rather than a group
