backdating

noun
assigning earlier date
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌdeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌdeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of assigning an earlier date to a document or event than the actual date it occurred

Examples

The backdating of documents
The investigation revealed the backdating of several important financial documents.
He was charged with backdating contracts to benefit from earlier terms.
Strict regulations are in place to prevent the backdating of official records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dating - the act of marking with a date
  • synonym: falsification - the act of making something false or misleading
