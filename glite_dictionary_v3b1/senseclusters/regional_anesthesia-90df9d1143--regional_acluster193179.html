Synonyms

  • nerve block anesthesia
    4.3 0.00/m
    nerve block anesthesia numbs a specific area by injecting near a nerve, while regional anesthesia means numbing a whole region by blocking nerves.
  • block anesthesia
    5.2 0.00/m
    block anesthesia applies an anesthetic to a nerve to numb its area, while regional anesthesia covers nerve-based numbing of a whole body region.
  • conduction anesthesia
    5.6 0.00/m
    conduction anesthesia blocks sensation by acting on the supplying nerve, while regional anesthesia describes nerve blockade that numbs a body region.
