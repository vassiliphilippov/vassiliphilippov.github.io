nerita

noun
marine gastropod of the genus Nerita
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈne.rɪ.tə/ (AmE), /ˈne.rɪ.tə/ (BrE)
A marine gastropod mollusc of the genus Nerita, having a short smooth or spirally ridged shell with a thick, often toothed outer lip and a toothed operculum.

Examples

A nerita shell
A characteristic feature of nerita is the thick, toothed outer lip of its shell.
Biologists collected several specimens of nerita for their research on gastropods.
The rocky shore was dotted with nerita shells of various sizes.
