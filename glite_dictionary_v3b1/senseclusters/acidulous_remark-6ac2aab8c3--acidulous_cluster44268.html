acidulous remark

noun
bitter or sharp comment
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪdʒələs rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjʊləs rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment that is sharp, bitter, or slightly sarcastic in tone

Examples

An acidulous remark
Even his compliments were tinged with an acidulous remark that left people uneasy.
She couldn't help making an acidulous remark about his choice of tie.
The meeting was disrupted by an acidulous remark from one of the participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland remark - a comment that is mild or lacking in sharpness
  • synonym: sarcastic remark - a comment meant to mock or convey contempt
