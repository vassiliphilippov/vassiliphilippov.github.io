epigastric artery

noun
artery supplying anterior abdominal wall
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk ˈɑːrtəri / (AmE), / ˌiːpɪˈɡæstrɪk ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
any of three arteries that supply blood to the anterior walls of the abdomen.

Examples

The epigastric artery supplies blood
A rupture of the epigastric artery can cause internal bleeding in the abdominal wall.
The inferior epigastric artery is often used as a landmark in hernia repair surgeries.
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the epigastric artery during the abdominal procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artery - a blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart
