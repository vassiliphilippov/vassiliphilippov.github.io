heart massage

noun
compression technique for cardiac arrest
7.2 2.7
Pronunciation: /hɑːrt mə.ˈsɑːʒ/ /; / /hɑːrt ˈmæs.ɑːʒ/ (AmE), /hɑːt ˈmæs.ɑːʒ/ /; / /hɑːt mə.ˈsɑːʒ/ (BrE)
an emergency medical procedure involving rhythmic compression of the heart to maintain circulation during cardiac arrest

Examples

Heart massage procedure
After several minutes of heart massage, the patient's pulse returned.
During open-heart surgery, surgeons may perform a direct heart massage if the heart stops beating.
The doctor immediately began heart massage to try to revive the patient.
