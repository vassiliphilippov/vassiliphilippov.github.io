gigawatt

adjective
measured in gigawatts
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡəˌwɑt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡəˌwɒt / (BrE)
producing, rated at, or measured in gigawatts; of a magnitude equal to one billion watts

Examples

Gigawatt power plant
The country plans to build a gigawatt wind farm to increase renewable energy production.
A gigawatt power station can supply electricity to millions of homes.
Scientists discussed the impact of a gigawatt laser in their latest research paper.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: megawatt - producing or measured in millions of watts
  • degree higher: terawatt - producing or measured in trillions of watts
