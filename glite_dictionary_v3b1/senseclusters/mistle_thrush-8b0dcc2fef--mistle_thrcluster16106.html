mistle thrush

noun
large European thrush species
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsəl θrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪsəl θrʌʃ / (BrE)
a large European songbird (Turdus viscivorus) with a speckled breast, noted for feeding on mistletoe berries

Examples

A mistle thrush bird
A pair of mistle thrushes built a nest in the tall oak tree.
The loud, fluting song of the mistle thrush can be heard early in spring.
We saw a mistle thrush feeding on berries in the old orchard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackbird - a common European thrush with all-black plumage (male)
  • synonym: song thrush - a similar European thrush species with a melodious song
