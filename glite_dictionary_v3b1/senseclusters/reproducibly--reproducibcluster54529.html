reproducibly

adverb
in a way that can be reproduced
9.9 5.1
Pronunciation: /riː.prə.ˈduː.sə.bli/ /; / /riː.prə.ˈdjuː.sə.bli/ (AmE), /riː.prə.ˈdjuː.sə.bli/ /; / /riː.prə.ˈduː.sə.bli/ (BrE)
In a manner that allows for consistent reproduction or results when repeated under the same conditions.

Examples

To work reproducibly
It is important that scientific results can be demonstrated reproducibly by independent researchers.
The experiment was conducted reproducibly across several laboratories to ensure its reliability.
The software analyzes data reproducibly, minimizing human error and variation.
