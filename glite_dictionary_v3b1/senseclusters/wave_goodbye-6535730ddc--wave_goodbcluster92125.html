wave goodbye

verb
to bid farewell with a waving gesture
Pronunciation: / weɪv ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / weɪv ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
to say farewell by moving your hand back and forth, usually as a gesture to someone leaving

Examples

To wave goodbye to friends
He felt a little sad as he waved goodbye to his friends at the end of the summer camp.
She smiled and waved goodbye before boarding the airplane.
They stood at the gate to wave goodbye to their grandparents as the taxi drove away.
