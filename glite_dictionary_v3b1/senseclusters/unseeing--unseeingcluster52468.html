unseeing

adjective
lacking sight or visual perception
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːɪŋ / (BrE)
not able to see; lacking the ability to perceive visually.

Examples

Unseeing after accident
After the accident, his gaze became unseeing, fixed on a distant point.
She turned her unseeing eyes toward the window, lost in thought.
The unseeing man walked carefully through the crowded market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blind - unable to see; without sight
