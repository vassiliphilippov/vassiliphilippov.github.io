snaggle

noun
small fabric snag
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡəl / (BrE)
a small snag or protruding loop of thread in fabric or other material

Examples

A snaggle in fabric
She noticed a snaggle on her sweater and tried to smooth it out.
The tailor fixed the snaggle in the dress before returning it to the customer.
Be careful not to create a snaggle when handling delicate fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snag - a small tear or catch in fabric
  • synonym: tear - a hole or split in something caused by pulling
