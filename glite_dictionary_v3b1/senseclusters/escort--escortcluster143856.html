escort

noun
person who accompanies another as a date
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛˌskɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɛːskɔːt / (BrE)
a person who accompanies another to a social event, especially as a date or companion

Examples

A dinner escort
Each guest was allowed to bring one escort to the formal dinner.
He asked his friend to be his escort at the company party.
She arrived at the gala with her escort, who was dressed in a sharp suit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: date - a person one goes out with socially or romantically
