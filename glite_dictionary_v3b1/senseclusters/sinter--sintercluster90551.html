sinter

verb
to fuse powdered metal by heating without melting
9.8 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.tə/ (BrE)
to cause (powdered ore or metal) to form a solid mass by heating without melting it completely

Examples

To sinter metal powder
After being pressed into shape, the parts are sintered rather than fully melted.
Modern manufacturing often sinters ceramic materials to achieve strength and durability.
The powdered metal is placed in a mold and then sintered at high temperatures.
