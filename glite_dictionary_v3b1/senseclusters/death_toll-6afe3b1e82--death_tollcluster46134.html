death toll

noun
number of deaths from an event
6.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /deθ toʊl/ (AmE), /deθ təʊl/ (BrE)
the number of people who have died as a result of a particular event, such as an accident, war, or disaster

Examples

As the storm swept through the region, the death toll continued to climb.
Officials confirmed that the death toll in the accident was higher than first reported.
The death toll from the earthquake rose to over three thousand.
