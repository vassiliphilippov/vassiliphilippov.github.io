bacchanalian

adjective
drunken festivity or revelry
Pronunciation: / ˌbækəˈneɪliən / (AmE), / ˌbækəˈneɪliən / (BrE)
characterized by or involving noisy, drunken revelry or wild celebration

Examples

A bacchanalian party
Laughter and music spilled from the bacchanalian festivities in the main hall.
The novel describes a bacchanalian feast that spirals into chaos as the night progresses.
The students threw a bacchanalian party to celebrate the end of exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orgiastic - characterized by unrestrained emotion or wild revelry
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; serious or restrained
