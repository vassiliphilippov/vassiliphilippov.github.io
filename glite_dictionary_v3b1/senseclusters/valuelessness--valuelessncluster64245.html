valuelessness

noun
lack of value or worth
Pronunciation: / ˈvæljuˌlɪsnəs / (AmE), / ˈvæljuːlɪsnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having no value or worth.

Examples

The valuelessness of things
He lamented the valuelessness of material possessions compared to true happiness.
The valuelessness of the currency caused widespread economic hardship.
The old painting was declared a forgery, adding to its valuelessness.
