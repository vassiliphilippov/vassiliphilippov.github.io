dookie hole

noun
humorous slang for dirty place or toilet
Pronunciation: / ˈduːki hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈduːki həʊl / (BrE)
A slang and humorous term for a filthy or unpleasant place, especially a bathroom or toilet.

Examples

A dookie hole place
After the storm, their basement turned into a real dookie hole.
He laughed when his friend called the messy garage a dookie hole.
The public restroom at the park was such a dookie hole that I couldn't bring myself to use it.
