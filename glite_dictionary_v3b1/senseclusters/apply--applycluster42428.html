apply

verb
to be relevant or appropriate
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪ / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ / (BrE)
to be relevant or suitable to a particular person, group, or situation

Examples

The rule doesn’t apply in this particular case.
These safety guidelines apply to everyone who works in the laboratory.
Her comments apply equally to both teams.
The new law does not apply in this particular circumstance.
These rules apply to all students, regardless of age.
