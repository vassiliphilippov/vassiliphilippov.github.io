hurry

noun
state of rushed activity or haste
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜːri /; / ˈhɜri / (AmE), / ˈhʌri / (BrE)
a state of rushing or urgency, often leading to carelessness

  • antonym: deliberation - careful thought, especially in making a decision
  • synonym: haste - excessive speed or urgency
  • synonym: rush - A sudden movement or surge, often due to urgency
