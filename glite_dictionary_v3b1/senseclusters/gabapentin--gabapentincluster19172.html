gabapentin

noun
drug for seizures and nerve pain
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæbəˈpɛntɪn / (AmE), / ˌɡabəˈpɛntɪn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat certain types of seizures and nerve pain, particularly in epilepsy and neuralgia

Examples

Gabapentin medication
He experienced drowsiness as a side effect of gabapentin.
Many patients with epilepsy use gabapentin as part of their treatment regimen.
The doctor prescribed gabapentin to help control her nerve pain after shingles.
