contract of hazard

noun
sale of land with no acreage warranty
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.trækt əv ˈhæz.ərd / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trækt əv ˈhæz.əd / (BrE)
a sale of a tract of land as a whole, without any warranty as to the actual acreage included

Examples

A contract of hazard sale
