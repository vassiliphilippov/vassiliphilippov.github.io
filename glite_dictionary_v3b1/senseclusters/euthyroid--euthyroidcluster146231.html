euthyroid

noun
person with normal thyroid
8.6
Pronunciation: /juː.ˈθaɪ.rɔɪd/ (AmE), /juː.ˈθaɪ.rɔɪd/ (BrE)
a person who has normal thyroid function or normal levels of thyroid hormones

Examples

A euthyroid patient

As a euthyroid, she did not require any thyroid medication.
Most people in the study were classified as euthyroid based on their hormone levels.
