abjectness

noun
extreme misery
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæbdʒɛktnəs / (AmE), / ˈæbdʒɛktnəs / (BrE)
the state of being extremely miserable, degraded, or wretched

Examples

The abjectness of poverty
The abjectness of the refugees' situation moved many people to donate supplies.
He could not bear the abjectness he felt after losing his job and home.
The novel describes the abjectness of life in the slums with vivid detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misery - a state of great suffering or unhappiness
