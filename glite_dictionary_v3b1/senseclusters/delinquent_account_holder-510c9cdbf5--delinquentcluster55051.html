delinquent account holder

noun
person with overdue debts
6.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈlɪŋ.kwənt ə.ˈkaʊnt ˈhoʊl.dər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈlɪŋ.kwənt ə.ˈkaʊnt ˈhəʊl.də/ (BrE)
a person or customer who has failed to pay debts on time, especially to a bank or financial institution

Examples

Delinquent account holder misses payments
After missing several payments, Jane was labelled a delinquent account holder by her credit card company.
Banks often contact delinquent account holders to arrange payment plans and avoid further penalties.
Being a delinquent account holder can have a negative impact on your credit score.
