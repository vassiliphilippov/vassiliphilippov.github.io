unsoaked

adjective
not made wet
3.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsoʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊkt / (BrE)
not having been soaked or made wet by liquid

Examples

The unsoaked beans
The unsoaked beans will take longer to cook.
He used an unsoaked sponge, so it did not clean the table well.
Please keep the documents unsoaked during the rainstorm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - not wet or damp
  • antonym: soaked - completely wet; saturated with liquid
