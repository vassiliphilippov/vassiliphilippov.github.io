lob

noun
high-arching throw or shot
5.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /lɑːb/ (AmE), /lɒb/ (BrE)
an act of hitting, throwing, or propelling something (especially a ball) in a high arc through the air, or the ball itself when hit or thrown this way (especially in sports)

Examples

A high lob
She hit a perfect lob over her opponent's head to win the point.
The artillery fired a lob of shells towards the enemy position.
The goalkeeper was caught off guard by a clever lob from midfield.
His lob landed just inside the baseline, catching the other player off guard.
She won the point with a clever lob over her opponent's head.
The tennis coach demonstrated how to perfect a defensive lob during practice.

  • synonym: throw - to send something through the air with force by a movement of the arm and hand
