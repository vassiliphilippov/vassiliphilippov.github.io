mockumentary

adjective
parody documentary style
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːkjuˈmɛn.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌmɒk.juˈmɛn.təri / (BrE)
presented in the style of a documentary but intended to parody or satirize, often using fictional events or characters

Examples

Mockumentary style film
The film's mockumentary approach made the fictional story feel surprisingly real.
Critics praised the show's mockumentary style for its clever use of humor and realism.
The director used a mockumentary format to satirize modern celebrity culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: documentary - a film or program presenting factual information
  • synonym: satirical - using humor or exaggeration to criticize or mock
