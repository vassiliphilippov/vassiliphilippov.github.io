papillote

noun
decorative candy wrapper
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpæpiˈjoʊt / (AmE), / ˌpapɪˈjɒt / (BrE)
a small decorative paper or foil wrapper used to cover a sweet or chocolate, especially for presentation

Examples

Chocolate papillote
Each chocolate in the box was wrapped in a shiny papillote to make it look special.
The bakery decorated the truffles with a colorful papillote for the holiday season.
She carefully removed the papillote before tasting the sweet inside.
