fruit sugar

noun
a sugar found in fruits and honey
Pronunciation: / fruːt ˈsʌɡər / (AmE), / fruːt ˈsʌɡə / (BrE)
a simple sugar (fructose) found naturally in many ripe fruits and honey

Examples

Fruit sugar in honey
Honey is rich in fruit sugar, giving it a naturally sweet taste.
Many energy bars use fruit sugar as a natural sweetener.
People with certain conditions may need to monitor their intake of fruit sugar.
