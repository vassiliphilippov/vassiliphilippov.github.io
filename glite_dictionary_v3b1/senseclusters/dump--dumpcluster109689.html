dump

noun
site for rubbish or waste
6.4
Pronunciation: / dʌmp / (AmE), / dʌmp / (BrE)
a place where waste materials, rubbish, or unwanted items are left, often in a large pile or area

Examples

City dump
The city closed the old dump because it became unsafe for the environment.
They took their broken furniture to the local dump on Saturday morning.
Years of neglect turned the area into an illegal dump full of trash and debris.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landfill - a place where waste is buried under the ground
  • antonym: recycling center - a site for sorting and processing recyclable materials
