wild thyme

noun
aromatic shrub, Thymus serpyllum
6.0 4.9
Pronunciation: /waɪld taɪm/ (AmE), /waɪld taɪm/ (BrE)
a small aromatic shrub (Thymus serpyllum) native to Europe and Asia, with small purple flowers, often found on banks and hillsides; also naturalized in North America
