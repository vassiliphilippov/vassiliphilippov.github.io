hydroxyapatite

noun
biomaterial for bone repair
8.6
Pronunciation: /haɪ.drɑːk.siː.ə.ˈpæt.aɪt/ /; / /haɪ.drɑːk.siː.ə.ˈæp.ə.taɪt/ (AmE), /haɪ.drɒk.siː.ə.ˈpæt.aɪt/ /; / /haɪ.drɒk.siː.ə.ˈæp.ə.taɪt/ (BrE)
