(in blockchain and cryptocurrency) a division of a blockchain (a hard fork) that creates a separate, incompatible ledger and may produce a new cryptocurrency.

Examples

Blockchain split
After the hard fork, the network experienced a split that created a separate cryptocurrency and chain.
Developers debated whether the code change would cause a split that risks replay attacks.
Investors tracked the exchange listings to see if the split would produce two tradable coins.
