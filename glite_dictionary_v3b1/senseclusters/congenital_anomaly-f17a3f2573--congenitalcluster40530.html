congenital anomaly

noun
defect present at birth

Examples

Synonyms

  • congenital abnormality
    5.4 0.07/m
    congenital abnormality is the clinical term doctors use for physical or functional problems at birth, while congenital anomaly is more formal.
  • congenital defect
    4.2 0.11/m
    congenital defect usually refers to a physical fault present at birth, while congenital anomaly focuses on structural or developmental differences.
  • congenital disorder
    4.9 0.06/m
    congenital disorder names a medical condition found at birth and can include ongoing illness, while congenital anomaly stresses structural defects.
  • birth defect
    3.2 1.82/m
    birth defect is the everyday phrase for a problem a baby has at birth, while congenital anomaly is the formal medical term.
