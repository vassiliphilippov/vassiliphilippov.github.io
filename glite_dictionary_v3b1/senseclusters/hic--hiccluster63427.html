hic

interjection
hiccup sound
Pronunciation: / hɪk / (AmE), / hɪk / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating the sound made when someone hiccups

Examples

Sudden hic
He suddenly stopped talking and let out a loud hic in the middle of dinner.
Every time she tried to speak, another hic interrupted her sentence.
The baby made a tiny hic after drinking her milk too quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiccup - the involuntary sound or action of a hiccup
