Vedanga

noun
ancient Indian texts on Vedic studies
Pronunciation: / veɪˈdɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / veɪˈdæŋɡə / (BrE)
any of a group of ancient Indian texts connected with the Vedas, dealing with subjects such as phonetics, grammar, etymology, meter, ritual, and astronomy.

Examples

Vedanga texts
Instruction in the Vedanga was essential for Vedic priests.
Scholars have studied the Vedanga to better understand ancient rituals and linguistic traditions.
The Vedanga include treatises on phonetics, grammar, and astronomy.
