quarterback

noun
player who leads offense in American football
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtərˌbæk /; / ˈkwɔːrdɚˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəbæk / (BrE)
the player in American or Canadian football who directs the team's offensive plays and is usually positioned behind the center

Examples

Star quarterback
The team's quarterback threw three touchdown passes in the first half.
Tom Brady is considered one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time.
Without a strong quarterback, the offense struggled to score points.
Every quarterback in the league dreams of leading his team to a championship.
The new quarterback showed great leadership on the field.
The team's quarterback threw three touchdown passes during the game.
