quarterback

noun
player who leads offense in American football
6.6 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtərˌbæk /; / ˈkwɔːrdɚˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəbæk / (BrE)
the player in American or Canadian football who directs the team's offensive plays and is usually positioned behind the center
