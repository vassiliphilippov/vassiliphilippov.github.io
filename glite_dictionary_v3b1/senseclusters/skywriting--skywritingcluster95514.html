skywriting

noun
writing in the sky made by smoke from an airplane
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
writing or drawings formed in the sky by smoke released from an aircraft, typically for advertising or entertainment purposes.

Examples

Skywriting in the sky
