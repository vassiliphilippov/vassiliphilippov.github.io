rattan palm

noun
tropical climbing palm with long, flexible stems
7.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈræt.ən pɑːm/ /; / /ˈræt.ən pɑːlm/ /; / /ˈræt.ən pæm/ (AmE), /ˈræt.ən pɑːm/ /; / /ˈræt.ən pɑːlm/ /; / /rə.ˈtæn pɑːm/ (BrE)
A climbing palm native to Sri Lanka and southern India, notable for its long stems used in making items such as Malacca canes and furniture.
