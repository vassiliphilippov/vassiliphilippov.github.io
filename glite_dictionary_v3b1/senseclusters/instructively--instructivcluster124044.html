instructively

adverb
in an informative or instructional manner
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstrʌktɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that provides information or instruction; in an informative manner.

Examples

Nod instructively
He nodded instructively toward the sign, guiding the visitors down the right path.
The manual is written instructively and is easy for beginners to follow.
The teacher spoke instructively, making sure everyone understood the lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informatively - in a manner that conveys information
  • antonym: unhelpfully - in a manner that does not provide instruction or help
