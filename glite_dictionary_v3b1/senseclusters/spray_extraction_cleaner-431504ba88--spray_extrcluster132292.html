spray extraction cleaner

noun
machine for deep cleaning textiles by spraying and extracting
3.5
Pronunciation: / spreɪ ɪkˈstrækʃən ˈkliːnər / (AmE), / spreɪ ɪkˈstrækʃən ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
a machine that cleans textile surfaces such as carpets, hard floors, upholstery, and mattresses by spraying a cleaning solution and extracting it along with dirt.

Examples

A spray extraction cleaner
A spray extraction cleaner is effective for cleaning both upholstery and mattresses.
Before returning the rental, he used a spray extraction cleaner on the car seats.
The company uses a spray extraction cleaner to remove deep-seated stains from carpets.
