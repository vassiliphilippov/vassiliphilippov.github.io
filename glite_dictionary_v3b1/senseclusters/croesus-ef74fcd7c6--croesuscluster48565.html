Croesus

noun
a very wealthy man
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːsəs / (AmE), / ˈkriːsəs / (BrE)
a very rich man; a person of great wealth

Examples

A modern Croesus
She dreamed of marrying a Croesus and living in luxury for the rest of her life.
Since he sold his company, people say he's a Croesus who can afford anything.
You don't have to be a Croesus to enjoy the finer things in life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tycoon - a wealthy, powerful businessperson
