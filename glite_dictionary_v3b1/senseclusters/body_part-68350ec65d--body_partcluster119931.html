body part

noun
anatomical segment of an organism
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi pɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi pɑːt / (BrE)
any component of a living organism, such as an organ, limb, or other anatomical segment.

Examples

Injured body part
He suffered an injury to his right body part during the football game.
Some animals can regenerate a lost body part over time.
The doctor asked her to identify which body part was in pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organ - a part of an organism with a specific function
