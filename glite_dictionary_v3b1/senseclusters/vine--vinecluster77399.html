vine

verb
to grow like a vine
5.4
Pronunciation: /vaɪn/ (AmE), /vaɪn/ (BrE)
to grow or spread by climbing, trailing, or winding like a vine

Examples

Vine up a wall
The morning glories vined along the fence, creating a beautiful green wall.
Over the years, the plant vined across the garden path and covered the stones.
She watched as the pumpkin quickly vined through the vegetable patch.
The morning glories will vine up the fence by midsummer.
Some plants vine along the ground instead of growing upright.
If you give them support, the cucumbers will quickly vine over the trellis.
