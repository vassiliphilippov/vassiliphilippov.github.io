hot toddy

noun
warm alcoholic drink with spices and sugar
9.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /hɑːt ˈtɑː.diː/ (AmE), /hɒt ˈtɒd.iː/ (BrE)
A mixed drink made with liquor, hot water, sugar, and spices, often served as a remedy for colds.

Examples

A hot toddy drink
He ordered a hot toddy at the bar to warm himself up.
On cold winter nights, I like to relax with a hot toddy by the fire.
Some people believe that a hot toddy helps soothe a sore throat.
