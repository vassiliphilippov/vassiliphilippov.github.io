Synonyms

  • conception
    4.6 16/m
    conception is the initial creation of an idea in the mind, while conceptualization is the more complete process of shaping that idea.
  • ideation
    7.9 0.69/m
    ideation is a creative, often fast process of generating ideas, while conceptualization is forming and refining an idea into a clear concept.
  • gestation
    6.8 0.31/m
    gestation is a slow mental development of a plan over time, while conceptualization is the act of forming the idea clearly.
