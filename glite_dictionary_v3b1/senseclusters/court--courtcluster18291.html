court

noun
hotel with direct access from rooms to parking area
Pronunciation: / kɔrt / (AmE), / kɔːt / (BrE)
A roadside hotel or motel with rooms that open directly onto a parking area, typically catering to motorists.

Examples

Roadside court
Parking was never a problem because each room at the court had its own space.
The old court has been renovated into a charming boutique motel.
We stayed at a small court on Route 66 during our road trip.
