pectoral arch

noun
shoulder girdle bones
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛktɚrəl ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɛktərəl ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
the bony structure in the shoulder region formed by the collarbones (clavicles) and shoulder blades (scapulae)
