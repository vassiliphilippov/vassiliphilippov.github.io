nub

noun
the essential or central point
Pronunciation: / nʌb / (AmE), / nʌb / (BrE)
the central or most important point or part of something, especially an argument or discussion

Examples

The nub of the issue
She explained the nub of her argument in a single sentence.
The nub of the debate was whether the law should be changed.
We finally reached the nub of the issue after hours of discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crux - the most important or decisive point
  • synonym: essence - the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality
  • antonym: periphery - the outer limits or edge, less important part
