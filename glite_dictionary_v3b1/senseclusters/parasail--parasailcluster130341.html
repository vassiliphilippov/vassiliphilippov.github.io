parasail

noun
parachute used in parasailing
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌseɪl / (AmE), / ˈpærəseɪl / (BrE)
a special parachute designed to lift a person into the air when pulled by a motorboat or car, used in the sport of parasailing

Examples

A parasail above water
A brightly colored parasail could be seen high above the beach.
She strapped herself into the parasail before the boat accelerated across the water.
Tourists lined up for their chance to ride the parasail over the waves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parachute - fabric device used for slowing descent from high altitudes
