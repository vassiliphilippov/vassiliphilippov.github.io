buckle

verb
to fasten with a buckle
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌk.əl / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.əl / (BrE)
to fasten or secure (something) with a buckle or buckles

Examples

To buckle a strap
He buckled the straps on his backpack and set out for his hike.
Make sure you buckle your shoes tightly so they don’t come off.
She quickly buckled her seatbelt before the car started moving.
