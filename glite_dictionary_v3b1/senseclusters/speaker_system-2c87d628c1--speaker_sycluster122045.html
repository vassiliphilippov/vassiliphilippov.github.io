speaker system

noun
device for reproducing sound
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːkər ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈspiːkə ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of loudspeakers arranged together to convert electrical signals into audible sound, typically for use in audio playback or public address.

Examples

A speaker system setup
He installed a high-end speaker system in his living room for a true home theater experience.
The band tested the speaker system before the concert to ensure the best audio quality.
The new speaker system filled the auditorium with clear and powerful sound.
