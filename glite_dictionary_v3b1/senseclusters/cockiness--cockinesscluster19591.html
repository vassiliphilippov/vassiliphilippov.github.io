cockiness

noun
overconfident or arrogant boldness
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːkiˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒkiˌnɪs / (BrE)
confident or arrogant behavior that shows a lack of respect for others; overbearing boldness or self-assurance

Examples

His cockiness annoyed others
His cockiness rubbed many of his colleagues the wrong way during meetings.
If you want to make friends here, try to leave your cockiness at the door.
She mistook his cockiness for genuine self-confidence at first.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - the quality of having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym: modesty - the quality of being unassuming or moderate in estimation of one's abilities
