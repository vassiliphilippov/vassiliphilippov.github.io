disconnected

adjective
not connected or unified; divided
4.1 4.1
Pronunciation: /dɪs.kən.ˈnek.tɪd/ (AmE), /dɪs.kən.ˈnek.təd/ (BrE)
lacking unity, logical connection, or smooth flow (especially in speech, writing, or thought)

Examples

Disconnected ideas
His argument felt disconnected from the main topic and was hard to follow.
