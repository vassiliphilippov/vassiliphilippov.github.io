mirrorless

adjective
without a mirror
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrərləs / (AmE), / ˈmɪrələs / (BrE)
lacking a mirror or not having a mirror present.

Examples

Mirrorless hallway
The hallway was completely mirrorless, making it feel smaller and darker.
She bought a mirrorless camera for her photography class.
His new apartment was mirrorless, so he had to buy one for the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mirrored - having a mirror or covered with mirrors
