Education Secretary

noun
government minister for education
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri /; / ˌɛʤəˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
the government minister or official in charge of the Department of Education, responsible for national education policy
