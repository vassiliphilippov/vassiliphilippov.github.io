setout

noun
arrangement of items
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛtˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɛtˌaʊt / (BrE)
an arrangement or display of items, or the act of organizing things for presentation or use

Examples

Buffet setout
The chef admired the setout of dishes on the buffet table.
She was praised for the neat setout of her presentation materials.
The setout of products in the store made it easy for customers to find what they needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - an arrangement of items for people to look at
  • synonym: layout - the way in which items are arranged or organized
