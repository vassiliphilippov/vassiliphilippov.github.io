Loss of the ability to move all or part of the body and sometimes to feel anything in it, caused by illness, injury, or damage to the nerves.

Examples

Paralysis of limbs

Temporary paralysis can occur after certain medical procedures.
The accident left her with complete paralysis in both legs.

Synonyms
