aphyllous

adjective
devoid of leaves (botany)
5.8
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪləs / (AmE), / əˈfɪləs / (BrE)
(of a plant) lacking leaves.

Examples

Aphyllous plant
During the dry season, the plant becomes aphyllous to conserve water.
Some succulents are naturally aphyllous and rely on their stems for photosynthesis.
The botanist described several aphyllous species found in the arid region.
