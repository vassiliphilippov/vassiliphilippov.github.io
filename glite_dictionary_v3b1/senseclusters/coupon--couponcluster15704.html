coupon

verb
to give discounts
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːpɑn / (AmE), / ˈkuːpɒn / (BrE)
to distribute or use coupons, especially to offer discounts or promotions

Examples

To coupon products
The store couponed its products during the holiday season to attract more customers.
Many companies have couponed new items to boost their sales.
She couponed her groceries and saved a significant amount of money last month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charge - to ask for payment for something
  • synonym: discount - to reduce the price of something
