jumbo jet

noun
very large jet airliner
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmboʊ dʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmbəʊ dʒɛt / (BrE)
a very large jet airliner, typically one capable of carrying a large number of passengers

Examples

A jumbo jet plane
Hundreds of passengers boarded the jumbo jet for the transatlantic flight.
Spotting a jumbo jet taking off is always impressive.
The airline introduced a new jumbo jet on its international routes.
