shop steward

noun
union member representing workers to management
5.8 0.15/m
Pronunciation: /  É…ëp ÀàstuÀê…ôrd / (AmE), /  É…íp ÀàstjuÀê…ôd / (BrE)
an employee elected by workers in a trade union to represent them in discussions and negotiations with management

Examples

Shop steward role
As a shop steward, she regularly communicates with both union members and the company management.
If you have any issues at work, talk to your shop steward for guidance.
The shop steward called a meeting to discuss workers' concerns about safety regulations.

Synonyms

  • steward
    5.0 0.80/m

  • union representative
    3.7 0.12/m
    union representative is a person chosen or appointed to represent members to management or authorities, while shop steward is the elected workplace representative.
