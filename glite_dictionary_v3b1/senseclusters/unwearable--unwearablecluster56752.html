unwearable

adjective
not fit to be worn
5.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàw…õr…ôb…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàw…õÀêr…ôbl / (BrE)
not suitable or fit to be worn; impossible or inappropriate to wear

Examples

An unwearable costume
He found the costume unwearable because it was far too small.
The coat was so torn and stained that it became completely unwearable.
These shoes are unwearable after years of heavy use.

Synonyms

  • unsuitable
    2.6 1.71/m
    unsuitable describes things that do not suit a situation or person, while unwearable means they cannot or should not be worn.
  • inappropriate
    3.7 21/m
    inappropriate is used for clothing or behavior that breaks social rules, while unwearable describes garments that cannot be worn safely or comfortably.
  • unfit
    3.7 2.31/m
    unfit often means not good enough or unable to meet a standard, while unwearable means something is impossible or wrong to wear.
