fumer

noun
smoke-producing device
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːmər / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmə / (BrE)
a device or apparatus that produces smoke or fumes, often used in laboratories or industrial settings

Examples

A laboratory fumer

A fumer was installed to safely vent the smoke from the experiment.
