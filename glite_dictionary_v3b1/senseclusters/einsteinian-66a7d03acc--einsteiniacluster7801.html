Einsteinian

adjective
relating to Albert Einstein or his theories
7.7
Pronunciation: / aɪnˈstaɪniən / (AmE), / aɪnˈstaɪniən / (BrE)
Relating to Albert Einstein or his theories, especially those concerning physics such as the theory of relativity.

Examples

Einsteinian relativity theory
Many physicists still debate the implications of Einsteinian relativity for our understanding of space and time.
The Einsteinian model replaced Newtonian ideas about gravity with a geometric theory of spacetime.
The Einsteinian revolution transformed modern physics in the twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Newtonian - relating to Isaac Newton or his theories, especially classical mechanics
