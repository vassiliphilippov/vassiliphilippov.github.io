uncemented

adjective
not bonded by cement
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛntɪd / (BrE)
not bonded or held together by mineral cement, remaining loose or unconsolidated, especially in geology or soil science

Examples

Uncemented sand layer
The geologist noted that the uncemented sand was easily eroded by wind and water.
Layers of uncemented gravel can be unstable and require special construction techniques.
The riverbank collapsed because it was made of uncemented soil lacking any natural binding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cemented - bonded or held together by mineral cement
  • synonym: unconsolidated - not compacted or cemented together; loose
