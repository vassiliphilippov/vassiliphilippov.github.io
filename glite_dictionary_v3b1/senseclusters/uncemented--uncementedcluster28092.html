uncemented

adjective
not bonded by cement
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛntɪd / (BrE)
not bonded or held together by mineral cement, remaining loose or unconsolidated, especially in geology or soil science
