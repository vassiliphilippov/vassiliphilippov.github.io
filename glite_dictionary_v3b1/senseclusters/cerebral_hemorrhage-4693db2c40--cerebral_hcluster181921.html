cerebral hemorrhage

noun
bleeding in the brain
Pronunciation: / səˈribɹəl ˈhɛmərɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈriːbrəl ˈhɛmərɪdʒ / (BrE)
Bleeding within the brain caused by a ruptured blood vessel.

Examples

A cerebral hemorrhage event
Early surgery can sometimes improve outcomes after a cerebral hemorrhage.
High blood pressure is a major risk factor for cerebral hemorrhage.
The patient suffered a cerebral hemorrhage following a severe head injury.
