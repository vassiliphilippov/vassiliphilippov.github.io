trying

verb
to make an effort
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtraɪɪŋ / (BrE)
to make an effort to do or accomplish something

Examples

Trying to help
She is trying to learn a new language before her trip.
Despite trying several times, he could not solve the puzzle.
They kept trying until they finally reached the top of the mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attempt - to try to do something
  • antonym: fail - to not succeed in doing something
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the desired result
