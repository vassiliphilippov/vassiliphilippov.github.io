Synonyms

  • try for
    3.1 4/m
    try for is used to attempt to reach a specific goal with planned effort, while trying means making an effort in general.
  • go for
    2.5 13/m
    go for is a casual phrase for attempting to get something or win, while trying covers any effort or attempt.
  • put forth
    4.1 2/m
    put forth sounds formal and means making a strong effort or showing work, while trying is the general act of effort.
  • strive
    3.7 7/m
    strive means working very hard over time to reach a goal, while trying can be a single or small effort.
  • push
    1.5 30/m
    push means making a forceful, determined effort to succeed, while trying simply describes making an effort without strong force.
  • take a stab at
    5.3 0.5/m
    take a stab at is a tentative, sometimes casual attempt when unsure, while trying is any effort to do something.
  • make a fist of
    8.2 0.00/m
    make a fist of often means making an effort to handle something difficult, sometimes succeeding, while trying only means making an effort.
