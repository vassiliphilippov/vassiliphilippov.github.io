reinvest

verb
to invest again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈvɛst / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈvɛst / (BrE)
to use money that has been earned or received to invest again, often in the same or a new business, project, or asset

Examples

He reinvested the interest from his savings account to increase his future returns.
After selling his shares, he chose to reinvest the money in a new startup.
