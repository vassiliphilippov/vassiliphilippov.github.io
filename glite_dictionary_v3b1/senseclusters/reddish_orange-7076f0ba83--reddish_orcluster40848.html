reddish orange

noun
a color between red and orange
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈɔːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A shade of orange that is closer to red than to yellow.

Examples

A reddish orange flower
A reddish orange flower stood out among the greenery.
She chose a reddish orange paint for the living room walls.
The sunset cast a reddish orange glow across the sky.
