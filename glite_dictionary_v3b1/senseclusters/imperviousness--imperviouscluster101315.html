imperviousness

noun
impenetrability; resistance to penetration
8.7 0.06/m

the quality or state of not allowing something, such as water, light, or people, to pass through or penetrate

Examples

Synonyms

  • impenetrability

    impenetrability says something cannot be entered or understood, while imperviousness talks about blocking things like water, light, or people.
