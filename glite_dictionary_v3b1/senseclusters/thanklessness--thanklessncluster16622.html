thanklessness

noun
lack of gratitude
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈθæŋkləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈθæŋkləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not showing or receiving gratitude or appreciation

Examples

Complete thanklessness
Her years of hard work were met with complete thanklessness from her colleagues.
He was discouraged by the thanklessness of his efforts to help the community.
The job often involves a lot of thanklessness, but she continues because she cares.
Despite his efforts, he was met with only thanklessness from his colleagues.
The thanklessness of the job made her question whether it was worth continuing.
Volunteers sometimes feel discouraged by the thanklessness of their important work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gratitude - the feeling of being thankful
  • synonym: ingratitude - lack of thankfulness or appreciation
