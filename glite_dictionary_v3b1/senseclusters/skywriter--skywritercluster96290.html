skywriter

noun
writes messages in sky
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person or aircraft that writes words or draws pictures in the sky using smoke from an airplane
