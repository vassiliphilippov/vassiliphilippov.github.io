tempestuously

adverb
with intense emotion
Pronunciation: / tɛmˈpɛstʃuəsli / (AmE), / tɛmˈpɛstjʊəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that is full of strong, turbulent emotion or activity

Examples

React tempestuously
The crowd reacted tempestuously to the controversial announcement.
Waves crashed tempestuously against the rocky shore during the storm.
She argued tempestuously, her voice rising with every word.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or quiet manner
  • synonym: passionately - in a way that shows strong feelings or emotions
