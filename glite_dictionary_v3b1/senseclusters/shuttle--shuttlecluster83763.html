shuttle

verb
travel back and forth repeatedly
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtl̩ / (BrE)
to travel frequently back and forth between two places, often as part of a regular service

Examples

To shuttle people
A small boat shuttled passengers back and forth across the river all day.
Each day, she shuttles between her home and the city for work.
The company shuttles its employees between the main office and the remote warehouse every hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commute - to travel regularly between home and work
