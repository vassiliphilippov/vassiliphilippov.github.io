lapwing

noun
type of large crested plover
Pronunciation: / ˈlæp.wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlap.wɪŋ / (BrE)
a large crested Old World plover, especially the northern lapwing, known for its rounded wings and distinctive flight.

Examples

A lapwing bird
A flock of lapwings was seen feeding in the wet meadow this morning.
During spring, the lapwing performs acrobatic courtship displays over the fields.
The iridescent feathers of the lapwing shimmer in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plover - a type of wading bird related to lapwings
