waffle on

verb
be indecisive or vague
9.0 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ëÀêf…ôl …ën / (AmE), / Ààw…íf…ôl …ín / (BrE)
to be indecisive or speak in a vague, evasive way, especially when avoiding making a clear decision or statement
