wedgy

adjective
wedge-shaped; uncomfortable
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛdʒi / (AmE), / ˈwɛdʒi / (BrE)
having the shape of a wedge or causing a feeling similar to a wedgie; informal and slang usage.

Examples

Wedgy shoes
After walking for hours, his shoes felt tight and a bit wedgy on his feet.
She wore a wedgy dress that flared out at the bottom.
The seat cushion was so wedgy that it made sitting uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortable - providing physical ease
  • synonym: wedge-shaped - having the shape of a wedge
