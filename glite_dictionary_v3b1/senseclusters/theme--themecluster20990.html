theme

verb
to give something a specific theme or style
Pronunciation: / θim / (AmE), / θiːm / (BrE)
to give a particular subject, style, or motif to something

Examples

To theme a party
Each year, the school themes its prom around a different decade.
The chef themed the dinner party menu with Mediterranean flavors.
We are theming the conference rooms to match different countries.
They themed the party around a tropical island, complete with palm trees and beach music.
The amusement park is themed after famous fairy tales to attract families with children.
For the event, the organizers themed each room to represent a different country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorate - to add ornaments or details for appearance
  • synonym: design - to plan and fashion artistically or skillfully
  • synonym: personalize - to make something suitable for a particular person or purpose
