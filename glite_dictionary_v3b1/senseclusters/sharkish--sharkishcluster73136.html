sharkish

adjective
predatory or aggressive
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːkɪʃ / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics similar to a shark, especially being predatory, aggressive, or ruthless

Examples

Sharkish business tactics
The new manager's sharkish tactics quickly intimidated the entire team.
Her sharkish instincts helped her close difficult business deals.
He adopted a sharkish approach when negotiating the contract terms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentle - kind and not aggressive
  • synonym: predatory - seeking to exploit or oppress others
  • synonym: ruthless - having no pity or compassion for others
