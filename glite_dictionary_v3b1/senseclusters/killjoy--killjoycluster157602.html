killjoy

noun
person who spoils others' fun
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlˌdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɪlˌdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
a person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment or pleasure of others

Examples

A killjoy at parties
Her strict rules made her seem like a real killjoy to the rest of the group.
Nobody wanted to invite Tom to the party because he was known as a killjoy.
Please don't be a killjoy—let us have a little fun tonight!

  • synonym: spoilsport - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
