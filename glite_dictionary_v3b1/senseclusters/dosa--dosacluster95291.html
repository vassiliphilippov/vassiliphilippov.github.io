dosa

noun
South Indian pancake
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊ.sə/ (AmE), /ˈdəʊ.sə/ (BrE)
a thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, commonly eaten in South India

Examples

She learned how to make a traditional dosa from her grandmother's recipe.
