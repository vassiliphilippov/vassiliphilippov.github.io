dosa

noun
South Indian pancake
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈdəʊsə / (BrE)
a thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, commonly eaten in South India

Examples

A crispy dosa
For breakfast, he enjoyed a crispy dosa with coconut chutney and sambar.
The restaurant is famous for its giant masala dosa that fills the entire plate.
She learned how to make a traditional dosa from her grandmother's recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pancake - a flat, round cake made from batter and cooked on a griddle
