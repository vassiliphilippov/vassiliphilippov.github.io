embellish

verb
make (something) more beautiful by decoration
4.8 5.1
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈbel.ɪʃ/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈbel.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
to make something more beautiful or attractive by adding decorative details

Examples

To embellish a dress
She embellished her dress with sparkling sequins for the party.
The artist embellished the plain room with colorful tapestries and paintings.
The chef embellished the dish with edible flowers and fresh herbs.
He decided to embellish the wedding cake with edible flowers.
She likes to embellish her stories with dramatic details.
The artist used bright colors to embellish the mural on the wall.

The artist likes to embellish his paintings with bright colors and intricate patterns.
