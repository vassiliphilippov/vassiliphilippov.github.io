unsymmetric

adjective
lacking symmetry
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.sɪˈmɛt.rɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɪˈmɛt.rɪk / (BrE)
not having symmetry; lacking balance or identical arrangement on both sides of a dividing line or around a center.

Examples

An unsymmetric shape
Her haircut was strikingly unsymmetric, drawing attention to one side of her face.
Scientists studied the unsymmetric distribution of particles in the experiment.
The design appeared deliberately unsymmetric, creating a sense of dynamic movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asymmetric - not symmetrical; lacking symmetry
  • antonym: symmetrical - having symmetry; same on both sides
