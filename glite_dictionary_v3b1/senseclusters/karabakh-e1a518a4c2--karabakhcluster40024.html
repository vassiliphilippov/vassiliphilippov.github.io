Karabakh

proper noun
historic Caucasus region
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəbɑːk /; / ˈkɑːrəˌbɑːk / (AmE), / ˈkærəbɑːk /; / ˈkærəbæk / (BrE)
a region in the South Caucasus, known for its historical, cultural, and political significance, and sometimes for its disputed status
