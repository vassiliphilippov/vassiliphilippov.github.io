chimney breast

noun
part of wall enclosing a chimney
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmni brɛst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni brɛst / (BrE)
The part of a wall that protrudes into a room to house and enclose the lower part of a chimney.
