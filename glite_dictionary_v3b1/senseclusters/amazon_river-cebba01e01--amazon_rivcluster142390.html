Amazon River

proper noun
major South American river
Pronunciation: / ˈæməzɑn ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈaməzən ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
the world's second longest river, flowing eastward across northern South America from the Andes to the Atlantic Ocean

Examples

The Amazon River basin
Researchers traveled deep into the Amazon River basin to study its biodiversity.
The Amazon River carries more water than any other river in the world.
The Amazon River is home to thousands of unique plant and animal species.
