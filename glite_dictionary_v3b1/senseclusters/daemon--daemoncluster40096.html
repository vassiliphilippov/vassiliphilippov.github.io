daemon

noun
evil supernatural being or spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːmən / (AmE), / ˈdiːmən / (BrE)
An evil supernatural being or spirit; often associated with mischief or harm in mythology and folklore.

Examples

Evil daemon spirit
In the story, the hero must confront the daemon terrorizing his dreams.
Legends tell of a daemon haunting the ruined castle at the edge of the village.
The ancient Greeks believed that a daemon could influence a person's fate, for good or ill.

  • synonym: demon - an evil spirit or devil
