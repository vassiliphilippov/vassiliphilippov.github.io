hurt a fly

verb
to be extremely gentle, not harm anyone
3.8 4.1
Pronunciation: /hɜːrt ə flaɪ/ (AmE), /hɜːt ə flaɪ/ (BrE)
(usually in negative constructions) to be so gentle or harmless that one would not harm even the smallest or most innocent creature; to not harm anyone.

Examples

She is so kind that she wouldn't hurt a fly.
