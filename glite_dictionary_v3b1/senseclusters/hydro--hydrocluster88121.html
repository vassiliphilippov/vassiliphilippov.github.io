hydro

noun
water-generated electricity
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊ / (BrE)
electricity generated by water power, or a facility that produces such electricity

Examples

Hydro power plant
The city gets most of its electricity from hydro.
Many rural communities rely on hydro for their power supply.
The new hydro plant will provide clean energy to thousands of homes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydroelectricity - electricity produced by the energy of moving water
  • antonym: thermal - electricity generated from heat, especially from burning fuel
