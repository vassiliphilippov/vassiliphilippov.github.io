Synonyms

  • hydroelectricity
    3.9 0.2/m
    hydroelectricity is electricity produced from moving water at dams and rivers, while hydro can mean that electricity or the power plant.
  • hydropower
    3.6 1/m
    hydropower means energy from moving water that turns turbines to make electricity, while hydro can mean that energy or the plant.
  • waterpower
    3.9 0.02/m
