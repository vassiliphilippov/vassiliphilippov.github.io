storytime

noun
exaggerated tale
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈstɔːritaɪm / (BrE)
an exaggerated or unlikely account told as a story, often used sarcastically to suggest someone is inventing or fabricating events

Examples

Tall storytime
Everyone rolled their eyes when he started another storytime about his supposed adventures.
She always has a dramatic storytime ready when she is late to meetings.
After hearing his wild storytime, nobody was convinced he was telling the truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tall tale - an exaggerated or improbable story
  • antonym: truth - the quality or state of being true
