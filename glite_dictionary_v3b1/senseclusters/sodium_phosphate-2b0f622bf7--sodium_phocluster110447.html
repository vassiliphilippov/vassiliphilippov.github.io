sodium phosphate

noun
a phosphate salt of sodium, used as a laxative and cleaner
5.1 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdiəm ˈfɑsfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdiəm ˈfɒsfeɪt / (BrE)
a white crystalline salt, Na3PO4, or any of several related phosphates of sodium, used in medicine as a laxative and in industry for cleaning and water treatment
