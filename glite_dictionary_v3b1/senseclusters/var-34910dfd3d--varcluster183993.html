VAR

noun
Video Assistant Referee (football)
Pronunciation: / viː eɪ ɑɹ / (AmE), / viː eɪ ɑː / (BrE)
(in football (soccer)) an official or a system that assists the on-field referee by reviewing incidents using video replays and advising on decisions.

Examples

The VAR system
Late in the game the referee checked the VAR before awarding the penalty.
After a long review, the VAR overturned the goal for an offside violation.
Fans argued that the VAR ruined the flow of the match, but referees defended it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: video assistant referee - an official or system that reviews match footage to help refereeing decisions
