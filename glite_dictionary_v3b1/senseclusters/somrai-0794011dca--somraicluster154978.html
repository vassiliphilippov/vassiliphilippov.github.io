Somrai

proper noun
a Chadic language of Chad
Pronunciation: / sʌmˈraɪ / (AmE), / sʌmˈraɪ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Tandjile Region of Chad, particularly northeast of Lai.

Examples

Somrai language
He grew up speaking both French and Somrai at home.
Linguists are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of Somrai.
The Somrai language is spoken by several communities in southern Chad.
