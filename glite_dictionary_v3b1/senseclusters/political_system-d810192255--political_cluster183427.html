political system

noun
the organization of power in a society
1.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the set of institutions, processes, and people that organize, govern, and exercise authority in a society

Examples

A political system
Democracy is just one type of political system found around the world.
Many citizens are dissatisfied with the current political system and are calling for change.
The political system of the country underwent major reforms in the last decade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anarchy - a situation where there is no government or control
  • synonym: government - the group of people governing a community or country
  • synonym: regime - a particular government or system of government
