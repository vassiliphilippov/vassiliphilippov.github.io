Synonyms

  • drop down
    1.8 2/m
    drop down is used for moving from a higher to a lower place, while fall is a general, often sudden downward movement.
  • descend
    2.7 7/m
    descend is a more formal verb for going down slowly or in control, while fall often means dropping suddenly by gravity.
  • come down
    2.8 10/m
    come down describes moving or becoming lower in casual talk or prices, while fall stresses a physical drop by gravity.
  • plunge
    3.8 4/m
    plunge means to fall or drop very quickly and with force, while fall is a general downward movement.
  • plummet
    5.8 4/m
    plummet means to fall steeply and suddenly, often used for prices or numbers, while fall is general downward movement.
  • go down
    1.9 15/m
    go down is used when amounts or levels become lower or when things move down slowly, while fall implies a drop by gravity.
