overbuild

noun
excessive construction
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌbɪld / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌbɪld / (BrE)
the act or result of constructing too many buildings or developing an area excessively

Examples

Urban overbuild
The rapid overbuild in the city center led to traffic congestion and a lack of green spaces.
Local residents protested against the overbuild of luxury apartments in their neighborhood.
Strict zoning laws were introduced to prevent further overbuild in the historic district.
