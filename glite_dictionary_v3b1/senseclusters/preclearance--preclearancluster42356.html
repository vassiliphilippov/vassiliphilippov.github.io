preclearance

noun
advance border checks
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈklɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈklɪərəns / (BrE)
the process of completing customs and immigration checks before beginning a journey, usually at the departure location

Examples

Airport preclearance process
International travelers appreciated the convenience of preclearance at their home airport.
After completing preclearance, passengers could enter the country without further checks.
The airport introduced preclearance to speed up the arrival process for visitors.
