semiquantitatively

adverb
approximately measuring
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌkwɑntɪˈteɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌkwɒntɪˈteɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that measures or estimates something approximately, rather than exactly or precisely

Examples

To measure semiquantitatively
The scientists measured the chemical concentration semiquantitatively because the equipment was not highly accurate.
In this study, the data were analyzed semiquantitatively to provide a general overview of the results.
The technician reported the findings semiquantitatively due to the lack of precise measuring tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approximately - in a way that is close to the exact amount but not completely accurate
  • antonym: precisely - in an exact and accurate way
