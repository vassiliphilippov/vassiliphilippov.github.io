playa

noun
slang womanizer
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪə / (AmE), / ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person, usually a man, who has many casual romantic or sexual relationships and often seduces others

Examples

A smooth playa
Everyone knew him as a playa who was never interested in a serious relationship.
She warned her friend that the charming guy at the party was a notorious playa.
Despite his reputation as a playa, he claimed he was ready to settle down.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithful - loyal and devoted in a romantic relationship
  • synonym: player - a person who dates or seduces many people
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who has many casual sexual relationships with women
