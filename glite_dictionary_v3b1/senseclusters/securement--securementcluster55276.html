securement

noun
act of securing
8.2
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrmənt / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊəmənt / (BrE)
the act of making something secure, especially by providing collateral or creating a legal security interest to guarantee a debt or obligation

Examples

Securement of assets
The securement of assets was necessary before the contract could be finalized.
Proper securement of collateral is required by most banks when issuing large loans.
Legal documents outlined the securement process for the new business agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collateralization - the act of providing collateral for a loan
  • synonym: guarantee - a promise or assurance that something will be done or provided
