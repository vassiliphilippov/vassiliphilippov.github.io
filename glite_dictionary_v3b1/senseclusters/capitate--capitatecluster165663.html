capitate

noun
largest wrist bone; central carpal bone
6.1 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.ɪ.tət/ /; / /kə.ˈpɪt.ət/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.ɪ.tət/ /; / /kə.ˈpɪt.ət/ (BrE)
the largest bone in the wrist, located in the center of the carpal bones, with a rounded head that articulates with the third metacarpal bone

Examples

Capitate bone
An X-ray revealed an injury to the capitate following the fall.
Fractures of the capitate are rare compared to other carpal bones.
The capitate is the largest of the carpals and lies at the center of the wrist.
