Amon-Ra

proper noun
Egyptian sun god, supreme deity
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːm.ˈɔːn rɑː/ /; / /ˈæ.mən rɑː/ (AmE), /ˈæ.mən rɑː/ /; / /ə.ˈmɒn rɑː/ (BrE)
An Egyptian sun god, regarded as the supreme deity of the universe in whom the gods Amen and Ra were merged, especially venerated during the period of Theban supremacy.

Examples

Priests of Amon-Ra played a central role in Theban society.
The temple was dedicated to Amon-Ra, the most powerful god in ancient Egypt.
