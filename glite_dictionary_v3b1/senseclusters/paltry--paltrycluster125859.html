paltry

adjective
insignificant; very small or worthless
8.2 5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔltri /; / ˈpɔl.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːltri /; / ˈpɔːl.tri / (BrE)
very small or unimportant; not worth considering

Examples

A paltry sum
He was disappointed by the paltry sum he received for his old car.
She dismissed his paltry excuse and demanded a proper explanation.
The company offered a paltry bonus to its employees despite record profits.
Many workers are struggling to survive on a paltry wage.
She made a paltry contribution to the fundraising campaign, disappointing the organizers.
The bonus he received was a paltry sum compared to his hard work.
