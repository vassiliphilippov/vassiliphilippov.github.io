yeah, yeah

interjection
expression of indifference or impatience
4.5
Pronunciation: / jɛə jɛə / (AmE), / jɛə jɛə / (BrE)
an informal expression used to show indifference, impatience, or lack of interest in what someone is saying

Examples

"Yeah, yeah, I know"
"Yeah, yeah, I'll do it later," she replied without looking up from her phone.
He kept insisting he would fix it, and I just said, yeah, yeah, to move on.
Whenever my brother starts telling the same story again, I just nod and say yeah, yeah.
