Brazil nut

noun
large edible nut from South America
5.8
Pronunciation: / brəˈzɪl nʌt / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl nʌt / (BrE)
A large, three-sided edible nut from a South American tree, with white oily meat and a hard brown shell.

Examples

A Brazil nut snack
Harvesting Brazil nuts plays a crucial role in supporting local economies in the Amazon.
She cracked open a Brazil nut and offered it to her friend.
The trail mix included almonds, walnuts, and pieces of Brazil nut.
