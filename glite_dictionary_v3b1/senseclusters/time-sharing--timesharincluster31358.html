time-sharing

noun
shared use by schedule
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪm.ʃeɪr.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈtaɪm.ʃer.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪm.ʃeə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of dividing and allocating available time or resources among multiple people, tasks, or activities according to a schedule

Examples

Time-sharing the lab
The company introduced time-sharing to allow different teams to use the laboratory throughout the week.
Effective time-sharing of the computer system ensured that all users could complete their work on time.
Parents practiced time-sharing of the family car so everyone could get to their activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allocation - the act of distributing resources or time for a specific purpose
  • synonym: scheduling - the process of planning when tasks or events will happen
