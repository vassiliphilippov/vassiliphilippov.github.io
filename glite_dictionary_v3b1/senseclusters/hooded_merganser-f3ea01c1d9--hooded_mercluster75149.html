hooded merganser

noun
small North American duck with crest
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdɪd mɜɹˈɡænsɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʊdɪd mɜːˈɡansə / (BrE)
a small North American duck with a distinctive high circular crest on the male's head

Examples

The hooded mergansers swim
Birdwatchers spotted several hooded mergansers swimming near the marsh.
During spring, the male hooded merganser displays its crest to attract females.
The hooded merganser can be seen diving for fish in many North American lakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: merganser - a fish-eating diving duck
