hike

verb
walk for pleasure or exercise
Pronunciation: / haɪk / (AmE), / haɪk / (BrE)
to take a long walk, especially in the countryside or wilderness, for pleasure or physical exercise

Examples

To hike a trail
Last summer, she hiked across several states on the Appalachian Trail.
They spent the afternoon hiking through the national park.
We decided to hike up the mountain trail early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ramble - to walk for pleasure in the countryside
  • synonym: trek - to make a long or arduous journey, especially by foot
