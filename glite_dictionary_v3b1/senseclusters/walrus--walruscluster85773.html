walrus

noun
large tusked Arctic marine mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔl.rəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːl.rəs / (BrE)
a large marine mammal of Arctic regions, with long ivory tusks, whiskers, and thick blubber, found in northern seas

Examples

A walrus with tusks
A walrus can weigh more than a ton and has impressive tusks.
The walrus uses its whiskers to search for food on the ocean floor.
We spotted a walrus basking on the ice during our Arctic expedition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seal - a marine mammal with flippers and no tusks
  • synonym: walruses - plural form of walrus
