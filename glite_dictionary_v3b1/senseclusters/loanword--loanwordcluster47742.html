loanword

noun
word taken from another language
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈləʊnˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A word taken into one language from another without translation.

Examples

A loanword from English
Scholars often study how loanwords are adopted into different languages.
The English language contains many loanwords from French and Latin.
‘Karaoke’ is a loanword borrowed from Japanese.

Related Terms

  • synonym: borrowing - the process of taking words from other languages
