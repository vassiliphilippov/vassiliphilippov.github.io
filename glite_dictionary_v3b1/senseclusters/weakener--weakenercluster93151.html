weakener

noun
something that makes weaker
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkənər / (AmE), / ˈwiːkənə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something weaker or causes a loss of strength or intensity

Examples

A weakener of strength
Adding too much water is a common weakener of concrete mix strength.
Doubt can be a powerful weakener of determination in any ambitious plan.
The resignation of key leaders acted as a major weakener of party unity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strengthener - a person or thing that makes something stronger
