patty shell

noun
small hollow shell of puff pastry
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpæti ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈpæti ʃɛl / (BrE)
A small, hollow shell made of puff pastry, typically used to hold savory or sweet fillings in appetizers or desserts.

Examples

A patty shell
Cooks often use a patty shell as an edible container for creamed seafood or vegetables.
She bought frozen patty shells to save time for the party appetizers.
The chicken mixture was spooned into a patty shell and served hot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vol-au-vent - a small hollow case of puff pastry filled with a savory mixture
