spit

noun
rod for holding meat over fire
6.4
Pronunciation: / spɪt / (AmE), / spɪt / (BrE)
a long, thin rod or skewer used for holding meat over a fire while it is being roasted

Examples

A metal spit
After the hike, we grilled sausages on a spit by the campfire.
He slowly turned the lamb on the spit as it roasted over the fire.
The medieval feast featured a whole pig cooked on a spit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skewer - a thin metal or wooden stick used to hold food together or in place while cooking
