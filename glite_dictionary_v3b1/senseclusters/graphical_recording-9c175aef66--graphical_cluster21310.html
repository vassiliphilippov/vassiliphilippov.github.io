graphical recording

noun
visual representation of data as plotted points or lines
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪkəl rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪkəl rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a visual representation of relationships between quantities, shown as points or lines plotted with reference to axes

Examples

A graphical recording of data
A clear graphical recording can help explain complex results to a wider audience.
Modern lab equipment produces a digital graphical recording of each experiment.
The scientist analyzed the graphical recording to identify trends in the data.
