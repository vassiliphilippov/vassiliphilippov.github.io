Trimurti

noun
the Hindu triad of gods: Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva
9.9
Pronunciation: / trɪˈmʊrti / (AmE), / trɪˈmʊːti / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) the triad of the three principal gods: Brahma the creator, Vishnu the preserver, and Shiva the destroyer.

Examples

The Trimurti gods
In later Hindu philosophy, the concept of the Trimurti symbolizes creation, preservation, and destruction.
The statue represents all three deities of the Trimurti in a single form.
The temple is dedicated to the Trimurti, honoring Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva.
