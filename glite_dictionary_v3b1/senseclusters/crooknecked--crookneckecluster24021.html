crooknecked

adjective
curved-necked
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊkˌnɛkt / (AmE), / ˈkrʊkˌnɛkt / (BrE)
having a neck or stem that is curved or bent, especially referring to certain types of squash

Examples

Crooknecked squash
The gardener planted several crooknecked squash in the vegetable patch.
A crooknecked gourd hung from the vine, its stem twisting in a spiral.
She chose a crooknecked pumpkin for the autumn display because of its unusual shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curved - having a shape that is bent or not straight
  • antonym: straight - not curved or bent; having no bends or angles
