time loan

noun
loan repayable by a certain date
7.5
Pronunciation: / taɪm loʊn / (AmE), / taɪm ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan that must be repaid on or before a specified date

Examples

A time loan agreement
She negotiated the terms of the time loan with her bank manager.
The company secured a time loan to cover its short-term operating expenses.
Unlike a demand loan, a time loan has a fixed maturity date.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demand loan - loan repayable on demand at any time
