urchin

noun
mischievous street child
9.5 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːr.tʃən/ /; / /ˈɜːr.tʃɪn/ (AmE), /ˈɜː.tʃən/ /; / /ˈɜː.tʃɪn/ (BrE)
A poor and often mischievous child, especially one who lives or spends a lot of time in the streets of a city.

Examples
