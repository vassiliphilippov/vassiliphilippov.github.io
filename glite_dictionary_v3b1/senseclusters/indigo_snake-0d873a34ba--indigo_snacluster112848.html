indigo snake

noun
a large, nonvenomous blue snake of North America
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪɡoʊ sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪɡəʊ sneɪk / (BrE)
a large, dark-blue, nonvenomous snake found in southern North America and Mexico, known for invading burrows
