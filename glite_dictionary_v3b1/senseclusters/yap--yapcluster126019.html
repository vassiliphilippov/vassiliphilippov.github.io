yap

verb
to talk noisily
7.7
Pronunciation: / jæp / (AmE), / jæp / (BrE)
to talk continuously in a loud, annoying, or trivial way

Examples

Kids yap nonstop
The children would yap excitedly as they waited for the bus.
He tends to yap on the phone for hours about nothing important.
Please don't yap during the movie; it's distracting to everyone else.

Related Terms

  • antonym: be quiet - to stop talking or making noise
  • synonym: chatter - to talk rapidly about trivial matters
  • synonym: jabber - to talk quickly and excitedly, often making little sense
