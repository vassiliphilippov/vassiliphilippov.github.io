lymphoblastic

adjective
of immature white blood cells
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪmfoʊˈblæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɪmfəˈblæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of lymphoblasts, which are immature white blood cells

Examples

Lymphoblastic cells
The biopsy revealed a high number of lymphoblastic cells in the tissue sample.
Doctors often use the term lymphoblastic when diagnosing certain types of leukemia.
A lymphoblastic crisis can indicate rapid progression of the disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lymphocytic - relating to lymphocytes, a type of white blood cell
