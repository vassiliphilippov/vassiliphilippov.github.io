malabsorption syndrome

A medical condition characterized by poor absorption of nutrients from the digestive tract, leading to symptoms such as weight loss, bloating, and fatigue.

Examples

Malabsorption syndrome symptoms
A diagnosis of malabsorption syndrome often requires a combination of blood tests and imaging studies.
Symptoms such as diarrhea and bloating can indicate malabsorption syndrome.
The doctor suspected malabsorption syndrome when the patient continued losing weight despite eating well.
