purpose-made

adjective
specifically designed for a use
4.8 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀêp…ôs me…™d / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀêp…ôs me…™d / (BrE)
specially designed and made for a particular use or purpose.

Examples

Purpose-made tools
Each tool in the workshop was purpose-made for a specific task.
The furniture was purpose-made to fit the unique dimensions of the room.
They installed a purpose-made ramp to improve wheelchair access.

Synonyms

  • purpose-built
    5.6 0.49/m
    purpose-built is specially designed and built for a particular function or building, while purpose-made is made for any particular use.
  • tailor-made
    4.0 0.61/m
    tailor-made fits precise needs or measurements for a person or task, while purpose-made describes items made for a specific use.
  • custom-made
    3.8 1.35/m
    custom-made is made to an individual's preferences or requirements, while purpose-made is made to suit a particular function.
  • bespoke
    6.9 0.55/m
    bespoke refers mainly to clothing made to a person's measurements and style, while purpose-made means made for a particular use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom-made - made to a particular customer's specifications
  • antonym: off-the-shelf - available from stock and not custom made
