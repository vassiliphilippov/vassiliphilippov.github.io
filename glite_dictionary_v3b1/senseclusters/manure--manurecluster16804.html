manure

noun
organic fertilizer, especially animal excreta
Pronunciation: / məˈnʊr / (AmE), / məˈnjʊə / (BrE)
Animal or plant material, especially animal excreta mixed with litter, used to fertilize soil.

Examples

Cow manure
Composted manure is an excellent source of nutrients for vegetable gardens.
Farmers spread manure on their fields each spring to improve the soil.
The strong smell of manure was noticeable near the barn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to enhance plant growth
