circulation

noun
the spread or transmission of something
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɝː.kjəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.kjʊˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the spreading or transmission of something, such as information, news, or money, to a wider group or area

Examples

Circulation of news
The circulation of fake news on social media has become a major concern.
The new currency notes were put into circulation last month.
Wider circulation of these reports could help inform the public debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissemination - the action of spreading something widely
  • synonym: distribution - the act of giving or delivering something to people
