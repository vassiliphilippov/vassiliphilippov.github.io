chum in the water

noun
person or thing vulnerable to attack
Pronunciation: / tʃʌm ɪn ðə ˈwɑtər / (AmE), / tʃʌm ɪn ðə ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
someone or something that is exposed and vulnerable to attack or criticism, often attracting hostile or negative attention
