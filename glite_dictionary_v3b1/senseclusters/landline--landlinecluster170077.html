landline

noun
telephone line using wires or fiber optics
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)laɪn /; / ˈlan(d).laɪn / (BrE)
a telephone line that uses wires or fiber optics, not a mobile or cellular network

Examples

The landline phone
He called the office using the landline because his mobile phone battery was dead.
Many people are now disconnecting their landlines and relying solely on mobile phones.
We still have a landline at home in case of emergencies.
During the power outage, our landline was still working while the mobile network was down.
Please provide your landline number on the application form.
She prefers using her landline instead of a cell phone for important calls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cell phone - a portable telephone that uses cellular network
  • antonym: mobile phone - A wireless telephone that uses cellular networks.
