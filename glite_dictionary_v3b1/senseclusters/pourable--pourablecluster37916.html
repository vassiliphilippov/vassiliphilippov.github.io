pourable

adjective
able to be poured
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːrəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔːrəbl /; / ˈpɔːrəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be poured easily because of a suitable consistency or liquidity

Examples

Pourable sauce
The sauce should be pourable so it spreads evenly over the pasta.
Make sure the paint is pourable before you start using it on the walls.
If the mixture is not pourable, add a little more milk to thin it out.
The chocolate must be melted until it is pourable before you add it to the cake batter.
Concrete should be mixed to a pourable consistency for easy application in construction projects.
After heating, the wax became pourable and was carefully transferred into candle molds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liquid - having a consistency that flows easily
  • antonym: solid - having a firm and stable shape; not pourable
