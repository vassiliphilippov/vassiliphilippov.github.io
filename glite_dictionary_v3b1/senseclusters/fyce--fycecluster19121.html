fyce

noun
small mongrel dog
Pronunciation: / faɪs / (AmE), / faɪs / (BrE)
an old or dialect word for a small dog, especially a mongrel

A fyce dog

In the village, every household seemed to have a fyce guarding the yard.
The little fyce barked fiercely at the passing carriage despite its size.
