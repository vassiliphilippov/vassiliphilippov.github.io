Synonyms

  • aah
    6.9 11/m
    aah is a longer, softer sound showing pleasure, relief, or satisfaction after something nice, while ah shows quick surprise or realization.
  • oh
    1.0 871/m
    oh often marks sudden understanding or polite surprise in speech, while ah is more for brief feelings of wonder or relief.
