extremity

noun
farthest part of a limb (hand or foot)
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrɛm.ə.ti /; / ɪkˈstrɛmɪti / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrɛm.ɪ.ti /; / ɪkˈstrɛmɪti / (BrE)
a limb or the part of a limb that is farthest from the body, such as a hand, foot, arm, or leg
