nonpathological

adjective
not disease-related
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not related to or caused by disease or any abnormal medical condition

Examples

Nonpathological growth
The doctor confirmed the growth was nonpathological and not a cause for concern.
Some changes in the body are nonpathological and occur naturally with age.
The test results showed only nonpathological variations in the blood sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pathological - caused by or related to disease
