Caucasian walnut

noun
tree species with winged fruit, native to Caucasus
8.4
Pronunciation: /kɔː.ˈkeɪ.ʒən ˈwɔːl.nʌt/ /; / /kɔː.ˈkeɪ.zɪ.ən ˈwɔːl.nət/ /; / /kɔːk.ˈeɪ.ʒən ˈwɔːl.nʌt/ (AmE), /kɔː.ˈkeɪ.ʒən ˈwɔːl.nʌt/ /; / /kɔː.ˈkeɪ.zɪ.ən ˈwɔːl.nʌt/ (BrE)
