Thomas

the Apostle who doubted Jesus' resurrection

Examples

Thomas the Apostle
Many stories about Thomas appear in the New Testament, highlighting his doubts and eventual faith.
The phrase 'Doubting Thomas' originates from the Apostle's insistence on witnessing evidence before believing.
Tradition holds that Thomas traveled to India to spread Christianity after the resurrection.

Synonyms

  • Thomas the Apostle
    7.3 0.00/m
    Thomas the Apostle is the full, formal name used in books and church history, while Thomas is the shorter common name used in everyday writing.
  • Saint Thomas
    7.0 0.00/m
    Saint Thomas is the full honouring form used in formal church texts, while Thomas is the plain given name used by most readers.
  • St. Thomas
    7.0 0.06/m
    St. Thomas is the abbreviated title often seen on signs and maps, while Thomas is the simple personal name used in stories.
