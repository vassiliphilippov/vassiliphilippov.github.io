nothing less than the best

x
only the highest quality or standard
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ lɛs ðən ðə bɛst / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ lɛs ðən ðə bɛst / (BrE)
used to emphasize that only the highest quality or standard is acceptable or expected.

Examples

Nothing less than the best quality
For her wedding day, she wanted nothing less than the best in decorations and food.
Their commitment to quality ensures you'll receive nothing less than the best service every time.
When it comes to his children's education, he expects nothing less than the best.
