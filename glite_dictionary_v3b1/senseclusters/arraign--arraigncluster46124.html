arraign

verb
accuse or criticize openly
5.0 7.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈreɪn/ (AmE), /ə.ˈreɪn/ (BrE)
to accuse or criticize someone, especially to call them publicly to account for a fault or failing

Examples

To arraign a leader

Several critics arraigned the government for its handling of the crisis.
The manager was swiftly arraigned for the team's poor performance.
