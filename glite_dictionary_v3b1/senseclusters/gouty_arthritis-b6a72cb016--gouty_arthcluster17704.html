gouty arthritis

noun

A form of arthritis characterized by painful inflammation in the joints, especially the big toe and foot, caused by the accumulation of uric acid crystals.

Examples

Gouty arthritis pain
He experienced severe pain in his big toe due to gouty arthritis.
The patient developed gouty arthritis after years of untreated hyperuricemia.
Treatment for gouty arthritis often includes medication to reduce uric acid levels.
