spin around

verb
revolve quickly around own axis
Pronunciation: / spɪn əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / spɪn əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to revolve quickly and repeatedly around one's own axis

Examples

To spin around quickly
He tried to spin around quickly to catch a glimpse behind him.
The children like to spin around until they fall down laughing.
The ice skater began to spin around gracefully in the middle of the rink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whirl - to move or cause to move rapidly around and around
