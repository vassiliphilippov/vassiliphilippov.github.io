horntail

noun
wood-boring insect
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhɔːrn.teɪl/ /; / /ˈhɑːrn.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈhɔːn.teɪl/ (BrE)
a wasp-like insect with a long, horn-shaped tail, known for laying eggs in wood

Examples

A horntail insect
The horntail drills into tree trunks to lay its eggs safely inside the wood.
A large horntail was spotted near the fallen log in the forest.
Scientists studied the life cycle of the horntail to understand its impact on trees.
