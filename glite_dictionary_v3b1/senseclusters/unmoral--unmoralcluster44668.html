unmoral

adjective
not concerned with morality
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɔrəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒrəl / (BrE)
not concerned with morality or moral standards; neither moral nor immoral

Examples

Unmoral research
The scientist claimed his research was unmoral, focusing only on facts and not on ethical questions.
Some stories feature unmoral characters who act without considering right or wrong.
He argued that the law is unmoral and should not be judged by ethical standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amoral - not related to or lacking a sense of morality
  • antonym: immoral - not conforming to accepted standards of morality
  • antonym: moral - concerned with principles of right and wrong
