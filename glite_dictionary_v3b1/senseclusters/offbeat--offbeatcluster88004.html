offbeat

noun
unaccented beat in music
2.7 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːf.biːt/ /; / /ˈɑːf.biːt/ (AmE), /ˈɒf.biːt/ (BrE)
(in music) an unaccented beat, especially the last beat of a measure, opposite to the main strong beat.

Examples

A musical offbeat
Dancers listened for the offbeat to time their movements with the music.
In reggae music, the guitarist often plays on the offbeat to create a syncopated rhythm.
The drummer emphasized the offbeat, giving the song its catchy groove.
