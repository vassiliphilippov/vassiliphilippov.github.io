prerepublican

noun
supporter of former regime
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːrɪˈpʌblɪkən / (AmE), / ˌpriːrɪˈpʌblɪkən / (BrE)
a person who supports or identifies with the regime or institutions that existed before a republic was established

Examples

A prerepublican leader
The historian interviewed a prerepublican who remembered life before the revolution.
Many prerepublicans gathered to protest the new government's policies.
A vocal prerepublican argued for restoring the monarchy after the republic was declared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monarchist - a person who supports a monarchy
  • antonym: republican - a person who supports a republic or republican government
