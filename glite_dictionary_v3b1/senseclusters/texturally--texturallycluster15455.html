texturally

adverb
in terms of texture
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃərəli / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or concerns the texture of something

Examples

Texturally rich soup
The soup is texturally rich, with both smooth and chunky ingredients.
This fabric is texturally different from the one we used last year.
The cake is texturally light and airy, making it perfect for summer.
