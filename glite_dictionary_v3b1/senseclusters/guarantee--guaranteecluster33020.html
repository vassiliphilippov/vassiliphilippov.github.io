Synonyms

  • ensure
    2.8 110/m
    ensure means to take actions that make something happen, while guarantee usually adds a promise or formal backing for that outcome.
  • assure
    3.5 15/m
    assure usually comforts a person by saying something will happen, while guarantee often gives a formal or written promise of outcome.
  • warrant
    6.5 2.19/m
    warrant formally promises quality or validity, often in writing or law, while guarantee can be an everyday promise or assurance.
  • insure
    3.0 1.10/m
    insure usually means to protect against loss with insurance policies, while guarantee means to promise or ensure something will happen.
