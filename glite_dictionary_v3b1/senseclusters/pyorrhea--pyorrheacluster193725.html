pyorrhea

noun
discharge of pus (esp. gums)
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪəˈriə /; / ˌpaɪəˈrioʊə /; / paɪəˈriə / (AmE), / ˌpaɪəˈriːə /; / ˌpʌɪəˈrɪə /; / ˌpaɪ.əˈriː.ə / (BrE)
a serious gum disease that causes pus to be discharged from the gums and can lead to loose teeth
