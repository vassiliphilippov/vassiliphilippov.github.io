aminoglycoside

noun
antibiotic for infections
7.7
Pronunciation: / əˌminoʊˈɡlaɪkəˌsaɪd / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəʊˈɡlaɪkəsaɪd / (BrE)
a type of antibiotic used to treat serious bacterial infections, especially those caused by Gram-negative bacteria
