dulse

noun
edible red seaweed
Pronunciation: / dʌls / (AmE), / dʌls / (BrE)
a coarse red seaweed that is edible and often used as food, especially in the North Atlantic region

Examples

Red dulse seaweed
Many coastal communities harvest dulse for use in soups and salads.
She sprinkled chopped dulse over her scrambled eggs for extra flavor.
The local market sells dried dulse as a salty snack.
