antievolution

adjective
opposed to evolution
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌiːvəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting the theory of evolution, especially in biology

Examples

Antievolution views
The school board faced criticism for its antievolution stance in the science curriculum.
Some groups continue to spread antievolution arguments despite scientific consensus.
The documentary explored the history of antievolution movements in the twentieth century.
