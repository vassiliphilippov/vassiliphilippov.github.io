acquiescent

adjective
compliant and unresisting
Pronunciation: / ˌækwiˈɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌakwɪˈɛsənt / (BrE)
willing to accept or comply with something without protest or resistance

Examples

An acquiescent attitude
Her acquiescent attitude made her popular with supervisors but frustrated her peers.
The citizens remained acquiescent despite the unpopular new policies.
Though he had his own opinions, he was often acquiescent in team decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assertive - having or showing a confident and forceful personality
  • synonym: compliant - willing to agree with others or obey rules
  • synonym: submissive - ready to conform to the authority or will of others
