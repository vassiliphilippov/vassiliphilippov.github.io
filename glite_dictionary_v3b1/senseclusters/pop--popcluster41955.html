pop

verb
make a quick, sharp, explosive noise
1.1
Pronunciation: / pɑp / (AmE), / pɒp / (BrE)
to make or cause to make a short, sharp, explosive sound, or to fire a weapon with such a sound

Examples

To pop bubble wrap
He gently popped the plastic bubble wrap with his fingers.
She loves hearing the corn pop in the microwave when making popcorn.
The balloon popped suddenly during the party.
As the parade passed, celebratory cannons popped loudly in the distance.
During the night, someone popped off a few shots in the alley.
The hunter popped his rifle twice before the birds scattered.

  • synonym: snap - make a sharp cracking sound
