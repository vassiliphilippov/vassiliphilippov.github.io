clove tree

noun
evergreen tree cultivated for cloves
Pronunciation: / kloʊv tri / (AmE), / kləʊv triː / (BrE)
A tropical evergreen tree with red flowers, cultivated for its flower buds, which are harvested as cloves used in cooking and medicine.

Examples

A clove tree flower
A mature clove tree can provide thousands of buds each harvest season.
Indonesia is one of the largest producers of the clove tree in the world.
The farmers planted a new clove tree to boost their spice production.
