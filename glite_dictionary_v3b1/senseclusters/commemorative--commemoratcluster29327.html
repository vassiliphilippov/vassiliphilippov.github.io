commemorative

noun
object made to mark an event or honor a person
5.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛmɚətɪv / (AmE), / kəˈmɛmərətɪv / (BrE)
An object, especially a coin or postage stamp, issued to mark an event or honor a person.

Examples

A commemorative coin
Collectors eagerly sought the limited-edition commemoratives released for the anniversary.
He displayed his prized commemorative on a shelf in his study.
The royal wedding was celebrated with a special commemorative issued by the postal service.
