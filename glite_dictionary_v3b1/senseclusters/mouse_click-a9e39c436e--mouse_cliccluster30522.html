mouse click

noun
pressing a button on a computer mouse
Pronunciation: / maʊs klɪk / (AmE), / maʊs klɪk / (BrE)
an act of pressing and releasing a button on a computer mouse

Examples

A mouse click action
A double mouse click is required to launch most programs.
She accidentally sent the email with a single mouse click.
To open the document, just make a mouse click on the icon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: click - a sudden, short, sharp sound or action, especially as made by a computer mouse
