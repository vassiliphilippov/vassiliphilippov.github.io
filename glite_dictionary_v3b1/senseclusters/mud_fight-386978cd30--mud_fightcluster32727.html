mud fight

noun
playful contest involving mud
Pronunciation: / mʌd faɪt / (AmE), / mʌd faɪt / (BrE)
a playful or informal physical contest in which participants throw or smear mud at each other

Examples

A mud fight game
A spontaneous mud fight broke out during the outdoor festival.
Everyone was covered in dirt by the end of the mud fight at summer camp.
The children started a mud fight after the heavy rain left the playground soaked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: food fight - a playful fight in which people throw food at each other
