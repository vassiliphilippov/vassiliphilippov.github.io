intermuscular

adjective
between muscles
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmʌskjələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmʌskjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring between muscles

Examples

An intermuscular cyst
The doctor found an intermuscular cyst during the examination.
An intermuscular injection is given between the muscles rather than inside one.
Swelling in the intermuscular space can indicate an underlying injury.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intramuscular - located or occurring within a muscle
