cycle of rebirth

noun
the process of repeated rebirth in new forms
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.kəl əv rɪˈbɝːθ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.kəl əv rɪˈbɜːθ / (BrE)
the process, especially in Hinduism and Buddhism, of being born into a new life form repeatedly after death, until achieving liberation or enlightenment

Examples

The cycle of rebirth
He believed that good actions in this life could affect his position in the next turn of the cycle of rebirth.
Many Eastern religions teach about the cycle of rebirth and the path to ultimate liberation.
The cycle of rebirth is central to many philosophical discussions on the meaning of existence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reincarnation - the belief that the soul is reborn in a new body after death
