undoubtable

adjective
impossible to doubt
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdaʊtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdaʊtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to doubt; certain or unquestionable

Examples

An undoubtable fact
His loyalty to the team is undoubtable after all these years.
The evidence presented in court was undoubtable and convinced everyone.
Her commitment to her work is undoubtable and inspires her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doubtful - not certain; open to question
  • synonym: indisputable - impossible to question or argue against
