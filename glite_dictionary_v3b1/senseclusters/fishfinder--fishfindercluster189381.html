fishfinder

noun
professional fishing guide
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌfaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌfaɪndə / (BrE)
a person who locates fish for others, especially as a professional fishing guide

Examples

Expert fishfinder
The fishfinder knew exactly where to take us for the biggest catch of the day.
Hiring a local fishfinder made our fishing trip much more successful.
Every morning, the experienced fishfinder led the group to new fishing spots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guide - a person who leads or shows the way to others
