comics

noun
comedians, performers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmɪks / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪks / (BrE)
informal: comedians or people who perform comedy, especially in clubs or on stage

Examples

Stand-up comics
The club was packed with people eager to see their favorite comics perform live.
Several well-known comics started their careers in small local venues.
She dreams of joining the group of comics who make audiences laugh every night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comedian - a person who entertains by telling jokes or performing comedy
  • synonym: performer - a person who entertains an audience
