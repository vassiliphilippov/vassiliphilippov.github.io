pampas grass

noun
tall ornamental grass with silvery plumes
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæmpəs ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈpampəs ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a tall perennial grass native to the pampas of South America, with showy silvery plumes and dense clumps, often grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

Dried pampas grass
Dried pampas grass is popular in modern floral arrangements.
The large pampas grass in their garden swayed gently in the wind.
They decided to plant pampas grass for its decorative plumes in the front yard.
