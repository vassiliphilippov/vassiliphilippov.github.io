mud puddle

noun
small pool of muddy water
5.6
Pronunciation: / mʌd ˈpʌd.əl / (AmE), / mʌd ˈpʌd.əl / (BrE)
A small pool of muddy water, especially on the ground after rain.

Examples

A mud puddle on ground
After the rainstorm, a mud puddle formed at the end of the driveway.
She accidentally stepped into a mud puddle and ruined her white socks.
The children splashed in the mud puddle until their shoes were covered in dirt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puddle - a small pool of liquid, especially rainwater on the ground
