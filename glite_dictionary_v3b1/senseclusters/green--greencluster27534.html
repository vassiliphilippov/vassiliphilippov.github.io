green

verb
to turn or become green
3.2
Pronunciation: / ɡriːn / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
(of plants, land, or objects) to turn or become green in color, especially with new growth or foliage.

Examples

Fields green after rain
After a few days of rain, the fields began to green quickly.
In the early spring, the lawn will green as new grass shoots appear.
The empty lot has started to green since the city planted wildflowers.
