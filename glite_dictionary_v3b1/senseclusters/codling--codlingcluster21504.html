codling

noun
young codfish
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑd.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒd.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a young codfish, especially one that is not yet fully grown

Examples

Young codling fish
A codling is not considered suitable for commercial sale until it grows larger.
Several codlings were caught in the shallow coastal waters during spring.
The fisherman was careful to release the codling back into the sea to let it mature.
