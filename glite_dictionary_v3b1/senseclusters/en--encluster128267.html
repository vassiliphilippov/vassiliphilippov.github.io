en

x
prefix forming verbs
Pronunciation: / ɛn / (AmE), / ɛn / (BrE)
a prefix added to nouns or adjectives to form verbs meaning to cause to be or to put into a state

Examples

En prefix verb
The prefix en- in 'encourage' means to give courage.
Adding en- to 'large' creates 'enlarge', meaning to make larger.
In 'enable', the en- prefix means to make able.

Related Terms

  • synonym: em - a variant of the prefix 'en-' used before labial sounds
