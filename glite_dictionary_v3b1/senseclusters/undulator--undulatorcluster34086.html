undulator

noun
wavy mover
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈʌndjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that moves in a wavy or oscillating manner

Examples

A smooth undulator
The snake was an expert undulator, gliding smoothly through the grass.
In physics, an undulator is used to produce synchrotron radiation.
The gymnast became a graceful undulator as she performed her ribbon routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oscillator - something that moves back and forth in a regular pattern
  • synonym: wave - a movement or shape that rises and falls like a wave
