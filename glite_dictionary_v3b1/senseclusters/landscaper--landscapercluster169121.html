landscaper

noun
a garden or landscape designer/worker
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌskeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)ˌskeɪpə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is to design, arrange, and maintain outdoor landscapes and gardens attractively

Examples

A landscaper designs gardens
Many homeowners consult a landscaper before starting garden renovations.
The landscaper suggested adding more native plants for easier maintenance.
We hired a landscaper to redesign our backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gardener - someone employed to take care of a garden
