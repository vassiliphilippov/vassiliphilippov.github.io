pay-station

noun
coin-operated public telephone
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A public telephone operated by inserting coins to make a call.

Examples

Public pay-station
Before the era of mobile phones, people relied on the nearby pay-station to make important calls.
He searched for loose change to use the pay-station at the train station.
The travelers found a pay-station in the lobby to call a taxi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: payphone - A public telephone requiring payment to use
