beechdrops

noun
parasitic plant on beech
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌdrɑps / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌdrɒps / (BrE)
a small parasitic plant that grows on the roots of beech trees, lacking chlorophyll and depending on the tree for nutrients

Examples

Small beechdrops plant
We found a cluster of beechdrops growing beneath the large beech tree in the forest.
The botanist explained that beechdrops rely entirely on beech trees for their nutrients.
During our hike, I noticed some unusual beechdrops near the tree roots along the trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broomrape - a group of parasitic plants that attach to the roots of other plants
