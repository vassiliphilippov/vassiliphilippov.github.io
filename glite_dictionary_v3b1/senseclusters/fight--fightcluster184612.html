Synonyms

  • combat
    3.5 37/m
    combat is a formal military fight between armed forces, while fight is a general struggle used in many settings.
  • struggle
    2.9 23/m
    struggle is a long or hard effort against an opponent or difficulty, while fight usually means a direct physical contest.
  • fray
    6.5 3.81/m
    fray is a noisy or chaotic fight involving several people, while fight can mean any fight or contest between people or groups.
  • confrontation
    4.5 18/m
    confrontation is a direct face-to-face conflict or showdown, while fight is any struggle that may be physical or verbal.
  • clash
    3.2 7.02/m
    clash is a brief violent meeting or strong disagreement between groups, while fight covers many kinds of contests or battles.
