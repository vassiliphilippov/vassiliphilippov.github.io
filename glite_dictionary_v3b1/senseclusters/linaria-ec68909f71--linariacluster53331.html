Linaria

noun
genus of flowering herbs and subshrubs
9.8
Pronunciation: / lɪˈnɛəriə / (AmE), / lɪˈnɑːriə /; / lɪˈneəriə / (BrE)
a group of plants (herbs and subshrubs) in the plantain family, also called toadflax, with showy, spurred flowers that look like snapdragons

Examples
