drop in the ocean

noun
very small, insignificant amount
4.8
Pronunciation: / drɑp ɪn ði ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / drɒp ɪn ði ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
a very small or insignificant amount compared to what is needed or to a much larger whole

Examples

His efforts felt like a drop in the ocean in solving climate change.
The company's donation was a drop in the ocean compared to the total amount required.
The extra staff made only a drop in the ocean difference to the workload.
