instanter

adverb
immediately (legal)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstæntər / (AmE), / ɪnˈstæntə / (BrE)
immediately or without delay, especially as an instruction in legal contexts

Examples

To act instanter
The court ordered the defendant to appear instanter to address the urgent matter.
The lawyer requested that the documents be filed instanter to avoid further delay.
The judge demanded compliance instanter after hearing the arguments from both sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forthwith - immediately; without delay, often in legal contexts
  • synonym: immediately - without any delay; at once
  • antonym: later - at a time in the future; not now
