Gidar language

noun
Chadic language of northern Cameroon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡiːdɑr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡiːdɑː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, particularly in northern Cameroon, including areas such as the North Region and the Far North Region.
