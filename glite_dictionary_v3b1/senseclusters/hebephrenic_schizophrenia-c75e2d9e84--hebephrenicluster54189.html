hebephrenic schizophrenia

noun
disorganized subtype of schizophrenia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːbəˌfrɛnɪk skɪtsəˈfriːniə / (AmE), / ˌhiːbəˌfrɛnɪk skɪtsəˈfriːnɪə / (BrE)
a form of schizophrenia marked by disorganized thoughts, childish behavior, and inappropriate emotional responses, typically emerging during adolescence
