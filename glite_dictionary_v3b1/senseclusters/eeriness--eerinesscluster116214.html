eeriness

noun
strange and fear-inspiring quality
Pronunciation: / ˈɪri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɪə.ri.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being strange and mysterious in a way that inspires fear or unease

Examples

The eeriness of silence
She couldn't shake the eeriness she felt while walking through the silent forest.
The thick fog lent an eeriness to the deserted street.
There was a distinct eeriness in the abandoned house after midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eerie - strange and frightening
