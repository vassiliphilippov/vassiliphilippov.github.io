LWOP

noun
unpaid work absence
8.2
Pronunciation: /el ˈdʌb.əl.juː oʊ piː/ (AmE), /el ˈdʌb.əl.juː əʊ piː/ (BrE)
a period when an employee is absent from work without receiving pay

Examples

On LWOP after surgery
After her surgery, she had to take LWOP because she had no paid leave left.
Employees must submit a formal request to be granted LWOP for personal reasons.
He used LWOP to travel abroad for several months without losing his job.
