White River

proper noun
tributary of the Mississippi River in Missouri and Arkansas
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪt ˌrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt ˌrɪvə / (BrE)
a tributary of the Mississippi River that flows southeast through southern Missouri and northern Arkansas in the United States
