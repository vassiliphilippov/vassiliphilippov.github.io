long pillow

noun
a long, narrow bed pillow
2.3
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈpɪloʊ / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈpɪləʊ / (BrE)
a long, narrow pillow that is placed across a bed, often underneath the regular pillows for extra support or decoration

Examples

A long pillow on bed
After buying a new long pillow, he found it much easier to relax and read in bed.
She placed a long pillow under her knees for extra comfort while sleeping.
The bed was neatly arranged with a long pillow at the head and several cushions on top.
