bracket

verb
enclose in brackets (verb)
Pronunciation: / ˈbræk.ɪt /; / ˈbrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈbræk.ɪt /; / ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
to enclose (a word, phrase, or remark) in brackets, typically in written or printed text

Examples

To bracket a word

She is currently bracketing the citation to indicate an editorial insertion.
