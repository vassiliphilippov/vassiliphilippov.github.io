laxity

noun
lack of strictness or care
Pronunciation: / ˈlæk.sə.ti / (AmE), / ˈlæk.sɪ.ti / (BrE)
A lack of strictness or care; the quality of being careless and neglectful.

Examples

Regulatory laxity
Regulatory laxity contributed to the financial crisis.
The school was criticized for its laxity in enforcing safety protocols.
Under her leadership, laxity was replaced by discipline and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carelessness - lack of attention or care
  • synonym: negligence - failure to take proper care
  • antonym: strictness - the quality of being strict or precise
