raja

noun
an Indian king or prince
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːdʒə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdʒə / (BrE)
A king or prince in India, especially one ruling a territory or state.

Examples

The raja of India
During the festival, the raja rode through the city on a decorated elephant.
Historians have studied the lifestyles of several famous rajas from the Mughal era.
The ancient raja ruled his kingdom with justice and wisdom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: king - a male monarch; general term for a male ruler
  • degree higher: maharaja - a great king or high-ranking ruler in India
  • antonym: queen - a female ruler or the wife of a raja or king
