pouf

noun
thick cushion used as a seat or footstool
6.6 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / puÀêf /; / puf / (AmE), / puÀêf / (BrE)
A thick, low cushion that is used as a seat or footstool.

Examples

A soft pouf
He propped his feet up on the soft pouf after a long day.
She purchased a colorful pouf for the corner of her living room.
The children sat comfortably on the large pouf while playing games.
He rested his feet on the pouffe after a long day at work.
She sat on the pouffe in the corner while she tied her shoes.
The living room was decorated with a large rug and a blue pouffe.

Synonyms

  • tuffet
    9.9 0.01/m
    A tuffet is a small low footstool mainly for resting feet, while a pouf is a thicker cushion used for sitting or as a footrest.
  • hassock
    8.6 0.21/m
    A hassock is a firm cushion often used for kneeling or resting feet, while a pouf is a low, soft cushion used as a seat.
  • ottoman
    4.7 0.75/m
    An ottoman is an upholstered bench that often contains storage and serves as a seat, while a pouf is a simple low cushion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ottoman - a padded, upholstered seat or bench without a back or arms, often used as a footstool
