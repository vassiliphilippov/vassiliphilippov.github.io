reconcile

verb
accept something unpleasant as inevitable
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkənˌsaɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɛkənsaɪl / (BrE)
to accept or come to terms with something unpleasant or inevitable

Examples

To reconcile yourself
After a long struggle, she finally reconciled herself to the loss of her job.
He had to reconcile himself with the fact that things might never change.
Over time, they gradually reconciled themselves to living in a new country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accept - to consent to receive or undertake something offered
  • synonym: resign oneself (to) - to accept something unpleasant that cannot be changed
