crepuscular

adjective
active at twilight
Pronunciation: / krɪˈpʌskjələr / (AmE), / krɪˈpʌskjʊlə / (BrE)
active or occurring during twilight, especially at dawn or dusk

Examples

Crepuscular animals
Many bats are crepuscular, coming out to feed at dusk and dawn.
The crepuscular light made the landscape look mysterious and calm.
Deer often show crepuscular behavior, being most active at sunrise and sunset.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diurnal - active during the day
  • antonym: nocturnal - active during the night
