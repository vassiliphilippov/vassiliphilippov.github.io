computer science

noun
study of computers and computing
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpjuːtər ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
The study of computers and computational systems, encompassing their theory, design, development, and application.

Examples

Computer science study
Advancements in computer science have transformed the way we live and work.
He chose to major in computer science at university due to his interest in technology.
The field of computer science includes topics such as artificial intelligence and cybersecurity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informatics - the study of information processing, often as a synonym or branch of computer science
  • degree lower: software engineering - the discipline of designing and developing software systems
