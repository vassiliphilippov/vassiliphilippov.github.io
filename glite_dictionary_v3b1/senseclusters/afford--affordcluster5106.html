afford

verb
to provide or supply
3.0 13/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàf…îrd / (AmE), / …ôÀàf…îÀêd / (BrE)
to provide or supply (a result, opportunity, or resource); to yield or offer

Examples

Afford an opportunity
His generosity afforded us a chance to travel abroad.
The new technology affords an opportunity for quicker communication.
This window affords a beautiful view of the mountains.

Synonyms

  • yield
    3.6 28/m
    yield means to produce or bring forth a result or product, while afford means to offer or supply opportunities or resources.
  • give
    1.0 371/m
    give causes or supplies something like a result, feeling, or object, while afford means to provide or offer resources or chances.
  • provide
    1.9 98/m
    provide often means to earn or supply money and basic needs for people, while afford means to offer a result, opportunity, or resource.

  • synonym: provide - to make available or supply
