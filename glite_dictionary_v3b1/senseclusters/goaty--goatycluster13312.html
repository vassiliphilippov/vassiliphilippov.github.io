goaty

adjective
with a goatee
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊti / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊti / (BrE)
having a small, pointed beard resembling a goatee.

Examples

Goaty beard
He looked quite goaty after growing a small beard for the first time.
The actor's goaty appearance made him stand out in the crowd.
With his new goaty style, he received many compliments on his facial hair.
