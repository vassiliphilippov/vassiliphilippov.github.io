social worker

noun
person providing social assistance
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˌwɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to help people in need or at social disadvantage, often by offering support, advice, or practical assistance

Examples

A social worker helps people
Many social workers advocate for better resources in their communities.
The social worker visited the family to offer support after the flood.
Working as a social worker can be both challenging and rewarding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caseworker - a social worker who is employed to visit and assist individual clients
