bosky

adjective
covered with bushes or trees
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːski / (AmE), / ˈbɒski / (BrE)
(literary) Covered with bushes or trees; wooded or leafy.

Examples

A bosky grove
The old estate was surrounded by a bosky grove that teemed with birds.
The painting depicted a bosky hillside, dense with wild shrubs and foliage.
They strolled through a bosky park, enjoying the cool shade of the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bushy - full of or resembling bushes
  • synonym: wooded - covered with trees, forested
