dobe

noun
Doberman pinscher
Pronunciation: / doʊb / (AmE), / dəʊb / (BrE)
an informal term for a Doberman pinscher, a medium-to-large breed of dog known for its sleek build and alertness

Examples

Dobe dog
The police officer trained his dobe to assist in search and rescue missions.
At the dog park, a playful dobe chased after a frisbee with incredible speed.
Her family adopted a gentle dobe from the local animal shelter last year.
