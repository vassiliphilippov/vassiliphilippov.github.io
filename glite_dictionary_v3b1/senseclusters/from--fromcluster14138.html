from

preposition
indicating difference
Pronunciation: / frʌm / (AmE), / frɒm / (BrE)
used to indicate a difference or distinction between two or more things or people

Examples

Different from others
Her style is very different from her sister's, especially in the way she dresses.
This painting stands out from the others because of its bright colors.
The new policy is distinct from the previous one in several important ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: than - used to introduce the second element in a comparison
