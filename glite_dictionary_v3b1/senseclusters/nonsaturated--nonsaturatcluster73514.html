nonsaturated

adjective
not fully saturated
7.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈsæt.ʃə.reɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈsæt.ʃə.reɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
in chemistry, describing a compound that contains one or more double or triple bonds and is not fully saturated with hydrogen atoms

Examples

Nonsaturated fatty acid
A nonsaturated fatty acid has at least one double bond in its carbon chain.
The chemist explained that nonsaturated hydrocarbons are more reactive than saturated ones.
Olive oil is rich in nonsaturated fats, which are considered healthier than saturated fats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: saturated - containing only single bonds and fully saturated with hydrogen
  • synonym: unsaturated - containing double or triple bonds and not fully saturated with hydrogen
