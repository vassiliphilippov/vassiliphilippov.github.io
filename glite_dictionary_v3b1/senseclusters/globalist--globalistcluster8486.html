globalist

noun
supporter of global cooperation
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈgloʊ.bəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈgləʊ.bəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports international cooperation, integration, or global governance

Examples

A globalist leader
The globalist argued that international trade benefits all countries involved.

As a proud globalist, she supports open borders and international trade.

The term globalist is often used in political debates to criticize those who favor global cooperation.
He was called a globalist because of his work with international organizations.
