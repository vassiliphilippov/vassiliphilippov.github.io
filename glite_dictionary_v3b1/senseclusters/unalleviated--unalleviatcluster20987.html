unalleviated

adjective
not relieved
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈliːvieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈliːvieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not made less severe, intense, or painful; not relieved or eased

Examples

Unalleviated pain
Her anxiety remained unalleviated even after talking to her friends.
The suffering of the refugees was unalleviated by the lack of medical supplies.
Despite several attempts, the pressure on the team was unalleviated throughout the project.
