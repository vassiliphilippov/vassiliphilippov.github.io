flinter

noun
small flint chip
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪntər / (AmE), / ˈflɪntə / (BrE)
a small, sharp chip or flake of flint, often produced when flint is struck or worked.

Examples

A sharp flinter
He cut his finger on a sharp flinter that had fallen from the tool.
Archaeologists found a flinter among the ancient stone tools at the site.
Be careful when handling the rocks, as a flinter can easily pierce your skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flake - a small, thin piece that has broken off from something
  • synonym: splinter - a small, sharp fragment broken off from a larger object
