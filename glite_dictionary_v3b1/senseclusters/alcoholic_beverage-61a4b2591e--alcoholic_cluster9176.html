alcoholic beverage

noun
a drink containing alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.kəˈhɑ.lɪk ˈbɛv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌæl.kəˈhɒ.lɪk ˈbɛv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
A drink that contains alcohol, such as beer, wine, or spirits.
