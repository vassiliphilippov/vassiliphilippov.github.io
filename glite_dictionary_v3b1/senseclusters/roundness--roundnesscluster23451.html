roundness

noun
fullness and richness of vocal tone
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of a tone of voice that is full, rich, and smooth

Examples

Roundness of the voice
A singer's roundness in tone can make a performance truly memorable.
He admired the roundness in her spoken words during the poetry reading.
The roundness of her voice captivated the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flatness - lack of resonance or fullness in sound
  • synonym: richness - fullness and pleasing quality of sound, especially in music or speech
