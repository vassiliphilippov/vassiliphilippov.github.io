unsporting

adjective
not fair or sportsmanlike
1.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
Not behaving in a fair or sportsmanlike manner; violating accepted standards or rules.

Examples

Unsporting behaviour
Cheating is considered an unsporting way to try to win a competition.
His unsporting conduct disappointed both his teammates and the fans.
The referee penalized the player for an unsporting tackle during the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sportsmanlike - showing fairness and respect in sport or competition
  • synonym: unsportsmanlike - not showing fairness or respect in sport or competition
