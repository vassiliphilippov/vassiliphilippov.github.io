truffle

verb
to add truffles
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌfəl / (BrE)
to add truffles or truffle flavor to a dish, often by shaving or mixing them in

Examples

To truffle a dish
The chef truffled the pasta with thin slices of white truffle for a luxurious flavor.
At the restaurant, they often serve eggs that have been truffled for a rich aroma.
She truffles her homemade butter to give it a unique and earthy taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garnish - to decorate or add finishing touches to a dish
  • synonym: season - to add flavoring or spices to food
