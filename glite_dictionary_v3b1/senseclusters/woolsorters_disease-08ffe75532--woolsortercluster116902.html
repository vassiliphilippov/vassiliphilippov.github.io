woolsorter's disease

noun
infection from inhaling anthrax spores, severe respiratory illness
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊl.sɑːr.tərz dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /ˈwʊl.sɔːr.tərz dɪ.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /ˈwʊl.sɔː.təz dɪ.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
