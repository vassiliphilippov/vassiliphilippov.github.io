acatalectic

adjective
verse line with full syllables
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.kə.təˈlɛk.tɪk /; / ˌækəˌtəˈlɛktɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.kə.təˈlɛk.tɪk /; / ˌakəˈtalɛktɪk / (BrE)
(of a line of verse) having the full number of syllables as required by the meter; metrically complete

Examples

Unlike a catalectic verse, an acatalectic line includes the full number of feet.
