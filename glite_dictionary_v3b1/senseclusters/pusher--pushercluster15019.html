pusher

noun
an intrusive or excessively pushy person
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃər / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃə / (BrE)
a person who pushes themself forward in a forceful or intrusive way

Examples

An idea pusher
He earned a reputation as a pusher for always forcing his ideas onto the team.
Her aggressive style made her seem like a pusher at social events.
Nobody likes a pusher in the workplace who constantly interrupts others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busybody - a person who meddles in others' affairs
  • synonym: intruder - someone who enters a place or situation where they are not wanted
