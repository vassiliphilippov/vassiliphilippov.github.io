shed

verb
cast off hair, skin, or feathers
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ʃɛd / (AmE), / ʃɛd / (BrE)
(of an animal) to cast off hair, skin, feathers, or horns, usually as part of a natural process.

Examples

Snakes shed skin
Most dogs shed a lot of hair during the warmer months.
Snakes shed their skin several times a year as they grow.
The tree sheds its leaves every autumn.
