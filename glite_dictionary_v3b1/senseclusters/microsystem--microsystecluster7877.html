microsystem

noun
miniature device assembly
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a very small mechanical or electronic device or assembly, often created by microfabrication, that functions as a sensor, actuator, or component in larger systems

Examples

Sensor microsystem
The research team designed a microsystem to detect changes in temperature inside the spacecraft.
Advances in technology have made it possible to produce a microsystem that fits on the tip of a finger.
A microsystem can be used as a sensor in medical devices to monitor patient health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: MEMS - microelectromechanical systems; tiny integrated devices or systems
  • degree lower: nanodevice - an extremely small device built at the nanometer scale
