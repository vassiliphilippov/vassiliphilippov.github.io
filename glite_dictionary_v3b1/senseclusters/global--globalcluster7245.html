Synonyms

  • planetwide
    3.7 0.07/m
    planetwide describes effects that cover the whole planet, often physical or environmental, while global is used more broadly for world matters.
  • planetary
    4.7 3.09/m
    planetary often appears in scientific or space contexts about planets, while global usually means things affecting the whole Earth or world society.
