Saint Christopher

proper noun
Christian patron saint of travelers
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfər / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfə / (BrE)
A Christian martyr of the 3rd century, venerated as the patron saint of travelers.

Examples

Saint Christopher medal
Legend says that Saint Christopher once carried a child, who was Christ in disguise, across a dangerous river.
Many people wear a Saint Christopher medal for protection during their journeys.
Travelers often invoke Saint Christopher for safety before setting out on long trips.
