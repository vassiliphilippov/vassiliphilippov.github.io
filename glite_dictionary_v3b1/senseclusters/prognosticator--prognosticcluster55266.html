prognosticator

noun
person who predicts the future
Pronunciation: / prɑɡˈnɑːstɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / prɒɡˈnɒstɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who predicts or forecasts future events, often based on special knowledge or insight

Examples

A prognosticator predicts events
Many consider him the leading prognosticator in the world of finance.
Superstitious crowds often sought the advice of a local prognosticator before making important decisions.
The prognosticator predicted a harsh winter based on long-term climate patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forecaster - someone who predicts what will happen, especially in weather or business
  • synonym: predictor - someone who predicts future events
  • synonym: soothsayer - a person who is supposed to foresee the future
