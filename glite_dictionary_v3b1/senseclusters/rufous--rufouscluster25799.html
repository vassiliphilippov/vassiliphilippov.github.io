rufous

adjective
reddish-brown color
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈruːfəs / (AmE), / ˈruːfəs / (BrE)
having a reddish-brown color, often used to describe the fur, feathers, or leaves of animals and plants

Examples

Rufous feathers
The rufous tail of the fox helped it blend into the autumn leaves.
We spotted a rufous hummingbird feeding on the bright flowers in the garden.
The tree's rufous bark stood out against the green forest background.
