siloing

noun
storing in a silo
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of storing harvested crops, silage, or other bulk materials in a silo

Examples

Siloing of grain
The siloing of grain ensures it stays dry and safe throughout the winter months.
Modern farms use advanced equipment for efficient siloing of their crops after harvest.
Proper siloing can prevent spoilage and loss of valuable feed for livestock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harvesting - the process of gathering mature crops from the fields
  • synonym: storage - the act of keeping something in a place for future use
