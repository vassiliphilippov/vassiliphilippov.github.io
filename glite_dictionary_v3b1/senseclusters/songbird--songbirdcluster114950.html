songbird

noun
a bird with a melodious call
7.5 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɔːŋ.bɜːrd/ /; / /ˈsɑːŋ.bɜːrd/ (AmE), /ˈsɒŋ.bɜːd/ (BrE)
