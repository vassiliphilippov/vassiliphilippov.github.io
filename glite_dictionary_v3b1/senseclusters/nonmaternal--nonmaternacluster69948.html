nonmaternal

adjective
not motherly
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnməˈtɜrnəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnməˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)
not having or showing the typical feelings, behavior, or qualities associated with a mother

Examples

Nonmaternal feelings

Some people are naturally nonmaternal and prefer not to take on caregiving roles.
