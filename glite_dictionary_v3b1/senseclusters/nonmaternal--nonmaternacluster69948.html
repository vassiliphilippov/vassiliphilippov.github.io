nonmaternal

adjective
not motherly
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnməˈtɜrnəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnməˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)
not having or showing the typical feelings, behavior, or qualities associated with a mother

Examples

Nonmaternal feelings
Her nonmaternal response to the child's needs surprised her friends.
The teacher's nonmaternal approach focused more on discipline than on comfort.
Some people are naturally nonmaternal and prefer not to take on caregiving roles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maternal - having the qualities of a mother; motherly
  • degree lower: paternal - relating to or characteristic of a father
