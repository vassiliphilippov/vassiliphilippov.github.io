spontaneousness

noun
the quality of being spontaneous
5.1
Pronunciation: / spɑnˈteɪniəsnəs / (AmE), / spɒnˈteɪniəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being spontaneous and acting from natural feelings without constraint

Examples

The spontaneousness of actions
Her spontaneousness made even the most mundane outings feel like adventures.
I admire the spontaneousness with which children express their emotions.
The spontaneousness in his remarks made the speech memorable and engaging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spontaneity - the quality of being spontaneous and natural
