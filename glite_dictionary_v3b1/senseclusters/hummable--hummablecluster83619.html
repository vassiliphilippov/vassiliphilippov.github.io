hummable

adjective
easy to hum
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌməbəl / (AmE), / ˈhʌməbəl / (BrE)
easy to hum or having a simple, catchy melody that can be easily repeated by humming

Examples

A hummable tune
The movie's theme song is so hummable that everyone leaves the theater singing it.
She prefers hummable tunes that are easy to remember and sing along to.
Many pop songs are designed to be hummable and stick in your head all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catchy - easy to remember and pleasant to listen to
  • antonym: complex - difficult to understand or perform
  • synonym: melodic - having a pleasant and tuneful melody
