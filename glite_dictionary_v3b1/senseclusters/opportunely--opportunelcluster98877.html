opportunely

adverb
at a suitable or convenient time
Pronunciation: / əˈpɚ.tuːn.li / (AmE), / ɒˈpɔː.tʃuːn.li / (BrE)
At a suitable or convenient time; in a way that is well-timed or advantageous for a particular purpose.

Examples

Arrived opportunely late

The courier delivered the package opportunely, just before the office closed for the weekend.
The rain stopped opportunely, allowing the parade to continue as planned.
