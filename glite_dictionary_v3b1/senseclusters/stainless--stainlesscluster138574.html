stainless

adjective
not stained or tarnished
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnləs / (BrE)
not marked, stained, rusted, or tarnished; remaining clean and shiny

Examples

Stainless steel
The stainless countertop made the kitchen look modern and bright.
Even after years of heavy use, the tools remained stainless and shiny.
She preferred stainless appliances because they were easy to keep clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spotless - completely clean and free from any marks
  • antonym: tarnished - dull or stained, especially by exposure to air or moisture
