oxhead

noun
ox's head or symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌhɛd / (BrE)
the head of an ox, or a symbol or design representing an ox's head

Examples

An oxhead design

The pottery featured a painted oxhead as a decorative motif.
