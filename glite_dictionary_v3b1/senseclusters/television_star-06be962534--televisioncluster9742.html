television star

noun
a famous person in television shows
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən stɑr / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən stɑː / (BrE)
A famous actor, actress, or personality known for appearing regularly on television shows.
