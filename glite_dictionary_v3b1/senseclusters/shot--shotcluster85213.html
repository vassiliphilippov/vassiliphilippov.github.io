shot

noun
critical or mocking remark
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt / (AmE), / ʃɒt / (BrE)
an aggressive or critical remark aimed at a person and intended to hurt or criticize them

Examples

Take a shot at him
Every meeting, someone takes a shot at the new manager.
His parting shot left everyone in silence.
She couldn't resist making a shot about his poor cooking skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dig - a remark intended to criticize or mock someone
  • synonym: jab - a quick, sharp, critical remark at someone
