wine waiter

noun
waiter responsible for serving wine
Pronunciation: / waɪn ˈweɪtər / (AmE), / waɪn ˈweɪtə / (BrE)
a waiter in a hotel or restaurant whose responsibility is to manage and serve wine to guests

Examples

A wine waiter serves wine
At upscale restaurants, a wine waiter can enhance the dining experience with expert advice.
She asked the wine waiter for a suggestion that would complement her fish dish.
The wine waiter recommended a French red to pair with our meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sommelier - a trained and knowledgeable wine professional, typically working in fine restaurants
