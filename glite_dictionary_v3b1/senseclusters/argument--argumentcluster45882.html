argument

noun
contentious exchange or dispute
2.3 30/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀêr…°j…ôm…ônt / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀê…°j äm…ônt / (BrE)
A conversation or exchange in which people express strong disagreement, often in an angry or heated way.

Examples

A heated argument
A minor misunderstanding turned into a serious argument during the meeting.
Their argument grew so loud that the neighbors could hear them through the walls.
We had an argument about which movie to watch, but we eventually compromised.
