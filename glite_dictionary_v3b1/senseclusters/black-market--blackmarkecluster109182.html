black-market

adjective
illegally distributed or sold
Pronunciation: / ˌblækˈmɑrkət / (AmE), / ˌblækˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
Illegally distributed, sold, or traded; not authorized by law.

Examples

Black-market goods
Authorities confiscated several black-market medicines during the raid.
He bought a black-market phone that was imported without proper authorization.
The artist became famous for his black-market paintings, which were sold secretly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegal - not allowed by law
  • antonym: lawful - allowed by law
