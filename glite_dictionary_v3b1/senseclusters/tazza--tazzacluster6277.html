tazza

noun
shallow decorative cup
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːtsə / (AmE), / ˈtætsə / (BrE)
a shallow, wide cup or bowl, often on a stem, used for drinking or as a decorative object

Examples

A tazza cup
The museum displayed a Renaissance tazza made of silver.
She placed fruit in a crystal tazza on the dining table for decoration.
Collectors often seek antique tazzas for their unique designs and craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chalice - a large cup or goblet, typically used for drinking wine
  • synonym: goblet - a drinking glass with a stem and base
  • antonym: mug - a large cup, typically cylindrical and without a stem
