tonsillotomy

noun
partial tonsil removal
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːnsɪˈlɑːtəmi / (AmE), / ˌtɒnsɪˈlɒtəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which part of a tonsil is removed

Examples

Tonsillotomy surgery
The surgeon performed a tonsillotomy to relieve the child's breathing problems.
A tonsillotomy is sometimes preferred over a full tonsillectomy for less severe cases.
After the tonsillotomy, the patient recovered quickly and experienced less pain.
