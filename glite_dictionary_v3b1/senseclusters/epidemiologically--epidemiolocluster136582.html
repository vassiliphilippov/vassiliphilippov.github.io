epidemiologically

adverb
in disease study terms
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the study of how diseases spread and affect populations

Examples

To be epidemiologically linked
The two outbreaks are epidemiologically linked according to the health report.
Researchers analyzed the data epidemiologically to understand the spread of the virus.
The disease was epidemiologically traced back to a single source in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clinically - in a way that relates to the observation and treatment of actual patients
  • synonym: statistically - in a way that relates to statistics or data analysis
