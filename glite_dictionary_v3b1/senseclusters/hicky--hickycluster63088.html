hicky

noun
unsophisticated rural person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪki / (AmE), / ˈhɪki / (BrE)
an informal term for a person from a rural area who is considered unsophisticated or provincial

Examples

A hicky farmer
They laughed at him for being a hicky, but he knew more about farming than anyone.
Despite being called a hicky, she adapted quickly to city life.
The movie portrayed the main character as a lovable hicky from a small town.
