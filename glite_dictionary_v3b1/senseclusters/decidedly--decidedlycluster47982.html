decidedly

adverb
without doubt; unquestionably
5.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.dɪd.liː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.dəd.liː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.dɪd.liː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.dəd.liː/ (BrE)
