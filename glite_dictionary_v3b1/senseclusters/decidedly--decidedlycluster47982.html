decidedly

adverb
without doubt; unquestionably
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪdɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪdɪdli / (BrE)
Without doubt; in a way that leaves no room for uncertainty.

Examples

She was decidedly unhappy
She was decidedly unhappy with the results of the test.
The movie was decidedly better than the last one we saw.
The weather was decidedly colder than we expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certainly - without any doubt; definitely
  • antonym: doubtfully - in a way that is uncertain or questionable
