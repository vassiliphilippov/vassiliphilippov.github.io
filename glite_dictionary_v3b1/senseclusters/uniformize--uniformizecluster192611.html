uniformize

verb
make uniform or consistent
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈjunəfərˌmaɪz /; / ˈjuː.nɪ.fər.maɪz / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪfəˌmaɪz /; / ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔː.maɪz / (BrE)
to make uniform or consistent in form, character, or operation

Examples

To uniformize product labels
Efforts to uniformize the product labels have improved customer understanding.
Scientists worked to uniformize the measurements before analyzing the data.
The new policy aims to uniformize graduation requirements across all departments.
Government policies aim to uniformise health and safety regulations in the industry.
The company decided to uniformise its branding across all markets.
They hope to uniformise the quality of their products through new procedures.

  • synonym: standardize - to make something conform to a standard or norm
