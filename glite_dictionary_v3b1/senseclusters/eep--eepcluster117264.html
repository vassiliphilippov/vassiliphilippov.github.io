eep

noun
surprised squeak
9.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / iÀêp / (AmE), / iÀêp / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched sound made when someone is surprised or scared

Examples

A quiet eep
She let out a quiet eep when the lights went out.
He gave a sudden eep as the cat jumped onto his lap.
The mouse made a tiny eep and scurried away.

Synonyms

    9.6 0.23/m
