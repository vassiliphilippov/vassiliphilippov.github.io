classman

noun
member of a social class
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsmən / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsmən / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a particular social or economic class

Examples

Ambitious classman
In the novel, the ambitious classman worked hard to improve his family's status.
As a proud classman, he often attended exclusive social gatherings.
The story follows a young classman navigating the challenges of a rigid social hierarchy.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: aristocrat - a member of the highest social class
  • synonym: classmate - a fellow member of the same class in school or college
  • degree lower: commoner - a person who is not a member of the nobility or upper class
