discharger

noun
person who dismisses
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒər / (AmE), / dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a person who dismisses or removes someone from a job, office, or duty

Examples

Discharger of staff
As the discharger, she had to inform several employees that their contracts would not be renewed.
The role of discharger is often difficult because it involves making tough personnel decisions.
After the investigation, the discharger officially removed the officer from duty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissal - the act of removing someone from a job or position
  • synonym: employer - a person or organization that hires and may dismiss workers
  • antonym: hirer - a person who gives someone a job
