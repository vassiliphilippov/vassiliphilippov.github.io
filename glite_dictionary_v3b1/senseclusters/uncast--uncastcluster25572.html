uncast

adjective
without a medical cast
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkæst / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɑːst / (BrE)
not wearing or enclosed in a medical cast after an injury or treatment

Examples

An uncast arm
After the doctor checked his progress, he was allowed to keep his arm uncast for the rest of recovery.
The nurse explained that some fractures can heal safely while remaining uncast if properly supported.
She was relieved to see her leg uncast and free from the heavy plaster.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casted - having a medical cast applied
