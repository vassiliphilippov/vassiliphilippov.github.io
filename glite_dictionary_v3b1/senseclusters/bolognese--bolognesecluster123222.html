bolognese

noun
meat pasta sauce
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊləˈneɪz / (AmE), / ˌbɒləˈneɪz / (BrE)
a pasta dish or sauce made with ground meat, tomatoes, and herbs, often served with pasta

Examples

Spaghetti bolognese
She made a delicious bolognese for dinner, simmering the sauce for hours.
The restaurant is famous for its homemade bolognese served over fresh tagliatelle.
He ordered a plate of bolognese and a glass of red wine at the Italian bistro.
He simmered the bolognese for hours to bring out the flavors before serving it with spaghetti.
Vegetarians often make a meatless version of bolognese using lentils or mushrooms.
The restaurant is famous for its homemade pasta topped with a hearty bolognese.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marinara - a tomato-based pasta sauce without meat
  • synonym: ragù - an Italian meat-based sauce for pasta
