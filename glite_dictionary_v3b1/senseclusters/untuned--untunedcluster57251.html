untuned

adjective
not adjusted
6.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtuːnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtjuːnd / (BrE)
not adjusted to a specific frequency, channel, or condition, especially in radios, antennas, or electronic circuits

Examples

Untuned radio
The radio produced static because it was untuned to any station.
An untuned antenna can result in poor signal reception for your television.
Engineers quickly fixed the untuned circuit to restore proper communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tuned - adjusted to a particular frequency or condition
  • synonym: unadjusted - not changed to fit a particular requirement
