Luciferian

noun
follower of Bishop Lucifer
9.9
Pronunciation: /luː.sɪ.ˈfɪr.ɪ.ən/ /; / /lə.sɪ.ˈfɪr.ɪ.ən/ (AmE), /luː.sɪ.ˈfɪə.rɪ.ən/ /; / /lə.sɪ.ˈfɪə.rɪ.ən/ (BrE)
a member of a historical Christian group that followed Bishop Lucifer of Cagliari in the fourth century, sometimes considered heretical

Examples

Luciferian sect
The council debated whether the Luciferian movement should be condemned as heresy.
Historical records mention a Luciferian community active in Sardinia during the fourth century.
A Luciferian was exiled for refusing to accept the church's new doctrine.
