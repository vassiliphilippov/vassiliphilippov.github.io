senga root

noun
North American medicinal plant
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛŋɡə ruːt / (AmE), / ˈsɛŋɡə ruːt / (BrE)
An eastern North American plant that produces a terminal cluster of small white flowers and has roots used in medicine.

Examples

The senga root plant
Many people in the region harvest senga roots in the spring.
The senga root is recognized for its small white flowers and medicinal properties.
Traditional healers sometimes use senga root to prepare herbal remedies.
