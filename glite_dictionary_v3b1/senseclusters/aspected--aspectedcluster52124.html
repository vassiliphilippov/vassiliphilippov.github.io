aspected

adjective
astrological relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˈæspɛktɪd / (BrE)
in astrology, having a specific angular relationship with another planet or celestial body

Examples

Aspected by Mars
A planet is considered strongly aspected if it forms several major angles with others.
The astrologer explained that her Sun was positively aspected by Jupiter.
When a planet is poorly aspected, it may indicate challenges in that area of life.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: afflicted - negatively influenced by an astrological aspect
  • antonym: unafflicted - not influenced by any astrological aspect
