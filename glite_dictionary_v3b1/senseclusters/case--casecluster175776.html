case

noun
statement in support of an argument
2.0
Pronunciation: / keɪs / (AmE), / keɪs / (BrE)
a statement of facts and reasons put forward to support an argument or point of view

Examples

A case for change
She made a strong case for investing in renewable energy.
The lawyer presented his case before the judge.
You need to state your case clearly if you want them to listen.
