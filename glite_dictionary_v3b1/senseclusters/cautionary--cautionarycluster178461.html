Examples

A cautionary tale
Her failure served as a cautionary tale for her classmates.
The film ended with a cautionary message about the dangers of texting while driving.
The teacher gave a cautionary lecture about the consequences of plagiarism.
A cautionary symbol was placed on the fence to deter trespassers from entering the property.

Synonyms

  • monitory
    9.6 0.00/m
    monitory is used to give clear formal warnings in notices, while cautionary warns to prevent danger or bad outcomes.
  • admonitory
    8.3 0.02/m
    admonitory sounds like a rebuke from a person, while cautionary signals danger to help people avoid harm.
  • warning
    1.0 0.00/m
    warning is a direct note or sign that something is dangerous, while cautionary gives a general warning to prevent harm.
