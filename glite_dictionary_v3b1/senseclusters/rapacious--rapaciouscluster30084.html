rapacious

adjective
having a very strong appetite for food
Pronunciation: / rəˈpeɪʃəs / (AmE), / rəˈpeɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having an insatiable appetite for food; devouring or craving food in large amounts.

Examples

Rapacious appetite
A pack of rapacious wolves chased the herd through the forest.
His rapacious appetite was legendary at family gatherings.
The children attacked the pizza with rapacious enthusiasm after soccer practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ravenous - extremely hungry; very eager for food
  • synonym: voracious - wanting or devouring great quantities of food
