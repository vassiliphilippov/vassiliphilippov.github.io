anchovy pear

noun
West Indian fruit, often pickled
7.4 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.tʃoʊ.viː peər/ /; / /ˈæn.tʃə.viː peər/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tʃə.viː peə/ /; / /ˈæn.tʃəʊ.viː peə/ (BrE)
a West Indian tree (Grias cauliflora) that produces an edible fruit resembling a mango, or the fruit itself, often pickled and eaten as a condiment
