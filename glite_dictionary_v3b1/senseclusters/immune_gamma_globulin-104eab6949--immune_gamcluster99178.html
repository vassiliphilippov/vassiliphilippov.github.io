immune gamma globulin

noun
antibody protein produced in lymph tissue
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmjun ˈɡæmə ˈɡlɑblən / (AmE), / ɪˈmjuːn ˈɡæmə ˈɡlɒblɪn / (BrE)
a class of proteins in the blood produced by lymph tissue, functioning as antibodies in the immune system's response to pathogens
