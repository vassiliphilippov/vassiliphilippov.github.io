ibeji

noun
Yoruba sacred twins
Pronunciation: / iˈbeɪdʒi / (AmE), / iˈbeɪdʒi / (BrE)
either or both of a pair of twins, especially as regarded as sacred in Yoruba culture

Examples

Birth of ibeji
In Yoruba tradition, the birth of ibeji is celebrated with special rituals and songs.
The family believed their ibeji brought good fortune to the entire community.
Carved wooden figures often represent ibeji in Yoruba religious practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twin - one of two children born at the same birth
