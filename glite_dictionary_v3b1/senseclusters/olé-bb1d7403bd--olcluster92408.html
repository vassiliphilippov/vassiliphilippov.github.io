olé

noun
shout of approval
8.3
Pronunciation: / oʊˈleɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈleɪ / (BrE)
a shout of approval, praise, or encouragement, especially at sports events or performances

Examples

A loud olé
The crowd erupted in a loud olé after the dancer finished her routine.
Fans shouted olé as the soccer player skillfully dribbled past his opponents.
A chorus of olé filled the arena during the exciting performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - a shout expressing disapproval or displeasure
  • synonym: bravo - a shout of approval or praise, especially at a performance
