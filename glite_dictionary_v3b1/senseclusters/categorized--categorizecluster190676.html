categorized

adjective
arranged into categories
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬əɡəˌraɪzd /; / ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd /; / ˈkatɪɡərʌɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or placed into categories or groups based on shared characteristics.

Examples

To categorize items
All the survey responses were categorized according to age group.
Books in the library are categorized by subject to make them easier to find.
The museum’s collection is categorized into paintings, sculptures, and artifacts.
All the files were categorised by date and importance for easy access.
Books in the library are categorised according to subject matter.
The survey responses were categorised into three distinct groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classify - to arrange into groups based on similarities
  • antonym: uncategorised - not arranged into any specific categories (British spelling)
