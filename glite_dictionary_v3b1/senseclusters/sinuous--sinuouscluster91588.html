sinuous

adjective
having many curves or bends
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.ju.əs /; / ˈsɪnjuəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.ju.əs /; / ˈsɪnjuːəs / (BrE)
having many curves or moving in a smooth, graceful, and winding way

Examples

A sinuous path
Her sinuous movements resembled those of a dancer gliding across the stage.
The sinuous path of the river wound through the lush valley.
The artist drew a series of sinuous lines that gave the painting a sense of flow.
A sinuous line of smoke drifted up from the cottage chimney.
The dancer's sinuous movements captivated everyone in the audience.
The river followed a sinuous path through the valley.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stiff - not easily bent or changed in shape; rigid
  • antonym: straight - not curving or bending; extending in one direction only
  • synonym: supple - bending and moving easily and gracefully
  • synonym: winding - having a twisting or curving form
