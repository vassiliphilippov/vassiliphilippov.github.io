machine stitch

noun
stitch made by a sewing machine
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin stɪtʃ / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a sewing stitch made by a sewing machine, often involving one or more threads

Examples

A machine stitch pattern
A machine stitch is typically stronger and more uniform than a hand-made stitch.
For quick repairs, you can use a machine stitch instead of sewing by hand.
The seamstress used a machine stitch to finish the hem of the dress.
