Gullah

proper noun
African American culture
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌ.lə/ (AmE), /ˈgʌ.lə/ (BrE)
the language and culture of African American communities living in the coastal southeastern United States, especially South Carolina and Georgia

Examples

Gullah language
The Gullah community is known for its rich storytelling and vibrant crafts.
Many words in the Gullah language have roots in West African languages.
Tourists visit the Lowcountry to learn about Gullah traditions and cuisine.
