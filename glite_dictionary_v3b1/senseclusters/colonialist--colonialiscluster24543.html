colonialist

noun
supporter or participant in colonialism
4.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈloʊ.niː.əl.ɪst/ /; / /kə.ˈloʊ.nɪ.əl.ɪst/ (AmE), /kə.ˈləʊ.nɪə.lɪst/ /; / /kə.ˈləʊ.niː.əl.ɪst/ (BrE)
