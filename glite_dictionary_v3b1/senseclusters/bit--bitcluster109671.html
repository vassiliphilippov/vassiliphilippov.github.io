bit

noun
part of a key that lifts lock tumblers
Pronunciation: / bɪt / (AmE), / bɪt / (BrE)
the part of a key that enters a lock and raises the tumblers to unlock it

Examples

Key bit
If the bit is worn or damaged, the key may not open the lock properly.
She inspected the bit for signs of rust before trying it in the door.
The locksmith examined the bit of the old key to create a duplicate.
