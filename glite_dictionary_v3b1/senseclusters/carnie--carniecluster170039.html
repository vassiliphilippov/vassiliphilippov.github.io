carnie

noun
carnival worker
9.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêrni / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêni / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who works at a carnival or fair, especially operating rides or games

Examples

A carnie runs rides
The carnie waved us over to try our luck at the dart game.
After years as a carnie, he knew every trick to attract customers.
We chatted with a friendly carnie while waiting for the Ferris wheel.

Synonyms

  • carney
    9.6 0.19/m
    carney is a common carnival worker name seen in writing and speech, while carnie is the slang form people use in casual talk.
  • carny
    8.9 0.27/m
    carny is a casual word for someone working at rides and attractions, while carnie is the slang form often spelled with ie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: showman - a person who runs or works at a fair or circus
