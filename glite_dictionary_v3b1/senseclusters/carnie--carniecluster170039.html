carnie

noun
carnival worker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrni / (AmE), / ˈkɑːni / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who works at a carnival or fair, especially operating rides or games

Examples

A carnie runs rides
The carnie waved us over to try our luck at the dart game.
After years as a carnie, he knew every trick to attract customers.
We chatted with a friendly carnie while waiting for the Ferris wheel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attendee - a person who attends an event but does not work there
  • synonym: showman - a person who runs or works at a fair or circus
