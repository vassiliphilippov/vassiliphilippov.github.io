unemancipated

adjective
not freed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈmænsɪˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not freed from slavery, servitude, or legal restrictions; still under control or bondage.

Examples

Unemancipated slaves
Many people remained unemancipated even after the new laws were introduced.
The unemancipated population continued to work under harsh conditions.
She fought for the rights of unemancipated individuals in her country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emancipated - freed from legal, social, or political restrictions
  • synonym: enslaved - kept in slavery or bondage
