Synonyms

  • give
    1.0 210/m
    give often means donating money, time, or help directly, while contribute is more formal and often used for group efforts.
  • chip in
    3.2 0.95/m
    chip in is an informal phrasal verb for helping or paying together, while contribute is neutral and used in many contexts.
  • conduce
    9.0 0.15/m
    conduce means to help bring about a result in a general way, while contribute focuses on giving or adding to help.
  • add
    1.0 211/m
    add means to join or increase something like amount or feature, while contribute implies giving to help reach a shared goal.
