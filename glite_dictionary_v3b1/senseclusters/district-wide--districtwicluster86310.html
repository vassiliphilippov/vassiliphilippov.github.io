district-wide

adjective, adverb
covering an entire district
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪs.trɪkt waɪd/ /; / /dɪs.trɪkt ˈwaɪd/ (AmE), /ˈdɪs.trɪkt waɪd/ /; / /dɪs.trɪkt ˈwaɪd/ (BrE)
affecting, involving, or happening in all parts of a district; throughout the whole district

Examples

District-wide program
The new recycling program will be implemented district-wide starting next month.
A district-wide survey was conducted to gather feedback from all schools.
Budget cuts led to changes in transportation services district-wide this year.
