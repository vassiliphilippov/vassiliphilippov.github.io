Attila

proper noun
king of the Huns, 5th century
7.5
Pronunciation: / əˈtɪlə / (AmE), / əˈtɪlə / (BrE)
A king of the Huns (c. 406–453), known for leading the most successful invasions of the Roman Empire and often called 'Attila the Hun.'
