flowcharting

noun
creating process diagrams
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊˌtʃɑːrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfləʊˌtʃɑːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of creating a diagram that visually represents the steps in a process or system

Examples

Flowcharting a process
She spent the morning flowcharting the steps involved in the hiring process.
Good flowcharting can make complex procedures much easier to understand.
The team used flowcharting to plan the new customer service workflow.
