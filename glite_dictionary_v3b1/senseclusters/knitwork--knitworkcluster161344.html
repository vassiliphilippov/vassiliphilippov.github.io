knitwork

noun
fabric made by interlacing yarn loops
3.7 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈnɪ.twɜːrk/ /; / /ˈnɪt.wɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈnɪ.twɜːk/ /; / /ˈnɪt.wɜːk/ (BrE)
