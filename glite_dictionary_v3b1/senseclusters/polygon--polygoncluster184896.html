polygon

noun
closed plane figure with straight sides
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑː.liˌɡɑːn / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.ɪˌɡɒn / (BrE)
A flat, closed shape with three or more straight sides and angles.

Examples

A polygon shape
A regular polygon has all sides and angles equal.
Each side of a polygon must be a straight line.
The architect used a five-sided polygon in the design of the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polyhedron - solid shape with flat polygonal faces
  • degree higher: quadrilateral - four-sided polygon
  • degree lower: triangle - three-sided polygon
