Ibidiidae

noun
bird family including ibises and spoonbills
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌbɪd.iˈaɪ.diː / (AmE), / ɪˌbɪd.ɪˈaɪ.diː / (BrE)
a family of wading birds comprising the ibises and, in some classifications, the spoonbills

Examples

The Ibidiidae family

Taxonomists once classified spoonbills and ibises together in Ibidiidae.
The Ibidiidae are known for their long, down-curved bills.
