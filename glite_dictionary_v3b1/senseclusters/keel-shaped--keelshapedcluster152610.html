keel-shaped

adjective
shaped like a boat's keel
8.9
Pronunciation: / kil ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / kiːl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
shaped like the keel of a boat; having a longitudinal ridge or projecting edge like a boat's keel.

Examples

Keel-shaped petals
Some orchid petals are distinctly keel-shaped, forming a protective structure.
The designer opted for a keel-shaped hull to improve the boat's stability.
The seeds are keel-shaped, allowing them to burrow easily into the soil.
