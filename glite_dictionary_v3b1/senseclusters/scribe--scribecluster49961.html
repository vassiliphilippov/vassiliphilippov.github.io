scribe

noun
tool used to mark wood or metal
8.6 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / skraÉªb / (AmE), / skraÉªb / (BrE)
A sharp-pointed tool, like an awl, used for marking lines on wood or metal surfaces to be cut.

Examples

Metal scribe tool
A scribe is essential for accurate joinery in woodworking projects.
Before drilling, she ran a scribe along the metal sheet to outline where the holes should go.
The carpenter used a scribe to mark a straight line before cutting the plank.

Synonyms

  • scriber
    9.8 0.05/m
    scriber is a sharp-pointed tool used to mark guide lines on wood or metal before cutting, while scribe is the term for that same tool.
  • scratch awl
    7.9 0.00/m
    scratch awl is a sharp-pointed awl used to mark wood or metal before cutting, while scribe is the general term for that marking tool.
