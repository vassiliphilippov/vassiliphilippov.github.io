healthily

adverb
in a healthy way
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθɪli / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθɪli / (BrE)
in a way that promotes or maintains good health of body or mind

Examples

To healthily eat vegetables
She always tries to eat healthily by including plenty of vegetables in her meals.
After his illness, he began living more healthily and exercising regularly.
Doctors recommend sleeping healthily by keeping a consistent bedtime routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutritiously - in a way that provides good nutrition
  • antonym: unhealthily - in a way that is harmful to health
