provinciality

noun
narrow outlook
Pronunciation: / prəˌvɪnʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / prəˌvɪnʃiˈæləti / (BrE)
a narrowness of mind or limited outlook, often resulting from being concerned only with local or regional matters

Examples

Provinciality of thinking
The provinciality of the town made it difficult for outsiders to feel welcome.
Her writing was criticized for its provinciality and lack of global perspective.
Traveling abroad helped him overcome the provinciality of his early education.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitanism - an outlook that is open to and interested in the wider world
  • synonym: parochialism - a limited or narrow outlook, especially focused on a local area
