munchkin

noun
Oz character
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌntʃkɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃkɪn / (BrE)
a small fictional person from Munchkin Country in L. Frank Baum's Land of Oz stories

Examples

Oz munchkin
The munchkin offered Dorothy a warm welcome when she arrived in Oz.
Children dressed as a munchkin for the school play based on The Wizard of Oz.
In the story, each munchkin wore brightly colored clothes and pointed hats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dwarf - a small mythical person in folklore or fantasy
  • antonym: giant - a very large mythical person
