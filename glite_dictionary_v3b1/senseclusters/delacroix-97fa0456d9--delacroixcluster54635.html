Delacroix

proper noun
French Romantic painter (1798–1863)
Pronunciation: / də.ləˈkrwɑ / (AmE), / də.ləˈkrwɑ / (BrE)
(1798–1863) A French Romantic painter, renowned for his expressive brushwork and vibrant color, whose works influenced the development of modern art.
