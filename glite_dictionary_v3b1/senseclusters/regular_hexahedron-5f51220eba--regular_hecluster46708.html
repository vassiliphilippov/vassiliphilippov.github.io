regular hexahedron

noun
a solid with six equal square faces; cube
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr hɛk.səˈhiː.drɒn / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə hɛk.səˈhiː.drən / (BrE)
A three-dimensional solid figure with six equal square faces; a cube.

Examples

A regular hexahedron shape
A regular hexahedron has all its faces shaped as perfect squares.
In geometry class, we learned that a cube is actually a regular hexahedron.
The dice used in board games are typically shaped as a regular hexahedron.
