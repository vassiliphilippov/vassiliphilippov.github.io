scuba diver

noun
person who dives underwater using scuba gear
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːbə ˌdaɪvər / (AmE), / ˈskuːbə ˌdaɪvə / (BrE)
a person who swims underwater using a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (scuba)

Examples

A scuba diver explores
As a certified scuba diver, she enjoys discovering new underwater environments around the world.
During the training course, every scuba diver practiced emergency procedures in the pool.
The scuba diver explored the colorful coral reefs off the coast of Australia.
