hoisin

noun
Chinese sweet sauce
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪsɪn / (BrE)
a sweet, thick Chinese sauce made from soybeans, garlic, and spices, often used in Asian cooking

Examples

Hoisin sauce
She drizzled hoisin over the grilled chicken for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of hoisin to give the stir-fry a sweet taste.
He dipped his spring roll into hoisin before taking a bite.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plum sauce - a sweet Chinese sauce made from plums, often used with duck
  • degree lower: soy sauce - a salty, dark sauce made from fermented soybeans
