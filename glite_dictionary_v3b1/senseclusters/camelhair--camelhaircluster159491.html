camelhair

noun
soft fabric from camel hair
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməlˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈkæməlˌheə / (BrE)
a soft, light brown fabric made from the hair of a camel, often used for coats and suits

Examples

A camelhair coat
A classic camelhair blazer adds elegance to any wardrobe.
She wore a luxurious camelhair coat on the chilly autumn day.
The tailor recommended a camelhair fabric for the custom-made suit.
