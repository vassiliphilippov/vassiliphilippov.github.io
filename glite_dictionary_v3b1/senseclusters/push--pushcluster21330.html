push

verb
advocate or campaign vigorously for something
1.2 1.4
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ / (AmE), / pʊʃ / (BrE)
to make a determined effort to achieve something or to advance

Examples

To push for change
After years of effort, the group continued to push for stronger environmental laws.
