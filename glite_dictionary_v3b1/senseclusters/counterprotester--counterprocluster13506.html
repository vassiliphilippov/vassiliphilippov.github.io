counterprotester

noun
opposing demonstrator
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌproʊtɛstər / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌprəʊtɛstə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a demonstration to oppose another protest or group of protesters

Examples

A counterprotester group
A group of counterprotesters gathered across the street from the main rally.
Police kept the counterprotester and the original demonstrators separated to avoid conflict.
The news report showed counterprotesters holding signs with opposing messages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstrator - a person who takes part in a public protest or demonstration
  • antonym: protester - a person who publicly demonstrates strong objection to something
