longa

noun
early long note
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋɡə / (BrE)
a note in early music notation with a duration longer than a breve

Examples

A musical longa
In medieval manuscripts, the longa was used to indicate a note of extended duration.
The choir learned to recognize the longa among other ancient musical symbols.
A longa is rarely seen in modern music notation but was common in early polyphony.
