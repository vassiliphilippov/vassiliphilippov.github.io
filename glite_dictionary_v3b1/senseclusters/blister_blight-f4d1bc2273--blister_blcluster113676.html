blister blight

noun
fungal disease of tea plants
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪstɚ blaɪt /; / ˈblɪstər blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈblɪstə blaɪt /; / ˈblɪstə ˌblaɪt / (BrE)
a plant disease caused by a fungus, marked by blister-like spots or lesions on leaves, needles, or shoots (especially in tea plants or Scotch pines)
