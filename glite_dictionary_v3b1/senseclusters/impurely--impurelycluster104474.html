impurely

adverb
not purely
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpjʊrli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpjʊəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not pure or is mixed with other substances or elements

Examples

Impurely mixed chemicals
The chemicals reacted impurely because the solution contained several contaminants.
He believed the gold was formed impurely due to the presence of other metals.
The artist mixed the colors impurely, resulting in a muddy shade on the canvas.
