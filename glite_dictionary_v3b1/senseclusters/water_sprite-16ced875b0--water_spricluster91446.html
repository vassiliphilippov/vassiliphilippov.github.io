water sprite

noun
mythical water-dwelling fairy or spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ spraɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə spraɪt / (BrE)
a mythological fairy or spirit believed to inhabit bodies of water, especially in folklore or fantasy literature

Examples

A magical water sprite
Legends say a water sprite haunts the pond at the end of the village.
She dressed as a water sprite for the fantasy-themed costume party.
The children's book features a mischievous water sprite who befriends a lonely boy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dryad - tree-dwelling nymph or spirit in mythology
  • synonym: mermaid - mythical aquatic creature with a human female upper body and a fish tail
  • synonym: naiad - water nymph in Greek mythology
