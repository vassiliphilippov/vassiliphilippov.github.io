teacup

noun
amount a teacup holds
1.0 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtiÀåk åp / (AmE), / ÀàtiÀêk åp / (BrE)
An amount equal to the quantity that a teacup can contain, used as a unit of measure in cooking and other contexts.

Examples

A teacup of sugar
Add a teacup of sugar to the mixture and stir well.
He poured half a teacup of milk into the batter.
The recipe calls for one teacup of chopped nuts.

Synonyms
