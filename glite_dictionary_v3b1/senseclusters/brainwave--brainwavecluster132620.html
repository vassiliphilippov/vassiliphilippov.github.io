brainwave

noun
sudden clever idea; realization
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnˌweɪv / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌweɪv / (BrE)
A sudden clever idea or realization, especially one that resolves a complex situation.

Examples

A sudden brainwave
After hours of fruitless searching, Tom finally had a brainwave about where the keys might be.
She had a brainwave and suddenly knew how to solve the problem.
The team was stuck until Maria had a brainwave that changed their approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epiphany - a moment of sudden and great revelation or realization
  • synonym: insight - the capacity to gain an accurate and deep understanding of something
