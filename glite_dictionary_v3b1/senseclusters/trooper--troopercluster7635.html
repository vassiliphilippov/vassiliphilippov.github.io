trooper

noun
mounted police officer
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːpər / (AmE), / ˈtruːpə / (BrE)
a police officer who is a member of a state or mounted police force

Examples

State trooper

After ten years as a trooper, she was promoted to sergeant.
The trooper pulled over the car for speeding on the interstate.
