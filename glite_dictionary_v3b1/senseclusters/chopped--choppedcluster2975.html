chopped

adjective
cut into small pieces
Pronunciation: / tʃɑpt / (AmE), / tʃɒpt / (BrE)
(of food or other material) prepared by cutting into small pieces.

Examples

Chopped vegetables
She added some chopped parsley to garnish the dish.
The recipe calls for two cups of chopped onions.
The soup is full of fresh, chopped vegetables.
