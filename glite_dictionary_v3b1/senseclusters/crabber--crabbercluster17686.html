crabber

noun
person who catches crabs
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbər / (AmE), / ˈkræbə / (BrE)
a person who catches or hunts crabs, either professionally or as a hobby

Examples

An expert crabber
The experienced crabber set out early in the morning to check his traps.
Many tourists tried their luck as a crabber during the summer festival by the bay.
After hours on the water, the crabber returned with a full catch of blue crabs.
