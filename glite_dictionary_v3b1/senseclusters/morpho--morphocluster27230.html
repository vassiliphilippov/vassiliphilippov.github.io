morpho

noun
large tropical butterfly
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrfoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːfəʊ / (BrE)
a large, brightly colored butterfly native to Central and South America, especially known for its iridescent blue wings

Examples

Blue morpho butterfly
The brilliant blue wings of the morpho caught everyone's attention in the rainforest.
Scientists study the unique scales on the morpho to understand how its wings reflect light.
A morpho butterfly landed gently on a leaf, shimmering in the morning sun.
