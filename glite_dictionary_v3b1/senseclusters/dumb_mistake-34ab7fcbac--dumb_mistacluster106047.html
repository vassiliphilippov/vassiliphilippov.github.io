dumb mistake

noun
a foolish or unwise error
Pronunciation: / dʌm mɪˈsteɪk / (AmE), / dʌm mɪˈsteɪk / (BrE)
a foolish or unwise error; a mistake that seems especially careless or lacking in judgment

Examples

A dumb mistake made
Don't beat yourself up over one dumb mistake; everyone slips up sometimes.
He realized immediately that leaving his wallet at home was a dumb mistake.
It was just a dumb mistake, but it cost her the promotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slip-up - an error due to carelessness
