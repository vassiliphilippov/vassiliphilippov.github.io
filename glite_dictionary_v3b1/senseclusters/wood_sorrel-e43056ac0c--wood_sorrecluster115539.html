wood sorrel

noun
plant of genus Oxalis
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈsɔrəl / (AmE), / wʊd ˈsɒrəl / (BrE)
A small plant of the genus Oxalis, with clover-like leaves and typically yellow or white flowers, found in woodlands and meadows.

Examples

A patch of wood sorrel
A patch of wood sorrel was growing beneath the oak trees in the park.
Many hikers enjoy the tart flavor of wood sorrel when walking through the forest.
The leaves of the wood sorrel fold up at night, making it easy to spot during the day.
