kiam chye

noun
preserved salted mustard green vegetable
5.8
Pronunciation: / kiˈɑm tʃaɪ / (AmE), / kiˈam tʃaɪ / (BrE)
a type of salted and preserved mustard green or leafy vegetable, used especially in Southeast Asian Chinese cuisine
