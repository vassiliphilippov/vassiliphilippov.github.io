undramatic

adjective
lacking drama or excitement
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.drəˈmæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.drəˈmæt.ɪk / (BrE)
not exciting or full of drama; lacking strong emotion or effect

Examples

An undramatic event
His resignation was so undramatic that few people even noticed it.
She handled the crisis in a calm, undramatic way that reassured everyone.
The film's ending was surprisingly undramatic and left the audience unmoved.
