surfeit of confidence

noun
excessive self-assurance

An excessive or overabundant amount of self-assurance, often leading to overconfidence.

Examples

A surfeit of confidence
A surfeit of confidence can sometimes blind people to their own mistakes.
His surfeit of confidence made him underestimate the difficulty of the task.
She approached the meeting with a surfeit of confidence, which impressed some and annoyed others.

Synonyms

  • hyperconfidence

    hyperconfidence is a stronger, less common term for extreme belief in oneself, while surfeit of confidence describes an excess that leads to poor judgment.
