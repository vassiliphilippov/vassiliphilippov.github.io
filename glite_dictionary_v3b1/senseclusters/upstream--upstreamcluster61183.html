upstream

adjective
against a stream's current
3.5 0.89/m
Pronunciation: /  åpÀàstriÀêm / (AmE), /  åpÀàstriÀêm / (BrE)
in the direction opposite to the flow of a stream or river; towards the source

Examples

Upstream pollution
Pollution from factories located upstream can affect water quality.
The salmon swam upstream to reach their breeding grounds.
They paddled upstream for several miles before stopping for lunch.

Synonyms

  • upriver
    4.1 1.06/m

  • upline
    7.9 0.01/m
