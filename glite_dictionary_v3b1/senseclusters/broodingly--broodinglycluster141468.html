broodingly

adverb
in a serious manner
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbruːdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows deep, serious, or gloomy thought

Examples

Look broodingly at someone

He stared broodingly out the window, lost in his own dark thoughts.
The old castle stood broodingly on the hill, casting long shadows over the village.
She answered broodingly, her voice low and filled with worry.
