sutra

noun
aphoristic rule in Indian religious texts
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːtrə / (AmE), / ˈsuːtrə / (BrE)
a rule or concise statement, especially one forming part of a collection of aphoristic teachings in Hinduism, Buddhism, or Jainism, often in Sanskrit

Examples

A sutra text
Each Buddhist sutra presents a distinct aspect of the teachings of the Buddha.
The Yoga sutras of Patanjali are fundamental texts in Indian philosophy.
The scholar devoted years to translating ancient Sanskrit sutras into English.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphorism - a concise statement expressing a general truth
