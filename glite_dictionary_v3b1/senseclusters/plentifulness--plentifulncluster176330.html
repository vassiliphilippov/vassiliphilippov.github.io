plentifulness

noun
state of being abundant
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛn.tɪ.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈplɛn.tɪ.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having a large or full supply; abundance

Examples

The plentifulness of resources
Many were surprised at the plentifulness of resources found in the remote valley.
She appreciated the plentifulness of opportunities available in the city.
The plentifulness of the harvest ensured that no one went hungry that year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something; plentifulness
  • antonym: scarcity - a lack or shortage of something
