slot

noun
track or footprint of a deer or animal
Pronunciation: / slɑt / (AmE), / slɒt / (BrE)
the track or visible trail left by a deer or similar animal, especially its hoofprint

Examples

Deer slot in mud
Fresh slots were visible in the muddy clearing after last night's rain.
He recognized the slot of a stag among the forest leaves.
The hunter examined the slot to determine which way the animal had gone.
