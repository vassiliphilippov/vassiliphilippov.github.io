boilermaker

noun
metal container worker
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɔɪ.lər.meɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈbɔɪ.lə.meɪ.kə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to make, install, or repair large metal containers such as boilers or tanks

Examples

Skilled boilermaker
The experienced boilermaker carefully inspected the pressure vessel for any signs of damage.
After years of training, she became a skilled boilermaker working on industrial sites.
A team of boilermakers assembled the massive steel tank at the construction site.
