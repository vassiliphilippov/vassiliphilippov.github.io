ageist

adjective
prejudiced by age
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒɪst / (BrE)
showing prejudice or discrimination against people because of their age, especially older adults

Examples

Ageist comments
The company was criticized for its ageist hiring practices.
Many people are unaware that their comments can sound ageist to older colleagues.
Laws have been introduced to prevent ageist discrimination in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discriminatory - treating people unfairly based on group characteristics
  • antonym: fair - treating people equally and without prejudice
  • synonym: prejudiced - having an unreasonable dislike or preference based on personal characteristics
