husk

verb
remove the outer covering from
5.0
Pronunciation: / hʌsk / (AmE), / hʌsk / (BrE)
to remove the outer covering (husk) from grains, seeds, or corn

Examples

To husk corn
Farmers often husk corn before taking it to market.
It takes time to husk all the sunflower seeds by hand.
She husked the ears of corn, preparing them for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peel - to remove the outer covering from fruits or vegetables
  • synonym: shell - to remove the shell from nuts or seeds
