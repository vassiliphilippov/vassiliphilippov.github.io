neutral

adjective
having no strong color or hue
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnutʃrəl / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəl / (BrE)
(of a color) having no strong hue; not belonging to any particular color family, such as black, white, or gray.

Examples

Neutral color palette
She prefers neutral tones like gray and beige when decorating her home.
The walls were painted in a neutral color to match any furniture.
Wearing neutral shades can create a classic and timeless look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: achromatic - without color; not chromatic
  • antonym: colorful - having much or varied color; bright or distinctive in color
