baby-walker

noun
device for helping babies learn to walk
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbi ˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbi ˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a frame on wheels or casters that supports a baby learning to walk
