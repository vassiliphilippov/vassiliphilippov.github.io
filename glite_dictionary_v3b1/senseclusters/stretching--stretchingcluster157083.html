stretching

noun
exercise to extend muscles or limbs
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an exercise or act of gently extending muscles or something by pulling, especially to improve flexibility or make it longer

Examples

Stretching exercises
Stretching before a run can help prevent muscle injuries.
She spends ten minutes stretching every morning to keep her body limber.
The yoga class began with gentle stretching exercises to improve flexibility.
Before running, she spent several minutes on stretching exercises.
Daily stretching can improve your flexibility and reduce the risk of injury.
The stretching of the fabric made it easier to fit the furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contraction - the act of becoming shorter or smaller
  • synonym: cool-down - gentle exercise after intense activity
  • synonym: extension - the act of stretching or expanding something
  • synonym: warm-up - gentle exercise or activity before more intense exercise
