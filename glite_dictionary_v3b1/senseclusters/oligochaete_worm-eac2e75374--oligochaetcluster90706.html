oligochaete worm

noun
hermaphroditic annelid worm with single bristles
7.8 8.0
Pronunciation: /ɑː.lɪ.ɡə.ˈkiːt wɜːrm/ /; / /ə.lɪ.ɡə.ˈkiːt wɜːrm/ (AmE), /ɒ.lɪ.ɡə.ˈkiːt wɜːm/ /; / /ə.lɪ.ɡə.ˈkiːt wɜːm/ (BrE)
