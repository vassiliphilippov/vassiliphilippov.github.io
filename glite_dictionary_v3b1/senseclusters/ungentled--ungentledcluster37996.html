ungentled

adjective
not made gentle
9.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛntəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛntəld / (BrE)
not made gentle or not softened in manner or character

Examples

Ungentled horse
The ungentled horse was difficult to approach and refused to be saddled.
Her ungentled manner made it hard for others to feel comfortable around her.
The trainer specialized in working with ungentled animals that had never been handled by humans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: untamed - not domesticated or controlled
