foal

noun
young horse
5.6
Pronunciation: / foʊl / (AmE), / fəʊl / (BrE)
A young horse, especially one that is less than one year old.

Examples

A newborn foal
A newborn foal needs lots of care and attention.
Every spring, several foals are born on the farm.
The mare watched over her foal as it took its first steps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colt - a young male horse
  • synonym: filly - a young female horse
