pedestrianism

noun
competitive walking sport
7.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɛstriəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / pɪˈdɛstriənɪzəm / (BrE)
the sport or activity of competitive walking, especially over long distances

Examples

Pedestrianism race
Pedestrianism attracted large crowds in the 1800s, with some races lasting several days.
He became interested in pedestrianism after reading about famous long-distance walkers.
The decline of pedestrianism as a sport coincided with the rise of modern athletics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: racewalking - a competitive walking event with strict rules on technique
  • antonym: running - the sport or activity of moving rapidly on foot
