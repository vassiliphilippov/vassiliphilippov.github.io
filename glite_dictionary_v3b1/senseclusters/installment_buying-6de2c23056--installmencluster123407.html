installment buying

noun
purchase by paying in installments
3.6 2.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈstɔːl.mənt ˈbaɪ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈstɔːl.mənt ˈbaɪ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a system of purchasing goods or services by making a series of regular payments over a period of time rather than paying the full amount upfront.

Examples

Installment buying plan
Because of installment buying, she was able to get a new laptop and pay for it over several months.
Many households choose installment buying to afford expensive appliances without paying the total cost upfront.
The furniture store offers installment buying plans to make purchases more manageable for customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hire purchase - system of buying goods through installment payments (mainly UK term)
  • synonym: layaway - system of paying for goods gradually and receiving them when paid in full
