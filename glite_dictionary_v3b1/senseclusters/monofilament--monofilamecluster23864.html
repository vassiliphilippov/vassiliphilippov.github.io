monofilament

adjective
single-strand material
7.2
Pronunciation: /mɑː.nə.ˈfɪl.ə.mənt/ (AmE), /mɒn.ə.ˈfɪl.ə.mənt/ (BrE)
made from or consisting of a single continuous strand or filament, especially in yarn, fabric, or wire

Examples

Monofilament yarn
The fishing line is monofilament, making it strong and nearly invisible in water.
Athletic shoes often use monofilament mesh for better ventilation and durability.
A monofilament thread is preferred in some medical sutures for its smoothness and strength.
