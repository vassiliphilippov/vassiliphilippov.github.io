closer

noun
salesperson who finalises deals
Pronunciation: / klo az r / (AmE), / kl az / (BrE)
A salesperson who specialises in finalising sales or deals, persuading a prospective buyer to agree and completing the transaction.

Examples

Top closer seals deals
The company hired a top closer to seal a multimillion-dollar deal before quarter end.
As the negotiation stalled, their experienced closer stepped in and won the client's trust.
New startups often rely on skilled closers to convert leads into paying customers quickly.
