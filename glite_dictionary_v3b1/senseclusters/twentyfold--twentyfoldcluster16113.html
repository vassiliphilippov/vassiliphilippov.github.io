twentyfold

adverb
by twenty times
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɛntiˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtwɛntiˌfəʊld / (BrE)
by twenty times as much or as many

Examples

Grew twentyfold
The population of the city grew twentyfold in just fifty years.
After the new policy, production increased twentyfold compared to last year.
Their investment returned twentyfold profits, surprising everyone involved.
