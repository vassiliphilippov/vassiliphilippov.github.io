unite

verb
combine or possess qualities or features
1.0
Pronunciation: / juːˈnaɪt / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪt / (BrE)
to have, possess, or bring together (qualities, features, or characteristics) in combination

Examples

Unite skill and effort
He unites patience with determination to achieve his goals.
Her speech unites clarity with passion, making it very persuasive.
The artist unites tradition and innovation in his latest exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combine - to join or mix two or more things together
  • antonym: separate - to move or be moved apart; to stop being together or joined
