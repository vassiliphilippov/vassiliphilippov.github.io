turbomachinery

noun
energy-transferring machines
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜːrbəˈməʃɪnəri / (AmE), / ˌtɜːbəˈməʃɪnəri / (BrE)
machines such as turbines, compressors, and fans that transfer energy between a rotor and a fluid, or the engineering field that studies these machines

Examples

Engineers analyze turbomachinery to improve the performance of pumps and turbines in power plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turbine - a machine that produces power by spinning from the movement of fluid
