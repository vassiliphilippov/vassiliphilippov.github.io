hard currency

noun
premium in‑game currency
Pronunciation: / hɑrd ˈkʌrənsi / (AmE), / hɑːd ˈkʌrənsi / (BrE)
In video games, a premium in‑game currency (often bought with real money) used to purchase exclusive items, speed progress, or access special content.
