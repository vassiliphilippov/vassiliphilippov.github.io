Tetrazzini

noun
baked pasta dish with cream, mushrooms, and meat
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛtrəˈzini / (AmE), / ˌtɛtrəˈziːni / (BrE)
an American pasta dish baked in a creamy sauce with mushrooms, often including poultry or seafood
