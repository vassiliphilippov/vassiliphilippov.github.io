cake

noun
block of a solid substance
Pronunciation: / keɪk / (AmE), / keɪk / (BrE)
a block or mass of a solid substance, especially one shaped for convenient use, such as soap or wax

Examples

A cake of soap
A cake of beeswax was used for polishing the furniture.
He bought a cake of soap from the store.
The old laundry used a cake of washing powder for every load.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a long block of solid substance, often referring to soap or chocolate
