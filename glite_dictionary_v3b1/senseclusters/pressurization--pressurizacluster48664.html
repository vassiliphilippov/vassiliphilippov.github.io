pressurization

noun
applying pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɛʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of applying pressure or influence to persuade someone or a group to do something

Examples

Pressurization of a person
The team's pressurization of the new member made him feel uncomfortable.
She felt constant pressurization from her peers to join the club.
Without the manager's pressurization, the project would have taken much longer to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coercion - the act of forcing someone to do something by using pressure or threats
  • degree lower: encouragement - the act of giving support or confidence to someone
  • synonym: persuasion - the act of convincing someone to do or believe something
