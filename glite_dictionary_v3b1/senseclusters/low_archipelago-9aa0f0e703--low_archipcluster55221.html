Low Archipelago

proper noun
group of coral islands in French Polynesia
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˌɑːrtʃəˈpɛlɪˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ ˌɑːtʃɪˈpelɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a group of about 80 coral islands in French Polynesia, part of the Tuamotu Islands
