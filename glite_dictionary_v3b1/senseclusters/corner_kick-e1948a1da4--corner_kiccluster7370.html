corner kick

noun
kick from the corner in football/soccer
2.4 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îrn…ôr k…™k / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀên…ô k…™k / (BrE)
(in football/soccer) a free kick taken from the corner of the field, awarded to the attacking team when the ball has crossed the goal line, last touched by a defender.

Examples

A corner kick in football
A corner kick can create a great opportunity to score a goal in soccer.
She practiced taking corner kicks to improve her accuracy and power.
The team scored after a well-placed corner kick in the final minutes of the game.

Related Terms
