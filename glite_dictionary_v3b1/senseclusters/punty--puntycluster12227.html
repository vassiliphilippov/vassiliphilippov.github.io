punty

noun
glassblowing rod
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌnti / (AmE), / ˈpʌnti / (BrE)
a metal rod used in glassblowing to hold, support, or shape hot glass during the forming process

Examples

Glassblower's punty rod
The artist carefully attached the molten glass to a punty before shaping it.
A punty is essential for handling delicate glass pieces during the blowing process.
After reheating, she used the punty to add the final touches to her vase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pontil - a metal rod used in glassblowing to handle and shape glass
