toe

noun
front part of footwear covering the toes
Pronunciation: / toʊ / (AmE), / təʊ / (BrE)
the part of a shoe or other footwear that covers or protects the toes

Examples

Shoe toe
He accidentally spilled water on the toe of his sneaker.
My boots have a steel toe for added protection at work.
The toe of her shoe was scuffed from playing soccer.

  • antonym: heel - the back part of a shoe or foot
