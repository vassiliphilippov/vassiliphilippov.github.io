brattle

verb
make a rattling or clattering sound
4.2 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrætəl / (AmE), / ˈbrætəl / (BrE)
(of objects, especially metal or crockery) to make a loud rattling or clattering sound.

Examples

To brattle loudly
A sudden wind made the loose windowpane brattle against the frame.
Coins brattled in her pocket as she hurried down the street.
The dishes brattled noisily in the sink as he washed them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clatter - to make a continuous loud rattling sound
  • synonym: rattle - to make a rapid succession of short, sharp noises
