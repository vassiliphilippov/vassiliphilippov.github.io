menswear

adjective
for men's clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnzˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɛnzˌwɛə / (BrE)
relating to clothing, fashion, or design intended for men, especially when used before a noun.

Examples

Menswear collection
The designer launched a new menswear collection for the upcoming season.
He works for a magazine that focuses on menswear trends and style advice.
Many brands are expanding their menswear lines to include more casual options.
