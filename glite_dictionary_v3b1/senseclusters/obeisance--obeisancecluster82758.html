obeisance

noun
gesture expressing respect or submission
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbeɪsəns / (AmE), / əʊˈbeɪsəns / (BrE)
a gesture or act (such as bowing or bending the head) that shows respect, submission, or obedience

Examples

A obeisance gesture
As a sign of respect, she made a slight obeisance upon entering the temple.
He greeted the elder with an obeisance, lowering his head respectfully.
The knight offered an obeisance before the king, bowing deeply at the throne.
Her obeisance to the tradition was evident in the way she followed every rule.
In some cultures, it is customary to perform an obeisance when greeting elders.
The servant made an obeisance before the king, bowing his head low.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - to bend the head or upper part of the body as a sign of respect or greeting
  • antonym: defiance - open resistance or disobedience
  • synonym: respect - a feeling or expression of esteem or honor
