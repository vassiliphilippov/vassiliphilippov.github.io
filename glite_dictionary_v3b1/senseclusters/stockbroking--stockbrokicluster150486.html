stockbroking

noun
buying and selling stocks
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkˌbroʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌbrəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or profession of buying and selling stocks and shares on behalf of clients

Examples

Stockbroking business
He started his career in stockbroking at a major financial firm in London.
Many people turn to stockbroking for expert advice on buying shares.
The world of stockbroking can be fast-paced and highly competitive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brokerage - the business of acting as a broker in financial transactions
  • antonym: investing - the act of putting money into financial schemes or assets for profit
  • synonym: trading - the act of buying and selling financial assets
