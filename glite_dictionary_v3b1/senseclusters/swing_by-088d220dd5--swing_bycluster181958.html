swing by

verb
make a brief visit
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ baɪ / (AmE), / swɪŋ baɪ / (BrE)
to visit a place briefly, especially on the way to somewhere else

Examples

To swing by a place
I'll swing by your desk after the meeting to drop off the report.
If you have a minute, could you swing by my apartment this afternoon?
Let's swing by the supermarket on our way home and pick up some bread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call in - to pay a short, informal visit
  • synonym: drop by - to visit someone or somewhere informally and briefly
