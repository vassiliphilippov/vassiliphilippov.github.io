termination

noun
the ending of something, often a contract or period
4.3 3.8
Pronunciation: /tɜːr.mə.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /tɜːr.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /tɜː.mə.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /tɜː.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of ending something, such as a contract, agreement, or period

Examples

Termination of contract
The termination of his employment contract was effective as of June 30.
Upon termination of the insurance policy, coverage will no longer be provided.
We received a notice regarding the termination of our lease agreement.
Following the termination of the agreement, both parties moved on to new ventures.
Her employment contract specified the grounds for termination.
The sudden termination of the project surprised the entire team.
