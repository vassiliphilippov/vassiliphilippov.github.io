unrejuvenated

adjective
not refreshed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈdʒuːvəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈdʒuːvəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not feeling refreshed, renewed, or restored in energy or spirit

Examples

Feeling unrejuvenated after rest
Despite a full night's sleep, he woke up feeling unrejuvenated and sluggish.
The vacation left her surprisingly unrejuvenated, as she was still stressed about work.
After hours at the spa, he remained unrejuvenated and disappointed by the experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: refreshed - feeling rested, renewed, or full of energy
  • synonym: tired - feeling in need of rest or sleep
