cohost

verb
to host together
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈhoʊst / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈhəʊst / (BrE)
to organize or present an event together with one or more people

Examples

To cohost an event
They cohosted the conference to bring together experts from different fields.
The two celebrities will cohost the awards ceremony this year.
She has cohosted several radio shows with her colleague.
They decided to cohost the charity gala to combine their audiences.
Next week, she will cohost the morning radio show with a special guest.
The two comedians cohosted the awards ceremony, making the event lively and entertaining.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organize - to arrange or coordinate an event
