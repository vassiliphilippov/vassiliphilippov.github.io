in-your-face

adjective
blatantly aggressive or confrontational
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.jɚˈfeɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.jəˈfeɪs / (BrE)
blatantly aggressive, confrontational, or bold in manner or style

Examples

An in-your-face attitude

That advertisement is so in-your-face that it's hard to ignore.
The film's in-your-face style is not to everyone's taste.
