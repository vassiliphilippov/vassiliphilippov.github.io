sick

noun
people who are ill
2.1
Pronunciation: / sɪk / (AmE), / sɪk / (BrE)
(the sick) people who are ill or unwell, especially as a group requiring care or attention.

Examples

Care for the sick
Doctors worked tirelessly to assist the sick during the epidemic.
The hospital provides special services for sick who need long-term care.
Volunteers bring meals and comfort to the sick in their community.
