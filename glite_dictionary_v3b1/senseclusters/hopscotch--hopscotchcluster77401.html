hopscotch

verb
to skip between places
4.6 0.23/m

to move quickly from one place or situation to another, often skipping steps or stages

Examples

To hopscotch cities
He had to hopscotch across several cities in just one week for business meetings.
The journalist hopscotched from one breaking news story to another all day.
They hopscotch between different projects instead of focusing on just one.

Synonyms

  • leapfrog
    4.8 0.05/m
    leapfrog means jumping past stages or people to get ahead, while hopscotch emphasizes skipping steps while moving quickly between places.
  • leap
    4.4 3.44/m
    leap means to pass suddenly from one state or place to another without gradual change, while hopscotch involves skipping stages in quick moves.
