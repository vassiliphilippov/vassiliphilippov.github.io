agroecosystem

noun
farming ecological system
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡroʊˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌæɡrəʊˈiːkəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a farming system in which crops, animals, and their environment interact as a single ecological unit

Examples

A agroecosystem farming system
A well-managed agroecosystem can improve both crop yields and environmental sustainability.
Researchers study how biodiversity affects the balance of an agroecosystem.
The health of an agroecosystem depends on the interactions between soil, plants, and animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecosystem - a community of living organisms and their environment
  • antonym: monoculture - the cultivation of a single crop in a given area
