orgeat

noun
barley-based drink
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈʒæt / (AmE), / ˈɔːʒɑː / (BrE)
an archaic sweet drink or syrup made from barley, formerly used as a cooling beverage

Examples

Old orgeat barley drink
In the old recipe book they served orgeat as a cooling barley drink at summer fairs.
The hostess offered her guests a glass of orgeat on a hot afternoon.
Before lemonade became popular, people often drank orgeat to refresh themselves in summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barley water - a drink made by boiling barley in water
