white ant

noun
termite; wood-eating insect
6.5
Pronunciation: / waɪt ænt / (AmE), / waɪt ænt / (BrE)
A whitish, soft-bodied, ant-like social insect that feeds on wood; another name for a termite.

Examples

A white ant insect
In some countries, houses are protected against white ants because they can cause major structural damage.
Scientists study the tunnels built by white ants to learn about social insect behavior.
The old bookshelf was infested with white ants, so we had to throw it away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: termite - a soft-bodied insect that feeds on wood and lives in colonies
