anytime

interjection
polite response to thanks
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛniˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈɛniˌtaɪm / (BrE)
used as a polite response to thank someone, meaning you are happy to help or it was no trouble

Examples

Anytime as a reply
Thanks for your help! Anytime, just let me know if you need anything else.
She smiled and replied anytime when her friend thanked her for the advice.
If you ever need a ride, just ask. Anytime!

Related Terms

  • synonym: no problem - an informal way to say it was not a problem to help
  • synonym: you're welcome - a polite reply to someone who has thanked you
