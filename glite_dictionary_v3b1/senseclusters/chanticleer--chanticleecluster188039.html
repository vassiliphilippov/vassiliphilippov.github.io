chanticleer

noun
vain, boastful person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæntɪˌklɪr / (AmE), / ˈʃæntɪklɪə / (BrE)
a vain or boastful person, especially one who shows off or brags loudly, likened to a proud rooster in fables.

Examples

He is a chanticleer
He strutted into the meeting like a chanticleer, loudly announcing every accomplishment.
After winning the award, she became a real chanticleer at every gathering.
Nobody liked working with a chanticleer who always bragged about his successes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast or show off
  • synonym: show-off - someone who tries to attract attention by displaying their abilities or possessions
