Ensete

noun
genus of tropical banana-like plants
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈsiːti / (AmE), / ɛnˈsiːti / (BrE)
A genus of large, tropical, herbaceous plants in the banana family, native to Africa and Asia, including the Abyssinian banana.

Examples

Ensete plant leaves
Farmers use the leaves of Ensete to wrap and store food.
The Ensete plant is sometimes called the false banana due to its resemblance to the true banana.
The highlands of Ethiopia are well known for their cultivation of Ensete.
