choo

noun
train sound imitation
9.9
Pronunciation: /tʃuː/ (AmE), /tʃuː/ (BrE)
an imitation of the sound made by a steam train's whistle or engine, often used by children

Examples

A loud choo
The little boy shouted 'choo choo' as he played with his toy train.
You could hear a loud 'choo' as the steam engine started moving down the tracks.
She made a 'choo' sound to entertain the children during story time.
