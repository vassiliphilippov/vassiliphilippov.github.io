babyminder

noun
person who looks after babies for working parents
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.biˌmaɪnd.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bɪˌmaɪn.də / (BrE)
a person who looks after babies, usually in their own home, while the babies' parents are at work

Examples

A babyminder at home
Many parents rely on a trusted babyminder during busy workweeks.
She hired a babyminder to look after her daughter while she was at work.
The babyminder arrived early to prepare breakfast for the twins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysitter - a person who looks after children, usually temporarily, while the parents are away
  • synonym: childminder - a person who looks after children, often in their own home, as a job
  • synonym: nanny - a person, typically employed full-time, who takes care of children in their own home
