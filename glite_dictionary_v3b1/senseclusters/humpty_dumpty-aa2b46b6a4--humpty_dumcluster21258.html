Humpty Dumpty

proper noun
nursery rhyme character
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmpti ˈdʌmpti / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpti ˈdʌmpti / (BrE)
a character from a traditional English nursery rhyme, usually depicted as a large egg who falls from a wall
