Synonyms

  • bravely

  • valiantly
    5.5 0.85/m
    valiantly describes bold, determined effort often in hard struggle or danger, while courageously simply means acting with courage.
  • heroically
    4.9 0.78/m
    heroically suggests a dramatic, admired act like saving someone against great odds, while courageously names ordinary or brave action.
  • dauntlessly
    8.0 0.01/m

  • intrepidly
    7.7 0.04/m
