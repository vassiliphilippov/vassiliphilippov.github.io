courageously

adverb
in a courageous manner
Pronunciation: / kəˈreɪdʒəsli / (AmE), / kəˈreɪdʒəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows courage; bravely.

Examples

To courageously admit mistake
He courageously admitted his mistake in front of everyone.
She courageously defended her beliefs despite strong opposition from others.
The firefighter courageously rescued the family from the burning building.

  • synonym: fearlessly - without fear
