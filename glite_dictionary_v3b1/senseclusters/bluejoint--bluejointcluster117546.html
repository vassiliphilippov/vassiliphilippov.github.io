bluejoint

noun
tall wetland grass
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌdʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌdʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a tall grass native to North America, commonly found in wetlands and prairies

Examples

Tall bluejoint grass

Farmers often see bluejoint thriving in the low, marshy parts of their fields.
In the summer, the prairie glows with golden sunlight on the waving bluejoint.
