interservice

adjective
between military branches
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
relating to cooperation or activities involving different branches of the armed forces

Examples

Interservice cooperation
The interservice meeting brought together officers from the army, navy, and air force.
An interservice rivalry sometimes arises during joint military operations.
The government announced a new interservice initiative to improve communication between branches.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraservice - within a single branch of the armed forces
  • synonym: joint - involving two or more groups working together
