misquote

noun
an incorrect quotation
4.6 4.6
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈkwoʊt/ /; / /mɪs.ˈkwəʊt/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈkwəʊt/ (BrE)
An incorrect or inaccurate quotation; a statement that does not precisely reproduce what was originally said or written.

Examples

A famous misquote
Historians often have to correct a misquote that has been repeated for decades.
She was frustrated by the misquote attributed to her in the newspaper.
The article contains a misquote that changes the meaning of the original statement.
