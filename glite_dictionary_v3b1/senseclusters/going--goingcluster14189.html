going

adjective
in operation; active (esp. business)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a business or enterprise) operating and making progress; in full operation.

Examples

Going business

Investors are interested as long as the enterprise is a going concern.
The bakery is still a going concern, serving customers every day.
