mucker

noun
mining waste remover
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌk.ər/ (AmE), /ˈmʌk.ə/ (BrE)
a machine used in mining or tunneling to remove broken rock or waste material from the work area

Examples

Mining mucker machine
The workers operated a mucker to clear debris from the newly blasted tunnel.
After the explosion, a mucker was brought in to remove the loose rock quickly.
Modern mines often rely on a mucker to keep the work area clean and safe.
