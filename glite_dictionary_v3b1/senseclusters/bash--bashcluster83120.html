bash

verb
to criticize harshly
Pronunciation: / bæʃ / (AmE), / bæʃ / (BrE)
to criticize someone or something strongly or unfairly, often in a harsh or public way

Examples

To bash a movie
Many reviewers were bashing the new film before it even opened in cinemas.
He was unfairly bashed by the media for a minor mistake.
People online often start bashing celebrities without knowing the full story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - to criticize or speak against forcefully
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
