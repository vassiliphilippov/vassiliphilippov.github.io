speak to

verb
talk to someone about something
1.7 58/m
Pronunciation: / spiÀêk tu / (AmE), / spiÀêk t ä / (BrE)
to talk to someone, especially to discuss something or to address a matter with them

Examples

To speak to the team
Could you speak to the team and explain the changes in the project?
I need to speak to my manager about taking some time off next week.
She finally spoke to her friend after days of avoiding the issue.

Synonyms

  • talk
    1.0 988/m
    talk is a general, everyday way to speak with someone, while speak to often means addressing a topic or matter.
  • discuss
    1.6 177/m
    discuss is to talk about something in detail with others, while speak to often simply means to address a person or matter.
  • address
    3.9 14/m
    address means to speak formally to someone or deal with an issue, while speak to is more general and less formal.
  • converse
    3.3 2.47/m
    converse is to take part in a friendly two-way talk, while speak to can mean talking in one direction or addressing an issue.
