prairie anemone

noun
a spring-flowering perennial plant
4.5 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpreə.riː ə.ˈnem.ə.nɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpreə.ri ə.ˈnem.ə.nɪ/ (BrE)
