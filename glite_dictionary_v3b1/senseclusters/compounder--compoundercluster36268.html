compounder

noun
person who mixes substances
7.6
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpaʊndər / (AmE), / kəmˈpaʊndə / (BrE)
a person who mixes or prepares substances, especially medicines or chemicals (such as in a pharmacy)

Examples

Pharmacy compounder
