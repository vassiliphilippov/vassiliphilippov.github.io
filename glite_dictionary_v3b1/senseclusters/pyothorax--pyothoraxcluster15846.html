pyothorax

noun
pus in chest cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: /paɪ.ə.ˈθɔːr.æks/ /; / /paɪ.ə.ˈθɑːr.æks/ (AmE), /paɪ.ə.ˈθɒr.æks/ (BrE)
a medical condition in which pus accumulates in the chest cavity between the lungs and the chest wall

Examples

Pyothorax chest infection
The doctor confirmed the presence of pyothorax after reviewing the patient's chest X-ray.
Treatment for pyothorax often involves antibiotics and draining the infected fluid.
If left untreated, pyothorax can lead to serious complications and requires prompt medical attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empyema - a collection of pus in a body cavity, especially the pleural cavity
  • antonym: pleurisy - inflammation of the tissues that line the lungs and chest cavity
