counterdemonstration

noun
protest opposing another protest
5.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /kaʊn.tɜːr.dem.ən.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kaʊn.tə.dem.ən.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A public protest or demonstration held to oppose or respond to another demonstration.

Examples

A counterdemonstration protest
A large counterdemonstration formed nearby, chanting opposing slogans.
Authorities were concerned about potential violence between the protest and the counterdemonstration.
The police kept the counterdemonstration separated from the original protest to avoid clashes.
