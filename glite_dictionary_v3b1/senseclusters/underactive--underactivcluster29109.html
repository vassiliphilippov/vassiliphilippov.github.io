underactive

adjective
less active than normal
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈæktɪv / (BrE)
less active than normal; functioning below the usual or expected level, especially medically.

Examples

An underactive thyroid
An underactive thyroid can cause fatigue and weight gain.
She was diagnosed with an underactive gland that required medical treatment.
Tests showed that his immune system was underactive compared to average levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypoactive - having abnormally low activity; sluggish, especially in medical contexts
  • antonym: overactive - more active than normal; excessively active
