overexuberant

adjective
excessively lively or ornate
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzuːbərənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪɡˈzjuːbərənt / (BrE)
extremely or excessively lively, energetic, enthusiastic, or abundant (often to the point of being too much)

Examples

An overexuberant garden
The garden was filled with overexuberant plants that spilled over every path.
Her overexuberant praise made everyone in the room feel slightly uncomfortable.
The party decorations were so overexuberant that the space felt crowded and chaotic.
The overexuberant puppy jumped on everyone who entered the room.
Her overexuberant response to the news made everyone laugh.
Some fans became overexuberant during the celebration and caused a disturbance.

  • degree lower: exuberant - full of energy, excitement, or abundance
  • antonym: restrained - controlled or limited in expression or style
