effendi

noun
title of respect in Ottoman Turkey
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛndi / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛndi / (BrE)
(historical) A title of respect or courtesy in Turkey and some other eastern countries, especially for a man of high social status or a government official.

Examples

The effendi title
In the novel, the wise old effendi offered counsel to the young sultan.
The family was proud to have an effendi among their ancestors.
The local mayor was always addressed as effendi by the townspeople.
