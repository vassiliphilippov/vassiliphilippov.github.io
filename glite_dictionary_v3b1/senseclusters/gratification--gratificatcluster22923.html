gratification

noun
state of being pleased or satisfied
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræt̬əfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌɡræt̬.ə.fəˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌɡræt.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the feeling or act of being pleased or satisfied when a desire or need is fulfilled

Examples

Great gratification
Finishing the project early brought him immense gratification.
She smiled with gratification at the praise she received from her teacher.
There is a deep sense of gratification in helping others achieve their goals.
He seeks gratification in helping others achieve their goals.
She felt a deep sense of gratification after completing the project successfully.
The instant gratification of social media can be addictive for some people.

  • synonym: satisfaction - the feeling of pleasure or contentment when a need or desire is fulfilled
