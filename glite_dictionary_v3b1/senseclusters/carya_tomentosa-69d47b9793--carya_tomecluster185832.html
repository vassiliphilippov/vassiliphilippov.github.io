Carya tomentosa

noun
a North American hickory tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛəriə toʊmɛnˈtoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈkɛːrɪə tɒmɛnˈtəʊsə / (BrE)
A species of North American hickory tree with smooth bark, 7 to 9 leaflets, and a hard-shelled edible nut.
