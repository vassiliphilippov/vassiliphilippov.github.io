cardiogenic

adjective
originating from the heart
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrdioʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
caused by or originating from the heart

Examples

Cardiogenic shock
The doctor diagnosed the patient with cardiogenic shock after the heart attack.
A cardiogenic cause was suspected due to the patient's history of heart disease.
Treatment for cardiogenic pulmonary edema often requires specialized cardiac care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardiac - relating to the heart
  • antonym: noncardiac - not related to the heart
