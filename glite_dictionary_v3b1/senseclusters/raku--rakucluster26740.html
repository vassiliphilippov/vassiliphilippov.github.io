raku

noun
Japanese pottery style
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkuː / (AmE), / ˈrɑːkuː / (BrE)
a type of Japanese pottery made by rapidly firing and cooling clay, often resulting in unique colors and textures

Collectors value raku pieces for their unpredictable patterns and colors.
