drop

noun
a small spherical amount of liquid
5.4
Pronunciation: / drɑp / (AmE), / drɒp / (BrE)
a small amount of liquid, or a very small amount of something

Examples

A drop of water
A drop of water fell from the faucet and landed in the sink.
Only a single drop of blood stained his shirt after the accident.
She watched a drop of rain trickle down the windowpane.
I haven't had a drop to drink all day.
The recipe calls for just a drop of vanilla extract.
There wasn't a drop of kindness in his reply.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bead - a small, round drop of liquid
  • synonym: dash - a small amount of something, especially liquid, added to something else
  • synonym: dribble - a small quantity of liquid that falls slowly in drops
  • degree higher: drop - a larger amount, such as a full portion or dose
  • synonym: globule - a small spherical drop of liquid
