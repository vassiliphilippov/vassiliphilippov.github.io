heel

noun
a morally contemptible person
Pronunciation: / hil / (AmE), / hiːl / (BrE)
an untrustworthy or contemptible person, especially a man who behaves badly toward others

Examples

A real heel
He turned out to be a real heel after all his empty promises.
Only a heel would treat his friends that way.
She was heartbroken when she discovered her boyfriend was such a heel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
  • synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person
  • synonym: villain - a wicked or cruel person
