futures

noun
possible outcomes ahead
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃərz / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃəz / (BrE)
the possible events, opportunities, or outcomes that may happen in the time ahead for a person or thing

Examples

Bright futures
The school encourages students to think about their futures and set personal goals.
Many parents worry about the futures of their children in a changing world.
Young people today have more choices and challenges shaping their futures than ever before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destiny - the events that will necessarily happen to a person in the future
  • antonym: past - the time or events that have already happened
  • synonym: prospects - the chances or possibilities for future success or advancement
