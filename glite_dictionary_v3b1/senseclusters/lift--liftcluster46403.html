lift

noun
cosmetic surgery to tighten facial skin
3.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /lɪft/ (AmE), /lɪft/ (BrE)
a cosmetic surgical procedure that removes wrinkles and signs of aging by tightening the facial skin, often by making an incision near the hairline and excising excess tissue

Examples

A face lift
After her lift, she looked ten years younger.
He was nervous before his first lift, but the results were impressive.
Many celebrities have undergone a lift to enhance their appearance.
