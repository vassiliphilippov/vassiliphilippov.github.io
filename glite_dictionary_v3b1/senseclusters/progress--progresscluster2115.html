progress

verb
develop or advance positively
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛs / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛs / (BrE)
to move forward, develop, or improve over time

Examples

To progress quickly
After months of practice, his piano skills began to progress noticeably.

The construction on the new bridge did not progress as quickly as expected due to bad weather.
As the meeting progressed, he became more confident in his ideas.
She hopes to progress steadily in her career over the next few years.
Technology continues to progress at an astonishing rate.
