spindle-shaped

adjective
tapering at both ends
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪndl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈspɪndl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
narrowing at both ends and wider in the middle, like a spindle; tapering at each end.

Examples

Spindle-shaped muscle fibers
Muscle fibers in the tissue appeared distinctly spindle-shaped under the microscope.
The artist carved a spindle-shaped handle for the drawer, giving it an elegant touch.
The bacterium has a spindle-shaped body that allows it to move easily through fluids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusiform - tapering at both ends; spindle-shaped
