merchant bank

noun
bank that processes credit card payments
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːtʃənt bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈmɜːtʃənt bæŋk / (BrE)
A bank that processes credit card transactions for businesses, typically providing merchants with credit for card receipts minus a processing fee.

Examples

Merchant bank processes payments
If your merchant bank is slow, you may not get paid for credit card sales promptly.
Some small businesses work with an online merchant bank to process their payments efficiently.
The retailer switched to a new merchant bank to obtain lower transaction fees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acquiring bank - a bank that processes credit or debit card payments on behalf of a merchant
