flower head

noun
dense cluster of flowers resembling a single bloom
1.1 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈflaʊ.ər hed/ /; / /ˈfloʊ.ər hed/ (AmE), /ˈflaʊ.ə hed/ /; / /ˈfləʊ.ə hed/ (BrE)
a dense, compact cluster of small flowers arranged so closely together that they appear as a single flower, as seen in plants like clover or daisies.
