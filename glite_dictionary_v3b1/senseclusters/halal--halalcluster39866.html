halal

adjective
permitted by Islamic dietary law
1.0
Pronunciation: / həˈlɑl /; / hɑːˈlɑːl / (AmE), / həˈlɑːl / (BrE)
allowed or prepared according to Islamic law, especially referring to food, drink, or practices

Examples

Halal food
Many restaurants in the city now offer halal dishes to cater to their Muslim customers.
The butcher guarantees that all his meat is halal and sourced from trusted suppliers.
They searched for a halal kitchen to ensure the meal met religious requirements.
All meat served at the restaurant is halal, prepared according to Islamic dietary rules.
Muslim consumers look for halal certification on food packaging when shopping at the supermarket.
The company offers a range of halal products to meet the needs of their Muslim customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kosher - (of food) prepared according to Jewish dietary law
