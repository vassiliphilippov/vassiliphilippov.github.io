geophytic

adjective
relating to geophytes (plants with underground storage organs)
8.5
Pronunciation: / dʒiːˈɑːfɪtɪk / (AmE), / dʒiːˈɒfɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of geophytes; living or growing as a geophyte.

Examples

Geophytic plant growth
A geophytic habit allows these plants to regrow after wildfires thanks to their underground organs.
Many desert plants adopt a geophytic lifestyle to survive long dry periods.
The tulip is a classic example of a geophytic species, storing nutrients in its bulb.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geophyte - a plant with perennating buds below the soil surface
