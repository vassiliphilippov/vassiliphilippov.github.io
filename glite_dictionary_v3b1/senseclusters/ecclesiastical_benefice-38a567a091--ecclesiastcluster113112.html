ecclesiastical benefice

noun
endowed church office with income
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəl ˈbɛnəfɪs / (AmE), / ɪˌkliːzɪˈæstɪkəl ˈbɛnɪfɪs / (BrE)
An endowed church office that provides income to its holder.
