cabinet

noun
piece of furniture for storage, often lockable
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbɪnət /; / ˈkæb.ɪ.nət / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪnɪt /; / ˈkæb.ɪ.nɪt / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with shelves, drawers, or compartments for storing or displaying things

Examples

Kitchen cabinet
He opened the antique cabinet to reveal a collection of rare coins.
She keeps her jewelry in a locked cabinet beside her bed.
The kitchen cabinet was filled with spices and cooking utensils.
He locked the important documents in a secure cabinet at the office.
The antique cabinet in the living room displays a collection of rare vases.
We installed a new cabinet in the kitchen to store our dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: closet - a small enclosed space or room for storing clothes or other items
  • synonym: cupboard - a piece of furniture with shelves for storing dishes, food, or other items
  • synonym: wardrobe - a large, tall cabinet or closet for storing clothes
