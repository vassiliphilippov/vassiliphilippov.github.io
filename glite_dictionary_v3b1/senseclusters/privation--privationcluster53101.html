privation

noun
the state of being deprived of essentials
9.0 7.4
Pronunciation: /praɪ.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prɪ.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ /; / /praɪ.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a state of not having the basic things needed for life, such as food, warmth, or money; severe hardship or deprivation
