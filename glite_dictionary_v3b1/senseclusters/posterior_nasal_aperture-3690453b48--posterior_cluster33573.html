posterior nasal aperture

noun
opening at back of nasal cavity
9.0
Pronunciation: / poʊˈstɪriər ˈneɪzəl ˈæpərtʃər / (AmE), / pɒˈstɪəriə ˈneɪzəl ˈapətʃə / (BrE)
either of the two openings at the back of the nasal cavity that lead into the nasopharynx

Examples

The posterior nasal aperture
An enlarged adenoid can block the posterior nasal aperture, causing breathing difficulties.
During development, the posterior nasal apertures form as part of the palate.
The surgeon examined the posterior nasal aperture for signs of obstruction.
