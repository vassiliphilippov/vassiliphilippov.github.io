patent application

noun
request for patent rights to an invention
Pronunciation: / ˈpætnt ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpætnt ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
an official request submitted to a patent office to obtain legal rights for an invention

Examples

A patent application form
Her patent application was rejected because the invention did not meet the criteria of novelty.
It can take years for a patent application to be approved by the authorities.
The company filed a patent application to protect its new smartphone technology.
