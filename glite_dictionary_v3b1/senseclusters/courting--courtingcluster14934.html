courting

noun
seeking romantic affection, esp. for marriage
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of seeking the romantic affection of someone, especially with the intention of marriage.

Examples

Courting a partner
In many cultures, courting involved formal visits and chaperoned meetings.
Modern courting often looks quite different from traditional rituals of the past.
Their courting lasted for over a year before he proposed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtship - the period during which a couple develops a romantic relationship before marriage
