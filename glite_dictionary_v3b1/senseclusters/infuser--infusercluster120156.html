infuser

noun
medical fluid pump
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfjuzər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjuːzə / (BrE)
a medical device that slowly delivers fluids or drugs into a patient's body, often through an intravenous or subcutaneous route

Examples

Medical infuser
The hospital uses an infuser to administer antibiotics over several hours.
Her pain medication was delivered by a portable infuser during her recovery.
The doctor checked the settings on the infuser to ensure the correct dosage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infusion pump - a device that delivers fluids, such as nutrients or medications, into a patient's body in controlled amounts
  • degree lower: syringe - a device used to inject fluids into or withdraw them from the body
