feed

noun
food for livestock
1.0 7.09/m
Pronunciation: / fid / (AmE), / fiÀêd / (BrE)
food given to domestic animals such as cattle, horses, or poultry

Examples

Animal feed bags
Rising grain prices have increased the cost of feed for livestock farmers everywhere.
The farmer bought a new supply of feed for his cows before winter arrived.
The store carries different types of animal feed, including pellets for chickens and meal for pigs.

Synonyms

  • feedstuff
    5.1 0.03/m
    feedstuff is any material used to feed livestock on farms, while feed is the food given to domestic animals.
  • provender
    9.9 0.04/m
    provender means food for livestock and farm animals in older or formal speech, while feed is common everyday animal food.
  • forage
    4.4 1.45/m
    forage is bulky grass or hay fed to grazing animals, while feed is general food given to domestic livestock.
  • fodder
    4.8 0.01/m
    fodder means to give hay or straw to farm animals, while feed names the food given to domestic animals.
