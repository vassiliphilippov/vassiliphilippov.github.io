controversialist

noun
person who enjoys arguing or disputing
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who frequently engages in disputes or arguments, especially over controversial matters

Examples

A controversialist debates
As a noted controversialist, she was often involved in heated public debates.
Despite being a skilled controversialist, he was admired for his sharp intellect and wit.
The columnist gained a reputation as a controversialist who never shied away from difficult topics.
