career politician

noun
someone whose main job is politics
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr pɑːˈlɪtɪʃən / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə pɒlɪˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person whose principal profession throughout life is politics

Examples

A career politician
After working in public service, she decided she did not want to become a career politician.
He was often criticized for being a career politician out of touch with ordinary citizens.
Many voters are frustrated with the influence that a career politician can wield within government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: politician - a person who is professionally involved in politics
