August plum

noun
wild plum tree with red-orange fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɡəst plʌm / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡəst plʌm / (BrE)
A wild plum tree native to eastern and central North America, bearing red-orange fruit with yellow flesh.

Examples

August plum tree
Birds often feed on August plum fruit as it ripens on the branches.
Children love picking the ripe, tart fruit of the August plum during August.
The woods behind our house are full of August plum trees in late summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wild plum - any species of plum that grows in the wild
