Echeveria

noun
ornamental succulent plant
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛtʃəˈvɛriə / (AmE), / ˌɛtʃɪˈvɪəriə / (BrE)
a type of ornamental succulent plant with fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves, often grown as a houseplant

Examples

A potted Echeveria
She placed a small Echeveria on her desk to brighten up her workspace.
The garden center had several varieties of Echeveria in different colors and sizes.
After a few weeks, the Echeveria began to produce new leaves in the center of its rosette.

Related Terms

  • synonym: succulent - a plant with thick, fleshy tissues for storing water
