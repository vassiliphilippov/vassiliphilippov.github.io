misadministration

noun
improper application
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the incorrect or improper giving or application of something, especially a drug, vaccine, or medical treatment

Examples

Misadministration of medicine
The hospital reviewed its procedures after a misadministration of medication led to patient complications.
Strict protocols are in place to prevent the misadministration of vaccines in the clinic.
A nurse reported the misadministration of a drug, which was quickly corrected by the medical team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: administration - the act of giving or applying something, especially a drug or treatment
  • synonym: maladministration - bad or inefficient management or administration
