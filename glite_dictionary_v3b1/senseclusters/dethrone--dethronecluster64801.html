dethrone

verb
remove from power or authority
Pronunciation: / dɪˈθroʊn / (AmE), / dɪˈθrəʊn / (BrE)
to remove a monarch or ruler from their position of power or authority

Examples

To dethrone a king
If the general succeeds, he may dethrone the president and seize power.
Rebels attempted to dethrone the unpopular king after years of unrest.
The queen was dethroned after accusations of corruption and abuse of power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depose - to remove from office suddenly and forcefully
  • antonym: enthrone - to install as a monarch or leader
