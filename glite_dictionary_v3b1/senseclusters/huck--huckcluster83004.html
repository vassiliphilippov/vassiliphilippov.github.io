huck

noun
long disc throw
9.9
Pronunciation: /hʌk/ (AmE), /hʌk/ (BrE)
a long, powerful throw of a disc, especially in ultimate frisbee, intended to cover a large distance

Examples

A long huck
He launched a huge huck that sailed across the entire field.
The team scored after a perfectly timed huck to the end zone.
She practiced her huck every day to improve her distance and accuracy.
