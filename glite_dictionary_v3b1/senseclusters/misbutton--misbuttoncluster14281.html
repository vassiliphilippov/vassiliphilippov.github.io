misbutton

verb
to fasten buttons incorrectly
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈbʌtən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈbʌtən / (BrE)
to fasten the buttons of a garment incorrectly so that they do not align with the buttonholes

Examples

To misbutton a coat
He realized he had misbuttoned his coat when he saw it looked uneven.
If you misbutton your shirt, the collar will not sit properly.
She quickly misbuttoned her blouse in her rush to leave the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: button - to fasten with buttons
  • antonym: unbutton - to undo the buttons of a garment
