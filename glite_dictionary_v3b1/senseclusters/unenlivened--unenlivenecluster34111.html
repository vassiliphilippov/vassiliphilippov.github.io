unenlivened

adjective
not made lively; lacking spirit
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɪnˌlaɪvənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɪnˌlaɪvənd / (BrE)
Not made lively or more interesting; lacking in energy, spirit, or brightness.

Examples

An unenlivened voice
His voice was unenlivened by enthusiasm, making the lecture feel tedious.
She found the party unenlivened and left after only an hour.
The movie remained unenlivened by any moments of humor or excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement
  • antonym: lively - full of life and energy
