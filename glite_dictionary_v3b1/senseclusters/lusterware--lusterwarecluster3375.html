lusterware

noun
pottery with a metallic sheen
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌstərˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈlʌstəˌweə / (BrE)
Pottery decorated with a metallic sheen, produced by adding metallic oxides to the glaze.

Examples

Shiny lusterware pottery
Collectors seek out lusterware for its unique shimmering glaze and vivid colors.

The museum's collection of lusterware includes pieces from the 18th and 19th centuries.
