coconut cake

noun
cake with shredded coconut
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkəˌnʌt keɪk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkənʌt keɪk / (BrE)
A cake made with shredded coconut mixed into the batter and often in the frosting.
