vegetarian

adjective
not containing meat or fish
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛdʒəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌvɛdʒɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
(of food) not containing meat or fish; suitable for vegetarians.

Examples

Vegetarian meal option

She prepared a vegetarian lasagna for the family dinner.
This restaurant is famous for its vegetarian dishes.
