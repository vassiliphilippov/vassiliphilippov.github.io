unintentional

adjective
not done on purpose; accidental
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪn.ˈten.ʃə.nəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪn.ˈten.ʃə.nəl/ (BrE)
Not done on purpose or with deliberate intent; accidental.

Examples

Unintentional mistake
His remarks were unintentional, but they still hurt her feelings.
She made an unintentional error in her calculations, which affected the results.
The damage to the painting was completely unintentional and happened during the move.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidental - happening by chance, not intended
  • antonym: intentional - done on purpose or deliberately
