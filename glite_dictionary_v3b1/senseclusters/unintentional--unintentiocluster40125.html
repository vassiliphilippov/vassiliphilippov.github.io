Synonyms

    6.6 1.62/m

  • accidental
    3.0 6.54/m
    accidental means caused by chance or luck, while unintentional covers mistakes or acts done without purpose.
  • unplanned
    3.5 1.99/m

  • unpremeditated
    7.5 0.11/m

  • unwitting
    6.7 1.27/m

  • undesigned
    7.3 0.03/m
