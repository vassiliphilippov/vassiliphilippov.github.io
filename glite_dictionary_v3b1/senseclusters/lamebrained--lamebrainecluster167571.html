lamebrained

adjective
foolish or silly
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪmˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈleɪmˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
very foolish or lacking good sense; silly or thoughtless

Examples

A lamebrained idea
It was a lamebrained idea to leave the door unlocked all night.
He came up with another lamebrained plan that was doomed to fail.
Don't listen to his lamebrained suggestions if you want things to go well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
