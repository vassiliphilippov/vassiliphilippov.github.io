counterpoise

noun
a balancing weight or force
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚˌpɔɪz / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.təˌpɔɪz / (BrE)
a weight or force that balances or offsets another weight or force

Examples

A counterpoise to stress
Her calm manner was a counterpoise to his nervous energy.
In the old clock, a small counterpoise keeps the pendulum swinging steadily.
The heavy stone acted as a counterpoise to the weight on the other side of the scale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterbalance - a weight that provides balance against another
