ballistically

adverb
relating to projectiles
8.5
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / bəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to the motion or science of projectiles or their flight path

Examples

To move ballistically
The rocket traveled ballistically after its engines shut off.
The scientist explained how the object moved ballistically through the atmosphere.
The probe was launched ballistically to minimize fuel use during its journey.
