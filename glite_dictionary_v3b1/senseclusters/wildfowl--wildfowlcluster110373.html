wildfowl

noun
wild game birds
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪld.faʊl/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪld.faʊl/ (BrE)
wild birds, especially ducks, geese, and similar species that are often hunted for sport or food

Examples

Migrating wildfowl
Many wildfowl migrate south for the winter to find warmer climates.
The reserve is home to a wide variety of wildfowl, including ducks and geese.
Hunters must follow strict regulations when shooting wildfowl during the season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic fowl - birds such as chickens or ducks that are kept by people
  • synonym: game bird - a bird hunted for sport or food
  • synonym: waterfowl - birds that live on or around water, such as ducks and geese
