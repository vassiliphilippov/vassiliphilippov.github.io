pancit

noun
Filipino noodle dish
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːnsɪt / (AmE), / ˈpæn.sɪt / (BrE)
a Filipino dish consisting of stir-fried noodles with vegetables, meat, or seafood

Examples

Filipino pancit dish
For her birthday, Maria prepared a large tray of pancit for all the guests.
Many Filipino families serve pancit during celebrations for good luck and long life.
The restaurant's special pancit includes shrimp, chicken, and fresh vegetables.

  • synonym: noodles - long, thin strips of pasta or dough
