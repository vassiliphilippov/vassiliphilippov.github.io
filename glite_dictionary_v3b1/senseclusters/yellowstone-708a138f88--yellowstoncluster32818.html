Yellowstone

proper noun
US national park
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a large national park in the United States famous for its geysers, hot springs, and diverse wildlife

Examples

Yellowstone National Park

The Old Faithful geyser is one of the most popular attractions in Yellowstone.
