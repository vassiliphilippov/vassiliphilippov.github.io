certificate of incorporation

noun
official document authorizing formation of a corporation
3.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /sər.ˈtɪf.ɪ.kət əv ɪn.kɔːr.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sə.ˈtɪf.ɪ.kət əv ɪn.kɔː.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
