luxurious restaurant

noun
expensive, high-end dining establishment
2.0
Pronunciation: / lʌɡˈʒʊriəs ˌrɛstəˈrɑːnt / (AmE), / lʌɡˈzjʊəriəs ˌrɛstərɒnt / (BrE)
A restaurant that is lavishly furnished, offers high-quality service, and serves expensive, gourmet food.

Examples

A luxurious restaurant
Dressing formally is usually expected when dining in a luxurious restaurant.
The city is famous for its many luxurious restaurants overlooking the waterfront.
They celebrated their anniversary at a luxurious restaurant downtown.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casual restaurant - a less formal and less expensive dining place
  • synonym: fine dining restaurant - a restaurant offering high-quality food and service
