internationality

noun
quality of being international
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.t̬ɚˌnæʃ.əˈnæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˌnæʃ.əˈnæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being international; extending across or relating to multiple nations

Examples

The internationality of relations
She values the internationality of the curriculum offered at her university.
The internationality of the award enhances its prestige in the scientific community.
The internationality of the conference attracted participants from more than forty countries.
