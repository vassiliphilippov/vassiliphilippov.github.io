comestible

adjective
suitable to be eaten; edible
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛstəbl̩ / (AmE), / kəˈmɛstɪbəl / (BrE)
suitable to be eaten as food; edible.

Examples

Comestible plants
Many plants are not comestible and can even be poisonous.
Only comestible mushrooms should be collected in the wild.
The inspector marked all comestible goods with a safety sticker.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inedible - not suitable for human consumption
