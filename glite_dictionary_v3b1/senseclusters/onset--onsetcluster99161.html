onset

noun
beginning; initial sound
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌsɛt / (BrE)
the beginning or start of something, or the initial sound in a syllable in linguistics

Examples

Onset of winter
The onset of winter brought heavy snow to the region.
Doctors are studying the onset of symptoms in patients with the new illness.
In the word 'cat', the onset is the 'c' sound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: end - the final part of something
  • synonym: start - the point in time or space at which something begins
