teleview

verb
to watch by television
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌvjuː / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvjuː / (BrE)
to view or watch something by means of television transmission

Examples

To teleview a show
Millions of people televiewed the historic moon landing live in 1969.
You can teleview the concert from the comfort of your living room.
The championship game was televiewed by fans around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to transmit a program or information by radio or television
  • synonym: watch - to look at something attentively
