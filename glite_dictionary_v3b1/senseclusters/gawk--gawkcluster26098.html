gawk

noun
an awkward or clumsy person
Pronunciation: / ɡɔk / (AmE), / ɡɔːk / (BrE)
a person who is awkward or clumsy, and often appears unintelligent

Examples

A clumsy gawk
He always felt like a gawk at social gatherings, tripping over his own feet.
She didn't want to be seen as a gawk among her more graceful friends.
The boy turned out to be more than just a gawk; he was actually very clever.
