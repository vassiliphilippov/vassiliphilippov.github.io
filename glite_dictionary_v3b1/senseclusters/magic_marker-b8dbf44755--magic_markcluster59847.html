Magic Marker

noun
brand of felt-tip marker pen
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪk ˈmɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈmædʒɪk ˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
a brand name for a felt-tip pen, often used informally to refer to any thick, colored marker with a writing tip made of felt

Examples

A Magic Marker pen
My little brother drew pictures all over the walls with a Magic Marker.
Please hand me a Magic Marker so I can write the sign.
She labeled each box with a Magic Marker to keep things organized.
