weatherworn

adjective
worn by the weather
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌwɔrn / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌwɔːn / (BrE)
Worn or eroded in appearance due to prolonged exposure to the weather.

Examples

Weatherworn stone statue
He admired the weatherworn statue standing in the garden.
Her weatherworn face told stories of years spent working outdoors.
The old barn’s weatherworn boards gave it a charming rustic look.
