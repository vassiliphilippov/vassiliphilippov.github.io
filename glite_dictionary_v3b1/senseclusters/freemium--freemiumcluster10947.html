freemium

noun
free with paid extras
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfriː.mi.əm/ /; / /ˈfriː.mɪ.əm/ (AmE), /ˈfriː.mi.əm/ /; / /ˈfriː.mɪ.əm/ (BrE)
a business model in which basic services are provided for free while advanced features require payment

Examples

Freemium business model
The popular app uses a freemium model, offering basic tools for free and charging for advanced options.
Many online services have adopted the freemium approach to attract a large user base.
He prefers freemium games because he can try them before deciding to pay for extra features.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subscription - a business model where users pay regularly for access to a service
