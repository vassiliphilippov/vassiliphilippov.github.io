revenger

noun
person seeking revenge
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɛndʒər / (AmE), / rɪˈvɛndʒə / (BrE)
a person who takes or seeks revenge for a wrong or injury

Examples

The revenger plans revenge
The revenger spent years planning how to get back at those who betrayed him.
In many classic tragedies, the revenger is driven by a deep sense of injustice.
She warned him not to become a revenger, but to let go of his anger.
The revenger plotted carefully, waiting for the perfect moment to strike back.
In many classic plays, the revenger is driven by a deep sense of justice.
She became a revenger after being betrayed by her closest friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avenger - a person who takes action to punish or get justice for a wrong
  • antonym: forgiver - a person who chooses to forgive rather than seek revenge
