clear liquid diet

noun
diet of transparent liquids for medical use
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪr ˈlɪkwɪd ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈklɪə ˈlɪkwɪd ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting only of transparent liquids, such as fat-free broth, clear juices, or gelatin, usually prescribed before or after surgery or during certain medical conditions

Examples

A clear liquid diet
A clear liquid diet is often prescribed when a patient has digestive issues.
After her surgery, the doctor recommended a clear liquid diet to help her recovery.
You may have to follow a clear liquid diet before certain medical procedures.
