spider crab

noun
crab with long legs and triangular body
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdər kræb / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə kræb / (BrE)
a crab with very long legs and a small, triangular body, belonging to several species in the family Majidae

Examples

The spider crab species
Fishermen often find spider crabs in their nets during crab season.
Many aquariums display the giant Japanese spider crab because of its impressive leg span.
The spider crab camouflaged itself among the rocks on the ocean floor.
