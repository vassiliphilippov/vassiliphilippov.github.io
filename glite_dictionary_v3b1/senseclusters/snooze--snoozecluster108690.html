snooze

noun
a short, light sleep
Pronunciation: / snuːz / (AmE), / snuːz / (BrE)
a short, light sleep, especially one taken during the day and not in bed

Examples

A quick snooze
After a long drive, she enjoyed a refreshing snooze in the car.
He likes to have a snooze during his lunch break at work.
I took a quick snooze on the sofa before dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nap - a short sleep, especially during the day
  • synonym: siesta - a rest or nap taken after lunch, especially in hot countries
