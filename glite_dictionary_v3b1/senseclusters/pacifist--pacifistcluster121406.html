pacifist

noun
person opposed to violence or war
4.6 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpæs.ə.fɪst/ /; / /ˈpæs.ɪ.fɪst/ (AmE), /ˈpæs.ɪ.fɪst/ /; / /ˈpæs.ə.fɪst/ (BrE)
A person who believes that violence, especially war, is unjustifiable and should be avoided in resolving disputes.

Examples

A pacifist leader
As a pacifist, she refused to support the military intervention.
The pacifist argued that dialogue, not violence, was the only way to bring lasting peace.
Throughout his life, Martin Luther King Jr. remained a committed pacifist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peace activist - person advocating peaceful methods to achieve goals
  • antonym: warmonger - person who encourages or advocates war
