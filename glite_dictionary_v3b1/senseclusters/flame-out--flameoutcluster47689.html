flame-out

noun
jet engine failure due to fuel or combustion issues
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmˌaʊt / (BrE)
the failure of a jet engine caused by interrupted fuel supply or faulty combustion, resulting in loss of power

Examples

Jet engine flame-out
A flame-out at high altitude can be extremely dangerous if not managed properly.
Engineers are investigating the cause of yesterday’s flame-out during the test flight.
The pilot reacted quickly after the sudden flame-out of the engine.
