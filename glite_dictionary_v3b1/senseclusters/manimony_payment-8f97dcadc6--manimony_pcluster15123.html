manimony payment

noun
financial settlement for divorced men
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌmoʊni ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈmænɪˌməʊni ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
A financial settlement awarded to a man following a divorce, especially as court-ordered support from an ex-wife.
