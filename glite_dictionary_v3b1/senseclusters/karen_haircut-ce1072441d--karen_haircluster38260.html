Karen haircut

noun
trendy short, angled hairstyle linked to stereotypes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛɹən ˌhɛɚˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈkæɹən ˌheəˌkʌt / (BrE)
A short, angled hairstyle often featuring layers and highlights, associated in popular culture with a demanding or entitled woman.

Examples

The Karen haircut style
His meme about the Karen haircut quickly went viral online.
She decided to get a Karen haircut before her big move to the city.
The stylist knew exactly what she meant when she requested a Karen haircut.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bob haircut - a short haircut for women where the hair is typically cut straight around the head at about jaw-level
