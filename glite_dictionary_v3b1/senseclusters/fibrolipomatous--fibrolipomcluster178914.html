fibrolipomatous

adjective
fibrous and fatty tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˌlɪpəˈmeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊˌlɪpəˈmeɪtəs / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the presence of both fibrous and fatty tissue

Examples

Fibrolipomatous tissue
The surgeon removed a fibrolipomatous mass from the patient's leg.
A fibrolipomatous growth can affect the function of nearby nerves.
Doctors identified the tumor as fibrolipomatous after examining the tissue under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lipomatous - relating to or consisting of fatty tissue
