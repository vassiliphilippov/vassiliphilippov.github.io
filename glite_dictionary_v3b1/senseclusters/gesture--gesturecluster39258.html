gesture

noun
touchscreen input movement
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒes.tʃər/ /; / /ˈdʒes.tʃɜːr/ (AmE), /ˈdʒes.tʃə/ (BrE)
(in computing and human–computer interaction) a movement or sequence of movements made on a touchscreen, touchpad, or other input device, performed to control software or issue a command.

Examples

Touchscreen gesture
To zoom in on the photo, use a two-finger gesture on the screen instead of the zoom buttons.
The update added a three-finger gesture to switch quickly between open apps and desktops.
Many phones let you assign a custom gestures to launch the camera or play music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinch - a two-finger movement on a touchscreen used to zoom in or out; an example of a gesture
  • synonym: swipe - a horizontal or vertical finger movement across a touchscreen used to scroll or change screens; a common type of gesture
