Jesuitic mindset

noun
clever or cunning way of thinking
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛz.juˈɪ.tɪk ˈmaɪnd.set / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛz.juˈɪ.tɪk ˈmaɪnd.set / (BrE)
A way of thinking characterized by cleverness, subtle reasoning, or, sometimes, cunning.
