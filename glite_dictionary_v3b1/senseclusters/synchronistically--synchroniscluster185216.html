synchronistically

adverb
by meaningful coincidence
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that involves meaningful or symbolic coincidences without an obvious causal connection

Examples

To meet synchronistically
The two friends met synchronistically just as they were both thinking about each other.
Several important events happened synchronistically, making her believe there was a deeper meaning.
He noticed that the song played synchronistically with his thoughts about his childhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coincidentally - in a way that happens by chance at the same time
