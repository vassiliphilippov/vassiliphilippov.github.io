tropicality

noun
tropical quality
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɑːpɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being tropical or characteristic of the tropics

Examples

The tropicality of the climate
The tropicality of the island attracts tourists seeking warm weather and lush vegetation.
Scientists study the tropicality of rainforests to understand their unique ecosystems.
The architect incorporated elements of tropicality into the building's design to suit the climate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: temperateness - the quality of being moderate or mild in climate
  • synonym: tropical - relating to or characteristic of the tropics
