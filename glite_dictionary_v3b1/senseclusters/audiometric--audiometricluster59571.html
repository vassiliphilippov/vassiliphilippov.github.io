audiometric

adjective
relating to hearing measurement
Pronunciation: / ˌɔdiəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːdiəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of hearing ability or the science of audiometry.

Examples

Audiometric evaluation
All job applicants must undergo an audiometric evaluation before starting work in the factory.
Recent studies have led to improvements in audiometric equipment.
The doctor performed an audiometric test to assess the patient's hearing loss.
