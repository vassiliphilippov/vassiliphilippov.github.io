centaurian

noun
human-horse creature
9.9
Pronunciation: /sen.ˈtɔːr.i.ən/ (AmE), /sen.ˈtɔː.ri.ən/ (BrE)
a mythical creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse, similar to a centaur

Examples

A centaurian warrior
The ancient painting depicted a fierce centaurian charging into battle.
Legends say a mysterious centaurian once protected the mountain village from danger.
Children listened in awe to stories about the wise old centaurian who lived in the forest.

Related Terms
