perfunctoriness

noun
lack of enthusiasm
9.9
Pronunciation: / pərˈfʌŋktəriˌnəs / (AmE), / pəˈfʌŋktərɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of doing something with little interest, care, or enthusiasm

Examples

The perfunctoriness of action
The perfunctoriness of his handshake made her doubt his sincerity.
She completed her chores with obvious perfunctoriness, barely paying attention to the details.
There was a sense of perfunctoriness in the way the report was written.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enthusiasm - strong excitement or interest
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
