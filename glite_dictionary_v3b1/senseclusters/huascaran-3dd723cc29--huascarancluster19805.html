Huascaran

proper noun
highest mountain in Peru
Pronunciation: / wɑːˌskɑːˈrɑːn / (AmE), / wɑːsˌkɑːˈrɑːn / (BrE)
a mountain in the Andes of Peru, notable for being the country's highest peak at 22,205 feet (6,768 meters)
