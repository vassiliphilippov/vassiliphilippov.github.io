Synonyms

  • railway
    1.1 2/m
    railway is tracks and infrastructure for trains over a wide area, while railroad also names the company that runs them.
  • railway
    1.0 1/m
    railway is a company that runs trains for passengers or freight, while railroad also refers to the tracks and system.
  • railway system
    2.7 0.04/m
    railway system is an organized network run to carry people or goods by train, while railroad can mean either company or tracks.
  • railroad line
    2.7 0.09/m
    railroad line is a company operating train services along a particular route, while railroad means the entire system or the company that owns it.
