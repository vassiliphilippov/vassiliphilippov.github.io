amusia

noun
inability to perceive music
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪˈmjuːziə / (AmE), / æˈmjuːziə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which a person is unable to recognize or produce musical tones or rhythms

Examples

Amusia disorder
After the accident, he developed amusia and could not recognize familiar songs.
People with amusia may find it difficult to enjoy or participate in musical activities.
Her diagnosis of amusia explained why she struggled to sing in tune.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tone-deafness - the inability to distinguish between different musical pitches
