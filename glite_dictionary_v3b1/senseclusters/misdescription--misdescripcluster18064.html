misdescription

noun
false description
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdɪˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
an incorrect, false, or misleading description of something

Examples

A misdescription of product
The company was fined for providing a misdescription of its product's safety features.
He claimed that the advertisement was a clear misdescription of what was actually offered.
The contract was voided due to a misdescription of the property boundaries.
The contract was voided due to a misdescription of the property.
A misdescription in the advertisement led to confusion among potential buyers.
The lawyer argued that the misdescription was unintentional and should not affect the agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deception - the act of making someone believe something that is not true
  • antonym: description - a statement that gives details about something
  • synonym: misrepresentation - the act of giving a false or misleading account
  • antonym: truth - the quality or state of being true
