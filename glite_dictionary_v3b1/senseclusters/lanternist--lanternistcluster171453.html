lanternist

noun
lantern maker or seller
Pronunciation: / ˈlæntərnɪst / (AmE), / ˈlæntənɪst / (BrE)
a person who makes or sells lanterns, especially as a craft or trade

Examples

Village lanternist
The lanternist displayed his handmade lanterns at the village fair every autumn.
As a skilled lanternist, she used traditional techniques to create beautiful glass lanterns.
In the old city, a lanternist was known for crafting unique designs for local festivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftsman - a skilled worker who makes things by hand
  • synonym: merchant - a person who buys and sells goods
