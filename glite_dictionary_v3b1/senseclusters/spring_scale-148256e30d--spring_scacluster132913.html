spring scale

noun
device that measures weight using a spring
4.3 3.5
Pronunciation: /sprɪŋ skeɪl/ /; / /sprɪŋ skeɪ.əl/ (AmE), /sprɪŋ skeɪl/ /; / /sprɪŋ skeɪ.əl/ (BrE)
