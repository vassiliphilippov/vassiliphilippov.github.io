Coast Range

proper noun
mountain ranges along the Pacific coast of North America
5.6
Pronunciation: / koʊst ˈreɪndʒ / (AmE), / kəʊst ˈreɪndʒ / (BrE)
a series of mountain ranges along the Pacific coast of North America, extending from southeastern Alaska through Canada and the United States to northern Mexico

Examples

The Coast Range mountains
Dense forests cover much of the Coast Range in Oregon and Washington.
The Coast Range provides a natural barrier between the sea and the inland valleys.
The hikers planned to cross the Coast Range before reaching the Pacific shore.
