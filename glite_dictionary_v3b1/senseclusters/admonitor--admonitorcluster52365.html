admonitor

noun
person who warns
Pronunciation: / ædˈmɑːnɪtər / (AmE), / ædˈmɒnɪtə /; / ədˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
a person who warns or advises someone, especially to correct their behavior

Examples

A strict admonitor
The teacher acted as an admonitor when she warned the students about their behavior.
He respected his friend as an honest admonitor who always pointed out his mistakes.
In the meeting, an admonitor cautioned the team against rushing the project.
He acted as an admonitor to his younger colleagues, guiding them away from common mistakes.
The teacher became an admonitor when she noticed students breaking the rules.
As an admonitor, she always reminded her friends to stay focused on their goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counselor - someone who gives guidance or advice
  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or points out faults
