soft roe

noun
fish sperm used as food
Pronunciation: / sɔft ɹoʊ / (AmE), / sɒft rəʊ / (BrE)
the sperm or sperm-filled reproductive gland of a fish, used as food, with a creamy texture

Examples

Grilled soft roe
Grilled soft roe is considered a delicacy in some parts of Europe.
Some people are unfamiliar with soft roe, but it is prized in traditional Japanese cuisine.
The chef prepared a creamy pate using fresh soft roe from river trout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hard roe - the eggs of a female fish, as distinct from soft roe
