  • superfluity
    8.7 0.12/m

  • surplusage
    9.9 0.06/m
    surplusage appears in legal or old texts to mean extra material, while excess is everyday language for too much.
  • superabundance
    7.9 0.16/m

  • overabundance
    5.7 0.45/m

  • plethora
    7.7 3.30/m

  • overplus
    9.8 0.00/m
    overplus is an old-fashioned word for extra remaining goods, while excess is the common modern word for too much.
