slug

noun
a heavy punch with the fist
Pronunciation: / slʌɡ / (AmE), / slʌɡ / (BrE)
a heavy blow with the fist, especially in boxing or informal fighting

Examples

A slug to the face
After taking a slug to the stomach, he doubled over in pain.
He gave his brother a quick slug on the arm for teasing him.
The boxer delivered a powerful slug to his opponent’s jaw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clout - a heavy blow with the hand or a hard object
  • synonym: punch - to strike with a closed fist
  • degree lower: tap - a light or gentle blow
