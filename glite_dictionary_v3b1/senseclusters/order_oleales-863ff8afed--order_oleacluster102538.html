order Oleales

noun
a plant order including the olive family
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər oʊˈliːəˌliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əʊˈliːəˌliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of flowering plants, coextensive with the family Oleaceae, sometimes included in the order Gentianales in some classifications

Examples

The order Oleales
Botanists sometimes debate whether the order Oleales should be merged with Gentianales.
The order Oleales contains plants such as olives, lilacs, and jasmines.
The order Oleales is recognized by its members' opposite leaves and four-part flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Oleaceae - the olive family, the main family within order Oleales
