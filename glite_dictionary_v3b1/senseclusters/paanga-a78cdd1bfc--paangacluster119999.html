pa'anga

noun
currency unit of Tonga
Pronunciation: / pɑˈɑːŋə / (AmE), / pɑˈɑːŋə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Tonga, equal to 100 seniti.

Examples

One pa'anga coin
A single pa'anga is divided into one hundred seniti.
She exchanged her dollars for pa'anga at the airport upon arrival in Nuku'alofa.
The price of bread in Tonga is usually listed in pa'anga on shop shelves.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: seniti - the fractional monetary unit of Tonga (1/100th of a pa'anga)
