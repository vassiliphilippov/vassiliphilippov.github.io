overrich

adjective
excessively rich
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈrɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈrɪtʃ / (BrE)
containing or having too much richness, often to an excessive or unpleasant degree

Examples

An overrich cake
The chocolate cake was so overrich that I could only eat a small piece.
Some perfumes are overrich and can be overwhelming in a small room.
He found the sauce overrich and preferred something lighter with his meal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - lacking strong features or richness
  • synonym: cloying - so sweet or rich as to be unpleasant
  • degree lower: rich - having a lot of richness or intensity
