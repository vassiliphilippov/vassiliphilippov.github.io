antianxiety agent

noun
drug that alleviates anxiety
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiænˈzaɪəti ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌæntiænˈzaɪəti ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a drug used to reduce stress or tension without affecting mental clarity

Examples

An antianxiety agent drug
Long-term use of an antianxiety agent should be monitored by a healthcare professional.
Some patients may require an antianxiety agent before undergoing surgery to alleviate preoperative stress.
The doctor prescribed an antianxiety agent to help manage her panic attacks.
