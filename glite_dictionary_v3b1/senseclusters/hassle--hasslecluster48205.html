hassle

verb
annoy or bother persistently
Pronunciation: / ˈhæsəl / (AmE), / ˈhæsəl / (BrE)
to annoy or trouble someone repeatedly or persistently

Examples

To hassle people
She felt hassled by constant calls from telemarketers.
Stop hassling me about my homework—I said I'd get it done.
The manager hassles his team over minor mistakes every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bother - to cause minor annoyance to someone
  • synonym: harass - to annoy or trouble someone repeatedly
