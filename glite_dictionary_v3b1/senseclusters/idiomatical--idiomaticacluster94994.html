idiomatical

adjective
relating to idiom or idiomatic style
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪdioʊˈmætɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɪdiəˈmætɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of idiom, or conforming to the natural expressions of a language.

Examples

An idiomatical expression
Her speech was remarkably idiomatical for a non-native speaker.

The translator struggled to find an idiomatical equivalent in the target language.
