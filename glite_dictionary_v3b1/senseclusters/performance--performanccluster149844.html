performance

noun
manner of functioning or operating
3.0 68/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàf…îrm…ôns / (AmE), / p…ôÀàf…îÀêm…ôns / (BrE)
the way in which a machine, system, or process functions or operates

Examples

Performance of the engine
After years of use, the car's performance started to decline.
The new software improved the overall performance of the computer.
They tested the performance of several engines under different conditions.

Synonyms

  • functioning
    3.4 11/m
    functioning shows how a machine runs in practice, while performance describes its overall quality, speed, and reliability.
  • operation
    3.4 34/m
    operation means the action or process of a machine working, while performance focuses on how well the machine does that work.
  • system performance
    3.7 0.46/m
    system performance measures a system's speed, reliability, and resource use, while performance is the general way the system operates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: malfunction - failure to function properly
  • synonym: operation - the fact or condition of functioning or being active
