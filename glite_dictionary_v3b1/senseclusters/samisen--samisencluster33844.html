samisen

noun
Japanese three-stringed instrument
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmɪsɛn / (AmE), / ˈsæmɪsɛn / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese stringed instrument with a long neck, three strings, a fretless fingerboard, and a rectangular soundbox, played with a plectrum.

Examples

Traditional samisen instrument
Long ago, the samisen accompanied many traditional Japanese dramas and dances.
She purchased a samisen as a souvenir of her trip to Japan.
The musician played a haunting melody on the samisen during the performance.
