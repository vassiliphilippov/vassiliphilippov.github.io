Black Pope

noun
powerful Jesuit leader or influential Vatican figure
9.9
Pronunciation: / blæk poʊp / (AmE), / blæk pəʊp / (BrE)
a colloquial or conspiratorial term for the Superior General of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits) or any powerful, behind-the-scenes figure in the Catholic Church, allegedly wielding great influence in the Vatican

Examples

The Black Pope leader
Historically, the Superior General of the Jesuits has been nicknamed the Black Pope.
Some conspiracy theories allege that the Black Pope has more power than the actual Pope.
The media sometimes refers to hidden Vatican power brokers as the Black Pope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Pope - the Bishop of Rome and leader of the Catholic Church
  • synonym: Superior General - the chief superior of a religious order, especially the Jesuits
