tightener

noun
something that restricts
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtənər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtənə / (BrE)
a person, policy, or thing that makes rules, conditions, or resources stricter or less available

Examples

A policy tightener
The government introduced a new tightener to control spending in public departments.
For many borrowers, the bank's latest policy was a significant tightener on loan approvals.
The updated regulations acted as a tightener for companies hoping to enter the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relaxer - a person or thing that makes rules or conditions less strict
  • synonym: restriction - a rule or condition that limits what is possible
