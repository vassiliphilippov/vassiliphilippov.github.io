Jinghpo

noun
a Tibeto-Burman language of Myanmar and nearby regions
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋ.poʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋ.pəʊ / (BrE)
the language of the Jingpo people, a member of the Kachinic branch of the Tibeto-Burman language family, spoken primarily in northern Myanmar and surrounding areas

Examples

Jinghpo language
Her thesis focused on the grammar and vocabulary of Jinghpo.
Several schools in Kachin State now offer classes in Jinghpo as part of their curriculum.
The government launched a program to preserve the Jinghpo language among younger generations.
