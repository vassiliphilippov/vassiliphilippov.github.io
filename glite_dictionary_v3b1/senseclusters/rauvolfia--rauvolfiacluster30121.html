rauvolfia

noun
genus of plants used in medicine
Pronunciation: / raʊˈvɑlfiə / (AmE), / raʊˈvɒlfɪə / (BrE)
A genus of shrubs and small trees native to tropical regions, with leaves in whorls and cymose flowers, yielding substances used medicinally as emetics, purgatives, or antihypertensive agents.
