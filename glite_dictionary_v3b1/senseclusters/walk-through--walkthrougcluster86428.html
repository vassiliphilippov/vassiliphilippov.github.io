walk-through

noun
pedestrian passageway through a building
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔk.θru / (AmE), / ˈwɔːk.θruː / (BrE)
A covered pedestrian passageway allowing people to walk through the ground floor of a building.

Examples

Covered walk-through
Security cameras were installed in the walk-through for the safety of residents and visitors.
The new apartment complex features a well-lit walk-through connecting the main street to the courtyard.
We used the walk-through to reach the other side of the building during the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: passageway - a hall or corridor that allows passage through a building
