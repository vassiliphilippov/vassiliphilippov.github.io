hard-nosed approach

noun
pragmatic, tough method or attitude
9.0
Pronunciation: / hɑrd ˈnoʊzd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / hɑːd ˈnəʊzd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a tough, realistic, and practical method or attitude, especially one that is determined and uncompromising

Examples

A hard-nosed approach to problems
Journalists admired her hard-nosed approach to uncovering the truth.
Sometimes a hard-nosed approach is necessary to solve difficult problems.
The manager's hard-nosed approach to negotiations led to a favorable deal for the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pragmatic approach - a sensible and practical way of dealing with things
  • antonym: soft approach - a gentle or lenient method or attitude
