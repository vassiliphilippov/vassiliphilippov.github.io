Sabinea carinalis

noun
tropical tree with crimson flowers from Dominica
Pronunciation: / səˈbin.i.ə kəˈrɪn.ə.lɪs / (AmE), / səˈbiː.ni.ə kəˈrɪn.ə.lɪs / (BrE)
A small tree native to Dominica, notable for its clusters of large crimson flowers that bloom before its pinnate leaves appear.
