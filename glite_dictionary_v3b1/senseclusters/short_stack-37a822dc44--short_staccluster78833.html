short stack

noun
small stack, esp. of food (e.g. pancakes)
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt stæk / (AmE), / ʃɔːt stæk / (BrE)
a small stack or pile of items, especially a small serving of pancakes or other food items

Examples

A short stack of pancakes
For brunch, she served each guest a short stack topped with maple syrup.
He ordered a short stack of pancakes at the breakfast diner.
The librarian handed me a short stack of books to review.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stack - a neat pile of objects arranged one on top of another
