pivotal

adjective
crucially important; central
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪv.ə.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈpɪv.ə.təl / (BrE)
Of crucial importance; central or key to the success or outcome of something.

Examples

A pivotal role
His evidence proved to be pivotal in solving the case.
The discovery played a pivotal role in the development of modern medicine.
The meeting was a pivotal moment in her career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central - of greatest importance; principal or essential
  • synonym: crucial - of great importance or significance
  • antonym: minor - lesser in importance, seriousness, or significance
