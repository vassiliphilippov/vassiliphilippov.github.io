cheerleading

noun
sport of leading cheers
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrˌliːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəˌliːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport or activity in which people lead organized cheers and perform routines to support a team at events

Examples

School cheerleading team
She joined the cheerleading team to support her school's basketball games.
Many students enjoy cheerleading because it combines dance, gymnastics, and teamwork.
The cheerleading squad practiced their new routine for the upcoming competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dance - a series of movements set to music, often performed as entertainment
  • synonym: gymnastics - a sport involving exercises and performances requiring physical strength and flexibility
