Bonaire

proper noun
Caribbean island; part of the Netherlands
Pronunciation: / boʊˈnɛr / (AmE), / bəʊˈneə / (BrE)
An island in the Caribbean, part of the Netherlands, known for its popular resorts and as a destination for diving, with its capital at Kralendijk.

Examples

Bonaire island
Many tourists visit Bonaire each year to enjoy its world-class scuba diving sites.

The capital city of Bonaire is Kralendijk, known for its vibrant waterfront.
