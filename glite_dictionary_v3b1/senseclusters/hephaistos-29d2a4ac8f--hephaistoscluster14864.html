Hephaistos

proper noun
Greek god of fire and metalworking
9.9
Pronunciation: /hɪ.ˈfaɪ.stəs/ /; / /hə.ˈfaɪ.stəs/ (AmE), /hɪ.ˈfaɪ.stɒs/ /; / /hə.ˈfaɪ.stɒs/ (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the god of fire, metalworking, and craftsmanship, often depicted as lame; identified with the Roman god Vulcan.

Examples

In ancient myths, Hephaistos crafted armor for the gods and heroes.
