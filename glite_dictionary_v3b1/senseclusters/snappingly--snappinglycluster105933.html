snappingly

adverb
with a sudden snap
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsnæpɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes or makes a sudden, sharp snapping or clicking sound

Examples

The lid shuts snappingly
The lid closed snappingly, making a loud sound in the quiet room.
He attached the bracelet snappingly around her wrist before she noticed.
The pieces fit together snappingly, locking into place with a satisfying click.
The fire crackled snappingly in the quiet cabin as the night grew colder.
She closed the book snappingly, startling everyone in the room.
The dog barked snappingly at the stranger approaching the gate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sharply - in a sudden or abrupt manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a gentle or even manner without sudden movement
  • antonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet manner
