misgovernance

noun
bad government
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsɡəˈvɜrnəns / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈɡʌvənəns /; / ˌmɪsɡəˈvɜːnəns / (BrE)
the act or state of governing in a poor or ineffective way, often leading to negative consequences

Examples

Years of misgovernance
Years of misgovernance resulted in widespread poverty and unrest in the region.
The report highlighted several cases of misgovernance within the public health sector.
Citizens demanded accountability after evidence of misgovernance surfaced in the city council.
Years of misgovernance left the hospitals underfunded and the public trust shattered.
The report blamed widespread misgovernance for the country's economic crisis.
Citizens protested against the misgovernance that led to failing public services.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good governance - the act of governing in an effective and responsible way
  • synonym: maladministration - inefficient or dishonest administration
  • synonym: mismanagement - the process of managing something badly or wrongly
