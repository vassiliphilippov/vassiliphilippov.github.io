redemptively

adverb
in a redeeming way
8.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdɛmptɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈdɛmptɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves repaying, buying back, or cancelling a debt or obligation through redemption

Examples

To pay redemptively
The company acted redemptively by settling its debts ahead of schedule.
He spoke redemptively about the need to correct past financial mistakes.
The bonds were redemptively repurchased before their maturity date.
