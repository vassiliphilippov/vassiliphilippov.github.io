sunrise industry

noun
new and rapidly growing industry
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌraɪz ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈsʌnraɪz ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
an industry that is new and expanding rapidly, often associated with innovation and growth

Examples

A sunrise industry
Many entrepreneurs are entering the technology sector, viewing it as a sunrise industry.
Renewable energy is considered a sunrise industry due to increasing global demand.
The government plans to invest more in sunrise industries to boost economic growth.
