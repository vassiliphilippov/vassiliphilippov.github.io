sipper

noun
drinking cup with spout
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪp.ə/ (BrE)
a cup, bottle, or small container (often with a spout or straw) used for drinking liquids easily or slowly

Examples
