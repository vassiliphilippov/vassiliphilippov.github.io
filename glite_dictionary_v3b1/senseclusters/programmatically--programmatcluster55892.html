programmatically

adverb
in an organized way
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊɡrəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌprəʊɡrəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a planned, organized, or systematic way as part of a program or policy

Examples

Programmatically allocate funds
The organization allocates resources programmatically to ensure every region receives support.
Funds are distributed programmatically according to the guidelines set by the board.
The new policy will be implemented programmatically over the next three years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: randomly - without a plan or pattern
  • synonym: systematically - in a methodical and organized way
