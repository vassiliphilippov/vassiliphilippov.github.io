civic pride

noun
pride in one's city or community
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪk ˌpraɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪk ˌpraɪd / (BrE)
a feeling of satisfaction and responsibility towards one's city, shown by its citizens

Examples

Civic pride in city
A sense of civic pride inspired people to volunteer for the city's clean-up day.
Her civic pride was evident in the way she spoke about her hometown.
The festival was organized to foster civic pride among residents.
