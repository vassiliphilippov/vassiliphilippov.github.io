unconfounded

adjective
not confused or mixed
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈfaʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfaʊndɪd / (BrE)
not mixed up, confused, or influenced by other factors; clear and distinct in meaning or effect

Examples

The unconfounded results
The experiment produced unconfounded results, making the findings more reliable.
Researchers worked hard to ensure their data remained unconfounded by outside influences.
It is important that the analysis is unconfounded to draw accurate conclusions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confounded - mixed up with something else so that the true effect is unclear
  • synonym: unambiguous - clear and not open to more than one interpretation
