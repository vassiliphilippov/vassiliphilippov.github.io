skin

noun
a person's life or safety (figurative)
2.5
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
(figurative) A person's life or personal safety, especially regarded as something to be preserved or protected.

Examples

Save his skin
He was only interested in saving his own skin when trouble started.
She risked her skin to help the refugees escape.
They lied to protect their political skins from scandal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neck - figurative term for personal safety or life, as in 'save one's neck'
