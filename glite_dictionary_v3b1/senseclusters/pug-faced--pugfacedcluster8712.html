pug-faced

adjective
having a flat, wrinkled face (like a pug)
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌɡ feɪst / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡ feɪst / (BrE)
Having a short, flat, and wrinkled face like that of a pug dog.

Examples
