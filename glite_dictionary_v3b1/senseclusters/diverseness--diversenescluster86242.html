diverseness

noun
the state of being diverse; variety
2.8
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɜrsnəs / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜːsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being diverse; variety or difference within a group, set, or collection

Examples

The diverseness of ideas
Her projects show a remarkable diverseness in approach and style.
The diverseness of the city's population leads to a rich cultural scene.
The museum prides itself on the diverseness of its art collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diversity - the quality or state of having many different forms, types, or ideas
  • antonym: uniformity - the quality or state of being the same; lack of variety
