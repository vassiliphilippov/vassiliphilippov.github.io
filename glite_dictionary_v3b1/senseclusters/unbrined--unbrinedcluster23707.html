unbrined

adjective
not soaked in brine
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbraɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbraɪnd / (BrE)
not soaked or treated in brine, especially referring to food that has not been salted in a brine solution

Examples

Unbrined turkey
The chef prefers to use unbrined turkey for a more natural flavor.
Some people find unbrined olives less salty and more appealing.
The instructions specify that only unbrined cheese should be used in this dish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brined - soaked or treated in a saltwater solution
