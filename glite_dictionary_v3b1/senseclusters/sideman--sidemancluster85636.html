sideman

noun
supporting musician
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdmæn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdmæn / (BrE)
a musician who plays in a band or group but is not the main performer or leader

Examples

Jazz sideman
The talented sideman played saxophone during the jazz concert.
As a sideman, he supported many famous singers throughout his career.
She started as a sideman before becoming a solo artist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: band member - a person who plays in a musical group
  • antonym: frontman - the main singer or leader of a band
