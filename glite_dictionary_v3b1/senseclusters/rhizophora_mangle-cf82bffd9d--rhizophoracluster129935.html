Rhizophora mangle

noun
species of red mangrove tree
Pronunciation: / raɪˌzɑːfəˈrɑː ˈmæŋɡəl / (AmE), / raɪˌzɒfəˈrɔː ˈmaŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
A tropical tree or shrub, commonly known as the red mangrove, with prop roots that form dense thickets in coastal regions and fruit that germinates while still attached to the tree.
