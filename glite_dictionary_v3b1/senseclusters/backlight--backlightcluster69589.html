backlight

noun
light behind object
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.laɪt/ (BrE)
a light source positioned behind an object, especially used to illuminate screens or create visual effects

Examples

Phone backlight
The backlight on my phone makes it easy to read messages at night.
Photographers often use a backlight to create a glowing outline around their subjects.
If the backlight fails, the laptop screen becomes very hard to see.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illumination - the act of lighting something up
  • antonym: spotlight - a strong beam of light focused on a particular area
