communion

noun
Christian religious ceremony
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunjən / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnjən / (BrE)
a religious ceremony in which people share bread and wine to remember the Last Supper of Jesus Christ

Examples

Children often receive their first communion as an important milestone in their faith.
During the service, the priest invited everyone to participate in communion.
