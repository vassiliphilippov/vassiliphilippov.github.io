argyle

noun
diamond-shaped knit pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrˌɡaɪl /; / ˈɑrɡaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡaɪl / (BrE)
a pattern of colored diamonds, often used in knitted clothing such as socks and sweaters, or a sock with this pattern
