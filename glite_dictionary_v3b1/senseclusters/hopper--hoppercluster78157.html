hopper

noun
baseball hit that bounces along the ground
8.1 0.45/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêp…ôr / (AmE), / Ààh…íp…ô / (BrE)
(Baseball) A hit that travels along the ground, usually bouncing one or more times before being fielded.

Examples

A slow hopper to third
He hit a slow hopper to third that gave the runner time to reach home.
The batter managed only a weak hopper that barely left the infield.
The shortstop fielded the hopper and threw to first base for the out.

Synonyms

  • groundball
