perseveringly

adverb
in a persevering manner
3.3
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪvərɪŋli / (AmE), / pəˈsɪːvərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows perseverance; with steady and determined effort despite difficulty or delay.

Examples

To perseveringly practice
He perseveringly practiced every day until he finally mastered the piece.
She worked perseveringly towards her goal, refusing to give up despite many setbacks.
The team pursued their research perseveringly, even when early results were discouraging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: persistently - in a continuing manner despite obstacles
