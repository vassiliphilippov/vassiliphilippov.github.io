free

adjective
completely without; lacking
2.3
Pronunciation: / friː / (AmE), / friː / (BrE)
Not containing or affected by; completely without.

Examples

Free of errors
His writing was completely free of unnecessary jargon.
She prefers snacks that are free from artificial flavors and preservatives.
The report was remarkably free of factual errors.
