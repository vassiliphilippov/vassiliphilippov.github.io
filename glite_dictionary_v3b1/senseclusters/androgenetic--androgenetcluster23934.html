androgenetic

adjective
relating to androgenesis
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌændroʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌandrədʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from androgenesis, a type of reproduction involving only the male genetic material

Examples

Androgenetic reproduction
An androgenetic individual inherits its nuclear DNA exclusively from its father.
Certain types of plants can be bred using androgenetic techniques to produce male-derived offspring.
The biologists studied the androgenetic development of the embryos in their experiment.
