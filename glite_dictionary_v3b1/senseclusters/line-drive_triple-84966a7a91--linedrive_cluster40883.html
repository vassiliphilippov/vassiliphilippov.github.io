line-drive triple

noun
baseball: triple from a line drive
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪn ˌdraɪv ˈtrɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn ˌdrʌɪv ˈtrɪpəl / (BrE)
In baseball, a triple that is achieved when the batter hits a line drive.

Examples

Line-drive triple hit
He stunned the crowd with a line-drive triple in the bottom of the eighth inning.
Her speed turned what seemed like a double into an impressive line-drive triple.
The batter hit a line-drive triple to right field, driving in two runs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: line drive - a sharply hit baseball that travels in a straight, flat trajectory
  • synonym: triple - a hit in baseball allowing the batter to reach third base
