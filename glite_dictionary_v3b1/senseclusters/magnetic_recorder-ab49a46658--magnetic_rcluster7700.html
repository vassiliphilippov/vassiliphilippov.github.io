magnetic recorder

noun
device for recording on magnetic media
3.5
Pronunciation: / mægˈnɛtɪk rɪˈkɔrdər / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪk rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
a device used for recording sound, images, or data on magnetic media such as tape or disks

Examples

A magnetic recorder device

Many documentaries from the 1970s were preserved on magnetic recorders before digital technology became widespread.
The journalist used a magnetic recorder to capture the entire interview for later transcription.
