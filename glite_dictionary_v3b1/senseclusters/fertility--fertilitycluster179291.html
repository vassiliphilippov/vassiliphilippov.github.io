Examples

Fertility rate
Comparing the fertility of different regions helps demographers understand population trends.
Recent economic changes have led to a decline in the fertility of many countries.
The government launched new programs to increase rural fertility rates.

Synonyms

    2.8 0.90/m

  • birth rate
    2.5 2.60/m
    birth rate is the common everyday phrase for live births per 1,000 people, while fertility names that population rate.
  • natality
    9.2 0.01/m
    natality is a formal technical term for births per 1,000 people, while fertility is the more general population birth rate.
