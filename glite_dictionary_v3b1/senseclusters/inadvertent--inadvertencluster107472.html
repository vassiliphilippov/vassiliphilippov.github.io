Not done intentionally; happening by accident or without being intended.

Examples

An inadvertent mistake
His inadvertent comment about the surprise party almost ruined the secret.
She made an inadvertent error in her calculations, which caused a delay in the project.
The fire was started by an inadvertent spark from the machinery.

Synonyms

  • unintentional
    4.1 2.38/m
    unintentional means not done on purpose in everyday speech, while inadvertent is a slightly more formal word used in writing.
  • unintended
    4.1 5.43/m
    unintended often describes an unexpected result or effect, while inadvertent focuses on an action not done on purpose.
  • accidental
    3.0 6.54/m
    accidental means happening by chance or without planning, while inadvertent stresses that someone did not mean to do the action.
  • unpremeditated
    7.5 0.11/m
    unpremeditated means done without prior planning, often as a sudden act, while inadvertent highlights lack of conscious intent.
  • unwitting
    6.7 1.27/m
    unwitting describes acting without knowing or being aware, while inadvertent describes an action that happens without deliberate intent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned
  • antonym: intentional - done on purpose, deliberately
