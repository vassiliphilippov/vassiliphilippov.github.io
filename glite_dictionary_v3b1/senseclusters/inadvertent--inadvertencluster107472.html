inadvertent

adjective
unintentional; accidental
6.6 5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.əd.ˈvɜːr.tənt/ /; / /ɪn.æd.ˈvɜːr.tənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.əd.ˈvɜː.tənt/ /; / /ɪn.æd.ˈvɜː.tənt/ (BrE)
Not done intentionally; happening by accident or without being intended.

Examples

An inadvertent mistake
His inadvertent comment about the surprise party almost ruined the secret.
She made an inadvertent error in her calculations, which caused a delay in the project.
The fire was started by an inadvertent spark from the machinery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned
  • antonym: intentional - done on purpose, deliberately
