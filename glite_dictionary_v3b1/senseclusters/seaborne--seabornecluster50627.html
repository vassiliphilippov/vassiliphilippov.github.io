seaborne

adjective
conveyed by sea
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈsiːbɔːn / (BrE)
(of goods, people, or activities) conveyed or carried by sea.

Examples

Seaborne cargo

Rising fuel costs have made seaborne transport more expensive in recent years.
The seaborne invasion was launched at dawn under cover of fog.
