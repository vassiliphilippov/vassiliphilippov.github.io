spruce

noun
evergreen tree of genus Picea
Pronunciation: / sprus / (AmE), / spruːs / (BrE)
A type of evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Picea, with needle-like leaves and cones, commonly found in northern temperate regions.

Examples

A spruce tree
A rare species of spruce can only be found in this region.
The forest was filled with tall spruce trees covered in snow.
We decorated the living room with branches from a spruce during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fir - an evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Abies
  • synonym: pine - an evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Pinus
