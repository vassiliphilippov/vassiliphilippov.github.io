Acoma

proper noun
Native American people
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈækoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈækəmə / (BrE)
a Native American people of New Mexico and the name of their historic pueblo, also known as Sky City

Examples

Acoma people
The Acoma have lived in their pueblo for centuries, preserving unique traditions and crafts.
Tourists visit the Acoma Pueblo, often called Sky City, to learn about its history and culture.
Many members of the Acoma community participate in annual festivals and ceremonies.
