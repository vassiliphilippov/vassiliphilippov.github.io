public lavatory

noun
toilet facility open to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈlævətɔri / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈlævətəri / (BrE)
a toilet facility that is open and available for use by the general public, usually in a public place

Examples

A public lavatory
Many tourists rely on finding a public lavatory when exploring unfamiliar cities.
She asked a police officer where the nearest public lavatory was located.
The city council installed a new public lavatory near the park entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public toilet - a toilet available for public use
  • synonym: restroom - a room with toilets for public use (mainly US)
