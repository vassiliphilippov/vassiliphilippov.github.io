booboisie

noun
group considered naive or foolish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbuːboʊˈwɑːzi / (AmE), / ˌbuːbəˈwɑːziː / (BrE)
the class or group of people regarded as naive, unintelligent, or lacking in sophistication

Examples

The booboisie group
He dismissed his critics as mere members of the booboisie, unwilling to think for themselves.
Mencken often wrote scathing critiques of the booboisie and their influence on American culture.
The satirical article poked fun at the booboisie for their unquestioning acceptance of popular trends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bourgeoisie - the middle class, typically with reference to materialistic values or conventional attitudes
