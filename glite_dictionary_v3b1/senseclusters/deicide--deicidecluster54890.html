deicide

noun
destruction of a god
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.ə.saɪd/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.ə.saɪd/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.ə.saɪd/ /; / /ˈdeɪ.ə.saɪd/ (BrE)
the rejection, overthrow, or destruction of the authority or influence of a god, especially used figuratively.

Examples

Cultural deicide
Some philosophers have discussed the idea of cultural deicide in the context of modern secular societies.
The novel explores the consequences of a symbolic deicide in a world losing its faith.
Historians debated whether the revolution amounted to a political deicide by removing religious influence from government.
