bunch

noun
group of similar things
Pronunciation: / bʌntʃ / (AmE), / bʌntʃ / (BrE)
A number of things of the same type that are held or grouped together.

Examples

A bunch of grapes
A bunch of keys dangled from his belt as he walked.
She handed me a bunch of fresh flowers from her garden.
We saw a bunch of kids playing soccer in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cluster - a group of similar things or people positioned or occurring closely together
