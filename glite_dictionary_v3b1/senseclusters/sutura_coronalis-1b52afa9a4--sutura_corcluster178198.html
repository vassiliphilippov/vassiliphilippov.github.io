sutura coronalis

noun
suture between frontal and parietal skull bones
Pronunciation: / suːˌtʊrə kɔːˈroʊnəlɪs / (AmE), / suːˌtjʊərə kɒˈrəʊnəlɪs / (BrE)
the suture between the frontal and parietal bones of the skull

Examples

The sutura coronalis bone
Damage to the sutura coronalis can affect the shape of the skull during development.
Surgeons must be aware of the location of the sutura coronalis during cranial procedures.
The sutura coronalis is a key landmark in cranial anatomy.
