willful neglect

noun
deliberate failure to care for responsibilities
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪlfəl nəˈɡlɛkt / (AmE), / ˈwɪlfʊl nɪˈɡlɛkt / (BrE)
deliberate and intentional failure to care for someone or something one is responsible for

Examples

Willful neglect of duties
His habitual lateness was seen not as forgetfulness, but as willful neglect of his obligations.
Social services investigated the parents for willful neglect of their children.
The company was sued for willful neglect of safety standards, resulting in several injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negligence - failure to take proper care of something or someone
