piker

noun
person who quits
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪkə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who withdraws from an activity, agreement, or challenge, especially at the last moment

Examples

A quitting piker
Everyone was surprised when the usually enthusiastic player became a piker and left the team.
Don't be a piker; we need your help to finish this project.
He was called a piker for backing out of the bet at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity
  • synonym: quitter - a person who gives up easily or does not finish something
