do by halves

verb
do something incompletely or with little effort
9.4
Pronunciation: / duː baɪ hælvz / (AmE), / duː baɪ hɑːlvz / (BrE)
to do something incompletely, or without full effort or commitment

Examples

To do by halves work
He tends to do by halves and leave jobs unfinished.
If you’re going to help, don’t do by halves—put your heart into it.
She never does by halves when it comes to organizing an event.
