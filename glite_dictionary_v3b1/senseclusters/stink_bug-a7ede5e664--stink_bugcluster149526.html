stink bug

noun
unpleasant person
8.4
Pronunciation: / stɪŋk bʌɡ / (AmE), / stɪŋk bʌɡ / (BrE)
an informal, derogatory term for a person who is unpleasant, annoying, or disagreeable

Examples

He is a stink bug
Nobody wanted to sit next to the stink bug in class because he was always rude.
She called her brother a stink bug after he played another prank on her.
At the meeting, his constant complaints made him seem like a real stink bug.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delight - a person who is very pleasant or enjoyable
  • synonym: nuisance - a person or thing causing inconvenience or annoyance
  • synonym: pest - an annoying or troublesome person
