Synonyms

  • confidentially
    4.4 0.5/m
    confidentially is used in formal settings to keep information secret and limited, while privately is more general and everyday.
  • in private
    2.4 12/m
    in private means being with only close people and away from others, while privately can mean not public or not shared.
  • secretly
    1.4 12/m
    secretly is done by hiding actions or facts from others, while privately can mean mere personal privacy without hiding.
  • in secret
    2.5 3/m
