tubule

noun
a small tube, especially in biology
7.7 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtuÀê.bjuÀêl / (AmE), / ÀàtjuÀê.bjuÀêl / (BrE)
a very small tube, especially one found in biological structures or tissues

Examples

A tubule in tissue
Damage to a renal tubule can affect the body's ability to regulate fluids.
Each kidney contains many narrow tubules that filter waste from the blood.
The laboratory examined a tubule structure found inside the plant stem.

Synonyms

  • ductule
    9.7 0.00/m
    ductule is a tiny body channel that mainly carries fluids like bile or tears, while tubule refers to any very small tube in tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tube - a long, hollow cylinder used to move or contain liquids or gases
