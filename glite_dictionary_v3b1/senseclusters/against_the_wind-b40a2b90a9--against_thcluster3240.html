Synonyms

  • into the wind
    3.9 1.16/m
    into the wind means moving directly toward the air that pushes on you, while against the wind describes moving opposite to it.
  • upwind
    3.5 0.12/m
    upwind describes moving toward the source of the wind, while against the wind is a common phrase for moving opposite to it.
  • windward
    7.5 0.00/m
    windward means on the side facing the wind or toward its source, while against the wind focuses on moving opposite the wind.
  • to the windward
    8.1 0.01/m
    to the windward is used in sailing to mean toward the wind, while against the wind is a general term for moving into it.
