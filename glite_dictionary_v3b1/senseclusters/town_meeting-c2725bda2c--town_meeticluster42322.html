town meeting

noun
meeting of town residents for community matters
3.2
Pronunciation: / taʊn ˈmitɪŋ /; / taʊn ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / taʊn ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting of the people in a town to discuss or make decisions about local issues or government

Examples

The town meeting discussion
At the town meeting, residents raised concerns about taxes and public safety.
Many important local decisions are made during the town meeting.
The annual town meeting will be held at the community center next Saturday.
At the town meeting, residents voted to approve the new budget proposal.
Every spring, the community gathers for the annual town meeting to discuss important local issues.
The town meeting system encourages direct participation in government decisions.
