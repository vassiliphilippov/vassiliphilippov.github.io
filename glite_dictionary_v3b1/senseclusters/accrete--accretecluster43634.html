accrete

verb
to grow by gradual accumulation
8.2
Pronunciation: / əˈkrit / (AmE), / əˈkriːt / (BrE)
to grow or increase by gradual accumulation, or to become attached by accretion

Examples

To accrete layers
A new narrative began to accrete around the historical events as more stories were told.
Mineral deposits accrete on the surface of the cave walls over time.
Over millions of years, planets can accrete material from surrounding dust and gas.
