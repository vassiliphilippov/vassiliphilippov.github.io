fueling

noun
supplying or taking on fuel
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːəlɪŋ /; / ˈfjulɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of supplying or taking on fuel, especially to a vehicle, engine, or machine.

Examples

Proper fueling procedures
Proper fueling procedures are essential for the safety of the ship.
The fueling of the aircraft was completed before sunrise.
The pit crew managed fueling quickly during the race.
After several hours of fuelling, the ships were ready to continue their voyage.
Proper fuelling is essential for the smooth operation of the generator.
The fuelling of the aircraft was completed in less than twenty minutes.
