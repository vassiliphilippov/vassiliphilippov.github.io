bird of prey

noun
predatory bird such as an eagle or hawk
Pronunciation: / bɝd əv preɪ /; / ˌbɝːd əv ˈpreɪ / (AmE), / bɜːd əv preɪ /; / ˌbɜːd əv ˈpreɪ / (BrE)
a bird, such as an eagle, hawk, or owl, that hunts, kills, and eats other animals
