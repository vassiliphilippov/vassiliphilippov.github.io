nosy

adjective
offensively inquisitive; overly interested in others
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzi / (BrE)
Showing too much interest in other people's private affairs; offensively or annoyingly inquisitive.

Examples

A nosy neighbour
I wish you weren’t so nosy about my private calls.
My nosy coworker is always asking personal questions about my family.
The nosy neighbor peeked over the fence to see what we were doing in the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discreet - careful not to cause embarrassment or attract too much attention
  • synonym: inquisitive - eager for knowledge, often in a way that may be intrusive
  • synonym: prying - enquiring impertinently or unnecessarily into something
