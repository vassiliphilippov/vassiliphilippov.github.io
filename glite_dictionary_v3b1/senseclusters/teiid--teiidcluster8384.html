teiid

noun
a tropical New World lizard (family Teiidae)
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtiː.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈtiː.ɪd / (BrE)
A tropical New World lizard belonging to the family Teiidae, characterized by a long tail and large rectangular scales on the belly.

Examples

A teiid lizard
Many teiids exhibit bright coloration to warn off predators.

The teiid darted across the sand and disappeared beneath a bush.
