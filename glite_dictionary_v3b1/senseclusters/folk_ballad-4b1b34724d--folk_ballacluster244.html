folk ballad

noun
traditional song from common people
5.2
Pronunciation: / foʊk ˈbæl.əd / (AmE), / fəʊk ˈbæl.əd / (BrE)
A traditional narrative song sung by ordinary people, forming part of a region's cultural heritage.

Examples

A folk ballad song
Many folk ballads tell stories of love, tragedy, or historical events in simple language.
The singer captivated the audience with a haunting folk ballad passed down through generations.
This region is famous for its rich tradition of folk ballad performances at local festivals.

  • synonym: folk song - a traditional song passed orally among people
