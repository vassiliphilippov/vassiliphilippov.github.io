jaborandi

noun
medicinal South American plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːbɔːˈrændi /; / ˌdʒæbəˈrændi / (AmE), / ˌdʒæbɔːˈrændi /; / ˌdʒæbəˈrændi / (BrE)
a South American plant whose leaves are used in medicine to produce pilocarpine and other extracts, often for treating conditions like glaucoma or to promote sweating
