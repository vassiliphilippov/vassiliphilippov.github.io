cane toad invasion

noun
spread of cane toads causing ecological harm
7.8 6.1
Pronunciation: /keɪn toʊd ɪn.ˈveɪ.ʒən/ /; / /keɪn təʊd ɪn.ˈveɪ.ʒən/ (AmE), /keɪn təʊd ɪn.ˈveɪ.ʒən/ (BrE)
