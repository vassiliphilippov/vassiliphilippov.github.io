soar above the rim

verb
jumping high to score in basketball
Pronunciation: / sɔr əˈbʌv ðə rɪm / (AmE), / sɔː əˈbʌv ðə rɪm / (BrE)
to jump very high during basketball play, especially when attempting to score by dunking the ball

Examples

To soar above the rim in basketball

The crowd cheered as he soared above the rim for a thunderous dunk.
Young players dream of one day soaring above the rim on a professional court.
