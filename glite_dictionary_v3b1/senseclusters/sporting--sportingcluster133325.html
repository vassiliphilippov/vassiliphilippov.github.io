sporting

adjective
involving risk or fair opportunity
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
showing willingness to take risks or accepting uncertainty, especially in the context of a fair chance or opportunity; characterized by adventurous or chivalrous behavior.

Examples

Sporting chance
He took a sporting chance and doubled his bet on the final race.
Her sporting attitude meant she was willing to risk failure for the possibility of great success.
It's a sporting approach to give your opponent an opportunity to win.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks and try new things
  • antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence
