computer-aided design

noun
use of computers for design work
5.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpjuː.tər ˈeɪ.dɪd dɪ.ˈzaɪn/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpjuː.tə ˈeɪ.dɪd dɪ.ˈzaɪn/ (BrE)
the use of computer software to assist in creating, modifying, analyzing, or optimizing a design, especially in engineering, architecture, and manufacturing

Examples

Computer-aided design software
