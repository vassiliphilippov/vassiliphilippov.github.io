affecting

adjective
emotionally moving
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈfɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
producing strong feelings of sadness, sympathy, or pity; moving emotionally.

Examples

An affecting speech
Her affecting speech brought many listeners to tears.
It was an affecting scene that stayed with him for years.
The documentary is an affecting account of the refugees' journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moving - causing strong feelings of sadness or sympathy
  • synonym: touching - evoking strong feelings of tenderness or sorrow
  • antonym: unemotional - not showing or evoking emotion
