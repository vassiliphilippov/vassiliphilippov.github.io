handrailing

verb
to use a handrail
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhænˌdreɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhænˌdreɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
to move by holding or using a handrail for support or balance, especially while walking up or down stairs

Examples

To handrail down steps
After her injury, she carefully handrailed down the steps to avoid slipping.
The elderly man handrailed along the corridor for extra stability.
Children are often taught to handrail when using the stairs at school.
