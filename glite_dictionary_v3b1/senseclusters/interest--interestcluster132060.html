interest

noun
group with common aims in an activity
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrəst / (AmE), / ˈɪntrəst / (BrE)
(usually plural) A social group or organization whose members have common aims and control some area of activity, especially to promote or protect their shared business or professional concerns.

Examples

Business interests
Powerful financial interests played a key role in shaping the legislation.
Several interests collaborated to support the environmental initiative.
The agricultural interests opposed the new trade regulations in parliament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lobby - a group seeking to influence legislators on a particular issue
