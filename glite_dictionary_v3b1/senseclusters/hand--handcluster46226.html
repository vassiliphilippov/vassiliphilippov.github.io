hand

noun
the end part of the arm, with fingers and thumb
Pronunciation: / hænd / (AmE), / hænd / (BrE)
the part at the end of the human arm (or the forelimb of some animals) used for holding and grasping things

Examples

Wash your hands
Children should always wash their hands before meals.
He had a bandage wrapped around his injured hand.
She reached out her hand to shake his.
A kangaroo can use its strong hands to box with opponents.
Chimpanzees have remarkably dexterous hands capable of using tools.
The orangutan carefully peeled the fruit with its hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foot - the lower extremity of the leg, used for standing or walking
  • synonym: paw - the foot of an animal having claws
