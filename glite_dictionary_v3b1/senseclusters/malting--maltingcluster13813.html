malting

adjective
suitable for malt production
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
suitable for use in the process of making malt, especially referring to grain varieties intended for malting.

Examples

Malting barley variety
Farmers selected a malting barley variety to supply the local brewery.
The quality of malting wheat is crucial for producing high-grade malt.
Only certain grains are considered malting and used in the beer industry.
