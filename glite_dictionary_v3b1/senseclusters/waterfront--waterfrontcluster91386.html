waterfront

noun
land or area next to water, often in a city
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
the part of a town or city next to or along a body of water, especially where there are docks, wharves, or piers

Examples

The waterfront area
She enjoys taking evening walks on the waterfront to watch the boats.
The city's waterfront was redeveloped into a lively entertainment district.
There are many restaurants and shops along the waterfront.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dockyard - an area with docks where ships are repaired or built
  • synonym: harbor - a place on the coast where vessels may find shelter
