expectably

adverb
as expected
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛktəbli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛktəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is expected or predictable

Examples

The crowd expectably grew
Expectably, the restaurant was crowded on a Saturday night.
The results were expectably positive after months of hard work.
She expectably declined the invitation due to her busy schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predictably - in a way that can be predicted
  • antonym: surprisingly - in a way that is unexpected
