jewel

noun
person of outstanding worth or value
3.5 3.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íuÀê…ôl / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀê…ôl / (BrE)
a person who is highly valued or admired, especially for outstanding qualities; someone considered as precious as a gemstone

Examples

Family jewel
Everyone agrees that their grandmother is the real jewel of the family.
She is truly a jewel in our team, always going above and beyond.
The teacher called her student a jewel for his kindness and hard work.
