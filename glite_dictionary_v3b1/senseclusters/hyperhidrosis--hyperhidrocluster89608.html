hyperhidrosis

noun
excessive sweating disorder
6.8 7.9
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.haɪ.ˈdroʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.haɪ.ˈdrəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by excessive and profuse sweating, often more than what is needed to regulate body temperature.

Examples

Severe hyperhidrosis condition
Many people with hyperhidrosis feel self-conscious about their condition in social situations.
The patient was diagnosed with hyperhidrosis after experiencing uncontrollable sweating even at rest.
Treatments are available to help manage the symptoms of hyperhidrosis.
