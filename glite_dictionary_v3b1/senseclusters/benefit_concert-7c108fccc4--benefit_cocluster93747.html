benefit concert

noun
concert for charity
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnɪfɪt ˌkɑnsɚt / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪfɪt ˌkɒnsət / (BrE)
A concert organized to raise money or provide aid for a charitable cause.

Examples

A benefit concert for charity
Last year's benefit concert raised thousands of dollars for cancer research.
Many famous artists performed at the benefit concert for hurricane relief.
The musicians organized a benefit concert to support victims of the earthquake.
