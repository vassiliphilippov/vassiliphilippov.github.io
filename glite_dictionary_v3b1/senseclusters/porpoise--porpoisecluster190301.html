porpoise

noun
small blunt-snouted marine mammal
7.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpɔːr.pəs/ /; / /ˈpɔːr.pɪs/ (AmE), /ˈpɔː.pɪs/ /; / /ˈpɔː.pəs/ (BrE)
A small, toothed marine mammal related to whales and dolphins, with a blunt snout and a torpedo-shaped body.

Examples

A porpoise swims
Marine biologists study the behavior of the harbor porpoise in coastal waters.
Unlike dolphins, a porpoise has a shorter and more rounded snout.
We spotted a porpoise swimming playfully near the boat.
