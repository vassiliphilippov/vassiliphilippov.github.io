clankety

adjective, adverb
noisy and metallic
Pronunciation: / ˈklæŋkəti / (AmE), / ˈklæŋkəti / (BrE)
making or happening with a series of loud, metallic, clanking noises

Examples

A clankety noise
The old washing machine made a clankety noise as it finished the spin cycle.
We could hear the clankety approach of the delivery truck from down the street.
His bicycle was so clankety that everyone heard him coming before they saw him.
The old train moved clankety along the tracks, echoing through the valley.
As the wind picked up, the metal sign swung clankety against the post.
He pushed the shopping cart clankety across the uneven parking lot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clanking - making repeated loud metallic sounds
  • synonym: noisily - in a loud or disruptive manner
  • antonym: quietly - in a way that makes little or no noise
  • antonym: silent - making no sound
