Zygophyllum

noun
genus of tropical herbs and shrubs
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈɡɒfɪləm /; / zaɪˈɡɑːfɪləm / (AmE), / zaɪˈɡɒfɪləm / (BrE)
a genus of tropical or subtropical herbs or shrubs, often with ill-smelling foliage, some species of which have flower buds used as capers
