reopening

noun
opening again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of opening something again after it has been closed

Examples

The reopening of museum
The reopening of the museum attracted many visitors on its first day.
After months of renovation, the reopening of the restaurant was highly anticipated.
The government announced the reopening of schools starting next week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: closure - the act of closing something
  • synonym: opening - the act of making something open or accessible
