a type of glaucoma in which the iris blocks the drainage angle of the eye, causing a rapid increase in intraocular pressure

Examples

Acute angle-closure glaucoma
Acute angle-closure glaucoma is a medical emergency that requires immediate treatment.
Patients with angle-closure glaucoma often experience sudden eye pain and blurred vision.
Regular eye exams can help detect angle-closure glaucoma before it causes permanent vision loss.
