plexiglass

noun
weather-resistant transparent plastic material
8.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈplek.sɪ.glæs/ /; / /ˈplek.siː.glæs/ (AmE), /ˈplek.sɪ.glɑːs/ /; / /ˈplek.siː.glɑːs/ (BrE)

Examples
