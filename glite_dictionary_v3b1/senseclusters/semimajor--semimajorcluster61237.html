semimajor

adjective
half major axis
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈmeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈmeɪdʒə / (BrE)
relating to or denoting half of the major axis of an ellipse

Examples

Semimajor axis length
The semimajor axis is a key measurement in calculating the area of an ellipse.
Astronomers often refer to the semimajor axis when describing planetary orbits.
The length of the semimajor axis determines the size of the ellipse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: semiminor - relating to half the minor axis of an ellipse
