cartridge

noun
removable holder for a record player's needle
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a record player arm that holds the needle and converts vibrations into an electrical signal
