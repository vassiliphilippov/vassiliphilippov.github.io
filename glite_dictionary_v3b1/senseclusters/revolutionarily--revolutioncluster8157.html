revolutionarily

adverb
in a radical way
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛvəˌluːʃəˈnɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌrɛvəˌluːʃəˈnɛərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or causes a major and fundamental change

Examples

Revolutionarily change a product
The company revolutionarily redesigned its product to meet modern needs.
She approached the problem revolutionarily, introducing ideas no one had considered before.
The new law was revolutionarily different from previous regulations in the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservatively - in a way that avoids change or innovation
  • synonym: radically - in a way that is very different from the usual or traditional
