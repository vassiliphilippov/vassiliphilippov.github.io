colorfully

adverb
in a vivid manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚfəli / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəli / (BrE)
in a way that is bright, vivid, or full of lively colors

Examples

Colorfully decorated room
The parade floats were colorfully decorated with ribbons and flowers.
Children dressed colorfully for the school festival, wearing bright shirts and hats.
The artist painted the mural colorfully, making the wall stand out in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks brightness or vividness
  • synonym: vividly - in a way that is bright or intense in color
