seared

adjective
surface burned or browned with heat
4.5
Pronunciation: / sɪrd / (AmE), / sɪəd / (BrE)
having the surface burned or browned quickly with intense heat, especially in cooking

Examples

A seared crust
A seared crust adds flavor and texture to the fish.
The chef placed the seared steak on a warm plate before serving.
Vegetables can be quickly seared in a hot pan to bring out their sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charred - burned or scorched on the surface
  • antonym: raw - not cooked or treated with heat
