city boy

noun
male city dweller, often sophisticated
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪti bɔɪ / (BrE)
A man or boy who lives in a city, often characterized by sophisticated manners and fashionable clothing.

Examples

A city boy
Having grown up in New York, he was a true city boy with little experience of rural life.
The city boy stood out in his tailored suit and polished shoes.
Though he loved the countryside, he remained a city boy at heart, preferring the bustle of urban life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: country boy - a boy or man from a rural area, often unsophisticated or rustic
