miscategorize

verb
to assign to wrong group
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈkætəɡəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈkatəɡəraɪz /; / ˌmɪsˈkætəɡəraɪz / (BrE)
to place something in the wrong category or group by mistake
