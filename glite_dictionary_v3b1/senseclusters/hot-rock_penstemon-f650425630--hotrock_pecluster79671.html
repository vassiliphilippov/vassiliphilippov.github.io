hot-rock penstemon

noun
a cream-flowered perennial plant of the western US
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑt ˌrɑk pɛnˈstimən / (AmE), / ˌhɒt ˌrɒk pɛnˈstiːmən / (BrE)
a perennial plant with clumping stems and cream-colored flowers, native to the western United States, especially found from Washington to Wyoming and southward to California and Utah

Gardeners in Utah often plant hot-rock penstemon for its attractive cream-colored flowers.
