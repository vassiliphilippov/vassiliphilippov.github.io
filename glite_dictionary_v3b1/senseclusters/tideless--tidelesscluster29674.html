tideless

adjective
without tides
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdləs / (BrE)
not affected by tides; completely calm or still (especially of water)

Examples

Tideless lake water
The lake remained tideless throughout the summer, perfect for early morning swims.
Sailing was easy on the tideless waters of the sheltered bay.
At dawn the fishing quay lay tideless, the sea's surface unbroken and mirror-like for miles.
The calm, tideless lake was perfect for an early morning swim.
Unlike the ocean, the reservoir remained tideless throughout the year.
They enjoyed sailing on the tideless waters of the inland bay.
