fireable

adjective
easily ignited
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərəbl / (BrE)
capable of being set on fire; easily ignited or combustible

Examples

Fireable material
The old wooden barn was extremely fireable and needed extra safety precautions.
Keep fireable materials away from open flames to prevent accidents.
Because the chemicals are highly fireable, they must be stored in special containers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combustible - able to catch fire and burn easily
  • synonym: flammable - easily set on fire
  • antonym: nonflammable - not easily set on fire
