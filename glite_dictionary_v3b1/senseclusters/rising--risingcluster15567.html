rising

noun
upward movement or ascent
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
an upward movement or a process of moving to a higher position

Examples

The rising of the sun
The rising of the sun over the mountains was breathtaking.
The actor's popularity experienced a steady rising throughout the year.
There was a sudden rising in the hot-air balloon's altitude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascent - a movement upward; the act of rising or climbing
  • antonym: descent - a movement downward; the act of coming down
