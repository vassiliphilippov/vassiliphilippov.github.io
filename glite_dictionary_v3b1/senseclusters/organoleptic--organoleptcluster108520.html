organoleptic

adjective
perceived by senses
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrɡənoʊˈlɛptɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənəˈlɛptɪk / (BrE)
relating to qualities of a substance as perceived by the senses, especially taste, smell, or touch

Examples

Organoleptic properties
The organoleptic analysis of the wine included tasting and smelling for subtle differences.
Food scientists use organoleptic tests to evaluate new products before they reach the market.
The organoleptic qualities of the fruit were affected by how it was stored.
