coprostanol

noun
sewage contamination marker
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈprɑːstənɒl /; / koʊˈprɑːstəˌnɔl / (AmE), / kəʊˈprɒstənɒl / (BrE)
a chemical compound produced from cholesterol during digestion, found in human feces, and used to detect human sewage contamination
