Averrhoa

noun
genus of trees including carambola
Pronunciation: / ˌævərˈhoʊ.ə /; / ˌævərˈhoʊə / (AmE), / ˌævəˈrəʊ.ə /; / ˌævəˈrəʊə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical trees native to the East Indies, with pinnate leaves, including the carambola (star fruit)
