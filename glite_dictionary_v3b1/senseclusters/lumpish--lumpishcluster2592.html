lumpish

adjective
mentally sluggish or dull
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpɪʃ / (BrE)
slow-witted, dull, or mentally sluggish; lacking alertness or liveliness of mind.
