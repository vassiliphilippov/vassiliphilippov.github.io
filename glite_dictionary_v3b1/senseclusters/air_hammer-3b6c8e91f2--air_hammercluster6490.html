air hammer

noun
hammer powered by compressed air
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɛr ˈhæmər / (AmE), / eə ˈhamə / (BrE)
A hammering tool powered by compressed air, used especially in metalworking and construction to deliver rapid, forceful blows.

Examples

An air hammer tool
Always wear ear protection when operating an air hammer due to its loud noise.
An air hammer can make tasks like chipping and cutting metal much easier.
The mechanic used an air hammer to loosen the rusty bolts quickly.
