cheep

verb
make a high-pitched bird sound
3.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / t Éip / (AmE), / t ÉiÀêp / (BrE)
(of a young bird) to make a short, high-pitched sound.

Examples

To cheep softly
I heard the tiny birds cheeping in the early morning.
The hungry chicks cheep loudly whenever their mother returns to the nest.
Whenever they're frightened, the ducklings cheep in quick, anxious bursts.
