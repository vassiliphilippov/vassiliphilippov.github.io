normalization

noun
restoring diplomatic relations

(in politics, diplomacy) the process of restoring normal diplomatic relations between states or groups after a period of tension, conflict, or diplomatic isolation.

Examples

Normalization of relations
After decades of sanctions, the governments announced the normalisation of diplomatic ties and reopened their embassies.
Experts said the normalisation process would take years and require confidence-building measures between the nations.
Public opinion resisted the normalisation, fearing it might excuse previous human rights abuses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: estrangement - a state of separation or loss of friendly relations; the opposite of re-establishing ties
  • synonym: rapprochement - the establishment or resumption of harmonious relations, especially between countries
