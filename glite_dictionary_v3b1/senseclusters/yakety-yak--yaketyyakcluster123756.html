yakety-yak

noun
noisy or trivial chatter
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjæk.ə.tiˌjæk / (AmE), / ˈjæk.ɪ.tiˌjæk / (BrE)
noisy or trivial chatter; incessant talking, especially about unimportant matters

Examples

Endless yakety-yak
After hours of yakety-yak, the meeting finally came to an end.
I wish you would stop your yakety-yak and listen to me for a moment.
The children’s yakety-yak made it hard to concentrate on my work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - continuous, meaningless talking
  • synonym: chatter - rapid, trivial, or incessant talk
