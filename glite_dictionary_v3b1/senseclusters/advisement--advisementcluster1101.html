advisement

noun
careful consideration
9.8 5.4
Pronunciation: /əd.ˈvaɪz.mənt/ /; / /æd.ˈvaɪz.mənt/ (AmE), /əd.ˈvaɪz.mənt/ /; / /æd.ˈvaɪz.mənt/ (BrE)
careful thought or consideration, especially before making a decision (often after receiving advice)

Examples

After due advisement
After due advisement, the committee announced their final recommendation.
The judge held the case under advisement until all evidence was reviewed.
The proposal was taken under advisement before any decision was made.
The board took the new policy under advisement before announcing their decision.
After much advisement, she chose the university that best suited her goals.
The judge will take your comments into advisement before ruling on the case.
