mucinous

adjective
containing or relating to mucin
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈmjuːsɪnəs / (BrE)
Relating to or containing mucin, a protein found in mucus.

Examples

Mucinous material
Biopsies revealed mucinous material within the tissue sample.
Some cysts are classified as mucinous because they contain a gelatinous substance.
The pathologist identified a mucinous tumor in the patient's colon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serous - containing or producing a watery fluid like serum
