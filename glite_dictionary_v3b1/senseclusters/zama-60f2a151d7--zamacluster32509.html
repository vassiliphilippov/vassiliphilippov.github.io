Zama

proper noun
site of Scipio's victory over Hannibal, 202 BC
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈzɑːmə / (BrE)
the site of the decisive battle in 202 BC where Scipio defeated Hannibal, ending the Second Punic War

Examples

Zama battle site
Many historians regard Zama as one of the most important battles in classical antiquity.
Scipio Africanus earned his title after the victory at Zama.
The Battle of Zama marked the end of Carthage's attempts to challenge Rome's dominance.
