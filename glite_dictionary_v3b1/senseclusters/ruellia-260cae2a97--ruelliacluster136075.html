Ruellia

noun
ornamental flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ruˈɛliə / (AmE), / ruˈɛliə / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Ruellia, often with showy tubular flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental and also called wild petunia

Examples

A Ruellia flower
The Ruellia in her garden bloomed with bright purple flowers all summer.
Many landscapers choose Ruellia for its ability to attract butterflies and add color.
He identified the plant as Ruellia because of its distinctive tubular blossoms.
