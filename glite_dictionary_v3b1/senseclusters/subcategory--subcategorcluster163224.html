subcategory

noun
smaller group within category
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbkætəˌɡɔri / (AmE), / ˈsʌbkætɪˌɡɔːri / (BrE)
a group or class that is a smaller, more specific part of a larger category

Examples

A product subcategory
Each subcategory in the report contains detailed information about a specific topic.
The teacher asked students to choose a subcategory of science for their projects.
In the library, books are organized by category and then by subcategory.

  • synonym: division - the act or process of separating into parts or groups
