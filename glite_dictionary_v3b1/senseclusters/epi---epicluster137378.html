Pronunciation: / ˈɛpi / (AmE), / ˈɛpi / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'upon', 'over', 'above', 'after', or 'affecting', used to form words that indicate something is on top of, follows, or impacts something else

Examples

epi- prefix meaning
The word 'epicenter' uses the prefix epi- to mean 'upon the center'.
In 'epilogue', the prefix epi- shows that it comes after the main story.
Medical terms like 'epidermis' use epi- to mean 'upon the skin'.
The word epi- is used in 'epidemic' to mean 'upon the people'.
