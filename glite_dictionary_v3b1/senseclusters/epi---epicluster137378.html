Pronunciation: /ˈep.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈep.ɪ/ (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'upon', 'over', 'above', 'after', or 'affecting', used to form words that indicate something is on top of, follows, or impacts something else

Examples

epi- prefix meaning
The word 'epicenter' uses the prefix epi- to mean 'upon the center'.
In 'epilogue', the prefix epi- shows that it comes after the main story.
Medical terms like 'epidermis' use epi- to mean 'upon the skin'.
The word epi- is used in 'epidemic' to mean 'upon the people'.
In medical terms, epi- often indicates something occurring on or above a structure.
Public health professionals use the prefix epi- when discussing the spread of diseases.
The prefix epi- in 'epidermis' means the outer layer of skin.
In 'epicenter', epi- means above the center of an earthquake.
The word 'epilogue' uses epi- to mean something added at the end.
