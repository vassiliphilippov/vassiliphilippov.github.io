stain

verb
to color a specimen for microscopic study
1.7
Pronunciation: / steɪn / (AmE), / steɪn / (BrE)
to treat a specimen with a dye or chemical that colors certain components for microscopic examination

Examples

To stain a slide
After the tissues are stained, their structures become easier to observe under the microscope.
Different dyes are used when staining samples to identify specific cell components.
The technician will stain the slide to highlight the bacteria.
