vampish

adjective
seductively attractive
Pronunciation: / ˈvæmpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈvæmpɪʃ / (BrE)
behaving in a seductively attractive or flirtatious manner, often in a way that draws attention

Examples

Vampish style
She wore a vampish dress to the party, turning heads as she entered the room.
His new novel features a vampish character who uses charm to get what she wants.
The actress gave a vampish performance that captivated the entire audience.
