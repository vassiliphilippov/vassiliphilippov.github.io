prefrontal lobe

noun
front part of the brain's frontal lobe
Pronunciation: / priːˈfrʌntəl loʊb / (AmE), / priːˈfrʌntəl ləʊb / (BrE)
the front part of the frontal lobe in the brain, involved in complex cognitive behavior and decision-making

Examples

The prefrontal lobe area
Damage to the prefrontal lobe can affect a person's ability to plan and make decisions.
Researchers have found that the prefrontal lobe is active during tasks requiring self-control.
The prefrontal lobe continues to develop well into early adulthood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontal lobe - the part of the brain located at the front of each cerebral hemisphere
