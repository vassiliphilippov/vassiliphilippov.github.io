nonpredictive

adjective
not able to forecast
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprɪˈdɪktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprɪˈdɪktɪv / (BrE)
not able to accurately forecast or indicate future outcomes or results

Examples

Nonpredictive test results
The test results were nonpredictive of the patient's future health.
Because the data was nonpredictive, the researchers could not draw any firm conclusions.
A nonpredictive model is not useful for making accurate forecasts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: predictive - able to forecast or indicate future outcomes
  • synonym: unreliable - not able to be trusted or depended on
