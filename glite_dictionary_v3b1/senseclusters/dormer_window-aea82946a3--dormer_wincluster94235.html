dormer window

noun
window projecting from a sloping roof
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrmɚ ˌwɪndoʊ /; / ˈdɔrmər ˌwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːmə ˌwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a window that projects vertically from a sloping roof, often set in a small structure built out from the roof
