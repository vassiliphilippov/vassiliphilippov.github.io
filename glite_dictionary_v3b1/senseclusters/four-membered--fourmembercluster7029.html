four-membered

adjective
having a ring with four members (chemistry)
8.2
Pronunciation: / fɔr ˈmɛm.bɚd / (AmE), / fɔː ˈmɛm.bəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure composed of four atoms or groups.

Examples

A four-membered ring

The four-membered structure of the molecule increases ring strain compared to larger rings.
