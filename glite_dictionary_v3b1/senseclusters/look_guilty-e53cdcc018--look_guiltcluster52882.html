look guilty

verb
appear to feel guilt or shame
3.0
Pronunciation: / lʊk ˈɡɪlti / (AmE), / lʊk ˈɡɪlti / (BrE)
to have a facial expression or demeanor that suggests one feels guilt, remorse, or shame about something

Examples

He looked guilty
He looked guilty when confronted about the missing money.
The manager could tell that Lisa looked guilty after she broke the vase.
When the teacher asked who cheated, several students looked guilty and avoided eye contact.
