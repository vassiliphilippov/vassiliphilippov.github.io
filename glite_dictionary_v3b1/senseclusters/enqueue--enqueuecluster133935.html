enqueue

verb
to add to queue
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkjuː / (AmE), / ɪnˈkjuː / (BrE)
to add something to the end of a queue or waiting list so it will be processed in order

Examples

To enqueue a job
The system will enqueue your print job until the printer is available.
When a new message arrives, the server will enqueue it for processing.
You must enqueue each task so they are handled in the correct order.
Please enqueue your request, and we will process it in order.
The system will automatically enqueue any files you upload for scanning.
At the busy restaurant, guests are enqueued on a waiting list for tables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: add - to put something together with something else
  • antonym: dequeue - to remove an item from the front of a queue
  • synonym: queue - to arrange items in a line or sequence
