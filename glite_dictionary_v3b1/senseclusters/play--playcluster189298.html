Synonyms

  • act
    2.1 19/m
    act means perform a role on stage or screen, while play is a general verb for taking a part in a show.
  • play the role of
    3.0 2.51/m
    play the role of expresses doing a specific character in a production, while play is the shorter verb for performing.
  • portray
    3.7 9.34/m
    portray means show a character's traits or feelings in performance, while play focuses on taking the role itself.
  • play a part
    2.5 2.50/m
    play a part can mean join a cast or help a result, while play mainly means performing a character on stage or screen.
  • playact
    6.7 0.15/m
    playact means act in a show or pretend in a showy way, while play means simply to perform a role in a production.
