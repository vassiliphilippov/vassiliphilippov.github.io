tappet wrench

noun
spanner with fixed-width parallel jaws
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpɪt rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈtæpɪt rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a spanner with parallel jaws at a fixed width, typically used to adjust tappets or other parts in machinery
