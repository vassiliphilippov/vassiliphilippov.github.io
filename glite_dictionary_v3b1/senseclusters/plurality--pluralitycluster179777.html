plurality

noun
large number or variety; multitude
Pronunciation: / plʊˈræl.ə.ti / (AmE), / plʊəˈral.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a large number or variety of people or things, not specified or counted precisely.

Examples

A plurality of ideas
A plurality of approaches can lead to innovative solutions.
The city offers a plurality of cultural events throughout the year.
There was a plurality of opinions expressed at the forum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multitude - a large number of people or things
  • synonym: variety - a number of different types or things
