overstand

verb
to stand above
9.6
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈstænd/ /; / /ˈoʊ.vər.stænd/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈstænd/ /; / /ˈəʊ.və.stænd/ (BrE)
to stand or be positioned above something, projecting over or towering above it

Examples

To overstand the valley
The ancient castle ruins overstand the valley, visible from miles away.
Tall cliffs overstand the beach, providing shade in the afternoon.
A massive oak tree overstands the small cottage, offering protection from the wind.
