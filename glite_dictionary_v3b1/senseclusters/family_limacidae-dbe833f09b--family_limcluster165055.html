family Limacidae

noun
a family of slugs in Stylommatophora
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli lɪˈmæsɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli lɪˈmæsɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of slugs belonging to the order Stylommatophora, notable for their soft, unsegmented bodies and lack of a prominent shell.

Examples

Family Limacidae slugs
Many common garden slugs belong to the family Limacidae and can be found throughout Europe.
Researchers discovered a new species within the family Limacidae in the forests of Eastern Europe.
The family Limacidae is characterized by its members' inability to retract into a shell.
