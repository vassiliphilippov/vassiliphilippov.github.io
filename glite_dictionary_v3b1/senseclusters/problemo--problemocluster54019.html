problemo

noun
troublemaker (humorous)
9.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈbleɪmoʊ / (AmE), / prəˈbleɪməʊ / (BrE)
an informal, humorous term for a person who causes trouble or is difficult to deal with

Examples

A real problemo
He is such a problemo that nobody wants to work with him on group projects.
