overtraining

noun
excessive exercise
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
exercising too much or too intensely without enough rest, resulting in fatigue, decreased performance, or injury

Examples

Athlete overtraining
Many athletes experience overtraining when they push themselves too hard without enough recovery time.
Symptoms of overtraining include constant tiredness, irritability, and frequent injuries.
To avoid overtraining, it is important to balance workouts with proper rest and nutrition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overexertion - excessive physical effort leading to tiredness or harm
  • antonym: rest - a period of relaxation or recovery after activity
