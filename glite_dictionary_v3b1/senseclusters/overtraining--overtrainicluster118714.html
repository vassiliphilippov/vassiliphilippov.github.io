Examples

Athlete overtraining
Many athletes experience overtraining when they push themselves too hard without enough recovery time.
Symptoms of overtraining include constant tiredness, irritability, and frequent injuries.
To avoid overtraining, it is important to balance workouts with proper rest and nutrition.
