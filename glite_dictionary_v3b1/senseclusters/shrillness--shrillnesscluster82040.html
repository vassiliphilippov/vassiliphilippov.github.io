shrillness

noun
piercing high-pitched sound quality
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being high-pitched, sharp, and often unpleasant or piercing (especially of a sound or voice)

Examples

The shrillness of voice
She disliked the shrillness in his voice during arguments.
Some birds are known for the shrillness of their calls, which can carry for miles.
The shrillness of the alarm made everyone cover their ears.
Critics remarked on the shrillness of his voice during the speech.
The shrillness of her laughter rang through the empty hall.
