caper

noun
playful or daring crime, e.g. robbery
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪpə / (BrE)
a crime, especially one that is daring or playful, such as a robbery or theft, often with a sense of adventure or mischief

Examples

A daring caper
He loved telling stories of his youthful capers with a mischievous grin.
The movie follows a group planning a daring casino caper in Las Vegas.
The police finally caught the gang behind the famous diamond caper in Paris.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heist - a robbery, especially one involving elaborate planning
  • synonym: robbery - the crime of stealing from a place or person, especially using force or the threat of force
