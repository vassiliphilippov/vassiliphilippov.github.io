dominator

noun
one with control
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːməˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪˌneɪtə /; / ˈdɒmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that has control or power over others

Examples

A strong dominator
The team captain was the clear dominator on the field, leading every play.
In the business world, a true dominator often sets the trends others follow.
The new software quickly became the dominator in the market due to its innovative features.
The medieval king was known as the dominator of his vast empire.
In ancient texts, the conqueror is often referred to as a dominator of lands and people.
The title dominator was reserved for those who held absolute authority in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ruler - a person who governs or controls others
  • synonym: sovereign - a supreme ruler, especially a monarch
  • antonym: subject - a person under the control of a ruler
  • antonym: subordinate - a person under the authority or control of another
