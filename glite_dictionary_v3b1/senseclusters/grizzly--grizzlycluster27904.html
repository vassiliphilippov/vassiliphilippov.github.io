grizzly

noun
a large North American bear
9.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgrɪz.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgrɪz.lɪ/ (BrE)
a large, powerful brownish-gray bear found in the uplands of western North America, known for its size and distinct shoulder hump

Examples
