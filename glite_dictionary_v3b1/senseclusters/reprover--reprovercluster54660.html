reprover

noun
person who expresses disapproval or criticism
3.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpruvɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈpruːvə / (BrE)
a person who expresses disapproval or criticism, often by pointing out faults or blaming others

Examples

A strict reprover
