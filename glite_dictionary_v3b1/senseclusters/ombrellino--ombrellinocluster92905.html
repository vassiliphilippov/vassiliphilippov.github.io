ombrellino

noun
small umbrella
Pronunciation: / ˌɑmbrəˈliːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɒmbrɛˈliːnəʊ / (BrE)
a small handheld umbrella used for protection from rain or sun

Examples

Handheld ombrellino
She always carried a colorful ombrellino to shield herself from the midday sun.
A compact ombrellino fits easily into most handbags for unexpected rain.
Tourists in the city often use a ombrellino to stay cool during summer walks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasol - a light umbrella used to give shade from the sun
  • synonym: umbrella - a device for protection against rain or sun, usually larger
