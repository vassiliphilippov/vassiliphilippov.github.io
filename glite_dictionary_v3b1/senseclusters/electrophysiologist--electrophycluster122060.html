electrophysiologist

noun
specialist in bioelectricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌfɪziˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor or scientist who studies the electrical activity of biological cells and tissues

Examples

The electrophysiologist studies signals
The electrophysiologist explained how electrical signals control the heartbeat.
After years of training, she became a leading electrophysiologist in the hospital.
An electrophysiologist may use special equipment to study nerve cell activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardiologist - a doctor who specializes in the heart and its diseases
  • synonym: neurologist - a doctor who specializes in the nervous system
