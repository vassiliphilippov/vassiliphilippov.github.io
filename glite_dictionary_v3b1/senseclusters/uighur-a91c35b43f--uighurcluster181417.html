Uighur

noun
member of Turkic ethnic group in Xinjiang
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːɡər / (AmE), / ˈwiːɡə / (BrE)
A member of a Turkic ethnic group native to Xinjiang and neighboring regions, especially in northwestern China.

Examples

Uighur people
Activists have spoken out about human rights concerns affecting the Uighur community.
Many Uighur people preserve their traditional culture despite external pressures.
The documentary explores the daily life of a young Uighur woman in Xinjiang.
