saturation

noun
intensity or purity of a color
4.6 4.1
Pronunciation: /sætʃ.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sætʃ.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
The intensity of a color, defined by its freedom from dilution with white and the vividness of its hue.

Examples

Saturation of color
Adjust the saturation on your monitor to make the colors appear more vibrant.
As the sun set, the saturation of the sky's colors began to deepen.
The vivid saturation of the artist's paintings drew the viewer's attention immediately.
