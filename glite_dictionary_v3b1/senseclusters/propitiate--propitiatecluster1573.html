propitiate

verb
to appease or win favor
Pronunciation: / prəˈpɪʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / prəˈpɪʃieɪt / (BrE)
to win or regain the favor of a person, spirit, or god by doing something that pleases them; to appease or pacify.

Examples

To propitiate the gods
Appeasing rituals were performed to propitiate the ancient gods.
He tried to propitiate his boss after arriving late to the meeting.
The villagers made offerings to propitiate the angry spirits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appease - to calm or pacify by acceding to demands or soothing anger
  • antonym: enrage - to cause to become very angry
  • synonym: pacify - to calm, soothe, or bring peace to someone
