blueing

noun
substance used to whiten laundry or hair
3.1 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbluː.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbluː.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a substance, usually a blue dye or powder, added to laundry or hair to counteract yellowing and give a whiter or bluish appearance

Examples
