A conversation or exchange of ideas between two or more people (including as part of books, plays, or movies); also a literary composition presented as a conversation between characters.

Examples

Dialog in a play
The play begins with a brief dialog between the two main characters.
Their dialog revealed the deep misunderstanding between them.
Writers often use dialog to develop characters and advance the plot.
She memorized her dialog for the upcoming scene in the drama club performance.
The witty dialog between the main characters made the play entertaining.
Writers often study real conversations to create believable dialog for their stories.
In the play, much of the humor comes from the quick dialogue between the two sisters.
Teachers encouraged an open dialogue with students to discuss the issue.
The dialogue between the main characters revealed their hidden feelings.
