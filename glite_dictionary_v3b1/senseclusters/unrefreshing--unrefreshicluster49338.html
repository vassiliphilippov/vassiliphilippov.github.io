unrefreshing

adjective
not revitalizing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ / (BrE)
not making you feel fresh, energetic, or revitalized

Examples

An unrefreshing drink

Despite the cold drink, it tasted flat and unrefreshing on the hot afternoon.
He found the air in the crowded room heavy and unrefreshing.
After a night of unrefreshing sleep, he still felt tired in the morning.
The lukewarm shower was unrefreshing after her long run in the heat.
Despite the breeze, the air remained unrefreshing and heavy all afternoon.
