Indian summer

noun
warm spell in autumn
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndiən ˈsʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˌɪndiən ˈsʌmə / (BrE)
A period of unusually warm, dry weather occurring in autumn, especially after a frost.

Examples

A warm Indian summer
After a chilly September, an unexpected Indian summer brought people back to the parks.
This year's Indian summer made the harvest season especially pleasant.
We enjoyed a beautiful Indian summer that lasted well into October.
