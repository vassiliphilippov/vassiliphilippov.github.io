rump roast

noun
cut of beef/veal from hindquarters
6.3
Pronunciation: / rʌmp roʊst / (AmE), / rʌmp rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of beef or veal taken from the fleshy hindquarters of the animal, often cooked by roasting

Examples

A large rump roast
At the supermarket, I found a large rump roast on sale.
He prepared a delicious rump roast with potatoes and carrots for Sunday dinner.
The butcher recommended the rump roast for a slow-cooked meal.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: round steak - a cut of beef from the rear leg of the animal
  • degree higher: sirloin - a cut of beef from the back of the animal, just above the rump
