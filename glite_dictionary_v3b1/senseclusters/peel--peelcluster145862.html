peel

verb
remove one's clothing
7.7 3/m
Pronunciation: / pil / (AmE), / piÀêl / (BrE)
(informal) To take off all or part of your clothes, especially in a way that is noticeable or deliberate.

Examples

To peel off clothes
He started to peel off his jacket as soon as he entered the warm house.
She peeled out of her muddy uniform after soccer practice.
The children laughed as they peeled off their raincoats and boots by the door.
