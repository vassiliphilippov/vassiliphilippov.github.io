battle station

noun
military position assigned for combat
4.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a designated position or location for military personnel to occupy during combat or when preparing for military action, especially on a ship or in a fortified location

Examples
