grandee

noun
Spanish or Portuguese nobleman of high rank
Pronunciation: / ɡrænˈdi / (AmE), / ɡrænˈdiː / (BrE)
a nobleman of the highest rank, especially in Spain or Portugal

Examples

A grandee of Spain
He aspired to become a grandee in the circles of Madrid's nobility.
Several grandees attended the lavish banquet at the palace.
The king was accompanied by a powerful grandee of the Spanish court.
