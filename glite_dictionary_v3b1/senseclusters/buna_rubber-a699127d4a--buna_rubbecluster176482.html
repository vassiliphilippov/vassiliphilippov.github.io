Buna rubber

noun
synthetic rubber from butadiene
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːnə ˈrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈbjuːnə ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
A synthetic rubber made by polymerizing butadiene, sometimes with added styrene or acrylonitrile, used in tires and various industrial products.

Examples

Buna rubber tires
During the war, Buna rubber was a vital substitute for natural rubber in tires.
Modern advancements have improved the durability of Buna rubber products.
The factory specializes in producing Buna rubber for the automotive industry.
