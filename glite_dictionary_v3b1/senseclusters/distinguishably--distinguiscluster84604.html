distinguishably

adverb
in a distinct manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli / (BrE)
in a way that can be recognized as different or distinct from others

Examples

Look distinguishably different
The twins are distinguishably different in their personalities, even though they look alike.
Each artist's style is distinguishably unique, making their work easy to identify.
The flavors in the dish are distinguishably separate, allowing you to taste each ingredient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distinctly - in a clear and noticeable way
  • antonym: indistinctly - in a way that is not clear or easy to recognize
