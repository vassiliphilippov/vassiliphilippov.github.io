cirrus cloud

noun
a thin, wispy high-altitude cloud
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪr.əs klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɪr.əs klaʊd / (BrE)
A thin, wispy cloud composed of fine ice crystals, typically found at high altitudes.
