Portuguese Guinea

proper noun
former name for Guinea-Bissau
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔrtʃɪˈgiːz ˈɡɪni / (AmE), / ˌpɔːtʃʊˈɡiːz ˈɡɪni / (BrE)
the former name for Guinea-Bissau, a territory on the northwestern coast of Africa that was a Portuguese colony until gaining independence in 1974

Examples

Portuguese Guinea colony
In 1974, Portuguese Guinea officially became known as Guinea-Bissau.
Many historical documents still refer to Guinea-Bissau by its colonial name, Portuguese Guinea.
The struggle for independence in Portuguese Guinea was one of Africa's longest colonial wars.
