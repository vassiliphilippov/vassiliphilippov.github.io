subliterate

adjective
below literate level
8.9
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈlɪtərət / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɪtərət / (BrE)
having reading and writing skills below the level considered literate

Examples

Subliterate adults
The organization provides classes for subliterate adults to improve their reading skills.
Many subliterate individuals struggle to fill out basic forms or read instructions.
The survey revealed a surprisingly high number of subliterate people in the region.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: illiterate - unable to read or write
  • degree higher: literate - able to read and write
