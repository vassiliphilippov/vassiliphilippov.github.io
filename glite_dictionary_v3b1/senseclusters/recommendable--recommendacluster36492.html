recommendable

adjective
worthy of recommendation
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkəˈmɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəˈmɛndəbəl / (BrE)
worthy of being recommended or suggested to others as good or suitable

Examples

Recommendable for restaurants
The new restaurant is definitely recommendable for anyone who enjoys Italian cuisine.
Her performance was so impressive that it was truly recommendable to others.
This book is highly recommendable for students interested in world history.
