angloid accent

noun
accent resembling English speakers
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋ.lɔɪd ˈæk.sənt / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.lɔɪd ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
an accent that resembles the speech of English speakers, especially when adopted by non-native speakers
