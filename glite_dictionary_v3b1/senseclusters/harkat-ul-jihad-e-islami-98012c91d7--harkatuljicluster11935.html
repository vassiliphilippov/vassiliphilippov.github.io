Harkat-ul-Jihad-e-Islami

proper noun
Pakistani Islamist militant group
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑrkət ʊl dʒɪˌhɑd iː ɪsˈlɑmi / (AmE), / ˌhɑːkət ʊl dʒɪˌhɑːd iː ɪsˈlɑːmi / (BrE)
an Islamist militant group based in Pakistan, known for seeking to establish an Islamic government and for its links and cooperation with the Taliban in Afghanistan.
