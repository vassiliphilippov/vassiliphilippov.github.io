personnel casualty

noun
military loss by death or capture
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsəˈnɛl ˈkæʒjəlti / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsəˈnel ˈkaʒjʊəlti / (BrE)
a military member lost by death or capture during a conflict

Examples

Personnel casualties in battle
After the battle, the army faced significant losses in equipment and personnel casualties.
Minimizing personnel casualties is a top priority for military commanders during any operation.
The official report listed twenty personnel casualties after the surprise attack.
