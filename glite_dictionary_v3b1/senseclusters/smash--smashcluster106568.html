smash

noun
great success, especially in entertainment
2.3
Pronunciation: / smæʃ / (AmE), / smæʃ / (BrE)
a great and conspicuous success, especially in entertainment, such as a popular song, film, or show

Examples

A smash hit
Her debut album was an instant smash and broke several sales records.
That new TV series is a smash with audiences all over the country.
The movie became a smash at the box office and surprised its producers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flop - a complete failure, especially in entertainment
  • synonym: hit - a very successful venture, especially in entertainment
