pandan

noun
weaving plant leaves
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpæn.dən/ /; / /ˈpæn.dæn/ (AmE), /ˈpæn.dən/ /; / /ˈpæn.dæn/ (BrE)
the dried or processed leaves of the pandan plant, used as a flexible material for weaving mats, baskets, hats, and other handicrafts

Examples

Pandan mats and baskets

Colorful mats made of pandan are popular souvenirs in many Southeast Asian markets.
She learned how to prepare and dye pandan for weaving traditional hats.
