rat cheese

noun
humorous term for unappealing food
5.4
Pronunciation: / ræt tʃiːz / (AmE), / ræt tʃiːz / (BrE)
a humorous term for unappealing or old food, often used jokingly to describe something that looks or smells unpleasant to eat

Examples

Old rat cheese
Grandpa opened the fridge and pulled out what he called rat cheese for a midnight snack.
The leftovers had a strange odor, so we jokingly nicknamed them rat cheese.
They served some questionable snacks, referring to them as rat cheese to break the ice.
