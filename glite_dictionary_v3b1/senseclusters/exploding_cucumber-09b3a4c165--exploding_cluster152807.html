exploding cucumber

noun
Mediterranean vine with forcefully ejecting fruit
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊdɪŋ ˈkjuːkʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊdɪŋ ˈkjuːkʌmbə / (BrE)
A Mediterranean vine with oblong fruit that, when ripe, ejects its seeds and juice forcefully when touched.

Examples

The exploding cucumber fruit
Gardeners are often surprised when the fruit of the exploding cucumber bursts suddenly upon being touched.
Scientists have studied the mechanism by which the exploding cucumber propels its seeds several meters away.
The exploding cucumber is famous for dispersing its seeds by shooting them out forcefully when ripe.
