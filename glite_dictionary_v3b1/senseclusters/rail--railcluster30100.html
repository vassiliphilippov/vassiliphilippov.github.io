rail

noun
small wading bird with long toes (family Rallidae)
9.9 7.9
Pronunciation: /reɪl/ (AmE), /reɪl/ (BrE)
A small wading bird of the family Rallidae, with short wings and very long toes adapted for running on soft mud.
