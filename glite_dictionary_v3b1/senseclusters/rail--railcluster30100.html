rail

noun
small wading bird with long toes (family Rallidae)
Pronunciation: / reɪl / (AmE), / reɪl / (BrE)
A small wading bird of the family Rallidae, with short wings and very long toes adapted for running on soft mud.

Examples

A waterbird rail
Several species of rail are threatened by habitat loss in wetland areas.
The long toes of a rail help it walk on floating vegetation without sinking.
We spotted a rare rail darting across the marsh at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coot - an aquatic bird of the rail family, with lobed toes and a white bill
  • synonym: crake - a small, short-billed rail-like bird of the family Rallidae
