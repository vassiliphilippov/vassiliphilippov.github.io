marbleizing

noun
imitation marble texture or effect
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːb(ə)laɪzɪŋ /; / ˈmɑːbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
A texture or effect that resembles the appearance of marble, often achieved by special painting or finishing techniques.

Examples

Decorative marbleising is often applied to walls to imitate expensive stone surfaces.
The marbleising on the paper made it look elegant and unique.
