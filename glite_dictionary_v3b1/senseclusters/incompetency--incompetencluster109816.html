incompetency

noun
lack of ability or qualification
4.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkɑːm.pə.tən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkɒm.pɪ.tən.siː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈkɒm.pə.tən.siː/ (BrE)
The lack of physical or intellectual ability or qualifications to perform a task or duty effectively.

Examples
