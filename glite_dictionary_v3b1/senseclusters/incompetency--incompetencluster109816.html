incompetency

noun
lack of ability or qualification
6.8 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / …™nÀàk…ëÀêmp…ôt…ônsi / (AmE), / …™nÀàk…ímp…™t…ônsi / (BrE)
The lack of physical or intellectual ability or qualifications to perform a task or duty effectively.

Examples

Her incompetency caused errors
Her incompetency led to several mistakes in the project's execution.
Questions were raised about the manager's incompetency after repeated failures to meet deadlines.
The team's incompetency became evident during the crisis.
