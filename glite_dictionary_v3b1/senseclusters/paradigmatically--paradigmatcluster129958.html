paradigmatically

adverb
as a model
Pronunciation: / ˌpærədaɪɡˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpærədɪɡˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or serves as a model or example for others

Examples

Think paradigmatically
The scientist approached the problem paradigmatically, using established theories as a guide.
Her leadership style was paradigmatically effective, inspiring others to follow her example.
The company paradigmatically set industry standards with its innovative practices.
