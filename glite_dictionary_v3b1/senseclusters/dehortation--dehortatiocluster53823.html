dehortation

noun
advising against action
9.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.hɔːr.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.hɔːr.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.hɔː.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.hɔː.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Repeated dehortation

The teacher's dehortation against cheating was clear and firm.
Her dehortation made him reconsider his plan to drop out of school.
