psychopathically

adverb
in a psychopathic way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈpæθɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈpæθɪkli / (BrE)

She manipulated her colleagues psychopathically to get what she wanted.
He lied psychopathically without any sign of guilt or concern for the truth.
