ten-gallon hat

noun
broad-brimmed cowboy hat
9.7 4.7
Pronunciation: /ten ˈgæl.ən hæt/ (AmE), /ten ˈgæl.ən hæt/ (BrE)
a broad-brimmed hat with a high, soft crown, traditionally worn by American cowboys and ranchers

Examples

A dusty ten-gallon hat

The sheriff tipped his ten-gallon hat as he greeted the townsfolk.
Tourists love posing for photos in a ten-gallon hat at the rodeo.
