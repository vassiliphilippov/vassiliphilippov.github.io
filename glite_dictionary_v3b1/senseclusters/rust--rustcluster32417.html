rust

noun
strong reddish-brown color
Pronunciation: / rʌst / (AmE), / rʌst / (BrE)
A strong reddish-brown color, resembling the color of iron oxide.

Examples

Rust color
She wore a rust dress that matched the autumn leaves perfectly.
The artist mixed colors to create the perfect rust for his landscape painting.
The walls of the old house were painted a warm shade of rust.
