costa

noun
coastal region
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊstə / (AmE), / ˈkɒstə / (BrE)
a coastal region, especially in Spanish-speaking countries, often referring to a seaside area popular with tourists

Examples

The Spanish costa
Many tourists visit the costa for its sunny beaches and lively resorts.
They bought a holiday home on the costa to enjoy the warm climate year-round.
The costa is famous for its seafood restaurants and beautiful ocean views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coast - the land next to the sea
  • antonym: inland - away from the coast, toward the interior of a country
