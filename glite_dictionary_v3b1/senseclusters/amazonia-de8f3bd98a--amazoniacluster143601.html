Amazonia

proper noun
mythical land of Amazons
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈzoʊniə / (AmE), / ˌæməˈzəʊniə / (BrE)
a fictional or mythical land said to be inhabited by the Amazons, legendary female warriors

Examples

Mythical Amazonia
The explorer dreamed of finding Amazonia, a land ruled by fierce warrior women.
Legends describe Amazonia as a hidden kingdom deep within the ancient forests.
Many stories have been told about the brave women who lived in Amazonia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Atlantis - a legendary lost island mentioned in ancient myths
  • synonym: El Dorado - a mythical city of gold sought by explorers
