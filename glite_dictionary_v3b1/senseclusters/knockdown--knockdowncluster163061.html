knockdown

noun
an act of knocking opponent down (boxing)
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈnɒk.daʊn / (BrE)
an act of knocking an opponent to the ground with a blow, especially in boxing or martial arts

Examples

A knockdown punch
After the knockdown, the referee began the count.
Despite suffering a knockdown, he managed to get back on his feet and continue fighting.
The boxer scored a spectacular knockdown in the second round.
