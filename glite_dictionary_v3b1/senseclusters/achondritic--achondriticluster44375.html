achondritic

adjective
relating to achondrites (stony meteorites)
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌkɑnˈdrɪtɪk /; / ˌeɪˌkɒnˈdrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌkɒnˈdrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an achondrite (a type of stony meteorite that does not contain chondrules or granular structures)
