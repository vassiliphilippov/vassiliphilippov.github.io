pitchy

adjective
very black; like pitch or jet
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃi / (BrE)
Very black or dark, resembling pitch; as dark as jet or coal.

Examples

Pitchy darkness
She wore a pitchy dress that stood out in the dimly lit room.
The cave plunged into pitchy darkness, swallowing every trace of light.
The sky was pitchy after the storm, with no stars visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coal-black - black like coal
  • synonym: ebony - deep black like ebony wood
  • synonym: jet-black - completely black, like jet
