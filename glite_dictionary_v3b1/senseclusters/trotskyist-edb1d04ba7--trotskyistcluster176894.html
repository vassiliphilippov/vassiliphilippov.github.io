Trotskyist

noun
supporter of Trotsky's socialist theory
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑtskiɪst / (AmE), / ˈtrɒtskiɪst / (BrE)
a supporter of Trotsky's theory that socialism should be established globally through ongoing revolution

Examples

Trotskyist supporter
As a Trotskyist, he opposed the policies imposed by Stalin.
She became a committed Trotskyist after reading about the Russian Revolution.
The meeting was organized by a group of local Trotskyists to discuss international workers' rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Marxist - a supporter of the political theories of Karl Marx
  • antonym: Stalinist - a supporter of the policies of Joseph Stalin
