abysm

noun
a deep chasm or gulf (often figurative)
6.5 9.7
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a deep or seemingly bottomless pit, gulf, or chasm, often used figuratively to describe something unfathomable or immeasurably profound

Examples
