complex concept

noun
difficult or intricate idea
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmˌplɛks kɑnˈsɛpt / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pleks ˈkɒn.sept / (BrE)
an idea or notion that is difficult to understand because it involves many interconnected parts or aspects

Examples

A complex concept
Learning calculus introduces students to many complex concepts that require careful study.
The documentary breaks down the complex concept of quantum mechanics into understandable terms.
The scientist struggled to communicate the complex concept to a non-technical audience.
