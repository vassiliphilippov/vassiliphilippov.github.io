boon

noun
helpful benefit; blessing
4.4 3.44/m
Pronunciation: / bun / (AmE), / buÀên / (BrE)
a thing that is helpful or beneficial; a blessing or advantage

Examples

A boon for health
Access to clean water is considered a great boon for the village.
The mild climate proved to be a boon for farmers throughout the region.
This new highway is a real boon to commuters in the area.

Synonyms

    1.0 118/m
    a benefit is a practical advantage or help in a situation, while a boon is often a welcomed or lucky gift.
  • blessing
    1.4 16/m

  • plus
    1.0 3.68/m
    a plus is a casual way to say a small advantage, while a boon is a stronger, more notable benefit.
