fruitiness

noun
taste or smell of fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːtinəs / (AmE), / ˈfruːtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of tasting, smelling, or having the abundance of fruit (especially in food or drink)

Examples

The fruitiness of juice
The fruitiness of the juice made it especially refreshing on a hot day.
Wine experts often comment on the fruitiness present in certain red wines.
She enjoyed the subtle fruitiness in the new dessert recipe.
The wine's fruitiness made it especially popular among those who enjoy sweet flavors.
She appreciated the fruitiness of the dessert, which was made with fresh berries.
The fruitiness of the orchard was evident in the abundance of apples and pears that year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bitterness - a sharp, often unpleasant taste
  • antonym: blandness - lack of strong flavor or character
  • synonym: fruitful - producing much fruit or results
  • synonym: sweetness - the quality of tasting sweet
