glamor

verb
cast a magical spell over; bewitch
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæmər / (AmE), / ˈɡlæmə / (BrE)
(archaic) To cast a magical spell over someone or something; to enchant or bewitch.

Examples

To glamor with magic
He claimed that the faeries had glamoured the woods, making it impossible to find his way home.
In old legends, witches would glamour unsuspecting travelers and lead them astray.
The sorceress tried to glamour the prince, but he resisted her spell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewitch - to cast a spell over; to charm or enchant
