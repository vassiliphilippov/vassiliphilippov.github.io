unplaned

adjective
not smoothed wood
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpleɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪnd / (BrE)
not smoothed or leveled by a planer, especially referring to wood surfaces that remain rough.

Examples

Unplaned wood surface
The old barn was built with unplaned timber, giving it a rustic appearance.
He preferred the natural look of unplaned boards for his woodworking project.
Because the wood was unplaned, it felt rough to the touch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: planed - smoothed or leveled by a planer
  • synonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface
