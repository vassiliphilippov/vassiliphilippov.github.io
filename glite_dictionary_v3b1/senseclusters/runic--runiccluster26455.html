runic

adjective
relating to ancient runes/alphabet
Pronunciation: / ˈruː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈruː.nɪk / (BrE)
relating to or written in runes, the characters of an early Germanic alphabet

Examples

Runic alphabet
She became fascinated by runic alphabets used by the Vikings.
The archaeologist discovered a runic inscription on the ancient stone.
The museum displayed several artifacts with runic markings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rune - a letter from an ancient Germanic alphabet
