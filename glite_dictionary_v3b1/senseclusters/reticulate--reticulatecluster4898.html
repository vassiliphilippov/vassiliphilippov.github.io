reticulate

adjective
having a network-like structure
Pronunciation: / rəˈtɪkjʊlət / (AmE), / rɪˈtɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
having a pattern or structure like a net; forming or resembling a network.

Examples

Reticulate pattern
A reticulate network of canals distributes water evenly across the agricultural fields.
Many reptiles have reticulate patterns on their skin that help with camouflage.
The leaf's reticulate veins create a beautiful, web-like design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: networked - having the structure of a network
  • synonym: reticulated - having an arrangement or appearance like a network
