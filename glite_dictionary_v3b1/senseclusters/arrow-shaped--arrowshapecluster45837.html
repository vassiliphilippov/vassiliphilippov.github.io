Synonyms

  • sagittiform
    9.9 0.00/m
    sagittiform is a technical botany word for leaves that point forward and lack flaring base lobes, while arrow-shaped is more general.
  • arrowlike
    4.1 0.01/m
    arrowlike describes a shape that tapers to a sharp tip and looks like an arrow, while arrow-shaped names the arrowhead form.
  • arrowed
    6.0 0.00/m
    arrowed means shaped like an arrow or tapering to a point, while arrow-shaped stresses the classic arrowhead without flaring base lobes.
