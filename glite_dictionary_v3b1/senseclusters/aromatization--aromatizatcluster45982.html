aromatization

noun
adding fragrance
Pronunciation: / əˌroʊmətəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌrəʊmətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adding aroma or fragrance to something to make it smell pleasant

Examples

Aromatization with oils
The aromatization of the room with essential oils created a relaxing atmosphere.
Chefs often focus on the aromatization of dishes to enhance the dining experience.
The aromatization of the air in the spa made guests feel welcome and comfortable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deodorization - the process of removing or neutralizing odors
  • synonym: perfuming - the act of adding perfume or scent
