Physeter catodon

noun
the sperm whale (large toothed whale)
Pronunciation: / faɪˈsiːtər kəˈtoʊdɑn / (AmE), / fɪˈsiːtə kəˈtəʊdɒn / (BrE)
The sperm whale, a large whale with a distinctive block-shaped head containing spermaceti and oil, also known as a source of ambergris.
