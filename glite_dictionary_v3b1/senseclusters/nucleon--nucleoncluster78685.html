nucleon

noun
proton or neutron in atomic nucleus
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːkliˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːklɪɒn / (BrE)
either of the two particles, a proton or a neutron, that make up the nucleus of an atom

Examples

A nucleon particle
During radioactive decay, a nucleon can be transformed from a neutron to a proton.
Protons and neutrons are the two types of nucleon found in every atomic nucleus.
The atomic nucleus is composed of nucleons held together by the strong force.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutron - a neutrally charged subatomic particle in the nucleus
  • synonym: proton - a positively charged subatomic particle in the nucleus
