tweezers

noun
small gripping tool
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːzərz / (AmE), / ˈtwiːzəz / (BrE)
a small tool with two arms used for picking up or holding very small objects

Examples

A pair of tweezers is essential for handling small electronic components.
