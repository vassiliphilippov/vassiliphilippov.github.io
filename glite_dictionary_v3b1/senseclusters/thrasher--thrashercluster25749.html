thrasher

noun
American songbird able to mimic birdsongs
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈθræʃər / (AmE), / ˈθræʃə / (BrE)
A thrush-like American songbird belonging to the family Mimidae, noted for its ability to mimic the songs of other birds.

Thrasher bird singing

Many birdwatchers are thrilled to spot a thrasher in their backyard.
The brown thrasher is known for its remarkable ability to mimic a variety of birdsongs.
