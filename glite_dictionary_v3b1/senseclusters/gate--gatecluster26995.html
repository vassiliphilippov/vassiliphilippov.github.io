gate

verb
confine a student as punishment at school
7.0 7.2
Pronunciation: /geɪt/ (AmE), /geɪt/ (BrE)
(of a school or institution) to restrict a student's movement to the dormitory or campus as a form of punishment.

Examples

To gate a student
If you break the rules again, you might get gated for the remainder of the term.
She was gated last month and couldn't leave the campus even on weekends.
The school decided to gate him for a week after he missed curfew.
