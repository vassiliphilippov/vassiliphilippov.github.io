long drink

noun
a large drink served in a tall glass
3.1
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ drɪŋk / (AmE), / lɒŋ drɪŋk / (BrE)
A drink served in a tall glass, typically consisting of a larger quantity of liquid than a standard serving.

Examples

A long drink glass

Some cocktails are best enjoyed as a long drink over ice.
