popple

noun
small rounded hill
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒpəl / (BrE)
a small rounded hill or knoll, especially in Scottish or regional dialects

Examples

A grassy popple
The sheep grazed peacefully on the grassy popple near the old stone wall.
From the top of the popple, we could see the entire village spread out below us.
Children ran up and down the popple during their afternoon playtime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hillock - a small hill or mound
  • synonym: knoll - a small, rounded hill
