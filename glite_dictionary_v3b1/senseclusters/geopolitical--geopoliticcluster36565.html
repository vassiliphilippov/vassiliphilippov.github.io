geopolitical

adjective
relating to international politics and geography
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː.oʊ.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əʊ.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the influence of geography, economics, and demography on politics and international relations

Examples

Geopolitical issues
Climate change has become a major geopolitical issue affecting many nations worldwide.
The country's geopolitical position gives it significant influence in regional affairs.
They discussed the geopolitical consequences of the new trade agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economic - relating to economy or economics (sometimes paired contextually)
  • synonym: political - relating to government or public affairs
