geopolitical

adjective
relating to international politics and geography
3.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /dʒiː.oʊ.pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /dʒiː.əʊ.pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
