musician

noun
professional or amateur music performer or composer
1.4
Pronunciation: / mjuˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / mjuˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who plays, composes, conducts, or performs music, either professionally or as a hobby

Examples

A musician plays music
After years of dedication, she became a renowned musician in the jazz community.
The musician practiced the piano for several hours every day.
The orchestra hired a talented musician to compose their new symphony.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a famous musician.
Many musicians practice for hours every day to master their instruments.
The musician performed an original piece at the jazz festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - person who pursues an activity for pleasure, not as a profession
  • synonym: composer - person who writes music
  • synonym: conductor - person who directs a musical performance
  • synonym: performer - a person who performs music or other forms of art on stage
