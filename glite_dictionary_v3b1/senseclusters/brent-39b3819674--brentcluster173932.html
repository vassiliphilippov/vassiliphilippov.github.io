Brent

proper noun
crude oil benchmark
Pronunciation: / brɛnt / (AmE), / brɛnt / (BrE)
a major type of crude oil from the North Sea, widely used as a global price benchmark

Examples

Brent crude oil
The price of Brent often sets the standard for oil markets worldwide.
Many countries use Brent as a reference when negotiating oil contracts.
Fluctuations in Brent prices can impact the global economy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Dubai crude - a Middle Eastern crude oil used as a price benchmark
  • synonym: WTI - a major American crude oil used as a price benchmark
