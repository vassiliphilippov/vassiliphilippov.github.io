trinational

adjective
involving three countries
Pronunciation: / traɪˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / traɪˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
involving, relating to, or agreed upon by three different countries

Examples

Trinational agreement
The three governments formed a trinational task force to address the environmental crisis.
A trinational summit was held to discuss trade agreements among the neighboring countries.
The trinational park is managed jointly by authorities from all three nations.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: binational - involving or relating to two countries
  • degree higher: multinational - involving or relating to several countries
