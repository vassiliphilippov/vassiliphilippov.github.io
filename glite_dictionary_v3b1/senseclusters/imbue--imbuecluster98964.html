imbue

verb
to suffuse or dye with color
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbju / (AmE), / ɪmˈbjuː / (BrE)
to permeate or suffuse a material or object with color; to dye or tint deeply

Examples

To imbue fabric with color
Morning sunlight seemed to imbue the mountains with a soft pink glow.
The artist sought to imbue the canvas with a rich, golden hue.
The dye will imbue the fabric with deep, vibrant colors.
