biofertilizer

noun
microbial plant growth enhancer
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈfɜːrt̬.əˌlaɪ.zər / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˈfəː.tɪ.laɪ.zə / (BrE)
A substance containing living microorganisms that, when applied to seeds, plants, or soil, enhances plant growth by increasing the availability of nutrients.

Examples

Use biofertilizers in soil
Farmers are increasingly using biofertilizers to improve crop yields in a sustainable way.
Researchers are developing new types of biofertilizers that can work in a wider range of soils.
The application of biofertilizer to the soil helped the plants grow stronger and healthier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to increase its fertility
