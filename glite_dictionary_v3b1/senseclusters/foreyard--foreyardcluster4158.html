foreyard

noun
horizontal spar on foremast for foresail
8.5 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːr.jɑːrd/ /; / /ˈfɔːr.jɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.jɑːd/ (BrE)
the horizontal spar attached to the lower part of the foremast on a square-rigged ship, from which the foresail is set
