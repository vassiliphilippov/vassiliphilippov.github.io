foreyard

noun
horizontal spar on foremast for foresail
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌjɑːd / (BrE)
the horizontal spar attached to the lower part of the foremast on a square-rigged ship, from which the foresail is set

Examples

The foreyard spar
Damage to the foreyard made it impossible to raise the foresail properly.
He stood on the foreyard, gazing out at the open sea as the ship sailed on.
The sailors climbed the rigging to secure the foreyard before the storm hit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mainyard - the yard on the mainmast of a square-rigged ship
  • synonym: mizzenyard - the yard on the mizzenmast of a square-rigged ship
