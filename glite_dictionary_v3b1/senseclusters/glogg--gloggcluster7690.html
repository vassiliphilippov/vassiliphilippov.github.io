glogg

noun
Scandinavian spiced hot punch
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌɡ / (AmE), / ɡlɒɡ / (BrE)
A Scandinavian spiced punch made with claret and aquavit, often including spices, raisins, orange peel, and sugar, served hot.

Examples

A cup of glogg
During the holidays, we enjoyed a steaming cup of glogg with friends.
He enhanced his glogg recipe with extra cardamom and a splash of brandy.
Traditional glogg is often served at Scandinavian Christmas markets.
