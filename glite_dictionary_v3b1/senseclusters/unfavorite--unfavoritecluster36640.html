unfavorite

adjective
not preferred
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
not liked or preferred; less favored than others in comparison.

Examples

Unfavorite choice
After the cheating scandal he became the unfavorite player among long-time supporters.
Despite her talent, she was often the unfavorite child in the family.
The movie quickly became the unfavorite among critics after its poor reviews.
