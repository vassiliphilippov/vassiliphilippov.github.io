microbeam

noun
narrow radiation beam
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌbiːm / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌbiːm / (BrE)
a very narrow beam of radiation or particles used for precise scientific or medical applications

Examples

Microbeam radiation therapy
The laboratory uses a microbeam to study the effects of radiation on single cells.
A microbeam allows scientists to target specific areas without damaging surrounding tissue.
Researchers developed a new microbeam technique for analyzing microscopic samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a line or stream of particles or energy
  • synonym: laser - a device that emits a concentrated beam of light
