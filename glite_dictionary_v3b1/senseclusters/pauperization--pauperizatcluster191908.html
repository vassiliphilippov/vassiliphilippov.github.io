pauperization

noun
state of extreme poverty
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔːpərɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌpɔpərɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌpɔːpərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɔːpəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
