undrunk

adjective
not carried away
9.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdrʌŋk / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrʌŋk / (BrE)
not overwhelmed or carried away by a strong feeling, power, or success; remaining unaffected or sober-minded.

Examples

Undrunk by power
Despite the team's huge success, the coach stayed undrunk by fame and focused on improvement.
She remained undrunk by the excitement, calmly making decisions while others celebrated.
Even after the promotion, he was undrunk by power and treated everyone with respect.

  • synonym: sober - not affected by strong emotion or excitement
