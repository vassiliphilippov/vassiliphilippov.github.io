tortoiseshell butterfly

noun
a brightly colored butterfly resembling tortoiseshell
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrt̬əsˌʃɛl ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtəsʃɛl ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
any of several brightly colored butterflies of the genus Aglais, especially the small tortoiseshell, having variegated markings resembling tortoiseshell; larvae typically feed on nettle plants.

Examples

The tortoiseshell butterfly wings
The vibrant wings of the tortoiseshell butterfly make it easy to identify among other species.
We spotted a tortoiseshell butterfly basking in the sun on the garden wall.
Young naturalists often observe the tortoiseshell butterfly feeding on nectar from wildflowers.
