folk

noun
traditional community music
1.0
Pronunciation: / foʊk / (AmE), / fəʊk / (BrE)
traditional music, often passed down orally, that expresses the culture and life of a community or group of people

Examples

Folk music festival
Many folk songs tell stories of hardship and hope.
She enjoys listening to Irish folk in the evenings.
The festival featured live folk from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folk music - traditional music characteristic of a community or people
  • antonym: pop - modern popular music, especially with mass appeal
