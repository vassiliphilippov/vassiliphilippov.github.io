fogless

adjective
without fog
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔɡləs / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡləs / (BrE)
free from fog or not affected by fog

Examples

Fogless windows
The windows stayed fogless even after a hot shower in the bathroom.
We enjoyed a fogless sunrise at the top of the mountain.
The city was unusually fogless for this time of year, making driving much easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clear - free from cloud, mist, or fog
  • antonym: foggy - full of or covered with fog
