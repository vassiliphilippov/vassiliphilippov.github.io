surprint

noun
additional printing over an existing design
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːˌprɪnt / (BrE)
something printed over an already printed design, especially a stamp or document.

Examples

A surprint on stamps
Rare stamps often have a surprint indicating a new value or purpose.
The collector specialized in finding errors in the surprint on old stamps.
The museum displayed a banknote with a commemorative surprint.
