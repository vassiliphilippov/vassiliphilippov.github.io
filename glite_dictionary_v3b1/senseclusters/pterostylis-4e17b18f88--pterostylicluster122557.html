Pterostylis

proper noun
genus of terrestrial orchids
Pronunciation: / tɛrəˈstaɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌptɛrəˈstɪlɪs / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial orchids native to Australia, New Zealand, and the western Pacific region.
