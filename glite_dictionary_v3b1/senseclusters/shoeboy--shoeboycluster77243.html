shoeboy

noun
boy who shines shoes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy employed to shine or clean shoes, especially in the past

Examples

The shoeboy shines shoes
The shoeboy waited patiently outside the busy train station for his first customer of the day.
In old photographs, you can often see a shoeboy working on the city streets.
The hotel hired a shoeboy to keep guests' shoes looking polished and clean.

Related Terms

  • antonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services
  • synonym: shoeshine - a person who shines shoes for payment
