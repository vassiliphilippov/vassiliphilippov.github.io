misapply

verb
use wrongly or inappropriately
6.1 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪs.əˈplaɪ / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.əˈplaɪ / (BrE)
to use something in a way that is wrong, inappropriate, or not intended

Examples

Funds were misapplied during the construction project, leading to budget issues.
If you misapply this rule, the results could be misleading.
The principle was misapplied in this experiment, affecting the outcome.
