your guess is as good as mine

x
I don't know; uncertain answer
1.0
Pronunciation: / jɔɹ ɡɛs ɪz æz ɡʊd æz maɪn /; / jɔr ɡɛs ɪz æz ɡʊd æz maɪn / (AmE), / jɔː ɡɛs ɪz æz ɡʊd æz maɪn / (BrE)
used to say that you do not know the answer to a question, and the other person probably does not know either

Examples

Your guess is as good as mine
Honestly, your guess is as good as mine about how this movie will end.
If you're wondering when the bus will arrive, well, your guess is as good as mine.
When asked about the new management plan, I said, 'your guess is as good as mine.'
If you’re asking how it works, your guess is as good as mine!
Nobody knows who will win the election; your guess is as good as mine.
“When do you think the package will arrive?” “Your guess is as good as mine.”

Related Terms

  • synonym: I have no idea - used to say you do not know the answer
  • degree higher: your guess is better than mine - used to humorously indicate someone else knows more
