regulation

noun
state of being controlled or governed
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛgjəˈleɪʃən /; / ˌrɛɡjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛgjʊˈleɪʃən /; / ˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act, process, or state of controlling or managing something by rules or laws

  • antonym: deregulation - the removal of regulations or restrictions
  • synonym: standardization - the process of making things conform to a standard
