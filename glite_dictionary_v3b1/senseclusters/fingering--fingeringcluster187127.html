fingering

noun
touching with the fingers
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of touching or handling something with one's fingers

Examples

Fingering the goods
He was caught fingering the merchandise in the shop, raising suspicions of theft.
Repeated fingering of the artwork can damage its fragile surface.
The detective noticed the fingering of the drawer’s handle by the suspect.
