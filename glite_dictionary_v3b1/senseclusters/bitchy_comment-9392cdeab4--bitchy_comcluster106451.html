bitchy comment

noun
spiteful or mean remark
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtʃi ˈkɑːmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃi ˈkɒmənt / (BrE)
a remark that is spiteful, mean, or harshly critical, often delivered in a petty or malicious way

Examples

A bitchy comment made
He regretted his bitchy comment as soon as he saw her reaction.
She always makes bitchy comments about what people are wearing.
Sometimes, their meetings are filled with bitchy comments and unnecessary drama.
