rubber

noun
condom; contraceptive sheath
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
(informal) A contraceptive device made of thin rubber or latex, worn over the penis during intercourse to prevent pregnancy or sexually transmitted infections; a condom.

Examples

A rubber condom
He realized he'd forgotten to bring a rubber with him to the date.
His friend discreetly handed him a spare rubber before the party.
In some countries, asking for a rubber in a pharmacy could cause confusion.

Related Terms
