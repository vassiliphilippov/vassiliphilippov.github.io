conterminous

adjective
having the same extent or duration
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàt…úr.m…ô.n…ôs / (AmE), / k…ônÀàt…úÀê.m…™.n…ôs / (BrE)
Having the same extent, scope, or duration as something else; coextensive or coincident in limits.

Examples

Conterminous periods
The treaty established borders that are conterminous with those of the previous agreement.
The two research projects were conterminous in both scope and duration.
Their powers were conterminous, each lasting exactly five years.
