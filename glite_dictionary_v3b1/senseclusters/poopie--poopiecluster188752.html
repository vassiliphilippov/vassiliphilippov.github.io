poopie

noun
childish word for feces
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːpi / (AmE), / ˈpuːpi / (BrE)
a childish or informal word for feces or excrement, often used by or with young children

Examples

A stinky poopie
The little boy told his mother he needed to go poopie before bedtime.
She laughed when her daughter said she had made a poopie in her diaper.
Parents often use the word poopie when toilet training their toddlers.
