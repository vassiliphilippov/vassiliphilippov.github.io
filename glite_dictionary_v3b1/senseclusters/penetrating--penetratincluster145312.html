penetrating

adjective
able to pierce or affect deeply
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.əˌtreɪ.t̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.ɪˌtreɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to enter, pierce, or affect deeply; having the power to penetrate.

Examples

A penetrating wind
A penetrating chill swept through the house when the door was left open.
She has a penetrating gaze that makes people uncomfortable.
The penetrating noise from the alarm woke everyone in the building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt - not sharp or able to pierce
  • synonym: sharp - able to cut or pierce easily
