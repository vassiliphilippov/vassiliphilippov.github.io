Tursiops

noun
genus of dolphins
Pronunciation: / tɝˈsaɪ.ɑps / (AmE), / tɜːˈsaɪ.ɒps / (BrE)
A genus of marine mammals in the family Delphinidae, which includes the bottlenose dolphins.
