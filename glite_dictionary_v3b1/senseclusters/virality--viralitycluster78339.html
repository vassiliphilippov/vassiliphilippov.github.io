virality

noun
rapid online spread
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈræləti / (AmE), / vaɪˈræləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of spreading rapidly and widely, especially through the internet or social media

Examples

Social media virality
The virality of the meme led to millions of shares within just a few hours.
Marketers study virality to understand how content spreads so quickly online.
Achieving virality can make a product or idea instantly famous around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or not well known
  • synonym: popularity - the state of being liked or admired by many people
