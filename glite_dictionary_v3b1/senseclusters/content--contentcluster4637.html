content

noun
state of satisfaction or happiness
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛnt / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnt / (BrE)
a state of satisfaction or happiness with what one has or is experiencing

Examples

A sense of content
After a long walk in the countryside, he felt a deep sense of content.
Many people spend their lives seeking content rather than material success.
She ate her meal to her heart’s content, enjoying every bite.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contentment - the feeling of being contented; state of happiness and satisfaction
  • antonym: discontent - a feeling of dissatisfaction or unhappiness
