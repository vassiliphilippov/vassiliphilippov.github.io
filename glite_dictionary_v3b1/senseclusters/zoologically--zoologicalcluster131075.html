zoologically

adverb
in animal science terms
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌzoʊ.əˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌzəʊ.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the science or study of animals

Examples

Classified zoologically
The two animals are zoologically classified in different families despite their similar appearance.
Researchers examined the fossils zoologically to determine their evolutionary relationships.
Although they share a habitat, the species are zoologically unrelated.
