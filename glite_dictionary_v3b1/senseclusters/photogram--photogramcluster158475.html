photogram

noun
camera-less photo image
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəɡræm / (BrE)
a photographic image made by placing objects directly onto light-sensitive paper and exposing it to light, without using a camera

Examples

A photogram image
The artist displayed a striking photogram made with feathers and coins at the gallery.
In her photography class, she learned how to create a photogram using only sunlight and paper.
Each photogram is unique because the arrangement of objects changes the final image.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photograph - an image made using a camera and light-sensitive material
  • synonym: scanogram - an image created by placing objects on a scanner instead of photographic paper
