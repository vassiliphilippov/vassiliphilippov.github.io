tech hub

noun
center for technology startups and innovation
Pronunciation: / tɛk hʌb / (AmE), / tɛk hʌb / (BrE)
a place or region where technology companies, especially startups, are concentrated and innovation is encouraged

Examples

A tech hub city
Dozens of new companies have launched since the tech hub opened its doors last year.
Silicon Valley is considered the world's leading tech hub for startups and established technology firms.
The city is investing in infrastructure to become a major tech hub in the region.
