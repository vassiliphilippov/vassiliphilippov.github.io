carby

noun
informal for carburettor
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrbi / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbi / (BrE)
an informal term for a carburettor, the device in older petrol engines that mixes air and fuel for combustion

Examples

Clean the carby
He cleaned the carby to fix the engine's rough idle.
The mechanic said the carby needed replacing after years of use.
Older cars often have a carby instead of a fuel injection system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carburettor - a device in petrol engines that mixes air and fuel
  • antonym: fuel injector - a device that injects fuel directly into an engine
