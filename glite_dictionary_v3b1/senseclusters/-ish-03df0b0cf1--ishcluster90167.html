-ish

x
approximate suffix
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪʃ / (AmE), / ɪʃ / (BrE)
used as a suffix to mean 'approximately', 'somewhat', or 'like' when added to words, especially times, numbers, or adjectives

Examples

Seven-ish time
Let's meet at seven-ish if that works for you.
Her dress was a blue-ish color that matched her eyes.
He said he would arrive at noon-ish, but he was a bit late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: around - approximately; about a certain time or amount
  • antonym: exactly - precisely; without any approximation
