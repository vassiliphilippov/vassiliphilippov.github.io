chronological record

noun
account of events in time order
6.2 5.0
Pronunciation: / krəˌnɑːlədʒɪkəl ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / krəˌnɒlədʒɪkəl ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a written account of events arranged in the order in which they occurred, especially year by year

Examples

A chronological record of events
Medical staff keep a chronological record of a patient's symptoms and treatments in their file.
The chronological record revealed how the company expanded its operations internationally year by year.
The historian maintained a detailed chronological record of the town's development over several decades.
