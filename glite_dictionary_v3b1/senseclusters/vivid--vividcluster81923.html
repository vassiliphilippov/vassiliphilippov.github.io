vivid

adjective
evoking lifelike images in the mind
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪvɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɪvɪd / (BrE)
producing strong, clear, and lifelike images or feelings in the mind

Examples

Vivid memory
His memory of the accident is still so vivid that it feels like it happened yesterday.
She gave a vivid description of her journey through the rainforest.
The author’s vivid storytelling makes the characters come alive in your imagination.
His novel is filled with vivid descriptions that bring the setting to life.
She still has a vivid memory of her first day at school.
The dream was so vivid that it felt like reality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clear - easy to perceive or understand; not vague or confused
  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness, interest, or clarity
  • synonym: graphic - giving a clear and explicit picture
  • synonym: lifelike - very similar to the person or thing represented
