Synonyms

  • obtain
    2.6 75/m
    obtain means getting something often by effort or request, while acquire stresses gaining or developing skills or qualities over time.
  • procure
    6.8 3.33/m
    procure is to get something by special effort or arrangements, while acquire is more general about gaining or developing things or skills.
  • get
    1.0 1472/m
    get is an informal, very general verb for receiving or obtaining, while acquire suggests effort, learning, or formal obtaining over time.
