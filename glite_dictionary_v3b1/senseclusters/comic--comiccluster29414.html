comic

noun
picture story magazine
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪk / (BrE)
a magazine or book that tells a story using pictures and words, often in a series of panels

Examples

A comic book
Every Saturday, I buy a new comic from the bookstore near my house.
He has a large collection of superhero comics in his room.
The comic made me laugh with its funny characters and clever jokes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graphic novel - a book-length story told using comic-strip format
  • synonym: manga - Japanese comic books or graphic novels
