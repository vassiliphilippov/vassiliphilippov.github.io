parmigiano

noun
Italian hard cheese
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrmɪˈdʒɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɑːmɪˈdʒɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a hard, dry Italian cheese with a strong flavor, often grated over pasta dishes

Examples

Grated parmigiano cheese
She grated fresh parmigiano over her homemade lasagna before serving it.
The chef recommended adding a sprinkle of parmigiano to enhance the flavor of the risotto.
At the market, he bought a wedge of authentic parmigiano imported from Italy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheddar - a firm, sharp-tasting English cheese
  • synonym: parmesan - a hard, aged Italian cheese similar to parmigiano
