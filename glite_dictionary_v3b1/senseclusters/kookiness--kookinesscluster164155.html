kookiness

noun
playful eccentricity
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːkinəs / (AmE), / ˈkuːkinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being strange, eccentric, or quirky, often in a playful or odd way

Examples

Her kookiness showed
Her kookiness made everyone at the party smile.
The actor is known for his kookiness both on and off the screen.
A little kookiness can make life much more interesting.
Her kookiness made her stand out at every party she attended.
Some people appreciate a little kookiness in their friends because it keeps life interesting.
The film's kookiness attracted a cult following among fans of quirky comedies.

  • antonym: normality - the state of being usual or expected
  • synonym: quirkiness - the quality of being unusual in an interesting or amusing way
  • synonym: weirdness - the quality of being very strange or unusual
