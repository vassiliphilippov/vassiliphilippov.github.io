unsound

adjective
not based on valid reasoning
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaʊnd / (BrE)
not based on valid or reliable reasoning or evidence; containing a fallacy, error, or being unreliable or faulty.
