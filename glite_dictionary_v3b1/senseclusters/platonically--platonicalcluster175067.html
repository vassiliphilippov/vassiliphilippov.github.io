platonically

adverb
non-romantic friendship
5.8
Pronunciation: / pləˈtɑnɪkli / (AmE), / pləˈtɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is friendly and affectionate but not romantic or sexual

Examples

Love platonically
They have always cared for each other platonically, never crossing the line into romance.
Even though they spent a lot of time together, their relationship remained strictly platonically based.
She values being able to connect platonically with friends of any gender.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friendlily - in a friendly manner
  • antonym: romantically - in a way that involves love or romance
