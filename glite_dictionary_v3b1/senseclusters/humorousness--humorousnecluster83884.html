humorousness

noun
the quality of being humorous
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmərəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being funny or amusing; the characteristic of provoking laughter or amusement

Examples

Natural humorousness
Despite the tense situation, he couldn't help but admire her natural humorousness.
Her humorousness always lightened the mood at even the dullest meetings.
The teacher's humorousness made learning enjoyable for the whole class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humor - the quality of being amusing or comic
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious, not funny or joking
