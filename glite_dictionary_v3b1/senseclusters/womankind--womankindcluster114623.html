womankind

noun
all women collectively
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌm.ənˌkaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈwʊm.ənˌkaɪnd / (BrE)
all women considered as a group, in contrast to men

Examples

All womankind
He dedicated his research to the advancement of womankind in science.
The novel celebrates the strength and resilience of womankind.
Throughout history, womankind has overcome many obstacles to achieve equality.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: mankind - all humans collectively, sometimes men specifically
  • synonym: womanhood - the state of being a woman; women collectively
