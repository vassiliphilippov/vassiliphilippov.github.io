credo

noun
set of beliefs or principles
7.5 2/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkr…õdo ä / (AmE), / ÀàkriÀêd…ô ä / (BrE)
A set of beliefs or principles that guide someone's actions or a group's ideology.

Examples

A credo of beliefs
'Work hard, play hard' has always been his credo in life.
Her personal credo is to treat others with kindness and respect.
The organization operates according to a strict credo of honesty and transparency.
