daypack

noun
small backpack
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌpæk / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌpæk / (BrE)
a small backpack for carrying essentials on short trips, hikes, or daily outings

Examples

A lightweight daypack is perfect for carrying essentials during city sightseeing.
He always keeps a rain jacket in his daypack just in case the weather changes.
