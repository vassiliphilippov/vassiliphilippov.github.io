hoped-for

adjective
wanted or expected with hope
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpt fɔr / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpt fɔː / (BrE)
that is wanted or expected, especially with hope or desire for a positive result

Examples

The hoped-for result
After years of hard work, she finally received the hoped-for promotion.
The rain that the farmers had hoped-for arrived just in time to save the crops.
Their hoped-for victory eluded them despite their best efforts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticipated - regarded as likely; expected
  • antonym: unexpected - not expected or anticipated
