Mullah Mohammed Omar

proper noun
Afghan Taliban leader (c. 1950–2013)
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊlɑː moʊˈhæməd ˈoʊmɑːr / (AmE), / ˈmʊlɑː məˈhæməd ˈɒːmɑː / (BrE)
(c. 1950–2013) An Afghan spiritual and political leader who founded and led the Taliban, establishing a strict interpretation of Islamic law in Afghanistan.

Examples

Mullah Mohammed Omar led Taliban
Mullah Mohammed Omar led the Taliban during their control of Afghanistan in the late 1990s.
