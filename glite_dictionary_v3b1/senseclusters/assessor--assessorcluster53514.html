assessor

noun
person who evaluates
4.3
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛsər / (AmE), / əˈsɛsə / (BrE)
a person who evaluates or judges the quality, value, or importance of something

Examples

Independent assessor
An independent assessor was hired to review the safety standards of the building.
The assessor carefully examined each artwork before giving a final value.
Every year, a tax assessor visits the neighborhood to update property values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appraiser - a person who estimates the value of something, especially property
  • synonym: evaluator - a person who judges or determines the value of something
  • synonym: judge - a person who forms an opinion or decision about something
