Plattdeutsch

noun
Low German dialect of northern Germany
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑtˌdɔɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈplatˌdɔɪtʃ / (BrE)
A German dialect, also known as Low German, spoken mainly in northern Germany and parts of the Netherlands.

Examples

Plattdeutsch dialect
He spoke fluent Plattdeutsch with his grandparents, especially during family gatherings.
Many traditional songs from northern Germany are written in Plattdeutsch.
The conference included lectures delivered in both standard German and Plattdeutsch.
