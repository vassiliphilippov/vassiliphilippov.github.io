haul-away

noun
waste removal vehicle
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔl əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːl əˌweɪ / (BrE)
a vehicle or truck used to remove and transport waste, debris, or unwanted items

Examples

A haul-away truck
The old refrigerator was picked up by the haul-away early this morning.
After the renovation, a haul-away came to collect all the construction debris.
They scheduled a haul-away to remove the pile of unwanted furniture from the driveway.
