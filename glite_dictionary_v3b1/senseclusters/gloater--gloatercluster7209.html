gloater

noun
person who boasts
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊtər / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊtə / (BrE)
a person who shows excessive or malicious satisfaction, especially at someone else's misfortune or failure

Examples

A gloater after winning
After winning the contest, he became an unbearable gloater around his friends.
Nobody likes a gloater who rubs their victory in everyone else's face.
She tried not to be a gloater when her team won the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: humble person - someone who does not show off or take pleasure in others' failures
