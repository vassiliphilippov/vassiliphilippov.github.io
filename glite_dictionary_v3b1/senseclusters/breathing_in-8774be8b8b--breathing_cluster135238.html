Synonyms

  • inhalation
    4.1 1/m
    inhalation is the formal medical word for taking air into the lungs, while breathing in is the everyday phrase.
  • inspiration
    4.1 0.07/m
    inspiration often refers to drawing air into the lungs and can also mean a sudden idea, while breathing in is literal.
  • aspiration
    5.9 0.3/m
    aspiration is used in medicine for inhaling substances into the lungs and can be accidental, while breathing in is neutral.
  • intake
    3.5 0.3/m
    intake often means the amount or flow of air taken in, while breathing in refers to the simple act of drawing air into the lungs.
