mull

verb
think deeply about; consider carefully
Pronunciation: / mʌl / (AmE), / mʌl / (BrE)
to think about something deeply and for a long time, especially before making a decision

Examples

To mull a decision
After receiving the job offer, I needed some time to mull it over.
He likes to mull his ideas before sharing them with the team.
She sat by the window to mull over the choices she had to make.
She mulled the job offer carefully for several days before finally replying to the company.
He mulled over the idea of moving abroad before talking to his family.
After the meeting, the team mulled the proposal before making a final decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consider - think carefully about, especially in making decisions
  • antonym: decide - to make a choice after thinking
  • antonym: ignore - refuse to consider or pay attention to
  • synonym: ponder - think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
