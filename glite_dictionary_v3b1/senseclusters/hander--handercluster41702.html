hander

noun
person who hands items
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhændər / (AmE), / ˈhændə / (BrE)
a person who gives or passes something to another person by hand

Examples

The hander passes documents
The hander quickly passed the documents to the manager during the meeting.
In the relay race, the hander made sure the baton was securely given to the next runner.
As a tool hander, she ensured the mechanic always had the right equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giver - a person who gives something
  • antonym: receiver - a person who receives something
