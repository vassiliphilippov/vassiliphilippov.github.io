doorsill

noun
horizontal piece at the bottom of a doorway
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔːrˌsɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌsɪl / (BrE)
the horizontal piece forming the bottom of a doorway, providing support and passage between rooms or into a building

Examples

The doorsill of a house
Rainwater had started to seep under the old wooden doorsill.
She wiped her shoes carefully on the doorsill before entering the house.
They replaced the damaged doorsill to prevent drafts from coming inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: threshold - the strip of wood, stone, or metal at the bottom of a doorway
