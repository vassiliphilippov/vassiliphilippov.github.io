reptile-like

adjective
cold or unemotional in demeanor
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛp.taɪlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɛp.taɪlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) cold, unemotional, or unfeeling in manner, resembling the demeanor of a reptile.

Examples

Reptile-like behavior
His reptile-like stare made everyone uncomfortable during the interview.
She always maintained a reptile-like calm, even under pressure.
The CEO's reptile-like attitude left her colleagues feeling uneasy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: warm-hearted - kind, sympathetic, and caring
