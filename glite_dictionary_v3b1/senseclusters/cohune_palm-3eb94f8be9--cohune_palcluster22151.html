cohune palm

noun

Pronunciation: /kə.ˈhuːn pɑːm/ /; / /koʊ.ˈhuːn pɑːm/ (AmE), /kə.ˈhjuːn pɑːm/ /; / /kə.ˈhuːn pɑːm/ (BrE)
A tall tropical American palm tree (Attalea cohune) whose large nuts yield valuable oil and a substance used as vegetable ivory.

Examples
