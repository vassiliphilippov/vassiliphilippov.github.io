periodogram

noun
frequency strength chart
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɪə.rɪ.ə.ˈgræm/ /; / /pə.rɪ.ə.ˈgræm/ (AmE), /pɪə.rɪ.ə.ˈgræm/ /; / /pə.rɪ.ə.ˈgræm/ (BrE)
a graph or chart that shows the strength of different frequencies in a set of data, often used to analyze signals
