Reubenite

noun
tribe of Reuben member
Pronunciation: / ˈruːbəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈruːbənaɪt / (BrE)
a member of the tribe of Reuben, one of the twelve tribes of Israel in the Hebrew Bible

Examples

A Reubenite leader
The Reubenite families settled east of the Jordan River according to the biblical account.
As a Reubenite, he was proud of his tribe's heritage and traditions.
The ancient territory of the Reubenite tribe lay to the east of the Dead Sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Israelite - a member of the ancient Hebrew nation
  • synonym: Levite - a member of the tribe of Levi in the Hebrew Bible
