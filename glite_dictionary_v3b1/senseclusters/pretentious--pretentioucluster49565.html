Synonyms

  • ostentatious
    7.7 1.07/m
    ostentatious is visibly showy and designed to attract attention, while pretentious stresses pretending to be more cultured or important.
  • highfalutin
    9.3 0.26/m
    highfalutin is an informal word for pretending to be very important or clever, while pretentious is more general and formal.
  • high-sounding
    8.2 0.07/m
    high-sounding describes grand words or ideas that lack real meaning, while pretentious means trying to impress others by false importance.
  • showy
    3.2 0.67/m
    showy is about bright or fancy appearance meant to catch the eye, while pretentious focuses on seeming more cultured or important.
  • pompous
    5.8 2.40/m
    pompous acts with an exaggerated sense of self-importance and formality, while pretentious stresses trying to impress by seeming superior.
