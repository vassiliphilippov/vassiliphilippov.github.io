gunpoint

noun
threat with a gun
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
the condition of being threatened with a gun in order to force someone to do something

Examples

Forced at gunpoint
The bank manager was forced to open the safe at gunpoint during the robbery.
She signed the documents at gunpoint, fearing for her life.
The hostages were held at gunpoint until the police arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coercion - the act of making someone do something by force or threats
  • synonym: duress - threats or force used to make someone do something
