Asio otus

noun
species of owl with long ear tufts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪsi.oʊ ˈoʊtəs / (AmE), / ˈæsɪəʊ ˈəʊtəs / (BrE)
A slender European owl with long ear tufts, found mainly in coniferous forests; also known as the long-eared owl.
