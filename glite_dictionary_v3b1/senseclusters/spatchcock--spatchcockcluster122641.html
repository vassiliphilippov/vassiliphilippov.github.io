spatchcock

verb
insert words into a text
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspætʃˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈspætʃˌkɒk / (BrE)
to interpolate or insert words, especially in a way that is awkward or hasty, into a sentence or story

Examples

To spatchcock words in
She spatchcocked several unnecessary comments into the middle of the discussion.
The author decided to spatchcock a few extra jokes into the final draft of the novel.
Trying to spatchcock an explanation into his report made it sound less convincing.
