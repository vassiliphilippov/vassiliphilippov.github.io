frothing

adjective
covered with or producing foam or froth
7.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɑː.θɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfrɔː.θɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfrɒ.θɪŋ/ (BrE)
