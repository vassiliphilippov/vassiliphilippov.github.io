momo

noun
South Asian dumpling
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊməʊ / (BrE)
a steamed or fried dumpling filled with meat or vegetables, popular in South Asian cuisine

Examples

A plate of momo
We ordered a plate of chicken momo at the restaurant.
She learned how to make delicious vegetable momo from her grandmother.
The street vendor sold hot, spicy momo to hungry customers on a cold evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dumpling - a small mass of dough cooked by boiling, steaming, or frying, often with a filling
  • synonym: gyoza - a Japanese dumpling filled with meat or vegetables
