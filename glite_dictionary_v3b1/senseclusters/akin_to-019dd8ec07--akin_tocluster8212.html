akin to

adjective
similar to; comparable to
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪn tu / (AmE), / əˈkɪn tuː / (BrE)
having a similar quality or character to something; comparable to.

Examples

Akin to shock
His reaction was akin to shock when he heard the unexpected news.
The architecture of the new museum is akin to that of ancient Greek temples.
The experience of skydiving is akin to flying for the first time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unlike - dissimilar or different from
