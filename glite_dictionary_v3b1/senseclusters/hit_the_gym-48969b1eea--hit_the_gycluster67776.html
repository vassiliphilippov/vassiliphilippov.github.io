hit the gym

verb
go to the gym to exercise
Pronunciation: / hɪt ðə dʒɪm / (AmE), / hɪt ðə dʒɪm / (BrE)
to go to the gym or fitness center in order to exercise

Examples

To hit the gym regularly
After work, I usually hit the gym for an hour to unwind.
He's been hitting the gym regularly to prepare for the marathon.
She decided to hit the gym instead of going out for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: work out - to exercise, especially for physical fitness
