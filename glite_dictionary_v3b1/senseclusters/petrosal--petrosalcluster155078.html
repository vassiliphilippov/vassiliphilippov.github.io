petrosal

adjective
of the petrous bone
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊsəl / (AmE), / pɛˈtrəʊsəl / (BrE)
relating to the petrous part of the temporal bone in the skull

Examples

Petrosal bone area
The surgeon carefully avoided the petrosal region during the procedure.
Damage to the petrosal bone can affect hearing and balance.
An infection in the petrosal area may lead to serious complications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petrous - relating to the hard, dense part of the temporal bone
