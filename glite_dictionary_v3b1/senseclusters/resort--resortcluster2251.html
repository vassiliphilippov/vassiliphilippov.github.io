resort

noun
thing or person relied on for help or security
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is turned to for help, protection, or a solution, especially as a final option

Examples

A resort for help
Charities often become a resort for people in desperate circumstances.
The family saw their lawyer as a last resort during the legal dispute.
When negotiations failed, military action became the only resort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recourse - the use of someone or something as a source of help in a difficult situation
  • synonym: refuge - something providing shelter or protection
  • synonym: resource - something that can be used for support or help
