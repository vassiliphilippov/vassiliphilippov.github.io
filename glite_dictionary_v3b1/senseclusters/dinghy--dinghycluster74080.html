dinghy

noun
small open rowing or sailing boat
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋi / (BrE)
a small open boat, often with a shallow draft, used for rowing, sailing, or as a tender to larger vessels

Examples

A small dinghy boat
Each cabin had its own dinghy for exploring the nearby islands.
The yacht was anchored offshore, so we took the dinghy to reach the beach.
They rowed the dinghy across the calm lake early in the morning.
