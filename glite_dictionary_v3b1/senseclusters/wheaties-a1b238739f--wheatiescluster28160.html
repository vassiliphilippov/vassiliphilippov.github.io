Wheaties

noun
U.S. wheat penny
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːtiz / (AmE), / ˈwiːtiz / (BrE)
an informal name for a U.S. one-cent coin produced from 1909 to 1958, featuring two wheat stalks on the reverse side
