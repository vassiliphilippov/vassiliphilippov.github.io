Wikipedia

verb
to check on Wikipedia
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪkɪˈpiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌwɪkɪˈpiːdiə / (BrE)
to look up or check information by searching on Wikipedia, especially informally.

Examples

To Wikipedia a topic
Before the quiz, he quickly Wikipediaed the main facts about the topic.
If you are unsure about the event, just Wikipedia it for a summary.
They decided to Wikipedia the author to learn more about her background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: research - to investigate or study a subject in detail
