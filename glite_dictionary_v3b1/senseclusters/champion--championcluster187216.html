champion

noun
active supporter or defender
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæm.pi.ən /; / ˈtʃæmpiən / (AmE), / ˈtʃæm.pi.ən /; / ˈtʃæmpiən / (BrE)
a person who strongly supports or defends a person, cause, or belief

Examples

Champion of justice
Every cause needs a dedicated champion to fight for its success.
He became the leading champion of reform in the local government.
She was a passionate champion of women's rights throughout her career.
He has always been a champion of free speech, refusing to back down.
Many animal welfare groups have found a champion in the renowned actress.
She became a champion for children's rights after witnessing injustice.

  • antonym: opponent - someone who opposes or competes against another
