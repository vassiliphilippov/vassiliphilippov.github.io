mere mortal

noun
ordinary person (not special or powerful)
Pronunciation: / mɪr ˈmɔrtəɫ / (AmE), / mɪə ˈmɔːtəɫ / (BrE)
an ordinary person, especially as contrasted with someone who is considered to have special abilities, status, or power

Examples

A mere mortal person
Despite his fame, the actor insists he's just a mere mortal at heart.
No mere mortal could solve that complex puzzle so quickly.
Winning the championship was a dream for any mere mortal in the sport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ordinary person - a person without special abilities or status
  • antonym: superhuman - a person with abilities much greater than ordinary humans
