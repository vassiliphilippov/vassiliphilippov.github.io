last quarter

noun
lunar phase following the full moon
1.8 3.4
Pronunciation: /læst ˈkwɔːr.tər/ (AmE), /lɑːst ˈkwɔː.tə/ (BrE)
the final phase of the Moon's cycle when half of the Moon's disk appears illuminated, following the full moon and preceding the new moon

Examples

The last quarter Moon
Astronomers observed a dramatic change as the Moon entered its last quarter.
The last quarter of the Moon will be visible in the early hours of the morning.
Tides are generally weaker during the last quarter phase.

Related Terms

  • antonym: first quarter - the phase when half the Moon is illuminated and increasing
