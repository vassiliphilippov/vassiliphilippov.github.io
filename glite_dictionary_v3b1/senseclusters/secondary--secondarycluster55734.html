Synonyms

  • lower-ranking
    3.8 0.13/m
    lower-ranking describes people or jobs with less status or rank, while secondary can mean less important or simply later.
  • second
    1.0 395/m
    second means coming directly after the first in order, while secondary often suggests lower importance or a later, less central role.
  • subaltern
    9.9 0.50/m
    subaltern refers to someone lower in rank or status, often in formal or military settings, while secondary is more general.
