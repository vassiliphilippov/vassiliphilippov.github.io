Columbia

proper noun
poetic US name; place
Pronunciation: / kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a historical or poetic name for the United States, or the name of various places, rivers, and institutions in North America

Examples

Poetic Columbia
The Columbia River is one of the largest rivers in North America.
In the 19th century, poets often referred to the United States as Columbia.
She graduated from Columbia University with a degree in engineering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: United States - the country in North America also known as the USA
