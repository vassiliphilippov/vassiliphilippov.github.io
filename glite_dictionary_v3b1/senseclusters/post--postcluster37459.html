post

noun
upright marker pole or stake
Pronunciation: / poʊst / (AmE), / pəʊst / (BrE)
a long, sturdy pole or stake set upright in the ground, used to support something or as a marker

Examples

A fence post
A white post was placed at each corner of the field to mark the boundaries.
The goal was scored when the ball bounced off the post and into the net.
The runners gathered at the starting post before the race began.
A wooden post supported the mailbox by the roadside.
Each fence post was painted white to match the house.
He tied the horse to the post near the entrance of the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marker - an object used to indicate a position or boundary
  • synonym: pole - a long, slender, rounded piece of wood or metal, typically set upright
  • synonym: stake - a strong wooden or metal post used as a marker or support
