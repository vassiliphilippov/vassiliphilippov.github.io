Examples

A fence post

Synonyms

  • stake
    4.8 7.08/m
    a stake is a pointed post driven into the ground for marking or fencing, while a post is sturdier and often permanent support or marker.
  • pole
    1.0 25/m
    a pole is a long, thin round rod used for support or tools, while a post is thicker, stronger, and set firmly in the ground.
  • upright
    5.5 0.45/m
    an upright is a vertical support that forms part of a frame, while a post is a standalone sturdy stake used as marker or support.
