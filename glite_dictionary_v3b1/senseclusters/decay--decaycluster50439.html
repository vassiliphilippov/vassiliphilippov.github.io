Synonyms

  • rot away
    4.2 0.8/m
    rot away means slowly breaking down, often in plants or wood, while decay covers many kinds of gradual falling into ruin.
  • ruin
    3.5 3/m
    ruin describes becoming unusable or destroyed, often from damage or neglect, while decay focuses on natural breakdown over time.
  • deteriorate
    5.9 7/m
    deteriorate means getting worse in condition or quality, often in formal contexts, while decay implies physical breakdown or rot.
