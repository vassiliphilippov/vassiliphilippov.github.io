recontact

verb
to contact again
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkɑːntækt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkɒntækt / (BrE)
to get in touch with someone again after a previous contact

Examples

To recontact a client
If we do not hear back, we will recontact you in a few days.
The researcher decided to recontact participants for a follow-up interview.
Please recontact our office if your address changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contact - to communicate with someone
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately not pay attention to someone
