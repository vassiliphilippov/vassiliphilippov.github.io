pseudomorphic

adjective
imitating appearance
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈmɔːrfɪk /; / ˌsuːdəˈmɔːrfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈmɔːfɪk /; / ˌsjuːdəˈmɔːfɪk / (BrE)
