hemocyte

noun
invertebrate blood cell
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːməˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhiːməˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a blood cell in invertebrates that plays a role in immune responses and other bodily functions

Examples

Hemocyte blood cell
Scientists observed an increase in hemocyte activity after the insect was exposed to bacteria.
The immune system of many invertebrates relies on specialized hemocytes to fight infections.
Researchers counted the number of hemocytes present in the crab's circulatory fluid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erythrocyte - a red blood cell in vertebrates
  • synonym: leukocyte - a white blood cell in vertebrates
