eggdrop soup

noun
Chinese soup with beaten eggs in broth
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌdrɑp suːp / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌdrɒp suːp / (BrE)
A Chinese soup consisting of beaten eggs poured into hot broth, creating fine strands or flakes.
