tortoise-like

adjective
withdrawn or cautious
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtəsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtəsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
showing a tendency to withdraw, act cautiously, or avoid attention, as if retreating into a shell like a tortoise

Examples

Tortoise-like behavior
After the argument she became tortoise-like, avoiding calls and staying inside for days.
His tortoise-like behavior made it difficult for him to make new friends at school.
Whenever faced with criticism, he would respond in a tortoise-like manner, withdrawing from the conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outgoing - friendly and eager to meet new people
  • synonym: reserved - not openly expressing feelings or thoughts
  • synonym: shy - nervous or uncomfortable with other people
