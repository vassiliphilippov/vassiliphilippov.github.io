dissever

verb
to divide or separate into parts
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛvər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛvə / (BrE)
to separate or divide something into parts; to break apart

Examples

To dissever rope
A sharp blade can easily dissever rope into smaller pieces.
After centuries of unity, the kingdom was finally dissevered by civil war.
The harsh words dissevered their friendship beyond repair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divide - to separate into parts, groups, or sections
  • synonym: separate - to set or keep apart; to divide
  • antonym: unite - to join together into a single whole
