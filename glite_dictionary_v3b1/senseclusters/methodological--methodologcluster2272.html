methodological

adjective
relating to methods or methodology
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθ.ə.dəˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛθ.ə.dəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the system of methods and principles used in a particular discipline or study.

Examples

A methodological review
A methodological review helps ensure consistent procedures across experiments.
She proposed a new methodological approach to data collection.
The study was criticized for its serious methodological flaws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: methodical - done according to a systematic or established procedure
