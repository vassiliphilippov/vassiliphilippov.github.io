palliasse

noun
straw- or sawdust-filled mattress
Pronunciation: / ˈpæliˌæs / (AmE), / ˈpaliˌas / (BrE)
a narrow or thin mattress stuffed with straw or similar material, often used as a simple bed

Examples

A palliasse mattress
During his travels, he slept on a simple palliasse laid out in the corner of the room.
She replaced the old palliasse with a new, thicker mattress for more comfort.
The soldiers were issued only a palliasse and a blanket for their bunks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mattress - a large, flat pad to support the reclining body
