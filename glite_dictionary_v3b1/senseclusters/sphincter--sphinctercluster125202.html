sphincter

noun
ring-shaped muscle that closes a body opening
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪŋktɚ / (AmE), / ˈsfɪŋktə / (BrE)
A ring of muscle that contracts to close a natural opening in the body.

Examples

The sphincter muscle
Regular exercises can strengthen the pelvic floor muscles, including the anal sphincter.
The sphincter at the end of the esophagus helps prevent acid reflux.
The doctor explained how a damaged sphincter can cause incontinence.
