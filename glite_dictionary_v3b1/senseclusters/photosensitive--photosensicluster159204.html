photosensitive

adjective
sensitive to light
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
sensitive to visible light or other forms of radiant energy, often responding physically or chemically.

Examples

Photosensitive skin reaction
Children with certain medical conditions can be extremely photosensitive and must avoid direct sunlight.
The new film is highly photosensitive, allowing for clearer photographs in low-light conditions.
UV lamps should be handled with care around photosensitive chemicals to prevent unwanted reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: light-sensitive - sensitive to light (synonym)
  • antonym: photophobic - adverse or sensitive to light; avoiding light (antonym)
