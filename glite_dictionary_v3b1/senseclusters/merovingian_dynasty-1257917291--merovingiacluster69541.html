Merovingian dynasty

noun
early Frankish royal family (c. 500–750 AD)
9.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈrɑː.vɪn.dʒən ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ /; / /me.ˈrɑː.vɪn.dʒən ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ (AmE), /mə.ˈrɒ.vɪn.dʒən ˈdɪn.ə.stiː/ /; / /me.ˈrɒ.vɪn.dʒən ˈdɪn.ə.stiː/ (BrE)
the Frankish royal family that ruled parts of present-day France and Germany from about 500 to 750 AD, founded by Clovis I.

Examples
