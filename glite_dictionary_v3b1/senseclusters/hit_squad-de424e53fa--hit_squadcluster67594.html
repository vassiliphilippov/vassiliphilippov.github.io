hit squad

noun
group hired to assassinate
7.5
Pronunciation: / hɪt skwɑd / (AmE), / hɪt skwɒd / (BrE)
a group of people hired or organized to kill someone, especially for political or criminal reasons

Examples

A hit squad group
A notorious hit squad operated in the city during the 1980s.
He lived in fear after hearing that a hit squad had been sent after him.
The police believe a hit squad was responsible for the recent attack.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone for political or monetary reasons
