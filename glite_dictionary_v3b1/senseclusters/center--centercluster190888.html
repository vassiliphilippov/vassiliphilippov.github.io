center

noun
politically moderate people; centrists
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɚ /; / ˈsɛn(t)ər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
politically moderate persons or groups, especially as opposed to those on the far left or right; the centrists in politics
