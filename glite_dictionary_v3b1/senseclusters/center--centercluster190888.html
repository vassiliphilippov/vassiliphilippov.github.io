center

noun
politically moderate people; centrists
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɚ /; / ˈsɛn(t)ər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
politically moderate persons or groups, especially as opposed to those on the far left or right; the centrists in politics

Examples

Center in politics
In recent years, the country's center has shifted toward more progressive policies.
Many voters identify with the center rather than the extremes of the political spectrum.
The party is trying to appeal to the center ahead of the upcoming election.
Debate often focuses on the extremes, while the centre is overlooked.
Many voters in the election identified themselves as members of the centre.
The new policy aims to attract support from the political centre.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrist - a person whose political opinions are not extreme; moderate
  • antonym: left - the group or position supporting progressive or socialist policies
  • antonym: right - the group or position supporting conservative policies
