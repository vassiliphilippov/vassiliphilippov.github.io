frou-frou

adjective
elaborately showy; excessively decorative
9.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfruÀêÀåfruÀê / (AmE), / ÀàfruÀê.fruÀê / (BrE)
very elaborate, showy, or excessively decorative in style or appearance, often to the point of being overly ornate

Examples

Very frou-frou style
I don't usually go for frou-frou decor, but the room looked beautiful.
She thought the wedding invitations were far too frou-frou for her taste.
That restaurant serves good food without any frou-frou presentation.
