puparium

noun
insect pupal shell
Pronunciation: / pjuˈpɛriəm / (AmE), / pjuˈpɛːriəm /; / pjuːˈpeəriəm / (BrE)
the hardened outer shell or case formed by some insect larvae during the pupal stage to protect the developing insect inside

Related Terms

  • synonym: cocoon - a silky case spun by insect larvae for protection during development
  • antonym: larva - the immature, wingless, feeding stage of an insect
  • synonym: pupa - an insect in its inactive, immature form between larva and adult
