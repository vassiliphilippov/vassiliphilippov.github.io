puparium

noun
insect pupal shell
9.9
Pronunciation: /pjuː.ˈper.iː.əm/ /; / /puː.ˈper.iː.əm/ (AmE), /pjuː.ˈpeə.riː.əm/ /; / /puː.ˈpeə.riː.əm/ (BrE)
the hardened outer shell or case formed by some insect larvae during the pupal stage to protect the developing insect inside
