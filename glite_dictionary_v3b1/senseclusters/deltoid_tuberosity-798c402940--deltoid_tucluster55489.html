deltoid tuberosity

noun
raised area of humerus for deltoid muscle attachment
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛl.tɔɪd tuˌbɛɹˈɒs.ɪ.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈdɛl.tɔɪd tjuːˌbɛrˈɒs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a rough, raised area on the lateral surface of the humerus bone where the deltoid muscle attaches

Examples

The deltoid tuberosity bone
A fractured humerus near the deltoid tuberosity can affect arm mobility.
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the deltoid tuberosity during orthopedic procedures.
The deltoid tuberosity is an important landmark for muscle attachment in the upper arm.
