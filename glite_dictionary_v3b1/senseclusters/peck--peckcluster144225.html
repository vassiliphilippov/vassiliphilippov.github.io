peck

noun
a light or quick kiss
Pronunciation: / pɛk / (AmE), / pɛk / (BrE)
a quick, light kiss, typically on the cheek

Examples

A quick peck on the cheek
He gave his grandmother a quick peck on the cheek before leaving.
He leaned in for a peck, but she turned away and laughed.
She greeted her friend with a friendly peck at the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kiss - to touch or press with the lips as an expression of affection
  • degree higher: smooch - a long, affectionate kiss
