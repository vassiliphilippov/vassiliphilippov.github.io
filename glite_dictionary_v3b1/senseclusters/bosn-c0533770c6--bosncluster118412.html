bo's'n

noun
petty officer overseeing crew on a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊsən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊsən / (BrE)
a petty officer on a merchant ship who is in charge of the deck crew and oversees their work

Examples

The bo's'n supervises crew
After years as a deckhand, he was promoted to bo's'n aboard the cargo ship.
The bo's'n gave orders to the sailors as the storm approached.
The crew respected the bo's'n for his strict but fair leadership.
