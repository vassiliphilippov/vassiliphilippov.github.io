oysterman

noun
oyster harvester
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪstərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstəˌmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to gather, farm, or sell oysters

Examples

The oysterman harvests oysters
The experienced oysterman taught his son how to harvest oysters from the bay.
Every morning, the oysterman rows out to check his oyster beds.
Local restaurants buy their shellfish directly from a trusted oysterman.
The oysterman checked his traps early every morning before sunrise.
Local restaurants buy their oysters directly from a trusted oysterman on the coast.
After a long day at sea, the oysterman returned with a boat full of fresh oysters.
