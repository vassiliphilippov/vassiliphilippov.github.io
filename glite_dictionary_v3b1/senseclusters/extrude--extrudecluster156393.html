extrude

verb
force material through a die or opening
5.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈstruːd/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈstruːd/ (BrE)
to form or shape a substance by forcing it through a specially shaped opening

Examples

To extrude metal
Molten metal is extruded to create rods and sheets for construction.
The factory uses a machine to extrude plastic pipes of various diameters.
To make pasta, the dough is extruded through different shaped dies.
