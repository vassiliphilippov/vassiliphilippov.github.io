callused

adjective
hardened skin
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.əst / (AmE), / ˈkæl.juːst / (BrE)
having hard, thickened skin that has developed from repeated friction or pressure

Examples

Callused hands
After years of playing guitar, his fingertips became callused and tough.
The farmer's callused palms were a sign of his hard labor.
She wore gloves to prevent her hands from getting callused during the workout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardened - made or become hard or tough
  • antonym: soft - having a smooth or gentle texture, not hard
