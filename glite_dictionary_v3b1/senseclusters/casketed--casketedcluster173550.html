casketed

adjective
enclosed in a box
Pronunciation: / ˈkæskɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑːskɪtɪd / (BrE)
enclosed or contained within a small ornamental box or case, especially for safekeeping or display

Examples

Casketed jewels
The precious necklace was casketed in velvet and displayed in the gallery.
Each artifact was carefully casketed to protect it from dust and damage.
The family heirloom remained casketed for decades, untouched by anyone.
