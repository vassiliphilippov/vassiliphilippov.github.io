criminal possession

noun
illegal possession of prohibited property
1.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nəl pə.ˈzeʃ.ən/ /; / /ˈkrɪm.ə.nəl pə.ˈzeʃ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nəl pə.ˈzeʃ.ən/ /; / /ˈkrɪm.ə.nəl pə.ˈzeʃ.ən/ (BrE)
(law) The act of having or controlling property that is prohibited by law or may be possessed only under certain circumstances, making such possession subject to criminal sanctions.

Examples

Criminal possession of stolen property
He faced two counts of criminal possession of stolen property.
New laws have expanded what is considered criminal possession in the state.
The suspect was charged with criminal possession of a firearm after the search.
