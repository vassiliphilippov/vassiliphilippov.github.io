zebra crossing

noun
pedestrian crossing with striped markings
3.5 2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈziːbrə ˈkrɔːsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈzɛbrə ˈkrɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of pedestrian crossing on a road, marked with broad white stripes, where pedestrians have the right of way.

Examples

A zebra crossing
Always stop when someone is waiting at the zebra crossing.
She waved to thank the driver as she walked across the zebra crossing.
The school installed a new zebra crossing to keep children safe.
