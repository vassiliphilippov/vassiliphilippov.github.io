pollock

noun
North Atlantic food and game fish
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlək / (AmE), / ˈpɒlək / (BrE)
A commercially important North Atlantic fish of the cod family, valued as food and game fish.

Examples

A pollock fish
Many fish sticks are made from pollock because of its mild flavor.
Scientists are monitoring the population of Atlantic pollock to ensure sustainable fishing.
The fisherman caught a large pollock off the coast of Norway.
