jet

verb
travel by jet plane
Pronunciation: / dʒɛt / (AmE), / dʒet /; / dʒɛt / (BrE)
to travel by jet plane or to travel quickly, especially by airplane

Examples

To jet across cities
She often jets to New York for business meetings.
They jetted across the Atlantic in under six hours.
We jetted off to Paris for the weekend.
She is jetting to the party now and will arrive soon.
Business executives often jet around the world for important meetings.
They jetted off to Paris for a spontaneous weekend getaway.
