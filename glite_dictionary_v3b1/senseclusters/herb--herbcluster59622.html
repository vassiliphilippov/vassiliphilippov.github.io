herb

noun
non-woody flowering plant
1.0 5.63/m
Pronunciation: / h…ùÀêb / (AmE), / h…úÀêb / (BrE)
a plant that does not have a permanent woody stem and often dies down to the ground after flowering; many herbs are cultivated for their flowers, leaves, or medicinal properties.

Examples

Wild herb
A dandelion is a common herb found in many gardens.
Some gardeners grow herbs for their medicinal benefits as well as their beauty.
This region's native plants include a variety of flowering herbs and wildflowers.

Synonyms

  • herbaceous plant
    4.5 0.11/m
    herbaceous plant is non-woody and dies back each season to regrow from roots, while herb people use adds flavor, scent, or medicine.

Related Terms
