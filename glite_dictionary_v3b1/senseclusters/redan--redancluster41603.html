redan

noun
V-shaped fortification
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdæn / (AmE), / rɪˈdæn / (BrE)
a V-shaped fortification projecting outward from a defensive line, used to provide cover and fire in two directions

Examples

Military redan
The soldiers took cover behind the redan during the attack.
Engineers constructed a redan to strengthen the defensive line on the hillside.
From the top of the redan, the defenders could fire in two directions at the advancing enemy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bastion - a projecting part of a fortification allowing defensive fire in several directions
  • synonym: rampart - a defensive wall or bank around a fort
