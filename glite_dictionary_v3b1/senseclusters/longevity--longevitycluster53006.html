longevity

noun
duration in a job or position
Pronunciation: / lɑnˈdʒɛvəti / (AmE), / lɒnˈdʒɛvəti / (BrE)
the length of time a person remains in a particular job, position, or organization; duration of service

Examples

Longevity in a job
Few athletes achieve such longevity in professional sports.
Her impressive longevity at the company earned her widespread respect among her colleagues.
The longevity of his political career surprised many critics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tenure - the period of holding a position or office
