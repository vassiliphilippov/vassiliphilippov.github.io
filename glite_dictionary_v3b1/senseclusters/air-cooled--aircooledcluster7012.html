air-cooled

adjective
cooled by circulating air
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌkuːld / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌkuːld / (BrE)
(of an engine, machine, or device) cooled by circulating air rather than by liquid.

Examples

Air-cooled engine
Some generators are air-cooled to simplify maintenance and reduce the risk of leaks.
The mechanic explained how the air-cooled system differed from modern water-cooled engines.
The vintage motorcycle was equipped with an air-cooled engine that performed well on country roads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: water-cooled - cooled by circulating water
