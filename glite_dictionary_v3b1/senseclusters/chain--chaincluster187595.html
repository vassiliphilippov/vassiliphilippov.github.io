chain

noun
necklace made of linked objects
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
A necklace made by stringing metal links, beads, or similar objects together and worn around the neck.

Examples

Gold chain necklace
He gave her a silver chain as an anniversary gift.
She wore a delicate gold chain around her neck every day.
The jeweler displayed a beautiful pearl chain in the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necklace - an ornamental chain or string of beads worn around the neck
