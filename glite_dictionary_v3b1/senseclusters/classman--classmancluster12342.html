classman

noun
student in same year
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsmən / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsmən / (BrE)
a student who is in the same class or year as another student, especially at a school or college

Examples

College classman
He met his old classman at the college reunion.
Each classman received a special pin to mark their graduation year.
She and her classman worked together on the final project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classmate - a fellow student in the same class or course
  • synonym: schoolmate - a fellow student at the same school
