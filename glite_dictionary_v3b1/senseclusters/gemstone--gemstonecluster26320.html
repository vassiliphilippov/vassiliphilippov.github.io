Synonyms

  • gem
    1.3 2.79/m

  • jewel
    1.8 6.95/m
    a jewel can mean the polished stone or the finished ornament set in jewelry, while a gemstone names the stone itself.
  • precious stone
    3.4 0.52/m
    a precious stone names a rare, highly valued gem like diamond or ruby, while a gemstone covers both precious and semi-precious stones.
  • stone
    4.5 4.31/m
