gemstone

noun
a stone used in jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmstəʊn / (BrE)
a precious or semi-precious stone that has been cut and polished for use in jewelry

Examples

A gemstone necklace
Emeralds and sapphires are popular types of gemstone used in fine jewelry.
She chose a deep blue gemstone for the pendant on her necklace.
The collector displayed each gemstone under a bright light to show its brilliance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jewel - a precious stone, typically one that has been cut and polished; sometimes used more generally for ornamental items
