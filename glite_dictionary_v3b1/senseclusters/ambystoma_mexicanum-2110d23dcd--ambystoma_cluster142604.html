Ambystoma mexicanum

noun
Mexican salamander (axolotl)

A species of salamander native to the lakes of Mexico, known for remaining in its larval form throughout its life; commonly called the axolotl.

Examples

The Ambystoma mexicanum is an important model organism in scientific research due to its ability to regenerate limbs.
Unlike most salamanders, the Ambystoma mexicanum usually retains its gills and aquatic lifestyle throughout its life.
