hired gun

noun
person hired for a tough task
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚd ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
a person who is employed to perform a specific task, especially one that is difficult or requires ruthlessness

Examples

A hired gun solves problems
He became the firm's top hired gun for solving complex legal disputes.
She made a reputation as a hired gun who could turn failing businesses around.
The company relied on a hired gun for the tough negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consultant - an expert hired to give advice or take action in a specific area
  • synonym: mercenary - a person primarily concerned with making money at the expense of ethics; a freelance soldier
