demolisher

noun
one who destroys buildings
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːlɪʃər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or machine that destroys or tears down buildings or other structures

Examples

A demolisher machine
The old factory was brought down by a powerful demolisher in just a few hours.
As a skilled demolisher, he knew exactly where to place the explosives for a safe collapse.
The city hired a demolisher to clear the site for new construction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: builder - a person who constructs buildings
  • synonym: destroyer - someone or something that destroys
