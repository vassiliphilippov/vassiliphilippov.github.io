Fanti

proper noun
Ghanaian ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌti / (AmE), / ˈfænti / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group in Ghana, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

A Fanti person
The Fanti have a long history of fishing along the coast of Ghana.
She is learning to speak Fanti to communicate with her grandmother.
Many Fanti festivals celebrate their heritage and community values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Ewe - another major ethnic group in Ghana
