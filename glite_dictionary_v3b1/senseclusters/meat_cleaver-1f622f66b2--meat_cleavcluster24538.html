meat cleaver

noun
large square-bladed butcher's knife
7.8
Pronunciation: / mit ˈkliːvər / (AmE), / miːt ˈkliːvə / (BrE)
a large knife with a rectangular blade, used by butchers for cutting meat and bones

Examples

A sharp meat cleaver
A sharp meat cleaver is essential for preparing large cuts of meat.
She purchased a new meat cleaver for her kitchen.
The butcher used a meat cleaver to chop through the bone.
