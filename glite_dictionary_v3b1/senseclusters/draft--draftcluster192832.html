draft

noun
act of pulling a load
8.1
Pronunciation: / dræft / (AmE), / drɑːft / (BrE)
the act of moving a load by drawing or pulling, especially with animals or machinery

Examples

Draft horses pull
In early railroads, horse teams provided the draft necessary to move the carriages.
The oxen performed the draft that pulled the heavy plow through the fields.
The wagon was designed to reduce resistance during draft by a team of mules.
The efficiency of the engine has improved the process of draught in transporting heavy materials.
The strong horse was used for draught work on the farm.
This wagon is designed for easy draught across rough terrain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draft - act of moving a load by drawing or pulling (American spelling)
  • synonym: haul - the act of pulling something with effort
  • antonym: push - the act of exerting force to move something away
