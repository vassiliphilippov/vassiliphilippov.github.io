dopiness

noun
state of being dazed
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊpinəs / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being slow-witted, silly, or appearing dazed or confused

Examples

Comic dopiness
His dopiness made everyone laugh during the meeting.
After staying up all night, she felt a strong sense of dopiness at work.
The movie's main character is known for his lovable dopiness and silly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertness - the quality of being quick to notice or respond
  • synonym: dullness - the quality of lacking intelligence or quickness
