invitingly

adverb
in an appealing or attractive way
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is attractive, appealing, or encouraging someone to approach or participate.

Examples

Smiled invitingly
She smiled invitingly at her friends, encouraging them to join the conversation.
The aroma of fresh bread wafted invitingly from the small bakery on the corner.
The door was left invitingly open, as if welcoming guests to enter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tantalizingly - in a tempting or teasing manner
