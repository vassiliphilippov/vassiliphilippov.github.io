lockage

noun
fee for passing through a canal lock
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɒkɪdʒ / (BrE)
a fee charged for passing through a lock in a canal or waterway

Examples

Lockage fee
Barges moving through the waterway are required to pay a lockage.
The lockage for commercial vessels has increased over the past year.
The company factored the cost of lockage into their shipping rates.
