posterior

adjective
at or towards the rear; at the back
3.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈstɪriɚ / (AmE), / pɒˈstɪəriə / (BrE)
located at or towards the back or rear of something, especially in anatomy.

Examples

Posterior side
In human anatomy, the posterior side of the body refers to the back.
Some fish have bright markings on their posterior fins to warn predators.
The posterior part of the brain controls many visual functions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anterior - situated at or towards the front, especially in anatomy
