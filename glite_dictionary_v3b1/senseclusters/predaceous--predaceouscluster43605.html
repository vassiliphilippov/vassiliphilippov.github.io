predaceous

adjective
living by preying on others
7.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdeɪʃəs / (AmE), / prɪˈdeɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of animals or people) living by preying on others or victimizing others for personal gain.

Examples

Predaceous people exploit
He warned against trusting predaceous individuals who exploit the vulnerable.
Some companies have developed predaceous business strategies to eliminate competitors.
The lions are predaceous hunters that stalk their prey in the savanna.
