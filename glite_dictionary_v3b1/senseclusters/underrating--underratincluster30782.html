underrating

noun
an estimate that is too low
2.4 4.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.də.ˈreɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ən.də.ˈreɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈreɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ən.də.ˈreɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
an estimate or assessment that is lower than the true or actual value

Examples

An underrating of talent
His talents have long been subject to underrating by critics.
Insurance claims are sometimes denied because of the underrating of property values.
The company suffered huge losses due to the underrating of project costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underestimate - a lower assessment than the actual value; to judge as less than the real worth
