ring

noun
criminal organization or group
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ / (BrE)
a group of people involved in illegal activities, often working together as a criminal organization

Examples

A criminal ring
A drug ring was responsible for distributing cocaine throughout the region.
Authorities finally broke up the notorious gambling ring operating in the city.
The police uncovered an international ring smuggling rare artifacts across borders.
