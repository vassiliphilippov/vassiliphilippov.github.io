pliant

adjective
easily bent or flexible
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈplaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈplaɪənt / (BrE)
easily bent, shaped, or molded; physically flexible or adaptable

Examples

Yoga practitioners often strive for a pliant body that moves with ease.
