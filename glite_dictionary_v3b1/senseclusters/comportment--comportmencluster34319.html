comportment

noun
formal or dignified behavior
2.7 6.6
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɔrtmənt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɔːtmənt / (BrE)
The way in which a person behaves or conducts themselves, especially in a formal or dignified manner.

Examples

Calm comportment
Even under pressure, his calm comportment made a strong impression on the audience.
Her impeccable comportment earned her the respect of colleagues at every event.
The judge's stern comportment emphasized the seriousness of the proceedings.
