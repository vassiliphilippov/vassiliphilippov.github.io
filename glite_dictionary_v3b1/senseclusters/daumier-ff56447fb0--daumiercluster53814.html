Daumier

proper noun
French satirical artist and painter
Pronunciation: / doʊˈmjɛr / (AmE), / dəʊˈmjɛː / (BrE)
Honoré Daumier (1808–1879), a French painter, caricaturist, and printmaker, renowned for his satirical lithographs of bourgeois society.

Examples

Daumier lithographs
Many critics consider Daumier's lithographs masterpieces of social commentary and artistic technique.
Studying Daumier gives insight into political satire during nineteenth-century France.
The exhibition featured several paintings and sculptures by Daumier.
