voluntaristic

adjective
based on voluntary action
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌvɑːləntəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌvɒləntəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
based on or characterized by voluntary action rather than by force or legal obligation

Examples

Voluntaristic approach
The organization follows a voluntaristic approach, encouraging people to participate without any pressure.
Her voluntaristic attitude inspired others to join the community project willingly.
A voluntaristic system relies on individuals choosing to help rather than being required to do so.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coercive - using force or threats to make someone do something
  • synonym: voluntary - done by choice or free will
