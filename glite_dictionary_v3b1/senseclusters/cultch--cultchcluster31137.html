cultch

verb
to seed oyster beds
9.9
Pronunciation: / kʌltʃ / (AmE), / kʌltʃ / (BrE)
to spread broken shells, stones, or other hard material on the sea bottom to provide a surface for oyster larvae or other shellfish to attach and grow

Examples

To cultch a bay
The marine biologists plan to cultch the bay to help restore the oyster population.
Local fishermen cultched the shallow waters with shells to encourage new oyster growth.
Each spring, volunteers gather to cultch the estuary and support the shellfish industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seed - to plant or introduce something to encourage growth
