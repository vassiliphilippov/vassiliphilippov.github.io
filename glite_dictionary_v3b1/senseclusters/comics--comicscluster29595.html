comics

noun
illustrated stories
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmɪks / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪks / (BrE)
stories or strips that combine drawings and text, usually published in magazines, books, or online

Examples

Collecting comics
He has a large collection of comics from different countries and decades.
Many people enjoy reading comics online as well as in print.
The library hosts a special event for fans of comics every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comic strip - a short series of drawings telling a story, often in newspapers
  • synonym: graphic novel - a book-length story told with drawings and text
