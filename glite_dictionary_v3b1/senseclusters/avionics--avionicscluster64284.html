avionics

noun
electronics for aircraft or spacecraft
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæviˈɑnɪks / (AmE), / ˌaviˈɒnɪks / (BrE)
The science and technology concerned with the development and use of electronic systems and devices in aircraft and spacecraft.

Examples

Modern avionics systems
Advances in avionics have greatly improved flight safety and efficiency.
Engineers specializing in avionics are in high demand in the aerospace industry.
Modern aircraft rely extensively on avionics for navigation, communication, and flight control.
