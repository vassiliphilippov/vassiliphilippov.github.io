kirsch

noun
clear brandy from cherries
Pronunciation: / kɪrʃ / (AmE), / kɪəʃ / (BrE)
A clear, colorless brandy distilled from the fermented juice of black morello cherries, often used in cooking and as a drink.

Examples

A glass of kirsch
She enjoyed sipping a small glass of kirsch after dinner.
The recipe called for a generous splash of kirsch to enhance the cherry flavor.
Traditional Black Forest cake is often made with kirsch for an authentic taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherry brandy - brandy flavored or made from cherries
