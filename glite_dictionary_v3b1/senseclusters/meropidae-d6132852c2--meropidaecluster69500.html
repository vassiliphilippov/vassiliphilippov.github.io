Meropidae

noun
family of bee-eating birds
Pronunciation: / məˈrɑːpɪˌdiː / (AmE), / məˈrɒpɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of brightly colored, slender birds known as bee-eaters, found mainly in Africa, Eurasia, and Australasia.
