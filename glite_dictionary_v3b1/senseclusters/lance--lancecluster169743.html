lance

verb
move swiftly and forcefully
3.0
Pronunciation: / læns / (AmE), / lɑːns / (BrE)
to move swiftly and forcefully, as if cutting through something

Examples

To lance forward
A falcon lanced through the blue sky after its prey.
Shouts erupted as the runner lanced past his opponents toward the finish line.
The speedboat lanced across the waves, leaving a white spray in its wake.
