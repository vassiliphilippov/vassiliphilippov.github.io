inwale

noun
boat interior support
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌweɪl / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌweɪl / (BrE)
a long, horizontal support running along the inside of a boat's hull to add strength and rigidity

Examples

Boat inwale support
The old canoe's inwale was replaced to ensure the boat remained sturdy.
After inspecting the inwale, the builder decided it needed extra reinforcement.
A strong inwale helps the hull keep its shape during rough water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunwale - the upper edge or rail of a boat's side
  • antonym: outwale - a longitudinal structural member along the outside of a boat's hull
