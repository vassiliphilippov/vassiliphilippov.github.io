accountably

adverb
in a responsible way
5.8
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊntəbli / (AmE), / əˈkaʊntəbli / (BrE)
in a way that shows responsibility and willingness to explain or justify actions

Examples

Act accountably
The manager acted accountably by explaining every decision to the team.
All financial records must be kept accountably for future audits.
She handled the project accountably, ensuring every step was documented and justified.
The manager acted accountably by admitting the mistake and correcting it promptly.
Public officials are expected to behave accountably when handling community funds.
She always tries to respond accountably to feedback from her team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irresponsibly - in a way that shows lack of responsibility
  • synonym: responsibly - in a way that shows responsibility
  • synonym: transparently - in a way that is open and easy to understand
