mesocarp

noun
middle layer of a fruit's pericarp
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…õs…ôÀåk…ërp / (AmE), / Ààm…õs…ô äÀåk…ëÀêp / (BrE)
the middle layer of the pericarp (fruit wall) in a fruit, situated between the outer exocarp and the inner endocarp

Examples

The mesocarp layer
In some fruits, the mesocarp is dry and not edible.
The fleshy mesocarp of a peach is the part we usually eat.
The thickness of the mesocarp can vary greatly between different types of fruit.
