pink bollworm

noun
larva pest damaging cotton bolls
9.5
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk ˈboʊlˌwɜrm / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈbəʊlˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a small moth larva that feeds on the seeds within cotton bolls, known for being a significant pest to cotton crops

Examples

The pink bollworm larva
Farmers implemented various methods to control the pink bollworm and reduce crop losses.
Infestation by the pink bollworm can significantly lower the yield and quality of cotton.
The pink bollworm has devastated cotton fields throughout the southern United States.
