basket fish

noun
starfish-like animal with branched arms
8.5 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæ.skɪt fɪʃ/ /; / /ˈbæ.skət fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.skɪt fɪʃ/ /; / /ˈbɑː.skət fɪʃ/ (BrE)
A starfish-like marine animal with slender, complexly branched, interlacing arms radiating from a central disc, belonging to genera such as Euryale, Astrophyton, or Gorgonocephalus.
