basket fish

noun
starfish-like animal with branched arms
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskɪt fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt fɪʃ / (BrE)
A starfish-like marine animal with slender, complexly branched, interlacing arms radiating from a central disc, belonging to genera such as Euryale, Astrophyton, or Gorgonocephalus.

Examples

A basket fish animal
Divers were thrilled to spot a rare basket fish clinging to the coral at dawn.
Scientists study the feeding habits of the basket fish to learn more about deep-sea ecosystems.
The arms of a basket fish form intricate patterns, making them fascinating to observe in the wild.
