spill

noun
a sudden fall or tumble
7.5
Pronunciation: / spɪl / (AmE), / spɪl / (BrE)
a sudden fall, especially from an upright position or while in motion

Examples

Take a spill
During the race, one of the skiers had a bad spill on the slope.
He laughed off his spill on the wet floor, insisting he was fine.
She took a spill while riding her bicycle and scraped her knee.
