Caledonian

adjective
of Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˌkælɪˈdoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈdəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to Scotland, especially the Scottish Highlands, or to things of Scottish origin, often in historical contexts

Examples

Caledonian mountains
The Caledonian landscape is known for its rugged mountains and deep lochs.
Many legends are rooted in Caledonian history and folklore.
The Caledonian pine forests are among the oldest woodlands in Europe.
