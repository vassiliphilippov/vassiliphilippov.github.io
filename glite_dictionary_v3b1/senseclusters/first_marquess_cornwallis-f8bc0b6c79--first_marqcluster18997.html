First Marquess Cornwallis

proper noun
British commander in American War of Independence
9.8
Pronunciation: / fɜrst mɑrˈkwɛs kɔrnˈwɑlɪs / (AmE), / fɜːst mɑːˈkwɪs kɔːnˈwɒlɪs / (BrE)
(1738–1805) British military commander and colonial administrator; led British forces in the American War of Independence and was defeated at Yorktown.
