Orange Free State

proper noun
former province in central South Africa
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪndʒ friː steɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ friː steɪt / (BrE)
a former province in central South Africa, colonized by the Boers, now called Free State since 1997.

Examples
