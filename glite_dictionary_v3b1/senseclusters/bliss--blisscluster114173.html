bliss

verb
to make blissful
6.4
Pronunciation: / blɪs / (AmE), / blɪs / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel great happiness or joy; to make blissful.

Examples

To bliss someone out
The surprise picnic blissed her, lifting her spirits for the entire weekend.
He blissed out when he heard the good news about his promotion.
The music at the festival blissed everyone in the crowd.
