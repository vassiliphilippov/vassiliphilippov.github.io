liberalness

noun
generosity; open-mindedness
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbərəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪb(ə)rəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being generous and open-minded in behavior and temperament

Examples

Personal liberalness
Her liberalness was evident in the way she always helped those in need.
Many admired his liberalness when it came to sharing resources with less fortunate communities.
The liberalness of the new policy encouraged creativity and collaboration in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generosity - the quality of being generous
  • synonym: liberality - the quality of giving freely; generosity
