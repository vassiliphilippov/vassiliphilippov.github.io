uncalculating

adjective
sincere, not scheming
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkælkjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not acting in a scheming, self-interested, or manipulative way; sincere and straightforward.

Examples

Uncalculating kindness
Her uncalculating kindness made everyone feel at ease.
He gave an uncalculating answer, showing he had nothing to hide.
People appreciated her uncalculating generosity and open nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calculating - acting in a planned, often self-interested or manipulative way
  • synonym: honest - truthful and sincere, not deceitful
  • antonym: scheming - planning or acting in a secret or dishonest way
