oligomeric

adjective
few-unit structure
9.8
Pronunciation: /ə.lɪ.ˈɡoʊ.mər.ɪk/ (AmE), /ə.lɪ.ˈɡəʊ.mə.rɪk/ (BrE)
consisting of a few repeating units, especially referring to molecules made of a small number of monomers

Examples

Oligomeric protein units
The enzyme is oligomeric, meaning it is made up of several protein subunits.
Researchers studied the oligomeric state of the molecule to understand its function.
Some toxins are oligomeric proteins that assemble into larger complexes in the body.
