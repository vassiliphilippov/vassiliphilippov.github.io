hat tip

noun
gesture of acknowledgment or respect
Pronunciation: / ˈhæt tɪp / (AmE), / ˈhæt tɪp / (BrE)
an act or expression of acknowledgment, respect, or thanks, especially in writing or speech

Examples

A hat tip to experts
At the end of the talk, the speaker gave a hat tip to her research team.
Many developers offer a hat tip to open-source contributors in their documentation.
The journalist included a hat tip to the original source in his article.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nod - a slight inclination of the head as a sign of respect or acknowledgment
