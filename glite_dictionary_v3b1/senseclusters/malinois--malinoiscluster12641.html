malinois

noun
Belgian fawn-colored sheepdog breed
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmæ.lɪ.nwɑ / (AmE), / ˈmal.in.wɑː / (BrE)
A breed of medium-sized, fawn-colored short-haired sheepdog originally from Belgium, often used as a working or police dog.

Examples

A malinois dog
My friend adopted a malinois because of their intelligence and energy.
The malinois excelled in the agility competition at the dog show.
The police department uses a trained malinois for search and rescue missions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Belgian shepherd - a group of sheepdogs from Belgium, including the Malinois
  • synonym: German shepherd - a similar but distinct breed of large herding dog often used for police work
