instream

adjective
within streamed media
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌstriːm / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌstriːm / (BrE)
occurring within or delivered during a continuous digital media stream, especially referring to advertising or content inserted into streamed media.

Examples

Instream ads
The platform introduced instream ads that play during live video broadcasts.
Many podcasts now include instream sponsorship messages between segments.
Viewers complained about the frequency of instream advertisements interrupting their favorite shows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mid-roll - an advertisement that plays during streamed content
  • antonym: outstream - an advertisement that appears outside of streamed media content
  • synonym: pre-roll - an advertisement that plays before streamed content begins
