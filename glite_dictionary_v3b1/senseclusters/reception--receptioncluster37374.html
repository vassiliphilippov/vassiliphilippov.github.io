reception

noun
formal party, especially after a wedding
1.8
Pronunciation: / ɹɪˈsɛp.ʃən / (AmE), / ɹɪˈsɛp.ʃən / (BrE)
a formal social gathering, especially held after a ceremony such as a wedding, to welcome guests

Examples

Wedding reception
After the graduation ceremony, there was a small reception in the courtyard.
The company hosted a formal reception for its international clients.
We attended a beautiful wedding reception at the hotel ballroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a large formal meal for many people, often to celebrate an event
  • synonym: ceremony - a formal religious or public occasion, especially one celebrating an event
