undecagon

noun
eleven-sided polygon
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɛkəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɛkəɡən / (BrE)
A plane figure with eleven straight sides and eleven angles.

Examples

A regular undecagon
In geometry class, we learned how to construct a regular undecagon using only a compass and straightedge.
The ancient coin was shaped like an undecagon, making it unique among the other circular pieces.
To calculate the area of an undecagon, you need to know the length of its sides.
