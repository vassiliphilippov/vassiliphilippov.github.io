tap

verb
hit lightly, gently touch
Pronunciation: / tæp / (AmE), / tæp / (BrE)
to hit or touch something lightly, or the light sound or result of such an action

Examples

To tap the table
He tapped the glass with his pen and everyone fell silent.
She tapped me on the arm to get my attention.
The teacher gently tapped the board to emphasize her point.
Could you please stop tapping your foot? It's very distracting.
He absentmindedly tapped on the window pane, waiting for an answer.
She started to tap her pencil against the desk during the meeting.
