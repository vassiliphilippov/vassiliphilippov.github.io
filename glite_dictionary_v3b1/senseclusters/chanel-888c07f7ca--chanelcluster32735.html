Chanel

proper noun
French fashion designer
3.4
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈnɛl / (AmE), / ʃəˈnɛl / (BrE)
Gabrielle 'Coco' Chanel (1883–1971), a French fashion designer and businesswoman who founded the Chanel fashion house and transformed women's fashion.

Examples

Chanel the designer
The museum's exhibit celebrated Chanel for her influential designs that changed women's fashion worldwide.
Many people admire Chanel for her innovative approach to style and elegance.
In the 1920s, Chanel introduced the iconic little black dress to the world.
