caretaking

noun
temporary management
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrˌteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeəˌteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the temporary management or administration of an organization, office, or public body until a permanent official is appointed

Examples

Caretaking administration
The board was responsible for the caretaking of the company until a new CEO was hired.
During the transition period, a committee handled the caretaking of the organization.
After the principal retired, the vice principal took over the caretaking of the school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interim administration - temporary leadership until a permanent replacement is found
  • antonym: permanent management - ongoing, official management by a designated leader
