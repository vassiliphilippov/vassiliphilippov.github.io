family man

noun
man devoted to family life
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli mæn / (AmE), / ˈfæməli mæn / (BrE)
a man who places great importance on his family and family life

Examples

A family man at home
After his son was born, he became more of a family man and less interested in late nights out.
He has always been known as a family man who spends weekends with his wife and children.
The politician promoted his image as a devoted family man during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bachelor - an unmarried man, especially one not in a permanent relationship
