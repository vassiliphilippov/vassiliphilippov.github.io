compact powder

noun
pressed facial cosmetic powder
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpækt ˈpaʊ.dɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈpækt ˈpaʊ.də / (BrE)
a cosmetic powder for the face, pressed into a solid form and stored in a compact case, used to reduce shine and set makeup

Examples

Compact powder case
After finishing her foundation, she applied a layer of compact powder for a matte finish.
Many women prefer compact powder because it is easy to carry and apply.
She reached into her purse and took out her compact powder to touch up her makeup.
