numbing

adjective
causing numbness or insensitivity
5.6 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌm.bɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈnʌm.bɪŋ/ (BrE)
causing a loss of physical or emotional sensation; making someone unable to feel or react.

Examples

Numbing pain
She stared at the news in numbing disbelief, unable to process what had happened.
The numbing cold made it difficult for anyone to stay outside for long.
The pain was so intense that the doctor applied a numbing cream to the skin.
