light-green

adjective
pale green color
2.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /laɪt griːn/ (AmE), /laɪt griːn/ (BrE)
of a pale shade of green, like the color of fresh grass; of the color between blue and yellow in the color spectrum, but lighter than standard green

Examples

A light-green color
A carpet of light-green grass covered the entire hillside.
She chose a light-green dress for the spring wedding.
The walls were painted a soft light-green to brighten up the room.
