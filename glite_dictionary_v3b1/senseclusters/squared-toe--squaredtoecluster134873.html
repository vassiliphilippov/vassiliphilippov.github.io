squared-toe

adjective
having a toe section that is square-shaped
9.5
Pronunciation: / skwɛrd toʊ / (AmE), / skwɛːd təʊ / (BrE)
(of shoes or boots) having a toe section that is squared off rather than rounded or pointed.

Examples

Squared-toe boots
He bought a pair of squared-toe boots for the wedding.
The designer’s latest collection features several squared-toe styles.
These squared-toe shoes are very popular this season.
