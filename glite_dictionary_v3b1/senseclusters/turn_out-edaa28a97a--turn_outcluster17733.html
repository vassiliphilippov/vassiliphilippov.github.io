Synonyms

  • crowd
    1.6 8/m
    crowd means to fill a place in large numbers and may cause cramped space, while turn out focuses on people coming for an event.
  • gather
    1.0 50/m
    gather often implies people or things come together or are brought together, while turn out usually highlights many people arriving for an event.
  • attend
    1.7 50/m
