secondo

noun
Italian main course
7.7
Pronunciation: / seˈkondoʊ / (AmE), / seˈkɒndəʊ / (BrE)
the main course in a traditional Italian meal, usually consisting of meat or fish

Examples

A secondo dish
For her secondo, she chose roasted chicken with potatoes and rosemary.
The waiter recommended the grilled sea bass as the secondo of the evening.
After enjoying the pasta, we eagerly awaited the arrival of the secondo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antipasto - the appetizer course in an Italian meal
  • synonym: main course - the principal dish in a meal
