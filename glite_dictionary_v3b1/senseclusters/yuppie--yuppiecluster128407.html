yuppie

noun
young urban professional with an affluent lifestyle
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌpi / (AmE), / ˈjʌpi / (BrE)
a young middle-class professional who has a well-paid job and an affluent lifestyle, usually in a city

Examples

Young yuppies in cities
Critics say yuppies are more concerned with image than substance.
He was the classic yuppie, always dressed in expensive suits and driving a sports car.
The neighborhood was once popular with artists but is now attracting more yuppies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bohemian - a person, especially an artist or writer, who lives unconventionally
  • synonym: professional - someone who works in a profession, especially a well-paid city job
