looking glass

noun
old-fashioned term for mirror
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkɪŋ ˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈlʊkɪŋ ˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a mirror, especially one used for dressing

Examples

A looking glass mirror
He noticed a crack running through the looking glass, distorting his reflection.
She admired her new hat in the looking glass before leaving the room.
The antique looking glass hung above the fireplace, framed in delicate gold.
