sist

verb
to set upright; to stop
Pronunciation: / sɪst / (AmE), / sɪst / (BrE)
(archaic) to cause something to stand upright or to bring something to a stop or halt.

Examples

To sist a banner
The knight ordered his squire to sist the broken banner in the ground to mark the camp.
In ancient ceremonies, priests would sist sacred objects before the altar.
The commander signaled the troops to sist their march at the city gates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erect - to set something upright
  • synonym: halt - to bring to a stop
