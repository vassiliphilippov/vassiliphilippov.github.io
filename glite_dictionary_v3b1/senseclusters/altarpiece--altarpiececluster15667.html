altarpiece

noun
decorative panel behind an altar
Pronunciation: / ˈɔltɚˌpis / (AmE), / ˈɔːltəˌpiːs / (BrE)
A painted or carved panel or screen placed above and behind an altar in a church, often decorated with religious imagery.

Examples

A altarpiece in church
Restorers spent years carefully cleaning the altarpiece to reveal its original colors.
The church’s magnificent altarpiece was painted by a famous Renaissance artist.
Tourists flocked to see the intricate carvings on the medieval altarpiece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reredos - a decorated screen or wall behind an altar
  • synonym: retable - an architectural structure above and behind an altar
