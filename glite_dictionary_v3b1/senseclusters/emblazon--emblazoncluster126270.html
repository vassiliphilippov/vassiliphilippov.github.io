emblazon

verb
decorate with heraldic arms or designs
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbleɪzən / (AmE), / ɪmˈbleɪzən /; / ɪmˈbleɪz(ə)n / (BrE)
to decorate or adorn something in a vivid or noticeable way, often with symbols, designs, or bright colors

The banners emblazoned with the team logo lined the stadium walls.
The family crest was emblazoned on the front gate for all to see.
