Callitrichaceae

noun
family of aquatic dicotyledonous herbs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkælɪtrɪˈkeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌkælɪtrɪˈkeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous aquatic herbs, commonly known as the water-starwort family, found in freshwater habitats worldwide

Examples

Callitrichaceae aquatic plants
Botanists recently reclassified some genera previously included in the Callitrichaceae.
