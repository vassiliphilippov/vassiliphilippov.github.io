Mother Carey's chicken

noun
storm petrel (bird)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌðər ˈkɛriz ˌtʃɪkən / (AmE), / ˌmʌðə ˈkɛəriz ˌtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
A common name for various small- to medium-sized storm petrels, especially those found in the North Atlantic.

Examples

Mother Carey's chicken bird
Despite the rough weather, a lone Mother Carey's chicken circled the ship all afternoon.
Old sailors used to say that when Mother Carey's chicken appeared, a storm was on its way.
We spotted a flock of Mother Carey's chickens skimming just above the waves during the voyage.
