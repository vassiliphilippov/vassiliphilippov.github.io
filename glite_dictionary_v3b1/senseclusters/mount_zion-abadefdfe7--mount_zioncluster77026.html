Mount Zion

proper noun
a historic hill in Jerusalem
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈzaɪ.ən / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈzaɪ.ən / (BrE)
A hill in Jerusalem, originally a fortress captured by King David, later associated with the Temple and often used as a synonym for Jerusalem.

Examples

Mount Zion hill Jerusalem
In many hymns, Mount Zion is celebrated as a symbol of hope and faith.
Pilgrims traveled to Mount Zion to visit the ancient sacred sites.
The view from Mount Zion offers a panoramic overlook of old Jerusalem.
