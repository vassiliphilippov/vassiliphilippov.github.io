proofreading

noun
checking for errors
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːfˌriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpruːfˌriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of reading written text to find and correct errors before it is published or printed

Examples

Proofreading a paper
She spent the afternoon proofreading her research paper for mistakes.
Careful proofreading can prevent embarrassing errors in published books.
Before submitting his article, he did a final round of proofreading to catch any typos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editing - the process of preparing written material by correcting and revising it
  • antonym: writing - the activity of producing written text
