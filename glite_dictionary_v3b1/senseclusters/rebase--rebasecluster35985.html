rebase

verb
to remount artwork
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbeɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbeɪs / (BrE)
to mount or attach an artwork or fragile object onto a new support or backing for preservation or display

Examples

To rebase an artwork
The museum staff needed to rebase the ancient mosaic to prevent further damage.
Experts decided to rebase the painting onto a more stable canvas during conservation.
After years of deterioration, the sculpture was carefully rebased for safe exhibition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detach - to remove something from its support or backing
  • synonym: restore - to repair or return something to its original condition
