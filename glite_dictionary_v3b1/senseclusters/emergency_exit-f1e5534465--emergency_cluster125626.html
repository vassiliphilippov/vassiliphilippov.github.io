emergency exit

noun
door or route for emergency escape
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi ˈɛɡzɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi ˈɛɡzɪt / (BrE)
a special door or stairway designed for use as an escape route in case of fire or other emergency

Examples

The emergency exit door
Please do not block the emergency exit with any furniture or equipment.
The building was evacuated safely through the emergency exit during the fire drill.
There is an emergency exit located at the end of each hallway for your safety.
