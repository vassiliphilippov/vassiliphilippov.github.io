nonresponsibility

noun
lack of accountability
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɪˌspɑnsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the state of not being responsible or not being held accountable for something, either in general or in a legal/official way

Examples

The nonresponsibility of managers
The manager was frustrated by the team's nonresponsibility when problems arose.
A culture of nonresponsibility can harm an organization's reputation and effectiveness.
His repeated nonresponsibility for mistakes eventually led to his dismissal.
The company claimed nonresponsibility for any loss caused by third parties.
A clause of nonresponsibility was included to protect the landlord from certain damages.
He argued that his signature on the document implied nonresponsibility for the project's outcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irresponsibility - lack of a sense of responsibility
  • antonym: responsibility - the state of being accountable for something
