nonresponsibility

noun
lack of accountability
8.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.spɑːn.sə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /nən.rɪ.spɑːn.sə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.spɒn.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /nən.rɪ.spɒn.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the state of not being responsible or not being held accountable for something, either in general or in a legal/official way
