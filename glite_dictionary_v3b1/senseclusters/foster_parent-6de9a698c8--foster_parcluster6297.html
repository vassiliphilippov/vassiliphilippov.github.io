foster parent

noun
adult caring for a child not legally adopted
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstər ˌpɛrənt / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə ˌpeərənt / (BrE)
a person who cares for a child in place of the child's biological parents without legally adopting the child

Examples

A foster parent cares
After years in the foster care system, Jamie found a loving foster parent who treated her like family.
Many foster parents develop strong bonds with the children in their care.
The agency carefully screens every foster parent to ensure the well-being of the children.

Related Terms
