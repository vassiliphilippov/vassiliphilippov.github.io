faggotish style

noun
offensive term for flamboyant fashion
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡətɪʃ staɪl / (AmE), / ˈfaɡətɪʃ staɪl / (BrE)
A highly offensive term for flamboyant or eccentric fashion, often intended as an insult to gay men or people perceived as effeminate.

Examples

A faggotish style insult
He was mocked for his faggotish style at the party, which many found offensive.
Some critics unfairly dismissed the designer's creations as having a faggotish style.
The article condemned the media’s tendency to label expressive clothing choices as faggotish style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subdued style - a more restrained or understated fashion style
