ack

verb
to acknowledge receipt
Pronunciation: / æk / (AmE), / æk / (BrE)
to send or give an acknowledgment that something has been received or noted, especially in electronic communication

Examples

To ack a message
Please ack this email so we know you received the updated schedule.
If you get this message, just ack to confirm.
He forgot to ack the file transfer, so the sender was unsure it arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acknowledge - to confirm that something has been received or noticed
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately not acknowledge or respond
