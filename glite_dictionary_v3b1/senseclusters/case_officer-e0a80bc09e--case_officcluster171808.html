case officer

noun
intelligence staff agent handler
Pronunciation: / keɪs ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / keɪs ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
an intelligence agent responsible for handling and supervising covert sources or operatives

Examples

A case officer recruits agents
Each case officer is trained to recruit and manage multiple assets overseas.
She worked as a case officer for several years before being promoted.
The case officer met his informant in a crowded market to avoid detection.
