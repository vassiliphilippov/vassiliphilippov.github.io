fundagelical belief

noun
strict fundamentalist evangelical religious beliefs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfʌndəˈdʒɛlɪkəl bəˈliːf / (AmE), / ˌfʌndəˈdʒɛlɪkəl bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
A set of religious beliefs characterized by strict fundamentalism combined with evangelical zeal, often associated with uncompromising or fervent activism.

Examples

Fundagelical belief activism
His fundagelical belief led him to participate in every church event with unwavering enthusiasm.
Many criticized the politician for letting his fundagelical beliefs influence his decisions on public policy.
The organization's fundagelical belief system sometimes alienated those with more moderate views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evangelical - relating to enthusiastic or zealous Christian outreach or belief
  • synonym: fundamentalist - a person who believes in the strict, literal interpretation of scripture
