airtightness

noun
flawless security
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌtaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈeəˌtaɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being so secure, complete, or flawless that something cannot be criticized, disputed, or compromised

Examples

Airtightness of an argument
The lawyer was confident in the airtightness of her argument, leaving no room for objections.
The airtightness of the security system impressed the inspectors during their review.
He admired the airtightness of the plan, which anticipated every possible problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vulnerability - the quality of being open to attack or criticism
