lightweight pram

noun
a light, portable baby stroller
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt ˈpræm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt ˈpræm / (BrE)
A baby carriage that is lighter and easier to carry or maneuver than standard models.

Examples

A lightweight pram for babies
Her lightweight pram was perfect for taking their baby on long walks around the city.
The lightweight pram made it easy to navigate the park.
We packed the lightweight pram for our weekend trip because it fits easily in the car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pram - a vehicle for carrying a baby, used especially in British English
  • synonym: stroller - a chair on wheels for transporting babies or young children
