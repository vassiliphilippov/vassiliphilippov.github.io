Morus

noun
genus of mulberry trees
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrəs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the family Moraceae, commonly known as mulberries.

Examples

Morus alba tree
Botanists have classified white mulberry under Morus alba.
Historical texts describe the cultivation of Morus for use in sericulture.
The genus Morus includes several species of deciduous trees known for their edible fruit.
