fishing license

noun
official permit to fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌlaɪsns /; / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
An official permit granting the holder the legal right to fish during a specified period, typically issued by a government authority.

Examples

Annual fishing licenses
Annual fishing licenses are available online for both residents and visitors.
He was fined for fishing without a valid fishing license.
You must obtain a fishing license before you can fish in these waters.
She applied for a fishing licence before the start of the season.
Without a fishing licence, anglers can face heavy fines.
You must have a valid fishing licence to fish in this river.
