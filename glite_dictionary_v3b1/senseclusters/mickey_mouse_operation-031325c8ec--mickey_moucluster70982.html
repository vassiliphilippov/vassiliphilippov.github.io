Mickey Mouse operation

noun
trivial or poorly organized activity
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪki maʊs ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪki maʊs ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an organization, business, or activity regarded as trivial, insignificant, or poorly organized

Examples

A Mickey Mouse operation
He lost faith in the company, calling it a Mickey Mouse operation after months of disorganization.
Many investors avoid putting money into a Mickey Mouse operation with no track record.
Nobody takes a Mickey Mouse operation like that seriously in our industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious operation - an enterprise considered important or professionally run
  • synonym: sham - something that is not what it purports to be; a false or insincere thing
