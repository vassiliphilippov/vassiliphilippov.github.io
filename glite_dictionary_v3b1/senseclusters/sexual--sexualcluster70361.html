sexual

adjective
relating to sex or the sexes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛksjʊəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving sex or the sexes, especially the processes by which offspring are produced.

Examples

Sexual reproduction

Some fungi produce sexual spores during specific stages of their life cycle.
The study focused on the sexual behavior of various animal species.
