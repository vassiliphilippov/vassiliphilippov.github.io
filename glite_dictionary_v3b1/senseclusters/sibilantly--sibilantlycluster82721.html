sibilantly

adverb
with a hissing sound
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪbɪləntli / (AmE), / ˈsɪbɪləntli / (BrE)
in a way that produces a hissing or s-like sound

Examples

To speak sibilantly
The wind moved sibilantly through the tall grass late at night.
She whispered sibilantly so that only her friend could hear her secret.
Steam escaped sibilantly from the kettle as the water began to boil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hissingly - in a manner that makes a hissing sound
