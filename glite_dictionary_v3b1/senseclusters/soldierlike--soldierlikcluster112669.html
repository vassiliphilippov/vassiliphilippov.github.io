soldierlike

adjective
having qualities appropriate to a soldier
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊl.dʒərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.dʒəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities or demeanor appropriate to a soldier; showing discipline, courage, or military bearing.

Examples

Soldierlike behavior
He handled the situation in a very soldierlike manner, displaying discipline and resolve.
Her soldierlike appearance and strict posture made her stand out in the crowd.
The soldierlike determination in his face inspired confidence in his team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: military - having to do with soldiers or armed forces
  • synonym: warlike - showing readiness to fight or aggression similar to soldiers
