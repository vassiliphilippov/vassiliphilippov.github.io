celestially

adverb
in a heavenly way
7.7
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛstʃəli / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛstɪəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the sky or heavens, often suggesting something heavenly or divine

Examples

Stars shine celestially
The stars shone celestially on the clear night, filling the sky with light.
Her voice rose celestially above the orchestra, enchanting everyone in the audience.
The painting was celestially beautiful, with soft colors and glowing light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: earthly - in a way that relates to the earth or is not spiritual
  • synonym: heavenly - in a way that is like heaven or the heavens
