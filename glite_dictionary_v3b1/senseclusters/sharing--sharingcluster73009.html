sharing

adjective
generous; willing to share with others
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeərɪŋ / (BrE)
willing to give part of what one has to others; generous and unselfish in distributing or sharing things with others.

Examples

A sharing person
His sharing nature made him popular among his classmates.
She is known as a sharing neighbor who always offers help to those in need.
The company fosters a sharing culture where employees support and learn from each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generous - showing kindness towards others, especially in giving
  • antonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; chiefly concerned with one's own personal profit or pleasure
