premeditatedly

adverb
in a planned way
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmɛdɪˌteɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmɛdɪˌteɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is planned or thought out before being done

Examples

Act premeditatedly carefully
The suspect acted premeditatedly, having planned every detail of the robbery.
She premeditatedly avoided the meeting to make a statement.
The attack was carried out premeditatedly, not in the heat of the moment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidentally - in a way that happens by chance, not planned
  • synonym: deliberately - in a way that is done on purpose
  • synonym: intentionally - in a way that is meant or planned
