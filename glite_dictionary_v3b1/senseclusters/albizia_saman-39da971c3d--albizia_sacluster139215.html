Albizia saman

noun
large tropical American tree, also called rain tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ælˈbɪziə səˈmæn / (AmE), / ælˈbɪziə səˈmæn / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree with feathery bipinnate leaves and clusters of flowers with crimson stamens; its seed pods are eaten by cattle. Also known as the rain tree.
