twiddly

adjective
fidgety or restless
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪdli / (AmE), / ˈtwɪdli / (BrE)
characterized by small, repeated movements of the fingers or hands, often showing restlessness or fidgetiness

Examples

Twiddly fingers
He felt twiddly before the interview and kept tapping his fingers on the table.
The children grew twiddly during the long car ride and couldn't sit still.
She became twiddly while waiting for the results, twisting her ring nervously.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing nervousness or restlessness
  • synonym: fidgety - unable to stay still, restless
  • synonym: restless - unable to relax or be still
