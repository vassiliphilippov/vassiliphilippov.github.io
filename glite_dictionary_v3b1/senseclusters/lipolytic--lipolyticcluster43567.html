lipolytic

adjective
fat-breaking
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪpəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɪpəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing the breakdown of fats in the body

Examples

Lipolytic activity
The doctor explained that a lipolytic enzyme helps the body break down stored fat.
Researchers are studying lipolytic activity to develop new treatments for obesity.
A diet high in certain nutrients can increase lipolytic processes in the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lipogenic - relating to the formation of fat
  • synonym: lipolysis - the process of breaking down fats
