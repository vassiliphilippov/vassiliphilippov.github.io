ming tree

noun
dwarfed ornamental tree grown in a container
Pronunciation: / mɪŋ tri / (AmE), / mɪŋ triː / (BrE)
a dwarfed evergreen conifer or shrub, trained and shaped to have flat-topped, asymmetrical branches and grown in a container, often as an ornamental plant

Examples

A real ming tree
Collectors value an old ming tree for its intricate branches and historical significance.
She carefully pruned her ming tree to maintain its unique, flat-topped shape.
The antique dealer displayed a beautiful ming tree in a ceramic pot on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonsai - a Japanese art of growing miniature trees in containers
