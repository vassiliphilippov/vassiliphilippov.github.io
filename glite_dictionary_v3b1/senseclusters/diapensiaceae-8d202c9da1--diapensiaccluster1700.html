Diapensiaceae

noun
family of evergreen plants (botany)
Pronunciation: / daɪ.əˌpɛn.siˈeɪ.si.i /; / daɪəˌpɛnsiˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / daɪ.əˌpɛn.siˈiː.si.iː /; / daɪəˌpensiˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of small, evergreen plants found in northern temperate regions, sometimes placed in its own order (Diapensiales) in botany
