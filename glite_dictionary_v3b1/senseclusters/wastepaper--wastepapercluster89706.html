wastepaper

noun
used, unwanted paper
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
paper that has been used and is no longer needed or wanted, usually thrown away or recycled

Examples

A wastepaper basket

Stacks of wastepaper quickly filled the small bin under her desk.
Please throw any wastepaper into the recycling bin near the door.
The office collects wastepaper every Friday for recycling.
He found an old letter among the wastepaper in the trash can.

Related Terms

  • antonym: document - a written or printed paper that provides information
  • synonym: recyclable - material that can be processed and used again
  • synonym: scrap paper - paper left over or used for notes and then discarded
