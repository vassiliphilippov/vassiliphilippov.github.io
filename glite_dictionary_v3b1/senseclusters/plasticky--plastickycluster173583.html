plasticky

adjective
like plastic
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪki / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪki / (BrE)
having the look, feel, or quality of plastic, often suggesting something is cheap or artificial

Examples

A plasticky feel
The cheap chair felt plasticky and broke after a few weeks.
I prefer glass bottles because plastic ones often have a plasticky taste.
Her raincoat looked a bit plasticky compared to the more expensive ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plastic - made of or resembling plastic
  • antonym: wooden - made of or resembling wood
