sukuk

noun
Islamic financial certificate
Pronunciation: / suːˈkuːk / (AmE), / suːˈkuːk / (BrE)
an Islamic financial certificate representing ownership in an asset, structured to comply with Sharia law

Examples

Islamic sukuk certificate
Many investors prefer sukuk because they comply with Islamic financial principles.
The government raised funds for infrastructure projects by issuing sukuk to the public.
International markets have seen a growing demand for sukuk in recent years.
