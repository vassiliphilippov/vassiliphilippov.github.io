  • assaultive
    8.2 0.10/m
    assaultive means likely to attack physically, while aggressive covers forceful or hostile actions not only physical.
  • confrontational
    7.0 2.56/m

  • antagonistic

  • hostile
    4.6 13/m

  • bellicose
    8.8 0.59/m
