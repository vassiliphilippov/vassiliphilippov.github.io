Ustilago

proper noun
fungus genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌstɪˈleɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌʌstɪˈlɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of fungi that cause smut diseases in plants, especially affecting cereal crops

Examples

Ustilago infects wheat
Ustilago infects wheat and can cause significant crop losses if not controlled.
Researchers are studying how Ustilago spreads among different types of cereal plants.
Farmers monitor their fields for signs of Ustilago to protect their harvests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smut - a fungal disease of plants causing black powdery spores
