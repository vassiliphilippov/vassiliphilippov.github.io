motionlessly

adverb
without movement; without moving
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊʃənləsli / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃənləsli / (BrE)
In a way that does not involve any movement; without moving.

Examples

Sit motionlessly
She waited motionlessly for her name to be called, barely even breathing.
The cat watched the bird motionlessly from its hiding place.
The deer stood motionlessly as the hikers passed by.
