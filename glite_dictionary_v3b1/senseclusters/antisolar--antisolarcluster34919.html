antisolar

adjective
opposite the sun
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsoʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
located or directed away from the sun or opposite to the sun's position

Examples

Antisolar direction
The rainbow appeared in the antisolar direction, directly opposite the setting sun.
Meteorologists track the antisolar point to predict where certain atmospheric phenomena will occur.
The shadow always points in the antisolar direction during the day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solar - relating to or directed toward the sun
