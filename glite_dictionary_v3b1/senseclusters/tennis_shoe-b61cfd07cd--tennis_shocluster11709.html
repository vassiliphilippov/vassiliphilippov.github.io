tennis shoe

noun
light athletic shoe with rubber sole
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnɪs ʃu / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ʃuː / (BrE)
A light, casual shoe made of canvas or similar material with a flexible rubber sole, originally designed for playing tennis.

Examples

A pair of tennis shoes
He slipped on his tennis shoes before heading out for a run in the park.
Her new tennis shoes are comfortable and perfect for everyday wear.
Students are required to wear tennis shoes during gym class for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sneaker - athletic shoe with a rubber sole, often used as a synonym in American English
  • synonym: trainer - athletic shoe, term primarily used in British English
