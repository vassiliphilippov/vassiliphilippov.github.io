neatly

adverb
in a tidy or orderly way
Pronunciation: / ˈnitli / (AmE), / ˈniːtli / (BrE)
In a tidy, orderly, or careful manner.

Examples

To neatly arrange books
He neatly arranged his books in alphabetical order.
She folded the towels neatly and stacked them on the shelf.
The report was neatly organized and easy to follow.

Related Terms
