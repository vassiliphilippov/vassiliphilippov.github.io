kB

noun
unit of digital information (1000 bytes)
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪl.ə.baɪt/ /; / /keɪ biː/ (AmE), /ˈkɪl.ə.baɪt/ /; / /keɪ biː/ (BrE)
