kB

unit of digital information (1000 bytes)

Examples

Synonyms

  • KB
    3.0 0.9/m
    KB is the capitalized unit shown in file lists and menus, while kB is the same kilobyte written with different capitalization.
  • kilobyte
    3.6 0.3/m
    kilobyte is the full word used in speech and writing, while kB is the short unit abbreviation seen in computer displays.
  • K
    3.5 0.2/m
    K is a single-letter shorthand often used to mean a thousand in casual lists, while kB specifically refers to a kilobyte data unit.
