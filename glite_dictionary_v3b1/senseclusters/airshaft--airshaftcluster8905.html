Synonyms

  • air shaft
    4.7 0.16/m
    air shaft is the common two-word form used in building and mine descriptions, while airshaft is the single-word variant.
  • ventilation shaft
    4.5 0.13/m
    ventilation shaft moves fresh and stale air and can run vertically or horizontally, while airshaft usually means a vertical passage.
  • airway
    4.5 0.04/m
    airway is a duct used for mine ventilation and may run between levels, while airshaft names a vertical passage inside buildings or mines.
