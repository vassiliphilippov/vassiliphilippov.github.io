airshaft

noun
vertical ventilation passage
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈer.ʃæft/ (AmE), /ˈeə.ʃæft/ (BrE)
a vertical passage in a building or mine that allows air to circulate between different levels or spaces

Examples

Mine airshaft
The workers climbed up the airshaft to check for any blockages.
Fresh air entered the old apartment building through a narrow airshaft in the center.
Miners rely on the airshaft to provide oxygen deep underground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tunnel - an underground passage, often for transportation
  • synonym: ventilation shaft - a passage for air to move through a building or underground area
