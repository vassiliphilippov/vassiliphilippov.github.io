impermissible

adjective
not allowed; not permitted
Pronunciation: / ˌɪm.pɚˈmɪs.ə.bəl /; / ˌɪmpərˈmɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪm.pəˈmɪs.ə.bəl /; / ˌɪmpəˈmɪsɪbəl / (BrE)
not allowed or not permitted by rules, laws, or accepted standards

Examples
