flecked

adjective
marked with spots or dots
Pronunciation: / flɛkt / (AmE), / flɛkt / (BrE)
Marked with small spots or patches of color or light; having a pattern of small dots or flecks.

Examples

Flecked hair
Her gray hair was flecked with strands of silver.
The artist used a brush flecked with white paint to create a snowy effect.
The robin's eggs were flecked with brown spots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speckled - covered with many small spots or marks
  • synonym: spotted - marked with spots
