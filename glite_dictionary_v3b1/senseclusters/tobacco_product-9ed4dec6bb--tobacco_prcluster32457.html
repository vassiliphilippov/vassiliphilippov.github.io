tobacco product

noun
product made from tobacco leaves
2.4
Pronunciation: / təˈbækoʊ ˈprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
A product partly or wholly made from tobacco leaves and intended for consumption, such as cigarettes, cigars, or chewing tobacco.

Examples

Tobacco products like cigarettes
All sales of tobacco products to minors are prohibited by law.
She decided to quit all tobacco products for the sake of her health.
The government launched a campaign warning about the dangers of using tobacco products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chewing tobacco - tobacco for chewing, not smoking
  • synonym: cigar - a thick roll of tobacco leaves for smoking
  • synonym: cigarette - a thin cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking
