microinch

noun
unit of length
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌɪntʃ / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to one millionth of an inch, used in precision measurement

Examples

Precision instruments can measure thickness down to a single microinch.
In high-tech manufacturing, even a few microinches can make a significant difference.
