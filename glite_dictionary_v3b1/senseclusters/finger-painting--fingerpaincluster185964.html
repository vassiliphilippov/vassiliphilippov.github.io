finger-painting

noun
painting made with fingers
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌpeɪntɪŋ /; / ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌpeɪntɪŋ /; / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a way of painting by spreading paint with your fingers instead of using brushes; also, a picture made in this way
