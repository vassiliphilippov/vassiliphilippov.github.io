finger-painting

noun
painting made with fingers
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪŋ.gər ˈpeɪn.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfɪŋ.gər ˈpeɪn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪŋ.gə ˈpeɪn.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfɪŋ.gə ˈpeɪn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
