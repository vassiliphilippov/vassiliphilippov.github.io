Xiphosurus polyphemus

noun
Atlantic horseshoe crab (marine arthropod)
Pronunciation: / ˌzɪfəsəˈrʊs ˌpɒlɪˈfiːməs / (AmE), / ˌzɪfəsjʊˈrʊs ˌpɒlɪˈfiːməs / (BrE)
a large marine arthropod of the Atlantic coast of North America, commonly known as the Atlantic horseshoe crab, with a domed, horseshoe-shaped carapace and a stiff pointed tail
