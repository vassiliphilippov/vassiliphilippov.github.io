foobar

noun
total confusion or failure
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːbɑːr / (AmE), / ˈfuːbɑː / (BrE)
a situation of complete disorder, failure, or confusion, often due to mistakes or errors (informal)

Examples

A total foobar

After the miscommunication, the event planning became a foobar and had to be rescheduled.
If we do not fix these errors soon, the whole project will be a foobar.
