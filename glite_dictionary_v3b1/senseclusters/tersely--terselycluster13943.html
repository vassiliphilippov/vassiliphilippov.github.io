tersely

adverb
in a brief and concise manner
6.4 0.47/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…úrsli / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀêsli / (BrE)
In a short, concise, and often abrupt manner.

Examples

To speak tersely
He summarized the report tersely, wasting no time on unnecessary details.
She tersely replied that she had no further comment on the issue.
The instructions were tersely written, making them easy to follow.
