shrine

verb
to enclose in a shrine
Pronunciation: / ʃraɪn / (AmE), / ʃraɪn / (BrE)
to enclose or preserve something in or as in a shrine, often to honor or protect it

Examples

To shrine a memory
Many cultures shrine important documents to preserve their legacy.
Over time, the city decided to shrine the statue in the new temple.
The ancient relics were shrined by the monks for protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enshrine - to preserve something as sacred or cherished
