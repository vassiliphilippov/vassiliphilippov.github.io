shop clerk

noun
salesperson in a shop or store
1.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: /  É…ëp kl…úrk / (AmE), /  É…íp kl…ëÀêk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to serve customers and sell goods in a shop or store

Examples

A shop clerk serves customers
A friendly shop clerk greeted every customer who entered the store.
After waiting in line, she asked the shop clerk about a return policy.
The shop clerk helped me find the right size for my jacket.

Synonyms

    2.4 0.26/m

  • shop assistant
    1.2 0.06/m

  • clerk
    1.1 6.13/m
    a clerk can work in many offices or shops and handle sales, while a shop clerk works specifically in a retail store.
  • salesperson
    2.2 2.21/m
    a salesperson sells products or services in many settings, while a shop clerk works mainly in a shop serving local customers.
