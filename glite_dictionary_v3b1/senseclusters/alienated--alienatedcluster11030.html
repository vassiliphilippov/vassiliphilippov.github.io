Synonyms

  • estranged
    6.7 2.27/m
    estranged describes people who become distant after a broken relationship, while alienated describes a wider, general feeling of separation from others.
  • isolated
    4.2 4.98/m
    isolated means being or feeling physically separated with little contact, while alienated emphasizes emotional distance or loss of connection with people.
  • left out
    3.6 4.61/m
    left out feels like being excluded from a group or activity, while alienated is a deeper, longer sense of social separation.
  • disengaged
    4.7 0.09/m
    disengaged describes a person who is not interested or involved in something, while alienated expresses feeling cut off from people.
  • out of place
    3.5 3.94/m
    out of place means feeling awkward or that you do not fit a situation, while alienated is a stronger, social or emotional separation.
