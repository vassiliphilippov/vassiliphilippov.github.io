readymade

noun
artwork from object
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈre.dɪ.meɪd/ /; / /ˈre.də.meɪd/ (AmE), /ˈre.dɪ.meɪd/ /; / /ˈre.də.meɪd/ (BrE)
an ordinary manufactured object presented as art, especially in the context of modern or conceptual art

Examples

A readymade artwork
The museum's new exhibit features a famous readymade by Marcel Duchamp.
She created a readymade by displaying a simple kitchen chair as a piece of art.
Many modern artists have experimented with the concept of the readymade in their work.
