parodos

noun
Greek theatre entrance
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpær.ə.doʊs/ (AmE), /ˈpær.ə.dɒs/ (BrE)
a side entrance or passage in an ancient Greek theatre used by the chorus or actors to enter and exit the orchestra
