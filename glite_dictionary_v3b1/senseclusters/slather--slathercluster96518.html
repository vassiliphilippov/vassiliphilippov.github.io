slather

verb
spread thickly or liberally
Pronunciation: / ˈslæðɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɑːðə / (BrE)
to spread a substance thickly or liberally over something

Examples

To slather sauce thickly
He slathered barbecue sauce onto the ribs before putting them on the grill.
She likes to slather her toast with peanut butter in the mornings.
The children slathered sunscreen all over their arms and faces before going outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smear - spread a substance thinly or carelessly over a surface
  • synonym: spread - cover a surface with a layer of a substance
