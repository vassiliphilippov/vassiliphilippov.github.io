semihysterical

adjective
partly hysterical
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi hɪˈstɛrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi hɪˈstɛrɪkəl / (BrE)
showing some but not full signs of being extremely emotional or out of control

Examples

A semihysterical reaction
He became semihysterical when he realized he had lost his passport at the airport.
Her semihysterical reaction to the surprise party made everyone laugh.
The crowd grew semihysterical as the band finally appeared on stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing strong emotions or excitement
  • degree higher: hysterical - extremely emotional or out of control
