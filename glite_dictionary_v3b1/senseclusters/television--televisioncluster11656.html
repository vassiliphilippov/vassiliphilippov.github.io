television

noun
system for broadcasting visual images and sound
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən /; / ˈtɛl.əˌvɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən /; / ˈtɛl.ɪˌvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a system for sending and receiving visual images and sound, used to broadcast programs for entertainment, information, or education
