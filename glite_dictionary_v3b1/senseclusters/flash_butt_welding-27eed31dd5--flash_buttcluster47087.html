flash butt welding

noun
welding technique using electric arc to join metals
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: / flæʃ bʌt ˈwɛldɪŋ / (AmE), / flæʃ bʌt ˈwɛldɪŋ / (BrE)
A welding process in which two pieces of metal are joined by creating an electric arc between them to melt and fuse the ends, commonly used for joining metal pipes or rods.

Examples

Flash butt welding process
Advances in flash butt welding have improved the strength and reliability of pipeline joints.
Proper alignment is critical in flash butt welding to ensure a strong, uniform bond between the metal pieces.
The company used flash butt welding to join the steel rails seamlessly along the track.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butt welding - welding metals end-to-end by fusing their surfaces
