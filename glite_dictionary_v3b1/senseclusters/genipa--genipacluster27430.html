genipa

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈniːpə / (AmE), / dʒɛˈniːpə / (BrE)
A tropical tree of the genus Genipa, bearing yellow flowers and edible fruit with a thick rind.

Examples

The genipa tree
Farmers often cultivate genipa for its durable wood and attractive flowers.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of the genipa in the Amazon rainforest.
The genipa tree produces large fruits that are both edible and used for making dyes.
