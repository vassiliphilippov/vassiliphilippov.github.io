Johnsonian

adjective
of Samuel Johnson
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɑnˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒnˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to Samuel Johnson or to his literary style, especially marked by clarity, wit, and balanced prose

Examples

Johnsonian prose
The critic praised the Johnsonian elegance of her writing style.
His arguments were delivered in a Johnsonian manner, full of wit and precision.
Many admire the Johnsonian clarity found in his essays and reviews.
