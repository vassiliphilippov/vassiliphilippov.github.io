panela

noun
unrefined cane sugar
8.1
Pronunciation: / pɑˈneɪlə /; / pəˈneɪlə / (AmE), / pəˈnɛlə /; / pəˈneɪlə / (BrE)
a solid block or loaf of unrefined whole cane sugar, used as a sweetener in cooking and drinks, especially in Latin American cuisine
