general knowledge

noun
widely known or accessible information
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.ər.əl ˈnɑː.lɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.ər.əl ˈnɒ.lɪdʒ / (BrE)
information or facts that are widely known and available to anyone, rather than being specialized or secret

Examples

General knowledge quiz
A quiz show often tests contestants on their general knowledge of history, science, and culture.
Having a broad general knowledge can be helpful in many different careers.
She reads the newspaper daily to improve her general knowledge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: common knowledge - information that is generally known and accepted as true
