preplacement

adjective
before assignment
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
happening or done before a placement, especially referring to activities, tests, or procedures carried out prior to assigning people or things to positions.

Examples

Preplacement medical exam
A preplacement medical exam is required before starting work in certain industries.
The school organized a preplacement workshop to prepare students for their internships.
All candidates must complete a preplacement assessment before being assigned to a project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postplacement - happening after a placement or assignment
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before something important as a preparation
