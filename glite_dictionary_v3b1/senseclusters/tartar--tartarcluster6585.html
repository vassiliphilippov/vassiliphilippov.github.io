tartar

noun
a salt used in baking powder

A white, crystalline salt, especially potassium bitartrate, used mainly in baking powder to promote rising in baked goods.

Examples

Tartar in baking
Baking powder contains both an acid, such as tartar, and a base.
She bought some tartar from the grocery store to use in her baking.
The recipe calls for half a teaspoon of tartar to help the cake rise.
