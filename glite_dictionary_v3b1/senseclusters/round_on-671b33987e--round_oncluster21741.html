round on

verb
attack or criticize suddenly
8.3 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / raÊŠnd É‘n / (AmE), / raÊŠnd É’n / (BrE)
to suddenly turn on someone and attack or criticize them, especially verbally

Examples

To round on a person
After hearing the unfair comments, she rounded on her colleague with a sharp response.
He rounded on his team after the disappointing match.
The politician rounded on reporters who questioned his honesty.
