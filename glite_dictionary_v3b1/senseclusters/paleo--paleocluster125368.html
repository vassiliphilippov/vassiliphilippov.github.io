paleo

adjective
ancient or prehistoric
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪlioʊ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪlɪəʊ / (BrE)
relating to ancient times, especially the prehistoric era, or to a diet based on foods presumed to have been eaten then

Examples

Paleo diet plan
The paleo diet encourages eating foods that our ancient ancestors might have consumed.
Some museums display paleo artifacts from early human history.
He prefers a paleo lifestyle, focusing on natural foods and outdoor activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
  • synonym: prehistoric - relating to the period before written history
