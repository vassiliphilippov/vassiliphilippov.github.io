white-rumped shrike

noun
a shrike species with a white rump
8.9
Pronunciation: /waɪt rʌmpt ʃraɪk/ /; / /hwaɪt rʌmpt ʃraɪk/ (AmE), /waɪt rʌmpt ʃraɪk/ (BrE)
