flacket

noun
small bottle or flask
an old term for a small bottle or flask, often used to carry liquids

Examples

A flacket of water
He carried a flacket of water on his journey.
The traveler filled his flacket at the mountain stream before continuing.
In old tales, a flacket of wine was a common companion for long walks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flask - a small container for liquids
