shoulder

noun
cut of meat from the animal's upper front leg
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə / (BrE)
a cut of meat taken from the upper front leg and adjoining part of an animal, especially a pig, lamb, or other livestock

Examples

Shoulder of pork
He asked the butcher for a cut from the shoulder, as it works well for pulled pork.
Lamb shoulder is often used in stews because of its rich flavor and tenderness.
The recipe calls for a boneless pork shoulder to be slow roasted for several hours.
