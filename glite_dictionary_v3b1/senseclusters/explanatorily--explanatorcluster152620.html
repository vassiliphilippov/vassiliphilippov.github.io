explanatorily

adverb
in an explanatory way
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌsplænəˈtɔːrəli / (AmE), / ɪkˌsplænəˈtɔːrəli / (BrE)
in a manner that serves to explain or clarify something

Examples

Speak explanatorily clearly
She spoke explanatorily so that everyone could follow the instructions easily.
The guide answered the tourists' questions explanatorily, making the history come alive.
He paused and added, explanatorily, that the process would take several hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarifyingly - in a way that makes something clearer
  • antonym: obscurely - in a way that is difficult to understand
