metofluthrin

noun
mosquito repellent chemical
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈfluːθrɪn / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈfluːθrɪn / (BrE)
a synthetic chemical used as an insect repellent, especially effective against mosquitoes

Examples

Metofluthrin insect repellent
The new mosquito coils contain metofluthrin to help keep insects away.
Researchers found that metofluthrin is highly effective in repelling mosquitoes indoors.
Many portable devices now use metofluthrin to protect people from mosquito bites.
