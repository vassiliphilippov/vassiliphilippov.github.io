Dumetella carolinensis

proper noun
North American bird, gray catbird
Pronunciation: / ˌduːməˈtɛlə ˌkærəˌlaɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌdjuːmɪˈtɛlə ˌkærəlaɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A North American songbird of the mockingbird family, commonly known as the gray catbird, noted for its call that resembles a cat's mewing.
