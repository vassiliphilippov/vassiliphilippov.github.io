jumelle

noun
opera binoculars
Pronunciation: / ʒuːˈmɛl / (AmE), / ʒuːˈmɛl / (BrE)
a small pair of binoculars, especially designed for use at the opera or theater

Examples

Opera jumelle
She brought her jumelle to the theater to see the actors more clearly.
The elegant jumelle fit perfectly in her evening bag.
He borrowed a jumelle from his neighbor to watch the performance from the balcony.
