receptivity

noun
willingness to receive ideas or influences
4.6 5.0
Pronunciation: /rɪ.sep.ˈtɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ /; / /riː.sep.ˈtɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /rɪ.sep.ˈtɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ /; / /riː.sep.ˈtɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
