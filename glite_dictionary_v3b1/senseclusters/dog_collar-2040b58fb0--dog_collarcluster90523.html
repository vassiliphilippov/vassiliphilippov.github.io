dog collar

noun
close-fitting choker necklace
8.9
Pronunciation: / dɔɡ ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A type of necklace that fits closely around a woman’s neck, resembling the shape of a traditional dog collar.

Examples

A jeweled dog collar

The vintage shop had a collection of antique dog collars from the 1920s.
