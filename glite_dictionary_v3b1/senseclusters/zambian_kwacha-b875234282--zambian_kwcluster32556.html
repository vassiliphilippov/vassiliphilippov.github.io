Zambian kwacha

noun
currency of Zambia
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈzæmbiən kwɑːtʃə / (AmE), / ˈzæmbiən kwætʃə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Zambia, equal to 100 ngwee.

Examples

The Zambian kwacha currency
All transactions in the country are conducted in the official currency, the Zambian kwacha.
She exchanged her dollars for Zambian kwacha at the airport upon arrival in Lusaka.
The price of bread rose significantly after the value of the Zambian kwacha declined.
