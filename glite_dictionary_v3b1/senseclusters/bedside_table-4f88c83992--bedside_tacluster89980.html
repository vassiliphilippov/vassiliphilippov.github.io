bedside table

noun
table next to bed
1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbed.saɪd ˈteɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈbed.saɪd ˈteɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
a small table placed next to a bed for holding personal items, such as a lamp, books, or medical equipment

Examples

A bedside table lamp
She kept her glasses and a book on the bedside table for easy access at night.
The hospital room had a bedside table where the patient could keep water and medication.
He placed his phone on the bedside table before going to sleep.
She placed a glass of water on her bedside table before going to sleep.
The alarm clock rang loudly from the bedside table every morning.
He keeps his favorite book on the bedside table for late-night reading.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dresser - a piece of furniture with drawers for storing clothes
  • synonym: nightstand - a small table or cabinet beside a bed for holding items
  • synonym: overbed table - a movable table designed to fit over a bed, often used in hospitals
