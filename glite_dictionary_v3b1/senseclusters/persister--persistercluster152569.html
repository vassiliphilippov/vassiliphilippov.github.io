persister

noun
persistent person
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪstər / (AmE), / pəˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a person who continues firmly in a course of action or belief despite difficulty or opposition

Examples

A true persister
Even when faced with setbacks, the persister never lost hope in achieving her goals.
A true persister keeps working hard, no matter how many times he fails.
The team's success was due to the efforts of their most determined persister.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perseverer - a person who keeps trying despite obstacles
  • antonym: quitter - a person who gives up easily
