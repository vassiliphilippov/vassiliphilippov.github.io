Primula veris

noun
flowering meadow plant (cowslip)
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌprɪmjələ ˈvɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˌprɪmjʊlə ˈvɛːrɪs / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant in the primrose family, commonly called cowslip, with fragrant yellow or occasionally purple flowers, native to Europe and often found in meadows and grassy places.

In Britain, Primula veris is often seen growing wild along hedgerows and grassy banks.
