playful pinch

noun
light, teasing squeeze
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪfəl pɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl̩ pɪntʃ / (BrE)
a light squeeze made in a teasing or affectionate manner

Examples

A playful pinch
A playful pinch can sometimes break the ice in a conversation.
He responded to her joke with a playful pinch on her arm.
She greeted him with a playful pinch on the cheek, making him laugh.
