concern

noun
anxious feeling; worry
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝn / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a feeling of worry, anxiety, or care about someone or something

Examples

A mother's concern
His mother’s concern for his health kept her awake at night.
There was growing concern about the safety of the building.
You could hear the concern in her voice as she spoke.
His parents showed great concern when he didn't come home on time.
She expressed her concern for the victims of the disaster.
The teacher's concern for her students was obvious to everyone.
