do me a favour

verb
request for help or courtesy
1.0
Pronunciation: / duː mi ə ˈfeɪvər / (AmE), / dəʊ mi ə ˈfeɪvə / (BrE)
used to ask someone to help you or do something for you as a courtesy or service

Examples

Please do me a favour
Could you do me a favour and check if the door is locked?
If you have a moment, would you do me a favour and send this email?
She asked him to do me a favour by dropping off the package at the post office.
