North Platte

proper noun
river in Colorado, Wyoming, and Nebraska
5.6
Pronunciation: /nɔːrθ plæt/ (AmE), /nɔːθ plæt/ (BrE)
a river in the western United States that rises in northern Colorado, flows through Wyoming, and then southeast through Nebraska, where it joins the South Platte River to form the Platte River

Examples

The North Platte River
The North Platte merges with the South Platte near the city of North Platte, Nebraska.
The North Platte provides water for irrigation across much of western Nebraska.
We camped along the banks of the North Platte during our road trip through Wyoming.
