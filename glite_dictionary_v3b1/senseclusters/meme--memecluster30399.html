meme

noun
cultural idea or behavior passed by imitation
Pronunciation: / miːm / (AmE), / miːm / (BrE)
an idea, behavior, or style that spreads from person to person within a culture by imitation or other non-genetic means

Examples

Cultural meme
Language itself can be viewed as a complex system of transmitted memes.
Some behaviors become entrenched as memes that are passed down through generations.
The concept of a meme explains how ideas can rapidly spread within a society.
