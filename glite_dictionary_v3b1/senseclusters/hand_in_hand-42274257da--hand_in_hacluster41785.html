Synonyms

    in concert with is used when people or groups act together in harmony, while hand in hand feels more everyday partnership.
  • in tandem
    3.5 1.54/m
    in tandem describes two things moving or working in step, while hand in hand suggests a close link or shared goal.
  • together
    1.0 216/m

  • jointly

  • side by side
