ringing

noun
loud, resonant quality or sound
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
The quality of being loud and resonant, often producing a deep, clear, echoing sound.

Examples

Ringing voice
He gave a speech with ringing tones of conviction and purpose.
Her voice had a ringing clarity that captivated the audience.
The ringing of church bells echoed through the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resonance - the quality in a sound of being deep, full, and reverberating
