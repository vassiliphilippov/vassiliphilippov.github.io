daddle

verb
to walk unsteadily
Pronunciation: / ˈdædəl / (AmE), / ˈdædəl / (BrE)
to walk unsteadily or in a tottering, staggering way

Examples

To daddle unsteadily
The child would daddle behind his parents, taking tiny, uncertain steps.

After the long hike, she could only daddle back to the campsite, exhausted.
Children sometimes daddle when they are pretending to be very old.
