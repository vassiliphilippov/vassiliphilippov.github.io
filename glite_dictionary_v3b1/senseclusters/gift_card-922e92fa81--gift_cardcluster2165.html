gift card

noun
prepaid store card given as a gift
Pronunciation: / ɡɪft kɑrd / (AmE), / ɡɪft kɑːd / (BrE)
a prepaid card, often given as a present, that can be used as payment for goods or services at a particular store or group of stores

Examples

A gift card purchase
If you don't know what to buy, a gift card is always a safe choice.
Many retailers offer gift cards that can be used both online and in-store.
She received a gift card for her favorite bookstore on her birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voucher - a document that can be exchanged for goods or services
