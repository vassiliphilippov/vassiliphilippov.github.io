laurel sumac

noun
aromatic Californian evergreen shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrəl ˈsuːmæk / (AmE), / ˈlɒrəl ˈsjuːmæk / (BrE)
a small aromatic evergreen shrub native to California, with cluster-like leaves and whitish berries, sometimes classified in the genus Rhus

Examples

A laurel sumac shrub
During their hike, they identified a laurel sumac growing beside the trail.
Fire-resistant landscaping in southern California often incorporates native plants like laurel sumac.
The leaves of the laurel sumac have a pungent, aromatic scent when crushed.
