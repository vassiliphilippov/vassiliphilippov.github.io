radiometry

noun
measurement of radiation
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiˈɑːmətri / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiˈɒmətri / (BrE)
the scientific measurement of electromagnetic radiation, especially the intensity of light

Examples

Accurate radiometry measurement
Accurate radiometry is essential for analyzing satellite images of the Earth's surface.
The laboratory specializes in radiometry to ensure precise measurement of light sources.
Advances in radiometry have improved the calibration of astronomical telescopes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photometry - the measurement of visible light as perceived by human eyes
  • synonym: spectroscopy - the study of the interaction between matter and electromagnetic radiation
