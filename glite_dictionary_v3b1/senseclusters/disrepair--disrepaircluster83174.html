The state of being in poor condition due to neglect or lack of maintenance; in need of repair.

Examples

In disrepair
Much of the railway network is now in a state of disrepair.
The garden wall was crumbling and clearly in disrepair.
The old house had fallen into disrepair after years of neglect.

Synonyms

  • decrepitude
    9.3 0.22/m
    decrepitude shows extreme wear and weakness from long use, while disrepair means an object needs repair from neglect.
