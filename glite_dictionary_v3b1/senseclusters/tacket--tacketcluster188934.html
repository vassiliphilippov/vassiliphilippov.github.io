tacket

verb
to fasten with tacks
Pronunciation: / ˈtækɪt / (AmE), / ˈtækɪt / (BrE)
to fasten or secure something using small nails or tacks, especially called tackets.

Examples

To tacket fabric
The craftsman used a hammer to tacket the fabric onto the chair frame.
Before painting, she decided to tacket the canvas securely to the wooden stretcher.
They needed to tacket the poster to the wall so it would not fall down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nail - to fasten something with nails
  • antonym: remove - to take something away or detach it
