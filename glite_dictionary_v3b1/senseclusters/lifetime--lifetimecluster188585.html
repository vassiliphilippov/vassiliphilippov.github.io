lifetime

noun
period during which something exists or functions
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪftaɪm /; / ˈlaɪfˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪftaɪm /; / ˈlaɪf.taɪm / (BrE)
the whole period of time that a person, animal, or thing is alive or exists

Examples

A lifetime of work
Her achievements will be remembered long after her lifetime.
The warranty covers the product for its entire lifetime.
This computer has a projected lifetime of five years before it becomes obsolete.
She traveled to many countries during her lifetime.
The museum displays artifacts that have survived several human lifetimes.
He collected stamps as a hobby throughout his entire lifetime.
