Keb

proper noun
Egyptian god of the earth
Pronunciation: / kɛb / (AmE), / kɛb / (BrE)
An Egyptian god of the earth, regarded as the father of Osiris and Isis in ancient mythology.

Examples

Keb god of earth
In ancient Egyptian mythology, Keb was revered as the god of the earth.
Statues depicting Keb frequently show him lying beneath the sky goddess Nut.
The story of Osiris often mentions Keb as his divine father.

Related Terms
