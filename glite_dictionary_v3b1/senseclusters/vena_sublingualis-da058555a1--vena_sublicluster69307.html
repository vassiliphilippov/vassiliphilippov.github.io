vena sublingualis

noun
a vein beneath the tongue
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə ˌsʌb.lɪŋˈɡwæ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə ˌsʌb.lɪŋˈɡwaː.lɪs / (BrE)
A small vein under the tongue that is a tributary of the lingual vein.

Examples

The vena sublingualis vein
Swelling near the vena sublingualis can sometimes indicate an underlying medical condition.
The dentist identified the prominent vena sublingualis during the oral examination.
The medication was absorbed quickly due to its proximity to the vena sublingualis.
