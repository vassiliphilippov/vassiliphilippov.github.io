prophetically

adverb
in a prophetic manner
5.0 7.4
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛtɪkli / (AmE), / prəˈfɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that predicts or foreshadows future events; in a way that resembles or relates to prophecy.

Examples

To prophetically warn
He prophetically warned them of the risks, which later became clear to everyone.
She spoke prophetically about the changes that would soon take place.
The novel begins with a prophetically bleak description of future society.
