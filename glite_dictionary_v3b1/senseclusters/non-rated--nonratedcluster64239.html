non-rated

adjective
without official rating
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈreɪtɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been given an official rating or classification, especially by a regulatory authority

Examples

Non-rated movie
The movie was released in a non-rated version to avoid censorship issues.
Some video games are non-rated because they have not been reviewed by the official board.
Parents should be cautious when allowing children to watch non-rated content online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rated - given an official rating or classification
  • synonym: unrated - not assigned an official rating
