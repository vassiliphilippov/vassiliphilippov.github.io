termitarium

noun
artificial termite nest
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜːrmɪˈtɛəriəm / (AmE), / ˌtɜːmɪˈteəriəm / (BrE)
an artificial or enclosed nest designed for keeping and studying termites, especially in laboratories or exhibitions

Examples

Lab termitarium
The researchers built a termitarium to observe the colony's behavior in a controlled environment.
Children at the science museum watched termites working inside a glass termitarium.
A well-designed termitarium allows scientists to study termite social structure and communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formicarium - an artificial ant nest for observation or study
  • antonym: termite mound - a natural nest built by termites in the wild
