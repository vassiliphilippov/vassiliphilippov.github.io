exclusivistic

adjective
favoring one viewpoint
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌskluːsɪˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɪkˌskluːsɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
having an attitude or approach that excludes other viewpoints or people, believing only one group or idea is valid

Examples

An exclusivistic attitude
Her exclusivistic attitude prevented her from considering other perspectives during the debate.
The organization adopted an exclusivistic policy, allowing only members of a single faith to join.
Critics argue that an exclusivistic approach can hinder cooperation between different communities.
The organization adopted an exclusivistic approach, allowing only certain members to participate in events.
Critics argued that the policy was too exclusivistic and discouraged diversity within the group.
Her exclusivistic attitude made it difficult for new people to join the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - limiting to only one group or viewpoint
  • antonym: inclusive - accepting or including many different groups or viewpoints
