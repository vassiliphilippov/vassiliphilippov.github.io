dissonant chord

noun
music: chord producing tension or discord

Examples

A dissonant chord
Modern music often features a dissonant chord to challenge traditional harmonies.
She winced as the pianist struck a particularly harsh dissonant chord.
The composer used a dissonant chord to build suspense in the symphony.

Synonyms

  • dissonance
    7.7 0.34/m
    dissonance is a musical quality of clashing notes that feels tense, while a dissonant chord is the specific group of notes causing that tension.
  • discordance
    8.4 0.02/m
    discordance is a general lack of harmony in sound or ideas, while a dissonant chord is a specific musical group that sounds tense.
