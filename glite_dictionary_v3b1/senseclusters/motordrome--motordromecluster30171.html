motordrome

noun
racing track
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈməʊtədˌdrəʊm / (BrE)
a circular or oval track, often with steeply banked sides, used for motorcycle or automobile racing

Examples

A motordrome race
The old motordrome was famous for its daring motorcycle stunts in the 1920s.
Crowds cheered as cars sped around the steep banks of the motordrome.
He dreamed of racing his bike on a real motordrome one day.

  • synonym: racetrack - a track used for racing vehicles or horses
