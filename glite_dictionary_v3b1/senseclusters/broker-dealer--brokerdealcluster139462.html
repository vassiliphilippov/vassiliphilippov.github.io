broker-dealer

noun
a financial intermediary acting as both broker and dealer
6.1 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbro äk…ôr ÀådiÀêl…ôr / (AmE), / Ààbr…ô äk…ô ÀådiÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a person or firm in the financial industry that acts both as a broker (buying and selling securities on behalf of clients) and as a dealer (trading for their own account)

Examples

A broker-dealer firm
Many investors choose to work with a broker-dealer for access to a wider range of financial products.
Regulations require a broker-dealer to disclose conflicts of interest to clients.
The firm recently registered as a broker-dealer with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broker - a person who buys and sells securities on behalf of others
  • synonym: dealer - a person or firm that buys and sells securities for their own account
