upswell

verb
to surge upward
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌswɛl / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌswɛl / (BrE)
to increase or rise suddenly in amount, degree, or intensity; to surge or swell upward or outward

Examples

To upswell with excitement
A sense of excitement began to upswell in the crowd as the band took the stage.
Emotions can upswell unexpectedly during important life events.
Public support tends to upswell after a major crisis brings people together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subside - to become less intense or to decrease
  • synonym: surge - to rise or move forward suddenly and powerfully
