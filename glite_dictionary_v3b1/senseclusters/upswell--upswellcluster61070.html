upswell

verb
to surge upward
7.2
Pronunciation: /ʌp.ˈswel/ /; / /ˈʌp.swel/ (AmE), /ʌp.ˈswel/ /; / /ˈʌp.swel/ (BrE)

Public support tends to upswell after a major crisis brings people together.
