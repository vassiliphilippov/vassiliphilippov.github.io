1 Samuel

proper noun
book of the Old Testament about Saul and David
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrst ˈsæmjuəl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈsæmjʊəl / (BrE)
the first of two books in the Old Testament recounting the stories of Saul and David, including the rise of Israel's monarchy
