genus Lophius

noun
type genus of Lophiidae (anglerfish)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈloʊfiəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈlɒfiəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Lophiidae, a group of anglerfish known as monkfish or goosefish.

Examples

The genus Lophius
Marine biologists have conducted extensive research on the distribution of the genus Lophius.
Members of the genus Lophius are characterized by their broad, flat heads and large mouths.
The genus Lophius includes several species of monkfish found in the Atlantic Ocean.
