Synonyms

  • union
    2.3 60/m
    union is the general name for an organized group of workers that bargains collectively, while labor union is the full phrase for the same body.
  • trade union
    2.8 0.5/m
    trade union is a term for worker groups that negotiate specific employment conditions, while labor union is a broader name for worker organizations.
  • trades union
    2.7 0.01/m
    trades union is a phrase variant that also describes collective bargaining groups, while labor union is the standard more common term.
  • brotherhood
    3.5 8/m
    brotherhood often feels like a fraternal group of workers with close ties, while labor union focuses on negotiation and formal collective rights.
