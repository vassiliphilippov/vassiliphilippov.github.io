interpenetrate

verb
penetrate or blend into each other
5.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈpen.ə.treɪt/ /; / /ɪn.tər.ˈpen.ɪ.treɪt/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈpen.ə.treɪt/ /; / /ɪn.tə.ˈpen.ɪ.treɪt/ (BrE)
