Rhincodon typus

noun
species of large, plankton-feeding shark (whale shark)
9.9
Pronunciation: /rɪn.kə.ˈdɑːn ˈtaɪ.pəs/ (AmE), /rɪn.kə.ˈdɒn ˈtaɪ.pəs/ (BrE)
