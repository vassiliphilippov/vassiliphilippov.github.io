Rhincodon typus

noun
species of large, plankton-feeding shark (whale shark)
9.9
Pronunciation: / raɪnˈkoʊdɒn ˈtaɪpəs / (AmE), / rɪŋˈkəʊdɒn ˈtɪpʊs / (BrE)
a very large, slow-moving, spotted shark that resembles a whale and feeds mainly on plankton; commonly known as the whale shark, it is found in warm surface waters around the world
