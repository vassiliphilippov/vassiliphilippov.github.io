pruning hook

noun
long-handled tool for pruning trees
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnɪŋ hʊk / (AmE), / ˈpruːnɪŋ hʊk / (BrE)
a tool with a long handle and a curved blade, sometimes with a clipper, used for pruning small trees and shrubs

Examples

A pruning hook tool
He sharpened his pruning hook before trimming the overgrown hedge.
Old farming manuals often illustrated the proper technique for using a pruning hook.
The gardener used a pruning hook to reach the higher branches of the apple tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pruning shears - hand-held scissors for cutting plants
  • degree lower: sickle - curved blade tool for cutting grass or crops
