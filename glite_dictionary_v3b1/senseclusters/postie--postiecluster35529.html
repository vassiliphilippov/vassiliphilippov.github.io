postie

noun
mail carrier
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊsti / (AmE), / ˈpəʊsti / (BrE)
an informal term for a person whose job is to deliver mail to homes and businesses

Examples

Friendly postie
The postie always greets us with a smile when delivering the mail.
Our local postie braves all kinds of weather to bring our letters.
Children waved as the postie cycled down the street with his bag of parcels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courier - a person who delivers messages or packages
  • synonym: mailman - a person who delivers mail
  • synonym: postman - a man who delivers mail
