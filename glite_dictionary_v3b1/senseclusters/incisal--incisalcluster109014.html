incisal

adjective
of tooth cutting edge
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsaɪzəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsaɪzəl / (BrE)
relating to the cutting edge or surface of the front teeth, especially the incisors

Examples

The incisal edge
The dentist checked the incisal edges of her front teeth for chips.
Wear on the incisal surface can indicate teeth grinding at night.
A small filling was placed on the incisal corner of his incisor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occlusal - relating to the chewing surface of the back teeth
