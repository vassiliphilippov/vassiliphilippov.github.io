black-headed snake

noun
small ground snake with a black head
Pronunciation: / ˌblækˈhɛdəd sneɪk / (AmE), / ˌblækˈhɛdɪd sneɪk / (BrE)
a small, secretive, ground-dwelling snake, typically found from the central United States to Argentina, known for its black-colored head

Examples
