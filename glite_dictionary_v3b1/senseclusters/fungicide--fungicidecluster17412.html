fungicide

noun
chemical that kills or prevents fungi
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋɡɪˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡɪsʌɪd / (BrE)
a chemical or substance used to destroy or prevent the growth of fungi, especially on crops or plants

Examples

Use fungicide spray
Always wear gloves when handling fungicide to avoid skin irritation.
Farmers sprayed fungicide on their crops to prevent mold and mildew.
The use of a fungicide helped protect the roses from black spot disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbicide - a substance used to destroy unwanted plants
  • synonym: pesticide - a substance used to destroy pests
