counterbattery

adjective
against enemy artillery
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈbætəri / (BrE)
relating to military actions or equipment used to detect, attack, or neutralize enemy artillery positions.

Examples

Counterbattery radar system
The army deployed counterbattery systems to track and destroy enemy rocket launchers.
Modern counterbattery radars can quickly pinpoint the source of incoming fire.
Effective counterbattery operations can reduce the threat from hostile artillery units.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artillery - large military guns used in warfare
  • synonym: defensive - intended to protect against attack
