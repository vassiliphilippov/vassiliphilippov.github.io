songwriting

noun
composing songs
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔŋˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or process of creating songs by composing both lyrics and music

Examples

Songwriting process
She has been passionate about songwriting since she was a teenager.
Many famous artists started their careers in songwriting before performing on stage.
Taking a class in songwriting helped him express his emotions through music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composition - the act of creating music or a musical work
  • synonym: lyricist - a person who writes the words for songs
