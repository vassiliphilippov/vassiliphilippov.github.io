radial

adjective
radiating from a central point
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəl /; / ˈreɪdɪəl / (BrE)
arranged like rays or radiating lines from a common central point

Examples

Radial pattern
Radial heat is generated when energy spreads outward in all directions from a hot object.
The artist used radial lines in the design, creating a sense of movement.
The pattern on the sunflower displays a radial symmetry, with seeds spiraling outward from the center.
Several cities have a radial road system, with main highways fanning outward from downtown.
Sunflowers display a beautiful radial arrangement of seeds at their centers.
The starfish is known for its radial symmetry, with arms extending from a central body.

  • synonym: radiating - spreading out from a central point
