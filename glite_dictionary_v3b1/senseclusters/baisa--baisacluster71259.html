baisa

noun
Omani currency unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪsə / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsə / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Oman, equal to one thousandth of an Omani riyal.

Examples

One baisa coin
Exchanging a few baisas at the airport was enough to buy some snacks.
She counted the baisa coins before handing them to the cashier.
The price was clearly marked as 500 baisa on the package.
