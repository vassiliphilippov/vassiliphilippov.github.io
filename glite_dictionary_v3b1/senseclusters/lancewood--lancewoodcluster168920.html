lancewood

noun
strong elastic wood from tropical trees
Pronunciation: / ˈlænsˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈlɑːnsˌwʊd / (BrE)
a strong, straight, and elastic wood from certain tropical trees, used for making durable items such as shafts, sporting goods, building materials, and tool handles
