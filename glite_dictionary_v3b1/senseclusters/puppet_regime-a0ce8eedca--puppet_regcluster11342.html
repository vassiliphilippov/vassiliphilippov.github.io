puppet regime

noun
government controlled by an outside power

Pronunciation: /ˈpʌp.ɪt reɪ.ˈʒiːm/ /; / /ˈpʌp.ət reɪ.ˈʒiːm/ /; / /ˈpʌp.ɪt rə.ˈʒiːm/ (AmE), /ˈpʌp.ɪt reɪ.ˈʒiːm/ /; / /ˈpʌp.ɪt rə.ˈʒiːm/ /; / /ˈpʌp.ət reɪ.ˈʒiːm/ (BrE)
A government that is appointed by and under the control of a foreign or external authority, often lacking real independence.

Examples

A puppet regime government
History books often debate whether the new authorities formed a legitimate government or a puppet regime.
Many citizens resented living under a puppet regime imposed by foreign powers.
The occupying forces installed a puppet regime that answered only to their interests.
