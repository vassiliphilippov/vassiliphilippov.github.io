tertiary syphilis

noun
third and severe stage of syphilis
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrʃieri ˈsɪfɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːʃəri ˈsɪfɪlɪs / (BrE)
the third and most severe stage of syphilis, marked by serious damage to internal organs such as the brain, spinal cord, heart, and liver

Examples

Tertiary syphilis stage
Tertiary syphilis can cause severe neurological problems if left untreated.
In the third stage, tertiary syphilis affects multiple internal organs.
The patient's heart condition was eventually linked to tertiary syphilis.
