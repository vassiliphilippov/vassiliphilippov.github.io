staphylococcal enterotoxin B

noun
potent bacterial toxin; biological agent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstæfɪləˌkɑkəl ˌɛntəroʊˈtɑksɪn bi / (AmE), / ˌstæfɪləˌkɒkəl ˌɛntərəʊˈtɒksɪn bi / (BrE)
a potent toxin produced by the bacterium Staphylococcus aureus, sometimes used as a biological warfare agent due to its ability to cause severe illness.
