garbage dump

noun
place for dumping waste
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ dʌmp / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ dʌmp / (BrE)
a place where waste materials are taken and left, often in large quantities

Examples

A garbage dump site
A strong odor came from the nearby garbage dump every summer evening.
Seagulls circled above the garbage dump, searching for food scraps.
The city is planning to close the old garbage dump next year.

Related Terms
