stalker

noun
person who follows or harasses others
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔkər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who moves stealthily or follows and observes someone, typically with unlawful or obsessive intent

Examples

A stalker follows someone
Security cameras caught the stalker prowling around the neighborhood late at night.
She lived in fear after learning that a stalker had been following her for months.
The police arrested the stalker outside the celebrity's home after a week of surveillance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pursuer - someone who follows another
  • antonym: victim - person who is harmed or harassed by a stalker
