inferior labial artery

noun
artery supplying the lower lip
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriɚ ˈleɪbiəl ˈɑrˌtɛri / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriə ˈleɪbɪəl ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
an artery branching from the facial artery that supplies blood to the lower lip

Examples

The inferior labial artery
Bruising of the lower lip can sometimes indicate injury to the inferior labial artery.
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the inferior labial artery.
The inferior labial artery is essential for the blood supply to the lower lip.
