cranially

adverb
toward the skull
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪniəli / (AmE), / ˈkreɪniəli / (BrE)
in a direction or manner toward or relating to the skull or head

Examples

Spread cranially
The doctor noted that the tumor had spread cranially from its original location.
In some animals, the heart is positioned more cranially than in humans.
The surgeon made an incision just cranially to the vertebra.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caudally - in a direction toward the tail or lower part of the body
