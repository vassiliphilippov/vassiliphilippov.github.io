germ warfare

noun
use of bacteria as a weapon in war
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɝm ˈwɔrˌfɛr / (AmE), / dʒɜːm ˈwɔːˌfeə / (BrE)
the use of bacteria or other microorganisms to intentionally cause disease as a weapon in war

Examples

Germ warfare weapons
Many nations have signed treaties to ban the practice of germ warfare.
The use of germ warfare during the conflict caused widespread panic among civilians.
There are concerns that terrorist groups might resort to germ warfare in the future.
