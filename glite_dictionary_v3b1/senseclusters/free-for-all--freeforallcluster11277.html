free-for-all

noun
multiplayer ‘every-player-for-themselves’ mode
4.1
Pronunciation: / ccfri f5979 c854l / (AmE), / ccfrid0 f59 c854d0l / (BrE)
(in gaming) a multiplayer game mode or match in which every player competes against all other players, typically with no teams or alliances.

Examples

Game free-for-all
The tournament's final round was a chaotic free-for-all where every player fought alone for the prize.
They added a new free-for-all mode so friends could play without forming teams or alliances online.
In casual lobbies, players prefer a relaxed free-for-all that focuses on quick reflexes rather than strategy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deathmatch - a multiplayer game mode in which players compete individually to get the most kills; often synonymous with free-for-all.
  • antonym: team deathmatch - a multiplayer mode where players are divided into teams and compete against other teams; contrasts with free-for-all.
