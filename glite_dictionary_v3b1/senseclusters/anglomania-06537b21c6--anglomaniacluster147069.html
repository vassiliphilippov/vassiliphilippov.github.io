Anglomania

noun
excessive enthusiasm for English things
9.3 9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡloʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌaŋɡləʊˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
An excessive or obsessive enthusiasm for English language, culture, or things related to England.

Examples

Anglomania for England
Despite criticism, his Anglomania drove him to visit London every year.
Her Anglomania was evident in her collection of British teapots and novels.
The rise of Anglomania in the city led to more English-style cafes opening up.
