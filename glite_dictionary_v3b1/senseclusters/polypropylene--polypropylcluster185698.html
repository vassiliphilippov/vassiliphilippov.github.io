polypropylene

noun
a synthetic polymer used in plastics
3.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.ˈprɑːp.ə.liːn/ (AmE), /pɒ.lɪ.ˈprɒp.ə.liːn/ (BrE)
a tough, flexible synthetic polymer made from propylene, used especially for making ropes, containers, and packaging materials

Examples

Polypropylene plastic material
Due to its lightweight nature, polypropylene is often chosen for reusable shopping bags.
Many food containers are made from polypropylene because it is durable and resistant to chemicals.
The ropes used on the sailboat were constructed from polypropylene fibers for added strength and buoyancy.
