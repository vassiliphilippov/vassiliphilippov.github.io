bay

noun
a reddish-brown horse
6.5
Pronunciation: / beɪ / (AmE), / beɪ / (BrE)
a horse of a reddish-brown color, especially with a black mane, tail, and lower legs

Examples

A bay horse
Among all the horses, the bay caught everyone's attention with its striking color.
She admired the glossy coat of the bay standing in the paddock.
The rider brought out his finest bay for the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chestnut - a horse of a red or reddish-brown color with a similar or lighter mane and tail
