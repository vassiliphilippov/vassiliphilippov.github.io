cambion

noun
demon-human offspring
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmbiən / (AmE), / ˈkæmbiən / (BrE)
a mythical being believed to be the offspring of a demon and a human

Examples

A cambion child
In medieval folklore, a cambion was often feared for its supernatural abilities.
The story tells of a cambion who struggled to find acceptance among humans.
Some legends claim that a cambion inherits magical powers from its demonic parent.
