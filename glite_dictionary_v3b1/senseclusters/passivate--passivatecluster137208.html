passivate

verb
to reduce reactivity
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪˌveɪt / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪveɪt / (BrE)
to make a material less chemically reactive by forming a protective surface layer

Examples

To passivate metal
Engineers often passivate stainless steel to prevent it from rusting in harsh environments.
To improve durability, manufacturers passivate electronic components during production.
The process to passivate aluminum involves creating a thin oxide layer on its surface.
