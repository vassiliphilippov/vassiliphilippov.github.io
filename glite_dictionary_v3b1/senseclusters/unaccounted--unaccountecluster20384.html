unaccounted

adjective
not explained or tracked
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈkaʊntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkaʊntɪd / (BrE)
not included, explained, or tracked in a count or record

Examples

Items unaccounted for
After the fire, several items were still unaccounted in the warehouse inventory.
The money raised at the event was unaccounted for in the final report.
Many hours went unaccounted during the investigation into the incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: missing - not present or cannot be found
  • antonym: recorded - noted or included in a record
