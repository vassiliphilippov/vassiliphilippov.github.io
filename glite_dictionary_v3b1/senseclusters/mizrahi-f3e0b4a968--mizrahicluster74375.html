Mizrahi

noun
Jew of Middle Eastern descent
Pronunciation: / mɪzˈrɑːhi / (AmE), / mɪzˈrɑːhi / (BrE)
a Jewish person whose family origins are in the Middle East or North Africa

Examples

A Mizrahi Jew
The festival celebrated the unique traditions of the Mizrahi community.
Many Mizrahi Jews brought their culinary heritage to Israel.
She is proud of her Mizrahi roots and family history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Ashkenazi - a Jewish person of Central or Eastern European descent
  • synonym: Sephardi - a Jewish person of Spanish or Portuguese descent
