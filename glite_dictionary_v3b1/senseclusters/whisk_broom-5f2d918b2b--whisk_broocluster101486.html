whisk broom

noun
small short-handled broom
Pronunciation: / wɪsk ˌbruːm / (AmE), / wɪsk ˌbruːm / (BrE)
A small, short-handled broom used for brushing clothes or small areas.

Examples

A whisk broom handle
He kept a whisk broom in his desk drawer for small messes.
She quickly grabbed the whisk broom to clean the crumbs off her jacket.
The tailor used a whisk broom to remove lint from the suit.
