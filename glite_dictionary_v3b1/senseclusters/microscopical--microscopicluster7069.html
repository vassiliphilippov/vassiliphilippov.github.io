microscopical

adjective
extremely small; visible only with a microscope
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.krəˈskɑː.pɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌmɪ.krəˈskɒ.pɪ.kəl / (BrE)
So small as to be visible only with the aid of a microscope; extremely minute.

Examples

Microscopical particles
Only a microscopical trace of the substance remained after the reaction.
The differences between the two minerals are microscopical and require special equipment to detect.
Under microscopical examination, the sample revealed hidden structures not seen with the naked eye.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macroscopic - visible to the naked eye; not requiring a microscope
  • synonym: microscopic - so small as to be visible only under a microscope
