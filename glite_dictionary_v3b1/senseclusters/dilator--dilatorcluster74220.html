dilator

noun
surgical instrument for widening openings
Pronunciation: / daɪˈleɪtər / (AmE), / daɪˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a surgical instrument used to widen or expand an opening or organ in the body

Examples

Surgical dilator tool
After the incision, the doctor inserted a dilator to widen the cervical canal.
Nasal dilators are sometimes used to improve airflow in patients with breathing difficulties.
The surgeon used a dilator to gently stretch the narrowed blood vessel.
