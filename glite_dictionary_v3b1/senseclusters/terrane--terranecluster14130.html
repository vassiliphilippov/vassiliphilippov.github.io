terrane

noun
region of land
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈreɪn / (AmE), / təˈreɪn / (BrE)
an area or region of land; territory, especially in archaic or literary use.

Examples

Ancient terrane
The ancient king claimed the entire terrane as his own.
Legends spoke of a hidden terrane beyond the great river.
Explorers mapped the uncharted terrane in their quest for new lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domain - an area owned or controlled by a ruler or government
  • synonym: territory - an area of land under the jurisdiction of a ruler or state
