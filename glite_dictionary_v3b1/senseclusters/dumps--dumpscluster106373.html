dumps

noun
mildly depressed or low mood
Pronunciation: / dʌmps / (AmE), / dʌmps / (BrE)
a mildly depressed or low-spirited state, especially used in the phrase 'in the dumps'

Examples

To be in the dumps
A rainy weekend can leave anyone in the dumps if they had outdoor plans.
After failing the exam, he was really in the dumps for a few days.
Whenever she feels in the dumps, she listens to her favorite music to cheer up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blues - a state of mild sadness or melancholy
