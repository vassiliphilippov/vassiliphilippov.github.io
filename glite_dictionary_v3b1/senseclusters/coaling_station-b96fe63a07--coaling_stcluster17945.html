coaling station

noun
seaport where ships refuel with coal
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a seaport facility where ships take on supplies of coal for fuel

Examples

A coaling station port
During the age of steamships, the availability of a coaling station was crucial for long-distance sea travel.
Many former coaling stations have now been repurposed into commercial ports.
The naval fleet stopped at a coaling station to replenish their fuel before continuing their journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refueling station - a place where vehicles or vessels take on fuel
