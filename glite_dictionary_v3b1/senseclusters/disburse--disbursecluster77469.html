disburse

verb
to pay out money from a fund
7.4 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / d…™sÀàb…úrs / (AmE), / d…™sÀàb…úÀês / (BrE)
to pay out money, especially from a fund or for a specific purpose

Examples

To disburse funds
After reviewing the budget, the committee disbursed money to several community projects.
The organization will disburse the grant funds to the selected applicants next month.
The treasurer is responsible for disbursing payment to all vendors by the end of the quarter.
