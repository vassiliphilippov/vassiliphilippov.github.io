clean

adjective
unrestricted; unconditional
1.0 17/m
Pronunciation: / klin / (AmE), / kliÀên / (BrE)
free from problems, restrictions, marks, or complications; smooth, flawless, or without discredit

Examples

The candidate won a clean victory without any legal disputes or challenges.
The committee gave the project a clean approval to proceed immediately.
Applicants with a clean criminal record are preferred for this position.

The rocket achieved a clean launch with no technical issues.

  • unblemished

  • unsullied

  • spotless

  • pristine

  • clear
