churn butter

verb
make butter by agitating cream
8.1
Pronunciation: / tʃɝn ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / tʃɜːn ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
to make butter by agitating cream until it separates into butter and buttermilk, typically using a churn
