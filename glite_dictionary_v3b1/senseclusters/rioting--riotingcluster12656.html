rioting

noun
violent group disorder
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.ɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.ɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
violent and disorderly behavior by a group of people, typically as a public disturbance

Examples

Public rioting
Rioting broke out in the city after the verdict was announced.
Images of rioting filled the news broadcasts for days.
The authorities imposed a curfew to prevent further rioting in the streets.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: protest - a public demonstration expressing strong objection to a policy or situation
  • synonym: riot - a violent disturbance of the peace by a crowd
