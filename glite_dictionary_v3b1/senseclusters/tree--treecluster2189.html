tree

noun
a tall woody plant with a trunk and branches
1.0
Pronunciation: / triː / (AmE), / triː / (BrE)
A perennial plant with a single main stem or trunk, supporting branches and leaves, typically growing to a considerable height.

Examples

A tall tree
A single tree stood in the middle of the empty field.
In autumn, the maple trees turn brilliant shades of red and gold.
The old oak tree in our backyard provides shade all summer long.
