sozzled

adjective
very drunk; intoxicated
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑzəld / (AmE), / ˈsɒzəld / (BrE)
Very drunk; intoxicated, especially to a noticeable or excessive degree.

Examples

Completely sozzled
After three glasses of wine, he was completely sozzled and started singing loudly.
She stumbled out of the party, looking utterly sozzled.
We found him sozzled on the sofa, mumbling about old times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
