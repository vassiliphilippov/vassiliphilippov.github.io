Saturday

proper noun
day after Friday
Pronunciation: / ˈsæɾɚˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsætədeɪ / (BrE)
the day of the week between Friday and Sunday, often considered a day for rest or leisure

Examples

Saturday morning market
We always go to the farmers' market on Saturday mornings.
Her favorite yoga class is held every Saturday afternoon.
On Saturday, the park is usually full of families enjoying the weekend.
We usually visit my grandparents on Saturday.
The market is busiest every Saturday morning when locals come to shop.
She likes to sleep in late on Saturday after a long week at work.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Sunday - the day of the week after Saturday
