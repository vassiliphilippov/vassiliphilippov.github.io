tax-free

adjective
not subject to tax
2.2
Pronunciation: / tæksˈfri / (AmE), / tæksˈfriː / (BrE)
(of goods, income, or transactions) not subject to taxation; exempt from tax.

Examples

Tax-free goods
All purchases made at the airport are tax-free for international travelers.
Many cross-border workers enjoy tax-free income under certain treaties.
She invested her savings in a tax-free bond to maximize her returns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax-exempt - not required to pay tax; exempt from taxation
  • antonym: taxable - subject to tax
