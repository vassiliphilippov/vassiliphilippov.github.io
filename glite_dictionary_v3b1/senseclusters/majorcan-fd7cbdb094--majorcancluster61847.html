Majorcan

noun
person from Majorca
Pronunciation: / məˈdʒɔrkən / (AmE), / məˈdʒɔːkən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the island of Majorca (Mallorca) in Spain, or the Catalan dialect spoken there
