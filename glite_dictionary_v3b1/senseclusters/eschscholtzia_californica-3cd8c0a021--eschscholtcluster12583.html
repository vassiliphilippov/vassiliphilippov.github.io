Eschscholtzia californica

noun
plant known as the California poppy
Pronunciation: / ɛʃˈʃɔltziə ˌkælɪˈfɔrnɪkə / (AmE), / ɛʃˈʃɒltziə ˌkælɪˈfɔːnɪkə / (BrE)
A plant species native to the Pacific coast of North America, widely cultivated for its bright yellow to red flowers; commonly known as the California poppy.
