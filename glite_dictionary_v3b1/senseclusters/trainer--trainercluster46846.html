trainer

noun
person who teaches or instructs skills
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnə / (BrE)
a person who teaches skills or provides instruction, especially in a sport, profession, or for animals

Examples

A dog trainer
She hopes to become a professional dog trainer after completing her course.
The trainer prepared the team for the upcoming championship.
The new fitness trainer helped me design a personalized workout routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coach - person who trains athletes or performers
  • synonym: instructor - person who teaches a particular skill
  • antonym: trainee - person who is being trained
