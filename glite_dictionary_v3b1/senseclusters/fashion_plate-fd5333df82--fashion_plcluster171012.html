fashion plate

noun
person concerned with appearance and dress
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃ.ən pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən pleɪt / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who is extremely concerned with his dress and appearance, often following the latest fashions

Examples

A real fashion plate
At every party, she arrived as the fashion plate of the evening, drawing everyone's attention.
Ever since college, Michael has been a true fashion plate with every outfit meticulously chosen.
The magazine featured a famous fashion plate known for setting new style trends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dandy - a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance
