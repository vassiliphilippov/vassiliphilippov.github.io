line

noun
military fortified position
Pronunciation: / laɪn / (AmE), / laɪn / (BrE)
a fortified or strategic position forming the most forward position of troops, especially in military conflict

Examples

A front line
Enemy forces broke through the defensive line during the night.
Heavy fighting continued on the eastern line for several days.
The soldiers took up positions along the line before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: front - the foremost line or position in a battle or conflict
  • antonym: rear - the position behind the front line in a military formation
