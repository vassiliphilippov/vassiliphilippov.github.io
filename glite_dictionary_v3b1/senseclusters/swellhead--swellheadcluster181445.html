swellhead

noun
conceited or self-important person
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈswɛlˌhɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is conceited and self-centered

Examples

A swellhead person
Ever since he got that promotion, he’s become a real swellhead around the office.
Her friends warned her not to become a swellhead after winning the prize.
Nobody likes a swellhead who always talks about himself at every gathering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - someone who boasts a lot
  • synonym: egotist - a person who is excessively conceited or self-absorbed
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast about their achievements
