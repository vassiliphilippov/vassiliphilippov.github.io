virality

noun
infectiousness level
7.0
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈræləti / (AmE), / vaɪˈræləti / (BrE)
the degree to which a virus or infectious agent can spread quickly between hosts

Examples

Virality of a virus
Scientists are studying the virality of the new virus to understand its potential impact.
The virality of the infection made it difficult to contain within the community.
Public health officials are concerned about the high virality of this particular strain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagiousness - the quality of being easily spread from person to person
  • synonym: infectiousness - the ability of a disease to be transmitted from one person to another
