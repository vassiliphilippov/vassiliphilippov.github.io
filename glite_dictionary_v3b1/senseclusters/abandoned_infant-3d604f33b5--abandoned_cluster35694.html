abandoned infant

noun
child left without care; parents unknown
Pronunciation: / əˈbændənd ˈɪnfənt / (AmE), / əˈbændənd ˈɪnfənt / (BrE)
A child who has been left without care and whose parents are unknown.

Examples

An abandoned infant
An abandoned infant was found at the train station and taken to a shelter.
Hospitals must report cases where an abandoned infant is discovered on the premises.
The authorities placed the abandoned infant in the care of a foster family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foundling - an infant found after being abandoned by its parents
