mortuary

noun
place for keeping dead bodies before burial
7.5 1.45/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…îÀêrt Éueri / (AmE), / Ààm…îÀêtj ä…ôri / (BrE)
a building or room where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation

Examples

A mortuary building
Flowers lined the entrance to the mortuary on the day of the funeral.
She worked as an assistant in the hospital mortuary.
The body was taken to the mortuary for identification.

Synonyms

  • dead room
    9.1 0.00/m
    dead room is a simple name for a place where bodies are kept, while mortuary is the more formal term for that room.
  • morgue
    6.5 5.32/m
    morgue is a room where bodies stay for identification or autopsy, while a mortuary stores bodies before burial or cremation.
  • funeral home
    3.4 2.62/m
    funeral home is a business that prepares bodies and holds services, while a mortuary mainly stores bodies before burial or cremation.
  • funeral parlor
    3.4 0.39/m
    funeral parlor is the room used for viewings and small ceremonies, while a mortuary is mainly a place to keep bodies.
