braggy

adjective
boastful; inclined to brag
Pronunciation: / ˈbræɡi / (AmE), / ˈbraɡi / (BrE)
tending to boast or talk with excessive pride about oneself or one's achievements; boastful.

Examples

A braggy attitude
His braggy attitude annoyed his coworkers.
She sounded a bit braggy when describing her promotion at dinner.
Try not to come across as braggy during your interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boastful - showing excessive pride in oneself or one's achievements
  • antonym: modest - not talking much about oneself or one's achievements
