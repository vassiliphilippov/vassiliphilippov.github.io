antidiabetic drug

noun
drug for treating diabetes mellitus
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.daɪˈæb.ɪ.tɪk drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌanti.daɪˈab.ɪ.tɪk drʌɡ / (BrE)
a drug used to prevent or manage diabetes mellitus by controlling blood sugar levels

Examples

An antidiabetic drug medicine
Metformin is the most commonly used antidiabetic drug worldwide.
Some patients require more than one antidiabetic drug to effectively manage their diabetes.
The doctor prescribed an antidiabetic drug to help control her blood sugar levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypoglycemic agent - a drug that lowers blood sugar, used to treat diabetes
  • synonym: insulin - a hormone and drug used to control blood sugar in diabetes
