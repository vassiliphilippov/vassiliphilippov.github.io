deathliness

noun
quality of resembling death
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθlinəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛθlinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of resembling or suggesting death, especially in appearance, atmosphere, or feeling

Examples

Deathliness in the air
A chill deathliness filled the abandoned house as we stepped inside.
The deathliness of the winter landscape made the village seem deserted.
There was a strange deathliness in the silence after the storm passed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lifelessness - the state of lacking life or vitality
  • antonym: vitality - the state of being full of life and energy
