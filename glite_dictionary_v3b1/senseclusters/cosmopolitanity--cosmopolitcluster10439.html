cosmopolitanity

noun
state of being cosmopolitan
Pronunciation: / kɑz.moʊˌpɑ.ləˈtæn.ə.ti / (AmE), / kɒz.məˌpɒl.ɪˈtæn.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being cosmopolitan, especially characterized by a worldwide rather than limited or provincial scope or outlook

Examples

Global cosmopolitanity
Academic discussions often highlight the importance of cosmopolitanity in shaping global citizenship.
Her cosmopolitanity was apparent in her appreciation for diverse cultures and ideas.
The city's cosmopolitanity attracted visitors from all over the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmopolitanism - the ideology or practice of being cosmopolitan
  • antonym: provincialism - the state or quality of being limited in perspective to a narrow region
