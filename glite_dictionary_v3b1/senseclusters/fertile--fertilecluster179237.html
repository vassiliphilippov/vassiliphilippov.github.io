fertile

adjective
able to produce many offspring or fruits
2.5 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfɜːr.təl/ /; / /ˈfɜːr.taɪl/ (AmE), /ˈfɜː.təl/ /; / /ˈfɜː.taɪl/ (BrE)
(of people, animals, or plants) able to produce young, seeds, or fruit; able to reproduce or produce many offspring/fruits

Examples

Fertile animals

The soil in this area is so fertile that crops thrive year after year.
