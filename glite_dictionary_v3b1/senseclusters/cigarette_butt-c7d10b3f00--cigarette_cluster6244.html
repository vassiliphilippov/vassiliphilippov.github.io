cigarette butt

noun
small remnant left from a smoked cigarette
5.9
Pronunciation: / sɪɡəˌrɛt bʌt / (AmE), / sɪɡərɛt bʌt / (BrE)
the small remnant of a cigarette left after it has been smoked

Examples

A cigarette butt on street
He flicked the cigarette butt into the street, not noticing the trash can nearby.
Please do not drop your cigarette butt on the ground; use the designated bins.
The beach cleanup volunteers collected dozens of cigarette butts from the sand.

Related Terms
