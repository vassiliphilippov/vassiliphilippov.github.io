Puranic

adjective
relating to the Hindu Puranas
Pronunciation: / pʊˈrɑːnɪk / (AmE), / pjʊəˈrɑːnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the Puranas, a genre of ancient Hindu religious texts.

Examples

Puranic texts
He is a scholar specializing in Puranic texts and ancient Indian history.
Many stories in Hindu mythology are preserved in Puranic literature.
The temple rituals follow Puranic traditions that have been practiced for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Vedic - relating to the Vedas, ancient Hindu scriptures
  • synonym: epic - relating to ancient Indian epics like the Mahabharata or Ramayana
