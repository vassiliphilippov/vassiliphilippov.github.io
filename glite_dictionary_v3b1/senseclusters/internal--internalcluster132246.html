existing or happening within a country, organization, or group; domestic or not involving outsiders

Examples

Internal affairs
Many of the company's problems stem from internal conflicts rather than outside competition.
The government increased spending to address internal security issues.
This new law is designed to regulate internal affairs, not international relations.
All internal communications should be kept confidential to the staff.
The company conducted an internal investigation to address the complaints.
There was an internal dispute between members of the management team.

Synonyms

  • intragroup
    7.2 0.02/m
    intragroup refers to events or relations that occur inside a single group or institution, while internal is a broader term for something inside an organization, country, or group.
  • interior
    4.1 0.62/m
    interior refers to domestic or government affairs of a country and sounds formal, while internal is a general word for things inside any group or organization.
