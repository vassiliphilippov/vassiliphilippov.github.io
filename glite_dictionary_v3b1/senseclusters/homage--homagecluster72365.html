homage

verb
to pay tribute
7.5
Pronunciation: /oʊ.ˈmɑːʒ/ /; / /ə.ˈmɑːʒ/ /; / /ˈhɑː.mɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmɑːʒ/ /; / /ˈhɒ.mɪdʒ/ (BrE)
to show respect or pay tribute to someone or something, often by imitating or referencing them

Examples

To homage a classic movie
The director homaged classic films by recreating their most famous scenes.
Many artists have homaged their mentors in their own work.
The new building design homages the city's architectural history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disregard - to pay no attention or respect to someone or something
  • synonym: honor - to show great respect or admiration for someone or something
