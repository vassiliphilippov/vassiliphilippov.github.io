arch support

noun
support device for foot arch
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɑrtʃ səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ɑːtʃ səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a device or part of a shoe designed to support the arch of the foot and improve comfort or alignment

Examples

Extra arch support
Doctors often recommend shoes with extra arch support for people with flat feet.
My running shoes have built-in arch support that helps prevent foot pain.
The new insoles offer excellent arch support during long walks.
