icebreaker

noun
an action to relax tension or start conversation
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
an action, remark, or activity intended to relieve tension or get conversation going in a formal or awkward situation

Examples

Icebreaker activity
A simple introduction exercise can act as an effective icebreaker in a group of strangers.
Her witty story served as the perfect icebreaker at the dinner party.
The team played a silly game as an icebreaker before starting the meeting.
