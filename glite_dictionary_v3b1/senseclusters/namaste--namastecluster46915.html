namaste

noun
respectful greeting gesture
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːməsteɪ / (AmE), / ˈnʌməsteɪ / (BrE)
a respectful greeting or gesture of peace, performed with a slight bow and hands pressed together, commonly used in South Asia

Examples

A namaste gesture
At the end of the yoga class, everyone said namaste to the instructor.
He greeted his grandmother with a respectful namaste when she entered the room.
During the ceremony, the children performed a traditional namaste to welcome the guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farewell - an act of saying goodbye
  • synonym: greeting - a polite word or gesture of welcome or recognition
