adhesive material

noun
substance that bonds surfaces
Pronunciation: / ədˈhisɪv məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ədˈhiːsɪv məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
A substance used to unite or bond surfaces together.

Examples

Apply adhesive material
Before attaching the panels, apply the adhesive material evenly on the surface.
The tiles are held in place by a special adhesive material.
You should use a strong adhesive material to fix the broken vase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adhesive - a substance that causes adhesion
  • synonym: glue - an adhesive used for sticking objects together
  • degree lower: sealant - a substance used to block the passage of fluids
