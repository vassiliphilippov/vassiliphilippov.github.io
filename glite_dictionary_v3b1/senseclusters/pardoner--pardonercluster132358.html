pardoner

noun
medieval seller of church indulgences
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrdənər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdnə /; / ˈpɑːdənə / (BrE)
(historical) a person, especially a church official in medieval times, who was authorized to grant pardons or sell indulgences for sins
