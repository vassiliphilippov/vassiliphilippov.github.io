rice paddy

noun
flooded field for growing rice
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪs ˌpædi / (AmE), / ˈraɪs ˌpædi / (BrE)
A field that is irrigated or flooded for the cultivation of rice.

Examples

A rice paddy field
Farmers worked all day in the rice paddy under the hot sun.
Her boots were muddy after she crossed the rice paddy.
The lush green rice paddy stretched to the horizon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paddy field - a field where rice is grown in standing water
