doorbell

noun
button at a door that rings or buzzes
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrˌbɛl / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌbɛl / (BrE)
a push button device at an outer door that sounds a ringing or buzzing signal when pressed, used to announce a visitor's presence

Examples

The doorbell rings
I heard the doorbell ring and went to see who was at the door.
Please press the doorbell if you arrive after 7 PM.
The doorbell is broken, so just knock when you get here.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzzer - an electrical device that makes a buzzing sound, often used as a signal
