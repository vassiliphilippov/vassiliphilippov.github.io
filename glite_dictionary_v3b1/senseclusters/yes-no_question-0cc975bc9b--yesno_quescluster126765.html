yes-no question

noun
question answerable by 'yes' or 'no'
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌjɛs ˈnoʊ ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˌjɛs ˈnəʊ ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
A question that can be answered simply with 'yes' or 'no'.

Examples

A yes-no question
Avoid using only yes-no questions in your survey, as they limit detailed responses.
Please answer each yes-no question on the form before moving to the next section.
The interviewer began with a simple yes-no question to ease the candidate into the conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open-ended question - a question that requires more than a yes or no answer
