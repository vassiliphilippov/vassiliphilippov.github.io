Scrooge McDuck

proper noun
extremely wealthy and miserly fictional duck
7.9
Pronunciation: / skruːdʒ məkˈdʌk / (AmE), / skruːdʒ məkˈdʌk / (BrE)
a fictional character portrayed as an extremely wealthy and miserly duck, often used as a symbol of extreme frugality and accumulation of riches

Examples

Scrooge McDuck character
My brother is such a Scrooge McDuck when it comes to sharing his money.
People called him Scrooge McDuck for refusing to donate even a penny.
She joked that her boss swims in cash like Scrooge McDuck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ebenezer Scrooge - fictional miser from Dickens' 'A Christmas Carol'
  • synonym: miser - person who hoards wealth and spends very little
