tweaker

noun
methamphetamine user
9.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtwiÀêk…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàtwiÀêk…ô / (BrE)
an informal and often offensive term for a person who uses methamphetamine, typically showing nervous or unpredictable behavior

Examples

The abandoned house was rumored to be a place where tweakers gathered late at night.
