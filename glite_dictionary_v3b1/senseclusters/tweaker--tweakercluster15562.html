tweaker

noun
methamphetamine user
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːkər / (AmE), / ˈtwiːkə / (BrE)
an informal and often offensive term for a person who uses methamphetamine, typically showing nervous or unpredictable behavior

Examples

A drug tweaker
The police arrested a tweaker after neighbors reported erratic, loud shouting all night.
She was worried her brother might become a tweaker if he kept hanging out with the wrong crowd.
The abandoned house was rumored to be a place where tweakers gathered late at night.
