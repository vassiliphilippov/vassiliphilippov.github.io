undercut

noun
stroke imparting reverse spin (sports)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkʌt / (BrE)
(in sports) a stroke that imparts reverse spin to the ball, causing it to rotate backward as it moves.

Examples

Backhand undercut
His backhand undercut made the ball drop sharply over the net.
The coach demonstrated how to execute an effective undercut during the match.
You can control the pace of the game with a well-placed undercut in table tennis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slice - a stroke that puts sidespin or backspin on the ball (sports)
