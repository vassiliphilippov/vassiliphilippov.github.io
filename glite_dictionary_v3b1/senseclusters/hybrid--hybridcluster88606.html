hybrid

noun
offspring of genetically different parents
7.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.brɪd/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.brəd/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.brɪd/ /; / /ˈhaɪ.brəd/ (BrE)
An organism that is the offspring of parents of different species, breeds, varieties, or genetic stocks, especially as a result of breeding.

Examples

A hybrid animal
A mule is a classic example of a hybrid between a horse and a donkey.
Modern agriculture often relies on hybrid crops for higher yields.
The hybrid was developed by crossing two different species of roses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbreed - an animal or plant that is a mix of two different breeds or varieties
  • antonym: purebred - an animal bred from parents of the same breed or variety
