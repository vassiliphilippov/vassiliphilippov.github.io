boarding

noun
the act of getting onto a ship or aircraft
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of passengers and crew getting onto a ship, aircraft, or vehicle

Examples

Boarding the plane
Boarding will begin at Gate 10 in approximately ten minutes.
Please have your passport and boarding pass ready at the gate.
The cruise ship completed boarding well before its scheduled departure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disembarkation - the act of leaving a ship, aircraft, or vehicle
  • synonym: embarkation - the act of going onto a ship or aircraft (especially at the start of a journey)
