eclamptic

adjective
relating to eclampsia
Pronunciation: / ɪˈklæmptɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈklæmptɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by eclampsia, a serious complication during pregnancy involving seizures

Examples

Eclamptic seizures
The patient was admitted to the hospital with eclamptic symptoms requiring immediate treatment.
Doctors closely monitor pregnant women for any signs of eclamptic complications.
Prompt care is essential for women who become eclamptic during pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eclampsia - a serious condition in pregnancy marked by seizures
  • degree lower: preeclamptic - relating to preeclampsia, a condition before eclampsia in pregnancy
