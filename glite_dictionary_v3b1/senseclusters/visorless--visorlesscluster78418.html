visorless

adjective
without a visor
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪzərləs / (AmE), / ˈvaɪzələs / (BrE)
lacking a visor or a projecting front brim, especially on a hat or helmet

Examples

Visorless helmet
He wore a visorless helmet during the race.
The new cap design is completely visorless for a modern look.
Some cyclists prefer visorless helmets for better visibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brimless - without a brim or projecting edge
  • antonym: visored - having a visor or projecting front brim
