unformatted

adjective
without structure
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔːrmætɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːmætɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Unformatted text file
The report was submitted as an unformatted text file, making it hard to read.
He sent me an unformatted email with no paragraphs or headings.
The data appeared unformatted and required additional processing before use.
