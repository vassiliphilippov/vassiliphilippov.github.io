airwoman

noun
female aircraft operator or crew
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈeəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who operates or serves as a member of the crew of an aircraft, especially in a military context

Examples

Military airwoman
Many airwomen served with distinction during the war.
She became the first airwoman in her squadron to receive the highest honor.
The airwoman skillfully landed the plane despite difficult weather conditions.
