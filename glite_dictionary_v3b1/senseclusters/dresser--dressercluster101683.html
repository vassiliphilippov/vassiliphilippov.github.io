dresser

noun
theater assistant for actors' costumes
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsə / (BrE)
a person who assists actors by helping them with their costumes and wardrobe during performances

Examples

Theater dresser
Every leading actor relies on a skilled dresser to handle quick costume changes.
She worked as a dresser in the theater for over a decade.
The dresser prepared the costumes backstage before the actors' entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wardrobe assistant - a person responsible for managing costumes in theater or film
