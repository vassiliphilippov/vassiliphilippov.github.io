bluishness

noun
the quality of being somewhat blue
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being somewhat blue or tinged with blue.

Examples

Slight bluishness in petals
He noticed a slight bluishness in the petals, making the flowers look unusual.
Some gemstones are prized for the unique bluishness they display under sunlight.
The bluishness of the morning sky signaled a clear, cold day ahead.
