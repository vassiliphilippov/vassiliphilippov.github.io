bioterrorist

noun
person using biological weapons
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˈtɛrərɪst / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˈtɛrərɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses biological agents, such as viruses or bacteria, to cause harm or spread fear for political or criminal purposes

Examples

A bioterrorist attack
The government increased security measures to prevent a bioterrorist attack on the city.
Scientists work to identify potential threats that a bioterrorist might use.
After the incident, authorities searched for the suspected bioterrorist responsible for the outbreak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bioterrorism - the use of biological agents to cause harm or fear
  • synonym: terrorist - a person who uses violence or threats to achieve political aims
