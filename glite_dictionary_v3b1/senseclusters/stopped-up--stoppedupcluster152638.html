stopped-up

adjective
blocked, filled opening or passage
3.7
Pronunciation: / stɑpt ˈʌp / (AmE), / stɒpt ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of an opening or passage) blocked so that nothing can pass through, often due to being filled with something.

Examples

Stopped-up sink
My kitchen sink was stopped-up for hours until the plumber arrived.
She had trouble breathing because her nose was stopped-up from the cold.
We couldn't use the bathtub because the drain was completely stopped-up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blocked - unable to pass through due to obstruction
  • antonym: clear - free from blockage or obstruction
  • synonym: clogged - blocked with accumulated matter
