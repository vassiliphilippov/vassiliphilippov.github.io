disliker

noun
person who dislikes online content
5.6
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈlaɪkər / (AmE), / dɪsˈlaɪkə / (BrE)
a person who shows disapproval of online content by clicking a dislike button on a website or app

Examples

Online disliker
The video received several comments from a frequent disliker who never left positive feedback.
After posting her opinion, she noticed a new disliker appeared on her profile.
Some users ignore the dislikers and focus on the positive responses instead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or negative opinions
  • antonym: supporter - a person who approves of or supports something
