genial

adjective
pleasantly mild or comforting (weather, climate)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiniəl /; / ˈdʒiːniəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːniəl / (BrE)
friendly, cheerful, or pleasantly warm (of a person, manner, or atmosphere)

Examples

Genial smile
His genial manner put everyone at ease during the meeting.
Thanks to its genial climate, the region attracts visitors all year round.
We enjoyed a picnic under the genial sunshine in early spring.
Despite his fame, the author remained a genial and approachable person.
She greeted the guests with a genial smile that immediately put everyone at ease.
The manager was known for his genial personality and welcoming attitude toward customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affable - friendly, good-natured, or easy to talk to
  • synonym: cordial - warm and friendly
  • antonym: harsh - unpleasantly rough or severe (of weather or conditions)
  • antonym: unfriendly - not friendly or kind
