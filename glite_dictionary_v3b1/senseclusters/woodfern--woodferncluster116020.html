woodfern

noun
a type of woodland fern (genus Dryopteris)
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌfɜrn / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌfəːn / (BrE)
Any of various ferns of the genus Dryopteris, typically found in woodlands.

Examples

A woodfern plant
Botanists found several species of woodfern thriving in the old forest reserve.
He planted a woodfern near the stream to add some greenery to the landscape.
The shady garden was filled with lush woodferns growing between the trees.
