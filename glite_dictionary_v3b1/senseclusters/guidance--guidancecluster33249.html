Synonyms

  • advice
    1.0 90/m
    advice is a specific suggestion about what to do, while guidance is broader help or information to solve a problem.
  • counsel
    4.4 6/m
    counsel is formal advice often about major choices, while guidance is broader help for solving problems or making decisions.
  • direction
    1.0 6/m
    direction gives clear, specific steps on how to act, while guidance provides general help to solve a problem.
  • pointer
    2.8 2/m
