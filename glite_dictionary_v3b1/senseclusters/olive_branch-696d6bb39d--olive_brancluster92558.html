olive branch

noun
gesture of peace or reconciliation
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv bræntʃ /; / ˈɑlɪv bræntʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv brɑːntʃ / (BrE)
a gesture or symbol showing a desire for peace or to end a disagreement

Examples
