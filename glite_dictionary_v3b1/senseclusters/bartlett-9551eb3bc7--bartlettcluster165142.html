Bartlett

noun
a variety of juicy yellow pear
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrtlət / (AmE), / ˈbɑːtlɪt / (BrE)
A variety of pear that is juicy and yellow when ripe, commonly grown for its sweet flavor.

Examples

A ripe Bartlett pear

The Bartlett is often used in fruit salads because of its juicy sweetness.
They planted several Bartlett pear trees in their backyard orchard.
