tautog

noun
a large edible Atlantic coastal fish
9.7
Pronunciation: / tɔˈtɔg / (AmE), / tɔˈtɒg / (BrE)
a large, dark-colored edible fish found along the Atlantic coast of North America, often sought by anglers for food

Examples

The tautog fish
Fishermen along the coast prize the tautog for its tasty, firm flesh.
The tautog is sometimes called blackfish due to its dark coloration.
We caught several large tautogs near the rocky reefs yesterday.
