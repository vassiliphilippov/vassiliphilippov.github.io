IGN

proper noun
French mapping agency
Pronunciation: / iː dʒiː ɛn / (AmE), / iː dʒiː ɛn / (BrE)
the French national agency responsible for producing maps and geographic data (Institut géographique national)

Examples

French IGN maps
Before planning his cycling route, he checked the IGN website for the latest maps.
The IGN provides detailed topographic data for all regions of France.
Researchers used IGN maps to study changes in the French landscape over time.
