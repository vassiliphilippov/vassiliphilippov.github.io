chivalrously

adverb
in a gallant or courteous manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪvəlrəsli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪvəlrəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows gallantry, courtesy, or noble behavior, especially towards others.

Examples

To chivalrously open doors
He chivalrously opened the door for his elderly neighbor.
She thanked him as he chivalrously handed her back the lost wallet.
The knight chivalrously defended the honor of the lady in the court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gallantly - in a brave or courteous manner
  • antonym: rudely - in a discourteous or impolite manner
