penetrate

verb
spread or diffuse through
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnɪˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈpenɪtreɪt / (BrE)
(of a substance or influence) to spread or diffuse through something; to pass into or affect all parts of something.

Examples

To penetrate the crowd
A sense of hope penetrated the crowd despite the difficult circumstances.
The powerful aroma of freshly baked bread penetrated the entire house.
The sound of the alarm penetrated every corner of the building.

  • synonym: permeate - to spread or diffuse through
