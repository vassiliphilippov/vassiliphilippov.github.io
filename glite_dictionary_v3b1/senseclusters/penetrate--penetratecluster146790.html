Synonyms

    6.0 1/m

  • pervade
    6.7 1/m
    pervade fills a place or feeling so you notice it everywhere, while penetrate focuses on passing into and affecting all parts.
  • diffuse
    5.5 2/m
    diffuse spreads something widely across an area or to many people, while penetrate means to pass into and affect every part.
