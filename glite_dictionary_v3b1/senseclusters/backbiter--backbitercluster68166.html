backbiter

noun
someone who slanders others behind their back
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbaɪtə / (BrE)
a person who maliciously speaks about someone in their absence, typically to damage their reputation

Examples

A backbiter at work
He was labeled a backbiter after making false accusations behind his friend's back.
No one trusts a backbiter in the office, as they always spread rumors about colleagues.
The group disapproved of the backbiter who tried to tarnish everyone's reputation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backstabber - someone who betrays another in their absence
  • synonym: slanderer - someone who falsely speaks bad things about others
