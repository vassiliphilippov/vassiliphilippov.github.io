assumptive

adjective
accepted as true without proof
3.6 6.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsʌmp.tɪv/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsʌmp.tɪv/ (BrE)
Accepted as true or real without proof; based on assumption rather than evidence.

Examples

Assumptive reasoning

She made several assumptive statements without any supporting data.
The team's assumptive attitude led them to overlook crucial evidence.
