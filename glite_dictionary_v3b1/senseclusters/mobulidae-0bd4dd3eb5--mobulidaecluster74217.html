Mobulidae

noun
family of large rays (mantas, etc.)
Pronunciation: / moʊˈbjuːlɪˌdiː /; / moʊˈbjuːlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / məʊˈbjuːlɪˌdiː /; / məʊˈbjuːlɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of large rays without venomous spines, including manta rays and devil rays
