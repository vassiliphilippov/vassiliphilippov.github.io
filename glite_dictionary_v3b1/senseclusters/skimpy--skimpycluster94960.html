skimpy

adjective
revealing or barely covering clothing
4.9 0.70/m

(of clothing) very small and revealing, covering only a small part of the body.

Examples

Synonyms

  • scantily clad
    6.5 0.49/m
    scantily clad means wearing very little clothing in public, while skimpy describes garments that are small and revealing.
  • scanty
    7.1 0.10/m
    scanty usually means barely enough to cover the body and may seem inappropriate, while skimpy stresses being small and revealing.
  • brief
    3.6 0.50/m
    brief refers to very short length, often showing bare skin at the hem, while skimpy implies deliberately revealing coverage.
