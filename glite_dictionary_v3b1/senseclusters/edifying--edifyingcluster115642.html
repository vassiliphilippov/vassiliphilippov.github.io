edifying

adjective
morally or intellectually uplifting
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
providing moral or intellectual instruction or improvement; uplifting or enlightening

Examples

An edifying book
Reading biographies of great leaders can be truly edifying.
She found his lectures both engaging and edifying.
The film was entertaining and edifying, leaving the audience deep in thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corrupting - causing someone to become morally depraved
  • synonym: informative - providing useful or interesting information
  • synonym: instructive - useful for imparting knowledge or information
