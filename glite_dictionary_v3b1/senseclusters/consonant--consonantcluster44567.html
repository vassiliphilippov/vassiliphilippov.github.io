consonant

adjective
in agreement; harmonious
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.sə.nənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sə.nənt / (BrE)
in agreement or harmony with something; consistent

Examples

Consonant with the rules
Her proposal was consonant with the company's long-term goals.
Such actions are not consonant with the spirit of our agreement.
The two theories are consonant in most important respects.

Related Terms
