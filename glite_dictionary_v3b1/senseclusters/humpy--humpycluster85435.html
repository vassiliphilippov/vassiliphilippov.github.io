humpy

noun
simple Australian shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpi / (BrE)
a small, simple shelter made from natural materials, especially in Australia

Examples

A humpy shelter
The travelers built a humpy to sleep in overnight.
During their camping trip, they constructed a humpy from branches and leaves.
Many early settlers in Australia lived in a humpy before building permanent homes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hut - a small, simple building or shelter
  • synonym: shack - a roughly built hut or cabin
