auteurist

noun
supporter of auteur theory
9.3
Pronunciation: /oʊ.ˈtɜːr.ɪst/ /; / /ə.ˈtɜːr.ɪst/ (AmE), /əʊ.ˈtɜː.ɪst/ /; / /ə.ˈtɜː.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports auteur theory or considers film directors as the primary creative force behind a movie

Examples

Auteurist critic
The auteurist argued that the director's vision shaped every aspect of the film.
Many critics with an auteurist perspective focus on the director's unique style.
As an auteurist, she believed that true cinema reflects the personality of its creator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auteur - a film director seen as the main creative influence on a film
  • antonym: collaborator - someone who works jointly with others, especially in creative projects
