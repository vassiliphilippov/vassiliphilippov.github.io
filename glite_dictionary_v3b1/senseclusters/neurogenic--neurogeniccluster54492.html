neurogenic

adjective
arising from nerve tissue
2.8 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
arising in or caused by nerve tissue or nervous system activity

Examples

Neurogenic shock
Neurogenic shock is a medical emergency requiring immediate attention.
Some types of chronic pain are classified as neurogenic if they result from nerve damage.
The patient suffered from neurogenic bladder dysfunction following the spinal cord injury.

Related Terms
