gallant

adjective
showing great dignity or nobility
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælənt / (AmE), / ˈɡalənt / (BrE)
having or displaying great dignity, nobility, or impressive grandeur

Examples

Gallant speech
He gave a gallant speech that moved everyone in the audience to tears.
The gallant procession marched through the city streets with banners flying.
The castle stood on the hill in a truly gallant manner, commanding respect from all who saw it.
