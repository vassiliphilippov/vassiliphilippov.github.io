oversnow

verb
to cover with snow
7.8
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsnoʊ/ /; / /ˈoʊ.vər.snoʊ/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsnəʊ/ /; / /ˈəʊ.və.snəʊ/ (BrE)
to cover or bury something with snow, especially as a result of heavy snowfall

Examples

To oversnow a road
The blizzard will oversnow the entire village by morning.
After the storm, the cars were completely oversnowed and difficult to find.
Heavy winds can quickly oversnow the hiking trails, making them hard to follow.
