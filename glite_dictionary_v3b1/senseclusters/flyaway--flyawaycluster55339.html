flyaway

adjective
capricious; whimsical or flighty
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪ.əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪ.əˌweɪ / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) guided by whim and fancy; capricious or flighty.

Examples

Flyaway personality
Her flyaway nature made it hard for her to commit to a single plan.
People described him as flyaway due to his ever-changing whims.
The flyaway decisions of youth often lead to unexpected adventures.
