rigatoni

noun
short, ridged tube-shaped pasta
Pronunciation: / ˌrɪɡəˈtoʊni / (AmE), / ˌrɪɡəˈtəʊni / (BrE)
a type of pasta in the shape of short, large tubes with ridges along their length

Examples

A plate of rigatoni
I prefer rigatoni because the sauce clings well to its ridges.
She cooked a pot of rigatoni with tomato sauce for dinner.
The restaurant's specialty is baked rigatoni with cheese and vegetables.
