dividedness

noun
state of being split
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪdɪdnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being separated, split, or lacking unity among people or things

Examples

Team dividedness
The dividedness within the team made it difficult to reach a decision.
Social dividedness can lead to misunderstandings and conflict in a community.
After the debate, the dividedness among the students became more apparent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: division - the act or process of separating into parts or groups
  • antonym: unity - the state of being joined together or in agreement
