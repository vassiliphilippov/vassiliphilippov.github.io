chasse

noun
quick gliding ballet step
Pronunciation: / ʃæˈseɪ / (AmE), / ʃæˈseɪ / (BrE)
a quick gliding ballet step in which one foot always leads, often used as a connecting movement

Examples

A chasse step
Children learning ballet often practice the chasse to improve their coordination and balance.
Her routine included a fast chasse before the leap, adding grace to the movement.
The dancer executed a perfect chasse across the stage, seamlessly linking two jumps.
