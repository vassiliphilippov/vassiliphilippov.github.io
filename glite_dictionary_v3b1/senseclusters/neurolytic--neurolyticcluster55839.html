neurolytic

adjective
nerve-destroying
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the destruction of nerve tissue, especially to relieve pain

Examples

Neurolytic treatment
A neurolytic block can provide long-term pain relief for patients with cancer.
Doctors may recommend a neurolytic procedure when other pain treatments are ineffective.
The neurolytic agent was injected to destroy the targeted nerve fibers.
