agree

verb
be consistent or match
4.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈgriː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈgriː/ (BrE)
to match, be consistent, or go well together (of things, statements, or characteristics)

Examples

The stories agree well
Her account of the event does not agree with what others have reported.
The figures in the report do not agree with those provided by the accountant.
The results of the second experiment agree closely with the initial findings.
The curtains and sofa agree perfectly, creating a calm atmosphere in the room.
The wine and the main course agreed surprisingly well, enhancing the flavors of both.
Their opinions do not always agree, but on this project they complement each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmonize - to be in agreement or consistent; to blend or match well
  • synonym: match - to be the same as or equal to something else
