antidiscrimination

noun
prevention of unfair treatment
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪdɪskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntɪdɪskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the policy or practice of preventing unfair treatment of people based on group characteristics such as race, gender, or religion

Examples

Antidiscrimination policy
The new law includes strong antidiscrimination provisions to protect minority groups.
Many companies have adopted antidiscrimination policies to create a fair workplace.
Activists praised the government for its commitment to antidiscrimination efforts in education.
