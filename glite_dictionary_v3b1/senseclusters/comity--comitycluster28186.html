comity

noun
mutual respect and harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.mə.ti / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.mɪ.ti / (BrE)
a state of mutual respect, harmony, and courteous behavior, especially among nations or groups

Examples

Mutual comity among nations
Despite their differences, there was a strong sense of comity among the committee members.
International organizations strive to promote comity in global relations.
The nations maintained comity through regular diplomatic meetings.

  • antonym: discord - lack of agreement or harmony
