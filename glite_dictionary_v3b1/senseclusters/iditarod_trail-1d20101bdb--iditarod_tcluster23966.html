Iditarod Trail

proper noun
historic trail in Alaska; route of the Iditarod race
7.6
Pronunciation: /ɪd.ˈɪt.ər.ɔːd treɪl/ (AmE), /ɪd.ˈɪt.ə.rɒd treɪl/ (BrE)
