howling

noun
loud emotional cry or sound
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
a long, loud, emotional cry or sound, especially one expressing pain, amusement, or strong feeling

Examples

A howling of pain
A howling of anguish echoed through the dark hallways.
The eerie howling from the woods frightened the children.
Their howling grew louder as the jokes became more outrageous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: howl - a long, loud, doleful cry or wail
