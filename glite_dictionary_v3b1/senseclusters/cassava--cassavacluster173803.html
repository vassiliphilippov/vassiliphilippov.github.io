cassava

noun
tropical plant with starchy roots
7.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈsæv.ə/ /; / /kə.ˈsɑː.və/ (AmE), /kə.ˈsɑː.və/ /; / /kə.ˈsæv.ə/ (BrE)
