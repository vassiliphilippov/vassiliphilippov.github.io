damaged

adjective
harmed or spoiled; less useful or valuable
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈdæmɪdʒd / (BrE)
harmed, injured, or spoiled so that it is less useful or valuable

Examples

Damaged goods
Insurance will not cover damaged property from natural disasters.
Some of the books were damaged during the move.
The damaged car was taken to the mechanic for repairs.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ruined - completely destroyed or severely damaged
