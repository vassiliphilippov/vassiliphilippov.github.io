bravas

noun
applause for female performer
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːvəz / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːvəz / (BrE)
shouts or calls of approval directed at a female performer, used as the plural of 'brava'.

Examples

Loud bravas for her
The audience shouted bravas after the soprano's stunning performance.
Loud bravas filled the theater as the lead actress took her final bow.
She smiled with gratitude as the crowd offered enthusiastic bravas at the end of the show.
