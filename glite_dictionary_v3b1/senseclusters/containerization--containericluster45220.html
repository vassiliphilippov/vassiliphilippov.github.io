containerization

noun
use of containers
Pronunciation: / kənˌteɪnəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / kənˌteɪnəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of using standardized containers to transport goods or to package and deploy software applications

Examples

Containerization of goods
The shipping industry was transformed by containerization in the twentieth century.
Modern software deployment often relies on containerization to ensure consistency across environments.
Thanks to containerization, goods can be moved quickly and efficiently between ships, trains, and trucks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: container - a large box or receptacle used for transporting goods
  • synonym: packaging - the process of wrapping or boxing goods for transport or sale
