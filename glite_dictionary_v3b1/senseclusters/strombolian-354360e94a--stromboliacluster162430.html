Strombolian

noun
person from Stromboli
Pronunciation: / strɒmˈboʊliən / (AmE), / strɒmˈbəʊliən / (BrE)
a person who comes from or lives on the island of Stromboli in Italy

Examples

A Strombolian resident
A proud Strombolian welcomed visitors to the island with stories of its famous volcano.
The traditional dishes prepared by a Strombolian often include fresh seafood and local vegetables.
Many Strombolians have families who have lived on the island for generations.
