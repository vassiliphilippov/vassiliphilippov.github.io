toy spaniel

noun
very small spaniel dog breed
Pronunciation: / tɔɪ ˈspæniəl / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈspænɪəl / (BrE)
A very small breed of spaniel dog, kept mainly as a pet or companion.

Examples

A toy spaniel dog
Many people find the toy spaniel to be an ideal companion for apartment living.
She adopted a toy spaniel because of its friendly temperament and small size.
The toy spaniel sat quietly on its owner's lap during the meeting.
