north light

noun
consistent natural light
5.7
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ laɪt / (AmE), / nɔːθ laɪt / (BrE)
steady, indirect light that comes from the north, often preferred in art and photography for its consistency

Examples

North light in studios
Painters often choose studios with north light to avoid harsh shadows on their canvases.
The photographer praised the north light for giving her portraits a soft, even tone.
Architects design some rooms to capture north light for its reliable illumination throughout the day.
