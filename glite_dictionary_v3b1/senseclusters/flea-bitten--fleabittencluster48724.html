flea-bitten

adjective
shabby or worn out; dilapidated
Pronunciation: / ˈfliˌbɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈfliːˌbɪtn̩ / (BrE)
(informal) Shabby, worn out, or in poor condition from age or neglect.

Examples

A flea-bitten apartment
He lives in a flea-bitten apartment above a noisy bar.
She donated her old, flea-bitten armchair to the charity shop.
The travelers spent the night in a flea-bitten motel on the outskirts of town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decrepit - worn out or ruined because of age or neglect
  • synonym: run-down - in poor condition from neglect or overuse
