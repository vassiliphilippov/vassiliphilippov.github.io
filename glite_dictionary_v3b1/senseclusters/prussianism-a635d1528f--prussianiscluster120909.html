Prussianism

noun
strict discipline
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprʌʃənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈprʌʃənɪzəm / (BrE)
strict military discipline and authoritarian practices, especially those associated with Prussia or its traditions

Examples

Strict Prussianism
