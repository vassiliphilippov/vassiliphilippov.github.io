pyro

noun
fireworks operator
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪrəʊ / (BrE)
a person who works with or operates fireworks and flame effects, especially as a pyrotechnician

Examples

Concert pyro operator
The concert's pyro set up an impressive display of flames and sparks during the final song.
After years of training, she became the lead pyro for the city's annual fireworks show.
Safety is always the top priority for any professional pyro working at large events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pyrotechnician - a person skilled in the use of fireworks
