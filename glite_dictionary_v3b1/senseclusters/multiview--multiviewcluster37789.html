multiview

noun
multiple views display
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌvjuː / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌvjuː / (BrE)
a display mode or screen layout that shows multiple camera feeds or image views at the same time

Examples

Security multiview
The security system uses multiview to let staff monitor several areas at once.
During the video call, I switched to multiview to see all participants on my screen.
The sports broadcast offered a multiview option so fans could watch different camera angles simultaneously.
