spar

verb
furnish with spars (beams or poles)
8.2
Pronunciation: / spɑr / (AmE), / spɑː / (BrE)
to furnish (a ship or structure) with spars (long poles or beams, especially on a ship)

Examples

To spar a ship
Older shipyards still occasionally spar wooden schooners for traditional regattas.
The crew worked late into the night to spar the new vessel before departure.
They sparred the entire fleet with stronger masts after the storm.
