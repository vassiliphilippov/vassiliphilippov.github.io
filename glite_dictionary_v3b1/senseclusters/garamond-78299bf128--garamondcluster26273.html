Garamond

proper noun
serif typeface
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡærəmɒnd / (AmE), / ˈɡærəmɒnd / (BrE)
a classic serif typeface widely used in printing and digital design for its elegant and readable style

Examples

Garamond typeface

Many classic novels are printed in Garamond because of its readability and beauty.
Switching the document to Garamond made the text look more professional and refined.
