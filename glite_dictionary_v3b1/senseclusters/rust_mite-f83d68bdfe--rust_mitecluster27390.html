rust mite

noun
plant pest causing rust-like discoloration
7.3
Pronunciation: / rʌst maɪt / (AmE), / rʌst maɪt / (BrE)
a tiny mite that infests plants and causes a reddish-brown discoloration on leaves or fruit

Examples

A rust mite infestation
A magnifying glass is needed to see a rust mite on the plant's surface.
Gardeners often inspect leaves for signs of rust mite activity in the spring.
The citrus orchard suffered severe damage due to an infestation of rust mites.
