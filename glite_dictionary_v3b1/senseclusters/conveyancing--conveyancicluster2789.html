conveyancing

noun
legal transfer of property ownership
Pronunciation: / kənˈveɪjənsɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈveɪənsɪŋ / (BrE)
the legal process of transferring the ownership of property from one person to another

Examples

Property conveyancing
Mistakes in the conveyancing documents delayed the sale of the property.
She hired a solicitor to handle the conveyancing when she bought her first house.
The process of conveyancing can sometimes take several weeks to complete.
