theatric

adjective
dramatic or theatrical
6.7
Pronunciation: / θiˈætrɪk / (AmE), / θiˈætrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the theater or acting, or behaving in a dramatic or exaggerated way

Examples

A theatric performance
Her theatric performance on stage captivated the entire audience.
He made a theatric entrance, waving his arms and bowing deeply.
The debate was filled with theatric gestures and passionate speeches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dramatic - full of drama or excitement
  • antonym: subtle - not obvious or showy
  • synonym: theatrical - relating to the theater or exaggerated in manner
