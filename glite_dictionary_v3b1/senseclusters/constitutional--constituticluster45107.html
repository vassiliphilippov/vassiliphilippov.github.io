constitutional

noun
regular walk for health
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.stəˈtuː.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
A walk taken regularly for the sake of one's health or exercise.

Examples

A morning constitutional
Doctors used to recommend a daily constitutional to maintain good health.
Every afternoon, the old gentleman enjoyed his constitutional around the park.
She invited her friend to join her for a brisk morning constitutional.
