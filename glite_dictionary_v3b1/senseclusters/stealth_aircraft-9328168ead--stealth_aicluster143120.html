stealth aircraft

noun
aircraft designed to avoid radar detection
Pronunciation: / stɛlθ ˈɛr.kræft / (AmE), / stɛlθ ˈeə.krɑːft / (BrE)
An aircraft designed using technology that makes it difficult to detect by radar.

Examples

Stealth aircraft technology
Military analysts discussed the effectiveness of stealth aircraft in modern warfare.
Several stealth aircraft participated in the nighttime training mission.
The new stealth aircraft was designed to evade enemy radar and minimize detection.
