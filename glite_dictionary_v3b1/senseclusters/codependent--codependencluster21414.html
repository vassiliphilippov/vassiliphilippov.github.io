codependent

noun
emotionally dependent person
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌkəʊdɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
a person who relies excessively on another for emotional support and may enable that person's unhealthy behaviors

Examples

A codependent person
She realized she was a codependent after always putting her partner's needs before her own.
Therapy helped him understand the patterns that made him a codependent in his relationships.
Many codependents struggle to set healthy boundaries with loved ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enabler - a person who encourages or allows another's unhealthy behavior
  • antonym: independent - a person who does not rely on others for emotional support
