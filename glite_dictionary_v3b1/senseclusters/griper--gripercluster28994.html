griper

noun
person who clutches
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪpər / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪpə / (BrE)
a person who holds or seizes something tightly, especially by clutching or grasping firmly

Examples

Strong griper
The toddler was a griper, refusing to let go of his favorite toy all afternoon.
During the storm, the griper held onto the railing with all his strength.
She became a griper whenever she felt nervous in crowded places.

  • antonym: releaser - a person who lets go or releases something
