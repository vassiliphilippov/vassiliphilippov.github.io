haggardness

noun
tired appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈhæɡərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈhæɡədnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of looking tired, worn out, or exhausted, especially in appearance

Examples

The haggardness of face
After days of travel without rest, the haggardness in her eyes was impossible to miss.
The haggardness of the survivors told a story of hardship and endurance.
Despite his haggardness, he managed to smile and greet everyone warmly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatigue - extreme tiredness or exhaustion
  • antonym: freshness - the quality of looking lively or energetic
