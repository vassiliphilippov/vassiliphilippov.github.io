inshore

adjective
coming toward land from the sea
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈɪnʃɔː / (BrE)
(of wind or current) coming from the sea toward the land.

Examples

Inshore wind
A strong inshore wind delayed the boats' departure.
Surfers prefer inshore breezes for better waves near the beach.
The forecast warned of dangerous inshore currents after the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offshore - of wind or current: blowing away from land toward the sea
  • synonym: onshore - (of wind or current) coming from the sea toward the land
