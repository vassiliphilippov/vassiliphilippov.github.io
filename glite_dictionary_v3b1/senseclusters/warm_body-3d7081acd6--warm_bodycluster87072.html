warm body

noun
person or animal offering warmth or comfort
1.1
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
a person or animal who provides heat or physical comfort, especially by being nearby or in contact

Examples

A warm body nearby
After her surgery, she was grateful for the warm body of her cat lying beside her.
On a cold winter night, there's nothing better than having a warm body next to you.
The puppy became his favorite warm body to cuddle when he felt lonely.
