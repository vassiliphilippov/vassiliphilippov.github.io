oxidoreduction

noun
reversible redox (oxidation-reduction) reaction
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːksɪdoʊrɪˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪdəʊrɪˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
A reversible chemical reaction in which one substance is oxidized and another is reduced; a redox reaction.

Examples

Oxidoreduction reaction
Enzymes known as oxidoreductases catalyze oxidoreduction in biochemical pathways.
The experiment demonstrated the principle of oxidoreduction using an iron-copper cell.
The process of cellular respiration involves multiple oxidoreduction reactions to produce energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redox - a chemical reaction involving both oxidation and reduction
