simultaneously

adverb
at the same time
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪ.məlˈteɪ.ni.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌsɪm.əlˈteɪ.ni.əs.li / (BrE)
at the same time; occurring or done together in the same instant

Examples

Happens simultaneously

The two events occurred simultaneously, leaving everyone surprised.
