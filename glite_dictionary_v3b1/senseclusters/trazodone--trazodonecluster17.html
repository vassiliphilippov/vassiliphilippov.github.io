trazodone

noun
antidepressant medication
Pronunciation: / ˈtræz.əˌdoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtraz.əˌdəʊn / (BrE)
A non-tricyclic antidepressant drug, used primarily to treat depression and as a sedative.

Examples

trazodone medication
He switched to trazodone after not responding well to other antidepressants.
Patients taking trazodone should be aware of potential side effects like drowsiness.
The doctor prescribed trazodone to help manage her insomnia and depression.
