cannibalism

noun
the eating of one's own kind
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.ə.bəˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈkan.ɪ.bə.lɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the practice of eating the flesh of one's own species

Examples

Cannibalism practice
Cases of cannibalism have been reported during periods of extreme famine.
Some animal species exhibit cannibalism as part of their survival strategy.
The novel explores the psychological effects of cannibalism on its characters.
