lighthearted

adjective
cheerful and carefree
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
cheerful and carefree; not burdened by worry or serious concerns.

Examples

A lighthearted joke
Despite the tense situation, he managed to offer a lighthearted comment that eased the mood.
Her lighthearted attitude made even stressful days more enjoyable for everyone around her.
It was a lighthearted comedy that kept the audience smiling throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefree - free from worries or responsibilities
  • synonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • antonym: serious - solemn or not playful in character
