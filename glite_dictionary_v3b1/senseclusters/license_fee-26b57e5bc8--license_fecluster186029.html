license fee

noun
payment for permission to do something by license
2.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.səns fiː/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.səns fiː/ (BrE)
an official payment made to the government or authority for permission to carry out a particular activity, trade, or profession

Examples

The annual license fee for broadcasting has increased this year.
You must pay a license fee before you can sell alcohol legally.
