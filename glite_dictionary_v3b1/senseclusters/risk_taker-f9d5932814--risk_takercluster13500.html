risk taker

noun
person willing to take risks
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪsk ˌteɪkər / (AmE), / ˈrɪsk ˌteɪkə / (BrE)

Sarah is a natural risk taker, always eager to try new and challenging activities.
