BCC

noun
hidden email recipient
3.9
Pronunciation: /biː siː siː/ (AmE), /biː siː siː/ (BrE)
a copy of an email sent to someone whose address is hidden from the other recipients (blind carbon copy)

Examples

BCC field in email
She always puts her supervisor in the BCC field when sending important emails.
To keep the recipients private, he used BCC for the entire mailing list.
If you want to avoid reply-all chaos, consider using BCC for large groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: CC - a copy of an email sent to additional recipients whose addresses are visible
