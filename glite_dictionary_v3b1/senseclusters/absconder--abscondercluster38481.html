absconder

noun
person who runs to avoid arrest
Pronunciation: / æbˈskɑndər / (AmE), / æbˈskɒndə / (BrE)
a person who hides or runs away to avoid arrest, prosecution, or punishment

Examples

An absconder from police
After years as an absconder, he was finally caught by law enforcement in another country.
She was suspected of helping the absconder avoid capture by providing him shelter.
The police are searching the city for the absconder who escaped from prison last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fugitive - a person who is running away, especially from the law
