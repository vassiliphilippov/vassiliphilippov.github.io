Vesuvian

adjective
volcanic or explosive
Pronunciation: / vəˈsuːviən / (AmE), / vəˈsuːviən / (BrE)
relating to or resembling Mount Vesuvius, especially in terms of volcanic activity or explosive force

Examples

Vesuvian eruption
The scientist described the explosion as having a Vesuvian force that shook the entire region.
Her Vesuvian temper made everyone in the office nervous during meetings.
The artist painted a Vesuvian landscape inspired by the famous Italian volcano.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling strong emotion or activity
  • synonym: volcanic - relating to or produced by a volcano
