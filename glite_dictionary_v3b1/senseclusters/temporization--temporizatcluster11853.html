temporization

noun
temporary dental filling
9.5
Pronunciation: /tem.pə.raɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /tem.pə.raɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the placement of a temporary dental restoration to protect a tooth until a permanent treatment is applied

Examples

Dental temporization
The dentist performed temporization after removing the decay to protect the tooth until the next appointment.
Proper temporization helps prevent sensitivity and infection before the permanent crown is placed.
She was advised that temporization would keep her tooth safe until the final restoration was ready.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanent restoration - a long-lasting dental treatment such as a crown or bridge
  • synonym: temporary filling - a short-term dental material used to protect a tooth
