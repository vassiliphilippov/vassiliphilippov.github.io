Russophobia

noun
fear or dislike of Russia
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌsəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌrʌsəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
a strong dislike or fear of Russia, its people, or its policies

Examples

Russophobia fear
The rise of Russophobia has affected diplomatic relations between several countries.
Some media outlets have been accused of promoting Russophobia through biased reporting.
He wrote an essay analyzing the roots of Russophobia in Western societies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Russophilia - admiration or love for Russia or its people
  • synonym: xenophobia - fear or dislike of foreigners or strangers
