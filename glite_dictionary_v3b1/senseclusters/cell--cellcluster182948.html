cell

noun
mobile phone (informal, esp. US)
2.7
Pronunciation: / sɛl / (AmE), / sɛl / (BrE)
(informal) A mobile phone, especially in American English; a portable telephone that uses cellular network technology.

Examples

Call my cell
I'll give you a call on my cell when I get there.
Many people no longer have a landline, relying only on a cell for communication.
She forgot her cell at home and couldn't contact anyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landline - A telephone that uses a wired connection
  • synonym: mobile phone - A portable telephone that uses radio signals to communicate
