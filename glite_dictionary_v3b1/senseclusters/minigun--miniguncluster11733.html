minigun

noun
rapid-fire machine gun
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌɡʌn / (BrE)
a type of machine gun that can fire bullets at a very high rate, often used on vehicles or aircraft

Examples

A minigun machine gun
The helicopter was equipped with a powerful minigun for defense.
Soldiers took cover as the minigun unleashed a stream of bullets across the field.
In the movie, the hero used a minigun to hold off the attackers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machine gun - a gun that can fire many bullets quickly
  • antonym: rifle - a long gun designed to be fired from the shoulder
