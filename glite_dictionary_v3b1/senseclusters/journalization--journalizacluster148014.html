journalization

noun
recording in a journal
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɜrnələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜːnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of recording events or transactions in a journal, especially in accounting or record-keeping

Examples

Accurate journalization of accounts
Accurate journalization is essential for maintaining reliable financial records in any business.
The accountant completed the journalization of all transactions before preparing the monthly report.
Proper journalization helps prevent errors and ensures transparency in bookkeeping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: posting - the act of transferring journal entries to ledger accounts
