upset stomach

noun
digestive discomfort or mild digestive disorder
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛt ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛt ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
A mild disorder of the digestive system causing discomfort, nausea, or heartburn.

Examples

An upset stomach pain
Eating too quickly gave me an upset stomach during the meeting.
He stayed home from school because of an upset stomach and mild nausea.
Many people experience an upset stomach after trying new foods abroad.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: heartburn - a form of indigestion felt as a burning sensation in the chest
  • synonym: indigestion - discomfort or pain in the stomach associated with difficulty in digesting food
