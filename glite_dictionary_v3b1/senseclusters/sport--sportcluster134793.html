sport

noun
activity involving physical exertion and competition
Pronunciation: / spɔrt / (AmE), / spɔːt / (BrE)
An activity involving physical exertion, skill, and competition, usually played according to rules for entertainment or recreation.

Examples

A popular sport
Football is the most popular sport in many countries around the world.
She excels at almost every sport she tries, from tennis to swimming.
The Olympic Games showcase a wide variety of sport from archery to wrestling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: game - an activity that one engages in for amusement or fun
  • synonym: recreation - activity done for enjoyment when not working
