going

noun
progress toward a goal; process of advancing
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making progress toward a goal or completing something, especially relating to how easy or difficult it is

Examples

Tough going
Despite the obstacles, we made steady going toward our goal.
It can be tough going to learn a new language as an adult.
The first part of the hike was easy going, but the last mile was steep and rocky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progress - forward or onward movement toward a destination or goal
  • synonym: slog - a spell of difficult, tiring work or progress
