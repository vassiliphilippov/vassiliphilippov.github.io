Synonyms

  • judicial decision
    5.7 0.40/m
    a judicial decision is a court's ruling on a case, while judgment is the formal written decision issued by the court.
  • adjudication
    8.2 1.43/m
    adjudication is the legal process of deciding a dispute, while judgment is the specific court decision that follows that process.
  • determination
    4.0 10/m
    a determination is an official decision reached after review, while judgment is the court's formal written decision in a legal case.
  • decree
    5.7 9.40/m
    a decree is a binding order often issued by an authority, while judgment is the court's formal decision recorded in its documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decree - official order issued by a legal authority
  • synonym: opinion - a formal statement by a judge, especially the explanation for a legal decision
  • synonym: sentence - the punishment assigned to a defendant found guilty by a court
  • synonym: verdict - the decision reached by a jury or judge in a court case
