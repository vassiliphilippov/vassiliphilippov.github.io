judgment

noun
legal document with judicial reasoning
2.8 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌdʒ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌdʒ.mənt/ (BrE)
a formal decision or document given by a court of law

Examples

Court judgment
