dicumarol

noun
anticoagulant drug, superseded by warfarin
Pronunciation: / daɪˈkjuːməˌrɒl /; / daɪˈkuːməˌrɔl / (AmE), / dɪˈkjuːmərɒl /; / dɪˈkuːmərəʊl / (BrE)
an anticoagulant drug formerly used to prevent blood clotting, now largely replaced by warfarin
