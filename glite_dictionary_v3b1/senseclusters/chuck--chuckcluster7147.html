chuck

noun
device for holding workpiece or tool
2.2
Pronunciation: / tʃʌk / (AmE), / tʃʌk / (BrE)
a device with adjustable jaws used for holding a workpiece in a lathe or a tool in a drill

Examples

Drill chuck
Be sure to remove the drill bit from the chuck when you're finished.
The machinist tightened the chuck before starting the lathe.
This model features a quick-release chuck for fast bit changes.
