noncommunication

noun
refusal to communicate
8.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kə.mjuː.nɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nɑːn.kə.mjuː.nə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /nɒn.kə.mjuː.nɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nɒn.kə.mjuː.nə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the deliberate refusal to speak or share information with others, often as a form of protest or avoidance

Examples
