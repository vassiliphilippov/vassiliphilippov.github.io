aerodynamic

adjective
relating to movement through air
4.6 4.7
Pronunciation: /er.oʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪk/ (AmE), /eə.rəʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪk/ (BrE)
having a shape or design that reduces air resistance and allows smoother movement through air; relating to the science of how air moves around objects

Examples

Aerodynamic shape
Birds have naturally aerodynamic bodies that help them glide effortlessly through the air.
Cyclists often wear aerodynamic helmets to reduce wind resistance.
