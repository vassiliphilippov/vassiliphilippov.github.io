decision maker

noun
automated system that makes decisions
Pronunciation: / d\u026a\u02c8s\u026a\u0292\u0259n \u02ccme\u026ak\u0259r / (AmE), / d\u026a\u02c8s\u026a\u0292.\u0259n \u02ccme\u026a.k\u0259 / (BrE)
a computer program, algorithm, or automated system that makes decisions or determines outcomes without direct human intervention.

Examples

Automated decision maker
The automated decision maker approved the loan in under a minute, surprising the applicants.
Many companies now test a decision maker that scores resumes before human review begins.
City planners use a machine-learning decision maker to adjust traffic signals during rush hour.
