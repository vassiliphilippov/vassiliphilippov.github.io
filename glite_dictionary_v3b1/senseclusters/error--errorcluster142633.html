error

noun
misconception from incorrect information
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrɚ /; / ˈɛr.ɚ /; / ˈɛrər / (AmE), / ˈɛrə /; / ˈɛr.ər / (BrE)
a mistake or something that is not correct (such as in a belief, calculation, action, or decision)

Examples

Simple errors in calculation can lead to incorrect results.
The pilot admitted that a technical error caused the delay in landing.
