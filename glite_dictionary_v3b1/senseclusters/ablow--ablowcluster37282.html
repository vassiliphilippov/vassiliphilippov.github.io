ablow

adverb
in a blowing manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈbloʊ / (AmE), / əˈbləʊ / (BrE)
in a blowing or wind-driven manner; used to describe something being blown by the wind (archaic)

Examples

Curtains hang ablow
The curtains hung ablow in the open window as the wind swept through the room.
With the storm coming, the flags were ablow atop the ship's mast.
She watched the leaves ablow across the empty field on a blustery day.
