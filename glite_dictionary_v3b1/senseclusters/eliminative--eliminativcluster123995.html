eliminative

adjective
denying existence
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪmɪnətɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪmɪnətɪv / (BrE)
characterized by denying the existence or validity of a concept or category, especially in philosophical contexts.

Examples
