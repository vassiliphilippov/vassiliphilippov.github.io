external

adjective
relating to other countries
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɜr.nəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜː.nəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving other countries; foreign rather than domestic.

Examples

External relations
Diplomats handle both domestic and external affairs.
Many countries rely on external investment to support their growth.
The government discussed its external economic policy at the summit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic - relating to one's own country; not foreign
  • synonym: foreign - from or relating to a country that is not one's own
