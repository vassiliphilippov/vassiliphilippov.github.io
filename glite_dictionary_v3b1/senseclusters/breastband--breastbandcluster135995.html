breastband

noun
horse chest strap
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbrest.bænd/ /; / /ˈbrest.bənd/ (AmE), /ˈbrest.bænd/ /; / /ˈbrest.bənd/ (BrE)

Examples

Older carriages often used a breastband instead of a heavy collar for lighter loads.
