crane fly

noun
long-legged, mosquito-like fly
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪn flaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪn flaɪ / (BrE)
a long-legged, slender fly that resembles a large mosquito but does not bite

Examples

A crane fly insect
A crane fly landed on my window, startling me with its long legs.
Despite its appearance, a crane fly does not bite humans or animals.
We found several crane flies hovering near the porch light last night.
