quiet oasis

noun
calm place amid chaos
Pronunciation: / ˈkwaɪət oʊˈeɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət əʊˈeɪsɪs / (BrE)
A peaceful and calm place or situation within or surrounded by noise, stress, or activity.

Examples

A quiet oasis in city
He found a quiet oasis in the small, deserted park surrounded by traffic.
Her garden is a quiet oasis in the heart of the bustling city.
The library became a quiet oasis for students during exam week.

  • synonym: sanctuary - a place of refuge or safety
