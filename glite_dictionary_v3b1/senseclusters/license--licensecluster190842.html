Examples

To license a company
After passing several tests, the company was finally licensed to operate.
She is licensed to teach in three different countries.
The state licenses doctors to ensure they meet strict professional standards.
After passing the exam, she was licenced as a pharmacist.

The government must licence all operators before they can provide service.

Synonyms

  • authorize
    4.4 31/m
    authorize gives official power or legal right to act, while license is the formal permit often tied to meeting specific standards.
  • sanction
    6.3 3.61/m
    sanction means formal approval by an authority, while license usually refers to a specific permit given after checks.
  • grant
    3.3 35/m
    grant officially gives permission or rights in general, while license is a specific permit to do regulated activities.
