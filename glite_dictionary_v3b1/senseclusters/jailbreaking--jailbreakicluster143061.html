jailbreaking

noun
removing device restrictions
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪlˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪlˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing software restrictions on a device to allow unauthorized changes or installations

Examples

Phone jailbreaking
Many users try jailbreaking their phones to install apps not available in the official store.
After jailbreaking his tablet, he was able to customize the operating system in new ways.
Some manufacturers warn that jailbreaking a device can void its warranty.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locking - restricting a device to prevent unauthorized changes
  • synonym: rooting - removing restrictions on an Android device to gain full access
