school teacher

noun
teacher in a school (not college)
1.0
Pronunciation: / skuːl ˈtitʃər / (AmE), / skuːl ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
A teacher who works in a school below the college level, especially at primary or secondary level.

Examples

School teacher in class
Becoming a school teacher requires both passion for education and a strong knowledge of your subject.
My mother was a school teacher for over thirty years before she retired.
The school teacher encouraged her students to always ask questions and think critically.
