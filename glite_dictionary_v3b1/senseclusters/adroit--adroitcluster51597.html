adroit

adjective
clever and skillful
Pronunciation: / əˈdrɔɪt / (AmE), / əˈdrɔɪt / (BrE)
clever and skillful, especially in handling difficult situations or using one’s hands and mind effectively

Examples

An adroit negotiator
His adroit handling of the negotiations resulted in a successful agreement for both parties.
She gave an adroit performance during the debate, impressing everyone with her quick thinking.
With adroit fingers, the artist quickly sketched the intricate design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or action; lacking skill
  • antonym: inept - lacking skill or ability
  • synonym: skilful - having or showing skill
