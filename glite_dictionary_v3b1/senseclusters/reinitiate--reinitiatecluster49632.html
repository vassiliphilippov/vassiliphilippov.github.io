reinitiate

verb
to initiate again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪt / (BrE)
to perform an initiation ceremony for someone again, or to admit someone again into a group or society

Examples

To reinitiate a member
After several years away, the club decided to reinitiate him with a special ceremony.
The organization will reinitiate former members who wish to return and meet the requirements.
She was happy to reinitiate her membership after being absent for so long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: initiate - to begin or introduce someone into a group or activity
