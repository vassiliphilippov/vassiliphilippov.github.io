autolatry

noun
worship of oneself
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊˈlætri / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈlætri / (BrE)
worship of oneself; excessive self-admiration

Examples

Autolatry of oneself
He was accused of autolatry after publishing a glowing autobiography about himself.
Her constant self-praise bordered on autolatry, making her unpopular with her colleagues.
Some critics argue that celebrity culture encourages a kind of autolatry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narcissism - excessive self-love or self-centeredness
