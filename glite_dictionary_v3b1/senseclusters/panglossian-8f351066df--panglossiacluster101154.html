Panglossian

adjective
unrealistically optimistic
Pronunciation: / pænˈɡlɑːsiən / (AmE), / pænˈɡlɒsiən / (BrE)
extremely or unrealistically optimistic, especially in a way that ignores real problems or difficulties

Examples

Panglossian view
Despite the obvious challenges, her Panglossian attitude kept the team hopeful.
Critics accused the mayor of having a Panglossian view of the city's economic future.
It is not helpful to be Panglossian when serious issues need to be addressed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cynical - believing people are motivated by self-interest; distrustful of human sincerity
  • synonym: optimistic - expecting the best possible outcome
  • antonym: realistic - accepting things as they are and dealing with them practically
