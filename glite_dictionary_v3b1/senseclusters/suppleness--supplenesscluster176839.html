suppleness

noun
adaptability; mental or character flexibility
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌplənəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌplɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to adapt easily to new situations or changes, especially in mind or character

Examples

Suppleness of mind
A leader's suppleness of mind helps a team adapt to change.
Her suppleness allowed her to navigate complex negotiations with ease.
The company's suppleness in strategy was key to surviving the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the quality of bending easily or being easily adapted
  • antonym: rigidity - inability or unwillingness to change or adapt
