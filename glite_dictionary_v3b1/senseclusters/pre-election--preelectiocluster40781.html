pre-election

adjective
before an election
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
relating to the period or events occurring before an election

Examples

Pre-election debate
The candidates participated in several pre-election rallies to gain support.
Media coverage increased during the pre-election period as voters prepared to make their choices.
Many promises are made in pre-election speeches, but not all are kept afterward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electoral - relating to elections or voting
  • antonym: post-election - relating to the period after an election
