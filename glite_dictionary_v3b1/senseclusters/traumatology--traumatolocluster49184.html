traumatology

noun
medical field treating injuries and wounds
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɔməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌtrɔːməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the treatment of injuries and wounds, especially those resulting from accidents

Examples

The traumatology department
After the accident, he was referred to the traumatology department for immediate care.
Recent advances in traumatology have greatly improved survival rates for severe injuries.
She decided to specialize in traumatology after witnessing several serious accidents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthopedics - the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles
  • synonym: surgery - the branch of medical practice that treats injuries, diseases, and deformities by physical removal, repair, or readjustment of organs and tissues
