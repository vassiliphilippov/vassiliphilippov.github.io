abuelita

noun
affectionate term for older woman
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːbweɪˈliːtə / (AmE), / ˌabweɪˈliːtə / (BrE)
an affectionate term for an older woman, especially used like 'grandma' even if she is not a relative

Examples

Friendly abuelita next door
The children greeted their neighbor with a warm hug, calling her abuelita as a sign of respect.
Everyone in the community loved abuelita for her kindness and delicious homemade food.
Even though she was not related to them, they affectionately referred to her as abuelita.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandma - an informal term for grandmother
  • synonym: granny - an affectionate term for grandmother
