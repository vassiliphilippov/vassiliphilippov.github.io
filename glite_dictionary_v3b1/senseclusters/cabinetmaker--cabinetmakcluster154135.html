cabinetmaker

noun
person who makes fine furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbɪnətˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪnətˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make fine furniture and cabinets, especially by hand

Examples

A cabinetmaker makes furniture
Many antiques are the work of a master cabinetmaker from the nineteenth century.
She apprenticed with a skilled cabinetmaker to learn traditional woodworking techniques.
The cabinetmaker spent weeks crafting a custom bookshelf for their living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures
