grocery

noun
shop selling food and household items
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊsəri / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri / (BrE)
A shop or store where food and other household supplies are sold.

Examples

A local grocery

She walked to the grocery to pick up some fresh vegetables for dinner.
The new grocery in town offers a wide variety of organic products.
