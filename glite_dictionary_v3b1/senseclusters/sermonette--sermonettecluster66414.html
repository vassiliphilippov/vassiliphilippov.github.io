sermonette

noun
short sermon
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜːrməˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌsɜːməˈnɛt / (BrE)
a short or brief sermon, often delivered on radio or television

Examples

Radio sermonette
The pastor gave a brief sermonette at the end of the radio program.
Each morning, the station broadcasts a sermonette to inspire its listeners.
Before the main service, the minister shared a thoughtful sermonette with the congregation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homily - a short talk on a religious or moral topic
  • synonym: sermon - a speech on a religious or moral subject
