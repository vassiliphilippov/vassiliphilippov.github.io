kaputt

interjection
broken or ruined
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈpʊt / (AmE), / kəˈpʊt / (BrE)
an exclamation used to indicate that something is broken, ruined, or no longer working

Examples

Kaputt! It stopped
When the old radio stopped making any sound, he sighed and said, 'kaputt.'
After the computer froze for the third time, she declared it was 'kaputt.'
The toy car crashed into the wall and was completely 'kaputt.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: busted - broken or not working
  • antonym: fixed - repaired or working again
