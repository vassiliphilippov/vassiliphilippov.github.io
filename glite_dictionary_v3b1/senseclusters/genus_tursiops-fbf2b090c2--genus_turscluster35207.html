genus Tursiops

noun
a dolphin genus including bottlenose dolphins
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs tərˈsaɪ.ɑps / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs tɜːˈsaɪ.ɒps / (BrE)
A genus of marine mammals within the dolphin family (Delphinidae), including species such as the common bottlenose dolphin.

The genus Tursiops is well-known for including the highly intelligent bottlenose dolphins.
The genus Tursiops is widely distributed in warm and temperate seas around the world.
