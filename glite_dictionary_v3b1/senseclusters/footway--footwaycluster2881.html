footway

noun
pedestrian path
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌweɪ /; / ˈfʊt.weɪ / (BrE)
a path or walkway for people to walk on, especially alongside a road or for public use

Examples

A footway beside road
The new footway makes it safer for children to walk to school.
Cyclists are not allowed to ride on the footway in this area.
A row of trees lines the footway along the busy street.
The city council decided to repair the damaged footway near the school.
Joggers often use the footway along the river for their morning exercise.
Please keep your bicycle off the footway to avoid disturbing pedestrians.
The old footway runs alongside the river and is popular with morning joggers.
Local residents requested repairs to the damaged footway near the school.
Maps of the area show every public footway connecting the villages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: footpath - a narrow path for people to walk on, especially in the countryside
  • synonym: pavement - a path for pedestrians, especially in British English
  • antonym: roadway - the part of a road used by vehicles
  • synonym: sidewalk - a paved path for pedestrians beside a road
  • synonym: walkway - a path for walking, often raised or paved
