morena

noun
dark-haired woman
9.3
Pronunciation: / moʊˈreɪnə / (AmE), / məˈreɪnə / (BrE)
a woman with dark hair and skin, especially in Spanish-speaking cultures

Examples

A beautiful morena
The artist painted a portrait of a striking morena with deep brown eyes.
In many songs, the word morena is used to describe a beautiful woman with dark features.
She was proud of her heritage as a morena in her community.
