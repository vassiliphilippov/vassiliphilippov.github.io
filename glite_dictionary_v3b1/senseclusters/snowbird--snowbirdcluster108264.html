snowbird

noun
small North American finch seen in winter
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a small bird that is commonly seen in winter, especially in northern or Arctic regions
