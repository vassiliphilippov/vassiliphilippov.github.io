ethnomasochistic attitude

noun
self-hatred for one's own ethnicity
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊ.mæˈsɑː.kɪstɪk ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊ.mæˈsɒ.kɪstɪk ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an attitude of self-hatred or disdain toward one's own ethnicity

Examples

An ethnomasochistic attitude problem
Educational programs strive to address issues without encouraging an ethnomasochistic attitude among students.
Some commentators accused the artist of promoting an ethnomasochistic attitude in his work.
The rise of an ethnomasochistic attitude among certain intellectuals sparked heated debates about cultural identity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ethnocentrism - belief in the superiority of one's own ethnic group
  • synonym: self-hatred - intense dislike or loathing of oneself
