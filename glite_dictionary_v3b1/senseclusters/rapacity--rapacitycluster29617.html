rapacity

noun
extreme greed or gluttony
9.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / rəˈpæsəti / (AmE), / rəˈpæsɪti /; / rəˈpæsəti / (BrE)
extreme greed for wealth, food, or possessions; the quality of being excessively grasping or voracious

Examples

Uncontrolled rapacity for money
Her rapacity for sweets was well known among her friends.
The rapacity of the corrupt officials knew no limits.
Unchecked rapacity led to the depletion of natural resources in the region.
Historians condemned the conqueror's rapacity as he stripped the land of all its riches.
The rapacity of the corporation led to widespread criticism and legal action.
Unchecked rapacity can undermine trust and stability in any society.
