odour of sanctity

noun
aura of holiness, moral purity
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdər əv ˈsæŋktəti / (AmE), / ˈəʊdə əv ˈsæŋktɪti / (BrE)
an aura or reputation of holiness and moral purity

Examples

An odour of sanctity
Many believed the philanthropist's work was marked by an odour of sanctity rare in public life.
The aging monk carried an odour of sanctity that inspired respect throughout the monastery.
The author's biography was written with such reverence, it gave her achievements an odour of sanctity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piety - devoutness, reverence for the divine
  • synonym: sanctity - holiness, the state of being sacred
