Goodyear

proper noun
surname; inventor's name
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdˌjɪə / (BrE)
a family name used as a surname, notably associated with Charles Goodyear, the inventor of vulcanized rubber

Examples

Charles Goodyear
Charles Goodyear is remembered for inventing the process of vulcanizing rubber.
The Goodyear family played an important role in American industrial history.
Many people recognize the name Goodyear from the famous tire company.
