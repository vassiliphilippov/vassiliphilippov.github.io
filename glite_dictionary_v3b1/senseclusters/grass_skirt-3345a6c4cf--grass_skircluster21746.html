grass skirt

noun
skirt made from long grass blades
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɡræs skɝt / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs skɜːt / (BrE)
A skirt made from long blades of grass, traditionally worn in some Pacific Island cultures as part of ceremonial dress.

Examples

A colorful grass skirt
At the luau, each dancer donned a colorful grass skirt and flower lei.
She bought a grass skirt and a coconut bra for the tropical-themed party.
The children wore grass skirts during the school performance about Polynesian culture.
