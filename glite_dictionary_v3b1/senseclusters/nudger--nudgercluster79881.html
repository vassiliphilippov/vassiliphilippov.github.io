nudger

noun
someone who nudges others
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌdʒər / (AmE), / ˈnʌdʒə / (BrE)
a person who nudges someone else, typically by giving a gentle push to get their attention or encourage an action

Examples

A gentle nudger
He can be a persistent nudger if you hesitate to make a decision.
She acted as a gentle nudger to remind the group of their goals.
The alarm clock is my daily nudger to get out of bed on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nudge - to push someone or something gently, especially with your elbow to attract their attention
