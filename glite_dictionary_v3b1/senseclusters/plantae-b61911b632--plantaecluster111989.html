Plantae

noun
kingdom including all plants
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.tiː / (AmE), / ˈplæn.tiː / (BrE)
The taxonomic kingdom that includes all living and extinct plants.

Examples

The Plantae kingdom
Ferns, conifers, and grasses are all members of Plantae.
Most green organisms that photosynthesize belong to the Plantae kingdom.
The kingdom Plantae includes a vast diversity of species, from mosses to flowering trees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Animalia - the taxonomic kingdom comprising all animals
