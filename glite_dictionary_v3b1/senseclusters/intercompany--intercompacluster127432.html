intercompany

noun
transaction between companies
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkˈʌmpəni / (BrE)
a transaction, transfer, or adjustment that occurs between two companies, especially within the same corporate group

Examples

Intercompany transaction
The finance team reviewed all intercompany transactions to ensure accurate consolidation of accounts.
An intercompany loan was arranged to support the new subsidiary's operations.
Auditors pay close attention to intercompany balances during the annual review.

Related Terms

  • antonym: external - involving parties outside a company or group
  • synonym: intragroup - occurring within a group of related companies
