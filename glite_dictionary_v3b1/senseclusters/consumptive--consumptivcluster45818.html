consumptive

adjective
tending to consume or use up
Pronunciation: / kənˈsʌmptɪv / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmptɪv / (BrE)
Tending to consume or use resources, often wastefully or destructively.

Examples

Consumptive use
Fires that spread rapidly can have a highly consumptive effect on forests.
Many modern appliances are surprisingly consumptive of electricity.
The company's consumptive approach to resources raised concerns among environmentalists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wasteful - using something in a careless or unnecessary way
