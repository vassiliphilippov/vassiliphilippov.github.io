dika nut

noun
edible seed of wild mango
Pronunciation: / ˈdiː.kə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈdiː.kə nʌt / (BrE)
the edible, oil-rich seed of the wild mango tree, used as food and for extracting oil

Examples

Ground dika nuts
She added ground dika nuts to the soup to thicken its consistency.
The locals harvested dika nuts from the forest to press for oil.
The market sells bags of roasted dika nuts as a popular snack.
