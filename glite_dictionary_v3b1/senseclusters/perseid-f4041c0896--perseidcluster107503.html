Perseid

noun
descendant of Perseus
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrsiˌɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsiːɪd / (BrE)
a descendant or member of the family of the mythical Greek hero Perseus

Examples

A royal Perseid
According to legend, the king claimed he was a Perseid to strengthen his right to rule.
The ancient family traced their lineage back to the Perseid bloodline.
In the story, she proudly declared herself a Perseid, a descendant of Perseus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Perseus - a hero in Greek mythology, slayer of Medusa
