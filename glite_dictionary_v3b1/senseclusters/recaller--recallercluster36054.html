recaller

noun
product withdrawal initiator
7.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔlər / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːlə / (BrE)
a person or organization that initiates the withdrawal of a product from the market due to safety or quality concerns

Examples

Product recaller
The recaller issued a public notice to inform customers about the faulty batch of products.
As the official recaller, the company was responsible for organizing the return of the unsafe toys.
Government agencies sometimes act as the recaller when imported goods fail safety tests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a person or company that supplies goods to stores or businesses
  • synonym: manufacturer - a company that makes products for sale
