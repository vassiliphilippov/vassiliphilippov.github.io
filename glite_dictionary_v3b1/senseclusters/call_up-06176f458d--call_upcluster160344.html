call up

verb
recall or remember; bring to mind
3.2 1.09/m
Pronunciation: / k…îl  åp / (AmE), / k…îÀêl  åp / (BrE)
to cause to recall or bring to mind a memory or piece of information; to remember something.

Examples

To call up memories
Hearing that song always calls up memories of my childhood.
She called up the image of her grandmother's smile in her mind.
Try to call up the details of the meeting for your report.
