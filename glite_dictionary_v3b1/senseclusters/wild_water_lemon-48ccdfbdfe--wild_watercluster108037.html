wild water lemon

noun
a tropical American passion flower
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈwɔtɚ ˈlɛmən / (AmE), / waɪld ˈwɔːtə ˈlɛmən / (BrE)
A tropical American passion flower (Passiflora species) with finely divided bracts; emits a strong odor when the stems are crushed.

Examples

The wild water lemon plant
Botanists found wild water lemon thriving in the humid tropical forest.

When crushed, the stem of wild water lemon releases a pungent odor.
