densitometric

adjective
measuring density
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛnsɪtəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɛnsɪtəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of density, especially of bone or tissue, in medical or scientific contexts

Examples

Densitometric bone scan
A densitometric analysis can help doctors assess the risk of osteoporosis in patients.
The laboratory uses densitometric techniques to evaluate the mineral content of bones.
Recent advances in densitometric equipment have improved the accuracy of bone density measurements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: densitometry - the measurement of density, especially in medical imaging
