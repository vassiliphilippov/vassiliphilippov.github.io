tracker

noun
wearable activity monitor
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækər / (AmE), / ˈtrækə / (BrE)
a small electronic device or wearable app that records and monitors physical activity or health metrics such as steps, heart rate, or sleep.

Examples

A fitness tracker
She wore a tracker to monitor her daily steps and sleep.
Many runners compare different trackers to find the most accurate heart-rate readings.
My tracker alerted me when my resting heart rate rose unexpectedly last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fitness tracker - a device or app specifically designed to monitor physical activity and health metrics
  • synonym: wearable - an electronic device worn on the body that performs computing or monitoring functions
