centerless

adjective
without a center
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɚləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntələs / (BrE)
having no central authority, organization, or main focus; decentralized or lacking a single focal point

Examples

Centerless organization
The organization adopted a centerless structure to encourage innovation and local decision-making.
A centerless network can be more resilient because it does not rely on a single point of control.
Their movement remained centerless, allowing each group to pursue its own goals independently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralized - controlled by a single central authority
  • synonym: decentralized - not controlled by a single central authority
