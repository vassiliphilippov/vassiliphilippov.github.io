frangibility

noun
the quality of being easily broken
Pronunciation: / ˌfrændʒəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌfrændʒɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being easily broken, damaged, or destroyed.
