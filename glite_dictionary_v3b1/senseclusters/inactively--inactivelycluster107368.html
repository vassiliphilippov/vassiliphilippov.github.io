inactively

adverb
without activity
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæktɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves little or no activity or movement

Examples

Sit inactively by window
He sat inactively by the window, watching the rain fall.
The machinery remained inactively in the corner of the workshop for months.
After her surgery, she spent several weeks inactively recovering at home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: actively - in a way that involves taking action or being busy
  • synonym: passively - in a way that does not take action or initiative
