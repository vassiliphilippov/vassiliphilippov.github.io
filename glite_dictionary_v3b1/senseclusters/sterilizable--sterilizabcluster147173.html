sterilizable

adjective
can be sterilized
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr.ə.laɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be made completely free from bacteria or other living microorganisms

Examples

Sterilizable surgical tools
All surgical tools must be sterilizable to ensure patient safety during operations.
The laboratory uses only sterilizable glassware to prevent contamination of experiments.
Reusable bottles should be sterilizable to maintain hygiene standards in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfectable - able to be cleaned to remove most germs
