prototypal

adjective
representing the original or typical example
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈtaɪpəl / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈtaɪpəl / (BrE)
representing or constituting an original type after which other similar things are patterned; original or typical of its kind

Examples

Prototypal example
Her reaction was prototypal of the community's response to the crisis.
His leadership style was considered prototypal for future generations of CEOs.
The museum features a prototypal example of modernist architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archetypal - very typical of a certain kind or representing an original model
