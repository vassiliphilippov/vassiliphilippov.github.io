sanitation

noun
the act of keeping places clean and hygienic
5.4 2.4
Pronunciation: /sæn.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sæn.ə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sæn.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sæn.ə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process or state of keeping places clean and hygienic to prevent disease, usually by removing waste and promoting health

Examples

Public sanitation system
After the flood, workers focused on sanitation to ensure the water was safe to drink.
Proper sanitation is essential to prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
The lack of adequate sanitation in the area led to a public health crisis.
Access to proper sanitation is essential for preventing the spread of diseases.
Poor sanitation in the area led to a serious health crisis.
The city invested millions in improving water supply and sanitation facilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hygiene - practices to maintain health and prevent disease
