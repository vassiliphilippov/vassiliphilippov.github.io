overdeveloped

adjective
excessively developed
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərdɪˈvɛləpt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvədɪˈvɛləpt / (BrE)
having grown or been developed too much, often in a way that is excessive or unnatural

Examples

Overdeveloped muscles
The overdeveloped muscles of the athlete made him look unnatural.
Many people complain that the overdeveloped coastline has lost its natural beauty.
Her sense of responsibility was so overdeveloped that she struggled to relax.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excessive - more than is necessary or normal
  • antonym: underdeveloped - not developed enough or lacking growth
