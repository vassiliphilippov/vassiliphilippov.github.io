ghatti gum

noun
Indian gum from dhawa tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑti ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈɡati ɡʌm / (BrE)
an Indian gum obtained from the dhawa tree, used as a substitute for gum arabic in food and industry

Examples

Ghatti gum from tree
Many manufacturers use ghatti gum as a less expensive alternative to gum arabic in their products.
Supplies of ghatti gum are exported from India to various parts of the world for industrial use.
The thickening properties of ghatti gum make it valuable in processed foods and beverages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gum arabic - a gum exuded by some acacia trees, used in food and industry
