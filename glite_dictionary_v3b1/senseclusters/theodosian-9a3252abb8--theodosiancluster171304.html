Theodosian

noun
supporter of Theodosius
Pronunciation: / θiːəˈdoʊziən / (AmE), / θiːəˈdəʊziən / (BrE)
a person who lived under or supported the rule, policies, or dynasty of the Roman emperor Theodosius

Examples

A Theodosian supporter
The Theodosian influence on Roman law can still be seen in some modern legal systems.
As a proud Theodosian, he defended the emperor's decisions in public debates.
Many Theodosians supported the spread of Christianity throughout the empire.
