producible

adjective
able to be made
3.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈduːsəbəl / (AmE), / prəˈdjuːsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be made, created, or manufactured

Examples

A producible product
The new alloy is highly producible using current manufacturing techniques.
We need to find a more producible solution to meet the growing demand.
This design is not only efficient but also easily producible at scale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manufacturable - able to be manufactured or produced
  • antonym: unproducible - not able to be made or created
