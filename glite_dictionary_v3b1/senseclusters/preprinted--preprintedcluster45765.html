preprinted

adjective
already printed
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈprɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈprɪntɪd / (BrE)
already printed on paper or a form before being used or filled in

Examples

Preprinted labels
Please use the preprinted labels to address all outgoing packages.
The contract comes with preprinted terms that cannot be changed.
She filled out her information on a preprinted application form.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blank - not filled in or printed
  • synonym: printed - produced by printing
