shock and awe

noun
use of overwhelming force to impress or intimidate
8.7 5.1
Pronunciation: / ʃɑk ənd ɔ / (AmE), / ʃɒk ənd ɔː / (BrE)
a strategy or effect characterized by the use of overwhelming force or spectacular action intended to astonish, intimidate, or impress

Examples

Shock and awe strategy
The advertising team's shock and awe approach captured the attention of the entire industry.
The general believed a display of shock and awe would force the enemy to surrender quickly.
The government launched a shock and awe campaign to win public support for the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intimidate - to frighten or overawe, especially in order to make someone do what one wants
  • synonym: overwhelm - to defeat or affect strongly; to overpower
