laughed dweebily

verb
laughed in a socially awkward way
Pronunciation: / læft ˈdwiːbɪli / (AmE), / lɑːft ˈdwiːbɪli / (BrE)
Laughed in a way that is socially awkward or nerdy, often betraying a lack of confidence or grace.

Examples

To laugh dweebily nervously
He laughed dweebily at the professor's pun, drawing curious looks from the rest of the class.
They laughed dweebily after their own joke, highlighting their uncomfortable manner.
Whenever she gets nervous, she laughed dweebily, making everyone feel a bit awkward.
