interiorly

adverb
inwardly; privately
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɪriərli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriəli / (BrE)
inwardly or in one's inner thoughts or feelings; privately within oneself.

Examples

Interiorly feels worried
Although he appeared calm, he was interiorly struggling with anxiety.
She kept her disappointment interiorly and smiled at the good news.
He was interiorly proud of his achievements, even if he never spoke about them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inwardly - in one's mind or inner feelings
  • antonym: outwardly - in a way that is seen or shown to others
