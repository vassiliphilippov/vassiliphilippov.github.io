Connaraceae

noun
a family of tropical woody plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˌnærəˈsiːi /; / kəˌnɛr.əˈsiː.i / (AmE), / kɒˌnærəˈsiːi /; / kɒn.əˈreɪ.siː.iː / (BrE)
a family of mostly tropical climbing shrubs or small trees, closely related to Leguminosae, found mainly in Africa and Asia
