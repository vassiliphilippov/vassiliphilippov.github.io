encephalitis

noun
inflammation of the brain (medical)
7.5 6.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A serious condition in which the brain becomes inflamed, usually as a result of a viral infection.

Examples

Viral encephalitis
Doctors warned that encephalitis can be life-threatening if not treated promptly.
The patient was diagnosed with encephalitis after experiencing severe headaches and confusion.
Treatment for viral encephalitis often involves antiviral medications and supportive care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meningitis - inflammation of the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
