circuitousness

noun
the quality of being indirect or roundabout
Pronunciation: / sərˈkjuːɾiəsnəs / (AmE), / səˈkjuːɪtəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being roundabout, indirect, or not straightforward.

Examples

The circuitousness of route
Negotiations were slowed by the circuitousness of the bureaucratic process.
She admired the novel's plot for its intricate circuitousness rather than for any straightforward resolution.
The circuitousness of his explanation confused the entire audience.
