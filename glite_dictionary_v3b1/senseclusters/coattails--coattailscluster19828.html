coattails

noun
influence by association
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtˌteɪlz / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtˌteɪlz / (BrE)
the influence or advantage gained by being associated with a successful or powerful person

Examples

On the coattails of success
He won the election by riding the coattails of a popular candidate.
Some employees advance their careers by staying on the coattails of influential managers.
She realized she could not rely on his coattails forever and needed to prove herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independence - the state of not relying on others
  • synonym: influence - the power to affect others or events
