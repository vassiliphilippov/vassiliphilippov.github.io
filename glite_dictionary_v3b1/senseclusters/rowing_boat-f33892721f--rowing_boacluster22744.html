rowing boat

noun
small boat propelled by oars
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊɪŋ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊɪŋ bəʊt / (BrE)
A small boat that is propelled using oars, especially for recreation or sport.

Examples
