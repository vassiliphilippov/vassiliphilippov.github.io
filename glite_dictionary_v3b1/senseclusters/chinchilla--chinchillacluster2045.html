chinchilla

noun
expensive silvery-grey fur of a chinchilla
Pronunciation: / tʃɪnˈtʃɪlə / (AmE), / tʃɪnˈtʃɪlə / (BrE)
the soft, expensive, silvery-grey fur obtained from the chinchilla, often used to make coats and other garments

Examples

Chinchilla fur coat
She wore a luxurious chinchilla coat to the gala.
The designer's new collection features chinchilla trims on winter jackets.
Vintage chinchilla is highly prized for its extraordinary softness and color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fur - the short, fine, soft hair of certain animals used for garments
