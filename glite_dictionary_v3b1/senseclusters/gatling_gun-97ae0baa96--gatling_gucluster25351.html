Gatling gun

noun
early multi-barreled machine gun
Pronunciation: / ˈɡætlɪŋ ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡatlɪŋ ɡʌn / (BrE)
an early type of machine gun with several barrels arranged in a circle, which fired in sequence as they rotated

Examples

A Gatling gun machine
During the demonstration, the museum curator showed how a Gatling gun operated using a hand crank.
Soldiers used a Gatling gun to defend the fort against a much larger attacking force.
The Gatling gun revolutionized warfare in the late nineteenth century by greatly increasing the rate of fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machine gun - automatic gun that fires bullets rapidly
