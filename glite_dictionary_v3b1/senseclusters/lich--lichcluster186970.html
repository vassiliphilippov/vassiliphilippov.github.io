lich

noun
undead sorcerer
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɪtʃ / (AmE), / lɪtʃ / (BrE)
an undead sorcerer or wizard in fantasy fiction, usually immortal and possessing powerful magic

Examples

Evil lich wizard
The ancient lich guarded the treasure with powerful spells and undead minions.
Defeating a lich often requires destroying its phylactery to end its immortality.
In the game, the final boss was a lich who commanded an army of skeletons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necromancer - a wizard who practices magic involving the dead
  • synonym: vampire - an undead creature that feeds on the blood of the living
  • degree lower: zombie - a reanimated corpse in folklore and fiction
