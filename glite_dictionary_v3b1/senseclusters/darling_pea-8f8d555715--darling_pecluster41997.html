darling pea

noun
Australian poisonous plant (Swainsona genus)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrlɪŋ pi / (AmE), / ˈdɑːlɪŋ piː / (BrE)
either of two species of Australian plants of the genus Swainsona, known for being poisonous to sheep

Examples

The darling pea plant
After the sheep grazed on darling pea, several of them became ill.
Farmers in Australia are cautious of the darling pea, as it can be toxic to livestock.
The striking flowers of the darling pea often conceal its dangerous effects on animals.
