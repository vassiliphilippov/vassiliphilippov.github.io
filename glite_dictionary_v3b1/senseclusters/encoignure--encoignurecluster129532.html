encoignure

noun
corner recess
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ˈkwɑːn.jʊr / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈkwɑːn.jʊə / (BrE)
a small space or recess formed by the meeting of two walls, especially in the corner of a room

Bookshelves fitted perfectly into the encoignure, creating a cozy reading nook.
