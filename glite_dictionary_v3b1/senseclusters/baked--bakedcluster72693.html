baked

adjective
intoxicated; high (slang)
Pronunciation: / beɪkt / (AmE), / beɪkt / (BrE)
informal: extremely intoxicated or high on drugs, especially cannabis.

Examples

He is baked
After the party, he was baked and could hardly stand.
She admitted she had gotten baked and missed the early meeting the next day.
By midnight, several students appeared baked and were laughing at nothing at all.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunk - intoxicated by alcohol; similar in meaning but usually alcohol-specific
  • synonym: high - under the influence of drugs, experiencing altered perception
  • synonym: stoned - intoxicated or dazed from using drugs, especially cannabis
