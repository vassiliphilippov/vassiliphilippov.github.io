ficelle

noun
thin French bread
Pronunciation: / fiˈsɛl / (AmE), / fiˈsɛl / (BrE)
a long, thin French bread that is similar to a baguette but narrower

Examples

A ficelle loaf
At the bakery, she ordered a ficelle to go with her cheese and wine.
The restaurant served a warm ficelle alongside the soup as a starter.
He enjoyed a fresh ficelle with butter for breakfast every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baguette - a long, thin loaf of French bread
  • synonym: breadstick - a thin, crisp stick of bread
