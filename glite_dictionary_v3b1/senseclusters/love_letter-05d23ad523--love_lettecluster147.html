love letter

noun
a letter expressing romantic affection
Pronunciation: / lʌv ˈlɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / lʌv ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a written message expressing romantic affection to someone

Examples

A love letter to someone
He was nervous as he slipped the love letter into her locker after class.
She wrote a heartfelt love letter to her boyfriend while he was away at college.
The box contained dozens of old love letters exchanged by her grandparents.
