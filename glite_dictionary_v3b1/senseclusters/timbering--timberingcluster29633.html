timbering

noun
supporting with beams
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or method of supporting a structure, especially in mining or construction, with wooden beams or timbers

Examples

Mine timbering
Timbering is essential for keeping tunnels safe during mining.
The workers completed the timbering of the shaft before excavation began.
Proper timbering prevents cave-ins and ensures the stability of underground passages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bracing - the use of supports to strengthen or steady a structure
  • synonym: shoring - the act of supporting a structure with props or supports
