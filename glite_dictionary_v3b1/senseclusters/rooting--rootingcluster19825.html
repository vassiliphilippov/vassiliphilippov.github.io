rooting

noun

(computing) the act or process of obtaining root or superuser privileges on a device or system, typically to bypass restrictions or install custom software.

Examples

Rooting a phone
Many users avoid voiding warranties, but some still try rooting their phones to access advanced settings.
After rooting the router, she installed custom firmware that improved network performance and features.
Cybersecurity courses often explain rooting as gaining superuser privileges for repair, testing, or customization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jailbreak - the process of removing software restrictions on a device (especially iOS); similar concept for certain platforms.
  • synonym: root - to obtain root or superuser access on a device; to gain highest-level privileges.
