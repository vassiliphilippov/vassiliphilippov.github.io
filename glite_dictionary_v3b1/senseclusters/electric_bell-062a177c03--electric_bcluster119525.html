electric bell

noun
bell powered by electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk bɛl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk bɛl / (BrE)
A bell that rings when activated by the magnetic effect of an electric current.

Examples

An electric bell rings
An electric bell is often found in traditional alarm systems.
The old school building still uses an electric bell to signal the end of each period.
When the button is pressed, an electric bell rings in the next room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doorbell - a bell in a building, rung usually by visitors outside
