anywhen

noun
unspecified time
Pronunciation: / ˈɛniˌwɛn / (AmE), / ˈɛniˌwɛn / (BrE)
an unspecified or indeterminate time; any moment or period without a fixed point.

Examples

At anywhen
The meeting could be scheduled for anywhen, so please keep your calendar open.
He said he might visit anywhen this month, but gave no specific date.
You can submit your application at anywhen before the deadline.
