synchrotron

noun
a type of particle accelerator
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋ.krəˌtrɑn / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.krəˌtrɒn / (BrE)
A type of cyclotron in which charged particles are accelerated by a constant-frequency electric field as their orbital path is increased by a varying magnetic field.

Examples

A synchrotron accelerator
Many modern particle experiments rely on the precise control of a synchrotron.
Scientists used a synchrotron to examine the molecular structure of the protein.
The new research facility houses a powerful synchrotron used for advanced materials analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyclotron - a type of particle accelerator using a constant magnetic field and a varying electric field
