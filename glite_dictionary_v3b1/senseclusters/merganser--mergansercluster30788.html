merganser

noun
a type of crested fish-eating diving duck
9.9
Pronunciation: / mərˈɡæn.zər / (AmE), / mɜːˈɡæn.zə / (BrE)
a large diving duck with a crested head and a slender, hooked bill with serrated edges, known for eating fish

Examples

A merganser duck
A pair of mergansers glided silently along the surface of the lake, searching for fish.
The wildlife photographer captured several images of mergansers diving for their lunch.
We spotted a male merganser with its distinctive crested head and narrow bill.
