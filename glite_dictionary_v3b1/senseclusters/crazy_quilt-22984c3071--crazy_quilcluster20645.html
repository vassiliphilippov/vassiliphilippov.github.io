crazy quilt

noun
patchwork quilt with no set design
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪzi kwɪlt / (AmE), / ˈkreɪzi kwɪlt / (BrE)
a patchwork quilt made of irregular, varied pieces of fabric; also used to describe something made up of many different or mismatched parts

Examples

A crazy quilt pattern
Her grandmother made a beautiful crazy quilt from scraps of old dresses.
Learning to sew a crazy quilt requires creativity rather than following patterns.
The crazy quilt on the bed caught everyone's attention with its vibrant mix of fabrics.
Her memories of childhood formed a crazy quilt of images and emotions.
The artist filled the canvas with a crazy quilt of vibrant colors and shapes.
The city's architecture is a crazy quilt of historic and modern styles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patchwork - needlework made by sewing together pieces of fabric in different designs
