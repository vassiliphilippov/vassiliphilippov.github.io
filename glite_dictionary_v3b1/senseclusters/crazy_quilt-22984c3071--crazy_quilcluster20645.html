crazy quilt

noun
patchwork quilt with no set design
6.5 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪzi kwɪlt / (AmE), / ˈkreɪzi kwɪlt / (BrE)
a patchwork quilt made of irregular, varied pieces of fabric; also used to describe something made up of many different or mismatched parts
