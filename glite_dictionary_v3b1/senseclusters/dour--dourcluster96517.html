dour

adjective
stern or unfriendly in manner or appearance
Pronunciation: / daʊər /; / daʊr / (AmE), / dʊə, daʊə /; / ˈdʊə ˈdaʊə /; / daʊə / (BrE)
serious, unfriendly, or gloomy in manner or appearance; stern and unyielding
