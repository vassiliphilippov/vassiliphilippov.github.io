Cydonian

adjective
of Cydonia
Pronunciation: / saɪˈdoʊniən / (AmE), / saɪˈdəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to Cydonia, either an ancient city in Crete or a region on the planet Mars

Examples

Cydonian landscape on Mars
The Cydonian region on Mars is famous for the so-called 'Face on Mars' formation.
Archaeologists discovered ancient artifacts in the Cydonian ruins on Crete.
Scientists are analyzing the soil composition of the Cydonian plains using data from Mars rovers.
