grandiosity

noun
exaggerated self-importance
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrændiˈɑːsəti / (AmE), / ˌɡrændiˈɒsəti / (BrE)
an attitude or behavior of believing oneself to be more important, powerful, or talented than others

Examples

Personal grandiosity
His grandiosity made it difficult for him to accept criticism from his colleagues.
The politician's grandiosity was evident in every speech he gave.
She was criticized for her grandiosity and lack of empathy toward others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - an attitude of superiority and self-importance
  • antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's own importance
