rubberneck

verb
stare curiously or intrusively
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌ.bər.nek/ (AmE), /ˈrʌ.bə.nek/ (BrE)
to stare or look with great curiosity, especially at accidents or unusual events, often causing delay or distraction

Examples

To rubberneck at crashes
Drivers tend to rubberneck during major accidents, causing further traffic delays.
Please don't rubberneck when passing the construction site; it's unsafe.
Several tourists stopped to rubberneck at the car crash by the roadside.
Drivers slowed down to rubberneck at the scene of the accident, causing a long traffic jam.
Tourists often rubberneck when they see celebrities walking down the street.
The crowd began to rubberneck as the fire trucks arrived at the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gawk - to stare openly and stupidly
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention
  • synonym: ogle - to stare at in a lecherous manner
