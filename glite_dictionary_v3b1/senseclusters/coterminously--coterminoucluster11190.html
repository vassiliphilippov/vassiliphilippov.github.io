coterminously

adverb
in a coterminous manner; sharing boundaries or limits
7.1
Pronunciation: / koʊˈtɜːrmɪnəsli / (AmE), / kəʊˈtɜːmɪnəsli / (BrE)
In a manner where boundaries or limits are shared or coincide; at the same time or in the same place.

Examples

Run coterminously together
The river and the border run coterminously for several miles.
The two districts developed coterminously, sharing resources and infrastructure.
Their tenures in office ended coterminously, marking a significant change in leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concurrently - at the same time
