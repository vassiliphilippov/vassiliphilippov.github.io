glary

noun
blinding brightness
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgle.riː/ /; / /ˈgleə.riː/ (AmE), /ˈgleə.riː/ /; / /ˈgle.riː/ (BrE)
a bright, blinding light or strong reflected brightness that is uncomfortable or hurts the eyes

Examples

A glary from headlights
The glary from the car's headlights made it difficult to see the road ahead.
She shielded her eyes from the glary reflecting off the snow.
A sudden glary from the puddle made her squint and blink for several seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzle - a bright light that makes it hard to see
  • synonym: glare - a strong, harsh, and dazzling light
  • antonym: shade - an area where direct light is blocked
