stargazing

noun
observing stars in the night sky
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of observing the stars in the night sky, often as a hobby or for scientific study

Examples

Stargazing at night
Every summer, the family goes stargazing in the mountains to enjoy the clear night sky.
Her passion for stargazing led her to join the local astronomy club.
The desert is one of the best places for stargazing due to its minimal light pollution.
