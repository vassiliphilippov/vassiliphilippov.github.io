chorusing

noun
group singing
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of singing or making sounds together as a group, especially in unison

Examples

Frogs chorusing together
The frogs' chorusing filled the night air after the rain.
Morning birds greeted the sunrise with joyful chorusing in the trees.
The children enjoyed chorusing their favorite song together at the assembly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: singing - the act of producing musical tones with the voice
  • antonym: solo - a performance by a single person
