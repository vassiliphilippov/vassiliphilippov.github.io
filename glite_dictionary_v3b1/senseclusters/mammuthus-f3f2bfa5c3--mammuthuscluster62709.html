Mammuthus

noun
genus of extinct mammoths
Pronunciation: / məˈmjuːθəs / (AmE), / məˈmjuːθəs / (BrE)
a genus of extinct large, hairy elephants known as mammoths, which lived during the Pleistocene epoch

Examples

Mammuthus remains
Many fossils of Mammuthus have been found in the Siberian permafrost.
Scientists are studying DNA extracted from Mammuthus remains to learn more about their extinction.
The extinct genus Mammuthus includes several species of mammoths that once roamed the Northern Hemisphere.
