semi-sheer

adjective
partly transparent
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈʃɪr / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈʃɪə / (BrE)
partly transparent, allowing some light to pass through but not completely clear

Examples

A semi-sheer blouse
She wore a semi-sheer blouse over her tank top for a stylish layered look.
The curtains were semi-sheer, letting in soft sunlight while still providing some privacy.
He chose a semi-sheer fabric for the dress to create an elegant, delicate effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opaque - not letting any light pass through
  • degree higher: sheer - completely or very nearly transparent
  • synonym: translucent - allowing light to pass through but not detailed shapes
