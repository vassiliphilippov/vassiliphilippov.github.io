wring out

verb
extract liquid by twisting or squeezing
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ aʊt /; / ɹɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / rɪŋ aʊt /; / ɹɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
to remove liquid from something (especially cloth) by twisting or squeezing it tightly

Examples

She wrung out the dishcloth and wiped down the counter.
