fumy

adjective
full of fumes
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːmi / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmi / (BrE)
full of fumes, smoke, or vapor; having a smoky or vaporous quality

Examples

Fumy air
The fumy atmosphere in the factory made it hard to breathe.
After the fire, the house was left with a fumy smell that lingered for days.
He opened the window to let the fumy air escape from the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free from smoke, mist, or anything that makes vision difficult
  • synonym: misty - full of mist or fine droplets in the air
  • synonym: smoky - filled with or smelling of smoke
