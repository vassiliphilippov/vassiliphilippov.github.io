Hanseatic

adjective
of Hanseatic League
Pronunciation: / ˌhænsiˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˌhænsiˈætɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Hanseatic League, a medieval association of trading cities in northern Europe

Examples

Hanseatic League city
The Hanseatic merchants controlled much of the trade in the Baltic Sea region.
Many Hanseatic cities still celebrate their rich trading history with annual festivals.
The architecture in this port reflects its Hanseatic heritage from the Middle Ages.
