accord

noun
formal agreement between nations
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔrd / (AmE), / əˈkɔːd / (BrE)
a formal written agreement between two nations or political entities

Examples

Peace accord
After months of negotiations, an accord was finally reached between the warring nations.
The Paris Accord on climate change set ambitious environmental goals for all signatories.
The leaders of the two countries signed an historic accord to promote peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement between parties
  • antonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one
  • synonym: treaty - a formally concluded and ratified agreement between countries
