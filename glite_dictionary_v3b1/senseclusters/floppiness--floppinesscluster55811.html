floppiness

noun
soft, loose quality
4.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfl…ëÀêpin…ôs / (AmE), / Ààfl…ípin…ôs / (BrE)
the quality of being soft, loose, or lacking firmness or stiffness

Examples

Toy floppiness
The floppiness of the stuffed animal made it easy for children to hug.
He noticed the floppiness in the old mattress as soon as he lay down.
The dog's ears had a charming floppiness that made everyone smile.

Synonyms

  • limpness
    4.1 0.07/m
    limpness is a lack of firmness often after being wet or tired, while floppiness is general softness or loose structure.
  • flabbiness
    5.0 0.12/m
    flabbiness describes soft, loose body tissue from weak muscles, while floppiness means general lack of firmness or stiffness.
  • squishiness
    5.3 0.00/m
    squishiness feels soft and easily pressed like a sponge, while floppiness means lacking firmness or stiffness in form.
  • sagginess
    6.7 0.00/m
    sagginess is droopy and hangs down from lost support, while floppiness is a broader lack of firmness or stiffness.
  • slackness
    4.9 0.02/m
    slackness means being loose because tension is low, while floppiness means softness or lack of stiffness in material or shape.
