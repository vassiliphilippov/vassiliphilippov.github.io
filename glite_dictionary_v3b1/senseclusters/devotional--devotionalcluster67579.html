devotional

noun
short religious service
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvoʊʃənəl / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊʃənəl / (BrE)
a short religious service or exercise, especially one for private worship

Examples

Morning devotional service
A devotional was held before the main ceremony to offer prayers.
He attends a morning devotional at his church every day before work.
The students began their day with a brief devotional led by the principal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prayer meeting - a meeting for prayer and worship, especially among Christians
