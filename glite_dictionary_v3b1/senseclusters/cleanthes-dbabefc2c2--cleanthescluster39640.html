Cleanthes

proper noun
ancient Greek Stoic philosopher
9.9
Pronunciation: / kliˈænθiːz / (AmE), / klɪˈænθiːz / (BrE)
(c. 330–230 BC) An ancient Greek philosopher who succeeded Zeno of Citium as the head of the Stoic school.

Examples
