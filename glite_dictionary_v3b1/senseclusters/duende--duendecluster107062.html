duende

noun
folklore spirit
Pronunciation: / duˈɛndeɪ / (AmE), / duˈɛndeɪ / (BrE)
a small supernatural creature or spirit in Spanish and Latin American folklore, often mischievous or protective

Examples

A duende spirit
In many stories, a duende is said to protect children from harm.
Some villagers believe a duende lives in the old forest near their homes.
The legend describes the duende as both a trickster and a guardian spirit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elf - a small, often mischievous supernatural being in folklore
  • synonym: goblin - a mischievous, often ugly supernatural creature in folklore
  • synonym: sprite - a small, playful supernatural being or spirit
