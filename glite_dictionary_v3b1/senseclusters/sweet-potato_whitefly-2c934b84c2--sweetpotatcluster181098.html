sweet-potato whitefly

noun
a pest insect species affecting crops
Pronunciation: / ˌswit pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈwaɪtˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˌswiːt pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈwaɪtˌflaɪ / (BrE)
a small whitefly (Bemisia tabaci) that infests sweet potatoes and many other crops, known for transmitting plant viruses and causing agricultural damage

Examples

Sweet-potato whitefly infestation
Infestations of sweet-potato whitefly can reduce crop yields and spread plant viruses.
Scientists are developing new strategies to control sweet-potato whitefly populations in greenhouses.
The sweet-potato whitefly has become a major problem for farmers in recent years.
