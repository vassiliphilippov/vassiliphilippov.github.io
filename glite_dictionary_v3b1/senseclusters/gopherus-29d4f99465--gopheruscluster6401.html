Gopherus

noun
a genus of North American gopher tortoises
9.9
Pronunciation: / goʊˈfɛr.əs / (AmE), / gəʊˈfɛr.əs / (BrE)
A genus of North American tortoises known as gopher tortoises, characterized by their burrowing habits and found in arid or semi-arid regions.
