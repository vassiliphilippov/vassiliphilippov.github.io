acai

noun
tropical palm tree
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɑːˌsaɪˈi / (AmE), / ˌæsaɪˈiː / (BrE)
a tall tropical palm tree native to Central and South America that produces small, dark purple berries called acai berries

Examples

Acai palm tree
The acai is valued for its nutritious berries, which are used in many health foods.
Farmers in Brazil harvest acai from the wild to sell at local markets.
An acai can grow up to 25 meters tall in the Amazon rainforest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palm - a type of tropical tree with large leaves and no branches
