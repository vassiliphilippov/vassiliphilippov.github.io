solitaire

noun
card game for one person
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlɪˌtɛr / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪˌtɛə / (BrE)
a card game for one person, in which the player tries to arrange cards in a particular order according to set rules

Examples

Play solitaire cards
Every evening, she relaxes by playing a game of solitaire on her computer.
He shuffled the cards and set them out for a quick round of solitaire.
While waiting for her train, she passed the time with solitaire on her phone.
