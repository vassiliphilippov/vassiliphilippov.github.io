regimented

adjective
strictly controlled or organized
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒ.əˌmɛn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒ.ɪ.mɛn.tɪd / (BrE)
controlled or organized according to strict rules, often limiting individual freedom or flexibility.

Examples

A regimented routine
Her daily routine is so regimented that she wakes up at the same time every day.
Many people find a regimented environment stifling and prefer more flexibility.
The school adopted a regimented approach to discipline and class schedules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strict - demanding total control or rigid adherence to rules
