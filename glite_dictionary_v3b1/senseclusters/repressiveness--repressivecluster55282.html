repressiveness

noun
tendency to suppress feelings
8.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈprɛsɪvnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈprɛsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or tendency of holding back or suppressing emotions, desires, or impulses

Examples

Emotional repressiveness
His repressiveness prevented him from sharing his true feelings with others.
The repressiveness of the environment made it difficult for anyone to express disagreement.
She struggled with her own repressiveness when trying to talk about her problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhibition - the act of restraining or holding back impulses or feelings
  • antonym: openness - the quality of being open and willing to express emotions or thoughts
