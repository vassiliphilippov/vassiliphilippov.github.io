sandpit

noun
area or box of sand for children to play in
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈsændˌpɪt / (BrE)
a shallow container or an area outdoors filled with sand for children to play in

Examples

Children's sandpit
After the rain stopped, the sandpit was too wet to use.
Many parks have a sandpit next to the playground.
The children spent the afternoon building castles in the sandpit.
