reinstallation

noun
restoring to office
5.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.stə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪn.stə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.stə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪn.stə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of restoring someone to a previous position, office, or rank after removal or absence

Examples

Reinstallation of a leader
The reinstallation of the president was celebrated with a formal ceremony at the capital.
After the investigation cleared her name, her reinstallation as director was announced.
The union demanded the immediate reinstallation of the workers who had been unfairly dismissed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinstatement - the act of giving someone back their former job or position
