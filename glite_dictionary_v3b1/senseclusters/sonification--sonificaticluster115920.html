sonification

noun
data as sound
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of representing data or information as sound to help people understand patterns or features

Examples

Sonification of data
Scientists used sonification to turn climate data into a series of musical notes.
The sonification of stock market trends helped visually impaired users understand the changes.
In the art installation, sonification made the movement of the planets audible to the audience.
Researchers used sonification to help visually impaired people interpret scientific data.
The sonification of stock market trends allowed listeners to hear changes in real time.
Artists often experiment with sonification to create unique audio experiences from everyday information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audification - direct translation of data into sound without mapping to musical parameters
  • synonym: auditory display - the use of sound to present information
  • antonym: visualization - the representation of data as images or graphics
