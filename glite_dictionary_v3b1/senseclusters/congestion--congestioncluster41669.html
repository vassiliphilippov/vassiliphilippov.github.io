A condition of overcrowding, particularly when movement is hindered or slowed, such as with traffic or people.

Examples

Traffic congestion
During the holidays, shopping centers often suffer from severe congestion.
Morning congestion on the main highway caused commuters to be late for work.
The city is taking steps to reduce congestion in the downtown area.

Synonyms

  • over-crowding
    3.1 0.00/m
    over-crowding means too many people packed into a place and feeling uncomfortable, while congestion means crowding that slows movement.
  • jam
    2.7 3.51/m
    a jam is a sudden tight blockage of vehicles or people that stops movement, while congestion is a wider, ongoing overcrowding problem.
