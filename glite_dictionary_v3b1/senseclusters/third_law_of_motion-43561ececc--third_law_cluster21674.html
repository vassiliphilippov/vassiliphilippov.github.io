third law of motion

noun
Newton's law: action equals reaction
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /θɜːrd lɑː əv ˈmoʊ.ʃən/ /; / /θɜːrd lɔː əv ˈmoʊ.ʃən/ (AmE), /θɜːd lɔː əv ˈməʊ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Newton's third law of motion
