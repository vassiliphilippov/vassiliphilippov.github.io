vasculitis

noun
inflammation of a blood vessel
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvæskjəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌvæskjʊˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of a blood vessel or blood vessels, often causing changes in the walls of the vessels

Examples

Vasculitis inflammation
Some types of vasculitis can cause serious complications if not diagnosed and managed early.
The doctor suspected vasculitis when she noticed inflammation in the patient's blood vessels.
Treatment for vasculitis may include corticosteroids to reduce swelling and immune response.
