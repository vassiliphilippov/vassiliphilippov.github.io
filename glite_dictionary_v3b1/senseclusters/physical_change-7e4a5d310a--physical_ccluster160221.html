physical change

a process in which a substance changes its physical state or appearance but not its chemical composition

Examples

A physical change in state
During evaporation, only a physical change occurs, not a chemical one.
Ice melting into water is a classic example of a physical change.
No new substances are produced during a physical change like boiling or freezing.
