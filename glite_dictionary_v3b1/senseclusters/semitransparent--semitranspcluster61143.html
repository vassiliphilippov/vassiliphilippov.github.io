Examples

Synonyms

  • semitranslucent
    6.6 0.01/m
    semitranslucent looks frosted and lets light through but hides shapes, while semitransparent means partly see-through but not clear.
  • translucent
    4.8 4.08/m
    translucent lets light pass so you cannot see details, while semitransparent stresses partial see-through and less clarity.
  • semi-sheer
    5.6 0.01/m
    semi-sheer is thin fabric that you can partly see through, while semitransparent is a broader term for partial transparency.
  • semiopaque
    6.9 0.00/m
    semiopaque blocks more light and looks mostly solid, while semitransparent implies more light passes and partial clarity.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: transparent - allowing light to pass through so objects behind can be seen clearly
