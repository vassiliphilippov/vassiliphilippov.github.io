inter-group communication

noun
communication between different groups
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˌɡruːp kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌɡruːp kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the exchange of information or messages between different groups, especially within organizations or societies

Examples

Effective inter-group communication
Effective inter-group communication is essential for collaboration between different departments.
The manager encouraged inter-group communication to reduce misunderstandings in the workplace.
They organized regular meetings to improve inter-group communication within the company.
