yew

noun
evergreen tree or shrub with red berries
7.2 5.4
Pronunciation: / juː / (AmE), / juː / (BrE)
An evergreen tree or shrub with flat needle-like leaves and red, cup-shaped berries, belonging to the genus Taxus, often used for hedges and ornamental planting.

Examples

Yew tree
Yew hedges are popular in formal landscaping for their dense foliage and attractive appearance.
Some ancient churches in England are surrounded by yew trees, which are often considered sacred.
The garden features a centuries-old yew tree that is carefully trimmed each year.
