replicant

noun
human-like android
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛplɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛplɪkənt / (BrE)
a fictional or artificial robot or android that closely resembles and behaves like a human being (especially in science fiction)

Examples

A human-like replicant
In the science fiction film, the detective must determine who is a replicant and who is human.
The replicant struggled to understand emotions that real people take for granted.
Some believe that a replicant could eventually pass for a human in every way.
In the science fiction movie, a replicant struggles to understand its own identity.
The detective was unsure if the suspect was a human or a replicant.
Some believe that replicants could one day blend into society unnoticed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance
  • antonym: human - a person; a member of the species Homo sapiens
  • synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically
