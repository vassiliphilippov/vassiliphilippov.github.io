Bodo-Garo

noun
language group of Assam, India
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊdoʊˈɡɑroʊ / (AmE), / ˌbəʊdəʊˈɡɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
a group of related Kamarupan languages spoken primarily in the state of Assam in northeastern India

Examples

Bodo-Garo languages
Linguists have studied the Bodo-Garo family to understand its links to other Asian languages.
Many speakers of Bodo-Garo languages live in rural communities throughout Assam.
The Bodo-Garo languages are an important part of Assam's cultural heritage.
