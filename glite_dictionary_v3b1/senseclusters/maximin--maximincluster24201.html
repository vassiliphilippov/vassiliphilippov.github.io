maximin

adjective
maximizing the minimum
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksɪˌmɪn / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪˌmɪn / (BrE)
relating to a principle or strategy that seeks to maximize the minimum possible outcome or benefit.

Examples

Maximin strategy choice
The committee adopted a maximin strategy to ensure the least advantaged members received support.
In game theory, a maximin approach helps players secure the best of the worst-case outcomes.
Social policies based on the maximin principle aim to improve conditions for those with the least resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - favoring equal treatment or outcomes for all people
  • antonym: minimax - relating to minimizing the possible maximum loss
