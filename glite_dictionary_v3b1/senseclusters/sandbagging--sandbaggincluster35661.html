sandbagging

noun
pretending to be weaker
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsænd.bæɡ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsænd.bæɡ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of pretending to be less skilled or capable in order to gain an advantage later

Examples

Game sandbagging
Some players use sandbagging to surprise their opponents in the final round.
The coach warned the team against sandbagging during practice games.
Accusations of sandbagging can damage a competitor's reputation in tournaments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluff - to pretend to have a stronger position or ability than one actually does
  • antonym: show off - to display one's abilities or achievements to impress others
  • synonym: underplay - to deliberately make something seem less important or skilled
