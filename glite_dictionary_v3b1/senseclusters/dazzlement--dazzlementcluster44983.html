dazzlement

noun
state of amazement
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdæzəlmənt / (AmE), / ˈdæzlmənt / (BrE)
a state of being amazed or overwhelmed by something extremely impressive, beautiful, or bright

Examples

Child's dazzlement
The children stared in dazzlement at the magician's incredible tricks.
Her performance left the entire audience in a state of dazzlement.
He gazed at the city lights with pure dazzlement in his eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonishment - a feeling of great surprise or wonder
  • antonym: boredom - the state of being bored or uninterested
  • synonym: wonder - a feeling of amazement and admiration
