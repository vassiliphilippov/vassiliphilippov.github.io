automated

adjective
operated by automation or machines
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtəˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a device or process) operated by machines or computers rather than by people.

Examples

Automated passport control
Airport security now uses automated passport control machines.
The company installed an automated system to increase production efficiency.
The factory relies on automated assembly lines to build cars.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual - operated by hand, not by automation
