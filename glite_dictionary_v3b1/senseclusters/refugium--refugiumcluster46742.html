refugium

noun
place of safety
9.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfjuːdʒiəm / (AmE), / rɪˈfjuːdʒiəm / (BrE)
a place that provides safety or shelter for people or groups during times of danger or difficulty

Examples

A mountain refugium
The remote valley served as a refugium for wildlife during the harsh winter.
During the war, the old monastery became a refugium for displaced families.
Scientists study how certain forests act as a refugium for endangered species.

Related Terms

  • antonym: danger - the possibility of harm or threat
  • synonym: refuge - a place providing protection or shelter
  • synonym: sanctuary - a safe place or shelter from danger
