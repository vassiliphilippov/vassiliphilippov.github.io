merch

verb
to sell products
8.9
Pronunciation: / mɜrtʃ / (AmE), / mɜːtʃ / (BrE)
to promote, display, or sell products, especially merchandise, often in a retail or informal setting

Examples

To merch new shirts
The band plans to merch their new T-shirts at every concert this summer.
She learned how to merch products effectively by arranging them attractively in the store.
Influencers often merch exclusive items through their social media channels.
