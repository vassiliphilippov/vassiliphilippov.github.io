ax head

noun
metal cutting part of an ax
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæks hɛd / (AmE), / ˈæks hɛd /; / ˈaks hɛd / (BrE)
the metal cutting part at the end of an ax, used for chopping or splitting

The blacksmith forged a new axe head for the broken tool.
The old axe head was rusty but still very sharp.
