trophogenic

adjective
promoting nutrient production
Pronunciation: / ˌtroʊfəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrɒfəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the production of nutrients or growth, especially in an ecological context

Examples

The trophogenic layer
The trophogenic layer of the ocean is where most photosynthesis occurs.
Scientists study trophogenic zones to understand how nutrients support aquatic life.
A trophogenic environment encourages the rapid growth of algae and other plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutritive - providing nourishment or nutrients
  • synonym: trophic - relating to nutrition or feeding
