neoconservative

noun
supporter of neoconservatism
6.3 7.0
Pronunciation: /niː.oʊ.kən.ˈsɜːr.və.tɪv/ (AmE), /niː.əʊ.kən.ˈsɜː.və.tɪv/ (BrE)
a person who supports neoconservatism, a political movement that favors traditional values, strong government, and an assertive foreign policy

Examples

A neoconservative politician

Many policies introduced by the administration reflected neoconservative principles.
She identified as a neoconservative because of her views on foreign affairs and national security.
