head nurse

noun
nurse in charge of other nurses
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɛd nɜrs / (AmE), / hɛd nɜːs / (BrE)
the nurse who is in charge of and oversees other nurses in a hospital or medical institution

Examples

Head nurse in charge
After years of hard work, she was promoted to head nurse of the surgical ward.
If you have any concerns, please speak to the head nurse on duty.
The head nurse coordinated the patient care with remarkable efficiency.

Related Terms
