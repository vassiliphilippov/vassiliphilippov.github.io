chemical mace

noun
disabling spray causing eye irritation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl meɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl meɪs / (BrE)
a liquid spray, originally a trademark, that temporarily incapacitates a person by irritating the eyes and causing dizziness; used for self-defense and crowd control.

Examples

Chemical mace spray
Exposure to chemical mace can cause severe eye irritation and temporary blindness.
She carries chemical mace in her purse for self-defense while walking home at night.
The police used chemical mace to disperse the crowd during the protest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pepper spray - a spray derived from chili peppers used for self-defense
  • synonym: tear gas - a gas that causes severe eye irritation, used in crowd control
