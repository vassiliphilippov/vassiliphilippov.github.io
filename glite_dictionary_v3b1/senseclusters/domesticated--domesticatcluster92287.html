domesticated

adjective
tamed for human use
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /də.ˈme.stə.keɪ.təd/ /; / /də.ˈme.stɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /də.ˈme.stɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ /; / /də.ˈme.stə.keɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
(of an animal or plant) tamed and adapted for life with humans, especially for farming, companionship, or other domestic use.

Examples

Domesticated animals
Maize is a domesticated crop grown worldwide for food and livestock feed.
Many domesticated animals depend on humans for survival.
The domesticated cat differs significantly from its wild ancestors.
