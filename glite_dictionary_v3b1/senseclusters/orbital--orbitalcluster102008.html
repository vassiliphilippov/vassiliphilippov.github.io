orbital

adjective
relating to an orbit or path around something
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.bɪ.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈɔː.bɪ.təl / (BrE)
relating to or involving an orbit, especially the path of a celestial body or object around another.

Examples

Orbital path
During the orbital revolution, the spacecraft collected valuable data.
Scientists measured the planet's orbital velocity with precision instruments.
The satellite maintains a stable orbital path around the Earth.
