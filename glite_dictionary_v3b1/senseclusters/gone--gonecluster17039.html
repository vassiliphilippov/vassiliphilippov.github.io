gone

adjective
completely exhausted; extremely tired
Pronunciation: / ɡɑn / (AmE), / ɡɒn / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely tired or completely exhausted; drained of energy or vitality.

Examples

Completely gone after running
After running the marathon, I felt absolutely gone and could barely walk.
By midnight, everyone at the party was completely gone and ready for bed.
She looked so gone after working three double shifts in a row.
