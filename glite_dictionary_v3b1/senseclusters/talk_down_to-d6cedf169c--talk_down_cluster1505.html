talk down to

verb
speak condescendingly to someone
1.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /tɔːk daʊn tə/ /; / /tɑːk daʊn tə/ (AmE), /tɔːk daʊn tə/ /; / /tɔːk daʊn tuː/ (BrE)

Examples
