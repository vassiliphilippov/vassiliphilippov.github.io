field pennycress

noun
foetid Eurasian weed with round flat pods
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˈpɛniˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˈpɛniˌkrɛs / (BrE)
A Eurasian weed with a strong smell, having round, flat seed pods; now naturalized throughout North America.

Examples

Field pennycress plant
Gardeners often struggle to control field pennycress because of its rapid spread and persistence.
Scientists are studying field pennycress as a potential source of biofuel due to its oil-rich seeds.
The field behind the farmhouse was overgrown with field pennycress by late spring.
