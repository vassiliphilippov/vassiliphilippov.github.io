sod

noun
informal term for a man or boy
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑd / (AmE), / sɒd / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) A man or boy, often used in an affectionate, mildly disparaging, or pitying way.

Examples

Poor sod
He’s a lucky sod to have won the lottery two times in a row.
Poor sod, he doesn’t know what he’s getting into.
That cheeky sod finished all the biscuits before anyone else arrived.
