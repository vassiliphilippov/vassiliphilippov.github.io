consortium

noun
association of companies for a specific purpose
6.7 7.15/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàs…îr.ti.…ôm / (AmE), / k…ônÀàs…îÀê.ti.…ôm / (BrE)
an association of two or more organizations or companies formed to undertake an enterprise or project together

Examples

A consortium of companies
As a member of the consortium, the company shared resources with its partners.
Several universities formed a consortium to develop the new research facility.
The companies entered into a consortium to bid on the government contract.

Synonyms

  • syndicate
    5.9 0.78/m
    a syndicate is a small group of companies formed for a specific business project, while a consortium is a wider partnership for a joint enterprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alliance - a union formed for mutual benefit, especially between organizations or countries
  • synonym: partnership - association of two or more people or organizations working together
