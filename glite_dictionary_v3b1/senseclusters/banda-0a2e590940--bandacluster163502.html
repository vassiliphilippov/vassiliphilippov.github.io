Banda

proper noun
Central African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈbændə / (AmE), / ˈbændə / (BrE)
an ethnic group native to Central Africa, especially in the Central African Republic and surrounding regions

Examples

Banda people
The Banda have a rich tradition of wood carving and music in Central Africa.
Anthropologists have studied the social structure of the Banda for many years.
Many Banda communities live in the Central African Republic and neighboring countries.
