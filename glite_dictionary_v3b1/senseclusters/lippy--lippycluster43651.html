lippy

adjective
rude or cheeky
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪpi / (AmE), / ˈlɪpi / (BrE)
rude or cheeky, especially by talking back or showing disrespect to authority

Examples

Lippy teenager
If you get lippy with your boss, you might get in trouble.
Her lippy comments annoyed the teacher during class.
He was being lippy and refused to follow the instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheeky - showing a lack of respect in a playful or rude way
  • synonym: impudent - not showing due respect for another person
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness and respect
