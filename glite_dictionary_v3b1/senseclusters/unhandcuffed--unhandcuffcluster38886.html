unhandcuffed

verb
to remove handcuffs
6.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhændˌkʌft / (AmE), / ʌnˈhændˌkʌft / (BrE)

Examples

The police unhandcuffed her once they realized she was not involved in the incident.
He felt relieved when the officers finally unhandcuffed him at the station.
