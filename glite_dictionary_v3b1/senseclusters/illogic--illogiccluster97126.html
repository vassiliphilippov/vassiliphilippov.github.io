illogic

noun
invalid or incorrect reasoning
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
The quality or state of being illogical; invalid or incorrect reasoning.

Examples

The illogic in reasoning
Critics were quick to point out the illogic in her reasoning.
His argument was full of illogic and failed to convince the audience.
The illogic of the new policy became clear after several problems arose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irrationality - the quality of being illogical or unreasonable
  • antonym: logic - correct or valid reasoning
