physical therapist

noun
health professional treating injuries by physical methods
3.1 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
a healthcare professional who treats injury or physical dysfunction through exercises, manual therapy, and other physical methods
