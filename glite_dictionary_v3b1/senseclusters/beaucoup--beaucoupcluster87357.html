beaucoup

adverb
very much; a lot
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / bo äÀàkuÀê / (AmE), / b…ô äÀàkuÀê / (BrE)
very much or a lot; used informally to indicate a large amount or number.

Examples

A beaucoup of people
There were beaucoup people at the concert last night.
She has beaucoup experience working with international clients.
We received beaucoup compliments on our performance.

Synonyms

  • tons
    2.1 0.03/m
    tons is very informal and means a very large amount in casual speech, while beaucoup is an informal way to say a lot.
  • great deal
    1.6 37/m
    great deal is a neutral phrase used with 'of' to mean a large amount, while beaucoup is more informal and casual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: a lot - a large amount or number
  • antonym: few - a small number of people or things
  • synonym: many - a large number of people or things
