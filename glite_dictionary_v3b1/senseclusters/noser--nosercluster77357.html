noser

noun
overly curious person
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊzər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who is overly interested in other people's private matters or affairs

Examples

A noser in your business
Don't be such a noser; let people handle their own problems.
She was known as the office noser because she always wanted to know everyone's secrets.
If you keep acting like a noser, people might stop trusting you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busybody - a person who meddles in others' affairs
  • synonym: gossip - a person who likes to talk about other people's private lives
  • antonym: private - not concerned with others' affairs
