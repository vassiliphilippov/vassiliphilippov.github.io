Alger

proper noun
American author of inspirational adventure stories
Pronunciation: / ˈældʒər / (AmE), / ˈældʒə / (BrE)
Horatio Alger (1832–1899), an American author famous for his inspirational adventure stories for boys, emphasizing that virtue and hard work can overcome poverty.

He wrote his essay on the enduring influence of Alger and his novels.
