good

noun
a commodity or tradable product
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɡʊd / (BrE)
A raw material or product that is traded or sold, typically in large quantities, especially to be used in manufacturing or production processes.

Examples

Exported goods
Exporters must declare every good that crosses the border for trade.
Oil is one of the most important goods in the global economy.
The company imports raw goods like steel and copper for its factories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commodity - A raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold
