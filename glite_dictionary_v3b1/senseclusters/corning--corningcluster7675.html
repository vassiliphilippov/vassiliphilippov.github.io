corning

noun
granulation process
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of forming a substance into grains or granules, or the granulated material produced by this process

Examples

The corning of salt
The corning of salt makes it easier to sprinkle evenly over food.
Traditional gunpowder production involved the careful corning of the mixed ingredients.
After corning, the material was sifted to separate different grain sizes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granulation - the act or process of forming into grains or granules
