Huginn

proper noun
Scandinavian male name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːɡɪn / (AmE), / ˈhjuːɡɪn / (BrE)
a masculine given name in Scandinavia, originally from Old Norse meaning 'thought', also known as one of Odin's ravens in Norse mythology

Examples

Huginn as a name
In Norse legends, Huginn and Muninn are the two ravens who serve Odin.
The boy was named Huginn after the mythological bird symbolizing thought.
Many Scandinavian parents choose Huginn as a unique and meaningful name for their sons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Muninn - the other raven of Odin in Norse mythology, meaning 'memory'
