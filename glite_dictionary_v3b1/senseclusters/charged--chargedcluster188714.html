charged

adjective
having a net electric charge
6.6
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
(of a particle, object, or system) having a net positive or negative electric charge.

Examples

Charged particle
Some cosmic rays contain highly charged particles traveling at nearly the speed of light.
The charged capacitor stored enough energy to power the device.
The scientist demonstrated how a charged balloon could attract small pieces of paper.
