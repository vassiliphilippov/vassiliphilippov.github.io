Synonyms

  • out of order
    2.1 0.7/m
    out of order is the sign seen on a broken public machine, while down describes a system or service not working.
  • out of service
    3.5 0.6/m
    out of service describes something taken offline for repair or routine work, while down stresses that the system is not working.
  • unavailable
    3.4 6/m
    unavailable means not reachable or at hand for any reason, while down specifically means a machine or service is not working.
  • nonworking
    3.9 0.01/m
    nonworking describes something that does not function or is not in use, while down usually means a system or service is not working.
  • non-operating
    4.9 0.00/m
    non-operating is a formal term for not running within business or systems, while down is common talk for a service not working.
  • off
    1.0 0.5/m
    off means powered down or switched off intentionally, while down suggests the service or system is failing or not working.
