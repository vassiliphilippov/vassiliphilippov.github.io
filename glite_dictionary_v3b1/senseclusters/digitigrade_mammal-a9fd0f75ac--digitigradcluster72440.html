a mammal that walks on its toes, with only the digits touching the ground, such as dogs, cats, and horses

Examples

Digitigrade mammals like dogs
Cats are digitigrade mammals, which means they walk on their toes instead of their heels.
The adaptation of being a digitigrade mammal allows for greater speed in predators like cheetahs.
Unlike humans, who are plantigrade, most dogs are digitigrade mammals.
