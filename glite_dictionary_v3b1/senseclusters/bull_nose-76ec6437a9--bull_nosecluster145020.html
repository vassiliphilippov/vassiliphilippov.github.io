bull nose

noun
swelling disease of pigs' snouts
Pronunciation: / bʊl noʊz / (AmE), / bʊl nəʊz / (BrE)
a disease of pigs characterized by swelling and inflammation of the snout

Examples

Bull nose disease
Effective sanitation and early treatment can help prevent bull nose in pig populations.
Outbreaks of bull nose have affected several farms this season, leading to concerns among pig farmers.
The veterinarian diagnosed the pig with bull nose after noticing the severe swelling of its snout.
