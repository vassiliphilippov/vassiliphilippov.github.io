akebia

noun
edible purple fruit
Pronunciation: / əˈkiːbiə / (AmE), / əˈkiːbiə / (BrE)
an edible fruit with a long purple pod and sweet white pulp, produced by plants of the genus Akebia

Examples

An akebia fruit

After peeling the purple skin, she enjoyed the sweet pulp of the akebia.
In some regions, people use akebia in desserts and traditional dishes.
