gratuitousness

noun
unnecessary quality
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈtuːɪtəsnəs / (AmE), / ɡrəˈtjuːɪtəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unnecessary, uncalled for, or lacking good reason

Examples

The gratuitousness of violence
The gratuitousness of his comments made the meeting uncomfortable for everyone.
Critics pointed out the gratuitousness of the special effects in the movie.
She was annoyed by the gratuitousness of the extra paperwork required for the application.

Related Terms

  • antonym: necessity - the quality of being needed or required
  • synonym: superfluity - an excessive or unnecessary amount or quality
