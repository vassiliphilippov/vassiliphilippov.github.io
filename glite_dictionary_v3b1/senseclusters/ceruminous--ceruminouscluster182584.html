ceruminous

adjective
relating to earwax or its production
Pronunciation: / səˈruː.mɪ.nəs / (AmE), / sɛˈruː.mɪ.nəs / (BrE)
relating to or producing cerumen (earwax), especially referring to glands in the ear canal that secrete earwax

Examples

Ceruminous ear glands
Inflammation of the ceruminous glands can cause excessive earwax and discomfort.
Specialized ceruminous glands in the ear canal help produce protective earwax.
The anatomy textbook described the function of ceruminous glands in detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cerumen - a soft yellowish substance (earwax) secreted in the ear canal
