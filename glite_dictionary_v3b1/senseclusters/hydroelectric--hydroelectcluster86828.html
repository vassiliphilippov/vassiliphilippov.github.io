hydroelectric

adjective
relating to generating electricity from water power
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊɪˈlɛktrɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊɪˈlɛktrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using the generation of electricity by water power

Examples

Hydroelectric power plant
Many rural communities benefit from hydroelectric plants for electricity.
The new dam will greatly increase the region's supply of hydroelectric energy.
They invested in hydroelectric technology to reduce their reliance on fossil fuels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydropower - power derived from the energy of moving water
  • antonym: thermal - relating to or caused by heat, as in 'thermal power'
