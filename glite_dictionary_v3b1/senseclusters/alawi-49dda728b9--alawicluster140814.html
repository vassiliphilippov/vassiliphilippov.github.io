Alawi

proper noun
Moroccan royal dynasty
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæləwi / (AmE), / ˈæləwi / (BrE)
the royal dynasty that has ruled Morocco since the seventeenth century, also known as the Alaouite dynasty

Examples

Alawi royal dynasty
The Alawi dynasty has played a central role in Morocco's history for centuries.
King Mohammed VI is a member of the Alawi royal family.
Many historical sites in Morocco were built during the Alawi period.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Saadi - the Moroccan dynasty that preceded the Alawi dynasty
