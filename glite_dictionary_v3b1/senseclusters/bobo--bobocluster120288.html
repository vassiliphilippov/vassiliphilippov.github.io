bobo

noun
affluent bohemian
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a person who combines wealthy, middle-class tastes with unconventional, bohemian values and lifestyle

Examples

Bobo lifestyle
The new café attracts a bobo crowd who appreciate both organic food and modern art.
He considers himself a bobo, enjoying luxury brands while supporting alternative music festivals.
Many bobos choose to live in urban neighborhoods known for creativity and high-end shops.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bohemian - a person with artistic or unconventional social habits
  • degree lower: bourgeois - a person belonging to the middle class, often with conventional attitudes
  • synonym: hipster - a person who follows the latest trends, especially those outside the cultural mainstream
