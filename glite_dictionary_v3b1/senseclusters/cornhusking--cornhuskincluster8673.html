cornhusking

noun
social gathering for removing corn husks
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌhʌskɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌhʌskɪŋ /; / ˈkɔːnhʌskɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing the husks from ears of corn, often as a social or communal event
