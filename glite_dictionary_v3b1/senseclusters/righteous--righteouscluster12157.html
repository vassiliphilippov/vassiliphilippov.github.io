righteous

adjective
morally justified or right
1.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈraɪ.tʃəs/ (AmE), /ˈraɪ.tʃəs/ (BrE)
morally right or justified; acting according to what is considered morally good or proper

Examples

Righteous anger
He felt a righteous anger at the unfair treatment of the workers.
She believed her actions were righteous and for a greater good.
They fought for a righteous cause, seeking justice for all.
Many admired the righteous actions of the leader during the crisis.
She believed her decision was both just and righteous.
The community honored him as a righteous man who stood up for justice.
