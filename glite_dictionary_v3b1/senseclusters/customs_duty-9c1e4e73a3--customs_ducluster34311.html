customs duty

noun
tax on imported or exported goods
3.5 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk åst…ômz ÀåduÀêti / (AmE), / Ààk åst…ômz ÀådjuÀêti / (BrE)
a tax imposed by a government on goods imported into or exported from a country

Examples

High customs duty
Due to the high customs duty, many people prefer to buy electronics locally.
The company had to pay a significant customs duty on the imported machinery.
Travelers are often required to declare items that may be subject to customs duty.

Synonyms

  • tariff
    5.6 14/m
    tariff is the official tax rate set on imports and exports, while customs duty is the tax collected at a country's border.
  • duty
    3.0 7.14/m
    duty is a general tax paid to the government on many items, while customs duty is the specific tax charged at the border on imports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tariff - a tax or duty to be paid on a particular class of imports or exports
