locomote

verb
move or travel from place to place
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəˌmoʊt / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəməʊt / (BrE)
to move or travel from one place to another; to make progress by changing position

Examples

To locomote slowly
After his injury, he found it difficult to locomote without assistance.
Certain animals locomote using specialized limbs adapted for swimming.
Researchers studied how bacteria locomote by using tiny flagella.

Related Terms

  • synonym: move - to change position or location
  • synonym: travel - to make a journey, typically over a distance
