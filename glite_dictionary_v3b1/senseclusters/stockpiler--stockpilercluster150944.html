stockpiler

noun
machine for piling materials
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkˌpaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌpaɪlə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to stack or arrange bulk materials into large piles, especially in industrial settings

Examples

Industrial stockpiler
The mining company invested in a new stockpiler to handle increased production.
A stockpiler efficiently arranges coal into large, organized piles at the port.
Operators must be trained to use the stockpiler safely in busy industrial environments.
