cribbage

noun
a card game for two players
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪb.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪb.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
A card game, usually for two players, in which each player is dealt six cards and discards one or two, scoring points by making certain combinations.

Examples

Cribbage card game
He taught his daughter how to keep score in cribbage.
My grandparents have a beautiful wooden cribbage board they use every weekend.
We sat by the fire playing cribbage until midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinochle - a card game for two or more players with scoring combinations
