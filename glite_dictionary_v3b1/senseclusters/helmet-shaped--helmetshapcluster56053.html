helmet-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a helmet
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlmɪt ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmɪt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a convex shape similar to that of a helmet

Examples

Helmet-shaped headpieces
Many ancient warriors wore helmet-shaped headpieces to cover their heads completely.
She admired the helmet-shaped architecture of the new observatory dome.
The flower has a helmet-shaped bloom that protects its pollen from rain.
