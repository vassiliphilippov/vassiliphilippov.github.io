pharmacol

noun
abbreviation for pharmacology
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrməkɒl / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməkɒl / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for pharmacology, the scientific study of drugs and their effects on living organisms
