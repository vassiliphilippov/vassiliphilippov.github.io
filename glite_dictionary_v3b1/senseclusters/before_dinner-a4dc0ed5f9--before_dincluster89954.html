before dinner

adverb
earlier than the evening meal
Pronunciation: / bɪˈfɔr ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔː ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
At a time earlier than the evening meal.

Examples

Wash hands before dinner
Please wash your hands before dinner.
She usually goes for a walk before dinner to clear her mind.
They set the table before dinner every night.
