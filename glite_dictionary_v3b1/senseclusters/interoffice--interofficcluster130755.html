interoffice

adjective
between company offices
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
happening or used between different offices within the same company or organization

Examples

Interoffice meeting
The manager sent an interoffice email to coordinate the project between departments.
All interoffice mail is delivered twice a day to ensure quick communication.
They scheduled an interoffice meeting to discuss the new company policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: internal - within an organization
  • antonym: intraoffice - within a single office
