chimneyed

adjective
with chimneys
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmniːd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmniːd / (BrE)
having one or more chimneys, or marked by the presence of chimneys.

Examples

Chimneyed house
The chimneyed manor was visible from across the fields.
They admired the row of chimneyed houses lining the old street.
A chimneyed cottage is typical in this part of the countryside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chimneyless - without a chimney
