Xiphias gladius

noun
scientific name for swordfish
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪfi.əs ˈɡlædi.əs / (AmE), / ˈzɪfi.as ˈɡlædi.as / (BrE)
the scientific name for the swordfish, a large marine fish with a long, sword-like upper jaw, found in warm and temperate oceans worldwide

Xiphias gladius is prized by anglers for its speed and power.
