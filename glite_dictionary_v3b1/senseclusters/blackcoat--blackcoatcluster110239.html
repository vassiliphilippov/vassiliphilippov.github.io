blackcoat

noun
old term for priest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈblækˌkəʊt / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a clergyman or priest, especially one who wears a black coat as part of traditional dress

Examples

The village blackcoat
The village blackcoat visited every home during the holidays.
In old novels, the blackcoat often played a central role in village life.
The children listened quietly as the blackcoat told stories from the Bible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clergyman - a male member of the clergy
  • synonym: priest - a person authorized to perform religious ceremonies
