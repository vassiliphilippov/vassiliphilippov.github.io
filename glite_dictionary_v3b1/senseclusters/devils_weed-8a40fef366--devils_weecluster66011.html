devil's weed

noun
tropical poisonous plant; pasture weed
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlz ˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz ˌwiːd / (BrE)
a tropical poisonous plant with sprawling stems, known for its spiny fruit and as a harmful pasture weed
