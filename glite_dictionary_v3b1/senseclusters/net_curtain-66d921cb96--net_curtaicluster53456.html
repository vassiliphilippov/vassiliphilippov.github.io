net curtain

noun
sheer curtain made of net fabric
1.2 2.4
Pronunciation: / nɛt ˈkɝː.tən / (AmE), / nɛt ˈkɜː.tən / (BrE)
A thin, often sheer curtain made of net-like fabric, used to allow light into a room while preventing people outside from seeing in.

Examples

A net curtain window
She washed the net curtains to freshen up the look of the bedroom windows.
Through the net curtain, morning sunlight streamed softly into the room.
We replaced the old net curtain in the living room with a heavier drape for more privacy.
