Hadrosauridae

noun
family of duck-billed dinosaurs
9.9
Pronunciation: /hæ.drə.ˈsɑːr.ɪ.diː/ /; / /hæ.drə.ˈsɔːr.ɪ.diː/ (AmE), /hæ.drə.ˈsɔːr.ɪ.diː/ (BrE)
a family of large, herbivorous dinosaurs with duck-billed snouts that lived during the Upper Cretaceous period

Examples

Hadrosauridae family dinosaurs
Fossils of Hadrosauridae have been discovered on several continents, shedding light on their wide distribution.
Scientists believe that Hadrosauridae traveled in herds and were among the most common herbivores of their time.
