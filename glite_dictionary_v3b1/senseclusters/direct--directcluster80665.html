Examples

Direct current
In a direct relationship, both variables increase or decrease together.
The force applied to the spring has a direct effect on its extension.
There is a direct correlation between the number of hours studied and exam results.
A direct current is used in most battery-powered devices.
Solar panels generate direct current before it is converted for home use.
Unlike alternating current, direct current flows in only one direction.
The planet resumes its direct path after a period of retrograde motion.

When a planet is direct, its apparent movement aligns with Earth's revolution around the sun.

Synonyms

  • unidirectional
    6.8 0.47/m
    unidirectional means movement in only one direction, while direct often describes current or motion that goes in a single, standard direction.
  • verbatim
    6.0 0.02/m
    verbatim repeats words exactly from a source, while direct in speech means quoting the original words rather than giving a summary.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alternating - (of a current) changing direction periodically
  • antonym: indirect - not quoting the exact words, paraphrased
  • antonym: inverse - opposite mathematical relationship: a decrease in one leads to an increase in another
  • antonym: retrograde - (of planets) moving in the direction opposite to the usual eastward motion
  • synonym: verbatim - in exactly the same words as used originally
