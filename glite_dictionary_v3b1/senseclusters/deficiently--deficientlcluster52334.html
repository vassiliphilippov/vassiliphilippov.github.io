deficiently

adverb
inadequately
6.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɪʃəntli / (AmE), / dɪˈfɪʃəntli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks something necessary or is not adequate

Examples

Written deficiently
The report was written deficiently and lacked important details.
If the engine runs deficiently, it may need to be repaired.
The students were taught deficiently, resulting in poor exam results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inadequately - in a way that is not sufficient or enough
  • antonym: sufficiently - in a way that is enough or adequate
