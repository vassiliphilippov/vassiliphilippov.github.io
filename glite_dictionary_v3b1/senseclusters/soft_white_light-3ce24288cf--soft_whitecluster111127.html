soft white light

noun
gentle, non-harsh white illumination
5.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /sɑːft waɪt laɪt/ /; / /sɔːft waɪt laɪt/ (AmE), /sɒft waɪt laɪt/ /; / /sɔːft waɪt laɪt/ (BrE)
