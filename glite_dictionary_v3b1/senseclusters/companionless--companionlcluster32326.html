companionless

adjective
without a companion
6.5
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjənləs / (AmE), / kəmˈpænjənləs / (BrE)
having no companion; being alone or without anyone to be with

Examples

The old dog wandered the streets companionless after its owner passed away.
He spent the holiday companionless, missing the company of his family and friends.
