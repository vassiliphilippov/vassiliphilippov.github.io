In education, the practice of adjusting teaching methods, content, and assessment to meet the diverse abilities, readiness levels, and interests of individual learners.

Examples

Classroom differentiation
Teachers used differentiation strategies to support both struggling and advanced students in the lesson.
Effective differentiation requires assessing students' readiness, interests, and learning profiles regularly.
The school's professional development focused on practical methods for classroom differentiation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: differentiated instruction - teaching that is planned and delivered to address students' varying learning needs and preferences
  • synonym: individualized instruction - instruction tailored to the specific needs or pace of a single learner
