term of enlistment

noun
period of military service commitment
Pronunciation: / tɝm əv ɪnˈlɪstmənt / (AmE), / tɜːm əv ɪnˈlɪstmənt / (BrE)
the fixed period of time that a person agrees to serve in the military after joining

Examples

The term of enlistment period
After completing his term of enlistment, he decided not to re-enlist and left the army.
Changing the term of enlistment could affect both recruitment and retention in the armed forces.
The standard term of enlistment in the navy is four years, but some contracts are longer.
