whispering

noun
speaking very softly or murmuring
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪspərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of speaking very softly without vibrating the vocal cords, so only the breath is heard.

Examples

Secret whispering
I heard a whispering behind the door, but I couldn't make out the words.
The whispering of the wind through the trees was calming.
There was a constant whispering in the library as students studied together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whisper - Speak very softly using one's breath rather than one's vocal cords
