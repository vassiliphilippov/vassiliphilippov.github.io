coast rhododendron

noun
rhododendron shrub of Pacific North America
9.0
Pronunciation: / koʊst ɹoʊdəˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / kəʊst rəʊdəˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
a medium-sized rhododendron native to the Pacific coast of North America, with large rosy flowers spotted with brown
