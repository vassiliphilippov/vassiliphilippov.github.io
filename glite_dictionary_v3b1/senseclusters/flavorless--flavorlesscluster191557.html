flavorless

adjective
lacking taste or flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvɚləs /; / ˈfleɪvərləs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvələs / (BrE)
lacking any noticeable taste or flavor; bland or insipid.

Examples

A flavorless dish
Many people find airline food to be rather flavorless and unappetizing.
She added extra spices to the flavorless vegetables to make them tastier.
The soup was disappointingly flavorless despite its colorful appearance.
He pushed aside the flavourless rice and reached for the spiced vegetables.
Many people complain that supermarket strawberries are flavourless compared to homegrown ones.
The soup was completely flavourless, so I added extra salt and pepper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bland - lacking strong flavor; mild or insipid
  • synonym: insipid - lacking flavor or zest; dull
  • antonym: savory - having a pleasant, strong, or spicy flavor
  • synonym: tasteless - without flavor
