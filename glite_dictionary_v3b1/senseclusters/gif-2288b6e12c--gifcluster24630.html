GIF

verb
to send an animated image
4.2
Pronunciation: / dʒɪf / (AmE), / dʒɪf / (BrE)
to send or create a GIF (an animated or short looping image) to express a reaction or idea, especially in messaging or on social media

Examples

To GIF a reaction
She decided to GIF her excitement to the group chat instead of typing a long message.
Whenever someone tells a funny story, he loves to GIF the perfect reaction.
I often GIF my friends when words just aren't enough to express how I feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: message - to send a written or visual communication electronically
  • synonym: text - to send a written message by phone or online
