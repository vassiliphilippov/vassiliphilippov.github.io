nubile

adjective
(of young women) marriageable or sexually mature
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːbaɪl / (AmE), / ˈnjuːbaɪl / (BrE)
(of a girl or young woman) suitable for marriage, especially in regard to age or physical maturity.

Examples

Nubile young woman
In several cultures, only nubile daughters are introduced to potential suitors.
Stories often reference nubile princesses awaiting marriage.
The painting depicted a nubile maiden standing by a window.
