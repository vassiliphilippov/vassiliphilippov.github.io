collaborate

verb
work together on a project
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæbəˌreɪt / (AmE), / kəˈlæbəreɪt / (BrE)
to work jointly with others, especially in an intellectual or creative endeavor

Examples

To collaborate on a project
Scientists from different countries often collaborate to solve complex problems.
She decided to collaborate with local artists on the community mural.
The two companies collaborated to develop a new environmentally friendly product.
