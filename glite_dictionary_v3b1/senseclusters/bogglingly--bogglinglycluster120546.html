bogglingly

adverb
extremely surprisingly
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑɡlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbɒɡlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely surprising, confusing, or difficult to understand

Examples

A bogglingly complex trick
The magician performed a bogglingly complex trick that left the audience speechless.
It is bogglingly difficult to imagine how they built such a massive structure without modern tools.
The amount of information available online is bogglingly vast and constantly growing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonishingly - in a way that causes great surprise
  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to understand
  • synonym: confusingly - in a way that causes confusion
