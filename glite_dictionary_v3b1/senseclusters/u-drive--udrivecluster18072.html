u-drive

noun
a rented car for self-drive use
Pronunciation: / ˈjuˌdraɪv / (AmE), / ˈjuːˌdraɪv / (BrE)
a car rented for temporary use, typically by the day or week, in which the renter drives themselves

Examples

Rent a u-drive
After her flight landed, she picked up a u-drive at the airport.
Every summer, tourists flock to town and reserve a u-drive to explore the area.
They decided to rent a u-drive for their road trip across the state.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hire car - a car rented for temporary use (mainly British English)
  • synonym: rental car - a car that is rented, especially for short-term use
