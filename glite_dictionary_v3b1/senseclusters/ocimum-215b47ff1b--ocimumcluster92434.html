Ocimum

noun
genus of aromatic herbs (basil family)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːsɪməm / (AmE), / ˈɒsɪməm / (BrE)
a genus of aromatic herbs in the mint family (Lamiaceae), including basil, used in cooking, medicine, or as ornamental plants

Examples
