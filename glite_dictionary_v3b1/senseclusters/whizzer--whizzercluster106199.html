whizzer

noun
spinning toy or device
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzər / (AmE), / ˈwɪzə / (BrE)
a small device or toy that spins rapidly and makes a whirring sound when moving

Examples

A spinning whizzer
The child launched a plastic whizzer that spun across the lawn making a loud whirring sound.
He bought a new whizzer at the toy store, and it spun for several minutes.
When you pull the string, the whizzer spins quickly and makes a buzzing noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinner - a toy or device that spins around a central point
  • synonym: top - a toy that spins on a point when set in motion
