if

conjunction
introducing a condition
Pronunciation: / ɪf / (AmE), / ɪf / (BrE)
used to introduce a condition or a situation that must happen before another can occur

Examples

If it rains
Let me know if you need any help with your homework.
We can go for a walk if it stops raining this afternoon.
If you study hard, you will pass the exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unless - except if; introducing the only situation in which something will not happen
  • synonym: when - at or during the time that
