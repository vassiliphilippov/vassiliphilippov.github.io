tip

verb
give gratuity for service
2.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /tɪp/ (AmE), /tɪp/ (BrE)
to give a small amount of money to someone who has provided a service, in addition to the basic payment

Examples

To tip the driver
Did you remember to tip the taxi driver after your ride?
It's common in the US to tip servers fifteen to twenty percent of the bill.
Many restaurants now include a suggested amount to tip at the bottom of the receipt.
