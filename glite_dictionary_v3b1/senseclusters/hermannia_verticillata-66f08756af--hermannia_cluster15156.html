Hermannia verticillata

noun
African shrub with yellow, honey-scented flowers
Pronunciation: / hərˈmɑːni.ə vərˌtɪsɪˈleɪtə / (AmE), / həˈmɑːnɪə ˌvɜː.tɪ.sɪˈleɪ.tə / (BrE)
An African shrub with trailing stems and slender, honey-scented yellow flowers, either solitary or in pairs.

Hermannia verticillata yellow flowers
