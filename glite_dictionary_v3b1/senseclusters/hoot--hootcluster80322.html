hoot

verb
to make a loud sound
2.2
Pronunciation: / huːt / (AmE), / huːt / (BrE)
to make a loud, deep sound like that of an owl or a car horn

Examples

Hoot a horn
The owl began to hoot as night fell in the quiet forest.
Drivers sometimes hoot their horns to warn others at busy intersections.
We heard someone hooting with laughter at the back of the theater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honk - to make a loud sound like a car horn
  • antonym: whisper - to speak or make a sound very quietly
