mindfully

adverb
with awareness or deliberate attention
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪndfəli / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndfəli / (BrE)
In a careful, deliberate, or attentive manner; with full awareness or intention.

Examples

To mindfully walk
By walking mindfully through the park, she noticed the beauty all around her.
He tried to respond mindfully instead of reacting out of anger.
She ate her lunch mindfully, savoring each bite and appreciating the flavors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefully - in a way that is done with attention or caution
  • antonym: heedlessly - without paying attention; carelessly
