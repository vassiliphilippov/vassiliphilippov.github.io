presentational

adjective
relating to presentation
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the act of presenting or a presentation, especially in psychology or philosophy.

Examples

Strong presentational skills
Her presentational skills were praised by all the conference attendees.
In philosophy, some theories focus on presentational aspects of perception.
The lecturer used a presentational style that made complex ideas easy to follow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presentationalism - a philosophical doctrine emphasizing the importance of presentation in perception or knowledge
