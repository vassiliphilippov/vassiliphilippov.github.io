feverfew

noun
a European medicinal herb with small white flowers
9.9 0.21/m

A bushy, aromatic European perennial herb (Tanacetum parthenium) with clusters of small, white, daisy-like flower heads, traditionally valued for medicinal uses.

Examples

The feverfew plant
Many herbalists recommend feverfew for the prevention of migraine headaches.
People have used feverfew as a traditional remedy for centuries.
The garden was full of blooming feverfew beside the lavender and rosemary.

Synonyms
