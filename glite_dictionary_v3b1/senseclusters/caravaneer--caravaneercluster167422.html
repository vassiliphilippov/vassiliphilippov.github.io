caravaneer

noun
caravan leader
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəvəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌkærəvəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who leads or travels with a caravan, especially across deserts or long trade routes

Examples

Desert caravaneer
The experienced caravaneer knew every oasis along the desert route.
As a caravaneer, he was responsible for the safety of both people and camels.
Stories of the legendary caravaneer were told in every market town along the Silk Road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caravan leader - a person in charge of a caravan
  • synonym: guide - a person who leads or directs others on a journey
