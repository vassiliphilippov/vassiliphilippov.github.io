Australian coral snake

noun
a small venomous Australian snake with red, black, and white bands
Pronunciation: / əˈstreɪliən ˈkɔrəl sneɪk / (AmE), / əˈstreɪliən ˈkɒrəl sneɪk / (BrE)
a small, venomous but generally harmless snake native to Australia, noted for its striking red, black, and white markings

Examples

The Australian coral snake species
Despite being venomous, an Australian coral snake is considered harmless to humans.
