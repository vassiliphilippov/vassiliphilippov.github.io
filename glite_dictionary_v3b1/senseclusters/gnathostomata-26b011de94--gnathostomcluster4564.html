Gnathostomata

noun
jawed vertebrates group
9.9
Pronunciation: / næθəˈstɑːmətə /; / næθ.əˈstɑː.mə.tə / (AmE), / ˌnæθəʊˈstɒmətə /; / nath.əˈstɒ.mə.tə / (BrE)
a major group of vertebrate animals that have both upper and lower jaws, including fishes, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals
