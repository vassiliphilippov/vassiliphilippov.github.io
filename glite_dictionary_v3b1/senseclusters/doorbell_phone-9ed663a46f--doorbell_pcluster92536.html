doorbell phone

noun
two-way entry communication device
4.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…îrÀåb…õl fo än / (AmE), / Ààd…îÀêb…õl f…ô än / (BrE)
A device or system that enables two-way audio communication between someone at an entrance and someone inside a building, often integrated with a doorbell to control entry.

Examples

Modern doorbell phones
Modern doorbell phones often include video capabilities in addition to audio communication.
She answered the doorbell phone to ask the delivery driver to leave the package at the door.
The new apartment complex installed a doorbell phone at every entrance for residents' security.
