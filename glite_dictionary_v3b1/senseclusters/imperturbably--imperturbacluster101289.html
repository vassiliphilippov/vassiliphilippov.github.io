imperturbably

adverb
calmly, unshakably
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpərˈtɜrbəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈtɜːbəbli / (BrE)
in a calm and unshakable manner, not easily upset or disturbed

Examples

To answer imperturbably
Despite the chaos around him, he responded imperturbably to every question.
The pilot handled the emergency imperturbably, reassuring everyone on board.
She waited imperturbably for the results, showing no sign of anxiety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed way
  • antonym: nervously - in an anxious or worried way
  • synonym: serenely - in a calm and peaceful manner
