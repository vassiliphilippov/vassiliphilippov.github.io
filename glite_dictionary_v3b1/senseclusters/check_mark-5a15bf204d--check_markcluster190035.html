check mark

noun
social-media verification badge
3.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8t\u0283\u025bk m\u0251rk / (AmE), / \u02c8t\u0283\u025bk m\u0251\u02d0k / (BrE)
(on social media and other online platforms) a small symbol, often blue, placed next to a user or organization name to show that the account has been verified as authentic or official.

Examples

Blue check mark badge
Public figures often display a blue check mark to indicate verification.

She was thrilled when the platform gave her profile a check mark showing it was authentic.
