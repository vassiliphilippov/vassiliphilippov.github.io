Synonyms

  • intercrossing
    7.0 0.00/m
    intercrossing is breeding between distinct groups to make hybrids, while interbreeding is a general term for mating across populations or species.
  • crossing
    7.2 0.3/m
    crossing is the genetic act of mating different varieties to make hybrids, while interbreeding includes any mating across populations or species.
  • crossbreeding
    4.4 0.1/m
    crossbreeding mixes breeds or varieties to create hybrids with certain traits, while interbreeding can be natural and less goal-oriented.
  • cross
    5.9 5/m

  • cross-fertilization
