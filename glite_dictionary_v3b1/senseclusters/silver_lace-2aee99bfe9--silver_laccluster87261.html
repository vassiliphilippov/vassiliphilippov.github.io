silver lace

noun
shrubby perennial plant with silvery leaves and white flowers
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ leɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə leɪs / (BrE)
a shrubby perennial plant from the Canary Islands, known for its white flowers and silvery, down-covered leaves and stems; sometimes classified in the genus Chrysanthemum

Examples

Silver lace plant
Botanists sometimes debate whether silver lace belongs to the genus Chrysanthemum.
She planted silver lace along the garden border to add texture and contrast.
The silver lace is popular among gardeners for its unique silvery foliage and delicate white blossoms.
