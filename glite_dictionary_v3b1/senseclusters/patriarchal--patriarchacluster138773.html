patriarchal

adjective
controlled or dominated by men
4.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /peɪ.trɪ.ˈɑːr.kəl/ /; / /peɪ.tri.ˈɑːr.kəl/ (AmE), /peɪ.trɪ.ˈɑː.kəl/ /; / /peɪ.tri.ˈɑː.kəl/ (BrE)
