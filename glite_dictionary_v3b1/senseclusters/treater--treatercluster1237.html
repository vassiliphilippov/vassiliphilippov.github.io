treater

noun
person giving treatment
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːtər / (AmE), / ˈtriːtə / (BrE)
a person, especially a medical professional, who provides treatment to someone

Examples

Medical treater
The treater carefully explained the procedure to the patient before starting.
Each treater in the clinic follows strict hygiene protocols to ensure safety.
During the study, the treater recorded all observations in the patient's file.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caregiver - a person who provides care to someone, especially the sick or elderly
  • antonym: patient - a person who receives medical treatment
