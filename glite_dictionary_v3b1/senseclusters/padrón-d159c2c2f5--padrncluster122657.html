padrón

noun
Spanish green pepper
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈðɾoʊn / (AmE), / pəˈdrɒn / (BrE)
a small green pepper from Padrón, Spain, often fried and served as a tapa in Spanish cuisine

Examples

Fried padrón peppers
We ordered a plate of padrón at the Spanish restaurant to share as an appetizer.
Some padrón peppers are mild, but occasionally you get a spicy one.
The chef quickly fried the padrón and sprinkled them with coarse sea salt.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: jalapeño - a medium-sized, spicy green chili pepper
  • synonym: shishito - a mild, slender green pepper popular in Japanese cuisine
