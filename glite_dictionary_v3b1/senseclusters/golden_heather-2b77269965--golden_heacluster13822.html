golden heather

noun
North American heath-like plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈhɛðər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈhɛðə / (BrE)
A North American evergreen plant with heath-like appearance and yellow flowers, belonging to the genus Hudsonia.

Examples

Golden heather plant
During our hike, we spotted patches of golden heather brightening the heath in early summer.
Gardeners value golden heather for its evergreen leaves and masses of yellow blooms.
The golden heather grows wild along sandy North American coastlines.
