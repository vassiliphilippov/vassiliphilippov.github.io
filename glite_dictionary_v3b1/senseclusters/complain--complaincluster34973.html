complain

verb
express dissatisfaction or unhappiness
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpleɪn / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪn / (BrE)
to say that you are not satisfied or unhappy about something, often in a way that suggests you want it to be changed or improved

Examples

To complain about service
He complained that the food was cold and asked for a replacement.
If you have any problems with the service, please complain to the manager.
She often complains about the weather when it rains for several days in a row.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grumble - to complain in a quiet or bad-tempered way
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
