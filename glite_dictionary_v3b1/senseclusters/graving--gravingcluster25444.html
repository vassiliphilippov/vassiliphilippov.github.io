graving

noun
carving or engraving
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of carving or engraving, especially on a surface such as wood or metal

Examples

The graving of wood
The detailed graving on the silver plate showed remarkable craftsmanship.
He admired the ancient graving found on the stone monument.
The museum displayed tools used for graving in the medieval period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carving - the act of shaping something by cutting material away
  • synonym: engraving - the process of cutting or carving a design onto a hard surface
