voodooism

noun
Caribbean religion mixing African and Catholic elements
Pronunciation: / ˈvuːduːˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈvuːduːɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
A religion practiced chiefly in the Caribbean, especially Haiti, that combines West African beliefs, Roman Catholic influences, witchcraft, and animistic deities.

Examples

Voodooism religion
He traveled to Haiti to study the ceremonies and culture of voodooism.
Some people misunderstand the true beliefs behind voodooism.
Voodoo priests play an important role in the rituals of voodooism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voodoo - a religion combining elements from African religions and Roman Catholicism, mainly practiced in Haiti
