starting pitcher

noun
main pitcher who begins a baseball game
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtɪŋ ˈpɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ ˈpɪtʃə / (BrE)
In baseball, a pitcher who is designated to begin the game for their team.

Examples

As a rookie, she quickly earned her place as a starting pitcher on the roster.
If the starting pitcher struggles early, the coach might call in a reliever.
