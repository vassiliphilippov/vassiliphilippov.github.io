whomp

verb
defeat decisively
8.8 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / wÉ‘mp / (AmE), / wÉ’mp / (BrE)
to defeat or beat someone decisively and overwhelmingly

Examples

To whomp the other team
If you don't study, the test might really whomp you.
The home team managed to whomp their rivals with a score of 6-0.
They whomped the competition and took home the trophy.
