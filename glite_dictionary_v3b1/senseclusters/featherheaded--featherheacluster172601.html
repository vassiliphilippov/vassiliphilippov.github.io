featherheaded

adjective
silly or foolish
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðərˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəhɛdɪd / (BrE)
silly or lacking good judgment; foolish or thoughtless

Examples

Featherheaded decision
It was a featherheaded decision to leave his keys in the car overnight.
Her featherheaded remarks made everyone laugh, but no one took her seriously.
He can be quite featherheaded when it comes to important matters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
