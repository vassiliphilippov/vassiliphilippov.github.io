gamekeeping

noun
wildlife management
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of managing, protecting, and breeding wild game animals on private land, especially for hunting purposes

Examples

Gamekeeping on land
He decided to pursue a career in gamekeeping after developing a passion for wildlife.
Modern gamekeeping involves both protecting animals and maintaining their habitats.
The estate hired a new staff member to oversee gamekeeping and prevent illegal hunting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservation - the protection and management of natural resources
  • antonym: poaching - the illegal hunting or capturing of wild animals
