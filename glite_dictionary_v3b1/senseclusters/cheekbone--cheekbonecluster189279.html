cheekbone

noun
bone under the eye forming the cheek
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃikˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːkˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the bone below the eye that forms the prominence of the cheek

Examples
