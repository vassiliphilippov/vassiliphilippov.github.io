balloon

noun
large bag filled with gas or heated air
3.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / bəˈlun / (AmE), / bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a large flexible bag filled with gas or heated air, which rises and floats in the atmosphere

Examples

Hot-air balloon ride
She let go of the helium balloon, and it floated up into the sky.
The festival featured a giant balloon shaped like a dinosaur that attracted many visitors.
The hot air balloon drifted slowly over the countryside at sunrise.
