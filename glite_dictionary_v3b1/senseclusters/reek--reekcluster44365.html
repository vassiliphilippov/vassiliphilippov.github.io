Pronunciation: / riːk / (AmE), / riːk / (BrE)
A strong and unpleasant smell; a stench.

Examples

A reek of smoke
Even after washing the clothes, a reek of smoke lingered.
He wrinkled his nose at the reek coming from the old fishing boat.
The reek of rotting garbage filled the alley on a hot summer day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stench - a strong and very unpleasant smell
