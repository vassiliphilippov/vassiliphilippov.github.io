Pronunciation: / riÀêk / (AmE), / riÀêk / (BrE)
A strong and unpleasant smell; a stench.

Examples

A reek of smoke

He wrinkled his nose at the reek coming from the old fishing boat.
The reek of rotting garbage filled the alley on a hot summer day.

Synonyms

    3.5 4.63/m

  • stink
    1.1 3.99/m
    stink is a more informal word used as both noun and verb in speech, while reek is mainly a noun for a strong smell.
