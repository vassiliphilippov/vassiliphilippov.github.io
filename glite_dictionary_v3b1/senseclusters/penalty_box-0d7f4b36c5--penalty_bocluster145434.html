penalty box

noun
area where penalized ice hockey players sit
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəlti bɑks / (AmE), / ˈpɛnəlti bɒks / (BrE)
an area in ice hockey or similar sports where players sit to serve time after committing a penalty

Examples

The penalty box in hockey
After the fight, both players were sent to the penalty box for five minutes.
He watched the rest of the game from the penalty box after a hard foul.
The team had to kill a penalty while their captain sat in the penalty box.
After spending five minutes in the penalty box, she returned to the game with renewed energy.
He watched his team score a goal while he was still in the penalty box.
The referee sent him to the penalty box for high-sticking.

Related Terms
