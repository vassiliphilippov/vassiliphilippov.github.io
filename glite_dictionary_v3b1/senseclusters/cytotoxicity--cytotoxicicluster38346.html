cytotoxicity

noun
toxicity to living cells
Pronunciation: / saɪtəˈtɑksɪti / (AmE), / saɪtəˈtɒksɪti / (BrE)
the quality or degree of being toxic to living cells

Examples

Cytotoxicity level
A low level of cytotoxicity is desirable in materials used for implants.
Researchers are concerned about the cytotoxicity of certain nanoparticles.
The cytotoxicity of the new drug was tested on human liver cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toxicity - the degree to which a substance is poisonous
