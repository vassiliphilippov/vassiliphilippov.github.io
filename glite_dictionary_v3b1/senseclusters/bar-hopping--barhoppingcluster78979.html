bar-hopping

noun
visiting multiple bars
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑr ˌhɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ˌhɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of visiting several bars or pubs in one night, usually for drinks and socializing

Examples

A night of bar-hopping
They planned a night of bar-hopping to celebrate their friend's birthday.
After dinner, we went bar-hopping in the city center with a group of colleagues.
Some tourists enjoy bar-hopping to experience the local nightlife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pub crawl - an event where people visit several pubs or bars in succession
