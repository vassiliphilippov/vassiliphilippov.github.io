Pronunciation: / ˈjɑːdə / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdə / (BrE)
an informal filler word used to indicate continuation or that more of the same could be said, similar to 'and so on'

Examples

Yada yada yada
She told me about her day, her meetings, her emails, yada, until I lost track.
The instructions were to fill out the form, sign it, send it in, yada.
He always complains about the weather, his job, yada, but never does anything about it.
