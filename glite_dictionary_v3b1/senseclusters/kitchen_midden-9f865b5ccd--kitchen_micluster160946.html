kitchen midden

noun
prehistoric refuse heap found in archaeology
9.8 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪtʃ.ən ˈmɪd.ən/ (AmE), /ˈkɪtʃ.ɪn ˈmɪd.ən/ /; / /ˈkɪtʃ.ən ˈmɪd.ən/ (BrE)
