pudge

noun
a short fat person (informal)
Pronunciation: / pʌdʒ / (AmE), / pʌdʒ / (BrE)
a short and fat person, often used in an affectionate or teasing way

Examples

A little pudge
Ever since he was a kid, his older brothers called him pudge.
He jokes about being a pudge after the holidays.
She was always a bit of a pudge in school but never let it bother her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beanpole - a very tall and thin person (informal)
  • synonym: chub - a plump or chubby person (informal)
