shewbread

noun
temple offering bread
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌbrɛd / (BrE)
bread placed on a table in the ancient Jewish temple as a continual offering to God

Examples

The shewbread on table
The priest placed the shewbread on the table in the sanctuary every Sabbath.
In ancient times, shewbread symbolized the covenant between God and the Israelites.
The ritual of replacing the shewbread was an important part of temple worship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offering - something presented to a deity as a religious act
  • synonym: sacrifice - an act of offering something to a deity
