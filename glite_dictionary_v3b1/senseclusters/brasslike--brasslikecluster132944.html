brasslike

adjective
resembling a brass instrument
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːslaɪk / (BrE)
resembling the sound, appearance, or qualities of a brass instrument, especially in being bright, metallic, or resonant.

Examples

Brasslike bright sound
She chose a lampshade with a brasslike sheen that matched the décor.
The trumpet player's solo had a distinctive brasslike quality that dominated the song.
The wind howled through the pipes, creating an eerie, brasslike sound throughout the night.
