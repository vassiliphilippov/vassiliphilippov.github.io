beet green

noun
young leaves of the beetroot plant
Pronunciation: / bit ɡrin / (AmE), / biːt ɡriːn / (BrE)
the edible young leaves of the beetroot plant, often cooked or used in salads

Examples

Fresh beet greens
Fresh beet greens can be added to salads for a boost of nutrients.
She sautéed the beet greens with garlic and olive oil for a healthy side dish.
The market had beautiful bunches of beet greens next to the root vegetables.
