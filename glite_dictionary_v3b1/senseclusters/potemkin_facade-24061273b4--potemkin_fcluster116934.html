Potemkin facade

noun
deceptive outward appearance
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtɛmkɪn fəˈsɑd / (AmE), / pəʊˈtɛm.kɪn fəˈsɑːd / (BrE)
A deceptive or superficial appearance or front, especially created to hide an unpleasant reality.

Examples

A Potemkin facade hides truth
Critics claim that the new reforms are a Potemkin facade designed to appease the public without real change.
The company's sustainability initiatives were little more than a Potemkin facade hiding ongoing environmental harm.
The leadership maintained a Potemkin facade of stability, though the organization was in chaos behind the scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: façade - The front of a building; a deceptive outward appearance
  • synonym: mask - Something that conceals reality
  • synonym: veneer - A superficially pleasing appearance covering the true nature
