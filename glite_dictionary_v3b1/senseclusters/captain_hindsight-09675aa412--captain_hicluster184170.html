Captain Hindsight

noun
one who criticizes with hindsight
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.tən ˈhaɪnd.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪn ˈhaɪnd.sʌɪt / (BrE)
A person who points out mistakes or criticizes decisions only after the outcome is known.

Examples

Captain Hindsight points mistakes
He acted as Captain Hindsight, blaming everyone after the project failed.
It's easy to be Captain Hindsight after the decision has already been made.
She didn't want to sound like Captain Hindsight, but she thought they should have planned ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Monday morning quarterback - a person who criticizes or judges something after the event
