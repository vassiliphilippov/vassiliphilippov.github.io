qua

preposition
in the role of
Pronunciation: / kwɑː / (AmE), / kwɑː / (BrE)
in the capacity or character of someone or something; as being.

Examples

As qua representative
He attended the meeting qua representative of the company.
The judge, qua interpreter of the law, must remain impartial.
She was invited qua expert to provide her opinion on the matter.
