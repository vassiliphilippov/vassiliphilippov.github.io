dowd

noun
unfashionable person
9.9
Pronunciation: / daʊd / (AmE), / daʊd / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or unfashionable person, especially a woman who dresses in a plain or dull way

Examples

An old-fashioned dowd
Despite her talents, some people unfairly called her a dowd because of her simple style.
He worried that his sister would be seen as a dowd at the party.
The magazine article argued that being a dowd is just a matter of perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dowdy - a person, especially a woman, who is unfashionably dressed
  • antonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion and stylish clothes
