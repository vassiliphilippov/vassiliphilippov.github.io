siloed

verb
to isolate groups
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪloʊd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪləʊd / (BrE)
to keep people, groups, or information separate so that they do not share resources or knowledge with each other

Examples

To siloed departments
The company siloed its marketing and sales teams, making collaboration difficult.
When departments are siloed, important information often fails to reach everyone who needs it.
Leaders worked to break down siloed structures and encourage more open communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: integrate - to combine or bring together different parts or groups
  • synonym: isolate - to keep something or someone apart from others
