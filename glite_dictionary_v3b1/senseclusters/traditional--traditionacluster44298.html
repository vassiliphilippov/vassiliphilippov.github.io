Synonyms

  • customary
    3.5 3/m
    customary describes actions people commonly do in a community, while traditional refers to long established customs and older ways.
  • conventional
    2.8 8/m
    conventional means following common rules or standards in society, while traditional emphasizes long established customs and family practices.
  • age-old
    4.2 2/m
    age-old describes something that has lasted for a very long time, while traditional refers to customs that are long but not necessarily ancient.
