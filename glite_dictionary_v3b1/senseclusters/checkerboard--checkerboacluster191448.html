checkerboard

noun
alternating-square pattern indicating transparency
3.7
Pronunciation: / \u02c8t\u0283\u025bk\u0259r\u02ccb\u0254rd / (AmE), / \u02c8t\u0283\u025bk\u0259b\u0254\u02d0d / (BrE)
a pattern of alternating colored squares (often gray and white) used in digital imaging and design to indicate transparent or empty areas behind an image.
