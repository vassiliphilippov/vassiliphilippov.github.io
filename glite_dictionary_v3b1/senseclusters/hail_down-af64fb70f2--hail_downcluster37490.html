hail down

verb
signal a vehicle to stop
9.3 3.7
Pronunciation: /heɪl daʊn/ (AmE), /heɪl daʊn/ (BrE)
to signal or call for a vehicle, especially a taxi or bus, to stop so that you can get in

Examples

To hail down a taxi
It took her several minutes to hail down a taxi in the rain.
Tourists often struggle to hail down a cab during rush hour.
You can hail down a bus on this route if there's a safe spot to stop.
