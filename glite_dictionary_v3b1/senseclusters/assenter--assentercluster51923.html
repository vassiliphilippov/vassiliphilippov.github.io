assenter

noun
a person who gives assent or approval
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛntər / (AmE), / əˈsentə / (BrE)
a person who agrees or gives approval or assent to something

Examples

An assenter agrees
As the proposal was read aloud, every assenter nodded in agreement.
She was known as a frequent assenter at public meetings, always supporting new initiatives.
The committee was divided, with only one assenter and several who opposed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agree - to have the same opinion, to assent
  • antonym: dissenter - a person who disagrees or refuses to assent
