passer

noun
person accepted as another ethnicity/race
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsə / (BrE)
a person who is accepted or claims to be a member of a different ethnic or racial group than their own, often to avoid discrimination.
