unattuned

adjective
not in harmony
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈtuːnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈtjuːnd / (BrE)
not aware of, responsive to, or in harmony with something or someone

Examples

Unattuned to feelings
He was completely unattuned to the emotions of those around him.
The new manager seemed unattuned to the company's culture and values.
Children may feel misunderstood if parents are unattuned to their needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attuned - aware of and responsive to something
  • synonym: inattentive - not paying attention
  • synonym: insensitive - not noticing or caring about others' feelings or needs
