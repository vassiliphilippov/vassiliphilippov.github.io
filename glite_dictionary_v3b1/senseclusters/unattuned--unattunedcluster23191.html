unattuned

adjective
not in harmony
7.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.ˈtuːnd/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈtuːnd/ /; / /ʌn.ə.ˈtjuːnd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.ˈtjuːnd/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈtjuːnd/ (BrE)

Examples
