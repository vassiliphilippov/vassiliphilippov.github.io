Africana

noun
African studies field
Pronunciation: / ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnə / (AmE), / ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
an academic field focused on the history, culture, politics, and experiences of African peoples and the African diaspora

Examples

Africana studies program
She is passionate about Africana and hopes to contribute to research on African cultures.
The university offers a wide range of Africana courses covering history, literature, and politics.
Many students choose Africana as their major to better understand the African diaspora.

Related Terms

  • synonym: African studies - the study of the history and cultures of Africa
  • synonym: diaspora studies - the study of dispersed communities and their cultures
