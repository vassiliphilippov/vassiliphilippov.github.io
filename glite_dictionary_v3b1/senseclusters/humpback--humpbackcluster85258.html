humpback

noun
person with a hunched back
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmpˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhʌmp.bæk / (BrE)
a person whose back is hunched due to an abnormal curvature of the upper spine; someone with a pronounced hunchback

Examples

A humpback person
In Victorian times, people would sometimes hide their humpbacks under layers of clothing.
Medical advances have made it possible to treat spinal deformities that cause a humpback.
The old man was a humpback, walking slowly with the aid of a cane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunchback - a person with a hunchback (hump on the back)
