neighborhood

noun
surrounding area or locality
1.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪ.bər.hʊd/ (AmE), /ˈneɪ.bə.hʊd/ (BrE)
an area or community within a city or town, especially where people live near each other

Examples

Quiet neighborhood
Let me know if you are ever in my neighborhood and want to stop by.
There are several good restaurants in the neighborhood of the university.
We heard sirens coming from the neighborhood near the river last night.
After moving to a new neighborhood, he quickly made friends with his neighbors.
She grew up in a quiet neighborhood on the edge of the city.
The entire neighborhood came together for the annual block party.
Everyone in our neighborhood knows each other and often gathers for events.
She moved to a different neighborhood to be closer to her office.
The new restaurant quickly became popular throughout the entire neighborhood.
