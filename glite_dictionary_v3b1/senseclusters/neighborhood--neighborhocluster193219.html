Synonyms

  • locality
    3.9 1/m
    locality is a named area often used in maps or reports, while neighborhood is a lived-in part of a town.
  • community
    2.8 100/m
    community is a group of people who share life or interests, while neighborhood is the local area where those people live.
  • residential area
    2.1 0.8/m
    residential area has mainly houses and flats for people to live in, while neighborhood may also include shops and streets.
  • residential district
    3.1 0.06/m
    residential district is a larger, often official part of a town made of homes, while neighborhood feels smaller and more local.
  • vicinity
    3.7 6/m
    vicinity means the area close to a place in general, while neighborhood is the specific part where people live near each other.
