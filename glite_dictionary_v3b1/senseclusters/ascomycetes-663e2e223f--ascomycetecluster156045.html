Ascomycetes

noun
class of sac fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæskəˈmaɪsitiːz /; / ˌæskoʊˈmaɪsitiːz / (AmE), / ˌæskəʊˈmaɪsiːtiːz / (BrE)
a large class of fungi, known as sac fungi, that produce spores in sac-like structures called asci
