Ascomycetes

noun
class of sac fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: /æs.kə.ˈmaɪ.siːts/ (AmE), /æs.kə.ˈmaɪ.siːts/ (BrE)
a large class of fungi, known as sac fungi, that produce spores in sac-like structures called asci
