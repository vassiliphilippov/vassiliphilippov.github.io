fenestration

noun
surgical creation of an opening in the cochlea
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛnəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɛnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which a new opening is made into the cochlea to restore hearing lost due to osteosclerosis

Examples

Ear fenestration surgery
Advances in fenestration techniques have improved outcomes for people with otosclerosis.
After his fenestration surgery, John noticed a significant improvement in his hearing.
The surgeon performed a fenestration to help the patient regain some hearing ability.
