Xanthium

noun
a genus of coarse, burr-producing herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈzænθiəm / (AmE), / ˈzanθɪəm / (BrE)
a genus of coarse herbs with small heads of greenish flowers that produce burrs covered with hooked bristles

The Xanthium plant
