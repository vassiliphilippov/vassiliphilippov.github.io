friendly relationship

noun
state of friendship or goodwill
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛn(d)li rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn(d)li rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a relationship between people who are friends or act in a kindly and supportive way toward each other

Examples

A friendly relationship between neighbors
A friendly relationship can help resolve misunderstandings before they become serious.
The two companies developed a friendly relationship over years of cooperation.
Their friendly relationship made working together much easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friendship - the state of being friends
  • antonym: hostile relationship - a relationship marked by hostility or antagonism
