drag-ass

noun
a lazy or unmotivated person (slang)
Pronunciation: / dræɡ æs / (AmE), / dræɡ ɑːs / (BrE)
A person who is excessively dull, lazy, or unmotivated.

Examples

A drag-ass person
Don’t be such a drag-ass; let’s have some fun!
He's known as a drag-ass at work, never getting much done.
Nobody wants a drag-ass on their team when things need to move quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: go-getter - a person who is energetic and determined to succeed
  • synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
