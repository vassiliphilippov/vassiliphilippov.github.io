recap

verb
summarize briefly
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈriː.kæp / (BrE)
to repeat or summarize the main points of something briefly

Examples

To recap the story
At the end of the meeting, she recapped the key decisions for everyone's benefit.
Could you recap what happened in yesterday's episode?
Let me quickly recap the main findings of the report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recapitulate - state again the main points; summarize
  • synonym: summarize - give a brief statement of the main points
