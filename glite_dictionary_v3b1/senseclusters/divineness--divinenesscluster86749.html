divineness

noun
quality of being divine
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪnnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being divine, godlike, or having the nature of a deity

Examples

The divineness of gods
The artist tried to express the divineness of the goddess in her sculpture.
Many religions teach about the divineness of certain beings or forces.
He was awed by the sense of divineness that filled the ancient temple.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divinity - the state or quality of being divine
  • synonym: godliness - the quality of being like a god or showing reverence for God
  • antonym: mortality - the state of being mortal or human
