mental test

noun
standardized psychological or intelligence test
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.tl tɛst / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl tɛst / (BrE)
a standardized procedure used to measure an individual's sensitivity, memory, intelligence, aptitude, personality, or similar mental attributes

Examples

Take a mental test
Before being accepted, all candidates must take a mental test to assess their cognitive abilities.
She excelled in every mental test administered during the research study.
The school's mental tests help identify students who may need additional support.
