broom sedge

noun
tall grassy plant of southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈbrum ˌsɛdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbruːm ˌsɛdʒ / (BrE)
a tall, tufted grass found in the southeastern United States, often growing in old fields and meadows
