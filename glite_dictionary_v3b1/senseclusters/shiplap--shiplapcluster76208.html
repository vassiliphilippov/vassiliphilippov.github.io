shiplap

verb
to cover with boards
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpˌlæp / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌlæp / (BrE)
to cover a wall or surface with shiplap boards, typically for decorative or protective purposes

Examples

To shiplap a wall
They decided to shiplap the kitchen walls to give the space a farmhouse feel.
The contractor will shiplap the exterior to improve both appearance and weather resistance.
Many homeowners choose to shiplap accent walls for a rustic look.
