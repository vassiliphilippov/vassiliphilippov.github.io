neuroscientist

noun
scientist studying the nervous system
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who specializes in the study of the nervous system, especially the brain.

Examples

A neuroscientist studies brain
As a leading neuroscientist, he has published numerous studies on brain plasticity.
Her work as a neuroscientist involves researching how memories are formed in the brain.
The neuroscientist explained the effects of sleep on neural activity during the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurobiologist - a scientist specializing in the biology of the nervous system
  • degree lower: psychologist - a scientist who studies mental processes and behavior
