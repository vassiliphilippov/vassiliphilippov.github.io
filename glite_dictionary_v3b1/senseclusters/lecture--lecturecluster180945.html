lecture

verb
deliver an educational talk
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛktʃə / (BrE)
to deliver an educational talk to an audience, especially as a part of teaching at a university or college

Examples

To lecture on history

He was invited to lecture at an international conference next month.
She has lectured at several prestigious universities across Europe.
