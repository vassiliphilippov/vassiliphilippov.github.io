Synonyms

  • estate agent
    2.6 2.65/m
    estate agent is the common British term for property sales and lettings, while real estate agent is standard elsewhere.
  • real estate broker
    3.7 0.73/m
    real estate broker is a licensed professional who may run a brokerage, while real estate agent usually acts for buyers or sellers.
  • house agent
    2.7 0.01/m
    house agent arranges sale or letting mainly of houses, while real estate agent covers houses, land, and other properties.
