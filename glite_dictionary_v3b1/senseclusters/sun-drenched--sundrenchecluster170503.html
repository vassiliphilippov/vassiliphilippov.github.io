sun-drenched

adjective
full of sunlight; bathed in sunshine
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn.drɛntʃt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.drɛntʃt / (BrE)
Receiving or covered in a large amount of sunlight; bathed in sunshine.

Examples

A sun-drenched room
He dreamed of living in a sun-drenched apartment overlooking the city skyline.
The photos show a sun-drenched terrace filled with colorful flowers and comfortable chairs.
They spent their vacation on a sun-drenched tropical island, relaxing by the sea.
