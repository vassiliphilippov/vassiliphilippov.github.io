photosphere

noun
360° spherical photograph
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.təs.fɪr/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təs.fɪə/ (BrE)
a spherical 360-degree photograph or image that lets the viewer look in all directions from a single viewpoint.

Examples

360-degree photosphere
She uploaded a stunning photosphere of the plaza for virtual visitors.
Travel bloggers often include interactive photospheres to give readers a sense of place.
Using a special app, he stitched several shots into a seamless photosphere of the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panorama - a wide-angle view or representation of a physical space; panoramas may be partial or full 360°
  • synonym: spherical panorama - a panoramic image projected onto a sphere to provide a full 360° view
