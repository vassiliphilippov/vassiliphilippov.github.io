go home

verb
return to one's home
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ hoʊm / (AmE), / ɡəʊ həʊm / (BrE)
to return to one's home or place of residence

Examples

To go home early

She usually goes home by train every evening.
After the movie ended, everyone decided to go home.
It's getting late, so maybe we should go home now.
She was tired and wanted nothing more than to go home and rest.

  • antonym: leave home - to depart from one's home
