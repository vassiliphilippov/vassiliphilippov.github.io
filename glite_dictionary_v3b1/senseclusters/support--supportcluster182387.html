support

noun
musical background part
4.2 2.54/m
Pronunciation: / s…ôÀàp…îrt / (AmE), / s…ôÀàp…îÀêt / (BrE)
a musical part, either vocal or instrumental, that provides background or accompaniment for other musical parts

Examples

Support vocals
Her voice is strong enough for lead roles, but she often sings support in the chorus.
In the arrangement, the strings act as a gentle support behind the main melody.
The choir provided support for the soloist during the performance.

Synonyms

    5.1 2.02/m

  • backup
    3.1 3.07/m
    backup provides extra or reserve musical parts often used in recordings, while support is the broader term for accompaniment.
