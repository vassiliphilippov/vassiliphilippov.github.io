pen

noun
writing instrument with ink
Pronunciation: / pɛn / (AmE), / pɛn / (BrE)
an instrument for writing or drawing with ink, typically consisting of a metal nib or ball that dispenses ink onto paper

Examples

A blue pen
He handed me a pen to fill out the application.
I prefer to write my notes in blue pen rather than pencil.
She pulled a pen from her bag and began signing the forms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pencil - a writing instrument using graphite instead of ink
