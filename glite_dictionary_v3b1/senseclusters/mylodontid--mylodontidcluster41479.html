mylodontid

noun
extinct ground sloth of the Mylodontidae family
Pronunciation: / maɪˈlɒdəntɪd / (AmE), / mɪˈlɒdəntɪd / (BrE)
an extinct edentate mammal belonging to the family Mylodontidae, related to ground sloths and known from fossil records in the Americas.

Examples

A mylodontid skeleton
A large mylodontid skeleton is displayed at the natural history museum.
Scientists believe the mylodontid adapted to a variety of habitats during the Ice Age.
The fossilized remains of a mylodontid were discovered in the Patagonian caves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ground sloth - an extinct large herbivorous sloth of the Americas
