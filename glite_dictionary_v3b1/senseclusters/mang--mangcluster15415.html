mang

noun
slang for man
9.9
Pronunciation: / mæŋ / (AmE), / mæŋ / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for 'man' or 'dude', especially used in Caribbean English

Examples

Hey mang
He greeted his friend with a cheerful 'What's up, mang?'
In some Caribbean communities, people often call each other mang as a sign of friendship.
You hear the word mang a lot in reggae songs and street conversations.
