counterbattery

verb
to attack enemy artillery
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈbætəri / (BrE)
to attack or suppress enemy artillery by firing at its positions or directing fire against it

Examples

To counterbattery enemy guns
The army decided to counterbattery the enemy guns to reduce incoming fire.
Commanders ordered their units to counterbattery after detecting hostile artillery positions.
Effective counterbattery operations can quickly silence enemy cannons on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bombard - to attack with continuous artillery fire
  • antonym: defend - to protect from attack
