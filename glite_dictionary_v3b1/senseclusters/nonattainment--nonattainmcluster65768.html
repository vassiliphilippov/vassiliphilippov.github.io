nonattainment

adjective
not meeting standards
8.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ə.ˈteɪn.mənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ə.ˈteɪn.mənt/ (BrE)
not meeting a required standard, especially used to describe areas that do not meet official air quality standards.

Examples

Nonattainment area status
The city was classified as a nonattainment area due to high levels of air pollution.
New regulations target nonattainment regions to help them achieve cleaner air standards.
Industries in nonattainment zones must take extra steps to reduce emissions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - meeting required standards or regulations
  • synonym: noncompliant - not following rules or standards
