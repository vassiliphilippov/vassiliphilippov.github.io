bruise

verb
crush food into small pieces
4.7 3.0
Pronunciation: / bruːz / (AmE), / bruːz / (BrE)
to damage or crush fruit, vegetables, or plant tissue so that it becomes soft or discolored

Examples

To bruise fruit
Gently bruise the mint leaves before adding them to the drink to release their flavor.
Some recipes suggest you lightly bruise the garlic cloves instead of chopping them.
To make the sauce, first bruise the berries with a fork.
Be careful not to bruise the apples when packing them for shipping.
Rough handling can easily bruise soft fruits like peaches and plums.
She bruised the mint leaves gently to release their flavor before adding them to the drink.
