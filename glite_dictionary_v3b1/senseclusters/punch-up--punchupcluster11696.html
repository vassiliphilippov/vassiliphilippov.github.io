punch-up

noun
adding or sharpening jokes in a script
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌntʃ.ʌp/ /; / /ˈpʌnʃ.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈpʌntʃ.ʌp/ /; / /ˈpʌnʃ.ʌp/ (BrE)
