tea wagon

noun
serving cart for tea or refreshments
5.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ti ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / tiː ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
A small cart on wheels, used for serving tea or light refreshments.

Examples

A tea wagon with treats

The silver teapot stood on the tea wagon next to plates of sandwiches and cakes.
We brought out the tea wagon when our guests arrived for afternoon tea.
