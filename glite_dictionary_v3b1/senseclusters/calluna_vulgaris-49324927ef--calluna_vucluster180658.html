Calluna vulgaris

noun
evergreen shrub; common heather
Pronunciation: / kəˈlunə vʌlˈɡɛrɪs / (AmE), / kəˈluːnə vʌlˈɡɛːrɪs / (BrE)
A low evergreen shrub native to Europe and Asia, commonly known as heather, cultivated widely for its pink or purple flowers.
