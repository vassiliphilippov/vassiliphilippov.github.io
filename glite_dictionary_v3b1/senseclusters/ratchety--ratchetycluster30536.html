ratchety

adjective
noisy and jerky
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrætʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈrætʃɪti / (BrE)
making a harsh, irregular noise or moving in a jerky, rough way, like a worn-out machine

Examples

Ratchety machine

He drove a ratchety old truck that rattled down the road.
After years of use, the bicycle had become quite ratchety and hard to ride.
