Made difficult or slowed down; unable to progress freely.

Examples

Heavily impeded progress
Heavy traffic meant our journey was impeded by frequent stops and delays.
Her recovery was impeded by a lack of proper nutrition and rest.
The construction work left the walkway impeded by barriers and equipment.

Synonyms

  • obstructed
    4.6 0.01/m
    obstructed is often a physical, stronger blocking that stops movement, while impeded means slowed or made more difficult.
  • bogged down
    5.8 1.28/m
    bogged down describes being stuck by details or problems and unable to move, while impeded means slowed but not always completely stuck.
