butterfly ray

noun
a broad, flat stingray with a short tail
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ ˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪ ˌreɪ / (BrE)
A stingray of the family Gymnuridae, characterized by a very broad, flat body and a short tail.

Examples

A butterfly ray fish
Divers spotted a group of butterfly rays near the coral reef.
The butterfly ray glided gracefully over the sandy ocean floor.
Unlike many other rays, the butterfly ray has an extremely short tail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stingray - a large, flat sea fish with a long, thin tail, often with a poisonous sting
