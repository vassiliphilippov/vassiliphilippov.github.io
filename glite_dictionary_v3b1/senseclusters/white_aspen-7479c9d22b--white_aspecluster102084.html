white aspen

noun
poplar tree with white bark and whitish leaves
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈæspən / (AmE), / waɪt ˈaspən / (BrE)
A type of poplar tree native to or widely cultivated in the United States, with white bark and leaves that have whitish undersides.

Examples

A white aspen tree
A row of white aspen trees was planted along the property line for shade and beauty.
In autumn, the leaves of the white aspen turn a brilliant yellow before falling.
The forest was full of white aspen, their bark gleaming brightly in the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poplar - a type of tall tree of the genus Populus
