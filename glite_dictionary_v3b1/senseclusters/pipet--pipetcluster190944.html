pipet

verb
to transfer liquid
Pronunciation: / paɪˈpɛt / (AmE), / paɪˈpɛt / (BrE)
to use a pipette to measure or transfer a small amount of liquid

Examples

Always wear gloves when you pipette chemicals to avoid skin contact.
She spent the afternoon pipetting samples for the experiment.
