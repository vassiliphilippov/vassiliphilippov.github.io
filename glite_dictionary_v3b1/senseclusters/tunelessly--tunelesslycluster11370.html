tunelessly

adverb
without melody or tune
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːn.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈtjuːn.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner lacking melody or musical quality; not producing a tune.

Examples

To chant tunelessly
A group of children chanted tunelessly in the back of the classroom.
She began to hum tunelessly while focusing on her artwork.
The radio played softly as he sang tunelessly along with the melody.
