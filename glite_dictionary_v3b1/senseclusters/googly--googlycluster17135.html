googly

adjective
silly or bulging-eyed
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːɡli / (AmE), / ˈɡuːɡli / (BrE)
looking silly or exaggerated, especially with large, rolling, or protruding eyes

Examples

Googly eyes
The cartoon character had googly eyes that made everyone laugh.
She wore a hat decorated with googly eyes for the costume party.
The toy's googly expression made it look both funny and friendly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug-eyed - having large, protruding eyes
  • antonym: normal - not exaggerated or silly in appearance
