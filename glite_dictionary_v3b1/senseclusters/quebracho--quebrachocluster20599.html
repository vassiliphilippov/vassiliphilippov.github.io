quebracho

noun
hardwood South American tree
Pronunciation: / keɪˈbrɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / keɪˈbrætʃəʊ / (BrE)
a South American tree with extremely hard wood, often used for tannin extraction and durable timber

Examples

A quebracho tree
The tannin industry relies on quebracho for its high-quality extract.
Many railway sleepers in South America are made from quebracho because of its durability.
The craftsman admired the strength and density of the quebracho he used for his project.
