rukh

noun
giant mythical bird
Pronunciation: / rʊk / (AmE), / rʊk / (BrE)
a giant mythical bird from Middle Eastern legends, said to be large enough to carry off elephants

Examples

A giant rukh bird
In the ancient tale, a rukh swooped down and carried away a large animal.
Travelers spoke in awe of the enormous rukh they believed lived in distant mountains.
Legends say the rukh was so powerful it could lift an elephant with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: griffin - a mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle
  • synonym: roc - a legendary giant bird in Middle Eastern mythology
