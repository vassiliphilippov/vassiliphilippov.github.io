meet the deadline

verb
finish on time; by a set date
Pronunciation: / mit ðə ˈdɛdlaɪn / (AmE), / miːt ðə ˈdɛdlaɪn / (BrE)
to finish a task or project by the time it is due

Examples

The team worked overtime to meet the deadline for the new software release.
