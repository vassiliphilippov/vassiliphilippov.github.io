dentist

noun
a person qualified to treat teeth and gums
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tɪst / (BrE)
A person who is qualified to examine and treat people's teeth and gums.

Examples

The dentist office
I have an appointment with the dentist next week to check my teeth.
Many children are nervous when they visit the dentist for the first time.
The dentist recommended brushing twice a day for healthy gums.
