be seen dead

verb
refuse to be involved due to embarrassment or dislike
Pronunciation: / bi sin dɛd / (AmE), / bi siːn ded / (BrE)
to refuse to be present at, involved with, or associated with something, especially because it is considered embarrassing or beneath one's standards

Examples
