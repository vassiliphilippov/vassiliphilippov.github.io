hasher

noun
food chopping tool
Pronunciation: / ˈhæʃər / (AmE), / ˈhæʃə / (BrE)
a kitchen machine or tool used for mincing or chopping food to make hash or similar dishes

Examples

Food hasher machine
The chef used a hasher to quickly prepare the potatoes for the breakfast hash.
After buying a new hasher, she found it much easier to chop vegetables for soups.
In the restaurant kitchen, the old hasher was replaced with a more efficient model.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grinder - a device for crushing or grinding food into smaller pieces
  • synonym: mincer - a machine or tool for cutting food, especially meat, into small pieces
