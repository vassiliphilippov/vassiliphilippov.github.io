inti

noun
former currency unit of Peru
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈinti / (AmE), / ˈinti / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Peru from 1985 to 1991, replaced by the nuevo sol

Examples

The inti currency
In the late 1980s, Peru's economy struggled under hyperinflation of the inti.
The inti was replaced by the nuevo sol due to massive devaluation.
Tourists visiting Lima before 1991 would have paid for goods using the inti.
