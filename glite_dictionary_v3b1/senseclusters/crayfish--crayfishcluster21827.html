crayfish

noun
large spiny marine crustacean (no big pincers)
4.8 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkreɪ.fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈkreɪ.fɪʃ/ (BrE)
a large edible marine or warm-water crustacean with a spiny shell and no large pincers, resembling a lobster but found in oceans or coastal waters
