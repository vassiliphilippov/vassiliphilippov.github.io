mid-December

noun
the middle part of December
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪd dɪˈsɛmbɚ / (AmE), / ˌmɪd dɪˈsɛmbə / (BrE)
the middle part of the month of December, typically around the 13th to 19th

Examples

Cold weather in mid-December
The weather turned unusually cold in mid-December last year.
They plan to finish the construction by mid-December.
We usually host our annual party in mid-December.
