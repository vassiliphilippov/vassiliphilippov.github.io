pyrazinamide

noun
tuberculosis drug
Pronunciation: / paɪˌræzɪˈnæmaɪd / (AmE), / paɪˌræzɪˈnæmaɪd / (BrE)
a drug used in combination with other medications to treat tuberculosis

Examples

Pyrazinamide drug treatment
The doctor prescribed pyrazinamide to help treat her tuberculosis.
Patients with tuberculosis often take pyrazinamide along with other medications for several months.
Some people may experience side effects when taking pyrazinamide as part of their treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isoniazid - a drug used to treat tuberculosis
  • synonym: rifampicin - an antibiotic used in tuberculosis treatment
