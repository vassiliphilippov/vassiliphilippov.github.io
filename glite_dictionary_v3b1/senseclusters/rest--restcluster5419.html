rest

verb
remain in a particular state
Pronunciation: / rɛst / (AmE), / rɛst / (BrE)
(of a thing or state) to stay the same or remain unchanged; to continue to be in a particular condition.

Examples

The roads rest covered
Even after the storm had passed, the roads rested covered in snow for days.
Let the decision rest until we have more information.
You can rest assured that your application will be carefully reviewed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remain - to continue to exist, especially after other parts or things have been removed, used, or destroyed
  • synonym: stay - to continue to be in a particular place or condition
