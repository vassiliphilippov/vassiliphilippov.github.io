unpatriotic

adjective
not showing love for one's country
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌpeɪtriˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpætriˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
not showing love or loyalty towards your own country; lacking patriotism.

Examples

Unpatriotic remarks
He was accused of making unpatriotic remarks about the war.
Some viewed the protest as unpatriotic during a time of national crisis.
Waving a foreign flag at the event was seen by many as unpatriotic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disloyal - not loyal to a person, country, or cause
  • antonym: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to one's country
