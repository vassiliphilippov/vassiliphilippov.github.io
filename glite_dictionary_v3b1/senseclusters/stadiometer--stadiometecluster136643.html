stadiometer

noun
height measuring device
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsteɪdiˈɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌsteɪdiˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to measure a person's height accurately, typically in a medical or clinical setting

Examples

Medical stadiometer
The doctor used a stadiometer to check the patient's height during the annual exam.
Children are often measured with a stadiometer at their regular health checkups.
A stadiometer provides more accurate height measurements than a wall ruler.

Related Terms

  • synonym: measuring tape - a flexible ruler used to measure length or distance
  • antonym: scale - a device for measuring weight or mass
