Examples

Synonyms

  • in agreement
    2.1 5.41/m
    in agreement is a common phrase for sharing the same opinion in speech and writing, while concurring is a more formal adjective.
  • agreed
    1.0 0.00/m
    agreed describes a decision or opinion reached together, while concurring focuses on people or opinions agreeing at the same time.
  • concordant
    7.3 0.32/m

  • in concert
    4.1 2.13/m
