electrolyze

verb
to use electric current for chemical change
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktroʊˌlaɪz /; / ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
