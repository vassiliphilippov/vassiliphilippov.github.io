Eurotium

noun
genus of fungi in Eurotiales
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊtiəm / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊtiəm / (BrE)
a genus of fungi found in food, soil, and indoor environments, known for causing food spoilage and sometimes used in fermentation
