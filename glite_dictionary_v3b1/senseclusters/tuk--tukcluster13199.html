tuk

noun
light tapping sound
9.9
a light, sharp knock or short tapping sound, especially on a door or surface

Examples

A soft tuk on the door
She heard a soft tuk on the window late at night.
A gentle tuk at the door signaled that someone was waiting outside.
The pencil made a quick tuk as it fell onto the desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock - a sound made by hitting a surface, especially a door
  • synonym: tap - a light touch or knock, especially with a finger or small object
