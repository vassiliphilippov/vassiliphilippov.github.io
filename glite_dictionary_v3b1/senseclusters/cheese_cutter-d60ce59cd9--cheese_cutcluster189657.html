cheese cutter

noun
utensil for slicing cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiz ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːz ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil consisting of a board or handle with a wire or blade, used for slicing cheese

Examples

A cheese cutter tool
She used a cheese cutter to slice perfect pieces for the platter.
The wire on my old cheese cutter snapped, so I had to buy a new one.
You get more even slices with a cheese cutter than with an ordinary knife.
