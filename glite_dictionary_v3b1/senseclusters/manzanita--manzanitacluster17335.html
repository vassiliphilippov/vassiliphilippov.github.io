manzanita

noun
evergreen shrub or tree with red berries
Pronunciation: / ˌmænzəˈniːtə / (AmE), / ˌmænzəˈniːtə / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree native to western North America, with glossy leaves, smooth reddish bark, and small edible red berries
