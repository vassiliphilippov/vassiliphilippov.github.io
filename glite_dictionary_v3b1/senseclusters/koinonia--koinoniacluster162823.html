koinonia

noun
Christian fellowship or spiritual communion
Pronunciation: / kɔɪˈnoʊniə / (AmE), / kɔɪˈnəʊniə / (BrE)
Christian fellowship or spiritual communion with God or fellow believers, especially as characteristic of the early Christian church.

Examples

Christian koinonia fellowship
Early Christians placed a strong emphasis on koinonia, gathering for prayer and shared meals.

The sense of koinonia among the congregation created a welcoming community for new members.
