crunchingly

adverb
with a loud crisp sound
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that produces a loud, crisp sound when chewed or crushed

Examples

Eat crunchingly loud
He ate the carrot crunchingly, making everyone in the room turn their heads.
The snow packed crunchingly under their boots as they walked through the forest.
She munched crunchingly on the fresh celery during her lunch break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a way that makes a lot of noise
  • antonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle way
