codename

verb
to assign secret name
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdˌneɪm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdˌneɪm / (BrE)
to give a secret or special name to something, especially for secrecy or confidentiality

Examples

To codename a mission
The military operation was codenamed Eagle to keep its purpose secret.
During development, the new phone was codenamed Project Aurora.
They codenamed the investigation to avoid drawing public attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alias - to give a false or alternative name
  • antonym: identify - to recognize or name someone or something
