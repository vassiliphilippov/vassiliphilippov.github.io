iliolumbar vein

noun
branch vein draining iliac/lumbar region
Pronunciation: / ɪliˌoʊˈlʌmbɚ veɪn / (AmE), / ɪlɪəʊˌlʌmbə veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that is a branch of the internal iliac vein and drains blood from the iliac and lumbar regions

Examples

The iliolumbar vein branch
A blockage in the iliolumbar vein can affect blood flow in the pelvic area.
During the surgery, special care was taken to avoid damaging the iliolumbar vein.
The iliolumbar vein drains blood from muscles in the lower back and pelvis.
