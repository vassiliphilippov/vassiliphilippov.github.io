state-sponsored terrorism

noun
terrorism supported by a government
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˌspɑnsɚd ˌtɛrərˈɪzəm / (AmE), / steɪt ˌspɒnsəd ˌtɛrərˈɪzəm / (BrE)
Terrorism actively supported or carried out by a government, either against its own people or by aiding terrorist activities abroad.

Examples

State-sponsored terrorism activities
Investigations revealed evidence linking the regime to state-sponsored terrorism in neighboring countries.
Many countries have been accused of state-sponsored terrorism to suppress dissent within their borders.
The international community condemned acts of state-sponsored terrorism following several high-profile incidents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in pursuit of political aims
