prekindergarten

adjective
before kindergarten
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌkɪndərˈɡɑːrtən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌkɪndəˈɡɑːtən / (BrE)
relating to children, programs, or activities for the stage before kindergarten.

Examples

Prekindergarten children
The new prekindergarten program helps children develop basic social and learning skills.
Many parents look for prekindergarten activities to prepare their children for school.
A prekindergarten classroom often focuses on play-based learning and early literacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preschool - relating to the period before a child starts school
