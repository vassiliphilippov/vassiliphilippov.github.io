chowder

noun
a thick soup or stew
4.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃaʊ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃaʊ.də/ (BrE)
A thick soup or stew typically made with milk, potatoes, onions, and sometimes bacon or seafood.
