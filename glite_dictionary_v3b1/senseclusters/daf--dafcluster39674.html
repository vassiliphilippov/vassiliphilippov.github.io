daf

noun
Middle Eastern frame drum
Pronunciation: / dɑːf / (AmE), / dɑːf / (BrE)
a large Middle Eastern frame drum used in traditional music, often played with the hands

Examples

The musician played a lively rhythm on the daf during the festival performance.
She learned to play the daf as part of her study of Persian music.
The sound of the daf echoed through the hall, adding energy to the dance.
