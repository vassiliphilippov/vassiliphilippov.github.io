subglacial

adjective
beneath a glacier
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈɡleɪʃəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈɡleɪsɪəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath a glacier

Examples

Subglacial rivers
Researchers discovered a network of subglacial rivers flowing beneath the Antarctic ice.
The team used radar to map the subglacial landscape hidden from view.
Unique microorganisms were found living in subglacial lakes deep below the glacier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: submarine - located or occurring under the sea
  • antonym: supraglacial - located or occurring on the surface of a glacier
