selvage

noun
border or edge of fabric, especially carpets
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.vɪdʒ /; / ˈsɛlvɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.vɪdʒ /; / ˈsɛlvɪdʒ / (BrE)
the finished edge of a piece of fabric, often woven to prevent fraying and sometimes decorated
