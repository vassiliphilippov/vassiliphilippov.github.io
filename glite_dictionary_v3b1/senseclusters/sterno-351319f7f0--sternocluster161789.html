Sterno

noun
canned gel fuel
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜːrnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈstɜːnəʊ / (BrE)
a brand of canned or jellied fuel (usually alcohol) used for heating food, especially at events; sometimes dangerously consumed as an intoxicant
