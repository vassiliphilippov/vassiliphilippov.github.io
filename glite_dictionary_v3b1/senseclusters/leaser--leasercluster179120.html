leaser

noun
person who leases
Pronunciation: / ˈliːsər / (AmE), / ˈliːsə / (BrE)
a person or company that takes a lease on property or goods; a lessee or tenant

Examples

Leaser of an apartment
The leaser agreed to maintain the apartment in good condition during the rental period.
As the new leaser of the office space, the company negotiated better terms with the landlord.
The contract clearly states the responsibilities of both the leaser and the property owner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lessee - a person who holds the lease of a property; tenant
  • antonym: lessor - a person or company that leases property to another; landlord
  • synonym: tenant - a person who rents property from a landlord
