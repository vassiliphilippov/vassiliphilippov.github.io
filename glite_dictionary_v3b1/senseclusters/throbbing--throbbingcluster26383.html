throbbing

adjective
pounding or pulsating strongly
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɑbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθrɒbɪŋ / (BrE)
pounding or beating with a strong, regular rhythm, especially so as to be felt or heard; pulsating intensely.

Examples

Throbbing pain
He pressed his hand to his throbbing wound as he waited for help.
She felt a throbbing pain in her temples after the long meeting.
The throbbing bass from the speakers filled the room with energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beating - striking repeatedly with a regular rhythm
  • synonym: pulsating - expanding and contracting with a strong regular movement
