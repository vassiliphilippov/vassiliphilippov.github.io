foreignly

adverb
in an unfamiliar way
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrənli / (AmE), / ˈfɒrənli / (BrE)
in a way that is foreign, unfamiliar, or not native to a place or group

Examples

Decorate foreignly
She decorated her apartment foreignly, using styles from many different countries.
The food was prepared foreignly, with spices the guests had never tasted before.
He behaved foreignly at the meeting, surprising his colleagues with his new mannerisms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: familiarly - in a way that is well known or easily recognized
  • synonym: strangely - in a way that is unusual or unfamiliar
