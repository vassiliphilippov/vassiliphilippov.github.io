car insurance

noun
insurance for cars against theft or accidents
Pronunciation: / kɑr ɪnˈʃʊrəns / (AmE), / kɑː ɪnˈʃɔːrəns / (BrE)
insurance that covers vehicles and their owners against losses from theft, accidents, or damage involving cars

Examples

Car insurance policy

You are legally required to have car insurance if you want to drive on public roads.
