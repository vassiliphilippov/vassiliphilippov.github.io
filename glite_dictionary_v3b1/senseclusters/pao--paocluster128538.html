pao

noun
Chinese ceremonial robe
Pronunciation: / paʊ / (AmE), / paʊ / (BrE)
a long traditional robe or gown worn in historical Chinese dress, often for ceremonial occasions

Examples

Ceremonial pao
During the parade, the performer wore a bright red pao decorated with golden patterns.
The museum displayed an ancient silk pao from the Ming dynasty.
He chose a blue pao for the traditional wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hanfu - traditional Chinese clothing from the Han dynasty
  • synonym: kimono - a traditional Japanese robe with wide sleeves
