bunkie

noun
bunk bed companion
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋki / (BrE)
a person who shares a bunk bed or sleeping space with another, especially in a dormitory or military setting

Examples

Camp bunkie
During summer camp, I quickly became friends with my bunkie.
My bunkie in the army slept heavily and left his boots by the ladder every morning.
After lights out, my bunkie and I would whisper stories until we fell asleep.
