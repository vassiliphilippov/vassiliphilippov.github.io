afternoon tea

noun
a light midafternoon meal with tea and snacks
2.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /æf.tər.ˈnuːn tiː/ /; / /əf.tər.ˈnuːn tiː/ (AmE), /ɑːf.tə.ˈnuːn tiː/ /; / /əf.tə.ˈnuːn tiː/ (BrE)
