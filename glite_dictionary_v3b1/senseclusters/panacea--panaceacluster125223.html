panacea

noun
universal cure or remedy
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /pæn.əˈsiː.ə/ (AmE), /pæn.əˈsiː.ə/ (BrE)
A remedy that is believed to cure all diseases or difficulties; a universal solution.

Examples

A panacea for problems
Many people mistakenly believe that technology is a panacea for all educational challenges.
The new policy was presented as a panacea for the country’s economic troubles, but it soon proved inadequate.
There is no panacea for the stress of modern life, but healthy habits can help.
