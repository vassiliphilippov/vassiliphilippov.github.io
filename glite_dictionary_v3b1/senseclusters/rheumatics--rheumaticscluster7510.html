rheumatics

noun
joint or muscle pain
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌruːməˈtɪks / (AmE), / ˌruːməˈtɪks / (BrE)
an old term for pain or disease affecting the joints or muscles, especially due to rheumatism

Examples

Pain from rheumatics
She complained that her rheumatics were worse in cold weather.
The old man said his rheumatics made it hard to get out of bed in the morning.
Doctors rarely use the term rheumatics today, preferring more specific diagnoses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthritis - inflammation of the joints causing pain and stiffness
  • synonym: rheumatism - pain and inflammation in the joints or muscles
