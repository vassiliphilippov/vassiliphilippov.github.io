collar

noun
object worn or placed around the neck
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlər /; / ˈkɑlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
a band or ring placed around an animal's neck, often for identification or restraint, or a similar object worn around the neck for decoration or ceremony

Examples

Dog collar
At the festival, each dancer received a flower collar as part of the ceremony.
In historical times, prisoners were sometimes punished with a heavy iron collar.
The dog wore a bright red collar with an identification tag.
After the kitten got loose, we searched the neighborhood for her blue collar.
All pets must wear a collar with identification when outside.
The dog wore a red collar with a small silver tag attached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necklace - an ornamental chain or string of beads, worn around the neck
