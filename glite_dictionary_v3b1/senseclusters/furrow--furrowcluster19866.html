furrow

verb
make creased or wrinkled
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜroʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːrəʊ / (BrE)
to make lines, wrinkles, or creases in something, especially on the skin or face

Examples

To furrow your brow
A deep frown furrowed his forehead when he heard the news.
She furrowed her brow in concentration as she read the instructions.
The old man furrowed his brow, recalling a distant memory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrinkle - to make a small line or fold in a surface, especially the skin
