Synonyms

  • succession
    6.3 3.86/m
    succession refers to the legal transfer of property or a title by law or will, while inheritance is what is received.
  • patrimony
    8.2 0.75/m
    patrimony is family property passed down through generations or left to institutions, while inheritance is what an heir receives.
  • heritage
    3.7 1.34/m
    heritage often means cultural traditions or historic places kept by a group, while inheritance usually means money or property received after death.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heritage - something inherited from the past, especially traditions or cultural property
  • synonym: legacy - something left to a person by a will
