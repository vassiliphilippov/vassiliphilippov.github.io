A meeting or discussion involving participants in different locations who are connected using telecommunications devices such as video or telephone links.

Examples

A teleconference meeting
Because of travel restrictions, the board meeting was conducted as a teleconference.
Our department holds a weekly teleconference to discuss project updates across offices.
She participated in the teleconference from her home office using her laptop.

Synonyms

  • telecon
    4.9 0.00/m
    telecon is an informal short name for a telephone or video meeting, while teleconference is the formal term for the same meeting.
  • teleconferencing
    3.9 0.18/m
    teleconferencing refers to the technology and process of meeting by phone or video, while teleconference names the meeting itself.
  • conferencing
    3.7 0.89/m
    conferencing describes the general practice of connecting people by audio or video, while teleconference refers to one specific meeting.
