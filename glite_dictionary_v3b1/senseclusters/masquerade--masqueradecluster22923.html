masquerade

noun
costume worn at a masquerade party
Pronunciation: / ˌmæskəˈreɪd / (AmE), / ˌmæskəˈreɪd / (BrE)
a costume worn as a disguise, especially at a masquerade party, often including a mask

Examples

A masquerade costume
Each guest wore an elaborate masquerade to conceal their identity.
He won the prize for best masquerade at the ball.
Her masquerade was so convincing that no one recognized her all evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costume - an outfit worn to create the appearance characteristic of a particular period, person, or place
  • synonym: disguise - something worn to conceal one's identity
