Landsat

proper noun
Earth observation satellites
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.sæt/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.sæt/ (BrE)
a series of American satellites (and the program operating them) that capture images of Earth's surface for mapping, scientific, and environmental monitoring

Examples

Scientists used Landsat data to track changes in the Amazon rainforest over several decades.
