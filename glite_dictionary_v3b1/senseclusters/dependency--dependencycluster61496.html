dependency

noun
state of relying on someone or something
1.5 3.6
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpen.dən.siː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpen.dən.siː/ (BrE)
The state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else for support or existence.

Examples

Financial dependency
Her financial dependency on her parents made it difficult for her to move out on her own.
Many people are trying to break free from their dependency on smartphones.
The company’s dependency on a single supplier created risks for their supply chain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independence - the state of being free from outside control; self-sufficiency
  • synonym: reliance - dependence on or trust in someone or something
