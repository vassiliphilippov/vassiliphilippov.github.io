Guinea-Bissau peso

noun
former currency unit of Guinea-Bissau
Pronunciation: / ˌgɪni bɪˈsaʊ ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌgɪni bɪˈsaʊ ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Guinea-Bissau, equal to 100 centavos, used between 1975 and 1997

Examples

The Guinea-Bissau peso banknotes
Banknotes of the Guinea-Bissau peso have become rare collector's items.
Historians often mention the Guinea-Bissau peso when discussing the country's economic past.
The Guinea-Bissau peso was replaced by the West African CFA franc in 1997.
