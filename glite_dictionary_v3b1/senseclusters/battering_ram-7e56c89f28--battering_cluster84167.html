battering ram

noun
device for breaking down doors or walls
4.8 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.ər.ɪŋ ræm/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.ər.ɪŋ ræm/ (BrE)
a heavy object used to break down doors or walls, or a person or thing that forcefully overcomes obstacles

Examples

He acted as a battering ram during negotiations, relentlessly pushing his agenda.
