Aristolochiales

noun
order of flowering plants (botanical term)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrɪˌstoʊlɒˈkaɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌærɪˌstɒlɒˈkɪːliːz / (BrE)
