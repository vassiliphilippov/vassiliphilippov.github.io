large-cap

adjective
of stocks from high market capitalization companies
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrdʒ kæp / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒ kæp / (BrE)
(of stocks) belonging to companies with a market capitalization of five billion dollars or more; relating to such companies.

Examples

Large-cap stocks

The fund shifted its focus from large-cap companies to emerging mid-cap firms this year.
