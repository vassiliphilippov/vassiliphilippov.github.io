fluidic

adjective
smooth and flowing
6.7
Pronunciation: / fluˈɪdɪk / (AmE), / fluˈɪdɪk / (BrE)
having smooth, flowing, or continuously changing movement or form, like a fluid in motion.

Examples

Fluidic movements
The dancer's fluidic movements captivated everyone in the audience.
The design of the car features a fluidic shape that reduces air resistance.
Her speech had a fluidic rhythm, making it pleasant to listen to.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flowing - moving smoothly and continuously
  • antonym: rigid - not flexible or flowing; stiff
