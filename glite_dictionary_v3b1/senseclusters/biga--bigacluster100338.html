biga

noun
Italian bread starter
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːɡə / (AmE), / ˈbiːɡə / (BrE)
a type of Italian bread starter made from flour, water, and yeast, used to improve flavor and texture

Examples

Italian biga starter
The baker prepared a biga the night before making the dough.
Using a biga gives Italian bread a richer flavor and a chewier texture.
She learned to make a traditional biga for her homemade ciabatta.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poolish - a wet bread starter of Polish origin
  • synonym: sourdough - a bread starter made from fermented flour and water
