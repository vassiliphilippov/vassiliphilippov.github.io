this

determiner
nearby reference
1.0
Pronunciation: / ðɪs / (AmE), / ðɪs / (BrE)
used to refer to a person, thing, or idea that is near in place, time, or context

Examples

This book
This is the book I was talking about.
Can you hand me this pen on the table?
This morning was colder than I expected.
