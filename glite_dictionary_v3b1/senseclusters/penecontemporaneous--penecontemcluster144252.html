penecontemporaneous

adjective
nearly same age
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːniˌkɑːntɛm.pəˈreɪ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌpiːniˌkɒntɛm.pəˈreɪ.ni.əs / (BrE)
formed or occurring at almost the same time as something else, especially in geology

Examples

penecontemporaneous fossils
The fossils in these two layers are penecontemporaneous, indicating they were deposited almost simultaneously.
Geologists determined that the volcanic eruptions were penecontemporaneous based on radiometric dating.
The sedimentary deposits are considered penecontemporaneous because of their closely matching ages.
