rose-mauve

adjective
mauve color with a rosy tint
Pronunciation: / roʊz mɔːv / (AmE), / rəʊz mɔːv / (BrE)
of a mauve color tinged with rose or pink; a shade of mauve with a rosy hue

Examples

Rose-mauve color shade
Artists often blend pink and violet to achieve the desired rose-mauve shade in their paintings.
She chose a rose-mauve dress for the spring wedding, which suited her complexion beautifully.
The drapes were a soft rose-mauve that complemented the room’s gentle lighting.

  • degree higher: rose - a shade of pink resembling the color of a rose
