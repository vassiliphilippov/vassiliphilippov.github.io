whiny-ass titty baby

noun
offensive slang for an overly sensitive, childish complainer
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪni ˌæs ˈtɪti ˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪni ˌæs ˈtɪti ˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
A highly offensive slang term for someone who is excessively sensitive, immature, or complains in a childish way.

Calling your boss a whiny-ass titty baby probably isn't a good idea if you want to keep your job.
Every time we lose, one teammate turns into a total whiny-ass titty baby, blaming everyone else.
Stop being a whiny-ass titty baby and deal with your responsibilities like an adult.
