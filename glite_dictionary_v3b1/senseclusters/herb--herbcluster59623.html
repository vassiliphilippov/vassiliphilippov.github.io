herb

noun
aromatic plant used in cooking
2.4
Pronunciation: / hɜrb / (AmE), / hɜːb / (BrE)
A plant or part of a plant, such as leaves or stems, valued for its flavor, scent, or medicinal properties, especially one used to add flavor to food.

Examples

Fresh herb leaves
Growing your own herbs at home is both practical and enjoyable.
She sprinkled fresh herbs like basil and parsley over the pasta before serving.
This soup is flavored with a mixture of dried herbs and spices.
