fartlek

noun
training method with alternating running speeds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrt.lɛk / (AmE), / ˈfɑːt.lɛk / (BrE)
a training method for runners involving alternating periods of fast and moderate exercise during a continuous workout

Examples

Fartlek training session
Many runners find fartlek training more enjoyable than structured intervals.
Our coach included a fartlek session to improve our speed and endurance.
She prefers fartlek workouts because they allow flexibility and variation in her runs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interval training - training method involving alternating periods of intense and less intense activity
