transbay

adjective
across a bay
Pronunciation: / trænzˈbeɪ / (AmE), / trænzˈbeɪ / (BrE)
relating to travel, transport, or infrastructure that crosses a bay, especially in urban areas

Examples

Transbay bridge
The city approved a new transbay bridge to improve traffic flow between neighborhoods.
A transbay ferry service operates daily, connecting commuters to downtown.
The transbay tunnel project will shorten travel times for thousands of passengers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrabayside - within the same side of a bay
  • synonym: transcontinental - spanning or crossing a continent
