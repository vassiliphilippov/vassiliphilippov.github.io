outgassing

noun
release of trapped gas
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌɡæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌɡæsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which gas that was trapped or absorbed in a material is released, often into the atmosphere or a vacuum

Examples

Outgassing from materials
Engineers checked the satellite for outgassing before it was launched into space.
Newly manufactured plastics may experience outgassing when exposed to a vacuum.
The strange odor in the room was caused by outgassing from the new carpet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: degassing - the removal or release of gas from a material or environment
