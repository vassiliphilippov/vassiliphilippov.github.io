Italian monetary unit

noun
former basic currency of Italy
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtæljən ˈmɑnɪˌtɛri ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈtæl.jən ˈmɒn.ɪ.tri ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency formerly used in Italy, notably the lira before the adoption of the euro

Examples
