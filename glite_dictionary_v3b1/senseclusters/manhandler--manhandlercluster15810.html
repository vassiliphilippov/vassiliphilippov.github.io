manhandler

noun
person who supervises
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhændlər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌhændlə / (BrE)
a person who manages, supervises, or directs other people, especially workers or agents

Examples

A team manhandler
The company hired a new manhandler to oversee the construction team.
As a strict manhandler, she made sure everyone followed the safety procedures.
The manhandler assigned tasks to each worker at the start of the shift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manager - a person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees the work of others
  • antonym: worker - a person who does a job, especially one that involves physical labor
