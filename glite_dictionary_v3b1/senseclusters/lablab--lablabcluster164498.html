lablab

noun
a tropical plant; hyacinth bean
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæb.læb / (AmE), / ˈlæb.læb / (BrE)
A tropical Old World plant (Lablab purpureus), also known as hyacinth bean, widely cultivated for its edible pods, seeds, and as a forage crop.

Examples

lablab beans
In some cuisines, lablab beans are cooked as a source of protein.
The farmer planted lablab along the fence to provide both food and shade.
The purple flowers of the lablab attract pollinators throughout the summer.
