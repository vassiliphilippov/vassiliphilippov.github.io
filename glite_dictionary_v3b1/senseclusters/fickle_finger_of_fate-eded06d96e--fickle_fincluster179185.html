fickle finger of fate

noun
the unpredictable nature of destiny
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪkəl ˈfɪŋɡər əv feɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪkəl ˈfɪŋɡə əv feɪt / (BrE)
used to describe the unpredictable and often whimsical nature of fate or destiny, especially when it causes unexpected changes, often for the worse

Examples

The fickle finger of fate
He blamed the fickle finger of fate for the series of mishaps that ruined his presentation.
Sometimes the fickle finger of fate intervenes and our plans fall apart unexpectedly.
The artist often drew inspiration from the fickle finger of fate guiding her through life's twists and turns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destiny - the events that will necessarily happen to a person or thing in the future
  • synonym: fate - the development of events beyond a person's control
  • synonym: fortune - chance or luck as an arbitrary force affecting events
