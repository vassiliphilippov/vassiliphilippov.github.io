prefilter

verb
to filter in advance
6.2
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈfɪl.tər/ /; / /prɪ.ˈfɪl.tər/ (AmE), /priː.ˈfɪl.tə/ /; / /prɪ.ˈfɪl.tə/ (BrE)
to remove unwanted material or items in an initial filtering step before the main filter or processing stage

Examples

To prefilter data
Before analyzing the data, we need to prefilter it to exclude irrelevant entries.
The system will automatically prefilter emails to reduce spam before they reach your inbox.
Technicians prefiltered the air to remove dust particles before starting the experiment.
