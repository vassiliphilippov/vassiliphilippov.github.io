cuke

noun
(informal) cucumber
9.2
Pronunciation: / kjuːk / (AmE), / kjuːk / (BrE)
(informal) A cucumber; a long, green vegetable with a crisp, watery flesh, eaten raw or pickled.

Examples

A fresh cuke
I sliced a fresh cuke for the salad.
If you like your sandwiches crunchy, add a few cuke slices.
She brought pickled cukes to the picnic.
