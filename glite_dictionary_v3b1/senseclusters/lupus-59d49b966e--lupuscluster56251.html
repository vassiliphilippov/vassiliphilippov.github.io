Lupus

proper noun
constellation in the southern sky
Pronunciation: / ˈluː.pəs / (AmE), / ˈluː.pəs / (BrE)
A constellation in the southern sky, located near Centaurus.

Examples

Lupus constellation
According to ancient mythology, Lupus represents a wolf being hunted by Centaurus.
Astronomers often locate Lupus by starting at the nearby Centaurus constellation.
The constellation Lupus is visible during the Southern Hemisphere's winter months.
