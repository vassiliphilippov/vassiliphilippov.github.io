James Bay

proper noun
bay in northeastern Canada
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒeɪmz ˈbeɪ / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪmz ˈbeɪ / (BrE)
a large, shallow bay forming the southern extension of Hudson Bay in Canada, located between western Quebec and northeastern Ontario.
