dependance

noun
addiction to a substance
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndəns / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndəns / (BrE)
The state of being abnormally tolerant to and reliant on a habit-forming substance, especially drugs or alcohol; addiction.

Examples

Dependance on alcohol
His dependance on painkillers became a serious problem after the accident.
The doctor warned about the risk of developing a dependance on sleeping pills.
Treatment programs are available for those struggling with alcohol dependance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addiction - the condition of being addicted to a substance or activity
  • antonym: independence - freedom from being controlled or reliant on something
