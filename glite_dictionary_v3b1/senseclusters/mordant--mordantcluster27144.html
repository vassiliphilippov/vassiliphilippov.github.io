mordant

adjective
able to eat away by chemical action
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…îrdnt / (AmE), / Ààm…îÀêdnt / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially a strong acid) capable of destroying or eating away by chemical action.

Examples

Mordant acid
Always wear protective gloves when handling mordant chemicals in the laboratory.
The mordant acid corroded the metal surface within hours.
The statue was damaged by years of exposure to mordant substances in acid rain.
