loin

noun
cut of meat from an animal's back
Pronunciation: / lɔɪn / (AmE), / lɔɪn / (BrE)
a cut of meat from the side and back of an animal, between the ribs and the rump

Examples

Grilled loin steak
Lamb loin is especially tender and flavorful.
She ordered a grilled loin of pork for dinner.
The butcher recommended the beef loin for roasting.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: sirloin - a cut of beef from the back, just above the loin
