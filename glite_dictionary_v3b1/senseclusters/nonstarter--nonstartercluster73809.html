nonstarter

noun
unworkable or unacceptable proposal
Pronunciation: / nnstrt59r / (AmE), / n52nst51d0t59 / (BrE)
a proposal, plan, or idea that is unacceptable, impractical, or unlikely to succeed or be approved.

Examples

A nonstarter idea
Investors rejected the plan outright, calling the expansion proposal a nonstarter without clearer revenue forecasts.
During the meeting, several executives described the cost-cutting idea as a nonstarter and moved on to alternatives.
Most of the proposed merger terms were nonstarters, leaving lawyers to renegotiate from scratch before Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dead end - a situation or proposal that leads nowhere or has no chance of success
  • antonym: go-ahead - official approval to proceed with a plan; opposite of a nonstarter
  • synonym: no-go - a plan or idea that will not proceed or be approved
