bow

noun
knot with two loops, as in shoelaces
Pronunciation: / boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
a knot with two loops and two loose ends, often used to fasten or decorate things like shoelaces or gifts

Examples

Tie a bow
He struggled to keep the bow on the package from coming undone.
She tied her shoelaces in a neat bow before leaving for school.
The little girl wore a red bow in her hair.
He tied the bow perfectly, making the gift look beautiful.
She wore a bright red bow in her hair for the party.
The presents were wrapped in shiny paper with big bows on top.
