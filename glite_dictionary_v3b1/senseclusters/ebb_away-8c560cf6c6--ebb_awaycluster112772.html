ebb away

verb
to recede or diminish gradually
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɛb əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ɛb əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to flow back or recede, especially gradually; to diminish or fade away slowly

Examples

The tide ebbs away
As evening approached, the last rays of sunlight began to ebb away behind the hills.
At low tide, the water would ebb away from the rocky shoreline, revealing a strip of sand.
Her strength seemed to ebb away with each passing hour during the illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recede - to move back from a previous position
  • synonym: wane - to decrease in size, extent, or degree
