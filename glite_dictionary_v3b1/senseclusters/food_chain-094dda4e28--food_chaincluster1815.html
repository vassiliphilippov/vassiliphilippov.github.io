food chain

noun
system for producing and supplying food
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːd tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈfuːd tʃeɪn / (BrE)
the system and sequence of production, processing, distribution, and sale by which food moves from producers to consumers

Examples

Global food chain
The global food chain was disrupted by the drought, causing price spikes for basic staples.
Farmers and processors met to redesign the local food chain and shorten delivery times.
Consumers demanded transparency about origins across every link in the food chain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: food system - the broader social, economic, and environmental structures involved in producing, distributing, and consuming food
  • synonym: supply chain - the network of people, organizations, resources and activities involved in moving a product from supplier to customer
