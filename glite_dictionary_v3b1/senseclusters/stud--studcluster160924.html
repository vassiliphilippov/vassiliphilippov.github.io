stud

noun
decorative rounded protuberance
5.6
Pronunciation: / stʌd / (AmE), / stʌd / (BrE)
an ornament consisting of a circular, rounded projection, typically used as decoration on surfaces such as shields, belts, or vaults

Examples

A belt stud
Decorative studs were placed along the belt's length for extra flair.
The ancient shield was adorned with a golden stud in the center.
The ceiling's plaster was covered in small, raised studs for ornamentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boss - a knob, stud, or other rounded protrusion, often on armor or decoration
