melter

noun
machine for melting
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmel.tər/ (AmE), /ˈmel.tə/ (BrE)
a machine or device that heats solid materials until they become liquid

Examples

Industrial melter
The glass factory uses a large melter to turn sand into liquid glass.
Scrap metal is loaded into the melter before being shaped into new products.
Technicians monitor the temperature of the melter to ensure the process runs smoothly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freezer - a device used to make things solid by freezing
  • synonym: furnace - an enclosed structure for heating materials to very high temperatures
