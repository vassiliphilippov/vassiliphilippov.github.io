bay

noun
aromatic Mediterranean tree; source of bay leaves
1.2
Pronunciation: / beɪ / (AmE), / beɪ / (BrE)
a small Mediterranean evergreen tree (Laurus nobilis) with aromatic, glossy leaves used in cooking and with small blackish berries, historically used to crown victors in ancient Greece

Examples

Bay leaves in soup
A mature bay can be pruned into an attractive garden shrub.
In ancient times, athletes wore a crown made of bay leaves.
The recipe calls for one bay leaf to add flavor to the stew.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laurel - another name for the bay tree or its leaves
