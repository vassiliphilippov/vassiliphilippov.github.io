transformative

adjective
causing major change
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɔrmətɪv / (AmE), / trænsˈfɔːmətɪv / (BrE)
causing a major or important change in someone or something

Examples

Transformative change
The new law had a transformative impact on the education system.
Her year abroad was a transformative experience that changed her outlook on life.
Digital tools can be transformative for small businesses looking to grow.
The new technology had a transformative effect on the way people communicate.
Her research introduced a transformative approach to solving complex mathematical problems.
The company implemented a transformative process to improve efficiency across all departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revolutionary - causing or relating to a complete or dramatic change
  • antonym: static - not changing or lacking movement
