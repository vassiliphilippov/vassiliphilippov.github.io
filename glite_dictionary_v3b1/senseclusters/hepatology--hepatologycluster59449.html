hepatology

noun
study of the liver
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛpəˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌhɛpəˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that studies, diagnoses, and treats diseases of the liver and related organs (such as the gallbladder and bile ducts)
