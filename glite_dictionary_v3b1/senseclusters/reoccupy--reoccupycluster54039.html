reoccupy

verb
to take back possession
5.6
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈɔː.kjuː.paɪ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈɔː.kjuː.paɪ/ (AmE), /riː.ˈɒk.jʊ.paɪ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈɒk.jʊ.paɪ/ (BrE)
to take possession of or return to a place, position, or job after having left it
