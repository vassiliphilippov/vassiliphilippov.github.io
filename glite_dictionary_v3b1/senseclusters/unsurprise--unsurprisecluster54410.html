unsurprise

noun
lack of surprise
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʌn.sɚˈpraɪz / (AmE), / ʌn.səˈpraɪz / (BrE)
a lack or absence of surprise in response to something expected or unsurprising

Examples

A look of unsurprise
His face showed only unsurprise when he heard the predictable results.
There was a sense of unsurprise among the team after the announcement.
She accepted the news with complete unsurprise, as if she had expected it all along.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amazement - a feeling of great surprise or wonder
  • synonym: indifference - a lack of interest or concern
