bubble-like

adjective
resembling a bubble
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbəl laɪk / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəl laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a bubble in shape, appearance, or texture

Examples

Bubble-like surface
The artist created a bubble-like effect on the surface of the sculpture.
Tiny bubble-like shapes appeared in the glass as it cooled.
Her dress was made of a soft, bubble-like fabric that shimmered in the light.
