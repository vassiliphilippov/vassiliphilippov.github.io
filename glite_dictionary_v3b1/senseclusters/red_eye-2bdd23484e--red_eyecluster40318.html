red eye

noun
irritated or tired eye
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd aɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd aɪ / (BrE)
an eye that appears red due to irritation, tiredness, or illness

Examples

Have a red eye from tiredness
After staying up all night studying, he woke up with a painful red eye.
Allergies can sometimes cause a red eye and mild discomfort.
She used eye drops to soothe her red eye after a long night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloodshot - having red or inflamed eyes
  • antonym: clear eye - an eye without redness or irritation
