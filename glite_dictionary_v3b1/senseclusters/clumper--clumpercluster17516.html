clumper

noun
heavy-footed walker
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈklʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who walks with heavy, noisy steps, often making a clumping sound

Examples

A noisy clumper
The children heard a clumper coming down the hallway and quickly hid their toys.
As a clumper, he was never able to sneak up on anyone without being noticed.
The old wooden stairs creaked loudly under the weight of the clumper each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stomper - a person who walks with loud, heavy steps
  • antonym: tiptoer - a person who walks quietly on their toes
