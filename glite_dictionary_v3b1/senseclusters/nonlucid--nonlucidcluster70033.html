nonlucid

adjective
not aware in dream
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈluːsɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈluːsɪd / (BrE)
describing a dream in which the dreamer is not aware that they are dreaming

Examples

Nonlucid dream
She often has nonlucid dreams and cannot control what happens in them.
Most people experience nonlucid dreams every night without realizing it.
After learning about lucid dreaming, he tried to notice when his dreams were nonlucid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lucid - aware of dreaming while in a dream
