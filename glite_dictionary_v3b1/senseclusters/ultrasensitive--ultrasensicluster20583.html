ultrasensitive

adjective
extremely easily upset
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
extremely easily upset, offended, or emotionally affected by things that happen or are said

Examples

Ultrasensitive to criticism
He became ultrasensitive to any criticism after the incident at work.
Some people are ultrasensitive to loud noises and need a quiet environment.
Her ultrasensitive nature made her react strongly to even minor comments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypersensitive - easily affected or offended by things
  • antonym: insensitive - not easily affected or offended by things
