uptake

noun
adoption or acceptance
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌteɪk / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌteɪk / (BrE)
the process of accepting, understanding, or starting to use something new, such as an idea or technology

Examples

Uptake of new ideas
The uptake of the new recycling program exceeded the city's expectations.
There has been a slow uptake of electric vehicles in rural areas.
The company encouraged the uptake of its latest software among employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoption - the act of beginning to use or accept something new
  • antonym: rejection - the act of refusing to accept or use something
