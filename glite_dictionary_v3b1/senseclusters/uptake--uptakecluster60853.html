uptake

noun

the process of accepting, understanding, or starting to use something new, such as an idea or technology

Examples

Uptake of new ideas
The uptake of the new recycling program exceeded the city's expectations.
There has been a slow uptake of electric vehicles in rural areas.
The company encouraged the uptake of its latest software among employees.

Synonyms

  • adoption
    3.5 19/m
    adoption is the act of starting to use something new by groups or companies, while uptake covers both learning and use.
  • take-up
    4.1 0.08/m
    take-up is the measured rate people begin using a product or service, while uptake can include understanding and use.
