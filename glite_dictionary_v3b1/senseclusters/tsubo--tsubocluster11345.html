tsubo

noun
Japanese storage jar
9.9
Pronunciation: / tsuːˈboʊ / (AmE), / tsuːˈbəʊ / (BrE)
a large traditional Japanese jar or vase, usually made of pottery or ceramic, used for storage or decoration

Examples

A ceramic tsubo
The museum displayed a rare tsubo from the Edo period in its main hall.
She carefully cleaned the old ceramic tsubo that had been passed down in her family.
Collectors often seek out antique tsubo for their unique glazes and historical value.
