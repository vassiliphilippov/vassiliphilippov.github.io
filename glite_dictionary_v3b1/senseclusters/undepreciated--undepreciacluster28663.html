undepreciated

adjective
not depreciated
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not reduced in value by depreciation; still recorded at its original value, especially in accounting.

Examples

Undepreciated equipment value
The equipment was still listed as undepreciated in the company's financial statements.
After several years, the building remained undepreciated due to its continued high value.
Some assets are intentionally kept undepreciated for accounting or tax purposes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depreciated - reduced in value over time, especially in accounting
