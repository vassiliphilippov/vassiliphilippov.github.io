prepuberty

noun
the period just before puberty
6.6 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / priÀàpjuÀêb…öti / (AmE), / priÀêÀàpjuÀêb…ôti / (BrE)
the period immediately before puberty during which the body begins to undergo physical growth and changes leading to sexual maturity

Examples

Before prepuberty growth
Diet and exercise habits developed in prepuberty can influence long-term health.
Many children experience a rapid growth spurt during prepuberty.
Teachers are trained to recognize the signs of prepuberty in their students.

Synonyms

  • prepubescence
    8.4 0.01/m
    prepubescence is the stage when body and feelings start changing before sexual maturity, while prepuberty marks the immediate lead up to puberty.
  • preadolescence
    6.8 0.02/m
    preadolescence is the period of late childhood just before adolescence, while prepuberty refers to the immediate physical changes that begin before puberty.
