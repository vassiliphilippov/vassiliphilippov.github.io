Junipero Serra

proper noun
Spanish missionary in California, 1713–1784
Pronunciation: / huːˈniː.pə.roʊ ˈsɛr.ə / (AmE), / ˌhjuː.nɪˈpeə.rəʊ ˈser.ə / (BrE)
(1713–1784) A Spanish Franciscan missionary who established missions in California and played a major role in its early European settlement.
