counter

noun
sideboard-like dining room furniture with storage
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntɚ /; / ˈkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntə /; / ˈkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
a long flat surface or piece of furniture where business is done or items are served or stored

Examples

Kitchen counter
She placed the serving dishes on the counter in the dining room before dinner.
The antique counter had several drawers for storing silverware and linens.
They displayed their china on the shelves of the old oak counter.
Please place your order at the counter and someone will assist you shortly.
She leaned on the counter while waiting for her coffee to be prepared.
The cashier stood behind the counter and greeted the next customer in line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a counter across which alcoholic drinks or refreshments are served
  • synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat or sloping surface for writing, often with drawers
  • synonym: sideboard - a piece of furniture used for serving food and storing dining room items
