Saturnian

adjective
of Saturn or Latin verse
9.3
Pronunciation: / səˈtɜːrniən / (AmE), / səˈtɜːniən / (BrE)
relating to the planet Saturn or, in poetry, to a type of metrical line used in Latin verse

Examples

Saturnian rings
The probe captured stunning images of the Saturnian atmosphere during its mission.
Ancient poets sometimes wrote in the Saturnian meter for special occasions.
Scientists study the Saturnian moons to learn more about the outer solar system.
