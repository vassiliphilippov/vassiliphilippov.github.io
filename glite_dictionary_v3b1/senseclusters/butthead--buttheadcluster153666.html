butthead

proper noun
animated TV character
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌhɛd / (BrE)
the name of a fictional animated character known for his sarcastic and dim-witted personality in the television series Beavis and Butt-Head

Examples

Butthead cartoon character
The cartoon Butthead is famous for his sarcastic laugh and silly comments.
