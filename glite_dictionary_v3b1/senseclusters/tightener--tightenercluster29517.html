tightener

noun
tool for tightening
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtənər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtənə / (BrE)
a device or tool used to make something tighter or more secure

Examples

A tightener tool
He used a tightener to adjust the straps on his backpack.
The mechanic reached for a tightener to secure the bolts on the engine.
A simple tightener can make sure your shoelaces stay in place during a run.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fastener - a device that attaches or secures things together
  • antonym: loosen - to make something less tight or secure
