yohimbe

noun
West African medicinal tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / joʊˈhɪmbeɪ / (AmE), / jəʊˈhɪmbeɪ / (BrE)
a West African tree whose bark is used in herbal medicine, especially as a stimulant or aphrodisiac

Examples

Yohimbe tree
Many traditional healers use yohimbe bark to treat certain health conditions.
Some people take yohimbe supplements for increased energy or libido.
The effects of yohimbe can vary depending on the dosage and preparation.
