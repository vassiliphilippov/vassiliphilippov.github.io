Army of Muhammad

noun
militant group active in Kashmir, founded 2000
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmi əv məˈhɑməd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi əv məˈhaməd / (BrE)
a militant Islamic group founded in 2000, active mainly in Kashmir, known for kidnappings aimed at securing the release of imprisoned militants; closely aligned with al-Rashid Trust

Examples

Army of Muhammad group
Several governments have designated the Army of Muhammad as a terrorist organization due to its activities.
The Army of Muhammad claimed responsibility for the attack in the Kashmir region last year.
The Army of Muhammad demanded the release of prisoners in exchange for kidnapped hostages.
