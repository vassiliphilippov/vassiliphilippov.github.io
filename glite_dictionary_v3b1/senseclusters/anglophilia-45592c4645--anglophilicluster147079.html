Anglophilia

noun
admiration for England and its culture
7.6 7.1
Pronunciation: /æŋ.gloʊ.ˈfɪl.ɪə/ /; / /æŋ.glə.ˈfɪl.ɪə/ (AmE), /æŋ.gləʊ.ˈfɪl.ɪə/ /; / /æŋ.gloʊ.ˈfɪl.ɪə/ (BrE)
