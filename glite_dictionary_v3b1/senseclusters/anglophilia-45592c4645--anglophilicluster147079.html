Anglophilia

noun
admiration for England and its culture
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡləˈfɪliə / (AmE), / ˌaŋɡləˈfɪliə / (BrE)
strong admiration or fondness for England, English people, or English culture and customs.

Examples

Anglophilia for England
Her Anglophilia was evident in her love of British literature and afternoon tea.
His collection of Beatles memorabilia was just one sign of his lifelong Anglophilia.
Many Americans develop Anglophilia after spending time studying in London.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Anglophobia - fear or dislike of England or English things
