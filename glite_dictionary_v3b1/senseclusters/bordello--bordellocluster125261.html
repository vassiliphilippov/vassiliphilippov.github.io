bordello

noun
brothel; house of prostitution
8.1
Pronunciation: / bɔrˈdɛloʊ / (AmE), / bɔːˈdɛləʊ / (BrE)
A house or building where prostitutes work and sexual services are offered for payment.

Examples

A bordello house
Rumors spread that the old mansion had once operated as a bordello during the 1920s.
She grew up near a bordello, which gave the neighborhood a notorious reputation.
The police raided the bordello after receiving several complaints from neighbors.
