nappy

noun
baby's absorbent undergarment (British)
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpi / (AmE), / ˈnæpi / (BrE)
A piece of soft cloth or other material fastened around a baby's bottom and between its legs to absorb and contain urine and feces; known as a diaper in American English.
