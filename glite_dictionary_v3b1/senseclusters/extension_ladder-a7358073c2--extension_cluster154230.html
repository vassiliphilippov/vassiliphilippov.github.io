extension ladder

noun
adjustable-length ladder
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃən ˌlædər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən ˌlædə / (BrE)
A ladder consisting of two or more sections that can be slid to increase its length.
