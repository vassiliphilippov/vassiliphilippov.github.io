surf music

noun
music genre linked to surf culture
Pronunciation: / sɝf ˈmjuzɪk / (AmE), / sɜːf ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
A style of popular music associated with surf culture, especially in Southern California, characterized by reverb-heavy guitar sounds and themes related to surfing.

Examples

Surf music style
Bands like The Beach Boys helped bring surf music into the mainstream during the 1960s.
He has a huge collection of vintage surf music vinyl records in his room.
The energetic rhythms of surf music evoke the spirit of the California coastline.
