Pashtoon

noun
member of an Afghan-Pakistani ethnic group
9.4
Pronunciation: / pʌʃˈtuːn / (AmE), / pʌʃˈtuːn / (BrE)
a member of a mountain-dwelling ethnic group native mainly to eastern Afghanistan and northwestern Pakistan, known for their distinct language and culture

Examples

Pashtoon tribes
He studied the history of Pashtoon tribes in the border regions.
Many Pashtoon communities continue to follow traditional tribal customs.
The Pashtoon people have a long and rich cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pathan - an alternative name for a member of this ethnic group
