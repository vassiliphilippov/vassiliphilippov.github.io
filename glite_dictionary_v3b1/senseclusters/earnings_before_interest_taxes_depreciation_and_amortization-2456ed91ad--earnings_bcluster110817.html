earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization

noun
profit measure before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrnɪŋz bɪˈfɔr ˈɪntrəst ˈtæksɪz dɪˌpriʃiˈeɪʃən ənd əˌmɔrtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɜːnɪŋz bɪˈfɔː ˈɪntrɪst ˈtæksɪz dɪˌpriːʃɪˈeɪʃən ənd əˌmɔːtɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
