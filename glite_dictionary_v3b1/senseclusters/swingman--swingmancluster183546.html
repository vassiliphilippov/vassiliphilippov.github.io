swingman

noun
versatile baseball pitcher
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋˌmæn / (BrE)
a sports player who can play more than one position, especially in baseball (pitcher and reliever) or basketball (guard and forward)

Examples

Basketball swingman
The coach used their most reliable swingman to fill in for an injured starter.
As a swingman, he pitched both in the bullpen and as a starter throughout the season.
The team's swingman was praised for his flexibility and strong performance in multiple roles.
The team's swingman filled in at both shooting guard and small forward during the season.
As a talented swingman, he adapted quickly to different roles on the basketball court.
Coaches value a swingman for the flexibility he brings to the lineup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relief pitcher - a pitcher who enters the game after the starting pitcher
  • antonym: specialist - a player who focuses on one position or skill
  • synonym: starting pitcher - a pitcher who begins the game for a team
  • synonym: utility player - an athlete who can play several positions
