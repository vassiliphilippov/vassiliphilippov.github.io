mooncalf

noun
deformed creature (archaic)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌkæf / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌkɑːf / (BrE)
an abnormal or deformed animal or person, especially one believed in the past to be affected by the moon's influence

Examples

Deformed mooncalf
In old tales, a mooncalf was said to be born during a full moon and feared by villagers.
The farmer was shocked when his cow gave birth to a mooncalf with unusual features.
Superstitions once blamed the appearance of a mooncalf on the mysterious power of the moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monster - a creature that is very large, ugly, or frightening
  • antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; not abnormal
