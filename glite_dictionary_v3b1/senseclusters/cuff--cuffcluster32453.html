cuff

noun
turned-back end of a sleeve or trouser leg
5.4
Pronunciation: / kʌf / (AmE), / kʌf / (BrE)
the end part of a sleeve or trouser leg, where the material is turned back or finished with a separate band

Examples

Shirt cuff
He wore a white shirt with gold links in the cuffs.
She rolled up her sleeves, exposing the patterned cuffs.
The cuffs of her jeans were frayed after years of wear.
