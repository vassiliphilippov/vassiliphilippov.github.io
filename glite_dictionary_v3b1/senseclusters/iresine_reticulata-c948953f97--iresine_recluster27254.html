Iresine reticulata

noun
ornamental South American plant species
Pronunciation: / aɪˈriːsiːn ˌrɛtɪˈkjuːlətə / (AmE), / aɪˈriːsiːn ˌrɛtɪˈkjuːlətə / (BrE)
A South American plant with green to purple or red branches, ornamental green to purple foliage, and spikes of small woolly flowers with dry membranous bracts, often grown for its decorative leaves.
