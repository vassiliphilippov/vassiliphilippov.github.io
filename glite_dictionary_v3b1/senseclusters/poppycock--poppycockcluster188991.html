poppycock

noun
foolish or senseless talk
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpiˌkɑk /; / ˈpɑpiˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɒpɪˌkɒk /; / ˈpɒpɪkɒk / (BrE)
foolish or absurd talk; nonsense

Examples

Nonsense poppycock
Don't give me that poppycock; I know what really happened here.
His claims about time travel are pure poppycock, and nobody takes them seriously.
The article was dismissed by experts as complete poppycock.
Most experts agree that his theory is a bunch of poppycock.
She dismissed the rumors as utter poppycock.
The story he told us was absolute poppycock and no one believed it.
