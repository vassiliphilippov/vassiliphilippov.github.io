semihard

adjective
moderately difficult negative example (ML)
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈse.mɪ.hɑːrd/ /; / /ˈse.mə.hɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈse.mɪ.hɑːd/ /; / /ˈse.mə.hɑːd/ (BrE)
(in machine learning) (of a negative training example) moderately difficult to distinguish from a positive example; used in training methods (e.g., semihard negative mining) to improve model discrimination.
