Pharaoh's chicken

noun
small African and Eurasian vulture
Pronunciation: / fəˈroʊz ˈtʃɪkən / (AmE), / fəˈrəʊz ˈtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
a small mostly white vulture of Africa and southern Eurasia, also known as the Egyptian vulture

Examples

The Pharaoh's chicken is often seen near ancient Egyptian ruins scavenging for food.
Unlike many other vultures, the Pharaoh's chicken has striking white plumage and a yellow face.
