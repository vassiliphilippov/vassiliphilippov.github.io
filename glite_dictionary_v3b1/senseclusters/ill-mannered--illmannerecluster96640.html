ill-mannered

adjective
rude or impolite in behavior
2.2
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈmænərd / (AmE), / ɪlˈmænəd / (BrE)
Having or showing bad manners; rude or impolite in behavior.

Examples

Ill-mannered behavior
His ill-mannered comments made the meeting uncomfortable for everyone.
Many teachers will not tolerate ill-mannered behavior in the classroom.
The ill-mannered guest interrupted everyone at the dinner table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rude - offensively impolite or ill-mannered
  • antonym: well-mannered - behaving in a polite and courteous way
