death duty

noun
tax on a deceased person's estate
Pronunciation: / dɛθ ˈduti / (AmE), / dɛθ ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
a tax paid on the estate of a person who has died

Examples

Pay death duty
His family struggled to pay the death duty after he passed away.
Many people set up trusts to minimize death duty liabilities.
The government increased the death duty rates in the recent budget.
