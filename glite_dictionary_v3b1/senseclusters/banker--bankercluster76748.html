banker

noun
person in charge of funds in a gambling game
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkə / (BrE)
the person who manages the bank or funds in a gambling game, distributing or collecting bets

Examples

The banker deals cards
Each round, the role of banker rotates among the players at the table.
In baccarat, the banker is responsible for handling all bets and payouts.
The banker placed the chips in the center and began to deal the cards.
