concentration

noun
gathering of military forces
5.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /kɑːn.sən.ˈtreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kɒn.sən.ˈtreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of bringing people or things together in one place; a high density or crowding in an area

Examples

Concentration of troops
During the campaign, the concentration of military assets was crucial for success.
Rapid concentration of forces allowed the army to surprise the enemy.
The concentration of troops along the border signaled an imminent offensive.
A sudden concentration of vehicles caused a traffic jam on the highway.
The high concentration of people in the city center makes it difficult to move freely.
There was a noticeable concentration of shops and restaurants along that street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: density - the degree to which something is compacted or crowded
  • antonym: dispersion - the process of spreading things over a wide area, opposite of concentration
