grayness

noun
the state or quality of being gray
3.5 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪnəs /; / ˈɡreɪnɪs / (BrE)
a neutral color that is midway between white and black; the quality or state of being gray in color
