advective

adjective
relating to advection
Pronunciation: / ædˈvɛktɪv / (AmE), / ædˈvɛktɪv / (BrE)
Relating to or resulting from the horizontal movement of a substance, such as heat or moisture, by the flow of a fluid, especially in meteorology and physics.

Examples

Advective heat transfer
Meteorologists study advective processes to understand weather patterns.
The advective flow of moisture can lead to increased rainfall in certain regions.
The advective transport of heat plays a crucial role in ocean currents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advection - the horizontal movement of a property (e.g., heat, moisture) in a fluid
