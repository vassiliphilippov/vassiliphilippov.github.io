bump

verb
move an online post to the top; revive discussion
7.8 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / bÊŒmp / (AmE), / bÊŒmp / (BrE)
(informal) to post a short message in an online forum, comment thread, or feed in order to move the thread or post back to the top and attract new attention or replies.

Examples

To bump a thread
She bumped the thread to the top of the forum with a quick 'any updates?' message.
If nobody replies, moderators sometimes bump a post to remind people about unanswered questions.
He avoided bumping his sales listing repeatedly to avoid annoying potential buyers on the marketplace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pin - to fix a post at the top of a forum or feed so it remains visible
  • synonym: revive - to bring a discussion or activity back to life or attention
