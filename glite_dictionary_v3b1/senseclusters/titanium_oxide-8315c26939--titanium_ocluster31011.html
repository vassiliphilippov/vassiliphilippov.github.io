titanium oxide

noun
white pigment and industrial compound
5.6
Pronunciation: / taɪˈteɪniəm aɪˈksaɪd / (AmE), / tɪˈteɪnɪəm ˈɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a white powder used as a pigment and in various industrial processes because of its high covering power and durability

Examples

Titanium oxide pigment
