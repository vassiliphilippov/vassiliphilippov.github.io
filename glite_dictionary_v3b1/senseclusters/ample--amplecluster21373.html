Synonyms

  • plentiful
    2.5 5/m
    plentiful is common and describes things that exist in large amount, while ample focuses on having enough size or space.
  • abundant
    3.0 12/m
    abundant is a slightly formal word for very large amounts, while ample often means enough room or supply in everyday use.
  • generous
    1.3 10/m
    generous can mean freely given or large in amount, while ample simply means more than enough in size or amount.
  • copious
    6.8 2/m
    copious often describes large written or spoken quantities, while ample describes enough physical space, time, or amount.
  • bountiful
    4.9 1/m
    bountiful suggests rich, giving harvests or gifts and sounds warm or poetic, while ample is neutral and practical about size.
