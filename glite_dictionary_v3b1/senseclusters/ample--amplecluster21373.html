ample

adjective
more than enough; plentiful
Pronunciation: / ˈæmpəl / (AmE), / ˈæmpəl /; / ˈampl̩ / (BrE)
more than enough; plentiful or large in amount, size, or capacity

Examples

An ample amount
She had ample time to finish her assignment before the deadline.
The hotel room offered ample space for all our luggage and belongings.
We received ample warnings about the approaching storm.
She brought ample supplies to last the entire trip.
The kitchen has ample space for a large dining table and chairs.
We were given ample warning before the storm arrived.
The evidence against the suspect was ample to convict him.
There was ample room for everyone in the spacious hall.
They stored an ample amount of food for the winter months.
