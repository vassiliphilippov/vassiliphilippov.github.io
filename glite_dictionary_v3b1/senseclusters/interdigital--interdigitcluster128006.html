interdigital

adjective
between fingers or toes
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdɪdʒɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdɪdʒɪtəl / (BrE)
located or occurring between the fingers or toes

Examples

Interdigital space
The nurse cleaned the interdigital area to prevent infection after the surgery.
Athletes are prone to fungal infections in the interdigital spaces of their feet.
He noticed redness in the interdigital region between his fingers.

Related Terms
