shepherd's pie

noun
meat pie topped with mashed potato
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛpɚdz paɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpədz paɪ / (BrE)
A baked dish consisting of minced meat (typically lamb or beef) cooked with vegetables and gravy, topped with mashed potatoes and browned in the oven.

Examples

A shepherd's pie dish
After the holidays, we used leftover lamb to make a hearty shepherd's pie.
My grandmother makes a delicious shepherd's pie every Sunday for our family dinner.
The restaurant's special tonight is shepherd's pie served with steamed vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cottage pie - meat pie with mashed potato topping, usually using beef instead of lamb
