bedtime

noun
the usual time to go to bed
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛd.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.taɪm / (BrE)
the time at which a person usually goes to bed in the evening or night

Examples

The bedtime hour
Even on weekends, he tries to keep the same bedtime for better sleep.
It's almost bedtime, so please get ready to sleep.
She reads a story to her children every night before bedtime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lights-out - the time when lights must be turned off, usually in institutions
