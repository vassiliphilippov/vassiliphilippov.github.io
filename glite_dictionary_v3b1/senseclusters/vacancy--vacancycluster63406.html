vacancy

noun
an empty space or area
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.kən.si / (AmE), / ˈveɪ.kən.si / (BrE)
An empty area or space; a place that is unoccupied or devoid of contents.

Examples

A vacancy in space
After the crowd dispersed, the stadium echoed with a strange vacancy.
Peering through the telescope, she was amazed by the endless vacancy of space.
The explorers discovered a vast vacancy where the building once stood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fullness - the state of containing as much as possible
  • synonym: gap - an opening or empty space in something
  • synonym: void - completely empty space
