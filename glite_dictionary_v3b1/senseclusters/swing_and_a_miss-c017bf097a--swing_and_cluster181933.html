swing and a miss

noun
an unsuccessful attempt
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ ənd ə mɪs / (AmE), / swɪŋ ənd ə mɪs / (BrE)
an attempt that fails to achieve the desired result; an unsuccessful effort

Examples

A swing and a miss
Her suggestion for the project was a real swing and a miss.
Their marketing campaign turned out to be a swing and a miss despite high hopes.
When it came to predicting the winner, he had a swing and a miss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: failure - lack of success in achieving something
