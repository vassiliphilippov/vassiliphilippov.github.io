dumpy

adjective
unattractive, dirty; like a garbage dump
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈdʌmpi / (BrE)
(of a place or thing) unattractive and dirty, resembling a garbage dump.

Examples

A dumpy apartment
She moved out of her dumpy old flat as soon as she could afford better.
The apartment was so dumpy that I didn't want to take off my shoes.
We stayed in some dumpy hotel outside the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dirty - covered or marked with an unclean substance
  • synonym: messy - untidy or disordered
  • antonym: tidy - neat, clean, and in order (opposite meaning)
