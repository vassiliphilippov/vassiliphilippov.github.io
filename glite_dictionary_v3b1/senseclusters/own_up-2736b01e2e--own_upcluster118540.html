own up

verb
admit wrongdoing or mistake
4.4
Pronunciation: / oʊn ʌp / (AmE), / əʊn ʌp / (BrE)
to admit or acknowledge that you have done something wrong or made a mistake

Examples

To own up to mistakes
If you own up now, the consequences will be less severe.
It's better to own up to your mistakes than to cover them up.
She finally owned up to breaking the vase after hours of questioning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confess - to admit that you have done something wrong or illegal
  • antonym: deny - to say that something is not true or that you did not do something
