St. Martin

proper noun
Caribbean island split between France and the Netherlands
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈmɑːrtɪn / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈmɑːtɪn / (BrE)
An island in the western Leeward Islands of the Caribbean, administered jointly by France and the Netherlands.
