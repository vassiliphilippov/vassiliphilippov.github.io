respectful

adjective
showing respect; polite
1.1 1.1
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspekt.fəl/ /; / /rə.ˈspekt.fəl/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspekt.fəl/ /; / /rə.ˈspekt.fəl/ (BrE)
showing or feeling respect, deference, or admiration toward someone or something; being polite and considerate

Examples

A respectful nod
He gave a respectful nod to the teacher as he entered the room.
She listened in a respectful silence while her friend spoke.
The children were always respectful towards their elders.
He gave a respectful bow before leaving the room.
She addressed the elderly man in a respectful manner.
The students listened in respectful silence as the principal spoke.
