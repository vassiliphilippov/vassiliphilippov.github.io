unshrink

verb
to restore size or amount
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃrɪŋk / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃrɪŋk / (BrE)
to increase in size, amount, or importance again after having been reduced or diminished

Examples

To unshrink a company
The company managed to unshrink its workforce after a period of layoffs.
With careful care, you might be able to unshrink your favorite wool sweater.
New policies helped the city unshrink its budget for public services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restore - to bring something back to its former condition
  • antonym: shrink - to become smaller in size, amount, or importance
