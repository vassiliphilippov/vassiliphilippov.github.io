shop window

noun
online product display
Pronunciation: / 8351p c8w6ando8a / (AmE), / 8352p c8w6and598a / (BrE)
the online display of products on a retailer's website, app, or social media — the digital equivalent of a shop's physical window used to attract customers.

Examples

Online shop window
The retailer's online shop window showcases new arrivals and seasonal deals to browsing customers.
She refreshed the homepage shop window with large images to increase clicks and conversions.
Many small brands use Instagram as their primary shop window to reach international audiences quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital storefront - a retailer's website or app where products are displayed and sold online
  • synonym: display window - a physical window used to display goods in a shop; the traditional equivalent
