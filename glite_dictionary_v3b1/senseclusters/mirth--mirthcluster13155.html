mirth

noun
great merriment or joy
Pronunciation: / mɝːθ / (AmE), / mɜːθ / (BrE)
great joy, amusement, or happiness, especially as expressed in laughter

Examples

Mirth and joy
Amid the mirth of the celebration, she quietly slipped away to reflect.
His joke brought a burst of mirth to the otherwise dull meeting.
The room was filled with mirth as old friends reunited and shared stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glee - delight or open joy
  • antonym: sorrow - feeling of deep distress or sadness
