rendering machine

noun
high-performance computer for CGI rendering
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈren.də.rɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈren.də.rɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
A high-performance computer or dedicated hardware system used to generate (render) computer-generated images, animations, or visual effects by processing 3D scenes, lighting, textures, and frames.

Examples

3D rendering machine
The studio upgraded its rendering machine to speed up CGI workflows.
They farmed out frames across several rendering machines to finish the animation before the deadline.
A powerful rendering machine can reduce processing time for complex lighting and effects runs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: render farm - a group of rendering machines working together to process frames or scenes faster
  • synonym: render node - a single computer or server that performs rendering tasks within a render farm
