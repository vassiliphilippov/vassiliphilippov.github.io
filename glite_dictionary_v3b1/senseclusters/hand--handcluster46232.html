hand

noun
unit of length (4 inches), for measuring horses
6.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /hænd/ (AmE), /hænd/ (BrE)
a unit of length equal to four inches (approximately 10.16 centimeters), used especially for measuring the height of horses at the withers

Examples

Sixteen hands high
The horse measured sixteen hands at the withers, making it quite tall.
They bought a pony that was only twelve hands high, suitable for their children.
When asked about the horse’s height, the trainer replied it was fifteen hands.
