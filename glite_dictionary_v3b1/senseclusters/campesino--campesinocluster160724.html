campesino

noun
rural Latin American farmer
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkæmpəˈsiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkampɛˈsiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a rural farmer or agricultural laborer, especially in Latin American countries

Examples

A campesino farmer
The campesino rose early every morning to tend to his crops.
Many campesinos work together during the harvest season in rural villages.
The government introduced new policies to support campesino communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • synonym: peasant - a poor farmer or agricultural laborer
