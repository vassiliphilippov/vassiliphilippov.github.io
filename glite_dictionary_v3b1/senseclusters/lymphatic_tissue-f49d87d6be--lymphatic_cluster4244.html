lymphatic tissue

noun
tissue of the lymphatic system
6.1 3.5
Pronunciation: /lɪm.ˈfæt.ɪk ˈtɪʃ.uː/ /; / /lɪm.ˈfæt.ɪk ˈtɪs.juː/ (AmE), /lɪm.ˈfæt.ɪk ˈtɪs.juː/ /; / /lɪm.ˈfæt.ɪk ˈtɪʃ.uː/ (BrE)
