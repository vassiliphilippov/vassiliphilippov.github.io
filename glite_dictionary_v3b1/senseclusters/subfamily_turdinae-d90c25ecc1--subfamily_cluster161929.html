subfamily Turdinae

noun
a subfamily of thrushes (birds)
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌfæməli tɜrˈdaɪni / (AmE), / sʌbˌfæmɪli tɜːˈdaɪniː / (BrE)
a taxonomic subfamily including thrushes, used in some classifications of birds

Examples

The subfamily Turdinae
In some taxonomies, the subfamily Turdinae encompasses the true thrushes.
Recent genetic studies have led to debates over the circumscription of the subfamily Turdinae.
The subfamily Turdinae includes many familiar woodland birds known for their melodious songs.
