gulder

noun
a loud outburst or uproar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌldər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌldə / (BrE)
a loud, resonant outburst or uproar, especially of laughter or noise from a crowd

Examples

As soon as the joke landed, a gulder filled the entire room.
The gulder from the audience surprised even the performers.
