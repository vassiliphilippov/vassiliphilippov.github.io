lip synchronization

noun
matching lip movements to speech in audio-video
1.4
Pronunciation: / lɪp ˌsɪŋkrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / lɪp ˌsɪŋkrənaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n /; / lɪp ˌsɪŋkrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of matching audio with video so that a speaker's lip movements correspond exactly with the recorded speech

Examples

Accurate lip synchronization
Accurate lip synchronization is essential in animated movies to make characters appear realistic.
She worked for hours on lip synchronization to ensure the music video looked flawless.
The film's lip synchronization was slightly off, making the dialogue seem unnatural.
Animators pay close attention to lip synchronisation to make characters look more realistic.
Poor lip synchronisation in the movie was distracting, as the voices didn't match the actors' mouths.
With perfect lip synchronisation, the audience couldn't tell that the dialogue had been dubbed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dub - to provide a new soundtrack for a film, especially in a different language
  • synonym: dubbing - the process of adding new dialogue to a film or video
  • synonym: lip sync - the process of matching a person's lips with spoken or sung words
