Canadian French

noun
variety of French spoken in Canada
5.4
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈneɪ.diː.ən frentʃ/ /; / /kə.ˈneɪ.dɪ.ən frentʃ/ (AmE), /kə.ˈneɪ.diː.ən frentʃ/ /; / /kə.ˈneɪ.dɪ.ən frentʃ/ (BrE)
