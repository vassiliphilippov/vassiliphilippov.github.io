coliform

noun
indicator bacteria
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
a type of bacteria found in soil, water, or feces, used as an indicator of contamination

Examples
