opulently

adverb
in a rich or luxurious manner
7.3 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀê.pj…ô.l…ônt.li / (AmE), / Àà…íp.j ä.l…ônt.li / (BrE)
In a way that is rich, luxurious, and impressive.

Examples

To live opulently
He lived opulently, spending lavishly on clothes and travel.
The dining room was opulently decorated with gold-plated furniture and sparkling chandeliers.
The hotel lobby is opulently furnished to impress its wealthy guests.

Synonyms

  • luxuriously
    5.1 0.33/m
    luxuriously means very comfortable and elegant, while opulently suggests wealth and a showy, highly decorated style.
  • sumptuously
    8.4 0.14/m
    sumptuously describes rich, finely decorated food or rooms, while opulently focuses more on wealth and grand, showy display.
  • lavishly
    5.6 1.41/m
    lavishly means given or done in large, generous amounts, while opulently suggests costly, rich show that displays wealth.
  • richly
    3.4 2.28/m
    richly describes strong color, taste, or decoration, while opulently points to wealthy, luxurious surroundings used to impress others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: simply - in a straightforward or plain manner
  • synonym: sumptuously - in a splendid and expensive-looking way
