unvendible

adjective
not fit for sale
4.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɛndɪbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or fit for sale; unsalable.

Examples

An unvendible product
After the recall, all remaining products on the shelves were unvendible.
Spoiled goods in the market quickly become unvendible and must be discarded.
The produce was deemed unvendible due to its poor appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unsalable - not able to be sold
  • antonym: vendible - fit for sale; able to be sold
