lesser calamint

noun
aromatic perennial herb
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsɚ kəˈlæmɪnt / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə kəˈlæmɪnt / (BrE)
A low-growing, strongly aromatic perennial herb (Clinopodium calamintha) native to southern Europe and Great Britain, often naturalized in the United States.

Examples

The lesser calamint plant
Many beekeepers plant lesser calamint to attract pollinators to their hives.
She brewed a soothing tea using fresh lesser calamint leaves from her backyard.
The garden was fragrant with the scent of lesser calamint blooming among the other herbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calamint - any plant of the genus Clinopodium, including lesser calamint
