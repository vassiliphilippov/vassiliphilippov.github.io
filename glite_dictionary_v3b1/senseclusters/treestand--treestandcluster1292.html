treestand

noun
tree sales booth
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌstænd / (BrE)
a small stall or booth where trees, especially Christmas trees, are sold

Examples

Christmas treestand market
We bought a fir tree from the local treestand near the supermarket.
Every December, a treestand appears on Main Street selling fresh Christmas trees.
The family visited the treestand to pick out the perfect tree for their living room.
