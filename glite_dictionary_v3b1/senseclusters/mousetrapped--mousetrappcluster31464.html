mousetrapped

adjective
tricked and trapped
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsˌtræpt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsˌtræpt / (BrE)
tricked or caught in a situation that is difficult to escape from

Examples

Feeling mousetrapped
She felt mousetrapped after signing the contract without reading the fine print.
Many customers were mousetrapped by the misleading advertisement.
He realized too late that he had been mousetrapped into a bad deal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceived - tricked into believing something that is not true
  • antonym: free - not restricted or trapped
  • synonym: trapped - unable to escape from a difficult situation
