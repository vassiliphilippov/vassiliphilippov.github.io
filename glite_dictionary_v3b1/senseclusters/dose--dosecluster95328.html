dose

noun
infection caught through sexual contact
Pronunciation: / doʊs / (AmE), / dəʊs / (BrE)
(informal, archaic) A communicable infection transmitted by sexual intercourse or genital contact, especially a sexually transmitted infection such as gonorrhea.
