adventure

noun
exciting or risky experience or activity
2.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃɚ / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
an unusual, exciting, and sometimes hazardous experience or activity, especially one involving exploration or risk

Examples

A wild adventure
Our trip through the jungle turned into a real adventure when our guide got lost.
She has always dreamed of embarking on an adventure around the world.
The movie is full of adventure from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploit - a bold or daring feat
  • antonym: ordeal - a difficult or unpleasant experience
