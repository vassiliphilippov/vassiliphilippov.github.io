nongenerative

adjective
not generative
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndʒəˈnɛrətɪv /; / nɑnˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒndʒəˈnɛrətɪv /; / nɒnˈdʒɛnərətɪv / (BrE)
not able to produce or create something new; not using a generative process or model

Examples
