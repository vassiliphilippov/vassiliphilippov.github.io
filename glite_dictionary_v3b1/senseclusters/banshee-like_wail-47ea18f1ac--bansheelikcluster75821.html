banshee-like wail

noun
mournful, piercing cry
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbænʃi laɪk weɪl / (AmE), / ˈbanʃi laɪk weɪl / (BrE)
a loud, mournful, and piercing cry resembling the traditional wail attributed to a banshee

Examples

A banshee-like wail
A banshee-like wail pierced the night when the news was announced.
As thunder rolled, her banshee-like wail sent shivers down my spine.
The sudden banshee-like wail from the baby startled everyone in the room.
