caretaking

noun
providing care
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrˌteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeəˌteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of looking after and caring for someone or something, especially as a responsibility

Examples

Caretaking of children
The caretaking of the historic building was entrusted to a local organization.
She took on the caretaking of her younger siblings after their parents fell ill.
Long-term caretaking can be emotionally and physically demanding for family members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caregiving - the activity of providing care for someone, especially the elderly or sick
  • antonym: neglect - the failure to care for someone or something properly
