narcissus

noun
a bulbous plant with showy flowers
4.2 5.8
Pronunciation: / nɑːrˈsɪsəs / (AmE), / nɑːˈsɪsəs / (BrE)
a bulbous plant of the genus Narcissus, having erect, narrow leaves and showy yellow or white flowers, often grown as an ornamental.

Examples

A narcissus flower
A single narcissus stood out among the green leaves, attracting bees and butterflies alike.
She planted a row of narcissus bulbs along the walkway for a burst of color each year.
The narcissus blooms early in the spring, brightening gardens with its white and yellow petals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daffodil - another name for the narcissus, especially those with yellow flowers
