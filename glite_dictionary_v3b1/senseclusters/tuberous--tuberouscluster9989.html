tuberous

adjective
swollen like a tuber (botany)
7.7 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtuÀêb…ôr…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàtjuÀêb…ôr…ôs / (BrE)
(of a plant or root) having or resembling a tuber; swollen underground and serving as a storage organ.

Examples

Tuberous roots
Many orchids have tuberous roots that store nutrients underground.
Some weeds produce tuberous stems that make them hard to eradicate.
The tuberous shape of the dahlia's root system helps it survive dry seasons.
