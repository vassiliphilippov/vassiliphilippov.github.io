underchief

noun
deputy to chief
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌtʃiːf / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌtʃiːf / (BrE)
a person who holds a rank or position directly below the chief in an organization or tribe

Examples

The underchief manages
The underchief was responsible for managing the daily affairs of the village.
After the chief retired, the underchief was chosen to lead the tribe.
Each department had an underchief who reported directly to the main chief.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chief - the leader or person with the highest authority in a group
  • synonym: deputy - a person appointed to assist or act in place of a superior
  • degree lower: subordinate - a person lower in rank or position
