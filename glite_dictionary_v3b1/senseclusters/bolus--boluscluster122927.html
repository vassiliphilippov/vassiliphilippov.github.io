bolus

noun
large pill used in veterinary medicine
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊləs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊləs / (BrE)
a large pill or tablet, especially as used in veterinary medicine

Examples

A bolus tablet
A bolus is sometimes used to provide a single, large dose of medication in livestock.
The veterinarian administered a bolus to treat the cow's infection.
This treatment involves giving the animal a bolus rather than a series of smaller tablets.
