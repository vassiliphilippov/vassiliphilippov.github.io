Hindoo

adjective
archaic: relating to Hinduism or Hindus
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪn.du / (AmE), / ˈhɪn.duː / (BrE)
(Archaic) Relating to or supporting Hinduism; of or pertaining to the Hindu religion or people.

Examples

Hindoo temple art
He studied Hindoo art and culture during his travels in India.
Many British writers in the 19th century described Indian customs as Hindoo practices.
The Hindoo religion has a vast and ancient tradition of philosophy.
