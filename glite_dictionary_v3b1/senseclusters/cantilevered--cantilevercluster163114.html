cantilevered

adjective
supported at one end
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæntɪˌlivɚd / (AmE), / ˈkæntɪˌliːvəd / (BrE)
supported at only one end and projecting outward without additional support

Examples

A cantilevered balcony
The architect designed a cantilevered balcony that extends over the garden.
A cantilevered beam allows for open space beneath the structure.
They marveled at the cantilevered walkway jutting out from the cliff.

Related Terms
