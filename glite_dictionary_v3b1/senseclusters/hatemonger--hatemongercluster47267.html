hatemonger

noun
person who incites hatred
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪtˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈheɪtˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
A person who encourages or incites hatred towards others, especially by spreading hostile opinions or propaganda.

Examples

A hatemonger spreads hate
Communities must stand firm against every hatemonger who tries to divide them.
He earned a reputation as a notorious hatemonger during the election campaign.
The radio host was accused of being a hatemonger for his inflammatory speeches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demagogue - a political leader who seeks support by appealing to prejudice and emotion
  • antonym: peacekeeper - a person who tries to maintain or promote peace
