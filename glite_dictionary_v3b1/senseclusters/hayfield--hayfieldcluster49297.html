hayfield

noun
field for growing hay
6.9 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈheɪ.fiːld/ /; / /ˈheɪ.fɪld/ (AmE), /ˈheɪ.fiːld/ /; / /ˈheɪ.fɪld/ (BrE)
