fading away

noun
gradual disappearance or weakening
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdɪŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdɪŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
a gradual diminishing in brightness, loudness, or strength until almost nothing remains

Examples

The fading away light
He watched the fading away of the sunset colors with a heavy heart.
The fading away of the music signaled the end of the ceremony.
The patient's strength showed a slow fading away over several weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fade-out - the process of becoming gradually less distinct, especially in film or music
  • synonym: vanishing - the process of disappearing completely
