unresisting

adjective
offering no resistance; passive
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɪst.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɪst.ɪŋ / (BrE)
not putting up resistance; willing to accept or submit without opposition.

Examples

Unresisting attitude
His unresisting attitude made the negotiations much smoother.
She remained unresisting as the guards led her away.
The citizens were surprisingly unresisting in the face of new regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resistant - offering opposition or defense
