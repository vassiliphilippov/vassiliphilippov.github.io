idle

noun
state of a running engine at minimal speed
3.8 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.dəl/ (BrE)
the state of an engine or other machine running without load and at low speed, especially while not in use

Examples

Engine idle

The mechanic listened to the engine's idle to check for any unusual noises.
