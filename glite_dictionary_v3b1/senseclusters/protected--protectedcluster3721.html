protected

adjective
kept safe from harm or danger
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktɪd / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪd / (BrE)
kept safe from harm, danger, or injury; guarded or shielded from potential threats or loss

Examples

A protected witness
A protected witness testified in the high-profile case.
Certain areas of the rainforest are now protected by government law.
The eggs were kept in a protected enclosure to prevent predators from reaching them.
He felt protected from the cold by his thick coat.
Some species are protected by law to prevent them from becoming extinct.
The rare plant is protected by a fence to prevent damage from animals.
