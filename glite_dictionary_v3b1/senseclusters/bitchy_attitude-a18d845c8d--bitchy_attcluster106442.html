bitchy attitude

noun
spiteful or malicious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtʃi ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃi ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an irritatingly rude, spiteful, or malicious manner or demeanor, often characterized by sarcasm or cutting remarks

Examples

Bitchy attitude at work
Her bitchy attitude made working with her extremely difficult for the whole team.
Sometimes his bitchy attitude gets him into trouble with friends and colleagues.
The manager didn't tolerate anyone with a bitchy attitude in the office.
