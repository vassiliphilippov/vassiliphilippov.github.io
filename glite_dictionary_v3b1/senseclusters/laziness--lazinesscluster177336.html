laziness

noun
relaxed, easygoing atmosphere or activity
5.5 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪzi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈleɪzi.nəs / (BrE)
a state or atmosphere characterized by relaxed and easy activity, often inducing comfort or a lack of urgency

Examples

Holiday laziness

The laziness of the summer afternoon made it hard for anyone to work.
There was a pleasant laziness about the morning as we sipped our coffee on the porch.
