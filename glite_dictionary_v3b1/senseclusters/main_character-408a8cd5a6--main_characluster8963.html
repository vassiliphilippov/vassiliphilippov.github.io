main character

noun
the chief figure in a story or narrative
Pronunciation: / meɪn ˈkɛrɪktər / (AmE), / meɪn ˈkarɪktə / (BrE)
the most important or central figure in a story, play, film, or other narrative

Examples

The main character in a story
She always tries to relate her life to the main character in her favorite TV show.
The main character faces a difficult dilemma at the end of the movie.
The main character undergoes significant growth throughout the novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protagonist - the leading character or one of the major characters in a drama, movie, novel, or other narrative
