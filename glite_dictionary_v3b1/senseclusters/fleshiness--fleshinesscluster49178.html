fleshiness

noun
having more than average fat or soft tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈflɛʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having more than average fat or soft tissue; plumpness or corpulence

Examples

The fleshiness of face
As he aged, a certain fleshiness appeared in his face and hands.
Some artists prefer to capture the fleshiness of the human body in their paintings.
The fleshiness of the ripe peaches made them particularly juicy and delicious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leanness - the quality of having little fat or flesh; thinness
  • degree higher: obesity - the condition of being very fat or overweight
  • synonym: plumpness - the quality of being pleasantly fat or rounded
