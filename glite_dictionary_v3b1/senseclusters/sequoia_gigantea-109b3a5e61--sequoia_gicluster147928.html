A species of extremely tall evergreen tree native to the southern Sierra Nevada in California, known as the giant sequoia and recognized as the largest living organism by volume.

Examples

The Sequoia gigantea tree
Scientists study the Sequoia gigantea to understand how these trees survive for millennia.
The Sequoia gigantea can live for over three thousand years, making it one of the oldest living trees.
We visited a grove of Sequoia gigantea in California, dwarfed by their massive trunks.
