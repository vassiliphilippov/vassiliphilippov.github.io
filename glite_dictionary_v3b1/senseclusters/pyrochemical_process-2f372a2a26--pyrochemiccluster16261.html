pyrochemical process

noun
chemical process at high temperatures
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪroʊˈkɛmɪkəl ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərəʊˈkɛmɪkəl ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a chemical process that takes place at elevated temperatures, often to induce or accelerate a chemical reaction

Examples

A pyrochemical process at high temperature
A pyrochemical process can be used to recycle nuclear fuel in high-temperature environments.
Research into the pyrochemical process has led to more energy-efficient methods in metallurgy.
The extraction of metals from ores often involves a pyrochemical process to separate valuable elements.
