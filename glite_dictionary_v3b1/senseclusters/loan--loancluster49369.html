loan

noun
a word borrowed from another language
Pronunciation: / loʊn / (AmE), / ləʊn / (BrE)
a word adopted from another language and used as part of the vocabulary of the borrowing language

Examples

A language loan

Many culinary terms in English, such as 'soufflé', are French loans.
The English word piano is actually a loan from Italian.
