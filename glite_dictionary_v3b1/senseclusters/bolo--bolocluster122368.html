bolo

noun
cord necktie with ornamental clasp
9.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊ.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊ.ləʊ/ (BrE)
a cord fastened around the neck with an ornamental clasp, worn as a necktie, especially in Western fashion

Examples

A bolo tie
He wore a silver bolo with a turquoise clasp to the wedding.
The cowboy adjusted his bolo before stepping onto the stage.
Western wear often features a bolo instead of a traditional necktie.
