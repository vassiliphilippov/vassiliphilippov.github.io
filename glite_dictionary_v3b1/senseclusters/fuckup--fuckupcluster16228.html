fuckup

noun
incompetent person who makes mistakes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈfʌk.ʌp / (BrE)
a person who repeatedly makes serious mistakes, especially due to incompetence or carelessness

Examples

He is a fuckup
Despite being labeled a fuckup, he was determined to prove everyone wrong.
Everyone knew Tom was a fuckup after he lost the company thousands of dollars.
She always worried they would see her as a fuckup because of one bad decision.
