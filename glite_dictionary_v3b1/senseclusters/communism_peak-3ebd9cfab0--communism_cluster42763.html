Communism Peak

proper noun
former name of Tajikistan's highest mountain
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪzəm piːk / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪzəm piːk / (BrE)
the former name for Ismail Samani Peak, the highest mountain in the Pamir range in northeastern Tajikistan, near the Chinese border (24,590 feet/7,495 meters high)

Examples

Communism Peak mountain
In 1960, a Soviet expedition successfully climbed Communism Peak for the first time.
Many climbers still refer to Ismail Samani Peak by its former name, Communism Peak.
The challenging weather conditions on Communism Peak have tested the skill of many mountaineers.
