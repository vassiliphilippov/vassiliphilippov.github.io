Livonian

noun
Finnic language of Latvia and Estonia
Pronunciation: / lɪˈvoʊniən / (AmE), / lɪˈvəʊniən / (BrE)
the Finnic language formerly spoken by the Livonian people of Latvia and Estonia

Examples

Livonian language
Few people today can understand the ancient texts written in Livonian.
She is studying endangered languages, including Livonian and Votic.
The last native speaker of Livonian passed away in the 21st century.
