Hydrozoa

noun
a class of coelenterate animals (hydras, jellyfish)
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈzoʊə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪdrəˈzəʊə /; / ˌhʌɪdrəʊˈzəʊə / (BrE)
a class of mostly marine animals (coelenterates) including hydras and some jellyfish, with a life cycle that usually has both polyp and medusa stages
