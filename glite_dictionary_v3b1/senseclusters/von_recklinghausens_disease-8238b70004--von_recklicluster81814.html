von Recklinghausen's disease

noun
hereditary disorder with skin spots and neurofibromas
Pronunciation: / vɑn ˈrɛklɪnˌhaʊzənz dɪˈziz / (AmE), / vɒn ˈrɛklɪŋˌhaʊzənz dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
an inherited disorder characterized by numerous neurofibromas, skin spots, and often developmental abnormalities; also known as neurofibromatosis type 1
