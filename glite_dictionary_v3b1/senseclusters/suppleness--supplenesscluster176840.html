suppleness

noun
flexible and graceful quality
6.4 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌp.lə.nəs/ /; / /ˈsʌp.lɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsʌp.lə.nəs/ /; / /ˈsʌp.lɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being flexible and bending easily without breaking (physical flexibility or gracefulness)

Examples

Physical suppleness
The dancer's suppleness was evident in the way she moved across the stage.
Tigers are admired for the suppleness and speed of their bodies in the wild.
Yoga can improve the suppleness of your joints and muscles.
A dancer needs both strength and suppleness to perform fluid movements.
Her yoga practice greatly improved the suppleness of her muscles.
The suppleness of the leather made it ideal for crafting gloves.
