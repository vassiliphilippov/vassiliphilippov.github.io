(of an object or substance) likely to decay, deteriorate, or spoil, especially in a short time.

Examples

Perishable goods
Flowers are highly perishable and should be delivered quickly to retain their freshness.
Fruit and vegetables are perishable goods that need to be stored carefully.
You should keep all perishable food items in the refrigerator to prevent spoilage.

Synonyms

  • spoilable
    5.8 0.00/m
    spoilable describes things that go bad quickly, often food, while perishable stresses short life and need for fast use or storage.
  • decayable
    6.5 0.00/m
    decayable means able to rot or decompose, often by natural processes, while perishable usually means items spoil quickly and need fast use.
