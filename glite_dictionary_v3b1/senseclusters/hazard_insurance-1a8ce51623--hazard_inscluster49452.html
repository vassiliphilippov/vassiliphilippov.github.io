hazard insurance

noun
insurance covering damage risks like fire and storms
8.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhæz.ərd ɪn.ˈʃʊr.əns/ (AmE), /ˈhæz.əd ɪn.ˈʃɔː.rəns/ /; / /ˈhæz.əd ɪn.ˈʃʊə.rəns/ (BrE)
insurance that provides protection against specified risks, such as damage from storms or fires

Examples

Hazard insurance policy
After the hurricane, the claim for damages was covered by their hazard insurance policy.
Most mortgage lenders require homeowners to carry hazard insurance to protect the property against fire and other risks.
The cost of hazard insurance can vary depending on the location and type of risk involved.
