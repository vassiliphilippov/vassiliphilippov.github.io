intentionally

adverb
deliberately; on purpose
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈten.ʃə.nə.liː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈten.ʃə.nə.liː/ (BrE)
Deliberately; in a way that is done on purpose rather than by accident.

Examples

Done intentionally
He broke the vase intentionally to make a point during the discussion.
If you intentionally avoid the meeting, your absence will be noticed.
She intentionally left her phone at home to enjoy a quiet evening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidentally - in a way that happens by chance, not deliberately
  • synonym: deliberately - in a way that is planned or intended
