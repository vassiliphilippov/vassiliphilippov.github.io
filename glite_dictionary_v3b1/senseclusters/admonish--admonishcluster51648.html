admonish

verb
warn or advise firmly
4.6 5.5
Pronunciation: / ədˈmɑːnɪʃ /; / ædˈmɑːnɪʃ /; / ədˈmɑnɪʃ / (AmE), / ədˈmɒnɪʃ / (BrE)
