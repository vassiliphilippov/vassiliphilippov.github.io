unsupportive

adjective
not giving help or support
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈpɔrtɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈpɔːtɪv / (BrE)
not giving help, encouragement, or support to someone or something

Examples

An unsupportive friend
He found his colleagues unsupportive during the difficult project.
Her family was unsupportive when she decided to change careers.
It is especially hard to cope with stress in an unsupportive environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: supportive - giving help or encouragement
