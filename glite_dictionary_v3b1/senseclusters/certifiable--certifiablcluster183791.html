certifiable

adjective
legally insane or crazy
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.tɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.tə.faɪ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.tɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ˈsɜː.tə.faɪ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
suffering from a mental disorder severe enough to meet the criteria for legal commitment to a psychiatric hospital; often used informally to describe someone as insane or crazy.

Examples

Certifiable crazy behavior
If he keeps acting like this, people will think he's certifiable.
She was deemed certifiable and was taken to a psychiatric hospital for treatment.
That idea is so wild, it sounds absolutely certifiable to me.
