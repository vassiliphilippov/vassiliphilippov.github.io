practicalism

noun
focus on practicality
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪkəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪkəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a belief in or emphasis on practical approaches and results rather than abstract theories or ideas

Examples

Practicalism in thinking
Her practicalism led her to focus on solutions rather than abstract ideas.
The team's practicalism helped them complete the project efficiently and on time.
He admired her practicalism when dealing with everyday challenges at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pragmatism - a practical approach to problems and affairs
  • antonym: theorism - a focus on theories rather than practical results
