parasitize

verb
to exploit as a parasite
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌsaɪtˌaɪz /; / ˈpærəˌsaɪtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌsɪtaɪz /; / ˈpærəsɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to live on or in another organism and benefit at its expense, often causing harm to the host
