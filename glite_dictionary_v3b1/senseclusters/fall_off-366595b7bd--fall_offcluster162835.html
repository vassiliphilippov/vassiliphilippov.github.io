fall off

verb
decline sharply or drop off
2.2
Pronunciation: / fɔl ɔf /; / fɔl ɑf / (AmE), / fɔːl ɒf / (BrE)
to decrease, decline, or become less in amount, quality, or intensity

Examples

Interest in the project has fallen off since the initial excitement.
