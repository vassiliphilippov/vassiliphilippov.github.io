SDLC

noun
software development process
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs diː ɛl ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌɛs diː ɛl ˈsiː / (BrE)
the process or set of steps followed to develop and maintain software, from planning to deployment and maintenance

Examples

The SDLC process
Every software project should follow an SDLC to ensure quality and efficiency.
The team discussed which SDLC model would best fit their new application.
Understanding the SDLC helps developers manage each stage of software creation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agile - an iterative approach to software development
  • synonym: software engineering - the discipline of designing and building software systems
  • synonym: waterfall model - a traditional SDLC model with sequential phases
