forging

noun
shaped metal piece
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
a piece of metal that has been shaped by heating and hammering or pressing

Examples

A steel forging
The blacksmith created a strong forging for the new gate.
Automotive companies often use steel forgings for engine parts.
Each forging must be inspected for cracks before it is used.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casting - a metal object made by pouring molten metal into a mold
  • antonym: machining - shaping metal by cutting or grinding
