barkless

adjective
without bark
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrkləs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkləs / (BrE)
lacking bark, especially referring to a tree or plant without its outer protective covering

Examples

Barkless tree trunk
After the storm, several barkless branches were scattered across the forest floor.
The disease left many trees barkless and vulnerable.
A barkless trunk is more likely to be damaged by insects and weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered by any protective layer
  • antonym: barked - having bark or covered with bark
