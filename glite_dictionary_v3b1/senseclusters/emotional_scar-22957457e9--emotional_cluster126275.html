emotional scar

noun
lasting psychological trauma
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənl skɑr / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl skɑː / (BrE)
a lasting psychological injury or trauma caused by a distressing experience

Examples

An emotional scar from trauma
She carries an emotional scar from the accident, even though her physical injuries have healed.
The emotional scar from his childhood took years to heal.
Therapy helped him address the emotional scar left by the divorce.

Related Terms
