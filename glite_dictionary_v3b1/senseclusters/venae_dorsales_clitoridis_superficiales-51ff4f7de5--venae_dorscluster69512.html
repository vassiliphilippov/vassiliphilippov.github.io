venae dorsales clitoridis superficiales

noun
superficial dorsal veins of the clitoris
Pronunciation: / ˌviː.niː dɔːrˈsælɛs klɪˈtɔːrɪdɪs ˌsuː.pɚˈfɪʃ.iˌæl.is / (AmE), / ˌviː.niː dɔːˈsælɛs klɪˈtɒrɪdɪs ˌsuː.pəˈfɪʃ.iˌæl.is / (BrE)
