Synonyms

  • range of mountains

  • chain of mountains
    3.0 0.04/m
    chain of mountains shows linked peaks like a metal chain laid across land, while mountain chain is the geographic phrase.
  • range
    2.5 7.63/m
    range can mean an area, choice, or hills in everyday use, while mountain chain always means connected mountains.
  • chain
    7.0 1.34/m

  • cordillera
    7.9 0.08/m
