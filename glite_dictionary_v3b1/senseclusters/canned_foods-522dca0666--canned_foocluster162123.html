canned foods

noun
foods preserved in cans
3.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /kænd fuːdz/ (AmE), /kænd fuːdz/ (BrE)
Foods that have been preserved by being sealed in airtight cans, typically for long-term storage.

Examples
