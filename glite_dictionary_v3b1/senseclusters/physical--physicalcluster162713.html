physical

noun
medical examination
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
a medical examination to check a person's general health, often required for school, work, or sports.

Examples

A physical exam
All students must get a physical before joining the sports team.
My company requires a yearly physical for all employees to monitor their health.
She scheduled her physical with the doctor to make sure everything was fine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: checkup - a routine medical examination to assess health
  • synonym: screening - a test or examination to detect health problems
