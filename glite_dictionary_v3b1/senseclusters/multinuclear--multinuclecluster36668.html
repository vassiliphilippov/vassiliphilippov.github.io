multinuclear

adjective
involving multiple nuclei
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
relating to or involving more than one type of atomic nucleus, especially in scientific techniques like NMR.

Examples

Multinuclear NMR analysis
The chemist used a multinuclear approach to analyze the sample's structure.
Advanced multinuclear NMR techniques can provide detailed information about complex molecules.
Researchers compared multinuclear and mononuclear experiments to study different elements in the compound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mononuclear - involving only one type of atomic nucleus
