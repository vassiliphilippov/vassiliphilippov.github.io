preggo glow

noun
radiant look of a pregnant woman
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛɡoʊ ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈprɛɡəʊ ɡləʊ / (BrE)
A radiant and healthy appearance often attributed to pregnant women, thought to be caused by hormonal changes during pregnancy.

Examples

The preggo glow
Everyone commented on her preggo glow as she entered the room smiling.
Her friends noticed the unmistakable preggo glow that seemed to light up her face.
With her preggo glow and cheerful demeanor, she looked happier than ever.
