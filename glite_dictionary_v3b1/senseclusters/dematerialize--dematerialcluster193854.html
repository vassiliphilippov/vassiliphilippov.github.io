dematerialize

verb
become immaterial or disappear
7.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /diː.mə.ˈtɪr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ /; / /dɪ.mə.ˈtɪr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /diː.mə.ˈtɪə.riː.ə.laɪz/ /; / /dɪ.mə.ˈtɪə.riː.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to become immaterial or disappear from view; to cease to have a physical form

Examples

In science fiction stories, characters often dematerialise and reappear elsewhere.
The magician made the coin dematerialise right before our eyes.
