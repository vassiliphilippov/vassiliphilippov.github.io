uvulitis

noun
inflammation of the uvula
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːvjəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌjuːvjʊˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the uvula, the small fleshy extension at the back of the soft palate.

Examples

Uvulitis inflammation
Allergic reactions can sometimes cause uvulitis, leading to discomfort and breathing difficulties.
Swelling and redness of the uvula are common symptoms of uvulitis.
The patient was diagnosed with uvulitis after presenting with a sore throat and difficulty swallowing.
