muddily

adverb
unclearly or with mud
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdɪli / (AmE), / ˈmʌdɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is unclear or covered with mud

Examples

Runs muddily through
After the storm, the river ran muddily through the valley.
He explained his idea so muddily that no one understood what he meant.
The windows were so dirty that the sunlight came in muddily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • synonym: unclearly - in a way that is not clear or easy to understand
