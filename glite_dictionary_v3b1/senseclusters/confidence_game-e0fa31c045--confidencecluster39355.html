confidence game

noun
fraudulent scheme based on gaining trust
5.9 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnfɪdəns ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈkɒnfɪdəns ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a dishonest scheme in which a person is tricked into trusting someone, usually to be cheated or defrauded, especially in gambling or sales

Examples

A confidence game trick
He fell victim to a confidence game that promised huge returns on investment.
Police warned locals about a new confidence game targeting elderly residents.
The confidence game relied on gaining the trust of unsuspecting tourists.
