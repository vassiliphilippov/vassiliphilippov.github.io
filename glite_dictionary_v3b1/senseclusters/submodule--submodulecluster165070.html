submodule

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈsʌb.mɑːd.juːl/ /; / /ˈsʌb.mɑːdʒuːl/ (AmE), /ˈsʌb.mɒd.juːl/ /; / /ˈsʌb.mɒdʒuːl/ (BrE)
