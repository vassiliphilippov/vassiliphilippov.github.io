Tenrecidae

noun
family of tenrecs (insectivorous mammals)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛnˈrɛsɪˌdiː /; / tɛnˈrɛsɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌtɛnˈrɛsɪdiː /; / tɛnˈrɛsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
