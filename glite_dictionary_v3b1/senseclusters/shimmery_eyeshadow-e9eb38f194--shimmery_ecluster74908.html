shimmery eyeshadow

noun
eyeshadow with a shiny finish
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪməri ˈaɪˌʃædoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪməri ˈaɪˌʃædəʊ / (BrE)
eyeshadow that has a shiny, light-reflecting finish, often used to create a sparkling effect on the eyelids

Examples

A shimmery eyeshadow look
Her makeup artist selected a shimmery eyeshadow to highlight her eyes for the red carpet event.
Many beauty influencers recommend using shimmery eyeshadow in the inner corners of the eyes.
The palette includes both matte and shimmery eyeshadow shades for versatile looks.
