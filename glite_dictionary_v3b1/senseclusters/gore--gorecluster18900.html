gore

noun
triangular piece of cloth used in sewing
Pronunciation: / ɡɔr / (AmE), / ɡɔː / (BrE)
a piece of cloth, usually triangular or tapering, that is used in making garments, umbrellas, or sails to provide shape or fullness

Examples

A gore panel
Sailmakers often add a gore to improve the sail's curve and performance.
She sewed a triangular gore into the sleeve to allow for more movement.
The skirt was made with several gores to give it extra flare.
