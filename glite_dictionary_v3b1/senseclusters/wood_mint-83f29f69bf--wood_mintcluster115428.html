wood mint

noun
North American herbaceous plant (Blephilia genus)
Pronunciation: / wʊd mɪnt / (AmE), / wʊd mɪnt / (BrE)
an American herb of the genus Blephilia, with hairy leaves and clusters of purplish or bluish flowers, found in woodlands

Examples
