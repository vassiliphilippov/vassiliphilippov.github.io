digital communication

noun
transmission of digitized information electronically
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒ.ɪ.t̬əl kəˌmjun.ɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒ.ɪ.təl kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the electronic transmission of information that has been encoded digitally, especially for storage and processing by computers

Examples

Digital communication technology
Advancements in digital communication have revolutionized the way we share information and collaborate remotely.
Modern businesses rely heavily on digital communication to connect with customers around the world.
The course covers both analog and digital communication systems in depth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: analog communication - transmission of information by continuously variable signals
