consumerization

noun
adapting for consumers
7.0
Pronunciation: / kənˌsuːmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / kənˌsjuːmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which products or services originally intended for businesses become adapted for and widely used by general consumers

Examples

Consumerization of products
The consumerization of smartphones has changed how people communicate and access information.
Many companies are responding to the consumerization of workplace technology by allowing employees to use their own devices.
The trend of consumerization has led to more user-friendly software in the business world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: democratization - the process of making something accessible to everyone
  • antonym: professionalization - the process of making something suitable for professionals
