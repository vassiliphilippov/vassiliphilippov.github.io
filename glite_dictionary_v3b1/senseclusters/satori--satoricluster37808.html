satori

noun
sudden spiritual enlightenment (Zen)
7.8
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈtɔri / (AmE), / səˈtɔːri / (BrE)
In Zen Buddhism, a sudden moment of spiritual enlightenment or awakening.

Examples

Sudden satori moment
After years of meditation, she finally experienced satori during a silent retreat.
Many seek satori, but only a few truly understand its meaning.
Zen masters teach that satori cannot be forced, only recognized when it arrives.
