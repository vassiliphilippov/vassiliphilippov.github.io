line storm

noun
violent rainstorm near an equinox
Pronunciation: / laɪn stɔrm / (AmE), / laɪn stɔːm / (BrE)
a violent rainstorm occurring near or at the time of an equinox, often moving in a line across a region

Examples

A line storm approaches
Farmers prepared for flooding as another line storm approached the area.
Heavy winds and rain from the line storm caused widespread damage overnight.
The region was battered by a line storm just as spring began.
