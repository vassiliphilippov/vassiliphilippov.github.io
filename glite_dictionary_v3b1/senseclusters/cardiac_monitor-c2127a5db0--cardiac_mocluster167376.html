cardiac monitor

noun
electronic device for monitoring heart activity
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.diː.æk ˈmɑːn.ɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.dɪ.æk ˈmɒn.ɪ.tə/ (BrE)
an electronic device used for the continuous observation of the heart's activity, typically measuring heart rate and rhythm

Examples

A cardiac monitor device
Advances in technology have made the cardiac monitor more compact and portable.
Nurses checked the cardiac monitor regularly for any signs of abnormal heart rhythms.
The patient's heart rate was measured with a cardiac monitor throughout the surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrocardiograph - machine that records the electrical activity of the heart
