cellulosic

adjective
made from or containing cellulose
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛl.jəˈloʊ.sɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛl.jʊˈləʊ.sɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or made from cellulose, especially in reference to fibers, materials, or products derived from cellulose.

Examples

Cellulosic fibers
Many fabrics are made from cellulosic fibers such as rayon and acetate.
Scientists are researching cellulosic materials as alternatives to plastics.
The company developed a new cellulosic biofuel using agricultural waste products.
