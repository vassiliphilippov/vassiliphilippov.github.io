particularistic

adjective

Showing or relating to the exclusive concern for the interests of a particular group, class, or sect, rather than for society as a whole.

Examples

Particularistic group interests
Academic debates sometimes revolve around particularistic versus universalistic approaches.
Her views are often seen as particularistic, focusing narrowly on her own community.
The organization was criticized for its particularistic policies that favored certain members.

Synonyms

  • exclusivistic
    9.8 0.02/m
    exclusivistic rejects other views and protects one group's members, while particularistic shows concern mainly for that group's interests instead of society.
  • parochial
    8.1 2.12/m
    parochial looks only at local or narrow issues and daily concerns, while particularistic cares for a specific group's interests above wider society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: universalistic - relating to universalism; concerning all people or things rather than particular groups
