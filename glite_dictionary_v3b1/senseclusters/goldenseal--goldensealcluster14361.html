goldenseal

noun
a North American herbal plant
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldn̩ˌsil / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldnˌsiːl / (BrE)
a perennial herb (Hydrastis canadensis) of eastern North America, having a thick yellow rootstock and large rounded leaves, used in herbal medicine.

Examples

Goldenseal root extract
Goldenseal is endangered in some areas due to overharvesting.
Many herbalists recommend goldenseal as a natural remedy for digestive issues.
The roots of goldenseal are often dried and used in traditional medicine.
