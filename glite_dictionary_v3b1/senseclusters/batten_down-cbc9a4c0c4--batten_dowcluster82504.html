batten down

verb
secure or furnish with battens
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbætən daʊn / (AmE), / ˈbætən daʊn / (BrE)
to furnish or secure something with battens, typically for protection or reinforcement

Examples

To batten down hatches
Before the storm, the crew worked quickly to batten down the vessel's hatches.
It is essential to batten down all loose materials during construction in case of sudden bad weather.
Workers were hired to batten down the scaffolding to withstand high winds.
