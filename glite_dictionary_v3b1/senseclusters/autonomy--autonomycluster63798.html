autonomy

noun
personal independence; self-government
5.7 7.08/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàt…ën…ômi / (AmE), / …îÀêÀàt…ín…ômi / (BrE)
the ability to make your own decisions independently; personal independence or self-government

Examples

Personal autonomy
After years of strict rules, he finally experienced the autonomy he had always desired.
Many employees value autonomy in the workplace and prefer to set their own schedules.
The teenager wanted more autonomy in making her own choices about her future.

Synonyms

  • self-direction
    6.1 0.17/m
    self-direction is actively guiding your own actions and learning, while autonomy is the broader ability to make independent decisions.
  • self-determination
    4.3 7.02/m
    self-determination is the right or ability of people or nations to choose their course, while autonomy focuses on personal independent decision-making.
  • self-government
    4.3 1.74/m
    self-government is when a group runs its own affairs, while autonomy is personal ability to make independent choices.
  • independence
    3.0 35/m
    independence is freedom from outside control often used for countries, while autonomy emphasizes personal or local decision-making.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dependence - the state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else
  • synonym: independence - the state of being independent, especially personal self-reliance
