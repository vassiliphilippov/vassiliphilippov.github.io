captivity

noun
state of being a slave or held prisoner
1.3 3.7
Pronunciation: / kæpˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / kæpˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state of being kept somewhere and not allowed to leave, such as being a prisoner or under someone else's control
