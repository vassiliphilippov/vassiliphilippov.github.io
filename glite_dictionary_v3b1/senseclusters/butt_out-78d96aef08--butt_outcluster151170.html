butt out

verb
stop interfering; withdraw from involvement
3.0
Pronunciation: / bʌt aʊt / (AmE), / bʌt aʊt / (BrE)
(informal) To stop involving oneself in someone else's affairs; to stop interfering or intruding.

Examples

To butt out now
