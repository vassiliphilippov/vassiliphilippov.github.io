arui

noun
wild sheep of northern Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːruːi / (AmE), / ˈɑːruːi / (BrE)
a wild sheep native to northern Africa, known for its curved horns and shaggy mane

Examples

The arui sheep
Many zoos now keep the arui as part of their conservation programs.
The arui is well adapted to the rocky terrain of the Sahara region.
We spotted a herd of aruis grazing on the cliffs during our expedition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aoudad - another name for the arui, the Barbary sheep
