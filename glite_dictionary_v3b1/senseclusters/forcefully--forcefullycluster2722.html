forcefully

adverb
with great strength or intensity
3.5 4.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îrs.f…ô.li / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀês.f…ô.li / (BrE)
In a way that is full of force, strength, or energy; with strong effect or intensity.

Examples

To close forcefully

The manager forcefully argued his point during the meeting.
The wind blew forcefully against the windows all night.
