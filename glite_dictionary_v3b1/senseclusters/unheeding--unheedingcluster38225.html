unheeding

adjective
not paying attention; heedless
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhidɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
not paying attention or taking notice of something; disregarding or heedless.

Examples

Unheeding advice
He made the decision unheeding his friends’ advice to be cautious.
She walked through the storm, unheeding the fierce wind and driving rain.
The company proceeded with the plan, unheeding the environmental concerns raised by locals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentive - paying close attention to something
  • synonym: heedless - showing a reckless lack of care or attention
