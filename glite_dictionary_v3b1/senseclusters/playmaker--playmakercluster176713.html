playmaker

noun
key attacking player in sports
2.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪ.meɪ.kər/ /; / /ˈpleɪ.mə.kər/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪ.meɪ.kə/ /; / /ˈpleɪ.mə.kə/ (BrE)
