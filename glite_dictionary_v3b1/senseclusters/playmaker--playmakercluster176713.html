playmaker

noun
key attacking player in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a player (especially in team sports) who controls the flow of play and creates opportunities for teammates to succeed

Examples

The playmaker controls game
As the team's playmaker, she set up two goals with her vision and passing.
Every great soccer team needs an effective playmaker to unlock defenses.
The coach praised his star playmaker for controlling the tempo of the game.
The basketball team's playmaker set up several scoring chances for his teammates.
As a playmaker in the band, she always knew how to bring everyone together for a great performance.
Coaches often rely on a skilled playmaker to change the course of a game.

  • antonym: follower - a person who follows the lead of others
  • antonym: goal scorer - player who scores goals
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or guides others
  • synonym: midfielder - player who plays mainly in the middle portion of the field
