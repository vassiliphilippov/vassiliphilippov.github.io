Nipmuc

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪpmʌk / (AmE), / ˈnɪpmʌk / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people originally from central Massachusetts and surrounding regions

Examples

A Nipmuc person
The Nipmuc have lived in the central Massachusetts area for centuries.
A local museum features artifacts from the Nipmuc and their traditional way of life.
Many descendants of the Nipmuc continue to celebrate their heritage today.
