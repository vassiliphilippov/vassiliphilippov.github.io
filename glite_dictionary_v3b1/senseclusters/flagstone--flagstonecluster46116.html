flagstone

noun
flat stone used for paving
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈflæɡ.stəʊn / (BrE)
A flat, thin piece of stone used for paving floors, paths, or patios.

Examples

Flat flagstone path
She admired the moss that grew between the flagstones in the courtyard.
The garden path was lined with irregular flagstone slabs.
They replaced the old wooden floor with a sturdy flagstone one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slab - a large, thick, flat piece of stone or concrete used for paving or construction
