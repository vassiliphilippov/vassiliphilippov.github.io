interdisciplinary

adjective
involving multiple fields of study
3.4 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdɪsəˌplɪˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdɪsɪplɪnəri / (BrE)
involving two or more academic, professional, or artistic disciplines or fields of study

Examples

Interdisciplinary study program

The new curriculum promotes interdisciplinary studies across the sciences and humanities.
The university encourages interdisciplinary research projects that involve both scientists and artists.
