dimethyl

adjective
having two methyl groups
Pronunciation: / daɪˈmɛθəl / (AmE), / daɪˈmiːθəl / (BrE)
describing a molecule or compound that contains two methyl groups attached to it

Examples

Dimethyl compound
The chemist analyzed a dimethyl compound to determine its properties.
Adding a dimethyl group can change the behavior of a molecule in a reaction.
Researchers studied the effects of dimethyl substances in pharmaceutical development.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: trimethyl - containing three methyl groups
