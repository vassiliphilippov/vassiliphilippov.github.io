offishness

noun
distant manner
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːf.ɪʃ.nəs/ /; / /ˈɔːf.ɪʃ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈɒf.ɪʃ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being distant, unfriendly, or not wanting to mix with others

Examples

People sometimes mistake offishness for arrogance, but it can simply mean someone is shy.
