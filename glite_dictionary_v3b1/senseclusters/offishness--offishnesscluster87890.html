offishness

noun
distant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːfɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being distant, unfriendly, or not wanting to mix with others

Examples

His offishness at party
His offishness at the party made it difficult for anyone to start a conversation with him.
Despite her talent, her offishness kept her from making many friends at work.
People sometimes mistake offishness for arrogance, but it can simply mean someone is shy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloofness - the state of being emotionally distant or uninvolved
  • antonym: friendliness - the quality of being kind and pleasant to others
