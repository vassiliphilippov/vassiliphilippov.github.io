tracheoscopy

noun
trachea examination
8.7
Pronunciation: / treɪkiˈɑːskəpi / (AmE), / trəˈkiːəskəpi / (BrE)
a medical examination of the trachea using a special instrument to view its interior

Examples

Tracheoscopy examination
The doctor performed a tracheoscopy to check for airway blockages.
A tracheoscopy can help diagnose problems in the windpipe after an injury.
During the tracheoscopy, the specialist carefully examined the patient's trachea for signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bronchoscopy - a medical examination of the airways using a bronchoscope
  • synonym: laryngoscopy - a medical examination of the larynx using a laryngoscope
