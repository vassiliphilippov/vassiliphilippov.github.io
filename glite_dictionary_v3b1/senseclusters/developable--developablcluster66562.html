developable

adjective
can be flattened
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpəbəl / (BrE)
in geometry, able to be unfolded or flattened into a plane without stretching or tearing

Examples

Developable surface
A cylinder is a simple example of a developable surface that can be flattened without stretching.
In architecture, developable surfaces are often used to design curved roofs that can be made from flat materials.
Unlike a sphere, a cone is a developable surface because it can be opened out into a flat shape.
