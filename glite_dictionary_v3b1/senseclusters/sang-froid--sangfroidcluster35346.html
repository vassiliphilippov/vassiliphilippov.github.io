sang-froid

noun
calmness under pressure
Pronunciation: / sɑ̃ˈfrwɑ / (AmE), / sɒ̃ˈfrwɑ / (BrE)
calmness and composure, especially in difficult or dangerous situations

Examples

Maintain sang-froid
Despite the chaos around her, she maintained her sang-froid throughout the crisis.
His sang-froid impressed the interviewers, even when faced with difficult questions.
In stressful situations, a leader's sang-froid can inspire confidence in the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composure - calm, self-controlled state of mind
  • antonym: nervousness - the state of being easily agitated or alarmed
