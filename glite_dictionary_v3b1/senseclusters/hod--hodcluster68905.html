hod

noun
brick or mortar carrier for construction
8.2
Pronunciation: / hɑd / (AmE), / hɒd / (BrE)
a portable container, typically shaped like a trough and attached to a pole handle, used for carrying bricks or mortar, especially on the shoulder in construction work

Examples

A hod of bricks
Each hod carried enough bricks for several rows of the wall.
He filled the hod with mortar and carefully balanced it as he worked.
The laborer lifted the heavy hod onto his shoulder and began climbing the scaffold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mortarboard - a flat board with a handle used by masons to carry mortar
