boardy

adjective
stiff or rigid
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɔːr.dɪ/ /; / /ˈbɑːr.dɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbɔː.dɪ/ (BrE)
having a texture, stiffness, or appearance similar to that of a board; stiff or rigid

Examples

A boardy fabric
The fabric felt boardy after being left out in the sun for days.
His hair became boardy from using too much styling gel.
The old bread had a boardy texture and was difficult to chew.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rigid - unable to bend or be forced out of shape
