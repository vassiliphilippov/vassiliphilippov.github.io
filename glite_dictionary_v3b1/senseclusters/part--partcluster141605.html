part

noun
assigned role or duty
1.0 340/m
Pronunciation: / p…ërt / (AmE), / p…ëÀêt / (BrE)
the role, responsibility, or contribution of a person in a group, activity, or performance

Examples

Her part in the play

Everyone must do their part to keep the environment clean.
Parents play an important part in their children's education.

There was no mistake on his part; the error came from the office.
