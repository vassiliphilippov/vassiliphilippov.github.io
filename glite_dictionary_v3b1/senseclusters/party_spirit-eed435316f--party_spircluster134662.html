party spirit

noun
strong loyalty to a political party
7.3 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrti ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
Strong loyalty or devotion to a particular political party, often accompanied by partisan feeling and bias.
