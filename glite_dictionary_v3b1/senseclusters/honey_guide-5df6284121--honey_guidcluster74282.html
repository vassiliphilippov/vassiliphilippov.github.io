honey guide

noun
small bird that eats beeswax and honey
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌni ɡaɪd / (AmE), / ˈhʌni ɡaɪd / (BrE)
a small bird of tropical Africa and Asia, known for feeding on beeswax, honey, and bee larvae

Examples

A honey guide bird
Researchers observed a honey guide feasting on the wax left behind after a hive was raided.
The honey guide is famous for leading people and animals to wild bee nests.
Unlike most birds, the honey guide can digest beeswax as a major part of its diet.
