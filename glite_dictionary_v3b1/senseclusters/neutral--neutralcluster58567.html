neutral

noun
subtle color
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtrəl / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəl / (BrE)
a color such as gray, beige, or white that is not bright or strong and easily matches other colors

Examples

Soft neutral for walls
The designer recommended using neutrals on the walls to create a calm atmosphere.
She prefers to wear neutrals because they go well with any accessory.
Many modern homes feature neutrals like gray and beige for a timeless look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beige - a pale sandy yellowish-brown color
  • antonym: bright color - a vivid or strong color
  • synonym: gray - a color between black and white
  • synonym: white - the color of fresh snow or milk
