laudability

noun
quality of being praiseworthy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlɔːdəˈbɪlət̮i / (AmE), / ˌlɔːdəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being deserving of praise

Examples

The laudability of actions
Few questioned the laudability of their environmental initiatives, which brought real change.
His speech emphasized the laudability of striving for excellence in every endeavor.
The laudability of her charitable actions was recognized by the entire community.
