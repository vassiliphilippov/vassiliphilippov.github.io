salesperson

noun
someone whose job is selling goods or services
2.2 2.21/m
Pronunciation: / Ààse…™lzÀåp…ùÀês…ôn / (AmE), / Ààse…™lzÀåp…úÀês…ôn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sell products or services, especially in a store or for a business

Examples

A salesperson in store
Every salesperson in the store was wearing a blue badge.
If you need help finding anything, just ask a salesperson for assistance.
The salesperson offered detailed information about each model of laptop.

Synonyms

  • sales representative
    2.7 0.37/m
    sales representative often visits customers for a company and sells business products, while salesperson is a general seller in stores or firms.
  • salesclerk
    2.4 0.26/m
    salesclerk sells goods behind a shop counter to walk-in customers, while salesperson can mean any seller in stores or businesses.
  • shop clerk
    1.9 0.05/m
    shop clerk helps customers and sells items inside a store all day, while salesperson also covers business or field sales beyond shops.
  • shop assistant
    1.2 0.06/m
    shop assistant mainly helps shoppers and gives advice in a store, while salesperson focuses on making sales for a company or shop.
