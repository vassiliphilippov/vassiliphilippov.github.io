commercial treaty

noun
treaty on international trade and commerce
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝːʃəl ˈtriːti / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl ˈtriːti / (BrE)
an agreement between two or more countries regulating trade and commerce between them

Examples

A commercial treaty agreement
A commercial treaty can eliminate tariffs and create new business opportunities.
Negotiations for a new commercial treaty broke down after several months.
The two countries signed a commercial treaty to encourage trade and investment.
