skater

noun
person who skateboards
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who rides a skateboard, especially to perform tricks or stunts

Examples

Skateboard skater
The young skater landed a difficult trick in front of a cheering crowd.
Many skaters gather at the park after school to practice together.
She became a skilled skater after years of dedication and hard work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rollerblader - a person who skates using inline skates
  • synonym: skateboarder - a person who rides a skateboard
