fishing rod

noun
rod or pole used for fishing
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ rɑd / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ rɒd / (BrE)
a long, thin stick or pole used in fishing to cast and control a fishing line

Examples
