fancy as all get-out

adjective
extremely fancy or extravagant
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfænsi əz ɔl ˈɡɛt aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfænsi əz ɔːl ˈɡɛt aʊt / (BrE)
Extremely fancy or extravagant; very elaborate or ostentatious.

Examples

Fancy as all get-out clothes
He showed up to work fancy as all get-out, wearing a tuxedo for a regular meeting.
That wedding cake was fancy as all get-out, covered in elaborate sugar flowers and intricate designs.
The restaurant was fancy as all get-out, with chandeliers and gold-plated cutlery everywhere.
