redeye

noun
overnight flight with little sleep
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌaɪ / (BrE)
an overnight flight where passengers typically get little sleep and may arrive with red eyes from fatigue

Examples

Take a redeye flight
He hates taking a redeye because he can never sleep on planes.
I took the redeye from Los Angeles and arrived in New York just after sunrise.
If you book the redeye, you'll have the full day ahead once you land.
