cardoon

noun
Mediterranean plant with edible stalks, related to artichoke
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈduːn / (AmE), / kɑːˈduːn / (BrE)
a Mediterranean plant related to the artichoke, grown for its edible stalks and roots
