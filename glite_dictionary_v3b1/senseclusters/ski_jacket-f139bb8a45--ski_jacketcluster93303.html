ski jacket

noun
insulated jacket for skiing
Pronunciation: / ski ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ski ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a warm, weatherproof jacket designed for skiing, typically featuring insulation and windproof or waterproof materials

Examples

A ski jacket pocket
His ski jacket is waterproof and has plenty of pockets.
I forgot my ski jacket, so I had to rent one at the resort.
She wore a bright red ski jacket to keep warm on the slopes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorak - a waterproof jacket, typically with a hood
  • synonym: parka - a large windproof jacket with a hood, often lined with fur or faux fur
