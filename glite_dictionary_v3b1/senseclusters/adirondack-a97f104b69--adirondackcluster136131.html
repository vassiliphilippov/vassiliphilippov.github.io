Adirondack

noun
person from Adirondacks
Pronunciation: / ˌædəˈrɑːndæk / (AmE), / ˌædəˈrɒndæk / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the Adirondack region of northeastern New York State

Examples

An Adirondack resident
As an Adirondack, she grew up surrounded by forests and mountains.
Many Adirondacks take pride in their region's natural beauty and outdoor traditions.
The festival brought together Adirondacks from small towns across the area.
