house sitter

noun
person who looks after a house when owners are away
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊs ˌsɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs ˌsɪtə / (BrE)
a person who stays in and looks after someone's house while the owners are away, usually without payment

Examples

A house sitter stays
As a house sitter, she made sure the plants were watered and the mail was collected.
The house sitter stayed for two weeks and kept the house in perfect condition.
We hired a house sitter to look after our pets while we were on vacation.
