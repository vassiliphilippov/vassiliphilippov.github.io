unbolt

verb
remove or undo a bolt
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈboʊlt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbəʊlt / (BrE)
to remove or undo the bolt securing something, especially a door or window

Examples

To unbolt the door
Before leaving, always check that you have unbolted the windows.
He unbolts the gate every morning so the kids can play outside.
She quickly unbolted the door to let in her friend.
