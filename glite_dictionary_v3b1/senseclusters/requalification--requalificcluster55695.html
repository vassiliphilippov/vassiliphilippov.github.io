requalification

noun
qualifying again
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌkwɑːlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of qualifying again for a competition, event, or position by meeting required standards

Examples

Requalification for the finals
After her injury, her strong performance led to requalification for the national team.
The new rules required all participants to undergo requalification before the next round.
His requalification was celebrated by his teammates after months of hard work.
