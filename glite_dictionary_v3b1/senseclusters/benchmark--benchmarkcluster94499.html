benchmark

verb
to compare to standard
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːrk/ (AmE), /ˈbentʃ.mɑːk/ (BrE)
to measure or compare something against a standard or best practice

Examples

To benchmark results
The school benchmarked its test scores against national averages to identify areas for improvement.
We regularly benchmark our products to ensure they meet industry standards.
The new software was benchmarked to compare its speed with leading competitors.
