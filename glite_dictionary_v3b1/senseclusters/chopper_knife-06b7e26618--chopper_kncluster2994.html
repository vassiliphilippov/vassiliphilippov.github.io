chopper knife

noun
large knife for chopping food
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːpər naɪf / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpə naɪf / (BrE)
a large knife with a broad blade, used for chopping or mincing meat, vegetables, and other ingredients

Examples

A chopper knife blade
His old wooden-handled chopper knife had served him well in the kitchen for years.
She prefers using a chopper knife when preparing large amounts of vegetables for soup.
The chef reached for his chopper knife to efficiently cut through the pork ribs.
