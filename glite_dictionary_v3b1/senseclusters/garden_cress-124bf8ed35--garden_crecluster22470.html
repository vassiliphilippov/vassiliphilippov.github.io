garden cress

noun
peppery salad green
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən krɛs / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn krɛs / (BrE)
A fast-growing leafy plant with peppery-flavored leaves, often cultivated for use in salads and as a garnish.

Examples

Fresh garden cress
She sprinkled fresh garden cress on the egg sandwich for extra flavor.
The salad was topped with a handful of spicy garden cress leaves.
We planted garden cress in the windowsill because it grows quickly indoors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cress - a family of plants with small, pungent leaves, often used in salads
