strategic operation

noun
military action for long-term objectives
Pronunciation: / strəˈtiːdʒɪk ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / strəˈtiːdʒɪk ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a military action or series of actions carefully planned to achieve long-term or overarching goals

Examples

A strategic operation plan
After months of planning, the army launched a strategic operation in the northern region.
Intelligence gathering was crucial to the success of the strategic operation.
The general outlined a strategic operation aimed at cutting off enemy supply lines.
