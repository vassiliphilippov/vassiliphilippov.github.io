scaredy-cat

interjection
teasing exclamation
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛrdi kæt / (AmE), / ˈskeədi kæt / (BrE)
an exclamation used to tease someone for being afraid or unwilling to do something

Examples

Scaredy-cat! shouted
When he refused to go into the dark room, his friends called him a scaredy-cat.
Don't be such a scaredy-cat—it's just a harmless roller coaster.
She laughed and shouted scaredy-cat as her brother hesitated at the edge of the pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicken - an informal term for someone who is easily frightened
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
