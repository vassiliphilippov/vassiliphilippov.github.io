useful

adjective
serving a practical purpose; helpful
1.0 87/m
Pronunciation: / Ààjusf…ô…´ / (AmE), / ÀàjuÀêsf äl / (BrE)
able to be used for a practical purpose or in a helpful way; of service or benefit.

Examples

A useful tool
Keeping a notebook can be useful for organizing your daily tasks.
She found his advice quite useful during the interview process.
This tool is extremely useful for repairing small household items.

Synonyms

    1.0 11/m

  • helpful
    1.0 51/m
    helpful is someone or something that gives assistance or makes tasks easier, while useful describes something that serves a practical purpose.
