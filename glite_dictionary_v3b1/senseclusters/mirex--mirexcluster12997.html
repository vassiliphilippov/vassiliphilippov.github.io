mirex

noun
banned insecticide
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪrɛks / (AmE), / ˈmaɪrɛks / (BrE)
a toxic insecticide formerly used to control fire ants and other pests, now banned due to environmental concerns

Examples

Traces of mirex can still be found in some soil decades after its use was stopped.
