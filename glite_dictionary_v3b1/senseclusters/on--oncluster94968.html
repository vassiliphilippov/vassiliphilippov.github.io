adjective, adverb
activated; in operation (device/system)
1.0 14/m
Pronunciation: / É‘n / (AmE), / É’n / (BrE)
in operation, switched on, or activated (of a device, machine, or system)

Examples

Please leave the lights on while you are in the room.

Synonyms

  • in operation
    3.2 2/m
    in operation describes something currently working or in use, while on is the short word for a device being switched on.
  • operational
    4.1 4/m
    operational often means ready and able to work in a system, while on refers to a device being switched on.
