on

adjective, adverb
activated; in operation (device/system)
1.0 15/m
Pronunciation: / É‘n / (AmE), / É’n / (BrE)
in operation, switched on, or activated (of a device, machine, or system)

Examples

On the lights
Is your computer on or should I turn it on for you?
Please leave the lights on while you are in the room.
The radio was already on when I walked in.
Make sure the television is on before you try to change the channel.
Please leave the kitchen light on until I get home.
The alarm system stays on all night for security reasons.

Synonyms

  • in operation
    3.2 2.22/m
    in operation describes something currently working or in use, while on is the short word for a device being switched on.
  • operational
    4.1 4.68/m
    operational often means ready and able to work in a system, while on refers to a device being switched on.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off - not operating; deactivated (device/system)
