journalist

noun
writer or reporter for news media
2.3 54/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ùÀê.n…ô.l…™st / (AmE), / Ààd í…úÀê.n…ô.l…™st / (BrE)
A person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites, or prepares news to be broadcast.

Examples

A journalist writes news
As a journalist, he is committed to reporting the truth without bias.
She has worked as a journalist for several major newspapers over the past decade.
The journalist conducted interviews with witnesses at the scene of the accident.

Synonyms

  • newswriter
    3.3 0.02/m
    a newswriter writes news stories for newspapers or broadcast outlets, while a journalist edits, investigates, and prepares news across many media.
  • reporter
    1.1 112/m
    a reporter investigates and presents news for newspapers, TV, or radio, while a journalist also writes, edits, and produces stories across media.
  • newsperson
    2.8 0.03/m
    a newsperson is a gender-neutral term for someone who reports or edits news, while a journalist is the usual general name in media.
