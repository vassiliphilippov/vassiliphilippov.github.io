junky

adjective
poor quality
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
of poor quality, badly made, or considered worthless

Examples

Junky headphones
He threw away the junky headphones that stopped working after a week.
The apartment was filled with junky furniture that looked ready to fall apart.
She regretted buying such a junky toy for her nephew's birthday.
He regretted buying such a junky phone when it stopped working after a month.
The furniture looked nice online, but it turned out to be really junky in person.
Avoid those junky souvenirs that break before you even get home.
