self-propelling

adjective
able to move by its own power
Pronunciation: / sɛlf prəˈpɛlɪŋ /; / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛlf prəˈpɛlɪŋ /; / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
able to move by itself, without being pushed or pulled by something else
