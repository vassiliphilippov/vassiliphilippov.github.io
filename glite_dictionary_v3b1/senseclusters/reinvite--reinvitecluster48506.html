reinvite

verb
to invite again
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈvaɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈvaɪt / (BrE)
to invite someone again to an event or place after a previous invitation or cancellation

Examples

To reinvite a guest
After the misunderstanding was cleared up, she decided to reinvite her friend to the party.
The manager chose to reinvite the guest speaker for next year's conference.
If you accidentally remove someone from the group, you can always reinvite them.
