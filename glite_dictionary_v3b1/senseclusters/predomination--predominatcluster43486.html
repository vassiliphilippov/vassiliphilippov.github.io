predomination

noun
state of being predominant
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː.dɑː.məˈneɪ.ʃən /; / ˌpriːˌdɑːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriː.dɒm.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən /; / ˌpriːˌdɒmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of being the most important, powerful, or noticeable thing among others
