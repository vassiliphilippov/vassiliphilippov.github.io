braziletto

noun
tree for dye and bows
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbræzɪˈlɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbræzɪˈlɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a tropical tree whose hard, reddish wood is used for making dye and bows for string instruments

Examples

Braziletto tree wood
The craftsman selected braziletto for its strength and beautiful color when making the violin bow.
Historically, braziletto was exported for use in textile dyeing and fine woodworking.
The reddish hue of braziletto wood makes it popular among instrument makers.
