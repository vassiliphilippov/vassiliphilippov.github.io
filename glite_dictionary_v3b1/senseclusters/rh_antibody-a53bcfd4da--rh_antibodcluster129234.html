Rh antibody

noun
antibody against Rh factor in blood
Pronunciation: / ɑr eɪtʃ ˈæn.tiˌbɑː.di / (AmE), / ɑː ɪtʃ ˈæn.tɪˌbɒd.i / (BrE)
An antibody produced against the Rh (Rhesus) factor on red blood cells, often significant in blood transfusions and pregnancy.

Examples

Rh antibody test
If the mother has an Rh antibody, special precautions may be needed during pregnancy.
Testing for Rh antibody is a routine part of prenatal care.
The presence of Rh antibody can cause complications in blood transfusions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Rh factor - an antigen present on red blood cells of most people
  • synonym: antibody - a protein produced in response to antigens
