Pieter Breughel

proper noun
Flemish painter (1525–1569)
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpitər ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡəl / (BrE)
(1525–1569) A Flemish painter known for his detailed landscapes and peasant scenes, regarded as one of the greatest artists of the Dutch and Flemish Renaissance.
