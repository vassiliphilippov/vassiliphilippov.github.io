overexercise

noun
excessive physical activity
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɛksərsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
excessive or too frequent physical activity that may lead to negative health effects

Examples

Risk of overexercise
Some athletes suffer injuries due to overexercise and not allowing their bodies enough time to recover.
Doctors warn that overexercise can be just as harmful as not exercising at all.
Her fatigue was a result of months of overexercise without proper nutrition or rest.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: exercise - physical activity to improve health or fitness
  • synonym: overtraining - exercising too much without enough rest, leading to fatigue or injury
  • antonym: rest - a period of relaxation or inactivity
