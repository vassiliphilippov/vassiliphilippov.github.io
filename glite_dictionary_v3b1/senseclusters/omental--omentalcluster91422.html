omental

adjective
of the omentum
Pronunciation: / oʊˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / əʊˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to the omentum, a fold of the peritoneum in the abdomen

Examples

Omental cyst
The doctor discovered an omental cyst during the abdominal surgery.
An omental biopsy was performed to diagnose the cause of the swelling.
The scan revealed omental thickening, which required further investigation.

Related Terms
