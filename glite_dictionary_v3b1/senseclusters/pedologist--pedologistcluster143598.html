pedologist

noun
soil scientist
9.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / pɪˈdɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies soils, including their formation, classification, and mapping

Examples

Pedologist examines soil
The pedologist analyzed the soil samples to determine their mineral content.
A pedologist can help farmers improve crop yields by understanding soil composition.
During the expedition, the pedologist mapped different soil types across the region.
