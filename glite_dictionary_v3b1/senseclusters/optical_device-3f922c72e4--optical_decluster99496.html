optical device

noun
device for producing or controlling light
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑptɪkəl dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a device designed to produce, modify, or control light, especially for visual or scientific purposes

Examples

An optical device for science
A microscope is an optical device that allows us to see tiny objects in detail.
Cameras rely on at least one optical device to focus light onto the sensor.
The laboratory contains every kind of optical device needed for advanced experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lens - a piece of glass or other transparent material used to focus or disperse light in optical devices
