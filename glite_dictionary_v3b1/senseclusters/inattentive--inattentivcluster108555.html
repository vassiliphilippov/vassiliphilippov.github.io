inattentive

adjective
not paying attention; careless
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv / (BrE)
not paying attention or showing a lack of care or consideration

Examples

An inattentive driver
An inattentive driver can easily cause an accident on busy roads.
Her inattentive response made it clear she was thinking about something else.
The teacher grew frustrated with the inattentive students at the back of the classroom.
He was criticized for being inattentive to his guests at the party.
Her inattentive driving nearly caused an accident on the highway.
The teacher noticed that some students were inattentive during the lecture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentive - paying close attention; alert and focused
  • synonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: negligent - failing to take proper care in doing something
