replay

noun
an event played again, especially a game
1.4 2.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈpleɪ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈpleɪ/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈpleɪ/ /; / /riː.ˈpleɪ/ (BrE)
an event, especially a game or sporting match, that is played again after an earlier contest ended inconclusively or to review the outcome

Examples

A replay match
After the controversial result, the teams agreed to a replay of the match next week.
Many fans watched the replay to see if the goal should have counted.
The referee ordered a replay due to bad weather affecting the first game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rematch - a second match between the same opponents
