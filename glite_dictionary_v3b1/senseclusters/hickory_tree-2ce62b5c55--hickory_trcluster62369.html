hickory tree

noun
American hardwood tree with edible nuts
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪkəri triː / (AmE), / ˈhɪkəri triː / (BrE)
An American hardwood tree of the genus Carya, bearing edible nuts and valued for its strong wood.

Examples

The hickory tree wood
Furniture made from hickory tree wood is prized for its strength and durability.
