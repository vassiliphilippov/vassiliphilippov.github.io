piercing

noun
body hole for jewelry
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪrsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪəsɪŋ / (BrE)
a small hole made in the body, usually to wear jewelry such as earrings or studs

Examples

A nose piercing
She got a new piercing in her nose to celebrate her birthday.
Many teenagers choose to get a piercing as a form of self-expression.
After the piercing healed, he started wearing a small silver stud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earring - a piece of jewelry worn on the ear, often through a piercing
  • synonym: tattoo - a permanent design made on the skin with ink
