lovable

adjective
inspiring love or affection
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəb(ə)l / (BrE)
Inspiring or worthy of love or affection.

Examples

Lovable person
Even though he was a little clumsy, his lovable nature won everyone over.
She has a lovable sense of humor that makes her fun to be around.
The puppy was so lovable that everyone wanted to take him home.
