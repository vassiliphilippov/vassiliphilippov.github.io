oriented

adjective
adjusted or directed in relation to something
4.4 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.iː.ənt.ɪd/ /; / /ˈɔːr.iː.ən.təd/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.rɪ.ənt.ɪd/ /; / /ˈɔː.riː.ən.təd/ (BrE)
