heath pea

noun
European herb with edible tubers
8.0 8.9
Pronunciation: /hiːθ piː/ (AmE), /hiːθ piː/ (BrE)
A European herb (Lathyrus linifolius) bearing small edible tubers, sometimes used in Scotland to flavor whisky.
