heath pea

noun
European herb with edible tubers
8.0
Pronunciation: / hiːθ piː / (AmE), / hiːθ piː / (BrE)
A European herb (Lathyrus linifolius) bearing small edible tubers, sometimes used in Scotland to flavor whisky.

Examples

Heath pea tubers
Local distillers sometimes used heath pea tubers to add a unique flavor to their whisky.
The botanical garden has a rare specimen of the heath pea on display.
The roots of the heath pea were once widely gathered for food in some parts of Scotland.
