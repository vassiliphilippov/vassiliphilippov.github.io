grain merchant

noun
person or business selling food grains
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪn ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / ɡreɪn ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells food grains such as wheat, rice, or corn

Examples
