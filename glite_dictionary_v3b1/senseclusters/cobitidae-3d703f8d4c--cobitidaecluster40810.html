Cobitidae

noun
family of freshwater fishes (loaches)
Pronunciation: / koʊˈbɪt.ɪˌdi / (AmE), / kəʊˈbɪt.ɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of small, slender freshwater fishes commonly known as loaches, found mainly in Eurasia.
