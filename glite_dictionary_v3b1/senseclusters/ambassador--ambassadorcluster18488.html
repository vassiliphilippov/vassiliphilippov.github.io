ambassador

noun
person serving as a representative or promoter
5.7 4.5
Pronunciation: / æmˈbæsədər / (AmE), / æmˈbæsədə / (BrE)
a person who acts as a representative or promoter of a particular activity, cause, or group, especially in an informal capacity

Examples

Charity ambassador
As an ambassador for the charity, she speaks at events and visits local schools.
He acted as an ambassador of the sport, inspiring young athletes across the country.
The company appointed her as their brand ambassador to promote their new line of products.
