unticketed

adjective
without a ticket
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɪkɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪkɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having a ticket or not requiring a ticket for entry or participation

Examples

Unticketed guests
The security guard asked all unticketed visitors to wait outside the entrance.
Only unticketed passengers need to purchase a ticket before boarding the train.
The event was free, so everyone was unticketed and could enter without registration.
