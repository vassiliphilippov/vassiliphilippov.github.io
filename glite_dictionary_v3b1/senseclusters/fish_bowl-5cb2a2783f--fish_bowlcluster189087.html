fish bowl

noun
glass bowl for keeping pet fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃ ˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌbəʊl / (BrE)
a clear, usually round container used to keep small fish as pets

Examples

A fish bowl with goldfish
After cleaning the fish bowl, he returned it to its stand by the window.
She kept her goldfish in a large fish bowl on the kitchen table.
The children gazed curiously at the colorful fish swimming inside the fish bowl.

  • synonym: aquarium - a tank for keeping aquatic animals or plants
