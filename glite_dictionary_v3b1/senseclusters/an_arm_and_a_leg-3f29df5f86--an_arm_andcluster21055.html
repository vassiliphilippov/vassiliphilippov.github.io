an arm and a leg

noun
very high price; great expense
2.6
Pronunciation: / ən ɑrm ənd ə lɛg / (AmE), / ən ɑːm ənd ə lɛg / (BrE)
a very high price or a large amount of money

Examples

Costs an arm and a leg
Getting a ticket to the final match could set you back an arm and a leg.
She wanted the dress, but it was going for an arm and a leg.
The repairs to our car cost an arm and a leg last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cost a fortune - to cost a large amount of money
  • antonym: dirt cheap - very inexpensive or low in price
