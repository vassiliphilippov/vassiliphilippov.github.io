qtr

noun
housing for personnel
9.5
Pronunciation: / kjuː ti ɑːr / (AmE), / kjuː ti ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for quarters, meaning a place where people live or are housed, especially for personnel such as soldiers or workers
