anthropologist

noun
person who studies anthropology
1.3 4.8
Pronunciation: /æn.θrə.ˈpɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ /; / /æn.θroʊ.ˈpɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /æn.θrə.ˈpɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in anthropology, especially the study of human societies and cultures and their development

Examples

An anthropologist studies cultures
As an anthropologist, she is fascinated by the ways different cultures celebrate marriage.
Many anthropologists conduct fieldwork in remote regions to gather firsthand data about human societies.
The anthropologist spent several years living among the indigenous tribe to better understand their customs.
