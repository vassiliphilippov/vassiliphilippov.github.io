Ariadne

proper noun
mythical daughter of Minos; aided Theseus
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæriˈædni / (AmE), / ˌæriˈadni / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the daughter of Minos and Pasiphae who helped Theseus escape the Minotaur's labyrinth by giving him a thread.

Examples

Ariadne in mythology
According to legend, Ariadne gave Theseus a thread to help him find his way out.
The painting depicted Ariadne abandoned on the island of Naxos.
The story of Ariadne and the labyrinth is one of the most famous Greek myths.
