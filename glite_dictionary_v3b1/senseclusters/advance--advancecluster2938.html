advance

adjective
situated ahead or going before
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
done or given ahead of time; situated ahead of others

Examples

Advance team
The advance party arrived to set up camp before the main group.
The general sent an advance team to survey the area.
They deployed an advance unit to secure the perimeter.
An advance payment is required to secure your reservation.
The weather service issued an advance warning about the approaching storm.
We received an advance copy of the book before its official release.
