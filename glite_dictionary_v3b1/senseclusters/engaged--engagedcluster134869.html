engaged

adjective
unavailable for use (busy telephone, toilet, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
unavailable or busy because already in use, reserved, or occupied (such as a telephone line, seat, or room)

Examples

Engaged phone line
I tried to call her, but the line was engaged every time.
In the UK, a red light means the phone booth is engaged and cannot be used.
The sign on the restroom door showed it was engaged, so I waited in the hallway.
All the meeting rooms are currently engaged, so we'll have to wait.
She found the toilet engaged when she arrived.
The line was engaged every time I tried to call her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: available - not reserved; free for use
  • synonym: busy - (of a telephone line) currently in use; not free
  • synonym: occupied - being used by someone; not free
