mushmelon

noun
sweet orange-fleshed melon
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃˌmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃˌmɛlən / (BrE)
a type of melon with sweet, orange flesh, commonly known as muskmelon or cantaloupe

Examples

A ripe mushmelon
She sliced a ripe mushmelon for dessert after dinner.
The farmer sold fresh mushmelons at the local market every summer.
A bowl of chilled mushmelon is perfect on a hot day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cantaloupe - a round melon with orange flesh and a netted rind
  • synonym: muskmelon - a melon with sweet, fragrant orange flesh
  • antonym: watermelon - a large melon with green rind and red, juicy flesh
