antirevolutionary

noun
opponent of revolution
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌrɛvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
a person who opposes revolution or revolutionary change, especially for political reasons

Examples

An antirevolutionary leader
The antirevolutionary argued that sudden change would only bring chaos to the country.
Many antirevolutionaries supported the government during the period of political unrest.
As an outspoken antirevolutionary, she published articles warning against radical reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterrevolutionary - a person who acts against a revolution or seeks to reverse its changes
  • antonym: revolutionary - a person who supports or takes part in a revolution
