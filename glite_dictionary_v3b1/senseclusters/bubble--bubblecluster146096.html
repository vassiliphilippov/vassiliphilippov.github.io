bubble

noun
a transparent dome-shaped covering
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.əl / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl / (BrE)
a dome-shaped covering made of transparent glass or plastic, typically used to enclose or protect something while allowing visibility

Examples

A glass bubble cover
The new sports car features a small glass bubble over the driver's seat for extra visibility.
The pilot sat beneath a clear bubble that gave a wide view of the sky.
We watched the animals through the observation bubble at the zoo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dome - a rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure
