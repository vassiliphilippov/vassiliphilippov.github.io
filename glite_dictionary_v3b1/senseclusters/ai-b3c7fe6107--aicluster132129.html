AI

noun
insertion of semen to cause pregnancy without intercourse
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪˈaɪ / (BrE)
the introduction of semen into the reproductive tract of a female, typically the oviduct or uterus, by means other than sexual intercourse, usually for the purpose of reproduction
