evaporometer

noun
instrument for measuring water evaporation rate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæpəˈrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌvapəˈrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
An instrument used for measuring the rate at which water evaporates.

Examples

A evaporometer device
After a week, data from the evaporometer showed a significant increase in evaporation due to higher temperatures.
Meteorological stations often include an evaporometer among their measurement instruments.
The scientist used an evaporometer to record the rate of water loss from the pan.

  • synonym: hygrometer - instrument for measuring humidity
