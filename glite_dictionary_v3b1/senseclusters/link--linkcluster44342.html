link

verb
make a logical or causal connection
2.5
Pronunciation: / lɪŋk / (AmE), / lɪŋk / (BrE)
to make a logical or causal connection between things or ideas

Examples

To link facts
Can you link this theory with what we learned last week?
It is difficult to link these symptoms to a single disease.
The investigators tried to link the suspect to the stolen goods using new evidence.
