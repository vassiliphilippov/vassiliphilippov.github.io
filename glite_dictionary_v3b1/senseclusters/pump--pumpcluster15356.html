pump

noun
device to move liquid or gas
Pronunciation: / pʌmp / (AmE), / pʌmp / (BrE)
a device that forces liquid or gas to move somewhere by pressure or suction

Examples

A water pump
Firefighters used a portable pump to remove water from the street.
The water pump stopped working, so the basement flooded.
This type of pump is often found in aquariums and ponds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressor - a device that increases the pressure of a gas
