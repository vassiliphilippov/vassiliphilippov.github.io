Undergoing rapid growth, success, or profitability; very lively and expanding quickly.

Examples

Booming business

Tourism in the city was booming throughout the summer months.

Synonyms

    roaring is very lively and highly profitable, often used for strong trade or markets, while booming means rapidly growing and successful.
  • boomy
    7.0 0.00/m
    boomy sounds informal and describes an economy or market that expands fast, while booming is the standard neutral term for rapid growth.
  • thriving
    3.8 0.42/m
    thriving describes people or businesses growing healthily and well, while booming stresses quick, loud success and fast expansion.
  • prospering
    3.5 0.00/m
    prospering stresses steady financial gain for a person or business, while booming highlights rapid, lively expansion and high demand.
  • flourishing
    4.2 0.02/m
    flourishing means growing healthily and looking strong in many ways, while booming focuses on very fast financial or market growth.
  • prosperous
    4.1 7.64/m
    prosperous describes a person, place, or business that is well off and wealthy, while booming stresses quick, recent growth.
  • burgeoning
    6.5 0.67/m
    burgeoning means starting to grow quickly and expand from a small base, while booming implies already large, lively success.
  • fast-growing
    3.5 5.67/m

  • soaring
    3.9 0.00/m
