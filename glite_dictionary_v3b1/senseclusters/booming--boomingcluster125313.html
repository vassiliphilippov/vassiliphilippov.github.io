booming

adjective
growing rapidly and successfully
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbuːmɪŋ / (BrE)
Undergoing rapid growth, success, or profitability; very lively and expanding quickly.

Examples

Booming business
The tech sector is currently booming thanks to increased investment and innovation.
They moved to a booming town where new businesses were opening every week.
Tourism in the city was booming throughout the summer months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: declining - becoming less successful or weaker
  • synonym: flourishing - growing or developing successfully
  • synonym: thriving - growing or developing vigorously
