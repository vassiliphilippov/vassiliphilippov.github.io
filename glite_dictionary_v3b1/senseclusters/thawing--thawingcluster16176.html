thawing

noun
process of becoming unfrozen
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming liquid or soft as a result of warming after being frozen

Examples

Thawing frozen food
After several hours of thawing, the turkey was ready to be cooked.
Proper thawing of frozen food is essential to prevent bacteria growth.
The thawing of the ice gave way to rushing streams in the spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melting - the changing of a solid into a liquid by heat
