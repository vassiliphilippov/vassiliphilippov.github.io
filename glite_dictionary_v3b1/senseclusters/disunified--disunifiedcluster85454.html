disunified

adjective
lacking unity
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈjuːnɪfaɪd / (AmE), / dɪsˈjuːnɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not brought together as a single unit; lacking unity or cohesion

Examples

A disunified team
After the leadership dispute, the team remained disunified and struggled to make decisions.
The country was disunified by years of internal conflict and differing political views.
Efforts to reform the system failed because the committee was too disunified to agree on changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divided - separated into parts or groups; not united
  • antonym: unified - brought together as a single unit; united
