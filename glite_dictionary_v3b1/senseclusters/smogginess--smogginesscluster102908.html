smogginess

noun
presence of smoky, foggy air pollution
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑːɡi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsmɒɡ.i.nəs / (BrE)
The condition or quality of being full of or affected by smog.

Examples

The smogginess outside
Due to the smogginess in the air, outdoor activities were discouraged.
Many residents complained about the increased smogginess after the new factories opened.
The smogginess of the city was particularly bad during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear, free from pollution or cloudiness
  • synonym: smog - a mixture of smoke and fog causing air pollution
