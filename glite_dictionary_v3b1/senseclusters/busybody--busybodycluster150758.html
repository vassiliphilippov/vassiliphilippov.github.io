busybody

noun
a meddling person

Examples

A busybody person

The neighborhood busybody couldn't resist asking about the new family moving in.

Synonyms

  • meddler
    6.6 0.22/m
    a meddler interferes in other people's business in a direct, often official way, while a busybody is more gossipy and informal.
  • buttinsky
    9.9 0.02/m

  • quidnunc
    9.9 0.01/m
