laban

noun
fermented milk drink
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbɑːn / (AmE), / ˈlæbæn / (BrE)
a traditional Middle Eastern drink made from fermented milk or yogurt, often served cold and sometimes salted

Examples

A glass of laban
On a hot day, nothing is more refreshing than a cold glass of laban.
The restaurant served traditional dishes alongside homemade laban.
He learned to make laban from his grandmother using fresh yogurt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ayran - a cold yogurt-based drink popular in Turkey and neighboring regions
  • synonym: kefir - a fermented milk drink with a sour taste, originating from the Caucasus
