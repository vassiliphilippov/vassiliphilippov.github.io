rosette

noun
rose-shaped ornament or badge
3.5
Pronunciation: / roʊˈzɛt / (AmE), / rəʊˈzɛt / (BrE)
an ornament or badge in the shape of a rose, often used as a decoration or symbol of achievement or office

Examples

A rosette badge
Each official wore a red rosette to signify their position.
She received a gold rosette at the flower arranging competition.
The winning horse wore a bright blue rosette pinned to its bridle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: badge - a small object or emblem worn to show membership, achievement, or authority
