ironical

adjective
characterized by irony; showing irony
Pronunciation: / aɪˈrɑnɪkəl / (AmE), / aɪˈrɒnɪkəl / (BrE)
characterized by irony; showing or expressing irony, especially when the outcome is the opposite of what was expected

Examples

Ironical twist
It was ironical that their attempt to avoid trouble only led to more of it.
She gave an ironical smile when she heard about the so-called victory.
The ironical twist in the story left readers surprised and amused.
