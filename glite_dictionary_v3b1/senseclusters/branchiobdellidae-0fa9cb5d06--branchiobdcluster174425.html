Branchiobdellidae

noun
family of small annelid worms with adhesive sucker
Pronunciation: / ˌbræŋkiˌɑbˌdɛlˈɪdiː /; / ˌbræn.ki.əbˌdɛl.ɪˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌbraŋkɪˌɒbdɛˈlɪdɪiː /; / ˌbran.kɪ.ɒbˌdɛl.ɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a family of small annelid worms with an adhesive sucker at the back end, often found living on crayfish and once thought to be modified leeches
