candle holder

noun
holder for candles
Pronunciation: / ˈkændl hoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈkændl həʊldə / (BrE)
An object with one or more sockets designed to hold candles upright.

Examples

A candle holder stand
He bought a colorful candle holder at the local crafts fair.
She placed a silver candle holder on the dining table for a more elegant look.
The antique candle holder was passed down through generations in her family.
