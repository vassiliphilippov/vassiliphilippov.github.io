leaning

noun
deviation from vertical position
3.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈliː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
the state of being slanted or not upright; a tilt or slant in a structure or object

Examples

Leaning of the wall

A slight leaning in the fence was visible after the heavy storm.
Engineers measured the leaning of the tower to assess its stability.
The old bookshelf had a noticeable leaning to the left after the earthquake.
