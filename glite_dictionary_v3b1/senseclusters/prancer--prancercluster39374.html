prancer

noun
high-stepping spirited horse
Pronunciation: / ˈprænsər / (AmE), / ˈprɑːnsə / (BrE)
A spirited or lively horse, especially one that moves with high, springy steps.

Examples

A lively prancer
Only the finest prancer was chosen for the royal procession.
The prancer trotted energetically around the paddock, drawing everyone's attention.
The parade featured a beautiful prancer leading the carriage.
