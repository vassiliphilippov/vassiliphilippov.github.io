outshoot

verb
to produce new shoots
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààa ät ÉuÀêt / (AmE), / Ààa ät ÉuÀêt / (BrE)
to produce new shoots or branches, especially as a plant grows or regenerates.

Examples

Plants outshoot new stems
In spring, the old hedgerow began to outshoot new green stems along its length.
After the heavy rain, the rose bushes quickly outshot several fresh branches.
Gardeners hope their plants will outshoot vigorously after being pruned.
