gametangium

noun
cell or organ where gametes develop
Pronunciation: / ɡəˌmiːˈtændʒiəm / (AmE), / ɡəˌmiːˈtændʒiəm / (BrE)
a cell or organ in which gametes (reproductive cells) are produced or develop, especially in plants, fungi, and algae

Examples

The gametangium cell
In some fungi, the gametangium may fuse with another structure to facilitate sexual reproduction.
Scientists observed the formation of zygotes inside the mature gametangium.
The gametangium ensures the development of reproductive cells in many types of algae.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamete - a mature sexual reproductive cell
