tread

verb
(of male birds) to mate by mounting
Pronunciation: / trɛd / (AmE), / trɛd / (BrE)
(of a male bird) to mate with a female by mounting her.

Examples

Some bird species exhibit ritual displays before the male attempts to tread the female.
When the male pheasant treads the female, he balances carefully on her back.
