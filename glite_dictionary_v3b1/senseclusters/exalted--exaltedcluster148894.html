exalted

adjective
very joyful or proud
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔltɪd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːltɪd / (BrE)
feeling or showing great happiness, pride, or a sense of being elevated in status or spirit

Examples

Exalted mood
After winning the championship, the team felt exalted and celebrated all night.
He spoke in an exalted tone, full of pride and happiness.
The artist was exalted by the praise she received for her new painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depressed - feeling very sad and without hope
  • synonym: elated - very happy and excited
  • synonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction from achievements
