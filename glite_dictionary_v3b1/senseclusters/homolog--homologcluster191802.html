homolog

noun
counterpart in another system
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊməˌlɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɒməlɒɡ / (BrE)
a person or thing that has the same position, function, or status as another in a different place, system, or organization

Examples

Diplomatic homolog
The French ambassador met with his homolog from Germany to discuss trade agreements.
In biology, a gene in one species may have a homolog in another species with a similar function.
Each department has a homolog in the partner organization to coordinate activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterpart - a person or thing that has the same function or position as another
  • synonym: equivalent - a person or thing equal to another in value, function, or meaning
