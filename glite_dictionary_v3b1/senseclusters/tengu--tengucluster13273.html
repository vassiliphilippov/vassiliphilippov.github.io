tengu

noun
arrogant person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛŋɡu / (AmE), / ˈtɛŋɡu / (BrE)
a person who is arrogant, proud, or self-important, likened to the supernatural creature from Japanese folklore

Examples

A proud tengu
After his promotion, he acted like a tengu, ignoring his colleagues' suggestions and praise.
People started calling her a tengu because she boasted about her achievements all the time.
Don't be a tengu; it's better to stay modest even when you succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egotist - a person who is excessively self-centered or conceited
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
