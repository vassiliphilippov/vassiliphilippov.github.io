Campanian

adjective
of Campania or Cretaceous stage
9.5
Pronunciation: / kæmˈpeɪniən / (AmE), / kæmˈpeɪniən / (BrE)
relating to a stage of the Late Cretaceous period or to the region of Campania in Italy

Examples

Campanian period fossils
The Campanian stage is known for its rich deposits of dinosaur fossils.
Archaeologists discovered ancient pottery from the Campanian region of Italy.
The museum's exhibit features rocks from the Campanian period of the Cretaceous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Campania - a region in southern Italy
  • synonym: Cretaceous - relating to the geological period between the Jurassic and Paleogene
