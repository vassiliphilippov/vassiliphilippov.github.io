sec

adjective
moderately dry (of champagne)
Pronunciation: / sɛk / (AmE), / sɛk / (BrE)
(of champagne or other sparkling wine) moderately dry; less sweet than 'demi-sec' but sweeter than 'brut'.

Examples

Sec sparkling wine
If you prefer your sparkling wine less sweet, look for a sec on the menu.
The sommelier recommended a sec champagne to pair with our seafood platter.
This bottle of champagne is labeled as sec, so it should taste moderately dry.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: brut - very dry (of champagne)
  • degree lower: demi-sec - somewhat sweet (of champagne)
