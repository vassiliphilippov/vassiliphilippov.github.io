reappropriation

noun
giving new meaning
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking cultural elements, images, or objects and giving them new meanings or contexts, often by artists or groups

Examples

Reappropriation of objects
The museum's exhibit featured the reappropriation of everyday objects as modern art.
Through reappropriation, the community reclaimed symbols that once had negative meanings.
Many artists use reappropriation to challenge traditional ideas about culture and ownership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriation - the act of taking something for one's own use, often without permission
  • synonym: reinterpretation - the act of giving something a new or different meaning
