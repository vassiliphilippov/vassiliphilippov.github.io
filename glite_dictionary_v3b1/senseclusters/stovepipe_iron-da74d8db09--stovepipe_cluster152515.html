stovepipe iron

noun
thin plate iron for stovepipes
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊvˌpaɪp ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈstəʊvˌpaɪp ˈaɪən / (BrE)
Plate iron that is thinner than tank iron, used especially in making stovepipes and similar items.

Examples

Stovepipe iron sheet
Historically, stovepipe iron was widely used for domestic heating systems.
