nongrass

adjective
not a grass
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
not belonging to or characteristic of the grass family of plants

Examples

Nongrass plants
The botanist identified several nongrass species growing alongside the wheat.
Wildflowers and other nongrass plants added color to the meadow.
The restoration project focused on reintroducing native nongrass vegetation to the area.
