satirically

adverb
in a satirical manner
Pronunciation: / səˈtɪrɪkli / (AmE), / səˈtɪrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that uses satire, humor, irony, or ridicule to criticize or expose faults.

Examples

To speak satirically
He often comments satirically on political events during his stand-up routines.
She satirically described the corporate world as a jungle filled with wild animals.
The author satirically challenged the beliefs of society in her latest novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ironically - in an ironic manner, often with opposite meaning to literal words
