anorexic

noun
person with anorexia nervosa
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈrɛksɪk / (AmE), / ˌanəˈrɛksɪk / (BrE)
a person who suffers from anorexia nervosa, an eating disorder characterized by an intense fear of gaining weight and a distorted body image

Examples

An anorexic patient
Doctors emphasize the importance of early intervention for an anorexic to improve outcomes.
She spoke about her experiences as an anorexic during the support group meeting.
The documentary followed the daily life of an anorexic struggling with recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorexia nervosa - an eating disorder involving self-starvation and excessive weight loss
