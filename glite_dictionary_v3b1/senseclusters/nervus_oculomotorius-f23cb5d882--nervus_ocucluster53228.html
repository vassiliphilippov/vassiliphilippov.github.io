nervus oculomotorius

noun
third cranial nerve controlling eye muscles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrvəs oʊˌkjuːloʊmoʊˈtɔriəs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvʊs əʊˌkjuːləʊməʊˈtɔːriəs / (BrE)
the third cranial nerve, which supplies most of the muscles that move the eyeball and control the eyelid and pupil

Examples

nervus oculomotorius nerve function
Damage to the nervus oculomotorius can cause double vision and drooping of the eyelid.
Proper functioning of the nervus oculomotorius is essential for coordinated eye movement.
The surgeon carefully avoided the nervus oculomotorius during the procedure.
