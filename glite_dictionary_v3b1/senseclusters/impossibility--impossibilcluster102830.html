impossibility

noun
something that cannot be done
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɑːsəˈbɪlɪti /; / ɪmˌpɑːsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɒsəˈbɪlɪti /; / ɪmˌpɒsəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
