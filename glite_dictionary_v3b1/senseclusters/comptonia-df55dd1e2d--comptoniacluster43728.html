Comptonia

noun
genus of aromatic North American shrubs (sweet fern)
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑmpˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / kɒmpˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of North American shrubs in the family Myricaceae, known for aromatic, fern-like leaves (especially the sweet fern, Comptonia peregrina)
