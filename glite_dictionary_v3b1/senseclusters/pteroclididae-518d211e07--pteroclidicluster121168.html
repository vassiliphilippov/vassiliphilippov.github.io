Pteroclididae

noun
family of birds (sandgrouse)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈklɪdəˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌtɛərəˈklɪdɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of birds known as sandgrouse, inhabiting dry regions of the Old World, characterized by stocky bodies and long pointed wings.

Examples

The family Pteroclididae includes several species of sandgrouse found across Africa and Asia.
