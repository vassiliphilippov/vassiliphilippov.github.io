snow gum

noun
Australian/Tasmanian tree with smooth shedding bark
7.3 7.0
Pronunciation: /snoʊ gʌm/ (AmE), /snəʊ gʌm/ (BrE)
A small to medium-sized Australian or Tasmanian tree with smooth white to light-grey bark that sheds in patches or strips.

Examples

The snow gum tree
The snow gum forests at higher altitudes are uniquely beautiful in winter.
The smooth bark of the snow gum peels away in curling strips.
We spotted a stand of snow gums on the slopes of the mountain.
