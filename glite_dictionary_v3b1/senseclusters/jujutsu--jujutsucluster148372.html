jujutsu

noun
Japanese martial art of self-defense
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / dÊ’uËˆdÊ’utsu / (AmE), / dÊ’uËËˆdÊ’uËtsuË / (BrE)
A Japanese martial art of self-defense that uses throws, holds, and the attacker's own force, traditionally practiced without weapons.

Examples

Jujutsu martial art
He has been practicing jujutsu for five years to improve his self-defense skills.
Many police officers study jujutsu to learn effective restraint techniques.
Traditional jujutsu focuses on using an opponent's strength against them.
