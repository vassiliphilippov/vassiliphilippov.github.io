ticktacktoe

noun
simple game of crosses and circles
3.8 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪk.tæk.toʊ/ /; / /ˈtɪk.tək.toʊ/ (AmE), /ˈtɪk.tæk.təʊ/ /; / /ˈtɪk.tək.təʊ/ (BrE)
A game in which two players take turns marking spaces in a 3×3 grid with crosses and circles, trying to complete a row of three of their own marks.

Examples

Play ticktacktoe game
He quickly drew a grid for ticktacktoe on the back of his notebook.
The rules of ticktacktoe are easy enough for young children to understand.
We played several rounds of ticktacktoe while waiting for our food to arrive.
