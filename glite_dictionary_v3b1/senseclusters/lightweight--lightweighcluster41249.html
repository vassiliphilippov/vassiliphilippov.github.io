lightweight

noun
weight class between featherweight and welterweight
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt.weɪt / (BrE)
A weight class in boxing, wrestling, mixed martial arts, and other combat sports for competitors who are heavier than featherweights but lighter than welterweights.

Examples

Lightweight boxing champion
He became the world lightweight champion after a stunning victory.
Several lightweights will compete in the upcoming boxing tournament.
She moved up from featherweight to the lightweight division this year.
