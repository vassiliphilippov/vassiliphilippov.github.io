GED

noun
high school equivalency test
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː iː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː iː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a test that certifies a person has high school-level academic skills, often taken by adults who did not complete high school

Examples

Take the GED test
After dropping out, he decided to earn his GED to improve his job prospects.
Many community colleges accept students who have a GED instead of a traditional diploma.
She was proud to pass the GED exam after months of preparation.
