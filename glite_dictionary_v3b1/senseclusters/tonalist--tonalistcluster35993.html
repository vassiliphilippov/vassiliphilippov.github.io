tonalist

noun
supporter of tonal music
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.nə.lɪst/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.nə.lɪst/ (BrE)
a musician or composer who creates or supports music based on traditional keys and harmony

Examples

Famous tonalist composer
The young tonalist received an award for his symphony rooted in classical harmony.
Many 20th-century composers rejected atonality and identified as tonalists.
Her music teacher, a dedicated tonalist, emphasized the importance of key signatures in every lesson.

  • synonym: composer - a person who writes music
