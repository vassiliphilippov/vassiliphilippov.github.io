network

verb
to connect devices
3.2 1.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…õtÀåw…ùk / (AmE), / Ààn…õtÀåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
to connect computers, devices, or systems so they can communicate and share information

Examples

To network computers
The IT department is networking all the computers in the new office.
After networking the devices, employees could easily share files and printers.
He spent the afternoon networking the classroom laptops for the students.
All the computers in the office are networked together for easy file sharing.
The company networked its branches to improve communication and efficiency.
Schools in the region have networked their resources to support online learning.

Synonyms

  • connect
    2.7 31/m
    connect joins devices or systems for direct communication, while network creates a system of linked devices that share information.
  • interconnect
    4.7 0.54/m
    interconnect links systems together so they work as one, while network means creating a structure for devices to share information.
  • link-up
    3.7 0.08/m
    link-up connects two or more devices or organizations to work together, while network means forming a wider system for devices to communicate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disconnect - to separate or break a connection
  • synonym: link - to connect or join together
