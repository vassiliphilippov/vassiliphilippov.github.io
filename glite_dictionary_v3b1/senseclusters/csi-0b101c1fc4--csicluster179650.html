CSI

noun
crime scene investigators
Pronunciation: / siː ɛs aɪ / (AmE), / siː ɛs aɪ / (BrE)
a team or unit responsible for investigating and analyzing evidence at a crime scene

Examples

CSI team
The CSI arrived quickly to secure the area and collect evidence.
After the robbery, the CSI spent hours analyzing the fingerprints left behind.
A member of the CSI explained the process of gathering DNA samples to the reporters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detective - a person who investigates crimes
  • synonym: forensics - scientific methods used to solve crimes
