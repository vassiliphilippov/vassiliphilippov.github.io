eir

pronoun
gender-neutral possessive
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / ɛə / (BrE)
a gender-neutral possessive pronoun used instead of 'his' or 'her', referring to a person of any gender

Examples

Eir own notebook
Each student should bring eir own notebook to class.
If anyone forgets eir ticket, please let the organizer know.
A participant may choose to share eir thoughts during the discussion.
