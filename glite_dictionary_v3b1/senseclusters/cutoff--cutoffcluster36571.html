cutoff

noun
shorter alternative route
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɒf / (BrE)
a road, path, or route that provides a shorter alternative to the usual way

Examples

A cutoff road
Local residents use a dirt cutoff to get to the main highway faster.
The map shows a cutoff that saves drivers nearly fifteen miles.
We took the cutoff through the hills to avoid the morning traffic.
