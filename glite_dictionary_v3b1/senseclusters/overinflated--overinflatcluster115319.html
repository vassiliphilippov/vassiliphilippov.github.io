overinflated

adjective
too much air or value
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪd / (BrE)
filled with too much air, or described as having an excessive value or importance

Examples

An overinflated ball
The overinflated ball bounced too high during the game.
Critics argued that the company's overinflated reputation did not match its actual performance.
Be careful not to use an overinflated tire, as it can be dangerous on the road.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflated - having lost air or reduced in value or importance
  • degree lower: inflated - filled with air or made to seem more important than it is
