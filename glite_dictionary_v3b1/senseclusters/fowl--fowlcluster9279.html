fowl

noun
domesticated bird kept for eggs or meat
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
A domesticated bird such as a chicken or duck, especially one kept for its eggs or meat, thought to descend from the red jungle fowl.

Examples

Farm fowl eggs
It is important to keep fowl healthy to ensure a good supply of eggs.
Many rural households keep some fowl for food and as pets.
The farmer raised hundreds of fowl on his property each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicken - a common domestic fowl kept for its eggs and meat
  • synonym: duck - a waterfowl often domesticated for eggs or meat
