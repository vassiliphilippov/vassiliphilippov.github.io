augend

noun
number in addition to which another is added
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːˌdʒɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɔːdʒɛnd / (BrE)
a number to which another number, called the addend, is added in an addition operation

Examples

The augend number
In the equation 7 + 5 = 12, the number 7 is the augend.
Teachers often ask students to identify the augend and the addend in simple addition problems.
When adding 4 to 8, 8 is the augend and 4 is the addend.

  • synonym: addend - a number that is added to another in addition
