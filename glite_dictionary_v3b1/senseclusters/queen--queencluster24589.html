queen

noun
the best or most important of its kind (figurative)
Pronunciation: / kwin / (AmE), / kwiːn / (BrE)
a woman or thing that is considered the best, most important, or most outstanding of her or its kind

Examples

Queen of the city
Many call New Orleans the queen of the South for its vibrant culture.
Shakespeare is often regarded as the queen of English playwrights in terms of influence and acclaim.
The RMS Queen Mary was once the queen of transatlantic ocean liners.
As a performer, she is widely considered the undisputed queen of pop music.
She was crowned the queen of the chess tournament after an impressive victory.
The queen of the science fair presented her winning project to the entire school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: king - a man or thing personified as the best or most important of its kind
