proturan

noun
minute primitive wingless insect
6.8
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtʊrən / (AmE), / prəʊˈtjʊərən / (BrE)
A minute, primitive, wingless and eyeless insect of the order Protura, characterized by a cone-shaped head and typically inhabiting damp soil or decaying matter.
