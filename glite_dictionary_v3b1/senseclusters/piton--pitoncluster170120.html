piton

noun
metal spike anchor for climbing
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌtɑn / (AmE), / ˈpiːtɒn / (BrE)
a metal spike with a hole used by climbers for anchoring a rope to rock or ice

Examples

pitons for climbing
During the ascent, he relied on several pitons to protect against a fall.
Modern climbers sometimes prefer removable devices over the traditional piton.
The climber hammered a piton into the rocky crevice to secure his rope.
