kohen

noun
Jewish priestly class
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈheɪn / (AmE), / kəʊˈheɪn / (BrE)
a member of the Jewish priestly class, traditionally believed to be descended from Aaron

Examples

A kohen blessing
He is a kohen and performs special blessings during Jewish ceremonies.
Traditionally, a kohen has certain roles in synagogue services.
The family proudly traced their heritage back to a line of kohanim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: levite - a member of the Hebrew tribe of Levi, assisting priests in religious duties
  • antonym: rabbi - a Jewish religious teacher and leader
