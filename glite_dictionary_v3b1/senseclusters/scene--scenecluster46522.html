scene

noun
display of anger or emotion in public
4.1 6.96/m
Pronunciation: / siÀên / (AmE), / siÀên / (BrE)
an occasion when someone behaves in a loud, angry, or emotional way in public, often causing embarrassment to others

Examples

Make a scene
He stormed out, creating a huge scene that embarrassed everyone.
Please don't make a scene in front of the guests.
She was upset but managed not to cause a scene at the restaurant.
