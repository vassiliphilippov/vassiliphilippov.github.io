thermosiphon

noun
passive heat-transfer device
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːrmoʊˌsaɪfən /; / ˈθɜrmoʊˌsaɪfən / (AmE), / ˈθɜːməʊˌsaɪfən / (BrE)
a device or system that moves liquid or air by natural convection (using temperature differences), without a pump
