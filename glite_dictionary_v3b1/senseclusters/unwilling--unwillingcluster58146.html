unwilling

adjective
not wanting or refusing to do something
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈwɪl.ɪŋ/ /; / /ʌn.ˈwɪl.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈwɪl.ɪŋ/ /; / /ən.ˈwɪl.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
