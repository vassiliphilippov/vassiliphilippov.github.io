muffler

noun
device reducing noise or vibration
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌflər /; / ˈmʌflɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌflə / (BrE)
a device that reduces noise or vibrations, especially in vehicles

Examples

Car muffler
Modern recording studios often use a muffler to limit unwanted acoustical oscillations.
Some musicians place a muffler inside their drum to reduce the volume and resonance.
The muffler on my car needs to be replaced because it's making too much noise.
Driving with a damaged muffler can result in a hefty fine due to noise regulations.
The car's new muffler made the ride much quieter and more comfortable.
The mechanic replaced the muffler because it was making a loud rattling noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: silencer - device for reducing noise produced by machinery or firearms
