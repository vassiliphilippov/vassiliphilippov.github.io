muffler

noun
device reducing noise or vibration
6.4 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌf.lər/ (AmE), /ˈmʌf.lə/ (BrE)

Examples

Car muffler
Modern recording studios often use a muffler to limit unwanted acoustical oscillations.

The car's new muffler made the ride much quieter and more comfortable.
