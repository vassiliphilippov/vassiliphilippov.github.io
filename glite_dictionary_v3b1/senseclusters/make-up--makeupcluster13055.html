make-up

noun
replacement for a cancelled/missed event
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (BrE)
an event or activity that takes place as a replacement for one that was previously cancelled or missed

Examples

Make-up exam
Because the game was rained out, a make-up was scheduled for next Saturday.
Students who missed the exam must attend a make-up session next week.
The canceled concert has a make-up date in early May.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rescheduling - the process of arranging for something to occur at a new time
