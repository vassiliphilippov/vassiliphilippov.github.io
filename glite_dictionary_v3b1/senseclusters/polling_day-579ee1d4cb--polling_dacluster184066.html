polling day

noun
election day; day when votes are cast
3.6 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.lɪŋ deɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.lɪŋ deɪ/ (BrE)
The day on which an election is officially held and votes are cast.

Examples

The polling day
In the UK, polling day is usually held on a Thursday.
Local businesses often offer discounts to customers who have voted on polling day.
Voters eagerly queued up outside the school on polling day to cast their ballots.
