drift

noun
general sense or main idea
1.2
Pronunciation: / drɪft / (AmE), / drɪft / (BrE)
the general meaning, sense, or main idea of something spoken or written

Examples

Catch his drift
Before I argued, I wanted to make sure I caught his drift.
Can you explain the drift of the article in simpler terms?
I didn't understand every detail, but I got the drift of what she was saying.
