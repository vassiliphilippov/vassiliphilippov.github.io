well-made

adjective
skillfully constructed
Pronunciation: / wɛl ˈmeɪd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈmeɪd / (BrE)
Skillfully or solidly constructed; made with care and attention to detail.

Examples

A well-made film
He always appreciates a well-made film with strong acting and direction.
The furniture in the living room is well-made and should last for years.
This jacket is well-made, so I don't expect it to fall apart easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poorly made - badly constructed or assembled
  • synonym: well-built - sturdily and strongly constructed
