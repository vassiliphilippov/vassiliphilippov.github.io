subjugated

adjective
brought under control by force
3.1
Pronunciation: / səbˈdʒuːɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / sʌbˈdʒuːɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Brought under domination or control, especially by conquest; reduced to submission.

Examples

The subjugated people
After the invasion, the local customs and languages of the subjugated region were suppressed.
The subjugated nation struggled for decades to regain its independence.
Throughout history, subjugated peoples have fought against their oppressors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free - not under the control or in the power of another; able to act or be as one wishes
  • synonym: oppressed - subjected to cruel or unjust treatment
