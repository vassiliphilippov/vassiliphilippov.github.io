dutifulness

noun
quality of being dutiful

Examples

Quiet dutifulness
He performed his tasks with quiet dutifulness, never seeking attention or praise.
Her dutifulness impressed her teachers and made her a role model among her classmates.
Their family's dutifulness to tradition was clear during every holiday celebration.
