tripper

noun
person experiencing psychedelic drug effects
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpər / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpə / (BrE)
(slang) A person who has taken a psychedelic drug and is experiencing its hallucinogenic effects.

Examples

A drug tripper
He acted like a tripper even though he hadn't taken any drugs.
The club was full of trippers dancing to psychedelic music.
The documentary interviewed a longtime tripper about his experiences with LSD.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acid head - a person who uses LSD (slang)
  • synonym: user - a person who takes drugs (especially habitually)
