modulo

preposition
except for details
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdʒəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒdjʊləʊ / (BrE)
except for or apart from; used to indicate that something is true if a small difference or detail is ignored

Examples

Modulo small errors
The results are identical modulo a few minor calculation errors.
Her argument is valid modulo some small assumptions that need clarification.
The two versions of the document are the same modulo formatting differences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apart from - excluding; except for
  • synonym: except - not including; other than
