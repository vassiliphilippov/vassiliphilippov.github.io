raspberry drizzle

noun
raspberry-based dessert sauce
Pronunciation: / ˈræzˌbɛri ˈdrɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈrɑːzbəri ˈdrɪzl̩ / (BrE)
A sweet sauce made from raspberries, typically used as a topping for desserts.

Examples

A raspberry drizzle topping
She decorated the cake with a raspberry drizzle for an elegant finish.
The cheesecake was topped with a generous raspberry drizzle that added a burst of color and flavor.
You can make a simple raspberry drizzle by blending fresh raspberries with sugar.
