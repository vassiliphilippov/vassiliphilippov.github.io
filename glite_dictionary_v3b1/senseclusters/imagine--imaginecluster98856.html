Synonyms

  • picture
    2.9 16/m
    picture is a common verb for forming a clear mental image, while imagine is a broader word for thinking of something not real.
  • visualize
    3.8 5/m
    visualize means actively creating a detailed mental image, while imagine covers making any mental image or idea, not only visual ones.
  • conceive of
    5.3 3/m
    conceive of means to think up or imagine something, often as a possibility, while imagine is more general and everyday.
  • envision
    4.1 17/m
    envision often means to picture a future situation or plan, while imagine applies to any idea or image in the mind.
  • envisage
    6.8 2/m
    envisage means to form a mental picture of a possible event, while imagine is broader and used in many contexts.
  • conceive
    5.2 8/m
    conceive often means to develop an idea or plan in the mind, while imagine focuses on forming any mental picture or image.

  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember or be unable to bring to mind
