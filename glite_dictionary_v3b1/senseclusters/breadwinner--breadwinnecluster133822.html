breadwinner

noun
main income earner in a household
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdˌwɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌwɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose earnings are the main source of income for a family or household

Examples

The breadwinner of family
After losing his job, Tom struggled with the pressure of being the only breadwinner in the family.
In many households, both parents now act as breadwinners.
She became the family breadwinner after her husband retired due to illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earner - a person who earns money; wage earner
