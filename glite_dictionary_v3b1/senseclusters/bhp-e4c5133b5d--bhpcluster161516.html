BHP

sym
engine power unit
7.7
Pronunciation: / biː eɪtʃ piː / (AmE), / biː eɪtʃ piː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for brake horsepower, a unit measuring the power output of an engine before losses from auxiliary components

Examples

Engine BHP rating
The sports car boasts 300 BHP, making it one of the fastest in its class.
Manufacturers often advertise the BHP of their vehicles to highlight engine performance.
He compared the BHP ratings of several motorcycles before making his purchase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horsepower - a unit of power used to measure engine strength
  • synonym: kilowatt - a metric unit of power equal to 1,000 watts
