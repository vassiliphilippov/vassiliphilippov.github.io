Luo

noun
a Nilotic language of central Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈluː.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈluː.əʊ / (BrE)
a Nilotic language spoken chiefly in the Adamawa Region and parts of eastern Nigeria and Cameroon
