licensing

noun
permission for use
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ / (BrE)
the business practice of granting permission to use intellectual property, a brand, or a product, usually for payment

Examples

Licensing a brand
The company increased profits through licensing its logo to other businesses.
International licensing allows brands to expand into new markets without direct investment.
She works in licensing, negotiating deals for the use of popular characters in merchandise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorization - the act of giving official permission
  • synonym: franchising - the practice of allowing others to operate a business using a brand's name and system
  • antonym: prohibition - the act of forbidding something
