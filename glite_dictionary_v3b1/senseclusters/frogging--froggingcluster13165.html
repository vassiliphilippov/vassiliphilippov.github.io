frogging

noun
undoing knitting
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of undoing knitted or crocheted fabric by pulling out the stitches to correct mistakes or reuse the yarn

Examples

To frog a sweater
She spent the afternoon frogging her sweater after noticing a mistake in the pattern.
Many knitters find frogging frustrating, but it is sometimes necessary to fix errors.
Before starting a new project, he finished frogging the old scarf to reuse the yarn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: knitting - the craft of making fabric by interlocking yarn with needles
  • synonym: unraveling - the act of undoing knitted or woven fabric
