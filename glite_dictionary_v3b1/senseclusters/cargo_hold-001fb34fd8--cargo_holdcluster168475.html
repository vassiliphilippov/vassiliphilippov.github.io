cargo hold

noun
area for storing cargo on ships or aircraft
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrˌgoʊ hoʊld / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ həʊld / (BrE)
the part of a ship or aircraft where cargo is stored during transport

Examples

The cargo hold of a ship
After the storm, water seeped into the cargo hold, damaging some shipments.
The crew inspected the cargo hold to ensure all goods were securely fastened.
The plane's cargo hold was loaded with medical supplies for the relief effort.

Related Terms
