ontogenic

adjective
development of organism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑntəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒntəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the development or origin of an individual organism from its earliest stage to maturity

Examples

Ontogenic development
Researchers observed ontogenic changes in the behavior of the animal as it matured.
The study focused on the ontogenic development of the butterfly from larva to adult.
Understanding ontogenic processes is essential in developmental biology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ontogenetic - relating to the development of an individual organism
  • antonym: phylogenetic - relating to the evolutionary development of a species
