jocko

noun
slang for monkey
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒkəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a monkey or sometimes used as a nickname for a person or figure resembling a monkey

Examples

A wooden jocko
The old toy shop had a wooden jocko on display.
He called his mischievous little brother jocko as a joke.
A carved jocko stood at the entrance, greeting visitors with a grin.
