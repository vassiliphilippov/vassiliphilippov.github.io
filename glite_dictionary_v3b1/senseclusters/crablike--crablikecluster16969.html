crablike

adjective
resembling a crab
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkræbˌlaɪk / (BrE)
moving or appearing in a way that resembles a crab, especially by moving sideways

Examples

Crablike sideways movement
The dancer moved in a crablike manner across the stage during the performance.
We watched the animal's crablike movement as it scuttled along the beach.
The children imitated a crablike walk during the game in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sideways - moving or facing to the side
