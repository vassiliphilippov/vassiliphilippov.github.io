snideness

noun
deceitful quality
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnaɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈsnaɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being false, deceitful, or lacking authenticity or sincerity

Examples

Fake snideness
The snideness in his apology made it clear he did not mean what he said.
She was disappointed by the snideness of the compliments she received at the event.
Detecting the snideness in the offer, he decided not to trust the deal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falseness - the quality of not being genuine or true
  • antonym: sincerity - the quality of being genuine and honest
