balance wheel

noun
rate-regulating wheel in machines, especially watches
8.4 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæl.əns wiːl/ /; / /ˈbæl.əns hwil/ (AmE), /ˈbæl.əns wiːl/ /; / /ˈbæl.əns hwil/ (BrE)
a wheel in a mechanical device that regulates the rate of movement, especially one oscillating against a hairspring in a timepiece to control its beat

Examples

The balance wheel in clocks
Damage to the balance wheel can disrupt the rhythm of a mechanical clock.
Replacing a broken balance wheel requires specialized tools and skills.
The old watchmaker carefully adjusted the balance wheel to ensure accurate timekeeping.

  • synonym: escapement - mechanism in a clock or watch that controls the motion of the gear train
