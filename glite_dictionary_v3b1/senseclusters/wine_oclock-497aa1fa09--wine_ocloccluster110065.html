wine o'clock

noun
an informal time to drink wine
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /waɪn ə.ˈklɑːk/ /; / /waɪn oʊ.ˈklɑːk/ (AmE), /waɪn ə.ˈklɒk/ /; / /waɪn əʊ.ˈklɒk/ (BrE)
An informal time designated for relaxing and drinking wine, often at the end of the day or to mark the start of leisure time.
