brayer

noun
ink-spreading roller
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪə / (BrE)
a hand-held roller used to spread ink evenly in printmaking or similar crafts

Examples

Ink brayer
The artist rolled the brayer over the ink to prepare for printing.
After cleaning the brayer, she started a new color for her next print.
A soft rubber brayer is ideal for spreading ink smoothly on the plate.
