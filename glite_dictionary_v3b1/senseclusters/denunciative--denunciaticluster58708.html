denunciative

adjective
expressing warning of punishment
4.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈnʌnsɪətɪv / (BrE)
(of a statement or act) expressing warning of punishment or condemnation.

Examples

Denunciative remarks
His denunciative remarks made it clear that such behavior would not be tolerated.
The judge delivered a denunciative speech against corruption in the courtroom.
The letter was written in a denunciative tone, warning of consequences for further violations.
