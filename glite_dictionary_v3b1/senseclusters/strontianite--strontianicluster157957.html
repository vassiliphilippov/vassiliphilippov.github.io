strontianite

noun
a mineral of strontium carbonate
8.6
Pronunciation: / strɒnˈtiːəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / strɒnˈtɪənaɪt / (BrE)
A mineral consisting of strontium carbonate (SrCO₃), typically found in fibrous or columnar crystals.

Examples

A strontianite mineral
Collectors prize strontianite specimens for their rare and striking crystal formations.
Pure strontianite is an important source of strontium for industrial use.
