wheat beer

noun
pale, cloudy beer brewed with wheat
Pronunciation: / wiːt bɪr / (AmE), / wiːt bɪə / (BrE)
a pale, often cloudy and effervescent beer brewed with a high proportion of wheat, typically using top fermentation

Examples

A glass of wheat beer
I ordered a tall glass of wheat beer to go with my meal.
Many craft breweries offer a refreshing wheat beer during the summer months.
The bartender recommended their house wheat beer for its light, citrusy flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ale - a type of beer brewed with top fermentation, usually darker and not as cloudy as wheat beer
  • antonym: lager - a type of beer made by bottom fermentation and stored for aging
