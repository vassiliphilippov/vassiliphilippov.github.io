unbuttoned

adjective
uninhibited; lacking constraint
9.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbʌtənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌtənd / (BrE)
(of behavior or style) free from constraint, reserve, or conventional propriety; open or uninhibited.

Examples

Unbuttoned style
During the party, his unbuttoned laughter and stories put everyone at ease.
The author's unbuttoned style makes the memoir both entertaining and honest.
The film's unbuttoned humor was refreshing compared to more traditional comedies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buttoned-up - restrained or very reserved in expression or behavior
  • synonym: uninhibited - not restrained or held back; expressing oneself freely
