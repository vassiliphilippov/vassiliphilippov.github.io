standardized

adjective
made uniform by standards
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstændərdaɪzd /; / ˈstændəɹdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzd / (BrE)
brought into conformity with a standard; made uniform or consistent according to a set standard.

Examples

A standardized test
All students must take the standardized test at the end of the year.
The new regulations required standardized labels on all products.
We followed a standardized procedure to ensure consistent results.
A standardised format ensures that the data can be easily compared.
All students took the standardised test at the end of the term.
The government introduced standardised procedures to improve efficiency across departments.
