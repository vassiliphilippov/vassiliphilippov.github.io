unbrainwashed

adjective
not successfully brainwashed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈbreɪnˌwɑʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈbreɪnˌwɒʃt / (BrE)
not having been successfully subjected to brainwashing; not brainwashed.

Examples

An unbrainwashed student
A few unbrainwashed students questioned the ideology being taught at the camp.
Even after years in captivity, she remained unbrainwashed and loyal to her beliefs.
The documentary highlights individuals who emerged unbrainwashed from intense propaganda efforts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brainwashed - having been subjected to intensive forced indoctrination
