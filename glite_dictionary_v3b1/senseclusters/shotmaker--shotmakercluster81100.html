shotmaker

noun
skilled scorer
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a player who is skilled at making successful shots, especially in sports like basketball or tennis

Examples

Star shotmaker
The team's best shotmaker scored thirty points in last night's game.
As a young tennis player, she quickly gained a reputation as a talented shotmaker.
Coaches value a reliable shotmaker who can perform under pressure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defender - a player whose main role is to prevent the other team from scoring
  • synonym: scorer - a player who scores points in a game
  • synonym: shooter - a player who takes shots, especially in basketball
