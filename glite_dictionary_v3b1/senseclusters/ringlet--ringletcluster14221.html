ringlet

noun
small circular formation of concentric circles
7.2 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a small circular band or a round formation made up of concentric circles, such as those seen in leaves or flower petals
