coast lily

noun
orange-flowered Pacific coast lily
Pronunciation: / koʊst ˈlɪli / (AmE), / kəʊst ˈlɪli / (BrE)
an orange-flowered lily native to the Pacific coast of the United States, known for its striking blooms

Examples

The coast lily flower
Botanists are working to conserve the endangered coast lily in its native habitat.
Photographers often travel to see the coast lily in peak flowering season.
The coast lily blooms in vibrant orange along the California shore in late spring.
