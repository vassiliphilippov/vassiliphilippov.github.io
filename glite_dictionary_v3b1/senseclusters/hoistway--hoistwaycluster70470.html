hoistway

noun
elevator shaft
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɔɪst.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhɔɪst.weɪ/ (BrE)
a vertical shaft or passage in a building through which an elevator, lift, or hoist moves up and down
