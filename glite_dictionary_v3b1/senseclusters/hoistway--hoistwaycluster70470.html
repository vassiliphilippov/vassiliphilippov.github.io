hoistway

noun
elevator shaft
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔɪstˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪstˌweɪ / (BrE)
a vertical shaft or passage in a building through which an elevator, lift, or hoist moves up and down

Examples

Elevator hoistway
The technician checked the hoistway before starting the elevator repairs.
Debris in the hoistway can cause serious safety hazards for elevator users.
Building codes require that every hoistway be properly enclosed and ventilated.
The building inspector checked the hoistway to ensure it met all safety regulations.
During construction, workers installed steel beams around the hoistway for extra support.
The old warehouse still had a large hoistway where a freight elevator once operated.
