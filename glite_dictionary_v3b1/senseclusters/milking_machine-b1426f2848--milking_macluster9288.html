milking machine

noun
machine for milking cows mechanically
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine that uses suction to extract milk from cows or other dairy animals mechanically.

Examples

A milking machine system
After cleaning the milking machine, the workers prepared it for the next herd of cows.
Modern milking machines help ensure the hygienic collection of milk.
The farmer invested in a new milking machine to increase efficiency on the dairy farm.
