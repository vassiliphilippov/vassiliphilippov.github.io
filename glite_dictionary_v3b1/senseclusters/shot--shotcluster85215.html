shot

adjective
showing different colors from angles
6.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt / (AmE), / ʃɒt / (BrE)
(of fabric or material) displaying different colors when viewed from different angles, due to threads of different colors being woven together.

Examples

Shot silk fabric
He admired the shot taffeta curtains that looked emerald or gold depending on the sunlight.
The dress was made from shot silk that shimmered purple and blue in the evening light.
The scarf's shot fabric changed color with every movement she made.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iridescent - showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
