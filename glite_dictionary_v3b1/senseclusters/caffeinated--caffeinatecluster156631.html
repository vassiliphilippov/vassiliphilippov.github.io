caffeinated

adjective
containing caffeine
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæfɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
containing caffeine or made lively or alert by caffeine (especially drinks, foods, or people)

Examples

Caffeinated coffee

She avoids caffeinated beverages in the evening to help her sleep.
Many people prefer caffeinated coffee to help them wake up in the morning.
The label clearly states whether the drink is caffeinated or not.
