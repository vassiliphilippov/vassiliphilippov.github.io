caffeinated

adjective
containing caffeine
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæfɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
containing caffeine or made lively or alert by caffeine (especially drinks, foods, or people)

Examples

Caffeinated coffee
She prefers caffeinated coffee in the morning to help her wake up.
The energy drink is highly caffeinated and should be consumed in moderation.
After drinking two caffeinated sodas, he felt much more alert during the meeting.
She avoids caffeinated beverages in the evening to help her sleep.
Many people prefer caffeinated coffee to help them wake up in the morning.
The label clearly states whether the drink is caffeinated or not.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decaffeinated - having had most or all caffeine removed
  • synonym: stimulated - made more active or alert
  • synonym: stimulating - having an energizing or activating effect
