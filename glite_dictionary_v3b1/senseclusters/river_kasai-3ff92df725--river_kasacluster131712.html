River Kasai

proper noun
river in southwestern Africa
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvɚ kəˈsaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə kəˈsaɪ / (BrE)
A river in southwestern Africa, rising in Angola and flowing northeast into the Congo River, partially forming the border between Angola and Congo.

Examples

The River Kasai banks
A network of villages lies along the banks of the River Kasai.
During the rainy season, the River Kasai often floods its surrounding plains.
The River Kasai forms part of the border between Angola and the Democratic Republic of Congo.
