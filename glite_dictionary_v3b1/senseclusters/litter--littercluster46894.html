litter

verb
(animal) give birth to a litter
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬ər / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə / (BrE)
(of an animal) to give birth to a litter of young at one time.

Examples

To litter puppies
Our dog is expected to litter any day now.
The cat littered six kittens last spring under the porch.
The fox littered in a hidden den deep in the forest.
