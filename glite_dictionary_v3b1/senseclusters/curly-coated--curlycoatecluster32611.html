curly-coated

adjective
having curly hair or fur
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrli ˌkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli ˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a coat of hair or fur that is curly.

Examples

A curly-coated dog
A curly-coated sheep grazed in the field near the fence.
She adopted a curly-coated puppy from the animal shelter.
The curly-coated retriever is known for its distinctive fur.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight-haired - having hair or fur that is straight rather than curly
