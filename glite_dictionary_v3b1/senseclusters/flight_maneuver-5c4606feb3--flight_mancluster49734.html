flight maneuver

noun
movement or action performed by an aircraft
2.4
Pronunciation: / flaɪt məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / flaɪt məˈnjuːvə / (BrE)

Examples

The instructor demonstrated a complex flight maneuver during the training session.
The pilot performed a flight maneuver to avoid turbulence during the descent.
