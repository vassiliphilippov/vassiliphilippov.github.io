Saint Maarten

proper noun
island in the Caribbean Sea, split between France and the Netherlands
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈmɑːrtən / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
An island in the western Leeward Islands, administered jointly by France (Saint-Martin) and the Netherlands (Sint Maarten).
