bevel

noun
angled edge or surface
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛvəl /; / c8b5bv59l / (AmE), / ˈbɛvəl /; / c8b5bv59l / (BrE)
an edge or surface that is sloped at an angle other than 90 degrees, or a visual effect that makes something look like it has a sloped edge

Examples

A bevel edge
Adjust the saw to achieve the correct bevel for this joint.
He cut a bevel on the wood so the pieces would fit together smoothly.
The glass top has a decorative bevel along the edge.
The app button uses a subtle bevel to suggest depth on the flat surface.
In Photoshop she added a soft bevel to the icon to make it look raised.
Modern UI guidelines warn against heavy bevels because they can make interfaces look dated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chamfer - a beveled edge connecting two surfaces
  • synonym: emboss - to give a surface a raised or recessed relief; a similar graphic effect often used with bevels.
