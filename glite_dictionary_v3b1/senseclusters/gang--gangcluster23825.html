gang

verb
to act as an organized group
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɡæŋ / (AmE), / ɡæŋ / (BrE)
(of people or things) to move or act together as an organized group.

Examples

To gang up together
Several small companies might gang up to compete against larger corporations.
The children tended to gang together during recess for games.
The tools ganged together performed the task more efficiently.
