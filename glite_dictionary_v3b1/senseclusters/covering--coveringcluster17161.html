covering

noun
thing that covers, protects, or conceals
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvərɪŋ /; / ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvərɪŋ /; / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
something that is placed over or around something else, especially as a layer for protection, shelter, or concealment

Examples

A covering layer
Snow acted as a natural covering for the sleeping plants.
The couch was protected by a covering to keep it clean.
The wound was healing well beneath its sterile covering.
A thin covering of moss grew on the surface of the old stone wall.
The ground was hidden beneath a covering of fresh snow.
The turtle's shell acts as a protective covering for its body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coating - a layer of a substance covering a surface
  • synonym: cover - something that covers or conceals
  • antonym: exposure - the condition of being unprotected or uncovered
  • synonym: sheath - a protective covering, usually close-fitting
