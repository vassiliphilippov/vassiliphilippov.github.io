at a lower place

adverb
in a lower location
Pronunciation: / æt ə ˈloʊ.ɚ pleɪs / (AmE), / at ə ˈləʊ.ə pleɪs / (BrE)
in or to a place that is lower than another point

Examples

At a lower place in room
He stored the tools at a lower place to make them easier to reach.
The book fell at a lower place on the shelf after I bumped it.
The cave entrance is at a lower place on the mountain than the summit.
