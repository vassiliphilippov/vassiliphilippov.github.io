suit of armor

noun
complete protective metal coverings for the body
Pronunciation: / suːt əv ˈɑːrmər /; / suːt əv ˈɑrmər / (AmE), / suːt əv ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
a complete set of metal coverings worn to protect the whole body in battle, especially by soldiers in the past
