gracile

adjective
slender and graceful
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræs.aɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡræs.aɪl / (BrE)
(especially of a person or animal) slender and graceful in appearance.

Examples

A gracile body
She admired the gracile branches of the willow tree swaying in the breeze.
The dancer's gracile arms moved fluidly across the stage, captivating the audience.
The skeleton showed gracile features, suggesting a lightweight frame adapted for speed.
