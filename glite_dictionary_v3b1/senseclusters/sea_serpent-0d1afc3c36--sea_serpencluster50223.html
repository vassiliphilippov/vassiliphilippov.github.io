sea serpent

noun
mythical marine creature like a snake or dragon
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌsɝː.pənt / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌsɜː.pənt / (BrE)
a legendary large marine creature that resembles a snake or dragon, often featured in folklore and maritime tales

Examples

A sea serpent legend
Some people claimed to have seen a sea serpent off the coast, but there was no proof.
Stories of the sea serpent were passed down by sailors over generations.
The old map depicted the route with drawings of a menacing sea serpent.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dragon - a large, serpentine legendary creature in mythology
  • synonym: kraken - a legendary sea monster; giant octopus or squid in folklore
