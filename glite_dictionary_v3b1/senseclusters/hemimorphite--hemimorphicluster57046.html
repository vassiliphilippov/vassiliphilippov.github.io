hemimorphite

noun
a white mineral; zinc ore
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmiˈmɔːrfaɪt / (AmE), / ˌhiːmɪˈmɔːfaɪt / (BrE)
a white or colorless mineral, commonly occurring as a zinc ore, consisting of hydrated zinc silicate
