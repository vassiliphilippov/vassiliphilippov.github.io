retoucher

noun
image editor
6.7
Pronunciation: / riˈtʌtʃər / (AmE), / riˈtʌtʃə / (BrE)
a person who improves or alters images, photographs, or artworks by making small changes or corrections

Examples

Photo retoucher
The magazine hired a skilled retoucher to enhance the cover photo.
A professional retoucher can remove blemishes and adjust lighting in digital portraits.
Many advertising agencies rely on a retoucher to perfect their promotional images.

As a professional retoucher, he spends hours adjusting colors and removing imperfections from photos.

After years of experience, she became a respected retoucher in the art restoration field.
