gun mike

noun
long, directional microphone
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn maɪk / (AmE), / ɡʌn mʌɪk / (BrE)
A highly directional microphone with a long, narrow barrel that allows it to pick up sound from a distance in a specific direction.

Examples

A gun mike microphone
Because of its narrow pickup pattern, a gun mike is ideal for recording wildlife from a distance.
The audio engineer set up a gun mike at the edge of the stage to capture the actors' voices clearly.
They used a gun mike on the film set to reduce background noise and focus on the dialogue.

  • synonym: shotgun microphone - a highly directional microphone of the same type
