soft

adjective
not protected against attack
7.3
Pronunciation: / sɔft / (AmE), / sɒft / (BrE)
(of a place, person, or thing) not protected against attack, especially by military or terrorist action.

Examples

Soft target
Because the warehouse lacked proper security, it was viewed as a soft spot by the thieves.
Many public places are considered soft targets during times of heightened security.
Terrorists often look for soft locations where defenses are weak or nonexistent.
