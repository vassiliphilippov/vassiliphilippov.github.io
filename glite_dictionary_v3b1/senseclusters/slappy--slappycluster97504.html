slappy

noun
playful light slap
Pronunciation: / ˈslæpi / (AmE), / ˈslæpi / (BrE)
a light, quick slap or hit, usually given in a playful or friendly way

Examples

A quick slappy
He gave his friend a quick slappy on the back to celebrate the win.
She laughed and returned the playful slappy during their game.
A gentle slappy on the arm let him know she was joking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pat - a gentle touch or tap with the hand
  • degree higher: smack - a sharp slap or blow, often louder or harder
