golden touch

noun
talent for consistent success
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən tʌtʃ / (BrE)
an exceptional ability to achieve success easily in various activities or ventures

Examples

The golden touch
After his latest invention succeeded, everyone said he had a golden touch.
Her golden touch in managing teams led to record profits for the company.
She seems to have the golden touch in every project she takes on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Midas touch - the supposed ability to turn any venture into a success; another idiom with the same meaning
