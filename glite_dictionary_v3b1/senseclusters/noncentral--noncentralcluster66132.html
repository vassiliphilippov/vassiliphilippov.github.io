noncentral

adjective
not in the center
6.3
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
not located in, relating to, or forming the center; away from the center.

Examples

Noncentral location
The new hospital was built in a noncentral district to serve more rural communities.
Many employees prefer to live in noncentral neighborhoods for a quieter environment.
The event was held in a noncentral location to avoid city traffic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: central - located in or forming the center
  • synonym: peripheral - situated on the edge or periphery; not central
