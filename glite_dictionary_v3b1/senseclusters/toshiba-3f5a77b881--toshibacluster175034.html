Toshiba

noun
Toshiba brand product
Pronunciation: / toʊˈʃiːbə / (AmE), / təʊˈʃiːbə / (BrE)
a product made by the Toshiba company, especially a laptop, television, or other electronic device

Examples

A Toshiba laptop
She bought a new Toshiba for her college classes because it was lightweight and reliable.
The office uses several Toshibas for presentations and video conferences.
After years of use, his old Toshiba still works perfectly.
