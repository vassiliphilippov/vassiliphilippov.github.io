burglarproof

adjective
secure against burglary
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrɡlərˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡləˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to be secure against burglary; difficult or impossible for burglars to break into

Examples

burglarproof locking system
