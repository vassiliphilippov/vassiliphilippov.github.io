approaching

adjective
happening soon; imminent
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an event or time) happening or likely to happen soon; imminent.

Examples

An approaching storm
Everyone was nervous about the approaching storm.
The company is preparing for the approaching deadline.
We are excited about the approaching holidays with our families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forthcoming - about to happen or appear
  • synonym: imminent - about to happen
  • antonym: past - having already happened
