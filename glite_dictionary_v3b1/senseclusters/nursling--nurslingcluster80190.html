nursling

noun
infant tended by a nurse
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːs.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɜːs.lɪŋ / (BrE)
an infant or young child being cared for by a nurse or nursemaid, especially one being breastfed

Examples

A nursling baby
As a tiny nursling, he depended entirely on his caregiver for warmth and nutrition.
She held the nursling gently, rocking the baby to soothe its cries.
The nursling slept peacefully in her cradle, watched over by her devoted nurse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infant - a very young child or baby
