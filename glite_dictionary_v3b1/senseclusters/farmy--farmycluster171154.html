farmy

adjective
like a farm
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmi / (BrE)
having qualities, smells, or characteristics that are typical of a farm or rural farm life

Examples

A farmy smell
The kitchen had a farmy aroma after they brought in fresh vegetables from the fields.
She loved the farmy atmosphere of the countryside, with its open fields and animals.
His boots were covered in mud and had a farmy smell after a day of work.
The kitchen had a farmy feel, with wooden beams and rustic decorations.
She wore a farmy outfit, complete with boots and a straw hat.
The air smelled farmy after the fields were plowed in the spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agricultural - connected with farming or agriculture
  • synonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than the city
  • synonym: rustic - having a simple, rural, or country style
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
