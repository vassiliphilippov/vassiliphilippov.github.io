military capability

noun
armed forces' capacity for military action
1.0 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tər.iː keɪ.pə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tər.ɪ keɪ.pə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmɪl.ɪ.trɪ keɪ.pə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
