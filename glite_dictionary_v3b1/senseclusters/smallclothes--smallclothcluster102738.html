smallclothes

noun
historical men's underwear
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
old-fashioned term for men's underwear or undergarments, especially breeches and garments worn beneath outer clothing

Examples

Men's smallclothes
In the 18th century, gentlemen always wore smallclothes beneath their coats and waistcoats.
The museum displayed a set of smallclothes once owned by a famous historical figure.
He laughed at the idea of wearing smallclothes instead of modern boxers or briefs.
