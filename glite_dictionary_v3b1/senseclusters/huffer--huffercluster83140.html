huffer

noun
irritable or sulky person
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfər / (AmE), / ˈhʌfə / (BrE)
a person who reacts with irritation or sulks when offended or upset

Examples

A sulking huffer
After losing the game, he became a huffer and refused to talk to anyone.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the huffer because she always pouted when things didn't go her way.
Instead of discussing the problem, he acted like a huffer and left the room in silence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grump - a person who is often irritable or in a bad mood
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to resolve conflicts and calm others
  • synonym: sulker - a person who sulks or withdraws in silence
