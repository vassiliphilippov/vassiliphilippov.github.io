reoccurrence

noun
return of a condition
6.2 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀê…ôÀàk…úÀêr…ôns / (AmE), / ÀåriÀê…ôÀàk år…ôns / (BrE)
the return of a disease, symptom, or condition after a period of improvement or remission

Examples

Reoccurrence of cancer
The doctor warned that there was a risk of reoccurrence if the treatment was stopped too early.
After several months without symptoms, the patient experienced a sudden reoccurrence of the infection.
Regular check-ups help detect the reoccurrence of chronic conditions at an early stage.

Synonyms

  • recrudescence
    9.9 0.06/m
    recrudescence is the sudden return of an unwanted disease after a quiet remission, while reoccurrence is a general term for disease coming back.
  • recurrence
    5.0 3.49/m
    recurrence is an event that happens again, often at regular intervals, while reoccurrence describes a disease returning after a period of improvement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recurrence - the act of something happening again, especially a medical condition
  • synonym: relapse - the return of an illness or symptoms after improvement
  • antonym: remission - a period during which symptoms of a disease are reduced or disappear
