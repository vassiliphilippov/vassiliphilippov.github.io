tartar sauce

noun
mayonnaise-based sauce served with seafood
7.2 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːr.tər sɔːs/ /; / /ˈtɑːr.tər sɑːs/ (AmE), /ˈtɑː.tə sɔːs/ (BrE)
A mayonnaise-based sauce mixed with chopped pickles and often capers, shallots, parsley, or hard-boiled egg, typically served with seafood, especially fried fish.

Examples

Tartar sauce with fish
She ordered fried cod with extra tartar sauce on the side.
The chef prepared his own version of tartar sauce by adding fresh herbs.
Would you like some tartar sauce with your fish and chips?
A spoonful of tartare sauce adds a zesty flavor to fried calamari.
He ordered fish and chips with extra tartare sauce on the side.
Many people dip their shrimp in tartare sauce instead of cocktail sauce.

  • synonym: mayonnaise - a thick, creamy dressing of egg yolks, oil, and vinegar or lemon juice
