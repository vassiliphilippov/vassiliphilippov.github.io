Pluto

proper noun
dwarf planet in the outer solar system
Pronunciation: / ˈpluːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpluːtəʊ / (BrE)
a dwarf planet in the outer solar system that was formerly classified as the ninth planet; it follows an elliptical orbit around the sun

Examples

Pluto the dwarf planet
Scientists reclassified Pluto as a dwarf planet in 2006.
The New Horizons spacecraft sent back detailed images of Pluto in 2015.
When I was a child, Pluto was still considered the ninth planet in our solar system.
