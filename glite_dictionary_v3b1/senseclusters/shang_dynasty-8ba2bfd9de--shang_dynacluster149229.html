Shang dynasty

proper noun
early Chinese imperial dynasty
Pronunciation: / ʃɑŋ ˈdaɪnəsti / (AmE), / ʃaŋ ˈdɪnəsti / (BrE)
the Chinese imperial dynasty that ruled from about the 18th to the 12th centuries BC, recognized as one of the earliest recorded dynasties in Chinese history

Examples

The Shang dynasty rule
Archaeologists have discovered bronze artifacts dating back to the Shang dynasty.
Many oracle bones from the Shang dynasty period have provided insight into ancient Chinese culture.
The Shang dynasty is known for its advances in metallurgy and the development of early Chinese writing.
