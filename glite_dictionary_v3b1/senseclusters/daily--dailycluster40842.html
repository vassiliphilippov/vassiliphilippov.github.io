daily

noun
newspaper published every day
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪli / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli / (BrE)
A newspaper that is published every day except, often, Sunday.

Examples

A daily newspaper
He buys a daily on his way to work every morning.
Most dailies include a weekend supplement with additional features.
The daily reported extensively on the local election results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newspaper - a publication printed on paper and issued regularly, usually daily or weekly
