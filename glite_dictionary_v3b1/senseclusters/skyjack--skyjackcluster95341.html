skyjack

verb
hijack an aircraft
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌdʒak / (BrE)
to seize control of an aircraft by threat or force, especially as an act of air piracy

Examples

To skyjack a plane
It was headline news when terrorists skyjacked a jet over the Atlantic Ocean.
The group attempted to skyjack the commercial flight mid-journey.
There are strict security measures to prevent anyone from trying to skyjack a plane today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hijack - to unlawfully seize control of a vehicle, especially an airplane
