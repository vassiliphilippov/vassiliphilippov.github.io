whiskery beard

noun
long, bristly beard
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪskəri ˈbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈwɪskəri ˈbɪəd / (BrE)
A beard that is long, bristly, and resembles animal whiskers.

Examples

A whiskery beard
His whiskery beard gave him a rugged, outdoorsy appearance.
She playfully tugged at her grandfather's whiskery beard as he told her stories.
The old fisherman had a thick, whiskery beard that stuck out beneath his cap.

Related Terms
