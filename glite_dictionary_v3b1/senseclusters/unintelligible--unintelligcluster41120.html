unintelligible

adjective
impossible to understand (speech, writing, etc.)
6.0 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbəl /; / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlədʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒɪbəl /; / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to understand

Examples

An unintelligible voice
His accent was so thick that his words were almost unintelligible to outsiders.
She tried to call for help, but her voice was unintelligible over the sound of the crashing waves.
The announcement was so distorted by static that it became unintelligible to everyone in the room.
His accent was so thick that his speech became unintelligible to most of the audience.
The note was written in such a hurry that it was almost unintelligible.
The tape recording was old and unintelligible after years of damage.
