Synonyms

  • indecipherable
    8.8 0.7/m
    indecipherable means too hard to read or decode, while unintelligible is a broader word for things that cannot be understood.
  • impenetrable
    8.3 1/m

  • uninterpretable
    8.2 0.07/m

  • opaque
    8.0 2/m
    opaque looks like a formal description for text that hides its meaning, while unintelligible stresses that it cannot be understood.
  • inscrutable
    8.9 1/m

  • unfathomable

  • ungraspable
