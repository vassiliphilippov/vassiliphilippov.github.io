griddler

noun
person using griddle
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪdlər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪdlə / (BrE)
a person who cooks food on a griddle or operates a griddling appliance

Examples

Skilled griddler
The experienced griddler quickly prepared breakfast sandwiches for the morning crowd.
As a griddler, she takes pride in making perfect pancakes every time.
During the festival, the griddler worked nonstop flipping burgers and sausages for hungry visitors.
