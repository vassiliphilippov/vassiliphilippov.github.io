Jenny Lind

proper noun
famous Swedish soprano (1820–1887)
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛni lɪnd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛni lɪnd / (BrE)
(1820–1887) A Swedish operatic soprano renowned for her exceptional vocal talent, who achieved great fame during her tours in Europe and the United States, especially under the management of P. T. Barnum.
