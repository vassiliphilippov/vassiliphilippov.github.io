Jenny Lind

proper noun
famous Swedish soprano (1820–1887)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛni lɪnd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛni lɪnd / (BrE)
(1820–1887) A Swedish operatic soprano renowned for her exceptional vocal talent, who achieved great fame during her tours in Europe and the United States, especially under the management of P. T. Barnum.

Examples

Jenny Lind soprano singer
Jenny Lind captivated American audiences during her 1850 tour managed by P. T. Barnum.
A popular style of cradle in the 19th century was named after Jenny Lind due to her widespread fame.
Many music historians praise Jenny Lind for her unique vocal range and stage presence.
