draw

verb
to inhale air or smoke
3.3
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
to suck in or take (air or smoke) into the mouth or lungs

Examples

To draw a breath
He paused to draw a deep breath before speaking.
She drew on her cigarette and exhaled slowly.
Taking a moment, he drew in the cool morning air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exhale - to breathe out air from the lungs
  • synonym: inhale - to breathe in (air, gas, or smoke) into the lungs
