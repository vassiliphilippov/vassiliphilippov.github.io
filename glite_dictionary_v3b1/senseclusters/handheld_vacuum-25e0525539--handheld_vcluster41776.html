handheld vacuum

noun
small portable vacuum cleaner
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌhɛld ˈvæk.jum / (AmE), / ˈhændˌhɛld ˈvæk.juːm / (BrE)
A small, portable vacuum cleaner designed to be held in one hand, typically used for cleaning small areas or spills.

Examples

A handheld vacuum cleaner
After the kids spilled cereal on the rug, a handheld vacuum made cleanup easy.
I use a handheld vacuum to quickly clean crumbs from the kitchen counter.
The car seats were covered in sand, so she grabbed her handheld vacuum and started cleaning.
