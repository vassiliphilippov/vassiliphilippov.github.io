brain-damaged

adjective
having suffered damage to the brain
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪn ˌdæmɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn ˌdamɪdʒd / (BrE)
Having sustained damage or injury to the brain, resulting in impaired cognitive or physical abilities.

Examples

Brain-damaged patient
After the fall, he was left brain-damaged and unable to return to work.
She has worked for years with brain-damaged patients in the rehabilitation center.
The charity provides specialized support for brain-damaged children and their families.
