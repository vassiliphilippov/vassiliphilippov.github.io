inanity

noun
extreme silliness or lack of meaning
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnænəti / (AmE), / ɪˈnænɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being extremely silly or lacking meaning or ideas

Examples

The inanity of talk
Many viewers were frustrated by the inanity of the television show’s dialogue.
She could not bear the inanity of their small talk at the party.
Some critics dismissed the movie for its sheer inanity and lack of substance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - language or ideas that have no meaning or make no sense
  • antonym: wisdom - the quality of having knowledge, experience, and good judgment; opposite of silliness or emptiness in ideas
