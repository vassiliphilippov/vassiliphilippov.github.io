crab legs

noun
edible legs of a crab
2.8
Pronunciation: / kræb lɛgz / (AmE), / krab lɛgz / (BrE)
the legs of a crab, especially those served as food, often from large species such as king crabs or snow crabs

Examples

A plate of crab legs
She ordered a plate of crab legs with garlic butter for dinner.
Steamed crab legs are a popular dish at seafood restaurants.
The buffet featured fresh crab legs alongside shrimp and oysters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crab meat - the flesh of a crab eaten as food
