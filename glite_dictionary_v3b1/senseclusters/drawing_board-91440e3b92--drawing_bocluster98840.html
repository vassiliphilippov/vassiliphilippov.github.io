drawing board

noun
board used for making drawings
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A smooth, flat board on which paper is placed for making drawings or plans.

Examples

A drawing board for art
Art students are required to bring a drawing board to class every week.
He sat in front of his drawing board for hours, perfecting the design.
The architect placed her blueprint on the drawing board to add the final details.
