rough exterior

noun
tough or unfriendly outward appearance
7.2
Pronunciation: / rʌf ɪkˈstɪriɚ / (AmE), / rʌf ɪkˈstɪəriə / (BrE)
an outward appearance or manner that seems tough, unfriendly, or harsh, often hiding a gentler nature

Examples

A rough exterior appearance
Although her rough exterior intimidated new employees, she was always willing to help those in need.
Despite his rough exterior, he volunteers at the animal shelter every weekend.
Many people were surprised to find warmth beneath his rough exterior.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soft heart - a kind, gentle, or compassionate nature
