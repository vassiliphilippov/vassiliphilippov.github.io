ridged

adjective
having raised lines or ridges
8.2
Pronunciation: / rɪdʒd / (AmE), / rɪdʒd / (BrE)
having one or more raised lines or bands on the surface; shaped like a ridge

Examples

A ridged surface
She ran her fingers along the ridged edge of the old coin.
The cactus leaf has a ridged surface that helps it retain water.
The mountain trail was narrow and ridged by centuries of footsteps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrugated - shaped into alternate ridges and grooves
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; opposite of ridged
