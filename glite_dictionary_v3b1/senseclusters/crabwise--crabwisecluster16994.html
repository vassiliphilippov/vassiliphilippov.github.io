crabwise

adverb
moving at an angle or sideways
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkræb.waɪz / (BrE)
moving or progressing at an angle or sideways, like a crab.

Examples

To move crabwise
She edged crabwise along the narrow ledge, careful not to look down.
The crowd moved crabwise to avoid the obstacle in the middle of the street.
The ship drifted crabwise toward the harbor, carried by the current.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sideways - moving or situated to the side
