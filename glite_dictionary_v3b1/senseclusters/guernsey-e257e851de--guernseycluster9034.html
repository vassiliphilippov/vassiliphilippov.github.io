Guernsey

noun
breed of dairy cattle from Guernsey
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrnzi / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːnzi / (BrE)
a breed of dairy cattle, originally from the island of Guernsey, known for their high-quality milk production

Examples

A group of Guernseys grazed peacefully in the lush pasture.

Their award-winning cheese is made exclusively from Guernsey milk.
