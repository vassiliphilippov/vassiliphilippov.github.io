forge

verb
shape or form with effort, often metalwork
6.4 3.8
Pronunciation: /fɔːrdʒ/ (AmE), /fɔːdʒ/ (BrE)
to shape or make something, especially metal, by heating and hammering it; more generally, to create something with effort
