terawatt

noun
unit of power
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəˌwɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɛrəˌwɒt / (BrE)
a unit of power equal to one trillion (1,000,000,000,000) watts

Examples

One terawatt power
The solar farm is expected to produce nearly one terawatt of electricity by 2030.
Global energy consumption could reach several terawatts as technology advances.
A single terawatt is enough to power hundreds of millions of homes.
The new solar farm is expected to generate over one terawatt of electricity each year.
Global energy consumption could reach several terawatts by the middle of the century.
Scientists measured the power output of the storm in terawatts.

  • degree higher: petawatt - a unit of power equal to one quadrillion watts
