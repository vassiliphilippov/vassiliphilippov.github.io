na-na na-na boo-boo

x
childish taunt or mocking phrase
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑː ˌnɑː ˌbuː ˌbuː / (AmE), / ˈnɑː ˌnɑː ˌbuː ˌbuː / (BrE)
A childish expression used to taunt, mock, or gloat over someone, often after a defeat or in playful rivalry.

Examples

Na-na na-na boo-boo taunt
He stuck out his tongue and said na-na na-na boo-boo to tease his friend.
She shouted na-na na-na boo-boo when her brother couldn't catch her.
The children chanted na-na na-na boo-boo after winning the game.
