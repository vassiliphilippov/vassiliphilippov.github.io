posthuman

adjective
beyond human limits
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
relating to a state or being that transcends traditional human limitations, often through advanced technology or evolution

Examples

Posthuman society
The film depicts a posthuman society where artificial intelligence has surpassed human intelligence.
Some philosophers debate the ethical implications of a posthuman future.
In science fiction, posthuman characters often possess abilities far beyond those of ordinary humans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: human - relating to or characteristic of people
  • synonym: transhuman - relating to the transition from human to a more advanced state through technology
