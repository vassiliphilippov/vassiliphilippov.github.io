Mauritian rupee

noun
currency unit of Mauritius
Pronunciation: / mɔːˈrɪʃən ruːˈpi / (AmE), / məˈrɪʃən ruːˈpiː / (BrE)
the official currency of Mauritius, equal to 100 cents

Examples

The Mauritian rupee currency
Over the past year, the Mauritian rupee has remained relatively stable against the US dollar.
The price of the souvenir was clearly marked in Mauritian rupees rather than in euros or dollars.
Tourists often exchange their dollars for Mauritian rupees upon arrival on the island.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dollar - currency unit used in the United States and other countries
  • synonym: rupee - currency unit used in several South Asian countries
