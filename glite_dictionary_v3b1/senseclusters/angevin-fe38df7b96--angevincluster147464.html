Angevin

adjective
of Anjou or its dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˈændʒəvɪn / (AmE), / ˈændʒəvɪn / (BrE)
relating to Anjou in France or to the royal dynasty that originated there, especially those who ruled parts of England and France in the Middle Ages

Examples

Angevin kings
The Angevin empire once stretched from Scotland to the Pyrenees.
Historians often debate the influence of Angevin rulers on English law.
The castle was built during the Angevin period of French history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Plantagenet - relating to the Plantagenet royal family of England
