nut case

noun
eccentric or crazy person
8.4
Pronunciation: / nʌt keɪs / (AmE), / nʌt keɪs / (BrE)
an eccentric or crazy person, often behaving in a whimsical or unpredictable way

Examples

A nut case person
Don't pay any attention to him; he's just a total nut case.
She acts like a nut case whenever she's excited about an idea.
The conspiracy theorist was dismissed as a harmless nut case by most people.
