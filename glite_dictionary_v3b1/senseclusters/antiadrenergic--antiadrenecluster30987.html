antiadrenergic

adjective
blocking adrenergic effects (medical)
7.7 8.6
Pronunciation: /æn.ti.æ.drə.ˈner.dʒɪk/ (AmE), /æn.ti.æ.drə.ˈneə.dʒɪk/ (BrE)
