nonreportable

adjective
not suitable for reporting
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈpɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be formally reported, especially because results are invalid, contaminated, or do not meet required standards.

Examples

Nonreportable test result
The laboratory marked the blood test as nonreportable due to a technical error during analysis.
If a sample is nonreportable, the results cannot be included in the official study data.
Contaminated specimens are often classified as nonreportable and require retesting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invalid - not acceptable or not meeting required standards
  • antonym: reportable - able to be formally reported or recorded
