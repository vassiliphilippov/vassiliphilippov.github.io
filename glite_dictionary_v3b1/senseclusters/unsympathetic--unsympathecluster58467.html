unsympathetic

adjective
not suited to one's tastes or expectations
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
not suited to your tastes, temperament, or expectations; not compatible or congenial

Examples

Unsympathetic environment

The manager assigned him to a role that was entirely unsympathetic to his skills and interests.
Working with such an unsympathetic team made her consider changing departments.
