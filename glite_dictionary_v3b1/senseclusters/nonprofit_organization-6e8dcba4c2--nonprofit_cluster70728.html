nonprofit organization

noun
organization not operated for profit
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈprɑː.fɪt ɔːr.ɡən.ə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈprɑː.fət ɔːr.ɡə.nɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈprɒf.ɪt ɔː.ɡə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
An organization that operates for purposes other than making a profit, typically to further a social cause or public benefit.

Examples

Many nonprofit organizations
Many nonprofit organizations rely on donations and grants to fund their activities.
She volunteers at a nonprofit organization that supports literacy programs across the country.
The museum is run by a nonprofit organization dedicated to preserving local history.
