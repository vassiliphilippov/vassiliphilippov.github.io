DPI

sym
dry powder inhaler
6.1
Pronunciation: / diː piː aɪ / (AmE), / diː piː aɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for dry powder inhaler, a handheld device that delivers medication to the lungs as a dry powder

Examples

Asthma DPI device
The doctor prescribed a DPI for her asthma to make medication delivery easier.
Many patients prefer using a DPI because it does not require coordination to activate.
A DPI is often recommended for people who have difficulty using traditional inhalers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: MDI - metered dose inhaler, a device that delivers medication as an aerosol spray
  • synonym: inhaler - a device for administering medication by inhalation
