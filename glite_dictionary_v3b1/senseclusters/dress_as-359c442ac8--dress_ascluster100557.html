dress as

verb
wear a costume representing someone or something
Pronunciation: / drɛs æz / (AmE), / drɛs æz /; / drɛs az / (BrE)
to put on clothing or a costume to look like someone or something else, especially for an event or occasion

Examples

To dress as a character
At the Halloween party, many guests dressed as their favorite movie characters.
For the school play, he dressed as a famous explorer and delivered his lines with enthusiasm.
She dressed as a firefighter to entertain the children at the community event.
At the party, she dressed as Cleopatra and won a prize for best costume.
For Halloween, I decided to dress as a vampire.
Many children dress as their favorite superheroes during carnival.

  • synonym: dress up - to put on special or fancy clothes, often as a costume
