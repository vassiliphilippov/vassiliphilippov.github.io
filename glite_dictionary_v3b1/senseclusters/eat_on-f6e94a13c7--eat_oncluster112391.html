eat on

verb
persistently worry or annoy
4.9
Pronunciation: / it ɑn / (AmE), / iːt ɒn / (BrE)
to worry or cause anxiety in a persistent or nagging way

Examples

Guilt eats on him
Guilt began to eat on him after the argument with his friend.
The thought of losing her job continued to eat on her for weeks.
These unresolved issues eat on people until they're addressed.
