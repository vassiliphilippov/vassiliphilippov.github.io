retell

verb
tell again or narrate anew

Examples

She loves to retell old family tales during holidays.

Synonyms

  • recount
    4.1 11/m
    recount focuses on telling past events in clear detail from experience, while retell means saying the same story again, possibly with changes.
  • narrate
    4.0 5/m
    narrate tells events or stories often in a formal or dramatic way, while retell means saying a story again, possibly changed for listeners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fictionalize - to present a true story or event with invented elements, making it fiction
  • antonym: invent - to create something not previously existing (contrasted with telling what has already occurred)
  • synonym: recount - to tell in detail; narrate
  • synonym: repeat - to say or do something again
  • synonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points
