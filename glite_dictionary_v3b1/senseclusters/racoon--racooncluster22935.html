racoon

noun
nocturnal mammal with black facial mask
1.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / r…ôÀàkuÀên / (AmE), / r…ôÀàkuÀên / (BrE)
A small, nocturnal mammal native to North and Central America, known for its dexterous front paws and distinctive black facial mask.

Examples

A racoon animal
A racoon rummaged through the trash cans outside our cabin last night.
The park ranger warned us not to feed the wild racoons.
With its clever paws, the racoon managed to open the cooler and steal our sandwiches.
