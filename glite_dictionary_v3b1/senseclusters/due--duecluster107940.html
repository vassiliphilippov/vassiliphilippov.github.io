due

noun
regular payment or membership fee
Pronunciation: / du / (AmE), / djuː / (BrE)
a regular payment or fee that must be paid, especially to remain a member of an organization

Examples

Pay dues monthly
Late payment of dues may result in suspension of membership.
The annual due for the professional association is quite reasonable.
You can't access the club's facilities unless your dues are paid in full.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fee - a payment made for professional services or privileges
  • synonym: subscription - a payment given at regular intervals to belong to an organization or receive a service
