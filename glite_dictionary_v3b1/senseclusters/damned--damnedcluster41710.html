damned

adjective
condemned to eternal punishment in Hell
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmd / (AmE), / ˈdamd / (BrE)
Condemned to eternal punishment in Hell; doomed to damnation.

Examples

Damned souls
In her fear, she prayed not to be counted among the damned.
The old preacher spoke of damned souls condemned by their wickedness.
Those who led sinful lives were believed to be damned for all eternity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blessed - made holy; enjoying heavenly reward; antonym of 'damned' in religious contexts
