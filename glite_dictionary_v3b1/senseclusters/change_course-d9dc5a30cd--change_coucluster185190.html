change course

verb
to alter direction at sea
1.2
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪndʒ kɔrs / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ kɔːs / (BrE)
(of a vessel) to alter direction, especially by shifting from one side to another at sea.

Examples

To change course at sea
Due to strong winds, the crew had to change course several times during the voyage.
If you see another vessel ahead, it's important to change course early to avoid collision.
The captain ordered the ship to change course to avoid the approaching storm.
