almondy

adjective
almond-shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːməndi / (AmE), / ˈɑːməndi / (BrE)
shaped like an almond; oval and tapering to a point at one or both ends.

Examples

Almondy eyes
Her eyes were large and almondy, giving her a striking, exotic look.
The artist painted the leaves with a distinct almondy shape to add elegance to the design.
He admired the almondy curve of the antique mirror's frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oval - having a rounded, elongated shape like an egg
  • antonym: round - having a circular or spherical shape
