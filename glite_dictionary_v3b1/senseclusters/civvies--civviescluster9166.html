civvies

noun
civilian clothes (not uniform)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪviz / (AmE), / ˈsɪviz / (BrE)
informal clothes worn by civilians, especially as opposed to a military uniform

Examples

Wearing civvies again
After months in the army, it felt strange to wear civvies again.
He changed out of his uniform and into his civvies before leaving the base.
Soldiers on leave often look forward to spending time in their civvies.
