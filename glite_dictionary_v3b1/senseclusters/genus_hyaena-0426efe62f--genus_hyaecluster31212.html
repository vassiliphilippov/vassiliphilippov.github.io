genus Hyaena

noun
the type genus of hyenas
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs haɪˈiːnə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs hʌɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
The scientific genus that is the type genus of the family Hyaenidae, comprising certain species of hyenas.

Examples

The genus Hyaena species
Taxonomists have long debated which species should be classified under the genus Hyaena.
The genus Hyaena includes the striped hyena, one of the most widespread species of its family.
Unlike other hyenas, members of the genus Hyaena possess certain unique dental features.
