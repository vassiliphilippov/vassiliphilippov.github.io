Examples

Synonyms

  • conveyance
    6.0 0.26/m
    conveyance is the act of transmitting information in a formal sense, while communication covers everyday speaking, writing, or other expressive acts.
  • transmission
    3.6 5.79/m
    transmission is the sending of a message often one-way or technical, while communication includes two-way speech, writing, and emotional sharing.
  • intercommunication
    6.2 0.11/m
    intercommunication is mutual exchange between people or systems, while communication can be one-way or two-way and covers many media.
