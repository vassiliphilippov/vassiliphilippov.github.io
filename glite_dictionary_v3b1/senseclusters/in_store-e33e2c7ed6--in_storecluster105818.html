in store

adjective
awaiting; in readiness for the future
Pronunciation: / ɪn stɔr / (AmE), / ɪn stɔː / (BrE)
waiting to happen or be experienced; in readiness for someone or something in the future.

Examples

Surprises in store
No one knows what surprises are in store for us next year.
She wondered what adventures lay in store on her journey.
There is a lot of hard work in store if you take this job.
