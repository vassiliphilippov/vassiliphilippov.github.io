imaginer

noun
person who imagines
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪnər / (AmE), / ɪˈmædʒɪnə / (BrE)
a person who imagines or creates ideas in their mind

Examples

A creative imaginer
As a child, she was a natural imaginer who loved to invent stories and games.
Every inventor is an imaginer at heart, always thinking beyond what exists.
Writers and artists are often described as imaginers because of their creative minds.
As a child, he was known as an imaginer who could turn anything into an adventure.
Writers and artists are often great imaginers of new worlds and possibilities.
The company values imaginers who can bring fresh ideas to the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamer - a person who has many ideas or plans, often imaginative
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts and deals with things as they really are
  • synonym: visionary - a person with original ideas about what the future could be like
