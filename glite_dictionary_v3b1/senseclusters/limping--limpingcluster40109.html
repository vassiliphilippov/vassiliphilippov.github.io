A condition or disability in which walking is impaired, typically due to an injury or deformity of the legs or feet.

Examples

A limping walk
Athletes sometimes develop limping following a serious knee injury.
His limping became more pronounced after the accident.
The limping was obvious as she made her way across the room.

Synonyms

  • lameness
    4.7 0.12/m
    lameness is a medical condition of poor or painful walking, while limping describes the actual uneven movement from injury.
  • limp
    3.3 2.32/m
    a limp is an uneven walk you can see, while limping is the continuous action or condition that produces that gait.
