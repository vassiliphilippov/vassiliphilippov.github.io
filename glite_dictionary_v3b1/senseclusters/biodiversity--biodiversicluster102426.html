biodiversity

noun
variety of life forms in a habitat or globally
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.dɪˈvɝː.sə.ti / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊ.dɪˈvɜː.sɪ.ti / (BrE)
the variety of plant and animal life in a particular habitat or in the world as a whole

Examples

High biodiversity areas
A decline in biodiversity can have serious consequences for the environment.
Conservation efforts aim to protect biodiversity in threatened ecosystems.
The Amazon rainforest is famous for its exceptional biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecosystem - a biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment
