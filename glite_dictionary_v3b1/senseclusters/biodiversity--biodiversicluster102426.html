biodiversity

noun
variety of life forms in a habitat or globally
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.dɪ.ˈvɜːr.sə.tiː/ /; / /baɪ.əʊ.dɪ.ˈvɜːr.sə.tiː/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.dɪ.ˈvɜː.sɪ.tiː/ /; / /baɪ.əʊ.daɪ.ˈvɜː.sɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the variety of plant and animal life in a particular habitat or in the world as a whole

Examples
