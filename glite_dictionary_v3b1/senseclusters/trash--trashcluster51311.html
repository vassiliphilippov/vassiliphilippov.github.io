trash

verb
to damage badly
4.0
Pronunciation: / træʃ / (AmE), / træʃ / (BrE)
to damage something badly or destroy it so that it is no longer useful or valuable

Examples

To trash a room
The teenagers trashed the old warehouse during the night.
After the wild party, they found the living room completely trashed.
He accidentally trashed his laptop by spilling water on it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: repair - to fix or restore something that is damaged
  • synonym: ruin - to spoil or destroy something completely
  • synonym: wreck - to destroy or severely damage something
