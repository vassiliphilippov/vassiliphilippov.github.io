small game

noun
wild animals hunted for sport or food
2.9
Pronunciation: / smɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / smɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
Wild animals of small size, such as rabbits or pheasants, that are hunted for sport or food.

Examples

Hunting small game
He spent the afternoon tracking small game through the wooded area.
Hunting small game requires skill, patience, and knowledge of animal habitats.
Licenses for small game differ from those required to hunt big game animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: big game - large wild animals hunted for sport or food, such as deer or lions
