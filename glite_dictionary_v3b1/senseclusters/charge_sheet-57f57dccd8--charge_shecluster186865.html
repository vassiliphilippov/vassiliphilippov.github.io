charge sheet

noun
police record of offenses or arrests
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrdʒ ʃit / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ ʃiːt / (BrE)
an official written record of charges made, typically listing offenses and arrests at a police station

Examples

The charge sheet list
According to the charge sheet, the accused was involved in three separate incidents.
The lawyer requested a copy of the charge sheet before preparing her defense.
The police added the new suspect's name to the charge sheet after the arrest.
