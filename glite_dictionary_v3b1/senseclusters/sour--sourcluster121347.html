sour

adjective
showing bad temper or mood
4.4
Pronunciation: / saʊɚ / (AmE), / saʊə / (BrE)
displaying a resentful, unfriendly, or moody attitude; showing ill humor or sulkiness

Examples

Sour mood
After losing the match, her mood turned distinctly sour for the rest of the evening.
He gave me a sour look when I suggested we start early.
The manager's sour attitude made everyone uncomfortable during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: glum - looking or feeling dejected
  • synonym: morose - gloomy or sullen in mood
