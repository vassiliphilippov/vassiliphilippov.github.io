prosthodontic

adjective
relating to dental prosthetics
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːsθoʊˈdɑn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌprɒsθəˈdɒn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to prosthodontics, the branch of dentistry concerned with the design, manufacture, and fitting of artificial replacements for teeth and other parts of the mouth.

Examples

Prosthodontic treatment
Advances in prosthodontic technology have greatly improved the quality of dental implants.
She decided to pursue a career in prosthodontic dentistry after dental school.
The patient was referred to a prosthodontic specialist for new dentures.

Related Terms
