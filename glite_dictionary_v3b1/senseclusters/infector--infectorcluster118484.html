infector

noun
one who spreads harm
8.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfek.tər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfek.tə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that spreads harmful ideas, behaviors, or influences to others, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Infector of fear
The rumor mill became a powerful infector of fear throughout the office.
Social media can act as an infector of negative attitudes among young people.
He was seen as an infector of bad habits in his group of friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protector - someone who shields others from harm or negative influences
  • synonym: spreader - someone or something that distributes or transmits something widely
