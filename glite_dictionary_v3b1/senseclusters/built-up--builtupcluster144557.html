built-up

adjective
densely developed area
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪltˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌbɪltˈʌp / (BrE)
(of an area) containing a large number of buildings; densely developed or populated.

Examples

A built-up area
Many built-up parts of the city suffer from heavy traffic congestion.
The school is located in a built-up suburban neighborhood.
They prefer living away from built-up areas, closer to nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rural - relating to the countryside, less populated
  • synonym: urban - relating to a city or densely populated area
