Camarasaurus

proper noun
Jurassic dinosaur
Pronunciation: / kəˌmɛrəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / kəˌmɑːrəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a large, long-necked herbivorous dinosaur from the Jurassic period, known for its box-shaped skull

Examples

The Camarasaurus skull
The paleontologist carefully reconstructed the skull of a Camarasaurus for the new exhibit.
Fossils of Camarasaurus have been found in many parts of North America.
Children were amazed by the towering skeleton of Camarasaurus at the natural history museum.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Stegosaurus - a herbivorous dinosaur with plates along its back from the Jurassic period
