dokey

interjection
informal agreement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊki / (AmE), / ˈdəʊki / (BrE)
an informal interjection used to express agreement or acceptance, similar to 'okay'

Examples

Dokey then
"I'll meet you at the cafe at six." "Dokey, see you then!"
If you want to start now, dokey, I'm ready.
"Can you send me the files by tomorrow?" "Dokey, I'll do it tonight."
