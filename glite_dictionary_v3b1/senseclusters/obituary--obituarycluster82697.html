obituary

noun
death notice with short biography
3.1 3.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbɪtʃuˌɛri / (AmE), / əˈbɪtjʊəri /; / əʊˈbɪtjʊəri / (BrE)
a published notice of a person's death, usually with a short biography or account of their life

Examples
