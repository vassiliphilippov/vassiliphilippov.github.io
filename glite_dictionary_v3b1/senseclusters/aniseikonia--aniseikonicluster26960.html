aniseikonia

noun
visual defect with unequal images in eyes
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.nɪ.saɪ.ˈkoʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /æ.nɪ.saɪ.ˈkoʊ.niː.ə/ (AmE), /ə.nɪ.saɪ.ˈkəʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /æ.nɪ.saɪ.ˈkəʊ.niː.ə/ (BrE)
A visual defect in which the shape and size of the image seen by each eye are different, causing difficulties with binocular vision.
