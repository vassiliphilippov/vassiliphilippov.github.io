quiz

noun
short test with few questions
3.1
Pronunciation: / kwɪz / (AmE), / kwɪz / (BrE)
a short test or examination, typically consisting of a few questions, often given to students

Examples

A school quiz
He scored full marks on the general knowledge quiz.
Please complete the online quiz before tomorrow's class.
The teacher gave us a surprise quiz on the chapter we studied yesterday.
