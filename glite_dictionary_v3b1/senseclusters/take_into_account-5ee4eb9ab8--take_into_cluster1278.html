take into account

verb
consider when judging or deciding
1.3
Pronunciation: / teɪk ˈɪntu əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / teɪk ˈɪntuː əˈkaʊnt /; / teɪk ˈɪntjuː əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
to consider or include something when judging a situation or making a decision

Examples

To take into account ideas
She always takes into account her team's ideas before finalizing the project.
The judge will take into account your previous good behavior before making a decision.
We must take into account the possibility of rain when planning the event.
The manager failed to take into account the impact of the changes on employees.
When making a decision, it's important to take into account all relevant information.
You should take into account possible traffic delays when planning your journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consider - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
  • synonym: take into consideration - to consider or include in decision-making
