shillyshally

noun
hesitation; procrastination
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪliˌʃæli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪliˌʃæli / (BrE)
the act of hesitating or procrastinating instead of taking immediate action

Examples

Endless shillyshally
After an hour of shillyshally, they finally agreed on a course of action.
His constant shillyshally annoyed his colleagues, who preferred to make decisions quickly.
We lost a great opportunity because of too much shillyshally on the team's part.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decisiveness - ability to make decisions quickly
  • synonym: procrastination - the act of delaying or postponing something
