blistered

adjective
with raised bubbles
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈblɪs.tərd/ (AmE), /ˈblɪs.təd/ (BrE)
having blisters or raised bubbles on the surface, often caused by heat, moisture, or chemical action

Examples

Blistered peppers
The chef served blistered peppers as a spicy appetizer at the restaurant.
After hours in the sun, the paint on the old fence became blistered and started to peel.
The hiker's feet were sore and blistered after walking for miles in wet boots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bubbled - having bubbles or raised areas on the surface
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without raised areas
