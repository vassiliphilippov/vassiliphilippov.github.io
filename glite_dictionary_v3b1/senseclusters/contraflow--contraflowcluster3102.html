contraflow

adjective
opposite-direction traffic
7.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêntr…ôÀåflo ä / (AmE), / Ààk…íntr…ôÀåfl…ô ä / (BrE)
used to describe a lane, route, or traffic arrangement that moves in the opposite direction to the normal flow of traffic

Examples

Contraflow bus lane
A contraflow bus lane lets buses travel against the usual direction of traffic on this street.
During roadworks, a contraflow system was set up to keep traffic moving in both directions.
Cyclists use a contraflow cycle lane to ride safely opposite to the main traffic flow.

Synonyms
