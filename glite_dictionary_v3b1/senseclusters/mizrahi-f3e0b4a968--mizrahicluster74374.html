Mizrahi

adjective
Jewish Middle Eastern
7.9
Pronunciation: / mɪzˈrɑːhi / (AmE), / mɪzˈrɑːhi / (BrE)
relating to Jews of Middle Eastern or North African descent, their culture, or traditions

Examples

Mizrahi culture
The Mizrahi community has preserved many unique culinary traditions from the Middle East.
She wore a beautiful dress inspired by Mizrahi designs at the cultural event.
Many Mizrahi Jews celebrate holidays with music and customs from their ancestral countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Ashkenazi - relating to Jews of Central or Eastern European descent
  • synonym: Sephardic - relating to Jews of Spanish or Portuguese descent
