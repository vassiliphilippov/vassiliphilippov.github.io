physiotherapeutic

adjective
relating to physical therapy
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzioʊˌθɛrəˈpjutɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or used in physical therapy; concerning the treatment of disease or injury through physical methods such as exercise or massage rather than drugs or surgery.

Examples

Physiotherapeutic treatment
He received several physiotherapeutic sessions after his knee surgery.
Modern hospitals are equipped with advanced physiotherapeutic devices for rehabilitation.
The doctor recommended a physiotherapeutic approach to help her recover from the injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physical therapy - treatment of injuries or illnesses through physical methods
