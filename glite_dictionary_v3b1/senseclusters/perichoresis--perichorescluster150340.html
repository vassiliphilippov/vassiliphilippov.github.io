perichoresis

noun
intimate union
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪkəˈriːsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪkəˈriːsɪs /; / ˌpɛrɪkɒˈriːsɪs / (BrE)
the close, reciprocal union or mutual indwelling between persons or things, especially used in Christian theology to describe the relationship within the Trinity or between Christ's divine and human natures
