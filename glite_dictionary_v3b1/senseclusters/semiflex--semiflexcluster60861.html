semiflex

adjective
partly flexible
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌflɛks / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌflɛks / (BrE)
partly flexible; able to bend or move to some extent but not fully flexible

Examples

Semiflex cable
The semiflex cable is ideal for installations that require some movement but not complete flexibility.
We chose a semiflex hose because it can bend slightly without kinking.
This semiflex material is used in products that need to hold their shape but still allow a little give.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • degree lower: rigid - not able to bend or be changed easily
