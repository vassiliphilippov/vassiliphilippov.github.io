mythological figure

noun
character from a myth or legend
1.5
Pronunciation: / mɪˌθɑləˈdʒɪkəl ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / mɪˌθɒləˈdʒɪkəl ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
a character from a myth or legend, typically having supernatural attributes or involved in traditional stories of a culture

Examples

A mythological figure costume
He dressed as a mythological figure for the costume party.
Many cultures have a mythological figure representing the sun or the moon.
The story revolves around a mythological figure who can control the weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: historical figure - a person who actually lived in history, as opposed to legend
  • synonym: legendary figure - a character famous for legendary exploits or stories
