Riksmaal

noun
former Norwegian language standard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪksˌmɔl / (AmE), / ˈrɪksˌmɔːl / (BrE)
the former official written standard of Norwegian, which in 1929 was officially renamed Bokmål.

Examples

Riksmaal written standard
During the early 20th century, Riksmaal was the main written standard used in Norway.
Linguists often study Riksmaal to understand the development of modern Norwegian.
The transition from Riksmaal to Bokmål reflected political and cultural changes in Norwegian society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bokmål - the current official written standard of Norwegian, formerly called Riksmaal
