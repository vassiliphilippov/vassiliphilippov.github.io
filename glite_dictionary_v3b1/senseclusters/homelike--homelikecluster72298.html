homelike

adjective
cozy and comfortable like home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
(of a place or atmosphere) having the comfortable and cozy feeling of home; making people feel at ease as if they were at home.

Examples

A homelike atmosphere
She decorated her apartment with warm colors to give it a more homelike atmosphere.
The small café had a homelike charm that made everyone feel welcome.
We found the inn so homelike that we decided to extend our stay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homely - (British) simple and cozy in a way that makes you feel comfortable
  • synonym: homey - (of a place) cozy and comfortable like home
