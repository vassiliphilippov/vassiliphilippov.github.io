purse

noun
sum of money offered as a prize
5.7
Pronunciation: / pɝs / (AmE), / pɜːs / (BrE)
a sum of money, especially as a prize in a competition or a fund for a particular purpose

Examples

A purse prize
She won the tournament and collected the entire purse as her award.
The purse for the championship boxing match reached a record one million dollars.
This year's marathon offers a larger purse than ever before to attract top athletes.
She donated generously from her own purse to support the relief efforts.
The team collected money from sponsors to create a purse for the tournament winner.
They established a common purse to cover all household expenses.
