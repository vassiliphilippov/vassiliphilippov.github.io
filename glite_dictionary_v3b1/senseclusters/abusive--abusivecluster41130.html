abusive

adjective
using physical or psychological maltreatment
Pronunciation: / əˈbjusɪv / (AmE), / əˈbjuːsɪv / (BrE)
involving the use of physical or psychological harm, especially through cruel or violent treatment

Examples

Abusive relationship
He had a history of engaging in abusive relationships.
She took legal action against her abusive employer.
The child was removed from his abusive household for his own safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruel - willingly causing pain or suffering to others
  • antonym: kind - showing a gentle, friendly, or considerate nature
