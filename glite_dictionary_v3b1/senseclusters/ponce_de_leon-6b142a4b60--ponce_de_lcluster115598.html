Ponce de Leon

proper noun
Spanish explorer who discovered Florida
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːns də leɪˈɒn, ˈpɒnseɪ də leɪˈɒn / (AmE), / ˈpɒns deɪ leɪˈɒn, ˈpɒnseɪ deɪ leɪˈɒn / (BrE)
(1460–1521) A Spanish explorer who accompanied Columbus's second voyage in 1493 and in 1513 discovered Florida while searching for the legendary Fountain of Youth.

Examples

Ponce de Leon explorer
Ponce de Leon is often mentioned in discussions about early Spanish exploration of America.
Many people believe that Ponce de Leon searched for the Fountain of Youth in Florida.
The history book contains a detailed account of Ponce de Leon's explorations in the New World.
