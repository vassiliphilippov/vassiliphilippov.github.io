photojournalism

noun
news reporting through photographs
2.1 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.tə.dʒɜːr.nə.lɪz.əm/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.tə.dʒɜː.nə.lɪz.əm/ (BrE)
The practice of reporting news and telling stories mainly through photographs rather than written articles.

Examples

Award-winning photojournalism
Award-winning photojournalism has captured some of the most iconic moments in history.
Digital technology has transformed the field of photojournalism in recent years.
She studied photojournalism at university to combine her love of photography and storytelling.
