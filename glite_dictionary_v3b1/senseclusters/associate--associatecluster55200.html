associate

verb
to spend time with (people) regularly
2.4
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsiˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsieɪt / (BrE)
to spend time with a particular group of people, especially regularly; to be connected socially with someone

Examples

To associate with friends
Ever since starting school, she has associated with a new group of friends.
He tends to associate with artists and musicians in the city.
You shouldn't associate with people who bring out the worst in you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affiliate - to officially connect or associate with a group or person
