mousekin

noun
timid or shy person
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊskɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊskɪn / (BrE)
a timid or very quiet person, often used affectionately to describe someone who is shy or reserved

Examples

A shy mousekin
Despite her talents, the mousekin in the group rarely spoke up during meetings.
He was a gentle mousekin who preferred reading to joining loud parties.
At the family gathering, their youngest daughter acted like a mousekin, quietly observing everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrovert - a person who is outgoing and enjoys socializing
  • synonym: introvert - a person who is more comfortable alone or in small groups
  • synonym: wallflower - a shy or reserved person who tends to stay on the sidelines
