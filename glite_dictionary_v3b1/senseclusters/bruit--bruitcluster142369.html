bruit

verb
spread a rumor or report
9.9
Pronunciation: / brut / (AmE), / brʊɪt / (BrE)
to spread a rumor or report widely; to report or voice about, usually as gossip

Examples

To bruit the news
It was bruited that the company would be sold to a foreign investor.
Rumors of a merger were quickly bruited throughout the financial community.
The scandal was bruited about town before the facts were established.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to make widely known
  • synonym: rumor - to spread unverified information
