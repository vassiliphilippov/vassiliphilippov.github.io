mudhole

verb
to defeat soundly
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌhəʊl / (BrE)
to defeat or beat someone decisively, especially in a contest or competition (informal, US)

Examples

To mudhole a team
If they keep playing like this, they'll mudhole the other team in the finals.
The coach promised the fans that his players would mudhole their long-time rivals.
We watched our school mudhole the competition at the state championship.
