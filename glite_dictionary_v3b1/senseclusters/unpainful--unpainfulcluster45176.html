unpainful

adjective
not causing pain
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪn.fəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪn.fəl / (BrE)
not accompanied by pain; causing no pain.

Examples

An unpainful injection
Most vaccinations are quick and unpainful when administered correctly.
She described the healing process as completely unpainful and stress-free.
The procedure was surprisingly unpainful for the patient.

  • synonym: painless - not feeling or causing pain
