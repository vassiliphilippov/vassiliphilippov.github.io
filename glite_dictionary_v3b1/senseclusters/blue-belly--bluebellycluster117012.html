blue-belly

noun
a western North American lizard with a blue belly
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛli / (BrE)
a common western North American lizard (especially the western fence lizard) with a distinctive bluish belly, often seen on logs or rocks

Examples

A blue-belly lizard
A blue-belly can often be identified by the distinctive coloration on its underside.
The children tried to catch a quick blue-belly near the old log pile.
While hiking, we saw a blue-belly sunning itself on a warm rock.
