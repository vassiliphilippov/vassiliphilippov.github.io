ree

verb
to shriek loudly
9.9
Pronunciation: / riː / (AmE), / riː / (BrE)
to make a loud, high-pitched, and often prolonged cry or shriek.

Examples

Birds ree loudly
The startled bird began to ree as it flew away from the tree.
At night, the fox would ree loudly, its cry echoing through the valley.
Children laughed when they heard the parrot ree unexpectedly in the quiet room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scream - to make a loud, piercing cry
  • synonym: shriek - to make a loud, sharp, high-pitched sound
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very softly or quietly
