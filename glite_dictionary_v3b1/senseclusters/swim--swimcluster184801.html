swim

noun
an act of swimming
Pronunciation: / swɪm / (AmE), / swɪm / (BrE)
an act or period of moving through water by using the arms and legs

Examples

A morning swim
After a long hike, they enjoyed a refreshing swim in the lake.
She goes for a swim every morning to stay fit.
The children raced to see who could finish their swim the fastest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swimming - the activity or sport of moving through water using the body
