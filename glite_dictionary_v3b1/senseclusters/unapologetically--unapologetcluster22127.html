unapologetically

adverb
without regret
6.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.pə.lɑː.ˈdʒe.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.pə.lɒ.ˈdʒe.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows no regret, shame, or need to apologize; openly and without trying to hide or soften something

Examples

Unapologetically honest
He unapologetically expressed his opinions, even when they were unpopular.
The artist unapologetically embraced her unique style, ignoring criticism from others.
They unapologetically pursued their goals, never feeling the need to justify their choices.
He spoke unapologetically about his unconventional career choices during the interview.
The artist creates unapologetically bold paintings that challenge traditional styles.
They live unapologetically, embracing their true selves without concern for others' opinions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apologetically - in a way that shows regret or asks for forgiveness
  • antonym: ashamedly - in a way that shows shame or embarrassment
  • synonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous way
  • synonym: defiantly - in a way that shows resistance or bold disobedience
  • synonym: unashamedly - in a way that shows no shame or embarrassment
