hallucinator

noun
one who hallucinates
8.9
Pronunciation: / həˈluːsəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / həˈluːsɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that experiences or produces hallucinations

Examples

The hallucinator sees visions
The doctor noted that the hallucinator described vivid and unusual visions during the examination.
Researchers studied the effects of the drug on a known hallucinator in the clinical trial.
A hallucinator may have difficulty distinguishing between reality and imagination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hallucinant - something that causes hallucinations
  • synonym: hallucinogen - a substance that induces hallucinations
