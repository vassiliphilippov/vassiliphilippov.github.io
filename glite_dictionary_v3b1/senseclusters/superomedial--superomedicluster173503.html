superomedial

adjective
above and toward midline
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpəroʊˈmiːdiəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərəʊˈmiːdiəl / (BrE)
located above and toward the midline of the body or an organ

Examples

The superomedial margin
The superomedial margin of the orbit is formed by the frontal bone.
Swelling was noted in the superomedial region of the patient's right knee.
The superomedial aspect of the thigh is closest to the body's midline and upper part.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inferolateral - located below and away from the midline
