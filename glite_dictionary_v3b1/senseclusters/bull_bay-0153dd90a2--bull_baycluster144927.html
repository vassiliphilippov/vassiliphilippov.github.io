bull bay

noun
evergreen tree with large white flowers
7.8
Pronunciation: / bʊl beɪ / (AmE), / bʊl beɪ / (BrE)
an evergreen tree of the southern United States, having large, stiff, glossy leaves and huge, fragrant white flowers; Magnolia grandiflora.

Examples

The bull bay tree
Many southern gardens feature the bull bay as a focal point for its year-round beauty.
The bull bay can grow quite tall, providing ample shade with its large leaves.
The bull bay is prized for its stunning, aromatic blossoms and glossy foliage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: southern magnolia - another common name for Magnolia grandiflora
