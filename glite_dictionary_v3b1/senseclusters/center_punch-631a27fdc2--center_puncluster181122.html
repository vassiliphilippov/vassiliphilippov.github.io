center punch

verb
make an indentation to guide drilling
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntər pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə pʌntʃ / (BrE)
to make a small indentation or hole in a surface as a guide for drilling

Examples

To center punch metal
Before drilling, it's important to center punch the metal to keep the bit from slipping.
He always center punches a mark on the wood for accuracy.
The technician center punched each spot before starting the holes.
