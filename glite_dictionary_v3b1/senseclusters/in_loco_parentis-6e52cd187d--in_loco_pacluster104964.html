in loco parentis

adverb
in the place of a parent
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˌloʊkoʊ pəˈrɛntɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˌləʊkəʊ pəˈrɛntɪs / (BrE)
In the place of a parent; having the responsibilities and duties of a parent.

Examples

In loco parentis role
As a guardian, she assumed a role in loco parentis after the parents moved abroad.
Teachers are responsible for their students in loco parentis while at school.
The school acts in loco parentis when supervising students during field trips.
