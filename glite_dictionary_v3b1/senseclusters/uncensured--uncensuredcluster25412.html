uncensured

adjective
not criticized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛnʃərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛnʃəd / (BrE)
not criticized, condemned, or officially disapproved of.

Examples

Uncensured mistake
The manager's mistake went uncensured despite its impact on the project.
No one wanted to speak up, so the behavior remained uncensured by the group.
His controversial remarks did not go uncensured for long.

Related Terms

  • antonym: censured - officially criticized or condemned
  • synonym: uncriticized - not criticized or found fault with
