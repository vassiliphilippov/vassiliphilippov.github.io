suborder Brachyura

noun
group of crustaceans; true crabs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˌɔrdər brəˈkjuːərə / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˌɔːdə brəˈkjʊərə / (BrE)
A group of crustaceans, including true crabs, characterized by a reduced abdomen folded against the underside of the body.

Examples

Suborder Brachyura crabs
Biologists classify the blue crab within the suborder Brachyura.
Research on the suborder Brachyura reveals a wide range of adaptations for life in various habitats.
The suborder Brachyura includes most of the crabs commonly found along the coast.
