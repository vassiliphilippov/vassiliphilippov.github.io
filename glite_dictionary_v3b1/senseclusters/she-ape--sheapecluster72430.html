she-ape

noun
offensive term for an unruly woman
Pronunciation: / ʃiː eɪp / (AmE), / ʃiː eɪp / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a wild or unruly girl or woman behaving in an unpleasant way.

Examples

A she-ape woman
At the meeting, she lashed out and behaved like a total she-ape.
He insultingly called her a she-ape after their heated argument.
The tabloids often portray outspoken women as she-apes, which is both unfair and offensive.
