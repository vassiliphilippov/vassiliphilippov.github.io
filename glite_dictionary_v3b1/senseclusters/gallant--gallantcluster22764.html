gallant

noun
attentive or escorting man
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlænt /; / ˈɡæl.ənt / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.ənt / (BrE)
a man who is attentive, polite, or chivalrous, especially toward women

Examples

A gallant escort
As a true gallant, he offered her his arm as they walked.
Every lady at the party seemed to have a gallant by her side.
He was her gallant for the evening, escorting her to the ball.
The gallant helped the lady across the busy street without hesitation.
At the ball, every gallant tried to impress the guests with his manners.
Stories of the gallant who saved the village were told for generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boor - a rude or insensitive person
  • synonym: gentleman - a chivalrous, courteous man
  • synonym: hero - a man admired for courage or noble qualities
