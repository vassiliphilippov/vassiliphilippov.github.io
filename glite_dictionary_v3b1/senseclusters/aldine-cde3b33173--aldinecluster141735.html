Aldine

adjective
of Aldus Manutius press
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈælˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌdiːn / (BrE)
relating to books printed by Aldus Manutius or his press in Venice, especially during the Renaissance

Examples

Aldine edition
The museum displayed an Aldine edition of a famous Greek classic.
Collectors highly value Aldine books for their historical significance and fine printing.
She wrote her thesis on the influence of Aldine typography in Renaissance Europe.
