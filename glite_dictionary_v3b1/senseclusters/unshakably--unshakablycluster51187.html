unshakably

adverb
in an unwavering, determined manner
2.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈʃeɪ.kə.bli/ /; / /ən.ˈʃeɪ.kə.bli/ /; / /ʌn.ˈʃeɪ.kə.blɪ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈʃeɪ.kə.bli/ /; / /ən.ˈʃeɪ.kə.bli/ /; / /ʌn.ˈʃeɪ.kə.blɪ/ (BrE)
