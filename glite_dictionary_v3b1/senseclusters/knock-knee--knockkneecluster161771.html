knock-knee

noun
condition where knees angle inward
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnɑːk.niː/ (AmE), /ˈnɒk.niː/ (BrE)
a condition in which the knees angle inward and touch one another when the legs are straightened

Examples
