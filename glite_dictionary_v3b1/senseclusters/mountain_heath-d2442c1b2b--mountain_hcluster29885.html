mountain heath

noun
small alpine flowering shrub
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntn ˌhiːθ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˌhiːθ / (BrE)
a small shrub with tiny evergreen leaves and pink or purple flowers, typically found on Alpine summits and high ground in Asia, Europe, and the United States

Examples

Mountain heath plants
Hikers passed through fields of mountain heath as they climbed higher into the mountains.
Scientists are studying the adaptation of mountain heath to harsh alpine climates.
The slopes were covered in mountain heath blooming with delicate pink flowers.
