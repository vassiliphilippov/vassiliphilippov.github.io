bronze medal

noun
third-place medal in competitions
Pronunciation: / brɑnz ˈmɛd.əl / (AmE), / brɒnz ˈmɛd.əl / (BrE)
a medal made of bronze, typically awarded for third place in a competition or event

Examples

Win the bronze medal
After a tough match, the home team secured the bronze medal in the tournament.

Winning a bronze medal at the Olympics is a significant achievement for any athlete.
