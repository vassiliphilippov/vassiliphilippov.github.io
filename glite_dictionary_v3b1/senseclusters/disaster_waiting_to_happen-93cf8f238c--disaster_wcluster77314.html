disaster waiting to happen

noun
likely trouble or failure
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzæstɚ ˈweɪtɪŋ tə ˈhæpən / (AmE), / dɪˈzɑːstə ˈweɪtɪŋ tə ˈhæpən / (BrE)
a situation that is very likely to result in serious trouble or failure

Examples

A disaster waiting to happen
Allowing children to play near the busy street is a disaster waiting to happen.
The rickety bridge is a disaster waiting to happen if it isn't repaired soon.
With all those exposed wires, that old building is a disaster waiting to happen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident waiting to happen - a person or thing likely to cause trouble or harm
