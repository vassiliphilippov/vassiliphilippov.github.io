unsatisfactory

adjective
not good enough; not giving satisfaction
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsætɪsˈfæktəri / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsætɪsˈfæktəri / (BrE)
not good enough to meet a particular standard or to be acceptable; not giving satisfaction

Examples

An unsatisfactory answer
His explanation for the delay was unsatisfactory and left us with more questions.
The results of the experiment were deemed unsatisfactory by the review panel.
We received unsatisfactory service at the restaurant last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptable - satisfactory or allowable
  • synonym: inadequate - not sufficient or suitable
  • antonym: satisfactory - meeting requirements; adequate
