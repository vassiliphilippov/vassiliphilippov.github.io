shortsighted

adjective
lacking foresight or vision
1.5 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɔːrt.saɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈʃɔːt.saɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
not thinking about the future or possible consequences; lacking foresight

Examples

Shortsighted decision
Critics argued that the new law was a shortsighted solution to a complex problem.
Her shortsighted decision to cut research funding hindered innovation in the industry.
The company's shortsighted strategy ignored the long-term risks involved.
It would be shortsighted to ignore the possible effects of climate change.
Many criticized the new law as shortsighted and likely to cause future problems.
The company's shortsighted decision to cut research funding hurt its long-term prospects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foresighted - having or showing awareness of and planning for the future
  • synonym: imprudent - not showing care for the consequences of an action
  • synonym: myopic - lacking foresight or discernment; narrow-minded
