corduroyed

adjective
with corduroy ridges
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdʒəˌrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdjʊˌrɔɪd / (BrE)
made of or covered with corduroy, or having ridges resembling corduroy fabric

Examples

Corduroyed road surface
The old road was corduroyed to help wagons pass through the muddy forest.
He wore a corduroyed jacket that was both stylish and warm.
The trail had a corduroyed surface, making it easier for hikers to walk after rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ridged - having raised lines or ridges
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without ridges
