freeholder

noun
owner of a freehold estate
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
A person who owns a freehold estate in land or property.

Examples

A freeholder owns land
As a freeholder, she enjoys full ownership rights over her property.
The freeholder is responsible for the maintenance of the building's structure.
The council met with the freeholder to discuss lease extensions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leaseholder - person who holds property by lease, not by freehold
