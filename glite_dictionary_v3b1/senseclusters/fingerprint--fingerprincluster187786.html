fingerprint

verb
take an impression of fingerprints
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌprɪnt / (BrE)
to take an impression of a person's fingerprints, usually for identification purposes

Examples

To fingerprint a person
All new employees must be fingerprinted before starting work at the bank.
She was surprised when airport security asked to fingerprint her upon arrival.
The police fingerprinted the suspect as part of the investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: identify - to establish the identity of somebody or something
