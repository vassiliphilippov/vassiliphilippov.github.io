plane

noun
carpenter's tool for smoothing wood
4.3 7.1
Pronunciation: / pleɪn / (AmE), / pleɪn / (BrE)
a hand tool with a sharp blade used for smoothing or shaping wood surfaces

Examples

A wood plane
After using the saw, he finished the wood with a plane for a sleek surface.

He used a plane to smooth the rough edges of the table.
