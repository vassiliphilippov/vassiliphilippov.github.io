voodoo

noun
magical charm
7.7 8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvuːduː / (AmE), / ˈvuːduː / (BrE)

Examples

Voodoo charm

Some believed the mysterious illness was caused by a voodoo placed on the family.
The merchant sold handmade voodoo charms to curious tourists.
