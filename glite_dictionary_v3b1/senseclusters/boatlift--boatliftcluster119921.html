boatlift

noun
emergency boat transport
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊt.lɪft/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊt.lɪft/ (BrE)
an organized operation to transport people or goods by boat, especially in emergencies or when other transport is unavailable

Examples

Rescue boatlift
During the hurricane, a massive boatlift helped rescue stranded residents from flooded neighborhoods.
The 9/11 boatlift became the largest water evacuation in American history.
Volunteers organized a boatlift to deliver supplies to the isolated island community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airlift - an operation to transport people or goods by aircraft, especially in emergencies
  • synonym: evacuation - the process of moving people from a dangerous place to safety
