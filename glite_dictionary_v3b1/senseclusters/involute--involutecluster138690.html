involute

verb
to curl inward
9.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈvoʊ.luːt/ /; / /ˈɪn.və.luːt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈvɒl.juːt/ /; / /ˈɪn.və.luːt/ (BrE)
to curl inward or to return to a previous or original condition

Examples

Leaves involute at night
As the sun set, the leaves began to involute and close up for the night.
Some plants involute their petals to protect themselves from cold temperatures.
After the swelling decreased, the tissue started to involute and return to normal.

  • antonym: unfold - to open or spread out from a folded state
