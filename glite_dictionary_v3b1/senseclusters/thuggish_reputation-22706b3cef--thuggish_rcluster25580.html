thuggish reputation

noun
reputation for violence or criminality
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌɡɪʃ ˌrɛpjəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈθʌɡɪʃ ˌrɛpjʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
A reputation for being violent, aggressive, or involved in criminal behavior.

Examples

Thuggish reputation for violence
Despite his thuggish reputation, he was known to be kind to animals.
The company's thuggish reputation made new clients wary of doing business with them.
The football team gained a thuggish reputation after several brawls on the pitch.
