quickie

noun
hurried repair or quick job
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪki / (AmE), / ˈkwɪki / (BrE)
a task or activity that is done quickly, often with little attention to detail

Examples

A quickie task
That shelf looks unstable, probably because it was hung up as a quickie.
The mechanic gave my car a quickie to get it running until I could schedule a full repair.
We had to do a quickie on the leaking faucet before the guests arrived.
He grabbed a quickie breakfast before heading to work.
The meeting was just a quickie to update everyone on the project status.
She wrote a quickie note before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marathon - a long and demanding activity or event
  • synonym: patch - a temporary or quick fix to something broken
  • synonym: rush job - a task done quickly, often with less care
