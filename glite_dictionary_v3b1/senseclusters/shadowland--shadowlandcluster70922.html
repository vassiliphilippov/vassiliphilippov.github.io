shadowland

noun
realm of the dead
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊˌlænd / (BrE)
a mythical or imagined place where the spirits of the dead are believed to dwell

Examples

The shadowland of spirits
In the old legend, the hero must journey through the shadowland to rescue his friend.
Some cultures believe that dreams are a way to visit the shadowland and speak with ancestors.
The poet described her grief as wandering alone in a silent shadowland.
