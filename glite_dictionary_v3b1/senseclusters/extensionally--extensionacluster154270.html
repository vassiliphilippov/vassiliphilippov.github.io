extensionally

adverb
by actual instances
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃənəli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the actual instances or members of a set, rather than their meaning or definition

Examples

To be extensionally equal
The two concepts are extensionally the same, even though their definitions differ.
Philosophers often compare terms extensionally to see if they refer to the same things.
The categories match extensionally, but their descriptions are not identical.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intensionally - in a way that relates to the meaning or definition of something
