spur of the moment

noun
done impulsively, without planning
Pronunciation: / spɝː əv ðə ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / spɜː əv ðə ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
done impulsively, without forethought or planning; prompted by a sudden feeling or desire

Examples

A spur of the moment decision
It was a spur of the moment decision to join the road trip with my friends.
She bought the expensive shoes on a spur of the moment and later regretted it.
We booked our vacation at the spur of the moment, without any plans or reservations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impulsive - acting suddenly without forethought
  • antonym: premeditated - planned or thought out in advance
