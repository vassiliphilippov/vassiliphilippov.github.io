hausen

noun
species of sturgeon for caviar
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊzən / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzən / (BrE)
A large sturgeon (Huso huso) found in the Black and Caspian seas, valued for its caviar and isinglass.

Examples

A large hausen fish
Fishermen caught a massive hausen in the Caspian Sea last spring.
Strict regulations protect the hausen from overfishing due to its endangered status.
The hausen is renowned for producing the finest caviar in the world.

  • synonym: beluga - Another name for the hausen; the beluga sturgeon
