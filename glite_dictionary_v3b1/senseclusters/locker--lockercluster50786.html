locker

noun
a storage trunk, often in barracks
4.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑː.kər/ (AmE), /ˈlɒ.kə/ (BrE)
a small or large secure container or compartment, usually with a lock, for storing personal belongings in places like schools, gyms, or barracks
