locker

noun
a storage trunk, often in barracks
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈlɒkə / (BrE)
a small or large secure container or compartment, usually with a lock, for storing personal belongings in places like schools, gyms, or barracks

Examples

School locker
Each recruit was assigned a locker to store personal items during training.
Old photographs were found in a battered locker in the barracks.
The soldier kept his valuables in a locker at the foot of his bed.
I forgot the combination to my gym locker and had to ask for help.
Most employees keep their personal belongings in a locker at work.
She placed her bag in the locker before heading to class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabinet - a piece of furniture with shelves or drawers for storing items
  • synonym: chest - a large strong box for storage
  • synonym: safe - a secure lockable box for valuables
