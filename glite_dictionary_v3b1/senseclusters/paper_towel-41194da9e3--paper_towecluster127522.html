paper towel

noun
disposable absorbent cleaning towel
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpɚ ˌtaʊəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌtaʊəl / (BrE)
a disposable towel made of absorbent paper, used especially for cleaning up spills or drying hands

Examples

A roll of paper towels
He reached for a roll of paper towels to wipe the countertop.
Please dry your hands with a paper towel after washing them.
She grabbed a paper towel to clean up the coffee spill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: towel - piece of absorbent cloth or paper for drying or wiping
