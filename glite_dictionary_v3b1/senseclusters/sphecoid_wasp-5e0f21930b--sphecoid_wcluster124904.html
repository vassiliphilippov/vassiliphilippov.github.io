sphecoid wasp

noun
a solitary wasp of the Sphecoidea superfamily
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsfiːkɔɪd wɑsp / (AmE), / ˈsfiːkɔɪd wɒsp / (BrE)
any of various solitary wasps of the superfamily Sphecoidea, typically non-social and known for their burrowing or hunting behavior

A sphecoid wasp can often be seen digging a burrow in sandy soil to lay its eggs.
