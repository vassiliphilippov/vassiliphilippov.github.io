hardtop

noun
car with a fixed, rigid roof
4.3 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːrd.tɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːd.tɒp/ (BrE)
a car with a fixed rigid roof that resembles a convertible in style but does not have a retractable top

Examples

A hardtop car
His new hardtop has all the elegance of a convertible but none of the maintenance worries.
They restored a classic 1967 Mustang hardtop and took it to the car show.
Unlike the convertible, the hardtop provides better protection during bad weather.
