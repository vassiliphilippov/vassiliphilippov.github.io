fixable

adjective
able to be attached
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪksəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪksəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be fastened, secured, or attached in place.

Examples

Fixable hooks
The hooks are fixable to any flat surface in the kitchen.
Is this panel fixable to the wall without drilling holes?
The new shelves are lightweight and easily fixable with adhesive strips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachable - able to be attached or joined to something
  • antonym: removable - able to be taken off or separated
