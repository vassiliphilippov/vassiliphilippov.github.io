launderer

noun
criminal money handler
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːndərər / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndərə / (BrE)
a person who illegally makes money from crime appear legitimate by disguising its origin

Examples

Money launderer
The police arrested the launderer after a long investigation.
Authorities discovered that the launderer had moved millions of dollars through fake companies.
A skilled launderer can make illegal funds seem like legitimate business income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - a person who breaks the law
  • antonym: law-abider - a person who follows the law
  • synonym: money launderer - a person who processes illegal money to make it appear legal
