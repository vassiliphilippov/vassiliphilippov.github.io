subtotal

noun
the total of part of a series or group
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtoʊtəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌbtəʊtəl / (BrE)
a partial sum representing the total of a subset of items, before any final additions or deductions

Examples

The subtotal amount
After listing all the expenses, she calculated the subtotal before adding the final fees.
Please check that the subtotal matches the sum of the individual items on your bill.
The subtotal for your groceries is $35.67, before tax is added.
