nap

noun
soft/fuzzy surface of fabric
9.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /næp/ (AmE), /næp/ (BrE)
the soft or raised, fuzzy surface on certain fabrics, such as velvet or corduroy

Examples

Velvet nap
Dust can get trapped easily in the nap of the upholstery.
Make sure the nap of the velvet runs in the same direction on all pieces.
The jacket’s nap gives it a luxurious feel.
Always brush the nap of corduroy before sewing for an even appearance.
The rug's nap should be vacuumed in the direction of the fibers to maintain its look.
Velvet has a soft nap that gives it its characteristic sheen.
