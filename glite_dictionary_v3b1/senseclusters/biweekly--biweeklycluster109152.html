biweekly

adverb
twice a week
8.4
Pronunciation: / baɪˈwiːkli / (AmE), / baɪˈwiːkli / (BrE)
Twice a week; occurring or done every two times per week.

Examples

Meet biweekly
He attends physical therapy biweekly to accelerate his recovery.
Our team holds biweekly meetings on Mondays and Thursdays.
The newsletter is sent out biweekly, so you'll get two issues every week.
