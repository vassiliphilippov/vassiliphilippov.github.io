fluent

adjective
moving smoothly and easily
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːənt / (AmE), / ˈfluːənt / (BrE)
moving in a smooth, graceful, and effortless way; not stiff or awkward in movement.

Examples

Fluent movement
She admired the fluent motion of the river as it wound through the valley.
The dancer's fluent movements captivated the audience throughout the performance.
With a fluent sweep of his arm, he completed the intricate painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graceful - having or showing grace or elegance in movement
