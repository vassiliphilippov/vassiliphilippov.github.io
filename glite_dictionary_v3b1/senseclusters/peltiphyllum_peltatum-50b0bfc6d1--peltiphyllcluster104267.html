Peltiphyllum peltatum

noun
perennial herb native to the Sierra Nevada
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛl.tɪˈfɪl.əm pɛlˈteɪ.təm / (AmE), / ˌpɛl.tɪˈfɪl.əm pɛlˈtɛɪ.təm / (BrE)
a rhizomatous perennial herb with large round peltate leaves and clusters of white to bright pink flowers, native to stream banks in the Sierra Nevada of California

Examples
