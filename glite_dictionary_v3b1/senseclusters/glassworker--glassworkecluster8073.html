glassworker

noun
person who works cutting and fitting glass
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːswɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut, shape, or handle flat glass, especially for windows and doors

Examples

Skilled glassworker installs windows
After years as a glassworker, he opened his own glass supply shop.
Skilled glassworkers are essential in restoring old stained glass windows.
The glassworker measured each pane carefully before cutting it to fit the frame.
