numinously

adverb
spiritually mysteriously
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːmɪnəsli / (AmE), / ˈnjuːmɪnəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that suggests a mysterious, spiritual, or divine presence

Examples

To feel numinously
The ancient forest was numinously quiet as the mist rolled in at dawn.
She spoke numinously about her experiences in the remote monastery.
The painting was numinously beautiful, evoking a sense of awe in every viewer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mundanely - in a way that is ordinary or lacking spiritual significance
  • synonym: mysteriously - in a way that is difficult to understand or explain
  • synonym: spiritually - in a way that relates to the spirit or soul
