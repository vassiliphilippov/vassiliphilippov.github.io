officiousness

noun
intrusive or meddlesome helpfulness
7.1 7.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈfɪʃ.əs.nəs/ (AmE), /ə.ˈfɪʃ.əs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being assertively or intrusively helpful, especially when such help is unwanted or unrequested

Examples

His officiousness annoyed
His officiousness at the meeting annoyed several colleagues who hadn't asked for his input.
No one appreciated his officiousness in constantly offering advice where it wasn't needed.
She displayed a kind of officiousness that made people reluctant to work with her.
