marmalade tree

noun
tropical American fruit tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrməˌleɪd ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməleɪd ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tropical American tree with wood similar to mahogany and sweet, edible, egg-shaped fruit, sometimes classified in the genus Calocarpum

Examples

The marmalade tree fruit
The marmalade tree produces sweet fruit that is enjoyed throughout Central America.
The wood from the marmalade tree is valued for its resemblance to mahogany.
We planted a marmalade tree in the botanical garden to attract more visitors.
