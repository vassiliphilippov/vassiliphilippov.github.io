in brief

adverb
concisely; in a few words
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪn briːf / (AmE), / ɪn briːf / (BrE)
Used to introduce a summary or express something in a concise way; in a few words.

Examples

In brief summary
In brief, the project was a huge success despite a few minor setbacks.
In brief, the results confirm our initial hypothesis.
He explained, in brief, how the system works.

Related Terms

  • antonym: at length - in a detailed or thorough way
  • synonym: briefly - using few words; concisely
