aljamiado

adjective
Romance in Arabic script
Pronunciation: / alxaˈmjɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / alxaˈmjɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
written in a Romance language, especially Spanish, using the Arabic script

Examples

Aljamiado script
The ancient text was preserved in aljamiado form by the Moriscos.
Researchers study aljamiado manuscripts to learn about cultural exchanges in medieval Spain.
Some Spanish folk tales survive only in aljamiado versions written centuries ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transliterated - written in the letters of another alphabet
