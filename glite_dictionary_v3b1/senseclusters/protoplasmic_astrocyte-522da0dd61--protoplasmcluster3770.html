protoplasmic astrocyte

noun
type of astrocyte in grey matter
9.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˌplæzmɪk ˈæstroʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˌplæzmɪk ˈastrəʊˌsʌɪt / (BrE)
a type of astrocyte located in the grey matter of the brain and spinal cord, characterized by their numerous short, branched processes
