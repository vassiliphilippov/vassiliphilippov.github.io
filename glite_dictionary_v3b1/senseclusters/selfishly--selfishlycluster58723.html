selfishly

adverb
in a self-centered or egotistical manner
1.1 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.fɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.fɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows concern only for oneself, without regard for the needs or feelings of others.

Examples

Act selfishly
He acted selfishly, thinking only of his own gain.
She selfishly refused to share her notes with the rest of the group.
The children selfishly grabbed the largest pieces of cake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruistically - in a way that shows selfless concern for others
  • synonym: egotistically - in a self-centered or egotistical manner
