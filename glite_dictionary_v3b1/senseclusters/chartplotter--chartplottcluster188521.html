chartplotter

noun
chart-making tool
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrtˌplɑtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtˌplɒtə / (BrE)
a person or computer program that creates graphical charts or graphs to display numerical or statistical data

Examples

Modern chartplotters can quickly turn raw numbers into easy-to-read graphs.
She became skilled at using the chartplotter to present complex statistics clearly.
