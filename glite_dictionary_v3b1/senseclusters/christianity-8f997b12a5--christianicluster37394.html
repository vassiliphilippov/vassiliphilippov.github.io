Christianity

noun
the Christian religion
5.4 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪstʃiˈænəti / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstiˈænəti / (BrE)
The religion based on the teachings of Jesus Christ, originating from the life and teachings presented in the New Testament, and recognizing Jesus as the savior.

Examples

Christianity as a religion
Art and music in the Middle Ages were deeply influenced by Christianity.
Many people in Europe and the Americas practice Christianity as their main religion.
The principles of Christianity are rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ.
