antinational sentiment

noun
opposition to national interests
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈsɛn.tə.mənt / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈsɛn.tɪ.mənt / (BrE)
opposition or hostility toward national interests or patriotism

Examples

Antinational sentiment rises
Any form of antinational sentiment can be controversial, especially in times of conflict.
He was reprimanded after his social media post was deemed to reflect antinational sentiment.
The journalist's article was criticized for expressing antinational sentiment during a sensitive time.
