aguardiente

noun
anise-flavored spirit
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːɡwɑːrˈdjɛnteɪ / (AmE), / ˌaɡwɑːˈdjɛnteɪ / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic spirit, usually distilled from sugarcane or fruit, popular in Spanish-speaking countries (sometimes flavored or homemade)
