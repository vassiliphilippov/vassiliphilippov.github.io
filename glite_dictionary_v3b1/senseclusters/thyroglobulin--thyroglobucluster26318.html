thyroglobulin

noun
thyroid gland protein precursor
Pronunciation: / ˌθaɪroʊˈɡlɑːbjəlɪn / (AmE), / ˌθaɪərəʊˈɡlɒbjʊlɪn / (BrE)
an iodine-containing protein produced by the thyroid gland, serving as a precursor for thyroid hormones
