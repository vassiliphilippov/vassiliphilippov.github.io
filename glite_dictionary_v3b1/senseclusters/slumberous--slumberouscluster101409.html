slumberous

adjective
suggestive of sleep; quiet and tranquil
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌmbɚəs / (AmE), / ˈslʌmbərəs / (BrE)
quiet, peaceful, or making you feel sleepy; suggestive of sleep or drowsiness

Examples

A slumberous haze
A slumberous haze hung over the quiet village as the sun set.
The slumberous music made everyone feel calm and relaxed after dinner.
The library's slumberous atmosphere made it an ideal place to read and reflect.
A slumberous silence fell over the room as night approached.
He gazed at her with slumberous eyes, barely managing to stay awake.
The slumberous afternoon heat made everyone feel lazy and slow.
