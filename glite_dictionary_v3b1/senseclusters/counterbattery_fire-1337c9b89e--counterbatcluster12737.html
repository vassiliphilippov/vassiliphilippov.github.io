counterbattery fire

noun
artillery fire against enemy artillery
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈbætəri ˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈbætəri ˌfaɪə / (BrE)
artillery fire directed at enemy artillery to neutralize or destroy it

Examples

Accurate counterbattery fire
Accurate counterbattery fire can significantly reduce enemy bombardment in a conflict.
The army responded quickly with counterbattery fire to suppress the enemy's artillery positions.
They relied on radar systems to coordinate counterbattery fire during the battle.
