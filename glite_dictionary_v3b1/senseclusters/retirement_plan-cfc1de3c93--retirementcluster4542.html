retirement plan

noun
financial plan for post-retirement income
3.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪərmənt plæn / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt plæn / (BrE)
a financial arrangement in which money is set aside to provide income after a person retires from work

Examples

A retirement plan account
It's important to review your retirement plan every few years to ensure it meets your future needs.
Many companies offer a 401(k) retirement plan as part of their employee benefits package.
She began contributing to her retirement plan as soon as she started working full-time.
