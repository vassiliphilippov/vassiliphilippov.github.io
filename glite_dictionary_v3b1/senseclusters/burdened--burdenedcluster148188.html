burdened

adjective
carrying a heavy weight or load
5.7 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdnd / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdnd / (BrE)
Carrying a heavy weight or load; weighed down physically.

Examples

Burdened with snow
After the snowstorm, the branches were burdened with thick layers of ice.
She walked slowly, burdened by the heavy backpack on her shoulders.
The donkey was burdened with sacks of grain for the long journey.
