annual community picnic

noun
yearly community gathering and meal
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ju.əl kəˈmju.nə.ti ˈpɪk.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.əl kəˈmjuː.nə.ti ˈpɪk.nɪk / (BrE)
a gathering organized annually for members of a community to socialize, often featuring food and recreational activities

Examples

The annual community picnic
Our town's annual community picnic includes games, music, and a barbecue for everyone.
The whole neighborhood looks forward to the annual community picnic every summer.
Volunteers gathered early in the morning to set up for the annual community picnic in the park.
