First World War

noun
global war from 1914 to 1918
2.8
Pronunciation: /fɜːrst wɜːrld wɔːr/ /; / /fɜːrst wɜːrld wɑːr/ (AmE), /fɜːst wɜːld wɔː/ (BrE)
