blacksmithing

noun
metalworking craft
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈblæksmɪθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblæksmɪθɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or occupation of shaping, making, or repairing things from metal by heating and hammering it by hand

Examples

Blacksmithing craft
He became interested in blacksmithing after watching a demonstration at the local fair.
Traditional blacksmithing requires skill, patience, and a strong understanding of metals.
Many people take up blacksmithing as a hobby to create custom tools and art.
He learned blacksmithing from his grandfather, who was a skilled craftsman in the village.
Many traditional tools are still made using blacksmithing techniques passed down through generations.
The museum offers blacksmithing demonstrations to show how horseshoes and tools were made in the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forging - shaping metal by heating and hammering
  • synonym: metalworking - the process of working with metals to create objects
  • synonym: smithing - the craft of shaping metal by forging or hammering
