usherette

noun
female usher in a theatre or cinema
9.3 5.8
Pronunciation: /ʌ.ʃə.ˈret/ (AmE), /ʌ.ʃə.ˈret/ (BrE)
a woman whose job is to show people to their seats in a theatre, cinema, or similar venue

Examples

A usherette at theatre
In classic cinemas, a uniformed usherette would often sell ice cream during the intermission.
The usherette greeted guests at the entrance, handing out programs for the show.
When the lights dimmed, an usherette guided us to our seats with her flashlight.
