July 4

noun
U.S. Independence Day (July 4 holiday)
Pronunciation: / dʒuˈlaɪ ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / dʒuˈlaɪ ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
The national holiday in the United States commemorating the adoption of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776; known as Independence Day.

Examples

July 4 holiday
Many businesses close on July 4 in observance of the holiday.
On July 4, Americans celebrate with fireworks and parades across the country.
