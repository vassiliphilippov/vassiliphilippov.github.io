budding

adjective
beginning to develop
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌdɪŋ / (BrE)
beginning to develop or show signs of future success in a particular role or activity

Examples

Budding artist
He's a budding scientist with a keen interest in genetics.
The young actress is a budding star in the film industry.
Their company is still budding, but it shows great promise for the future.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emerging - starting to become apparent or prominent
  • antonym: established - having been in existence for a long time and therefore recognized and generally accepted
  • synonym: nascent - just coming into existence and beginning to display signs of future potential
