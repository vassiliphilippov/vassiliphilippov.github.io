cordless vacuum

noun
vacuum cleaner without a cord
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdləs vəˈkjum / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdləs vəˈkjuːm / (BrE)
A vacuum cleaner that operates on battery power and does not require a cord for electricity.

Examples

A cordless vacuum cleaner
After switching to a cordless vacuum, he was able to clean without worrying about outlets.
I love my cordless vacuum because it makes cleaning the stairs so much easier.
The new cordless vacuum has a battery life of nearly an hour.
