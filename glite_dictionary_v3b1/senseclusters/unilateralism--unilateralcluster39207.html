unilateralism

noun
policy of independent national action
9.8 9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈlætərəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈlatərəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the policy or practice of acting independently, especially in international relations, without consulting or seeking support from other nations

Examples

Unilateralism in policy
Her book explores the effects of unilateralism on global cooperation.
Some leaders argue that unilateralism allows for faster policy decisions during crises.
The government's move toward unilateralism has been criticized by its allies.

Related Terms
