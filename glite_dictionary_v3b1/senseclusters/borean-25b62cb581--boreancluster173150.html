Borean

noun
northerner (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːriən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːriən / (BrE)
a person from northern regions, especially in archaic or literary contexts

Examples

A Borean traveler
The ancient legend tells of a brave Borean who ventured beyond the icy mountains.
In old poems, the Borean is often depicted as hardy and resilient.
Few had ever seen a Borean so far south before the great migration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: northerner - a person from the north
  • antonym: southerner - a person from the south
