owlclaws

noun
western U.S. herb; toxic to sheep
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊlˌklɔz / (AmE), / ˈaʊlˌklɔːz / (BrE)
A stout perennial herb native to the western United States, with flower heads that have drooping orange-yellow rays; known to cause spewing sickness in sheep.
