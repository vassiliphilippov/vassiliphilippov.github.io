what

determiner
exclamatory determiner
1.0
Pronunciation: / wʌt / (AmE), / wɒt / (BrE)
used before a noun phrase in exclamations to express surprise, admiration, or strong feeling

Examples

What a surprise
What a beautiful day it is after the long winter!
I can't believe what luck we had finding this place.
What an incredible performance that was by the young pianist!

Related Terms

  • synonym: such - used to emphasize the quality or degree of something
