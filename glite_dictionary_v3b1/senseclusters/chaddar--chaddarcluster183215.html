chaddar

noun
traditional head covering cloth
6.0 9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑː.dər/ /; / /ˈtʃæd.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɑː.də/ /; / /ˈtʃæd.ə/ (BrE)
a large piece of cloth worn as a head covering, veil, or shawl by Muslim and Hindu women, especially in South Asia
