chaddar

noun
traditional head covering cloth
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑː.dər / (AmE), / ˈtʃæd.ə / (BrE)
a large piece of cloth worn as a head covering, veil, or shawl by Muslim and Hindu women, especially in South Asia

Examples

A chaddar shawl
Many women in the village use a chaddar both as a veil and a shawl.
She draped a chaddar over her head before leaving the house.
The colorful chaddar she wore was passed down from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hijab - a veil worn by some Muslim women covering the hair, neck, and sometimes the face
  • synonym: shawl - a piece of fabric worn over the shoulders or head
