hobo camp

noun
gathering place for hoboes
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊboʊ kæmp / (AmE), / ˈhəʊbəʊ kæmp / (BrE)
a place where hoboes gather and camp, often a temporary or makeshift settlement

Examples

A hobo camp site
Authorities cleared out the hobo camp after neighbors complained about safety concerns.
During the Great Depression, many people found shelter in a hobo camp on the outskirts of the city.
The novel described life in a hobo camp with vivid, gritty detail.

  • synonym: shantytown - an improvised settlement of temporary dwellings
