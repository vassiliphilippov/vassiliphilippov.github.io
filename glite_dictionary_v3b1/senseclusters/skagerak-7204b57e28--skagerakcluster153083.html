Skagerak

proper noun
strait between Denmark and Norway
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːɡəˌrɑːk / (AmE), / ˈskɑːɡəræk / (BrE)
a broad strait of the North Sea lying between the Jutland peninsula of Denmark and the coast of southern Norway.

Examples

The Skagerak strait
Oil tankers regularly pass through the Skagerak between Norway and Denmark.
The Skagerak is known for its unpredictable weather and strong currents.
The fishing fleet sailed through the Skagerak on its way to the North Sea.
