hay bale

noun
a tightly packed bundle of hay
4.9
Pronunciation: / heɪ beɪl / (AmE), / heɪ beɪl / (BrE)
A large bundle of hay that has been tightly packed and shaped, usually for storage or transport.

Examples

A hay bale bundle
A single hay bale can feed several animals during the winter months.
Children played hide-and-seek among the hay bales stacked in the barn.
The farmer loaded each hay bale onto the trailer by hand.
