cageful

noun
amount filling a cage
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪdʒfʊl / (AmE), / ˈkeɪdʒfʊl / (BrE)
the quantity or number of things or animals that can fill a cage

Examples

A cageful of birds
The pet store received a cageful of colorful parrots this morning.
He carried a cageful of mice to the laboratory for the experiment.
After the rescue, volunteers released a cageful of injured birds back into the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxful - the amount that fills a box
  • synonym: handful - the amount that can be held in one hand
