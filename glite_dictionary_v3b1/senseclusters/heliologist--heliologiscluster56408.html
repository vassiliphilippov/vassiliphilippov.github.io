heliologist

noun
sun worship scholar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌhiːliˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scholar who studies the sun's role in religion, mythology, or ancient cultures, especially sun worship

Examples

Ancient heliologist studies
The heliologist presented new findings about sun rituals in ancient Egypt at the conference.
As a heliologist, she spent years studying the symbolism of the sun in early civilizations.
Many temples were identified by the heliologist as being aligned with the sunrise on solstices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archaeologist - a person who studies ancient cultures through artifacts
  • synonym: mythologist - a scholar who studies myths and mythology
