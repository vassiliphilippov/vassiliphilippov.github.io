council member

noun
member of a council
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl ˌmɛm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl ˌmɛm.bə / (BrE)
A person who is an elected or appointed member of a council, especially a governing or advisory group.

Examples

A council member votes
As a newly elected council member, she is responsible for representing her district.
Every council member was present at last night’s meeting.
The council member proposed a new initiative to improve local parks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: councillor - a person who is a member of a council
