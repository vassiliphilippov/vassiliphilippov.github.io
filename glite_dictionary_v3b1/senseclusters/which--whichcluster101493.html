which

pronoun
question about choice
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ / (AmE), / wɪtʃ / (BrE)
used to ask or refer to one or more items from a known group or set

Examples

Which color is best
Which of these books did you read last summer?
She could not decide which dress to wear to the party.
Please tell me which route is the fastest to the airport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: what - used to ask for information about something
