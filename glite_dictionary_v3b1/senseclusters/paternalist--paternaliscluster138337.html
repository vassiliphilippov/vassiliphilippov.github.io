paternalist

noun
controlling authority figure
8.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɝːnəlɪst / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who manages or controls others in a way that is protective but limits their freedom, like a parent would

Examples

A strict paternalist
The new policy was designed by a paternalist who believed employees needed strict guidance.
Critics argued that the mayor acted as a paternalist by making decisions without public input.
Some people appreciate a paternalist leader, while others find the approach too controlling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian - a person who enforces strict obedience to authority
  • antonym: libertarian - a person who believes in individual freedom and minimal government control
