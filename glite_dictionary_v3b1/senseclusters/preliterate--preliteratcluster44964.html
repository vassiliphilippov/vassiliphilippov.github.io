preliterate

adjective
not yet able to read and write
Pronunciation: / priˈlɪt̬ərət / (AmE), / priˈlɪtərət / (BrE)
not having learned to read and write; occurring or existing before the development of written language

Examples

Preliterate children
Children in the preliterate stage often enjoy listening to stories before they can read them themselves.
Many anthropologists study preliterate societies to understand human cultural development.
The myths were passed down orally in a preliterate community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illiterate - unable to read or write
  • antonym: literate - able to read and write
