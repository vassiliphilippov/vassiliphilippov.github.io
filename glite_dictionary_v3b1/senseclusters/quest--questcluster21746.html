quest

verb
(of dogs) search for a scent in hunting
Pronunciation: / kwɛst / (AmE), / kwɛst / (BrE)
(of a dog, especially in hunting) to search for the scent or track of game.

Examples

A dog quests scent
During the hunt, the beagle quested along the hedge for the scent of rabbits.
The hunting dog was trained to quest for pheasants in the field.
Their spaniel eagerly quests through the underbrush whenever they visit the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: search - to look for something carefully and thoroughly
  • synonym: track - to follow the trail or scent of something
