Amia

noun
genus of freshwater fish (bowfin)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.mi.ə / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater fish, including the bowfin, that is the only living genus of the family Amiidae.
