key of the street

noun
knowledge essential for street life
Pronunciation: / ki əv ðə strit / (AmE), / kiː əv ðə striːt / (BrE)
vital knowledge or practical understanding needed to navigate street culture or urban life successfully

Examples

The key of the street
Many believe that surviving in tough neighborhoods requires more than luck—it takes the key of the street.
She credits her success to the key of the street she learned growing up in the city.
Without the key of the street, he found it difficult to fit in with his peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: streetwise - having the experience and knowledge necessary to deal with the potential difficulties or dangers of life in an urban environment
