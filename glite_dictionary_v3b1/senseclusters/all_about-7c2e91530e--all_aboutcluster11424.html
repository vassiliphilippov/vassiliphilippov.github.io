all about

adjective
entirely focused on or concerned with
Pronunciation: / ɑl əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ɔːl əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
completely focused on or concerned with; having something as its main subject or purpose.

Examples

All about family
For her, the holiday season is all about family traditions and togetherness.
His speech was all about the importance of education for future generations.
The new initiative is all about improving customer satisfaction across the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centered on - focused on something as the main subject
  • synonym: concerned with - relating to or focused on a particular subject
