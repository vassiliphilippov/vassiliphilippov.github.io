California nutmeg

noun
evergreen tree of California, Torreya californica
Pronunciation: / kælɪˈfɔrniə ˈnʌtmɛɡ / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːniə ˈnʌtmɛɡ / (BrE)
an evergreen tree (Torreya californica) native to California, with a fruit resembling a nutmeg but possessing a strong turpentine flavor

Examples

The California nutmeg tree
Gardeners admire the elegant foliage of the California nutmeg for landscaping.
The California nutmeg grows in the mountain forests of northern California.
Though its seeds resemble nutmeg, the California nutmeg has a strong turpentine taste.
