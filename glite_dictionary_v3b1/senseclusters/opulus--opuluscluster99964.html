opulus

noun
flowering shrub genus
Pronunciation: / ˈɑpjələs / (AmE), / ˈɒpjʊləs / (BrE)
a genus of flowering shrubs, including the guelder rose, known for their clusters of white flowers and red berries

Examples

The opulus shrub
The opulus in the park bloomed with clusters of delicate white flowers in early summer.
Gardeners often plant opulus for its attractive red berries and ornamental value.
Birds are drawn to the bright berries produced by the opulus each autumn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guelder rose - a species of opulus with white flowers and red berries
  • synonym: viburnum - a genus of shrubs or small trees including opulus
