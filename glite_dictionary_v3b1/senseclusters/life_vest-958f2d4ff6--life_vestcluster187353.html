life vest

noun
buoyant jacket for water safety
1.9
Pronunciation: / laɪf vɛst / (AmE), / laɪf vest / (BrE)
a sleeveless jacket filled with buoyant material or inflatable sections, worn to help keep a person afloat in water

Examples

Wear a life vest
Before boarding the boat, everyone was required to put on a life vest.
Children must wear a life vest when swimming in the lake.
The airline demonstrated how to use the life vest under your seat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: life jacket - a sleeveless, buoyant jacket for keeping someone afloat in water
  • synonym: life preserver - a device designed to keep a person from drowning
