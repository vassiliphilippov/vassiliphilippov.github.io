catfish

verb
deceive with a fake online identity (esp. romantically)
6.1 8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkat.fɪʃ /; / ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
to deceive someone by pretending to be another person online, usually by using a fake identity
