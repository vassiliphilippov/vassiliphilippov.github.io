sawhorse

noun
framework supporting wood for sawing
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌhɔːs / (BrE)
A frame, usually with four legs, used to support wood for sawing.

Examples

A pair of sawhorses
A pair of sawhorses supported the workbench in his garage.
He placed the wooden board on the sawhorse before cutting it.
Make sure the sawhorse is sturdy before you start sawing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trestle - A framework similar to a sawhorse, often used for supporting planks or boards
