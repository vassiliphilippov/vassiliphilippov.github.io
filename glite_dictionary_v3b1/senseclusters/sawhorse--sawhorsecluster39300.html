sawhorse

noun
framework supporting wood for sawing
5.1 0.47/m

A frame, usually with four legs, used to support wood for sawing.

Examples

A pair of sawhorses

He placed the wooden board on the sawhorse before cutting it.
Make sure the sawhorse is sturdy before you start sawing.
