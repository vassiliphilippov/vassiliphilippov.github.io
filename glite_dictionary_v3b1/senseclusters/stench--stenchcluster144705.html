stench

noun
a strong unpleasant smell
Pronunciation: / stɛntʃ / (AmE), / stɛntʃ / (BrE)
a very unpleasant and strong smell

Examples

A strong stench
An awful stench was coming from the garbage bins behind the restaurant.
She covered her nose, unable to bear the stench in the old abandoned house.
The stench of burning plastic filled the air after the fire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aroma - a pleasant smell, usually from food or drink
  • synonym: odor - a particular smell, especially an unpleasant one
  • synonym: smell - the sense of perceiving odors
