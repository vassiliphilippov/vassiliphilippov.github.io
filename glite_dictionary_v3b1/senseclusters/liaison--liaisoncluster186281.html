liaison

noun
person or means of communication between groups
7.3 5.5
Pronunciation: /liː.ˈeɪ.zɑːn/ /; / /liː.ˈeɪ.zən/ (AmE), /liː.ˈeɪ.zɒn/ /; / /liː.ˈeɪ.zən/ (BrE)

Examples
