qualitative analysis

noun
analytical chemistry method for identifying components
4.8 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑː.lə.teɪ.tɪv ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈkwɑː.lə.tɪv ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒl.ɪ.tə.tɪv ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈkwɒl.ɪ.tɪv ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)

Examples

Qualitative analysis of substances
Chemists often begin with qualitative analysis before proceeding to more precise measurements.
