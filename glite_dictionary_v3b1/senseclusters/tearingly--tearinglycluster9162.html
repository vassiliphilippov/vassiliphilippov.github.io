tearingly

adverb
with strong emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtɪə.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that produces or expresses strong emotion, especially causing tears or moving someone to tears

Examples

Tearingly sad speech
The speech was delivered so tearingly that many listeners wiped their eyes.
She sang the song tearingly, leaving the audience deeply moved.
His letter was written tearingly, revealing the depth of his feelings.
