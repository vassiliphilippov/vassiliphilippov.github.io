unbaked

adjective
not baked; raw
1.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbeɪkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbeɪkt / (BrE)
not cooked by baking; still raw or not yet placed in the oven

Examples

Unbaked dough
The unbaked cookies should be chilled before going into the oven.
She accidentally served the pie with an unbaked crust.
Leave the unbaked bread dough to rise for another hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baked - cooked by dry heat in an oven
  • synonym: raw - not cooked or processed
