constructive-metabolic

adjective
of or relating to anabolism
2.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌktɪv ˌmɛtəˈbɑlɪk / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌktɪv ˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to anabolism, the metabolic process involving the building up of complex molecules in living organisms.

Examples

constructive-metabolic process
A constructive-metabolic reaction synthesizes proteins from amino acids within cells.
During growth spurts, the body increases its constructive-metabolic activity to build muscle and bone.
The hormone insulin plays a key role in regulating constructive-metabolic processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anabolic - relating to or promoting anabolism
  • antonym: catabolic - relating to catabolism, breaking down molecules
