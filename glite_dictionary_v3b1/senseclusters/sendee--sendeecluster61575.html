sendee

noun
intended recipient of a message
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈdi / (AmE), / sɛnˈdi / (BrE)
the person who is intended to receive a message or communication

Examples

The sendee of message
Each sendee should acknowledge receipt of the documents within 24 hours.
Please make sure the sendee clearly understands the instructions before sending the email.
The letter was returned because the sendee's address was incorrect.
