Russophile

adjective
admiring Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈrʌsəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
showing support for or admiration of Russia, its people, or its culture

Examples

Russophile views
His Russophile attitude was evident in his collection of Russian art and literature.
The diplomat's Russophile stance shaped the country's foreign policy decisions.
Many Russophile organizations promote cultural exchange between Russia and other nations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Anglophile - showing admiration for England or English culture
  • antonym: Russophobic - having a strong dislike or fear of Russia or Russians
