photoresist

verb
to apply photoresist
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəʊrɪˈzɪst / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊrɪˈzɪst / (BrE)
to coat or treat a surface with a photoresist material for use in photolithography or patterning processes

Examples

To photoresist a wafer
Technicians photoresist the glass plates before exposing them to ultraviolet light.
You must carefully photoresist the wafer to ensure a uniform coating for the next step.
After cleaning, the team will photoresist each substrate in preparation for the circuit patterning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coat - to cover a surface with a layer of substance
  • antonym: etch - to cut or corrode a material using chemicals
