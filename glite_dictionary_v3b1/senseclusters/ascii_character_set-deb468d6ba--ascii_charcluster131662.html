ASCII character set

noun
characters defined by the ASCII standard
9.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæs.kiː ˈkær.ək.tər sɛt/ (AmE), /ˈæs.kiː ˈkær.ək.tə sɛt/ (BrE)
