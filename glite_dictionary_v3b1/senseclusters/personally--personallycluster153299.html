personally

adverb
in a personal manner
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənəli /; / ˈpɝːsənəli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəli / (BrE)
in a personal way; being directly involved or present oneself

Examples

Personally check everything

Please do not take my remarks personally; I was talking in general terms.

I am grateful that you personally attended the meeting to address our concerns.
She decided to deliver the package personally instead of asking her assistant.
The CEO personally welcomed each new employee on their first day.
