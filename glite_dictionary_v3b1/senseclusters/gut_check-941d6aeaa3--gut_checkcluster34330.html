gut check

noun
instinctive self-assessment
4.0 7.0
Pronunciation: /gʌt tʃek/ (AmE), /gʌt tʃek/ (BrE)
an instinctive evaluation or assessment based on one's feelings rather than analysis or evidence

Examples

A gut check moment
Before signing the contract, she did a gut check to see if it felt right.
His gut check told him to trust the new partner despite initial doubts.
Sometimes a quick gut check is more valuable than hours of analysis.
