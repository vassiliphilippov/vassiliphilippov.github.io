imbiber

noun
person who drinks alcohol (often heavily)
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbaɪbɚ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbaɪbə / (BrE)
a person who drinks alcoholic beverages, especially in large amounts

Examples

The festival attracts every kind of imbiber, from wine enthusiasts to beer lovers.
