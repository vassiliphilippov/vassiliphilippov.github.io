Jews' harp

noun
small plucked musical instrument held in the mouth
5.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /dʒuːz hɑːrp/ (AmE), /dʒuːz hɑːp/ (BrE)
A small lyre-shaped musical instrument played by placing it between the teeth and plucking a flexible metal tongue while changing the shape of the mouth cavity.

Examples
