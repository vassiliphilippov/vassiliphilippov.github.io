bioengineer

verb
to modify organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
to design, create, or modify organisms or biological systems using engineering and technology

Examples

To bioengineer new cells
Scientists can bioengineer bacteria to produce useful medicines.
The team hopes to bioengineer crops that can survive extreme weather conditions.
Researchers bioengineered a new type of yeast for more efficient biofuel production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - to design or build something using scientific principles
  • synonym: genetically modify - to change the genetic structure of an organism
