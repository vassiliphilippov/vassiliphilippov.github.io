pressman

noun
person who operates a printing press
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsmən / (AmE), / ˈprɛsmən / (BrE)
a man whose occupation is to operate a printing press or is engaged in printing

Examples

Skilled pressman at work
As a skilled pressman, he could diagnose and fix any problem with the machinery.
The pressman carefully watched the paper as it rolled off the press.
The printing company hired an experienced pressman to oversee the production of the new newspaper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printer - person whose job is printing
