microscopic lens

noun
lens for magnifying small objects
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.krəˈskɑ.pɪk lɛnz / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəˈskɒ.pɪk lɛnz / (BrE)
a lens used for magnifying extremely small objects, typically as part of a microscope

Examples

Microscopic lens for viewing
Dust particles invisible to the naked eye became distinct under the microscopic lens.
Repairing a microscopic lens requires precise handling due to its delicate structure.
The scientist adjusted the microscopic lens to get a clearer view of the cell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnifying glass - a simple lens used to produce a magnified image of an object
  • synonym: objective lens - the main magnifying lens in a microscope
