nightmare

noun
frightening dream
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt.mɛr / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.mɛə / (BrE)
A very frightening or unpleasant dream.

Examples

A bad nightmare
After the accident, he began suffering from frequent nightmares.
She woke up suddenly from a nightmare, her heart pounding.
The little boy was afraid to sleep because he kept having the same nightmare.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dream - a series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring in a person's mind during sleep
  • degree higher: night terror - a sleep disorder causing feelings of terror or dread during sleep
