mussitate

verb
speak indistinctly or mumble
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌsɪteɪt / (BrE)
to speak indistinctly or mumble, usually in a low voice

Examples

To mussitate quietly
During the lecture, several students began to mussitate among themselves.
She mussitated a response no one could quite hear.
The old man would mussitate to himself as he strolled through the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enunciate - to pronounce words or parts of words clearly
  • synonym: mumble - to speak quietly and not clearly enough for someone to understand you
