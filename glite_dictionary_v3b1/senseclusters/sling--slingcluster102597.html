sling

noun
single-strap crossbody bag
3.0
Pronunciation: /slɪŋ/ (AmE), /slɪŋ/ (BrE)
a small bag or satchel with a single strap, worn across the body or over one shoulder to carry personal items.

Examples

A sling bag
She kept her phone and wallet in a small sling while walking through the crowded market.
He prefers a lightweight sling for travel so he can reach passports quickly at security.
At the festival, she adjusted her sling to keep it secure while dancing with friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbody bag - a bag worn with a strap across the body so it rests on the opposite hip
  • synonym: shoulder bag - a bag carried over one shoulder using a strap
