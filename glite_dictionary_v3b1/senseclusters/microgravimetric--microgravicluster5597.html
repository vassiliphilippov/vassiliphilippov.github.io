microgravimetric

adjective
measuring tiny gravity changes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌɡrævəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌɡrævɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of very small changes or differences in gravitational fields, especially for scientific or geophysical purposes
