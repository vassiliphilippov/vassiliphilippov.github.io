Arno River

proper noun
river in central Italy
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrnoʊ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈɑːnəʊ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in central Italy that flows from the Apennines through Florence and Pisa to the Ligurian Sea.

Examples

The Arno River in Italy
