seed

verb
arrange competitors in a tournament by rank
Pronunciation: / sid / (AmE), / siːd / (BrE)
to arrange the ranking and placement of players or teams in a sports tournament so that the strongest competitors do not meet in early rounds

Examples

To seed players
He was seeded third, so he avoided facing the top player early on.
The tournament organizers seed teams to ensure a fair competition.
They seeding the players based on their performance throughout the season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rank - to assign a position within a grading or ordering system
