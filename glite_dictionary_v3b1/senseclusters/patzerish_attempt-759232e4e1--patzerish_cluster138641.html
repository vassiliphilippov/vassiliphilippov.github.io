patzerish attempt

noun
inept or clumsy effort (esp. in chess)
Pronunciation: / ˈpætsərɪʃ əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / ˈpætsərɪʃ əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
an attempt or effort that is clumsy, inept, or amateurish, especially in chess or similar games

Examples

A patzerish attempt at chess
Despite his enthusiasm, every patzerish attempt at chess ended in a quick defeat.
Her patzerish attempt at solving the puzzle only made things worse.
The coach shook his head at the team's patzerish attempt to execute the new play.
