zombify creativity

verb
dull or deaden creativity
6.2 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈzɑːm.bɪ.faɪ kriː.eɪ.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈzɒm.bɪ.faɪ kriː.eɪ.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
to make someone’s creativity dull, lifeless, or less effective, as if turning it into that of a zombie

Examples

To zombify creativity with stress
Don't let stress and fatigue zombify creativity in your artistic pursuits.
Long meetings late in the day can zombify creativity and leave teams uninspired.
Social media overuse may zombify creativity by encouraging mindless scrolling instead of original thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stifle creativity - to suppress or inhibit creativity
