communitarian

adjective
community-focused
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
emphasizing or relating to the importance of the community over the individual, often with shared responsibilities or resources

Examples

Communitarian values
The new policy reflects a communitarian philosophy that values social responsibility.
Many believe that a communitarian approach can strengthen local neighborhoods.
Her communitarian values influenced her decision to volunteer at the community center.
The new policy encourages communitarian values by promoting shared spaces and group activities.
A communitarian approach to problem-solving often leads to stronger neighborhood bonds.
Many intentional communities are founded on communitarian principles of cooperation and mutual support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - involving all members of a group as a whole
  • synonym: collectivist - favoring the group over the individual
  • antonym: individualistic - emphasizing individual freedom and self-reliance
