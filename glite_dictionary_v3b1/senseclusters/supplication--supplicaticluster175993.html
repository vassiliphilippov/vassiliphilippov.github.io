supplication

noun
humble or earnest request
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌplɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌsʌplɪˈkeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an earnest or humble request or prayer, especially asking for help from someone in authority or from God
