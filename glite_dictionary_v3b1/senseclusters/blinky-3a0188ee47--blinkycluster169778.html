Blinky

proper noun

Examples

Blinky red ghost
In the original Pac-Man game, Blinky relentlessly chased the player through narrow corridors of the maze.
Many players consider Blinky the most aggressive ghost in the Pac-Man series.
When Pac-Man eats a power pellet, Blinky temporarily turns blue and flees.
