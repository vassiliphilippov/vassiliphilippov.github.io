flat

noun
theatre scenery panel
2.5
Pronunciation: / flæt / (AmE), / flæt / (BrE)
a piece of stage scenery made from a wooden frame covered with painted canvas, used as part of a set to represent walls or backgrounds

Examples

Stage flat
Each flat was painted to look like an ornate library wall for the play.
She designed a new flat to create a forest backdrop for the production.
The crew quickly set up the flat to resemble a city street.
