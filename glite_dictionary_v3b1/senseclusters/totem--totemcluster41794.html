totem

noun
symbolic animal, plant, or object for a clan
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.təm / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təm / (BrE)
An object, animal, or plant regarded as the symbol of a family, clan, or group, especially among some Indigenous peoples.

Examples

Animal totem
Each tribe selected a unique animal as its totem.
The eagle is the totem of many Native American clans.
The wolf served as a powerful totem for the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emblem - a symbolic object used as a distinctive badge
