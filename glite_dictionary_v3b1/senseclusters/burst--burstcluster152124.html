burst

noun
rapid simultaneous discharge of guns
2.9
Pronunciation: / bɝst / (AmE), / bɜːst / (BrE)
a quick and simultaneous discharge of firearms

Examples

A burst of gunfire
A sudden burst of gunfire echoed through the valley.
The soldiers fired a burst from their rifles as the enemy advanced.
There was a loud burst as the weapons discharged almost in unison.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusillade - a series of shots fired or missiles thrown all at the same time or in quick succession
