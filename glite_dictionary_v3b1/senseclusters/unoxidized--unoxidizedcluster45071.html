unoxidized

adjective
not oxidized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑksɪˌdaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɒksɪˌdaɪzd / (BrE)
not combined with oxygen or not having undergone the process of oxidation

Examples

Unoxidized metal
The laboratory stored the unoxidized metal in a sealed container to prevent rust.
You can see the difference between the unoxidized and oxidized samples by their color.
The unoxidized copper wire conducts electricity more efficiently than the corroded one.
