burthen

verb
to weigh down with a load
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrðən / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθən / (BrE)
(archaic) To weigh down with a load; to burden or oppress.

Examples

To burthen the mind
She felt burthened by the constant worries of her family.
The old horse was burthened with heavy sacks of grain along the narrow road.
The ship was burthening under the weight of its precious cargo as it sailed into the harbor.
