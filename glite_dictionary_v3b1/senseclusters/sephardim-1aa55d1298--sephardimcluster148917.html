Sephardim

noun
Jews of Spanish descent
Pronunciation: / səˈfɑrdɪm / (AmE), / səˈfɑːdɪm / (BrE)
Jews whose ancestors came from Spain and Portugal (the Iberian Peninsula), especially those with distinct religious and cultural traditions
