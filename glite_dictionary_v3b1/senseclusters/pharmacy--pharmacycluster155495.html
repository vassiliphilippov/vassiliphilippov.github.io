pharmacy

noun
shop selling medicinal drugs
2.3 6.47/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…ërm…ôsi / (AmE), / Ààf…ëÀêm…ôsi / (BrE)
A shop or store where medicinal drugs are prepared or sold, and often other health-related products are available.

Examples

A pharmacy store
I went to the pharmacy to pick up my prescription.
Most pharmacies also sell toiletries and personal care items.
There's a 24-hour pharmacy near the hospital if you need medicine late at night.
