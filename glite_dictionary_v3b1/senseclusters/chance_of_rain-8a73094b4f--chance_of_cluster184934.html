chance of rain

noun
likelihood of rain in a forecast
Pronunciation: / tʃæns əv reɪn / (AmE), / tʃɑːns əv reɪn / (BrE)
the probability or likelihood that it will rain within a specific period or area

Examples

High chance of rain
According to the forecast, there's a high chance of rain in the afternoon.
If the chance of rain increases, I’ll bring an umbrella to work.
The chance of rain for tomorrow is only twenty percent, so we should be fine for our picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probability - the extent to which something is likely to happen
