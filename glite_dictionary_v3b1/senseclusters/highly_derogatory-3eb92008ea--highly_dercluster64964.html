highly derogatory

adjective
extremely insulting or offensive
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli dɪˈrɑɡəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli dɪˈrɒɡətəri / (BrE)
(of a word or remark) extremely insulting or expressing strong disapproval; likely to offend deeply.

Examples

A highly derogatory remark
Certain slurs are considered highly derogatory and should never be used in polite conversation.
Labels like that are not just disrespectful but highly derogatory and harmful.
The article was criticized for its highly derogatory language toward minority groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complimentary - expressing praise or approval
  • degree lower: derogatory - showing a critical or disrespectful attitude
  • synonym: offensive - causing someone to feel deeply hurt, upset, or angry
