Yucca gloriosa

noun
a yucca species of the southeastern US
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌkə ɡloʊˈriːoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈjʌkə ɡlɒˈriːəsə / (BrE)
A species of yucca native to the southeastern United States, similar to the Spanish bayonets but with a shorter trunk and smoother leaves.
