sansevieria

noun
tropical houseplant with sword-shaped leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsæn.səˈvɪr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˌsænsɪˈvɪəriə / (BrE)
A tropical plant with fleshy, sword-shaped, often mottled leaves, commonly grown as a houseplant or for its fiber.

Examples

A sansevieria plant
A sansevieria can thrive indoors with minimal care, making it popular in offices and homes.
Some varieties of sansevieria are valued for their strong fibers, used in making ropes.
The thick leaves of the sansevieria help it retain water during dry periods.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fiber plant - plant grown for its fiber, a broader category
  • synonym: snake plant - common name for sansevieria, especially Sansevieria trifasciata
