Kirgiz

noun
a member of a Central Asian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / kɪrˈɡiz / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡiːz / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic ethnic group traditionally inhabiting Kyrgyzstan and neighboring regions of Central Asia and Siberia

Examples

A Kirgiz family
A group of Kirgiz migrated across the mountains in the 19th century.
Many Kirgiz still practice traditional nomadic herding in the highlands.
The Kirgiz are known for their distinctive equestrian games and yurt-dwelling culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kyrgyz - a member of the same Turkic ethnic group
