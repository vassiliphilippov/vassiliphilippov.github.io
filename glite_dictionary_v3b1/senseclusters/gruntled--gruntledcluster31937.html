gruntled

verb
to complain
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌntəld / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌntəld / (BrE)
to grumble or complain in a dissatisfied manner

Examples

Workers gruntled loudly
The workers gruntled about the long hours and lack of breaks.
He gruntled softly as he read the disappointing news in the letter.
They gruntled all evening about the new parking rules at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: grumble - to complain quietly or in a low voice
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
