seigneury

noun
authority or domain of a feudal lord
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪn.jə.ri / (AmE), / ˈseɪn.jʊəri /; / ˈseɪn.jəri / (BrE)
the land, estate, or authority of a feudal lord, especially in France or French Canada

Examples

The history of the seigneury is central to understanding the region's colonial past.
The land was organized into a seigneury controlled by a powerful French noble.
