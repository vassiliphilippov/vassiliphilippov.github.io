talk out of turn

verb
to speak inappropriately or unwisely
4.2 5.3
Pronunciation: /tɔːk aʊt əv tɜːrn/ /; / /tɑːk aʊt əv tɜːrn/ /; / /tɔːk aʊt ə tɜːrn/ (AmE), /tɔːk aʊt əv tɜːn/ /; / /tɔːk aʊt ə tɜːn/ (BrE)
