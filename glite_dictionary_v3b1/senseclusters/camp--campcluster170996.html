camp

adjective
exaggerated, theatrical style (humorous/satirical)
Pronunciation: / kæmp / (AmE), / kæmp / (BrE)
deliberately exaggerated, artificial, or ostentatiously theatrical in style, especially for humorous or satirical effect.

Examples

Camp fashion
Her camp performance made the entire audience laugh at the intentionally over-the-top gestures.
Many fans love the camp humor in classic drag shows.
The film's camp aesthetic turned its low-budget sets into a source of amusement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campy - deliberately exaggerated and theatrical, especially for comic effect
  • synonym: kitschy - art or objects considered to be in poor taste because of excessive garishness or sentimentality, but sometimes appreciated in an ironic or knowing way
  • antonym: tasteful - showing good aesthetic judgment or refinement
