light at the end of the tunnel

noun
sign that a situation will improve
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪt ət ði ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈtʌnəl / (AmE), / laɪt ət ði ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈtʌnəl / (BrE)
a sign or indication that a difficult or unpleasant situation will soon end or improve

Examples

A light at the end of the tunnel
After many unsuccessful job applications, he finally saw a light at the end of the tunnel when he was offered an interview.
The team is still facing challenges, but they're starting to see some light at the end of the tunnel.
When her treatment began working, it felt like there was a light at the end of the tunnel.
