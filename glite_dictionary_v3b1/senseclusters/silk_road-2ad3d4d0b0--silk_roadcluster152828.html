Silk Road

proper noun
modern China-led trade and infrastructure initiative
4.5
Pronunciation: /sɪlk roʊd/ (AmE), /sɪlk rəʊd/ (BrE)
(in contemporary geopolitics) a name used (often as 'New Silk Road') for China-led networks of trade corridors, transport links, and infrastructure projects associated with the Belt and Road Initiative.

Examples

Modern Silk Road projects
China promotes the Silk Road as a network of ports, railways, and trade corridors across continents.
European officials criticized the Silk Road projects for lacking transparency and creating long-term debt risks.
After negotiations, the city signed a memorandum to join the new Silk Road logistics hub next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Belt and Road Initiative - China's global development and infrastructure strategy promoting transport and economic links across many countries.
  • synonym: New Silk Road - an alternative modern name for contemporary trade and infrastructure corridors inspired by the historic Silk Road.
