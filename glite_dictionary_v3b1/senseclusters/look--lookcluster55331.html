look

noun
an act of looking at something
1.0
Pronunciation: / lʊk / (AmE), / lʊk / (BrE)
an act of turning one's eyes toward something or someone to see them

Examples

A curious look
He gave her a curious look as she entered the room.
Let me have a look at those documents before you send them.
With just one look, she understood everything he meant.
