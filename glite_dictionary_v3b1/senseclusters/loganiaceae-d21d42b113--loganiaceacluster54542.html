Loganiaceae

noun
family of dicotyledonous flowering plants
Pronunciation: / loʊˌɡæniˈeɪsi.i /; / loʊˌɡæniˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / lɒˌɡæniˈeɪsi.i /; / ləʊˌɡænɪˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous flowering plants in the order Gentianales, including trees, shrubs, and climbing plants, some of which are poisonous

Loganiaceae plant family
