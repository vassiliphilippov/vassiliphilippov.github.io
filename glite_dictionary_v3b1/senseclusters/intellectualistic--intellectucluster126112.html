intellectualistic

adjective
overly theoretical
9.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tə.lek.tʃuː.ˈɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.lek.tʃuː.ˈɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
focused too much on intellectual or theoretical ideas, often ignoring practical or emotional aspects

Examples
