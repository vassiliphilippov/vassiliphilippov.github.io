paramedical

adjective
trained to assist medical staff
1.4 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.əˈmɛd.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌpær.əˈmɛd.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to people trained to assist doctors and nurses, especially in emergency medical situations, but who are not fully qualified medical doctors

Examples

Paramedical staff
He completed his training as a paramedical worker and joined the search and rescue team.
Several paramedical staff assisted the nurses during the disaster response.
The organization provides paramedical support during sporting events and festivals.
