unmonitored

adjective
not supervised
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɑːnɪtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒnɪtəd / (BrE)
not watched, checked, or supervised by anyone.

Examples

Unmonitored area
The security cameras were broken, leaving the entrance unmonitored all night.
Sensitive data should never be stored on an unmonitored computer system.
The children played in an unmonitored area behind the school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monitored - watched or checked regularly
  • synonym: unsupervised - not being watched or directed by someone in authority
