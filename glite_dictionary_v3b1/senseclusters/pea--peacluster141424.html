pea

noun
small round green seed used as food
1.0 7.55/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê / (AmE), / piÀê / (BrE)
a small, round, green seed that is eaten as a vegetable, taken from the pod of the pea plant

Synonyms

  • green pea
    1.0 0.28/m
    green pea is picked young and eaten fresh as a tender seed, while pea is the general small round seed from the pod.
  • garden pea
    1.0 0.01/m
    garden pea is the edible seed or sometimes the pod eaten from the garden plant, while pea refers to the seed from the pod.
