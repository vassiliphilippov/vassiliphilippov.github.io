dowry

noun
property or money given at marriage
6.0 1.67/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdaÊŠri / (AmE), / ËˆdaÊŠÉ™ri / (BrE)
money or property that a woman brings to her husband at marriage

Examples

A dowry payment
Her family gave a substantial dowry when she married.
In some cultures, the dowry tradition still plays an important role.
The dispute arose over the amount of dowry to be paid.

Synonyms

  • dower
    9.9 0.00/m
    dower is a formal legal gift given under a marriage contract, while dowry is the common payment or property a woman brings.
  • portion
    9.7 0.26/m
    portion is a general share or amount used in many situations, while dowry specifically means the property a woman brings at marriage.
