stick to one's last

verb
restrict oneself to one's expertise
8.1 7.4
Pronunciation: /stɪk tə wʌnz læst/ /; / /stɪk tuː wʌnz læst/ /; / /stɪk tə wʌns læst/ (AmE), /stɪk tə wʌnz lɑːst/ /; / /stɪk tuː wʌnz lɑːst/ (BrE)
to restrict oneself to one's area of expertise and not attempt tasks beyond one's skill or knowledge

Examples
