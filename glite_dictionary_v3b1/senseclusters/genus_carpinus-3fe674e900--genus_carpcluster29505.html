genus Carpinus

noun
genus of trees (hornbeams)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs kɑːrˈpaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs kɑːˈpɪ.nəs / (BrE)
A genus of mostly deciduous monoecious trees and shrubs known as hornbeams, sometimes placed in the subfamily Carpinaceae.

Examples

Genus Carpinus trees
Botanists often debate the classification of the genus Carpinus within plant families.
The genus Carpinus includes trees commonly known as hornbeams, which are valued for their hard wood.
There are many species within the genus Carpinus that are cultivated for their attractive foliage.
