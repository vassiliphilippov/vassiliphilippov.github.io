in tow

adverb
accompanied by someone or something
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪn toʊ / (AmE), / ɪn təʊ / (BrE)
accompanied by someone or something, especially as part of a group or following closely behind

Examples

Arrive in tow
At the conference, he arrived with several assistants in tow.
She brought her little brother in tow to the movie theater.
Walking down the street with the dog in tow, she greeted her neighbors.
