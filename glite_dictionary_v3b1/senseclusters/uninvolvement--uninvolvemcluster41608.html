uninvolvement

noun
emotional detachment
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈvɑlvmənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɒlvmənt / (BrE)
the state of not being involved, connected, or taking part in something (including emotionally, in responsibility, or participation)

  • antonym: involvement - the act of participating or being emotionally engaged
