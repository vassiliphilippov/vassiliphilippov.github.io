chemist's shop

noun
pharmacy; shop selling medicines (British)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪsts ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪsts ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where medicines and other health-related products are sold; a pharmacy, especially in British English.

Examples

The chemist's shop
I stopped by the chemist's shop to pick up a prescription for my headache.
Many chemist's shops also sell toiletries and cosmetics in the UK.
She asked for advice on cold remedies at the local chemist's shop.
