multitasking

noun
doing multiple tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌtæskɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌtɑːskɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of doing several tasks at the same time

Examples

Good multitasking skills
Effective multitasking can help you manage a busy schedule at work.
Some people believe that multitasking reduces the quality of their work.
She was multitasking by answering emails while talking on the phone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: focus - concentration on a single task or activity
  • synonym: task switching - the process of alternating attention between different tasks
