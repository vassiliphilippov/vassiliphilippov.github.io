phenylethylene

noun
colorless oily liquid monomer for polystyrene
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛnɪlˈɛθɪˌlin / (AmE), / ˌfiːnaɪlˈɛθɪliːn / (BrE)
A colorless oily liquid hydrocarbon used as the monomer for making polystyrene plastics; also known as styrene.

Examples

Phenylethylene monomer
Exposure to phenylethylene vapors should be limited in industrial environments.
In chemical laboratories, phenylethylene is often stored in airtight containers to prevent evaporation.
The production of polystyrene requires a steady supply of phenylethylene.

Related Terms
