sea pen

noun
featherlike marine colonial animal
Pronunciation: / ˈsi pɛn / (AmE), / ˈsi pɛn / (BrE)
a colonial marine animal with a soft, featherlike body, belonging to the order Pennatulacea, found especially in warm waters

The biologist explained that a sea pen is actually a colony of tiny polyps working together.
