race against time

noun
urgent effort to beat a deadline
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪs əˈɡɛnst taɪm / (AmE), / ˈreɪs əˈɡɛnst taɪm / (BrE)
an urgent situation in which someone must complete a task or achieve something before a deadline or before it is too late

Examples

A race against time challenge
It became a race against time to submit the application before the midnight deadline.
The doctors began a race against time to save the patient's life after the accident.
With the storm approaching, rescue workers were in a race against time to reach the stranded hikers.
