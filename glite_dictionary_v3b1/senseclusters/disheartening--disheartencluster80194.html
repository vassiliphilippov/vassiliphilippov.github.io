disheartening

adjective
causing discouragement or loss of morale
4.6
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈhɑrtənɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈhɑːtnɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a loss of confidence, hope, or enthusiasm

Examples

A disheartening result
Her rejection letter was disheartening, but she decided to try again.
It can be disheartening to see little progress after so much effort.
The team's defeat was deeply disheartening to the fans.
