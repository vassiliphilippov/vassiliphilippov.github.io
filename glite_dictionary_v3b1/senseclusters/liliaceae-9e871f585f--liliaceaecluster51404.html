Liliaceae

noun
the lily family of flowering plants
9.3
Pronunciation: / lɪˌliˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / lɪˌlɪˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, commonly known as the lily family, including many ornamental and economically important species

Examples
