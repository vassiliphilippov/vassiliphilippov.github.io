legend of Johnny Appleseed

noun
American folk tale of Johnny Appleseed
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒənd əv ˌdʒɑːni ˈæpəlˌsid / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒənd əv ˌdʒɒni ˈæp(ə)lˌsiːd / (BrE)
The traditional story or folklore about Johnny Appleseed, a legendary figure celebrated for planting apple trees across parts of the United States.

Examples

The legend of Johnny Appleseed
Every year, the town holds a festival celebrating the legend of Johnny Appleseed.
The legend of Johnny Appleseed has been told to children for generations in the United States.
The legend of Johnny Appleseed reminds us of the importance of nature and generosity.
