bootie

noun
short ankle fashion boot
3.5 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀêti / (AmE), / ÀàbuÀêti / (BrE)
A short, low boot that usually covers the ankle; a fashionable ankle boot often worn by adults.

Examples

Fashion bootie
She bought new leather booties to wear with skinny jeans and a wool coat.
For the concert she chose a suede bootie that matched her vintage dress.
This season's catalog features several heeled booties in bright colors and metallic finishes.

Synonyms
