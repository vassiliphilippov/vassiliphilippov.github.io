uvular

adjective
relating to the uvula
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːvjələr / (AmE), / ˈjuːvjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the uvula, the small fleshy extension at the back of the soft palate; also, in linguistics, describing a sound produced with the uvula.

Examples

Uvular swelling
A sore throat can sometimes cause uvular swelling.
She studied the uvular consonants found in certain Caucasian languages.
The French 'r' is sometimes pronounced as a uvular sound.
