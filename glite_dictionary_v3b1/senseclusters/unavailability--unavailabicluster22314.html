unavailability

noun
the state of not being available
Pronunciation: / ʌnəˌveɪləˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ʌnəˌveɪləˈbɪləti / (BrE)
The quality or state of not being available when needed.

Examples

The unavailability of product
Due to the unavailability of the product, customers turned to alternative brands.
Her unavailability for the meeting was noted by the manager.
The unavailability of reliable information delayed the decision-making process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: availability - the quality of being able to be used or obtained
