arm bone

noun
any bone in the human arm
2.7 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / …ërm bo än / (AmE), / …ëÀêm b…ô än / (BrE)
any of the bones forming the structure of the human arm, particularly the humerus, radius, or ulna.

Examples

The arm bone
During the fall, he bruised his arm bone but fortunately did not break it.
She wore a cast to help her broken arm bone heal properly.
The X-ray showed that the arm bone was fractured just above the elbow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humerus - the bone of the upper arm or forelimb, forming joints at the shoulder and the elbow
  • synonym: radius - the thicker and shorter of the two bones in the human forearm
  • synonym: ulna - the thinner and longer of the two bones in the human forearm
