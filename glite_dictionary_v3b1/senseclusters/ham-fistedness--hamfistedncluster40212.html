ham-fistedness

noun
clumsiness or heavy-handedness
Pronunciation: / ˈhæm ˌfɪstɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈhæm ˌfɪstɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being clumsy, awkward, or lacking dexterity, often resulting in excessive or heavy-handed actions.

Examples

The ham-fistedness approach
Critics pointed out the ham-fistedness of the director's treatment of the sensitive subject.
Her ham-fistedness in handling delicate negotiations only made the situation worse.
The ham-fistedness of his attempt at cooking resulted in a kitchen disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clumsiness - the quality of being awkward or lacking in coordination
  • antonym: dexterity - skill and grace in physical movement, especially with the hands
