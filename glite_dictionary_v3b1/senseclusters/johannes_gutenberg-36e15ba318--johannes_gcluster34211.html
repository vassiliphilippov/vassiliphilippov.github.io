Johannes Gutenberg

proper noun
German inventor of the printing press
5.2
Pronunciation: /dʒoʊ.ˈhæn.əs ˈguː.tən.bɜːrɡ/ /; / /joʊ.ˈhæn.əs ˈguː.tən.bɜːrɡ/ (AmE), /dʒəʊ.ˈhæn.ɪs ˈguː.tən.bɜːg/ /; / /jəʊ.ˈhæn.ɪs ˈguː.tən.bɜːg/ (BrE)
(1400–1468) A German inventor and printer, famous for introducing movable type printing to Europe and developing the printing press.

Examples

Johannes Gutenberg inventor
