Anthropocene

adjective
human-influenced era
Pronunciation: / ˈænθrəpəˌsiːn / (AmE), / ˈænθrəpəˌsiːn / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from human activities that have a significant impact on Earth's environment or ecosystems

Examples

Anthropocene changes
Many scientists study Anthropocene changes to understand how humans alter the planet.
The Anthropocene period is marked by rapid environmental shifts due to industrialization.
Climate change is often discussed as a key feature of the Anthropocene age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: human-caused - resulting from human actions
  • antonym: natural - existing in or caused by nature, not by humans
