keep it juicy

verb
sensationalize or make content engaging
9.3
Pronunciation: / kip ɪt ˈdʒusi / (AmE), / kiːp ɪt ˈdʒuːsi / (BrE)
to keep a story, report, or other content deliberately vivid, sensational, or attention-grabbing in order to maintain audience interest.

Examples

Keep it juicy for readers
Editors told the columnist to keep it juicy so readers would not stop scrolling.
She decided to keep it juicy by adding a surprising twist to the interview.
Podcasters often keep it juicy when discussing celebrity rumors to boost audience numbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensationalize - to present information in a deliberately dramatic or shocking way to attract attention
  • antonym: tone down - to make a story or statement less dramatic or extreme
