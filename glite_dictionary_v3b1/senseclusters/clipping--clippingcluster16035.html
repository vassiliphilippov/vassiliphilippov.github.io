clipping

noun
excerpt from a newspaper or magazine
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
a small piece cut from a newspaper or magazine, typically containing an article, photograph, or advertisement

Examples

A newspaper clipping
He glued a sports clipping onto the project board for his presentation.
My grandmother keeps a clipping of my first published article in her scrapbook.
The detective found an old newspaper clipping in the victim's wallet.
