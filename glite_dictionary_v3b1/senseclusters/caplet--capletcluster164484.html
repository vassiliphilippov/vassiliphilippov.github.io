caplet

noun
small coated pill
Pronunciation: / ˈkæplət / (AmE), / ˈkæplɪt / (BrE)
a small, smooth, coated pill shaped like a capsule, designed to be easy to swallow

Examples

A caplet pill
The doctor recommended taking one caplet every eight hours for the pain.
She prefers a caplet because it is easier to swallow than a regular tablet.
Each caplet contains 500 milligrams of the active ingredient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsule - a small container with medicine inside, usually with a gelatin shell
  • synonym: tablet - a small, solid dose of medicine in a flat, round shape
