Staffordshire bullterrier

noun
a muscular English dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈstæfərdˌʃɪr ˈbʊlˌtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈstæfədʃə ˈbʊlˌtɛriə / (BrE)
a strong, stocky breed of dog from England, with a broad skull and a smooth, short coat

Examples

The Staffordshire bullterrier dog
At the dog show, the Staffordshire bullterrier impressed the judges with its muscular build and calm demeanor.
Her family adopted a young Staffordshire bullterrier from the rescue center last year.
The Staffordshire bullterrier is known for its loyalty and affectionate nature.
