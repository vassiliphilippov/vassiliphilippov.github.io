tour

noun
journey visiting several places
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /tʊr/ /; / /tɔːr/ (AmE), /tʊə/ /; / /tɔː/ (BrE)
a journey or trip in which one visits several places, often returning to the starting point, especially for pleasure, study, or business

Examples

A tour of Europe

The city offers a bike tour that covers all the major attractions.
We went on a guided tour of the ancient castle.
