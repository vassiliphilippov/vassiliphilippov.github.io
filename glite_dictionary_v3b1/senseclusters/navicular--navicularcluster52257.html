navicular

noun
largest wrist bone near the thumb
Pronunciation: / nəˈvɪkjəlɚ /; / nəˈvɪkjələr / (AmE), / nəˈvɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
a boat-shaped bone in the foot or wrist, important for joint movement
