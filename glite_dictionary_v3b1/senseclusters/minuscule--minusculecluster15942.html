minuscule

adjective
written in lowercase letters
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnəˌskjul / (AmE), / mɪˈnʌskjuːl / (BrE)
(of letters or writing) written in small, lowercase characters rather than capital letters.

Examples

Minuscule letter form
In German, all nouns start with a capital letter, not a minuscule one.
Many modern poets prefer to write their work entirely in minuscule letters.
The artist labeled the diagram using only minuscule characters for consistency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lowercase - letters in the smaller form, as opposed to capitals
  • antonym: majuscule - written in capital letters
