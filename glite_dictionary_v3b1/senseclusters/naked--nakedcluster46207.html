naked

adjective
lacking any cover; bare
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
not covered by anything; bare, especially referring to something that is normally covered

Examples

Naked branches
The naked branches stood stark against the winter sky.
They had to climb across the naked rocks to reach the water.
Without its usual blanket of snow, the hillside looked naked and exposed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not clothed or covered; exposed
  • antonym: covered - having a cover on or over; not exposed
