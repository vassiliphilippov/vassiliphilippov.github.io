coreferential

adjective
referring to the same thing (linguistics)
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊrəˌfɛˈrɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌkɔːrɪfəˈrɛnʃəl / (BrE)
(Linguistics) Denoting or relating to two or more expressions (such as pronouns or noun phrases) that refer to the same person or thing in a text.

Examples

Coreferential expressions
In the sentence 'Jane said she was tired,' 'Jane' and 'she' are coreferential expressions.
Linguists study how coreferential relationships affect understanding in complex sentences.
The analysis identified coreferential noun phrases throughout the document.

  • synonym: referential - relating to reference in language
