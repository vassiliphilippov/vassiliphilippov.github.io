natural process

noun
process occurring in nature
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈnætʃ.ər.əl ˈprɑː.səs/ /; / /ˈnætʃ.rəl ˈprɑː.səs/ /; / /ˈnætʃ.ər.əl ˈprɔː.səs/ (AmE), /ˈnætʃ.ər.əl ˈprəʊ.səs/ /; / /ˈnætʃ.rəl ˈprəʊ.səs/ /; / /ˈnætʃ.ə.rəl ˈprɒ.səs/ (BrE)
A process that occurs in or is produced by nature, rather than by human action or intention.

Examples

A natural process of erosion
Erosion is a natural process that shapes landscapes over thousands of years.
Photosynthesis is a natural process by which plants produce energy from sunlight.
The decomposition of organic matter is a natural process vital to soil health.

  • antonym: artificial process - a process created or controlled by humans
