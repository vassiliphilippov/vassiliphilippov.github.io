nonathlete

adjective
not athletic
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈæθliːt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈæθliːt / (BrE)
not participating in sports or organized athletic activities; not an athlete

Examples

Nonathlete students
The school organized a special program for nonathlete students interested in learning about healthy lifestyles.
As a nonathlete, she preferred reading and art to playing sports after class.
The gym offers beginner classes designed for nonathlete participants of all ages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: athletic - physically active and good at sports
  • synonym: inactive - not engaging in physical activity
