Cherokee language

noun
the language of the Cherokee people
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrəki ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛrəki ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Iroquoian language spoken by the Cherokee people, primarily in the southeastern United States
