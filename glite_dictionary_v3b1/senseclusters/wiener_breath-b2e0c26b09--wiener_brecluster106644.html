wiener breath

noun
bad breath from eating hot dogs
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwinər ˌbrɛθ / (AmE), / ˈwiːnə ˌbrɛθ / (BrE)
Bad breath caused by eating hot dogs or similar sausages, often noticeable and unpleasant.

Examples

Wiener breath smell
After eating two chili dogs, he apologized for his wiener breath.
No one wanted to sit next to him because his wiener breath lingered all afternoon.
She always chews gum to cover up wiener breath after lunch.
