nonsegmental

adjective
not divided into segments
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌsɛɡˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌsɛɡˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
(of a body or structure) not divided into segments.

Examples

Nonsegmental body structure
Some flatworms have a nonsegmental body structure.
The disease manifests as both segmental and nonsegmental forms.
Unlike insects, annelids can be nonsegmental or segmented depending on the species.

Related Terms

  • antonym: segmental - divided into segments
