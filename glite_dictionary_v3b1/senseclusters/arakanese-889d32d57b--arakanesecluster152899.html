Arakanese

noun
ethnic group of Myanmar
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrəkəˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌɑːrəkəˈniːz / (BrE)
a member of the Arakanese ethnic group from Rakhine State in Myanmar, or the Arakanese people collectively

Examples

The Arakanese have a rich cultural heritage that is celebrated throughout Rakhine State.

An estimated thousand Arakanese attended the cultural festival in the city.
