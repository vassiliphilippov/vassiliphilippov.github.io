rambunctiousness

noun
noisy energy
Pronunciation: / ræmˈbʌŋkʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ræmˈbʌŋkʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being noisy, energetic, and difficult to control

Examples

The rambunctiousness of puppies
The rambunctiousness of the puppies made it hard to keep the house tidy.
Despite their rambunctiousness, the children were well-liked by their teachers.
After a long day, she found the rambunctiousness of the playground overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boisterousness - the quality of being noisy and lively
  • antonym: calmness - the state of being quiet and peaceful
  • synonym: rowdiness - the state of being noisy and disorderly
