antifundamentalist

adjective
opposed to fundamentalism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
opposed to or against fundamentalist beliefs, movements, or people

Examples

Antifundamentalist views
The organization took an antifundamentalist approach to education reform.
Her antifundamentalist views often sparked debates within the community.
Many young people today identify with antifundamentalist movements seeking social change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fundamentalist - strictly adhering to basic principles, especially in religion
  • synonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
