cesta

noun
jai alai basket
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛstə / (AmE), / ˈsɛstə / (BrE)
a curved basket attached to the hand, used to catch and throw the ball in jai alai

Examples

Jai alai cesta
The player skillfully caught the ball in his cesta during the match.
Each jai alai competitor must master the use of the cesta to succeed.
The curved shape of the cesta allows for incredibly fast throws.
