nonrestrictive

adjective
not limiting; adds extra information (linguistics)
6.7 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈstrɪktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈstrɪktɪv / (BrE)
(of a clause, phrase, or modifier) not restricting or limiting the meaning of the word it modifies, but simply adding extra information.
