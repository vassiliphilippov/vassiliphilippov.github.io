screwbean

noun
North American shrub or tree with twisted pods
Pronunciation: / ˈskruːˌbin / (AmE), / ˈskruːˌbiːn / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico that bears spirally twisted seed pods

Examples

The screwbean shrub
Local wildlife often feeds on the twisted pods produced by the screwbean.
Researchers are studying how the screwbean adapts to harsh desert climates.
The screwbean thrives in arid regions of the southwestern United States.
