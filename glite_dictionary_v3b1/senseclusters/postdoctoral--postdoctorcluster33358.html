postdoctoral

adjective
after doctoral degree; research phase
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˌdɑkˈtɔr.əl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˌdɒkˈtɔː.rəl / (BrE)
relating to study or research undertaken after earning a doctoral degree

Examples

Postdoctoral research position
Many graduates pursue postdoctoral positions to gain additional experience before seeking faculty jobs.
She accepted a postdoctoral fellowship at the university to continue her research on climate change.
The postdoctoral researchers presented their findings at the international conference.
