noun

9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.pər.sfɪr/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.pə.sfɪə/ (BrE)

Examples

A hypersphere in geometry
In advanced geometry, a hypersphere generalizes the concept of a sphere to higher dimensions.
Calculating the volume of a hypersphere requires different formulas than for a regular sphere.
Physicists sometimes use the idea of a hypersphere when discussing the shape of the universe.
