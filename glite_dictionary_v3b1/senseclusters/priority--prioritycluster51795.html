priority

noun
the condition of being more important or urgent
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /praɪ.ˈɔːr.ə.tiː/ /; / /praɪ.ˈɔːr.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /praɪ.ˈɒr.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /praɪ.ˈɔːr.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
