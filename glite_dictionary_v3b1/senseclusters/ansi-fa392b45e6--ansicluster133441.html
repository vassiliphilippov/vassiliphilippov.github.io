ANSI

proper noun
US standards organization
Pronunciation: / ˈænsi / (AmE), / ˈænsi / (BrE)
the American National Standards Institute, an organization that develops and publishes technical standards in the United States

Examples

ANSI safety standards
The new helmet complies with ANSI safety standards for protective equipment.
Many computer keyboards follow ANSI layout guidelines in the United States.
Before approval, the product must meet ANSI requirements for electrical safety.

Related Terms
