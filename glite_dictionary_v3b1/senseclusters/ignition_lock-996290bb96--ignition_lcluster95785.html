ignition lock

noun
vehicle ignition security device
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɪʃən lɑk / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɪʃən lɒk / (BrE)
a locking mechanism in a vehicle's ignition system that prevents it from being started without the correct key

Examples

The ignition lock system
He lost his keys and had to replace the ignition lock in his vehicle.
Modern cars often use electronic systems instead of a traditional ignition lock.
The mechanic diagnosed a problem with the ignition lock, which was preventing the car from starting.
