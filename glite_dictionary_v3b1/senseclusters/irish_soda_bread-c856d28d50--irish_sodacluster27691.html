Irish soda bread

noun
round loaf made with soda and buttermilk
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈsoʊdə brɛd / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˈsəʊdə brɛd / (BrE)
A round loaf of bread made with baking soda and buttermilk, often containing caraway seeds and raisins.

Examples

A Irish soda bread loaf
My grandmother always prepared Irish soda bread with raisins for our Sunday brunch.
The Irish soda bread had a unique texture thanks to the buttermilk and caraway seeds.
You can find freshly baked Irish soda bread at the local bakery around St. Patrick's Day.
