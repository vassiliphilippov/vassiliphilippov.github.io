cochairman

noun
joint committee leader
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrmən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈtʃeəmən / (BrE)
a person who shares the position of chairman with one or more others in a committee or organization

Examples

The cochairmen meet
The two cochairmen worked together to organize the annual conference.
As cochairman, he shared responsibilities with his colleague during the negotiations.
The board elected a new cochairman to help lead the committee.
