errantry

noun
knightly wandering
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrəntri / (AmE), / ˈɛrəntri / (BrE)
the quality or practice of wandering in search of adventure, especially as performed by knights in medieval times

Examples

Knight's errantry quest
The legend describes the knight's years of errantry as he sought to prove his valor.
Stories of errantry often involve quests, dragons, and distant kingdoms.
Inspired by tales of errantry, he dreamed of traveling the world in search of adventure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventure - an unusual and exciting or daring experience
  • synonym: chivalry - the medieval knightly system with its moral and social code
