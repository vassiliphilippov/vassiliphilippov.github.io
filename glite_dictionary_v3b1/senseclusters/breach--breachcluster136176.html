breach

verb
make an opening in a barrier or wall

Pronunciation: / briÀêt É / (AmE), / briÀêt É / (BrE)
to make an opening or gap in a wall, barrier, or defense

Examples

To breach a wall

The army finally breached the city walls after a long siege.
