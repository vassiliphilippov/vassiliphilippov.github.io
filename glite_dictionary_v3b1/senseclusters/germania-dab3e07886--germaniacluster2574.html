Germania

proper noun
symbolic figure of Germany
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒərˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / dʒɜːˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
an allegorical female figure symbolizing Germany, often depicted in art and political imagery

Examples

Germania statue
The statue of Germania stands proudly at the Niederwalddenkmal monument overlooking the Rhine.
Artists in the 19th century often painted Germania to inspire feelings of national unity.
In political cartoons, Germania is frequently shown wearing armor and holding the German flag.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Britannia - the female personification of Britain
  • synonym: Marianne - the national personification of France as a female figure
