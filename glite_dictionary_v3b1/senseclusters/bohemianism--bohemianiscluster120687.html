bohemianism

noun
unconventional, artistic lifestyle
7.2
Pronunciation: / boʊˈhiːmiəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / bəʊˈhiːmi.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the unconventional lifestyle or conduct associated with bohemians, often marked by artistic, literary, or spiritual pursuits and disregard for social norms

Examples

Life of bohemianism
After leaving her corporate job, Anna embraced a life of bohemianism and creativity.
His poetry reflected the bohemianism that defined his generation's values.
She was drawn to the bohemianism of the city's vibrant art scene.
