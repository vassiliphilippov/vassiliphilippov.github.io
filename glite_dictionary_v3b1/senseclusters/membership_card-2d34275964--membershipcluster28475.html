membership card

noun
card proving membership in an organization
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛm.bɚʃɪp kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈmɛm.bəʃɪp kɑːd / (BrE)
an official card that shows a person is a member of a club, organization, or group

Examples

A membership card application
He applied for a library membership card to borrow books.
Many gyms require you to show a membership card before using the facilities.
She presented her membership card at the entrance to gain access to the club.
