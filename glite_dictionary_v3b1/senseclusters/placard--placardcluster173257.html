placard

noun
publicly displayed sign or notice
Pronunciation: / ˈplæk.ɑrd / (AmE), / ˈplæk.ɑːd / (BrE)

Examples

He made a homemade placard to support his team's championship game.
Protesters held up a placard demanding climate action during the rally.
