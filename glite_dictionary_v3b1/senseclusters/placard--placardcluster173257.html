placard

noun
publicly displayed sign or notice
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈplæk.ɑrd / (AmE), / ˈplæk.ɑːd / (BrE)
a large notice or sign displayed in a public place, especially for advertising or information

Examples

A protest placard
A brightly colored placard in the window advertised the store's grand opening.
He made a homemade placard to support his team's championship game.
Protesters held up a placard demanding climate action during the rally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billboard - large outdoor board for displaying advertisements
  • synonym: poster - large printed notice or picture for display in a public place
