belly-flop

verb
fail spectacularly
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli flɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛli flɒp / (BrE)
(informal) to fail completely and often embarrassingly; to be an unsuccessful or disastrous failure.

Examples

The plan belly-flopped badly
The product launch was expected to belly-flop because marketing failed to create interest.
After the harsh reviews, the new play belly-flopped and closed after only two performances.
Critics warned the comedian would belly-flop if he relied on tired jokes and predictable gags.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flop - to fail completely, especially in public or commercially
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the intended result; the opposite of failing
