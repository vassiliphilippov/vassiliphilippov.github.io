witness

noun
testimony or declaration of religious faith
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪtnɪs / (BrE)
an open declaration of religious faith, especially by speaking about or demonstrating one's beliefs through actions

Examples

A living witness
His life serves as a powerful witness to his spiritual beliefs.
Members of the congregation were encouraged to bear witness at the service.
She gave witness to her faith through acts of charity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: testimony - a formal statement, especially given in a religious or legal context
