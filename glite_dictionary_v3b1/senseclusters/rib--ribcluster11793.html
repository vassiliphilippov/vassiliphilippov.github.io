rib

noun
curved bone of the chest
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪb / (AmE), / rɪb / (BrE)
any of the long curved bones which form the rib cage, enclosing and protecting the chest organs in humans and other vertebrates

Examples

Broken rib bone
Many animals have a similar structure to the human rib cage.
She broke a rib while playing football last weekend.
The doctor examined his chest to see if any ribs were fractured.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sternum - the breastbone in the center of the chest
