could care less

verb
idiom meaning not to care at all
2.5 9.1
Pronunciation: / kʊd kɛr lɛs / (AmE), / kʊd keə lɛs / (BrE)
used to indicate that one does not care at all about something; often used sarcastically to mean the opposite of the literal words

Examples

I could care less about it
Frank said he could care less about the outcome of the match.
I could care less what they think about my decision.
She acts like she could care less about the gossip, but I think otherwise.
