choke up

verb
become unable to speak from emotion
Pronunciation: / tʃoʊk ʌp / (AmE), / tʃəʊk ʌp / (BrE)
To become so emotional that it is difficult to speak.

Examples

Choke up with emotion
He tried not to choke up while delivering the farewell speech.
Many people choke up during weddings or graduation ceremonies.
She began to choke up as she spoke about her late husband.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break up - to become emotionally overwhelmed and start crying
  • antonym: compose oneself - to regain self-control, especially after being emotional
