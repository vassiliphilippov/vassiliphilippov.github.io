racecard

noun
list of races and horses at a race meeting
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈreɪsˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a printed or digital program for a race meeting, listing the races and the names of the horses competing

Examples

A racecard program
Before the event began, she carefully studied the racecard to choose her favorite horses.
He misplaced his racecard, so he had to ask a friend which horses were racing next.
The racecard was available online for fans to review the schedule and runners.
