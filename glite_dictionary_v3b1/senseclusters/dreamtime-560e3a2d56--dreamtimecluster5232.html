Dreamtime

noun
dreamlike period
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈdriːmtaɪm / (BrE)
a period or state of dreaming, imagination, or a dreamlike experience, often used poetically or metaphorically

Examples

Peaceful Dreamtime
As the music played softly, she drifted into a peaceful Dreamtime filled with childhood memories.
Writers often find inspiration during their own private Dreamtime late at night.
The painting captures a magical Dreamtime where reality and fantasy blend together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
  • synonym: reverie - a state of being lost in one's thoughts or daydreams
