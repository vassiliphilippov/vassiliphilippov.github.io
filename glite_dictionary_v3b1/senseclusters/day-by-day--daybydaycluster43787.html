day-by-day

adjective
happening or changing every day
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪ baɪ ˈdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ baɪ ˈdeɪ / (BrE)
happening or changing every day; continuing or progressing daily.

Examples

A day-by-day process
His recovery has been a day-by-day process, improving little by little.
The day-by-day changes in the weather made planning difficult.
We monitor the patient's progress on a day-by-day basis to ensure safety.
