barbacoa

noun
slow-roasted meat
6.3
Pronunciation: /bɑːr.bə.ˈkoʊ.ə/ /; / /bɑːr.bə.ˈkoʊ/ (AmE), /bɑː.bə.ˈkəʊ.ə/ /; / /bɑː.bə.ˈkəʊ/ (BrE)
a method of cooking meat by slow-roasting it, traditionally in a covered pit or over coals, especially in Mexican cuisine

Examples

Barbacoa cooking method
The chef prepared the beef as barbacoa, slow-cooking it until it was tender and flavorful.
Authentic barbacoa is often served with tortillas, onions, and fresh cilantro.
They learned how to make traditional barbacoa during their trip to Mexico.
