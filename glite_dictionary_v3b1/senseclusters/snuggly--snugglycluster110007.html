snuggly

adverb
in a cuddly way
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡli / (BrE)
in a way that expresses or invites cuddling or close physical comfort

Examples

Curled up snuggly
The puppy curled up snuggly in her lap as she read her book.
He wrapped the blanket snuggly around his shoulders to keep warm.
The children fit snuggly together on the small couch, watching cartoons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cozily - in a comfortable and warm way
  • degree higher: tightly - in a firm or close way
