Abelia

noun
a genus of flowering shrubs
Pronunciation: / əˈbiːliə / (AmE), / əˈbiːliə / (BrE)
a genus of chiefly East Asian flowering shrubs in the honeysuckle family, often cultivated for their attractive flowers and foliage

Examples

Abelia flowering shrubs
Botanists classify several ornamental shrubs under the genus Abelia.
Gardeners often choose Abelia for its resilience and colorful foliage.
