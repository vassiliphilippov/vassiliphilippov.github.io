embryogenesis

noun
embryo development process
9.0
Pronunciation: /em.bri.oʊ.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ /; / /em.bri.ə.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /em.bri.əʊ.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ /; / /em.bri.oʊ.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
the process by which an embryo forms and develops from a fertilized egg or single cell

Examples

Embryogenesis process
