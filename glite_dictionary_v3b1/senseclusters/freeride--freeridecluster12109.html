freeride

verb
to ride with tricks
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌraɪd / (BrE)
to ride a bike, snowboard, or skis in a style that emphasizes freedom, tricks, or challenging descents without following a set course

Examples

To freeride a mountain
Many snowboarders love to freeride down untouched mountain slopes after a fresh snowfall.
He prefers to freeride rather than follow the marked trails at the bike park.
They spent the afternoon freeriding through the forest, jumping over rocks and fallen trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freestyle - to perform sports without strict rules or set patterns
  • antonym: race - to compete in speed against others on a set course
