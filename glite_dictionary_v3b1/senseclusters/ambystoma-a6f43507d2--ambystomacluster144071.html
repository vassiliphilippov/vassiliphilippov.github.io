Ambystoma

proper noun
salamander genus
Pronunciation: / æmˈbɪstoʊmə / (AmE), / æmˈbɪstəmə / (BrE)
a genus of salamanders native mainly to North America, including species like the axolotl and tiger salamander

Examples

Ambystoma genus
The Ambystoma genus is well known for including the axolotl, a unique aquatic salamander.
Scientists study Ambystoma species to learn about regeneration and development in amphibians.
The tiger salamander, a member of Ambystoma, is common in many parts of North America.

Related Terms
