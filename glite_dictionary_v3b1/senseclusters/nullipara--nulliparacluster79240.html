nullipara

noun
woman who has never given birth
9.9
Pronunciation: / nʌˈlɪpərə / (AmE), / nʌˈlɪpərə / (BrE)
a woman who has never given birth to a child

Examples

A nullipara woman
As a nullipara, she was at higher risk for certain complications.
Her medical records indicated that she was a nullipara at the time of admission.
The study compared the pregnancy outcomes of nullipara and multipara women.
