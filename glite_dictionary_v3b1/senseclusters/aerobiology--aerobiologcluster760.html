aerobiology

noun
study of airborne life
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of airborne organisms and biological particles, such as pollen, spores, and bacteria

Examples

Aerobiology study pollen
Researchers in aerobiology monitor pollen levels to help people with allergies.
Advances in aerobiology have improved our understanding of how diseases spread through the air.
The field of aerobiology is important for studying airborne bacteria and their effects on health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecology - the study of relationships between living things and their environment
  • synonym: microbiology - the study of microscopic organisms
