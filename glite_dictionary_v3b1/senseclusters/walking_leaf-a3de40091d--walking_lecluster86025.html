walking leaf

noun
tropical insect mimicking a leaf
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkɪŋ lif / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ liːf / (BrE)
A tropical insect that resembles a flattened leaf, found commonly in southern Asia and the East Indies.

Examples

Walking leaf insect
In the wild, a walking leaf can be incredibly difficult to spot due to its camouflage.
Scientists study the walking leaf to learn more about evolutionary adaptations.
The walking leaf is a master of disguise, blending seamlessly among real leaves.
