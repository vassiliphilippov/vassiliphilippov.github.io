open the batting

verb
start a cricket innings as first batsman
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.pən ðə ˈbæt̬.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən ðə ˈbæt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
to begin a team's innings in cricket as one of the first two batsmen

Examples

To open the batting in cricket
He was chosen to open the batting for his country in the final match.
In tomorrow's game, it will be risky to open the batting on a damp pitch.
Whenever she opens the batting, the team usually gets off to a strong start.
