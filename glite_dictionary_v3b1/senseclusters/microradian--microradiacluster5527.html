microradian

noun
a unit of angular distance
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈreɪdiən / (AmE), / ˌmɪkrəʊˈreɪdiən / (BrE)
A unit of angular measurement equal to one thousandth of a milliradian, or one millionth of a radian.

Examples

One microradian unit
Astronomers can detect tiny angular movements as small as one microradian.
In precision engineering, measurements are sometimes specified in microradians for greater accuracy.
The laser beam was adjusted by just a few microradians to hit the target perfectly.
