three-lane

adjective
having three traffic lanes, with a central passing lane
3.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /θriː leɪn/ (AmE), /θriː leɪn/ /; / /friː leɪn/ (BrE)
(of a road) having three lanes, typically with one lane for traffic in each direction and a center lane for passing.

Examples

A three-lane road
A three-lane configuration is common on rural roads to allow safer overtaking.
Be careful when driving in the three-lane section; the center lane is for passing only.
The old highway was converted into a three-lane road to improve traffic flow.
