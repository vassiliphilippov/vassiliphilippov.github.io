twistle

noun
forked branch or stream
an old word for a forked branch or a small stream, especially where a stream divides or meets another

Examples

A twistle in the stream
The old map showed a twistle winding through the valley.
Children played near the twistle where the two streams met.
He carved his walking stick from a sturdy twistle he found in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branch - a part of a tree that grows out from the trunk
  • synonym: brook - a small stream
