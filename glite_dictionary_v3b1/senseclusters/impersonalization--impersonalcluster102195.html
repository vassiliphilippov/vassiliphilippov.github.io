impersonalization

noun
making less personal
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɜrsənələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɜːsənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something less personal or more detached, often by removing individual attention or emotion

Examples

Impersonalization of workplaces
The impersonalization of modern workplaces can make employees feel disconnected from their colleagues.
Many people dislike the impersonalization of online customer support systems.
Some argue that the impersonalization of education through technology reduces meaningful teacher-student interaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depersonalization - the process of making something impersonal or lacking individuality
  • antonym: personalization - the act of making something personal or tailored to an individual
