urgent

adjective
requiring immediate action
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
requiring immediate attention or action; very important and needing to be dealt with at once.

Examples

An urgent problem
It's urgent that we repair the leak in the roof before the next rainstorm.
She left him an urgent message asking for help as soon as possible.
The hospital is admitting only urgent cases during the emergency.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: critical - extremely important or vital, often at a decisive moment
  • degree lower: important - having great significance or value
  • synonym: pressing - requiring immediate attention or action
