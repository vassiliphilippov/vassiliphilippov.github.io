dowdily

adverb
in an unfashionable manner
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊ.dɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈdaʊ.dɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is unfashionable or lacking in style; in a dowdy manner.

Examples

Dressed dowdily
Despite her talent, she presented herself dowdily and without any flair for fashion.
He was criticized for appearing dowdily at the formal event.
She always dresses dowdily, preferring comfort over style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dowdy - unfashionable and unattractive in appearance
  • antonym: stylishly - in a fashionable or elegant manner
