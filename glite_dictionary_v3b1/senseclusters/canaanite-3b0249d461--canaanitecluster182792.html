Canaanite

noun
member of an ancient Semitic people of Canaan
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.nəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.nə.naɪt / (BrE)
a member of an ancient Semitic people who lived in the region of Canaan before its conquest by the Israelites

Examples

Ancient Canaanite people
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts belonging to the Canaanite civilization in the region of modern-day Israel.
Many ancient texts mention the Canaanite people as skilled traders and builders.
The Canaanite religion was known for its pantheon of gods and goddesses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Israelite - a member of the ancient Hebrew nation that conquered Canaan
  • synonym: Phoenician - a member of an ancient Semitic people closely related to the Canaanites
