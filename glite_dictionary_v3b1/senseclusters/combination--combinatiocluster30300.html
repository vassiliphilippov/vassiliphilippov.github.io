Synonyms

  • mixture
    1.6 16/m
    mixture is used for things or ingredients physically mixed together, while combination is a more general joining of elements.
  • mix
    1.0 24/m
    mix refers to the act or result of putting things together, while combination suggests a single unit formed from parts.
  • blend
    2.3 9.08/m
    blend often means a smooth mix of similar parts like coffee or music, while combination is any joining of things.
  • fusion
    4.1 4.05/m
    fusion describes two things joining to become one, often in art or science, while combination is more general joining.
  • amalgamation
    7.5 0.75/m
    amalgamation often names a formal joining of companies or groups into one, while combination is a general, everyday joining.
  • intermixture
    7.0 0.06/m
    intermixture is a rare or formal word for a physical mixing of substances, while combination covers many kinds of joining.
  • conglomeration
    7.4 0.49/m

  • aggregation
