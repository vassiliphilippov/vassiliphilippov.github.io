combination

noun
group of things joined together
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːm.bɪˈneɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkɑːmbəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.bɪˈneɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a group or mixture of things, qualities, or people that have been joined or mixed together to form a single unit or result
