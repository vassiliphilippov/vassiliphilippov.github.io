compañero

noun
comrade or companion
8.9
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɑːnˈjɛɾoʊ / (AmE), / kəmˌpænˈjɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a companion or comrade, especially one who shares in a political or social movement

Examples

Political compañero
The union leader addressed every compañero in the crowd with respect and encouragement.
During the protest, she marched alongside her trusted compañero from the student group.
He considered his fellow volunteers not just colleagues but true compañeros in the cause.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ally - a person who supports or cooperates with another
  • synonym: comrade - a fellow member of a group, especially in a political context
  • antonym: opponent - a person who is against or competes with another
