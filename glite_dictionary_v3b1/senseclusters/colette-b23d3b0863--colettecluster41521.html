Colette

proper noun
French novelist (1873–1954)
Pronunciation: / koʊˈlɛt / (AmE), / kəˈlɛt / (BrE)
(1873–1954) French novelist noted for her works exploring female experience and sexuality, including 'Gigi' and the Claudine series.

Examples

Colette novels
Colette's novels are celebrated for their vivid evocation of Parisian life.
