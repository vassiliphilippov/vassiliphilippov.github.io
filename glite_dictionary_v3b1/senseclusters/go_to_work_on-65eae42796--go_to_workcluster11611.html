go to work on

verb
begin working on a task
3.2 1.56/m
Pronunciation: / …°o ä t…ô w…úrk …ën / (AmE), / …°…ô ä t…ô w…úÀêk …ín / (BrE)
to begin working seriously on a task or project.

Examples

To go to work on a project
She plans to go to work on her assignment as soon as she gets home.
The team quickly went to work on the proposal after receiving feedback from management.
We should go to work on fixing the car before it gets dark.
