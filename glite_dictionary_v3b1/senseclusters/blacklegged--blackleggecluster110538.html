blacklegged

adjective
having black legs
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌlɛɡd / (AmE), / ˈblækˌlɛɡd / (BrE)
having black legs, especially used to describe animals or insects with legs that are black in color

Examples

Blacklegged tick
The blacklegged tick is known to transmit Lyme disease.
We observed a blacklegged bird perched on the fence near the lake.
Some species of ants are easily identified by their blacklegged appearance.
