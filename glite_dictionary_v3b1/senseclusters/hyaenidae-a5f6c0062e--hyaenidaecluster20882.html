Hyaenidae

noun
the hyena family of mammals
Pronunciation: / haɪˈɛnɪˌdi / (AmE), / haɪˈiːnɪˌdiː / (BrE)
The biological family that comprises hyenas, carnivorous mammals native to Africa and Asia.

Examples

The Hyaenidae family
Fossils indicate that ancient members of Hyaenidae were once found in Europe as well.
Researchers have identified four living species within the Hyaenidae.
The Hyaenidae family is known for its unique bone-crushing jaws.
