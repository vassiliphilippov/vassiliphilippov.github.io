lantern-jawed

adjective
having a prominent, gaunt jaw
Pronunciation: / ˈlæn.tɚn dʒɑːd / (AmE), / ˈlæn.tən dʒɔːd / (BrE)
having a long, thin face with a prominent jaw that gives a gaunt or angular appearance

Examples

Lantern-jawed face
Her lantern-jawed appearance made her stand out in the crowd.
I recognized him immediately by his lantern-jawed features.
The old captain was a lantern-jawed man with a deep voice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: round-faced - having a face with a round, soft shape
  • synonym: square-jawed - having a broad and prominent jawline
