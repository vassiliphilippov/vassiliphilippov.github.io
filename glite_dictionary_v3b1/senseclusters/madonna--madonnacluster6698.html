madonna

noun
respected mother figure
7.0
Pronunciation: / məˈdɑnə / (AmE), / məˈdɒnə / (BrE)
a woman who is greatly respected and admired, often seen as a caring or motherly figure

Examples

The madonna figure
The villagers saw her as the madonna of their community, always offering help and guidance.
In the small town, she became a madonna figure to many children in need.
People admired her gentle nature, calling her the madonna of the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matron - a mature woman with an established social position
  • synonym: patroness - a woman who supports or protects others
