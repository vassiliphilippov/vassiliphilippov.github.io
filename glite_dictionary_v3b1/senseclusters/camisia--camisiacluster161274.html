camisia

noun
historical women's undergarment
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiːziə / (AmE), / kəˈmiːziə / (BrE)
a historical undergarment or simple shirt, often worn next to the skin under other clothes; especially a woman's shift or a man's tunic in medieval times
