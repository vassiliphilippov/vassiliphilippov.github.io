inclinometer

noun
measuring instrument for slope or dip angle
4.3 7.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.klɪn.ə.ˈme.tər/ (AmE), /ɪn.klɪn.ə.ˈme.tə/ (BrE)
an instrument for measuring the angle of slope, tilt, or elevation, especially in engineering, navigation, or construction
