jupati

noun
a tall Brazilian feather palm
Pronunciation: / dʒuːˈpɑːti / (AmE), / dʒuːˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a tall Brazilian feather palm with very large leathery pinnate leaves, whose strong stems are used for structural purposes

Examples

The jupati palm
Artisans weave baskets from the large leaves of the jupati tree.
The jupati is valued throughout Brazil for its structural versatility.
The villagers built shelters using the strong stems of the jupati palm.
