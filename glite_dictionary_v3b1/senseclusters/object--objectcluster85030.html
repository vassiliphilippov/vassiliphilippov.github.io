object

noun
purpose or goal aimed for
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈɒbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
a purpose or goal that someone aims to achieve; the reason for an action or activity

Examples

Main object of the plan
Her main object in moving to the city was to find better work opportunities.
The object of the game is to score more points than your opponent.
The object of this exercise is to improve your memory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aim - a goal or intended outcome
  • synonym: end - the final purpose or aim
  • synonym: goal - an aim or desired result
  • antonym: means - a method for achieving something, not the aim itself
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
