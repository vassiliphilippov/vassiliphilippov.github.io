hideousness

noun
extreme ugliness or repulsiveness
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdiəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɪdiəsnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being extremely ugly, unpleasant, or repulsive.

Examples

The hideousness of crime
Despite the hideousness of the crime scene, the detectives proceeded with their investigation.
She recoiled in shock at the hideousness of the mask.
The hideousness of the abandoned building made people avoid walking past it at night.
