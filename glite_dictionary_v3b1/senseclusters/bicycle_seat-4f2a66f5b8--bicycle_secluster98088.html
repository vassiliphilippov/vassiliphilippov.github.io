bicycle seat

noun
seat for a bicycle rider
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪsɪkəl siːt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ siːt / (BrE)
the part of a bicycle on which the rider sits

Examples

A cushioned bicycle seat
A cushioned bicycle seat can reduce discomfort during long rides.
She adjusted the bicycle seat to make her ride more comfortable.
The bicycle seat was worn out and needed to be replaced.
