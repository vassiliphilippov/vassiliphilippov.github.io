faltering

adjective
unsteady or hesitant
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔltərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːltərɪŋ / (BrE)
Lacking strength or steadiness, especially in speech or movement.

Examples

Faltering voice
He took a few faltering steps before regaining his balance.
Her faltering voice betrayed her anxiety about the interview.
The company's faltering progress worried investors.
