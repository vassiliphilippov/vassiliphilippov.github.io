dyspareunia

noun
painful intercourse
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪspəˈruːniə / (AmE), / ˌdɪspəˈruːniə / (BrE)
pain experienced during sexual intercourse

Examples

Dyspareunia during intercourse
She discussed her dyspareunia with her doctor to find possible causes and treatments.
Persistent dyspareunia can affect both physical and emotional well-being in relationships.
The specialist recommended therapy to help manage the patient's dyspareunia symptoms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anorgasmia - inability to achieve orgasm during sexual activity
  • synonym: vaginismus - involuntary muscle spasms causing painful intercourse
