Desmognathus

noun
a genus of dusky salamanders
Pronunciation: / dɛzˌmɑːɡˈneɪθəs / (AmE), / dɛzˌməɡˈneɪθəs / (BrE)
A genus of salamanders in the family Plethodontidae, known commonly as dusky salamanders, native to North America.
