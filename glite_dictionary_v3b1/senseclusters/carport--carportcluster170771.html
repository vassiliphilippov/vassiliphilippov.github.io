carport

noun
shelter for cars with open sides
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɔːt /; / ˈkɑːˌpɔːt / (BrE)
a shelter for one or more cars, usually with a flat roof supported by posts and open at the sides
