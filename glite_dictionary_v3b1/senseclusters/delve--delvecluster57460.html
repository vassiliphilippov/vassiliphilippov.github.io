delve

verb
to investigate deeply
4.7
Pronunciation: / dɛlv / (AmE), / dɛlv / (BrE)
to investigate or search deeply and thoroughly into a subject or topic

Examples

To delve into research
She decided to delve into the archives to uncover the town's hidden history.
The journalist delved into the details of the case to find the truth.
Before writing his book, he would often delve into scientific journals for research.
The journalist decided to delve into the details of the financial scandal.
Before writing her thesis, she had to delve into many scientific articles.
He loves to delve into history to uncover forgotten stories.

  • synonym: probe - to investigate or examine something closely
  • antonym: skim - to read or examine something quickly and superficially
