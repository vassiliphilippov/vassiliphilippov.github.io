rare

adjective
exceptional in character or quality
Pronunciation: / rɛr / (AmE), / reə / (BrE)
remarkable or exceptional in character, quality, or extent; unusually excellent or impressive

Examples

Rare talent
His paintings are admired for their rare beauty and originality.
It was a rare opportunity to meet someone so inspirational.
She possesses a rare talent for understanding complex scientific ideas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: common - ordinary or frequently occurring
  • synonym: exceptional - unusually good or outstanding
