ghost

noun
memory of a disturbing past experience
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊst / (AmE), / ɡəʊst / (BrE)
a faint, barely perceptible trace or suggestion of something; a very slight indication, or a memory or image of a past experience, especially one that is disturbing or associated with regret or trauma

Examples

A ghost of regret
Memories of the accident haunted her like a ghost every night.
The ghost of his past mistakes lingered and affected his decisions.
Try as he might, he could not shake the ghost of his childhood regrets.
Not even a ghost of an apology was offered after the mistake.
She felt a ghost of doubt as she entered the room.
There was a ghost of a smile on his lips as he listened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hint - a very slight or indirect indication
  • synonym: phantom - something that exists only as a mental image
  • synonym: specter - a haunting or disturbing image of the past
  • synonym: trace - a very small amount of something
