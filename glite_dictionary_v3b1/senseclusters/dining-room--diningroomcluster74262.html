dining-room

noun
room where meals are eaten
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or other building where meals are eaten

Examples

The dining-room table
The dining-room overlooks the garden and is decorated in warm colors.
They set the table in the dining-room for the party.
We gathered in the dining-room for Sunday lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kitchen - room where food is prepared and cooked
  • antonym: living room - room in a house for relaxing and socializing
