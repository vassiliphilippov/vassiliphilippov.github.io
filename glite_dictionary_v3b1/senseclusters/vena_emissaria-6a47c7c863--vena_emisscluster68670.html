vena emissaria

noun
vein linking skull sinuses to exterior veins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə ɪˈmɪs.ə.ri.ə / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə ɪˈmɪs.ə.ri.ə / (BrE)
any of several veins in the scalp and head that connect blood sinuses inside the skull with veins outside the skull

Examples

The vena emissaria vein
Infections can sometimes spread through the vena emissaria due to its connection between the scalp and intracranial structures.
The vena emissaria plays an important role in draining blood from inside the skull to the outside.
The surgeon carefully avoided the vena emissaria during the cranial procedure.
