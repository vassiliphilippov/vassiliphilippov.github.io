quantum

noun
a discrete quantity or amount
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑn.təm / (AmE), / ˈkwɒn.təm / (BrE)
a discrete or specific amount of something, especially used to refer to the smallest possible unit in physics or by analogy in other contexts

Examples
