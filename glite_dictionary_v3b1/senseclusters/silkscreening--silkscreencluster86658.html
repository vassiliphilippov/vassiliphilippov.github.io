silkscreening

noun
printing with mesh screen
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkˌskriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkˌskriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a printing technique in which ink is pushed through a mesh screen to create an image on a surface

Examples

Silkscreening a T-shirt
She took a class in silkscreening to design her own T-shirts.
Many artists use silkscreening to produce colorful posters and prints.
The process of silkscreening allows for bold and vibrant images on fabric.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lithography - a printing process using a flat surface treated to repel ink except where needed
  • synonym: screen printing - a method of printing using a mesh screen to transfer ink
