vena ophthalmica

noun
vein draining the eye
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə ɑːf.ˈθæl.mɪ.kə/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə ɒf.ˈθæl.mɪ.kə/ (BrE)
Either of two veins that drain blood from the eye and empty into the cavernous sinus.

Examples

Vena ophthalmica vein
Damage to the vena ophthalmica can lead to serious complications affecting vision.
Infections can sometimes spread from the face to the brain via the vena ophthalmica.
The vena ophthalmica plays a significant role in draining blood from the eye region.
