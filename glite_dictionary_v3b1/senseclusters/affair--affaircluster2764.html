affair

noun
secret or illicit sexual relationship
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛr / (AmE), / əˈfeə / (BrE)
a sexual relationship, often secret or illicit, especially when at least one partner is married to someone else

Examples

A secret affair
Rumors spread quickly when news of their affair became public.
She ended her marriage after discovering her husband's secret affair.
The novel centers around an affair between two colleagues at work.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fling - a short, casual sexual relationship
  • synonym: relationship - a romantic or sexual involvement with someone
