genus Sciurus

noun
genus of typical tree squirrels
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs saɪˈjʊərəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs sɪˈjʊərəs / (BrE)
the principal genus of the family Sciuridae, including the typical moderate-sized arboreal squirrels found in many parts of the world
