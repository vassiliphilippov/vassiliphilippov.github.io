mandola

noun
a stringed instrument like a large mandolin
6.2 7.4
Pronunciation: /mæn.ˈdoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /mæn.ˈdəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
a musical instrument of the lute family, similar to but larger than a mandolin, with a deeper tone

Examples

A mandola instrument

The musician played a beautiful melody on his mandola during the concert.
Unlike the mandolin, the mandola produces a richer, lower sound.
