plantable

adjective
suitable for planting
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈplæntəbəl / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntəbəl / (BrE)
suitable for being planted or able to be placed in soil to grow

Examples

Plantable pots
These biodegradable pots are plantable and can be placed directly into the garden.
Only plantable bulbs should be stored for next season's flowers.
The soil must be prepared before any plantable seeds are added.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unplantable - not suitable for planting
