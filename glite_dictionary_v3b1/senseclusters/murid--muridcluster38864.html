murid

noun
Sufi disciple
Pronunciation: / muˈriːd / (AmE), / mʊˈriːd / (BrE)
a follower or disciple in Sufism who seeks spiritual guidance from a teacher or master

Examples

A Sufi murid
The murid listened carefully to the teachings of his Sufi master.
Each murid is expected to follow the spiritual practices given by their guide.
As a murid, she dedicated herself to daily meditation and study.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disciple - a follower or student of a teacher or leader
  • antonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser
