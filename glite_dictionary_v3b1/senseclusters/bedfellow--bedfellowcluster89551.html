bedfellow

noun
temporary associate or partner
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a person or thing closely connected with another, often temporarily or unexpectedly, for a particular purpose

Examples

Unlikely bedfellows
Environmental groups and oil companies became unlikely bedfellows during the negotiations.
In politics, unusual alliances can make bedfellows of rivals.
The crisis created strange bedfellows in the business world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partner - a person who takes part in an activity with another
