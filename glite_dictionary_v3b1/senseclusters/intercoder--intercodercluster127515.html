intercoder

adjective
between coders
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkoʊdər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkˈəʊdə / (BrE)
relating to the comparison, agreement, or differences between the work of two or more coders.

Examples

Intercoder agreement
The researchers calculated intercoder reliability to ensure consistency in their data analysis.
A high level of intercoder agreement is important for the validity of the study.
They met to resolve intercoder differences found during the coding process.
