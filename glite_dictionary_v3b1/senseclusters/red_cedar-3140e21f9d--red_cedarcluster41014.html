red cedar

noun
a juniper tree native to eastern North America
3.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àår…õd ÀàsiÀêd…ôr / (AmE), / Àår…õd ÀàsiÀêd…ô / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree of the juniper family, native to eastern North America, with a conical shape, reddish aromatic wood, and needle-like leaves.

Examples

Eastern red cedar tree
Many birds nest in the dense foliage of the red cedar tree.
The aromatic wood of the red cedar is often used to make chests and closets.
We planted a row of red cedars along the eastern fence for privacy and shade.

  • Juniperus virginiana

  • red juniper
