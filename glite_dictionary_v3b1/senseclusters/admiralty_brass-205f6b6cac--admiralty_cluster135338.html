Admiralty brass

noun
a corrosion-resistant brass alloy
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.mɚ.əl.ti ˈbræs / (AmE), / ˌæd.mɪ.rəl.ti ˈbrɑːs / (BrE)
