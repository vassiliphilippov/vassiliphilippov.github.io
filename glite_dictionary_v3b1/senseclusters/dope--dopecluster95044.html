dope

verb
use drugs to enhance sports performance
6.2
Pronunciation: / doʊp /; / ˈdoʊpɪŋ / (AmE), / dəʊp /; / ˈdəʊpɪŋ / (BrE)
to give or take drugs (especially illegally) to influence performance, such as in sports or with animals

Examples

There are strict penalties for athletes who are found to be doping.
The athlete was banned for doping before the championship race.
Officials are increasing efforts to catch those doping in professional sports.
He denied ever doping during his entire career.
