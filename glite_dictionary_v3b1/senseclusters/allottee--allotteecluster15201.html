allottee

noun
recipient of an allotment
Pronunciation: / əˌlɑːˈtiː / (AmE), / əˌlɒˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is officially given or assigned something, such as land, property, shares, or rights

Examples

The allottee receives shares
Each allottee was given a certificate confirming their ownership of the new shares.
The government announced the names of the allottees who would receive land in the housing project.
As an allottee, she was responsible for maintaining the property she had been assigned.
Each allottee received a certificate for their new shares.
The government announced the names of the land allottees after the lottery.
As an allottee, she was responsible for maintaining the property she received.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allocator - a person or entity that allots or assigns something
  • antonym: grantor - a person who gives or assigns something
  • synonym: recipient - a person who receives something
