Synonyms

    7.3 0.04/m

  • luxuriously
    5.1 0.33/m
    luxuriously focuses on comfort and elegant living, while richly describes lavish look, texture, or strong abundance more broadly.
  • lavishly
    5.6 1.41/m
    lavishly shows great generosity or excess spending, while richly highlights richness in appearance, detail, or quality.
  • luxuriantly
    7.2 0.11/m
    luxuriantly often pictures thick growth or abundant detail, while richly focuses on wealth, luxury, or ornate decoration.
  • lushly
    4.7 0.07/m
