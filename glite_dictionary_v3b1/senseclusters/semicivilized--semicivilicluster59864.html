semicivilized

adjective
partly civilized
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
partly developed in culture or manners but not fully civilized

Examples

A semicivilized society
The early settlers considered the region to be semicivilized compared to their homeland.
Some historians described the ancient kingdom as semicivilized due to its mix of traditions.
The novel portrays a semicivilized society struggling to adopt new customs.
