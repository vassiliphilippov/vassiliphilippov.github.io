fibrositis

noun
inflammation of fibrous tissue, esp. in muscles
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbrəˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of white fibrous tissue, especially muscle sheaths, often causing pain and stiffness

Examples

Fibrositis pain
Doctors sometimes confuse fibrositis with other muscular disorders due to similar symptoms.
Regular physical therapy can help relieve the symptoms of fibrositis.
The patient complained of chronic muscle pain, which was later diagnosed as fibrositis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: myositis - inflammation of muscle tissue
  • synonym: tendinitis - inflammation of a tendon
