incommensurate

adjective
not matching in extent or degree
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kəˈmɛn.sər.ət / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kəˈmɛn.sjər.ət / (BrE)
not corresponding in size, degree, or extent; disproportionate or inadequate compared to something else.

Examples

Incommensurate salary and work
His responsibilities at work are incommensurate with the salary he receives.
Many felt the punishment was incommensurate with the minor offense committed.
The effort required was incommensurate to the minimal results achieved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commensurate - corresponding in size or degree; proportionate
