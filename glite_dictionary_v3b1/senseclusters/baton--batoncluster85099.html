baton

noun
police officer's club or stick
5.5
Pronunciation: / bəˈtɑn / (AmE), / bəˈtɒn / (BrE)
a short, thick stick carried and used as a weapon by police officers

Examples

Police baton
Police officers are trained in the safe use of the baton to subdue suspects.
The officer carried a baton at his side during his patrol.
The protest turned violent when someone tried to snatch a baton from the police.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truncheon - a thick stick carried as a weapon by police officers (British use)
