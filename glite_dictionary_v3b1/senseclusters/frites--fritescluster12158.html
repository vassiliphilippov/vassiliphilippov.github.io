frites

noun
thin fried potatoes
Pronunciation: / frits / (AmE), / frit / (BrE)
thin, crispy pieces of fried potato, especially as served in Belgium and France

Examples

A plate of frites
We ordered a plate of frites with our burgers.
The restaurant is famous for its homemade frites served with a variety of sauces.
In Belgium, people often eat frites as a snack on the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: French fries - thin strips of deep-fried potato
  • synonym: chips - fried potato strips, especially in British English
