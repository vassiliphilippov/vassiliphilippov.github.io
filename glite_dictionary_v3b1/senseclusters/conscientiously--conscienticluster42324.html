conscientiously

adverb
in a careful and responsible manner
5.4 0.72/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀên. ÉiÀàen. É…ôs.li / (AmE), / Àåk…ín. ÉiÀàen. É…ôs.li / (BrE)
In a thorough and careful way, showing great attention to duty or responsibility.

Examples

Work conscientiously
He always completes his assignments conscientiously, never missing a step.
She conscientiously reviewed every detail of the report before submitting it.
The nurse conscientiously attended to her patients throughout her shift.
