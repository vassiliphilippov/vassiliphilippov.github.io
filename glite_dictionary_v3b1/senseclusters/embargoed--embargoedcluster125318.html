embargoed

verb
to prohibit trade or release
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊd / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊd / (BrE)
to officially prohibit trade with a country or prevent the release of information, goods, or services.

Examples

To embargo trade goods
The government embargoed all shipments to that country after the sanctions were announced.
Sensitive documents were embargoed until the official press conference took place.
The company embargoed the release of its new product due to safety concerns.

  • synonym: ban - to officially forbid something
