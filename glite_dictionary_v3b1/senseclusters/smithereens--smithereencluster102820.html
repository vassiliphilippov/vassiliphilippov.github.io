smithereens

noun
small pieces or fragments
9.2
Pronunciation: / smɪðəˈrinz / (AmE), / smɪðəˈriːnz / (BrE)
Small pieces or fragments, especially as a result of being smashed or blown apart.

Examples

Smithereens blown apart
After the explosion, the old shed was blown to smithereens.
Her phone fell and smashed into smithereens on the pavement.
The villain threatened to blast the safe to smithereens if his demands were not met.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fragments - small pieces broken off or separated from something
  • synonym: shards - broken pieces, usually of glass, ceramic, or metal
  • antonym: whole - something complete, not broken into pieces
