tonoplast

noun
vacuole membrane
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊnəˌplæst / (AmE), / ˈtəʊnəˌplɑːst /; / ˈtɒnəˌplɑːst / (BrE)
the membrane that surrounds the vacuole in a plant cell, controlling what moves in and out
