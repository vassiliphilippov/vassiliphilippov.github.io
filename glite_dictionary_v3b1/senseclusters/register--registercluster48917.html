register

noun
official record book
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
an official written record or book where names, events, or transactions are listed

Examples

A hotel register
Every visitor must sign the register at the front desk.
The school keeps a daily register of student attendance.
The wedding register was filled with well-wishes from guests.
All students must sign their names in the register every morning.
The birth register contains records dating back to the nineteenth century.
The hotel maintains a register of all guests who have stayed there.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ledger - a book or other collection of financial accounts
  • synonym: logbook - a book for recording details of a journey or event
