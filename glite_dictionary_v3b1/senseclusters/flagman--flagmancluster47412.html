flagman

noun
worksite flag marker
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈflæɡ.mən/ /; / /ˈflæɡ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈflæɡ.mən/ /; / /ˈflæɡ.mæn/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to place flags to mark positions, hazards, or underground utilities at a worksite or for surveying

Examples

Utility flagman
The flagman carefully marked the area to show where the gas lines were buried.
Before the construction began, a flagman placed colored flags to indicate safe zones.
Surveyors relied on the flagman to identify important points along the new road.
