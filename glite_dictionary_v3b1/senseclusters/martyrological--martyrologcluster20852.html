martyrological

adjective
about martyrs' history
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrtiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɑːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study, record, or history of martyrs and their suffering

Examples

Martyrological records
The church archives contain many martyrological documents from the early centuries.
She wrote a martyrological essay about the persecution of religious minorities.
A martyrological perspective can help us understand the sacrifices made by these individuals.
