preheat

verb
heat in advance, especially an oven
2.1 7.00/m
Pronunciation: / priÀàhit / (AmE), / priÀêÀàhiÀêt / (BrE)
to heat (an appliance, especially an oven) to a particular temperature before using it

Examples

To preheat the oven
Always preheat the oven before baking a cake for best results.
She forgot to preheat the pan, so the pancakes took longer to cook.
The recipe says to preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

Synonyms
