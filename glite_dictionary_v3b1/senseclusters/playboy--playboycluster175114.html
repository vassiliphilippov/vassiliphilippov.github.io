playboy

noun
a man devoted to pleasure and leisure
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a wealthy man who spends his time enjoying himself and seeking pleasure, especially in the company of women

Examples

A playboy lifestyle
Despite his playboy image, he was also known for his generosity to charities.
He gained a reputation as a playboy during his younger years, rarely taking anything too seriously.
The millionaire lived the life of a playboy, traveling the world and attending lavish parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philanderer - a man who frequently enters into casual romantic relationships
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
