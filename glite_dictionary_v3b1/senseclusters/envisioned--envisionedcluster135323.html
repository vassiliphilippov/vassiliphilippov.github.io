envisioned

adjective
imagined or pictured in the mind
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪʒənd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪʒənd / (BrE)
imagined or conceived in the mind, especially as a mental image of a possible future or scenario

Examples

An envisioned future
In his speech, the president described an envisioned future of prosperity and peace.
She worked tirelessly toward her envisioned goal of becoming a doctor.
The company presented its envisioned design for the new building to investors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagined - formed in the mind; not real but thought of
  • antonym: real - actually existing or happening; not imagined
