hamburger bun

noun
bread roll for hamburgers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmˌbɝɡɚ bʌn / (AmE), / ˈhæmbəːɡə bʌn / (BrE)
a round, soft bread roll sliced horizontally and typically used to hold a hamburger patty and its accompaniments
