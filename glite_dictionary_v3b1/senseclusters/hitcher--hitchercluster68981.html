hitcher

noun
person seeking rides
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈhɪtʃə / (BrE)
a person who tries to get a free ride from passing vehicles by standing at the roadside and signaling drivers

Traveling as a hitcher can be unpredictable but also full of adventure.
