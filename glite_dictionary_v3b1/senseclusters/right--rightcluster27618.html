right

adverb
toward/on the right side; sometimes figurative
Pronunciation: / raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
Toward or on the right side; in the direction of the right, sometimes used figuratively to indicate conservative or right-wing positions.

Examples

Turn right
He glanced right before crossing the street to check for oncoming cars.
The debate has shifted right over the past decade, especially on economic issues.
Turn right at the intersection and continue until you reach the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: left - toward or on the left side; opposite of 'right'
