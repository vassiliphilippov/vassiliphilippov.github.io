Mahican

noun
a Native American people from the Hudson Valley
9.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈhɪ.kən/ /; / /ˈmɑː.hɪ.kən/ (AmE), /mə.ˈhɪ.kən/ /; / /ˈmɑː.hɪ.kən/ (BrE)
