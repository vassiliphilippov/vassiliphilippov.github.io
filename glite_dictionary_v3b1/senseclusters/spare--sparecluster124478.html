spare

noun
extra item or replacement part
3.1
Pronunciation: / spɛr / (AmE), / spɛə / (BrE)
an extra thing kept in reserve to use as a replacement if needed (such as a tire or part)

Examples

A spare tire
Always carry a spare tire in your car when traveling long distances.
Do you have a spare for this machine in case it breaks down?
The mechanic kept several spares in his toolbox so he could fix problems quickly.
He had to use the spare because he drove over a sharp nail.
Make sure your spare is properly inflated before taking a long road trip.
She opened the trunk to get the spare when her tire went flat on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replacement - something that takes the place of another
  • synonym: replacement tire - a tire used to replace a damaged one
