Bradley method of childbirth

noun
natural childbirth preparation method
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbrædli ˌmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldbɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈbrædli ˌmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldbɜːθ / (BrE)
a method of preparing for natural childbirth through education, exercises, nutrition, breathing techniques, and relaxation, with emphasis on the father's involvement

Examples

The Bradley method of childbirth

The Bradley method of childbirth emphasizes nutrition and breathing techniques during pregnancy and delivery.
