cytostatic

adjective
inhibiting cell growth
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtəˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəˈstætɪk / (BrE)
inhibiting or slowing the growth and division of cells

Examples

Cytostatic effect on cells
The doctor recommended a cytostatic medication to help control the spread of cancer cells.
Researchers are developing new cytostatic agents to treat aggressive tumors.
Some chemotherapy drugs have a cytostatic effect rather than killing the cells outright.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiproliferative - inhibiting cell growth or reproduction
  • antonym: cytotoxic - toxic to cells; causing cell death
