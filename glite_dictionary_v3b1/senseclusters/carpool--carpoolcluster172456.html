carpool

verb
to share rides
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrˌpul / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌpuːl / (BrE)
to share a car ride with other people, especially to save money or reduce traffic

Examples

If you carpool with neighbors, you can take turns driving each week.
Many employees carpool to the office to reduce their transportation costs.
We started to carpool with our neighbors to help the environment.
If you carpool, you can use the special lane on the highway.
