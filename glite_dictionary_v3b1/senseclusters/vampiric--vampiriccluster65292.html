vampiric

adjective
like a vampire
7.9
Pronunciation: / væmˈpɪrɪk / (AmE), / væmˈpɪrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a vampire, especially in appearance, behavior, or atmosphere

The novel's vampiric themes attracted fans of gothic horror.
