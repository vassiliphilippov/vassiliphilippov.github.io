famous

adjective
widely known and esteemed
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪməs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪməs / (BrE)
known about by many people, especially for notable achievements or qualities.

Examples

A famous person
Her grandfather was a famous poet who inspired many young writers.
That restaurant is famous for its homemade pasta and desserts.
The city is famous for its ancient ruins and vibrant festivals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infamous - well known for being bad or evil
  • synonym: renowned - known and admired by many people
