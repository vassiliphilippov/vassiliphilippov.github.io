boca

noun
Spanish word for mouth
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊkə / (AmE), / ˈbəʊkə / (BrE)
a Spanish word meaning 'mouth', often used in English place names, especially in the United States.

Examples

Boca Raton
The city of Boca Raton is famous for its beautiful beaches.
Many places in the southwestern United States have names that include boca.
We stopped at Boca Chica to watch the rocket launch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mouth - the opening in the face used for eating and speaking
