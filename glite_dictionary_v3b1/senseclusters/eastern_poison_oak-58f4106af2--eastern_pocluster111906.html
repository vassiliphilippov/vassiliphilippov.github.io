eastern poison oak

noun
poisonous shrub causing skin rash
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈistərn ˈpɔɪzn oʊk / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈpɔɪzn əʊk / (BrE)
a poisonous shrub native to the southeastern United States that causes skin irritation or a rash upon contact

Examples

The eastern poison oak plant
After brushing against eastern poison oak, she developed an itchy rash on her arm.
Hikers should avoid touching eastern poison oak because its oil can trigger an allergic reaction.
The forest ranger pointed out the dense patch of eastern poison oak along the trail.
