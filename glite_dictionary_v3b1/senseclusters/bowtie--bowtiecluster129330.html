bowtie

noun
a necktie tied in a bow, worn by men
5.1 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊ.taɪ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.taɪ / (BrE)
a narrow, shoelace-like necktie tied in a bow, worn especially by men on formal occasions

Examples

A bowtie for men
Her grandfather collected colorful bowties from all over the world.
James wore a black bowtie with his tuxedo to the wedding.
The magician adjusted his red bowtie before stepping onto the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascot - a broad necktie with wide pointed wings, often folded over and secured with a pin
  • synonym: cravat - a strip of fabric worn as a necktie, especially in earlier fashion
  • synonym: necktie - a piece of cloth worn around the neck, tied in a knot at the front
