downcast

adjective
feeling sad or discouraged
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnkɑːst / (BrE)
sad, discouraged, or lacking in hope; feeling despondent

Examples

Downcast mood
After losing the championship, the team appeared downcast in the locker room.
His downcast demeanor made everyone around him worry.
She looked downcast when she heard the disappointing news.

  • synonym: dejected - sad and depressed; dispirited
