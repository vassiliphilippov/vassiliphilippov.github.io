save energy

verb
use less energy; conserve energy
3.7
Pronunciation: / seɪv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / seɪv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
to use less energy or to reduce the amount of energy you use, often by being more efficient or conserving effort

Examples

To save energy efficiently
Cyclists often draft behind others in a race to save energy during the early laps.
Turning off the lights when you leave a room can help you save energy.
Using public transportation is a great way to save energy and reduce your carbon footprint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conserve energy - to use energy carefully in order to avoid waste
