ghostland

noun
haunted place
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstˌlænd / (BrE)
a place thought to be inhabited or haunted by ghosts or spirits

Examples

Haunted ghostland
The old castle was rumored to be a ghostland where strange noises echoed at night.
Tourists visited the deserted village, fascinated by its reputation as a ghostland.
After dark, the forest felt like a ghostland filled with mysterious shadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirit world - a realm believed to be inhabited by spirits
