giant foxtail

noun
a weed grass (Setaria faberi) widespread in the US
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈfɑkˌsteɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈfɒksteɪl / (BrE)
