breathe

verb
allow air to pass through (material)
5.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /briːð/ /; / /brɪð/ (AmE), /briːð/ (BrE)
(of a material or fabric) to allow air to pass through, enabling ventilation.

Examples

Let fabric breathe
Cotton allows the skin to breathe better than synthetic fabrics.
Good running shoes need to breathe to keep your feet cool and dry.
This sports jacket is made from a fabric that can breathe during intense activity.
