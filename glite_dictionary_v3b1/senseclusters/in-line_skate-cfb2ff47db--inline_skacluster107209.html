in-line skate

noun
boot with wheels in a single line
8.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.laɪn skeɪt/ /; / /ɪnˈlaɪn skeɪt/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.laɪn skeɪt/ /; / /ɪnˈlaɪn skeɪt/ (BrE)
A boot with a single line of small wheels attached to the sole, used for skating on smooth surfaces.
