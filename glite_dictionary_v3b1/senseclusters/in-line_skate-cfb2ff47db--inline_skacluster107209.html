in-line skate

noun
boot with wheels in a single line
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn laɪn skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn laɪn skeɪt / (BrE)
A boot with a single line of small wheels attached to the sole, used for skating on smooth surfaces.

Examples

A pair of in-line skates
He put on his in-line skates and headed to the park for some exercise.
She learned how to balance on one in-line skate before moving onto both.
The store carries several brands of in-line skates for beginners and experts alike.

Related Terms
