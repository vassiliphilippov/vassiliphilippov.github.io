Kirghiz

noun
member of a Central Asian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / kɪrˈɡiz / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡiːz / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic-speaking ethnic group native to Kyrgyzstan and neighboring regions of Central Asia

Examples
