viability

noun
ability to grow and develop (biology)
6.7 5.3
Pronunciation: /vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /vaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
