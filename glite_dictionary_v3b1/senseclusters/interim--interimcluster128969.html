interim

adjective
temporary; for an intervening period
6.3 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.təˌrɪm / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tər.ɪm / (BrE)
serving or intended to serve for a short period until something more permanent is established; temporary.

Examples

Interim manager
An interim solution was put in place until the full system was operational.
The company appointed an interim manager while searching for a permanent replacement.
They signed an interim agreement to keep negotiations moving forward.

Related Terms
