umbellifer

noun
plant in the parsley family
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɛlɪfər / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɛlɪfə / (BrE)
any of a large group of aromatic plants in the parsley family (Umbelliferae), typically with umbrella-shaped flower clusters.

Examples

Umbellifers like parsley
Many common herbs, such as parsley and dill, are actually umbellifers.
Some umbellifers have medicinal properties used in traditional remedies.
The carrot is a well-known edible umbellifer cultivated worldwide.
