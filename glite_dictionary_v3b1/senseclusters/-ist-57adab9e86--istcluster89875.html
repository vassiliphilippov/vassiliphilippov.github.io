-ist

adjective
relating to an ideology
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪst / (AmE), / ɪst / (BrE)
used as a suffix to form adjectives meaning 'relating to or characteristic of a particular ideology, movement, style, theory, or person'.

Examples

-ist style
The term 'feminist' uses the -ist suffix to show a connection to feminism.
A 'cubist' painting is one that is cubist in style, relating to the Cubism movement.
Her views are often described as 'realist', with the -ist suffix indicating a philosophical approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -ic - suffix forming adjectives meaning 'of or pertaining to'
