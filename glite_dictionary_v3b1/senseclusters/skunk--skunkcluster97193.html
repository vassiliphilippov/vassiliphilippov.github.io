skunk

noun
small mammal that sprays a foul odor
5.6
Pronunciation: / skʌŋk / (AmE), / skʌŋk / (BrE)
A small North American mammal known for its ability to spray a foul-smelling liquid as a defense mechanism.

Examples

A skunk sprays
Skunks are known for their distinctive black and white stripes.
A skunk crossed the road in front of our car last night.
The dog got sprayed by a skunk and smelled terrible for days.
