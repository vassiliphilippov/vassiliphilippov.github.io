wide-body aircraft

noun
large twin-aisle airliner
6.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /waɪd ˈbɑː.di ˈer.kræft/ (AmE), /waɪd ˈbɒd.i ˈeə.krɑːft/ (BrE)
