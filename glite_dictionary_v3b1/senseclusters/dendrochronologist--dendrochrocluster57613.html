dendrochronologist

noun
tree ring scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛndroʊkrəˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌdɛndrəʊkrəˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies tree rings to determine the age of trees and past environmental conditions

Examples

A dendrochronologist studies rings
The dendrochronologist examined the ancient timber to estimate when the building was constructed.
A dendrochronologist can reveal past climate changes by analyzing tree ring patterns.
Museums often consult a dendrochronologist to help date wooden artifacts accurately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archaeologist - a scientist who studies human history through artifacts
  • synonym: paleobotanist - a scientist who studies ancient plants
