frail

noun
basket for dried fruit
2.1
Pronunciation: / freɪl / (AmE), / freɪl / (BrE)
a basket, especially one made of rushes, used for holding dried fruit such as raisins or figs

Examples

A frail of raisins
A frail of dates sat in the corner, ready to be weighed and sold.
The worker filled a frail with freshly dried raisins from the sun.
Traders carried frails of figs to the market each morning in the old town.
