annulet

noun
ring-shaped molding on a column
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.jə.lət / (AmE), / ˈanjʊlɪt / (BrE)
A small, ring-shaped molding, often found at the top of a column in classical architecture.

Examples

Column annulet
A simple annulet encircles the top of the pillar, just below the capital.
Restorers carefully repaired each annulet to preserve the column's classical style.
The fluted shaft is decorated with an annulet near its capital.
