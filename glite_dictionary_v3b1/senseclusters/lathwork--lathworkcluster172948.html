lathwork

noun
wood or metal strips
Pronunciation: / ˈlæθˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈlɑːθˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a structure or surface made from thin strips of wood or metal arranged in a pattern, often used as a support or decoration

Examples

Wooden lathwork pattern
The craftsman repaired the damaged lathwork on the old garden fence.
Sunlight filtered through the decorative lathwork above the porch.
During renovations, they discovered original lathwork hidden behind the plaster ceiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framework - a basic supporting structure
  • synonym: lattice - a structure of crossed strips forming a pattern
