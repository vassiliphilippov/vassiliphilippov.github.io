Turkish coffee

noun
traditional unfiltered coffee drink
4.1 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜr.kɪʃ ˈkɑ.fi / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.kɪʃ ˈkɒf.i / (BrE)
A traditional style of coffee made by boiling finely ground coffee beans, often with sugar, resulting in a strong, unfiltered drink.

Examples

A cup of Turkish coffee
A small cup of Turkish coffee is often served with a piece of sweet lokum.
Many people enjoy Turkish coffee after a meal because of its rich flavor.
She learned how to prepare authentic Turkish coffee during her trip to Istanbul.

Related Terms

  • synonym: espresso - Strong coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans
