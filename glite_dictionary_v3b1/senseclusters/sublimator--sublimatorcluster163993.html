sublimator

noun
one who redirects impulses
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌblɪˌmeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsʌblɪˌmeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who redirects strong emotions or unacceptable impulses into socially acceptable or creative activities

Examples

Creative sublimator
As a skilled sublimator, she turned her anxiety into beautiful music.
The therapist praised him for being a sublimator rather than acting on his anger.
Many artists are natural sublimators, channeling strong feelings into their creative work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impulsive - a person who acts on urges without redirecting them
  • synonym: reformer - a person who makes changes to improve something
