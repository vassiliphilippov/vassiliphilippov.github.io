flower cluster

noun
group of closely growing flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊər ˌklʌstər / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə ˌklʌstə / (BrE)
a group of flowers growing closely together on a plant; an inflorescence

Examples

A flower cluster on plant
A single flower cluster contains many small blossoms tightly packed together.
Pollinators are attracted to the bright flower clusters of the plant.
The lilac bush was covered in fragrant flower clusters each spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflorescence - the arrangement of flowers on a plant
