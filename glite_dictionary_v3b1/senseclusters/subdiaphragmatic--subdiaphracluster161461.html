subdiaphragmatic

adjective
below the diaphragm
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbdaɪəfrəgˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌbdaɪəfrəgˈmætɪk / (BrE)
located or occurring below the diaphragm in the body

Examples

A subdiaphragmatic mass
The doctor identified a subdiaphragmatic mass during the ultrasound examination.
Treatment was required for the patient's subdiaphragmatic infection after surgery.
A subdiaphragmatic abscess can cause pain and fever if not treated promptly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: supradiaphragmatic - located or occurring above the diaphragm
