Fahrenheit thermometer

noun
thermometer using Fahrenheit scale
Pronunciation: / fəˈrɛn.haɪt θərˈmɑ.mə.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / fəˈrɛn.haɪt θəˈmɒ.mɪ.tə / (BrE)
a thermometer that is graduated according to the Fahrenheit temperature scale.

Examples

A Fahrenheit thermometer
She bought a Fahrenheit thermometer for her kitchen to ensure food safety.
The laboratory used a Fahrenheit thermometer to record the temperature changes during the experiment.
We compared the readings of a Fahrenheit thermometer with those of a Celsius one.
