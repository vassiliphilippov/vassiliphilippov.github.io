noil

verb
to remove short fibers
9.9
Pronunciation: /nɔɪl/ (AmE), /nɔɪl/ (BrE)
to remove short fibers from wool, silk, or other textile fibers during processing

Examples

To noil the wool
Before spinning, workers noil the wool to ensure only the longest fibers remain.
The factory uses machines to noil silk, improving the quality of the final fabric.
It is important to noil the fibers thoroughly for a smoother yarn.
