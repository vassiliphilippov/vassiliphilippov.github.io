cosmopolitical

adjective
global political ideas
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to global or universal political ideas, systems, or concerns that affect the whole world

Examples

Cosmopolitical ideas
The organization promotes cosmopolitical solutions to address climate change on a global scale.
Her cosmopolitical perspective influenced her work in international relations.
A cosmopolitical approach encourages cooperation among nations for the common good.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmopolitan - including or relating to people from many different countries
  • antonym: nationalistic - having strong identification with one's own nation and interests
