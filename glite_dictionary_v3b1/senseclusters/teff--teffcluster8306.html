teff

noun
African cereal grass and edible grain
9.9 7.9
Pronunciation: /tef/ (AmE), /tef/ (BrE)
A fine grain from an African grass, important as a cereal crop for its edible seeds and used for forage and hay.

Examples

Teff grain
Ethiopian injera is traditionally made from teff flour, giving it a unique taste and texture.
Farmers in East Africa often grow teff because it thrives in poor soils and variable climates.
The nutritional profile of teff makes it a popular choice for health-conscious consumers worldwide.
