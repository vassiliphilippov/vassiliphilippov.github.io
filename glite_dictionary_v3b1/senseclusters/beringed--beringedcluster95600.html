beringed

adjective
having a ring attached
Pronunciation: / bɪˈrɪŋd / (AmE), / bɪˈrɪŋd / (BrE)
wearing or fitted with a ring or metal band, especially for identification purposes, as with birds or animals.

Examples

Beringed bird
The scientist tracked the beringed owl as it migrated across the continent.
Each beringed sheep in the flock could be easily identified by its colored tag.
A beringed dove was spotted in the garden, its metal band glinting in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ringed - having one or more rings attached or encircling
  • antonym: unmarked - not marked or identified with a ring or band
