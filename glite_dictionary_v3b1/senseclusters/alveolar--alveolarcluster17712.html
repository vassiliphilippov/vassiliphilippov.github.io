alveolar

adjective
relating to lung air sacs
Pronunciation: / ælˈviələr / (AmE), / ælˈviːələ / (BrE)
Relating to the small air sacs in the lungs where gas exchange occurs.

Examples

Alveolar sacs
Doctors observed inflammation in the alveolar region of the patient's lungs.
Smoking can cause damage to alveolar tissue, leading to breathing difficulties.
The alveolar structure plays a critical role in the respiratory process.
