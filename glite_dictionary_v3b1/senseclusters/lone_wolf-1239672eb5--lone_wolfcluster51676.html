lone wolf

noun
person who prefers to act alone
1.7 4.1
Pronunciation: /loʊn wʊlf/ (AmE), /ləʊn wʊlf/ (BrE)
a person who prefers to act or work alone rather than with others

Examples

A lone wolf worker
As a lone wolf in the business world, she built her company from the ground up.
Despite being a lone wolf at school, he made a few close friends over time.
She has always been a lone wolf, preferring to work on projects by herself.
Although he was respected at work, Mark remained a lone wolf who rarely joined social events.
He found success as a lone wolf author, writing and publishing his novels independently.
She has always been a lone wolf, preferring to travel the world by herself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individualist - someone who acts independently or is self-reliant
  • synonym: introvert - a person who tends to be more focused on internal thoughts and feelings than external sources of stimulation
  • antonym: social butterfly - a person who is socially active and enjoys being around people
  • antonym: team player - a person who works well as a member of a team
