homework

noun
schoolwork done at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌwɜːk / (BrE)
work assigned to be completed at home, especially by students as part of their studies.

Examples

To do homework
It's important to check your homework for errors before handing it in.
She finished her homework before dinner so she could watch television.
Teachers assign homework to help students practice what they've learned in class.
