monkeyface

noun
animal with monkey-like face
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkiˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌfeɪs / (BrE)
a common name for certain animals whose face resembles that of a monkey

Examples

Monkeyface fish
The monkeyface prickleback is a fish found along the Pacific coast.
Some people call the flower with a face-like pattern a monkeyface.
The guide pointed out a monkeyface eel hiding among the rocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prickleback - a type of elongated fish, some species called monkeyface
