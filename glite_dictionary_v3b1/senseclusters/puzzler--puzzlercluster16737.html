puzzler

noun
puzzle enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌzlər / (AmE), / ˈpʌzlə / (BrE)

Examples

A skilled puzzler
As a lifelong puzzler, he spends hours working on crosswords every weekend.
The club welcomes any puzzler interested in brainteasers and logic games.
Every puzzler in the group brought a different type of challenge to the meeting.
