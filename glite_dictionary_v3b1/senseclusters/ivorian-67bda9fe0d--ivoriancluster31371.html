Ivorian

noun
person from Côte d'Ivoire
5.2
Pronunciation: / aɪˈvɔːriən / (AmE), / aɪˈvɔːriən / (BrE)
a person who was born in, or is a citizen or native of, Côte d'Ivoire

Examples

An Ivorian footballer
The famous footballer is an Ivorian who has played for several European clubs.
Many Ivorians celebrate their Independence Day with music and traditional food.
An Ivorian artist exhibited her paintings at the international art fair.
