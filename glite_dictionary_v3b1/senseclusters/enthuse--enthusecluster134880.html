enthuse

verb
express with enthusiasm
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈθuz / (AmE), / ɪnˈθjuːz / (BrE)
to speak or express oneself with enthusiasm; to say something excitedly

Examples

To enthuse about travel
He began to enthuse about his recent trip to Japan.
She enthused over the new technology during the presentation.
Teachers often enthuse their students by sharing personal stories.
