climbing iron

noun
iron spike for climbing on ice
7.2 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈklaɪ.mɪŋ ˈaɪ.ərn/ /; / /ˈklaɪ.mɪŋ aɪrn/ (AmE), /ˈklaɪ.mɪŋ ˈaɪ.ən/ (BrE)
a spiked metal device attached to footwear to help with climbing or to prevent slipping, such as on ice, trees, or poles
