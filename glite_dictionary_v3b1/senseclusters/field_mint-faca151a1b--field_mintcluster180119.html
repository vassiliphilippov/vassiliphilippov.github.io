field mint

noun
a species of wild mint plant
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfiːld mɪnt / (BrE)
A wild mint plant (Mentha arvensis) native to Europe and Asia and widely naturalized in North America, with aromatic leaves used for flavoring.

Examples

Field mint plant
Field mint can be found growing wild along many country roads.
She harvested some field mint leaves to use in her tea.
The meadow was filled with the fresh scent of field mint after the rain.
