airbag

noun
vehicle safety device
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a safety device in vehicles that inflates rapidly during a collision to protect passengers from injury

Examples

Car airbag deployment
The airbag deployed instantly when the car hit the tree.
Modern vehicles are equipped with at least one airbag for each passenger.
After the accident, the mechanic checked if the airbag system needed to be replaced.
The airbag deployed instantly when the car crashed into the barrier.
Modern vehicles are equipped with at least one airbag for each front seat.
After the accident, the driver credited the airbag with saving her life.
