hypospray

verb
to inject without needle
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpoʊˌspreɪ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəʊˌspreɪ / (BrE)
to inject a drug or medicine through the skin without a needle, using a hypospray device

Examples

To hypospray medicine
The nurse will hypospray the medication to avoid using a needle.
Doctors can hypospray vaccines quickly during mass immunization campaigns.
Some patients prefer to hypospray insulin rather than use traditional injections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inject - to put a drug or other substance into the body using a needle or device
