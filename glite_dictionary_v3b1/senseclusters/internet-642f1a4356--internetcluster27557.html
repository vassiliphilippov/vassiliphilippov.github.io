Internet

proper noun
global computer network
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntɚˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌnɛt / (BrE)
the global computer network that connects millions of computers and devices worldwide

Examples

On the Internet
She found the answer quickly on the Internet.
Many people use the Internet to stay in touch with friends and family around the world.
The Internet has changed the way we access information and communicate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: World Wide Web - a system of interlinked documents and resources on the Internet
  • antonym: intranet - a private network accessible only to an organization's staff
