mmol

noun
unit of measurement
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmˌmoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛmˌməʊl / (BrE)
a unit of measurement equal to one thousandth of a mole, used in chemistry and medicine

Examples

mmol per liter
The laboratory reported the glucose level as 5.2 mmol per liter.
Doctors often use mmol to measure substances in blood tests.
A reading of 3 mmol per liter may indicate low blood sugar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millimole - a unit equal to one thousandth of a mole
  • degree higher: mole - the SI base unit for amount of substance
