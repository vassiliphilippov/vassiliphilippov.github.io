spiderish

adjective
web-like or delicate
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdərɪʃ / (BrE)
thin, delicate, or web-like in appearance, resembling the fine threads or structure of a spider's web.

Examples

Spiderish threads
The artist drew spiderish lines across the canvas, creating a sense of fragility.
She wore a spiderish scarf that was almost transparent in the sunlight.
His signature was so spiderish that it was difficult to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filmy - very thin and light, like a film or web
  • synonym: gossamer - extremely light, delicate, or tenuous
  • antonym: thick - having a large distance between opposite sides; not thin or delicate
