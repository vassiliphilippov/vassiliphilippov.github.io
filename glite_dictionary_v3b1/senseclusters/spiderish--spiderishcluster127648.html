spiderish

adjective
web-like or delicate
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdərɪʃ / (BrE)
thin, delicate, or web-like in appearance, resembling the fine threads or structure of a spider's web.

Examples

Spiderish threads

His signature was so spiderish that it was difficult to read.
