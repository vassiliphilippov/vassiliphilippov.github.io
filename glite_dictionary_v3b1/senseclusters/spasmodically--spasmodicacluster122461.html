spasmodically

adverb
in sudden, irregular bursts
6.5
Pronunciation: / spæzˈmɑːdɪkli /; / spæzˈmɑdɪkli / (AmE), / spæzˈmɒdɪkli / (BrE)
in sudden, brief, or irregular bursts; happening intermittently or unevenly

Examples

Work spasmodically
She worked spasmodically, sometimes making great progress and other times being idle for weeks.
The old car engine started spasmodically before finally stalling.
Thunder rumbled spasmodically in the distance throughout the night.
He sobbed spasmodically as he recalled the traumatic memory.
Her hand twitched spasmodically as the effects of the medication wore off.
Lights flickered spasmodically during the thunderstorm, causing the room to alternately brighten and darken.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermittently - at irregular intervals; from time to time
  • antonym: steadily - in a continuous, regular, and even manner
