Citrus aurantifolia

noun
species of lime tree (key lime)
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtrəs ɔːˌræntɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəs ɔːˌrantɪˈfəʊliə / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, widely cultivated for its small, round, greenish-yellow fruit known as the key lime.

Examples

Citrus aurantifolia tree
Botanists have studied the resilience of Citrus aurantifolia in saline soils.
Many chefs prefer juice from Citrus aurantifolia for its distinctive tart flavor.
The tropical climate is ideal for growing Citrus aurantifolia commercially.
