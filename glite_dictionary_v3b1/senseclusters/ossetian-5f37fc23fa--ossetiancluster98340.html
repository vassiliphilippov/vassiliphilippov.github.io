Ossetian

noun
person from Ossetia
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈsiːtiən / (AmE), / ɒˈsiːtiən / (BrE)
a person who is from Ossetia, a region in the Caucasus, or belongs to the Ossetian ethnic group

Examples

An Ossetian person
The Ossetian community in the city organizes cultural events every summer.
She learned about Ossetian traditions while traveling through the Caucasus.
Many Ossetians speak both their native language and Russian fluently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Georgian - a person from Georgia, a country in the Caucasus
  • synonym: Russian - a person from Russia
