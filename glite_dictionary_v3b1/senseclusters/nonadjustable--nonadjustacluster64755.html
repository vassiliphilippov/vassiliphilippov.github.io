nonadjustable

adjective
fixed, unchangeable
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈdʒʌstəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈdʒʌstəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be changed, moved, or modified

Because the seat is nonadjustable, it may not be comfortable for everyone.
