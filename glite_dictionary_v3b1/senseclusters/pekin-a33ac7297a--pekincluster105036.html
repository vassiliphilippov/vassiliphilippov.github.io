Pekin

proper noun
old name for Beijing; duck breed
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɪn / (BrE)
an old name for Beijing, the capital of China, or a breed of domestic duck known for its white feathers and meat
