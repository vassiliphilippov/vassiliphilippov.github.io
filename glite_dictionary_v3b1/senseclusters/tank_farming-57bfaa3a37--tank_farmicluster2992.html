tank farming

noun
growing plants in water with nutrients
4.6 4.2
Pronunciation: /tæŋk ˈfɑːr.mɪŋ/ /; / /teɪŋk ˈfɑːr.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /tæŋk ˈfɑː.mɪŋ/ /; / /teɪŋk ˈfɑː.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
a method of growing plants without soil by using water that contains dissolved nutrients; a form of hydroponics

Examples

Tank farming method
Tank farming can help conserve water compared to traditional soil-based agriculture.
Many commercial greenhouses use tank farming to maximize their vegetable yields throughout the year.
The science fair project demonstrated how tank farming enables rapid plant growth in a controlled environment.
