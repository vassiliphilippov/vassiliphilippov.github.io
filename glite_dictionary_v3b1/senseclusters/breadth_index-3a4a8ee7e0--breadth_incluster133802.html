breadth index

noun
ratio of skull width to length (percentage)
Pronunciation: / brɛdθ ˈɪndɛks / (AmE), / brɛdθ ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
a ratio, expressed as a percentage, of the maximum width to the maximum length of a skull

Examples

The breadth index ratio
A scientist calculated the breadth index to compare the cranial features of different populations.
Changes in the breadth index may reflect evolutionary adaptations over time.
