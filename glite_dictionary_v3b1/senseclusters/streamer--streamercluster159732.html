streamer

noun
newspaper headline running across the page
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmər / (AmE), / ˈstriːmə / (BrE)
a newspaper headline that runs across the entire width of the page, usually in large type

Examples

A newspaper streamer
That old newspaper has a dramatic streamer about the moon landing.
The editor decided to use a bold streamer for the breaking news.
The front page featured a streamer announcing the election results.
