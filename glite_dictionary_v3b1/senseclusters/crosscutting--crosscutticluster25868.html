crosscutting

adjective
affecting multiple areas
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌkʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to or affecting multiple areas, topics, or fields at the same time

Examples

Crosscutting issues
The committee discussed crosscutting themes that influence both education and healthcare policies.
Climate change is a crosscutting issue that affects many sectors of society.
The project team focused on crosscutting challenges shared by different departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multidisciplinary - involving several academic or professional fields
  • antonym: specialized - limited to a specific area or subject
