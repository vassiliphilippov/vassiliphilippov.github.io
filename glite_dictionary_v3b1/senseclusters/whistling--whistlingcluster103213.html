whistling

noun
signaling using a whistle or whistling
4.3 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪs.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhwɪs.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪs.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhwɪs.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of signaling or attracting attention by making a whistling sound, especially by blowing a whistle

Examples

Loud whistling for help
Her whistling brought the dog running back to her side.
The referee's whistling signaled a foul and stopped the play.
There was a loud whistling at the end of the match as the coach called the players off the field.
