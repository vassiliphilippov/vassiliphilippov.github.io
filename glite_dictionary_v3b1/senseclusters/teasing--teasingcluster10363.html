Showing a playful or mildly mocking intent, especially in a way that provokes or ridicules in a light-hearted manner.

Examples

A teasing smile
He gave his brother a teasing look, hinting at a private joke.
Her teasing smile made everyone in the room feel at ease.
The teasing tone in her voice meant she wasn't being completely serious.

Synonyms

    5.7 0.00/m

  • mocking
    4.5 0.05/m
    mocking is harsh, scornful ridicule that aims to hurt, while teasing is milder, playful or gently provocative.
