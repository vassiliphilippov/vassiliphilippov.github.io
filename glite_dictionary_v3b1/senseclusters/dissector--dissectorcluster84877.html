dissector

noun
person or tool for dissection
Pronunciation: / daɪˈsɛktər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛktə / (BrE)
a person or tool that carefully cuts apart and examines specimens, especially for scientific or medical study

Examples

Lab dissector tool
The dissector used fine instruments to separate the layers of tissue in the lab.
A skilled dissector is essential for teaching anatomy to medical students.
The new dissector made it easier to examine the internal organs of the specimen.
The biology student used a dissector to separate the tissues during the experiment.
A skilled dissector can reveal the internal structure of an organism with great precision.
The kit included a dissector along with other essential laboratory tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anatomist - a scientist who studies the structure of living things
  • synonym: dissector - a person who dissects
  • synonym: scalpel - a small, sharp knife used for surgery or dissection
