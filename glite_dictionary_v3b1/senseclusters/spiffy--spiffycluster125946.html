spiffy

adjective
stylish and neat in appearance
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪfi / (AmE), / ˈspɪfi / (BrE)
stylish and neat in appearance; smartly dressed or up-to-date in fashion and manners

Examples

A spiffy blue suit
He wore a spiffy blue suit to the job interview and made a great impression.
Her spiffy new sneakers were the talk of the class.
They gave the office a spiffy makeover before the grand opening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dowdy - unfashionable and lacking smartness in appearance
