butthurt

noun
petty resentment
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtˌhɜrt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌhɜːt / (BrE)
an angry or resentful feeling of being offended, especially about something minor or trivial

Examples

Show butthurt after losing
He was still butthurt days after losing the video game to his friend.
Social media can make people feel butthurt over the smallest comments.
She accused him of being butthurt when he complained about the harmless joke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: offense - the feeling of being hurt or upset by something said or done
  • synonym: resentment - a feeling of anger or displeasure about something unfair
