butthurt

noun
petty resentment
9.8 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb åtÀåh…úrt / (AmE), / Ààb åtÀåh…úÀêt / (BrE)
an angry or resentful feeling of being offended, especially about something minor or trivial

Examples

Show butthurt after losing
He was still butthurt days after losing the video game to his friend.
Social media can make people feel butthurt over the smallest comments.
She accused him of being butthurt when he complained about the harmless joke.

Synonyms

  • offense
    3.9 8.78/m
    offense is hurt feelings or anger caused by an insult, while butthurt shows a petty, annoyed reaction to small slights.
  • umbrage
    9.8 0.58/m
    umbrage is a formal feeling of offense or annoyance at a remark, while butthurt is informal and suggests childish or petty resentment.
