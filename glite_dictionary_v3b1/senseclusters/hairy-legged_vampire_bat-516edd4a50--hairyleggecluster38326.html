hairy-legged vampire bat

noun
species of vampire bat in the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛri ˌlɛɡd ˈvæmpaɪr bæt / (AmE), / ˈheəri ˌlɛɡd ˈvæmpaɪə bæt / (BrE)
a species of vampire bat found in tropical America, including southern California and Texas, similar in size and habits to Desmodus rotundus

Examples

A hairy-legged vampire bat colony
Researchers found a colony of hairy-legged vampire bats roosting in the abandoned building.
The hairy-legged vampire bat primarily feeds on the blood of birds in its native habitat.
Unlike other species, the hairy-legged vampire bat can also be found as far north as Texas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Desmodus rotundus - the common vampire bat
