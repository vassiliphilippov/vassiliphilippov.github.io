rosary

noun
prayer beads; Catholic prayer tool
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌzɛri / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzəri / (BrE)
A string of beads used to count prayers, especially in Catholic devotion, or the prayers recited using them.

Examples

A rosary bead
Many Catholics keep a rosary as a reminder of their faith.
She carried a rosary in her hand during the ceremony.
The elderly woman recited her prayers, letting each bead of the rosary slip through her fingers.
