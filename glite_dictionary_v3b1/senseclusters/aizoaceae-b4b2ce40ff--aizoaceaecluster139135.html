Aizoaceae

noun
family of succulent plants, mostly South African
Pronunciation: / aɪˌzoʊˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / aɪˌzəʊˈiːsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of succulent herbs or small shrubs, mainly found in South Africa, including carpetweeds and fig marigolds
