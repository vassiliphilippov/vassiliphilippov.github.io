ultima Thule

noun
mythical far-northern land; ultimate limit
Pronunciation: / ˈʌl.tɪ.mə ˈθjuː.li / (AmE), / ˈʌl.tɪ.mə ˈθjuː.li / (BrE)
the distant northernmost region of the world, as believed by ancient geographers; often used metaphorically to mean a distant, unknown, or ultimate limit

Examples

The distant ultima Thule
For centuries, explorers searched for ultima Thule at the edge of the known world.
Metaphorically, he called his far-fetched dreams his own ultima Thule.
The poet described his travels as a journey to ultima Thule, beyond the familiar.
