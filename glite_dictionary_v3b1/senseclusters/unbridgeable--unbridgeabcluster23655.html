unbridgeable

adjective
impossible to bridge or overcome
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbrɪdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrɪdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to bridge or overcome; too great or severe to be crossed, connected, or reconciled

Examples

An unbridgeable divide
Decades of mistrust had created an unbridgeable divide between the communities.
Some cultural differences can seem unbridgeable, but honest dialogue often helps.
The two leaders faced an unbridgeable gulf in their opinions, making compromise impossible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bridgeable - able to be bridged or overcome
  • synonym: insurmountable - impossible to overcome
