unwoven

adjective
not woven; made without weaving
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwoʊvən / (AmE), / ʌnˈwəʊvən / (BrE)
Not woven; made without interlacing threads.

Examples

An unwoven fabric
She preferred an unwoven rug for its natural and rustic appearance.
The unwoven strands of fiber were soft and easily separated.
The basket was made from unwoven strips of bamboo, giving it a unique look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: woven - made by interlacing threads; constructed by weaving
