eugenically

adverb
in a genetic-improving way
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˈdʒɛnɪkli / (AmE), / juːˈdʒɛnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or promotes the improvement of genetic qualities, especially in humans.
