streetcar track

noun
rails for streetcars or trams
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌkɑr træk / (AmE), / ˈstriːtkɑː træk / (BrE)
the set of rails on which streetcars or trams operate in a city or town

Examples

The streetcar track
A new extension of streetcar track was laid to reach the university district.
The city decided to repair the streetcar track after several complaints from commuters.
Workers spent all night maintaining the streetcar track to ensure a smooth ride the next morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tramway - track or road along which trams run
