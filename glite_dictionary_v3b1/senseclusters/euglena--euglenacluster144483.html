euglena

noun
single-celled green freshwater organism
Pronunciation: / juˈɡliːnə / (AmE), / juːˈɡliːnə / (BrE)
a minute single-celled green freshwater organism with a single flagellum, often classified as algae or protozoa

Examples

A euglena cell
A euglena can photosynthesize due to the chloroplasts within its cell.
Researchers studied how euglena moves using its flagellum.
The pond water was teeming with euglena under the microscope.
