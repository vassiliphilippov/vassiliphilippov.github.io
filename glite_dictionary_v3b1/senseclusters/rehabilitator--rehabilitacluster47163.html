rehabilitator

noun
person aiding recovery
Pronunciation: / ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who helps someone or something recover or return to a normal state after illness, injury, or difficulty

Examples

Wildlife rehabilitator
The wildlife rehabilitator cared for injured animals until they could be released.
After his accident, a skilled rehabilitator helped him regain his strength and confidence.
The hospital hired a new rehabilitator to support patients during their recovery process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caretaker - a person who looks after people, animals, or property
  • synonym: therapist - a person trained to help others recover from physical or mental problems
