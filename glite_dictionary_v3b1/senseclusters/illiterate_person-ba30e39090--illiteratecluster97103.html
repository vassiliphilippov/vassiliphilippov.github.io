illiterate person

noun
person unable to read or write
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪt̬.ər.ət ˈpɝː.sən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪt.ər.ət ˈpɜː.sən / (BrE)
a person who is unable to read or write.

Examples

An illiterate person
An illiterate person may struggle with everyday tasks like reading medicine labels.
As a child, she volunteered to teach a neighboring illiterate person how to read.
The government launched programs to help every illiterate person learn basic reading skills.
