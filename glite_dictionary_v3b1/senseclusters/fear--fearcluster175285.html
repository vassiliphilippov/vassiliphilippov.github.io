fear

noun
profound respect or awe
6.3 4.06/m

A feeling of profound respect or awe, often mixed with apprehension, especially toward someone or something regarded as powerful.

Examples

Fear of God
His actions were guided by a deep fear of his ancestors' judgment.
The old traditions instilled a fear of breaking sacred customs.
There is a sense of fear and reverence in the way they approach the law.

Synonyms

  • reverence
    6.3 4.03/m

  • veneration
