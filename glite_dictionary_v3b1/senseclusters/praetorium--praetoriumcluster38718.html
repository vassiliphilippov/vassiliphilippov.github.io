praetorium

noun
tent of a Roman general
9.1
Pronunciation: / priːˈtɔriəm / (AmE), / priːˈtɔːriəm / (BrE)
The tent or headquarters of an ancient Roman general.

Examples

Ancient praetorium tent
Archaeologists discovered the remains of a praetorium near the ancient Roman battlefield.

The praetorium served not only as a command post but also as the general’s residence.
