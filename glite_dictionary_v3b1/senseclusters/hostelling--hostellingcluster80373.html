hostelling

verb
to stay in hostels
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑstəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɒstəlɪŋ / (BrE)
to stay in hostels, especially while traveling, often as a budget-friendly way to see different places.

Examples

To go hostelling
After college, they spent a summer hostelling through Southeast Asia to experience different cultures.
Many young travelers enjoy hostelling because it is affordable and social.
She met people from all over the world while hostelling across Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backpacking - to travel carrying your belongings in a backpack, often staying in inexpensive places
  • antonym: luxury travel - to travel with expensive and comfortable accommodations
