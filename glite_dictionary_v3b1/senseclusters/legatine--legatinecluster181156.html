legatine

adjective
of a papal legate
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡəˌtaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡətiːn / (BrE)
relating to a papal legate or the authority of a papal legate

Examples

Legatine authority
The council was held under legatine authority in the twelfth century.
A legatine mission was sent to resolve the dispute between the bishops.
He issued a legatine decree on behalf of the pope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papal - relating to the pope or the papacy
