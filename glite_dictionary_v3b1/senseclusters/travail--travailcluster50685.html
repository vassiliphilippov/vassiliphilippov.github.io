travail

noun
arduous effort, hard work
9.4
Pronunciation: / trəˈveɪl / (AmE), / trəˈveɪl / (BrE)
Intensive physical or mental effort; hard work or toil, often marked by difficulty or struggle.

Examples

Years of travail
After years of travail, she finally completed her thesis and graduated with honors.
His novel describes the travail of refugees fleeing war-torn regions.
The team's travail during the long winter paid off with a successful harvest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ease - lack of difficulty or effort, comfort
  • synonym: labor - productive work, often physical
  • synonym: toil - hard, continuous work
