pollera

noun
traditional Latin skirt
9.9
Pronunciation: /poʊ.ˈje.rə/ /; / /pə.ˈleɪ.rə/ (AmE), /pəʊ.ˈje.rə/ /; / /pəʊ.ˈleɪ.rə/ (BrE)
