pollera

noun
traditional Latin skirt
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈjeɪrə /; / poʊˈjeɾə / (AmE), / pɒˈjɛərə / (BrE)
a traditional wide, colorful skirt or full costume (skirt, blouse, and decorations) worn by women in some Latin American countries, especially for festivals and folk dances
