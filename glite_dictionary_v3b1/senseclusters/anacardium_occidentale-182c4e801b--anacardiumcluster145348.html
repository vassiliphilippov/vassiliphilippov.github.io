Anacardium occidentale

noun
tropical evergreen tree; the cashew tree
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.ˈkɑːr.diː.əm ɑːk.sɪd.ˈen.təl/ (AmE), /æn.ə.ˈkɑː.dɪ.əm ɒk.sɪd.ˈen.təl/ (BrE)
A tropical American evergreen tree known for producing cashew nuts, with kidney-shaped edible seeds that are safe to eat only after roasting.
