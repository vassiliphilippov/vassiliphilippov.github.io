tribalist

noun
group loyalist
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtraɪ.bə.lɪst/ (AmE), /ˈtraɪ.bə.lɪst/ (BrE)
a person who strongly supports their own group or community, often disregarding outsiders

Examples

A political tribalist
The tribalist refused to cooperate with anyone outside his own community.
During the election, each tribalist group promoted its own interests above all else.
Critics argue that a tribalist mindset can lead to division and misunderstanding.
