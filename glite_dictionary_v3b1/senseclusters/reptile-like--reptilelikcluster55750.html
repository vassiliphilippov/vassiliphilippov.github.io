reptile-like

adjective
resembling a reptile in appearance
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrep.taɪl.laɪk/ /; / /ˈrep.taɪ.əl.laɪk/ /; / /ˈrep.təl.laɪk/ (AmE), /ˈrep.taɪl.laɪk/ /; / /ˈrep.taɪ.əl.laɪk/ /; / /ˈrep.təl.laɪk/ (BrE)
resembling a reptile in appearance or texture; having scales, dry, bumpy, or scaly skin or surface.

Examples

Reptile-like skin texture
The gallery displayed a bronze statue whose surface looked distinctly reptile-like and caught the light.
She wore a costume with reptile-like scales that rustled as she walked across the stage.
Scientists described the fossil's skin impressions as reptile-like, showing overlapping bony plates and a rough texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reptilian - relating to or characteristic of reptiles; often used to describe scaly appearance
  • synonym: scaly - covered in or having small, plate-like structures like scales
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface without roughness; opposite of scaly or bumpy
