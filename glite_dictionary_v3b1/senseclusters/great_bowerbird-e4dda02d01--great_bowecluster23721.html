great bowerbird

noun
large Australian bowerbird species
Pronunciation: / ˌɡreɪt ˈbaʊ.ər.bɜrd / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt ˈbaʊ.ə.bɜːd / (BrE)
a large species of bowerbird native to northern Australia, known for building elaborate structures to attract mates
