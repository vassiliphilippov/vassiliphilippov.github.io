dustup

noun
an angry argument
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌʌp / (BrE)
a short, angry argument or dispute

Examples

A dustup with neighbor
He tried to avoid another dustup with his neighbor.
The meeting ended in a minor dustup over budget issues.
There was a dustup between the two players during the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fight - a violent confrontation or heated argument
  • synonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
