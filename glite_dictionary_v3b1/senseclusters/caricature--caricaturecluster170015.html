caricature

verb
to exaggerate features for comic effect
7.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkær.ɪ.kə.tʃər/ /; / /ˈke.rɪ.kə.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈkær.ɪ.kə.tʃə/ /; / /ˈkær.ɪ.kə.tʃʊə/ (BrE)
to represent or imitate someone or something in a way that exaggerates their distinctive features, often for comic or satirical effect

Examples

To caricature a politician
He caricatured the celebrity's mannerisms during the comedy routine.
Many political cartoons caricature public figures to make a point.
The artist caricatured her boss in the company newsletter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lampoon - to publicly criticize by using ridicule or sarcasm
  • synonym: satirize - to mock or criticize using satire
