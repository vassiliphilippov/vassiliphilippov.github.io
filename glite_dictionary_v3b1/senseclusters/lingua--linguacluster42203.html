lingua

noun
common language
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪŋ.ɡwə/ (AmE), /ˈlɪŋ.ɡwə/ (BrE)
a language, especially one used as a common means of communication between people who do not share a native language

Examples

Global lingua
English has become a global lingua for business and science.
Swahili serves as a lingua in many parts of East Africa.
In the past, Latin was the main lingua for scholars across Europe.
