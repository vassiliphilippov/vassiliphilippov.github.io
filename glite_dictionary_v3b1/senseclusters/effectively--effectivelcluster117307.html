effectively

adverb
in an efficient or successful manner
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that produces the desired result; efficiently and successfully.

Examples

Work effectively
She managed the team effectively, ensuring every project was completed on time.
The new software allows us to communicate more effectively with our customers.
To study effectively, it's important to take regular breaks and avoid distractions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: efficiently - in a well-organized and productive way
  • antonym: ineffectively - in a manner that does not produce the desired result
