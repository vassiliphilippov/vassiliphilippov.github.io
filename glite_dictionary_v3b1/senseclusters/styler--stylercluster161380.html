styler

noun
hair or design tool
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlə / (BrE)
a device, tool, or software used to arrange, style, or change the appearance of hair or digital content

Examples

A styler tool
She used a styler to curl her hair before the event.
The new app includes a styler that lets you change the look of your photos easily.
He bought a multi-purpose styler for both straightening and waving his hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editor - software used to modify or arrange digital content
  • synonym: hairdryer - an electrical device for drying and styling hair
  • synonym: straightener - a device used to make hair straight
