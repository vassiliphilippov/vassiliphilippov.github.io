malariologist

noun
malaria specialist
Pronunciation: / məˌlɛriˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / məˌleəriˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or medical expert who studies or treats malaria

Examples

A malariologist studies malaria
The malariologist traveled to Africa to conduct field research on malaria transmission.
A leading malariologist developed a new method for diagnosing malaria in remote areas.
At the conference, the malariologist shared insights on the latest malaria treatments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epidemiologist - a scientist who studies the spread of diseases
  • synonym: parasitologist - a scientist who studies parasites
