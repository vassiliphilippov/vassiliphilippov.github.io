hold

verb
to formally declare or decide (esp. legally)
2.6 5.4
Pronunciation: /hoʊld/ (AmE), /həʊld/ (BrE)
(of a judge, court, or authority) to formally decide or declare something, especially in a legal case.

Examples

The court holds a decision
The court held that the company was not liable for damages.
The judge held that the evidence was inadmissible in this case.
The jury held him responsible for his actions.
