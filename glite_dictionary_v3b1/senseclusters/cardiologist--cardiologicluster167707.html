cardiologist

noun
heart specialist (medical doctor)
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑr.diˈɑ.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɑː.diˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and disorders of the heart

Examples

A cardiologist doctor
A cardiologist can help manage conditions such as arrhythmias, heart failure, and hypertension.
After her heart attack, Maria made an appointment with a cardiologist for a thorough examination.
My father visits his cardiologist twice a year to monitor his heart health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardiology - the study of the heart and its functions
