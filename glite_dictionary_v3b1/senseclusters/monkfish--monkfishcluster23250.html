monkfish

noun
a large, wide-mouthed marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkˌfɪʃ /; / ˈmʌŋk.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a large marine fish with a broad, flat head and body, also called anglerfish or goosefish
