splash of color

noun
small area of added color
2.3
Pronunciation: / splæʃ əv ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / splæʃ ɒv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
A small area or quantity of color added to something, especially to make it more lively or attractive.

Examples

A splash of color
She wore a gray dress, but a red scarf provided a splash of color.
Spring flowers always bring a splash of color to the garden.
The living room needed a splash of color, so we added some bright cushions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dash of color - a small but noticeable amount of color
