overly researchy

adjective
excessively academic in style
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚli ˈriːsɜːrtʃi / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəli ˈriːsɜːtʃi / (BrE)
Excessively academic or formal in style, making it difficult for casual readers to engage with.

Examples

Overly researchy style
Her lectures are sometimes criticized for being overly researchy rather than engaging and approachable.
Some feedback described the proposal as overly researchy, suggesting it needed a more conversational tone.
The article was overly researchy and difficult for the general public to understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academic - relating to education or scholarship
  • antonym: accessible - easy to understand or approach
