historian

noun
expert or writer on history
4.1
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔːriən / (AmE), / hɪˈstɔːriən / (BrE)
a person who studies, writes about, and is an authority on history

Examples

A historian studies history
As a military historian, she specializes in the study of World War II.
Many people regard Herodotus as the first true historian.
The historian spent years researching documents from the medieval period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronicler - a person who writes accounts of important or historical events
  • antonym: novelist - a writer of novels, contrasted with writers of non-fiction such as historians
