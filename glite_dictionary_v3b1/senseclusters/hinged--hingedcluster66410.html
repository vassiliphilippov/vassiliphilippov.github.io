hinged

adjective
dependent on something
4.3
Pronunciation: /hɪndʒd/ (AmE), /hɪndʒd/ (BrE)
dependent on or determined by something else, often used with 'on' or 'upon'.

Examples

Hinged on success
The success of the project hinged on the team's ability to meet the deadline.
Her decision to move abroad hinged on whether she received the job offer.
The outcome of the negotiations hinged on a single unresolved issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contingent - dependent on certain conditions or circumstances
  • antonym: independent - not influenced or determined by something else
