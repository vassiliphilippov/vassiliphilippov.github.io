directress

noun
female director (archaic)
9.6
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktrəs / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktrɪs / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a woman who is a director, especially of a school or organization

Examples

The directress of school
The directress of the school retired after many years of service.
In the early 20th century, the directress was responsible for overseeing all the teachers.
The directress welcomed the new students at the start of the term.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - a person who leads or manages an organization or activity
