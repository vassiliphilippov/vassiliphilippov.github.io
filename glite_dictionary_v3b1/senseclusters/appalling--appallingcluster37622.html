appalling

adjective
causing shock or dismay
5.4
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing shock, horror, or dismay; extremely bad or unpleasant.

Examples

An appalling situation
It is appalling that so many children go hungry every day.
The crime scene was found in an appalling state.
The refugees endured appalling conditions during their journey.
