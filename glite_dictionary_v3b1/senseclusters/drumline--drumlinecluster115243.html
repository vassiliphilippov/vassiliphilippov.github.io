drumline

noun
repeated drum rhythm
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a repeated sequence of drum beats that provides a rhythmic accompaniment in music, especially in marching bands or songs

Examples

Catchy drumline
The energetic drumline kept the crowd moving throughout the concert.
Every marching band performance features a skilled drumline leading the rhythm.
The song's catchy drumline made it popular on the radio.

  • synonym: ostinato - a continually repeated musical phrase or rhythm
