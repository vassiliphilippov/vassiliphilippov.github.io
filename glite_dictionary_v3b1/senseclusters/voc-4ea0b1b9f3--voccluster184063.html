VOC

noun
volatile organic compound
7.7
Pronunciation: / viː oʊ siː / (AmE), / viː əʊ siː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for volatile organic compound, a chemical that easily becomes a vapor and can pollute the air

Examples

Low VOC paint
The new paint is labeled as low VOC to reduce indoor air pollution.
Scientists monitor VOC levels in the atmosphere to study air quality.
Many cleaning products release VOC that can affect your health if used in closed spaces.

Related Terms
