eye infection

noun
an infection of the eye, especially eyelid gland
1.0
Pronunciation: / aɪ ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / aɪ ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
an infection affecting the eye, especially an infection of the sebaceous gland of the eyelid

Examples

An eye infection symptom
Children are especially susceptible to an eye infection after touching their eyes with unwashed hands.
If untreated, an eye infection can cause significant discomfort and redness.
The doctor prescribed antibiotic drops to treat her eye infection.

Related Terms
