since

conjunction
from a past time
1.0
Pronunciation: / sɪns / (AmE), / sɪns / (BrE)
used to introduce the time when something started and to connect two clauses, meaning from that time until now or another point

Examples

Since he left
He has lived in London since he finished university.
They have been friends since childhood and still talk every week.
I have not eaten meat since I became a vegetarian two years ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: after - following a particular event or time
  • antonym: before - earlier than a particular time or event
