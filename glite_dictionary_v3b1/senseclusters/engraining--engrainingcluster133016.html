engraining

verb
to dye deeply
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
to dye wool, yarn, or cloth so that the color deeply penetrates the fibers and becomes long-lasting
