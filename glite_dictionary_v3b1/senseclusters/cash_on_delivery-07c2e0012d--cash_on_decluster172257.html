cash on delivery

noun
payment upon delivery of goods
3.8 2.4
Pronunciation: /kæʃ ən dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ /; / /kæʃ ɑːn dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ (AmE), /kæʃ ən dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ /; / /kæʃ ɒn dɪ.ˈlɪv.ər.iː/ (BrE)
A method of paying for goods when they are delivered, rather than in advance, with payment made in cash to the delivery person.

Examples

Cash on delivery payment
If you choose cash on delivery, make sure you have the exact amount ready.
The company only accepts cash on delivery for first-time customers.
We ordered the groceries cash on delivery as we didn't want to pay online.
