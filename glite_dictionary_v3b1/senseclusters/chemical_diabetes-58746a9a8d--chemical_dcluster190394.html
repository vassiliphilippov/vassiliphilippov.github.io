chemical diabetes

noun
mild, asymptomatic form of diabetes mellitus
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl daɪəˈbiːtiz / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl daɪəˈbiːtiːz / (BrE)
A mild form of diabetes mellitus marked by abnormal results in diagnostic tests without obvious symptoms.

Examples

Chemical diabetes diagnosis
Early intervention is important even in cases of chemical diabetes to prevent progression.
Her doctor diagnosed her with chemical diabetes after finding abnormal glucose tolerance test results.
Many people with chemical diabetes do not experience typical symptoms of diabetes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diabetes mellitus - a chronic disease marked by high blood sugar
