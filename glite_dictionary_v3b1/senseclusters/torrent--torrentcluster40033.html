torrent

noun
overwhelming outpouring or flood
4.4 1.85/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…îÀêr…ônt / (AmE), / Ààt…ír…ônt / (BrE)
A large amount of something that comes suddenly and forcefully; an overwhelming outpouring, usually of words, emotions, or things.

Examples

Torrent of words
She burst into a torrent of tears when she heard the news.
Social media was flooded with a torrent of comments during the live broadcast.
The politician faced a torrent of criticism after his remarks.

  • flood
