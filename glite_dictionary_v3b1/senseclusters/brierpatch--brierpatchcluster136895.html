brierpatch

noun
tangled area of prickly plants
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪərˌpætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪəˌpatʃ / (BrE)
a dense, tangled area of prickly shrubs or bushes, especially briers

Examples

A brierpatch of bushes
After escaping, the rabbit hid in a brierpatch to avoid predators.
The hikers had to find a way around a thick brierpatch on the trail.
Wild berries often grow in the shelter of a sprawling brierpatch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brier - a prickly plant or shrub, especially of the rose family
  • synonym: thicket - a dense group of bushes or trees
