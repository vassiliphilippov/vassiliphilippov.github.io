incumbent

adjective
necessary as a duty or responsibility
1.3 5.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkʌm.bənt/ /; / /ɪŋ.ˈkʌm.bənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkʌm.bənt/ /; / /ɪŋ.ˈkʌm.bənt/ (BrE)
(of a duty) necessary for someone as a responsibility or obligation; morally binding.

Examples

Incumbent duty
As a manager, it is incumbent upon you to ensure your team's safety.
It is incumbent on all citizens to obey the law.
The judge stated that it was incumbent on the company to provide accurate information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obligatory - required by a legal, moral, or other rule; compulsory
  • antonym: optional - available to be chosen but not compulsory
