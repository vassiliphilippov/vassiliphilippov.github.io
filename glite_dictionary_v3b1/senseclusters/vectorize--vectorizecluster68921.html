vectorize

verb
to convert to vector
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛktəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɛktəraɪz / (BrE)
to convert an image or data into a vector format, often for easier processing or scalability

Examples

To vectorize an image
Many artists vectorize their drawings to make them scalable for different uses.
The software can vectorize scanned images for easier editing and resizing.
You should vectorize the logo before sending it to the printer to ensure high quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitize - to convert information into a digital format
  • antonym: rasterize - to convert a vector image into a raster (pixel-based) format
