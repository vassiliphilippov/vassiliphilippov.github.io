hailer

noun
public praiser
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪlər / (AmE), / ˈheɪlə / (BrE)
a person who publicly praises, applauds, or proclaims someone or something as excellent

Examples

A hailer of talent
The journalist became a well-known hailer of the city's new art scene.
As a passionate hailer of innovative technology, she often spoke at conferences.
He was recognized as a hailer of young talent in the music industry.
