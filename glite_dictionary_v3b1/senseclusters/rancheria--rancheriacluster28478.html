rancheria

noun
small rural settlement
Pronunciation: / ˌræn.tʃəˈriː.ə / (AmE), / ˌræn.tʃəˈriː.ə / (BrE)
a small rural settlement or village, especially one inhabited by Native Americans in the southwestern United States
