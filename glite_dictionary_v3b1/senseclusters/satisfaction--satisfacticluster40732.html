satisfaction

noun
pleasure from fulfilling a need or desire
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsæt̬ɪsˈfækʃən /; / ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən / (AmE), / ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən / (BrE)
a pleasant feeling you get when you achieve something or when your needs or desires are fulfilled

Examples

Completing the marathon gave him a deep sense of satisfaction.
She smiled with satisfaction as she saw her exam results.
The company aims to improve customer satisfaction with its new service policy.
Customer satisfaction is our top priority at this company.
He expressed satisfaction with the results of the negotiation.
She felt a deep satisfaction after completing the marathon.
