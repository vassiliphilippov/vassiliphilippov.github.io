for one

adverb
as an example among others
5.0
Pronunciation: / fər wʌn / (AmE), / fə wʌn / (BrE)
Used to indicate that someone is a particular example among several possibilities.

Examples

For one example
For one, I think the proposal is very reasonable and should be considered.
Her sister, for one, did not like the new rules at all.
Many people dislike early mornings; my roommate, for one, prefers to sleep until noon.
