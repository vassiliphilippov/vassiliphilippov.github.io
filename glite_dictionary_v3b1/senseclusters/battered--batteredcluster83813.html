battered

adjective
subjected to repeated abuse or injury
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.ɚd / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əd / (BrE)
Suffering from repeated physical or emotional abuse, often showing visible or psychological signs of injury.

Examples

Battered woman
After years in a violent relationship, she finally sought help as a battered woman.
He volunteers at a shelter that supports battered families escaping dangerous situations.
Social workers intervened after signs that the child was battered became apparent at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abused - subjected to cruel or violent treatment repeatedly
  • antonym: protected - not subjected to harm or abuse
