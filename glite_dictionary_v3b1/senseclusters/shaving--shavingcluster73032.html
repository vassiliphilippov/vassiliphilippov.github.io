shaving

noun
removing hair with a razor
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of removing hair from the body, especially the face, by using a razor

Examples

Morning shaving routine
Electric razors have made shaving much faster and easier for many people.
He started his morning with a shaving routine to look fresh for work.
Many men dislike shaving every day because it can irritate their skin.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: beard - hair growing on the lower part of a man's face
  • synonym: depilation - the removal of hair, especially from the body
  • synonym: hair removal - the general process of eliminating hair from the body
