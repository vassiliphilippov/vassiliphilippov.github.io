wanted notice

noun
law enforcement announcement of a person sought
1.0 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːntɪd ˌnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɒntɪd ˌnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
an official public statement issued by law enforcement to inform the public that a person is sought for questioning or arrest

Examples

A wanted notice
A wanted notice was circulated throughout the city to help catch the fugitive.
The wanted notice included a photograph and detailed description of the person in question.
The police issued a wanted notice for the suspect after the robbery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wanted poster - a printed notice, often with a photograph, seeking a criminal's capture
