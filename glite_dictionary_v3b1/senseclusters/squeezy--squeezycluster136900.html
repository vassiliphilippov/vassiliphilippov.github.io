squeezy

adjective
easy to squeeze
Pronunciation: / ˈskwiːzi / (AmE), / ˈskwiːzi / (BrE)
easy to squeeze or compress, especially referring to containers or objects designed to be pressed by hand

Examples

Squeezy bottle
He prefers using a squeezy tube of toothpaste because it is easier to handle.
The squeezy ketchup bottle made it simple to add just the right amount to her fries.
Many cleaning products now come in squeezy containers for convenience.
