cultivation

noun
preparing land for growing crops
2.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /kʌl.tɪ.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kʌl.tɪ.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of preparing and using land for growing crops, especially on a large scale

Examples

Large-scale cultivation of land

Many rural communities depend on the cultivation of wheat for their livelihoods.
The cultivation of rice requires careful management of water resources.
Intensive cultivation of the land led to higher crop yields.
Modern technology has greatly improved the cultivation of rice in many countries.
The cultivation of wheat requires fertile soil and plenty of sunlight.
