Examples

To rack money
For years, the mob had been racking money from shopkeepers in the neighborhood.
The corrupt officials tried to rack extra fees from small business owners.
The gang racked large sums from local merchants through threats.

Synonyms

  • extort
    8.1 1.71/m
    extort is a legal term for taking money by threats or force, while rack refers to the same act done by intimidation.
  • wring
    7.4 2.08/m
    wring means to forcefully extract money from someone over time, while rack focuses on taking it by threats or intimidation.
  • squeeze
    4.8 3.75/m
    squeeze means to pressure someone to give money or help, while rack implies harsh force, threats, or intimidation.
  • gouge
    8.3 0.53/m
    gouge means to charge or take money unfairly, often by overcharging, while rack implies getting it by force or threats.
