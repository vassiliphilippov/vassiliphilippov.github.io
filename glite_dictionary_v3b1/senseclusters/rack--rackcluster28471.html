Synonyms

  • extort
    8.1 2/m
    extort is a legal term for taking money by threats or force, while rack refers to the same act done by intimidation.
  • wring
    7.4 1/m
    wring means to forcefully extract money from someone over time, while rack focuses on taking it by threats or intimidation.
  • squeeze
    4.8 4/m
    squeeze means to pressure someone to give money or help, while rack implies harsh force, threats, or intimidation.
  • gouge
    8.3 0.4/m
