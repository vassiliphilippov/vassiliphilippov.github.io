bajillion

adjective
extremely numerous
Pronunciation: / bəˈdʒɪljən / (AmE), / bəˈdʒɪljən / (BrE)
informal: extremely large in number or amount; countless or innumerable.

Examples

Bajillion stars
There were a bajillion stars visible in the night sky.
He has told me that story a bajillion times already.
The website received a bajillion comments after the article was published.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countless - too many to be counted
  • antonym: few - a small number
  • synonym: infinite - without limits; endless
