tactuality

noun
quality of being touchable
9.9
Pronunciation: / tækˈtʃuːəlɪti / (AmE), / tækˈtʃuːəlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being perceptible or experienced by touch

Examples

The tactuality of touch
The tactuality of the sculpture invited visitors to run their hands over its smooth surface.
Designers considered the tactuality of materials when creating products for visually impaired users.
Children enjoyed the tactuality of the sand as they played on the beach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intangibility - the quality of not being able to be touched or physically felt
  • synonym: tangibility - the quality of being able to be touched or felt
