enframing

noun
conceptual framing process
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfreɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfreɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of framing or enclosing something, especially in a conceptual or technological context

Examples

Enframing of reality
Philosophers debate the impact of enframing on how we perceive reality.
The artist's work explores the enframing of nature through technology.
Heidegger's essay discusses enframing as a key concept in understanding modern technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosure - the act of surrounding or closing off something
  • synonym: framing - the act of constructing or shaping something conceptually
