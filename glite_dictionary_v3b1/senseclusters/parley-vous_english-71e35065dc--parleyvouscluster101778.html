Parley-vous English

x
playful way to ask if someone speaks English
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrli ˈvu ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌpɑːli ˈvu ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
(humorous) Do you speak English? Used jokingly to ask whether someone understands or can use English.

Examples

Parley-vous English joke
After hearing my accent, she asked, 'Parley-vous English?' with a mischievous grin.
Confused by the instructions, he joked, 'Parley-vous English?'
Whenever tourists seem lost, someone inevitably says 'Parley-vous English?' as a joke.
