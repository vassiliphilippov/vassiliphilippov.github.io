sensitized

adjective
having an allergy or excessive susceptibility
6.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.sə.taɪzd/ /; / /ˈsen.sɪ.taɪzd/ (AmE), /ˈsen.sə.taɪzd/ /; / /ˈsen.sɪ.taɪzd/ (BrE)
Having developed an allergy or an unusual or excessive susceptibility, especially to a specific substance or factor.

Examples

Sensitized to chemicals
After years of exposure to the chemical, she became sensitized and developed a severe rash.
Some people are sensitized to peanuts and must avoid them completely.
The laboratory mice were sensitized to the allergen for experimental purposes.
After years of exposure, Emily became sensitised to chemicals in cleaning products.
Some people are sensitised to pollen and suffer intense allergic reactions during spring.
The doctor warned that repeat contact could leave you sensitised to latex gloves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allergic - having an allergy to a particular substance
  • antonym: desensitised - having reduced sensitivity or allergy
  • antonym: immune - protected or exempt from a particular disease or the effect of something
  • synonym: sensitized - having developed an allergy or abnormal sensitivity (American spelling)
