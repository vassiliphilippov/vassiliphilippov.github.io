teacherage

noun
teachers' residence
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːtʃərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a house or building provided for teachers to live in, especially near a school in rural areas

Examples

A teacherage house
The school district built a new teacherage to attract more qualified teachers to the rural area.
She moved into the teacherage when she started her first teaching job in the countryside.
The old teacherage was renovated to provide comfortable housing for the staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boarding house - a house where people pay to live and have meals
  • synonym: dormitory - a building where people, such as students, live together
