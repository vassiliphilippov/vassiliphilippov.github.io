Synonyms

  • go down
    1.9 11/m
    go down is the common phrasal verb for moving to a lower level in many situations, while descend sounds more formal.
  • come down
    2.8 8/m
    come down implies moving toward the speaker or point of reference or decreasing, while descend simply means going to a lower position.
  • drop down
    1.8 2/m
    drop down suggests a sudden or quick movement from high to low, while descend is neutral and may be slow or controlled.
  • fall
    1.0 150/m
    fall means to move down suddenly, often because of gravity, while descend can be controlled or slow.
  • sink
    1.0 20/m
    sink often means to go down slowly, sometimes under water or emotionally, while descend is neutral for any downward movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascend - to move or go up from a lower to a higher position
  • synonym: fall - to move or drop downward, typically rapidly and freely
