desalinator

noun
salt-removing machine
Pronunciation: / diːˈsæləˌneɪtər / (AmE), / diːˈsælɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a machine or device that removes salt from seawater to make it suitable for drinking or use

Examples

A desalinator machine
The ship was equipped with a desalinator to provide fresh water.
After installing a desalinator, the island community no longer depended on rainwater.
Portable desalinators are essential for long ocean voyages where fresh water is limited.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desalination plant - a facility where seawater is processed to remove salt
  • synonym: water purifier - a device that removes impurities from water
