epidermoid

noun
benign skin cyst
8.9
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.ˈdɜːr.mɔɪd/ /; / /ə.pɪ.ˈdɜːr.mɔɪd/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.ˈdɜː.mɔɪd/ /; / /ə.pɪ.ˈdɜː.mɔɪd/ (BrE)
a usually benign cyst formed from skin cells, typically found just beneath the skin surface

Examples

Epidermoid cyst
The doctor explained that the epidermoid on his scalp was harmless and could be removed easily.
After noticing a lump under her skin, she learned it was an epidermoid that required minor surgery.
Most epidermoids are painless and grow slowly beneath the skin.
