bunyip

noun
foolish person (Australia)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌn.jɪp / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.jɪp / (BrE)
an odd, foolish, or ridiculous person, used informally in Australian English

Examples

Silly bunyip
Stop acting like a bunyip and help me solve this problem.
He made a bunyip of himself at the party by telling silly jokes.
My uncle is a real bunyip when it comes to fixing things around the house.
