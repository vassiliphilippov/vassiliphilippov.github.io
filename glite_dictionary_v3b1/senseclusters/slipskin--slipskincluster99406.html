slipskin

noun
fruit with loose skin
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌskɪn / (BrE)
a fruit, especially a grape, whose skin separates easily from the flesh

Examples

A slipskin grape
The slipskin grape is popular for making jelly because its skin peels off easily.
She prefers slipskin varieties since they are easier to eat without peeling tools.
When you squeeze a slipskin grape, the flesh pops out cleanly from the skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freestone - a fruit whose flesh separates easily from the stone
