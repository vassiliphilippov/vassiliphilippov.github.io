jut out

verb
extend or project beyond a surface
6.1 3.4
Pronunciation: / dʒʌt aʊt / (AmE), / dʒʌt aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

At the corner, the ledge juts out, making it easy for pigeons to perch.
The balcony seemed to jut out dangerously over the street below.
