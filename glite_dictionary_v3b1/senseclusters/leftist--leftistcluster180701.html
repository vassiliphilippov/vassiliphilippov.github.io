leftist

noun
person with left-wing political views
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛftɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɛftɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or belongs to the political left, especially someone who advocates progressive or socialist policies

Examples

A leftist politician
During the debate, a leftist challenged the conservative panelist's views on social policy.
She is known as a prominent leftist in local politics.
The demonstration attracted many leftist activists and supporters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progressive - a person who advocates social reform, often associated with the political left
  • antonym: rightist - a person who belongs to the political right
