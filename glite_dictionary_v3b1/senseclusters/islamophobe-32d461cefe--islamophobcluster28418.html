Islamophobe

noun
person hostile to Islam
Pronunciation: / ɪsˈlɑːməˌfoʊb / (AmE), / ɪsˈlɑːməfəʊb / (BrE)
a person who harbors prejudice, fear, or hostility toward Muslims or Islam

Examples

An Islamophobe person
The politician was accused of being an Islamophobe after making several controversial statements.
Many organizations work to challenge the views of Islamophobes in society.
The documentary explored how Islamophobe attitudes have affected Muslim communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bigot - a person who is intolerant toward those with different opinions or beliefs
  • antonym: tolerant - showing willingness to accept beliefs or behaviors different from one's own
  • synonym: xenophobe - a person who dislikes or is prejudiced against people from other countries
