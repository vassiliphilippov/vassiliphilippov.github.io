unusually

adverb
to a remarkable extent

Pronunciation: /  ånÀàjuÀê íu…ôli / (AmE), /  ånÀàjuÀê í ä…ôli / (BrE)

Examples

An unusually fast runner

The painting features unusually vibrant colors that catch the viewer's eye.
The weather was unusually warm for early spring this year.
