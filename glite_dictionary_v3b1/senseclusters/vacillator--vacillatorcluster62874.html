vacillator

noun
person who hesitates or wavers
9.4 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈvæs.ə.leɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈvæs.ɪ.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈvæs.ə.leɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈvæs.ɪ.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who is indecisive or frequently changes their opinions or decisions, often hesitating to act

Examples

Instead of being decisive, she became a vacillator during moments of crisis.
The committee grew frustrated with the vacillator who could never commit to a plan.
