Jamestown weed

noun
poisonous tropical weed with trumpet-shaped flowers
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪmz.taʊn wiːd / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz.taʊn wiːd / (BrE)
A poisonous tropical weed with large trumpet-shaped flowers and prickly fruits, known for its rank-smelling foliage.

Examples

Jamestown weed plant
Early colonists in Virginia suffered after accidentally ingesting Jamestown weed.
The field was overrun with Jamestown weed, making the area dangerous for livestock.
The rank odor of Jamestown weed is often a warning sign to gardeners.
