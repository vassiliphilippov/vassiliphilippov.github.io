tornadic

adjective
like a tornado
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈnædɪk / (AmE), / tɔːˈnædɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of a tornado

Examples

Tornadic storm
The meteorologist warned of tornadic conditions developing in the region this evening.
Residents were urged to take shelter due to the threat of tornadic storms approaching the city.
The sky turned dark and the wind took on a tornadic intensity before the storm hit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - free from disturbance or strong winds
  • synonym: tornado - a violent rotating column of air extending from a thunderstorm to the ground
