tessellation

noun
pattern of shapes fitted together
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛsəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtɛsɪˈleɪʃən /; / ˌtɛsɪˈleɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an arrangement or pattern of shapes closely fitted together without gaps or overlaps, especially used in art, tiling, or decoration
