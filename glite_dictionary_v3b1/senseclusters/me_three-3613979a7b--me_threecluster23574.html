me three

x
informal agreement or shared feeling
9.9
Pronunciation: / mi ˈθri / (AmE), / mi ˈθriː / (BrE)
Used informally to express that you agree with or feel the same way as someone else, typically after another person has said "me too".

Examples

Me three said
"I can't wait for the weekend," Ella exclaimed. "Me three," said Marcus.
After two people said they loved the movie, Sarah chimed in with, "me three!"
When Alex said he needed a vacation, Jamie smiled and replied, me three.
