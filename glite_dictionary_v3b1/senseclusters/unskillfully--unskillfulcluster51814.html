unskillfully

adverb
without skill
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɪlfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɪlfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of skill or ability

Examples

To unskillfully try
He unskillfully tried to fix the computer, making the problem worse.
The cake was decorated unskillfully, with uneven icing and crooked letters.
They unskillfully handled the negotiations, resulting in a poor agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a way that is not skillful or graceful
  • synonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks grace or skill
  • antonym: skillfully - in a way that shows skill or ability
