Synonyms

  • flatware
    2.6 0.53/m
    flatware refers to forks, knives, and spoons used at the table in American English, while cutlery is the general term.
  • silverware
    1.1 2.18/m
    silverware means metal forks, knives, and spoons often made of steel or silver, while cutlery is the neutral name for these utensils.
  • eating utensil
    1.0 0.14/m
    eating utensil names a single tool like a fork, knife, or spoon used to eat food, while cutlery refers to the set.
  • silver
