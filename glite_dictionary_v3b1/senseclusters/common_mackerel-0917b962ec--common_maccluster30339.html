common mackerel

noun
a species of mackerel, food fish of the North Atlantic
5.7 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈmækərəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈmækərəl / (BrE)
a mackerel (Scomber scombrus) found in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean, valued as food and recognized by its greenish-blue body with dark bars and small or absent scales
