antiship

noun
weapon for attacking ships
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈʃɪp / (BrE)
a weapon, especially a missile, designed to attack or destroy ships at sea

Examples

An antiship missile
The navy tested a new antiship missile during the military exercise.
Modern antiship weapons can target vessels from great distances.
The submarine was equipped with several antiship systems for defense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antitank - a weapon designed to destroy tanks
  • synonym: torpedo - an underwater missile used to attack ships
