transcriptionally

adverb
relating to transcription
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃənəli / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the process of copying genetic information from DNA to RNA

Examples

Transcriptionally active genes
The protein is transcriptionally regulated in response to environmental changes.
Some genes are transcriptionally silent in certain types of cells.
Researchers found that the mutation affects the gene transcriptionally rather than at the protein level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetically - in a way that relates to genes or heredity
