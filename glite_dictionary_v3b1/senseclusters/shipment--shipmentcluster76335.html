Synonyms

  • cargo
    2.3 30/m
    cargo refers to goods carried by ship, plane, or truck, while shipment is a specific batch moved together by transport.
  • lading
    8.8 0.2/m
    lading is the goods recorded as loaded on a ship or truck, while shipment is the batch moved together.
  • freight
    3.9 4/m
    freight means goods carried for commercial transport or the cost to move them, while shipment is the actual batch of goods.
  • consignment
    5.6 0.2/m
    consignment is a batch sent to a seller or store on specific terms, while shipment simply means goods moved together.
