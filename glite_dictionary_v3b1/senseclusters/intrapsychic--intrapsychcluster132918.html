intrapsychic

adjective
within the mind
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈsaɪkɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈsaɪkɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Intrapsychic mental processes

Understanding intrapsychic dynamics can help explain certain patterns of behavior.
He experienced an intrapsychic conflict between his desires and his values.
