scrabble

noun
rough or aimless drawing
Pronunciation: / ˈskræb.əl / (AmE), / ˈskræb.əl / (BrE)
a rough or aimless drawing, usually made absentmindedly or without artistic purpose

Examples

A child's scrabble
A child's scrabble often covers more paper than their intended drawing.
He absentmindedly made a scrabble with his pen while talking on the phone.
She filled the margins of her notebook with little scrabbles during the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doodle - a rough or aimless drawing made while a person’s attention is otherwise occupied
