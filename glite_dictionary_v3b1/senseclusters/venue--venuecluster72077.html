venue

noun
legal location of a trial or hearing
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnju / (AmE), / ˈvɛnjuː / (BrE)
(Law) The jurisdiction or location where a trial is conducted or a legal case is heard.

Examples

Court venue
Determining the proper venue is crucial for ensuring a fair trial.
Jurisdiction and venue are often addressed in the opening statements of a case.
The defense attorney filed a motion to change the venue due to pretrial publicity.
