naturalize

verb
make more natural or lifelike
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃərəˌlaɪz /; / ˈnætʃɚəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəlaɪz /; / ˈnætʃrəlʌɪz / (BrE)
to make something appear more natural or lifelike
