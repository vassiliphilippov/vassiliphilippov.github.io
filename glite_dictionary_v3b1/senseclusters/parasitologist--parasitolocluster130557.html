parasitologist

noun
parasite scientist
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəsaɪˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌpærəsaɪˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies parasites and their interactions with hosts

Examples

A parasitologist studies parasites
The parasitologist discovered a new species of parasite in the rainforest.
A team led by a parasitologist investigated the outbreak of the disease.
After years of research, the parasitologist published her findings on malaria transmission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microbiologist - a scientist who studies microorganisms
  • synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals
