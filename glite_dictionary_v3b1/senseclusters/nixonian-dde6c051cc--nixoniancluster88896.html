Nixonian

adjective
of Richard Nixon's style
Pronunciation: / nɪkˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / nɪkˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the policies, style, or actions of Richard Nixon, especially those involving secrecy or political maneuvering

Examples

Nixonian tactics
The administration's Nixonian approach to handling the press drew widespread criticism.
Some historians argue that the leader's tactics were distinctly Nixonian in their secrecy and complexity.
Accusations of Nixonian behavior surfaced after the government refused to release key documents.
