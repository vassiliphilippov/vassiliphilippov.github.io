chuck-will's-widow

noun
large nightjar bird of the southern US
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃʌk wɪlz ˈwɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtʃʌk wɪlz ˈwɪdəʊ / (BrE)
a large nocturnal bird (Caprimulgus carolinensis) of the southern United States, related to the whip-poor-will, with a distinctive repetitive call
