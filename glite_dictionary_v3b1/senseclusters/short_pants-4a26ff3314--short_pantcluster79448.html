short pants

noun
trousers ending above the knee
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt pænts / (AmE), / ʃɔːt pænts / (BrE)
trousers that end at or above the knee, usually worn in warm weather or for sports or casual occasions (also called shorts)

Examples

A pair of short pants
He wore short pants to the picnic because the weather was hot.
In the early 20th century, boys commonly wore short pants until they reached a certain age.
Schoolchildren often wore short pants as part of their summer uniform.
Children often play outside in short pants during the summer months.
During the heatwave, many people wore short pants to stay cool.
He packed several pairs of short pants for his tropical vacation.
