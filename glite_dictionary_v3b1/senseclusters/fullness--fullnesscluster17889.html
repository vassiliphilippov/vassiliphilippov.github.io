Examples

Synonyms

  • repleteness
    8.9 0.00/m
    repleteness is complete and often used in formal contexts to mean being fully supplied, while fullness means being simply full.
  • plenitude
    8.3 0.56/m
    plenitude suggests a large, rich amount or full state in formal speech, while fullness focuses on being filled to capacity.
  • plentitude
    8.1 0.04/m
    plentitude often means an abundant supply or plenty in general, while fullness stresses a filled condition or capacity.
