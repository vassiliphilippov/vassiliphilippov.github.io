nut

noun
eccentric or crazy person (informal)
8.5
Pronunciation: / nʌt / (AmE), / nʌt / (BrE)
an eccentric or crazy person, often in a humorous or light-hearted way

Examples

A crazy nut
Everyone in the neighborhood thinks Mr. Jenkins is a harmless nut with his wild stories.
My uncle is a real nut when it comes to collecting vintage radios.
Some people called her a nut, but she just loved to think differently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional or slightly strange views or behavior
  • degree lower: enthusiast - a person filled with enthusiasm, possibly to an unusual extent
  • degree higher: maniac - a person exhibiting extreme wildness or madness
