hulk

noun
very large or heavy person or thing
Pronunciation: / hʌlk / (AmE), / hʌlk / (BrE)
a very large or heavy person, animal, or thing; someone or something impressive in size or strength

Examples

A giant hulk
Despite his size, the gentle hulk had a soft heart.
Moving that old piano required a hulk of a man to lift it.
The football player was a real hulk compared to his teammates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dwarf - a person or thing much smaller than usual
  • synonym: giant - a person or thing of great size
