peacekeeping operation

noun
international military action to keep peace
Pronunciation: / ˈpisˌkipɪŋ ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌkiːpɪŋ ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a military activity intended to preserve or restore peace, especially where international military forces enforce a truce between hostile groups or nations

Examples

A peacekeeping operation mission
After years of conflict, a peacekeeping operation was launched to monitor the ceasefire.
Many countries contributed troops to the international peacekeeping operation in the Balkans.
The United Nations authorized a new peacekeeping operation to help stabilize the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peacekeeping - the maintenance of peace by military or police forces
