red eye

noun
overnight flight
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌaɪ / (BrE)
a late-night or overnight flight that departs late in the evening and arrives the next morning, often causing tiredness.

Examples

Take a red eye flight
She booked a red eye to save time and arrived early in the morning for her meeting.
Taking the red eye can be exhausting, but it lets you maximize your workday.
He tried to sleep on the red eye but still felt tired when he landed.
