embittered

adjective
angry and resentful
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɪtərd / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɪtəd / (BrE)
feeling or showing deep anger, resentment, or bitterness, especially because of unfair treatment or disappointment

Examples

Embittered by failure
After years of being overlooked, he grew embittered and withdrawn from his colleagues.
The embittered athlete spoke openly about the unfair decisions that affected her career.
She felt embittered by the constant disappointments she faced at work.
After losing the promotion, he became embittered toward his colleagues.
Years of unfair treatment left her feeling embittered and withdrawn.
The embittered community struggled to trust the new leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitter - feeling or showing anger and disappointment
  • antonym: content - feeling satisfied and not angry or resentful
  • synonym: resentful - feeling bitterness or anger about being treated unfairly
