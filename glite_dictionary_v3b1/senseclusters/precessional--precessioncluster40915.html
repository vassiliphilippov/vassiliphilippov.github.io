precessional

adjective
of axis movement
9.9
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈseʃ.ə.nəl/ /; / /prə.ˈseʃ.ə.nəl/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈseʃ.ə.nəl/ /; / /prə.ˈseʃ.ə.nəl/ (BrE)

Examples

The precessional cycle

Scientists study precessional changes to understand long-term climate patterns.
