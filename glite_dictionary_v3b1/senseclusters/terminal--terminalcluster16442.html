terminal

adjective
predicted to cause death (illness, condition)

(of an illness or condition) predicted to lead to death, especially slowly; fatal or incurable.

Examples

Terminal illness
Hospice care often supports those with terminal conditions in their final months.
She was diagnosed with terminal cancer last year.
The doctor informed the family that the patient had a terminal illness.
