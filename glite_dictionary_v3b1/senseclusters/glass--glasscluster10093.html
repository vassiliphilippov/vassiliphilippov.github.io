glass

verb
to make glossy
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
to cover or make a surface smooth and shiny like glass, often by applying a glossy coating

Examples

To glass the vase
The potter will glass the vase to give it a beautiful, reflective finish.
After sanding the wood, they decided to glass the surface for extra shine.
The artist carefully glassed the sculpture, making it look smooth and glossy.

  • synonym: glaze - to coat with a smooth, shiny surface
  • synonym: polish - to make something smooth and shiny by rubbing
