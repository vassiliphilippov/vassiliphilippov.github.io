rinsing

noun
removal of soap with clean water
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪnsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪnsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of removing soap or detergent from something by washing it with clean water, usually as the final stage of cleaning

Examples

Thorough rinsing water
After washing the dishes, a thorough rinsing removed all the suds.
Hair can feel sticky if you skip the final rinsing step while shampooing.
She gave the vegetables a quick rinsing under cold water before cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rinse - to wash something with clean water to remove soap or dirt
