protective garment

noun
clothing providing protection
5.8
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktɪv ˈɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪv ˈɡɑːmənt / (BrE)
A piece of clothing designed to shield the wearer from injury, hazards, or harmful substances.

Examples

A protective garment
Laboratory workers must wear protective garment to handle dangerous chemicals safely.
The firefighter's protective garment is made of heat-resistant material.
Welders rely on specialized protective garment to prevent burns and injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protective clothing - clothing worn to minimize exposure to hazards
