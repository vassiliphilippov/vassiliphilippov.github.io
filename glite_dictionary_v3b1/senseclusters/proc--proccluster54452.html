proc

noun
automatic game effect
9.9
Pronunciation: / prɑk / (AmE), / prɒk / (BrE)
an automatic effect or action that is triggered under certain conditions in a video game, often granting a bonus or special ability

Examples

A weapon proc
The sword's proc activated and healed the player during the final battle.
Many players rely on a random proc to deal extra damage in tough fights.
When the shield's proc triggered, it gave the character a burst of protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buff - a temporary increase in a character's abilities in a game
  • synonym: trigger - an event or condition that causes something to happen in a game
