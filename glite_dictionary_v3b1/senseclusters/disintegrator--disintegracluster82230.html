disintegrator

noun
cause of breakdown
8.4
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtər / (AmE), / dɪsˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes something to break apart or fall into pieces, especially an organization or group.

Examples

A disintegrator of teams
The new policy acted as a disintegrator within the company, leading to confusion and conflict.
He was seen as a disintegrator because his actions divided the community.
Rumors can be a powerful disintegrator of trust among friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destroyer - a person or thing that destroys something
  • antonym: unifier - a person or thing that brings things or people together
