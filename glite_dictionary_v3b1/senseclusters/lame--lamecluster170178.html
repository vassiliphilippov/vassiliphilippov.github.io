lame

noun
armor plate
Pronunciation: / leɪm / (AmE), / leɪm / (BrE)
a thin, flat piece of metal, especially one of the overlapping plates used in armor

Examples

Armor lame
The knight's armor was made of several lames joined together for flexibility.
Each lame was carefully shaped to fit over the next, creating a protective layer.
Museum visitors admired the intricate lame that once protected medieval warriors.
