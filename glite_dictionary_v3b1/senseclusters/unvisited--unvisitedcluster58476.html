unvisited

adjective
not yet viewed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɪzɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɪzɪtɪd / (BrE)
not yet opened, clicked, or viewed by a user, especially referring to a hyperlink, webpage, or file.

Examples

Unvisited link
The report showed that many unvisited links remained on the website after the update.
She checked her email and found several unvisited messages in her inbox.
The analytics dashboard highlights which pages are still unvisited by users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unopened - not yet opened or accessed
  • antonym: visited - already opened, clicked, or viewed by a user
