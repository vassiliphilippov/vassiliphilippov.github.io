richness

noun
deep, vivid hue or color quality
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a strong, deep, and vivid color or hue

Examples

Richness of color
Artists often strive for richness in their paintings by using layered pigments.
The richness of her dress's crimson hue made it stand out in the crowd.
The richness of the sunset's colors left everyone in awe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intensity - the strength or vividness of color; how bright or strong a color is
  • antonym: palor - lack of color, paleness
