powder horn

noun
container for carrying gunpowder
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə hɔːn / (BrE)
A container made from the hollow horn of an animal, used for carrying gunpowder, especially in the past.

Examples

A powder horn container
A beautifully carved powder horn was displayed in the museum's colonial exhibit.
Early hunters often carried a powder horn slung across their shoulder.
The soldier filled his powder horn before heading into battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartridge box - a container for carrying cartridges for a firearm
