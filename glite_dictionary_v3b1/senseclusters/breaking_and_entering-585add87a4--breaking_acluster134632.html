Synonyms

  • burglary
    3.0 7/m
    burglary is a legal charge used in courts for entering to steal, while breaking and entering describes the act of illegal forced entry.
  • break-in
    3.9 4/m
    break-in names a sudden illegal entry to steal or vandalize, while breaking and entering is the formal phrase for that act.
  • housebreaking
    8.2 0.03/m
