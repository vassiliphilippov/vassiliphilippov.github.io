pretty

verb
to make attractive
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪti / (AmE), / ˈprɪti / (BrE)
to make something look more attractive or pleasant, often by adding decorations or improvements

Examples

She prettied up the living room with new curtains and cushions.
They pretty the garden every spring by planting colorful flowers.
