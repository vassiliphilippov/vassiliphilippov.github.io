sluggy

adjective
slow and lacking energy
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡi / (BrE)
moving or feeling slow and lacking energy, as if sluggish or tired

Examples

Feeling sluggy after lunch
After a big lunch, I always feel a bit sluggy and want to take a nap.
He was too sluggy to go for a run early in the morning.
The rainy weather made everyone in the office feel sluggy and unmotivated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lethargic - feeling a lack of energy or enthusiasm
  • synonym: sluggish - moving slowly or lacking energy
