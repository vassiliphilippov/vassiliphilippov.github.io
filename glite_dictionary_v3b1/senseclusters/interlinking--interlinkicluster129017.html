interlinking

noun
internal linking practice (SEO)
4.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈlɪŋ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ən.tər.ˈlɪŋ.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈlɪŋ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ən.tə.ˈlɪŋ.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in web publishing and SEO) the practice or technique of creating internal links between pages on a website to improve navigation, user experience, and search-engine optimization.

Examples

Website interlinking
A robust content strategy relies on interlinking across website pages to boost discoverability.
Good interlinking helps users find related articles and increases the site's search rankings.
During the audit, they recommended improving interlinking between category pages and product descriptions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: internal linking - the practice of linking one page of a website to another page on the same site
  • synonym: internal links - links that connect pages within the same website; used to support navigation and SEO
