armed combat

noun
battle between armed forces
1.0 4.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrmd ˈkɑmˌbæt / (AmE), / ɑːmd ˈkɒm.bæt / (BrE)
a battle or engagement in which opposing military forces use weapons against each other

Examples

Armed combat mission
He was injured during an armed combat mission overseas.
International law sets rules for the conduct of soldiers during armed combat.
The two countries were drawn into armed combat after months of rising tensions.
