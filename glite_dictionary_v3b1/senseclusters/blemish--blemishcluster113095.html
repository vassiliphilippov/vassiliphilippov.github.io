blemish

noun
a mark or flaw spoiling appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛmɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈblɛmɪʃ / (BrE)
a small mark, flaw, or imperfection that spoils the appearance of something, especially on the skin

Examples

A skin blemish
She was self-conscious about the blemish on her cheek before the big event.
The antique vase had only one minor blemish, but it affected its value.
Using concealer can help cover a blemish for a flawless look.
