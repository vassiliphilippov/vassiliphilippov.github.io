editorially

adverb
in an editorial way
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəli / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to editing, editors, or the editorial process of a publication

Examples

Editorially review content
Editorially, the newspaper strives to remain unbiased in its reporting.
The article was editorially revised to improve clarity and accuracy.
The magazine is editorially independent from its advertisers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editorial - relating to the preparation of material for publication
