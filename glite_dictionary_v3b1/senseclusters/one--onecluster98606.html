one

noun
a single person or thing
Pronunciation: / wʌn / (AmE), / wʌn / (BrE)
a single person or thing; a single unit distinct from others, often used to refer to a particular individual or item when the identity is known or has been mentioned.

Examples

No one else
No one could have predicted such an outcome.
She is the only one who knows the truth.
This one is my favorite out of all the paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individual - a single human being or distinct object
  • antonym: none - not any; not a single person or thing
