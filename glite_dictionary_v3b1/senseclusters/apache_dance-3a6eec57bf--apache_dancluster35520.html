apache dance

noun
a dramatic French vaudeville dance
9.0 8.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpætʃ.iː dæns/ /; / /ə.ˈpætʃ.i dæns/ /; / /ə.ˈpætʃ.iː dɑːns/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpætʃ.i dɑːns/ /; / /ə.ˈpætʃ.iː dɑːns/ (BrE)
