garlic butter

noun
butter flavored with garlic
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
Butter that has been flavored with mashed or minced garlic, often used as a spread or for cooking.

Examples

Garlic butter spread
She spread garlic butter on warm bread for an appetizer.
The steak was topped with a generous spoonful of garlic butter.
You can easily make garlic butter by mixing minced garlic into softened butter.
