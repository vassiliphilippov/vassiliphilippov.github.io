cultural experience

noun
exposure to arts or traditions
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃɚəl ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ɪkˈspɪərɪəns / (BrE)
an activity or event that involves participation in or exposure to the arts, customs, or lifestyle of a society or community

Examples

A cultural experience event
The festival was a fantastic cultural experience for everyone involved.
Traveling abroad provides a unique cultural experience that broadens your understanding of the world.
Visiting museums is often considered an important cultural experience for students.
