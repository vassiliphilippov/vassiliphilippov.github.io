astrochemistry

noun
chemistry of space
Pronunciation: / ˌæstroʊˈkɛmɪstri / (AmE), / ˌæstrəʊˈkɛmɪstri / (BrE)
the scientific study of the chemical composition and reactions of matter in space, especially in stars and interstellar clouds

Examples

Astrochemistry research
Researchers in astrochemistry analyze the molecules found in distant galaxies.
Advances in astrochemistry have revealed how complex organic compounds form in space.
The field of astrochemistry combines knowledge from both astronomy and chemistry.
