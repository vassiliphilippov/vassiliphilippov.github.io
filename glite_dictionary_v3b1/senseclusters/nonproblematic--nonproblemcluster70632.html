nonproblematic

adjective
not causing problems
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌprɑbləˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌprɒbləˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not causing problems or difficulties; not problematic

Examples

A nonproblematic situation
The new policy was considered nonproblematic by most members of the committee.
Doctors found the side effects of the treatment to be largely nonproblematic for patients.
Her suggestion was so nonproblematic that everyone agreed without hesitation.

Related Terms
