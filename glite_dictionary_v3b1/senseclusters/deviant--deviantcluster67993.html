deviant

adjective
markedly different from the norm
Pronunciation: / ˈdiː.vi.ənt / (AmE), / ˈdiː.vi.ənt / (BrE)
departing markedly from accepted standards, especially in social or moral behavior

Examples

Deviant behavior
His deviant behavior eventually led him into conflict with the law.
Teachers are trained to recognize deviant conduct in the classroom.
The artist was known for her deviant views on society and culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aberrant - departing from an accepted standard
  • antonym: conventional - based on or in accordance with what is generally done or believed
  • antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected
