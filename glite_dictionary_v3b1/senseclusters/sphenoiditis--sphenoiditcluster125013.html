sphenoiditis

noun
inflammation of sphenoid sinus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsfiːnɔɪˈdaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌsfiːnɔɪˈdaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the sphenoid sinus, a cavity located deep within the skull behind the eyes

Examples

Sphenoiditis inflammation
The patient complained of headaches, which were later traced to sphenoiditis.
A CT scan revealed that she was suffering from sphenoiditis.
Treatment for sphenoiditis often includes antibiotics and nasal decongestants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhinosinusitis - inflammation of the nasal cavity and sinuses
  • synonym: sinusitis - inflammation of any of the sinuses
