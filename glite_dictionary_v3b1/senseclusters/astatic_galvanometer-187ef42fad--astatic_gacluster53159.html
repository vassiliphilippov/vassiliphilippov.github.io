astatic galvanometer

noun
galvanometer with astatic coils
5.1
Pronunciation: / əˈstætɪk ˌɡælvəˈnɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / əˈstætɪk ˌɡælvəˈnɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a type of galvanometer with a moving magnet and astatic coils designed to cancel the effect of the Earth's magnetic field

Examples

An astatic galvanometer device
Researchers selected an astatic galvanometer to improve the accuracy of their sensitive measurements.
The astatic galvanometer is used in experiments where the Earth's magnetic field must be neutralized.
Unlike ordinary galvanometers, an astatic galvanometer is much less affected by environmental magnetic disturbances.
