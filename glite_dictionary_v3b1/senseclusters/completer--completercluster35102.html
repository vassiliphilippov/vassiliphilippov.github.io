completer

noun
auto-suggestion tool
6.1
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpliːtər / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːtə / (BrE)
a software tool or feature that suggests or automatically fills in the rest of a word, code, or text as you type

Examples

Text completer
The completer in my email app helps me finish addresses quickly.
Many programmers rely on a code completer to speed up their workflow.
As you type, the completer suggests possible words to save you time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocorrect - a feature that automatically corrects spelling or typing errors
