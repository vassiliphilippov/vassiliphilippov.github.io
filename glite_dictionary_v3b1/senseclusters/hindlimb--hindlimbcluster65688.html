hindlimb

noun
back leg or rear limb of an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndˌlɪm / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndlɪm / (BrE)
a posterior limb or appendage, such as a back leg, especially in four-legged animals

Examples

A hindlimb muscle
A kangaroo's large hindlimb muscles enable it to move quickly across open plains.
An injury to the cheetah's hindlimb affected its ability to sprint.
The frog uses its powerful hindlimbs to leap great distances between lily pads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forelimb - the anterior appendage or front leg of an animal
