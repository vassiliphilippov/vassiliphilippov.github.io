unhedged

adjective
not protected financially
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛdʒd / (BrE)
not protected against possible financial loss or risk, especially in investments or currency exchange.

Examples

Unhedged investment
The company left its foreign earnings unhedged and suffered from currency fluctuations.
Investors with unhedged positions may face greater losses if the market moves unexpectedly.
He warned that an unhedged portfolio could be very risky in volatile times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exposed - open to risk or danger without protection
  • antonym: hedged - protected against financial risk or loss
