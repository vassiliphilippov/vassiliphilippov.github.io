Boolean

adjective
relating to Boole's logic system
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbuː.li.ən / (AmE), / ˈbuː.li.ən / (BrE)
Relating to a system of algebra or logic developed by George Boole, involving logical operators such as AND, OR, and NOT.

Examples

Boolean expression
A Boolean expression can only be true or false.
The search engine uses Boolean operators to refine your results.
You can filter the database by applying Boolean logic to your queries.
