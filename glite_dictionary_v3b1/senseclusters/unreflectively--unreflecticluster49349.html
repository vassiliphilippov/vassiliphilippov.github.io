unreflectively

adverb
without careful thought
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that shows little or no careful thought or consideration

Examples

To agree unreflectively
She unreflectively agreed to the plan without asking any questions.
Many people unreflectively follow trends without considering their own preferences.
He unreflectively repeated the rumor, not realizing it was false.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impulsively - in a way that is done without thinking carefully
  • antonym: thoughtfully - in a way that shows careful thought or consideration
