angular distance

noun
apparent angle between two objects
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələr dɪˈstɑːns / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡjʊlə dɪˈstɑːns / (BrE)
the apparent angle between two objects as seen by an observer, measured in degrees, minutes, and seconds

Examples

Angular distance between stars
Astronomers calculated the angular distance between the stars to determine their positions.
From my vantage point, the angular distance between the mountaintops seemed quite small.
The angular distance between the two planets can be measured with a telescope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angular separation - the angle between two directions from a given point to two objects
