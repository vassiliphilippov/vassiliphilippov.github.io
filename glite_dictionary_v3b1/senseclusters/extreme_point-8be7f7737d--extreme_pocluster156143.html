extreme point

noun
furthest point from the center
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrim pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːm pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the point that is farthest from the center or middle of something, often used in geometry and geography

Examples

The extreme point of mountain

The extreme point of the peninsula is marked by a lighthouse.
