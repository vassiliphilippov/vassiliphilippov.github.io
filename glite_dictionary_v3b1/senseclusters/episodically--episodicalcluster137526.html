episodically

adverb
in an episodic or occasional way
6.3 6.0
Pronunciation: /ep.ɪ.ˈsɑː.dɪ.kliː/ /; / /ep.ɪ.ˈsɔː.dɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /ep.ɪ.ˈsɒ.dɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
In an episodic manner; occurring or appearing at irregular intervals or in separate episodes.

Examples

To work episodically
He worked on the novel episodically, often taking long breaks between writing sessions.
The TV series was released episodically, with one episode airing each week.
The symptoms appeared episodically over the course of several months.
