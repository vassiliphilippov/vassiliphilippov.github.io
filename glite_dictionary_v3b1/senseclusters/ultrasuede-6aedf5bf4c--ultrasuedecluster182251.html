Ultrasuede

noun
brand of synthetic suede fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌsweɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəˌsweɪd / (BrE)
a brand of synthetic suede fabric made from a blend of polyester and polyurethane, designed to resemble natural suede

Examples

Ultrasuede fabric
Many designers prefer Ultrasuede for car interiors due to its versatility and appearance.
She bought a jacket made from Ultrasuede because it was easier to clean than real suede.
The sofa was upholstered in Ultrasuede, giving it a luxurious yet durable feel.
