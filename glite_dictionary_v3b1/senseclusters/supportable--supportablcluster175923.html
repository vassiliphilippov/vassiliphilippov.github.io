supportable

adjective
able to be justified
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be justified, defended, or maintained as reasonable or valid

Examples

Supportable argument
The theory is only supportable if new evidence is discovered.
Her decision was not supportable given the facts presented in court.
It is a supportable claim, but it still needs more research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defensible - able to be defended or justified
  • antonym: indefensible - impossible to defend or justify
  • synonym: justifiable - able to be shown as reasonable or right
