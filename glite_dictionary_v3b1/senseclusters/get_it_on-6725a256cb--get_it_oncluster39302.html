get it on

verb
start or engage in an activity
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to start or become actively involved in an activity, especially with energy or enthusiasm

Examples

To get it on and dance
Everyone was waiting for the band to get it on and play the first song.
It's time to get it on with our group discussion.
She encouraged us to get it on and make the most of the afternoon.
