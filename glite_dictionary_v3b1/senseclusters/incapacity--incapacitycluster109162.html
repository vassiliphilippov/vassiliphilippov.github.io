incapacity

noun
lack of intellectual ability
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.kə.ˈpæs.ə.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.kə.ˈpæs.ɪ.ti/ (AmE), /ɪn.kə.ˈpæs.ɪ.ti/ /; / /ɪn.kə.ˈpæs.ə.ti/ (BrE)
the state of not being able to do something because of a lack of physical or mental ability or power
