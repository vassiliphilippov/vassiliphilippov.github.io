La-La Land

noun
state of unrealistic or blissful ignorance
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑˌlɑ lænd / (AmE), / ˈlɑːˌlɑː lænd / (BrE)
a state of being out of touch with reality, especially characterized by blissful ignorance or fanciful thinking

Examples

La-La Land state
After the anesthesia, he was floating in La-La Land and didn't recognize anyone in the room.
Some critics argue that the plan is based on ideas from La-La Land, not reality.
Whenever my boss proposes impossible deadlines, I think she's living in La-La Land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamworld - an imaginary or unrealistic realm or state of mind
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
