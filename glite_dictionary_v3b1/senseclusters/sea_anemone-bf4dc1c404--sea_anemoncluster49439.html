sea anemone

noun
flower-like marine animal with tentacles
9.7 6.0
Pronunciation: / si əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / si əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
a marine animal that looks like a flower, having a ring of tentacles around its mouth, but does not form a hard skeleton like corals
