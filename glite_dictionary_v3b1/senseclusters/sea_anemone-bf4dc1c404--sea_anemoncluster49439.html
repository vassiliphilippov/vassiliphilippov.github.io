sea anemone

noun
flower-like marine animal with tentacles
9.7
Pronunciation: / si əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / si əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
a marine animal that looks like a flower, having a ring of tentacles around its mouth, but does not form a hard skeleton like corals

Examples

A sea anemone animal
Clownfish are known for their symbiotic relationship with the sea anemone.
The diver marveled at the bright colors of the sea anemone attached to the rock.
Unlike corals, a sea anemone does not build a hard skeleton.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coral - marine animals forming colonies of polyps, often building hard skeletons
