faro

noun
gambling card game
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
A gambling card game in which players bet against the dealer on which cards will be drawn from the deck.

Examples

The faro game
He quickly lost all his money at the faro table.
In the 1800s, faro was one of the most popular games in the American West.
The saloon was filled with men playing faro late into the night.
