vinegarishness

noun
quality of being sour like vinegar
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪnəɡərɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɪnɪɡərɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being sour or sharp-tasting like vinegar

Examples

Slight vinegarishness in wine
Some wines develop a slight vinegarishness if stored improperly.
The vinegarishness of the sauce overwhelmed the delicate flavor of the fish.
There was a distinct vinegarishness to the salad dressing that I didn't enjoy.

  • synonym: sourness - the quality of having a tart or acidic taste
