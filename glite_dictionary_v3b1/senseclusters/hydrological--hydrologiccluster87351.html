hydrological

adjective
about water movement
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the movement, distribution, or properties of water in the natural environment

Examples

Hydrological cycle
Scientists study hydrological patterns to predict floods and droughts.
The region's hydrological system is affected by seasonal rainfall and snowmelt.
Understanding hydrological cycles is important for managing water resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorological - relating to the science of weather and atmosphere
