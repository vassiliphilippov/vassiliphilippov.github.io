amphibious demonstration

noun
military feint using land and sea forces
6.4
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiəs ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / æmˈfɪbiəs ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a military operation involving land and sea forces, carried out to deceive the enemy and influence their actions unfavorably

Examples

An amphibious demonstration operation
Historical records show that an amphibious demonstration was key to the success of the campaign.
The fleet conducted an amphibious demonstration along the enemy coast to draw attention away from the main landing site.
The general ordered an amphibious demonstration to mislead the opposing forces before the real assault began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amphibious operation - a military operation involving both land and naval forces
