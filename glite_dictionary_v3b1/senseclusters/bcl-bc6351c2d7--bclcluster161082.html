BCL

noun
B-cell lymphoma cancer
Pronunciation: / bi si ɛl / (AmE), / biː siː ɛl / (BrE)
an abbreviation for B-cell lymphoma, a type of cancer that affects B lymphocyte white blood cells

Examples

BCL diagnosis
The oncologist confirmed that the patient had BCL after reviewing the test results.
Treatment options for BCL may include chemotherapy, radiation, or immunotherapy.
Early diagnosis of BCL can improve the chances of successful treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leukemia - a cancer of blood-forming tissues, including bone marrow and lymphatic system
  • synonym: lymphoma - a type of cancer that begins in the lymphatic system
