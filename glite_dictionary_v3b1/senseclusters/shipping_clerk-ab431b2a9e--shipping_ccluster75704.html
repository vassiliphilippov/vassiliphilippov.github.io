shipping clerk

noun
employee overseeing shipment and receipt of goods
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɪp.ɪŋ klɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈʃɪp.ɪŋ klɑːk/ (BrE)
