shipping clerk

noun
employee overseeing shipment and receipt of goods
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpɪŋ klɜrk / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpɪŋ klɑːk / (BrE)
an employee whose job is to oversee the shipment and receipt of goods in a warehouse or business

Examples

Shipping clerk checks packages
As a shipping clerk, she was responsible for checking packages for damage upon arrival.
Our company is looking to hire an experienced shipping clerk to manage inventory records.
The shipping clerk prepared the paperwork for the outgoing deliveries each morning.

Related Terms
