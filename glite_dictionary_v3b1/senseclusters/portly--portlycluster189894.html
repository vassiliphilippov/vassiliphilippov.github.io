portly

adjective
(of a man) somewhat fat or corpulent
6.1 0.97/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…îrtli / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀêtli / (BrE)
(especially of a man) having a rather large, rounded body; somewhat fat, typically in a dignified or stately way.

Examples

A portly man
Even as he grew older and portly, he carried himself with pride and dignity.
The portly gentleman struggled to button his suit jacket after lunch.
The mayor, a portly man with a booming laugh, greeted everyone at the event.

Synonyms

  • stout
    3.9 0.28/m
    stout describes a solid, pleasantly heavy build often used for women, while portly suggests a dignified, stately heaviness often used for men.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slender - gracefully thin
  • synonym: stout - (of a person) somewhat fat or thickset, especially for women
