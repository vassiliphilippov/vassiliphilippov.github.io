dress hanger

noun
hanger shaped for garments
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛs ˌhæŋər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛs ˌhæŋə / (BrE)
a hanger shaped like human shoulders, used to hang clothes such as dresses, shirts, or jackets

Examples

A wooden dress hanger
A wooden dress hanger helps maintain the shape of jackets and coats.
She hung her new dress on a dress hanger to keep it wrinkle-free.
The closet was filled with dress hangers holding suits and evening gowns.
