paradigmatically

adverb
in relation to a paradigm
9.5
Pronunciation: /pæ.rə.dɪg.ˈmæt.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /pæ.rə.dɪg.ˈmæt.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that relates to a paradigm or set of alternatives, especially considering possible substitutions within a system.

Examples

Analyze paradigmatically
In linguistics, words can be analyzed paradigmatically to see which ones can replace each other in a sentence.
The researcher compared the terms paradigmatically to understand their roles in the grammatical system.
Pronouns like 'he', 'she', and 'they' are grouped paradigmatically because they can substitute for one another.
