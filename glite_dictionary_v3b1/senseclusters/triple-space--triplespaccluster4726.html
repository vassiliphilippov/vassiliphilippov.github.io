triple-space

verb
type text with double empty lines between each line
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpəl speɪs / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpl speɪs / (BrE)
to type or format text so that there are two empty lines between each line of text

Examples

To triple-space text
Please triple-space your essay to make it easier to read.
She triple-spaced the document as requested by her professor.
When editing, I prefer to triple-space my notes for clarity.
