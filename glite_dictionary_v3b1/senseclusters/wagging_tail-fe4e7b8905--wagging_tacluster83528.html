wagging tail

noun
tail moving side to side in happiness/excitement (dogs)
8.7 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡɪŋ teɪl / (AmE), / ˈwæɡɪŋ teɪl / (BrE)
(in dogs) a tail that is moving from side to side as a sign of happiness or excitement.

Examples

A wagging tail
A wagging tail is often a clear indication that a dog is friendly and happy.
The dog's wagging tail showed how excited it was to see its owner.
Whenever treats are mentioned, her puppy greets her with a wagging tail.
