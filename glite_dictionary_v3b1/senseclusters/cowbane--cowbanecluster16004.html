cowbane

noun
poisonous plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a poisonous plant of the parsley family that is harmful to livestock if eaten

Examples

Poisonous cowbane plant
Farmers warn that cowbane can be deadly to cattle if ingested.
The field was cleared of cowbane to protect the grazing sheep.
Children are taught to avoid touching cowbane because of its toxic properties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemlock - a poisonous plant of the parsley family
  • synonym: water hemlock - a highly poisonous plant related to cowbane
