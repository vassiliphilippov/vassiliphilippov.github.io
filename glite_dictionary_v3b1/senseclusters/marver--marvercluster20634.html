marver

verb
to shape hot glass
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːr.vər/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.və/ (BrE)
to shape, smooth, or flatten hot molten glass by rolling or pressing it on a flat surface during glassblowing

Examples

To marver hot glass
The glassblower will marver the molten glass to achieve a perfectly smooth surface.
After gathering the glass, she carefully marvered it to prepare for the next step.
It is important to marver the glass evenly to avoid imperfections in the final piece.
