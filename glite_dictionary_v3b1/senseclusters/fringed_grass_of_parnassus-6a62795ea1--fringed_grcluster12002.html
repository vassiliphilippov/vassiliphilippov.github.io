fringed grass of Parnassus

noun
bog plant with fringed white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒd ɡræs əv pɑrˈnæsəs / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ɡrɑːs ɒv pɑːˈnasəs / (BrE)
a bog plant native to western North America, with broadly heart-shaped basal leaves and cream or white saucer-shaped flowers with fringed petals
