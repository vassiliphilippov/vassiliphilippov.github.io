nondeterministic

adjective
not having a fixed outcome
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˌtɜrmɪˈnɪstɪk /; / ˌnɑndɪtɚˈmɪnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
describing a process, algorithm, or model that can have different outcomes even with the same input (often used in computer science)
