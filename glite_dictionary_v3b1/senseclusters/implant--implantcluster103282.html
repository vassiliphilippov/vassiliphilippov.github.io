implant

verb
(of embryo) attach in uterus
3.9 6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplænt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɑːnt / (BrE)
(of a fertilized egg) to become attached to and embedded in the lining of the uterus.

Examples

Egg implants in uterus
Doctors monitored how the transferred embryo implanted without intervention.
The embryo implanted itself into the uterine wall during the early stage of development.
When the fertilized egg successfully implants, pregnancy can proceed normally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embed - to become fixed firmly in a surrounding substance
