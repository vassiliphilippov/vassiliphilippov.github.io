adjuster

noun
person who handles insurance claims
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒʌstɚ / (AmE), / əˈdʒʌstə / (BrE)
a person who investigates insurance claims and recommends settlements to resolve them

Examples

Insurance adjuster
After the car accident, an adjuster was sent by the insurance company to assess the damage.
Each adjuster handles dozens of insurance claims every month.
The homeowner was relieved when the adjuster approved her claim for repairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: claims assessor - person who evaluates insurance claims to determine compensation
  • antonym: insurer - company or person that provides insurance coverage
