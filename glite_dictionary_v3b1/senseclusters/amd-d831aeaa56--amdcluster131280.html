AMD

noun
age-related eye disease causing vision loss
4.8 1.56/m

age-related macular degeneration; a medical condition that causes progressive deterioration of the macula, leading to loss of central vision, usually in older adults.

Examples

AMD eye disease
Recent advances in treatment have helped slow the progression of AMD.
Regular eye exams are important in detecting early signs of AMD.
The doctor explained that my grandmother has AMD, which is why her central vision is blurry.
