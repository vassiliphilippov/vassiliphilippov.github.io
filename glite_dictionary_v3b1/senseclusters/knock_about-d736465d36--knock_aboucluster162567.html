knock about

verb
remain alive or active
6.8
Pronunciation: / nɑk əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / nɒk əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) To continue to be alive, present, or active, especially in an unremarkable way.

Examples

Old friends knocking about
My grandfather is still knocking about at ninety.
That rumor has been knocking about for years.
There are a few old cars knocking about in the garage.
