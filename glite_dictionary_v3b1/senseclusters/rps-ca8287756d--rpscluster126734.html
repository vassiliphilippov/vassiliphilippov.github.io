RPS

noun
hand game of chance
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɑr pi ɛs / (AmE), / ɑː piː ɛs / (BrE)
a game in which players simultaneously form one of three shapes with their hand—rock, paper, or scissors—to determine a winner
