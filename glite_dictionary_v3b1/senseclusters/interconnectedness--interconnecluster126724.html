interconnectedness

noun
mutual connection or relationship
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚkəˈnɛktɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkəˈnɛktɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being mutually connected or related to each other

Examples

Global interconnectedness
Advances in technology have increased the interconnectedness of people across the globe.
Many spiritual traditions emphasize the interconnectedness of all living things.
The interconnectedness of global economies makes local events impactful worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a link or relationship between people or things
  • antonym: isolation - the state of being separate from others
