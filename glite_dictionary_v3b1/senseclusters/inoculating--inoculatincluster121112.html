inoculating

noun
act of administering a vaccine
6.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈnɑː.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ɪ.ˈnɑː.kjə.leɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈnɒ.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ɪ.ˈnɒ.kjə.leɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
