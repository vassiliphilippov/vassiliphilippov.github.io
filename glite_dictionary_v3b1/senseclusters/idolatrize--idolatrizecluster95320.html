idolatrize

verb
to worship as idol
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɑːləˌtraɪz / (AmE), / aɪˈdɒləˌtraɪz / (BrE)
to treat or worship someone or something as an idol, showing excessive admiration or devotion

Examples

To idolatrize a star
Many people idolatrize famous athletes, following their every move on social media.
It is unhealthy to idolatrize someone to the point of ignoring their flaws.
Some children idolatrize their teachers, seeing them as perfect role models.

Related Terms

  • antonym: despise - to strongly dislike or have contempt for someone or something
  • synonym: idolize - to admire or worship someone or something greatly
  • synonym: revere - to feel deep respect or admiration for someone or something
