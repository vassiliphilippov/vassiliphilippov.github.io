Erythronium californicum

noun
a California wildflower with creamy white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛˌrɪθroʊniəm ˌkælɪˈfɔrnɪkəm / (AmE), / ɛˌrɪθrəʊniəm ˌkælɪˈfɔːnɪkəm / (BrE)
a species of flowering plant in the lily family native to California, known as the California dogtooth violet, with creamy white flowers sometimes tinged with yellow.
