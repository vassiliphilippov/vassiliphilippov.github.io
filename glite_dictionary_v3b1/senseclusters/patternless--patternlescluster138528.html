patternless

adjective
without a design
Pronunciation: / ˈpætərnləs / (AmE), / ˈpætənləs / (BrE)
having no visible, regular, or repeated design or arrangement

Examples

A patternless design
The artist chose a patternless canvas to highlight the bold colors of her painting.
He preferred patternless shirts because they matched with everything in his wardrobe.
The living room was decorated with patternless curtains in a soft, neutral shade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decorated - having a pattern or ornamentation
  • synonym: plain - having no pattern or decoration
