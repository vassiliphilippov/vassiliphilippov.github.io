unboundedness

noun
the state of being limitless
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbaʊndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈbaʊndɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having no limits or bounds; the state of being infinite

Examples

The unboundedness of space
Her imagination seemed to have an unboundedness that inspired everyone around her.
The concept of unboundedness is central to certain branches of mathematics.
We cannot easily comprehend the unboundedness of the universe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finiteness - the quality of having limits or bounds
  • synonym: infinitude - the state of being infinite or having no limit
  • synonym: limitlessness - the quality of having no limits
