livestream

noun
real-time video broadcast
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪvstriːm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvstriːm / (BrE)
a live video broadcast that is transmitted over the internet in real time

Examples

A livestream event
Thousands of fans tuned in to the livestream of the championship game.
The museum offered a livestream of the new exhibition for people who could not attend in person.
She hosted a livestream to answer questions from her followers in real time.
