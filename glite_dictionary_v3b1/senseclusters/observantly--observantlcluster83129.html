observantly

adverb
in an observant or attentive way
1.5
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝːvəntli / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːvəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful observation or attentiveness.

Examples

Look observantly at details
Children often look observantly at new environments, taking in every detail.
She listened observantly to the instructions before starting the task.
The detective watched the suspects observantly, noting every subtle gesture.
