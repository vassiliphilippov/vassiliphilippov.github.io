skin

verb
remove the skin from
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
to remove the skin from something, especially a fruit, vegetable, or animal

Examples

To skin a rabbit
I always skin the peaches before making a pie.
She carefully skinned the rabbit for dinner.
You need to skin the tomatoes before adding them to the sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pare - remove the outer covering from something, especially with a knife
  • synonym: peel - remove the outer covering of a fruit or vegetable
