nutriment

noun
substance for nourishment
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrɪmənt / (BrE)
a substance that provides nourishment or promotes growth and health

Examples

Healthy nutriment
Certain seeds provide excellent nutriment for birds during the winter.
Soil rich in minerals supplies ample nutriment for healthy plant growth.
The soup was prepared to give the sick child extra nutriment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutrient - a substance that provides nourishment essential for growth and life
