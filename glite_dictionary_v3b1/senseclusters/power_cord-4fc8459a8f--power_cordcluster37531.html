power cord

noun
electrical cable for appliances
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊɚ kɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə kɔːd / (BrE)
a flexible cable that supplies electrical power to an appliance or device from a power source

Examples

The power cord plug
Always unplug the power cord before cleaning the appliance for your safety.
She tripped over the power cord stretched across the floor.
The laptop wouldn't turn on because its power cord was missing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extension cord - a long, flexible electric cable with a plug at one end and one or more sockets at the other
