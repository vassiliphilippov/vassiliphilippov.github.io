homestead

noun
family home with adjoining land
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌstɛd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm.stɛd /; / ˈhəʊmstɛd / (BrE)
a house and the land around it, especially in the countryside or used for farming

Examples
