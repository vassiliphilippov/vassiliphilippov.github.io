sundried

adjective
dried naturally by the sun
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌdraɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌdraɪd / (BrE)
(of food or other material) dried naturally by exposure to the sun.

Examples

Sundried apricots
He prefers sundried apricots over the regular, as they have a richer taste.
She topped the salad with sundried tomatoes for extra flavor.
The sundried figs are sweet and chewy, perfect for snacking.
