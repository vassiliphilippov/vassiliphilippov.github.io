aww

interjection
expression of affection
Pronunciation: / ɑː / (AmE), / ɔː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express affection, sympathy, or delight, especially in response to something cute or touching

Examples

Aww, so cute
When she saw the tiny kitten sleeping, she said, "aww, that's so sweet!"
He showed me a picture of his new puppy, and I couldn't help but say, "aww!"
The children hugged their mother, and everyone watching said, "aww, how adorable!"

Related Terms

  • synonym: oh - an exclamation expressing surprise or emotion
  • antonym: ugh - an exclamation expressing disgust or annoyance
