nonrecurring

adjective
not repeated
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈkɜr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈkɜː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
not happening again or not repeating; not expected to occur repeatedly

Examples

The company reported a nonrecurring expense in its annual statement.

In mathematics, a nonrecurring decimal does not repeat any pattern of digits.
