spirit

verb
fill with liveliness or enthusiasm
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
to fill someone or something with liveliness, courage, or enthusiasm

Examples

To spirit up courage
Her speech really spirited the team before the big game.
The music spirited the crowd into a joyful frenzy.
We tried to spirit him up after his disappointment.
