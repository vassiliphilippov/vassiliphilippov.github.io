twitcher

noun
birdwatching enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtʃə / (BrE)
a person who travels to see rare birds as a hobby

Examples

A twitcher spots birds
The twitcher traveled hundreds of miles to spot a rare owl in Scotland.
Many twitchers keep detailed lists of all the birds they have seen.
A group of twitchers gathered early in the morning to catch a glimpse of the migrating stork.
The experienced twitcher kept a detailed list of every rare bird he had seen.
Dozens of twitchers gathered at the reserve to catch a glimpse of the rare stork.
As a dedicated twitcher, she often traveled long distances for a single sighting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birder - a person who observes or identifies wild birds
  • synonym: birdwatcher - someone who watches and studies wild birds
  • synonym: ornithologist - a scientist who studies birds
