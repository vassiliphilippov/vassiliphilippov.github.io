warn

verb
order to stay away or leave
Pronunciation: / wɔrn / (AmE), / wɔːn / (BrE)
to order someone to go away or stay away from a place as a warning, often because they are not welcome or are trespassing

Examples

To warn trespassers
Security warned us not to enter the construction site without permission.
She warned the children off the dangerous frozen lake.
The landowner warned the hikers to leave his property immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warn off - to order someone to keep away from a place
