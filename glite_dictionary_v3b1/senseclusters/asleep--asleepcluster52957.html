asleep

adjective
numb; lacking sensation in a limb
2.9 0.7/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàslip / (AmE), / …ôÀàsliÀêp / (BrE)
(of a limb or body part) numb or lacking sensation, usually as a result of temporary interference with blood flow or nerve signals.

Examples

Hand asleep
After sitting awkwardly, my hand fell asleep and I couldn't feel my fingers.
If your foot is asleep, wait a few moments for the tingling to go away.
She shook her leg because it had gone asleep during the long meeting.
