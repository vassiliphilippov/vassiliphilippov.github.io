independista

noun
independence supporter
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪpɛnˈdistə / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪpɛnˈdiːstə / (BrE)
a supporter or advocate of independence, especially in a political context

Examples

A proud independista
The independista movement gained momentum after the election results were announced.
Many young people identified as independistas and participated in the rally.
The government met with independista leaders to discuss possible reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: separatist - a person who supports separation from a larger group or country
  • antonym: unionist - a person who supports political union, especially with a larger country
