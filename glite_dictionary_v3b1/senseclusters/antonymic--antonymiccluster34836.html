antonymic

adjective
relating to antonyms
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəˈnɪmɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntəˈnɪmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of antonyms, or having the nature of being opposite in meaning

Examples

Antonymic words
The two words have an antonymic relationship, meaning they are opposites.
In language studies, antonymic pairs like 'light' and 'dark' are often discussed.
Her explanation focused on the antonymic nature of the terms 'success' and 'failure'.
