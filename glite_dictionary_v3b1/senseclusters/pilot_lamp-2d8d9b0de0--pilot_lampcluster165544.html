pilot lamp

noun
small indicator light on a device
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət læmp / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət lamp / (BrE)
a small light on a device that indicates whether power is on or a circuit is functioning

Examples

A pilot lamp light
A red pilot lamp on the dashboard signals that something needs your attention.
If the pilot lamp isn't lit, check if the appliance is plugged in.
The pilot lamp above the switch glows when the machine is running.
