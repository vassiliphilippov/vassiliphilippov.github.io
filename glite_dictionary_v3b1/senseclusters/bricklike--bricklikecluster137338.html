bricklike

adjective
resembling a brick
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a brick in shape, appearance, or texture

Examples

Bricklike shape
The old factory had a bricklike facade that gave it an industrial feel.
Her suitcase was so heavy and bricklike that she struggled to lift it onto the train.
The dessert was supposed to be light, but it turned out dense and bricklike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blocky - having a shape like a block; chunky or solid
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or blocky
