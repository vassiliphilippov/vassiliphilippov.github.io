tandem trailer

noun
truck rig with two trailers in line
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtændəm ˈtreɪlər / (AmE), / ˈtændəm ˈtreɪlə / (BrE)
A trucking rig consisting of two trailers attached in a line, one behind the other, and pulled by a single tractor unit.

Examples

A tandem trailer rig
Driving a tandem trailer requires special attention during turns and lane changes.
The company uses a tandem trailer to transport large quantities of goods across state lines.
They upgraded their fleet by adding several new tandem trailers for long-haul deliveries.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: road train - a truck with multiple trailers pulled in series
  • degree lower: tractor-trailer - a truck with a single trailer attached
