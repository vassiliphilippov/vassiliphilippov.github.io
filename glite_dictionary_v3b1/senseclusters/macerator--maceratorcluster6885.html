macerator

noun
device for breaking up tissue
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪ.sə.reɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪ.sə.reɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a machine or device that breaks up, grinds, or crushes material (such as waste, food, or biological tissue) into smaller pieces for disposal or processing
