field-strip

verb
to disassemble for cleaning
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌstrɪp / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌstrɪp / (BrE)

Examples

To field-strip a rifle

The manual explains how to field-strip the device for routine maintenance.
