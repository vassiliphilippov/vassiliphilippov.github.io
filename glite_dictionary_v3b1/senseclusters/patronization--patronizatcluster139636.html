patronization

noun
financial support
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpætrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of giving financial support or official help to an individual, organization, or activity

Examples

Patronization of the arts
The local theater survived thanks to the patronization of several generous donors.
Without continued patronization, the community center would have to close its doors.
Artists often depend on the patronization of art lovers to pursue their work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neglect - the act of not giving necessary care or attention
  • synonym: sponsorship - the act of supporting an event, activity, or organization financially
  • synonym: support - help or assistance, especially financial
