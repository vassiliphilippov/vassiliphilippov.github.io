remanufacturer

noun
restorer of used products
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.mænjəˈfæk.tʃərər / (AmE), / ˌriː.mænjʊˈfæk.tʃər.ər / (BrE)
a person or company that restores used products to a like-new or original condition for resale or reuse

Examples

The remanufacturer restores products
The remanufacturer carefully restored the old printer so it could be sold again.
Many car parts are rebuilt by a remanufacturer to meet original quality standards.
A reputable remanufacturer will test all products before returning them to the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes products from raw materials
  • synonym: refurbisher - a person or company that renovates or repairs used items
