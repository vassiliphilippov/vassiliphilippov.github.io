adhesion

noun
faithful support or attachment
Pronunciation: / ædˈhiʒən / (AmE), / ədˈhiːʒən / (BrE)
Faithful support or attachment to a cause, party, or belief.

Examples

Adhesion to a cause
During the conflict, the adhesion of several states shifted the balance of power.
His adhesion to the new political movement was unwavering, even in difficult times.
The senator's adhesion to strict environmental policies earned her much respect.
