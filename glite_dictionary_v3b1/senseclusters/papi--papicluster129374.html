papi

noun
informal for father
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈpæpi / (BrE)
an affectionate or informal term for one's father, grandfather, or a male loved one (especially in Spanish-speaking families)

Examples

My papi hugs me
When I was little, I always ran to my papi when I needed help.
She smiled and said, "papi, can you read me a story tonight?"
At the family party, everyone greeted their papi with hugs and laughter.
Every summer she visited her papi who told stories about the old village by the sea.
My papi always brings me sweets when he comes to visit.
At the family reunion, all the grandchildren gathered around their papi for a group photo.
