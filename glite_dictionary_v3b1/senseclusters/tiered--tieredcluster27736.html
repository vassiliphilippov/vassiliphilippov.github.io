tiered

adjective
arranged in tiers or layers
Pronunciation: / tɪrd / (AmE), / tɪəd / (BrE)
having or arranged in tiers or layers, especially in levels one above the other

Examples

Tiered seating
The stadium has tiered seating to give everyone a better view.
Their plan introduced a tiered pricing system based on usage.
We admired the tiered wedding cake decorated with flowers.
