roast lamb

noun
lamb prepared by roasting
4.6
Pronunciation: / roʊst læm / (AmE), / rəʊst læm / (BrE)
lamb that is cooked by roasting, or a cut of lamb suitable for roasting

Examples

A roast lamb dinner
For Easter dinner, she prepared a traditional roast lamb with rosemary and garlic.
The restaurant’s specialty is roast lamb served with mint sauce.
We enjoyed a delicious meal of roast lamb and vegetables last night.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lamb chop - a small cut of lamb, usually from the rib or loin
  • synonym: roast beef - beef cooked by roasting
