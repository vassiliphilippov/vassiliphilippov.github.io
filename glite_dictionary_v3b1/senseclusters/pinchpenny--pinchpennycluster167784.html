pinchpenny

noun
miserly person
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪntʃˌpɛni / (AmE), / ˈpɪntʃˌpɛni / (BrE)
a person who is extremely unwilling to spend money; a miser

Examples

An old pinchpenny
Despite his wealth, the old pinchpenny refused to donate to any charity.
No one expected the pinchpenny to pay for dinner at the restaurant.
Her friends joked that she was a pinchpenny because she always looked for the cheapest option.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miser - a person who hoards money and spends as little as possible
  • antonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money carelessly or wastefully
