cybernated

adjective
enhanced with cybernetics
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪbərˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbənˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
augmented or enhanced by cybernetic implants, electronic prostheses, or integrated machine components

Examples

Cybernated body parts
After the accident, he became cybernated with advanced robotic limbs.
The science fiction novel describes a future where many people are cybernated to improve their abilities.
Some athletes considered becoming cybernated to gain a competitive edge, but the rules forbade it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bionic - having artificial body parts, especially electromechanical ones
  • synonym: cybernetic - relating to the science of communication and control in machines and living things
  • antonym: organic - relating to or derived from living matter, not artificial or mechanical
