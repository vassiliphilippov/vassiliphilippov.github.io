recommender

noun
person or system suggesting
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkəˈmɛndər / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəˈmɛndə / (BrE)
a person or system that suggests items, options, or actions you might like or find useful

Examples

Music recommender system
The movie recommender on the website helped me find films I had never heard of before.
As a trusted recommender, she always knows which books I will enjoy.
Many online stores use a recommender to suggest products based on your previous purchases.
