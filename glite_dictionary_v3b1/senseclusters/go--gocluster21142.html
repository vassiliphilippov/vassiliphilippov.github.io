go

verb
to be in the correct place or situation
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to be suitable for, fit, match, or belong with something else

Examples

This key won't go in the lock; it's the wrong size.

The modern table doesn't really go with the antique chairs.
These shoes go perfectly with your outfit.
