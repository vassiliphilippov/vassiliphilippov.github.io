ohia

noun
Hawaiian flowering tree
Pronunciation: / oʊˈhiːə / (AmE), / əʊˈhiːə / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to Hawaii, especially known for its bright red flowers and ecological importance

Examples

Hawaiian ohia tree

Many native birds depend on the nectar of the ohia flower for food.
The bright red blossoms of the ohia add color to the Hawaiian landscape.
