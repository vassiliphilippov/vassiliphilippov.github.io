loose-jowled

adjective
having sagging folds beneath the chin
Pronunciation: / lusdʒaʊld / (AmE), / luːsdʒaʊld / (BrE)
Having sagging or drooping folds of flesh beneath the chin or lower jaw.

Examples

A loose-jowled dog
Her loose-jowled bulldog snored softly on the porch.
The old man was loose-jowled and heavyset, with a deep, rumbling voice.
Years of indulgence had made his face loose-jowled and his features softer.
