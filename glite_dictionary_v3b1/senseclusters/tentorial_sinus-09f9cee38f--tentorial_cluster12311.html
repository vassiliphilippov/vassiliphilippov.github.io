tentorial sinus

noun
venous channel in the brain's dura mater
9.9
Pronunciation: /ten.ˈtɑːr.ɪ.əl ˈsaɪ.nəs/ /; / /ten.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.əl ˈsaɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ten.ˈtɔː.rɪ.əl ˈsaɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
an unpaired venous channel in the dura mater of the brain, located near the tentorium cerebelli

Examples
