train

noun
long back section of a dress or gown
9.0
Pronunciation: / treɪn / (AmE), / treɪn / (BrE)
the long back section of a dress or gown that trails along the floor behind the wearer

Examples

A wedding dress train
As she walked down the aisle, her train flowed gracefully behind her.
The bride's train was decorated with intricate lace patterns.
The designer added a dramatic train to the evening gown for a regal effect.
