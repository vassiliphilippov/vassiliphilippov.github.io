earnest

noun
serious intention
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrnɪst / (AmE), / ˈɜːnɪst / (BrE)
a serious and sincere state of mind or intention

Examples

Earnest intention
She spoke with great earnest about her plans for the future.
His earnest was evident in the way he prepared for the exam.
The team's earnest could be seen in their dedication to the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frivolity - lack of seriousness or sense
  • synonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious or earnest
  • synonym: sincerity - the quality of being honest and genuine
