all right

adverb
expression of agreement or acknowledgment
3.1
Pronunciation: / ɑl ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈraɪt / (BrE)
used to express agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment, often at the beginning of a sentence

Examples

All right, let's go
All right, I see what you mean now.
All right, I’ll come with you.
All right, let’s get started with the meeting.
