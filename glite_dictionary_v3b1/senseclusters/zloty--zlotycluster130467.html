zloty

noun
Polish currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈzloʊti / (AmE), / ˈzlɒti / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Poland, equal to 100 groszy.

Examples

The Polish zloty
In 2021, the value of the zloty fell slightly compared to the euro.
She exchanged all her euros for zloty before arriving in Warsaw.
The price of the train ticket was fifty zloty.
