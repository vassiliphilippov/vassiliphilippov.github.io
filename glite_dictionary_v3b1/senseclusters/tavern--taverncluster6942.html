tavern

noun
virtual social gathering place
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtævərn / (AmE), / ˈtævən / (BrE)
A virtual gathering place in an online game, forum, or community, modelled on a traditional tavern and used for socializing, chatting, trading, or organising activities.

Examples

Online tavern chat
After the update, the guild members met in the tavern to share tips and plan quests.
New players often hang out in virtual taverns to ask questions and make new friends.
The community created a text tavern channel for casual chat and weekend event sign-ups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatroom - an online space for people to communicate by text, voice, or video
  • synonym: lobby - a shared online area where players meet before starting a game or activity
  • synonym: pub - a social place where people meet and drink; the virtual tavern borrows this social function
