sorbent

noun
absorbent or adsorbent material
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːrbənt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːbənt / (BrE)
A material that has the capacity to take up another substance by absorption or adsorption.

Examples

Common sorbent material
Activated carbon is a common sorbent used in air and water purification.
Oil spills are often treated with sorbent materials to contain and remove the oil.
The laboratory tested a new sorbent to clean up the chemical spill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorbent - a substance that soaks up liquids
  • synonym: adsorbent - a material that attracts and holds substances on its surface
