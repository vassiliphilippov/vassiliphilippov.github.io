mid-September

noun
the middle part of September
4.1
Pronunciation: / mɪd səpˈtɛmbɚ / (AmE), / mɪd səpˈtɛmbə / (BrE)
the period in the middle of September, usually around the 13th to the 17th of the month.

Examples

Leaves change in mid-September
Leaves start changing color around mid-September in this region.
The deadline for applications falls in mid-September each year.
We expect the construction to be finished by mid-September at the latest.
