parametric

adjective
relating to parameters (esp. in math, science)
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or expressed in terms of one or more parameters; involving parameters in mathematical or scientific contexts.

Examples

Parametric equations
Physicists often use parametric equations to describe curves in space.
The researchers compared parametric and nonparametric models in their analysis.
This statistical method assumes a parametric distribution of the data.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonparametric - not relying on parameters or assumptions about their distribution (especially in statistics)
