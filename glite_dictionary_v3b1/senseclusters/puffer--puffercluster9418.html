puffer

noun
poisonous delicacy made from pufferfish
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌfər / (AmE), / ˈpʌfə / (BrE)
a delicacy made from a pufferfish, which is highly dangerous due to a potent nerve poison found in its ovaries and liver

Examples

Japanese puffer dish
In Japan, puffer is considered a delicacy despite the risks involved in preparing it.
Only specially trained chefs are allowed to serve puffer in most restaurants.
The chef explained the process of safely removing the poison from the puffer before cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pufferfish - a fish from the family Tetraodontidae, known for its ability to inflate and its potent poison
