votive

adjective
dedicated as a vow or offering
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.tɪv / (BrE)
offered, dedicated, or performed in fulfillment of a vow or pledge, especially as a religious offering

Examples

Votive offering
She presented a votive offering at the shrine as thanks for her recovery.
The ancient temple displayed numerous votive statues left by worshippers.
The church was filled with votive candles, each lit for a prayer or wish.
