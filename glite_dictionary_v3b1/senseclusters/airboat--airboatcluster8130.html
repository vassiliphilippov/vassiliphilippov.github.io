airboat

noun
flat-bottomed boat
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a flat-bottomed boat with an aircraft-style propeller, used for travel in shallow or marshy waters

Examples

A fast airboat ride
We took an exciting ride on an airboat to explore the swamp's wildlife.
The guide expertly maneuvered the airboat through the shallow marshes.
An airboat can travel quickly over water where regular boats would get stuck.
The guide steered the airboat smoothly across the shallow marsh.
We spotted several alligators while riding in an airboat through the Everglades.
Because of its design, an airboat can travel where regular boats cannot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: canoe - a narrow, lightweight boat pointed at both ends and open on top
  • synonym: hovercraft - a vehicle that travels over land or water on a cushion of air
