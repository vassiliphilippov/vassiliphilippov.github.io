Technicolor

adjective
very brightly coloured; vivid
6.2 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8t\u025bkn\u026a\u02cck\u028cl\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8t\u025bkn\u026a\u02cck\u028cl\u0259 / (BrE)
Having very bright, intensely saturated colours, especially in a way that recalls the vivid look of films made with the Technicolor process.

Examples
