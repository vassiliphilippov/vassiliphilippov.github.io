Technicolor

adjective
very brightly coloured; vivid
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtek.nɪ.kʌ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈtek.nɪ.kʌ.lə/ (BrE)
Having very bright, intensely saturated colours, especially in a way that recalls the vivid look of films made with the Technicolor process.

Examples

Technicolor style
The film's opening montage used a Technicolor scheme that made every scene feel dreamy and bold.
She bought a dress in a Technicolor print that turned heads at the summer festival.
The designer recommended a Technicolor palette for the brand to create a retro yet eye-catching identity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: muted - having colours that are soft, pale, or toned down; opposite of intensely coloured
  • synonym: saturated - having colours that are deep and intense rather than pale or muted
  • synonym: vivid - producing strong, clear impressions on the senses; intensely bright or distinct
