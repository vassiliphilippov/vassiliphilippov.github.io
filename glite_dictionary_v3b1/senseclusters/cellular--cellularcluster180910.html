cellular

adjective
relating to mobile-phone networks
3.5 5.54/m
Pronunciation: / c8s5blj59l59 / (AmE), / c8s5blj8al59 / (BrE)
Relating to mobile-phone networks or services that use a system of radio 'cells' for wireless communication; used of phones, plans, coverage, and signals.

Examples

Cellular network
The new cellular plan offers nationwide data with no roaming fees.
Her cellular signal dropped to one bar while driving through the mountains.
They bought a cellular modem to provide internet access over the mobile network.

Synonyms

  • mobile
    3.4 26/m
    mobile refers to devices or apps used on the go, while cellular refers to phone networks, coverage, and radio-based signals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mobile - relating to mobile phones or mobile-phone networks; used as an adjective in British English.
  • synonym: mobile phone - a phone that connects to a cellular network for wireless communication.
