variegated horsetail

noun
a species of horsetail plant
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛriəˌɡeɪtɪd ˈhɔrˌsteɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɑːrɪəˌɡeɪtɪd ˈhɔːsteɪl / (BrE)
a species of horsetail (Equisetum variegatum) found in northern North America, Greenland, and northern and central Europe, noted for its slender, jointed stems with variegated coloring
