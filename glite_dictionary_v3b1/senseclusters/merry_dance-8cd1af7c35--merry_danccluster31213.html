merry dance

noun
lively, joyful festive dance
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛri dæns / (AmE), / ˈmɛri dɑːns / (BrE)
a lively and joyful dance performed as part of a celebration or festivity

Examples

A merry dance celebration
At the wedding reception, everyone joined the merry dance with laughter and clapping.
During the parade, the performers broke into a merry dance that delighted the crowd.
Villagers gathered in the square for a merry dance to celebrate the harvest.
