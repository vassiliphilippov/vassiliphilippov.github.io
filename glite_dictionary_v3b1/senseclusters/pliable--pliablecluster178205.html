pliable

adjective
easily bent or flexible
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈplaɪ.ə.bəl /; / ˈplaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈplaɪ.ə.bəl /; / ˈplaɪəbəl / (BrE)
easily bent, shaped, or able to adapt to different situations; flexible or adaptable

Examples

Use a pliable wire for this project so you can twist it easily into different forms.
