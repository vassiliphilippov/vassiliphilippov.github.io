Chagas disease

noun
parasitic tropical illness
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑɡəs dɪˌziz /; / ˈtʃɑːɡəs dɪˌziːz / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːɡəs dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
a tropical disease found mainly in Latin America, caused by the parasite Trypanosoma cruzi and spread by insects, which can cause serious heart or digestive problems years after infection
