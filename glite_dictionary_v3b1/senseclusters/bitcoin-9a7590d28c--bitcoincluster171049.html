Bitcoin

noun
digital currency
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtkɔɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɪtkɔɪn / (BrE)
a digital currency that operates without a central authority, used for online transactions and investment

Examples

Buy Bitcoin online
He invested in Bitcoin when the price was much lower than it is today.
Many online stores now accept Bitcoin as a form of payment.
She received 0.05 Bitcoin as payment for her freelance design work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cash - physical money in the form of coins or notes
  • synonym: cryptocurrency - a digital currency secured by cryptography
