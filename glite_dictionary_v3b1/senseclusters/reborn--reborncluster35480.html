reborn

adjective
spiritually renewed or converted
5.2 1.37/m
Pronunciation: / riÀàb…îrn / (AmE), / riÀàb…îÀên / (BrE)
Having undergone a spiritual renewal or conversion, especially in a religious sense.

Examples

Reborn Christian
After attending the retreat, she felt entirely reborn and committed to her faith.
He described himself as a reborn Christian after his transformative experience.
Many people seek a sense of purpose through becoming reborn in their spiritual lives.
