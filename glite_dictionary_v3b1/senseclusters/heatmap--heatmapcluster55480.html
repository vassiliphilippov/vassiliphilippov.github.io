heatmap

noun
colored data chart
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtˌmæp / (AmE), / ˈhiːtˌmæp / (BrE)
a graphical representation of data in which values are shown as colors to indicate intensity or frequency

Examples

A heatmap chart
The heatmap showed which areas of the city had the highest temperatures last summer.
By analyzing the heatmap, the team identified the most popular sections of their website.
A heatmap can make complex data easier to understand at a glance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chart - a visual representation of data
  • synonym: graph - a diagram showing the relation between variable quantities
  • antonym: table - an arrangement of data in rows and columns
