ventrally

adverb
toward the belly/front side
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntrəli / (AmE), / ˈvɛntrəli / (BrE)
In or towards the ventral (front or belly) side of an organism.

Examples

Access ventrally during surgery
During surgery, the organ was accessed ventrally to avoid major blood vessels.
Some species of snake have scales that are enlarged ventrally to aid locomotion.
The fish is colored more brightly ventrally than dorsally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dorsally - in the direction of the back side (dorsal)
