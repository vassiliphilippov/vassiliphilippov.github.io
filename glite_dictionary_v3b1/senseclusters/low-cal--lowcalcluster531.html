low-cal

adjective
low in calories
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊˈkæl / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈkæl / (BrE)

Examples

Low-cal snacks
Many people choose low-cal snacks when trying to lose weight.
She switched to a low-cal salad dressing to keep her meals lighter.
This restaurant offers several low-cal options on their menu.
