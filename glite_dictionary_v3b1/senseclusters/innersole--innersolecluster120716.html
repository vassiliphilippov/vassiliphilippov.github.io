innersole

noun
the inner sole of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnərˌsoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɪnəsəʊl / (BrE)
the inner part of a shoe or boot, forming a layer on which the foot rests, often removable or cushioned for comfort

Examples

The innersole of a shoe
I took the innersole out to wash it after a long hike.
Replacing the innersole made my old shoes much more comfortable.
The doctor recommended a special innersole to support my arches.
