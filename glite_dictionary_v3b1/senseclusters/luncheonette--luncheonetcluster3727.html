luncheonette

noun
small informal restaurant
Pronunciation: / ˌlʌntʃəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌlʌntʃəˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small, informal restaurant that serves light meals and snacks, often at a counter

Examples

A small luncheonette

The luncheonette on the corner is famous for its homemade pies.
After shopping, they relaxed at a cozy luncheonette with coffee and sandwiches.
We stopped at a luncheonette for a quick sandwich before heading back to work.
The old luncheonette on Main Street is famous for its homemade pies.
Many people enjoyed breakfast at the local luncheonette every morning.
