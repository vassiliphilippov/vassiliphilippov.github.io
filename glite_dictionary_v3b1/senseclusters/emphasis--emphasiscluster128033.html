Examples

Synonyms

  • accentuation
    6.1 0.06/m
    accentuation is the act of giving strong, deliberate importance to something, while emphasis is the general attention that makes it stand out.
  • stress
    3.2 2.14/m
    stress signals strong importance for a specific point, while emphasis names the overall attention given to make that point noticed.
  • focus
    3.5 88/m
    focus means a tight concentration on one subject or task, while emphasis means giving something special importance to notice it.
  • accent
    3.7 2.60/m
    accent is a feature that makes one part stand out by contrast, while emphasis is the attention given to show importance.
