shade

noun
artistic representation of shadows
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ʃeɪd / (BrE)
an area in an artistic picture or drawing that shows the effect of shadows, typically created using darker pigment or shading techniques

Examples

A shade in art
Proper use of shade makes the objects in a drawing appear three-dimensional.
The artist carefully added shade to the portrait to create depth and realism.
Using different pencils, he practiced drawing shade under the tree's branches.

Related Terms

  • antonym: highlight - an area in a picture or design that is strongly illuminated
  • synonym: shadow - a dark area produced by a body coming between light and a surface
