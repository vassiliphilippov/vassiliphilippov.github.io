most fun

adjective
maximum enjoyment (humorous/informal use)
1.8
Pronunciation: / moʊst fʌn / (AmE), / məʊst fʌn / (BrE)
A humorous or emphatic way to express the greatest degree of enjoyment or amusement, sometimes used in informal or playful contexts.

Examples

The most fun day
Spending the afternoon at the lake with friends is honestly the most fun I can imagine.
That rollercoaster ride was the most fun part of our trip to the amusement park.
The party last night was the most fun I've had in years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: least fun - the smallest amount of fun; the opposite of 'most fun'
  • synonym: mostest fun - an exaggerated informal superlative of 'fun'
