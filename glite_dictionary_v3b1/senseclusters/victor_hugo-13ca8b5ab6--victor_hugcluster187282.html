Victor Hugo

proper noun
French Romantic poet, novelist, and dramatist
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktər ˈhjuːgoʊ /; / ˈvɪktər ˈhjuːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə ˈhjuːgəʊ /; / ˈvɪktə ˈhjuːɡəʊ / (BrE)
(1802–1885) French poet, novelist, and dramatist; leader of the Romantic movement; author of 'Les Misérables' and 'The Hunchback of Notre-Dame'
