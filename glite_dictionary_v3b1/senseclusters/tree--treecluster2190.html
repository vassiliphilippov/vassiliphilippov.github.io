tree

noun
branching diagram or structure
6.0 21/m
Pronunciation: / triÀê / (AmE), / triÀê / (BrE)
A diagram or structure that branches from a single root, representing relationships between elements, such as a family tree or a structure in mathematics or computing.

Examples
