tree

noun
branching diagram or structure
Pronunciation: / triː / (AmE), / triː / (BrE)
A diagram or structure that branches from a single root, representing relationships between elements, such as a family tree or a structure in mathematics or computing.

Examples

A family tree
In computer science, a binary tree organizes data hierarchically.
The linguist drew a tree to illustrate the structure of the sentence.
We traced our ancestors on a complex genealogical tree.
