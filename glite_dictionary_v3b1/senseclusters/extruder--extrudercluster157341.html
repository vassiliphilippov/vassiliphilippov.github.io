extruder

noun
machine part for shaping
7.4
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈtruː.dər/ /; / /ek.ˈtruː.dər/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈtruː.də/ /; / /ek.ˈtruː.də/ (BrE)
the part of a machine, especially a 3D printer, that pushes material through a nozzle to form objects or shapes
