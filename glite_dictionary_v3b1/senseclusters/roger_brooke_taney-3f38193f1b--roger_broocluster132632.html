Roger Brooke Taney

proper noun
U.S. Chief Justice famous for Dred Scott decision
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑdʒər broʊk ˈtɑːni / (AmE), / ˈrɒdʒə brʊk ˈtɔːni / (BrE)
(1777–1864) United States jurist who served as Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, known for the Dred Scott decision declaring that slaves and their descendants could not be citizens.
