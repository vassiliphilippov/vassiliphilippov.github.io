workmanlike

adjective
competently and skillfully done
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
done with the skill, competence, and efficiency of a good worker, though not necessarily with creative flair or originality

Examples

A workmanlike performance
She delivered a workmanlike performance, meeting all the requirements without any mistakes.
The film is workmanlike, displaying solid craftsmanship but little originality.
The report was a workmanlike summary of the situation, clear but uninspired.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateurish - lacking professional skill; done in an unskilled way
  • synonym: competent - having the necessary ability or skill to do something successfully
