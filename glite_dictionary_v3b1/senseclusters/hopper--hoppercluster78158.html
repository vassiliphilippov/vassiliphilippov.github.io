hopper

noun
funnel-shaped container for dispensing materials
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈhɒpə / (BrE)
a funnel-shaped container that receives and dispenses materials, allowing them to pass by gravity into another receptacle below

Examples

Coffee hopper
Please fill the hopper with coffee beans before brewing.
The grain poured from the hopper into a waiting truck below.
The machine jams if the hopper becomes overloaded with material.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chute - a sloping channel or slide for conveying things to a lower level
