mononucleotide

noun
single nucleotide molecule
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑnoʊˈnuːkliəˌtaɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈnjuːkliətaɪd / (BrE)
a molecule made of a single nucleotide unit, the basic building block of DNA and RNA (usually with one phosphate group)
