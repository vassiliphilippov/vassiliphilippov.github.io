pneumatology

noun
study of the Holy Spirit
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of theology concerned with spiritual beings or phenomena, especially the Holy Spirit

Examples

Christian pneumatology
He devoted years to studying pneumatology and its role in Christian doctrine.
The professor's lecture on pneumatology explored different interpretations of the Holy Spirit.
Modern scholars often debate the importance of pneumatology in religious studies.
