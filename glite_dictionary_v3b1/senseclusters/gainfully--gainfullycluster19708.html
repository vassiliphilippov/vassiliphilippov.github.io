gainfully

adverb
in a way that provides income
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪnfəli / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪnfəli / (BrE)
In a way that earns money or provides financial profit.

Examples

To be gainfully employed
After a long period of unemployment, she was finally gainfully employed as a project manager.
He was asked if he was currently gainfully engaged in any profession.
Many people dream of being gainfully employed in a field they are passionate about.
