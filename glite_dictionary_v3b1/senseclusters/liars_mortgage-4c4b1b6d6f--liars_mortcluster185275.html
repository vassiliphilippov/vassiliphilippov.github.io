liar's mortgage

noun
fraudulent or deceptive mortgage arrangement
7.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.ərz ˈmɔːr.ɡɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈlaɪ.ərz ˈmɔːr.ɡədʒ/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.əz ˈmɔː.ɡɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈlaɪ.əz ˈmɔː.ɡədʒ/ (BrE)
