development

noun
gradual process of growth or advancement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the process of gradually growing, changing, or becoming more advanced, mature, or elaborate over time; improvement or advancement

Examples

Skill development
Her writing showed significant development after she took several creative workshops.
The development of renewable energy technologies has accelerated over the past decade.
We watched the slow development of the case as investigators gathered more evidence.
In chess, careful development of your pieces is important in the opening.
The new policy led to significant development in the region's economy.
The rapid development of the city transformed it into a bustling metropolis within a decade.
Her career saw rapid development after she obtained her degree.
The development of new technologies has greatly impacted the way we communicate.
The company's success is largely due to its investment in research and development.
