piperidine

noun
organic chemical compound
9.0
Pronunciation: / paɪˈpɛrɪˌdin / (AmE), / pɪˈpɛrɪdiːn / (BrE)
a colorless liquid chemical compound with a six-membered ring containing five carbon atoms and one nitrogen atom, used in chemistry and found in many medicines
