aphasiac

adjective
affected by aphasia
Pronunciation: / əˈfeɪziæk / (AmE), / əˈfeɪziæk / (BrE)
having or relating to aphasia; affected by a loss of the ability to use or understand language due to brain injury

Examples

Aphasiac symptoms
The patient was described as aphasiac after suffering a severe stroke.
Doctors developed special communication strategies for their aphasiac clients.
Her aphasiac condition made it difficult for her to express her thoughts clearly.
