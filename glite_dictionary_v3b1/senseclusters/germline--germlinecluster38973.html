germline

adjective
heritable genetic
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜːmlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmlaɪn / (BrE)
relating to genes, cells, or mutations that are present in reproductive cells and can be passed to offspring

Examples
