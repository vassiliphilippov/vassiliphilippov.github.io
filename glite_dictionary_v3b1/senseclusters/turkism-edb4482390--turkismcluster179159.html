Turkism

noun
Turkish cultural feature
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrkɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪzəm / (BrE)
a custom, word, or feature that is characteristic of Turkish people or culture

Examples

A local Turkism
The local language contains several Turkisms due to historical contact with Turkish traders.
Wearing a fez is considered a Turkism in some Balkan countries.
That greeting is a common Turkism found in regional dialects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Anglicism - a word or feature characteristic of English
  • synonym: Turkishism - a word or feature borrowed from Turkish
