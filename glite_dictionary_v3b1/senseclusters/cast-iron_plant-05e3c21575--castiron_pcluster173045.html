cast-iron plant

noun
tough evergreen houseplant (Aspidistra elatior)
Pronunciation: / ˌkæstˈaɪərn plænt / (AmE), / ˌkɑːstˈaɪən plɑːnt / (BrE)
an evergreen perennial plant with large tough basal leaves, widely grown as a foliage houseplant, especially for its resilience
