bloodwork

noun
blood tests
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌwɜːk / (BrE)
tests performed on a blood sample to assess health or help diagnose medical conditions

Examples

Bloodwork tests
The doctor ordered bloodwork to check her cholesterol levels.
Routine bloodwork can help detect health problems early.
He was nervous about the results of his bloodwork after feeling unwell for weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood test - a medical test using a sample of blood
  • antonym: urinalysis - a test of urine to check for disease or health conditions
