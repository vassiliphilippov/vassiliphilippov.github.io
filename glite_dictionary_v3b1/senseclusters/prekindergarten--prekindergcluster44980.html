prekindergarten

noun
early childhood class
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkɪndərˌɡɑːrtən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkɪndəˌɡɑːtən / (BrE)
a class or educational program for young children before they start kindergarten

Examples

Many parents enroll their children in prekindergarten to help them develop social skills before starting school.
The new community center offers a free prekindergarten program for local families.
Her son made his first friends in prekindergarten and still keeps in touch with them.
