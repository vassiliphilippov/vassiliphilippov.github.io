Synonyms

  • clothed
    1.0 0.03/m
    clothed is having clothes on in a general way, while dressed often suggests wearing specific clothes for a style or occasion.
  • attired
    5.4 0.07/m
    attired is used for formal or stylish clothing, while dressed is more common and can mean any clothes for an occasion.
  • garbed
    7.4 0.02/m
    garbed describes wearing a distinctive or formal style, while dressed is general and covers everyday or special clothing choices.
  • clad
    6.5 2.87/m
    clad often appears in set phrases or poetic use to show covering, while dressed is normal everyday or occasion clothing.
