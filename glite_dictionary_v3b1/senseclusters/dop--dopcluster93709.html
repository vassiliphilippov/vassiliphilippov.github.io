dop

noun
gemstone holder tool
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑp / (AmE), / dɒp / (BrE)
a tool or material used to hold a gemstone securely while it is being cut or polished

Examples

Gemstone dop
The jeweler attached the sapphire to a dop before starting the polishing process.
A dop is essential for keeping the gemstone steady during cutting.
She carefully removed the finished stone from the dop after hours of work.
