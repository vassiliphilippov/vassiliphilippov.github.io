committedness

noun
quality of being dedicated or steadfast
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being dedicated and steadfast in purpose or loyalty

Examples

Committedness to a project
Her committedness to the project inspired everyone on the team to work harder.
Successful leaders are often defined by their committedness to their values and goals.
The organization benefited from the committedness of its volunteers.
