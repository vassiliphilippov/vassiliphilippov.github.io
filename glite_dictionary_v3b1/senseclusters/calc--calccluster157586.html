calc

noun
informal calculation
Pronunciation: / kælk / (AmE), / kælk / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a calculation or the result of a calculation

Examples

A quick calc
I did a quick calc to see if we had enough money for lunch.
Can you check your calc on the final total before we submit it?
Her calc showed that the project would take three more weeks to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calculation - the process of using mathematics to find an answer
  • synonym: estimate - an approximate calculation or judgment
