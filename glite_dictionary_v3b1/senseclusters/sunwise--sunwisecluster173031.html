sunwise

adverb
toward the sun
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in the direction toward the sun or facing the sun.

Examples

Walk sunwise toward sunrise
The hikers walked sunwise along the ridge, heading directly toward the sunrise.
She arranged the chairs sunwise so everyone could enjoy the morning light.
The flowers in the garden always seem to turn sunwise as the day begins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-sunwise - in a direction away from the sun
  • synonym: sunward - toward the sun
