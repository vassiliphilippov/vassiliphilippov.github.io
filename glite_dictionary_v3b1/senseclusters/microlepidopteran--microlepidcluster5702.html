microlepidopteran

noun
tiny moth species
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌlɛpɪˈdɑptərən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərən / (BrE)
a very small moth belonging to a group of tiny lepidopteran insects

Examples

A microlepidopteran moth
The entomologist discovered a new species of microlepidopteran in the rainforest.
Many microlepidopterans are so small they can be mistaken for tiny flies.
Researchers use special nets to collect microlepidopterans for scientific study.

Related Terms

  • antonym: butterfly - a brightly colored lepidopteran insect, usually active by day
  • synonym: lepidopteran - an insect of the order including butterflies and moths
  • synonym: moth - an insect related to butterflies, usually active at night
