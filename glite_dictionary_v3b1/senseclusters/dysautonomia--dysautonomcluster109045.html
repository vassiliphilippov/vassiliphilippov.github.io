dysautonomia

noun
autonomic nervous disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsˌɔtəˈnoʊmiə / (AmE), / ˌdɪsˌɔːtəˈnəʊmiə / (BrE)
a disorder in which the autonomic nervous system does not function properly, causing various symptoms like dizziness and irregular heartbeat

Examples

dysautonomia disorder
After years of unexplained symptoms, she was finally diagnosed with dysautonomia by a specialist.
People with dysautonomia may experience dizziness and irregular heartbeats.
Managing dysautonomia often requires a combination of medication and lifestyle changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomic neuropathy - damage to the nerves of the autonomic nervous system
  • antonym: homeostasis - the body's ability to maintain stable internal conditions
