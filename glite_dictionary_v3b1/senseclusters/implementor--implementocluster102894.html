implementor

noun
person who carries out plans
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpləˌmɛntər / (AmE), / ˈɪmplɪˌmɛntə / (BrE)
a person or thing that puts a plan, system, or decision into effect

Examples

Implementor of a plan
The implementor of the new policy worked closely with all departments to ensure success.
As the main implementor, she was responsible for turning the proposal into reality.
A good implementor pays attention to details and follows the plan carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executor - a person who carries out a task or plan
  • antonym: planner - a person who makes plans
