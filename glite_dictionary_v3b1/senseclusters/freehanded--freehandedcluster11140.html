freehanded

adjective
generous; giving freely
Pronunciation: / ˌfriːˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌfriːˈhændɪd / (BrE)
generous or giving freely; not sparing with one's resources or gifts

Examples

Freehanded giver
He was so freehanded with his praise that everyone felt encouraged.
Her freehanded nature made her popular among her friends.
The company became known for its freehanded donations to various charities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generous - willing to give more of something, especially money, than is strictly necessary or expected
  • antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
