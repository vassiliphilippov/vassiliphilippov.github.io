unreturnable

adjective
cannot be returned
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈtɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈtɜːnəbl / (BrE)
not able to be returned to the place, person, or store it came from

Examples

Unreturnable items
The clearance items are unreturnable, so please choose carefully before buying.
Because the dress was custom-made, it was considered unreturnable by the store.
He realized too late that the discounted laptop was unreturnable after opening the box.

Related Terms

  • antonym: returnable - able to be returned to the place or person it came from
