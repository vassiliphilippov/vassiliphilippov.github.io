tablespoonful

noun
an amount a tablespoon holds
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəlˌspuːnfʊl / (AmE), / ˈteɪblˌspuːnfʊl / (BrE)
An amount that fills a tablespoon, especially as a measure in cooking or medicine.

Examples

One tablespoonful sugar
Add one tablespoonful of sugar to the mixture and stir well.
She measured a tablespoonful of olive oil for the salad dressing.
The doctor advised him to take a tablespoonful of the medicine after meals.

  • degree lower: teaspoonful - an amount that fills a teaspoon
