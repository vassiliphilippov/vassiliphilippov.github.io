behavior

noun
manner of response of objects or substances
2.8
Pronunciation: / bɪˈheɪvjɚ /; / bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪviə /; / bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
the manner in which an object, substance, or system acts or responds under particular conditions, especially as observed in scientific or technical studies
