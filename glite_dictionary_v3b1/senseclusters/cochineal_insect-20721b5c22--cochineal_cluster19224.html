cochineal insect

noun
insect producing red dye cochineal
7.8 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.tʃɪ.niːl ˈɪn.sekt/ /; / /ˈkɑː.tʃə.niːl ˈɪn.sekt/ (AmE), /ˈkɒt.ʃɪ.niːl ˈɪn.sekt/ /; / /ˈkɒt.ʃə.niːl ˈɪn.sekt/ (BrE)
A Mexican red scale insect that feeds on cacti and is the source of the red dye known as cochineal.

Examples

The cochineal insect dye
Farmers in Mexico sometimes harvest the cochineal insect from prickly pear cacti to produce natural dyes.
For centuries, artists used pigment made from the cochineal insect to create vivid reds in their paintings.
The bright red color in the fabric was achieved using dye from the cochineal insect.
