comparative

adjective
relating to comparison
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpær.ə.tɪv /; / kəmˈpæɹ.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / kəmˈpær.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to or based on comparison between two or more things; considered in relation to something else rather than absolutely
