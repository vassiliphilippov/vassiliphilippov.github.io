Synonyms

  • outside
    1.0 20/m
    outside is used for things coming from or relating to the area beyond a group or place, while external is more formal and general.
  • outer
    1.0 19/m
    outer describes the outer side or part far from a center, while external means coming from the outside in general.
  • extrinsic
    7.7 0.2/m
    extrinsic is a formal term for something caused or added from outside, while external is everyday and can be physical or general.
