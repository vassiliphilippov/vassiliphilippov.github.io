external

adjective
located or originating outside
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɜrnəl /; / ɪkˈstɝːnəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəl / (BrE)
located, happening, or coming from outside a particular place, system, or body; outer

Examples

External wall
All external doors should be kept locked after hours for security.
Doctors examined him for external injuries after the accident.
The company faced significant external competition from overseas brands.
All external cables must be protected from weather damage.
Only external noise could be heard from the quiet classroom.
The company faced significant external pressure to change its policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extraneous - irrelevant or unrelated to the subject being dealt with; coming from outside
  • antonym: internal - located or existing inside something; opposite of 'external'
