orbitotomy

noun
eye socket surgery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrbɪˈtɑːtəmi / (AmE), / ˌɔːbɪˈtɒtəmi / (BrE)

Examples

A skilled surgeon is required to perform an orbitotomy safely and effectively.
