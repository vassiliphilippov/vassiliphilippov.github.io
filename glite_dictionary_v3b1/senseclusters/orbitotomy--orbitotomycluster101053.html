orbitotomy

noun
eye socket surgery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrbɪˈtɑːtəmi / (AmE), / ˌɔːbɪˈtɒtəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation in which the orbit of the eye is opened to access its contents

Examples

An orbitotomy operation
The doctor recommended an orbitotomy to remove the mass pressing on the optic nerve.
After the orbitotomy, the patient was closely monitored for any signs of infection.
A skilled surgeon is required to perform an orbitotomy safely and effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craniotomy - surgical opening of the skull
  • synonym: orbitectomy - surgical removal of the eye socket or its contents
