squinch

verb
cross one's eyes
Pronunciation: / skwɪntʃ / (AmE), / skwɪntʃ / (BrE)
to cross one's eyes as if in strabismus

Examples

To squinch your eyes
He made a silly face and began to squinch his eyes at his sister.
If you squinch your eyes like that, they might stay crossed!
She tried to squinch her eyes to amuse the baby.
