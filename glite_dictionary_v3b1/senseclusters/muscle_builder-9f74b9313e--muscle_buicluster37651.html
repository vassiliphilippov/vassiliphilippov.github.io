muscle builder

noun
person building large muscles through exercise
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsəl ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəl ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person who engages in special exercises to develop large and strong muscles

Examples

A muscle builder trains
After seeing his transformation, she was inspired to become a muscle builder herself.
He spent years training as a muscle builder to compete in fitness competitions.
Many muscle builders follow strict diets to maximize their results.
