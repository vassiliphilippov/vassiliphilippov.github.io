muralist

noun
artist who paints murals
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmjʊrəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmjʊːrəlɪst / (BrE)
an artist who paints murals, typically large works of art on walls or ceilings

Examples

A muralist paints walls
Many cities invite a muralist to transform plain walls into colorful stories.
She became a muralist after studying fine arts at university.
The Mexican muralist Diego Rivera is known for his vibrant public artworks.
