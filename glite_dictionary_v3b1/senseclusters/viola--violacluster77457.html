viola

noun
bowed string instrument, deeper than violin

Pronunciation: /vaɪ.ˈoʊ.lə/ /; / /viː.ˈoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /vaɪ.ˈəʊ.lə/ /; / /viː.ˈəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
