baritone horn

noun
a brass wind musical instrument
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrəˌtoʊn hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbarɪtəʊn hɔːn / (BrE)
a brass wind instrument with a range between the trombone and the tuba, known for its mellow tone

Examples

Play the baritone horn
He has played the baritone horn in the city orchestra for over a decade.
She prefers the warm sound of the baritone horn over the brighter trumpet.
The school band purchased a new baritone horn for the music program.

Related Terms

  • synonym: euphonium - a similar brass instrument, often used interchangeably with baritone horn
  • degree lower: trombone - a brass wind instrument with a similar but slightly lower range
  • degree lower: tuba - the largest and lowest-pitched brass wind instrument
