ranch

noun
large livestock farm
Pronunciation: / ræntʃ / (AmE), / rɑːntʃ / (BrE)
a large farm, especially in North America or Australia, where cattle or other animals are bred and raised

Examples

A cattle ranch
He left the city to manage his family's ranch in Montana.
The children enjoyed riding horses during their stay at the ranch.
They own a huge ranch in Texas where they raise hundreds of cattle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farm - an area of land used for growing crops or raising animals
