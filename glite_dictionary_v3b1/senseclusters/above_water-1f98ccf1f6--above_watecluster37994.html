above water

adjective
financially solvent or stable
4.1
Pronunciation: / əˈbʌv ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / əˈbʌv ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
having enough money to avoid financial failure or debt; financially stable or solvent.

Examples

Stay above water
Despite difficult times, the company managed to stay above water by cutting unnecessary expenses.
She took extra jobs to keep her family's finances above water.
Small businesses often struggle to remain above water during economic downturns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in the black - not in debt; financially solvent
  • antonym: in the red - in debt; having negative financial balance
