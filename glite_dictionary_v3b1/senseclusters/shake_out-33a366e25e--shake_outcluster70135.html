shake out

verb
remove dust by shaking
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk aʊt / (AmE), / ʃeɪk aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

To shake out crumbs

He shook out his jacket to remove the dirt after hiking.
She took the rug outside to shake out all the dust.
