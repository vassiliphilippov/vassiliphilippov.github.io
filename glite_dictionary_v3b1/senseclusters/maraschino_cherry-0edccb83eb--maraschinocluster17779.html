maraschino cherry

noun
cherry preserved in maraschino liqueur or syrup
Pronunciation: / ˌmærəˈskiːnoʊ ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈskiːnəʊ ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a cherry preserved in maraschino liqueur or a similar flavored syrup, often used as a garnish in cocktails and desserts

Examples

Cocktail maraschino cherry
After dinner, he enjoyed an old-fashioned garnished with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.
She loves her cocktails decorated with a bright red maraschino cherry.
The bartender placed a maraschino cherry on top of the sundae as a finishing touch.
