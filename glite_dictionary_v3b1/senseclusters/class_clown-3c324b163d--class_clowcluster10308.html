class clown

noun
student who entertains classmates in class
4.0
Pronunciation: / klæs klaʊn / (AmE), / klɑːs klaʊn / (BrE)
a student who frequently amuses or entertains classmates by behaving in a silly or disruptive manner during lessons

Examples

The class clown jokes
Although she was the class clown, her grades never suffered.
The class clown made everyone laugh, but sometimes distracted the teacher from the lesson.
Tom was known as the class clown who always had a joke ready for every situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joker - a person who jokes or makes others laugh, especially habitually
  • synonym: prankster - someone who plays practical jokes or tricks
