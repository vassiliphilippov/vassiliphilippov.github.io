currant

noun
small tart berry used in jams
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a small, tart, red or black berry used especially for making jams and jellies

Examples

Red currant jam
Dried currants are sometimes added to scones for extra flavor.
He made homemade jam from black currant and sugar.
The currant bushes in her garden were heavy with ripe fruit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gooseberry - a similar small, tart berry from the same botanical family
