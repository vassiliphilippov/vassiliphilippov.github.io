half-life

noun
time for amount to decrease by half
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌlaɪf /; / ˈhæf ˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌlaɪf /; / ˈhɑːf ˌlaɪf / (BrE)
the time it takes for something (especially a radioactive substance) to decrease to half its original amount
