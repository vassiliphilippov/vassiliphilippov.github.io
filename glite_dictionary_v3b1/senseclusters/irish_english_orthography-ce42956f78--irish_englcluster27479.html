Irish English orthography

noun
spelling system of English in Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːrˈθɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the system of English spelling conventions used in Ireland, often similar to British English but with some regional differences

Examples

Irish English orthography rules
Many schools in Ireland teach Irish English orthography rather than American spelling rules.
The word 'centre' as spelled in Irish English orthography differs from the American 'center'.
Writers who use Irish English orthography may prefer 'analyse' instead of 'analyze'.
