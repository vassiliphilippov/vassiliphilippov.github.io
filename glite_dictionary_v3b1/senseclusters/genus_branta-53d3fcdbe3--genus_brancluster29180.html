genus Branta

noun
a genus of wild geese
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈbræntə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈbrantə / (BrE)
A genus of wild geese that includes several species native to the Northern Hemisphere, such as the Canada goose and the barnacle goose.
