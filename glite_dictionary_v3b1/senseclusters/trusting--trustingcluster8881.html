trusting

adjective
inclined to believe others easily
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstɪŋ / (BrE)
inclined to believe or confide in others readily; showing or feeling trust in others

Examples

A trusting person
Children are often trusting of new adults until taught otherwise.
His trusting expression made it difficult to lie to him.
She has a very trusting nature, always seeing the best in people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gullible - easily deceived or tricked; too trusting
  • antonym: suspicious - inclined to doubt or mistrust others
