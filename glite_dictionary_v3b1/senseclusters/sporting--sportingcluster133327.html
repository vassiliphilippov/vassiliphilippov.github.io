sporting

adjective
devoted to gambling and pleasurable pursuits
3.7 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
Interested in or devoted to the pursuit of pleasure, especially gambling or games of chance.

Examples

Sporting life
He led a sporting life, frequenting horse races and betting parlors.
In Victorian London, some sporting gentlemen became well known for their extravagant bets and lavish entertainment.
The novel describes the lavish parties thrown by sporting men in the city's gambling halls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissipated - living a life of excessive pleasure, especially regarding gambling or drinking
  • synonym: gambling - the activity of betting money on games of chance
