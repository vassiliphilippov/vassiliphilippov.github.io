stuffer

noun
tool or person for filling
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
a person or tool used to fill or pack something tightly, especially with material or stuffing

Examples

Sausage stuffer
The factory worker operated the stuffer to fill sausages quickly and efficiently.
She bought a new stuffer to help make homemade pillows for her family.
A mechanical stuffer is often used in toy manufacturing to pack plush animals with cotton.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packer - a person or machine that packs items
