country borage

noun
aromatic herb native to South Asia and Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri ˈbɔrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˈbɒrɪdʒ / (BrE)
an aromatic, fleshy-leaved herb native to parts of India, Sri Lanka, and South Africa, sometimes classified under the genus Plectranthus
