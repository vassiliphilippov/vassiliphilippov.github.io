drum majorette

noun
female baton twirler with a marching band
2.4
Pronunciation: / drʌm ˌmeɪdʒəˈrɛt / (AmE), / drʌm ˌmɑːdʒəˈrɛt /; / drʌm ˌmædʒəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a female performer who leads or accompanies a marching band or parade, usually twirling a baton and sometimes performing dance routines
