lexicalization

noun
creation of a word for a concept
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛksələlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌlɛksɪlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɛksəlʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n /; / ˌlɛksɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming a word or phrase, or of expressing a concept or meaning by a single word in a language

Examples

The lexicalization process

Metaphors often undergo lexicalisation and become part of everyday speech.
The lexicalisation of new technology terms is rapidly changing our vocabulary.
