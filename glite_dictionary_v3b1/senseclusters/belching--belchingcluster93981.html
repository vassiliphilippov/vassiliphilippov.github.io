belching

noun
expelling gas from the stomach through the mouth
9.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbel.tʃɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbel.tʃɪŋ/ (BrE)
The act of expelling gas noisily from the stomach through the mouth, usually as a reflex action.

Examples

Belching after eating
Belching after a large meal is common and usually harmless.
Frequent belching may be a sign of a digestive disorder.
He apologized for belching loudly during the meeting.
