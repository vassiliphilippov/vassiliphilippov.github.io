prestamp

verb
to stamp in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈstæmp / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈstæmp / (BrE)
to apply a stamp or official mark to something before it is used or sent

Examples

To prestamp envelopes
The company prestamped all the envelopes to make mailing easier for customers.
Before the event, organizers prestamped the tickets to prevent counterfeiting.
You should prestamp the postcards so people can send them without extra effort.
