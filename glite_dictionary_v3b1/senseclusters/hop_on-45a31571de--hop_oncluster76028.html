hop on

verb
to get onto or mount (quickly/lightly)
3.1 2.3
Pronunciation: /hɑːp ɑːn/ /; / /hɑːp ən/ (AmE), /hɒp ɒn/ /; / /hɒp ən/ (BrE)

Examples
