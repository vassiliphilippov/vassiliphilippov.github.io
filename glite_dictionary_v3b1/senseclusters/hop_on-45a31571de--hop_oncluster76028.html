hop on

verb
to get onto or mount (quickly/lightly)
Pronunciation: / hɑp ɑn / (AmE), / hɒp ɒn / (BrE)
to get onto the back or top of something such as a horse, bicycle, bus, etc., often quickly or lightly

Examples

To hop on a bus
Before the trail ride started, she hopped on her horse effortlessly.
He likes to hop on his bike and ride through the neighborhood every morning.
The children laughed as they hopped on the bus for their field trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dismount - to get down from something one has mounted, such as a horse or bicycle
  • synonym: mount - to get up onto something, especially an animal or bicycle
