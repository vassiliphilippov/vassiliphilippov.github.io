babble

verb
make bubbling sounds as water flows
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbæb.əl / (AmE), / ˈbæb.əl / (BrE)
(of a stream, etc.) to flow in an irregular way with a gentle, continuous bubbling sound.

Examples

A stream babbles
A small creek began to babble over the smooth stones in the valley.
The spring rains made the brook babble more loudly than usual this year.
You could hear the water babbling as we walked along the forest path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gurgle - to flow with a bubbling or rippling sound
  • synonym: murmur - of a stream: to make a low, continuous sound as water flows
