Erolia

noun
genus of small wading birds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈroʊliə / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊliə / (BrE)
a genus of small wading birds in the family Scolopacidae, including some stints and sandpipers

Examples

Erolia sandpipers
Many taxonomists debate whether Erolia should be merged with the genus Calidris.
Several species in the genus Erolia migrate over long distances each year.
The bird identified as Erolia minutilla is commonly known as the least sandpiper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Calidris - genus of sandpipers closely related to Erolia
