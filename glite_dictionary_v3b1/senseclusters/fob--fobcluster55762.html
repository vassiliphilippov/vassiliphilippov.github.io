fob

noun
electronic key or access device
6.8 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / f51b / (AmE), / f52b / (BrE)
A small electronic device, often on a keyring, used to unlock doors, start vehicles, or authorize access and payments (typically using RFID, NFC, or Bluetooth).

Examples

Key fob
She waved her fob at the reader to gain entry to the office.
Many apartment buildings now issue fobs that residents use instead of physical keys.
He programmed a spare fob to let the cleaning staff into the secure storage room.

Synonyms

  • key fob
    3.8 0.02/m
    key fob is often attached to a key ring and opens cars or buildings, while fob can mean any small access or payment device.

Related Terms

  • synonym: key fob - a small electronic device attached to a keyring used for keyless entry or remote access
