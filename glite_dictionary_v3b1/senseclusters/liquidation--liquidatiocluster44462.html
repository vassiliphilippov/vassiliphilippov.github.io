liquidation

noun
murder or elimination of a rival
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of killing or eliminating people, especially for political or criminal reasons

Examples

Political liquidation
Several witnesses disappeared after the liquidation of the political dissident.
The gang leader ordered the liquidation of his main rival to consolidate power.
The secret police were reportedly responsible for the liquidation of opposition members.
After the war, evidence of the liquidation of entire villages was uncovered.
Historians still debate the circumstances surrounding the liquidation of the ethnic minority.
The regime's brutal liquidation of political dissidents shocked the international community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassination - the targeted killing of an important person, often for political reasons
  • synonym: extermination - the complete destruction or killing of a group
