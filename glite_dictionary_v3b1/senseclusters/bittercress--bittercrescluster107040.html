bittercress

noun
herb of the genus Cardamine
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪt̬ɚˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɪtəˌkrɛs / (BrE)
Any of various herbs of the genus Cardamine, having usually pinnate leaves and clusters of white, pink, or purple flowers, found worldwide except Antarctica.

Examples

Wild bittercress plants
Botanists identified several species of bittercress growing along the riverside.

Wild bittercress often appears in gardens during the spring months.
