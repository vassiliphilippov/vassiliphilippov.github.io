Messrs

noun
formal plural title
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsərz / (AmE), / ˈmɛsəz / (BrE)
a formal written abbreviation for 'Misters', used before the names of two or more men

Examples

Messrs Brown and Green
Messrs Brown and Green have been invited to speak at the conference.
The letter was addressed to Messrs Lee, Kim, and Patel.
Please contact Messrs Johnson and Williams for further information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Misters - the plural form of 'Mister', used for two or more men
  • degree lower: Mr - a title used before a man's surname or full name
