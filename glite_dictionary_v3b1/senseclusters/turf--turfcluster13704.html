turf

noun
grass-covered surface layer of soil
2.7 3.04/m
Pronunciation: / t…ùf / (AmE), / t…úÀêf / (BrE)
the surface layer of soil held together by the roots of grass, often used for lawns or sports fields

Examples

Green turf
After the storm, patches of turf needed to be replaced on the golf course.
Players ran across the freshly watered turf as the match began.
The landscaper laid new turf in the backyard to create a lush green lawn.
