ladder-back chair

noun
chair with slatted backrest
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlædər ˌbæk ˈtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈladə ˌbak ˈtʃɛə / (BrE)
A wooden chair with a backrest consisting of two uprights connected by horizontal slats, resembling the rungs of a ladder.

Examples

A ladder-back chair
She bought a matching set of ladder-back chairs to go with her rustic farmhouse table.
The antique ladder-back chair in the dining room has been passed down for generations.
The craftsman skillfully repaired the broken slat of the ladder-back chair.
