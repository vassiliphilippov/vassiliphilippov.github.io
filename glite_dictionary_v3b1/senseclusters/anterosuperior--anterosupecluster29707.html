anterosuperior

adjective
in front and above
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊsuːˈpɪriər / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊsjuːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
located in front and above a reference point, especially in anatomical contexts

Examples

The anterosuperior region
The anterosuperior region of the shoulder is often examined for injuries.
Surgeons must be careful when operating near the anterosuperior part of the pelvis.
The anterosuperior aspect of the vertebra is visible in this X-ray image.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anteroinferior - located in front and below a reference point
  • antonym: posterior - located at the back of a reference point
