declination

noun
polite refusal
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a polite or formal refusal, especially of an invitation or offer

Examples

A polite declination
Despite the generous proposal, he sent a formal declination in reply.
Her gracious declination of the dinner invitation was appreciated by the host.
The company's declination of the offer was delivered with careful politeness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptance - the act of agreeing to receive or do something offered
  • synonym: refusal - the act of declining or rejecting something
