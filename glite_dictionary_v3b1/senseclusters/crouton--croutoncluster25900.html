crouton

noun
small toasted or fried bread piece for soup or salad
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːtɑn / (AmE), / ˈkruːtɒn / (BrE)
A small piece of toasted or fried bread, often added to soups or salads for extra flavor and texture.

Examples

Crunchy croutons
Homemade croutons make any salad more delicious.
She added a handful of garlic croutons to her tomato soup.
The Caesar salad was topped with crunchy croutons and freshly grated cheese.
