spelunking

verb
to explore deeply
Pronunciation: / spəˈlʌŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / spəˈlʌŋkɪŋ / (BrE)

The journalist spent hours spelunking in the archives for hidden details about the case.
He loves spelunking into complex topics to uncover new insights.
