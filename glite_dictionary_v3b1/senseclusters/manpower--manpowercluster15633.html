Synonyms

  • workforce
    2.4 12/m
    workforce refers to all employed or available people in an organization or country, while manpower highlights the number available for work.
  • labor force
    2.2 2/m
    labor force includes all people able and available to work in an area, while manpower often means the staff or workers a group actually has.
  • labor resources
    3.3 0.01/m
    labor resources speak of workers as an economic supply to use, while manpower names the actual people available to do work.
