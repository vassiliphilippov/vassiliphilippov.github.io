manpower

noun
the workforce; available workers
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌpaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈmænˌpaʊə / (BrE)
the total number of people available or employed to do work, especially in a particular organization or sector

Examples

Available manpower
During the war, there was a critical shortage of manpower in the factories.
The company needs more manpower to finish the project on schedule.
We must allocate our manpower efficiently to maximize productivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staff - the people employed by an organization
  • synonym: workforce - all the workers considered collectively
