material

noun
information or data used as a basis
Pronunciation: / məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
information, ideas, or data that can be used as a basis for creating or developing something

Examples

Material for a story
His personal experiences provided rich material for his novel.
The documentary uses archival material to tell the story of the city's past.
The journalist gathered plenty of material for her article during the interviews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: content - the information or ideas contained in something such as a book or speech
  • synonym: source - the place, person, or thing from which something originates
