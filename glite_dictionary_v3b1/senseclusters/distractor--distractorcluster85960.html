distractor

noun
bone-separating device
8.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈstræk.tər/ /; / /də.ˈstræk.tər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈstræk.tə/ /; / /də.ˈstræk.tə/ (BrE)
a medical device used to gradually separate bones or tissues to encourage new bone growth or correct deformities

Examples

Jaw distractor device
The surgeon attached a distractor to the patient's jaw to help lengthen the bone.
A distractor is often used in procedures to correct bone deformities in children.
After the operation, the distractor was adjusted daily to gradually move the bone segments apart.
