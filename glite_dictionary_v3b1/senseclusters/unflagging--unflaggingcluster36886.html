unflagging

adjective
persistent; never tiring or weakening
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
never becoming tired or less determined; persistent and tireless

Examples

Unflagging energy
Despite many setbacks, his unflagging optimism kept him moving forward.
Her unflagging support for the charity inspired everyone involved.

Despite many setbacks, his unflagging enthusiasm for the project never waned.
Her unflagging optimism inspired everyone around her to keep going.

  • synonym: indefatigable - incapable of being tired out; persistent
  • synonym: persistent - continuing firmly in a course of action in spite of difficulty
