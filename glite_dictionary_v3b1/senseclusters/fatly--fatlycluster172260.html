fatly

adverb
in a fat manner
Pronunciation: / ˈfætli / (AmE), / ˈfætli / (BrE)
in a way that suggests fatness or fullness

Examples

Cat sits fatly
The cat sat fatly on the warm windowsill, looking very content.
He laughed fatly, his cheeks shaking with each chuckle.
The goose waddled fatly across the yard after being fed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavily - in a way that is heavy or weighty
  • antonym: slenderly - in a way that is slim or thin
