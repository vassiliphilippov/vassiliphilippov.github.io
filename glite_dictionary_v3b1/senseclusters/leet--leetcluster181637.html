leet

noun
online writing style
Pronunciation: / liːt / (AmE), / liːt / (BrE)
a style of online writing that replaces letters with numbers or symbols to create alternative spellings

Examples

Leet writing style
Many gamers use leet to create unique usernames online.
The message was written entirely in leet, making it hard to read for newcomers.
He enjoys translating regular sentences into leet for fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leet speak - an alternative spelling system using numbers and symbols
  • antonym: standard English - the conventional form of the English language
