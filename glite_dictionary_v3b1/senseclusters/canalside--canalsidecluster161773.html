canalside

noun
area beside canal
Pronunciation: / kəˈnælˌsaɪd / (AmE), / kəˈnælˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area or land located next to a canal

Examples

The canalside cafe
They built a new café along the canalside to attract more visitors.
Joggers enjoy the peaceful path by the canalside every morning.
The festival was held on the grassy canalside near the old bridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riverside - the land or area next to a river
  • synonym: waterside - the land next to any body of water
