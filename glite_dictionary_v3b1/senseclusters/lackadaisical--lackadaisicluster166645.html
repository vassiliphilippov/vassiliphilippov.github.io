lackadaisical

adjective
uninspired or lazy
Pronunciation: / ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəl /; / ˌlæk.əˈdeɪ.zɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌlakəˈdeɪzɪkəl /; / ˌlæk.əˈdeɪ.zɪ.kəl / (BrE)
showing little energy, enthusiasm, or interest; lazy or careless in attitude or manner

Examples

A lackadaisical attitude
After a lackadaisical performance at work, he was warned by his supervisor.
Her lackadaisical attitude toward her studies led to poor grades.
The team's lackadaisical defense allowed their opponents to score easily.
He gave a lackadaisical wave and wandered off without saying goodbye.
Her lackadaisical attitude towards her studies worried her parents.
The team played in a lackadaisical manner, showing little effort to win the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetic - showing or involving great activity or vitality
  • synonym: languid - showing a lack of energy or enthusiasm
  • synonym: listless - lacking energy or enthusiasm
