conversational phrase to introduce or emphasize a point
7.5 15/m
Pronunciation: / juÀê no ä w åt / (AmE), / juÀê n…ô ä w…ít / (BrE)
Used to introduce a remark, draw attention to a new topic, or emphasize a point in conversation.

Examples

You know what, I think
