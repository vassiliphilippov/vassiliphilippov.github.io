Synonyms

  • return
    1.0 50/m
    return is a neutral word for sending something to its owner or place, while send back emphasizes sending it away again.
  • give back
    1.0 30/m
    give back means returning an item to its owner after use, while send back focuses on sending it back to where it came from.
  • take back
    2.1 30/m
    take back often means bringing someone or something back yourself, while send back highlights sending an item back by post or request.
  • pass-back
    3.4 0.07/m
    pass-back usually means handing something back to the person who had it, while send back covers returning it to its source in general.
