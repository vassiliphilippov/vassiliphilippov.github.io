brailler

noun
braille writing machine
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪlər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪlə / (BrE)
a machine used to write in braille, enabling visually impaired people to produce braille text

Examples

A brailler machine
The student used a brailler to complete her assignments independently.
Many schools for the blind provide each classroom with at least one brailler.
After learning to use the brailler, he could write letters to his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braille - a system of writing for the visually impaired using raised dots
  • synonym: typewriter - a machine for writing characters similar to those produced by a printer's type
