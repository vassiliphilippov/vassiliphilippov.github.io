drone

noun
pipe of a bagpipe producing a continuous tone
9.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /droʊn/ /; / /drəʊn/ (AmE), /drəʊn/ (BrE)
a continuous, low, monotonous sound or the part of a musical instrument (like a bagpipe) that makes this sound

Examples
