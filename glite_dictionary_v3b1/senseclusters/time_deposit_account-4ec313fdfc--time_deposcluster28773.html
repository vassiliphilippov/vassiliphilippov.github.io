time deposit account

noun
bank account with fixed-term deposit conditions
6.2
Pronunciation: / taɪm dəˈpɑzɪt əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / taɪm dɪˈpɒzɪt əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a bank account in which money is deposited for a fixed term and typically can only be withdrawn after notice is given or at the end of the agreed period, often with interest penalties for early withdrawal

Examples

A time deposit account term
Banks often offer better rates on a time deposit account than on a regular savings account.
He decided to put his savings into a time deposit account to earn higher interest.
You may face a penalty if you withdraw money early from your time deposit account.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certificate of deposit - a bank certificate with a fixed maturity date and specified interest rate, related to time deposit accounts
  • degree lower: savings account - a bank account that earns interest but allows easier withdrawals than a time deposit account
