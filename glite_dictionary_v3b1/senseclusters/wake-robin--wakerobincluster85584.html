wake-robin

noun
woodland plant of the genus Trillium
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪk ˌrɑːbɪn / (AmE), / ˈweɪk ˌrɒbɪn / (BrE)
a woodland plant of the genus Trillium, with three leaves and a single three-petaled flower, found especially in North America
