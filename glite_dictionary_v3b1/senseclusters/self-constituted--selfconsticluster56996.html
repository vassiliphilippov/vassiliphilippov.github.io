self-constituted

adjective
formed or established by itself
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf.kənˈstɪtʃu.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.kənˈstɪtjuː.tɪd / (BrE)
having formed or established itself without external authority; self-appointed or self-created.

Examples

A self-constituted leader
He claimed authority as a self-constituted representative of the community.
Many revolutionary groups are self-constituted and operate outside of traditional structures.
The committee was a self-constituted group with no official backing from the city council.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-appointed - having assigned a position or responsibility to oneself, without approval
