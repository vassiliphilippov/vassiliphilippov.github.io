spoon

noun
former type of golf club
Pronunciation: / spuːn / (AmE), / spuːn / (BrE)
a type of wooden golf club with a slightly elevated face, formerly used for lofted shots

Examples

A wooden spoon
Collectors sometimes display antique spoons alongside other classic clubs.
He chose his spoon for the approach shot to the green.
In early golf, players used a spoon to achieve more loft.
