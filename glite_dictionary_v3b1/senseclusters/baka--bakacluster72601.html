baka

noun
slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈbækə / (BrE)
a foolish or silly person, used as a slang insult borrowed from Japanese

Examples

He is a baka
He laughed and called his friend a baka after the silly mistake.
In some anime shows, characters often shout baka when someone does something foolish.
She jokingly said 'Don't be such a baka!' when he forgot his keys again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
