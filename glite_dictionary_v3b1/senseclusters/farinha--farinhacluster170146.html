farinha

noun
ground grain powder
Pronunciation: / fəˈriːnjə / (AmE), / fəˈriːnjə / (BrE)
a finely ground powder made from cereal grains or starchy plants, used in baking and cooking

Examples

Wheat farinha for baking
She measured two cups of farinha before sifting it into the cake batter.
In Brazilian cuisine, farinha is often sprinkled over stews and meats.
The bakery uses only organic farinha to make its breads and pastries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flour - a powder made by grinding grains, used in baking
  • degree lower: meal - coarsely ground grain, less fine than flour
