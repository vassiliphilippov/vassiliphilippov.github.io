patrolwoman

noun
female police officer who patrols an area
4.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊlwʊmən / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊlwʊmən / (BrE)
A female police officer whose job is to patrol a specific area to maintain public order and safety.

Examples

A patrolwoman on duty
A patrolwoman assisted the lost child and helped her find her parents.
After years of service, the experienced patrolwoman was promoted to sergeant.
The patrolwoman walked her beat through the quiet neighborhood every morning.
