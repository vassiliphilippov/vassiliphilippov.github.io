regius

adjective
royal; kingly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒiəs / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒiəs / (BrE)
used in scientific Latin names to mean 'royal' or 'kingly', especially in species names

Examples

Regius python
The python species known as regius is often called the royal python.
Several plants have the specific epithet regius to indicate their majestic appearance.
The Latin word regius is commonly used in scientific names to honor royalty.
