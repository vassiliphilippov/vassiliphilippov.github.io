outstate

adjective
from another state
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌsteɪt / (AmE)
located in or coming from a different state than the one being referred to; not in-state.

Examples

Outstate tuition fees
She paid higher tuition because she was considered an outstate student.
Many outstate visitors come to the city for the annual festival.
The university offers special programs for outstate applicants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-state - located in or coming from the same state being referred to
  • synonym: out-of-state - from a different state than the one being referred to
