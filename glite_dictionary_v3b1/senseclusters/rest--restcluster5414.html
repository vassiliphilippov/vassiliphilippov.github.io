rest

verb
place or support on something
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɛst / (AmE), / rɛst / (BrE)
to place something so that it is supported or steady, often on or against something else

Examples

To rest your hand
He rested his hand gently on her shoulder to comfort her.
Please rest the ladder firmly against the wall before climbing.
She rested her elbows on the desk as she read the letter.
