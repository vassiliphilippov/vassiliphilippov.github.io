crash-dive

verb
descend steeply and rapidly
Pronunciation: / kræʃ daɪv / (AmE), / kræʃ daɪv / (BrE)
to descend very steeply and rapidly, especially of an aircraft or submarine making a sudden dive

Examples

To crash-dive quickly
The pilot had to crash-dive to avoid a mid-air collision.
The submarine began to crash-dive as soon as the alarm sounded.
With little warning, the roller coaster seemed to crash-dive straight towards the ground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascend - to go up or rise
  • synonym: plunge - move or fall suddenly and quickly downward
