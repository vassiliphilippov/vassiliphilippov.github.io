clearing

noun
removing solids from a liquid
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of removing suspended solid particles from a liquid, often to make it clear or pure

Examples

Clearing of water
After filtration, the clearing of the solution increased its transparency.
Centrifugation aids in the clearing of biological samples by removing particulate matter.
The clearing of water is an essential step in many municipal treatment plants.
