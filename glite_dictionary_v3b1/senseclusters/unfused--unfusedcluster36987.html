unfused

adjective
not joined together
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfjuzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfjuːzd / (BrE)

Two unfused parts

In the experiment, the chemicals stayed unfused despite being mixed together.
The artist left the colors unfused to create a striking visual effect.
