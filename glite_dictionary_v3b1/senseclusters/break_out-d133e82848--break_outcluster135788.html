break out

verb
start suddenly (war, fighting, disease)
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /breɪk aʊt/ (AmE), /breɪk aʊt/ (BrE)
to start suddenly, especially something unpleasant like a fire, war, or disease

Violence broke out after the match ended.
