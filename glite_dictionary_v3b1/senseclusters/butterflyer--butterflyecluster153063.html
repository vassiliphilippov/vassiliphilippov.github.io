butterflyer

noun
butterfly enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərˌflaɪər / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪə / (BrE)
a person who collects, breeds, or studies butterflies, either professionally or as a hobby

Examples

Expert butterflyer

As a dedicated butterflyer, she kept detailed notes on every butterfly she observed.
Many butterflyers share their findings with local nature groups and museums.
