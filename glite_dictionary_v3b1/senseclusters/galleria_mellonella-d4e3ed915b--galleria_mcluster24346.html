Galleria mellonella

noun
a moth species whose larvae eat bee honeycombs
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɪəriə ˌmɛləˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɪəriə ˌmɛləˈnɛlə / (BrE)
a species of moth whose larvae, called waxworms, live in and feed on bee honeycombs, often considered a pest in beekeeping
