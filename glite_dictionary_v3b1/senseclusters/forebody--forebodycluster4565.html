forebody

noun
front part of body
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌbɒdi / (BrE)
the front part of an animal's body, especially the head and thorax in insects or the anterior region in other animals

Examples

Forebody of an insect
The forebody of the beetle is covered in a hard, shiny shell.
Scientists measured the forebody to compare it with the rest of the animal's anatomy.
In some species, the forebody is much larger than the hindbody.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anterior - the front part of the body or nearer to the head
  • antonym: hindbody - the rear part of an animal's body
