Homo antecessor

noun
extinct human species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmoʊ ænˈtɛsər /; / ˈhoʊmoʊ ænˈtɛsəsər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməʊ ænˈtɛsəsə / (BrE)
an extinct early human species known from fossils found in Spain, believed to have lived about one million years ago

Many researchers believe Homo antecessor could be a common ancestor of later human species.
