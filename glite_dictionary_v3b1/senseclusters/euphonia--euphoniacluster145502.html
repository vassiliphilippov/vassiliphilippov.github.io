euphonia

noun
pleasant sound
Pronunciation: / juːˈfoʊniə / (AmE), / juːˈfəʊniə / (BrE)
a quality of sound that is pleasing or harmonious to the ear

Examples

Beautiful euphonia of music
The gentle euphonia of the piano filled the quiet room with warmth.
She admired the euphonia in the singer's voice during the performance.
Nature's euphonia can be heard in the harmonious songs of birds at dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cacophony - a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds
  • synonym: melody - a sequence of musical notes that is pleasant to listen to
