oleate

noun
salt of oleic acid
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊliˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊliˌeɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester that is formed from oleic acid

Examples

Sodium oleate

Sodium oleate is commonly used as an emulsifier in various products.
Researchers studied the effects of oleate on cell membranes in the laboratory.
