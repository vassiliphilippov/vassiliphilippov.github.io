voraciousness

noun
excessive or insatiable desire
Pronunciation: / vɔˈreɪʃənəsnəs /; / vɔˈreɪʃəsnəs /; / vəˈreɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / vəˈreɪʃəsnəs /; / vɒˈreɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having an excessive or insatiable desire or appetite (for food, wealth, experience, etc.)

Examples

Some investors approach the stock market with a voraciousness that borders on obsession.
The voraciousness of his curiosity led him to read every book in the library.
