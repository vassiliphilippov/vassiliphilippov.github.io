Huxleyan

adjective
relating to Thomas Huxley
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌksli.ən / (AmE), / ˈhʌksliːən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Thomas Huxley, the English biologist and advocate of Darwin's theory of evolution

Examples

Huxleyan evolutionary theory
Her scientific approach was decidedly Huxleyan in its advocacy for evolutionary theory.
Many admired his Huxleyan defense of science against religious dogma.
The debate adopted a Huxleyan perspective, blending skepticism with empirical evidence.
