Straussian

adjective
of Leo Strauss's ideas
Pronunciation: / straʊsiən / (AmE), / straʊsiən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, philosophy, or followers of Leo Strauss, a 20th-century political philosopher

Examples

Straussian philosophy debate
The scholar presented a Straussian reading of Plato's Republic in her lecture.
Many debates in political theory reference Straussian interpretations of classical texts.
His approach to philosophy is often described as distinctly Straussian by his colleagues.
