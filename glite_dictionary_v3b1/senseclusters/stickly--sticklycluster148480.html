stickly

adjective
thin or slender
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkli / (AmE), / ˈstɪkli / (BrE)
thin, slender, or resembling a stick in shape or appearance

Examples

Stickly branches
The stickly branches barely supported the weight of the bird.
He wore a stickly tie that looked out of place with his suit.
The plant's stickly stems made it difficult to weave into a basket.

Related Terms
