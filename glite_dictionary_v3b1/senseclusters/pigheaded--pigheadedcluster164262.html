pigheaded

adjective
stubborn and foolishly obstinate
8.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪg.hed.ɪd/ /; / /ˈpɪg.hed.əd/ (AmE), /ˈpɪg.hed.ɪd/ /; / /ˈpɪg.hed.əd/ (BrE)
unreasonably stubborn and unwilling to change one's opinion or behavior; obstinately stupid

Examples

A pigheaded person
Despite all the evidence, he remained pigheaded and refused to admit he was wrong.
Her pigheaded insistence on ignoring good advice led to unnecessary problems.
It was a pigheaded decision that no one agreed with.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstinate - stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or chosen course of action
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas or opinions
  • synonym: stubborn - having or showing dogged determination not to change one's attitude or position
