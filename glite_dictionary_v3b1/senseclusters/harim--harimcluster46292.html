harim

noun
women's quarters
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈriːm / (AmE), / həˈriːm / (BrE)
the part of a Muslim household reserved for women, traditionally off-limits to men outside the family

Examples

Palace harim
The palace's harim was beautifully decorated and strictly guarded.
Only female relatives and servants were allowed inside the harim of the house.
Stories about life in the harim have fascinated historians for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harem - the separate part of a Muslim household for wives, concubines, and female servants
