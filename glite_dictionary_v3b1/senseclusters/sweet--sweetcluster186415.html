sweet

adverb
in an affectionate or loving manner (poetic/informal adverb)
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
In an affectionate, loving, or charming manner; sometimes used as a poetic or informal variant of 'sweetly'.

Examples

He spoke sweet words
He sang to her sweet beneath the stars outside her window.
She smiled sweet and thanked everyone for the lovely surprise.
The couple spoke sweet words to each other in the quiet café.
