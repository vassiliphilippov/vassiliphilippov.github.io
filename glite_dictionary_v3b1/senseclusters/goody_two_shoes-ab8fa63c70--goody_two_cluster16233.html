goody two shoes

noun
person who is overly virtuous or well-behaved
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdi tu ˌʃuz / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi tuː ˌʃuːz / (BrE)
a person who behaves in an extremely virtuous or well-behaved manner, often regarded as annoying or smug by others

Examples

The goody two shoes student
No one wanted to sit with the goody two shoes at lunch because she never broke the rules.
She was labelled a goody two shoes for always volunteering to help the teachers.
Stop being such a goody two shoes and join us for some harmless fun!

Related Terms

  • synonym: goody-goody - a person who behaves in a smugly virtuous way
