microorganism

noun
organism visible only under a microscope
5.1 3.33/m
Pronunciation: / Àåma…™.k…πo äÀà…îÀê…π.…°…ôÀån…™z.…ôm / (AmE), / Àåma…™.k…π…ô äÀà…îÀê.…°…ôn…™z.…ôm / (BrE)
An organism that is too small to be seen with the naked eye, such as bacteria, protozoa, and some fungi and algae.

Examples

A microorganism cell
A single microorganism can multiply rapidly under the right conditions.
Proper sanitation helps prevent the spread of harmful microorganisms.
Scientists are studying a new microorganism found in deep-sea vents.

Synonyms

  • micro-organism
    3.9 0.01/m
    micro-organism is the hyphenated form sometimes used in writing for tiny organisms seen by microscope, while microorganism is the common single-word form.
  • microbe
    5.1 6.78/m
    microbe is an informal word often used for disease-causing tiny organisms, while microorganism is the broader scientific term for all microscopic life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacterium - a type of single-celled microorganism
  • synonym: germ - informal term for a microorganism, especially one that causes disease
