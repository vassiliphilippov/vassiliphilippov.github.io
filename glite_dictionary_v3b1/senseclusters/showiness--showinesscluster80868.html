showiness

noun
extravagant or flashy display
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ.i.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being extravagant, flashy, or intended to attract notice by elaborate display.

Examples

The showiness of jewelry
He disliked the showiness of expensive jewelry and preferred subtle accessories.
Some critics felt that the movie's visual showiness distracted from its story.
The showiness of the decorations overwhelmed the small, simple room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flamboyance - extravagant confidence or elaborateness in behavior or style
  • antonym: modesty - quality of being moderate or reserved, lacking extravagance
