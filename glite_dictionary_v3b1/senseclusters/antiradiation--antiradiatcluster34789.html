antiradiation

noun
radiation shield
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a material or device designed to shield or absorb harmful radiation to protect people or equipment

Examples

Antiradiation shield
The hospital installed antiradiation panels in the X-ray room to protect staff and patients.
Scientists developed a new antiradiation suit for workers handling radioactive materials.
The spacecraft was equipped with antiradiation shielding to withstand cosmic rays during its journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrier - something that blocks or protects from something else
  • synonym: shield - a protective barrier against danger or harm
