speak of

verb
mention or talk about
1.9 31/m
Pronunciation: / spiÀêk …ôv / (AmE), / spiÀêk …ív / (BrE)
to talk about or mention someone or something in speech or writing

Examples

To speak of someone
During the interview, she spoke of her experiences volunteering abroad.
He never spoke of the events that happened that night.
Historians often speak of the importance of cultural exchange in ancient times.

Synonyms

  • mention
    1.0 180/m
    mention is a brief reference in speech or writing, while speak of means to talk about someone or something more generally.
  • make mention of
    3.6 0.32/m
    make mention of is a formal way to refer briefly to something, while speak of fits both speech and writing in general use.
  • talk of
    2.5 6.37/m
    talk of is used in conversation to mention or discuss topics often briefly, while speak of also covers writing as well as speech.
  • talk about
    1.3 418/m
    talk about means to discuss a subject in conversation, often at length, while speak of can also mean simply to mention.

Related Terms
