Synonyms

  • social welfare
    2.5 0.8/m
    social welfare is government aid for individuals or groups, often as broad programs, while welfare focuses on assistance to those in need.
  • public assistance
    2.5 0.8/m
    public assistance gives direct financial help to families in urgent need, while welfare covers broader government support programs.
  • social assistance
    3.2 0.06/m
    social assistance provides benefits to keep household income above legal minimums, while welfare describes a wider set of aid programs.
