welfare

noun
government-provided economic assistance
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈwɛl.fɛə / (BrE)
Governmental provision of financial or other assistance to individuals in need, especially through organized programs.

Examples

On welfare support
After losing her job, she had to rely on welfare to support her family.
He grew up in a neighborhood where many residents depended on welfare.
The debate over welfare reform dominated last night's political discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefit - a government grant or payment to help those in need
  • synonym: social security - a government system providing monetary assistance to people with an inadequate or no income
