Governmental provision of financial or other assistance to individuals in need, especially through organized programs.

Examples

On welfare support
After losing her job, she had to rely on welfare to support her family.
He grew up in a neighborhood where many residents depended on welfare.
The debate over welfare reform dominated last night's political discussion.

Synonyms

  • social welfare
    2.5 2.24/m
    social welfare is government aid for individuals or groups, often as broad programs, while welfare focuses on assistance to those in need.
  • public assistance
    2.5 1.29/m
    public assistance gives direct financial help to families in urgent need, while welfare covers broader government support programs.
  • social assistance
    3.2 0.14/m
    social assistance provides benefits to keep household income above legal minimums, while welfare describes a wider set of aid programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefit - a government grant or payment to help those in need
  • synonym: social security - a government system providing monetary assistance to people with an inadequate or no income
