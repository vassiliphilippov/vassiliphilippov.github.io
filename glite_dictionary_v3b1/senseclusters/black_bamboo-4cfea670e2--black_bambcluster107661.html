black bamboo

noun
bamboo species with black stems
Pronunciation: / blæk bæmˈbu / (AmE), / blak bamˈbuː / (BrE)
a bamboo species with slender green stems that turn glossy black as they mature

Examples

The black bamboo plant
After a few years, the black bamboo culms developed their characteristic glossy black color.
He bought several black bamboo plants to add an exotic touch to his backyard.
The garden features a row of black bamboo along the fence, creating a dramatic contrast.
