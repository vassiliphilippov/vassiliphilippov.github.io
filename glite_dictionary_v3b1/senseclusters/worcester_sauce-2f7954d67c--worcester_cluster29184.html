Worcester sauce

noun
savory condiment for flavoring food
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊstər sɔs / (AmE), / ˈwʊstə sɔːs / (BrE)
a piquant, dark brown sauce made from vinegar, molasses, spices, and other ingredients, used to flavor food

Examples

A bottle of Worcester sauce
I always keep a bottle of Worcester sauce in my kitchen for marinades.
She added a dash of Worcester sauce to the stew for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of Worcester sauce to enhance the taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Worcestershire sauce - another name for Worcester sauce, especially in British and American English
