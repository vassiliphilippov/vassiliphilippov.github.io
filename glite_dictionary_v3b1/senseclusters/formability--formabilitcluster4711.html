formability

noun
ability to be shaped
6.0
Pronunciation: /fɑːr.mə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /fɔːr.mə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /fɔː.mə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)
the ability of a material to be shaped or formed without breaking or cracking

Examples

Metal formability test
Engineers test the formability of metals before using them in car manufacturing.
High formability makes this plastic ideal for creating complex shapes.
The formability of the material allows it to be used in a wide range of products.

Related Terms
