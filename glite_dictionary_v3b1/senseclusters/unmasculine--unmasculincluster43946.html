unmasculine

adjective
lacking masculine traits
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæskjəlɪn / (AmE), / ʌnˈmæskjʊlɪn / (BrE)
not having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with men or boys

Examples

Unmasculine behavior
Some people unfairly labeled his gentle behavior as unmasculine.
Wearing bright colors was once considered unmasculine in certain cultures.
He never let stereotypes about being unmasculine stop him from pursuing his interests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminate - having qualities traditionally associated with women
  • antonym: masculine - having qualities traditionally associated with men
