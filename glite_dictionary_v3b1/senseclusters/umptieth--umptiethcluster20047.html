umptieth

adjective
last in an indefinite series (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈʌmp.tiˌɪθ / (AmE), / ˈʌmptiːɪθ / (BrE)
(informal) Used to indicate the last in a long and indefinite series; often humorous or exaggerated.

Examples

The umptieth time
For the umptieth time, please turn off the lights when you leave the room.
He watched his favorite movie for the umptieth time this weekend.
I just heard that story for the umptieth time today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: umpteenth - an indefinitely large number in a series; very many
