nymphette

noun
young female spirit
Pronunciation: / nɪmˈfɛt / (AmE), / nɪmˈfɛt / (BrE)
a small or young nymph, often depicted as a fairy-like female spirit in mythology or folklore

Examples

A fairy nymphette
In the garden, the children imagined a tiny nymphette dancing among the flowers at dusk.
The story described a nymphette who guided lost travelers through the enchanted forest.
Legends say that a nymphette appears by the stream whenever the moon is full.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy - a small imaginary being with magical powers
  • synonym: nymph - a mythological spirit of nature in the form of a young woman
