AirDrop

verb
send files wirelessly between devices
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈer.drɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈeə.drɒp/ (BrE)
to transfer files or other data directly between nearby devices using a short-range wireless sharing feature (originally the Apple feature called AirDrop).

Examples

To AirDrop photos
She used AirDrop to send the vacation photos to her colleague before the meeting.
If we both enable AirDrop, I can quickly transfer the PDF from my phone to yours.
Students often AirDrop notes to classmates between lectures when they miss key points.

Related Terms

  • synonym: share - to give a copy of a file or information to someone else, often electronically
  • synonym: transfer - to move something from one place or person to another, especially data or files
