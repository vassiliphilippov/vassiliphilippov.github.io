mongrelized

adjective
offensive, mixed origin
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋɡrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡrɪˌlaɪzd / (BrE)

The label mongrelized is considered highly offensive and inappropriate in modern discussions.
