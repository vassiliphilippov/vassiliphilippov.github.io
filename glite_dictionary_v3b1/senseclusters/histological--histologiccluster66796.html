histological

adjective
relating to tissue structure (histology)
Pronunciation: / hɪstəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / hɪstəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the microscopic structure of tissues or the study of tissues (histology)

Examples

Histological features
Each tumor was classified based on its histological features.
The diagnosis was confirmed through a histological examination of the tissue sample.
There were no significant histological differences observed between the treated and control groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: histological - pertaining to histology; alternate spelling 'histologic' common in American English
