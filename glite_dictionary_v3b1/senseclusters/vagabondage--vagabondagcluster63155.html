vagabondage

noun
wandering without a permanent home
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvæɡəˌbɑndɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvaɡəbɒndɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or condition of wandering from place to place without a permanent home or fixed purpose

Examples

The vagabondage life
Her vagabondage across Europe inspired many of her later paintings.
The novel depicts a life of vagabondage on the open road.
The years he spent in vagabondage taught him resilience and resourcefulness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wandering - the act of moving from place to place without purpose
