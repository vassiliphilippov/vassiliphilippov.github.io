Mekong River

proper noun
large river in Southeast Asia
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.kɔŋ ˈrɪv.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈmiː.kɒŋ ˈrɪv.ə / (BrE)
a major river in Southeast Asia, flowing from Tibet through China, Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, and Vietnam, and into the South China Sea
