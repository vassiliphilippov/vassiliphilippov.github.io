pulseless

adjective
without a detectable pulse
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlslɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsləs / (BrE)
having no detectable pulse; not showing any signs of a heartbeat or circulation

Examples

Pulseless patient
After the accident, she was unresponsive and appeared pulseless at the scene.
If a patient is pulseless, defibrillation may be required to restore cardiac activity.
The paramedics found the victim pulseless and immediately began CPR.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: dead - no longer alive
  • synonym: lifeless - without signs of life or motion
