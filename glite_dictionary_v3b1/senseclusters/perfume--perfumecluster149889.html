perfume

noun
pleasant or distinctive fragrance
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.fjuːm / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fjuːm / (BrE)
a pleasant and distinctive smell or fragrance, especially one that is sweet or appealing

Examples

A sweet perfume
A lovely perfume from the bakery drifted out onto the street, enticing passersby.
She recognized his presence by the subtle perfume that lingered after he left the room.
The perfume of blooming roses filled the garden every morning.

  • antonym: stench - a strong and very unpleasant smell
