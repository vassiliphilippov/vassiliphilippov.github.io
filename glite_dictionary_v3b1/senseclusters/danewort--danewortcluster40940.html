danewort

noun
dwarf European elder shrub
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪnwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪnwɜːt / (BrE)
a dwarf European elder shrub (Sambucus ebulus) with pink flowers and a strong, unpleasant odor

Examples

The danewort shrub
Some herbalists once believed danewort possessed medicinal properties, despite its nauseous odor.
The hedgerow was dotted with danewort in midsummer, its pink flowers almost hidden by foliage.
We identified the strong smell coming from the danewort growing near the old fence.
