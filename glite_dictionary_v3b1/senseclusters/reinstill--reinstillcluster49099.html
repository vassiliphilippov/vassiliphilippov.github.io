reinstill

verb
to put liquid again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈstɪl / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈstɪl / (BrE)
to put a liquid, especially medicine, into a body part again, usually drop by drop.

Examples

To reinstill eye drops
The doctor instructed her to reinstill the eye drops every four hours after the procedure.
If you miss a dose, simply reinstill the medication as soon as you remember.
After swimming, he had to reinstill the ear drops to prevent infection.
