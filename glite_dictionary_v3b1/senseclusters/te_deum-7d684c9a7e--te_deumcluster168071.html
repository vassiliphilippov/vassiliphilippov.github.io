Te Deum

noun
ancient Christian hymn of praise
Pronunciation: / ti ˈdeɪəm / (AmE), / ti ˈdeɪʊm / (BrE)
a Christian hymn of praise and thanksgiving dating from the early church, traditionally sung on special occasions

Examples

The Te Deum hymn
A solemn Te Deum was sung after the victory was declared.
Many composers have written their own settings of the Te Deum over the centuries.
The choir performed the Te Deum to celebrate the cathedral's anniversary.
