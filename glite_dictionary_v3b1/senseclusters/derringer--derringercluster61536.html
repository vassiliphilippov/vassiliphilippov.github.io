derringer

noun
small, short-barreled pocket pistol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈdɛrɪndʒə / (BrE)
a small, easily concealed pistol of large caliber with a short barrel, typically used as a pocket firearm.

Examples

A derringer pistol
A derringer is often depicted in old Western movies as a gambler's last resort.
She found an antique derringer at the gun collector's exhibit.
The detective slipped a derringer into his pocket before leaving the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pistol - a small firearm designed to be held in one hand
