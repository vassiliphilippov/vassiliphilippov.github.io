snowless

adjective
without snow
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊləs / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊləs / (BrE)
having no snow present or covering an area

Examples

Snowless winter
The mountains remained snowless throughout the entire winter season.
We were disappointed to find the ski resort completely snowless in January.
Last December was unusually warm and snowless in our region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: snowy - covered with or having a lot of snow
