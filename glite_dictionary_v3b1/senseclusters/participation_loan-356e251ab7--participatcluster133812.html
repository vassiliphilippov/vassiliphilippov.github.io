participation loan

noun
loan shared by multiple banks
4.8 5.9
Pronunciation: /pɑːr.tɪ.sə.ˈpeɪ.ʃən loʊn/ /; / /pɑːr.tɪ.sɪ.ˈpeɪ.ʃən loʊn/ (AmE), /pɑː.tɪ.sɪ.ˈpeɪ.ʃən ləʊn/ /; / /pɑː.tɪ.sə.ˈpeɪ.ʃən ləʊn/ (BrE)
