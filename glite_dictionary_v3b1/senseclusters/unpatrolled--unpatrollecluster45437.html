unpatrolled

adjective
not guarded by patrols
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpəˈtroʊld / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəˈtrəʊld / (BrE)
not watched, guarded, or monitored by patrols or security personnel

Examples

An unpatrolled area
The hikers entered an unpatrolled section of the park, where few people ever go.
It is risky to leave your car in an unpatrolled parking lot overnight.
The border remained unpatrolled for several hours due to the storm.
