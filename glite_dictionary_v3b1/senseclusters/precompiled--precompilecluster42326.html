precompiled

adjective
prepared in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkəmˈpaɪld / (AmE), / ˌpriːkəmˈpaɪld / (BrE)
prepared or converted into a usable form, especially by compiling, before being needed or used

Examples

Precompiled modules
The application includes several precompiled modules to speed up installation.
You can download a precompiled version of the program for your operating system.
Using precompiled headers can significantly reduce build times in large projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compiled - converted into a machine-readable form by a compiler
  • antonym: dynamic - created or processed at the time of use rather than in advance
