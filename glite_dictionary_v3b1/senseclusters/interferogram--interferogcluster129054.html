interferogram

noun
interference pattern record
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜrfərəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːfərəˌɡræm / (BrE)
an image or pattern created by the interference of waves, used in scientific research to analyze properties or changes
