critically

adverb
in a critical or analytical manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəli / (BrE)
In a way that involves careful judgment or analysis, especially in identifying errors or weaknesses.

Examples

To critically analyze
His performance was critically analyzed by the panel of judges.
The architect evaluated the blueprints critically, searching for any potential design flaws.
You should read the article critically to understand its biases and assumptions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analytically - in an analytical manner
  • antonym: uncritically - in a way that lacks critical judgment
