stake driver

noun
a North American bittern bird
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪk ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪk ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
A North American bittern (Botaurus lentiginosus), a wading bird of the heron family, known for its booming call and somewhat smaller size than the European bittern.
