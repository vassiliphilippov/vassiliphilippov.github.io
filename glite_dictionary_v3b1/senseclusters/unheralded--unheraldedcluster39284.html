unheralded

adjective
not recognized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛrəldɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛrəldɪd / (BrE)
not publicly praised, recognized, or celebrated for achievements or qualities

Examples

Unheralded hero
The unheralded scientist made a discovery that changed the field forever.
Many unheralded workers keep the company running smoothly behind the scenes.
Her unheralded efforts eventually led to a major breakthrough for the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: celebrated - publicly praised and recognized
  • synonym: unsung - not celebrated or praised, especially for achievements
