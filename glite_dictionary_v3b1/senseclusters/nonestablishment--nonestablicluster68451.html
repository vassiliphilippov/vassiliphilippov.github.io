nonestablishment

adjective
not part of establishment
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˈstæblɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈstæblɪʃmənt / (BrE)
not belonging to or supporting established institutions, authorities, or traditional elites; independent of the establishment

Examples

Nonestablishment views
The nonestablishment candidate appealed to voters tired of traditional party politics.
Many young artists prefer a nonestablishment approach to expressing their ideas.
Her nonestablishment views set her apart from the mainstream members of the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiestablishment - opposed to or actively challenging the establishment
  • antonym: establishment - the dominant group or authority in society
