postable

adjective
suitable for mailing
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or acceptable for sending through the postal service; able to be mailed

Examples

Postable letter
Please make sure your package is postable before bringing it to the post office.
Some items are too large or fragile to be considered postable by the postal service.
The document was folded to fit into a standard envelope and become postable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mailable - able to be sent by mail
  • antonym: unmailable - not suitable for sending by mail
