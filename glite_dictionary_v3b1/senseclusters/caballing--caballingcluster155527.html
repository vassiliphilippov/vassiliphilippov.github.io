caballing

noun
secret plotting
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈbælɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈbælɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of forming a secret group to plot or scheme, especially for political purposes

Examples

Caballing for power
Rumors of caballing within the organization caused concern among the staff.
The politician denied any involvement in caballing against his rivals.
Years of caballing eventually led to a major shift in leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intrigue - the practice of making secret plans
  • antonym: openness - the quality of being open and transparent
