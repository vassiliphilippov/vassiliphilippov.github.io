maieutic method

noun
Socratic teaching by question and answer
6.3 9.1
Pronunciation: /maɪ.ˈjuː.tɪk ˈmeθ.əd/ /; / /meɪ.ˈjuː.tɪk ˈmeθ.əd/ (AmE), /meɪ.ˈjuː.tɪk ˈmeθ.əd/ /; / /maɪ.ˈjuː.tɪk ˈmeθ.əd/ (BrE)
A method of teaching through questions and answers, often to help students discover truths for themselves; famously used by Socrates.
