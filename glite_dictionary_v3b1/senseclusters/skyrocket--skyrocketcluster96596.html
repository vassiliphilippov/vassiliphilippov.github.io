skyrocket

noun
firework sent high into the sky
7.1 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈskaɪ.rɑː.kət/ (AmE), /ˈskaɪ.rɒk.ɪt/ (BrE)
a firework or device that is shot high into the sky and explodes or sends a signal

Examples

A colorful skyrocket

A skyrocket was used to send a lifeline from the shore to the stranded ship.

They set off a skyrocket to celebrate the New Year at midnight.
