bushiness

noun
thick, full quality
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being thick, full, and resembling a bush in appearance

Examples

The bushiness of hedge
The bushiness of the hedge made it perfect for privacy in the garden.
He admired the bushiness of her curly hair, which framed her face beautifully.
The artist captured the bushiness of the fox's tail in great detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fullness - the state of being full or well filled
  • synonym: thickness - the quality of being thick or dense
  • antonym: thinness - the quality of being thin or sparse
