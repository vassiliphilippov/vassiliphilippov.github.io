butternut squash

noun
buff-colored winter squash; sweet orange flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌnʌt skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtənʌt skwɒʃ /; / ˈbʌtənʌt ˌskwɒʃ / (BrE)
a type of winter squash with buff-colored skin, sweet orange flesh, and a bottle-like shape, commonly used in cooking
