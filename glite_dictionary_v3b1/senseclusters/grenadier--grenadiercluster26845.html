grenadier

noun
soldier equipped with grenades
1.8 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪr / (AmE), / ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪə / (BrE)
A soldier, especially in the past, who was equipped with grenades or a member of certain elite infantry regiments.

The museum has a uniform worn by a British grenadier during the 18th century.
