pinfish

noun
small spiny-finned fish of the southeastern US
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small spiny-finned fish (Lagodon rhomboides) found in bays along the southeastern coast of the United States, similar to the sea bream
