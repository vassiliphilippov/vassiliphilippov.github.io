nonbiological

adjective
not genetically related
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not related by birth or genetics; not connected by biological ties

Examples

Nonbiological parent
She considers her nonbiological siblings to be just as important as her blood relatives.
The child was raised by his nonbiological parents after being adopted as an infant.
Many families today include nonbiological members who are deeply loved and valued.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoptive - related by adoption rather than by birth
  • antonym: biological - related by birth or genetics
