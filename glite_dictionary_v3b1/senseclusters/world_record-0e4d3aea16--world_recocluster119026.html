world record

noun
the best achievement globally in a field
Pronunciation: / wɜrld ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
the best achievement that has ever been officially recorded anywhere in the world, especially in a particular sport or other competitive activity

Examples

A world record time
A new world record was set at the championship yesterday.
He's training hard, hoping to set a world record in swimming.
She broke the world record for the 100-meter sprint.
