pedalo

verb
to use pedal boat
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɛdəˌləʊ / (BrE)
to operate or travel in a small pedal-powered boat by turning its pedals

Examples

To pedalo on water
We decided to pedalo around the lake for an hour on a sunny afternoon.
Tourists often pedalo along the river to enjoy the city views from the water.
They love to pedalo together every summer when they visit the seaside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedal - to move by pushing pedals with your feet
  • antonym: row - to move a boat using oars
