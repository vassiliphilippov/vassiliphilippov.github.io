authority

noun
official permission or approval
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈθɔr.ə.ti / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
official permission or approval to do something

Examples

Authority to act
She acted with full authority from the board to negotiate the contract.
The department finally received authority to hire new staff members.
Without the proper authority, the application process cannot move forward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorization - the act of giving official permission or approval
  • antonym: prohibition - the act of forbidding something by law or authority
