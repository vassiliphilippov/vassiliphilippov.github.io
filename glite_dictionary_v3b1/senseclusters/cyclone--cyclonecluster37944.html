cyclone

noun
violent rotating windstorm
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.kloʊn /; / ˈsaɪkloʊn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.kləʊn /; / ˈsaɪkləʊn / (BrE)
a violent, rotating windstorm, especially in tropical or subtropical regions, that can cause damage and stormy weather

Examples

A cyclone season
Indian farmers often fear the loss of crops during the cyclone season.
Meteorologists issued a warning as a powerful cyclone approached the region.
The cyclone destroyed several homes along the coast, leaving widespread devastation in its wake.
Damage from the cyclone included flooding, downed trees, and widespread power outages.
Meteorologists are tracking the movement of a cyclone forming off the southern coast.
The coastal city was evacuated when a powerful cyclone approached from the ocean.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anticyclone - a weather system with high pressure at its center, winds circulate opposite to a cyclone
  • synonym: hurricane - a type of intense tropical cyclone in the Atlantic
  • synonym: typhoon - a tropical cyclone in the western Pacific
