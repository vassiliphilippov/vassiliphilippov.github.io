tapper

noun
person or device tapping
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpər / (AmE), / ˈtæpə / (BrE)
a person or device that taps a tree to collect sap or other substances

Examples

Tree tapper
The tapper carefully inserted a spout into the tree to collect the sap.
In some regions, a tapper uses special tools to harvest rubber from trees.
A modern tapper can be a machine designed to automate the process of extracting liquids.
The tapper rose before dawn to begin collecting sap from the maple trees.
Each tapper is responsible for dozens of trees during the harvest season.
In some countries, a tapper uses special tools to extract latex from rubber trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person or device that gathers or collects something
  • synonym: extractor - a person or device that removes something from a source
  • synonym: harvester - a person who gathers crops or natural products
  • antonym: planter - a person who plants crops or trees
