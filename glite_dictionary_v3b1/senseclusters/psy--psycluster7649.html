relating to the mind
8.9
Pronunciation: / saɪ / (AmE), / saɪ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'mind' or 'mental', used in words related to psychology or the mind

Examples

Psy- prefix
The psy- in 'psychology' refers to the study of the mind.
Words like 'psychiatry' and 'psychic' begin with the combining form psy-.
In medical terms, psy- often indicates something related to mental health.
