Buffalo Bill Cody

proper noun
American showman famous for his Wild West Show
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəloʊ bɪl ˈkoʊdi / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ bɪl ˈkəʊdi / (BrE)
(1846–1917) American showman and bison hunter, famous for his Wild West Show that popularized the myth of the American West.

Examples

Buffalo Bill Cody Wild West
Buffalo Bill Cody traveled across the United States, staging reenactments of cowboy life with his Wild West Show.
Many consider Buffalo Bill Cody a key figure in shaping popular images of the American frontier.
Museums dedicated to Buffalo Bill Cody can be found in Wyoming and elsewhere.
