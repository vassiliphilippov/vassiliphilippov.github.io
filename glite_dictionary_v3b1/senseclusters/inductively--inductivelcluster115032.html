inductively

adverb
by generalizing from cases
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌktɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌktɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves reasoning from specific cases or observations to general principles or rules

Examples

Analyze data inductively
The researcher examined the data inductively to develop a new theory.
Students were encouraged to think inductively when solving complex problems.
By observing several experiments, she reasoned inductively to find a common pattern.
