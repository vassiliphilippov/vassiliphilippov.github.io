Holodiscus

proper noun
North American shrub genus
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊloʊˈdɪskəs / (AmE), / ˌhɒləʊˈdɪskəs / (BrE)
a genus of shrubs native to North America, known for clusters of small white flowers and commonly called oceanspray

Examples

Holodiscus shrubs
Holodiscus is often found growing on hillsides and in forests across western North America.
Gardeners appreciate Holodiscus for its attractive white flower clusters in late spring.
Wildlife such as birds and insects are attracted to the blossoms of Holodiscus shrubs.

Related Terms
