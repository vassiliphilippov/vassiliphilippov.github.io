Llud

proper noun
Celtic warrior god
Pronunciation: / luːd / (AmE), / ljuːd / (BrE)
A Celtic warrior god, particularly associated with British mythology.

Examples

Llud the warrior god
Ancient legends speak of Llud defending Britain from invaders.
Some historians compare Llud to other warrior gods in Celtic tradition.
The story of Llud and Llefelys is central to Welsh mythology.
