infect

verb
contaminate a computer with malware
3.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfekt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfekt/ (BrE)
(computing) to contaminate or harm a computer, device, or digital system by introducing malware, a virus, or other malicious code.

Examples

To infect a computer
The ransomware attack could infect thousands of company servers before teams detected it.
After opening the unknown attachment, her laptop was quickly infected with spyware that stole passwords.
Security software helps prevent malicious emails from infecting user devices across the corporate network.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - to remove malware or restore a system to a safe, uninfected state
  • synonym: compromise - in computing, to gain unauthorized access to or control of a system or data
