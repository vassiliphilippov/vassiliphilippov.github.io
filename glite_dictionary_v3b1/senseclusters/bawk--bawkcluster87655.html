bawk

noun
fowl clucking sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɔk / (AmE), / bɔːk / (BrE)
a clucking or cackling sound made by a chicken or other fowl

Examples

A chicken bawk
The sudden bawk from the hen startled everyone in the yard.
Every morning, the chickens greet the farmer with a loud bawk.
A soft bawk came from the coop as the hens settled for the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cluck - the short, sharp sound made by a chicken
  • antonym: crow - the loud cry of a rooster
