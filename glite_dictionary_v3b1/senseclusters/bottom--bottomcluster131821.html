bottom

adjective
lowest position; situated at the bottom
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdəm /; / ˈbɑːt̬əm / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
situated at the lowest or deepest position; lowest in place, rank, or level

Examples

Bottom drawer
He kept the documents in the bottom drawer of his desk.

They found the old photo album at the bottom of the box.
Being in the bottom group motivated him to work harder.
She was the bottom student in her class that year, but she improved greatly later.
The bottom team will be relegated to a lower division next season.
