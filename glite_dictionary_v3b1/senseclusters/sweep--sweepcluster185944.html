sweep

noun
a broad, arc-shaped movement
Pronunciation: / swip / (AmE), / swiːp / (BrE)
a wide, curving movement or gesture, or a broad extent or range

Examples

A wide sweep
He gestured in a wide sweep to emphasize his point.
The painter finished the mural in a single broad sweep of the brush.
With a graceful sweep of her hand, she indicated the path ahead.
From the hilltop, we admired the sweep of the valley below.
The sweep of her influence extended across the entire organization.
The novel captures the sweep of twentieth-century history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arc - a curved shape or movement
  • synonym: expanse - a wide continuous area, especially of land or sea
