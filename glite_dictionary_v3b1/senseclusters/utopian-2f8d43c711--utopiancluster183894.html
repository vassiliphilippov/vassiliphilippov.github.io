Utopian

adjective
relating to a perfect or ideal society
6.8 2/m
Pronunciation: / juÀêÀàto ä.pi.…ôn / (AmE), / juÀêÀàt…ô ä.pi.…ôn / (BrE)
of or relating to an ideal or perfect society; resembling a utopia.

Examples

Utopian society
He outlined a Utopian vision for the future of education in his speech.
Many critics argue that her proposals are too Utopian to be practical.
The novel describes a Utopian society where everyone lives in harmony.

Synonyms

  • utopian
    7.9 2/m
    utopian describes idealistic, visionary ideas that often seem impractical and unrealistic, while Utopian describes things related to a perfect imagined society.
  • ideal
    4.2 20/m
    ideal means perfect or most suitable in general situations, while Utopian refers to an imagined perfect society and often sounds unrealistic.
