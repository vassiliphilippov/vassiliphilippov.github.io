unanticipated

adjective
not expected or predicted
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ænˈtɪs.əˌteɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ænˈtɪs.ɪˌteɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not expected or predicted; occurring without forewarning.

Examples

An unanticipated event
Due to unanticipated expenses, the company had to revise its annual budget.
Her resignation was so unanticipated that it took everyone by surprise.
The discovery of the ancient artifact was entirely unanticipated by the research team.
