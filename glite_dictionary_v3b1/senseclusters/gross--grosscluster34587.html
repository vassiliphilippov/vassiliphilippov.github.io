gross

adjective
visible to the naked eye
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊs / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs / (BrE)
(of a feature or structure) large enough to be visible to the naked eye, not microscopic.

Examples

Gross features
Most gross anatomical features are apparent without magnification.
The specimen showed gross irregularities that could be identified by sight alone.
This mineral deposit is gross, not requiring microscopic examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: microscopic - too small to be seen except under a microscope
