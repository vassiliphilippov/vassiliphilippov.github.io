being a chameleon

noun
adapting easily to different situations
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːɪŋ ə kəˈmiːliən / (AmE), / ˈbiːɪŋ ə kəˈmiːliən / (BrE)
the quality or act of adapting easily to different situations or environments, often by changing one's behavior or appearance to fit in

Examples

Being a chameleon in life
Being a chameleon made it easier for him to navigate various social circles.
In his career, being a chameleon allowed him to thrive in many different roles.
She prided herself on being a chameleon in the ever-changing world of fashion.
