righto

interjection
agreement or acknowledgment
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈraɪtəʊ / (BrE)
used to express agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment, similar to 'okay' or 'alright'

Examples

Righto, I agree
"Can you send me the report by noon?" "Righto, I'll get it done."
When the teacher asked if everyone understood, the class replied, "Righto!"
"We're leaving in five minutes." "Righto, I'll grab my coat."

Related Terms

  • synonym: alright - used to express agreement or confirmation
  • antonym: no - used to express disagreement or refusal
  • synonym: okay - used to show agreement or acceptance
