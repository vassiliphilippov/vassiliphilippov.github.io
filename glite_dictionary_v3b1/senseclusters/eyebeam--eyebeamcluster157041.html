eyebeam

noun
poetic glance or ray
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌbiːm / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌbiːm / (BrE)
a poetic or archaic term for a ray or glance emitted from the eye, often symbolizing vision or connection

Examples

A eyebeam of light
The poet described love as an eyebeam that connects two souls.
In the old tale, a single eyebeam was said to reveal the truth.
She felt the warmth of his eyebeam across the crowded room.
