copolymerize

verb
polymerize together to form a copolymer
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /koʊ.pə.ˈlɪm.ə.raɪz/ /; / /koʊ.poʊ.ˈlɪm.ə.raɪz/ (AmE), /kəʊ.pə.ˈlɪm.ə.raɪz/ (BrE)
(of two or more different monomers) to combine and form a copolymer through the process of polymerization.

Examples

To copolymerize monomers
Scientists discovered that the two chemicals can copolymerize under specific temperature conditions.
Some monomers will not readily copolymerize due to differences in their chemical reactivity.
The researchers aimed to copolymerize styrene with acrylonitrile to produce a stronger material.
Certain plastics are produced when chemists copolymerise styrene and butadiene.
Scientists managed to copolymerise the two substances to create a new material.
The company invested in technology to efficiently copolymerise multiple monomers in a single process.
