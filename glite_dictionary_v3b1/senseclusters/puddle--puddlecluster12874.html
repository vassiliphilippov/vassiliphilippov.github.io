puddle

verb
subject metal to puddling
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdəl / (AmE), / ˈpʌdəl / (BrE)
(in metallurgy) To subject iron or another metal to the process of puddling, or to form (metal) by puddling.

Examples

Puddle iron process
In the 19th century, blacksmiths often puddled iron to create wrought iron.
To reduce impurities, the worker must puddle the molten metal carefully.
Traditional foundries would puddle iron to improve its quality before casting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puddle (noun) - a small pool of liquid, especially of rainwater on the ground
