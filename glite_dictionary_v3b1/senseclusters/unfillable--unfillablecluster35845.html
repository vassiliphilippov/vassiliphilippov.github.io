unfillable

adjective
impossible to fill
6.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪləbəl / (BrE)
impossible to fill, satisfy, or occupy

Examples

An unfillable position

There was an unfillable gap in the team that no new hire could replace.
Some losses leave an unfillable emptiness in our lives.
