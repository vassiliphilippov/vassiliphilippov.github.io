sober

verb
make less drunk or more sensible
4.1 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
to become or make someone less drunk or more serious and realistic

Examples

To sober up quickly
Coffee helped sober him before his morning meeting.
She tried to sober her friend with some cold water after the party.
The news quickly sobered everyone in the room.
Her enthusiasm for the project soon sobered when she saw the amount of work involved.
The harsh criticism sobered him and made him reconsider his strategy.
We all sobered as we listened to the serious news.
