soursop

noun
tropical fruit with tart pulp
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊrˌsɑp / (AmE), / ˈsaʊəsɒp / (BrE)
a large, spiny tropical fruit with sour, juicy pulp, or the small tropical tree (Annona muricata) that bears this fruit
