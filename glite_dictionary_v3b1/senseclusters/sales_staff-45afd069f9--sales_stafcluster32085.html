sales staff

noun
employees responsible for sales in a business
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlz ˌstæf / (AmE), / ˈseɪlz ˌstɑːf / (BrE)
the employees of a company who are responsible for selling its products or services

Examples

The sales staff team
Our company's sales staff exceeded their quarterly targets this year.
She manages a large sales staff across several regional offices.
The sales staff received training on the latest product line.
