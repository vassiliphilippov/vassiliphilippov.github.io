drilled

adjective
trained by repeated practice
4.7
Pronunciation: / drɪld / (AmE), / drɪld / (BrE)
trained in a skill or activity by repeated practice or instruction, especially to achieve discipline or proficiency

Examples

Well drilled soldiers
New recruits must be thoroughly drilled in emergency procedures before deployment.
The coach was proud of his drilled players during the championship game.
Their drilled responses to questions impressed the visiting inspectors.
