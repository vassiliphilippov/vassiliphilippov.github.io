superstitiously

adverb
in a superstitious way
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈstɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈstɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows belief in or is influenced by superstition.

Examples

To superstitiously avoid ladders
He superstitiously avoided walking under any ladders on his way to work.
She always superstitiously wears her lucky necklace during exams.
The old woman superstitiously sprinkled salt over her shoulder after spilling it.
