housebroken

adjective
trained pet not to soil indoors
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌbroʊkən / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌbrəʊkən / (BrE)
(Of a pet) trained to urinate and defecate only outside the house or in a designated place inside.

Examples

Housebroken pet
It can be challenging to get a cat fully housebroken if it has lived outside.
Their dog was not housebroken, so there were frequent accidents in the living room.
We adopted a housebroken puppy from the animal shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: house-trained - (Of a pet) trained to urinate and defecate in a designated place; chiefly British equivalent
