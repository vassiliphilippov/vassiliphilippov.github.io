butter-bean plant

noun
plant that produces butter beans
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚ biːn plænt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə biːn plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bush bean plant, especially cultivated in the southern United States, that produces small, flat, edible seeds known as butter beans.

Examples

The butter-bean plant bush
Gardeners in the South often prefer butter-bean plants for their reliable yield.
My grandmother grows butter-bean plants every summer for their tender beans.
The fields were lined with rows of butter-bean plants waiting to be harvested.
