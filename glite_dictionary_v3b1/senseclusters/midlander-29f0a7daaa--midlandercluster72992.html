Midlander

adjective
from the Midlands
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌlændər / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌlændə / (BrE)
relating to the Midlands region or to people who live there, especially in England.

Examples

Midlander accent
The Midlander accent is distinct from those in the north or south of England.
She wore traditional Midlander clothing to the cultural event.
Many Midlander customs are celebrated during the annual festival.
