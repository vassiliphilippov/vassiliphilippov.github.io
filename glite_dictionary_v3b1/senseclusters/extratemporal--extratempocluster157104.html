extratemporal

adjective
outside temporal region
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the temporal region or temporal bone of the skull

Examples

Extratemporal mass
The surgeon identified an extratemporal mass that required further investigation.
Imaging revealed that the abnormality was extratemporal and did not affect the temporal bone.
Researchers studied extratemporal lesions to understand their impact on brain function.

Related Terms

  • antonym: temporal - relating to the temple or temporal bone of the skull
