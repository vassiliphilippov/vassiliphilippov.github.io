assortment

noun
act of classifying into categories
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔrtmənt / (AmE), / əˈsɔːtmənt / (BrE)
the action or process of arranging things into different classes or categories based on type

Examples

Careful assortment of items
Effective inventory management depends on the proper assortment of goods by category.
The careful assortment of materials made the recycling process much easier.
The museum's impressive assortment of artifacts was organized by era and region.
