shoddy workmanship

noun
poorly done or inferior-quality work
8.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɑː.dɪ ˈwɜːrk.mən.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈʃɒ.dɪ ˈwɜːk.mən.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
work that is poorly done or of inferior quality, especially due to carelessness or use of bad materials.

Examples

Shoddy workmanship complaints
Customers complained about shoddy workmanship after the repairs did not last a week.
Many accidents are caused by shoddy workmanship during construction.
The inspector noted several cases of shoddy workmanship in the building's structure.
