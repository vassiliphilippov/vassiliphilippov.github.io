sewage disposal plant

noun
facility for treating and disposing of sewage
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuɪdʒ dɪˈspoʊzəl plænt / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪdʒ dɪˈspəʊzəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a facility where sewage is treated and disposed of, typically by removing contaminants and processing the waste

Examples
