city room

noun
newspaper department for local news
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti ruːm / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ruːm / (BrE)
the section of a newspaper's editorial department responsible for editing and producing local news stories

Examples

The city room office
After the mayor's announcement, reporters hurried back to the city room to file their stories.
Budget cuts forced the newspaper to reduce the size of its city room staff.
The young journalist spent her afternoons in the city room learning from experienced editors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newsroom - a room where news is processed and edited in a newspaper, TV, or radio station
