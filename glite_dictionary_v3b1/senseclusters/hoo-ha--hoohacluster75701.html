hoo-ha

noun
commotion or noisy fuss
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhuˌhɑ / (AmE), / ˈhuːˌhɑː / (BrE)
a commotion, noisy fuss, or excitement, especially when it is unnecessary or about something unimportant

Examples

A hoo-ha over nothing
Let’s not make a big hoo-ha about it and just solve the problem quietly.
The new policy created a hoo-ha among students and teachers alike.
There was quite a hoo-ha in the office when the news broke.
After the hoo-ha died down, people quickly lost interest in the story.
He couldn't understand all the hoo-ha about the new smartphone release.
There was a huge hoo-ha when the celebrity arrived at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commotion - a state of confused and noisy disturbance
  • synonym: fuss - unnecessary excitement or activity
  • synonym: uproar - a loud and impassioned noise or disturbance
