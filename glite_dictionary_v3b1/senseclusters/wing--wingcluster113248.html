wing

noun
(British) vehicle wheel covering; fender
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋ / (BrE)
(British) The part of a motor vehicle that covers and protects the wheel and wheel arch from mud or water splashes; called a fender in American English.

Examples

A car's wing
In older British cars, the wings are often made of chrome.
She noticed some mud splashes on the wing after driving through puddles.
The car's front wing was dented after the minor accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fender - the panel that covers a vehicle's wheel (American term)
