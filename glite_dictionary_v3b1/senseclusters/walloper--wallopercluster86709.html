walloper

noun
a very hard hitter
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑl.ə.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɒl.ə.pə / (BrE)
a person who hits with great force or strength, especially in sports or fighting

Examples

A walloper punch
The boxer was known as a walloper in the ring for his powerful punches.
The new player is a walloper and can change the game's momentum with one swing.
You don't want to mess with him; he's a real walloper on the field.

  • synonym: slugger - a person who hits hard, especially in baseball or boxing
