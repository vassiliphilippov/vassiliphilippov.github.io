ear wrap

noun
jewelry worn around the ear rim
Pronunciation: / ɪr ræp / (AmE), / ɪə ræp / (BrE)
A decorative piece of jewelry that wraps around the rim of the ear and is worn without requiring a piercing.

Examples

A ear wrap jewelry
She chose a sparkling ear wrap to complement her evening dress.
The artist crafted a silver ear wrap that curled along the edge of the ear.
Unlike traditional studs, an ear wrap makes a bold statement without piercing the skin.
