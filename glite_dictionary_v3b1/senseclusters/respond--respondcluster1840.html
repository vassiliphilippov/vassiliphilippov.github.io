respond

reply or answer verbally

to reply or react to something that is said or done

Examples

To respond to a question
Please raise your hand if you wish to respond during the discussion.
She paused before she responded to the reporter's question.
The teacher asked a question, and several students responded with thoughtful answers.
How do you usually respond to criticism at work?
She didn't respond to my email until the next morning.
The immune system will respond to the presence of bacteria.

Synonyms

  • answer
    1.0 154/m
    answer gives a direct reply to a question or request, while respond can mean any reply or action to something said or done.
  • reply
    1.0 56/m
    reply is usually a spoken or written answer to a message, while respond also covers actions or feelings as a reaction.
  • return
    1.9 15/m
    return often means a reply in formal or old-fashioned speech, while respond is common and used in many modern situations.
  • react
    2.8 42/m
    react means show a feeling or physical action because of something, while respond can mean replying by words or by action.

  • synonym: react - to behave in a particular way in response to something
  • synonym: reply - to answer, especially in speech or writing
