embrittle

verb
make or become brittle
9.5 7.3
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈbrɪt.əl/ /; / /em.ˈbrɪt.əl/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈbrɪt.əl/ /; / /em.ˈbrɪt.əl/ (BrE)
to make or become brittle and likely to break easily

Examples

To embrittle metal
Exposing the metal to high temperatures can embrittle it, making it prone to cracking.
Repeated washing with harsh chemicals may embrittle these plastic components over time.
The process can embrittle glass, reducing its durability in everyday use.
