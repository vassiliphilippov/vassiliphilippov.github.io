wayfinding

noun
route navigation process
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈweɪ.faɪn.dɪŋ/ /; / /weɪ.ˈfaɪn.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈweɪ.faɪn.dɪŋ/ /; / /weɪ.ˈfaɪn.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process or system of helping people find their way in a place, especially using signs, maps, or other guides

Examples

Good lighting and clear maps help with wayfinding in large hospitals.
