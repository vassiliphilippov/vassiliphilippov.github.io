Examples

A revocable trust
A revocable trust can be changed or cancelled by its creator at any time.
The contract was signed under revocable terms, allowing either party to withdraw with notice.
They issued a revocable permit for the new construction project.

His license was revocable if he failed to meet certain conditions.

Synonyms

  • rescindable
    9.1 0.01/m
    rescindable is a legal term for something annulled by official power, while revocable is the general word for things that can be cancelled.
  • recallable
    5.1 0.00/m
    recallable is used for products, messages, or people that are called back, while revocable describes permissions or agreements that can be cancelled.
