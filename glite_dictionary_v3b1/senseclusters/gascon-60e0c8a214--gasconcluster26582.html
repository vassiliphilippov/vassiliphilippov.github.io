Gascon

noun
Occitan dialect
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæskən / (AmE), / ˈɡæskən / (BrE)
a variety of the Occitan language spoken in the Gascony region of southwestern France

Examples

Gascon language
She learned to speak Gascon while living in a small village in southwestern France.
Many traditional songs in the region are sung in Gascon rather than standard French.
Linguists are working to preserve the Gascon dialect for future generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: French - the official language of France
  • synonym: Occitan - a Romance language spoken in southern France and nearby regions
