bumpee

noun
person denied boarding
9.6
Pronunciation: / bʌmˈpiː / (AmE), / bʌmˈpiː / (BrE)
a person who is denied boarding or displaced from a flight, event, or reservation due to overbooking or allocation changes

Examples

Flight bumpee
The airline offered compensation to every bumpee who missed their original flight.
As a bumpee, he had to wait several hours for the next available seat.
Event organizers apologized to each bumpee affected by the last-minute changes.
