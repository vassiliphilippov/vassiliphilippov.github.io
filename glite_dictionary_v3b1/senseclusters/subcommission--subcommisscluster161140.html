subcommission

noun
smaller group in commission
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbkəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌbkəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a smaller group within a commission formed to focus on specific tasks or issues

Examples

The subcommission meeting
The subcommission was tasked with drafting the new environmental guidelines.
Each subcommission reports its findings to the main commission at the end of the month.
A subcommission on education reform presented its recommendations to the board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
