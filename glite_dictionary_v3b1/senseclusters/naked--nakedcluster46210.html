naked

adjective
undisguised or direct (emotion, truth, etc.)
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkəd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
(of feelings, truth, or qualities) not disguised, concealed, or moderated; expressed in a pure, direct, or unembellished manner.

Examples

Naked truth
His naked fear was clear on his face during the interview.
In her speech, she expressed naked honesty about her struggles.
The report revealed the naked truth about the company's failures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or disguised; openly expressed
  • antonym: concealed - kept hidden or disguised
