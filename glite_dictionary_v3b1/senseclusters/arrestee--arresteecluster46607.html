arrestee

noun
person under arrest
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˌrɛsˈti / (AmE), / əˌrɛsˈtiː / (BrE)

Lawyers were assigned to represent each arrestee in court.
After the protest, several arrestees were released without charges.
