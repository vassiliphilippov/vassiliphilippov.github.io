intercellular

adjective
between cells
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɛljələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛljʊlə / (BrE)
existing or occurring between cells, especially in tissues of plants or animals.

Examples

Intercellular communication
Researchers studied the intercellular communication in cancer cells.
Some bacteria move by swimming through intercellular fluids in tissues.
The plant's nutrients are transported through intercellular spaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracellular - existing or occurring within a cell
