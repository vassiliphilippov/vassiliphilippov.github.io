hole

noun
one playing period on a golf course
2.4
Pronunciation: / hoʊl / (AmE), / həʊl / (BrE)
A single unit of play on a golf course, starting from the tee and ending at the green.

Examples

A golf hole
Each hole on this course offers unique challenges for golfers.
She finished the last hole with an impressive birdie.
They played nine holes before the rain started.
