Gnostic

adjective
relating to Gnosticism or its adherents
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːstɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɒstɪk / (BrE)
relating to Gnosticism or its adherents; associated with the beliefs, practices, or writings of the Gnostics

Examples

Gnostic texts

The museum's exhibit includes several Gnostic manuscripts from the second century.
The scholar devoted years to studying Gnostic texts and traditions.
