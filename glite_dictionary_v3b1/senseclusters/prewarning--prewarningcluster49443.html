prewarning

noun
advance alert
Pronunciation: / priːˈwɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / priːˈwɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
an advance notice or alert given before a possible danger or event occurs

Examples

A prewarning message
The weather service issued a prewarning about the approaching storm to help people prepare.
Drivers received a prewarning on their phones before the road was closed due to flooding.
A loud alarm acts as a prewarning before the machinery starts operating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alert - a signal or message to warn of danger
  • synonym: warning - a statement or sign that something bad may happen
