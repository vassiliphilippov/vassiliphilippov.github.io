harmonica

noun
small rectangular wind musical instrument
2.9 1.62/m
Pronunciation: / h…ëÀêrÀàm…ëÀê.n…™.k…ô / (AmE), / h…ëÀêÀàm…ín.…™.k…ô / (BrE)
a small, rectangular musical instrument with a row of metal reeds, played by blowing or drawing air through holes along one side

Examples

A harmonica player
He took out his harmonica and played a blues tune by the campfire.
Learning to play the harmonica can be both fun and challenging.
The band's music featured a lively harmonica solo.
