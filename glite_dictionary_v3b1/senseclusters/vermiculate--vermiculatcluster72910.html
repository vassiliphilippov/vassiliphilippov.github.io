vermiculate

adjective
decorated with wormlike markings
Pronunciation: / vərˈmɪkjələt / (AmE), / vəˈmɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
decorated or marked with wormlike, winding, or sinuous lines or tracery, especially in stone or other material

Examples

Vermiculate stonework
The old building's facade featured vermiculate stonework that resembled winding trails.
The sculptor created a vermiculate pattern on the marble surface, adding a sense of movement.
We admired the artist's vermiculate designs, which meandered across the entire canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vermicular - having a shape or appearance like that of a worm
