bourride

noun
Provençal fish stew
Pronunciation: / buːˈriːd / (AmE), / buːˈriːd / (BrE)
a traditional Provençal fish stew made with garlic, olive oil, and often served with aioli

Examples

Traditional bourride stew
The restaurant is famous for its flavorful bourride served with crusty bread.
She learned to make bourride while traveling in the south of France.
A bowl of hot bourride is perfect for a chilly evening by the sea.
