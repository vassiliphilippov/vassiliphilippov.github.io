self-conceited

adjective
excessively self-important or vain
7.7 0.01/m

Having or showing an excessively high opinion of one's own worth or ability; vain and egotistical.

Examples

A self-conceited person
He became so self-conceited after his promotion that he ignored his old friends.
Her self-conceited remarks about her achievements made everyone uncomfortable.
The self-conceited attitude of the celebrity alienated many of her fans.
