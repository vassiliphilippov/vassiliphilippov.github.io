pulsing

noun
short electrical wave in electronics
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsɪŋ / (BrE)
(electronics) a sudden transient wave or signal in an electrical circuit, or a series of such signals.

Examples

Electronic pulsings series
A series of pulsings made the electronic circuit behave unpredictably.
Radio interference was traced back to a pulsing created by a nearby device.
The engineer observed a pulsing on the screen, indicating a problem with the power supply.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulse - a single vibration or short burst of energy in a medium, especially electricity
