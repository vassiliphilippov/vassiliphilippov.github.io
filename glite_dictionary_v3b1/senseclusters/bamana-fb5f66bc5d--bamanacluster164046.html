Bamana

proper noun
West African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / bəˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / bəˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group mainly found in Mali, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Bamana people language
The Bamana have a rich tradition of sculpture and mask-making.
Many people in Mali speak the Bamana language in daily life.
Researchers studied the agricultural practices of the Bamana community.
