cornstalk

noun
the stem of a corn plant
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌstɑk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌstɔːk / (BrE)
the main stem or stalk of a corn plant, supporting the leaves and ear(s)

Examples

Dry cornstalks
After the harvest, only dry cornstalks remained standing in the empty field.
She used a dried cornstalk as a makeshift walking stick.
The wind rustled through the cornstalk as the sun set over the fields.
