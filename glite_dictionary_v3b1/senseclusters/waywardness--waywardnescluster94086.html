waywardness

noun
unpredictable behavior
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪwərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈweɪwədnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being difficult to control, unpredictable, or disobedient

Examples

The waywardness of puppy
The waywardness of the puppy made training a real challenge for its owner.
His waywardness often led him into trouble at school and at home.
Despite her waywardness, her friends admired her independent spirit.
The teacher was frustrated by the student's constant waywardness in class.
Her waywardness made it difficult for her to fit in with strict social groups.
Despite his parents' efforts, his waywardness only seemed to increase as he grew older.
