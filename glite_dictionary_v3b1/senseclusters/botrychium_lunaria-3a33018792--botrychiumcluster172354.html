Botrychium lunaria

noun
species of small fern (moonwort)
Pronunciation: / boʊˈtrɪkiəm luːˈnɛəriə / (AmE), / bəʊˈtrɪkiəm luːˈnɑːriə / (BrE)
A small fern known as moonwort, found in America, Eurasia, and Australia, with rounded leaflets resembling a crescent moon.
