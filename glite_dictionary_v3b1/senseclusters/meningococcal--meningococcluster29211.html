meningococcal

adjective
related to meningitis bacteria
Pronunciation: / məˌnɪndʒəˈkɑkəl / (AmE), / məˌnɪndʒəˈkɒkəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the bacterium Neisseria meningitidis, which can cause meningitis and related diseases

Examples

Meningococcal vaccine
The meningococcal vaccine is recommended for teenagers before starting college.
Doctors quickly identified the infection as meningococcal and began treatment.
A meningococcal outbreak in the city led to a public health alert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meningitis - inflammation of the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
