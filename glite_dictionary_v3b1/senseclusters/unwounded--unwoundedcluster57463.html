unwounded

adjective
not wounded; uninjured
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwuːndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwuːndɪd / (BrE)
Not wounded; not having received any physical injury.

Examples

Completely unwounded
Despite the chaos, he emerged from the building completely unwounded.
Several birds flew away unwounded after the predator left.
The rescuers found her conscious and unwounded among the wreckage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uninjured - not injured; unharmed
  • antonym: wounded - having received an injury or wound
