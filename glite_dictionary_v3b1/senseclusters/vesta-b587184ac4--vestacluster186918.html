Vesta

proper noun
the brightest asteroid; fourth discovered
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstə / (AmE), / ˈvɛstə / (BrE)
the brightest asteroid in the solar system, and the fourth to be discovered, located in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter
