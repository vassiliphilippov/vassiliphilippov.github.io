car wheel

noun
wheel used on an automobile
Pronunciation: / kɑr wiːl / (AmE), / kɑː wɪəl / (BrE)
A wheel fitted with a tire, rim, and often a hubcap, designed to be part of a car and enable its movement.

Examples

A car wheel with tire
A flat car wheel can leave you stranded on the side of the road.
He noticed that the front car wheel was missing its hubcap.
The mechanic replaced the damaged car wheel after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheel - a circular object that revolves on an axle and is fixed below a vehicle or other object to enable it to move easily over the ground
