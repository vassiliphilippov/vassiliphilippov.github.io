close corporation

noun
company with few owners; shares not publicly traded

A corporation that is owned by a small number of shareholders and whose shares are not available to the public on the open market.

Examples

A close corporation
A close corporation allows owners to maintain privacy over business affairs.
After selling his startup, Tony registered it as a close corporation to keep control among family members.
Many small businesses begin as a close corporation to avoid public reporting requirements.

Synonyms
