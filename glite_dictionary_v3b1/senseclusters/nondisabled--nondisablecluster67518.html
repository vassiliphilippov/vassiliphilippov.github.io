nondisabled

adjective
without disability
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn dɪsˈeɪbəld / (AmE), / ˌnɒn dɪsˈeɪbəld / (BrE)

Examples

Nondisabled children

The event was made accessible to nondisabled and disabled participants alike.
