gall bladder

noun
organ that stores bile
4.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /gɔːl ˈblæd.ər/ (AmE), /gɔːl ˈblæd.ə/ (BrE)
a small organ beneath the liver in which bile is stored before being released into the intestine

Examples

The gall bladder stores bile
Bile is stored in the gall bladder until food enters the small intestine.
Gallstones can form inside the gall bladder, causing pain and other digestive problems.
The surgeon removed the patient's gall bladder due to severe inflammation.
