flowering hazel

noun
ornamental shrub with early yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚɪŋ ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
an Asiatic deciduous shrub cultivated for its drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom before the leaves emerge
