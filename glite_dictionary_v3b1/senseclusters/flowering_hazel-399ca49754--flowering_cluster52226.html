flowering hazel

noun
ornamental shrub with early yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚɪŋ ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
an Asiatic deciduous shrub cultivated for its drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom before the leaves emerge

Examples

Flowering hazel shrub
Horticulturists recommend flowering hazel as an early source of color before most trees leaf out.
The flowering hazel brightens our garden each spring with its delicate yellow blooms.
We planted a row of flowering hazels along the walkway for their beautiful late-winter blossoms.
