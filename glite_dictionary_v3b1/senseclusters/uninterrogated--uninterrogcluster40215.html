uninterrogated

adjective
not examined
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛrəˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not questioned, examined, or investigated; left without scrutiny or challenge

Examples

Claims uninterrogated
Many of the report's claims remained uninterrogated by the committee.
The theory was accepted for years, but its core ideas were largely uninterrogated.
Leaving assumptions uninterrogated can lead to serious errors in research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unchecked - not examined or controlled
  • synonym: unquestioned - not doubted or challenged
