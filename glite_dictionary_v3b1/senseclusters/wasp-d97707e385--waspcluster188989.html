WASP

noun
White Anglo-Saxon Protestant; member of a dominant US group
8.2
Pronunciation: / wɑsp / (AmE), / wɒsp / (BrE)
a white person of Anglo-Saxon descent who is a member of a Protestant denomination, especially one considered part of the American social elite

Examples

American WASPs
Many American elites were historically WASPs, influencing politics and society.
She felt like an outsider among the old-money WASP families at her university.
The political candidate was often described as a classic WASP from New England.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Anglo-Saxon - a person of British descent, especially from England
  • synonym: Protestant - a member or follower of any of the Western Christian churches that are separate from the Roman Catholic Church
