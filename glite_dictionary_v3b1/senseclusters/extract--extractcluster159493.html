extract

noun
a substance obtained by soaking or steeping
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrækt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrækt / (BrE)
a substance obtained by soaking or steeping a plant or other material, typically in water, to draw out its active ingredients

Examples

Vanilla extract
Add a teaspoon of extract to the batter for extra flavor.
She bought vanilla extract for her baking recipes.
The herbal extract is believed to have soothing properties.
