Leontopodium alpinum

noun
alpine plant known as edelweiss
Pronunciation: / liˌɑːntəˈpoʊdiəm ælˈpaɪnəm / (AmE), / liˌɒntəˈpəʊdiəm ælˈpiːnəm / (BrE)
A small alpine perennial plant native to Europe, with woolly white leaves and star-shaped flower heads, commonly known as edelweiss.
