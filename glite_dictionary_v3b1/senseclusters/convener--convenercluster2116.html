convener

noun
person who organizes meetings
5.7 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàvin…ôr / (AmE), / k…ônÀàviÀên…ô / (BrE)
the person responsible for calling people together for meetings, especially of a committee or group

Examples

The convener calls meetings
As convener, it is your responsibility to send out the agenda before each meeting.
She served as the convener of the planning committee for several years.
The convener called everyone to order and began the discussion promptly at nine.

Synonyms

  • convocant
    9.9 0.06/m
    convocant is a person who formally summons others to assemble for a meeting, while convener is the one who calls and runs committee meetings.
  • organizer
    2.8 11/m
    organizer arranges and coordinates people for events and activities, while convener mainly calls and leads formal committee meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairperson - person who presides over a meeting
