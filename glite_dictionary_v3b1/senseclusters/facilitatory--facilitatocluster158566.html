facilitatory

adjective
promoting or aiding neural activity
3.7 8.0
Pronunciation: / fəˈsɪl.ɪ.təˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / fəˈsɪl.ɪ.təˌtɔː.ri / (BrE)
helping to promote or increase a process, especially neural activity in the nervous system.

Examples

Facilitatory role in learning
Certain neurotransmitters are known to play a facilitatory role in cognitive functions such as learning and memory.
Researchers observed that the facilitatory influence was more pronounced in the treated group.
The drug has a facilitatory effect on neural transmission, increasing the rate of synaptic firing.
