for tea

preposition
to have tea as a meal or event
2.0 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / f…ôr ti / (AmE), / f…ô ÀàtiÀê / (BrE)
to take part in tea as a light meal or social occasion, especially in the afternoon or early evening

Examples

Come for tea

Synonyms

  • afternoon tea
    1.7 0.47/m

  • tea party
    1.4 0.51/m
