complaint

noun
medical condition or disorder
6.1
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
a medical condition, disorder, or illness, especially one that persists or recurs; a cause for expressing discomfort or distress

Examples

A stomach complaint
Arthritis is a common complaint among older adults.
He went to the clinic with a stomach complaint that had persisted for weeks.
The doctor asked if I had any complaint other than the cough.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disease - a disorder of structure or function in a living thing
  • synonym: illness - an unhealthy condition of body or mind
