deathlessness

noun
eternal existence
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛθləsnɪs /; / ˈdɛθləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of never dying or being immortal, including lasting fame or eternal existence in memory

Examples

Deathlessness in memory
The artist hoped that her work would grant her deathlessness in the eyes of future generations.
Legends and stories can give a sense of deathlessness to historical figures.
Many people dream of achieving deathlessness through their accomplishments or creations.
Many myths describe the deathlessness of ancient gods and heroes.
The philosopher wrote about the human desire for deathlessness and eternal life.
Some believe that scientific advances may one day lead to deathlessness for humans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immortality - the state of living forever or never being forgotten
  • antonym: mortality - the state of being subject to death
  • antonym: oblivion - the state of being forgotten or unknown
