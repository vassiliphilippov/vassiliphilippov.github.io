arborescent

adjective
tree-like in form or branching
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbəˈrɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌɑːbəˈrɛsənt / (BrE)
having a form or structure resembling that of a tree, especially in branching pattern.

Examples

Arborescent structure
Certain fungi develop arborescent structures to maximize their surface area.
The scientist described the mineral vein as having an arborescent appearance due to its branch-like patterns.
The sea was dotted with arborescent coral formations, each rising like a miniature forest.
