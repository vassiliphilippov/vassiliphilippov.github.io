intermodule

adjective
between modules
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɒdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɒdjuːl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between modules, especially within a system or structure

Examples

Intermodule connection
The intermodule cables must be properly connected to ensure data flows smoothly.
Engineers tested the intermodule communication to prevent system failures.
A fault in the intermodule interface caused the device to malfunction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intramodule - occurring within a single module
