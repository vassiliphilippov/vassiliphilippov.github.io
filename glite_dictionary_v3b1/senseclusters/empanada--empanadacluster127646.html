empanada

noun
stuffed pastry
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmpəˈnɑdə / (AmE), / ˌɛmpəˈnɑːdə / (BrE)
a baked or fried pastry filled with meat, cheese, vegetables, or other ingredients, popular in Spanish-speaking and Latin American cuisine

Examples

A empanada with meat
She ordered a chicken empanada and a salad for her lunch.
The bakery sells fresh empanadas every morning with a variety of fillings.
He learned how to make a traditional beef empanada from his grandmother.
We ordered a plate of beef empanadas to share as an appetizer.
Her grandmother taught her how to make traditional chicken empanadas from scratch.
At the festival, vendors sold sweet empanadas filled with fruit and cinnamon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dumpling - a small piece of dough filled with meat or vegetables and cooked
  • synonym: pastie - a baked pastry filled with meat and vegetables, especially in British cuisine
  • synonym: turnover - a baked or fried pastry with a sweet or savory filling
