West Indian satinwood

noun
tree with smooth, lustrous wood from the West Indies
Pronunciation: / wɛst ˈɪndiən ˈsætn̩ˌwʊd / (AmE), / wɛst ˈɪndiən ˈsætnˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tree native to the West Indies, known for its smooth, lustrous, and slightly oily wood, often used in fine woodworking

Examples

West Indian satinwood tree
Furniture makers prize West Indian satinwood for its smooth texture and durability.
She ordered a custom table made of West Indian satinwood for her dining room.
The cabinet was crafted from rare West Indian satinwood, giving it a beautiful sheen.
