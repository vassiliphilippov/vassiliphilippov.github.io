West Indian satinwood

noun
tree with smooth, lustrous wood from the West Indies
4.7 8.3
Pronunciation: /west ˈɪn.diː.ən ˈsæt.ən.wʊd/ (AmE), /west ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈsæt.ɪn.wʊd/ (BrE)
