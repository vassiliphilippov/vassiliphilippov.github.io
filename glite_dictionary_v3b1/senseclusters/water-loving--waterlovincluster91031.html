water-loving

adjective
thriving with lots of water
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)
(especially of plants) thriving in or requiring a large amount of water.

Examples

Water-loving plants
Ferns are typically considered water-loving species and thrive in damp environments.
Her balcony is filled with water-loving flowers that flourish after heavy rain.
The garden features many water-loving plants that require constant moisture.
