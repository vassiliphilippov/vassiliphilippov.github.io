snaggy

adjective
full of difficulties
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡi / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡi / (BrE)
full of small problems, delays, or difficulties; likely to encounter snags.

Examples

Snaggy project
The project became snaggy when unexpected technical issues started to appear.
Their journey was snaggy due to frequent delays and roadblocks along the way.
We found the process surprisingly snaggy despite our careful planning.
