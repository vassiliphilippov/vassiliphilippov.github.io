bloodletting

noun
indiscriminate or large-scale killing
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌlɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌlɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
Indiscriminate or large-scale killing or slaughter, especially in a violent or political context.

Examples

Political bloodletting
Historians still debate the causes behind the sudden bloodletting during the uprising.
Rival factions called for an end to the cycle of bloodletting in the region.
The bloodletting that followed the coup shocked the entire nation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloodbath - an event involving the violent killing of many people
  • synonym: bloodshed - the killing or wounding of people, typically in large numbers
  • synonym: massacre - the killing of many people, especially those who cannot defend themselves
