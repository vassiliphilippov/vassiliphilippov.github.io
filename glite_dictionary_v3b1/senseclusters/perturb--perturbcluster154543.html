perturb

verb
to disturb or throw into confusion
7.7 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàt…ùÀêb / (AmE), / p…ôÀàt…úÀêb / (BrE)
to cause something or someone to be greatly disturbed, confused, or disordered

Examples

To perturb the balance
International events can perturb the balance of power in unexpected ways.
The loud noise outside perturbed her concentration, making it hard to finish her work.
The sudden announcement seemed to perturb even the most experienced staff members.
