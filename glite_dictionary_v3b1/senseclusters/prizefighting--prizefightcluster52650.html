prizefighting

noun
boxing for money
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪzˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪzˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport in which people fight each other, usually with boxing, for money or prizes

Examples

Prizefighting matches

The history of prizefighting is filled with legendary boxers and famous matches.
Many people consider prizefighting both a sport and a form of entertainment.
