Natrix natrix

noun
a harmless European grass snake
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.trɪks ˈneɪ.trɪks / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.trɪks ˈneɪ.trɪks / (BrE)
a harmless European snake, also known as the grass snake, distinguished by a bright yellow collar and commonly found in England

Examples

The Natrix natrix snake
Gardeners in rural England may spot a Natrix natrix basking in the sun.
The Natrix natrix is often seen swimming in ponds and ditches during the summer.
Unlike adders, Natrix natrix poses no threat to humans and is protected in many regions.
