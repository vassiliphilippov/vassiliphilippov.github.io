blitzed

adjective
very drunk or tired
Pronunciation: / blɪtst / (AmE), / blɪtst / (BrE)
very drunk or extremely tired, especially in informal contexts

Examples

Blitzed and drunk
He was so blitzed after the concert that he could barely walk straight.
After working a double shift, she felt completely blitzed and went straight to bed.
They got blitzed at the bar and told hilarious stories all night.
