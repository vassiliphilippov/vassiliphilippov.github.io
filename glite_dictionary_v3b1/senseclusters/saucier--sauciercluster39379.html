saucier

noun
sauce chef
8.1
Pronunciation: / soʊˈsjeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊsieɪ / (BrE)
a chef in a professional kitchen who specializes in preparing sauces

Examples

A saucier chef
The saucier prepared a rich béarnaise for the steak.
In a large kitchen, the saucier is responsible for all the sauces and gravies.
She trained for years to become the head saucier at the famous restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chef - a professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel
  • antonym: pastry chef - a chef who specializes in making pastries and desserts
