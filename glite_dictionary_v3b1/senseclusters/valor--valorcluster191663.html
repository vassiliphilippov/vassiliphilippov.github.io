valor

noun
heroic courage in battle
Pronunciation: / ˈvælər / (AmE), / ˈvælə / (BrE)
great courage, especially in the face of danger in battle; bravery regarded as a heroic quality

Stories of their valour have inspired generations.
The knight was renowned for his valour on the battlefield.
