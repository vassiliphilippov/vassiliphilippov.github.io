Chamaecytisus

noun
genus of flowering trees or subshrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪmiːsaɪˈtaɪsəs /; / ˌkeɪmiːˌsaɪˈtaɪsəs / (AmE), / ˌkamɪsɪˈtɪsəs /; / ˌkamiːˌsɪˈtaɪsəs / (BrE)
