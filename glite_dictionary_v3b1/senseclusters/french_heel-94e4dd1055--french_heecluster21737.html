French heel

noun
a high, narrow women's shoe heel
7.5 5.9
Pronunciation: /frentʃ hiːl/ (AmE), /frentʃ hiːl/ (BrE)
a fairly high, narrow heel on women's shoes, often slightly curved inward at the back

Examples

A French heel shoe
Many ballet-inspired shoes feature a slightly curved French heel for a graceful look.
She admired the elegant French heel on her new evening shoes.
The shop specializes in vintage pumps with a classic French heel.
