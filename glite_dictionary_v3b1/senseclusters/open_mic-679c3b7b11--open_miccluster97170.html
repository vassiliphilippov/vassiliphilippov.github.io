open mic

noun
public performance event for anyone
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən maɪk / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən maɪk / (BrE)
an event in which anyone can perform, usually music, poetry, or comedy, often taking turns on stage

Examples

Open mic night
He gained confidence as a musician by performing at open mic events.
She read her poetry at the local open mic last Friday night.
The comedy club hosts an open mic every Wednesday for aspiring comedians.

Related Terms

  • synonym: talent show - an event where people perform to show their talents
