unassimilated

adjective
not integrated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈsɪməˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈsɪmɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not fully integrated or adapted to a group, culture, or environment

Examples

Unassimilated immigrants
Many unassimilated immigrants struggle to feel at home in a new country.
The unassimilated traditions of the community remained distinct for generations.
He felt unassimilated at his new school, unsure how to fit in with his classmates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alienated - feeling isolated or estranged from a group
  • antonym: assimilated - fully integrated or adapted to a group or culture
