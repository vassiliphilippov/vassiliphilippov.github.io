genus Gloriosa

noun
genus of flowering plants; includes glory lily
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡloʊˈriː.oʊ.sə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡləʊˈrɪə.sə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants, sometimes placed in the family Colchicaceae, containing the species known as the glory lily.
