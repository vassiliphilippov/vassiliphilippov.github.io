EFL

noun
English language teaching
3.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåiÀê …õf Àà…õl / (AmE), / ÀåiÀê …õf Àà…õl / (BrE)
the teaching or study of English by people whose first language is not English, especially in a non-English-speaking country

Examples

EFL classes abroad
Many universities offer EFL programs for international students.
She hopes to become an EFL teacher and work abroad.
The school specializes in intensive EFL courses for business professionals.

Synonyms

  • ELT
    5.3 0.01/m
    ELT covers the general field of teaching English to non-native speakers, while EFL focuses on teaching in non-English-speaking countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ESL - English as a Second Language, teaching English to non-native speakers in an English-speaking country
