papyrologist

noun
scholar of papyrus texts
Pronunciation: / pəˌpaɪˈrɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / pəˌpɪˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies ancient manuscripts written on papyrus

Examples

The papyrologist studies papyrus
The papyrologist spent years deciphering the ancient Egyptian texts found in the tomb.
As a leading papyrologist, she was invited to lecture at several international conferences.
A team led by a skilled papyrologist uncovered new insights into daily life in ancient Greece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archaeologist - a person who studies human history through excavation and artifacts
  • synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
