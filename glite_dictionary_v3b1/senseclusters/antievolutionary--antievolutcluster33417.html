antievolutionary

adjective
opposed to evolution
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌiːvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting the theory of evolution

Examples

Antievolutionary beliefs
The politician's antievolutionary stance drew criticism from the scientific community.
Some textbooks were removed due to their antievolutionary content.
The debate featured several antievolutionary arguments challenging mainstream science.

Related Terms
