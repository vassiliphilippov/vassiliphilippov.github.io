Hyoscyamus niger

noun
poisonous herb known as henbane
Pronunciation: / haɪˌɑːsiˈæməs ˈnaɪɡər / (AmE), / haɪˌɒsɪˈeɪməs ˈnaɪɡə / (BrE)
A poisonous Old World herb, also known as henbane, with sticky, hairy leaves and yellow-brown flowers, producing hyoscyamine and scopolamine.
