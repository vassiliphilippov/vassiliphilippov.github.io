RPS

noun
renewable energy policy
8.4
Pronunciation: /ɑːr piː es/ (AmE), /ɑː piː es/ (BrE)
an abbreviation for Renewable Portfolio Standard, a government policy requiring a certain percentage of electricity to come from renewable energy sources

Examples

State RPS policy
The state introduced a strict RPS to encourage investment in solar and wind power.
Many utilities must meet their RPS targets by purchasing renewable energy credits.
The new law increased the RPS requirement to 50 percent by 2030.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Renewable Portfolio Standard - a regulation requiring utilities to use more renewable energy
  • synonym: feed-in tariff - a policy that pays producers for renewable energy supplied to the grid
