asocial

adjective
hostile to social norms or interaction
5.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / əˈsəʊʃl / (BrE)
Not interested in or actively hostile to social interaction or norms; disruptive or disregarding of normal social standards.

Examples

Asocial behavior
Children sometimes display asocial actions when they feel excluded or threatened.
His asocial tendencies made it hard for him to maintain friendships at work.
The judge noted that the defendant's asocial behavior posed a risk to the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antisocial - actively opposed to social order or the interests of society
