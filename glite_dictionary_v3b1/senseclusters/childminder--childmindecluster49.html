childminder

noun
person caring for children
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪldˌmaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldˌmaɪndə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to look after children, usually in their own home and often while the parents are at work

Examples

The childminder cares
The childminder arrived early to prepare breakfast for the children before school.
We hired a childminder to look after our son while we are at work.
A good childminder creates a safe and fun environment for kids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysitter - a person who looks after children for a short time, usually in the child's home
  • synonym: nanny - a person employed to care for children in the employer's home
