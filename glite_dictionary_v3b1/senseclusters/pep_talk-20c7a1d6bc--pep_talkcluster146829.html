pep talk

noun
motivational talk to boost morale
Pronunciation: / pɛp tɑk / (AmE), / pɛp tɔːk / (BrE)
a speech or conversation meant to encourage or motivate someone or a group

Examples

A pep talk before game
Before the game, the coach gave the team a pep talk to boost their confidence.
I needed a pep talk from my manager before making the big presentation.
She always knows how to give a great pep talk when we are feeling down.
After hearing the manager's pep talk, the team felt ready to take on their competitors.
Sometimes a simple pep talk from a friend can make a huge difference in your mood.
The teacher gave her students a quick pep talk before the final exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discourage - to cause someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm
  • synonym: motivation - the reason or reasons one has for acting or behaving in a particular way
  • synonym: motivational speech - a speech intended to inspire or encourage an audience
