loneliness

noun
condition of feeling alone and isolated
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊnli.nɪs /; / ˈləʊn.li.nəs / (BrE)
the state of being alone and feeling sad or isolated because of it

Examples

Deep loneliness
He experienced profound loneliness after moving to a new city.
Many elderly people struggle with loneliness during the holidays.
The novel explores themes of loneliness and human connection.
After moving to a new city, she struggled with a deep sense of loneliness.
For many elderly people, loneliness can be as debilitating as a physical illness.
The sudden loneliness following his friend's departure was hard to bear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: companionship - the state of being with someone; company
  • antonym: company - the state of being with others
  • synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, especially when this is peaceful or pleasant
