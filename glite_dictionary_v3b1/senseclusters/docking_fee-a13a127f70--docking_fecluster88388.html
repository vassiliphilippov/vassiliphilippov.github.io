docking fee

noun
charge for docking a vessel
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑkɪŋ fi / (AmE), / ˈdɒkɪŋ fiː / (BrE)
a charge paid for the use of a dock by a vessel

Examples

The docking fee charge
The docking fee varies depending on the size of your vessel and the location of the dock.
The marina charges a docking fee for every boat that stays overnight.
When we budgeted for our sailing trip, we forgot to include the docking fee at each port.
