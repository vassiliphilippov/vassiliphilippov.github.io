prepackaging

noun
advance preparation
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈpækɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpækɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of preparing and presenting something in advance to make it more attractive or acceptable, often to influence decisions

Examples

Prepackaging a proposal
The prepackaging of the report made it easier for the committee to approve the project.
Effective prepackaging can help a product stand out in a crowded market.
The marketing team focused on the prepackaging of their ideas before the big presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packaging - the process of wrapping or preparing something for sale or use
  • synonym: presentation - the way in which something is shown or arranged
