stepping motor

noun
motor that rotates in discrete steps
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpɪŋ ˌmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈstɛpɪŋ ˌməʊtə / (BrE)
an electric motor that moves or rotates in small, precise, discrete steps, often used in devices requiring precise positioning

Examples

A stepping motor moves
Each time a pulse is sent to the stepping motor, it rotates a set number of degrees.
The 3D printer uses a stepping motor to carefully control the movement of the print head.
When precise positioning is needed, engineers often choose a stepping motor rather than a standard electric motor.
