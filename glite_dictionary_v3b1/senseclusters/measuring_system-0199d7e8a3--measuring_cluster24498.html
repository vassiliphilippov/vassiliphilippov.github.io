measuring system

noun
instrument for determining quantities
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒ(ə)rɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
An instrument or set of instruments designed to determine the extent, amount, quantity, or degree of something.

Examples
