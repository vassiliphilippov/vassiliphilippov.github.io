search party

noun
group organized to find someone missing
4.1
Pronunciation: / sɜrtʃ ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a group of people organized to look for someone who is missing or lost.

Examples

A search party
Despite hours of effort, the search party found no trace of the lost dog.
Local volunteers quickly formed a search party to look for the missing child in the woods.
The police organized a search party after the hiker failed to return.
