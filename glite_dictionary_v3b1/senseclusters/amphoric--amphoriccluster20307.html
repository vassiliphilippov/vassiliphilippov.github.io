amphoric

adjective
hollow, blowing sound heard in the chest
3.6
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɔrɪk / (AmE), / æmˈfɒrɪk / (BrE)
(of a sound, esp. in medical auscultation) resembling the hollow blowing sound produced by blowing across the mouth of a bottle, typically indicating a cavity in the lung.

Examples

Amphoric breathing sound
Amphoric breathing can be a sign of a pulmonary cavity.
Auscultation revealed amphoric resonance over the affected lung area.
The doctor noticed an amphoric quality to the patient's breath sounds.
