noun
main vocal melody
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the main vocal melody and lyrics composed for a song, especially in pop music production

Examples

Catchy topline melody
The songwriter was asked to create a catchy topline for the new dance track.
She specializes in writing toplines that make songs memorable and radio-friendly.
A strong topline can turn a simple beat into a hit song.
