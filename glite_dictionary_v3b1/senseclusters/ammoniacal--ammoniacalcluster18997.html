ammoniacal

adjective
containing or like ammonia
Pronunciation: / əˌmoʊniˈækəl / (AmE), / əˌmɒniˈækəl / (BrE)
Relating to or containing ammonia, or having a smell similar to ammonia.

Examples

Ammoniacal solution
An ammoniacal solution is commonly used to clean certain metals.
The laboratory reported an ammoniacal odor coming from the chemical solution.
Wastewater treatment plants often deal with ammoniacal compounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ammonia - a colorless gas with a pungent smell, NH₃
