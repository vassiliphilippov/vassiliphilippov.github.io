Averroes

proper noun
Arabian philosopher and commentator on Aristotle

Pronunciation: /ə.ˈve.r.oʊz/ /; / /ˈæv.ə.r.oʊz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈve.r.əʊz/ /; / /ˈæv.ə.r.əʊz/ (BrE)
