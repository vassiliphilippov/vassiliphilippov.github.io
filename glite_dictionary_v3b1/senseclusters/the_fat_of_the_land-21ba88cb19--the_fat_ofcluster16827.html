the fat of the land

noun
the best resources or comforts available
9.9
Pronunciation: / ðə fæt əv ðə lænd / (AmE), / ðə fæt ɒv ðə lænd / (BrE)
the best and most abundant things that a place or situation has to offer, often enjoyed as a reward for hard work or good fortune

Examples

Enjoy the fat of the land
After the harvest, the family feasted on the fat of the land.
As a reward, he was given the right to live off the fat of the land.
They worked hard for decades before finally enjoying the fat of the land.
