nurseryman

noun
man who raises plants commercially
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːsərimæn / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsərimæn / (BrE)
a man whose occupation is growing and caring for plants, especially in a nursery or garden devoted to raising plants for sale

Examples

A nurseryman grows plants
After years as a nurseryman, he opened his own plant nursery.
Many local parks rely on the expertise of a skilled nurseryman.
The nurseryman helped me choose the best shrubs for my garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gardener - someone who tends and cultivates a garden
