lowlight

noun
worst moment
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a moment or part of an event that is disappointing, less impressive, or the worst part.

Examples

The lowlight of the game
The team's defeat in the final was the lowlight of their otherwise successful season.
For many viewers, the technical problems during the concert were a clear lowlight.
The rainy weather was the lowlight of our vacation, but we still had fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disappointment - a feeling of sadness or displeasure caused by something not meeting expectations
  • antonym: highlight - the best or most exciting part of an event
