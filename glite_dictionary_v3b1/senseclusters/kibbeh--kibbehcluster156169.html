kibbeh

noun
Middle Eastern meat dish
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪbi / (AmE), / ˈkɪbi / (BrE)
a Middle Eastern dish made of ground spiced meat and bulgur wheat, often shaped into balls or patties

Examples

A kibbeh dish
We ordered kibbeh as an appetizer at the Lebanese restaurant.
My grandmother taught me how to make traditional kibbeh for family gatherings.
The chef served baked kibbeh with a side of yogurt sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falafel - a Middle Eastern dish of deep-fried balls made from ground chickpeas or fava beans
  • synonym: meatball - a ball of ground meat mixed with other ingredients
