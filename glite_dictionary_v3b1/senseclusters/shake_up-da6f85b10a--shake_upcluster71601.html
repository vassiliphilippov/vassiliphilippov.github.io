shake up

verb
make something fluffier by shaking
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk ʌp / (AmE), / ʃeɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to make something fuller, looser, or fluffier by shaking it

Examples

Shake up the pillows
Before guests arrive, I always shake up the cushions to make the sofa look inviting.
Could you shake up the flour bag to loosen it before measuring?
She shook up the comforter to give it more volume and softness.
