wine

noun
alcoholic drink from fermented grapes
Pronunciation: / waɪn / (AmE), / waɪn / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink made from the fermented juice of grapes or other fruits.

Examples

A glass of wine
He poured a glass of wine to enjoy with dinner.
Many people prefer red wine over white for its bold flavors.
The vineyard produces some of the finest wine in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beer - alcoholic drink made from fermented barley or other grains
  • synonym: cider - alcoholic drink made from fermented apple juice
