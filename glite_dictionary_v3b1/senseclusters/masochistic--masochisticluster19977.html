masochistic

adjective
deriving pleasure from suffering or abuse
Pronunciation: / ˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmasəˈkɪstɪk / (BrE)
Deriving pleasure, especially sexual gratification, from being abused, dominated, or made to suffer.

Examples

Masochistic pleasure
His masochistic enjoyment of harsh criticism surprised his friends.
Some people have masochistic tendencies and find satisfaction in enduring discomfort.
The story features a masochistic character who seeks out situations that cause pain.
