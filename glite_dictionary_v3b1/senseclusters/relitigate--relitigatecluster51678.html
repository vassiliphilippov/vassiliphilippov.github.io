relitigate

verb
to try case again
8.0
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈlɪt.ɪ.geɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈlɪt.ɪ.geɪt/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈlɪt.ɪ.geɪt/ /; / /riː.ˈlɪt.ɪ.geɪt/ (BrE)
