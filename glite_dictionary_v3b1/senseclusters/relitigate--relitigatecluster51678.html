Synonyms

  • rehear
    7.2 0.11/m
    rehear is a court hearing a case again, often on appeal or with new evidence, while relitigate means bringing the issue back after it was decided.
  • retry
    5.4 0.43/m
    retry means trying a trial again, usually after a hung jury or invalid verdict, while relitigate means taking a judged issue back to court.
  • reargue
    6.6 0.04/m
    reargue means arguing a case again, often to focus on legal points, while relitigate means bringing the whole matter back for a new court decision.
