brainless

adjective
lacking intelligence; very stupid
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnləs / (BrE)
not showing intelligence; very stupid or lacking sense

Examples

A brainless action
He acted in a completely brainless way during the meeting.
It was a brainless decision to leave the keys in the car.
Some reality television shows are criticized for being brainless entertainment.
