unburn

verb
to prevent burning
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɜrn / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːn / (BrE)
to prevent something from burning or being damaged by fire.

Examples

To unburn wood
They used a special coating to unburn the wooden beams in the old house.
Firefighters worked quickly to unburn the area around the cabin before the flames arrived.
The new material can help unburn furniture during wildfire season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protect - to keep something safe from harm or damage
