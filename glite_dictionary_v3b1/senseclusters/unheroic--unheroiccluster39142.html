unheroic

adjective
lacking bravery
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnhɪˈroʊɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnhɪˈrəʊɪk /; / ˌʌnhɪəˈrəʊɪk / (BrE)
not showing bravery, courage, or qualities typical of a hero

Examples
