cockbrained idea

noun
foolish or nonsensical idea
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkˌbreɪnd aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌbreɪnd aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea that is foolish, senseless, or absurd

Examples

A cockbrained idea
Launching such a risky campaign was a truly cockbrained idea.
She dismissed his cockbrained idea without a second thought.
That cockbrained idea is not going to work.
