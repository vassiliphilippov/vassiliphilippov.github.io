promise

noun
a commitment to do (or not do) something
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈprɑː.mɪs/ /; / /ˈprɑː.məs/ (AmE), /ˈprɒ.mɪs/ /; / /ˈprɒ.məs/ (BrE)

Examples
