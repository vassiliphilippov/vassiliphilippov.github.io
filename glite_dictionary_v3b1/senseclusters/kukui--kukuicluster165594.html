kukui

noun
oil from kukui tree
Pronunciation: / kuːˈkuːi / (AmE), / kuːˈkuːi / (BrE)
an oil obtained from the seeds of the kukui tree, used in cooking, cosmetics, or as a lamp oil

Examples

Kukui oil for cooking
Traditional Hawaiian dishes sometimes use kukui oil for its unique flavor.
She massaged kukui into her hair to keep it soft and shiny.
In the past, people used kukui as a natural lamp oil for lighting their homes.

Related Terms
