Ayurvedic

noun
practitioner of Ayurveda
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪjərˈveɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪjʊəˈveɪdɪk / (BrE)
a person who practices or is trained in Ayurveda, the traditional system of medicine from India

Examples

An Ayurvedic doctor
She consulted an experienced Ayurvedic to treat her persistent digestive problems.
The Ayurvedic recommended herbal remedies and dietary changes for better health.
Many people visit a local Ayurvedic for natural treatments and holistic advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ayurvedist - a practitioner of Ayurveda
  • synonym: doctor - a person qualified to practice medicine
