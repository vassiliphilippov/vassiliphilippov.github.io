career ladder

noun
stages of advancement in a career
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr ˈlædər / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə ˈlædə / (BrE)
a series of stages or levels through which one may advance in a profession or organization

Examples

Climb the career ladder
Many people hope to climb the career ladder to achieve leadership positions.
She is steadily moving up the career ladder at her company.
Starting in an entry-level job is often the first step on the career ladder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promotion - the act of moving to a higher rank or position
