Trachinotus falcatus

noun
a large Atlantic game fish (the permit)
9.9
Pronunciation: / trəˌkɪnoʊtəs fælˈkeɪtəs / (AmE), / trəˌkɪnətəs fælˈkeɪtəs / (BrE)
a large game fish of the family Carangidae, found in the western Atlantic Ocean, especially around the West Indies, prized by anglers
