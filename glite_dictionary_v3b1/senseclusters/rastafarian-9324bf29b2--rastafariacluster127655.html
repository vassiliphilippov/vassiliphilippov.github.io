Rastafarian

noun
follower of the Rastafari religion
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑːstəˈferiən /; / ˌræstəˈfɛriən / (AmE), / ˌrastəˈfɑːrɪən / (BrE)
a follower of Rastafari or Rastafarianism, a religion and social movement that began in Jamaica
