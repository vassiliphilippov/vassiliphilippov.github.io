control

noun
spirit guiding a medium during a séance
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
(in spiritualism) a spirit or spiritual agency believed to guide or assist a medium during a séance.

Examples

Spirit control
During the séance, the control took over and spoke through the medium.
Each control is said to have a distinct personality and background.
The medium claimed that her control was guiding her through the message from beyond.
