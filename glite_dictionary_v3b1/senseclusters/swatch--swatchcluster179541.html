swatch

noun
sample piece of material
6.5
Pronunciation: / swɑtʃ / (AmE), / swɒtʃ / (BrE)
A small sample piece of cloth or other material, used to demonstrate its color, texture, or pattern.

Examples

A swatch of fabric
Please choose your paint color from this swatch book.
The designer handed me a swatch of the fabric so I could feel its quality.
We compared several swatches before choosing the final color scheme for the curtains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sample - a small part or quantity intended to show what the whole is like
