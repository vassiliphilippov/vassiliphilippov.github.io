distressed

adjective
experiencing financial difficulty
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɹɛst / (AmE), / dɪˈstɹɛst / (BrE)
experiencing financial trouble or difficulty; being in a state of economic hardship

Examples

Distressed company
Many distressed businesses seek government assistance during economic downturns.
Several distressed homeowners are struggling to keep up with their mortgage payments.
The bank specializes in purchasing distressed assets from companies facing bankruptcy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bankrupt - declared in law unable to pay debts
  • synonym: insolvent - unable to pay debts owed
  • antonym: prosperous - financially successful, flourishing
