brut

adjective
very dry champagne
Pronunciation: / bruːt / (AmE), / bruːt / (BrE)
(of champagne) extremely dry, containing very little sugar.

Examples

Brut champagne
Her preference is always for brut rather than sweet champagne.
The sommelier recommended a brut sparkling wine to pair with our appetizer.
We celebrated with a bottle of brut champagne to mark the occasion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - wine or champagne that is not sweet
  • degree lower: extra dry - champagne sweeter than brut but drier than sec
  • degree lower: sec - champagne that is slightly less dry than brut
