anastasis

noun
Christian resurrection scene
Pronunciation: / əˈnæstəsɪs / (AmE), / əˈnæstəsɪs / (BrE)
a scene in Christian art, especially Eastern Orthodox, showing Christ's descent to the dead or triumph over death

Examples

Anastasis icon
The church's altar was decorated with a beautiful anastasis icon showing Christ lifting Adam and Eve.
Art historians studied the details of the anastasis fresco to understand its religious symbolism.
During the tour, the guide explained the meaning of the anastasis depicted in the ancient mosaic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crucifixion - the execution of Jesus by being nailed to a cross
  • synonym: resurrection - the act of rising from the dead
