cartop

noun
car roof carrier
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtɒp /; / ˈkɑːˌtɒp / (BrE)
the roof of a car used for carrying things, or a rack or carrier attached to the roof for transporting luggage or equipment
