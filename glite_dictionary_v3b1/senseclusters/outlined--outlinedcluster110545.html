outlined

adjective
clearly defined in shape or boundary
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlaɪnd / (BrE)
having a shape, edge, or boundary that is clearly shown or defined against a background.

Examples

Light outlined shape

The mountain was outlined against the bright morning sky.
