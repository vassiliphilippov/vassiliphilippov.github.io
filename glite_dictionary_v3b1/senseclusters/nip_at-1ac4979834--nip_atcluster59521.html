nip at

verb
bite or touch lightly, often playfully
Pronunciation: / nɪp æt / (AmE), / nɪp æt / (BrE)
to bite, pinch, or touch something lightly, often in a teasing or playful manner

Examples

To nip at ankles
During the hike, brambles would sometimes nip at our ankles as we passed.
The cold wind would nip at her cheeks as she walked home in the evening.
The kitten loves to nip at loose threads on the blanket.
