wile

verb
to spend time pleasantly
8.2
Pronunciation: / waɪl / (AmE), / waɪl / (BrE)
to spend time in a relaxed or pleasant way, especially in the phrase 'wile away'

Examples

To wile away time
They wiled away the afternoon by chatting in the garden.
On rainy days, she wiles away the hours reading mystery novels.
He wiled away his time waiting for the train by listening to music.
They love to wile away summer afternoons by the lake.
On rainy days, she would wile away the hours reading novels.
He found ways to wile away the long train journey with music and games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idle - to spend time doing nothing important
  • synonym: kill time - to do something to make time seem to pass more quickly
  • synonym: pass - to spend time doing something
  • antonym: work - to engage in activity or labor
