antitragic

adjective
not tragic
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈtrædʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈtrædʒɪk / (BrE)
not having the qualities of tragedy, or going against the usual tragic conventions (especially in literature or drama)

Examples

The director chose an antitragic approach, ending the story with hope instead of despair.
Critics praised the novel's antitragic elements for challenging traditional expectations.
Her antitragic interpretation of the character brought a fresh perspective to the classic play.
