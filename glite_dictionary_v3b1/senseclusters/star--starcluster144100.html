star

adjective
most important performer or role
Pronunciation: / stɑr / (AmE), / stɑː / (BrE)
Of or relating to the most important performer, role, or feature in a particular context.

Examples

Star player
He landed the star role in the blockbuster movie this summer.
Only the best athletes qualify for a star position on the team.
She was the star performer in the play, earning rave reviews for her role.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leading - having a principal or important role
  • antonym: supporting - having a secondary or less prominent role
