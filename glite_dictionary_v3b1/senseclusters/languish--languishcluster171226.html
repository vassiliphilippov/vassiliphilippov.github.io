languish

verb
lose strength or health, often from grief
3.9 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlæŋ.gwɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈlæŋ.gwɪʃ/ (BrE)
to become weak, lose strength, or suffer from neglect, hardship, or sorrow
