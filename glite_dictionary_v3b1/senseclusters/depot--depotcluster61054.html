depot

noun
station for loading or unloading passengers or goods
6.3 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːpoʊ, dəˈpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɛpəʊ, ˈdiːpəʊ / (BrE)
A place where buses, trains, or other vehicles regularly stop to load or unload passengers or goods.

Examples

Bus depot
All the packages are sorted at the central depot before being sent out for delivery.
The bus arrived at the depot just before midnight, and everyone hurried inside.
When we reached the train depot, we waited for our luggage to be unloaded.
