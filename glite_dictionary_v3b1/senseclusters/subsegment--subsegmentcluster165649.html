subsegment

noun
smaller division
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsɛɡmənt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsɛɡmənt / (BrE)
a smaller part or section within a larger segment

Examples

A subsegment of the market
The marketing team analyzed each subsegment of the population to tailor their campaigns.
In biology, a subsegment of the chromosome was found to contain the mutated gene.
The report breaks down the data into several subsegments for easier comparison.
The report divided the market into several subsegments for detailed analysis.
Each subsegment of the river was tested for water quality separately.
The teacher asked the students to summarize each subsegment of the chapter.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: segment - a part or section of something larger
  • synonym: subdivision - a part of something that has been divided
