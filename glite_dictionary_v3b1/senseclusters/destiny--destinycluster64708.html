destiny

noun
circumstances or condition in life
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdes.tɪ.niː/ /; / /ˈdes.tə.niː/ (AmE), /ˈdes.tɪ.niː/ /; / /ˈdes.tə.niː/ (BrE)
the things that will happen to someone or something in the future, often believed to be controlled by a mysterious or supernatural force

Examples
