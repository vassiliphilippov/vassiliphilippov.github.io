rear light

noun
red lamp at the back of a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ɹɪr laɪt / (AmE), / rɪə laɪt / (BrE)
a red lamp mounted at the back of a motor vehicle, used to indicate its presence and position, especially at night or in poor visibility

Examples

A rear light on car
He replaced a broken rear light before his vehicle inspection.
In foggy conditions, the rear lights help other drivers see your car easily.
The car's rear light stopped working, making it dangerous to drive at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taillight - red warning light at the rear of a vehicle
