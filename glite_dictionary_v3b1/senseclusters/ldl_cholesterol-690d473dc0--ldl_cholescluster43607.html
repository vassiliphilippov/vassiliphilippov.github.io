LDL cholesterol

noun
cholesterol in low-density lipoproteins; 'bad' cholesterol
3.8 0.65/m

Examples

LDL cholesterol levels
A diet high in saturated fat can increase your LDL cholesterol levels.

The blood test showed that his LDL cholesterol was above the recommended range.
