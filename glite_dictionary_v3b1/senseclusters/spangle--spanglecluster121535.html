spangle

noun
small shiny decorative piece
Pronunciation: / ˈspæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈspæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
A small, flat, shiny piece of material used to decorate clothing, costumes, or accessories.

Examples

A gold spangle
Each spangle on the jacket caught the light, creating a dazzling effect.
She sewed a gold spangle onto the costume for added sparkle.
The dancer’s dress glittered with every spangle stitched into the fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sequin - a small, shiny, disk-shaped ornament used to decorate clothing
