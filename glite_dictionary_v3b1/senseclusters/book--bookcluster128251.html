book

verb
to record a criminal charge (police)
6.4
Pronunciation: / bʊk / (AmE), / bʊk / (BrE)
(of the police) to officially record a charge against someone in a police register.

Examples

Police book a suspect
After the protest, several activists were booked by the police for unlawful assembly.
She was booked for driving under the influence late last night.
The officer booked him on suspicion of theft and took him to the station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrest - to take someone into custody by legal authority
  • synonym: charge - to formally accuse someone of a crime
