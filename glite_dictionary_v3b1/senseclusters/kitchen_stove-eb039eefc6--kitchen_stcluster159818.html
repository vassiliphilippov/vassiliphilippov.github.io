kitchen stove

noun
cooking appliance in a kitchen
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən stoʊv / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃ(ə)n stəʊv / (BrE)
A stove used in the kitchen for cooking food, typically powered by gas or electricity.

Examples

A kitchen stove burner
A modern kitchen stove often includes both gas burners and an electric oven.
She simmered the soup slowly on the kitchen stove for hours.
The old kitchen stove needed to be replaced after it broke down last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oven - enclosed compartment for baking or roasting food
  • synonym: range - a stove with an oven, especially one used for cooking in a kitchen
