oversalted

adjective
too much salt
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsɔltɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɔːltɪd / (BrE)
containing more salt than is desirable or healthy

Examples

An oversalted stew
The stew was oversalted, making it almost impossible to finish.
If you find the sauce oversalted, try adding a bit of cream to balance the flavor.
She apologized for the oversalted vegetables at dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor, especially salt
  • degree lower: salty - containing or tasting of salt
