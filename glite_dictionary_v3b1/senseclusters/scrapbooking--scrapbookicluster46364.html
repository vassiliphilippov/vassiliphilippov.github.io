scrapbooking

noun
decorative album-making
Pronunciation: / ˈskræpˌbʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskræpˌbʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of creating decorated albums to preserve photographs, memories, and mementos

Examples

Scrapbooking hobby
Many people enjoy scrapbooking as a creative way to remember special occasions.
She started scrapbooking to organize her family photos and ticket stubs.
There are entire stores dedicated to supplies for scrapbooking enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: album - a book for keeping photographs or other items
  • synonym: journaling - the activity of keeping a personal diary or record
