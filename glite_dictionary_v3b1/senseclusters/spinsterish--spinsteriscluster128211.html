spinsterish

adjective
prim or old-fashioned
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnstərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈspɪnstərɪʃ / (BrE)
excessively prim, old-fashioned, or having habits or appearance typical of a stereotypical unmarried older woman

Examples

A spinsterish woman
Her spinsterish insistence on formal table manners embarrassed the younger guests at dinner.
The teacher's spinsterish attitude made the classroom feel strict and old-fashioned.
He found her spinsterish way of dressing charmingly out of date.
Her spinsterish habits included knitting and keeping everything very tidy.
The room had a spinsterish neatness that made it feel old-fashioned.
He teased his sister about her spinsterish preference for quiet evenings at home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times, not old-fashioned
  • synonym: old-maidish - having qualities stereotypically associated with an unmarried older woman
  • synonym: prim - very formal and proper in behavior or appearance
  • synonym: prudish - very easily shocked by matters relating to sex or nudity
