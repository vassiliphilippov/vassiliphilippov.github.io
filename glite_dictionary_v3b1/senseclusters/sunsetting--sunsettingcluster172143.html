sunsetting

noun
time sun sets
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
the time or process when the sun goes down and daylight ends; sunset.

Examples

A beautiful sunsetting
They admired the sunsetting as the sky turned pink and orange over the harbor.
The photographer waited patiently to capture the perfect sunsetting over the mountains.
During our walk, we paused to watch the peaceful sunsetting on the beach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sunrise - the time in the morning when the sun appears above the horizon
  • synonym: sunset - the time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon
