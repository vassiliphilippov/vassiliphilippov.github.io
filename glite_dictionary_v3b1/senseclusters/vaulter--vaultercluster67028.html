vaulter

noun
athlete who pole vaults
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔltər / (AmE), / ˈvɔːltə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes by jumping over a high bar using a long pole for assistance

Examples

A vaulter clears bar
Every successful vaulter relies on both skill and courage to clear the bar.
She trained for years before becoming the top vaulter in her country.
The vaulter sprinted down the runway, readying for his record-breaking leap.
