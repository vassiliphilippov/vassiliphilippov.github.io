nerf

noun
power reduction (games)
8.3
Pronunciation: /nɜːrf/ (AmE), /nɜːf/ (BrE)
a reduction in the power or effectiveness of something, especially a character or item in a game, usually as a result of a deliberate update or change.

Examples

A nerf to weapons
After the latest update, the weapon received a significant nerf that disappointed many players.
Gamers complained about the unexpected nerf to their favorite character's abilities.
The developers announced a nerf to balance the overpowered items in the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buff - an increase in the power or effectiveness of something, especially in games
  • synonym: debuff - a negative effect that reduces a character's abilities in games
