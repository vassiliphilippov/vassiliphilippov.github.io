king mackerel

noun
a large Atlantic food and game fish
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋ ˈmækərəl / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ ˈmækərəl / (BrE)
a large mackerel (Scomberomorus cavalla) with a long, pointed snout, valued as a food and game fish found along the western Atlantic coast from the United States to Brazil.

Examples

Grilled king mackerel
Grilled king mackerel is a popular dish in many coastal restaurants.
She caught a huge king mackerel off the coast of Florida last summer.
The king mackerel is prized by many sport fishermen for its fighting ability.
