seest

verb
archaic 'see' (thou)
Pronunciation: / siːɪst / (AmE), / siːɪst / (BrE)
archaic second person singular present form of 'see', used with 'thou' in early English.

Examples

Thou seest the truth
O, how clearly thou seest the truth now!
Dost thou not seest the stars shining above us?
When seest thou the dawn, remember my words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: see - to perceive with the eyes
