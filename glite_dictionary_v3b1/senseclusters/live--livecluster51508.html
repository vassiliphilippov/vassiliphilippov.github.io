Synonyms

  • reside
    3.0 11/m
    reside is a formal verb often used for someone's official home, while live is a common everyday word for having a home.
  • inhabit
    3.5 8/m
    inhabit is used for people or animals living in an environment, while live is the general word for having a home.
  • dwell
    3.2 3/m
    dwell is a more formal or old-fashioned verb often found in writing, while live is the usual everyday verb for living somewhere.
  • occupy
    3.0 9/m
    occupy often means to take or hold a space or building, while live focuses on having a home in a place.
  • settle
    2.5 30/m
    settle usually stresses moving and making a home, often permanently, while live simply means to have a home in a place.
