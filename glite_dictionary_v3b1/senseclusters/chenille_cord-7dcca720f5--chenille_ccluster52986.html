chenille cord

noun
soft, tufted embroidery cord
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈnil kɔrd / (AmE), / ʃəˈniːl kɔːd / (BrE)
a soft, tufted cord with a velvety texture, used in embroidery and textile crafts

Examples

A thick chenille cord
For her craft project, she chose a thick chenille cord for added texture.
She used a bright red chenille cord to outline the flowers on the cushion.
The chenille cord gave the embroidery a luxurious, raised effect.
