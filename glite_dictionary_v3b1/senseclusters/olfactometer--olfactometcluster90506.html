olfactometer

noun
smell testing device
Pronunciation: / ɑlˌfækˈtɑmɪtər / (AmE), / ɒlˌfækˈtɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to measure or test a person's sense of smell

Examples

The olfactometer device

Doctors used an olfactometer to evaluate the patient's sense of smell after the accident.
Researchers developed a new olfactometer to deliver precise concentrations of different odors.
