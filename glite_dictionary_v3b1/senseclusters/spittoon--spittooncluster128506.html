spittoon

noun
receptacle for spit
4.5
Pronunciation: / spɪˈtun / (AmE), / spɪˈtuːn / (BrE)
a container used for spitting into, typically found in public places such as bars or waiting rooms, especially in the past

Examples

A spittoon container
He absentmindedly spat into the spittoon as he read the morning paper.
In the old saloon, a brass spittoon sat on the floor beside every table.
The museum displayed a ceramic spittoon from the 19th century.
