blonde moment

noun
a lapse of judgment or forgetfulness
6.3
Pronunciation: / blɑnd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / blɒnd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
An instance of forgetfulness, foolishness, or a lapse in judgment, often perceived as silly or absent-minded.

Examples

A blonde moment of forgetfulness
I couldn't believe my blonde moment when I locked my car keys inside the car.
She admitted it was a blonde moment to forget her own phone number.
We've all had a blonde moment or two during stressful times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brain fart - a temporary mental lapse or failure to reason correctly
  • synonym: senior moment - a temporary lapse in memory, usually attributed to aging
