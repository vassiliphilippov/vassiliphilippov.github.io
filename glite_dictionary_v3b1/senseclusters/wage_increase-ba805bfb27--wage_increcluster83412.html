wage increase

noun
an increase in salary or wages
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdʒ ɪnˌkriːs / (AmE), / ˈweɪdʒ ɪnˌkriːs / (BrE)
an increase in the amount of money paid to a worker or employee as wages or salary

Examples

A wage increase
A wage increase can help offset the rising cost of living.
After negotiations, the workers secured a 5% wage increase.
The company announced a wage increase for all hourly employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pay rise - an increase in the amount one is paid (chiefly British)
  • synonym: raise - an increase in salary or wages
  • antonym: wage cut - a reduction in wages or salary
