stemming

noun
word root reduction
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of reducing words to their root or base form, often used in language processing and search engines

Examples

Stemming words
Stemming helps search engines find relevant results by matching different forms of a word.
Many text analysis tools use stemming to simplify language data.
Without stemming, a search for 'run' might miss results for 'running' or 'runner'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemmatization - the process of reducing words to their dictionary or base form
