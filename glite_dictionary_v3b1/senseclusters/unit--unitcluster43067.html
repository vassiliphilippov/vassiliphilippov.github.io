unit

noun
group within an organization
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a group of people, especially within an organization, regarded as part of a larger whole; such as a section of the military or a team subdivision

Examples

A military unit
After an injury, the player returned to the offensive unit and helped his team win.
Each unit in the army was assigned a specific mission during the operation.
The company reorganized its marketing unit to focus on digital strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: division - a distinct group or section within a larger organization
  • synonym: team - a group of people working together as part of a larger structure
