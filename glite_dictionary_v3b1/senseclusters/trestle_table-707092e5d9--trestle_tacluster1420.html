trestle table

noun
table on trestles as supports
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛsəl ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɹɛsəl ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
A table consisting of a flat top supported by trestles, often designed to be portable or easily assembled.

Examples

Rows of trestle tables
Rows of trestle tables filled the community hall for the event.
The craftsman built a sturdy trestle table from reclaimed wood.
We dined at a large trestle table set in the garden.
