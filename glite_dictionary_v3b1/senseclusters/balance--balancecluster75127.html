balance

noun
regulating wheel in timepieces
3.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əns /; / ˈbæləns / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns /; / ˈbæləns / (BrE)
a device or object used for weighing or for keeping things steady or in equilibrium (such as a scale, wheel, or counterweight)

Examples

A kitchen balance
A damaged balance can cause the entire clock to lose or gain time.
The balance in an old pocket watch must be finely tuned for accurate timekeeping.
Watchmakers carefully examine the balance to ensure the movement runs smoothly.
A balance was needed to measure the exact mass of the sample.
In the old clock mechanism, a small balance ensures the gears function precisely.
The scale was tipped until an equal balance was added to the other side.
A traditional balance was used to compare the weights of the two objects.
He carefully adjusted the balance until the pointer was level, indicating equal weight on both sides.
The laboratory technician placed the powder on the balance to measure its mass accurately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance wheel - the oscillating wheel in a timepiece that regulates its timing
  • synonym: counterweight - a weight used to balance another weight
  • synonym: scale - an instrument for weighing, often with a platform or spring mechanism
