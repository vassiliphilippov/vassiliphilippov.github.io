lotion

noun
liquid preparation for the skin
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a liquid that you put on your skin to moisturize, protect, or treat it

Examples

Sunscreen lotion
Many people use sunscreen lotion to protect their skin from the sun's rays.
She applied a generous amount of lotion to her dry hands every evening.
The hotel bathroom provided complimentary bottles of body lotion.
She applied a soothing lotion to her sunburned shoulders after coming back from the beach.
The doctor recommended an antiseptic lotion for treating minor cuts and scrapes.
This moisturizing lotion helps keep your skin soft during the winter months.
