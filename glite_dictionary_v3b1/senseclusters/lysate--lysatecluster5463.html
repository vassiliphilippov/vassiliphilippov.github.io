lysate

noun
cell contents fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪseɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪseɪt / (BrE)
a substance (fluid, mixture, or preparation) containing the contents released from cells that have been broken open, often used in research or medicine

Examples

After centrifugation, the lysate was carefully collected for further analysis.
