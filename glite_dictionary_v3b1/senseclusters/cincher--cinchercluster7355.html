cincher

noun
decisive factor
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪntʃər / (AmE), / ˈsɪntʃə / (BrE)
a fact, action, or event that decisively settles a matter or ensures a particular outcome

Examples

The final cincher
The last-minute goal was the cincher that secured their victory in the tournament.
Her impressive presentation became the cincher in convincing the board to approve the project.
The expert's testimony served as the cincher in the court case, leaving no doubt about the verdict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dealbreaker - a factor that ends negotiations or makes agreement impossible
  • synonym: decider - something that determines the result
