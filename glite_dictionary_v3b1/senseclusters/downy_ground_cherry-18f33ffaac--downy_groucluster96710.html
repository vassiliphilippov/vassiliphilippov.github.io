downy ground cherry

noun
American annual plant with yellow berries in a husk
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊni ˈɡraʊnd ˌtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni ˈɡraʊnd ˌtʃɛri / (BrE)
A decorative American annual plant with round, fleshy yellow berries enclosed in a bladder-like husk.
