tenselessness

noun
absence of tense
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈten.sə.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈten.sləs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈten.sə.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈten.sləs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking grammatical tense in a language or verb form

Examples

Tenselessness in verbs
Linguists study tenselessness in languages that do not mark verbs for past, present, or future.
The tenselessness of certain verb forms can make translation into English challenging.
Some African languages are known for their tenselessness in expressing time.
