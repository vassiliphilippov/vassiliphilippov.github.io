linguistic

adjective
relating to language or linguistics
2.6
Pronunciation: / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to language or the scientific study of language

Examples

Linguistic abilities
Her linguistic abilities allowed her to learn several languages quickly.
The professor gave a detailed analysis from a linguistic perspective.
There are many linguistic similarities between Spanish and Italian.
A linguistic analysis can reveal subtle patterns in everyday communication.
His research focuses on linguistic differences among regional dialects.
She has a strong interest in linguistic theory and language acquisition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lingual - relating to the tongue or speech
  • synonym: linguistical - relating to language (less common variant)
  • antonym: nonlinguistic - not involving or related to language
