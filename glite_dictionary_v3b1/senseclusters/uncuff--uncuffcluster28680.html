uncuff

verb
to undo a cuff
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌf / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌf / (BrE)
to let down, unfold, or undo the cuff of a sleeve, trouser leg, or other garment

Examples

To uncuff a sleeve
After work, she liked to uncuff her sleeves and relax at home.
He paused to uncuff his pants before wading into the shallow stream.
The tailor showed her how to properly uncuff the jacket for a different style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cuff - to fold or turn up the end of a sleeve or trouser leg
  • synonym: unroll - to open or spread out something that was rolled up
