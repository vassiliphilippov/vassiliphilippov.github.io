Palace of Versailles

proper noun
grand royal palace near Paris, France
6.1
Pronunciation: / pəˈlæs əv vɛrˈsaɪ / (AmE), / pəˈlæs əv vɛəˈsɑːj / (BrE)
the grand palace built in the 17th century for King Louis XIV, located southwest of Paris, famous for its architecture, gardens, and historical significance

Examples

The Palace of Versailles gardens

Tourists from around the world visit the Palace of Versailles to admire its magnificent gardens.
