timber

verb
to cut trees for timber
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbə / (BrE)
to cut down trees and prepare them for use as timber, especially as part of logging operations

Examples

To timber a forest
The company began timbering the forest to supply wood for construction.
Strict regulations now control where and how timbering can take place.
Local workers have been timbering in these mountains for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deforest - to clear an area of trees
  • synonym: log - to cut down trees for wood
  • antonym: plant - to put seeds or young trees in the ground to grow
