flee

verb
run away quickly, especially from danger
3.3
Pronunciation: / fliː / (AmE), / fliː / (BrE)
to run away from a place or situation of danger quickly

Examples

To flee danger
Hundreds of residents had to flee their homes when the wildfire approached.
Many refugees are fleeing violence and persecution in their home country.
When the alarm sounded, the thieves fled the building without being caught.

Related Terms

  • synonym: escape - to break free from confinement or control
  • antonym: stay - to remain in a place, not leave
