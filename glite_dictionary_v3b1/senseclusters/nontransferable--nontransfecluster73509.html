nontransferable

adjective
not able to be transferred
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.trænsˈfɜr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.trɑːnsˈfɜː.rə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be transferred from one person, place, or ownership to another.

Examples

A nontransferable ticket
Her airline voucher was nontransferable, making resale impossible.
The pass is nontransferable, so you cannot give it to a friend or family member.
These tickets are strictly nontransferable and may only be used by the person named.

Related Terms

  • antonym: transferable - able to be transferred
