why not

x
expresses agreement or willingness
Pronunciation: / waɪ nɑt / (AmE), / waɪ nɒt / (BrE)
used to express agreement, acceptance, or a willingness to do something when there is no good reason not to

Examples

Why not watch a movie
If you’re free tonight, why not watch a movie together?
She asked if I would join her for a walk and I said, “Why not?”
“Do you want to come with us to the café?” “Why not?”
