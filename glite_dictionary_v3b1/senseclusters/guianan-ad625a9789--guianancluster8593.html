Guianan

noun
person from Guiana
Pronunciation: / ɡiˈænən / (AmE), / ɡiˈænən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Guiana, a region in northern South America

Examples

A Guianan family
A Guianan described the traditional fishing techniques used by families along the riverbanks.
The festival featured music and dance performed by local Guianans.
Many Guianans speak several languages due to the region's diverse cultural influences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Brazilian - a person from Brazil, a country in South America
  • synonym: Guyanese - a person from Guyana, a country in northern South America
