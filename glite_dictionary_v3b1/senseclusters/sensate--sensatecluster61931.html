sensate

adjective
able to perceive sensation
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnˌseɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛnseɪt / (BrE)
having physical sensation; able to perceive or feel things through the senses.

Examples

sensate beings
Many animals are classified as sensate beings, capable of feeling pleasure and pain.
The study focused on the development of sensate tissue after reconstructive surgery.
Unlike rocks, which are insensate, living organisms are at least minimally sensate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensitive - quick to detect or respond to slight changes or signals
