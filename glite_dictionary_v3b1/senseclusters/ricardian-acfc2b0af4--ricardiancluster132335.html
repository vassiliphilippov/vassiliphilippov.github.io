Ricardian

noun
supporter of Richard III
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑːrdiən / (AmE), / rɪˈkɑːdiən / (BrE)
a person who supports or admires King Richard III, especially one who defends his reputation

Examples

Ricardian historian
The Ricardian argued that Richard III was unfairly portrayed by Shakespeare.
Many Ricardians believe that Richard III was not responsible for the deaths of the princes in the Tower.
A group of Ricardians gathered to commemorate the anniversary of Richard III's coronation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Tudor - a supporter of the Tudor dynasty, often opposed to Ricardians
  • synonym: royalist - a supporter of a king or monarchy
