in a nutshell

adverb
briefly; in summary
7.2 2.8
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə ˈnʌtʃˌɛl / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈnʌtʃɛl / (BrE)
used to introduce a summary or to say something in a very brief and clear way

Examples

In a nutshell summary

In a nutshell, the project was a complete success.
If you want my opinion in a nutshell, I think we should postpone it.
She described the whole situation in a nutshell at the meeting.
