pyrogenic

adjective
produced by intense heat
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
Produced or formed by or under conditions of intense heat, especially relating to geological processes.

Examples

Pyrogenic rocks
Scientists studied the pyrogenic deposits left after the ancient volcanic eruptions.
Some minerals only form in pyrogenic environments of extreme heat and pressure.
The area is known for pyrogenic rocks created deep within the earth's crust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: igneous - (of rock) formed by the solidification of molten magma
