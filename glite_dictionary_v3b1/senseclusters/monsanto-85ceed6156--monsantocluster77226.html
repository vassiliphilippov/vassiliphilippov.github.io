Monsanto

proper noun
Portuguese village
Pronunciation: / mɒnˈsæntoʊ / (AmE), / mɒnˈsæntəʊ / (BrE)
a village in Portugal known for its hilltop houses built among large boulders and its medieval castle

Examples

Monsanto village
Tourists often visit Monsanto to see its unique stone houses and breathtaking views.
The medieval castle in Monsanto stands on a hilltop overlooking the surrounding countryside.
Many people consider Monsanto one of the most picturesque villages in Portugal.
