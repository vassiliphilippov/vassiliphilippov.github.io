moderationist

noun
person who drinks alcohol in moderation
6.3 9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who drinks alcoholic beverages in moderation, as opposed to someone who abstains completely

Examples

Moderationist drinker
As a moderationist, he enjoyed an occasional glass of wine with dinner but never overindulged.
She considered herself a moderationist, believing that responsible enjoyment of alcohol was entirely possible.
The club welcomes both moderationists and teetotalers to its social events.
