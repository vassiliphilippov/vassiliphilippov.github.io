chlorofucin

Examples

Brown chlorofucin
Brown algae contain chlorofucin, which allows them to absorb light at different wavelengths than land plants.
Researchers discovered that chlorofucin plays a crucial role in the photosynthesis of diatoms.
Unlike the chlorophyll found in green plants, chlorofucin is unique to some algae and flagellates.
