empennage

noun
tail assembly of an aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.pə.nɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒm.pɪ.nɑːʒ / (BrE)
the tail assembly of an aircraft, including the stabilizing surfaces on the rear part

Examples

Aircraft empennage
Modern aircraft empennage designs often improve stability and control.
Technicians inspected the empennage for any signs of corrosion.
The empennage of the plane was damaged during the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fuselage - the main body of an aircraft
  • synonym: tailplane - the horizontal stabilizing surface of an aircraft's tail
