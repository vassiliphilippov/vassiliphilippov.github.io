palletize

verb
to load onto pallets
Pronunciation: / ˈpælɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpælɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to place goods or materials onto pallets for easier storage or transport

Examples

To palletize goods
The warehouse staff palletize all incoming shipments to speed up storage.
Before being loaded onto the truck, the goods were carefully palletized and wrapped.
It is safer to palletize heavy boxes rather than move them individually.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stack - to arrange items in a pile or organized group
  • antonym: unload - to remove goods from a vehicle or container
