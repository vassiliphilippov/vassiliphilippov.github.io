unconsenting

adjective
without permission
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsɛntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsɛntɪŋ / (BrE)
not having given permission or agreement to something

Examples

Unconsenting participants
The documentary raised ethical questions about filming unconsenting participants.
It is illegal to share personal information about unconsenting individuals.
The study was criticized for including data from unconsenting subjects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consenting - having given permission or agreement
  • synonym: nonconsensual - done without the agreement of all parties involved
