rounded

adjective
having a smooth, curved shape
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
having a smooth, curved, or approximately circular shape rather than being jagged or angular

Examples

A rounded shape
His handwriting is smooth and rounded, making the letters easy to read.
The child drew a house with rounded windows and a curved roof.
The hills in this region are gently rounded rather than steep or pointed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curved - bent in a smooth, continuous way
  • antonym: jagged - having rough, sharp points protruding
