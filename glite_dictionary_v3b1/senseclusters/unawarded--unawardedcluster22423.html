unawarded

adjective
not given as award
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈwɔrdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈwɔːdɪd / (BrE)
not given or granted as an award, prize, or recognition

Examples

Several unawarded grants
Several unawarded grants were returned to the funding organization at the end of the year.
The unawarded medals remained in the box after the ceremony was canceled.
Due to a lack of qualified applicants, some scholarships remained unawarded this semester.

  • synonym: withheld - not given or held back
