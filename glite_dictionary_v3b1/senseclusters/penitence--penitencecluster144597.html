penitence

noun
remorse for past wrongdoing
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnɪtəns / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪtəns / (BrE)
the feeling of regret or sorrow for having done something wrong; remorse for one's past actions or conduct

Examples

Show penitence for mistakes
Acts of charity were seen as a sign of his penitence for past mistakes.
She expressed genuine penitence for her harsh words after the argument.
The thief showed no penitence, even after being convicted in court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrition - the state of feeling remorseful and penitent
  • synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
