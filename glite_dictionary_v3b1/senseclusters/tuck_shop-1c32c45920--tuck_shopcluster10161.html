tuck shop

noun
British school shop selling sweets and snacks
3.7
Pronunciation: / tʌk ʃɑp / (AmE), / tʌk ʃɒp / (BrE)
a small shop, especially in a school, selling sweets, snacks, and sometimes drinks

Examples

School tuck shop
At lunchtime, the students rushed to the tuck shop to buy crisps and chocolate bars.
Our school tuck shop sells a variety of snacks and bottled drinks.
She volunteered to help run the tuck shop during the school fair.
