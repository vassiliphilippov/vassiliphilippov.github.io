nonfood

noun
products not food
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌfuːd / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌfuːd / (BrE)
products or items that are not food, especially when considered as a group or category

Examples

Nonfood items
The store sells both food and nonfood items such as cleaning supplies and toiletries.
After the disaster, volunteers distributed nonfood essentials like blankets and soap to the affected families.
Many supermarkets have expanded their nonfood sections to include clothing and electronics.
