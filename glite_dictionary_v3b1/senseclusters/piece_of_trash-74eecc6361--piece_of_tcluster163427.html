piece of trash

noun
something worthless; of poor quality
Pronunciation: / pis əv træʃ / (AmE), / piːs əv træʃ / (BrE)
an object, idea, or person regarded as worthless or of very poor quality

Examples

A piece of trash item
He couldn't believe his luck when he found a valuable coin among what others called a piece of trash.
Many critics dismissed the film, calling it a piece of trash unworthy of attention.
Maria referred to the broken toy as just another piece of trash in the attic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junk - old or discarded items that are considered useless or of little value
  • synonym: rubbish - things that are thrown away because they are no longer useful; waste material
  • antonym: treasure - something very valuable
