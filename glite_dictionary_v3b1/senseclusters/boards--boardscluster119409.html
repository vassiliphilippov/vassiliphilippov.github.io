boards

noun
barrier around an ice hockey rink
Pronunciation: / bɔrdz / (AmE), / bɔːdz / (BrE)
The wooden or insulated panels that form the outer barrier around an ice hockey rink.

Examples

Hockey boards
He slammed his opponent into the boards during the heated third period.
Spectators pressed up against the boards to get a closer view of the action.
The puck ricocheted off the boards and landed in front of the net.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rink - the ice surface on which ice hockey is played
