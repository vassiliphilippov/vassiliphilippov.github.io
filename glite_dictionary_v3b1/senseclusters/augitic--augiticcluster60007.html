augitic

adjective
containing or relating to augite mineral
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈdʒɪtɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈdʒɪtɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or containing augite, a dark green or black mineral of the pyroxene group
