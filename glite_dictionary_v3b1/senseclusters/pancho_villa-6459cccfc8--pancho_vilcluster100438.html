Pancho Villa

proper noun
Mexican revolutionary leader (1878–1923)
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑntʃoʊ ˈviː.jə / (AmE), / ˈpæntʃəʊ ˈviː.jə / (BrE)
(1878–1923) A Mexican revolutionary leader, noted for his role in the Mexican Revolution and prominent guerrilla warfare against government forces.

Examples

Pancho Villa leader
Pancho Villa became a folk hero in Mexico for his daring raids and revolutionary tactics.
Many historians have studied the impact of Pancho Villa on the outcome of the Mexican Revolution.
The stories of Pancho Villa are still told across northern Mexico and the American Southwest.
