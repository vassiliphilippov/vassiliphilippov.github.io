Pancho Villa

proper noun
Mexican revolutionary leader (1878–1923)

Pronunciation: /ˈpæn.tʃoʊ ˈvɪl.ə/ /; / /ˈpæn.tʃoʊ ˈviː.jə/ (AmE), /ˈpæn.tʃəʊ ˈvɪl.ə/ /; / /ˈpæn.tʃəʊ ˈviː.jə/ (BrE)
