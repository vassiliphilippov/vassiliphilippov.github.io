alkalinization

noun
making more alkaline
8.1
Pronunciation: /æl.kə.lɪ.nə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /æl.kə.lɪn.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /æl.kə.lɪ.nə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /æl.kə.lɪn.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of making something more alkaline or less acidic, usually by increasing its pH

Examples

The alkalinization of water
