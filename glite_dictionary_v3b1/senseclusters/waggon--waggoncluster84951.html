waggon

noun
vehicle for carrying goods, drawn by animals or tractor
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
a heavy vehicle with four wheels, usually pulled by animals or a tractor, used for transporting goods

Examples

A horse-drawn waggon
A horse-drawn waggon is still used in some rural communities.
The farmers loaded the hay onto the waggon and drove to the market.
The museum displayed an old wooden waggon from the 19th century.
