rebirth

noun
the soul's new life in another body after death
1.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈbɜːrθ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈbɜːrθ/ /; / /rə.ˈbɜːrθ/ (AmE), /riː.ˈbɜːθ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈbɜːθ/ /; / /rə.ˈbɜːθ/ (BrE)
the belief or process by which, after death, a soul begins a new life in a different human body; reincarnation

Examples

Belief in rebirth
Many religions teach that rebirth allows the soul to learn and grow over countless lifetimes.
Some people believe that memories of a previous life are possible through rebirth.
The concept of rebirth is central to many Eastern philosophies and spiritual practices.
