rebirth

noun
the soul's new life in another body after death

the belief or process by which, after death, a soul begins a new life in a different human body; reincarnation

Examples

Belief in rebirth
Many religions teach that rebirth allows the soul to learn and grow over countless lifetimes.
Some people believe that memories of a previous life are possible through rebirth.
The concept of rebirth is central to many Eastern philosophies and spiritual practices.

Synonyms

  • reincarnation
    7.1 2/m
    reincarnation is the usual religious word for a soul being born again in a new body, while rebirth is the broader term for that process.
  • transmigration
    9.9 0.2/m
    transmigration means a soul's passage into another body in religious or philosophical thought, while rebirth names the same new-life process.
  • metempsychosis
    9.9 0.04/m
    metempsychosis refers to a soul moving into another human or animal body in older writings, while rebirth focuses on starting a new human life.
