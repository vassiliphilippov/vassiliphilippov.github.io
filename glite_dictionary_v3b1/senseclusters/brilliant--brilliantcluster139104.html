brilliant

adjective
shining intensely; very bright
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪljənt / (AmE), / ˈbrɪljənt / (BrE)
full of light; shining intensely, giving off a strong, bright light

Examples

Brilliant light
A brilliant flash of lightning lit up the entire night sky.
The brilliant sunlight made it difficult to see without sunglasses.
The diamond sparkled with a brilliant glow under the light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzling - so bright as to blind or impress greatly
  • antonym: dim - not bright; lacking in light
