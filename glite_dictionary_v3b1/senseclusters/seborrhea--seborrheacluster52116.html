seborrhea

noun
excessive oiliness of the skin
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛbəˈriːə / (AmE), / ˌsiːbəˈriːə / (BrE)
A medical condition in which overactivity of the sebaceous glands causes the skin to become abnormally oily.

Examples

Oily seborrhea skin
People with seborrhea often experience discomfort and excessive skin shine on their faces.
The dermatologist diagnosed her with seborrhea after noticing persistent oily patches on her scalp.
Topical treatments are available to help control the symptoms of seborrhea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seborrhoeic dermatitis - skin inflammation caused by seborrhea
