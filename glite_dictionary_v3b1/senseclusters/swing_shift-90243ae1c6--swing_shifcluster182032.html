swing shift

noun
evening work shift
6.2
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ ʃɪft / (AmE), / swɪŋ ʃɪft / (BrE)
A work shift that takes place during the evening, typically from late afternoon until midnight.

Examples

The swing shift work
After a month on the swing shift, he found it difficult to adjust to a regular schedule.
Many factories offer a swing shift in addition to the day and night shifts.
She prefers working the swing shift because she can sleep in during the morning.
