idle pulley

noun
pulley for guiding or tensioning a belt
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdəl ˈpʊli / (AmE), / ˈaɪdl ˈpʊli / (BrE)
a pulley mounted on a shaft to press against and guide or tighten a belt in a mechanical system

Examples

An idle pulley guides belt
A misaligned idle pulley can cause the belt to slip off during operation.
Regular inspection of the idle pulley ensures the drive belt runs smoothly and stays at proper tension.
The mechanic replaced the worn idle pulley to fix the squeaking noise in the engine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idler pulley - a pulley used to maintain tension or guide a belt, an alternative form
