hakeem

noun
Muslim or Unani physician
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈkim / (AmE), / hɑːˈkiːm / (BrE)
a physician, especially one who practices traditional Muslim or Unani medicine

Examples

A hakeem doctor

Many people still trust the advice of a hakeem over modern doctors in rural areas.
The village hakeem prescribed herbal remedies for many common ailments.
