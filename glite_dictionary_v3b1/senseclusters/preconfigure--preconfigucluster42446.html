preconfigure

verb
to set up in advance
5.3
Pronunciation: /priː.kən.ˈfɪ.gjər/ /; / /prɪ.kən.ˈfɪ.gjər/ (AmE), /priː.kən.ˈfɪ.gjə/ /; / /prɪ.kən.ˈfɪ.gjə/ (BrE)
to set up or arrange a device, system, or software in advance so it is ready for use

Examples

To preconfigure a system
The IT team will preconfigure your account before you start work.
Many smartphones come preconfigured with popular apps already installed.
You can preconfigure the settings to make setup faster for new users.
The laptops were preconfigured with all the necessary software before being shipped to customers.
You can preconfigure your email account so it is ready to use after installation.
Many routers are preconfigured to work with most internet providers out of the box.
