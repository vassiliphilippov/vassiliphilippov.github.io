docuseries

noun
documentary series
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːkjuˌsɪriz / (AmE), / ˈdɒkjuˌsɪəriːz / (BrE)

Examples

A docuseries on history
The new docuseries explores the history of space exploration.
She binge-watched a fascinating docuseries about famous art heists.
Many streaming platforms now feature original docuseries on a variety of topics.

Related Terms
