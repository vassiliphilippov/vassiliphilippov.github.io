grantmaking

noun
giving financial support
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræntˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːntˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of providing funds or financial support to projects, organizations, or individuals, usually by a foundation or institution

Examples

Grantmaking process
The organization specializes in grantmaking to support local community projects.
Effective grantmaking requires careful evaluation of each applicant's needs and goals.
Many foundations have strict guidelines for their grantmaking activities each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donation - the act of giving money or goods to help a person or organization
  • synonym: funding - the act of providing money for a particular purpose
