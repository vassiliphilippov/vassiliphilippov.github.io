revoice

verb
to make voiced again
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈvɔɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈvɔɪs / (BrE)
to make a sound or consonant voiced again, or to change it from voiceless to voiced, especially in phonetics

Examples

To revoice a consonant
The linguist asked the students to revoice the initial consonant for clearer pronunciation.
In some dialects, speakers may revoice certain sounds at the beginning of words.
The teacher demonstrated how to revoice a voiceless consonant during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • antonym: devoice - to make a sound voiceless or remove voicing
  • synonym: voice - to pronounce a sound using vocal cord vibration
