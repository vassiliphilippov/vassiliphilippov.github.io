pitch

noun
sports field, especially for soccer or rugby
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃ / (AmE), / pɪtʃ / (BrE)
an area of ground specially prepared and marked out for playing sports such as soccer or rugby

Examples

Soccer pitch
He spent an hour marking the lines on the soccer pitch before the match.
Recent heavy rain left the pitch in a muddy, unplayable state.
The teams walked onto the pitch amid the cheers of thousands of fans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: field - an area of grass used for sports, especially in American English
