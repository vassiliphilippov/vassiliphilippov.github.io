psyche

noun
the human mind or soul
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪki / (AmE), / ˈsaɪki /; / ˈsʌɪki / (BrE)
the human mind, soul, or spirit, especially as the center of thought, feeling, and consciousness

Examples

Dreams reveal the hidden landscapes of the psyche.
Years of war left scars on the national psyche.
