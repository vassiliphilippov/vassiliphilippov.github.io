homely

adjective
unattractive in appearance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmli / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) unattractive in appearance; lacking in physical beauty or proportion.

Examples

The homely building stood out among the modern glass towers downtown.
