cemetery

noun
burial ground; place for graves
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛm.əˌtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈsɛm.ɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
An area of land, usually outside a town or city, set aside for the burial of the dead.

Examples

A cemetery area
A new cemetery was established on the outskirts of the growing city.
The old cemetery was surrounded by large oak trees and ancient headstones.
They visited the cemetery every year to place flowers on their grandfather's grave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burial ground - an area of land where people are buried
  • synonym: graveyard - a place, often next to a church, where people are buried
