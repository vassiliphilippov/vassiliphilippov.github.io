pentobarbital sodium

noun
a barbiturate sedative drug
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛntoʊˌbɑːrbɪˌtɑːl ˈsoʊdiəm / (AmE), / ˌpɛntəʊˌbɑːbɪˌtɑːl ˈsəʊdiəm / (BrE)
a barbiturate drug used as a sedative, hypnotic, and antispasmodic, often under the trade name Nembutal

Examples

pentobarbital sodium drug
An overdose of pentobarbital sodium can cause serious side effects and must be administered with care.
The doctor prescribed pentobarbital sodium to help control the patient's seizures.
Veterinarians sometimes use pentobarbital sodium for animal anesthesia.
