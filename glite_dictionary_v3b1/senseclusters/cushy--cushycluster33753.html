cushy

adjective
easy or undemanding (job or situation)
6.5 1.26/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆkuÊƒi / (AmE), / ËˆkÊŠÊƒi / (BrE)
(of a job or situation) undemanding, easy, or requiring little effort and causing little hardship.

Examples

A cushy job

Everyone envies his cushy lifestyle, traveling the world without any real responsibilities.
He landed a cushy job at the bank that pays well and requires little overtime.
