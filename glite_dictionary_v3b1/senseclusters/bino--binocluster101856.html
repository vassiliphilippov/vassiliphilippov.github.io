bino

noun
informal for binoculars
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪnəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for binoculars, a device used to view distant objects with both eyes

Birdwatchers often carry a bino when exploring the forest trails.
