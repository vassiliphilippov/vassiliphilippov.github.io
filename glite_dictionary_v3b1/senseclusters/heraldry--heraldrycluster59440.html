heraldry

noun
the study of coats of arms and armorial bearings
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛr.əl.dri /; / ˈhɛr.əl.dɹi / (AmE), / ˈhɛr.əl.dri /; / ˈhɛr.əld.ri / (BrE)
