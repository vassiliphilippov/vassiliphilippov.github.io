unharness

verb
remove the harness from an animal
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɑrnɪs /; / ʌnˈhɑrnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɑːnɪs / (BrE)
to remove a harness from an animal, especially a horse or other draft animal

Examples

To unharness the horses
After the long journey, they stopped to unharness the tired horses.
She carefully unharnessed her dog once they returned home from the sled ride.
The stablehand was trained to unharness the ponies quickly and efficiently.
After the long journey, the farmer stopped to unharness the horses from the wagon.
The new manager tried to unharness the team's potential by encouraging creative thinking.
It is important to unharness your imagination when working on innovative projects.

  • synonym: release - to set free from confinement or restraint
