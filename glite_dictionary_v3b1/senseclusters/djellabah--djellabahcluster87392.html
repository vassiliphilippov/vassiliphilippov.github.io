djellabah

noun
North African robe
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈlɑːbə / (AmE), / dʒɛˈlæbə / (BrE)
a long, loose-fitting outer robe with a hood, traditionally worn in North Africa

Examples

A djellabah robe
He wore a traditional djellabah to protect himself from the desert sun.
The market was filled with people in colorful djellabahs and headscarves.
She admired the embroidery on the blue djellabah displayed in the shop window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloak - a loose outer garment, often sleeveless
  • synonym: kaftan - a long, loose garment with wide sleeves, worn in various cultures
