presbyope

noun
person with age-related farsightedness
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛz.biˌoʊp / (AmE), / ˈprɛzbɪəʊp / (BrE)
a person who has presbyopia, typically experiencing farsightedness due to aging and loss of lens elasticity
