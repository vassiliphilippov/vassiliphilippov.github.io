Hamilton

proper noun
Lin‑Manuel Miranda's Broadway musical
Pronunciation: / ˈhæməltən / (AmE), / ˈhæm(ə)ltən / (BrE)
A 2015 Broadway musical by Lin-Manuel Miranda that tells the life story of Alexander Hamilton using hip-hop, R&B, and traditional musical theatre styles.
