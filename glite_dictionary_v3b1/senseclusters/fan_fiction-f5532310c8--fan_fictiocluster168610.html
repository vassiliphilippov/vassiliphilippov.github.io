fan fiction

noun
derivative creative work
Pronunciation: / fæn ˈfɪkʃən / (AmE), / fæn ˈfɪkʃən / (BrE)
a story or work created by fans that uses characters or settings from existing books, movies, or shows

Examples

Fan fiction stories
Some reviewers called the new detective novel little more than fan fiction because of its familiar characters.
The film was criticized as fan fiction for relying heavily on established storylines from earlier movies.
He dismissed the sequel as fan fiction that failed to bring anything new to the series.
Many websites allow users to share their fan fiction with a global audience.
She started writing fan fiction based on her favorite science fiction series.
Some fan fiction stories become so popular that they attract thousands of readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fanfic - an informal abbreviation for fan fiction
  • antonym: original - a work that is new and not derived from something else
  • antonym: original fiction - stories with original characters and settings, not based on existing works
  • synonym: pastiche - a work that imitates the style of another artist or period
