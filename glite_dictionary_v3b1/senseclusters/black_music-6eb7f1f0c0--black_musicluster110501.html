black music

noun
music by African-American musicians
1.0
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / blæk ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music created by Black artists, especially genres like blues, jazz, gospel, hip hop, and other styles with roots in Black communities in the US and around the world
