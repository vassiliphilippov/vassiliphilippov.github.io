amphibious landing

noun
military invasion by land, sea, and air
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiəs ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / æmˈfɪbiəs ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
a coordinated military operation in which forces land on a hostile shore using combined land, sea, and air resources, typically as part of an invasion

Examples

An amphibious landing operation
Historical accounts often study the logistics of a successful amphibious landing.
The general devised a complex amphibious landing to surprise the enemy forces.
Thousands of troops participated in the amphibious landing at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landing operation - a military action involving the movement of troops onto land
