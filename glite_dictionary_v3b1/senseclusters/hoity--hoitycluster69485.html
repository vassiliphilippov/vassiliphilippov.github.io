hoity

adjective
proud or snobbish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔɪti / (AmE), / ˈhɔɪti / (BrE)
behaving in a proud, snobbish, or superior manner

Examples

Hoity attitude
She acted so hoity at the party that people avoided talking to her.
His hoity remarks made everyone feel uncomfortable during the meeting.
Don't be so hoity just because you won the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
