malrotation

noun
tooth misalignment
Pronunciation: / ˌmælroʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmalrəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an abnormal rotation of a tooth around its long axis, causing misalignment in the dental arch

Examples

Tooth malrotation
The dentist identified malrotation of the canine tooth during the examination.
Braces are often used to correct malrotation and restore proper alignment.
Severe malrotation can affect both the appearance and function of the teeth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malocclusion - improper alignment of the teeth when the jaws are closed
  • antonym: rotation - the act of turning around an axis
