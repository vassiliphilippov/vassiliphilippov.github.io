anecdotage

noun
collection of anecdotes
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪkˌdoʊtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈænɪkˌdəʊtɪdʒ / (BrE)
a collection of amusing or interesting anecdotes, often about a person's life or experiences

Examples

A rich anecdotage
The author's anecdotage provided a humorous glimpse into her years on the stage.
He entertained the dinner guests with a lively anecdotage from his travels around the world.
Her grandfather's anecdotage was filled with stories from his childhood in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anecdotes - short, amusing or interesting stories about real incidents or people
  • synonym: memoir - a written account of someone's personal experiences
