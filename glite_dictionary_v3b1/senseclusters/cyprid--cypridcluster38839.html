cyprid

noun
freshwater seed shrimp
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪprɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪprɪd / (BrE)
a small freshwater bivalved crustacean, also known as a seed shrimp, belonging to the family Cyprididae

Examples

Freshwater cyprid crustacean
The scientist examined the cyprid under a microscope to study its shell structure.
A cyprid can often be found in ponds and slow-moving streams.
Children collected water samples and discovered a tiny cyprid swimming among the plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ostracod - a small crustacean with a bivalve shell, also called seed shrimp
