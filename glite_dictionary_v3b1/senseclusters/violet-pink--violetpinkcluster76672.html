violet-pink

adjective
pink color with a tinge of lavender
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪələtˌpɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələtˌpɪŋk / (BrE)
A shade of pink tinged with lavender or violet, resulting in a faintly purplish pink color.

Examples

Violet-pink dress
She wore a dress of violet-pink satin to the party.
The artist chose a violet-pink shade for the sunset in her painting.
These flowers have delicate violet-pink petals that stand out in the garden.

  • degree lower: rose - a pinkish-red color
