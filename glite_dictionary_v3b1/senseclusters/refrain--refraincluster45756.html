refrain

noun
repeated part of a song
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfreɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈfreɪn / (BrE)
the part of a song that is repeated after each verse, often sung by a group of singers with or after the soloist

Examples

Sing the refrain
After each stanza, the refrain brought a sense of unity to the performance.
Everyone joined in during the refrain, filling the hall with music.
The soloist sang the verse, and the choir joined for the refrain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chorus - the part of a song repeated after each verse; refrain
