Sapindaceae

noun
family of trees and shrubs including lychee and maple
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpɪnˈdeɪsiˌi /; / ˌsæpɪnˈdeɪsi.i / (AmE), / ˌsapɪnˈdeɪsɪˌiː /; / ˌsapɪnˈdeɪsɪ.iː / (BrE)
a family of tropical and subtropical trees and shrubs, often with leathery fruits and sometimes toxic, including plants like lychee and maple
