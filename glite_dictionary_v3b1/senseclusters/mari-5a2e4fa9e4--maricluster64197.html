Mari

noun
member of a rural Finno-Ugric people in Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːri / (AmE), / ˈmɑːri / (BrE)
a member of a rural Finno-Ugric people living in eastern Russia, mainly along the Volga and Kama rivers

Examples

Mari people
A group of Mari gathered to celebrate a traditional festival in the village.
Several Mari have been recognized for their contributions to Russian folk music.
The Mari people have their own distinct language and traditions.
