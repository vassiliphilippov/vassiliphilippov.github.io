nonnaturalness

noun
lack of naturalness
8.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈnætʃrəlnəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈnætʃrəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unnatural or not appearing natural

Examples

The nonnaturalness of landscape
The nonnaturalness of the landscape made it clear that it had been heavily modified by humans.
Some viewers disliked the nonnaturalness of the dialogue in the film.
Her performance was criticized for its nonnaturalness and lack of genuine emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artificiality - the quality of being artificial rather than natural
  • antonym: naturalness - the quality of being natural or genuine
