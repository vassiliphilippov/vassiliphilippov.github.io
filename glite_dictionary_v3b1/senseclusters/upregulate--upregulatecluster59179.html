upregulate

verb
to increase cellular activity
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʌp.rə.ɡjə.leɪt/ /; / /ˈʌp.rɪ.ɡjə.leɪt/ (AmE), /ˈʌp.rə.ɡjʊ.leɪt/ /; / /ˈʌp.rə.ɡjə.leɪt/ (BrE)
to increase the quantity or activity of a molecule, gene, or process within a cell

Examples

To upregulate gene activity
The new medication can upregulate the expression of certain genes in the liver.
Researchers found that exercise may upregulate proteins involved in muscle growth.
Some environmental factors can upregulate the immune response in the body.
