fiddlesticks

noun
bow for a fiddle
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪdəlˌstɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɪdəlˌstɪks / (BrE)
the bow or bows used to play a fiddle or similar violin-like instrument

Examples

A violin fiddlestick
The musician carefully tightened the horsehair on his fiddlesticks before the concert began.
She bought new fiddlesticks for her violin after the old ones wore out.
During the lesson, the teacher explained how to hold the fiddlesticks properly for better sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - a curved stick with horsehair used to play string instruments
