benthopelagic

adjective
near bottom and open water
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnθoʊpəˈlædʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɛnθəʊpəˈlædʒɪk / (BrE)
living or occurring near the bottom and in the open water of a sea or lake

Examples

Benthopelagic zone animals
Many benthopelagic species can be found feeding both near the seabed and in the open water.
The benthopelagic zone supports a unique community of fish adapted to both habitats.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of benthopelagic organisms in the deep lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benthic - relating to the bottom of a sea or lake
  • synonym: pelagic - relating to the open sea or water away from the shore and bottom
