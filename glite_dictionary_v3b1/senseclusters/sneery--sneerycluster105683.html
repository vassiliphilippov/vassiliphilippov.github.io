sneery

adjective
mocking or contemptuous
Pronunciation: / ˈsniːri / (AmE), / ˈsniːri / (BrE)
showing or expressing a sneering, mocking, or contemptuous attitude

Examples

A sneery look
She gave him a sneery look when he tried to explain his mistake.
His sneery comments made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
The critic's sneery tone was obvious in her review of the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mocking - making fun of someone in a cruel or unkind way
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness or honor towards someone
  • synonym: scornful - showing or feeling contempt or disdain
