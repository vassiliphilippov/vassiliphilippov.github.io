projection

noun
the quality of sound carrying over a distance
6.3
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the quality of a sound that makes it clear and able to carry across a distance; the ability to make one's voice or music easily heard by an audience

Examples

Voice projection
Her strong projection made it easy for everyone at the back to hear her speech.
In musical theater, learning proper projection is essential for performers to reach the audience.
The singer's projection filled the entire concert hall, even without a microphone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear, easily heard, or understood
  • synonym: resonance - the quality in a sound of being deep, full, and reverberating
