productivity

noun
rate of producing goods or services
5.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /prə.dʌk.ˈtɪv.iː/ /; / /proʊ.dʌk.ˈtɪv.iː/ /; / /prɑː.dʌk.ˈtɪv.iː/ (AmE), /prɒ.dʌk.ˈtɪv.iː/ /; / /prə.dʌk.ˈtɪv.iː/ (BrE)
the rate at which goods or services are produced, especially compared to the amount of work, time, or resources used
