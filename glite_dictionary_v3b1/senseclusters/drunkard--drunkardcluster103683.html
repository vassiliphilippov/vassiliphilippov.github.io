drunkard

noun
a habitual excessive drinker
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌŋ.kɚd / (AmE), / ˈdrʌŋ.kəd / (BrE)
a person who habitually drinks large amounts of alcohol and is frequently drunk

Examples

A drunkard man
Everyone in the village knew him as a drunkard who could not keep a job.
She refused to marry a drunkard, no matter how wealthy he was.
The novel tells the tragic story of a drunkard and his family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcoholic - a person addicted to alcohol
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcohol
