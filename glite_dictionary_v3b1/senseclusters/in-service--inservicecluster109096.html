in-service

adjective
employee training
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
relating to training or education provided to employees while they are working in their jobs

Examples

In-service training
The company offers regular in-service workshops to help employees develop new skills.
All nurses are required to complete annual in-service training sessions.
During the in-service day, teachers learned about new classroom technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on-the-job - taking place while working at a job
  • antonym: pre-service - occurring before starting a job or service
