smokehouse

noun
building for curing meat or fish with smoke
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building or room where meat or fish is cured with smoke.

Examples

A smokehouse building
The aroma coming from the smokehouse could be smelled across the countryside.
The old farm has a restored smokehouse where hams are cured every year.
They built a small smokehouse to preserve fish during the winter months.
