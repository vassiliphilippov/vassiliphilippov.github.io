pond apple

noun
tropical American evergreen tree with edible fruit
4.1
Pronunciation: / pɑnd ˈæpəl / (AmE), / pɒnd ˈæpəl / (BrE)
a small evergreen tree of tropical America (Annona glabra) with edible fruit, primarily used as grafting stock

Examples

Pond apple tree
Farmers often use pond apple trees as rootstock for grafting other fruit varieties.
The pond apple thrives in wet, marshy soils along riverbanks and swamps.
The fruit of the pond apple is edible, but more often it is valued for its resilient root system.
