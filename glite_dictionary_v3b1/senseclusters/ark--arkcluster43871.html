ark

noun
Noah's boat in the biblical flood
Pronunciation: / ɑrk / (AmE), / ɑːk / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the large boat built by Noah to save his family and two of every kind of animal from the flood.

Examples

Noah's ark
Scientists discovered an ancient structure said to resemble the biblical ark.
The children's book included illustrations of animals boarding the ark.
The story of Noah's ark is found in the Book of Genesis.
