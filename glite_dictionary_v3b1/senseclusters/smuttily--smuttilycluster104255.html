smuttily

adverb
in an obscene or indecent manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌt̬.ɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈsmʌt.ɪ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is obscene, indecent, or lewd.

Examples

To look smuttily
She glanced smuttily at him after making a double entendre.
The comedian spoke smuttily, drawing laughter from some and discomfort from others.
The story was written smuttily, full of suggestive jokes and innuendo.
