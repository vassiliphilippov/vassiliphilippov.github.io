methodologically

adverb
in a systematic or methodological manner
2.3 6.2
Pronunciation: /meθ.ə.də.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /meθ.ə.də.ˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is based on a system of methods or principles, especially in academic or scientific work

Examples

To study methodologically

To ensure valid results, the experiment must be conducted methodologically.
