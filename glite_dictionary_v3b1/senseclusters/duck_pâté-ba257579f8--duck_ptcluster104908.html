duck pâté

noun
spread made from duck liver
Pronunciation: / dʌk pæˈteɪ / (AmE), / dʌk pæˈteɪ / (BrE)
A rich, spreadable paste made from duck liver, often flavored with herbs and spices.

Examples

A duck pate spread
For the appetizer, we ordered the duck pâté with toasted baguette slices.
She prefers duck pâté over chicken liver pâté for its richer flavor.
The gourmet store sells homemade duck pâté during the holiday season.
