Synonyms

    1.0 62/m
    inform simply means giving someone facts or news in general, while notify implies an official or formal announcement to them.
  • advise
    2.7 18/m
    advise often means giving guidance or a formal warning, while notify focuses on officially telling someone facts.
  • serve notice
    6.5 0.30/m

  • give notice
    3.7 0.79/m
