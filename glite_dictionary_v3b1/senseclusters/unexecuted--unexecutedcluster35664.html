unexecuted

adjective
not carried out
6.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪk.ˈsek.juː.tɪd/ /; / /ən.ɪk.ˈsek.juː.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪk.ˈsek.juː.tɪd/ /; / /ən.ɪk.ˈsek.juː.tɪd/ (BrE)
not carried out, completed, or made legally effective (especially about contracts or documents)

Examples
