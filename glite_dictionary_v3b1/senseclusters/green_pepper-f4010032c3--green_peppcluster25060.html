green pepper

noun
unripe sweet pepper (vegetable)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /griːn ˈpep.ər/ (AmE), /griːn ˈpep.ə/ (BrE)
A variety of sweet pepper that is harvested while still green and unripe; it turns red when fully ripe.

Examples

A green pepper fruit
As it ripens, a green pepper will gradually turn red on the plant.
I added chopped green pepper to the salad for extra crunch.
Some people prefer using green pepper in stir-fry because of its mild flavor.
