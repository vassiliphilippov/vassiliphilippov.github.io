leno

noun
open-weave fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈliːnəʊ / (BrE)
a lightweight, open-weave fabric made by twisting adjacent warp yarns to create a mesh-like texture

Examples

A leno fabric
The summer blouse was sewn from delicate leno to keep it cool and breathable.
Designers often choose leno for curtains because of its airy, see-through quality.
She admired the intricate pattern of the leno used in the scarf.
