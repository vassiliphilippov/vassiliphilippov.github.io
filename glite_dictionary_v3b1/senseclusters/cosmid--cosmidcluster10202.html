cosmid

noun
gene-cloning vector in genetics
7.3 9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːz.mɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkɒz.mɪd/ (BrE)
(genetics) a type of cloning vector derived from a bacteriophage, used to transfer and clone genes or gene fragments in genetic engineering.

Examples

A cosmid vector
