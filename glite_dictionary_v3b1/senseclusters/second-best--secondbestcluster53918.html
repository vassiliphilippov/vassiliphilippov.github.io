second-best

adjective, adverb
next in rank after the best
Pronunciation: / ˈsek.ənd ˌbɛst /; / ˌsɛkənd ˈbɛst / (AmE), / ˌsek.ənd ˈbest /; / ˌsɛkənd ˈbɛst / (BrE)
next after the best in quality, achievement, or status; not the best but better than the rest

No one likes feeling like the second-best choice.
They fought hard but finished second-best to their rivals.
