cinnamon snail

noun
sweet pastry with cinnamon and sugar
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəmən sneɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən sneɪl / (BrE)
A sweet baked pastry made from rolled dough spread with cinnamon and sugar, sometimes with raisins, sliced before baking.

Examples

A cinnamon snail pastry
I baked a batch of cinnamon snails for the holiday brunch.
She enjoyed a fresh cinnamon snail with her morning coffee.
The bakery’s signature pastry is the cinnamon snail, filled with plump raisins.
