commoditize

verb
to make common
7.3
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɑːdəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / kəˈmɒdətaɪz / (BrE)
to make a product or service common and interchangeable, often reducing its uniqueness or value

Examples

To commoditize products
As more companies enter the market, their products become commoditized and compete mainly on price.
Streaming services risk being commoditized if they do not offer unique content.
The smartphone industry has largely commoditized features that were once considered innovative.

Related Terms

  • antonym: differentiate - to make something distinct or unique
  • synonym: standardize - to make things conform to a standard
