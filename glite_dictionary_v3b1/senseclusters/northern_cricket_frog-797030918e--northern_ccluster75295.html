northern cricket frog

noun
a frog species of eastern and central US
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɹðɚn ˈkrɪkɪt frɑg / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈkrɪkɪt frɒg / (BrE)
a small species of cricket frog native to the eastern and central United States, known for its jumping ability and distinctive call

Examples

The northern cricket frog is often found near ponds and streams in the Midwest.
