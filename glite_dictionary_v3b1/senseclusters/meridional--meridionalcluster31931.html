meridional

adjective
relating to a meridian (north-south direction)
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /mə.rɪ.ˈdoʊ.nəl/ /; / /mɪ.rɪ.ˈdɪə.nəl/ (AmE), /mə.rɪ.ˈdɒ.nəl/ /; / /mɪ.rɪ.ˈdɪə.nəl/ (BrE)
of or relating to a meridian; situated or moving along a north-south direction, especially in geography or astronomy.

Examples

Meridional wind direction
In meteorology, meridional winds are those that flow from north to south.
The meridional distance between the two cities was carefully measured by the team of geographers.
The ancient astronomers tracked the meridional passage of the sun across the sky.
