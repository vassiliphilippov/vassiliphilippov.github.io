camerawork

noun
technique of filming
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmərəˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈkæmərəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the way a camera is operated to film or photograph a scene, especially in movies or television

Examples

Creative camerawork
The creative camerawork in the documentary made the story more engaging.
Critics praised the film's camerawork for its innovative use of angles and lighting.
Good camerawork can turn an ordinary scene into something visually stunning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinematography - the art or technique of motion-picture photography
  • synonym: photography - the art or practice of taking and processing photographs
