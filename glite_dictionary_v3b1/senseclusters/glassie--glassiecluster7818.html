glassie

noun
slang for glass bottle
a slang term for a glass bottle of drink or a drink served in a glass, especially in a bar or pub

Examples

A cold glassie
He ordered a cold glassie at the local pub after work.
Would you like a glassie or a can with your meal?
The bartender handed her a fresh glassie filled with cider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottle - a container, typically made of glass, used to hold liquids
  • synonym: pint - a glass of beer, especially in a pub
