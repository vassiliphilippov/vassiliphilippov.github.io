garret

noun
small room or space under a roof
7.0 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡær.ət / (AmE), / ˈɡær.ɪt / (BrE)
a small room or open space at the top of a house, just below the roof, often used for storage or as a living space

Examples

A garret room
After the fire, they stored most of their books in the garret at the top of the house.
Old trunks and forgotten paintings were discovered in the dusty garret above the main floor.
She rented a tiny garret in Paris to write her novel.
