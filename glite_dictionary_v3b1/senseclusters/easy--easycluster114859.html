easy

Examples

  • uncomplicated
    3.2 1.67/m
    uncomplicated is free from parts or steps that confuse you, while easy means it does not need much skill.
  • undemanding
    3.5 0.27/m
    undemanding asks little time, skill, or patience, often used for people or tasks, while easy is more general.
  • effortless
    2.6 2.58/m

  • elementary
    1.0 1.23/m

  • nonchallenging

    nonchallenging describes tasks that lack difficulty or surprise, often in formal lists, while easy is the common everyday word.
