bottom lurker

noun
fish living on the bottom of water
8.6 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.təm ˈlɜːr.kər/ (AmE), /ˈbɒt.əm ˈlɜː.kə/ (BrE)
a fish that lives close to or hides on the bottom of a body of water, often remaining motionless to avoid predators or ambush prey

Examples

Bottom lurker fish
Aquariums often contain bottom lurkers to help keep the tank clean and balanced.
Some bottom lurkers use camouflage to blend in with sand or rocks on the lakebed.
The angler was hoping to catch a bottom lurker like a catfish or a flounder.
