nat

noun
Burmese spirit
Pronunciation: / næt / (AmE), / næt / (BrE)
a spirit in traditional Burmese (Myanmar) religion believed to influence human affairs and natural events

Examples

Burmese nat spirit
Many people in Myanmar make offerings to a nat to seek protection and good fortune.
The festival honored the village's guardian nat with music and traditional dances.
Legends say that each mountain in the region is home to a powerful nat.

  • synonym: spirit - a supernatural being or force
