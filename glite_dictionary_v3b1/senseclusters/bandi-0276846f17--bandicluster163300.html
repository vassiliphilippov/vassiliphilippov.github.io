Bandi

proper noun
North Korean writer
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːndi / (AmE), / ˈbændi / (BrE)
the pen name of a North Korean writer known for stories depicting everyday life in North Korea

Examples

Bandi the writer

Bandi remains anonymous to protect his identity from the North Korean authorities.
