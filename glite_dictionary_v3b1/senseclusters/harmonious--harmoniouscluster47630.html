harmonious

adjective
well-matched; blending nicely
Pronunciation: / hɑrˈmoʊniəs /; / hɑːrˈmoʊniəs / (AmE), / hɑːˈməʊniəs /; / hɑːˈmɒniəs / (BrE)
having parts or elements that fit or blend together in a pleasing or consistent way

The design of the new extension was harmonious with the existing building.
