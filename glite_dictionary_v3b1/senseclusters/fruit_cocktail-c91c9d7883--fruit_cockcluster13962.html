fruit cocktail

noun
mixture of diced or sliced fruits
Pronunciation: / fruːt ˈkɑkˌteɪl / (AmE), / fruːt ˈkɒkteɪl / (BrE)
A mixture of pieces of various kinds of fruit, usually served as a dessert or in syrup.

Examples

A fruit cocktail dessert
For breakfast, he enjoyed yogurt topped with fresh fruit cocktail.
She served a bowl of fruit cocktail as a refreshing dessert after dinner.
The canned fruit cocktail contained peaches, pears, and grapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruit salad - a dish consisting of mixed pieces of fruit, sometimes with added sugar or syrup
