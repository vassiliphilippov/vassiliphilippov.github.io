unfledged

adjective
arrow: without feathers
9.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflɛdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈflɛdʒd / (BrE)
not having feathers or not fully developed (used for birds or things like arrows)

Examples

Unfledged bird

It is dangerous to use an unfledged arrow, as it will not travel accurately.

Rescuers carefully placed the unfledged birds back in their nest.
She found an unfledged robin on the ground beneath the tree.
The unfledged chicks huddled together for warmth in the nest.
