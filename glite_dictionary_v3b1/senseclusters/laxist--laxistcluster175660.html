laxist

adjective
permissive or tolerant
Pronunciation: / ˈlæksɪst / (AmE), / ˈlæksɪst / (BrE)
not strict; showing a permissive or tolerant attitude toward rules or standards

Examples

Laxist rules
The school was criticized for its laxist policies on student attendance.
A laxist attitude toward deadlines can lead to missed opportunities.
Some parents are more laxist about enforcing household rules than others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lenient - not strict; allowing a lot of freedom
  • antonym: strict - demanding that rules are closely followed
