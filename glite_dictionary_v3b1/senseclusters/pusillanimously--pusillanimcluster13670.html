In a manner that shows a lack of courage or determination; timidly or cowardly.

Examples

To pusillanimously avoid conflict
He pusillanimously declined to confront his opponent during the debate.
The leader retreated pusillanimously in the face of criticism, refusing to defend her position.
The soldiers pusillanimously abandoned their posts without putting up any resistance.
