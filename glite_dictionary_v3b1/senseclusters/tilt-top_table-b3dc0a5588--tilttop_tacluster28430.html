tilt-top table

noun
a table with a hinged tilting top
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪlt tɑp ˈteɪbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtɪlt tɒp ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a small table with a top that can be tilted to a vertical position, usually supported by a central pedestal

Examples

A tilt-top table upright
After the meal, he tilted the tilt-top table upright to make more room for dancing.
She admired the craftsmanship of the mahogany tilt-top table in the living room.
The antique tilt-top table in the corner can be folded up to save space.

Related Terms
