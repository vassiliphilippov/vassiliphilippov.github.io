signal box

noun
railway building for controlling train signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nəl bɑks / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nəl bɒks / (BrE)
a building from which the signals and points on a railway are controlled to direct train movements

Examples

A signal box building
A restored signal box is now a small museum open to visitors.
The old signal box at the station was closed when the lines were automated.
The operator in the signal box coordinated several trains passing through the junction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signal tower - a structure from which railway signals are operated
