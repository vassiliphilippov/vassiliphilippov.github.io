globe thistle

noun
prickly plant with spherical bluish flower heads
Pronunciation: / ɡloʊb ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / ɡləʊb ˈθɪsəl / (BrE)
Any of various prickly-leaved plants of the genus Echinops, with dense, spherical heads of bluish flowers.

Examples

A globe thistle flower
A single globe thistle added striking color to the garden in midsummer.
She planted several globe thistles along the fence for their ornamental value.
The bluish flower heads of the globe thistle attract many bees and butterflies.
