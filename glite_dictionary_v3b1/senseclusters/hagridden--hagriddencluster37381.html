hagridden

adjective
tormented by nightmares or fears
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæɡˌrɪd.n̩ / (AmE), / ˈhæɡˌrɪd.n̩ / (BrE)
Tormented or harassed by persistent nightmares or oppressive and unreasonable fears.

Examples

Hagridden by nightmares
During her illness, she was hagridden by irrational fears that would not leave her.
He looked hagridden after weeks of torment from his recurring dreams.
The poet described himself as hagridden, unable to escape visions that plagued his sleep.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carefree - free from worry or anxiety
  • synonym: haunted - troubled or disturbed by something, especially memories or fears
