Examples

Retroactive law effect
Her promotion was made retroactive to January, so she received additional pay.

Synonyms

  • ex post facto
    9.9 0.28/m
    ex post facto applies to laws or decisions that affect events before they were made, while retroactive describes actions taking effect from an earlier date.
  • retro
    7.2 3.01/m
    retro refers to a law, payment, or policy that takes effect from a past date, while retroactive describes laws, decisions, or actions affecting earlier events.
