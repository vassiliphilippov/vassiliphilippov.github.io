top

verb
cut the top part off
4.1 1.48/m
Pronunciation: / tÉ‘p / (AmE), / tÉ’p / (BrE)
to cut the top part off something, especially a tree or bush

Examples

To top a tree
After several years, they decided to top the tall pine trees.
The gardener will top the hedge to keep it neat.
You should top the bushes before new growth appears in the spring.

Synonyms

  • poll
    8.3 0.02/m
    poll means cutting off the upper branches or horns of something, while top refers to removing a plant's highest part.
  • pinch
    6.1 0.56/m
    pinch means removing a plant's growing tip by hand to make it bushier, while top means cutting off the plant's highest part.
  • lop
    4.2 0.34/m
    lop means cutting off large branches to tidy a tree, while top means removing only the plant's highest part.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prune - to trim a tree, bush, or plant by cutting away dead or overgrown branches
