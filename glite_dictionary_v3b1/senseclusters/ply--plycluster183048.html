ply

verb
join by twisting or weaving
Pronunciation: / plaɪ / (AmE), / plaɪ / (BrE)
to join together layers, strands, or threads by twisting, weaving, or molding

Examples

To ply threads together
He spent the afternoon plying strips of fabric into a colorful rug.
She learned to ply fibers together to make sturdy rope.
The craftsman plied several layers of wood to create a strong board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twist - to form into a spiral by winding together
  • synonym: weave - to form fabric by interlacing threads
