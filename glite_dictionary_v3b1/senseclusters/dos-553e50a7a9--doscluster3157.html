DoS

noun
denial-of-service attack
7.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / di o ä …õs / (AmE), / diÀê …ô ä …õs / (BrE)
(computing) an attack that makes an online service unavailable by overwhelming it with traffic or resource requests.

Examples

DoS attack online
The site went down after a DoS attack overwhelmed its servers.
Security teams detected a large-scale DoS that saturated the company's bandwidth for hours.
To protect the network, engineers deployed filters to block incoming DoS traffic during the incident.

Synonyms
