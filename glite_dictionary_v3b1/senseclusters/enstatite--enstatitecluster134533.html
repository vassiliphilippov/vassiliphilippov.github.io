enstatite

noun
magnesium silicate mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnstəˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛnstətaɪt / (BrE)
a magnesium silicate mineral (MgSiO3) of the pyroxene group, found in meteorites and certain rocks
