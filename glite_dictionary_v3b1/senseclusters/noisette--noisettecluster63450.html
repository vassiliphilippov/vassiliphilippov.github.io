noisette

noun
small meat cut or hazelnut dish
8.8
Pronunciation: / nwɑːˈzɛt / (AmE), / nwɑːˈzɛt / (BrE)
a small round piece of meat, especially lamb, or a dish flavored with hazelnuts

Examples

Lamb noisette
She ordered the lamb noisette at the French restaurant for a special treat.
The chef prepared a chicken noisette with a creamy hazelnut sauce.
For dessert, they enjoyed a chocolate cake topped with a noisette glaze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medallion - a small, round piece of meat
