merry-go-round

noun
rotating website images/banners
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈme.rɪ.ɡoʊ.raʊnd/ /; / /ˈmeɪ.rɪ.ɡoʊ.raʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈme.rɪ.ɡəʊ.raʊnd/ (BrE)
a rotating sequence of images, banners, or content blocks on a website or app, usually cycling automatically or by user control.

Examples

Website merry-go-round
The homepage features a merry-go-round of rotating banners that link to current promotions.
Designers often replace heavy merry-go-round components with accessible, keyboard-friendly alternatives for mobile users.
When too large, the auto-playing merry-go-round distracted users and slowed the page's loading time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carousel - a web UI component that displays rotating images or content; synonymous with merry-go-round in digital contexts
  • synonym: slider - a component that slides or cycles through content items on a webpage; similar to a carousel
