PO

noun
noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard
Pronunciation: / pi oʊ / (AmE), / piː əʊ / (BrE)
a noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard with a rank comparable to sergeant in the Army

Examples

Navy PO
After ten years of service, she was promoted to PO in the Coast Guard.
As a PO, he was responsible for the discipline and training of sailors.
The PO gave clear instructions to the new recruits.
