Synonyms

  • counteraction
    6.3 0.09/m
    counteraction is an action taken to oppose or reduce an effect, while neutralization often means canceling an earlier action's force or result.
  • nullification
    8.6 0.22/m
    nullification is a formal act that makes a law or decision void, while neutralization covers canceling many kinds of effects or forces.
