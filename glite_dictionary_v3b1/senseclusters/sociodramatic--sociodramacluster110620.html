sociodramatic

adjective
relating to sociodrama
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊdrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsi.əʊdrəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to acting out social situations or roles, especially to explore feelings, relationships, or improve social skills
