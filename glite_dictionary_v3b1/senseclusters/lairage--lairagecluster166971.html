lairage

noun
temporary animal holding
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈleərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a place where animals are kept temporarily before being slaughtered or transported

Examples

The lairage area
The cattle waited in the lairage before being moved to the abattoir.
Inspectors checked the lairage to ensure the animals had enough space and water.
After arriving at the port, the sheep were kept in lairage overnight before transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abattoir - a slaughterhouse where animals are killed for meat
  • synonym: pen - an enclosure for holding animals
