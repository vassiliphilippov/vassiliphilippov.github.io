bark louse

noun
insect living on plant bark
Pronunciation: / bɑrk laʊs / (AmE), / bɑːk laʊs / (BrE)
a small insect that lives on the bark of plants, feeding chiefly on fungi, algae, and decaying plant material

Examples

A bark louse insect
After pruning the branches, he noticed several bark lice moving along the trunk.
Gardeners often overlook bark lice because they are harmless to healthy plants.
The rough bark of the old tree was teeming with bark lice when examined closely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booklouse - a small insect closely related to the bark louse, often found in books and paper products
