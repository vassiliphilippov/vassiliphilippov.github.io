bailiwick

noun
district of authority or specialty
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪlɪˌwɪk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪlɪˌwɪk / (BrE)
the district or area within which a bailiff or official has jurisdiction or authority; a person's particular area of interest, skill, or responsibility

Examples

Her bailiwick of science
Matters of science fall within her bailiwick.
Tax collection was once the bailiwick of the local sheriff.
The court had no authority outside its bailiwick.

  • synonym: jurisdiction - the official power to make legal decisions and judgments
