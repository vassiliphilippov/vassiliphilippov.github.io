squatting

noun
a leg-strengthening exercise
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɑtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskwɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
an exercise in which a person repeatedly bends their knees to lower their body into a crouching position and then stands up again, typically to strengthen the leg muscles

Examples

Gym squatting exercise
He incorporates squatting into his fitness routine to build stronger thighs.
Many athletes use weighted squatting for increased strength and power.
The trainer demonstrated proper squatting technique to avoid knee injuries.
