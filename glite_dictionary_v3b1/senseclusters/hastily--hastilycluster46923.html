hastily

adverb
in a hurried or hasty manner
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪstɪli / (AmE), / ˈheɪstɪli / (BrE)
in a hurried or hasty manner; done quickly and often without care or thought

Examples

To hastily finish
Realizing he was late, he hastily finished his breakfast.
She hastily packed her bags and rushed out the door.
The report was hastily written and contained many errors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carefully - done with care and attention
  • synonym: hurriedly - in a hurried manner
