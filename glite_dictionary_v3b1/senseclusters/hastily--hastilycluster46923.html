Examples

To hastily finish

Synonyms

  • hurriedly
    3.2 3.82/m
    hurriedly means acting quickly with little care, while hastily often also suggests rushing and a lack of thought.
  • in haste
    2.8 0.50/m
    in haste describes doing something quickly, often carelessly, while hastily focuses on the hurried manner of the action.
  • precipitately
    8.3 0.07/m
    precipitately is done very quickly and without thought, while hastily is more general and fits many hurried actions.
  • rashly
    5.6 0.32/m
    rashly means acting without care and in a risky way, while hastily mainly means acting quickly and often carelessly.
