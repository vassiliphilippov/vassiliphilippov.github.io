maulstick

noun
artist's stick for steadying hand
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔlˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɔːlstɪk / (BrE)
A long stick with a padded head, used by artists to support the hand holding the brush while painting.

Examples

A maulstick for painting
She always uses a maulstick when working on fine details in her portraits.
The old maulstick in his studio was covered with paint from years of use.
The painter rested his hand on the maulstick to achieve delicate brushstrokes.
