sporadic

adjective
irregular; scattered in occurrence
9.5
Pronunciation: / spəˈrædɪk / (AmE), / spəˈradɪk / (BrE)
Occurring at irregular intervals or only in a few places; scattered or isolated.

Examples

A sporadic event
Her attendance at meetings has been sporadic lately due to her busy travel schedule.
Reports of the rare bird's sightings remained sporadic over the last decade.
The company faced sporadic interruptions in its power supply throughout the week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frequent - occurring often or at regular intervals
  • degree lower: occasional - happening from time to time, less regular than frequent
