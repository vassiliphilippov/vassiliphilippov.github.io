long-suffering

adjective
patiently enduring pain or trouble
3.8
Pronunciation: / lɔŋˈsʌfərɪŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋˈsʌfərɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Long-suffering wife

He admired his long-suffering friend, who never spoke a word of complaint.
The long-suffering teacher listened calmly as her students argued yet again.
