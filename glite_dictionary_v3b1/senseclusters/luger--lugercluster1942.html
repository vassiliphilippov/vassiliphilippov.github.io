luger

noun
person who races the luge
Pronunciation: / ˈluːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈluːɡə / (BrE)
a person who competes in luge racing, a winter sport where athletes slide down an icy track on a small sled

Examples

A luger on sled
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a champion luger and competing for her country.
During the Olympics, the spotlight often falls on the daring lugers from around the world.
The experienced luger completed the icy course in record time.
