concert

noun
musical performance by musicians or singers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsɚt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsət / (BrE)
a performance of music by musicians or singers, usually given in public and not involving theatrical staging

Examples

A live concert
The concert began with a lively orchestral piece that thrilled the audience.
Tickets for the band's concert sold out within minutes.
We're going to a concert at the park this weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recital - a musical performance given by one performer or a small group
