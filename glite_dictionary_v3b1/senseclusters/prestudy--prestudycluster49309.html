prestudy

verb
to study in advance
5.3
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈstʌ.diː/ /; / /ˈpriː.stʌ.diː/ (AmE), /priː.ˈstʌ.diː/ /; / /ˈpriː.stʌ.diː/ (BrE)

Examples

Students who prestudy often feel more confident during class discussions.
