obsolesce

verb
become obsolete
Pronunciation: / ˌɑb.səˈlɛs / (AmE), / ˌɒb.səˈlɛs / (BrE)
to become obsolete or fall into disuse

Examples

To obsolesce gradually
If a law is no longer effective, it may gradually obsolesce and fall out of use.
Many traditions obsolesce over generations, replaced by new customs and practices.
Some technologies quickly obsolesce as new innovations emerge in the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: become obsolete - lose usefulness or no longer in use
  • synonym: outdate - become outdated or obsolete
