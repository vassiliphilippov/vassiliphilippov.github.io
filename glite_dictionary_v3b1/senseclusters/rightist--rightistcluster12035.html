rightist

noun
a member of a right-wing political group
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or is a member of a right-wing political party or holds conservative political views

Examples

A rightist politician
During the debate, the rightist expressed strong opposition to government intervention.
He has always identified as a rightist and supports conservative economic policies.
Several rightists attended the rally to voice their opinions on tax reform.
