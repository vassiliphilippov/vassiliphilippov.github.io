humble

adjective
of low social rank or birth
1.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌm.bəl/ /; / /ˈʌm.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈhʌm.bəl/ /; / /ˈʌm.bəl/ (BrE)
of low social rank, position, or status; not grand or impressive; modest or lowly (such as origins, jobs, or things)
