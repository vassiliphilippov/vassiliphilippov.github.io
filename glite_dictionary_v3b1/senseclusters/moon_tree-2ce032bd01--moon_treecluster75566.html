Moon tree

noun
tree grown from seeds taken to the moon
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmun ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈmuːn ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree grown from seeds that were taken to the moon as part of a space mission, especially Apollo 14.

Examples

A Moon tree seed
Botanists carefully monitor each Moon tree to study its growth and health.
Many people visit the park just to see the Moon tree thriving there.
The Moon tree was planted as a symbol of space exploration and friendship.
