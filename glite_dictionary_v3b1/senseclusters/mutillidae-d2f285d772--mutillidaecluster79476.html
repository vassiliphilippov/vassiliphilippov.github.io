Mutillidae

noun
a family of wingless wasps (velvet ants)
Pronunciation: / mjuːˈtɪlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / mjuːˈtɪlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of wasps, commonly called velvet ants, whose females are wingless and often look like ants
