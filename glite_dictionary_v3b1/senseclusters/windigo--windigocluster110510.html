windigo

noun
greedily destructive person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.dɪ.goʊ/ /; / /ˈwɪn.də.goʊ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.dɪ.gəʊ/ /; / /ˈwɪn.də.gəʊ/ (BrE)
a person who is extremely greedy or destructive, especially one who consumes resources or profits without regard for others

He acted like a windigo, taking more than his fair share and leaving little for others.
