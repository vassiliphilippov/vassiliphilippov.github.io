beckon

verb
appear inviting or attractive
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛk.ən / (AmE), / ˈbɛk.ən / (BrE)
to appear inviting or attractive, suggesting that someone should approach or take an interest

Examples

A chance beckons
A promising future beckons those who work hard and stay dedicated to their goals.
The aroma of freshly baked bread beckoned from the open kitchen door.
The warm glow of the fireplace beckoned us to come inside and escape the cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invite - to encourage someone to come or take part
  • antonym: repel - to drive away or discourage from approaching
