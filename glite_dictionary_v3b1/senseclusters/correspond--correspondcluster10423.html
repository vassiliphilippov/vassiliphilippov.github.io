correspond

verb
be equivalent or parallel to something else
2.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /kɔː.rə.ˈspɑːnd/ /; / /kɔː.rɪ.ˈspɑːnd/ (AmE), /kɒ.rə.ˈspɒnd/ /; / /kɒ.rɪ.ˈspɒnd/ (BrE)
to be similar, match, or be equivalent to something else in function, character, amount, or position
