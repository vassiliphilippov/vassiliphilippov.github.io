fresh food

noun
unpreserved food
4.0
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ fuːd / (AmE), / frɛʃ fuːd / (BrE)
Food that has not been preserved by canning, dehydration, freezing, or smoking.

Examples

Fresh food daily
Eating fresh food is considered healthier than eating packaged meals.
The chef insists on using only fresh food in his restaurant.
We buy fresh food from the farmers' market every Saturday.

Related Terms
