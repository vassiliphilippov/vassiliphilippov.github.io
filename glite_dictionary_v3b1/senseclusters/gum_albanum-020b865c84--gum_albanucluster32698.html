gum albanum

noun
a bitter aromatic gum resin, like asafetida
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm ælˈbeɪnəm / (AmE), / ɡʌm alˈbɑːnəm / (BrE)
a bitter, aromatic gum resin similar to asafetida, used in traditional medicine and sometimes as a flavoring.

Examples

Aromatic gum albanum
Suppliers often confuse gum albanum with asafetida due to their similar appearance.
The bitter aroma of gum albanum is said to repel certain insects.
Traditional healers sometimes used gum albanum to prepare medicinal tonics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asafetida - a fetid, bitter resin obtained from certain plants, used in medicine and cooking
