queue

noun
a line of people or vehicles waiting
2.9 1.71/m
Pronunciation: / kjuÀê / (AmE), / kjuÀê / (BrE)
a line of people, vehicles, or things waiting their turn to proceed or be attended to

Examples

A queue of people
Cars lined up in a queue at the traffic light.
The school children formed a queue at the cafeteria door.
We had to wait in a long queue for tickets to the show.

Synonyms

  • waiting line
