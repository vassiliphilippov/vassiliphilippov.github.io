bonnetless

adjective
without a bonnet
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːnɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈbɒnɪtləs / (BrE)
lacking a bonnet, especially referring to a vehicle or machine without the hinged cover over the engine

Examples

Bonnetless car engine
The mechanic inspected the bonnetless car to assess the damage to its exposed engine.
Driving a bonnetless vehicle can be dangerous because the engine is unprotected.
The old truck sat bonnetless in the field, rusting in the rain.
