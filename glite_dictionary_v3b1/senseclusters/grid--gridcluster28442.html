grid

noun
parallel bars for grilling food
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪd / (AmE), / ɡrɪd / (BrE)
a cooking utensil consisting of parallel metal bars, used for grilling fish or meat over a fire

Examples

Cooking grid
After cleaning the grid, she prepared it for the next round of grilling.
He placed the fish on the grid over the campfire.
Traditional barbecue uses a metal grid to cook the meat evenly.
