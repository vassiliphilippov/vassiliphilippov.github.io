subvocalizer

noun
someone who mouths words silently
8.9
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈvoʊ.kəˌlaɪ.zər /; / sʌbˈvoʊ.kə.laɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / sʌbˈvəʊ.kəˌlaɪ.zə /; / sʌbˈvəʊ.kə.laɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person who silently articulates speech or words without making audible sounds, often moving the lips silently
