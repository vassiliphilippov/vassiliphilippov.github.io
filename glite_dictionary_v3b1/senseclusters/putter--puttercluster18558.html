putter

noun
golfer making putts
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌtər / (AmE), / ˈpʌtə / (BrE)
A golfer who is making putts, especially during the act of putting on the green.

Examples

A careful putter
As an inexperienced putter, she often missed short-distance strokes.
Each putter in the tournament competed for precision on the tricky greens.
The putter studied the green carefully before making his shot.
