roll

noun
swaying gait
1.2
Pronunciation: / roʊl / (AmE), / rəʊl / (BrE)
a swaying movement, a wave, an act of rolling, or rotary motion (as a noun)

Examples

A swaggering roll

Related Terms

  • synonym: rotation - the act of turning around a central point or axis
  • synonym: swagger - a confident, sometimes swaying walk
  • synonym: turn - the act of rotating or causing something to rotate
  • synonym: wave - a moving ridge or swell on the surface of a liquid, especially the sea, caused by the wind
