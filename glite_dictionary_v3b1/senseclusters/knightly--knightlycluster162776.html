knightly

adjective
chivalrous or courteous towards women
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtli / (BrE)
showing qualities or behavior associated with knights, such as chivalry, courtesy, bravery, or honor

Examples

Knightly behavior
He acted in a truly knightly manner, holding the door open for her.
His knightly gestures made everyone around him smile with admiration.
In medieval tales, heroes are often praised for their knightly devotion to ladies.
He performed a knightly deed by rescuing the child from danger.
Her knightly sense of justice inspired everyone in the group.
The ceremony honored those who had shown knightly courage in difficult times.

  • antonym: cowardly - lacking courage or bravery
