potentiality

noun
inherent capacity to develop or become
Pronunciation: / pəˌtɛn.ʃiˈæ.lə.t̬i /; / pəˌtɛnʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / pəˌtɛn.tʃiˈæl.ɪ.ti /; / pəˌtɛnʃɪˈælɪti / (BrE)
the inherent ability or capacity for something to develop, grow, or come into existence in the future

Examples

The potentiality to grow
Every seed carries within it the potentiality to grow into a mighty tree.
She saw great potentiality in the young artist's work, despite his lack of experience.
The new discovery revealed the potentiality for life to exist on other planets.
Many believe that education helps individuals to realize their potentiality.
The team examined the potentiality of the new technology to revolutionize the industry.
The young artist showed great potentiality for creative growth in her early works.

Related Terms

  • antonym: actuality - the state of being real or actual
  • synonym: potential - the possibility of something happening or becoming real
