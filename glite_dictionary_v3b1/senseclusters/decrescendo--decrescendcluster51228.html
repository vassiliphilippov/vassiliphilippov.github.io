decrescendo

adjective
gradually decreasing in loudness (music)
5.9 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdi.krəˈʃɛn.doʊ / (AmE), / ˌdiː.krɛˈʃɛn.dəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) gradually decreasing in loudness or intensity; also, a passage performed with diminishing volume.
