citronella

noun
insect-repellent oil
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlə / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from certain tropical grasses, commonly used as an insect repellent and in fragrances

Examples

Citronella oil
Many people burn citronella candles outdoors to keep mosquitoes away during summer evenings.
The spray contains citronella to help protect against insect bites while hiking.
She prefers using natural citronella oil instead of chemical repellents in her home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eucalyptus oil - an essential oil used as an insect repellent and in medicine
  • synonym: lemongrass - a tropical grass whose oil is used in cooking and fragrances
