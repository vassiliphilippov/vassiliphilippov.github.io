sunbonnet

noun
large bonnet to shade the face from sun
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbɑnɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbɒnɪt / (BrE)
a large brimmed bonnet, typically worn by girls and women, designed to shade the face from the sun

Examples

A sunbonnet for girls
Old photographs show children in sunbonnets to protect them from the midday sun.
She wore a pretty sunbonnet as she walked through the fields.
The handmade sunbonnet was decorated with delicate embroidery.
