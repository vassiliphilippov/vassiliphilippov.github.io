sulfate

verb
to convert into a sulfate
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.feɪt / (BrE)
to treat or react (a substance) with sulfuric acid or a sulfate, or to convert a substance into a sulfate, often through chemical reaction

Examples

To sulfate a metal
Industrial processes often sulfate metals to produce useful compounds.
Scientists sulfated the organic matter during the experiment to study the reaction.
The battery plates can sulfate over time if not maintained properly.

  • synonym: sulfonate - treat with or convert into a sulfonate
