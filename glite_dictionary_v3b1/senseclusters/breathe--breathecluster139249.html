breathe

verb
take a brief break to relax
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːð / (AmE), / ˈbriːð / (BrE)
to take a short break from one's activities in order to relax or recover.

Examples

Step out to breathe
After a busy morning, she stepped outside to breathe and clear her mind.
He was so focused on his work that he barely had time to breathe all day.
Let's breathe for a moment before starting the next task.

  • synonym: rest - to stop work or movement in order to relax
  • antonym: work - to exert effort or be engaged in activity
