Leucaena leucocephala

noun
tree native to tropical America with mimosa-like flowers
Pronunciation: / luːˈsiːnə ˌluːkoʊsɪˈfɑːlə / (AmE), / luːˈsiːnə ˌluːkɪˌsiːˈfɑːlə / (BrE)
A species of low, scrubby tree native to tropical and subtropical North America, with white or yellow-tinged flowers resembling those of the mimosa and long, flattened seed pods.
