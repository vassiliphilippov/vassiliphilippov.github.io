hieroglyphical

adjective
resembling hieroglyphic writing
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪrəˈɡlɪfɪkəl /; / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl /; / ˌhʌɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl / (BrE)
written in or resembling hieroglyphs or a pictorial writing system
