contentedness

noun
state of being contented or satisfied
4.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛntɪdnəs / (AmE), / kənˈtɛntɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the state of feeling satisfied and at ease with your situation or life

Examples

Deep contentedness
After years of searching, she finally achieved a deep contentedness in her new home.
He radiated contentedness as he watched his grandchildren play in the garden.
Meditation helped him develop a lasting sense of contentedness despite life's challenges.
