yarder

noun
machine for hauling logs in logging
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdər / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdə / (BrE)
a powered machine using one or more winches for hauling logs from the forest to a collection point in logging operations

Examples

A yarder machine
After cutting, the logs were attached to cables and hauled away by the yarder.
Modern yarders are often equipped with computerized controls for precise operation.
The logging crew used a yarder to move the felled trees up the steep hillside.

  • synonym: winch - a machine for lifting or pulling heavy objects
