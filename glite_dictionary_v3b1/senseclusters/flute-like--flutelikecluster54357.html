flute-like

adjective
resembling a flute
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfluːtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a flute in shape, especially being long, narrow, and cylindrical or having parallel grooves.

Examples

Flute-like columns
The architect designed columns with a flute-like appearance for the grand entrance.
She admired the flute-like stems of the delicate wildflowers in the meadow.
The vase had a flute-like shape, perfect for displaying a single rose.

  • synonym: tubular - having the shape of a tube
