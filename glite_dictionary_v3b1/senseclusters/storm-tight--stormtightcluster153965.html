storm-tight

verb
to secure for storms
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈstɔːrm.taɪt/ (AmE), /ˈstɔːm.taɪt/ (BrE)
to make something secure against a storm by fastening, sealing, or preparing it so wind and rain cannot enter

Examples

To storm-tight a house
Residents rushed to storm-tight their homes as the hurricane warnings grew more urgent.
Before leaving for vacation, they decided to storm-tight the beach house just in case.
It is important to storm-tight all windows and doors before a major storm hits.
