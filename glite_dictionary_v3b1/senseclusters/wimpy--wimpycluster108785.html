wimpy

adjective
weak or ineffectual
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmpi / (AmE), / ˈwɪmpi / (BrE)
feeble, weak, or lacking strength or courage; ineffectual or timid

Examples

A wimpy person
He seemed too wimpy to stand up to his boss.
She called him wimpy for refusing to ride the roller coaster.
The soup was rather wimpy and lacked flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strong - having great strength or power
  • synonym: weak - lacking physical or mental strength
