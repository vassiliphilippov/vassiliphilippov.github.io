cedar-scented

adjective
smelling like cedar wood
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsidɚ ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having a smell or fragrance similar to that of cedar wood.

Examples

cedar-scented paper
His wardrobe was lined with cedar-scented paper to protect his clothes from moths.
She bought a cedar-scented candle to give her living room a fresh, woodsy smell.
The spa uses cedar-scented oils for their relaxing massage treatments.
