nonfissionable

adjective
not capable of nuclear fission
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɪʃənəbl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɪʃənəbl̩ / (BrE)
not capable of undergoing nuclear fission

Examples

Nonfissionable nuclear material
Because it is nonfissionable, this isotope cannot sustain a nuclear chain reaction.
The reactor was designed to separate fissionable and nonfissionable material.
Uranium-238 is considered nonfissionable under normal circumstances.

  • antonym: fissionable - capable of undergoing nuclear fission
