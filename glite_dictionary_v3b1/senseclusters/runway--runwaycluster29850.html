runway

noun
paved strip for aircraft to take off and land
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrʌn.weɪ / (BrE)
a long, level surface, typically paved, used by aircraft for taking off and landing

Examples

Airport runway
After landing, the pilot steered the jet off the runway and toward the terminal.
Heavy fog covered the runway, delaying several flights at the airport.
The plane taxied slowly onto the runway and prepared for takeoff.
