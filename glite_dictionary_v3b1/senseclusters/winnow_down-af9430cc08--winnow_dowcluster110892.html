winnow down

verb
reduce a group by eliminating options
9.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.oʊ daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.əʊ daʊn/ (BrE)
to reduce a large group or list to a smaller, more select number by eliminating less suitable options

Examples

To winnow down options
She spent hours winnowing down the huge pile of applications to find the best ones.
The committee will winnow down the candidates to just five for the final interview.
We managed to winnow down our vacation options to the three most affordable places.
