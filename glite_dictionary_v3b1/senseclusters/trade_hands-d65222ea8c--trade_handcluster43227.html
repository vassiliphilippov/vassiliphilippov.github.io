trade hands

verb
change ownership or possession
Pronunciation: / treɪd hændz / (AmE), / treɪd hændz / (BrE)
to change ownership or possession, especially as goods, property, or assets are bought and sold

Examples

To trade hands privately
Many famous paintings trade hands privately through dealers and auction houses.
Rare baseball cards often trade hands for hundreds of dollars among collectors.
The property may trade hands several times before construction even begins.
