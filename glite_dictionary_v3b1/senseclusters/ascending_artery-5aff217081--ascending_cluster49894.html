ascending artery

noun
branch artery of the colon
6.4
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛndɪŋ ˈɑːrtɪri / (AmE), / əˈsɛndɪŋ ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
the branch of the ileocolic artery that supplies blood to the ascending colon

Examples

The ascending artery
Damage to the ascending artery may lead to localized tissue death in the ascending colon.
If the ascending artery is blocked, it can compromise blood supply to the large intestine.
The surgeon carefully identified the ascending artery before performing the colon resection.
