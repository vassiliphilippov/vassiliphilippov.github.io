fronting

noun
placing at the front
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of placing something at the front or bringing it forward for emphasis or attention

Examples

Fronting the display
The fronting of the new product display attracted more customers to the store.
In her speech, the fronting of key issues helped clarify her main points.
Architectural fronting can make a building appear more open and inviting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: backgrounding - the act of placing something in a less prominent position
  • synonym: highlighting - the act of emphasizing or drawing attention to something
