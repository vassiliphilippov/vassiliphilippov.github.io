wingwoman

noun
female pilot in flying formation
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female pilot who positions her aircraft beside and slightly behind the leader in a flying formation.

Examples

Fighter wingwoman
As a wingwoman, she relied on excellent communication with the team's lead pilot.
The experienced wingwoman maintained perfect distance from the squadron leader during the air show.
The flight instructor praised her ability to act as an effective wingwoman in stressful situations.
