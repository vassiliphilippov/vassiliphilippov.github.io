mythological

adjective
relating to myths; not factual
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to, based on, or told in traditional myths; lacking factual or historical basis.

Examples

Greek mythological characters
He has a fascination with Greek mythological characters such as Zeus and Apollo.
Many ancient civilizations have mythological stories to explain natural phenomena.
The novel features several mythological creatures that do not exist in real life.
