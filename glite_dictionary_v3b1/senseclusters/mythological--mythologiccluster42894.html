mythological

adjective
relating to myths; not factual
9.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /mɪ.θə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ /; / /mɪ.θə.ˈlɔː.dʒɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /mɪ.θə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
