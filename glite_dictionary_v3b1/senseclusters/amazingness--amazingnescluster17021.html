amazingness

noun
quality of impressiveness
Pronunciation: / əˈmeɪzɪŋnəs / (AmE), / əˈmeɪzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely impressive or causing great surprise

Examples

The amazingness of performance
The amazingness of her performance earned her a standing ovation from the audience.
He could not believe the amazingness of the view from the mountain peak.
Everyone talked about the amazingness of the new technology at the conference.
