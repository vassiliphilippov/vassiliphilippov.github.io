nondomesticated

adjective
not tamed
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Unlike dogs, wolves are nondomesticated and behave very differently around people.
