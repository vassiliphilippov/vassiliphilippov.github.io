baby bird

noun
a young unfledged bird
1.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.biː bɜːrd/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪ.biː bɜːd/ (BrE)
a young bird that has not yet developed its feathers enough to fly or leave the nest

Examples

A baby bird in nest
It's important not to touch a baby bird unless it is in danger.
The baby bird chirped loudly, waiting for its mother to return with food.
We rescued a baby bird that had fallen from its nest during the storm.
