headnote

verb
to add summary note
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.noʊt/ (AmE), /ˈhed.nəʊt/ (BrE)
to write or add a brief summary or explanatory note at the beginning of a legal case, judgment, or other text

Examples

To headnote a case
The editor asked her to headnote the article with a concise explanation of its findings.
Law students often headnote important cases to help with future study and reference.
Before publishing the report, he decided to headnote each section for clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annotate - to add notes or comments to a text
  • synonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points
