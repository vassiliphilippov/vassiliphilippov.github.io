gilled

adjective
having gills
Pronunciation: / ɡɪld / (AmE), / ɡɪld / (BrE)
having gills; provided with gills, especially for breathing underwater.

Examples

Gilled salamander
Scientists observed a gilled salamander in the pond.
The gilled mushroom can be distinguished by the lines under its cap.
The tadpole remained gilled for several weeks before developing lungs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gill-less - lacking gills
