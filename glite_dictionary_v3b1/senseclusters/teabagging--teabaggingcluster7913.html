teabagging

verb
to dip tea bag
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːˌbæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌbæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
to dip a tea bag repeatedly in hot water to brew tea to the desired strength

Examples

To teabag the water
She spent a moment teabagging the mug to get the right strength before adding milk.
He prefers teabagging his cup several times to make the tea stronger.
After teabagging the water, he removed the bag and added sugar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brew - to make a hot drink by mixing it with hot water
