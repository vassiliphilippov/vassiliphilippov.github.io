crusting

adjective
having a hard layer
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrʌstɪŋ / (BrE)
covered with or forming a hard, dry outer layer or crust.

Examples

Crusting paint
The crusting paint on the old fence began to flake off after the storm.
Doctors examined the crusting skin to determine if it was healing properly.
After the spill dried, a crusting residue was left on the kitchen floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scabby - covered with or resembling a scab
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or crusted
