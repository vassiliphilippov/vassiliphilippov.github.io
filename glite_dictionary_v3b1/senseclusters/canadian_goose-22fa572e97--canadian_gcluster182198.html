Canadian goose

noun
large North American wild goose
5.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪ.di.ən ɡus / (AmE), / kəˈneɪ.di.ən ɡuːs / (BrE)
a large North American wild goose with a black head and neck, white cheek patches, and a loud, honking call; commonly found near lakes and rivers

Examples

A Canadian goose swimming
A single Canadian goose was swimming on the pond early this morning.
The park is often filled with Canadian geese during migration season.
We saw a flock of Canadian geese flying south for the winter.
