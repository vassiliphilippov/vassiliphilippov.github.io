Swedish meatball

noun
traditional Swedish meat dish
Pronunciation: / ˈswidɪʃ ˈmitˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈmiːtbɔːl / (BrE)
a small ball of ground meat, typically pork and beef, seasoned and simmered in stock, served as a traditional Swedish dish

Examples

Traditional Swedish meatballs
Her grandmother taught her how to make authentic Swedish meatballs from scratch.
I ordered Swedish meatballs with creamy sauce at the Scandinavian restaurant.
The buffet included a tray of delicious Swedish meatballs alongside potatoes and lingonberry jam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meatball - a ball of ground meat, often mixed with other ingredients
