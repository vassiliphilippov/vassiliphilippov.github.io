rosewood

noun
deep reddish-brown color
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊz.wʊd/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊz.wʊd/ (BrE)
a deep reddish-brown color or shade, named for the appearance of rosewood timber and used in paints, finishes, textiles, and cosmetics.

Examples

The designer recommended rosewood lipstick for a subtle reddish-brown look on all skin tones.
