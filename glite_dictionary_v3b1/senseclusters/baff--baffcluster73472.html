baff

noun
puzzling thing or person
9.9
something or someone that is very difficult to understand or solve, causing confusion or frustration

The instructions for the new device were a complete baff to everyone in the office.
Despite hours of research, the scientist found the phenomenon to be a persistent baff.
Her sudden change in behavior was a real baff to her friends.
