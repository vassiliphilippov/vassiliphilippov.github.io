STEM

noun
Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (fields)
Pronunciation: / stɛm / (AmE), / stɛm / (BrE)
An acronym for the academic and professional fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics; used to refer collectively to those subjects, related education programs, or careers.

Examples

STEM education programs
Many schools are expanding their STEM programs to boost science and math education.
She chose a STEM degree because she enjoys coding, physics, and solving engineering problems.
The company prioritizes hiring graduates with STEM backgrounds for roles in research and development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: STEAM - an alternative educational approach that adds the arts to STEM subjects
  • synonym: STEM education - educational programs and curricula focused on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics
