souslik

noun
central Eurasian ground squirrel
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːslɪk / (AmE), / ˈsuːslɪk / (BrE)
A large ground squirrel native to central Eurasia, known for living in burrows.

Examples

A souslik burrow
Researchers tracked the movement of a colony of sousliks near the river.
The souslik is easily spotted in the grasslands during the summer months.
Unlike other ground squirrels, the souslik hibernates for a considerable part of the year.
