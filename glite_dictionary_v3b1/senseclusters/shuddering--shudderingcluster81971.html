shuddering

adjective
shaking convulsively or violently
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌdɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌdərɪŋ / (BrE)
shaking or trembling convulsively or violently, often as a result of fear, cold, or strong emotion

Examples

A shuddering body
He recalled the accident with a shuddering sense of dread.
Her voice was barely audible over the shuddering of the engine.
The child stood in the doorway, shuddering with cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quivering - trembling or shaking with a slight rapid motion
  • antonym: still - not moving at all
  • synonym: trembling - shaking slightly, especially from cold or fear
