fertile period

noun
time in menstrual cycle when conception is likely
4.1 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfɜːr.təl ˈpɪr.ɪ.əd/ /; / /ˈfɜːr.taɪl ˈpɪr.ɪ.əd/ (AmE), /ˈfɜː.taɪl ˈpɪə.rɪ.əd/ /; / /ˈfɜː.təl ˈpɪə.rɪ.əd/ (BrE)
the phase in the menstrual cycle during which fertilization is most likely to occur, typically around ovulation.
