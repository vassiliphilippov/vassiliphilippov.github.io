Odobenus

noun
genus of walrus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊdəˈbiːnəs / (AmE), / ˌɒdəˈbiːnəs / (BrE)
A genus of marine mammals comprising the walrus, the only living member of the family Odobenidae.

Examples

The Odobenus walrus
Odobenus is distinguished by its prominent tusks and whiskered face.
Scientists have studied Odobenus fossils to learn about the evolution of pinnipeds.
The genus Odobenus contains only one living species, the walrus.

Related Terms
