underbill

noun
lower bird beak
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈʌndəbɪl / (BrE)
the lower part of a bird's beak, located beneath the upper bill

Examples

A bird's underbill
The woodpecker used its underbill to help grip the tree bark while searching for insects.
A scientist measured the length of the underbill to compare different bird species.
The finch's underbill was perfectly shaped for cracking open seeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mandible - the lower jaw or lower part of a bird's beak
  • antonym: upper bill - the upper part of a bird's beak
