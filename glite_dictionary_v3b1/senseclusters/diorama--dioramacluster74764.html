diorama

noun
three-dimensional scene model or series of pictures
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈræmə / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈrɑːmə / (BrE)
a three-dimensional model or a series of pictures depicting a continuous scene, often used for artistic or educational display

Examples

A diorama display
A series of dioramas along the hallway depict different habitats around the world.
She spent weeks crafting a detailed diorama for her science class project.
The museum features a stunning diorama of ancient Rome in miniature.
