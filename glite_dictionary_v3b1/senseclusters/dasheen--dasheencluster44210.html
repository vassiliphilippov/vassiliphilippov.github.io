dasheen

noun
tropical starchy edible root plant
9.8
Pronunciation: / dəˈʃin / (AmE), / dəˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a tropical plant (Colocasia esculenta) grown for its large, edible starchy root, used as a staple food in some regions; also, the root itself
