oil

noun
paint made with pigment in oil
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl / (AmE), / ɔɪl / (BrE)
A type of paint made with pigment suspended in oil, used by artists for painting.

Examples

Oil painting
Many museums showcase famous oils from the 19th century.
She prefers working in oil because of its rich colors and texture.
The artist created a beautiful landscape using oil on canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oil paint - paint in which pigment is suspended in oil; used by artists
  • antonym: watercolor - a painting using pigments suspended in water
