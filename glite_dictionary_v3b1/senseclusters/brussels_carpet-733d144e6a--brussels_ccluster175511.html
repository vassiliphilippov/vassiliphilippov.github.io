Brussels carpet

noun
carpet with looped wool pile pattern
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌsəlz ˈkɑrpɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrʌsəlz ˈkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
A type of carpet with a strong linen warp and a heavy pile of colored woolen yarns arranged in uncut loops to form a pattern.

Examples

A Brussels carpet pattern
Our antique Brussels carpet has retained its vibrant patterns for decades.
The stately home was decorated with a beautiful Brussels carpet in the hallway.
They chose a Brussels carpet for the dining room because of its durability and elegance.
