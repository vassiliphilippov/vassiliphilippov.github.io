hugging

noun
affectionate or intimate physical holding
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌɡɪŋ / (BrE)
affectionate touching or holding of another person, often as a form of play or preliminary intimacy without sexual intercourse or genital contact

Examples

Hugging a friend
In some cultures, hugging is a common way to greet friends and family members.
They spent the afternoon hugging and talking quietly in the park.
While hugging often precedes kissing, it does not have to be romantic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embracing - the act of holding someone closely in one's arms
