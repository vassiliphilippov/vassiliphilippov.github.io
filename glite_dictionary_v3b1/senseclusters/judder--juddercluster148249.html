judder

noun
video playback irregular motion
8.6
Pronunciation: / c8d928cd5a / (AmE), / c8d928cd59 / (BrE)
a visible irregular or jerky motion in video or animation, typically caused by frame-rate mismatch, uneven frame timing, or display refresh issues.

Examples

Video judder effect
The film showed noticeable judder during pans on the streaming app.
During the slow camera pan the editor complained about visible judder between mixed frame rates.
Adjusting the TV's motion smoothing removed most of the judder that had distracted viewers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - moving or playing back without jerks, interruptions, or visible artifacts
  • synonym: stutter - an interruption or unevenness in motion or sound; in video, a similar brief interruption of smooth motion
