urochord

noun
notochord in the tail of some larval marine animals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊr.oʊˌkɔrd /; / ˈjʊrəˌkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəˌkɔːd / (BrE)
a notochord found in the tail of the larval stage of certain primitive marine animals, especially tunicates
