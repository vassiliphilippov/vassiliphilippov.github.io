crease-resistant

adjective
resistant to wrinkling (fabric)
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːs rɪˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈkriːs rɪˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
(of fabric) not likely to develop wrinkles or creases easily; resistant to wrinkling.

Examples

Crease-resistant fabric
I prefer buying crease-resistant trousers for work because they need less ironing.
Many uniforms are designed with crease-resistant fabric to withstand frequent wear.
The travel shirt is made from crease-resistant cotton, so you look neat all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrinkle-free - (of fabric) not developing wrinkles; not creasing easily
