concealer

noun
makeup to hide flaws
2.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsiː.lər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsiː.lə/ (BrE)
a cosmetic product used to hide skin imperfections such as blemishes, dark circles, or redness

Examples

Liquid concealer
She applied concealer to hide the blemishes on her cheeks before going out.
A good concealer can make dark circles under your eyes much less noticeable.
He used a small brush to blend the concealer into his skin for a natural look.
She dabbed a small amount of concealer on the blemish before applying her makeup.
Many people use concealer to cover dark circles under their eyes for a fresher look.
The makeup artist recommended a green-tinted concealer to reduce the appearance of redness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrector - a cosmetic used to neutralize skin discoloration
  • synonym: foundation - a cosmetic base applied to the face for an even skin tone
  • synonym: highlighter - a cosmetic used to emphasize certain features of the face
