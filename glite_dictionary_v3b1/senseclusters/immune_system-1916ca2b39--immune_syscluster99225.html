immune system

noun
body's defense system against disease
2.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈmjuːn ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈmjuːn ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
The organs, tissues, and cells in the body that work together to protect it from infection and disease by producing an immune response.

Examples

A healthy immune system
A healthy immune system can help your body fight off viruses and bacteria.
Certain vaccines strengthen the immune system by exposing it to a harmless version of the disease.
People with weakened immune systems are at higher risk of infections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immune response - the reaction of the immune system to foreign substances
