task

noun
piece of work to be done
Pronunciation: / tæsk / (AmE), / tɑːsk / (BrE)
a piece of work or duty that needs to be done, especially one that is assigned

Examples

A difficult task
Each student was given a different task during the group project.
Organizing the annual conference proved to be a challenging task.
She completed the task ahead of schedule and impressed her manager.
Each employee was given a different task to complete by the end of the day.
Finishing the project on time was a difficult task for the team.
The teacher assigned a writing task for homework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignment - a specific task or piece of work assigned to someone
  • synonym: chore - a routine or minor task, often household-related
  • synonym: duty - a moral or legal obligation; a task or action someone is required to perform
  • synonym: job - paid position of regular employment or a specific piece of work
