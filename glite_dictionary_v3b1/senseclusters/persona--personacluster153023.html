persona

noun
user archetype for design
5.4
Pronunciation: / pərˈsoʊnə / (AmE), / pəˈsəʊnə / (BrE)
(in marketing and user‑experience research) a semi‑fictional character created to represent a typical user or customer, used to guide product design, features, and messaging.

Examples

User persona
The design team developed a detailed persona to represent busy, budget-conscious smartphone users.
Marketing created a persona describing a frequent buyer to guide the campaign's tone and offers.
During testing, the team asked whether the app met the needs of their primary persona.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archetype - a typical example or model of a person used to represent a category
  • synonym: user profile - a description of a typical user's characteristics, needs, and behaviours
