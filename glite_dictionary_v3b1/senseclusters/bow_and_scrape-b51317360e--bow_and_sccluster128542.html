bow and scrape

verb
act very submissively or obsequiously
6.1
Pronunciation: / baʊ ənd skreɪp / (AmE), / baʊ ənd skreɪp / (BrE)
to behave in an excessively submissive or obsequious manner, especially to gain favor or approval

Examples

To bow and scrape politely
Many employees felt they had to bow and scrape to keep their jobs during the merger.
She refused to bow and scrape before anyone, insisting on being treated as an equal.
The advisor spent most of the meeting bowing and scraping to the new director.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kowtow - to act in an excessively subservient manner
  • antonym: stand up to - to resist or confront someone confidently
