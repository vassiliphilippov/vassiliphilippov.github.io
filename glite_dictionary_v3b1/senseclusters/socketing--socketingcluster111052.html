socketing

noun
fitting into a socket
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒkətɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of fitting something into a socket or being fitted into a socket

Examples

Secure socketing of parts
Proper socketing of the joint is crucial for stability in mechanical assemblies.
The technician checked the socketing of each wire to ensure a secure connection.
Accurate socketing helps prevent damage to both the plug and the socket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insertion - the act of putting something into something else
  • antonym: removal - the act of taking something out
