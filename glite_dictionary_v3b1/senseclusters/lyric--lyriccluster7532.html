lyric

noun
short poem expressing personal emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪrɪk / (BrE)
a short poem that expresses personal feelings and emotions, typically in a songlike style

Examples

A lyric poem
Many famous lyric poems focus on themes of love and longing.
She recited a moving lyric at the literary gathering.
The poet composed a lyric celebrating the beauty of spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegy - a mournful poem, usually about death
  • synonym: ode - a formal poem praising something or someone
