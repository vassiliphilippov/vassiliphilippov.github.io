minette

noun
affectionate term for woman
9.9
Pronunciation: / miˈnɛt / (AmE), / miˈnɛt / (BrE)
an affectionate or playful term for a young or attractive woman, used informally

Examples

Pretty minette

At the café he whispered something to his companion, calling her minette with a smile.
She blushed when he affectionately called her minette in front of their friends.
