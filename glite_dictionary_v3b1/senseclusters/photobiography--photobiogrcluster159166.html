photobiography

noun
biography with photos
7.3
Pronunciation: /foʊ.toʊ.baɪ.ˈɑː.grə.fiː/ /; / /ə.toʊ.baɪ.ˈɑː.grə.fiː/ (AmE), /fəʊ.təʊ.baɪ.ˈɒ.grə.fiː/ (BrE)
a biography or story told mainly through photographs, usually with brief text or captions

Examples

A photobiography book
The photobiography of the famous explorer featured hundreds of images from his journeys.
She published a photobiography to share her childhood memories through pictures and stories.
A well-made photobiography can bring a person's history to life in a unique way.
The author released a photobiography that captured her travels across five continents.
A photobiography of the famous musician was displayed at the gallery, featuring rare family photos.
Students created a photobiography of their school to celebrate its 100th anniversary.
