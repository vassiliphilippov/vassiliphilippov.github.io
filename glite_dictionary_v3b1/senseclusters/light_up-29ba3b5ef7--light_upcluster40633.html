light up

verb
begin to smoke (by lighting)
Pronunciation: / laɪt ʌp / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to begin to smoke a cigarette, cigar, or pipe by lighting it

Examples

To light up a cigarette
After finishing his lunch, Tom decided to light up on the patio.
As soon as she stepped outside, she reached for her cigarette and lit up.
You can't light up inside the restaurant due to the new smoking ban.
