Synonyms

  • use
    1.0 1714/m
    use is a general word for putting something to work, while apply often means using a rule, method, or tool.
  • employ
    2.2 35/m
    employ means making use of something for a purpose, while apply also covers using tools or putting rules into effect.
  • put into practice
    3.7 0.86/m
    put into practice is to implement an idea in real life, while apply can mean both using and enforcing methods.
  • utilize
    4.9 31/m
    utilize means make practical use, often in formal or technical settings, while apply is more general for methods or tools.
  • implement
    3.7 78/m
    implement means carry out a plan, law, or decision, while apply focuses on using a method, tool, or rule.
