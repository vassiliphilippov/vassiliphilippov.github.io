Kurd

noun
member of the Kurdish ethnic group
Pronunciation: / kɜrd / (AmE), / kɜːd / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group mainly living in Kurdistan, traditionally pastoral and predominantly Muslim; the largest ethnic group without an independent state

Examples

Kurds in Kurdistan
Many Kurds live in southeastern Turkey, Iraq, Iran, and Syria.
She is proud of her heritage as a Kurd.
The rights of Kurds have long been a subject of political discussion.
