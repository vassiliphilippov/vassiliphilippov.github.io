noun

Synonyms

  • bishopric
    9.7 0.1/m
    bishopric names the district a bishop governs in many churches, while diocese is the widely used term for that same district.
  • episcopate
    9.9 0.00/m
    episcopate refers to the area under a bishop or the bishop's office in formal church language, while diocese names the local district.
  • eparchy
    9.9 0.01/m
    eparchy is the district term used in the Eastern Orthodox Church, while diocese is the general term used in many other Christian traditions.
  • bishopry
    9.9 0.00/m
    bishopry often refers to the office, rank, or authority of a bishop in older language, while diocese names the bishop's district.
