ankylosing

adjective
stiff or fused joints
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkəˌloʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaŋkɪləʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
describing bones or joints that have become stiff or fused together, often due to disease or injury

Examples

Ankylosing joints
The doctor explained that ankylosing joints can severely limit movement.
Early treatment can help prevent ankylosing of the affected bones.
The patient was diagnosed with a condition causing ankylosing of the spine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fused - joined together into a single unit
  • antonym: mobile - able to move freely
