doomster

noun
pessimistic person
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈduːmstər / (AmE), / ˈduːmstə / (BrE)
a person who always expects or predicts disaster, ruin, or the worst possible outcome

Examples

Pessimistic doomster
He is known as a doomster because he always predicts that things will go wrong.
The doomster in the group warned that the project would fail, but everyone else remained hopeful.
Don't listen to the doomster—sometimes things do work out for the best.
The media called him a doomster for warning about the risks of climate change.
Some investors ignore the doomsters and remain confident in the market's future.
She was labeled a doomster after predicting the company's collapse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doomsayer - a person who predicts disaster or misfortune
  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
  • synonym: pessimist - a person who tends to see the worst aspect of things
