Acherontia atropos

noun
European moth with skull-like markings
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkɛr.ən.ti.ə ˈæ.trə.pɒs / (AmE), / əˈkɛr.ən.ti.ə ˈæ.trə.pɒs / (BrE)
A large European hawkmoth with markings on its thorax resembling a human skull, commonly known as the 'death's-head hawkmoth'.
