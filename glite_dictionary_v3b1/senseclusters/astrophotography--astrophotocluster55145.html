astrophotography

noun
photographing the night sky
6.7
Pronunciation: /æs.troʊ.fə.ˈtɑː.grə.fiː/ (AmE), /æs.trəʊ.fə.ˈtɒg.rə.fiː/ (BrE)
the practice of taking photographs of stars, planets, and other objects in space or the night sky

Examples

Astrophotography of stars
He set up his camera in the desert to try astrophotography for the first time.
Modern technology has made astrophotography accessible to amateur astronomers around the world.
The magazine featured breathtaking astrophotography of the Milky Way and distant nebulae.
He bought a special camera for astrophotography to capture detailed images of the Milky Way.
Many enthusiasts travel to remote areas for better astrophotography opportunities away from city lights.
Her award-winning astrophotography was featured in a science magazine.
