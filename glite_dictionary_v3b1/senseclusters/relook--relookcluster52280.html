relook

verb
to review again
5.3 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàl äk / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàl äk / (BrE)
to look at or consider something again, especially to reassess or review it

Examples

To relook at results
The team decided to relook at the proposal before making a final decision.
We should relook our travel plans in case there are better options available.
After receiving feedback, she chose to relook at her presentation slides.
