microstate

noun
very small country
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsteɪt / (BrE)
a very small country, especially one with a small population or land area

Examples

European microstate
Liechtenstein is a European microstate known for its beautiful mountains.
Many microstates rely on tourism as a major part of their economy.
San Marino, a historic microstate, is completely surrounded by Italy.

Related Terms
