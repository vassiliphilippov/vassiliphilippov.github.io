BMus

noun
Bachelor of Music degree
Pronunciation: / ˌbiː ˈmjuːz / (AmE), / ˌbiː ˈmjuːz / (BrE)
A Bachelor of Music; an undergraduate academic degree awarded for a course in music.

Examples

A BMus degree
A BMus is often the first step toward a career in professional music performance.
Many universities offer a BMus with various specializations, such as composition or music education.
She graduated with a BMus from the Royal Academy of Music in 2015.
