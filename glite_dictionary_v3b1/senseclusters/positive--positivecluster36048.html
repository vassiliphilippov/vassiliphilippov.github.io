positive

adjective
indicating increase or progress (maths, physics)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːzətɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪv / (BrE)
(of a direction, quantity, or value) reckoned, situated, or tending in the direction regarded as indicating increase or progress.

Examples

Positive slope
The graph shows a positive slope, indicating an upward trend.
There was a positive increase in the number of volunteers this year.
When moving east is defined as positive, traveling west would be negative direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negative - in a direction or sense opposite to that regarded as positive
