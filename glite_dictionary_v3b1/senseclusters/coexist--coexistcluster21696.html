coexist

verb
exist together at the same time or place
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ɪg.ˈzɪst/ /; / /koʊ.ɪk.ˈsɪst/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ɪg.ˈzɪst/ /; / /kəʊ.ɪk.ˈsɪst/ (BrE)
