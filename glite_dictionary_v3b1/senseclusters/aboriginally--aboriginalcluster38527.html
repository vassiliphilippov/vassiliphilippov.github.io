aboriginally

adverb
in an indigenous manner
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənəli / (AmE), / ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the original inhabitants of a place or their customs

Examples

Aboriginally settled land
The land was aboriginally inhabited by several distinct tribes.
Many traditional ceremonies are still performed aboriginally in remote communities.
The art displayed in the museum was aboriginally created centuries ago.
