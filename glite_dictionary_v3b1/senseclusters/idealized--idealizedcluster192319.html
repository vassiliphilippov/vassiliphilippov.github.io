idealized

adjective
represented as perfect or better than real
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪəlaɪzd / (BrE)
represented as being perfect or better than reality, often to an unrealistic degree.

Examples

An idealized image
His childhood memories were viewed through an idealized lens, making everything seem perfect.
Many historical figures are idealized in stories long after their deaths.
The film presents an idealized version of family life that rarely exists in reality.
His idealised vision of family life did not match the complex reality he faced.
In the novel, the countryside is presented in an idealised manner, far removed from reality.
Many paintings from that era depict women in an idealised form, emphasizing beauty over accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idealized - regarded or represented as perfect or better than reality (US spelling)
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they really are
