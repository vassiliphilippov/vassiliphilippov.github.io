whiteout

verb
cover errors with correction fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪt.aʊt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt.aʊt / (BrE)
to cover written or printed errors with a white liquid correction fluid

Examples

To whiteout a mistake
After realizing the error, I whited out the extra digit.
She quickly whiteouted the mistake on the report before submitting it.
You can whiteout any typos using a correction pen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross out - draw a line through written words to remove them
  • synonym: erase - remove marks or written material by rubbing or scraping
