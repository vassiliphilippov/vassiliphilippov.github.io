lavash

noun
thin flatbread
Pronunciation: / ləˈvɑʃ / (AmE), / ləˈvɑːʃ / (BrE)
a soft, thin flatbread traditionally baked in Middle Eastern and Caucasian cuisines

Examples

Soft lavash bread
We enjoyed grilled vegetables wrapped in lavash at the restaurant.
She bought fresh lavash from the local bakery for the family dinner.
Traditional Armenian meals often include lavash served with cheese and herbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naan - a soft, leavened flatbread from South Asia
  • synonym: pita - a round, pocket-style flatbread from the Middle East
