India-rubber plant

noun
tropical tree grown as a houseplant
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiə ˌrʌbər plænt / (AmE), / ˈɪndiə ˌrʌbə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A large tropical Asian tree, often grown indoors as a houseplant, that is a source of Assam rubber.
