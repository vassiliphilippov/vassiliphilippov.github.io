viva

noun
oral academic examination
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈviːvə / (AmE), / ˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
An oral examination, especially one for an academic qualification, conducted by spoken communication.

Examples

PhD viva
At the end of her PhD, she had to pass a viva before receiving her degree.
He spent weeks preparing for his viva with the external examiner.
The results of the viva will determine whether you are awarded your doctorate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oral exam - an examination conducted orally rather than in writing
  • antonym: written exam - an examination conducted by written answers
