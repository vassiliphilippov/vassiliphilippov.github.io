diagnostic

adjective
indicative of a disease or condition
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.əg.ˈnɑːs.tɪk/ (AmE), /daɪ.əg.ˈnɒs.tɪk/ (BrE)
related to identifying a disease or problem, or characteristic of a particular condition

Examples

Diagnostic sign

This software provides diagnostic information to help identify system errors quickly.
