overwrap

verb
to add outer wrapping
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈræp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈræp / (BrE)
to wrap something with an additional outer layer or covering, especially for protection or presentation

Examples

To overwrap a package
To prevent damage, they overwrapped the electronics with a layer of foam before packing.
The bakery overwraps its cakes in decorative paper for special occasions.
For extra security, she decided to overwrap the gift box with clear plastic.
