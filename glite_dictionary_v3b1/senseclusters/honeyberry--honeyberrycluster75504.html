honeyberry

noun
edible blue berry shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌbɛri / (BrE)
a shrub of the honeysuckle family, Lonicera caerulea, grown for its sweet, edible blue berries

Examples

Honeyberry bush
The farmer planted several honeyberry bushes to diversify his fruit crop.
Children enjoyed picking ripe honeyberries straight from the shrubs in early summer.
A jar of homemade honeyberry jam makes a delicious gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueberry - a small, sweet, edible blue fruit from a different plant
  • synonym: honeysuckle - a climbing shrub with sweet-smelling flowers, related to honeyberry
