handrailing

noun
support rail on stairs
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhænˌdreɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌreɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a rail fixed alongside stairs, ramps, or walkways to provide support and safety for people using them

Examples

A handrailing on stairs
The carpenter installed a sturdy handrailing along the new staircase for added safety.
She gripped the handrailing tightly as she climbed the slippery steps on a rainy day.
The building code requires a handrailing on both sides of the ramp for accessibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banister - the structure formed by the handrail and its supports on a staircase
  • synonym: handrail - a rail designed to be held for support on stairs or ramps
