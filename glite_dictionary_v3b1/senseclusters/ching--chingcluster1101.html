ching

noun
ringing metallic sound
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, ringing sound, especially one made by metal or glass striking another object

Examples

A coin ching
The coin hit the floor with a loud ching that echoed through the room.
She dropped the keys on the glass table, producing a sharp ching.
As the glasses touched in a toast, a cheerful ching filled the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a clear, musical ringing sound
  • synonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • antonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
