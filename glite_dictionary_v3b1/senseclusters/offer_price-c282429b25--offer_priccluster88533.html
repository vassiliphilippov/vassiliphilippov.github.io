offer price

noun
buyer's proposed price
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0254f\u0259r \\u02ccpra\u026as / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0252f\u0259 \\u02ccpra\u026as / (BrE)
The price proposed by a buyer when negotiating to purchase something; the buyer's bid in a sale or auction.

Examples

House offer price
They submitted an offer price of $312,000 for the house.
After negotiations, she raised her offer price to stay competitive with other buyers.
The seller rejected the initial offer price and asked for a counteroffer with repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asking price - the price a seller requests for an item; often compared with the buyer's offer
  • synonym: bid - a price that a buyer offers to pay for something, often used in auctions or negotiations
