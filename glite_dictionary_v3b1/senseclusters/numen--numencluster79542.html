numen

noun
divine spirit or power (esp. Roman religion)
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.mən / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.mən / (BrE)
A divine power or spirit believed to be present in a place, object, or phenomenon, especially in ancient Roman religion.
