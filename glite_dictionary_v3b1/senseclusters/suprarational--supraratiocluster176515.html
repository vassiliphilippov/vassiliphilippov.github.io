suprarational

adjective
beyond reason
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈræʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈræʃənəl / (BrE)
going beyond or transcending what can be understood by reason alone

Examples

Suprarational experience
The poet described a suprarational sense of beauty that words could not explain.
Some spiritual experiences are considered suprarational because they cannot be fully understood by logic.
Her belief in destiny was rooted in a suprarational conviction beyond scientific proof.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrational - not based on reason or logic
  • antonym: rational - based on reason or logic
  • synonym: transcendent - going beyond ordinary limits; surpassing
