capitally

adverb
with great seriousness
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtəli / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəli / (BrE)
in a way that is of great importance, seriousness, or severity

Examples

Capitally important issues
The law states that certain crimes can be capitally punished.
He was capitally responsible for the project's success.
The mistake was capitally important and could not be ignored.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seriously - in a serious or important way
  • antonym: trivially - in a way that is not important or serious
