reiterative

adjective
involving repetition or recurrence
Pronunciation: / riˈɪt̬ɚˌeɪt̬ɪv / (AmE), / riːˈɪt.ər.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
involving or characterized by repetition or frequent recurrence, especially of an action or process

Examples

A reiterative process
His reiterative explanations ensured that everyone understood the instructions.
Some traditional songs have a reiterative chorus to help listeners remember the melody.
The author used a reiterative style, emphasizing the main theme by repeating it throughout the novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iterative - involving repetition, especially in mathematics or linguistics
  • synonym: repetitive - involving or characterized by repetition
