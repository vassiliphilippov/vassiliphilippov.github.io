Shuha

adjective
of non-Kokka Shinto sects
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃuːˈhɑː / (AmE), / ʃuːˈhɑː / (BrE)
Relating to any Shinto sect other than Kokka Shinto.

Examples

Shuha sects
Many local shrines in Japan are classified as part of Shuha sects.
Scholars often compare the beliefs of Shuha groups to those of Kokka Shinto.
The ceremony followed traditional Shuha practices rather than official state rituals.
