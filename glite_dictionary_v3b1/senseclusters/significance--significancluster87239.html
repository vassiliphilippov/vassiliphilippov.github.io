Synonyms

  • message
    1.0 130/m
    message is the main idea someone wants to communicate, while significance covers both that idea and its wider importance or meaning.
  • substance
    4.3 13/m
    substance is the essential core or main idea of an argument, while significance adds the idea's broader meaning or importance.
  • meaning
    1.0 80/m
