shopping and dining

noun
buying goods and eating out
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpɪŋ ənd ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ ənd ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activities of buying goods and eating meals at restaurants or cafes, often as a combined leisure activity

Examples

Shopping and dining with friends
Her favorite way to relax is by indulging in shopping and dining with friends.
Many malls now offer a variety of shopping and dining experiences under one roof.
We planned a weekend in the city focused on shopping and dining at new places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dining - the activity of eating a meal, especially in a restaurant
  • synonym: shopping - the activity of buying goods, especially from stores
