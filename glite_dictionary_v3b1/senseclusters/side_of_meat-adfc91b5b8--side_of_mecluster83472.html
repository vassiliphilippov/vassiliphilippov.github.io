side of meat

noun
half an animal's carcass for food
Pronunciation: / saɪd əv mit / (AmE), / saɪd əv miːt / (BrE)
a lengthwise half of an animal's carcass that has been dressed and is used as food

Examples

A side of meat
Each side of meat is carefully inspected for quality before it reaches the store.
The butcher hung a side of meat in the cold room to age before cutting it.
We ordered a side of meat for the big family barbecue.
