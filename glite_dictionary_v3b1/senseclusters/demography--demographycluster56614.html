demography

noun
study of human populations
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the characteristics and changes of human populations, such as size, growth, density, and distribution

Examples

Study of demography
Advances in demography help governments prepare for future changes in population size and age.
She decided to pursue a degree in demography to better understand global population trends.
The report includes detailed demography of the country's urban and rural regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: population studies - the study of population structure and dynamics
  • synonym: sociology - the study of society and social behavior
