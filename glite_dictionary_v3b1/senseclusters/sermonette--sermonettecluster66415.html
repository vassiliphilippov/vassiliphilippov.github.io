sermonette

noun
short moral lecture
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜːrməˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌsɜːməˈnɛt / (BrE)
a short talk or piece of writing that gives moral advice, often in a didactic or critical way and not always religious

Examples

A moral sermonette
The teacher ended the class with a brief sermonette about the importance of honesty.
He wrote a sermonette in the newsletter urging readers to recycle more.
She dismissed his lecture as a patronizing sermonette about healthy eating at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homily - a speech or piece of writing giving moral advice, often religious
  • synonym: lecture - a formal talk on a subject, often giving advice or instruction
