otalgia

noun
ear pain; pain in the ear
8.6
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtældʒiə / (AmE), / əʊˈtældʒiə / (BrE)
pain in the ear, especially when localized to the middle or inner ear

Examples

Chronic otalgia
Chronic otalgia may indicate a serious underlying ear condition.
Her doctor diagnosed the sudden otalgia as a result of an inner ear infection.
The child was complaining of otalgia and discomfort after swimming.
