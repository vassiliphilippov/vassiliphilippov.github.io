wax mallow

noun
tropical/subtropical plant of Malvaviscus genus
6.3
Pronunciation: / wæks ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / wæks ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
Any of several tropical or subtropical plants of the genus Malvaviscus, with brilliant, bell-shaped drooping flowers resembling partially opened hibiscus flowers.

Examples

The wax mallow flower
Many gardeners value the wax mallow for its striking resemblance to the hibiscus, despite being a different genus.
The wax mallow in her garden bloomed with bright, drooping red flowers throughout the summer.
The botanist explained how the wax mallow attracts hummingbirds with its brilliant, bell-shaped blossoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hibiscus - a tropical plant with large, showy flowers
  • synonym: mallow - any plant of the Malvaceae family, including genera like Malva and Malvaviscus
