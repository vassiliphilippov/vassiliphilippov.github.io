nonmatching

adjective
not forming a pair
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈmætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
not corresponding in appearance, style, or type with something else; not forming a pair or set.

Examples

Nonmatching earrings
She accidentally wore nonmatching earrings to the party.
The living room was decorated with nonmatching chairs around the table.
His outfit included a jacket and pants in nonmatching colors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: matching - corresponding in appearance or forming a pair
  • synonym: mismatched - not matching or not paired correctly
