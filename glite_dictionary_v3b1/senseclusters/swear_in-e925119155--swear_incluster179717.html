swear in

verb
administer an oath for office
4.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /sweər ɪn/ (AmE), /sweə ɪn/ (BrE)
to administer an oath to someone, especially as part of officially admitting them to a position or office

Examples

To swear in a president
After the election, all members of parliament must be sworn in before taking office.
The Chief Justice will swear in the new president during the ceremony.
The mayor was officially sworn in at a public event attended by thousands.
