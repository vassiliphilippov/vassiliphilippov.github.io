surface-to-air missile

noun
missile launched from ground or ship at aircraft
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.fəs tə er ˈmɪs.əl/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.fɪs tə eə ˈmɪs.əl/ (BrE)
a guided missile designed to be launched from the ground or a ship and aimed at destroying aircraft in flight

Examples

A surface-to-air missile system
Navy ships were equipped with advanced surface-to-air missiles to protect against aerial threats.
News reports confirmed that a surface-to-air missile had brought down the aircraft.
The military installed several surface-to-air missiles around the base for defense.
