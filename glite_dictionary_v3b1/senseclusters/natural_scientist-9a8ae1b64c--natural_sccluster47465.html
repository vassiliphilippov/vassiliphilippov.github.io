natural scientist

noun
expert in natural history (botany, zoology)
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ˈsʌɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a person who is knowledgeable about natural history, especially in the fields of botany and zoology

Examples

A natural scientist studies plants
As a natural scientist, she spent years exploring rainforests and cataloguing new species.
Darwin was a renowned natural scientist whose observations changed the course of biology.
Many natural scientists contributed valuable research to our understanding of ecosystems.
