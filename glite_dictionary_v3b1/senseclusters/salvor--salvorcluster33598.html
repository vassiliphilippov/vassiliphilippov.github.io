salvor

noun
person who salvages property, especially from shipwrecks
7.6 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsæl.vər/ (AmE), /ˈsæl.və/ (BrE)
a person or company engaged in salvaging property, especially from shipwrecks or other disasters; particularly in maritime law, one who recovers cargo, ships, or other property at sea
