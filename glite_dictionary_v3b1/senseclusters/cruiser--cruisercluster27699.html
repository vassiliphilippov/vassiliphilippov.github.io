cruiser

noun
large motorboat with living quarters
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːzər / (AmE), / ˈkruːzə / (BrE)
a large motorboat equipped with living accommodations such as a cabin and plumbing, designed for people to live on board while traveling or at anchor

Examples

Luxury cruiser boat
He dreams of retiring and sailing a cruiser around the world.
The family bought a new cruiser equipped with two bedrooms and a small kitchen.
They spent the summer living aboard their cruiser as they traveled along the coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yacht - a medium-sized sailboat or motorboat used for recreation
