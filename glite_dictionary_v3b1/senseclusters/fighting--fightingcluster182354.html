fighting

noun
the act of physical struggle or contest
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of taking part in a physical struggle, conflict, or contest.

Examples

Street fighting
Fighting erupted when the rival groups met in the city square.
The ongoing fighting in the region has forced many families to flee their homes.
There was intense fighting between the two teams during the final match.
