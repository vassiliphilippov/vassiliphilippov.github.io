sickle lucerne

noun
a plant with yellow flowers and curved seed pods
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkəl lʊˈsɜrn / (AmE), / ˈsɪkəl ljuːˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a European plant of the legume family, naturalized in North America, with yellow flowers and sickle-shaped seed pods

Examples

Sickle lucerne plant
In some regions, sickle lucerne is valued as both a forage crop and a cover plant.
