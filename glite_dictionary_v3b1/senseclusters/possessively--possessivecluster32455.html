possessively

adverb
in a possessive manner
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛsɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈzɛsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner showing a desire to own or control something or someone.

Examples

To speak possessively
He spoke possessively about their shared apartment, making it clear he wanted control.
She clung possessively to his arm as they walked through the crowded room.
The cat curled possessively around its favorite toy, refusing to let anyone else near.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jealously - in a jealous manner; with envy or protectiveness
