inkatha

noun
Zulu celebratory dance
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
a traditional Zulu dance performed to celebrate special occasions

Examples

Inkatha dance celebration
The community gathered to watch the inkatha during the annual harvest festival.
Performers practiced the inkatha for weeks to prepare for the wedding celebration.
Tourists were invited to join the inkatha and experience Zulu culture firsthand.
