crystal

noun
fine, transparent glassware
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstl̩ /; / ˈkrɪs.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstl /; / ˈkrɪs.təl / (BrE)
fine, high-quality clear glass, often used for decorative glassware or ornaments
