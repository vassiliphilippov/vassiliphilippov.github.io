bine

noun
twining climbing plant (e.g. hops)
7.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / baɪn / (AmE), / baɪn / (BrE)
A twining plant, especially one whose stems climb by winding around a support, such as the hop plant used to flavor beer.

Examples

A bine plant
Hops are a type of bine commonly used in brewing beer.
The bine wound itself around the garden trellis throughout the summer.
Unlike a vine, a bine climbs by twisting its stem rather than using tendrils.
