tapioca

noun
cassava starch used as a food thickener
1.9 4.8
Pronunciation: /tæ.pi.ˈoʊ.kə/ /; / /tə.pi.ˈoʊ.kə/ (AmE), /tæ.pi.ˈəʊ.kə/ /; / /tə.pi.ˈəʊ.kə/ (BrE)
a starchy substance in the form of hard white grains, obtained from cassava and used in puddings and other foods to thicken them

Examples

Tapioca pudding
Tapioca pudding is a popular sweet treat made with milk and sugar.
Bubble tea contains chewy balls made from tapioca starch.
Many people use tapioca to thicken fruit pie fillings and desserts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cassava - a tropical plant with edible starchy roots
