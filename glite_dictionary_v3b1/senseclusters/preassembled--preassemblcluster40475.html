preassembled

adjective
already put together
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːəˈsɛmbəld / (AmE), / ˌpriːəˈsɛmbəld / (BrE)
already put together before being sold, delivered, or used

Examples

A preassembled bookshelf
The bookshelf was preassembled, so I could use it right away.
Buying a preassembled computer saved me a lot of time and effort.
All the furniture in the hotel room was preassembled for convenience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembled - put together from separate parts
  • synonym: ready-made - already made and ready to use
  • antonym: unassembled - not yet put together
