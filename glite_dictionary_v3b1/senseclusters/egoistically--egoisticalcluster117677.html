in a manner that is self-centered or focused only on oneself

Examples

To act egoistically
She egoistically took credit for the team's success without mentioning anyone else.
Acting egoistically can damage relationships with friends and colleagues.
He egoistically ignored the advice given by others, believing only his opinion mattered.

Synonyms

  • egotistically

  • narcissistically
