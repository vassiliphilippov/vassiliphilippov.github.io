axonometric

adjective
3D drawing style
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæksənoʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌæksənəʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
describing a type of drawing that represents a three-dimensional object on a two-dimensional surface without using perspective

Examples

Axonometric drawing
The engineer created an axonometric diagram to clearly show the machine's internal structure.
Students learned how to make axonometric sketches in their technical drawing class.
An axonometric view helps viewers understand the spatial relationships in a building design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isometric - a type of axonometric drawing with equal angles between axes
  • antonym: perspective - a drawing technique that shows objects as they appear to the eye with depth
