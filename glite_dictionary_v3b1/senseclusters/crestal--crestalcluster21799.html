crestal

adjective
of a crest or top
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛstl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstl̩ / (BrE)
relating to the crest or top of a structure, especially in anatomy

Examples

Crestal bone
The surgeon carefully examined the crestal bone before placing the dental implant.
A crestal incision was made to access the top of the ridge during the procedure.
Changes in crestal bone height can indicate the success of periodontal treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apical - relating to the tip or apex of a structure
