fold up

verb
to make (something) compact by folding
Pronunciation: / foʊld ʌp /; / foʊld ˈʌp / (AmE), / fəʊld ʌp /; / fəʊld ˈʌp / (BrE)
to bend, collapse, or arrange something so it becomes compact or easier to store
