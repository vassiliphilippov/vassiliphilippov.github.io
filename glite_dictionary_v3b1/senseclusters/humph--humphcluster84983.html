humph

interjection
expression of annoyance
9.9
Pronunciation: / hʌmf / (AmE), / hʌmf / (BrE)
an interjection used to show doubt, annoyance, or dissatisfaction

Examples

Humph of annoyance
"Humph, I don't see why we have to wait so long," he muttered.
She crossed her arms and said, "humph, that was not what I expected at all."
When told the news, he simply replied, "humph, is that so?"

Related Terms

  • synonym: hmm - an interjection expressing hesitation or doubt
  • synonym: ugh - an interjection expressing disgust or annoyance
