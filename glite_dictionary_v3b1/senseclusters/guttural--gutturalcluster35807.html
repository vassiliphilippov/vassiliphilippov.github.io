Examples

Guttural voice

Synonyms

    4.4 0.06/m
    croaky is rough and rasping like a frog's call, while guttural is a harsh throat sound resembling a crow's croak.
  • throaty
    6.1 0.93/m
    throaty is low and husky as if made deep in the throat, while guttural is harsher and more like a croak.
  • gravelly
    6.5 0.62/m
    gravelly is rough and grating with a sand-like texture, while guttural is a harsher throat sound with animal-like croaks.
  • hoarse
    3.4 2.62/m
    hoarse is a rough, weak voice often from illness or shouting, while guttural is a strong harsh sound made in the throat.
