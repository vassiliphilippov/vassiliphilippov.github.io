home care

noun
care at home
2.8
Pronunciation: /hoʊm ker/ /; / /hoʊm keər/ (AmE), /həʊm keə/ (BrE)
medical or personal assistance provided to people in their own homes, especially for the elderly or disabled

Examples

Elderly home care

Many families arrange home care for their elderly relatives to ensure they are comfortable.
The agency provides professional home care services for people with chronic illnesses.
