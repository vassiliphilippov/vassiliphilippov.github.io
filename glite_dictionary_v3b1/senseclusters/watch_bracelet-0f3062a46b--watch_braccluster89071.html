watch bracelet

noun
band attaching a wristwatch to the wrist
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ ˈbreɪslət / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ˈbreɪslət / (BrE)
a band, often made of metal links, designed to attach a wristwatch to the wrist

Examples

A watch bracelet band
He replaced the old watch bracelet with a new stainless steel one.
Some luxury watches come with a gold watch bracelet.
The jeweler resized my watch bracelet to fit my wrist perfectly.
