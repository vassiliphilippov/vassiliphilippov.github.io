wheelwright

noun
person who makes or repairs wooden wheels
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈwiːlˌraɪt / (BrE)
a person who makes and repairs wooden wheels, especially for carts and wagons

Examples

A wheelwright repairs wheels
As a wheelwright, he combined woodworking expertise with metalworking skills.
The village wheelwright was skilled in crafting sturdy cart wheels from ash wood.
Years ago, every small town had a resident wheelwright to keep carriages in working order.
