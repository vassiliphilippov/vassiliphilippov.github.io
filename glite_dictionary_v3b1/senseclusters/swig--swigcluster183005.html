swig

noun
a large swallow or drink
Pronunciation: / swɪɡ / (AmE), / swɪɡ / (BrE)
a large and hurried swallow or drink, especially of an alcoholic beverage

Examples

A big swig of water
He took a long swig of water after finishing the race.
She offered me a swig of her lemonade on the hot afternoon.
With a quick swig from his flask, she tried to calm her nerves.
