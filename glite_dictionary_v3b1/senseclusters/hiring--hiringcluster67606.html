hiring

noun
act of employing
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving someone a job or employing someone for work

Examples

Company hiring event
The company increased hiring to meet the growing demand for its products.
After months of slow business, the restaurant finally resumed hiring new staff.
Many graduates are hopeful that hiring will improve in the coming year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firing - the act of dismissing someone from a job
  • synonym: recruitment - the process of finding and hiring new employees
