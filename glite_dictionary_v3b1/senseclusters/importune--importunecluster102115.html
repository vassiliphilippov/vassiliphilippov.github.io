importune

verb
plead or beg persistently
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪm.pɔːrˈtuːn / (AmE), / ˌɪm.pɔːˈtjuːn / (BrE)
to ask someone persistently and urgently for something, often in a way that is annoying or difficult to ignore

Examples

To importune a favour
He tried to importune his boss for a day off, sending several emails throughout the week.
Reporters importuned the celebrity for an interview outside the courthouse.
She continued to importune him for money, never letting up despite his refusals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beg - to ask someone earnestly for something
