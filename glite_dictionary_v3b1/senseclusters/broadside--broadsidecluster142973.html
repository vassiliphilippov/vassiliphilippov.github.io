broadside

noun
the entire side of a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd.saɪd / (BrE)
the whole side of a ship or vessel from stem to stern

Examples

The ship's broadside
The damaged vessel drifted with its broadside exposed to the rough waves.
The old warship displayed a row of cannons along its massive broadside.
Tourists took photos as the ferry docked broadside to the pier.
