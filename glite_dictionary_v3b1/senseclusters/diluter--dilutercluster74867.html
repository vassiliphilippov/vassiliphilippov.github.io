diluter

noun
device for dilution
Pronunciation: / daɪˈluːtər / (AmE), / daɪˈluːtə / (BrE)
a device or machine used to reduce the concentration of a liquid or gas by mixing it with another substance

Examples

Automatic diluter machine
The laboratory technician used a diluter to prepare the chemical solutions accurately.
Modern diluters can process hundreds of samples in a short time.
A high-quality diluter ensures consistent results in medical testing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concentrator - a device that increases the concentration of a substance
  • synonym: dilution - the process of making a liquid less concentrated
