founder

noun
hoof inflammation in horses
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndə / (BrE)
inflammation of the laminae connecting the hoof wall to the underlying structures of a horse's foot, often resulting in lameness

Examples

Horse founder
If a horse overeats grain, it may be at risk for developing founder.
Proper hoof care can help prevent founder in horses, which can be very debilitating.
The veterinarian diagnosed the horse with founder after observing its painful gait and swollen hooves.
