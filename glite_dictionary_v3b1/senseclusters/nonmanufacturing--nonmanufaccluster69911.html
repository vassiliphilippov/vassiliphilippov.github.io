nonmanufacturing

noun
service sector
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnmænjuˈfæktʃərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmænjuˈfæktʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
the part of an economy consisting of services and activities that do not involve the production of manufactured goods

Examples

Nonmanufacturing sector
The city has seen rapid growth in its nonmanufacturing sector over the last decade.
Jobs in nonmanufacturing include roles in healthcare, education, and finance.
Government policies are encouraging investment in nonmanufacturing industries to diversify the economy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manufacturing - the process of making goods in factories
  • synonym: service industry - businesses that provide services rather than goods
