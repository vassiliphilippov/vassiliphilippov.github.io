founder

noun
person who establishes something
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndə / (BrE)
a person who establishes an organization, institution, or settlement

Examples

Founder of a company
As the founder of the charity, she played a crucial role in its early success.
Steve Jobs is widely recognized as a founder of Apple Inc.
The museum displayed portraits of the school's founder in the main hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: originator - a person who creates or initiates something
  • antonym: successor - a person who follows another in a position
