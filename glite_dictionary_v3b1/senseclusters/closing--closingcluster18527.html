closing

noun
approaching a destination; narrowing a gap
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving closer to a particular destination or of narrowing a gap between two things

Examples

Closing the gap
As the race entered the final lap, the closing of the gap between the runners became evident.
The pilot adjusted course to increase the closing speed with the refueling aircraft.
The ship's rapid closing on the coast required immediate action from the crew.
