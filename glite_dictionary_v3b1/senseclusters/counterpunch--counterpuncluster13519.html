counterpunch

noun
a punch thrown in response to an attack

Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.tər.pʌntʃ/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.tə.pʌntʃ/ (BrE)
A punch thrown in response to an opponent's attack, especially in boxing.

Examples

A counterpunch in boxing
A well-timed counterpunch can change the outcome of a match.

The boxer landed a sharp counterpunch after dodging his opponent's jab.
