panko

noun
Japanese breadcrumb
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpæŋkəʊ / (BrE)
a type of light, flaky breadcrumb commonly used in Japanese and Western cooking to create a crispy coating

Examples

Light panko crumbs
She coated the chicken in panko before frying it for extra crunch.
The chef prefers using panko instead of regular breadcrumbs for a lighter texture.
You can find panko in the international aisle of most supermarkets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breadcrumb - small pieces of dried bread used in cooking
