mass

noun
a large amount or number (group)
1.0
Pronunciation: / mæs / (AmE), / mæs /; / mɑːs / (BrE)
a large group, quantity, or accumulation of people or things

Examples

A mass of people
A mass of tourists crowded the square during the festival.
He inherited a mass of old photographs from his grandmother.
There is a mass of evidence supporting the new theory.
A mass of paperwork quickly piled up on her desk.
Protesters gathered in a mass outside the government building.
The company has accumulated a mass of data about its customers.
A mass of people gathered outside the stadium, waiting for the gates to open.
In the distance, a mass of clouds signaled an approaching storm.
The basement was filled with a mass of old newspapers and magazines.
