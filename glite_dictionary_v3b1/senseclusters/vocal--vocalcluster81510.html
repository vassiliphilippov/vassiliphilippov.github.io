vocal

adjective
full of the sound of voices
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.kəl / (BrE)
(of a place) full of the sound of voices; noisy with people talking or shouting.

Examples

Vocal crowd
After the concert, the streets nearby became vocal with fans celebrating and singing.
The playground is especially vocal on sunny afternoons when children are out playing.
The school cafeteria was always vocal during lunchtime, filled with students' animated conversations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisy - full of or making a lot of noise
  • antonym: quiet - making very little noise
