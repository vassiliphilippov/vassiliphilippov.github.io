prodigy

noun
impressive example or feat
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːdədʒi / (AmE), / ˈprɒdɪdʒi / (BrE)
An impressive or extraordinary example of a particular quality or achievement.

Examples

A prodigy of science
Many considered her recovery a prodigy of medical science.
The chef's creation was hailed as a prodigy of culinary art.
Their teamwork on the project was described as a prodigy of cooperation.
