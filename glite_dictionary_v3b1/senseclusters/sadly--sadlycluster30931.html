sadly

adverb
in a sad manner
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsædli / (AmE), / ˈsædli / (BrE)
In a sad or sorrowful manner; with sadness.

Examples

He spoke sadly
He shook his head sadly and turned away from the window.
She spoke sadly about the loss of her old friend.
The team members sadly accepted their defeat after the final match.
