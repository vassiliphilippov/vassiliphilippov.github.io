inst

adjective
of the present month (abbreviation, formal)
Pronunciation: / ɪnst / (AmE), / ɪnst / (BrE)
(abbreviation) of the present month; used in formal correspondence to refer to dates within the current month.

Examples

The inst month
I received your payment on the 5th inst, as mentioned in your letter.
Please reply to my email of the 12th inst at your earliest convenience.
The meeting scheduled for the 18th inst has been postponed.
