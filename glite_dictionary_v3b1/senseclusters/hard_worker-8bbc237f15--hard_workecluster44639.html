hard worker

noun
person who works diligently
Pronunciation: / hɑrd ˈwɝːkər / (AmE), / hɑːd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who works with great effort and diligence

Examples

A hard worker person
As a hard worker, he often stays late to finish important projects.
Many employers value a hard worker over someone with just good qualifications.
She is known as a hard worker who always goes the extra mile for her team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
