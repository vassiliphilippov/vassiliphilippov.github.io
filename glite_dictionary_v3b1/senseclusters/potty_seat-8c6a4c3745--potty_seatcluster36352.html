potty seat

noun
child's toilet training seat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑti ˌsit / (AmE), / ˈpɒti ˌsiːt / (BrE)
a small seat, often placed on top of a regular toilet or as a standalone, used by young children when learning to use the toilet

Examples

A potty seat for toddlers
Parents often use a potty seat to help their toddlers transition out of diapers.
The nursery provides each child with their own potty seat for hygiene reasons.
We bought a colorful potty seat to make toilet training more appealing for our child.

Related Terms

  • synonym: potty - a child's small toilet for training
  • synonym: toilet seat - the seat part of a conventional toilet
