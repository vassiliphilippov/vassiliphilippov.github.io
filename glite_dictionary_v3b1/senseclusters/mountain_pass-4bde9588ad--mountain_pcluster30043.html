mountain pass

noun
navigable route through a mountain range
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tən pæs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn pɑːs / (BrE)
a navigable route through a mountain range, typically lower than the surrounding peaks

Examples

A mountain pass route
Many ancient trade routes crossed difficult mountain passes to connect civilizations.
The fog made it dangerous to drive through the mountain pass at night.
We hiked up to the mountain pass before sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pass - a route over or through mountains
