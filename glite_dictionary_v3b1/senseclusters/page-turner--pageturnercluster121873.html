page-turner

noun
very engaging, exciting book
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪdʒ ˌtɝːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪdʒ ˌtɜːnə / (BrE)
A book so engaging and exciting that the reader is eager to keep reading.

Examples

A page-turner book
Her memoir is a true page-turner, filled with suspense and emotion from start to finish.
I'm looking for a good page-turner to take on my vacation.
That detective novel was such a page-turner that I finished it in one night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bestseller - a book that sells in large numbers
  • synonym: potboiler - a book or work produced quickly to make money rather than for artistic merit
