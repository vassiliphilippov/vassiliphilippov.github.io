Eucalyptus tereticornis

noun
a tall Australian eucalyptus tree species
9.8
Pronunciation: / juːˈkælɪptəs təˌrɛtɪˈkɔrnɪs / (AmE), / juːˈkælɪptəs tɛˌrɛtɪˈkɔːnɪs / (BrE)
A tall species of eucalyptus tree native to Queensland, New South Wales, and Victoria, known for its smooth bark and significant timber value.

Examples

Tall Eucalyptus tereticornis tree

The Eucalyptus tereticornis is commonly found along riverbanks in eastern Australia.
The timber from Eucalyptus tereticornis is valued for construction and fencing.
