velociraptor

noun
small, fast carnivorous dinosaur
Pronunciation: / vəˈlɑsɪˌræptər / (AmE), / vɪˌlɒsɪˈræptə / (BrE)
a small, fast-moving carnivorous dinosaur of the genus Velociraptor, which lived during the Late Cretaceous period and is believed to be closely related to birds.

Examples

The velociraptor dinosaur
In the movie, the velociraptor chased the scientists through the jungle.
Paleontologists believe that velociraptor may have had feathers.
The fossilized remains of a velociraptor were discovered in Mongolia.
