popcorn pisser

noun
person who overreacts to films or events
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑp.kɔrn ˈpɪs.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɒp.kɔːn ˈpɪs.ə / (BrE)
(slang) A person who is easily frightened, overly anxious, or reacts with extreme amusement or excitement, especially during movies or events.
