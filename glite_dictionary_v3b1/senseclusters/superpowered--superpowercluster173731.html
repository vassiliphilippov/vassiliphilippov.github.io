superpowered

adjective
having extraordinary powers
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌpaʊərd / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌpaʊəd / (BrE)
having special abilities or powers that are much greater than those of ordinary people

Examples

A superpowered hero
The superpowered hero saved the city from disaster with her incredible strength.
In the comic book, a superpowered villain threatened to take over the world.
Scientists imagined a superpowered human who could run faster than any animal.
