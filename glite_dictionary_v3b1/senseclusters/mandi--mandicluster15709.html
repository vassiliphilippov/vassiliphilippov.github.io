mandi

noun
South Asian market
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌndi / (AmE), / ˈmʌndi / (BrE)
a market, especially in South Asia, where agricultural products are bought and sold

Examples

Village mandi
Farmers bring their crops to the mandi early in the morning to sell them.
The mandi was crowded with people buying fresh fruits and vegetables.
He visits the mandi every week to purchase grains for his shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bazaar - a marketplace, especially in the Middle East or South Asia
  • synonym: market - a place where goods are bought and sold
