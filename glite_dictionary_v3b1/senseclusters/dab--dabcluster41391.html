dab

noun
dance move; head dropped, arm raised
Pronunciation: / dæb / (AmE), / dæb / (BrE)
a dance move in which a person drops their head while raising an arm and elbow, often resembling a sneezing gesture

Examples

Victory dab
After scoring the goal, he celebrated with a dab on the field.
She taught her little brother how to do the dab at the party.
The dab quickly became popular on social media as a meme.
