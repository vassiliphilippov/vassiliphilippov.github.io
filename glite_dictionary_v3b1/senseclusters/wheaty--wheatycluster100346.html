wheaty

adjective
like wheat
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːti / (AmE), / ˈwiːti / (BrE)
having the taste, smell, or qualities characteristic of wheat

Examples

Wheaty smell
The bakery was filled with a warm, wheaty scent every morning.
She preferred the wheaty flavor of whole grain bread over white bread.
The beer had a slightly wheaty aftertaste that lingered pleasantly.

Related Terms
