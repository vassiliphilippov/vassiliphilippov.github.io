coaxially

adverb
sharing an axis
7.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈæksiəli / (AmE), / kəʊˈæksiəli / (BrE)
in a way that two or more objects share the same axis or center line

Examples

Aligned coaxially
The two pipes are aligned coaxially to ensure a smooth flow of water.
The engineer installed the antennas coaxially for optimal signal transmission.
The gears must be positioned coaxially to function correctly in the machine.
