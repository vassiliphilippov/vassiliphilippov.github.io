nonsubscriber

noun
person who does not agree
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səbˈskraɪbər / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səbˈskraɪbə / (BrE)
a person who does not subscribe to, support, or agree with a service, publication, organization, or idea

Examples

Nonsubscriber to the service
As a nonsubscriber to the magazine, she could not access the premium articles.
He was known as a nonsubscriber to traditional political views in his community.
The club welcomed both subscribers and nonsubscribers to the open discussion.
The online course offers some free content for nonsubscribers to try before joining.
Only nonsubscribers need to see the promotional advertisements on the website.
Special discounts are not available to nonsubscribers of the magazine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissenter - a person who disagrees with commonly held views
  • antonym: subscriber - a person who agrees with or supports a belief, idea, or service
  • synonym: user - a person who uses a service or product
