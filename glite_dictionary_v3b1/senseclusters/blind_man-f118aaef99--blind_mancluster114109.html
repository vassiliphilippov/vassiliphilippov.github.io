blind man

noun
man without sight
Pronunciation: / blaɪnd mæn / (AmE), / blaɪnd mæn / (BrE)
a man who cannot see because of blindness

Examples

A blind man walks
The blind man listened carefully to the sounds around him as he walked.
A blind man gave a moving speech about overcoming challenges at the event.
The guide dog helped the blind man cross the busy intersection safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blind person - someone who cannot see
  • antonym: sighted man - a man who can see
