streetcar

noun
electric rail vehicle for urban transport
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈstriːtkɑː / (BrE)
A wheeled vehicle that runs on rails along public streets and is powered by electricity, used for carrying passengers within a city or town.

Examples

An electric streetcar
He could see the approaching streetcar from his apartment window.
Many cities have brought back the streetcar as an eco-friendly way to commute.
The streetcar glided quietly down the tracks, stopping at every corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tram - rail vehicle for urban passenger transport, especially in British English
