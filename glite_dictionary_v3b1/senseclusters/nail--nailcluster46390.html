nail

verb
attach with a nail
1.9
Pronunciation: / neɪl / (AmE), / neɪl / (BrE)
to attach, fasten, or secure something using a nail or nails

Examples

To nail a board
He carefully nailed the sign to the front gate so everyone could see it.
She is nailing shelves onto the wall to create more storage space.
The workers nailed the wooden planks together to build the fence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detach - to unfasten or remove something that was fixed
  • synonym: fasten - to fix or secure something firmly
