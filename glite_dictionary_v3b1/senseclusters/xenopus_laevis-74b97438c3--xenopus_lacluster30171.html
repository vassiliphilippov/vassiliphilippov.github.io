Xenopus laevis

noun
species of African clawed frog
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌziːnəpəs ˈleɪvɪs / (AmE), / ˌziːnəpəs ˈleɪvɪs / (BrE)
A species of tongueless aquatic frog native to Africa, commonly known as the African clawed frog, used extensively in scientific research.
