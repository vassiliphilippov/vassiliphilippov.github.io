puppy

noun
an inexperienced young person
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpi / (AmE), / ˈpʌpi / (BrE)
an inexperienced or naive young person, especially one regarded as presumptuous or overconfident

Examples

A cocky puppy
Don’t let that cocky puppy fool you; he still has a lot to learn.
He was hardly more than a puppy when he started working at the firm.
The young puppy strutted into the meeting, brimming with confidence but lacking experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greenhorn - a person who is new to or inexperienced at something
  • synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
  • antonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a particular field
