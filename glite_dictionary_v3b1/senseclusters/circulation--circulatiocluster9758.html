circulation

noun
total number of sold/distributed copies (media)
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /sɜːr.kjə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sɜːr.kjʊ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sɜː.kjə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sɜː.kjʊ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
