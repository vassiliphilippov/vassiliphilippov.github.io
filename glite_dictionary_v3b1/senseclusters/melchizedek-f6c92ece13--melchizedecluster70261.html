Melchizedek

proper noun
biblical priest-king
Pronunciation: / mɛlˈkɪzədɛk / (AmE), / mɛlˈkɪzɪdɛk / (BrE)
a priest and king mentioned in the Bible, often regarded as a symbol of righteousness and peace

Examples

Melchizedek as a symbol
The mysterious figure of Melchizedek appears in the Book of Genesis as both king and priest.
Many scholars debate the significance of Melchizedek in biblical history.
In some traditions, Melchizedek is seen as a symbol of eternal priesthood.
