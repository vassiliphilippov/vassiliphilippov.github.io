Synonyms

  • submergence
    8.4 0.10/m
    submergence is the process of being completely covered by water, while sinking is going down below a liquid's surface.
  • going under
    8.1 0.18/m
    going under is used for a ship or boat that falls below the water, while sinking covers any object moving down.
  • submersion
    6.2 0.08/m
    submersion means putting something fully under water on purpose, while sinking describes objects that go down by themselves.
  • sinkage
    7.4 0.02/m
    sinkage refers to the measured amount or settling of things, while sinking is the general act of going below the water.
