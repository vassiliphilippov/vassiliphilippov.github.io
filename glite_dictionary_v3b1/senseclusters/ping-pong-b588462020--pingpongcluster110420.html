Ping-Pong

noun
table tennis game played with paddles and a light ball
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋ pɑŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ pɒŋ / (BrE)
A game similar to tennis, played on a table with small paddles and a lightweight ball, where players hit the ball back and forth over a net.

Examples

Play Ping-Pong game
He became the school Ping-Pong champion after months of practice.
They spent the afternoon playing Ping-Pong and laughing together.
We set up a Ping-Pong table in the garage for friendly matches.
