Westphalian

adjective
state sovereignty principle
9.9
Pronunciation: /west.ˈfeɪ.liː.ən/ /; / /west.ˈfeɪ.lɪ.ən/ (AmE), /west.ˈfeɪ.liː.ən/ /; / /west.ˈfeɪ.lɪ.ən/ (BrE)
relating to the principle of state sovereignty and territorial integrity, especially as established by the Peace of Westphalia in 1648

Examples

Westphalian system

Many scholars debate whether the Westphalian model still applies in today's globalized world.
The concept of Westphalian sovereignty emphasizes the independence of each nation-state.
