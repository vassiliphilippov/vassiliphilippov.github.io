cryopreserve

verb
to freeze for preservation
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪoʊprɪˈzɜrv / (AmE), / ˌkraɪəʊprɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
to preserve cells, tissues, or organisms by cooling them to very low temperatures to prevent decay

Examples

To cryopreserve stem cells
Researchers cryopreserve stem cells so they can be used in future medical treatments.
It is common to cryopreserve eggs for women who want to delay pregnancy.
The laboratory successfully cryopreserved tissue samples for long-term storage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeze - to make something become solid by cooling
  • antonym: thaw - to become unfrozen or to warm up after being frozen
