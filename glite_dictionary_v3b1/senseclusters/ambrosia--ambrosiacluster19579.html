ambrosia

noun
fruit dessert with oranges, bananas, and coconut
7.6
Pronunciation: / æmˈbroʊʒə / (AmE), / æmˈbrəʊziə / (BrE)
A dessert made from oranges, bananas, and shredded coconut, often mixed with other fruits or whipped cream.

Examples

Fruit ambrosia salad
For dessert, she served a bowl of ambrosia topped with extra coconut flakes.
My grandmother's recipe for ambrosia includes pineapple in addition to oranges and bananas.
Nothing reminds me of summer picnics like a chilled dish of ambrosia.
