devastation

noun
overwhelming grief or emotional shock
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛvəˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛvəˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of extreme shock, grief, or overwhelming sadness, often after a traumatic event or great loss

Examples

Emotional devastation
She struggled to cope with the devastation she felt after losing her job.
The news of his friend's accident left him in a state of devastation for days.
There was a look of utter devastation on his face when he heard the verdict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: despair - the complete loss or absence of hope
  • antonym: relief - a feeling of reassurance and relaxation following release from anxiety or distress
