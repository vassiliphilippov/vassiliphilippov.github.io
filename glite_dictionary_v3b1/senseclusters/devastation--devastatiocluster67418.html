devastation

noun
overwhelming grief or emotional shock
2.6 4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛvəˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛvəˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

There was a look of utter devastation on his face when he heard the verdict.
