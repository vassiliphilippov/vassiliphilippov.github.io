shaykh

noun
Arab tribal leader
8.9
Pronunciation: /ʃeɪk/ (AmE), /ʃeɪk/ (BrE)
a tribal chief or respected leader, especially in Arab regions, who holds authority over a clan or community

Examples

Tribal shaykh
The shaykh welcomed visitors to the village and offered them traditional hospitality.
After the meeting, the shaykh made a decision that affected the entire community.
Young men often seek advice from the shaykh before making important life choices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chief - the leader of a tribe or clan
  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and is guided by another
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
