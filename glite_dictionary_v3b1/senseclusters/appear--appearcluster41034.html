appear

verb
come into sight; become visible
1.5
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪr / (AmE), / əˈpɪə / (BrE)
to come into sight, become visible, or begin to exist

Examples

Appear in the sky
A rainbow may appear in the sky after a rainstorm.
She was surprised when he appeared at her front door unannounced.
The ship suddenly appeared on the horizon, exciting all the passengers.
A strange light appeared in the sky just after midnight.
It is not yet known when life first appeared on Earth.
Suddenly, a new species appeared in the fossil record.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emerge - to come out into view
  • antonym: vanish - to disappear from view
