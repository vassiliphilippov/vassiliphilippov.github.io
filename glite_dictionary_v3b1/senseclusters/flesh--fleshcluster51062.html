flesh

noun
the human body; physical substance
6.4
Pronunciation: / flɛʃ / (AmE), / flɛʃ / (BrE)
the human body regarded as physical substance, especially in contrast to the spirit or soul

Examples

Flesh and spirit
He tried to resist temptation, but his flesh gave in.
In the old tale, the battle between spirit and flesh is a central theme.
Some philosophers believe the flesh is weak compared to the mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body - the physical structure of a person or an animal
  • antonym: spirit - the nonphysical part of a person, contrasted with the body
