nubble

verb
to make bumpy
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌb.əl/ (AmE), /ˈnʌb.əl/ (BrE)
to form or cover with small rounded lumps or nubs, giving a textured surface.

Examples

To nubble the surface
The artisan nubbled the clay surface to create an intentionally rough, tactile finish.
The fabric was nubbled to give it a unique, textured appearance.
Over time, the old sweater nubbled from frequent washing and wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bump - to make or become raised in small lumps
  • antonym: smooth - to make a surface even and without lumps
