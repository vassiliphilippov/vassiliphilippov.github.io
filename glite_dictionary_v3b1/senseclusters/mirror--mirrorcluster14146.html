mirror

noun
faithful representation or reflection
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrər / (AmE), / ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
something that accurately represents or reflects something else, especially in a faithful or true way.

Examples

A mirror of society
A true friend can be a mirror for your character, reflecting your strengths and flaws.
His novels are a mirror of the times in which he lived.
History often serves as a mirror for society's values and beliefs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distortion - a representation that twists or misrepresents the facts
  • synonym: reflection - an image, representation, or counterpart
