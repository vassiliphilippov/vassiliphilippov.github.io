disembody

verb
free from a body or physical form
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəmˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪmˈbɒdi / (BrE)
to free (something) from a body, physical form, or concrete reality

Examples

To disembody thoughts
He tried to disembody his thoughts from material concerns.
Some philosophies seek to disembody the mind from the physical world.
The virtual experience can disembody users from their immediate environment.
