look back

verb
turn to see behind oneself
Pronunciation: / lʊk bæk / (AmE), / lʊk bæk / (BrE)
to turn one's head or eyes to see what is behind oneself

Examples

To look back at someone
As I left the building, I didn't look back even once.
He was told not to look back while climbing the stairs.
She heard a noise behind her and quickly looked back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glance back - to quickly look behind oneself
  • antonym: look forward - to turn one's gaze ahead or think about the future
