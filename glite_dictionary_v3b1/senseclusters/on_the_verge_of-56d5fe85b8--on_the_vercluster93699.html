on the verge of

preposition
almost about to experience or do something
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə vɜrdʒ əv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə vɜːdʒ əv / (BrE)
very close to experiencing something or to doing something; almost at the point of a particular event or feeling

Examples

The city is on the verge of major reforms that could change everything.
