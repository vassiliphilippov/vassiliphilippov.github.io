mediastinal

adjective
of the mediastinum
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdiəˈstaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːdiəˈstaɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to the mediastinum, the area in the chest between the lungs

Examples

Mediastinal masses
The doctor ordered a scan to check for mediastinal masses in the patient's chest.
Enlarged mediastinal lymph nodes can be a sign of infection or cancer.
The surgeon carefully avoided the mediastinal structures during the procedure.
