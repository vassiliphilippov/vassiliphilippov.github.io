thalamic

adjective
of the thalamus
Pronunciation: / θəˈlæmɪk / (AmE), / θəˈlæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the thalamus, a part of the brain that processes sensory information

Examples

Thalamic stroke
The MRI revealed a thalamic lesion that explained the patient's symptoms.
Researchers are studying thalamic activity to better understand sleep disorders.
Damage to thalamic regions can affect how the brain processes sensory signals.

  • synonym: cerebral - relating to the brain or cerebrum
