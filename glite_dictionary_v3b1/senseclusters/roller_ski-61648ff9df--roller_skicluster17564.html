roller ski

noun
a ski with wheels for dry training
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər ski / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə skiː / (BrE)
A ski fitted with wheels, used for training on dry pavement when snow is unavailable.

Examples

A roller ski with wheels
After a few lessons, she became confident using her new roller skis on the bike path.
Some athletes use roller skis as part of their off-season training routine.
The cross-country team practiced on roller skis over the summer to stay in shape.
