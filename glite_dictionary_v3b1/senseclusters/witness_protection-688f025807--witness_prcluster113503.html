witness protection

noun
official program to protect criminal case witnesses
4.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪt.nəs prə.ˈtek.ʃən/ /; / /ˈwɪt.nɪs prə.ˈtek.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈwɪt.nəs prə.ˈtek.ʃən/ /; / /ˈwɪt.nɪs prə.ˈtek.ʃən/ (BrE)
an official program that protects individuals who testify as witnesses in criminal cases from threats or harm, often by relocating and providing them with new identities
