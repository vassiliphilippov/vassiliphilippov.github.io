fascinator

noun
person or thing that attracts
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that strongly attracts interest, attention, or admiration

Examples

A real fascinator
With his quick wit and confidence, he became the fascinator of the evening.
The new sculpture was a real fascinator for visitors at the museum.
Her unique style made her a fascinator among her classmates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charmer - a person who attracts and delights others
  • synonym: magnet - a person or thing that attracts others strongly
  • antonym: repellent - a person or thing that causes others to avoid or dislike them
