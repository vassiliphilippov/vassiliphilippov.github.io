letrozole

noun
fertility medication
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈle.trə.zoʊl/ (AmE), /ˈle.trə.zəʊl/ (BrE)
a medication used to induce ovulation and treat infertility, especially in women with certain reproductive conditions

Examples

Letrozole for ovulation
Her doctor prescribed letrozole to help regulate her ovulation cycle.
Many fertility specialists use letrozole as a first-line treatment for certain types of infertility.
After taking letrozole, she noticed changes in her menstrual cycle as expected.
