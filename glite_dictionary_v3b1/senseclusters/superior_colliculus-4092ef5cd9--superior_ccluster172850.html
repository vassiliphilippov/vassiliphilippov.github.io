superior colliculus

noun
a visual center in the midbrain
9.9
Pronunciation: /suː.ˈpɪr.iː.ər kə.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ /; / /sʊ.ˈpɪr.iː.ər kə.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ (AmE), /suː.ˈpɪə.riː.ə kɒ.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ /; / /sjuː.ˈpɪə.riː.ə kɒ.ˈlɪk.jʊ.ləs/ (BrE)
a paired structure on the dorsal midbrain that acts as a principal visual center, connecting signals from the retina to the striate cortex and coordinating visual processing and reflexes
