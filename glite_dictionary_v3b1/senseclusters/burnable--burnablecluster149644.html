burnable

adjective
able to be destroyed to reduce token supply
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːr.nə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈbɜː.nə.bəl/ (BrE)
(in cryptocurrency) able to be permanently removed or destroyed from circulation according to a protocol (for example, by sending to an irrecoverable address or executing a burn function).

Examples

Burnable tokens
The project issued burnable tokens that would be burned to decrease total supply.
Investors favored the burnable coin because scheduled burns could make the asset more scarce.
The developer explained how to make tokens burnable by sending them to an irrecoverable address.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burn - in cryptocurrency, to destroy tokens or coins so they cannot be spent or retrieved
