neo-baroque

noun
art or architecture imitating Baroque style
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊ bəˈroʊk / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊ bəˈrəʊk / (BrE)
A style in art or architecture that is inspired by or imitates the forms, motifs, and characteristics of 17th-century Baroque.

Examples

The neo-baroque style
Her paintings are a modern interpretation of the neo-baroque, blending digital media with traditional techniques.
Several theaters in the city showcase neo-baroque architecture, echoing the grandeur of the original Baroque period.
The museum's new wing was designed in a distinctly neo-baroque style, with elaborate ornamentation and dramatic lighting.
