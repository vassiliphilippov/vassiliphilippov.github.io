pong

noun
sharp bouncing sound
Pronunciation: / pɑŋ / (AmE), / pɒŋ / (BrE)
a short, sharp sound made when something hits or bounces off a surface

Examples

A loud pong sound
The tennis ball made a loud pong as it bounced off the table.
Every time the paddle hit the ball, there was a distinct pong in the quiet room.
He laughed when the rubber ball produced a sharp pong against the wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
