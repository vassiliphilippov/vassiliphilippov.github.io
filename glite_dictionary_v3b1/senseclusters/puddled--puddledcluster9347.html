puddled

adjective
mentally confused
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdəld / (AmE), / ˈpʌdəld / (BrE)
mentally confused or unable to think clearly, often due to tiredness or stress

Examples

Feeling puddled
After the long exam, she felt puddled and couldn't remember simple facts.
By the end of the double shift, he was completely puddled and needed a break.
The heat made everyone so puddled that no one could focus on the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear-headed - able to think clearly and sensibly
  • synonym: muddled - confused and unable to think clearly
