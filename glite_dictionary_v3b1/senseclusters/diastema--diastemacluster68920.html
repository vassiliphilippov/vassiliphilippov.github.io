diastema

noun
gap between two teeth
5.1
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæstəmə / (AmE), / daɪˈastɪmə / (BrE)
A space or gap between two teeth, especially the upper front teeth.

Examples

A diastema gap
Children often have a diastema that closes as their permanent teeth come in.
Some people consider a diastema between their front teeth to be a distinctive feature.
The orthodontist recommended closing the diastema for aesthetic reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gap - an opening or space between two things
