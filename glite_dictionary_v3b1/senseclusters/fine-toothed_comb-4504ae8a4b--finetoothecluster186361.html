fine-toothed comb

noun
comb with closely spaced teeth
9.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /faɪn tuːθt koʊm/ /; / /faɪn tuːðd koʊm/ /; / /faɪn tuːθt kuːm/ (AmE), /faɪn tuːθt kəʊm/ /; / /faɪn tuːθt kuːm/ (BrE)
a comb with teeth set very close together, used for precise grooming, detangling hair, or removing small items like lice
