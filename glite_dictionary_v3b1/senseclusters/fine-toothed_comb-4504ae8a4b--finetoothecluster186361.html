fine-toothed comb

noun
comb with closely spaced teeth
9.9
Pronunciation: / faɪn tuːθt koʊm /; / ˌfaɪn ˈtuːθt koʊm / (AmE), / faɪn tuːθt kəʊm /; / ˌfaɪn ˈtuːθt kəʊm / (BrE)
a comb with teeth set very close together, used for precise grooming, detangling hair, or removing small items like lice

Examples

A fine-toothed comb for lice

The nurse used a fine-toothed comb to check the child’s hair for lice.
A fine-toothed comb is especially useful for managing lice or very curly hair.
Investigators went over the crime scene with a fine-toothed comb to find any evidence.
She carefully ran the fine-toothed comb through her daughter's hair to remove tangles.
