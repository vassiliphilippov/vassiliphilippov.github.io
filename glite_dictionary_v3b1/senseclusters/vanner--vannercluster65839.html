vanner

noun
van driver or seller
Pronunciation: / ˈvænər / (AmE), / ˈvænə / (BrE)
a person who drives or works from a van, especially someone who sells or delivers goods from it

Examples

Bread vanner
The vanner parked his van outside the market to sell fresh bread to customers.
Every morning, the vanner delivers newspapers to homes across the city.
We bought ice cream from a friendly vanner at the park last weekend.

  • synonym: vendor - a person who sells goods
