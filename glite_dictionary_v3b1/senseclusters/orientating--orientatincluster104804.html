orientating

adjective
determining or positioning with respect to a reference point
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪənˌteɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
positioning or determining one's bearings, physically or intellectually; aligning to a reference point or system.

Examples

To orientate yourself
As she walked through the museum, her orientating skills helped her navigate the complex corridors.
The orientating process for new students includes campus tours and introductory lectures.
The hikers found the trail markers useful for orientating themselves in the dense forest.
