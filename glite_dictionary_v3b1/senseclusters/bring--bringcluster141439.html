Synonyms

  • cause
    1.0 262/m
    cause is a general word for making something happen, while bring often means causing someone or something to be in a particular state.
  • give rise
    3.5 7.73/m
    give rise means to cause something to happen or exist, while bring often focuses on causing someone or something to reach a state.
  • induce
    5.3 17/m
    induce often sounds formal and shows a deliberate act that makes something start, while bring is more general and everyday.
  • engender
    6.9 4.87/m
    engender is used for creating feelings or situations in a formal style, while bring is used broadly to cause a state or result.
