beach

verb
come or be brought onto a beach
5.4 1.0/m
Pronunciation: / biÀêt É / (AmE), / biÀêt É / (BrE)
(of a ship, boat, animal, or object) to come or be brought onto a beach or shore; to run aground on land beside water.

Examples

To beach a boat
A large whale was found beached on the shore early this morning.
During the evacuation, several landing craft beached along the coast.
The fishermen decided to beach their boat before the storm arrived.
