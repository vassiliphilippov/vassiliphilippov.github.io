scintigram

noun
diagnostic image from radioactive tracer
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.tɪˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.tɪˌɡræm / (BrE)
an image produced by detecting and recording the distribution of a radioactive tracer in the body, used especially in nuclear medicine diagnostic tests

Examples

A scintigram image
A thyroid scintigram can help diagnose hyperthyroidism or thyroid cancer.
The doctor reviewed the scintigram to locate areas of abnormal bone activity.
The patient was scheduled for a cardiac scintigram to assess blood flow to the heart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scan - a medical examination that produces images of inside the body
