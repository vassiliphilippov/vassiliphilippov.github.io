milfoil

noun
herbaceous plant with divided leaves and small flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪl.fɔɪl /; / ˈmɪlˌfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.fɔɪl /; / ˈmɪlˌfɔɪl / (BrE)
a plant with finely divided, feathery leaves, often found in meadows or water (such as yarrow or water milfoil)

Examples

Milfoil plant leaves
After the rain, the smell of milfoil was especially strong along the hedgerow.
Gardeners often plant milfoil for its hardy nature and attractive, feathery foliage.
The meadow was dotted with patches of milfoil swaying gently in the breeze.
The pond was full of milfoil growing near the surface.
She identified the delicate leaves of milfoil along the edge of the meadow.
Fishermen often remove milfoil from their nets after a day on the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterweed - an aquatic plant commonly found in ponds and streams
  • synonym: yarrow - another name for Achillea millefolium, a herbaceous plant with finely divided leaves
