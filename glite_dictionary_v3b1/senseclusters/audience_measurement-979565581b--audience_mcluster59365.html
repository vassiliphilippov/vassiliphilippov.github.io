audience measurement

noun
estimating the size of an audience
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdiəns ˈmɛʒərmənt / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəns ˈmɛʒəmənt / (BrE)
the process of determining the number and characteristics of people who are part of an audience, especially for television, radio, or online media

Examples

Audience measurement process
Advancements in technology have made audience measurement more accurate for streaming platforms.
Television networks rely on audience measurement to determine which shows are most popular with viewers.
The company specializes in audience measurement and provides data to advertisers.
