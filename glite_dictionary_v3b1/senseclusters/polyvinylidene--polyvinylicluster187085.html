polyvinylidene

noun
synthetic polymer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˌvaɪˈnɪlɪˌdin / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˌvɪˈnɪlɪˌdiːn / (BrE)
a synthetic polymer used in making plastics and films, especially for packaging and protective coatings

Examples

Polyvinylidene plastic
Many food containers are made from polyvinylidene because it provides an effective barrier to moisture.
The company developed a new film using polyvinylidene for improved durability and flexibility.
Researchers are studying the recycling process of polyvinylidene to reduce plastic waste.
