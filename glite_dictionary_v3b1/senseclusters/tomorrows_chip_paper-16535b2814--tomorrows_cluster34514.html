tomorrow's chip paper

noun
news soon forgotten; insignificant
Pronunciation: / təˈmɑːroʊz tʃɪp ˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / təˈmɒrəʊz tʃɪp ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
news or information that is unimportant and will quickly be forgotten

Examples

Tomorrow's chip paper news
He dismissed their complaints as tomorrow's chip paper, not worth worrying about.
Politicians know that negative headlines are just tomorrow's chip paper.
The rumor was everywhere yesterday, but it'll be tomorrow's chip paper soon enough.
