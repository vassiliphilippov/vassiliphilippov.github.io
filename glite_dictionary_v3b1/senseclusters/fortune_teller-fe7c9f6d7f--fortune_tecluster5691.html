fortune teller

noun
person claiming to predict the future
2.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.tʃən ˈtel.ər/ /; / /ˈfɔːr.tʃuːn ˈtel.ər/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.tʃuːn ˈtel.ə/ /; / /ˈfɔː.tʃən ˈtel.ə/ (BrE)
a person who claims to predict another person's future, typically using cards, palm reading, or other mystical means

Examples

A fortune teller reads cards
At the fair, a fortune teller predicted that I would meet someone special within the year.
Skeptics questioned the methods used by the fortune teller but others eagerly awaited their predictions.
The fortune teller studied my palm and said I would soon travel to a distant country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prophet - a person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of the future
  • antonym: skeptic - a person inclined to doubt or question claims, especially of the supernatural
  • synonym: soothsayer - someone who predicts the future
