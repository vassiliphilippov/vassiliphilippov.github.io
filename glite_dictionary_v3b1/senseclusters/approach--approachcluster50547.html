approach

noun
way or method of dealing with something
2.6
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a way of dealing with a problem or carrying out a task; a method or strategy

Examples

A new approach
Her approach to teaching makes the lessons both engaging and effective.
They took a cautious approach to expanding the business.
We need a new approach if we want to solve this issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoidance - the act of keeping away from a problem instead of dealing with it
  • synonym: method - a way of doing something, especially in a systematic way
  • synonym: strategy - a plan of action intended to achieve a specific goal
  • synonym: technique - a special way of doing something
