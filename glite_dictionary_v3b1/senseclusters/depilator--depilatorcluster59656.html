depilator

noun
hair removal product/device
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛpəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛpɪleɪtə / (BrE)
A substance or device used for the temporary removal of unwanted body hair.

Examples

A depilator cream
Before waxing became popular, people often used a chemical depilator.
She applied a depilator to her legs before going to the beach.
This depilator is gentle on sensitive skin and easy to use at home.
