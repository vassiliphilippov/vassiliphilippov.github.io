conditioned

adjective
physically fit due to training
1.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɪʃ.ənd / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃ.ənd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) physically fit as a result of regular exercise or training.

Examples

A conditioned athlete
After months of training, the athlete was fully conditioned for the marathon.
Her conditioned body allowed her to recover quickly from injuries.
The soldiers must remain conditioned to meet the demands of their missions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fit - in good physical shape; healthy and strong
