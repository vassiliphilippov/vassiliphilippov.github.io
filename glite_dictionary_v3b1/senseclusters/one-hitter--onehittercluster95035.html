one-hitter

noun
baseball game allowing only one hit
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌnˌhɪtər / (AmE), / ˈwʌnˌhɪtə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a game in which the pitcher allows the opposing team only one hit.

Examples

A one-hitter game
Fans cheered as he completed his first career one-hitter against a top-ranked team.
It takes great skill and concentration to achieve a one-hitter in professional baseball.
The pitcher threw a one-hitter last night, nearly achieving a no-hitter.
