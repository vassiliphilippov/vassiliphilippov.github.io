lariat

noun
noosed rope for catching animals
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlæriət / (AmE), / ˈlæriət / (BrE)
a long rope with a noose at one end, used especially for catching cattle or horses

Examples

A lariat rope
A lariat can be a vital tool for any rancher working with cattle.
She practiced twirling the lariat during the rodeo competition.
The cowboy expertly threw his lariat over the steer to catch it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lasso - a rope with a loop used for catching animals, especially cattle and horses
