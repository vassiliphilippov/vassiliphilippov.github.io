galvanized

adjective
suddenly motivated
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɡælvənaɪzd / (BrE)
suddenly excited or motivated to take action, often as a result of a strong influence or event

Examples

Galvanized by a speech
The team was galvanized by their coach's passionate speech before the final match.
After hearing the news, the citizens became galvanized and started organizing relief efforts.
Her words galvanized the audience, inspiring many to volunteer for the cause.

  • synonym: inspired - filled with the urge or ability to do something
  • synonym: motivated - having a reason or enthusiasm to act
