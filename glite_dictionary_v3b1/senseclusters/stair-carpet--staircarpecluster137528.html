stair-carpet

noun
carpet designed for stairs
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr ˌkɑːɹ.pət / (AmE), / ˈsteə ˌkɑː.pɪt / (BrE)
A strip of carpet specifically made for covering stairs, often fitted on each step for safety and decoration.

Examples

A stair-carpet strip
She chose a dark-colored stair-carpet to help hide scuff marks from shoes.
The stair-carpet needed cleaning after the party, as it had collected a lot of dirt.
We replaced the old stair-carpet with a new, patterned runner last weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: runner - a long, narrow strip of carpet for a hallway or stairs
