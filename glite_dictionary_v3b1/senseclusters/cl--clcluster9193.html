cl

noun
centiliter; metric volume unit
Pronunciation: / siː ɛl / (AmE), / siː ɛl / (BrE)
a metric unit of volume equal to one hundredth of a liter

Examples

cl of liquid
A standard drink often contains about 2 cl of alcohol.
On the packaging, the contents are listed as 33 cl.
The recipe calls for 10 cl of orange juice.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: dl - deciliter; metric unit equal to one tenth of a liter
  • degree lower: ml - milliliter; metric unit equal to one thousandth of a liter
