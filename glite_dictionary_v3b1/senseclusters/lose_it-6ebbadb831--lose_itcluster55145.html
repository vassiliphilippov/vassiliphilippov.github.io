lose it

verb
lose emotional control
1.0 6.0
Pronunciation: / luːz ɪt / (AmE), / luːz ɪt / (BrE)
to suddenly lose control of your emotions, especially by becoming very angry or upset

Examples

To lose it suddenly
He was trying to stay calm, but eventually he lost it and started yelling at everyone.

She completely lost it when she saw the state of the kitchen.

She tried to stay calm but eventually lost it during the meeting.
Sometimes, when the stress gets too much, he just loses it.
