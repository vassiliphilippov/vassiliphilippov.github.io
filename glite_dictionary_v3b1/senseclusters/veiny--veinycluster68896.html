veiny

adjective
marked with veins
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪni / (AmE), / ˈveɪni / (BrE)
having thin lines, streaks, or vein-like markings, especially of a different color or material.

Examples

Veiny marble
The artist chose a veiny piece of marble for the sculpture to add visual interest.
She admired the veiny patterns running through the wooden tabletop.
The stone tiles were especially veiny, giving the floor a unique appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - without any markings or patterns
  • synonym: streaked - marked with lines or bands of color
