mestranol

noun
synthetic estrogen in oral contraceptives
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmestrəˌnɔl / (AmE), / ˈmestrənɒl / (BrE)
a synthetic estrogen used in combination with a progestin as an ingredient in oral contraceptives

Examples

mestranol in pills

Some early birth control pills contained mestranol as their primary estrogen component.
The dosage of mestranol in oral contraceptives is carefully regulated.
