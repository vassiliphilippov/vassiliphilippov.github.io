avoidance

noun
the act of avoiding something
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈvɔɪdəns / (AmE), / əˈvɔɪdəns / (BrE)
the action of keeping away from something or preventing something from happening

Examples

Avoidance of problems
His constant avoidance of the issue frustrated his colleagues.
She practiced avoidance by walking a different route to work.
The avoidance of sugary drinks can benefit your health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avoid - keep away from or stop oneself from doing something
  • antonym: confrontation - a hostile or argumentative situation or meeting between opposing parties
