misframing

noun
incorrect presentation
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of presenting information or an issue in a misleading or incorrect way

Examples

Misframing of facts
The misframing of the statistics caused many readers to misunderstand the report.
Political debates are often influenced by the misframing of key issues.
Careful analysis revealed that the documentary suffered from significant misframing of the facts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarification - the act of making something clearer or easier to understand
  • synonym: distortion - the act of twisting or altering information from its true meaning
  • synonym: misrepresentation - the act of giving a false or misleading account
