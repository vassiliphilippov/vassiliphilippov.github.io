iron-handed approach

noun
strict or harsh method
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪrn ˈhændɪd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌaɪən ˈhændɪd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a strict or harsh method that shows little flexibility or tolerance, often associated with authoritarian leadership or control

Examples

An iron-handed approach
Critics argue that an iron-handed approach may damage morale among employees.
Despite public protest, the leader remained committed to his iron-handed approach to governance.
The new mayor's iron-handed approach to crime led to a significant drop in illegal activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iron-fisted - ruling or controlling with firmness and a lack of mercy
  • antonym: lenient - showing tolerance or mercy; not strict
