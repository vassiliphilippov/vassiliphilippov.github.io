Gonorhynchus

noun
genus of slender marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊnəˈrɪŋkəs /; / ˌɡoʊ.nɔːˈrɪŋ.kəs / (AmE), / ˌɡɒnəˈrɪŋkəs /; / ˌɡɒ.nəˈrɪŋ.kəs / (BrE)
