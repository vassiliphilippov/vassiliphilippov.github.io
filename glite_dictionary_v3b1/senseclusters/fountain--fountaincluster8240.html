fountain

noun
water feature or device with flowing water
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊntn̩ /; / ˈfaʊn.tən / (AmE), / ˈfaʊntən /; / ˈfaʊn.tɪn /; / ˈfaʊntɪn / (BrE)
a structure or device that sends water into the air, often for decoration or drinking
