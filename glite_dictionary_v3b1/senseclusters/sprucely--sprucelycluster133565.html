sprucely

adverb
in a neat or fashionable way
8.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàspruÀêsli / (AmE), / ÀàspruÀêsli / (BrE)
in a neat, tidy, and fashionable manner

Examples

To dress sprucely
He always arrives sprucely dressed for the meeting.
She decorated her office sprucely, giving it a modern and organized feel.
The guests were sprucely attired for the formal dinner.

Synonyms

  • trimly

  • stylishly
    3.5 0.6/m

  • modishly
