trap door

noun
door in floor or ceiling
4.5
Pronunciation: / træp dɔr / (AmE), / træp dɔː / (BrE)
a hinged or sliding door set into a floor or ceiling, used to allow or prevent access through it

Examples

A trap door in floor
A sudden creak beneath her feet revealed a trap door in the old library.
The magician vanished through a hidden trap door on stage.
They discovered a trap door in the attic that led to a secret room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatch - an opening in the floor, roof, or wall, often with a cover
