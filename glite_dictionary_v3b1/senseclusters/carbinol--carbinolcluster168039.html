carbinol

noun
methanol; simple alcohol
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrbɪnɒl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbɪnɒl / (BrE)
a chemical compound also known as methanol, the simplest alcohol with the formula CH3OH

Examples

Carbinol solvent
The laboratory used carbinol as a solvent in the experiment.
Exposure to carbinol vapors can be hazardous without proper ventilation.
Chemists often refer to methanol as carbinol in older scientific literature.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ethanol - a common alcohol found in alcoholic drinks
  • synonym: methanol - the simplest alcohol, also called wood alcohol
