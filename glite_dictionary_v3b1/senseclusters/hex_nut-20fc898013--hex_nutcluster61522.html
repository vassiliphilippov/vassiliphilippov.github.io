hex nut

noun
six-sided nut for bolts
6.1
Pronunciation: / hɛks nʌt / (AmE), / hɛks nʌt / (BrE)
a nut with six flat sides, usually used on a bolt or threaded rod

Examples

A hex nut on bolt
The hex nut became loose after months of heavy vibration.
Tighten the hex nut with a wrench to secure the bolt.
You need a standard hex nut to fit that size of screw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nut - a small metal block, usually hexagonal, with a hole through the center for screwing onto a bolt
