dib

noun
informal claim
9.1
Pronunciation: / dɪb / (AmE), / dɪb / (BrE)
an informal claim or right to something, often used to reserve it before others

Examples

Call dib on something
He shouted dib to make sure he got the front seat in the car.
If you want the last slice of pizza, you should call dib quickly.
She always tries to get dib on the best spot at the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dibs - an informal claim or right to something
  • synonym: right - a legal or moral entitlement to have or do something
