carnival

noun
festival with processions and celebrations
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrnɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːnɪvəl / (BrE)
A public festival typically involving street processions, music, dancing, and general merrymaking, often held before Lent in many cultures.

Examples

Street carnival parade
After the parade, everyone joined the joyous carnival celebrations in the main square.
Colorful floats and costumes are a highlight of the city's famous carnival.
The annual carnival attracts thousands of visitors from across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: festival - a day or period of celebration
