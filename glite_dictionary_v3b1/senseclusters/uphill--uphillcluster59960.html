uphill

adverb
upward on a hill or slope
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌhɪl / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌhɪl / (BrE)
in an upward direction on a hill or slope

Examples

Walk uphill fast
Cyclists find the route especially challenging when heading uphill.
The road winds uphill through dense forest before reaching the summit.
We had to walk uphill for nearly an hour to reach the waterfall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downhill - in a downward direction on a hill or slope
