peashooter

noun
weak or ineffective thing
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is weak, ineffective, or insignificant, especially when compared to something stronger or more powerful

Examples

A peashooter solution
The new software was a peashooter compared to the advanced programs used by competitors.
Calling him a peashooter in the debate was an obvious insult to his influence.
Their arguments were mere peashooters against the overwhelming evidence presented.
