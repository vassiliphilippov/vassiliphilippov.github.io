Phyllocladaceae

noun
family of evergreen coniferous trees and shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪloʊˌklædəˈsiː.i /; / ˌfɪləˌklædəˈsiː.i / (AmE), / ˌfɪləkˌlædəˈsiː.i / (BrE)
a family of evergreen trees and shrubs, mainly found in the southern hemisphere, with flattened, leaf-like branches
