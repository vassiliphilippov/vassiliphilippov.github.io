firebrat

noun
a small, silvery insect found in warm, humid places
6.1 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.ər.bræt/ /; / /ˈfaɪr.bræt/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə.bræt/ (BrE)
A small, wingless insect (Thermobia domestica) with a silvery appearance, living in warm, humid places such as near furnaces or water heaters.
