spinning wheel

noun
graphical loading indicator
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈspɪn.ɪŋ wiːl/ /; / /ˈspɪn.ɪŋ hwiːl/ /; / /ˈspɪn.ɪŋ wɪl/ (AmE), /ˈspɪn.ɪŋ wiːl/ /; / /ˈspɪn.ɪŋ hwiːl/ (BrE)
(in computing and user interfaces) a circular graphical indicator that spins to show that a process is in progress or the system is busy.
