spinning wheel

noun
graphical loading indicator
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɪŋ wiːl / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ wiːl / (BrE)
(in computing and user interfaces) a circular graphical indicator that spins to show that a process is in progress or the system is busy.

Examples

Loading spinning wheel
The app showed a spinning wheel while it synchronized dozens of large files.
A small spinning wheel appeared on the screen, signaling the system was temporarily busy.
After clicking save, he waited as the spinning wheel completed and the document reopened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busy indicator - an element that signals the user that an application or device is occupied
  • synonym: loading indicator - a UI element that shows progress or that the system is busy
  • synonym: loading spinner - a small animated graphic used in interfaces to indicate loading or processing
