predark

noun
time before darkness
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌdɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌdɑːk / (BrE)
the period of time just before it becomes dark in the evening; dusk or twilight

Examples

At predark
We tried to finish our hike before predark so we would not get lost.
The children played outside until predark made it hard to see the ball.
Birds often become more active during predark as the sun sets.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: daylight - the period when it is light outside during the day
  • synonym: dusk - the time just after sunset before it is completely dark
  • degree higher: night - the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise
  • synonym: twilight - the soft light in the sky when the sun is below the horizon
