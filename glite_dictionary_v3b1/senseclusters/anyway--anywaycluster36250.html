Synonyms

  • anyhow
    2.1 2.72/m
    anyhow sounds more informal and often closes a remark, while anyway introduces or returns to a point after an interruption.
  • in any case
    2.8 5.01/m
    in any case is neutral and states that something still holds, while anyway often links or supports a previous remark in speech.
  • in any event
    3.9 3.85/m
    in any event sounds slightly formal and stresses the outcome, while anyway is more general and often used in casual speech.
  • at any rate
    3.7 2.24/m
    at any rate is used to return or limit a point in conversation, while anyway adds or supports a comment after a pause.
