brutalist

noun
Brutalist-style building
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈbruːtəlɪst / (BrE)
a building designed in the Brutalist architectural style, often featuring raw concrete and bold, block-like shapes

Examples

A brutalist building
The new library is a striking brutalist that stands out among the older brick buildings.
Many people find the appearance of a brutalist harsh, but others appreciate its bold design.
A famous brutalist was recently renovated to serve as a contemporary art museum.
