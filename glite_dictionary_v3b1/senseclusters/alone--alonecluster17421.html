Synonyms

  • solo
    2.9 1.99/m
    solo describes doing an activity without others, often public or planned, while alone simply means being by oneself.
  • unaccompanied
    4.6 0.50/m
    unaccompanied means having no companion and is often used in formal contexts like travel documents, while alone is more general.
  • solitary
    4.6 11/m
    solitary describes something or someone living or placed apart, often with a sense of isolation, while alone is neutral.
  • solitarily
    6.8 0.03/m
    solitarily means acting in solitude and sounds formal or literary, while alone is the common everyday word people use.
