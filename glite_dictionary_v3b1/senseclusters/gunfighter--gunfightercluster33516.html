gunfighter

noun
skilled shooter in Old West
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person skilled in the use of guns, especially one who fought duels in the American Old West

Examples

He dressed as a famous gunfighter for the historical reenactment.
