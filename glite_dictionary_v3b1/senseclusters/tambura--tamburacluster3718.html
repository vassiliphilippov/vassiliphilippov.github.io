tambura

noun
South Asian string instrument
9.6
Pronunciation: / tɑmˈbʊrə / (AmE), / tæmˈbʊərə / (BrE)
a long-necked stringed instrument used in South Asian music to provide a continuous drone

Examples

Indian tambura music
The musician played a gentle melody on the tambura during the concert.
A tambura is often used to create a rich background drone in Indian classical performances.
She learned to tune the tambura before her first music lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sitar - a plucked stringed instrument used in Indian classical music
  • synonym: tanpura - an alternative spelling for tambura, a drone instrument in Indian music
