pillaring

noun
supporting with pillars
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪlərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of leaving or creating pillars of material to support a structure, especially in mining

Examples

Pillaring in mining
The safety of the mine depended on careful pillaring throughout the excavation process.
Modern mining techniques often combine pillaring with other support systems for added security.
Improper pillaring can increase the risk of a tunnel collapse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collapse - the sudden failure or falling in of a structure
  • synonym: roof bolting - a method of supporting mine roofs using long bolts
