undertone

noun
a pale or subdued color beneath the main color
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʌn.dɚˌtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈʌn.də.təʊn / (BrE)
a pale or subdued color that appears beneath the main color, especially one that subtly influences the overall tone

Examples

Skin undertone
Her skin has warm undertones, so gold jewelry suits her best.
The artist added a soft pink undertone to give the portrait more depth.
The blue paint has a green undertone that becomes visible in sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overtone - a color, shade, or quality that is more prominent or apparent than an undertone
