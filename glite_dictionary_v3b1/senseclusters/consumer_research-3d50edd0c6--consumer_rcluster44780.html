consumer research

noun
study of consumer motives and behavior
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmɚ rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmə rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the systematic study of the motives, preferences, and behaviors of consumers in order to inform business decisions, especially in marketing

Examples

Consumer research study
Many companies use consumer research to identify new market opportunities.
She works for an agency that specializes in consumer research for global brands.
The results of the consumer research showed that customers preferred environmentally friendly packaging.
