chipping

noun
the act of chipping
5.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪp.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪp.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of breaking off small pieces from something, or the small pieces that are broken off

Examples

Paint chipping
Repeated chipping of the paint weakened the surface of the old door.
The sculptor spent hours on careful chipping to reveal the figure hidden in the stone.
There was a lot of chipping during the renovation, leaving debris everywhere.
The playground was covered with chippings to make it safer for children.
Workers spread stone chippings on the new driveway to create a solid surface.
After the tree was cut down, the ground was scattered with wood chippings.
