dischargeable

adjective
able to be released
5.1
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be emitted, released, or expelled, especially referring to a liquid, gas, or substance.

Examples

Dischargeable waste liquid
The waste from the factory is not dischargeable into the local water supply under current regulations.
Only certain types of treated water are dischargeable into the environment.
The company must prove that the gas is safely dischargeable before receiving a permit.

  • synonym: releasable - able to be released or set free
