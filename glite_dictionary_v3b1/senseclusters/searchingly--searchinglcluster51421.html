searchingly

adverb
in a scrutinizing or probing manner
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːtʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsɜːtʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that involves close, careful, or penetrating examination or scrutiny.

Examples

Look searchingly at
He was silent for a moment, gazing searchingly out the window as he considered his answer.
She looked at him searchingly, trying to discern the truth in his words.
The detective questioned the suspect searchingly to uncover inconsistencies in his story.
