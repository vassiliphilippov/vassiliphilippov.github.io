Examples

Rapid ascent
Her rapid ascent within the company surprised her colleagues.
The ascent of the hot-air balloon drew cheers from the crowd.
The group celebrated their successful ascent to the mountain summit.
Her successful ascent of the challenging cliff inspired the rest of the group.
The mountain climbers began their ascent early in the morning to reach the summit by noon.

Synonyms

  • ascension
    7.5 1.13/m
    ascension is a formal or ceremonial rising often with religious or grand tone, while ascent is a neutral act of moving upward.
  • climb
    1.6 7.62/m
    climb is a physical or effortful upward movement often done by people, while ascent is more neutral and can be figurative.
  • rising
    3.4 3.89/m
    rising is a slow or continuing upward process often used for trends or levels, while ascent focuses on a single upward act.
  • elevation
    4.1 1.42/m
    elevation is the formal name for height or being raised, often as a result, while ascent highlights the act of going up.
  • rise
    2.4 11/m
    rise is a general noun for an increase, slope, or getting higher in many contexts, while ascent names the upward act.
