diacetyl

noun
buttery flavor compound
8.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ə.ˈse.təl/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.æs.ɪ.təl/ (AmE), /daɪ.ə.ˈse.təl/ /; / /ˈdaɪ.æs.ɪ.təl/ (BrE)
a chemical compound with a buttery flavor, used as a food flavoring and in industry; it can be harmful if inhaled

Examples
