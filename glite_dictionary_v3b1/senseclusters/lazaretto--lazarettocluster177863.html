lazaretto

noun
locker at the stern of a ship
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌlæzəˈrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌlæzəˈretəʊ / (BrE)
A small storage locker at the stern of a boat or between the decks of a ship, used for holding equipment or supplies.

Examples

Boat lazaretto
Before we docked, the captain checked the lazaretto to ensure all safety gear was accounted for.
Fishing rods and spare ropes were neatly arranged in the lazaretto.
The emergency supplies were safely stored in the lazaretto at the back of the yacht.
