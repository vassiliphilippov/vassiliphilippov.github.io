Symphyla

noun
minute soil-dwelling arthropods (class Symphyla)
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈfaɪlə / (AmE), / sɪmˈfɪlə / (BrE)
a small class of tiny, soil-dwelling arthropods that look like centipedes, sometimes called garden centipedes
