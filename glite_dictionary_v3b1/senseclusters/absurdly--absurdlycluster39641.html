absurdly

adverb
in an unreasonable or excessive way
5.0 2.00/m
Pronunciation: / …ôbÀàs…ùdli / (AmE), / …ôbÀàs…úÀêdli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely unreasonable, illogical, or to an excessive degree.

Examples

To absurdly laugh
He laughed absurdly at his own terrible joke, making everyone uncomfortable.
It was absurdly cold for May, and we had to wear winter coats.
The dress was absurdly expensive for such a simple design.
