nonclerical

adjective
not of the clergy
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈklɛrɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈklɛrɪkəl / (BrE)
not relating to the clergy or religious officials

Examples

Nonclerical staff
He was hired for a nonclerical role in the church's administrative office.
The organization employs both clerical and nonclerical staff to manage its activities.
Her duties were strictly nonclerical, focusing on financial management rather than religious services.
