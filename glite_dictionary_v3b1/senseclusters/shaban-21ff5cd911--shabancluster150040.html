Shaban

proper noun
Islamic calendar month
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈbɑːn / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈbɑːn / (BrE)
the eighth month of the Islamic lunar calendar, occurring before Ramadan

Examples

Month of Shaban
Many Muslims prepare for Ramadan during the month of Shaban.
The eighth month, Shaban, is considered a time for extra prayers and fasting.
Important religious events are sometimes observed in Shaban before the arrival of Ramadan.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Rajab - the seventh month of the Islamic calendar, preceding Shaban
  • degree higher: Ramadan - the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, observed by fasting
