resignee

noun
recipient of rights
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzɪˈniː / (AmE), / ˌrɛzɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person to whom an office, property, or rights have been transferred or surrendered, especially in legal or historical contexts

Examples

Estate resignee
The resignee accepted the title and responsibilities after the formal transfer of the estate.
In old legal documents, the resignee is often named as the new holder of the property.
After the contract was signed, the resignee became the official owner of the business rights.
