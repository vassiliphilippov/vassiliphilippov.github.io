incontinence

noun
inability to control urination or defecation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑːn.tən.əns / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒn.tɪ.nəns / (BrE)
The inability to control the elimination of urine or feces from the body.

Examples

Incontinence products
Products designed for incontinence can help people maintain their independence.
Some medications can increase the risk of incontinence in elderly patients.
The patient experienced incontinence after the surgery, requiring additional care.
