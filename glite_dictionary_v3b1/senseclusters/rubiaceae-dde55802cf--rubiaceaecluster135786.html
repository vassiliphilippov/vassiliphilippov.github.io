Rubiaceae

noun
family of tropical plants (includes coffee, gardenia)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ruːˈbaɪ.əˌsiː.iː /; / ˌruːbiˈeɪsiːˌiː / (AmE), / ruːˌbɪˈeɪ.siː.iː /; / ˌruːbɪˈeɪsɪiː / (BrE)
a large family of mainly tropical trees, shrubs, and herbs, including important plants such as coffee and gardenia
