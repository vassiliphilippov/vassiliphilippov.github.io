baize

noun
coarse green fabric for gaming tables
Pronunciation: / beɪz / (AmE), / beɪz / (BrE)
a coarse, bright green fabric, typically napped to resemble felt, and commonly used to cover gaming tables

Examples

Green baize fabric
A fresh layer of green baize made the billiard room look elegant.
He replaced the worn baize on his old card table.
The pool table was covered in baize to give it a smooth playing surface.
