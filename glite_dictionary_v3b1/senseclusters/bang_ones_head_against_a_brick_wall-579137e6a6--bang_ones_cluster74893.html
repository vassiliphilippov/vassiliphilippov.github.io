bang one's head against a brick wall

verb

3.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /bæŋ wʌnz hed ə.ˈgenst ə brɪk wɔːl/ /; / /bæŋ wʌnz hed ə.ˈgeɪnst ə brɪk wɔːl/ (AmE), /bæŋ wʌnz hed ə.ˈgeɪnst ə brɪk wɔːl/ /; / /bæŋ wʌnz hed ə.ˈgenst ə brɪk wɔːl/ (BrE)

Examples
