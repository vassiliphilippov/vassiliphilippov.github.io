bazaar

noun
charitable sale event
3.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈzɑr / (AmE), / bəˈzɑː / (BrE)
An event where miscellaneous items are sold, often to raise money for charity.

Examples

Charity bazaar
Many volunteers donated homemade goods for the charity bazaar.
She found unique crafts at the annual church bazaar.
The local school hosted a bazaar to raise funds for new equipment.
