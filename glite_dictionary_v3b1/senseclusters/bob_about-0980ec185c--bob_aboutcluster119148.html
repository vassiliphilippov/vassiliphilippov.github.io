bob about

verb
move repeatedly with no fixed path or direction
6.7
Pronunciation: / bɑb əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / bɒb əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to move up and down or around repeatedly, usually in a light or floating manner, without a fixed direction

Examples

To bob about on water
After the storm, debris could be seen bobbing about in the harbor.
Small boats bob about on the surface of the lake during windy days.
The children loved to watch the rubber ducks bob about in the bath.
