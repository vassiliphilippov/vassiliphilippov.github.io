ostrich

noun
largest African flightless bird
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstrɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒstrɪtʃ / (BrE)
a large, fast-running African flightless bird with two-toed feet, known as the largest living bird

Examples

Wild ostrich running
At the wildlife reserve, we watched a group of ostriches grazing in the field.
The ostrich is famous for its powerful legs and high running speed.
Unlike most birds, the ostrich cannot fly but can outrun many predators on land.
