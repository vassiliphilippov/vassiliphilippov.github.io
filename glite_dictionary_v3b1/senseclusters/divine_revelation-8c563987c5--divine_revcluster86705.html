divine revelation

noun
knowledge revealed by a deity
Pronunciation: / dəˈvaɪn ˌrɛvəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn ˌrɛvəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the communication of knowledge or truth to humans by a deity or supernatural power

Examples

Divine revelation in texts
Some theologians debate the interpretation of divine revelation in sacred texts.

Throughout history, many religions have been founded on principles of divine revelation.
