Beretta

noun
firearm brand
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɛtə / (AmE), / bəˈrɛtə / (BrE)
a brand of handgun or firearm, especially a semi-automatic pistol made by the Italian company Beretta

Examples

Beretta pistol
The officer drew his Beretta as he entered the building.
Many police departments issue a Beretta as their standard sidearm.
He cleaned his Beretta carefully after returning from the shooting range.
