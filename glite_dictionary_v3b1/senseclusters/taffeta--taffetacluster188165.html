taffeta

noun
crisp, smooth, lustrous fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈtæfətə / (AmE), / ˈtafɪtə / (BrE)
a crisp, smooth, and lustrous fabric, often used for evening dresses, linings, and formal wear

Examples

A taffeta dress
Her wedding gown was made of shimmering taffeta and delicate lace.
She wore a pink taffeta dress to the formal dinner.
The curtains were sewn from bright blue taffeta to brighten up the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organza - a thin, plain weave, sheer fabric, often used in evening wear
  • synonym: satin - a smooth, glossy fabric, typically silk, with a lustrous surface
