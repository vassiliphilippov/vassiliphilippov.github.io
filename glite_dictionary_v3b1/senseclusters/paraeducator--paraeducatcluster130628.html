paraeducator

noun
educational assistant
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈɛdʒəˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈɛdjuːkeɪtə / (BrE)
a school employee who assists teachers by supporting students and helping with instructional or supervisory tasks, often in or outside the classroom

Examples

The paraeducator helps students
The paraeducator worked closely with students who needed extra help in reading and math.
As a paraeducator, she supported children with disabilities in a variety of learning environments.
The school hired a new paraeducator to assist with the after-school tutoring program.
The paraeducator supervised the lunchroom and took attendance every morning before class.
A paraeducator often helps monitor students during recess and assists with classroom organization.
Our school hired a new paraeducator to support students with special needs.
The paraeducator worked closely with the teacher to support students during group activities.
A paraeducator often provides extra help to children who need additional assistance in class.
Many schools rely on a paraeducator to help manage large classrooms and individual student needs.
