forbear

noun
ancestor or forefather
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌbɛr / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌbeə / (BrE)
a person in your family from past generations; an ancestor

Examples

A forbear from history
He discovered that one of his forbears fought in the Revolutionary War.
Many traditions have been passed down from our forbears over the centuries.
She was proud to learn about the achievements of her forbear in the 1800s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestor - a person from whom one is descended
  • antonym: descendant - a person who is descended from a particular ancestor
