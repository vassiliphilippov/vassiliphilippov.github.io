earthily

adverb
in a natural way
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθɪli / (AmE), / ˈɜːθɪli / (BrE)
in a direct, simple, or natural way, often connected to the earth or nature

Examples

To laugh earthily
She laughed earthily, her voice full of warmth and honesty.
The chef described his cooking style earthily, focusing on fresh, local ingredients.
He spoke earthily about his childhood in the countryside, making everyone feel at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naturally - in a way that relates to nature or is not artificial
  • synonym: simply - in a plain or uncomplicated way
