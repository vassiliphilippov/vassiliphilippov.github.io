reduction

noun
act of making something smaller
Pronunciation: / rəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something smaller in size, amount, degree, or price

Examples

Reduction in price
After switching to energy-efficient bulbs, she noticed a reduction in her electricity bill.
The company announced a reduction in staff to cut costs.
There has been a significant reduction in air pollution over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decrease - the process of becoming less
  • antonym: increase - the act or process of becoming larger or greater
