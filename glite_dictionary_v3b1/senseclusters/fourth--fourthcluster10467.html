fourth

noun
one of four equal parts; quarter
1.4
Pronunciation: / fɔrθ / (AmE), / fɔːθ / (BrE)
Each of four equal parts into which something can be divided; one quarter.

Examples

A fourth of cake
A fourth of the total votes were cast by mail this year.
After spending a fourth of his savings, he decided to slow down on shopping.
She divided the cake into four fourths and gave one to each child.
