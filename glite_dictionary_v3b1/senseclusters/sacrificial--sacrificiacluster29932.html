sacrificial

adjective
given up for others
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
offered or given up for the benefit of someone or something else, often involving loss or risk

Examples

Sacrificial act
The soldier made a sacrificial decision to protect his team during the mission.
Her sacrificial efforts ensured her family had enough food during hard times.
The animal was used in a sacrificial ritual in the ancient ceremony.

  • antonym: selfish - putting one's own needs before others
  • synonym: selfless - putting others' needs before one's own
