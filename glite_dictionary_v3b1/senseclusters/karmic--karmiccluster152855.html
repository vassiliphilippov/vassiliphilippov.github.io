karmic

adjective
related to karma
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrmɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to karma or the idea that actions (in this life or past lives) have consequences

Examples

Karmic justice
Many people believe that karmic justice will eventually catch up with those who do wrong.
She saw her sudden good luck as a karmic reward for years of kindness.
The story explores the karmic consequences of the characters' actions over time.
Some people believe that karmic forces determine what happens in their lives.
He felt his bad luck was a result of karmic consequences from past actions.
The story explores the idea of karmic justice and moral responsibility.
Many people believe that karmic events are the result of actions from previous lives.
He felt a karmic connection with the stranger he met on the train.
Some traditions teach that karmic debts must be repaid over many lifetimes.
