unhedged

adjective
without a hedge
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛdʒd / (BrE)
not surrounded or separated by a hedge; lacking a hedge as a boundary or protection

Examples

Unhedged garden
The unhedged garden was easily visible from the street.
Farmers worried that their unhedged fields would suffer from strong winds.
An unhedged property can feel more open but less private.

  • synonym: open - not enclosed or protected; exposed
