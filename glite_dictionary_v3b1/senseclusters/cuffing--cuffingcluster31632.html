cuffing

noun
slap with open hand
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌf.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkʌf.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of striking someone with an open hand, usually as a slap or light blow

Examples

Playful cuffing in class
The teacher warned the boys that cuffing each other was not acceptable in class.
He received a quick cuffing on the back of his head for not paying attention.
During the heated discussion, a sudden cuffing silenced the room.
