brush back

verb
move hair or something away from face
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ bæk / (AmE), / brʌʃ bak / (BrE)
to move something, especially hair, away from the face by brushing it with the hand or a brush

Examples

To brush back hair
As the wind picked up, he had to constantly brush back his bangs.
He quickly brushed back his hair before stepping out the door.
She brushed back a lock of hair that had fallen into her eyes.
