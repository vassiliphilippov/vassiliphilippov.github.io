family Danaidae

noun
group of tropical butterflies including monarchs
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌdænɪˈaɪdiˌi / (AmE), / ˈfamɪli ˌdanɪˈeɪdiː / (BrE)
A small family of usually tropical butterflies, including the monarch butterflies.

Examples

The family Danaidae butterflies
Many members of the family Danaidae are found in tropical regions around the world.
Scientists have studied the family Danaidae to understand butterfly migration patterns.
The family Danaidae is notable for its colorful and migratory species such as the monarch butterfly.
