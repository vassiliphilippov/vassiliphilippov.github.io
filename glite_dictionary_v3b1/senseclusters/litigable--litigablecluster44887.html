litigable

adjective
can be litigated
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtɪɡəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪtɪɡəbəl / (BrE)
able to be taken to court or subject to legal action

Examples

Litigable dispute
The dispute over the property was considered litigable by the legal team.
Only litigable claims will be reviewed by the court this session.
The lawyer explained which issues were litigable and which were not.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actionable - giving sufficient reason for legal action
  • antonym: nonlitigable - not able to be taken to court
