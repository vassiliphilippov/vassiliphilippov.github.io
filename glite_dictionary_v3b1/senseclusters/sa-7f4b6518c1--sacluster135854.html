SA

noun
Nazi paramilitary group (1921–1945)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈes.eɪ/ (AmE), /ˈes.eɪ/ (BrE)
A Nazi paramilitary organization (Sturmabteilung) founded by Hitler in 1921, instrumental in his rise to power but later overshadowed by the SS.

The SA played a crucial role in Hitler's ascent during the early years of the Nazi Party.
