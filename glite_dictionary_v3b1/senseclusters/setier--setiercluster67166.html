setier

noun
obsolete French measure
Pronunciation: / sɛˈtjɛr / (AmE), / sɛˈtjɛə / (BrE)
an old French unit of dry or liquid measure, now obsolete

Examples

A setier of grain
The merchant recorded the shipment as twenty setier of grain.
In old French recipes, quantities were sometimes given in setier rather than liters.
Historians found records listing wine measured by the setier in medieval trade documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bushel - a unit of dry measure used for grain
  • synonym: liter - a metric unit of volume
