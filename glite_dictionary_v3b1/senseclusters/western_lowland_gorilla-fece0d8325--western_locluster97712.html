western lowland gorilla

noun
smallest and most widespread gorilla subspecies
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstɚn ˈloʊˌlænd ɡəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈləʊˌlænd ɡəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
the smallest and most widespread subspecies of gorilla, native to the forests of western Central Africa

Examples

A western lowland gorilla family
At the zoo, we saw a family of western lowland gorillas playing in their enclosure.
Researchers are studying the diet and social behavior of the western lowland gorilla in its natural environment.
The western lowland gorilla is endangered due to poaching and loss of habitat.
