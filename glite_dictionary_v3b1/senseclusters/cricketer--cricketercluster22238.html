cricketer

noun
person who plays cricket
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪk.ɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪk.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who plays cricket, especially as a member of a team

Examples

A cricketer on team
Every aspiring cricketer dreams of scoring a century at Lord's.
She was the youngest cricketer to play for her national team.
The former cricketer now works as a television commentator.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: batsman - a cricket player who tries to score runs while batting
  • degree lower: bowler - a cricket player who delivers the ball to the batsman
  • synonym: player - a person who takes part in a sporting game
