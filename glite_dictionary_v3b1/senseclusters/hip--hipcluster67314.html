hip

noun
area below waist and above thigh
Pronunciation: / hɪp / (AmE), / hɪp / (BrE)
the part of the body below the waist and above the thigh, including the joint connecting the leg to the torso

Examples

Dancers often sway their hips to the rhythm of the music.
She placed her hand on her hip as she spoke.
After her fall, the doctor examined her hip for any possible fractures.
Athletes need flexible hips to perform certain movements efficiently.
He had surgery to replace his right hip with an artificial joint.
After the accident, she had to undergo hip replacement surgery.
He injured his hip while playing football and needed physical therapy.
The doctor examined her hip to check for signs of arthritis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knee - the joint between the thigh and the lower leg
  • synonym: pelvis - the large, bony structure at the base of the spine to which the limbs are attached
  • synonym: shoulder - the ball-and-socket joint connecting the arm and the torso
  • degree lower: thigh - the part of the leg between the hip and the knee
