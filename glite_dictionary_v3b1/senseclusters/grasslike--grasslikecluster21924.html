grasslike

adjective
resembling grass
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːslaɪk / (BrE)
resembling grass in appearance, texture, or characteristics.

Examples

Grasslike leaves
Some flowers grow on grasslike stems that bend easily in the wind.
The plant has grasslike leaves that are long and narrow.
This sculpture features a grasslike texture to mimic the look of a meadow.
