count out

verb
to declare the loser (boxing, by a count)
6.6 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / kaÊŠnt aÊŠt / (AmE), / kaÊŠnt aÊŠt / (BrE)
(in boxing) to declare a competitor the loser by counting to the prescribed number when they fail to rise after being knocked down.

Examples

To count out a boxer
He was counted out in the final round, losing the match by knockout.
If you can't get up before the referee finishes the ten count, you'll be counted out.
The referee began to count out the boxer after he was knocked to the canvas.

Synonyms
