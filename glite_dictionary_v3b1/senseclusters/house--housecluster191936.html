house

noun
theater or cinema building
5.9 4.09/m
Pronunciation: / haÊŠs / (AmE), / haÊŠs / (BrE)
A building or venue where theatrical performances or films are presented to an audience.

Examples

A theater house
She works as an usher in the local house downtown.
The house is sold out for tonight's premiere.
The audience filed into the house and took their seats.

Synonyms

  • theater
    1.0 27/m
    theater is the common word for a building that shows plays or films, while house often names the venue itself.
  • cinema
    1.0 4.45/m
    cinema is a building that shows films to the public, while house can mean a venue for plays or movies.
  • movie theater
    1.0 5.10/m
    movie theater is the American term for a place showing films, while house may also refer to a general performance venue.
  • movie house
    1.6 0.67/m
    movie house is an informal or slightly old-fashioned name for a cinema, while house is the wider venue word for performances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auditorium - the part of a theater, concert hall, or other public building where the audience sits
  • synonym: theater - a building where plays, shows, or other forms of entertainment are performed
