cagework

noun
framework of bars
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪdʒˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈkeɪdʒˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a framework or structure consisting of bars or wires arranged like a cage, often used for protection or support

Examples

Metal cagework frame
The lamp was protected by a metal cagework.
The old greenhouse had a delicate cagework supporting its glass panels.
Birds perched on the cagework that surrounded the garden fountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framework - a basic supporting structure
  • synonym: lattice - a structure of crossed strips or bars
