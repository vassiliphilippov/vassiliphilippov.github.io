caballero

noun
Spanish horseman
9.6
Pronunciation: / kɑːbəˈjeɪroʊ / (AmE), / kæbəˈjeərəʊ / (BrE)
a horseman or cavalryman, especially in historical or rural Spanish-speaking contexts

Examples

Caballero on horseback
The skilled caballero guided his horse through the mountain passes during the long journey.
At the festival, a caballero performed impressive riding tricks for the crowd.
In old western movies, the hero was often a brave caballero defending his village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cavalryman - a soldier who fights on horseback
  • synonym: horseman - a person who rides horses
