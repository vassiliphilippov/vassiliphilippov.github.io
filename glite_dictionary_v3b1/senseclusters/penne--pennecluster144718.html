penne

noun
short tube-shaped pasta
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛneɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɛneɪ / (BrE)
A type of pasta in the form of short tubes with diagonally cut ends, often served with sauce.

Examples

A penne pasta
I prefer penne because the sauce fills the tubes nicely.
She cooked a pot of penne for the family dinner.
The chef recommended the penne with tomato and basil sauce for tonight’s special.

Related Terms

  • synonym: macaroni - short, curved, tube-shaped pasta
  • synonym: rigatoni - large tube-shaped pasta with ridges
