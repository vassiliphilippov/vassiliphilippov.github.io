counterterror

adjective
meant to prevent terrorism
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈtɛrər / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈterə / (BrE)
(of methods, actions, or strategies) intended to prevent or combat terrorism.

Examples

Counterterror measures
International cooperation has strengthened several counterterror initiatives.
Many agencies work closely on counterterror intelligence sharing.
The government increased its counterterror efforts after the recent attacks.
