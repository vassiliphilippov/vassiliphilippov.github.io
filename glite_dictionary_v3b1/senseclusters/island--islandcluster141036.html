island

verb
to isolate completely
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
to separate or isolate something or someone as if on an island.

Examples

To island a village
The rising river islanded the village, making it unreachable by car.
After the landslide, the road was blocked and the community was islanded for days.
Heavy snow islanded several families in their homes until help arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connect - to join or link together
  • synonym: isolate - to separate from others or keep apart
