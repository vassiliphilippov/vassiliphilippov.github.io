means

noun
wealth; substantial financial resources
Pronunciation: / minz / (AmE), / miːnz / (BrE)
wealth or financial resources; the money or capital that someone possesses, especially in significant amount.

Examples

She has the means
After years of hard work, she finally accumulated the means to travel the world.
He comes from a family of considerable means and influence.
Philanthropists often use their means to support charitable causes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • synonym: wealth - abundance of valuable possessions or money
