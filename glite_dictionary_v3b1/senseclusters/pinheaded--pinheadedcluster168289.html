pinheaded

adjective
very foolish
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
very foolish or lacking in common sense

Examples

Pinheaded mistake
It was a pinheaded decision to leave the door unlocked.
Calling your boss by the wrong name was a truly pinheaded mistake.
He made a pinheaded comment that embarrassed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idiotic - very stupid or lacking intelligence
  • antonym: sensible - showing good judgment or reason
