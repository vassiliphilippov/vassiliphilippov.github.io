Dopastat

noun
trade name for dopamine used to treat shock
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊpəˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈdəʊpəˌstæt / (BrE)
A trade name for a drug preparation of dopamine, used especially to treat shock and low blood pressure.

Examples

Dopastat drug treatment
Dopastat should be given by a healthcare professional under close monitoring.
Doctors may prescribe Dopastat in cases of severe shock.
The hospital administered Dopastat to stabilize the patient's blood pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dopamine - a monoamine neurotransmitter crucial in the brain, and the active ingredient in Dopastat
