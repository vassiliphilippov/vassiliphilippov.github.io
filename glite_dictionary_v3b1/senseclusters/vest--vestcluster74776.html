vest

noun
men's collarless undershirt
Pronunciation: / vɛst / (AmE), / vɛst / (BrE)
A collarless undergarment for the upper part of the body, worn by men, typically next to the skin.

Examples

A cotton vest
He put on a clean vest before getting dressed for work.
In the UK, a vest is typically worn beneath a shirt for warmth.
She bought a pack of cotton vests for her son.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undershirt - an undergarment worn on the upper body under a shirt
  • antonym: waistcoat - a sleeveless upper-body garment, worn over a shirt and under a jacket
