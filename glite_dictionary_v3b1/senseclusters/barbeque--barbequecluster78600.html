barbeque

noun
outdoor rack or grill for cooking meat
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː / (BrE)
a rack or grill for cooking meat over hot charcoal, usually outdoors

Examples

A barbeque grill
He built his own brick barbeque next to the patio.
The old barbeque needed cleaning before the party started.
We cooked steaks on the barbeque in the backyard all afternoon.
