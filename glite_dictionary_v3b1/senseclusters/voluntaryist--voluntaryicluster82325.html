voluntaryist

adjective
favoring voluntary agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːlənˌtɛriɪst / (AmE), / ˈvɒlənˌtɛəriɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the belief that social and political relations should be based on voluntary agreement rather than government force

Examples

Voluntaryist ideas
The organization adopted a voluntaryist approach, relying on donations instead of government funding.
Her voluntaryist beliefs led her to oppose new laws that increased state power.
A voluntaryist society would depend on mutual agreements rather than enforced regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: libertarian - supporting individual freedom and minimal government intervention
  • antonym: statist - favoring government control over social or economic affairs
