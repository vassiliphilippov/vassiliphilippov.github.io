chart

noun
map for air or sea navigation
Pronunciation: / tʃɑɹt / (AmE), / tʃɑːt / (BrE)
A map, especially one designed to assist navigation by air or sea.

Examples

A nautical chart

Pilots rely on an aeronautical chart for accurate navigation.
The ship's captain consulted the chart to plot a safe course through the reefs.
