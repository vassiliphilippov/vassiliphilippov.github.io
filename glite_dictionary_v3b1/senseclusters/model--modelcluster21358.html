model

noun
type or design of a product
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a particular type or design of a product made by a company

Examples

The latest model car
She bought the latest model of her favorite running shoes last week.
That car model was discontinued several years ago due to low sales.
The new smartphone model offers improved battery life and a better camera.

Related Terms

  • synonym: make - the brand or manufacturer of a product, especially cars
  • synonym: version - a form or variant of something differing in certain respects
