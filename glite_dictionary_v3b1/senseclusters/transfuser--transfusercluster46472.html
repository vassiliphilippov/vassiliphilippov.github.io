transfuser

noun
fluid transfer device
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfjuzər / (AmE), / trænsˈfjuːzə / (BrE)
a device or person that transfers blood or other fluids from one body to another

Examples

The transfuser device
The transfuser was carefully sterilized before the operation began.
A skilled transfuser is essential during complex surgical procedures involving blood loss.
Modern hospitals use an automated transfuser to ensure safe blood transfer between patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donor - a person who gives blood or an organ to another
  • synonym: transfusion - the process of transferring blood or fluid into a person
