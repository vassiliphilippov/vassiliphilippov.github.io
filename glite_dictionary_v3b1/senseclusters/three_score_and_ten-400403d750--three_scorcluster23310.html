three score and ten

noun
seventy years (age, lifespan)
Pronunciation: / θri ˈskɔr ənd tɛn / (AmE), / θri ˈskɔːr ənd tɛn / (BrE)
seventy years; specifically used to refer to a lifespan of seventy years

Examples

A three score and ten lifespan
He often quoted the saying that man's years are three score and ten in his speeches.
Her grandmother lived to the age of three score and ten before passing peacefully.
Upon reaching three score and ten, she decided to travel the world.
