check

noun
pattern of squares or crossed lines (textile)
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
A pattern of squares or crossed lines, especially as used in textiles, resembling a checkerboard.

Examples

Check fabric
Her dress was made of a bold blue check fabric that caught everyone's eye.
Many school uniforms feature skirts with a traditional check pattern.
The tablecloth had a red-and-white check that gave the room a cozy feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plaid - a pattern of crossed horizontal and vertical bands, especially in multiple colors
  • antonym: stripe - a long, narrow band distinguished by color or texture
  • synonym: tartan - a woolen cloth with a distinctive pattern of checked or crisscrossed lines
