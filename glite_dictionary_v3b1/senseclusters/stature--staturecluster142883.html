stature

noun
level of respect or reputation
Pronunciation: / ˈstætʃər / (AmE), / ˈstatʃə / (BrE)
a high level of respect, importance, or reputation that someone has, often as a result of achievement or development

Examples

High stature
Despite his young age, his stature in the academic community is remarkable.
She achieved international stature as a scientist after her groundbreaking research.
The company has gained stature over the past decade through innovation and leadership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or unimportant
  • synonym: prestige - respect and admiration felt for someone based on their achievements or quality
  • synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions that are generally held about someone or something
