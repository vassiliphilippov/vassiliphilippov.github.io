subteen

noun
child under thirteen
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtiːn / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtiːn / (BrE)
a child who is younger than thirteen years old, especially one approaching the teenage years

Examples

A subteen child
The summer camp offers special activities for subteen children.
As a subteen, she was eager to join the older kids in their games.
Many subteens enjoy reading adventure books and playing sports after school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preteen - a child who is not yet a teenager, typically between 9 and 12 years old
  • antonym: teenager - a person aged between 13 and 19 years old
