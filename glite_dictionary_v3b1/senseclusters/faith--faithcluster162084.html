faith

noun
loyalty or allegiance; trust
Pronunciation: / feɪθ / (AmE), / feɪθ / (BrE)
loyalty or allegiance to a person or cause; trust in someone's ability or reliability.

Examples

Keep the faith
He felt she had broken his faith by revealing his secret.
Her faith in her friends never wavered, even during difficult times.
The team succeeded because of their strong faith in the coach's strategy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: betrayal - the act of being disloyal or breaking faith
  • synonym: loyalty - faithfulness or allegiance to a person, group, or cause
  • synonym: trust - belief in someone's reliability or honesty
