saccadic

adjective
relating to rapid eye movements
8.8
Pronunciation: / səˈkædɪk / (AmE), / səˈkadɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the rapid, jerky movements of the eyes as they shift from one point of fixation to another.

Examples

Rapid saccadic motion
Abnormal saccadic motion can indicate neurological conditions.
Reading efficiency depends in part on the smoothness of saccadic transitions between words.
The doctor measured the patient's saccadic eye movements during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saccade - a rapid movement of the eye between fixation points
