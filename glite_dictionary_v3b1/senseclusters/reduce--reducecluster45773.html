reduce

verb

(of a person) to lose weight; to become slimmer, especially by dieting.

Examples

To reduce weight
After months of exercise and dieting, she managed to reduce by ten pounds.
Doctors often advise patients to reduce for health reasons.
He decided to reduce before his college reunion.

Synonyms

    slim down is used for losing body weight in everyday speech, while reduce is the neutral verb for becoming slimmer.
  • slim
    2.4 0.53/m
    slim means to become thinner or make smaller, often about body weight, while reduce is more general and formal.
  • slenderize
    7.3 0.03/m

  • trim down
    3.4 0.22/m
