spin-out

verb
to lose vehicle control
6.1
Pronunciation: /spɪn aʊt/ (AmE), /spɪn aʊt/ (BrE)
to lose control of a vehicle so that it rotates or spins, often due to slippery conditions.

Examples

To spin-out on ice
The truck began to spin-out on the icy highway, but the driver managed to stop safely.
If you brake too hard on wet roads, your car might spin-out unexpectedly.
During the race, one of the drivers spun-out after hitting a patch of oil.
