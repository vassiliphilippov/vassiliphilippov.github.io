whipstitching

noun
diagonal over-edge sewing stitch
6.2 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpˌstɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪpˌstɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
A sewing stitch made by passing the needle in and out over the edge of fabric diagonally to bind or finish the edge.

Examples

Decorative whipstitching
Decorative whipstitching added a rustic touch to the leather wallet.
He repaired the torn seam with careful whipstitching along the edge.
The seamstress used a whipstitching technique to finish the raw edges of the fabric.
