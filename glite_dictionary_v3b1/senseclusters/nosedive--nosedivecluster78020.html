nosedive

noun
steep dive by an aircraft
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊz.daɪv / (AmE), / ˈnəʊz.daɪv / (BrE)
A steep, sudden downward movement with the front end of an aircraft pointing down.

Examples

Plane nosedive
A sudden nosedive caused panic among the passengers.
The pilot quickly corrected the nosedive and regained control.
The plane went into a nosedive after losing engine power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dive - a steep downward drop or movement
