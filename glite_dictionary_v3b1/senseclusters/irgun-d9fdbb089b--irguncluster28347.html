Irgun

proper noun
Jewish paramilitary group
Pronunciation: / ɪrˈɡun / (AmE), / ɪˈɡuːn / (BrE)
a Jewish paramilitary organization active in Palestine from 1931 to 1948, involved in the struggle for the establishment of Israel

Examples

The Irgun fighters
The Irgun carried out several operations during the final years of the British Mandate in Palestine.
Many historians debate the impact of the Irgun on the creation of the State of Israel.
Some members of the Irgun later became prominent figures in Israeli politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Haganah - a Jewish paramilitary organization in British Mandate Palestine
  • synonym: Lehi - a Jewish militant group in Palestine, also known as the Stern Gang
