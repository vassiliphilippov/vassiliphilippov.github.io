deaf

verb
to make unable to hear
1.5 5.9
Pronunciation: /dɛf/ (AmE), /dɛf/ (BrE)
(of a loud noise or other cause) to make someone unable to hear, either temporarily or permanently.

Examples

To deaf someone

Repeated exposure to loud music can eventually deaf a person.
The explosion was so loud it nearly deafed him for life.
