unrecorded

adjective
not documented
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈkɔrdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkɔːdɪd / (BrE)
not officially written down, observed, or documented

Examples

An unrecorded event
The archaeologists found unrecorded artifacts buried beneath the ancient city.
There are many unrecorded cases of the disease in remote villages.
His contribution to the project remained unrecorded in the official documents.
Many important events in history remain unrecorded because no one wrote them down.
The meeting was unrecorded, so there is no official transcript available.
Some traditional songs have been lost because they were unrecorded and passed down only orally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recorded - officially written down or documented
  • synonym: undocumented - not officially recorded or written down
