whooping

verb
to shout loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwuːpɪŋ / (BrE)
making a loud, enthusiastic cry or shout, often to show excitement or approval.

Examples

People whooping loudly
The crowd started whooping as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
Fans were whooping and waving flags after their team won the match.
She could hear her friends whooping with joy from across the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: booing - shouting to show disapproval
  • synonym: cheering - shouting in support or encouragement
