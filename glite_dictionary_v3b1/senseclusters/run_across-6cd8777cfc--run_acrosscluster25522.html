run across

verb
find or encounter by chance
Pronunciation: / rʌn əˈkrɔs / (AmE), / rʌn əˈkrɒs / (BrE)
to find or discover someone or something by chance, usually unexpectedly while doing something else.

Examples

To run across a letter
She ran across an email from her old professor while searching her inbox.
While browsing the library, I ran across a book I thought was out of print.
You might run across some interesting antiques at the flea market.
