Nintinugga

proper noun
Babylonian goddess of healing
Pronunciation: / nɪn.tɪˈnuːɡə / (AmE), / nɪn.tɪˈnʌɡə / (BrE)
the Babylonian goddess of healing and consort of Ninurta in Mesopotamian mythology

Examples

Nintinugga goddess healing
Ancient texts describe Nintinugga as a powerful healer among the Babylonian deities.
Artifacts dedicated to Nintinugga have been found in southern Mesopotamia.
Some myths portray Nintinugga working alongside her consort, Ninurta, to cure illnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ninurta - Sumerian god of war, agriculture, and healing, husband of Nintinugga
