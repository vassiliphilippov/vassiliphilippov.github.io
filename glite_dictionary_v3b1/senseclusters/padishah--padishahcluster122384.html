padishah

noun
dominant leader
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːdɪʃɑː / (AmE), / ˈpædɪʃɑː / (BrE)
a person who is the most important or dominant figure in a particular field, often used figuratively for a leader or ruler

Examples

Padishah of business
He was considered the padishah of modern architecture for his innovative designs.
In the world of jazz, she became the undisputed padishah through her talent and influence.
Many called him the padishah of cinema for his groundbreaking films and influence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emperor - a sovereign ruler of great power and rank
  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and is guided by another
  • synonym: king - a male ruler of a country or a dominant figure
