dishonesty

noun
lack of honesty or integrity
Pronunciation: / dɪˈʃɑnəsti /; / dɪˈʃɑːnəsti / (AmE), / dɪsˈɒnɪsti /; / dɪˈʃɒnɪsti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being dishonest; behavior such as lying, cheating, or stealing

Examples

Dishonesty in behavior
Her parents were disappointed by her dishonesty when she lied about her grades.
Many people believe that dishonesty in politics is a serious problem.
The manager was fired because of repeated dishonesty about company finances.
He was fired from his job because of repeated acts of dishonesty.
The politician denied any dishonesty during the investigation.
Trust is destroyed in a relationship when dishonesty is involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceit - the act of deceiving someone by concealing or misrepresenting the truth
  • antonym: honesty - the quality of being honest
