arcade game

noun
coin-operated fast-paced video game
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkeɪd ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪd ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A type of video or electronic game featuring short, fast-paced levels, simple controls, and increasing difficulty, originally designed for play on arcade machines.

Examples

A arcade game machine
Many gamers appreciate the simplicity and excitement of a well-designed arcade game.
She spent hours perfecting her skills on the latest arcade game at the shopping mall.
The new retro bar features several classic arcade games from the 1980s.
