Linnaea borealis

noun
creeping northern evergreen subshrub (twinflower)
Pronunciation: / lɪˈneɪ.ə bɒˈraɪ.əlɪs / (AmE), / lɪˈneɪ.ə bɔːˈraɪ.əlɪs / (BrE)
A creeping evergreen subshrub native to northern Europe and Asia, bearing delicate, fragrant, tubular, bell-shaped pink flowers usually in pairs; also known as twinflower.
