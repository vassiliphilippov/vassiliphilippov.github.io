butanone

noun
volatile ketone solvent used in industry
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuː.təˌnoʊn / (AmE), / ˈbjuː.tə.nəʊn / (BrE)
a colorless, flammable liquid ketone (CH3COC2H5), used as a solvent in resins, lacquers, adhesives, and cleaning fluids

Examples

Butanone solvent
Industrial cleaners often contain butanone because of its effective solvent properties.
The laboratory ordered several liters of butanone for use in their chemical synthesis experiments.
You should wear gloves when handling butanone due to its flammable and potentially hazardous nature.
