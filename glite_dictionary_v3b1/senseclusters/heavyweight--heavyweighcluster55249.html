heavyweight

noun
a large, powerful, or influential person
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛviˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɛviweɪt /; / ˈhɛviˌweɪt / (BrE)
a person of great size, strength, or influence, either physically or in terms of importance
