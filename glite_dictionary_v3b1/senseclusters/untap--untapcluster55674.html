untap

verb
to remove a tap
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæp / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæp / (BrE)
to remove a tap or spout from a container, especially a cask or keg, usually after use

Examples

You should always untap the cask once it is empty to prevent leaks.
