untap

verb
to remove a tap
7.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæp / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæp / (BrE)
to remove a tap or spout from a container, especially a cask or keg, usually after use

Examples

To untap a keg
After the party, the bartender needed to untap the empty keg before storing it away.
The workers carefully untapped the barrel to prepare it for cleaning.
You should always untap the cask once it is empty to prevent leaks.
