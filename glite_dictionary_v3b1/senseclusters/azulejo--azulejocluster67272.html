azulejo

noun
decorative tile panel
Pronunciation: / ˌæzuˈleɪhoʊ /; / ˌɑːzuˈleɪhoʊ / (AmE), / ˌæzʊˈleɪhəʊ / (BrE)
a painted, tin-glazed ceramic tile or a decorative panel made from such tiles, especially used in Spain and Portugal
