mump

verb
to mutter complaints
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌmp / (AmE), / mʌmp / (BrE)
to mutter or mumble something indistinctly, often as a complaint or in annoyance

Examples

To mump complaints
He would often mump about the extra work given to him after hours.
Instead of speaking up, she chose to mump her complaints quietly in the corner.
The children mumped under their breath when told to clean their room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: grumble - to complain in a quiet or low voice
  • synonym: mumble - to speak quietly and not clearly
