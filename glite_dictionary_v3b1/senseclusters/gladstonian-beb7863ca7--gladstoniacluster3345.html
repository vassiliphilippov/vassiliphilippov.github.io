Gladstonian

noun
supporter of Gladstone
Pronunciation: / ɡlædˈstoʊniən / (AmE), / ɡlædˈstəʊniən / (BrE)
a supporter or follower of William Gladstone or his political principles and policies

Examples

A Gladstonian supporter
As a proud Gladstonian, he advocated for free trade and parliamentary reform.
The debate featured a Gladstonian arguing against imperial expansion.
Many historians study the influence of Gladstonians on 19th-century British politics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - a person who supports traditional political views
  • synonym: liberal - a person who supports liberal political ideas
