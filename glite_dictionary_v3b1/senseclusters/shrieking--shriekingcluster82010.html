Synonyms

  • shriek
    2.7 3.99/m
    shriek is a sudden, very high human cry often from fear or surprise, while shrieking refers to that loud, piercing sound as ongoing.
  • scream
    1.0 19/m
    scream is a loud, sharp cry people make in fear or pain, while shrieking is a thinner, more piercing high sound.
  • screech
    2.9 2.03/m
    screech is a harsh, grating, very high sound from brakes or birds, while shrieking is more human and piercing.
  • squeal
    2.9 2.09/m
