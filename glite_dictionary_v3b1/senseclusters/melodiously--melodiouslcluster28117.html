Examples

To play melodiously

The birds sang melodiously in the early morning light.
The wind chimes tinkled melodiously in the gentle breeze.

Synonyms

    4.1 0.02/m
    tunefully sounds pleasant and easy to hum, while melodiously describes any sound that is tuneful and pleasing to listen to.
  • melodically
    3.8 0.12/m
    melodically sounds like a clear melody, while melodiously focuses on being tuneful and pleasant to listen to.
  • mellifluously
    8.3 0.02/m

  • musically
    3.4 2.70/m
