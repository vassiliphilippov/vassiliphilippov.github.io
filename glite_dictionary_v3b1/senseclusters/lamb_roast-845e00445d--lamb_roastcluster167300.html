lamb roast

noun
cut of lamb suitable for roasting
Pronunciation: / læm roʊst / (AmE), / læm rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of lamb prepared for roasting, typically cooked as a whole joint or large portion in an oven

Examples

A lamb roast dinner
She prepared a delicious lamb roast for Sunday dinner.
The chef recommended marinating the lamb roast overnight for extra flavor.
We served the lamb roast with roasted potatoes and seasonal vegetables.
