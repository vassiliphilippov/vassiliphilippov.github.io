be stumped

verb
to be confused or unable to answer
6.4 0.66/m
Pronunciation: / bi st åmpt / (AmE), / biÀê st åmpt / (BrE)
to be unable to think of an answer or solution to a question or problem; to be confused or perplexed

Examples

To be stumped by a question
She was stumped by the final question on the exam.
The interviewer was stumped when the candidate gave an unexpected answer.
Whenever complicated math problems come up, I often am stumped for a solution.
