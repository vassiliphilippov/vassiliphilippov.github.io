stoke

verb
add fuel or stir up a fire
Pronunciation: / stoʊk / (AmE), / stəʊk / (BrE)
to add fuel to or stir up a fire, making it burn more strongly; to tend to a fire

Examples

To stoke the fire
During winter, someone needs to stoke the furnace regularly.
He got up in the night to stoke the dying fire.
She used a long poker to stoke the embers back to life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extinguish - to put out a fire
  • synonym: tend - to take care of or look after, especially a fire
