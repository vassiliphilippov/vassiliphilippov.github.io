furnisher

noun
information provider
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrnɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or organization that provides or reports information or data, especially to an official agency or record

Examples

Credit furnisher
The credit bureau relies on each furnisher to submit accurate financial data.
A furnisher must ensure that all reported information is up to date and correct.
Banks often act as a furnisher when they report customer payment histories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provider - a person or organization that supplies something
  • antonym: recipient - a person or organization that receives something
