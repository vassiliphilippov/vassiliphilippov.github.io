bound

adjective
certain or destined (by fate)
3.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /baʊnd/ (AmE), /baʊnd/ (BrE)
certain or destined to do or experience something, especially as if determined by fate (usually followed by 'to')

Examples

Bound to happen
An argument was bound to arise after that controversial decision.
It was bound to rain after such dark clouds gathered above the city.
She is bound to succeed with her determination and talent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destined - certain to happen or intended by fate
  • synonym: doomed - certain to suffer something unpleasant or undesirable
  • antonym: unlikely - not probable or expected to happen
