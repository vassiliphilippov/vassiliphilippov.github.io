tapenade

noun
olive, caper, and anchovy spread
8.5 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtæ.pə.nɑːd/ /; / /ˈtɑ.pə.nɑːd/ (AmE), /ˈtæ.pə.nɑːd/ /; / /ˈtɑ.pə.nɑːd/ (BrE)
A savory spread or paste made from finely chopped or pureed capers, black olives, and anchovies mixed with olive oil, popular in Mediterranean cuisine.
