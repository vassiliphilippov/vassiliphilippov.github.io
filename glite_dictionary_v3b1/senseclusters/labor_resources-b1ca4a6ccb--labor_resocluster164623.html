labor resources

Examples

Synonyms

  • workforce
    2.4 23/m
    workforce means people employed or available in a company or industry, while labor resources means the overall supply of workers.
  • labor force
    2.2 6.80/m
    labor force includes everyone able and available to work in an area, while labor resources stresses the usable supply of workers.
  • labor pool
    3.7 0.46/m
    labor pool is the group of workers employers can hire, while labor resources is the general available supply of workers.
  • manpower
    3.7 4.17/m
    manpower refers to the number of people available to work, while labor resources sounds broader and less about counting heads.
