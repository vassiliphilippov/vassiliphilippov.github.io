arthritically

adverb
like arthritis
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈθrɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ɑːˈθrɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is affected by or resembles the symptoms of arthritis, especially stiffness or pain in movement

Examples

Move arthritically slow
After sitting for hours, she stood up arthritically, wincing at the stiffness in her knees.
The old dog moved arthritically across the yard, his joints clearly bothering him.
He climbed the stairs arthritically, each step slow and careful due to his joint pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: painfully - in a way that causes or shows pain
  • synonym: stiffly - in a way that is not flexible or is difficult to move
