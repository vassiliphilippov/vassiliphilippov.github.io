Scotch terrier

noun
a small Scottish breed of terrier dog
4.1
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / skɒtʃ ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
an old Scottish breed of small, long-haired terrier, typically black, with erect tail and ears

Examples

A Scotch terrier dog

In Victorian times, the Scotch terrier gained popularity as a companion animal in Scotland.
The Scotch terrier is known for its wiry black coat and sturdy build.
