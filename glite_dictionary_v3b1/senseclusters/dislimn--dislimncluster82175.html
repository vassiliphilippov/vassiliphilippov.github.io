dislimn

verb
to blur outline
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈlɪm / (AmE), / dɪsˈlɪm / (BrE)
to erase, blur, or make indistinct the outline or image of something

Examples

Fog dislimns shapes
As the fog thickened, the outlines of the trees began to dislimn in the distance.
Time had dislimned the features of the portrait, leaving only faint traces of color.
Painful memories slowly dislimned from her mind as the years passed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: define - to make clear or distinct
  • synonym: erase - to remove or wipe out completely
