roads

noun
partly sheltered anchorage for ships
5.2
Pronunciation: / roʊdz / (AmE), / rəʊdz / (BrE)
A partly sheltered area of water near the shore where ships can anchor safely but which is not as protected as a harbor.

Examples

The roads off coast
During the storm, the vessels found relative safety in the roads off the coast.
Several ships were anchored in the roads awaiting permission to enter the harbor.
The old town overlooked the roads, where merchant ships gathered before docking.
