pitted

adjective
having small cavities or holes
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪt.ɪd/ /; / /ˈpɪt.əd/ (AmE), /ˈpɪt.ɪd/ /; / /ˈpɪt.əd/ (BrE)
