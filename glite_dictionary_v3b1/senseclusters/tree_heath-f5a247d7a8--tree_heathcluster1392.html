tree heath

noun
Tasmanian evergreen shrubby tree
Pronunciation: / tri hɛθ /; / tri hiθ / (AmE), / triː hiːθ / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree called 'tree heath', found in regions such as Tasmania and the Mediterranean, sometimes with fragrant flowers
