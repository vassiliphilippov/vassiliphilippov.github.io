bill

noun
statement of money owed
Pronunciation: / bɪl / (AmE), / bɪl / (BrE)
a printed or written statement of money owed for goods delivered or services provided

Examples

An electricity bill
She opened the envelope and found a bill for last month's electricity.
The waiter brought the bill to our table at the end of the meal.
You must pay the bill in full before we can process your order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invoice - a list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due
  • antonym: receipt - a written acknowledgment of having received goods, money, etc.
