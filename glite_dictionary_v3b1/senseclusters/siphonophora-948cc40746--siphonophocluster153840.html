Siphonophora

noun
order of marine colonial hydrozoans
Pronunciation: / saɪˌfɒnəˈfɔrə /; / ˌsaɪfəˈnɑfərə / (AmE), / sɪˌfɒnəˈfɔːrə /; / ˌsɪfənəˈfɔːrə / (BrE)
an order of marine colonial animals (hydrozoans), such as the Portuguese man-of-war, made up of specialized individuals that function together as one organism
