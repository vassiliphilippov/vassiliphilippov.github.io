polyethnic

noun
person of mixed ethnicity
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈɛθnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɛθnɪk / (BrE)
a person who belongs to or descends from several different ethnic groups

Examples

A polyethnic person
As a polyethnic, he enjoyed learning about the different cultures in his family history.
The school celebrated diversity by inviting polyethnic students to share their traditions.
Growing up polyethnic gave her a unique perspective on cultural identity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monoethnic - a person belonging to a single ethnic group
  • synonym: multiracial - a person whose parents are of different races
