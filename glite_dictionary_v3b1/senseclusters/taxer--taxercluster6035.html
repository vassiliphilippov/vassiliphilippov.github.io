taxer

noun
person who levies taxes
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksər / (AmE), / ˈtæksə / (BrE)
a person, especially a government official, who levies or imposes taxes

Examples

Government taxer
As a government taxer, she ensured everyone paid their fair share.
Some see the taxer as a necessary figure; others view them with suspicion.
The new law aims to reduce the power of the taxer over local businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax collector - an official responsible for collecting taxes
