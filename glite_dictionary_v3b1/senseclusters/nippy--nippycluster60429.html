nippy

adjective
briskly cold or invigorating (weather)
6.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈnɪp.iː/ /; / /ˈnɪp.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnɪp.ɪ/ /; / /ˈnɪp.iː/ (BrE)
(of weather) cold and brisk in a way that is stimulating and refreshing.

Examples
