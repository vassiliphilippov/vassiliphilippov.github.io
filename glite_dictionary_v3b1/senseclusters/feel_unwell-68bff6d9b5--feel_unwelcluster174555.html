feel unwell

verb
to experience illness or discomfort
1.0
Pronunciation: / fil ənˈwɛl / (AmE), / fiːl ʌnˈwɛl / (BrE)
to experience illness or a general sense of being unwell, usually without specifying the cause

Examples

To feel unwell suddenly
I started to feel unwell after lunch, so I left work early.
If you feel unwell, it's best to rest and drink plenty of fluids.
She suddenly began to feel unwell during the meeting and had to excuse herself.
