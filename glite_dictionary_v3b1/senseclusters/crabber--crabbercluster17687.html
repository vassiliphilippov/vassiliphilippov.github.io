crabber

noun
habitual complainer
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbər / (AmE), / ˈkræbə / (BrE)
a person who complains frequently or is always finding fault

Examples

A grumpy crabber
Nobody wanted to sit next to the crabber because he always complained about everything.
The team's crabber was never satisfied with any of the solutions offered.
She tried not to be a crabber during meetings, but sometimes her frustration showed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complainer - someone who expresses dissatisfaction frequently
  • synonym: grumbler - a person who complains often
  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and positive
