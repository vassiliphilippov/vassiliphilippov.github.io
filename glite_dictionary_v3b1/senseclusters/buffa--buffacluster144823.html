buffa

noun
comic opera style
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːfə / (AmE), / ˈbʊfə / (BrE)
a comic opera, especially in the Italian tradition, characterized by light, humorous themes and lively music

Examples

A classic buffa
The festival will feature a classic buffa that showcases the wit and humor of Italian opera.
Many audiences enjoy the lively characters and playful music found in a buffa.
She performed in a buffa that delighted the crowd with its comic scenes and energetic arias.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opera buffa - an Italian comic opera genre
  • antonym: opera seria - a serious or tragic opera genre
