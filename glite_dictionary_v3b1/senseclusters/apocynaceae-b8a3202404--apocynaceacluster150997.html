Apocynaceae

noun
family of tropical plants with milky sap
9.9
Pronunciation: /æ.pə.sɪ.ˈneɪ.siː/ (AmE), /æ.pə.sɪ.ˈneɪ.siː/ (BrE)
a family of tropical trees, shrubs, or herbs with milky sap and showy flowers, some of which are used for medicine
