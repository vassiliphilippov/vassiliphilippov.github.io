batterer

noun
person who batters food
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.ər.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.ə.rə/ (BrE)
a cook or kitchen worker who coats food with a liquid batter before frying

Examples

Skilled batterer in the kitchen
The batterer carefully coated each piece of chicken before placing it in the fryer.
At the seafood restaurant, the batterer is responsible for preparing the fish for frying.
A skilled batterer can make the perfect crispy coating for fried vegetables.
