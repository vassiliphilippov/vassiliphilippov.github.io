bawdy

adjective
humorously indecent or vulgar
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔdi / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdi / (BrE)
dealing with sexual matters in a comical or coarse way; humorously indecent or vulgar

Examples

Bawdy jokes
She often wrote bawdy songs that poked fun at society's taboos.
The comedian's bawdy jokes made the whole audience blush and laugh at the same time.
Their conversation quickly turned bawdy, full of double entendres and raucous laughter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaste - abstaining from indecency or sexual matters, especially in speech or writing
  • synonym: lewd - crude and offensive in a sexual way
  • synonym: ribald - referring to speech or behavior that is coarse or vulgar, especially in a humorous way
