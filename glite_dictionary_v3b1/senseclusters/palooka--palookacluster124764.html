palooka

noun
second-rate boxer; inept person
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːkə / (AmE), / pəˈluːkə / (BrE)
An incompetent or second-rate boxer; by extension, a clumsy or inept person.

Examples

A palooka boxer
After losing every round, the young boxer was dismissed as just another palooka.
He fought like a palooka, swinging wildly without any strategy.
The movie tells the story of a palooka who dreams of becoming a champion one day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bum - an inferior or incompetent boxer
  • antonym: champion - an outstanding prizefighter
