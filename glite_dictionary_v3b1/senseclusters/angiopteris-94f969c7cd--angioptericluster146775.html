Angiopteris

noun
genus of large tropical ferns
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiˈɑptərɪs / (AmE), / ˌandʒɪˈɒptərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical ferns with massive fronds, including large tree ferns found mainly in the Old World tropics.

Angiopteris tropical fern
