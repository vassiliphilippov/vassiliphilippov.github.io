cor anglais

noun
woodwind instrument similar to the oboe
Pronunciation: / kɔɹ ɑŋˈɡleɪ / (AmE), / kɔːr ˈæŋɡleɪ / (BrE)
a double-reed woodwind instrument similar to the oboe but pitched lower, typically in F

Examples

The cor anglais instrument
A mellow tone distinguishes the cor anglais from other woodwinds in the ensemble.
She learned to play both the oboe and the cor anglais in college.
The orchestra featured a beautiful solo on the cor anglais during the second movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oboe - a double-reed woodwind instrument, higher in pitch than the cor anglais
