Examples

A clear-eyed view
Despite the excitement, he remained clear-eyed about the potential risks involved.
Her clear-eyed analysis of the situation helped the company make the right choice.
The biography provides a clear-eyed look at the challenges the artist faced throughout her career.

Synonyms

  • clear-sighted
    5.5 0.04/m
    clear-sighted sees practical consequences and understands situations quickly, while clear-eyed focuses on objective, unemotional judgment of what is true.
  • clearheaded
    4.9 0.02/m
    clearheaded is free from mental confusion and makes sensible choices, while clear-eyed suggests precise, cool, and emotionally distant judgment.
  • clear-thinking
    4.2 0.21/m
    clear-thinking means using logic and steady thought to solve problems, while clear-eyed adds an extra sense of detached, unemotional realism.
