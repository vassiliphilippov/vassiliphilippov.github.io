uncultured

adjective
lacking education or refinement
4.5 4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌl.tʃɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌl.tʃəd / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking education, taste, or appreciation for art, literature, or intellectual pursuits; not refined or cultivated.

Examples

An uncultured person
He was considered uncultured because he showed little interest in literature or music.
Some find his manners uncultured, but he is very kind at heart.
The critics dismissed the artist as uncultured and lacking sophistication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cultured - having refined taste or education in the arts
  • synonym: refined - having or showing well-bred feeling, taste, etc.
