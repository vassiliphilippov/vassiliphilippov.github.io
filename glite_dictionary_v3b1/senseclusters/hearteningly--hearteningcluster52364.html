hearteningly

adverb
encouragingly
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrtənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtənɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that gives encouragement or makes someone feel more hopeful

Examples

Hearteningly positive
The news was hearteningly positive after weeks of uncertainty.
She smiled hearteningly at her friend before the big exam.
The community responded hearteningly to the call for volunteers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouragingly - in a way that makes someone lose confidence or hope
  • synonym: encouragingly - in a way that gives support or confidence
