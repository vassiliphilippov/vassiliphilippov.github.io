run scared

verb
act anxiously or fearfully, often due to threat or pressure
Pronunciation: / rʌn ˈskɛrd / (AmE), / rʌn ˈskeəd / (BrE)
to act nervously or anxiously, especially in response to fear, pressure, or threat of failure

Examples

To run scared in competition
He tends to run scared whenever he faces tough competition.
Politicians sometimes run scared in the run-up to elections.
The company started to run scared after negative reports in the press.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panic - to feel a sudden, uncontrollable fear or anxiety
  • antonym: stand firm - to remain resolute or unyielding in the face of adversity
