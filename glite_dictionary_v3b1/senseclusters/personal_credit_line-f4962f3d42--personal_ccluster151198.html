Synonyms

  • personal line of credit
    4.8 0.00/m
    personal line of credit is the phrase banks commonly use in documents for an individual's borrowing limit, while personal credit line names the same consumer arrangement.
  • line of credit
    3.1 1/m
    line of credit covers lending for businesses or people and can be general, while personal credit line refers specifically to an individual's borrowing limit.
  • credit line
    3.7 0.3/m
    credit line names the amount a lender agrees to lend up to, while personal credit line signals that the limit is for an individual borrower.
  • bank line
    4.6 0.00/m
    bank line often means a bank's approved maximum for a customer, often used for business credit or overdrafts, while personal credit line is for an individual consumer.
