rich person

noun
a wealthy individual
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪtʃ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)

Examples

A rich person with money

It's not uncommon for a rich person to own multiple houses around the world.
