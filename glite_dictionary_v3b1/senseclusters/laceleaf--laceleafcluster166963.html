laceleaf

noun
tropical houseplant
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈleɪsˌliːf / (BrE)
a tropical plant of the genus Anthurium, grown as a houseplant for its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and colorful spathe

Examples

Tropical laceleaf houseplant
She placed a laceleaf on her desk to add a touch of greenery to the office.
The bright red spathe of the laceleaf made it a popular choice among houseplant enthusiasts.
Caring for a laceleaf requires keeping it in a warm, humid environment with indirect sunlight.

Related Terms
