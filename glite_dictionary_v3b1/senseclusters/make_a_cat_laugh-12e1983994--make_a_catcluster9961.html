make a cat laugh

verb
be extremely funny; cause great amusement
5.5
Pronunciation: / meɪk ə kæt læf / (AmE), / meɪk ə kæt lɑːf / (BrE)
to be extremely funny or cause great amusement, often suggesting that something is so absurdly funny that even a cat would laugh

Examples

To make a cat laugh
Her silly dance moves would make a cat laugh.
Some of these comedy films are enough to make a cat laugh.
That joke was so ridiculous it could make a cat laugh.
