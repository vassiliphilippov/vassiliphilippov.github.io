museological

adjective
relating to museums
Pronunciation: / ˌmjuziəˈlɑdʒɪkəl /; / ˌmjuːziəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmjuːziəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to museums, their practices, or the study and organization of museums

Examples

Museological study
The exhibition followed strict museological guidelines to protect the ancient artifacts.
Her research focused on museological methods for displaying fragile textiles.
Modern museological practices emphasize both preservation and public engagement.
The new gallery was praised for its museological approach to displaying ancient artifacts.
Her research focuses on museological methods for engaging visitors in interactive exhibits.
A museological perspective can help museums adapt to changing cultural expectations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curatorial - relating to the work of a curator in a museum or gallery
  • synonym: museum - a building where objects of historical, scientific, or artistic interest are kept and shown
