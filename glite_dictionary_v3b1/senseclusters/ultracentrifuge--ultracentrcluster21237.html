ultracentrifuge

verb
separate using an ultracentrifuge
7.8 8.3

to subject (something) to the process of separation by means of an ultracentrifuge.

Examples

To ultracentrifuge samples
After collecting the cell lysate, scientists ultracentrifuge it to separate the proteins from other components.
The samples were ultracentrifuged for two hours at 100,000g to pellet the membranes.
We will ultracentrifuge the virus suspension to purify the viral particles before analysis.
