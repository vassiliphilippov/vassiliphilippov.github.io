hammer-headed attitude

noun
stubborn or inflexible mindset
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmər ˌhɛdɪd ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈhæmə ˌhɛdɪd ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
A stubborn or inflexible attitude that refuses to change or consider other points of view.

Examples

A hammer-headed attitude refuses change
Despite repeated advice, he stuck to his hammer-headed attitude and made no changes to his plan.
Her hammer-headed attitude kept her from accepting helpful criticism at work.
It's difficult to negotiate with someone who has such a hammer-headed attitude toward compromise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open-mindedness - willingness to consider new ideas
  • synonym: stubbornness - the quality of being unwilling to change one's mind
