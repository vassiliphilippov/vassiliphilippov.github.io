outskirt

noun
area at the edge of a city or town

an area at the edge of a city or town, far from its center

Examples

The outskirts of town
New housing developments have appeared on the outskirts of town.
The airport is located on the outskirts, several miles from the city centre.
Wildlife is often seen roaming the outskirts during early mornings.

Synonyms

  • edge of town
    3.0 1.07/m
    edge of town marks where the town ends and the countryside begins, while outskirt refers to the area outside the town center.
  • fringe
    4.0 4.24/m
    fringe means the outer border of an area and is more general, while outskirt refers to land outside a town center.
