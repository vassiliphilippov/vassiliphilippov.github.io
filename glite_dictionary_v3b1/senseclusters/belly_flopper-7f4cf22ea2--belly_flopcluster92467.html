belly flopper

noun
a dive landing flat on the stomach
9.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɛl.ɪ ˈflɑːp.ər/ /; / /ˈbɛl.ɪ ˈflɔːp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbɛl.ɪ ˈflɒp.ə/ (BrE)
A dive in which a person lands flat on their abdomen, causing the stomach to take the full force of impact with the water, usually resulting in a painful sensation.

Examples

A belly flopper dive
After slipping on the diving board, she ended up being a spectacular belly flopper.
Every summer, someone tries to be the best belly flopper at the neighborhood pool party.
The crowd winced as he became a true belly flopper at the pool competition.
