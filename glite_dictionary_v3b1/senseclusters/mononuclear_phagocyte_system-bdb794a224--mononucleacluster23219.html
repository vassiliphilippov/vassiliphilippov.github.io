mononuclear phagocyte system

biological system of macrophages

A system in the body made up of widely distributed macrophages, both free and fixed, that are derived from bone marrow and play a role in immune defense and removal of cellular debris.

Examples

Liver Kupffer cells are a major component of the mononuclear phagocyte system.

The mononuclear phagocyte system helps clear pathogens and debris from tissues throughout the body.
