X-ed

verb
to close a window
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksˌt / (AmE), / ˈɛksˌt / (BrE)
to close a window, dialog, or application by clicking the 'X' button in a computer interface

Examples

He X-ed the window
If you accidentally X-ed the document, you can reopen it from the recent files list.
She quickly X-ed the annoying pop-up before it could load any ads.
Whenever the program freezes, I just X-ed it and restart.
