intellective

noun
mental act
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈlɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈlɛktɪv / (BrE)
a mental act or process involving understanding or intellectual thought

Examples

Intellective process
Solving the puzzle required a great deal of intellective effort and concentration.
Her intellective abilities were evident in the way she analyzed complex problems.
The course focuses on developing students' intellective skills through challenging assignments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cognition - the mental process of acquiring knowledge and understanding
  • antonym: intuition - the ability to understand something immediately, without conscious reasoning
