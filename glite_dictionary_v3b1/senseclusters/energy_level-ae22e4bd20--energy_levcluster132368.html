energy level

noun
atmosphere or liveliness of a place or group
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈen.ər.dʒiː ˈlev.əl/ (AmE), /ˈen.ə.dʒiː ˈlev.əl/ (BrE)
the general atmosphere, mood, or liveliness of a place or group of people at a particular time.
