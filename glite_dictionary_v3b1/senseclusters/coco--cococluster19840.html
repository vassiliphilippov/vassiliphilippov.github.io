coco

noun
tall tropical palm tree bearing coconuts
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.kəʊ / (BrE)
A tall palm tree, widely grown in tropical regions, that bears coconuts as its fruit.

Examples

A coco palm
Farmers harvest coconuts from the coco every season.
In many tropical countries, the coco is an important resource for food and materials.
The coco grows well in sandy soils near the coastline.
