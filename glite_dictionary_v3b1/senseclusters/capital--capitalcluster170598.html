capital

adjective
uppercase letter form
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
(of a letter) written or printed in its large form, used at the beginning of sentences and proper nouns.

Examples

Capital letters only
Make sure every sentence starts with a capital letter.
Please write your name using a capital letter at the beginning.
The word 'London' starts with a capital L.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowercase - (of a letter) written in its small form
  • synonym: majuscule - large lettering, either capital or uppercase letters
