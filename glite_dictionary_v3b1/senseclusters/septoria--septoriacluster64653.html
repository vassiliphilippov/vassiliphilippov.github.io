septoria

noun
fungal leaf disease
Pronunciation: / sɛpˈtɔːriə / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɔːriə / (BrE)
a plant disease caused by fungi of the genus Septoria, producing small dark spots on leaves and weakening the plant

Examples

Septoria leaf spots
The tomato crop was badly affected by septoria after several weeks of rainy weather.
Gardeners should remove infected leaves to help control the spread of septoria in their plants.
Early detection of septoria can prevent serious damage to vegetable gardens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blight - a plant disease causing damage or death to plants
  • synonym: leaf spot - a plant disease causing spots on leaves
