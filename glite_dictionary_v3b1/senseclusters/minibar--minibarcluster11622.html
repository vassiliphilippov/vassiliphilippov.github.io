minibar

noun
small drinks cupboard, often in hotels
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌbɑː / (BrE)
A small cabinet or cupboard, often found in hotel rooms, equipped to hold bottles and provide drinks and snacks.

Examples

Hotel minibar
She opened the minibar to grab a chilled bottle of water after her long journey.
The hotel room featured a well-stocked minibar with snacks and beverages.
Their living room has a stylish minibar tucked away in the corner for entertaining guests.
