gearless

adjective
without equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪrləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪələs / (BrE)
not wearing, carrying, or equipped with the necessary tools, equipment, or protective clothing for an activity

Examples

Gearless hiker
The hiker went gearless to challenge himself and test his survival skills.
She entered the workshop gearless, forgetting her safety goggles and gloves at home.
Attempting the race gearless made the experience much more difficult for the participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: equipped - provided with the necessary items for a particular purpose
  • synonym: unprepared - not ready or equipped for something
