roadie

noun
band equipment assistant
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.diː/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.diː/ (BrE)
a person who travels with a band or performer and helps set up, transport, and manage their equipment

Examples

Band roadie sets up
The roadie arrived early to set up the stage before the concert began.
Without the help of a skilled roadie, the band would struggle to get ready on time.
After the show, the roadie quickly packed up all the instruments and cables.
The roadie arrived early to set up the sound system before the band performed.
After the concert, the roadies quickly packed up all the instruments and lights.
He worked as a roadie for several famous rock bands during the summer.
