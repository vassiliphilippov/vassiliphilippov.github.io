zapper

noun
device that kills via electric current
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈzæpər / (AmE), / ˈzapə / (BrE)
an electrical device that uses electric currents to injure or kill living things, especially insects

Examples

Electric zapper
He accidentally touched the zapper and got a mild shock.
The restaurant has a zapper hanging near the window to keep flies away.
We bought a new zapper to deal with the mosquitoes in our backyard.
