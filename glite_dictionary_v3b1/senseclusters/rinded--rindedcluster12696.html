rinded

adjective
having an outer skin
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈraɪndɪd / (BrE)
having a rind or outer skin, especially referring to food such as cheese or fruit

Examples

Some fruits are rinded, making them easier to peel and eat.
He prefers rinded bread for its crunchy crust and unique flavor.
