haven

noun
sheltered port or harbor

Pronunciation: /ˈheɪ.vən/ (AmE), /ˈheɪ.vən/ (BrE)
A sheltered place, especially a port or harbor, where ships can take refuge or unload and load cargo safely.

Examples

The fishing boats found a haven from the storm in the small bay.
