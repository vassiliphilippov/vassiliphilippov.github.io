propagator

noun
person who spreads news or ideas
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.pəˌɡeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈprɒp.əˌɡeɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who spreads news, ideas, or information to others

Examples

A propagator of ideas
As a propagator of new theories, she often sparked lively debate among her peers.
False information can easily be amplified by an unwitting propagator.
The internet has turned many individuals into propagators of news and opinions.
