Examples

Synonyms

  • close-packed
    4.9 0.04/m
    close-packed is packed together very tightly with almost no space between items, while packed is more general.
  • compressed
    3.7 0.02/m
    compressed is pressed together by force to make something smaller, whereas packed means put closely but not always squeezed.
  • dense
    3.2 1/m
    dense describes materials with parts close together throughout, while packed describes items pushed into a tight space.
  • serried
    9.6 0.03/m
    serried describes rows standing very close with little space between them, whereas packed can mean many items squeezed together in any shape.
  • tight
    1.0 50/m
    tight means fixed, close, or not loose, while packed refers to many things pressed or fitted into a small space.
  • wedged
    4.3 0.00/m
    wedged is stuck tightly into a space so it cannot move, whereas packed simply means items are placed closely together.
