packed

adjective
pressed or compressed; tightly packed
3.9
Pronunciation: / pækt / (AmE), / pækt / (BrE)
pressed together or compressed, often into a tight mass or space

Examples

Packed tightly
She opened the freezer and found it packed with frozen dinners.
The concert hall was so packed that there was barely room to stand.
The path was covered in packed snow, making it slippery to walk on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressed - pressed into a smaller space; made compact
  • antonym: loose - not firmly or tightly packed; not compacted
