remonstrance

noun
formal protest or objection
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɑnstrəns / (AmE), / rɪˈmɒnstrəns / (BrE)
the act of expressing formal or earnest opposition, protest, or objection

Examples

A remonstrance letter
Despite their remonstrance, the policy was implemented without changes.
She wrote a letter of remonstrance to the local council regarding the new regulations.
The committee issued a remonstrance against the proposed budget cuts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protest - an expression or declaration of objection or disapproval
