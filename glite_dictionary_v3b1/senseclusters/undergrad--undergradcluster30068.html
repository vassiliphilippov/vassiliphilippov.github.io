undergrad

noun
university student without a first degree
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌɡræd / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌɡræd / (BrE)
a university or college student who has not yet received a first degree

Examples

An undergrad student
As an undergrad, he participated in several research projects.
During her freshman year, she was an undergrad majoring in biology.
Most undergrads live in the campus dormitories during their first two years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postgrad - a student engaged in postgraduate studies after earning a first degree
  • synonym: undergraduate - a student at a university or college who has not yet earned a first degree
