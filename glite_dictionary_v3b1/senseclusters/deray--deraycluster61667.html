deray

verb
to disturb order
9.9 0.00/m
to disturb or throw into disorder; to disrupt the order or peace of something

Examples

To deray a meeting
The sudden noise did deray the peaceful gathering.
A single shout was enough to deray the careful plans they had made.
In old tales, a mischievous spirit would often deray the village festival.

  • derail
