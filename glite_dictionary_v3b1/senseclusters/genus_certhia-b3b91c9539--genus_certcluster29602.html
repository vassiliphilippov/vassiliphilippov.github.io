genus Certhia

noun
genus of treecreeper birds
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsɜːr.θi.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsɜː.θɪ.ə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Certhiidae, comprising the treecreepers, small songbirds known for their climbing behavior.
