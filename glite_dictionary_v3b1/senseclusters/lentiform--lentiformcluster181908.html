lentiform

adjective
lens-shaped; convex on both sides
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛn.t̬ɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.tɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of a lens or lentil; convex on both sides.

Examples

A lentiform shape
A lentiform lens focuses light effectively due to its shape.
Some seeds have a lentiform appearance, being curved on both sides.
The lentiform nucleus is a prominent structure in the brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biconvex - having both surfaces convex
