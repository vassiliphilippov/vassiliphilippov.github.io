Germanist

noun
a specialist in Germanic studies
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜrmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmənɪst / (BrE)
a specialist in the study of Germanic languages, culture, or literature, especially German

Examples

A Germanist scholar
As a leading Germanist, she has published extensively on Goethe and German Romanticism.
He became a respected Germanist after years of research on medieval German literature.
The university recently hired a Germanist to expand its German studies program.
