leaf disease

noun
disease affecting plant leaves
Pronunciation: / lif dɪˈziz / (AmE), / liːf dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease of a plant that primarily affects its leaves or foliage.

Examples

Leaf disease in plants
Proper crop rotation can help prevent leaf disease in tomato plants.
The farmer was worried about a new leaf disease spreading through his wheat fields.
The horticulturist identified the spots as signs of a serious leaf disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blight - a plant disease, especially one caused by fungi, affecting various parts of plants
  • synonym: rust - a fungal disease causing reddish or brownish spots on plant leaves
