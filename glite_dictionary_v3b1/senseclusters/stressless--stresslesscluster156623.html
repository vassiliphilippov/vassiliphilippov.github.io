stressless

adjective
free from strain
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstres.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈstres.ləs/ (BrE)
not subject to mechanical stress or strain; free from forces that cause deformation or internal stress in materials or structures

Examples

Stressless material
The engineer ensured the bridge joints remained stressless after the repairs.
A stressless environment is crucial for testing the true properties of new materials.
After the cooling process, the glass panels were completely stressless and ready for installation.
