hurf

noun
nonsense remark
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɜrf / (AmE), / hɜːf / (BrE)
a nonsensical or unintelligible remark, message, or piece of writing, often blustering or meaningless.

Examples

A confusing hurf
The politician's speech was dismissed as a hurf by critics who found it confusing.
After reading the email, she realized it was just a hurf with no real information.
Sometimes online comments are nothing but hurf and add little to the discussion.
