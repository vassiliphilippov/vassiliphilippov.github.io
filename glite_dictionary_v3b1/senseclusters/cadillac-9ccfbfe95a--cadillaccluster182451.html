Cadillac

noun
top-quality example
Pronunciation: / ˈkædɪˌlæk / (AmE), / ˈkædɪlæk / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing considered the best, most luxurious, or highest-quality example of its kind

Examples

The Cadillac of phones
This new smartphone is the Cadillac of mobile devices, offering unmatched performance and design.
Many chefs consider this knife set the Cadillac of kitchen tools.
For years, that hotel has been known as the Cadillac of luxury accommodations in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Rolls-Royce - something regarded as the best of its kind
  • synonym: gold standard - the best or most reliable example of something
