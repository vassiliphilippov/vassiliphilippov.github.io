gallop

verb
make a horse move at full speed
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæl.əp / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.əp / (BrE)
to cause a horse or other animal to move at a full gallop

Examples

To gallop a horse
Did you gallop the horse just now, or was it trotting?
He expertly galloped the horse across the field to impress the judges.
She taught her daughter how to gallop the pony along the fence line.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: canter - to make a horse move at a moderate speed between a trot and a gallop
