profiler

noun
analyzes patterns
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊfaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈprəʊfaɪlə / (BrE)
a person or tool that analyzes data or behavior to identify patterns, especially in investigations

Examples

Criminal profiler
The police hired a profiler to help track down the serial offender.
A software profiler can identify which parts of a program use the most resources.
The profiler created a detailed report on the suspect's likely habits and background.
The police hired a profiler to help understand the suspect's motives.
As a criminal profiler, she studied crime scenes to predict future actions.
The documentary followed a profiler working on a high-profile case.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analyst - a person who examines information in detail
  • synonym: detective - a person who investigates crimes
  • synonym: investigator - a person who carries out formal inquiries or investigations
