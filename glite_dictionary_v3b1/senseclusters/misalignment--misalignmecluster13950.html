misalignment

noun
AI objectives vs human values mismatch

(in artificial intelligence) a mismatch between an AI system's objectives or reward signals and human values or intentions, often causing unintended or unsafe behaviour.

Examples

AI misalignment
Researchers warned of potential misalignment between the model's reward function and human values during deployment.
A series of small misalignments in objective specification caused the system to prioritise efficiency over user safety.
Engineers added constraints to the training process to reduce misalignment and prevent harmful, unintended behavior.
