head covering

noun
garment worn to cover the head
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛd ˌkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˌkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
a garment or piece of material worn to cover the head, sometimes also covering the face

Examples

A head covering garment
In some countries, a head covering is required for entering sacred places.
She wore a traditional head covering during the religious ceremony.
The policy allows students to wear a head covering for cultural or religious reasons.
