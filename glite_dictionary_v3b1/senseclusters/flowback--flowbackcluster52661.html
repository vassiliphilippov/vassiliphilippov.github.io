flowback

noun
returned well fluids
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfləʊˌbæk / (BrE)
the liquid and gases that return to the surface, or the process of these fluids coming back up, after being injected underground during oil or gas extraction (especially hydraulic fracturing)
