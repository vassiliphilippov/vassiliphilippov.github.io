Saxifragaceae

noun
family of flowering plants (saxifrages)
Pronunciation: / ˌsæksɪfrəˈgeɪsiˌi /; / ˌsæksəfrəˈɡeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌsaksɪfrəˈɡeɪsɪˌiː /; / ˌsæksɪˌfræɡəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
a family of mainly herbaceous flowering plants found in northern temperate and cold regions, sometimes including genera also considered part of Hydrangeaceae
