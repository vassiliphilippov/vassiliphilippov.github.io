rent-a-womb

noun
informal term for surrogacy arrangement
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛnt ə wum / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt ə wuːm / (BrE)
an informal term for a surrogacy arrangement or service, in which a woman carries and delivers a baby for another person or couple

Examples

Many couples look for rent-a-womb options when they are unable to conceive naturally.
