ribcage

noun
chest bones
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbˌkeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbˌkeɪdʒ / (BrE)
the set of curved bones in the chest that surround and protect the heart and lungs

Examples

Broken ribcage
The X-ray showed that his ribcage was not fractured after the accident.
She took a deep breath, feeling her ribcage expand as her lungs filled with air.
Protective gear is designed to shield the ribcage during contact sports.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spine - the column of bones along the back
  • synonym: thorax - the part of the body between the neck and the abdomen
