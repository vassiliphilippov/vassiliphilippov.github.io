frother

noun
enthusiastic person
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔːðər / (AmE), / ˈfrɒθə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is extremely enthusiastic or passionate about a particular activity or interest, especially in Australian or New Zealand English

Examples

Surfing frother
Jake is a real frother when it comes to surfing and never misses a good wave.
The club is full of frothers who talk about mountain biking all day long.
As a frother for live music, she attends every concert in town.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casual - a person with only a mild or occasional interest in something
  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive and single-minded enthusiasm
