assertively

adverb
in a confident, forceful manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / əˈsɝː.t̬ɪv.li / (AmE), / əˈsɜː.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a confident and forceful way, showing one's opinions or needs clearly and firmly.

Examples

To speak assertively
Children should be encouraged to express their feelings assertively rather than passively.
She spoke assertively during the meeting, ensuring her ideas were heard.
The manager assertively addressed the concerns of the staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidently - in a self-assured manner
  • antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking-confidence way
