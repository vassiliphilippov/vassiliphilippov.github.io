Lepiota rubrotincta

noun
a mushroom with a pink to red cap
Pronunciation: / ˌliːpiˈoʊtə ˌruːbroʊˈtɪŋktə / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈəʊtə ˌruːbrɒˈtɪŋktə / (BrE)
A species of agaric mushroom characterized by a relatively small pink to red cap and white gills and stalk.
