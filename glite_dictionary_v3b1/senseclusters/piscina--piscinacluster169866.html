piscina

noun
church stone basin
Pronunciation: / pɪˈsiːnə / (AmE), / pɪˈsiːnə / (BrE)
a stone basin in a church used for draining water from sacred rituals, such as washing communion vessels

Examples

Church piscina
The medieval church features a beautifully carved piscina near the altar.
During the restoration, workers discovered an ancient piscina hidden behind the choir stalls.
Water from the sacred vessels is poured into the piscina after each service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: font - a basin for holding baptismal water in a church
