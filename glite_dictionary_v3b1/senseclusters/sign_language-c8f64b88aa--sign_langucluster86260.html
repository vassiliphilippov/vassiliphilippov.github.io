sign language

noun
language using hand signs and gestures
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪn ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ /; / saɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪn ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ /; / saɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a language that uses hand signs, gestures, and facial expressions to communicate, especially used by people who are deaf or hard of hearing
