feminize

verb
to make or become more feminine
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛm.əˌnaɪz /; / ˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɛm.ɪ.naɪz /; / ˈfɛmɪnʌɪz / (BrE)
to give someone or something more feminine qualities, appearance, or character, or to become more feminine

Examples

The company aims to feminise its branding to appeal to a broader demographic.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: feminine - having qualities traditionally associated with women
  • antonym: masculinise - to make something more masculine
