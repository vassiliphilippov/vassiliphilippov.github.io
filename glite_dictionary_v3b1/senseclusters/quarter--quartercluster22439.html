quarter

noun
rear part of a ship
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
the rear part or section of a ship, especially the area on either side of the stern

Examples

Port quarter
A cannon was mounted at the port quarter to defend against boarding.
The captain stood on the quarter watching the crew below.
The storm caused heavy damage to the starboard quarter of the vessel.
