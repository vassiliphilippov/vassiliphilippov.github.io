talebearer

noun
one who spreads gossip or rumors
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌbɛərə / (BrE)
a person who spreads gossip or rumors, often indiscreetly or maliciously

Examples

A talebearer spreads gossip
He warned his son not to trust a talebearer who spreads stories without knowing the truth.
Many friendships have been ruined by a malicious talebearer in the group.
The talebearer delighted in sharing secrets about her coworkers at every opportunity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidant - a person entrusted with private matters (as antonym)
  • synonym: gossiper - a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others
  • synonym: rumormonger - a person who spreads rumors
