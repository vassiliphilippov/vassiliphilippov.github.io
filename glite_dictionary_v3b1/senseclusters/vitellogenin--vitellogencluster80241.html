vitellogenin

noun
egg yolk protein
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɪˌtɛləˈdʒɛnɪn / (AmE), / vɪˌtɛləˈdʒɛnɪn / (BrE)
a protein made by female or egg-laying animals that is used to form egg yolk and can be measured to study animal health
