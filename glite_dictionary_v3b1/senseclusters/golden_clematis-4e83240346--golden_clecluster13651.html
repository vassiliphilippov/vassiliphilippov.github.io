golden clematis

noun
a clematis plant with yellow flowers
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən kləˈmætɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən klɪˈmætɪs / (BrE)
a Chinese species of clematis with toothed leaves and large yellow flowers, often grown as an ornamental climber
