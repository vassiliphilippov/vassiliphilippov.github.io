uncompanionable

adjective
not friendly
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈpænjənəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpænjənəbl / (BrE)
not friendly or pleasant to be with; difficult to get along with

Examples

An uncompanionable person
His uncompanionable attitude made group activities uncomfortable for everyone.
She found her new roommate rather uncompanionable and preferred to spend time alone.
Despite his skills, his uncompanionable manner kept him isolated from his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unsociable - not enjoying or seeking the company of others
