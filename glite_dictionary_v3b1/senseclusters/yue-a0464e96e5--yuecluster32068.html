Yue

noun
Chinese dialect group spoken in Canton and Hong Kong
Pronunciation: / juː / (AmE), / juː / (BrE)
the group of Chinese dialects spoken in Guangdong (Canton), Hong Kong, and neighboring regions, most notably including Cantonese

Examples

Yue dialects
Yue is one of the major language groups in southern China.
Linguists study Yue to understand the diversity of Chinese dialects.
Many overseas Chinese communities speak Yue as their first language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Cantonese - the most widely spoken variety of Yue Chinese
