preliteracy

adjective
before reading skills
Pronunciation: / priːˈlɪtərəsi / (AmE), / priːˈlɪtərəsi / (BrE)
relating to the development of skills or activities that prepare children to read and write before formal instruction begins

Examples

Preliteracy activities
The preschool curriculum includes many preliteracy activities to help children get ready for reading.
Parents can support preliteracy development by reading aloud and playing word games at home.
Teachers use songs and rhymes as part of their preliteracy instruction for young learners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: early childhood education - education for young children before primary school
  • antonym: literacy - the ability to read and write
