external nasal vein

noun
vein draining blood from the external nose
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɜrnəl ˈneɪzəl veɪn / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəl ˈneɪzəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the external nose, emptying into the angular or facial vein
