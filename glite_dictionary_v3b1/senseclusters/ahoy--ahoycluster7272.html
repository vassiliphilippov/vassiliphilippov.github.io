ahoy

noun
nautical greeting
8.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈhɔɪ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈhɔɪ/ (BrE)
a call or shout used as a greeting or to attract attention, especially by sailors at sea

Examples

A loud ahoy
The captain's loud ahoy echoed across the water as the ship approached the harbor.
With a cheerful ahoy, the sailor greeted his friends on the dock.
A sudden ahoy from the lookout warned the crew of another vessel nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hail - a call to attract attention, especially at sea
