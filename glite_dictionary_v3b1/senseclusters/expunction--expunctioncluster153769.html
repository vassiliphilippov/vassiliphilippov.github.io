expunction

noun
deletion or erasure, esp. of a record
8.1 8.8
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈspʌŋk.ʃən/ /; / /ɛk.ˈspʌŋk.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈspʌŋk.ʃən/ /; / /ɛk.ˈspʌŋk.ʃən/ (BrE)
