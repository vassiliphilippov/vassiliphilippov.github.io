embosser

noun
device for raised designs
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbɑsər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɒsə / (BrE)
a device, machine, or person that creates raised designs or letters on a surface, especially for decoration or authentication

Examples

An embosser machine
The official used an embosser to add a raised seal to the document.
Modern embossers can create intricate patterns on invitations and cards.
She purchased a personalized embosser for marking her books with her initials.
The company used an embosser to add its logo to official documents.
She bought an embosser to personalize her wedding invitations with a raised monogram.
An embosser can make certificates look more professional and secure.
