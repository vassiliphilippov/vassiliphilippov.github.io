Usenet

noun
online discussion network
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːzˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈjuːzˌnɛt / (BrE)
a worldwide system of online discussion groups and the community of people who participate in them

Examples

Usenet discussion groups

The culture of Usenet influenced the development of online communities and internet etiquette.
Some academic discussions from the 1990s can still be found in Usenet archives.
