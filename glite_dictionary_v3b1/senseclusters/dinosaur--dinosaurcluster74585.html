dinosaur

noun
extinct reptile of the Mesozoic era
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
any of a group of extinct reptiles that lived during the Mesozoic era, often characterized by large size and diverse forms

Examples

Large dinosaurs
Many children are fascinated by the idea of enormous dinosaurs roaming the earth.
The dinosaur fossils were discovered deep beneath the rocky cliffs.
The museum's exhibit includes a full-scale skeleton of a dinosaur.
