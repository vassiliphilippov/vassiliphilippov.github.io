book agent

noun
person who sells books
1.0
Pronunciation: / bʊk ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / bʊk ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sell books, often by visiting potential buyers or working as a traveling salesperson

Examples

Book agent sells books
Her uncle worked as a book agent traveling from town to town across the region.
In the past, rural families often met a book agent when they needed new textbooks.
The book agent came to our door every spring offering the latest encyclopedias.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookseller - person who sells books, especially in a shop
  • synonym: salesman - a person whose job is to sell things
