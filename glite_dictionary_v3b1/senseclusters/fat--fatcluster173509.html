fat

noun
excess bodily weight
3.6
Pronunciation: / fæt / (AmE), / fæt / (BrE)
Excess bodily weight; the state of being overweight or having too much body fat.

Examples

Childhood fat
Children are often teased for their fat at school.
He struggles with losing fat despite exercising regularly.
Her doctor worries that too much fat could affect her health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obesity - the condition of being grossly overweight
  • antonym: thinness - the quality or state of having little body fat
