wampus

noun
mythical creature
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːmpəs / (AmE), / ˈwɒmpəs / (BrE)
a mythical or imaginary creature featured in North American tall tales or folklore

Examples

The wampus cat
The children whispered about a wampus lurking in the shadows of the old forest.
Local legends describe the wampus as a mysterious beast with glowing eyes.
Some campers claim to have heard strange noises they blamed on a wampus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptid - an animal whose existence is unsubstantiated
  • synonym: monster - an imaginary or legendary creature, often large and frightening
