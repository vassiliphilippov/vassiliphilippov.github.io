quaestio

noun
formal question
Pronunciation: / kwɛsˈtiːoʊ / (AmE), / kwɛsˈtiːəʊ / (BrE)
a formal question or topic for discussion, especially in medieval philosophy or law

Examples

A quaestio for debate
The philosopher presented a new quaestio for debate at the conference.
In medieval universities, students often discussed a quaestio as part of their studies.
The legal scholar wrote an article analyzing the quaestio of justice in ancient law.
