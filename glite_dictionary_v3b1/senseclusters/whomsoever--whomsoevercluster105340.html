whomsoever

pronoun
formal whoever
9.9 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / huÀêmÀàso äÀå…õv…ôr / (AmE), / huÀêmÀàs…ô äÀå…õv…ô / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or very formal word meaning whoever or anyone whom

Examples

To whomsoever it may concern
The invitation is open to whomsoever wishes to attend the ceremony.
The prize will be awarded to whomsoever completes the challenge first.
The document may be given to whomsoever you trust with this responsibility.
