donative

noun
formal gift or donation
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊnətɪv / (AmE), / ˈdəʊnətɪv / (BrE)
a formal or legal term for a gift or donation given freely

Examples

A donative contract
The museum was able to expand its collection thanks to a large donative from a local family.
In legal documents, a donative is often distinguished from a payment for services.
The university established a scholarship fund with the help of a generous donative.
The charity was grateful for the large donative it received from the foundation.
A significant donative helped the university build a new library.
The lawyer explained the tax implications of making a donative to the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donation - something that is given to a person or organization, especially money
  • synonym: gift - something given voluntarily without payment in return
