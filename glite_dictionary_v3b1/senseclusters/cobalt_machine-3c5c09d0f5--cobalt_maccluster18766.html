cobalt machine

noun
radiation therapy device for cancer treatment
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌbɔlt məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌbɒlt məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A device used to treat cancer by delivering gamma rays from cobalt-60 radioisotopes.

Examples

A cobalt machine treatment
Because the cobalt machine emits gamma rays, strict safety protocols must be followed during operation.
During the 1970s, many regional centers used the cobalt machine as their main radiotherapy tool.
The hospital recently acquired a new cobalt machine for advanced cancer treatments.
