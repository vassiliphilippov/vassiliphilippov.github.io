Richmond

proper noun
capital city of Virginia, USA
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃ.mənd / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃ.mənd / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Virginia, located in the east-central region of the United States, and formerly the capital of the Confederacy during the American Civil War

Examples

Richmond city
Many government offices are located in Richmond, the capital of Virginia.
