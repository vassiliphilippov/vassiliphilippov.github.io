hang gliding

noun
flying sport using a hang glider
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of flying in a light, non-motorized aircraft called a hang glider, typically launched from a hill or cliff

Examples

Hang gliding sport
He took up hang gliding and quickly became an expert at soaring above the coastal cliffs.
Many thrill-seekers travel to the region solely for its excellent hang gliding opportunities.
The weather was perfect for hang gliding over the mountains this weekend.

Related Terms
