hydrostatic head

noun
pressure in liquid shown as vertical column height
3.6 6.7
Pronunciation: /haɪ.drə.ˈstæt.ɪk hed/ /; / /haɪ.droʊ.ˈstæt.ɪk hed/ (AmE), /haɪ.drə.ˈstæt.ɪk hed/ /; / /haɪ.drəʊ.ˈstæt.ɪk hed/ (BrE)
the pressure at a specific point in a liquid, expressed as the vertical height of a column of the liquid required to produce that pressure
