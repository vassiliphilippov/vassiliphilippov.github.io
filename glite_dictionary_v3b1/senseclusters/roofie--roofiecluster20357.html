roofie

verb
to drug secretly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːfi / (AmE), / ˈruːfi / (BrE)
to secretly give someone a sedative or incapacitating drug, especially by adding it to their drink without their knowledge

Examples

To roofie someone
The suspect tried to roofie his victim by slipping something into her drink.
She warned her friends to watch their drinks so no one could roofie them at the party.
Authorities are investigating reports that someone attempted to roofie guests at the club.
