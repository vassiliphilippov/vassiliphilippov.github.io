silencer

noun
device reducing exhaust noise
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪlən sər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪlənsə / (BrE)
a tubular device fitted to an exhaust system, such as on a car or motorcycle, to reduce noise produced by the engine

Examples

Car silencer
Cars in the city are required to have a functioning silencer to meet noise regulations.
The silencer on his motorcycle was damaged, making it unusually loud.
The mechanic replaced the old silencer to make the car run more quietly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muffler - device for reducing the noise emitted by the exhaust system of an internal combustion engine
