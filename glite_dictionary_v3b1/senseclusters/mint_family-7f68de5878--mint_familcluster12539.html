mint family

noun
botanical family of aromatic herbs (Lamiaceae)
5.6 3.3
Pronunciation: / mɪnt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / mɪnt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of aromatic herbs and shrubs, including mint, thyme, sage, and rosemary, with flowers resembling lips and four one-seeded nutlets
