cunning

adjective
clever and deceitful
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
clever at achieving things, often by tricking or deceiving people; sly or crafty

Examples

Cunning trick
