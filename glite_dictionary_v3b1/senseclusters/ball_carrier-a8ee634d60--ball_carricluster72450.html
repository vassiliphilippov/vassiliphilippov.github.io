ball carrier

noun
player carrying the ball in football/rugby

Pronunciation: /bɔːl ˈkæ.rɪ.ər/ /; / /bɑːl ˈkæ.rɪ.ər/ (AmE), /bɔːl ˈkæ.rɪ.ə/ (BrE)
