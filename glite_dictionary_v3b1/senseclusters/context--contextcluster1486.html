The circumstances, facts, or conditions that surround and help to explain a situation, event, or statement.

Examples

Political context
It is important to understand the political context before making decisions.
The meaning of a word often changes depending on its context.
Without proper context, her comments might be misunderstood.

Synonyms

    background provides earlier information or circumstances that explain a topic, while context is the wider situation that surrounds and clarifies it.
  • setting
    3.2 65/m
    setting names the place and time where something happens, whereas context covers all facts and conditions that explain it.
