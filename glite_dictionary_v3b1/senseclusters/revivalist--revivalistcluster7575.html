noun

(in non-religious contexts) a person who promotes, revives, or restores past cultural styles, trends, or traditions.

Examples

A revivalist trend
The fashion editor is a revivalist, bringing back 1970s silhouettes on the seasonal runway shows.
As a revivalist, he restores traditional pottery techniques and teaches them at community workshops every summer.
Local revivalist groups organized a festival celebrating folk music, dance, and forgotten culinary recipes.
