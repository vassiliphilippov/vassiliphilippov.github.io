right atrium

noun
right upper heart chamber
3.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /raɪt ˈeɪ.tri.əm/ /; / /raɪt ˈeɪ.triː.əm/ (AmE), /raɪt ˈeɪ.tri.əm/ /; / /raɪt ˈeɪ.triː.əm/ (BrE)
the right upper chamber of the heart that receives deoxygenated blood from the body

Examples
