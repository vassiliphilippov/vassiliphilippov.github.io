strong drink

noun
distilled alcoholic beverage
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ drɪŋk / (AmE), / strɒŋ drɪŋk / (BrE)
An alcoholic beverage that has been distilled, such as whisky, gin, or vodka, rather than just fermented.

Examples

A glass of strong drink
In some cultures, serving strong drink at celebrations is a tradition.
She avoided strong drink after realizing its effects on her health.
The bar offers a range of strong drink, from vodka to whiskey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beer - alcoholic drink made by fermentation, not distilled
  • synonym: liquor - alcoholic drink that is distilled
  • synonym: spirit - a distilled alcoholic drink
