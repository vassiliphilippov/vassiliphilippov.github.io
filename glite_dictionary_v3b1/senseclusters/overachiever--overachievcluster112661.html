overachiever

noun
person who exceeds expected achievement
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərəˈtʃivər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈtʃiːvə / (BrE)
a person who does better or achieves more than expected, especially academically or professionally

Examples

An overachiever student
Despite her modest background, she quickly earned a reputation as an overachiever at university.
Some teachers worry that an overachiever might burn out from constant pressure to excel.
The company is looking to hire overachievers who consistently go beyond their goals.
