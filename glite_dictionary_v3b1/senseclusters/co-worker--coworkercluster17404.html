co-worker

noun
a person one works with
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
A person with whom one works, typically someone at the same level or in a similar position within an organization.

Examples

A co-worker at office
Her co-worker helped her resolve a tricky problem at the office.
I discussed the project with my co-worker before presenting it to the team.
Most co-workers try to support each other during busy times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colleague - a person with whom one works in a profession or business
