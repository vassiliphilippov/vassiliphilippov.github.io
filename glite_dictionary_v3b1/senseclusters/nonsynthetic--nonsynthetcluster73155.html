nonsynthetic

adjective
natural, not produced by chemical synthesis
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.sɪnˈθɛt̬.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.sɪnˈθɛt.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance or product) involving or derived from living organisms, and not produced by chemical synthesis; free from artificial chemical treatments or additives.

Examples

A nonsynthetic product
All the makeup products in this line are completely nonsynthetic.
Organic farmers prefer to use nonsynthetic fertilizers to enrich their soil.
These cheeses are made using nonsynthetic ingredients and traditional methods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: synthetic - produced by chemical synthesis, not natural
