power walking

noun
brisk walking exercise
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌwɑ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌwɔː.kɪŋ / (BrE)
A form of brisk walking performed as exercise, involving rapid movement and vigorous swinging of the arms.

Examples

Power walking exercise
Doctors recommend power walking as an effective way to improve cardiovascular fitness without undue strain.
He joined a morning power walking group to stay motivated and healthy.
She prefers power walking in the park over jogging because it is easier on her knees.
