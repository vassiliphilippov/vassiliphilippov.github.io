constitutional

adjective
relating to a constitution or constitutional law
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.stəˈtuː.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
authorized or in accordance with the constitution of a country, especially relating to laws or rights based on fundamental political principles

Examples

Constitutional rights
Freedom of speech is a constitutional guarantee in many countries.
The Supreme Court upheld the constitutional right to protest peacefully.
They argued that the new law was not constitutional and should be repealed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legal - relating to the law
  • antonym: unconstitutional - not allowed by the constitution
