installment plan

noun
payment system by regular installments
7.1 2.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈstɔːl.mənt plæn/ /; / /ɪn.ˈstɑːl.mənt plæn/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈstɔːl.mənt plæn/ (BrE)
A system of paying for goods or services by making several smaller payments over a period of time rather than one full payment at once.

Examples

An installment plan option
The store offers an installment plan for purchases over $500.
Using an installment plan, he was able to afford the expensive appliance.
We bought our new sofa on an installment plan so we could pay over twelve months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layaway - a system of paying for goods with installments before taking possession
