unbuilt

adjective
undeveloped land
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪlt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪlt / (BrE)
not containing buildings or structures; left undeveloped or in a natural state

Examples

Unbuilt land
The city plans to preserve the unbuilt areas along the river for public parks.
Much of the countryside remains unbuilt, offering beautiful views of nature.
Developers are interested in purchasing the last unbuilt plots in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: built-up - covered with buildings or structures
  • synonym: undeveloped - not built on or improved; in a natural state
