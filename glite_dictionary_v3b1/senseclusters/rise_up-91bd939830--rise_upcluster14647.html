rise up

verb
(of animals) stand on hind legs
Pronunciation: / raɪz ʌp / (AmE), / raɪz ʌp / (BrE)
(of a four-legged animal) to stand up on its hind legs, often suddenly or as a reaction to fear or excitement.

Examples

The horse rises up
If you wave a flag, the pony might rise up and rear back.
Suddenly, the circus elephant tried to rise up on its hind legs to reach the fruit.
The frightened stallion began to rise up as the thunder echoed.
