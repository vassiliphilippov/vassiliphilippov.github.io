atenolol

noun
beta blocker medication
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛnəˌlɑl / (AmE), / əˈtɛnəlɒl / (BrE)
A medication in the class of beta blockers, taken orally to treat conditions such as high blood pressure and angina.

Examples

The doctor prescribed atenolol to control her high blood pressure.
