lifeblood

noun
blood as the source of life
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌblʌd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌblʌd / (BrE)
the blood regarded as the source or seat of life and vitality in a living being

Examples

Losing lifeblood
Her injury was severe, and her lifeblood was draining away with every passing moment.
Many ancient cultures believed that lifeblood held a person's spirit and strength.
The vampire legend revolves around the consumption of human lifeblood.
