cafetorium

noun
school dining and event hall
Pronunciation: / ˌkæfəˈtɔːriəm / (AmE), / ˌkæfəˈtɔːriəm / (BrE)
a large room in a school that serves as both a cafeteria and an auditorium

Examples

School cafetorium
The school uses the cafetorium for both lunch and assemblies throughout the year.
Parents gathered in the cafetorium to watch the student talent show after dinner.
During rainy days, students eat and play games in the cafetorium instead of outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auditorium - a large room or hall for public gatherings or performances
  • synonym: cafeteria - a place where people eat meals, especially in a school or workplace
