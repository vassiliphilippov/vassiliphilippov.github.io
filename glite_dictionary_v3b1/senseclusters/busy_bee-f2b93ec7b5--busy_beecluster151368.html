busy bee

noun
an active and hardworking person
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪzi bi / (AmE), / ˈbɪzi biː / (BrE)
a person who is always busy, active, and works hard

Examples

A busy bee worker
Ever since she started her new job, she's been a real busy bee around the office.
My grandmother is a busy bee, always finding something to do even on weekends.
The volunteers were busy bees as they prepared meals for the charity event.
As a busy bee, he rarely takes breaks during the day.
Lisa is a busy bee, always moving from one task to another.
My grandmother has been a busy bee since she retired, joining several clubs.

  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works long and hard hours
