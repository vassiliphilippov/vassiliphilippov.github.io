culpable

adjective
deserving blame or censure
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌl.pə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.pə.bəl / (BrE)
Deserving blame or responsibility for a wrongdoing or fault.

Examples

Culpable negligence
Due to his culpable negligence, the project failed to meet its deadline.
She admitted her actions were culpable and apologized to the team.
The company was found culpable for the environmental disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blameworthy - deserving blame; worthy of blame
  • antonym: innocent - not deserving blame; free from guilt
