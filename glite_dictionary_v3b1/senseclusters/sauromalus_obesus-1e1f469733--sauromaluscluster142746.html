Sauromalus obesus

noun
large herbivorous lizard of SW US and Mexico
Pronunciation: / sɔːˈrɒmələs oʊˈbiːsəs / (AmE), / sɔːˈrɒmæləs əʊˈbiːsəs / (BrE)
A large, herbivorous lizard native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and Mexico, commonly known as the common chuckwalla.

A Sauromalus obesus can grow quite large, with some adults reaching up to 76 centimeters in length.

The Sauromalus obesus is well adapted to the rocky deserts of Arizona and California.
