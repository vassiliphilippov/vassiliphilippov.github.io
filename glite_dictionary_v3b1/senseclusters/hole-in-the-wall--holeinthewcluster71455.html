hole-in-the-wall

noun
small, inconspicuous place; modest shop or restaurant
3.7 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊl.ɪn.ðə.wɔːl/ /; / /ˈhoʊl.ən.ðə.wɔːl/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊl.ɪn.ðə.wɔːl/ /; / /ˈhəʊl.ən.ðə.wɔːl/ (BrE)
a small, modest, and often hidden place (especially a restaurant, cafe, or store) that may not look special but is known for its quality or uniqueness
