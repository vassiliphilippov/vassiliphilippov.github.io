presence

noun
impression that something is present
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzəns / (AmE), / ˈprɛzəns / (BrE)
the feeling or impression that someone or something, especially something invisible, is present in a place

Examples

A strange presence
A strange presence could be felt throughout the silent forest.
Many believe the old house is haunted by some mysterious presence.
She sensed a cold presence in the empty room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absence - state of being absent or not present
