chiliastic

adjective
relating to the millennium or millenarian beliefs
Pronunciation: / kɪliˈæstɪk / (AmE), / kɪlɪˈastɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by belief in a coming thousand-year period of peace and happiness, especially in Christian theology; millenarian.

Examples

Chiliastic beliefs
During the 19th century, many religious movements developed chiliastic expectations for society.
His writings are full of chiliastic themes inspired by early Christian doctrine.
The group espoused a chiliastic vision, expecting the imminent arrival of a thousand-year era of peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millenarian - believing in or relating to a coming millennium of peace
