autonomist

adjective
favoring self-government
Pronunciation: / ɔˈtɑːnəmɪst / (AmE), / ɔˈtɒnəmɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to autonomy or policies that promote self-government or self-management for groups or regions

Examples

Autonomist movement
The new law was seen as an autonomist step toward greater regional independence.
Many autonomist movements have emerged in response to centralized government policies.
The party's autonomist stance appealed to voters seeking more local control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomous - having the right or power to govern oneself
  • antonym: centralist - favoring central control rather than local self-government
