Holland

proper noun
region in the Netherlands; sometimes used for the whole country
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːlənd / (AmE), / ˈhɒlənd / (BrE)
A region in the western part of the Netherlands, sometimes used informally to refer to the entire country.

Examples

Holland region
He grew up in Holland, near the city of Amsterdam.
Many tourists mistakenly refer to the whole country as Holland instead of the Netherlands.
The tulip fields in Holland attract visitors from all over the world each spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: the Netherlands - the country of which Holland is a region
