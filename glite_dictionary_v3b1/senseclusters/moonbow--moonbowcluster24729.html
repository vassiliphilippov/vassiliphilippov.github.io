moonbow

noun
rainbow from moonlight
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnbəʊ / (BrE)
a rainbow produced by moonlight rather than sunlight

Examples

A moonbow after rain
We stayed up late hoping to catch a glimpse of a moonbow after the evening rain.
A rare moonbow appeared over the waterfall, glowing softly in the night sky.
Photographers traveled to the mountains to capture the beauty of a moonbow on a clear night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rainbow - an arc of colors formed in the sky by sunlight and water droplets
