overblouse

noun
protective outer garment
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌblaʊs / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌblaʊs / (BrE)
a loose blouse worn over other clothes, either for protection or as a fashion layer

Examples

A overblouse layer
She wore a light overblouse to keep her shirt clean while painting.
The overblouse added a stylish layer to her summer outfit.
Before cooking, he put on an overblouse to protect his clothes from stains.
She chose a floral overblouse to wear with her white skirt for the party.
The lightweight overblouse was perfect for the warm spring afternoon.
Many office workers prefer an overblouse for a comfortable yet professional look.

  • synonym: tunic - a loose garment, typically reaching to the hips or knees
