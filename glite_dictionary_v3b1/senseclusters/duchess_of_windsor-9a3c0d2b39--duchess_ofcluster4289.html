Duchess of Windsor

proper noun
title of Wallis Simpson, wife of Edward VIII
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌtʃ.əs əv ˈwɪn.zər / (AmE), / ˈdʌtʃ.ɪs əv ˈwɪn.zə / (BrE)
(1896–1986) The title held by Wallis Simpson, an American divorcee whose marriage to Edward VIII in 1937 prompted his abdication from the British throne.
