transcontinentally

adverb
across continents
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌtrænskɒntɪˈnɛntəli / (BrE)
in a way that crosses or spans an entire continent or continents

Examples

To fly transcontinentally
The airline operates flights transcontinentally between Europe and Asia every day.
Goods are shipped transcontinentally to reach customers on different continents.
He dreamed of traveling transcontinentally by train from one coast to the other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercontinentally - between or among continents
