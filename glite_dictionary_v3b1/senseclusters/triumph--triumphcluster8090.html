triumph

noun
a great victory or success
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪ.əmf /; / ˈtraɪəmf / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.əmf / (BrE)
a great victory or achievement, or the feeling of great joy or satisfaction that comes from it

Examples

A triumph over fear
Her research represented a personal triumph over adversity.
The new bridge is hailed as an engineering triumph across the region.
Winning the championship was a remarkable triumph for the inexperienced team.
Her smile radiated triumph as she accepted the award on stage.
The army marched through the city in a spirit of triumph after the battle.
Winning the championship was a moment of pure triumph for the whole team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defeat - the act of losing or being overcome in a contest or struggle
  • synonym: victory - an act of defeating an enemy or opponent in a battle, game, or other competition
