ruff

noun
dog's short bark
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
a short, sharp sound made by a dog, similar to a bark

Examples

A puppy's ruff
The puppy let out a playful ruff when it saw its owner.
We heard a sudden ruff from the backyard as the mail carrier approached.
Every time the doorbell rings, the dog gives a quick ruff to alert us.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bark - the loud, sharp sound made by a dog
  • degree lower: growl - a low, threatening sound made by a dog
