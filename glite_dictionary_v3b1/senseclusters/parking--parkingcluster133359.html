parking

noun
registering a domain to display ads
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːr.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in computing and internet business) the practice of registering a domain name and placing a temporary webpage or landing page (often with advertisements) to monetize or reserve the name.

Examples

Parking a domain
The startup earns revenue by parking premium domain names on ad-supported landing pages.
After buying several domains, he tried parking them while he developed his business plan.
Domain parking can generate small income from clicks before a site is ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domain monetization - methods of earning revenue from domain names, such as ads, affiliate links, or resale
  • synonym: domain parking - the practice of hosting a placeholder page for a registered domain, usually showing ads
