woolies

noun
warm woolen clothes
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊliz / (AmE), / ˈwʊliz / (BrE)
informal term for warm clothes made of wool, such as sweaters or socks

Examples

Thick woolies in winter
Before heading out into the cold, she made sure to wear her thick woolies.
He packed his woolies for the winter trip to the mountains.
When the temperature dropped, everyone reached for their favorite woolies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumper - a knitted garment worn on the upper body, especially in British English
  • synonym: sweater - a warm knitted piece of clothing worn on the upper body
