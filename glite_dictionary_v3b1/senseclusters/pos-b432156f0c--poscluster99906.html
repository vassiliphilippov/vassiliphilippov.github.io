POS

noun
grammatical category
Pronunciation: / pi oʊ ɛs / (AmE), / piː əʊ ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'part of speech', a grammatical category such as noun, verb, or adjective

Examples

POS tag in grammar
The teacher asked us to identify the POS of each word in the sentence.
In linguistics, understanding the POS helps analyze sentence structure.
You can use a dictionary to check the POS of unfamiliar words.
