Lycopsida

noun
class of vascular plants (club mosses)
Pronunciation: / laɪˈkɑpsɪdə /; / laɪˈkɑːpsɪdə / (AmE), / laɪˈkɒpsɪdə /; / lɪˈkɒpsɪdə / (BrE)
a class of vascular plants that includes club mosses and their relatives, such as Lycopodiales, Isoetales, Selaginellales, and extinct Lepidodendrales
