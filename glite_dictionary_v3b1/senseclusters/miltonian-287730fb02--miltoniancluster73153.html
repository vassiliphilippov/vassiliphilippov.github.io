Miltonian

noun
John Milton admirer
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪlˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / mɪlˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
a person who admires, studies, or follows the works or ideas of the poet John Milton

Examples

A true Miltonian
As a dedicated Miltonian, he could recite passages from Paradise Lost by heart.
The university's English department is known for its group of passionate Miltonians.
She became a Miltonian after reading several of his epic poems in college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Miltonic - relating to or characteristic of John Milton or his works
  • synonym: Shakespearean - a person who admires or studies the works of William Shakespeare
