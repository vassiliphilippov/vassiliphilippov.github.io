comet-like

adjective
resembling a comet
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmətˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance or characteristics of a comet, especially with a bright head and a tail.

Examples

A comet-like tail
A comet-like object appeared in the telescope's field of view.
She wore a shimmering dress with a comet-like train that sparkled as she walked.
The fireworks left a comet-like trail of light in the night sky.
