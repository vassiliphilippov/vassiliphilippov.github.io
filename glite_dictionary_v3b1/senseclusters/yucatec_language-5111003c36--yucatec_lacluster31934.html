Yucatec language

noun
Mayan language of the Yucatán Peninsula
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːkəˈtɛk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌjuːkəˈtɛk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Mayan language spoken primarily by the Yucatec people of the Yucatán Peninsula in Mexico and parts of Belize and Guatemala.

Examples

The Yucatec language spoken
Several ancient manuscripts were written in the Yucatec language.
She decided to study the Yucatec language to help preserve indigenous culture.
The Yucatec language is still widely spoken in rural areas of the Yucatán Peninsula.
