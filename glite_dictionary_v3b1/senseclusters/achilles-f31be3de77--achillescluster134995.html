Achilles

proper noun
person with a weakness
7.3
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪliz / (AmE), / əˈkɪliːz / (BrE)
a person who is strong or talented but has a particular weakness or vulnerability.

Examples

A hidden Achilles
Despite his many talents, his impatience was his Achilles.
She was a brilliant leader, but her pride proved to be her Achilles.
For all his strength, his trust in others was his Achilles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Achilles' heel - a particular weakness in someone or something otherwise strong
