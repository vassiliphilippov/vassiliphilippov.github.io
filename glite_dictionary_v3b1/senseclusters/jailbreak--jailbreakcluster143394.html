jailbreak

verb
to remove device restrictions
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪlˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪlˌbreɪk / (BrE)
to remove software restrictions from a device, especially a smartphone, to allow unauthorized changes or apps

Examples

To jailbreak a phone
Many users choose to jailbreak their phones to install apps not available in the official store.
After he jailbroke his tablet, he was able to customize its appearance completely.
It is important to understand the risks before you jailbreak your device.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lock - to restrict access or changes to a device's software
  • synonym: root - to gain privileged control over a device's operating system
