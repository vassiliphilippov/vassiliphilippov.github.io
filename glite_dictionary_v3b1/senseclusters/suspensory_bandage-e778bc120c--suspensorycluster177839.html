suspensory bandage

noun
bandage to support a body part
4.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈspen.sər.iː ˈbæn.dɪdʒ/ (AmE), /sə.ˈspen.sə.riː ˈbæn.dɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a bandage, usually made of elastic fabric, designed to support and hold up a dependent body part, such as the scrotum or a pendulous breast.

Examples

A suspensory bandage supports
After surgery, the doctor applied a suspensory bandage to support the patient's scrotum during recovery.
Proper placement of the suspensory bandage is essential to prevent swelling in the affected area.
The nurse recommended wearing a suspensory bandage for extra comfort after the breast operation.
