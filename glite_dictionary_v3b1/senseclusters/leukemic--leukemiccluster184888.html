leukemic

adjective
relating to leukemia
Pronunciation: / luˈkimɪk / (AmE), / luːˈkiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by leukemia, a cancer of the blood or bone marrow

Examples

Leukemic cells
The patient was diagnosed as leukemic after several blood tests confirmed the presence of abnormal cells.
Doctors are developing new treatments for leukemic children to improve survival rates.
Researchers studied the effects of the drug on leukemic cells in the laboratory.
