dominos

noun
tile-based matching games
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊ.məˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈdɒm.ɪ.nəʊz / (BrE)
A family of games played with small rectangular tiles, each divided into two squares with a number of spots, where players match the tiles according to the number of spots.

Examples

A set of dominos
A set of dominos can provide hours of entertainment for all ages.
Every Sunday evening, the family gathers to play dominos around the kitchen table.
He explained the rules of dominos to the children before the game began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominoes - games played with rectangular numbered tiles
