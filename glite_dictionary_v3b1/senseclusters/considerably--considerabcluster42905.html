considerably

adverb
to a great extent; much
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪdɚəbli / (AmE), / kənˈsɪdərəbli / (BrE)
To a great extent or degree; much; significantly.

Examples

Improved considerably
Her health has improved considerably since she started exercising regularly.
Painting the walls a lighter color made the room appear considerably larger.
The cost of living has increased considerably over the past ten years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: significantly - in a sufficiently great or important way
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree or extent
  • synonym: substantially - to a great or significant degree
