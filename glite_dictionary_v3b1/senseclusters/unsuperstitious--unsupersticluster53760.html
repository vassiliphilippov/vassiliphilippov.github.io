unsuperstitious

adjective
not superstitious
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsuːpərˈstɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsuːpəˈstɪʃəs / (BrE)
not believing in or influenced by superstitions or irrational beliefs.

Examples

An unsuperstitious person
Despite growing up in a traditional family, she remained unsuperstitious throughout her life.
His unsuperstitious attitude made him question every old myth he heard.
Many scientists are unsuperstitious and rely only on evidence and facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rational - based on reason or logic
  • antonym: superstitious - believing in or influenced by superstitions
