greenbacker

noun
supporter of paper money
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.bæk.ər/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.bæk.ə/ (BrE)
a person, especially in 19th-century US history, who supported the use of paper money not backed by gold
