statuesque

adjective
tall and dignified like a statue
Pronunciation: / ˌstætʃuˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌstatjuˈɛsk /; / ˌstatjʊˈɛsk / (BrE)
(especially of a woman) attractively tall, graceful, and dignified, in a way that is reminiscent of a statue
