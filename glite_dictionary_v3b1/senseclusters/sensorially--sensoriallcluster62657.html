sensorially

adverb
relating to the senses
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈsɔːriəli / (AmE), / sɛnˈsɔːriəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or involves the senses or sensory experience

Examples

To be sensorially engaging
The artist designed the installation to be sensorially engaging for visitors of all ages.
The spa offers treatments that are sensorially relaxing, using soothing sounds and gentle lighting.
Children learn best in classrooms that are sensorially stimulating, with colors, textures, and sounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensually - in a way that relates to physical pleasure or the senses
