hotdogging

noun
showy sports stunts
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌdɔɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌdɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of performing daring or showy stunts, especially to attract attention in sports

Examples

Extreme sports hotdogging
The coach discouraged hotdogging because it can be dangerous for inexperienced athletes.
Fans cheered as the snowboarder was hotdogging with flips and spins on the halfpipe.
Some players think hotdogging is fun, but others see it as disrespectful to the sport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandstanding - performing in a way meant to impress others
  • antonym: modesty - the quality of not drawing attention to oneself
  • synonym: showboating - the act of showing off to attract attention
