go from zero to hero

verb
achieve major success from nothing
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ frəm ˈzɪəɹoʊ tə ˈhiɹoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ frəm ˈzɪərəʊ tə ˈhɪərəʊ / (BrE)
to make a dramatic transformation from being completely unsuccessful or unknown to achieving great success or recognition

Examples

To go from zero to hero
After months of hard work, she managed to go from zero to hero in her new company.
The young athlete went from zero to hero after scoring the winning goal in the final.
With proper training, anyone can go from zero to hero in coding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fall from grace - to descend from a position of success to failure or disgrace
  • synonym: rise to the top - to achieve a high level of success or position after starting at a low point
