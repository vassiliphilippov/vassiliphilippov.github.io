rendezvous

noun
meeting, often romantic
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːn.deɪˌvuː / (AmE), / ˈrɒn.deɪˌvuː / (BrE)
a meeting arranged between people, often at a specific time and place (sometimes romantic or secret)

Their secret rendezvous was discovered by the authorities.
