presenter

noun
show or event host
2.7
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɛntər / (AmE), / prɪˈzɛntə / (BrE)
a person who hosts or introduces a television, radio, or live program, or explains something to an audience

Examples

TV presenter
The presenter introduced the guest speakers at the start of the ceremony.
Each presenter brought a unique style to the morning news program.
The awards were handed out by a well-known television presenter.
The presenter introduced the next segment with a smile and clear voice.
As a presenter, she always keeps the audience engaged during the show.
The science program's presenter explained the experiment in simple terms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anchor - a main news presenter on television or radio
  • synonym: announcer - a person who introduces programs or gives information on radio or television
  • antonym: guest - a person invited to appear on a program or event
  • synonym: host - a person who introduces and leads a program or event
