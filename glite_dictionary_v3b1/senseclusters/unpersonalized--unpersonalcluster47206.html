unpersonalized

adjective
impersonal; formal
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpɜːrsənəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnpɜːsənəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not showing personal feeling, warmth, or individual attention; impersonal or formal in tone or style

Examples

Unpersonalized reply
The company sent an unpersonalized email to all customers, which felt cold and distant.
Her speech was so unpersonalized that it failed to connect with the audience.
Many people dislike receiving unpersonalized holiday cards that lack a handwritten note.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impersonal - not influenced by, showing, or involving personal feelings
  • antonym: personalized - made or done for a particular person; showing individual attention
