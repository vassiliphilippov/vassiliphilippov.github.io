reconcentrate

verb
to focus again
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkənˈsɛntreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːkənˈsɛntreɪt / (BrE)
to concentrate again or bring something back into a concentrated state

Examples

To reconcentrate on work
After the phone call, he needed a moment to reconcentrate on his studies.
The team took a short break to reconcentrate before the final round.
She closed her eyes and tried to reconcentrate after being interrupted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concentrate - to focus one's attention or effort
  • antonym: distract - to draw attention away from something
  • synonym: refocus - to direct attention or effort again
