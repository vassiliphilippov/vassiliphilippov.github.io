overnight

verb
to stay or send overnight
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.naɪt/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.naɪt/ (BrE)
to stay somewhere for the night or to send something so that it arrives the next day

Examples

To overnight a package
We overnighted the documents to the client so they would arrive by morning.
She overnighted at her friend's house after the late party.
The company overnights important packages to ensure fast delivery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispatch - to send something to a destination
  • synonym: stay - to remain in a place for a period of time
