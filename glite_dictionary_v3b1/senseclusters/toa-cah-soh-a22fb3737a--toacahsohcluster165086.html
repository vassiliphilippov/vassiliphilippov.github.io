TOA-CAH-SOH

noun
mnemonic for trigonometric ratios
Pronunciation: / ˌtiː oʊ eɪ ˌkɑː soʊ / (AmE), / ˌtiː əʊ eɪ ˌkɑː səʊ / (BrE)
a mnemonic phrase used to remember the definitions of the sine, cosine, and tangent functions in trigonometry: Tangent = Opposite/Adjacent, Cosine = Adjacent/Hypotenuse, Sine = Opposite/Hypotenuse
