bee

noun
hairy-bodied nectar-collecting insect
1.0 21/m
Pronunciation: / bi / (AmE), / biÀê / (BrE)
A flying insect with a hairy body, known for collecting nectar and pollen, and often living in colonies; includes both social and solitary species.

Examples

A bee hive
A bee landed on the flower and began collecting pollen.
Many people are afraid of being stung by a bee during the summer.
The honey produced by a bee is stored in hexagonal wax cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ant - a social insect living in organized colonies
  • synonym: wasp - a slender-bodied, often stinging insect related to bees and ants
