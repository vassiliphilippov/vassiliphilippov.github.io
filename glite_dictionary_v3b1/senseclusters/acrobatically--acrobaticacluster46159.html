acrobatically

adverb
in a skillful way
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌækrəˈbætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌækrəˈbætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows skill, agility, or cleverness, like an acrobat (can be literal or figurative)

Examples

To move acrobatically
The gymnast landed acrobatically after performing a difficult routine.
He acrobatically dodged the obstacles in the crowded hallway.
The politician acrobatically avoided answering the difficult question during the interview.
The gymnast flipped acrobatically through the air during her routine.
He leaped acrobatically over the obstacle, amazing the crowd.
The cat jumped acrobatically from the windowsill to the bookshelf.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsily - in an awkward or unskillful way
  • synonym: deftly - in a skillful and quick way
  • synonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
