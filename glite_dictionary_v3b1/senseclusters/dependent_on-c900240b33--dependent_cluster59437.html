Synonyms

    contingent on expresses that something depends on certain conditions and often suggests possibility, while dependent on states direct reliance.
  • dependent upon
    3.4 4.61/m

  • contingent upon
    6.4 0.92/m
    contingent upon sounds formal and often appears in reports, while dependent on describes general reliance in everyday contexts.
  • dependent
    2.8 16/m
    dependent describes a state or person without the preposition, while dependent on shows what something relies on.
  • contingent
    6.8 3.23/m

  • hinged
    4.3 0.00/m
    hinged often appears in informal speech to show a key point or reason, while dependent on is neutral and literal.
