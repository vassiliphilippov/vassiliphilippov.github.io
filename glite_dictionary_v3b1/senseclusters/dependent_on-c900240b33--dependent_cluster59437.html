dependent on

adjective
relying on conditions or circumstances
1.9 2.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpen.dənt ən/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpen.dənt ən/ (BrE)
determined or decided by the existence or occurrence of something else; relying on certain conditions or factors

Examples

Dependent on government approval
Funding for the project is dependent on government approval.
Her success at the competition was dependent on months of hard work and training.
The outcome of the match is dependent on the weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contingent on - subject to the fulfillment of certain conditions
  • antonym: independent of - not influenced or determined by anything else
