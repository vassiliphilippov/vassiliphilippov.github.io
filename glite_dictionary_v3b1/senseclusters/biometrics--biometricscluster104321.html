biometrics

noun
identification using physical or behavioral traits
4.1
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.ˈme.trɪks/ /; / /baɪ.ə.ˈme.trɪks/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.ˈme.trɪks/ /; / /baɪ.ə.ˈme.trɪks/ (BrE)
The measurement and use of people's physical or behavioral characteristics (such as fingerprints, face, or voice) to identify or verify them, especially for security and authentication.

Examples

Biometrics security check
Many smartphones now use biometrics for unlocking and authorizing mobile payments quickly.
The airport upgraded its security systems to include facial recognition and other biometrics checks.
Banks increasingly combine passwords with biometrics to reduce fraud during online transactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biometric authentication - The process of verifying a person's identity using biological traits such as fingerprints or facial patterns.
  • synonym: identity verification - The process of checking that someone is who they claim to be, often using documents or biometric data.
