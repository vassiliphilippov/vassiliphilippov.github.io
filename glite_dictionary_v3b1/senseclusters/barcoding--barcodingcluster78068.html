barcoding

noun
labeling with codes
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkəʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of labeling items with machine-readable codes to identify and track them

Examples

Product barcoding
Most supermarkets rely on barcoding to speed up the checkout process and reduce errors.
The introduction of barcoding has made inventory management much more efficient for warehouses.
With barcoding, each product can be tracked from the factory to the store shelf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: labeling - the act of attaching labels to items for identification
  • synonym: scanning - the act of reading barcodes or codes with a machine
