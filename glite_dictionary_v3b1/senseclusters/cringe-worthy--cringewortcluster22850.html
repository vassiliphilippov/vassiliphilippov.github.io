cringe-worthy

adjective
causing embarrassment or shame
Pronunciation: / krɪndʒˌwɝːði / (AmE), / krɪndʒˌwɜːði / (BrE)
Causing embarrassment or shame; so awkward, embarrassing, or uncomfortable as to provoke a cringe.

Examples

Cringe-worthy moment
He gave a cringe-worthy speech full of outdated jokes that made the audience uncomfortable.
Her attempt at singing in public turned out to be a cringe-worthy experience for everyone.
The movie's dialogue was so cringe-worthy that I had to look away.
