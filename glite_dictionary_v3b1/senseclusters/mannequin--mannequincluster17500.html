mannequin

noun
life-size model for displaying clothes
2.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmænəˌkɪn / (AmE), / ˈmænɪkɪn / (BrE)
a life-size model of a human figure, used by tailors, dressmakers, and in shop displays to show clothing and accessories

Examples

Store mannequin
He adjusted the jacket on the mannequin before the grand opening.
Several mannequins were arranged to model the store's spring collection.
The new dress looked stunning on the mannequin in the shop window.
