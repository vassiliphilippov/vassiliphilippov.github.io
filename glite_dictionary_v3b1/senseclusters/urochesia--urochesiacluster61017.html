urochesia

noun
urine passage from the anus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊroʊˈkiːziə / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈkiːziə / (BrE)
the passage of urine from the anus, usually as a result of an abnormal connection between the urinary tract and the rectum or anus

Examples

Urochesia can indicate the presence of a fistula between the urinary tract and the rectum.
