greenish-brown

adjective
brown tinged with green (color)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnɪʃˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged with green; having a hue between brown and green.

Examples

A greenish-brown color
She wore a scarf of greenish-brown wool on the chilly autumn day.
The frog’s greenish-brown coloring helped it blend into the marsh.
The walls were painted a greenish-brown that gave the room an earthy feel.
