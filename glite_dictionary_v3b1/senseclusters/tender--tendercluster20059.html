tender

adjective
of a boat, easily heels over
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə / (BrE)
(of a boat) inclined to heel over easily when under sail, especially due to insufficient stability.

Examples

Tender boat at sea
After shifting the ballast, the vessel became less tender and more stable at sea.
Due to its tender nature, the boat is not recommended for inexperienced sailors.
The racing yacht was surprisingly tender and required careful handling in strong winds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stable - (of a boat) unlikely to heel over under sail; steady
