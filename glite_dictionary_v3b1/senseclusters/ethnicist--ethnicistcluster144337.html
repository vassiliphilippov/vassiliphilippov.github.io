ethnicist

adjective
favoring an ethnic group
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθnɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈɛθnɪsɪst / (BrE)
showing preference for or discrimination against people based on their ethnic group

Examples

Ethnicist policies
The new law was criticized as ethnicist because it gave advantages to one group.
Many activists spoke out against the ethnicist policies implemented by the administration.
An ethnicist attitude can lead to unfair treatment of minority communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - believing in equal rights and opportunities for all people
  • synonym: racist - showing prejudice or discrimination based on race
