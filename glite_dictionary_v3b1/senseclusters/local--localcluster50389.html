local

noun
resident of an area
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəl / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from a particular area or community

Examples

A local resident
The locals welcomed us warmly when we arrived in the village.
Many locals gather at the market every Saturday morning.
Tourists often ask locals for recommendations on where to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resident - a person who lives in a particular place
  • antonym: visitor - a person who is in a place temporarily
