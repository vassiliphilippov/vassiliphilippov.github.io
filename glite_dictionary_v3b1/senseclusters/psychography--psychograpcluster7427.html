psychography

noun
psychological profile
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a written description or profile of a person's psychological characteristics or personality traits

Examples

A psychography report
The psychologist created a detailed psychography to better understand her patient's behavior.
A thorough psychography can help employers assess a candidate's suitability for a job.
The novel included a fascinating psychography of its mysterious main character.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biography - a written account of someone's life
  • synonym: profile - a summary of a person's characteristics
