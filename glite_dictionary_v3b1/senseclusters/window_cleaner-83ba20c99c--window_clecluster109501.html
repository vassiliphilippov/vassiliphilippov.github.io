window cleaner

noun
person who cleans windows professionally
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˌklinər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌkliːnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean windows, especially of buildings or vehicles, for payment

Examples

A window cleaner works

She watched the window cleaner work fearlessly on the high-rise ladder.
The window cleaner arrived early to wash the office building's glass facade.
