equinovarus

noun
foot twisted inward
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwɪnoʊˈvɛərəs / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪnəʊˈvɑːrəs / (BrE)
a deformity of the foot in which it is twisted downward and inward

Examples

A case of equinovarus
The child was born with equinovarus and required corrective surgery.
Physical therapy can help improve mobility in patients with equinovarus.
Doctors identified equinovarus in the infant during a routine examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clubfoot - a congenital deformity in which the foot is twisted out of shape or position
  • antonym: equinovalgus - a deformity of the foot in which it is twisted downward and outward
