laniard

noun
cord and hook to fire cannon
Pronunciation: / ˈlænjərd / (AmE), / ˈlanjəd / (BrE)
A cord with a hook used to fire certain types of cannon.

Examples

Laniard for a cannon
A sudden jerk of the laniard sent the cannon roaring into action.
The gunner gripped the laniard and awaited the command to fire.
To avoid premature ignition, he handled the laniard with great caution.
