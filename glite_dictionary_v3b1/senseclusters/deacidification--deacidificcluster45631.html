deacidification

noun
removal of acid
8.1
Pronunciation: /diː.ə.sɪ.dɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.ə.sɪ.dɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.ə.sɪ.dɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.ə.sɪ.dɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

The deacidification of library archives is essential for preserving historical documents.
Specialists use deacidification techniques to extend the life of old newspapers.
Without deacidification, many valuable books would deteriorate much faster.
