Proboscidea

noun
order of mammals: elephants, mammoths
Pronunciation: / proʊˌbɑːsɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / prəˌbɒsɪˈdiːə / (BrE)
an order of large mammals that includes elephants and extinct relatives such as mammoths and mastodons, known for their long trunks
