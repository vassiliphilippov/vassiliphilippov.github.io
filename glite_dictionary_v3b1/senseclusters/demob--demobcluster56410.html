demob

verb
release from military service
9.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / diËˆmÉ‘b / (AmE), / diËˆmÉ’b / (BrE)
(British) To release (someone) from military service; to demobilize.

Examples

To demob soldiers
After the war, thousands of soldiers were demobbed within a few months.
His father was demobbed from the army in 1946 and returned home.
Many young men struggled to find work after being demobbed.

Synonyms

    7.5 0.49/m
