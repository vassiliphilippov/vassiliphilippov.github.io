talipes calcaneus

noun
type of clubfoot with upward-pointing toes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtæləˌpiːz kælˈkeɪniəs / (AmE), / ˈtælɪpiːz kælˈkɑːnɪəs / (BrE)
A form of clubfoot in which the toes point upward and the person walks on the heel of the foot.

Examples

A talipes calcaneus deformity
A pronounced talipes calcaneus can make walking difficult if not corrected early.
The newborn was diagnosed with talipes calcaneus and referred to an orthopedic specialist.
Treatment for talipes calcaneus often involves physical therapy and sometimes surgery.
