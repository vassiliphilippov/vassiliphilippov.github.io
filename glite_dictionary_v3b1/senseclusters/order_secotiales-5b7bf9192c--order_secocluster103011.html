order Secotiales

noun
an order of fungi in class Gasteromycetes
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər siˌkoʊtiˈeɪliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə siˌkəʊtiˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi in the class Gasteromycetes, consisting of certain secotioid or gastroid forms

Examples

Order Secotiales fungi
Many members of the order Secotiales are adapted to arid environments where wind dispersal is limited.
The order Secotiales is distinguished by its secotioid fruit bodies and diverse spore dispersal mechanisms.
The taxonomy of the order Secotiales has been revised with the advent of molecular phylogenetics.
