Sicilian pizza

noun
pizza with a thick, rectangular crust
3.2
Pronunciation: / sɪˈsɪliən ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / sɪˈsɪliən ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
A style of pizza with a thick, rectangular crust, typically baked in a pan and topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and other ingredients.

Examples

A slice of Sicilian pizza
Her favorite lunch is a slice of Sicilian pizza with plenty of cheese and tomato sauce.
The restaurant is famous for its authentic Sicilian pizza baked in a rectangular pan.
We ordered a large Sicilian pizza for the party because everyone loves the thick crust.
