batt

noun
battalion abbreviation
9.3
Pronunciation: / bæt / (AmE), / bæt / (BrE)
an abbreviation for battalion, a military unit consisting of several companies or batteries

Examples

Army batt
The 3rd batt was assigned to guard the eastern perimeter overnight.
Orders were sent to the 1st batt to prepare for immediate deployment.
Reinforcements from another batt arrived just before sunrise.
