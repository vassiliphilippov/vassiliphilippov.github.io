ceil

verb
to round up
Pronunciation: / siːl / (AmE), / siːl / (BrE)
to round a number up to the nearest whole number, regardless of its decimal part

Examples

To ceil a number
If you ceil 4.3, you get 5 as the result.
The function will ceil any decimal value to the next integer.
To avoid errors, always ceil the calculated number of pages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: floor - to round a number down to the nearest whole number
  • synonym: round up - to increase a number to the next whole number
