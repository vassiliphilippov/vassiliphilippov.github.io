messianism

noun
belief in a savior
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsiəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmɛsiənɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief that a person, group, or movement will bring about salvation, deliverance, or radical improvement to society or the world

  • antonym: realism - the attitude of accepting things as they are, without idealism
