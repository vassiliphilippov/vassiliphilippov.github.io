thud and blunder

noun
clumsy or mistake-prone approach
Pronunciation: / θʌd ənd ˈblʌndər / (AmE), / θʌd ənd ˈblʌndə / (BrE)
a situation or method characterized by clumsy mistakes, carelessness, or repeated failures
