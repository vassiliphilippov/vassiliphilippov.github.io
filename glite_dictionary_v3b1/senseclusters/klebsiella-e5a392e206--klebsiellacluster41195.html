Klebsiella

noun
genus of rod-shaped bacteria, some pathogenic
9.3
Pronunciation: /kleb.sɪ.ˈel.ə/ /; / /klɪb.sɪ.ˈel.ə/ (AmE), /kleb.sɪ.ˈel.ə/ /; / /klɪb.sɪ.ˈel.ə/ (BrE)
