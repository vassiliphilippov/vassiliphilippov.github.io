ceremonious

adjective
marked by formal ceremony or etiquette
1.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /sə.rə.ˈmoʊ.nɪ.əs/ /; / /sə.rə.ˈmoʊ.njəs/ (AmE), /sə.rə.ˈməʊ.nɪ.əs/ /; / /sə.rə.ˈməʊ.njəs/ (BrE)
marked by or showing formal ceremony, etiquette, or excessive formality

Examples

A ceremonious greeting
He greeted each guest in a ceremonious manner, bowing politely as they arrived.
