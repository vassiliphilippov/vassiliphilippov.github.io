inferno

noun
the abode of Satan; hell
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɜrnoʊ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːnəʊ / (BrE)
(in Christianity) The place of eternal punishment for the wicked, considered as the abode of Satan and evil forces; hell.

Examples

Eternal inferno
Many sermons warned parishioners of the torments in the inferno awaiting the unrighteous.
Medieval art often depicts sinners suffering in the inferno for eternity.
The novel's villain was convinced he was destined for the inferno.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hell - In many religions, the place of punishment for the wicked after death
