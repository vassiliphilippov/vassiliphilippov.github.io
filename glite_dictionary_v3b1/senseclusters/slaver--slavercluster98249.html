slaver

verb
dribble saliva from the mouth
Pronunciation: / ˈsleɪ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɑː.və / (BrE)
to allow saliva to dribble or run from the mouth; to drool

Examples

To slaver over food
His mouth slavered as he stared at the enormous feast before him.
The baby would often slaver when she was teething.
The hungry dog began to slaver at the smell of cooked meat.
