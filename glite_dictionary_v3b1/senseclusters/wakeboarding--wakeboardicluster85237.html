wakeboarding

verb
to ride a wakeboard
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪkˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪkˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
to ride a wakeboard over water while being pulled by a boat or cable as a sport

Examples

To go wakeboarding
We spent the afternoon wakeboarding behind the speedboat on the lake.
She learned to wakeboard during her summer vacation at the water sports camp.
Many people enjoy wakeboarding because it combines elements of surfing and waterskiing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surfing - to ride on ocean waves using a surfboard
  • synonym: waterskiing - to ski on water while being pulled by a boat
