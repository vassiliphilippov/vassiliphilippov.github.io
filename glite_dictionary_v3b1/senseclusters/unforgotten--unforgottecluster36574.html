unforgotten

adjective
still remembered
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnfərˈɡɑːtən / (AmE), / ˌʌnfəˈɡɒtən / (BrE)
still remembered or not forgotten, often because of lasting importance or impact

Examples

An unforgotten hero
The hero's sacrifice remained unforgotten by the entire village for generations.
Her words of encouragement were unforgotten long after she had left the school.
The tragedy of that day is unforgotten by those who witnessed it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgotten - no longer remembered
  • synonym: memorable - worth remembering or easily remembered
