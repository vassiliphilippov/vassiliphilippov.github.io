eventing

noun
horse competition
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɛntɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛntɪŋ / (BrE)
a horse competition that combines dressage, cross-country, and show jumping in one event

Examples

Eventing competition
She has been training for eventing since she was a teenager.
The eventing competition attracted riders from all over the country.
Many spectators enjoyed watching the cross-country phase of eventing at the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dressage - a form of competitive horse training and performance
  • synonym: show jumping - a horse competition where riders jump over obstacles
