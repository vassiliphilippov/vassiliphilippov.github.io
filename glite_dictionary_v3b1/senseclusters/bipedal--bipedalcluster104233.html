bipedal

adjective
having two feet; walking on two legs
5.3 5.7
Pronunciation: / baɪˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / baɪˈpiːdəl / (BrE)
(of an animal or being) walking on two feet or using two legs for locomotion.

Examples

Bipedal animal movement
Humans are bipedal creatures, using their two legs for movement.
The researchers observed the bipedal posture of the dinosaur fossils.
Unlike most mammals, kangaroos display bipedal hopping as their primary mode of travel.
