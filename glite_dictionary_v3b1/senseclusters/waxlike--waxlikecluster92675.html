waxlike

adjective
resembling wax in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwæks.laɪk / (BrE)
having a pale, smooth, or translucent appearance like that of wax

Examples

A waxlike face
After the long illness, his face became unnaturally waxlike and gaunt.
He stared at her waxlike complexion, concerned about her health.
The corpse lay in the coffin, its waxlike features frozen in time.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: sallow - of a pale-yellow or unhealthy skin color
  • synonym: waxy - having the appearance or texture of wax
