phlegmon

noun
spreading tissue inflammation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛɡˌmɑn / (AmE), / ˈflɛɡmən / (BrE)
a spreading inflammation of soft tissue, usually caused by infection and not confined to a single area

Examples

A phlegmon infection
The surgeon explained that the phlegmon needed immediate treatment to prevent further spread.
After several days of fever and swelling, a phlegmon developed in her leg.
Antibiotics were prescribed to control the infection caused by the phlegmon.
