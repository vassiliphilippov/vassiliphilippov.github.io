founding

noun
the act of establishing or starting something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of establishing or starting something, especially an institution or organization, for the first time

Examples

The founding of a charity
She was present at the founding of the charity and supported its mission.
The founding of the university dates back to the 19th century.
The company's founding involved several prominent business leaders.
