September elm

noun
autumn-flowering elm tree of the southeastern US
7.9
Pronunciation: / sɛpˈtɛmbɚ ɛlm / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɛmbə ɛlm / (BrE)
A species of elm tree native to the southeastern United States, known for flowering in autumn.

Examples

The September elm tree
Botanists visited Tennessee to study mature specimens of the September elm.
The September elm is rare in many parts of its native range due to habitat loss.
Unlike most elms, the September elm displays its flowers in the fall rather than in spring.
