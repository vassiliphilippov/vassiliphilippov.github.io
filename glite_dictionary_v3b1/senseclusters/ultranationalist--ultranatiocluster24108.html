ultranationalist

adjective
extremely nationalistic
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈnæʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈnæʃənəlɪst / (BrE)
extremely nationalistic; strongly supporting aggressive or exclusionary policies that put one's nation above all others

Examples

Ultranationalist policies
The party's ultranationalist stance led to stricter immigration laws and increased tensions with neighboring countries.
Many citizens were alarmed by the rise of ultranationalist rhetoric in the media.
Protesters gathered to oppose the ultranationalist policies that threatened minority rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitan - open to and comfortable with people from all countries
  • degree lower: nationalist - strongly supporting the interests of one's nation
  • degree lower: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to one's country
