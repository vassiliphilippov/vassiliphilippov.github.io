click

verb
to press mouse button
1.0
Pronunciation: / klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
to press a button on a mouse or similar device to select or activate something on a computer screen

Examples

To click a button
To open the document, simply click the icon on your desktop.
She clicked the link to visit the website her friend recommended.
If you click this button, the program will start installing.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: double-click - to press a mouse button twice quickly
  • antonym: drag - to hold down a mouse button while moving the mouse
  • synonym: tap - to touch lightly, especially on a touchscreen
