genus Jynx

noun
genus of birds called wrynecks
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈdʒɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈdʒɪŋks / (BrE)
A genus of small Old World woodpeckers known as wrynecks, characterized by their twisting neck movements.
