hidden pathway

noun
a concealed or secret route
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdən ˈpæθˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɪd.ən ˈpɑːθ.weɪ / (BrE)
a route that is concealed or not easily seen, often used secretly or discovered unintentionally

Examples

A hidden pathway in forest
Legend says there's a hidden pathway in the forest leading to the ancient ruins.
She carefully followed the hidden pathway, hoping not to be discovered.
The children stumbled upon a hidden pathway behind the old stone wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open road - a route that is clearly visible and accessible
  • synonym: secret passage - a passage that is hidden or used for secret movement
