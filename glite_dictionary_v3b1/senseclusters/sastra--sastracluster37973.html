sastra

noun
Indian scripture
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːstrə / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːstrə / (BrE)
a Sanskrit term for a treatise or scripture, especially in Indian philosophy or religion

Examples

Hindu sastra
The ancient sastra contains detailed teachings on philosophy and ethics.
Scholars spent years translating the sastra into modern languages for wider study.
He quoted a passage from a well-known sastra during his lecture on Indian traditions.
