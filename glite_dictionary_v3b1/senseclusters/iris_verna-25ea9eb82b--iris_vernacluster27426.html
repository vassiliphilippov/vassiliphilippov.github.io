Iris verna

noun

Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪs ˈvɜrnə / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪs ˈvɜːnə / (BrE)
A low-growing spring-flowering North American iris species with bright blue to lilac flowers.

Examples

Iris verna blooms early in the spring, brightening woodlands with its delicate flowers.
Gardeners often plant Iris verna for its attractive blue-lilac blossoms and low maintenance.
The native habitat of Iris verna includes dry woods and open fields across the eastern United States.
