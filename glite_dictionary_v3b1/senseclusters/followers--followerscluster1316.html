followers

noun
social-media subscribers
Pronunciation: / c8f51lo8a5az / (AmE), / c8f52l598a59z / (BrE)
(on social media) accounts or people who subscribe to a user's profile and receive that user's posts or updates; not necessarily supporters.

Examples

Instagram followers
Her followers on the app grew rapidly after the viral post.
Many of his new followers come from abroad and rarely comment on his photos.
She checks her notifications to see which followers liked the announcement today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience - the group of people who see or engage with someone's published content
  • synonym: subscribers - people or accounts who sign up to receive content or updates from a service or profile
