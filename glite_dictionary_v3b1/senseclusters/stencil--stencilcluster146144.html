stencil

noun
sheet with cut-out design for painting/printing
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / ˈstɛnsəl / (BrE)
a thin sheet of material with a design cut out, used to produce a pattern on a surface by allowing ink, paint, or other substance to pass through the holes

Examples

A stencil sheet
He used a stencil to paint numbers on the parking spaces.
The artist created a beautiful mural with the help of a stencil.
You can buy a stencil set to decorate your walls easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a covering used to protect or define an area when painting or printing
  • synonym: template - a shaped piece used as a pattern to make consistent copies
