presubmission

adjective
before formal submission
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːsəbˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːsəbˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
relating to or occurring before a formal submission, especially of documents or applications.

Examples

Presubmission meeting
The team held a presubmission meeting to review the final details of the proposal.
A presubmission checklist helps ensure all requirements are met before sending the application.
She requested a presubmission review to identify any issues with her manuscript.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsubmission - relating to or occurring after a formal submission
  • synonym: preliminary - coming before the main event or activity
