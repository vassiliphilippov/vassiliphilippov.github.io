wren-tit

noun
small brown songbird of California
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn ˌtɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛn ˌtɪt / (BrE)
A small brown songbird of western North America, especially California, that resembles a wren.

Examples

A wren-tit bird
Birdwatchers often hope to spot a wren-tit because of its secretive nature and distinctive call.
The wren-tit is often found skulking in chaparral thickets along the Californian coast.
While hiking, we saw a pair of wren-tits rustling through the underbrush.
