external drive

noun
data storage device connected outside a computer
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɝːnəl draɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəl draɪv / (BrE)
a data storage device housed separately from a computer and connected by a cable, often with its own power supply

Examples

An external drive device
I backed up all my photos onto an external drive for extra safety.
The technician recommended using an external drive for transferring large files.
You can expand your laptop's storage easily by adding an external drive.
