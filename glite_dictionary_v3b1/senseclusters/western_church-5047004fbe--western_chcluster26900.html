Western Church

noun
Roman Catholic branch of Christianity

Examples

Synonyms

  • Roman Catholic Church
    3.7 1.57/m
    Roman Catholic Church is the common formal name for the Vatican-led faith, while Western Church is a broader historical term.
  • Roman Church
    3.4 0.15/m
    Roman Church is a shorter, older name used in writing for the Vatican faith, while Western Church stresses its Western origins.
  • Church of Rome
    4.7 0.09/m
    Church of Rome refers to the local Roman community and heritage, while Western Church names the wider Vatican-centered denomination.
