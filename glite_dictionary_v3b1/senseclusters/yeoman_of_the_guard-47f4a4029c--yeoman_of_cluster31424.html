Yeoman of the Guard

noun
member of British monarch's ceremonial bodyguard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊmən əv ðə ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈjəʊmən əv ðə ɡɑːd / (BrE)
an officer in the ceremonial bodyguard serving the British monarch, wearing a distinctive Tudor uniform

Examples

Yeoman of the Guard uniform
Each Yeoman of the Guard is easily recognized by his striking Tudor uniform.
Historically, a Yeoman of the Guard also played a role in protecting the monarch at feasts and ceremonies.
The Yeoman of the Guard stood watch during the royal procession at Buckingham Palace.
