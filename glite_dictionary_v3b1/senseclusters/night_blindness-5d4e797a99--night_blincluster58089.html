A condition in which it is difficult or impossible to see clearly in dim light, typically due to a deficiency of vitamin A or a disorder of the retina.

Examples

Night blindness condition
A vitamin A deficiency is a common cause of night blindness in some countries.
People with night blindness often struggle to drive at dusk or in poorly lit environments.
The doctor diagnosed him with night blindness after he reported difficulty seeing at night.
