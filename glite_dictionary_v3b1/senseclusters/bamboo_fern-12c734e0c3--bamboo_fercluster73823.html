bamboo fern

noun
a Japanese fern with broad pinnate fronds
4.3
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbuː fɝːn / (AmE), / bamˈbuː fɜːn / (BrE)
a fast-growing, sturdy Japanese fern cultivated for its attractive broad, dark-green pinnate fronds

Examples
