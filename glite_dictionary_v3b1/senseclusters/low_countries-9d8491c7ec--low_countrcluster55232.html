Low Countries

proper noun
region in western Europe (Belgium, Luxembourg, Netherlands)
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˈkʌntriz / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈkʌntriz / (BrE)
the lowland region of western Europe bordering the North Sea, comprising Belgium, Luxembourg, and the Netherlands

Examples
