unfamiliarly

adverb
in an unfamiliar way
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnfəˈmɪljɚli / (AmE), / ʌnfəˈmɪliəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not familiar or well known

Examples

Speak unfamiliarly about customs
He spoke unfamiliarly about the customs of the remote village.
The landscape appeared unfamiliarly empty after the trees were cut down.
She felt unfamiliarly nervous in the new environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strangely - in a way that is unusual or not familiar
