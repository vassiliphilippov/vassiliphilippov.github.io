watch cap

noun
knitted cap worn by sailors
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ kæp / (AmE), / wɒtʃ kap / (BrE)
a close-fitting knitted cap, typically of dark blue wool, worn by sailors and others in cold weather

Examples

A thick watch cap
During the winter hike, he wore a thick watch cap to stay warm.
She bought a navy blue watch cap at the army surplus store.
The sailor pulled his watch cap down over his ears to block the wind.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: balaclava - a close-fitting cap covering the head and neck
  • synonym: beanie - a small, close-fitting cap
  • synonym: stocking cap - a long, knitted woolen cap
