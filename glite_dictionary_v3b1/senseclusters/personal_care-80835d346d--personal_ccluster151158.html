personal care

noun
assistance with daily self-care tasks
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsənl ˈkɛr / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənl ˈkeə / (BrE)
the assistance provided to people who are unable to fully care for themselves, often including help with bathing, dressing, eating, and other daily activities

Examples

Personal care assistance
After her surgery, she required temporary personal care until she could regain her independence.
Many elderly people rely on home aides for personal care like bathing and dressing.
The facility provides personal care services to residents who need daily assistance.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: nursing care - medical and personal care provided by nurses
  • synonym: self-care - care of oneself, especially without professional help
