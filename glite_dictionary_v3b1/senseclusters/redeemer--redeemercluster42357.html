redeemer

noun
one who redeems or buys back something
5.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdimɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈdiːmə / (BrE)
a person who redeems or buys back notes, merchandise, or commercial paper.

Examples

Redeemer of a coupon
As the redeemer of the gift certificate, she chose a new jacket.
Only the rightful redeemer can exchange the merchandise for cash.
The redeemer of the promissory note presented his claim at the bank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buyer - a person who purchases something
  • antonym: seller - a person who sells something
