foundation garment

noun
undergarment for shaping the body
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ˈɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ˈɡɑːm(ə)nt / (BrE)
a woman's undergarment designed to shape or support the body's contours, such as a girdle or corset

Examples

A foundation garment for shaping
Many women once relied on a foundation garment for support and shaping.
She wore a foundation garment under her dress to achieve a smooth silhouette.
The new foundation garment is both comfortable and nearly invisible under clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corset - a close-fitting undergarment extending from below the chest to the hips, worn to shape the figure
  • synonym: girdle - an elasticized undergarment for women worn to shape the waist and hips
