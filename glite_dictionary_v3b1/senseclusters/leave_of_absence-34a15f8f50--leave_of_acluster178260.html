leave of absence

noun
authorized time away from work or duty
Pronunciation: / liv əv ˈæbsəns / (AmE), / liːv əv ˈæbsəns / (BrE)
a period of time when an employee or student is officially allowed to be away from work or duty

Examples

A leave of absence period
After her leave of absence, she returned to work with renewed energy.
She took a leave of absence from her job to care for her family.
The university granted him a leave of absence for medical reasons.
