primrose

noun
plant with clusters of bright flowers (Primula)
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪmˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈprɪmrəʊz / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Primula, typically with clusters of brightly colored flowers and rosettes of basal leaves, commonly found in temperate regions

Examples

A primrose flower
A single primrose stood out among the green leaves in the garden.
She picked a bouquet of primrose flowers for her grandmother.
Wild primroses carpet the woods in early spring, adding a splash of color to the forest floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowslip - a related species in the Primula genus with yellow flowers
