tinnevelly senna

noun
a medicinal shrub, source of senna leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: /tɪn.ˈnev.ə.liː ˈsen.ə/ (AmE), /tɪn.ˈnev.ə.liː ˈsen.ə/ (BrE)
