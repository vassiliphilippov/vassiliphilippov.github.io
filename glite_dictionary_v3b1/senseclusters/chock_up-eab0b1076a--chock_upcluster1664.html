chock up

verb
to pack or crowd to capacity
9.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /tʃɑːk ʌp/ (AmE), /tʃɒk ʌp/ (BrE)

Examples

Vendors chock up their shelves with goods before the holiday season.
We managed to chock up the entire trunk with luggage for our trip.
