podge

noun
small lump or clump
9.5
Pronunciation: / pɑdʒ / (AmE), / pɒdʒ / (BrE)
a small lump, clump, or compact mass of a substance, often soft or sticky.

Examples

A podge of clay
He found a greasy podge of clay stuck to the underside of the table.
A podge of dough remained on the mixing bowl after baking.
She wiped a podge of jam off the kitchen counter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blob - a small drop or lump of a thick liquid or soft substance
  • synonym: clump - a compact mass of something, especially soft or wet
