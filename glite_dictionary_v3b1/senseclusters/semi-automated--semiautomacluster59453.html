semi-automated

adjective
partly machine-operated
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi ˈɔtəˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi ˈɔːtəməteɪtɪd / (BrE)
partly operated by machines and partly by people

Examples

A semi-automated system
The company installed a semi-automated assembly line to improve efficiency while keeping some manual checks.
A semi-automated process allows workers to oversee machines and intervene when necessary.
Many modern warehouses use semi-automated systems to balance speed and quality control.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: automated - fully operated by machines without human intervention
  • degree lower: manual - operated entirely by people without machines
