semi-automated

adjective
partly machine-operated
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈse.mɪ ˈɔː.tə.meɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ˈse.maɪ ˈɔː.tə.meɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈse.mɪ ˈɔː.tə.meɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
