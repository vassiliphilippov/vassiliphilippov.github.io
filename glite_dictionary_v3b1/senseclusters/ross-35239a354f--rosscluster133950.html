Ross

proper noun
American seamstress who made the first US flag
5.9
Pronunciation: / rɑs / (AmE), / rɒs / (BrE)
(Betsy Ross) An American seamstress reputed to have made the first American flag at the request of George Washington (1752–1836).

Examples

Ross sewing flag
Many history books credit Ross with sewing the first American flag.
Some question whether Ross actually designed the original Stars and Stripes.
The story of Ross has become an important part of American folklore.
