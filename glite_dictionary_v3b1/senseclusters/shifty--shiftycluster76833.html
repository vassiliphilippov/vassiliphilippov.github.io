shifty

adjective
changing position or direction
5.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɪf.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈʃɪf.tiː/ (BrE)
(of something) changing position or direction, especially frequently or unpredictably.

Examples

Shifty winds
After hours of shifty winds, the sailors found it hard to maintain their course.
Clouds moved across the sky in a shifty manner, blocking and revealing the sun in turns.
The crowd grew shifty as it slowly dispersed in different directions.
