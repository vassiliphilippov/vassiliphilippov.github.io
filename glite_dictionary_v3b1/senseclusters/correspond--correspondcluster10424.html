correspond

verb
exchange written messages with someone
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːr.əˈspɑːnd / (AmE), / ˌkɒr.ɪˈspɒnd / (BrE)
to communicate with someone by exchanging letters or messages, especially regularly over a period of time

Examples

Correspond by letter
During the war, soldiers would often correspond with their families back home.
She likes to correspond with friends she met while traveling.
They continued to correspond for years after graduating from university.
