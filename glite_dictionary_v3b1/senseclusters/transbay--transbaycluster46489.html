transbay

noun
bay-crossing transport
6.1
Pronunciation: / trænzˈbeɪ / (AmE), / trænzˈbeɪ / (BrE)
a transport service, trip, or route that crosses a bay, especially connecting cities or areas across a harbor

Examples

Transbay bus route
The city expanded its transbay bus service to help commuters reach downtown faster.
A new transbay tunnel is being constructed to improve public transportation between the two cities.
She relies on the transbay train every morning to get to her job across the water.

  • synonym: ferry - a boat or ship for carrying people and vehicles across water
