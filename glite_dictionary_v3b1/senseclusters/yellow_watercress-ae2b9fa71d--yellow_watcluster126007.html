yellow watercress

noun
a plant sometimes used in salads or as a weed
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈwɔtərˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈwɔːtəkres / (BrE)
an annual or biennial plant growing in damp areas, sometimes used in salads or as a potherb; also considered a weed in some regions

Examples

Yellow watercress plant
Farmers often consider yellow watercress a troublesome weed in irrigated fields.
She added fresh yellow watercress to the salad for extra flavor and color.
Wild yellow watercress can quickly take over wet garden beds if not controlled.
