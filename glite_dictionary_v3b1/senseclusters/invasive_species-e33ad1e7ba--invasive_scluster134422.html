A non-native plant or animal species that spreads rapidly in a new environment and disrupts local ecosystems.

Examples

Invasive species spread rapidly
Conservationists are working to control the spread of invasive species in the region.
New regulations were established to prevent the import of potential invasive species.
The lake ecosystem suffered after several invasive species were introduced.

Synonyms

  • invasive
    4.8 0.07/m
    invasive labels plants or animals that spread fast and harm local nature, while invasive species is the noun for those non-native organisms.
  • invader
    4.2 1.08/m
    invader names an organism that enters and often outcompetes native species, while invasive species refers to non-native species that spread and disrupt ecosystems.
