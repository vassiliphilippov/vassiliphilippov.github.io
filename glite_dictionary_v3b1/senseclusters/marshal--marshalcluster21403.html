marshal

noun
event supervisor
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃəl / (BrE)
a person who organizes, manages, or supervises an event, group, or activity, often ensuring order and safety

Examples

Event marshal
The marshal made sure all the runners stayed on the correct path during the marathon.
At the festival, a marshal helped direct the crowd to the exit safely.
Each parade float was accompanied by a marshal to maintain order along the route.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges and manages an event or activity
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an event or activity
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees or manages others
