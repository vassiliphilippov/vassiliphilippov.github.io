Tasmania

proper noun
Australian island state
3.4
Pronunciation: / tæzˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / tazˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
an Australian state consisting of the island of Tasmania and nearby smaller islands, located south of the mainland, known for its unique wildlife, wilderness, and colonial history
