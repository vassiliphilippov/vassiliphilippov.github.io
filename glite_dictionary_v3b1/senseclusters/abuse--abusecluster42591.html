abuse

noun
cruel or violent treatment
3.2 44/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàbjus / (AmE), / …ôÀàbjuÀês / (BrE)
cruel or violent treatment of a person or animal, especially regularly or repeatedly

Examples

Child abuse
Many organizations are working to prevent child abuse worldwide.
The investigation revealed years of abuse at the institution.
Victims of abuse often need significant emotional and psychological support.

Synonyms

  • maltreatment
    7.0 0.87/m
    maltreatment is cruel or harmful treatment that often causes physical or emotional harm, while abuse stresses repeated or ongoing harm.
  • ill-treatment
    6.4 0.18/m
    ill-treatment is cruel, inhumane care or handling over time, while abuse often implies repeated violent or controlling behavior.
  • mistreatment
    4.0 1.71/m
    mistreatment is treating someone unfairly or badly in many ways, while abuse usually means cruel or violent repeated treatment.
  • cruelty
    3.2 7.64/m
    cruelty is the choice to cause pain or suffering to others, while abuse often refers to harmful actions done repeatedly.
  • inhuman treatment
    8.3 0.03/m
    inhuman treatment is deliberate causing of great pain or suffering, while abuse covers cruel or violent treatment, often repeated.
  • brutality
    5.6 8.05/m
    brutality is extreme violent or savage acts that may be sudden, while abuse often means ongoing cruel treatment over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mistreatment - the action of treating someone badly or unfairly
  • synonym: neglect - failure to care for properly, often as a form of harm
