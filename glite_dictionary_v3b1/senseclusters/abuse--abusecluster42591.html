abuse

noun
cruel or violent treatment
Pronunciation: / əˈbjus / (AmE), / əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
cruel or violent treatment of a person or animal, especially regularly or repeatedly

Examples

Child abuse
Many organizations are working to prevent child abuse worldwide.
The investigation revealed years of abuse at the institution.
Victims of abuse often need significant emotional and psychological support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: care - the provision of what is necessary for the health or welfare of someone
  • synonym: mistreatment - the action of treating someone badly or unfairly
  • synonym: neglect - failure to care for properly, often as a form of harm
