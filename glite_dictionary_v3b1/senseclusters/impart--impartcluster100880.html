  • teach
    1.0 300/m
    teach gives direct instruction to help someone learn, while impart means to communicate knowledge without full teaching.
  • instruct
    2.6 7/m
    instruct tells someone exactly how to do something in a formal way, while impart covers passing on knowledge more generally.
  • educate
    2.2 50/m
    educate gives someone knowledge or skills over time through training, while impart is a quicker act of sharing knowledge.
  • transmit
    3.7 12/m
    transmit sends information or signals between places or devices, while impart is usually a human act of teaching or explaining.
  • pass along
    2.5 3/m
    pass along means to relay information from one person to another, while impart often implies teaching or careful explanation.
  • share
    1.0 40/m
    share is to tell or show something with others in a friendly way, while impart feels more formal and instructive.
  • give
    1.0 700/m
