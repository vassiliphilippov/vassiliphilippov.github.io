Aphis

noun
genus of sap-sucking insects (aphids)
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.fɪs/ /; / /ˈæ.fɪs/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.fɪs/ /; / /ˈæ.fɪs/ (BrE)
