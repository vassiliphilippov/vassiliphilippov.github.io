Aphis

noun
genus of sap-sucking insects (aphids)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.fɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small sap-sucking insects in the family Aphididae, many of which are pests that can damage fruit trees and vegetables.

Examples

Aphis insect pests
Gardeners often use insecticidal soap to control Aphis infestations on their vegetables.
Scientists studied the lifecycle of an Aphis species to find better pest management strategies.
The Aphis genus includes several species known to damage apple and pear orchards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphid - a small insect that sucks sap from plants; pest
