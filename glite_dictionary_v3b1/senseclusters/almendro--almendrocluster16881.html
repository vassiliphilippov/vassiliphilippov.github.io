almendro

noun
almond tree
Pronunciation: / alˈmendroʊ / (AmE), / alˈmendrɒ / (BrE)
a tree (Prunus dulcis) grown in temperate regions for its edible seeds (almonds) and attractive spring flowers

Examples

Flowering almendro tree
The almendro in our backyard is full of white blossoms every spring.
Farmers harvest almonds from the almendro after the flowers have fallen.
Many gardens feature an almendro for its beautiful and fragrant flowers.

  • synonym: almond tree - a tree that produces almonds
