Synonyms

  • exchange
    1.8 20/m
    exchange means giving and receiving items often formally or in shops, while swap is a casual, everyday trade between people.
  • trade
    1.0 26/m
    trade often suggests business or payment for goods, while swap feels informal and is used between friends or collectors.
  • interchange
    4.1 0.26/m
    interchange implies mutual replacement or parts that fit each other, while swap is simple giving one thing for another between people.
  • switch
    2.0 39/m
    switch means change from one thing or activity to another, while swap means exchange two things between people.
