snakeskin

noun
shed snake skin
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪkˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈsneɪkˌskɪn / (BrE)
the outer skin that a snake sheds as it grows, often found as a thin, dry layer

Examples

A shed snakeskin
She found a snakeskin in the garden after the snake had shed it.
The artist used pieces of snakeskin to create a unique texture in his sculpture.
A snakeskin can sometimes be found near rocks where snakes like to hide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scales - the small, hard plates covering the skin of reptiles and fish
  • synonym: shedding - the process of losing or casting off a covering or layer
