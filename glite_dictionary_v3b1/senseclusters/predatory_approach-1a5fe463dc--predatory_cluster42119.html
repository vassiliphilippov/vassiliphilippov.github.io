predatory approach

noun
aggressive, exploitative tactics
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛdəˌtɔri əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈprɛdəˌtɔːri əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an aggressive and exploitative way of gaining an advantage over others, especially in business or competition

Examples

A predatory approach to business
Her predatory approach to negotiations left many competitors wary of working with her.
Many firms criticize their rivals' predatory approach to acquiring smaller companies.
The court investigated whether the corporation's predatory approach breached fair competition laws.
