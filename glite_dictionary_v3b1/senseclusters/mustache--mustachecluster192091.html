mustache

noun
large bushy moustache
Pronunciation: / məˈstæʃioʊ / (AmE), / məˈstæʃiəʊ / (BrE)
A large, bushy moustache, especially one with hair growing down the sides of the mouth.

Examples

A flamboyant mustachio
His flamboyant mustachio was the talk of the party.
In the portrait, a gentleman with a prominent mustachio stares confidently at the viewer.
The actor twirled his impressive mustachio before delivering his villainous speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moustache - Hair grown on the upper lip; general term for facial hair above the lip
