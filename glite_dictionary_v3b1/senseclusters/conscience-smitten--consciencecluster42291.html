conscience-smitten

adjective
troubled by guilt or remorse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.ʃəns ˌsmɪt.ən / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəns ˌsmɪt.ən / (BrE)
Troubled or deeply affected by feelings of guilt or remorse due to conscience.

Examples

Conscience-smitten feeling
He felt conscience-smitten after lying to his friend about what happened.
The conscience-smitten employee apologized for his mistake and tried to make amends.
Unable to sleep, she was conscience-smitten by the choices she had made.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remorseful - feeling or expressing regret or guilt for wrongdoing
  • antonym: unrepentant - not feeling remorse or regret for one's actions
