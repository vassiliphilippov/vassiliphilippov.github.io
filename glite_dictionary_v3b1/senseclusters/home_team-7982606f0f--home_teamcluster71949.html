home team

noun
team playing at its own venue
4.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊm tim / (AmE), / həʊm tiːm / (BrE)
the team that is playing in its own venue during a sports event, typically with the support of local fans

Examples

The home team wins
Even though the weather was terrible, the home team had strong support from their fans.
The crowd erupted in cheers when the home team scored the winning goal.
Wearing their traditional colors, the home team dominated the first half of the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: away team - the team playing at their opponent's venue
