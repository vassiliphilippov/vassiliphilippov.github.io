fornix

noun
arch-shaped structure, often anatomical
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrnɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnɪks / (BrE)
An arch-shaped structure in the body or architecture, especially the arched roof of an anatomical space.

Examples

Anatomical fornix
In anatomy class, we studied the fornix as part of the limbic system.
The fornix of the brain plays an important role in memory function.
The vault of the ancient temple resembled a giant fornix in shape.
