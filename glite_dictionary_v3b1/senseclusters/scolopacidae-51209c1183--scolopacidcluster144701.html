Scolopacidae

noun
family of wading birds (sandpipers etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˌskoʊləˈpæsəˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌskɒləˈpæsɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of wading birds that includes sandpipers, woodcocks, snipes, tattlers, curlews, godwits, and dowitchers.
