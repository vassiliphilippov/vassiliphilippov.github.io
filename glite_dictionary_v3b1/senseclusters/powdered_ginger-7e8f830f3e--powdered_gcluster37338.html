powdered ginger

noun
dried, ground ginger used as a spice
6.6 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.dərd ˈdʒɪn.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.dəd ˈdʒɪn.dʒə/ (BrE)
