powdered ginger

noun
dried, ground ginger used as a spice
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚd ˈdʒɪndʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdəd ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
Ginger root that has been dried and ground into a fine powder, used as a spice in cooking and baking.

Examples

Powdered ginger spice
If you don't have fresh ginger, you can substitute powdered ginger instead.
She sprinkled powdered ginger into the cookie dough for extra zest.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of powdered ginger to add a warm, spicy flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh ginger - ginger root in its natural, undried form
  • synonym: ground ginger - ginger root dried and ground into powder
