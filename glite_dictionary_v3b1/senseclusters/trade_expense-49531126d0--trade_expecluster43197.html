trade expense

noun
business cost for running a trade
Pronunciation: / treɪd ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / treɪd ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
an ordinary and necessary cost incurred in the regular operation of a business or trade, especially for tax purposes

Examples

A trade expense item
The accountant explained which items could be classified as a trade expense for tax deductions.
The company reviewed each trade expense to ensure compliance with financial regulations.
Travel costs to meet with clients are often considered a trade expense by businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: business expense - a cost incurred in the course of business or trade
