Caps Lock

noun
keyboard uppercase key
2.6
Pronunciation: /kæps lɑːk/ /; / /kæps lɔːk/ (AmE), /kæps lɒk/ (BrE)
a key on a computer keyboard that, when turned on, makes all typed letters uppercase until turned off
