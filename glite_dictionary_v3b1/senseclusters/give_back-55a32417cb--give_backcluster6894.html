give back

verb
to contribute or help others in return
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv bæk / (AmE), / ɡɪv bæk / (BrE)
to contribute time, money, or resources to help others, especially as an act of gratitude or community support

Examples

To give back to charity
After achieving financial stability, he wanted to give back by supporting local charities.
Many successful athletes give back to their hometowns by funding youth programs.
Volunteering at the animal shelter is one way she likes to give back to her community.
