distro

noun
software variant
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɪstrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a version or release of software (especially an operating system) or for a distribution of goods

Examples

Linux distro
Many people choose a Linux distro that best fits their needs and hardware.
The new distro offers improved security features and a modern user interface.
He tested several distros before deciding which one to install on his laptop.
The open-source community released a new distro of the operating system last week.
After the concert, volunteers managed the distro of leftover food to local shelters.
He downloaded a popular Linux distro to try out on his old laptop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distribution - a complete software package or operating system release
  • synonym: version - a particular form or variant of something
