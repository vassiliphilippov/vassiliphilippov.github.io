credent

adjective
willing to believe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːdənt / (AmE), / ˈkriːdənt / (BrE)
willing to believe or trust something easily; credulous or trusting

Examples

Credent people
The credent villagers believed every rumor that spread through the town.
In his credent nature, he trusted strangers without hesitation.
A credent audience may accept false promises without question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: credulous - too ready to believe things; easily fooled or cheated
  • antonym: skeptical - not easily convinced; having doubts or reservations
