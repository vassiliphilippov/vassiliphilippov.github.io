twistedly

adverb
in a perverse way
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪstɪdli / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstɪdli / (BrE)
in a cruel, perverse, or morally warped manner.

Examples

Twistedly cruel
He laughed twistedly when he saw the chaos he had caused.
The villain in the story acted twistedly, enjoying the suffering of others.
She smiled twistedly as she revealed her cruel plan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kindly - in a gentle or caring manner
  • synonym: sadistically - in a way that takes pleasure in others' pain
