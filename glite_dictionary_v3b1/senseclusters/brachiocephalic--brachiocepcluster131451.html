brachiocephalic

noun
major blood vessel
8.9
Pronunciation: /bræk.i.oʊ.sə.ˈfæl.ɪk/ (AmE), /bræk.i.əʊ.sə.ˈfæl.ɪk/ (BrE)
a large blood vessel that supplies blood to the arm and head, especially the brachiocephalic artery or vein
