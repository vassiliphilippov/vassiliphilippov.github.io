brachiocephalic

noun
major blood vessel
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbreɪkioʊsɪˈfælɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrækiəʊsɪˈfælɪk / (BrE)
a large blood vessel that supplies blood to the arm and head, especially the brachiocephalic artery or vein

Examples

Brachiocephalic vessel
The surgeon identified the brachiocephalic before proceeding with the heart bypass procedure.
Damage to the brachiocephalic can lead to serious complications involving blood flow to the brain and arm.
During the anatomy lecture, the professor pointed out the location of the brachiocephalic on the diagram.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artery - a blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart
  • synonym: vein - a blood vessel that carries blood toward the heart
