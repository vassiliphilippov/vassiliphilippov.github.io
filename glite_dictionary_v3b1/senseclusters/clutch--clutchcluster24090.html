clutch

verb
to hold tightly or firmly
3.0 3.4
Pronunciation: / klʌtʃ / (AmE), / klʌtʃ / (BrE)
to grasp or hold something tightly, especially with the hands or due to a strong emotion

Examples

To clutch a bag
