Christmas fern

noun
North American evergreen fern
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməs fɝn / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs fɜːn / (BrE)
a North American evergreen fern (Polystichum acrostichoides) with pinnate leaves and dense clusters of lance-shaped fronds, often remaining green at Christmas time
