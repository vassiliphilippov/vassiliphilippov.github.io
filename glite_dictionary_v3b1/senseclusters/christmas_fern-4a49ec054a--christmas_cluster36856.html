Christmas fern

noun
North American evergreen fern
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməs fɝn / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs fɜːn / (BrE)
a North American evergreen fern (Polystichum acrostichoides) with pinnate leaves and dense clusters of lance-shaped fronds, often remaining green at Christmas time

Examples

The Christmas fern plant
Gardeners often select the Christmas fern for shady areas because of its year-round foliage.
Hikers spotted several Christmas ferns growing along the wooded trail during their December walk.
The Christmas fern remains green throughout the winter, adding life to the forest floors.
