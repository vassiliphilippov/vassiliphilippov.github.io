golden aster

noun
North American plant with yellow aster-like flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈæstə / (BrE)
any of several North American flowering plants of the genus Chrysopsis, having bright yellow, aster-like flower heads

Examples

Golden aster flowers
Gardeners often plant golden aster for its bright yellow blooms.
The fields were dotted with golden aster as summer approached its peak.
We identified a rare variety of golden aster during our hike in the national park.
