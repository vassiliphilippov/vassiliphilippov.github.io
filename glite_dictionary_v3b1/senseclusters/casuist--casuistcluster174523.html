casuist

noun
person using subtle, often specious reasoning
7.5 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkæz.juː.ɪst/ /; / /ˈkæʒ.uː.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈkæz.juː.ɪst/ /; / /ˈkæʒ.uː.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who uses detailed or subtle reasoning to solve moral or ethical problems, sometimes in a tricky or overly clever way
