hangman's rope

noun
rope used for execution by hanging
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋmənz roʊp / (AmE), / ˈhæŋmənz rəʊp / (BrE)
a rope used to execute someone by hanging, especially as part of a legal death sentence

Examples

Legends say that a hangman's rope brings misfortune to anyone who owns it.
The hangman's rope was prepared carefully before the execution.
