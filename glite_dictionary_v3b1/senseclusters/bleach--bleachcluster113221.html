bleach

noun
chemical for whitening or disinfecting
Pronunciation: / bliːtʃ / (AmE), / bliːtʃ / (BrE)
a chemical agent used to make things white or colorless, often for cleaning or disinfecting purposes

Examples

A bottle of bleach
It is important to use bleach carefully when cleaning bathrooms.
Many people rely on bleach to disinfect surfaces during flu season.
She accidentally spilled bleach on her shirt and ruined the color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfectant - a substance used to destroy bacteria and viruses
  • antonym: dye - a substance used to add color to materials
