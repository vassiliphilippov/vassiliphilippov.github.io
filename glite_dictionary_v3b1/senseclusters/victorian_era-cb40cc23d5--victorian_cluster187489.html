Victorian era

noun
period of Queen Victoria's reign in Britain
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔːriən ˈɛrə /; / vɪkˈtɔriən ˈɛrə / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːrɪən ˈɪərə /; / vɪkˈtɔːriən ˈɪərə / (BrE)
the period in British history during the reign of Queen Victoria (1837–1901), known for its distinctive social, cultural, and political characteristics
