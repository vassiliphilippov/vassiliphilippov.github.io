reproacher

noun
a person who finds fault or blames
Pronunciation: / rɪˈproʊtʃər / (AmE), / rɪˈprəʊtʃə / (BrE)
a person who expresses disapproval or criticism, especially by finding fault or imputing blame

Examples

A reproacher criticizes others
A constant reproacher can bring down the morale of a group.
The reproacher never missed a chance to highlight the team's mistakes.
Tired of being called a reproacher, he tried to focus on the positives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admirer - a person who regards someone or something with respect or warm approval
  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses unfavorable opinions or judgments
