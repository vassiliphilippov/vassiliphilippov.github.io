roguery

noun
mischievous or dishonest behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊɡəri / (AmE), / ˈrəʊɡəri / (BrE)
reckless, mischievous, or dishonest behavior that annoys or discomforts others

Examples

A roguery act
A glint of roguery sparkled in her eyes as she played the prank.
His reputation for roguery made him unpopular with his colleagues.
The children's roguery kept their parents on their toes all afternoon.
