seropositive

adjective
positive blood test
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪroʊˈpɑzətɪv / (AmE), / ˌsɪərəʊˈpɒzətɪv / (BrE)
having a positive result in a blood test for a specific antibody or infection

Examples

Seropositive test result
The patient was found to be seropositive for the virus after routine screening.
Many seropositive individuals require ongoing medical monitoring and support.
A seropositive result indicates the presence of specific antibodies in the blood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seronegative - having a negative result in a blood test for a specific antibody or infection
