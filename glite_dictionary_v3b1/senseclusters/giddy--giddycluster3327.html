giddy

adjective
frivolous or silly; lacking seriousness
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪdi / (AmE), / ˈɡɪdi / (BrE)
lacking seriousness or behaving in a light-hearted or frivolous way; silly or playful.

Examples

Giddy laughter
Her giddy laughter made everyone in the office smile.
I felt a bit giddy after hearing the ridiculous rumor.
The students grew giddy with excitement on the last day of school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious - thoughtful, with gravity or earnestness
  • synonym: silly - lacking in seriousness or common sense; foolish
