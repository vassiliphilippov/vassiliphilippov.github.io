Neritina

noun
genus of aquatic snails
Pronunciation: / nɛrɪˈtiːnə /; / ˌnɛrɪˈtiːnə / (AmE), / nɛrɪˈtiːnə /; / ˌnɛrɪˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a genus of small to medium-sized aquatic snails in the family Neritidae, often found in brackish waters and known for their decorative, brightly colored shells
