Alsatian

noun
a large breed of shepherd dog
Pronunciation: / ælˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ælˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a large breed of dog, also known as the German Shepherd, often used in police work and as a guide for the blind

Examples

Alsatian police dog
Her grandfather has trained an Alsatian as a guide dog for the visually impaired.
Many people admire the loyalty and intelligence of an Alsatian.
The police force uses an Alsatian to assist in their search operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: German Shepherd - a large, intelligent breed of working dog
