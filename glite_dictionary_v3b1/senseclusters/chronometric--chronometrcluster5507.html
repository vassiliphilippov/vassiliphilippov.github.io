chronometric

adjective
measuring time
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊnəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrɒnəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of time or to devices that measure time

Examples

Chronometric instruments
The scientist used chronometric equipment to record the exact duration of each experiment.
Advances in chronometric technology have improved the accuracy of modern clocks.
The study relied on chronometric analysis to measure participants' reaction times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporal - relating to time
