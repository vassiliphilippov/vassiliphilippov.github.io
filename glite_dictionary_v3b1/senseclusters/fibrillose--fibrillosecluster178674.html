fibrillose

adjective
covered with fine fibrils
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪ.brəˌloʊs / (AmE), / ˈfɪb.rɪ.ləʊs / (BrE)
(of a surface) covered with fine fibers or fibrils, more or less evenly distributed across it.

Examples

Fibrillose surface texture
A fibrillose texture is often found in certain species of fungi and seeds.
The botanist noted the fibrillose stems that were easily distinguished by their fine hairy coating.
The mushroom's cap was distinctly fibrillose, with tiny threads covering its entire surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrillous - covered with fibrils; having a fibrous appearance
