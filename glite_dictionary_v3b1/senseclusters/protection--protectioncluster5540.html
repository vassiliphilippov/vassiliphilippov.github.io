protection

noun
money extorted by criminals for safety
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
money paid to criminals in exchange for a promise not to be harmed, especially as part of organized crime activity

Examples

Pay protection money
Many businesses refused to pay protection, despite ongoing threats.
Police uncovered a protection racket operating across the city.
The restaurant owner was forced to pay protection to the local gang.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extortion - obtaining something, especially money, by force or threats
