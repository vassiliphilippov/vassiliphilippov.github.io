apprenticeship

noun
period/position of being an apprentice
4.5
Pronunciation: / əˈprɛn.tɪs.ʃɪp / (AmE), / əˈprɛn.tɪs.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or period of time during which a person is an apprentice, learning a trade or skill from a skilled employer

Examples

Complete an apprenticeship
After completing his apprenticeship, he became a certified electrician.
She found her apprenticeship invaluable in developing practical skills.
The company offers an apprenticeship program for students interested in carpentry.
