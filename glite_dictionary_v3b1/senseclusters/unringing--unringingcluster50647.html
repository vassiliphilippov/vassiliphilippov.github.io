unringing

noun
stopping ringing sound
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of stopping a ringing sound or causing something to cease ringing

Examples

Unringing of the alarm
The unringing of the alarm was a relief after minutes of loud noise.
After the ceremony, the unringing of the bells signaled the end of the event.
He waited for the unringing of his phone before continuing the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ringing - the act or sound of something ringing
  • synonym: silencing - the act of making something silent
