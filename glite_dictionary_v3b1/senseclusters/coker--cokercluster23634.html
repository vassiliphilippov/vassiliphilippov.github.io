coker

noun
coke-producing machine
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkə / (BrE)
a machine or industrial unit used to produce coke from coal or oil by heating it in the absence of air

Examples

Industrial coker unit

A malfunction in the coker caused a temporary shutdown at the plant.
Technicians monitor the coker closely to ensure efficient coke production.
