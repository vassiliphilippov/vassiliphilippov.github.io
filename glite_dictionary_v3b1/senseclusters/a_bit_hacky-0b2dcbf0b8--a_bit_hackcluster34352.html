a bit hacky

adjective
somewhat makeshift or unprofessional
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt ˈhæki / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈhæki / (BrE)
Somewhat unprofessional, makeshift, or done in a way that is not elegant or proper, often as a quick fix.

Examples

A bit hacky design
Her design for the poster looked a bit hacky compared to the others.
The temporary code fix was a bit hacky but got the job done.
We made a bit hacky changes to the website just to meet the deadline.
