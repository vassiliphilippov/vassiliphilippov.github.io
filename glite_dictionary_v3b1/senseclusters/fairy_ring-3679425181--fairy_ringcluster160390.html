fairy ring

noun
ring of mushrooms formed by fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeəri rɪŋ / (BrE)
A naturally occurring ring or arc of mushrooms, typically forming at the edge of underground fungal growth.

Examples

A fairy ring of mushrooms
She studied the growth patterns of a fairy ring for her biology project.
The gardener explained that the persistent fairy rings in the lawn were caused by underground fungi.
We noticed a large fairy ring in the field behind our house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mushroom ring - a ring or arc formed by mushrooms resulting from fungal growth
