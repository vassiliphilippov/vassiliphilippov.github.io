puff of steam

noun
short burst of steam
Pronunciation: / pʌf əv stim / (AmE), / pʌf əv stiːm / (BrE)
a short, sudden burst or emission of steam

Examples

A puff of steam
A sudden puff of steam escaped from the pressure cooker.
She watched the kettle for the first puff of steam to signal the water was boiling.
The old locomotive released a loud puff of steam as it started moving.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: jet of steam - a powerful stream of steam
