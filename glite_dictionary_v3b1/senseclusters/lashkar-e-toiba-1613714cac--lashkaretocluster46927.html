Lashkar-e-Toiba

proper noun
Islamist militant group in South Asia
9.9
Pronunciation: / læʃˌkɑr eɪ toʊˈiːbə / (AmE), / læʃˌkɑː eɪ təʊˈiːbə / (BrE)
an Islamist militant group based in Pakistan, known for carrying out attacks in Kashmir and India with the aim of establishing Islamic rule in the region
