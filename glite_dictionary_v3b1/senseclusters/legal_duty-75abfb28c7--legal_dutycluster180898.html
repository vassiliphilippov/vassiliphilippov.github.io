legal duty

noun
obligation imposed by law
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl ˈduːti /; / ˈliɡəl ˈduti / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
an obligation or responsibility that a person must follow because it is required by law

Examples

A legal duty to act
A doctor has a legal duty to maintain patient confidentiality.
Employers have a legal duty to provide a safe workplace.
Failure to fulfill a legal duty can result in liability for damages.
As a company director, he has a legal duty to act in the best interests of shareholders.
Doctors have a legal duty to maintain patient confidentiality.
Failure to comply with your legal duty can result in serious consequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duty - a moral or legal obligation; a responsibility
  • synonym: legal obligation - an obligation arising from the law
  • synonym: obligation - something a person is bound to do, especially by law or moral duty
  • antonym: right - a legal entitlement to have or do something
