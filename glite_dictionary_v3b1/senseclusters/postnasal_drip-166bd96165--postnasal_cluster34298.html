postnasal drip

noun
mucus dripping from nasal cavity to throat
7.2 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊst.neɪ.zəl drɪp/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊst.neɪ.zəl drɪp/ (BrE)
A condition in which excessive mucus is produced by the nasal mucosa and flows down the back of the throat, often causing coughing or throat irritation.

Examples

Postnasal drip symptoms
Doctors often recommend saline sprays to help treat postnasal drip and its symptoms.
Her persistent cough was eventually traced to postnasal drip caused by allergies.
Many people experience postnasal drip during cold or allergy season, leading to throat discomfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhinitis - inflammation of the nasal mucous membrane
  • synonym: sore throat - pain or irritation of the throat
