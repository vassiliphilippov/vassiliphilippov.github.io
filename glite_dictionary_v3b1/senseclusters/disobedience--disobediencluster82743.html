Synonyms

  • noncompliance
    7.7 2/m
    noncompliance is a neutral, formal term for failing to follow rules or orders, while disobedience is broader and more general.
  • insubordination
    8.5 1.0/m
    insubordination is a workplace act of refusing orders and respect, while disobedience can be any failure to obey authority.
  • defiance
    5.3 6/m
    defiance is bold, open resistance that shows attitude, while disobedience simply means not following rules or orders.
