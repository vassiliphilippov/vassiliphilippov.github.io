disobedience

noun

1.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ə.ˈbiː.dɪ.əns/ (AmE), /dɪs.ə.ˈbiː.dɪ.əns/ (BrE)

Examples

Civil disobedience
Civil disobedience has often been used as a means of protesting unjust laws.

His constant disobedience caused problems at school.
Teachers must address disobedience promptly to maintain classroom order.
