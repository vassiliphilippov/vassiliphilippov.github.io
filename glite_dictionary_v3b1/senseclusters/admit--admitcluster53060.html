admit

verb
allow the possibility of
Pronunciation: / ədˈmɪt / (AmE), / ədˈmɪt / (BrE)
to allow or afford the possibility (usually used with 'of')

Examples

Admits of doubt
Such behavior admits of only one explanation—he must have been desperate.
The evidence admits of various interpretations, so the jury must consider all possibilities.
This theory admits of no exceptions according to current understanding.
