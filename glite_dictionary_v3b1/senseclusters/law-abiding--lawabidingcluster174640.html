law-abiding

adjective
obeying the law
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔː əˌbaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɔː əˌbaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
Obeying and respecting the law; not breaking or disregarding laws or rules.

Examples

A law-abiding citizen
Most law-abiding citizens pay their taxes on time.
She was known as a law-abiding member of her community.
The organization is proud of its reputation as a law-abiding entity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criminal - someone who breaks the law; opposite of law-abiding
