bigmouth

noun
large mouth
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
a person or animal that has a physically large mouth, especially in comparison to others of its kind

Examples

A bigmouth fish
The fish was known as a bigmouth because of its unusually wide jaws.
In the zoo, the hippopotamus opened its bigmouth to show off its teeth.
The sculpture depicted a creature with a comically exaggerated bigmouth.
