uncontradictory

adjective
free of contradiction
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkɒntrəˈdɪktəri / (AmE), / ˌʌnkɒntrəˈdɪktəri / (BrE)
not containing any contradiction; not denied or disputed by others; consistent
