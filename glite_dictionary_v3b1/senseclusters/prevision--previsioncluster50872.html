prevision

noun
prophetic vision or prediction
Pronunciation: / priˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / prɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
a prophetic vision or prediction, especially as revealed in a dream or supernatural experience

Examples

A dream prevision
During the storm, he was haunted by a vivid prevision of disaster.
Some cultures revere individuals who experience prevision as a sign of spiritual power.
The ancient oracle claimed to receive prevision of great events through her dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: premonition - a strong feeling that something is about to happen
