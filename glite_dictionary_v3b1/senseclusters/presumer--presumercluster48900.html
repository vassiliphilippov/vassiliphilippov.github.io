presumer

noun
producer-consumer
8.6
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsuːmər / (AmE), / prɪˈsjuːmə / (BrE)
a person who both produces and consumes goods or services, especially by participating in the creation or design process

Examples

Presumer of products
A presumer might help design a product before it is released to the public.
Many technology companies now seek feedback from presumers during the development stage.
The rise of presumer culture has changed how brands interact with their customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consumer - a person who buys and uses goods or services
  • antonym: producer - a person or company that makes goods or services
  • synonym: prosumer - a person who produces and consumes goods or services
