goetia

noun
spirit in occult ritual
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈiːʃə / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈiːtɪə / (BrE)
a spirit or demon invoked in magical rituals, especially those described in occult grimoires

Legends say that a skilled sorcerer can control a goetia to gain secret knowledge.
