grand duke

noun
a male ruler ranking below a king
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd duk / (AmE), / ɡrænd djuːk / (BrE)
a male ruler of a territory ranking just below a king, often sovereign of a grand duchy or a prince of high rank

Examples

The grand duke rules
A grand duke often enjoyed privileges similar to those of monarchs.
After the king's abdication, the grand duke assumed control over the territory.
The title of grand duke was once held by the ruler of Luxembourg.
