slushie

noun
icy flavored drink
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈslʌʃi / (BrE)
a sweet, flavored drink made of crushed or finely shaved ice, often served cold in a cup

Examples

A cold slushie
On a hot summer afternoon, nothing is more refreshing than a cold slushie from the corner store.
He spilled his blue raspberry slushie all over the car seat.
The kids lined up to buy a slushie after playing soccer in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slush - a drink made from partially melted ice and flavoring
  • antonym: smoothie - a thick, cold drink made from blended fruit and ice
