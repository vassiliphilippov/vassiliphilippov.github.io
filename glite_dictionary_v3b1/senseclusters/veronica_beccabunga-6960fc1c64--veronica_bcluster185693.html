Veronica beccabunga

noun
a European trailing plant with blue flowers
Pronunciation: / vəˈrɑnɪkə ˌbɛkəˈbʌŋɡə / (AmE), / vəˈrɒnɪkə ˌbɛkəˈbʌŋɡə / (BrE)
