aerobically

adverb
with oxygen
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɛˈroʊbɪkli / (AmE), / ɛəˈrəʊbɪkli / (BrE)

Examples

Train aerobically

Swimming aerobically helps improve both heart and lung function.
Doctors recommend exercising aerobically several times a week for better health.
