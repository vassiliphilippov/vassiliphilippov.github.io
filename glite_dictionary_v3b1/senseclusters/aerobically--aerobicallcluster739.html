aerobically

adverb
with oxygen
Pronunciation: / ɛˈroʊbɪkli / (AmE), / ɛəˈrəʊbɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or requires oxygen, especially during physical activity or exercise

Examples

Train aerobically
The athletes trained aerobically to increase their endurance for the marathon.
Swimming aerobically helps improve both heart and lung function.
Doctors recommend exercising aerobically several times a week for better health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anaerobically - in a way that does not require oxygen
