turn one's back on

verb
to ignore or abandon someone or something
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /tɜːrn wʌnz bæk ən/ /; / /tɜːrn wʌns bæk ən/ /; / /tɜːrn wʌnz bæk ɑːn/ (AmE), /tɜːn wʌnz bæk ən/ /; / /tɜːn wʌns bæk ən/ /; / /tɜːn wʌnz bæk ɒn/ (BrE)
