PIR

noun
motion-detecting sensor
Pronunciation: / piː aɪ ɑːr / (AmE), / piː aɪ ɑː / (BrE)
a sensor that detects motion by measuring infrared radiation from objects in its field of view

Examples

PIR sensor
The alarm was triggered when the PIR detected movement in the hallway.
Many modern lighting systems use a PIR to automatically turn on when someone enters the room.
Installing a PIR near the entrance improved the building's security.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infrared sensor - a device that detects infrared radiation
  • synonym: motion sensor - a device that detects movement in an area
