intelligence

noun
secret information about an enemy or rival
1.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtel.ə.dʒəns/ /; / /ɪn.ˈtel.ɪ.dʒəns/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtel.ɪ.dʒəns/ /; / /ɪn.ˈtel.ə.dʒəns/ (BrE)
