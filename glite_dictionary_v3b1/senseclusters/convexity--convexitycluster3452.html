convexity

noun
outward-curving shape or surface
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛksəti /; / kɑnˈvɛksəti / (AmE), / kɒnˈvɛksəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being convex; having a surface that curves or bulges outward, like the outside of a sphere or circle
