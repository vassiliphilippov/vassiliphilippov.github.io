fumet

noun
pleasant aroma
Pronunciation: / fuːˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmeɪ / (BrE)
a pleasant or strong aroma, especially one from cooking or perfume

Examples

Fumet of bread
The fumet of fresh bread drifted from the bakery and tempted everyone on the corner.
A delicate fumet of herbs filled the kitchen as the soup simmered on the stove.
She noticed the subtle fumet of perfume as she entered the elegant room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aroma - a distinctive, typically pleasant smell
  • synonym: fragrance - a sweet or pleasant smell
  • antonym: stench - a very unpleasant smell
