calciferol

noun
a form of vitamin D, prevents rickets
9.5 6.3
Pronunciation: /kæl.sɪ.ˈfɜːr.əl/ (AmE), /kæl.sɪ.ˈfɜː.əl/ (BrE)
a fat-soluble form of vitamin D (vitamin D2) that helps prevent rickets and is important for maintaining healthy bones

Examples

Calciferol vitamin D
Children who lack calciferol in their diet are at greater risk of developing rickets.
Milk is often fortified with calciferol to prevent vitamin D deficiency.
The doctor prescribed calciferol supplements to help improve her bone health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cholecalciferol - vitamin D3, related form of vitamin D
  • synonym: ergocalciferol - another name for calciferol (vitamin D2)
