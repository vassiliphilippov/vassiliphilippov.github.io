loaning

noun
the act of lending money or property
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving money or property to someone with the expectation that it will be returned, usually with interest

Examples

The loaning of money
She was praised for her generosity in the loaning of her books to classmates.
The loaning of money to friends can sometimes lead to awkward situations.
The bank specializes in the loaning of funds to small businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lending - the act of giving something to someone to be returned later
