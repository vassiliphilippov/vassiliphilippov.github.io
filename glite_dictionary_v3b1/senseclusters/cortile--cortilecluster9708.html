cortile

noun
open courtyard
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈtiːleɪ / (AmE), / kɔːˈtiːleɪ / (BrE)
an open courtyard, especially one surrounded by buildings or arcades, often found in Italian architecture

Examples

A cortile courtyard
The villa featured a peaceful cortile where guests could relax in the shade.
Sunlight streamed into the cortile, illuminating the ancient stone walls.
They enjoyed their morning coffee in the quiet cortile behind the house.
