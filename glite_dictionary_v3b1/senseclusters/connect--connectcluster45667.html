Synonyms

  • interconnect
    4.7 0.2/m
    interconnect links systems so they work together across networks and devices, while connect means joining devices or systems by any link.
  • network
    3.2 0.4/m
    network connects computers and devices so they can share information, while connect is a general verb for joining devices or systems.
  • interface
    4.2 1/m
    interface links systems or programs at a point so they exchange data, while connect focuses on making the basic link between devices.
  • link
    1.1 20/m
    link joins two or more things directly, while connect often implies a wider setup or active communication between devices or systems.
  • link up
    3.0 0.5/m
    link up joins people or devices informally or temporarily, while connect is neutral and fits formal technical setups and lasting links.
  • hook up
    3.7 10/m
    hook up attaches cables or sets devices to work together, often informally, while connect is the general technical verb for joining systems.
  • interlink
    4.5 0.2/m
    interlink joins things so they become closely related or dependent, while connect simply means joining devices or systems for communication.
