connect

verb
to plug an electrical device into a power source
3.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈnekt/ (AmE), /kə.ˈnekt/ (BrE)
to join or link by means of communication equipment, networks, or systems (such as phones, computers, or electrical devices)
