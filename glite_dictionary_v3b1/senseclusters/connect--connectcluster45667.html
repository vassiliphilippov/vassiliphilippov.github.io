connect

verb
to plug an electrical device into a power source
3.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkt / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt / (BrE)
to join or link by means of communication equipment, networks, or systems (such as phones, computers, or electrical devices)

Examples

To connect a device
After moving the lamp, she remembered to connect it before turning it on.
Make sure you connect the charger to the outlet or your phone won’t charge.
The technician will connect all of the appliances in the kitchen this afternoon.
Every classroom will soon be connected to the school's network.
The technician will connect your phone line tomorrow morning.
We were unable to connect the two offices because the internet was down.
After dialing the number, it took a moment for the operator to connect the call.
Could you connect me to Mr. Thompson, please?
Our IT department will connect your computer to the company network.

  • synonym: link - to make a physical or electronic connection between things
