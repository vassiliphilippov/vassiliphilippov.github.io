trial

verb
to test something
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəl / (BrE)
to test or try out something to see how well it works before making a final decision

Examples

To trial a product
The company trialed the new software before launching it.
We trialed several teaching methods to find the most effective one for our students.
Before introducing the new menu, the restaurant trialed the dishes with a small group of customers.
The company is trialing a new software update with selected users.
Before launching the product, they trialed it in several small markets.
Researchers are trialing a new method to improve crop yields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evaluate - to judge or assess the value or quality of something
  • synonym: test - to try something to see how well it works
