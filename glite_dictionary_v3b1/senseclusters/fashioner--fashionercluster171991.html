fashioner

noun
creator or maker
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃənər / (AmE), / ˈfæʃənə / (BrE)
a creator or maker, especially used in a literary or archaic sense to refer to God as the creator of the world

Examples

Fashioner of the world
In the ancient hymn, the poet praised the fashioner of the heavens and the earth.
The philosopher wrote about the fashioner who shaped the universe from chaos.
Many cultures have stories about a divine fashioner responsible for the creation of all things.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creator - someone who brings something into existence
  • synonym: maker - a person or being that makes or creates something
