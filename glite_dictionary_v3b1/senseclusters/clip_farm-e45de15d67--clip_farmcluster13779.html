clip farm

noun
service producing many short videos
Pronunciation: / klɪp fɑrm / (AmE), / klɪp fɑːm / (BrE)
a business or service that produces a large volume of short videos, often for social media or online platforms

Examples

A clip farm business
Many influencers now rely on a clip farm to edit and upload their videos efficiently.
The marketing company operated a clip farm to generate viral content for clients.
The rise of the clip farm has changed how short-form videos are distributed online.
