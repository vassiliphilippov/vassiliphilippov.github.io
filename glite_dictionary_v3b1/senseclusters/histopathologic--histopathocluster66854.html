histopathologic

adjective
microscopic tissue study
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstoʊˌpæθəˈlɑːdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəʊˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)

Examples

A histopathologic analysis is essential for diagnosing many types of cancer.
