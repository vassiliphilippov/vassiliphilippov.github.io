backmost

adjective
farthest at the back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈbakməʊst / (BrE)
farthest at or toward the rear of something; situated at the very back.

Examples

The backmost row
Please sit in the backmost row if you don't want to be called on during class.
She found her missing shoe wedged in the backmost corner of the closet.
The library's backmost shelves held rare, old books seldom seen by visitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frontmost - farthest at the front
  • synonym: rearmost - farthest back in position; nearest the rear
