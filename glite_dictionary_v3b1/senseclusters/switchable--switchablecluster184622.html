switchable

adjective
can be switched
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃəbəl /; / ˈswɪtʃəbl / (BrE)
able to be turned on, off, or changed between different states or properties, usually by a switch or external control

Examples

Switchable settings
This lamp is switchable between three different brightness levels.
The system is fully switchable from manual to automatic mode.
Many modern devices are switchable to save energy when not in use.
The new smart windows are switchable between transparent and opaque with the touch of a button.
Scientists are testing switchable materials that respond to changes in temperature.
This device features a switchable mode for both manual and automatic operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustable - able to be changed or adapted
  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or adjusted
