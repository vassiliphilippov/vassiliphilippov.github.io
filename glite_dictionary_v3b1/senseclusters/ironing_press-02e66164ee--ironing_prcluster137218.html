ironing press

noun
appliance for pressing clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnɪŋ prɛs / (AmE), / ˈaɪənɪŋ prɛs / (BrE)
a home appliance for dry or steam pressing clothes, usually with a large heated surface and pressure mechanism

Examples

A ironing press machine
She bought a new ironing press to make her laundry routine faster.
The hotel has an ironing press available for guest use in the laundry room.
Using an ironing press gives clothes a crisp, professional finish with less effort.
