microfossil

noun
a fossil visible only with a microscope
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌfɑsəl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌfɒsɪl / (BrE)
A fossil so small that it can only be studied with a microscope.

Examples

A microfossil under microscope
Analysis of the sediment revealed numerous microfossils previously unknown to science.
Identifying each microfossil required the use of an advanced electron microscope.
The researchers discovered a microfossil that provided clues about early microbial life.
