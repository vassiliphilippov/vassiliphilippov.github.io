eschatological

adjective
relating to the end of the world
Pronunciation: / ˌɛskəˌtɑlɑˈdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛskətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the end of the world or the ultimate destiny of humanity, especially as discussed in theology or philosophy

Examples

Eschatological themes
Her novel explores eschatological themes through a science fiction lens.
Some philosophers debate the eschatological significance of technological advancements.
The preacher delivered an eschatological sermon about the end times and human destiny.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apocalyptic - relating to the end of the world or great destruction
  • synonym: prophetic - relating to prophecy, especially about the future or end times
