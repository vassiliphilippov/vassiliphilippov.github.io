fluid and graceful

adjective
smooth and elegant in movement
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːɪd ənd ˈɡreɪsfəl / (AmE), / ˈfluːɪd ənd ˈɡreɪs.fəl / (BrE)
moving in a way that is smooth and elegant, without abrupt changes or awkwardness

Examples

A fluid and graceful movement
His handwriting is fluid and graceful, almost as if each letter flows into the next.
The ballerina's performance was fluid and graceful, captivating everyone in the audience.
The otter swam with fluid and graceful motions through the clear water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsy - awkward and lacking grace in movement
