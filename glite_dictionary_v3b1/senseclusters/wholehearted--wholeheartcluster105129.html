wholehearted

adjective
completely sincere and enthusiastic
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhəʊlˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
showing or characterized by complete sincerity and enthusiasm; given with total commitment or devotion.

Examples

Wholehearted support
His wholehearted dedication to the project inspired everyone around him.
She gave her wholehearted support to the team throughout the season.
The proposal was met with wholehearted enthusiasm by all the committee members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • antonym: halfhearted - without real effort, interest, or enthusiasm
