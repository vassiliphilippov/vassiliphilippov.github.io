Synonyms

  • scrumptious
    3.7 0.82/m

  • toothsome
    7.2 0.08/m

  • luscious
    4.3 1.42/m
    luscious describes a rich, often sweet texture that tempts the senses, while tasty simply names food that tastes good.
  • flavorful
    2.3 2.25/m
    flavorful says food has a strong or distinctive taste, while tasty focuses on general pleasantness of the flavor.
  • flavorsome
    4.3 0.02/m

  • pleasant-tasting
    3.0 0.01/m
