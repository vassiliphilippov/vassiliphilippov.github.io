tasty

adjective
having a pleasant flavor
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪsti / (AmE), / ˈteɪsti / (BrE)
Having a pleasant flavor; pleasing to the sense of taste.

Examples

A tasty meal
She loves experimenting with spices to make her dishes more tasty.
The chef prepared a tasty meal for the guests.
These crackers are surprisingly tasty for a low-calorie snack.
