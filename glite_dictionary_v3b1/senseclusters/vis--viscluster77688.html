vis

noun
force or strength
9.9
Pronunciation: /vɪs/ (AmE), /vɪs/ (BrE)
a Latin term meaning force or strength, used in legal or academic English to refer to power or effectiveness.

Examples

Vis major
The contract was declared void vis major, meaning due to a superior force.
In legal discussions, the term vis is often used to refer to power or authority.
The phrase 'vis vitae' translates as the force of life, with vis meaning strength.
