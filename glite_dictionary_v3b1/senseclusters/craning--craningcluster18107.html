craning

noun
lifting with a crane
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of lifting or moving heavy objects using a crane

Examples

The craning of beams
The craning of steel beams onto the building site required careful coordination.
Safety measures must be followed during craning to prevent accidents.
The port was busy with the craning of containers onto waiting trucks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoisting - the act of lifting something, especially with mechanical help
  • antonym: lowering - the act of moving something downwards
