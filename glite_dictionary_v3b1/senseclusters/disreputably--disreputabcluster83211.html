disreputably

adverb
in a disreputable manner
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.rɪˈpjuː.təb.li / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.rɪˈpjuː.təb.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes people to have a bad opinion; in a manner that lacks respectability or good reputation.

Examples

Behave disreputably
He behaved disreputably during the negotiations, damaging his company's standing.
She disreputably avoided paying her taxes for several years.
The bar was known for being frequented by disreputably dressed patrons and shady characters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disreputable - not considered to be respectable
  • antonym: respectably - in a respectable manner
