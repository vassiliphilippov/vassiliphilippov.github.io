immune globulin

noun
antibody protein produced in immune response
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmjun ˈɡlɑbjəlɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈmjuːn ˈɡlɒbjʊlɪn / (BrE)
any of a class of proteins produced by lymph tissue in vertebrates that act as antibodies in the immune response
