distempered

adjective
disordered or unhealthy
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtɛmpərd / (AmE), / dɪsˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
in a disturbed, disordered, or unhealthy state, especially referring to mind or health

Examples

Distempered mind
The distempered dog was kept away from the others for safety reasons.
After days of little sleep, he felt distempered and unable to concentrate at work.
The old house had a distempered atmosphere that made visitors uneasy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disturbed - showing signs of mental or emotional trouble
  • antonym: healthy - in good physical or mental condition
  • synonym: unwell - not healthy; ill
