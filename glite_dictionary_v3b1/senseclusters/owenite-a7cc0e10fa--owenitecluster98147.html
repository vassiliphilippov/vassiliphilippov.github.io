Owenite

noun
follower of Robert Owen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊənaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊənaɪt / (BrE)
a supporter or follower of the social and economic ideas of Robert Owen, especially regarding cooperative communities

Examples

An Owenite leader
In the 19th century, many Owenites established experimental communities based on cooperation.
The Owenite philosophy influenced later social reform movements in Europe and America.
As an Owenite, she advocated for better working conditions and communal living.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialist - a person who supports or believes in socialism
  • synonym: utopian - a person who believes in or pursues idealistic social reform
