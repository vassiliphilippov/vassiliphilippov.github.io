foliate

adjective
having or resembling leaves
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.li.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.li.eɪt / (BrE)
having or resembling leaves, or having a specified kind or number of leaves (often used as a combining form).

Examples

Foliate leaves
In botanical terms, a trifoliate plant is one that is foliate with three distinct leaflets.
Many architectural details in the cathedral are foliate, decorated with leaf-like motifs.
The fossil showed a foliate pattern along its edges, resembling delicate leaves.
