iridaceous plant

noun
bulbous plant of the Iridaceae family
7.8 7.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.rɪ.ˈdeɪ.ʃəs plænt/ (AmE), /ɪ.rɪ.ˈdeɪ.ʃəs plɑːnt/ (BrE)
