oil filter

noun
device for cleaning engine oil
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪl ˌfɪltɚ / (AmE), / ˈɔɪl ˌfɪltə / (BrE)
a device that removes impurities from the oil circulating in an internal-combustion engine to protect engine components

Examples

A oil filter device
A clogged oil filter can cause significant damage to your engine over time.
Every car engine needs a clean oil filter to ensure efficient performance.
He replaced the oil filter during the routine maintenance of his vehicle.
