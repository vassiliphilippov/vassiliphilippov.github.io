cosmozoan

noun
believer in panspermia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməˈzoʊən / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈzəʊən / (BrE)
a person who believes that life on Earth originated from outer space, especially as a supporter of the panspermia theory

Examples

A cosmozoan scientist
The scientist, a well-known cosmozoan, presented evidence supporting the idea that life came from space.
As a cosmozoan, he often debated with colleagues who believed life began on Earth itself.
The documentary featured a cosmozoan explaining the theory of panspermia to a general audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abiogenist - a person who believes life originated from non-living matter on Earth
  • synonym: panspermist - a person who supports the theory that life exists throughout the universe and was spread to Earth
