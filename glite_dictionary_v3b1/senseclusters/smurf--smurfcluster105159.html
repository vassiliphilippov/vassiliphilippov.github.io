smurf

verb
to evade financial reporting
9.9
Pronunciation: /smɜːrf/ (AmE), /smɜːf/ (BrE)
to make many small financial transactions to avoid detection or reporting requirements, especially for money laundering.

Examples

To smurf money
Criminals often smurf large sums of money into smaller deposits to avoid bank scrutiny.
The investigators discovered that the suspects had smurfed the funds through dozens of accounts.
To bypass reporting laws, they decided to smurf the cash in several small transactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: launder - to conceal the origins of illegally obtained money
