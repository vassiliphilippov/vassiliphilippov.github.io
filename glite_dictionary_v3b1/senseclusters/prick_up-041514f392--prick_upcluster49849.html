prick up

verb
(of ears) to raise upright; become erect
Pronunciation: / prɪk ʌp / (AmE), / prɪk ʌp / (BrE)
(of the ears) to stand erect or be raised suddenly, especially in alertness or attention.

Examples

To prick up ears
As soon as the horses pricked up their ears, we knew something was approaching.
The dog pricked up his ears at the sound of footsteps outside.
Whenever she hears her name, the cat immediately pricks up her ears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perk up - to become more lively or attentive
