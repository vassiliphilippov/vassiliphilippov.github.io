grassiness

noun
amount of grass
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being covered with or having a lot of grass

Examples

Grassiness of the field
The grassiness of the meadow attracted many wild animals in the spring.
After the rainy season, the grassiness of the fields increased noticeably.
We enjoyed a picnic on the hill, appreciating its soft grassiness underfoot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - the state of being empty of vegetation or life
  • synonym: lushness - the quality of being rich and abundant in vegetation
