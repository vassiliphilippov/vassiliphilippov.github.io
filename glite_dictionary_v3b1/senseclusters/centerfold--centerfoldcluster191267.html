centerfold

noun
magazine's middle foldout page
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɚˌfoʊld /; / ˈsɛntərˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈsɛntəfəʊld /; / ˈsɛntəˌfəʊld / (BrE)
the middle spread of a magazine, especially featuring a large photograph, map, or similar item, often as a foldout
