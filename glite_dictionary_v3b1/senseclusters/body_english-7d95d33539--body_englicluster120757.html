body English

noun
motion to influence ball/object's direction after release
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdi ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ /; / ˈbɑdi ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
