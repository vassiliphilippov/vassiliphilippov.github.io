blight canker

noun
fire blight stage marked by cankers
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /blaɪt ˈkæŋ.kər/ (AmE), /blaɪt ˈkæŋ.kə/ (BrE)
a stage of fire blight disease in plants, marked by the appearance of lesions or cankers on branches or stems
