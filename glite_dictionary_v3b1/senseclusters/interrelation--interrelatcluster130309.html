interrelation

noun
mutual relationship or connection
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntərɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
The way in which two or more things or people are connected and affect each other.

Examples

The interrelation of ideas
Cultural differences can influence the interrelation of individuals in a global workplace.
Scientists studied the interrelation of various factors affecting climate change.
The interrelation between mental health and physical health is well-documented.

Related Terms

  • antonym: isolation - the state of being separate from others
  • synonym: relationship - the way in which two things are connected
