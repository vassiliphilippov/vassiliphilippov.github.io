wireless

noun
system for transmitting signals without wires
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərləs /; / ˈwaɪr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪələs /; / ˈwaɪə.lɪs /; / ˈwaɪəlɪs / (BrE)
