frozen

adjective
fixed and unable to be changed
3.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfroʊ.zən/ (AmE), /ˈfrəʊ.zən/ (BrE)
(of prices, assets, or other values) fixed and unable to be changed, moved, or undone.

Examples

Frozen prices
During the economic crisis, the government ordered frozen prices on essential goods.
My salary has remained frozen for three years, despite rising living costs.
The bank accounts were frozen pending the outcome of the investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fixed - securely placed or fastened; not able to be changed
  • antonym: fluid - able to change or easily move; not fixed
