gallery

noun
spectators at a golf or tennis match
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləri / (AmE), / ˈɡaləri / (BrE)
the spectators at a golf or tennis match, considered collectively

Examples

The tennis gallery
Excited applause broke out from the gallery after the tennis player's ace.
He gestured to the gallery to quiet down before serving for match point.
The gallery watched in silence as the final putt rolled toward the hole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience - the assembled spectators or listeners at a public event
  • synonym: crowd - a large number of people gathered together, often as spectators
  • antonym: players - the participants in a game, in contrast to the gallery (the spectators)
