crabbily

adverb
in an irritable way
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbɪli / (AmE), / ˈkræbɪli / (BrE)
in a bad-tempered, irritable, or grouchy manner

Examples

To crabbily complain
She crabbily told her coworkers to stop making so much noise in the office.
After missing the bus, he crabbily complained about the city's public transport.
The child crabbily refused to eat his vegetables at dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive manner
  • synonym: irritably - in an easily annoyed or angry manner
