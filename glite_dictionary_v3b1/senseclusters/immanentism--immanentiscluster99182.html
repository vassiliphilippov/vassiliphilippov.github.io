immanentism

noun
belief in divine within
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmənəntɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɪmənəntɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief that everything, including the divine or ultimate reality, exists within the material or natural world and not outside it
