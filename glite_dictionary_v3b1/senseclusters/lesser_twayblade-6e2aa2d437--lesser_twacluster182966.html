lesser twayblade

noun
a small orchid with two leaves and greenish or reddish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsər ˈtweɪˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈtweɪbleɪd / (BrE)
a small orchid with two triangular leaves and a short, loose cluster of green to reddish flowers, found in Europe, Asia, North America, and Greenland
