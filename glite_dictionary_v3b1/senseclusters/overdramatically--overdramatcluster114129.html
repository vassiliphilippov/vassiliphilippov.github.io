overdramatically

adverb
excessively dramatic manner
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚdrəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvədrəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is more dramatic or exaggerated than necessary or appropriate

Examples

Act overdramatically
She threw her hands in the air overdramatically when she lost the game.
He overdramatically recounted the story, making everyone laugh at his performance.
The actor overdramatically delivered his lines, drawing attention from the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggeratedly - in an exaggerated or overstated way
  • antonym: subtly - in a delicate or understated way
