cinder block

noun
lightweight concrete building block
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndɚ blɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɪndə blɒk / (BrE)
A lightweight concrete building block made with cinder as aggregate, commonly used in construction.

Examples

A cinder block wall
He used a cinder block to keep the door propped open during the move.
Many garages and basements have cinder block walls due to their strength and affordability.
The garden wall was built from sturdy cinder blocks stacked with mortar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeze block - a British term for a lightweight concrete block
