choreograph

verb

3.9 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.iː.ə.græf/ /; / /ˈkɔːr.ɪ.ə.græf/ (AmE), /ˈkɒr.iː.ə.grɑːf/ /; / /ˈkɒr.ɪ.ə.græf/ (BrE)

Examples
