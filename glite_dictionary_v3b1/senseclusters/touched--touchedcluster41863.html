touched

adjective
emotionally affected; moved
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃt / (AmE), / tʌtʃt / (BrE)
deeply affected by emotion, especially in a way that moves one to respond

Examples

Touched by kindness
He looked touched as the audience applauded his performance.
I felt genuinely touched when they remembered my birthday.
She was clearly touched by the heartfelt letter he sent her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moved - feeling strong emotion, especially sympathy or sadness
