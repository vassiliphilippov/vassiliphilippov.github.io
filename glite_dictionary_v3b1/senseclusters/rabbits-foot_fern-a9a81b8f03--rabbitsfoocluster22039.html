rabbit's-foot fern

noun
tropical fern with scaly rhizomes and bluish-green fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪts fʊt fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈræbɪts fʊt fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical fern with brown, scaly rhizomes, cultivated for its large, deeply lobed bluish-green fronds, native to the Americas
