raw beauty

noun
striking, natural beauty
Pronunciation: / rɔ ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / rɔː ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
beauty that is striking and impressive in its natural, unrefined state

Examples

Raw beauty of nature
Standing at the edge of the canyon, I was awestruck by its raw beauty.
There is a raw beauty in the artist's unpolished work that captivates viewers.
Travelers are often drawn to Iceland for the raw beauty of its volcanic landscapes.
