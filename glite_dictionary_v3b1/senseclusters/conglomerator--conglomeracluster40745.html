conglomerator

noun
grouping organizer
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡlɑːmɚˌeɪtɚ / (AmE), / kənˈɡlɒməˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or device that brings together different things or companies into a single group or entity

Examples

The conglomerator acquires companies
The conglomerator acquired several smaller firms to expand its market reach.
A modern conglomerator can merge data from multiple sources into one report.
As a skilled conglomerator, she brought together experts from different fields for the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregator - a person or tool that collects and combines items or information
  • antonym: separator - a person or device that divides things into separate parts
