nutraceutical

adjective
health-benefit foods
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to foods or products that provide health benefits in addition to basic nutrition

Examples

Nutraceutical products
Many nutraceutical supplements claim to improve heart health and boost immunity.
The nutraceutical industry is growing rapidly as people seek natural ways to stay healthy.
Some yogurts are marketed as nutraceutical foods because they contain added probiotics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: functional - designed to have a specific health benefit
  • synonym: medicinal - having healing properties
