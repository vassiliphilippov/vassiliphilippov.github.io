cupboard love

noun
affection shown for personal gain
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌbɚd lʌv / (AmE), / ˈkʌbəd lʌv / (BrE)
affection that is expressed in order to obtain a reward or personal gain, rather than from genuine feeling

Examples

cupboard love for treats
He suspected her sudden cupboard love was only a trick to get more dessert.
The toddler’s cupboard love vanished as soon as the cookies were gone.
Their friendship sometimes felt like cupboard love, based more on favors than affection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genuine love - love that is sincere and not motivated by gain
  • synonym: insincere - not expressing genuine feelings
