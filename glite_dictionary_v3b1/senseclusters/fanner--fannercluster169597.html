fanner

noun
instigator of conflict
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfænər / (AmE), / ˈfænə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately stirs up or encourages strong feelings, activity, or conflict

Examples

Fanner of trouble
The journalist was called a fanner of controversy after publishing the critical article.
Some accused the politician of being a fanner of division during the heated debate.
Online trolls often act as fanners by spreading rumors and provoking arguments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitator - a person who urges others to protest or rebel
  • synonym: instigator - a person who initiates or provokes something
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to create peace or resolve conflict
