examen

noun
detailed spiritual self-examination
Pronunciation: / ɪgˈzæmən / (AmE), / ɪgˈzɑːmən / (BrE)
A formal and detailed self-examination of conscience, especially as practiced daily by Jesuits.

Examples

Daily examen practice
Each evening, the Jesuit priest performed his examen to reflect on the day's blessings and failings.
Practicing the examen has become an essential part of her spiritual routine.
The retreat included instructions on how to incorporate the examen into daily life.
