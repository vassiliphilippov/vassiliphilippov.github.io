unsoured

adjective
not become sour or spoiled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaʊɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaʊəd / (BrE)
(of food or liquid) not having become sour or spoiled.

Examples

The unsoured cream
Check to make sure the cream is unsoured before adding it to the sauce.
She prefers the taste of unsoured yogurt in her recipes.
The milk was still unsoured after sitting out overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fresh - recently made or obtained; not spoiled
  • antonym: soured - having become sour or turned bad
