office building

noun
building with multiple offices
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːfɪs ˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs ˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
A building that contains offices where business or professional work is carried out.

Examples
