hide-and-seek

noun
children's hiding game
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdənˈsiːk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪd ən ˈsiːk / (BrE)
a children's game in which one player closes their eyes and counts while the others hide, then tries to find them

Examples

Play hide-and-seek game
He counted to twenty before setting off to find his friends in hide-and-seek.
The children spent hours playing hide-and-seek in the backyard.
When it started to rain, their game of hide-and-seek quickly moved indoors.
