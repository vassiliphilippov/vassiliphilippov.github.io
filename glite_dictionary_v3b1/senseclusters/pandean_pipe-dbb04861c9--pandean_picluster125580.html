pandean pipe

noun
primitive wind instrument made of parallel pipes
7.3 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.di.ən paɪp / (AmE), / ˈpandiːən paɪp / (BrE)
a primitive wind instrument made of several parallel pipes of gradually increasing length, bound together and played by blowing across the tops

Examples

A pandean pipe instrument

He crafted a pandean pipe from reeds he found by the river.
The shepherd played a soft melody on his pandean pipe as dusk fell over the hills.
