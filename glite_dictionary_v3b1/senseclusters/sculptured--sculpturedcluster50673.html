sculptured

adjective
shaped by carving or cutting
2.2 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskʌlp.tʃərd/ /; / /ˈskʌl.tʃərd/ (AmE), /ˈskʌlp.tʃəd/ /; / /ˈskʌl.tʃəd/ (BrE)
shaped or formed as if by sculpture; carefully carved, molded, or designed, either literally or in appearance
