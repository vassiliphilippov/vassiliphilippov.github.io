inflatus

adjective
swollen or distended
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪtəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtəs / (BrE)
swollen or distended, especially as a result of internal pressure, often used in medical or anatomical contexts

Examples

Inflatus abdomen
The surgeon noted that the patient's abdomen was inflatus after the procedure.
In rare cases, an inflatus bladder can cause severe discomfort and require immediate attention.
The medical report described the tissue as inflatus due to trapped air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflated - reduced in size or pressure
  • synonym: distended - swollen due to internal pressure
  • synonym: swollen - enlarged or puffed up, often due to fluid
