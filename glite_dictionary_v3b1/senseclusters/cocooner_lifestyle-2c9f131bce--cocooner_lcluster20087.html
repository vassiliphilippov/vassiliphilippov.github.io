cocooner lifestyle

noun
comfort-focused, stay-at-home lifestyle
6.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈkunər ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / kəˈkuːnə ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living that emphasizes comfort, relaxation, and staying at home rather than seeking outside social activities.

Examples

The cocooner lifestyle
As remote work becomes more common, the cocooner lifestyle appeals to people seeking comfort indoors.
During the rainy season, I fully adopted a cocooner lifestyle and spent weekends reading at home.
Many people turned to a cocooner lifestyle during the pandemic, finding solace in nesting at home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrovert - a person who enjoys socializing and external activities, opposite of a homebody
  • synonym: hermit - a person living in solitude, often for comfort or religious reasons
