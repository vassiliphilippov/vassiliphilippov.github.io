dried fruit

noun
fruit preserved by drying
1.0
Pronunciation: / draɪd fruːt / (AmE), / draɪd fruːt / (BrE)
Fruit that has been preserved by having most of its water content removed through drying.

Examples

Dried fruit snack
Dried fruit such as apricots and raisins can be a healthy snack option.
She bought a variety of dried fruit to use in her homemade granola.
The recipe calls for mixing nuts and dried fruit into the batter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh fruit - fruit that has not been dried or preserved
