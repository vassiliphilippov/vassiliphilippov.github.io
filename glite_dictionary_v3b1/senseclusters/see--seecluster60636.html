see

verb
visit a place for entertainment or sightseeing
Pronunciation: / siː / (AmE), / siː / (BrE)
to visit a place or attend an event, especially for entertainment or as a tourist

Tourists from all over the world come to see the Great Wall of China.

Have you ever seen a Broadway show in New York?
Millions of people will see the championship broadcast live.
We decided to see a movie together on Friday night.
