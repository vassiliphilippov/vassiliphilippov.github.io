buzzer-beater

noun
last-moment successful shot before a buzzer
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌzɚ ˌbiːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌzə ˌbiːtə / (BrE)
a successful shot in a sporting event, especially basketball, that is made just before the game or period ends and the buzzer sounds

Examples

A buzzer-beater shot
Every young basketball player dreams of hitting the perfect buzzer-beater one day.
He sank a buzzer-beater to clinch the victory for his team.
The crowd erupted when she made that incredible buzzer-beater at the end of the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: game-winner - a shot or play that results in winning the game
