buzzer-beater

noun
last-moment successful shot before a buzzer
5.8 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌz.ər ˈbiː.tər/ /; / /ˈbʌz.ər ˈbɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈbʌz.ə ˈbiː.tə/ /; / /ˈbʌz.ə ˈbɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a successful shot in a sporting event, especially basketball, that is made just before the game or period ends and the buzzer sounds

Examples
