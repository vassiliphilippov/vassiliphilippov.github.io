hazel

noun
wood from a hazel tree
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
The fine-grained wood obtained from a hazel tree (genus Corylus) or the Australian hazel (genus Pomaderris), used especially for making walking sticks and wickerwork.

Examples

Hazel wood furniture
Baskets woven from hazel are both strong and flexible.
The artisan carved a beautiful cane from hazel.
They gathered branches of hazel for the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hazelnut - the nut of the hazel tree
