thankful

adjective
feeling or expressing gratitude
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθæŋkfəl / (AmE), / ˈθaŋkfəl / (BrE)
feeling or expressing gratitude; pleased and relieved about something

Examples

To be thankful
After finding his lost wallet, he was extremely thankful to the person who returned it.
I am thankful that the weather stayed clear for our outdoor wedding.
She felt truly thankful for all the support she received during her recovery.
