atactic

adjective
lacking motor coordination (medical)
Pronunciation: / əˈtæktɪk / (AmE), / əˈtaktɪk / (BrE)
lacking motor coordination, typically as a result of ataxia; relating to or characterized by uncoordinated movements.

Examples

An atactic posture
Certain cerebellar diseases can result in an atactic posture.
Neurologists observed atactic movements in her upper limbs during the examination.
The patient's gait was described as atactic, with frequent unsteady steps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ataxia - the loss of full control of bodily movements
