transplacement

noun
replacement by substitution
9.3
Pronunciation: / trænsˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / trænsˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
the act of replacing one thing with another, especially by putting a substitute in the original position

Examples

Transplacement of equipment
The transplacement of the old statue with a modern sculpture changed the look of the park.
Her careful transplacement of the missing book ensured the library's collection was complete.
The engineer oversaw the transplacement of outdated equipment with new, more efficient machines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replacement - the act of substituting one thing for another
  • synonym: substitution - the act of putting one thing in place of another
