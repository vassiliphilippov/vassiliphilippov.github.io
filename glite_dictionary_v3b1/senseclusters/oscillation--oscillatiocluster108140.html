oscillation

noun
repeated movement or change between states
7.2 1.89/m

the process of moving repeatedly between two positions, states, or conditions, often in a regular rhythm

Examples

Mood oscillation
She noticed a regular oscillation between optimism and pessimism in his attitudes.
The oscillation of the stock market made investors uneasy.
The fan's oscillation helped cool the entire room.

Synonyms

  • periodic motion
    4.1 0.01/m

  • vibration
    3.2 7.26/m
    vibration is a rapid shaking or small back-and-forth movement, while oscillation is a broader term for repeated movement between states.
