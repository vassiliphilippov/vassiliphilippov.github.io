merde

noun
offensive insult
Pronunciation: / mɛrd / (AmE), / mɛəd / (BrE)
an offensive term for a contemptible or worthless person, borrowed from French slang for excrement

Examples

Called him a merde
He stormed out of the meeting, calling his boss a merde in front of everyone.
Some people use the word merde as a harsh insult when they are angry.
She was offended when he referred to her as a merde after their argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
  • synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • synonym: scum - a person regarded as despicable or worthless
