bungee jumping

noun
jumping from a height with an elastic cord
3.1 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌndʒi ˌdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌndʒi ˌdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
