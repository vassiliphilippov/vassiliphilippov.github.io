closer

noun
person who closes something
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzər / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzə / (BrE)
a person who closes something, such as a building, business, or event, especially at the end of a day or activity

Examples

Night closer at the store
After everyone else leaves, the closer checks each room to make sure all the lights are off.
She works as a closer at the local café, locking up after everyone leaves.
The closer is responsible for setting the store alarm each night.
