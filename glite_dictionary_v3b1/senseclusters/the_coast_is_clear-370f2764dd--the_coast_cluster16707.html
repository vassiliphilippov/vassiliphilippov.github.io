the coast is clear

x
safe to proceed; no danger present
Pronunciation: / ðə koʊst ɪz klɪr / (AmE), / ðə kəʊst ɪz klɪə / (BrE)
used to indicate that it is safe to proceed or take action because there is no danger of being caught or observed

Examples

When the coast is clear
He peeked around the corner and whispered that the coast is clear.
Wait until the coast is clear before you open the door.
We can leave now—the coast is clear and no one is watching.
