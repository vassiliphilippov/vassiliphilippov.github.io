quad

noun
muscle at the front of the thigh
6.1
Pronunciation: / kwɑd / (AmE), / kwɒd / (BrE)
the large muscle at the front of the thigh that is responsible for extending the leg at the knee; short for quadriceps

Examples

Strong quad muscle
After months of rehabilitation, his quad finally regained its strength.
She felt a sharp pain in her quad after the uphill sprint.
Stretching your quads before running can help prevent injuries.
