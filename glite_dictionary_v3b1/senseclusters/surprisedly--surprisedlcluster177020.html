surprisedly

adverb
in a surprised manner
Pronunciation: / sərˈpraɪzɪdli / (AmE), / səˈprʌɪzɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows surprise; in the manner of someone who is surprised.

Examples

Looked up surprisedly
He looked up surprisedly when his name was called.
She surprisedly blurted out her response before realizing it.
The cat glanced surprisedly at the sudden noise from the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surprisingly - in a way that causes surprise or is unexpected
