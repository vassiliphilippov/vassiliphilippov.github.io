canebrake rattlesnake

noun
a venomous rattlesnake of the southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnˌbreɪk ˈrætəlˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnbrɛɪk ˈrætəlsneɪk / (BrE)
a large venomous rattlesnake (Crotalus horridus) found in the southeastern United States, typically inhabiting wooded areas and canebrakes
