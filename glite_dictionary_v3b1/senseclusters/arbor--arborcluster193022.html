arbor

noun
garden structure for climbing plants
6.7 1.11/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ërb…ôr /; / Àà…ëÀêrb…ôr / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀêb…ô / (BrE)
a framework in a garden or park that supports climbing plants, often creating a shaded area beneath

Examples

Garden arbor
A wooden arbor in the backyard was covered with blooming roses.
The vines climbed high over the arbor, forming a perfect green canopy.
They sat beneath the arbor to escape the midday sun.
She dreamed of building a beautiful arbour in her garden where she could read in peace.
The old wooden arbour at the end of the path was covered in wisteria and ivy.
They sat together beneath the arbour, enjoying the cool shade and the scent of roses.
