root

noun
origin or source; where something begins
1.0
Pronunciation: / ruːt /; / rut / (AmE), / ruːt / (BrE)
the place, source, or origin of something; the underlying cause or beginning

Examples

Jazz music's roots can be traced back to African and Caribbean rhythms.

Researchers are still studying the roots of this disease.
The roots of this tradition can be traced back to ancient times.
Understanding the root of the problem is essential for finding a solution.
