right-handedness

noun
preference for using the right hand
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪtˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌraɪtˈhændɪdnəs / (BrE)
the preference for using the right hand for tasks and activities, such as writing or eating

Examples

Right-handedness preference
Her right-handedness made it easier for her to use standard scissors.
Studies have explored the genetic aspects of right-handedness and left-handedness.
The teacher noticed his right-handedness when he picked up a pencil to write.
