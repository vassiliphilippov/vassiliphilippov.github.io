bioreactor

noun
device for cell growth
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊriˈæktər / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊriˈæktə / (BrE)
a vessel or device used to grow living cells or microorganisms under controlled conditions, often for producing substances, research, or treating waste
