turacou

noun
large crested African bird
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊr.ə.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʊər.əkəʊ / (BrE)
A large, brightly crested bird native to Africa, known for its vivid plumage and strong flight.

Examples

A turacou bird
A flock of turacous flew over the river, their crests glowing in the sunlight.
Bird enthusiasts visited the reserve hoping to spot a rare turacou among the trees.
The forests of East Africa are home to the colorful turacou.
