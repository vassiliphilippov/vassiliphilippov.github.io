sistrum

noun
ritual musical rattle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstrəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪstrəm / (BrE)
an ancient musical instrument with a handle and metal parts that is shaken to make a jingling sound, used in religious or ritual ceremonies, especially in African and Egyptian traditions
