ISI

noun
Pakistan's main intelligence agency
7.8
Pronunciation: / aɪ ɛs aɪ / (AmE), / aɪ ɛs aɪ / (BrE)
the Inter-Services Intelligence; the main intelligence agency of Pakistan, known for its significant influence on the country's military and political affairs and its links with various groups in the region.

Examples

The ISI agency
Many analysts believe the ISI operates with considerable independence from Pakistan's civilian government.
Reports suggest the ISI provided support to certain militant groups in Afghanistan.
The ISI has been accused of interfering in neighboring countries' internal affairs.
