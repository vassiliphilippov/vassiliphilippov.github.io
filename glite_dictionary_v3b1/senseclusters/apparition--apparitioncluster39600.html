apparition

noun
an unexpected appearance
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌapəˈrɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act of appearing or becoming visible, especially in a sudden or unexpected way

Examples

Sudden apparition
The magician's trick relied on the apparition of objects seemingly out of nowhere.
The sudden apparition of a deer startled the hikers on the quiet trail.
The villagers spoke for years about the apparition that appeared at dusk near the old mill.
