sword lily

noun
plant with sword-shaped leaves and striking flowers
Pronunciation: / sɔrd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / sɔːd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Gladiolus, with sword-shaped leaves and tall spikes of bright, showy flowers

Examples

Bright sword lilies
Gardeners often plant sword lilies for their dramatic foliage and long-lasting flowers.
