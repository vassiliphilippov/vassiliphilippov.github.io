cribbage board

noun
scoring board used in cribbage
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪbɪdʒ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪbɪdʒ bɔːd / (BrE)
A board with regularly spaced holes and pegs, used for keeping score in the card game cribbage.

Examples

A cribbage board game
After each hand, players moved their pegs along the cribbage board to keep track of the score.
She placed the cribbage board and a deck of cards on the table for their weekly game night.
The antique cribbage board had beautifully carved details and colorful pegs.
