silkiness

noun
the quality of being silky
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkinəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being smooth, soft, and glossy like silk

Examples

The silkiness of hair
He admired the silkiness of her hair as he ran his fingers through it.
The silkiness of the scarf made it feel luxurious against her skin.
The chocolate had an unexpected silkiness that delighted everyone at the table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roughness - the quality of being rough or coarse to the touch
  • synonym: smoothness - the quality of having a smooth surface or texture
