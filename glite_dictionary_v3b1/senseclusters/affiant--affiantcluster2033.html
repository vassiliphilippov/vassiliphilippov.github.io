affiant

noun
person making a sworn affidavit
6.7
Pronunciation: / əˈfaɪənt / (AmE), / əˈfaɪənt / (BrE)
A person who makes a sworn written statement (an affidavit) for use as evidence in court.

Examples

The affiant signs affidavit
Each affiant attests that the information is accurate under penalty of perjury.
The affiant signed the statement in the presence of a notary public.
The court reviewed the evidence provided by the affiant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deponent - person who gives sworn evidence or deposition
