gain a life of its own

verb
become uncontrollable or self-sustaining
5.1 6.2
Pronunciation: /geɪn ə laɪf əv ɪts oʊn/ (AmE), /geɪn ə laɪf əv ɪts əʊn/ (BrE)
(of an idea, rumor, project, etc.) to become independent and uncontrollable, often growing or spreading beyond its original context or intent.

Examples

To gain a life of its own
Sometimes a simple idea can gain a life of its own and become much bigger than expected.
The project gained a life of its own after attracting attention from major investors.
The story began as a joke but soon gained a life of its own, spreading across the internet.
