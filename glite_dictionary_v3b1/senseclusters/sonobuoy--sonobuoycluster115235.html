sonobuoy

noun
underwater sound detector
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑnəˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɒnəˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a floating device used to detect and transmit underwater sounds, often for military or research purposes

Examples

A sonobuoy device
The navy deployed a sonobuoy to track the movement of submarines in the area.
Researchers used a sonobuoy to record the sounds of whales migrating along the coast.
After detecting unusual noises, the team quickly launched a sonobuoy for further investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buoy - a floating device used as a marker or signal at sea
  • synonym: hydrophone - an underwater microphone for detecting sound
