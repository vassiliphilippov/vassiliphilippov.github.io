boost

noun
act of lifting or pushing upward
1.8 0.61/m
Pronunciation: / bust / (AmE), / buÀêst / (BrE)
an act of lifting or pushing someone or something upward, especially to help them reach a higher place

Examples

A boost over the wall
Can you give me a boost so I can reach the top shelf?
She needed a boost to climb over the wall during the hike.
The child received a gentle boost from his father to get onto the swing.

Synonyms

  • lift
    1.0 1.08/m
    lift is a neutral action of moving something upward, while boost implies an extra push used to help reach a higher place.
  • raise
    2.9 0.67/m
    raise often means lifting something to a new position in a formal way, while boost suggests giving an extra push to help reach higher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lift - an act of raising someone or something to a higher position
