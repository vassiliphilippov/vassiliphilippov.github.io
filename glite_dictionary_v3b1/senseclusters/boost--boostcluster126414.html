boost

noun
act of lifting or pushing upward
1.8 0.6/m
Pronunciation: / bust / (AmE), / buÀêst / (BrE)
an act of lifting or pushing someone or something upward, especially to help them reach a higher place

Examples

A boost over the wall
Can you give me a boost so I can reach the top shelf?
She needed a boost to climb over the wall during the hike.
The child received a gentle boost from his father to get onto the swing.
