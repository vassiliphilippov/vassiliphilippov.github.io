muslin

noun
light, plain cotton fabric
6.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌz.lɪn/ /; / /ˈmʌz.ən/ (AmE), /ˈmʌz.lɪn/ /; / /ˈmʌz.ən/ (BrE)
A lightweight, finely woven cotton fabric, typically plain in weave and used especially for clothing and curtains.

Examples

Lightweight muslin fabric
Artists often use muslin to stretch over wooden frames for painting backdrops.
She bought several yards of muslin to make new curtains for the living room.
The delicate dress was made of white muslin and adorned with tiny flowers.
