valedictory

noun
farewell speech at graduation
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌvæl.əˈdɪk.tɔr.i / (AmE), / ˌvæl.ɪˈdɪk.təri / (BrE)

Examples

A valedictory at graduation

She delivered a heartfelt valedictory at her high school graduation ceremony.
The university president praised the valedictory given by the top student of the class.
