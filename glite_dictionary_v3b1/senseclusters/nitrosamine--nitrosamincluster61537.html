nitrosamine

noun
carcinogenic chemical compound
Pronunciation: / naɪˈtroʊsəˌmiːn / (AmE), / naɪˈtrɒsəˌmiːn /; / nɪˈtrɒsəˌmiːn / (BrE)
a chemical compound containing a nitroso group attached to an amine, often formed from nitrites and amines, some of which are known to cause cancer
