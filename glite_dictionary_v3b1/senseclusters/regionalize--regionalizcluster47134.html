regionalize

verb
to restrict treatment area
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.dʒə.nə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈriː.dʒə.nə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to limit medical treatment or anesthesia to a specific region of the body

Examples

To regionalize anesthesia
The doctor decided to regionalize the anesthesia to avoid affecting the patient's entire body.
Modern techniques allow surgeons to regionalize pain management for certain procedures.
By choosing to regionalize the treatment, the medical team minimized side effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: localize - to restrict something to a particular area
