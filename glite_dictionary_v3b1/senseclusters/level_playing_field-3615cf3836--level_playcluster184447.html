level playing field

noun
fair and equal conditions for competition
5.7 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlev.əl ˈpleɪ.ɪŋ fiːld/ (AmE), /ˈlev.əl ˈpleɪ.ɪŋ fiːld/ (BrE)

Examples

Government subsidies help create a level playing field for local businesses competing with international firms.
The new regulations will ensure a level playing field for small and large companies alike.
We must work together to build a level playing field in the job market.
