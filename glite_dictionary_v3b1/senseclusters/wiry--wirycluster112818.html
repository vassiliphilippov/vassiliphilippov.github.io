wiry

adjective
stiff, coarse hair texture
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪəri / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəri / (BrE)
(of hair) stiff and coarse like wire.

Examples

Wiry hair
He ran his hands through his wiry beard, scratching his chin thoughtfully.
Her wiry red hair stood out in tight, springy curls.
The dog's wiry fur made it difficult to brush smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarse - rough in texture; not smooth or soft
