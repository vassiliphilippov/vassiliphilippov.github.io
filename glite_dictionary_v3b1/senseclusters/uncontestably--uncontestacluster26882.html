uncontestably

adverb
without dispute
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈtɛstəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈtɛstəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be disputed or challenged

Examples

An uncontestably strong case
The evidence was uncontestably in favor of the defendant's innocence.
He is uncontestably the most experienced member of the team.
The painting is uncontestably a masterpiece of modern art.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arguably - in a way that can be supported by argument or debate
  • synonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be doubted or argued against
