prose poem

noun
prose with poetic qualities
Pronunciation: / proʊz poʊəm / (AmE), / prəʊz pəʊəm / (BrE)
A piece of writing in prose that has the structural or rhythmic qualities of poetry.

Examples

A prose poem about life
His latest book features a prose poem about city life.
She read a prose poem that evoked the changing seasons.
The prose poem blurs the line between poetry and narrative.
