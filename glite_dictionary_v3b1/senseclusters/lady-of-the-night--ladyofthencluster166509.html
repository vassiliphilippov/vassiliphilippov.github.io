lady-of-the-night

noun
West Indian shrub with fragrant flowers
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌleɪ.di əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌleɪ.di əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
a West Indian shrub (Brunfelsia americana) with fragrant, showy yellowish-white flowers, often blooming at night
