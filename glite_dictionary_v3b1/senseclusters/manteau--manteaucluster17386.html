manteau

noun
layer or covering
9.9
Pronunciation: / mænˈtoʊ / (AmE), / mænˈtəʊ / (BrE)
a layer or covering that surrounds something, especially the Earth's mantle or a figurative role or responsibility

Examples

Earth's manteau
Geologists study the Earth's manteau to understand how heat moves inside the planet.
The leader took on the manteau of responsibility after the founder retired.
A thick manteau of clouds covered the mountain peaks all morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: covering - something that covers or encloses
  • synonym: mantle - a layer of the Earth between the crust and the core; a covering or role
