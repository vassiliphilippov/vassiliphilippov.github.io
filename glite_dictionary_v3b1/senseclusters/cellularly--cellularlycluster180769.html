cellularly

adverb
in cell-like units
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛljələrli / (AmE), / ˈsɛljʊləli / (BrE)
in a way that is organized into small, separate units or compartments, like cells.

Examples

Organized cellularly into teams
The organization was structured cellularly to allow each team to operate independently.
In biology, tissues are often described cellularly based on their microscopic structure.
The new security system is designed cellularly, so a breach in one area does not affect the others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modularly - in a way that uses separate modules or units
