jack up

verb
increase or raise significantly
Pronunciation: / dʒæk ʌp / (AmE), / dʒæk ʌp / (BrE)
to increase or raise something, especially by a large amount or suddenly

Examples

To jack up prices
Management plans to jack up production to meet increased demand.
The landlord decided to jack up the rent without warning.
They often jack up the prices during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lower - to make something less in amount, level, or strength
  • synonym: raise - to increase the amount, level, or strength of something
