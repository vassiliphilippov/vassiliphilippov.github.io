pistil

noun
female reproductive part of a flower
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪstəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪstɪl / (BrE)
the female reproductive part of a flower, typically consisting of the ovary, style, and stigma

Examples

The pistil of flower
Bees transfer pollen to the pistil during the process of pollination.
The pistil is essential for seed development in flowering plants.
The botanist dissected the flower to examine its pistil under a microscope.
