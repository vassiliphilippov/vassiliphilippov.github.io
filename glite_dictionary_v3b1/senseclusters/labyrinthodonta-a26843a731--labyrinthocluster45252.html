Labyrinthodonta

noun
extinct group of prehistoric amphibians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlæb.əˌrɪn.θəˈdɒn.tə /; / ˌlæbəˌrɪnθəˈdɑntə / (AmE), / ˌlæb.ɪˌrɪn.θəˈdɒn.tə /; / ˌlæbərɪnθəˈdɒntə / (BrE)
an extinct group of large amphibians with solid, flattened skulls and conical teeth, that lived from the Devonian to Triassic periods
