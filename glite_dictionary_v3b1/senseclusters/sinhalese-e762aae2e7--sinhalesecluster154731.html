Sinhalese

noun
a person from the largest ethnic group of Sri Lanka
6.6
Pronunciation: / sɪn.həˈliz / (AmE), / sɪn.həˈliːz / (BrE)
a member of the largest ethnic group native to Sri Lanka, known for their own language and culture

Examples

A Sinhalese person
Many Sinhalese people speak both Sinhala and English fluently.
The Sinhalese have a rich tradition of dance and music.
The capital city, Colombo, is home to a large Sinhalese community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Tamil - a member of the other major ethnic group native to Sri Lanka and southern India
