cementless

adjective
without cement
6.5
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛntləs / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛntləs / (BrE)
not using or containing cement, especially referring to medical implants fixed without cement.

Examples

cementless knee implant
The doctor recommended a cementless knee implant for the younger patient.
Advances in cementless joint replacements have improved recovery times for many people.
Some surgeons prefer cementless procedures because they allow for natural bone growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cemented - fixed or attached using cement
