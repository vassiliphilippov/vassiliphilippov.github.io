maldigestion

noun
impaired digestion
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmældɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / ˌmældɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
impaired or abnormal digestion of food in the digestive system

Examples

Severe maldigestion symptoms
Patients with maldigestion may experience bloating, gas, and abdominal pain after eating.
A doctor can help diagnose the cause of maldigestion and recommend appropriate treatment.
Chronic maldigestion can lead to nutritional deficiencies if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: digestion - the process of breaking down food in the body
  • synonym: indigestion - discomfort or pain in the stomach associated with difficulty in digesting food
