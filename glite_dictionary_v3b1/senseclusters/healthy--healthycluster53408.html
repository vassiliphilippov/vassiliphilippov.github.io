healthy

adjective
in good physical or mental condition
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθi / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi / (BrE)
having good health in body or mind; free from illness or injury

Examples

Healthy body
Having a healthy mind is just as important as having a healthy body.
She eats a healthy diet and exercises every day.
The doctor said that my heart is healthy for my age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fit - in good physical health, especially as a result of exercise
  • antonym: unhealthy - not healthy; likely to make you ill or sick
