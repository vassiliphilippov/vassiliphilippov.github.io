project

verb
draw a projection of something
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛkt / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkt / (BrE)
To draw or represent an object or figure by means of projection, as in technical drawing or geometry.

Examples

To project a shape
Students are learning how to project a cube onto a plane in their geometry class.
The architect projected the building's layout onto paper to show the elevation view.
The engineer used specialized software to project the three-dimensional structure onto a two-dimensional blueprint.
