motto

noun
guiding phrase or slogan
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒtəʊ / (BrE)
A short phrase or sentence that expresses the guiding beliefs or ideals of a person, group, or institution.

Examples

A motto phrase
Every scout lives by the motto "Be prepared."
She adopted the motto "Never give up" as her guiding principle.
The school’s motto is "Knowledge is power."

Related Terms

  • synonym: catchphrase - A well-known sentence or phrase, especially one associated with a particular famous person
  • synonym: slogan - A short and striking or memorable phrase used in advertising or promotion
