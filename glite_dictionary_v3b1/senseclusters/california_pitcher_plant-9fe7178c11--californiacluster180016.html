California pitcher plant

noun
a carnivorous bog plant of California
Pronunciation: / kælɪˈfɔrniə ˈpɪtʃɚ plænt / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːniə ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a marsh or bog plant of northern California and Oregon having solitary pendulous yellow-green flowers and twisted, winged pitcher-shaped leaves
