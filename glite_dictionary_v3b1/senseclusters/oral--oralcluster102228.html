oral

adjective
spoken, not written
2.7 12/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îr.…ôl / (AmE), / Àà…îÀê.r…ôl / (BrE)
spoken rather than written; communicated by means of speech.

Examples

Oral tradition stories
Folktales have been passed down through generations by oral tradition.
In some cultures, history is preserved primarily through oral storytelling.
The contract was only an oral agreement, not written down.

Synonyms

  • verbal
    3.2 3.74/m
    verbal usually means relating to words and is often spoken, while oral specifically means spoken aloud and focuses on speech.
