caution

verb
warn or advise somebody about danger
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːʃən / (BrE)
to warn someone, especially about a possible danger or problem

Examples

To caution a child
Parents often caution their children to be careful when crossing the street.
She cautioned her colleagues about the potential risks of the new procedure.
The police cautioned him not to enter the dangerous building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warn - to inform someone about a possible danger or problem
