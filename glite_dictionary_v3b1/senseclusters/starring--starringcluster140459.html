starring

adjective
most important performer or role
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a performer or role) being the most important or featured in a show, film, or performance.

Examples

Starring role
His starring performance received praise from critics around the world.
She landed the starring role in the new Broadway musical.
The film features several big names, but Tom Hanks is the starring actor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leading - most important or main in a performance or group
  • antonym: supporting - having a less important role than the main performer
