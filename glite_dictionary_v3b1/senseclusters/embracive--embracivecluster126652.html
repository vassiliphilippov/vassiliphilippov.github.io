embracive

adjective
enclosing or enveloping
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbreɪsɪv / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪsɪv / (BrE)
surrounding, enclosing, or holding closely in a way that covers or envelops something.

Examples

Embracive branches
The old oak's embracive branches formed a green canopy over the path.
She felt safe in the embracive warmth of her grandmother's arms.
The embracive fog wrapped the city in a soft, silent blanket.
