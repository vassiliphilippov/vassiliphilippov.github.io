subfield

noun
subset field in math
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌfiːld / (BrE)
a subset of a mathematical field that is itself a field under the same operations

Examples

Math subfield
The rational numbers form a subfield of the real numbers.
Every subfield of the complex numbers contains the rational numbers.
Understanding each subfield helps mathematicians study the structure of larger fields.
