weigher

noun
person who officially weighs and records items
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈweɪ.ə / (BrE)
an official whose job is to weigh items and record their weight, especially in contexts where accuracy is required.

Examples

Certified weigher official
As a certified weigher, she was responsible for ensuring the scales were accurate.
The company hired a new weigher to oversee the daily weighing of goods.
The port weigher checked each shipment before it was loaded onto the ship.
