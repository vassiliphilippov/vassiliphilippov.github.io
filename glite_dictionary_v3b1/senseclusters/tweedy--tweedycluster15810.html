tweedy

adjective
having a rough, tweed-like surface
6.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtwidi / (AmE), / ÀàtwiÀêdi / (BrE)
(of textiles) having a rough, woolly surface similar to tweed fabric.

Examples

Tweedy fabric texture
His favorite scarf was thick and tweedy, ideal for winter days.
She wore a tweedy jacket that complemented the autumn chill perfectly.
The armchair was covered in a tweedy fabric that felt sturdy and rustic.
