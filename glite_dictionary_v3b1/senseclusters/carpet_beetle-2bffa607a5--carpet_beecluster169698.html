carpet beetle

noun
small beetle pest feeding on fabrics
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.pɪt ˌbiː.təl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt ˌbiː.təl / (BrE)
a small beetle whose larvae are household pests that feed on woolen fabrics and other animal products

Examples

Carpet beetle larvae
If you notice small holes in your sweater, carpet beetle larvae might be to blame.
Professional pest control was needed to get rid of the carpet beetles in the old attic.
The museum closed temporarily after a carpet beetle infestation was discovered in the textile room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beetle - an insect with hard wing cases, belonging to the order Coleoptera
