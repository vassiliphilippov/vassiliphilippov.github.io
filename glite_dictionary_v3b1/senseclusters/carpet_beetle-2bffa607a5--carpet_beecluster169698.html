carpet beetle

noun
small beetle pest feeding on fabrics
4.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.pət ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.pɪt ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
a small beetle whose larvae are household pests that feed on woolen fabrics and other animal products
