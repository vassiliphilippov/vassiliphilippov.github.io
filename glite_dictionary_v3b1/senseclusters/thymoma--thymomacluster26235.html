thymoma

noun
tumor of thymus
9.1
Pronunciation: / θaɪˈmoʊmə / (AmE), / θaɪˈməʊmə / (BrE)
a tumor that develops from the tissue of the thymus gland, usually found in the chest

Examples

A thymoma tumor
The surgeon removed the thymoma to prevent further complications.
A thymoma can sometimes cause symptoms like chest pain or coughing.
After the diagnosis of thymoma, the patient began a course of treatment.
