tantalizer

noun
promotional teaser or preview
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtæn.tə.laɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈtæn.tə.laɪ.zə/ (BrE)
A short promotional item (such as a teaser, trailer, or preview) produced to arouse curiosity or desire for an upcoming product, event, or release.

Examples

The studio released a short tantaliser that hinted at the game's darker themes.
Marketing used the early screenshots as a clever tantaliser to build excitement before the announcement.
Festival organizers posted a musical tantaliser online and tickets sold out within hours.
