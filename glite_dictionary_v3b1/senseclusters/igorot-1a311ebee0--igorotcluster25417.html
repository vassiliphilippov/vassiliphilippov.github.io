Igorot

noun
Philippine language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiːɡəˌrɒt / (AmE), / ˈiːɡəˌrɒt / (BrE)
a language or dialect spoken by the Igorot peoples of the northern Philippines

Examples

Igorot language
The linguist recorded the last fluent speaker of a rare Igorot dialect in the mountain village.
Many Igorot languages are still spoken in the Cordillera region of the Philippines.
She studied an Igorot language as part of her research on indigenous cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kankanaey - a language spoken by the Kankanaey people, one of the Igorot groups
