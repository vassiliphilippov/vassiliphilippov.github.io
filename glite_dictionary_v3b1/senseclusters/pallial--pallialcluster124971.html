pallial

adjective
of the cortex
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpæliəl / (AmE), / ˈpæliəl / (BrE)

Examples

Pallial cortex
The pallial region of the brain is involved in sensory processing.
Researchers studied the pallial structures to understand higher cognitive functions.
Damage to pallial areas can affect memory and perception.
