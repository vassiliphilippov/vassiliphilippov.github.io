beyond a shadow of a doubt

adverb
with absolute certainty; without any doubt
5.4
Pronunciation: / biˈjɑnd ə ˈʃædoʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd ə ˈʃædəʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt /; / biˈɒnd ə ˈʃadəʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
with complete certainty; without any possibility of doubt

Examples

Beyond a shadow of a doubt certain
It was clear beyond a shadow of a doubt that the painting was a forgery.
She is beyond a shadow of a doubt the best candidate for the job.
The evidence proved his innocence beyond a shadow of a doubt.
She knew beyond a shadow of a doubt that she had made the right decision.
The jury was convinced beyond a shadow of a doubt that the defendant was innocent.
This experiment proves beyond a shadow of a doubt that the theory is correct.
