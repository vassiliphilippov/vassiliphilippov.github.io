pterygoid plexus

noun
network of veins near the pterygoid muscles
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈter.ɪ.ɡɔɪd ˈplek.səs/ /; / /ˈter.ə.ɡɔɪd ˈplek.səs/ (AmE), /ˈter.ɪ.ɡɔɪd ˈplek.səs/ /; / /ˈter.ə.ɡɔɪd ˈplek.səs/ (BrE)
a network of veins located near the pterygoid muscles, draining blood from that area into the internal maxillary and anterior facial veins
