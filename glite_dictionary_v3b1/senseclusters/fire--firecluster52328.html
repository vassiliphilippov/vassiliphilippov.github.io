Synonyms

  • shoot
    1.8 148/m
    shoot means to fire a gun at someone or something in an act of aiming, while fire is the general act of discharging weapons.
  • discharge
    5.8 0.64/m
    discharge is a formal term for causing a weapon to go off, while fire is the common verb for shooting guns.
  • open fire
    4.1 4.40/m
    open fire means to begin shooting at people or things in a sudden attack, while fire simply means to shoot a weapon.
