brigandage

noun
banditry and robbery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪɡəndɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪɡəndɪdʒ / (BrE)
the practice of robbery and violent crime carried out by a group of bandits

Examples

Brigandage in mountains
The mountains were notorious for brigandage during the 19th century.
Travelers feared brigandage on the remote roads after sunset.
The government sent troops to suppress brigandage in the troubled region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banditry - robbery and violence by bandits
  • antonym: lawfulness - the quality of obeying the law
  • synonym: robbery - the act of stealing from someone using force or threat
