fidget-bottom

noun
restless or fidgety person
9.6 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪdʒ.ət.bɑː.təm/ /; / /ˈfɪdʒ.ɪt.bɑː.təm/ (AmE), /ˈfɪdʒ.ɪt.bɒt.əm/ /; / /ˈfɪdʒ.ət.bɒt.əm/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is unable to sit still or is habitually restless

Examples
