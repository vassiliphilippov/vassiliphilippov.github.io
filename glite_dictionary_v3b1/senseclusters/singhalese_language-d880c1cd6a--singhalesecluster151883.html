Singhalese language

noun
language of Sri Lanka
8.4
Pronunciation: / sɪŋɡəˈliz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / sɪŋɡəˈliːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Indic language spoken by the majority of people in Sri Lanka

Examples

Singhalese language spoken
He is fluent in the Singhalese language as well as Tamil and Hindi.
Many Sri Lankan children learn both English and Singhalese language in school.
The novel was originally written in the Singhalese language before being translated into English.
