counterargue

verb
to present an opposing argument
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.tər.ɑːr.gjuː/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.tə.rɑː.gjuː/ (BrE)
to respond to an argument by presenting an opposing viewpoint or reasoning

Examples

To counterargue a point
During the meeting, she was prepared to counterargue every point raised by her colleague.
He tried to counterargue the proposal, but the evidence was too strong.
It is important to listen carefully before you counterargue in a discussion.

  • synonym: rebut - to argue against or refute a statement or claim
