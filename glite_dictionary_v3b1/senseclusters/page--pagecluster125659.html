page

verb
to turn pages
2.7
Pronunciation: / peɪdʒ / (AmE), / peɪdʒ / (BrE)
to turn the pages of a book, magazine, or document, especially in a casual or searching way

Examples

To page through a book
She paged through the magazine while waiting for her appointment.
He pages through his notes before every exam to refresh his memory.
The librarian paged through the old book to find the missing chapter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flip - to turn over pages quickly
  • synonym: leaf - to turn pages, especially quickly or casually
