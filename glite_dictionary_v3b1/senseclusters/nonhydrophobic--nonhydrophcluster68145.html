nonhydrophobic

adjective
not water-repellent
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnhɑɪdrəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
not repelling water; having no tendency to avoid or repel water

Examples

Nonhydrophobic coating
The nonhydrophobic coating allowed water to soak into the fabric easily.
Scientists tested whether the nonhydrophobic material would absorb or repel moisture.
A nonhydrophobic surface is ideal for applications where water spreading is desired.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrophilic - having a strong affinity for water
  • antonym: hydrophobic - repelling or avoiding water
