raiser

noun
one who increases or lifts
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈreɪzə / (BrE)
a person who collects or raises money, support, or other things for a cause, project, or organization

Examples

A fund raiser
She is a talented raiser of funds for several nonprofit organizations.
The new device acts as a raiser to lift heavy boxes onto the shelf.
As a raiser of awareness, his campaign reached thousands of people.
The school hired a professional raiser to help secure funding for new equipment.
As a dedicated raiser, she organized several events to support the local hospital.
The charity depends on each raiser to reach out to potential donors in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booster - someone or something that increases or supports something
  • antonym: donor - a person who gives money or goods to a cause
  • synonym: fundraiser - a person or event that raises money for a cause
  • antonym: lowerer - a person or thing that brings something down or reduces it
