revengeful

adjective
disposed to seek revenge
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɛndʒfəɫ / (AmE), / rɪˈvɛndʒf(ʊ)l / (BrE)
Having or showing a desire to seek revenge; inclined to retaliate for a wrong.

Examples

Revengeful attitude
After the argument, he became increasingly revengeful towards his former friend.
Her revengeful attitude made it difficult for her to move on from past grievances.
The revengeful tone of the letter alarmed everyone who read it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiving - willing to forgive; not holding a grudge or seeking revenge
  • synonym: vindictive - having or showing a strong or unreasoning desire for revenge
