windsurf

verb
ride on water using a board with a sail
3.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪnd.sɜːrf/ (AmE), /ˈwɪnd.sɜːf/ (BrE)
to ride standing on a board with an attached sail, using the wind for movement across the water

Examples

To windsurf on water
It's difficult to windsurf when the wind suddenly dies down.
Last summer, I learned how to windsurf on a lake near my house.
Many tourists come here just to windsurf in the clear blue waters.
