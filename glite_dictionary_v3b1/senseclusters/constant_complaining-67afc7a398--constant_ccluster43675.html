constant complaining

noun
repeatedly expressing dissatisfaction
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnstənt kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or habit of expressing dissatisfaction repeatedly over a period of time

Examples

Constant complaining about problems
He lost patience with his roommate's constant complaining about the weather.
Her constant complaining made it difficult for others to enjoy the meeting.
The coach addressed the team's constant complaining by encouraging a more positive attitude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grumbling - the act of complaining in a low voice or murmuring manner
  • synonym: whining - the act of complaining in an annoying or persistent way
