dictatorship

noun
absolute rule by one person or group
2.1
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈteɪtɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / dɪkˈteɪtəʃɪp / (BrE)
A form of government in which absolute power is concentrated in the hands of a single ruler or small group, not restricted by laws, constitutions, or opposition.

Examples

A dictatorship government
After the coup, the country fell under a brutal dictatorship that suppressed political opposition.
Many citizens fled the nation to escape the oppressive dictatorship in search of freedom.
The history book analyzed how dictatorship can emerge when democratic institutions are weak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocracy - a system of government by one person with absolute power
  • antonym: democracy - a system of government by the whole population or all eligible members, typically through elected representatives
