geeky

adjective
technical or nerdy
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡiki /; / ˈɡiːki / (AmE), / ˈɡiːki / (BrE)
showing interest in technical, specialized, or niche subjects, often in a way that seems unfashionable or socially awkward
