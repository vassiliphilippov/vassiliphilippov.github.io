mukhtar

noun
village leader
Pronunciation: / mʊkˈtɑr / (AmE), / mʊkˈtɑː / (BrE)
the elected head or leader of a village or local community in some Middle Eastern and South Asian countries

Examples

Village mukhtar
The mukhtar called a meeting to discuss the new water project for the village.
Villagers often seek advice from the mukhtar when facing local disputes.
After the election, the new mukhtar promised to improve the community's roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chieftain - the leader of a tribe or clan
  • synonym: mayor - the elected head of a city, town, or municipality
