seagoing

adjective
suitable for sea travel
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a ship or vessel) suitable for or capable of operating on the open sea.

Examples

Seagoing cargo ship
Her father spent his life as the captain of a seagoing cargo ship.
Many seagoing ships are equipped with advanced navigation systems.
The seagoing vessel was designed to withstand the harshest ocean storms.
