evaluable

adjective
able to be assessed
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæljuəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈvæljuəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be judged, measured, or assessed for value or quality

Examples

Evaluable results
The effectiveness of the new policy is evaluable through regular performance reviews.
Only projects with clear goals are truly evaluable by the committee.
The data collected must be evaluable to support scientific conclusions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inestimable - impossible to estimate or assess
  • synonym: measurable - able to be measured or quantified
