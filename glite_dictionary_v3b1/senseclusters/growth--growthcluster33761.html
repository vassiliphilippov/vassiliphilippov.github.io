growth

noun
a mass or collection of things that have grown
1.4
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
a mass of something that has developed, such as plants, hair, or an area of vegetation

Examples

Thick growth of hair
He was worried when he noticed a new growth on his arm.
The doctor examined the growth on her skin.
There was a thick growth of weeds behind the house.
A thick growth of bushes blocked the path completely.
After the fire, the growth in the area was slow to return.
We could see a patchy growth of weeds along the old fence.

  • synonym: vegetation - plants in a particular area or habitat
