Jezreelite

noun
person from Jezreel
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛzrəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛzrəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a person from the ancient city of Jezreel in Israel

Examples

A Jezreelite man
The vineyard belonged to a Jezreelite named Naboth in the biblical account.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts believed to have been used by a Jezreelite thousands of years ago.
Legends from the region often mention a wise Jezreelite who advised the king.
