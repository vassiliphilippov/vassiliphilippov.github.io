Sister

noun
title for a nun in Catholic Church
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstər / (AmE), / ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
(in the Roman Catholic Church) a title or form of address given to a nun.

Examples

Sister Maria
Sister Maria greeted us warmly when we arrived at the convent.
Many people sought guidance from Sister Agnes in times of trouble.
The students learned from Sister Jane during religion class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nun - a woman who has taken religious vows in a Christian order
