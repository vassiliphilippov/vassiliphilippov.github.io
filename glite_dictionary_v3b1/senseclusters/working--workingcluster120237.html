Synonyms

  • functioning
    3.1 0.17/m
    functioning means carrying out its normal job or task reliably, while working means being usable or operational in general.
  • functional
    3.2 8.59/m
    functional describes something that works as intended for its purpose, while working is a more general state of use or operation.
  • operational
    4.1 4.68/m
    operational means ready or in use, often for systems or services, while working is a broader everyday word.
  • operating
    3.9 0.00/m
    operating refers to being in active use or running in a process, while working covers general usability and function.
  • running
    1.5 0.00/m
    running describes a machine or system that is currently switched on and moving, while working is more general about usability.
  • in operation
    3.2 2.22/m
    in operation means actively being used now, while working can also mean generally usable or able to perform tasks.
  • operable
    4.6 0.25/m
    operable means able to be used or worked, while working usually means it actually functions or is currently in use.
  • operative
    4.8 1.93/m
    operative often means having effect or being in force, while working focuses on machines or things being usable and functioning.
