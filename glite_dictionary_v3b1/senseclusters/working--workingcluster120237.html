working

adjective
sufficient for practical use or purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkɪŋ /; / ˈwɝː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ /; / ˈwɜː.kɪŋ / (BrE)
functioning or able to be used; operational or adequate for practical use

Examples

Working condition
Do you have a working understanding of how the new software operates?
She has a working knowledge of French, enough to travel and get by on her own.
The plan needs a working majority to be approved by the board.
After the repairs, the radio was finally working again.
Please make sure all the working parts are lubricated before you start the engine.
The elevator was not in working condition, so we had to use the stairs.
