Battle of Wake Island

noun
World War II battle on Wake Island, 1941
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv weɪk ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv weɪk ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
a battle in December 1941 during World War II, in which Japanese forces captured Wake Island after a determined defense by United States marines
