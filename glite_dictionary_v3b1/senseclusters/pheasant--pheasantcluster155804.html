pheasant

noun
meat of a pheasant (used as food)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈfɛzənt / (BrE)
The flesh of a pheasant, used as food, typically braised or roasted.

Examples

Roast pheasant dish
In autumn, many restaurants offer dishes featuring local pheasant as a seasonal delicacy.
The chef recommended braising the pheasant to keep the meat tender and flavorful.
We had roasted pheasant with wild mushrooms for dinner last night.
