vaporetto

noun
Venetian waterbus
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvɑːpəˈrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌvæpəˈrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a motorized boat used as public transportation on the canals of Venice, Italy

Examples

The vaporetto boat

Locals often use the vaporetto to commute between different parts of Venice.
Tourists enjoy riding the vaporetto for its scenic views along the Grand Canal.
We boarded the vaporetto at the main stop to travel along the Grand Canal.
Tourists often use a vaporetto to reach the famous islands near Venice.
The vaporetto was crowded with commuters heading home after work.
