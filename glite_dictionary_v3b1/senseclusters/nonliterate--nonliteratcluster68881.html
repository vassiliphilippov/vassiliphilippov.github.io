nonliterate

adjective
lacking a system of writing
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈlɪt̬ɚət / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɪtərət / (BrE)
(of a society) not having developed a system of writing.

Examples

Nonliterate society
Anthropologists have studied several nonliterate societies to understand how traditions are passed down orally.
The transmission of stories and knowledge in nonliterate communities typically depends on spoken language and performance.
Trade in nonliterate cultures often relies on memory and verbal agreements rather than written contracts.
