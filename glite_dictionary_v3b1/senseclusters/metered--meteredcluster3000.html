metered

adjective
controlled by a meter
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtərd / (AmE), / ˈmiːtəd / (BrE)
controlled, measured, or regulated by a device that records usage or quantity, such as a meter

Examples

Metered parking spot
Parking is only allowed in metered spaces along this busy street.
The apartment has metered water, so tenants pay for what they use.
Electricity in the building is metered separately for each unit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regulated - controlled or managed by rules or a system
  • antonym: unmetered - not controlled or measured by a meter
