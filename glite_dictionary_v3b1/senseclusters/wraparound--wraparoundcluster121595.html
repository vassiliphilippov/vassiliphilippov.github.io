wraparound

noun
garment wrapped around the body
9.4 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈræp.ə.raʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈræp.ə.raʊnd/ (BrE)
a garment, such as a dress or coat, that fastens by being wrapped around the body and tied or secured, rather than having buttons or zips

Examples

A wraparound dress
He chose a warm wraparound coat for the winter weather.
She wore a stylish wraparound to the summer party, which fit her perfectly.
The boutique offers several wraparound dresses in bright, floral patterns.
