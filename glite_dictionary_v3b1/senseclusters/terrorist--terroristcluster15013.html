terrorist

noun
player on the 'terrorist' team (video games)
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈte.rə.rɪst/ (AmE), /ˈte.rə.rɪst/ (BrE)
(in online multiplayer shooter games) a player who is a member of the 'terrorist' team or faction, a role label used to distinguish one side from the opposing 'counter-terrorists'.

Examples

Terrorist team
In the final round, the terrorist planted the bomb and forced the counter-terrorists to rush.
Our team switched sides; the terrorists had to defend the hostages for three consecutive rounds.
He complained that the new map gave an unfair advantage to terrorists when attacking the bombsite.

Related Terms

  • antonym: counter-terrorist - a player on the opposing team (the 'counter-terrorists') in many shooter games
  • synonym: player - a person who plays a video game; the typical referent of this sense
