muzzily

adverb
unclearly or confusedly
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzɪli / (AmE), / ˈmʌzɪli / (BrE)
in a confused, unclear, or mentally foggy manner

Examples

Thinking muzzily
He spoke muzzily after waking up from a deep sleep.
Her thoughts drifted muzzily as she tried to focus on the lecture.
The medicine made him answer questions muzzily during the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see, hear, or understand
  • synonym: unclearly - in a way that is not clear or easy to understand
  • synonym: vaguely - in a way that is not definite or clear
