alabaster

noun
compact, white mineral used in carving
Pronunciation: / ˈæləˌbæstər /; / ˈæl.əˌbæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈaləbɑːstə /; / ˈæl.əˌbæs.tə / (BrE)
a smooth, white or lightly tinted mineral used for carving and decorative objects
