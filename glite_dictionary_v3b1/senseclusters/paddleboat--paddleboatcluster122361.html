paddleboat

noun
pedal-powered boat
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a small boat that is moved by pedals turning a paddle wheel, usually for recreation on calm water

Examples

A lake paddleboat
We spent the afternoon riding a paddleboat around the quiet lake.
Children laughed as they tried to steer the paddleboat in a straight line.
You can rent a paddleboat at the park for a relaxing trip on the water.
