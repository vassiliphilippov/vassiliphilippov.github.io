three-base hit

noun
baseball hit reaching third base
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌθriː beɪs hɪt / (AmE), / ˌθriː beɪs hɪt / (BrE)
(in baseball) a hit that allows the batter to reach third base safely.

Examples

A three-base hit in baseball
