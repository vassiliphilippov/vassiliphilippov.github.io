burr

verb
to make a humming sound
7.9
Pronunciation: /bɜːr/ (AmE), /bɜː/ (BrE)
to make a whirring or humming sound similar to that of a burr or buzzing insect

Examples

Bees burr loudly
The bees were burring around the flowers in the garden.
The old fan burrs loudly whenever it is turned on.
In the quiet room, I could hear the phone burring on the table.
