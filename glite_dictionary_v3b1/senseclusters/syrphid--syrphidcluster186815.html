syrphid

adjective
of hoverflies
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrfɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪd / (BrE)
relating to, resembling, or characteristic of flies of the family Syrphidae, commonly known as hoverflies.

Many syrphid adults mimic bees or wasps to avoid predators.
