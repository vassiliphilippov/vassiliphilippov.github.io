Synonyms

  • air flow
    2.8 0.19/m
    air flow is the measured or controlled movement of air in a system, while flow of air covers general movement from natural or mechanical forces.
  • current of air
    3.5 0.10/m
    current of air is a flow driven by pressure that can be strong and fast, while flow of air describes movement or circulation.
  • air current
    3.3 0.43/m
    air current is a moving band of air that often has noticeable speed, while flow of air names movement caused by forces or machines.
  • airstream
    4.2 0.02/m
    airstream is a steady, well-defined wind or airflow you can feel, while flow of air covers any movement of air.
  • circulation
    4.0 2.38/m
    circulation is movement of air within a system or space, often continuous, while flow of air refers to any directed or natural movement.
