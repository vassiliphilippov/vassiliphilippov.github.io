fustiness

noun
musty or stale quality
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌstinəs / (AmE), / ˈfʌstinəs / (BrE)
the quality of smelling stale, musty, or being old-fashioned

Examples

Fustiness of an attic
The fustiness of the old attic made it unpleasant to spend time there.
After years without ventilation, the room was filled with a strong fustiness.
He disliked the fustiness of traditions that no longer made sense.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freshness - the quality of being clean, new, or not stale
  • synonym: mustiness - the quality of smelling damp, moldy, or stale
