nonstrategic

adjective
for local use
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnstrəˈtiːdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnstrəˈtiːdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to weapons, forces, or actions intended for local or limited use rather than for large-scale or national strategic purposes

Examples

Nonstrategic nuclear weapons
The agreement focused on reducing nonstrategic nuclear weapons in border regions.
Commanders discussed the deployment of nonstrategic forces for the upcoming operation.
Many experts argue that nonstrategic missiles still pose significant risks in regional conflicts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strategic - relating to large-scale or long-term military planning and objectives
  • synonym: tactical - relating to actions or weapons used for short-term or local objectives
