underserved

adjective
lacking adequate services
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈsɜrvd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈsɜːvd / (BrE)
not receiving enough resources, services, or attention compared to what is needed or deserved

Examples

Underserved communities
The new clinic aims to help underserved neighborhoods that lack basic healthcare.
Many underserved students struggle to access quality educational resources.
Charities often focus on underserved populations in rural and remote areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neglected - not given enough care or attention
  • antonym: privileged - having more advantages or resources than others
