onlay

verb
to apply a covering
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌleɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌleɪ / (BrE)
to attach or apply a thin covering or decorative piece onto the surface of something

Examples

To onlay gold leaf
The craftsman decided to onlay gold leaf onto the wooden frame for a luxurious finish.
To restore the antique box, she carefully onlaid a thin layer of decorative veneer.
Artists sometimes onlay different materials to create unique textures on their sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overlay - to cover the surface of something with a layer
  • antonym: remove - to take something away from a surface
