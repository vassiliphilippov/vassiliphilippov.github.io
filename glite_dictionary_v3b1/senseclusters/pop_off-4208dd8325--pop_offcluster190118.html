pop off

verb
perform exceptionally well (slang)

(informal, slang) To perform exceptionally well or suddenly produce an outstanding play or performance; often used for notable moments in gaming, sports, or music.

Examples

To pop off in game
During the stream, the new player popped off with an impressive multi-kill that shocked viewers.
She tends to pop off during the final minutes, scoring and creating chances for teammates.
After a slow start, he was popping off in the second half and carried the game.
