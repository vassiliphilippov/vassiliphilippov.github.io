nonindulgent

adjective
not indulgent; strict or restrained
7.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ɪn.ˈdʌl.dʒənt/ /; / /nɑːn.ə.ˈdʌl.dʒənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ɪn.ˈdʌl.dʒənt/ /; / /nɒn.ə.ˈdʌl.dʒənt/ (BrE)
Not allowing oneself or others to enjoy pleasures; strict or restrained in giving or receiving indulgence.

Examples

A nonindulgent boss
A nonindulgent boss may expect high standards without much leniency.
Her nonindulgent parenting style emphasized discipline and clear boundaries.
The diet required a nonindulgent attitude towards sweets and rich foods.
