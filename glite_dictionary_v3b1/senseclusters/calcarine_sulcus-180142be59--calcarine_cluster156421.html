calcarine sulcus

noun
groove in the occipital lobe of the brain
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkæl.kə.riːn ˈsʌl.kəs/ /; / /ˈkæl.kə.raɪn ˈsʌl.kəs/ (AmE), /ˈkæl.kə.riːn ˈsʌl.kəs/ /; / /ˈkæl.kə.raɪn ˈsʌl.kəs/ (BrE)
