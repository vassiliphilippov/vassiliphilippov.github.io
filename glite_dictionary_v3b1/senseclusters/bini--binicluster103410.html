bini

noun
Nigerian cap
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːni / (AmE), / ˈbiːni / (BrE)
a round, close-fitting cap traditionally worn in Nigeria, often as part of cultural or ceremonial dress

Examples

A bini cap
He wore a colorful bini to the celebration.
The traditional bini is often decorated with intricate patterns and bright colors.
During the festival, many elders proudly donned their bini as a symbol of heritage.
