discommendation

noun
expression of disapproval
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəˌmɛnˈdeɪʃən /; / ˌdɪskɒmənˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəˌmɛnˈdeɪʃən /; / ˌdɪskɒmənˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of expressing disapproval or criticism of someone or something, including withdrawing a previous recommendation

Examples

Discommendation from the committee
The proposal met with discommendation from several members of the committee.
His actions drew public discommendation in the local press.
Despite the discommendation, she continued to pursue her innovative ideas.
The committee issued a formal discommendation after learning new facts about the candidate.
Public discommendation of the product led to a sharp decline in sales.
Following the controversy, the organization made a public discommendation of its earlier support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commendation - praise or approval
  • synonym: condemnation - strong disapproval or criticism
  • antonym: endorsement - an act of giving approval or support
  • synonym: retraction - the act of taking back a statement or recommendation
