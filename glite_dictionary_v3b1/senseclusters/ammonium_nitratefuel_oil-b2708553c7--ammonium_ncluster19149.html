ammonium nitrate/fuel oil

noun
mixture used as an industrial explosive
7.1
Pronunciation: / əˌmoʊniəm ˈnaɪtreɪt fjuːəl ɔɪl / (AmE), / əˌməʊniəm ˈnaɪtreɪt fjuːəl ɔɪl / (BrE)
An industrial explosive consisting of a mixture of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, widely used in mining and construction.

Examples

Ammonium nitrate/fuel oil explosive
Large-scale excavations often rely on ammonium nitrate/fuel oil due to its cost-effectiveness.
Strict safety protocols are followed when storing ammonium nitrate/fuel oil on construction sites.
The quarry used ammonium nitrate/fuel oil to blast through the solid rock.
