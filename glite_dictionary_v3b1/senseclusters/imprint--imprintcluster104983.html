imprint

verb
mark or stamp by pressure
6.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈprɪnt/ /; / /ˈɪm.prɪnt/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈprɪnt/ /; / /ˈɪm.prɪnt/ (BrE)
to mark or stamp (something) with or as if with pressure, so that an image or impression is made

Examples

To imprint a logo
She imprinted her initials onto the leather wallet as a personal touch.
The child's muddy shoes imprinted tracks on the clean kitchen floor.
The company logo was imprinted on every product for brand recognition.
