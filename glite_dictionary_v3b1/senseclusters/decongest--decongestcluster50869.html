decongest

verb
to clear blockage
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛst / (AmE), / ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛst / (BrE)
to relieve or clear a blocked or congested area, especially in the body such as the nose or chest

Examples

Certain medications are designed to decongest blocked airways quickly.
