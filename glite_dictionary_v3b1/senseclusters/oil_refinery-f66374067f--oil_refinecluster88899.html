oil refinery

noun
factory for processing crude oil
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl rɪˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / ɔɪl rɪˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
a factory where crude oil is processed and converted into useful products such as gasoline, diesel, and kerosene
