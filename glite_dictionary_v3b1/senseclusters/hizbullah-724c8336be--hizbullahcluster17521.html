Hizbullah

noun
Shiite Islamist group in Lebanon
9.8
Pronunciation: / hɪz.bəˈlɑː / (AmE), / hɪz.bʊˈlɑː / (BrE)
A Shiite Islamist political and militant group based in Lebanon, known for its ties to Iran and involvement in Lebanese and regional conflicts.

Examples

Hizbullah group
Many countries have designated Hizbullah as a terrorist organization.
Sanctions were imposed on individuals linked to Hizbullah.
The influence of Hizbullah in Lebanese politics has grown over the years.
