shamefaced

adjective
modest or shy; showing embarrassment
5.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈʃeɪm.feɪst/ (AmE), /ˈʃeɪm.feɪst/ (BrE)
showing or feeling shame, embarrassment, or guilt; modest or shy because of feeling ashamed
