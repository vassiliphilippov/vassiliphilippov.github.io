bookbinder

noun
person who binds books
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌbaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌbaɪndə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to assemble and bind the pages and covers of books

Examples

A bookbinder at work
She apprenticed as a bookbinder in her early twenties.
The antique bookbinder carefully restored the old manuscript.
The library hired a local bookbinder to repair damaged volumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binder - person who binds books or other materials
