claims adjustor

noun
insurance claims investigator and settler
Pronunciation: / kleɪmz ədˈʒʌstɚ / (AmE), / kleɪmz ədˈdʒʌstə / (BrE)
a person who investigates insurance claims and recommends settlements to resolve them

Examples

The claims adjustor investigates
After reviewing the evidence, the claims adjustor negotiated a fair settlement for the client.
The claims adjustor arrived promptly to assess the damage after the storm.
The insurance company assigned a claims adjustor to handle our case.
