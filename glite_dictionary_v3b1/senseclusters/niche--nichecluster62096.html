niche

verb
to place suitably
Pronunciation: / nɪtʃ / (AmE), / niːʃ / (BrE)
to place or fit something into a suitable or specific position or role

Examples

To niche a vase
The curator niched the rare vase in a glass display case for everyone to see.
She niched herself in a role that perfectly matched her skills and interests.
The architect niched the bookshelf into the wall to save space in the room.
