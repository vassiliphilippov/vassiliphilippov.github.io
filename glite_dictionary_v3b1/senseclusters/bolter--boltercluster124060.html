bolter

noun
person who leaves suddenly
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊltər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊltə / (BrE)
a person who leaves suddenly or unexpectedly, especially from a group or organization

Examples

Group bolter
The committee was surprised when another bolter left during the crucial vote.
He gained a reputation as a bolter after quitting several teams without warning.
At the first sign of trouble, the bolter disappeared from the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deserter - a person who abandons a group or duty
  • antonym: stayer - a person who remains in a group or situation
