yours

pronoun
possessive pronoun
Pronunciation: / jɔɹz / (AmE), / jɔːz / (BrE)
used to indicate that something belongs to or is connected with the person or people being spoken to

Examples

Is this yours
Is this notebook yours or should I give it to someone else?
The decision is entirely yours to make, so choose carefully.
If you forgot your keys, are these yours on the table?

Related Terms

  • antonym: hers - possessive pronoun for a female person
  • antonym: his - possessive pronoun for a male person
  • antonym: mine - possessive pronoun for the speaker
  • antonym: ours - possessive pronoun for the speaker and others
  • antonym: theirs - possessive pronoun for other people
