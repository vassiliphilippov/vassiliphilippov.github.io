stave

noun
group of five lines for writing music
Pronunciation: / steɪv / (AmE), / steɪv / (BrE)
(music) A set of five horizontal lines and four spaces on which musical notes are written.

Examples

Musical stave
Each stave in the score represents a different instrument.
The melody was written across a single stave using treble clef.
Young students learn how to place notes correctly on the stave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staff - (music) set of horizontal lines for musical notation (US English)
