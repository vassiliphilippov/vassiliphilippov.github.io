snap

noun
fastener that snaps shut
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
a small fastener used on clothing or accessories that closes with a sharp, snapping sound

Examples

A jacket snap
Modern purses often close with a magnetic snap instead of a zipper.
She replaced the broken snap on her jacket with a new one.
The baby’s onesie has convenient snaps along the legs for easy changing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: button - a small, usually round object sewn onto clothing to fasten it
