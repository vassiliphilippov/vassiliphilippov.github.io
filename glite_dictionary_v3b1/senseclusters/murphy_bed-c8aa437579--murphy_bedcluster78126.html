Murphy bed

noun
foldable bed stored in a cabinet or wall
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːfi bɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɜːfi bɛd / (BrE)
A bed that can be folded or swung into a cabinet or wall when not in use, saving space in a room.

Examples

A Murphy bed cabinet
During the day, the Murphy bed stays hidden behind the cabinets, making the studio apartment feel much bigger.
The apartment came fully furnished, including a fold-down Murphy bed for overnight guests.
We installed a Murphy bed in the guest room to make better use of the space.
