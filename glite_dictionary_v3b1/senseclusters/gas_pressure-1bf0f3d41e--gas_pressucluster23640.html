gas pressure

noun
pressure exerted by a gas
2.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /gæs ˈpreʃ.ər/ (AmE), /gæs ˈpreʃ.ə/ (BrE)
the pressure exerted by a gas on the walls of its container or in an environment

Examples

Gas pressure in container
Changes in temperature will affect the gas pressure within a sealed container.
Lowering the volume of a container increases its gas pressure if the temperature remains constant.
The gas pressure inside the cylinder must be monitored carefully for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pressure - the continuous physical force exerted on or against an object
