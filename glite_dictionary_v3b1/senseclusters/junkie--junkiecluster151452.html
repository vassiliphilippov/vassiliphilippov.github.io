junkie

noun
person addicted to drugs (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
an informal term for a person addicted to drugs, especially illegal ones

Examples

A drug junkie
He spent years on the streets as a junkie, struggling to overcome his addiction.
Some people use the word junkie to describe anyone with an intense dependency, but it's often considered offensive.
The film tells the story of a young junkie trying to rebuild her life.
The police found evidence that the suspect was a junkie living in the abandoned building.
He is a real sports junkie and never misses a game on TV.
After losing his job, he became more of a junkie than ever before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to a substance or activity
  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol or uses drugs
  • synonym: user - a person who uses drugs, especially illegal drugs
