abolitionary

adjective
relating to abolition, esp. of slavery
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈlɪʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌabəˈlɪʃənəri / (BrE)
Relating to or favouring abolition, especially the abolition of slavery.

Examples

The senator supported abolitionary legislation aimed at ending slavery in the territories.
They formed an abolitionary society to campaign against the slave trade.
