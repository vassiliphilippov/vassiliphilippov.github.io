tank

noun
armored military vehicle with a cannon
Pronunciation: / tæŋk / (AmE), / tæŋk / (BrE)
A large, enclosed, heavily armored military vehicle equipped with a cannon and moving on caterpillar tracks.

Examples

A military tank
During the parade, children cheered when a historic tank passed by.
He watched as the massive tank rolled across the battlefield, its gun firing.
The army deployed a tank to support the infantry assault.
