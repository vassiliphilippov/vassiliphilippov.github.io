hanger

noun
object from which something is hung
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋə / (BrE)
an object from which something can be hung, such as for keeping clothes or equipment suspended

Examples

Clothes hanger
A plant pot dangled from a ceiling hanger above the kitchen table.
She placed her coat on a hanger in the closet.
The shop sold wooden hangers for organizing scarves and belts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hook - a curved piece of metal or plastic used to hang things
