electric organ

noun
electronic keyboard imitating a pipe organ
1.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
A keyboard musical instrument that electronically produces sounds similar to those of a pipe organ.

Examples

An electric organ keyboard
He practiced every afternoon on the electric organ in the community center.
Many jazz musicians favor the rich, distinctive sound of an electric organ.
The church replaced its old pipe organ with a modern electric organ.
