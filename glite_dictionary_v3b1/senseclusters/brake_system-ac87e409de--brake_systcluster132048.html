brake system

noun
mechanism for slowing or stopping a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪk ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
the mechanism made up of several parts that works together to slow or stop a motor vehicle by applying brakes

Examples

Car brake system
A faulty brake system can lead to dangerous accidents on the road.
Regular maintenance of your vehicle's brake system is essential for your safety.
The mechanic inspected the brake system to ensure the car was safe to drive.
