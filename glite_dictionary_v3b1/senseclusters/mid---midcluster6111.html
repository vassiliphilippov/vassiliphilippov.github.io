mid-

adjective
prefix meaning 'middle'
Pronunciation: / mɪd / (AmE), / mɪd / (BrE)
added to nouns to form words that refer to the middle part, period, or position of something

Examples

Midmorning
She arrived at work in the midmorning, just before a meeting.
The concert took place in mid-September, when the weather was pleasant.
We scheduled our family reunion for mid-August so everyone could attend.
