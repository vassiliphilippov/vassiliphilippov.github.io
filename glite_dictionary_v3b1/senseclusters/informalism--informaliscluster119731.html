informalism

noun
abstract art movement
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɔːrməˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːməlɪzəm / (BrE)
a post-World War II abstract art movement emphasizing spontaneous gesture, texture, and improvisation rather than formal structure

Examples

Informalism in painting
The gallery's new exhibition features several masterpieces of informalism from the 1950s.
Many artists turned to informalism to express emotion through bold gestures and textures.
She wrote her thesis on the influence of informalism in European modern art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstract expressionism - an art movement focused on expressive, non-representational painting
  • antonym: cubism - an art movement characterized by geometric shapes and fragmented forms
