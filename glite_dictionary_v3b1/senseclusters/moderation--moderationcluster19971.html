moderation

noun
the quality of avoiding excesses
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the quality of keeping within reasonable limits and avoiding excess or extremes; restraint or temperance in actions, feelings, or habits

Examples

Moderation in eating
Even during celebrations, he advised moderation rather than overindulgence.
He believed that moderation in all things was the key to a healthy life.
Practicing moderation helped her maintain a balanced diet and regular exercise routine.
Moderation is the key to balancing work and relaxation.
Drinking in moderation can be part of a healthy lifestyle.
He believes in moderation when it comes to eating sweets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excess - an amount or behavior that goes beyond what is allowed or usual
  • synonym: restraint - the action or trait of holding back or controlling oneself
