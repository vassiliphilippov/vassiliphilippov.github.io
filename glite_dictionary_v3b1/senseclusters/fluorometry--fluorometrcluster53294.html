fluorometry

noun
measurement of fluorescence
Pronunciation: / flʊˈrɑːmətri / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɒmətri / (BrE)
the scientific measurement of fluorescence to analyze or identify substances

Examples

Fluorometry measurement
The laboratory technician used fluorometry to determine the presence of specific proteins in the sample.
Advances in fluorometry have improved the detection of pollutants in water.
Researchers rely on fluorometry for sensitive analysis of biological materials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colorimetry - the measurement of the concentration of substances by color intensity
  • synonym: spectrophotometry - the measurement of how substances absorb light at different wavelengths
