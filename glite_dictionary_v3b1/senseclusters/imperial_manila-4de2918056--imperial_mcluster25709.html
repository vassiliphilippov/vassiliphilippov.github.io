Imperial Manila

proper noun
Manila as centralized political/economic hub (pejorative)
9.3
Pronunciation: / 6amc8p6ari59l m59c8n6al59 / (AmE), / 6amc8p6a59ri59l m59c8n6al59 / (BrE)
A pejorative term for Manila used to describe the city as the country's centralized political and economic hub that dominates decision-making and resources, often implying neglect of the provinces.

Examples

Imperial Manila controls resources
Many politicians accused Imperial Manila of prioritizing urban projects while rural roads decayed.
Activists campaigned for funds to be distributed beyond Imperial Manila to support struggling provinces.
New policies aim to reduce the dominance of Imperial Manila over national budget decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centralization - the concentration of administrative and decision-making power in a single center (often the capital)
  • antonym: decentralization - the distribution of administrative power away from a central authority to regional or local levels
