starter

noun
player who starts a game
4.1 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːr.tər/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.tə/ (BrE)
a player who is in the starting lineup of a team sport and begins the game on the field or court

Examples

Team starter
He was named a starter for the championship game due to his consistent performance.
Injuries forced the team to use several substitutes instead of their usual starters.
The coach announced the list of starters before the match began.
