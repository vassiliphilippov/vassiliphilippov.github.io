ass

noun
donkey; long-eared domesticated animal
3.6
Pronunciation: / æs / (AmE), / ɑːs / (BrE)
A hardy, sure-footed animal of the horse family, smaller than a horse and with longer ears; a donkey.

Examples

Pack ass
Farmers often use an ass to carry loads across rough terrain.
He could hear the braying of an ass in the nearby field.
The ass slowly made its way up the steep mountain path.
