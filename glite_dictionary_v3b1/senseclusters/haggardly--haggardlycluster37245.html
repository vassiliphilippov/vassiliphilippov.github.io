haggardly

adverb
in a tired or worn-out manner
8.4 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhæɡ.ərd.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhæɡ.əd.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that shows tiredness, worry, or exhaustion; in a haggard manner.

Examples

To speak haggardly
After the sleepless night, she spoke haggardly to her colleagues at work.
He haggardly explained what had happened, his face drawn with fatigue.
The survivor staggered haggardly into the camp, barely able to stand.
