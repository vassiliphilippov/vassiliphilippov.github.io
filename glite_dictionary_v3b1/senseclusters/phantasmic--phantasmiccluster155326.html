phantasmic

adjective
dreamlike or fantastical
Pronunciation: / fænˈtæzmɪk / (AmE), / fænˈtæzmɪk / (BrE)
having a dreamlike, fantastical, ghostly, or unreal quality; seeming imaginary or like an illusion
