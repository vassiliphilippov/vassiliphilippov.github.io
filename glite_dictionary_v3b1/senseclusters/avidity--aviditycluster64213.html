avidity

noun
eager desire; enthusiasm
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈvɪdəti / (AmE), / əˈvɪdəti / (BrE)
eager desire or enthusiasm for something; strong enthusiasm or eagerness to do or obtain something

Examples

Strong avidity for knowledge
Despite early failures, her avidity for scientific discovery never faded.
Her avidity for learning new languages led her to travel around the world.
The avidity with which he started each new project was inspiring to his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: eagerness - keen or enthusiastic desire
