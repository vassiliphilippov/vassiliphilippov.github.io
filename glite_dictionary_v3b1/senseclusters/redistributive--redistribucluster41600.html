redistributive

adjective
sharing resources equally
Pronunciation: / ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjətɪv / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪv / (BrE)
relating to the process of distributing resources or wealth more equally among people or groups

Examples

A redistributive policy
The new policy has a strong redistributive effect, aiming to reduce income inequality.
Many social programs are designed to be redistributive in nature, helping those with fewer resources.
Critics argue that redistributive measures can discourage economic growth if not implemented carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - believing in or promoting equal rights and opportunities
  • antonym: inequitable - unfair or unequal in treatment or distribution
