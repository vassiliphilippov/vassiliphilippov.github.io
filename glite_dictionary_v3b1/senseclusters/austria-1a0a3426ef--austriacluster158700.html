Austria

proper noun
country in central Europe
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔstriə / (AmE), / ˈɒstriə / (BrE)
A country in central Europe, known for its mountainous terrain and long history as a political and cultural center, especially during the Habsburg rule.
