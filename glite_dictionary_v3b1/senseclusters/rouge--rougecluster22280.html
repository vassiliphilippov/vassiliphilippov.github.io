rouge

noun
red or pink cheek makeup
5.3 0.89/m
Pronunciation: / ruÀê í / (AmE), / ruÀê í / (BrE)
a red or pink powder or cream used as a cosmetic to give colour to the cheeks

Examples

A rouge compact
My grandmother always kept a compact of rouge in her purse.
She dabbed a little rouge on her cheeks before leaving the house.
The actress wore bright red rouge for her stage performance.

Synonyms

  • blusher
    3.1 0.12/m
    blusher is a common British name for cheek powder or cream used to shape the cheeks, while rouge sounds more French and is used in fashion.
  • blush
    2.8 2.58/m
    blush is an everyday word for cheek color or a cosmetic and also a verb for reddening, while rouge is mainly the noun for the product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blusher - a cosmetic used to give a warm color to the cheeks
