nuclear-powered ship

noun
ship propelled by nuclear reactor energy
1.0 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnuː.kliː.ər ˈpaʊ.ərd ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈnjuː.kliː.ər ˈpaʊ.ərd ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈnjuː.kli.ə ˈpaʊ.əd ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈpaʊ.əd ʃɪp/ (BrE)
