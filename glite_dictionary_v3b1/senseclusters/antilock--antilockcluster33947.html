antilock

noun
brake safety system
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌlɒk / (BrE)
a device or system in a vehicle that prevents the wheels from locking during braking to maintain steering control

Examples

An antilock system
The new car model comes equipped with an antilock to improve safety during sudden stops.
Mechanics recommend checking the antilock regularly to ensure it functions properly in emergencies.
Drivers appreciate the antilock for helping them maintain control on slippery roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ABS - an abbreviation for antilock braking system
  • synonym: brake - a device for slowing or stopping a vehicle
