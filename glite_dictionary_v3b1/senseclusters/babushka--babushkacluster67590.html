babushka

noun
a Russian-style women's headscarf
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈbuʃkə / (AmE), / bəˈbʊʃkə / (BrE)
A woman's headscarf folded into a triangle and tied under the chin, traditionally worn by Russian peasant women.

Examples

A babushka scarf
In the market, women in traditional babushkas sold fresh vegetables and fruit.
She wore a brightly colored babushka to shield herself from the cold wind.
The elderly woman tied her babushka neatly under her chin before leaving the house.
