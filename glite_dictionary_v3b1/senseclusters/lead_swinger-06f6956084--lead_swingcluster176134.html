lead swinger

noun
central figure leading social dancing
3.8
Pronunciation: / lid ˈswɪŋər / (AmE), / liːd ˈswɪŋə / (BrE)
A person who is a prominent or central figure in social activities, especially one who leads or encourages dancing.

Examples

The lead swinger dances
As the lead swinger of the group, he inspired even the shyest guests to join in.
At every wedding, the lead swinger fills the dance floor with energy and excitement.
People looked to the lead swinger for cues on which dance would come next.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dancer - a person who dances or performs dance
  • antonym: wallflower - a person who does not participate in dancing or social activities
