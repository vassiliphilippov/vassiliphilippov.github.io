OMS

noun
medical services office
Pronunciation: / oʊ ɛm ɛs / (AmE), / əʊ ɛm ɛs / (BrE)
a department in an embassy or large organization that provides medical care and health services to staff and their families

Examples

OMS medical check
Before deployment, the soldier visited the OMS for a routine medical examination and vaccinations.
The embassy's OMS offers health check-ups and emergency care for all staff members.
If you feel unwell while working abroad, you should contact the OMS immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinic - a place where people receive medical treatment
  • synonym: infirmary - a place in an institution for the care of those who are ill
