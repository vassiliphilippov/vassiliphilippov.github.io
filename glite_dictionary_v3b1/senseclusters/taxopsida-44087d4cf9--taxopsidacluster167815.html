Taxopsida

noun
botanical class or subdivision for yews
9.9

a taxonomic class or subdivision in some botanical classification systems, comprising the yews (family Taxaceae) and their relatives among the gymnosperms

Examples

Taxopsida class plants
Modern classifications sometimes place the yews in Taxopsida rather than in their own family.
Some textbooks refer to the yews and their relatives as members of Taxopsida.
The class Taxopsida includes plants that are mostly evergreen conifers.
