crunchingly

adverb
with intense calculation
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves intensive calculation, problem-solving, or data processing, often with great effort.

Examples

Work crunchingly hard
The team worked crunchingly late into the night to finish the project on time.
She reviewed the financial statements crunchingly to ensure every detail was correct.
Engineers analyzed the data crunchingly before presenting their findings to management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intensively - in a highly concentrated or focused manner
  • synonym: laboriously - in a way that requires considerable effort and time
