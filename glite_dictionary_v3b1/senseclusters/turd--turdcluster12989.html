turd

noun
worthless or bad thing (slang/vulgar)
9.6
Pronunciation: / tɝd / (AmE), / tɜːd / (BrE)
an extremely bad, worthless, or disappointing person or thing (informal, often vulgar)

Examples

A real turd

His apology was just a weak turd of an excuse.
They tried fixing the old computer, but honestly, it's still a turd.
