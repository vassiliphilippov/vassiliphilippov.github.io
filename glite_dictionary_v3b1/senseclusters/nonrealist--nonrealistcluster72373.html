nonrealist

noun
opponent of realism
8.5
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈriː.ə.lɪst/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈriː.lɪst/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈriː.ə.lɪst/ /; / /nɒn.ˈriː.lɪst/ (BrE)
a person who does not believe in or follow realism, especially in art or philosophy

Examples

A nonrealist thinker
The philosopher was labeled a nonrealist because he rejected the existence of objective reality.
Many modern artists are considered nonrealists due to their abstract and experimental styles.
Her approach to literature marked her as a nonrealist among her more traditional peers.
