trainable

adjective
able to learn
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be taught or made to learn new skills or behaviors

Examples

A trainable dog
Some dog breeds are more trainable than others, making them easier for first-time owners.
The new software is highly trainable and adapts quickly to user preferences.
Children at a young age are especially trainable when it comes to learning languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teachable - able to be taught or instructed
  • antonym: untrainable - not able to be taught or trained
