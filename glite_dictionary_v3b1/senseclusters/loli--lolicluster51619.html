loli

noun
anime girl archetype
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊli / (AmE), / ˈlɒli / (BrE)
a slang term for a young or young-looking girl character in anime or manga, often depicted with childlike features

Examples

Anime loli character
Many fans enjoy stories featuring a loli as the main character.
The artist is known for drawing loli characters with large eyes and innocent expressions.
Some anime series include a loli to appeal to certain audiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moe - a term for cute or endearing characters in anime or manga
  • antonym: shota - a young or young-looking boy character in anime or manga
