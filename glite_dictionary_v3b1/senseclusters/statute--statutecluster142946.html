statute

noun
written law enacted by a legislature
Pronunciation: / ˈstætʃuːt / (AmE), / ˈstatjuːt / (BrE)
a written law made by a legislative body

Examples

A statute law
A new statute was enacted to regulate online commerce.
The company must comply with every statute related to employee safety.
Violating a statute can result in heavy fines and other penalties.
The city council met to discuss amendments to the existing statute on noise control.
The new statute requires all businesses to recycle their waste materials.
Under the current statute, you must be at least eighteen to vote.

  • synonym: ordinance - an authoritative order or decree, especially by a municipality
  • synonym: regulation - a rule made and maintained by an authority
