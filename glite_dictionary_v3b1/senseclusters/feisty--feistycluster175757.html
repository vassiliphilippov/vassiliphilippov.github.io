feisty

adjective
lively and courageous
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪsti / (AmE), / ˈfaɪsti / (BrE)
full of lively energy, determination, and spirit; often willing to fight, argue, or stand up for oneself

Examples

A feisty puppy
Despite her small size, the feisty puppy barked at the much larger dog.
He admired her feisty attitude in the face of adversity.
The debate became heated when the feisty candidate challenged the others' views.
Despite her small size, the feisty kitten hissed and arched her back at the dog.
Grandpa describes her as a feisty woman who never backed down from an argument.
He grew more feisty when his ideas were challenged in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirited - full of energy, enthusiasm, or courage
  • antonym: timid - lacking courage or confidence
