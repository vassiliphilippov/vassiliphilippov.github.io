piggy bank

noun
a child's coin bank, often pig-shaped
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡi bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡi baŋk / (BrE)
a small container, often shaped like a pig, used by children for saving coins or money
