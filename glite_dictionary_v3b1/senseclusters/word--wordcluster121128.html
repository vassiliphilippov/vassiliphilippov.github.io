word

verb
to phrase or express in words
Pronunciation: / wɝːd / (AmE), / wɜːd / (BrE)
to express something in particular words; to phrase or state something in a specific way

Examples

To word a question
Please word your answer carefully so that there is no misunderstanding.
She struggled to word her feelings about the situation.
The contract was worded in very precise legal language.
