check

noun
obstruction of an opponent in ice hockey
3.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
an act of obstructing an opponent, especially by using the body or stick, in ice hockey to prevent their progress or possession of the puck

Examples

A body check
Body checks are allowed in professional ice hockey but penalized if too aggressive.
He was sent to the penalty box for an illegal check during the second period.
The defenseman delivered a solid check along the boards to stop the opposing forward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: block - an act of stopping or hindering an opponent's movement
