green smut

noun
rice disease with green fungal powder
6.1 8.6
Pronunciation: /griːn smʌt/ (AmE), /griːn smʌt/ (BrE)
a disease of rice in which the grains are covered by a green powder composed of fungal conidia

Examples

Green smut disease
Farmers reported widespread green smut in their rice paddies after the rainy season.
Researchers are developing new ways to control green smut outbreaks in rice fields.
The presence of green smut reduces both the yield and quality of the rice crop.
