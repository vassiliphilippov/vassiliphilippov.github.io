remover

noun
substance used for removing something
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmuvɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈmuːvə / (BrE)
a substance or product used to take something away, especially by dissolving or cleaning it from a surface

Examples

Stain remover spray
I spilled ink on my shirt and used a stain remover to get it out.
Many people use a gentle makeup remover at the end of the day.
She bought a new paint remover to strip the old cabinets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solvent - a substance, usually liquid, that dissolves another substance
