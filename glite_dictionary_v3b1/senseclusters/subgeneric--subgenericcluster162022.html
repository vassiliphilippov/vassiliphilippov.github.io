subgeneric

adjective
below genus rank
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbdʒəˈnɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌbdʒɪˈnɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or denoting a rank below that of genus in biological classification

Examples

Subgeneric classification
The scientist described several subgeneric differences among the species in the study.
A subgeneric classification helps organize large genera into more manageable groups.
The new butterfly was placed in a unique subgeneric category based on its wing patterns.
