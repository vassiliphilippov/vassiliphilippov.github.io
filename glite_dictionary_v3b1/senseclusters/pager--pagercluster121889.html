pager

noun
electronic message-receiving device
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪdʒə / (BrE)
a small electronic device that receives and signals messages, usually by emitting beeps or vibrations, used especially to contact someone who is not near a telephone

Examples

A pager device
Doctors often carried a pager before mobile phones became common.
Her pager beeped in the middle of the meeting, signaling an urgent message.
The hospital still uses a pager system to alert medical staff to emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beeper - another term for pager, especially emphasizing the beeping alert
