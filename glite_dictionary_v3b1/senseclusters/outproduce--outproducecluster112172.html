outproduce

verb
to produce more
6.1 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Àåa ätpr…ôÀàduÀês /; / Àåa ätpr…ôÀàdus / (AmE), / Àåa ätpr…ôÀàdjuÀês / (BrE)
to produce more of something than another person, group, or competitor

Examples
