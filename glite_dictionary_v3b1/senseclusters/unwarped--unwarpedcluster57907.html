unwarped

adjective
not bent or twisted
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔrpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɔːpt / (BrE)
not bent, twisted, or distorted; remaining flat or in its original shape

Examples

Unwarped wood
The table was perfectly unwarped after years of careful use.
He selected an unwarped piece of wood for the new shelf.
It is important that the glass remains unwarped for a clear view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: straight - not curved, bent, or twisted
  • antonym: warped - bent or twisted out of shape
