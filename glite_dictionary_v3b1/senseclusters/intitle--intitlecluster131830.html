search operator
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪtəl / (BrE)
a search operator used to find web pages containing specific words in the title

Examples

intitle: travel
If you want to find articles about travel, use intitle:travel in your search.
The intitle operator helps narrow down results to pages with your keyword in the title.
Typing intitle:news will show you web pages with 'news' in their titles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inurl - a search operator to find pages with specific words in the URL
  • synonym: site - a search operator to limit results to a specific website
