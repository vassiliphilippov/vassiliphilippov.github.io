provocatively

adverb
in a provocative manner
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɑːkətɪvli / (AmE), / prəˈvɒkətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that causes a strong reaction, especially deliberately; in a manner intended to provoke.

Examples

Dressed provocatively
He dressed provocatively to make a bold statement at the party.
She glanced at him provocatively before making her next move.
The artist spoke provocatively about politics, challenging the audience's views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provocative - causing a strong reaction or emotion; intended to provoke
