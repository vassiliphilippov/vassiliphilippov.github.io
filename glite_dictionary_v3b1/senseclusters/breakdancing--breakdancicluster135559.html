breakdancing

noun
acrobatic street dance
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
a style of energetic street dance featuring acrobatic and athletic movements, often performed to hip-hop music

Examples

Breakdancing performance
He amazed the crowd with his breakdancing skills during the talent show.
Many young people learn breakdancing as part of hip-hop culture.
The competition featured impressive breakdancing routines from dancers around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: b-boying - another term for breakdancing, especially in hip-hop culture
  • synonym: hip-hop dance - a broader category of street dance styles performed to hip-hop music
