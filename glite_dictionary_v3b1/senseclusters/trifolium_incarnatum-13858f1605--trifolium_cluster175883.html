Trifolium incarnatum

noun
a species of crimson-flowered clover
Pronunciation: / traɪˈfoʊliəm ɪnˈkɑrnaːtəm / (AmE), / traɪˈfəʊliəm ɪnˈkɑːnætəm / (BrE)
a species of clover native to southern Europe, with spiky heads of crimson flowers, cultivated as a forage plant
