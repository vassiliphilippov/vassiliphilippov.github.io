modulation

noun
the act of adjusting or changing something
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdʒəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of changing or adjusting something, especially to achieve a particular effect or balance

Examples

Careful modulation of color
Careful modulation of color is essential in landscape painting.
The modulation of her voice captured the audience's attention.
The lighting designer achieved the mood with subtle modulation of intensity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustment - the process of making small changes to improve a result
  • synonym: alteration - a change or modification
