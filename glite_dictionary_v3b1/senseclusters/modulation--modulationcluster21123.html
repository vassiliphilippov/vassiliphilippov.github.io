modulation

noun
the act of adjusting or changing something

Examples

Careful modulation of color

Synonyms

    adjustment is a small change to make something fit or work better, while modulation is fine control to reach balance or effect.
  • modification
    3.9 18/m
    modification is a larger or more lasting change to suit a need, while modulation is adjusting levels to achieve a balance or effect.
