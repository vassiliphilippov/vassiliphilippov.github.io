radioactive dating

noun
dating method using radioactive isotopes
5.8 3.9
Pronunciation: /reɪ.di.oʊ.ˈæk.tɪv ˈdeɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /reɪ.di.əʊ.ˈæk.tɪv ˈdeɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of determining the age of an object by measuring the amount of radioactive material it contains, commonly used with isotopes such as carbon-14
