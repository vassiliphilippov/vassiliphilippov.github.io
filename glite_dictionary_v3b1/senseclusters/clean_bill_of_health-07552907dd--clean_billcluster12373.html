clean bill of health

noun
statement confirming health or good condition
6.3 2.8
Pronunciation: /kliːn bɪl əv helθ/ (AmE), /kliːn bɪl əv helθ/ (BrE)
an official statement that someone or something is healthy, in good condition, or free from faults
