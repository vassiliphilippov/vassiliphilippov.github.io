clean bill of health

noun
statement confirming health or good condition
Pronunciation: / klin bɪl əv hɛlθ / (AmE), / kliːn bɪl əv hɛlθ / (BrE)
an official statement that someone or something is healthy, in good condition, or free from faults

Examples

A clean bill of health
After the annual inspection, the restaurant received a clean bill of health from the health department.
The doctor gave her a clean bill of health following her surgery.
The engineer examined the bridge and issued a clean bill of health for continued use.
After months of worry, he was relieved to receive a clean bill of health from his doctor.
The audit concluded with a clean bill of health for the organization's financial records.
The inspector gave the restaurant a clean bill of health after the hygiene check.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill of health - official certificate stating health status, especially of a ship
  • synonym: clean bill - a statement confirming everything is in good order, often used in inspection contexts
