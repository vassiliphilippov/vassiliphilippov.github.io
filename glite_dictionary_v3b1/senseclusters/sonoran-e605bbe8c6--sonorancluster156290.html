Sonoran

noun
person from Sonora
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔɹən / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrən / (BrE)
a person who is from the Mexican state of Sonora or the Sonoran Desert region

Examples

A Sonoran artist
The famous artist is a proud Sonoran who often paints desert landscapes.
Many Sonorans celebrate their unique cultural traditions during local festivals.
As a Sonoran, she grew up surrounded by the beauty of the desert.
