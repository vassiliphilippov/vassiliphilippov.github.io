twain

noun
two items; a pair (archaic/literary)
Pronunciation: / tweɪn / (AmE), / tweɪn / (BrE)
two items of the same kind; a pair or couple, especially used in archaic or literary contexts.

Examples

The twain met
From the twain emerged a lasting friendship.
Legend says never the twain shall meet.
The twain were never seen apart in the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: couple - two people or things that are together
  • antonym: one - single item or unit
  • synonym: pair - two things that are used together or regarded as a unit
