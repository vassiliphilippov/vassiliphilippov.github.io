jocular

adjective
humorous; joking in manner
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑː.kjələr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
fond of joking or characterized by joking and good humor; humorous or playful.

Examples

A jocular tone
Despite the seriousness of the situation, she maintained a jocular tone.
He adopted a jocular approach to teaching, making every lesson entertaining.
James’s jocular remarks always lighten the mood at our meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humorous - funny or containing humor
  • antonym: serious - not joking; earnest in manner
