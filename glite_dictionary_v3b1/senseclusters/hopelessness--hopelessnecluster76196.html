hopelessness

noun
state of having no hope
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpləsnɪs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpləsnɪs / (BrE)
A feeling or state of having no hope; despair or lack of expectation for success or improvement.

Examples

Feeling hopelessness
After months of searching for a job without success, he was overwhelmed by hopelessness.
Her eyes revealed a deep hopelessness that words could not express.
The novel captures the hopelessness felt by people living through hard times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: despair - the complete loss or absence of hope
  • antonym: hope - a feeling of expectation and desire for a certain thing to happen
