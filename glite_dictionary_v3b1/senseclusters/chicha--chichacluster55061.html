chicha

noun
fermented grain drink
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːtʃə / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːtʃə / (BrE)
a traditional Latin American beverage made from fermented corn or other grains

Examples

Drink chicha at a festival
At the festival, we enjoyed a cup of homemade chicha with the local villagers.
The recipe for chicha has been passed down through generations in her family.
Tourists often try chicha when visiting rural areas in South America.
