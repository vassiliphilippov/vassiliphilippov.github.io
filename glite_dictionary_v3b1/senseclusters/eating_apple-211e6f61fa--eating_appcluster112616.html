eating apple

noun
apple for eating raw
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈitɪŋ ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈiːtɪŋ ˌapəl / (BrE)
An apple variety primarily grown or used for eating raw rather than for cooking.

Examples

A eating apple variety
Many eating apples have a sweeter taste compared to cooking varieties.
She prefers a crisp eating apple as a snack during the day.
The orchard grows several types of eating apples, including Gala and Fuji.
