copper pyrites

noun
a yellow copper and iron sulfide mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpər paɪˈraɪtiːz / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə paɪˈraɪtiːz / (BrE)
a yellow mineral consisting of copper and iron sulfide (CuFeS₂), used as an important source of copper; also known as chalcopyrite

Examples

Yellow copper pyrites
Extraction of copper from copper pyrites is an important industrial process.
Samples of copper pyrites display a distinctive yellow, metallic luster.
The miners discovered rich veins of copper pyrites deep within the hillside.
