earthmoving phase

noun
construction stage involving soil excavation
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌmuːvɪŋ feɪz / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌmuːvɪŋ feɪz / (BrE)
the stage of a construction project in which earth or soil is excavated, moved, or shaped to prepare the site for further building work
