defunctness

noun
state of no longer existing or functioning
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfʌŋkt.nəs / (AmE), / dɪˈfʌŋkt.nəs / (BrE)
The state or condition of no longer existing or being in use.

Examples

The defunctness of the old railway station made it a haunting sight in the town.
The committee discussed the defunctness of certain outdated policies that no longer served their purpose.
With the rise of digital streaming, the defunctness of video rental stores has become obvious.
