gearing

noun
arrangement of gears; geartrain
3.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪ.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgɪə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the arrangement of gears in a machine, used to transmit force or change motion or torque

Examples

Adjustable gearing system
Bicycles with adjustable gearing allow riders to tackle different types of terrain.
Improperly aligned gearing can cause excessive noise and mechanical failure.
The gearing in the clock needed to be repaired after years of use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gearbox - the housing containing a system of gears
  • synonym: geartrain - a connected set of gears transmitting motion
