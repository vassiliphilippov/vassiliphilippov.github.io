loosely

adverb
in a loose or relaxed way
3.0 3.12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀêsli / (AmE), / ÀàluÀêsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not firm, tight, or strictly controlled; in a relaxed or free manner

Examples

Loosely tied shoes
She tied the scarf loosely around her neck for a casual look.
The idea is loosely based on true events but includes fictional elements.
The team members are loosely connected, working independently on different projects.
He held the leash loosely, letting the dog walk ahead.
The books were stacked loosely on the shelf, ready to be rearranged.
The scarf was draped loosely around her neck.

Synonyms

  • slackly
    5.8 0.06/m
    slackly is tied or held without tightness, while loosely often means not firmly held or strictly controlled.
  • relaxedly
    4.2 0.02/m
    relaxedly is calm and unhurried, while loosely often means not tight or not strictly controlled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freely - without constraint or restriction
  • antonym: tightly - in a firm or secure manner
