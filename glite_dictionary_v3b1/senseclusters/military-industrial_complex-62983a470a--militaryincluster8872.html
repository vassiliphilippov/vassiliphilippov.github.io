military-industrial complex

noun
national military and arms industries network
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛri ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈkɑmplɛks / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪtəri ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈkɒmplɛks / (BrE)
