pigging

noun
pipeline cleaning process
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cleaning or inspecting a pipeline by sending a device called a pig through it

Examples

Pipeline pigging
Regular pigging helps maintain the efficiency of oil and gas pipelines.
The engineers scheduled pigging to remove buildup from the water supply line.
After pigging, the pipeline was free of blockages and operated smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pipeline cleaning - the removal of debris or buildup from inside a pipeline
  • synonym: pipeline inspection - the examination of pipelines to check their condition
