Karbala

proper noun
holy city in central Iraq
8.7 0.38/m
Pronunciation: / k…ërÀàb…ëÀêl…ô / (AmE), / k…ëÀêÀàb…ëÀêl…ô / (BrE)
A city in central Iraq, regarded as a holy city by Shiite Muslims, known for being the site of the tomb of Husayn ibn Ali, the grandson of the Prophet Muhammad, who was killed there in 680 AD.

Examples

Karbala city in Iraq
Many pilgrims visit Karbala each year to commemorate the martyrdom of Husayn ibn Ali.
Religious ceremonies in Karbala draw visitors from across the world.
The Battle of Karbala holds great historical significance for Shia Muslims.
