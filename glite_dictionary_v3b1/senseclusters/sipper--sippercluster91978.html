sipper

noun
person who sips a drink
2.8 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪp.ə/ (BrE)
a person who drinks a liquid in small amounts at a time, rather than gulping

Examples

A slow sipper
As a slow sipper, he always finished his coffee long after everyone else.
She's more of a sipper than a drinker, savoring her tea throughout the afternoon.
The wine tasting attracted both seasoned connoisseurs and casual sippers.
