senor

noun
Spanish title for a man
Pronunciation: / seɪˈnjɔr / (AmE), / seˈnjɔː / (BrE)
a Spanish courtesy title or form of address for a man, similar to 'Mr' or 'sir' in English

Examples

Senor Garcia
'Senor Garcia will be joining us for dinner this evening.'
During the meeting, everyone addressed him as 'senor' out of respect.
The waiter greeted us with a polite 'senor' and a friendly smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: senora - Spanish title for a married woman
  • synonym: senorita - Spanish title for an unmarried young woman
