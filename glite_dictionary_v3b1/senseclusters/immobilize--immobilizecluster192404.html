immobilize

verb
prevent from moving
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz /; / ɪˈmoʊ.bɪˌlaɪz /; / ɪˈmoʊ.bəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊbɪlaɪz /; / ɪˈməʊbəlaɪz /; / ɪˈməʊ.bɪ.laɪz / (BrE)
to prevent someone or something from moving or acting; to make immobile or powerless

The surprise attack helped to immobilize the enemy troops, leaving them completely defenseless.
