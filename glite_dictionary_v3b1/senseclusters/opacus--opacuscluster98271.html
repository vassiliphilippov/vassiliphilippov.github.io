opacus

noun
dense cloud layer
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpeɪkəs / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪkəs / (BrE)
a type of cloud layer that is thick enough to completely block the sun or moon from view

Examples

A layer of opacus
The weather report mentioned a thick layer of opacus covering the entire region.
We could not see the sun at all because of the persistent opacus overhead.
Pilots must be cautious when flying through opacus as visibility can be greatly reduced.

  • synonym: stratus - a low, uniform layer of cloud
