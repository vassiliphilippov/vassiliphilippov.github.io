unpolled

adjective
not surveyed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊld / (BrE)
not having been surveyed or asked for an opinion in a poll

Examples

Unpolled voters
The unpolled voters could change the outcome of the election.
Many rural areas remained unpolled due to difficult access.
Analysts warned that unpolled groups might introduce unexpected results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polled - having been surveyed or asked for an opinion
  • synonym: unsurveyed - not examined or questioned in a survey
