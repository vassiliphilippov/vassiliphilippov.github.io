arrears

noun
money overdue or unpaid debts
Pronunciation: / əˈrɪrz / (AmE), / əˈrɪəz / (BrE)
money that is owed and should have been paid earlier; being behind on payments

Examples

Arrears on payments
Because his loan was in arrears, the bank sent him several warning letters.
She paid the rent in arrears after finally receiving her paycheck.
The company was struggling with salaries that were months in arrears.
After missing two payments, the loan was in arrears and subject to late fees.
Her account showed several months of arrears, requiring immediate payment.
The company has been threatened with legal action due to arrears in rent.
