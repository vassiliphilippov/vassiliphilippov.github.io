flight surgeon

noun
doctor specializing in aviation medicine
4.8 5.2
Pronunciation: /flaɪt ˈsɜːr.dʒən/ /; / /flaɪt ˈsɜːr.dʒɪn/ (AmE), /flaɪt ˈsɜː.dʒən/ /; / /flaɪt ˈsɜː.dʒɪn/ (BrE)
a medical officer who specializes in aviation medicine, responsible for the health and fitness of aircrew and astronauts

Examples

Flight surgeon monitors pilots

Every astronaut must be cleared by a flight surgeon before a mission.
The flight surgeon conducted medical checks before the crew boarded the aircraft.
