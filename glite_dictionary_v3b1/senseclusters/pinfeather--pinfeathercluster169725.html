pinfeather

noun
stiff feather remnant
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪn.feð.ər/ (AmE), /ˈpɪn.feð.ə/ (BrE)
a short, stiff feather or quill left in the skin of a bird after plucking, often sharp to the touch
