brass

noun
section in a band/orchestra playing brass instruments
Pronunciation: / bræs / (AmE), / brɑːs / (BrE)
a musical instrument made of brass, such as a trumpet or trombone, or the group of musicians who play these instruments in a band or orchestra

She learned to play several brass instruments, including the trumpet and trombone.
The orchestra's brass section played a powerful fanfare to open the concert.
