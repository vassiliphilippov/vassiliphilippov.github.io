relock

verb
to lock again
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈlɑk / (AmE), / ˌriːˈlɒk / (BrE)
to lock something again after it has been unlocked

Examples

To relock the door
After checking the windows, please relock the front door before you leave.
He forgot to relock his bike, and it was stolen from the rack.
The security guard will relock all the gates at the end of the day.

  • antonym: unlock - to open or release a lock
