plaster

verb
to apply a plaster cast to a limb
3.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈplæs.tər/ (AmE), /ˈplɑː.stə/ (BrE)
to cover or immobilize a part of the body with a plaster cast, or to cover a wound with a medical dressing

Examples

To plaster a broken leg
After the accident, they had to plaster his leg for six weeks.
She watched the nurse carefully plaster his broken wrist in the emergency room.
The doctor will plaster your arm to help the bone heal properly.
After the fall, he plastered ointment onto his scraped knee.
Old remedies often involved plastering herbal pastes on injuries.
The nurse quickly plastered the wound to prevent infection.

  • synonym: cast - a hard protective covering applied to immobilize a body part
