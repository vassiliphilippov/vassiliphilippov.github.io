transisthmian

adjective
on both sides of isthmus
Pronunciation: / trænzˈɪsθmiən / (AmE), / trænzˈɪsθmiən / (BrE)
occurring on or relating to both sides of an isthmus, especially in reference to the distribution of species or phenomena.

Examples

Transisthmian migration
The transisthmian migration of animals has fascinated biologists for decades.
Researchers studied the transisthmian plant species found on both sides of the narrow land bridge.
A transisthmian corridor can influence the spread of diseases between continents.
