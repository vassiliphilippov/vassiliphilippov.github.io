baton

noun
relay race tube
6.1
Pronunciation: / bəˈtɑn / (AmE), / bəˈtɒn / (BrE)
a hollow tube that is passed from one runner to another in a relay race

Examples

Relay race baton
Each runner must pass the baton smoothly to the next teammate during the relay.
She dropped the baton at the handover, causing her team to lose valuable seconds.
The shiny baton gleamed as it exchanged hands during the final leg of the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relay - a race in which team members take turns running parts of the distance
