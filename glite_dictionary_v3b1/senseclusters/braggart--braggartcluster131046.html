braggart

noun
a boastful person
Pronunciation: / ˈbræɡərt / (AmE), / ˈbræɡət / (BrE)
a person who boasts or brags excessively, especially in a loud or self-important way

Examples

A braggart person
Everyone at the party avoided the braggart who wouldn't stop talking about his wealth.
She found his braggart attitude off-putting and arrogant.
The braggart claimed he could complete the project twice as fast as anyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boaster - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest - not showing excessive pride, humble
