pink sand verbena

noun
coastal plant with pink or white flowers
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk sænd vərˈbinə / (AmE), / pɪŋk sand vəˈbiːnə / (BrE)
A prostrate herb with clusters of deep pink to white flowers, native to coastal dunes from British Columbia to Baja California.
