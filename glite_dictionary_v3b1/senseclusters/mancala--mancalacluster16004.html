mancala

noun
game board with pits
8.8
Pronunciation: / mænˈkɑːlə / (AmE), / mænˈkɑːlə / (BrE)
a board with rows of small pits or cups, used to play traditional mancala games involving moving stones or seeds.

Examples

A wooden mancala board
The children gathered around the mancala to play after dinner.
He bought a handcrafted mancala as a souvenir from his trip to Africa.
Learning the rules of mancala was easy, but mastering the strategy took time.
