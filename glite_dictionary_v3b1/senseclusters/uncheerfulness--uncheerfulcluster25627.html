uncheerfulness

noun
gloomy or pessimistic sadness
3.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɪr.fəl.nəs /; / ˌʌnˈtʃɪɚ.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪə.f(ə)l.nəs /; / ˌʌnˈtʃɪə.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
a state or quality of being gloomy, sad, or lacking cheerfulness

Examples

A feeling of uncheerfulness
Despite the festive decorations, a sense of uncheerfulness lingered in the room.
He spoke with such uncheerfulness that it was hard to remain optimistic.
Her uncheerfulness cast a shadow over the entire group during the trip.
Despite the rain and general uncheerfulness, they tried to stay positive.
Her persistent uncheerfulness affected the entire team's mood during the project.
There was a certain uncheerfulness in the air after the news was announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality or state of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: gloom - a state of partial or total darkness; also, a feeling of sadness or depression
