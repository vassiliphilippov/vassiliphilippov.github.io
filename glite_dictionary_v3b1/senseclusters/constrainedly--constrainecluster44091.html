constrainedly

adverb
in a constrained manner
7.7 6.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈstreɪndɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈstreɪndɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows restraint, stiffness, or lack of naturalness; in a constrained manner.

Examples

To smile constrainedly
He smiled constrainedly, unable to hide his discomfort with the situation.
She answered constrainedly, clearly struggling to express her true feelings.
The guests chatted constrainedly at the formal dinner, avoiding controversial subjects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in an awkward manner
  • antonym: naturally - in a natural manner, without constraint
