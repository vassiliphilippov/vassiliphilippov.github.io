coachmaker

noun
bus body builder
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtʃˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that designs and builds the bodies of motor coaches or buses

Examples

Modern coachmaker
The coachmaker unveiled a new design for luxury buses at the trade show.
She trained as a coachmaker and now leads a team building modern coaches.
Many historic cities once had a coachmaker on nearly every street.

  • synonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes goods for sale
