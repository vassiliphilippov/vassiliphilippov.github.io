telecommuter

noun
remote worker
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪkəˈmjuːtər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkəˈmjuːtə / (BrE)
a person who works from home or another remote location using the internet or other electronic communication tools

Examples

A telecommuter at home
As a telecommuter, she enjoys a flexible work schedule and avoids daily traffic.
Many telecommuters set up dedicated home offices to stay productive.
The company hired several telecommuters to reduce the need for office space.

Related Terms

  • antonym: office worker - a person who works in a traditional office setting
  • synonym: remote worker - a person who works outside a traditional office, often from home
