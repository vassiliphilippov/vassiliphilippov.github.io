Synonyms

  • act
    1.8 18/m
    an act is a single staged performance by one or more entertainers, while a routine is a planned sequence often repeated.
  • number
    6.5 7/m
    a number is often a musical or dance piece in a program, while a routine is any planned short performance.
  • turn
    5.4 2/m
    a turn is a short slot for one performer in a variety show, while a routine is a full prepared sequence.
  • bit
    4.6 3/m
    a bit is a very short, often comic piece inside a show, while a routine is usually longer and more worked out.
