stop

noun
organ control for pipe selection (music)
3.4
Pronunciation: / stɑp / (AmE), / stɒp / (BrE)
(music) a knob or handle on a pipe organ that is used to control which set of pipes sounds, changing the quality of the sound produced.

Examples

Organ stop knob
Each stop on the new organ was labeled with the name of the pipe set it controlled.
She explained how pulling out the right stop could transform the entire sound of the organ.
The organist carefully adjusted each stop to achieve the desired tone for the piece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: register - a set of pipes controlled by a stop on an organ
