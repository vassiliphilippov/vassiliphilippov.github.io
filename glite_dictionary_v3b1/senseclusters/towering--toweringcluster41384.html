towering

adjective
very tall or exceptionally high
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaʊ.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Very tall, impressive, or rising high above others.

Examples

Towering rock cliffs
Cliffs of towering rock rose above the coastline, creating a dramatic landscape.
He stood beneath the towering trees, feeling very small in the ancient forest.
The towering skyscrapers dominated the city skyline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lofty - of imposing height; high or tall
  • antonym: low - not high or tall; below average height
