utterer

noun
someone or something that utters sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈʌt̬ərər /; / ˈʌtərər / (AmE), / ˈʌtərə / (BrE)
a person who speaks, produces vocal sounds, or expresses something in speech or writing

Examples

Wise utterer
As an utterer of wisdom, she was highly respected in her community.
Parrots are known as effective utterers of human words.
The utterer of the threat was never identified by the police.
During the debate, each utterer was given five minutes to make their case.
He was known as an utterer of wise proverbs in his community.
The utterer of such harsh words offended many in the audience.
