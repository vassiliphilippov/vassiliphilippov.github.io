breathing room

noun
space or freedom to operate or move
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbriðɪŋ ˌrum / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
sufficient space or freedom to move, act, or think without restriction; room to operate or feel comfortable

Examples

Need breathing room
After a hectic week, I need some breathing room to relax and recharge.
He shifted his chair to create a little breathing room during the crowded meeting.
The new regulations give businesses more breathing room to innovate and grow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: space - unoccupied area or room
