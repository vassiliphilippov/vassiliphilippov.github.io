cold fish

noun
aloof, unemotional person (idiom)
2.1
Pronunciation: / koʊld fɪʃ / (AmE), / kəʊld fɪʃ / (BrE)
a person who is distant, unemotional, or lacking in warmth and friendliness

Examples

A cold fish person
Despite his impressive skills, he's often called a cold fish by his colleagues.
Many people see the CEO as a cold fish, but her friends say she's just shy.
She acted like a cold fish during the family gathering, barely speaking to anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice queen - a woman who is emotionally cold or distant
  • antonym: warm personality - a person who is friendly and emotionally open
