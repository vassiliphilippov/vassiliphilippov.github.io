Scirpus cyperinus

noun
a North American sedge species
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪr.pəs saɪ.pəˈraɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪə.pəs sɪ.pəˈraɪ.nəs / (BrE)
A species of sedge native to eastern North America, characterized by numerous clustered woolly spikelets.
