amorphic

adjective
without definite shape
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔːrfɪk / (AmE), / əˈmɔːfɪk / (BrE)
having no definite shape or form; shapeless

Examples

An amorphic shape
The artist created an amorphic sculpture that seemed to flow without boundaries.
Some minerals are amorphic and do not form regular crystals.
The cloud took on an amorphic appearance as it drifted across the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amorphous - lacking a clear shape or structure
  • synonym: shapeless - without a distinct or regular shape
  • antonym: structured - having a clear or definite shape or arrangement
