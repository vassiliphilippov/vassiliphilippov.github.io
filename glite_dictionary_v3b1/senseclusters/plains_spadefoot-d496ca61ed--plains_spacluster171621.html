plains spadefoot

noun
species of spadefoot toad in North American plains
8.4
Pronunciation: /pleɪnz ˈspeɪd.fʊt/ (AmE), /pleɪnz ˈspeɪd.fʊt/ (BrE)
a species of spadefoot toad (Spea bombifrons) found in the plains, hills, and river bottoms of central North America, especially east of the Rocky Mountains

Examples

Plains spadefoot toad
During spring rains, plains spadefoot toads emerge from burrows to breed in temporary pools.
Researchers observed a population of plains spadefoot near the riverbanks this summer.
The call of the plains spadefoot is often heard at night on the open prairie.
