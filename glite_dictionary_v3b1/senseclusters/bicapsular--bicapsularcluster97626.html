bicapsular

adjective
having two capsules or two-part capsule
9.9
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkæpsjələr / (AmE), / baɪˈkapsjʊlə / (BrE)
(technical) Divided into two capsules or having a capsule composed of two parts, especially in anatomy or botany.

Examples

A bicapsular structure

The botanist noted the rare bicapsular arrangement in the examined plant.
The flower produced a bicapsular fruit, each section containing numerous seeds.
