facing

noun
lining on the edge of a garment
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
a strip of material sewn to the edge of a garment as a lining for ornamentation or to strengthen the edge

Examples

A facing on a collar
A cotton facing was applied to the hem for extra durability.
The dressmaker added a silk facing to give the collar a clean edge.
Without a proper facing, the jacket's edge would have frayed easily.
