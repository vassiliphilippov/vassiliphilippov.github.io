manna gum

noun
Australian eucalyptus tree yielding false manna
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmænə ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈmænə ɡʌm / (BrE)
a tall Australian eucalyptus tree (Eucalyptus viminalis), known for exuding a sugary substance called false manna

Examples

The manna gum tree
A sticky, sweet substance called false manna can be collected from the bark of the manna gum.
Koalas feed on the leaves of the manna gum, which are a staple in their diet.
The manna gum is often found along riverbanks in southeastern Australia.

Related Terms
