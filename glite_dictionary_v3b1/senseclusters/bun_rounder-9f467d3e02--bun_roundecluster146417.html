bun rounder

noun
machine for shaping dough balls
Pronunciation: / bʌn ˈraʊndər / (AmE), / bʌn ˈraʊndə / (BrE)
a machine that divides dough into small portions and shapes each into a uniform ball, typically for making buns or rolls

Examples

A bun rounder machine
The bakery invested in a new bun rounder to increase production efficiency.
Using a bun rounder ensures that each roll has the same size and texture.
Workers feed dough into the bun rounder, which shapes it into perfect balls.
