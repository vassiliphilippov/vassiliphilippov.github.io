localized

adjective
adapted for a specific locale (software)
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.kə.laɪzd/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.kə.laɪzd/ (BrE)
(of software, websites, or digital content) adapted for the language, cultural conventions, and regional settings of a particular locale.

Examples

Localized app
The app is localized for Japanese users, including language, date formats, and currency.
We hired a team to ensure the website was properly localized for regional spelling and legal notices.
A localized marketing campaign used local idioms and holiday references to increase engagement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adapt - to change something so it is suitable for a new purpose or situation
  • antonym: internationalize - to design software or content so it can be easily adapted for different locales
  • synonym: translate - to convert text or speech from one language into another
