diagnosis

noun
identifying the nature or cause of a problem
Pronunciation: / daɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / daɪəɡˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the act of identifying the nature or cause of a problem, particularly in medicine or other fields

Examples

A diagnosis of illness
A correct diagnosis is essential for successful treatment.
Early diagnosis of the issue allowed the engineers to fix the machine quickly.
The doctor made a diagnosis after reviewing all of the patient's test results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: identification - the act of identifying something or someone
  • antonym: prognosis - a prediction of the likely course of a disease or problem
