round

noun
song with overlapping vocal parts
6.2
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
a song for several voices in which each voice starts at a different time, so that the parts overlap harmoniously

Examples

Sing a round
Singing a round requires careful listening so the parts fit together.
The choir performed a lively round at the school concert.
We learned to sing a round in music class, starting with 'Row, Row, Row Your Boat.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: canon - musical composition where parts enter successively and imitate each other
