carabao

noun
Philippine water buffalo
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəˈbaʊ / (AmE), / ˌkærəˈbaʊ / (BrE)
A domesticated water buffalo native to the Philippines, used especially as a draught animal.

Examples

A carabao animal
During the festival, a parade of decorated carabaos marched through the crowded streets.
Many children in the village grew up learning how to care for the family's carabao.
The carabao is an essential animal for rice farmers in the Philippines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water buffalo - large domesticated bovid used for draught and dairy in Asia
