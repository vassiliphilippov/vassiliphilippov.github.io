feel like a plonker

verb
to feel foolish or embarrassed
8.4
Pronunciation: / fil laɪk ə ˈplɑŋkər / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈplɒŋkə / (BrE)
to feel foolish or silly, often after making a mistake or behaving awkwardly

Examples

To feel like a plonker after mistake
After tripping over my own feet in front of everyone, I really felt like a plonker.
Missing the meeting because I forgot the time made me feel like a plonker.
She felt like a plonker when she realized she’d been talking to the wrong person all along.

  • synonym: make a fool of oneself - to behave in a way that makes you look silly or foolish
