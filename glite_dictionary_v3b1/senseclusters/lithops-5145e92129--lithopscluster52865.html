Lithops

noun
genus of South African succulents
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθɑps / (AmE), / ˈlɪθɒps / (BrE)
A genus of small, stemless South African succulents, commonly called 'living stones', known for their stone-like appearance and ability to survive in arid environments.
