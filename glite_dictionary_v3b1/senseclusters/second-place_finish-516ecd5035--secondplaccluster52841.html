second-place finish

noun
finishing in second position
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd pleɪs ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd pleɪs ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
a result in a competition where a competitor finishes in second position

Examples

A second-place finish in race
After his second-place finish in the race, he promised to train harder for next time.
Despite a second-place finish, she was proud of her performance.
The athlete's second-place finish was celebrated by her teammates.
