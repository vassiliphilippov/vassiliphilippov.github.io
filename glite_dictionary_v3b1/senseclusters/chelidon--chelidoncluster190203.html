chelidon

noun
rare word for swallow
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛlɪˌdɒn / (AmE), / ˈkɛlɪˌdɒn / (BrE)
an old or rare term for a swallow, a small migratory bird known for its forked tail and graceful flight

Examples

The chelidon bird
In ancient poetry, the chelidon was celebrated as a harbinger of spring.
The old manuscript described the flight of the chelidon over the river.
Few people today use the word chelidon to refer to the common swallow.
