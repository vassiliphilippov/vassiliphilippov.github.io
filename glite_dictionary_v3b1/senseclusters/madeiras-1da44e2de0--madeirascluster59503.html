Madeiras

proper noun
volcanic islands in the Atlantic, part of Portugal
Pronunciation: / məˈdɛrəz / (AmE), / məˈdeərəz / (BrE)
a group of volcanic islands in the Atlantic Ocean, west of Morocco, forming an autonomous region of Portugal
