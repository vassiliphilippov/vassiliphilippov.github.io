alien

noun
a person who does not belong in a setting
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.li.ən / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.li.ən / (BrE)
a person who does not feel or is not considered as belonging to a particular group, place, or environment

Examples

An alien in school
After moving to the new city, she often felt like an alien among her classmates.
As a newcomer, he couldn't help feeling like an alien at the family reunion.
His unconventional ideas made him seem like an alien in the corporate world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outsider - a person who is not accepted as a member of a group
