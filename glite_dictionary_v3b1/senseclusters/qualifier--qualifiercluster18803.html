qualifier

noun
person/team advancing in a competition
4.7 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑː.ləˌfaɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɒl.ɪˌfaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person or team that succeeds in a qualifying round and is eligible to take part in the next stage of a competition or tournament

Examples

Tennis qualifier
After her impressive performance in the heats, she became a qualifier for the national finals.
Only four qualifiers from each group will advance to the next round.
The last qualifier surprised everyone by reaching the semifinals.
