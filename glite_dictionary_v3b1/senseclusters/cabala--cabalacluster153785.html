cabala

noun
esoteric mystical tradition or interpretation
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːlə / (BrE)
A secret or esoteric tradition or system of mystical interpretation, often associated with or resembling the Kabbalah.

Examples

The cabala tradition
Many new-age groups incorporate aspects of cabala into their spiritual practices.
Some scholars spend years studying ancient cabala in search of hidden wisdom.
The magician claimed to possess secret knowledge rooted in cabala.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kabbalah - Jewish mystical tradition of esoteric interpretation
