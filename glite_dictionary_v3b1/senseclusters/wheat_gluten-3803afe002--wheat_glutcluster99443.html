wheat gluten

noun
gluten protein from wheat
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /wiːt ˈgluː.tən/ /; / /hwiːt ˈgluː.tən/ (AmE), /wiːt ˈgluː.tən/ /; / /hwiːt ˈgluː.tən/ (BrE)
the gluten protein prepared or extracted from wheat, used especially in food products such as seitan or as a baking ingredient

Examples

Wheat gluten protein
Bread's chewy texture often comes from the presence of wheat gluten in the dough.

Some people with celiac disease must strictly avoid all forms of wheat gluten.
