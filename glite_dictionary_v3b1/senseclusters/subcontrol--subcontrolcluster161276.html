subcontrol

noun
secondary control mechanism
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbkənˌtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈsʌbkənˌtrəʊl / (BrE)

Examples

A subcontrol mechanism
The technician adjusted the subcontrol to fine-tune the machine's performance.
Each section of the system is managed by a separate subcontrol for greater efficiency.
If the main panel fails, the subcontrol can still operate essential functions.
