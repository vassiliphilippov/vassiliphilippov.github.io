rotor head

noun
hub of a helicopter's rotor system
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊtər hɛd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtə hɛd / (BrE)
the central part of a helicopter's rotor system to which the rotor blades are attached and about which they rotate

Examples

Helicopter rotor head
A damaged rotor head can seriously affect a helicopter's stability and safety.
Technicians replaced the rotor head as part of the routine helicopter maintenance.
The engineer inspected the rotor head for signs of wear before every flight.
