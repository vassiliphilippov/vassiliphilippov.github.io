veritas

proper noun
Roman goddess of truth
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛrɪtɑs / (AmE), / ˈvɛrɪtæs / (BrE)

Examples

The statue of veritas stood in the temple as a symbol of honesty.
Many universities use veritas in their mottos to represent the pursuit of truth.
