coll.

noun
abbreviation for collection
8.4
Pronunciation: / kɑl / (AmE), / kɒl / (BrE)
an abbreviation for collection, commonly used in bibliographies or museum records to refer to a group of items.

Examples

Smith coll.
The catalog lists the painting as part of the Smith coll. at the city museum.
In the bibliography, several rare books are marked with coll. to indicate their source.
The exhibition features artifacts from the private coll. of Dr. Anderson.
