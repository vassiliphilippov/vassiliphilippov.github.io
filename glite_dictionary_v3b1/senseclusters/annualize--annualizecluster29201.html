annualize

verb
to adjust to yearly rate
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈænjuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈanjʊəlaɪz /; / ˈænjuəlaɪz / (BrE)
to calculate or express something as if it happens every year, or to make something happen every year

Examples

The company decided to annualize its quarterly profits for a clearer financial overview.

The accountant will annualize your monthly expenses to help with budgeting.

To compare costs fairly, you should annualize the monthly expenses.
They plan to annualize the festival so that it becomes a regular event each year.
