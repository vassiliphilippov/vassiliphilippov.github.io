proprietorial

adjective
relating to ownership
Pronunciation: / prəˌpraɪəˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / prəˌpraɪəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to an owner or showing the attitude of someone who owns something

Examples

Proprietorial attitude
He gave her a proprietorial look as if he owned the place.
The museum guards watched the visitors with a proprietorial attitude.
She spoke in a proprietorial tone about the family business.
She placed a proprietorial hand on the back of the chair, making it clear it was hers.
His proprietorial attitude toward the project annoyed his colleagues.
The dog gave a proprietorial bark when strangers approached the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communal - shared by all members of a community
  • antonym: detached - not showing emotional involvement or ownership
  • synonym: possessive - showing a desire to own or control
  • synonym: proprietary - relating to ownership or an owner
