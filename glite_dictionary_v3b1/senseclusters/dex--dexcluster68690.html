dex

noun
game agility stat
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɛks / (AmE), / dɛks / (BrE)
a numerical attribute or informal abbreviation for dexterity, meaning agility, reflexes, or skill (especially in video games or movement)

Examples

High dex stat
If your dex is high, your character can dodge enemy attacks more easily.
She increased her dex to improve her chances of picking locks in the game.
A low dex score makes it harder to avoid traps and move quickly.
In video games, high dex allows your character to dodge attacks more easily.
Her dex made her an excellent pickpocket in the role-playing game.
You need good dex to master complex guitar solos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agility - the ability to move quickly and easily
  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of skill or grace in movement
  • synonym: dexterity - skill and ease in using the hands or body
  • antonym: strength - a numerical attribute representing physical power in games
