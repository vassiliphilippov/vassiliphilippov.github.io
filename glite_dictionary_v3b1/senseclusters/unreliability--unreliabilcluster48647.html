unreliability

noun
lack of reliability or trustworthiness
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˌlaɪ.əˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˌlaɪ.əˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of not being dependable or trustworthy

Examples

Chronic unreliability
His chronic unreliability made it impossible for the team to trust him with important tasks.
The unreliability of the service has caused many customers to switch to competitors.
They grew frustrated by the unreliability of the old car, which often broke down without warning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependability - the quality of being able to be depended on
  • antonym: reliability - the quality of being trustworthy or dependable
