unreliability

noun
lack of reliability or trustworthiness
4.6 4.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.riː.laɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ʌn.riː.laɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples

Chronic unreliability

The unreliability of the service has caused many customers to switch to competitors.
They grew frustrated by the unreliability of the old car, which often broke down without warning.
