Mordvinian language

noun
Finno-Ugric language of the Mordvinians
Pronunciation: / mɔrdˈvɪniən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɔːdˈvɪniən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Finno-Ugric language spoken by the Mordvinian people of Russia, comprising two main dialects: Erzya and Moksha.

Mordvinian language dialects

The Mordvinian language uses the Cyrillic script in its written form.
