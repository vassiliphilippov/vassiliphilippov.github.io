ringworm bush

noun
tropical shrub used as cure for ringworm
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋwɝm ˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋwəːm ˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a tropical shrub, especially in the Americas, with yellow flowers and large leaves, whose juice is used to treat ringworm and poisonous bites; sometimes classified in the genus Cassia
