watch dubs

verb
view translated voiceover versions of media
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ dʌbz / (AmE), / wɒtʃ dʌbz / (BrE)
to view films, TV shows, or other media with dialogue that has been translated and recorded in a different language from the original

Examples

To watch dubs online
He doesn't mind if his friends watch dubs instead of the original version.
Many anime fans prefer to watch dubs rather than read subtitles.
When traveling, she would often watch dubs of her favorite shows in local languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dub - a version of a film or television program in which the original spoken language has been replaced with another language
  • antonym: watch subs - to view media using subtitles rather than dubbed audio
