upvalley

noun
upper part of valley
6.6

the upper part of a valley, especially the area closer to the valley's source or head.

Examples

The upvalley is cooler
The air in the upvalley is often cooler and fresher than in the lower areas.
Farmers in the upvalley rely on mountain streams for irrigation.
Wildlife is more abundant in the quiet forests of the upvalley.
