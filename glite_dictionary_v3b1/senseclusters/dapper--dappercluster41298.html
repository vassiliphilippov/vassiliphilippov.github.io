dapper

adjective
neatly and stylishly dressed (man)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdæpər / (AmE), / ˈdapə / (BrE)
(of a man) neat and stylish in appearance and manner.

Examples

A dapper man
Even in casual clothes, Daniel manages to appear quite dapper.
He always looks dapper in his tailored suit and polished shoes.
The dapper gentleman greeted everyone with a warm smile at the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sloppy - messy or untidy in appearance or dress
  • synonym: smart - (especially of a person) neat and stylish in appearance
