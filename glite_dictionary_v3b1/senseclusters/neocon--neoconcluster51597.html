neocon

noun
supporter of neoconservatism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnioʊˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈniːəʊˌkɒn / (BrE)
a person, especially a politician or intellectual, who advocates or subscribes to neoconservatism, typically favoring assertive foreign policy and free-market capitalism

Examples

A neocon politician
Although he was once a liberal, he later became a leading neocon.
Several prominent neocons supported the military intervention overseas.
She wrote an article criticizing the influence of neocons on foreign policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neoconservative - a supporter of neoconservatism, especially in politics
