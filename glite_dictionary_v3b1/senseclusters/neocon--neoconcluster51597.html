neocon

noun
supporter of neoconservatism
9.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.oʊ.kɑːn/ /; / /ˈniː.oʊ.kən/ (AmE), /ˈniː.əʊ.kɒn/ /; / /ˈniː.əʊ.kən/ (BrE)
