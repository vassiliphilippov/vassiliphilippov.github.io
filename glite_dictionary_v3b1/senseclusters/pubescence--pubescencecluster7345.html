pubescence

noun
period when puberty begins
6.6
Pronunciation: / pjuˈbɛsəns / (AmE), / pjuːˈbɛsns / (BrE)
the period in a person's life when the sex glands become functional and secondary sexual characteristics develop; the onset of puberty
