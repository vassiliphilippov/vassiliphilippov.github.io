infrapatellar

adjective
below the kneecap
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrəpəˈtɛlər / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrəpəˈtɛlə / (BrE)

Examples

Pain in the infrapatellar area can indicate a ligament injury.
