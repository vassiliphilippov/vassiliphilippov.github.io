wharf rat

noun
brown rat found around wharves or docks
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɔrf ræt / (AmE), / wɔːf ræt / (BrE)
a brown rat that lives around wharves, docks, or waterfronts, often considered a pest

Examples

A wharf rat at the docks
Local fishermen complained that wharf rats were getting into their bait and supplies.
She spotted a large wharf rat darting behind the crates near the wharf.
The old warehouses were infested with wharf rats that scurried along the docks at night.
