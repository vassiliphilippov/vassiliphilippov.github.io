applique

noun
decorative fabric piece
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæplɪˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌæplɪˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a decorative piece of fabric sewn onto a larger piece of material to create a design or pattern

Examples

A fabric applique
The quilt was covered in colorful applique shapes that formed a beautiful pattern.
She learned how to sew applique onto her shirts to make them unique.
Many traditional costumes feature applique as a form of decoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidery - decorative needlework on fabric
  • synonym: patch - a piece of material used to cover or decorate a surface
