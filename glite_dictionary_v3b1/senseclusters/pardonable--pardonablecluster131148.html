pardonable

adjective
able to be forgiven or excused
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrdənəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdənəbl̩ / (BrE)
That can be forgiven or excused; deserving to be pardoned.

Examples

Pardonable mistake
Arriving late was pardonable because of the heavy traffic.
Her actions, while hurtful, were ultimately pardonable in light of her apology.
His mistake was serious but still pardonable given the circumstances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forgivable - able to be forgiven
  • antonym: unpardonable - unable to be forgiven
