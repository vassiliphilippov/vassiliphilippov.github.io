hammer in

verb
teach through repetition and emphasis
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmər ɪn / (AmE), / ˈhamə ɪn / (BrE)
to teach or instill something by repeating it forcefully and frequently

Examples

To hammer in lessons
My parents always hammered in the value of honesty when I was growing up.
Teachers hammer in the basics of grammar until students remember them.
The coach tried to hammer in the importance of teamwork during every practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum into - to forcefully teach by continual repetition
