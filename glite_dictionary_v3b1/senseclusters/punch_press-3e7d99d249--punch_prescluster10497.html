punch press

noun
machine for cutting or shaping metal sheets
Pronunciation: / pʌntʃ prɛs / (AmE), / pʌntʃ prɛs / (BrE)
A machine that uses a die to cut or shape metal sheets by applying pressure, typically operated by electric or hydraulic power.

Examples

A punch press machine
A modern punch press can be programmed to cut a variety of shapes quickly and accurately.
Safety guards must always be used when working with a punch press.
The technician operated the punch press to create holes in the metal sheets.

  • synonym: press - a machine that applies pressure to shape or compact materials
