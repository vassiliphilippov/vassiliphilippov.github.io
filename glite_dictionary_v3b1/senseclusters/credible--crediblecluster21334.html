Synonyms

  • believable
    3.7 4.59/m
    believable feels easy to accept as true in everyday talk, while credible suggests stronger proof or expert support.
  • convincing
    3.4 11/m
    convincing shows strong reasons or arguments that make people believe, while credible means generally believable or trustworthy.
  • plausible
    7.2 14/m
    plausible seems reasonable and could be true, while credible suggests reliable evidence that makes people accept it.
  • trustworthy
    2.1 4.32/m
    trustworthy is a person or source you can depend on, while credible describes something that can be believed.
  • reliable
    2.7 30/m
    reliable works consistently and can be depended on, while credible focuses on being believable or accepted as true.
