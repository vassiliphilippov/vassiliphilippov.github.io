professional golf

noun
paid participation in golf
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənl ˈɡɑlf / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈɡɒlf / (BrE)
The sport of playing golf as a paid occupation rather than as an amateur.

Examples

Professional golf tournaments
He has been competing in professional golf tournaments for over a decade.
Many young players aspire to make a career in professional golf.
Women's professional golf has grown in popularity worldwide.

  • antonym: amateur golf - playing golf non-professionally, not for money
