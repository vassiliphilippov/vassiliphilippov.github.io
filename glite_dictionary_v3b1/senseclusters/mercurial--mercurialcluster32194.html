mercurial

adjective
with qualities of the god Mercury
9.5
Pronunciation: / mɚˈkjʊriəl / (AmE), / məˈkjʊəriəl / (BrE)
Having characteristics such as eloquence, shrewdness, swiftness, or thievishness attributed to the god Mercury.

Examples

Mercurial wit
His mercurial wit and eloquence won over even his harshest critics.
She possessed a mercurial swiftness and cunning that kept her enemies off guard.
The diplomat's mercurial shrewdness helped negotiate a difficult treaty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eloquent - fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
  • synonym: shrewd - having sharp judgment; astute
  • synonym: swift - moving or capable of moving very fast
