Rajab

proper noun
seventh month of the Islamic calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈrædʒəb / (AmE), / ˈrædʒæb / (BrE)
the seventh month of the Islamic calendar, observed as one of the four sacred months in Islam

Examples

Rajab month
During Rajab, some communities observe special prayers and fasting.
The festival was held in Rajab, a sacred month for many Muslims.
This year, the start of Rajab was marked by a public celebration.
