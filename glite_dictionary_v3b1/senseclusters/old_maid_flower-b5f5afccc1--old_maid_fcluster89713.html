old maid flower

noun
common name for zinnia flowers
8.1
Pronunciation: / oʊld meɪd ˈflaʊər / (AmE), / əʊld meɪd ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
a common name for any plant of the genus Zinnia, cultivated for their brightly colored flower heads

Examples

Bright old maid flowers
Because old maid flowers are hardy and vibrant, they are a favorite among gardeners.
Marigolds and old maid flowers add splashes of color to her garden each summer.
She planted rows of old maid flowers along the walkway for a cheerful entrance.
