football

noun
team sports with a ball and goals
1.0 64/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf ätÀåb…îl / (AmE), / Ààf ätb…îÀêl / (BrE)
Any of several team sports played with a round or oval ball in which two teams try to kick, carry, or propel the ball into each other's goal.

Examples

To play football
He has played football every weekend since childhood.
She bought a new football for her brother's birthday.
The World Cup is the most famous football tournament in the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: basketball - another sport involving teams and a ball, but with a hoop rather than goals
  • synonym: rugby - a form of football played with an oval ball that can be kicked or carried
  • synonym: soccer - a form of football played primarily with the feet and a round ball; known as 'football' outside North America
