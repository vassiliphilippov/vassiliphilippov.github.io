line management

noun
supervision of direct output activities
Pronunciation: / laɪn ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / laɪn ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the administration and supervision of employees and activities that directly contribute to an organization's main output

Examples

Effective line management
Effective line management ensures that company goals are directly supported by staff activities.
She was promoted to line management after demonstrating strong leadership skills in her team.
Training for line management focuses on supervising employees and improving productivity.
