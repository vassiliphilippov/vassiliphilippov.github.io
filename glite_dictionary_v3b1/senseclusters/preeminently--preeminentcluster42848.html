preeminently

adverb
to a superior or distinguished degree
8.3
Pronunciation: / priˈɛmənəntli / (AmE), / priˈɛmɪnəntli / (BrE)
To the highest or most distinguished degree; with superiority over others.

Examples

To preeminently stand out
She was preeminently qualified for the demanding position.
The art collection was preeminently modern, focusing on contemporary works.
This research field is preeminently important for technological innovation.
