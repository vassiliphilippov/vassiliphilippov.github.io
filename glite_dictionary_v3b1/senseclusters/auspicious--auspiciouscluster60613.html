auspicious

adjective
suggesting success; favorable
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈspɪʃəs / (AmE), / ɔːˈspɪʃəs / (BrE)
Suggesting a successful or favorable outcome; indicating good luck or positive circumstances.

Examples

An auspicious day
The ceremony was held on what was considered an auspicious day for the community.
The team's early win was seen as an auspicious sign for the rest of the season.
Their business partnership began under auspicious circumstances, leading to years of success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: favorable - giving or indicating a good chance of success
  • antonym: inauspicious - suggesting unsuccessful or unfavorable circumstances; unlucky
