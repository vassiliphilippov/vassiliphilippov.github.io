sub-assembly

noun
unit assembled separately for larger product
3.3 5.7
Pronunciation: /səb.ə.ˈsem.bliː/ /; / /sʌb.ə.ˈsem.bliː/ (AmE), /səb.ə.ˈsem.blɪ/ /; / /sʌb.ə.ˈsem.blɪ/ (BrE)
