matchable

adjective
able to be equaled
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmætʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be equaled or rivaled in quality, skill, or value by someone or something else

Examples

Matchable talent
Her skills on the piano are rarely matchable by other students in the class.
The team's record this season is hardly matchable by any of their rivals.
He set a standard of excellence that was barely matchable in the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comparable - able to be compared in quality or value
  • antonym: unbeatable - impossible to defeat or surpass
