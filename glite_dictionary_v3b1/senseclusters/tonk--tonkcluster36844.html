tonk

noun
metallic thudding sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɑŋk / (AmE), / tɒŋk / (BrE)
a loud, metallic or hollow sound, similar to a clonk or thud

Examples

A loud tonk
The dropped pan hit the floor with a loud tonk.
He heard a sudden tonk as the metal pipe struck the ground.
A tonk echoed through the garage when the wrench slipped from his hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clonk - a dull, heavy sound made by a hard object
  • synonym: thud - a dull sound made by something heavy hitting a surface
