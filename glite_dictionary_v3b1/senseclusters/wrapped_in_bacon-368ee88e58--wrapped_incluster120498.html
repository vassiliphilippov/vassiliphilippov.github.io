wrapped in bacon

adjective
prepared covered in bacon
4.7
Pronunciation: / ræpt ɪn ˈbeɪkən / (AmE), / ræpt ɪn ˈbeɪkən / (BrE)
cooked or prepared by being completely covered in bacon strips, usually before baking or frying.

Examples

Wrapped in bacon snacks
He loves dates wrapped in bacon for holiday snacks.
The cookbook features several dishes wrapped in bacon to add flavor and texture.
The scallops were wrapped in bacon and served as an appetizer.
