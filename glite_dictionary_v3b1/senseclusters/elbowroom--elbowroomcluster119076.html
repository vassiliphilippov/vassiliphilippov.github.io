elbowroom

noun
freedom of movement
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlboʊˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈɛlbəʊˌruːm / (BrE)
enough space to move or act freely without restriction

Examples

Enough elbowroom
The new apartment gives us much more elbowroom than our old place.
During the meeting, everyone felt cramped and wished for a little more elbowroom.
Creative people need elbowroom to develop their ideas without interference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freedom - the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants
  • antonym: restriction - a limitation or control on movement or action
  • synonym: space - an area that is free or unoccupied
