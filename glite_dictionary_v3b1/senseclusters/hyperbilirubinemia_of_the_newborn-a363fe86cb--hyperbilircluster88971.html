Examples

Hyperbilirubinemia of the newborn condition
Doctors monitor babies closely for signs of hyperbilirubinemia of the newborn after birth.
Most cases of hyperbilirubinemia of the newborn are mild and resolve within a few days without treatment.
Phototherapy is sometimes used to treat hyperbilirubinemia of the newborn if bilirubin levels become too high.
