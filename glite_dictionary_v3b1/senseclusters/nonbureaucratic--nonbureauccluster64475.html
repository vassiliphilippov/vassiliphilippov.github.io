nonbureaucratic

adjective
without bureaucracy
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌbjʊrəˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
not involving or characteristic of bureaucracy, especially avoiding complex rules and procedures

Examples

A nonbureaucratic system
The company adopted a nonbureaucratic structure to encourage faster decision-making.
She appreciated the nonbureaucratic process that allowed her to get help quickly.
A nonbureaucratic approach can make organizations more flexible and responsive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bureaucratic - involving complicated rules and procedures
  • synonym: efficient - working in a well-organized and productive way
