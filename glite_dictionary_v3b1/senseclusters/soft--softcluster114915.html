soft

adjective
phonetics: hissing or hushing speech sound
7.9 0.30/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ”ft / (AmE), / sÉ’ft / (BrE)
(of a speech sound) produced with the back of the tongue raised toward the hard palate; characterized by a hissing or hushing sound, as in the case of 's' and 'sh.'

Examples

Soft consonant
In Russian, a soft consonant changes the meaning of the word.
Some languages distinguish between soft and hard sounds in their alphabets.
The teacher explained that the letter 's' has both soft and hard pronunciations.
