demolished

adjective
torn down; destroyed (structure)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑlɪʃt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃt / (BrE)
(of a building or structure) torn down and broken up; destroyed.

Examples

A demolished building
After the fire, only the remains of the demolished house could be seen.
The old warehouse was demolished to make way for a shopping center.
The stadium stood empty for years before it was finally demolished.

  • synonym: destroyed - ruined or put an end to existence
