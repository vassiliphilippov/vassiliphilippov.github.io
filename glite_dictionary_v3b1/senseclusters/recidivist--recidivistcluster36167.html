recidivist

noun
person who repeatedly offends criminally
8.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /rə.ˈsɪ.dɪ.vɪst/ /; / /rɪ.ˈsɪ.dɪ.vɪst/ (AmE), /rə.ˈsɪ.dɪ.vɪst/ /; / /rɪ.ˈsɪ.dɪ.vɪst/ (BrE)
