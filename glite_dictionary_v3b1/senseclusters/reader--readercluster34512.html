reader

noun
portable electronic book reader
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdər / (AmE), / ˈriːdə / (BrE)
a portable electronic device for reading digital books and other electronic documents (also called an e‑reader).

Examples

E-book reader
She bought a compact reader to carry her library of EPUBs on trips.
Travelers often prefer a lightweight reader over several paper novels to save luggage space.
The museum shop stocked a simple reader preloaded with guidebooks and maps for visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: e‑reader - a dedicated electronic device designed for reading e-books and digital publications
