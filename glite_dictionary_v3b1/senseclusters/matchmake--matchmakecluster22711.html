matchmake

verb
to connect people
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃˌmeɪk / (AmE), / ˈmætʃˌmeɪk / (BrE)
to introduce or connect people for non-romantic purposes, such as business partnerships or professional collaborations

Examples

To matchmake business partners
The organization helps matchmake entrepreneurs with experienced mentors in their field.
At the conference, volunteers will matchmake attendees based on their professional interests.
Our team aims to matchmake companies seeking partners for joint projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: network - to interact with others to exchange information and develop contacts
