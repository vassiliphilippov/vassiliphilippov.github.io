sudorific

noun
medicine that induces sweating

Examples

A sudorific herb
Herbalists sometimes use elderflower as a natural sudorific.
In the eighteenth century, physicians commonly recommended sudorifics for treating colds and flus.
The doctor prescribed a sudorific to help reduce the patient's fever.
