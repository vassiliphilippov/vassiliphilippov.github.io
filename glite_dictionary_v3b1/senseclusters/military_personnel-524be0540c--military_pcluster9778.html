military personnel

noun
members of the armed forces
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛri pɚˈsɑːnəl /; / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈpɜrsəˌnɛl / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛəri pəˈsɒnəl /; / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛːri pəˈsɒnəl / (BrE)
people who serve in the armed forces, such as soldiers, sailors, and airmen (collectively)
