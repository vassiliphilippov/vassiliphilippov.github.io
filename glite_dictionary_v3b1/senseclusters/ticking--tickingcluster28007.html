ticking

noun
strong fabric for mattresses and pillows
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
A strong, tightly-woven fabric, typically with a striped pattern, used for covering mattresses and pillows.

Examples

Ticking fabric cover
She chose blue-and-white striped ticking for the pillowcases.
The mattress was covered with sturdy ticking to protect it from wear.
Upholsterers often use ticking for its durability and tight weave.
