Cewa

noun
Bantu-speaking ethnic group of parts of southern Africa
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛwə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛwə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu-speaking ethnic group found in Malawi, eastern Zambia, and northern Zimbabwe

Examples

Cewa people
A delegation of Cewa elders attended the conference on cultural preservation.
Many Cewa communities continue to practice traditional farming methods.
The Cewa have rich traditions of song and storytelling.
