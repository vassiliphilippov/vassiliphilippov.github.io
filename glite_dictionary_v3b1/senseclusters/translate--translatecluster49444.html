translate

verb
be translatable in a certain way
Pronunciation: / trænzˈleɪt / (AmE), / trɑːnsˈleɪt /; / trænsˈleɪt / (BrE)
to express the meaning of spoken or written words in a different language

Examples

To translate a sentence
Certain puns simply can't translate without losing their original charm.
Some idioms do not translate well into other languages.
This novel translates surprisingly well into French, preserving much of its humor.
During the conference, Tom will translate for the visiting delegates.
She can translate medical documents from Spanish to English with ease.
The software can quickly translate text into several different languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpret - to translate spoken words orally
  • synonym: render - to express or represent in another form or language
  • antonym: transcribe - to put (thoughts, speech, or data) into written form
  • synonym: translatable - able to be translated into another language
