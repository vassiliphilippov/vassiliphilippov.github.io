Synonyms

  • detonate
    6.5 5/m
    detonate is a technical term for causing an explosive to go off, while explode is the general sudden bursting event.
  • blow up
    2.7 13/m
    blow up means to destroy or be destroyed by a strong explosion, while explode is more neutral and covers many burst types.
  • go off
    3.2 12/m
    go off is used for alarms or bombs that suddenly make a loud noise or activate, while explode is broader and more physical.
  • blast
    4.9 6/m
    blast means to break apart or clear an area with strong force, while explode focuses on the sudden bursting action.
  • burst
    1.4 10/m
    burst means to break open often from internal pressure, while explode implies a violent release of energy and loud noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow up - destroy by causing to explode
  • synonym: burst - to break open or apart suddenly and forcefully
  • synonym: detonate - to cause to explode, especially with a sudden and violent energy
  • antonym: implode - to collapse or burst inward violently, as with the opposite of explode
