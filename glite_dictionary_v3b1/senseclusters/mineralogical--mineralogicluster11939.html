mineralogical

adjective
relating to minerals
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnərəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɪnərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to minerals or the scientific study of minerals

Examples

A mineralogical study
The mineralogical analysis revealed a high concentration of quartz in the rock sample.
Researchers studied the mineralogical differences between volcanic and sedimentary rocks.
A mineralogical survey was conducted to identify valuable resources in the region.
The museum has a mineralogical collection featuring rare crystals from around the world.
Her mineralogical research contributed to the discovery of a new mineral.
Students attended a mineralogical field trip to study rock formations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biological - relating to living organisms and life processes
  • synonym: geological - relating to the study of the Earth's physical structure and substances
