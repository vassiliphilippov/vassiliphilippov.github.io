webber

noun
website creator
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbər / (AmE), / ˈwɛbə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who designs, develops, or maintains websites

Examples

A website webber
The company hired a skilled webber to improve their online presence.
As a freelance webber, she manages several clients' websites at once.
Many webbers work remotely from different parts of the world.
