any

determiner
referring to one, some, or all, not specific
Pronunciation: / ˈɛni / (AmE), / ˈɛni / (BrE)
used to refer to one, some, or every member of a group of people or things, without specificity.

Examples

Any person can join
If any problems arise, let me know immediately.
Is there any coffee left in the pot?
You may borrow any book you like from the shelf.

Related Terms

  • antonym: every - referring to all the members of a group considered individually
  • antonym: some - referring to an unspecified amount or number of things or people
