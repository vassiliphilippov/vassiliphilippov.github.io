Fujiyama

proper noun
highest mountain in Japan; extinct volcano
7.4
Pronunciation: /fuː.dʒiː.ˈjɑː.mə/ /; / /fʊ.dʒɪ.ˈjɑː.mə/ (AmE), /fuː.dʒɪ.ˈjɑː.mə/ /; / /fuː.dʒiː.ˈjɑː.mə/ (BrE)
an extinct volcano in south central Honshu, Japan, known as the country's highest peak and noted for its symmetrical, snow-capped summit; regarded as sacred and a frequent site for pilgrimages
