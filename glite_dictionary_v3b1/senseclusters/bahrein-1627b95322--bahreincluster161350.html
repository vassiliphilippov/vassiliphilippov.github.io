Bahrein

proper noun
island country in the Persian Gulf
Pronunciation: / bɑːˈreɪn / (AmE), / bɑːˈreɪn / (BrE)
an island country in the Persian Gulf, off the coast of Saudi Arabia, known for its oil resources and modern development; alternative spelling of Bahrain.

Examples

Bahrein island country
Many travelers visit Bahrein for its impressive modern architecture and vibrant culture.
Oil revenues played a major role in the rapid development of Bahrein throughout the 20th century.
The capital city of Bahrein is Manama, located on the northern coast of the island.
