clicker

noun
device for signaling
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪkər / (AmE), / ˈklɪkə / (BrE)
a small handheld device that makes a clicking sound, often used for signaling, counting, or training animals

Examples

Dog training clicker
The teacher used a clicker to keep track of how many students answered the question.
Dog trainers often use a clicker to mark good behavior during lessons.
She pressed the clicker to advance to the next slide in her presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counter - a device used to count things
  • synonym: remote - a device used to control something from a distance
