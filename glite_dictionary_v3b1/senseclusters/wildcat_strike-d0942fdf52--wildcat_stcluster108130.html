wildcat strike

noun
an unauthorized workers' strike
6.4 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪld.kæt straɪk/ /; / /ˈwaɪ.əld.kæt straɪk/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪld.kæt straɪk/ (BrE)
an unofficial strike by workers carried out without the authorization of their trade union

Examples

A wildcat strike
A wildcat strike can lead to disciplinary action since it is not approved by the union.
Management was caught off guard by the sudden wildcat strike at the factory.
The workers launched a wildcat strike after their demands for improved safety were ignored.
