Feldenkrais

noun
movement awareness method
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛldənˌkraɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɛldənˌkraɪs / (BrE)
a method of gentle movement and awareness exercises designed to improve body function and movement

Examples

Feldenkrais exercises
After her injury, she started practicing Feldenkrais to regain flexibility and ease of movement.
Many people find that Feldenkrais helps them become more aware of their posture and daily habits.
The Feldenkrais method is often recommended for those seeking gentle ways to improve mobility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Alexander Technique - a method for improving posture and movement habits
  • synonym: yoga - a practice involving physical postures, breathing, and meditation
