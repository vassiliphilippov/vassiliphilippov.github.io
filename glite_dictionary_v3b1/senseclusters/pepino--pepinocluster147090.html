pepino

noun
sweet fruit plant
9.4
Pronunciation: / pɛˈpinoʊ / (AmE), / pɛˈpiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a small evergreen shrub (Solanum muricatum) cultivated for its sweet, edible fruit, also called pepino melon

Examples

Pepino plant
The farmer grew several pepino plants in his garden for their sweet fruit.
She tasted a ripe pepino for the first time while traveling in South America.
A single pepino shrub can produce dozens of fruit each season.
