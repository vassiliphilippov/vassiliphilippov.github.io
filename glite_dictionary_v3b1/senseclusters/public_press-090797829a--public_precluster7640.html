public press

noun
the collective news-gathering print media
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk prɛs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk prɛs / (BrE)
the newspapers and magazines collectively, especially as news-gathering and publishing organizations

Examples

The public press
Freedom of the public press is essential to a functioning democracy.
Many people distrust the public press due to concerns about bias in reporting.
The public press played a crucial role in informing citizens about the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press - the media, especially newspapers and journalists collectively
