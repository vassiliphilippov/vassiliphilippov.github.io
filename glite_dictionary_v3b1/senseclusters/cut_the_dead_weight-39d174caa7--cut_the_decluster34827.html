cut the dead weight

verb
remove unnecessary or burdensome elements
Pronunciation: / kʌt ðə dɛd weɪt / (AmE), / kʌt ðə dɛd weɪt / (BrE)
to remove people or things that are unnecessary or slowing progress

Examples

To cut the dead weight
He realized he needed to cut the dead weight from his schedule to reduce stress.
The company decided to cut the dead weight by laying off unproductive staff.
To save time on the project, let's cut the dead weight and focus on the essentials.

  • synonym: streamline - to make a process or organization more efficient by removing unnecessary parts
