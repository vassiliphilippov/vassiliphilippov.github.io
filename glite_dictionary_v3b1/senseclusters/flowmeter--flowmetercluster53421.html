flowmeter

noun
fluid rate measuring device
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfloʊ.miː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈfləʊ.miː.tə/ (BrE)
a device used to measure the rate at which a liquid or gas flows through a system

Examples

A flowmeter device
The technician installed a flowmeter to monitor the water supply in the laboratory.
A faulty flowmeter can lead to inaccurate readings in industrial processes.
Engineers checked the flowmeter regularly to ensure the system was operating correctly.
The engineer checked the flowmeter to ensure the correct amount of gas was being delivered.
A flowmeter is essential in many laboratories to monitor chemical reactions.
They installed a new flowmeter to track the water usage in the building.
