dup

verb
to duplicate informally
8.6
Pronunciation: / dʌp / (AmE), / dʌp / (BrE)
to make a copy of something, especially a file or recording; to duplicate (informal)

Examples

To dup a disk
Can you dup this disk for me before the meeting starts?
He duped the video files so everyone could have a copy.
It's easy to dup your photos onto a backup drive.

  • synonym: duplicate - to make an exact copy of something
  • antonym: erase - to remove or delete something
