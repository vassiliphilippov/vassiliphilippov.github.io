get used to

verb
become accustomed to something or someone
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈjust tu /; / ɡɛt ˈjuzd tu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈjuːst tuː /; / ɡɛt ˈjuːzd tuː / (BrE)
to become familiar or comfortable with something so that it no longer feels strange or difficult

Examples

To get used to a new job
After several weeks, I finally got used to waking up early for work.
It can take time for children to get used to a new school.
You'll soon get used to driving on the left side of the road in the UK.
It takes time to get used to working night shifts.
She quickly began to get used to the local customs.
You will get used to the city noise after a few weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accustom - to make someone or yourself familiar with something so that it seems normal or usual
  • synonym: adapt - to adjust to new conditions or a new environment
