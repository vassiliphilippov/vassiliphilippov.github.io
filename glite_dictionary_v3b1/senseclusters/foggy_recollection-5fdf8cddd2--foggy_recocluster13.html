foggy recollection

noun
unclear or vague memory
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔɡi ˌrɛkəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡi ˌrɛkəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a vague or indistinct memory of something; an unclear recollection

Examples

A foggy recollection of events
After the accident, he was left with only a foggy recollection of what happened.
My foggy recollection makes it hard to remember the exact details of the meeting.
She has a foggy recollection of her childhood visits to the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear memory - a memory that is distinct and easy to recall
  • synonym: hazy recollection - an indistinct or faint memory
