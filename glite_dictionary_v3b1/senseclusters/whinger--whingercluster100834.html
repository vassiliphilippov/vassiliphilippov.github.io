whinger

noun
habitual complainer
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person who complains or whines frequently, especially about unimportant matters

Examples

A whinger complains
Nobody likes working with a constant whinger who finds fault with every little thing.
Instead of being a whinger, try to focus on the positive aspects of your job.
His friends teased him for being a whinger whenever plans did not go his way.
