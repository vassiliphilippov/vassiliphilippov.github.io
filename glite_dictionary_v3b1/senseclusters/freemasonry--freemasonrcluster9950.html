freemasonry

noun
instinctive fellowship among like-minded people
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌmeɪsənri / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌmeɪsənri / (BrE)
a natural or instinctive sense of fellowship and sympathy among people with similar interests or experiences

Examples

The freemasonry of journalists

There is a certain freemasonry that draws artists together, no matter their background.
Travelers often experience a freemasonry that makes strangers into friends overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camaraderie - a feeling of friendship and trust among people who spend a lot of time together
  • synonym: solidarity - unity or agreement of feeling or action, especially among individuals with a common interest
