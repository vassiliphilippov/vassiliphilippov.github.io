discretion

noun
refined taste; tact
4.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɛʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɛʃ(ə)n /; / dɪˈskrɛʃən / (BrE)
the ability to make careful, wise, and sensitive judgments, especially to avoid causing embarrassment or to handle private matters
