hypercholesterolemic

noun
person with high cholesterol
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərkəˌlɛstərəˈlimɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəkɒˌlɛstərəˈliːmɪk / (BrE)
a person or animal with an abnormally high level of cholesterol in the blood

Examples

Hypercholesterolemic person
The doctor recommended a special diet for the hypercholesterolemic to help reduce cholesterol levels.
Researchers studied how a hypercholesterolemic responds to different types of medication.
A hypercholesterolemic should avoid foods high in saturated fat and cholesterol.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperlipidemic - a person with abnormally high levels of lipids in the blood
  • antonym: normocholesterolemic - a person with normal cholesterol levels in the blood
