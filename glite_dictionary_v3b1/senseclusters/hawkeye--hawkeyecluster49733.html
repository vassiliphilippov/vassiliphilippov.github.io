hawkeye

noun
Iowa resident or supporter
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːkˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːkˌaɪ / (BrE)
a person from Iowa, especially a student, athlete, or supporter of the University of Iowa and its sports teams

Examples

Iowa hawkeye
She has been a loyal hawkeye since her first year at the University of Iowa.
Many hawkeyes gathered downtown to celebrate the team's victory.
As a proud hawkeye, he always wears black and gold on game days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Iowan - a native or resident of Iowa
