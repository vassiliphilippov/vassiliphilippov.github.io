midrashic

adjective
of Jewish interpretation
Pronunciation: / mɪdˈrɑːʃɪk / (AmE), / mɪdˈræʃɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Midrash, a traditional Jewish method of interpreting biblical texts

Examples

Midrashic interpretation
The professor delivered a midrashic analysis of the biblical story in her lecture.
Many midrashic traditions offer creative explanations for difficult passages in scripture.
He wrote a midrashic commentary that explored hidden meanings in the ancient texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rabbinic - relating to rabbis or Jewish religious teachings
