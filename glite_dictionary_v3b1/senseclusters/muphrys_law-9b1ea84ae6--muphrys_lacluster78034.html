Muphry's law

noun
errors in proofreading text about proofreading
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌfɹiz lɑ / (AmE), / ˈmʌfɹiz lɔː / (BrE)
the principle that any piece of writing about proofreading or editing is likely to contain typographical or language errors, especially in corrections
