dime

verb
to pay in installments
9.1
Pronunciation: / daɪm / (AmE), / daɪm / (BrE)
to pay for something in small amounts or installments rather than all at once

Examples

To dime the payment
Instead of buying the phone outright, she chose to dime it over several months.
He prefers to dime expensive items so he can manage his budget more easily.
They decided to dime their new furniture rather than pay the full price immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: installment - a sum of money paid in parts over time
  • antonym: lump sum - a single payment of the entire amount owed
  • synonym: pay off - to finish paying a debt or amount owed
