ill-affected

adjective
discontented or hostile toward authority
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪl əˈfɛktɪd / (AmE), / ɪl əˈfɛktɪd / (BrE)
discontented or hostile, especially toward authority; having an unfriendly or antagonistic attitude.

Examples

An ill-affected attitude
His ill-affected demeanor made it clear he did not trust those in charge.
Several ill-affected members of the committee voiced their objections loudly during the meeting.
The ill-affected citizens gathered to protest the new laws imposed by the government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disaffected - discontented and resentful toward authority
  • antonym: loyal - showing firm and constant support
