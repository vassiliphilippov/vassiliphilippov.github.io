vena canaliculi cochleae

noun
vein of the cochlear canal
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə kə.ˈnæl.ɪ.kjʊ.liː ˈkɑːk.li.iː/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə kə.ˈnæl.ɪ.kjʊ.liː ˈkɒk.li.iː/ (BrE)
