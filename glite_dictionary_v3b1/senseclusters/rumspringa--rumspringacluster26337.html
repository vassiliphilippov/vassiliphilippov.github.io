rumspringa

noun
Amish coming-of-age period
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmˌsprɪŋə /; / ruːmˈsprɪŋə / (AmE), / ˈrʌmˌsprɪŋə /; / ruːmˈsprɪŋə / (BrE)
a period when Amish teenagers are allowed to experience life outside their community before deciding whether to join the church
