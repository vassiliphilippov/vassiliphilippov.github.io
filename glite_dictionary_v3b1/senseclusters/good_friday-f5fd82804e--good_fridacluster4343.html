Good Friday

proper noun
the Friday before Easter in the Christian calendar
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (BrE)
The Friday before Easter, observed by Christians as the anniversary of the crucifixion of Jesus Christ.

Examples

Good Friday holiday
Good Friday is a public holiday in several countries around the world.
Many Christians attend church services on Good Friday to remember the crucifixion of Jesus.
The procession through the city streets is a tradition on Good Friday.
