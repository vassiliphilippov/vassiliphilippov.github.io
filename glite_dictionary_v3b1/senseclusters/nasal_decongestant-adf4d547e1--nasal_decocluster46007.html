nasal decongestant

noun
medicine that relieves nasal congestion
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪzəl dɪˈkɑndʒəstənt / (AmE), / ˈneɪzəl dɪˈkɒndʒɛstənt / (BrE)
A medicine that helps relieve nasal congestion caused by colds, allergies, or respiratory infections.

Examples

A nasal decongestant spray
My doctor recommended a nasal decongestant for my seasonal allergies.
Overuse of a nasal decongestant spray can actually worsen congestion with time.
You can use a nasal decongestant to reduce your stuffy nose during a cold.
