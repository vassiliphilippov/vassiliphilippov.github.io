nebulousness

noun
hazy quality
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛbjələsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɛbjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being vague, unclear, or lacking definite form or boundaries

Critics pointed out the nebulousness in the politician's promises during the campaign.
Her argument was weakened by the nebulousness of her main points.
