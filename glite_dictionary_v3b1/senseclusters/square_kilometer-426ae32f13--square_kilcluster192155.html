square kilometer

noun
unit of area (1,000,000 m²)
2.5
Pronunciation: / skwɛr kɪˈlɑmɪtɚ /; / skwɛr kəˈlɑməˌtɚ / (AmE), / skwɛə kɪˈlɒmɪtə /; / skwɛː kɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to one million square meters, commonly used to measure land or large surfaces

Examples

One square kilometer
That country covers more than two million square kilometers of land.
The rainforest stretches for hundreds of square kilometers across the region.
The wildlife reserve occupies an area of just one square kilometer.
Only one square kilometre of forest remains after years of logging.
The city expanded rapidly, growing from 12 to 30 square kilometres in just two decades.
The national park covers over 5,000 square kilometres of pristine wilderness.
