nanitic

noun
first worker ant
Pronunciation: / nəˈnɪtɪk / (AmE), / nəˈnɪtɪk / (BrE)
a small worker ant produced by a newly mated queen, typically forming the first generation of workers in a colony

Examples

A nanitic ant
The first nanitic emerged from the egg and began helping the queen care for new larvae.
Because nanitics are smaller, they are less effective at defending the colony than later workers.
A colony's survival often depends on the hard work of its initial nanitic ants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: worker ant - a sterile female ant that performs labor for the colony
