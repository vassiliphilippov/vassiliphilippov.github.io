beatnik

noun
nonconformist of the Beat Generation
8.7 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbiːt.nɪk/ (AmE), /ˈbiːt.nɪk/ (BrE)
a young person in the 1950s and 1960s who belonged to the Beat Generation, known for nonconformist attitudes, unconventional dress, and a bohemian lifestyle

Examples

A beatnik style
He adopted the look of a beatnik, complete with a beret and dark turtleneck.
In the 1950s, coffeehouses were filled with beatniks reading poetry and discussing philosophy.
The film portrays a group of beatniks living on the fringes of society.
He dressed like a beatnik, favoring dark turtlenecks and berets.
In the 1950s, many beatniks gathered in coffeehouses to listen to jazz and discuss poetry.
The novel captures the attitude and lifestyle of the beatnik generation.
