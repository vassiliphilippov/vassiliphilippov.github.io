Papanicolaou smear

noun
test for abnormal cervical cells
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpæpəˈnɪkəˌlaʊ smɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌpæpənɪˈkælaʊ smɪə / (BrE)
a test involving the collection of cells from the cervix and uterus to examine for abnormal or pre-cancerous changes

Examples

A Papanicolaou smear can help detect cervical cancer at an early stage.
