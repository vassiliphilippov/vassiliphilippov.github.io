grazer

noun
frequent snacker
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgreɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈgreɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person who eats small amounts of food frequently throughout the day instead of having regular meals

Examples

A grazer at work
As a grazer, he prefers to eat small portions throughout the day rather than big meals.
Being a grazer can make it difficult to keep track of how much you eat.
She realized she was a grazer when she kept reaching for snacks every hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dieter - a person who follows a controlled eating plan
  • synonym: snacker - a person who eats snacks between meals
