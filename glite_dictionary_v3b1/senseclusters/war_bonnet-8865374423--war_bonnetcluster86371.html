war bonnet

noun
Native American headdress
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔr ˌbɑnɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔː ˌbɒnɪt / (BrE)
a traditional feathered headdress worn by some Native American leaders, especially during ceremonies

Examples

A war bonnet headdress
The chief wore a beautiful war bonnet decorated with eagle feathers at the festival.
Many museums display a war bonnet to teach visitors about Native American culture.
He admired the craftsmanship of the war bonnet in the historical photograph.

Related Terms
