chip

noun
token used in gambling
3.5
Pronunciation: / tʃɪp / (AmE), / tʃɪp / (BrE)
A small, flat, round token used in gambling to represent money.

Examples

Casino chips
Before leaving the casino, Sarah exchanged her chips for cash at the counter.
Each chip on the poker table represented a different denomination.
He placed a red chip on the roulette table before the wheel spun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: token - an item that represents a value or right, often used in games or gambling
