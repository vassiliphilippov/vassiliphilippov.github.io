confidentially

adverb
in a private or secret way
4.4 0.70/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀên.f…™Ààd…õn. É…ô.li / (AmE), / Àåk…ín.f…™Ààden. É…ôli / (BrE)
in a way that is meant to be private or secret, intended not to be shared with others

Examples

Speak confidentially with
Please let me know if you have any concerns, and I will handle them confidentially.
She whispered confidentially in my ear so that no one else could hear.
The information was shared confidentially with the board members only.

  • covertly
