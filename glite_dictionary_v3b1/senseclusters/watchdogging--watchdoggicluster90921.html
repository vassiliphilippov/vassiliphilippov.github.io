watchdogging

noun
monitoring for compliance
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃˌdɑɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌdɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of monitoring or overseeing activities to ensure they are conducted properly and ethically

Examples

Watchdogging government actions
The organization is dedicated to watchdogging the actions of large corporations.
Effective watchdogging can help prevent corruption in public offices.
Journalists play a key role in watchdogging government activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oversight - the act of supervising or monitoring something
  • synonym: supervision - the act of watching and directing what someone does
