Phalguna

noun
twelfth month of the Hindu calendar
Pronunciation: / pɑlˈɡuːnə / (AmE), / pɑːlˈɡuːnə / (BrE)
the twelfth month of the Hindu calendar, usually falling in February and March, celebrated in various Hindu festivals

Examples

The month Phalguna
Farmers in India often prepare for the harvest as Phalguna approaches.
Many traditional festivals are celebrated during Phalguna, including Holi.
The month of Phalguna usually corresponds to February and March in the Gregorian calendar.
