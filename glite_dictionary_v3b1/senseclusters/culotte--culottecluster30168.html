culotte

noun
divided skirt resembling loose trousers
7.2
Pronunciation: / kuːˈlɒt / (AmE), / kjuːˈlɒt / (BrE)
A divided skirt resembling a pair of loose-fitting trousers, worn by women.

Examples

Culottes for women
Culottes became popular as a practical alternative to skirts in the 20th century.
She wore her favorite culottes to the summer picnic for comfort and style.
The dress code required women to wear skirts, but culottes were allowed for cycling trips.
