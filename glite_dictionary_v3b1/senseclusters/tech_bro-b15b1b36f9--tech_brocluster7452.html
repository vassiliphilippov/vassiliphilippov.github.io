tech bro

noun
archetype of young man in tech culture
Pronunciation: / tɛk broʊ / (AmE), / tɛk brəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a young man who works in or identifies closely with the technology industry and its culture, often implying a stereotypical attitude or style

Examples

A tech bro culture
As a self-identified tech bro, he preferred t-shirts and startup talk over formal meetings.
Critics argue that tech bros contribute to the lack of diversity in Silicon Valley.
The company's culture was dominated by tech bros who worked late and talked about their side projects.
