kidlike

adjective
childlike qualities
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪdlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics typical of a child, such as innocence, playfulness, or simplicity

Examples

Kidlike wonder
His kidlike curiosity led him to ask many questions during the tour.
She approached the project with a kidlike sense of wonder and excitement.
Despite his age, his kidlike laughter filled the room with joy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - fully grown and mature; not showing childlike qualities
  • synonym: childlike - having the good qualities of a child, such as innocence or honesty
