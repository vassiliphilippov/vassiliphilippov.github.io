unostentatious

adjective
not showy; modest or restrained
9.3 6.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɑːs.tən.ˈteɪ.ʃəs/ /; / /ʌn.ə.stən.ˈteɪ.ʃəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɒs.tən.ˈteɪ.ʃəs/ /; / /ʌn.ə.stən.ˈteɪ.ʃəs/ (BrE)
not showy, flashy, or designed to attract attention; modest, simple, or restrained in appearance or manner

Examples

An unostentatious style
Her unostentatious generosity won her the admiration of many coworkers.
The restaurant's unostentatious decor made it feel warm and welcoming.
They preferred an unostentatious lifestyle despite their immense wealth.
She wore an unostentatious dress to the party, avoiding flashy accessories.
The mansion, though large, was decorated in an unostentatious style that reflected the owner's modesty.
The restaurant’s unostentatious atmosphere made it popular with locals who preferred simplicity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modest - not overly showy or boastful
  • antonym: ostentatious - showy and intended to attract notice
  • synonym: understated - presented or expressed in a subtle and effective way; not obvious or showy
