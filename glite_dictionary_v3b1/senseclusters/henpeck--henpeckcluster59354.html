henpeck

verb
to nag persistently
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnˌpɛk / (AmE), / ˈhɛnˌpɛk / (BrE)
to criticize or control a partner, especially a husband, in a persistent or domineering way

Examples

To henpeck a husband
He felt his wife would often henpeck him about household chores.
Some people believe it is unfair to henpeck their partners over small mistakes.
He complained that his mother used to henpeck his father about every little thing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nag - to annoy or criticize repeatedly
  • antonym: support - to give encouragement or help
