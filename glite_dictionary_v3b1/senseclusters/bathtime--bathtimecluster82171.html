bathtime

noun
time for a bath
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθtaɪm / (BrE)
the time when someone, especially a child, takes a bath

Examples

Children's bathtime
The children look forward to bathtime every evening.
After a long day, she enjoys a relaxing bathtime before dinner.
Parents often make bathtime fun by adding toys and bubbles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bedtime - the time at which someone goes to bed
