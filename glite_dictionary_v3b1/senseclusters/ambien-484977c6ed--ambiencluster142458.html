Ambien

proper noun
sleep medication brand
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbiən / (AmE), / ˈæmbiən / (BrE)
a brand name for a prescription medication used to treat insomnia and help people sleep

Examples

Ambien medication
Her doctor prescribed Ambien to help her sleep through the night.
Some people rely on Ambien when they have trouble falling asleep.
It is important to use Ambien only as directed by a healthcare professional.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sleeping pill - a medication taken to help induce sleep
  • synonym: zolpidem - the generic name for the active ingredient in Ambien
