hatha

noun
yoga style with postures
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːtə / (AmE), / ˈhʌtə / (BrE)
a style of yoga that emphasizes physical postures and breathing exercises to promote balance and flexibility

Examples

A hatha class
Many beginners start with hatha to learn basic yoga postures and breathing techniques.
The hatha class helped her improve her flexibility and reduce stress.
He prefers hatha because it moves at a slower pace than other yoga styles.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: vinyasa - a dynamic style of yoga with flowing movements
  • synonym: yoga - a physical, mental, and spiritual practice from India
