five spice powder

noun
Chinese spice blend
Pronunciation: / faɪv spaɪs ˈpaʊdər / (AmE), / faɪv spaɪs ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a Chinese seasoning made by grinding star anise, fennel, pepper, cloves, and cinnamon
