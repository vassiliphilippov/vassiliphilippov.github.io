delectation

noun
great pleasure or delight
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːlɛkˈteɪʃən /; / ˌdɛlɛkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːlɛkˈteɪʃən /; / ˌdiːlɛkˈteɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
great pleasure or delight, especially from enjoying something

Examples

Musical performances were arranged for the delectation of the guests.

These rare books are placed here for the delectation of serious collectors.
A box of fine chocolates was brought forth for everyone's delectation.
He read out the poem for the delectation of the guests.
The chef prepared a stunning meal for our delectation.
