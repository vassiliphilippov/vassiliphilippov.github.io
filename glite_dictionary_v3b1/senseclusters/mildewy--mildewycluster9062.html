mildewy

adjective
damp and musty
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlduːi / (AmE), / ˈmɪldjuːi / (BrE)
smelling or looking like mildew, especially damp and musty

Examples

Mildewy basement smell
After weeks of rain, the old books smelled mildewy and unpleasant.
We opened the closet and found our coats were a bit mildewy from the damp air.
The abandoned house had a mildewy carpet that needed to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - smelling clean and not stale or musty
  • synonym: musty - having a stale, moldy, or damp smell
