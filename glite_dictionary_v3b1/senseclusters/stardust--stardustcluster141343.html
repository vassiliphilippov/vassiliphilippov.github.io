stardust

noun
a dreamy or romantic quality
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrˌdʌst / (AmE), / ˈstɑːdʌst / (BrE)
a magical, dreamy, or romantic quality that makes something seem special or enchanting

Examples

Stardust in her eyes
She looked at him with eyes full of stardust, lost in a romantic daydream.
The director sprinkled a little stardust on the film, making it truly enchanting.
Travel writers often add stardust to their stories to evoke wonder and excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magic - the power of apparently influencing events by mysterious forces; enchanting quality
  • antonym: realism - the quality of being true to life; a practical or unsentimental attitude
