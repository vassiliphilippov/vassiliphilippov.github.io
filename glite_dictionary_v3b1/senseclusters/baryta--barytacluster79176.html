baryta

noun
barium compound used in chemistry and industry
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈraɪtə / (AmE), / bəˈraɪtə / (BrE)
any of several chemical compounds of barium, especially barium oxide or barium hydroxide, used in various industrial and laboratory applications.
