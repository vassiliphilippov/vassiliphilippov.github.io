fence charger

noun
device that powers an electric fence
5.3
Pronunciation: / fɛns ˈtʃɑrdʒər / (AmE), / fɛns ˈtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a device that supplies electrical energy to an electric fence, enabling it to deliver a shock when touched

Examples

Electric fence charger
If the fence charger fails, the electric fence will lose its effectiveness.
Solar-powered fence chargers are popular in remote areas without easy access to electricity.
The farmer installed a new fence charger to keep his cattle from wandering off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: energizer - a device that supplies or regulates electrical energy to a system, especially to an electric fence
