white-breasted nuthatch

noun
a North American songbird with a white breast
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈbrɛstɪd ˈnʌtˌhætʃ / (AmE), / waɪt ˈbrɛstɪd ˈnʌtˌhætʃ / (BrE)
a small bluish-grey songbird of eastern North America, with a black cap and a white breast

Examples

A white-breasted nuthatch bird
A pair of white-breasted nuthatches built their nest in the old woodpecker hole.
The white-breasted nuthatch is often seen foraging for insects along tree trunks.
We watched a white-breasted nuthatch climbing headfirst down the oak tree in the garden.

Related Terms
