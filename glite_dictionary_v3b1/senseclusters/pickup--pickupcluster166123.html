pickup

noun
light truck with open cargo area
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpɪkʌp / (BrE)
a small truck with an open cargo area and low sides, usually with a tailgate

Examples

A pickup truck
After the storm, many branches were hauled away in a pickup.
He loaded his tools into the back of his pickup before heading to the job site.
They decided to buy a pickup for their weekend camping trips.
