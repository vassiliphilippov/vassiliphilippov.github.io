mousseline

noun
light, airy dessert
Pronunciation: / ˌmuːsəˈliːn / (AmE), / ˌmuːsəˈliːn / (BrE)
a light, airy, or smooth food preparation, such as a sauce, dessert, or purée, often made with whipped cream or egg whites

Examples

Strawberry mousseline
The chef topped the strawberries with a delicate mousseline.

She learned to make a vegetable mousseline that was both light and flavorful.
A delicate chicken mousseline was served alongside fresh seasonal vegetables.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: custard - a sweet dessert made with milk, eggs, and sugar
  • synonym: mousse - a light, creamy dessert made with whipped cream or egg whites
  • synonym: purée - food that has been blended into a smooth, creamy consistency
