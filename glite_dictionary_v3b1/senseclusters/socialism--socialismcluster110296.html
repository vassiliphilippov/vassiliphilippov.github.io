socialism

noun
political and economic system based on collective ownership
6.9 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a political and economic system or theory in which the means of production, distribution, and exchange are owned or controlled by the community or the state
