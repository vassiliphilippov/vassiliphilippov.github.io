hypercholesterolemic

adjective
high blood cholesterol
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərkəˌlɛstərəˈlimɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəkɒˌlɛstərəˈliːmɪk / (BrE)
having a high level of cholesterol in the blood

Examples

Hypercholesterolemic patient
A hypercholesterolemic patient should follow a special diet to lower cholesterol levels.
Doctors often prescribe medication for hypercholesterolemic individuals to reduce the risk of heart disease.
The study focused on the effects of exercise in hypercholesterolemic adults.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperlipidemic - having a high level of lipids in the blood
  • antonym: normocholesterolemic - having a normal level of cholesterol in the blood
