crackleware

noun
glazed china with fine surface cracks
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækəlˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkrækəlˌwɛə / (BrE)
Glazed china featuring a decorative network of fine surface cracks in its glaze.

Examples

Glazed crackleware pottery
Collectors often seek out crackleware because of its unique, decorative crackled effect.
Her teapot was a fine piece of crackleware from the early twentieth century.
The antique shop displayed a collection of crackleware vases behind the counter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crackle glaze - a type of ceramic glaze featuring a network of fine cracks
