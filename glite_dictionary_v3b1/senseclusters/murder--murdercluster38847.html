murder

verb
to kill deliberately and unlawfully
3.0 41/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ùÀêd…ôr / (AmE), / Ààm…úÀêd…ô / (BrE)
to kill someone deliberately and with premeditation, especially when it is unlawful

Examples

To murder a person
He plotted for weeks before finally murdering his business partner.
Investigators believe she was murdered because she knew too much about the crime.
The mafia boss ordered his rivals to be murdered during the night.

Synonyms

  • commit murder
    5.4 1.73/m
    commit murder appears in legal texts to mean unlawful killing, while murder is the common verb for deliberately killing someone.
  • slay
    7.5 2.32/m
    slay often sounds literary and means violent killing, while murder is the legal word for deliberately and unlawfully killing someone.
  • dispatch
    7.7 0.90/m
    dispatch often means to kill quickly, sometimes for animals or soldiers, while murder is the illegal, deliberate killing of a person.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassinate - to kill an important person for political or religious reasons
  • antonym: save - to prevent the death of someone
  • synonym: slaughter - to kill in a violent or brutal way
