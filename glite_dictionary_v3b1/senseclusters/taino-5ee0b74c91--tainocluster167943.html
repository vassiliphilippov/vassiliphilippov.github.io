Taino

noun
Caribbean indigenous language
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈiːnoʊ / (AmE), / taɪˈiːnəʊ / (BrE)
the Arawakan language traditionally spoken by the Taíno people of the Caribbean, now extinct or nearly extinct

Examples

Taino language words
Researchers have found only a few written records of the Taino language.
Many Caribbean place names come from words in Taino.
The last fluent speakers of Taino died out centuries ago.
