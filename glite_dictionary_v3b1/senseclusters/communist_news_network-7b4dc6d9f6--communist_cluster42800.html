Communist News Network

noun
satirical nickname for biased news media
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.mju.nɪst nuːz ˈnɛt.wɝk / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ju.nɪst njuːz ˈnɛt.wɜːk / (BrE)
A satirical or mocking nickname for a news organization, used to suggest it exhibits a strong left-wing or pro-communist bias.

Examples

Communist News Network nickname
He sarcastically referred to the news station as the Communist News Network during the discussion.
Many dismissed the reports from the Communist News Network as blatant propaganda.
Online commentators often use Communist News Network to describe media they perceive as left-leaning.
