count

verb
determine the number of things
3.3
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt / (AmE), / kaʊnt / (BrE)
to determine the total number of things or people, or to recite numbers in order

Examples

To count the chairs
He counted each chair to make sure there were enough for everyone.
She asked the children to count the apples in the basket.
You should always count your money before leaving the store.
Each morning, he would count the stairs as he climbed to his apartment.
My daughter learned to count to fifty before starting kindergarten.
The teacher asked the children to count from one to ten out loud.
