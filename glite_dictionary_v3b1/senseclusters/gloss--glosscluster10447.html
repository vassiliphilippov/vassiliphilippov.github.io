gloss

noun
an explanatory note for a word in a text
7.8
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑs / (AmE), / ɡlɒs / (BrE)
an explanation or translation of a word or phrase, or a list of technical terms with explanations

Examples

A helpful gloss
I struggled with the passage until I found a gloss that clarified its meaning.
Scholars added a modern English gloss to the Latin text for clarity.
The medieval manuscript included a helpful gloss in the margin to explain the rare term.
If you are confused by the jargon, consult the gloss provided in the appendix.
Students found the gloss particularly helpful for understanding the technical vocabulary in the text.
The author included a gloss at the end of the book to clarify unfamiliar scientific terms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annotation - a note added to explain or comment on a text
  • synonym: glossary - an alphabetical list of terms and their definitions, especially in a book
