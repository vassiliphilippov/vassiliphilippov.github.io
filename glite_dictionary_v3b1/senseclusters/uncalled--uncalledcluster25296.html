uncalled

adjective
not summoned or chosen
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːld / (BrE)
not summoned, asked, or chosen to speak, participate, or appear.

Examples

Uncalled volunteers
Many volunteers remained uncalled after the list of names was read aloud.
He felt disappointed when his name was uncalled during the award ceremony.
Several students were left uncalled as the teacher ended the discussion early.

Related Terms

  • antonym: selected - chosen or picked out from a group
  • synonym: uninvited - not asked or requested to attend or participate
