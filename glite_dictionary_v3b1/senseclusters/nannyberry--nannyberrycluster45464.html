nannyberry

noun
edible shrub fruit
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnæniˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈnæniˌbɛri / (BrE)
the edible dark-blue or black fruit of the nannyberry shrub, often used in jams or preserves

Examples

A nannyberry jam
She picked a basket of ripe nannyberries from the shrubs behind the house.
The homemade jam was made entirely from wild nannyberry collected in the fall.
Birds often eat the nannyberry before people have a chance to harvest it.
