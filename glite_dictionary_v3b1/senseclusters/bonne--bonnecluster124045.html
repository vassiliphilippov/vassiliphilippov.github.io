bonne

noun
female domestic servant
Pronunciation: / bɑn / (AmE), / bɒn / (BrE)
a female domestic servant, especially one who looks after children in a household

Examples

A bonne for children
The family employed a bonne to help with the daily chores and childcare.
In Paris, it was common for middle-class households to have a bonne living with them.
The bonne prepared breakfast every morning before the children woke up.
The wealthy family hired a bonne to help care for their young children.
As a bonne, she was responsible for both cleaning and supervising the kids.
During her childhood in Paris, her bonne would take her to the park every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employer - a person who hires someone to work in their home
  • synonym: governess - a woman employed to teach and train children in a private household
  • synonym: housekeeper - a person employed to manage household chores
  • synonym: maid - a female domestic worker
  • synonym: nanny - a woman employed to care for children in a household
