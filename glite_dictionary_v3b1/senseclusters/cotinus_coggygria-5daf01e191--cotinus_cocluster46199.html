Cotinus coggygria

noun
Eurasian shrub with plume-like flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.tɪ.nəs kɑːg.ɪ.ˈgriː.ə/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.tɪ.nəs kɒg.ɪ.ˈgriː.ə/ (BrE)
