apronful

noun
amount carried in apron
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪprənˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈeɪprənˌfʊl / (BrE)
as much as can be carried in an apron at one time

Examples

An apronful of apples
She brought in an apronful of apples from the orchard.
He carried an apronful of fresh laundry to the bedroom.
The children gathered an apronful of wildflowers from the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armful - as much as can be carried in the arms at one time
  • synonym: handful - as much as can be held in one hand
