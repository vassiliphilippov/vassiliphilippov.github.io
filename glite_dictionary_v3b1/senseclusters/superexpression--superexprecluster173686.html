superexpression

noun
exaggerated display
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
an expression or display of emotion or feeling that is especially strong or exaggerated

Examples

During the meeting, his superexpression of frustration was impossible to ignore.
