Examples

A tempting offer
It's tempting to eat chocolate late at night, even though I know I shouldn't.
She found the offer from the rival company extremely tempting.

  • irresistible
    3.8 5.94/m

  • alluring
    5.2 1.83/m

  • seductive
    5.5 5.04/m
    seductive often suggests sexual or strong personal attraction and may feel manipulative, while tempting is a more general urge despite caution.
  • tantalizing
    6.3 2.09/m

  • beguiling
    7.4 0.58/m
