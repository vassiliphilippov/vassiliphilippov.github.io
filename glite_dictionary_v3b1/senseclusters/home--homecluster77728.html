home

noun
military base for starting/ending missions
1.3
Pronunciation: / hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
The base or location where military personnel are stationed and from which missions begin and finish.

Examples

Military home base
After the aircraft completed its operation, it safely returned to home.
Pilots checked in at home before setting out on their assignments.
The soldiers were ordered back to home after the mission ended.

Related Terms

  • synonym: base - a place where military operations are centered
  • antonym: front - the area where fighting takes place in a war, opposite of home base
