gluhwein

noun
hot spiced wine
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːˌvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡluːˌvaɪn / (BrE)
a hot spiced red wine drink traditionally served in German-speaking countries, especially during Christmas

Examples

A cup of gluhwein
We enjoyed a cup of gluhwein while walking through the Christmas market.
The aroma of gluhwein filled the air at the winter festival.
She served homemade gluhwein to her guests on a cold December evening.
