Clematis texensis

noun
woody Texan vine with scarlet flowers
Pronunciation: / kləˈmætɪs tɛkˈsɛnɪs / (AmE), / klɪˈmætɪs tɛkˈsɛnɪs / (BrE)
A species of woody vine native to Texas, noted for its showy, solitary, nodding scarlet flowers.
