a non-native organism that enters, establishes itself in, and often harms an ecosystem by outcompeting or displacing native species.

Examples

Invasive invader
The introduced plant quickly became an invader, outcompeting native wildflowers across the meadow.
Scientists warned that zebra mussels were an aquatic invader clogging pipes and harming fish populations.
After years of neglect, the coastal reserve was overrun by a vigorous invader from overseas.

Synonyms

  • invasive species
    4.4 0.78/m
    invasive species spread quickly and harm local ecosystems, while an invader is any non-native organism that enters and establishes itself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alien species - a species introduced, intentionally or accidentally, into an area where it does not naturally occur
  • synonym: invasive species - a species that is non-native to an ecosystem and whose introduction causes or is likely to cause harm
  • antonym: native species - a species that occurs naturally in a particular region or ecosystem
