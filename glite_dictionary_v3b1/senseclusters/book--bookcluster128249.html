book

noun
set of rules or standards
6.0 4.91/m
Pronunciation: / bÊŠk / (AmE), / bÊŠk / (BrE)
a set of rules or prescribed standards used as a guide for decision-making or conduct

Examples

Book of rules
New employees are expected to follow the book when handling customer complaints.
Some managers believe in flexibility, while others always go by the book.
The judge strictly adhered to the book in her decision.
