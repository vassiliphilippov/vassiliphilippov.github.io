gunsmithing

noun
firearm repair and design
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or profession of designing, making, or repairing firearms

Examples

Gunsmithing craft
He became interested in gunsmithing after restoring an old rifle with his grandfather.
Many technical schools offer courses in gunsmithing for those who want to work with firearms.
The art of gunsmithing requires patience, precision, and a deep understanding of mechanics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmithing - the craft of working with iron and steel to make tools or objects
