Lappic

noun
Uralic language(s) of the Sámi (Lapps)
6.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpɪk / (AmE), / ˈlapɪk / (BrE)
any of the Uralic languages spoken by the Sámi (also known as Lapps), especially in northern parts of Scandinavia, Finland, and Russia.

Examples

Lappic language
Few contemporary texts are written in Lappic, making historical records invaluable for scholars.
He devoted his research to the study of Lappic dialects in Scandinavia.
The Lappic languages belong to the Uralic language family, related to Finnish and Estonian.
