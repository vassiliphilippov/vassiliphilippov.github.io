close

adjective
tightly woven (of textiles)
1.0
Pronunciation: / kloʊs / (AmE), / kləʊs / (BrE)
tight, dense, or fitting closely (such as textiles, clothing, haircuts, or crowded spaces)

Examples

Close fit
A close mesh net was required to keep the tiny insects out.
The artisans preferred a close fabric for making durable upholstery.
This shirt is made of a close weave cotton that feels smooth against the skin.
After his illness, his once long beard was trimmed close to his chin.
He always keeps his hair close in the summer for comfort.
The recruits were given close haircuts on their first day of training.
He wore a pair of close jeans that were comfortable for cycling.
Make sure your shoes are a close fit to avoid blisters.
The dress has a close fit that highlights her figure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cramped - uncomfortably small or restricted
  • antonym: loose - not fitting tightly or closely
  • antonym: open - (of textiles) having threads woven with greater space between them
  • synonym: short - hair cut so that the hair is not long
  • antonym: spacious - having ample space
  • synonym: tight - fitting closely and firmly, especially too closely
