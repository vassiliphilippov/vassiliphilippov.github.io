European dewberry

noun
creeping European bramble with edible berries
8.3
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən ˈduˌbɛri / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈdjuːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a creeping bramble native to Europe that bears small, dark bluish-black edible berries known as dewberries

Examples

The European dewberry fruit
Farmers in northern Europe sometimes cultivate the European dewberry for its fruit.
The tart flavor of the European dewberry makes it perfect for jams and desserts.
We found several European dewberries growing along the woodland path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bramble - a prickly shrub, especially one that bears berries
  • synonym: dewberry - a small, dark, edible berry from several bramble species
