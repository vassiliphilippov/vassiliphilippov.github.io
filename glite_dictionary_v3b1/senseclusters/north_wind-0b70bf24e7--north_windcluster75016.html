north wind

noun
wind blowing from the north
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ wɪnd / (AmE), / nɔːθ wɪnd / (BrE)
a wind that blows from the north toward the south

Examples

A strong north wind
Sailing was difficult because a strong north wind swept across the bay.
The north wind brought a chill to the village, signaling the approach of winter.
When the north wind blows, temperatures usually drop sharply.

Related Terms

  • antonym: south wind - a wind that blows from the south toward the north
