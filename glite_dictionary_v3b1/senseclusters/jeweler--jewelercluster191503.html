jeweler

noun
person/business dealing in jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːələr /; / ˈdʒuːəlɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələ / (BrE)
a person or business that makes, sells, or repairs jewelry (and sometimes watches)

  • synonym: jewelry - objects such as rings, necklaces, or bracelets that are made of valuable metals and stones
