prescriptive

adjective
recommending actions (analytics)
7.2
Pronunciation: / pr6ac8skr6apt6av / (AmE), / pr6ac8skr6apt6av / (BrE)
(in analytics and decision-making) relating to methods, models, or systems that recommend specific actions or decisions to achieve optimal outcomes, often using optimization, simulation, or rules derived from data.

Examples

Prescriptive analytics model
The company switched to prescriptive analytics to optimize inventory and pricing strategies.
Our team used a prescriptive model that suggested daily reorder points to reduce stockouts.
The factory's prescriptive maintenance system recommended specific repairs and reduced downtime significantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actionable - providing specific, practical recommendations that can be acted on
  • antonym: descriptive - relating to summarizing or describing data rather than recommending actions
