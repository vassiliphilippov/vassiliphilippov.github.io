evergreen hit

noun
a song always popular over time
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌɡrin hɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn hɪt / (BrE)
a song or piece of music that retains its popularity and appeal over a long period of time

Examples

An evergreen hit song
Despite being released decades ago, the song remains an evergreen hit worldwide.
Mariah Carey's Christmas song has become an evergreen hit played every December.
The band's only evergreen hit still brings back memories of the 1980s.
