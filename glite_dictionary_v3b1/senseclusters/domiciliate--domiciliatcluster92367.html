domiciliate

verb
to establish residence in a place
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑmɪˈsɪliˌeɪt /; / ˌdɑːməˈsɪlieɪt /; / ˌdoʊ.mɪˈsɪl.i.eɪt / (AmE), / ˌdɒmɪˈsɪlieɪt /; / ˌdɒ.mɪˈsɪl.i.eɪt / (BrE)
