nondisabled

noun
person without disability
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdɪsˌeɪbəld / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdɪsˌeɪbəld / (BrE)
a person who does not have a physical or mental disability, often used collectively for people without disabilities

Examples

A nondisabled person
The new policy ensures that both nondisabled and disabled students have equal access to all facilities.
Some events are designed to be inclusive for both nondisabled and disabled participants.
Awareness campaigns encourage the nondisabled to understand the challenges faced by people with disabilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abled - a person without a disability
  • antonym: disabled - a person with a physical or mental disability
