super-automatic espresso machine

noun
fully automated espresso machine
1.6 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk es.ˈpre.soʊ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈsjuː.pə ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk es.ˈpre.səʊ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
A type of espresso machine that automates the processes of grinding coffee beans, tamping the grounds, and brewing espresso, often with automatic milk frothing and dispensing features.

Examples

A super-automatic espresso machine
Many offices invest in a super-automatic espresso machine to offer employees a quick and consistent coffee experience.
The new super-automatic espresso machine makes it easy to enjoy café-quality coffee at home with minimal effort.
Unlike manual models, a super-automatic espresso machine can brew espresso and froth milk with just the push of a button.
