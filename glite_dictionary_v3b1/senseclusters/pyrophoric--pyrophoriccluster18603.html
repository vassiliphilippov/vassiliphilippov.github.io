pyrophoric

noun
spontaneously igniting substance
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈfɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌpaɪrəˈfɒrɪk / (BrE)
a substance that ignites spontaneously when exposed to air, often at or below room temperature
