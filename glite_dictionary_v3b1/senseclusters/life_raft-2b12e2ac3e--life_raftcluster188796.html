life raft

noun
emergency raft for abandoning ship
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf ræft / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf rɑːft / (BrE)
a small, inflatable or rigid raft used for emergency evacuation at sea when a ship must be abandoned

Examples

Inflatable life raft
During the drill, every passenger practiced how to board a life raft quickly and safely.
The life raft was spotted by rescuers floating near the wreckage.
The crew inflated the life raft and climbed aboard after abandoning the vessel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lifeboat - a sturdy boat for rescuing people at sea
