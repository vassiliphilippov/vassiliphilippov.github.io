ambassadress

noun
female ambassador
7.2
Pronunciation: / æmˈbæsəˌdrɛs / (AmE), / æmˈbæsədrɪs / (BrE)
A female ambassador; a woman appointed as an ambassador.

Examples

The ambassadress to France
As the first female ambassadress to France, she played a vital diplomatic role.

The reception was hosted by the American ambassadress at her residence.
