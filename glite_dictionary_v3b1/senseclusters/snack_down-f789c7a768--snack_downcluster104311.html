snack down

verb
eat snacks quickly or casually
6.5
Pronunciation: / snæk daʊn / (AmE), / snæk daʊn / (BrE)
to eat snacks, especially in a quick or casual manner

Examples

To snack down crackers
She likes to snack down on crackers while watching TV in the evening.
The kids snacked down on cookies as soon as they got home from school.
We were so hungry after the hike that we snacked down on everything in the bag.
