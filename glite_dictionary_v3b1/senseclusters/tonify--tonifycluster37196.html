tonify

verb
to apply toner
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊnɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊnɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to treat a photographic print or image with a chemical toner to alter its color or tonal quality

Examples

To tonify a photo
The photographer decided to tonify the image to give it a warmer appearance.
You can tonify black-and-white prints to create different artistic effects.
After developing the photo, she chose to tonify it for a vintage look.
