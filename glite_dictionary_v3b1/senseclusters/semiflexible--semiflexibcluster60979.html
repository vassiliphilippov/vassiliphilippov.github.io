semiflexible

adjective
partly bendable
4.6
Pronunciation: /se.mɪ.ˈflek.sə.bəl/ /; / /sə.mɪ.ˈflek.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /se.mɪ.ˈflek.sɪ.bəl/ /; / /sə.mɪ.ˈflek.sɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
partly but not fully flexible; able to bend or be shaped to some extent but not completely

Examples

A semiflexible rod
The semiflexible rod can be shaped gently but will resist sharp bends.
Many biological polymers are semiflexible, allowing them to support structure while remaining adaptable.
Engineers chose a semiflexible material for the bridge to balance strength and flexibility.
The semiflexible hose can be bent around obstacles but will not twist sharply.
We used a semiflexible rod to reach inside the narrow pipe.
This material is semiflexible, making it suitable for both rigid and slightly curved surfaces.
