petechial

adjective
small skin spots
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪˈtiːkiəl / (AmE), / pɪˈtiːkɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by small red or purple spots caused by bleeding under the skin

Examples

Petechial spots
The nurse observed several petechial spots on the patient's lower legs during the examination.
A petechial rash can be a sign of a serious medical condition requiring immediate attention.
Doctors often check for petechial marks when diagnosing certain blood disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purpuric - relating to purple discolorations on the skin from bleeding
