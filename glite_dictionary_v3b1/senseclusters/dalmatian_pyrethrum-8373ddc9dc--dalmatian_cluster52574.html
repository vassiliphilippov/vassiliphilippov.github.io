Dalmatian pyrethrum

noun
Balkan plant used for insecticide
Pronunciation: / dælˈmeɪʃən paɪˈriːθrəm / (AmE), / dælˈmeɪʃən pɪˈriːθrəm / (BrE)
A white-flowered plant native to the Balkan region, with pinnate, white, and silky-hairy leaves on the underside, used as a source of insecticide; sometimes classified in the genus Chrysanthemum.
