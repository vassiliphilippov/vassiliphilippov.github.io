Korinthos

proper noun
modern Greek port city near ancient Corinth
8.1
Pronunciation: / koʊˈrɪnθoʊs / (AmE), / kɒˈrɪnθɒs / (BrE)
A modern Greek port city near the site of ancient Corinth, second in importance only to Athens in ancient times.

Examples

City of Korinthos
After Athens, Korinthos was once the most prominent city in ancient Greece.
Many tourists visit Korinthos to see the archaeological sites nearby.
The port of Korinthos plays a vital role in regional trade.
