coffer

noun
strongbox or chest for valuables
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːfər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfə / (BrE)
a strong chest or box used for storing money, valuables, or important documents

Examples

A coffer of gold
After the fundraiser, the charity's coffer was full enough to support several new projects.
The old castle's coffer contained documents dating back centuries.
The priceless jewels were locked away in a heavy coffer beneath the floorboards.
