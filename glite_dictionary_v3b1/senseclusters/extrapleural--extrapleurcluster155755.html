extrapleural

adjective
outside the pleura
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈplʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈplʊərəl / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the pleura, the membrane surrounding the lungs

Examples

Extrapleural approach
The doctor recommended an extrapleural approach to minimize the risk of lung injury.
An extrapleural mass was detected during the patient's chest scan.
Surgeons sometimes use extrapleural techniques to avoid opening the pleural cavity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pleural - relating to the pleura, the membrane around the lungs
