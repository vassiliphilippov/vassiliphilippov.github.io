hang

verb
listen attentively
Pronunciation: / hæŋ / (AmE), / hæŋ / (BrE)
to listen with close or intense attention, especially to every word that someone says

Examples

Hang on every word
As a child, she would hang on her grandmother's advice.
Reporters hung on the coach's every comment during the press conference.
The audience hung on every word of the speaker's story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attend - to pay attention to
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to
