weigh

verb
determine weight of something
3.4
Pronunciation: / weɪ / (AmE), / weɪ / (BrE)

Each apple weighs about 200 grams on average.
The baby weighs just over 3 kilograms at birth.
This suitcase weighs 15 kilograms, so it's too heavy for carry-on luggage.
