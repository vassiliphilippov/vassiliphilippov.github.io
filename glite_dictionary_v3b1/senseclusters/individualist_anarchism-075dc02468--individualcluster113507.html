individualist anarchism

noun
anarchist ideology focused on individual autonomy
Pronunciation: / ɪn.dɪˈvɪdʒ.u.ə.lɪst ˈæn.ɚˌkɪz.əm / (AmE), / ɪn.dɪˈvɪdʒ.u.ə.lɪst ˈæn.əˌkɪz.əm / (BrE)
a tradition within anarchism that emphasizes the importance of the individual and their autonomy over external authorities such as society, traditions, or ideological systems
