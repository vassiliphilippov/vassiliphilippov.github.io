endospore

noun
dormant spore inside bacteria or algae
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndoʊˌspɔr / (AmE), / ˈɛndəʊˌspɔː / (BrE)
a tough, dormant, asexual spore formed within the cells of certain bacteria and algae, capable of surviving harsh conditions
