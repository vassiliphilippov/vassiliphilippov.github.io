edgily

adverb
nervously or tensely
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdʒɪli / (AmE), / ˈɛdʒɪli / (BrE)
in a nervous, tense, or irritable manner

Examples

Speak edgily
He answered the reporter's questions edgily, clearly uncomfortable with the topic.
The children waited edgily for the results of the competition.
She glanced edgily at the clock, worried she would be late.

Related Terms
