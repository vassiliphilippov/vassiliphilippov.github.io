thana

noun
police jurisdiction area
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
the area or district under the authority of a particular police station, especially in South Asia

Examples

Thana area
Residents must register complaints with the thana that covers their neighborhood.
The new officer was assigned to the central thana in the city.
Each thana is responsible for maintaining law and order in its area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precinct - a district of a city or town defined for police purposes
  • synonym: station - a police station or its area of responsibility
