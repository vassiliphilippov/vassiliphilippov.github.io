gumming

noun
chewing ineffectually, like without teeth
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
Ineffectual chewing as if without teeth.

Examples

Gumming food quietly
Due to his dentures being misplaced, he spent the meal quietly gumming his food.
The toddler sat in her high chair gumming a soft piece of banana.
You could see the old dog gumming the biscuit, unable to bite through it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chewing - the act of biting and grinding food in your mouth
