layer cake

noun
cake with stacked layers and filling
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪər keɪk / (AmE), / ˈleɪə keɪk / (BrE)
A cake made of two or more layers stacked with sweet filling between, usually covered with frosting.

Examples

A layer cake with frosting
He baked a three-tier layer cake for his sister's birthday.
She served a delicious layer cake filled with strawberry jam at the party.
The bakery's chocolate layer cake is their most popular dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sponge cake - a light, airy cake made with eggs, sugar, and flour
