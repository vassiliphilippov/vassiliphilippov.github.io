unjudged

adjective
not evaluated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒʌdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒʌdʒd / (BrE)
not having been judged, evaluated, or given a decision or verdict

Examples

Left unjudged works
The paintings were left unjudged after the art show was canceled unexpectedly.
Several applications remained unjudged due to a shortage of reviewers.
He preferred to keep his work unjudged until it was completely finished.

Related Terms

  • antonym: judged - having been evaluated or given a decision
  • synonym: unevaluated - not having been assessed or appraised
