numbers game

noun
illegal daily lottery
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚz ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbəz ɡeɪm / (BrE)
an illegal lottery in which people bet on numbers in the hope of winning money, typically operated outside government regulation

Examples

Illegal numbers game
Many people in the neighborhood secretly played the numbers game despite its illegality.
The numbers game was especially popular during the early twentieth century in urban areas.
The police arrested several suspects for running a numbers game in the city.
