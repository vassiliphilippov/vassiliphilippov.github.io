unmannerly behavior

noun
rude or inappropriate conduct
8.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænɚli bɪˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænəli bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
rude or inappropriate conduct; behavior that lacks good manners or proper social etiquette

Examples

Unmannerly behavior at dinner
Guests were appalled by Mark's unmannerly behavior at the formal dinner.
Her repeated unmannerly behavior in meetings started to bother her colleagues.
Teachers often have to address students' unmannerly behavior in the classroom.

  • synonym: rudeness - lack of manners or respect
