mensae

noun
flat-topped landforms
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnsaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛnsiː / (BrE)
the plural of mensa, referring to flat-topped, steep-sided landforms found on Mars or other planets in planetary geology

Examples

Martian mensae
The rover photographed several eroded mensae rising from the dusty Martian plain under a pale sky.
Scientists studied the formation of mensae to understand the geological history of Mars.
Satellite images revealed a cluster of mensae near the planet's equator.
