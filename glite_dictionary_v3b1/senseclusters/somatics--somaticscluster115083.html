somatics

noun
mind-body movement field
8.1
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈmæt.ɪks/ (AmE), /sə.ˈmæt.ɪks/ (BrE)
the study or practice of the connection between mind and body, especially through movement, body awareness, or therapy
