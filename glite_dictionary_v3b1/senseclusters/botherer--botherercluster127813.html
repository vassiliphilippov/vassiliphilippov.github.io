botherer

noun
person who worries
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑðərər / (AmE), / ˈbɒðərə / (BrE)
a person who worries excessively or is easily upset by small things

Examples

Worried botherer
My mother is a constant botherer, always anxious about every little detail.
He became known as a botherer because he stressed over minor issues at work.
Try not to be a botherer and enjoy the moment without worrying so much.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm person - someone who remains relaxed and does not worry easily
  • synonym: worrier - a person who worries a lot
