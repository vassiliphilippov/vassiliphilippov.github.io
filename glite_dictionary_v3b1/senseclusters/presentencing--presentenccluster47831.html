presentencing

adjective
before sentencing
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈsɛntənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈsɛntənsɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the period or procedures before a court announces a sentence

Examples

Presentencing report
The court requested a presentencing investigation to gather more information about the defendant.
A presentencing hearing was scheduled to review the circumstances of the case.
Lawyers submitted their arguments during the presentencing phase of the trial.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsentencing - relating to the period after a court announces a sentence
  • synonym: pretrial - relating to the period before a trial begins
