icing

noun
formation of ice on a surface
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the formation of ice or frost on a surface, especially as a natural or hazardous process

Examples

Road icing
Icing can occur on roads overnight, making driving dangerous.
Aircraft must avoid icing conditions during flight to ensure safety.
The weather report warned of possible icing on the power lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frosting - formation of a thin layer of ice or frost
  • antonym: melting - the process of becoming liquid as a result of heat
