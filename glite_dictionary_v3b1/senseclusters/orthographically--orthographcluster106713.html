orthographically

adverb
relating to spelling
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the spelling or writing system of a language

Examples

Orthographically similar words
The two words are orthographically similar but have different meanings.
Some languages are more orthographically consistent than others, making them easier to spell.
The name was orthographically altered to match local spelling conventions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: phonologically - in a way that relates to the sounds of a language
  • synonym: spelling - the way in which a word is written
