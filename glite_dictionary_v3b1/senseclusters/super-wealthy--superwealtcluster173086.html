super-wealthy

noun
extremely rich people
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈwɛlθi / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈwɛlθi / (BrE)
people who are extremely rich, possessing much more money and assets than most others in society

Examples

The super-wealthy pay less
The new tax law was criticized for favoring the super-wealthy at the expense of ordinary citizens.
Luxury brands often target the super-wealthy with exclusive products and personalized services.
Many believe the super-wealthy should contribute more to address social inequality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billionaires - people whose wealth is at least one billion units of currency
  • degree lower: millionaires - people whose wealth is at least one million units of currency
  • antonym: poor - people with little or no money or assets
