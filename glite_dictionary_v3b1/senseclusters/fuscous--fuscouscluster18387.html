fuscous

adjective
dusky brownish-gray color
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌskoʊs / (AmE), / ˈfʌskəs / (BrE)
(of color) dusky or dark brownish-gray.

Examples

The moth had fuscous wings with subtle white markings.
