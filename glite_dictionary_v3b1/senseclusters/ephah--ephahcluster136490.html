ephah

noun
ancient Hebrew unit of dry measure
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.fə/ /; / /ˈe.fə/ (AmE), /ˈiː.fə/ /; / /ˈe.fə/ (BrE)
an ancient Hebrew unit of dry measure, about the size of a bushel (around 22 liters), used in biblical times
