juryman

noun
male member of a jury
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʊriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəriˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who is a member of a jury in a court of law

Examples

Juryman in court
As a juryman, he understood the seriousness of his duty in the trial.
It was the responsibility of every juryman to listen carefully to the evidence presented.
The court selected a juryman from the pool of eligible citizens.
The juryman took his responsibility very seriously during the trial.
Each juryman was instructed not to discuss the case outside the courtroom.
After hours of deliberation, the juryman finally reached a verdict with the rest of the jury.
