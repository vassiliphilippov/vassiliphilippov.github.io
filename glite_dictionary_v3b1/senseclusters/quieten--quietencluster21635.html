quieten

verb
make or become calm or quiet
Pronunciation: / ˈkwaɪ.ə.tən / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪ.ə.tən /; / ˈkwaɪ.ɪ.tən / (BrE)
to make someone or something quiet or calmer; to reduce noise or agitation
