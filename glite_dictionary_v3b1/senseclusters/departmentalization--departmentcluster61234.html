departmentalization

noun
division into departments
Pronunciation: / dɪˌpɑːrtˌmɛntəlɪˈzeɪʃən /; / dɪˌpɑːrtˌmɛntələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtməntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of dividing an organization into separate departments or units, each with specific responsibilities

Examples

Effective departmentalization can improve communication and efficiency within large organizations.

The new manager restructured the office through departmentalization by product line.
Effective departmentalization can help organizations respond better to customer needs.
