silesia

noun
sturdy cotton fabric for pockets/linings
Pronunciation: / sɪˈliːziə / (AmE), / sɪˈliːziə / (BrE)
A sturdy twill-weave cotton fabric, typically used for making pockets and linings in garments.

Examples

A bolt of silesia
I bought a bolt of silesia for lining several jackets.
The tailor used silesia to reinforce the inside pockets of the coat.
Traditional trousers often have silesia in the waistband and pocket linings.
