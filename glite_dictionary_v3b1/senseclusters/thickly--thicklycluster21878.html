thickly

adverb
in a thick and dense manner
3.6 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈθɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /ˈθɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
in a dense, heavy, or concentrated way; with a thick layer or with things close together
