humph

verb
to express annoyance
8.9
Pronunciation: /hʌmf/ /; / /həmf/ (AmE), /hʌmf/ /; / /həmf/ (BrE)
to make a short, low sound to express disapproval, irritation, or doubt

Examples

He humphs loudly
When asked to help with the chores, he just humphs and walks away.
She humphed loudly to show her disagreement with the decision.
Instead of arguing, he simply humphs and ignores the comment.

Related Terms
