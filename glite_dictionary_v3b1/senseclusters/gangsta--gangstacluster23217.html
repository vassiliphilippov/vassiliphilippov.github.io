gangsta

noun
member of a youth gang (slang)
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæŋstə / (AmE), / ˈɡæŋstə / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a youth gang or who imitates the style or attitude of a gangster, especially in hip-hop culture

Examples

He tries to act like a gangsta in his music videos.
Some teenagers dress and talk like a gangsta to impress their friends.
The movie follows a young man who wants to be seen as a real gangsta in his neighborhood.
