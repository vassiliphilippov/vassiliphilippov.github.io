aurorean

adjective
of or like the dawn
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈrɔriən / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɔːriən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the dawn; resembling the light or atmosphere of dawn.

Examples

Aurorean light
A soft, aurorean light filled the room at daybreak.
Poets often use aurorean imagery to evoke a sense of renewal and beauty.
The sky turned an aurorean pink as the sun rose over the hills.
