hemoprotein

noun
protein containing heme group
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmoʊˈproʊtiːn / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊˈprəʊtiːn / (BrE)
A protein that contains a heme group (a compound of iron and porphyrin) as its prosthetic group, found in proteins such as hemoglobin and myoglobin and essential in various biological processes such as oxygen transport and cellular respiration.
