unsifted

adjective
not sieved
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪftɪd / (BrE)
not having been passed through a sieve to remove lumps or large particles

Examples

Unsifted flour
The baker preferred to use unsifted flour for a denser texture in the bread.
Please add two cups of unsifted powdered sugar to the mixture.
Some recipes specify whether the flour should be unsifted or sifted before measuring.
