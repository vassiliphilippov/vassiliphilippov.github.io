personnel

noun
department managing employees
Pronunciation: / pɝː.səˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.səˈnɛl / (BrE)
the department within an organization that is responsible for hiring, training, and managing employees and policies related to staff

Examples

Personnel department
After the merger, several changes were made in the personnel department’s policies.
If you have any questions about your contract, please contact the personnel department.
The company’s personnel office handles all employee benefits and training programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: human resources - the department dealing with hiring and employee relations
