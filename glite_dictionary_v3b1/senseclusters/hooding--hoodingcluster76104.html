hooding

noun
placing a hood
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of putting a hood over someone or something, especially as part of a ceremony or as a form of punishment

Examples

Graduation hooding ceremony
The university held a hooding ceremony for its new graduates.
The prisoner was subjected to hooding as a form of punishment.
During the ritual, the hooding of the falcon signaled the start of the hunt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capping - the act of placing a cap on someone, often in a ceremony
  • antonym: unhooding - the act of removing a hood from someone or something
