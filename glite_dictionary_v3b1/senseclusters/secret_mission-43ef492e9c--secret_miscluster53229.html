secret mission

noun
a covert or confidential assignment
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkrət ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a covert or confidential assignment, especially one involving espionage or sensitive operations

Examples

A secret mission
Her expertise in languages made her an ideal candidate for the secret mission.
The spy was given a secret mission to retrieve the stolen documents.
They left at midnight to begin their secret mission behind enemy lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: covert operation - a military or intelligence operation kept secret from the public
  • antonym: public mission - an assignment or task that is open and not secret
