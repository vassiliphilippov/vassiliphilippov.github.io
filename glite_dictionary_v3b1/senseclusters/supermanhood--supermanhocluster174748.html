supermanhood

noun
exceptional manliness
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌmænhʊd / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəmænˌhʊd / (BrE)
the quality of possessing exceptional manliness, courage, strength, or assertiveness beyond the ordinary

Examples

Heroic supermanhood
The soldier's supermanhood inspired his entire unit during the dangerous mission.
Stories of supermanhood often highlight acts of extraordinary bravery and strength.
He was admired for his supermanhood in facing challenges that others feared.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery
  • synonym: heroism - great bravery and courage
  • synonym: manliness - the qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength and courage
