glycolytically

adverb
relating to glycolysis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlaɪkəˈlɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɡlaɪkəˈlɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or involves glycolysis, the breakdown of glucose in cells

Examples

Produce energy glycolytically
Under low oxygen conditions, the cells produced energy glycolytically rather than through aerobic respiration.
Some cancer cells grow rapidly because they metabolize glucose glycolytically even when oxygen is present.
Researchers measured how efficiently the bacteria could break down sugar glycolytically in different environments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aerobically - in a manner involving oxygen
  • antonym: anaerobically - in a manner not involving oxygen
