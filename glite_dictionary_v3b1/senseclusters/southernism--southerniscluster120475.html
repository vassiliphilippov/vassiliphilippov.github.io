southernism

noun
attitude or trait of US Southerners
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðərnˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsʌðənɪzəm / (BrE)
an attitude, custom, or expression characteristic of people from the southern United States

Examples

A polite southernism custom
He explained the southernism of always greeting neighbors, no matter the time of day.
His gentle manners were a classic southernism that set him apart from his northern colleagues.
The writer's novel is full of southernism, both in dialogue and in attitudes toward family and tradition.
