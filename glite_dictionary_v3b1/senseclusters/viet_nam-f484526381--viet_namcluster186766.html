Viet Nam

proper noun
country in Southeast Asia
2.7
Pronunciation: / viːˈɛt ˌnɑːm / (AmE), / viːˈet ˌnæm / (BrE)
A country in Southeast Asia on the eastern edge of the Indochinese Peninsula, officially a communist state, and known for achieving independence from France in 1945.
