paintless

adjective
without paint
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntləs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntləs / (BrE)
having no paint or lacking a painted surface

Examples

A paintless surface
After years of sun and rain, the barn stood paintless and weathered.
The artist preferred to work with paintless wood for a natural look.
The paintless walls made the room feel unfinished and cold.

Related Terms

  • antonym: painted - covered with paint
  • synonym: unpainted - not covered with paint
