wired

adjective
containing hidden surveillance devices
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəd / (BrE)
fitted with hidden electronic listening devices for surveillance or eavesdropping.

Examples

Wired for spying
He noticed something unusual in the office and wondered if it had been wired.
Journalists were warned that their phones might be wired for surveillance.
The police suspected the room was wired and checked for hidden microphones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bugged - having hidden electronic listening devices placed for surveillance
