discontentedly

adverb
in a dissatisfied manner
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows dissatisfaction or lack of contentment

Examples

Sigh discontentedly
He sighed discontentedly as he looked out the window on the rainy afternoon.
She answered discontentedly, making it clear she was unhappy with the decision.
The children shuffled discontentedly when their game was cut short.
