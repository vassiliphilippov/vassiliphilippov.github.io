Heideggerian

adjective
of Heidegger's philosophy
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdəˈɡɛriən / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdɪˈɡɛːrɪən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, philosophy, or methods of Martin Heidegger

Examples

Heideggerian philosophy
The seminar focused on Heideggerian concepts of being and time.
Her analysis took a distinctly Heideggerian approach to the question of existence.
Many modern philosophers have been influenced by Heideggerian thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: existentialist - relating to existentialism or its philosophers
  • synonym: phenomenological - relating to phenomenology, a philosophical approach
