aseptically

adverb
emotionlessly, clinically
8.6
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛptɪkli / (AmE), / əˈsɛptɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is emotionally cold, clinical, or lacking warmth or feeling

Examples

Aseptically describe events
She spoke aseptically about the accident, showing no emotion in her voice.
The report was written aseptically, focusing only on facts and avoiding personal feelings.
He aseptically recounted the events, as if he were not personally involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinically - in a way that is unemotional or detached
  • synonym: coldly - in an unemotional or unfriendly manner
  • antonym: warmly - in a friendly or affectionate manner
