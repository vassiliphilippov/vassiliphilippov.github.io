patcher

noun
person or tool for patching
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃər / (AmE), / ˈpætʃə / (BrE)
a person or tool that applies patches, especially to software or damaged materials

Examples

Wall patcher tool
The patcher fixed the hole in the wall with a quick application of plaster.
Before launching the game, the patcher downloaded and installed the latest updates.
A skilled patcher can restore old clothes to look almost new.

Related Terms

  • synonym: installer - a person or tool that installs software or equipment
  • synonym: repairer - a person who fixes or restores something
