tonger

noun
person using tongs
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːŋər / (AmE), / ˈtɒŋə / (BrE)
a person who uses tongs to handle or move objects, especially in cooking or working with hot or delicate items

Examples

Food tonger
The tonger at the street food stall flipped the kebabs using long metal tongs and steady hands.
During the barbecue, the tonger carefully turned the hot sausages to cook them evenly.
In the glass factory, a skilled tonger moved the molten pieces with precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chef - a professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant
  • synonym: handler - a person who handles or manages something
