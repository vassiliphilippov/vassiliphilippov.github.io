aerosol

noun
container for spray under pressure
4.9 3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.əˌsɑl / (AmE), / ˈeə.rəˌsɒl / (BrE)
A container in which a substance is held under pressure and released as a fine spray, usually by means of a propellant gas.

Examples

Aerosol spray can
Air fresheners are often sold in aerosol containers for convenience.
Always shake the aerosol well before applying the product.
She used an aerosol to spray the paint evenly onto the wall.
