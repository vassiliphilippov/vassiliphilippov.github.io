bitter end

noun
end of a cable attached to a ship's bitt
5.2 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪt.ər end/ (AmE), /ˈbɪt.ə end/ (BrE)
(Nautical) The inboard end of a line or cable, especially the end that is secured to a bitt on a ship.

Examples

Ship's bitter end

The sailor made sure the bitter end was securely fastened to the deck.
