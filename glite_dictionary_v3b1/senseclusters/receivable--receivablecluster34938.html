receivable

adjective
awaiting receipt of payment
6.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsiːvəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːvəbəl / (BrE)
(of a sum of money) due to be received; awaiting payment.

Examples

Receivable balance
He reviewed the receivable balances on the quarterly financial report.
The accountant flagged the overdue receivable for follow-up with the customer.
The company listed several receivable amounts that had not yet been paid by clients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: payable - (of a sum of money) required to be paid
