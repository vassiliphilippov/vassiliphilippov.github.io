myelopathy

noun
spinal cord disease
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪəˈlɑːpəθi / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəˈlɒpəθi / (BrE)
a disease or disorder affecting the spinal cord

Examples

Myelopathy disease
The doctor suspected myelopathy after the patient reported numbness and weakness in his arms.
Early diagnosis of myelopathy can help prevent permanent spinal cord damage.
Physical therapy is often recommended for patients recovering from myelopathy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiculopathy - a disease of the nerve roots
