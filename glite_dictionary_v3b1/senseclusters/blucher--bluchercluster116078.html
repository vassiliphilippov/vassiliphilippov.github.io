blucher

noun
high shoe or boot with laces over the tongue
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbluːtʃə / (BrE)
A type of high shoe or boot with laces running over the tongue, usually featuring two side flaps for the laces.
