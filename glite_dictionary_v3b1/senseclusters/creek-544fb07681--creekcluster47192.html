Creek

noun
a Native American people of the southeastern US
8.4 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / k…πik / (AmE), / kriÀêk / (BrE)
a member of the Creek Confederacy, especially the Muskogee people, originally from Georgia and Alabama but now chiefly in Oklahoma

Examples
