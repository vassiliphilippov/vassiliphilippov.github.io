submitter

noun
person who submits or yields
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪtər / (AmE), / səbˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a person who yields to the authority, control, or will of another person or force

Examples

A submitter to authority
In the story, the submitter accepts her fate, unlike the rebellious protagonist.
She was criticized for being a submitter rather than standing up for herself.
The submitter obeyed every command given without question.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resister - a person who resists or opposes
  • synonym: submissive - inclined or ready to submit to others
