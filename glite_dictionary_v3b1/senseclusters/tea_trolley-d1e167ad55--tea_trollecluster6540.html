tea trolley

noun
wheeled cart for serving tea or snacks
1.4
Pronunciation: / ti ˈtrɑli / (AmE), / ti ˈtrɒli / (BrE)
a small wheeled cart used for serving tea or light refreshments, typically in a domestic or social setting

Examples

A tea trolley with cups
After lunch, she arranged cups and plates neatly on the tea trolley for afternoon tea.
The hostess wheeled the tea trolley into the drawing room, offering guests a selection of cakes and sandwiches.
They inherited a vintage tea trolley from their grandmother, which they use during family gatherings.
