candy apple

noun
apple coated with candy shell
Pronunciation: / ˈkændi ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkændi ˌæpəl / (BrE)
An apple coated with a hard, shiny candy shell, typically made from caramelized sugar.

Examples

A candy apple on stick
Kids lined up at the stand for a delicious candy apple on a stick.
Making a candy apple at home can be a fun holiday tradition.
She bought a bright red candy apple at the autumn fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toffee apple - apple coated with toffee, especially in British English
