tasklike

adjective
methodical or routine
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtæskˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɑːskˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a task in being methodical, routine, or systematic

Examples

A tasklike method
Her tasklike approach helped her finish the project efficiently.
He completed his chores in a tasklike manner, following the same steps every time.
The meeting was run in a tasklike way, with little room for creativity or discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spontaneous - done without planning or method
  • synonym: systematic - done according to a fixed plan or system
