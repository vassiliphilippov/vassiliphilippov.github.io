array

noun
fine or decorative clothing
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ / (AmE), / əˈreɪ / (BrE)
elaborate or impressive clothing, especially worn for special occasions; finery.

Examples

A fine array of clothes
He entered the ballroom dressed in a dazzling array of velvet and lace.
She admired the splendid array of silks and jewels on display.
The guests arrived in their finest array for the royal banquet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finery - elaborate or showy clothes and accessories
  • synonym: raiment - clothing, especially fine or decorative garments
