murder mystery

noun
story genre about solving a murder
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrdər ˈmɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə ˈmɪstəri / (BrE)
A story or film about a murder and the process of finding out who committed it.

Examples

A murder mystery story
Last weekend, we attended a live murder mystery dinner at the old mansion.
She enjoys reading murder mystery novels before bed every night.
The group gathered to watch a classic murder mystery film on Friday evening.

Related Terms
