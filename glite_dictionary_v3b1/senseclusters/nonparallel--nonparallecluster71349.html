nonparallel

adjective
not parallel; converging or diverging
3.5 5.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈpær.ə.ləl/ /; / /nə.ˈpær.ə.ləl/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈpær.ə.ləl/ /; / /nə.ˈpær.ə.ləl/ (BrE)
(of lines, paths, or surfaces) not parallel; tending to meet or diverge.

Examples

Nonparallel lines
