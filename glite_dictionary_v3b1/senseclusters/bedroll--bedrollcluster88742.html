bedroll

noun
roll of bedding for carrying
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.rəʊl / (BrE)
A blanket or sleeping bag rolled up for easy transport, used for sleeping outdoors or while travelling.

Examples

A bedroll for camping
He strapped his bedroll to his backpack before setting out on the trail.
She packed her bedroll and tent, ready for a weekend of camping.
The hikers slept under the stars with only a bedroll for comfort.
