nonfictional prose

noun
prose writing based on facts
9.1 4.3
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɪkʃənl proʊz / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɪkʃənl prəʊz / (BrE)
prose writing about real events, people, or facts, rather than imaginary stories or fiction.

Examples

Nonfictional prose writing
His latest work is a collection of nonfictional prose about his travels in South America.
Many historians express their research through nonfictional prose published in academic journals.
The essay is a popular form of nonfictional prose that explores real-life topics and personal reflections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fictional prose - prose writing that is invented or imaginary
  • synonym: nonfiction - writing that is about real events and facts, not imagined
