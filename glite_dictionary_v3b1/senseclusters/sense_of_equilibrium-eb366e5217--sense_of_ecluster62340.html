sense of equilibrium

noun
system for balance in the inner ear
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛns əv iˌkwɪlɪˈbriəm / (AmE), / ˌsɛns əv iˌkwɪlɪˈbriːəm / (BrE)
the sensory system in the inner ear that detects the position and movement of the head, helping to maintain balance

Examples
