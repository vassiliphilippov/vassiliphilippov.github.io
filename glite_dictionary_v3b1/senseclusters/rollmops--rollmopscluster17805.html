rollmops

noun
pickled herring fillets rolled around a filling
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlˌmɑps / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlˌmɒps / (BrE)
A dish consisting of pickled herring fillets rolled around a savory filling, typically a pickle or onion.
