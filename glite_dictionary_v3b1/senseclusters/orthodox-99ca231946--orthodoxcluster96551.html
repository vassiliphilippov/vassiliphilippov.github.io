Orthodox

adjective
relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrθədɑks / (AmE), / ˈɔːθədɒks / (BrE)
Of or relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church, its traditions, practices, or members.

Examples

Orthodox Church service
Many Orthodox Christians celebrate Easter on a different date than Western churches.
The ceremony followed the traditional Orthodox liturgy.
The icon was painted in the distinctive Orthodox style.
