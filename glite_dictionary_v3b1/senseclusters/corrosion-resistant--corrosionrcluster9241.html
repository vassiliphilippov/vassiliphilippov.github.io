corrosion-resistant

adjective
not easily damaged by corrosion
Pronunciation: / kəˌroʊʒən rɪˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / kəˌrəʊʒən rɪˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
(of a material or substance) able to withstand damage caused by corrosion.

Examples

Corrosion-resistant material
Stainless steel is widely used in kitchen appliances because it is corrosion-resistant.
The new bridge was constructed using corrosion-resistant alloys to increase its lifespan.
We opted for corrosion-resistant paint to protect the metal surfaces from the salty sea air.
