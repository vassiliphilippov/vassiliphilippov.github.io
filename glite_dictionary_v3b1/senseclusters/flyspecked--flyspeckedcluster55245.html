flyspecked

adjective
marked with small spots
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌspɛkt / (BrE)
covered with or marked by small spots or specks, especially those made by flies.

Examples

Flyspecked pages
The old map was so flyspecked that some names were hard to read.
After years in the attic, the painting became flyspecked and faded.
She wiped the flyspecked mirror before using it each morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or stains
  • synonym: spotted - marked with spots or dots
  • synonym: stained - marked or discolored with a spot or spots
