timberland

noun
forest-covered land; land with trees
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbɚˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbəlænd / (BrE)
land that is covered with trees or forest, especially and typically suitable for timber production.

Examples

Timberland forest area
Farmers converted several acres of timberland into pasture over the past decade.
Much of the state’s timberland was preserved as a wildlife reserve.
The company invested in a large timberland in the Pacific Northwest for sustainable logging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodland - land covered with trees, smaller than a forest
