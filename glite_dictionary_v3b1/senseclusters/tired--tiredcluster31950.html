Synonyms

  • weary
    3.2 9.68/m
    weary is mentally and physically worn after long effort, while tired mainly means needing rest or sleep.
  • exhausted
    2.0 3.89/m
    exhausted feels completely used up after hard work or long days, while tired simply means low energy needing rest.
  • worn out
    2.9 1.25/m
    worn out describes someone very tired from too much activity, while tired is the general need to rest.
  • fatigued
    4.1 0.86/m
    fatigued sounds more formal or medical and means deeply tired, while tired is common everyday feeling needing rest.
  • dog-tired
    6.4 0.18/m
    dog-tired is an informal, very strong way to say someone is completely exhausted, while tired is a general, milder need for rest.
  • drained
    3.4 0.00/m
    drained feels empty of energy often after stress or emotion, while tired mainly means physical need to rest or sleep.
