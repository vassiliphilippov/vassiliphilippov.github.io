tulip orchid

noun
a Mexican orchid with yellow flowers

Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.lɪp ˈɔːr.kɪd/ /; / /ˈtjuː.lɪp ˈɔːr.kɪd/ (AmE), /ˈtjuː.lɪp ˈɔː.kɪd/ /; / /ˈtʃuː.lɪp ˈɔː.kɪd/ (BrE)
