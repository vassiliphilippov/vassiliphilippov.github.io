physicality

noun
preoccupation with bodily drives or appetites
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzɪˈkælɪti / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
A strong focus on or preoccupation with bodily drives, appetites, or sensual experiences.

Examples

Sensual physicality
His writing is marked by an intense physicality, often focusing on hunger, thirst, and sensual need.
Some critics argue that the novel's physicality overshadows its deeper philosophical themes.
The artist sought to capture the physicality of human desire in her paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensuality - enjoyment of or preoccupation with the senses or physical pleasure
  • antonym: spirituality - concern with the spirit rather than the body or material things
