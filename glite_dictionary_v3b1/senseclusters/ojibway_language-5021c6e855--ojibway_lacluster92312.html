Ojibway language

noun
language of the Ojibwa people
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈdʒɪbweɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əʊˈdʒɪbweɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Algonquian language spoken by the Ojibwa people, primarily in parts of Canada and the northern United States.
