RN

adverb
informal 'right now'
9.9
Pronunciation: /raɪt naʊ/ /; / /ɑːr en/ (AmE), /raɪt naʊ/ /; / /ɑː en/ (BrE)
Informal abbreviation used in writing to mean 'right now' (immediately, at this moment).

Examples

Busy RN
I can't join the call RN — I'll be free in about an hour.
She asked me to send the revised slides RN, because the client needs them.
He said he'd be at the office RN, so I waited by the reception desk.
