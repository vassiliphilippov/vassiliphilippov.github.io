overbooking

noun
too many bookings
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.bʊk.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.bʊk.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or practice of selling, allocating, or scheduling more tickets, seats, or resources than are actually available, expecting not all will be used at the same time
