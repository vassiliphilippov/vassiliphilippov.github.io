Putinise the media

verb
impose government control over media (like Putin)
Pronunciation: / ˈpuː.tɪn.aɪz ðə ˈmiː.di.ə / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.tɪn.aɪz ðə ˈmiː.di.ə / (BrE)
to bring the media under strict government influence or control, as done by Vladimir Putin's administration in Russia

Examples

To Putinise the media strictly
Fears have grown that new legislation could Putinise the media in this country.
The journalist warned that any move to Putinise the media would erode press freedoms.
The opposition accused the prime minister of trying to Putinise the media ahead of the national elections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: control the media - exercise authority over media organizations and content
