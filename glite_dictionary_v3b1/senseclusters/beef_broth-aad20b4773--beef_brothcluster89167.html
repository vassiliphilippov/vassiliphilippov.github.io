beef broth

noun
liquid stock made from beef
Pronunciation: / ˈbif brɑθ / (AmE), / ˈbiːf brɒθ / (BrE)
A clear, flavorful liquid made by simmering beef and sometimes vegetables, used as a base for soups and sauces.

Examples

Canned beef broth
Canned beef broth is a convenient option for quick meals.
She used beef broth as the base for her homemade vegetable soup.
The recipe calls for two cups of beef broth to enhance the flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beef stock - a liquid prepared by simmering beef bones, often richer than beef broth
  • synonym: chicken broth - a clear stock made with chicken
