training school

noun
juvenile correctional institution
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
A correctional institution designed for the detention, discipline, and rehabilitation of young or first-time offenders.

Examples

Juvenile training school
After his arrest, he was sent to a training school for rehabilitation.
Many former residents of the training school went on to lead successful lives.
The state established its first training school for juvenile offenders in 1902.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juvenile detention center - facility where young offenders are held in detention
  • synonym: reform school - a school for the correctional training of young offenders
