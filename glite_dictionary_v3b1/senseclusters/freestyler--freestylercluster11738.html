freestyler

noun
improvising performer
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːstaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈfriːstaɪlə / (BrE)
a person who performs or competes by improvising rather than following a fixed routine or script

Examples

Rap freestyler
The talented freestyler amazed the judges with his original rap verses.
As a freestyler, she prefers to create her dance moves on the spot.
Many swimmers admire the freestyler for his speed and unique technique.

Related Terms

  • antonym: choreographer - a person who plans and arranges dance routines
  • synonym: improviser - a person who creates or performs spontaneously
