countian

noun
person who tallies
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntiən / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntiən / (BrE)
a person who counts or makes a formal tally, especially of votes or inventories

Examples

Election countian
The election committee appointed a countian to tally the votes after the polls closed.
Each countian was responsible for verifying the inventory in their assigned section.
During the recount, the countian carefully checked each ballot for accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auditor - a person who examines and verifies accounts
  • synonym: teller - a person who counts votes or money
