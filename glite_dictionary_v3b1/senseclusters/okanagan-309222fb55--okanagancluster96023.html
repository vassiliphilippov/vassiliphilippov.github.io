Okanagan

proper noun
Canadian region or people
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊkəˈnɑːɡən / (AmE), / ˌəʊkəˈnɑːɡən / (BrE)
a region in British Columbia, Canada, known for its lakes and vineyards, or the Indigenous people native to that area

Examples

The Okanagan valley
The Okanagan is a popular destination for tourists seeking beautiful lakes and warm weather.
Many wineries in the Okanagan produce award-winning wines each year.
The Okanagan people have a rich cultural heritage in British Columbia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Okanagan Valley - a valley in British Columbia, Canada, famous for wine production
  • synonym: Syilx - the Indigenous people also known as the Okanagan
