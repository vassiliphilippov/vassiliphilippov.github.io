desecration

noun
act of disrespecting something sacred
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛsəˈkreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛsɪˈkreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of treating a holy place or object with violent disrespect; the act of depriving something of its sacred character

Examples

The desecration of a cemetery
Many people were outraged at the desecration of the cemetery by graffiti and litter.
The film dealt with the desecration of religious symbols during the conflict.
The vandals' desecration of the ancient temple shocked the entire community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profanation - the act of treating something sacred with disrespect
  • synonym: sacrilege - the violation or profanation of anything sacred
