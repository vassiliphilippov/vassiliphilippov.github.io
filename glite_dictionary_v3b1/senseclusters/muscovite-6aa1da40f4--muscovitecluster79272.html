Muscovite

noun
a resident or native of Moscow
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌskoʊˌvaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌskəvaɪt / (BrE)
a native or resident of Moscow, the capital of Russia

Examples

As a Muscovite, she was proud of her city’s rich history and vibrant arts scene.
Many well-known Muscovites attended the city’s annual cultural festival.
The famous poet was a lifelong Muscovite, seldom leaving the capital except for short trips.
