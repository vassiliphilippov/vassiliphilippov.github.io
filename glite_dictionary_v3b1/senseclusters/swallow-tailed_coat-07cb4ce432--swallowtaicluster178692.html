swallow-tailed coat

noun
man's formal jacket with tails
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːloʊ teɪld koʊt / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ teɪld kəʊt / (BrE)
A man's formal jacket with a front cut away and two long tapering tails at the back, worn for full-dress occasions.

Examples

A swallow-tailed coat
He looked very distinguished in his swallow-tailed coat at the gala.
In the nineteenth century, no gentleman attended the ball without a swallow-tailed coat.
The orchestra conductor wore a classic swallow-tailed coat during the evening performance.
