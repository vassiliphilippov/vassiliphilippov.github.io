Shaka

noun
Hawaiian hand gesture
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɑː.kə/ (AmE), /ˈʃɑː.kə/ (BrE)
a hand gesture with the thumb and little finger extended, used as a greeting or sign of approval, especially in Hawaiian and surf culture

Examples

Shaka hand sign
He flashed a Shaka to his friends as he paddled out to catch the next wave.
Locals often greet each other with a friendly Shaka at the market.
After winning the competition, she gave the crowd a big Shaka from the stage.
