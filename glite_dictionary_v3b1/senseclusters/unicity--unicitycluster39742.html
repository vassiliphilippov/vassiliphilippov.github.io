unicity

noun
state of unity
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈjuː.nɪ.sɪ.tiː/ /; / /juː.ˈnɪ.sɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈjuː.nɪ.sɪ.tiː/ /; / /juː.ˈnɪ.sɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
