arolla pine

noun
European pine tree yielding cembra nuts
Pronunciation: / əˈroʊlə paɪn / (AmE), / əˈrɒlə paɪn / (BrE)
A large European pine tree (Pinus cembra) with five needles per bundle, producing edible cembra nuts and a resinous sap.

Examples
