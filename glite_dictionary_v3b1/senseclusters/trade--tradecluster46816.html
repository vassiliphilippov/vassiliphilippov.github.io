Synonyms

  • exchange
    1.8 20/m
    exchange means giving something and receiving something else in return, while trade often implies buying or selling in business.
  • swap
    2.1 5.26/m
    swap means exchanging one thing directly for another, while trade often involves buying, selling, or wider business deals.
  • change
    1.0 22/m
    change means replacing or altering something with another similar item, while trade means giving something to get a different or new item.
