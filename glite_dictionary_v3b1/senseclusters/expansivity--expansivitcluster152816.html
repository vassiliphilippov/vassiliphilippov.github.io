expansivity

noun
friendliness and openness in speech
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspænˈsɪvəti / (AmE), / ɪkˌspansˈɪvɪti / (BrE)
a quality of being open, friendly, and talkative, often making conversation freely and warmly

Examples

Social expansivity
Despite his usual expansivity, he remained quiet during the tense meeting.
Her natural expansivity made everyone around her feel instantly comfortable and welcomed.
The expansivity of the host brought life to the otherwise dull gathering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expansiveness - quality of being open and communicative
  • antonym: reserve - quality of being restrained and not openly communicative
