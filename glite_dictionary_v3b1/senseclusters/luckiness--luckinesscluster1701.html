luckiness

noun
state of being lucky
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌkinəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌkinɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having good luck or experiencing favorable outcomes by chance.

Examples

Good luckiness
Despite his undeniable luckiness, he remained humble about his good fortune.
Her luckiness seemed almost magical as everything worked out in her favor.
Many people admired his luckiness in always winning competitions.
