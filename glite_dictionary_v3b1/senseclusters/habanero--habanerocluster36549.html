habanero

noun
person from Havana
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːbəˈnɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˌhabəˈnɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a person who was born in or lives in Havana, the capital city of Cuba

Examples

A habanero resident
As a proud habanero, she often described Havana's streets and cafes to visiting friends.
Many habaneros celebrate their city's rich history and vibrant culture.
The habanero welcomed tourists with stories about life in Havana.
