noted

adjective
well known and admired
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtɪd / (BrE)
well known and admired for a particular quality or skill

Examples

A noted artist
His noted generosity made a lasting impact on the community.
She is noted for her groundbreaking research in neuroscience.
The gallery displayed works by several noted artists from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illustrious - well known, respected, and admired
  • antonym: obscure - not well known, unknown
