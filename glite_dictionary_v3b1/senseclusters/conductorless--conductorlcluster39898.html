conductorless

adjective
without a conductor
8.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌktərləs / (AmE), / kənˈdʌktələs / (BrE)
not containing or lacking a material, component, or path that conducts electricity or heat

Examples

Conductorless material
The new material is completely conductorless, making it ideal for electrical insulation.
A conductorless design reduces the risk of accidental electric shocks in sensitive equipment.
Because the device was conductorless, it could not transmit any electrical current.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conductive - having the ability to conduct electricity or heat
  • synonym: insulating - preventing the passage of electricity or heat
