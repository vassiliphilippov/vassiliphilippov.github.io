Mary II of England

proper noun
Queen of England (1689–1694), ruled with William III
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛri səˈkənd əv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (AmE), / ˈmɛːri səˈkənd ɒv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (BrE)
Queen of England, Scotland, and Ireland (1689–1694), who reigned jointly with her husband William III and was the eldest daughter of James II.

Examples

Mary II of England queen
Mary II of England reigned jointly with her husband William III during the Glorious Revolution.
