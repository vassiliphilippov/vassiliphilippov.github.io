uninvite

noun
withdrawal of invitation
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪt / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing an invitation that was previously given

Examples

A sudden uninvite
After the argument, she received an unexpected uninvite to the wedding.
The company sent an official uninvite to the speaker due to scheduling conflicts.
He was surprised by the sudden uninvite from the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinvitation - the act of canceling or retracting an invitation
  • antonym: invitation - a request for someone to attend an event or join an activity
