disablement

noun
act of making unusable
Pronunciation: / dɪˈseɪbəlmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈseɪblmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of making something unable to function or be used

Examples

Disablement of equipment
The disablement of the security cameras allowed the intruders to enter unnoticed.
After the disablement of the vehicle, it could not be driven any further.
Routine maintenance includes the temporary disablement of certain systems for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deactivation - the process of making something inactive or not working
  • antonym: enabling - the act of making something able to function or be used
