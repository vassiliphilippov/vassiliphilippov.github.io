hoedown

verb
to dance energetically
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊˌdaʊn / (BrE)
to take part in or dance at a lively country or folk dance event, often with spirited movements

Examples

To hoedown with friends
After dinner, everyone gathered in the barn to hoedown until midnight.
The band played upbeat music, encouraging the crowd to hoedown together.
At the festival, she taught the children how to hoedown in the traditional style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: square dance - to dance a traditional American folk dance with four couples
  • antonym: waltz - to dance a smooth, progressive ballroom dance
