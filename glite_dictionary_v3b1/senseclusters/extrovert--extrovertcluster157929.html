extrovert

noun
an outgoing, socially confident person
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkstrəˌvɝt / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrəˌvɜːt / (BrE)
a person who is outgoing, socially confident, and concerned more with external realities and social interaction than inner thoughts and feelings

Examples

A friendly extrovert
Although he is an extrovert, he sometimes needs time alone to recharge.
As an extrovert, she thrives in large social gatherings and enjoys meeting new people.
The workplace benefits from both extrovert and introvert personalities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: introvert - a person who is more focused on internal thoughts and feelings than external activities
