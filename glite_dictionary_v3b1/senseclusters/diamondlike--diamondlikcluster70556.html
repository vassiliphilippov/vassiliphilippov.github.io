diamondlike

adjective
resembling a diamond
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪməndˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəməndˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape, appearance, or qualities of a diamond, especially being rhombus-shaped or arranged in a diamond pattern

Examples

Diamondlike pattern
The artist painted a series of diamondlike shapes across the canvas to create a geometric effect.
Her necklace featured a diamondlike pendant that sparkled in the sunlight.
The windows were decorated with diamondlike patterns that caught the morning light beautifully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: circular - having the shape of a circle
  • synonym: diamond-shaped - shaped like a diamond or rhombus
  • synonym: rhombic - having the shape of a rhombus
