upcoast

adverb
toward the coast's upper part
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌkoʊst / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkəʊst / (BrE)
in the direction along or toward the coast, especially northward or up a coastline

Examples

Travel upcoast
We decided to travel upcoast to explore the remote beaches.
Fishermen often move upcoast during the summer to follow the fish.
The storm was reported to be heading upcoast toward the northern towns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downcoast - in the direction along or toward the lower part of the coast
  • antonym: inland - toward or in the interior of a country, away from the coast
