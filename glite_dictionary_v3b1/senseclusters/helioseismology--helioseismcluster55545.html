helioseismology

noun
study of Sun's vibrations
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːlioʊˌsaɪzˈmɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌhiːliəʊˌsaɪzˈmɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of vibrations and waves on the Sun's surface to investigate its internal structure

Examples

Helioseismology studies waves
Through helioseismology, scientists can map the Sun's internal layers and understand its dynamics.
Advances in helioseismology have revealed details about the Sun's core and rotation.
The field of helioseismology uses solar oscillations to study processes deep within the Sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asteroseismology - the study of vibrations and waves in stars other than the Sun
  • synonym: seismology - the scientific study of earthquakes and the movement of waves through the Earth
