with eyes open

adverb
fully aware of risks or realities
1.3
Pronunciation: / wɪð aɪz ˈoʊ.pən / (AmE), / wɪð aɪz ˈəʊ.pən / (BrE)
fully aware of the possible risks or realities of a situation; not naive or unaware

Examples

With eyes open fully
Going into the merger with eyes open helped them prepare for any difficulties.
She accepted the challenges with eyes open, knowing what could go wrong.
You must invest in this business with eyes open to possible losses.
