disappropriation

noun
withdrawal of funds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of cancelling or withdrawing a previously granted allocation of money, funds, or resources, especially by a government or organization

Examples

Government disappropriation of funds
The sudden disappropriation of funds left the project without the resources it needed to continue.
After the investigation, the committee recommended the disappropriation of the previously approved budget.
Government officials debated the disappropriation of resources from underperforming programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appropriation - the act of officially setting aside money or resources for a specific purpose
  • synonym: retraction - the act of taking something back or withdrawing it
