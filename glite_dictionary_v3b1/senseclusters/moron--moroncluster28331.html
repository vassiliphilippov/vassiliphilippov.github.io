moron

noun
offensive term for a foolish person
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔːrɑn / (AmE), / ˈmɒrɒn / (BrE)
an offensive or insulting word for someone who is very foolish or lacks intelligence (sometimes used humorously about oneself)

Examples

A moron person
I can't believe he acted like such a moron during the meeting.
Only a moron would leave their keys in the car.
She called him a moron after he broke the window by accident.
He laughed and said, 'What a moron I am!' after making a silly mistake.
I felt like a moron when I realized I'd left my keys in the car.
Please don’t call yourself a moron; everyone makes mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who behaves in a silly or unwise way
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid or foolish person
