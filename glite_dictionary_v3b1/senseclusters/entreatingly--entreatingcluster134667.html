entreatingly

adverb
in a beseeching manner
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtritɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtriːtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that expresses a sincere or earnest request; beseechingly.

Examples

To speak entreatingly
He spoke entreatingly, trying to persuade them to let him stay.
She looked entreatingly at her teacher, hoping for a second chance.
The child held out her hand entreatingly for another cookie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imploringly - in a pleading or begging manner
