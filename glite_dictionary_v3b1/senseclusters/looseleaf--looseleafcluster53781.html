looseleaf

adjective
with easily removable pages
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈluːsˌliːf / (BrE)
(of a notebook, binder, or paper) having individual pages that can be easily removed or rearranged.

Examples

A looseleaf notebook
He organized his recipes in a looseleaf notebook for convenience.
She prefers using a looseleaf binder so she can add or remove notes easily.
The class was required to submit their work on looseleaf paper.
