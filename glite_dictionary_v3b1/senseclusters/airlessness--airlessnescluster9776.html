airlessness

noun
lack of fresh air
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈer.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈeə.r.ləs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈeə.ləs.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of having no air or no fresh air in a space, causing stuffiness or making it hard to breathe

Examples

Room airlessness
The airlessness of the small attic made it difficult to stay there for long.
After hours in the packed conference room, the airlessness became almost unbearable.
He opened a window to relieve the airlessness that had built up overnight.
The airlessness inside the sealed chamber made it dangerous to enter without proper equipment.
Astronauts must wear special suits to survive the airlessness of outer space.
The airlessness in the old basement made it difficult to breathe for more than a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atmosphere - the layer of gases surrounding a planet
  • synonym: stuffiness - the quality of being unpleasantly warm and lacking fresh air
  • synonym: vacuum - a space entirely devoid of matter, including air
  • antonym: ventilation - the movement of fresh air into a space
