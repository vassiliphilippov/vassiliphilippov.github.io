apple tree

noun
tree that bears apples
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.əl ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Malus, especially one that produces firm, rounded, edible fruits known as apples.

Examples

The apple tree blooms
The apple tree is in full bloom, covering the garden with fragrant flowers.
Under the old apple tree, children gathered to taste the fresh fruit in autumn.
We planted a young apple tree in our backyard last spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apple - the rounded fruit produced by an apple tree
  • degree lower: pear tree - a tree that bears pears
