  • paint
    1.0 50/m

  • stain
    3.2 10/m
    stain means marking or changing a surface color often permanently or accidentally, while color simply means to add or change color in general.
  • colorize
    4.8 0.2/m

  • tint
    3.6 3/m

  • touch
