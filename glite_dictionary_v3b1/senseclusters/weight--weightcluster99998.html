weight

noun
exercise equipment for weightlifting
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / weɪt / (AmE), / weɪt / (BrE)
a heavy object, especially one used for exercise, to hold things down, or as a burden

Examples

Lift a weight
He picked up a weight and began his morning workout routine.
She increased the weight she was lifting as her strength improved.
The gym is equipped with a variety of weights for different strength exercises.
She placed a weight on the papers so they wouldn't blow away.
The door wouldn't close until we put a heavy weight against it.
The fisherman used a weight to keep his line at the bottom of the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbell - a long bar with weights attached at each end, used for weightlifting
  • synonym: burden - a load, typically a heavy one
  • synonym: dumbbell - a short bar with a weight at each end, used for exercise
  • synonym: load - a heavy or bulky thing that is being carried or is about to be carried
