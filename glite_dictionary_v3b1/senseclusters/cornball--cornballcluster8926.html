cornball

noun
silly or goofy person
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a person who is silly, goofy, overly sentimental, or considered unsophisticated or old-fashioned

Examples

A cornball at dinner
Everyone laughed when the cornball started telling his ridiculous jokes at dinner.
She can be a real cornball when she tries to cheer people up.
At the reunion, Mark played the cornball and wore a silly costume.
They teased him as a cornball for wearing clothes that were out of style.
Her cornball jokes made everyone laugh, even if they were a bit old-fashioned.
Some city kids called the new student a cornball because of his rural accent.

  • synonym: hick - a person from the countryside regarded as unsophisticated
  • synonym: joker - someone who likes to make jokes or act playfully
  • antonym: serious person - someone who behaves in a thoughtful or earnest way
  • antonym: sophisticate - a person with refined tastes and manners
