nonadherence

noun
failure to comply
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnədˈhɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒnədˈhɪərəns / (BrE)
failure or refusal to follow a rule, guideline, or prescribed treatment

Examples

Nonadherence to treatment
Nonadherence to medication can lead to serious health problems.
The doctor discussed the risks of nonadherence with her patient during the appointment.
Researchers are studying the causes of nonadherence in chronic disease management.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adherence - the act of following a rule or treatment
  • synonym: noncompliance - failure to act according to a rule or instruction
