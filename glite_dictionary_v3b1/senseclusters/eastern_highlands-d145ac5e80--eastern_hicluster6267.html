Eastern Highlands

proper noun
mountain range in eastern Australia
Pronunciation: / ˈistərn ˈhaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈhaɪləndz / (BrE)
a mountain range along the eastern coast of Australia, extending from Queensland through New South Wales to Victoria

Examples

The Eastern Highlands mountains
Many rivers in Australia originate in the Eastern Highlands before flowing towards the coast.
The Eastern Highlands have a unique ecosystem, with diverse flora and fauna found nowhere else.
Tourists enjoy hiking and sightseeing throughout the Eastern Highlands due to its stunning landscapes.
