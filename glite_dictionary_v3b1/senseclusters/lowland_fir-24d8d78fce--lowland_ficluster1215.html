lowland fir

noun
tall fir tree of the Pacific Northwest
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlænd ˌfɜr / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlænd ˌfɜː / (BrE)
A tall fir tree native to the Pacific coast of northwestern America, known for its long curved branches and deep green leaves.
