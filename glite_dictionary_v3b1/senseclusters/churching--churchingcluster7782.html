churching

verb
to bless after childbirth
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɜrtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
to perform the Christian rite of blessing a woman after childbirth, or more generally to bring someone to church for a religious ceremony

Examples

To church a mother
The priest was churching the new mother as part of the traditional ceremony.
After her recovery, she looked forward to churching and returning to the community.
In some cultures, churching is still an important ritual for women after childbirth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baptize - to perform the Christian rite of baptism
  • synonym: bless - to ask for divine favor or protection for someone
