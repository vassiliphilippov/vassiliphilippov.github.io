hazer

noun
rodeo assistant rider
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzər / (AmE), / ˈheɪzə / (BrE)
a person who rides alongside a steer or other animal in a rodeo event to keep it moving straight and under control

Examples

Rodeo hazer
The hazer played a crucial role in helping the steer wrestler during the competition.
Without a skilled hazer, the steer might not run straight in the arena.
Each hazer must coordinate closely with the main rider to ensure a successful run.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rodeo clown - a performer who distracts animals and protects riders in rodeos
  • synonym: steer wrestler - a rodeo competitor who wrestles steers to the ground
