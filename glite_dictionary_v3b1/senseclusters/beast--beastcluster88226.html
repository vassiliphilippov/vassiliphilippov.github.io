beast

noun
cruel or savage person
6.5 5.54/m
Pronunciation: / bist / (AmE), / biÀêst / (BrE)
A person who is extremely cruel, savage, or lacking in human feeling.

Examples

A cruel beast
Despite his charming appearance, he could be a real beast when angered.
She called her captor a beast after seeing his cruel behavior.
The dictator was described as a beast for the atrocities he committed against his people.

Synonyms

  • savage
    7.9 3.72/m
    savage is violently brutal and often wild in action, while beast describes a person who is very cruel and without human feeling.
  • monster
    3.3 12/m
    monster is a cruel, inhuman person people fear, while beast describes someone lacking human feeling and showing savage cruelty.
  • brute
    5.1 3.45/m
    brute is a rough, violent person who acts by force, while beast highlights a loss of human feeling and mercy.
