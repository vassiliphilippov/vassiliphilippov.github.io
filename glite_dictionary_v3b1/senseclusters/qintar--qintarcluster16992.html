qintar

noun
Albanian monetary subunit (1/100 of a lek)
Pronunciation: / kinˈtɑr / (AmE), / kinˈtɑː / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Albania, equal to one hundredth of a lek; 100 qindarka equal 1 lek.

Examples

One qintar coin
Prices in the market were sometimes listed in both leks and qintar for convenience.

The rare coin collector proudly displayed a 1926 qintar in his album.
