accentuate

verb
emphasize, make more prominent
6.6 4.9
Pronunciation: /æks.ˈen.tʃuː.eɪt/ /; / /əks.ˈen.tʃuː.eɪt/ (AmE), /əks.ˈen.tʃuː.eɪt/ /; / /æks.ˈen.tʃuː.eɪt/ (BrE)
to make something more noticeable or prominent; to emphasize a feature or aspect

Examples

To accentuate a feature
She wore a belt to accentuate her waist.
The lighting was designed to accentuate the beauty of the artwork on display.
This report will accentuate the importance of sustainable practices in business.
He chose a tie that would accentuate the blue of his eyes.
Her statement served to accentuate the main point of the argument.
The lighting in the gallery was designed to accentuate the colors in each painting.

  • synonym: highlight - to make something stand out or more noticeable
