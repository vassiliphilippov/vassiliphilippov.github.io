halutz

noun
Jewish pioneer in Palestine
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈluːts / (AmE), / hɑːˈluːts / (BrE)
a Jewish pioneer, especially one who settled or prepared to settle in Palestine before the establishment of the state of Israel
