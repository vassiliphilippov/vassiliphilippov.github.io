Norseman

noun
male native or inhabitant of Norway
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrsmən / (AmE), / ˈnɔːsmən / (BrE)
a man from Norway, especially one from the historical Norse or Viking period

Examples

A Norseman from Norway
A Norseman would often travel great distances by sea in search of new lands.
Archaeologists discovered the grave of a Norseman dating back over a thousand years.
The fierce reputation of the Norseman spread across medieval Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Norwegian - a person from Norway
  • synonym: Viking - a member of the Scandinavian seafaring people (8th–11th centuries)
