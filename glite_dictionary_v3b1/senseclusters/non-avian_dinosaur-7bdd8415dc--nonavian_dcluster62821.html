non-avian dinosaur

noun
dinosaur that is not a bird
9.9 7.9
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈeɪviən ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / nɒn ˈeɪviən ˈdaɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
any dinosaur species that is not a bird, especially those that lived before the mass extinction at the end of the Cretaceous period

Examples

The mass extinction event 66 million years ago wiped out all non-avian dinosaur species.
