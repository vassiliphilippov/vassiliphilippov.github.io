non-avian dinosaur

noun
dinosaur that is not a bird
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈeɪviən ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / nɒn ˈeɪviən ˈdaɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
any dinosaur species that is not a bird, especially those that lived before the mass extinction at the end of the Cretaceous period

Examples

Non-avian dinosaurs fossils
Fossils of non-avian dinosaurs provide crucial clues about prehistoric ecosystems.
Some paleontologists study non-avian dinosaurs to learn about the evolutionary links to modern birds.
The mass extinction event 66 million years ago wiped out all non-avian dinosaur species.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avian dinosaur - dinosaur that is a bird or closely related to birds
  • synonym: dinosaur - large extinct reptile of the Mesozoic Era
