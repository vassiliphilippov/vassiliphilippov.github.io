thorned

adjective
with thorns
Pronunciation: / θɔrnd / (AmE), / θɔːnd / (BrE)
having thorns or sharp, pointed projections

Examples

A thorned branch
The thorned branches made the path difficult to walk through.
We had to be careful around the thorned bushes in the garden.
The thorned stem protected the plant from being eaten by animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spiny - covered with or having many spines or sharp points
