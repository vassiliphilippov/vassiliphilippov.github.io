catechin

noun
a flavonoid compound found in plants
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬ɪˌkɪn / (AmE), / ˈkætɪkɪn / (BrE)
a crystalline compound, classified as a flavonoid, found in various plants and used for its antioxidant properties

Examples

Green tea catechin
Green tea is rich in catechin, which is believed to have health benefits.
Laboratory tests showed that pure catechin could inhibit the growth of certain bacteria.
The researchers analyzed the catechin content in different herbal extracts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flavonoid - a class of plant compounds with antioxidant properties
