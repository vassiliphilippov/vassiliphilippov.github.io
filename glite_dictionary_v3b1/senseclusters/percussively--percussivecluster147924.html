percussively

adverb
with striking action
6.4
Pronunciation: / pərˈkʌsɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈkʌsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves striking or beating, especially to produce sound or rhythm

Examples

Play percussively on piano
The pianist struck the keys percussively to add excitement to the piece.
Raindrops fell percussively on the metal roof during the storm.
She tapped the table percussively to get everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a soft or mild manner
  • synonym: rhythmically - in a way that follows a regular pattern or beat
