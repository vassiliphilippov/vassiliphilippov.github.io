Bering Standard Time

noun
time zone in the 11th hour west of Greenwich
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ ˈstændərd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɛərɪŋ ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
The standard time for the eleventh time zone west of Greenwich, based on the 165th meridian west, formerly used in the Midway Islands.

Examples

Bering Standard Time zone
Flights were scheduled to arrive at 3:00 PM Bering Standard Time.
Maps from the 20th century may still display Bering Standard Time for some Pacific territories.
The Midway Islands once observed Bering Standard Time, putting them 11 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time.
