Tylenchus tritici

noun
parasitic roundworm species affecting wheat
Pronunciation: / taɪˈlɛŋkəs traɪˈtaɪsaɪ / (AmE), / taɪˈlɛŋkəs trɪˈtɪsiː / (BrE)
a small roundworm species that is parasitic on wheat plants, causing damage to crops

Examples

Tylenchus tritici worm pest
Tylenchus tritici is a significant pest in wheat-growing regions worldwide.
Farmers monitor their crops closely for signs of Tylenchus tritici infestation.
The spread of Tylenchus tritici can cause substantial losses in wheat yield.
