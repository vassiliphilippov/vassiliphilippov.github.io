apocryphal

adjective
relating to the Apocrypha (biblical writings)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑː.krə.fəl / (AmE), / əˈpɒ.krɪ.fəl / (BrE)
Relating to or belonging to the Apocrypha, a group of ancient writings not included in the standard biblical canon.

Examples

Apocryphal books
Certain books rejected by the Protestant churches are considered apocryphal in their tradition.
She wrote a thesis on the apocryphal texts of the Old Testament.
The origins of the apocryphal stories in early Christian writings are still debated.
