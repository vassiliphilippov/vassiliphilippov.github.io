valise

noun
small overnight suitcase
Pronunciation: / vəˈliːs / (AmE), / vəˈliːs / (BrE)
A small suitcase or overnight bag used for carrying clothing and belongings on short trips.

Examples

A valise bag
He packed a change of clothes in his valise before leaving for the weekend trip.
She placed the valise on the hotel bed and began to unpack.
The old man carried a battered valise filled with travel souvenirs.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bag - a general term for a container used for carrying things
  • synonym: suitcase - a rectangular travelling bag for carrying clothes
  • degree higher: trunk - a large, strong container for transporting clothes and other possessions
