seigniorial

adjective
of a feudal lord
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːnjəˈrɪəl / (AmE), / ˌsiːnjəˈrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to a lord or the rights and authority of a feudal lord

Examples

Seigniorial rights

Many seigniorial privileges were challenged during the revolution.
The castle stood as a symbol of seigniorial power in the region.
