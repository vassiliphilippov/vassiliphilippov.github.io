wad

noun
small mass of soft material
Pronunciation: / wɑd / (AmE), / wɒd / (BrE)
a small lump or mass of soft material, or something chewable pressed together

Examples

A wad of tissue
A wad of paper towels was used to mop up the spilled juice.
He threw away the dirty wad of cotton after cleaning his wound.
She stuffed a wad of tissue into the hole to stop the leak.
He spat the wad onto the ground after losing the taste.
He took a fresh wad of chewing tobacco from his pocket and put it in his mouth.
She always keeps a wad of gum in her bag to chew after meals.
