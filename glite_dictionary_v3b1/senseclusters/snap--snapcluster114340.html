snap

noun
something very easy to do
3.3
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
an easy task or something requiring little effort; a thing that is very easy to do

Examples

A snap to finish
He fixed the problem in a snap, impressing everyone in the room.
Passing that test was a snap for her since she had studied so much.
The instructions were so clear that assembling the furniture was a snap.

Related Terms

  • antonym: challenge - a task that is difficult or demands effort
  • synonym: cinch - something that is very easy to do
  • synonym: doddle - a task that is very easy
