trail

noun
a mark or track left behind
1.4 12/m
Pronunciation: / treÉªl / (AmE), / treÉªl / (BrE)
a visible mark, track, or sign left behind by a person, animal, or thing that has passed

Examples

A trail of footprints
A trail of footprints led down to the riverbank.
She noticed a trail of cookie crumbs on the kitchen counter.
The hikers followed the trail of broken branches through the forest.

Synonyms

  • trackway
    7.0 0.06/m
    a trackway is a path of repeated ground marks made by many walks or vehicles, while a trail is any single visible track left behind.
  • trace
    3.3 20/m
    a trace is a very small sign or amount that shows something was there, while a trail is a clear visible mark left by passage.
