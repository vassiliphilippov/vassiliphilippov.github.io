trail

noun
a mark or track left behind
1.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /treɪl/ (AmE), /treɪl/ (BrE)
a visible mark, track, or sign left behind by a person, animal, or thing that has passed

Examples

A trail of footprints
A trail of footprints led down to the riverbank.
She noticed a trail of cookie crumbs on the kitchen counter.
The hikers followed the trail of broken branches through the forest.
