matchable

adjective
able to be matched
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmætʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be found, identified, or paired as a match, especially by a search, pattern, or filter.

Examples

If your profile is not matchable, you may not appear in search results.
