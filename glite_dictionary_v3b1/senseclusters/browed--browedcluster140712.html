browed

adjective
having eyebrows
Pronunciation: / braʊd / (AmE), / braʊd / (BrE)
having eyebrows or a particular type of brow, often used in combination to describe appearance

Examples

High browed statue
The ancient statue was browed in a way that suggested wisdom and age.
She admired the strong, darkly browed features of the portrait.
The artist painted a softly browed face to give the character a gentle look.
