subspecialist

noun
narrow-field expert
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈspɛʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈspɛʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a professional who has advanced expertise in a very specific area within a broader field, especially in medicine

Examples

Medical subspecialist
After initial tests, the doctor referred her to a subspecialist for a more detailed evaluation.
A pediatric subspecialist focuses on treating rare childhood diseases.
Hospitals often employ a subspecialist to handle complex cases in cardiology.
