grave

noun
burial place for a dead body
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪv / (AmE), / ɡreɪv / (BrE)
a place in the ground where a dead body is buried, often marked by a tombstone

Examples

A grave site
After the funeral, friends gathered at the grave to pay their respects.
Every year, she visits her father's grave to leave fresh flowers.
The grave was marked with a simple wooden cross and a nameplate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coffin - box in which a dead body is buried or cremated
  • synonym: crypt - underground room or vault for burial
  • synonym: tomb - large vault, chamber, or stone structure for burial
