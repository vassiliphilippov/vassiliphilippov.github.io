pediatrician

noun
doctor specializing in children's health
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdiəˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪəˈtrɪʃən /; / ˌpiːdiəˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in the medical care of children and infants.

Examples

Pediatrician for children
After completing medical school, she decided to become a pediatrician.
Many parents consult a pediatrician for their child's yearly check-ups.
The hospital is hiring a new pediatrician to join the children's ward.
After medical school, she decided to become a paediatrician and work with children.
Parents often consult a paediatrician for advice on vaccinations and nutrition.
The paediatrician examined the baby thoroughly before giving a diagnosis.
