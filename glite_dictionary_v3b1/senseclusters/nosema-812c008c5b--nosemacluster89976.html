Nosema

noun
bee disease by parasite
9.9
Pronunciation: / noʊˈsiːmə / (AmE), / nəʊˈsiːmə / (BrE)
a disease of adult bees caused by microsporidian parasites of the genus Nosema, resulting in weakened colonies and reduced lifespan

Examples

Nosema infection in bees
The beekeeper noticed that several hives were infected with Nosema after a cold, wet spring.
Treating Nosema early can help prevent the collapse of bee colonies.
