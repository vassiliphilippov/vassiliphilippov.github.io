rattlesnake plantain

noun
a small orchid with mottled leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræɾl̩ˌsneɪk ˈplænˌtaɪn / (AmE), / ˈratl̩ˌsneɪk ˈplænˌteɪn / (BrE)
any of several small orchids with mottled or striped leaves and spikes of small yellowish-white flowers growing in a twisted raceme, found in temperate and tropical regions

Examples

Rattlesnake plantain leaves
She identified the distinctive leaves of the rattlesnake plantain during her hike in the woods.
Some gardeners cultivate rattlesnake plantain for its unique variegated foliage.
The forest understory was dotted with rattlesnake plantain blooming among the ferns.
