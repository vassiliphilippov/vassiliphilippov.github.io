multiracial

adjective
involving people of different races
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
consisting of, involving, or relating to people of several different races

Examples

A multiracial group
A multiracial coalition of activists campaigned for equal rights.
The city prides itself on being a multiracial community, welcoming people from diverse backgrounds.
The school promotes a multiracial curriculum that reflects the diversity of its students.
