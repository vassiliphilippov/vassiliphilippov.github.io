suck

verb
be very bad or unpleasant (informal)
2.1
Pronunciation: / sʌk / (AmE), / sʌk / (BrE)
(informal) To be very bad, unpleasant, or of poor quality; to be objectionable.

Examples

This movie sucks
It sucks when your plans are ruined at the last minute.
My job sucks sometimes, but I need the money.
The weather really sucks today, so let's stay indoors.
