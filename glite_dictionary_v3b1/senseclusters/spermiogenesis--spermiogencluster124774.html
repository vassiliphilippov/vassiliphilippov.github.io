spermiogenesis

noun
formation of mature sperm
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌspɜːrmioʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌspɜːmɪəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (BrE)
the final stage of sperm cell development in which mature sperm are formed from spermatids

Examples

Final spermiogenesis stage
During spermiogenesis, spermatids undergo changes to become fully functional sperm cells.
Scientists study spermiogenesis to better understand male fertility and reproductive health.
Any disruption in spermiogenesis can lead to problems with sperm quality and count.
