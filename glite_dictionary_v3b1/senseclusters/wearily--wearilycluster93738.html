wearily

adverb
in a tired or exhausted way
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈwɪərɪli / (BrE)
In a tired or exhausted manner; showing fatigue.

Examples

To sigh wearily
After a long day of work, she sighed wearily and sat down on the couch.
He gazed wearily at the documents scattered across his desk.
The old man wearily climbed the stairs to his apartment.
