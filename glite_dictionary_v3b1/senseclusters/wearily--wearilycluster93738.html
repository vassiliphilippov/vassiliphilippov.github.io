Examples

To sigh wearily

Synonyms

  • tiredly
    1.6 0.54/m
    tiredly is used to show simple, visible tiredness in actions or speech, while wearily often suggests ongoing fatigue and low energy.
  • exhaustedly
    4.9 0.05/m
    exhaustedly is used for extreme, heavy tiredness after hard work, while wearily describes normal tired or worn-out manner.
  • lethargically
    6.7 0.08/m
    lethargically is slow and lacking energy, often suggesting sluggish movement, while wearily focuses on showing tiredness or fatigue.
  • unenergetically
    6.5 0.00/m
    unenergetically sounds more formal and means acting without energy, while wearily emphasizes visible tiredness and low physical or mental strength.
