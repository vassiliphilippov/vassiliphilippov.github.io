woot

verb
to cheer loudly
9.9 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / wuÀêt / (AmE), / wuÀêt / (BrE)
to shout 'woot' or make a loud exclamation to express excitement or approval, especially in a group setting

Examples

To woot together
The crowd began to woot as the band took the stage for their encore.
She couldn't help but woot when her team scored the winning goal.
Everyone in the audience wooted loudly at the end of the performance.
