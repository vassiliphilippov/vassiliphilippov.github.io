panache

noun
feathered plume on a helmet
8.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈnæʃ / (AmE), / pəˈnaʃ / (BrE)
a plume of feathers, especially one worn on a helmet as an ornament

Examples

Panache of feathers
Her costume included a golden helmet topped with an extravagant panache.
The general's panache signified his rank during the parade.
The knight rode proudly, his helmet adorned with a bright red panache.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plume - an ornamental feather or tuft of feathers
