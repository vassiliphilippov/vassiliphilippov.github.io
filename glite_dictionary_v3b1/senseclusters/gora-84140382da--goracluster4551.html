Gora

proper noun
Tagore's 1910 novel
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊrə / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːrə / (BrE)
the title of a 1910 novel by Rabindranath Tagore, set in colonial India and exploring themes of identity and politics

Examples

The themes of identity and social reform are central to Gora.
