nonliquid

adjective
not liquid or cashable
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
not in a liquid state or not easily converted into cash or ready money

Examples

Nonliquid assets
The company held several nonliquid assets, such as real estate and equipment.
Because the funds were nonliquid, they could not be accessed immediately in an emergency.
Some investments are considered nonliquid because they cannot be quickly sold for cash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illiquid - not easily converted into cash
  • antonym: liquid - easily converted into cash or in a liquid state
