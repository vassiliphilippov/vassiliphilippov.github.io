viewport

noun
small viewing window
Pronunciation: / ˈvjuːˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈvjuːˌpɔːt / (BrE)
a small window or opening that allows someone to look into or view another space, often in a door or vehicle

Examples

Spacecraft viewport
The astronaut looked through the viewport to see the stars outside the spacecraft.
A security guard checked the visitor through the viewport in the heavy metal door.
The old safe had a tiny viewport so you could see the dials inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peephole - a small hole in a door for looking through
  • synonym: window - an opening in a wall or vehicle to let in light or air and allow people to see out
