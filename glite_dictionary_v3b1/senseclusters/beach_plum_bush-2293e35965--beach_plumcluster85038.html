beach plum bush

noun
North American coastal shrub with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / biːtʃ plʌm bʊʃ / (AmE), / biːtʃ plʌm bʊʃ / (BrE)
a seacoast shrub of northeastern North America with showy white flowers and edible purple fruit

Examples

The beach plum bush flowers
The spring blossoms of the beach plum bush attract many bees and other pollinators.
We found a beach plum bush growing near the dunes, its purple fruit ripe for picking.
Wildlife often feed on the berries of the beach plum bush along the shoreline.
