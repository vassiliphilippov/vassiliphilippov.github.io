ochlocracy

noun
rule by the mob; mob government
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈklɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / ɒˈklɒkrəsi / (BrE)
Government by the masses or mob; rule or control by a disorderly crowd.

Examples

Ochlocracy by mob
Historians warn that unchecked dissent can lead to ochlocracy rather than true democracy.
In an ochlocracy, laws are easily overturned by the current passions of the mob.
The prime minister condemned the violent protests as a dangerous slide toward ochlocracy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autocracy - government by a single person with absolute power; antonym of ochlocracy
  • antonym: democracy - government by the people, often contrasted with ochlocracy
  • synonym: mob rule - government or control by a disorderly crowd; synonym of ochlocracy
