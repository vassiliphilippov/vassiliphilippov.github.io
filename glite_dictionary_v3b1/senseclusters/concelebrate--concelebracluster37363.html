concelebrate

verb
to lead a ceremony together
9.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛlɪˌbreɪt /; / kənˈsɛləˌbreɪt / (AmE), / kɒnˈsɛlɪˌbreɪt / (BrE)
to lead or perform a religious ceremony, especially the Mass or Eucharist, together with other officiants
