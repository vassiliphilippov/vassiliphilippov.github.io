dramaturgically

adverb
in a theatrical way
Pronunciation: / ˌdræməˈtɜrdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdræməˈtɜːdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is theatrical or designed to create a dramatic effect, often for show.

Examples

Dramaturgically make an entrance
He entered the meeting dramaturgically, making sure all eyes were on him.
The announcement was made dramaturgically, with music and dramatic lighting.
She always tells stories dramaturgically, using gestures and changes in her voice.
