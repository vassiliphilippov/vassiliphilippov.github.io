wheelway

noun
road for vehicles
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlˌweɪ / (BrE)
a road or path constructed for the use of wheeled vehicles such as bicycles or carriages

Examples

Forest wheelway
The old wheelway through the forest is now overgrown with grass and shrubs.
Cyclists enjoy riding along the newly paved wheelway that connects the two towns.
The city plans to restore the historic wheelway for recreational use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pathway - a path or track for walking or cycling
  • synonym: roadway - a road, especially one used by vehicles
