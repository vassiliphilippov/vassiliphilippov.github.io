Objectivist

noun
Objectivist movement poet
9.9
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a poet associated with the early 20th-century Objectivist movement, emphasizing clarity, sincerity, and precise language

Examples

Objectivist poet
The Objectivist poets valued directness and clarity in their writing.
She wrote her thesis on the influence of Objectivist poetry in American literature.
Many Objectivist works are studied for their precise use of language and imagery.
