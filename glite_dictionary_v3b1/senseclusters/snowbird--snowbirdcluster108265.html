snowbird

noun
winter migrant
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a person, especially an older adult, who moves to a warmer region for the winter months

Examples

Retired snowbird travels south
Every year, thousands of snowbirds travel south to escape the cold northern winters.
The small town's population doubles when snowbirds arrive in November.
Many snowbirds own homes in both Canada and Florida to enjoy the best weather year-round.

Related Terms

  • synonym: migrant - a person who moves from one place to another, often for seasonal reasons
  • antonym: resident - a person who lives in one place permanently
