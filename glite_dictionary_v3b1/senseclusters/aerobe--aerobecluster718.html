aerobe

noun
organism needing oxygen to live
4.5 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.oʊb / (AmE), / ˈɛː.rəʊb / (BrE)
An organism, especially a bacterium, that requires oxygen to survive and grow.

Examples

An aerobe organism
Doctors identified the bacteria as an aerobe due to its requirement for oxygen.
In the laboratory, researchers grew the aerobe in an oxygen-rich environment.
When exposed to air, the aerobe thrives while anaerobic organisms may perish.
