crisp

adjective
pleasantly cold and fresh (weather)
5.1
Pronunciation: / krɪsp / (AmE), / krɪsp / (BrE)
(of air or weather) pleasantly cold, fresh, and invigorating.

Examples

Crisp autumn air
It's the kind of crisp autumn day that makes you want to be outside.
The air felt crisp and clean after the rain.
We enjoyed a crisp morning walk through the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nippy - chilly and sharp; cold and brisk
  • synonym: snappy - brisk, lively, or chilly (weather)
