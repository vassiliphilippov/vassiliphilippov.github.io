Rallidae

noun
family of birds: rails, crakes, gallinules, coots
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrælɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈralɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of medium-sized birds including rails, crakes, gallinules, and coots, often found in wetlands.

Examples

Among the Rallidae, the coot is perhaps the most commonly observed by birdwatchers.
