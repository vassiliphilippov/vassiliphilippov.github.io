trustingly

adverb
in a trusting manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows trust or confidence in someone or something.

Examples

To trustingly hand keys
She trustingly handed her keys to the neighbor while she was on vacation.
The child looked trustingly into the doctor's eyes before the checkup began.
They trustingly followed the guide along the unfamiliar trail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: suspiciously - in a manner showing distrust or doubt
