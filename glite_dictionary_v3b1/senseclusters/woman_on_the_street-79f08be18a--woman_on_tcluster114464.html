woman on the street

noun
ordinary or typical woman
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmən ɑn ðə strit / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən ɒn ðə striːt / (BrE)
an ordinary woman, especially one who is not famous or in a position of authority; a typical or average member of the public

Examples

An ordinary woman on the street
As a woman on the street, she had never been asked by the media to share her views before.
Reporters often ask a woman on the street for her thoughts to represent public opinion.
The interview featured a woman on the street giving her opinion about the new law.
