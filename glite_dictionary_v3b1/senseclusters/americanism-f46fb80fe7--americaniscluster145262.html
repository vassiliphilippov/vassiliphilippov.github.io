Americanism

noun
a custom peculiar to the U.S.
3.8
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kəˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kəˌnɪz.əm / (BrE)
a custom, practice, or way of life that is characteristic of the United States or its people

Examples

Modern Americanism trends
Fast food culture is often cited as a modern Americanism influencing global eating habits.
Halloween is an Americanism that has spread to many other countries.
Some critics see tipping in restaurants as a needless Americanism.

Related Terms
