all day

adverb
for the whole day
Pronunciation: / ɔl deɪ / (AmE), / ɔːl deɪ / (BrE)
for the entire duration of the day; continuously or throughout the whole day.

Examples

All day long
He could talk about sports all day without getting bored.
I've been waiting for the delivery all day and it's still not here.
We spent all day at the beach enjoying the sunshine.
