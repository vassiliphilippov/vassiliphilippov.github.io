cage of expectations

noun
being limited by societal norms
Pronunciation: / keɪdʒ əv ɛkˌspɛkˈteɪʃənz / (AmE), / keɪdʒ əv ɛkˌspɛkˈteɪʃənz / (BrE)
A metaphorical state of being restricted or limited by societal or external pressures and norms.

Examples

Living in a cage of expectations
After years of living in a cage of expectations, he finally pursued his own dreams.
Many people struggle to break free from the cage of expectations built by family and peers.
The film depicts the protagonist escaping the cage of expectations that limited her true potential.

Related Terms

  • synonym: social pressure - influence exerted by society to conform to certain behaviors or norms
