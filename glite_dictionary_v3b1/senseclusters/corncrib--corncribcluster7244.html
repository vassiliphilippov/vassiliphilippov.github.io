corncrib

noun
structure for storing and drying corn
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrn.krɪb / (AmE), / ˈkɔːn.krɪb / (BrE)
a structure, typically with slatted sides, used for storing and drying ears of corn on a farm

Examples

A corncrib on farm
Farmers once relied on the corncrib to store their harvest through the winter.
The children played hide and seek around the corncrib on the family farm.
The old corncrib behind the barn still stands after a century of use.
