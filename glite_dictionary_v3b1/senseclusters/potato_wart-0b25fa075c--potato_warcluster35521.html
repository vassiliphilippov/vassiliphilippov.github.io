potato wart

noun
fungal potato disease with warty growths
9.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪtoʊ wɔrt / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ wɔːt / (BrE)
a fungal disease of potatoes characterized by dark, warty, spongy growths on the tubers
