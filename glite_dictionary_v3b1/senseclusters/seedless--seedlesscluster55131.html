seedless

adjective
without seeds (in fruit or plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːdləs / (BrE)
(of fruit or plants) lacking seeds.

Examples

Seedless watermelons
Many people prefer seedless watermelons for convenience.
She bought a bag of seedless oranges at the market.
This variety of grape is seedless and easy to eat.
