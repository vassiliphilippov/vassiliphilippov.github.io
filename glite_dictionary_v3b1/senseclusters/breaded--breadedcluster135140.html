breaded

verb
to supply with bread
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdɪd / (BrE)
archaic: to give or supply someone with bread; to feed with bread

Examples

To bread the poor
During the flood, the volunteers breaded hungry families with loaves each morning.
In times of hardship, the church breaded the poor in the village.
The kind woman breaded travelers who passed by her cottage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feed - to give food to someone
  • antonym: starve - to suffer or die from lack of food
