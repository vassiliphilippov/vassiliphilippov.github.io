wet suit

noun
close-fitting garment for cold water activities
3.3 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…õt suÀêt / (AmE), / Ààw…õt sjuÀêt / (BrE)
A close-fitting garment made of neoprene or similar material, worn in cold water to retain body heat, especially by divers, surfers, and swimmers.

Examples

A wet suit for diving
Divers rely on a wet suit to stay warm underwater during long swims.
He put on his wet suit before heading out for an early morning surf.
The rental shop offers wet suits in a variety of sizes for all guests.
