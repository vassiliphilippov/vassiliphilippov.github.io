lifelogging

noun
digital self-recording
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌlɔɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌlɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of continuously recording and storing personal data, activities, and experiences using digital devices
