teem in

verb
to exist in large quantities somewhere
Pronunciation: / tim ɪn / (AmE), / tiːm ɪn / (BrE)
to be present in a place in very large numbers or amounts

Examples

Markets teem in fruits
The city's markets teem in fresh fruits and colorful vegetables every morning.
The forests teem in wildlife, making them perfect for eco-tours.
The old library teems in rare manuscripts and historical artifacts.

Related Terms
