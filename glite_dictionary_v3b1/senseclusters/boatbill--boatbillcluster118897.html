boatbill

noun
tropical heron with broad bill
8.8 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊt.bɪl/ /; / /ˈboʊt.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊt.bɪl/ /; / /ˈbəʊt.bəl/ (BrE)
A tropical American heron with a broad, boat-shaped bill, related to night herons.

Examples

A boatbill heron
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot a boatbill in the rainforest.
Its unique, spoon-shaped bill distinguishes the boatbill from other herons.
The boatbill can be found along the rivers and streams of Central and South America.
