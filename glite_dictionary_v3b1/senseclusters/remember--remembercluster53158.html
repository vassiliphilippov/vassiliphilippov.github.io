Synonyms

  • recall
    2.4 82/m
    recall is a slightly more formal verb used for bringing facts to mind, while remember is common everyday speech.
  • recollect
    3.7 1.91/m
    recollect is more formal and often shows effort to bring an event to mind, while remember is more general and common.
  • retrieve
    4.1 3.98/m
    retrieve often describes getting facts or data back into the mind or a system, while remember means ordinary recalling.
  • think back
    1.9 4.52/m
    think back is a phrasal verb for actively trying to recall past events, while remember is the simple general verb.
