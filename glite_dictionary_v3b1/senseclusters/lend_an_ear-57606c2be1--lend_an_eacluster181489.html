lend an ear

verb
listen attentively or sympathetically
3.3
Pronunciation: / lɛnd ən ɪr / (AmE), / lɛnd ən ɪə / (BrE)
to listen sympathetically or attentively to someone

Examples

To lend an ear to friends
It’s important to lend an ear to friends when they’re facing difficulties.
She appreciated that her colleague would lend an ear when she was stressed.
Whenever you need to talk, I’m always happy to lend an ear.
