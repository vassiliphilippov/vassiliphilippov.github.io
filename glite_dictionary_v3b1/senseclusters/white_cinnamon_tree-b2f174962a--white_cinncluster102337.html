white cinnamon tree

noun
tree with white aromatic bark and purple-red flowers
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈsɪn.ə.mən triː / (AmE), / waɪt ˈsɪn.əm.ɪn ˌtriː / (BrE)
a large evergreen shrub or small tree with white aromatic bark, leathery leaves, and small purple to red flowers in terminal clusters
