therian

noun
person identifying as animal
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪriən / (AmE), / ˈθɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who identifies as having the spirit, mind, or identity of a non-human animal, often as part of a subculture

Examples

Online therian
At the online forum, several therians discussed their experiences of feeling connected to wolves or big cats.
She realized she was a therian after years of feeling a deep connection to foxes.
The documentary explored the lives of therians and how they express their animal identities.
