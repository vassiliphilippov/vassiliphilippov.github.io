cluttered

adjective
filled with too many things; messy
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌtɚd / (AmE), / ˈklʌtəd / (BrE)
(of a place or space) filled with too many things, making it disorganized or messy.

Examples

A cluttered room
After weeks of neglect, the garage became so cluttered that there was no space to park the car.
She found it hard to relax in the cluttered living room.
The desk in his office was always cluttered with papers and coffee cups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tidy - neat and orderly
