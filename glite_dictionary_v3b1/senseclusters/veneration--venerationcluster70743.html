Synonyms

  • reverence
    6.3 2/m
    reverence is deep, awed respect shown quietly in church or to elders, while veneration is the term for that religious honor.
  • adoration
    6.4 0.3/m
    adoration is strong loving worship often aimed at a god or saint, while veneration is serious respect and honor, not always love.
  • homage
    6.8 3/m
    homage is public, formal honor shown at ceremonies or speeches, while veneration is deep, often private religious respect or awe.
