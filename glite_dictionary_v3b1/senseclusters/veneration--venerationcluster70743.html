veneration

noun
great respect or reverence
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛnəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɛnəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
great respect or reverence for someone or something, especially in a religious context

Examples

Veneration for elders
Her veneration for her grandfather was evident in the stories she told about him.
The statue is an object of veneration for many pilgrims who visit the temple each year.
The tradition survives as an act of veneration for ancestors in their culture.
His veneration for the saints was evident in his daily prayers and rituals.
She spoke of her grandmother with such veneration that everyone listened in silence.
The old temple is treated with veneration by the local community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoration - deep love and respect, especially in a religious context
  • antonym: disrespect - lack of respect or esteem for someone or something
  • synonym: reverence - deep respect or awe for someone or something
