lathery

adjective
covered with or like lather
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlæðəri / (AmE), / ˈlɑːðəri / (BrE)
covered with or resembling lather; frothy or foamy in appearance or texture.

Examples

Lathery soap foam
After scrubbing the dog, her hands were lathery and slippery.
He rinsed off the lathery suds from the car after washing it.
The barber used a lathery cream to soften his beard before shaving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foamy - full of or covered with a mass of small bubbles
  • synonym: soapy - containing or resembling soap or soap suds
