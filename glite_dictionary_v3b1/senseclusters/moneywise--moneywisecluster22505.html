moneywise

adjective
financially prudent
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌwaɪz / (BrE)
having skill, knowledge, or good judgment in managing money; financially shrewd or prudent

Examples

A moneywise person
Being moneywise helped her build up her savings over the years.
It is important to be moneywise when planning for retirement.
His moneywise decisions allowed him to avoid unnecessary debt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagant - spending money carelessly or excessively
  • synonym: frugal - careful in spending money or resources
  • synonym: thrifty - using money carefully and not wastefully
