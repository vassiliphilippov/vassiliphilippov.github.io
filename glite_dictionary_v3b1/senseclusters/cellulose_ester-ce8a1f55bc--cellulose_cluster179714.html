cellulose ester

noun
an ester derived from cellulose and an acid
6.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsel.jʊ.loʊs ˈes.tər/ /; / /ˈsel.juː.loʊs ˈes.tər/ (AmE), /ˈsel.jʊ.ləʊs ˈes.tə/ /; / /ˈsel.juː.ləʊs ˈes.tə/ (BrE)
any compound formed by treating cellulose with an acid, resulting in an ester; used especially in plastics, films, and fibers.

Examples

A cellulose ester compound
