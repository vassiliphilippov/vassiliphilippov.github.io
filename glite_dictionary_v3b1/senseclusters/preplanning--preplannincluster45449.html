preplanning

noun
advance preparation
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈplænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈplænɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making plans or arrangements before something happens

Examples

Effective preplanning process
Effective preplanning is essential for a successful conference.
The team spent several weeks on preplanning before launching the new project.
Without proper preplanning, unexpected issues may arise during construction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preparation - the process of getting ready for something
