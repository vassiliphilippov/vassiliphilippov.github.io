macroinvertebrate

noun
large spineless animal
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊɪnˈvɜrtɪbrət / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊɪnˈvɜːtɪbrət / (BrE)
an animal without a backbone that is large enough to be seen without a microscope (such as an insect, worm, or snail)
