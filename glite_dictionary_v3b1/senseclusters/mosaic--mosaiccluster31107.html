mosaic

noun
pattern made of many small pieces
5.9
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪ.ɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪ.ɪk / (BrE)
a pattern or image composed of many small, often colored, pieces or elements arranged to create a design, or something that resembles such a pattern.

Examples

Mosaic of fields
Satellite images revealed a mosaic of farmland stretching for miles.
She arranged the photos on her wall in a mosaic to create a beautiful display.
The tablecloth featured a mosaic of bright colors and geometric shapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collage - an artistic composition made of various materials mounted on a surface
