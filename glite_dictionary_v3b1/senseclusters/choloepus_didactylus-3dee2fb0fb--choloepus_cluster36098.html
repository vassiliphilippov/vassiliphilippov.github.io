A species of sloth, also known as Linnaeus's two-toed sloth, native to South America and distinguished by having two long claws on each front foot.

Examples

The Choloepus didactylus sloth
Researchers observed a Choloepus didactylus feeding on leaves and fruit in its natural habitat.
The Choloepus didactylus spends most of its life hanging from tree branches in the rainforest canopy.
Unlike three-toed sloths, the Choloepus didactylus has only two claws on each front foot.
