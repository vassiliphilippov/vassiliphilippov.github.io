ethmoidal sinus

noun
small air cavity in the ethmoid bone by the nose
4.3
Pronunciation: / ɛθˈmɔɪdəl ˈsaɪnəs / (AmE), / ɛθˈmɔɪdəl ˈsaɪnəs / (BrE)
One of several small air-filled cavities in the ethmoid bone, located between the nose and the eyes and opening into the nasal cavity.

Examples

The ethmoidal sinus cavity
A CT scan revealed a blockage in her left ethmoidal sinus.
Infections sometimes spread from the nasal cavity to the ethmoidal sinus, leading to sinusitis.
The doctor explained that inflammation in the ethmoidal sinus was causing my recurring headaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontal sinus - a paranasal sinus located in the forehead region
  • synonym: maxillary sinus - a large paranasal sinus in the upper jaw
  • degree higher: paranasal sinus - any of the air-filled spaces surrounding the nasal cavity
  • synonym: sphenoidal sinus - a paranasal sinus located behind the ethmoid sinuses
