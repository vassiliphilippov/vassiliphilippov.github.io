epicheirema

noun
syllogism with supporting statement(s) for premises
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈkaɪrɪmə / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkʌɪərɪmə / (BrE)
A syllogism in which a statement supporting one or both of the premises is given together with the premises themselves.

An epicheirema syllogism
Each epicheirema contains an explicit reason supporting its premise within the syllogism.
