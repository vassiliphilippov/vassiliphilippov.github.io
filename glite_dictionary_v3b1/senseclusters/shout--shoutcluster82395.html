shout

noun
a loud cry or call
Pronunciation: / ʃaʊt / (AmE), / ʃaʊt / (BrE)
a loud cry or call, often made to express strong emotion, protest, or attract attention

Examples

A loud shout
A sudden shout from the back of the hall caught everyone's attention.
The angry crowd let out a deafening shout as the announcement was made.
The children responded to the invitation with a loud shout of joy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cry - a loud vocal sound to express emotion or attract attention
  • antonym: whisper - a very quiet way of speaking
