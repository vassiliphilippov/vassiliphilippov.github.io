rascally

adjective
mischievously playful or impish
Pronunciation: / ˈræs.kəli / (AmE), / ˈrɑːs.kəli / (BrE)
Playful in a mischievous or boldly impish way, often in a manner that is appealing rather than harmful.

Examples

Rascally grin
He flashed a rascally smile before playing another practical joke.
Her rascally sense of humor made everyone laugh at the party.
The boy gave his mother a rascally grin after hiding her shoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mischievous - causing trouble in a playful way
  • synonym: roguish - playfully mischievous
