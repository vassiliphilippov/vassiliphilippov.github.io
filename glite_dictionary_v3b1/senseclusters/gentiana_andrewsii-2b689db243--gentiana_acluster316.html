Gentiana andrewsii

noun
North American gentian species with closed blue or white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnʃiˈænə ˌænˈdruːziː / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnʃɪˈɑːniə ˌænˈdrjuːziː / (BrE)
A species of gentian native to eastern North America, known for its tubular blue or white flowers that remain mostly closed.
