stuff

verb
fill food with a stuffing
4.3
Pronunciation: / stʌf / (AmE), / stʌf / (BrE)
to fill (food, especially meat or vegetables) with a mixture of ingredients before cooking

Examples

To stuff a turkey
For Thanksgiving, we always stuff the turkey with a savory bread mixture.
She likes to stuff bell peppers with rice and cheese before baking them.
You should stuff the chicken loosely to allow for even cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fill - to make full; to put something into a space until it is full
