cycloalkene

noun
cyclic hydrocarbon with double bond(s)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkloʊælˈkiːn / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkləʊælˈkiːn / (BrE)
a cyclic hydrocarbon containing a closed ring of carbon atoms with one or more double bonds, but without aromatic properties

Examples

A cycloalkene molecule
Researchers synthesized a new cycloalkene with promising pharmaceutical potential.
Some cycloalkene compounds are important intermediates in organic synthesis.
The structure of a cycloalkene makes it more reactive than its cycloalkane counterpart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alkene - a hydrocarbon containing at least one carbon–carbon double bond
  • antonym: cycloalkane - a cyclic hydrocarbon with only single bonds
