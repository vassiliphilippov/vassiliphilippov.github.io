strenuousness

noun
the quality of requiring great effort
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛnjuəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈstrɛnjʊəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of requiring or showing great effort and energy

Examples

The strenuousness of exercise
She underestimated the strenuousness involved in training for a marathon.
The strenuousness of the hike left everyone exhausted but exhilarated.
The job’s strenuousness made it unsuitable for people with health conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ease - the absence of difficulty or effort
  • synonym: effortfulness - the quality of requiring effort
