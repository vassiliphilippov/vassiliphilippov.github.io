ethnobiology

noun
study of people and nature
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how different cultures interact with, use, and understand plants and animals in their environment

Examples

Ethnobiology study plants
She decided to study ethnobiology to learn how indigenous communities use local plants for medicine.
The conference on ethnobiology brought together researchers from around the world.
Through ethnobiology, scientists document traditional knowledge about animals and their roles in different cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropology - the study of human societies and cultures
  • synonym: biology - the scientific study of living organisms
  • synonym: ethnobotany - the study of how people use and understand plants
