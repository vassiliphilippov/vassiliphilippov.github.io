arm band

noun
decorative band worn on the arm
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrm ˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈɑːm ˌbænd / (BrE)
A band worn around the upper arm, used as a decorative accessory or to signify something.

Examples

A colorful arm band
At the festival, everyone received a colorful arm band as a souvenir.
She wore a silver arm band to match her bracelet.
The team captain put on a red arm band before the game.
