strip show

noun
erotic dance performance involving undressing
5.7
Pronunciation: / strɪp ʃoʊ / (AmE), / strɪp ʃəʊ / (BrE)
A form of entertainment where a performer gradually removes their clothing, usually to music, as an erotic display.

Examples

A strip show performance
She surprised everyone by organizing a private strip show for his birthday party.
The club advertised a strip show every Friday night.
The law in that city strictly regulates where a strip show can take place.
