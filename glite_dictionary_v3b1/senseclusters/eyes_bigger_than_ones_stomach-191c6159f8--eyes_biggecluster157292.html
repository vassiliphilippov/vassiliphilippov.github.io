eyes bigger than one's stomach

x
takes more food than can eat
5.1
Pronunciation: / aɪz ˈbɪɡər ðən wʌnz ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / aɪz ˈbɪɡə ðən wʌnz ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
used to describe someone who takes more food than they can actually eat, often because their desire exceeds their capacity

Examples

Eyes bigger than one's stomach at dinner
Every Thanksgiving, Dad has eyes bigger than his stomach and ends up leaving food on his plate.
I ordered so much at the buffet, but my eyes were bigger than my stomach and I couldn't finish it.
She realized her eyes were bigger than her stomach when she saw how much food she had ordered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bite off more than one can chew - to take on more than one can manage; to overestimate one's capabilities
  • synonym: overeat - to eat more food than necessary or healthy
