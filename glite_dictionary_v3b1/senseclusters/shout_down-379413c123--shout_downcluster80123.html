shout down

verb
silence by shouting over
2.9 5.0
Pronunciation: /ʃaʊt daʊn/ (AmE), /ʃaʊt daʊn/ (BrE)
to prevent someone from speaking or being heard by shouting loudly and persistently

Examples

To shout down a speaker
Angry audience members kept shouting down anyone who disagreed with them.
She was shouted down when she attempted to present her argument.
The protesters tried to shout down the speaker during his speech.
