nasolabial

adjective
between nose and mouth
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˈleɪbiəl / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˈleɪbɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the area or lines between the nose and the corners of the mouth

Examples

Nasolabial folds
The doctor noticed deep nasolabial folds on the patient's face.
Cosmetic treatments can help reduce the appearance of nasolabial lines.
Aging often causes the nasolabial area to become more pronounced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perioral - relating to the area around the mouth
