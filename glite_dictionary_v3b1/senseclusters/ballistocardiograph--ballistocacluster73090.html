ballistocardiograph

noun
medical device for measuring heart's force via body recoil
9.9 9.4
Pronunciation: /bæl.ɪs.toʊ.ˈkɑːr.diː.ə.græf/ /; / /bə.lɪs.toʊ.ˈkɑːr.diː.ə.græf/ (AmE), /bæl.ɪs.təʊ.ˈkɑː.diː.ə.grɑːf/ /; / /bə.lɪs.təʊ.ˈkɑː.diː.ə.grɑːf/ (BrE)
