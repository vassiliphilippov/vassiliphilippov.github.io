chango

proper noun
Yoruba thunder god
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃæŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a variant spelling of Shango, the Yoruba god of thunder and fire worshiped in Afro-Caribbean religions
