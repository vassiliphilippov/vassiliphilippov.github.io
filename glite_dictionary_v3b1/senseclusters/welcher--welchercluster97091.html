welcher

noun
person who avoids payment
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛltʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɛltʃə / (BrE)
a person who fails to pay money owed, especially after losing a bet

Examples

A welcher on bets
No one wanted to gamble with a known welcher.
After losing the bet, he was called a welcher for refusing to pay.
The group avoided playing cards with any welcher who did not honor their debts.
After losing the game, he was called a welcher for refusing to pay his debt.
No one trusted the welcher at the poker table after he failed to settle up last week.
If you make a bet, don't be a welcher when it's time to pay.
