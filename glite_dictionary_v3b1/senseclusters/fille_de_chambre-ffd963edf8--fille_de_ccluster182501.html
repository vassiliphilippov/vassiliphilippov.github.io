fille de chambre

noun
maid responsible for bedrooms (historical)
Pronunciation: / fiːl də ʃɑːmbrə / (AmE), / fiːl də ʃɒ̃bʁ / (BrE)
A maid employed to clean and care for bedrooms, especially in hotels; now mainly used in historical contexts.

Examples

The fille de chambre cleans
In the grand hotels of Paris, every floor had its own fille de chambre to attend to guests' needs.
She was hired as a fille de chambre at the historic manor, responsible for keeping the rooms immaculate.
The fille de chambre quietly tidied the suite while the guests were out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chambermaid - A maid who cleans and maintains bedrooms in a hotel or similar establishment.
