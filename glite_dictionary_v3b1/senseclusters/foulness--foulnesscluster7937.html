foulness

noun
strong offensive smell
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfaʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a strong, unpleasant, and offensive smell

Examples

Foulness of the air
Despite the foulness of the sewage, the workers continued their repairs.
She was overwhelmed by the foulness coming from the clogged drain.
The foulness of the garbage dump made it difficult to breathe near the site.
