jelly-like

adjective
having a jelly-like consistency or appearance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛliˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛliˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a consistency or appearance similar to jelly; soft, wobbly, and semi-solid.

Examples

Jelly-like texture
A strange, jelly-like film covered the surface of the pond.
Her legs felt jelly-like after the long, exhausting hike.
The sauce thickened into a jelly-like substance after cooling in the fridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gelatinous - having a jelly-like consistency
  • antonym: solid - firm and stable in shape, not liquid or fluid
