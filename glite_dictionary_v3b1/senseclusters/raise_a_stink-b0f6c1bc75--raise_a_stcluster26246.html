raise a stink

verb
complain loudly; protest vigorously
9.2 5.9
Pronunciation: /reɪz ə stɪŋk/ (AmE), /reɪz ə stɪŋk/ (BrE)
to complain loudly or take strong action to express discontent or objection

Examples

To raise a stink loudly
She will raise a stink if you try to change her schedule without asking.
The customers threatened to raise a stink if their complaints were ignored.
The residents raised a stink over the plan to cut down the trees in the park.
