delimitation

noun
marking or setting of a boundary
Pronunciation: / dɪˌlɪməˈteɪʃən /; / dɪˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of setting boundaries or the boundary itself that marks the limits of something
