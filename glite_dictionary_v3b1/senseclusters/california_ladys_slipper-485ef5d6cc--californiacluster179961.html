California lady's slipper

noun
native orchid of the US West Coast
Pronunciation: / kælɪˌfɔrnjə ˈleɪdiz ˌslɪpər / (AmE), / kælɪˌfɔːniə ˈleɪdiz ˌslɪpə / (BrE)
a species of wild orchid (Cypripedium californicum) native to southwestern Oregon and northern California, noted for its yellow-green flowers with distinctive white pouches
