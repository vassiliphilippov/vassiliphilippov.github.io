hocus

interjection
magic exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊkəs / (BrE)
an exclamation used by magicians or performers when pretending to perform magic or a trick

Examples

Hocus on stage
The magician waved his wand and shouted hocus to amaze the children.
With a quick movement and a loud hocus, the coin disappeared from her hand.
He said hocus as part of his magic act to entertain the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abracadabra - a word said by magicians when performing a magic trick
  • synonym: presto - an exclamation used to indicate something has happened suddenly, often in magic
