wideout

noun
football pass receiver
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdˌaʊt / (BrE)
a player in American football who lines up near the sideline to catch passes

Examples

A wideout player
The rookie wideout scored his first touchdown in last night's game.
Coaches praised the veteran wideout for his speed and reliable hands.
Every wideout practiced running routes before the big championship match.
