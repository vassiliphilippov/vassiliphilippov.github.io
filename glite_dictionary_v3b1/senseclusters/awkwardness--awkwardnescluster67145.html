awkwardness

noun
difficulty caused by size or shape
7.2 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɔː.kwərd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.kwəd.nəs/ (BrE)
difficulty in carrying or managing something due to its large size, shape, or weight.

Examples

Awkwardness of the box

The awkwardness of the large sofa made it difficult to carry upstairs.
They struggled with the awkwardness of the heavy box as they passed through the doorway.
