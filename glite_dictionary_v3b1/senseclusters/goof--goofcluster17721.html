goof

verb
to act playfully
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɡuːf / (AmE), / ɡuːf / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or playful way, often without serious purpose

Examples

To goof around
The children were goofing off in the backyard instead of doing their homework.
Sometimes it's good to spend an afternoon just goofing around with friends.
He was goofing during the meeting and made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool around - to behave in a silly or playful way
  • synonym: mess around - to spend time doing unimportant things in a playful way
  • antonym: work - to do tasks or activities with serious purpose
