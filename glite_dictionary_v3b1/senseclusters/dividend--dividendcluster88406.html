dividend

noun
number to be divided by another
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪvɪˌdɛnd / (AmE), / ˈdɪvɪdɛnd / (BrE)
the number that is to be divided by another number.

Examples

Math dividend
In the equation 12 ÷ 3 = 4, the number 12 is called the dividend.
To solve the problem, first identify the dividend and the divisor.
When you divide a dividend by a divisor, you get a quotient.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divisor - a number by which another number is divided
