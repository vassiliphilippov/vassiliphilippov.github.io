symbolic figure

noun
person or thing representing a deeper idea
Pronunciation: / sɪmˈbɑlɪk ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlɪk ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
a person or thing that stands for or represents an idea or concept with deeper meaning beyond their literal presence

Examples

A symbolic figure of power
In mythology, Zeus serves as a symbolic figure of power and authority.
Martin Luther King Jr. became a symbolic figure of the civil rights movement.
The dove is often used as a symbolic figure of peace in artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icon - a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol
  • synonym: symbol - something that stands for or suggests something else
