position paper

noun
written statement of an official stance
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɪʃ.ən ˌpeɪ.pɚ /; / pəˈzɪʃ.ən ˈpeɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃ.ən ˌpeɪ.pə /; / pəˈzɪʃ.ən ˈpeɪ.pə / (BrE)
a written document that states and argues for an organization's or individual's viewpoint or policy on a particular issue

Examples
