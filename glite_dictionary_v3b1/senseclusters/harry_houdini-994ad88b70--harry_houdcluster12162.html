Harry Houdini

proper noun

Pronunciation: / ˈhɛri huːˈdiːni / (AmE), / ˈhæri huːˈdiːni / (BrE)
(1874–1926) a famous Hungarian-born American magician and escape artist, renowned for his sensational escape acts involving chains, handcuffs, and locked containers.
