curvy road

noun
road with many bends
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːvi ˈroʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːvi ˈrəʊd / (BrE)
a road that has many curves and bends, not straight

Examples

A curvy road
Drivers should slow down when approaching a curvy road in poor weather conditions.
She enjoys cycling along the curvy road that winds through the village.
The mountain drive was challenging because of the curvy road and sharp turns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight road - a road without any significant curves or bends
  • synonym: winding road - a road with many turns or curves
