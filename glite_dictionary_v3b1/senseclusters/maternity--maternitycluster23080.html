maternity

noun
motherly warmth and tenderness
Pronunciation: / məˈtɜrnəti / (AmE), / məˈtɜːnəti / (BrE)
The quality of having or showing the tenderness, warmth, and affection characteristic of a mother.

Examples

Maternity care
Even without children of her own, she displayed a natural maternity that everyone admired.
Her maternity shone through as she lovingly cared for her younger siblings.
The teacher's maternity was evident in the way she comforted her students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motherliness - the quality of being motherly; maternal affection and tenderness
  • antonym: paternity - the quality or state of being a father; the paternal relationship
