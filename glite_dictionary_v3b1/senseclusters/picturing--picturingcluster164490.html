picturing

noun
the act of creating mental images
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of creating mental images or representations in the mind; visual imagery.

Examples

Picturing a scene in mind
Effective meditation often relies on the picturing of calming images or landscapes.
Her picturing of the story's setting helped immerse the readers in the scene.
The artist's process includes the picturing of concepts before committing them to canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagination - the faculty of forming new ideas or images in the mind
  • synonym: visualization - the process of forming a mental picture of something
