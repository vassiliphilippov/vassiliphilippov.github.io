The act or process of creating mental images or representations in the mind; visual imagery.

Examples

Picturing a scene in mind

Synonyms

  • visualization
    5.2 2.48/m
    visualization is a deliberate technique for forming clear mental images to help understanding or goals, while picturing is general visual imagery.
  • imagining
    3.5 1.50/m

  • envisioning
    5.1 0.04/m
