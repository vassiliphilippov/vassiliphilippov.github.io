pollbook

noun
voter registration list
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlˌbʊk / (BrE)
an official list of registered voters used at a polling place to verify eligibility to vote

Examples

Voter pollbook
Before casting her vote, the official checked her name in the pollbook.
Each polling station maintains a pollbook to ensure only registered voters participate.
The pollbook was updated to include new voters before the election.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ballot - a process of voting, in writing and typically in secret
  • synonym: register - an official list or record of names or items
