white baneberry

noun
North American plant with white berries
9.9
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈbeɪnˌbɛri / (AmE), / waɪt ˈbeɪnˌbəri / (BrE)
A North American herbaceous plant (Actaea pachypoda) bearing clusters of small, white, poisonous berries.

Examples

The white baneberry plant
Children should never touch the white baneberry plant because its berries are highly toxic.
Hikers often admire the striking appearance of the white baneberry in the forest understory.
The white baneberry is sometimes called 'doll's eyes' due to the appearance of its fruit.
