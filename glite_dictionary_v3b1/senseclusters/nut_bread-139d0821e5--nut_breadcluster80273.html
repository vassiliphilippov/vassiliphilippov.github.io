nut bread

noun
bread made with chopped nuts
4.5
Pronunciation: / nʌt brɛd / (AmE), / nʌt brɛd / (BrE)
A sweet or savory bread made with chopped nuts mixed into the dough or batter.

Examples

A slice of nut bread
Many people enjoy spreading butter on warm slices of nut bread.
She baked a fresh loaf of nut bread for breakfast.
The nut bread was moist and full of crunchy walnuts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruit bread - bread made with pieces of fruit or dried fruit
