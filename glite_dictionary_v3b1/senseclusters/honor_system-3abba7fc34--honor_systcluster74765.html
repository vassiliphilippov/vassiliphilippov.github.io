honor system

noun
system based on trust and honesty
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnər ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈɒnə ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system in which people are trusted to act honestly without external checks or supervision

Examples

The honor system works
The school cafeteria operates on the honor system, allowing students to take snacks and pay later.
The university relies on an honor system for take-home exams to ensure fairness.
Under the honor system, employees are expected to report their hours accurately.
