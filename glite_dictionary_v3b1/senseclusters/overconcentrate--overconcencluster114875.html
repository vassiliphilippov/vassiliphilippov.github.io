overconcentrate

verb
to focus excessively
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkɒnsəntreɪt / (BrE)
to focus attention, effort, or thought too much on something, often to the detriment of other aspects

Examples

To overconcentrate on problems
If you overconcentrate on your weaknesses, you may overlook your strengths.
Students sometimes overconcentrate on memorizing facts instead of understanding concepts.
Try not to overconcentrate on one task and neglect the others on your list.

  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
