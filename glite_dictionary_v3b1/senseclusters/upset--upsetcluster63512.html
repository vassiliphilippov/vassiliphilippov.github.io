upset

adjective
mildly physically distressed (esp. stomach)
2.8 1.9
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛt / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛt / (BrE)
(of the stomach or digestive system) experiencing mild physical discomfort, often involving nausea or digestive trouble.

Examples

Upset stomach
After eating the street food, he had an upset stomach all night.
Ginger tea is often used to soothe an upset stomach.
Her son stayed home from school due to an upset stomach.
