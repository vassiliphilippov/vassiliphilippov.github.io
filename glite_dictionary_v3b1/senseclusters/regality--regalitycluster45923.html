regality

noun
royal authority
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡæləti / (AmE), / rɪˈɡælɪti / (BrE)
the rights, powers, or authority belonging to a monarch or sovereign, or an area under such royal control

Examples

Regality of the king
The ancient castle was once the center of regality in the region.
He spoke with an air of regality that impressed everyone in the room.
During the ceremony, the symbols of regality were displayed for all to see.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monarchy - a form of government with a monarch at the head
  • synonym: sovereignty - supreme power or authority, especially of a state or ruler
  • antonym: subordination - the state of being under the authority or control of another
