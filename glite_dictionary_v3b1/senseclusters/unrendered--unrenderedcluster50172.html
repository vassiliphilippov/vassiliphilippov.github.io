unrendered

adjective
not plastered
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛndərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛndəd / (BrE)
not covered with a layer of plaster or cement on a wall or surface

Examples

Unrendered brick wall
The cottage had an unrendered brick exterior that gave it a rustic appearance.
They decided to leave one wall unrendered to showcase the original stonework.
The unrendered surface made the building look unfinished compared to its neighbors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or decorated; exposed
  • antonym: rendered - covered with a layer of plaster or cement
