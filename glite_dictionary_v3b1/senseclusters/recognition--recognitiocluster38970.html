recognition

noun
clear understanding or realization
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of becoming aware of or understanding something clearly and distinctly

Examples

Sudden recognition
After months of denial, she finally had a recognition of the truth.
His sudden recognition of the pattern helped solve the puzzle.
There has been growing recognition that climate change is an urgent problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • synonym: realization - the act of becoming fully aware of something as a fact
