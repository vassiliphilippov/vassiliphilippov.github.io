Mass card

noun
card stating a Mass will be said for the deceased
6.8
Pronunciation: / mæs kɑrd / (AmE), / mæs kɑːd / (BrE)
(in the Roman Catholic Church) a card sent to someone who has lost a loved one, stating that a Mass will be offered in memory of the deceased person.

Examples

A Mass card sent
Many people in the community signed the Mass card before it was delivered to the church.
She sent a Mass card to express her condolences to the bereaved family.
The family received several Mass cards after their grandmother passed away.
