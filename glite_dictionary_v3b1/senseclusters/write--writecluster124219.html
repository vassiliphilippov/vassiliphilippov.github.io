write

verb
contact by letter or written message
Pronunciation: / raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
to contact or communicate with (someone), especially by sending a letter or written message

Examples

To write a letter
He promised he would write me as soon as he settled in Paris.
Please write to your grandmother while you're away at college.
You can always write them if you have any questions or concerns.

Related Terms
