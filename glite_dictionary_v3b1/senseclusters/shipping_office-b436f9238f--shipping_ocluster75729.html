shipping office

noun
office for shipping agents and seamen hiring
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpɪŋ ˌɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpɪŋ ˌɒfɪs / (BrE)
an office where shipping agents conduct business and where seamen are hired for work on ships

Examples

The shipping office hires
After several days of searching, he finally found work through the shipping office at the harbor.
Every morning, sailors would line up outside the shipping office to look for available jobs.
The manager of the shipping office coordinated the hiring of crew members for the next voyage.
