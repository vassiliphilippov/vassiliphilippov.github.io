look-alike

noun
advertising audience similar to existing customers
4.4 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8l\u028ak \u0259\u02ccla\u026ak / (AmE), / \u02c8l\u028ak \u0259\u02ccla\u026ak / (BrE)
(in digital advertising) an audience segment created to include users who share key characteristics with a brand's existing customers, used to target similar potential customers.

By testing different look-alike settings, the team improved ad performance and reduced cost per conversion.
