fairy story

noun
implausible or deceptive story
3.0 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri ˌstɔri / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
an interesting but highly improbable story, often told as an excuse or to deceive

Examples

An unbelievable fairy story
He tried to get out of trouble with a complicated fairy story about lost documents.
No one believed his fairy story about why he was late to the meeting.
She dismissed the rumor as just another fairy story with no evidence.
