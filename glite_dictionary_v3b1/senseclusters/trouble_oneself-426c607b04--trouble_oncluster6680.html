trouble oneself

verb
make an effort to do something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌbəl wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˈtrʌbəl wʌnˈself / (BrE)
to make an effort to do something, especially when it is not strictly necessary; to take the trouble to do something

Examples
