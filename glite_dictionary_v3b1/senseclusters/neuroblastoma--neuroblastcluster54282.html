neuroblastoma

noun
malignant tumor of nerve cells (usually in children)
7.4 8.3
Pronunciation: /nʊr.oʊ.blæs.ˈtoʊ.mə/ /; / /nuːr.oʊ.blæs.ˈtoʊ.mə/ (AmE), /njuː.rəʊ.blæs.ˈtəʊ.mə/ /; / /njʊə.rəʊ.blæs.ˈtəʊ.mə/ (BrE)
A malignant tumor arising from embryonic nerve cells, most commonly occurring in children and often spreading rapidly to other parts of the body.

Examples

Child neuroblastoma tumor
Researchers are developing new therapies to improve survival rates for children with neuroblastoma.

Treatment for neuroblastoma often includes chemotherapy, surgery, and sometimes a bone marrow transplant.
