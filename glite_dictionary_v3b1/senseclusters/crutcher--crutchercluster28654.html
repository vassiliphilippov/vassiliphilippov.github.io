crutcher

verb
to help walk with crutches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌtʃər / (AmE), / ˈkrʌtʃə / (BrE)
to support or help someone walk by using or providing a crutch or crutches

Examples

To crutcher a patient
The nurse will crutcher him to the bench while the doctor examines the wound.
After his injury, friends would often crutcher him across the hallway at school.
Volunteers were needed to crutcher patients from the ambulance to the clinic entrance.

  • synonym: support - to help someone stand or move
