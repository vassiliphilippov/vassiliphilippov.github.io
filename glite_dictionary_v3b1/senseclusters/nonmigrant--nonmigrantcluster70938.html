nonmigrant

adjective
not migrating
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmaɪɡrənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrənt / (BrE)
not migrating or not moving from one place to another, especially remaining in the same area year-round

Examples

Nonmigrant birds stay
Many nonmigrant animals can be found in this forest throughout the entire year.
Unlike migratory species, nonmigrant birds do not travel long distances seasonally.
The study focused on the behavior of nonmigrant populations in changing climates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: migratory - moving from one place to another, especially seasonally
  • synonym: sedentary - staying in one place; not migrating
