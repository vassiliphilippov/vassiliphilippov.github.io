non-issue

noun
something no longer relevant or significant
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɪʃu / (AmE), / nɒnˈɪʃu / (BrE)
a topic or concern that is no longer relevant, significant, or worthy of discussion

Examples

A non-issue now
After the new policy was implemented, the old complaints became a non-issue.
He insisted that her past was a non-issue in their relationship.
With technology improvements, battery life is now a non-issue for most smartphones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irrelevance - the state of not being relevant
  • antonym: matter - a topic or issue under consideration
