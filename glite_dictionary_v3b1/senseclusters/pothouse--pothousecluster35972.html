pothouse

noun
disreputable tavern or bar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small, typically disreputable tavern or bar, often serving simple meals.

Examples

A pothouse tavern
He stumbled out of the pothouse as the sun was coming up.
The sailors frequented a dingy pothouse near the docks after work.
Years ago, that street was lined with every kind of pothouse imaginable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic beverages are served
  • synonym: pub - a place where alcoholic drinks are served; public house
