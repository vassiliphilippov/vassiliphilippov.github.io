arr

noun
abbreviation for arrival
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɑr / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'arrival', used on timetables or schedules to indicate the time something arrives

Examples

Arr time listed
On the airport monitor, the arr time for Flight 203 is 7:45 AM.
The bus schedule lists both arr and departure times for each stop.
Check the arr column to see when your train will reach the station.
