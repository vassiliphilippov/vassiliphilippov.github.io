Synonyms

  • meet halfway
    3.5 0.06/m
    meet halfway means each side gives up part of what they want to reach agreement, while compromise can be broader.
  • give and take
    3.6 0.2/m
    give and take means both people freely make mutual concessions in everyday life, while compromise often refers to settling a dispute.
  • split the difference
    4.1 0.3/m
    split the difference means taking the middle position, often on numbers, while compromise covers many kinds of give and take.
  • settle with
    4.1 0.9/m
    settle with means reach an agreement or resolve a claim with someone, while compromise emphasizes mutual concessions between sides.
  • settle for
    3.6 6/m
    settle for means accept something less than hoped, while compromise usually involves both sides giving up something to agree.
  • settle
    3.6 7/m
    settle means accept or agree to something, often to end a matter, while compromise stresses mutual giving by both sides.
