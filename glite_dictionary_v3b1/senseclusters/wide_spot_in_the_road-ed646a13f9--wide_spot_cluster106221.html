wide spot in the road

noun
a place beside a road to stop
4.3
Pronunciation: / waɪd spɑt ɪn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / waɪd spɒt ɪn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
a place along a road where the road briefly widens, allowing vehicles to pull over or stop temporarily

Examples
