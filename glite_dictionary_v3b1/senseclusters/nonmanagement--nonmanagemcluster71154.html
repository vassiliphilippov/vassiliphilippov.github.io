nonmanagement

adjective
not managerial
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
not relating to management; used to describe employees, roles, or activities that are not part of management.

Examples

Nonmanagement staff members
The company announced a pay raise for all nonmanagement staff this year.
Only nonmanagement employees are eligible for the new overtime policy.
Training sessions will be held separately for management and nonmanagement personnel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: managerial - relating to management or managers
  • synonym: staff - employees who are not part of management
