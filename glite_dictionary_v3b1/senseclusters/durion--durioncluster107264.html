durion

noun
tree with spiny edible fruit, Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊriən / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərɪən / (BrE)
A tree of Southeast Asia that bears edible oval fruit with a hard, spiny rind.

Examples

A durion tree
Several farmers now cultivate durion as a commercial crop in the region.
The durion tree is prized in Southeast Asia for its unique, spiny fruit.
The spiky durion fruits are harvested when they are fully ripe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: durian - tree and its large, spiny, strong-smelling fruit
