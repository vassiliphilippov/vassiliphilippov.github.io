Catholic Monarchs

proper noun
Ferdinand and Isabella, joint monarchs of Spain
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkæθ.ə.lɪk ˈmɑː.nərks/ /; / /ˈkæθ.lɪk ˈmɑː.nərks/ (AmE), /ˈkæθ.ə.lɪk ˈmɒn.əks/ /; / /ˈkɑː.θə.lɪk ˈmɒn.əks/ (BrE)
the title given to Ferdinand V of Aragon and Isabella I of Castile, whose marriage united Spain and who jointly ruled as monarchs in the late 15th and early 16th centuries
