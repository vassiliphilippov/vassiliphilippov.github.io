cutting onions

noun
crying or appearing to cry, often due to emotion
3.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌt.ɪŋ ˈʌn.jənz/ (AmE), /ˈkʌt.ɪŋ ˈʌn.jənz/ (BrE)
(informal) Used humorously to refer to someone appearing to cry or being moved to tears, especially when strong emotions are involved; originally referring to the literal act of cutting onions, which often causes eyes to water.

Examples

To cut onions
Don't mind me, I'm just cutting onions after seeing that heartwarming reunion video.
The movie's ending was so touching that it felt like someone was cutting onions in the room.
Whenever he talks about his late dog, you can tell he's not just cutting onions.
