ourselves

pronoun
reflexive 'we'
Pronunciation: / aʊɚˈsɛlvz / (AmE), / aʊəˈsɛlvz / (BrE)
the reflexive and emphatic form of 'we', used when the group including the speaker does something to or for themselves, or to emphasize that they did it alone

Examples

We did ourselves
We built the treehouse ourselves without any help from adults.
After the meeting, we congratulated ourselves on a job well done.
We should remind ourselves to stay positive during difficult times.
We ourselves will handle the arrangements, so you do not need to worry.
The teachers ourselves organized the event without any outside help.
We must ask ourselves if this is truly the right decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ours - possessive form of 'we'
  • synonym: themselves - reflexive form of 'they'
