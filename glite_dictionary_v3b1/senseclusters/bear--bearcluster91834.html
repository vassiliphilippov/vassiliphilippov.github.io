bear

noun
investor expecting falling prices
6.8
Pronunciation: / bɛr / (AmE), / beə / (BrE)
a person who expects prices on the stock market to fall and often sells shares with the intention of buying them back later at a lower price

Examples

A bear investor
In times of uncertainty, bear sentiment tends to dominate Wall Street.
Many bears have started selling off their portfolios ahead of the market downturn.
The bear predicts that stock prices will decline sharply over the next few months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bull - an investor who expects prices to rise and buys with the hope of selling at a higher price
