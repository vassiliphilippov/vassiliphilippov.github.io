representatively

adverb
in a typical way
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvli / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that serves as a typical example or reflects the characteristics of a group or whole

Examples

Representatively chosen members
The committee was chosen representatively to reflect the diversity of the community.
The results were gathered representatively from different regions to ensure fairness.
Each department was representatively included in the final decision-making process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atypically - in a way that is not typical or usual
  • synonym: typically - in a way that is usual or characteristic
