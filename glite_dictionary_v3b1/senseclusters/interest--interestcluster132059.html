interest

noun
curiosity or concern about something
3.2 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrəst /; / ˈɪntrəst, ˈɪntəˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈɪnt(ə)rɪst /; / ˈɪntrəst, ˈɪntəˌrɛst /; / ˈɪntrəst / (BrE)
the feeling of wanting to know more about something, or something that is engaging or enjoyable

Examples
