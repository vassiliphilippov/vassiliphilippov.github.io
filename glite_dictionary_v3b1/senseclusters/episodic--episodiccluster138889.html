Synonyms

  • intermittent
    6.5 3/m
    intermittent means happening at irregular times and stopping between, while episodic means appearing in separate, infrequent episodes.
  • sporadic
    7.0 4/m
    sporadic comes in scattered, rare occurrences across time or place, while episodic refers to events that come in separate episodes.
  • off-and-on
    3.8 0.2/m
    off-and-on happens by stopping and starting in everyday speech, while episodic describes distinct, infrequent episodes.
  • irregular
    3.5 2/m
    irregular does not follow a fixed pattern or rhythm, while episodic specifically means occurring in separate, infrequent episodes.
  • nonperiodic
    7.6 0.02/m
    nonperiodic lacks regular recurrence in time and sounds formal, while episodic suggests occasional, discrete events people notice.
  • aperiodic
    8.2 0.04/m
    aperiodic describes a technical lack of periodic cycle, while episodic focuses on occasional, separate events people experience.
  • noncyclical
    7.2 0.01/m
    noncyclical says something does not follow a repeating cycle, while episodic highlights isolated, irregular occurrences or episodes.
