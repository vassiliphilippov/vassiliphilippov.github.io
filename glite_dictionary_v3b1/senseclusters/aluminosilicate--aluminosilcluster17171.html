aluminosilicate

noun
aluminum-silicon compound
8.5
Pronunciation: / əˌluːmənoʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt / (AmE), / əˌljuːmɪnəʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt / (BrE)
a compound made of aluminum, silicon, and oxygen, commonly found in minerals and used in ceramics and glass

Examples

A aluminosilicate mineral
The laboratory analyzed the aluminosilicate content in the rock sample.
Many ceramics are strengthened by adding aluminosilicate to the mixture.
Glass manufacturers often use aluminosilicate for its durability and heat resistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feldspar - a group of aluminosilicate minerals found in rocks
  • synonym: zeolite - a type of aluminosilicate mineral used as a catalyst or filter
