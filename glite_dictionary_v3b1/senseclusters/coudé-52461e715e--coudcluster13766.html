coudé

adjective
bent or curved
Pronunciation: / kuːˈdeɪ / (AmE), / kuːˈdeɪ / (BrE)
bent or curved, especially referring to a medical instrument with an angled tip

Examples

Coudé catheter tip
The doctor chose a coudé catheter to help navigate the patient's urethra.
A coudé tip is often used when a standard straight catheter cannot pass easily.
Medical staff are trained to recognize when a coudé instrument is necessary for certain procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curved - having a smoothly bending shape
  • antonym: straight - not bent or curved
