conflictual

adjective
involving conflict
8.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈflɪktʃuəl / (AmE), / kənˈflɪktʃuəl / (BrE)
involving conflict, disagreement, or tension (between people, groups, or within a person)

Examples

Conflictual situation

The negotiations became increasingly conflictual as both sides refused to compromise.
She felt conflictual emotions about moving to a new city for her job.
A conflictual relationship with his parents made his teenage years difficult.
