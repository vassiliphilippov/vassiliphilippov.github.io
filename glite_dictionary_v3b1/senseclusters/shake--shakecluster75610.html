Examples

Synonyms

  • vibration
    3.2 7.26/m
    a vibration is a rapid, often continuous back-and-forth movement in objects, while a shake is any brief or broad motion.
  • jiggle
    3.9 0.34/m
    a jiggle is a small, irregular wobble in light objects, while a shake is a larger or more forceful movement.
  • quivering
    4.4 0.53/m
    quivering is a slight, quick trembling of small parts or voice, while a shake is a wider, stronger motion.
  • tremor
    5.3 2.42/m
    a tremor is an involuntary, often medical shaking of body parts, while a shake can be voluntary or general motion.
  • tremble
    2.7 2.65/m
    a tremble is a sudden body quiver from cold, fear, or strong feeling, while a shake is any back-and-forth movement.
  • shiver
    1.4 4.33/m
    a shiver is an involuntary trembling from cold or fear that runs through the body, while a shake is any shaking movement.
