shake

noun
trembling or shivering motion
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
a trembling, shivering, or repeated back-and-forth motion; the act or result of shaking

Examples

A sudden shake
A sudden shake ran through his body when he stepped into the icy water.
He always gets a little shake in his hands when he's excited about something.
She tried to hide her shake of fear as she spoke to the audience.
After the earthquake, the shake of the building made everyone nervous.
He gave the jar a good shake to mix the ingredients.
She could feel a slight shake in her hands from excitement.

  • synonym: shudder - a sudden, strong movement, often from fear or cold
  • synonym: tremor - an involuntary shaking movement
