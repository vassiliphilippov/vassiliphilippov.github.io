misdistribution

noun
unequal allocation
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
the incorrect or unfair allocation of something among people or groups

Examples

Misdistribution of supplies
The misdistribution of medical supplies caused shortages in rural areas.
Critics argue that misdistribution of wealth is a major cause of social unrest.
Government reforms aim to correct the misdistribution of educational resources.
