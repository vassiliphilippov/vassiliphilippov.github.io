treatability

noun
ability to be treated
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtriːtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌtriːtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be treated, especially by medical or other interventions

Examples

Treatability of cancer
The treatability of certain cancers has improved with advances in medicine.
Researchers are studying the treatability of rare genetic disorders.
Doctors must assess the treatability of a condition before recommending a course of action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curability - the ability of a disease or condition to be cured
  • antonym: incurability - the state of not being able to be cured or treated
