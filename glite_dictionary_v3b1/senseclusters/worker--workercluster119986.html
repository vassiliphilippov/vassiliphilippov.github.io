worker

noun
person who gets things done
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkər /; / ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who is employed to do a job or who gets things done, especially involving physical or routine tasks

Examples

Factory worker
Instead of waiting for things to happen, he is a proactive worker who makes things happen.
Sarah is a true worker; she always follows through on her promises.
We need a worker like John on this project to keep everything moving forward.
As a construction worker, he often starts his day at sunrise.
She is a reliable worker who always finishes her tasks on time.
The factory hired several new workers this month to increase production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doer - a person who actively does things rather than just planning or thinking
  • antonym: dreamer - a person who spends much time thinking or dreaming rather than taking action
  • synonym: employee - a person employed for wages or salary
  • antonym: employer - a person or organization that hires workers
