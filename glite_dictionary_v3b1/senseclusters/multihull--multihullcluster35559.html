multihull

noun
boat with multiple hulls
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌhʌl / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌhʌl / (BrE)
a boat or ship with more than one hull, such as a catamaran or trimaran

Examples

A multihull boat
The new multihull design allows for greater speed and stability on the water.
Sailors often choose a multihull for long ocean voyages because of its comfort.
During the race, the multihull easily outpaced the traditional boats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catamaran - a boat with two parallel hulls
  • antonym: monohull - a boat with a single hull
