committee

noun
group appointed for a specific task
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪti / (AmE), / kəˈmɪti / (BrE)
a group of people chosen or formed to discuss, manage, or deal with a specific issue or activity

Examples

A committee meeting
A special committee was created to investigate the incident.
She serves on the safety committee at her workplace.
The school formed a committee to plan the annual fundraising event.
Each committee meets monthly to review progress and set new goals.
He volunteered to serve on the fundraising committee for the charity event.
The school formed a committee to organize the annual science fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: board - a group of persons having managerial or advisory duties
  • synonym: commission - an official group given a particular task or responsibility
  • synonym: panel - a small group of people brought together to discuss or judge something
