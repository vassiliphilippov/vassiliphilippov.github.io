cheek by jowl

adverb
very close together
Pronunciation: / tʃik baɪ dʒaʊl / (AmE), / tʃiːk baɪ dʒəʊl / (BrE)
Very close together; in close proximity.

Examples

Cheek by jowl together
In the old city, shops and homes stand cheek by jowl along narrow streets.
The commuters were packed cheek by jowl on the morning train.
Wildlife and industry exist cheek by jowl in this rapidly growing region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: side by side - directly next to each other
