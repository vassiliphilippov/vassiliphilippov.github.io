opinionize on

verb
express opinions about something
7.0
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪnjəˌnaɪz ɑn / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjəˌnaɪz ɒn / (BrE)
to express personal opinions about a particular subject, often in a deliberate or overt manner

Examples

To opinionize on politics
He was invited to opinionize on current events during the radio show.
It's easy to opinionize on social issues from the comfort of your home.
She tends to opinionize on topics she knows little about.

Related Terms
