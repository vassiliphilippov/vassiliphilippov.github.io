darkness

noun
absence of moral or spiritual values
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrknəs / (AmE), / ˈdɑːknəs / (BrE)
A state or quality of lacking moral or spiritual values; wickedness or evil.

Examples

Inner darkness
He struggled to resist the darkness tempting him away from virtue.
Many feared the growing darkness in society as corruption spread.
The story explores the darkness that can reside in the human heart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evil - profound immorality or wickedness
  • antonym: goodness - the quality of being morally good or virtuous
