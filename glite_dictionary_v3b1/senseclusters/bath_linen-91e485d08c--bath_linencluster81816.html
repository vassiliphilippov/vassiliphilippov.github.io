bath linen

noun
towels and linens for bathroom use
1.8
Pronunciation: / bæθ ˈlɪnən / (AmE), / bɑːθ ˈlɪnən / (BrE)
towels, washcloths, and other linens used in the bathroom for drying or cleaning oneself

Examples

Soft bath linen
After a long soak, she wrapped herself in soft bath linen.
The cupboard was stacked with neatly folded bath linen.
The hotel provides fresh bath linen every day for all guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: table linen - linens used for dining tables such as tablecloths and napkins
  • synonym: towel - a piece of absorbent cloth or paper used for drying
