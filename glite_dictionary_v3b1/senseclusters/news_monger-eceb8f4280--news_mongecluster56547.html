news monger

noun
person who spreads news or rumors
Pronunciation: / ˈnuz ˌmɑŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːz ˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who habitually spreads news or rumors, often indiscriminately

Examples

A news monger spreads stories
In a small town, a news monger can make even minor events seem important.
She disliked being labeled as a news monger, insisting she simply kept people informed.
The news monger quickly shared every detail of the incident with anyone who would listen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gossip - a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others
  • synonym: rumormonger - a person who spreads rumors
