bluish tint

noun
slight blue coloration
Pronunciation: / ˈbluː.ɪʃ tɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbluː.ɪʃ tɪnt / (BrE)
a slight shade or hint of blue in the color of something

Examples

A bluish tint on glass
His new glasses have a subtle bluish tint to help with computer screens.
The evening sky showed a bluish tint as the sun set behind the buildings.
The glass windows had a bluish tint that reduced the glare from the sun.
