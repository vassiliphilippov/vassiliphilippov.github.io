graphics

noun
visual representations in media
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪks /; / ˈɡræf.ɪks / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪks /; / ˈɡraf.ɪks / (BrE)
photographs, drawings, diagrams, or other visual images used in digital or printed media

Examples

Computer graphics
Modern video games use highly advanced graphics to create realistic worlds.
She designed the graphics for the marketing brochure.
The graphics in this textbook make the scientific concepts much easier to understand.
Magazines often use colorful graphics to attract readers' attention.
The designer spent hours choosing the right graphics for the children's book.
The layout combines both text and graphics to make the information clearer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artwork - the art or illustrations in a publication or product
  • synonym: illustration - a visual representation used to clarify or decorate text
  • antonym: text - written words in a publication, as opposed to images
