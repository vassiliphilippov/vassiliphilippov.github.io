high-pressure job

noun
stressful or demanding job
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ ˈprɛʃɚ dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ ˈprɛʃə dʒɒb / (BrE)
a job that involves a lot of stress or intense demands

Examples

A high-pressure job role
After years in a high-pressure job, she decided to pursue a less stressful career.
He quit his high-pressure job to find a better work-life balance.
Working in finance can be a high-pressure job with strict deadlines and high expectations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low-pressure job - a job that is not stressful or demanding
  • synonym: stressful job - a job that causes stress
