shmooze

noun
friendly informal chat
8.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: /  ÉmuÀêz / (AmE), /  ÉmuÀêz / (BrE)
an informal and friendly chat, often intended to gain favor or build a relationship

Examples

A friendly shmooze
He always likes a shmooze with old friends when he visits.
The event was more than just business; it was a chance for a good shmooze.
We enjoyed a pleasant shmooze over coffee after the meeting.

Synonyms

    8.7 0.11/m

  • jawboning
    8.2 0.01/m

  • chin-wag
    8.4 0.00/m

  • small talk
