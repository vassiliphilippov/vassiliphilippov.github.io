counternotice

noun
legal response notice
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.tər.noʊ.tɪs/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.tər.noʊ.təs/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.tə.nəʊ.tɪs/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.tə.nəʊ.təs/ (BrE)
a formal written response to a legal notice, especially to dispute or challenge a claim such as a copyright takedown request

Examples

File a counternotice
After receiving a takedown request, the website submitted a counternotice to restore the removed content.
Filing a counternotice is an important step in challenging a copyright claim.
The artist's lawyer prepared a counternotice to dispute the allegations of infringement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: notice - an official written communication or warning
