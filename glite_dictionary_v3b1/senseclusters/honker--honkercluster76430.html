honker

noun
loud honking sound
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑŋkər / (AmE), / ˈhɒŋkə / (BrE)
a loud honking sound, especially one made by a goose or a car horn

Examples

A loud honker
A loud honker startled the children as the truck drove by.
We could hear the honker of geese flying overhead early in the morning.
The driver pressed the honker to warn the cyclist in the street.
