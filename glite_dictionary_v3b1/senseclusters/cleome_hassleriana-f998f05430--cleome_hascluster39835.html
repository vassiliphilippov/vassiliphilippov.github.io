Cleome hassleriana

noun
ornamental plant with spider-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / kliˈoʊmi hɑːsləˈriːənə / (AmE), / kliːˈəʊmiː hasləˈriːənə / (BrE)
A plant native to South America, cultivated for its long-lasting spider-shaped flowers that range from white to pink-purple, commonly known as spider flower.
