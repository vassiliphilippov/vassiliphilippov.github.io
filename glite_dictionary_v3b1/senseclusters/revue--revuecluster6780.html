revue

noun
theatrical show with sketches and songs
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a type of theatrical entertainment featuring a series of short sketches, songs, dances, and sometimes comedy, often with a topical or satirical theme

Examples

A revue show
The comedians in the revue had the audience laughing all night.
The theater is putting on a revue that lampoons recent political events.
We attended a musical revue featuring songs from classic Broadway shows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: variety show - a show with many different short performances of music, dance, and comedy
