poche

noun
small recess or pocket
Pronunciation: / poʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / pɒˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a small pocket or recessed space, especially in architecture or music, often used to conceal or contain something

Examples

Hidden poche space
The architect designed a poche to conceal the structural column.
In classical music, a poche can refer to a small section set aside for a specific instrument.
The wall's poche provided a hidden space for storage without disrupting the room's design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: niche - a shallow recess in a wall
