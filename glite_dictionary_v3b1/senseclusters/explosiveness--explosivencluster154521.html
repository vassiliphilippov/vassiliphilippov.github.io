explosiveness

noun
sudden force or energy
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being sudden, forceful, or energetic, like an explosion

Examples

The explosiveness of the blast
The explosiveness of the volcano surprised the nearby villagers.
Her explosiveness on the basketball court made her a valuable player.
The speech was delivered with such explosiveness that it energized the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being peaceful and not showing strong emotion or energy
  • synonym: intensity - the quality of being very strong or extreme
