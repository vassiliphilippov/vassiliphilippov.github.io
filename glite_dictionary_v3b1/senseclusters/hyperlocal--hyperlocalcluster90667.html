hyperlocal

noun
local news outlet
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈloʊkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈləʊkəl / (BrE)
a newspaper, website, or service that provides news or information focused on a very small local area or community

Examples

A hyperlocal website
The new hyperlocal covers only the events happening in our neighborhood.
Many people rely on a hyperlocal for updates about local schools and businesses.
A hyperlocal can help small communities stay informed about important issues.
