forgetter

noun
one who makes others forget
8.8
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛtər / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a person who causes someone else to forget something, especially through influence or action

Examples

A skilled forgetter
The skilled interrogator acted as a forgetter, making witnesses question their own memories.
In the story, the magician was known as a forgetter who could erase memories with a word.
Some believe that time itself is the greatest forgetter of all our troubles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influencer - a person who affects the thoughts or actions of others
  • antonym: reminder - a person or thing that helps someone remember
