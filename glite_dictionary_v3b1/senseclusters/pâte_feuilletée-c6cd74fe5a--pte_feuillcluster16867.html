pâte feuilletée

noun
flaky, buttery pastry dough
4.7
Pronunciation: / pɑt fœj.teɪ / (AmE), / pɑːt fœj.teɪ / (BrE)
a rich, light, flaky pastry dough made with layers of butter and dough, used for pastries such as croissants and mille-feuille

Examples

Classic pate feuilletee pastry
Classic French desserts like mille-feuille start with high-quality pâte feuilletée.
The chef rolled out the pâte feuilletée carefully to achieve perfect layers in her pastry.
You can buy ready-made pâte feuilletée from gourmet food stores.
