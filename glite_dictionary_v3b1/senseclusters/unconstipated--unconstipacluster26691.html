unconstipated

adjective
not constipated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkɑːnstəˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒnstɪˌpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not suffering from constipation; having regular bowel movements.

Examples

Unconstipated patient
Doctors were pleased to hear that their patient was unconstipated following the surgery.
Since changing her diet, she has remained unconstipated for several weeks.
The medication helped him feel unconstipated after days of discomfort.
