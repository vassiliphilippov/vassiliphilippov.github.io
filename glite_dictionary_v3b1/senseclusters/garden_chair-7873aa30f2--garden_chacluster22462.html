garden chair

noun
outdoor chair for gardens or lawns
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn̩ tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn̩ tʃɛə / (BrE)
a chair designed to be used outdoors, typically in a garden or on a lawn

Examples

A garden chair outside
After the rain stopped, he wiped the garden chair and sat outside.
She enjoyed reading in her garden chair on sunny afternoons.
The wooden garden chair needed a fresh coat of paint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deck chair - a folding chair, typically with a canvas seat, used outdoors
  • synonym: lawn chair - a folding chair for outdoor use, often on a lawn
