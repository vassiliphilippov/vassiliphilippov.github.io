forty-fourth

adjective
ordinal number: number 44th
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)

Examples

This is the forty-fourth anniversary of their founding.
