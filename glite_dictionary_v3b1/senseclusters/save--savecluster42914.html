save

verb
prevent need for expense or effort
Pronunciation: / seɪv / (AmE), / seɪv / (BrE)
to prevent the need for spending time, money, or effort; to make something unnecessary

Examples

To save time
Arriving early can save you the trouble of waiting in a long line.
Buying in bulk can save you a significant amount of money over time.
This shortcut will save you from having to repeat the entire process.
Organizing your files now will save you time later when you need to find something important.
These tips could save you the hassle of dealing with last-minute travel problems.
Using public transport can save you a lot of money on gas and parking.
