Synonyms

  • downslope
    3.7 0.57/m
    downslope names the lower side of a hill you can walk down, while declivity is a more formal word for that slope.
  • decline
    3.5 0.95/m
    decline refers to a slope or fall often used for land or rates, while declivity focuses on a hill's downward slope.
  • descent
    3.7 8.23/m
    descent describes the act of going down or a sloping path, while declivity names the slope itself on a hill.
  • declination
    9.1 0.04/m
    declination describes a tilt or slope used in maps or instruments, while declivity means a hill's downward slope.
  • declension
    8.8 0.00/m
    declension can mean a bend or slope but is mainly a grammar term, while declivity strictly means a downward slope.
