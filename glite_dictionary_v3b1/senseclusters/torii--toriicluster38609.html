torii

noun
Japanese shrine gate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːriˌi / (AmE), / ˈtɔːriˌi / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese gate marking the entrance to a Shinto shrine, symbolizing the transition from the ordinary to the sacred

Examples

A red torii gate
A bright red torii stood at the entrance to the peaceful Shinto shrine.
Tourists often take photos under the famous torii at Itsukushima Island.
Passing through the torii, visitors feel a sense of respect for the sacred space beyond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy, often dedicated to a deity
