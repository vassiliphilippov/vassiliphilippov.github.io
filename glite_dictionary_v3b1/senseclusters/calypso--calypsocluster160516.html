calypso

noun
a rare orchid with a single pink-white flower
7.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪpsoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlɪpsəʊ / (BrE)
a rare north temperate orchid (Calypso bulbosa) that bears a single white to pink flower, often marked with purple, on a reddish stalk above a single basal leaf

Examples

Wild calypso orchid
Because of its rarity and beauty, the calypso is protected in some areas.
The delicate calypso is a favorite among wildflower enthusiasts in North American forests.
We spotted a single blooming calypso tucked among the moss in the bog.
