Great Depression

noun
worldwide economic crisis of the 1930s
Pronunciation: / ˌɡreɪt dɪˈprɛʃən /; / ɡreɪt dɪˈprɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt dɪˈprɛʃən /; / ɡreɪt dɪˈprɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
