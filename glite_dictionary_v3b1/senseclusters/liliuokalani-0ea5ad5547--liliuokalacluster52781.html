Liliuokalani

proper noun
last queen of Hawaii (1838–1917)
9.9
Pronunciation: / liˌliuːoʊkəˈlɑːni /; / liːˌliːuːˌoʊkoʊˈlɑːni / (AmE), / liˌliːuːəʊkəˈlɑːni /; / liːˌliːuːˌɒkəˈlɑːni / (BrE)
(1838–1917) The last reigning queen of the Hawaiian Islands, known for her efforts to preserve Hawaiian independence and culture, and as a symbol of national identity and resistance.
