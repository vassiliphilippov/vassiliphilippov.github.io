Liliuokalani

proper noun
last queen of Hawaii (1838–1917)

Pronunciation: / liˌliuːoʊkəˈlɑːni /; / liːˌliːuːˌoʊkoʊˈlɑːni / (AmE), / liˌliːuːəʊkəˈlɑːni /; / liːˌliːuːˌɒkəˈlɑːni / (BrE)
