spent grain

noun
residue of malt/grains after brewing
Pronunciation: / spɛnt ɡreɪn / (AmE), / spɛnt ɡreɪn / (BrE)
the residue of malt and grains left after mashing and lautering during brewing, often used as animal feed.

Examples

The spent grain residue
After the brewing process, large quantities of spent grain are left behind.
Researchers are exploring new ways to use spent grain in sustainable packaging materials.
The brewery donates its spent grain to local farmers for use as cattle feed.
