multipole

adjective
with multiple poles
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltipoʊl / (AmE), / ˈmʌltipəʊl / (BrE)
having or consisting of more than two poles, especially in devices, fields, or systems involving electricity or magnetism

Examples

Multipole switch
The engineer recommended a multipole switch for the complex electrical system.
A multipole antenna can transmit and receive signals in several directions.
They studied the multipole expansion of the electric field in their physics class.

Related Terms
