mushroomer

noun
wild mushroom gatherer
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃˌruːmər / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃˌruːmə / (BrE)
a person who gathers or hunts for wild mushrooms, especially as a hobby or for food

Examples

Forest mushroomer
The experienced mushroomer could identify dozens of edible fungi in the forest.
Every autumn, local mushroomers gather in the woods to search for rare species.
As a beginner mushroomer, she always double-checked her finds with a guidebook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forager - a person who searches for wild food resources
  • synonym: hunter-gatherer - a person who lives by hunting animals and gathering plants
