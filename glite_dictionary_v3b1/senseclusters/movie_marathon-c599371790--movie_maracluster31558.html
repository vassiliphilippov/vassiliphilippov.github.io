movie marathon

noun
watching several movies in succession
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːvi ˈmærəˌθɑn / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˈmærəˌθɒn / (BrE)
an event or session in which several movies are watched one after another without significant breaks

Examples

A movie marathon night
After bringing home the trilogy, we planned a Saturday movie marathon.
Every year, my friends and I have a movie marathon to celebrate Halloween.
Rainy days are perfect for a cozy movie marathon with family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binge-watch - to watch multiple media works in rapid succession
  • synonym: film marathon - an event of watching several films in succession
