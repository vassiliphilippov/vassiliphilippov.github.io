cot-quean

noun
man who meddles in domestic tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑtˌkwin / (AmE), / ˈkɒtˌkwiːn / (BrE)
A man who is excessively concerned with domestic duties or who takes on domestic tasks, typically considered the domain of women.

Examples

The cot-quean man
In the old play, the cot-quean character caused much amusement for the audience.
Some considered him a cot-quean because he spent so much time in the kitchen.
Unlike his brothers, John was proud to be a cot-quean and handle the household chores.
