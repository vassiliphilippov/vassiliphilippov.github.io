provocative

adjective
intended to arouse sexual desire
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɑːkətɪv / (AmE), / prəˈvɒkətɪv / (BrE)
intended or likely to arouse sexual desire or interest, especially by being sexually suggestive or alluring.

Examples

Provocative outfit
His comments about her provocative behavior made her uncomfortable.
She wore a provocative dress to the party, catching everyone's attention.
The advertisement featured a provocative image that sparked controversy for being too suggestive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alluring - powerfully attractive or tempting; seductive
  • antonym: modest - not intended to attract attention, especially of a sexual nature
