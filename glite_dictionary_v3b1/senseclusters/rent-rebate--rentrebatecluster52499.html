rent-rebate

noun
partial refund on rent, often from government
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛnt riˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrent riˌbeɪt / (BrE)
A partial refund or deduction on rent, usually provided by a local government authority to help eligible tenants with housing costs.

Examples

A rent-rebate payment
After losing her job, she applied for a rent-rebate from the local council.
The government introduced a new rent-rebate scheme to assist low-income families.
To qualify for a rent-rebate, you must submit proof of your current income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebate - A partial refund to someone who has paid too much for tax, rent, etc.
