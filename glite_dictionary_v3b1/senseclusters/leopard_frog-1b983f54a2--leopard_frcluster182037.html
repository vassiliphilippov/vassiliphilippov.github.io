leopard frog

noun
a North American frog with dark oval spots
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛpərd frɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəd frɒɡ / (BrE)
a common North American green or brownish frog with white-edged dark oval spots on its body

Examples

The leopard frog jumps
Children love watching leopard frogs leap into the pond during the spring.
Many leopard frogs have distinctive white lines running along their backs and legs.
The scientist carefully observed the leopard frog in its natural wetland habitat.
