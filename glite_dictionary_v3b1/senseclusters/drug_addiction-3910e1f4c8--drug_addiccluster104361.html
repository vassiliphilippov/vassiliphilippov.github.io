drug addiction

noun
compulsive dependence on drugs
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ əˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / drʌɡ əˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
a chronic condition where a person cannot stop using drugs, even though it causes harm

Examples

Drug addiction treatment
Effective treatment programs are essential for those suffering from drug addiction.
Many people struggle for years to overcome drug addiction and rebuild their lives.
The documentary explores the causes and consequences of drug addiction in small communities.
Drug addiction places a heavy burden on families and health care systems worldwide.
His life changed dramatically after he overcame drug addiction.
Many people struggling with drug addiction require professional help to recover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addiction - the state of being addicted to something
  • synonym: drug abuse - the habitual taking of addictive or illegal drugs
  • synonym: drug dependence - state resulting from habitual drug use, marked by withdrawal symptoms
  • antonym: sobriety - the state of not being under the influence of drugs or alcohol
  • synonym: substance abuse - excessive use of addictive substances such as drugs or alcohol
