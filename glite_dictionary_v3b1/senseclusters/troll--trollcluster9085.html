troll

noun
online troublemaker
Pronunciation: / troʊl / (AmE), / trəʊl / (BrE)
a person who deliberately tries to upset or provoke others online by posting offensive or disruptive messages

Examples

An internet troll
The forum had to ban a persistent troll who kept starting arguments with other users.
Ignoring a troll is often the best way to stop them from causing trouble online.
She reported the troll to the website administrators after receiving several rude messages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyberbully - a person who uses the internet to harass or intimidate others
  • antonym: moderator - a person who oversees online discussions and enforces rules
