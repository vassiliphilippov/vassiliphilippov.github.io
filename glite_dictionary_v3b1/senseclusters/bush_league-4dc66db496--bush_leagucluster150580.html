bush league

noun
minor sports league; inferior category
8.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / b ä É li…° / (AmE), / b ä É liÀê…° / (BrE)
(in North American sports, especially baseball) a minor league or a league of teams not regarded as major league; also used figuratively to describe something as unsophisticated or of lower quality.

Examples

A bush league team
He spent his first few years playing in the bush league before making it to the majors.
Some critics dismissed the new team as strictly bush league in their performance.
Using outdated equipment like that is really bush league for a professional organization.
