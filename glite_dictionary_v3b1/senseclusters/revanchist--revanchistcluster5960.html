revanchist

adjective
seeking lost territory
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑːntʃɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈvɑːnʃɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to a policy of seeking to recover lost territory or status, often through aggressive means

Examples

Revanchist policy
The leader's revanchist ambitions worried neighboring countries and international observers.
A revanchist attitude can lead to renewed conflicts over disputed borders.
Some politicians adopted a revanchist stance after the loss of the territory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irredentist - supporting the recovery of territory considered lost or historically belonging to one's nation
  • antonym: pacifist - opposed to war or violence as a means of settling disputes
