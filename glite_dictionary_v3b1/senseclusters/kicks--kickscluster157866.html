kicks

noun
informal excitement
Pronunciation: / kɪks / (AmE), / kɪks / (BrE)
informal enjoyment, excitement, or pleasure that someone gets from an activity or experience

Examples

Kicks from adventure
She gets her kicks from trying new and adventurous foods.
Some people find kicks in extreme sports like skydiving or bungee jumping.
He joined the club just for the kicks and to meet new people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boredom - the state of being bored or lacking excitement
  • synonym: thrill - a sudden feeling of excitement and pleasure
