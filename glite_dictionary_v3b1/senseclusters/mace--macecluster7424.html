mace

verb
to spray with Mace
Pronunciation: / meɪs / (AmE), / meɪs / (BrE)
to attack or spray someone with Mace, a chemical irritant used for self-defense

Examples

To mace an attacker
The security guard maced the intruder when he refused to leave the building.
During the altercation, one of the participants was maced by the police.
She carries a can of spray in case she needs to mace an attacker.
The woman maced her attacker to escape.
Security guards are trained not to mace people unless absolutely necessary.
He accidentally maced himself while trying to use the spray.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - to use force against someone
  • synonym: pepper spray - a chemical spray used for self-defense that irritates the eyes
