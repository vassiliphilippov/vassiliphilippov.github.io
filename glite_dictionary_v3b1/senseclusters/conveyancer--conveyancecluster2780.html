conveyancer

noun
lawyer specializing in property transfers
Pronunciation: / kənˈveɪ.ənsər / (AmE), / kənˈveɪ.ənsə / (BrE)
a lawyer or legal professional who specializes in the transfer of property ownership

Examples

Experienced conveyancer
Every reputable real estate firm works closely with an experienced conveyancer.
She hired a conveyancer to ensure the property transfer went smoothly.
The conveyancer prepared all the legal documents for the sale of the house.
