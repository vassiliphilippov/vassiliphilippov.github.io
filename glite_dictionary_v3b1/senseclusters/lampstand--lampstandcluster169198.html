lampstand

noun
lamp holder
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmpˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈlæmpˌstænd / (BrE)
a stand or holder for a lamp or candles, sometimes used in religious or ceremonial settings

Examples

A lampstand holder
The ornate lampstand in the living room was made of polished brass.
She bought a new lampstand to match the modern decor of her bedroom.
He accidentally knocked over the lampstand while cleaning the corner.
The ornate lampstand in the temple was made of pure gold.
She placed a small oil lamp on the antique lampstand by the window.
During the ceremony, the priest lit each branch of the lampstand in turn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candelabrum - a large branched holder for candles
  • synonym: lamp base - the bottom part of a lamp that provides support
  • synonym: menorah - a seven-branched lampstand used in Jewish tradition
  • antonym: table - a piece of furniture with a flat top and legs
