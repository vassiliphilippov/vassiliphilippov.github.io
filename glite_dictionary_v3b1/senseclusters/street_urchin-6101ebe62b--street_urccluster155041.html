street urchin

noun
a homeless or neglected child living on the streets
9.3 6.1
Pronunciation: /striːt ˈɜːr.tʃən/ /; / /striːt ˈɜːr.tʃɪn/ (AmE), /striːt ˈɜː.tʃən/ /; / /striːt ˈɜː.tʃɪn/ (BrE)
