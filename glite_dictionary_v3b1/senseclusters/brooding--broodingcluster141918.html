brooding

noun
persistent gloomy meditation; deep reflection
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbruːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a state of dwelling persistently and gloomily on a problem or difficulty; deep, somber, and often morbid reflection.

Examples

Endless brooding
His constant brooding over lost opportunities kept him from moving forward.
Prolonged brooding about your mistakes won't help you solve them.
You could sense a mood of brooding in the room after the bad news was announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: musing - a period of reflection or thought
  • synonym: rumination - deep or considered thought about something, often persistent
