citation form

noun
standard dictionary form of a word
9.2
Pronunciation: / saɪˈteɪʃən fɔrm / (AmE), / saɪˈteɪʃən fɔːm / (BrE)
the standard form of a word used as the headword in dictionaries and lexical entries

Examples

The citation form of a word
In many dictionaries, verbs are listed in their citation form, which is typically the infinitive.
Language learners are often advised to memorize words in their citation form.
The citation form of 'ran' is 'run'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemma - the canonical form of a word, as used in dictionaries
