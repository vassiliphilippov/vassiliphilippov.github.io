Synonyms

  • chemical reaction
    3.1 2.16/m
    a chemical reaction is a specific chemistry process where substances form new compounds, while chemical change names that transformation generally.
  • reaction
    3.0 10/m
    a reaction is any process in which substances undergo chemical transformation, while chemical change emphasises the alteration of atomic or molecular structure.
  • chemical process
    3.5 0.40/m
    a chemical process is a series of steps changing composition at molecular level, while chemical change focuses on the final transformation.
  • chemical action
    3.5 0.01/m
    a chemical action is an often older or less common phrase for chemical activity in reactions, while chemical change names the resulting transformation.
