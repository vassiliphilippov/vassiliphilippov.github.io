rightward

adverb
toward conservatism
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtwərd / (AmE), / ˈraɪtwəd / (BrE)
toward a more conservative or right-wing political position or policy

Examples

Rightward political shift
The party shifted rightward after the election, focusing on stricter economic policies.
Public opinion has moved rightward in response to recent security concerns.
Many voters noticed a rightward trend in the government's new legislation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leftward - toward a more liberal or left-wing political position
