mental measurement

noun
quantification of mental abilities or functions
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.təl ˈmɛʒ.ər.mənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl ˈmɛʒ.ə.mənt / (BrE)
the process or technique of quantifying mental functions or abilities, such as intelligence, memory, or aptitude, using standardized tests or assessments
