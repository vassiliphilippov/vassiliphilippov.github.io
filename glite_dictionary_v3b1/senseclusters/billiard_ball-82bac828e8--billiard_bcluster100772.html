billiard ball

noun
ball used in billiards
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪljɚd bɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəd bɔːl / (BrE)
a hard ball used to play billiards, typically made of resin or similar material

Examples

A billiard ball set
Each billiard ball is polished before the start of a major tournament.
He carefully lined up his shot and struck the billiard ball with precision.
The impact of the billiard ball sent it spinning across the green felt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cue ball - the white ball in cue sports struck by the cue stick
