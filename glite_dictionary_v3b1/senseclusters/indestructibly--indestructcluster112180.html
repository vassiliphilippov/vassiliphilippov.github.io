indestructibly

adverb
in an unbreakable way
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktəbli / (BrE)
in a manner that cannot be destroyed or broken

Examples

Built indestructibly
The ancient fortress was constructed indestructibly to survive centuries of conflict.
Some materials are engineered indestructibly for use in extreme environments.
The legend claimed the sword was forged indestructibly by the gods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragilely - in a way that is easily broken or damaged
  • synonym: invincibly - in a way that cannot be defeated or overcome
