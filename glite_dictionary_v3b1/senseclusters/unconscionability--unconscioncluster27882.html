unconscionability

noun
extreme unfairness
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌkɑːnʃənəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌkɒnʃənəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely unfair, unreasonable, or morally unacceptable, especially in legal agreements or actions
