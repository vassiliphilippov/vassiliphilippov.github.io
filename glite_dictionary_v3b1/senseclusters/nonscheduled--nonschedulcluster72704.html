nonscheduled

adjective
not legally regulated
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈskɛdʒuːld / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈʃɛdjuːld / (BrE)
not classified under a legal or regulatory schedule and not subject to special controls or restrictions

Examples

Nonscheduled drugs
The medication is nonscheduled, so it can be purchased without a prescription.
Because the chemical is nonscheduled, it is not subject to strict government controls.
Importing nonscheduled substances is generally easier than importing regulated drugs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scheduled - classified under a legal or regulatory schedule
  • synonym: unscheduled - not included in a legal or regulatory schedule
