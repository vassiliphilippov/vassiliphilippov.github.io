mature

adjective
fully developed and ready to use or eat
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃʊr, məˈtʊr /; / məˈtʃʊr / (AmE), / məˈtʃʊə, məˈtjʊə /; / məˈtjʊə /; / məˈtjʊə, məˈtʃʊə / (BrE)
fully developed or ready to be used, eaten, or enjoyed (of food, animals, birds, ideas, etc.)

Examples
