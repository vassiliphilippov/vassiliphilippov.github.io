Synonyms

    a connection is a relation that joins people, things, or ideas closely, while a link is a more general channel or tie.
  • linkage
    3.9 1/m
    a linkage often means the act or system of joining parts in technical or formal use, while a link is a simple connection.
  • tie
    2.4 15/m
    a tie often shows a personal or business bond based on feelings or interest, while a link is a neutral connection.
  • nexus
    7.9 4/m
