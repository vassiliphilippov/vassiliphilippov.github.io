preciosity

noun
excessive refinement or fastidiousness
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːʃiˈɑːsəti / (AmE), / ˌpriːsɪˈɒsɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being overly refined, fastidious, or affected in style or taste

Examples

Artistic preciosity
Some artists are admired for their preciosity, while others see it as a flaw.
The author's preciosity made the novel difficult to read for some critics.
There was a certain preciosity in her speech that set her apart from her peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affectedness - artificial or pretentious behavior or style
  • antonym: naturalness - the quality of being natural or unaffected
