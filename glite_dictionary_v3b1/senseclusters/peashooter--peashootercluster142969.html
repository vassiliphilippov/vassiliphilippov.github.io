peashooter

noun
small weak gun
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
an informal term for a small or weak gun that is not very powerful or effective

Examples

A small peashooter gun
The old detective laughed at the rookie's tiny peashooter and handed him a real weapon.
He waved his peashooter around, but no one took him seriously.
Compared to modern firearms, that antique is just a peashooter.
