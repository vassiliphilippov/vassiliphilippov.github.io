Kyoto

proper noun
city in central Japan; former capital
Pronunciation: / kiˈoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / kiˈəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
A city in central Japan, known for its historic temples, gardens, and as a former capital of Japan.

Examples

Kyoto city
Kyoto is renowned for its beautiful cherry blossoms in spring.
During our trip to Japan, we spent three days exploring the ancient temples of Kyoto.
Many traditional arts, such as tea ceremony and ikebana, are still practiced in Kyoto.
