gentleman's gentleman

noun
a male personal attendant or valet
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩.mənz ˈdʒɛn.tl̩.mən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl.mənz ˈdʒɛn.tl.mən / (BrE)
a male personal servant, especially a valet, who attends to the needs of a gentleman employer

Examples

A gentleman's gentleman servant
In Victorian England, a gentleman's gentleman was considered an essential staff member in upper-class households.
Jeeves, the famous gentleman's gentleman in fiction, is known for his intelligence and discretion.
The wealthy aristocrat hired a gentleman's gentleman to help him dress every morning.
