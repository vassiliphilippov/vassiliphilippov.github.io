antiblue light bulb

noun
light bulb reducing blue light emissions
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈbluː ˈlaɪt ˌbʌlb / (AmE), / ˌantiˈbluː ˈlaɪt ˌbʌlb / (BrE)
a light bulb designed to reduce the emission of blue light, minimizing exposure that may affect sleep or eye health

Examples

An antiblue light bulb
He installed an antiblue light bulb in his bedroom to improve his sleep quality.
Many offices now use antiblue light bulbs to create a more comfortable work environment.
The antiblue light bulb is popular among people concerned about eye strain from screens.
