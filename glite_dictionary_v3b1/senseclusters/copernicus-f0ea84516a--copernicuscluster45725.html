Copernicus

proper noun
EU earth observation programme
Pronunciation: / koʊˈpɜrnɪkəs / (AmE), / kəʊˈpɜːnɪkəs / (BrE)
The European Union's earth observation programme that provides free satellite data, services and geospatial information for environmental monitoring, climate change and security.

Examples

Copernicus programme
Researchers downloaded satellite imagery from the Copernicus programme to monitor land use.
The city used Copernicus data to map flood risk areas after the heavy rains last month.
Startups build apps that combine weather sensors and Copernicus satellite images for farmers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earth observation - the gathering of information about the Earth's physical, chemical and biological systems, often by satellites
  • synonym: satellite imagery - images of Earth's surface collected by satellites used for mapping and monitoring
