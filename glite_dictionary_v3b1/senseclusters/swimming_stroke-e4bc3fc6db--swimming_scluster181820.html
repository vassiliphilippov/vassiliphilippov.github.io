swimming stroke

noun
method of swimming movement
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪmɪŋ stroʊk / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ strəʊk / (BrE)
a specific method or technique of moving the arms and legs in water to move the body forward while swimming

Examples

The swimming stroke freestyle
Freestyle is the fastest swimming stroke used in races.
She practiced each swimming stroke for the competition.
The butterfly is considered one of the most challenging swimming strokes to master.
