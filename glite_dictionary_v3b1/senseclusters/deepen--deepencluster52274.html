deepen

verb
make stronger or more intense
3.6 7/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêp…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêp…ôn / (BrE)
to make or become stronger, more intense, or more serious (such as feelings, situations, or problems)

Examples

Deepen a problem
Lack of communication can deepen misunderstandings in any relationship.
The crisis continued to deepen as new information emerged each day.
Their friendship deepened over the years as they shared more experiences.
As the night wore on, her curiosity began to deepen.
The crisis will likely deepen if immediate action is not taken.
Their friendship deepened over years of working together.
