morphologic

adjective
relating to form or structure, esp. in geology
Pronunciation: / mɔr.fəˈlɑ.dʒɪk /; / ˌmɔːrfəˈlɑːdʒɪk / (AmE), / mɔː.fəˈlɒ.dʒɪk /; / ˌmɔːfəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
