renourish

verb
to provide nourishment again
5.3
Pronunciation: / riˈnɜrɪʃ / (AmE), / riˈnʌrɪʃ / (BrE)
to provide nourishment to something or someone again, especially after a period of deprivation

Examples

To renourish the soil
After the drought, farmers worked hard to renourish the soil for the next planting season.
The rescue team needed to renourish the stranded hikers who had run out of supplies.
Doctors carefully planned how to renourish the patient after weeks of illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refeed - to feed again after a period without food
  • antonym: starve - to suffer or die from lack of food
