courthouse

noun
building for court and county offices
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building where a court of law is held and legal cases are heard

Examples

Lawyers gathered outside the courthouse after the verdict was announced.
The city plans to renovate the historic courthouse in the town square.
