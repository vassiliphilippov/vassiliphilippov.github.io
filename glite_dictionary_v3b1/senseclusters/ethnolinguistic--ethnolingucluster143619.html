ethnolinguistic

adjective
language and culture
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to both a specific language and the cultural group that speaks it

Examples

Ethnolinguistic group
The region is known for its rich ethnolinguistic diversity, with many groups speaking different languages.
Researchers study ethnolinguistic identity to understand how language shapes cultural belonging.
Government policies often consider ethnolinguistic groups when planning education programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultural - relating to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society
  • synonym: linguistic - relating to language or linguistics
