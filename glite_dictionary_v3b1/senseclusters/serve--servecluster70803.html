serve

verb
provide or present food or drink
3.8
Pronunciation: / sɝv / (AmE), / sɜːv / (BrE)
to give, present, or offer food or drink to someone, especially as part of a meal or service

Examples

Serve dinner
At the shelter, volunteers serve hot meals to those in need every evening.
She served tea and biscuits to her guests in the afternoon.
The hotel staff serve breakfast from 6 a.m. to 10 a.m.
At the restaurant, the waiter serves each course with great care.
Could you please serve the guests some tea while I finish cooking?
She served a delicious dinner to her family last night.

  • synonym: supply - to make available for use
