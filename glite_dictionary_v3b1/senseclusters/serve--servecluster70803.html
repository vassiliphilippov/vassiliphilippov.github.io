Synonyms

    1.4 2.55/m
    serve up presents food or drink to people at a table, while serve means giving or offering food in many settings.
  • wait
    4.0 10/m
    wait means acting as a waiter who serves customers in a restaurant, while serve covers giving food or drink in many situations.
  • wait on
    3.4 0.81/m
    wait on means attend to and bring food to someone at their table, while serve is the general act of giving food or drink.
  • dish up
    2.2 0.26/m
    dish up means put food onto plates from a shared dish, while serve can mean offering, presenting, or bringing food to people.
