turkey oak

noun
species of North American oak tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝki oʊk /; / ˈtɝːki oʊk /; / ˈtɝki ˌoʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki əʊk /; / ˈtɜːki ˌəʊk / (BrE)
