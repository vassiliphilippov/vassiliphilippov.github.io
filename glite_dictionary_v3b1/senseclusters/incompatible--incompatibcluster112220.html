incompatible

adjective
not suitable to your tastes or needs
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl / (BrE)
Not suitable to your tastes or needs; unpleasant or disagreeable for you.

Examples

Incompatible lifestyle choices
She found the job incompatible with her creative ambitions.
The food served at the event was completely incompatible with his dietary preferences.
Their personalities were incompatible, so they struggled to get along as roommates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: congenial - pleasant or suitable to someone's character or tastes
