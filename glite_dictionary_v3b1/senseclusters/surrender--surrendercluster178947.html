Synonyms

  • capitulation
    8.3 0.6/m
    capitulation is a formal, often negotiated giving up in war or talks, while surrender can be any act of yielding or defeat.
  • yielding
    5.3 0.01/m
    yielding is giving in during an argument or contest, while surrender covers broader loss of control or hope in many situations.
  • giving up
    3.2 4/m
    giving up is stopping trying after effort and admitting defeat, while surrender can also mean handing over control or rights.
  • relinquishment
    8.2 0.3/m
    relinquishment is formally giving up a right, claim, or role, while surrender can be an emotional or military act of defeat.
  • submission
    4.2 4/m
    submission is accepting another's control or authority, often as a state of obedience, while surrender is giving up or admitting defeat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resignation - acceptance of something undesirable but inevitable
  • antonym: resistance - refusal to accept or comply; attempt to prevent something by action or argument
