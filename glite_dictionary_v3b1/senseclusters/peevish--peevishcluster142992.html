peevish

adjective
easily irritated or annoyed
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːvɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpiːvɪʃ / (BrE)
easily irritated or annoyed, especially over minor matters; showing a querulous or fretful disposition.

Examples

A peevish answer
After a long, tiring day, she gave a peevish response to a simple question.
Children tend to become peevish when they are hungry or tired.
His peevish attitude made working with him difficult for everyone on the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
  • antonym: placid - calm and not easily annoyed
