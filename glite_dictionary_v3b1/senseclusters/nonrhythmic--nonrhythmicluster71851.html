nonrhythmic

adjective
lacking or without rhythm
3.1 5.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ /; / /nə.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ /; / /nə.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ (BrE)
Not characterized by or lacking a regular or definite rhythm; deliberately without rhythm.

Examples

Nonrhythmic drumming
He found the nonrhythmic drumming unsettling compared to the steady beats he was used to.
Some forms of modern art music embrace nonrhythmic patterns to create a unique soundscape.
The composer's latest piece is deliberately nonrhythmic, challenging our expectations of meter.
