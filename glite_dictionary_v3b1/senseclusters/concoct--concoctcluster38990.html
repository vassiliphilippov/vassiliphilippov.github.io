concoct

verb
prepare by mixing ingredients
Pronunciation: / kənˈkɑkt / (AmE), / kənˈkɒkt / (BrE)
to prepare or make something, especially food or drink, by mixing different ingredients together

Examples

To concoct a meal
She managed to concoct a delicious meal from leftover vegetables and spices.
The bartender will concoct a special cocktail for the event.
