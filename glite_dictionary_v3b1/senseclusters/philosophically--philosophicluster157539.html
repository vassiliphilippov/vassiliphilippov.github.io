philosophically

adverb
in a philosophical or calm manner
Pronunciation: / fɪˌlɑːsəˈfɪkli /; / ˌfɪləˈsɑfɪkli / (AmE), / fɪˌlɒsəˈfɪkəli /; / ˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to philosophy or shows calmness, wisdom, or acceptance toward difficult situations
