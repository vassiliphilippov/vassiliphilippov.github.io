Hispania

proper noun
poetic name for Spain
Pronunciation: / hɪˈspæniə / (AmE), / hɪˈspɑːniə / (BrE)
the ancient Roman name for the region that is now Spain and Portugal (the Iberian Peninsula), sometimes used in poetry or history
