multibladed

adjective
with multiple blades
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈbleɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈbleɪdɪd / (BrE)
having more than one blade, especially referring to tools or devices with multiple cutting or rotating edges

Examples

A multibladed propeller
The engineer designed a multibladed propeller to improve the boat's speed and efficiency.
She bought a multibladed razor for a closer and smoother shave.
A multibladed fan can circulate air more effectively than a single-bladed one.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: double-edged - having two cutting edges or blades
  • antonym: single-bladed - having only one blade
