left atrium

noun
upper left heart chamber
4.1 3.7
Pronunciation: /left ˈeɪ.triː.əm/ (AmE), /left ˈeɪ.triː.əm/ (BrE)
the upper left chamber of the heart that receives oxygenated blood from the lungs and pumps it into the left ventricle

Examples
