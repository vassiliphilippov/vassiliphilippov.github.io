misgovern

verb
govern badly
2.4 6.6
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈɡʌv.ən/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈɡʌv.ən/ (BrE)
to govern or manage a country, organization, or people in a poor or improper way

Examples

To misgovern a country
If leaders misgovern, it is the people who suffer the consequences most.
She accused the administration of having misgoverned for over a decade.
The regime continued to misgovern the country, leading to widespread discontent.
