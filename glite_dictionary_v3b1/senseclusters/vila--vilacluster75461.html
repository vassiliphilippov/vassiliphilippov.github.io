vila

noun
Slavic fairy spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈviːlə / (AmE), / ˈviːlə / (BrE)
a fairy or supernatural female being in Slavic folklore, often associated with nature and dancing

The villagers believed that a vila lived in the forest and protected the animals.
