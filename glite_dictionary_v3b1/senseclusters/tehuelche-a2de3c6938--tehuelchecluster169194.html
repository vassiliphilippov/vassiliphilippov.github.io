Tehuelche

noun
Patagonian indigenous language
Pronunciation: / teɪˈwɛltʃeɪ / (AmE), / teɪˈwɛltʃeɪ / (BrE)
the indigenous language of the Tehuelche (Aonikenk) people, formerly spoken in Patagonia, South America

Examples

Tehuelche language
Linguists are working to document the Tehuelche language before it disappears completely.
She studied Tehuelche to understand the culture and traditions of Patagonia's indigenous people.
Many words for local animals in Tehuelche have no direct translation in Spanish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aonikenk - another name for the Tehuelche people and their language
  • synonym: Mapudungun - the language of the Mapuche people of southern Chile and Argentina
