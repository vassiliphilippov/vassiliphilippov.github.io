columbarium

noun
vault for urns containing cremated remains
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌləmˈbɛriəm /; / ˌkɑːləmˈbɛriəm /; / ˌkə.ləmˈbɛr.i.əm / (AmE), / ˌkɒləmˈbɑːrɪəm /; / ˌkɒləmˈbeəriəm /; / ˌkɒl.əmˈbeə.ri.əm / (BrE)
a building or structure with niches in the walls for holding funeral urns containing the ashes of cremated people (sometimes, historically, also used for nesting pigeons or doves)
