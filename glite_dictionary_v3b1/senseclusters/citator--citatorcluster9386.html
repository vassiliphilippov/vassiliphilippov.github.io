citator

noun
person who cites sources
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who cites sources or references, especially in scholarly or legal writing

Examples

A careful citator
As a careful citator, he ensured every fact in his report was properly referenced.
The journal valued her reputation as a reliable citator in academic publishing.
Every legal document needs a skilled citator to track all relevant cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: author - a person who writes books, articles, or other texts
  • synonym: referencer - a person who provides references or citations
