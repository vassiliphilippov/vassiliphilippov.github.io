myoepithelial

noun
glandular contractile cell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪoʊˌɛpɪˈθiliəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəʊˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
a cell with both muscle-like (contractile) and epithelial properties, commonly found in certain glands

Examples

A myoepithelial cell
A myoepithelial cell helps squeeze secretions out of certain glands.
Researchers studied the role of myoepithelial cells in the function of sweat glands.
Damage to myoepithelial cells can affect the normal release of saliva.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epithelial cell - a cell forming the lining of body surfaces and cavities
  • synonym: muscle cell - a cell specialized for contraction
