turducken

verb
to nest items
Pronunciation: / tɚˈdʌkən / (AmE), / təˈdʌkən / (BrE)
to combine or nest several items by placing one inside another, like the dish turducken.

Examples

To turducken desserts
For the party she will turducken the appetizers by placing small tarts inside larger pastry shells.
The programmer decided to turducken several functions into a single complex module.
He likes to turducken gifts by wrapping smaller boxes inside bigger ones for fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nest - to place one thing inside another
  • antonym: separate - to keep things apart or uncombined
