bibliomania

noun
obsession with collecting books
8.0 8.0
Pronunciation: /bɪb.lɪ.oʊ.ˈmeɪ.nɪ.ə/ (AmE), /bɪb.lɪ.əʊ.ˈmeɪ.nɪ.ə/ (BrE)
an excessive or obsessive fondness for acquiring and possessing books

Examples

Bibliomania for books
Collectors who suffer from bibliomania often have more books than they could ever hope to read.
His bibliomania led him to spend every weekend scouring used bookstores for rare volumes.
The professor's office reflected his bibliomania, with stacks of books lining every surface.
