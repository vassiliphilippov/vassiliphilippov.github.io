rain gear

noun
waterproof clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪn ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ɡɪə / (BrE)
clothing or equipment worn to protect yourself from getting wet in the rain

Examples

Rain gear clothes
Before leaving for the hike, he packed his rain gear in case of a sudden downpour.
Children should always bring rain gear to school during the wet season.
The festival continued despite the weather, with everyone wearing their rain gear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raincoat - a waterproof coat worn to protect against rain
  • synonym: umbrella - a device used for protection against rain
