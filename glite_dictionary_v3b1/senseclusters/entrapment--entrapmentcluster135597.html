entrapment

noun
act of trapping
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtræpmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtræpmənt / (BrE)
the act of trapping someone or something, often by trickery or by setting a trap

Examples

Animal entrapment
The animal escaped from the entrapment set by hunters.
The detective warned about possible entrapment during undercover operations.
Many people are unaware of the risks of entrapment in online scams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capture - the act of catching or trapping someone or something
  • antonym: release - the act of setting someone or something free
