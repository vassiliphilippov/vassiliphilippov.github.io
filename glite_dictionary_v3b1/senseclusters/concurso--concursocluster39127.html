concurso

noun
bankruptcy proceedings
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔnˈkuɾsoʊ / (AmE), / kɒnˈkʊə.səʊ / (BrE)
a legal procedure for handling the debts of a person or company that cannot pay them, similar to bankruptcy proceedings

Examples

Company concurso
After months of financial trouble, the company entered concurso to resolve its debts.
The court appointed an administrator to oversee the concurso process for the failing business.
Many creditors attended the hearing to discuss the terms of the concurso.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bankruptcy - the legal status of being unable to pay debts
  • synonym: insolvency - the state of not having enough money to pay debts
