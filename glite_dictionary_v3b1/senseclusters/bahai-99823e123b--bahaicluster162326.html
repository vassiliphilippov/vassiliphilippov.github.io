Bahai

noun
a follower of the Bahá'í faith
6.8
Pronunciation: / bəˈhaɪ / (AmE), / bəˈhɑːi / (BrE)
a follower or adherent of the Bahá'í faith, a monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of humankind

Examples

A Bahai believer
As a Bahai, she believes in the unity of all religions.
He is a devoted Bahai serving his local community.
Several Bahais gathered to celebrate their holy day.
