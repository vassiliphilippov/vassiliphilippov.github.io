free agent

noun
athlete available to sign with any team
3.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /friː ˈeɪ.dʒənt/ (AmE), /friː ˈeɪ.dʒənt/ (BrE)
(sports) a professional athlete who is eligible to sign a contract with any team because they are not currently under contract to a specific team.

Examples

A free agent signs
After his contract ended, Tom became a free agent and signed with another team.
Negotiations are ongoing as the free agent weighs offers from different clubs.
The basketball star will be a free agent next season, drawing interest from several teams.
