guaracha

noun
Cuban dance and music
Pronunciation: / ɡwəˈrɑːtʃə / (AmE), / ɡwəˈrɑːtʃə / (BrE)
a lively Cuban dance and the fast-paced music that accompanies it, often featuring humorous lyrics

Examples

Cuban guaracha dance
The band played a lively guaracha that filled the dance floor with energy.
She learned to dance the guaracha during her trip to Havana.
Traditional guaracha songs often include witty and playful lyrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rumba - a Cuban dance and music style with a slow-quick-quick rhythm
  • synonym: salsa - a popular Latin American dance and music style with lively rhythms
