guaracha

noun
Cuban dance and music
Pronunciation: / ɡwəˈrɑːtʃə / (AmE), / ɡwəˈrɑːtʃə / (BrE)
a lively Cuban dance and the fast-paced music that accompanies it, often featuring humorous lyrics

Examples

Cuban guaracha dance

Traditional guaracha songs often include witty and playful lyrics.
