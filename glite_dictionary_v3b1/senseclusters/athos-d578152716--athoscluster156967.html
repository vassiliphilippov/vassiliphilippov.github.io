Athos

proper noun
monastic peninsula in Greece
Pronunciation: / ˈæθoʊs / (AmE), / ˈæθɒs / (BrE)
an autonomous peninsula in northeastern Greece, known for its historic Greek Orthodox monasteries and religious significance
