selection

noun
passage chosen from a larger work
4.1
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a passage or piece taken from a larger written work, musical composition, or similar source, usually presented on its own

Examples

A selection of poems
She read a selection of poems aloud at the event.
The anthology included a selection from the author's early novels.
This selection from the symphony showcases the composer's most beautiful melodies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excerpt - a short extract from a film, broadcast, or piece of music or writing
  • synonym: passage - a portion of a written work or music; a section or segment
