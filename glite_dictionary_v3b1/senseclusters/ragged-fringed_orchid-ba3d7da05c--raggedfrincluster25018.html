ragged-fringed orchid

noun
North American orchid with lacerated lips
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡɪd ˈfrɪndʒd ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈræɡɪd ˈfrɪndʒd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
