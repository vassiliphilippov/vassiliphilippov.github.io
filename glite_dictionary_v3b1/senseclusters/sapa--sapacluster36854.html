sapa

noun
small stream
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈpɑː / (AmE), / sɑːˈpɑː / (BrE)
a small stream or brook, especially as used in Philippine English and some regional dialects

Examples

A quiet sapa
Children played by the sapa that wound its way behind their village.
During the dry season, the sapa almost disappeared, leaving only a trickle of water.
Farmers rely on the sapa to irrigate their rice fields throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brook - a small, natural stream of fresh water
  • degree higher: river - a large, natural stream of water flowing in a channel
