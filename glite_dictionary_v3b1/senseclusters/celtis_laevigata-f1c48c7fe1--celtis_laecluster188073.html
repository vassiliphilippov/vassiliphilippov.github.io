Celtis laevigata

noun
a deciduous tree of the southern US; sugarberry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.tɪs ˌleɪ.vɪˈɡeɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˈkɛl.tɪs ˌliː.vɪˈɡɑː.tə / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to the southern United States, known for its small black berries and soft yellowish wood; commonly called sugarberry.
