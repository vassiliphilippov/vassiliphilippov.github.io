Hawaiian dancing

noun
dance style from Hawaii, often performed by women
3.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /hə.ˈwaɪ.ən ˈdæns.ɪŋ/ /; / /hə.ˈwaɪ.jən ˈdæns.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /hə.ˈwaɪ.ən ˈdɑːns.ɪŋ/ /; / /hə.ˈwaɪ.jən ˈdɑːns.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
