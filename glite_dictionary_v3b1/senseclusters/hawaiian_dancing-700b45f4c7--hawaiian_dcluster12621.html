Hawaiian dancing

noun
dance style from Hawaii, often performed by women
3.3
Pronunciation: / həˈwaɪ.i.ən ˈdæn.sɪŋ / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i.ən ˈdans.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dance originating in Hawaii, often involving graceful movements of the hands and hips to tell a story, commonly performed by women.

Examples

Hawaiian dancing style
Many tourists try Hawaiian dancing during their visit to Honolulu.
She wore a grass skirt and performed traditional Hawaiian dancing on stage.
The children enjoyed watching the Hawaiian dancing at the cultural festival.
