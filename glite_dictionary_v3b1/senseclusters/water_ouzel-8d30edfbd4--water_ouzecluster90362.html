water ouzel

noun
small diving bird, also called dipper
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˈuːzəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈuːzəl / (BrE)
a small, robust bird that lives near fast-flowing streams and dives underwater to feed, especially the dipper.

Examples

The water ouzel bird
The water ouzel is often seen bobbing on rocks beside mountain streams.
The distinctive call of the water ouzel echoed through the narrow valley.
We spotted a water ouzel diving fearlessly into the icy water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dipper - another name for the water ouzel, a small aquatic bird
