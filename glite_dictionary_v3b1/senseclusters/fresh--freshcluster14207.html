fresh

adjective
of a cow, newly calved and lactating
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ / (AmE), / frɛʃ / (BrE)
(of a cow) having recently calved and therefore able to give milk.

Examples

Fresh cow
After calving, the cow is considered fresh and starts producing milk.
The dairy farmer checked whether the fresh cows had adequate food and water.
The veterinarian inspected the fresh heifers to ensure their good health.
