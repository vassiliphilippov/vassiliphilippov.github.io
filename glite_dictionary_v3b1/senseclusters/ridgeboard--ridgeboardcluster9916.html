ridgeboard

noun
roof peak board
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a horizontal board at the highest point of a roof where the upper ends of the rafters are fastened

Examples

The ridgeboard of roof
The carpenter nailed each rafter to the ridgeboard to ensure the roof was stable.
Before installing the shingles, they checked that the ridgeboard was perfectly level.
A strong ridgeboard is essential for supporting the structure of a pitched roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rafter - a sloping beam supporting a roof
  • synonym: ridge - the top edge of a roof where two sloping sides meet
