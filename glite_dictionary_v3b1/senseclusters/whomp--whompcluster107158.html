whomp

noun
loud heavy sound
8.1
Pronunciation: /wɑːmp/ (AmE), /wɒmp/ (BrE)

Examples

She dropped her heavy bag on the floor, and it hit with a satisfying whomp.
We heard a sudden whomp from upstairs and rushed to see what had fallen.
