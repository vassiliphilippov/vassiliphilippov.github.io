Synonyms

  • fleecy
    4.2 0.1/m
    fleecy is a soft, brushed fabric surface like sheep's fleece, while woolly means having the texture or look of wool.
  • fuzzy
    1.1 2/m
    fuzzy is covered with fine light hairs or fibers, while woolly refers to thicker, wool-like fibers that feel curly or dense.
  • fluffy
    1.0 5/m
    fluffy is soft and light, like down or clouds, while woolly suggests a thicker, wool-like texture rather than airy softness.
  • downy
    4.2 0.4/m
    downy is covered with very fine, feather-like fluff, while woolly describes the thicker, wool-textured covering of animals or fabrics.
