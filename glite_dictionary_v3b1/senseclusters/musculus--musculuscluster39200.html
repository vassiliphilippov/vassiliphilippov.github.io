musculus

noun

the Latin word for 'little mouse', often used in etymology to explain the origin of the word 'muscle'.

Examples

Latin musculus origin
The Latin word musculus is the origin of the English word 'muscle'.
In ancient texts, musculus referred to a small mouse rather than a body part.
Etymologists often mention musculus when discussing the history of anatomical terms.
