nurturer

noun
one who cares for others
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrtʃərər / (AmE), / ˈnɜːtʃərə / (BrE)
a person who provides care, support, and encouragement to help someone grow or develop

  • synonym: protector - someone who keeps others safe from harm
