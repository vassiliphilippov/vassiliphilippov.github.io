unhittable

adjective
impossible to defeat
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to defeat, criticize, or harm, especially in a competition or argument

Examples

Unhittable champion
The champion boxer remained unhittable throughout the entire match, dodging every punch.
Her argument was so well-constructed that it seemed unhittable in the debate.
The new software's security features make it almost unhittable to hackers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or attacked
