churn

verb
to make butter by stirring cream
4.6 1.34/m
Pronunciation: / t É…úrn / (AmE), / t É…úÀên / (BrE)
to stir or agitate cream vigorously in order to make butter

Examples

To churn cream
Machines now churn butter much faster than people could by hand.
The farmer used a wooden stick to churn the cream into butter every morning.
You must churn the cream for at least twenty minutes to achieve the right consistency.
