halfpennyworth

noun
amount that can be bought for a halfpenny
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪ·pəniˌwɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈheɪ.pəniˌwɜːθ / (BrE)
An amount or quantity that can be bought for a halfpenny; a very small amount.

Examples

A halfpennyworth of milk
A halfpennyworth of milk was enough for his morning tea.
He bought a halfpennyworth of sweets from the village shop.
She received nothing but a halfpennyworth of sense from his advice.
