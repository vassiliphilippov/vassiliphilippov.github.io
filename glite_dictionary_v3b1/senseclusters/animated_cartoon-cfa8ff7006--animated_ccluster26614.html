animated cartoon

noun
film made from moving drawings
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd kɑːrˈtun / (AmE), / ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd kɑːˈtuːn / (BrE)
A film or television program made by photographing a series of drawings or images so they appear to move when played in rapid sequence.

Examples

An animated cartoon series
She dreams of creating her own animated cartoon series one day.
The children watched an animated cartoon every Saturday morning.
The studio released its first animated cartoon in the 1930s, which became an instant hit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartoon - A simple drawing showing features in a humorous or satirical way
