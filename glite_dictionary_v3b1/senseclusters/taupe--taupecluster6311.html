taupe

noun
greyish-brown color
Pronunciation: / toʊp / (AmE), / təʊp / (BrE)
a dark grayish-brown color, especially as used in fabrics and interior decoration

Examples

A taupe sweater
He wore a taupe sweater that matched his dress pants perfectly.
She painted her living room walls a soothing shade of taupe.
The designer recommended taupe curtains to complement the neutral tones in the room.

  • degree lower: beige - a pale sandy yellowish-brown color
