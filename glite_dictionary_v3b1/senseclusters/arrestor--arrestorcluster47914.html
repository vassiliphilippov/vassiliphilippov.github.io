arrestor

noun
surge protection device
7.5
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛstər / (AmE), / əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
a device that protects electrical equipment by diverting or limiting high-voltage surges to prevent damage

Examples

Lightning arrestor protection
The technician installed a new arrestor to protect the building's electrical system from lightning strikes.
Without a proper arrestor, sensitive equipment could be damaged by sudden power surges.
The engineer recommended upgrading the old arrestor to ensure better protection for the control panel.
