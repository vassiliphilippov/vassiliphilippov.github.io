retie

verb
tie again or differently
Pronunciation: / riˈtaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtaɪ / (BrE)

Examples

To retie a ribbon

Could you retie this knot for me? It's too loose.
The shoelaces came undone, so he stopped to retie them.
