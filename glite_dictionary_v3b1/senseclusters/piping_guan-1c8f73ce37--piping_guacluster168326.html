piping guan

noun
a large bird of Central and South America
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpɪŋ ɡwɑn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpɪŋ ɡwæn / (BrE)
a large, arboreal bird of the family Cracidae found in Central and South America, known for its piping calls
