Christmas cactus

noun
winter-flowering Brazilian houseplant cactus
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməs ˌkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ˌkaktəs / (BrE)
A popular houseplant native to Brazil, with flat jointed stems and rose-purple flowers that typically bloom in winter.

Examples

A Christmas cactus plant
My Christmas cactus is covered in beautiful pink blooms every December.
She gave her grandmother a Christmas cactus for the holidays.
The Christmas cactus needs only a little water during its dormant months.
