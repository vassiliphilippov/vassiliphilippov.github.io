theist

noun
person believing in a god or gods
9.2 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːɪst / (AmE), / ˈθiːɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in the existence of a god or gods, especially a creator who intervenes in the universe

Examples

A theist believes
Many theists find comfort in prayer and religious rituals.
She identifies as a theist and attends services regularly.
The debate between an atheist and a theist lasted for hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agnostic - a person who believes that it is impossible to know whether there is a god
  • antonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of a god or gods
  • synonym: deist - a person who believes that God created the universe but does not intervene in it
