featureless

adjective
lacking distinguishing characteristics
5.8 1.04/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfiÀêt É…ôrl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàfiÀêt É…ôl…ôz / (BrE)
having no distinctive or identifying features; lacking noticeable characteristics.

Examples

A featureless face
Her drawing depicted a featureless face, giving it an eerie, abstract quality.
On cloudy nights, the sky becomes a featureless gray dome above the city.
The desert stretched out as a featureless expanse as far as the eye could see.
