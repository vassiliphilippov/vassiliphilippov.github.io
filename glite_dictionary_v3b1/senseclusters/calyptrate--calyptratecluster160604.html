calyptrate

adjective
having calypters (in certain flies)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪptrət / (AmE), / kəˈlɪptrɪt /; / kəˈlɪptrət / (BrE)
having a calyptra or calypters (a cap-like or membranous covering), especially in certain flies, plants, or fungi
