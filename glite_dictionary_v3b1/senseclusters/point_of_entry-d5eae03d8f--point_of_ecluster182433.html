point of entry

noun
cybersecurity access route or vulnerability
6.0

(in computing, especially cybersecurity) a place, route, or vulnerability through which an attacker or malicious software can gain access to a system or network.

Examples

System point of entry
Experts identified a new point of entry for the malware in the corporate network.
The pentester mapped every potential point of entry before attempting to exploit the system.
To reduce risk, the team closed a small point of entry created by an outdated third-party plugin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack vector - a method or route used by an attacker to gain unauthorized access to a system
  • synonym: vulnerability - a weakness in software or a system that can be exploited to cause harm or gain access
