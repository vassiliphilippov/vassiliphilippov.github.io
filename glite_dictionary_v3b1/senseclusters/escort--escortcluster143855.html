escort

noun
act of accompanying for protection
3.4 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈe.skɔːrt/ /; / /ˈɪ.skɔːrt/ (AmE), /ˈe.skɔːt/ /; / /ˈɪ.skɔːt/ (BrE)
the act of accompanying someone, or a person or group who accompanies someone, especially to provide protection, assistance, or as a courtesy
