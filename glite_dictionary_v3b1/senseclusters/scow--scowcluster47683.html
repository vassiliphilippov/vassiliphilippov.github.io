scow

noun
flat-bottomed barge for bulk materials
9.9
Pronunciation: / skaʊ / (AmE), / skaʊ / (BrE)
a flat-bottomed boat or barge with sloping ends, used for transporting goods or materials, especially in shallow waters

Examples
