overinsure

verb
to insure excessively
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊr / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈʃʊə / (BrE)
to provide more insurance coverage for someone or something than is necessary or reasonable

Examples

To overinsure equipment
The company realized it had overinsured its equipment, resulting in unnecessary expenses.
If you overinsure your car, you may end up paying higher premiums for little extra benefit.
They were careful not to overinsure their home after reviewing their actual needs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insure - to provide insurance coverage for someone or something
  • antonym: underinsure - to provide less insurance coverage than is needed
