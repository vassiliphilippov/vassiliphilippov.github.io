Book of Jeremiah

proper noun
book of the Old Testament, prophecies of Jeremiah
Pronunciation: / ˌbʊk əv ˌdʒɛrɪˈmaɪə / (AmE), / ˌbʊk əv ˌdʒɛrɪˈmaɪə / (BrE)
