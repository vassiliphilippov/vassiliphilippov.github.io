salesgirl

noun
female salesperson in a store
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlzˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young woman whose job is to sell products in a shop or store; a female salesperson

Examples

A salesgirl in shop
A young salesgirl greeted customers with a bright smile as they entered the shop.
After asking for assistance, the salesgirl quickly showed him the latest arrivals.
The salesgirl helped me find my size in the shoe department.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saleswoman - an adult woman whose job is to sell products
