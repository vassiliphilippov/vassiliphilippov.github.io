loros

noun
Byzantine ceremonial scarf
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːroʊs / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrɒs / (BrE)
a long, ornate scarf or sash worn as a ceremonial vestment or symbol of rank in Byzantine and Eastern Christian traditions

Examples

The loros is an important symbol of authority in Eastern Orthodox liturgical tradition.
