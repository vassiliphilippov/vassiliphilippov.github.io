scrupulous

adjective
having strong moral principles; careful and honest
7.2 0.5/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskruÀêpj…ôl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàskruÀêpj äl…ôs / (BrE)
Very careful to do what is morally right and honest; acting with strict regard for what is considered proper or ethical.

Examples

Scrupulous honesty
A scrupulous accountant would never approve such questionable expense reports.
Despite pressure to bend the rules, he remained scrupulous in his business dealings.
She is known for her scrupulous attention to detail in every project she undertakes.

  • conscientious
