eloquence

noun
powerful and effective use of language
Pronunciation: / ˈɛl.ə.kwəns / (AmE), / ˈɛl.ə.kwəns / (BrE)
the ability to use language in a fluent, forceful, and effective way to persuade or impress others

Examples

Powerful eloquence
His eloquence made complex ideas easy to understand for all listeners.
She addressed the audience with such eloquence that even her critics listened intently.
The senator's eloquence during the debate impressed everyone in attendance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluency - ability to express oneself easily and articulately
  • antonym: inelegance - lack of skill or effectiveness in expression
