sporter

noun
person who shows off
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtə / (BrE)
a person who shows off or displays something, or a firearm modified for sporting purposes

Examples

Sporter of a watch
He is a proud sporter of vintage watches at every event.
The rifle was a custom sporter designed for hunting competitions.
As a sporter of rare sneakers, she always draws attention at school.
