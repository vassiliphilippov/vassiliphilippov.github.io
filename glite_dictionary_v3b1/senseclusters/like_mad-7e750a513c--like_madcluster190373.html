like mad

adverb
with great intensity or effort
3.3
Pronunciation: / laɪk mæd / (AmE), / laɪk mæd / (BrE)
(informal) With great speed, effort, or intensity; used to emphasize the force or vigor of an action.

Examples

Running like mad
He was running like mad to catch the last train.
They worked like mad to finish the project on time.
When the alarm went off, everyone scattered like mad.
