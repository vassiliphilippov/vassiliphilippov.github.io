coinciding

adjective
happening at the same time
Pronunciation: / kəˈɪnˌsaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪn.saɪ.dɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or existing at the same time as something else.

Examples

Two events coinciding
Several projects are coinciding, which makes scheduling difficult.
The festival is coinciding with my birthday this year.
We observed two coinciding events during our experiment.
