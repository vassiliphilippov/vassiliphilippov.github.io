brake pedal

noun
foot pedal for activating vehicle brakes
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪk ˌpɛd.əl / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk ˌpɛd.əl / (BrE)
the foot pedal in a vehicle that is pressed to activate the brakes

Examples

Press the brake pedal
She stepped hard on the brake pedal to avoid hitting the car in front.
The mechanic checked the brake pedal for any signs of wear.
When the brake pedal feels soft, it might indicate a problem with the brakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accelerator pedal - the foot pedal used to control a vehicle's speed
