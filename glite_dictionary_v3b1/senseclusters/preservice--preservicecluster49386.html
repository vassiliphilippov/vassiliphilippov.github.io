preservice

adjective
before starting work
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
relating to training or preparation that takes place before starting a job or profession

Examples

Preservice teacher training
All new teachers must complete preservice training before entering the classroom.
The university offers a preservice program for students planning to become nurses.
Her preservice experience helped her feel confident on her first day at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-service - relating to training during employment
