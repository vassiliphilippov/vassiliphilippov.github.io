cough up a hairball

verb
expel a hairball (animal)
Pronunciation: / kɑf ʌp ə ˈhɛrˌbɔl / (AmE), / kɒf ʌp ə ˈheəˌbɔːl / (BrE)
(of a cat or other animal) to expel a hairball from the mouth after coughing or retching.
