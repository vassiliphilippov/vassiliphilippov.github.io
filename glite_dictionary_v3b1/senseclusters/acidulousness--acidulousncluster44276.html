acidulousness

noun
state of being slightly acidic
4.9
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪdʒələsnəs / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being slightly acidic or sour

Examples

Slight acidulousness
Its slight acidulousness balanced the sweetness of the dessert perfectly.
Some people enjoy the acidulousness found in green apples and certain wines.
The acidulousness of the lemonade gave it a refreshing tang on a hot day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acidity - the quality of being acid; sourness
  • degree higher: bitterness - sharpness of taste; lack of sweetness
  • antonym: sweetness - the property of having a pleasant, sugary taste
