duck-billed

adjective
with a beak like a duck's
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌk bɪld / (AmE), / ˈdʌk bɪld / (BrE)
having a beak or snout resembling that of a duck; shaped like a duck's bill

Examples

Duck-billed dinosaur
Some dinosaurs, such as the duck-billed hadrosaur, had distinctive, flat snouts.
The duck-billed platypus is native to eastern Australia.
The artist sculpted a duck-billed creature for the museum exhibit.
