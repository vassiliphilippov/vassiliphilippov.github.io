Synonyms

  • chime
    1.7 1.35/m
    chime is a clear musical bell sound often from a few bells, while bell ringing is the sound of a whole set played ceremonially.
  • ringing
    1.0 2.92/m
    ringing is the clear resonant sound of a bell struck once, while bell ringing is the sound made by a set of bells played musically.
  • ring
    1.0 7.01/m
    ring is the sound from a bell, telephone, or similar object, while bell ringing is the musical sound of a set of bells played together.
