nervously

adverb
in a nervous or anxious way
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnɜːr.vəs.liː/ /; / /ˈnɜːr.vəs.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnɜː.vəs.liː/ /; / /ˈnɜː.vəs.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that shows nervousness, anxiety, or worry

Examples

To laugh nervously
He laughed nervously when asked about his plans for the future.
She glanced nervously around the crowded room before starting her speech.
The children waited nervously for their test results to be announced.
He glanced nervously at his watch as the interview ran late.
She laughed nervously before giving her speech in front of the class.
The dog barked nervously when it heard a strange noise outside the door.
