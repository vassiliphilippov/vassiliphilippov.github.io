teething

noun
baby's first teeth emerging
Pronunciation: / ˈtiðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːðɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which a baby's first teeth emerge through the gums

Examples

Teething baby symptoms
Red cheeks and drooling are common signs of teething in infants.
Several remedies are available to soothe discomfort during teething.
The baby is experiencing teething and has been fussy all week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dentition - the development and eruption of teeth
  • synonym: eruption - the process of teeth breaking through the gums
