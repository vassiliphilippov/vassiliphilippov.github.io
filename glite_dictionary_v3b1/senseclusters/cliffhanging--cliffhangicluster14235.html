cliffhanging

adjective
suspenseful, exciting
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
causing intense suspense or excitement, especially by leaving the outcome unresolved or uncertain

Examples

Cliffhanging ending
The final episode ended with a cliffhanging scene that left viewers desperate for answers.
She loves reading cliffhanging thrillers that keep her guessing until the last page.
The competition was so cliffhanging that no one could predict who would win until the very end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exciting - causing strong feelings of enthusiasm or eagerness
  • antonym: predictable - easy to guess or expect; not surprising
  • synonym: suspenseful - causing a feeling of excitement or anxious uncertainty
