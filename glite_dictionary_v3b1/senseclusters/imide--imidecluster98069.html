imide

noun
organic compound with –CONHCO– group
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪm.aɪd / (BrE)
a compound belonging to a class of organic chemicals containing the divalent group –CONHCO–, formed formally by replacing two oxygen atoms in an acid anhydride with NH groups
