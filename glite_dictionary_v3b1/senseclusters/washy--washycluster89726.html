washy

adjective
faded, weak in color
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃi / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃi / (BrE)
(of color) faded or weak; lacking in freshness or brilliance.

Examples

Washy colors
After years of sunlight, the curtains looked washy and lifeless.
She preferred bold colors to the washy pastels in the old room.
The artist avoided washy tones to create a more vibrant painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faded - having lost brightness or vividness of color
  • synonym: pale - light in color or lacking in intensity
