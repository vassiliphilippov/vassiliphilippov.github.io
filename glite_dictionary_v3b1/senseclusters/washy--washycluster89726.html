washy

adjective
faded, weak in color
2.4 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃi / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃi / (BrE)

Examples

Washy colors

She preferred bold colors to the washy pastels in the old room.
The artist avoided washy tones to create a more vibrant painting.
