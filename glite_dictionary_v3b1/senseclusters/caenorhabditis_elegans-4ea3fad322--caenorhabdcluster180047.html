Caenorhabditis elegans

noun
model roundworm species
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːnəˌræbˈdaɪtɪs ˈɛlɪɡænz / (AmE), / ˌsiːnəˌræbˈdaɪtɪs ˈɛlɪɡænz / (BrE)
a small, transparent roundworm (nematode) species widely used as a model organism in scientific and genetic research
