worrisomely

adverb
causing concern
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜːriˌsoʊmli / (AmE), / ˈwʌrɪsəmli / (BrE)
in a manner that causes worry or concern

Examples

Look worrisomely at results
The doctor looked worrisomely at the test results before speaking to the patient.
Prices have been rising worrisomely fast over the past few months.
He glanced worrisomely at the darkening sky as thunder rumbled in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarmingly - in a way that causes alarm or fear
  • antonym: reassuringly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried
