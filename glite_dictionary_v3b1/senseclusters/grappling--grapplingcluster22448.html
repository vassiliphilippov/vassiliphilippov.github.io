grappling

noun
a sport of hand-to-hand wrestling
7.6 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgræp.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgræp.ə.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgræp.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgræp.ə.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
a sport or act of close hand-to-hand combat where opponents try to seize, hold, or wrestle each other, usually without striking

Examples

Grappling sport
Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a popular form of grappling practiced worldwide.

He has years of experience in various forms of grappling, including judo and Brazilian jiu-jitsu.
The two opponents engaged in intense grappling throughout the match.
