sole

noun
flesh of a flatfish, used as food
8.3
Pronunciation: / soʊl / (AmE), / səʊl / (BrE)
the flesh of any of several species of flatfish, especially those of the family Soleidae, commonly eaten as food

Examples

Sole fillet
Fresh sole is considered a delicacy in many seafood restaurants.
He ordered sole fillet, served with steamed vegetables and rice.
The chef prepared grilled sole with a lemon butter sauce for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flounder - flesh of another type of flatfish also used for food
