through a different lens

adverb
from a new perspective
1.0
Pronunciation: / θru ə ˈdɪf(ə)rənt lɛnz / (AmE), / θruː ə ˈdɪfrənt lɛnz / (BrE)
used to describe considering or viewing something from a new or different perspective or viewpoint

Examples

Look through a different lens
After talking to her, he tried to look at the problem through a different lens.
The documentary encouraged viewers to see history through a different lens.
Travel can help you perceive your own culture through a different lens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: point of view - a particular way of thinking about or approaching something
