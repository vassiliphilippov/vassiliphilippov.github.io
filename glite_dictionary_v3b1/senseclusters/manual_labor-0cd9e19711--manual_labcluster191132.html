manual labor

noun
work involving physical effort
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.ju.əl ˈleɪ.bɚ /; / ˈmænjuəl ˈleɪbər / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ju.əl ˈleɪ.bə /; / ˈmænjuəl ˈleɪbə / (BrE)
work that is done by hand or with physical effort, rather than by machines or mental work
