hollering

noun
a loud shout or cry
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑlərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɒlərɪŋ / (BrE)
A very loud shout or cry, typically resembling the call of an animal.

Examples

Loud hollering noise
Sudden hollering in the hallway startled the students during their exams.
The hollering from the field woke everyone in the house.
Their hollering could be heard from half a mile away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bellow - a loud, deep shout or cry
