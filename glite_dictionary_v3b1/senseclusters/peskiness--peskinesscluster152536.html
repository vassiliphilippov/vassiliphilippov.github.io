peskiness

noun
annoying quality
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛskinəs / (AmE), / ˈpɛskinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being annoying or troublesome

Examples

The peskiness of flies
The peskiness of the flies made it difficult to enjoy our picnic.
He complained about the peskiness of constant interruptions while working from home.
Despite the peskiness of the noise outside, she managed to finish her book.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyance - the feeling or state of being irritated
  • synonym: irritation - the state of being irritated or bothered
  • antonym: pleasantness - the quality of being enjoyable or agreeable
