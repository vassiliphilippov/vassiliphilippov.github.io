interprofessional

adjective
between professions
6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.prə.ˈfeʃ.ə.nəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.prə.ˈfeʃ.ə.nəl/ (BrE)
involving or relating to cooperation or collaboration between people from different professions

Examples

Effective interprofessional communication
Effective interprofessional communication is essential for providing high-quality patient care in hospitals.
The university offers an interprofessional course where students from different fields work together on projects.
An interprofessional approach can help solve complex problems by combining expertise from various professions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraprofessional - occurring within a single profession
  • synonym: multidisciplinary - involving several academic or professional disciplines
