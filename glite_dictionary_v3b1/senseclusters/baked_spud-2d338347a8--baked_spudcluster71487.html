baked spud

noun
informal term for baked potato
9.9
Pronunciation: / beɪkt spʌd / (AmE), / beɪkt spʌd / (BrE)
An informal term for a baked potato, especially one served whole with toppings.

Examples

A baked spud with cheese
A baked spud makes a quick and filling lunch option.
Cafés often offer a variety of toppings for your baked spud.
She ordered a baked spud loaded with cheese for dinner.
