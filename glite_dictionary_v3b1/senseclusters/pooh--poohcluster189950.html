pooh

interjection
disgust exclamation
9.4 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / puÀê / (AmE), / puÀê / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express dislike or disgust, especially at a bad smell or something unpleasant

Examples

Pooh, that stinks
When he opened the old fridge, he exclaimed, "Pooh, what a terrible smell!"
She wrinkled her nose and said, "Pooh, who left the trash out?"
Walking past the dumpster, he muttered, "Pooh, that stinks!"

Synonyms
