daytime

noun
the period from sunrise to sunset
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.taɪm / (BrE)
the period of the day between sunrise and sunset when it is light outside

Examples

During the daytime
Most children are in school during the daytime.
Owls usually sleep in the daytime and hunt at night.
The flowers only open in the daytime when the sun is shining.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nighttime - the period of the day between sunset and sunrise
