pivotman

noun
center-pivot operator
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətˌman / (BrE)
a worker who operates, inspects, or maintains a center-pivot irrigation system on a farm

Examples

Farm pivotman
The pivotman checked the irrigation system every morning before starting the day's work.
A skilled pivotman can quickly identify and fix problems in the center-pivot equipment.
During the dry season, the pivotman played a crucial role in keeping the crops healthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • synonym: irrigator - a person who manages or operates irrigation systems
