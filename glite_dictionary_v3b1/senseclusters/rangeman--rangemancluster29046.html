rangeman

noun
area patrol worker
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who patrols or monitors a large area, such as a forest or range, to watch for hazards or problems

Examples

Rangeman patrols the forest
The rangeman checked the boundaries of the reserve every morning for signs of trespassing.
During the dry season, a rangeman is crucial for spotting wildfires early.
The national park hired a new rangeman to help protect the wildlife and visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ranger - a person who manages or protects a forest, park, or range
  • synonym: warden - a person responsible for the supervision of a particular place or activity
