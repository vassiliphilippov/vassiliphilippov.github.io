constituted

adjective
formally established or set up
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.stɪˌtuː.t̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.stɪ.tjuː.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a body or authority) brought into existence, set up, or established, especially in a formal or official sense.

Examples

A constituted committee
Only a constituted committee has the power to make those decisions.
The constituted authority of the city was respected by its citizens.
They rejected the rules imposed by the constituted body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: established - having been in existence for a long time and therefore recognized and generally accepted
