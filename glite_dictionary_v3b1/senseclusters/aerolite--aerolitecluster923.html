aerolite

noun
stony type of meteorite
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛərəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a stony meteorite composed chiefly of silicate minerals

Examples

A aerolite meteorite
After analysis, they confirmed the rock was an aerolite rather than a metallic meteorite.
Scientists discovered an aerolite in the desert, providing insights into the composition of early planets.
The museum's collection includes a rare aerolite believed to have landed thousands of years ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorite - a solid piece of debris from an object from space that survives passage through the atmosphere
