Examples

Strong ethnocentricity

Teachers are encouraged to challenge ethnocentricity in the classroom to promote understanding.
Ethnocentricity can lead to misunderstandings and conflicts between different cultural groups.

Synonyms

  • ethnocentrism
    8.8 0.82/m

  • cultural chauvinism
    8.9 0.01/m
    cultural chauvinism expresses aggressive pride and intolerance toward other cultures, while ethnocentricity names the general belief of cultural superiority.
