fetal movement

noun
the motion of a fetus within the uterus
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtəl ˈmuːvmənt /; / ˈfiːtəl ˈmuːv.mənt / (AmE), / ˈfiːtəl ˈmuːvmənt /; / ˈfəʊ.təl ˈmuːv.mənt / (BrE)
the motion of a fetus within the uterus, typically first noticed by the 16th week of pregnancy
