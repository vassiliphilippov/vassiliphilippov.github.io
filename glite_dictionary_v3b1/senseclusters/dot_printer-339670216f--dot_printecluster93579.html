dot printer

noun
printer producing text/graphics as dot patterns
Pronunciation: / dɑt ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / dɒt ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
a type of printer in which characters and graphics are produced as patterns of dots in a dot matrix

Examples

A dot printer machine
Although slower than laser models, a dot printer is reliable for multipart forms.
She could hear the distinctive noise of the dot printer in the office.
The company used a dot printer to produce multiple copies of the invoice quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dot matrix printer - printer that forms images from a matrix of dots
