semiprivate

adjective
partially private, esp. in hospital context
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈpraɪvət, ˌsɛmaɪˈpraɪvət / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈprʌɪvət, ˌsɛmaɪˈprʌɪvət / (BrE)
(of a room, space, or institution) offering privacy to some extent but shared by more than one person, especially in a hospital.

Examples

A semiprivate room
After her surgery, she was moved to a semiprivate room to recover.
Many patients prefer a semiprivate room because it is less expensive than a fully private one.
The hospital offers both private and semiprivate accommodations depending on your insurance.
