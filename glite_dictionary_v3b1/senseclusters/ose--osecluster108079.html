A word ending that makes adjectives meaning full of, like verbose

Examples

The suffix ose in adjectives
The word 'verbose' uses the suffix ose to mean 'full of words'.
Adding ose to 'grand' creates 'grandiose', meaning 'full of grandeur'.
Many English adjectives, like 'bellicose', end with the suffix ose.
