comprehensive examination

noun
major academic proficiency test
1.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /kəm.prɪ.ˈhen.sɪv ɪg.zæm.ɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kɑːm.prɪ.ˈhen.sɪv ɪg.zæm.ɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kɒm.prɪ.ˈhen.sɪv ɪg.zæm.ɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a major examination that tests a student's knowledge and mastery of a particular subject, often required in higher education before advancing to candidacy or completing a degree

Examples
