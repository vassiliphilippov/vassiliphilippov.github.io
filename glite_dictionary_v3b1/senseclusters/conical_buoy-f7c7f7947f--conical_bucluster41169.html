conical buoy

noun
cone-shaped navigational buoy
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnɪkəl ˈbui, boʊɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒnɪkəl ˈbɔɪ / (BrE)
A buoy that is shaped like a cone, commonly used as a navigational marker in waterways.

Examples

A conical buoy
Maintenance crews repainted the faded conical buoy to ensure its visibility.
Sailors were warned to stay on the starboard side of the conical buoy.
The harbor entrance was marked by a conical buoy to guide incoming ships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buoy - a floating device used as a marker or to guide ships
