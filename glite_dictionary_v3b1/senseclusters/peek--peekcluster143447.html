peek

verb
look quickly and secretly
1.1 7.36/m
Pronunciation: / piÀêk / (AmE), / piÀêk / (BrE)

Examples

To peek through curtains
He peeked through the curtains to see who was outside.
She couldn't resist peeking at her friend's text message.
The child tried to peek into the box before his birthday.
