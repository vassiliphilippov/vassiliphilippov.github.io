warrener

noun
person managing a rabbit warren
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛrənər / (AmE), / ˈwɒrɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to maintain a rabbit warren, especially for the breeding and management of rabbits

Examples

The warrener manages rabbits
As a child, he was fascinated by the work of the local warrener.
The warrener carefully inspected the burrows every morning for signs of predators.
The estate once employed a warrener to look after the rabbits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamekeeper - a person employed to take care of game and wildlife on an estate
