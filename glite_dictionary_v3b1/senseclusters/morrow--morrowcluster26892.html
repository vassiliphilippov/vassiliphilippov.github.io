morrow

noun
the next day (archaic)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒrəʊ / (BrE)
(archaic) the next day; the day after a particular day.

Examples

The morrow after battle
On the morrow of the battle, the fields were silent and calm.
The promise would be fulfilled on the morrow as soon as the sun rose.
They agreed to meet again on the morrow to discuss the terms.
