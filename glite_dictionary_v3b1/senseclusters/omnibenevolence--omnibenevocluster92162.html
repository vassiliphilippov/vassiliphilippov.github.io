omnibenevolence

noun
infinite goodness
Pronunciation: / ˌɑmnɪbəˈnɛvələns / (AmE), / ˌɒmnɪbəˈnɛvələns / (BrE)
the quality or doctrine of being all-loving and perfectly good, especially as an attribute of a deity
