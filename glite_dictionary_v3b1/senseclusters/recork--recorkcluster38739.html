recork

verb
to replace cork
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkɔrk /; / ˌriˈkɔrk / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkɔːk / (BrE)
to put a cork or stopper back into a bottle or container to seal it again after it has been opened
