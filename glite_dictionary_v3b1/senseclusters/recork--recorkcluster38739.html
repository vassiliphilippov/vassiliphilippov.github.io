recork

verb
to replace cork
5.3
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈkɔːrk/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkɔːrk/ (AmE), /riː.ˈkɔːk/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkɔːk/ (BrE)
