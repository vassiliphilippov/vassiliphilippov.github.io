burl

verb
remove knots from cloth
9.4 8.6
Pronunciation: /bɜːrl/ (AmE), /bɜːl/ (BrE)
to remove small knots or lumps from cloth, especially during manufacture

Examples

To burl cloth
Before the cloth can be dyed, it must first be burled and cleaned.
She spent hours burling each piece of wool by hand.
The textile worker carefully burled the fabric to achieve a smooth finish.
