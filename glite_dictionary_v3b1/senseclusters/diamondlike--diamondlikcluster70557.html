diamondlike

adjective
resembling a diamond
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəməndˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəməndˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having qualities similar to a diamond, especially in appearance or brilliance

Examples

Diamondlike shine
The artist used a diamondlike coating to make the sculpture shimmer in the sunlight.
Her necklace had a diamondlike shine, though it was made of glass.
The new material is valued for its diamondlike hardness and clarity.
