Mithraicism

noun
ancient Persian religion involving Mithras
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪθˈreɪ.ɪ.sɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌmɪθˈreɪ.ɪ.sɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
