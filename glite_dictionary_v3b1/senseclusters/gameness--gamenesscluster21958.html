gameness

noun
wild meat flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmnəs / (BrE)
the strong, distinctive smell or taste often found in the meat of wild animals such as deer or pheasant

Examples

Gameness of wild meat
Some people enjoy the gameness of wild boar, while others find it too strong.
The marinade helped to soften the gameness of the rabbit stew.
Chefs often use spices to balance the gameness in dishes made with venison.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blandness - the lack of strong flavor or taste
  • synonym: gamey - having the strong flavor or smell of wild animal meat
