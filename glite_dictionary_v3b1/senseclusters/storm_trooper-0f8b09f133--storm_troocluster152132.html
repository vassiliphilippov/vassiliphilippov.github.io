storm trooper

noun
member of the Nazi SA paramilitary group
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrm ˌtruːpər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːm ˌtruːpə / (BrE)
a member of the Nazi SA (Sturmabteilung), the paramilitary wing of the Nazi Party in Germany before and during World War II

Examples

Nazi storm trooper
Historically, storm trooper units played a crucial role in the rise of the Nazi party.
Many storm troopers participated in the violent events known as the Night of the Long Knives.
The storm troopers patrolled the streets, intimidating political opponents of the Nazi regime.
