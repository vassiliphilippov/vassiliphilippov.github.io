pin

noun
bowling target object
Pronunciation: / pɪn / (AmE), / pɪn / (BrE)
a slender, club-shaped wooden object used in bowling, set up in groups as a target to be knocked down by a ball

Examples

Bowling pin
After each frame, the machine resets the pins for the next player.
He knocked down every pin on his first roll, earning a strike.
The pins are arranged in a triangular formation at the end of the lane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowling ball - a heavy ball rolled at pins in the game of bowling
  • synonym: skittle - a similar pin used in the game of skittles
