pin

noun
bowling target object
4.2 1.2
Pronunciation: /pɪn/ (AmE), /pɪn/ (BrE)
a slender, club-shaped wooden object used in bowling, set up in groups as a target to be knocked down by a ball

Examples

The pins are arranged in a triangular formation at the end of the lane.
