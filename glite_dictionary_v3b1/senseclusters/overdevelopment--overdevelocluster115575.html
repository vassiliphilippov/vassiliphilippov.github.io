overdevelopment

noun
excessive building
5.6
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.dɪ.ˈvel.əp.mənt/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.dɪ.ˈvel.əp.mənt/ (BrE)
the process of building or expanding too much in an area, often causing crowding or environmental harm

Examples

Urban overdevelopment
The rapid overdevelopment of the coastal region has led to the loss of natural habitats.
Many people blame overdevelopment for the increased traffic and pollution in their neighborhood.
Local activists are campaigning against overdevelopment to protect the city's green spaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urban sprawl - the uncontrolled expansion of urban areas
