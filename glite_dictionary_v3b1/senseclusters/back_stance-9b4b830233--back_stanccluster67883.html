back stance

noun
martial arts stance with bent rear leg
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk ˌstæns / (AmE), / ˈbak ˌstæns / (BrE)
A stance used in martial arts where the rear leg is bent at the knee and the front leg is straight or slightly bent, providing stability and readiness for movement.

Examples

A back stance in karate
In karate, the back stance is essential for executing defensive moves effectively.
She shifted smoothly into a back stance before throwing a punch at her opponent.
The instructor corrected his position to ensure he had the proper form in the back stance.
