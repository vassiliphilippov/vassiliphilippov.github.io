intruding

adjective
projecting inward
1.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtruːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtruːdɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object or part) projecting inward into a space or cavity.

Examples

An intruding mass
A radiologist noted an intruding mass in the left lung lobe.
The intruding branch pressed against the window from outside.
The design was altered to avoid intruding corners into the walkway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protruding - projecting outward
