spatial arrangement

noun
the positioning of things in space
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪʃl əˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈspeɪʃl əˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
the way in which objects or elements are positioned and structured relative to each other in space

Examples

The spatial arrangement of objects
Biologists study the spatial arrangement of cells within tissues to understand organ function.
Good art often depends on the spatial arrangement of colors and shapes on the canvas.
The spatial arrangement of furniture can make a room feel larger or more cramped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layout - the way in which something is arranged, especially in regard to space
