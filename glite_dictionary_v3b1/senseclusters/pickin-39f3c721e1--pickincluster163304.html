pickin'

verb
to play by plucking
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪn / (BrE)
informal or dialectal form of 'picking', especially used to mean playing a stringed instrument by plucking or strumming

Examples

To pickin' a banjo
He spent the afternoon pickin' his guitar on the front porch.
You could hear her pickin' softly in the living room as the sun set.
They love pickin' bluegrass tunes together at family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: picking - playing a stringed instrument by plucking or strumming
  • synonym: strumming - playing a stringed instrument by sweeping the fingers or a pick across the strings
