ground floor

noun
floor at ground level of a building
1.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /graʊnd flɔːr/ (AmE), /graʊnd flɔː/ (BrE)
the floor of a building that is at or nearest to the level of the ground around the building

Examples

Ground floor apartment
Our apartment is on the ground floor, so it's easy to access from the street.
The museum's ground floor houses the main exhibition hall and gift shop.
They moved their elderly mother to the ground floor for easier mobility.

Related Terms
