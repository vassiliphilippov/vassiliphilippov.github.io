fantasyland

noun
unrealistic mindset
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfæntəsiˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈfæntəsiˌlænd / (BrE)
a state of mind where someone has unrealistic ideas or beliefs and ignores practical realities

Examples

Living in fantasyland
If you think you can finish all this work in one hour, you are living in fantasyland.
Politicians sometimes seem to operate in fantasyland instead of facing real problems.
Believing that money will solve all your problems is a sign of fantasyland thinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamworld - an imaginary or unrealistic world in someone's mind
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
