shipful

noun
amount to fill a ship
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpfʊl / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpfʊl / (BrE)
an amount or quantity that is enough to fill a ship

Examples

A shipful of grain
The port received a shipful of grain last week.
They exported a shipful of cars to Europe in a single voyage.
A shipful of tourists arrived on the island early this morning.
