anchorage

noun
state of being firmly secured
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋ.kɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.kər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
the condition or state of being firmly secured to a base or support

Examples

Strong anchorage needed
A lack of anchorage can lead to structural instability.
The surgeon ensured the implant had adequate anchorage in the bone.
Young trees require firm anchorage to withstand strong winds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachment - the act or state of attaching or being attached
  • synonym: security - the state of being safe or protected
