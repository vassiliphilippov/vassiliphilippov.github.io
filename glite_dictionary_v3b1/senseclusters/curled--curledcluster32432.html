curled

adjective
with hair shaped into curls
Pronunciation: / kɝld / (AmE), / kɜːld / (BrE)
(of hair) formed into curls or having been made to curl.

Examples

Curled hair
After the appointment at the salon, his once straight hair was perfectly curled.
She admired her curled hair in the mirror before leaving for the party.
The child’s curled locks bounced as she ran through the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curly - having curls or a curved shape, especially of hair
  • antonym: straight - (of hair) not curly or wavy
