unsifted

adjective
not filtered
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪftɪd / (BrE)
not carefully examined, checked, or filtered; not separated from unwanted parts or errors

Examples

Unsifted data
The report was based on unsifted information, which made its conclusions unreliable.
Using unsifted evidence in court can result in unfair judgments.
He warned that unsifted ideas might contain serious errors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: filtered - having been passed through a filter to remove unwanted parts
  • synonym: unchecked - not examined or controlled
  • synonym: unfiltered - not having been filtered or refined
