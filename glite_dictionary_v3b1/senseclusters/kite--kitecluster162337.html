kite

noun
a type of hawk; bird of prey
7.5
Pronunciation: / kaɪt / (AmE), / kaɪt / (BrE)
a small, graceful bird of prey of the hawk family, with long pointed wings, feeding chiefly on insects and small animals

Examples

A red kite bird
A red kite soared high above the countryside, searching for its next meal.
The conservation program successfully increased the number of kites in the region.
We spotted a graceful kite hovering effortlessly in the afternoon sky.

Related Terms
