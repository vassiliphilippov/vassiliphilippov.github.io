good-tempered

adjective
not easily annoyed; pleasant-natured
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈtɛmpɚd / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
Having a pleasant and cheerful disposition; not easily annoyed or made angry.

Examples

A good-tempered person
Even after waiting for hours, he remained remarkably good-tempered.
His good-tempered manner made him popular among his coworkers.
She is always good-tempered, no matter how stressful the situation becomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bad-tempered - easily annoyed or angered
  • synonym: even-tempered - calm and not easily angered
