turmeric

adjective
yellow–orange colour
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrmərɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːmərɪk / (BrE)
Having the warm yellow–orange colour of turmeric (the spice); used to describe shades in design, fashion, and branding.

Examples

Turmeric color paint
They painted the kitchen walls a deep turmeric to warm the small space.
She wore a turmeric dress to the exhibition and received many compliments.
The brand's logo used a muted turmeric tone that suggested warmth and approachability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mustard (colour) - a warm, slightly brownish yellow colour often used for fabrics and interiors
  • synonym: saffron (colour) - a vivid yellow–orange colour named after the saffron spice
