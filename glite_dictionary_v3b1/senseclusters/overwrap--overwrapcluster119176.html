overwrap

noun
protective outer wrapping
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌræp / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌræp / (BrE)

Examples

Plastic overwrap
The chocolate bars are sealed in foil and then covered with a plastic overwrap.
To keep the product fresh, an airtight overwrap is applied after packaging.
