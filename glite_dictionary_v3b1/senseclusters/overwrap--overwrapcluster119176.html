overwrap

noun
protective outer wrapping
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌræp / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌræp / (BrE)
a layer of material wrapped around a product or package for protection or presentation

Examples

Plastic overwrap
The chocolate bars are sealed in foil and then covered with a plastic overwrap.
To keep the product fresh, an airtight overwrap is applied after packaging.
The colorful overwrap made the gift box look more attractive on the shelf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packaging - materials used to wrap or protect goods
  • synonym: wrapper - a covering material for a product
