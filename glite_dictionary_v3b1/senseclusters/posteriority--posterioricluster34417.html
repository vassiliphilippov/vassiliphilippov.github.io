posteriority

noun
the state of coming after in time
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstəriˈɔrəti / (AmE), / ˌpɒstəriˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
The state or quality of occurring or coming after in time; later occurrence.

Examples

Posteriority of events
His findings proved the posteriority of the written document compared to the oral agreement.
In legal discussions, the posteriority of certain rights can influence the outcome of cases.
The posteriority of the second event made its impact even greater.
