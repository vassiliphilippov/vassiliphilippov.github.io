earth to

x
attention-getting phrase for the distracted
Pronunciation: / ɝːθ tu / (AmE), / ɜːθ tuː / (BrE)
used to get someone's attention when they seem distracted, often by calling their name as if making radio contact

Examples

Earth to someone
Earth to Sarah, are you paying attention to what I'm saying?
Earth to Tom! We're about to leave—are you coming or not?
He waved his hand, saying 'earth to Emma,' when she started daydreaming in class.
