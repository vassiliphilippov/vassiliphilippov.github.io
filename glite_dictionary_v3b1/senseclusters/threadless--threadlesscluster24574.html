threadless

adjective
without threads
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɛdləs / (AmE), / ˈθrɛdləs / (BrE)
lacking threads, especially in fabric, fasteners, or printed items

Examples

Threadless fabric
The new bicycle uses a threadless headset for easier adjustments and maintenance.
She preferred threadless earrings because they were more comfortable to wear all day.
The company introduced a threadless design for their reusable water bottles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seamless - without seams or obvious joins
  • antonym: threaded - having threads, especially for fastening or joining
