thou

noun
one thousand (money)
Pronunciation: / ðaʊ / (AmE), / ðaʊ / (BrE)
an informal way to refer to one thousand units, especially of money

Examples

Ten thou saved
He saved up ten thou to buy a new car.
The prize for first place is five thou in cash.
She borrowed two thou from her parents to pay her rent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grand - a slang term for one thousand dollars or pounds
  • synonym: k - an informal abbreviation for thousand, especially in money
