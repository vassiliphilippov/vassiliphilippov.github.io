Shakerism

noun
Shaker style or philosophy
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkərˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkərɪzəm / (BrE)
a style or philosophy emphasizing simplicity, utility, and plain craftsmanship, inspired by the beliefs and practices of the Shaker movement

Examples

Shakerism in design
The clean lines and functional design of the table reflect Shakerism in furniture making.
Many architects admire Shakerism for its focus on practicality and lack of unnecessary decoration.
The influence of Shakerism can be seen in modern minimalist interiors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minimalism - a style or approach that uses the fewest and simplest elements
  • antonym: ornateness - the quality of being highly decorated or elaborate
