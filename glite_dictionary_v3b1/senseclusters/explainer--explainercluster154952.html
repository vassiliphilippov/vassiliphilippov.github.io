explainer

noun
simple explanation piece
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspleɪnər / (AmE), / ɪkˈspleɪnə / (BrE)
a short article, video, or other media that explains a topic in a simple and clear way

Examples

An explainer article
The news site published an explainer to help readers understand the new tax rules.
I watched a short explainer about how electric cars work before buying one.
Many students found the explainer video useful for preparing for the exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guide - a document or video that helps people understand or do something
  • synonym: tutorial - a lesson or set of instructions teaching how to do something
