stone wall

noun
a fence made of rough stones
Pronunciation: / stoʊn wɑl / (AmE), / stəʊn wɔːl / (BrE)
a fence or barrier made by stacking rough stones, typically used to separate fields or mark boundaries

Examples

A stone wall fence
Moss grew thickly on the ancient stone wall along the country lane.
The old stone wall marked the boundary between the two farms.
They built a stone wall around the garden to keep the animals out.

  • synonym: wall - a continuous vertical brick or stone structure
