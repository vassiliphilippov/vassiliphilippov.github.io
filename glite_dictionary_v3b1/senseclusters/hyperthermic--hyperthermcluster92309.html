hyperthermic

adjective
excessively hot
8.4
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.ˈθɜːr.mɪk/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.ˈθɜː.mɪk/ (BrE)
having an abnormally high temperature, especially referring to objects, environments, or systems rather than living beings

Examples

Hyperthermic equipment
The laboratory equipment became hyperthermic after running continuously for several hours.
A hyperthermic environment can damage sensitive electronic devices if not properly cooled.
Engineers installed extra fans to prevent the system from becoming hyperthermic during peak operation.
