Skinnerian

adjective
relating to Skinner's behaviorist psychology
Pronunciation: / skɪˈnɛriən / (AmE), / skɪˈnɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of B. F. Skinner or his theory of behaviorist psychology

Examples

Skinnerian psychology
Her approach to therapy is rooted in Skinnerian theories of conditioning and response.
Many classrooms still use Skinnerian principles, such as reward and repetition, to shape student behavior.
The psychologist employed Skinnerian techniques to encourage positive behaviors in his patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: behaviorist - adhering to the psychological school of behaviorism
