inverted hang

noun
gymnastics move: hanging upside down
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɝtɪd hæŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɜːtɪd hæŋ / (BrE)
a gymnastic position performed by hanging upside down from rings or a bar, with the body fully extended or supported

Examples

Inverted hang on rings
During the routine, he transitioned smoothly from a standard hang to an inverted hang on the rings.
Practicing the inverted hang improves core and upper body strength.
The gymnast held an inverted hang for several seconds before dismounting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hang - to suspend oneself from the hands from a bar, rings, etc.
