espalier

noun
trained flat plant
Pronunciation: / ɛˈspæliər / (AmE), / ɛˈspalɪə / (BrE)
a plant, especially a fruit tree, trained to grow flat against a wall or frame in a decorative pattern

Examples

Apple tree espalier
The gardener carefully shaped the apple tree into an espalier along the garden wall.
Many vineyards use the espalier technique to maximize sunlight for their grapevines.
She admired the intricate espalier of pear trees lining the courtyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trellis - a framework of light wooden or metal bars used to support climbing plants
