devolutionary

adjective
about transferring power
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to the transfer of power or authority from a central body to a lower or local level

Examples

Devolutionary shift in power
The new law marked a devolutionary shift in the country's political structure.
Many regions supported the devolutionary reforms to gain more local control.
Critics argued that the devolutionary process could weaken national unity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralized - controlled by a central authority
  • synonym: devolved - having had power or authority transferred to a lower level
