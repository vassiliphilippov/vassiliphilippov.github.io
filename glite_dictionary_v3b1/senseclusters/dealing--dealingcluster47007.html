dealing

noun
act of conducting business transactions
3.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
the way someone acts or behaves when doing business or interacting with others, especially regarding fairness or honesty

Examples

Fair dealing with clients
He was known for his fairness in all dealing with clients and colleagues.
Honesty plays an important role in international dealing.
Their business dealing ended in mutual benefit for both parties.
Her reputation for dealing with clients fairly earned her many loyal customers.
In all his dealing with staff, he was known for his kindness and integrity.
The company's honest dealing set it apart from its competitors.
