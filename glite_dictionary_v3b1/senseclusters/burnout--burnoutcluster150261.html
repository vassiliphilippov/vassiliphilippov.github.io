burnout

noun
device failure from heat
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrnaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnaʊt / (BrE)
the failure of an electrical device or mechanical part due to overheating or excessive use

Examples

Lightbulb burnout
The sudden burnout of the generator left the hospital without power for an hour.
A burnout in the motor caused the machine to stop working unexpectedly.
Technicians replaced the fuse after a burnout damaged the circuit board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breakdown - a mechanical or electrical failure
  • synonym: malfunction - failure to function properly
