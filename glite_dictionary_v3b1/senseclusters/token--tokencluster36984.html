token

noun
something that serves as a sign or symbol
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.kən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.kən / (BrE)
something that represents or is a sign of a fact, feeling, event, or idea

Examples

Token of thanks
Please accept this card as a token of my gratitude for your help.
She gave him a token of her appreciation in the form of a small gift.
The medal was presented as a token of bravery by the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emblem - an object or image that represents an idea or quality
  • synonym: symbol - something that stands for or represents something else
