equipartition

noun
equal division
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwəpɑrˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪpɑːˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
the division of something into equal parts, especially the distribution of energy among components in physics

Examples

The equipartition law
The law of equipartition helps explain how energy is shared among molecules in a gas.
In statistical mechanics, equipartition is a key concept for understanding thermal equilibrium.
The teacher used the idea of equipartition to divide the class into equal groups for the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concentration - the action of focusing or gathering in one place
  • synonym: distribution - the way in which something is shared out among a group
