smarty

noun
clever or know-it-all person
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːti / (BrE)
a person who acts clever or like a know-it-all, often making smart or cheeky remarks that can annoy others

Examples

A smarty pants
Don't be such a smarty—let others have a chance to answer the question.
Everyone rolled their eyes when the smarty in class corrected the teacher again.
My little brother thinks he's a smarty because he finished the puzzle first.
He always acts like a smarty in class, making jokes when the teacher asks questions.
Nobody likes a smarty who interrupts with sarcastic comments all the time.
At the party, she played the smarty and made everyone laugh with her clever remarks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fool - a person who lacks good sense or judgment
  • synonym: know-it-all - someone who acts as if they know everything
  • synonym: smart aleck - a person who behaves as if they know everything
  • synonym: wisecracker - a person who makes witty or sarcastic remarks
