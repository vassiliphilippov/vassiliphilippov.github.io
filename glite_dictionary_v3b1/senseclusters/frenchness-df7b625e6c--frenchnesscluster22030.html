Frenchness

noun
quality or state of being French
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛntʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛntʃ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being French in character, culture, or identity.

Examples

Pride in Frenchness
He takes pride in his Frenchness, especially during international events.
Her Frenchness was evident from her elegant sense of style and love of good food.
The novel is praised for its strong sense of Frenchness in both setting and character.
