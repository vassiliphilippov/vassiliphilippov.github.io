loose

adjective
easily emptied (bowels/stools)
6.0 0.42/m
Pronunciation: / lus / (AmE), / luÀês / (BrE)
(of bodily waste or a bodily function) tending to empty easily or excessively, especially referring to bowels or stools.

Examples

Loose stools
After eating spoiled food, he experienced loose stools for several days.
Certain medications may cause loose bowels as a side effect.
Doctors recommend staying hydrated if you have loose movements.

Synonyms

  • lax
    7.9 0.08/m
    lax is a medical term used by doctors for bowels that empty too easily, while loose is the more common everyday word.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diarrhea - condition of having frequent and loose bowel movements
