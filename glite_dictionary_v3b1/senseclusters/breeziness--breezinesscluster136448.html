breeziness

noun
lively, cheerful quality
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːzinəs / (AmE), / ˈbriːzinɪs / (BrE)
a quality of being lively, cheerful, and relaxed in manner or style

Examples

Breeziness in conversation
Her breeziness put everyone at ease during the meeting.
The breeziness of her writing style made the article enjoyable to read.
There was a certain breeziness in his tone that made the conversation light and pleasant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liveliness - animated or energetic character; spiritedness
  • antonym: soberness - seriousness in manner or attitude
  • synonym: vivacity - the quality of being attractively lively and animated
