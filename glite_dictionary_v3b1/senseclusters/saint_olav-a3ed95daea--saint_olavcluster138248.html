Saint Olav

proper noun
King and patron saint of Norway
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈoʊˌlɑv / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈəʊˌlɑːv / (BrE)
King of Norway (995–1030) who became the country's patron saint after his death.
