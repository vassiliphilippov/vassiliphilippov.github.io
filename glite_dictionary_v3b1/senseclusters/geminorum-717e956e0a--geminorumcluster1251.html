the Latin genitive form used in the names of stars belonging to the constellation Gemini

Examples

Star Geminorum
The star Pollux is officially designated as Beta Geminorum in astronomical records.
Many stars in the constellation Gemini have names ending with Geminorum to show their association.
Astronomers refer to Castor as Alpha Geminorum when using its formal name.
