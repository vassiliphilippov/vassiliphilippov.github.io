Examples

Electric automobile battery
Many electric cars rely on a large lithium-ion automobile battery to drive the motor.
A damaged automobile battery reduced the vehicle's range, forcing the driver to stop and recharge.
Manufacturers guarantee their high-capacity automobile batteries for eight years or a set number of miles.
