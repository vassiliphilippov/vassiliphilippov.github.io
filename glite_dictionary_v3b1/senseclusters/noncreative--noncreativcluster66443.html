noncreative

noun
person lacking creativity
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kriˈeɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kriˈeɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
a person who lacks imagination or originality and does not show creativity, especially in work or ideas

Examples

A noncreative at work
Despite being called a noncreative, he contributed valuable insights to the project.
The company wanted to hire more innovators and fewer noncreatives for their new team.
She felt frustrated when her ideas were dismissed and she was labeled a noncreative.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creative - a person who is imaginative and original
  • synonym: unimaginative - lacking originality or creativity
