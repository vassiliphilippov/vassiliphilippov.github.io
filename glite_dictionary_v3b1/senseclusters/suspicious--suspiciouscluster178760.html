Examples

Suspicious of people

She grew suspicious of his evasive answers and refused to tell him anything personal.

Synonyms

    5.4 0.88/m

  • mistrustful
    5.2 0.23/m
    mistrustful expects people may be untrustworthy and keeps distance, while suspicious doubts others and avoids sharing private things.
  • untrusting
    4.9 0.11/m
    untrusting does not easily rely on others and stays guarded, while suspicious shows doubt and avoids close trust.
  • leery
    6.9 1.46/m

  • wary
    3.6 10/m
    wary moves carefully to avoid danger or risk, while suspicious mainly means not willing to trust other people's motives.
