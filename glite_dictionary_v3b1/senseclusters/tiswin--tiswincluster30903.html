tiswin

noun
Native American alcoholic drink
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪswɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɪswɪn / (BrE)
a traditional alcoholic drink made from fermented corn or fruit, especially prepared by some Native American peoples

Examples

Traditional tiswin drink
The Apache brewed tiswin for special ceremonies and gatherings.
During the festival, elders shared stories while drinking homemade tiswin around the fire.
Anthropologists studied the role of tiswin in traditional Native American rituals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beer - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grains
  • synonym: chicha - a traditional South American drink made from fermented corn
