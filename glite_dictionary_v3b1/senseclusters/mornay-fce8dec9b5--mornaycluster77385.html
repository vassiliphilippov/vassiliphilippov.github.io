Mornay

noun
cheese white sauce
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈneɪ / (AmE), / mɔːˈneɪ / (BrE)
a white sauce made with cheese, commonly used in cooking to add flavor to dishes

Examples

Mornay sauce recipe
The chef poured a rich Mornay over the steamed broccoli before serving.
She learned to make Mornay in her French cooking class last semester.
Gratin dishes often use Mornay to create a creamy, cheesy topping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: béchamel - a basic white sauce made from butter, flour, and milk
  • synonym: cheese sauce - a sauce made from cheese and milk or cream
