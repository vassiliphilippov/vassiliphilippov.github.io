subordinationist

adjective
favoring lower rank
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˌbɔːrdɪˈneɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / səˌbɔːdɪˈneɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
advocating or expressing the belief that one person or thing should be ranked lower or made subordinate to another

Examples

Subordinationist policy
The policy was criticized as subordinationist because it placed certain groups below others in the organization.
Her subordinationist views were at odds with the company's commitment to equal opportunity.
A subordinationist approach can lead to resentment among those who feel unfairly ranked.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - believing in the equality of all people
  • synonym: hierarchical - arranged in order of rank or authority
