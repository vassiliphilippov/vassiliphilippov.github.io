fairing

noun
Cornish spiced biscuit
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeərɪŋ / (BrE)
a small spiced biscuit, especially a traditional Cornish ginger biscuit often sold at fairs.

Examples

Cornish fairing biscuit
He bought a pack of fairings from the bakery while visiting Cornwall.
With her tea, she enjoyed a traditional Cornish fairing spiced with ginger and cinnamon.
The market stall was famous for its homemade fairings during the village fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biscuit - a small baked bread or cookie, especially in British English
  • synonym: gingerbread - a sweet, spiced cake or biscuit flavored with ginger
