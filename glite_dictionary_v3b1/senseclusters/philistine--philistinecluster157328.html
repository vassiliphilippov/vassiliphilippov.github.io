philistine

noun
person indifferent to culture or intellect
9.3 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…™l…™Àåstin / (AmE), / Ààf…™l…™stiÀên / (BrE)
a person who is indifferent to or has no appreciation for art, culture, or intellectual pursuits

Examples

A philistine at the museum
Despite living in a city full of museums, he remained a philistine who never visited them.
She called him a philistine when he dismissed her favorite novel as boring.
You don't have to be a philistine to dislike abstract art, but an open mind helps.

Synonyms

  • lowbrow
    8.1 0.08/m
    lowbrow rejects high art and prefers simple entertainment, while philistine is indifferent to culture and lacks appreciation for art.
  • anti-intellectual
    8.6 0.00/m
    anti-intellectual opposes intellectual activities and ideas, while philistine is simply indifferent to art, culture, or thoughtful pursuits.
