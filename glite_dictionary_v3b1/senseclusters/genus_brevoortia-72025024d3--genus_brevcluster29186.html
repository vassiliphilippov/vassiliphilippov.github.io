genus Brevoortia

noun
genus of fish (menhaden)
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs brɛˈvɔrt.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs briːˈvɔː.tɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of fish in the herring family, commonly known as menhaden, found along the Atlantic coast of North and South America.
