fluoxetine hydrochloride

noun
SSRI antidepressant medication
5.2
Pronunciation: / fluːˈɑːksətiːn ˌhaɪdroʊˈklɔːraɪd / (AmE), / fluːˈɒksətiːn ˌhaɪdrəʊˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
