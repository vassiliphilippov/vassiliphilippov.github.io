Synonyms

  • therefore
    3.2 70/m
    therefore is used in many contexts to state a clear result, while hence sounds slightly more formal or literary.
  • consequently
    3.4 4/m
    consequently sounds very formal and appears often in reports, while hence is shorter and finds use in writing and speech.
  • thus
    3.2 40/m
    thus is used to draw a conclusion or show a process in summaries, while hence directly marks a consequence.
  • accordingly
    3.3 7/m
    accordingly means acting in line with what was said or done and often guides actions, while hence simply states a conclusion.
