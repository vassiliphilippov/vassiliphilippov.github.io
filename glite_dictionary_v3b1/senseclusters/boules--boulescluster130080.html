boules

noun
balls for pétanque
Pronunciation: / bulz / (AmE), / buːlz / (BrE)
metal balls used in French games such as pétanque, thrown to land as close as possible to a target ball

Examples

Metal boules for a match
Each player selected their boules before the start of the pétanque match.
The shiny boules rolled across the gravel toward the small wooden target.
He practiced throwing his boules to improve his accuracy in the game.
