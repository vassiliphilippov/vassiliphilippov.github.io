tea cart

noun
wheeled cart for serving tea or food
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈti ˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˌkɑːt / (BrE)
A small wheeled cart used for serving tea, coffee, or light refreshments.

Examples

A tea cart with cups
After lunch, the tea cart was brought out, filled with fine china and homemade scones.

The waiter rolled the tea cart into the room, offering guests a selection of pastries and beverages.
