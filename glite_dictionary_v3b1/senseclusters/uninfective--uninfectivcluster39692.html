uninfective

adjective
not causing infection
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)

Examples

An uninfective virus

Doctors assured the public that the recovered patient was now uninfective and could return home.
After treatment, the bacteria became uninfective and posed no further threat.
