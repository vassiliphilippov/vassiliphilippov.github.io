Eton jacket

noun
waist-length, square-cut jacket (Eton College)
Pronunciation: / ˈiːtn̩ ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːtən ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a jacket that extends to the waist and is finished with a straight, square-cut bottom, traditionally worn by boys at Eton College

Examples

Classic Eton jacket
Boys dressed in the classic Eton jacket and tie are a familiar sight at the school.
He wore an Eton jacket to the formal school event, as was required by tradition.
The museum's exhibition featured an original Eton jacket from the nineteenth century.
