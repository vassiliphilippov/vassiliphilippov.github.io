tiptop

noun
the very highest point or degree
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈtɪpˌtɒp / (BrE)
the highest or utmost point, degree, or state; the pinnacle of achievement or development

Examples

Tiptop of her career
After years of hard work, she finally reached the tiptop of her profession.
This restaurant is considered the tiptop of fine dining in the city.
Winning the gold medal was the tiptop of his athletic career.

  • synonym: pinnacle - the most successful point; the culmination
  • synonym: summit - the highest point of a hill or achievement
