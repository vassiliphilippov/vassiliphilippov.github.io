wimp

noun
weak or timid person
2.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /wɪmp/ (AmE), /wɪmp/ (BrE)
a person who is weak, lacks self-confidence, or is easily frightened or indecisive

Examples

A total wimp

He was called a wimp for not speaking up in the meeting.
She refused to let anyone call her a wimp for being cautious.
