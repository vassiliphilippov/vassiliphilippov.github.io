exerciser

noun
person who works out
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksərˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who regularly does physical activity to improve or maintain fitness or health

Examples

Regular exerciser
The gym was filled with exercisers using treadmills and lifting weights early in the morning.
As a regular exerciser, she noticed improvements in her energy and mood.
Many exercisers prefer outdoor activities like running or cycling for variety.
