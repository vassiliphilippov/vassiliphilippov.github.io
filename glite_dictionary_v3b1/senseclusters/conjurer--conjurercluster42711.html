conjurer

noun
person who performs magic or conjury
6.5 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌn.dʒɜː.rər/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.dʒə.rə/ (BrE)
A person who performs magic or practices conjury, especially one believed to use supernatural powers as a witch doctor.

Examples

Tribal conjurer
Stories of a powerful conjurer who protected the tribe have been passed down for generations.
The conjurer performed secret rituals believed to ward off evil spirits.
The village conjurer was called upon to heal the sick child.

Related Terms
