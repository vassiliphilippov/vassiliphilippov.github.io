unlikable

adjective
not easy to like; evokes dislike
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlaɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪkəbl /; / ʌnˈlaɪkəbəl / (BrE)
not easy to like; having qualities that make people dislike someone or something

Despite his success, many found the CEO unlikable and distant.

The main character was so unlikeable that readers struggled to sympathize with his struggles.
