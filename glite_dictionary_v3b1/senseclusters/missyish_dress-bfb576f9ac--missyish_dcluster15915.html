missyish dress

noun
youthful, playful style of dress
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsi ɪʃ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɪsi ɪʃ drɛs / (BrE)
a dress that has a youthful, playful, and sometimes childlike style, resembling clothing for a young girl

Examples

A missyish dress style
She wore a missyish dress to the party, making her look years younger.
Some people find a missyish dress too childish for formal occasions.
The store specializes in missyish dresses with bright colors and playful patterns.
