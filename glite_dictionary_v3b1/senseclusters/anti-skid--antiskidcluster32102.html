anti-skid

noun
skid prevention system
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.tiː.skɪd/ /; / /ˈæn.taɪ.skɪd/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tɪ.skɪd/ /; / /ˈæn.tiː.skɪd/ (BrE)
a mechanical or electronic system in a vehicle designed to prevent the wheels from skidding, especially on slippery surfaces

Examples

Anti-skid system
The car's anti-skid activated automatically when it detected ice on the road.
Modern vehicles are often equipped with anti-skid to improve safety in wet conditions.
After the anti-skid was repaired, the driver felt more confident driving in the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-lock braking system - a system that prevents wheels from locking during braking
  • synonym: traction control - a system that helps prevent loss of traction of the wheels
