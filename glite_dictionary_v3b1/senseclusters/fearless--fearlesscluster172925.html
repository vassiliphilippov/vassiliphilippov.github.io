Synonyms

  • unafraid
    1.6 1.54/m
    unafraid does not feel fear in a situation, while fearless suggests bold courage without worrying about danger.
  • dauntless
    7.9 0.16/m
    dauntless is fearless and determined in danger, while fearless simply means not afraid of danger or difficulty.
  • intrepid
    7.9 1.64/m
    intrepid is adventurous and shows no fear in danger, while fearless focuses on lack of fear rather than adventure.
  • courageous
    3.3 7.18/m
    courageous shows strength to face fear or pain, while fearless implies not feeling fear in the same way.
  • brave
    1.0 25/m
    brave acts with courage in danger, while fearless stresses an absence of fear rather than bold feeling.
  • valiant
    5.3 0.36/m
    valiant shows great, often heroic courage in danger, while fearless simply means not feeling fear in hard situations.
  • bold
    2.7 14/m
    bold takes risks and may seem rude or confident, while fearless means lacking fear about danger or difficulty.
