shine

noun
brightness or glow of light

brightness or radiance produced by light; the quality of being bright and reflecting or emitting light.

Examples

A shine on shoes

  • brilliance
    4.5 1.07/m

  • sheen
    4.0 0.07/m

  • luminosity
    7.3 1.30/m

  • refulgence
