dandyism

noun
the style and manner of a dandy
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdæn.dɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈdæn.diː.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈdæn.dɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈdæn.diː.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
The style, manner, and attitudes characteristic of a dandy, especially marked by affected elegance in dress and manner.

Examples

Classic dandyism style
His obsession with tailored suits and polished shoes reflected a lifelong commitment to dandyism.
In the 19th century, dandyism was seen as a rebellion against social conventions.
The artist's flair for dandyism made him stand out at every gathering.
