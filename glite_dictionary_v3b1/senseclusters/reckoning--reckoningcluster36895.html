reckoning

noun
moment of judgment
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛkənɪŋ / (BrE)
a time when people are judged or must face the consequences of their actions

Examples

Day of reckoning
After years of corruption, the politician finally faced a reckoning in court.
Many believe that a reckoning is coming for companies that harm the environment.
The novel ends with a dramatic reckoning between the main characters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the act of pardoning someone for a mistake or wrongdoing
  • synonym: judgment - the act of forming an opinion or decision after careful thought
  • synonym: settlement - the act of resolving or paying a debt or dispute
