adversely

adverb
in a harmful or negative way
2.3
Pronunciation: / ædˈvɝːsli / (AmE), / ədˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
In a way that has a negative or harmful effect.

Examples

To be adversely affected
Many farmers were adversely affected by the drought this year.
New policies may adversely influence small businesses.
The company's reputation was adversely impacted by the scandal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: favorably - in a favorable manner, in a way that produces a positive effect
  • synonym: negatively - in a negative manner
