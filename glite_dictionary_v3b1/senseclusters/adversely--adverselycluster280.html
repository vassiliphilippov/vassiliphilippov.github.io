Synonyms

  • detrimentally
    7.2 0.04/m
    detrimentally causes harm in a clear, often formal way, while adversely is a general word for negative or harmful effects.
  • deleteriously
    9.0 0.01/m
    deleteriously sounds formal and often appears in medical or scientific writing, while adversely is common in general negative descriptions.
  • harmfully
    4.3 0.03/m
    harmfully describes actions that cause direct harm or injury, while adversely covers any negative or harmful effect more broadly.
  • damagingly
    4.4 0.01/m
    damagingly often paints a vivid image of physical damage or harm to someone's reputation, while adversely is a more general negative adverb.
  • negatively
    3.3 2/m
    negatively is common and can mean in a critical or opposite sense, while adversely specifically means causing harm.
  • disadvantageously
    7.4 0.00/m
    disadvantageously implies causing a loss or an unfavorable position, while adversely covers harm more generally and in many contexts.
