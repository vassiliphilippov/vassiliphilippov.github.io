cabildo

noun
church governing body
Pronunciation: / kəˈbiːldoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈbiːldəʊ / (BrE)
the governing body of clergy, such as priests or canons, responsible for administering a cathedral or collegiate church

Examples

Cathedral cabildo
The cabildo played a crucial role in organizing the cathedral's annual festival.
Members of the cabildo gathered to make decisions about church renovations.
The bishop consulted the cabildo before implementing new policies in the diocese.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chapter - the body of clergy in charge of a cathedral or collegiate church
  • synonym: parish council - a group managing the affairs of a parish church
