sponger

noun
person who lives off others
8.4 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈspʌn.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈspʌn.dʒə/ (BrE)
a person who persistently tries to gain advantage or support from others, often by taking without giving anything in return

Examples

Lazy sponger
After losing his job, he became a sponger who relied on his friends for meals.
His reputation as a sponger made it hard for him to make new friends.
No one wants to be seen as a sponger in a group project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benefactor - a person who gives help, especially financial help, to others
  • synonym: freeloader - a person who takes advantage of others' generosity without giving anything in return
  • synonym: parasite - a person who habitually relies on or exploits others and gives nothing in return
