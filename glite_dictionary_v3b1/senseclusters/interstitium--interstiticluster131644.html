interstitium

noun
space between cells
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈstɪ.ʃiː.əm/ /; / /ɪn.tər.ˈstɪ.ʃəm/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈstɪ.ʃiː.əm/ /; / /ɪn.tə.ˈstɪ.ʃəm/ (BrE)
the space or tissue between the cells or structures within an organ

Examples

Lung interstitium
Fluid can accumulate in the interstitium of the lungs during certain illnesses.
The interstitium plays a key role in the exchange of nutrients and waste between cells.
Researchers are studying how diseases affect the interstitium in different organs.
