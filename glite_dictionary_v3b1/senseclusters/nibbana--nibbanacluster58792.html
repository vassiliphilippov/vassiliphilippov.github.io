nibbana

noun
state of bliss
Pronunciation: / nɪˈbɑːnə / (AmE), / nɪˈbɑːnə / (BrE)
a state of profound peace, bliss, or calm, often used figuratively outside its strict Buddhist meaning

Examples

A quiet nibbana
After the long hike, she lay in the grass, tasting a quiet nibbana away from all noise.
He found his own nibbana in the peaceful solitude of the mountains.
For a moment, the world faded away and she experienced pure nibbana.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bliss - a state of great joy or happiness
  • synonym: nirvana - a state of perfect happiness or liberation
  • antonym: turmoil - a state of great disturbance or confusion
