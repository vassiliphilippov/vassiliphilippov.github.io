turnip-headed

adjective
foolish or dim-witted (of a person)
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.nɪp ˌhɛd.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.nɪp ˌhɛd.ɪd / (BrE)
Having foolish or dim-witted ideas or behavior; lacking intelligence or good judgment.

Examples

Turnip-headed mistake
His turnip-headed mistake cost the team a valuable point in the game.
She couldn't stand her manager's turnip-headed decisions during the project.
The turnip-headed driver caused a traffic jam by ignoring the road signs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dim-witted - lacking intelligence; slow to understand
  • synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
