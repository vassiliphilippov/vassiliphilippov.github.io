Mandan

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈmændæn / (AmE), / ˈmændæn / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people of North Dakota, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Mandan people
The Mandan are known for their earth lodges along the Missouri River.
She is studying the Mandan language as part of her research on Native American cultures.
Many Mandan traditions are still celebrated in North Dakota today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arikara - a Native American people historically living in North Dakota
  • synonym: Sioux - a group of Native American peoples of the northern Great Plains
