dotty

adjective
infatuated or obsessed with
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑti / (AmE), / ˈdɒti / (BrE)
infatuated with or extremely fond of someone or something; obsessed.

Examples

Dotty about music
Ever since he got that new game, he's been completely dotty over it.
My grandparents are still dotty about each other after fifty years of marriage.
She's absolutely dotty about classic movies and never misses a film festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy about - wildly enthusiastic or obsessed with someone or something
  • synonym: keen on - very much liking or interested in something or someone
