citrus

noun
a fruit like an orange or lemon
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪt.rəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪt.rəs / (BrE)
a fruit of the genus Citrus, such as an orange, lemon, lime, or grapefruit, characterized by a thick rind and juicy pulp, typically grown in warm regions.

Examples

A citrus fruit
She served a fresh citrus salad with oranges and grapefruits for breakfast.
The trees in their garden yield many types of citrus every year.
Vitamin C is abundant in citrus fruits like lemons and limes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemon - a yellow, oval citrus fruit with sour juice
  • synonym: orange - a round, sweet citrus fruit with a thick orange skin
