Adlerian

adjective
of Adler's psychology
9.8
Pronunciation: / ædˈlɪriən / (AmE), / ædˈlɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the psychological theories or methods developed by Alfred Adler, especially those emphasizing individual psychology and social interest

Examples

Adlerian therapy method
The counselor follows an Adlerian approach that focuses on social connections and personal growth.
Many modern therapists incorporate Adlerian principles into their treatment plans.
An Adlerian perspective emphasizes the importance of community and individual purpose.
