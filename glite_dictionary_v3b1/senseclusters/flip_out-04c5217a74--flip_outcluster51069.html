flip out

verb
react excitedly or in surprise
Pronunciation: / flɪp aʊt / (AmE), / flɪp aʊt / (BrE)
to react in an excited, delighted, or surprised way

Examples

To flip out with joy
He flipped out when he found out he had won the competition.
She nearly flipped out when she saw the surprise party waiting for her.
The kids flipped out over the news that school was canceled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freak out - to become very excited, angry, or afraid
