ill-shapen

adjective
badly formed; unattractive shape
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈʃeɪpən / (AmE), / ɪlˈʃeɪpən / (BrE)
Badly formed or out of shape, often to the point of being unattractive or ugly.

Examples

An ill-shapen hat
His ill-shapen hat barely clung to his head as he walked through the rain.
The ancient statue's features were ill-shapen from centuries of erosion.
The potter was disappointed by the ill-shapen vase that emerged from the kiln.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misshapen - badly shaped or deformed
  • antonym: well-formed - having a pleasing or proper shape
