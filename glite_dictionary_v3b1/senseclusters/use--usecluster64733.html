use

verb
exploit for personal advantage
1.0
Pronunciation: / juːz / (AmE), / juːz / (BrE)
to exploit a person, relationship, or resource as a means to achieve one's own goal or advantage, sometimes unfairly

Examples

To use a friend
He tried to use his friendship with the manager to get a promotion.
She felt betrayed when she realized she was being used for her connections.
Some people will use others for personal gain without any guilt.
