beekeeping

noun
the care and breeding of bees for honey
Pronunciation: / ˈbiˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of caring for and breeding bees to produce honey and other products

Examples

Beekeeping hobby
He took up beekeeping as a hobby after he retired.
Many people practice beekeeping to help support declining bee populations.
Modern beekeeping requires knowledge of bee biology and disease control.
