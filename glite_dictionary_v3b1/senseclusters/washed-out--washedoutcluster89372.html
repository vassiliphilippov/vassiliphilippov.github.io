washed-out

adjective
extremely tired; exhausted
Pronunciation: / wɑʃtˈaʊt / (AmE), / wɒʃtˈaʊt / (BrE)
Extremely tired or exhausted; lacking energy or effectiveness.

Examples

Washed-out worker
After working three double shifts, he looked completely washed-out and could barely stand.
By the end of the marathon, every runner appeared utterly washed-out.
She felt washed-out after spending the whole night caring for her sick child.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausted - Extremely tired; having no energy left
  • antonym: rested - Refreshed by rest; not tired
  • synonym: worn-out - Extremely tired or damaged from overuse
