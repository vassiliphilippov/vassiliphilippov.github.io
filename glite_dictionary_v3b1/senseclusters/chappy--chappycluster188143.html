chappy

adjective
rough or cracked
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃæpi / (BrE)
rough, cracked, or chapped, especially referring to skin or lips affected by cold, wind, or dryness

Examples

Chappy lips
After hours in the wind his lips were chappy and painful to the touch.
The cold winter air left her hands feeling dry and chappy.
He applied lip balm to soothe his chappy lips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chapped - rough and cracked, especially due to dryness or cold
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and unbroken surface; not rough or cracked
