autonomically

adverb
by autonomic control
Pronunciation: / ɔˌtɑːnəˈmɪkli / (AmE), / ɔːˌtɒnəˈmɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or controlled by the autonomic nervous system, without conscious effort

Examples

React autonomically
The pupils of the eyes adjust autonomically to changes in light.
Many bodily functions, such as digestion, occur autonomically without our awareness.
Her heart rate increased autonomically in response to the sudden fright.

Related Terms

  • synonym: involuntarily - without conscious control or intention
