darshana

noun
spiritual vision
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːrʃənə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːʃənə / (BrE)
a spiritually significant sight, vision, or the act of seeing or being seen by a deity or holy person in Indian traditions

Examples

Temple darshana
Pilgrims traveled for days to receive darshana of the sacred statue in the temple.
The guru granted darshana to his followers during the morning ceremony.
Many people seek darshana at holy sites to feel spiritually connected.
Pilgrims traveled for days to receive darshana at the sacred temple.
The devotees waited patiently for their turn to have darshana of the holy sage.
Many believe that a single darshana can bring peace and good fortune.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the act of becoming visible or being seen
  • synonym: audience - a formal meeting with an important person
  • synonym: blessing - a prayer or act of asking for divine favor
  • synonym: vision - a sight or something seen, often with spiritual meaning
