contour

noun
makeup technique to shape the face
4.2
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtʊr/ /; / /ˈkɑːn.tʊr/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.tʊə/ /; / /kən.ˈtʊə/ (BrE)
a makeup technique (or the effect produced by it) that uses darker and lighter cosmetics to shape, define, or emphasise facial features such as cheekbones and the nose.

Examples

Face contour makeup
She perfected her contour to accentuate cheekbones for the magazine photo shoot.
The makeup artist taught them how to create a natural-looking contour with cream and powder.
Many beauty influencers post videos showing step-by-step tips for a dramatic contour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contouring - the act or process of applying cosmetics to define the shape of the face
  • synonym: highlighting - a makeup technique using light-coloured cosmetics to bring forward or emphasise parts of the face
