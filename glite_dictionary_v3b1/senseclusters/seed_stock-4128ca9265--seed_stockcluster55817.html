seed stock

noun
seeds or tubers reserved for planting
3.6 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / sid st…ëk /; / siÀêd st…ëk / (AmE), / siÀêd st…ík / (BrE)
seeds or tubers that are kept or selected for planting future crops

Examples
