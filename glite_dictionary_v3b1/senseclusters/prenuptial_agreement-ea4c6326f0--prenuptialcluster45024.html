prenuptial agreement

noun
marriage contract about assets before wedding
6.1 0.56/m
Pronunciation: / priÀàn åp É…ôl …ôÀà…°riÀêm…ônt / (AmE), / priÀàn åp.t Éu.…ôl …ôÀà…°riÀê.m…ônt / (BrE)
a legal contract made by a couple before marriage that outlines the ownership of their respective assets should the marriage fail

Examples

Sign a prenuptial agreement
Many couples decide to sign a prenuptial agreement to protect their individual assets.
The lawyer explained the benefits and limitations of a prenuptial agreement before the wedding.
Their prenuptial agreement specified how property would be divided if they divorced.

Synonyms

  • ante-nuptial agreement
    8.4 0.00/m
    an ante-nuptial agreement is the formal legal term used in documents, while a prenuptial agreement is the usual modern name couples use.
  • prenup
    5.4 0.97/m
    prenup is the short, informal word people say in speech, while prenuptial agreement is the full legal phrase used in law or papers.
  • marriage contract
    3.6 0.35/m

  • marriage settlement
    6.4 0.01/m
    a marriage settlement is an older formal agreement often about long-term finances, while a prenuptial agreement is the modern pre-marriage asset plan.
