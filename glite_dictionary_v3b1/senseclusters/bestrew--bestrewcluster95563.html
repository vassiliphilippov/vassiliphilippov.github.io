bestrew

verb
scatter or cover by strewing
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈstru / (AmE), / bɪˈstruː / (BrE)
to cover or scatter (a surface) with things, typically by spreading them around

Examples

To bestrew papers
Her desk was bestrewn with notes and papers by the end of the day.
Petals were bestrewn along the aisle for the wedding procession.
The ground was bestrewn with fallen leaves after the storm.
