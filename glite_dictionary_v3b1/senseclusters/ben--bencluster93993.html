ben

noun
inner room (Scotland)
Pronunciation: / bɛn / (AmE), / bɛn / (BrE)
a Scottish term for the inner room or parlor of a house

Examples

The ben room
She invited us into the ben for tea after dinner.
The children played quietly in the ben while the adults talked in the kitchen.
In old Scottish cottages, the ben was often the warmest and most comfortable room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: but - the outer room in a traditional Scottish house
  • synonym: parlor - a sitting room in a private house
