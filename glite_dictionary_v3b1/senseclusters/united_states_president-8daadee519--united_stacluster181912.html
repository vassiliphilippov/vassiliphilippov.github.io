United States President

noun
head of state and government of the United States
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnəˈtaɪtɪd steɪts ˈprɛzɪdənt / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtɪd steɪts ˈprɛzɪdənt / (BrE)
the elected head of state and government of the United States of America, responsible for overseeing the executive branch and serving as the chief representative of the nation
