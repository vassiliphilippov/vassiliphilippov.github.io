longhair

noun
animal with long fur
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌheə / (BrE)
an animal, especially a cat or rabbit, that has long fur or a long coat

Examples

A fluffy longhair
The shelter had many playful kittens, but the fluffy gray longhair attracted the most attention.
She prefers adopting a longhair because of their beautiful, soft coats.
Grooming a longhair requires more effort than caring for a short-haired pet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Persian - a breed of long-haired cat
  • antonym: shorthair - an animal, especially a cat, with short fur
