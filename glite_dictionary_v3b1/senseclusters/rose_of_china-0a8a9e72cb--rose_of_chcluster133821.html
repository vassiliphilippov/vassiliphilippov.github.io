Rose of China

noun
shrubby Asian plant with large red flowers
6.4
Pronunciation: / roʊz əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / rəʊz əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
A large, showy shrub or small tree native to Asia, especially valued for its large single or double red to deep-red flowers.
