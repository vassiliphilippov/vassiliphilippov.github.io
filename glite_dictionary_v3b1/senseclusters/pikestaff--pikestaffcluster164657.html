pikestaff

noun
shaft of a pike weapon
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪkˌstæf / (AmE), / ˈpaɪk.stɑːf / (BrE)
The long wooden shaft or staff of a pike, a type of spear formerly used in warfare.

Examples

The pikestaff shaft
