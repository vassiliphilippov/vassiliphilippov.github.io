afebrile

adjective
not having a fever
Pronunciation: / eɪˈfiːbraɪl / (AmE), / əˈfiːbraɪl / (BrE)
not having a fever; free from fever.

Examples

An afebrile patient
Although he felt unwell, the child was afebrile during the examination.
Most patients with this condition are afebrile when first admitted.
The patient remained afebrile throughout her hospital stay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: febrile - having or showing symptoms of a fever
