capri

noun
mid-calf pants
Pronunciation: / kəˈpri / (AmE), / kəˈpriː / (BrE)
a style of close-fitting pants that end below the knee and above the ankle

Examples

A pair of capri pants
She bought a new pair of capri pants for her summer vacation.
Many people prefer wearing capri pants when the weather is warm but not hot.
His capri pants were comfortable for walking along the beach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shorts - short pants that end above the knee
  • synonym: trousers - an outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, with a separate part for each leg
