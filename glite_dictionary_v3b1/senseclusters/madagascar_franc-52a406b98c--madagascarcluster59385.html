Madagascar franc

noun
former currency of Madagascar
Pronunciation: / ˌmædəˈɡæskər ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / ˌmædəˈɡæskə ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency formerly used in Madagascar before being replaced by the Malagasy ariary in 2005

Examples

The Madagascar franc currency
Before 2005, people in Madagascar used the Madagascar franc for all transactions.
Collectors often seek old Madagascar francs as rare pieces from Africa’s monetary history.
The value of the Madagascar franc steadily declined before it was replaced by the ariary.
