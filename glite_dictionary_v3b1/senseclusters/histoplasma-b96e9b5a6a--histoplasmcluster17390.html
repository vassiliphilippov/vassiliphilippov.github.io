Histoplasma

proper noun
fungus genus
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstoʊˈplæzmə / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəʊˈplæzmə / (BrE)
a genus of fungi that can cause disease in humans and animals, especially lung infections

Examples

Histoplasma fungus infection
Doctors identified Histoplasma as the cause of the patient's respiratory illness.
Soil contaminated with bird droppings can contain Histoplasma spores.
Researchers are studying how Histoplasma infects both humans and animals.

  • synonym: histoplasmosis - a lung infection caused by Histoplasma fungi
