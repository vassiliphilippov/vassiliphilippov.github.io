obscenely

adverb
to an excessive or offensive degree
8.2
Pronunciation: / əbˈsinli / (AmE), / əbˈsiːnli / (BrE)
to a shockingly excessive or offensive degree

Examples

Obscenely rich
She was obscenely wealthy, owning multiple mansions around the world.
Some CEOs are paid obscenely high salaries compared to their employees.
The dessert was obscenely large, easily enough for four people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excessively - to a greater than normal or acceptable degree
  • antonym: moderately - to a moderate or reasonable degree
  • synonym: outrageously - to an extremely shocking or unacceptable degree
