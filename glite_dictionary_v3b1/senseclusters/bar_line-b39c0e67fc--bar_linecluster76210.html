bar line

noun
vertical line separating bars in music
2.3 2.8
Pronunciation: /bɑːr laɪn/ (AmE), /bɑː laɪn/ (BrE)
a vertical line on a musical staff that marks the boundary between bars or measures

Examples

A bar line in music
Each measure in the composition is separated by a bar line.
She mistakenly played through the bar line without pausing.
The conductor pointed to the bar line where the musicians should stop.
