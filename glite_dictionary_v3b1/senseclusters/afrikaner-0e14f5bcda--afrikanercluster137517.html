Afrikaner

noun
South African of Dutch descent, Afrikaans speaker
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæfrɪˈkɑnər / (AmE), / ˌafrɪˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
A member of a South African ethnic group descended mainly from Dutch settlers, whose first language is Afrikaans.

Examples

An Afrikaner family
During the 20th century, the Afrikaner community played a significant role in South African politics.
Many Afrikaner farmers own land that has been passed down for generations.
She is an Afrikaner and speaks Afrikaans at home with her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Afrikaans - The language spoken by Afrikaners
  • synonym: Boer - A South African of Dutch descent, especially a farmer
