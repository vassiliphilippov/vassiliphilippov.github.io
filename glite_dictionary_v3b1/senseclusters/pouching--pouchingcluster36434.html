pouching

noun
packaging in pouches
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.tʃɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.tʃɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of filling and sealing products into flexible pouches, especially in packaging or food manufacturing

Examples

Pouching snack foods
The company invested in new machines to speed up pouching for snack foods.
Strict hygiene standards are followed during the pouching of ready-to-eat meals.
Advances in pouching technology have made single-serve packaging more popular.
