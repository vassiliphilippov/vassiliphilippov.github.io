letter

noun
literal meaning of a rule or law
3.0 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
The precise, literal meaning of a rule, law, or instruction, as opposed to its spirit or intention.

Examples

The letter of the law
As a judge, he interprets contracts according to the letter of the law.
She insisted on following the instructions to the letter, even if it seemed unnecessary.
The company's policy must be obeyed to the letter to avoid penalties.
