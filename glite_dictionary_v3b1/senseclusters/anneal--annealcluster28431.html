anneal

verb
apply simulated annealing
Pronunciation: / əˈnil / (AmE), / əˈniːl / (BrE)
(in computing and optimization) to apply the simulated annealing algorithm to a problem or candidate solution in order to search for or approximate a global optimum.

Examples

To anneal a solution
Researchers anneal the solution using simulated annealing to escape local optima.
During tuning, the algorithm annealed thousands of candidate configurations to improve performance.
Their solver kept annealing the model parameters until the improvement between steps was negligible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimize - to make a system, design, or decision as effective or functional as possible; to search for the best solution
  • synonym: simulated annealing - a probabilistic optimization technique that mimics the cooling process of metallurgy to escape local optima
