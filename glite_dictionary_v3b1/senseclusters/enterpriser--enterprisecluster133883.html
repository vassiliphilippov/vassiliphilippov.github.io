enterpriser

noun
person who starts and manages a business
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntɚˌpraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɛntəˌprʌɪzə / (BrE)
a person who organizes and manages a business venture, taking on financial risks in the hope of profit

Examples

An enterpriser starts business
As an experienced enterpriser, she was always looking for new investment opportunities.
The enterpriser faced many challenges but remained determined to succeed.
The city honored the enterpriser for creating jobs in the local community.
