skyscape

noun
view of the sky
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a view or artistic representation of the sky, especially as seen in a painting or photograph

Examples

A skyscape painting

Photographers often wait for the perfect moment to shoot a dramatic skyscape at dawn.
Clouds and sunlight combined to create a breathtaking skyscape over the city.
