Frenchie

noun
French Bulldog; French person
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛntʃi / (AmE), / ˈfrɛntʃi / (BrE)
an informal term for a French Bulldog or, sometimes, a French person

Examples

A playful Frenchie dog
Her Frenchie loves to play in the park every morning.
Many people adore the playful nature of a Frenchie as a family pet.
He joked that he was a proud Frenchie after moving from Paris.
