Synonyms

  • tailfin
    5.4 0.02/m
    tailfin is the single-word form often used in writing or on labels, while tail fin is the common two-word aircraft term.
  • vertical fin
    4.3 0.02/m
    vertical fin is a technical name for the upright stabilizing surface, while tail fin is a more general, everyday term.
  • vertical stabilizer
    5.9 0.01/m
    vertical stabilizer is the formal engineering term used in manuals and reports, while tail fin is more casual and visible on the airplane.
  • vertical tail
    6.0 0.02/m
    vertical tail is the whole upright tail section often including several surfaces, while tail fin names the single vertical stabilizing surface.
