aboriginal

adjective
relating to original inhabitants
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənl /; / ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənəl / (AmE), / ˌabəˈrɪdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to people or things that have existed in a region from the earliest times; indigenous or original

Examples

Aboriginal culture
Some aboriginal tribes still live according to traditional customs.
The aboriginal people of the region have a rich cultural heritage.
The study focused on the aboriginal languages of North America.
Scientists study aboriginal rock formations to understand the Earth's early history.
Some species are considered aboriginal to this remote island, having evolved in isolation.
The region is covered in aboriginal forests dating back thousands of years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indigenous - originating or occurring naturally in a place; native
  • synonym: primeval - belonging to the earliest ages
  • synonym: primordial - existing at or from the beginning of time
