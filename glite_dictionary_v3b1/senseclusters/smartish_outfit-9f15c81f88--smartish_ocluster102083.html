smartish outfit

noun
somewhat stylish or formal outfit
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑrtɪʃ ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtɪʃ ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
An outfit that is somewhat stylish or slightly formal, but not fully smart or dressy.

Examples

A smartish outfit for events
A jacket and neat trousers made his smartish outfit perfect for the occasion.
She chose a smartish outfit for the dinner party, balancing comfort and style.
You don't need a suit, just wear a smartish outfit to the event.
