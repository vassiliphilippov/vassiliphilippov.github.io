countable

adjective
able to be counted
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəbl / (BrE)
able to be counted; existing in a quantity that can be individually enumerated.

Examples

A countable number
He found solace in his few countable mistakes rather than countless regrets.
Some nouns in English are countable, like 'apple' or 'book'.
The loan was given only against countable assets as collateral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: numerable - that can be numbered; countable
  • antonym: uncountable - not able to be counted; too numerous or too great to be counted
