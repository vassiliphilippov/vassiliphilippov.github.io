undrilled

adjective
not trained by drills
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdrɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɪld / (BrE)
not having been drilled, practiced, or prepared through drills or training

Examples

The undrilled recruits
The undrilled recruits struggled with the basic routines.
Because the team was undrilled, they made several mistakes during the emergency drill.
The undrilled students found it difficult to follow the marching instructions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drilled - trained or practiced through repeated exercises
  • synonym: untrained - not having received training or practice
