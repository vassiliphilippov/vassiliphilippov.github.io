transplant

verb
be able to survive after being moved
Pronunciation: / trænˈsplænt / (AmE), / trænˈsplɑːnt / (BrE)
(of a plant or other living thing) to be able to survive or continue growing after being moved from one place to another.

Examples

Cacti transplant well
Cacti can transplant successfully if their roots remain undisturbed.
Some trees simply do not transplant well and may not survive the move.
These seedlings usually transplant with little difficulty when handled carefully.

  • synonym: survive - continue to live or exist
