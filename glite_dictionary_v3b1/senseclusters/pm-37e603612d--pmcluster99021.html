P.M.

adverb
time after noon and before midnight
1.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåpiÀê Àà…õm / (AmE), / ÀåpiÀê Ààem / (BrE)
The period of time from noon until midnight; used to indicate time after midday.

Examples

At 3 P.M.
My flight departs at 3 P.M. tomorrow afternoon.
She usually finishes work by 6 P.M. each day.
The concert will start at 7 P.M. sharp.

Synonyms

  • PM
    1.0 0.00/m
    PM is the common, unpunctuated label for afternoon and evening times on clocks, while P.M. is the punctuated form used in writing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: A.M. - the period from midnight to noon
