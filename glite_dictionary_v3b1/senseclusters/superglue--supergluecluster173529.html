superglue

noun
strong fast adhesive
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌɡluː / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌɡluː / (BrE)
a very strong, quick-drying adhesive used to bond materials together permanently

Examples

A drop of superglue
He accidentally got superglue on his fingers and they stuck together.
The model airplane was repaired using a drop of superglue on the broken wing.
Always be careful when using superglue because it bonds skin instantly.
