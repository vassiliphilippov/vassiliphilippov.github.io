snooze

verb
sleep lightly for a short time
2.1 0.58/m
Pronunciation: / snuÀêz / (AmE), / snuÀêz / (BrE)
to sleep lightly or for a short period of time, often during the day or unintentionally

Examples

To snooze on the couch
I like to snooze on the couch after lunch.
She snoozed through most of the movie last night.
The cat is snoozing in a patch of sunlight.
