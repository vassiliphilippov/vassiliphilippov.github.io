hulk

verb
to loom large or dominate in appearance
6.7
Pronunciation: / hʌlk / (AmE), / hʌlk / (BrE)
to appear large, heavy, and imposing, often looming over or dominating the surroundings

Examples

Mountains hulk above
A mountain hulks above the valley, dominating the entire landscape.
Dark clouds hulked on the horizon as the storm approached.
The old factory hulked over the deserted streets, casting a long shadow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loom - to appear as a large, indistinct, and often threatening shape
