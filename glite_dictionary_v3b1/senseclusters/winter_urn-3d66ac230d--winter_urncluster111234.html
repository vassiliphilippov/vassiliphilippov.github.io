winter urn

noun
a fungus species (Urnula hiemalis)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ɝn / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ɜːn / (BrE)
A species of cup fungus (Urnula hiemalis) of the Sarcosomataceae family, typically found in early spring or winter on decaying wood.

Examples

The winter urn fungus
Researchers studied the ecological role of the winter urn in decomposing deadwood during winter.
The winter urn is recognizable by its dark, urn-shaped fruiting body and is often spotted in cooler seasons.
We discovered a cluster of winter urns growing on the decaying log near the riverbank.
