Intelligence Community

The group of U.S. government agencies and organizations responsible for carrying out intelligence activities, coordinated under the Director of National Intelligence.

Examples

The Intelligence Community agencies

Synonyms

  • United States Intelligence Community
    4.7 0.01/m
    United States Intelligence Community is the formal full name used in official documents and reports, while Intelligence Community is the shorter common term.
  • National Intelligence Community
    4.6 0.00/m
    National Intelligence Community names U.S. agencies led by the Director of Central Intelligence, while Intelligence Community is coordinated under the Director of National Intelligence.
