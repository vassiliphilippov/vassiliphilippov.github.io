Ligol

noun
variety of apple developed in Poland
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.ɡɔl / (AmE), / ˈliː.ɡɒl / (BrE)
A variety of apple developed in Poland, known for its firm, compact flesh and typically harvested in October.

Examples

The Ligol apple
Orchards in central Poland are famous for producing the Ligol apple.
She uses Ligol apples to bake a delicious autumn pie.
The Ligol is prized for its firm texture and sweet, slightly tart flavor.
