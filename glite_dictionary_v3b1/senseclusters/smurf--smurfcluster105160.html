smurf

verb
to use alternate account
9.9
Pronunciation: /smɜːrf/ (AmE), /smɜːf/ (BrE)
to play a video game using a low-level or alternate account to face less skilled opponents or hide one's true skill level

Examples

To smurf in games
Some players smurf to avoid tough competition and win matches more easily.
He was accused of trying to smurf in the tournament by using a new account.
Game developers are working to prevent users from smurfing in ranked matches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheat - to act dishonestly or unfairly to gain an advantage
  • antonym: play fair - to compete honestly and according to the rules
