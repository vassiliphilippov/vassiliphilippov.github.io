overlooking

adjective
having a view from above
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a place or position) having a view over something from a higher position.

Examples

Overlooking the river
Our apartment is on a hill overlooking the river.
The hotel has a beautiful terrace overlooking the city skyline.
They enjoyed breakfast on the balcony overlooking the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commanding - (of a position) offering a wide or impressive view
  • synonym: dominating - towering over and providing a view of something below
