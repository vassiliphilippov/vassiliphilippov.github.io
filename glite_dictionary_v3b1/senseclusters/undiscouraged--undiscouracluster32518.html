undiscouraged

adjective
not deterred; not discouraged
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈskɝːɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈskʌrɪdʒd / (BrE)
not having lost confidence or enthusiasm; not deterred or put off by disappointment or difficulty.

Examples

Undiscouraged by failure
Despite criticism from others, the artist continued undiscouraged with his work.
He walked into the interview undiscouraged by his previous rejections.
She remained undiscouraged even after several failed attempts to solve the problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouraged - having lost confidence or enthusiasm; made less hopeful
  • synonym: undaunted - not intimidated or discouraged by difficulty, danger, or disappointment
  • synonym: undeterred - not discouraged or prevented from acting
