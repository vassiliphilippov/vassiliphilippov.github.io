anna

noun
former coin and money unit of South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈænə / (AmE), / ˈænə / (BrE)
a former monetary unit and copper coin of Pakistan and India, equal to one sixteenth of a rupee.

Examples

An old anna coin
Before decimalization, transactions in India commonly involved the anna.

The price of a cup of tea was just one anna in their childhood days.
