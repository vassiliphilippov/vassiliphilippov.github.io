piker

noun
petty or insignificant person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪkə / (BrE)
a person regarded as petty, insignificant, or contemptibly small-minded, often used as an insult.

Examples

A petty piker
They called him a piker for refusing to contribute to the group gift.
No one took the piker seriously during the meeting because of his negative attitude.
She was unfairly labeled a piker just because she preferred to save her money.

Related Terms

  • antonym: big shot - an important or influential person
  • synonym: cheapskate - a person who is unwilling to spend money
