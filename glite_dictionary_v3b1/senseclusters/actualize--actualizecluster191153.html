actualize

verb
make real or concrete
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tʃu.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaktjʊəlʌɪz /; / ˈaktjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something real or give it actual existence; to make concrete or real.

Examples

To actualize a plan
New policies are needed to actualize the changes promised during the campaign.
She worked hard to actualize her childhood dreams of becoming an artist.
The company hopes to actualize its vision of a sustainable future by 2030.
She worked tirelessly to actualise her ambitious vision for the company.
The organization seeks to actualise social change through community projects.
Their dreams began to actualise after years of hard work and perseverance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: materialize - to become actual fact; to happen or appear
  • synonym: realize - to make real, to bring into existence
