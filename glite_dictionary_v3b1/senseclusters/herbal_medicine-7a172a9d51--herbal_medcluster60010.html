herbal medicine

noun
medicine made from plants
3.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ùÀêb…ôl Ààm…õd…™s…ôn / (AmE), / Ààh…úÀêb…ôl Ààm…õd…™s…ôn / (BrE)
A substance or preparation made from plants and used to prevent or treat disease or to promote general health.

Examples

A herbal medicine remedy
Many people use herbal medicine as a complementary therapy in addition to conventional treatments.
Research on the effectiveness of herbal medicine has increased significantly in recent years.
She visited an expert in herbal medicine to find natural remedies for her allergies.

Synonyms

  • botanical
    6.7 0.40/m
    botanical is a plant derived substance often used in formal medical products or studies, while herbal medicine is the general name for plant based treatments.
  • herbal concoction
    5.8 0.01/m
    herbal concoction is a simple mixed remedy often homemade for one illness, while herbal medicine covers wider plant based treatments for health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conventional medicine - the standard medical practices using drugs and surgery
  • synonym: phytotherapy - the treatment of disease by the use of plant extracts
