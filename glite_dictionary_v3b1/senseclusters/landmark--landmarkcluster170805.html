landmark

noun
important turning-point or event
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈlændˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an event or development that marks an important stage or turning point in history or progress

Examples

Landmark decision
Her election as the first female president was a landmark event for the country.
The Supreme Court decision was a landmark in civil rights history.
The discovery marked a landmark in medical research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milestone - an important event marking a significant stage or development
  • synonym: watershed - a critical turning point or dividing line in events
