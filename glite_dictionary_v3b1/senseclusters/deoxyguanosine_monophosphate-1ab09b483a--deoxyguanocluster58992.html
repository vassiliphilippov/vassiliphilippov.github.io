deoxyguanosine monophosphate

noun
a nucleotide building block of DNA
6.2 8.6
Pronunciation: / diˌɑksiˌɡwɑnəˌsin ˌmɑnoʊˌfɑsˌfeɪt / (AmE), / diˌɒksiˌɡwɑːnəˌsiːn ˌmɒnəˌfæsˌfeɪt / (BrE)
