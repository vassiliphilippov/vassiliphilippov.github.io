chromic

adjective
containing chromium(III)
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊmɪk / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊmɪk / (BrE)
containing or relating to chromium, especially when chromium has a valence of three

Examples

Chromic suture material
The surgeon used chromic sutures for the procedure to ensure proper healing.
A chromic compound was added to the solution to test its chemical properties.
Researchers studied the effects of chromic salts in industrial processes.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chromate - a compound containing chromium in a higher oxidation state
  • degree lower: chromous - containing chromium with a valence of two
