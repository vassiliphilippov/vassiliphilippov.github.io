burglary

noun
illegal entry to commit a crime
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːɡləri / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡləri / (BrE)
The act of entering a building illegally with the intent to commit a crime, especially theft.

Examples

A burglary at home
After the burglary, we installed a new alarm system in our house.
He was charged with burglary when he entered the building with the intention to steal.
The police arrested him for burglary after he was caught breaking into the store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robbery - the act of taking property unlawfully from a person or place by force or threat
  • synonym: theft - the act of stealing
