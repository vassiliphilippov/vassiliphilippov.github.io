The act of entering a building illegally with the intent to commit a crime, especially theft.

Examples

A burglary at home
After the burglary, we installed a new alarm system in our house.
He was charged with burglary when he entered the building with the intention to steal.
The police arrested him for burglary after he was caught breaking into the store.

Synonyms

  • breaking and entering
    5.6 0.6/m
    breaking and entering is a legal phrase for entering by force to commit theft, while burglary names the crime of unlawful entry with criminal intent.
  • break-in
    3.9 2/m
    a break-in is the everyday term for an illegal entry to steal, while burglary is the formal name for that crime.
  • housebreaking
    8.2 0.02/m
    housebreaking is illegal entry specifically into a house to commit a crime, while burglary can apply to unlawful entry of any building.
