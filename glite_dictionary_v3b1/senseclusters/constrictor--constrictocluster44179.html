constrictor

noun
large snake that kills by crushing
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrɪktər / (AmE), / kənˈstrɪktə / (BrE)
a large nonvenomous snake that kills its prey by coiling around and crushing it

Examples

A constrictor snake
A constrictor kills by squeezing rather than by injecting venom.
Many people fear the power of a constrictor when it wraps around its prey.
The boa is a well-known constrictor found in South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boa - a large nonvenomous constricting snake native to tropical America
  • synonym: python - a type of large constricting snake
