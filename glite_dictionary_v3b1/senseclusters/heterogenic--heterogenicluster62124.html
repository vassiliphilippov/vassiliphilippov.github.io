heterogenic

adjective
diverse elements
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Heterogenic mixture
The research team analyzed a heterogenic mixture of bacteria from the soil sample.
Because the group was heterogenic, it included people from many different backgrounds.
Doctors found the tumor to be heterogenic, made up of several distinct cell types.
