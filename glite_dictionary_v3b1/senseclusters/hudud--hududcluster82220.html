hudud

noun
Islamic law: fixed punishments for major crimes
Pronunciation: / huːˈduːd / (AmE), / huːˈduːd / (BrE)
in Islamic law, the fixed punishments ordained by God for certain serious crimes, such as theft, adultery, and apostasy

Examples

Hudud punishments
Debates over whether to enforce hudud have surfaced in several Muslim-majority countries.
The government announced new policies regarding the implementation of hudud in the region.
Under hudud, certain crimes such as theft and adultery carry fixed punishments under Islamic law.
