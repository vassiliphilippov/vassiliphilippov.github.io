microsite

noun
small specialized website
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a small, separate website created to focus on a specific topic, product, or event

Examples

A microsite for marketing
The marketing team built a microsite to showcase the upcoming festival's schedule and activities.
Each new product launch was supported by a dedicated microsite with detailed information and videos.
Visitors could enter the contest by registering on the special microsite created for the event.
