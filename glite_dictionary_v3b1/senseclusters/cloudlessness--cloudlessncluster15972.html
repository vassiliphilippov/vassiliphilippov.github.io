cloudlessness

noun
absence of clouds; clear sky
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊd.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈklaʊd.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having no clouds in the sky.

Examples

Clear cloudlessness
After days of rain, the sudden cloudlessness lifted everyone's spirits.
She gazed at the blue sky, struck by its absolute cloudlessness.
The cloudlessness of the afternoon made for a perfect picnic in the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cloudy - having clouds; not clear
