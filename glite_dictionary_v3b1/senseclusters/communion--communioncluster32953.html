communion

noun
intimate sharing of feelings and thoughts
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunjən / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnjən / (BrE)
the sharing or exchanging of intimate thoughts and feelings, especially on a spiritual or emotional level

Examples

Communion with nature
During their retreat, the group experienced a profound sense of communion with each other.
The best friendships are built on mutual trust and communion of the heart.
There was an unspoken communion between the artist and her audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alienation - the state of being isolated from a group or activity
  • synonym: communication - the act of conveying information or expressing thoughts and feelings
