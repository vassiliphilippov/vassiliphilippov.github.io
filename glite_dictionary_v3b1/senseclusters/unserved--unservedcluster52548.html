unserved

adjective
not given service
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɝːvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːvd / (BrE)
not having received what is needed, wanted, or due, especially service or attention

Examples

Unserved customers
Several unserved customers waited at the counter for their orders.
The remote village remained unserved by public transportation for many years.
Complaints increased about the number of unserved tables during the busy lunch hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention
  • antonym: served - having received service or attention
