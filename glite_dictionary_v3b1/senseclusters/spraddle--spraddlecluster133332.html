spraddle

verb
to spread legs wide
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈspræ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈspræ.dəl/ (BrE)
to sit, stand, or move with your arms or legs spread wide apart

Examples

To spraddle your legs
The child spraddled across the grass, enjoying the warm sunshine.
He spraddled his legs to keep his balance on the boat.
She spraddled awkwardly on the bench, taking up extra space.
The toddler would spraddle his legs when he tried to walk.
She spraddled her arms across the back of the sofa, taking up all the space.
The frog spraddled its legs as it leapt onto the lily pad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sprawl - to sit or lie with the limbs spread out awkwardly
  • degree lower: stand - to be in an upright position on the feet
  • synonym: straddle - to sit or stand with a leg on each side of something
