Hamiltonian

adjective
of Alexander Hamilton
8.3
Pronunciation: /hæm.ɪl.ˈtoʊ.niː.ən/ /; / /hə.mɪl.ˈtoʊ.niː.ən/ (AmE), /hæm.ɪl.ˈtəʊ.niː.ən/ /; / /hə.mɪl.ˈtəʊ.niː.ən/ (BrE)

Examples

Hamiltonian policies

Historians often debate the impact of Hamiltonian policies on the development of the U.S. economy.
Her views on banking are distinctly Hamiltonian, supporting a centralized financial system.
