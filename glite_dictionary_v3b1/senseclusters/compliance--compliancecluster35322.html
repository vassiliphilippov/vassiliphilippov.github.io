Examples

Compliance with rules

The king demanded compliance from the rebellious provinces.

Synonyms

  • abidance
    9.5 0.00/m
    abidance is formal and means following rules or laws, while compliance is a common word for obeying rules, requests, or authority.
  • obedience
    2.6 8.88/m
    obedience is acting under direct orders from an authority, while compliance covers following rules, laws, or requests more generally.
  • observance
    4.7 1.71/m
    observance is the careful practice of a law, custom, or ritual, while compliance is broader obedience to rules, requests, or authority.
  • conformance
    7.0 0.82/m
    conformance is matching a set technical standard or form, while compliance includes obeying laws, rules, or authority in many contexts.
  • conformity
    4.8 4.26/m
    conformity is behaving like others or social norms, while compliance is following rules, laws, or a person's requests or orders.
  • adherence
    6.3 6.29/m
    adherence is firm support or loyalty to beliefs, rules, or a group, while compliance is the act of obeying orders or laws.
