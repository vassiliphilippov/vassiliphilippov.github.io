rigsdaler

noun
old Danish-Norwegian currency
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡzˌdɑːlər / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡzˌdɑːlə / (BrE)
a former unit of currency used in Denmark and Norway before the 19th century

The museum displayed a rare rigsdaler from the early 19th century.
Before the krone was introduced, people in Norway used the rigsdaler for everyday purchases.
