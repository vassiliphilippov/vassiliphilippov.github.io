loose cannon

noun
unpredictable, potentially damaging person
5.1 5.9
Pronunciation: / lus ˈkæn.ən /; / lus ˈkænən / (AmE), / luːs ˈkæn.ən /; / luːs ˈkænən / (BrE)
a person who behaves in an unpredictable or uncontrollable way and may cause trouble or damage without meaning to

Examples
