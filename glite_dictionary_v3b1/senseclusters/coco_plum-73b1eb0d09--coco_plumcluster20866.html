coco plum

noun
tropical American fruit used for preserves
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌplʌm / (BrE)
A small, plum-shaped fruit with whitish to almost black skin, native to tropical America and often used for preserves.

Examples

Ripe coco plum fruit
In the local market, baskets of coco plum were displayed alongside other tropical fruits.
The coco plum tree thrives in coastal areas and produces a tasty fruit.
The jam was made from fresh coco plums picked that morning.
