vaporware

noun
unreleased product
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪpərˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəˌweə / (BrE)
a product, plan, or promise that is announced but never actually released or completed

Examples

A vaporware product
The much-hyped operating system turned out to be vaporware and was never released.
Critics accused the company of promoting vaporware to distract from their lack of real products.
After years of delays, many believed the new console was just vaporware.
The much-hyped new smartphone turned out to be vaporware and never reached the market.
Critics accused the company of announcing vaporware to distract from its lack of real progress.
Many people are tired of political vaporware and want to see real action instead of empty promises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty promise - a promise that is not intended to be kept
  • synonym: hoax - a false claim or deception
  • degree lower: prototype - an early sample or model of a product
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
  • antonym: release - a product that is made available to the public
