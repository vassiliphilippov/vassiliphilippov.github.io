tiger cub

noun
young tiger
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡɚ kʌb / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə kʌb / (BrE)
a young tiger, especially one that has not yet reached adulthood

Examples

A newborn tiger cub
A newborn tiger cub is completely dependent on its mother for survival.
The tiger cub playfully chased its sibling through the grass.
Visitors at the zoo watched the tiger cub take its first cautious steps outside the den.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tiger - a large carnivorous feline native to Asia
