shut one's gob

verb
tell someone to stop talking (informal, British)
Pronunciation: / ʃʌt wʌnz ɡɑb / (AmE), / ʃʌt wʌnz ɡɒb / (BrE)
(informal, British) Used to tell someone to stop talking or to be quiet.

Examples

To shut one's gob quickly
He told his friends to shut their gob and listen to the instructions.
If you’re going to complain all evening, just shut your gob.
You’d better shut your gob before you get yourself in more trouble.
