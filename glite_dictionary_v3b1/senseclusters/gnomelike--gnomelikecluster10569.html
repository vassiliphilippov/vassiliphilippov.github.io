gnomelike

adjective
resembling a gnome
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊmlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a gnome, especially in appearance or manner

Examples

Gnomelike figure
The storyteller had a gnomelike appearance, with a pointed hat and twinkling eyes.
Children were fascinated by the gnomelike figure tending the garden at dawn.
His gnomelike manner made him seem mysterious and wise beyond his years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dwarfish - small and resembling a dwarf
  • synonym: elfin - small and charmingly mischievous, like an elf
