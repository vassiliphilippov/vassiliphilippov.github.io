gaswoman

noun
woman employed by a gas company
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡæsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who works for a gas company, especially as a service operative or inspector

Examples

The gaswoman checks meters
As a gaswoman, she is responsible for ensuring the safety of household gas appliances.

The company was proud to hire its first female gaswoman in the region.
