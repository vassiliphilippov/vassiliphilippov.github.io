persecutor

noun
person who persecutes or torments others
7.9 0.67/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…úrs…™ÀåkjuÀêt…ôr / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀês…™ÀåkjuÀêt…ô / (BrE)

Examples

The persecutor attacks
