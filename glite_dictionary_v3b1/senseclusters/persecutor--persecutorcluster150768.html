persecutor

noun
person who persecutes or torments others
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsɪˌkjuːtər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsɪˌkjuːtə / (BrE)
a person who treats someone cruelly, especially because of their race, religion, or beliefs; someone who torments or harasses others persistently

Examples

The persecutor attacks
She finally stood up to her persecutor after years of harassment.
The dictator was known as a ruthless persecutor of dissenters.
Throughout history, many have fled their homes to escape religious persecutors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oppressor - a person who keeps others in hardship, especially by the unjust use of authority
  • antonym: victim - a person harmed, injured, or killed as a result of a crime, accident, or other event
