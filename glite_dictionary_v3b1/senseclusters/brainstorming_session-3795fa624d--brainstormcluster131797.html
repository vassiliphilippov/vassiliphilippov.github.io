brainstorming session

noun
meeting for generating ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnˌstɔrmɪŋ ˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌstɔːmɪŋ ˈsɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a meeting held to generate ideas and creative solutions, typically through open discussion without immediate criticism or judgment

Examples

A brainstorming session meeting
Each brainstorming session results in a list of creative solutions to consider.
The team made significant progress during the afternoon brainstorming session.
We scheduled a brainstorming session to come up with marketing ideas for the new product.
