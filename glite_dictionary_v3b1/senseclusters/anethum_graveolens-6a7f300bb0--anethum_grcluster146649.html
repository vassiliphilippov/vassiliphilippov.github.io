Anethum graveolens

noun
aromatic herb; botanical name for dill
Pronunciation: / əˈniːθəm ˌɡræviˈoʊlənz / (AmE), / əˈniːθəm ˌɡræviˈəʊlɛnz / (BrE)
an aromatic herb, native to Eurasia, with feathery leaves and seeds used in cooking, also known as dill
