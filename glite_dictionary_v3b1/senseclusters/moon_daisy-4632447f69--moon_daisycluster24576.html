moon daisy

noun
tall white-flowered Eurasian perennial plant
Pronunciation: / mun ˈdeɪ.zi / (AmE), / muːn ˈdeɪ.zi / (BrE)
a tall Eurasian perennial plant with white, daisy-like flowers, commonly found in fields and widely naturalized; often classified under the genus Chrysanthemum.

Examples

A moon daisy flower
A single moon daisy stood out among the taller grasses near the roadside.
Gardeners often plant moon daisy for its resilience and attractive white flowers.
The field was covered in blooming moon daisies, creating a beautiful white carpet under the summer sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxeye daisy - a similar European perennial with white petals and yellow center
