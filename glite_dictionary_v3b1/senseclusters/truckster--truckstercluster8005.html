truckster

noun
small utility vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌkstər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkstə / (BrE)
a small truck or utility vehicle used for transporting goods, especially over short distances

Examples

Utility truckster
The maintenance crew drove a truckster around the park to collect trash.
We loaded the boxes onto the truckster for delivery to the warehouse.
A truckster is perfect for transporting tools and equipment on a large campus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pickup - a small truck with an open back for carrying goods
  • synonym: van - a vehicle used for transporting goods or people
