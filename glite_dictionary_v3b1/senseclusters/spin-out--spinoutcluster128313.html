spin-out

noun
vehicle loss of control; new company
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈspɪnˌaʊt / (BrE)
an incident where a vehicle loses control and spins around, or a new company created from a larger organization

Examples

A company spin-out
The icy road caused a dangerous spin-out just before the intersection.
After the spin-out, the car came to rest facing the wrong direction.
The tech giant announced a spin-out to create a new independent company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skid - a sudden, uncontrolled slide of a vehicle
  • synonym: spinoff - a new company formed from part of an existing one
