seamster

noun
person who sews professionally
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmstər / (AmE), / ˈsiːmstə / (BrE)
A person, especially a man, whose occupation is making and altering garments.

Examples

A seamster at work
As a professional seamster, he tailored custom suits for a diverse clientele.
The seamster skillfully repaired the torn suit jacket in less than an hour.
The demand for a talented seamster increased as the local theater prepared for its new production.
