trachodont

noun
large duck-billed dinosaur of the Cretaceous
Pronunciation: / trəˈkɑːdɑnt / (AmE), / trəˈkædɒnt / (BrE)
a large duck-billed dinosaur from the Cretaceous period, known for its distinctive bill-shaped mouth

Examples

The trachodont dinosaur
Many paleontologists study the unique jaw structure of the trachodont.
The fossil of a trachodont was discovered in the Montana badlands.
The museum's exhibit features a full-scale model of a trachodont.
