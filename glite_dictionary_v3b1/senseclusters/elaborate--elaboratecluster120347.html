elaborate

adjective
detailed and intricate
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæbərət /; / ɪˈlæb.ɚ.eɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlæbərət /; / ɪˈlæb.ər.ət / (BrE)
very detailed, complicated, or intricate; having many parts or details

Examples

An elaborate design

They hosted an elaborate dinner party with multiple courses and ornate decorations.
She wore an elaborate costume with beads and sequins for the festival.
The architect designed an elaborate staircase that became the building's focal point.
The wedding decorations featured an elaborate arrangement of flowers and lights.
