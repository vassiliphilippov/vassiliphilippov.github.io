overtalkative

adjective
excessively talkative
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkətɪv / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtɔːkətɪv / (BrE)
talking much more than is usual or necessary; excessively talkative

Examples

An overtalkative student
The class was disrupted by an overtalkative student who would not stop answering every question.
Her overtalkative nature made it hard for others to share their opinions during the discussion.
He realized he was being overtalkative and tried to listen more to his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatty - liking to talk a lot in a friendly way
  • synonym: loquacious - tending to talk a great deal
  • antonym: taciturn - tending to say very little
