creep feed

noun
feed for young isolated animals
Pronunciation: / krip fid / (AmE), / kriːp fiːd / (BrE)
a special feed given to young animals, such as calves or piglets, which are kept in an area (a creep) that adult animals cannot access

Examples

Creep feed for piglets
Many farmers introduce creep feed to piglets to promote healthy growth before weaning.
The quality of creep feed can have a significant impact on the calves' weight gain.
Using creep feed helps young animals adjust to solid food more easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: starter feed - feed for young animals at the very early stage
