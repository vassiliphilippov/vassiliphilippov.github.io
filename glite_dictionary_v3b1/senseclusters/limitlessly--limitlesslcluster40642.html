limitlessly

adverb
without limits
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtləsli / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtləsli / (BrE)
in a way that has no limits or end; without any restriction or boundary

Examples

Expand limitlessly
The universe seems to expand limitlessly, beyond what we can observe.
With imagination, you can create limitlessly and never run out of ideas.
Technology is advancing limitlessly as new discoveries are made every year.
She was limitlessly proud of her children's achievements.
The possibilities for learning new things are limitlessly exciting in today's world.
He believed that human imagination could expand limitlessly if given the right tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endlessly - in a way that never ends
  • antonym: finitely - in a way that has limits or an end
  • synonym: infinitely - in a way that is endless or without limit
