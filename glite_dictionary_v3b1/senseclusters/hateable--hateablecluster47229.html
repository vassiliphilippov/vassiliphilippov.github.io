hateable

adjective
deserving hatred
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈheɪtəbəl / (BrE)
deserving to be hated or easy to hate

Examples

A hateable character
The movie's main character was so hateable that audiences cheered when he lost.
She found his arrogant attitude completely hateable after their argument.
Some villains are written to be intentionally hateable to make the story more engaging.

Related Terms
