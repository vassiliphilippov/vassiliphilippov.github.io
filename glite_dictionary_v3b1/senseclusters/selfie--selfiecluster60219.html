selfie

adjective
for self-photography
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlfi / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfi / (BrE)
relating to or designed for taking a photograph of oneself, especially with a smartphone or camera.

Examples

Selfie stick
She bought a new selfie stick to help her take better vacation photos.
The phone has a special selfie mode that automatically enhances your face.
He prefers using the selfie camera for quick video calls with friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-portrait - a picture or photograph that you take of yourself
