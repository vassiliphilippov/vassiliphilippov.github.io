city block

noun
rectangular area in a city surrounded by streets
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti blɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɪti blɒk / (BrE)
a rectangular area in a city that is surrounded by streets, usually containing several buildings

Examples

A city block in town
Every city block in the downtown area has its own unique character.
It took them ten minutes to walk around the entire city block.
Our apartment is located on a city block with a beautiful park nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: block - a section of a city surrounded by streets on all sides
