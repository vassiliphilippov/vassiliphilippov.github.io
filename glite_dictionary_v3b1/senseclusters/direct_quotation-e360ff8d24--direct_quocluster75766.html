Synonyms

  • direct discourse
    6.3 0.14/m
    direct discourse is the linguistic term for repeating a speaker's exact words in text, while direct quotation is the everyday label for those exact words.
  • quotation
    2.6 7.07/m
    quotation is a general word for a quoted passage or giving credit to a source, while direct quotation names the exact words often shown in quotation marks.
