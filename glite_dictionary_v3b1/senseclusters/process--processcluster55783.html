process

noun
mental activity, often unconscious
2.7 377/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀê.ses /; / Ààpro ä.ses / (AmE), / Ààpr…ô ä.ses /; / Ààpr…ís.…õs / (BrE)
a series of actions, steps, or changes (including in the mind) that happen to achieve a particular result or develop over time

Examples

Therapists often help patients uncover hidden mental processes that influence behavior.
Photosynthesis is a natural process essential for plant life.
The aging process affects everyone differently.

Learning a language is a gradual process that requires patience and practice.
The hiring process at the company includes several interviews and tests.
