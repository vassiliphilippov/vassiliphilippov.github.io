noun

Examples

Synonyms

  • relay station
    3.7 0.1/m
    a relay station amplifies and forwards signals across wide distances, while a repeater boosts and retransmits to extend a local communication link.
  • relay transmitter
    6.0 0.00/m
    a relay transmitter amplifies a signal during transmission to restore its strength, while a repeater focuses on extending range between stations.
  • regenerator
    6.7 0.00/m
    a regenerator restores weakened digital signals to full strength and shape, while a repeater mainly amplifies and retransmits to extend range.
