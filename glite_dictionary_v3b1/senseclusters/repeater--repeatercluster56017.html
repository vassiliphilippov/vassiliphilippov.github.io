repeater

noun
device that retransmits and amplifies signals
4.8 0.44/m
Pronunciation: / r…™Ààpit…ôr / (AmE), / r…™ÀàpiÀêt…ô / (BrE)
an electronic device that receives a signal and retransmits it at a higher power, used to extend the range of communication systems

Examples

Wi-Fi repeater
A repeater is essential for extending Wi-Fi reach in large buildings.
The radio station installed a repeater to improve coverage in remote areas.
The technician checked the repeater to ensure the signal was being transmitted correctly.

Synonyms

  • relay station
    3.7 0.13/m
    a relay station amplifies and forwards signals across wide distances, while a repeater boosts and retransmits to extend a local communication link.
  • relay transmitter
    6.0 0.01/m
    a relay transmitter amplifies a signal during transmission to restore its strength, while a repeater focuses on extending range between stations.
  • regenerator
    6.7 0.00/m
    a regenerator restores weakened digital signals to full strength and shape, while a repeater mainly amplifies and retransmits to extend range.
