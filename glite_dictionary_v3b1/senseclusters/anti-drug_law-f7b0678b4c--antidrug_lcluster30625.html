anti-drug law

noun
law prohibiting illegal drug activities
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.taɪ drʌɡ lɔ / (AmE), / ˈæn.ti drʌɡ lɔː / (BrE)
A law that prohibits the manufacture, sale, or use of illegal narcotic drugs.

Examples

The anti-drug law
Many activists argue that the anti-drug law needs to focus more on rehabilitation than punishment.
The government introduced a new anti-drug law to combat trafficking in the region.
Violating the country's anti-drug law can result in severe penalties.
