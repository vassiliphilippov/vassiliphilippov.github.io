thorough

adjective
complete and detailed; not superficial
5.2 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈθɜːr.oʊ/ /; / /ˈθɔːr.oʊ/ /; / /ˈθʌr.oʊ/ (AmE), /ˈθʌ.rə/ /; / /ˈθʌ.rəʊ/ (BrE)
done carefully and completely, with attention to every detail; not superficial or partial

Examples

Thorough check
After a thorough cleaning, the kitchen sparkled like new.
She completed a thorough investigation before making her decision.
The doctor gave him a thorough examination to check for any hidden problems.
He is known for being especially thorough in his research and preparation.
She conducted a thorough review of the report before submitting it.
The cleaning crew did a thorough job, leaving every corner spotless.

  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
