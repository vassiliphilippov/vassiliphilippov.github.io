push around

verb
treat someone in a bossy or bullying way
4.1
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / pʊʃ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to treat someone in a bossy, bullying, or unfair way; to boss or bully someone

Examples

To push around workers

If you let her, she'll just push around everyone in the group.
Nobody likes to be pushed around by their supervisor at work.
He was tired of being pushed around by his older siblings.
Whenever there's a disagreement, she tries to push around everyone else in the group.
You shouldn't let anyone push around you just because you're new at work.
