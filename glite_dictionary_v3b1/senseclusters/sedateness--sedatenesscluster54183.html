sedateness

noun
calm and dignified seriousness
6.5
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdeɪtnəs / (AmE), / sɪˈdeɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being calm, dignified, and serious

Examples

Calm sedateness
Despite his youth, he displayed a sedateness beyond his years.
Her sedateness during the crisis reassured everyone in the room.
The sedateness of the ceremony impressed all the guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frivolity - lack of seriousness; lightheartedness
  • synonym: gravity - seriousness and importance in manner
  • synonym: serenity - the state of being calm, peaceful, and untroubled
