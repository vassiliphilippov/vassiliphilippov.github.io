right

adjective
perpendicular to base; forming right angle
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /raɪt/ (AmE), /raɪt/ (BrE)
(of an angle, line, or shape) having an axis or sides at 90 degrees to a base; perpendicular or forming a right angle.

Examples

Right angle
A right prism is defined by having sides perpendicular to its base.
In geometry class, we learned that a right triangle contains one ninety-degree angle.
The architect designed a staircase that meets the wall at a right angle.
