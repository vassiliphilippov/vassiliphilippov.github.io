mailer

noun
email marketing service
Pronunciation: / \u02c8me\u026al\u025a / (AmE), / \u02c8me\u026al\u0259 / (BrE)
a software program or online service used to send mass or automated email messages (such as newsletters or marketing campaigns) to a list of recipients.

Examples

Email mailer software
The marketing team uses a mailer to send weekly promotional emails to thousands of subscribers.
After signup, the company automated welcome messages through their mailer, which personalizes content for users.
We compared several mailers for deliverability and chose the one with the best analytics dashboard.
