absorption

noun
integration of a cultural group into another
4.0 5.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrpʃən / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːpʃən / (BrE)
the process by which one cultural group is integrated or assimilated into harmony with another

Examples

Cultural absorption
Critics argue that forced absorption leads to loss of cultural heritage.
Government programs can either support or hinder the absorption of different communities into mainstream society.
The absorption of minority traditions often occurs when immigrants adapt to a new country's customs.
