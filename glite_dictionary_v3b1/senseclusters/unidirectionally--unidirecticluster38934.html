unidirectionally

adverb
in one direction
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪdəˈrɛkʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that moves or acts in only one direction

Examples

Moves unidirectionally
The signal travels unidirectionally from the transmitter to the receiver.
Some valves allow fluid to flow unidirectionally to prevent backflow.
Information is sent unidirectionally along the nerve fibers in the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bidirectionally - in two directions
  • synonym: one-way - moving or operating in a single direction
