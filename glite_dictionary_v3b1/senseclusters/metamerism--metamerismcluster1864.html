metamerism

noun
body segmentation
Pronunciation: / məˈtæmərɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪˈtæmərɪzəm / (BrE)
the condition of having a body divided into a series of similar, repeated segments

Examples

Animal metamerism
Earthworms are a classic example of animals that show metamerism in their body structure.
The study of metamerism helps scientists understand the evolution of complex organisms.
In biology class, we learned how metamerism is important for movement in segmented animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: segmentation - the division of something into separate parts or sections
