wheel horse

noun
horse nearest vehicle's wheels in a team
3.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /wiːl hɔːrs/ /; / /hwiːl hɔːrs/ (AmE), /wiːl hɔːs/ /; / /hwiːl hɔːs/ (BrE)
a draft horse harnessed behind others and positioned nearest the wheels of a vehicle, typically at the back of a team pulling a carriage or wagon

Examples

The wheel horse animal
Farmers often chose their strongest animals to serve as the wheel horse in a team.
In the old carriage teams, the wheel horse bore much of the weight during sharp turns.
The sturdy wheel horse was vital for steadying the wagon on rough roads.
