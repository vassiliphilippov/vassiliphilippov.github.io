Kremlinologist

noun
analyst of secret politics
9.9
Pronunciation: /krem.lɪn.ˈɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /krem.lɪn.ˈɒ.lə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
a person who analyzes or interprets the secretive internal politics or power struggles of a government or organization, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Company Kremlinologist
As a skilled Kremlinologist, he could predict the company's next move from subtle changes in leadership.
Journalists often act as Kremlinologists when trying to understand the government's secretive decisions.
After the board reshuffle, everyone became an amateur Kremlinologist speculating about the reasons behind it.
