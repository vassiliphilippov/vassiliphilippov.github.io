driverless

adjective
without a leader
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdraɪ.vər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈdraɪ.və.ləs/ (BrE)
lacking a person who leads, controls, or provides direction; without a driver or leader.

Examples

Driverless company
After the founder left, the company felt driverless and struggled to make decisions.
The project became driverless when no one stepped up to take charge.
Without clear direction, the team was left driverless and progress slowed.
