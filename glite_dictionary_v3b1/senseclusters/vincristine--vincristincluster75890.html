vincristine

noun
chemotherapy drug for cancer treatment
Pronunciation: / vɪnˈkrɪstin / (AmE), / vɪnˈkrɪstiːn / (BrE)
a drug derived from the periwinkle plant, used as a chemotherapy agent to treat cancers such as lymphoma

Examples

Vincristine chemotherapy drug
Researchers are studying ways to reduce the toxicity of vincristine in pediatric patients.
The doctor prescribed vincristine as part of the chemotherapy regimen.
Treatment with vincristine can cause side effects such as hair loss and neuropathy.
