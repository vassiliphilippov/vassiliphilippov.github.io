loon

noun
foolish or confused person (informal)
8.6
Pronunciation: / luːn / (AmE), / luːn / (BrE)
an informal and insulting term for a person who is foolish, crazy, or lazy

Examples

He's a real loon
Everyone thinks he's a loon for believing those conspiracy theories.
She laughed and called her brother a loon after his ridiculous suggestion.
The old loon on the corner is always shouting nonsense at passersby.
Don't let that loon talk you into skipping your responsibilities.
Everyone thought he was just a harmless loon who never did any real work.
Stop being such a loon and help us clean the garage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardworker - a person who works hard
  • synonym: layabout - a person who habitually does little or no work
  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill or wildly reckless
  • synonym: madman - a person who behaves in a wildly foolish way
  • antonym: sensible person - a person showing good sense or judgement
  • synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
