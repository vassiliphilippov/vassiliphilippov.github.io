hillbilly music

noun
early Southern U.S. country music
6.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɪl.bɪl.ɪ ˈmjuː.zɪk/ /; / /ˈhɪl.bə.li ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (AmE), /ˈhɪl.bɪl.ɪ ˈmjuː.zɪk/ /; / /ˈhɪl.bə.li ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (BrE)
