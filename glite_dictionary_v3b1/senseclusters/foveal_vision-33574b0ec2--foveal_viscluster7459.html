foveal vision

noun
sharp central vision from the fovea
6.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.vɪ.əl ˈvɪʒ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.vɪ.əl ˈvɪʒ.ən/ (BrE)
