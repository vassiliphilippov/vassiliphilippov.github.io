conventionality

noun
quality of being conventional or unoriginal
1.7 5.9
Pronunciation: /kə.nven.ʃə.ˈnæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /kə.nven.ʃə.ˈnæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
The quality of being conventional, especially as a lack of originality or creativity.

Examples

Conventionality in art
In literature, conventionality can make even a well-written story seem uninspired.
She was frustrated by the conventionality of his ideas, longing for originality and innovation.
The film's conventionality disappointed critics who were hoping for something more daring.
