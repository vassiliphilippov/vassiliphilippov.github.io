nonsusceptible

adjective
not easily affected
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səˈsɛptəbl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səˈsɛptəbl / (BrE)
not likely to be affected, influenced, or harmed by something, especially a disease or chemical.

Examples

nonsusceptible plants
These plants are nonsusceptible to most common garden pests.
The new material is nonsusceptible to water damage, making it ideal for outdoor use.
Some people are genetically nonsusceptible to certain viral infections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immune - protected from or not affected by something
  • synonym: resistant - not affected or harmed by something
  • antonym: susceptible - easily affected or influenced by something
