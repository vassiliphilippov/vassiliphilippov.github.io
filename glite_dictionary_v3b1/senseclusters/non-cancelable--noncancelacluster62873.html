non-cancelable

adjective
cannot be canceled
5.3
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈkæn.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈkæn.sə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be canceled, revoked, or undone once agreed upon or started

Examples

A non-cancelable contract
The contract is non-cancelable, so you must pay the full amount even if you change your mind.
Be sure to check if your reservation is non-cancelable before booking the hotel room.
Some subscriptions are non-cancelable after the initial trial period ends.

  • synonym: irreversible - impossible to change or undo
