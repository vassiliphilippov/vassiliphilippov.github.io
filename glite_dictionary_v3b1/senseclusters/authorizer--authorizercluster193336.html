authorizer

noun
one who grants authorization
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːθəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəraɪzə /; / ˈɔːθəˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a person or organization that grants permission or official approval for something to be done

Examples

Main authorizer of payments
As the main authorizer, she has the final say on any financial transactions.
Employees must obtain approval from an authorizer before accessing restricted areas.
The new policy requires each contract to be signed by an authorizer from the finance department.
Only the authoriser can grant you access to the restricted area.
Please consult your department’s authoriser before proceeding with expenses.
The contract was not valid without the signature of the authoriser.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approver - a person who gives approval
  • synonym: authorizer - alternative spelling of 'authoriser', mainly US English
  • antonym: refuser - a person who denies authorization or approval
  • antonym: rejector - a person who refuses permission
