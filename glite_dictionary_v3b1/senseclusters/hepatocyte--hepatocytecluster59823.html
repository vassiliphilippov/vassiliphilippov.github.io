hepatocyte

noun
liver cell
7.9
Pronunciation: / hɪˈpætəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / hɪˈpætəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a main type of cell in the liver that performs essential metabolic, detoxifying, and synthetic functions
