alertly

adverb
in an attentive, perceptive manner
3.8 2.7
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈlɜːr.tliː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈlɜːt.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner that is quick to notice and respond to things; attentively and perceptively.

Examples

To act alertly
Despite his fatigue, he reacted alertly to the sudden noise.
She listened alertly to every word of the announcement.
The dog waited alertly at the door for its owner to return.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distractedly - in a distracted manner, not paying full attention
