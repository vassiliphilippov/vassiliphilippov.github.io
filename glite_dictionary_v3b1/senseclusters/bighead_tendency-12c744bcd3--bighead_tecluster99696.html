bighead tendency

noun
habit of self-importance or arrogance
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌhɛd ˈtɛndənsi / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌhɛd ˈtɛndənsi / (BrE)
The quality or habit of having an inflated sense of one's own importance or abilities.

Examples

Bighead tendency to boast
Despite her achievements, she avoided any bighead tendency and remained humble.
His bighead tendency often led him to underestimate his colleagues.
The coach warned the team that a bighead tendency could harm their performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest view of one's importance
