dentil

noun
architectural ornament
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɛntɪl / (BrE)
a small, rectangular block used as a repeating decorative element in classical architecture, especially in cornices

Examples

Row of dentils
The ancient temple featured a row of dentils along its roofline.
Restorers carefully replaced missing dentils to match the original design of the building.
You can see the decorative dentil pattern just below the cornice on the facade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cornice - the uppermost section of moldings along the top of a wall or just below a roof
  • synonym: molding - a decorative strip used in architecture
