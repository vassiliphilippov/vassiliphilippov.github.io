Xantusiidae

noun
family of night lizards
Pronunciation: / zænˌtuːsiˈɪdiː / (AmE), / zanˌtuːsiˈɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, secretive lizards known as night lizards, native to the southwestern United States, Mexico, and Central America
