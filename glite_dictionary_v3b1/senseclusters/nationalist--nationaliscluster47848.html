nationalist

adjective
strongly supportive of one's own nation
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈnaʃ(ə)nəlɪst / (BrE)
showing strong support for one's own nation and putting its interests above those of others

Examples

Nationalist ideas
Her nationalist views were evident in her speeches about economic independence.
The movement grew out of nationalist sentiments following years of foreign rule.
The party adopted a nationalist stance, prioritizing domestic industries over foreign competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitan - including or containing people from many different countries; not nationalistic
  • synonym: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country
