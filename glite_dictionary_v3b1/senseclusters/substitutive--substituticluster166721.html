substitutive

adjective
chemical replacement
8.7
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈstɪt.uː.tɪv/ /; / /səb.ˈstɪt.ə.tɪv/ (AmE), /səb.ˈstɪt.juː.tɪv/ /; / /səb.ˈstɪt.ə.tɪv/ (BrE)
relating to a chemical reaction in which one atom or group in a molecule is replaced by another.

Examples

Substitutive reaction
A substitutive reaction occurs when one atom in a molecule is replaced by a different atom.
The chemist explained the substitutive process that led to the new compound.
In organic chemistry, substitutive mechanisms are common in the synthesis of various products.
