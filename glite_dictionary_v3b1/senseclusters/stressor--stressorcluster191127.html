stressor

noun
tool for overloading servers
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstres.ər/ (AmE), /ˈstres.ə/ (BrE)
a computer program or online service that overloads a website or server with traffic, often used in denial-of-service attacks

Examples

A stressor attack

Law enforcement agencies are cracking down on illegal stresser services sold online.
Many stresser tools are advertised as testing services but are often used for attacks.
