dialer

noun
phone call tool
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪlə / (BrE)
a device or software application that dials telephone numbers, either manually or automatically

Examples

Phone dialer app
He opened the dialer on his smartphone to make a call.
The old rotary dialer was replaced by a digital one in the office.
Many modern phones have a built-in dialer app for quick access to contacts.
The company uses a dialer to contact hundreds of customers every day.
He installed a new dialer app on his smartphone to make calls more efficiently.
Telemarketers often rely on a dialer to speed up their calling process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autodialer - a device or software that dials telephone numbers automatically
  • synonym: keypad - a set of buttons for entering numbers or letters
  • antonym: manual dial - the act of dialing a telephone number by hand
  • antonym: receiver - the part of a phone you hold to your ear
