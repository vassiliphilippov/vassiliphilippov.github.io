photovoltaic cell

noun
device converting sunlight to electricity
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊvoʊlˈteɪɪk sɛl / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊvɒlˈteɪɪk sɛl / (BrE)
A device that converts sunlight directly into electrical energy by means of a semiconductor.

Examples

A photovoltaic cell device
A single photovoltaic cell produces only a small amount of electricity, but many together can power a home.
Researchers are developing more efficient photovoltaic cells for use in space.
The house uses several photovoltaic cells on the roof to generate renewable energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solar cell - a synonym; a device that converts solar energy into electricity
