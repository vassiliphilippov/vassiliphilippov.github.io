Synonyms

  • undetermined
    4.6 0.9/m
    undetermined is not fixed and often used in formal reports, while open also means not decided and can mean available.
  • undecided
    2.9 3/m
    undecided means a person or issue has not chosen yet, while open can mean not decided or available for change.
  • unresolved
    4.4 3/m
    unresolved describes problems that remain unsolved or pending, while open can mean not decided or available to change.
  • up in the air
    4.7 1/m
    up in the air is an informal phrase for plans not yet decided, while open sounds neutral and can mean undecided.
  • available
    2.3 90/m
    available is free to use or obtain now, while open often means not decided and also can mean ready to be used.
