close order

noun
tight military formation for drill or marching
7.4 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / klo äs Àà…îrd…ôr / (AmE), / kl…ô äs Àà…îÀêd…ô / (BrE)
a military formation in which troops are arranged closely together, typically for drill or marching purposes

Examples

Close order formation
Instructions were given for the platoon to adopt close order as they prepared to march.
The soldiers marched in close order during the ceremonial parade.
Training drills often began with formations in close order before moving to more dispersed positions.
