batel

noun
small boat (archaic)
an old term for a small boat or skiff, especially used for rowing or fishing

Examples

A small batel
The fisherman rowed his batel across the quiet lake.
In old stories, villagers would cross the river in a wooden batel.
The ancient batel was found buried near the shore, preserved by mud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dinghy - a small open boat, often used as a tender
  • synonym: skiff - a small, light boat for rowing or sailing
