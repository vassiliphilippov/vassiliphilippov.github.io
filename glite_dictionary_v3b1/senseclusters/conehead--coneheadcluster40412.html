conehead

noun
katydid with pointed head
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊnˌhɛd / (BrE)
a large katydid or bush-cricket with an elongated, pointed head, belonging to the family Tettigoniidae

Examples

Conehead katydid
A large conehead katydid landed on the porch after the warm summer rain.
The distinctive pointed head makes the conehead easy to identify among other insects.
We spotted a green conehead hiding in the tall grass during our nature walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bush-cricket - a long-horned grasshopper, especially in the family Tettigoniidae
  • synonym: katydid - a large green insect related to the grasshopper, known for its loud song
