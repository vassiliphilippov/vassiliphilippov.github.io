scrobble

verb
to log music plays
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɑbəl / (AmE), / ˈskrɒbəl / (BrE)
to automatically record and share details of songs listened to online, typically through a music service or app

She likes to scrobble her favorite tracks so her friends can see what she is listening to.
