bell

noun
a shape like a traditional bell
Pronunciation: / bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)
a shape that is rounded at the bottom and narrow at the top, resembling a traditional bell.

Examples

Bell-shaped flower
Many flowers, such as bluebells, have a distinct bell shape.
The dress flared out in a bell at the hem.
The glass lampshade was molded in a bell shape.
