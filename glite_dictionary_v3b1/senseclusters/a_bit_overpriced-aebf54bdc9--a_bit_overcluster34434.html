a bit overpriced

adjective
slightly more expensive than fair
1.0
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt ˌoʊvərˈpraɪst / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˌəʊvəˈpraɪst / (BrE)
Slightly more expensive than is reasonable or fair for what is offered.

Examples

A bit overpriced tickets
Although the concert was enjoyable, tickets seemed a bit overpriced this year.
Some people find that trendy cafes can be a bit overpriced for what you get.
The shoes looked great, but they felt a bit overpriced for the quality offered.
