originate

verb
start a journey from a place (vehicles, transport)
Pronunciation: / əˈrɪdʒəˌneɪt / (AmE), / əˈrɪdʒɪneɪt / (BrE)
(of a service or vehicle, especially a train, bus, or plane) to begin its journey at a specified place.

Examples

Trains originate here
All buses on this route originate from the downtown terminal.
The 7:00 a.m. flight originates at Heathrow Airport.
This train originates in Chicago and continues all the way to Los Angeles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depart - to leave, especially when starting a journey
