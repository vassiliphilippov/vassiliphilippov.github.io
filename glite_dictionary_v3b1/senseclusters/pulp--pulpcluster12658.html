pulp

noun
soft, moist part of a fruit
3.0 1.09/m
Pronunciation: / pÊŒlp / (AmE), / pÊŒlp / (BrE)
The soft, moist, and often edible part of a fruit located inside the skin or rind.

Examples

Orange pulp
After juicing the watermelon, there was a lot of pulp left in the strainer.
The orange was sweet and juicy, and the pulp was easy to eat.
You can blend the pulp of the mango into a smoothie for extra flavor.
