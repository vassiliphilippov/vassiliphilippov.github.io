neb

noun
medicine mist device
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɛb / (AmE), / nɛb / (BrE)
a device that turns liquid medicine into a mist for inhalation, often used to treat respiratory conditions

Examples

A neb for asthma
The doctor recommended using a neb to help manage her asthma symptoms.
He set up the neb before giving his son the prescribed medication.
Many hospitals provide a neb for patients with severe breathing difficulties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhaler - a device for administering medicine by inhalation
  • synonym: nebulizer - a machine that delivers medicine as a mist to be inhaled
