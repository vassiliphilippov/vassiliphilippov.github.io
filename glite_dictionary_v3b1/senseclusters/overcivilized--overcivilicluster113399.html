overcivilized

adjective
excessively refined
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɪvəlaɪzd / (BrE)
having become too refined or lacking in natural qualities as a result of excessive civilization or social development

Examples

An overcivilized society
Some critics argue that modern society has become overcivilized and disconnected from nature.
The author describes the city as overcivilized, where people have lost touch with basic instincts.
He felt overcivilized after years of living in a highly structured environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: primitive - simple and not developed or refined
  • synonym: refined - very polite or cultured
