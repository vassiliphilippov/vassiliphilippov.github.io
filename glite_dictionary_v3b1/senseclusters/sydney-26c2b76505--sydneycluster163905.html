Sydney

proper noun
largest city in Australia
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪdni / (AmE), / ˈsɪdni / (BrE)
The largest city in Australia, located in southeastern Australia on the Tasman Sea; the capital of New South Wales and Australia's chief port.

Examples

Sydney city center
After moving to Sydney, he found a job in the city center within a week.
