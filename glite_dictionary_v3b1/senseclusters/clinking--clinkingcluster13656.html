clinking

adjective
making a sharp ringing sound
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
making a light, sharp ringing sound, like that produced when glasses are lightly tapped together

Examples

A clinking bell
A clinking bell hung from the shop's door, announcing each new customer.
He recognized her by the clinking sound of her keys as she entered the hallway.
The clinking of glasses filled the room as everyone toasted the happy couple.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jingling - making a light metallic ringing sound
  • synonym: rattling - making a series of short, sharp noises
