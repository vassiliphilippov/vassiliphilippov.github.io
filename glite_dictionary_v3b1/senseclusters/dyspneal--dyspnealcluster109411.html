dyspneal

adjective
having difficulty breathing
6.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈniːəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈniːəl / (BrE)
Suffering from or pertaining to difficulty in breathing; experiencing shortness of breath.

Examples

A dyspneal patient
Emergency staff are trained to recognize dyspneal symptoms in incoming patients.
He described himself as dyspneal during mild exertion due to his chronic illness.
The patient became dyspneal after climbing only a few stairs.
