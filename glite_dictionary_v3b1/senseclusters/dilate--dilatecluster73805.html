dilate

verb
become wider or larger
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪ.leɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.leɪt / (BrE)
to become wider, larger, or more open

Examples

Eyes dilate suddenly
Her eyes began to dilate with excitement as she heard the news.
The doctor explained that your pupils dilate in the dark to let in more light.
When heated, metal pipes can dilate slightly, affecting the fit of connections.
