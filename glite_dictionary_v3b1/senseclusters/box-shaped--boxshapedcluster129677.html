box-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a box
Pronunciation: / bɑks ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / bɒks ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a box; rectangular or cubic in form.

Examples

Box-shaped container
She stored her jewelry in a box-shaped container on the dresser.
The architect designed a box-shaped building that stood out among the older structures.
The artist made a box-shaped sculpture, emphasizing clean lines and right angles.
