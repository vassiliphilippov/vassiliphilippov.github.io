shaggy sweater

noun
sweater with a rough, fluffy texture
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæɡi ˈswɛtər / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡi ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
a sweater made of material with a rough, fluffy, or long-fibered texture

Examples

A shaggy sweater
He pulled on his favorite shaggy sweater before heading out into the chilly morning.
Her shaggy sweater made her look extra warm and comfortable during the winter months.
The shaggy sweater shed bits of fluff all over the new couch.
