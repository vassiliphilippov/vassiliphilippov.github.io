by trade

adverb
indicating profession or occupation
7.3
Pronunciation: / baɪ treɪd / (AmE), / baɪ treɪd / (BrE)
used to indicate someone's profession or occupation, especially when it is not immediately obvious from context.

Examples

By trade electrician
He is an electrician by trade, but he also teaches part-time.
My father is a carpenter by trade, though he now works in an office.
She became a chef by trade after years of working in other industries.
