cutthroat

noun
a person who murders by cutting the throat
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtˌθroʊt / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌθrəʊt / (BrE)
a person who murders another by cutting their throat; a ruthless murderer

Examples

A cutthroat murderer
Stories of cutthroats lurking on dangerous roads terrified travelers in olden times.
The detective suspected that the murder was committed by a cutthroat known to the victim.
The notorious cutthroat was finally brought to justice after years on the run.
