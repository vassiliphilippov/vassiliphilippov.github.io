pietistic

adjective
excessively or hypocritically pious
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪəˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpaɪəˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
Showing or characterized by exaggerated or hypocritical displays of piety or religious devotion.

Examples

Pietistic display
He gave a pietistic speech that sounded insincere to most listeners.
Her pietistic attitude alienated both friends and family.
The novel criticizes the pietistic hypocrisy of certain social classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sanctimonious - making a show of being morally superior to others
