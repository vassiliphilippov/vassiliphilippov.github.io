top

noun
first half of an inning in baseball
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
the first half of an inning in baseball or softball, when the visiting team is at bat

Examples

Top of the inning
Rain started falling heavily during the top of the fourth.
The Red Sox scored three runs in the top of the ninth inning.
Their pitcher was replaced in the top of the seventh.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bottom - the second half of an inning, when the home team is at bat
