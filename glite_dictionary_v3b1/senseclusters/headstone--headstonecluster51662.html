headstone

noun
grave marker stone
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.stəʊn / (BrE)
a stone placed at the head of a grave, typically inscribed with the deceased's name and dates

Examples

Grave headstone
After years of weathering, the inscription on the headstone became difficult to read.
His name and dates were engraved on the headstone in elegant script.
The family placed fresh flowers next to the headstone every Sunday.
