megalodon

noun
very large entity
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡələˌdɑn / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡələdɒn / (BrE)
an informal term for a very large or powerful person, organization, or thing, often dominating its field

Examples

A business megalodon
In the tech industry, the company became a megalodon by acquiring its competitors.
Many startups fear being crushed by a megalodon in their market.
The new player quickly turned into a megalodon, dominating the entire league.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giant - a person or thing of great size or power
  • antonym: minnow - a person or organization that is small or unimportant compared to others
  • synonym: titan - a person or thing of enormous strength, size, or influence
