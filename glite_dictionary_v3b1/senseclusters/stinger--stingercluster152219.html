stinger

noun
short post‑credits teaser scene
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋə / (BrE)
(in film and television) a short scene or shot shown at the end of a programme or after the credits, often teasing a future development or delivering a final joke.

Examples

A movie stinger
The director promised a final stinger after the credits that would set up the sequel.
Fans stayed through the end because rumours said there were secret stingers after the screening.
The show's closing stinger delivered a surprising joke while promising a new season storyline later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: post-credits scene - a short scene that appears after the end credits of a film or show
  • synonym: tag - a brief final scene or joke at the end of a film, programme, or episode
