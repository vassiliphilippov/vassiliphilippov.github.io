currying

noun
grooming with a brush
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌriɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning or brushing an animal, especially a horse, using a currycomb or brush

Examples

Currying a horse
Regular currying helps keep a horse's coat shiny and healthy.
The stablehand spent the morning currying the horses before their exercise.
Proper currying removes dirt and loose hair from the animal's body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brushing - the act of cleaning or smoothing with a brush
  • synonym: grooming - the act of cleaning and caring for an animal's coat
