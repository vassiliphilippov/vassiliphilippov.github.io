draftiness

noun
cold air currents
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftinəs / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftinəs / (BrE)
the quality of a place having cold air currents moving through it, often making it uncomfortable

Examples

Winter draftiness
The draftiness of the old apartment made it difficult to stay comfortable in winter.
Heavy curtains can help reduce the draftiness in a room with old windows.
We noticed the draftiness as soon as we walked into the basement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chilliness - the quality of being cold or cool
  • antonym: warmth - the quality of being warm or having heat
