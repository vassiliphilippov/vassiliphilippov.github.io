ecofeminist

noun
environmental feminist
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkoʊˈfɛmənɪst / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who connects environmental issues with feminist perspectives, advocating for both ecological and gender equality

Examples

An ecofeminist activist
The ecofeminist argued that environmental destruction and gender inequality are deeply connected.
As an ecofeminist, she campaigns for both women's rights and sustainable agriculture.
Many ecofeminists believe that caring for the earth is linked to social justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environmentalist - a person who is concerned with protecting the environment
  • synonym: feminist - a person who supports equal rights for women
