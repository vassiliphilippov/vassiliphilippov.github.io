ecofeminist

noun
environmental feminist
8.1
Pronunciation: /iː.koʊ.ˈfem.ɪ.nɪst/ /; / /ɪ.koʊ.ˈfem.ɪ.nɪst/ (AmE), /iː.kəʊ.ˈfem.ɪ.nɪst/ /; / /ɪ.kəʊ.ˈfem.ɪ.nɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

An ecofeminist activist

As an ecofeminist, she campaigns for both women's rights and sustainable agriculture.
Many ecofeminists believe that caring for the earth is linked to social justice.
