ripsaw

noun
handsaw for cutting with the grain
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈrɪpˌsɔː / (BrE)
a handsaw designed for cutting wood along the grain

Examples

A ripsaw tool
A ripsaw is essential for making clean, straight cuts with the grain of the wood.
He sharpened his old ripsaw before starting the project.
The carpenter selected a ripsaw to slice the board along its length.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crosscut saw - a saw for cutting across the grain of wood
