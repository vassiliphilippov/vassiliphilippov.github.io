Gene Kelly

proper noun
American dancer and actor
Pronunciation: / dʒiːn ˈkɛli / (AmE), / dʒiːn ˈkɛli / (BrE)
(1912–1996) an American dancer, actor, singer, and choreographer, best known for his roles in classic musical films such as 'Singin' in the Rain'.

Examples

Gene Kelly dancer actor
Gene Kelly starred in one of the most beloved musical films, 'Singin' in the Rain'.
Many aspiring dancers have cited Gene Kelly as a major influence on their careers.
The choreography of Gene Kelly combined athleticism and grace in an innovative way.
