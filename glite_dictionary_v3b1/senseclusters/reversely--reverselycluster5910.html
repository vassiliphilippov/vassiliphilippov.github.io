reversely

adverb
in an opposite or reversed way
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrsli / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
In an opposite direction, order, or manner; so as to be reversed.

Examples

In reversely opposite order
Her opinion was reversely opposed to his on this subject.
Light enters the fiber in one direction and exits reversely at the other end.
The process can also operate reversely, cooling materials instead of heating them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversely - introducing an idea that reverses what was previously said
