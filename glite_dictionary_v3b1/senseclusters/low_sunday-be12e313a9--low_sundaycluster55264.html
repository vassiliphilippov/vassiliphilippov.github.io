Low Sunday

noun
the Sunday after Easter in Christianity
7.9
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˈsʌndeɪ / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈsʌndeɪ / (BrE)
the Sunday following Easter, regarded as the first Sunday after Easter Day in the Christian calendar

Examples

Low Sunday after Easter
After the festivities of Easter, Low Sunday offers a quieter time for reflection.
Many churches hold special services on Low Sunday to mark the end of the Easter octave.
The choir sang a beautiful hymn during Low Sunday Mass this year.
