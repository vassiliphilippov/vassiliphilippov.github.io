my bad

interjection
informal apology for a mistake
Pronunciation: / maɪ bæd / (AmE), / maɪ bæd / (BrE)
used to admit responsibility for a mistake or error; an informal apology meaning 'my fault' or 'sorry'

Examples

My bad sorry
I totally forgot about our lunch plans—my bad!
If your project didn't print, my bad; I should have checked the printer ink.
It was my bad that I didn't lock the door last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorry - expressing an apology or regret
