Helladic civilization

noun
Bronze Age Greek mainland culture
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɛˈlædɪk sɪˌvɪləˈzeɪʃən /; / hɛˈlædɪk ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / hɛˈlædɪk sɪˌvɪləˈzeɪʃən /; / hɛˈlædɪk ˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the main Bronze Age culture of mainland Greece, which flourished from about 2500 to 1100 BC

Examples

The Helladic civilization period
