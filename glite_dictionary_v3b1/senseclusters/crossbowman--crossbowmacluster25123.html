crossbowman

noun
person using crossbow
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌboʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsbəʊmən / (BrE)
a soldier or person who uses a crossbow, especially in battle or warfare

Examples

The crossbowman aims

Medieval armies often included at least one crossbowman in each squad for ranged attacks.
