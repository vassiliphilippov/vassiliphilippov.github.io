under the hood

adverb
inside a vehicle's engine or internal workings
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ ðə hʊd / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə hʊd / (BrE)
inside the engine compartment of a vehicle; also, examining or relating to the internal workings or details of a system or device, especially beyond what is immediately visible.

Examples

Under the hood of a car
If you're curious about how the software works under the hood, check the documentation.
Our IT specialist spent hours looking under the hood to fix the server problem.
The new smartphone has cutting-edge technology under the hood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: behind the scenes - not visible to the public; happening privately or internally
  • antonym: surface - the outside layer or appearance; contrasted with what is internal or hidden
