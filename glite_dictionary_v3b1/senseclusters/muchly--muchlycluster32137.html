muchly

adverb
to a great extent
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌtʃli / (AmE), / ˈmʌtʃli / (BrE)
to a great degree or very much; chiefly used in older English or for humorous effect

Examples

Thank you muchly
He appreciated their help muchly and promised to return the favor.
The old letter read, 'I miss you muchly and hope to see you soon.'
She was muchly amused by the children's antics at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greatly - to a large or considerable extent
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree or extent
