graphophone

noun
early sound recorder
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡræfəfəʊn / (BrE)
an early device for recording and playing back sound, developed as an improvement on the phonograph

Examples

The graphophone device
The graphophone was a significant step forward in the history of sound recording technology.
At the museum, visitors can see a restored graphophone from the late nineteenth century.
Inventors improved upon the phonograph by creating the graphophone with better sound quality.
