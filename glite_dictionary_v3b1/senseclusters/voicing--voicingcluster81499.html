voicing

noun
use of the voice
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of producing sounds with the voice, especially in speaking, singing, or providing character voices

Examples

Voicing a character
Her voicing of the main character brought the animated film to life.
Proper voicing is important for singers to project their sound clearly.
The actor's unique voicing made the cartoon character instantly recognizable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: silence - the absence of sound or voice
  • synonym: vocalization - the act of producing sounds with the voice
