umbelliferous plant

noun
plant of the Umbelliferae family
Pronunciation: / ˌʌm.bəˈlɪf.ər.əs plænt / (AmE), / ˌʌm.bəˈlɪf.ər.əs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant belonging to the family Umbelliferae, typically aromatic and having umbrella-shaped flower clusters

Examples

Umbelliferous plants family
Botanists often study umbelliferous plants to understand their medicinal and aromatic properties.
Many culinary herbs, such as parsley and dill, are classified as umbelliferous plants.
The flowers of an umbelliferous plant usually form a characteristic umbrella-like shape.

Related Terms
