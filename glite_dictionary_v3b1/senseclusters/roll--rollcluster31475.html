roll

noun
rotation of aircraft around its axis
3.1 6.0
Pronunciation: / roʊl / (AmE), / rəʊl / (BrE)
a flight maneuver in which an aircraft rotates around its longitudinal axis without changing direction or losing altitude

Examples

An airplane roll
Learning how to perform a roll is essential in advanced flight training.

The pilot executed a perfect roll during the airshow.
