satyric

adjective
relating to or like a satyr
Pronunciation: / səˈtɪrɪk / (AmE), / səˈtɪrɪk / (BrE)
relating to, resembling, or characteristic of a satyr, especially with regard to lasciviousness or lewdness.

Examples

Satyric gaze
She was wary of his satyric gaze, which made her uncomfortable.
The painting depicted a satyric figure surrounded by dancing nymphs.
The story features a satyric character who is known for his mischievous behavior.
