pulmonary valve

noun
heart valve at right ventricle outlet
4.5 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpʊl.mə.nər.iː vælv/ (AmE), /ˈpʌl.mə.nə.riː vælv/ (BrE)
the valve located between the right ventricle of the heart and the pulmonary artery, which prevents blood from flowing back into the ventricle

Examples

The pulmonary valve function
A defective pulmonary valve can lead to backflow of blood into the heart.
Doctors can sometimes replace a damaged pulmonary valve with an artificial one.
The surgeon checked the condition of the pulmonary valve before beginning the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aortic valve - the valve between the left ventricle and the aorta
  • synonym: mitral valve - the valve between the left atrium and the left ventricle
  • synonym: tricuspid valve - the valve between the right atrium and the right ventricle
