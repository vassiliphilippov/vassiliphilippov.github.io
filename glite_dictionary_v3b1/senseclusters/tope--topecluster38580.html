tope

noun
small shark or monument
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊp / (AmE), / ˈtəʊp / (BrE)
a small species of shark found in temperate waters, or in South Asia, a Buddhist or Jain religious monument

Examples

A tope shark
The fisherman was excited to catch a tope off the coast this morning.
During their trip to India, they visited an ancient tope built centuries ago.
Marine biologists study the migration patterns of the tope in temperate seas.
