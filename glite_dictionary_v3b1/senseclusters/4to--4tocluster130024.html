4to

noun
book size made by folding sheet twice (quarto)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəʊ / (BrE)
A book size in which each sheet of paper is folded twice to form four leaves (eight pages); quarto.

Examples

A 4to book
Collectors often value 4tos for their manageable size and historical significance.
The library owns a rare Shakespeare 4to printed in 1600.
This edition is a large 4to with beautifully illustrated pages.
