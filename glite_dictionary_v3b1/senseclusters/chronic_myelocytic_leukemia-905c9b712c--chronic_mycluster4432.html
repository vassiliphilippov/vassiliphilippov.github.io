chronic myelocytic leukemia

A type of chronic leukemia characterized by an increased number of granular white blood cells, more common in older adults.

Examples

Chronic myelocytic leukemia in adults
Chronic myelocytic leukemia is most commonly found in adults over the age of 60.
After being diagnosed with chronic myelocytic leukemia, he started targeted therapy prescribed by his hematologist.
Patients with chronic myelocytic leukemia often show elevated white blood cell counts in blood tests.
