telecon

noun
informal teleconference
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
an informal term for a teleconference, a meeting held by telephone or video link

Examples

A team telecon
We have a telecon scheduled with the marketing team this afternoon.
During the telecon, everyone shared updates on their current projects.
She joined the telecon from her home office using her laptop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teleconference - a meeting held by telephone or video link
  • synonym: videoconference - a meeting held using video communication technology
