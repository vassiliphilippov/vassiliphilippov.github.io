Likert

noun
attitude rating scale
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪk.ərt/ (AmE), /ˈlɪk.ət/ (BrE)
a scale for measuring attitudes or opinions, usually with options from strong agreement to strong disagreement

Examples

Likert scale survey
The researchers used a Likert scale to measure participants' satisfaction with the service.
Each question on the survey was answered using a five-point Likert scale.
A typical Likert scale asks respondents to rate their agreement with a statement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rating scale - a tool for assigning a value to a response or opinion
  • synonym: survey - a method of collecting information from people
