stovewood

noun
wood for stoves
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊvˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈstəʊvˌwʊd / (BrE)
wood that has been cut into pieces of a size suitable for burning in a stove

Examples

Stacked stovewood
He stacked the stovewood neatly beside the cabin.
After chopping all morning, they had enough stovewood to last through the winter.
The old stove burned through a pile of stovewood every week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firewood - wood used as fuel for a fire
  • degree lower: kindling - small pieces of wood used to start a fire
