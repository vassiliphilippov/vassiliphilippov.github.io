hardball

noun
baseball, as opposed to softball
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A form of baseball played with a hard ball, used especially to distinguish it from softball.

Examples

A hardball game
After playing hardball for years, she found softball to be much easier.
Many professional athletes started their sports careers playing hardball as children.
The school offers both hardball and softball leagues for students.
