perchloroethylene

noun
dry cleaning solvent
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrklɔːroʊˈɛθɪliːn / (AmE), / ˌpɜːklɔːrəʊˈiːθɪliːn / (BrE)
a colorless, volatile chemical solvent widely used in dry cleaning and for removing grease

Examples

Perchloroethylene solvent
The dry cleaner uses perchloroethylene to remove tough stains from clothes.
Exposure to perchloroethylene should be minimized due to potential health risks.
Many industrial degreasers contain perchloroethylene because of its strong cleaning power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solvent - a substance that dissolves other substances
  • synonym: tetrachloroethylene - another name for perchloroethylene, a chemical solvent
