last resort

noun
final option in desperate situations
Pronunciation: / læst rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / lɑːst rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
the final option or action to take when all other possibilities have failed

Examples

A last resort option
Calling the police should be a last resort, not your first reaction.
If negotiations fail, military action may be considered a last resort.
She saw borrowing money from her parents as a last resort.
Borrowing money from friends should only be a last resort.
If all else fails, surgery will be used as a last resort.
Negotiation was their last resort before declaring a strike.

Related Terms

  • antonym: first resort - the initial or preferred option
  • synonym: last ditch - a final attempt or measure, especially when everything else has failed
