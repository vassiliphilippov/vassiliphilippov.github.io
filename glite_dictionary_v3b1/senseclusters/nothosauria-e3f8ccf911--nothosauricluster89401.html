Nothosauria

noun
suborder of extinct Triassic marine reptiles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊθəˈsɔriə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊθəˈsɔːrɪə / (BrE)
a suborder of extinct marine reptiles belonging to Sauropterygia, known from the Triassic period

Examples

Fossil Nothosauria bones
Fossils of Nothosauria have been discovered in sedimentary rocks dating back to the Triassic period.
Scientists believe that the Nothosauria adapted to an aquatic lifestyle with their elongated bodies and paddle-like limbs.
The study of Nothosauria provides valuable insights into the evolution of early marine reptiles.
