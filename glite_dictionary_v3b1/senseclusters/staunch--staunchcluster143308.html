staunch

verb
stop the flow of liquid, especially blood
Pronunciation: / stɑntʃ / (AmE), / stɔːntʃ / (BrE)
to stop the flow of a liquid, especially blood, from a wound or source

Examples

To staunch bleeding
He pressed hard on the cut to staunch the blood.
Quick action can staunch the flow of water from a broken pipe.
The nurse tried to staunch the bleeding with a clean cloth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: block - to obstruct or prevent movement or flow
  • synonym: stem - to stop or restrict the flow of something, especially liquid
