divinyl ether

noun
volatile liquid; former inhalation anesthetic
9.6
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvaɪnɪl ˈiːθər / (AmE), / daɪˈvɪnɪl ˈiːθə / (BrE)
a colorless, volatile, highly flammable liquid formerly used as an inhalation anesthetic

Examples

Due to its flammability, divinyl ether is no longer commonly used in modern medicine.
