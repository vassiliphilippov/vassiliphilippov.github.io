spread

noun
an impressive, abundant meal
4.1
Pronunciation: / sprɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd / (BrE)
a large meal with a variety of dishes, especially one that is impressive and abundant

Examples

A picnic spread
Everyone contributed to the picnic spread, making it a true feast for all.
For the family reunion, Grandma prepared quite a spread with all our favorite dishes.
We arrived at the party and found an incredible spread laid out on the dining table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a formal, elaborate meal for many people
  • synonym: feast - a large, celebratory meal
  • antonym: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
