political dissident

noun
opponent of official political policies
6.4
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl dɪˈsɪd.ənt / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl dɪˈsɪd.ənt / (BrE)
A person who actively opposes or challenges the official policies, doctrines, or authority of a political system or government.

Examples

A political dissident activist
As a political dissident, he faced constant surveillance and harassment.
Many political dissidents were forced into exile for speaking out against the regime.
The government arrested several political dissidents following the protests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissident - a person who opposes official policy, especially in an authoritarian state
