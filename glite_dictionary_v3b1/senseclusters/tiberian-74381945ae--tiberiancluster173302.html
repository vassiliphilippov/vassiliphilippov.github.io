Tiberian

adjective
Hebrew vocalization system
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈbɪriən / (AmE), / taɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the system of Hebrew pronunciation and vowel marks developed by the Masoretes of Tiberias in the Middle Ages

Examples

Tiberian vocalization
The Tiberian vocalization system is still used in the study of biblical Hebrew.
Scholars compare Tiberian pronunciation with other ancient Hebrew traditions.
The manuscript was annotated with Tiberian vowel marks for clarity.
