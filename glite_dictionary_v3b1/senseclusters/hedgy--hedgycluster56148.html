hedgy

adjective
full of hedges
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdʒi / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʒi / (BrE)
having many hedges, bordered by hedges, or resembling a hedge or hedgerow

Examples

Hedgy fields
The hedgy countryside was perfect for a quiet afternoon walk.
They bought a house on a hedgy lane that felt secluded and peaceful.
In spring, the hedgy borders of the garden burst into colorful bloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leafy - having many leaves or covered with foliage
  • antonym: open - not enclosed or bordered by anything
