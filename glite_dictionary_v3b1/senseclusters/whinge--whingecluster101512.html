whinge

verb
to complain persistently
Pronunciation: / wɪndʒ / (AmE), / wɪndʒ / (BrE)
to complain in an annoying, persistent, or trivial way, especially about something minor

Examples

To whinge about rain
He would often whinge about the weather, even on sunny days.

They whinged for hours about the slow service at the restaurant.
He tends to whinge whenever things do not go his way.
Stop whinging about the weather and enjoy your holiday.
She will whinge for hours if her coffee is not made just right.
