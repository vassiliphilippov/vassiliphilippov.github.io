narrow-minded

not willing to accept or consider ideas, beliefs, or behaviors that are different from one's own; lacking tolerance or flexibility

Examples

A narrow-minded person
Her parents are quite narrow-minded about what careers are acceptable for their children.
It is difficult to have a productive debate with someone so narrow-minded.
The committee was criticized for its narrow-minded approach to the problem.
His narrow-minded approach prevented any real progress on the project.
It is narrow-minded to judge people solely on their appearance.
She found her parents' narrow-minded views on education frustrating.

Synonyms

  • closed-minded
    5.2 0.39/m
    closed-minded describes someone who refuses new ideas or views, while narrow-minded means not open or tolerant.
  • intolerant
    4.6 3.83/m
    intolerant shows active dislike or refusal to accept others' views, while narrow-minded is more general unwillingness.
  • illiberal
    8.2 0.33/m
    illiberal sounds formal and often appears in politics, while narrow-minded is more common in everyday talk.
  • bigoted
    7.8 3.42/m
    bigoted is strongly hostile and unfair toward other groups, while narrow-minded can mean simply unwilling to accept difference.
  • unreceptive
    6.3 0.17/m
    unreceptive means not willing to listen to new ideas or offers, while narrow-minded covers wider intolerance.
  • insular
    8.1 1.39/m
    insular describes a person or place closed off from other cultures, while narrow-minded focuses on low tolerance for ideas.
  • blinkered
    8.1 0.28/m
    blinkered suggests a short, limited view like wearing blinders, while narrow-minded is a general lack of openness.
  • small-minded
    6.5 0.77/m
    small-minded means petty and spiteful about small things, while narrow-minded refers mainly to lack of openness.
  • hidebound
    9.9 0.23/m
    hidebound is stuck in old customs and resists change, while narrow-minded is not open to new views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bigoted - having strong, unreasonable beliefs and disliking others with different views

  • synonym: prejudiced - having or showing a dislike or distrust based on preconceived ideas
  • antonym: tolerant - showing willingness to allow opinions or behavior that you do not necessarily agree with
