Synonyms

  • closed-minded
    5.2 0.4/m
    closed-minded describes someone who refuses new ideas or views, while narrow-minded means not open or tolerant.
  • intolerant
    4.6 3/m
    intolerant shows active dislike or refusal to accept others' views, while narrow-minded is more general unwillingness.
  • illiberal
    8.2 0.08/m
    illiberal sounds formal and often appears in politics, while narrow-minded is more common in everyday talk.
  • bigoted
    7.8 2/m
    bigoted is strongly hostile and unfair toward other groups, while narrow-minded can mean simply unwilling to accept difference.
  • unreceptive
    6.3 0.04/m
    unreceptive means not willing to listen to new ideas or offers, while narrow-minded covers wider intolerance.
  • insular
    8.1 0.6/m
    insular describes a person or place closed off from other cultures, while narrow-minded focuses on low tolerance for ideas.
  • blinkered
    8.1 0.1/m
    blinkered suggests a short, limited view like wearing blinders, while narrow-minded is a general lack of openness.
  • small-minded
    6.5 0.8/m
    small-minded means petty and spiteful about small things, while narrow-minded refers mainly to lack of openness.
  • hidebound
    9.9 0.1/m
    hidebound is stuck in old customs and resists change, while narrow-minded is not open to new views.
