SCF

noun
gas volume unit
Pronunciation: / ɛs si ɛf / (AmE), / ɛs si ɛf / (BrE)
a unit of volume equal to one cubic foot of gas at standard temperature and pressure, used for measuring natural gas

Examples

1,000 SCF of gas
The contract specified delivery of 5,000 SCF of natural gas per day.
Engineers calculated the total SCF required to supply the factory for a month.
Natural gas prices are often quoted per SCF in the United States.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: MMscf - a unit representing one million standard cubic feet of gas
  • synonym: cubic foot - a unit of volume equal to a cube with sides of one foot
