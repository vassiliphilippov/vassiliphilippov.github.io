machinable

adjective
suitable for machining
4.1
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈʃiː.nə.bəl/ (AmE), /mə.ˈʃiː.nə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be shaped, cut, or worked on by a machine, especially in manufacturing processes

Examples

Machinable metal parts
Stainless steel is less machinable than aluminum, making it harder to cut and shape.
The engineer selected a highly machinable plastic for the prototype parts.
Choosing a machinable material can reduce production time and costs in manufacturing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unworkable - not able to be shaped or manipulated
  • synonym: workable - able to be shaped or manipulated
