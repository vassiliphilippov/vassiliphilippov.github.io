orbit

verb
move in a curved path around a body

to move in a curved path around a planet, star, or other body, especially as a result of gravitational forces

Examples

To orbit the Earth
If an object is thrown fast enough, it will orbit the planet instead of falling back down.
Satellites orbit the Earth at incredible speeds.
The probe has been orbiting Mars for over a year now.
