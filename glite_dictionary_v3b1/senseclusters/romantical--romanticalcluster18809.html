romantical

adjective
Romantic movement style
8.6
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæntɪkəl / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæntɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the Romantic movement in art, literature, or music, emphasizing emotion, nature, and imagination

Examples

Romantical poetry
The poet's romantical verses celebrated the beauty and power of untamed nature.
Her paintings have a distinctly romantical style, full of emotion and dramatic scenery.
The concert featured romantical music inspired by 19th-century composers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classical - relating to the Classical period in art, literature, or music
  • synonym: romantic - relating to the Romantic movement or its qualities
