clap

noun
unit of online approval (e.g., Medium)
7.9
Pronunciation: / klæp / (AmE), / klæp / (BrE)
A unit of online approval on certain publishing or social platforms; a count showing that a reader has expressed support or appreciation for a post or article.

Examples

A clap on Medium
The article earned thousands of claps on Medium after the author shared it widely.
She sent ten claps to the blogger to show strong support for the helpful guide.
I gave the post a single clap because the idea was interesting but briefly explained.

Related Terms

  • synonym: applause - a show of approval or praise (physical or metaphorical)
  • synonym: like - a single click or tap expressing approval of online content
  • synonym: upvote - an online vote indicating approval or agreement, often increasing content visibility
