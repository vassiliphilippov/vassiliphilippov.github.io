smirky

adjective
sly or smug expression
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɜːrki / (AmE), / ˈsmɜːki / (BrE)
showing or having a sly, smug, or self-satisfied smile or expression

Examples

Smirky smile
He gave a smirky grin after hearing the news.
Her smirky attitude made it clear she thought she had won.
The student looked smirky when he answered the question correctly before anyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grinning - smiling broadly, often in a self-satisfied way
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: smug - showing excessive pride or self-satisfaction
