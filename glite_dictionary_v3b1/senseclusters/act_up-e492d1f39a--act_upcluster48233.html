act up

verb
(of a body part/condition) cause pain again
Pronunciation: / ækt ʌp / (AmE), / ækt ʌp / (BrE)
(of a part of the body or a medical condition) to cause pain or become a problem again after a period of improvement.

Examples

My knee acts up
Her allergy tends to act up in the springtime.
If your back acts up again, you should see a doctor.
My knees start to act up whenever the weather changes.
