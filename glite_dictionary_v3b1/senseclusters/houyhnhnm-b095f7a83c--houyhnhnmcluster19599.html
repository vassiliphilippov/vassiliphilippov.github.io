Houyhnhnm

noun
intelligent horse in Swift's 'Gulliver's Travels'
Pronunciation: / huːˈhɪnəm / (AmE), / huːˈɪnəm / (BrE)
a member of a fictional race of intelligent horses who govern the Yahoos, in Jonathan Swift's novel 'Gulliver's Travels'
