genderless

adjective
without gender
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndələs / (BrE)
not having a specific gender or not associated with any gender

Examples

Genderless fashion style
The designer created a genderless clothing line that appeals to everyone.
Some languages use genderless pronouns to refer to people.
The new policy encourages a genderless approach to job titles and uniforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: androgynous - having both male and female characteristics or appearing neither male nor female
  • antonym: gendered - having or associated with a specific gender
