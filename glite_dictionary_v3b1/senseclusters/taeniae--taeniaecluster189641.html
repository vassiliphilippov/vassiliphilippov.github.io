taeniae

noun
parasitic tapeworms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːniˌiː / (AmE), / ˈtiːnɪˌiː / (BrE)
the plural form of taenia, referring to parasitic tapeworms of the genus Taenia that infect the intestines of vertebrates

Examples

Intestinal taeniae
The veterinarian found several taeniae in the dog's intestines during the laboratory examination.
Farmers must regularly check livestock for taeniae to prevent the spread of disease.
Microscopic analysis revealed the presence of taeniae in the contaminated meat sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tapeworms - parasitic flatworms that live in the intestines of animals
