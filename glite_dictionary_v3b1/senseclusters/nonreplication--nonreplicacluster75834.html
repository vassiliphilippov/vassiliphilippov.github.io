nonreplication

noun
failure to repeat results
8.7
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rə.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nɑːn.rɪ.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /nɒn.re.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nɒn.rɪ.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
