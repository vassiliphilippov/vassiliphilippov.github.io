Pinnotheridae

noun
family of tiny soft-bodied crabs
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪnəˈθɛrɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌpɪnəˈθɛrɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, soft-bodied crabs that often live inside or with other marine animals, such as mollusks
