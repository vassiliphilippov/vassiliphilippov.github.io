dust cloud

noun
a visible cloud of airborne dust
3.8 2.5
Pronunciation: /dʌst klaʊd/ (AmE), /dʌst klaʊd/ (BrE)
a mass of dust particles suspended in the air, forming a visible cloud

Examples

A large dust cloud

A sudden dust cloud made it hard for the runners to see the finish line.
The wind swept across the field, raising a dust cloud that obscured the crops.
