inkblot

noun
blot or spot made by ink
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkˌblɑt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌblɒt / (BrE)
A blot or spot made by ink, often irregular in shape; especially one used in psychological testing to interpret perceptions.

Examples

An inkblot test
An accidental inkblot ruined the letter she was writing.
Children often make up stories based on random inkblot patterns.
The psychologist showed him an inkblot and asked what he saw.
