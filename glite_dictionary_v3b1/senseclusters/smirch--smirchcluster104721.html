smirch

noun
a dirt mark or stain
6.5
Pronunciation: / smɝːtʃ / (AmE), / smɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a mark or stain, especially one made by dirt, that spoils the appearance of something

Examples

A smirch mark
She tried to wipe the smirch from her child's face before the photo was taken.
The fresh shirt was ruined by a smirch of ink on the sleeve.
Years of neglect had left a visible smirch on the once-beautiful painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean; absence of dirt or stains
  • synonym: smudge - a blurred or smeared mark, often left by rubbing
  • synonym: stain - a colored patch or dirty mark that is difficult to remove
