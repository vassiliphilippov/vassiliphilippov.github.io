fictionalize

verb
to turn real events into fiction
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪkʃənəˌlaɪz /; / ˈfɪk.ʃə.nəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪz /; / ˈfɪk.ʃə.nə.laɪz / (BrE)
to present real events, people, or facts as fiction, often by changing or adding details
