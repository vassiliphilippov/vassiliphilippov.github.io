CRS

noun
mapping coordinate system
8.2
Pronunciation: /siː ɑːr es/ (AmE), /siː ɑː es/ (BrE)
an abbreviation for Coordinate Reference System, a framework used in mapping and GIS to define how spatial data corresponds to real-world locations

Examples

CRS in mapping
Before importing the data, make sure the CRS matches the one used by your project.
Different countries may use a different CRS for their official maps.
The GIS analyst explained how to convert coordinates from one CRS to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coordinate system - a system for specifying positions in space using numbers or coordinates
  • synonym: projection - a method of representing the curved surface of the earth on a flat map
