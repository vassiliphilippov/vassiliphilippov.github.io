arete

noun
sharp mountain ridge
5.4
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛt / (AmE), / əˈrɛt / (BrE)
a sharp, narrow ridge of rock that forms between two valleys, typically found in rugged mountains

Examples

A sharp arete
Glacial erosion sculpted a dramatic arete that separates the two valleys.
The climbers carefully made their way along the exposed arete of the mountain.
The narrow arete offered breathtaking views on either side.
