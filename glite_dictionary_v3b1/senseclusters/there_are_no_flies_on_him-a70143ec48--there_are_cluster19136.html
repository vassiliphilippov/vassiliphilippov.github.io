there are no flies on him

x
quick-witted or clever (idiom)
Pronunciation: / ðɛr ɑr noʊ flaɪz ɑn hɪm / (AmE), / ðeə ɑː nəʊ flaɪz ɒn hɪm / (BrE)
used to say that a person is quick-witted, clever, or not easily deceived

Examples

There are no flies on him in business
Everyone knows there are no flies on him when it comes to business deals.
She tried to trick him, but there are no flies on him.
You won't catch him making a mistake—there are no flies on him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: naive - lacking experience and easily deceived
  • synonym: sharp - mentally quick and intelligent
