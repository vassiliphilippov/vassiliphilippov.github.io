douche

noun
body cavity cleansing with water or fluid
Pronunciation: / duːʃ / (AmE), / duːʃ / (BrE)
A stream of water or medicated fluid directed into or around a body cavity, especially the vagina, as part of medical cleansing or treatment.

Examples

A douche of water
Many health experts advise against frequent vaginal douches due to potential health risks.
She purchased a medicated douche on the advice of her physician.
The doctor recommended using a douche to help treat the infection.
