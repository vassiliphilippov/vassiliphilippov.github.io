revealing

adjective
showing or making known
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvilɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈviːlɪŋ / (BrE)
making something known or showing information that was previously hidden or secret

Examples

A revealing comment
His revealing comments shed light on the company's future plans.
She gave a revealing interview about her childhood experiences.
The dress was considered too revealing for the formal event.
Her revealing answer hinted at more than she intended to share.
His revealing smile let everyone know the secret was out.
The detective noticed a revealing fingerprint on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concealing - keeping something hidden or secret
  • synonym: exposing - making something visible, typically something hidden
  • synonym: telling - showing or indicating something, especially unintentionally
  • synonym: telltale - revealing or indicating something, often unintentionally or as evidence
