dimethyl sulfoxide

noun
chemical solvent, DMSO
Pronunciation: / daɪˈmɛθəl sʌlˈfɑːksaɪd /; / daɪˈmɛθəl sʌlˈfɒksaɪd / (AmE), / daɪˈmɛθəl sʌlˈfɒksaɪd /; / daɪˈmiːθəl sʌlˈfɒksaɪd / (BrE)
