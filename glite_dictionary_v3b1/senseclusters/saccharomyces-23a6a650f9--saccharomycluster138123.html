Saccharomyces

noun
genus of yeast used in fermentation
7.2
Pronunciation: /sæk.ə.roʊ.ˈmaɪ.siːz/ (AmE), /sæk.ə.rəʊ.ˈmaɪ.siːz/ (BrE)
a genus of single-celled yeasts that reproduce by budding and are used to ferment sugars, especially in baking and brewing
