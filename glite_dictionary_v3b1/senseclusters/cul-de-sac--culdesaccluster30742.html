Synonyms

  • dead-end street
    2.1 0.2/m
    dead-end street is the simple English name for a road closed at one end, while cul-de-sac is the French-derived term.
  • dead end
    2.7 1/m
    dead end is a short, informal name for a road that stops, while cul-de-sac is often used in maps or signs.
  • blind alley
    4.1 0.01/m
    blind alley is an older or regional term for a street with one exit, while cul-de-sac sounds more formal.
  • impasse
    8.1 0.04/m
    impasse is mainly used to mean a situation with no progress, while cul-de-sac names a physical dead-end street.
