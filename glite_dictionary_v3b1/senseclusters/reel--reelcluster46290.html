reel

verb
to feel shocked or unsteady
4.5
Pronunciation: /riːl/ /; / /ˈriː.əl/ (AmE), /riːl/ /; / /ˈriː.əl/ (BrE)
to feel very shocked, confused, or unsteady, often as a reaction to unexpected events

Examples

To reel from shock
He was reeling after hearing about the sudden job loss.
The city was left reeling by the unexpected storm that caused widespread damage.
She tried to focus, but her mind kept reeling from the shocking news.

Related Terms
