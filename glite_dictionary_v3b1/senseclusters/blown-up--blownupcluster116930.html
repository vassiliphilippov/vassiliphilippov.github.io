blown-up

adjective
viral; widely shared online
Pronunciation: / bloʊn ˈʌp / (AmE), / bləʊn ˈʌp / (BrE)
(informal) (of online content) that has become suddenly very popular and widely shared; viral.

Examples

Blown-up video online
The comedian's clip turned into a blown-up sensation on social media.
After the influencer shared it, the song was suddenly blown-up across multiple platforms.
They posted a short sketch that became blown-up overnight and reached millions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unnoticed - not seen or paid attention to; the opposite of widely shared
  • synonym: viral - rapidly and widely shared or viewed online
