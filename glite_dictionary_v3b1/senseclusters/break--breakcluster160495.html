break

noun
the act of breaking something
3.7 1.0
Pronunciation: / breɪk / (AmE), / breɪk / (BrE)
an act of breaking or causing something to separate into pieces, especially suddenly or by force; a fracture or crack in hard tissue such as a bone; an instance or act of something being broken or breaking apart
