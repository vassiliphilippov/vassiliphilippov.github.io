Genista tinctoria

noun
yellow-flowered dye shrub (dyer's greenweed)
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɪstə tɪŋkˈtɔriə / (AmE), / dʒɪˈnɪstə tɪŋkˈtɔːrɪə / (BrE)
A small Eurasian shrub with clusters of yellow flowers, yielding a dye; often found as a weed in Britain and the United States, and sometimes cultivated as an ornamental.
