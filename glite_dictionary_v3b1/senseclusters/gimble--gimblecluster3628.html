gimble

noun
small boring tool
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪm.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈgɪm.bəl/ (BrE)
a small hand tool for making holes, similar to a gimlet; now rare or dialectal

Examples

A gimble tool
The carpenter used a gimble to start a hole before inserting the screw.
In his grandfather's toolbox, he found an old gimble with a wooden handle.
Before power tools, a gimble was often used for small woodworking tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - a tool or machine for making holes in hard materials
  • synonym: gimlet - a small hand tool with a pointed screw tip for boring holes
