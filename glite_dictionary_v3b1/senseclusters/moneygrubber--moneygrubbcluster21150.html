moneygrubber

noun
person solely concerned with making money
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌɡrʌbɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌɡrʌbə / (BrE)
a person whose main concern is accumulating money, often to the exclusion of other interests or values

Examples

A moneygrubber person
His reputation as a moneygrubber made it hard for him to win public trust.
Many people saw the CEO as a moneygrubber who cared little for his employees.
She didn't want to be labeled a moneygrubber, so she donated part of her winnings to charity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
