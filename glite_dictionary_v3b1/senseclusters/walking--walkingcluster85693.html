walking

adjective
within walking distance; easily walkable
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Located or situated close enough to be reached by walking.

Examples

Walking distance

The store is within walking distance from my apartment.
