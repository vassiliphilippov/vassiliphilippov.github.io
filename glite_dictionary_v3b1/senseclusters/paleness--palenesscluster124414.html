paleness

noun
unnatural lack of skin color
1.0 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪl.nəs /; / ˈpeɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪl.nɪs /; / ˈpeɪlnɪs / (BrE)
