Synonyms

  • pallor
    8.3 0.89/m
    pallor is an often medical and literary word for unhealthy skin paleness, while paleness is more general.
  • pallidness
    8.2 0.00/m
    pallidness means lack of normal skin color and sounds more formal or rare, while paleness is common in speech.
  • pallidity
    9.9 0.00/m
    pallidity describes a state of lacking color and feels formal or clinical, while paleness is plain everyday description.
  • wanness
    9.7 0.00/m
    wanness looks old-fashioned or literary for someone looking very pale and weak, while paleness is neutral and common.
  • achromasia
    9.9 0.00/m
    achromasia is a medical term for an abnormal loss of skin color, while paleness is the everyday word for that condition.
