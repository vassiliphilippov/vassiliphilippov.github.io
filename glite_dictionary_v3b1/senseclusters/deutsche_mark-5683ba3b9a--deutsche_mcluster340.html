Deutsche Mark

noun
German currency before the euro
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɪtʃə mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈdɔɪtʃə mɑːk / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Germany before the adoption of the euro in 2002

Examples

Old Deutsche Mark coins
Collectors now seek rare Deutsche Mark coins and notes as valuable pieces of history.
Many tourists exchanged their dollars for Deutsche Marks when visiting Berlin before 2002.
The Deutsche Mark was replaced by the euro at the start of the new millennium.
