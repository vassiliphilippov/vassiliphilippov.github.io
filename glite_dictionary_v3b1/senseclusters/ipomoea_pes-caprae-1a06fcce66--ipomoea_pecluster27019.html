Ipomoea pes-caprae

noun
creeping coastal plant; beach morning glory
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.pəˈmiː.ə ˌpɛs.kəˈpreɪ / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.pəˈmiː.ɪə ˌpɛs.kæˈpraɪ / (BrE)
a sprawling perennial plant of coastal sand dunes, native from Florida to Texas, commonly known as beach morning glory or goat's foot.
