inclusionary

adjective
designed to include
6.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːʒəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːʒənəri / (BrE)
designed to include all people or groups, especially those who might otherwise be excluded

Examples

Inclusionary practices
The school implemented inclusionary practices to ensure all students felt welcome.
Many cities are adopting inclusionary zoning laws to promote diverse communities.
Her leadership style is highly inclusionary, encouraging input from everyone on the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusionary - designed to exclude certain people or groups
  • synonym: inclusive - including all people or groups
