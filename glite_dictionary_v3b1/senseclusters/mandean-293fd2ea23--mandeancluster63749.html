Mandean

noun
member of a Gnostic sect believing John the Baptist was the Messiah
9.9
Pronunciation: /mæn.ˈdiː.ən/ /; / /mæn.ˈdiːn/ (AmE), /mæn.ˈdiː.ən/ /; / /mæn.ˈdiːn/ (BrE)
