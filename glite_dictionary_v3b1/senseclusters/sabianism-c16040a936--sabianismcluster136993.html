Sabianism

noun
religion mentioned in the Quran, linked to Mandaeans
Pronunciation: / seɪˈbiː.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / seɪˈbiː.ə.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
the religion of a group mentioned in the Quran as entitled to Muslim religious toleration, often identified with the Mandaeans or the Elkesaites

Examples

Sabianism religion
He wrote extensively about Sabianism and its place among other monotheistic religions.
Medieval scholars often debated whether the Mandaeans practiced Sabianism as described in Islamic texts.
The Quran grants protection to practitioners of Sabianism alongside Jews and Christians.
