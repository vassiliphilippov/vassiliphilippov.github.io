phonemically

adverb
in a way relating to phonemes
9.5
Pronunciation: / fəˈnimɪkli / (AmE), / fəˈniːmɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to phonemes or phonemics; using or analyzing the sounds distinctive in a particular language.

Examples

Write phonemically
Instructions asked students to write each sentence phonemically rather than phonetically.
The analyst transcribed the words phonemically to illustrate the distinct sounds of the language.
The dictionary entries are organized phonemically, reflecting the contrastive units of sound.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: phonetic - relating to speech sounds; representing the sounds of speech
