juggle

noun
act of skillfully tossing and catching objects
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡl̩ / (BrE)
an act of throwing and catching several objects in the air at the same time, often as a performance or skill

Examples

A juggle with clubs
Each juggle took weeks of practice to perfect.
Her most impressive juggle involved five flaming torches at once.
The performer's juggle amazed the crowd at the circus.
