thurl

noun
hip joint of animal
Pronunciation: / θɜrl / (AmE), / θɜːl / (BrE)
the hip joint or region in the hindquarters of a quadruped, especially in cattle or horses

Examples

Horse's thurl
The farmer checked the horse's thurl for any swelling after the long ride.
In cattle judging, the position of the thurl is important for evaluating movement.
The veterinarian examined the injured thurl to determine the cause of the animal's limp.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shoulder - the joint connecting the forelimb to the body in animals
