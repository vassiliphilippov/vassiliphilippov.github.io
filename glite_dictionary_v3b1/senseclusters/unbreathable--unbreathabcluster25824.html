unbreathable

adjective
oppressively tense
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbriːðəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbriːðəbl̩ / (BrE)
so oppressive, tense, or unpleasant that it is difficult to stay in or tolerate, especially used figuratively of places or situations

Examples

Unbreathable atmosphere
The atmosphere in the office became unbreathable after the argument between the managers.
She found the tension at the dinner table almost unbreathable and struggled to make conversation.
After the bad news, the mood in the house was so unbreathable that everyone went to their rooms.
