quinoa

noun
high-protein edible seed used as a grain
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːnwɑː / (AmE), / ˈkiːnwɑː / (BrE)
a South American plant whose small edible seeds are high in protein and used as a grain substitute in cooking

Examples

Cooked quinoa seeds
After rinsing the quinoa, she cooked it with vegetables for a healthy dinner.
Gluten-free diets often recommend quinoa as a nutritious alternative to wheat.
Many people include quinoa in salads for extra protein and texture.
