stroke

noun
gentle touch with the hand
2.0
Pronunciation: / stroʊk / (AmE), / strəʊk / (BrE)
a gentle touch or caress made with the hand, or the act of moving your hand gently over something

Examples

Gentle stroke of the hand
A comforting stroke of the hand can calm nervous children.
His hand moved with a soft stroke over the smooth surface.
She gave the cat a gentle stroke along its back.
A reassuring stroke on the arm calmed her nerves.
He gave the cat a gentle stroke behind the ears.
With a soft stroke, she brushed the child's hair away from his face.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pat - a light stroke or tap, especially with the hand
