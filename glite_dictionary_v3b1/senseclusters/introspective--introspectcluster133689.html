introspective

adjective
inclined to self-examination
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈspɛk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈspɛk.tɪv / (BrE)
characterized by or inclined to examine one's own thoughts and feelings

Examples

An introspective person
She became more introspective after the sudden loss of her friend.
The retreat encouraged participants to become introspective and reflect on their personal growth.
Writers often have an introspective nature, spending hours pondering their thoughts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extroverted - outgoing and focused on the external world
  • synonym: reflective - thoughtful, especially about oneself
