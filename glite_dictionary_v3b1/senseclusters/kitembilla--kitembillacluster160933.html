kitembilla

noun
Indian gooseberry-like fruit with tart-sweet purple pulp
Pronunciation: / kɪˈtɛm.bɪ.lə / (AmE), / kɪˈtɛm.bɪ.lə / (BrE)
A maroon-purple fruit found in India, similar to a gooseberry, with tart-sweet purple pulp, often used for making preserves.
