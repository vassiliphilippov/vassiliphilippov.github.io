uncongealed

adjective
not solidified
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈdʒiːld / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈdʒiːld / (BrE)
not having solidified or thickened; still in a liquid or semi-liquid state

Examples

Uncongealed blood
The blood was still uncongealed when the doctor arrived at the scene.
After hours in the refrigerator, the soup remained uncongealed and watery.
The artist poured uncongealed wax onto the canvas to create a unique texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: congealed - having become thick or solid
  • synonym: liquid - in a fluid, not solid state
