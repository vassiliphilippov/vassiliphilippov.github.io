speaking trumpet

noun
cone-shaped device for amplifying the voice
6.4 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspiː.kɪŋ ˈtrʌm.pət/ /; / /ˈspiː.kɪŋ ˈtrʌm.pɪt/ (AmE), /ˈspiː.kɪŋ ˈtrʌm.pɪt/ /; / /ˈspiː.kɪŋ ˈtrʌm.pət/ (BrE)
