speaking trumpet

noun
cone-shaped device for amplifying the voice
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːkɪŋ ˌtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈspiːkɪŋ ˌtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
a cone-shaped device used to amplify and direct the human voice, especially before the invention of electronic loudspeakers

Examples

A speaking trumpet device
Before electronic amplification, performers used a speaking trumpet to address large crowds.
He found an old speaking trumpet in the ship's storage room, a reminder of earlier days.
The captain shouted orders through a speaking trumpet so the crew could hear over the wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: megaphone - a handheld device used to amplify the voice
