edible snail

noun
snail species eaten as food
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəbl̩ sneɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl̩ sneɪl / (BrE)
a type of snail that is suitable or commonly used for food, especially in European cuisine

Examples

The edible snail dish
Escargot is a French dish made from the edible snail Helix pomatia.
It takes special skill to prepare an edible snail properly for consumption.
Many gourmet restaurants serve edible snails as a delicacy.
