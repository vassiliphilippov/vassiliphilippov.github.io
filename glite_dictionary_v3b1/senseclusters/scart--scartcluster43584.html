scart

noun
audio-visual connector
8.7
Pronunciation: / skɑːrt / (AmE), / skɑːt / (BrE)
a type of connector used to link audio and video equipment, especially televisions and DVD players in Europe

Examples

Scart cable connection
He connected the DVD player to the TV using a scart cable.
Many older European televisions have a scart socket for connecting video devices.
The technician replaced the broken scart connector to restore the picture and sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: HDMI - a modern digital connector for audio and video devices
  • synonym: RCA - an older type of audio and video connector
