quaffer

noun
person who drinks heartily
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑfər / (AmE), / ˈkwɒfə / (BrE)
a person who drinks heartily, especially alcohol or other beverages, often in large quantities or with enthusiasm

Examples

A quaffer of beer
Although not a heavy quaffer herself, she enjoyed the festive atmosphere in the tavern.
At the party, John quickly gained a reputation as the most enthusiastic quaffer of craft beers.
The pub was filled with merry quaffers celebrating the holiday together.
