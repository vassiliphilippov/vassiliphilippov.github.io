water program

noun
plan to improve or manage water resources
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
an organized plan or set of actions for improving or managing a body of water to make it more useful

Examples

A water program plan
Community leaders discussed the benefits of the proposed water program at last night's meeting.
The city launched a new water program to clean up the river and improve recreation.
The government invested millions in a water program to manage drought and ensure water supply.
