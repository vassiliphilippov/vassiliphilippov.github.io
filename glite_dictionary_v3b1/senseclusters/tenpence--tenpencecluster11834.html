tenpence

noun
UK coin worth ten pennies
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌpɛns / (AmE), / ˈtɛnpəns / (BrE)
A coin of the United Kingdom equal to ten pennies, especially since the decimalisation of the currency in 1971.

Examples

A tenpence coin
He dropped a tenpence into the charity box before leaving the shop.
She found an old tenpence in her grandfather's collection of coins.
The price of the postcard was just tenpence when I visited London.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: penny - the basic monetary unit of the UK, equal to one hundredth of a pound
  • degree higher: shilling - a former British coin worth twelve old pence
  • synonym: tenpenny - costing ten pennies or worth ten pennies
