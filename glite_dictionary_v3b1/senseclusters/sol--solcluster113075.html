sol

noun
syllable for fifth note of scale (solfège)
7.5
Pronunciation: / soʊl / (AmE), / sɒl / (BrE)
the syllable used to name the fifth (dominant) note of a musical scale in the solfège (solmization) system; equivalent to the note G in fixed-do system.

Examples

Sol in music
In solfège, sol is followed by la as the sixth note.
In the key of C major, sol corresponds to the note G.
She practiced singing the scale from do up to sol and back down again.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: do - the first note of the scale in solfège
  • degree higher: la - the sixth note of the scale in solfège
  • synonym: so - alternate spelling for the same solfège note
