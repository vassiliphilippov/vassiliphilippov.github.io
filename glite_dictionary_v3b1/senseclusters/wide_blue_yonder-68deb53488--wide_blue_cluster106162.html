wide blue yonder

noun
the vast open sky
Pronunciation: / waɪd blu ˈjɑndɚ / (AmE), / waɪd blu ˈjɒndə / (BrE)
the vast open sky, especially as seen as inviting exploration or adventure

Examples

The wide blue yonder
After years in the city, she longed to escape to the wide blue yonder.
The adventurers set off into the wide blue yonder in search of new lands.
With retirement ahead, he looked forward to exploring the wide blue yonder.
