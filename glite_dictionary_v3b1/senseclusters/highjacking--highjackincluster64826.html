highjacking

noun
illegal seizure of a vehicle by force
5.8 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.dʒæk.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.dʒæk.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of unlawfully seizing a vehicle in transit, especially by force, often to commit robbery or extortion

Examples

A highjacking of a plane
After the highjacking, the police issued new safety guidelines for drivers.
He was convicted for his involvement in the highjacking of an armored truck.
The authorities are investigating a highjacking that took place on the highway last night.
