lukewarm

adjective
moderately warm (not hot or cold)
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈluːkˌwɔrm / (AmE), / ˈluːkˌwɔːm / (BrE)
(of liquid or food) only moderately warm; neither hot nor cold.

Examples

Lukewarm water
Please make sure the baby's bottle is lukewarm before feeding her.
She gave his idea a lukewarm reception, showing little enthusiasm.
The soup was only lukewarm, so I put it back in the microwave.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cold - having a low temperature, especially when compared to the temperature of the human body
  • degree higher: hot - having a high degree of heat or a high temperature
  • synonym: tepid - slightly warm; lukewarm
