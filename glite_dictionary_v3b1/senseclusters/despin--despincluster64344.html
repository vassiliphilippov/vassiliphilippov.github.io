despin

verb
to stop spinning
9.3
Pronunciation: / diːˈspɪn / (AmE), / diːˈspɪn / (BrE)
to reduce or stop the spinning motion of an object, especially a spacecraft or satellite

Examples

To despin a satellite
The engineers had to despin the satellite before it could deploy its solar panels.
Special thrusters are used to despin spacecraft after launch.
After entering orbit, the team worked quickly to despin the probe for its next maneuver.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spin - to turn around quickly in a circle
  • synonym: stabilize - to make something steady or stable
