bestially

adverb
in a beast-like or savage manner
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛstʃəli / (AmE), / ˈbɛstɪəli / (BrE)
In a violent, cruel, or savage manner resembling a beast.

Examples

To act bestially
She reacted bestially when threatened, as if all reason had left her.
The dictator ruled bestially, showing no mercy to his people.
The prisoner was bestially beaten by his captors.
