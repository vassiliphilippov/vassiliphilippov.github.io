holdback

noun
obstacle or restraint
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldˌbæk / (BrE)
something that prevents progress or holds something in place

Examples

A holdback to progress
Financial issues were the main holdback to the project's completion.
The lack of experience proved to be a significant holdback for the new team.
Heavy rain acted as a holdback during the construction of the bridge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aid - something that helps or supports progress
  • synonym: barrier - something that prevents movement or progress
  • synonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress
