terrazzo

noun
decorative flooring material
7.1
Pronunciation: / təˈrɑːtsoʊ / (AmE), / təˈrætsəʊ / (BrE)
a flooring or surface material made from chips of marble, glass, stone, or quartz set in cement or concrete and polished smooth
