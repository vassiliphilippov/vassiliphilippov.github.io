flop

verb
fall or drop heavily
2.3 2.0
Pronunciation: /flɑːp/ (AmE), /flɒp/ (BrE)
to fall, drop, sit, or lie down heavily and suddenly, often in a relaxed or tired way

Examples

He was so tired that he just flopped into the nearest chair without saying a word.
