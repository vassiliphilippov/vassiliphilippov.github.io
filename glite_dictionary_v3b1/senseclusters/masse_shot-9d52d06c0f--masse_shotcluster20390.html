masse shot

noun
billiards shot with a curved cue ball path
Pronunciation: / mæˈseɪ ʃɑt / (AmE), / mæˈseɪ ʃɒt / (BrE)
A shot in billiards in which the cue ball is struck with the cue held nearly vertically, causing it to curve sharply around another ball.

Examples

A masse shot in billiards
He demonstrated his skills with a masse shot that curved perfectly around the obstacle.
Learning to perform a masse shot requires practice and precise control of the cue.
The crowd cheered when she completed a difficult masse shot to sink the final ball.
