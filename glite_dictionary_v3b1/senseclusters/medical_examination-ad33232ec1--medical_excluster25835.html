medical examination

noun
doctor's health check-up
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmed.ɪ.kəl ɪɡ.zæm.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈmed.ɪ.kəl ɪɡ.zæm.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a thorough physical check-up by a doctor, often including various tests, to assess a person's health status.

Examples

Annual medical examination
Annual medical examinations are important for early detection of health problems.
Before starting the new job, she had to undergo a complete medical examination.
The doctor recommended a medical examination after he complained of persistent headaches.
