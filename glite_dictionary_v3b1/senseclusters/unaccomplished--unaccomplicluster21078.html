unaccomplished

adjective
not skilled or polished
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈkɑmplɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkɒmplɪʃt / (BrE)
lacking skill, experience, or refinement in a particular activity or field

Examples

Unaccomplished musician
The unaccomplished pianist struggled to keep up with the rest of the orchestra during the performance.
Despite being unaccomplished in painting, she decided to take an art class to improve her skills.
His unaccomplished writing style made the essay difficult to read and understand.
