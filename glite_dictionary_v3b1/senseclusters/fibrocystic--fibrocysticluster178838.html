fibrocystic

adjective
with fibrous tissue and cysts
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˈsɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbrəʊˈsɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the presence of both fibrous tissue and cysts in an organ or tissue

Examples

Fibrocystic breast changes
The doctor explained that fibrocystic breast changes are common and usually not harmful.
Her ultrasound showed fibrocystic tissue, which can sometimes cause discomfort.
Many women experience fibrocystic changes in their breasts during their reproductive years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrous - containing or resembling fibers
