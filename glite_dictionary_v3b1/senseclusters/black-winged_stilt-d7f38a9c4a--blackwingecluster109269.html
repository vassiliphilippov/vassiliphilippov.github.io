black-winged stilt

noun
a long-legged wading bird with black wings
Pronunciation: / ˌblæk ˌwɪŋd ˈstɪlt / (AmE), / ˌblæk ˌwɪŋd ˈstɪlt / (BrE)
a wading bird (Himantopus himantopus) of Europe, Africa, and Asia, with mostly white plumage and distinctive black wings

Examples

A black-winged stilt bird
A flock of black-winged stilts took flight as the sun set over the marsh.
Birdwatchers were delighted to spot a black-winged stilt wading in the lagoon.
The black-winged stilt nests in shallow wetlands across southern Europe and Asia.
