cluck

noun
the sound made by a hen
6.8
Pronunciation: / klʌk / (AmE), / klʌk / (BrE)
the short, sharp sound made by a hen, especially when calling her chicks

Examples

A hen's cluck
A sudden cluck came from the coop as the birds settled in for the night.
The mother hen gave a soft cluck to gather her chicks.
We heard the familiar cluck as we walked past the farmyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cackle - the loud, harsh sound made by a hen after laying an egg
