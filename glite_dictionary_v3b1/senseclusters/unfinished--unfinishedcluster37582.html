Synonyms

    4.0 0.00/m
    uncompleted sounds more formal and is less common in everyday speech, while unfinished is the neutral, common term.

    2.0 0.42/m
    undone often feels like not yet done or reversed, while unfinished describes something still incomplete or in progress.
  • incomplete
    3.4 11/m
    incomplete means parts or steps are missing, while unfinished focuses on the task not being brought to a final state.
  • unconcluded
    6.6 0.01/m
    unconcluded often refers to talks or decisions left open, while unfinished covers any work or project not completed.
