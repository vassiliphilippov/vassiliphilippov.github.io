yaje

noun
Amazonian hallucinogenic drink
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈheɪ / (AmE), / jɑːˈheɪ / (BrE)
a hallucinogenic drink made from Amazonian plants, traditionally used in spiritual ceremonies and also called ayahuasca

Examples

A yaje brew
The shaman prepared yaje for the ceremony.
Many travelers visit the Amazon to experience traditional yaje rituals with local guides.
Drinking yaje is believed to bring spiritual insight and healing in some cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ayahuasca - a hallucinogenic brew from the Amazon used in rituals
