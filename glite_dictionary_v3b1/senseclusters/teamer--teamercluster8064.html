teamer

noun
team member
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːmər / (AmE), / ˈtiːmə / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a team

Examples

Soccer teamer
As a dedicated teamer, he always encouraged his group during practice.
Every teamer must cooperate to achieve the best results in the competition.
The coach praised each teamer for their hard work and positive attitude.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opponent - a person who competes against you in a contest or game
  • synonym: teammate - a fellow member of the same team
