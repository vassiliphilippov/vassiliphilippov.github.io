supp

noun
informal for supplement
8.8
Pronunciation: / sʌp / (AmE), / sʌp / (BrE)
an informal short form of 'supplement', especially referring to a dietary supplement such as a vitamin or pill

Examples

A daily supp
He takes a daily supp to make sure he gets enough vitamins.
Many athletes use a protein supp after their workouts.
She asked her doctor if she should add a new supp to her routine.
