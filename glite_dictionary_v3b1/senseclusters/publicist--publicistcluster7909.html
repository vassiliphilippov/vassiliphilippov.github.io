publicist

noun
person who promotes publicity
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to generate publicity for a person, organization, or event

Examples

A publicist manages publicity
A skilled publicist can turn a minor news item into a big headline.
She hired a publicist to handle her book tour publicity.
The publicist arranged interviews with several major newspapers to boost the actor's profile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promoter - person who promotes something, especially an event or enterprise
