parallelization

noun
making things parallel
7.5
Pronunciation: /pær.ə.lə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pær.ə.lə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of arranging things so that they are parallel or side by side at the same distance apart

Examples

Parallelization of tracks
The parallelization of the train tracks was essential for the new railway system.
Accurate parallelization of the beams ensures the stability of the structure.
The technician checked the parallelization of the conveyor belts before starting the machine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alignment - the arrangement in a straight line or in correct relative positions
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving things apart or keeping them apart
