mechanical engineer

noun
person trained in machine design and construction
1.0
Pronunciation: / məˈkænɪkəl ɪnˈdʒɪnɪr / (AmE), / mɪˈkænɪkəl ɪnˈdʒɪnɪə / (BrE)
a person who is trained in the design, construction, and use of machines and mechanical systems

Examples

A mechanical engineer designs machines
A mechanical engineer must understand how different materials behave under stress.
After graduating from university, she found a job as a mechanical engineer at an automotive company.
The team’s mechanical engineer developed an innovative solution for the new engine design.
