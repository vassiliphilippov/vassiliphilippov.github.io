nongenetically

adverb
without genetic methods
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnɒndʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not involve genetic modification or inheritance

Examples

Improved nongenetically through breeding
The crops were improved nongenetically through selective breeding over many generations.
She prefers to treat her plants nongenetically, avoiding any genetic engineering techniques.
The disease was transmitted nongenetically, meaning it was not inherited from the parents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genetically - in a way that involves genes or genetic modification
