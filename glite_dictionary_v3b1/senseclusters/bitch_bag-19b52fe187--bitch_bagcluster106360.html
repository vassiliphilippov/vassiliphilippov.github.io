bitch bag

noun
slang insult for a critical or nagging person
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪtʃ bæg / (AmE), / bɪtʃ bæɡ / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A derogatory term for a person, typically a woman, who is considered overly critical, nagging, or bothersome.

Examples

A bitch bag insult
After two hours of complaining, he called his coworker a bitch bag and left the room.
The online forum warned against being a bitch bag when discussing sensitive topics.
You don’t have to be a bitch bag about every little mistake I make.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitch - offensive term for a spiteful or unpleasant woman
  • synonym: nag - person who persistently criticizes or complains
