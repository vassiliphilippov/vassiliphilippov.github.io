posada

noun
Christmas procession
9.5
Pronunciation: /pəʊ.ˈsɑː.də/ /; / /poʊ.ˈsɑː.də/ (AmE), /pəʊ.ˈsɑː.də/ (BrE)
a traditional Latin American celebration before Christmas, reenacting Mary and Joseph's search for lodging and ending with a communal party
