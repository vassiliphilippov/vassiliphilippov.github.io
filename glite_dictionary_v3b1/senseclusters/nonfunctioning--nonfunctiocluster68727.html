nonfunctioning

adjective
not active or working
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
not working or not performing its intended function

The hospital had to close one wing because the heating system was nonfunctioning.
A nonfunctioning streetlight made the intersection dangerous at night.
They replaced the nonfunctioning smoke detector to ensure safety in the building.
