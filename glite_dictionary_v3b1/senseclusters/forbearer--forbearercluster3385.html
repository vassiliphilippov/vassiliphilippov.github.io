forbearer

noun
ancestor or forefather
7.9
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈbɛrər / (AmE), / fɔːˈbɛərə / (BrE)
a person from whom one is descended; an ancestor

Examples

A family forbearer
She discovered that her great-great-grandfather was a famous forbearer in her family.
Many people feel a strong connection to their forbearers when learning about family history.
The traditions passed down by our forbearers still influence our lives today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestor - a person in your family who lived a long time ago
  • antonym: descendant - a person who is related to someone and lives after them
