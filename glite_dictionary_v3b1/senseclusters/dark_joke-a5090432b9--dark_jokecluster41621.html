dark joke

noun
joke with grim or morbid humor
Pronunciation: / dɑrk dʒoʊk / (AmE), / dɑːk dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke that uses grim, morbid, or disturbing themes, often making light of serious, tragic, or taboo subjects

Examples

A dark joke about death
He made a dark joke about the haunted house, which made some people uncomfortable.
Her dark jokes often walk the line between funny and offensive.
Sometimes people use a dark joke to cope with difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black humor - humor that makes light of subjects usually considered serious or taboo
  • synonym: gallows humor - grim or ironic humor in a desperate or hopeless situation
