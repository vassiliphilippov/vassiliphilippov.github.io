Chlorella

noun
genus of unicellular green algae
8.8
Pronunciation: / kləˈrɛlə / (AmE), / kləˈrɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of nonmotile, unicellular green algae, sometimes cultivated as a high-protein food source and for its vitamins.

Examples

Scientists are studying Chlorella as a sustainable food source.
