shook

adjective
very shocked (informal)
Pronunciation: / ʃʊk / (AmE), / ʃʊk / (BrE)
Informal slang: very surprised, shocked, or emotionally affected; overwhelmed.

Examples

Feeling shook
I was completely shook after hearing the unexpected news about the accident.
Fans were left shook by the actor's confession during the live interview last night.
Seeing the final twist in the show had my sister absolutely shook for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shocked - very surprised or upset by something unexpected
  • synonym: stunned - so surprised or shocked that you are temporarily unable to react
