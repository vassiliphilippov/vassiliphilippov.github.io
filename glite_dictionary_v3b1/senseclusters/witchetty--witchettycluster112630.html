witchetty

noun
Australian edible grub
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪti / (BrE)
a large, edible grub native to Australia, traditionally eaten by Indigenous Australians as a source of food

Examples

The witchetty grub
The witchetty is an important traditional food for many Indigenous Australians.
Tourists were curious to try a roasted witchetty during their visit to the outback.
She learned how to find and prepare a witchetty from local guides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: witchetty grub - the larva of several Australian moths, eaten as food
