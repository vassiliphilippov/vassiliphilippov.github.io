witchetty

noun
Australian edible grub
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪti / (BrE)
a large, edible grub native to Australia, traditionally eaten by Indigenous Australians as a source of food

Examples

The witchetty grub
