placement

noun
advertising integration in media
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪ.smənt/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪ.smənt/ (BrE)
the practice of integrating brands, products, or services into films, television shows, video games, or other media as a form of advertising.

Examples

Product placement
The film's director insisted on subtle placement of the sponsor's logo during the chase scene.
Many viewers complained that the show's frequent placement of products made scenes feel like advertisements.
Advertising agencies negotiated international placement deals to ensure the brand appeared in the video game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advertising - the activity of promoting products or services to consumers
  • synonym: product placement - the practice of integrating products or brands into media content for promotional purposes
