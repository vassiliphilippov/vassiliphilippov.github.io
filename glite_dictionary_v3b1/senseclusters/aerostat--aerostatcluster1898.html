aerostat

noun
lighter-than-air aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈɛroʊˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈeərəʊˌstæt / (BrE)
a lighter-than-air aircraft, such as a balloon or airship, that floats by using a gas lighter than air
