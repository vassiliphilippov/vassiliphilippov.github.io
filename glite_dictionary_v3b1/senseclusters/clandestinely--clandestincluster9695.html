clandestinely

adverb
secretly, in hiding
Pronunciation: / klænˈdɛstənli / (AmE), / klænˈdɛstɪnli / (BrE)
in a secret or hidden way, especially to avoid being noticed

Examples

To clandestinely negotiate
The two companies clandestinely negotiated a merger before making any public announcement.
He clandestinely entered the building late at night to avoid being seen.
Documents were clandestinely passed between the agents during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden
  • synonym: secretly - in a way that is hidden from others
