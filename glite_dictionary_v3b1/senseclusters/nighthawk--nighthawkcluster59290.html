nighthawk

noun
person active late at night
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌhɔk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪthɔːk / (BrE)
a person who prefers to be active or awake late at night

Examples

A nighthawk stays up late
As a devoted nighthawk, he finds early morning meetings unbearable.
My roommate is a total nighthawk and rarely goes to bed before 2 a.m.
The city's cafes are filled with nighthawks working or socializing long after midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: night owl - a person who habitually stays up late at night
