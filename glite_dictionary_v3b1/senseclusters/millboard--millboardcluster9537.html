millboard

noun
stiff pasteboard used in bookbinding
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
stiff, thick pasteboard used as a material for binding books or for making boxes and similar items.

Examples

Stiff millboard material
Antique books are often bound in sturdy millboard rather than leather.
Conservators selected high-quality millboard for repairing the damaged spine.
The old volume was restored using millboard to strengthen its covers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pasteboard - stiff, thin material made from paper pulp, often used for cards and boxes
