weblog

noun
online journal website
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈwɛbˌlɒg / (BrE)
a website where a person regularly writes and shares posts, often like a journal or about specific topics

Examples

Travel weblog
She started a weblog to document her travels around the world.
Many people use a weblog to share their thoughts and experiences with others online.
His weblog quickly gained followers interested in technology news and reviews.
She started a weblog to share her travel adventures with friends and family.
Many companies use a weblog to communicate news and updates to their customers.
His weblog quickly gained popularity because of his insightful technology reviews.
