tiresomely

adverb
in a tedious or boring manner
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪərsəmli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəsəmli / (BrE)
In a way that causes boredom or annoyance because of monotony or length; tediously.

Examples

To speak tiresomely
He spoke tiresomely about the same topic for hours.
She tiresomely repeated the instructions until everyone understood.
The meeting dragged on tiresomely, with little progress made.

Related Terms

  • antonym: interestingly - in a manner that arouses interest or holds attention
  • synonym: tediously - in a tiresomely monotonous manner
