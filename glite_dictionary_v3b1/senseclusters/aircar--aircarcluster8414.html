aircar

noun
flying car
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈeəˌkɑː / (BrE)
a vehicle that can function both as a car on roads and as an aircraft in the air

Examples

Flying aircar
The inventor demonstrated his new aircar by driving it down the street and then taking off into the sky.
In the future, commuters might use an aircar to avoid traffic jams and fly directly to work.
The company announced plans to release a commercial aircar within the next five years.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: airplane - a powered flying vehicle with fixed wings
  • degree lower: automobile - a road vehicle powered by an engine, used for transporting people
  • synonym: flying car - a car that is able to fly as well as drive on roads
