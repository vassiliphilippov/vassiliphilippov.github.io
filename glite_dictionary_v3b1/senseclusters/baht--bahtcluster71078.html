baht

noun
currency unit of Thailand
Pronunciation: / bɑt / (AmE), / bɑːt / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Thailand, equal to 100 satangs

Examples

The baht currency
She exchanged her dollars for baht at the airport.
The price of lunch in Bangkok is about 100 baht.
The souvenir cost 50 baht at the local market.
