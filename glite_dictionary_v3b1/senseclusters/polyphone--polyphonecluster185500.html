polyphone

noun
letter with multiple pronunciations
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːliˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪfəʊn / (BrE)
A letter or character that has two or more different pronunciations in different words or contexts.

Examples

A polyphone letter
In English spelling, encountering a polyphone can make pronunciation tricky for learners.
Teachers often point out the letter 'c' as a classic example of a polyphone.
The letter 'g' is a polyphone because it is pronounced differently in 'go' and 'giant'.
