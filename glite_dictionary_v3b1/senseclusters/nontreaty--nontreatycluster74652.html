nontreaty

adjective
not by treaty
7.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtriːti / (AmE), / nɒnˈtriːti / (BrE)
not established, governed, or recognized by a formal treaty

Examples

Nontreaty land
The land was acquired through nontreaty agreements.
Some indigenous groups have nontreaty rights that differ from those with formal treaties.
The dispute arose over nontreaty fishing areas along the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informal - not formal or official
  • antonym: treaty - an official agreement between countries or groups
