snowmaking

noun
artificial snow production
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or technology of producing artificial snow, especially for ski resorts or winter sports areas

Examples

Advances in snowmaking have allowed more areas to open for skiing earlier in the season.
Without reliable snowmaking, many winter sports events would have to be canceled due to lack of snow.
