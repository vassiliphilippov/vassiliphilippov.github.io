suitability

noun
being appropriate for a purpose
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.təˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌsuː.təˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality of being right or appropriate for a particular purpose or situation.

Examples

The suitability for a role
Questions were raised about the candidate’s suitability for the leadership role.
The committee discussed the suitability of the venue for the conference.
We need to assess the suitability of this material for outdoor use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriateness - the quality of being suitable or proper
  • antonym: unsuitability - the quality of not being suitable or appropriate
