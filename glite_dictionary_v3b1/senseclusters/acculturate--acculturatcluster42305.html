acculturate

verb
to assimilate culturally
5.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkʌl.tʃə.reɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkʌl.tʃə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to change or adapt to the cultural traits or social patterns of another group through contact or assimilation

Examples

To acculturate to customs
After moving to Canada, the family slowly began to acculturate to Canadian customs and traditions.
It is common for immigrants to acculturate while still preserving aspects of their original culture.
The exchange students were encouraged to acculturate by participating in local community events.
