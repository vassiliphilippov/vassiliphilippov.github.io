welting

noun
decorative seam strip
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛltɪŋ / (BrE)
a strip of material sewn into a seam, especially for reinforcement or decoration on furniture or clothing

Examples

Decorative welting
The sofa was accented with red welting that matched the curtains perfectly.
She chose a contrasting color for the welting on her handmade dress.
Adding welting to the cushion edges gave the chair a more finished look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piping - a narrow tube of fabric sewn into a seam for decoration
  • synonym: trim - decorative material added to the edge of something
