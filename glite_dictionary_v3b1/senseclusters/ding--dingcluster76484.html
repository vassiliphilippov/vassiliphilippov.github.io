ding

verb
make a ringing sound like a bell
Pronunciation: / dɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪŋ / (BrE)
to make a short, high-pitched ringing sound, like that of a small bell

Examples

To ding for attention
Her phone began to ding with new text messages.
The bell at the front desk dings when someone needs assistance.
The timer will ding when your food is ready.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: dong - to make a deep ringing sound, like a big bell
