synapse

verb
to connect nerve cells
6.5
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnæps / (AmE), / ˈsɪnæps / (BrE)
to form a connection between nerve cells to transmit signals.

Examples

Neurons synapse together
During brain development, neurons synapse rapidly to form complex networks.
Certain drugs can affect how nerve cells synapse and communicate in the brain.
Scientists study how damaged nerves synapse after injury to improve recovery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disconnect - to separate or break a connection
