phone tapper

noun
person who wiretaps telephones
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊn ˌtæpər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌtæpə / (BrE)
a person who secretly listens to telephone conversations by means of an electronic device connected to the telephone line
