veracity

noun
truthfulness; unwillingness to lie
9.9
Pronunciation: / vəˈræsəti / (AmE), / vəˈræsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being truthful or unwilling to tell lies; truthfulness

Examples

The veracity of facts
Her veracity was never in doubt among her friends and colleagues.
The lawyer questioned the veracity of the witness's statement.
The report's veracity was confirmed by independent sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dishonesty - lack of honesty; tendency to lie or deceive
  • synonym: truthfulness - the quality of being truthful
