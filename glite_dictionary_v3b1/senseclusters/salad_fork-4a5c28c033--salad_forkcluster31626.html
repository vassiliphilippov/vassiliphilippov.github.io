salad fork

noun
fork used for eating salads
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləd fɔrk / (AmE), / ˈsæləd fɔːk / (BrE)
a fork with a broad, often slightly curved shape, used for eating salads

Examples

The salad fork set
Please use the salad fork for your starter course.
She bought a new set of salad forks to match her silverware.
The table was set with a salad fork to the left of the dinner fork.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dessert fork - a small fork used for eating dessert
  • antonym: dinner fork - a larger fork used for eating the main course of a meal
