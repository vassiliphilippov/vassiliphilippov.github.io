military operation

noun
action or campaign by armed forces
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪl.ɪˌtɛr.i ˌɑː.pəˈreɪ.ʃən /; / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.ɪ.təri ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən /; / ˈmɪlɪtəri ɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an organized and coordinated action or series of actions carried out by armed forces to achieve a specific goal
