bullfight

noun
traditional spectacle where a bull is fought and killed
1.0 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.faɪt / (BrE)
A traditional Spanish, Portuguese, or Latin American public spectacle in which a matador confronts and usually kills a bull in an arena before an audience.

Examples

A bullfight event
He was fascinated by the costumes and rituals of the bullfight.
The ethics of the bullfight have been widely debated in recent years.
Thousands of tourists attend the bullfight in Seville every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrida - the Spanish term for a bullfight
  • synonym: matador - the main bullfighter in a bullfight
