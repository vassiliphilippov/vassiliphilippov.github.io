subjectivistic

adjective
favoring subjectivism
9.9
Pronunciation: / səbˌdʒɛktɪˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / səbˌdʒɛktɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of subjectivism, emphasizing personal feelings or opinions over objective facts

Examples

A subjectivistic view
The critic's subjectivistic review relied heavily on her own emotional response to the film.
A subjectivistic approach to art values personal interpretation over universal standards.
His argument was dismissed as too subjectivistic and lacking in objective evidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings; based on facts
  • synonym: subjective - based on personal feelings or opinions
