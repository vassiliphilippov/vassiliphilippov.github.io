yellow dock

noun
a Eurasian plant with broad leaves and bitter root
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ dɑk / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ dɒk / (BrE)
A perennial plant with broad, rounded leaves and a bitter root, native to Europe and commonly found as a weed in North America.

Examples

Yellow dock root
Herbalists often use yellow dock root for its traditional medicinal properties.
Large clusters of yellow dock can be seen growing along rural roadsides in summer.
The gardener tried to remove the yellow dock that had taken over the vegetable patch.
