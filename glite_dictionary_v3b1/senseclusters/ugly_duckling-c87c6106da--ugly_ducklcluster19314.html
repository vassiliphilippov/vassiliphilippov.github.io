ugly duckling

noun
person or thing that becomes attractive or successful
4.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli ˈdʌklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli ˈdʌklɪŋ / (BrE)
a person or thing that seems unattractive or unpromising at first but later becomes beautiful, successful, or admired

Examples

The ugly duckling story
As a child, she was considered an ugly duckling, but she grew up to be a celebrated actress.
Many teenagers feel like an ugly duckling during adolescence, only to gain confidence later in life.
The old building was an ugly duckling until it was renovated into a stunning art gallery.
Despite being the ugly duckling at school, he became a famous actor.
Growing up, Anna felt like an ugly duckling until she blossomed in college.
The company's early models were dismissed as ugly ducklings, but now they're highly sought after.

Related Terms

  • synonym: late bloomer - a person whose talents or capabilities become apparent later than usual
  • degree higher: swan - someone who is now beautiful or successful, in contrast to former awkwardness
  • synonym: wallflower - a person who is shy and unpopular, especially at social events
