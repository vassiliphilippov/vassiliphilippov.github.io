internetwork

noun
connected computer networks
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈnɛtwɜrk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnɛtwɜːk / (BrE)
a network formed by connecting two or more separate computer networks together

Examples

A global internetwork
The company set up an internetwork to connect its offices worldwide.
An internetwork allows different departments to share resources securely.
Building an internetwork can improve communication between separate branches of an organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: internet - the global system of interconnected computer networks
  • antonym: intranet - a private network accessible only to an organization's staff
