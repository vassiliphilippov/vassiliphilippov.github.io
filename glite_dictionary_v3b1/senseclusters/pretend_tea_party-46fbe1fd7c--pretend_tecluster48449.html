pretend tea party

noun
children's make-believe tea time
Pronunciation: / prɪˈtɛnd ti ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / prɪˈtɛnd tiː ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a children's play activity in which participants mimic having a formal tea, often using toy dishes and imaginary food and drink

Examples

A pretend tea party
She invited all her stuffed animals to a pretend tea party on Saturday afternoon.
The children used plastic cups and cookies at their pretend tea party.
The little girls set the table for their pretend tea party in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tea party - a social gathering for drinking tea
