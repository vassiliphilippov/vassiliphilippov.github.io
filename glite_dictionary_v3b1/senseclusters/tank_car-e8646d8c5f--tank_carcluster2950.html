tank car

noun
freight rail car for liquids or gases
Pronunciation: / tæŋk kɑr / (AmE), / tæŋk kɑː / (BrE)
A railroad freight car designed for transporting liquids or gases in bulk.

Examples

A tank car transports liquids
A chemical leak occurred when a tank car derailed near the station.
The freight train included several tank cars carrying liquefied natural gas.
The oil was transported across the country in a tank car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tanker - vehicle or ship for transporting liquids or gases
