namban

adjective
Japanese-European art style
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑːmˈbɑːn / (AmE), / næmˈban / (BrE)
relating to Japanese art or objects influenced by European contact, especially during the 16th and 17th centuries

Examples

Namban art style
The exhibition featured several namban paintings showing European ships arriving in Japan.
Collectors value namban screens for their unique blend of Japanese and Western artistic elements.
The namban influence is evident in the depiction of foreign figures and exotic objects.
