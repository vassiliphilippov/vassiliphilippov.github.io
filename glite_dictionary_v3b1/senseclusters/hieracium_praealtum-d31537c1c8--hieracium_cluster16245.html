Hieracium praealtum

noun
species of European hawkweed
Pronunciation: / haɪˈɛriəkiəm preɪˈæltəm / (AmE), / hɪəˈræsiəm priːˈæltəm / (BrE)
A European species of hawkweed, sometimes introduced into northeastern United States, where it can become a troublesome weed.
