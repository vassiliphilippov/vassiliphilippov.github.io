encourage

verb
promote or support growth
1.5 2.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɜrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ / (BrE)
to help the progress or development of something; to promote or foster growth

Examples

To encourage growth
Planting more trees will encourage biodiversity in the area.
The new program aims to encourage innovation among young entrepreneurs.
We need to encourage healthy eating habits in children from an early age.

Related Terms
