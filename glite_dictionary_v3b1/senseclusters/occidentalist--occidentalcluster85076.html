occidentalist

noun
supporter of the West
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪˈdɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪˈdɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes Western civilization, values, or institutions

Examples

Occidentalist supporter
As an occidentalist, he often spoke about the benefits of Western education systems.
The debate featured an occidentalist and a critic of Western influence discussing cultural values.
Some accused her of being an occidentalist because she admired European political models.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orientalist - a person who studies or supports Eastern cultures
  • synonym: westernizer - a person who advocates adopting Western ideas or culture
