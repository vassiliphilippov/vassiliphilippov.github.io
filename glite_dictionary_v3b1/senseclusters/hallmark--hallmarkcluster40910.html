hallmark

verb
to certify quality
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔlˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlmɑːk / (BrE)
to officially mark an item, especially precious metal, to certify its quality or authenticity

Examples

To hallmark gold
All gold jewelry must be hallmarked before it can be sold legally in many countries.
The silverware was hallmarked to guarantee its purity and origin.
Before leaving the workshop, each piece is carefully hallmarked by the craftsman.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authenticate - to prove that something is genuine or real
  • synonym: stamp - to mark with an official seal or sign
