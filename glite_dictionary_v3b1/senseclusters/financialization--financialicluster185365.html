financialization

noun
growth of finance sector
Pronunciation: / fəˌnænʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / faɪˌnænʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which finance, financial markets, and financial products become increasingly important and central in the economy and society
