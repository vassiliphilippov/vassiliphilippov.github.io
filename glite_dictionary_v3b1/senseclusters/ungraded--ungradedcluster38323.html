ungraded

adjective
not classified by level or grade
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡreɪdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪdɪd / (BrE)
not arranged or classified in levels according to a scale or hierarchy.

Examples

Ungraded assignments
The assignments remained ungraded until the teacher had more time.
The students were placed in an ungraded classroom where age was not a factor.
This produce is sold as ungraded fruit, so quality may vary.
