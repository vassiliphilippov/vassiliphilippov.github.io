assume

verb
take on responsibility or debt
3.7 3.58/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàsum / (AmE), / …ôÀàsjuÀêm / (BrE)
to take on responsibility for, or ownership of, something such as expenses, debts, or obligations, often belonging to another person

Examples

To assume a debt
If you assume the loan, you must also make all future payments.
She agreed to assume responsibility for her brother's medical bills.
The new company will assume all outstanding debts after the merger.

Synonyms

  • take over
    3.7 1.90/m
    take over means beginning to manage or control another person's debts or duties, while assume means officially take on those responsibilities.
  • accept
    2.5 3.86/m
    accept often means agreeing to pay or take on someone else's expenses, while assume implies taking full legal or formal responsibility for them.
  • bear
    3.9 49/m
    bear means to endure or pay a cost yourself, while assume means to accept responsibility or ownership of another's debts or obligations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bear - to take responsibility for something undesirable
  • antonym: disclaim - to refuse or give up responsibility for something
  • synonym: undertake - to take responsibility for something
