assume

verb
take on responsibility or debt
Pronunciation: / əˈsum / (AmE), / əˈsjuːm / (BrE)
to take on responsibility for, or ownership of, something such as expenses, debts, or obligations, often belonging to another person

Examples

To assume a debt
If you assume the loan, you must also make all future payments.
She agreed to assume responsibility for her brother's medical bills.
The new company will assume all outstanding debts after the merger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bear - to take responsibility for something undesirable
  • antonym: disclaim - to refuse or give up responsibility for something
  • synonym: undertake - to take responsibility for something
