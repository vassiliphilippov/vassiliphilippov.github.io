Examples

Synonyms

  • gloriole
    9.9 0.00/m
    gloriole is a small decorative ring of light painted around a saint in old art, while nimbus is the term for that ring.
  • glory
    8.6 8.17/m
    glory is a radiant light shown to mark sanctity in religious art, while nimbus is the name for that head ring.
  • aureole

  • aura
