cruise

noun
ocean trip for pleasure
2.8 20/m
Pronunciation: / kruÀêz / (AmE), / kruÀêz / (BrE)
a journey on a large ship taken for pleasure, usually visiting several places

Examples

A cruise ship
During the cruise, they visited several islands and explored new cities.
The couple enjoyed a luxury cruise for their anniversary.
They are going on a cruise to the Caribbean next month.

Synonyms

  • sail
    1.4 1.76/m
    sail is a small or short pleasure trip by boat often for recreation, while cruise is a large ship holiday visiting many ports.
  • voyage
    4.0 0.28/m
    voyage is a long journey by sea or boat often for travel, while cruise is a pleasure trip on a large ship visiting many places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voyage - a long journey by sea or in space
