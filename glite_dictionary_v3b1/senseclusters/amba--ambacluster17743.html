amba

noun
pickled mango condiment
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmbə / (AmE), / ˈæmbə / (BrE)
a tangy pickled mango condiment commonly used in Middle Eastern and Indian cuisine

Examples

Amba sauce
She added a spoonful of amba to her falafel for a burst of flavor.
The street vendor offered amba as a topping for the shawarma wrap.
Many people enjoy the tangy taste of amba with grilled meats and vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chutney - a spicy or savory condiment made from fruits or vegetables
  • synonym: relish - a condiment made of chopped vegetables or fruits, often pickled
