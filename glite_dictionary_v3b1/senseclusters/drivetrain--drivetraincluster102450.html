drivetrain

noun
vehicle power system
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdraɪv.treɪn/ (AmE), /ˈdraɪv.treɪn/ (BrE)
the system of parts in a vehicle or machine that transmits power from the engine or motor to the wheels or working parts
