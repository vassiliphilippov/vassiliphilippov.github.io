verification

noun
online account authenticity badge/status
3.8
Pronunciation: /ve.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /və.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ve.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ve.rə.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a mark, badge, or account status on an online platform indicating that the platform has confirmed the account's identity or authenticity.

Examples

Account verification
The platform granted verification to the celebrity's account after identity checks.
Users often request verification to avoid impersonation and increase follower trust.
Only public figures and brands usually receive verification during the account review process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confirmation - proof or evidence that something (or someone) is genuine or correct
  • synonym: verified account - an account marked by a platform as authentic, often with a visible badge
