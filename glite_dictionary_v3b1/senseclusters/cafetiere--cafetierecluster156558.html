cafetiere

noun
coffee press pot
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkæfəˈtjɛr / (AmE), / ˌkæfəˈtjeə / (BrE)
a pot, usually with a plunger and filter, used to make or serve coffee (also called a French press)

Examples

A cafetiere pot
She poured hot water into the cafetiere and waited for her coffee to brew.
After grinding fresh beans, he used a cafetiere to make a strong cup of coffee.
The hotel room included a small cafetiere for guests to prepare their own coffee.
She placed a floral china cafetiere on the tray before the guests arrived.
He poured freshly brewed coffee from the cafetiere into delicate porcelain cups.
The hotel breakfast included a cafetiere of strong black coffee for each table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: French press - a coffee pot with a plunger and filter for brewing coffee
  • synonym: coffee pot - a container for brewing or serving coffee
  • antonym: percolator - a type of pot for making coffee by cycling boiling water through grounds
  • antonym: teapot - a container for brewing or serving tea
