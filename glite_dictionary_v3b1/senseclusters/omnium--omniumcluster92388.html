omnium

noun
multi-event competition
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmniəm / (AmE), / ˈɒmniəm / (BrE)
a multi-event competition consisting of several different races or contests, especially in track cycling or finance

Examples

Track cycling omnium
She won the omnium at the national track cycling championships.
The omnium tests athletes' abilities across a range of cycling events.
In finance, an omnium can refer to a collection of different securities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decathlon - an athletic contest with ten different events
  • synonym: pentathlon - a sporting event with five different disciplines
