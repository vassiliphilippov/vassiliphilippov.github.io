prophet

noun
inspired speaker or teacher of God's will
6.3 22/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀê.f…™t / (AmE), / Ààpr…íf.…™t / (BrE)
a person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of the will of God; someone who speaks by divine inspiration

Examples

Religious prophet
Many religions regard their founders as great prophets who received divine messages.
The prophet spoke to the crowd, urging them to live with compassion and justice.
The ancient Israelites believed Moses was a prophet who led them out of Egypt.

Synonyms

  • revelator
    9.9 0.07/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: oracle - a person through whom a deity is believed to speak
  • synonym: seer - a person who claims to see or predict future events, often with supernatural insight
