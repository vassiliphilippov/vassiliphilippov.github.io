miscellanea

noun
collection of various items
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsəˈleɪniə / (AmE), / ˌmɪsɪˈleɪnɪə / (BrE)
A collection of miscellaneous or different types of items, especially written works or literary pieces.

Examples

A miscellanea of books
His library contains a fascinating miscellanea of rare books, letters, and maps.
She enjoys collecting a miscellanea of antiques from different countries.
The magazine published a monthly miscellanea featuring essays, poems, and short stories by different authors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miscellany - a mixture or collection of various things
