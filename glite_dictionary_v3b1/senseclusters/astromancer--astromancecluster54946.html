astromancer

noun
humorous astronomer
Pronunciation: / ˈæstrəˌmænsər / (AmE), / ˈæstrəˌmænsə / (BrE)
an informal or humorous term for someone who studies or is fascinated by celestial objects and astronomy

Examples

A science astromancer
The science club invited a well-known astromancer to give a talk about black holes.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an astromancer and exploring distant galaxies.
In the novel, the wise astromancer helps the heroes navigate by the stars.

Related Terms

  • antonym: astrologer - a person who studies the movements of celestial bodies to predict events
  • synonym: astronomer - a scientist who studies stars, planets, and other celestial bodies
