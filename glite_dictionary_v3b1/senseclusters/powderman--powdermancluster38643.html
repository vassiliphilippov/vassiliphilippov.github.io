powderman

noun
gunpowder supplier
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdəmən / (BrE)
a member of a military or naval crew responsible for carrying and supplying gunpowder to the guns

Examples

Ship's powderman
The powderman risked his life to deliver gunpowder to the cannons during the fierce battle.
On old warships, the powderman played a crucial role in keeping the guns supplied.
Without the powderman, the artillery crew would have quickly run out of ammunition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artilleryman - a soldier who operates or assists with large guns
  • synonym: loader - a person who loads ammunition or supplies into weapons
