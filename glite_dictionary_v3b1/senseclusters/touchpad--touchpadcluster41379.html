touchpad

noun
computer pointing device
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃˌpæd / (BrE)
a flat, touch-sensitive surface on a computer or electronic device used to control the cursor or other functions by touch

Examples

Laptop touchpad
Many laptops come with a built-in touchpad instead of a separate mouse.
He prefers using the touchpad for quick navigation while traveling.
If your touchpad stops working, you can connect an external mouse.
She used the touchpad on her laptop to scroll through the document quickly.
The new remote control features a touchpad for easy navigation of the menu.
Many smartphones now include a small touchpad area for gesture controls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: joystick - a stick used to control movement in electronic devices
  • synonym: mouse - a handheld device used to control a computer cursor
  • synonym: trackpad - a touch-sensitive input device for controlling a computer cursor
