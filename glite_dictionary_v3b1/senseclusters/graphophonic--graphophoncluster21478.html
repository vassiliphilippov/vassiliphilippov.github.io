graphophonic

adjective
letters and sounds
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræfəˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡræfəˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the relationship between written symbols (letters) and their corresponding sounds in a language

Examples

Graphophonic skills
Teachers often use graphophonic strategies to help students connect letters with their sounds.
Understanding graphophonic relationships is essential for early reading development.
The graphophonic approach can assist learners who struggle with decoding new words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthographic - relating to the conventional spelling system of a language
  • synonym: phonics - a method of teaching reading by correlating sounds with letters
