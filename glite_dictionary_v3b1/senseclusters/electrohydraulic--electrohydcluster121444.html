electrohydraulic

adjective
electric and fluid power
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊhaɪˈdrɑlɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊhaɪˈdrɒlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the combined use of electricity and fluid power in machinery or systems

Examples

Electrohydraulic control system
The engineers designed an electrohydraulic system to improve the efficiency of the machinery.
Modern elevators often use electrohydraulic controls for smoother and safer operation.
An electrohydraulic press can deliver precise force using both electrical and hydraulic components.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromechanical - relating to systems combining electrical and mechanical processes
  • degree lower: hydraulic - relating to the use of liquid fluid power
