spook

noun
haunting memory or mental image
5.1
Pronunciation: / spuːk / (AmE), / spuːk / (BrE)
An image or memory that haunts the mind, often associated with fear or anxiety.

Examples

Spook of the past
For years, the spook of his past mistakes haunted him in quiet moments.
Old traumas can sometimes return as spooks to trouble our sleep.
She tried to forget the spook that lingered after the accident, but it was never far away.
