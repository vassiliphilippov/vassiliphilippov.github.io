bad guy

noun
an antagonist or villain
1.0
Pronunciation: / bæd ɡaɪ / (AmE), / bæd ɡaɪ / (BrE)
an antagonist or opponent; a person who is against you or considered to be the villain in a situation

Examples

The bad guy in story
At first, she seemed like the bad guy, but her motives were later revealed.
He always plays the bad guy in our group games, much to everyone's amusement.
In most action movies, the bad guy is finally defeated by the hero.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enemy - a person actively opposed or hostile to someone
  • antonym: good guy - someone who is on your side or the hero in a situation
  • synonym: villain - a character whose evil actions or motives are central to the plot
