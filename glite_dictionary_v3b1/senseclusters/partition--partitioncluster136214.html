partition

noun
a dividing structure inside a space
3.1
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a structure, such as a wall or screen, that divides one area or space from another within a room or building

Examples

Office partition wall
Office cubicles are often separated by partitions to give workers privacy.
The classroom was divided by a temporary partition to accommodate two groups.
We installed a glass partition to create a small meeting area in the lobby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wall - a continuous vertical brick or stone structure that encloses or divides an area
