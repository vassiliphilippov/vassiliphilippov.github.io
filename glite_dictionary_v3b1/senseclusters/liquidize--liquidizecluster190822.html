to sell off or dispose of all merchandise or assets, often to convert them into cash

Examples

To liquidize assets
The company decided to liquidize its remaining stock before closing down.
They are planning to liquidize all their assets to settle outstanding debts.
When demand fell, she had to liquidize most of her inventory at discount prices.

Synonyms

  • liquidate
    7.3 1.48/m
    liquidate is selling assets to pay debts and close a business, while liquidize is selling off assets to convert them into cash.
  • sell off
    3.5 2.93/m
    sell off means selling quickly, often at low prices to remove surplus stock, while liquidize means selling assets to turn them into cash.
  • divest
    7.5 0.61/m
    divest means selling or giving up an investment or business part in a planned way, while liquidize means selling off all assets to get cash.
  • close out
    4.3 0.11/m
    close out means selling remaining stock, often at reduced prices when ending sales or accounts, while liquidize means disposing of all assets to get cash.
  • sell out
    2.8 2.26/m
    sell out means selling all items because of high demand so nothing remains, while liquidize means intentionally selling assets to convert them into cash.
