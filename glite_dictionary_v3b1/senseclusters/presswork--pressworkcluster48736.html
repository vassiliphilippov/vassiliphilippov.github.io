presswork

noun
printing with a press
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈprɛsˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the process or result of printing materials using a printing press

Examples

Book presswork
The presswork for the new book was completed ahead of schedule.
High-quality presswork can make a magazine look much more professional.
The publisher inspected the presswork before approving the final copies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manuscript - a document written by hand, not printed
  • synonym: printing - the process of producing text or images on paper
