rigger

noun
3D animation rigging specialist
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡə / (BrE)
(in computer animation and visual effects) a person who creates and implements a digital "rig" — the skeleton, controls, and deformation systems — for a 3D model so it can be animated.

Examples

3D rigger
The studio hired a skilled rigger to prepare the CGI character for realistic facial movement.
Several riggers collaborated with animators to build a reusable skeleton for the game's hero.
As a freelance rigger, she wrote custom control rigs that sped up the studio's animation process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animator - a person who creates motion for characters and objects; riggers provide the control systems animators use
  • synonym: character rigger - a specialist who builds rigs (skeletons and control systems) specifically for characters
