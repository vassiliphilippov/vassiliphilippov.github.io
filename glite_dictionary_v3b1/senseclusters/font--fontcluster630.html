font

noun
typeface in a specific size or style
Pronunciation: / fɑnt / (AmE), / fɒnt / (BrE)
a set of printed or digital type of a particular size and design used for letters and numbers

Examples

Change the font style
She chose a bold font for the headline to grab attention.
This document looks better when printed in a serif font.
You can change the size and style of the font in the settings menu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: typeface - the design of lettering that includes variations in size, weight, slope, etc.
