fishman

noun
human-fish creature
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌmæn / (BrE)
a person or creature with both human and fish characteristics, often found in myths or fantasy stories

Examples

A mythical fishman
The villagers believed a mysterious fishman lived deep beneath the waves.
In the story, a fishman emerged from the sea to warn the sailors of danger.
Legends describe the fishman as having scales, gills, and webbed hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mermaid - a mythical female being with the upper body of a woman and the tail of a fish
  • synonym: merman - a mythical male being with the upper body of a man and the tail of a fish
