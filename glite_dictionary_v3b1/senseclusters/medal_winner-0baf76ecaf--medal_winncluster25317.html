medal winner

noun
golf player winning in medal play
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdəl ˌwɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəl ˌwɪnə / (BrE)
In golf, the player who achieves the lowest score in medal play and wins the tournament.

Examples

A medal winner in golf
As a first-time medal winner, he felt immense pride in his golfing achievements.
Despite strong competition, she emerged as the medal winner at the annual golf championship.
The club honored last year's medal winner with a special award dinner.
