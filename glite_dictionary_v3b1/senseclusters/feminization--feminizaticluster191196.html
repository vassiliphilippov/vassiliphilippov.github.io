feminization

noun
process of developing female characteristics
9.0 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛmənəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌfɛmɪnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfiːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌfɛmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
