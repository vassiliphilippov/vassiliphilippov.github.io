Wiccan

noun
follower of Wicca religion
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkən / (AmE), / ˈwɪkən / (BrE)
a person who follows or practices Wicca, a modern pagan religious movement

Examples

A Wiccan celebrates
As a proud Wiccan, she celebrates the solstices with her community.
Many Wiccans honor the cycles of nature in their rituals.
The local library hosted a talk on modern paganism by a Wiccan priestess.
