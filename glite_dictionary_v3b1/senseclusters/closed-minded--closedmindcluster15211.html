closed-minded

adjective
unwilling to consider new ideas
1.0
Pronunciation: / kloʊzd ˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / kləʊzd ˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
unwilling to consider new ideas or opinions; intolerant of change or different viewpoints.

Examples

Closed-minded person
His closed-minded attitude made it difficult for the team to discuss innovative solutions.
It is hard to change a closed-minded person's opinion, no matter what evidence you present.
Some people become more closed-minded as they get older, refusing to accept different viewpoints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intolerant - not willing to accept different opinions
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas
