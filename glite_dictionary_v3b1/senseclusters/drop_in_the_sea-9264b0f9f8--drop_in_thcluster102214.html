drop in the sea

noun
an insignificant amount
4.8
Pronunciation: / drɑp ɪn ðə si / (AmE), / drɒp ɪn ðə siː / (BrE)
a very small or insignificant amount in comparison to what is needed or to the total

Examples

A tiny drop in the sea
His apology is only a drop in the sea after all the damage he's done.
My single complaint was a drop in the sea compared to the flood of feedback they received.
Spending that much money on relief was just a drop in the sea of what was required.

Related Terms
