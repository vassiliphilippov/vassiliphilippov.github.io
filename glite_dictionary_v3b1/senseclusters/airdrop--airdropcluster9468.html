airdrop

noun
short-range wireless file transfer
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈer.drɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈeə.drɒp/ (BrE)
a short-range wireless method for sending files or data between nearby devices (commonly via Bluetooth and Wi‑Fi); the term is often used for Apple's AirDrop feature and similar services.

Examples

Send files by AirDrop
She used her phone's AirDrop to send the photos to her friend's tablet instantly.
After the meeting, he quickly shared the presentation via airdrop to everyone in the room.
Students often AirDrop notes to classmates between lectures when discussing assignments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: file transfer - the movement of a file from one device or system to another
  • synonym: wireless transfer - sending data between devices without a wired connection, typically using Bluetooth or Wi‑Fi
