hieroglyphic

adjective
resembling hieroglyphic writing
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk /; / ˌhʌɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk / (BrE)
(of writing or symbols) resembling or relating to hieroglyphs, especially being difficult to read or interpret

Examples

Hieroglyphic symbols
Her handwriting was so hieroglyphic that even her teachers struggled to read it.
The archaeologist studied the hieroglyphic carvings on the ancient temple walls.
The old map was covered in hieroglyphic symbols that no one could decipher.
His journal included hieroglyphic characters that puzzled his friends.
Researchers have discovered hieroglyphic writing inside the tomb.
The ancient Egyptians used hieroglyphic inscriptions on their temple walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hieroglyphical - alternative, less common adjectival form meaning 'of or like a hieroglyph'
  • synonym: pictographic - written in or relating to pictorial writing symbols
