casein paint

noun
water-based paint made with milk protein
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsiːn peɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsiːn peɪnt / (BrE)
a water-based paint made with casein, a protein derived from milk, used especially in art and decorative painting

Examples

Casein paint for art
Artists appreciate casein paint for its quick-drying properties and velvety finish.
He created the murals using casein paint because it provides vibrant, matte colors.
Unlike oil or acrylics, casein paint is easily cleaned up with water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tempera - a painting medium using egg yolk as a binder
