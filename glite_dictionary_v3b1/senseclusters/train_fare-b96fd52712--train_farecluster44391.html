train fare

noun
cost of a train journey
Pronunciation: / treɪn fɛr / (AmE), / treɪn feə / (BrE)
the amount of money charged for a journey on a train

Examples

The train fare price
I was surprised by how high the train fare was for such a short trip.
She bought her train fare online to get a discount.
The company reimbursed the train fare for all employees attending the conference.
