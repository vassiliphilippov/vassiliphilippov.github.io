shaken

adjective
emotionally disturbed or shocked
3.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈʃeɪ.kən/ (AmE), /ˈʃeɪ.kən/ (BrE)

Examples

The sudden noise left everyone in the room feeling shaken and uneasy.
