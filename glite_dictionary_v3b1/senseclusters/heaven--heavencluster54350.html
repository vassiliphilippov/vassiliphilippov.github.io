heaven

noun
the dwelling place of God/gods or the blessed
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
In many religions, the place believed to be the home of God or the gods, and where good people go after death; a paradise.

Examples

Go to heaven
He said the view from the mountain peak was so beautiful it felt like being in heaven.
In some paintings, angels are shown descending from heaven to guide the faithful.
Many people believe that after you die, your soul goes to heaven if you have lived a good life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hell - the place believed to be the afterlife of suffering or punishment
  • synonym: paradise - an idealized place of happiness or perfection after death
