appeal

noun
urgent request for help or support
2.9 9.33/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàpiÀêl / (AmE), / …ôÀàpiÀêl / (BrE)
an earnest or urgent request for help, support, or action

Examples

A heartfelt appeal
Her emotional appeal moved many people to volunteer their time.
The charity launched an appeal for donations after the earthquake.
The mayor made an appeal to the community to conserve water during the drought.
