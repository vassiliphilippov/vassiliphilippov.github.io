photo shoot

noun
photography session
3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.toʊ ʃuːt/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təʊ ʃuːt/ (BrE)
an organized session where a series of photographs are taken, often for publication, promotion, or personal use

Examples

A photo shoot session
The fashion magazine organized a photo shoot with several famous models last week.
We spent the afternoon at the beach for a family photo shoot.
The advertising agency booked a studio for a product photo shoot.
The magazine scheduled a photo shoot with the celebrity for its next cover story.
Before the product launch, the team organized a photo shoot to capture high-quality images.
During the photo shoot, the photographer adjusted the lighting to highlight the model's features.
They planned a photo shoot at the beach to capture their family vacation memories.
The magazine organized a photo shoot with the new celebrity for its cover story.
After the photo shoot, she selected her favorite pictures to share online.
