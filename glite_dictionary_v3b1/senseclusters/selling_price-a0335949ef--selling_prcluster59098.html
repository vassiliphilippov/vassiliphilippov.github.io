selling price

noun
price at which something is sold
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlɪŋ ˌpraɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪŋ ˌpraɪs / (BrE)
the price at which something is offered or sold to buyers, especially in business or commerce

Examples

The selling price listed
Before negotiating, she checked the selling price listed online.
Rising costs have caused an increase in the selling price of many goods.
The car's selling price is much lower than its original value.
