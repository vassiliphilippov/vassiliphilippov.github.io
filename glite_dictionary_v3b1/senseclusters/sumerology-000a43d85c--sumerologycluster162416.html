Sumerology

noun
study of ancient Sumerians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːməˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌsuːməˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of the language, history, archaeology, and culture of the ancient Sumerians of Mesopotamia

Examples

Sumerology study
His passion for Sumerology led him to travel extensively throughout Iraq and study cuneiform tablets.
Recent discoveries in Sumerology have shed new light on early Mesopotamian writing.
She specialized in Sumerology during her graduate studies at the university.

  • synonym: Egyptology - the study of ancient Egyptian history, language, literature, and culture
