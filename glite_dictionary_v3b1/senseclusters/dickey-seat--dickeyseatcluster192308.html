dickey-seat

noun
small rear seat in old cars
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪki sit /; / ˈdɪki ˌsit / (AmE), / ˈdɪki siːt /; / ˈdɪki ˌsiːt / (BrE)
A small extra seat in the back of some old-fashioned two-seater cars, often folding and exposed to the weather.
