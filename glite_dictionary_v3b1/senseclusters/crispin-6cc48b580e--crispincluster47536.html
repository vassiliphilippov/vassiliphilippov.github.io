Crispin

proper noun
patron saint of shoemakers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪspɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspɪn / (BrE)
A Christian saint of the 3rd century, regarded as the patron saint of shoemakers, who was martyred for spreading Christianity.

Examples

Crispin the saint
Every October 25th, shoemakers celebrate the feast of Crispin as their patron saint.
She read about Saint Crispin in a book on Christian martyrs.
The legend of Saint Crispin has inspired many artisan guilds throughout Europe.
