neutral

adjective
not chemically reactive
5.2 4.2

not chemically reactive; having little or no ability to react with other substances

Examples

Neutral gas in chemistry
Because helium is a neutral gas, it does not easily participate in chemical reactions.
Some metals appear shiny and neutral because they are resistant to corrosion.
The laboratory uses neutral substances to prevent unwanted side reactions.
