northeasternmost

adjective
furthest northeast
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈiːstərnˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːstənməʊst / (BrE)
farthest toward the northeast compared to all others in a group or area

Examples

The northeasternmost village is often the first to see snowfall each winter.
