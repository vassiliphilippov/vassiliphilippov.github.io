Anton van Leuwenhoek

proper noun
Dutch pioneer microscopist (1632–1723)
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.tɔːn væn ˈluː.ən.hʊk/ /; / /ˈæn.tɔːn væn ˈleɪ.vən.hʊk/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tən væn ˈluː.ən.hʊk/ /; / /ˈæn.tɒn væn ˈleɪ.vən.hʊk/ (BrE)
(1632–1723) Dutch scientist known as the pioneer of microscopy, who was among the first to observe animal cells and described microbes, spermatozoa, and blood corpuscles.
