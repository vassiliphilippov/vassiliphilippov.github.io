lord over

verb
to dominate others
Pronunciation: / lɔrd ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / lɔːd ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to act in a superior or domineering way toward others

Examples

To lord over others
He always tries to lord over his younger siblings, telling them what to do.
Some managers lord over their teams instead of working with them as equals.
She disliked people who would lord over others just because of their job titles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - to intimidate or mistreat someone weaker
  • synonym: dominate - to have control or power over others
  • antonym: submit - to yield to the authority or control of another
