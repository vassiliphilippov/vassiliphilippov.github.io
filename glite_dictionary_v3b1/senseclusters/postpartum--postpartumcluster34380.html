postpartum

adjective
after childbirth
5.0 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / po ästÀàp…ërt…ôm / (AmE), / p…ô ästÀàp…ëÀêt…ôm / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the period immediately after childbirth

Examples

The postpartum period
Many women experience a range of emotional changes during the postpartum period.
Regular check-ups are important for both mother and baby in the weeks postpartum.
She joined a postpartum support group to connect with other new mothers.

Synonyms

  • postnatal
    4.1 0.40/m
    postnatal relates to the period after birth for mother or baby, while postpartum mainly refers to the mother's immediate after-birth period.
  • puerperal
    9.9 0.03/m
    puerperal is a formal medical word used for a woman soon after childbirth, while postpartum is more common in general use.
