transaxle

noun
combined transmission and axle
Pronunciation: / trænzˈæksəl / (AmE), / trænzˈæksəl / (BrE)
a single mechanical unit in a vehicle that combines the transmission and the axle, often used to improve weight distribution

Examples

A transaxle unit
The engineer explained how the transaxle improves the car's handling by balancing the weight.
Replacing the transaxle can be expensive due to its complex design.
Many sports cars use a rear-mounted transaxle to achieve better performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axle - a rod or shaft that rotates the wheels of a vehicle
  • synonym: transmission - the system in a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels
