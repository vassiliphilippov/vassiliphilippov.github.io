grounds

noun
coffee or tea residue; dregs
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊndz / (AmE), / ɡraʊndz / (BrE)
the solid particles left at the bottom of a liquid after brewing coffee or tea; dregs or residue

Examples

Coffee grounds

Turkish coffee is famous for leaving grounds used in fortune telling.
