nonmigrating

adjective
not migrating
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not moving from one place to another, especially not migrating seasonally as animals or birds do

Examples

Nonmigrating birds
Some species of geese are nonmigrating and remain in the same area all year.
The nonmigrating population of butterflies can be found in this region throughout the winter.
Unlike their migratory relatives, these birds are nonmigrating and nest locally every season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: migratory - moving from one place to another, especially seasonally
  • synonym: sedentary - staying in one place; not migrating
