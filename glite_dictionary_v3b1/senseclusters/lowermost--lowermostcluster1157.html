lowermost

adjective
at the lowest position
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊərˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈləʊəməʊst / (BrE)
situated at the lowest or deepest position; furthest down in location

Examples

The lowermost layer
Roots tend to grow towards the lowermost layer of soil where moisture is higher.
The lowermost branches of the tree were pruned last spring.
The lowermost drawer was jammed and wouldn't open.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottommost - furthest at the bottom
  • antonym: uppermost - furthest at the top or highest position
