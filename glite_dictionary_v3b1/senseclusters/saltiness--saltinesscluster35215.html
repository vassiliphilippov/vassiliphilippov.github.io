saltiness

noun
earthy or slightly coarse language or humor
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔl.ti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːl.ti.nəs / (BrE)
(of language or humor) the quality of being earthy, witty, or slightly coarse, often in an entertaining or direct way.

Examples

Saltiness in jokes
Her grandmother was known for the saltiness of her jokes at family gatherings.
Some critics argue that the saltiness of the novel's dialogue adds authenticity to the characters.
The comedian's saltiness was well-received by the late-night crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarseness - the quality of being rough or vulgar, especially in speech
  • synonym: earthiness - plain, natural, or unrefined quality, often in language or humor
  • antonym: refinement - the process of removing vulgarity or crudeness from language, making it more polite
