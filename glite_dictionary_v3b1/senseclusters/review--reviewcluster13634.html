review

verb
examine or look at again
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈvjuː/ /; / /rə.ˈvjuː/ /; / /riː.ˈvjuː/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈvjuː/ /; / /rə.ˈvjuː/ (BrE)
