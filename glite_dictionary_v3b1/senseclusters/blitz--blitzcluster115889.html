blitz

noun
intense military attack with bombing
Pronunciation: / blɪts / (AmE), / blɪts / (BrE)
a swift and intense military attack, especially one involving heavy aerial bombardment

Examples

Aerial blitz
Residents took shelter in underground bunkers to survive the nightly blitz.
The city suffered a severe blitz during the closing months of the war.
The sudden blitz caught the defenders completely off guard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bombardment - an attack by dropping bombs or shelling
