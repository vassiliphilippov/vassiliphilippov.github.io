microclimatologist

noun
small-scale climate scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊklaɪməˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊklaɪməˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies climate patterns and effects in small, specific areas

Examples

The microclimatologist studies weather
The microclimatologist collected data on temperature changes in the city garden.
A team led by a microclimatologist studied how buildings affect local wind patterns.
Farmers consulted a microclimatologist to understand the unique climate of their valley.
