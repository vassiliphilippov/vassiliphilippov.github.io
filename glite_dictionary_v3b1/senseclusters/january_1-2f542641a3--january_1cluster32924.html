January 1

noun
first day of the year; New Year's Day
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒænjuˈɛri fɜrst / (AmE), / ˌdʒan.juˈɛː.ri fɜːst / (BrE)
the first day of the year in the Gregorian calendar, often celebrated as New Year's Day.

Examples

January 1 holiday
Banks and businesses may close for the holiday on January 1.
Fireworks are often set off to celebrate January 1 around the world.
Many people make resolutions on January 1 to start the year with new goals.
