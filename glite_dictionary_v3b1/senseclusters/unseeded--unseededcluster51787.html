unseeded

adjective
no active seeders (file sharing)
7.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsiː.dɪd/ /; / /ən.ˈsiː.dɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsiː.dɪd/ /; / /ən.ˈsiː.dɪd/ (BrE)
(in peer-to-peer file sharing) having no active seeders; a torrent or file that currently cannot be downloaded because nobody is sharing the complete data.

Examples

Unseeded torrent fails
The unseeded torrent remained at zero percent because no peers had the complete file.
After uploading, they discovered the episode was instantly popular, but later it became unseeded.
Users flagged several unseeded files in the tracker to warn others they were unavailable.

Related Terms
