exhaust

verb
use up completely (resources)
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔst /; / ɪgˈzɔst / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst /; / ɪgˈzɔːst / (BrE)
to use up or completely deplete a resource, supply, or material

Examples

You should rest before you exhaust all your strength on the hike.
