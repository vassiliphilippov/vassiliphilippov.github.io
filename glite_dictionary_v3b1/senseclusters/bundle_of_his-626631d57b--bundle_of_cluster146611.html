bundle of His

noun
heart muscle fibers for electrical conduction
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌndəl əv hɪs / (AmE), / ˈbʌndəl əv hɪs / (BrE)
a bundle of specialized heart muscle fibers that conduct electrical impulses from the atrioventricular node to the ventricles, triggering their contraction

Examples

The bundle of His fibers
An abnormality in the bundle of His can lead to serious cardiac arrhythmias.
Electrical signals pass through the bundle of His before reaching the ventricles.
The surgeon carefully identified the bundle of His during the procedure to avoid damaging it.
