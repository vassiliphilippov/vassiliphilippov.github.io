hijinks

noun
boisterous and mischievous fun
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌdʒɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌdʒɪŋks / (BrE)
Boisterous and playful activities, often involving mischief or pranks, usually in a lighthearted or festive spirit.

Examples

Funny hijinks
After hours, the office became a place for occasional hijinks among coworkers.
The bachelor party was full of hijinks that kept everyone laughing all night.
Their summer camp memories were filled with pranks and other juvenile hijinks.
