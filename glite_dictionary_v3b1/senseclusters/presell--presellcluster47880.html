presell

noun
advance sale
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌsɛl / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌsɛl / (BrE)
a sale made before a product or service is available for delivery, often as a pre-order.

Examples

A presell order
The company reported strong presell numbers after announcing the limited edition model last week.
Many fans participated in the presell to secure their tickets before the official release.
The presell of the new smartphone exceeded all expectations, with thousands of units reserved in advance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pre-order - an order placed for a product before it is available
  • degree lower: sale - the exchange of a product or service for money
