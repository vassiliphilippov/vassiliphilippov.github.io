hibernaculum

noun
animal winter shelter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪbərˈnækjələm / (AmE), / ˌhaɪbəˈnækjʊləm /; / ˌhaɪbənˈækjʊləm / (BrE)
a place where an animal hibernates or spends the winter in a dormant state

Examples

The bats returned to their hibernaculum deep inside the abandoned mine for the winter.
Scientists studied the temperature inside the hibernaculum to understand how reptiles survive cold months.

The bears retreated to their hibernaculum as the first snow began to fall.
