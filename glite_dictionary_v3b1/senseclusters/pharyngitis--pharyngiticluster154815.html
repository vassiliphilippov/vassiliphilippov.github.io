pharyngitis

noun
inflammation of the pharynx (sore throat)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the pharynx, resulting in a sore throat and discomfort.

Examples

Pharyngitis sore throat
Most cases of pharyngitis are caused by viral infections, but some are bacterial.
Patients with pharyngitis are typically advised to rest, hydrate, and take pain relievers.
The doctor diagnosed her with pharyngitis after examining her sore throat and swollen glands.
