languidly

adverb
in a slow and unenergetic way
7.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪd.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪd.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a slow, relaxed, and unenergetic manner; lacking energy or enthusiasm

Examples

To languidly move
He languidly sipped his tea, unconcerned by the noise around him.
She languidly stretched out on the sofa, staring at the ceiling.
The cat languidly blinked in the afternoon sunlight, barely moving a muscle.
