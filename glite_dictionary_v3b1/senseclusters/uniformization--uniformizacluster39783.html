uniformization

noun
mathematical simplification
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnəˌfɔrməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˌfɔːmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process in mathematics of giving a space a uniform structure or representing a complex space in a simpler, uniform way

Examples

Uniformization theorem
The uniformization theorem is a fundamental result in the theory of Riemann surfaces.
Researchers discussed the uniformization of complex spaces at the international mathematics conference.
Understanding uniformization helps mathematicians simplify and analyze complicated geometric structures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalization - the process of making something more general or widespread
  • synonym: standardization - the process of making things conform to a standard
