luv

interjection
friendly address term
9.3
Pronunciation: / lʌv / (AmE), / lʌv / (BrE)
an informal and friendly way to address someone, especially used in British English

Examples

Thanks, luv
Could you, luv, hold this while I tie my shoelace?
Thanks for your help, luv, I really appreciate it.
All right, luv, I'll see you tomorrow at the shop.
