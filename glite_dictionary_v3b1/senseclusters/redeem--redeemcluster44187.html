redeem

verb
exchange vouchers or coupons for goods
2.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdim / (AmE), / rɪˈdiːm / (BrE)
to exchange (vouchers, coupons, or similar items) for goods, services, or money

Examples

To redeem a coupon
She redeemed her gift voucher for a free coffee at the café.
The store allows customers to redeem coupons both in person and online.
You can redeem your points for discounts at participating stores.
