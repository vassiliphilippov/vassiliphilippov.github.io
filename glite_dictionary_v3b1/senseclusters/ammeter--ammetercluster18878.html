ammeter

noun
instrument measuring electric current
Pronunciation: / ˈæmˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈamˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring the strength of electric current in amperes

Examples

A ammeter device
A digital ammeter provides a more accurate reading than an analog one.
Before repairing the device, always disconnect the ammeter to prevent damage.
The electrician used an ammeter to check the current flowing through the circuit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ohmmeter - an instrument used to measure electrical resistance
  • synonym: voltmeter - an instrument measuring electrical potential difference (voltage) between two points
