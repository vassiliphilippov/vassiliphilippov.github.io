cheer

verb
show support by shouting
1.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /tʃɪr/ (AmE), /tʃɪə/ (BrE)
to shout to show support, approval, or encouragement for someone or something

Examples

To cheer for a team
Fans cheered loudly when the home team scored a goal.
The crowd began to cheer as the band took the stage.
They were cheering for their classmates during the final competition.
She stood on the sidelines, cheering her friends during the race.
The fans cheered as the athlete crossed the finish line.
When our team scored the winning goal, everyone cheered loudly in the stadium.
Friends gathered outside the hospital to cheer the doctors and nurses.
She stood by the finish line to cheer on her teammate.
The crowd began to cheer as the runners entered the stadium.
