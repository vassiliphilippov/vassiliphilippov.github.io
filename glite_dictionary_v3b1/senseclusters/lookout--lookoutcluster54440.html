lookout

noun
the act of observing or watching out
2.7 1.13/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆlÊŠkËŒaÊŠt / (AmE), / ËˆlÊŠk.aÊŠt / (BrE)
the act of carefully observing something or keeping watch for danger

Examples

Keep lookout
Good lookout is essential when hiking in areas where wildlife may appear unexpectedly.
She kept a careful lookout for her friend in the crowded station.
The sailor maintained a vigilant lookout throughout the night to ensure the ship stayed on course.

  • observation
    3.0 35/m
