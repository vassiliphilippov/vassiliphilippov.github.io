stockroom

noun
room for storing goods in business
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a business where goods and supplies are stored before they are needed or sold

Examples

The stockroom door
Employees are not allowed in the stockroom without authorization.
New inventory is delivered to the stockroom every Monday morning.
The manager asked me to check the stockroom for more supplies.
