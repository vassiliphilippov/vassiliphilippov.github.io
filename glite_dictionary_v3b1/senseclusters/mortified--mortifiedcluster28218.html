Examples

Mortified tissue

The surgeon noted that the patient's foot was mortified and required urgent intervention.

Synonyms

  • gangrenous
    9.1 0.2/m
    gangrenous describes tissue that looks decayed and often smells bad from infection, while mortified refers to dead tissue in medical use.
  • necrotic
    8.2 0.3/m
    necrotic is the technical term doctors use in reports to name dead tissue, while mortified is an older medical word for the same condition.
