honeysuckle

noun
a climbing shrub or vine with fragrant flowers
7.1 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl /; / ˈhʌnɪˌsʌkəl / (BrE)
a climbing shrub or vine with sweetly scented, tubular flowers, often grown for decoration

Examples

Honeysuckle vine
Honeysuckle vines can quickly cover a trellis, creating natural shade and privacy.
