boneyard

noun
place for discarded items
8.0 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbo äniÀåj…ërd / (AmE), / Ààb…ô äniÀåj…ëÀêd / (BrE)
a place where old vehicles, equipment, or discarded items are stored or left to decay

Examples

Airplane boneyard
The old airplane was left to rust in the boneyard at the edge of the airport.
We found several classic cars in the boneyard behind the mechanic's shop.
After years of use, the broken machines ended up in the factory's boneyard.

Synonyms

  • junkyard
    3.5 1.89/m
    a junkyard stores old vehicles and parts for resale or repair, while a boneyard suggests long-term decay and abandonment.
  • scrapyard
    2.8 0.14/m
    a scrapyard collects and dismantles metal vehicles for recycling or sale, while a boneyard mainly stores old equipment left to decay.
