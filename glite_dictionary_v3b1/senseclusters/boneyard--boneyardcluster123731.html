boneyard

noun
place for discarded items
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊn.jɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊn.jɑːd/ (BrE)
a place where old vehicles, equipment, or discarded items are stored or left to decay

Examples

Airplane boneyard
The old airplane was left to rust in the boneyard at the edge of the airport.
We found several classic cars in the boneyard behind the mechanic's shop.
After years of use, the broken machines ended up in the factory's boneyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graveyard - a place where dead bodies are buried
  • synonym: junkyard - a place where old or discarded vehicles and other items are collected
