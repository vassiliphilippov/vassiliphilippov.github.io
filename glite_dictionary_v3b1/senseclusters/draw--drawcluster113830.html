draw

verb
achieve a base by being walked (baseball)
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
to select or choose something, especially at random, or to take a card from a deck

Examples

To draw a card
He rarely swings at bad pitches, so he often draws walks.
She hopes to draw a base next time she faces this pitcher.
The batter managed to draw a walk in the ninth inning.
A random sample was drawn from the population for the survey.
The finalists will be drawn from applicants meeting all the necessary criteria.
The students were drawn from different schools across the country.
Each player must draw a card from the deck before their turn.
Please draw one name out of the hat for the prize.
The winners will be drawn at random from all entries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choose - to decide or pick one from a number of options
  • synonym: pick - to select or choose from a group
  • synonym: select - to choose from a number of alternatives
  • synonym: walk - in baseball, a batter's advance to first base after four balls
