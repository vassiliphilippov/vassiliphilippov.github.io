heavy-limbed

adjective
having large or heavy limbs
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛviˈlɪmd / (AmE), / ˌhɛviˈlɪmd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having large, heavy, or thick limbs; moving or appearing with a sense of weight or sluggishness because of heavy limbs.

Examples

A heavy-limbed person
She woke up so heavy-limbed that even walking to the kitchen felt like a chore.
The heavy-limbed bear lumbered slowly through the forest.
The exhausted runner trudged forward, feeling heavy-limbed after the marathon.
