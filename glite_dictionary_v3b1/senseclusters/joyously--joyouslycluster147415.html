joyously

adverb
in a joyful or exultant manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪəsli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪəsli / (BrE)
In a manner showing great joy or happiness; gleefully or exultantly.

Examples

To joyously celebrate
She joyously accepted the award, her face alight with happiness.
The children ran joyously through the field under the bright spring sun.
The crowd joyously celebrated the team's unexpected victory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gleefully - in a gleeful or delightfully happy manner
  • antonym: miserably - in a wretched or unhappy manner
