harum-scarum

adjective
recklessly irresponsible or wild
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrəmˌskɛrəm / (AmE), / ˈharəmˌskarəm / (BrE)
Recklessly or cheerfully irresponsible; behaving in a wild, careless, or impetuous way.

Examples

Harum-scarum behavior
His harum-scarum spending habits soon landed him in trouble.
They lived a harum-scarum lifestyle in their youth, never thinking about tomorrow.
With her harum-scarum attitude, she was always up for an adventure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefree - free from worry or responsibility
  • antonym: cautious - careful to avoid danger or mistakes
  • synonym: reckless - showing a lack of care about danger or consequences
