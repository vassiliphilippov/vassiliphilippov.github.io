touchless

adjective
no physical contact
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃləs / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃləs / (BrE)
operating or functioning without the need for physical contact

Examples

Touchless check-in kiosks
The airport uses touchless check-in kiosks to speed up the boarding process.
Many public restrooms now feature touchless soap dispensers for better hygiene.
She appreciated the touchless payment system at the grocery store.
