rebar

noun
steel reinforcing bar
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbɑː / (BrE)
a steel bar or mesh of steel wires used to reinforce concrete in construction

Examples

Steel rebar rods
The construction crew installed rebar before pouring the concrete foundation.
Inspectors checked the placement of rebar to ensure the building would be structurally sound.
Rust on exposed rebar can weaken the strength of a concrete structure over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concrete - a building material made from cement, water, and aggregate
  • synonym: reinforcement - material used to strengthen concrete or other structures
