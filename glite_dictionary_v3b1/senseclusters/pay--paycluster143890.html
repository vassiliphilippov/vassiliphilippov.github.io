pay

verb
give or bestow attention, respect, etc.
Pronunciation: / peɪ / (AmE), / peɪ / (BrE)
to give or show something non-material, such as attention, respect, tribute, or compliments, to someone or something

Examples

To pay attention
It is important to pay attention during safety drills.
She refused to pay him any compliments after the performance.
You should always pay your respects at a funeral.
She always pays tribute to her mentors during award ceremonies.
The coach reminded the players to pay respect to the referee's decisions.
You should pay close attention to the instructions before starting the test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: give - to hand over or bestow something to someone
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately not pay attention to something
  • synonym: pay attention - to give close attention to something
