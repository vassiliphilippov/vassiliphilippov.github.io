boombox

noun
portable stereo system
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːmˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbuːmˌbɒks / (BrE)
a portable stereo system with built-in speakers, often used to play music loudly in public places

Examples

A boombox speaker
He carried his boombox to the park to play music for everyone.
In the 1980s, teenagers often listened to music on a boombox while hanging out with friends.

He carried his boombox to the park and played music for his friends.
The old boombox still works, even though it is over twenty years old.
She danced on the sidewalk while her boombox blasted her favorite songs.

  • antonym: headphones - a device worn on the ears to listen to audio privately
  • degree lower: radio - a device for receiving and playing broadcast audio
  • synonym: stereo - a system for playing music with two or more speakers
  • degree higher: stereo system - an audio system for playing music with two or more speakers
