benny

noun
informal bonus
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛni / (AmE), / ˈbɛni / (BrE)
an informal term for a benefit, bonus, or stroke of good luck

Examples

A work benny
Getting an extra day off was a nice benny from the company.
He considered the free lunch a welcome benny of the job.
Winning the raffle was an unexpected benny at the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: penalty - a disadvantage or loss as a result of an action
