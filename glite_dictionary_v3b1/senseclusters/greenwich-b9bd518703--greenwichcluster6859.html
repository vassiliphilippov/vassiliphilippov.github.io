Greenwich

proper noun
borough of London; site of prime meridian
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɛnɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɛnɪdʒ / (BrE)
A borough of Greater London on the River Thames, famous for being the site of the prime meridian and the basis for Greenwich Mean Time.

Examples

Greenwich Mean Time
Every year, thousands of tourists visit Greenwich to stand on the line dividing east from west.
The history of Greenwich is closely tied to navigation and timekeeping.
The prime meridian passes directly through Greenwich, establishing zero degrees longitude.
