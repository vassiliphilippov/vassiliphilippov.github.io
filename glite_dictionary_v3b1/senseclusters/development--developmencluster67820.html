development

noun
improving land or resources
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the process of making land, water, or natural resources more productive, profitable, or useful

Examples

Land development
Environmentalists debated the development of untouched forest areas for housing.
Rapid development of the desert region has increased local employment and infrastructure.
The city invested in the development of a new waterfront park to attract tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploitation - the act of using resources to derive benefit
  • synonym: improvement - the act of making something better
