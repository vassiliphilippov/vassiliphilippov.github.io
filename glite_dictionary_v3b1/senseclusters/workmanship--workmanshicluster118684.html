workmanship

noun
skill or quality in making something
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkmənʃɪp / (BrE)
The skill with which something is made or done, especially in a craft or trade.

Examples

Excellent workmanship
Customers appreciated the superb workmanship that went into the restoration of the old church.
The meticulous workmanship of the handmade table impressed everyone at the exhibition.
The shoes are expensive, but their high-quality workmanship ensures they last for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftsmanship - the quality of design and work shown in something made by hand; expertise in a craft
