quantifiable

adjective
able to be measured or quantified
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɑn.təˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌkwɒn.tɪˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be measured or expressed as a quantity

Examples

Quantifiable amount
Customer satisfaction is not always easily quantifiable.
The data must be quantifiable in order to support your hypothesis.
We set quantifiable goals so progress could be measured objectively.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immeasurable - too great or extreme to be measured
  • synonym: measurable - able to be measured
