percentage sign

noun
the symbol '%' for per cent
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɛntɪdʒ saɪn / (AmE), / pəˈsɛntɪdʒ sʌɪn / (BrE)
the symbol '%' used to indicate that a number is expressed as a proportion out of 100

Examples

The percentage sign

The percentage sign is used frequently in statistics and finance to denote proportions.
