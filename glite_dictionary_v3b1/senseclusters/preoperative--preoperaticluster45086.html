preoperative

adjective
before a surgical operation
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈɑpərətɪv / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈɒpərətɪv / (BrE)
happening or done before and in preparation for a surgical operation

Examples

The preoperative checklist
Her blood pressure was monitored closely as part of the preoperative checklist.
Patients received specific instructions to follow during the preoperative period.
The preoperative assessment revealed no underlying health issues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postoperative - happening or done after a surgical operation
