toque

noun
small round woman's hat
Pronunciation: / toʊk / (AmE), / təʊk / (BrE)
A small, round, close-fitting woman's hat, often without a brim.

Examples

A velvet toque
Her grandmother gave her a velvet toque as a gift.
In the 1920s, the toque became popular among fashionable women.
She wore a stylish toque to the wedding.
