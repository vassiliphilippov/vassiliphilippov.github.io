snap at

verb
bite or attempt to bite quickly
Pronunciation: / snæp æt / (AmE), / snæp ət / (BrE)
to bite or try to bite quickly and suddenly, often with a sharp movement of the jaws

Examples

To snap at someone
Don’t get too close to the cage or the dog might snap at you.
The angry goose snapped at my fingers when I tried to feed it.
The fish snapped at the bait as soon as it hit the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bite - to cut or grip with the teeth
  • synonym: nip - to pinch or bite sharply
