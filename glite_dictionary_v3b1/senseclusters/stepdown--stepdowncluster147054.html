stepdown

noun
reduction in level
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a reduction in level, size, or intensity, especially in voltage, care, or service

Examples

Stepdown unit
After surgery, the patient was moved to a stepdown unit for monitoring.
The engineer recommended a stepdown in voltage to protect the equipment.
A sudden stepdown in service quality led to customer complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decrease - a process of becoming less or smaller
  • antonym: increase - a process of becoming greater or larger
