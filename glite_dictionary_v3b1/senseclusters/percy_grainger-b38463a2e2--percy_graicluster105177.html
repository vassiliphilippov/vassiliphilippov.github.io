Percy Grainger

proper noun
Australian-born composer and collector of English folk songs
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.siː ˈgreɪn.dʒər/ /; / /ˈpɜːr.sɪ ˈgreɪn.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.siː ˈgreɪn.dʒə/ /; / /ˈpɜː.sɪ ˈgreɪn.dʒə/ (BrE)
(1882–1961) An Australian-born composer, arranger, and pianist who became known for his collection of English folk songs and his innovative musical works; he spent much of his life in the United States and Britain.
