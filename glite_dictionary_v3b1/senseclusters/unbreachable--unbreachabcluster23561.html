unbreachable

adjective
impossible to penetrate
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbriːtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbriːtʃəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to break through, enter, or overcome

Examples

An unbreachable wall
The castle's walls were so thick they seemed unbreachable to any attacker.
With its advanced security system, the vault was considered unbreachable by experts.
Their friendship formed an unbreachable bond that lasted for decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impregnable - impossible to capture or enter by force
  • synonym: invulnerable - impossible to harm or damage
  • antonym: vulnerable - able to be easily hurt or attacked
