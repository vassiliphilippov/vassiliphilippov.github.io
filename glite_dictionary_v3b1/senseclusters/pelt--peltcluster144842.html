pelt

noun
animal skin with hair or fur
6.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /pelt/ (AmE), /pelt/ (BrE)
the skin of an animal with the fur, wool, or hair still attached, especially after being removed and prepared for use

Examples

The hunter sold the fox's pelt at the market.
