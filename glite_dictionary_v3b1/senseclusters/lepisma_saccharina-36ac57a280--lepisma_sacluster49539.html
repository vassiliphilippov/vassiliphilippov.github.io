Lepisma saccharina

noun
a household insect, also called silverfish
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: / ləˈpɪzmə ˌsækəˈraɪnə / (AmE), / lɛˈpɪzmə ˌsakəˈrʌɪnə / (BrE)
