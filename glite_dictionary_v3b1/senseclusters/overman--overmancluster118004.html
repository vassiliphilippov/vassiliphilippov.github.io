overman

noun
victor or conqueror
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəmæn / (BrE)
a person who defeats, masters, or dominates others, especially as a victor or conqueror in a contest or conflict

Examples

The story follows an ambitious overman who rises to power through determination and skill.
In the final round, only one overman could claim the ultimate prize.
