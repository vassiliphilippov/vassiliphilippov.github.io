Scomber scombrus

noun
a species of Atlantic mackerel
Pronunciation: / ˈskɒmbər ˈskɒmbrəs / (AmE), / ˈskɒmbə ˈskɒmbrəs / (BrE)
A species of mackerel found in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean, valued as a food fish; its body is greenish-blue with dark bars and few or no scales.
