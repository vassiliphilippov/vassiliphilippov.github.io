wireless telegraphy

noun
sending telegrams by radio signals
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪrləs təˈlɛɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəlɪs tɪˈlɛɡrəfi / (BrE)
the sending of telegraphic messages without wires, especially by using radio signals or waves
