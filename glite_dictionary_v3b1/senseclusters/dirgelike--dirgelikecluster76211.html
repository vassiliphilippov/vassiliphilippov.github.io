dirgelike

adjective
mournful or solemn
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜrdʒˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɜːdʒˌlaɪk / (BrE)

The film's soundtrack was so dirgelike that it made the sad scenes even more powerful.
