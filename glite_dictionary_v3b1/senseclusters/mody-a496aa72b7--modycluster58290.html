MODY

noun
inherited diabetes type
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈməʊ.diː/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.diː/ (BrE)
a form of diabetes that is inherited and typically develops in adolescence or early adulthood

Examples

MODY diabetes

Genetic counseling is often recommended for families affected by MODY.
Unlike other forms of diabetes, MODY is caused by a single gene mutation.
