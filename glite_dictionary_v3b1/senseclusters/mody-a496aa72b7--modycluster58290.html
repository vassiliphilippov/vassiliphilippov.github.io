MODY

noun
inherited diabetes type
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊdi / (AmE), / ˈməʊdi / (BrE)
a form of diabetes that is inherited and typically develops in adolescence or early adulthood

Examples

MODY diabetes
After several tests, the doctor confirmed that he had MODY rather than type 1 diabetes.
Genetic counseling is often recommended for families affected by MODY.
Unlike other forms of diabetes, MODY is caused by a single gene mutation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: type 1 diabetes - a form of diabetes usually diagnosed in children and young adults
  • antonym: type 2 diabetes - a common form of diabetes often developing in adults
