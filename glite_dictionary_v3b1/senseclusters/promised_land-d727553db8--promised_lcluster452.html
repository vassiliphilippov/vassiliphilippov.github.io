promised land

noun
an ideal or blissful place or situation
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.mɪst lænd / (AmE), / ˈprɒm.ɪst lænd / (BrE)
a place or situation where one expects to find happiness, fulfillment, or peace, often after a long wait or struggle

Examples

The promised land of peace
After years of hard work, the researchers finally reached their promised land of groundbreaking discovery.
For some immigrants, America represented a new promised land full of opportunities.
She spent her life in search of her own promised land, a place where she could feel at peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heaven - a place or state of great happiness, often in religious contexts
  • antonym: hell - a place or state of great suffering, often contrasted with heaven
  • synonym: paradise - an ideal place or state of perfect happiness
