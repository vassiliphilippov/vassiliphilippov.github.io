spot-weld

verb
join metals with small welds
5.3 5.6
Pronunciation: /spɑː.ˈtweld/ /; / /spɔː.ˈtweld/ (AmE), /spɒ.ˈtweld/ (BrE)
to join metal parts together by making small, circular welds at several points using heat and pressure

Examples

To spot-weld metal
Many modern car bodies are spot-welded during the manufacturing process.
The technician will spot-weld the sheets together for extra strength.
These metal brackets are spot-welded to the frame at several points.
