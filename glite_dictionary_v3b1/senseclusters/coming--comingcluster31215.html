Synonyms

  • arrival
    1.0 22/m
    arrival is the act of reaching a place after travel and sounds more formal, while coming is more common and broader.
  • approach
    3.0 1.87/m
    approach is the process of something drawing nearer in time or space, while coming can mean either approach or arrival.
  • advent
    5.4 6.04/m
    advent is the arrival of an important or long-awaited thing, while coming is a general, everyday word for arrival or approach.
