coming

noun
approaching or impending arrival
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrival or approach of something or someone (such as an event, person, or time)

Examples

The coming of spring
Everyone was eagerly awaiting the coming of the new product.
The coming of spring brings new hope each year.
The coming of winter forced the animals to seek shelter.
People eagerly awaited the coming of the famous author to the city.
The coming of spring is always welcomed after a harsh winter.
With the coming of the digital age, many industries had to adapt quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advent - the arrival of a notable person or event
  • synonym: arrival - the act of reaching a place or event
  • antonym: departure - the act of leaving, opposite of arrival
