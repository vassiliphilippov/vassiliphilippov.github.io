party pooper

noun
person who spoils others' fun
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrti ˌpuːpər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˌpuːpə / (BrE)
A person who spoils other people's enjoyment or fun at social events.

Examples

A party pooper spoils fun
Don't be such a party pooper—join us on the dance floor!
I don't want to be a party pooper, but we should keep the noise down.
She called him a party pooper for leaving early.

Related Terms

  • synonym: killjoy - a person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment of others
  • synonym: wet blanket - a person who discourages others from having fun
