Guadalupe

proper noun
Virgin Mary title
Pronunciation: / ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ / (BrE)
a title for the Virgin Mary, especially honored in Mexico, and the name of a famous religious shrine near Mexico City

Examples

Virgin of Guadalupe
Thousands of pilgrims visit Guadalupe every December to celebrate the feast day.
The image of Guadalupe is an important symbol of Mexican identity and faith.
Many families keep a picture of Guadalupe in their homes for protection and blessings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Our Lady of Guadalupe - the Virgin Mary as she appeared in Mexico
  • synonym: Virgin Mary - the mother of Jesus in Christian tradition
