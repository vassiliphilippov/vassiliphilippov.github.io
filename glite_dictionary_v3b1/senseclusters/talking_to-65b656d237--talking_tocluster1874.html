talking to

noun
a stern reprimand; scolding
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔkɪŋ tu / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ tʊ / (BrE)
an occasion when someone is sternly reprimanded or scolded for improper behavior

Examples

A talking to from boss
After missing the deadline, Sarah got a serious talking to from her supervisor.
If you don't clean your room, you're going to get a talking to from Dad.
The coach gave the team a harsh talking to after their disappointing performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lecture - a formal or stern scolding given for misconduct
  • synonym: rebuke - an expression of sharp disapproval or criticism
