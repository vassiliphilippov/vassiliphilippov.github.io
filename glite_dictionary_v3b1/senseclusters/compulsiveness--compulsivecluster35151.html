compulsiveness

noun
the tendency to act on irresistible urges
4.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpʌl.sɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpʌl.sɪv.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or condition of being characterized by strong, irresistible urges to act, especially in repetitive or habitual ways

Examples

Strong compulsiveness
Her compulsiveness made it difficult for her to break unhealthy habits.
Some people struggle with compulsiveness when it comes to checking their phones.
The therapist addressed his client's compulsiveness regarding tidiness and order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsivity - the tendency to act on irresistible urges or impulses
  • synonym: impulsiveness - the tendency to act on sudden urges without thought
  • antonym: restraint - the ability to control one's impulses or urges
