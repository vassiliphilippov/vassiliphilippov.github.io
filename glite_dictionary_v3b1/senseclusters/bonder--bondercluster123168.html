bonder

noun
equipment operator
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑndər / (AmE), / ˈbɒndə / (BrE)
a person who operates bonding equipment or joins components together, especially in manufacturing or electronics

Examples

Electronics bonder
The company is looking for an experienced bonder to join their electronics assembly team.
As a bonder, she ensures that all components are securely joined during the manufacturing process.
The bonder carefully operated the machine to attach tiny wires to the circuit board.
