crusty

adjective
gruff or surly in manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌsti / (AmE), / ˈkrʌsti / (BrE)
(of a person or their manner) brusque, surly, or forbidding; easily irritated or gruff.

Examples

Crusty old man
Despite his crusty demeanor, he had a kind heart underneath.
Her crusty remarks sometimes made people uncomfortable, but she was honest.
The crusty old professor snapped at his students for talking during class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curmudgeonly - bad-tempered or surly, especially in older people
  • synonym: gruff - rough or abrupt in manner or speech
  • antonym: pleasant - friendly and agreeable in manner
