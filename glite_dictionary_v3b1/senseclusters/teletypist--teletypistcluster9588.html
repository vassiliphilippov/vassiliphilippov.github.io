teletypist

noun
teletype machine operator
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlitaɪpɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪtaɪpɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to operate a teletype machine to send and receive written messages

Examples

The teletypist sends messages
The teletypist relayed the message to the overseas office within minutes.
During the 1960s, every newsroom employed at least one skilled teletypist.
As a teletypist, she was responsible for sending urgent updates to the entire company.
