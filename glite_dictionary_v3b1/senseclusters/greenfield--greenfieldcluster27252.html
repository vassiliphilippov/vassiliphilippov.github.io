greenfield

noun
undeveloped land
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.fiːld/ /; / /ˈgriːn.fɪld/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.fiːld/ /; / /ˈgriːn.fɪld/ (BrE)
