Ding Dongs

noun
bell ringing sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋ dɔŋz / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋ dɒŋz / (BrE)
the repeated ringing sounds made by bells, especially when clear and resonant

Examples

Ding Dongs of bells
The Ding Dongs of the church bells echoed through the quiet village at noon.
Children laughed as the Ding Dongs from the ice cream truck signaled its arrival.
At the wedding, the joyful Ding Dongs of the bells filled the air with celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chimes - the musical sounds produced by bells or a set of bells
  • synonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
