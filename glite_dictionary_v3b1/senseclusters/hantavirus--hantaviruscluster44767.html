hantavirus

noun
rodent-borne virus
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːntəvaɪrəs /; / ˈhɑːntəˌvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈhantəvaɪrəs /; / ˈhantəˌvaɪrəs / (BrE)
a virus carried by rodents that can cause severe illness in humans, especially affecting the lungs or kidneys
