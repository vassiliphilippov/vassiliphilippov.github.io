nonfactual

adjective
not factual
6.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfæktʃuəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈfæktʃuəl / (BrE)
not based on facts or not containing factual information

Examples

Nonfactual report
The report was dismissed as nonfactual and misleading by several experts.
He often makes nonfactual claims that cannot be supported by evidence.
Teachers encourage students to avoid nonfactual information when writing research papers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: factual - based on facts or reality
  • synonym: untrue - not true; false
