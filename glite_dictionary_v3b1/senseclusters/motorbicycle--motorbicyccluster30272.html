motorbicycle

noun
bicycle with engine
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.tər.baɪ.sɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.tə.baɪ.sɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
a bicycle equipped with a small engine to assist pedaling, lighter and less powerful than a motorcycle

Examples

Electric motorbicycle
He bought a motorbicycle to make his daily commute faster and less tiring.
The motorbicycle is popular among students who need affordable transportation.
She rode her motorbicycle through the park, using the engine only on steep hills.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels powered only by pedaling
  • synonym: moped - a light two-wheeled vehicle with pedals and a small engine
  • degree higher: motorcycle - a two-wheeled vehicle powered by a much larger engine
