unoriginally

adverb
in an unoriginal manner
7.2 5.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ə.nəl.iː/ /; / /ʌn.ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ən.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ən.ə.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ən.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that lacks originality; in a manner that is not new or creative.

Examples

To speak unoriginally
His jokes fell flat because he delivered them unoriginally, without any new twist.
She completed the assignment unoriginally, repeating ideas that had already been discussed.
The novel was written unoriginally, following the same plot as many others before it.
