thurify

verb
perfume with incense, usually using a censer
Pronunciation: / ˈθʊrɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈθjʊərɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to perfume or fumigate (an area, person, or object), especially by burning incense in a censer as part of a religious ceremony

Examples

To thurify with incense
During the ceremony, acolytes thurified the congregation as a sign of blessing.
He watched the bishop thurify the sacred relics, filling the chapel with fragrant smoke.
The priest began to thurify the altar before the start of the Mass.
