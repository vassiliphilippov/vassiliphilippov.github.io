book binding machine

noun
machine for binding books
4.7
Pronunciation: / bʊk ˈbaɪndɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / bʊk ˈbaɪndɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used to fasten together the pages and cover of a book.

Examples

Modern book binding machines
Modern book binding machines can handle hundreds of books per hour.
