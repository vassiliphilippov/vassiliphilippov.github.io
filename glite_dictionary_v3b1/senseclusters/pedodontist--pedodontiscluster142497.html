pedodontist

noun
children's dental specialist
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdoʊˈdɑːntɪst / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəʊˈdɒntɪst / (BrE)
A dentist who specializes in the care and treatment of children's teeth.

Examples

A pedodontist for children
After noticing a cavity in her son's tooth, Jane scheduled an appointment with a pedodontist.
Many pedodontists also provide advice on how to prevent dental problems in children.
The pedodontist explained the importance of early oral hygiene to the children and their parents.
