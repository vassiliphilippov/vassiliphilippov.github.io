Qadhafi

proper noun
former Libyan leader (1969–2011)
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈgæ.də.fiː/ /; / /gə.ˈdɑː.fiː/ (AmE), /gə.ˈdɑː.fiː/ /; / /ˈgæ.də.fiː/ (BrE)
(1942–2011) Libyan military and political leader who ruled Libya from 1969 after leading a coup d'état, instituting his own form of government based on socialism and Islamic principles.

Examples

Qadhafi regime
Many historians consider Qadhafi one of the most controversial leaders of the late 20th century.
The regime under Qadhafi lasted more than four decades before its collapse in 2011.
Under Qadhafi, Libya experienced significant changes in its political and social structure.
