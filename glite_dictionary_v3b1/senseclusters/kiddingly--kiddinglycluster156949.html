kiddingly

adverb
jokingly; playfully
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkɪdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a joking or playful manner, not meant to be taken seriously

Examples

To speak kiddingly
She kiddingly suggested that they should all move to the beach for the summer.
When he called her the best chef in town, he was speaking kiddingly.
They kiddingly argued about who was the better singer during the car ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jokingly - in a humorous or playful way
  • antonym: seriously - in a sincere or earnest way
