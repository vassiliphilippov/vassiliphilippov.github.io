contraption

noun
strange or complicated device or machine
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræp.ʃən / (AmE), / kənˈtræp.ʃən / (BrE)
a strange or complicated device or machine, especially one whose purpose is not immediately clear

Examples

A strange contraption
He built a strange contraption to water the garden automatically.
No one could figure out how the contraption in the corner worked.
She carried the heavy contraption up the stairs with great effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: device - a thing made or adapted for a particular purpose, especially a mechanical or electronic one
  • synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic device with a particular purpose
