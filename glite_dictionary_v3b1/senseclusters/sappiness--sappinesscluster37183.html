sappiness

noun
juiciness in plants
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsæpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of containing sap or being full of juice, especially in plants; juiciness or vigorous growth

Examples

Plant sappiness
The sappiness of the young stems made them easy to bend without breaking.
After the rain, the garden was filled with the sappiness of new growth.
The farmer checked the sappiness of the vines to judge if they were ready for harvest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dryness - the quality of lacking moisture or juice
  • synonym: juiciness - the quality of being full of juice
