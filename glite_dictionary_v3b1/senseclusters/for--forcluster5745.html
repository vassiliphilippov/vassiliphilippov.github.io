for

preposition
on behalf of
Pronunciation: / fɔr / (AmE), / fɔː / (BrE)
on behalf of or representing someone, especially when acting as their spokesperson or agent

Examples

Speak for someone
He accepted the award for his colleague, who could not attend the ceremony.
The lawyer spoke for her client during the court hearing.
As the team captain, she answered questions for the entire group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: against - in opposition to someone or something
  • synonym: on behalf of - representing or speaking for someone else
