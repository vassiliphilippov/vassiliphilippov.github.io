Gullah

proper noun
ethnic group in the Lowcountry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌlə / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlə / (BrE)
members of an African American ethnic group living in the coastal Lowcountry of South Carolina and Georgia, known for their unique culture and language

Examples

The Gullah people
The Gullah have preserved many African traditions in their language and crafts.
Many tourists visit the Lowcountry to learn about Gullah culture and history.
The Gullah community is famous for its sweetgrass basket weaving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geechee - another name for the Gullah people or their descendants
