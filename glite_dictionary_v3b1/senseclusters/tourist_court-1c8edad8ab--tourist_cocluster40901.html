tourist court

noun
roadside hotel with direct room access
7.5 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtʊr.ɪst kɔːrt/ /; / /ˈtʊr.əst kɔːrt/ (AmE), /ˈtʊə.rɪst kɔːt/ /; / /ˈtɔː.rɪst kɔːt/ (BrE)
