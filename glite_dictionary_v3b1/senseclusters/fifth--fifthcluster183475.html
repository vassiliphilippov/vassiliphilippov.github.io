fifth

noun
liquor bottle size (1/5 US gallon)
Pronunciation: / fɪfθ / (AmE), / fɪfθ / (BrE)
A quantity of liquor equal to one fifth of a United States gallon, commonly used as a standard bottle size.

Examples

A fifth of whiskey
A fifth typically contains about 750 milliliters of liquor.
He bought a fifth of whiskey for the party.
The store sells fifths of vodka and rum on special.
