nontheoretical

adjective
practical, not theoretical
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnθiəˈrɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnθɪəˈrɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
based on practical experience or real situations rather than on theory or abstract ideas

Examples

Nontheoretical knowledge

Her nontheoretical approach to teaching helps students understand how concepts apply in real life.
We need nontheoretical solutions that address the actual problems people face every day.
