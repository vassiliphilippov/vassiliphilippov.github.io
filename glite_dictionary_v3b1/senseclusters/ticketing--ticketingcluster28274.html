ticketing

noun
issue tracking system
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the system or process of recording, managing, and tracking requests or issues using tickets, especially in customer support or IT services

Examples

Support ticketing
The company upgraded its ticketing platform to handle more customer requests efficiently.
Effective ticketing helps IT teams resolve technical issues faster and track progress.
Our ticketing system automatically assigns support requests to the appropriate staff member.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helpdesk - a service providing assistance and support for users
  • synonym: support system - a system for managing customer or technical support requests
