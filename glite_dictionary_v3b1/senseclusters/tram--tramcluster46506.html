tram

verb
to transport by tram
Pronunciation: / træm / (AmE), / træm / (BrE)
to move or transport something or someone using a tram or along a track

Examples

To tram equipment
The workers tram heavy equipment from the factory to the construction site every morning.
During the tour, visitors were trammed through the museum on a small electric vehicle.
Supplies are often trammed along the tracks to reach the remote mining camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carry - to move something from one place to another
  • synonym: convey - to transport or carry from one place to another
  • antonym: walk - to move by foot rather than by vehicle
