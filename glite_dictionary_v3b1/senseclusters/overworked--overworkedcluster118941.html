overworked

adjective
tired from too much work
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈwɜrkt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɜːkt / (BrE)
working too much and feeling tired or stressed because of excessive work

Examples

Overworked employee
After months without a vacation, she felt completely overworked and needed a break.
Many employees become overworked during busy seasons at the company.
He realized he was overworked when he started making frequent mistakes at his job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausted - extremely tired, especially from work
  • antonym: rested - having had enough rest and not tired
  • synonym: stressed - feeling mental or emotional strain
