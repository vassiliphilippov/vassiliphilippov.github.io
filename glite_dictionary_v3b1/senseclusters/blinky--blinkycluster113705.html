blinky

adjective
flashing repeatedly
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋki / (BrE)
blinking or flashing repeatedly, especially in a noticeable or distracting way

Examples

A blinky cursor
The blinky cursor on the screen made it hard to concentrate.
She covered the blinky lights on her router with tape so they would not disturb her at night.
A blinky toy caught the attention of the children in the dark room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flashing - shining or appearing suddenly and repeatedly
  • antonym: steady - not changing or moving; constant
