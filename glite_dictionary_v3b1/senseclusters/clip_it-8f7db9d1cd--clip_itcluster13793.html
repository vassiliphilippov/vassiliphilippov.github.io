clip it

verb
cut out or shorten a section; sometimes 'be quiet'
Pronunciation: / klɪp ɪt / (AmE), / klɪp ɪt / (BrE)
to cut out, remove, or shorten a section of something, often a video or document; sometimes used informally to mean 'be quiet' or 'stop talking'

Examples

To clip it short
If you think it's too long, just clip it down to the best moments.
She asked him to clip it before sending the video file.
When the conversation got out of hand, he told everyone to clip it and listen.
