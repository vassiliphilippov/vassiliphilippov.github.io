inkhorn

noun
pedantic person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɪŋk.hɔːrn/ /; / /ˈɪŋk.hɑːrn/ (AmE), /ˈɪŋk.hɔːn/ (BrE)

Examples

An inkhorn writer

Some readers find her style too inkhorn to be enjoyable or easy to understand.
