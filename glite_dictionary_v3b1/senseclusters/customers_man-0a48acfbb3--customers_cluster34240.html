customer's man

noun
managing clients' accounts in a service business
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəmərz mæn / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəməz mæn / (BrE)
a man employed by an advertising agency, brokerage, or similar service business to manage and maintain relationships with clients

Examples

A customer's man manages clients
After years as a customer's man, he knew how to anticipate his clients' needs.
Each brokerage firm assigned a customer's man to manage its biggest accounts.
The customer's man handled all communication between the agency and the client.
