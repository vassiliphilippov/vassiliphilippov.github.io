ere

preposition
before (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / ɛə / (BrE)
before a particular time or event, used in older or literary English

Examples

Ere the sun sets
He finished his work ere the sun had set.
The messenger arrived ere the battle began.
You must complete the task ere midnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: after - later than a particular time or event
  • synonym: before - earlier than a particular time or event
