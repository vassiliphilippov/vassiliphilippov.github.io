sheerness

noun
thin or transparent quality
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪrnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪənəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very thin, light, or almost transparent, especially in fabric or material

Examples

Sheerness of the fabric
The sheerness of her dress made it perfect for the hot summer day.
He admired the sheerness of the mountain stream's water as it flowed over the rocks.
Because of the sheerness of the fabric, a lining was added to the skirt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opacity - the quality of being difficult to see through
  • synonym: transparency - the quality of allowing light to pass through so that objects behind can be seen
