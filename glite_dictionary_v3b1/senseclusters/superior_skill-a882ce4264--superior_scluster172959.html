superior skill

noun
exceptionally advanced ability
Pronunciation: / suˈpɪriɚ skɪl / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriə skɪl / (BrE)
A level of ability or expertise that is much greater than what is typical or expected; exceptional talent or competence.

Examples

Superior skill level
Coaches often look for players with superior skill to strengthen their teams.
Her superior skill on the violin impressed the judges and audience alike.
The company's success can be attributed to the superior skill of its engineering team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expertise - advanced skill or knowledge in a particular field
  • antonym: incompetence - lack of ability or skill
  • synonym: talent - natural aptitude or skill
