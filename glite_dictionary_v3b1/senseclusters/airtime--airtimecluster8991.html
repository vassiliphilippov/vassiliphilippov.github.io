airtime

noun
time spent airborne
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈeətaɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time that a person, vehicle, or object spends in the air during a jump or flight

Examples

Get airtime jumping
The snowboarder achieved impressive airtime while performing tricks off the jump.
Pilots often compare their airtime after completing long flights.
The roller coaster gives riders a thrilling sense of airtime at the top of each hill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ground time - the amount of time spent on the ground between flights or jumps
  • synonym: hang time - the duration an object or person remains in the air
