outsideness

noun
state of not belonging
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.saɪd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.saɪd.nəs/ (BrE)
the state or feeling of being outside a group or not belonging to a community or social circle

Examples

Social outsideness
He often felt a sense of outsideness when he moved to a new city.
Her outsideness was clear at the party, where she knew no one.
The novel explores the outsideness experienced by immigrants in unfamiliar cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alienation - the feeling of being isolated or estranged from a group
  • antonym: belonging - the feeling of being accepted as part of a group
