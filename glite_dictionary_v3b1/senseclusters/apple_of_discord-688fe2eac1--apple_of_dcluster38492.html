apple of discord

noun
cause of dispute or conflict
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəl əv dɪsˌkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈæpəl əv dɪsˌkɔːd / (BrE)
an object or issue that causes disagreement or strife, especially one that triggers a dispute among people

Examples

The apple of discord
Her arrival at the party was the apple of discord that ruined the happy mood.
The inheritance became an apple of discord among the siblings, sparking years of legal battles.
The new policy proved to be an apple of discord within the committee, dividing members sharply.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bone of contention - an issue or subject that people argue about strongly
