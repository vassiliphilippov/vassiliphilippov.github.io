auxiliary pump

noun
supplementary backup pump
Pronunciation: / ɔɡˈzɪljəri pʌmp / (AmE), / ɔːɡˈzɪljəri pʌmp / (BrE)
a supplementary pump used as a backup or to assist the main pump when needed

Examples

A auxiliary pump backup
A second auxiliary pump was installed to increase system reliability.
Regular maintenance of the auxiliary pump ensures it works in emergencies.
The ship's auxiliary pump was activated after the main pump failed.
