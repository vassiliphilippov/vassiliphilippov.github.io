ammonium carbonate

noun
a white crystalline chemical compound
8.0 4.9
Pronunciation: / əˈmoʊniəm ˈkɑːrbəˌneɪt / (AmE), / əˈməʊniəm ˈkɑːbənət / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound (NH4)2CO3 used in smelling salts, baking powder, and in the manufacture of ammonium compounds.
