Synonyms

  • allotment
    4.6 0.2/m
    allotment is a planned share given by fixed rules, while allocation is the broader process of assigning resources for a purpose.
  • apportionment
    7.8 0.1/m
    apportionment divides resources among people according to rules, while allocation names the process or amount assigned for a particular purpose.
  • distribution
    3.2 3/m
    distribution is the physical or general act of spreading goods or items, while allocation specifies amounts or resources for a particular purpose.
  • assignment
    1.8 2/m
    assignment often means giving a task or item to someone, while allocation refers to assigning amounts or resources for a purpose.
  • appropriation
    6.4 3/m
    appropriation is an amount of money officially set aside for a purpose, while allocation is the general act or amount assigned.
