allocation

noun
distribution according to a plan
3.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /æl.ə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /æl.ə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of distributing or assigning resources, or the amount that is given for a particular purpose

Examples

Allocation of funds
Effective allocation of resources is crucial for organizational success.

Each department received its allocation of funds for the new project.
The water allocation for the region was reduced due to drought.
There was some debate about the fair allocation of resources among the different teams.
