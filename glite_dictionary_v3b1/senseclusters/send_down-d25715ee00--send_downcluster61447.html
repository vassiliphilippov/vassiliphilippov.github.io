send down

verb
temporarily suspend from college (UK)
8.4
Pronunciation: / sɛnd daʊn / (AmE), / sɛnd daʊn / (BrE)
(mainly British) To suspend or expel temporarily, especially a student from a college or university.

Examples

To send down a student
After the incident, she was sent down for one academic year.
If you break the rules again, the college may send down you for a term.
The university decided to send down the student for his repeated misconduct.
