round

noun
cut of beef from hindquarters
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
a cut of beef taken from the part of the hindquarters between the rump and the lower leg

Examples

Round steak
Round is often lean and works well for slow-cooked dishes.
At the butcher shop, I asked for two pounds of round for making stew.
The chef prepared a roast using a round cut for the holiday dinner.
