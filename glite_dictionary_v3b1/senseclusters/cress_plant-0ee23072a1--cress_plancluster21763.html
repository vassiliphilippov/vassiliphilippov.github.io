cress plant

noun
edible, pungent plant in the mustard family
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɛs plænt / (AmE), / krɛs plɑːnt / (BrE)
any of several plants in the mustard family (Cruciferae) with edible, pungent-tasting leaves, often used in salads or as garnish
