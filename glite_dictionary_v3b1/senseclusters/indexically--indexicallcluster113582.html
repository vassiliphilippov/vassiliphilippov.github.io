indexically

adverb
by an index
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is linked to or adjusted according to a numeric index or benchmark, such as inflation or a price index

Examples

Indexically adjusted payments
The contract states that payments will increase indexically with the cost of living each year.
Some pensions are adjusted indexically to protect retirees from inflation.
The value of the bond rises indexically as the consumer price index goes up.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixedly - in a way that does not change or adjust
  • synonym: proportionally - in a way that corresponds in size or amount to something else
