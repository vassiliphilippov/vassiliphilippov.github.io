Chrysolophus pictus

noun
a brightly colored Asian pheasant (golden pheasant)
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɪsəˈlɑfəs ˈpɪktəs / (AmE), / krɪsəˈlɒfəs ˈpɪktəs / (BrE)
A brightly colored crested pheasant, commonly known as the golden pheasant, native to the mountains of western and central Asia.
