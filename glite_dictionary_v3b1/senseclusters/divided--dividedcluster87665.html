divided

adjective
having lanes separated by a median (of a road)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪdɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of a road or highway) having a central strip or barrier separating lanes for traffic moving in opposite directions.

Examples

Divided highway
A divided boulevard runs through the center of town, separating the traffic directions.
It's much safer to drive on a divided road during rush hour.
The city recently expanded the divided highway to improve traffic flow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undivided - (of a road) without a median strip; all lanes together
