en garde

adjective
in a defensive stance (fencing)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ ˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / ɒ̃ ˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
used to warn a fencer to adopt a defensive stance; in fencing, in a position ready to fence or defend oneself

Examples

En garde position
Fencers must quickly assume the en garde position at the start of each bout.
She stood en garde, anticipating her opponent's next move.
The referee shouted 'en garde!' and the match began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on guard - in a state of readiness to defend oneself or respond to danger
