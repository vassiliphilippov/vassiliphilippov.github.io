hitman

noun
a male hired killer
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɪtmæn / (BrE)
a man who is hired to kill someone, especially with a gun, for payment

Examples

A hitman with a gun
Rumors spread that a hitman had been hired to eliminate the rival boss.
The movie follows a retired hitman trying to escape his violent past.
The notorious hitman was finally captured by the police after years on the run.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone important for political or religious reasons
  • synonym: killer - a person who kills another
