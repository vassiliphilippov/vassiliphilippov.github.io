FitnessGram

proper noun
school fitness program
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪtnɪsˌɡræm /; / ˈfɪtnəsˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈfɪtnɪsˌɡræm /; / ˈfɪtnəsˌɡræm / (BrE)
a fitness assessment program used in schools to measure students' physical health and fitness levels
