snippy

adjective
curt or rude
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪpi / (AmE), / ˈsnɪpi / (BrE)
speaking or behaving in a short, rude, or curt manner

Examples

A snippy answer
He became snippy when asked about the missed deadline.
Her snippy comments made the meeting uncomfortable for everyone.
Try not to be snippy with your friends when you are stressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curt - rudely brief in speech or manner
  • antonym: polite - showing good manners and respect
