groundout

verb
to hit ground ball out
8.3
Pronunciation: /graʊnd.ˈaʊt/ /; / /graʊn.ˈdaʊt/ (AmE), /graʊnd.ˈaʊt/ /; / /graʊn.ˈdaʊt/ (BrE)
to hit a ground ball in baseball that results in the batter or a runner being put out

Examples

To groundout to first base

She managed not to groundout and instead hit a single to left field.
If you groundout to shortstop, the inning could end quickly.
