Old English poetry

noun
poetry in the Old English language
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈpoʊətri / (AmE), / əʊld ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈpəʊɪtri / (BrE)
poetry composed in the Old English language, typically dating from the 7th to the 11th centuries, including works such as Beowulf and The Seafarer.
