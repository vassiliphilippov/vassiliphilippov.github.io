Baal

proper noun
ancient Semitic fertility deity
9.9 0.71/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ël / (AmE), / Ààb…ëÀêl / (BrE)
A title or name for various local fertility and nature deities worshipped by ancient Semitic peoples, considered a false god by the Hebrews.

Examples

Worship of Baal
Many ancient texts describe the worship of Baal throughout the Near East.
Modern archaeologists have uncovered temples dedicated to Baal in several ancient cities.
The Hebrews regarded Baal as a rival to their own god.
