feldspar

noun
a group of common rock-forming minerals
7.7 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfeld.spɑːr/ /; / /ˈfel.spɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈfeld.spɑː/ /; / /ˈfel.spɑː/ (BrE)
any of a group of hard crystalline minerals consisting of aluminum silicates of potassium, sodium, calcium, or barium, commonly found in igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks
