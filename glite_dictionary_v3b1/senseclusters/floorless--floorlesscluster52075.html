floorless

adjective
not full length
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈflɔːr.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈflɔː.ləs/ (BrE)
not reaching down to the floor; shorter than full length, especially of a garment or curtain

Examples

Floorless dress
The designer created a floorless gown to showcase the model's shoes on the runway.
They preferred floorless curtains in the living room for a more modern look.
Her floorless dress was perfect for dancing without tripping over the hem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full-length - reaching down to the floor or ankles
  • synonym: short - not long; having little length
