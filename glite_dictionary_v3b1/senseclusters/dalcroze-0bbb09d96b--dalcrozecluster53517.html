Dalcroze

adjective
music education method
Pronunciation: / dælˈkroʊz / (AmE), / dælˈkrəʊz / (BrE)
relating to the Dalcroze method of music education, which uses movement to teach rhythm and musical expression

Examples

Dalcroze exercises
The school introduced Dalcroze activities to help children feel rhythm through movement.
Her teaching style is strongly influenced by Dalcroze principles, focusing on body movement and musicality.
Many music educators attend workshops on Dalcroze techniques to enhance their classroom methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kodály - relating to the Kodály method of music education
  • synonym: Orff - relating to the Orff approach to music education
