spinney

noun
small area of trees; copse
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪni / (AmE), / ˈspɪni / (BrE)
A small area of trees and undergrowth; a copse, especially one that shelters game.

Examples

A spinney of trees
A narrow path wound its way through the shady spinney.
The rabbits disappeared into the dense spinney at the edge of the field.
They walked through the spinney and listened to birds singing in the branches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copse - a small group of trees
