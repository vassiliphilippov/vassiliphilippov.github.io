marshall

noun
highest military rank in some countries
8.0 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀêr É…ôl / (AmE), / Ààm…ëÀê É…ôl / (BrE)
(in some countries) a military officer of the highest rank, often responsible for commanding an entire branch of the armed forces or serving as a senior advisor.

Examples

Air marshall
After years of distinguished service, he was appointed marshall of the air force.
In the parade, the new marshall inspected the troops with great ceremony.
The title of marshall is rarely bestowed and is reserved for the most senior military leaders.
