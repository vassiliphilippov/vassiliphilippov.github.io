bathe

verb
to swim for enjoyment
5.2
Pronunciation: / beɪð / (AmE), / beɪð / (BrE)
to swim or immerse oneself in water for pleasure or recreation

Examples

To bathe in a lake
He bathed in the lake every morning during his holiday in the countryside.
On hot summer days, people often bathe in the river to cool off.
Tourists love to bathe at the secluded beach along the coast.
