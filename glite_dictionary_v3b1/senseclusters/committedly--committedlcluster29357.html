committedly

adverb
with strong dedication
6.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪtɪdli / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows strong dedication or loyalty to a cause, task, or person

Examples

To commit fully

The team worked committedly to finish the project before the deadline.
He pursued his studies committedly, determined to achieve the highest grades.
