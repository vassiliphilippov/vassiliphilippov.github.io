endorsement

noun
validating signature on a document
4.7 4.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɔrsmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɔːsmənt / (BrE)
a signature, especially on a document such as a check, that makes it valid or authorizes its negotiation

Examples

Check endorsement
Before depositing the check, she added her endorsement on the back.
The bank returned my check because it was missing an endorsement.
Without the required endorsement, the lottery winnings could not be claimed.
