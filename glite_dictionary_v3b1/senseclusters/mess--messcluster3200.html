mess

noun
portion of semiliquid food
9.3
Pronunciation: / mɛs / (AmE), / mɛs / (BrE)
a portion or dish of soft or semiliquid food, often prepared for one meal

Examples

A mess of food
A simple mess of rice and beans was all they had for dinner.
He served a steaming mess of oatmeal for breakfast.
They ate a mess of stew around the campfire.

Related Terms
