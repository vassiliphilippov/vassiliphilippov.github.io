puckish

adjective
playfully mischievous; impish
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpʌkɪʃ / (BrE)
having or showing a playful or mischievous sense of humor; naughtily or teasingly playful.

Examples

A puckish grin
He flashed a puckish grin before slipping away unnoticed.
Her puckish comments kept the meeting light and lively.
With a puckish twinkle in his eye, he hid her keys as a joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impish - mischievously playful or naughty
  • synonym: mischievous - causing or showing a fondness for causing trouble in a playful way
