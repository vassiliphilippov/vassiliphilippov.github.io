deck

noun
set of presentation slides
Pronunciation: / dɛk / (AmE), / dɛk / (BrE)
A set of presentation slides prepared for a talk, meeting, or pitch.

Examples

Presentation deck
She shared the deck with the team before the investor pitch.
He spent the weekend refining his deck to make the data easier for clients.
Before the meeting she printed two copies of each deck for the visiting executives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presentation - a talk or demonstration given to an audience, often using slides
  • synonym: slides - individual pages or images in a presentation; often called 'slides' informally
