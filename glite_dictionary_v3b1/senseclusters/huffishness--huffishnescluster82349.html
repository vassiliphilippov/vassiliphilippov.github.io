huffishness

noun
state of sulky resentment
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈhʌfɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
A feeling or quality of being sulky, irritable, or resentfully withdrawn.

Examples

Quiet huffishness
After not being invited, he displayed a quiet huffishness for the rest of the evening.
Her persistent huffishness made it difficult to resolve even minor disagreements.
There was a noticeable huffishness in his reply, suggesting he was offended by the criticism.
