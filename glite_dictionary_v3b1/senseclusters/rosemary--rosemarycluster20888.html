rosemary

noun
aromatic herb used in cooking
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzmɛri / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzməri / (BrE)
an aromatic evergreen shrub with narrow, needle-like leaves, used as a herb in cooking and for its fragrance

Examples

Fresh rosemary leaves
A sprig of rosemary can enhance the flavor of grilled chicken.
Dried rosemary is often found in Mediterranean recipes.
She added fresh rosemary to the lamb before roasting it.
A potted rosemary plant grows well on a sunny windowsill.
She sprinkled fresh rosemary on the roasted potatoes for extra flavor.
The scent of rosemary filled the kitchen while dinner was being prepared.
