Storm Troops

noun
Nazi paramilitary group (SA) active 1921-1943
9.0
Pronunciation: /stɔːrm truːps/ /; / /stɑːrm truːps/ (AmE), /stɔːm truːps/ (BrE)
A Nazi paramilitary organization, officially known as the Sturmabteilung (SA), created by Hitler in 1921 to support his rise to power, later overtaken by the SS after 1943.

Examples

The Storm Troops group
During the 1930s, the Storm Troops were notorious for their violent tactics against political opponents.
Many members of the Storm Troops were later purged during the Night of the Long Knives.
The Storm Troops played a major role in Hitler's rise to power before being marginalized by the SS.
