hilar

adjective
relating to a hilum (anatomy, botany)
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.lɑr / (AmE), / ˈhɪ.lɑː / (BrE)
of, relating to, or situated near a hilum, especially in the context of anatomy or botany.

Examples

Hilar lymph nodes
He noted hilar lymphadenopathy on the patient's chest X-ray.
The hilar area of the kidney contains the renal artery, vein, and pelvis.
The tumor was located at the hilar region of the lung.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hilum - a small indentation in an organ where structures such as blood vessels or nerves enter or leave
