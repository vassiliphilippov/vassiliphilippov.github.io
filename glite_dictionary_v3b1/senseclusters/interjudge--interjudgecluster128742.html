interjudge

verb
to judge as a group
7.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈdʒʌdʒ/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈdʒʌdʒ/ (BrE)
to evaluate or make a decision together with other judges as part of a group process

Examples

To interjudge results

Experts from different fields were invited to interjudge the science competition entries.
To avoid bias, the awards were interjudged by a diverse panel.
