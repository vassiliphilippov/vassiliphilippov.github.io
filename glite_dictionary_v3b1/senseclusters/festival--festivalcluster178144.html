festival

noun
organized performance event
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛs.tə.vəl / (AmE), / ˈfɛs.tɪ.vəl / (BrE)
an organized series of acts and performances, often held in one place, usually to celebrate a particular art, theme, or occasion

Examples

A music festival
Every summer, the city hosts a popular jazz festival that attracts visitors from around the world.
The arts festival features theater, dance, and visual exhibitions throughout the week.
Tickets for the literary festival sold out within days of being announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnival - a public festival or celebration, often involving music, dancing, and costumes
  • synonym: concert - a musical performance given in public, often as part of a festival
