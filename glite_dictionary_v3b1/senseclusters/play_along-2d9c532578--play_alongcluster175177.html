play along

verb
accompany musically
Pronunciation: / pleɪ əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / pleɪ əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to perform music as an accompaniment to another performer or group

Examples

To play along with music
At the recital, the pianist will play along while the children sing.
She enjoyed playing along to her favorite songs on the violin.
The guitarist likes to play along with recorded tracks at home.
