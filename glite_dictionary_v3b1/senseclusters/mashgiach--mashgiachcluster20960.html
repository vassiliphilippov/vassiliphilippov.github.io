mashgiach

noun
Jewish spiritual supervisor
Pronunciation: / mɑʃˈɡiːəx / (AmE), / mɑʃˈɡiːəx / (BrE)
a spiritual supervisor or moral guide in a Jewish school or yeshiva, responsible for students' religious and ethical development

Examples

Yeshiva mashgiach
The mashgiach offered guidance to students struggling with personal challenges at the yeshiva.
Every morning, the mashgiach spoke to the class about ethical behavior and responsibility.
Parents appreciated the mashgiach's dedication to the students' spiritual growth.
