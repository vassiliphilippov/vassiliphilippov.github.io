storyless

adjective
single-level building
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriləs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriləs / (BrE)
having only one level or floor; not containing multiple stories or floors

Examples

Storyless building
The new library is a storyless structure designed for easy access.
They preferred a storyless house because it was safer for their young children.
A storyless warehouse was built to store the heavy equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: multi-storey - having more than one floor or level
  • synonym: single-storey - having only one floor or level
