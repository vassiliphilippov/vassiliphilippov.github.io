navigable

adjective
easy to navigate (interfaces)
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnævɪɡəbəl / (AmE), / ˈnævɪɡəbl / (BrE)
(of a website, app, document, or other information structure) easy to move through or find information in; arranged so users can find what they need.

Examples

Navigable website
The app's menu is navigable, making it simple to find features.
Designers tested whether the website remained navigable on smaller screens and slow connections.
After the redesign, the site became much more navigable for first-time visitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confusing - not clear or easy to understand; difficult to find information in
  • synonym: user-friendly - designed so people can use it easily and without difficulty
