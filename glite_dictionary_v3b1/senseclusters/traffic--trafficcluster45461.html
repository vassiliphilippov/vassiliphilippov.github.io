traffic

noun
the movement of vehicles or people
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtræ.fɪk/ (AmE), /ˈtræ.fɪk/ (BrE)
the movement of vehicles or people along roads, streets, or other routes, especially the amount or flow at a particular time or place

Examples

Heavy traffic
During rush hour, traffic moves very slowly through the downtown area.
Heavy traffic delayed my arrival at the airport this morning.
The city has installed cameras to monitor traffic and improve safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flow - steady movement of traffic or other things
  • synonym: jam - a situation in which traffic cannot move forward due to overcrowding
