honey plant

noun
plant that produces nectar for honey
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌni plænt / (AmE), / ˈhʌni plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that produces nectar suitable for bees to make honey

Examples

A honey plant field
Beekeepers often plant fields of clover, a well-known honey plant, to help their hives thrive.
Lavender is a popular honey plant that attracts many bees in the summer.
The garden was full of honey plants, making it an ideal spot for local pollinators.
