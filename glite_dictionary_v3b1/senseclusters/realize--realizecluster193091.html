realize

verb
supply harmonies in figured bass (music)
Pronunciation: / ˈriːəˌlaɪz /; / ˈriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɪəlaɪz /; / ˈrɪə.laɪz / (BrE)
(in baroque music) To expand or complete a part by supplying the harmonies indicated in the figured bass.

Examples

Scholars often debate how best to realise the harmonies in early baroque works.
The continuo player was asked to realise the figured bass for tonight's performance.
