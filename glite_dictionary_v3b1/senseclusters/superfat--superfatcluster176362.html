superfat

verb
to add extra fat
8.9
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈfæt/ /; / /ˈsuː.pər.fæt/ (AmE), /sjuː.pə.ˈfæt/ /; / /ˈsjuː.pə.fæt/ (BrE)
to add extra fat or oil to a soap or cosmetic mixture so that some remains unsaponified, making the product more moisturizing

Examples

To superfat a recipe
Many soap makers choose to superfat their recipes to create a gentler product for sensitive skin.
If you superfat the mixture, the soap will be more moisturizing but may feel softer.
She learned how to superfat her homemade cosmetics for better skin benefits.
