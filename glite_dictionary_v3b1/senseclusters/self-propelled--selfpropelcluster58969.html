self-propelled

adjective
able to move by its own power
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld / (BrE)
able to move by itself using its own power, without being pushed or pulled

Examples

During the demonstration, the self-propelled artillery impressed the military officials.

Many modern lawn mowers are self-propelled, making them easier to use on large lawns.
The army deployed several self-propelled artillery units to the front lines.
The museum features a collection of early self-propelled vehicles and locomotives.
