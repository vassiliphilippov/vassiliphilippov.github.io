resignation

noun
formal act of giving up a job or position
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of officially leaving a job or position, or the written statement saying you are leaving

Examples

Submit a resignation
His sudden resignation surprised everyone in the office.
She handed in her resignation after much consideration.
The CEO's resignation led to significant changes at the company.
After much thought, he handed in his resignation to the company president.
His manager reluctantly accepted his resignation after years of dedicated service.
The board reviewed her resignation and appointed a new director.
