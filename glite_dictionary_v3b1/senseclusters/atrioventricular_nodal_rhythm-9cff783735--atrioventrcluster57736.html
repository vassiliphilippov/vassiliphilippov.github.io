atrioventricular nodal rhythm

noun
heart rhythm from AV node
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪtrioʊvɛnˌtrɪkjələr ˈnoʊdəl ˈrɪðəm / (AmE), / ˌeɪtrɪəʊvɛnˌtrɪkjʊlə ˈnəʊdəl ˈrɪðəm / (BrE)
a cardiac rhythm in which the heart is controlled by the atrioventricular node instead of the sinoatrial node

Examples

Atrioventricular nodal rhythm pattern
In cases of sinoatrial node failure, an atrioventricular nodal rhythm may take over.
The ECG showed an atrioventricular nodal rhythm instead of the expected sinus rhythm.
The patient developed an atrioventricular nodal rhythm after cardiac surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sinus rhythm - the normal rhythm of the heart originating from the sinoatrial node
