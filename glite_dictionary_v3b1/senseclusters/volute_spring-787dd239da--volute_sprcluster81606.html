volute spring

noun
a spring shaped like a coil
8.3
Pronunciation: / vəˈluːt ˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / vəˈluːt ˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
a mechanical spring formed in the shape of a spiral or coil, often used to absorb shock or store energy

Examples

A volute spring coil
He replaced the damaged volute spring with a new one designed for heavier loads.
Many trains use a volute spring under their buffers for shock absorption.
