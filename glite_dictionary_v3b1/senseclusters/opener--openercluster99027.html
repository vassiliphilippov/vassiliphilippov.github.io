(in baseball) a relief-style pitcher who begins the game and typically pitches only the first one or two innings before a conventional starter takes over.

Examples

Baseball opener
Before the big game, the manager announced his opener, surprising many fans.
The young opener threw two perfect innings before the regular starter entered the game.
Several teams have used openers this season to disrupt traditional pitching plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relief pitcher - a pitcher who enters the game after the starter; openers are often relief pitchers used to start a game
  • antonym: starting pitcher - the pitcher who traditionally begins the game and is expected to pitch multiple innings; contrasted with an opener
