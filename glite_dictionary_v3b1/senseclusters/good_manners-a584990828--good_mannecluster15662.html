good manners

noun
polite or courteous behavior
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈmænərz / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈmænəz / (BrE)
Polite and considerate behavior toward others; courtesy.

Examples

Good manners at table
Children are taught to show good manners at the dinner table.
Demonstrating good manners is important in both social and professional situations.
His good manners made a lasting impression on everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bad manners - impolite or rude behavior
  • synonym: politeness - the quality of being polite and respectful
