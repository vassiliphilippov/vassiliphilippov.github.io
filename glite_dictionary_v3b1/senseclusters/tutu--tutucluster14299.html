tutu

noun
short skirt worn by ballerinas
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːtuː / (AmE), / ˈtuːtuː / (BrE)
A very short, frilly skirt made of several layers of tulle, worn by ballerinas as part of their costume.

Examples

A tutu skirt
She carefully adjusted her tutu before the rehearsal began.
The ballerina wore a sparkling pink tutu for her performance.
Young dancers lined up in white tutus, ready to go on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballet skirt - a skirt worn in ballet, usually referring to the tutu or similar styles
