nin-sin

noun
Chinese medicinal herb (ginseng)
9.7
Pronunciation: / nɪn sɪn / (AmE), / nɪn sɪn / (BrE)
A Chinese herb with palmately compound leaves, small greenish flowers, and forked aromatic roots thought to have medicinal properties.

Examples

Nin-sin root
Nin-sin has been prized in Chinese medicine for hundreds of years.
Many herbal remedies include nin-sin for its reputed health benefits.
The traditional healer recommended a tea made from nin-sin roots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ginseng - a medicinal root widely used in Asia and herbal medicine
