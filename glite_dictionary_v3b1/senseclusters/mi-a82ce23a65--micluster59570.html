MI

noun
medical abbreviation for heart attack
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛmˈaɪ / (BrE)
A medical abbreviation for myocardial infarction, the destruction of heart tissue resulting from obstruction of the blood supply to the heart muscle (commonly called a heart attack).

Examples

MI diagnosis
Prompt treatment can greatly improve the chances of recovery after an MI.
The doctor confirmed that the patient had suffered an MI during the night.
The hospital tracks all cases of MI in its cardiac care unit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heart attack - sudden loss of blood supply to part of the heart
  • synonym: myocardial infarction - destruction of heart tissue caused by blocked blood supply
