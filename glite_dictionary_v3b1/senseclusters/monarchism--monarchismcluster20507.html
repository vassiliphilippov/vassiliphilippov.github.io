monarchism

noun
belief in monarchy as a political system
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnərkɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəkɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in and advocacy of monarchy as a political system

Examples

Monarchism belief
He studied the rise and fall of monarchism throughout European history.
Some conservatives in the 19th century strongly supported monarchism over republican ideas.
The revival of monarchism in Europe surprised many political analysts.

Related Terms
