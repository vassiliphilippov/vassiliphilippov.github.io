pasta salad

noun
cold salad with pasta as main ingredient
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑstə səˈlɑd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstə səˈlɑːd / (BrE)
A cold salad made with cooked pasta, often combined with vegetables, cheese, meat, or dressing.

Examples

A pasta salad dish
He prefers his pasta salad with a creamy Italian dressing.
She brought a bowl of pasta salad to the picnic.
The pasta salad was made with fusilli, olives, and cherry tomatoes.
