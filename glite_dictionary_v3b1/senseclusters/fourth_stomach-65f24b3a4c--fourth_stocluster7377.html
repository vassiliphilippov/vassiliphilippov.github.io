fourth stomach

noun
the final compartment of a ruminant's stomach
Pronunciation: / fɔrθ ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / fɔːθ ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
the fourth compartment of the stomach in a ruminant animal, where the main digestion of food occurs

Examples

The fourth stomach compartment
Rennin is an enzyme extracted from the fourth stomach of young calves.
The fourth stomach of a cow, known as the abomasum, plays a key role in digestion.
Veterinary students studied the structure of the fourth stomach in sheep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abomasum - the true stomach of ruminants corresponding to the fourth compartment
