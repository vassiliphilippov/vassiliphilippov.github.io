mania

noun
an intense obsession or enthusiasm
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
An intense, often irrational enthusiasm or obsession for something, especially one that is overwhelming or irresistible.

Examples

Shopping mania
His collecting mania led him to fill every room with rare coins and stamps.
Many people develop a mania for fitness and spend hours at the gym every day.
The sudden mania for fidget spinners swept through schools across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsession - an idea or thought that continually preoccupies or intrudes on a person's mind
