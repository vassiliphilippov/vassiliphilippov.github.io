Congo

proper noun
country in west-central Africa
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A republic in west-central Africa, officially known as the Republic of the Congo, which gained independence from France in 1960.

Examples

The Republic of Congo
After achieving independence in 1960, Congo has experienced significant political changes.
Many international organizations provide aid to rural areas in Congo.
The president of Congo delivered a speech at the United Nations yesterday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Democratic Republic of the Congo - a neighboring, larger country in central Africa, also sometimes referred to as 'Congo'
