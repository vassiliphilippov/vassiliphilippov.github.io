epithelium

noun
membranous tissue lining organs and surfaces
4.4 7.2
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.ˈθiː.li.əm/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.ˈθiː.li.əm/ (BrE)
Membranous tissue forming the outer layer of body surfaces and lining internal organs and cavities.

Examples

The epithelium layer
Damage to the intestinal epithelium can impair nutrient absorption.
Scientists observed abnormalities in the epithelium of the lungs during their study.
The epithelium acts as a barrier to protect underlying tissues from infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endothelium - thin layer of cells lining the interior of blood vessels and lymphatic vessels
