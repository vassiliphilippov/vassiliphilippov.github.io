chub

verb
to become chubby
9.1
Pronunciation: / tʃʌb / (AmE), / tʃʌb / (BrE)
to become or make chubby or plump, especially by gaining weight.

Examples

To chub up
After the holidays, he realized he had chubbed up a little from all the treats.
She chubbed out during her first year at college because of late-night snacks.
The puppy quickly chubbed up after being adopted into a loving home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slim - to become thinner
