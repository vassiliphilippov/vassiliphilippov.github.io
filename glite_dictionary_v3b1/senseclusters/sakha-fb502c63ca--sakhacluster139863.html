Sakha

proper noun
Russian republic in Siberia
9.5
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈkɑː / (AmE), / sɑːˈkɑː / (BrE)
a republic in northeastern Siberia, Russia, also known as Yakutia, known for its vast size and cold climate

Examples

Sakha Republic
The Lena River flows through the heart of Sakha, shaping its unique landscape.
Winters in Sakha are among the coldest on Earth, with temperatures dropping far below freezing.
Many indigenous peoples, including the Sakha, live in the vast territory of Sakha.
