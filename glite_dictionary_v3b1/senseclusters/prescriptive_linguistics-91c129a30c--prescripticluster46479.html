prescriptive linguistics

noun
rules-focused branch of linguistics
7.8 7.2
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈskrɪp.tɪv lɪŋ.ˈgwɪs.tɪks/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈskrɪp.tɪv lɪŋ.ˈgwɪs.tɪks/ (BrE)
the branch of linguistics concerned with establishing rules for how a language should be used, prescribing 'correct' usage rather than describing actual use

Examples
