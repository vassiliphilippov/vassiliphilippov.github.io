kickoff

noun
the starting kick in football
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɪkɒf / (BrE)
(in football) a kick from the center of the field that starts or resumes a game after a score.

Examples

Football kickoff
After the home team scored, the visiting team took the kickoff.
The game will begin with a kickoff at 3 PM sharp.
We arrived just in time to see the opening kickoff of the match.
