tin man

noun
unemotional person
8.1
Pronunciation: /tɪn mæn/ (AmE), /tɪn mæn/ (BrE)
a person who appears unemotional or lacking in compassion, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

A cold tin man
After years in the hard business, he had become something of a tin man to his friends.
People called her a tin man because she rarely showed her feelings at work.
Despite his reputation as a tin man, he cared deeply about his family.
