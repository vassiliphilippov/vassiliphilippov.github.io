tiltable

adjective
can be angled
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪltəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪltəbəl / (BrE)
able to be moved or adjusted so that it is at an angle or slanted position

Examples

A tiltable monitor
The tiltable monitor helps reduce glare from overhead lights.
She bought a tiltable mirror for her bathroom to make it easier to use.
Many modern cameras come with a tiltable screen for flexible shooting angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustable - able to be changed or moved to fit different needs
  • antonym: fixed - not able to be moved or changed
