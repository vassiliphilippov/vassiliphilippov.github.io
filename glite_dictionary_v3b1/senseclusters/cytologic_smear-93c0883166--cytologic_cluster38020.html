cytologic smear

noun
thin sample on slide for cell diagnosis
6.1
Pronunciation: / saɪ.təˈlɑ.dʒɪk smɪr / (AmE), / saɪ.təˈlɒ.dʒɪk smɪə / (BrE)
a thin sample of tissue or blood spread on a glass slide and stained for microscopic examination of cells, often used for diagnosis

Examples

A cytologic smear sample
A cytologic smear can help detect abnormal cells in tissue samples.
Routine screening often includes a cytologic smear for early signs of disease.
The doctor prepared a cytologic smear to examine the patient's blood cells under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pap smear - a cytologic smear of the cervix for cancer screening
