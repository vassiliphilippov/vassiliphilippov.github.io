wrapper

noun
covering material for wrapping items
Pronunciation: / ˈræpər / (AmE), / ˈræpə / (BrE)
a piece of paper, cellophane, or other material used to cover or enclose something, especially food or small items

Examples

Candy wrapper
He threw the candy wrapper in the bin after finishing the sweet.
Please remove the wrapper before eating the chocolate.
The sandwich was still fresh inside its plastic wrapper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover - something that lies on, over, or around something else
  • antonym: unwrapped - not covered with a wrapper or not enclosed
