ere

adverb
before in time
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / ɛə / (BrE)
before a particular time or event, especially in literary or archaic contexts.

Examples

Ere the bell rings
She had scarcely entered the room, ere the phone began to ring loudly.
The sun had not risen ere the travelers set out on their journey.
Finish your work ere the clock strikes midnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: after - at a later time than something
  • synonym: before - at an earlier time than something
