Senegambian

noun
person from Senegambia
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛnɪˈɡæmbiən / (AmE), / ˌsɛnɪˈɡæmbiən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the Senegambia region, which includes Senegal and the Gambia

Examples

A Senegambian person
A Senegambian cook taught us to make the traditional peanut stew from his village.
The museum features art created by Senegambian artists from both Senegal and the Gambia.
Many Senegambians celebrate cultural festivals that reflect the region's rich history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gambian - a person from the Gambia
  • synonym: Senegalese - a person from Senegal
