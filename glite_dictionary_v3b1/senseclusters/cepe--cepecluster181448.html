cepe

noun
edible wild mushroom
Pronunciation: / seɪp / (AmE), / seɪp / (BrE)
a large, edible wild mushroom, especially the species Boletus edulis, also known as porcini

Examples

Sliced cepe mushroom
The chef added sliced cepe to the risotto for extra flavor.
Fresh cepe mushrooms are highly prized in French cuisine during the autumn season.
We found a large cepe while foraging in the forest after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chanterelle - a type of edible wild mushroom with a yellow color
  • synonym: porcini - an edible wild mushroom, Boletus edulis
  • antonym: toadstool - a poisonous or inedible mushroom
