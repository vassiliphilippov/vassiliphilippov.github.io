closeted

adjective
kept secret
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːzətɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɒzɪtɪd / (BrE)
kept secret or hidden from public knowledge, especially regarding meetings, information, or actions

Examples

Closeted meeting
The committee held a closeted discussion to avoid public scrutiny.
Important decisions were made during a closeted session that was not announced to the press.
She remained closeted about her plans until everything was finalized.
