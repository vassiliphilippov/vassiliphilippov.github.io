tailgate

noun
gate at rear of a vehicle
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈteɪlɡeɪt / (BrE)
a hinged gate at the back of a vehicle, such as a truck, that can be lowered for loading or unloading

Examples

Truck tailgate
After the picnic, they cleaned off the muddy tailgate before heading home.
He leaned against the tailgate and watched the scenery go by.
The truck's tailgate was lowered to unload the heavy boxes.
