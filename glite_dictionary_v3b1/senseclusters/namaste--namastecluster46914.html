namaste

interjection
respectful greeting
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːməsteɪ / (AmE), / ˈnʌməsteɪ / (BrE)
a respectful greeting or farewell, often used with a slight bow and hands pressed together, meaning 'hello' or 'goodbye'

Examples

Say namaste politely
At the end of the yoga class, the instructor said namaste to everyone.
She greeted her elders with a polite namaste when she entered the room.
Before leaving the gathering, he smiled and said namaste to his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodbye - a word used when parting from someone
  • synonym: hello - a common greeting used when meeting someone
