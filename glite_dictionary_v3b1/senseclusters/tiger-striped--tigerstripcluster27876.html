tiger-striped

adjective
having tiger-like stripes
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡɚ ˌstraɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə ˌstraɪpt / (BrE)
Having stripes resembling those of a tiger, especially orange with black or dark stripes.

Examples

Tiger-striped pattern

The tiger-striped blanket added a wild touch to the living room décor.
The tiger-striped butterfly was easy to spot among the flowers.
