bedaubed

adjective
decorated in a garish or vulgar way
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdɔbd / (AmE), / bɪˈdɔːbd / (BrE)
Excessively or tastelessly ornamented; decorated in a vulgar or showy manner.

Examples

Bedaubed with jewelry
She arrived at the party bedaubed in cheap jewelry and flashy clothes.
The artist's new work is criticized as bedaubed rather than refined.
The walls were bedaubed with garish colors and glittering trinkets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: understated - presented or expressed in a subtle and effective way
