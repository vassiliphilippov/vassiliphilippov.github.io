mudding

noun
driving in mud
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of driving vehicles through muddy areas for enjoyment or sport

Examples

Off-road mudding
After the heavy rain, the group went mudding in their four-wheel-drive trucks.
He bought new tires specifically for mudding on the weekends with his friends.
Many people enjoy mudding as an adventurous outdoor hobby in rural areas.
