breastful

noun
full gulp (archaic)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstfʊl / (BrE)
an amount of liquid that fills the chest or lungs in a single breath or gulp (archaic)

Examples

A breastful of air
She drank a deep breastful of water and wiped her lips before speaking.
After running up the hill, he paused to take a breastful of fresh air.
The diver surfaced, gasping for a breastful before diving again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gulp - a large and hurried swallow
  • synonym: mouthful - an amount that fills the mouth
