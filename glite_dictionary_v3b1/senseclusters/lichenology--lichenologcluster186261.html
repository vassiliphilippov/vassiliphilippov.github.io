lichenology

noun
study of lichens
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪkəˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌlɪkɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of lichens, which are symbiotic organisms made of fungi and algae or cyanobacteria

Examples

Lichenology study
She became fascinated with lichenology after discovering colorful lichens on a hiking trip.
A career in lichenology often involves both fieldwork and laboratory research.
The university offers a specialized course in lichenology for biology students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mycology - the scientific study of fungi
