mammee tree

noun
tree with edible tropical fruit
Pronunciation: / mæˈmiː triː / (AmE), / mæˈmiː triː / (BrE)
A tropical American tree (Mammea americana) that bears an edible fruit with a leathery rind.

Examples

A mammee tree fruit
Farmers planted a mammee tree in the orchard to diversify their crops.
The mammee tree produces large, round fruits with sweet flesh.
The leathery rind of the mammee tree's fruit protects it from pests.
