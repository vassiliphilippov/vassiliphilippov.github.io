mounting

noun
supporting frame or structure
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a frame or structure on which something is supported, fixed, or displayed, especially for exhibition or use

Examples

Mounting for a picture
A proper mounting is essential to safely display rare coins in a collection.
The jewel’s delicate mounting highlighted the brilliance of the diamond.
The painting was placed in an elegant mounting before being hung in the gallery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frame - a rigid structure that surrounds or encloses something
  • synonym: setting - the mounting of a gem in jewelry
