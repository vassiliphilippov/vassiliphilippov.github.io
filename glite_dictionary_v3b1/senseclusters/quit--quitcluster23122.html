quit

verb
to resign or leave a position
1.0
Pronunciation: / kwɪt / (AmE), / kwɪt / (BrE)
to leave, resign, or give up a position or occupation

Examples

To quit a job
After ten years as CEO, she decided to quit her job and travel the world.
He quit his position as manager after a disagreement with the board.
Many employees are choosing to quit for better opportunities elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resign - to give up a job or position formally
  • synonym: retire - to leave a job permanently, usually due to age
