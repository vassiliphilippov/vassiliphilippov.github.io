cementlike

adjective
binding or unifying
5.3
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
acting to bind or hold things together, especially by providing cohesion or unity, often in a figurative sense

Examples

Cementlike unity
Her calm leadership had a cementlike quality that held the divided committee together during debates.
The team's cementlike spirit helped them overcome many challenges throughout the season.
Shared values can have a cementlike effect on a community, keeping people united.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cohesive - tending to stick together or unite
  • antonym: divisive - causing disagreement or disunity
