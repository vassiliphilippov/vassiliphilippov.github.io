dawn

noun
first light of day; early morning
1.0 19/m
Pronunciation: / d…în / (AmE), / d…îÀên / (BrE)
the time in the morning when the first light appears in the sky, before the sun rises

Examples

Dawn breaks
Birds began to sing as dawn broke over the city.
By dawn, the storm had passed and the sky was clear.
We left the campsite at dawn to avoid the midday heat.
