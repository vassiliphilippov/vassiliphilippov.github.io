mark

noun
crossed lines marking; sign or symbol
1.0 21/m
Pronunciation: / m…ërk / (AmE), / m…ëÀêk / (BrE)
a visible sign, line, spot, scratch, or stain on a surface

Examples

A mark on the wall
She drew a mark on the wall to indicate the starting point.
The carpenter used a crisscross mark to show where to cut.

She looked for any mark of improvement in his behavior.

There was a strange mark on the old letter, possibly a signature or seal.
