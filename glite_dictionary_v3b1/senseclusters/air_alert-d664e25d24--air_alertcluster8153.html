air alert

noun
state of airborne combat readiness
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɛr əˈlɜrt / (AmE), / eə əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
a military condition in which combat aircraft are airborne and ready to respond to an operation or threat

Examples

Air alert status

The base commander ordered an air alert as tensions in the region escalated.
