Mugil cephalus

noun
species of mullet fish
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːdʒɪl ˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˈmjuːdʒɪl ˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
A species of mullet, the flathead grey mullet, found in coastal temperate and tropical waters worldwide and widely fished, especially in the eastern United States.

Examples

Mugil cephalus fish species
Mugil cephalus is commonly found in estuaries and coastal waters around the world.
Fishermen along the Atlantic coast often target Mugil cephalus during the mullet run season.
Many cuisines feature dishes made from Mugil cephalus, also known as the flathead grey mullet.
