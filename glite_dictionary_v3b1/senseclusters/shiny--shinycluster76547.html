shiny

noun
rare alternate-coloured Pokémon (gaming)
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0283a\u026ani / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0283a\u026ani / (BrE)
(informal, gaming) a Pokémon or other game creature that has a rare alternate colour scheme; a rare colour variant often sought by players.

Examples

Catch a shiny creature
During the hunt, I finally found a shiny in the tall grass, a rare colour variant.
Collectors often trade their rare shinies to complete themed Pokémon teams for battles.
She felt proud when her first shiny hatched after hours of camping the egg.
