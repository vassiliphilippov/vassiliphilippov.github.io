freakin

adverb
emphatic intensifier
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkɪn / (AmE), / ˈfriːkɪn / (BrE)
used informally to add emphasis to a statement, often as a milder substitute for 'freaking' or 'fucking'

Examples

So freakin good
That movie was freakin amazing and I want to see it again.
I am freakin tired after working all day in the sun.
This pizza is freakin delicious, you have to try it.
