bruja

noun
female folk healer
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːhɑː / (AmE), / ˈbruːhɑː / (BrE)
a woman who practices folk medicine or traditional healing, often using herbs and home remedies

Examples

Healing bruja
The villagers often visited the local bruja for natural remedies and advice.
Stories about the mysterious bruja have been passed down for generations in the community.
When modern medicine failed, they turned to a respected bruja for help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healer - a person who helps others recover from illness, often using traditional methods
  • synonym: witch - a woman believed to have magical powers, often associated with folk practices
