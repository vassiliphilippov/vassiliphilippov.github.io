Jew's-ears

noun
edible ear-shaped fungus on wood
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːz ˌɪrz / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːz ˌɪəz / (BrE)
a widely distributed edible fungus (Auricularia auricula-judae) shaped like a human ear, commonly found growing on decaying wood
