melt

verb
become less visible or distinct
7.6
Pronunciation: / mɛlt / (AmE), / mɛlt / (BrE)
to gradually disappear or blend into something else, becoming less visible or distinct

Examples

To melt into shadows
As the fog thickened, the village seemed to melt into the landscape.
Her figure melted into the shadows as she walked away.
The colors on the horizon melted into one another at sunset.
As the night fell, the shadows melted into the darkness of the room.
The chameleon seemed to melt into the green leaves around it.
The guests tried to melt into the background to avoid attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix or combine so that the separate things are not easily distinguished
  • synonym: disappear - cease to be visible
  • synonym: fade - to gradually disappear or become less distinct
  • synonym: merge - to combine or cause to combine to form a single entity
