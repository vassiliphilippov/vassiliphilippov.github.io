ferrier

noun
old word for blacksmith
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛriər / (AmE), / ˈfɛriə / (BrE)
an old term for a blacksmith, especially one who makes and fits horseshoes

Examples

The ferrier fits horseshoes
The village ferrier was respected for his skill with iron and horseshoes.
In medieval times, every town needed a skilled ferrier to care for the horses.
The old sign above the workshop still read 'ferrier' despite the change in ownership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand
  • synonym: farrier - a specialist in equine hoof care and shoeing horses
