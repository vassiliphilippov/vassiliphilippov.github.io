benzoyl

adjective
containing benzoyl group
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnzoʊˌɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɛnzɔɪl / (BrE)
used in chemical names to show that a compound contains the benzoyl group, which is derived from benzoic acid

Examples

Benzoyl chloride compound
The chemist added a benzoyl group to the molecule to change its properties.
Benzoyl peroxide contains the benzoyl group and is used in acne treatments.
Researchers studied several benzoyl compounds for their potential medical applications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acetyl - used in chemical names to show a compound contains the acetyl group
