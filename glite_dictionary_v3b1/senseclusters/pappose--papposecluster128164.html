pappose

adjective
bearing pappi or feathery bristles (of plants)
9.9 9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpæp.oʊs/ (AmE), /ˈpæp.əʊs/ (BrE)
(of plants) having pappi or tufts of featherlike hairs or delicate bristles, such as those found on dandelions and thistles.

Examples

pappose plant seeds
Many thistle plants are pappose, producing seeds with feathery bristles for dispersal.
The botanist noted the pappose heads of the composite flowers in her field journal.
The dandelion’s pappose seeds are easily carried away by the wind.
