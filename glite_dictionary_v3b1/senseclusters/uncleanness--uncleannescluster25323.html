uncleanness

noun
the state of being dirty or unsanitary
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklin.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkliːn.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being dirty, filthy, or unsanitary

Examples

Continuous uncleanness
Continuous uncleanness in the area contributed to the spread of disease.
She was shocked by the uncleanness of the public restroom.
The uncleanness of the kitchen led to several complaints from the health inspector.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean
  • synonym: dirtiness - the state of being dirty
