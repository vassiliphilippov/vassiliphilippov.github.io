bucko

noun
slang for tough man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌkəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a bold, aggressive, or tough man, sometimes used as a familiar or challenging form of address

Examples

Listen, bucko
Calm down, bucko, there is no need to shout at everyone.
He strutted into the bar like a real bucko, looking for trouble.
"Watch your mouth, bucko, or you'll regret it," she warned.
