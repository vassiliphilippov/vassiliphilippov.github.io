battement

noun
rhythmic beat or stroke
Pronunciation: / bætˈmɑ̃ / (AmE), / bætˈmɑ̃ / (BrE)
a beat, stroke, or rhythmic pulsation; a repeated striking or flapping movement, especially of sound or motion

Examples

The battement of wings
The steady battement of the rain on the roof made it hard to sleep.
She listened to the battement of her own heart as she waited nervously.
The battement of wings filled the air as the birds took flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beat - a regular rhythmic sound or movement
  • synonym: pulse - a regular throbbing or rhythmic movement
