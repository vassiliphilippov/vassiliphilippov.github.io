Examples

The dharmakaya truth
The concept of dharmakaya is central to Mahayana Buddhist philosophy.
Many Buddhist texts describe the dharmakaya as the true nature of enlightenment.
Meditation practices often aim to realize the dharmakaya within oneself.
In Mahayana Buddhism, the dharmakaya is considered the unchanging essence of the Buddha.
Meditation can help practitioners realize their connection to the dharmakaya within themselves.
Teachers often describe the dharmakaya as the limitless foundation of all existence.
