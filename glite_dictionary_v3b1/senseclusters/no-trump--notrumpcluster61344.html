no-trump

noun
contract bridge: no suit as trump
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌtrʌmp / (AmE), / ˈnəʊˌtrʌmp / (BrE)
In contract bridge, a bid or contract in which no suit is designated as the trump suit for the hand.

Examples

A no-trump contract
A no-trump contract can be more challenging for beginners to play.
He made a bold bid of three no-trump in the final round of bridge.
She explained the advantages of playing no-trump versus a suit contract.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trump - A suit designated to outrank the others in a card game
