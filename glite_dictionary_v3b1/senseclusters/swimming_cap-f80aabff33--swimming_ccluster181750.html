swimming cap

noun
tight-fitting cap for swimming
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪmɪŋ kæp / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ kap / (BrE)
A tight-fitting cap worn to keep hair dry and protected while swimming.

Examples

A swimming cap fits
My new swimming cap is made of silicone and fits snugly on my head.
She always wears a swimming cap to keep her hair from getting wet at the pool.
The lifeguard reminded everyone to put on their swimming caps before entering the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathing cap - a cap worn while swimming to keep hair dry; often a synonym for swimming cap
