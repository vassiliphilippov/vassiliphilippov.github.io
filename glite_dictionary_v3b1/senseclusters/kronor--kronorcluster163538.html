kronor

noun
Swedish currency units
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊnɔr / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊnɔː / (BrE)
the plural form of the Swedish currency unit, krona

Examples

kronor in Sweden
He paid 200 kronor for the museum ticket in Stockholm.
The price of the meal was listed in both euros and kronor.
She exchanged her dollars for kronor before her trip to Sweden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: euro - the official currency of the eurozone
  • synonym: krona - the basic unit of currency in Sweden
