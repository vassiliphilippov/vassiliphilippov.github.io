katabasis

noun
descent or journey downward
Pronunciation: / kəˈtæbəsɪs / (AmE), / kəˈtæbəsɪs / (BrE)
a journey or descent from a higher place to a lower one, especially a symbolic or literal descent into the underworld

Examples

Hero's katabasis into darkness
In ancient myths, the hero's katabasis often leads to important discoveries or personal growth.
The novel describes a symbolic katabasis as the main character faces his deepest fears.
Many epic stories include a katabasis where the protagonist travels to the underworld.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anabasis - a journey or march from a lower place to a higher one
  • synonym: descent - the act of moving downward
