oyster bank

noun
place where oysters are bred and grown
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪstər bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə bæŋk / (BrE)
A place or stretch of ground in shallow water where oysters are bred and grown commercially or naturally.

Examples

The oyster bank area
Strict environmental regulations protect the oyster bank from overfishing and pollution.
The local fishermen harvest oysters from the oyster bank every spring.
Visitors can tour the oyster bank to learn how oysters are cultivated and harvested.
