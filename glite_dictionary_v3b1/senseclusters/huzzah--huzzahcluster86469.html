huzzah

noun
cheer of joy
9.9
Pronunciation: / həˈzɑ / (AmE), / həˈzɑː / (BrE)
a loud shout of joy, approval, or celebration

Examples

A loud huzzah
The crowd erupted in huzzah as the team scored the winning goal.
With a loud huzzah, the students celebrated the end of exams.
A chorus of huzzahs filled the hall when the winner was announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - a shout expressing disapproval
  • synonym: cheer - a shout of encouragement or joy
