ensuite

noun
bedroom-attached bathroom
3.6
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈswit / (AmE), / ɒnˈswiːt / (BrE)
a private bathroom that is directly connected to a bedroom

Examples

A modern ensuite
The hotel room included a spacious ensuite with a modern shower and large mirror.
She preferred to rent the apartment because it had an ensuite attached to the master bedroom.
Guests appreciated having their own ensuite during their stay at the bed and breakfast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathroom - a room containing a toilet and usually a shower or bathtub
  • antonym: shared bathroom - a bathroom used by more than one room or person
