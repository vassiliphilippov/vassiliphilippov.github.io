brain rot

noun
decline in mental sharpness or ability
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪn ˌrɑt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn ˌrɒt / (BrE)
A progressive decline in mental sharpness or ability, often attributed to lack of mental stimulation or excessive screen time.

Examples

Brain rot from screens
After college, she feared her mind would suffer from brain rot without new challenges.
The kids joked that summer break gives them brain rot from playing video games all day.
Too much reality TV can lead to serious brain rot if you're not careful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mental decline - gradual decrease in cognitive ability
  • antonym: mental sharpness - the quality of being quick and alert in thought
