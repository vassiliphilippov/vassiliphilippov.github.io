Examples

Extreme boniness
Despite his boniness, he remained surprisingly strong for his size.

Synonyms

  • emaciation
    8.6 0.12/m
    emaciation is extreme medical thinness from illness or starvation, while boniness emphasizes bones showing from lack of flesh.
  • gauntness
    8.4 0.03/m
    gauntness looks pale and hollow from illness or hunger, while boniness stresses the bones visibly sticking out.
  • scrawniness
    8.2 0.02/m
    scrawniness sounds informal and suggests weak thinness, while boniness specifically names skin and bones showing from lack of flesh.
