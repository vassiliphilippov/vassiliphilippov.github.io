Lothario

noun
a man who is a womanizer
Pronunciation: / loʊˈθɛərioʊ / (AmE), / ləˈθɑːriəʊ / (BrE)
a man who seduces women frequently and behaves selfishly or unscrupulously in his sexual relationships

Examples

A Lothario man
Everyone warned her about his history as a Lothario, but she ignored their advice.
He was known as a Lothario, charming women wherever he went.
The scandal ended the politician's reputation as a discreet Lothario.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithful - remaining loyal to a partner; not unfaithful
  • synonym: philanderer - a man who has many casual sexual relationships with women
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who often has casual sexual relationships with women
