mouch

verb
to wander aimlessly
9.9
Pronunciation: /muːtʃ/ /; / /maʊtʃ/ (AmE), /muːtʃ/ /; / /maʊtʃ/ (BrE)
to move about or wander in a slow, aimless, or lazy way, often without a clear purpose.

Examples

They mouched through the park, chatting and watching the world go by.
Instead of working, she spent the morning mouching about the city center.
