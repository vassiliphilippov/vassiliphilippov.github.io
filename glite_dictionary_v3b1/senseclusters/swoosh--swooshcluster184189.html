swoosh

noun
curved motion shape
5.1
Pronunciation: / swuːʃ / (AmE), / swuːʃ / (BrE)
a long, curved line or shape that suggests movement or speed, often used in art or design

Examples

A blue swoosh
The designer used a bright blue swoosh to make the logo stand out.
A red swoosh on the wall gave the room a sense of energy and movement.
She painted a large swoosh across the canvas to capture the feeling of speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arc - a part of the circumference of a circle or other curve
  • synonym: curve - a smoothly bending line without angles
