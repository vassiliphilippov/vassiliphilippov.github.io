unpruned

adjective
not trimmed
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpruːnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpruːnd / (BrE)
not trimmed or cut back, especially referring to plants or trees

Examples

Unpruned hedge
The unpruned hedge blocked the view from the window.
If left unpruned, the tree's branches can become weak and tangled.
Gardeners recommend not leaving roses unpruned for too many seasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overgrown - grown excessively, often due to lack of trimming
  • antonym: pruned - trimmed or cut back, especially of plants
