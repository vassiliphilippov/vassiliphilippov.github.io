tandoori

adjective
cooked in clay oven
Pronunciation: / tænˈdʊri / (AmE), / tænˈdʊəri / (BrE)
cooked in a traditional clay oven, usually with spices, especially in Indian cuisine

Examples

Tandoori chicken dish
We enjoyed spicy tandoori chicken at the new Indian restaurant last night.
The chef prepared a delicious tandoori fish using a blend of traditional spices.
Many people love the smoky flavor of tandoori dishes cooked in a clay oven.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baked - cooked by dry heat in an oven
  • synonym: grilled - cooked over direct heat, often on a grill
