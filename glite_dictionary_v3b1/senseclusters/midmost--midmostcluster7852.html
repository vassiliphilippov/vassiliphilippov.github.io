midmost

adverb
in the middle or at the center
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.məʊst / (BrE)
in the very middle or central part of something; at the center

Examples

Placed midmost

The statue stood midmost in the square, drawing the attention of all visitors.
