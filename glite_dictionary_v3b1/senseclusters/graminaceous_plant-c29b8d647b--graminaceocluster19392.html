graminaceous plant

noun

Pronunciation: /græ.mə.ˈneɪ.ʃəs plænt/ /; / /græ.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃəs plænt/ (AmE), /græ.mə.ˈneɪ.ʃəs plɑːnt/ /; / /græ.mɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃəs plɑːnt/ (BrE)
A plant belonging to the grass family, typically with hollow jointed stems and long narrow leaves.

Examples

Bamboo is a type of graminaceous plant that grows rapidly in many climates.
