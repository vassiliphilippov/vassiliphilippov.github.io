mulishly

adverb
in a stubborn or obstinate way
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːlɪʃli / (BrE)
In a stubborn or obstinate manner, like a mule.

Examples

To mulishly refuse
He mulishly insisted on following his own plan, ignoring everyone's advice.
She mulishly refused to admit she was wrong despite all the evidence.
The child sat mulishly at the table, refusing to eat her vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstinately - in a stubborn and determined way
  • synonym: stubbornly - in a stubborn or obstinate way
