tump

verb
to tip over
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʌmp / (AmE), / tʌmp / (BrE)
to tip over or cause something to fall over, especially accidentally.

Examples

To tump a bucket
Be careful not to tump the bucket when you carry it across the yard.
The canoe will tump if you stand up too quickly.
He accidentally tumped the box over while reaching for his tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: topple - to fall or make something fall over
  • antonym: upright - to set or remain in a vertical position
