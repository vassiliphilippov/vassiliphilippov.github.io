diffidence

noun
lack of self-confidence
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪf.ɪ.dəns / (AmE), / ˈdɪf.ɪ.dəns / (BrE)
shyness or lack of self-confidence; modesty or hesitation in acting or speaking due to self-doubt or reserve.

Examples

Her diffidence in speech
Despite her intelligence, diffidence kept her from taking credit for her work.
He approached the podium with diffidence, unsure if his opinion would be valued.
Her diffidence made her reluctant to speak up during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidence - belief in one's abilities or the absence of self-doubt
  • synonym: timidity - showing or feeling lack of courage or self-assurance
