keto

adjective
low-carb, high-fat diet
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.toʊ/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.toʊ/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.təʊ/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.təʊ/ (BrE)
relating to a diet that is very low in carbohydrates and high in fat, intended to promote ketosis in the body

Examples

Keto diet plan
Many people choose a keto lifestyle to help with weight loss and energy levels.
He prepared a keto meal with avocado, eggs, and salmon for breakfast.
Finding keto options at restaurants can sometimes be challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: low-carb - containing or relating to a small amount of carbohydrates
