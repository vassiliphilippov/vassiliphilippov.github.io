photorealistic

adjective
lifelike appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˌriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˌrɪəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
extremely lifelike in appearance, closely resembling a real photograph

Examples

Photorealistic images
The movie used photorealistic computer graphics to create lifelike animals.
Her photorealistic drawings are often mistaken for actual photographs.
The video game's photorealistic landscapes amazed players with their detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cartoonish - having a style similar to a cartoon, not lifelike
  • degree higher: hyperrealistic - even more detailed and lifelike than photorealistic
  • degree lower: realistic - showing things as they really are
