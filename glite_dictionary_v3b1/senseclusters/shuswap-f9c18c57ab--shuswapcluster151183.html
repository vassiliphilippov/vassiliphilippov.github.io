Shuswap

proper noun
Indigenous Canadian group
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːʃwɑːp / (AmE), / ˈʃuːʃwæp / (BrE)
a First Nations people of British Columbia, Canada, or their language or traditional territory

Examples

Shuswap people
The Shuswap have lived in the interior of British Columbia for thousands of years.
Many Shuswap people continue to speak their traditional language and practice their customs.
The Shuswap territory includes beautiful lakes and forests in western Canada.
