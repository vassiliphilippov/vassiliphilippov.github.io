ramble

noun
leisurely, aimless walk
Pronunciation: / ˈræm.bəl / (AmE), / ˈræm.bəl / (BrE)
a walk taken for pleasure, typically without a definite route or purpose, often in the countryside

Examples

A countryside ramble
She set off on a solitary ramble without any destination in mind.
Their afternoon ramble took them over winding hills and along hidden streams.
We enjoyed a peaceful ramble through the woods on Sunday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amble - a relaxed, easy walk; stroll
  • degree higher: hike - a long walk, usually in the countryside or wilderness
  • synonym: stroll - walk in a leisurely way, especially for pleasure
