Synonyms

  • different
    1.0 608/m
    different means not the same in form or quality, while dissimilar highlights a clear lack of likeness between two things.
  • unlike
    1.5 0.03/m
    unlike shows a contrast between two items, while dissimilar describes things that do not share the same appearance or nature.
  • unalike
    4.2 0.06/m
    unalike means not alike in appearance or character, while dissimilar is a more formal way to say things differ.
  • distinct
    3.5 24/m
    distinct looks at clear, separate features you can notice, while dissimilar points to an overall difference in kind or appearance.
  • disparate
    7.9 5.74/m
    disparate describes things that are of very different kinds and hard to compare, while dissimilar simply means not alike.
  • nonidentical
    5.0 0.01/m
    nonidentical means not exactly the same in detail, while dissimilar suggests a broader, noticeable difference in nature or looks.
