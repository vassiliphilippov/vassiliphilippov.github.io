diamondback

noun
large rattlesnake with diamond pattern
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəməndˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəməndˌbak / (BrE)
A large rattlesnake with diamond-shaped markings on its back, especially the eastern or western diamondback of North America.

Examples

The diamondback rattlesnake
Hikers are advised to watch out for diamondbacks in the desert.
The diamondback can deliver a venomous bite if threatened.
The diamondback is known for the distinctive rattling sound it makes as a warning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rattlesnake - a venomous American snake with a jointed rattle at the end of its tail
