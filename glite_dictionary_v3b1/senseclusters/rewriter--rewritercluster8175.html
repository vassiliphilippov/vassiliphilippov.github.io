rewriter

noun
person who revises or adapts written text
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈriːˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who edits, adapts, or revises written material, especially to make it suitable for publication

Examples

As a professional rewriter, she specializes in turning complex reports into accessible summaries.
The publisher hired a rewriter to adapt the article for younger readers.
