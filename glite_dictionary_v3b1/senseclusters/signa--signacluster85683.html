signa

noun
prescription instructions
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnə / (BrE)
the part of a medical prescription that gives instructions on how a medication should be taken

Examples

Prescription signa instructions
The pharmacist checked the signa to ensure the patient understood how to take the medication.
Doctors must write clear signa on every prescription to avoid confusion.
If the signa is unclear, the pharmacist will call the doctor for clarification.

Related Terms

  • synonym: directions - instructions on how to use something
  • synonym: dosage - the amount and frequency of a drug to be taken
