rifle range

noun
distance a rifle bullet can travel
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪfəl reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl reɪndʒ / (BrE)
the maximum distance over which a rifle bullet can be accurately or effectively fired

Examples
