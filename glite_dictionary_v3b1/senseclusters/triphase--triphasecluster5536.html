triphase

adjective
three-phase electrical
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌfeɪz / (AmE), / ˈtraɪˌfeɪz / (BrE)
relating to or involving three phases, especially in electrical systems such as power supply or motors

Examples

Triphase power system
The factory upgraded to a triphase power system to handle heavier machinery.
A triphase generator is more efficient for industrial applications than a single-phase one.
Electricians often install triphase wiring in commercial buildings for better power distribution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-phase - involving only one phase, especially in electricity
  • synonym: three-phase - involving three separate phases, especially in electricity
