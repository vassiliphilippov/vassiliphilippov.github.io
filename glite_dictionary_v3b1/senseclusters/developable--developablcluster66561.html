developable

adjective
can be processed
6.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being developed by photographic processing to produce a visible image

Examples

Developable film
The old negatives were still developable after many years in storage.
Only developable film can be used to create printed photographs in the darkroom.
She was relieved to hear that the damaged roll was still developable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: processable - able to be processed or treated in a particular way
  • antonym: undevelopable - not capable of being developed by photographic processing
