loke

noun
narrow lane or passage
Pronunciation: / loʊk / (AmE), / ləʊk / (BrE)
an old term for a small lane, narrow passage, or enclosed path, especially in rural areas

Examples

A quiet loke
The old mill was hidden down a quiet loke lined with wildflowers.
Villagers often walked along the loke to reach the fields at dawn.
A narrow loke separated the two rows of cottages in the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alley - a narrow passageway between or behind buildings
  • synonym: lane - a narrow road or path, especially in the countryside
