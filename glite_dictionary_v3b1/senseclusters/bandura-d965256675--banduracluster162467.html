Bandura

proper noun
surname; psychologist
Pronunciation: / bænˈdʊrə / (AmE), / bænˈdjʊərə / (BrE)
a surname, notably borne by the psychologist Albert Bandura, known for his work in social learning theory

Examples

Albert Bandura
Albert Bandura is famous for developing the concept of observational learning.
Many psychology students study the theories of Bandura in their courses.
The influence of Bandura can be seen in modern educational practices.
