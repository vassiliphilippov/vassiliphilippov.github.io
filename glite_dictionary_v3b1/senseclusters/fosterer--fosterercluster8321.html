fosterer

noun
supporter or encourager
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstərər / (AmE), / ˈfɒstərə / (BrE)
a person or organization that encourages, supports, or helps the growth or development of someone or something

Examples

Fosterer of talent
The organization became a key fosterer of innovation in the local community.
As a fosterer of young talent, she provided guidance and resources to aspiring artists.
The school acted as a fosterer of environmental awareness among its students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mentor - an experienced person who advises and helps someone less experienced
  • antonym: opponent - a person who disagrees with or fights against something
  • synonym: supporter - a person who gives help or encouragement
