prostheon

noun
anterior craniometric skull point
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑs.θi.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈprɒs.θi.ɒn / (BrE)
a craniometric point located at the most anterior point in the midline on the alveolar process of the maxilla

Examples

The prostheon point
Precise identification of the prostheon is essential in craniometric analysis.
Researchers use the prostheon as a reference point in anthropological studies.
The measurement from the prostheon to the nasion is important in facial reconstruction.
