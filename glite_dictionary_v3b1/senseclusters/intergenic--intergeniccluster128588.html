intergenic

adjective
between genes
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
located or occurring between genes on a DNA molecule

Examples

Intergenic region
Scientists discovered a mutation in the intergenic region that affected gene expression.
The study focused on how intergenic sequences influence the regulation of nearby genes.
Many regulatory elements are found in intergenic areas of the genome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intragenic - located within a gene
