good enough for

adjective
sufficient for a specific purpose
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf fɔr / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf fə / (BrE)
adequate or sufficient in quality or ability to satisfy a particular standard or purpose.

Examples

Good enough for the manager
If the report is good enough for the manager, we can submit it as is.
This dress is good enough for the party tonight, even if it's not perfect.
Your English is good enough for everyday conversation in most situations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inadequate - not good enough to meet a standard or need
  • synonym: sufficient - enough to meet the needs of a situation
