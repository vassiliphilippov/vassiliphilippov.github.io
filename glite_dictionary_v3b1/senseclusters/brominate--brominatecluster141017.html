brominate

verb
treat with bromine
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊ.mɪˌneɪt /; / ˈbroʊ.məˌneɪt /; / ˈbroʊməˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.mɪˌneɪt /; / ˈbrəʊ.mɪ.neɪt /; / ˈbrəʊməneɪt / (BrE)
to treat or react a substance with bromine, especially by adding bromine atoms to a chemical compound
