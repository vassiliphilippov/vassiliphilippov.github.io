sarcolemma

noun
membrane surrounding a muscle fiber
Pronunciation: / sɑrkoʊˈlɛmə / (AmE), / sɑːkəˈlɛmə / (BrE)
the thin, flexible membrane that surrounds a muscle fiber, enclosing its contractile components

Examples

The sarcolemma membrane
Muscle diseases can sometimes affect the integrity of the sarcolemma.
Researchers studied how the sarcolemma responds to different types of exercise.
The sarcolemma plays a crucial role in transmitting nerve impulses to the muscle fiber.
