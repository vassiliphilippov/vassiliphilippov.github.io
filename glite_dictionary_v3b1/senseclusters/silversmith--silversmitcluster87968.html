silversmith

noun
person who works with silver
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person whose job is making or repairing objects from silver

Examples

A silversmith makes silver
After years of apprenticeship, she became a skilled silversmith in her own right.
The silversmith crafted a beautiful set of silver spoons for the royal family.
The old silversmith's workshop was filled with ornate jewelry and delicate ornaments.
