Branchiopoda

noun
class of primitive freshwater crustaceans
9.9
Pronunciation: / bræn.kiˈɑ.pə.də /; / brænkiˈɑpədə / (AmE), / bræŋ.kɪˈɒ.pə.də /; / bræŋkɪˈɒpədə / (BrE)
a group of small, primitive aquatic crustaceans found mainly in freshwater, including fairy shrimps, brine shrimps, tadpole shrimps, and water fleas
