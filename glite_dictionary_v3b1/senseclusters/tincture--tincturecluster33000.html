tincture

noun
slight variation in color shade
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋktʃɚ /; / ˈtɪŋktʃər / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋktʃə / (BrE)
a slight trace or hint of a color, quality, or feeling

Examples

The atmosphere was calm, but with a tincture of tension beneath the surface.
There was a tincture of irony in his remarks during the meeting.
