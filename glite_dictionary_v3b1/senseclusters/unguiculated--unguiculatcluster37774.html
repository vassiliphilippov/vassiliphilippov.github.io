unguiculated

adjective
having or resembling claws or nails
Pronunciation: / ˌʌŋɡwɪˈkjuːleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌŋɡwɪˈkjuːleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or resembling claws or nails; furnished with a claw or clawlike appendage, as in certain animals or the petals of some flowers

Examples

Unguiculated petals
Many mammals are unguiculated, allowing them to grasp prey effectively.
Some botanists describe the petal as unguiculated due to its narrow, clawlike base.
The cat's unguiculated paws are well adapted for climbing.
