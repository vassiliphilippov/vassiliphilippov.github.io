ketoacidosis-resistant diabetes

noun
mild adult-onset diabetes resistant to ketoacidosis

A mild form of diabetes mellitus, usually developing gradually in adults, characterized by resistance to ketoacidosis and typically managed with diet and hypoglycemic agents rather than insulin injections.

Examples

ketoacidosis-resistant diabetes treatment
Because his condition was diagnosed as ketoacidosis-resistant diabetes, dietary changes and medication were effective treatments.
Many adults with ketoacidosis-resistant diabetes are able to manage their blood sugar without insulin injections.
Obesity and severe stress can precipitate ketoacidosis-resistant diabetes in susceptible individuals.

  • synonym: type 2 diabetes - a common form of diabetes developing in adults, often associated with obesity
