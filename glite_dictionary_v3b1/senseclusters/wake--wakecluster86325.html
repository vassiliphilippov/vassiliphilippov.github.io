wake

noun
a vigil held over a corpse before burial
4.4 5.8
Pronunciation: /weɪk/ (AmE), /weɪk/ (BrE)
a vigil held over a dead body before the funeral, especially one attended by family and friends

Examples

Family wake

Many cultures have traditions similar to a wake, where friends gather before the funeral.
The stories told at the wake brought both laughter and tears to those who attended.
