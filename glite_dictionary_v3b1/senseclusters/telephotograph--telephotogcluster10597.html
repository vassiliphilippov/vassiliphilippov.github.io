telephotograph

noun
photograph sent over a distance electronically
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈfoʊtəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈfəʊtəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A photograph transmitted and reproduced over a distance by electronic or telegraphic means.

Examples

Send a telephotograph
During the 1930s, it was revolutionary to transmit a telephotograph across continents.
He marveled at the clarity of the telephotograph sent from the remote broadcast station.
The newspaper received a telephotograph of the sports event minutes after it ended.
