sneakily

adverb
in a sly or secretive way
Pronunciation: / ˈsniːkɪli / (AmE), / ˈsniːkɪli / (BrE)
in a secretive or sly manner, so as not to be noticed

Examples

To sneakily look
He sneakily glanced at the list to see if his name was there.
She sneakily checked her phone under the desk during the meeting.
The child sneakily took another cookie when no one was looking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surreptitiously - in a secret or stealthy manner
