genus Salicornia

noun
genus of salt-tolerant plants (glassworts)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs sæl.ɪˈkɔːr.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs sæl.ɪˈkɔː.nɪ.ə / (BrE)
