growly

adjective
low rough sound
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊli / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊli / (BrE)
making or having a low, rough, or grumbling sound, like a growl.

Examples

Growly voice
The singer's growly voice gave the song a unique character.
Our old car makes a growly noise when it starts in the morning.
The bear let out a growly sound as it walked through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gruff - having a rough or harsh sound
  • antonym: smooth - having a soft or even sound
