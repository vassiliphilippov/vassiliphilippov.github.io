crystallized ginger

noun
candied gingerroot pieces
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd ˈdʒɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
Gingerroot that has been cooked in sugar syrup and coated with sugar, eaten as a sweet or used in baking.
