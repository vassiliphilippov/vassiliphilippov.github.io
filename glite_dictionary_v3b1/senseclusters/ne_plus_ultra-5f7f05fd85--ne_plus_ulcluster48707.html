ne plus ultra

noun
the ultimate or highest point; perfection
9.9
Pronunciation: / neɪ plʊˈsʌltrə / (AmE), / neɪ pluːs ˈʌltrə / (BrE)
the highest or ultimate point of excellence; the perfect or most extreme example of something

Examples

The ne plus ultra example
For many, the vintage sports car was the ne plus ultra of automotive engineering.
The chef's new dessert is considered the ne plus ultra of fine dining in the city.
This painting represents the ne plus ultra of Renaissance art.
