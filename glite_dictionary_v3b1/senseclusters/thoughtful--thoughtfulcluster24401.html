thoughtful

adjective
paying careful, attentive heed
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt.fəl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəl / (BrE)
Paying careful attention to something; attentive or heedful.

Examples

Thoughtful attention
A thoughtful driver always checks the road carefully before proceeding.
She remained thoughtful of her surroundings during the hike.
You must be thoughtful when following the instructor’s directions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: careless - not paying attention or not careful
  • synonym: heedful - paying careful attention
