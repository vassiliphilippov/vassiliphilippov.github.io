sitarist

noun
sitar player
Pronunciation: / sɪˈtɑːrɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈtɑːrɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the sitar, a traditional Indian stringed musical instrument

Examples

Famous sitarist performer
The famous sitarist performed at the international music festival last night.
As a skilled sitarist, she has toured many countries sharing Indian classical music.
The young sitarist practiced for hours to master the complex melodies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented
