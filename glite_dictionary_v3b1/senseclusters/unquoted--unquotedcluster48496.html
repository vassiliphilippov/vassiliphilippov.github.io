unquoted

adjective
not publicly listed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwəʊtɪd / (BrE)
not listed or traded on a public stock exchange; not having a publicly quoted price.

Examples

Unquoted company
The company remained unquoted to avoid the strict regulations of the stock market.
Many investors seek opportunities in unquoted firms before they go public.
Shares in the unquoted business were only available to a small group of private investors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: listed - officially listed and traded on a public stock exchange
  • synonym: private - not publicly traded or owned by shareholders
