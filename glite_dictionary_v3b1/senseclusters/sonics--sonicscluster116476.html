sonics

noun
audio qualities
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑnɪks / (AmE), / ˈsɒnɪks / (BrE)
the tonal qualities, clarity, and overall audio character of a recording, performance, or sound system

Examples

Album sonics

She chose headphones known for their excellent sonics and comfortable fit.
