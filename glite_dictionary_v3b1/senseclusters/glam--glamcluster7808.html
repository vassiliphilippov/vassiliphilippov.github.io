glam

noun
showy rock style
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɡlæm / (AmE), / ɡlæm / (BrE)
a style of rock music from the 1970s and 1980s known for flashy, theatrical fashion and catchy melodies

Examples

Glam rock music
The band's new album is full of glam influences and energetic guitar riffs.
Fans dressed in glam style with sequins and platform boots for the concert.
He loves the theatrical makeup and costumes that define glam music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glam rock - a rock music style with flamboyant costumes and theatrical performances
  • antonym: punk - a style of rock music with fast tempos and rebellious attitude
