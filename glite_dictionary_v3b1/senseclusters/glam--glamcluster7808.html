glam

noun
showy rock style
7.2
Pronunciation: /glæm/ (AmE), /glæm/ (BrE)
a style of rock music from the 1970s and 1980s known for flashy, theatrical fashion and catchy melodies

Examples

Fans dressed in glam style with sequins and platform boots for the concert.
He loves the theatrical makeup and costumes that define glam music.
