passage

noun
a journey by ship
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪdʒ / (BrE)

Examples

Book a passage
She booked her passage on a ship to America in hopes of a new life.
Storms made the Atlantic passage especially difficult that year.
The passage from England to India was both long and dangerous in the 18th century.
