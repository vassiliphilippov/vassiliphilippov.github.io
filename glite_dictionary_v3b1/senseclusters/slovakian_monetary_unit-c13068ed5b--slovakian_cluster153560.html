Slovakian monetary unit

noun
currency unit of Slovakia
Pronunciation: / sloʊˈvækiən ˈmʌnɪˌtɛri ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / sləʊˈvækiən ˈmʌnɪtəri ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
the official unit of currency used in Slovakia

Examples

The Slovakian monetary unit koruna
Before adopting the euro, the koruna served as the Slovakian monetary unit.
The euro replaced the Slovakian monetary unit in 2009.
Travelers needed to exchange their dollars for Slovakian monetary units upon arrival.

Related Terms
