iron-handed leadership

noun
strict, authoritarian management style

Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.ərn ˈhænd.ɪd ˈliː.dər.ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈaɪ.ərn ˈhænd.əd ˈliː.dər.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.ən ˈhænd.ɪd ˈliː.də.ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈaɪ.ən ˈhænd.əd ˈliː.də.ʃɪp/ (BrE)

Examples

Iron-handed leadership style

While iron-handed leadership can drive results, it may also create a culture of fear and resentment.
