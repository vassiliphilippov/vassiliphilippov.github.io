tube-shaped

adjective
having the form of a tube
3.0 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / tuÀêb  Ée…™pt / (AmE), / tjuÀêb  Ée…™pt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a tube; cylindrical and hollow, often allowing the passage of fluids or air.

Examples

Tube-shaped body
Certain sea creatures, like sponges, have tube-shaped bodies to filter water.
Engineers designed a tube-shaped conduit for cable installation.
The plant has long tube-shaped flowers that attract hummingbirds.

Synonyms

  • tubular
    5.1 1.46/m
    tubular is hollow and often used to carry fluids or air, while tube-shaped describes any object with a tube-like form.
  • cylindrical
    4.0 2.16/m
    cylindrical is a solid or hollow rounded shape with parallel sides, while tube-shaped suggests a hollow form that can carry fluids.
