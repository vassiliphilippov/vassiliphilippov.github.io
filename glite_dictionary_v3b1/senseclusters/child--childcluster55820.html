child

noun
young person; boy or girl
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪld / (AmE), / tʃaɪld / (BrE)
a young person who is not yet an adult, or someone's son or daughter

Even as an adult, she will always be her parents' child.
Every child deserves to feel safe and loved.
Their child started school at the age of five.
