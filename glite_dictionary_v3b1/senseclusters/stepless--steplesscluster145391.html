stepless

adjective
continuous; without steps
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpləs / (AmE), / ˈstɛpləs / (BrE)
having no steps, stages, or abrupt changes; continuous or smooth in progression

Examples

A stepless speed control
The fan offers stepless speed control, allowing you to adjust it smoothly to your preference.
With its stepless dimmer, the lamp can create any level of brightness you need.
The new transmission provides stepless acceleration for a smoother driving experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuous - happening without interruption or breaks
  • antonym: discrete - separate and distinct, not continuous
  • synonym: gradual - happening slowly over time without sudden changes
