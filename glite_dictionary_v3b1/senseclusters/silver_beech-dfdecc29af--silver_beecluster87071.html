silver beech

noun
New Zealand beech tree with silvery bark
6.2 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ ˌbiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌbiːtʃ / (BrE)
A species of beech tree native to New Zealand, noted for its pale, silvery bark.
