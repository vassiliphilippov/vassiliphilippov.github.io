Potamogale velox

noun
African amphibious mammal species
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˌtæməˈɡeɪli ˈviːlɑks / (AmE), / pəʊˌtæməˈɡɑːli ˈviːlɒks / (BrE)
A species of amphibious African insectivorous mammal resembling an otter, also known as the giant otter shrew.

Examples

The Potamogale velox mammal

Unlike otters, Potamogale velox prefers to remain hidden in dense riverbank vegetation.
