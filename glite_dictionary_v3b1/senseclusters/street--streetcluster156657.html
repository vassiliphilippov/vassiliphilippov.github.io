street

noun
urban area associated with poverty and crime
6.4 36/m
Pronunciation: / striÀêt / (AmE), / striÀêt / (BrE)
an urban environment characterized by poverty, crime, and social problems, especially as experienced by people living or working there

Examples

Life on the street
After losing his job, he feared he would end up on the street.
Many children who grow up on the street face daily dangers and challenges.
The charity works to support those living on the street and help them find shelter.

Synonyms

  • inner city
    3.2 5.82/m
    inner city is the central, often low-income part of a city with social problems, while street emphasizes the area's poverty and crime.
  • the street
    3.4 104/m
    the street describes the everyday urban community and culture, while street here stresses the area's poverty and social problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghetto - a part of a city, especially a slum area, occupied by a minority group or groups
  • synonym: slum - a squalid and overcrowded urban street or district
