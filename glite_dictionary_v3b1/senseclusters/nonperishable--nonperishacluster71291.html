nonperishable

noun
long-lasting food
3.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl / (BrE)
a food or product that can be stored for a long time without spoiling or going bad

Examples

A nonperishable like rice
During hurricane season, families stock up on nonperishables like rice and canned vegetables.
The food drive requested donations of nonperishables to help those in need.
She keeps a supply of nonperishables in her pantry for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canned food - food preserved and stored in a can
  • antonym: perishable - a food or product that spoils quickly
