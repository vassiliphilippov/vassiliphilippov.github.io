moped

noun
small motorbike with pedals and engine
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊˌpɛd / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌpɛd / (BrE)
a small motorbike with pedals, powered by a low-powered gasoline engine and capable of being pedaled or driven.

Examples

A moped with pedals
After buying a new moped, he enjoyed riding it around town on weekends.
In some cities, renting a moped is a popular way for tourists to explore local sights.
She commutes to work every day on her moped to avoid traffic.
