boko

noun
Western education
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a Hausa term referring to Western education, especially as distinct from traditional Islamic education in Nigeria

Examples

Western boko education
Some communities in northern Nigeria are skeptical of boko and prefer traditional forms of learning.
The debate over boko versus religious schooling continues to shape education policy in the region.
He believes that boko can provide more opportunities for young people in his village.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Islamic education - education based on Islamic teachings and traditions
  • synonym: education - the process of receiving or giving systematic instruction
