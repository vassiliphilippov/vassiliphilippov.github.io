Western Samoa

proper noun
former name of Samoa (country)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn səˈmoʊə / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən səˈməʊə / (BrE)
the former name for the independent country now known as Samoa, located on the western part of the Samoan Islands in the South Pacific

Examples

Western Samoa country
Many historical documents refer to the nation as Western Samoa.
The country was known as Western Samoa until it changed its name in 1997.
Tourists visiting Western Samoa in the 1980s enjoyed its unspoiled beaches.

Related Terms

  • antonym: American Samoa - the unincorporated US territory on the eastern Samoan islands
  • synonym: Samoa - the independent nation in the South Pacific formerly called Western Samoa
