Munjuk

noun
a Chadic language of Cameroon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊn.dʒʊk / (AmE), / ˈmʌn.dʒʊk / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon, particularly in the Mayo-Danay division and Maga subdivision

Examples

Munjuk language
He grew up bilingual, speaking both French and Munjuk at home.
Researchers have documented traditional stories in Munjuk for preservation efforts.
The Munjuk language is primarily spoken in the Mayo-Danay division of Cameroon.
