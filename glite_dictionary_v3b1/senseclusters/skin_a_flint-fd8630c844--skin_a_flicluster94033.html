skin a flint

verb
be extremely frugal or stingy
Pronunciation: / skɪn ə flɪnt / (AmE), / skɪn ə flɪnt / (BrE)
to be extremely frugal or miserly; to go to great lengths to save or withhold money

Examples

To skin a flint completely
He is so frugal, he could skin a flint and still complain about waste.
People say you have to skin a flint to survive hard times like these.
She's the sort who would skin a flint before parting with a penny.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - showing a readiness to give more of something than is necessary or expected
  • synonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
  • synonym: tightwad - a person who is very unwilling to spend money
