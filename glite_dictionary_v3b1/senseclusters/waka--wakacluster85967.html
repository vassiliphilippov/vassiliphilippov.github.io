waka

verb
to walk or go
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈwækə / (BrE)
to walk or go from one place to another, especially on foot (chiefly West African English, informal)

Examples

To waka home
After the party, she decided to waka home instead of taking a taxi.
We had to waka a long distance to reach the market.
He likes to waka around the city and explore new places.
