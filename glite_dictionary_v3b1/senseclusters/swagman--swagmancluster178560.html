swagman

noun
itinerant Australian laborer (historical)
4.5 9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈswæɡ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈswæɡ.mæn/ (BrE)
(in Australia, historical) a man who traveled from place to place in search of work, carrying his personal belongings in a bundle.
