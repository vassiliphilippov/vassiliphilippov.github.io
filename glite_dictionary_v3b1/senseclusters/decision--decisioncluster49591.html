decision

noun
a conclusion or judgment after consideration
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪʒən /; / dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
the act or result of making a choice or coming to a conclusion after thinking about options

Examples

Make a decision
After long discussions, the committee finally reached a decision about the new policy.
Her decision to move abroad surprised everyone in her family.
It's difficult to make a good decision without all the relevant facts.
After hours of debate, the committee finally reached a decision.
The decision to change careers was not an easy one for her to make.
You need to make a decision before the end of the week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choice - an act of selecting or making a decision when faced with two or more possibilities
  • synonym: conclusion - a judgment or decision reached by reasoning
  • synonym: determination - the act of coming to a decision or making up one's mind
  • antonym: indecision - inability to make a decision
