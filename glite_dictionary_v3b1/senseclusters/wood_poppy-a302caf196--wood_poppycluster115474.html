wood poppy

noun
yellow-flowered woodland plant
7.0
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / wʊd ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to woodland areas of the eastern United States, with bright yellow flowers; also called celandine poppy.
