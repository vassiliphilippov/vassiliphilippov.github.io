streamer

noun
online live broadcaster
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstriː.mər/ (AmE), /ˈstriː.mə/ (BrE)
A person who broadcasts live video or audio over the internet to an audience, often regularly and interactively.

Examples

Every evening, the streamer answered fans' questions while playing the new cooperative game.
Many streamers schedule regular shows to grow their audience and earn income.
