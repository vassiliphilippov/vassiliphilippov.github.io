streamer

noun
online live broadcaster
Pronunciation: / c8strid0m5a / (AmE), / c8strid0m59 / (BrE)
A person who broadcasts live video or audio over the internet to an audience, often regularly and interactively.

Examples

A popular streamer online
The popular streamer shared behind-the-scenes footage during a live broadcast.
Every evening, the streamer answered fans' questions while playing the new cooperative game.
Many streamers schedule regular shows to grow their audience and earn income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcaster - someone who transmits programmes or content to an audience
  • synonym: content creator - a person who produces material (video, audio, text) for online audiences
