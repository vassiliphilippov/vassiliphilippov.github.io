homicide

noun
the killing of one person by another
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːməˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɒmɪsaɪd / (BrE)
the act of killing one human being by another; murder or manslaughter

Examples

Rising homicide rates
Rising homicide rates have alarmed residents and local officials.
The detective specialized in investigating homicide cases throughout the city.
The jury found him not guilty of homicide after reviewing all the evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manslaughter - the unlawful killing of a person without premeditation
  • synonym: murder - the unlawful and intentional killing of a person
