power takeoff

noun
device transferring engine power to equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌteɪkˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌteɪkɒf / (BrE)
a device that transfers mechanical power from an engine to another piece of equipment, especially in vehicles like tractors

Examples
