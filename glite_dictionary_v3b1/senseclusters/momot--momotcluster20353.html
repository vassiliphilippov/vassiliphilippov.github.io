momot

noun
tropical American bird with green and blue plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊˌmɑt / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌmɒt / (BrE)
A tropical American bird related to the motmot, characterized by green and blue plumage and a resemblance to a blue jay.

Examples

The momot bird
Birdwatchers traveled great distances to glimpse the rare blue-green momot in its natural habitat.
The colorful momot perched silently on a branch in the rainforest, blending in with the foliage.
With its striking tail feathers, the momot is often mistaken for other tropical birds by inexperienced observers.
