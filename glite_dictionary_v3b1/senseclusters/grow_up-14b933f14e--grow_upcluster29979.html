grow up

verb
to become an adult
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊ ʌp / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to gradually change from being a child to being an adult; to reach adulthood

Examples

To grow up fast
Children in this village grow up surrounded by beautiful mountains.
She wants her son to grow up to be kind and confident.
When did you grow up and realize you wanted to be a doctor?

Related Terms

  • antonym: childish - appropriate to a child; immature (opposite in attitude)
  • synonym: mature - to become fully developed physically or mentally
