harvest home

noun
gathering and celebration of the harvest
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrvəst ˌhoʊm / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst ˌhəʊm / (BrE)
the gathering in of a ripened crop, traditionally celebrated as a festival at the end of the harvest season

Examples

Traditional harvest home
Each autumn, families gathered for a traditional harvest home filled with feasting and music.
The harvest home was an important event in rural communities, celebrating a year of hard work and abundance.
The village held a harvest home to mark the end of a successful growing season.
