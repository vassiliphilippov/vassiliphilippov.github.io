duh

interjection
obviousness interjection
Pronunciation: / də / (AmE), / də / (BrE)
used to show that something is obvious or should be easily understood, often with sarcasm or mild self-reproach

Examples

Duh, of course
You forgot your keys again? Duh, that's why I called you.
"Is water wet?" "Duh, of course it is!"
He asked if I wanted dessert after dinner and I said, "Duh, who wouldn't?"
I forgot to bring my umbrella and, duh, it started raining as soon as I left.
He asked if water is wet and everyone just said, duh.
She realized, duh, she had been using the wrong password all along.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no kidding - used to show that something is not surprising or is obvious
  • synonym: obviously - used to state that something is clear or self-evident
