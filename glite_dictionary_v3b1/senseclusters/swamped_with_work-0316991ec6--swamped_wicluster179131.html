swamped with work

adjective
overwhelmed by too much work
5.7
Pronunciation: / swɒmpt wɪð wɜrk / (AmE), / swɒmpt wɪð wɜːk / (BrE)
overwhelmed or inundated by a large amount of work or tasks

Examples

Swamped with work all day

Many employees feel swamped with work during the end-of-year rush.
