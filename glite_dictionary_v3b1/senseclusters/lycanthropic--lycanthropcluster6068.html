lycanthropic

adjective
wolf transformation delusion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪkənˈθrɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌlaɪkənˈθrɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to the belief or delusion that one can transform into a wolf, especially as a psychiatric condition.

Examples

Lycanthropic delusion
The patient exhibited lycanthropic behavior, insisting he transformed into a wolf during the full moon.
Her lycanthropic delusions required specialized psychiatric treatment and careful observation.
The novel features a character with a lycanthropic disorder, blurring the line between myth and reality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lycanthropy - the delusion of being able to transform into a wolf
