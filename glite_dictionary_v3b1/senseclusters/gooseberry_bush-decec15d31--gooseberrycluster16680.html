gooseberry bush

noun
a shrub with edible gooseberries
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːsbɛri ˌbʊʃ, ˈɡʊzbɛri ˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊzbəri ˌbʊʃ, ˈɡuːzbəri ˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a spiny Eurasian shrub bearing greenish-purple flowers and edible berries that are yellow-green or red-purple

Examples

A gooseberry bush plant
A gooseberry bush needs sunlight and well-drained soil to thrive.
After pruning the gooseberry bush, she collected a basket of berries for jam.
The gooseberry bush in our garden is full of ripe fruit every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gooseberry - a small edible berry, green when unripe and reddish or purple when ripe
