nongraphic

adjective
not explicit or vivid
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
not described or shown in vivid, explicit, or gory detail, especially regarding violence or sexual content
