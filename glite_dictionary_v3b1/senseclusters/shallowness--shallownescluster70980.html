shallowness

noun
the state of being shallow
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæloʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃaləʊnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having little physical depth.

Examples

Water shallowness
Check the shallowness of the water before letting children swim there.
Divers should be careful of the shallowness at this end of the pool.
The shallowness near the shore makes it safe for beginners to practice snorkeling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depth - the distance from the top or surface to the bottom of something
  • synonym: shallowness - lack of intellectual or emotional depth
