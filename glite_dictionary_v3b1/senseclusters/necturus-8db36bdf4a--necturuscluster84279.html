Necturus

proper noun
genus of aquatic salamanders (mudpuppies)
Pronunciation: / nɛkˈtʊr.əs / (AmE), / nɛkˈtjʊər.əs / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic salamanders in the family Proteidae, native to North America, commonly known as mudpuppies.
