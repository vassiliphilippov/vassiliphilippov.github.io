cocktail party

noun
afternoon party with cocktails
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːkˌteɪl ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl ˌpɑː.ti / (BrE)
A social gathering, usually held in the afternoon or early evening, where cocktails and other drinks are served.

Examples

A cocktail party event
She wore an elegant dress to the annual company cocktail party.
The cocktail party started at six and ended just before dinner.
We met a number of interesting people at last night's cocktail party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: banquet - large ceremonial meal or feast
  • synonym: reception - formal social gathering, often for welcome or celebration
