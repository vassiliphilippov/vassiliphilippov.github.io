vacantness

noun
emptiness or lack
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪkəntnəs / (AmE), / ˈveɪkəntnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being empty, unoccupied, or lacking content or activity

Examples

The vacantness of the room
The vacantness of the office after everyone left was almost unsettling.
He stared into the vacantness of the empty field, lost in thought.
There was a certain vacantness in her expression that worried her friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emptiness - the state of containing nothing
  • antonym: fullness - the state of being filled or occupied
