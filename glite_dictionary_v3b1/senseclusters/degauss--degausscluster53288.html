degauss

verb
remove magnetic properties
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˈɡaʊs / (AmE), / diˈɡaʊs / (BrE)
to remove or reduce the magnetic properties of (something), especially by exposing it to a reverse magnetic field

Examples

To degauss a monitor
Technicians needed to degauss the computer monitor to eliminate visual distortions.
The navy crew degaussed the ship to protect it from magnetic mines.
You should degauss old hard drives before disposal for security reasons.
