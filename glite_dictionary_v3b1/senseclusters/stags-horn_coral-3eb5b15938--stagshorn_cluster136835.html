stag's-horn coral

noun
a branching coral resembling stag antlers
6.0
Pronunciation: / stæɡz hɔrn ˈkɔɹ.əl / (AmE), / stæɡz hɔːn ˈkɒr.əl / (BrE)
a large branching coral whose shape resembles the antlers of a stag, commonly found in shallow tropical reefs
