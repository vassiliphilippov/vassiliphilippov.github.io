jog

verb
give a slight push to
Pronunciation: / dʒɑg / (AmE), / dʒɒg / (BrE)
to give a slight push or nudge to someone or something, especially to attract attention or remind

Examples

To jog his arm
A gentle breeze jogged the door open just a little.
Could you jog my elbow if I start to doze off during the meeting?
He jogged her arm to get her attention as they walked through the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nudge - to push gently or poke
  • synonym: prod - to poke with a finger or pointed object
