arrestor

noun
spark-stopping device
7.4
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛstər / (AmE), / əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
a device attached to engines, chimneys, or exhausts to stop sparks or burning particles from escaping and causing fires

Examples

Spark arrestor on exhaust
The mechanic installed an arrestor on the exhaust pipe to prevent wildfires.
Every fireplace in the cabin was fitted with an arrestor for safety.
Before lighting the stove, she checked that the arrestor was clean and working properly.
