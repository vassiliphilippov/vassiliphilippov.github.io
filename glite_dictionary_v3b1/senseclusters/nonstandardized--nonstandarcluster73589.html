nonstandardized

adjective
not adjusted to standard
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstændərdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstændədaɪzd / (BrE)
expressed in original units or not adjusted to a common or standardized scale, especially in statistics or measurement

Examples

Nonstandardized values reported
The results were presented as nonstandardized values to reflect the original measurement units.
Researchers often compare both standardized and nonstandardized coefficients in their analysis.
It is important to specify whether the data are nonstandardized or have been adjusted for comparison.

Related Terms

  • antonym: standardized - adjusted to a common or standard scale
