disaster area

noun
area affected by a major disaster
5.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈzæs.tər ˈe.riː.ə/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈzɑː.stə ˈeə.ri.ə/ (BrE)
an area or region that has been badly affected by a disaster (such as a natural catastrophe or major accident), often officially recognized for emergency aid

Examples

A disaster area
After the hurricane, the city was declared a disaster area by the government.
Emergency workers rushed supplies and aid into the disaster area after the earthquake struck.
Following severe flooding, the entire region was designated a disaster area to receive federal support.
After the hurricane, the entire coastal town was declared a disaster area by the government.
The wildfire left thousands homeless and turned the region into a disaster area.
When I walked into his room, it looked like a disaster area with clothes and books everywhere.
