round

noun
route news or information takes
5.7 4.1
Pronunciation: /raʊnd/ (AmE), /raʊnd/ (BrE)
a route or circuit along which information, news, or communication is passed from person to person, often repeatedly

Examples

The rumor quickly made the rounds at the local school.
The story is making the rounds in political circles.
