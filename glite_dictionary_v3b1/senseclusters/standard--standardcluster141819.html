standard

noun
basis of comparison; accepted level
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd / (AmE), / ˈstændəd / (BrE)
an accepted level of quality, rule, or example used as a basis for comparison or judgment

Examples

High standard of work
The school maintains a strict standard for academic performance.
This product has been tested and meets the highest standard of safety.
We need to establish a clear standard for evaluating these proposals.
Her work became the standard by which others were judged.
The new safety regulations set a new standard for the industry.
We expect employees to maintain the highest standards of honesty.
