go bankrupt

verb
become unable to pay debts
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈbæŋkˌrʌpt / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈbæŋkrʌpt / (BrE)
to become unable to pay debts; to become insolvent

Examples

To go bankrupt suddenly
After years of declining sales, the company finally went bankrupt.
If they continue spending recklessly, they could go bankrupt by the end of the year.
Many small businesses go bankrupt within their first five years of operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: become insolvent - to be unable to pay debts as they fall due
