froufrou

noun
person fond of frills
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːˌfruː / (AmE), / ˈfruːˌfruː / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who is very fond of frilly, decorative, or overly fashionable clothing and style

Examples

A froufrou at parties
Everyone teased Maria for being a froufrou who always chose the most delicate dresses.
At the party, her froufrou style made her stand out among the guests.
He joked that his sister was a true froufrou with her love of lace and ribbons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion
  • antonym: plain - lacking decoration or adornment
