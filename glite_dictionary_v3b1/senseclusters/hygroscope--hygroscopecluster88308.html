hygroscope

noun
device for detecting humidity changes
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɡrəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪɡrəskəʊp / (BrE)
an instrument for detecting and showing changes in the relative humidity of the atmosphere

Examples

A hygroscope instrument
Early weather stations often relied on a simple hygroscope made of organic materials.
The hygroscope responded quickly to the increase in atmospheric moisture.
The scientist used a hygroscope to monitor the humidity in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hygrometer - an instrument for measuring the amount of humidity in the air
