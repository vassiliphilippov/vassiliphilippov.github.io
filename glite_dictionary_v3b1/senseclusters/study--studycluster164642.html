study

noun
preliminary drawing for later elaboration
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
a drawing, painting, or musical composition made as a practice or exploration of ideas or techniques

Examples

An art study
In the museum, several studies for the famous mural are on display.
She kept a notebook filled with study drawings of hands and faces.
The artist worked on a study to perfect the composition before starting the final painting.
Chopin's studies are an essential part of every pianist's repertoire.
The violinist played a study to improve her left-hand agility.
This study focuses on developing finger strength and speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sketch - a rough or unfinished drawing or painting
  • synonym: étude - a short musical composition, typically for one instrument, designed as an exercise to improve technique or demonstrate skill
