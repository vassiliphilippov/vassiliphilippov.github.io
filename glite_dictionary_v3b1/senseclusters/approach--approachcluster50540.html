approach

verb
come nearer in time
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
(of an event, time, or state) to come nearer in time; to be about to happen or begin soon.

Examples

The deadline approaches
As the deadline approaches, the team is working extra hours to finish the project.
Autumn approached more quickly than we expected this year.
Her birthday is approaching, and she plans to celebrate with close friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: near - to come close in time or space
  • antonym: recede - to move farther away in time or space
