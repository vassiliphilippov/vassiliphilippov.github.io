Examples

Synonyms

  • come near
    2.1 0.03/m
    come near is used when an event is almost happening, while approach is the normal verb for something coming nearer in time.
  • draw near
    2.6 1.40/m
    draw near often sounds a little formal or poetic about time coming close, while approach is neutral everyday language.
  • draw close
    2.9 1.38/m

  • near
    1.0 9.37/m
    near can act as a verb but feels less common and more informal, while approach is the standard verb for time coming soon.
