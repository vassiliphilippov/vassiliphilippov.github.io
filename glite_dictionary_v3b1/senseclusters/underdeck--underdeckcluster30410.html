underdeck

adjective
beneath a deck
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌdɛk / (AmE), / ˈʌndədɛk / (BrE)
located beneath the deck of a ship or structure, often referring to equipment, fixtures, or spaces below the main deck

Examples

Underdeck storage area
The underdeck storage area was used to keep extra supplies safe from the weather.
Engineers installed new underdeck wiring to improve the ship's electrical system.
During the storm, the crew took shelter in the underdeck quarters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ondeck - located on the deck of a ship or structure
