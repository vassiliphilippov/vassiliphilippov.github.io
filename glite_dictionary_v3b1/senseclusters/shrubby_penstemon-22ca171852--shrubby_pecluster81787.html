shrubby penstemon

noun
a low bushy plant with showy violet flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrʌbi pɛnˈstiːmən / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌbi pɛnˈstiːmən / (BrE)
A low, bushy plant with large showy pale lavender or blue-violet flowers in narrow clusters at the ends of its stems.

Examples

A shrubby penstemon plant
Gardeners recommend shrubby penstemon for dry, sunny spots where other plants struggle to thrive.
