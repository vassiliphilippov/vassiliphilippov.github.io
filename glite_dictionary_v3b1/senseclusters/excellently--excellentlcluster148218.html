excellently

adverb
in an excellent way
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.sə.lənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɛks.əl.ənt.li / (BrE)
In an excellent way; to a very high standard.

Examples

Handled excellently
Despite the pressure, the team handled the situation excellently and achieved a great result.
She performed excellently in her exams, receiving top marks in every subject.
The chef prepared the meal excellently, impressing everyone at the table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poorly - in a poor or inferior way
  • synonym: wonderfully - in a wonderful way; extremely well
