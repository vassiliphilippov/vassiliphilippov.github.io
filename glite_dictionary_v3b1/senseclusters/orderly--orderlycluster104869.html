orderly

adjective
peaceful and free from disruption
3.5 2.26/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îrd…öli / (AmE), / Àà…îÀêd…ôli / (BrE)
peaceful and free from violence or disruption; characterized by calm and organized behavior or conditions

Examples

Orderly exit
Our departure from the stadium was orderly, with everyone waiting their turn to leave.
The classroom was unusually orderly after the teacher explained her expectations.
The protesters remained orderly throughout the demonstration, impressing the police with their discipline.

  • peaceable
