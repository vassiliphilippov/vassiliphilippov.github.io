schooler

noun
student at a school
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːlər / (AmE), / ˈskuːlə / (BrE)
a student who attends a school, usually specified by the level (such as high schooler, middle schooler, elementary schooler)

Examples

A schooler student
As a schooler, he participated in many extracurricular activities.
The program is designed to help any schooler improve their reading skills.
Every schooler must wear a uniform at this institution.
The elementary schooler was excited for her first day of class.
Many high schoolers participate in after-school sports and clubs.
As a middle schooler, he started learning a new language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • antonym: teacher - a person who teaches students in a school
