crawl

noun
very slow movement
Pronunciation: / krɔl / (AmE), / krɔːl / (BrE)
a very slow movement, especially of vehicles or progress

Examples

Traffic crawl
After the accident, the traffic slowed to a crawl for miles.
Progress on the project was at a crawl due to constant interruptions.
The marathon runners finished the last stretch at a crawl.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creep - to move slowly and carefully, often to avoid being noticed
