convincer

noun
persuasive person or thing
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɪnsər / (AmE), / kənˈvɪnsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that persuades someone to believe or do something

Examples

A strong convincer
The lawyer's argument was a strong convincer for the jury.
Seeing the results firsthand was the final convincer she needed to change her mind.
His passion for the project made him an effective convincer during the meeting.
Her passionate speech was a strong convincer for the new policy.
The evidence presented in court acted as a powerful convincer for the jury.
Sometimes a simple story can be the best convincer when trying to change someone's mind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doubter - a person who is not easily convinced
  • synonym: influencer - someone who affects others' opinions or actions
  • synonym: persuader - a person who convinces others to do or believe something
