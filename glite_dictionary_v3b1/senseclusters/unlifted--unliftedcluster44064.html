unlifted

adjective
not removed or ended
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪftɪd / (BrE)
not removed, ended, or cancelled, especially referring to a law, ban, restriction, or similar measure

Examples

Unlifted ban
The travel ban remained unlifted for several months after the outbreak.
Many restrictions were still unlifted by the end of the year.
The order was unlifted despite repeated appeals from the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in force - still active or effective, especially of a law or rule
  • antonym: lifted - removed or ended, especially of a ban or restriction
