cloudbuster

noun
weather control device
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdˌbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdˌbʌstə / (BrE)
a device claimed to influence or disperse clouds in order to affect the weather

Examples

A cloudbuster device
The scientist built a cloudbuster to test his theories about weather modification.
Many people were skeptical about whether the cloudbuster could actually disperse clouds.
During the drought, the community invited an inventor with a cloudbuster to try to bring rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rainmaker - a person or device that is believed to cause rain
