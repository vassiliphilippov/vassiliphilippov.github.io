ribbon-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a ribbon
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.ən ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.ən ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a ribbon; long and narrow with relatively flat surfaces.

Examples

Ribbon-shaped brooch
She wore a ribbon-shaped brooch pinned to her coat.
The bacteria formed ribbon-shaped colonies under the microscope.
The chef sliced the carrots into thin, ribbon-shaped strips for the salad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ribbonlike - resembling or similar to a ribbon in shape
