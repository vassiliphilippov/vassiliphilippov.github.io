warg

noun
fantasy wolf creature
Pronunciation: / wɑːrɡ / (AmE), / wɔːɡ /; / wɑːɡ / (BrE)
a large, wolf-like creature in fantasy stories, or an old word for a wolf

Examples

Legends say that a warg once guarded the mountain pass from intruders.
The villagers feared the howl of the warg echoing through the darkness.
