booster

noun
shoplifter; store thief
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːstər / (AmE), / ˈbuːstə / (BrE)
a thief who steals goods from a shop, especially by concealing them; a shoplifter

Examples

Store booster

The police caught the booster as he tried to leave the store with stolen electronics.
