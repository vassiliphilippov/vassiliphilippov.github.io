reverse

adjective
relating to backward gear in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrs / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs / (BrE)
(of a gear in a motor vehicle) causing the vehicle to move backward.

Examples

Reverse position
He shifted into reverse to back out of the garage.
Make sure the car is not in reverse before starting the engine.
The truck was stuck because it would only go in reverse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forward - (of a gear) causing the vehicle to move ahead
