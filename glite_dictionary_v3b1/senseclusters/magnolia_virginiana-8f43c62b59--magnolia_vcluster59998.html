Magnolia virginiana

noun
a North American shrub or small tree; sweetbay magnolia
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnoʊliə vɜrˌdʒɪˈneɪnə / (AmE), / mæɡˈnəʊliə vɜːdʒɪˈneɪnə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States, with small, fragrant white flowers, commonly called sweetbay magnolia.
