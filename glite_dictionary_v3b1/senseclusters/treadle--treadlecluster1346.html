treadle

noun
foot-operated lever
9.0 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtred.əl/ (AmE), /ˈtred.əl/ (BrE)
a lever operated by the foot, typically used to drive a machine such as a sewing machine or loom

Examples

Sewing machine treadle
After years of use, the treadle on the antique lathe needed repair.
He pressed the treadle to keep the loom working smoothly.
The old sewing machine was powered by a treadle that moved up and down under her foot.
