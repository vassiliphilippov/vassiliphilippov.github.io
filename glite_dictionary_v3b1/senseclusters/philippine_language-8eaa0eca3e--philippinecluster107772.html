Philippine language

noun
official language of the Philippines (based on Tagalog)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪlɪˈpin ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of the Philippines, based primarily on Tagalog and incorporating elements from other Philippine languages

Examples

The Philippine language Filipino
Filipino is the national Philippine language, taught in schools across the country.
Many people speak English as well as the Philippine language in daily life.
The Philippine language has evolved by incorporating words from other regional languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Filipino - the national language of the Philippines, based on Tagalog
  • degree lower: Tagalog - an Austronesian language spoken as a first language by a quarter of the population of the Philippines
