  • fusion bomb

    Produces energy by joining light atomic nuclei, sometimes including the lightest ones
    vs hydrogen bomb: A nuclear weapon that produces energy by fusing light atomic nuclei, such as hydrogen, at extremely high temperatures, resulting in an extremely powerful explosion.
