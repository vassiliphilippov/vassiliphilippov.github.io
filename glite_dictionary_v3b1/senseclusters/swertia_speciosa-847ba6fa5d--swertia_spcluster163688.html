Swertia speciosa

noun
a tall northwestern U.S. herb with white-green flowers
Pronunciation: / swɚˈtiː.ə spiˈsoʊ.sə / (AmE), / swɜːˈtiː.ə spiˈsəʊ.sə / (BrE)
A tall herb native to the northwestern United States, with panicles of white flowers tinged with green; sometimes classified within the genus Swertia.
