balsam herb

noun
aromatic Eurasian herb used for cooking and potpourri
6.5 9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑl.səm hɝb / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.səm hɜːb / (BrE)
A tansy-scented Eurasian perennial herb with buttonlike yellow flowers, used as a potherb, salad green, or for potpourri, tea, or flavoring.

Examples

Fresh balsam herb leaves
Fresh balsam herb leaves can add a unique flavor to salads or teas.

The balsam herb grows well in both sunny and partially shaded areas.
