chimneypiece

noun
shelf above fireplace
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪm.niˌpis / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪm.niˌpiːs / (BrE)
A shelf above a fireplace, usually projecting from the wall and often decorated.

Examples

A chimneypiece shelf
Family photos lined the chimneypiece, giving the room a warm and personal touch.
She placed the old clock carefully on the chimneypiece above the fireplace.
The marble chimneypiece was the focal point of the living room.
