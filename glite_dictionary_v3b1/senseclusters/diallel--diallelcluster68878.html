diallel

adjective
all genetic crosses
9.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈæl.əl/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈæl.əl/ (BrE)
involving all possible crosses among a set of genotypes or lines in genetics experiments

Examples

Diallel cross experiment
The researchers designed a diallel experiment to analyze the inheritance patterns among the plant varieties.
A diallel analysis can reveal which genetic lines contribute most to desirable traits.
In a diallel cross, every genotype is crossed with every other genotype in the group.
