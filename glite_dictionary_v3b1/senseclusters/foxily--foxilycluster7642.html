foxily

adverb
in a sly, cunning manner
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːksɪli / (AmE), / ˈfɒksɪli / (BrE)
In a sly or cunning manner, resembling that of a fox.

Examples

To smile foxily
He smiled foxily, clearly planning something that he hadn't revealed.
She foxily diverted attention away from her real intentions during the meeting.
The detective watched foxily from the shadows, waiting for the right moment to intervene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftily - in a clever, cunning, or crafty manner
  • antonym: openly - in an honest or straightforward manner
