facia

noun
fibrous tissue band in the body

Pronunciation: /ˈfeɪ.ʃə/ /; / /ˈfeɪ.siː.ə/ /; / /ˈfæ.ʃə/ (AmE), /ˈfeɪ.ʃə/ /; / /ˈfeɪ.siː.ə/ /; / /ˈfæ.ʃə/ (BrE)
A sheet or band of fibrous connective tissue that separates, supports, or binds together muscles, organs, or other structures in the body.

Examples
