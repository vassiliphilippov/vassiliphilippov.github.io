valedictory address

noun
formal farewell speech at graduation
Pronunciation: / ˌvæləˈdɪktəri əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ˌvalɪˈdɪktəri əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
a formal farewell speech, especially one delivered by an outstanding member of a graduating class during a graduation ceremony

Examples

The valedictory address speech
Her valedictory address touched on the challenges the class had overcome during the past four years.
Many parents were moved by the inspiring valedictory address given by the top graduate.
The student council president delivered a heartfelt valedictory address at the graduation ceremony.
