whiteboard

verb
to brainstorm visually
4.1
Pronunciation: /waɪt.ˈbɑːrd/ /; / /waɪt.ˈbɔːrd/ (AmE), /waɪt.ˈbɔːd/ (BrE)
to plan, develop, or explain ideas by writing or drawing on a whiteboard, often collaboratively.

Examples

To whiteboard ideas
The team will whiteboard their project ideas during the meeting to visualize possible solutions.
We often whiteboard complex problems to make them easier to understand for everyone.
Before starting the new campaign, they whiteboarded their strategy together in the conference room.
