humaniform

adjective
human-like shape
Pronunciation: / hjuːˈmænɪfɔːrm / (AmE), / hjuːˈmænɪfɔːm / (BrE)

Examples

Humaniform robot
The new android has a humaniform design to make interactions more comfortable for people.
Scientists are developing humaniform robots to assist in elderly care facilities.
