high explosive

noun
powerful, fast-acting chemical explosive
4.0
Pronunciation: / haɪ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv / (AmE), / haɪ ɪkˈspləʊsɪv / (BrE)
a powerful chemical explosive that detonates at a very high rate, producing a sudden, violent release of gas and energy

Examples

A high explosive charge

The military stores high explosives in secured bunkers to prevent accidental detonation.
