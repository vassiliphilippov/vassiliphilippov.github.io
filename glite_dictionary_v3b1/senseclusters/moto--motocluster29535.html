moto

noun
informal motorcycle
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəʊ / (BrE)
an informal word for a motorcycle, a two-wheeled motor vehicle powered by an engine

Examples

Ride a moto
He parked his moto outside the café before meeting his friends.
She loves riding her moto along the coastal roads on weekends.
The city streets were filled with the sound of motos weaving through traffic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: car - a four-wheeled motor vehicle for transporting people
  • synonym: motorcycle - a two-wheeled motor vehicle
  • degree lower: scooter - a small two-wheeled vehicle with a step-through frame
