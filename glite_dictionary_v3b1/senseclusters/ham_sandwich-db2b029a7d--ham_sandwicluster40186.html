ham sandwich

noun
sandwich with ham filling
Pronunciation: / hæm ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ham ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a sandwich with sliced ham as its main filling, usually served between two slices of bread

Examples

A ham sandwich with mustard
He ordered a ham sandwich with mustard for lunch.
I made myself a ham sandwich before heading out.
Would you like a ham sandwich or a salad for your meal?

  • degree higher: club sandwich - multi-layered sandwich with meat, bacon, lettuce, and tomato
