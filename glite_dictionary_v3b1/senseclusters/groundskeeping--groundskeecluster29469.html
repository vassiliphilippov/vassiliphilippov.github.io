groundskeeping

noun
outdoor maintenance work
3.7
Pronunciation: /graʊndz.ˈkiː.pɪŋ/ (AmE), /graʊndz.ˈkiː.pɪŋ/ (BrE)
the activity of maintaining and caring for the outdoor areas of a property, such as lawns and gardens

Examples

Groundskeeping work
He found a summer job in groundskeeping at the local park.
The university employs several people for groundskeeping to keep the campus looking beautiful.
Good groundskeeping can make a big difference in the appearance of a property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landscaping - the process of making a yard or other piece of land more attractive by altering the existing design
