xenotime

noun
a brownish-yellow yttrium phosphate mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈziːnəˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈzɛn.ə.taɪm / (BrE)
A brown to yellow mineral that is a crystalline phosphate of yttrium, often found in granitic rocks.

Examples

Brown xenotime mineral
Collectors prize xenotime specimens for their distinctive color and crystal habit.
Rare earth elements can be extracted from minerals such as xenotime.
The geologist discovered a crystal of xenotime embedded within the granite.
