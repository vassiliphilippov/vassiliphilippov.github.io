EBITDA

noun
financial measure of profit before certain deductions
8.1
Pronunciation: /iː.ˈbɪt.dɑː/ /; / /ə.ˈbɪt.dɑː/ /; / /e.ˈbɪt.dɑː/ (AmE), /iː.ˈbɪt.dɑː/ /; / /ə.ˈbɪt.dɑː/ /; / /e.ˈbɪt.dɑː/ (BrE)
Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization; a financial measure used to assess a company's operating performance.

Examples
