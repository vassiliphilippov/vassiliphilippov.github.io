profiteering

noun
unfair profit-making
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːfɪˈtɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌprɒfɪˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making unfair or excessive profits, especially by taking advantage of a crisis or emergency

Examples

War profiteering
The government introduced strict laws to prevent profiteering during the food shortage.
Many people accused the company of profiteering after prices soared following the hurricane.
Authorities are investigating reports of profiteering in the sale of medical supplies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploitation - the act of using someone or something unfairly for benefit
  • antonym: fairness - the quality of treating people equally or without cheating
