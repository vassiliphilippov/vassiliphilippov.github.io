mulberry

adjective
deep reddish‑purple colour
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.bər.iː/ /; / /ˈmʌl.briː/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.bə.riː/ /; / /ˈmʌl.briː/ (BrE)
A deep reddish‑purple colour, similar to the dark hue of ripe mulberry fruit; used to describe fabrics, paints, and cosmetics.

Examples

Mulberry color
She bought a mulberry dress for the wedding because the colour complemented her skin tone.
The designer used mulberry accents to bring warmth and depth to the room's neutral palette.
He chose a bold mulberry lipstick that matched the autumnal tones of his blazer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plum - a deep purple colour similar to mulberry, often used in fashion and design
