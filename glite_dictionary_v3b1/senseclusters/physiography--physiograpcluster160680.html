physiography

noun
study of earth's physical features
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪziˈɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌfɪziˈɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the physical features of the earth's surface, including landforms, climate, and soils

Examples

Study of physiography
Advances in physiography have improved our understanding of floodplain development.
He published several papers on the physiography of the Appalachian Mountains.
The course in physiography included field trips to study various landforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geomorphology - the study of the origin and evolution of landforms
  • synonym: topography - the arrangement of the natural and artificial physical features of an area
