audible

adjective
able to be heard
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːdəbl / (BrE)
able to be heard; loud enough to be perceived by the ear.

Examples

Audible sound
Her voice was barely audible over the noise of the crowd.
The announcement was clearly audible throughout the entire building.
There was an audible sigh of relief when the exam ended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inaudible - impossible to hear; not able to be heard
