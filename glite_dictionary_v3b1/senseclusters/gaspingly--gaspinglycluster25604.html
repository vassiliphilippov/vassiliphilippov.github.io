gaspingly

adverb
with gasping breaths
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæspɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːspɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves gasping or taking short, quick breaths

Examples

Breathing gaspingly
After climbing the steep hill, he spoke gaspingly to his friends.
The runner finished the race gaspingly, struggling to catch her breath.
He laughed gaspingly after the long, intense workout.
