uglification

noun
making ugly
Pronunciation: / ˌʌɡlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌɡlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something ugly or less attractive

Examples

The uglification of buildings
Many residents opposed the uglification of the park by the addition of large billboards.
The architect was blamed for the uglification of the once charming neighborhood.
Some people worry that rapid development leads to the uglification of city centers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beautification - the process of making something beautiful or more attractive
