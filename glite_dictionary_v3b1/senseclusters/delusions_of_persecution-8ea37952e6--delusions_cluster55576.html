delusions of persecution

noun
false belief that others intend to harm you
Pronunciation: / dəˈluːʒənz ʌv pərˌsɛˈkjuːʒən / (AmE), / dɪˈluːʒənz əv pɜːsɪˈkjuːʃən / (BrE)
the false belief, typically associated with paranoia, that others are intent on harming, harassing, or conspiring against one

Examples

Delusions of persecution belief
Her delusions of persecution led her to distrust even her closest friends and family members.
People with schizophrenia may experience delusions of persecution, believing that others are plotting against them.
The patient reported persistent delusions of persecution, fearing that coworkers were conspiring to harm him.
