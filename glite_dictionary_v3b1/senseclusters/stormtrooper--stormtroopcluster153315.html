stormtrooper

noun
elite or strict soldier
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrmtɹuːpər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmtruːpə / (BrE)

Examples

Elite stormtrooper

Historically, the term stormtrooper referred to elite German soldiers in World War I.
