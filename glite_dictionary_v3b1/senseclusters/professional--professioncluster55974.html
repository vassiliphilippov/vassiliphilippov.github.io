professional

noun
athlete who plays for pay
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənəl / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl / (BrE)
An athlete who competes for payment rather than as an amateur.

Examples

Professional athlete
After years as an amateur, she finally became a professional tennis player.
It's hard for amateurs to compete against professionals in such a demanding sport.
The team signed several new professionals in the off-season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - an athlete who does not get paid to compete
  • synonym: pro - informal synonym for 'professional' (athlete)
