obliquity

noun
slanted angle
8.9
Pronunciation: / əˈblɪkwəti / (AmE), / əˈblɪkwɪti / (BrE)
the state of being at an angle, slanted, or not straight

Examples

Roof obliquity
The architect designed the roof with a noticeable obliquity.
The Earth's obliquity affects the changing of the seasons.
The artist used the obliquity of the lines to create a sense of movement in the painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclination - a slant or slope from a straight position
  • antonym: straightness - the quality of being straight or not curved
