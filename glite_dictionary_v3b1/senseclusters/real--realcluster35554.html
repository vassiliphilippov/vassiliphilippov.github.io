real

adverb
very; really (informal intensifier)
6.0
Pronunciation: / riːəl / (AmE), / rɪəl / (BrE)
(informal) Used as an intensifier meaning 'very' or 'really', especially before adjectives and adverbs.

Examples

Real easy answer
