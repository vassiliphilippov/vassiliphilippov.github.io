midshipwoman

noun
female naval officer in training
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌʃɪpˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmɪdʃɪpˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female officer in training in the navy, holding a temporary rank similar to that of a midshipman

Examples
