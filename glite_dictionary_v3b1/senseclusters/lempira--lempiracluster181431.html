lempira

noun
currency unit of Honduras
Pronunciation: / lɛmˈpɪrə / (AmE), / lɛmˈpɪərə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Honduras, equal to 100 centavos

Examples

One lempira coin
Honduran prices are usually displayed in lempira rather than U.S. dollars.
She exchanged her dollars for lempira at the airport currency counter.
The souvenir cost 150 lempira at the local market.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: centavo - a fractional monetary unit (one hundredth) of the lempira and some other currencies
