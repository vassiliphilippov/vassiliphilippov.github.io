xylitol

noun
sugar substitute
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪlɪˌtɔl / (AmE), / ˈzɪlɪtɒl /; / ˈzaɪlɪtɒl / (BrE)
a sweet-tasting sugar substitute (sugar alcohol) used in foods, dental products, and medicines, often to help prevent tooth decay
