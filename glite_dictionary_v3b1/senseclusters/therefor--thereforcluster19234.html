therefor

adverb
for that; for it (formal/legal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈðɛr.fɔr / (AmE), / ˈðeə.fɔː / (BrE)
(formal, legal) For that or for it; referring to something previously mentioned, especially in legal contexts.

Examples

A receipt therefor
Payment was sent with the invoice, and a receipt therefor was issued.
The buyer returned the goods and requested a refund therefor.
The contract specified the obligations and the consideration therefor.
