reviver

noun
one who restores
6.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvaɪvər / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings something back to life, use, or popularity

Examples

The reviver of spirit
The new principal became the reviver of school spirit by organizing exciting events.
As a reviver of classic recipes, he brought forgotten dishes back to the menu.
The festival owes its success to the reviver who reintroduced it after many years.
The reviver of the ancient language worked tirelessly to teach new generations.
She was seen as the reviver of traditional crafts in her community.
A good leader can be the reviver of hope during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destroyer - a person or thing that causes destruction or ends something
  • synonym: rescuer - a person who saves someone or something from danger or harm
  • synonym: restorer - a person who brings something back to its original condition
