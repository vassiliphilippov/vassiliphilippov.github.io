sativa

noun
cultivated species name
Pronunciation: / səˈtiːvə / (AmE), / səˈtiːvə / (BrE)
a species name in botanical Latin meaning 'cultivated', used in the scientific names of certain plants.

Examples

Sativa species name
Oryza sativa is the scientific name for the common rice plant.
The label Cannabis sativa refers to a species of cannabis known for its tall growth.
Many cultivated plants, such as Allium sativa, use this term in their scientific names.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indica - a species name in botanical Latin meaning 'from India', used in plant names
