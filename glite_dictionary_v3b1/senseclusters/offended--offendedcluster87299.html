offended

adjective
emotionally hurt or annoyed
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛndɪd / (AmE), / əˈfɛndɪd / (BrE)
feeling hurt, upset, or annoyed, often because of something someone has said or done

Examples

Feel offended by words
After the comment, several members of the group felt offended and left early.
He looked offended when they dismissed his suggestion without any discussion.
Maria was clearly offended by his careless remark at dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulted - feeling disrespected or offended by someone's words or actions
  • antonym: pleased - feeling happy or satisfied
