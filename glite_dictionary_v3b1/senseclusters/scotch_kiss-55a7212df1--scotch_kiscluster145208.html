Scotch kiss

noun
butterscotch candy kiss
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ kɪs / (AmE), / skɒtʃ kɪs / (BrE)
A confection made of butterscotch candy shaped like a kiss, sometimes with a marshmallow center.

Examples

A Scotch kiss candy
He handed me a Scotch kiss wrapped in gold foil as a small treat.
She remembered her grandmother always keeping a jar of Scotch kisses on the counter.
The bakery sells homemade Scotch kisses filled with creamy marshmallow.
