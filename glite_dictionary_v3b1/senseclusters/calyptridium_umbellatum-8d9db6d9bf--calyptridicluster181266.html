Calyptridium umbellatum

noun
a western North American flowering plant species
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɪptrɪdiəm ʌmˈbɛlətəm / (AmE), / kəˌlɪptrɪdɪəm ʌmˈbɛlətəm / (BrE)
A plant species in western North America with pink clusters of densely packed flowers on prostrate stems, resembling upturned pads of cats' feet, typically found in coniferous forests.

Examples

Calyptridium umbellatum pink flowers
