Salafi

adjective
of early Islamic tradition
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑːfi / (AmE), / səˈlɑːfi / (BrE)
relating to Salafism or to the beliefs and practices of the earliest generations of Muslims.

Examples

Salafi beliefs
The group follows a strict Salafi interpretation of Islamic teachings.
Some communities have adopted Salafi practices to emulate the earliest Muslims.
The scholar is known for his Salafi views on religious law and tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthodox - following traditional or established beliefs
