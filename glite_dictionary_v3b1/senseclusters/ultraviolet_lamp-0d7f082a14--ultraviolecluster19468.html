ultraviolet lamp

noun
lamp emitting ultraviolet radiation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt læmp / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt læmp / (BrE)
a lamp that emits ultraviolet radiation, often used in scientific, medical, or industrial applications

Examples
