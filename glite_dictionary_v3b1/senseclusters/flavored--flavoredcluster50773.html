flavored

adjective
influenced by quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvərd / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
having a particular quality, influence, or character, often used figuratively to describe something affected by a certain style or element

Examples

Flavored story
The novel is flavored with humor that makes it enjoyable for all ages.
His comments were flavored by his experiences growing up abroad.
The documentary has a distinctly political flavored perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tinged - slightly colored or influenced by something
  • antonym: unaffected - not influenced or changed by something
