noser

noun
one who sniffs
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊzər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzə / (BrE)
a person or animal that smells or sniffs things, especially to locate or examine something by scent

A noser finds truffles

As a skilled noser, the dog quickly found the missing keys in the garden.
The wine expert, known as a talented noser, could identify subtle aromas in every glass.
