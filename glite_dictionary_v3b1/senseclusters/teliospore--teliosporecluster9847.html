teliospore

noun
resting spore of rust fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈlaɪ.əˌspɔr / (AmE), / tɛˈlɪ.əˌspɔː / (BrE)
a thick-walled resting spore formed in the final stage of the life cycle of certain fungi, such as rust fungi
