negativity

noun
habitual skepticism or disagreeableness
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛɡəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌnɛɡəˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
a tendency to be skeptical, disagreeable, or resistant to suggestions or commands, often characterized by pessimism or denial

Examples

Constant negativity
Her constant negativity made it difficult for the team to stay motivated.
His negativity about every proposal was wearing down their enthusiasm.
You need to let go of negativity if you want to enjoy life more.
