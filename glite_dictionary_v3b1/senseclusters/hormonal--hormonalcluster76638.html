hormonal

adjective
relating to hormones
Pronunciation: / hɔrˈmoʊnəl / (AmE), / hɔːˈməʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to or affected by hormones, or caused by the presence or action of hormones

Examples

Hormonal balance
Certain medications can disrupt the body's natural hormonal balance.

Teenagers often experience hormonal changes during puberty.
