endue

verb
give a quality or ability to
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdu / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjuː / (BrE)
to provide someone or something with a particular quality, ability, or characteristic

Examples

To endue with power
Her voice is endued with a power that moves all who hear it.
The author endues his characters with deep emotional complexity.
The legend says the gods endued the hero with extraordinary strength.
