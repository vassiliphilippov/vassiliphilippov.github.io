tick

noun
a metallic tapping sound
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɪk / (AmE), / tɪk / (BrE)
a short, light, metallic tapping sound, especially one produced by a clock or similar mechanism

Examples

Clock tick
He listened to the tick of the old grandfather clock in the quiet room.
I could hear the tick of my watch as I waited nervously for the interview to start.
The tick of the metronome helped her keep time while practicing the piano.

Related Terms

  • synonym: click - a short, sharp sound like that made by a switch or latch
