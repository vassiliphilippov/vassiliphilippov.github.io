Synonyms

  • milliard
    9.9 0.01/m
    milliard is an older British word for 1,000,000,000 used in formal lists, while billion now commonly names the same number.
  • one thousand million
    1.0 0.00/m
    one thousand million spells out the number with nine zeros, while billion is the shorter word commonly used in speech and writing.
  • one million million
    4.3 0.00/m
