reassess

verb
to evaluate again or reconsider
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈsɛs / (BrE)

Examples

To reassess the case

It's important to reassess your goals periodically to stay on track.
The company will reassess its policies in response to customer feedback.
