-ary

x
noun-forming suffix
Pronunciation: / ˌɛri / (AmE), / ˌəri / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns meaning a collection, body, or set of things or writings.

Examples

Collection with -ary ending
A glossary is a collection of terms and their definitions found at the end of a book.
The word 'library' uses the suffix -ary to indicate a place where books are kept.
A 'dictionary' is another example of a noun formed with -ary to mean a collection of words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -ory - a suffix forming nouns or adjectives, often denoting a place or relating to
