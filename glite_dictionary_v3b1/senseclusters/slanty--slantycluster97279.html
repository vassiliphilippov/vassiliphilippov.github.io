slanty

adjective
biased or partial
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈslænti / (AmE), / ˈslɑːnti / (BrE)
presenting information in a way that shows bias or favors one side over another

Examples

Slanty reporting
The news channel was criticized for its slanty coverage of the election results.
Some readers found the report too slanty to be considered objective journalism.
Teachers encourage students to avoid slanty arguments in their essays and present balanced views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biased - showing an unfair preference for or against something
  • antonym: impartial - treating all sides equally; not biased
