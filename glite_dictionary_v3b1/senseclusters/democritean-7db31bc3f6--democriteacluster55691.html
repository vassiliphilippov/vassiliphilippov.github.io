Democritean

adjective
of Democritus
Pronunciation: / dɪˌmɑːkrɪˈtiːən / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒkrɪˈtiːən / (BrE)
relating to the ancient Greek philosopher Democritus or his philosophical ideas, especially about atoms

Examples

Democritean theory of atoms
The Democritean theory of atoms laid the groundwork for later scientific discoveries.
Many scholars have studied the Democritean approach to understanding the natural world.
Her research focused on the influence of Democritean ideas in early modern philosophy.
