unfitness

noun
state of not being suitable
9.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose or position

Examples

Unfitness for the job
Concerns about the candidate's unfitness were raised during the interview process.
Physical unfitness can prevent applicants from entering certain professions.
The committee cited his unfitness for the role due to lack of experience.
