mousey

adjective
infested with mice
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsi / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsi / (BrE)
(of a place) infested with mice; having many mice present.

Examples

Mousey basement
That basement smells musty and appears a bit mousey after months of neglect.
The abandoned warehouse became increasingly mousey as winter set in.
We had to move out because the old apartment was just too mousey for comfort.
