alcohol-soluble

adjective
able to be dissolved in alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈælkoʊˌhɔl ˈsɑljəbəl / (AmE), / ˈælkəhɒl ˈsɒljʊbəl / (BrE)
(of a substance) able to be dissolved in alcohol.

Examples

Alcohol-soluble vitamins
Certain vitamins are alcohol-soluble and dissolve readily in ethanol.
The laboratory determined that the compound was alcohol-soluble through a series of tests.
The pigment is alcohol-soluble, making it easy to remove from most surfaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: water-soluble - able to be dissolved in water
