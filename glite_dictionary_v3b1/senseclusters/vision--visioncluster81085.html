vision

verb
to imagine the future
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
to imagine or form a mental picture of a possible future or goal

Examples

To vision the future
The team spent the afternoon visioning what their company could look like in five years.
She likes to vision her goals before making plans.
During the workshop, participants were encouraged to vision a better future for their community.
She visioned a future full of possibilities.
The architect visions a new city skyline in his designs.
He visioned himself succeeding in his chosen career path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: envision - to picture something in your mind, especially a future event
  • synonym: imagine - to form a mental image or concept of something
