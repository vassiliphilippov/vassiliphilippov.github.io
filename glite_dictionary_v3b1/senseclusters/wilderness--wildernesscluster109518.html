wilderness

noun
a confusing profusion or mass
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪldərnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪldənəs / (BrE)
A large or confusing mass of things, making it difficult to see or understand individual parts.

Examples

Wilderness of papers
Documents were lost in a wilderness of paperwork.
His ideas got buried in a wilderness of technical jargon.
The city skyline was a wilderness of glass and steel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumble - an untidy or confusing mixture
