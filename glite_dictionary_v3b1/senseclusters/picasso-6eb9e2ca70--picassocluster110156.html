Picasso

noun
artwork by Picasso
Pronunciation: / pɪˈkɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈkæsəʊ / (BrE)
a work of art, especially a painting or sculpture, created by Pablo Picasso

Examples

A Picasso painting
The museum recently acquired a rare Picasso for its modern art collection.
She dreamed of owning a Picasso ever since she studied art history in college.
A famous Picasso was displayed at the international exhibition last year.
