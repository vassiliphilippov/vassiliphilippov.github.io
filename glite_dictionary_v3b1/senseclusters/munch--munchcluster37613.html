munch

noun
a large bite of food
1.2
Pronunciation: / mʌntʃ / (AmE), / mʌntʃ / (BrE)
a large bite or mouthful of food taken while chewing noisily or enthusiastically

Examples

A big munch
Between munches, he managed to explain the movie plot.
She took a big munch of the crisp apple and smiled.
With each noisy munch, the cookies on the plate disappeared quickly.
