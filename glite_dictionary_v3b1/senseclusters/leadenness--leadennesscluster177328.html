leadenness

noun
heaviness or dullness
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlɛd.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being heavy, dull, or lacking energy or liveliness

Examples

Emotional leadenness
A sense of leadenness settled over the room after the disappointing news.
He struggled to shake off the leadenness in his limbs after waking up.
There was a strange leadenness in the air before the storm began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heaviness - the quality of being heavy
  • antonym: lightness - the quality of being light in weight or feeling
  • synonym: sluggishness - the quality of being slow or lacking energy
