drey

noun
squirrel's nest
Pronunciation: / dreɪ / (AmE), / dreɪ / (BrE)
a nest built by a squirrel, typically made of twigs and leaves, usually found in trees

Examples

A drey in tree
After the storm, the drey was still intact, sheltering the young squirrels.
In winter, you might spot a large drey high in the branches of an oak.
The squirrel carried leaves up the tree to build its drey.
