blowgun

noun
tube for shooting darts by blowing
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌɡʌn / (BrE)
A tube for shooting darts or other projectiles by blowing through it.

Examples

A blowgun tube
He fashioned a homemade blowgun from a piece of bamboo.
The tribe demonstrated their skill with a blowgun during the ceremony.
Using a blowgun, she quietly darted the animal for research purposes.
