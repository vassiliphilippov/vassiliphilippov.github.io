Zoysia

noun
type of warm-climate lawn grass
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊziə /; / ˈzɔɪ.zi.ə / (AmE), / ˈzɔɪziə /; / ˈzɔɪ.zɪ.ə / (BrE)
a type of low-growing grass, originally from Asia and New Zealand, often used for lawns in warm climates
