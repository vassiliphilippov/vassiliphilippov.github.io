Orphism

noun
art movement or mystical beliefs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrfɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːfɪzəm / (BrE)
a movement in art emphasizing pure abstraction and bright colors, or a set of mystical beliefs related to Orpheus in Greek mythology

Examples

Orphism art movement
The artist was inspired by Orphism to use vivid colors and abstract shapes in her paintings.
Many scholars study Orphism to understand ancient Greek religious practices.
The museum's new exhibit explores the influence of Orphism on modern art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mysticism - belief in spiritual truths beyond ordinary understanding
