silky tamarin

noun
golden South American monkey with silky mane
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki ˈtæmərɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ˈtamərɪn / (BrE)
A small golden South American monkey with long, silky hair forming a mane.

Examples

The silky tamarin monkey
Conservation efforts have increased to help protect the endangered silky tamarin.
The silky tamarin is known for its golden fur and striking mane.
Wildlife researchers observed a group of silky tamarins in the Amazon rainforest.
