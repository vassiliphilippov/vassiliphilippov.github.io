rebar

verb
to reinforce with steel
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbɑː / (BrE)
to strengthen or reinforce a structure, especially concrete, by inserting steel reinforcing bars

Examples

To rebar concrete
The engineers decided to rebar the foundation to ensure the building's stability.
Workers will rebar the bridge supports before pouring the concrete.
It is important to rebar any large concrete structure to prevent cracks over time.
