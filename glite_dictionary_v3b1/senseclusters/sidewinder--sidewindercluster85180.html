sidewinder

noun
a rattlesnake of southwestern US deserts
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌwʌɪndə / (BrE)
a small pale-colored rattlesnake (Crotalus cerastes) found in the deserts of the southwestern United States, known for its distinctive sideways movement.

Examples

Desert sidewinder
I spotted a sidewinder while hiking in Arizona last spring.
The sidewinder is known for slithering sideways across the hot desert sands.
The unique movement of the sidewinder helps it stay cool in extreme temperatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rattlesnake - any venomous snake of the genera Crotalus and Sistrurus, noted for its rattle-tipped tail
