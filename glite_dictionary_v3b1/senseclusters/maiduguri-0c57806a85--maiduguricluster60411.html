Maiduguri

proper noun
city in northeastern Nigeria
Pronunciation: / maɪˈduːɡʊri / (AmE), / maɪˈduːɡʊri / (BrE)
a city in northeastern Nigeria, known as an agricultural trading center and the capital of Borno State
