nipah

noun
virus or palm tree
Pronunciation: / ˈniːpɑː / (AmE), / ˈniːpɑː / (BrE)
a virus that causes severe disease in humans and animals, or a type of tropical palm tree

Examples

The nipah virus
The nipah virus outbreak led to strict health measures in the region.
Researchers are studying how nipah spreads between animals and humans.
The villagers harvested fruit from the nipah palm near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palm - a tropical tree with large leaves
  • synonym: virus - a microscopic infectious agent
