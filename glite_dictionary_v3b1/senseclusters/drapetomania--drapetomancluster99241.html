drapetomania

noun
discredited racist diagnosis
Pronunciation: / ˌdreɪpɪtoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌdreɪpɪtəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
a discredited historical term for a supposed mental illness that caused enslaved people to want to escape captivity

Examples

19th-century drapetomania
The term drapetomania was used in the 19th century to justify the oppression of enslaved people.
Modern scholars reject drapetomania as a racist and unscientific concept.
Historians now study drapetomania to understand the misuse of science in the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudoscience - a belief or practice mistakenly regarded as scientific
  • synonym: racism - prejudice or discrimination based on race
