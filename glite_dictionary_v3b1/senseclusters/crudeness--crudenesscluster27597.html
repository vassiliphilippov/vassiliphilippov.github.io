Synonyms

  • crudity
    8.1 0.11/m
    crudity names blunt, vulgar speech often used in writing, while crudeness is the everyday word for rude, coarse behavior.
  • coarseness
    6.4 0.19/m
    coarseness is rough, unrefined speech or manner that shows lack of polish, while crudeness emphasizes vulgar or tasteless rudeness.
  • vulgarity
    7.4 1.22/m
    vulgarity is speech or behavior that shocks by bad taste or obscenity, while crudeness is broadly rude and lacking refinement.
  • rudeness
    3.0 0.06/m
    rudeness is impolite or disrespectful acts in social settings, while crudeness often adds vulgar or tasteless language to that.
  • boorishness
    8.1 0.03/m
    boorishness is rough, insensitive behavior that ignores others' feelings, while crudeness focuses more on vulgar or tactless speech.
