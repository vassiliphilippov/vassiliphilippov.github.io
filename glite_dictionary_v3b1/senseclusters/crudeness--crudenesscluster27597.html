crudeness

noun
lack of refinement or tact; vulgarity
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːdnəs / (AmE), / ˈkruːdnəs / (BrE)
behavior or speech that is rude, vulgar, or lacking in tact and refinement.

Examples

Crudeness in speech
Despite the crudeness of his manners, he was honest and hardworking.
Many people were offended by the crudeness of the comments made at the meeting.
The comedian's jokes were criticized for their crudeness and lack of sensitivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarseness - lack of delicacy or refinement
  • antonym: refinement - elegance and politeness in manners or speech
  • synonym: vulgarity - rudeness or offensiveness; lack of refinement in manners or speech
