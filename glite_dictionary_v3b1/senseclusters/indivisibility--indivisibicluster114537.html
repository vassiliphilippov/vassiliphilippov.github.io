indivisibility

noun
impossibility of division
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˌvɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˌvɪzɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to divide or separate into parts

Examples

Indivisibility of rights
The indivisibility of human rights is a core principle in international law.
She spoke about the indivisibility of the team and their shared commitment to success.
Mathematicians discuss the indivisibility of certain numbers in number theory.

Related Terms
