button

noun
on-screen control (computing)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌt.ən/ (AmE), /ˈbʌt.ən/ (BrE)
a small device or icon that you press or click to control a machine, electronic equipment, or computer program

Please press the button to call the elevator.
The alarm will sound if you push this button under the desk.
