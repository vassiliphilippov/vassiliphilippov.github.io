astrocytoma

noun
brain tumor from astrocytes
Pronunciation: / ˌæstroʊsaɪˈtoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌæstrəʊsaɪˈtəʊmə / (BrE)
a type of tumor that develops in the brain or spinal cord from star-shaped glial cells called astrocytes
