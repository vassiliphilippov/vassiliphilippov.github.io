astrocytoma

noun
brain tumor from astrocytes
9.9
Pronunciation: /æs.trə.saɪ.ˈtoʊ.mə/ /; / /æs.troʊ.saɪ.ˈtoʊ.mə/ (AmE), /æs.trə.saɪ.ˈtəʊ.mə/ (BrE)
