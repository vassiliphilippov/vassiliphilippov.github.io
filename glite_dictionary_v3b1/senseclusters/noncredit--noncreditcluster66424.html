noncredit

noun
course without credit
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
a course or class that does not count toward a degree and does not provide academic credit

Examples

A noncredit class
She enrolled in a noncredit to learn basic photography skills.
Many adults take noncredit classes for personal enrichment or job training.
The university offers several noncredit workshops during the summer session.

Related Terms

  • antonym: credit course - a course that counts toward a degree and provides academic credit
