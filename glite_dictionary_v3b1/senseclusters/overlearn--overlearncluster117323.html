overlearn

verb
to practice excessively
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈlɜrn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlɜːn / (BrE)
to practice or study something so much that it is retained very well, even beyond initial mastery

Examples

To overlearn vocabulary
If you overlearn vocabulary, you will remember it even under stress.
The coach asked the team to overlearn their plays to avoid mistakes during the game.
She decided to overlearn the speech so she would not forget any part of it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memorize - to learn something so that you remember it exactly
  • degree lower: review - to go over material again to reinforce learning
