compo

noun
informal school essay
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːm.poʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a short piece of writing, especially a school essay or exercise

Examples

School compo assignment
For homework he wrote a short compo about his summer holiday for English class.
The teacher asked everyone to read their compo aloud to the class.
She struggled to finish her compo before the end of the lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: essay - a short piece of writing on a particular subject
