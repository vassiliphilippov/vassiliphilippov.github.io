mistletoe thrush

noun
a large European thrush that eats mistletoe berries
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ ˌθrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪsəltəʊ ˌθrʌʃ / (BrE)
a large European thrush (Turdus viscivorus) that feeds on mistletoe berries, noted for its spotted breast and loud, melodious song

Examples

The mistletoe thrush sings
Gardeners appreciate the mistletoe thrush for spreading mistletoe by eating its berries.
The mistletoe thrush is often seen in woodlands across Europe during the winter months.
When startled, a mistletoe thrush flies up, flashing its white underwings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrush - a medium-sized songbird with a spotted breast
