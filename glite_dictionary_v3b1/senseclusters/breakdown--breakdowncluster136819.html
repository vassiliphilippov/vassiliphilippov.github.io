A sudden failure of health, particularly mental health, often causing someone to be unable to function normally.

Examples

Mental breakdown
Following his breakdown, he took a leave of absence to recover.
She suffered a breakdown after months of continuous stress at work.
The news of the accident caused a complete breakdown of his mental health.

Synonyms

  • nervous breakdown
    5.3 1.42/m
    nervous breakdown is a severe, often medical episode of mental distress needing treatment, while breakdown is a sudden loss of normal functioning.
  • emotional breakdown
    6.3 0.14/m
    emotional breakdown is an intense wave of feelings that stops someone coping with life, while breakdown often means a sudden mental collapse.
  • crack-up
    7.2 0.08/m
    crack-up often means an informal or joking collapse and laughter, while breakdown describes a serious sudden failure to function mentally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - a sudden failure of health or strength
