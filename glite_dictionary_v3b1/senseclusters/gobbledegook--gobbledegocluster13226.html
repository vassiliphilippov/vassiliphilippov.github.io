gobbledegook

noun
nonsense language
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑːb.əl.dɪ.guːk/ /; / /ˈgɑːb.əl.də.guːk/ (AmE), /ˈgɒb.əl.dɪ.guːk/ /; / /ˈgɒb.əl.də.guːk/ (BrE)
language that is difficult to understand because it is full of jargon, technical terms, or nonsense

Examples

Legal gobbledegook
The legal document was filled with so much gobbledegook that I could not follow it.
He tried to impress us with technical gobbledegook, but nobody understood what he meant.
Whenever politicians speak in gobbledegook, people lose interest quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jargon - special words or expressions used by a particular group, often hard to understand
  • synonym: nonsense - words or language that have no meaning or make no sense
