bang

verb
make a loud, sharp noise
1.9
Pronunciation: / bæŋ / (AmE), / bæŋ / (BrE)
to hit, strike, close, or move something suddenly and noisily, often producing a loud sound

Examples

He banged the door as he left the room in anger.
Please try not to bang the window shut; it's very noisy.
