autophony

noun
hearing own voice loudly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑːfəni / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒfəni / (BrE)
a condition in which a person hears their own voice or other internal bodily sounds (like breathing or heartbeat) unusually loudly
