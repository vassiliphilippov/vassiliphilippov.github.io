ambler

noun
person walking leisurely
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.blɚ / (AmE), / ˈæm.blə / (BrE)
a person who walks at a slow, relaxed pace, especially for pleasure

Examples

A slow ambler
Even as a child, she was more of an ambler than a runner, preferring to take her time.
On holiday, he became a happy ambler, exploring the coastline at his own pace.
The ambler enjoyed the quiet streets of the city on Sunday mornings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stroller - person who walks in a slow, relaxed way
  • synonym: walker - person who walks, especially for exercise or pleasure
