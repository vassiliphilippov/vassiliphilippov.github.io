subtitle

noun
translation text shown in films/TV
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.taɪ.təl / (BrE)
a translation of dialogue in a foreign film or television programme, usually shown as text at the bottom of the screen

Examples

Movie subtitles
He turned on the subtitles because the characters were speaking too quickly.
I prefer watching movies with subtitles rather than dubbed versions.
The French film had English subtitles to help the audience understand the dialogue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caption - text displayed on a screen to provide information
