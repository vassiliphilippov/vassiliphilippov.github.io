redback

noun
venomous Australian spider
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbæk / (BrE)
a small, highly venomous Australian spider with a distinctive red mark on its back

Examples

A redback spider
The redback is often found in sheds and garages across Australia.
She recognized the dangerous redback by the bright red stripe on its back.
Experts warn that a redback bite can be very serious and requires medical attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: funnel-web spider - a highly venomous Australian spider
