osier

noun
flexible willow twig used in weaving
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.ʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.zɪə / (BrE)
a type of willow tree or its flexible branches, used for weaving baskets and furniture

Examples

Fresh osier twigs
He gathered a bundle of fresh osier twigs along the riverbank for weaving.
The artisan skillfully bent the osier into a basket shape.
Wicker furniture is often crafted from osier collected in the spring.
Several pieces of antique furniture were crafted from osier twigs.
The artisan skillfully wove baskets from osier collected along the riverbank.
The flexible nature of osier makes it ideal for traditional basketry.
