jejuni

noun
plural of jejunum
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈdʒuːnaɪ / (AmE), / dʒɪˈdʒuːnaɪ / (BrE)
the plural form of jejunum, referring to the middle section of the small intestine.

Examples

The jejuni examined
The surgeon carefully examined the jejuni for any signs of blockage or disease.
Samples were taken from several jejuni to test for infection.
During the procedure, the doctor noted inflammation in the patient's jejuni.

Related Terms

  • antonym: duodenum - the first section of the small intestine
  • antonym: ileum - the final section of the small intestine
  • synonym: jejunum - the middle section of the small intestine
