tock

verb
to make clock sound
6.0
Pronunciation: / tɑk / (AmE), / tɒk / (BrE)
to make a short, sharp sound like the ticking of a clock or a small striking object

Examples

To tock every second
The old wall clock would tock loudly every second in the quiet room.
As midnight approached, the only sound was the steady tocking of the grandfather clock.
She listened to the clock tock as she waited for the phone to ring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tick - to make a light, regular, clicking sound like a clock
