satiety

noun
state of being full; fullness
9.9 6.4
Pronunciation: / səˈtaɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / səˈtaɪ.ə.ti / (BrE)
the state of being satisfactorily full, especially after eating, so that no more can be taken

Examples

Feeling of satiety
After eating a large meal, a feeling of satiety settled over the group.
He stopped eating not out of willpower, but because satiety made any more food unappealing.
Nutritional experts recommend eating slowly to allow satiety to develop.
