hant

verb
to haunt as a ghost
9.9
Pronunciation: / hænt / (AmE), / hænt / (BrE)
to appear in or visit a place as a ghost, or to trouble someone in ghostly form, especially in dialect use

Examples

To hant a house
Some say the old house is hanted by the spirit of its former owner.
Local legends claim that ghosts hant the abandoned mill every night.
She believed her dreams were hanted by memories she could not escape.
