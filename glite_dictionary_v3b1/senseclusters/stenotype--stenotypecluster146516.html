stenotype

noun
shorthand typing machine
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛnoʊˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊˌtaɪp / (BrE)
a machine used for typing shorthand, especially by court reporters to record speech quickly and accurately

Examples

Stenotype machine
The court reporter operated the stenotype with remarkable speed during the trial.
Learning to use a stenotype requires special training in shorthand techniques.
Modern stenotype machines can connect directly to computers for real-time transcription.
