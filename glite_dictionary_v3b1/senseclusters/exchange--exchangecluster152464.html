exchange

noun
swap of atoms or ions in chemistry
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
A chemical process in which one atom, ion, or group swaps places with another within a molecule or compound.

Examples

Ion exchange
During the reaction, a sodium ion exchange occurs with a potassium ion.
Ion exchange is widely used in water purification systems.
The exchange of hydrogen ions plays a key role in acid-base reactions.
