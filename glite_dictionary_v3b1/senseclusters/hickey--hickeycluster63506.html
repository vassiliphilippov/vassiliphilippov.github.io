hickey

noun
small skin elevation or acne bump
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪki / (AmE), / ˈhɪki / (BrE)
A small, inflamed bump on the skin, such as a pustule or papule, commonly associated with acne.

Examples

A skin hickey
Acne often appears as a hickey or pimple, especially during adolescence.
He was embarrassed by the red hickeys that appeared before his big presentation.
The dermatologist examined the hickey on her forehead and recommended a topical cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papule - a small, raised, solid pimple or swelling on the skin
  • synonym: pustule - a small, inflamed, pus-filled swelling on the skin
