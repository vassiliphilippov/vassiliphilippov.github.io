plum opportunity

noun
a highly desirable or advantageous chance
8.1
Pronunciation: / plʌm ˌɑːpərˈtuːnəti / (AmE), / plʌm ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪti / (BrE)
a very good or especially desirable chance, often one that is rare or highly sought after

Examples

A plum opportunity
He didn't want to miss the plum opportunity to study abroad.
Landing that job was a plum opportunity for her career advancement.
The merger presented a plum opportunity for investors to profit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: golden opportunity - an especially favorable or advantageous chance
  • antonym: missed opportunity - an advantageous chance that was not taken
