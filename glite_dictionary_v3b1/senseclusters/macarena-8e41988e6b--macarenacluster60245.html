Macarena

noun
Spanish dance craze
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈreɪnə / (AmE), / ˌmakəˈreɪnə / (BrE)
a lively Spanish dance performed to a popular song, involving a sequence of arm and hip movements

Examples

Dance the Macarena
At the party, everyone got up to dance the Macarena together.
The Macarena became a worldwide sensation in the 1990s.
Children learned the Macarena for their school talent show performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conga - a lively Latin American dance performed in a line
  • synonym: salsa - a popular Latin American dance with energetic movements
