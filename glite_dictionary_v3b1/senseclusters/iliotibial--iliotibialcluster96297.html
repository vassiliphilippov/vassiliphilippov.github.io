iliotibial

adjective
outer thigh area
Pronunciation: / ˌɪlioʊˈtɪbiəl / (AmE), / ˌɪliəʊˈtɪbiəl / (BrE)
relating to the iliotibial band, a structure running along the outer thigh from the hip to the knee

Examples

The iliotibial band
The iliotibial band can become tight in athletes who run long distances.
Physical therapists often recommend stretches for the iliotibial region to prevent injury.
Pain on the outside of the knee may be related to iliotibial band syndrome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femoral - relating to the thigh or femur
