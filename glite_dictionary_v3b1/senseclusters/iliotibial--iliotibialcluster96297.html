iliotibial

adjective
outer thigh area
9.2
Pronunciation: /ɪ.lɪ.oʊ.ˈtɪ.bɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ɪ.lɪ.əʊ.ˈtɪ.bɪ.əl/ (BrE)
relating to the iliotibial band, a structure running along the outer thigh from the hip to the knee

Examples

Physical therapists often recommend stretches for the iliotibial region to prevent injury.
