microdebrider

noun
surgical cutting tool
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊdɪˈbraɪdər / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊdɪˈbraɪdə / (BrE)
a powered surgical instrument used to cut and remove soft tissue, especially during endoscopic procedures

Examples

A microdebrider tool
The surgeon used a microdebrider to carefully remove tissue during the sinus procedure.
A microdebrider allows for precise cutting and suction in endoscopic surgeries.
Advances in microdebrider technology have improved outcomes in nasal and sinus operations.
