Tractarianism

noun
principles of the Oxford Movement
8.5 9.9
Pronunciation: /træk.ˈter.i.ən.ɪz.əm/ (AmE), /træk.ˈteə.ri.ən.ɪz.əm/ (BrE)
the religious principles and doctrines associated with the Oxford Movement, especially as set forth in the 'Tracts for the Times' published in the early 19th century
