cutthroat

noun
ruthless competitor
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌt.θroʊt/ (AmE), /ˈkʌt.θrəʊt/ (BrE)
a person who acts ruthlessly or without fairness to achieve their goals, especially in business or competition

Examples

A cutthroat competitor

She refused to become a cutthroat, even if it meant slower career progress.
