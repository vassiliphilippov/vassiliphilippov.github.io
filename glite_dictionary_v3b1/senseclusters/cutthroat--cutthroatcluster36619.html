cutthroat

noun
ruthless competitor
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtˌθroʊt / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌθrəʊt / (BrE)
a person who acts ruthlessly or without fairness to achieve their goals, especially in business or competition

Examples

A cutthroat competitor
In the world of finance, a cutthroat will do anything to get ahead of the competition.
The company was known for hiring only the most aggressive cutthroats in the industry.
She refused to become a cutthroat, even if it meant slower career progress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ally - a person who supports or cooperates with another
  • synonym: opponent - someone who competes against or fights another
  • synonym: rival - a person competing with another for the same objective
