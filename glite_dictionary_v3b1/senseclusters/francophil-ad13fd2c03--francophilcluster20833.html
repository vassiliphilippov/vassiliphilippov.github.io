Francophil

noun
admirer of France or French culture
Pronunciation: / ˈfræŋkəˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈfræŋkəʊfɪl / (BrE)
A person who admires France, its people, or its culture.

Examples

A Francophil admirer
As a self-declared Francophil, she spends every summer in Paris.
The restaurant was founded by a devoted Francophil who wanted to bring authentic French cuisine to the city.
Though he lives in London, his Francophil tendencies are evident from his love of French literature and music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Francophile - A person who strongly admires France and French things
  • antonym: Francophobe - A person with a strong dislike or fear of France or French things
