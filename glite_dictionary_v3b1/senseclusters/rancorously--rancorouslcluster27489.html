rancorously

adverb
with deep bitterness
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈræŋkərəsli / (AmE), / ˈræŋkərəsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows deep bitterness or lasting resentment

Examples

To rancorously criticize
He rancorously criticized his opponent during the heated debate.
The two neighbors argued rancorously over the property line for years.
She replied rancorously when asked about the unfair decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amicably - in a friendly and peaceful way
  • synonym: bitterly - in a way that shows strong anger or resentment
  • synonym: resentfully - in a way that shows displeasure or anger about something unfair
