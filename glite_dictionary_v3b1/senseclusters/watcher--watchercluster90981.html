watcher

noun
guard or observer keeping watch
3.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑː.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈwɒ.tʃə/ (BrE)
a person who observes, monitors, or keeps watch over something or someone

Examples

A watcher at the gate
A watcher was posted at the entrance to alert the others of any intruders.
As the appointed watcher, she stayed awake while the rest of the group slept.
The night watcher walked silently along the fence, scanning for any sign of movement.
A storm watcher posted updates as the hurricane approached the coast.
Night watchers often report seeing shooting stars from the hilltop.
The bird watcher quietly observed the rare species through his binoculars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: observer - a person who observes; synonym for watcher
  • synonym: sentinel - a soldier or guard whose job is to stand and keep watch
