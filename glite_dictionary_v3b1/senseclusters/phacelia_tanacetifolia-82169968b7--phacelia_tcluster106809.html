Phacelia tanacetifolia

noun
hairy annual plant with blue or lilac flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈsiːliə ˌtænəsɛˈtɪfoʊliə / (AmE), / fəˈsiːliə ˌtanəsɛˈtɪfəliə / (BrE)
a hairy annual plant native to California and Mexico, with dense clusters of small blue to lilac or mauve flowers, often grown as an ornamental or for attracting pollinators
