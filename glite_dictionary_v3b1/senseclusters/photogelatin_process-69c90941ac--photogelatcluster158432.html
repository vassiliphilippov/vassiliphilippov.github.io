photogelatin process

noun
printing method using gelatin-coated plate
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊdʒəˈlætɪn ˈproʊsɛs / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊdʒəˈlætɪn ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a photomechanical printing method using a glass plate coated with gelatin to transfer an image, sometimes in multiple colors
