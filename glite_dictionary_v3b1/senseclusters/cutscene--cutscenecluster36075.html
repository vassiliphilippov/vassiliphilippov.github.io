cutscene

noun
video game story scene
5.1 0.49/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk åtÀåsiÀên / (AmE), / Ààk åtÀåsiÀên / (BrE)
a non-interactive video sequence in a video game that advances the story or provides information

Examples

A cutscene video
The opening cutscene set the tone for the entire adventure.
After completing the level, a short cutscene revealed the villain's next plan.
Some players choose to skip the cutscene to get back to the action quickly.

The game uses a cutscene to introduce each new character you meet.
