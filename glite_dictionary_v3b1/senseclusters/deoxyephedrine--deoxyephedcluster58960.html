deoxyephedrine

noun
a drug; amphetamine derivative used as a stimulant
7.3
Pronunciation: / diˌɑksiˈɛfəˌdriːn / (AmE), / diˌɒksɪˈɛfɪdriːn / (BrE)
A stimulant drug that is an amphetamine derivative, used medically as a nervous system stimulant and appetite suppressant; also known as methamphetamine or by the trade name Methedrine.

Examples

Deoxyephedrine stimulant drug
Deoxyephedrine is sometimes prescribed under strict medical supervision.
Illicit use of deoxyephedrine can have serious health consequences.
The synthesis of deoxyephedrine requires specialized laboratory equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: methamphetamine - a powerful central nervous system stimulant, also known as crystal meth
