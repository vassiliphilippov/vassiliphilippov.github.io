misfold

noun
incorrect folding
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌfəʊld / (BrE)
an instance of incorrect folding, especially of a protein or material, resulting in an abnormal structure

Examples

A protein misfold
A single misfold in a protein can lead to serious diseases such as Alzheimer's.
The engineer discovered a misfold in the material that weakened the structure.
Researchers are studying how a misfold affects the function of biological molecules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fold - a crease or bend formed by folding
  • synonym: malformation - an abnormal or faulty formation or structure
