9-11

noun
September 11, 2001 terrorist attacks
Pronunciation: / naɪn əˈlɛvən / (AmE), / naɪn ɪˈlɛvən / (BrE)
The terrorist attacks on September 11, 2001, in the United States, when hijacked airplanes were used to target significant buildings.

Examples

9-11 attacks
Every year, memorial ceremonies are held to honor the victims of 9-11.
She watched a documentary about 9-11 and its impact on New York City.
The events of 9-11 changed the world in many ways.
