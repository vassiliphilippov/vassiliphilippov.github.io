fish farm

noun
place where fish are raised for food
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ fɑrm / (AmE), / fɪʃ fɑːm / (BrE)
A place where fish are bred and raised in controlled conditions for commercial purposes, especially for food.

Examples

A fish farm
After visiting the fish farm, the students learned about sustainable aquaculture.
Raising trout in a fish farm requires maintaining clean water and careful feeding routines.
The company operates a fish farm that supplies fresh salmon to restaurants across the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish hatchery - a place where fish eggs are hatched and young fish are reared
  • synonym: fishery - a place where fish are reared and harvested
