scraggle

verb
to make messy marks
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈskræ.gəl/ (AmE), /ˈskræ.gəl/ (BrE)
to draw, write, or mark with uneven, jagged, or untidy strokes

Examples

To scraggle a signature
When nervous, he would scraggle his signature instead of writing it neatly.
The child loved to scraggle pictures all over the margins of her notebook.
Rain made the ink run, causing the words to scraggle across the page.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neaten - to make something tidy or orderly
  • synonym: scrawl - to write in a hurried, careless, or untidy way
  • synonym: scribble - to write or draw carelessly or hurriedly
