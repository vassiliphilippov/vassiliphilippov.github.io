English breakfast tea

noun
a strong black tea blend for breakfast
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈbrɛkfəst ti / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈbrɛkfəst tiː / (BrE)
