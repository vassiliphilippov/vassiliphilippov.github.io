entrada

noun
dramatic entrance
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtrɑːdə / (AmE), / ɛnˈtrɑːdə / (BrE)
a dramatic or notable entrance, especially by a performer or competitor in a public setting

Examples

Grand entrada
The actor made a grand entrada that captivated everyone in the theater.
Her unexpected entrada at the party turned all heads in her direction.
The champion's entrada into the arena was met with thunderous applause.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrance - the act of entering or coming into a place
  • antonym: exit - the act of leaving or going out
