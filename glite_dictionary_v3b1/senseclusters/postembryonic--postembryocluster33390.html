postembryonic

adjective
after embryonic stage
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˌɛm.briˈɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˌɛm.brɪˈɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring after the embryonic stage of development

Examples

Postembryonic development
The butterfly's postembryonic development includes the larval and pupal stages.
Researchers studied the postembryonic growth of fish in different environments.
Certain genes are only active during postembryonic stages in mammals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: embryonic - relating to an embryo or the earliest stage of development
  • synonym: larval - relating to the larva stage after the embryo in some animals
