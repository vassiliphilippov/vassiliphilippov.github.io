unfelt

adjective
not experienced
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɛlt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɛlt / (BrE)
not experienced, perceived, or genuinely felt emotionally or physically

Examples

An unfelt apology
His apology seemed unfelt, leaving everyone unconvinced by his words.
There was an unfelt tension in the room that no one wanted to acknowledge.
The praise she gave sounded unfelt and did not comfort her friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genuine - truly felt or experienced
  • synonym: insincere - not expressing genuine feelings
