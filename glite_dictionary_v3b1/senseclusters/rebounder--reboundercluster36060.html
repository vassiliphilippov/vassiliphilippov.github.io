rebounder

noun
sports practice device
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌbaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbaʊndə / (BrE)
a piece of sports equipment, such as a net or board, that returns a thrown, kicked, or hit ball to the player for practice

Examples

Practice rebounder
The coach set up a rebounder so the players could practice their passing skills alone.
Using a rebounder helps athletes improve their reaction time and accuracy.
He bought a small rebounder to work on his soccer shots in the backyard.
