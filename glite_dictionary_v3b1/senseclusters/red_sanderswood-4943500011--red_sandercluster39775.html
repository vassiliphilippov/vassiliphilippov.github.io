red sanderswood

noun
tree yielding valuable red dyewood
7.5
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈsændərzˌwʊd / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsandəzˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tree native to India and the East Indies, valued for its hard, fragrant timber used in cabinetry, and dark red heartwood used as a dyewood

Examples

Red sanderswood tree
Artisans value red sanderswood not only for its beauty, but also for the natural dye it produces.
