greenware

noun
eco-friendly software
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnwɛr / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnwɛə / (BrE)
software that is distributed on the condition that users take an environmentally friendly action or that is designed to reduce environmental impact

Examples

Greenware recycling app
The company released its new app as greenware, encouraging users to recycle in exchange for free access.
By offering greenware, developers hope to promote positive environmental actions among their users.
Many people appreciate greenware because it combines useful technology with environmental responsibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeware - software that is available free of charge
  • synonym: shareware - software distributed for free on a trial basis
