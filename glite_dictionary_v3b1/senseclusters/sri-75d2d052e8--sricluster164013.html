Sri

noun
South Asian honorific
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʃri / (AmE), / ʃriː / (BrE)
a respectful title or honorific in South Asia, often used before names and associated with prosperity or the Hindu goddess Lakshmi

Examples

Sri Lakshmi
The invitation was addressed to Sri Ram Kumar and his family.
Many temples are dedicated to Sri Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth.
In South Asia, people often use Sri as a mark of respect before a person's name.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mr. - a formal title used before a man's name
  • synonym: Mrs. - a formal title used before a married woman's name
