Camp David

proper noun
U.S. presidential retreat in Maryland
Pronunciation: / kæmp ˈdeɪvɪd / (AmE), / kæmp ˈdeɪvɪd / (BrE)
A country retreat in Maryland used by the President of the United States for meetings and recreation.

Examples

Camp David retreat
Many historic agreements have been negotiated at Camp David throughout the years.
Security at Camp David is extremely tight during presidential visits.
