Scandinavian country

noun
country in Scandinavia
Pronunciation: / skænˌdeɪviˈeɪn ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / skanˌdeɪvɪˈeɪn ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
any of the countries located in Scandinavia, typically Denmark, Norway, and Sweden.

Examples

A Scandinavian country in Europe
Each Scandinavian country has its own unique traditions and cultural heritage.
Norway is a Scandinavian country known for its fjords and mountainous terrain.
Sweden, a Scandinavian country, is renowned for its design and environmental policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Nordic country - any country of the Nordic region, including Scandinavia and other northern countries
