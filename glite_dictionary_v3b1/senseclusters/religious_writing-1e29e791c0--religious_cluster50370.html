religious writing

noun
writing regarded as sacred in a religion
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a text or piece of writing regarded as sacred or authoritative for the worship or teachings of a religion

Examples

Religious writing texts
Many people find comfort and guidance in religious writing during difficult times.
The exhibit featured several historical pieces of religious writing written on parchment.
The scholar spent years studying ancient religious writing from different cultures.
