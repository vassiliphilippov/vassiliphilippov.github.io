right-hand man

noun
most trusted male assistant
2.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /raɪt hænd mæn/ (AmE), /raɪt hænd mæn/ (BrE)
a person who is a trusted assistant or most important support to someone in authority, especially in business or administration

Examples

The right-hand man role
After his promotion, Tim became the CEO’s right-hand man and managed many of the company’s operations.
As the general’s right-hand man, he was included in all the major strategic decisions.
Everyone knew that Lisa was the manager’s right-hand man and could be trusted with important projects.
He has been my right-hand man for over a decade, handling all critical projects with dedication.
She relies on her right-hand man to organize meetings and keep her schedule on track.
When the boss is away, his right-hand man takes charge of all the major decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone in a more senior position
  • synonym: deputy - a person appointed as a substitute with power to act
  • antonym: left-hand man - an assistant who is less prominent or less trusted (rare)
