patent right

noun
exclusive legal right to an invention
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpætənt raɪt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtənt raɪt / (BrE)
the exclusive right granted by a government to an inventor to make, use, or sell an invention for a specified period of time

Obtaining a patent right is crucial for protecting new inventions in the marketplace.
