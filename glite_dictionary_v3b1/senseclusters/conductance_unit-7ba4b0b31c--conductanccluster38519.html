conductance unit

noun
unit measuring electrical conductance
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌktəns ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / kənˈdʌktəns ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a standard unit used to measure a material's ability to conduct an electric current
