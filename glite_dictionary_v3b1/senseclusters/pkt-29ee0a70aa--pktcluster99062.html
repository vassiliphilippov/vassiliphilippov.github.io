Pakistan time zone
Pronunciation: / piː keɪ tiː / (AmE), / piː keɪ tiː / (BrE)
the abbreviation for Pakistan Standard Time (UTC+5), the time zone used in Pakistan

Examples

6:00 PM PKT
The flight will arrive in Karachi at 6:00 PM PKT.
Please confirm if the deadline is set according to PKT or another time zone.
The live broadcast will begin at 8:30 PKT for viewers in Pakistan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: IST - the abbreviation for Indian Standard Time (UTC+5:30)
