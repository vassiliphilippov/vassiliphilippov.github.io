Arabian Sea

proper noun
northwestern arm of the Indian Ocean
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ.bi.ən si / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən siː / (BrE)
the part of the Indian Ocean located between the Arabian Peninsula and the Indian subcontinent

Examples

The Arabian Sea coast
Monsoon rains bring heavy storms to the Arabian Sea each summer.
Several countries share coastlines along the Arabian Sea, including Oman, India, and Pakistan.
The ship sailed for weeks across the Arabian Sea before reaching the port of Mumbai.
