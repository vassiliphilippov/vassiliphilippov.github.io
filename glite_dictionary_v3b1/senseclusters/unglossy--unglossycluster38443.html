unglossy

adjective
not shiny
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡlɑsi / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡlɒsi / (BrE)
not shiny or lacking a smooth, reflective surface

Examples

Unglossy paper
The unglossy pages of the book made it easier to read under bright lights.
She preferred an unglossy lipstick for a more natural appearance.
The artist chose an unglossy finish to give the sculpture a rustic feel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glossy - having a shiny, smooth surface
  • synonym: matte - having a dull, non-shiny surface
