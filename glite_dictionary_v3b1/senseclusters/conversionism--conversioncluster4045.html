conversionism

noun
belief in conversion
9.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜːrʒənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːʃənˌɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in or emphasis on the importance of personal religious conversion

Examples

Religious conversionism
The church's focus on conversionism attracted many new members seeking a personal faith experience.
Some religious movements are known for their strong emphasis on conversionism and personal transformation.
Debates about conversionism often arise in discussions about religious education and outreach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evangelism - the preaching or spreading of the Christian gospel
  • antonym: secularism - the belief that religion should not play a role in government or education
