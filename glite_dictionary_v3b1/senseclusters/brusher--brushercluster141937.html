brusher

noun
person or device that brushes
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃər / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃə / (BrE)
a person or device that brushes or cleans a surface using a brush

Examples

The brusher cleans streets
The street brusher cleaned the sidewalks every morning before the shops opened.
A new automatic brusher was installed to keep the factory floor spotless.
As a brusher, his job was to remove dust from the museum exhibits each day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleaner - a person or device that removes dirt or makes things clean
  • synonym: sweeper - a person or machine that sweeps floors or streets
