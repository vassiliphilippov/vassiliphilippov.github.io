narrow-bodied

adjective
having a slim or narrow body
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛroʊ ˌbɑdiːd / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ ˌbɒdid / (BrE)
having a relatively narrow or slender body or structure, especially compared to others of its type

Examples

Narrow-bodied fish
Certain fish species are more narrow-bodied, making them faster swimmers in streams.
The airline operates several narrow-bodied jets on its regional routes.
The museum displayed a narrow-bodied vase from ancient Greece.
