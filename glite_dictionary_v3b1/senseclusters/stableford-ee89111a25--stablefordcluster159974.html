Stableford

noun
golf scoring system
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪbəlˌfɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbəlˌfɔːd / (BrE)
a golf scoring system in which points are awarded for each hole based on the number of strokes taken

Examples

Stableford scoring system
The tournament used the Stableford format to encourage more aggressive play.
Many amateur golfers prefer Stableford because it rewards good holes and limits the impact of bad ones.
Our club introduced Stableford scoring to make competitions more enjoyable for all skill levels.
