flagstick

noun
golf hole marker
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈflæɡˌstɪk / (BrE)
a pole with a flag used to mark the location of the hole on a golf course

Examples

Golf flagstick
The golfer aimed his shot directly at the flagstick on the green.
After reaching the green, she removed the flagstick before putting.
A strong wind caused the flagstick to bend slightly during the tournament.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hole - the target area on a golf green
  • synonym: pin - the pole marking the hole on a golf green
