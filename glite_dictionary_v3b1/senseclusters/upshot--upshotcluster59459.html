upshot

noun
final result; outcome of events
6.3 1.30/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆÊŒpËŒÊƒÉ‘t / (AmE), / ËˆÊŒpÊƒÉ’t / (BrE)
the final result or outcome of a process or situation, especially one that is unexpected or arrived at after a series of events

Examples

The upshot of talks
After months of negotiations, the upshot was that both sides agreed to compromise.
The upshot of all his hard work was a well-deserved promotion.
The upshot of the investigation was that no charges were filed.
