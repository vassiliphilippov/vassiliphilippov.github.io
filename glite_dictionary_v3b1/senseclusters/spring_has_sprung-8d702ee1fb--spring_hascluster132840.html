spring has sprung

x
the spring season has arrived
4.8
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ hæz sprʌŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ hæz sprʌŋ / (BrE)
Used to signify that the spring season has arrived, often evoking a sense of renewal and the start of warmer weather.

Examples

Spring has sprung today
After months of cold, it finally feels like spring has sprung in the city.
Gardens across the region show that spring has sprung with colorful blossoms everywhere.
You can tell spring has sprung because the birds are singing again.
