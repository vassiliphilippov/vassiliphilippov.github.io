malt

verb
treat with malt or malt extract
Pronunciation: / mɔlt / (AmE), / mɔːlt / (BrE)
to treat or steep (grain) with malt or malt extract, typically in brewing or food production

Examples

To malt chocolate
Some confectioners malt milk chocolate to give it a distinct taste and texture.
The brewery carefully malts its barley to create a rich, flavorful beer.
To produce malt vinegar, the producer first malts barley before the fermentation process.
