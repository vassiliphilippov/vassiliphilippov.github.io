genially

adverb
in a friendly, cheerful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːniəli / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːniəli / (BrE)
In a friendly and cheerful manner; affably.

Examples

To speak genially
He greeted everyone genially as they entered the room.
She genially accepted their suggestions and implemented many of them.
The host genially offered refreshments to all of the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amiably - in a friendly or pleasant manner
  • antonym: gruffly - in a rough or surly manner
