topological

adjective
relating to topology
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːpəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌtɒpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving topology, especially the properties of a geometric object that are preserved under continuous deformations.

Examples

A topological change
Certain topological changes can transform one shape into another without tearing or gluing.
The mathematician gave a lecture on topological spaces and their properties.
This material exhibits unusual topological characteristics at the atomic level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: topology - the study of properties preserved through deformations, twistings, and stretchings of objects
