civetlike

adjective
musky animal scent
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a strong, musky, or animal-like smell similar to that of civet musk, especially in perfumes or odors

Examples

Civetlike scent
The new fragrance had a subtle civetlike undertone that made it stand out.
Some vintage perfumes are known for their distinctive civetlike aroma.
He noticed a civetlike scent lingering in the old leather-bound books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animalic - having a scent reminiscent of animals, often used in perfumery
  • antonym: fresh - having a clean, crisp, or light scent
  • synonym: musky - having a strong, heavy, and somewhat animal-like scent
