foundationless

adjective
without support
7.5
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃənləs / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃənləs / (BrE)
lacking a physical, logical, or factual base or support

Examples

Foundationless rumors
The rumors about the company closing were completely foundationless and caused unnecessary panic.
She dismissed his foundationless accusations with a calm explanation of the facts.
A foundationless claim can easily be disproved with proper evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baseless - without a basis in fact or reality
  • antonym: justified - having a good reason or basis
  • synonym: unsupported - not given support or justification
