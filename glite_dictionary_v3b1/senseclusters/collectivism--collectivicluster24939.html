collectivism

noun
group over individual
6.3
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈlek.tɪ.vɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /kə.ˈlek.tɪ.vɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the principle or practice of giving priority to the group or community over individual interests

Examples

Cultural collectivism
Many traditional societies emphasize collectivism to maintain harmony and cooperation among members.
The company's culture of collectivism encouraged employees to work together rather than compete.
Debates about collectivism and individualism often arise in discussions of political philosophy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communalism - the practice of living together and sharing responsibilities as a community
  • antonym: individualism - the principle of prioritizing individual interests over those of the group
