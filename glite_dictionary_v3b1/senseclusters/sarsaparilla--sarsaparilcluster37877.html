sarsaparilla

noun
a sweet, root-flavored soft drink
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsæs.pəˈrɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.spəˈrɪl.ə / (BrE)
a sweet, carbonated soft drink traditionally flavored with extract from sarsaparilla root, or sometimes with birch oil and sassafras.

Examples

A cold sarsaparilla
He ordered a cold sarsaparilla at the old-fashioned soda fountain.
Some people say that sarsaparilla tastes similar to root beer but milder and less sweet.
The company still bottles their original sarsaparilla recipe from 1895.
