goatish

adjective
lewd or lustful
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtɪʃ / (BrE)
having qualities or behavior thought to resemble a goat, especially in being lewd or lustful

Examples

A goatish smile
He gave her a goatish smile that made everyone uncomfortable.
The villain in the story was known for his goatish behavior at parties.
She avoided the man with the goatish grin in the corner of the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lecherous - showing excessive or offensive sexual desire
  • antonym: modest - behaving in a reserved or proper way
