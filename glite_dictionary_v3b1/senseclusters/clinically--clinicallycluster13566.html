clinically

adverb
in a clinical or medical manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪnɪkli / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to clinical practice or observation; in a manner characteristic of a clinic or medical context.

Examples

Described clinically
He described the incident clinically, without letting emotion influence his explanation.
Her symptoms improved, but he remained clinically cautious about her recovery.
The patient was clinically diagnosed with pneumonia after a thorough examination.
