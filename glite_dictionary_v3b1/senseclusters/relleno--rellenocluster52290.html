relleno

noun
stuffed dish
8.6
Pronunciation: / reɪˈjeɪnoʊ /; / reˈjeɪnoʊ / (AmE), / reɪˈjeɪnəʊ /; / reˈjeɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a stuffed dish (especially in Latin American cuisine) or the filling used inside such dishes

Examples

Cheese relleno
I ordered a delicious relleno stuffed with cheese and served with tomato sauce.
The chef prepared a vegetarian relleno using bell peppers and black beans.
At the festival, we tried a spicy relleno filled with ground beef and rice.
The chef prepared peppers with a spicy relleno inside.
She learned how to make a traditional chicken relleno from her grandmother.
Many Mexican dishes feature a flavorful relleno of cheese or meat.

  • synonym: stuffing - a mixture used to fill food before cooking
