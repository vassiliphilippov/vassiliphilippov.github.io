goldenness

noun
golden quality
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldənnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldənnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being golden in color or appearance

Examples

Goldenness of the fields
The goldenness of the wheat fields stretched as far as the eye could see.
She admired the goldenness of the antique frame in the sunlight.
Autumn leaves bring a special goldenness to the park each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the quality of lacking brightness or shine
  • synonym: radiance - a bright or glowing quality
