coloring material

noun
substance for adding color
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚɪŋ məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.ər.ɪŋ məˈtɪə.ri.əl / (BrE)
a substance used to add or change color in another material or surface

Examples

Natural coloring materials
Natural coloring materials are often preferred for food products to avoid artificial additives.
The artist experimented with a new coloring material to achieve deeper shades in her painting.
The workshop teaches children how to make their own coloring material from everyday ingredients.
Certain insects can produce a red colouring material used in food and cosmetics.
The artist selected a natural colouring material to create her earthy palette.
This paint contains a new synthetic colouring material that resists fading in sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - a natural or synthetic substance used to add color to or change the color of something
  • synonym: pigment - a substance that gives color to something, especially in paint or ink
