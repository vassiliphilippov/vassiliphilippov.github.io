auxinic

adjective
relating to or containing auxins
Pronunciation: / ɔɡˈzɪnɪk / (AmE), / ɔːkˈsɪnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing auxins, especially referring to substances or effects associated with plant hormones that regulate growth.

Examples

Auxinic herbicides
Researchers compared the effects of auxinic and non-auxinic herbicides on plant growth.
The auxinic activity in the tissue sample indicated the presence of growth hormones.
The horticulturist applied an auxinic spray to stimulate root formation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auxin - a class of plant hormones that regulate growth
