tengu

noun
Japanese festival figure
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛŋɡu / (AmE), / ˈtɛŋɡu / (BrE)
a carved or painted representation of a mythical Japanese creature, often used as a mask or decoration in festivals

Examples

Tengu festival mask
The parade featured a giant tengu mask carried by dancers in traditional costumes.
She bought a small wooden tengu as a souvenir from the festival market.
Many homes display a tengu figure to bring good luck and ward off evil spirits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goblin - a mischievous, mythical creature in folklore
  • synonym: oni - a demon or ogre in Japanese folklore
