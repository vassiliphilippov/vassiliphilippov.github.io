nonsocialist

noun
opponent of socialism
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a person, political party, or group that does not support socialism or socialist principles

Examples

Nonsocialist party
The new government was formed by a coalition of nonsocialists and moderate parties.
As a nonsocialist, she advocated for free market policies and private ownership.
Debates between socialists and nonsocialists shaped the country's economic direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: socialist - a person who supports socialism
