hairstreak

noun
small butterfly with striped underwings
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌstriːk / (AmE), / ˈheəstriːk / (BrE)
A small butterfly of the family Lycaenidae, typically bearing fine, hairlike lines or stripes on the underside of the wings.

Examples

A hairstreak butterfly
I spotted a rare brown hairstreak fluttering among the hedgerows.
Many hairstreaks have vivid blue markings on their wings, making them easy to identify.
The hairstreak is often seen perched quietly in sunny woodland clearings.
