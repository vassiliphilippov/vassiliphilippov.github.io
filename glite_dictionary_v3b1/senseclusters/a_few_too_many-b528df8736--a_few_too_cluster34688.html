a few too many

x
more than is desirable (esp. food/drink)
Pronunciation: / ə fju tuː məˈni / (AmE), / ə fjuː tuː ˈmɛni / (BrE)
a larger amount than is good or desirable, especially of food or drink; more than one should have had

Examples

Had a few too many drinks
After the party, he admitted he'd had a few too many and needed a taxi home.
She realized she'd bought a few too many souvenirs during her vacation.
Whenever there are birthdays at work, I end up eating a few too many pieces of cake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: too much - more than is reasonable or acceptable
