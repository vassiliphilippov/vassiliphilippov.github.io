shaddock

noun
large citrus fruit like grapefruit
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædək /; / ˈʃæd.ək / (AmE), / ˈʃadək /; / ˈʃæd.ək / (BrE)
a large, pear-shaped citrus fruit similar to a grapefruit, or the tree that produces this fruit

Examples

A shaddock fruit

The shaddock is much larger than a typical grapefruit and has a thicker rind.

The shaddock tree is prized for its unusually large, thick-skinned fruits.
Unlike grapefruit, the shaddock usually has a sweeter and less bitter flavor.
