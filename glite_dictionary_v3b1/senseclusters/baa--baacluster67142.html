baa

verb
make the cry of a sheep or lamb
Pronunciation: / bɑː / (AmE), / bɑː / (BrE)
(of a sheep or lamb) to make the characteristic bleating cry.

Examples

To baa loudly
Every morning, the sheep baa loudly waiting to be fed.
In the petting zoo, children laughed as the goats baaed for attention.
The lonely lamb began to baa when it couldn't find its mother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bleat - to make the wavering cry of a sheep, goat, or calf
