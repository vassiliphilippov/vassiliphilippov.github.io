parse

verb
analyze grammatical structure
Pronunciation: / pɑrs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
To analyze the grammatical structure of a sentence by dividing it into its constituent parts and determining their syntactic functions.

Examples

To parse a sentence
It can be challenging to parse long, complex sentences in academic texts.
Linguists regularly parse spoken language to study how meaning is constructed.
The student was asked to parse each sentence to demonstrate his understanding of grammar.
