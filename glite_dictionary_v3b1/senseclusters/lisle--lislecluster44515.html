lisle

noun
strong twisted cotton thread
7.4
Pronunciation: / laɪl / (AmE), / laɪl / (BrE)
a strong, tightly twisted cotton thread, often made from long-staple cotton, used especially for hosiery and underwear

Examples

Lisle thread
Lisle is valued in the textile industry for its strength and sheen.
High-quality socks are often made from lisle for added comfort and durability.
She preferred stockings made with lisle because of their smooth finish.
