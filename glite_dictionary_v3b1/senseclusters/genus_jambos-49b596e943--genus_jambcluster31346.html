genus Jambos

noun
former genus for rose apples
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈdʒæmˌboʊs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈdʒæmbɒs / (BrE)
a former genus in some botanical classifications that includes the rose apple (Eugenia jambos) and related species

Examples

Genus Jambos species
Modern botanists now place the species formerly in genus Jambos under Eugenia.
Some older texts list the rose apple under the genus Jambos.
The genus Jambos is no longer widely recognized in modern botanical classifications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eugenia jambos - the rose apple, a tropical fruit tree formerly classified in the genus Jambos
