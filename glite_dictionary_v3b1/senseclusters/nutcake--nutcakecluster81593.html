nutcake

noun
cake with nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌkeɪk / (BrE)
a cake made with nuts as a main ingredient

Examples

A slice of nutcake
She baked a delicious nutcake for the holiday party.
The bakery sells a popular walnut nutcake every winter.
He brought a homemade nutcake to share at the office lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruitcake - a cake containing dried fruit and nuts
  • synonym: pound cake - a rich cake made with equal parts flour, butter, sugar, and eggs
