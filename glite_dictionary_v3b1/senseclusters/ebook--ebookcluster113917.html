ebook

verb
to digitize a book
Pronunciation: / ˈiːbʊk / (AmE), / ˈiːbʊk / (BrE)
to publish or convert a book into an electronic format for reading on digital devices

Examples

To ebook a novel
Many independent authors choose to ebook their novels to reach a wider audience online.
The publisher plans to ebook all classic titles by the end of the year.
After finishing her manuscript, she decided to ebook it instead of seeking a print deal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitize - to convert information into a digital format
  • antonym: print - to produce a book or document in physical form
  • synonym: publish - to prepare and issue a book or other material for public sale
