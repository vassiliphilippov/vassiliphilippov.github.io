cementlike

adjective
resembling cement
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of cement, especially in texture or appearance

Examples

Cementlike surface
After drying, the paint formed a cementlike layer on the wall.
The chef noticed the sauce had become too thick and cementlike to use.
The mud in the construction site turned cementlike after a day in the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concrete - a hard, strong building material made from cement
  • antonym: powdery - having a texture like powder
