increasingly

adverb
more and more over time
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli / (BrE)
to an increasing degree; more and more over time

Examples

Becoming increasingly common
People are increasingly concerned about the effects of climate change.
Technology plays an increasingly important role in our daily lives.
The project has become increasingly difficult to manage over the past few months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decreasingly - to a decreasing degree; less and less over time
