emir

noun
Muslim ruler or chieftain
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɪr / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪə / (BrE)
a Muslim ruler or chieftain, especially in parts of Africa or Arabia

Examples

The emir rules
Each town in the desert was governed by its own emir.
The emir welcomed foreign diplomats to discuss peace in the region.
The young prince was proclaimed emir after his father's passing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prince - son of a monarch; ruler of a principality
  • synonym: sheikh - an Arab leader, especially of a tribe or community
