downbeat

adjective
sad or discouraged
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌbiːt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌbiːt / (BrE)
feeling or showing sadness, discouragement, or a lack of hope

Examples

A downbeat mood
After the disappointing results, the whole team felt downbeat about their chances.
She sounded downbeat when she talked about her job search.
Despite the downbeat mood in the office, they tried to stay positive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - feeling or showing happiness and optimism
  • synonym: depressed - feeling very sad and without hope
  • synonym: gloomy - feeling or showing sadness or a lack of hope
