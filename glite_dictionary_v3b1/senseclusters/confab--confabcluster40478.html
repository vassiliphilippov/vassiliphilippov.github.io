confab

noun
informal conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌfæb / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fæb / (BrE)
an informal conversation or discussion, often private or casual in nature

Examples

A quick confab
Sarah called Mark over for a quick confab by the coffee machine.
The two managers had a private confab before making their announcement.
They had a brief confab after the meeting to discuss their next steps.
