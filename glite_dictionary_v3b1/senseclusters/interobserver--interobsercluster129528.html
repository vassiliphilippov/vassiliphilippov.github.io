interobserver

adjective
between observers
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvər / (AmE), / ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːvə / (BrE)
relating to differences, comparisons, or agreement between two or more observers

Examples

Interobserver reliability
The researchers calculated interobserver reliability to ensure the results were consistent across all raters.
High interobserver agreement is essential for the validity of clinical studies.
Training sessions were held to improve interobserver consistency among the laboratory staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraobserver - relating to consistency or variation within a single observer
