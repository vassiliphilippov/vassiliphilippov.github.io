Ebenezer

proper noun
Biblical memorial stone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛbəˈniːzər / (AmE), / ˌɛbəˈniːzə / (BrE)
a stone set up by the prophet Samuel in the Bible to commemorate divine help or victory

Examples

Biblical Ebenezer stone
The prophet Samuel set up an Ebenezer after the Israelites' victory in battle.
In the church, a stone labeled Ebenezer reminds visitors of God's help.
The story of the Ebenezer is often mentioned in sermons about gratitude.
