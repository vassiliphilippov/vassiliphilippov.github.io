eagling

noun
golf score two under par
Pronunciation: / ˈiːɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of scoring an eagle in golf, that is, completing a hole in two strokes under par

Examples

A great eagling finish
He was thrilled after eagling the final hole to win the tournament.
Her impressive eagling of the par-five made the crowd cheer loudly.
The team celebrated their captain's eagling during the championship round.

Related Terms
