falling

adjective
decreasing; becoming less
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑlɪŋ /; / ˈfɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

There is concern over falling enrollments at the university.
