Sienese

noun
person from Siena
Pronunciation: / siˈeniːz / (AmE), / siˈeniːz / (BrE)
a person from Siena, a city in central Italy, or the people of Siena collectively

Examples

A Sienese person
The Sienese are famous for their annual horse race, the Palio di Siena.
Many Sienese artists contributed to the development of Italian Renaissance painting.
A group of Sienese welcomed the tourists with traditional music and food.
