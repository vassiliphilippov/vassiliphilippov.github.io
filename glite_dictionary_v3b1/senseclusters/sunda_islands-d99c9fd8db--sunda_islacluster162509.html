Sunda Islands

proper noun
island chain in western Malay Archipelago
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsʊn.də ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.də ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
a chain of islands in the western part of the Malay Archipelago, comprising the Greater Sunda Islands (including Borneo, Java, Sumatra, and Sulawesi) and the Lesser Sunda Islands, situated mostly in Indonesia.

Examples

The Sunda Islands chain
Indonesia's largest cities outside Java are located on the Sunda Islands.
Many spices that shaped world trade historically originated in the Sunda Islands.
The Sunda Islands are known for their rich biodiversity and volcanic activity.
