rescue party

noun
group sent to rescue
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛskju ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈrɛskjuː ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a group of people organized to rescue someone from danger or distress

Examples

A rescue party team
A rescue party was sent out to find the missing climbers after the avalanche.
Despite the dangerous conditions, the rescue party managed to reach the stranded hikers.
The villagers formed a rescue party to help those trapped by the flood.
