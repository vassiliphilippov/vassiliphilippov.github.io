one fell swoop

noun
all at once; in a single action
7.6 0.40/m
Pronunciation: / w ån f…õl swup / (AmE), / w ån f…õl swuÀêp / (BrE)
all at once; in a single, sudden action or event.

Examples

In one fell swoop
She addressed all the customers' complaints in one fell swoop during the meeting.
Winning the lottery allowed him to pay off all his debts in one fell swoop.
With a single click, he deleted hundreds of files in one fell swoop.
