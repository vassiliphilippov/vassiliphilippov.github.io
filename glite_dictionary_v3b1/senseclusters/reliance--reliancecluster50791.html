reliance

noun
firm trust or confidence
3.1 2.7
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈlaɪ.əns/ /; / /riː.ˈlaɪ.əns/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈlaɪ.əns/ (BrE)
trust or dependence on someone or something

Examples

Reliance on others
After years of working together, she had great reliance on her colleague's judgment.
Farmers in the region have a heavy reliance on rainfall for their crops.
The company's reliance on past success made it slow to adapt to new technology.
Her reliance on public transportation makes her morning commute much easier.
The country's reliance on imported oil has decreased in recent years.
Too much reliance on technology can create problems when systems fail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distrust - lack of trust or confidence
  • antonym: independence - the state of not relying on or being controlled by others
  • synonym: trust - firm belief in the reliability of someone or something
