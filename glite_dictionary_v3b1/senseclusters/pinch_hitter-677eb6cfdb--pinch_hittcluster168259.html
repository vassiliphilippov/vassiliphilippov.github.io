pinch hitter

noun
substitute batter in baseball
7.3 5.4
Pronunciation: /pɪntʃ ˈhɪt.ər/ (AmE), /pɪntʃ ˈhɪt.ə/ (BrE)
a substitute batter in baseball; by extension, someone who temporarily takes another person's place, especially in a job or role
