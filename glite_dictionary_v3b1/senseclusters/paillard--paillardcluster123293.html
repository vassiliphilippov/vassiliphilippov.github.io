paillard

noun
thin pounded meat
9.1
Pronunciation: / paɪˈjɑrd / (AmE), / paɪˈjɑːd / (BrE)
a thin slice of meat that is pounded flat and quickly cooked, often grilled or sautéed

Examples

Chicken paillard
The chef prepared a tender paillard of chicken with a fresh herb salad.
For dinner, she ordered a veal paillard served with roasted vegetables.
A classic paillard is often finished with a squeeze of lemon and olive oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutlet - a small, thin piece of meat, often breaded and fried
  • synonym: escalope - a thin slice of meat, often veal or chicken, that is pounded and cooked
