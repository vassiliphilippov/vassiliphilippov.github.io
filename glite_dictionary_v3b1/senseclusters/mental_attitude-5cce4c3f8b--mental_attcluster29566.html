mental attitude

noun
way of thinking or feeling about something
1.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmen.təl ˈæt.ə.tuː/ /; / /ˈmen.təl ˈæt.ɪ.tuː/ (AmE), /ˈmen.təl ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /ˈmen.təl ˈæt.ə.tjuːd/ (BrE)
a particular way of thinking or feeling about something, often reflected in beliefs, values, and readiness to act

Examples

A positive mental attitude
