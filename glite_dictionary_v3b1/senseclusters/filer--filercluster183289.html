filer

noun
person who maintains files
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪlə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to organize and maintain files in an office or organization

Examples

Office filer
Each department has its own filer responsible for records management.
She was promoted from filer to office manager after two years of diligent work.
The filer ensured that all documents were organized and easy to find.
