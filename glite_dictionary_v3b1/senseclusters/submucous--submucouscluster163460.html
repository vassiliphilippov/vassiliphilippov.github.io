submucous

adjective
beneath mucous membrane
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈmjuːkəs / (AmE), / sʌbˈmjuːkəs / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath a mucous membrane

Examples

Submucous tumor
The surgeon removed a submucous tumor from the patient's nasal cavity.
A submucous cyst can sometimes cause difficulty in swallowing.
The endoscopy revealed a submucous lesion that required further testing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mucosal - relating to or affecting a mucous membrane
  • synonym: submucosal - situated or occurring beneath a mucous membrane
