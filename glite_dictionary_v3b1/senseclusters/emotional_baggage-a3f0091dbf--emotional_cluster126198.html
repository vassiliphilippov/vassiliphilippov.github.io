emotional baggage

noun
unresolved psychological issues from the past
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənl ˈbæɡɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl ˈbæɡɪdʒ / (BrE)
unresolved emotional issues or past experiences that continue to affect a person’s attitudes or behavior

Examples

Emotional baggage from past
His emotional baggage kept him from trusting people easily.
She spent years working through her emotional baggage from a difficult childhood.
Therapy helped Maria let go of the emotional baggage she had carried for so long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burden - something that is carried, especially a worry or responsibility
