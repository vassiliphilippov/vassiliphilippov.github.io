aminoglycoside

adjective
antibiotic class
8.4
Pronunciation: / əˌminoʊˈɡlaɪkəˌsaɪd / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəʊˈɡlaɪkəsaɪd / (BrE)
relating to or denoting antibiotics that belong to the aminoglycoside class, which are used to treat bacterial infections

Examples

Aminoglycoside drug treatment
The doctor prescribed an aminoglycoside antibiotic to treat the severe infection.
Some bacteria have developed resistance to aminoglycoside drugs, making treatment more difficult.
Patients receiving aminoglycoside medications require regular monitoring for possible side effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antibiotic - a drug used to treat bacterial infections
