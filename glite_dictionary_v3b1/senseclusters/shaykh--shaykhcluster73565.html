shaykh

noun
Muslim community leader
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
a respected religious leader, scholar, or elder in a Muslim community

Examples

Respected shaykh
The shaykh offered wise advice to the younger members of the village.
Many people traveled from distant towns to hear the shaykh speak.
After the ceremony, the shaykh greeted each guest personally.
The shaykh delivered a lecture on Islamic history to the students.
Many people traveled from distant towns to seek guidance from the shaykh.
She asked the shaykh for advice on religious matters before making her decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imam - a leader of prayer in a mosque
  • synonym: mufti - an Islamic legal expert who gives rulings
