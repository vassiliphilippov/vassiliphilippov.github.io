sidehill

adjective
on a hillside
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌhɪl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌhɪl / (BrE)
located on or extending along the side of a hill or slope

Examples

Sidehill cabin
The cabin was built on a sidehill to provide a view of the valley below.
Farmers often plant crops on sidehill fields to make use of sloped land.
A narrow sidehill path wound its way through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hillside - the sloping side of a hill
  • antonym: level - having a flat or even surface
