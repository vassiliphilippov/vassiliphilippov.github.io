nonworking

adjective
not employed
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
not employed or not engaged in paid work; not currently working

Examples

Nonworking adults
After the factory closed, he was nonworking for several months while he looked for a new job.
The government offers support programs for nonworking adults seeking employment.
Many nonworking parents choose to stay home to care for their children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unemployed - not having a paid job
  • antonym: working - having a paid job or employment
