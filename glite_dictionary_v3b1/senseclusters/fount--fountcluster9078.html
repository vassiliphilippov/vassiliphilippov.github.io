fount

noun
source of something
Pronunciation: / faʊnt / (AmE), / faʊnt / (BrE)
a person, place, or thing that is a source of something valuable, such as knowledge, wisdom, or information

Examples

Fount of knowledge
She was a fount of wisdom for her friends, always ready with helpful advice.
The library became a fount of information for the students working on their projects.
His creativity was a fount that inspired everyone in the team.
The library is a fount of information for curious students.
He became a fount of creative ideas during the brainstorming session.
Her kindness is a fount of comfort to everyone around her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fountain - a spring or source, often of water or metaphorically of something else
  • synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins
  • synonym: source - the place or person from which something originates
