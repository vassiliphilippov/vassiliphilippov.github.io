heart-shaped love-lock

noun
padlock shaped like a heart to symbolize love
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrt ʃeɪpt ˈlʌv ˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ʃeɪpt ˈlʌv ˌlɒk / (BrE)
a padlock in the shape of a heart, often used as a symbol of love and affection, typically attached to a public structure by couples as a romantic gesture

Examples

A heart-shaped love-lock on bridge
He surprised her with a heart-shaped love-lock to celebrate their engagement.
She fastened a heart-shaped love-lock to the bridge to commemorate their anniversary.
Tourists often engrave their names on a heart-shaped love-lock before attaching it to a famous landmark.
