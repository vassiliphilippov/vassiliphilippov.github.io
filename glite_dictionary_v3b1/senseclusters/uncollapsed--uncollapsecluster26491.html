uncollapsed

adjective
expanded or visible
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈlæpst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlæpst / (BrE)
not collapsed; in computing, describing a section or element whose contents are visible or expanded.

Examples

Uncollapsed menu
The menu remains uncollapsed so users can quickly access all available options.
If the sidebar is uncollapsed, you will see additional navigation links.
She prefers to keep the folder uncollapsed for easier file management.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collapsed - hidden or minimized so contents are not visible
  • synonym: expanded - opened up so that contents are visible
