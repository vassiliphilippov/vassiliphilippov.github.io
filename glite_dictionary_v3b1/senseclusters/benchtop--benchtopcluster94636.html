benchtop

noun
flat work surface
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛntʃˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛntʃˌtɒp / (BrE)

Examples

A clean benchtop

Please wipe down the benchtop after you finish preparing your meal.
He installed a new benchtop in his garage to make woodworking easier.
