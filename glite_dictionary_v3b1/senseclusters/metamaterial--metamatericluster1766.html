metamaterial

noun
engineered material
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtə məˈtɪriəl /; / ˌmɛtəməˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtə məˈtɪəriəl /; / ˌmɛtəˌməˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a material that is specially engineered to have properties not found in natural materials, often by designing its structure at a very small scale
