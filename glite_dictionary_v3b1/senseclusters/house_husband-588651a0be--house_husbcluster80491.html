house husband

noun
man who manages home while wife works
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /haʊs ˈhʌz.bənd/ /; / /haʊz ˈhʌz.bənd/ (AmE), /haʊs ˈhʌz.bənd/ /; / /haʊz ˈhʌz.bənd/ (BrE)
