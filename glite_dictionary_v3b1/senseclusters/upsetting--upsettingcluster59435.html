causing distress, anxiety, or emotional disturbance; making someone feel unhappy or worried.

Examples

Upsetting news
It can be upsetting to see animals treated poorly in documentaries.
She found his comments upsetting and left the room in tears.
The news of the accident was extremely upsetting to everyone involved.

Synonyms

  • distressing
    4.6 2.62/m
    distressing causes strong sorrow or pain in people, while upsetting makes someone feel unhappy or worried.
  • troubling
    4.0 8.94/m
    troubling points to serious worry or concern that grows, while upsetting means causing immediate unhappiness or anxiety.
  • disturbing
    4.2 17/m
    disturbing often suggests shocking or worrying images, while upsetting describes things that make someone feel unhappy or anxious.
  • unsettling
    4.2 4.72/m
    unsettling creates a quiet sense of unease or doubt, while upsetting makes a person feel unhappy or worried.
  • perturbing
    6.8 0.03/m
    perturbing sounds formal and is used in writing about worry, while upsetting is common and used in everyday speech.
  • disconcerting
    7.2 2.34/m
    disconcerting makes people feel confused or awkward in a situation, while upsetting mainly makes them sad or worried.
  • dismaying
    5.5 0.26/m
    dismaying expresses strong shock or deep disappointment, while upsetting covers general unhappiness or worry in many situations.
