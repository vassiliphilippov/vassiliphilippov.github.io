understanding

noun
support or sympathy for someone or something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
support or sympathy for a person, idea, or cause; an inclination to agree with or be loyal to something or someone.

Examples

Understanding for others

She always felt that her mother's understanding was with her during difficult times.
