(in cryptocurrency and decentralized finance) to provide or lock cryptocurrency assets (for example by staking or supplying liquidity) in a protocol to earn rewards, interest, or new tokens.

Examples

To farm crypto rewards

She started farming stablecoins in a liquidity pool to get higher interest.
They farmed rewards from multiple protocols before withdrawing their crypto holdings.
