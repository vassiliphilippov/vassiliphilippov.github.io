gutbucket

noun
rough music club
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌtˌbʌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌtˌbʌkɪt / (BrE)
a cheap, often noisy bar or club where raw blues or jazz music is played

Examples

A noisy gutbucket bar
The band played energetic blues in a crowded gutbucket downtown.
Locals gathered at the old gutbucket to enjoy live jazz every Friday night.
He loved the raw atmosphere of a true gutbucket more than any fancy club.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dive - a shabby or disreputable bar or club
  • synonym: nightclub - a place where people go to listen to music and dance at night
