nest

noun
set of furniture items fitting inside each other
Pronunciation: / nɛst / (AmE), / nɛst / (BrE)
a set of furniture pieces, such as tables or boxes, made to fit one inside another for storage or space-saving purposes

Examples

A nest of tables
A nest of boxes sat neatly in the corner, each fitting perfectly inside the next.
She bought a nest of tables for her living room to save space.
The antique shop displayed a beautiful nest of drawers from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stack - a set of similar objects placed one on top of another
