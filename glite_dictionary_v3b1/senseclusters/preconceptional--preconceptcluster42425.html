preconceptional

adjective
formed before evidence
8.6 0.00/m

relating to ideas, beliefs, or opinions formed before having evidence or experience.

Examples

Preconceptional bias
Her preconceptional attitude prevented her from listening to other points of view.
The study aimed to challenge preconceptional notions about the effectiveness of the new method.
It is important to avoid preconceptional judgments when meeting new people.
