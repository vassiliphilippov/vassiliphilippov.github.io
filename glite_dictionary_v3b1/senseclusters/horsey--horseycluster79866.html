horsey

noun
toy horse for children
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsi / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsi / (BrE)
a child's toy horse, especially a small model or a hobbyhorse used for play

Examples

A toy horsey
The little boy refused to go to bed without his favorite horsey beside him.
She spent the afternoon riding her wooden horsey around the living room.
At the toy store, he picked out a bright red horsey to take home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hobbyhorse - a toy horse on which a child can ride
  • synonym: rocking horse - a toy horse mounted on rockers for children to ride
