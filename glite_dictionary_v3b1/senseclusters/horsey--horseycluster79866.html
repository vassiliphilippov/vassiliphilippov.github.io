horsey

noun
toy horse for children
5.6 0.44/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…îrsi / (AmE), / Ààh…îÀêsi / (BrE)
a child's toy horse, especially a small model or a hobbyhorse used for play

Examples

A toy horsey

At the toy store, he picked out a bright red horsey to take home.

Synonyms

  • horsy

  • stick horse
    2.3 0.01/m

  • rocking horse
    1.3 0.24/m
