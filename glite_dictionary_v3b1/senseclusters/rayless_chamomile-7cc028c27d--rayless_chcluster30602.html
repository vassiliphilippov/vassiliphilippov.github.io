rayless chamomile

noun
aromatic Pacific weed with rayless yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪləs kəˈmoʊmil / (AmE), / ˈreɪləs kəˈmɒmaɪl / (BrE)
An annual aromatic plant found in Pacific coastal regions of the US and northeastern Asia, with bristle-pointed leaves and yellow flowers lacking ray florets.

Examples

Rayless chamomile plant
Rayless chamomile often invades disturbed soils along the Pacific coast.
Gardening guides warn that rayless chamomile can spread quickly if left uncontrolled.
The field contained patches of rayless chamomile mixed with other wildflowers.
