reconsecrate

verb
to consecrate again
Pronunciation: / riˈkɑnsəˌkreɪt / (AmE), / riˈkɒnsɪkreɪt / (BrE)
to consecrate again, especially after something has been desecrated

Examples

To reconsecrate the altar
After the vandalism, the church decided to reconsecrate the altar in a special ceremony.
Every generation may need to reconsecrate old traditions to keep them meaningful.
The sacred site was reconsecrated following years of neglect and misuse.
