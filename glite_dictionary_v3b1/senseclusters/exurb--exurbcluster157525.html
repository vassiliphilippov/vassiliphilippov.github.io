exurb

noun
area beyond suburbs
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡzɜːrb /; / ˈɛɡzɜrb / (AmE), / ˈɛksɜːb / (BrE)
a residential area beyond the suburbs of a city, often more rural and less densely populated, where people may commute to the city for work
