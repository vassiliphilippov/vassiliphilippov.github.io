highly combustible

adjective
very easily catches fire
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli kəmˈbʌstɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli kəmˈbʌstɪbəl / (BrE)
Very easily ignited; capable of catching fire and burning quickly.

Examples

Highly combustible material
Due to its highly combustible nature, gasoline should be kept away from open flames.
Many cleaning agents are highly combustible, so always read the label before using them near heat sources.
The chemicals stored in this cabinet are highly combustible and require special handling.
