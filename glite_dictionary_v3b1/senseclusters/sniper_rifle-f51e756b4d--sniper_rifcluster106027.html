sniper rifle

noun
high-precision long-range rifle
Pronunciation: / ˈsnaɪpɚ ˈraɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈsnaɪpə ˈraɪfəl / (BrE)
A high-precision, long-range rifle designed for use by snipers, often equipped with telescopic sights.

Examples

A sniper rifle with scope
A sniper rifle is often equipped with a powerful scope for greater accuracy.
Police recovered an illegal sniper rifle from the suspect's vehicle.
The soldier carefully aimed his sniper rifle at the distant target.
