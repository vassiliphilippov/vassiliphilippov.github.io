Panthera

noun
genus of big cats (lion, tiger, etc.)
Pronunciation: / pænˈθɛrə /; / pænˈθɪrə / (AmE), / panˈθɪərə /; / pænˈθɪərə / (BrE)
a genus of large cats that includes lions, tigers, leopards, jaguars, and the snow leopard, known for their ability to roar
