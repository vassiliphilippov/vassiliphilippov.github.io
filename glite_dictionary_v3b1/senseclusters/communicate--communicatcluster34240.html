communicate

verb
(of rooms) to be directly connected
7.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt / (BrE)
(of rooms or parts of a building) to be joined or connected so that it is possible to pass from one to another.

Examples

Rooms communicate inside
On the top floor, all the main rooms communicate with each other.
The bedrooms communicate through a short hallway.
The two offices communicate via a sliding door in the wall.
