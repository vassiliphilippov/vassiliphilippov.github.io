kaboom

noun
loud explosive sound
7.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈbuːm/ (AmE), /kə.ˈbuːm/ (BrE)
a loud explosive sound, especially one caused by an explosion or sudden impact

Examples

A sudden kaboom
There was a sudden kaboom as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
The old building came down with a huge kaboom during the demolition.
We heard a loud kaboom from the kitchen and rushed to see what happened.
