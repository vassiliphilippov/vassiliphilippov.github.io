relativization

noun
the act of making relative
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛlətɪvəˈzeɪʃən /; / rɛl.ə.t̬ə.vaɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛlətɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / rɪˌlæt.ɪ.vaɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something relative, or viewing it as relative rather than absolute.
