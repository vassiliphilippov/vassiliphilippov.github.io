knifelike

adjective
sharp or cutting, like a knife
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪf.laɪk /; / ˈnaɪflaɪk / (BrE)
(of pain or sensations) extremely sharp or cutting, as if caused by a knife
