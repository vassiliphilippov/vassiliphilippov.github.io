death star

noun
dominant or threatening entity
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ stɑː / (BrE)
A powerful or dominant organization, project, or entity, often regarded negatively or as a threat by others.

Examples

A death star company
Many employees feared that the merger would create a corporate death star in the industry.
Startups struggle to survive when a major player launches its own death star solution.
The tech giant's new app is seen as a death star destined to crush its rivals.
