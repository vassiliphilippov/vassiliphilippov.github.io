sport fisherman

noun
recreational angler
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrt ˈfɪʃərmən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːt ˈfɪʃəmən / (BrE)
a person who fishes for pleasure or competition rather than for food or profit

Examples

Avid sport fisherman
The experienced sport fisherman traveled to Alaska to catch salmon during the summer season.
As a dedicated sport fisherman, he always releases the fish he catches back into the water.
Many sport fishermen participate in tournaments to test their skills against others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angler - a person who fishes with a rod and line
  • antonym: commercial fisherman - a person who fishes for food or profit
