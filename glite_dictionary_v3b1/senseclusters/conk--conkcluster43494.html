conk

noun
light hit to head
6.9
Pronunciation: / kɑŋk / (AmE), / kɒŋk / (BrE)
a light or playful hit, especially to the head

Examples

A playful conk
He gave his friend a gentle conk on the head as a joke.
After the game, she laughed off the accidental conk she received from the ball.
The siblings often playfully conk each other during their games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - a gentle touch or hit
