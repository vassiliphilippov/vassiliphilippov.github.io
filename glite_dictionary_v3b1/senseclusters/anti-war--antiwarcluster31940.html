anti-war

adjective
opposed to war
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈwɔr / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈwɔː / (BrE)
opposed to war or supporting actions and ideas that promote peace

Examples

Anti-war protest
The group organized an anti-war rally in the city center to protest military action.
Her anti-war views were shaped by her family's experiences during the conflict.
Many artists created anti-war posters to express their desire for peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pacifist - opposed to war and violence
  • antonym: pro-war - supporting or favoring war
