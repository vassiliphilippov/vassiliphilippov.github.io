harrier

noun
a breed of hunting hound
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhæriər / (AmE), / ˈhæriə / (BrE)
a breed of hound resembling a foxhound but smaller, bred and used chiefly for hunting hares and rabbits

Examples

A harrier hound
She adopted a harrier because of its friendly temperament and energy.
The harrier is known for its skill in hunting hares over open countryside.
The pack of harriers chased the rabbit through the brush.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: foxhound - a large breed of hound used for fox hunting
