deer fly

noun
biting water fly
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪr flaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɪə flaɪ / (BrE)
a small biting fly found near water, known for its painful bite and often bothering humans and animals

Examples

A deer fly bite
I was bitten by a deer fly while fishing by the river.
The deer fly population increases during the summer months near wetlands.
She wore a hat to protect herself from deer flies on the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horsefly - a large biting fly that feeds on the blood of mammals
  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and sucks blood
