ligamentum teres uteri

noun
round ligament of the uterus
Pronunciation: / lɪɡəˈmɛntəm ˈtɪriːz juːˈtɪraɪ / (AmE), / lɪɡəˈmɛntəm ˈtɛːriːz juːˈtɪərɪ / (BrE)
a round ligament attached to the uterus on each side, running in front of and below the opening of the Fallopian tube and passing through the inguinal canal to the labia majora

Examples

Pain in the groin during pregnancy can sometimes be caused by the stretching of the ligamentum teres uteri.
