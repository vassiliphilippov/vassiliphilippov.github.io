orgy

noun
excessive or uncontrolled indulgence
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈɔː.dʒi / (BrE)
an occasion or act involving excessive or uncontrolled indulgence in something, especially food, drinking, or spending

Examples

Orgy of spending
After receiving her bonus, she went on an orgy of shopping and dining out.
He regretted the orgy of unhealthy eating that lasted all weekend.
The city celebrated the victory with an orgy of fireworks and parades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binge - a period of excessive indulgence in an activity
  • synonym: splurge - an act of spending money freely or extravagantly
