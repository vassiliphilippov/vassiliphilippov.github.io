jejunal

adjective
of the jejunum
9.3
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈdʒunəl / (AmE), / dʒɪˈdʒuːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the jejunum, which is the middle section of the small intestine

Examples

Jejunal biopsy
The surgeon performed a jejunal biopsy to diagnose the patient's digestive disorder.
A jejunal feeding tube is often used when patients cannot eat by mouth.
Doctors examined the jejunal tissue for signs of infection after the operation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gastric - relating to the stomach
  • synonym: intestinal - relating to the intestine
