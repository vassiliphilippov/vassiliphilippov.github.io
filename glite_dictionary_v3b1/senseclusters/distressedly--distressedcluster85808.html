distressedly

adverb
in a worn manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɛstlɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɛstlɪ / (BrE)
in a manner that appears worn, shabby, or artificially aged, especially referring to furniture, clothing, or finishes.

Examples

Paint distressedly for style
The designer painted the table distressedly to create a rustic, vintage look for the living room.
Many people prefer their jeans to be styled distressedly for a fashionable, worn-in appearance.
The cabinet was finished distressedly, making it look as if it had been used for decades.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pristinely - in a way that is clean and in perfect condition
  • synonym: shabbily - in a way that looks old or worn out
