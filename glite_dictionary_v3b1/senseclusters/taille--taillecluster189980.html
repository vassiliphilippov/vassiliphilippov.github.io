taille

noun
tenor oboe or part
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
a historical tenor oboe or the tenor instrumental or vocal part in baroque orchestras

Examples

Baroque taille part
The taille added a rich, mellow sound to the baroque orchestra's performance.
In some early music ensembles, the taille is played on a specially built tenor oboe.
The composer wrote a challenging passage for the taille in the second movement.
