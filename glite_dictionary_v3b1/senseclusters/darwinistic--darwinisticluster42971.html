darwinistic

adjective
competitive, survival-based
8.9
Pronunciation: /dɑːr.wɪ.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /dɑː.wɪ.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the application of natural selection and competition, especially in social, economic, or political contexts

Examples

Darwinistic competition
The company adopted a darwinistic approach, rewarding only the highest achievers and ignoring teamwork.
Some critics argue that a darwinistic society leaves vulnerable people without support.
His darwinistic attitude toward business meant he valued competition above all else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitive - involving or determined by competition
  • antonym: cooperative - involving mutual assistance in working toward a common goal
  • synonym: ruthless - having or showing no pity or compassion for others
