settle down

verb
become calm after disturbance
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬l daʊn / (AmE), / ˈsɛtl daʊn / (BrE)
to become quiet, calm, or peaceful after being agitated, excited, or disturbed

Examples

Kids settle down after play
After moving into their new home, things finally started to settle down.
It took the children a few minutes to settle down after playing outside.
The crowd began to settle down once the speaker took the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm down - become calmer or less agitated
  • antonym: get worked up - become agitated or excited
