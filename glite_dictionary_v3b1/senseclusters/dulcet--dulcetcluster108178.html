dulcet

adjective
sweet and pleasant, gentle (esp. of sounds)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌl.sɪt /; / ˈdʌl.sət / (AmE), / ˈdʌl.sɪt / (BrE)
(of a sound) sweet, soothing, and pleasant to hear

Examples

Dulcet tones
His voice was so dulcet that the audience was completely captivated.
She awoke to the dulcet sounds of birds singing outside her window.
The dulcet tones of the harp calmed everyone in the room.
Birdsong filled the morning air with a dulcet melody.
The dulcet sound of her voice soothed everyone in the room.
We listened to the dulcet notes of the piano drifting from the open window.
