parched

adjective
lightly roasted or toasted (esp. of grains)
5.2 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / p…ërt Ét / (AmE), / p…ëÀêt Ét / (BrE)
(of grains or seeds) toasted or roasted slightly, often by dry heat.

Examples

Parched grains
He offered us parched barley, a staple food in his region.
Long journeys often included bags of parched corn as an easy snack.
Native Americans commonly prepared parched seeds for winter storage.

Synonyms

  • toasted
    1.0 1.11/m
    toasted is browned on the surface by heat for taste, while parched describes grains lightly dried or heated until crisp.
  • roasted
    1.0 1.08/m
    roasted is cooked through by dry heat, often for meat, while parched means grains are only lightly dried or toasted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toasted - (of food) browned by exposure to dry heat
