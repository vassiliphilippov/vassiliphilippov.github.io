jaunting car

noun
Irish two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔntɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːntɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
an open, two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, formerly common in Ireland, used for carrying passengers

Examples

A jaunting car ride
Photographs from the 1800s often show families seated in a jaunting car drawn by a sturdy horse.
The novel describes a journey across Ireland in a jaunting car.
Tourists enjoyed a ride through the countryside in a traditional jaunting car.
