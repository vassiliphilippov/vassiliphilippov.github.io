immunofluorescence

noun
antibody-based fluorescent detection technique
9.6 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / …™Àåmjun.o ä.fl äÀàr…õs.…ôns / (AmE), / …™ÀåmjuÀê.n…ô ä.fl äÀàr…õs.…ôns / (BrE)
a technique in immunology where antibodies coupled to a fluorescent dye are used to detect specific antigens in a tissue sample under a microscope

Examples

Immunofluorescence technique
Advances in immunofluorescence have significantly improved diagnostic accuracy in pathology laboratories.
Researchers used immunofluorescence to visualize the distribution of specific proteins in the cell.
The presence of the virus was confirmed by immunofluorescence staining in the tissue samples.
