chugger

noun
fast drinker
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌɡə / (BrE)
a person who drinks a beverage, especially alcohol, quickly in one continuous action

Examples

Beer chugger
At the party, the chugger downed his beer in seconds and cheered loudly.
Everyone laughed as the fastest chugger finished his soda before anyone else.
He became known as a chugger after winning the drinking contest three years in a row.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gulp - to swallow a drink or food quickly
  • antonym: sipper - a person who drinks slowly in small amounts
