orchard apple tree

noun
tree cultivated for edible apples
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrtʃərd ˈæpəl tri / (AmE), / ˈɔːtʃəd ˈæpl triː / (BrE)
A Eurasian tree (Malus domestica) widely cultivated for its firm, rounded, edible fruits, commonly known as apples.

Examples

The orchard apple tree is a staple in many European and American farms.
