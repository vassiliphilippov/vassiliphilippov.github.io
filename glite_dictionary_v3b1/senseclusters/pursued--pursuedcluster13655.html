pursued

adjective
followed with hostile intent
Pronunciation: / pərˈsuːd / (AmE), / pəˈsjuːd / (BrE)
followed, especially with hostile or harmful intent

Examples

Pursued target
She felt like a pursued target, constantly looking over her shoulder.
The pursued prisoner darted through the alleyways, desperate to escape his captors.
Their eyes were filled with fear, like pursued animals fleeing a predator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunted - being followed to capture or kill
  • antonym: safe - not in danger; not being followed or threatened
