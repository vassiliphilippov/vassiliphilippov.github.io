succulently

adverb
in a fleshy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkjələntli / (AmE), / ˈsʌkjʊləntli / (BrE)
in a way that is thick, fleshy, and able to retain water, like a succulent plant.

Examples

Succulently growing leaves
The cactus leaves grew succulently after the heavy rains, storing water for the dry season.
Some desert plants survive by developing succulently thick stems that hold moisture.
The aloe vera plant expanded succulently, its leaves plump and full of water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juicily - in a juicy or moist manner
