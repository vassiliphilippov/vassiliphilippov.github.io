sober discussion

noun
serious, calm conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbər dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a conversation conducted in a serious, calm, and thoughtful manner, without exaggeration or emotional excess

We need to have a sober discussion before making any final decisions.
