sober discussion

noun
serious, calm conversation
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbər dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a conversation conducted in a serious, calm, and thoughtful manner, without exaggeration or emotional excess

Examples

A sober discussion
After the debate, the leaders sat down for a sober discussion about possible reforms.
Despite the tension, their sober discussion led to a mutual understanding.
We need to have a sober discussion before making any final decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lighthearted discussion - a conversation marked by a cheerful, casual, or carefree manner
  • synonym: serious discussion - a conversation marked by seriousness or gravity
