factum

noun
legal argument document
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.təm / (AmE), / ˈfæk.təm / (BrE)
a written document presenting the facts and legal arguments submitted to a court, especially in appellate cases

Examples

Submit a factum
The lawyer submitted a detailed factum to support her client's appeal.
Each party must file a factum before the appellate court reviews the case.
The judge carefully read the factum to understand the legal arguments presented.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brief - a written statement submitted to a court outlining the arguments of one side
