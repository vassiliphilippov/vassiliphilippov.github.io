nonlegislative

adjective
not about lawmaking
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌlɛdʒɪˈsleɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌlɛdʒɪˈsleɪtɪv / (BrE)

Examples

Nonlegislative duties

Her role is strictly nonlegislative and does not involve drafting any laws.
The committee's nonlegislative activities include public outreach and education.
