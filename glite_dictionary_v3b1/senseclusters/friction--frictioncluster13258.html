Synonyms

  • friction between
    4.0 0.8/m
    friction between stresses tension or lack of harmony between two groups, while friction names general tension among people or groups.
  • discord
    5.6 2/m
    discord means a lack of harmony or bad feeling between people, while friction describes general tension or disagreement.
  • dissension
    7.5 1/m
    dissension means open disagreement inside a group, while friction covers general tension or conflict between people or groups.
  • disagreement
    1.6 18/m
    disagreement is when people hold different opinions, while friction often implies ongoing tension or bad feeling between them.
  • conflict
    2.3 30/m
    conflict is a serious argument or fight between groups, while friction usually suggests milder, ongoing tension between people.
  • contention
    6.5 3/m
    contention is a heated dispute or point of strong opposition, while friction is broader tension that may be less heated.
  • clash
    3.2 8/m
    clash is a sudden violent fight or strong confrontation, while friction is softer, lasting tension or disagreement between people or groups.
