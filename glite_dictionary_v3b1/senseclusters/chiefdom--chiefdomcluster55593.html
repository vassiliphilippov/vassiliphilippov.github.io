chiefdom

noun
society led by chief
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːfdəm / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːfdəm / (BrE)
a society, territory, or community ruled by a chief, or the position of being a chief

Examples

The chiefdom was known for its complex social structure and ceremonial centers.
Trade between neighboring chiefdoms helped spread new ideas and technologies.

Each chiefdom had its own customs and leadership traditions.

Anthropologists studied the political structure of the ancient chiefdom in the region.
