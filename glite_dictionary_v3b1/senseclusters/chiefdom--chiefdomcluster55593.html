Synonyms

  • chieftaincy
    9.9 0.4/m
    chieftaincy names the authority or office held by a chieftain, while chiefdom refers to the land or society ruled by a chief.
  • chieftainship
    8.6 0.09/m
    chieftainship means the leadership or authority of a chieftain in a tribe, while chiefdom is the community or territory they rule.
  • chiefship
    6.6 0.05/m
    chiefship is the state or period of being a chief, but chiefdom usually names the territory or people under that chief.
