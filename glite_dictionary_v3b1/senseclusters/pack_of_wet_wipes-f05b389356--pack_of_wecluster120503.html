pack of wet wipes

noun
container of moist cleaning cloths
Pronunciation: / pæk əv wɛt waɪps / (AmE), / pak əv wɛt waɪps / (BrE)
a small bundle or container of moist disposable cloths used for cleaning skin or surfaces

A pack of wet wipes
