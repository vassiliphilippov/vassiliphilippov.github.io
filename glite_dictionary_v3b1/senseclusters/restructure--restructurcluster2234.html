restructure

verb
organize or form anew; change structure
4.4 6/m

to organize or form something in a new or different way, often in order to improve it or adapt to new circumstances

Examples

To restructure a company
After the merger, the company decided to restructure its management team.
Many organizations restructure their departments to increase efficiency and reduce costs.
She had to restructure her daily routine to include physical therapy after the surgery.
