hitting

verb
to strike forcefully
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
striking or making forceful contact with something or someone, usually with the hand or an object

Examples

To hit a ball
The baseball player was hitting home runs during the practice session.
She kept hitting the drum to keep the rhythm steady.
He apologized after hitting his friend by accident during the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: missing - failing to hit a target
  • synonym: punching - hitting with a closed fist
  • synonym: striking - delivering a blow or hit to something
