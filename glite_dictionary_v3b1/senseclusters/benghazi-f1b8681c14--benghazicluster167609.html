Benghazi

proper noun
the 2012 attack and related controversy
7.7
Pronunciation: /ben.ˈgɑː.ziː/ (AmE), /ben.ˈgɑː.ziː/ (BrE)
Used (especially in political or journalistic contexts) to refer to the 2012 attack on the U.S. diplomatic compound in Benghazi, Libya, and the subsequent political controversy.
