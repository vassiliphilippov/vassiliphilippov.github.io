boink

noun
light bouncing sound
Pronunciation: / bɔɪŋk / (AmE), / bɔɪŋk / (BrE)
a light, often comical sound made when something bounces or hits a surface softly

Examples

A funny boink
The rubber ball made a funny boink as it bounced off the wall.
We all laughed when the cartoon character landed with a loud boink.
She heard a soft boink when the tennis ball hit the net.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ping - a short, sharp, high-pitched sound
  • degree higher: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
