hematopoietic

adjective
relating to blood cell formation
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmətoʊpaɪˈɛtɪk /; / hiːməˌpɔɪˈɛtɪk /; / ˌhiːməˌpoʊ.iˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛmətəʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk /; / hiːməʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk /; / ˌhiːməʊ.pɔɪˈɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involved in the formation of blood or blood cells.
