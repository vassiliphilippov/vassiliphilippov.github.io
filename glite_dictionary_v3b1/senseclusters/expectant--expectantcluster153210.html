expectant

adjective
pregnant, soon to give birth
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛktənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛktənt / (BrE)
(of a woman) in an advanced stage of pregnancy; soon to give birth.

Examples

Expectant mother
During her expectant months, she read many books about parenting.
She joined a support group for expectant women in her third trimester.
The hospital has a special ward for expectant mothers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pregnant - having a baby developing in the body
