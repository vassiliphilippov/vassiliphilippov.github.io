haj

noun
the Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca
Pronunciation: / hɑːdʒ / (AmE), / hɑːdʒ / (BrE)
the Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca that is required as the fifth pillar of Islam, to be undertaken at least once in a lifetime by every able Muslim

Examples

The haj pilgrimage
Completing the haj is a dream for many practicing Muslims.
Her father saved for years so he could go on the haj with his family.
Muslims from all over the world gather in Mecca to perform the haj.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pilgrimage - a journey to a sacred place for religious reasons
