babysitter test

noun
test of childcare skills
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.biˌsɪt.ɚ tɛst / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.biˌsɪt.ə tɛst / (BrE)
a situation or scenario designed to assess a person's ability to care for children, especially under challenging circumstances

Examples
