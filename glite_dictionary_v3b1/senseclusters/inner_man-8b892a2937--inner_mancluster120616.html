inner man

noun
one's inner self or soul
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnɚ mæn / (AmE), / ˈɪnə mæn / (BrE)
a person's inner self or soul; one's spiritual or moral nature as distinct from the physical body

Examples

The inner man speaks
A true leader listens to the quiet voice of his inner man when making decisions.
She spends time meditating every morning to connect with her inner man.
Some people find that art and music nourish their inner man in profound ways.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outer man - a person's physical self or body
  • synonym: soul - the spiritual or immaterial part of a human being
