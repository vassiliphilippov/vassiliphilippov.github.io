pervertedly

adverb
in a twisted manner
Pronunciation: / pərˈvɜrtɪdli / (AmE), / pəˈvɜːtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is morally wrong, twisted, or deviates from what is considered normal or acceptable

Examples

Pervertedly enjoys chaos
He seemed to enjoy the chaos pervertedly, finding pleasure in others' discomfort.
The villain pervertedly admired the suffering he caused throughout the story.
She laughed pervertedly at the misfortune that befell her rivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deviantly - in a way that departs from accepted standards
  • antonym: morally - in a way that is considered right or good
