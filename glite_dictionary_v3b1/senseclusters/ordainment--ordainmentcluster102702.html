ordainment

noun
official appointment
8.5
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈdeɪn.mənt/ /; / /ɔːr.ˈdeɪn.mənt/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈdeɪn.mənt/ (BrE)
the act of officially establishing, ordering, or appointing something by authority

Examples

Ordainment of leaders
The ordainment of the new committee members was announced at the annual meeting.
After the ordainment of stricter regulations, the industry saw significant improvements.
His ordainment as the head of the department was met with widespread approval.
