tichel

noun
Jewish women's headscarf
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəl / (BrE)
a headscarf worn by some Jewish women, especially for religious or cultural reasons

Examples

A tichel headscarf

Many women in the community choose to wear a tichel as a sign of modesty.
Her colorful tichel matched the rest of her festive outfit.
