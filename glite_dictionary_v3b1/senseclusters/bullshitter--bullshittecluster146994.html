bullshitter

noun
dishonest talker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌʃɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌʃɪtə /; / ˈbʊlʃɪtə / (BrE)
a person who talks in a way that is not truthful, often making things up or bluffing without caring about the truth

Examples

A bullshitter talks
Everyone knew that Mark was a bullshitter who exaggerated his stories at every opportunity.
You can always spot a bullshitter by how confidently they invent details.
She called him a bullshitter when he claimed to have met the president.
Everyone knew he was a bullshitter who made up stories to impress people.
She called him out as a bullshitter when he started exaggerating his achievements.
You can always spot a bullshitter in a group discussion by how much they talk without substance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluffer - a person who pretends to know more than they do
  • synonym: liar - a person who tells lies
  • antonym: truth-teller - a person who always tells the truth
