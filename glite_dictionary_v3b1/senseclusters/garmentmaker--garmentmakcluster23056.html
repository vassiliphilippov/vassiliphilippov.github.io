garmentmaker

noun
person who makes garments
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrməntˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːməntˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making garments, especially by sewing

Examples

A garmentmaker sewing clothes
Her grandmother was a skilled garmentmaker who made clothes for the entire family.
Many traditional garmentmakers still prefer to use hand-sewing techniques.
The garmentmaker carefully stitched the pieces of fabric together to create a beautiful dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tailor - a person whose occupation is making or altering garments, especially men's suits
