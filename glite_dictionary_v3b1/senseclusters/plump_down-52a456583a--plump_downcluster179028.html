plump down

verb
set down heavily or noisily
2.5
Pronunciation: / plʌmp daʊn / (AmE), / plʌmp daʊn / (BrE)
to set something or oneself down with a sudden, heavy, or noisy motion

Examples

To plump down a bag
He plumped down his suitcase by the door with a sigh of relief.
She plumped down on the couch after a long day at work.
The cat plumped down in the armchair and immediately fell asleep.

Related Terms
