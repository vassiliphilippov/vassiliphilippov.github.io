rhubarb plant

noun
plant with edible stalks and poisonous leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈruˌbɑrb plænt / (AmE), / ˈruːˌbɑːb plɑːnt / (BrE)
a perennial plant with long green or reddish leafstalks, edible when cooked, while the leaves are poisonous
