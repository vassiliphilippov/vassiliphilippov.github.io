Synonyms

  • protect
    1.0 187/m
    protect keeps someone or something safe from harm in general, while defend often means fighting to stop a direct threat.
  • safeguard
    3.7 5.04/m
    safeguard means taking planned actions to make things safe, while defend often means actively fighting to stop attackers.
  • shield
    2.7 10/m
    shield covers or blocks someone from harm, while defend usually focuses on protecting by fighting back against the danger.
  • guard
    1.0 15/m
    guard watches over a place or person to stop harm, while defend often means fighting attackers to protect them.
  • fend off
    3.9 2.89/m
    fend off repels an attack or threat in the moment, while defend can mean ongoing protection or fighting to keep safe.
  • fight off
    3.7 2.42/m
    fight off succeeds in repelling an attack, while defend also includes protecting before an attack or helping others under threat.
  • fight back
    3.3 7.42/m
    fight back means to retaliate or resist after being attacked, while defend is broader protection and may not mean striking back.
  • fight
    1.0 88/m
    fight often means to struggle or try hard against something, while defend focuses on protecting people or things from harm or attack.
