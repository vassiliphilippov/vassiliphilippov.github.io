granola

noun
person with natural/eco lifestyle
7.8
Pronunciation: /grə.ˈnoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /grə.ˈnəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
informal, slang A person who prefers natural foods, environmentalism, outdoor activities, and a back-to-nature or health-focused lifestyle.
