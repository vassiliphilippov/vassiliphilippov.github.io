lek

noun
animal mating display site
Pronunciation: / lɛk / (AmE), / lɛk / (BrE)
a place where male animals gather to display and attract females for mating, especially in certain bird species

Examples

Bird lek display
Each spring, the grouse return to the same lek to perform their elaborate dances.
Scientists observed several males competing for attention at the lek early in the morning.
The lek was filled with the sounds of birds calling and displaying for potential mates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arena - a place where animals gather for mating displays
