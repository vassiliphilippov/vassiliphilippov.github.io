carinated

adjective
having a ridge or keel-like shape
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkærɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a ridge or shaped like a keel, especially resembling the keel of a ship; used chiefly of plant parts or animal structures with a prominent raised line or ridge

Examples

A carinated sternum
A carinated sternum is a key feature in the skeletal structure of flying birds.
The flower’s sepals are carinated, each with a distinct central ridge.
The leaf blades are carinated near the base, giving them a boat-like appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keeled - having a conspicuous ridge or keel
