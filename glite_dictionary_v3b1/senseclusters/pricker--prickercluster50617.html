pricker

noun
tool for making small holes
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkər / (AmE), / ˈprɪkə / (BrE)
a pointed hand tool used for making small holes, especially for brads or small screws

Examples

Woodworking pricker
A pricker is an essential tool for detailed woodwork projects.
Before starting the stitching, she made tiny holes with a pricker.
He used a pricker to mark the spots where each screw would go.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awl - a small pointed tool for piercing holes, especially in leather
