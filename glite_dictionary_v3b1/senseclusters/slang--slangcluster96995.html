slang

noun
informal, nonstandard language
2.7 6.3
Pronunciation: / slæŋ / (AmE), / slæŋ / (BrE)
informal language, words, or expressions used by particular groups and not considered appropriate in formal situations

Examples

Some slang terms quickly spread online and become widely used.

Related Terms

  • antonym: standard language - the form of a language accepted as the norm, especially in formal settings
