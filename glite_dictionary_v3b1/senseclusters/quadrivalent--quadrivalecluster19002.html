quadrivalent

adjective
having four parts
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑːd.rɪ.veɪ.lənt/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒd.rɪ.veɪ.lənt/ (BrE)
having four parts, components, or acting against four different targets, especially in medical or scientific contexts

Examples

Quadrivalent vaccine
The quadrivalent vaccine protects against four strains of the virus.
Researchers developed a quadrivalent antibody to target multiple disease pathways.
A quadrivalent structure is often more stable than one with fewer components.
