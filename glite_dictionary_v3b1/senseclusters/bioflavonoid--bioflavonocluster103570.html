bioflavonoid

noun
plant pigment compound
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˈfleɪvəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˈfleɪvənɔɪd / (BrE)
a plant compound (flavonoid), often yellow, found in fruits and vegetables, that may have health benefits and helps give plants their color
