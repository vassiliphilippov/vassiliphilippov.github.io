microfinance

noun
small-scale financial services
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊfaɪˌnæns / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊfaɪˌnæns / (BrE)
a system of providing small loans and financial services to people or small businesses with low income or no access to traditional banks

Examples

Microfinance programs
Many women started their own businesses thanks to microfinance programs in their village.
The organization offers microfinance to help small farmers invest in better equipment.
Access to microfinance can improve the lives of people in developing countries.
Many small businesses in rural areas rely on microfinance to get started.
The organization offers microfinance to help families improve their living conditions.
Through microfinance, entrepreneurs can access the funds they need to grow their businesses.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: banking - the business of managing money and providing loans
  • synonym: microcredit - the lending of very small amounts of money to poor borrowers
