overdominant

adjective
excessively controlling
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪnənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdɒmɪnənt / (BrE)
having or showing excessive dominance or control over others

Examples

Overdominant manager
The manager's overdominant approach made it difficult for employees to share their ideas.
Parents should avoid being overdominant so their children can develop independence.
His overdominant personality often led to conflicts within the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domineering - asserting one's will over others in an arrogant way
  • antonym: submissive - willing to obey or yield to others
