Abkhaz

noun
member of a Caucasian ethnic group in Abkhazia
Pronunciation: / æbˈkɑz / (AmE), / abˈkɑːz /; / æbˈkɑːz / (BrE)
a member of the Abkhaz ethnic group living mainly in Abkhazia, a region east of the Black Sea
