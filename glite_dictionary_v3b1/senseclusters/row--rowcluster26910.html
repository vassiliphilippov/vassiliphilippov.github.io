row

noun
horizontal line of things side by side
Pronunciation: / roʊ / (AmE), / rəʊ / (BrE)
a linear arrangement of numbers, letters, or symbols placed side by side, especially in a table or list

Examples

A row in a table
Enter each value in a separate row of the spreadsheet.
She highlighted the entire row to edit the information.
The data is displayed as a row of numbers across the screen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: column - a vertical division of numbers, letters, or symbols in a table
