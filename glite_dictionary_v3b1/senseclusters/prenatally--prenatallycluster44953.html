prenatally

adverb
before birth
4.4
Pronunciation: / priːˈneɪtəli / (AmE), / priːˈneɪtəli / (BrE)
in a way that occurs or is done before birth, especially during pregnancy

Examples

Detected prenatally
Some genetic conditions can be detected prenatally through specialized screening tests.
The mother received important medical advice prenatally to ensure a healthy pregnancy.
Doctors may recommend certain vitamins be taken prenatally for the baby's development.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postnatally - in a way that occurs after birth
