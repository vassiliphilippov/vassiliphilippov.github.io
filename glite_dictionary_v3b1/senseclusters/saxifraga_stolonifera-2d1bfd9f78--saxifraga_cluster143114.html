Saxifraga stolonifera

noun
species of ornamental perennial plant
Pronunciation: / ˌsæksɪˈfreɪɡə stoʊˌlɒnɪˈfɛrə / (AmE), / ˌsæksɪˈfrɑːɡə stɒˌlɒnɪˈfɪərə / (BrE)
A perennial plant of eastern Asia, known for its small red-and-white flowers and spreading by creeping stolons, often grown as a houseplant or in gardens.
