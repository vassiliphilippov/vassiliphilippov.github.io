oh dark thirty

noun
very early time in the morning
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊ dɑrk ˈθɜːrti / (AmE), / əʊ dɑːk ˈθɜːti / (BrE)
A humorous term for a very early time in the morning, typically around 12:30 a.m. or before dawn, used especially in military contexts.

Examples

Oh dark thirty morning
She arrived at the airport at oh dark thirty to avoid traffic.
Training started at oh dark thirty, long before the sun was up.
We had to catch our flight at oh dark thirty this morning, so I barely got any sleep.
