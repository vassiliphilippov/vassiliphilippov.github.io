hajj

noun
Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca
6.1 6.7
Pronunciation: /hædʒ/ /; / /hɑːdʒ/ /; / /hʌdʒ/ (AmE), /hædʒ/ /; / /hɑːdʒ/ /; / /hʌdʒ/ (BrE)
The pilgrimage to Mecca that every Muslim is expected to undertake at least once in their lifetime, if able; one of the Five Pillars of Islam.

Examples

Muslims from around the world travel to Mecca to perform the hajj each year.
