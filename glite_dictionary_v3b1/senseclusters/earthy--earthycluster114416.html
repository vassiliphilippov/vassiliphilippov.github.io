earthy

adjective
crude or indecent (humor, language)
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθi / (AmE), / ˈɜːθi / (BrE)
(of humor, language, or behavior) conspicuously crude or indecent; lacking refinement or delicacy.

Examples

Earthy humor
Her novels are known for their earthy sense of humor and candid language.
Some people appreciate earthy humor, while others may find it offensive.
The comedian's earthy jokes kept the audience both amused and blushing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarse - lacking refinement or delicacy; crude
  • antonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance or manner
  • synonym: vulgar - lacking sophistication; indecent or crude
