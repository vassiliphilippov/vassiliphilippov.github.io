the act of allowing oneself to enjoy something, especially something pleasurable or not necessary, sometimes with little self-restraint

Examples

A sweet indulgence
His weekend gambling spree was another indulgence that left him regretting his choices.
She apologized for her momentary indulgence in gossip at the office party.
Sometimes a small indulgence is harmless, but repeated carelessness can cause real problems.

Synonyms

    self-indulgence is excessive, uncontrolled pleasure seeking, while indulgence is allowing yourself enjoyment that may lack a little self-restraint.
  • self-gratification
    6.8 0.13/m
    self-gratification is the act or feeling of satisfying personal desires, while indulgence is allowing yourself enjoyment, sometimes with little restraint.
  • pampering
    4.1 0.69/m
    pampering is giving someone extra comfort or luxury, while indulgence is allowing yourself pleasure that may be unnecessary or excessive.
