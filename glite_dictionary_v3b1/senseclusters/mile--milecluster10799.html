mile

noun
a one-mile footrace
5.1
Pronunciation: / maɪl / (AmE), / maɪl / (BrE)
a footrace covering a distance of one mile (1,609.34 metres), especially in track and field competitions

Examples

A mile race
He trained for months to improve his time in the mile.
She won the gold medal in the mile at the national championships.
The school track team specializes in both the mile and the 800-meter events.
