nonethnic

adjective
not related to ethnicity
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɛθnɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈɛθnɪk / (BrE)
not related to, based on, or characteristic of any particular ethnicity or ethnic group

Examples

Nonethnic group
The organization welcomes both nonethnic and ethnic members from all backgrounds.
Some traditions are considered nonethnic because they are shared by people of many cultures.
The study focused on nonethnic factors that influence community development.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ethnic - relating to a particular ethnic group
