multiverse

noun
set of universes
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltɪˌvɜrs / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌvɜːs / (BrE)
a hypothetical set of multiple possible universes, including the one we live in, often discussed in science and science fiction

Examples

The multiverse theory
Some scientists believe our universe is just one part of a vast multiverse.
The idea of a multiverse is popular in many science fiction stories and movies.
If the multiverse exists, there could be infinite versions of ourselves in other universes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmos - the universe seen as a well-ordered whole
  • degree lower: universe - all of space, time, matter, and energy as a single whole
