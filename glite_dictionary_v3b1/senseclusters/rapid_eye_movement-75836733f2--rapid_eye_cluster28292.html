rapid eye movement

noun
stage of sleep with quick eye movements, dreaming
Pronunciation: / ˈræpɪd aɪ ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈræpɪd aɪ ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a stage of sleep characterized by quick, random movements of the eyes and vivid dreaming

Examples

Rapid eye movement sleep
Infants spend more time in rapid eye movement sleep than adults.
Most of our dreams occur during rapid eye movement sleep stages.
Scientists study rapid eye movement to better understand the nature of dreaming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: REM sleep - period of sleep marked by rapid eye movements and dreaming
