short division

noun
division method performed without written steps
2.4 4.4
Pronunciation: /ʃɔːrt dɪ.ˈvɪʒ.ən/ (AmE), /ʃɔːt dɪ.ˈvɪʒ.ən/ (BrE)
A method of division in which the steps are performed mentally or with minimal writing, rather than recording all intermediate calculations.

Examples

Short division method
If you can do the multiplication tables quickly, short division makes dividing numbers much faster.
Unlike long division, short division keeps your calculations neat and saves time.
We learned short division in primary school before moving on to more complicated strategies.
