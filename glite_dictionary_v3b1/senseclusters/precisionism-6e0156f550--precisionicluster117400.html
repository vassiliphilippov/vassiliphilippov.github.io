Precisionism

noun
American art movement
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪʒənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪʒənɪzəm / (BrE)
an early 20th-century American art movement characterized by precise, geometric forms and sharply defined representations in visual arts

Examples

Precisionism art movement
The museum's new exhibit features several masterpieces of Precisionism from the 1920s.
