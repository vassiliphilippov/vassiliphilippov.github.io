versed

adjective
knowledgeable or experienced in a subject
Pronunciation: / vɝst / (AmE), / vɜːst / (BrE)
having knowledge of or experience with something through study or practice; knowledgeable or skilled in a particular area

Examples

Not everyone is versed in the technical aspects of computer science.
She is versed in several foreign languages, making her an asset to the company.
