Averroist

adjective
of Averroes' philosophy
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛroʊɪst / (AmE), / əˈvɛrəʊɪst / (BrE)
relating to the philosophy or ideas of Averroes, especially those emphasizing reason and rational interpretation of Aristotle.

Examples

Averroist philosophy

Medieval scholars often debated Averroist interpretations of philosophy and religion.
Her thesis explored the influence of Averroist thought on European universities.
