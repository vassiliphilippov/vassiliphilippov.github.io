mallee

noun
type of low-growing Australian eucalyptus
Pronunciation: / ˈmæli / (AmE), / ˈmæli / (BrE)
A low-growing, bushy Australian eucalyptus tree, typically with several slender stems arising from a woody base.

Examples

The mallee tree
Ecologists studied the biodiversity found within the mallee woodlands.
Many species of mallee are adapted to survive bushfires by regenerating from a woody base.
The mallee covers large areas of southern Australia with its dense, shrubby growth.
