political scientist

noun
expert in political science
3.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in political science, particularly in systems of government and political activity

Examples

A political scientist studies government
After earning her doctorate, she became a renowned political scientist at a leading university.
As a political scientist, he specializes in comparative studies of electoral systems.
The government consulted a political scientist to better understand the effects of its new policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: politician - a person who is professionally involved in politics, especially as a holder of an elected office
