dimpled

adjective
with small hollows
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmpəld / (AmE), / ˈdɪmpəld / (BrE)
having small hollow marks or indentations, especially on the cheeks or chin, often visible when smiling

Examples

Dimpled cheeks
Her dimpled cheeks made her smile even more charming.
The golf ball's dimpled surface helps it travel farther through the air.
He gave a dimpled grin that made everyone in the room smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitted - marked with small depressions or indentations
  • antonym: smooth - having an even surface without indentations
