detassel

verb
to remove plant tassel
Pronunciation: / diˈtæsəl / (AmE), / diˈtæsəl / (BrE)
to remove the tassel from a plant, especially corn, to control pollination or produce hybrid seeds

Examples

To detassel corn
Every summer, students are hired to detassel cornfields in the Midwest.
Farmers detassel certain rows of corn to ensure the production of hybrid varieties.
Learning how to detassel properly is important for anyone working in seed corn production.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pollinate - to transfer pollen to a plant to allow fertilization
  • synonym: prune - to cut away unwanted parts of a plant
