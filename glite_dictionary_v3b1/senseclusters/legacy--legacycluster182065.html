legacy

noun
something left behind
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡəsi / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡəsi / (BrE)
something handed down or left behind from the past, especially money, property, or achievements

Examples

A lasting legacy
The scientist's discoveries became a legacy that inspired future generations.
She left a generous legacy to her grandchildren in her will.
The traditions of the village are a cultural legacy from ancient times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heritage - valued objects and qualities passed down from previous generations
  • synonym: inheritance - property or money received from someone who has died
