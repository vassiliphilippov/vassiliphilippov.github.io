pisco

proper noun
Peruvian port city
Pronunciation: / ˈpiskoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpiːskəʊ / (BrE)
a port city on the southern coast of Peru, known for its fishing industry and as the namesake of the pisco spirit

Examples

Pisco port city
We visited the port town of pisco and walked along the waterfront at sunset.

Many tourists travel to pisco before exploring the nearby Paracas National Reserve.
