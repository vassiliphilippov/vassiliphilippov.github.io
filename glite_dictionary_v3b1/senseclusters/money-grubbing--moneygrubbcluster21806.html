money-grubbing

noun
greedy pursuit of money
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌni ˌɡrʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌɡrʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of trying to get as much money as possible, often in a greedy or dishonest way

Examples

Corporate money-grubbing
The company's money-grubbing was obvious in its constant price increases and hidden fees.
Many people criticized the politician's money-grubbing during the fundraising campaign.
Some believe that money-grubbing has become too common in the entertainment industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avarice - extreme greed for wealth or material gain
  • antonym: generosity - the quality of being kind and giving to others
  • synonym: greed - an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth
