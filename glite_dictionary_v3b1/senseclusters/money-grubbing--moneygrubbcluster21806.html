money-grubbing

noun
greedy pursuit of money
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌn.ɪ ˈgrʌb.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmʌn.ɪ ˈgrʌb.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
