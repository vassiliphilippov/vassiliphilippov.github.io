pointe

noun
land projecting into water
7.9
Pronunciation: / pwænt / (AmE), / pwant / (BrE)
a point of land or promontory projecting into a body of water, often used in place names

Examples

Pointe lighthouse
The lighthouse stood at the very tip of the pointe, guiding ships safely to shore.
We stopped for a picnic on the grassy pointe overlooking the lake.
Many birds nest along the rocky pointe that juts into the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cape - a large headland extending into a body of water
  • synonym: peninsula - a piece of land almost surrounded by water or projecting out into a body of water
