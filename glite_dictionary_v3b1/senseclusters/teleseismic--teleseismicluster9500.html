teleseismic

adjective
distant earthquake-related
9.9
Pronunciation: /tel.ɪ.ˈsaɪz.mɪk/ (AmE), /tel.ɪ.ˈsaɪz.mɪk/ (BrE)
relating to earthquakes or seismic waves that originate at a great distance from the observation point

Examples

Teleseismic waves
Researchers use teleseismic waves to study the structure of the Earth's deep interior.
The teleseismic event was detected thousands of kilometers from its epicenter.
Advanced equipment is required to accurately record teleseismic signals from distant earthquakes.

  • synonym: seismic - relating to earthquakes or vibrations of the earth
