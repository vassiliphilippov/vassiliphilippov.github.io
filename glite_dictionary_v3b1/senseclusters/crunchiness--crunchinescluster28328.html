crunchiness

noun
eco-friendly lifestyle
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having beliefs, lifestyle, or appearance associated with environmentalism, natural living, or alternative 'back-to-nature' values, especially in an informal or stereotypical way

Examples

Her crunchiness shows
Her crunchiness was obvious from her homemade granola and commitment to zero-waste living.
Some people admire his crunchiness, while others find it a bit extreme.
The community center hosts workshops that celebrate crunchiness and sustainable practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eco-friendliness - the quality of being environmentally friendly
  • antonym: mainstream - conventional or typical beliefs and lifestyle
