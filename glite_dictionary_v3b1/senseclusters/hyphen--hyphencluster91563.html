hyphen

noun
punctuation mark used to join or divide words
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪfən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪfən / (BrE)
The punctuation mark ( – ), used to join words or parts of words, or to divide a word at the end of a line.

Examples

A hyphen in a word
Remember to use a hyphen after the prefix in 'pre-existing'.
The word 'well-known' contains a hyphen between 'well' and 'known'.
When a word doesn't fit at the end of a line, you may need to insert a hyphen.
