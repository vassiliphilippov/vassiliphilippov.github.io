yeast

verb
to add yeast
7.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / jiËst / (AmE), / jiËst / (BrE)
to add yeast to dough or a liquid in order to cause fermentation or make it rise

Examples

To yeast the dough
Before baking, she yeasted the dough to ensure a light and airy texture.
Some traditional recipes require you to yeast the mixture and let it rest overnight.
They always yeast their bread by hand for the best flavor.

Synonyms

  • leaven the dough
    4.4 0.00/m
