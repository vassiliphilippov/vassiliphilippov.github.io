carbine

noun
a light, short-barrelled rifle
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrˌbiːn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbiːn / (BrE)
a light automatic or semi-automatic rifle, often shorter than a standard rifle and designed for portability

Examples

A carbine rifle
Many police forces have adopted the carbine for use in close urban environments.
The lightweight carbine is ideal for paratroopers and special operations forces.
The soldier carried a carbine instead of a full-length rifle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rifle - a shoulder-fired firearm with a rifled barrel
