begetter

noun
originator or creator
7.7
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡɛtər / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a person who creates, originates, or brings something into existence

Examples

Begetter of ideas
He is often called the begetter of the new movement.
As the begetter of this idea, she received much recognition.
Historians debated who was the true begetter of the scientific revolution.
