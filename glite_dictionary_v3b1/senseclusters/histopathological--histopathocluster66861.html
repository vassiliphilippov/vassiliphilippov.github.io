histopathological

adjective
microscopic tissue analysis
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstoʊˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəʊˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the microscopic examination of tissue in order to study or diagnose disease

Examples

The histopathological findings
The histopathological findings revealed abnormal cell growth in the tissue sample.
Doctors rely on histopathological reports to confirm the presence of certain diseases.
A histopathological examination is essential for diagnosing many types of cancer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: histological - relating to the study of the microscopic structure of tissues
  • synonym: pathological - relating to the study of disease
