the rough

noun
area of long grass on a golf course
4.5
Pronunciation: / ðə rʌf / (AmE), / ðə rʌf / (BrE)
an area on a golf course where the grass is longer and more difficult to play from than the fairway

Examples

Ball in the rough
Golfers do their best to avoid the rough during tournaments.
He struggled to make a clean shot out of the rough on the 15th hole.
Her ball landed in the rough, adding extra difficulty to her next shot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fairway - the closely mown part of a golf course between the tee and the green
