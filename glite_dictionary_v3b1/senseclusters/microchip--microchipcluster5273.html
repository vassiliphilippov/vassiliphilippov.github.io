microchip

verb
to implant ID chip
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌtʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌtʃɪp / (BrE)
to implant a small electronic chip under the skin of an animal for identification purposes

Examples

To microchip a pet
All adopted pets must be microchipped before leaving the shelter.
The veterinarian microchipped the puppy to help ensure it could be identified if lost.
Many animal shelters now require that every cat and dog be microchipped for safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: identify - to recognize or establish the identity of someone or something
  • synonym: tag - to attach an identification label to an animal
