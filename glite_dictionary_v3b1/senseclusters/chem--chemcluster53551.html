chem

noun
British pharmacy
8.9
Pronunciation: / kɛm / (BrE)
an informal British term for a pharmacy or a pharmacist, especially a shop where medicines are dispensed

Examples

The chem on Main Street
She stopped by the chem to pick up her prescription after work.
If you need painkillers, the chem on the corner is open late.
My brother works at the local chem and helps customers with their medicines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemist - a shop or person that prepares and sells medicines in the UK
  • synonym: pharmacy - a shop or store where medicines are prepared and sold
