protease

noun
enzyme that breaks down proteins
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.tieɪz / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.ti.eɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that breaks down proteins into smaller peptides or amino acids through proteolysis

Examples

Digestive protease
A digestive protease helps to break down food proteins in the stomach.
Researchers are studying how a viral protease affects the replication of the virus.
Some bacteria secrete a protease that can degrade tissue proteins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enzyme - a substance produced by a living organism that acts as a catalyst
  • synonym: peptidase - an enzyme that breaks down peptides into amino acids, similar to proteases
  • synonym: proteinase - another term for a protease, an enzyme that breaks down proteins
