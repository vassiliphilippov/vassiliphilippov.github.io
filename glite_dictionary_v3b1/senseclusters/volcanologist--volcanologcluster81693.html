volcanologist

noun
volcano scientist
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌvɑːlkəˈnɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌvɒlkəˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies volcanoes and volcanic activity

Examples

A volcanologist studies volcanoes
The volcanologist traveled to Indonesia to study the recent eruption.
A team of volcanologists installed sensors on the mountain to monitor volcanic activity.
As a volcanologist, she often works in dangerous and remote locations.
The volcanologist collected rock samples near the active crater for further analysis.
After the eruption, a team of volcanologists arrived to assess the damage and monitor the site.
She became a volcanologist because she was fascinated by the power of volcanoes.
