spinout

noun
vehicle skidding accident
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈspɪ.naʊt/ (AmE), /ˈspɪ.naʊt/ (BrE)
an accident in which a vehicle loses control and spins around, often due to slippery conditions.

Examples

A car spinout
The icy road caused a dangerous spinout on the highway this morning.
After the sudden spinout, the driver managed to regain control of the car.
Heavy rain increases the risk of a spinout when taking sharp turns too quickly.
