holiday season

noun
period when many people take holidays
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːlɪdeɪ ˈsiːzən /; / ˈhɑlɪˌdeɪ ˈsizn̩ / (AmE), / ˈhɒlɪdeɪ ˈsiːzn̩ /; / ˈhɒlɪˌdeɪ ˈsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the period around major holidays, especially in late December, when people celebrate, travel, and there is increased activity and shopping
