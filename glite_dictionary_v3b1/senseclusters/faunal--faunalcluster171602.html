faunal

adjective
relating to animals
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔnəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the animals of a particular region, period, or environment

Examples

Faunal survey
Scientists conducted a faunal survey to document the animal species living in the wetland.
The faunal composition of the island changed after new predators were introduced.
Paleontologists study faunal remains to understand ancient ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: floral - relating to the plants of a particular region or period
  • synonym: zoological - relating to animals or the study of animals
