office

noun
group of office workers
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒf.ɪs / (BrE)
The group of professional or clerical workers employed in an office, regarded collectively.

Examples

The office laughs
After the announcement, the mood in the office was unusually upbeat.
Most of the office stayed late to finish the important project.
The entire office gathered in the conference room for the meeting.

  • synonym: staff - the group of people who work for an organization
