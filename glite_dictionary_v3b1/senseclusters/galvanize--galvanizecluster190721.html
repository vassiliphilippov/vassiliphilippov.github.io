galvanize

verb
stimulate muscles with electric shock
7.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgæl.və.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈgæl.və.naɪz/ (BrE)
To stimulate nerves or muscles by applying an electric shock, typically as part of a medical or physiological procedure.

Examples

To galvanize muscles
Early physiologists would galvanize frog legs to observe muscular contractions in response to electric currents.
In rare cases, doctors may galvanize certain nerves to restore movement after injury.
The therapist used electrodes to galvanize the patient's leg muscles during the rehabilitation session.
Early experiments attempted to galvanise frog legs with electric currents to study muscle contractions.
