water beetle

noun
aquatic beetle with swimming adaptations
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
an aquatic beetle, typically with a smooth oval body and flattened hind legs adapted for swimming
