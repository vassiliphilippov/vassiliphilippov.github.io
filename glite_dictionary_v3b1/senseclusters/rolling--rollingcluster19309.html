rolling

adjective
pronounced with a trill (esp. of 'r')
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a speech sound, especially an 'r') pronounced with a trill, as by vibrating the tongue or uvula.

Examples

Rolling 'r' sound
Her rolling speech reminded me of Spanish pronunciation.
Italian often features rolling consonants, especially the letter 'r'.
The actor exaggerated his rolling r's to sound more dramatic.
