panga

noun
large heavy knife for cutting or as a weapon
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈpaŋɡə / (BrE)
a large, heavy knife with a broad blade, used as a weapon or for cutting thick vegetation, especially in East and Southern Africa

Examples

A panga knife
She bought a new panga at the market before heading into the forest.
The panga can be a dangerous weapon if not handled carefully.
The farmer used a panga to clear the dense undergrowth along the path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machete - a broad, heavy knife used for cutting through thick vegetation
