camp

verb
set up a temporary outdoor shelter
3.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /kæmp/ (AmE), /kæmp/ (BrE)
to stay or live in a tent or temporary shelter, especially outdoors

Examples

We decided to camp near the lake for the weekend.
