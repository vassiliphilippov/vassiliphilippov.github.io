Hertz donut

noun
humorous pun for fictional donut or spare tire
Pronunciation: / hɝts ˈdoʊnət / (AmE), / hɜːts ˈdəʊnət / (BrE)
A humorous pun, either referring to a fictional type of donut or jokingly to a spare tire, playing on the phrase 'hurts, don't it?'

Examples

A Hertz donut joke
At the party, someone handed out a spare tire and called it a Hertz donut.
He offered his friend a Hertz donut as a prank after fixing the car.
The mechanic made everyone laugh with his Hertz donut joke.
