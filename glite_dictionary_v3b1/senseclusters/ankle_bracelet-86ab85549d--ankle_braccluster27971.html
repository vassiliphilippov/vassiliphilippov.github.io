ankle bracelet

noun
electronic monitoring device worn on the ankle
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæŋ.kəl ˈbreɪ.slət/ /; / /ˈæŋ.kəl ˈbreɪ.slɪt/ (AmE), /ˈæŋ.kəl ˈbreɪ.slət/ /; / /ˈæŋ.kəl ˈbreɪ.slɪt/ (BrE)
(law) an electronic device worn around the ankle that monitors a person's location or movement, often used as a condition of release or supervision.

Examples

Electronic ankle bracelet
Before his bail hearing, the judge ordered him to wear an ankle bracelet that reports his location.
The charity provided an ankle bracelet for the parolee so he could return to work.
If you break the rules, the monitoring company will receive alerts from your ankle bracelet immediately.
