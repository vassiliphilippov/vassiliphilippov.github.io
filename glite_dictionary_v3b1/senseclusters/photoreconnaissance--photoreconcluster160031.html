photoreconnaissance

noun
aerial photographic survey
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊrɪˌkɑːnəˈsɑːns / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊrɪˌkɒnəˈsɑːns / (BrE)
the use of aerial or satellite photography to gather information about an area, especially for military, survey, or environmental purposes
