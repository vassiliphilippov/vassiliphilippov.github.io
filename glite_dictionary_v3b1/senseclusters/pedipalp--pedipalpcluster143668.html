pedipalp

noun
spider mouth appendage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdɪˌpælp / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪˌpælp / (BrE)
one of a pair of appendages near the mouth of arachnids (such as spiders and scorpions), used for sensing, feeding, handling prey, or reproduction
