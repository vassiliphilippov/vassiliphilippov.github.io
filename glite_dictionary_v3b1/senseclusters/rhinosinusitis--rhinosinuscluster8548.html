rhinosinusitis

noun
chronic nasal inflammation
8.4
Pronunciation: /raɪ.noʊ.saɪ.ˈnaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /raɪ.noʊ.sɪn.ˈaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /raɪ.nəʊ.saɪ.ˈnaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /raɪ.nəʊ.sɪn.ˈaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
inflammation or infection of both the nasal passages and the sinus cavities, often causing congestion, facial pain, or headaches

Examples

Rhinosinusitis symptoms
The doctor diagnosed her with rhinosinusitis after weeks of nasal congestion and headaches.
Chronic rhinosinusitis can significantly affect a person's quality of life if left untreated.
Patients with rhinosinusitis often report a reduced sense of smell and facial pain.
The doctor diagnosed her with rhinosinusitis after she complained of a blocked nose and facial pressure.
Antibiotics are sometimes prescribed to treat acute rhinosinusitis caused by bacterial infection.
Symptoms of rhinosinusitis include nasal congestion, headache, and pain around the eyes.
The doctor diagnosed her with rhinosinusitis after she complained of congestion.
Antibiotics are sometimes prescribed to treat severe cases of rhinosinusitis.
Symptoms of rhinosinusitis include facial pain, nasal blockage, and a runny nose.
