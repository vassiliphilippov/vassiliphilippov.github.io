labial artery

noun
artery supplying blood to the lips
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bi.əl ˈɑːr.t̬ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bi.əl ˈɑː.tə.ri / (BrE)
an artery branching from the facial artery that supplies blood to the lips

Examples

Labial artery branch
Damage to a labial artery can cause significant swelling of the lip.
The labial artery provides the main blood supply to the upper and lower lips.
The surgeon cauterized the labial artery to control bleeding during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: facial artery - main artery supplying blood to the structures of the face
