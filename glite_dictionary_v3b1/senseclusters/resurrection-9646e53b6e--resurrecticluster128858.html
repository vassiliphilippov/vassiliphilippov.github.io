Resurrection

noun
Christ's rising from the dead (Christianity)
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
In Christian belief, the rising of Christ from the dead on the third day after the Crucifixion.

Examples

The Resurrection of Christ
Belief in the Resurrection gives hope to many Christians around the world.
Christians celebrate the Resurrection as the foundation of their faith.
The story of the Resurrection is told in all four Gospels.
