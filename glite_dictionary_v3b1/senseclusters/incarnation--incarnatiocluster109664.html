incarnation

noun
form or version
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkɑrˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnkɑːˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a particular form or version of someone or something, especially one of several that exist or have existed over time

Examples

Latest incarnation
The latest incarnation of the smartphone features a larger screen and improved battery life.
In each incarnation, the superhero has a different costume and set of powers.
The restaurant has gone through several incarnations since it first opened in the 1980s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manifestation - a visible or tangible form of something
  • synonym: version - a particular form of something differing in certain respects from an earlier form
