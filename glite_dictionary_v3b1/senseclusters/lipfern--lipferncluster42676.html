lipfern

noun
terrestrial fern of the genus Cheilanthes
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪpˌfɝn / (AmE), / ˈlɪpˌfəːn / (BrE)
Any of various terrestrial ferns of the genus Cheilanthes, found in arid and semiarid regions worldwide.
