Dugongidae

noun
family of aquatic mammals including dugongs
9.9
Pronunciation: / duːˈɡɑn.dɪˌdi /; / duːˌɡɑːnˈdʒɪdiː / (AmE), / djuːˈɡɒn.dɪˌdiː /; / djuːˌɡɒnˈdʒɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of aquatic mammals in the order Sirenia, including dugongs and the extinct Steller's sea cow
