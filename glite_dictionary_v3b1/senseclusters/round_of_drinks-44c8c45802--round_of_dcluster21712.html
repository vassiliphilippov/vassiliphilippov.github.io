round of drinks

noun
a set of drinks for a group
Pronunciation: / raʊnd əv drɪŋks / (AmE), / raʊnd əv drɪŋks / (BrE)
a set of drinks bought for all the members of a group at the same time, usually in a social setting

Examples

A round of drinks at bar
After work, everyone chipped in for a round of drinks at the bar.
It's your turn to buy a round of drinks for the table.
They celebrated their victory with a round of drinks at the local pub.

Related Terms

  • synonym: round - a single complete performance, occurrence, or cycle; in context, a turn of buying drinks
