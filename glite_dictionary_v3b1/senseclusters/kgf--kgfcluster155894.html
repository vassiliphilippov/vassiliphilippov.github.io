kgf

sym
unit of force
9.4
Pronunciation: / keɪ dʒi ɛf / (AmE), / keɪ dʒi ɛf / (BrE)
a unit of force equal to the force exerted by a mass of one kilogram under standard gravity

Examples

One kgf force
The maximum load the device can handle is 200 kgf.
Engineers measured the breaking point at 1,000 kgf during the test.
The specifications list the pressure in both kgf and newtons.
