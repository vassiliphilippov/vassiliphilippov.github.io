CPM

noun
joint movement device
7.4
Pronunciation: / si pi ɛm / (AmE), / siː piː ɛm / (BrE)
a medical device that moves a joint slowly and continuously to help recovery after surgery

Examples

CPM machine therapy
The patient used a CPM machine daily to improve knee flexibility after surgery.
Doctors often recommend CPM devices to speed up joint recovery following orthopedic procedures.
After his operation, he spent several hours each day with his leg in a CPM.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuous passive motion - a therapy using a device to move joints without patient effort
  • synonym: physical therapy - treatment to restore movement and function after injury or surgery
