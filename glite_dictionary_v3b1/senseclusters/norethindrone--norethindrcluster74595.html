norethindrone

noun
synthetic hormone in birth control and therapy
8.8
Pronunciation: / nɔːˌrɛˈθɪnˌdroʊn / (AmE), / nɔːˌrɛθˈɪndrəʊn / (BrE)
A synthetic progestin hormone used in oral contraceptives and in the treatment of endometriosis and menstrual disorders.

Examples

norethindrone hormone
Her doctor prescribed norethindrone to help manage her endometriosis symptoms.
Many oral contraceptive pills contain norethindrone as an active ingredient.
The side effects of norethindrone can include changes in menstrual flow and mild headaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progestin - a synthetic form of the hormone progesterone
