adamancy

noun
firm determination
Pronunciation: / ˈædəməntsi / (AmE), / ˈædəmənsi / (BrE)
the quality of being very determined and refusing to change one's mind

Examples

His adamancy in debate
Despite criticism, his adamancy helped him achieve his goals.
The manager's adamancy on the issue frustrated the rest of the team.
Her adamancy in negotiations impressed her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determination - the quality of being firm in purpose
  • antonym: flexibility - the quality of being willing to change or adapt
  • synonym: stubbornness - the quality of being unwilling to change one's mind
