unflappability

noun
calmness under stress
7.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.flæp.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ən.flæp.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ʌn.flæp.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ən.flæp.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of remaining calm and composed, especially in difficult or stressful situations

Examples

Her unflappability in crisis
Her unflappability during the emergency helped reassure everyone in the office.
The pilot's unflappability was evident as he calmly handled the unexpected turbulence.
People admired his unflappability when faced with difficult questions at the press conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composure - the state of being calm and in control of oneself
  • synonym: equanimity - mental calmness and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation
  • antonym: panic - a sudden feeling of great fear that cannot be controlled
