transhumant

adjective
seasonally migrating
9.9
Pronunciation: / trænzˈhjuːmənt / (AmE), / trænzˈhjuːmənt / (BrE)
moving seasonally between different regions, especially for grazing or living purposes.

Examples

Transhumant herders
The transhumant herders guided their cattle to the mountain meadows every spring.
Many transhumant communities rely on seasonal migration for their livelihoods.
The region is known for its transhumant traditions, with families moving between valleys and highlands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomadic - moving from place to place without a permanent home
  • antonym: sedentary - staying in one place; not moving seasonally
