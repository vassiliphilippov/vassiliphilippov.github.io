pseudomonad

noun
bacterium of the Pseudomonas genus
8.0
Pronunciation: / suːˈdoʊməˌnæd / (AmE), / sjuːˈdəʊməˌnad / (BrE)
any bacterium of the genus Pseudomonas, often producing a greenish, fluorescent, water-soluble pigment, with some species pathogenic to plants and animals

Examples

A pseudomonad bacterium
Researchers isolated a fluorescent pseudomonad from the infected leaf.
Some species of pseudomonad are important pathogens in both plants and humans.
The soil sample contained a high concentration of pseudomonads capable of degrading pollutants.
