gillnetting

verb
to fish with gillnets
8.2
Pronunciation: /gɪl.ˈnet.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgɪl.net.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /gɪl.ˈnet.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgɪl.net.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
to catch fish by using gillnets, which are nets that trap fish by their gills

Examples

To gillnet salmon
The fishermen spent the morning gillnetting in the bay to catch salmon for the market.
Strict regulations now limit gillnetting to protect endangered fish species.
Many coastal communities rely on gillnetting as a traditional method of fishing.

Related Terms
