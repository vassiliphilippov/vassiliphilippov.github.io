say when

verb
ask when to stop (pouring, serving, etc.)
Pronunciation: / seɪ wɛn / (AmE), / seɪ wen / (BrE)
used to ask someone to tell you when you should stop pouring a drink, serving food, or continuing with something

Examples

To say when pouring
He kept adding sugar to her tea until she finally had to say when.
Just let me know and I'll say when you should stop pouring.
Whenever the bartender is filling your glass, you simply say when.
