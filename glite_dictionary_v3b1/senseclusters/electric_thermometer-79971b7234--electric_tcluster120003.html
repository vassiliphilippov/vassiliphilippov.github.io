electric thermometer

noun
thermometer using thermoelectric current
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk θərˈmɑmɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument that measures temperature by detecting changes in thermoelectric current

Examples

An electric thermometer device
An electric thermometer provides quick and accurate readings compared to traditional mercury thermometers.
Maintenance staff checked the engine's heat levels with an electric thermometer after the machine was running.
The laboratory technician used an electric thermometer to monitor the temperature during the experiment.
