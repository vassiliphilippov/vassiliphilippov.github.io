ethinyl

noun
acetylene-derived group
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθɪnɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːθɪnɪl / (BrE)
a chemical group or radical derived from acetylene, often used in the synthesis of certain medications

Examples

Ethinyl radical
