abridger

noun
person who shortens a written work
Pronunciation: / əˈbrɪdʒɚ / (AmE), / əˈbrɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person who shortens or condenses a written work without losing its essential meaning

Examples

An abridger of books
As an experienced abridger, she transformed lengthy reports into concise summaries.
The abridger carefully removed unnecessary details to make the novel more accessible.
The publisher hired a skilled abridger to adapt the classic for younger readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condensor - a person who condenses written material
  • degree lower: editor - a person who prepares written material for publication
