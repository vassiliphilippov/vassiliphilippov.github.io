sapiential

adjective
relating to wisdom, especially divine
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpiˈɛnʃl̩ / (AmE), / ˌsapɪˈɛnʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by wisdom, especially divine or profound wisdom.

Examples

The design of the temple reflects sapiential principles in its symbolism and structure.
The philosopher praised the sapiential writings that offered guidance for a good life.
