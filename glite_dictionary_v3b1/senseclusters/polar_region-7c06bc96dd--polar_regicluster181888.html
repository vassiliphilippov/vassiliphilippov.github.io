polar region

noun
area around Earth's pole with cold climate
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlər ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlə ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
the area surrounding the North or South Pole of the Earth, characterized by extremely cold climates and icy landscapes

Examples

The polar region
Few plants can survive the extreme conditions found in a polar region.
Scientists are conducting research in the polar region to study the effects of climate change.
The sun does not rise for months during the winter in a polar region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Antarctic - the southernmost polar region of the Earth
  • synonym: Arctic - the northernmost polar region of the Earth
