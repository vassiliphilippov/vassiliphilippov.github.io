elastically

adverb
in a flexible way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæstɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that stretches or returns to its original shape after being pulled or deformed

Examples

Stretches elastically
The trampoline mat stretched elastically as the children jumped on it.
When compressed, the spring returns elastically to its original length.
The fabric responded elastically to the force, making it ideal for sportswear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that bends or adapts easily
  • antonym: rigidly - in a stiff or inflexible manner
