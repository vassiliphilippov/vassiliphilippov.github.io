road

noun
a public way for travel or transport
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /roʊd/ (AmE), /rəʊd/ (BrE)
a wide, hard surface built for vehicles and people to travel on, especially between places

Examples

Busy road

The road was blocked by fallen trees after the storm.
They drove down a narrow country road to reach the village.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: path - a way or track laid down for walking or made by continual treading
  • synonym: street - a public road in a city or town, usually with buildings on one or both sides
