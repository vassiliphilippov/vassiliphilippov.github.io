respectfulness

noun
quality of being respectful
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛktfəlnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing politeness and consideration for other people's feelings, wishes, or rights

Examples

Show respectfulness to others
A climate of respectfulness makes teamwork much more effective.
Her respectfulness towards her elders was admired by everyone in the community.
The teacher appreciated his respectfulness during classroom discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disrespectfulness - lack of respect; rudeness or lack of consideration
  • synonym: respect - polite regard for someone or something
