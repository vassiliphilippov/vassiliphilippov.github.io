misrule

verb
to govern badly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈruːl / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈruːl / (BrE)
to govern or manage in a bad, unjust, or ineffective way

Examples

To misrule a country
The country was misruled for years, leading to widespread poverty and unrest.
Historians agree that the king misruled his kingdom, causing many problems for his people.
If leaders misrule, citizens often lose trust in the government.

Related Terms

  • antonym: govern - to control or rule a country or group
  • synonym: misgovern - to govern poorly or incompetently
  • antonym: rule - to exercise authority or control over people or a place
