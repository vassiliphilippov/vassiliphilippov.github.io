bewilderment

noun
state of confusion or puzzlement
6.7
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪldərmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldəmənt / (BrE)
A feeling of being very confused and not able to understand something.

Examples

In bewilderment and confusion
He stared at the complicated diagram in bewilderment, unsure of where to start.
Her announcement was met with bewilderment from the entire staff.
The sudden change in schedule caused general bewilderment among the students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: confusion - the state of being confused
