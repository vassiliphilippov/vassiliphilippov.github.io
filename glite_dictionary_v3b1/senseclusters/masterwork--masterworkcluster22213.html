masterwork

noun
great artistic achievement
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstərˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəˌwɜːk /; / ˈmɑːstəwɜːk / (BrE)
a great or outstanding work of art, literature, music, or skill, created by a master or highly skilled person

Examples

A famous masterwork
The painting is widely regarded as the artist's greatest masterwork.
Many consider Beethoven's Ninth Symphony to be a masterwork of classical music.
The museum displayed several masterworks from the Renaissance period.
The painting is widely regarded as the artist's masterwork and hangs in a national museum.
After years of composing, she finally completed her musical masterwork to great acclaim.
Critics praised the film as a cinematic masterwork that set new standards in storytelling.

Related Terms
