pork belly

noun
side cut of fresh pork
1.7
Pronunciation: / pɔrk ˈbɛli / (AmE), / pɔːk ˈbɛli / (BrE)
the side cut of fresh pork from the belly of a pig, used in cooking or for making bacon

Examples

A slice of pork belly
He bought a thick slice of pork belly to use in his homemade ramen.
Many Asian cuisines feature pork belly in their traditional dishes.
The chef slow-roasted the pork belly until the skin was crispy and golden.
