iteration

noun
the act of repeating; repetition
Pronunciation: / ˌɪtəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪtəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the action of repeating something, such as a process or statement; a repeated occurrence or version of something

Examples

Each iteration of a test
Every iteration of the experiment brought them closer to a solution.
His speech was an iteration of the same points he made last week.
The new software design was developed after several iterations to improve its performance.
