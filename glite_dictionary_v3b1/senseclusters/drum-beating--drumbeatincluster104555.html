drum-beating

noun
beating a drum
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌbiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌbiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of beating a drum, especially to attract attention or show support for a cause or event

Examples

Festival drum-beating
The drum-beating at the festival could be heard from blocks away.
Protesters used drum-beating to draw attention to their cause.
Excited fans started drum-beating as the team entered the stadium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheering - shouting in support or encouragement
  • synonym: drumming - the act of playing a drum or drums
