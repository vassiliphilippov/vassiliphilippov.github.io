throwing stick

noun
a curved stick that returns when thrown
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊɪŋ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊɪŋ stɪk / (BrE)
a curved piece of wood that, when thrown correctly, returns to the thrower; commonly associated with the boomerang

Examples

Australian throwing stick
My grandfather brought back a throwing stick from his travels in Australia.
The Aboriginal people are skilled in crafting and using the throwing stick.
With the proper technique, a throwing stick will curve and return to your hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boomerang - a curved flat piece of wood that can return to the thrower
