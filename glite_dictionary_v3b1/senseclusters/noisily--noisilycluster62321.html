noisily

adverb
in a loud or noisy manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɪ.zə.li / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪ.zɪ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that makes a lot of noise, especially in a loud or unpleasant way.

Examples

To noisily make sounds
She noisily slurped her soup, drawing glances from nearby tables.
The audience noisily cheered as the band took the stage.
The children played noisily in the backyard all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loudly - in a loud manner
  • antonym: quietly - in a quiet manner
