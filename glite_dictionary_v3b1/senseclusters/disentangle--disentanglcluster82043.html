disentangle

verb
separate latent factors (ML)
7.4
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ən.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ /; / /dɪs.ɪn.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ (AmE), /dɪs.ən.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ /; / /dɪs.ɪn.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ (BrE)
(in machine learning and data analysis) to separate underlying factors of variation in data or learned representations so that they are independent or more easily interpreted.

Examples

To disentangle data features
The research team taught the network to disentangle color and shape features in the images.
After training, the autoencoder disentangled lighting from object identity in its latent variables.
They focused on disentangling the factors of variation to improve model interpretability and control.

Related Terms

  • antonym: entangle - to twist together or involve in a complex situation
  • synonym: factorize - to break something (e.g., data or matrices) into components or factors
  • synonym: separate - to cause things to be apart or no longer joined
