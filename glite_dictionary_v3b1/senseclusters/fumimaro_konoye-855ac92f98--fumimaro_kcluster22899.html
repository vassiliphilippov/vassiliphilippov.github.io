Fumimaro Konoye

proper noun
Japanese Prime Minister and statesman, 1891–1945
9.9
Pronunciation: / fuː-miː-ˈmɑː-roʊ koʊ-ˈnoʊ-jeɪ / (AmE), / fuː-miː-ˈmɑː-rəʊ kə-ˈnəʊ-jeɪ / (BrE)
(1891–1945) Japanese statesman who served as Prime Minister and played a key role in setting Japan's expansionist policies and forming alliances with Germany and Italy before World War II.
