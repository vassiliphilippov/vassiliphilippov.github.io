captioned

adjective
with added text
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpʃənd / (AmE), / ˈkæpʃənd / (BrE)
having a caption or text added to explain or describe something, especially in media or images

Examples

Captioned video
The video was captioned to make it accessible for people with hearing difficulties.
All the photos in the exhibition were captioned with interesting facts about the subjects.
She prefers watching captioned movies to improve her English vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subtitled - having written translations or transcriptions displayed on screen
  • antonym: unlabeled - without any descriptive text or label
