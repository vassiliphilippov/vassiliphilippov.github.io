cutaway model

noun
model with outer layer removed to show inside
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌt̬əˌweɪ ˈmɑdəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌtəˌweɪ ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a model or drawing of an object in which the outer layer is removed or omitted to show the internal structure or components

Examples

A cutaway model of heart
Students examined a cutaway model of the human heart during their biology lesson.
The car company's brochure featured a cutaway model to illustrate the new safety features inside the vehicle.
The museum displayed a cutaway model of a jet engine to help visitors understand how it works.
