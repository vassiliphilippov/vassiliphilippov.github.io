Prunus laurocerasus

noun
Eurasian evergreen shrub; cherry laurel
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpruːnəs ˌloʊroʊˈsɛrəs / (AmE), / ˌpruːnəs ˌlɔːrəˈsɛərəs / (BrE)
A Eurasian evergreen shrub or small tree with glossy leaves, clusters of white flowers, and fruit, often cultivated for hedges and yielding oil similar to bitter almond oil.
