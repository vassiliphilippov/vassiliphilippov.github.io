defeat

noun
an act of losing; failure in competition
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfit / (AmE), / dɪˈfiːt / (BrE)
an instance of losing a battle, contest, or competition; failure to win or succeed

Examples

A heavy defeat
After their unexpected defeat, the players gathered to discuss what went wrong.
Despite the defeat, the candidate vowed to run for office again in the next election.
The team suffered a crushing defeat in the final match and missed out on the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loss - an instance in which one does not win in a contest; the opposite of a win
  • antonym: victory - an instance of winning or succeeding in a struggle or contest
