superheater

noun
steam heating device
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəhˌhiːtə / (BrE)
a device that heats steam above its boiling point to improve efficiency, especially in steam engines or boilers

Examples

Steam superheater device
The superheater increased the efficiency of the steam engine by producing hotter, drier steam.
Engineers installed a new superheater to improve the power output of the factory's boiler system.
Without a functioning superheater, the locomotive could not reach its maximum speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boiler - a vessel used to generate steam by heating water
  • antonym: condenser - a device that cools steam to turn it back into water
