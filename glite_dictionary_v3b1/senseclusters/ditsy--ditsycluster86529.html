ditsy

adjective
silly or scatterbrained
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtsi / (AmE), / ˈdɪtsi / (BrE)
silly, scatterbrained, or lacking seriousness, often in a harmless or endearing way

Examples

Ditsy friend
Her ditsy comments made everyone laugh during the meeting.
Although he seems ditsy, he is actually very clever when it matters.
My ditsy friend forgot her keys at home again this morning.
