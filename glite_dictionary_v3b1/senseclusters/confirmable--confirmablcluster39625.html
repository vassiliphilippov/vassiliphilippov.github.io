confirmable

adjective
able to be verified or tested
4.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɝːməbl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈfɜːməbl̩ / (BrE)

Examples

A confirmable fact

The theory is confirmable through further scientific testing and observation.
We need confirmable data before drawing any conclusions about the results.
