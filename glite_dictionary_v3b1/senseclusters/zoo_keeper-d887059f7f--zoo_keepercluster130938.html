zoo keeper

noun
person who cares for animals in a zoo
2.7
Pronunciation: / zu ˈkiːpər / (AmE), / zuː ˈkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to care for the animals in a zoo and look after their enclosures

Examples

Zoo keeper cares animals
After years of study, she finally became a zoo keeper at the city zoo.
The zoo keeper fed the lions and checked their health every morning.
The children enjoyed talking to the zoo keeper during their school trip.
