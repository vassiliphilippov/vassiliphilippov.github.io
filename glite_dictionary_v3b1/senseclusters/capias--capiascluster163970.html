capias

noun
arrest warrant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪpiəs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪpɪas / (BrE)
a legal writ ordering the arrest of a person who has failed to appear in court

Examples

A capias order
The court issued a capias after the witness failed to attend the hearing.
A police officer served the capias to bring the defendant into custody.
If the accused does not appear, a capias may be ordered by the judge.
