overstressed

adjective
excessively emphasized
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈstrɛst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstrɛst / (BrE)
pronounced or emphasized with too much stress, especially on a syllable or word in speech

Examples

Overstressed syllable
The teacher noted that the student overstressed the second syllable in the word.
If the main idea is overstressed, the speech can sound unnatural to listeners.
In her performance, the phrase was overstressed, making it difficult to understand the intended meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emphasized - given special importance or stress
  • antonym: unstressed - not given emphasis or stress in speech
