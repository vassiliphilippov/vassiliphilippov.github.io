lackadaisically

adverb
in a lazy or unenthusiastic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌlæk.əˈdeɪ.zɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌlæk.əˈdeɪ.zɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a lazy, unenthusiastic, or careless manner.

Examples

To lackadaisically work
She lackadaisically flipped through the magazine, barely paying attention to the articles.
The student lackadaisically completed his homework, making several careless mistakes.
They wandered lackadaisically down the street, with no particular destination in mind.
