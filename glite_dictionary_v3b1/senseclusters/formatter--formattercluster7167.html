formatter

noun
printer control board
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrmætər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmətə / (BrE)
a circuit board or built-in controller in a printer that processes print data and manages printing functions

Examples

Printer formatter board
The technician replaced the formatter to restore the printer's ability to process documents.
If the formatter fails, the printer may not receive or interpret print jobs correctly.
A faulty formatter can cause error messages and prevent the printer from working.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - a device that manages the operation of a machine or system
  • synonym: processor - a component that processes data in electronic devices
