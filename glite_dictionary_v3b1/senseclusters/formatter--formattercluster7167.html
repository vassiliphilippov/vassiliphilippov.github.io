formatter

noun
printer control board
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.mæt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.mæt.ə/ (BrE)
a circuit board or built-in controller in a printer that processes print data and manages printing functions

Examples

Printer formatter board

If the formatter fails, the printer may not receive or interpret print jobs correctly.
