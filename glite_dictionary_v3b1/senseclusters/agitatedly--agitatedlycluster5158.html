agitatedly

adverb
nervously or excitedly
Pronunciation: / ˈædʒɪˌteɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈædʒɪteɪtɪdli /; / ˈædʒɪˌteɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a nervous, excited, or disturbed way; showing agitation or restlessness

Examples

To pace agitatedly
He paced back and forth agitatedly while waiting for the phone call.
The students whispered agitatedly when the fire alarm suddenly rang.
She explained the situation agitatedly, her hands trembling with emotion.
She spoke agitatedly on the phone, her voice trembling with worry.
The students waited agitatedly for the exam results to be posted on the board.
He paced agitatedly around the room, unable to sit still for even a minute.
