outbox

noun
folder for outgoing items
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtbɑks / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbɒks / (BrE)
a folder or tray where outgoing messages or documents are placed before being sent

Examples

Email outbox
She checked her outbox to make sure the email had been sent.
All outgoing letters are placed in the outbox before the mail carrier arrives.
If your message is still in the outbox, it has not been delivered yet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inbox - a folder or tray for incoming messages or documents
