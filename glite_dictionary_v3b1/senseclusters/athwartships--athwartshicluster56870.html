athwartships

adverb
side to side on ship
Pronunciation: / əˈθwɔrtˌʃɪps / (AmE), / əˈθwɔːtʃɪps /; / əˈθwɔːtˌʃɪps / (BrE)
across a ship from side to side, rather than along its length

Examples

athwartships across ship
The lifeboats were arranged athwartships to maximize available space on the deck.
To ensure stability, the barrels were lashed athwartships before the storm hit.
The crew moved the heavy equipment athwartships to balance the vessel properly.
The crew secured the barrels athwartships to prevent them from rolling during rough seas.
For better balance, the lifeboats were arranged athwartships on the deck.
The captain ordered the planks to be laid athwartships for extra support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: athwart - across or from side to side of something
  • antonym: fore-and-aft - along the length of a ship, from front to back
  • antonym: longitudinally - in the direction of the length of a ship
