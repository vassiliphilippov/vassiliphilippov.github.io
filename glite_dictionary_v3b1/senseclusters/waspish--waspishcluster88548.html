waspish

adjective
irritable; snappish or spiteful
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːspɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɒspɪʃ / (BrE)
easily irritated or quickly angered, often showing it in speech or manner; snappish or spiteful.

Examples

Waspish tone
He adopted a waspish tone when asked about his recent failures.
Her waspish remarks made everyone in the meeting uncomfortable.
It's best to avoid her in the mornings, as she can be quite waspish before coffee.
