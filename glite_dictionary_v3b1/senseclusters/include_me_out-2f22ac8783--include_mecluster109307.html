include me out

x
request to be excluded from involvement
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈklud mi aʊt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːd miː aʊt / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used to indicate that you do not wish to participate or be involved in something.

Examples

Please include me out
If you're going bungee jumping, please include me out from your plans.
When the conversation turned to office politics, I said, "include me out."
Whenever there are long meetings, he always asks to be included out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: count me out - to exclude oneself; to decline participation
