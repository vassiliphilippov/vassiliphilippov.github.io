serializable

adjective
can be executed serially
8.0
Pronunciation: /sɪr.ɪ.ə.ˈlaɪ.zə.bəl/ (AmE), /sɪə.rɪ.ə.ˈlaɪ.zə.bəl/ (BrE)
in database systems, describing transactions or schedules that can be arranged to produce the same result as if executed one at a time

Examples

Serializable transaction
The system ensures that all transactions are serializable to maintain data integrity.
Database designers often check if a schedule is serializable before approving it for use.
By making the process serializable, the risk of conflicting updates is minimized.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atomic - indivisible and irreducible in database operations
  • antonym: concurrent - happening or existing at the same time
