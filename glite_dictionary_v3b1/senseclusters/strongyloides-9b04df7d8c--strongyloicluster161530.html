Strongyloides

noun
parasitic roundworm genus
Pronunciation: / strɒnˈdʒɪlɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / strɒŋˈɡɪlɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
a genus of parasitic roundworms that can infect humans and animals, sometimes causing disease

Examples

Strongyloides parasite infection
The laboratory confirmed the presence of Strongyloides in the patient's stool sample.
Infections with Strongyloides are more common in tropical and subtropical regions.
Doctors treated the child for a Strongyloides infection after she returned from abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hookworm - a parasitic roundworm that infects the intestines
  • synonym: nematode - a type of roundworm
