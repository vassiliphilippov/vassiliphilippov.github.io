alluvial deposit

noun
sediment left by flowing water
6.1 5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈluː.vi.əl dɪˈpɑː.zɪt / (AmE), / əˈluː.vi.əl dɪˈpɒz.ɪt / (BrE)
Material such as clay, silt, sand, or gravel carried by running water and deposited when the flow slows, typically in riverbeds or floodplains.
