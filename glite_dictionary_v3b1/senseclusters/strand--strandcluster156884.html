strand

noun
poetic term for shoreline or beach
Pronunciation: / strænd / (AmE), / strænd / (BrE)
a shore or stretch of land bordering the sea, lake, or river, especially as referred to in poetry or literary contexts

Examples

The sandy strand
In his poem, the hero stood upon the stormy strand, gazing out to sea.
Many ancient tales begin and end upon the strand, where the waves meet the land.
They walked together at dusk along the deserted strand, the tide whispering secrets to their feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beach - a pebbly or sandy shore of a sea or lake
  • synonym: shore - the land along the edge of a sea, lake, or other large body of water
