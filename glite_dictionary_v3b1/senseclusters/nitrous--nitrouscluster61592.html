nitrous

adjective
containing or relating to nitrogen
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtrəs / (AmE), / ˈnɪtrəs /; / ˈnaɪtrəs / (BrE)
containing or relating to nitrogen or nitrous oxide, especially in chemistry, medicine, or engines

Examples

Nitrous compounds
Certain fertilizers contain nitrous compounds to help promote plant growth.
Engineers are studying the environmental impact of nitrous emissions from vehicles.
The doctor used nitrous oxide as an anesthetic during the procedure.
The dentist administered nitrous to help the patient relax during the procedure.
Some car enthusiasts use nitrous systems to boost engine performance.
The laboratory stored several tanks of nitrous for medical use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nitric - containing or relating to nitrogen, especially with a higher oxidation state
  • synonym: nitrous oxide - a colorless gas used as an anesthetic and in engines
  • antonym: oxygen - a gas essential for respiration and combustion
