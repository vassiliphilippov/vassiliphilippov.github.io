track record

noun
fastest time at a racetrack
Pronunciation: / træk ˈrɛkɚd / (AmE), / træk ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
the fastest time ever recorded for a particular distance at a specific racetrack, especially in horse or motor racing

Examples

Track record for the mile
Secretariat still holds the track record for the mile and a half at Belmont Park.
She set a new track record in the 400-meter hurdles at the stadium.
The track record was broken during last year's Grand Prix.

Related Terms

  • synonym: record - the best performance or highest achievement in a particular event
