fletched

adjective
with feathers on arrow
8.3
Pronunciation: /fletʃt/ (AmE), /fletʃt/ (BrE)
having feathers, vanes, or fins attached to help something (like an arrow, dart, or rocket) fly straight or stay stable in the air

Examples

A fletched dart flies straighter and more accurately than one without feathers.
The museum displayed several ancient fletched projectiles used by early hunters.
