calculatedly

adverb
deliberately, with intent
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈkælkjʊˌleɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a deliberate and carefully planned way, not by chance

Examples

To calculatedly plan
She calculatedly avoided answering the question to keep her plans secret.
The company calculatedly released the news late on a Friday to minimize attention.
He calculatedly complimented his boss to gain favor before the promotion decision.
He calculatedly paused before answering to make a stronger impression on the audience.
The company calculatedly released the product just before the holiday season.
She calculatedly distanced herself from the controversy to protect her reputation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidentally - by chance, not on purpose
  • synonym: deliberately - in a way that is done on purpose
  • synonym: intentionally - with intention or purpose
