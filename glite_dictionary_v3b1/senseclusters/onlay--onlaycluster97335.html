onlay

noun
dental restoration
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːn.leɪ/ /; / /ˈɔːn.leɪ/ (AmE), /ˈɒn.leɪ/ (BrE)
a dental restoration that covers one or more cusps of a tooth to repair damage or decay

Examples

Dental onlay for tooth
The dentist placed an onlay to restore the strength of the broken tooth.
After examining the cavity, she recommended an onlay instead of a full crown.
A ceramic onlay can blend in naturally with the rest of your teeth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crown - a dental cap that completely covers a damaged tooth
  • antonym: inlay - a dental restoration fitted into a cavity within a tooth
