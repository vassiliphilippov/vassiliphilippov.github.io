physio

noun
informal physiotherapist
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzioʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɪziəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a physiotherapist, a healthcare professional who treats injuries and physical problems through exercise and therapy

Examples

A physio helps recovery
After his surgery, he saw his physio twice a week for rehabilitation.
The football team relies on their physio to help players recover from injuries quickly.
She became friends with her physio during her long recovery process.

Related Terms
