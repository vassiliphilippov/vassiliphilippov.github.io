proofer

noun
error checker or dough tester
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːfər / (AmE), / ˈpruːfə / (BrE)
a person or machine that checks written material for errors or tests dough by allowing it to rise

Examples

The proofer checks errors
The proofer found several mistakes in the final draft of the report.
A commercial bakery uses a proofer to help dough rise before baking.
As a skilled proofer, she ensures every document is error-free before publication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proofreader - a person who checks written material for errors
  • synonym: tester - a person or device that tests something
