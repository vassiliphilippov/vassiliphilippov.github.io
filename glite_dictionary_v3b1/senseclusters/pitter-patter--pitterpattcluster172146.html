Synonyms

  • pit-a-pat
    8.9 0.01/m
    pit-a-pat is a quick, light rhythm often used for heartbeats or footsteps, while pitter-patter names soft repeated sounds like rain.
  • pitty-pat
    9.9 0.01/m
    pitty-pat is a short, quick tapping sound like small feet, while pitter-patter suggests gentle, repeated sounds from rain or steps.
  • pitty-patty
    9.9 0.00/m
    pitty-patty is a more playful or childish way to say quick light taps, while pitter-patter is the neutral term for such sounds.
