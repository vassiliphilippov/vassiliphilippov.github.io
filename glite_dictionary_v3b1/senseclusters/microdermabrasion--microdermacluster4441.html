microdermabrasion

noun
skin exfoliation procedure
7.4
Pronunciation: /maɪ.kroʊ.dɜːr.mə.ˈbreɪ.ʒən/ (AmE), /maɪ.krəʊ.dɜː.mə.ˈbreɪ.ʒən/ (BrE)
a cosmetic procedure that gently removes the outer layer of dead skin cells to improve skin appearance

Examples

Microdermabrasion treatment
After her microdermabrasion treatment, her skin felt smoother and looked brighter.
Many people choose microdermabrasion to reduce the appearance of fine lines and acne scars.
The dermatologist recommended microdermabrasion as a gentle way to refresh dull skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exfoliation - the process of removing dead skin cells from the surface of the skin
