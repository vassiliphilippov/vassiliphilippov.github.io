batshit

noun
extreme craziness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbætʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈbætʃɪt / (BrE)
extreme craziness, chaos, or wildly unreasonable behavior, especially in an informal or exaggerated sense

Examples

Total batshit
After the shocking news, the whole office went batshit for several minutes.
The party turned completely batshit when someone brought in a live goat.
His plan sounded so batshit that nobody believed he was serious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: madness - a state of being mentally ill or extremely foolish behavior
  • antonym: sanity - the state of having a healthy mind; rational behavior
