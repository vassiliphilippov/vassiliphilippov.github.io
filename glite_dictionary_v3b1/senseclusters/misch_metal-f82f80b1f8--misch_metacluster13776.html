misch metal

noun
pyrophoric alloy of rare-earth metals
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃ ˌmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃ ˌmɛt.l̩ / (BrE)
a pyrophoric alloy consisting mainly of a mixture of rare-earth metals, used especially in making flints for lighters

Examples

misch metal alloy
Because it is pyrophoric, misch metal ignites easily when struck or scraped.
Scientists developed a new type of misch metal to improve the performance of lighter flints.
The ignition system uses misch metal for its reliable spark-producing properties.
