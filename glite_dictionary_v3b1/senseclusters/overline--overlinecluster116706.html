overline

verb
to mark with a line above
7.2
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈlaɪn/ /; / /ˈoʊ.vər.laɪn/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈlaɪn/ /; / /ˈəʊ.və.laɪn/ (BrE)
to draw or place a horizontal line above a letter, word, or symbol, often for emphasis or notation

Examples

To overline a number
In some math problems, you need to overline repeating decimals to show the repeating part.
The teacher asked the students to overline the important terms in their notes.
To indicate a mean value, scientists often overline the variable symbol.
