reappropriation

noun
assigning funds again
8.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.prə.ˈpriː.eɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.ə.prə.ˈpriː.eɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ə.prə.ˈprɪ.eɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.ə.prə.ˈprɪ.eɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of assigning previously allocated money or resources to a new use, especially by a government or organization

Examples

Reappropriation of funds

The reappropriation of grant money allowed the organization to expand its community programs.
