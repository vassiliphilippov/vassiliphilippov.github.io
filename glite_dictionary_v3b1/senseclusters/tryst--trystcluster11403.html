tryst

verb
to meet secretly
Pronunciation: / trɪst / (AmE), / trɪst / (BrE)
to meet someone secretly, especially for a romantic purpose

Examples

To tryst with someone
They would tryst by the old oak tree every summer evening.
The couple trysted in secret, away from prying eyes.
She wondered if he would tryst with her again next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rendezvous - to meet at an agreed time and place, often secretly
  • antonym: separate - to go apart or not meet
