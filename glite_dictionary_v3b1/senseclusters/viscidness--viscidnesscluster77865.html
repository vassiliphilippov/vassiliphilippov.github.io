viscidness

noun
the state of being sticky or cohesive
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪsɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɪsɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sticky or cohesive; the state of having a thick, adhesive consistency

Examples

The viscidness of sap
Some tree saps are known for their extreme viscidness, sticking to anything that touches them.
The viscidness of the glue increases as it dries, creating a strong bond.
The viscidness of the syrup made it difficult to pour quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stickiness - the quality of being sticky
  • synonym: viscosity - the state of being thick and semi-fluid
