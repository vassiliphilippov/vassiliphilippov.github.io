Cree

proper noun
Indigenous Canadian people
8.2
Pronunciation: / kriː / (AmE), / kriː / (BrE)
a member of a large group of Indigenous peoples living mainly in Canada

Examples

The Cree people
The Cree have preserved many of their traditional customs and languages.
A Cree artist shared stories from her community at the cultural festival.
Many Cree live in the provinces of Manitoba and Saskatchewan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Inuit - an Indigenous people inhabiting Arctic regions of Canada, Greenland, and Alaska
  • synonym: Ojibwe - another Indigenous people of Canada and the northern United States
