basting

noun
moistening food during roasting
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of regularly moisten a roasting food with fat, juices, or other liquid to prevent it from drying out during cooking

Examples

Basting the chicken
After each basting, the chicken developed a beautiful golden color.
Frequent basting is the key to keeping the turkey juicy and full of flavor.
The recipe recommends basting every thirty minutes for best results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baste - to pour juices or melted fat over food while cooking
