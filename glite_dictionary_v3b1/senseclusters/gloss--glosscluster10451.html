gloss

noun
deceptively attractive appearance
7.6 5.6
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑs / (AmE), / ɡlɒs / (BrE)
a superficially attractive appearance or explanation that is meant to conceal something unpleasant or untrue

Examples

A false gloss
Beneath the gloss of her cheerful demeanor, she was deeply troubled.
He tried to give his actions a gloss of respectability, but his motives were quickly questioned.
The company added a gloss to its report to distract from declining profits.
