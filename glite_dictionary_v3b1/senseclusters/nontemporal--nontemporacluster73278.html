nontemporal

adjective
not involving time
7.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
not related to, involving, or dependent on time

Examples

Nontemporal concept
The concept of a soul is sometimes described as nontemporal in philosophical discussions.
Mathematical truths are often considered nontemporal because they do not change over time.
Some religious beliefs include nontemporal realms that exist outside the flow of time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: temporal - related to or involving time
  • synonym: timeless - not affected by the passage of time
