coolant system

noun
system using fluid for heat transfer
6.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkuː.lənt ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈkʊl.ənt ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈkuː.lənt ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
A system that uses a fluid to absorb and transfer heat from one place to another, typically to prevent overheating in engines or machinery.

Examples

The coolant system fails
A failure in the coolant system can cause serious damage to the machinery.
Technicians checked the coolant system for leaks during the routine maintenance.
The car's coolant system was flushed to prevent the engine from overheating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooling system - a system used to lower temperature or maintain coolness
