Strombolian

adjective
volcanic eruption type
9.9
Pronunciation: / strɑmˈboʊliən / (AmE), / strɒmˈbəʊliən / (BrE)
relating to a type of volcanic eruption marked by moderate, regular bursts of lava and gas.

Examples

Strombolian eruption
The volcano's Strombolian eruptions could be seen from miles away at night.
Scientists studied the Strombolian activity to better understand volcanic behavior.
A Strombolian eruption typically produces glowing lava fountains and loud explosions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Plinian - relating to a volcanic eruption with powerful, sustained columns of gas and ash
  • synonym: Vulcanian - relating to a volcanic eruption with short, violent explosions
