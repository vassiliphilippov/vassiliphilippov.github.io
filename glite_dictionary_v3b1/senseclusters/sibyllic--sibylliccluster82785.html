sibyllic

adjective
prophetic or oracular
9.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈbɪlɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈbɪlɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a prophet or prophecy; prophetic or oracular.

Examples

A sibyllic warning
Her sibyllic warnings were finally taken seriously after the disaster occurred.
The ancient text was filled with sibyllic messages that puzzled scholars for centuries.
The novelist's sibyllic tone hinted at events yet to unfold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oracular - giving wise or authoritative decisions or opinions, like an oracle
  • synonym: prophetic - accurately predicting what will happen in the future
