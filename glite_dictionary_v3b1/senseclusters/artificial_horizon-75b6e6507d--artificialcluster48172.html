artificial horizon

noun
gyroscopic aircraft instrument showing plane orientation
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːr.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl həˈraɪ.zən / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl həˈraɪ.zən / (BrE)
an instrument in aircraft, based on a gyroscope, that provides a visual indication of the aircraft's orientation relative to the earth's horizon

Examples

An artificial horizon instrument
A malfunctioning artificial horizon can lead to dangerous disorientation for pilots.
Modern cockpits display the artificial horizon digitally on multiple screens.
The artificial horizon helped the pilot maintain level flight in the dense fog.

  • synonym: attitude indicator - instrument displaying aircraft orientation relative to horizon
