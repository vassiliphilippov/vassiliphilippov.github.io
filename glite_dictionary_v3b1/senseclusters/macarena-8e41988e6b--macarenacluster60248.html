Macarena

proper noun
Spanish dance song
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈreɪnə / (AmE), / ˌmækəˈreɪnə / (BrE)
a popular Spanish dance-pop song by Los del Río, released in 1993, often played for a group dance of the same name
