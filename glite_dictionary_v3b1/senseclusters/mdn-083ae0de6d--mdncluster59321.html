MDN

noun
message read receipt
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm diː ˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌɛm diː ˈɛn / (BrE)
an electronic notification sent to a message sender to confirm that their message was received, displayed, or acted on

Examples

Email MDN notice
The email system automatically sends an MDN when the recipient opens the message.
He requested an MDN to confirm that his important document was received.
Some users disable the MDN feature to maintain their privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: read receipt - a notification that a message has been read by the recipient
