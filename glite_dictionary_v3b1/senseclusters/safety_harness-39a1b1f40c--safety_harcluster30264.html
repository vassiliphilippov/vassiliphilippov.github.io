safety harness

noun
device to prevent falling or injury
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪf.tiː ˈhɑːr.nəs/ /; / /ˈseɪf.tɪ ˈhɑːr.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈseɪf.tiː ˈhɑː.nəs/ /; / /ˈseɪf.tɪ ˈhɑː.nəs/ (BrE)
A strong belt or set of straps that fastens around a person and attaches to a secure object to prevent falling or injury.

Examples

A safety harness belt
Children are required to use a safety harness on some amusement park rides.
Every construction worker must wear a safety harness when working at heights.
The climber double-checked his safety harness before starting the ascent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harness - a set of straps for fastening or restricting movement
  • synonym: seat belt - a belt securing a person in a vehicle seat
