spread-eagle jump

noun
jump with arms and legs extended outward
Pronunciation: / ˌsprɛdˈiɡəl dʒʌmp / (AmE), / ˌsprɛdˈiːɡl dʒʌmp / (BrE)
a jump in which the arms and legs are extended outward to the sides, resembling an eagle in flight

Examples

Spread-eagle jump position
He practiced his spread-eagle jump repeatedly to perfect his form.
She wowed the audience with a spectacular spread-eagle jump during her routine.
The gymnast lost points because her spread-eagle jump was not fully extended.
