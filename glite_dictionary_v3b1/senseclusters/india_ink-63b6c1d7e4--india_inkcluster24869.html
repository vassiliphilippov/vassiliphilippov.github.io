India ink

noun
black ink for writing or drawing
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiə ɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈɪndi ə ɪŋk / (BrE)
A black ink traditionally used for writing, drawing, and printing, made from carbon black and a binder.

Examples

India ink for drawing
Calligraphers often prefer India ink for its deep black color and permanence.
He used India ink to create precise lines in his architectural drawings.
The artist spilled India ink all over his desk while inking a new comic strip.
