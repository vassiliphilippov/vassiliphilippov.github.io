Mellivora

noun
mammal genus (honey badger)
Pronunciation: / mɛlɪˈvɔrə / (AmE), / mɛlɪˈvɔːrə / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the weasel family, comprising only the honey badger (ratel).

Examples

Mellivora genus
Among mustelids, Mellivora stands out for its aggressive behavior and adaptability.
Scientists have long studied Mellivora because of the honey badger's remarkable resilience.
The Mellivora genus is represented solely by the honey badger, also known as the ratel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honey badger - A species of badger known for its strength and toughness, the only member of Mellivora
