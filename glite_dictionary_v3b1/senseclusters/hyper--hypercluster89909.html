hyper

adjective
overly energetic
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpə / (BrE)
very energetic, excited, or overly active, often more than is usual or desirable

Examples

A hyper child
After drinking two sodas, he became extremely hyper and could not sit still.
The children were hyper at the birthday party, running and shouting everywhere.
She gets hyper whenever she is excited about something new.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or excitement
  • synonym: energetic - having or showing a lot of energy
  • synonym: excitable - easily excited or stirred up
