(Biology) Development in certain insects characterized by incomplete metamorphosis, in which there is no pupal stage before reaching maturity.

Examples

heterometabolism in insects
Grasshoppers undergo heterometabolism, developing directly from nymphs to adults without a pupal stage.
Researchers studied the heterometabolism of true bugs to better understand their life cycle.
Unlike butterflies, insects displaying heterometabolism do not form a chrysalis or cocoon.
