proud

adjective
having great dignity or nobility
6.1
Pronunciation: / praʊd / (AmE), / praʊd / (BrE)
Having or displaying great dignity or nobility, often used to describe things or places that inspire respect or admiration.

Examples

Proud castle towers
The proud towers of the castle stood out against the skyline.
The general spoke of his proud army marching into the city.
We gazed at the proud peaks of the mountains, rising above the clouds.
