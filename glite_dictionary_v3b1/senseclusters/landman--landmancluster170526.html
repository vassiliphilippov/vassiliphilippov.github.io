landman

noun
land rights negotiator
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlændˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who manages land rights or negotiates land and mineral agreements, especially in the oil, gas, or energy industry

Examples

Oil landman
The landman visited several farms to discuss mineral rights with the owners.
As a landman, she specializes in negotiating contracts for oil companies.
Many companies hire a landman to handle property agreements before drilling begins.
The oil company hired a landman to secure drilling rights in the region.
As a landman, she often traveled to meet with property owners about mineral leases.
The landman explained the contract terms to the local farmers before they signed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agent - a person who acts on behalf of another
  • synonym: broker - a person who arranges transactions between buyers and sellers
