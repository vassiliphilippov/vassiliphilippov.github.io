embedment

noun
insertion into system
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɛdmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɛdmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of inserting or incorporating content, media, or code within another document or system so it appears as part of it

Examples

Embedment of a video
The embedment of interactive maps in news articles makes information more accessible to readers.
Proper embedment of code ensures that the website functions smoothly across all devices.
She checked the embedment of the image to confirm it appeared correctly in the document.

  • antonym: removal - the act of taking something out
