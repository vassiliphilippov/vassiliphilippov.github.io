engle

noun
archaic angel
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɛŋɡəl / (BrE)
an angel or spiritual being, especially in archaic or historical contexts

Examples

A shining engle
The old manuscript described a shining engle appearing to the shepherds at night.
In the stained glass window, a beautiful engle watched over the congregation.
Legends spoke of an engle guiding travelers through the dark forest.
