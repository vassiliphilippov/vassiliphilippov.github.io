boomy

adjective
deep, echoing sound
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbuː.miː/ (AmE), /ˈbuː.miː/ (BrE)
having a deep, loud, and echoing sound, often with too much bass or resonance

Examples

Boomy sound
The new subwoofer made the music sound much boomy in the living room.
Some people prefer a boomy speaker for watching action movies.
Her voice became boomy as she spoke in the empty hall.
The speakers made the music sound boomy in the small room.
He adjusted the equalizer to reduce the boomy effect in the audio.
The empty hall gave his voice a boomy quality that echoed off the walls.

  • antonym: quiet - making little or no noise
  • degree higher: thunderous - very loud and powerful in sound
