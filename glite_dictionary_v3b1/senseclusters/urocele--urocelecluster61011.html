urocele

noun
accumulation of urine in the scrotum (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrəˌsiːl / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəˌsiːl / (BrE)
A pathological condition in which urine leaks and accumulates in the scrotal sac.

Examples

A urocele in scrotum
If left untreated, a urocele can lead to infection and further complications.
The diagnosis of urocele requires careful examination and imaging techniques.
The surgeon explained that the patient's swelling was due to a urocele, not a simple hernia.
