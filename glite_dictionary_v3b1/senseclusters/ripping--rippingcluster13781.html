ripping

adjective
like the sound of violent tearing
4.3 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪp.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪp.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) resembling the noise of violent tearing or splitting, such as fabric or wood being ripped apart.

Examples

Ripping noise
She jumped at the ripping sound of fabric shredding in the next room.
The storm was accompanied by a ripping noise as trees were torn from the ground.
With a ripping crack, the lightning struck and split the old oak tree.
