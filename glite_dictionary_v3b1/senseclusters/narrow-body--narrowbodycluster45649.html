narrow-body

noun
commercial airliner with a single aisle
2.9 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈnær.oʊ.bɑː.dɪ/ (AmE), /ˈnær.əʊ.bɒ.dɪ/ (BrE)
a commercial airliner with a single aisle, typically narrower than wide-body aircraft

Examples

A narrow-body airliner
Because of its single aisle, a narrow-body typically boards and deplanes passengers slower than a wide-body.
Many airlines operate narrow-body jets on domestic routes due to their efficiency.
The airline recently upgraded its fleet with new narrow-body aircraft for regional flights.
