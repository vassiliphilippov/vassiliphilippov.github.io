Rabelais

proper noun
French satirical writer (1494–1553)

Pronunciation: / ræbəˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈræbəleɪ / (BrE)
(1494–1553) French Renaissance writer and satirist best known for his humorous and satirical works attacking medieval scholasticism.
