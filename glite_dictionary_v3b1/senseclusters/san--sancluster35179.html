san

numeral
Japanese for three
Pronunciation: / sɑːn / (AmE), / sæn / (BrE)
the number three in Japanese, written in romanized form

Examples

Counting san
When counting in Japanese, you say san for the number three.
The teacher wrote ichi, ni, san on the board to teach the students numbers.
In karate class, the instructor called out san as everyone took their third step.
