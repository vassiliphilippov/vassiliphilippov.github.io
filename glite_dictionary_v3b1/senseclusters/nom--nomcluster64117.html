nom

noun
informal nomination
Pronunciation: / nɑm / (AmE), / nɒm / (BrE)
an informal, shortened form of 'nomination', meaning a choice or recommendation for an award or position

Examples

An award nom
She was thrilled to receive a nom for best supporting actress at the awards ceremony.
The director's latest film earned him his first Oscar nom in years.
Even getting a nom for the prize was a huge honor for the young scientist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomination - the act of officially suggesting someone for a position or award
