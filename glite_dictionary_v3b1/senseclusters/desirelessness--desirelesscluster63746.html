desirelessness

noun
lack of motivation
9.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪərləsnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪəlɪsnəs /; / dɪˈzaɪələsnəs / (BrE)
a state of having no desire, motivation, or wants for anything

Examples

Total desirelessness
After the long illness, a sense of desirelessness made it hard for him to enjoy his hobbies.
Periods of desirelessness can be a symptom of depression or emotional exhaustion.
Her desirelessness surprised her friends, as she used to be very ambitious.
Many spiritual traditions value desirelessness as a path to inner peace.
He meditated daily in pursuit of desirelessness and freedom from attachment.
Some philosophies teach that desirelessness leads to true happiness and clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: contentment - a state of being satisfied and at ease
  • antonym: craving - a strong desire for something
  • synonym: detachment - a state of being emotionally uninvolved or indifferent
  • antonym: motivation - the reason or desire to do something
