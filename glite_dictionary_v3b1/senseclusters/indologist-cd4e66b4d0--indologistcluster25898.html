Indologist

noun
scholar of India
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scholar who studies the history, languages, and cultures of India

Examples

An Indologist studies India
The Indologist published a new book on ancient Indian philosophy.
As an Indologist, she spent years learning Sanskrit and Hindi.
Many Indologists travel to India to conduct field research on local traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
  • synonym: orientalist - a scholar who studies Asian cultures and languages
