text

noun
electronic message
Pronunciation: / tɛkst / (AmE), / tɛkst / (BrE)
a written message sent electronically, especially by mobile phone or messaging app

Examples

A text message
I received a text from my boss asking me to join the meeting.
She quickly replied to his text while waiting for the bus.
Please send me a text when you arrive at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: call - a voice communication by phone
  • synonym: email - an electronic message sent via email service
  • synonym: message - a piece of information sent from one person to another
