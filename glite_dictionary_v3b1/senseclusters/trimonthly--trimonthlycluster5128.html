trimonthly

adjective
three-month period
Pronunciation: / traɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / traɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
lasting for or occurring over a period of three months

Examples

Trimonthly report
The company releases a trimonthly report to update investors on its progress.

Their meetings are held on a trimonthly basis to review ongoing projects.
