compose

verb
make oneself calm or tranquil
2.3 3.8
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpoʊz/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpəʊz/ (BrE)
to make (someone, especially oneself) calm or tranquil; to bring oneself into a state of control or quiet, especially after strong emotion

Examples

To compose yourself
After the argument, she tried to compose herself and continue with her work.
It took a few moments for him to compose his thoughts after the shocking news.
She took a deep breath to compose herself before entering the room.

Related Terms
