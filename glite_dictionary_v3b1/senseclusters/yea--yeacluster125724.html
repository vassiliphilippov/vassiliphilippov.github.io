yea

adverb
emphatic archaic affirmation
Pronunciation: / jeɪ / (AmE), / jeɪ / (BrE)
(archaic) used to introduce or emphasize an affirmation, or to expand on a previous statement

Examples

Yea, truly
He was wise, yea, he was the wisest of them all.
I will go, yea, I will lead the way myself.
She spoke the truth, yea, she declared it openly before all.
He promised to help, yea, to devote all his resources if necessary.
She was grateful for his kindness, yea, she was moved to tears by it.
They rejoiced in their victory, yea, they celebrated for days afterward.
