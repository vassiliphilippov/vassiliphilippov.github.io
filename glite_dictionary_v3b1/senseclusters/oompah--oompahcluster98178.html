oompah

noun
brass band rhythm
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈuːmpɑː / (AmE), / ˈuːmpɑː / (BrE)
a style of music characterized by a strong, repeated brass rhythm, often played by brass bands

Examples

Oompah music
The lively oompah music filled the beer hall during the festival.
A traditional German band played oompah tunes that got everyone dancing.
He recognized the familiar oompah rhythm coming from the parade down the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: march - a piece of music with a strong, regular rhythm, often for marching
  • synonym: polka - a lively dance and music style with a strong rhythm
