pudic

adjective
of the genital organs
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːdɪk / (AmE), / ˈpjuːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the external genital organs, especially in anatomical or medical contexts

Examples

Pudic artery

Damage to the pudic nerve can result in loss of sensation in the area.
Anatomy students must learn the course of the pudic vessels in detail.
