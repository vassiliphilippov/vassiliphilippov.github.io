tactile agnosia

noun
loss of ability to identify objects by touch
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtæk.taɪl æɡ.ˈnoʊ.zɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈtæk.təl æɡ.ˈnoʊ.zɪ.ə/ (AmE), /ˈtæk.taɪl æɡ.ˈnəʊ.zɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈtæk.təl æɡ.ˈnəʊ.zɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a condition marked by the loss of the ability to recognize objects by touch, despite normal tactile sensation

Examples

Tactile agnosia after stroke
After her stroke, she developed tactile agnosia and struggled to recognize keys or coins by touch alone.
Diagnosis of tactile agnosia often involves neurological assessment and sensory testing.
Patients with tactile agnosia cannot identify objects placed in their hands despite having intact sensation.
