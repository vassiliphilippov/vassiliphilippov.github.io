puff

noun
an inhalation of smoke
2.7 1.43/m
Pronunciation: / pÊŒf / (AmE), / pÊŒf / (BrE)
an act of inhaling smoke from a cigarette, pipe, or similar object

Examples

A puff on a cigarette
He took a long puff on his cigar before answering the question.
She exhaled the puff of smoke into the cool night air.
With each puff, the room filled with the scent of tobacco.

Synonyms

  • drag
    7.1 1.53/m
    a drag is a single inhale of smoke from a cigarette or pipe, while a puff is a short, soft breath of smoke.
  • pull
    7.2 0.71/m
    a pull is a slow, steady inhale of smoke from a cigarette or pipe, while a puff is usually a quick, light breath.
  • draw
    4.1 4.67/m
    a draw is sucking smoke into the mouth or lungs, while a puff is a single quick inhale used when smoking.
