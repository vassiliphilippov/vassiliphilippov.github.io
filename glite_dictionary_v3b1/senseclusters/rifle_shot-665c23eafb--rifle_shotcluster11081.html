rifle shot

noun
single rifle discharge
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪfəl ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl ʃɒt / (BrE)
a single discharge or firing of a rifle, or the bullet fired from it

Examples

A loud rifle shot
The sudden rifle shot startled the birds in the forest.
He heard a distant rifle shot while hiking in the mountains.
A single rifle shot signaled the start of the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunshot - the firing of any gun or the sound it makes
  • synonym: pistol shot - a single discharge or firing of a pistol
