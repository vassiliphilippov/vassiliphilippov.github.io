Kalapooian language

noun
extinct Native American language of the Kalapuya
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈpuːiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌkæləˈpuːiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an extinct Penutian language formerly spoken by the Kalapuya people of western Oregon, USA

Examples

The Kalapooian language
Linguists have studied the Kalapooian language to understand its relationship with other Penutian languages.
The Kalapooian language was traditionally spoken in the Willamette Valley of Oregon.
Very few records of the Kalapooian language remain today, making reconstruction difficult.
