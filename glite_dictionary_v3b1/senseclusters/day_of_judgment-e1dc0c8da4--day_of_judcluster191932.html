Day of Judgment

noun
final day when God judges all people
Pronunciation: / deɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt /; / ˈdeɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / deɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt /; / ˈdeɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
In Christian belief, the future day at the end of the world (often described as following the final battle between good and evil) when God will judge every person and decide their eternal fate according to their deeds.

Stories of the Day of Judgment have shaped the beliefs of countless generations.

Many believers await the Day of Judgement as a time of final reckoning.
She feared for her soul and prayed to be forgiven before the Day of Judgement.
