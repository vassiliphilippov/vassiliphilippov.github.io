In Christian belief, the future day at the end of the world (often described as following the final battle between good and evil) when God will judge every person and decide their eternal fate according to their deeds.

Synonyms

  • Judgment Day

    Judgment Day names the final moment when God judges everyone, while Day of Judgment is the common Christian phrase for it.
  • Last Judgment
    9.3 0.37/m
    Last Judgment is the formal church phrase for God's final judging of all people, while Day of Judgment is more general.
  • Last Day
    8.7 1.26/m
    Last Day refers to the final day of time when God judges people, while Day of Judgment focuses on the act of judging.
  • day of reckoning
    8.7 0.01/m

  • crack of doom

  • eschaton

  • doomsday
