fiddlesticks

noun
nonsense; foolish talk
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪdəlˌstɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɪdəlˌstɪks / (BrE)
nonsense or foolish talk that is not worth taking seriously

Examples

That is fiddlesticks
When he claimed he could run a mile in three minutes, I said it was pure fiddlesticks.
She dismissed the rumors as fiddlesticks and refused to worry about them.
All that talk about the haunted house is just fiddlesticks if you ask me.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are silly or have no meaning
  • synonym: rubbish - something that is not true or not worth considering
  • antonym: sense - words or ideas that are reasonable and make sense
