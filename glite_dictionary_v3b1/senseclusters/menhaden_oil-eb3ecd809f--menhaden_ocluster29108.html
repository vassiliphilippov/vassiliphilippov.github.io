menhaden oil

noun
fatty oil from menhaden fish
Pronunciation: / mɛnˈheɪdən ɔɪl / (AmE), / mɛnˈheɪdən ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fatty oil obtained from the menhaden fish, used in making paints, inks, and in treating leather

Examples

Menhaden oil for paint
After refining, menhaden oil can be incorporated into industrial inks for better consistency.
Many artists appreciate the unique drying qualities of menhaden oil in their paint mixtures.
The manufacturer uses menhaden oil as a primary ingredient in the leather treatment process.
