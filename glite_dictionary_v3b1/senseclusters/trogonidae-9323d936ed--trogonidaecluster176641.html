Trogonidae

noun
family of tropical birds (trogons)
9.9
Pronunciation: / troʊˈɡɒnɪˌdiː / (AmE), / trəʊˈɡɒnɪˌdiː / (BrE)
The family of brightly colored tropical birds known as trogons, found mainly in Central and South America, Africa, and Asia.
