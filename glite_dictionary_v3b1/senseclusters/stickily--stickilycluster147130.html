In a sticky, viscous, or thick manner; with a texture or quality like that of something sticky.

Examples

Mud clings stickily
After the rain, the mud clung stickily to his boots as he walked home.
Sweaty and exhausted, his shirt clung stickily to his back under the hot sun.
The syrup poured stickily over the edge of the pancakes and pooled on the plate.

Synonyms

  • viscidly
    9.9 0.00/m
    viscidly means moving or feeling in a thick, syrup-like way, while stickily means having a sticky, tacky texture.
  • adhesively
    4.6 0.03/m
    adhesively describes sticking or attaching to a surface like glue, while stickily describes a messy, thick surface feeling.
