petrodollar

noun
oil export revenue
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtroʊˌdɑlər / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrəʊˌdɒlə / (BrE)
a US dollar earned by a country from selling oil (petroleum) to other countries
