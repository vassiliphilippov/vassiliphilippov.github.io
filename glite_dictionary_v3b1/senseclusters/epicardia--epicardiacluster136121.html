epicardia

noun
section of esophagus near the stomach
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈkɑrdiə / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkɑːdiə / (BrE)
the short section of the esophagus that extends from the diaphragm down to the stomach
