jock itch

noun
fungal infection of the groin
8.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɑk ɪtʃ / (AmE), / dʒɒk ɪtʃ / (BrE)
a fungal infection causing itching and irritation in the groin area, most commonly affecting men

Examples

Jock itch infection
Athletes are often prone to jock itch due to excessive sweating and tight clothing.
He visited the doctor after experiencing severe jock itch for several weeks.
Over-the-counter creams are effective treatments for jock itch.
