fyke

verb
to fish with a fyke
9.9
Pronunciation: / faɪk / (AmE), / faɪk / (BrE)
to catch fish, especially eels, using a fyke, which is a bag-shaped fish trap

Examples

To fyke eels
Local fishermen would fyke the river every spring to catch migrating eels.
They learned how to fyke from their grandfather, who had fished these waters for decades.
Permits are required if you want to fyke in the protected estuary area.
