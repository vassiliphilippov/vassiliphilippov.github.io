ward

noun
division of a hospital for patients
3.9
Pronunciation: / wɔrd / (AmE), / wɔːd / (BrE)
A separate room or division in a hospital for patients requiring similar kinds of care.

Examples

Hospital ward
She was moved to the intensive care ward after her operation.
The children's ward is decorated with bright colors to make young patients feel comfortable.
Visits to the ward are restricted to family members during flu season.

Related Terms
