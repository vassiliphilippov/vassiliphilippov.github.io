dada

noun
early 20th-century anti-art movement
8.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑː.dɑː / (AmE), / ˈdɑː.dɑː / (BrE)
an early 20th-century European art movement characterized by deliberate irrationality and the rejection of traditional aesthetic standards

Examples

Dada art movement
Many dada artists created works using found objects and random compositions.
Modern exhibitions often explore the influence of dada on contemporary art.
The dada movement challenged conventional ideas about art and meaning.
