copyrightable

adjective
eligible for copyright
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpiraɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiraɪtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be protected by copyright law as intellectual property

Examples

Copyrightable work
Not every idea is copyrightable; only original expressions can be protected.
The artist wanted to know if her new design was copyrightable under current law.
Software code is often copyrightable as a literary work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protected - legally safeguarded from unauthorized use
  • antonym: public domain - not protected by copyright and free for public use
