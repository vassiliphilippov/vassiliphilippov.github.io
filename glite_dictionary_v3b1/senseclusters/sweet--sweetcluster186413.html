sweet

adjective
having a pleasant fragrance
1.0 6.28/m
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiÀêt / (BrE)
having a pleasant or natural fragrance; fragrant or aromatic

Examples

Sweet rose scent
He picked a sweet rose and brought it to his wife.
Her hair smelled sweet after she washed it with lavender shampoo.
The air was sweet with the scent of blooming jasmine.
