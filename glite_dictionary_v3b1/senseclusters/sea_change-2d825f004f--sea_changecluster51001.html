sea change

noun
a significant transformation
9.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /siː tʃeɪndʒ/ (AmE), /siː tʃeɪndʒ/ (BrE)
a profound or notable transformation; a marked change in character, nature, appearance, or condition

Examples

A sea change in culture
Her promotion brought about a sea change in the company's working culture.
Over the past decade, there has been a sea change in attitudes toward climate change.
The election marked a sea change in the country's political landscape.
His experience abroad triggered a sea change in his worldview.
The introduction of the internet caused a sea change in the way we communicate.
There has been a sea change in attitudes toward renewable energy in the last decade.
