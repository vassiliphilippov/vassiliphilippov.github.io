racket

noun
illegal enterprise or criminal scheme
5.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈræk.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈræk.ɪt/ (BrE)
an illegal business or scheme, especially one that makes money through fraud, extortion, or other criminal means

Examples

Protection racket
He was arrested for running a protection racket in the city.
The government vowed to put an end to the drug racket operating in the region.
The police uncovered a racket involving fake lottery tickets sold online.

  • antonym: legitimate business - a legal and authorized business enterprise
