run

noun
score in baseball (one circuit of the bases)
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
A point scored in baseball by a player successfully rounding all four bases and reaching home plate.

Examples

Score a run
He hit a home run and brought in three runs for his team.
The pitcher gave up only one run over eight innings.
The team celebrated when their player scored a run in the last inning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: point - a unit of scoring in a game or sport
