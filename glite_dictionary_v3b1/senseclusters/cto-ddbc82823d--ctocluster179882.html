CTO

noun
chronic artery blockage
Pronunciation: / si ti oʊ / (AmE), / siː tiː əʊ / (BrE)
a complete blockage of a coronary artery that has existed for a long period, often requiring specialized treatment

Examples

Heart CTO diagnosis
The doctor diagnosed the patient with a CTO after reviewing the results of the angiogram.
Treating a CTO often requires advanced procedures performed by specialized cardiologists.
Because of his CTO, he experienced chest pain during physical activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coronary artery disease - a condition involving narrowed or blocked coronary arteries
  • degree higher: myocardial infarction - a heart attack caused by blocked blood flow to the heart muscle
