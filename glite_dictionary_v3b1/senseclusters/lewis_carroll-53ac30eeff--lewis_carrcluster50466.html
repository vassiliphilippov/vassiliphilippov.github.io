Lewis Carroll

proper noun
English author (pen name of Charles Dodgson)
Pronunciation: / ˈluːɪs ˈkæɹəl / (AmE), / ˈluːɪs ˈkærəl / (BrE)
the pen name of Charles Lutwidge Dodgson (1832–1898), an English author, mathematician, and logician best known for writing 'Alice's Adventures in Wonderland' and 'Through the Looking-Glass'.

Lewis Carroll author
Lewis Carroll is famous for creating the beloved story 'Alice's Adventures in Wonderland'.
