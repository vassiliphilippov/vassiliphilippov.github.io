unscannable

adjective
cannot be scanned
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskænəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskænəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be scanned, read, or processed by a scanner or scanning device

Examples

Unscannable barcode
The faded receipt was unscannable at the checkout counter.
If the QR code is unscannable, you may need to enter the information manually.
Some old documents are unscannable because the text has completely worn away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegible - not able to be read
  • antonym: scannable - able to be scanned or read by a scanner
