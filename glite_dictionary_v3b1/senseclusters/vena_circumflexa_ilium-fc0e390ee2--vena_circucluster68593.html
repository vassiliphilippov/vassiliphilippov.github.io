vena circumflexa ilium

noun
vein accompanying the circumflex iliac artery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə sɜr.kəmˈflɛk.sə ˈɪl.i.əm / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə sɜː.kəmˈflɛk.sə ˈɪl.i.əm / (BrE)
a vein that accompanies the artery of the same name, located in the pelvic region and draining blood from the iliac area

Examples

Vena circumflexa ilium vein
Damage to the vena circumflexa ilium can result in significant bleeding during pelvic fractures.
The vena circumflexa ilium is important in surgical procedures involving the pelvic region.
The vena circumflexa ilium parallels its corresponding artery along the pelvic brim.
