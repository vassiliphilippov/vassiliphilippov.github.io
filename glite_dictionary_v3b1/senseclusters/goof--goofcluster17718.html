goof

noun
a silly or incompetent person
Pronunciation: / ɡuf /; / ɡuːf / (AmE), / ɡuːf / (BrE)
a person who is silly, foolish, or amuses others by acting in a ridiculous way

Examples

A class goof
Everyone laughed when the goof tripped over his own feet on stage.
He admitted he was a goof for forgetting his own birthday.
Stop being such a goof and take this seriously.
Don't be such a goof—try to act seriously during the meeting.
Everyone thinks Jake is a goof, but his jokes always lighten the mood.
Her brother is a real goof who dances around the kitchen making funny faces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clown - a person who amuses others by playful or comic behavior
  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • antonym: genius - a person of exceptional intellect or talent
  • synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or who acts very stupidly
