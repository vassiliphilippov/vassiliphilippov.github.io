bichloride of mercury

noun
a poisonous compound of mercury used as a disinfectant
Pronunciation: / baɪˌklɔrɪd əv ˈmɜrkjʊri / (AmE), / baɪˌklɔːrɪd əv ˈmɜːkjʊəri / (BrE)
a poisonous white crystalline compound (HgCl₂) used formerly as a pesticide, antiseptic, and wood preservative
