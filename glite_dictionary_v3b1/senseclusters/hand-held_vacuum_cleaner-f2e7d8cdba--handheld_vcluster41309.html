hand-held vacuum cleaner

noun
small portable vacuum cleaner for small spills
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd ˌhɛld ˈvæk.jum ˌkliː.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌhɛld ˈvæk.juːm ˌkliː.nə / (BrE)
A small, portable vacuum cleaner, powered by batteries or mains electricity, designed for cleaning small areas or minor spills.
