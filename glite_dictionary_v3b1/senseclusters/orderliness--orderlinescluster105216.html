orderliness

noun
state of being orderly or well-arranged
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚli.nəs /; / ˈɔrdərliˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə.li.nəs /; / ˈɔːdəliːnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being neat, organized, and systematic

Examples

The orderliness of work
His attention to orderliness is evident in every aspect of his work.
Maintaining orderliness in the classroom helps students focus.
The orderliness of her office impressed all the visitors.
A sense of orderliness in the workplace can reduce stress and increase productivity.
Children can benefit from routines that instill orderliness in their daily activities.
Her orderliness impressed her colleagues, as every document was perfectly filed and labeled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorderliness - lack of regular or proper arrangement; a messy state
  • synonym: neatness - the quality of being neat, tidy, and clean
