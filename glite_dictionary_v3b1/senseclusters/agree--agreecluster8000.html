Synonyms

  • concur
    6.4 3/m
    concur is a formal verb meaning to share the same opinion, while agree is the common everyday word for saying yes.
  • hold with
    8.3 0.2/m
    hold with is used to say you approve of an idea or belief, while agree is more general and neutral in speech.
  • consent
    3.0 7/m
    consent means giving permission or saying yes to a plan, while agree can simply mean sharing an opinion or saying yes.
  • approve
    1.6 80/m
    approve is to officially accept or say something is good, while agree can mean simply having the same opinion without formality.
  • accept
    1.3 80/m
    accept often means to believe or take something as true or satisfactory, while agree focuses on sharing an opinion or saying yes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concur - to agree, to share the same opinion
  • synonym: consent - to give permission for something to happen
  • antonym: disagree - to have a different opinion; to not be in accord
  • antonym: refuse - to say or decide not to do something
