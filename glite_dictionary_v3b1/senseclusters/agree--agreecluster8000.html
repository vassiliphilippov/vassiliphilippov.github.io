agree

verb
to have the same opinion or reach consensus
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈɡri / (AmE), / əˈɡriː / (BrE)
to have the same opinion, to say yes, or to consent to something

Examples

To agree on a plan
After much discussion, they finally agreed on a solution to the problem.
Not everyone will agree with your point of view.
The two companies agreed to work together on the new project.
I completely agree with your assessment of the situation.
Not everyone will agree on the best solution to the problem.
They finally agreed after hours of discussion and negotiation.
After much discussion, she finally agreed to join the team.
He agreed to help his neighbor move on Saturday.
You must agree to the terms before continuing with the registration process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concur - to agree, to share the same opinion
  • synonym: consent - to give permission for something to happen
  • antonym: disagree - to have a different opinion; to not be in accord
  • antonym: refuse - to say or decide not to do something
