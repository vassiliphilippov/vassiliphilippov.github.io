washstand

noun
table or stand for washing basin and pitcher
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌstænd /; / ˈwɑːʃˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌstænd / (BrE)
a piece of furniture or fixture with a basin (and sometimes a water supply) used for washing hands and face, usually in a bedroom or bathroom

Examples

A washstand with basin
Before modern plumbing, every guest room had a washstand for freshening up in the morning.
She found the family photo tucked behind the old washstand in the attic.
The antique washstand in the bedroom held an elegant porcelain basin and pitcher.
He rinsed his hands quickly at the washstand before dinner.
She placed her toiletries neatly on the washstand by the window.
The old-fashioned washstand added charm to the guest room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wash-hand stand - British term for a washstand
