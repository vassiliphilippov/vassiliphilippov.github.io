seediness

noun
shabbiness or air of neglect
7.2 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːdinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being shabby, disreputable, or marked by a lack of cleanliness or elegance, often as a result of wearing worn or dirty clothing

Examples

The seediness of clothes
His clothes showed a certain seediness, making him look out of place at the upscale event.
She avoided that part of the city because of its growing seediness and reputation for crime.
The seediness of the old hotel was obvious from its stained carpets and peeling wallpaper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: shabbiness - the quality of being in poor condition through long use or lack of care
