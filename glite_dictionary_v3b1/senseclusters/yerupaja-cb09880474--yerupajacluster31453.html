Yerupaja

proper noun
Andean mountain peak in Peru
Pronunciation: / jɛˌruːpɑːˈhɑː / (AmE), / jɛˌruːpɑːˈhɑː / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes of Peru, rising to 21,709 feet (6,617 meters) and noted as the second highest mountain in Peru

Examples

Yerupaja mountain peak
