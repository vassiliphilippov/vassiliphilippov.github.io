nerve gas

noun
toxic chemical weapon affecting nerves
2.5
Pronunciation: / nɜrv ɡæs / (AmE), / nɜːv ɡæs / (BrE)
a poisonous gas that attacks the nervous system, causing paralysis or death, often used as a chemical weapon

Examples

Nerve gas attack
During the conflict, reports surfaced of nerve gas being used against civilians.
Exposure to nerve gas can quickly result in respiratory failure and death if not treated immediately.
The international treaty bans the production and use of nerve gas in warfare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical weapon - weapon that uses toxic chemicals to harm or kill
  • synonym: poison gas - toxic gas used as a weapon
