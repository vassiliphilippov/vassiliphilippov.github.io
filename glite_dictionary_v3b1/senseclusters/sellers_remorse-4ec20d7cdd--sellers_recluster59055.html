seller's remorse

noun
regret after selling something
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlɚz rɪˈmɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsɛləz rɪˈmɔːs / (BrE)
a feeling of regret or unhappiness experienced after selling something, especially when the decision is soon doubted

Examples

Seller's remorse feeling
After selling her childhood home, she experienced seller's remorse for weeks.
Many people experience seller's remorse after making quick decisions without careful thought.
The antique shop owner felt seller's remorse after letting the painting go too cheaply.
