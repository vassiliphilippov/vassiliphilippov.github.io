forger

noun
person who makes illegal copies
4.9 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrdʒər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːdʒə / (BrE)
a person who makes fake copies of documents, artwork, money, or similar items, usually for illegal purposes

Examples

Skilled forger
Art experts were able to identify the painting as the work of a skilled forger.
The forger was sentenced to prison for producing fake documents used in the scam.
The police arrested the forger after discovering several counterfeit passports in his apartment.
