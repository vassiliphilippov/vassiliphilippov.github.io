one-way street

noun
street where traffic flows one way only
1.6 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw ån we…™ strit / (AmE), / Ààw ån we…™ striÀêt / (BrE)
A street on which vehicles are only allowed to travel in one direction.

Examples

A busy one-way street
Drivers must pay attention to signs to avoid entering a one-way street from the wrong direction.
Parking is prohibited on this side of the one-way street during rush hour.
We got lost and accidentally turned down a one-way street, so we had to go around the block.
