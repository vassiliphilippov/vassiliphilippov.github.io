harmonize

verb
be suitable or go well together
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənaɪz / (BrE)
to be suitable, consistent, or pleasing together

Examples

Colors harmonize well
Her bright scarf harmonized beautifully with her coat.
The modern furniture does not harmonize with the old-fashioned décor of the room.
The plan must harmonize with the company's overall strategy to succeed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clash - to be in conflict or not agree
