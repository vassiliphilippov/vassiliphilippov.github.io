stardom

noun
the status of being a star
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrdəm / (AmE), / ˈstɑːdəm / (BrE)
the status or position of being widely recognized as a famous performer, especially in entertainment or sports

Examples

Achieve stardom
After years of hard work, she finally achieved stardom in the film industry.
He quickly rose to stardom after his breakthrough performance.
Many athletes dream of reaching stardom but only a few succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fame - the state of being known or talked about by many people
