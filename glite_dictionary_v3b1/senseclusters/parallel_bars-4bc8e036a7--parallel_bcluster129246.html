parallel bars

noun
gymnastic apparatus of two parallel bars
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌlɛl ˌbɑrz / (AmE), / ˈparəlɛl ˌbɑːz / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus consisting of two parallel wooden or metal bars fixed horizontally at the same height, supported by uprights, used especially in men's gymnastics

Examples

The parallel bars apparatus
Coaches inspected the parallel bars before the practice session began.
He performed a difficult routine on the parallel bars during the competition.
The gymnast dismounted elegantly from the parallel bars.
