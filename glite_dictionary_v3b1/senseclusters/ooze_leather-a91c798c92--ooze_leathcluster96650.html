ooze leather

noun
soft calfskin leather with suede finish
9.9
Pronunciation: / uz ˈlɛðɚ / (AmE), / uːz ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
A very soft leather made from the skins of calves, finished with a suede surface on the flesh side.

Examples

A ooze leather handbag
A vintage handbag made of ooze leather can feel remarkably soft to the touch.
The designer chose ooze leather because its suede finish gives a luxurious appearance.
The shoemaker specialized in crafting slippers from ooze leather for maximum comfort.
