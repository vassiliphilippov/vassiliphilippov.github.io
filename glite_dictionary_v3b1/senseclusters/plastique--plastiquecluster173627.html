plastique

noun
moldable explosive material
Pronunciation: / plæˈstik / (AmE), / plɑːˈstiːk / (BrE)
A powerful explosive material that is soft and can be easily molded to fit around objects.

Examples

Plastique explosive material
Plastic explosives like plastique can be shaped to fit almost any surface or object.
Security forces discovered several pounds of plastique hidden in the abandoned building.
The soldiers used plastique to demolish the bridge during the mission.
