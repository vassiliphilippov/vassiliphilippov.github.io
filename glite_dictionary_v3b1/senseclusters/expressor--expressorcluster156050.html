expressor

noun
one who expresses
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛsər / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that expresses something, especially a gene, idea, or feeling

Examples

Gene expressor
The expressor of the gene was identified through advanced laboratory techniques.
As an expressor of strong opinions, she often led lively discussions in class.
In the experiment, the expressor showed higher levels of the protein than the control group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conveyor - a person who communicates or transmits something
  • antonym: suppressor - a person or thing that prevents or restrains expression
