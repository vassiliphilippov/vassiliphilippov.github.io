steam organ

noun
musical instrument using steam whistles
Pronunciation: / stim ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / stiːm ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
a musical instrument consisting of a series of steam whistles sounded by means of a keyboard

Examples

An antique steam organ
He restored an antique steam organ and now displays it at festivals.
The circus parade was led by a brightly painted steam organ playing lively tunes.
You could hear the haunting sound of the steam organ from across the fairground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calliope - a musical instrument similar to a steam organ, often used at circuses and fairs
