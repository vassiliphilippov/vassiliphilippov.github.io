illusion

noun
a deceptive act, esp. in magic
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈluːʒən / (BrE)
an act or display that deceives the senses, especially a magic trick or feat that appears impossible or supernatural to onlookers

Examples

Magic illusion
Her favorite illusion was the classic saw-the-woman-in-half trick.
The magician amazed the audience with a breathtaking illusion involving disappearing coins.
The show featured an illusion so convincing that many thought it was real magic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magic - the art of producing illusions as entertainment
  • synonym: trick - an action intended to deceive or surprise
