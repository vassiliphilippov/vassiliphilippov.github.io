breacher

noun
barrier breaker
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbriːtʃə / (BrE)
a person or tool that breaks through barriers, especially in military or tactical situations

Examples

A door breacher
The breacher used specialized equipment to open the reinforced door during the raid.
In the team, the breacher is responsible for creating entry points quickly and safely.
A hydraulic breacher can cut through metal barriers in seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breaker - a person or thing that breaks something
  • antonym: defender - a person who protects or guards something
