carnet

noun
official travel document
8.4
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈneɪ / (AmE), / kɑːˈneɪ / (BrE)
an official document or booklet, especially one used for travel or to temporarily import goods across borders without paying customs duties

Examples

A travel carnet
You need a carnet to temporarily import your car into another country.
The customs officer checked his carnet before allowing him to cross the border.
She applied for a carnet to take her musical instruments on tour in Europe.
The customs officer checked the carnet before allowing the equipment into the country.
You must obtain a carnet if you plan to drive your car across several borders.
Without a valid carnet, the goods could not be temporarily imported for the exhibition.

  • synonym: permit - an official document giving permission to do something
  • synonym: visa - an official document allowing entry into a country
