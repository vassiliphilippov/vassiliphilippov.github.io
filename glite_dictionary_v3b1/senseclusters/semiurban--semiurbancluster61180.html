semiurban

adjective
partly urban area
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈɜrbən / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈɜːbən / (BrE)
partly urban in character, having features of both city and rural areas

Examples

A semiurban area
The new school was built in a semiurban area between the city and the countryside.
Many people prefer living in semiurban communities for a balance of convenience and nature.
The region's semiurban development has led to a mix of farms and apartment buildings.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: rural - relating to the countryside
  • synonym: suburban - relating to areas on the edge of a city
  • degree higher: urban - relating to a city or town
