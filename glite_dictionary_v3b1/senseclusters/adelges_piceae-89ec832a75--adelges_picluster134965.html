Adelges piceae

noun
insect pest of fir trees
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈdel.dʒiːz pɪ.ˈsiː.iː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈdel.dʒiːz pɪ.ˈsiː.iː/ (BrE)
A species of wingless aphid-like insect that infests and can kill fir trees, notably the Balsam Fir and Fraser Fir.
