benevolently

adverb
in a kindly, good-hearted manner
Pronunciation: / bəˈnɛvələntli / (AmE), / bɪˈnɛvələntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows kindness and a desire to do good or help others.

Examples

To benevolently help others
He benevolently donated to every local charity he could find.
She smiled benevolently at the children gathered around her.
The judge spoke benevolently, offering advice instead of punishment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kindly - in a kind manner
  • antonym: malevolently - in a manner showing a wish to do evil
