observation dome

noun
dome-shaped observatory structure
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑb.sɚˈveɪ.ʃən doʊm / (AmE), / ˌɒb.səˈveɪ.ʃən dəʊm / (BrE)
a dome-shaped structure, typically on top of a building, designed to house telescopes or to serve as a lookout, often having openings or sliding panels to allow observation of the sky or surroundings

Examples

The observation dome roof
Lightning struck the observation dome during last night's thunderstorm, but the equipment remained unharmed.
The new astronomy center features an impressive observation dome with a state-of-the-art telescope.
Visitors climbed to the top of the building to view the stars through the observation dome.
