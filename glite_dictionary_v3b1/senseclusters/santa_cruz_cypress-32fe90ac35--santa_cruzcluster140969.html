Santa Cruz cypress

noun
rare California cypress tree species
Pronunciation: / ˌsæn.tə ˈkruːz ˈsaɪ.prəs / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə ˈkruːz ˈsɪ.prəs / (BrE)
A rare cypress tree species native to California, taller than but closely related to Gowen cypress, and sometimes considered the same species.

Examples

The Santa Cruz cypress tree
Botanists are working to protect the endangered Santa Cruz cypress from habitat loss.
The Santa Cruz cypress is found only in a few isolated groves in California.
Unlike the Gowen cypress, the Santa Cruz cypress can grow to over 15 meters tall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gowen cypress - a closely related species of cypress native to California
