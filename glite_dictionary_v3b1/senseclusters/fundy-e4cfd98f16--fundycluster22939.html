Fundy

proper noun
Canadian bay
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndi / (AmE), / ˈfʌndi / (BrE)
a large bay on the Atlantic coast of Canada, famous for having the highest tides in the world

Examples

The Fundy Bay
Tourists travel from around the world to see the tides of the Fundy each year.
The coastline along the Fundy is known for its dramatic cliffs and unique rock formations.
Scientists study the marine life in the Fundy because of its extreme tidal changes.
