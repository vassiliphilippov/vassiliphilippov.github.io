lacrimal

adjective
relating to tears or tear glands
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlækrəməl / (AmE), / ˈlak.rɪ.məl / (BrE)
relating to tears or the glands, ducts, or passages in the body that produce tears

Examples

Lacrimal duct blockage
A blockage in the lacrimal duct can cause excessive tearing.
Damage to the lacrimal apparatus may result in dry eyes.
The doctor examined the lacrimal gland for signs of infection.
A blockage in the lacrimal duct can cause watery eyes and discomfort.
The lacrimal glands are responsible for producing tears that keep the eyes moist.
The surgeon carefully avoided the lacrimal structures during the eye operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tear duct - a passage for tears to drain from the eye
  • synonym: tearful - full of or shedding tears
