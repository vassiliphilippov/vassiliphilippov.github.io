unshiny

adjective
dull or plain
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃaɪni / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃaɪni / (BrE)
not shiny; dull or lacking gloss, brightness, or a reflective surface

Examples

An unshiny surface
The old car looked unshiny next to the brand new vehicles in the showroom.
She preferred unshiny jewelry that matched her understated style.
Despite its unshiny appearance, the café served excellent coffee and pastries.
The old coins looked unshiny after years of being stored in a drawer.
She preferred the unshiny finish of the wooden table to a glossy one.
His shoes were unshiny because he had not polished them in weeks.
