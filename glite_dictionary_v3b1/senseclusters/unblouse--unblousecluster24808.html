unblouse

verb
to undo blousing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblaʊs / (AmE), / ʌnˈblaʊs / (BrE)
to remove or undo the blousing of a garment, especially at the waist, so it hangs straight

Examples

To unblouse a shirt
After the ceremony, he decided to unblouse his shirt for comfort.
Soldiers often unblouse their trousers when off duty.
She quickly unbloused her jacket as she relaxed at home.
