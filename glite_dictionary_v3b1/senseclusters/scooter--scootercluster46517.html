scooter

noun
large northern black diving duck
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈskuːtə / (BrE)
a large black diving duck found in northern regions of the Northern Hemisphere, belonging to the genus Melanitta

Examples

A sea scooter duck

The male scooter is easily recognized by its all-black plumage and distinctive bill.
