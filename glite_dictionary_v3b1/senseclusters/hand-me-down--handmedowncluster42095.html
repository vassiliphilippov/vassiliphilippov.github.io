hand-me-down

noun
used item passed to another person
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd mi daʊn / (AmE), / ˈhænd mi daʊn / (BrE)
a piece of clothing or other item that is passed from one person to another after being used, especially within a family

Examples

A hand-me-down jacket
He didn't mind wearing a hand-me-down jacket as long as it kept him warm.
The family gratefully accepted a box of hand-me-downs for their newborn baby.
When I was a child, most of my clothes were hand-me-downs from my older sister.

Related Terms

  • synonym: castoff - an item, especially clothing, that is no longer wanted and given away
