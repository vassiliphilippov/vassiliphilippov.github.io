fundy

noun
slang for fundamentalist
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndi / (AmE), / ˈfʌndi / (BrE)
a slang term for a person with strict fundamentalist religious beliefs, especially in Christianity

Examples

A fundy Christian
Some people use the word fundy to describe those with very strict religious views.
He was called a fundy because of his unwavering beliefs and practices.
The online forum was criticized for mocking fundies and their traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fundamentalist - a person who strictly follows the basic principles of a religion
  • antonym: liberal - a person with more open or progressive religious beliefs
