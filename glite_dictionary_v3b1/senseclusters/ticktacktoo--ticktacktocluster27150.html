ticktacktoo

noun
game where players try to get three in a row
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪk.tækˌtuː / (AmE), / ˈtɪk.tækˌtuː / (BrE)
A game for two players in which Xs and Os are alternately placed in a grid of nine squares, the goal being to get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the opponent.

Examples

Play ticktacktoo game
After lunch, they passed the time with several rounds of ticktacktoo.
As a child, she loved playing ticktacktoo during long car rides.
We drew a grid on the paper and started a quick game of ticktacktoo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tic-tac-toe - a game in which players try to get three marks in a row on a 3×3 grid
