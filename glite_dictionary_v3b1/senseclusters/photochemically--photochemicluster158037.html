photochemically

adverb
by chemical action of light
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈkɛmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈkɛmɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner involving chemical changes caused by light or other electromagnetic radiation

Examples

Broken down photochemically
The pollutant was broken down photochemically when exposed to sunlight.
Some reactions in the atmosphere occur photochemically due to ultraviolet radiation.
The dye faded photochemically after several days in direct sunlight.
