sialic

adjective
containing sialic acid
Pronunciation: / saɪˈælɪk / (AmE), / saɪˈælɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing sialic acid, a type of sugar found in animal tissues

Examples

Sialic acid
The sialic components of the cell membrane are important for cell recognition.
Researchers studied the sialic content in various animal tissues to understand disease resistance.
A mutation affected the sialic acid levels on the surface of the red blood cells.
