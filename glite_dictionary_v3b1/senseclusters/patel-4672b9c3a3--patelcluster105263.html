Patel

noun
village headman in India
8.7
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtel/ /; / /pə.ˈteɪl/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtel/ /; / /pə.ˈteɪl/ (BrE)
a village headman or local chief historically responsible for administration and land matters in parts of India

Examples

Village Patel
The villagers respected the Patel for his wisdom and fairness in resolving disputes.
Traditionally, the Patel managed land records and collected taxes in the community.
During festivals, the Patel would organize celebrations and lead the village in rituals.
