resemblance

noun
similarity in appearance or features
3.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɛmbləns / (AmE), / rɪˈzɛmbləns / (BrE)
the fact of being similar in appearance or in other external or superficial characteristics to someone or something else

Examples

Strong resemblance
Critics noted the resemblance between her latest novel and her earlier work.
His painting bears no resemblance to the original scene.
There is a striking resemblance between the two sisters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: difference - the state of being unlike or dissimilar
  • synonym: similarity - the state of being similar
