Mexicanize

verb
to make more Mexican
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪkəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something or someone more Mexican in style, character, or influence, or to take on Mexican characteristics

Examples

To Mexicanize the menu
The chef wanted to Mexicanize the menu by introducing authentic regional dishes.
Some neighborhoods have begun to Mexicanize their festivals with music and food from Mexico.
The company decided to Mexicanize its branding to appeal to local customers.
The restaurant decided to Mexicanize its menu by adding traditional dishes like tacos and enchiladas.
As the neighborhood changed, many local festivals began to Mexicanize and include mariachi music.
Some businesses Mexicanize their branding to appeal to the growing Hispanic community.
