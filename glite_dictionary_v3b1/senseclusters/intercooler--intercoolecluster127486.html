intercooler

noun
engine air cooler
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌkuːlər / (AmE), / ˈɪntəkˌuːlə / (BrE)
a device that cools compressed air or gas in an engine or compressor to improve performance or efficiency

Examples

Car intercooler
The mechanic recommended installing a larger intercooler to improve the car's performance.
After upgrading the intercooler, the engine ran cooler and more efficiently.
A damaged intercooler can reduce the effectiveness of a turbocharged engine.
The mechanic recommended installing an intercooler to help the engine run more efficiently.
After adding a new intercooler, the car's performance improved noticeably on hot days.
Industrial compressors often use an intercooler to reduce the temperature of compressed air between stages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heat exchanger - a device that transfers heat between fluids
  • synonym: radiator - a device that cools engine coolant by transferring heat to the air
  • synonym: turbocharger - a device that increases engine power by forcing extra air into the combustion chamber
