meningeal

adjective
of the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / məˈnɪndʒɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the meninges, the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord

Examples

Meningeal inflammation
A lumbar puncture is performed to assess for meningeal inflammation.
Signs of meningeal irritation may include neck stiffness and headache.
The doctor suspected a meningeal infection based on the patient's symptoms.
