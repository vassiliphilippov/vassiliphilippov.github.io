mid-March

noun
the middle part of March
Pronunciation: / mɪd ˈmɑrtʃ / (AmE), / mɪd ˈmɑːtʃ / (BrE)
the middle part of March, usually around the 15th of the month

Examples

The mid-March period
Last year, a major snowstorm hit the city in mid-March.
The flowers usually start to bloom in mid-March every year.
