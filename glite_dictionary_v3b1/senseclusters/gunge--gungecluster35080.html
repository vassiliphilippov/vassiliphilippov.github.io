gunge

noun
thick sticky substance
Pronunciation: / ɡʌndʒ / (AmE), / ɡʌndʒ / (BrE)
a thick, sticky, and often dirty or unpleasant substance

Examples

Pipe gunge buildup
The pipes were blocked with a layer of gunge that smelled terrible.
She wiped the gunge off her shoes after walking through the muddy field.
Years of neglect had left the machine coated in thick gunge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean and free from dirt
  • synonym: goo - a sticky or slimy substance
  • synonym: gunk - an unpleasantly sticky or messy substance
  • synonym: sludge - thick, soft, wet mud or a similar viscous mixture
