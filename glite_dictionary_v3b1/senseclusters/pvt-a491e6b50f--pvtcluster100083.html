PVT

adjective
company type abbreviation
Pronunciation: / piː viː tiː / (AmE), / piː viː tiː / (BrE)
used as an abbreviation for 'private' in company names, indicating a privately held limited company

Examples

PVT Ltd company
The company was registered as PVT Ltd to limit public shareholding.
Many small businesses in India use PVT Ltd in their official names.
He started a new venture called Tech Solutions PVT Ltd last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: private - not publicly owned or traded
  • antonym: public - owned or traded by the public
