cassia

noun
a type of tropical or subtropical plant
Pronunciation: / ˈkæsiə /; / ˈkæsi.ə / (AmE), / ˈkæsiə /; / ˈkæsi.ə / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical plant of the genus Cassia, including trees and shrubs with yellow flowers, used for medicinal or culinary purposes

Some types of cassia are used as a substitute for true cinnamon in cooking.
