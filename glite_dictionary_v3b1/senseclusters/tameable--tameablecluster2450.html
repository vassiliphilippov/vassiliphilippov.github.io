tameable

adjective
able to be tamed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪməbəl / (AmE), / ˈteɪməbəl / (BrE)
(of an animal or thing) capable of being tamed or domesticated.

Examples

Tameable animals
Ferrets are among the most tameable of the small carnivores kept as pets.
Not every species is tameable; some retain their wild instincts no matter what.
Some wild animals are surprisingly tameable with enough patience and gentle handling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domesticate - to tame (an animal) and keep it as a pet or on a farm
  • antonym: untameable - not able to be tamed
