recapitalize

verb
to change capitalization
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkæpɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to change the capitalization of text or letters again, especially to correct or standardize it

Examples

To recapitalize a sentence

She had to recapitalize all the names in the list after the formatting error.
