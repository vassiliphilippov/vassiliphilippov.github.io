gastronomically

adverb
relating to food
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstrəˈnɑːmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the art or enjoyment of good food and eating

Examples

To eat gastronomically
The region is gastronomically rich, offering a variety of traditional dishes to visitors.
He was gastronomically adventurous and loved trying new foods wherever he traveled.
Paris is gastronomically renowned for its world-class restaurants and bakeries.
