interhemispheric

adjective
between hemispheres
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two hemispheres, especially the left and right hemispheres of the brain

Examples

The interhemispheric fissure
The interhemispheric fissure separates the left and right sides of the brain.
Researchers studied interhemispheric communication to understand how the two brain hemispheres share information.
Damage to interhemispheric pathways can affect coordination between both sides of the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrahemispheric - within a single hemisphere, especially of the brain
