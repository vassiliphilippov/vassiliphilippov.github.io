price cut

noun
reduction in price
Pronunciation: / praɪs kʌt / (AmE), / praɪs kʌt / (BrE)
A reduction in the usual or advertised price of goods or services.

Examples

A price cut
After the price cut, sales numbers increased significantly.
Customers rushed in to take advantage of the unexpected price cut on fresh produce.
The store announced a major price cut on all electronics this weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual cost; synonym for price cut
  • antonym: markup - an increase in the price; antonym of price cut
