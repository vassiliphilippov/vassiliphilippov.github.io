plantar

adjective
relating to the sole of the foot
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.tɑr / (AmE), / ˈplæn.tɑː / (BrE)
relating to the sole or underside of the foot.

Examples

Plantar wart
She developed a plantar wart on the bottom of her foot.
The plantar surface is prone to irritation in runners.
The doctor recommended special insoles to relieve plantar pain.
