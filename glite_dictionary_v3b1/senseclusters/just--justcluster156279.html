just

adverb
exactly; precisely
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst / (AmE), / dʒʌst / (BrE)
exactly; precisely; no more or less than what is stated or needed.

Examples

Just the same
It was just as you described—perfect in every detail.
She placed the vase just where I told her to.
The cake had just the right amount of sweetness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approximately - almost, but not completely, accurate or exact; roughly
  • synonym: exactly - in a way that is completely correct, accurate, or precise
