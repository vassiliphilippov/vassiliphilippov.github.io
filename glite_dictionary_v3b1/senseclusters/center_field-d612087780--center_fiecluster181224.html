center field

noun
central outfield position in baseball
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tɚ fiːld / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tə fiːld / (BrE)
the position in baseball in which the player fields balls hit to the central area of the outfield

Examples

Center field position
A long fly ball was caught in center field during the seventh inning.
He plays center field for the local baseball team.
She has excelled at center field since joining the varsity squad.
