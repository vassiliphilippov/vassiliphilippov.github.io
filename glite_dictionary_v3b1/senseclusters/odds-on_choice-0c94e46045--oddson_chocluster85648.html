odds-on choice

noun
most likely option or favorite
Pronunciation: / ɑdz ɑn tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ɒdz ɒn tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
the person or thing considered most likely to succeed or be selected out of a group of options

Examples

The odds-on choice
After his strong performance, John was the odds-on choice for the award.
She is the odds-on choice to become the next team leader.
The film is the odds-on choice to win Best Picture this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: favorite - the person or thing most likely to win or succeed
