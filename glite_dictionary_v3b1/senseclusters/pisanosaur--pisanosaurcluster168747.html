pisanosaur

noun
primitive dinosaur from Argentina
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪsəˈnɔːr / (AmE), / ˌpiːsəˈnɔː / (BrE)
a primitive dinosaur genus found in Argentina, considered one of the earliest known ornithischians

Examples
