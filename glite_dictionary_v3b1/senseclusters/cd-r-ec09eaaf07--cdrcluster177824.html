CD-R

noun
write-once compact disc
4.1 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / siÀê diÀê …ëÀêr / (AmE), / siÀê diÀê …ëÀê / (BrE)
A compact disc that can be recorded on only once; after writing, it becomes readable but cannot be rewritten.

Examples

A CD-R disc
He burned his favorite songs onto a CD-R so he could play them in his car.
Once you've saved data to a CD-R, you can't change or erase it.
The software requires you to use a blank CD-R for the installation.
