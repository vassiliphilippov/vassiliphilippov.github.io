sambar

noun
South Indian lentil stew
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmbɑr / (AmE), / ˈsæmbɑː / (BrE)
a spicy South Indian stew or dish made with lentils, vegetables, and spices, usually served with rice or dosa

Examples

Hot sambar soup
For breakfast, we had idli with hot sambar and coconut chutney.
She learned to cook sambar from her grandmother using fresh vegetables and homemade spices.
Many South Indian restaurants serve sambar as a side dish with rice or dosa.
We enjoyed a bowl of hot sambar with steamed rice for dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its delicious sambar served with crispy dosa.
She learned to cook authentic sambar from her grandmother's recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curry - a dish of meat or vegetables cooked in a spiced sauce
  • synonym: dal - an Indian dish made from lentils or other pulses
  • synonym: rasam - a South Indian spicy soup made with tamarind and spices
