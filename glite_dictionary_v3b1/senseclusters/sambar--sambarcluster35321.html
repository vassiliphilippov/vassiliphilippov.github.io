sambar

noun
South Indian lentil stew
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsæm.bɑːr/ /; / /ˈsɑːm.bɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈsæm.bɑː/ /; / /ˈsɑːm.bɑː/ (BrE)
