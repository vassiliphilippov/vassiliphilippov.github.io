to make someone or something poor, or to take away value, strength, or quality

Examples

To impoverish communities
Excessive taxation may impoverish communities over time, leading to social unrest.
Overfarming has gradually impoverished the soil, leaving it unable to support healthy crops.
The loss of natural resources can impoverish a nation both economically and ecologically.
Heavy taxes can impoverish entire communities over time.
The economic crisis threatens to impoverish thousands of families.
War and corruption may impoverish even the wealthiest of nations.
