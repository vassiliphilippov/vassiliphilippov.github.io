Basilosaurus

proper noun
extinct ancient whale
Pronunciation: / ˌbæsɪloʊˈsɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˌbæsɪləˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
an extinct genus of large, ancient whales that lived during the Eocene epoch

Examples

A Basilosaurus could grow up to 18 meters long, making it one of the largest predators of its time.
