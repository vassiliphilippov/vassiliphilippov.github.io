amnio

noun
informal term for amniocentesis
Pronunciation: / ˈæmniˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈamniəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for amniocentesis, a procedure in which amniotic fluid is extracted from a pregnant woman for diagnostic purposes

Examples

Have an amnio test
After her amnio, she rested for the remainder of the afternoon.
Some women feel anxious before having an amnio during pregnancy.
The doctor recommended an amnio to check for genetic disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amniocentesis - the medical procedure of extracting amniotic fluid for testing
