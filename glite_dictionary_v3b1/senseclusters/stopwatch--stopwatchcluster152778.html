stopwatch

noun
a device for measuring elapsed time
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
a small device or clock that you start and stop to measure the exact time of an event, such as a race

Examples

A stopwatch timer
Please reset the stopwatch before timing the next event.
She used a stopwatch to time each lap around the track.
The referee clicked the stopwatch and signaled the start of the race.
He glanced at the stopwatch as he crossed the finish line.
The coach used a stopwatch to time each runner's performance.
When the experiment began, she pressed the stopwatch to record the reaction time.

  • synonym: timer - a device used for measuring time intervals
