girlypop hit

noun
pop song appealing to young women
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrli pɑp hɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli pɒp hɪt / (BrE)
A pop song that is particularly appealing to young women, often characterized by upbeat, catchy melodies and themes relatable to a youthful female audience.

Examples

A girlypop hit
Her playlist is packed with girlypop hits from the early 2010s.
Teenagers often gravitate toward a catchy girlypop hit for parties and road trips.
The radio was playing another girlypop hit that everyone seemed to know the words to.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boy band hit - A pop song made popular by a boy band, also aimed at a young audience
  • synonym: bubblegum pop - A style of pop music with youthful appeal, catchy melodies, and light-hearted lyrics
