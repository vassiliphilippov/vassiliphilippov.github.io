requalify

verb
to regain qualification
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkwɑːləˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkwɒlɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to give someone official qualification or eligibility again, especially after it has lapsed or been removed

Examples

To requalify a nurse
After failing the exam last year, she worked hard to requalify as a nurse.
The athlete must requalify for the national team after recovering from his injury.
Many professionals need to requalify every few years to keep their licenses active.
