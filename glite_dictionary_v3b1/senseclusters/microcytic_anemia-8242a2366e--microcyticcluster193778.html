microcytic anemia

noun
anemia with abnormally small red blood cells
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪ.krəʊˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə /; / ˌmʌɪ.krə(ʊ)ˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə / (BrE)
A form of anemia in which the red blood cells (erythrocytes) are smaller than normal.
