marihuana

noun
plant smoked for psychoactive effect
Pronunciation: / ˌmæriˈhwɑːnə /; / ˌmɛrɪˈhwɑːnə / (AmE), / ˌmærɪˈwɑːnə /; / ˌmærɪˈhwɑːnə / (BrE)
the plant (Cannabis) whose dried leaves and flowers are used as a psychoactive drug or for medicinal purposes
