skinner

noun
person preparing or selling animal skins
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪnər / (AmE), / ˈskɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to prepare animal skins or trade in them

Examples

A skinner at the tannery
In the past, becoming a skinner was a common trade in rural communities.
Many trappers relied on the skinner to process their animal hides for market.
The skinner carefully removed the pelt from the carcass using a sharp knife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tanner - a person whose occupation is tanning animal hides
