chew on

verb
think carefully about
Pronunciation: / tʃu ɑn / (AmE), / tʃu ɒn / (BrE)
to think carefully about something for a period of time before forming an opinion or making a decision

Examples

To chew on an idea
He chewed on the idea for days before making a decision.
I like to chew on complex problems during my morning walks.
She needed time to chew on the proposal before responding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consider - to think about something carefully, especially in making decisions
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something
  • synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
