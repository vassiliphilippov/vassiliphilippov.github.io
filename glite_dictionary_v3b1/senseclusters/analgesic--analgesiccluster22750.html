analgesic

noun
pain-relieving drug
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.əlˈdʒi.sɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.əlˈdʒiː.zɪk / (BrE)
a drug or substance used to relieve pain without causing loss of consciousness

Examples

An analgesic tablet
Certain analgesic medications are not suitable for children.
The doctor prescribed an analgesic to manage her post-surgery pain.
You can purchase some analgesics over the counter for headaches.
