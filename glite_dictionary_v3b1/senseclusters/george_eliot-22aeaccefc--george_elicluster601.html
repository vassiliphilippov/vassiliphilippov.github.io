George Eliot

proper noun
British novelist; pen name of Mary Ann Evans
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈɛliət / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈɛlɪət / (BrE)
the pen name of Mary Ann Evans (1819–1880), a British novelist known for her psychologically insightful and realistic novels of Victorian society.
