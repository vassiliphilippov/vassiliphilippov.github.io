causally

adverb
in a manner relating to cause and effect
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔzəli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːzəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves a cause or causes.

Examples

Explained causally
He explained the phenomenon causally, focusing on the underlying mechanisms.
Researchers found the two variables are causally related in their study.
The events are causally connected, meaning one leads directly to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: causal - relating to or acting as a cause
