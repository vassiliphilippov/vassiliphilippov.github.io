sarcomatous

adjective
like a sarcoma
Pronunciation: / sɑrˈkoʊmətəs / (AmE), / sɑːˈkəʊmətəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a sarcoma, a type of malignant tumor

Examples

Sarcomatous tumor cells
The pathologist identified sarcomatous features in the tumor sample.
Doctors were concerned about the sarcomatous transformation of the tissue.
A sarcomatous mass was detected during the patient's examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benign - not harmful or cancerous, especially referring to tumors
  • synonym: malignant - very dangerous or harmful, especially referring to tumors
