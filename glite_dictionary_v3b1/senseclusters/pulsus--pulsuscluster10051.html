pulsus

noun
medical term for pulse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlsəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsəs / (BrE)
a medical term for the pulse, especially as felt in an artery during examination

Examples

The pulsus rate

An irregular pulsus can indicate underlying cardiovascular issues.
