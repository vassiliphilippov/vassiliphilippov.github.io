overvaluation

noun
the act of valuing something too highly
3.3 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərvælˈjuːeɪʃən /; / ˌoʊvərvæl.juˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəvælˈjuːeɪʃən /; / ˌəʊvəljuːˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of valuing something as worth more than it really is

Examples
