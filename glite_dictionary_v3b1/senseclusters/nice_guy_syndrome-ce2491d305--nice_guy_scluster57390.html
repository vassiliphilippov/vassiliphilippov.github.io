nice guy syndrome

noun
pattern of excessive agreeableness in men
6.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /naɪs gaɪ ˈsɪn.droʊm/ (AmE), /naɪs gaɪ ˈsɪn.drəʊm/ (BrE)
a tendency, typically among men, to be excessively accommodating or agreeable, often in hopes of receiving reward or approval in return

Examples

Nice guy syndrome behavior
He tends to fall into nice guy syndrome, always agreeing with everyone to avoid conflict.
Her brother overcame his nice guy syndrome and learned to set healthy boundaries.
