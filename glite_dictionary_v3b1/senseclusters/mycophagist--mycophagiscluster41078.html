mycophagist

noun
one who eats fungi or mushrooms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪˈkɑfədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪˈkɒfədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person or animal that eats fungi, especially mushrooms

Examples

Mycophagist eats mushrooms
Not only do humans enjoy mushrooms, but many insects are mycophagists as well.
Some animals, such as squirrels, can be considered mycophagists because they regularly consume mushrooms.
The naturalist documented the diet of the forest's mycophagists to better understand the local ecosystem.
