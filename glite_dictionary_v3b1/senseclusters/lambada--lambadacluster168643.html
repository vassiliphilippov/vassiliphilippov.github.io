lambada

noun
Brazilian dance music
8.0
Pronunciation: / lɑmˈbɑdə / (AmE), / ləmˈbɑːdə / (BrE)
a style of lively Brazilian dance music with a fast, syncopated rhythm, often associated with a sensual dance

Examples

Lambada music
The band played a lively lambada that had everyone moving to its tropical rhythm.
She learned to dance the lambada while traveling in Brazil last summer.
The energetic beat of the lambada filled the dance floor with excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: samba - a lively Brazilian dance and music style
  • synonym: tango - a passionate partner dance and music from Argentina
