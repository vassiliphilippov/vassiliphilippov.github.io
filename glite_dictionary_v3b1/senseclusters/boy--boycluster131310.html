boy

noun
informal reference to a man; group of male friends
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ / (BrE)
(informal) A friendly or familiar way of referring to a grown man, often used collectively to describe a group of male friends.

Examples

The boys hang out
After the game, the boys gathered at Jake's house to watch a movie.
Camping with the boys has become a summer tradition for us.
Every Friday night, he goes out with the boys for drinks.
