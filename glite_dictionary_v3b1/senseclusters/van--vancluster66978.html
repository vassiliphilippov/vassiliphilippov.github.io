van

noun
vehicle with enclosed cargo space
Pronunciation: / væn / (AmE), / væn / (BrE)
a vehicle, typically larger than a car and smaller than a truck, with an enclosed cargo area, used for transporting goods or people

Examples

Delivery van
The delivery company loaded the packages into the van for distribution across the city.
The plumber arrived in a white van filled with his tools and equipment.
We rented a van to move our furniture to the new apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truck - a large motor vehicle used for transporting goods
