cologne

noun
lightly scented perfumed liquid
2.8
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊn / (AmE), / kəˈləʊn / (BrE)
A lightly scented perfumed liquid made of essential oils and alcohol, worn on the body.

Examples

A bottle of cologne
He dabbed a bit of cologne on his wrists before leaving for the party.
The gift set included aftershave and a bottle of cologne.
The scent of his cologne lingered in the elevator long after he had left.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perfume - A fragrant liquid typically with a stronger scent, worn to provide a pleasant smell to the body
