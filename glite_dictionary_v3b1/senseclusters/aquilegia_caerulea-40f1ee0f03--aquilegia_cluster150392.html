Aquilegia caerulea

noun
Rocky Mountain columbine species
Pronunciation: / ˌækwɪˈliːdʒiə sɪˈruːliə / (AmE), / ˌakwɪˈliːdʒɪə sɪˈruːlɪə / (BrE)
