debunk

verb
expose as false or exaggerated
1.2
Pronunciation: / diˈbʌŋk / (AmE), / dɪˈbʌŋk / (BrE)
to show that an idea, belief, or claim is false or exaggerated, especially by exposing its pretensions or errors

Examples

To debunk false ideas
Several journalists worked to debunk the conspiracy theory.
She debunked the viral rumor with clear evidence.
The documentary aimed to debunk myths about health and nutrition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expose - to reveal something hidden or dishonest
  • synonym: unmask - to reveal the true character or nature of someone or something
  • antonym: validate - to confirm or support the truth of something
