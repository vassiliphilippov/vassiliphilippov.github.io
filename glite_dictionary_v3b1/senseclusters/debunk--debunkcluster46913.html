debunk

verb
expose as false or exaggerated
1.2 4.6
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈbʌŋk/ /; / /diː.ˈbʌŋk/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈbʌŋk/ /; / /diː.ˈbʌŋk/ /; / /diː.ˈbʊŋk/ (BrE)
to show that an idea, belief, or claim is false or exaggerated, especially by exposing its pretensions or errors

Examples

To debunk false ideas

The documentary aimed to debunk myths about health and nutrition.
