to show that an idea, belief, or claim is false or exaggerated, especially by exposing its pretensions or errors

Examples

To debunk false ideas

She debunked the viral rumor with clear evidence.
The documentary aimed to debunk myths about health and nutrition.

Synonyms

  • refute
    6.2 6.51/m
    refute shows an argument is false by clear evidence or logic, while debunk often adds public exposure of pretence.
  • disprove
    4.5 4.56/m
    disprove proves that a claim is not true with solid proof, while debunk often focuses on exposing pretence or exaggeration publicly.
  • confute
    9.9 0.07/m
    confute uses strong argument or evidence to show someone wrong, while debunk usually also shows the claim's fake or pretentious side.
  • falsify
    7.2 1.24/m
    falsify shows that an idea or result is false by evidence, while debunk often also exposes the trick or exaggeration behind it.
  • disconfirm
    7.4 0.22/m
    disconfirm finds that evidence does not support a theory, while debunk usually shows the idea is false and exposes its pretence.
