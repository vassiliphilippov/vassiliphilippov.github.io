costar

noun
fellow lead actor
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌstɑː / (BrE)
an actor who shares a leading or important role in a film, play, or show with another actor

Examples

Movie costar
She was thrilled to work with her famous costar on the new television series.
The two costars developed a strong friendship during the months of filming.
At the premiere, each costar thanked the director for the opportunity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: co-star - an actor who shares a leading role with another
  • synonym: lead - the main actor in a film, play, or show
  • antonym: supporting actor - an actor in a secondary role
