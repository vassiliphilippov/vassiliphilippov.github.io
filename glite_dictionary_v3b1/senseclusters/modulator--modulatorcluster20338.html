modulator

noun
signal control device
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.dʒə.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈmɒ.dʒə.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)

Examples

A digital modulator is essential in modern communication systems.
