nah

interjection
informal refusal
Pronunciation: / nɑː / (AmE), / nɑː / (BrE)
used informally to say 'no' or to refuse or reject something

Examples

Nah, no
"Are you coming to the party tonight?" "Nah, I have to study for my exam."
He asked if I wanted more dessert, but I just said nah and pushed my plate away.
"Did you like the movie?" "Nah, it wasn't really my type."

Related Terms

  • synonym: no - a word used to express refusal or disagreement
  • antonym: yeah - an informal way to say 'yes'
