caliche-topped

adjective
covered with caliche (hard calcium-rich soil)
8.8 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkæl.ɪ.tʃiː.tɑːpt/ /; / /kə.ˈliː.tʃiː.tɑːpt/ (AmE), /ˈkæl.ɪ.tʃiː.tɒpt/ /; / /kə.ˈliː.tʃiː.tɒpt/ (BrE)
(of ground or surface) covered with or topped by caliche, a hard layer of calcium carbonate-encrusted soil common in arid regions.

The trail winds over a caliche-topped ridge that gleams in the sunlight.
