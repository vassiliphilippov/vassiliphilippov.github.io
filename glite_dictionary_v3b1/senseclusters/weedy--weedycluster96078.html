weedy

adjective
full of or resembling weeds
3.0 0.94/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàwiÀêdi / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêdi / (BrE)
full of weeds or resembling weeds, especially with many wild, unwanted plants growing densely

Examples

Weedy garden
She followed a weedy trail through the neglected garden.
The abandoned lot became weedy after just one summer.
The fields grew weedy and wild when no one maintained them.

Synonyms

  • weedlike
    4.4 0.01/m
    weedlike is similar in appearance or growth to a weed, while weedy describes ground or plants full of unwanted weeds.
  • unweeded
    4.8 0.01/m
    unweeded means not cleared and often overgrown with plants, while weedy focuses on being full of or resembling weeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overgrown - covered with plants that have grown in an uncontrolled way
