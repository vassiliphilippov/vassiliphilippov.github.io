diemaker

noun
person who makes or repairs dies
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design, construct, or repair dies used in manufacturing processes such as stamping, cutting, or molding materials

Examples

A skilled diemaker
As a skilled diemaker, she crafted intricate molds for plastic components.
Finding a qualified diemaker can be challenging in regions without a strong manufacturing base.
The company hired an experienced diemaker to improve the production of automotive parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toolmaker - someone who makes precision tools or dies
