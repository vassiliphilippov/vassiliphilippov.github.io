throw off

verb
remove quickly (esp. clothing or quality)
6.9 3.8
Pronunciation: / θroʊ ˈɔf / (AmE), / θrəʊ ˈɒf / (BrE)
to remove or get rid of something quickly, whether it is a physical object or something affecting you
