unregretful

adjective
not feeling regret
2.6
Pronunciation: / ʌn.rɪˈɡrɛt.fəl / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈɡret.fəl / (BrE)
Not feeling or showing any regret; without remorse or sorrow for one's actions.

Examples

An unregretful choice
Even after hearing the consequences, she remained unregretful about her decision.
He offered an unregretful apology, showing no real remorse for the accident.
The politician appeared unregretful during the interview, despite public criticism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: regretful - feeling or expressing regret
  • antonym: remorseful - feeling deep regret or guilt for a wrong
