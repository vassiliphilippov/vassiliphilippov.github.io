homeomorphism

noun
topological equivalence
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmiəˈmɔːrfɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiəˈmɔːfɪzəm / (BrE)
a continuous, one-to-one and onto mapping between two topological spaces that has a continuous inverse, making them topologically equivalent
