pilomotor

adjective
hair movement response
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪloʊˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˌpɪləʊˈməʊtə / (BrE)

Examples

The pilomotor effect is often seen when someone is frightened or excited.
