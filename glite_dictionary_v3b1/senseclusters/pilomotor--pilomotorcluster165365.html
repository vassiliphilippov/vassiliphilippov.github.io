pilomotor

adjective
hair movement response
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪloʊˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˌpɪləʊˈməʊtə / (BrE)
relating to the movement or erection of hair, especially as controlled by nerves or muscles

Examples

Pilomotor reflex
A pilomotor reaction can make your skin develop goosebumps when you feel cold.
Doctors observed a pilomotor response in the patient after the nerve was stimulated.
The pilomotor effect is often seen when someone is frightened or excited.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrector pili - small muscles attached to hair follicles that cause hair to stand up
