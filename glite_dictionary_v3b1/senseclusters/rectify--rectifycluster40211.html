rectify

verb
to purify by removing impurities
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.tɪ.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
(technical) To purify a substance by removing impurities or unwanted elements, often using distillation or similar processes.

Examples

To rectify a liquid
Factories often rectify sugar to remove color and other impurities before packaging it for sale.
The distillation process is used to rectify alcohol, increasing its purity.
To obtain high-quality oil, one must rectify it by further processing.

  • synonym: refine - to make a substance pure by removing unwanted material
