functionalistic

adjective
emphasizing utility
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfʌŋkʃənəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfʌŋkʃənəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
designed or arranged to be practical and useful, emphasizing function over appearance or decoration

Examples

Functionalistic design style
The architect chose a functionalistic style for the office, focusing on efficiency and comfort.
Her apartment has a functionalistic design, with simple furniture and minimal decoration.
Many modern schools are built in a functionalistic way to maximize space and utility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decorative - intended to be attractive rather than useful
  • synonym: practical - focused on usefulness and effectiveness
  • synonym: utilitarian - designed to be useful rather than attractive
