unremarkable

adjective
ordinary; not noteworthy
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈmɑrkəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈmɑːkəbəl / (BrE)
not special, unusual, or interesting in any way; ordinary or typical.

Examples

An unremarkable event
Despite his impressive résumé, his interview was rather unremarkable and failed to stand out.
She wore an unremarkable dress that blended in with the crowd.
The building was so unremarkable that I walked past it twice without noticing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • antonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
