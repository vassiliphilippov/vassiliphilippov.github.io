Synonyms

  • stubbornness
    3.6 1.61/m
    stubbornness is the common everyday word for refusing to change, while obstinacy sounds slightly more formal and fixed.
  • obduracy
    9.9 0.07/m
    obduracy is formal and stresses stubborn persistence, while obstinacy simply means refusing to change opinion or behavior.
  • adamancy
    8.8 0.04/m
    adamancy is strong, firm refusal to change, often seen as stubborn determination, while obstinacy is general stubbornness.
  • intransigence
    8.7 1.05/m
    intransigence is refusal to compromise in arguments or politics, while obstinacy is personal stubbornness in choices.
  • unyieldingness
    8.1 0.00/m
    unyieldingness is firm, steady refusal to give way under pressure, while obstinacy is stubborn refusal to change.
  • pertinacity
    9.9 0.04/m
    pertinacity is persistent, focused determination to reach a goal, while obstinacy is more about refusing to change.
  • mulishness
    9.0 0.00/m
    mulishness is stubborn in a foolish, mule-like way and hard to manage, while obstinacy simply means refusing to change.
