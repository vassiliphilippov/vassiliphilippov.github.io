crumb

noun
small piece of bread or cake
4.9
Pronunciation: / krʌm / (AmE), / krʌm / (BrE)
a very small piece or fragment of bread, cake, or other baked food, often broken off from a larger piece

Examples

A crumb of bread
A single crumb of cake was left on the plate.
He found a crumb in his pocket hours after lunch.
She brushed the crumb off the table after breakfast.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chunk - a large thick piece of something
  • synonym: morsel - a small piece or amount of food
