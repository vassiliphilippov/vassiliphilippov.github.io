tendrilled

adjective
having tendrils
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndrɪld / (AmE), / ˈtɛndrɪld / (BrE)
having tendrils or resembling tendrils, especially in plants that use them to climb or attach.

Examples

Tendrilled vines
The tendrilled vines wrapped themselves tightly around the wooden trellis.
She admired the tendrilled stems of the pea plants in her garden.
Many climbing plants have tendrilled shoots that help them attach to nearby objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tendril - a slender, curling part of a plant used for climbing or support
