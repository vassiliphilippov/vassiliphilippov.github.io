nervine

noun
nerve-calming medicine
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvaɪn / (BrE)
a substance or medicine that calms or soothes the nerves or nervous system

Examples

A nervine remedy
The herbalist recommended a nervine to help with anxiety.
Chamomile tea is often used as a natural nervine to promote relaxation.
Doctors sometimes prescribe a nervine for patients with mild nervous disorders.
The herbalist recommended a nervine to help reduce her anxiety.
Chamomile is often used as a gentle nervine in traditional medicine.
Some people drink teas made from nervine herbs to promote relaxation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sedative - a drug that calms or relaxes someone
  • antonym: stimulant - a substance that increases activity in the body or nervous system
