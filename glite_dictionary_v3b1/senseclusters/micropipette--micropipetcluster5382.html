micropipette

noun
small liquid dispenser
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊpaɪˈpɛt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊpaɪˈpɛt / (BrE)
a laboratory instrument used to accurately measure and transfer very small volumes of liquid

Examples

A micropipette in lab
The technician used a micropipette to transfer a tiny amount of DNA solution.
Proper calibration of the micropipette is essential for accurate laboratory results.
Students learned how to handle a micropipette during their first biology lab session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dropper - a small tool for dispensing drops of liquid
  • synonym: pipette - a laboratory tool for measuring and transferring liquids
