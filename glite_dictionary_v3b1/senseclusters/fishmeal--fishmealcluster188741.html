fishmeal

noun
ground fish feed
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌmiːl / (BrE)
a protein-rich powder made from ground fish, used mainly as animal feed or fertilizer

Examples

Protein-rich fishmeal
The farmer mixed fishmeal into the chicken feed to increase its protein content.
Many commercial fertilizers contain fishmeal as a natural source of nutrients.
Aquaculture operations often rely on fishmeal to support healthy fish growth.

  • synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to help plants grow
