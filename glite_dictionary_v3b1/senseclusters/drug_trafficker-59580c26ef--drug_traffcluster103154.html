drug trafficker

noun
person involved in illegal drug trade
3.0
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ ˈtræfɪkɚ / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈtræfɪkə / (BrE)
a person who illegally buys and sells drugs, especially in large quantities

Examples

Drug traffickers sell drugs
International gangs often rely on drug traffickers to distribute narcotics across borders.
The authorities are increasing efforts to catch drug traffickers operating along the coastline.
The police arrested a well-known drug trafficker in the city last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug dealer - a person who sells illegal drugs, usually in smaller quantities
