drivable

adjective
reachable in one drive
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvəbəl / (BrE)
in golf, describing a hole that can be reached from the tee with a single drive

Examples

Drivable golf hole
The par-4 was drivable, tempting many golfers to go for the green in one shot.
Because the hole was drivable, the tournament saw several eagles during the round.
He chose a powerful club because the next hole was considered drivable by most players.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reachable - able to be reached or attained
  • antonym: unreachable - not able to be reached or attained
