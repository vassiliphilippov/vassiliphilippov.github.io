lamper eel

noun
jawless fish with sucking mouth (lamprey)
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmpər iːl / (AmE), / ˈlæmpə iːl / (BrE)
A primitive jawless fish, similar in shape to an eel, with a round sucking mouth and a rasping tongue; another name for a lamprey.

Examples

A lamper eel fish
Fishermen once spotted a rare lamper eel beneath the old bridge.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of the lamper eel in local streams.
The lamper eel clung to the side of the salmon as it swam upriver.
