oxycodone

noun
prescription painkiller
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪˈkoʊdoʊn / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪˈkəʊdəʊn / (BrE)
a strong prescription opioid drug used to relieve moderate to severe pain

Examples

Strong oxycodone painkiller
The doctor prescribed oxycodone to help manage her pain after surgery.
Patients should take oxycodone only as directed to avoid serious side effects.
He was warned about the risk of addiction when using oxycodone for chronic pain.
