nonepiscopal

adjective
not led by bishops
Pronunciation: / ˌnʌnɪˈpɪskəpəl / (AmE), / ˌnʌnɪˈpɪskəpəl / (BrE)
not governed by bishops or not relating to a church structure led by bishops

Examples

Nonepiscopal church structure
The denomination is nonepiscopal and does not have bishops overseeing its churches.
Many Protestant groups are organized in a nonepiscopal manner, emphasizing local church autonomy.
He preferred a nonepiscopal system where decisions are made by the congregation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congregational - relating to a church governed by its congregation
  • antonym: episcopal - relating to a church governed by bishops
