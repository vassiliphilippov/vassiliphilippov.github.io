blackjack

verb
to strike with a club
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈblækˌdʒæk / (BrE)
to hit or attack someone with a short, heavy club, especially as a weapon

Examples

To blackjack a guard
The thief blackjacked the night watchman before escaping with the valuables.
He was blackjacked from behind and never saw his attacker.
In the old movie, the villain blackjacks his rival to gain the upper hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bludgeon - to hit someone repeatedly with a heavy object
