disappointing

adjective
not as good as expected
1.9 1.3
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ə.ˈpɔɪn.tɪŋ/ /; / /dɪs.ə.ˈpɔɪn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /dɪs.ə.ˈpɔɪn.tɪŋ/ /; / /dɪs.ə.ˈpɔɪn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
Not as good as expected; failing to meet one's hopes or expectations.

Examples

A disappointing result
Her exam results were disappointing, considering how hard she had studied.
It was disappointing to see the team lose after leading for most of the game.
The movie's ending was disappointing after such an exciting beginning.
