ChapStick

noun
lip balm stick
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃæpˌstɪk / (BrE)
a small stick of lip balm used to prevent or treat chapped or dry lips, often used generically for similar products

Examples

A ChapStick for lips
She always keeps a ChapStick in her bag during the winter months.
After a long day in the sun, he reached for his ChapStick to soothe his lips.
Many people use ChapStick to prevent their lips from becoming dry and cracked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lip balm - a substance applied to lips to moisturize and protect them
  • degree higher: moisturizer - a product used to add moisture to the skin
