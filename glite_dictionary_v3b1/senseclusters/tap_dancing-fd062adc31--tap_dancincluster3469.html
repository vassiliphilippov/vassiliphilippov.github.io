tap dancing

noun
a rhythmic dance style using taps on the feet
2.5 3.0
Pronunciation: / tæp ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / tæp ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dance in which rhythmic tapping sounds are created with the feet, typically by wearing special shoes with metal plates.

Examples

Tap dancing shoes
He took tap dancing lessons to improve his coordination.
She has been practicing tap dancing for five years.
The children performed a lively tap dancing routine at the talent show.
