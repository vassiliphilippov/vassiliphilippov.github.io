desaturate

verb
to lower saturation
7.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ (AmE), /diː.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsæ.tʃə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to reduce the level of saturation, especially the amount of oxygen in the blood or tissues

Examples

Patient desaturates quickly

Doctors watch for signs that critically ill patients may desaturate unexpectedly.
