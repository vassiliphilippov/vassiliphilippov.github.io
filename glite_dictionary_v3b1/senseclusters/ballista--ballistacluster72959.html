ballista

noun
ancient missile weapon
1.0 8.5
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈlɪs.tə/ (AmE), /bə.ˈlɪs.tə/ (BrE)
an ancient missile weapon resembling a large crossbow, used for hurling stones or heavy bolts

Examples

A large ballista
A large ballista dominated the city gate, ready to repel attackers.
Historians believe that only skilled engineers could operate a ballista efficiently.
The Roman army used a ballista to launch stones at enemy fortifications.

  • synonym: crossbow - weapon for shooting projectiles, similar in form to a ballista but hand-held
