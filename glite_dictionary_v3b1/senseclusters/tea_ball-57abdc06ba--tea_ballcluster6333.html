tea ball

noun
perforated metal container for brewing loose tea
6.4
Pronunciation: / ti bɑl / (AmE), / tiː bɔːl / (BrE)
a small, perforated metal ball used to hold loose tea leaves while brewing tea

Examples

A tea ball infuser
A tea ball makes it easy to brew a single cup of loose-leaf tea.
After using the tea ball, she rinsed it out and set it to dry.
She placed the loose leaves in a tea ball before steeping them in hot water.
