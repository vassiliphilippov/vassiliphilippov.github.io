datelessness

noun
absence of a date
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of being without a date, or not having a date assigned to something

Examples

Datelessness of a letter
The datelessness of the old photograph made it difficult to place in the family history.
Because of its datelessness, the document could not be used as legal evidence.
He was frustrated by the datelessness of the invitation, unsure when the event would take place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timelessness - the state of being without a specific time or period
  • synonym: undated - not marked with a date
