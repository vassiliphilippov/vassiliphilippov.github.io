pea

noun
edible leguminous plant; its green seed
1.0 6.47/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê / (AmE), / piÀê / (BrE)
a climbing plant of the genus Pisum, with small white flowers and long green pods containing edible round green seeds

Examples

Pea plant
After shelling a basket of peas, he cooked them for dinner.
She added fresh peas to the salad for extra crunch and sweetness.
The children planted pea seeds in the garden last spring.

Synonyms

  • pea plant
    1.0 0.09/m
    pea plant is a legume with small white flowers and long pods used for food, while pea names the same climbing plant.
  • garden pea
    2.0 0.01/m
    garden pea is the variety grown in gardens for fresh green seeds, while pea is the general climbing plant name.
  • common pea
    1.0 0.00/m
    common pea is the usual edible variety often eaten fresh, while pea refers to the climbing plant that produces those round seeds.
  • Pisum sativum
    8.4 0.01/m
    Pisum sativum is the scientific name used in botany, while pea is the everyday name people use for the same plant.
