tuberose

noun
fragrant tropical flower; Mexican herb
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbəˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbəˌrəʊz / (BrE)
a tropical Mexican plant with spikes of highly fragrant, waxy white flowers, cultivated for use in perfumery and as an ornamental
