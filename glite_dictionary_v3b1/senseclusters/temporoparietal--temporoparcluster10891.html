temporoparietal

adjective
of temporal and parietal regions
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛmpəroʊpəˈraɪɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌtɛmpərəʊpəˈraɪɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to both the temporal and parietal regions of the skull or brain

Examples

Temporoparietal brain area
Damage to the temporoparietal area can affect language and spatial awareness.
The temporoparietal junction plays a key role in social cognition.
Researchers studied brain activity in the temporoparietal cortex during the experiment.
