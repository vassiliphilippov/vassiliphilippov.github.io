febrile atmosphere

noun
intensely excited or restless environment
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːbraɪl ˈætməˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈfiːbraɪl ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
A situation or environment filled with intense excitement, activity, or unrest.

Examples

A febrile atmosphere of excitement
Political rallies often generate a febrile atmosphere among enthusiastic supporters.
The febrile atmosphere in the office before the product launch was almost palpable.
There was a febrile atmosphere backstage as performers prepared for their entrance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm atmosphere - an environment free from excitement, agitation, or distress
  • synonym: tense atmosphere - an environment marked by nervousness or suspense
