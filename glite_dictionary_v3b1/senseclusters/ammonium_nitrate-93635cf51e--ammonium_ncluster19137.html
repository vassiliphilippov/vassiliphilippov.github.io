ammonium nitrate

noun
chemical compound used in fertilizer and explosives
2.8
Pronunciation: / əˌmoʊniəm ˈnaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / əˌməʊniəm ˈnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a white crystalline substance used as a fertilizer and in explosives and rocket propellants

Examples

Ammonium nitrate fertilizer
Ammonium nitrate is commonly used by farmers to improve crop yield in the fields.
Because of its chemical properties, ammonium nitrate is also used as a rocket propellant.
The explosion was caused by the accidental ignition of stored ammonium nitrate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explosive - a substance that causes an explosion
  • synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to increase its fertility
