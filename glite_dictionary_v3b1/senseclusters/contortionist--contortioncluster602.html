contortionist

noun
acrobatic performer who twists body
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɔɹʃənɪst / (AmE), / kənˈtɔːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who is able to twist and bend their body into unusual and extreme positions, often for entertainment as an acrobat

Examples

A contortionist performer
After years of training, he became a renowned contortionist in the circus.
Many contortionists start practicing their flexibility routines at a very young age.
The contortionist amazed the audience by fitting herself into a small glass box.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acrobat - a person who performs gymnastic or acrobatic feats
