unguardable

adjective
impossible to defend
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɑrdəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɑːdəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to guard, defend against, or block (especially in sports)

Examples

An unguardable shot
The star player's shot was so quick and precise that it was unguardable for most defenders.
Her serve in tennis is nearly unguardable when she hits it at full speed.
The coach called the new play unguardable after the team scored every time they used it.
The basketball star was nearly unguardable on the court, scoring at will against every defender.
Some remote borders are considered unguardable due to the difficult terrain and lack of resources.
The rare artifact was kept in an unguardable location, making it vulnerable to theft.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensible - able to be defended or protected
  • antonym: guarded - watched over or protected
  • synonym: unprotected - not guarded or defended
  • synonym: unstoppable - impossible to prevent or stop
