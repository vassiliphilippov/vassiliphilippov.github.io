knuckle

noun
joint of a finger
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈnʌk.l̩ / (BrE)
A joint of a finger, especially when the fist is closed, visible as a prominent bump.

Examples

Cracked knuckle
Boxers often injure a knuckle during a tough fight.
He cracked his knuckle loudly before starting the game.
She rubbed her sore knuckle after hitting it on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knuckle joint - the joint connecting two phalanges of a finger
