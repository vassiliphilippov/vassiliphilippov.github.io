Cadra figulilella

noun
moth pest of dried fruits and cereals
Pronunciation: / ˈkædɹə fɪgjuːlɪˈlɛlə / (AmE), / ˈkædɹə fɪgjʊlɪˈlɛlə / (BrE)
a small moth whose larvae attack dried fruits and cereal products, commonly known as the raisin moth

Examples

Cadra figulilella larvae damage
Cadra figulilella is a serious pest in storage facilities for dried fruits.
The presence of Cadra figulilella can lead to significant economic losses for farmers.
To control Cadra figulilella, regular inspection of dried fruit stocks is recommended.
