Orycteropus

proper noun
genus of mammals including the aardvark
Pronunciation: / ɔːˌrɪktəˈroʊpəs / (AmE), / ɔːˌrɪktəˈrəʊpəs / (BrE)
A genus of mammals comprising the aardvark, native to Africa, and the sole genus in the family Orycteropodidae.

Examples

Orycteropus species
