slot

noun
casino gambling machine
4.6
Pronunciation: / slɑt / (AmE), / slɒt / (BrE)
A machine in casinos into which money is inserted and that pays out winnings based on chance, typically by spinning reels; a slot machine.

Examples

A slot machine

Most of the slots in this casino have progressive jackpots.
