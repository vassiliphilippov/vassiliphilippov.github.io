Marcus Ulpius Traianus

proper noun
Roman Emperor Trajan (53–117)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrkəs ʌlˈpiːəs traɪˈɑːnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkəs ʌlˈpɪəs traɪˈɑːnəs / (BrE)
(53–117) Roman Emperor, commonly known as Trajan, renowned for expanding the Roman Empire eastward and initiating significant building projects.
