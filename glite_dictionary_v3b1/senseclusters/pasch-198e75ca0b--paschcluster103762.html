Pasch

noun
Jewish festival of Passover
Pronunciation: / pæsk / (AmE), / pɑːsk / (BrE)
The Jewish festival of Passover, commemorating the liberation of the Israelites from Egyptian slavery.

Examples

Jewish Pasch festival
Every spring, the family gathers to celebrate Pasch according to tradition.
Special foods are prepared for the occasion of Pasch.
The story of the Exodus is retold during Pasch in Jewish households worldwide.
