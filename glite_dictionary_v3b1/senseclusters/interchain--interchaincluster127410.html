interchain

noun
blockchain connector
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌtʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌtʃeɪn / (BrE)
a protocol, network, or system that connects multiple blockchains and enables the transfer of data or assets between them

Examples

A blockchain interchain
The company launched an interchain to allow seamless asset transfers between different blockchain networks.
Developers are working on an interchain solution to improve communication across various decentralized platforms.
Using an interchain, users can move tokens from Ethereum to other blockchains without a central exchange.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockchain - a digital ledger of transactions maintained across a network
  • synonym: interoperability - the ability of different systems to work together
