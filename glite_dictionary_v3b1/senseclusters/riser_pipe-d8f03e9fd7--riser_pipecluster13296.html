riser pipe

noun
vertical pipe in a building
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzər paɪp / (AmE), / ˈraɪzə paɪp / (BrE)
a vertical pipe, especially in a building, used to carry water, gas, or other fluids upward between floors

Examples

The riser pipe leaks
Inspectors checked the riser pipe for any signs of leakage or corrosion.
The riser pipe carries hot water to the upper floors of the apartment building.
The plumber replaced a damaged riser pipe during the renovations.
