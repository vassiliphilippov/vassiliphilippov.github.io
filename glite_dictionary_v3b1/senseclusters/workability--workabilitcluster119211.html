workability

noun
ease of use or shaping
3.7
Pronunciation: /wɜːr.kə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /wɜː.kə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of being easy to use, shape, or work with

Examples

Workability of metal
The workability of this metal allows it to be molded into many different shapes.
Before starting the project, the engineer tested the workability of the new material.
High workability is important when choosing clay for pottery classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malleability - the ability to be shaped or formed easily
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and difficult to shape
  • synonym: usability - the quality of being easy to use
