Harefoot

proper noun
King of England (1035–1040), son of Canute
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈheə.fʊt / (BrE)
Harold I (died 1040), King of England from 1035 to 1040, known as Harefoot, the illegitimate son of Canute who seized the throne in 1037.
