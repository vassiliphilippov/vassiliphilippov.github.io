fish oil

noun
oil from fish, used as a supplement
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ ɔɪl / (AmE), / fɪʃ ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil rich in omega-3 fatty acids, obtained from the bodies of oily fish, often used as a dietary supplement

Examples

Fish oil supplement
Doctors sometimes recommend fish oil for patients with heart problems.
Taking fish oil supplements may help lower blood pressure.
The label indicated that each capsule contained 1000 mg of fish oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cod-liver oil - oil obtained from the liver of cod fish, used as a dietary supplement
