donkey jacket

noun
a thick workman's jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑŋki ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A short, thick jacket made of heavy material, typically worn by manual laborers, especially in Britain.
