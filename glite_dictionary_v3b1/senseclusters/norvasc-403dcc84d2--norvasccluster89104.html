Norvasc

noun
brand of blood pressure medication
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrvæsk / (AmE), / ˈnɔːvæsk / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of amlodipine, a medication used to treat high blood pressure and angina pectoris.

Examples

After switching to Norvasc, he noticed an improvement in his chest pain.
