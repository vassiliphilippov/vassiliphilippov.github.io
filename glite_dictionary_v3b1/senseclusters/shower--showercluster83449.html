shower

noun
short period of rain or precipitation
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊ.ɚ /; / ˈʃaʊər / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊ.ə /; / ˈʃaʊə / (BrE)
a short period of rain or a sudden fall of things like confetti or petals

Examples

A rain shower
The picnic guests ran for cover when a brief shower swept across the park.
There were a few light showers this morning but the afternoon was clear.
We decided not to go hiking because a sudden shower started pelting the area.
A shower of confetti fell over the happy couple as they left the church.
She burst into a shower of tears as soon as she heard the news.
The campfire sent up a shower of golden sparks into the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cascade - a small waterfall or something resembling one in flow or appearance
  • degree higher: downpour - a heavy and prolonged fall of rain
  • degree lower: drizzle - light rain with very small droplets
