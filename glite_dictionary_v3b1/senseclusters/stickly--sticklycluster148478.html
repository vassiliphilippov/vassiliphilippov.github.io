stickly

adjective
full of sticks
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkli / (AmE), / ˈstɪkli / (BrE)
covered with or full of small sticks or twigs, often tangled or messy with woody debris.

Examples

Stickly forest floor
The forest floor was so stickly that it was hard to find a place to sit.
After the storm, the path became stickly with fallen branches and twigs.
We avoided the stickly area near the riverbank to keep our clothes clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brushy - covered with or resembling brush or bushes
  • antonym: clear - free from obstacles or debris
  • synonym: twiggy - full of or covered with twigs
