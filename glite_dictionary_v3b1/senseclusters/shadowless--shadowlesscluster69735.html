shadowless

adjective
without a shadow
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊləs / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊləs / (BrE)
having no shadow or not producing a shadow

Examples

A shadowless lamp
The operating room was illuminated by a shadowless lamp to help the surgeons see clearly.
On a cloudy day, the light is so diffuse that everything appears almost shadowless.
The photographer preferred shadowless lighting to avoid harsh contrasts in her portraits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bright - giving out or filled with much light
  • antonym: shady - full of or cast by shade or shadows
