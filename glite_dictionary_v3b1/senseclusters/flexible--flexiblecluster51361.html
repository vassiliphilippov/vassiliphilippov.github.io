Synonyms

  • adaptable
    3.4 2/m
    adaptable is able to change to suit different situations or uses, while flexible is willing or able to adjust easily.
  • adaptive
    4.9 6/m
    adaptive describes systems or traits that change to survive new conditions, while flexible focuses on willingness or ease of change.
  • elastic
    3.4 0.6/m
    elastic stretches and returns to shape under pressure, while flexible means willing or ready to bend or change.
  • pliable
    4.2 0.7/m
    pliable bends or takes a new shape easily, while flexible can mean willing to change ideas or plans.
  • pliant
    6.5 0.2/m
    pliant is easily influenced and ready to agree or change, while flexible is more neutral and simply adapts.
  • supple
    4.4 0.3/m
    supple moves or adapts smoothly like a body or mind, while flexible stresses readiness to change in situations.
  • limber
    4.7 0.05/m
    limber is chiefly about a body that bends easily for activity, while flexible covers ideas, plans, or objects changing too.
  • plastic
    5.5 0.7/m
    plastic is easily shaped or molded, often in technical or critical talk, while flexible is simply willing to adapt.
