magnetohydrodynamic

noun
electricity-generating device
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡnɪtoʊˌhaɪdroʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌmæɡnɪtəʊˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (BrE)
a device that generates electricity by moving a conducting fluid or gas through a magnetic field

Examples

Magnetohydrodynamic generator
The research team tested a new magnetohydrodynamic to improve energy efficiency in power plants.
A magnetohydrodynamic can generate electricity directly from hot gases without moving parts.
Engineers are exploring magnetohydrodynamic technology for cleaner and more efficient power generation.
