mesiobuccal

noun
front-cheek tooth root
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːzi.oʊˈbʌkəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːzi.əʊˈbʌkəl / (BrE)
the root or root canal of a tooth located at the front and cheek side, especially in a molar
