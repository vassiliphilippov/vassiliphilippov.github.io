watertight

adjective
impermeable to water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtətaɪt / (BrE)
not allowing water to pass in or out; impermeable by water.

Examples

Watertight container
Make sure the boat's hull is completely watertight before taking it out on the lake.
The document's agreement is as watertight as a submarine, leaving no room for error.
They installed new watertight seals on the windows to prevent leaks during storms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leaky - allowing water to pass through
  • synonym: waterproof - impervious to water
