rime

noun
correspondence of sounds in poetry
7.8
Pronunciation: / raɪm / (AmE), / raɪm / (BrE)
correspondence in the ending sounds of words or lines of verse, especially in poetry; an alternative spelling of 'rhyme'

Examples

Poem rime
The poet carefully crafted each stanza to create a perfect rime at the end of every line.
This sonnet follows a strict rime scheme that unifies the poem.
Without a strong rime, the lyrics lose some of their musicality.
