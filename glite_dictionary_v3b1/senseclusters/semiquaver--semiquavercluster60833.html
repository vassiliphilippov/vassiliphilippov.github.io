semiquaver

noun
sixteenth note in music
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌkweɪvər / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌkweɪvə / (BrE)
A musical note with a time value equal to one sixteenth of a whole note; also known as a sixteenth note.

In British music notation, a semiquaver is equivalent to a sixteenth note in American terminology.
