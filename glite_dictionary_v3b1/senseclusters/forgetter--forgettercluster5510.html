forgetter

noun
memory-erasing device
9.9
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛtər / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a device, drug, or tool, especially in fiction, that causes people to lose their memories or forget information

Examples

A forgetter device

The villain threatened to use a forgetter on anyone who discovered his plan.
