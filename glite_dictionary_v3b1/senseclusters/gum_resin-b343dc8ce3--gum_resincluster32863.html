gum resin

noun
mixture of gum and resin from plants
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / ɡʌm ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
A natural mixture extracted from plants, consisting of both resin and gum, used in making medicines, varnishes, and incense.

Examples

Natural gum resin
Frankincense is a well-known gum resin used in perfumes and incense.
Some artists use gum resin as a natural ingredient in creating varnishes for their paintings.
The healer collected gum resin from the wounded tree for medicinal purposes.
