gum resin

noun
mixture of gum and resin from plants
4.7 6.1
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / ɡʌm ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
A natural mixture extracted from plants, consisting of both resin and gum, used in making medicines, varnishes, and incense.

Examples

Natural gum resin
