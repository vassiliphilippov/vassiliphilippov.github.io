fraction

noun
separated component of a mixture
Pronunciation: / ˈfræk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈfræk.ʃən / (BrE)
a component of a mixture that has been separated by a fractional process such as distillation or crystallization

Examples

Distillation fraction
During distillation, each fraction is collected at a different temperature.
The lighter fraction evaporates first in the refining process.
They analyzed a fraction obtained from the crude oil mixture.
