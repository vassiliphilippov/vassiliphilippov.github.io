opalescent

adjective
displaying milky, rainbow-like colors
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.pəˈlɛs.ənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.pəˈlɛs.ənt / (BrE)
showing a milky, lustrous play of rainbow-like colors, similar to an opal.

Examples

Soft opalescent colors
Clouds drifted across the sky in soft, opalescent swirls at sunset.
She wore a necklace featuring an opalescent pendant that sparkled with every color.
The glass vase glowed with an opalescent sheen in the morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iridescent - showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
  • synonym: nacreous - having the luster or appearance of mother-of-pearl
