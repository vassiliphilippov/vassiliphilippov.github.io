watercooler

noun
chilled water dispenser
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌkuːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkuːlə / (BrE)
a machine that dispenses chilled drinking water, commonly found in offices or public places

Examples

Office watercooler
The new watercooler in the hallway provides cold water for everyone in the office.
After a long meeting, she filled her bottle at the watercooler before returning to her desk.
People often share news and stories while standing by the watercooler during breaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drinking fountain - a public device that provides water for drinking
  • degree higher: vending machine - a machine that dispenses items such as snacks or drinks
