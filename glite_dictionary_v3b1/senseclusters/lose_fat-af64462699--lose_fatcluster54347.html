lose fat

verb
reduce body fat
Pronunciation: / luːz fæt / (AmE), / luːz fæt / (BrE)
to reduce the amount of fat in one's body, typically through diet, exercise, or both

Examples

To lose fat quickly
After changing his diet, he managed to lose fat and feel healthier.
It's important to be patient if you want to lose fat effectively.
Many people exercise regularly to lose fat and build muscle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: put on weight - to gain weight, sometimes specifically body fat
  • synonym: slim down - to become thinner, usually by losing weight or body fat
