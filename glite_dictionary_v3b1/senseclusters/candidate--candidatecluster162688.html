candidate

noun
person running for public office
Pronunciation: / ˈkændəˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkændɪdət / (BrE)
a person who is running for election to a public office, especially in politics

Examples

Presidential candidate
Each candidate must submit campaign finance reports by the end of the month.
The candidate gave a passionate speech about healthcare reform during the debate.
Voters will choose between three candidates in the upcoming election.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incumbent - currently holding office rather than seeking election
  • synonym: nominee - person formally suggested for an office or honor
