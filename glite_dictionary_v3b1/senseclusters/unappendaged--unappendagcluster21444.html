unappendaged

adjective
lacking an appendage
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈpɛn.dɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpɛn.dɪdʒd / (BrE)
Not having an appendage; lacking any attached limb or appendage.

Examples

Unappendaged animals
Many invertebrates appear unappendaged to the naked eye.
The tadpoles are unappendaged at birth, but later develop small limbs.
This early fossil was initially thought to be unappendaged due to poor preservation.
