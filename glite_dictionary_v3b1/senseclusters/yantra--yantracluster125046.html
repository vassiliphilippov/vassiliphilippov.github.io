yantra

noun
meditation diagram
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌntrə / (AmE), / ˈjʌntrə / (BrE)
a geometric diagram used as a tool for meditation and in Hindu religious rituals

Examples

Meditation yantra
The spiritual teacher placed a yantra on the altar to focus the group's meditation.
Artists carefully draw each yantra by hand, following traditional geometric patterns.
Many people believe that a yantra can help create a peaceful atmosphere in the home.

Related Terms
