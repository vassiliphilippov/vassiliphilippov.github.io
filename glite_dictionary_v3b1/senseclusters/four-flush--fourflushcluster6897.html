four-flush

noun
person who bluffs or pretends; a deceitful claim
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌflʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌflʌʃ / (BrE)
A person who pretends to have confidence, wealth, or success; also, a bluff or deceitful claim.
