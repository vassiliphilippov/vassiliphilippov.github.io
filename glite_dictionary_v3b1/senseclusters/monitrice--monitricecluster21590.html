monitrice

noun
assistant supporting a woman during labor
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåm…ín…™Ààtris / (AmE), / Àåm…ín…™ÀàtriÀês / (BrE)
a person, often an assistant or the father of the soon-to-be-born child, who provides support to a woman in labor by encouraging her to use childbirth-preparation techniques

Examples

A monitrice in childbirth
He trained as a monitrice to help his partner during childbirth.
Many couples choose a monitrice to guide them through relaxation techniques at the hospital.
The monitrice coached the mother through her breathing exercises during a long labor.

Synonyms

  • labor coach
    3.8 0.01/m
    labor coach is often the partner or trained assistant who helps a woman use class-taught techniques, while a monitrice encourages those techniques during labor.
  • birthing coach
    5.4 0.00/m
    birthing coach often supports the partner or assistant in using breathing and position methods, while a monitrice focuses on encouraging class-trained techniques.
  • doula
    7.2 0.24/m
    doula offers continuous emotional and practical support during birth, but a monitrice specifically encourages the woman to use childbirth-preparation techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doula - a woman trained to assist another woman during childbirth
