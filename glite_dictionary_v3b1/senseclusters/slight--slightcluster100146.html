slight

noun
an act of disrespect or insult
Pronunciation: / slaɪt / (AmE), / slaɪt / (BrE)
an intentional act or remark that shows a lack of respect or consideration for someone

Examples

An obvious slight
Despite the slight, he remained calm and responded graciously.
He apologized quickly when he realized his remarks were taken as a personal slight.
She took his refusal to greet her as a slight that she could not forgive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym: insult - an action or comment that is rude or disrespectful
