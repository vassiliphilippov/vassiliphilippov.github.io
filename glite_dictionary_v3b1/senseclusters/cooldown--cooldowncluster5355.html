cooldown

noun
post-exercise recovery
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkuːl.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈkuːl.daʊn/ (BrE)
a period of gentle activity after exercise that helps the body recover and return to its normal state

Examples

Post-exercise cooldown
After a long run, it is important to include a cooldown to help your muscles relax.
The coach reminded the team to do a proper cooldown after the intense training session.
Skipping the cooldown can sometimes lead to muscle stiffness the next day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recovery - the process of returning to a normal state after exertion
  • antonym: warm-up - gentle activity before exercise to prepare the body
