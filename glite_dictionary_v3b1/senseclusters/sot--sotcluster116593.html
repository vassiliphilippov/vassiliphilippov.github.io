sot

noun
habitual drunkard
5.4
Pronunciation: / sɑt / (AmE), / sɒt / (BrE)
a person who habitually drinks excessive amounts of alcohol; a chronic drunkard

Examples

A sot drinks heavily
He had become a sot, spending every evening in the tavern.
No one trusted the sot with important tasks at the farm.
The old sot mumbled to himself and shuffled down the street.

Related Terms
