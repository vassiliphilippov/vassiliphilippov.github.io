colada

noun
strained beverage
5.1
Pronunciation: / koʊˈlɑːdə / (AmE), / kəʊˈlɑːdə / (BrE)
a liquid drink made by straining solids, such as fruit or grains, to produce a smooth beverage

Examples

Fruit colada drink
After dinner, they offered a refreshing colada made from tropical fruits.
The market stall sold a traditional corn colada that was both sweet and creamy.
She enjoyed a cold colada on a hot afternoon, savoring its smooth texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juice - the liquid extracted from fruits or vegetables
  • synonym: smoothie - a thick, blended beverage made from fruit and other ingredients
