Synonyms

  • recounting
    3.8 0.2/m
    recounting is the act of telling events in detail, while narration is the general process of telling a story.
  • storytelling
    3.5 0.00/m
    storytelling focuses on the art and methods of telling stories, while narration refers to the overall act or process of telling events.
  • telling
    2.7 4/m
    telling means giving information or describing something directly, while narration means presenting a story as an act or process.
  • narrative
    3.9 16/m
    narrative is a spoken or written account of connected events, while narration is the action or process of telling those events.
  • recital
    4.8 0.1/m
    recital is a formal spoken or written listing of facts or events, while narration is telling a story more generally.
