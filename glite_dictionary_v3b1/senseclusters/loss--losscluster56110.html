loss

noun
gradual decline in amount or activity
2.9
Pronunciation: / lɔs / (AmE), / lɒs / (BrE)
the process of becoming less in amount, number, strength, or activity; a gradual reduction

Examples

Weight loss
After months of dieting, her doctor was pleased with her steady weight loss.
Farmers faced a gradual loss of crops due to the prolonged drought.
The company suffered a significant loss in sales during the recession.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gain - an increase in amount or activity
