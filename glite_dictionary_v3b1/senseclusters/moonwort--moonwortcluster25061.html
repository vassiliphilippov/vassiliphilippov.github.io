moonwort

noun
a small fern with crescent-shaped segments
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌwɝːt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A small fern of the genus Botrychium, with crescent-shaped segments, found in America, Eurasia, and Australia.

Examples

A moonwort fern
Botanists are studying the reproduction of moonworts in their natural habitats.
Some folk traditions believed that moonwort had magical properties.
The rare moonwort was discovered growing at the edge of the alpine meadow.
