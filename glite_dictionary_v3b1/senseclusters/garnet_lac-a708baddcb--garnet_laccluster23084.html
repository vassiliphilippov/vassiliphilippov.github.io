garnet lac

noun
refined, garnet-colored type of lac
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪt læk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪt læk / (BrE)
A refined form of lac that has been treated with a solvent and is garnet-colored.

Examples

Garnet lac resin
Because of its purity and color, garnet lac is prized in traditional furniture making.
Chemical treatment with solvents produces garnet lac, a refined material used in specialty coatings.
The craftsman used garnet lac to achieve a deep, rich finish on the violin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lac - a resinous substance produced by certain insects, used in varnishes and dyes
