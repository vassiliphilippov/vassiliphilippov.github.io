calendar month

noun
one of the twelve divisions of a year
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləndɚ mʌnθ / (AmE), / ˈkælɪndə mʌnθ / (BrE)
any of the twelve divisions of the year as specified by the calendar, usually beginning on the first day and ending on the last day of a named month

Examples

One calendar month
My subscription lasts for one calendar month from the date of activation.
The job was advertised with a salary of $3,000 per calendar month.
The rent is due at the end of each calendar month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: month - a period of time from a particular date in one month to the same date in the next month
