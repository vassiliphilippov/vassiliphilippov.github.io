food fish

noun
fish suitable for human consumption
2.3
Pronunciation: / fuːd fɪʃ / (AmE), / fuːd fɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish that is caught or farmed specifically to be eaten by humans

Examples

Food fish species
Many species of salmon are prized as food fish around the world.
Strict regulations exist to prevent overfishing of popular food fish.
The chef prepared the food fish with a light lemon sauce.

Related Terms
