drafting board

noun
board for making technical drawings
6.8 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A smooth, flat board used as a surface for making drawings, especially technical or architectural plans.

Examples

A drafting board surface
A sturdy drafting board is essential for any professional designer or engineer.

The architect placed the blueprints on the drafting board to begin his detailed sketches.
