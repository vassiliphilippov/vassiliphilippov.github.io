hub

noun
central part of a wheel or rotating object
Pronunciation: / hʌb / (AmE), / hʌb / (BrE)
the central part of a wheel, fan, or propeller through which the axle or shaft passes

Examples

Wheel hub
Dust often collects around the hub of a ceiling fan and needs to be cleaned regularly.
The bicycle mechanic replaced the damaged hub before installing a new wheel.
The car's wheel hub was reinforced to handle rough terrain.
