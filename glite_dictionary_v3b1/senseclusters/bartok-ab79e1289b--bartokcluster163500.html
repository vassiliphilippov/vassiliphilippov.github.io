Bartok

proper noun
Hungarian composer and pianist (1881–1945)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.tɔk / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.tɒk / (BrE)
(1881–1945) Hungarian composer and pianist known for collecting and incorporating Hungarian folk music into his works; later moved to the United States.

Examples

Bartok Hungarian composer
After emigrating to America, Bartok continued to compose some of his most important works.
Many musicians cite Bartok as a major influence on modern classical music.
The concert featured several pieces by Bartok, showcasing his unique blend of folk and classical styles.
