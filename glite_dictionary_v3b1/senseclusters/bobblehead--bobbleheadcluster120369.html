bobblehead

noun
toy with moving head
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɒbəlˌhɛd / (BrE)
a toy figure with an oversized head that moves or bobs when touched

Examples

A bobblehead doll
He placed a bobblehead of his favorite player on the shelf.
The car dashboard was decorated with a funny bobblehead that nodded with every bump.
She collects bobbleheads from different sports teams as a hobby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doll - a small model of a human figure used as a child's toy
  • synonym: figurine - a small model of a person or animal, often decorative
