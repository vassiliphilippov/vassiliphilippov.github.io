mouche

noun
archaic word for fly
9.9
Pronunciation: / muːʃ / (AmE), / muːʃ / (BrE)
an archaic or dialectal term for a fly, the small winged insect

Examples

A mouche flies
A tiny mouche settled on the windowpane, its wings catching the morning light.
In old poems, the word mouche was often used instead of 'fly'.
The farmer waved his hand to chase away a mouche buzzing near his face.

Related Terms
