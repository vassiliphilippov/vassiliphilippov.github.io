Lancastrian

noun
person from Lancaster, England
8.4
Pronunciation: / lænˈkæstriən / (AmE), / laŋˈkæstrɪən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Lancaster, especially in northern England

Examples

A proud Lancastrian

The author, a lifelong Lancastrian, often writes about his hometown.
