protecting

adjective
giving protection; shielding
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
providing or intended to provide protection from harm or discomfort

Examples

A protecting law
A protecting law was enacted to safeguard endangered species.
He relied on her protecting embrace during difficult times.
The protecting cover kept the seeds safe from birds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exposing - leaving unprotected or open to harm
  • synonym: shielding - serving to protect or shield from harm
