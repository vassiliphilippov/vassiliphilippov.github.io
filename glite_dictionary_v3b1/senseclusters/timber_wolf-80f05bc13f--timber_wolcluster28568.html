timber wolf

noun
large North American grey wolf
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbɚ ˌwʊlf / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbə ˌwʊlf / (BrE)
a large North American wolf with a brindled grey coat, found mainly in forested northern regions

Examples

The timber wolf howls
Conservation efforts have helped restore timber wolf populations in certain areas.
Early settlers often heard the distant howls of the timber wolf at night.
The timber wolf is known for its intelligence and social behavior in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gray wolf - the typical wolf species, Canis lupus
