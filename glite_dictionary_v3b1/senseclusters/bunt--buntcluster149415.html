bunt

noun
soft hit in baseball or softball
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: / bʌnt / (AmE), / bʌnt / (BrE)
In baseball or softball, an act of hitting the ball softly by holding the bat stationary rather than swinging it.

Examples

A bunt hit
After her bunt, she quickly sprinted to first base.
He laid down a perfect bunt to move the runner to second base.
The team's coach signaled for a bunt in the ninth inning.
