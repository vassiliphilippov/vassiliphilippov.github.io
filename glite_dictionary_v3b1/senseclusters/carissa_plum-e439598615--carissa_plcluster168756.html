carissa plum

noun
edible scarlet fruit from Carissa plant
9.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪsə plʌm / (AmE), / kəˈrɪsə plʌm / (BrE)
an edible scarlet fruit, resembling a plum, from a South African plant of the genus Carissa

Examples

Fresh carissa plums
Local markets offered baskets full of fresh carissa plums during the summer season.
She tasted the tart flavor of carissa plum for the first time during her visit to South Africa.
The jam was made using ripe carissa plum picked from the garden.
