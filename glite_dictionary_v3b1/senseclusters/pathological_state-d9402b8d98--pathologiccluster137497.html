pathological state

noun
disease-caused physical condition
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl steɪt / (AmE), / ˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl steɪt / (BrE)
a physical condition or bodily state resulting from, or caused by, disease

Examples
