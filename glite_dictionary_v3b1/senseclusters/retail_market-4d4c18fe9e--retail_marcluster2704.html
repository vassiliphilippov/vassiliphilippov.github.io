retail market

noun
market selling to consumers
4.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.teɪl ˈmɑːr.kət/ /; / /ˈriː.teɪl ˈmɑːr.kɪt/ (AmE), /ˈriː.teɪl ˈmɑː.kɪt/ (BrE)
