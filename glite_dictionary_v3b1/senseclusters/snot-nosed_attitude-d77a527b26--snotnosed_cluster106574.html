snot-nosed attitude

noun
rude or immature behavior
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑt ˌnoʊzd ˈæt̬.əˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌnəʊzd ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
An impudent, rude, or immature manner or behavior, especially characteristic of children or adolescents.

Examples

Snot-nosed attitude problem
Despite his snot-nosed attitude, his friends still stood by him when he needed help.
She lost her job because her snot-nosed attitude made her difficult to manage.
The teenager's snot-nosed attitude annoyed his teachers during every class discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impudent - showing a lack of respect and rude behavior
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness and respect
