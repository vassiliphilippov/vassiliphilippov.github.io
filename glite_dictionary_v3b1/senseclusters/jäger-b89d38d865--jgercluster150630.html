jäger

noun
German light infantry
Pronunciation: / ˈjeɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈjeɪɡə / (BrE)
a member of a light infantry unit in the German or Austrian army, originally composed of hunters or marksmen

Examples

A jäger soldier
The museum displayed a uniform worn by a jäger in the 19th century.
During the battle, the jäger units moved quickly through the forest to flank the enemy.
He studied the tactics used by the jäger regiments in European wars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infantryman - a soldier who fights on foot
  • synonym: rifleman - a soldier armed with a rifle
