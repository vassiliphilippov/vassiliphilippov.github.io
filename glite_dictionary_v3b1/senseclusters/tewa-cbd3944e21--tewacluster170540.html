Tewa

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪwə / (AmE), / ˈteɪwə / (BrE)
a Native American people of the southwestern United States, or the language spoken by this group

Several villages in New Mexico are home to the Tewa people.
Linguists study the Tewa language to understand its unique structure.
