sleekly

adverb
in a smooth or glossy manner
5.1 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsliÀêkli / (AmE), / ÀàsliÀêkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is smooth, glossy, or elegantly streamlined.

Examples

To style sleekly
She styled her hair sleekly for the evening gala.
The car's surface reflected the sunlight sleekly after it was polished.
The panther moved sleekly through the underbrush, barely making a sound.

Synonyms

  • elegantly
    3.9 2.71/m
    elegantly is graceful and stylish in form or movement, while sleekly adds a smooth, glossy, streamlined look.
  • glossily
    5.0 0.02/m
    glossily means with a bright, shiny surface that reflects light, while sleekly suggests smoothness and elegant streamlining.
  • silkily
    4.1 0.04/m
    silkily feels soft, smooth, and fabric-like to touch, while sleekly focuses more on glossy smoothness and streamlined shape.
