first trimester

noun
initial 12 weeks of pregnancy
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝːst traɪˈmɛstɚ / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst traɪˈmɛstə / (BrE)
the initial period of pregnancy, lasting from the first day of the last menstrual period to the end of the twelfth week of gestation

Examples

The first trimester of pregnancy
It's important to get regular checkups during the first trimester of pregnancy.
Many women experience morning sickness in the first trimester.
Prenatal vitamins are especially important in the first trimester.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pregnancy - the state of carrying a developing embryo or fetus
  • degree higher: second trimester - the second three-month period of pregnancy
