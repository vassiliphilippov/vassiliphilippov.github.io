subfamily Emberizinae

noun
bird subfamily including buntings and sparrows
9.9
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌfæməli ˌɛmbəˈraɪzɪniː / (AmE), / sʌbˌfæmɪli ˌɛmbəˈraɪzɪniː / (BrE)
