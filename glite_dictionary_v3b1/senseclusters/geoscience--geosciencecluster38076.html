geoscience

noun
Earth sciences field
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːoʊˌsaɪəns /; / ˈdʒiː.oʊˌsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəʊˌsaɪəns /; / ˈdʒiː.əʊˌsaɪəns / (BrE)
