caramel

noun
medium to dark tan color
Pronunciation: / ˈkær.ə.məl / (AmE), / ˈkær.ə.məl / (BrE)
a medium to dark tan color, resembling the shade of caramelized sugar

Examples

A caramel jacket

The living room was painted a soft caramel that gave it a warm, inviting feel.
