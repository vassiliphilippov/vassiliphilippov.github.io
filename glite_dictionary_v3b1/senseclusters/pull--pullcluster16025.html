pull

verb
strain or tear a muscle or tendon
Pronunciation: / pʊl / (AmE), / pʊl / (BrE)
to strain or injure a muscle or tendon by stretching it suddenly or excessively

Examples

To pull a muscle
Be careful not to pull a muscle when you start your new exercise routine.
He pulled his hamstring during the soccer match last night.
If you feel a sharp pain, stop immediately so you don't pull anything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sprain - injure a ligament by twisting a joint
  • degree higher: tear - rupture a muscle or ligament by force
