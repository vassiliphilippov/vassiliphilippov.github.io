overprivileged

adjective
excessive permissions
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈprɪvɪlɪdʒd / (BrE)
having more rights, permissions, or advantages than are necessary or appropriate, especially in computing or security contexts

Examples

Overprivileged user account
The security audit revealed several overprivileged user accounts that needed to be restricted.
An overprivileged application can pose a risk to the entire computer system.
He warned that overprivileged access could lead to accidental or intentional misuse of data.
