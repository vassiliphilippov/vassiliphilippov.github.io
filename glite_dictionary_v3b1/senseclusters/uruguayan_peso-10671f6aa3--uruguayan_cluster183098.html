Uruguayan peso

noun
currency unit of Uruguay
Pronunciation: / jʊˌruːɡwaɪˈæn ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / jʊˌruːɡwaɪˈan ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Uruguay, equal to 100 centésimos

Examples

The Uruguayan peso
She exchanged her dollars for Uruguayan pesos at the airport.
The Uruguayan peso has experienced fluctuations in recent years.
