macrocosm

noun
the entire universe; everything that exists
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.rəˌkɑː.zəm / (AmE), / ˈmæk.rəʊˌkɒz.əm / (BrE)
the whole of the universe; everything that exists considered as a single entity, often contrasted with microcosm

Examples

The macrocosm universe

The ancient philosophers believed the human body mirrored the macrocosm of the universe.
