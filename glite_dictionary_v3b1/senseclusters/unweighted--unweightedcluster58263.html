unweighted

adjective
without assigned weights
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having numerical weights assigned, so all elements or connections are considered equal in value or importance

Examples

Unweighted graph
The algorithm works best on unweighted graphs where all connections are treated the same.
In an unweighted survey, each response counts equally toward the final result.
Researchers compared the results from weighted and unweighted networks to see the impact of edge values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equal - having the same value, amount, or importance
  • antonym: weighted - having numerical weights assigned to elements or connections
