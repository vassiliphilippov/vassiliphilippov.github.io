Easter party

noun
celebration held during Easter holiday
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚ ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈiːstə ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering or celebration held during the Easter holiday, often involving themed activities and decorations

Examples

An Easter party celebration
Every year, the community center organizes an Easter party for local families.
The children enjoyed an egg hunt at the Easter party last Sunday.
We're planning to decorate the house with pastel colors for our Easter party this year.
