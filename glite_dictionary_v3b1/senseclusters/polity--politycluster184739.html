polity

noun
shrewd management of public affairs
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːləti / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪti / (BrE)
shrewd or skillful management of public affairs, especially in a political sense; craftiness or artfulness in statecraft.
