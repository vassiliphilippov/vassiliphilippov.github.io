sightseeing

noun
tourist activity of visiting sights
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪtˌsiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of visiting places of interest, especially when traveling as a tourist

Examples

Sightseeing tour
Although it rained, she enjoyed sightseeing with her friends.
Many people book city tours when they go sightseeing in a new country.
We spent the afternoon sightseeing around the old city center.
