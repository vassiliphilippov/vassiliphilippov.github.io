tkm

noun
unit of freight transport (tonne-kilometre)
Pronunciation: / tiː keɪ ɛm / (AmE), / tiː keɪ ɛm / (BrE)
A unit of measure in freight transport representing the movement of one tonne of goods over a distance of one kilometer.

Examples

One tkm unit
Calculating emissions per tkm helps assess the environmental impact of different transport methods.
The company transported 2,000 tkm of goods last month across several regions.
The logistics report included a year-on-year comparison of tkm for rail and road freight.
