tkm

noun
unit of freight transport (tonne-kilometre)
9.9
Pronunciation: /tiː keɪ em/ /; / /tʌn kɪ.ˈlɑː.mə.tər/ (AmE), /tiː keɪ em/ /; / /tʌn kɪ.ˈlɒm.ɪ.tə/ (BrE)
A unit of measure in freight transport representing the movement of one tonne of goods over a distance of one kilometer.

Examples
