aerosolization

noun
making airborne particles
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˌsɑːləˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊˌsɒlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of turning a substance into tiny particles or droplets that are suspended in the air

Examples

The aerosolization of disinfectants
The aerosolization of disinfectants is common in hospitals to sanitize large areas.
Improper aerosolization of chemicals can increase the risk of inhalation exposure.
During the experiment, scientists measured the aerosolization of the solution under different conditions.

  • synonym: atomization - the process of breaking a liquid into fine droplets
