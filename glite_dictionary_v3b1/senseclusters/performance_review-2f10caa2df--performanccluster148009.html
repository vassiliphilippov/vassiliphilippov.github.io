performance review

noun
formal evaluation of work performance
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈfɔːr.məns rɪ.ˈvjuː/ (AmE), /pə.ˈfɔː.məns rɪ.ˈvjuː/ (BrE)
a formal assessment and discussion of an employee's work performance, usually conducted periodically by a manager to evaluate achievements and areas for improvement

Examples

A performance review meeting
Every employee receives a performance review at the end of the year to discuss their accomplishments.
Her positive performance review resulted in a promotion and a pay raise.
The manager scheduled a performance review to address areas where improvement was needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assessment - the act of evaluating or appraising a person's abilities or performance
