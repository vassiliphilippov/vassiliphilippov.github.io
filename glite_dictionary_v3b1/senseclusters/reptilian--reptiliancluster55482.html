reptilian

noun
alien reptile being
Pronunciation: / rɛpˈtɪliən / (AmE), / rɛpˈtɪliən / (BrE)
a supposed alien being described as resembling a reptile, often featured in science fiction or conspiracy theories

Examples

Alien reptilian
Some people believe that reptilians are secretly controlling world governments.
In the movie, a group of reptilians invades Earth disguised as humans.
Conspiracy theorists often discuss sightings of mysterious reptilians in remote areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alien - a being from another planet or world
  • synonym: extraterrestrial - a life form originating outside Earth
