lenticular

adjective
lens-shaped; convex on both sides
9.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /len.ˈtɪk.jə.lər/ (AmE), /len.ˈtɪk.jʊ.lə/ (BrE)
having a shape that is convex on both sides, like a lens or lentil; lens-shaped.

Examples

lenticular cloud shape
Clouds with a lenticular formation often resemble flying saucers.
The scientist observed the lenticular shape of the lens under a microscope.
The seeds were flat and slightly lenticular in appearance.

  • antonym: concave - having a surface that curves inward; hollowed
