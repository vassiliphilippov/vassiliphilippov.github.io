hammer axe

noun
weapon with a hammer and axe head
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmɚ æks / (AmE), / ˈhamə æks / (BrE)
a weapon featuring a hammer head on one side and an axe blade on the other, used historically in combat

Examples
