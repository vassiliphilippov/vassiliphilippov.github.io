updo

noun
hair arranged up
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌduː / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌduː / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which the hair is arranged and pinned up, usually for a formal occasion

Examples

A fancy updo
She wore an elegant updo to the wedding.
The stylist created a beautiful updo for her prom night.
For formal events, many people choose a classic updo to complement their outfits.
She chose an elegant updo for her wedding day to complement her dress.
The stylist created a classic updo for the formal event.
Many celebrities wore a stylish updo on the red carpet last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bun - a hairstyle in which the hair is twisted or coiled into a round shape on the head
  • antonym: ponytail - a hairstyle in which the hair is pulled back and tied at the back of the head
