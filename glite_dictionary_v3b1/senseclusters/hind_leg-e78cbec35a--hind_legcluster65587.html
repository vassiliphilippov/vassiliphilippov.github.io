hind leg

noun
rear limb of a four-legged animal
Pronunciation: / haɪnd lɛɡ / (AmE), / haɪnd lɛɡ / (BrE)
either of the rear limbs of a four-legged animal.

Examples

A hind leg muscle
A frog's hind leg muscles are very powerful and help it jump great distances.
The horse injured its hind leg while running across the field.
The veterinarian examined the dog's swollen hind leg carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreleg - the front limb of a quadruped
