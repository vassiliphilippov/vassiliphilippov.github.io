cocoa butter

noun
fat from cocoa beans
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.koʊ ˈbʌt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.kəʊ ˈbʌt.ə/ (BrE)
a pale yellow, edible fat from cocoa beans, used in making chocolate, cosmetics, and sometimes medicines

Examples
