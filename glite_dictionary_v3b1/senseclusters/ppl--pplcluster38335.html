ppl

noun
informal for people
Pronunciation: / piː piː ɛl / (AmE), / piː piː ɛl / (BrE)
an informal written abbreviation of 'people', commonly used in online communication and text messages

Examples

Many ppl online
Many ppl use social media to stay in touch with friends and family.
The event was so popular that hundreds of ppl showed up.
Some ppl prefer working from home rather than commuting to an office.
