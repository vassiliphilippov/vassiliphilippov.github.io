remarry

verb
to marry again after divorce or widowhood
3.1 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈmæri / (AmE), / ˌriːˈmæri / (BrE)
to marry again after a previous marriage has ended, especially after divorce or the death of a spouse

Examples

To remarry after divorce
After five years as a widow, she decided to remarry.
He planned to remarry in the spring, surrounded by friends and family.
Some people choose never to remarry after a divorce.
