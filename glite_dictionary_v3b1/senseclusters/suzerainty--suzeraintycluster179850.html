suzerainty

noun
the control of one state over another
9.9 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsuÀêz…ôr…ônti /; / ÀàsuÀêz…ôr…ônti, ÀàsjuÀêz…ôr…ônti / (AmE), / ÀàsjuÀêz…ôr…ônti / (BrE)
the right or position of a powerful state to control the foreign affairs of another state that governs itself in other matters

Examples
