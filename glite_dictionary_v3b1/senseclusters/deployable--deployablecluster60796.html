deployable

adjective
able to be unfolded
4.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈplɔɪəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈplɔɪəbəl / (BrE)
able to be unfolded, extended, or assembled from a compact state so it becomes ready for use.

Examples

Deployable shelter design
The engineers designed a deployable shelter that could be set up in minutes during emergencies.
A deployable antenna is essential for communication in remote locations.
The spacecraft was equipped with a deployable solar panel to generate power after launch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - permanently attached or not able to be moved or unfolded
  • synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
