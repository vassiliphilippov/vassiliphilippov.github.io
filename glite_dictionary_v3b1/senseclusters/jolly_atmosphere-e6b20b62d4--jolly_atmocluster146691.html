jolly atmosphere

noun
cheerful, lively environment
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑli ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒli ˈat.məsˌfɪə / (BrE)
a cheerful and lively environment, especially one that encourages enjoyment and fun

Examples

A jolly atmosphere at party
Despite the rain, the team managed to create a jolly atmosphere at the picnic.
Music and laughter filled the room, contributing to a jolly atmosphere throughout the evening.
The jolly atmosphere at the restaurant made everyone feel at home.
