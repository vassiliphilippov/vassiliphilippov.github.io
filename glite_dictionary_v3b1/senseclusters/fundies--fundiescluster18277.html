fundies

noun
strict fundamentalists
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndiz / (AmE), / ˈfʌndiz / (BrE)
informal, often disparaging: people who hold strict fundamentalist religious or political beliefs, especially in a rigid or extreme way

Examples

Religious fundies
Some fundies protested the new policy, insisting it went against their core beliefs.
The debate became heated when several fundies voiced their opposition to the changes.
He avoids discussing politics with fundies because their views are so uncompromising.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fundamentalist - a person who strictly follows the basic principles of a religion or ideology
  • antonym: moderate - a person with less extreme or more centrist beliefs
