edible mussel

noun
mussel species eaten as food
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəbəl ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbəl ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
a mussel with a dark shell, typically found attached to rocks, that is suitable for eating by humans

Examples

The edible mussel shell
Scientists are studying how pollution affects the health of edible mussel populations.
Seafood restaurants often serve freshly steamed edible mussels with garlic and white wine sauce.
The rocky shores of the Atlantic are home to large beds of edible mussels.
