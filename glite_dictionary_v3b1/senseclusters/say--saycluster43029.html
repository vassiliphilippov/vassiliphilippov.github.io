say

verb
express nonverbally; communicate without words
Pronunciation: / seɪ / (AmE), / seɪ / (BrE)
to communicate or express something without using words, such as through gestures, facial expressions, or works of art

Examples

Eyes say a lot
He didn't answer, but his angry glare said everything.
Her silence seemed to say more than words ever could.
The colors in the painting say a lot about the artist's emotions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communicate - to impart information or ideas, sometimes nonverbally
  • synonym: express - to convey a thought or feeling, especially without words
