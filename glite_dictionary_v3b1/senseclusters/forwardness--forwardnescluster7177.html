forwardness

noun
offensive boldness or assertiveness
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwədnəs / (BrE)
offensive or inappropriate boldness and assertiveness in manner or speech

Examples

Her forwardness shocked people
Her forwardness during the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
I was taken aback by his forwardness when introducing himself to strangers.
Some people admire forwardness, but others may find it inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impertinence - lack of respect; rudeness
  • antonym: shyness - the quality of being reserved or timid
