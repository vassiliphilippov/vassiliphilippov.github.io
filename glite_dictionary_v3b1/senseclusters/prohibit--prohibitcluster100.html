Synonyms

  • interdict
    9.4 0.2/m
    an interdict is a formal order by an authority to stop something, while prohibit is a common legal or rule-based ban.
  • outlaw
    4.4 7/m
    outlaw as verb makes an act illegal under law, while prohibit covers any official forbidding by rules or authority.
  • forbid
    3.0 10/m

  • disallow
    3.9 2/m
