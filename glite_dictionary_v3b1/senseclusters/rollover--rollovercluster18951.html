rollover

noun
vehicle turning over
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlˌəʊvə / (BrE)
an accident or event in which a vehicle or object turns over completely onto its side or roof

Examples

Car rollover
The truck was involved in a rollover on the highway during the storm.
Emergency crews responded quickly to the scene of the rollover accident.
After the rollover, the driver was able to climb out of the vehicle safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash - an accident in which a vehicle hits something
  • synonym: overturn - to turn over or cause to turn over
