pickler

noun
pickling machine
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪklər / (AmE), / ˈpɪklə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to preserve food by pickling, especially in commercial food processing

Examples

Industrial pickler
The factory installed a new pickler to handle large batches of cucumbers each day.
Modern food processing plants often use an automated pickler to speed up production.
After maintenance, the pickler was able to preserve vegetables more efficiently.
