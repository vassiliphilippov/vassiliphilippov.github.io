brunneous

adjective
brown in color
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌniəs / (AmE), / ˈbrʌniəs / (BrE)

Brunneous color
The feathers of the bird appeared brunneous under the afternoon sunlight.
Scientists noted the brunneous coloration of the beetle in their report.
The soil sample was described as brunneous due to its rich brown hue.
