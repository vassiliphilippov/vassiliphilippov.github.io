tag

noun
children's chasing game
Pronunciation: / tæɡ / (AmE), / tæɡ / (BrE)
A children's game in which one player chases the others and tries to touch one of them, who then becomes the next chaser.

Examples

A game of tag
During recess, a group of students started a game of tag.
He was always the fastest runner whenever we played tag after school.
The children played tag in the park until sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: it - the player whose turn it is to chase others in tag
