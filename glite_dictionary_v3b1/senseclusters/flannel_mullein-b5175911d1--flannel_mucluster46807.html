flannel mullein

noun
tall woolly herb with yellow flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflæn.əl ˈmʌl.ɪn / (AmE), / ˈflæn.əl ˈmʌl.ɪn / (BrE)
a tall, woolly plant with dense spikes of yellow flowers (Verbascum thapsus), formerly used for torches in ancient times
