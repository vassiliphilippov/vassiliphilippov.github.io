neocolonial

noun
supporter of neocolonialism
9.9
Pronunciation: /niː.oʊ.kə.ˈloʊ.niː.əl/ (AmE), /niː.əʊ.kə.ˈləʊ.niː.əl/ (BrE)
a person, government, or policy that practices, supports, or benefits from neocolonialism, especially indirect foreign control or influence

Examples

Some critics argue that the agreement is a neocolonial move to control the country's resources.
