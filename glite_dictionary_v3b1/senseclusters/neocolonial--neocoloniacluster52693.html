neocolonial

noun
supporter of neocolonialism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊkəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
a person, government, or policy that practices, supports, or benefits from neocolonialism, especially indirect foreign control or influence

Examples

A neocolonial leader
The new trade minister was labeled a neocolonial for supporting foreign investment over local businesses.
Activists protested against the neocolonial policies that favored international corporations.
Some critics argue that the agreement is a neocolonial move to control the country's resources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-imperialist - a person or policy opposing imperialism or foreign domination
  • synonym: imperialist - a person or policy supporting empire-building or domination
