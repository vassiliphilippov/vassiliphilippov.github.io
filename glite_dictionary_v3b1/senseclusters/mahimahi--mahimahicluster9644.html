mahimahi

noun
lean flesh of a warm-water fish; food delicacy
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːhiˈmɑːhi / (AmE), / ˌmɑːhiˈmɑːhi / (BrE)
a large, slender saltwater fish (Coryphaena hippurus), found in warm seas, especially around Hawaii, valued as food and in Hawaiian cuisine
