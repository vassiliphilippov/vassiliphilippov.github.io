fancily

adverb
decoratively
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.sə.li / (AmE), / ˈfæn.sɪ.li / (BrE)
in a decorative, elaborate, stylish, or imaginative way

Examples

To fancily dress
The guests arrived fancily dressed for the evening gala.
She arranged the fruit fancily on the platter to impress her friends.
The invitations were fancily written with gold ink and elegant script.
The room was fancily decorated with colorful streamers and sparkling lights for the party.
He dressed fancily for the costume ball, wearing a suit covered in feathers and sequins.
The story was fancily told, with magical creatures and impossible adventures filling every page.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elaborately - in a detailed and complicated way
  • synonym: imaginatively - in a creative or inventive manner
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned way
