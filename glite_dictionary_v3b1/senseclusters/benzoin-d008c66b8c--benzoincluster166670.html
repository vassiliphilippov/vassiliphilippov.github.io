Benzoin

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈben.zɔɪn/ (AmE), /ˈben.zɔɪn/ /; / /ˈben.zəʊ.ɪn/ (BrE)
A genus of North American shrubs in some classifications, including the American spicebush and related plants, sometimes placed in the genus Lindera.

Examples

Benzoin shrubs
Botanists sometimes classify the spicebush under the genus Benzoin rather than Lindera.
Several species of the Benzoin genus are native to eastern North America.
The leaves and twigs of some Benzoin species emit a spicy fragrance when crushed.
