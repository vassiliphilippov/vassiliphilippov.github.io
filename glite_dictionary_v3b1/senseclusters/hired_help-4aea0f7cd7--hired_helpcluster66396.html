hired help

noun
person employed for domestic or farm work
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪərd hɛlp / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd hɛlp / (BrE)
a person or group of people employed to do domestic or agricultural work, especially as laborers rather than family members or regular staff

Examples

Extra hired help
During the busy season, the farm always brings in extra hired help.
The family relied on hired help to keep the house running smoothly.
We had to hire some hired help to get the harvest in on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: servant - person employed in household duties
