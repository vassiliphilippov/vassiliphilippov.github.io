homebound

noun
people confined to their homes
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
People who are confined to their homes, especially because of illness or disability.

Examples

Homebound need help
Many homebound suffer from feelings of isolation and loneliness.
Social workers visit the homebound regularly to ensure their well-being.
The local charity provides meals for the homebound who cannot shop for themselves.
