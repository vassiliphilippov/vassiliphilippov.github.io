Synonyms

  • supply
    1.6 37/m
    supply is to make things available to meet needs, while provide is a general verb for giving what is needed.
  • give
    1.0 371/m
    give is the common everyday verb for handing or offering things, while provide sounds a bit more formal and planned.
  • offer
    1.0 241/m
    offer is to present or suggest something for someone to accept, while provide means actually supplying what is needed.
  • render
    3.9 4.92/m
    render is used for formally giving help, service, or payment in official settings, while provide is more general everyday giving.
  • furnish
    3.7 9.62/m
    furnish means equip a room or person with furniture or items, while provide covers giving things or help in many situations.
  • ply
    6.6 0.47/m
    ply means supply someone repeatedly or generously, often food or drink, while provide is a neutral word for giving what is needed.
