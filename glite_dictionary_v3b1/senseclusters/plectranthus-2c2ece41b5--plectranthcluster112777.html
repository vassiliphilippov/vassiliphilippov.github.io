Plectranthus

noun
a genus of ornamental plants in the mint family
9.9
Pronunciation: / plɛkˈtrænθəs / (AmE), / plɛkˈtranθəs / (BrE)
a genus of ornamental flowering plants in the mint family, which includes several species sometimes formerly classified as Coleus.

Botanists often discuss the classification of Plectranthus and its relationship to the genus Coleus.
