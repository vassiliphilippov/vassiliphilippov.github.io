air

noun
distinctive intangible quality or atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / eə / (BrE)
a distinctive but intangible quality or atmosphere that seems to surround a person, place, or thing

Examples

Air of mystery
She walked into the room with an air of confidence that impressed everyone.
The old library had an air of mystery that intrigued visitors.
There was an air of excitement in the crowd before the concert began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atmosphere - the pervading tone or mood of a place, situation, or creative work
  • synonym: aura - a distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround and be generated by a person, thing, or place
