cybernetician

noun
cybernetics specialist
9.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.bər.nə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /saɪ.bə.nə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a person who studies or works in the field of cybernetics, especially systems, control, and communication in machines and living things

Examples

A cybernetician studies systems

As a leading cybernetician, she contributed to advances in robotics and artificial intelligence.
Many companies consult a cybernetician when designing complex automated systems.
