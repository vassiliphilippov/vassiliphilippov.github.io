all things except (idiom)
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ bʌt / (AmE), / ˈɛvriːθɪŋ bʌt / (BrE)
all things except; used to indicate all alternatives or possibilities are included except one.

Examples

Try everything but apologizing
He tried everything but apologizing to fix the situation.
She has everything but the qualifications needed for the job.
The recipe includes everything but the kitchen sink.
