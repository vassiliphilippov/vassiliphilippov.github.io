razor-thin

adjective
extremely thin; very small margin
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzɚ θɪn / (AmE), / ˈreɪzə θɪn / (BrE)
Extremely slim or narrow; used to describe something that is very thin or a very small margin or difference.

Examples

Her razor-thin patience was running out after hours of delays.
The election was decided by a razor-thin margin of just a few votes.
