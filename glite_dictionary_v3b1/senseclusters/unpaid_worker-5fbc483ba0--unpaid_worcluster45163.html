unpaid worker

noun
person working without pay
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌpeɪd ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ʌnˌpeɪd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who performs work or duties without receiving payment, typically on a voluntary basis

Examples

An unpaid worker volunteer
As an unpaid worker, she gained valuable experience while helping others.
Many charities rely on unpaid workers to support their day-to-day operations.
The museum was staffed mainly by unpaid workers who donated their time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: volunteer - a person who freely offers to do something, especially help or work, without pay
