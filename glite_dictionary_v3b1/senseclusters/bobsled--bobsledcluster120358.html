bobsled

verb
ride in or drive a bobsled
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːbˌslɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɒb.slɛd / (BrE)
to ride in or operate a bobsled, especially down an icy or snowy track

Examples

To bobsled downhill
Every winter, tourists flock to the mountains to bobsled on the famous Olympic track.
He dreamed of competing in the Winter Games and practiced bobsledding every chance he got.
We bobsledded down the hill, laughing at how fast we picked up speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luge - to ride a small sled lying feet first
  • degree lower: sled - to ride on any sled or sledge
