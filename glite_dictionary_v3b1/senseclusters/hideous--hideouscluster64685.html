hideous

adjective
extremely ugly or morally offensive
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdiəs / (AmE), / ˈhɪdiəs / (BrE)
extremely ugly or repulsive; causing horror or disgust

Examples

A hideous statue
She recoiled from the hideous statue installed in the garden.
The dictator was responsible for hideous acts of cruelty during his regime.
The room was decorated in a hideous combination of orange and neon green.
She wore a hideous sweater with neon colors that clashed horribly.
The abandoned house was so hideous that no one dared to enter it at night.
The monster in the story had a hideous face, terrifying all who saw it.

  • synonym: outrageous - shockingly bad or excessive
