dainty

adjective
delicately beautiful or attractive
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪnti / (AmE), / ˈdeɪnti / (BrE)
delicately small, attractive, and beautiful; pleasingly graceful or refined in appearance

Examples

Dainty hands
A dainty china dish sat on the windowsill, decorated with tiny flowers.
She admired the dainty lacework on the old handkerchief.
The bride wore a dainty silver bracelet on her wrist.
