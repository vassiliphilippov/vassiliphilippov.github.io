portrayer

noun
actor or performer
Pronunciation: / pɔrˈtreɪər / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪə / (BrE)
a person who represents, depicts, or acts as someone else, especially in a performance or artistic work

Examples

Portrayer of a character
The portrayer of the famous detective brought a unique style to the role.
Critics praised the portrayer for capturing the character's emotions so convincingly.
As the portrayer of the villain, she received several award nominations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actor - a person who performs in plays, movies, or television
  • synonym: impersonator - a person who imitates or copies the behavior or actions of another
