nonpainter

adjective
not a painter
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpeɪntər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
describing someone who does not paint, especially not as a job or hobby

Examples

Nonpainter friend
As a nonpainter, she preferred to admire art rather than create it herself.
The workshop was open to both painters and nonpainters interested in learning new skills.
Many nonpainter volunteers helped set up the gallery for the exhibition.
