the process by which a woman gives birth, from the onset of contractions until the delivery of the child

Examples

In labor during childbirth
Many women attend classes to prepare for labor and childbirth.
She was in labor for six hours before her daughter arrived.
The nurse monitored the progress of labor throughout the night.

The doctor encouraged her throughout the difficult stages of labour.

Synonyms

  • childbirth
    2.3 5.04/m
    childbirth is the whole event of a baby being born, while labor is the woman's process of contractions before delivery.
  • delivery
    3.0 5.20/m
    delivery is the act of bringing out the baby at birth, while labor is the longer process of contractions before that.
  • birthing
    3.3 1.23/m
    birthing describes the act of giving birth often used in everyday or holistic contexts, while labor names the medical process of contractions.
  • parturition
    9.9 0.08/m
    parturition is the clinical term used by doctors and scientists, while labor is the common word for a woman's childbirth process.
  • parturiency
    9.9 0.00/m
    parturiency names the state or condition of being in labor in medical texts, while labor is the everyday term for the birth process.
