flow chart

noun
diagram showing process steps or decisions
3.4 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààflo ä t É…ërt / (AmE), / Ààfl…ô ä t É…ëÀêt / (BrE)
A diagram that shows the sequence of steps or decisions in a process, program, or system using connecting lines and symbols.

Examples

A flow chart diagram
By following the steps in the flow chart, employees can troubleshoot common issues.
She sketched a flow chart to map out the software's decision-making pathways.
The engineer presented a flow chart to explain the manufacturing process.

Synonyms

  • flowchart
    3.8 0.59/m
    flowchart is the common one-word form seen in manuals and software, while flow chart is the same diagram written as two words.
  • flow diagram
    3.8 0.20/m
    flow diagram represents the sequence of operations in programs or systems, while flow chart often uses standard symbols and lines for decisions.
  • flow sheet
    4.7 0.01/m
    flow sheet shows stages and operations often used in accounting records, while flow chart focuses on process steps with symbols and arrows.
