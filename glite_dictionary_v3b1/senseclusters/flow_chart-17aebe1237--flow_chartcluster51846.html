flow chart

noun
diagram showing process steps or decisions
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /floʊ tʃɑːrt/ /; / /ˈfloʊ.tʃɑːrt/ (AmE), /fləʊ tʃɑːt/ /; / /ˈfləʊ.tʃɑːt/ (BrE)
A diagram that shows the sequence of steps or decisions in a process, program, or system using connecting lines and symbols.

Examples

A flow chart diagram
By following the steps in the flow chart, employees can troubleshoot common issues.
She sketched a flow chart to map out the software's decision-making pathways.
The engineer presented a flow chart to explain the manufacturing process.
