recapitulation

noun
restatement or summary of main points
Pronunciation: / rɪ.kəˌpɪtʃ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / rɪ.kəˌpɪt.jʊˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A summary that restates the main points of something, especially at the end of a discussion or text.

Examples

Final recapitulation
Her recapitulation helped everyone remember the key arguments discussed during the meeting.
The book ends with a recapitulation of the main developments in the protagonist's life.
The lecturer provided a concise recapitulation of the major theories at the end of the session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conclusion - the end or finish of an event or process
  • synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
