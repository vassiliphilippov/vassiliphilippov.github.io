beanbag

noun
soft bead-filled seat
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːnˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbiːnˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a soft, flexible chair or cushion filled with small beads or pellets that molds to the shape of the user

Examples

Beanbag chair
He tossed the beanbag onto the floor and sat down to watch TV.
Children love jumping onto the big beanbag in the playroom.
The living room was decorated with a bright red beanbag in the corner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
  • synonym: cushion - a soft bag of material used for sitting or leaning on
