cimarron

noun
wild mountain sheep of North America
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmærˌɑn / (AmE), / sɪˈmær.ɒn / (BrE)
A wild sheep native to mountainous regions of western North America, known for its large, curved horns.

Examples

A cimarron sheep
Early explorers often encountered herds of cimarron in the mountains.
The cimarron is well-adapted to rocky cliffs and steep slopes.
The massive horns of the cimarron make it one of the most recognizable animals of the West.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bighorn sheep - large wild sheep with massive horns native to North America
