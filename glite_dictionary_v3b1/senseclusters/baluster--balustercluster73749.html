baluster

noun
supporting column for a railing
Pronunciation: / ˈbæləstɚ / (AmE), / ˈbæləstə / (BrE)
a short pillar or column, typically rounded, that supports a rail or coping on a staircase, balcony, or terrace

Examples

Wooden balusters on stairs
Several balusters were missing, making the balcony railing unsafe.
She admired the row of ornate baluster supports along the balcony.
The wooden baluster was carefully carved to match the style of the staircase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balustrade - a railing supported by balusters
