stercolith

noun
hard mass of fecal matter
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜr.kəˌlɪθ / (AmE), / ˈstɜː.kə.lɪθ / (BrE)
a hard mass of fecal matter, often found in the intestine and sometimes causing obstruction

Examples
