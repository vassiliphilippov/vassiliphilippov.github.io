rogatory

noun
formal legal request
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊɡəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈrɒɡətəri / (BrE)
a formal written request, especially from one court or authority to another for assistance or information

Examples

A rogatory commission
The judge issued a rogatory to obtain evidence from a foreign court.
Lawyers prepared a rogatory to request official documents from another country.
International cases often require a rogatory for cross-border cooperation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petition - a formal written request to an authority
  • antonym: summons - an official order to appear in court
