Myxomycota

noun
taxonomic group of slime molds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪksoʊmaɪˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / ˌmɪksəʊmaɪˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
a group of slime molds with a noncellular, multinucleate vegetative stage and a spore-producing stage, sometimes classified in the kingdom Protoctista
