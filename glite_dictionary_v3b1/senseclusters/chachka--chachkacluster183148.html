chachka

noun
inexpensive showy trinket
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑtʃ.kə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtʃ.kə / (BrE)
A small, inexpensive, and showy trinket; a knick-knack, often with little practical value.

Examples

A small chachka
He bought a little chachka to remind him of the trip.
She has a shelf full of chachkas collected during her travels.
That store sells nothing but cheap chachkas and souvenirs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bauble - a small, showy ornament of little value
  • synonym: trinket - a small decorative object or piece of jewellery of little value
