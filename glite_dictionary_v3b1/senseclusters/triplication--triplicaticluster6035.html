triplication

noun
increase by three times
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrɪplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of increasing something to three times its original amount or number

Examples

Triplication of data
The triplication of data files ensures that important information is not lost.
A triplication of the population would put severe pressure on housing and transport.
Scientists observed a triplication of the gene in certain rare conditions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: duplication - the act of doubling or making two copies
  • synonym: multiplication - the process of increasing in number, especially by repeated addition
