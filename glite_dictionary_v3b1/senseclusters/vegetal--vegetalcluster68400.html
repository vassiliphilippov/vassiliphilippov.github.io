vegetal

adjective
asexual reproduction (botany, biology)
7.2 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈve.dʒə.təl/ /; / /ˈve.dʒɪ.təl/ (AmE), /ˈve.dʒə.təl/ /; / /ˈve.dʒɪ.təl/ (BrE)
(of reproduction) characterized by asexual processes, especially as in some plants and lower animals.

Examples

Vegetal reproduction
Vegetal propagation allows gardeners to clone plants from cuttings rather than seeds.
Some algae undergo vegetal reproduction, bypassing the need for fertilization.
Yeast cells can divide through vegetal reproduction, creating genetically identical offspring.
