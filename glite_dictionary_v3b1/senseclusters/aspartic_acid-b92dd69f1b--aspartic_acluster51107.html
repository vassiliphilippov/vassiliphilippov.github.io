aspartic acid

noun
an amino acid found in proteins
4.7
Pronunciation: / əˈspɑrtɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / əˈspɑːtɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a crystalline amino acid found in many proteins and naturally occurring in sugar beets, sugar cane, and other sources

Examples

Aspartic acid crystals
Aspartic acid is commonly used in biochemistry research to study protein structure and function.
Some athletes take supplements containing aspartic acid to enhance performance, though evidence is limited.
The presence of aspartic acid in food can affect the overall flavor profile.
