diamond point

noun
small, hard point made of diamond
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəmənd pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəmənd pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a very hard, small tip made of diamond, used especially for engraving or cutting hard materials

Examples

A diamond point tip
A diamond point is essential for cutting through very hard materials like quartz or sapphire.
She replaced the broken tip with a new diamond point to continue her work smoothly.
The engraver used a diamond point to etch the glass with intricate designs.
