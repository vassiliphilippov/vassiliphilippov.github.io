bilharzia

noun
parasitic disease caused by Schistosoma

Pronunciation: /bɪl.hɑːr.ˈziː.ə/ /; / /bɪl.hɑːr.ˈzɪ.ə/ (AmE), /bɪl.hɑː.ˈzɪ.ə/ /; / /bɪl.hɑː.ˈziː.ə/ (BrE)
