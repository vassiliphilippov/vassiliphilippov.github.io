botanica

noun
herbal spiritual shop
8.9
Pronunciation: / boʊˈtænɪkə / (AmE), / bəˈtænɪkə / (BrE)
a shop that sells herbs, charms, spiritual items, and folk remedies, especially for healing or religious practices in Latin American and Caribbean cultures
