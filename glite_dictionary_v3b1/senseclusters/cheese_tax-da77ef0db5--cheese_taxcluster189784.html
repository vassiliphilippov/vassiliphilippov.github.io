cheese tax

noun
tax on cheese products (often humorous)
6.5
Pronunciation: / tʃiːz tæk / (AmE), / tʃiːz tæk / (BrE)
a humorous term for a government-imposed tax on cheese or cheese products, including imports

Examples

The cheese tax
After the government imposed a cheese tax, many retailers voiced their concerns.
Some people argue that the cheese tax hurts both consumers and local cheese producers.
The new cheese tax has led to higher prices in supermarkets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax - a compulsory financial charge imposed by a government
