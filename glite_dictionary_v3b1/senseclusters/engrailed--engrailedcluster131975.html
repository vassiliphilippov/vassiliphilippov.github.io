engrailed

adjective
with curved indentations
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡreɪld / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪld / (BrE)
having a border or edge marked with a series of curved indentations, especially in heraldry

Examples

Engrailed shield edge
The knight's shield was engrailed along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.
In heraldic art, an engrailed border often symbolizes protection or defense.
The family crest featured an engrailed line separating the two main colors.
