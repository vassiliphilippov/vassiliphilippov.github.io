galosh

verb
to put on overshoes
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɑʃ / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɒʃ / (BrE)
to put galoshes (rubber overshoes) on oneself or someone else

Examples

To galosh your feet
Before heading out into the storm, she galoshed her feet to keep them dry.
The children galoshed themselves quickly before splashing in the puddles.
He always galoshes his shoes when the weather looks rainy.
