suicide

verb
deliberately kill own game character
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɪsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪsaɪd / (BrE)
(in gaming) to deliberately cause one's own in-game character to die, typically to respawn, reset a situation, or escape a disadvantageous position.

Examples

To suicide a character
To escape the trap, he suicided his character and respawned at the safe zone.
The team agreed to suicide in practice to test their respawn strategy before the tournament.
After getting stuck under the map, she had to suicide to return to the battlefield quickly.
