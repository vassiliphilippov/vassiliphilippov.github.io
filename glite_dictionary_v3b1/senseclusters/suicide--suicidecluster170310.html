suicide

verb
deliberately kill own game character
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɪsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪsaɪd / (BrE)
(in gaming) to deliberately cause one's own in-game character to die, typically to respawn, reset a situation, or escape a disadvantageous position.
