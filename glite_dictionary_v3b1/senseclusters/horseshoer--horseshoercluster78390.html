horseshoer

noun
person who shoes horses
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsoʊər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsʃuːə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to fit horses with horseshoes

Examples

A horseshoer fits horseshoes
As a skilled horseshoer, she traveled around the county serving local horse owners.
Finding an experienced horseshoer was crucial for the health of the racehorses.
The horseshoer visited the stable every month to tend to the animals' hooves.
