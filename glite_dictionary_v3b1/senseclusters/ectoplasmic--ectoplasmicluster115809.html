ectoplasmic

adjective
of outer cell layer
Pronunciation: / ˌɛktəˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛktəˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of ectoplasm, the outer, gel-like layer of cytoplasm in certain cells

Examples

Ectoplasmic cell layer
The ectoplasmic region of the cell helps it maintain its shape and move.
Scientists observed changes in the ectoplasmic layer during the amoeba's movement.
The ectoplasmic material appeared clear and gel-like under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cytoplasmic - relating to the cytoplasm of a cell
  • antonym: endoplasmic - relating to the endoplasm, the inner part of a cell's cytoplasm
