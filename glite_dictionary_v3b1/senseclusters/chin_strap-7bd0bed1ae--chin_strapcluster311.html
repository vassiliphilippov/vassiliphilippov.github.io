chin strap

noun
strap under chin to secure headgear
3.2
Pronunciation: / tʃɪn stræp / (AmE), / tʃɪn stræp / (BrE)
A strap attached to a hat, helmet, or similar item that passes under the chin to hold it in place.

Examples

A chin strap on helmet
Make sure the chin strap on your bike helmet is securely fastened for safety.
The soldier tightened the chin strap on his helmet before heading into the field.
The wind was strong, but her hat stayed on thanks to the chin strap.
