dark blue

adjective
strongly Democratic (US politics)
6.3
Pronunciation: /dɑːrk bluː/ (AmE), /dɑːk bluː/ (BrE)
(politics, US) strongly supportive of the Democratic Party, used to describe voters, districts, states, or areas that reliably vote Democratic.
