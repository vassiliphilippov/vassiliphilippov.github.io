barbarously

adverb
in a cruel or brutal way
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbərəsli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbərəsli / (BrE)
In a cruel, brutal, or uncivilized manner.

Examples

To act barbarously
The ancient city was barbarously destroyed in the invasion.
The law prohibits the barbarously harsh punishment of criminals.
The prisoners were barbarously treated by their captors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelly - in a cruel manner
  • antonym: mercifully - in a merciful manner
