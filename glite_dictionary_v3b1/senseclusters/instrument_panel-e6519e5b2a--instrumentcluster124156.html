Examples

Synonyms

  • control panel
    3.4 1.59/m
    a control panel is a flat electrical surface with switches and meters for machines, while an instrument panel names the vehicle or device panel for monitoring.
  • dashboard
    3.4 2.23/m
    a dashboard sits in a vehicle or aircraft showing gauges and driver controls, while an instrument panel is the general technical panel used in machines and devices.
  • control board
    4.1 0.45/m
    a control board is an insulated electrical panel used inside equipment for wiring and control, while an instrument panel is the visible user panel with gauges and switches.
