dinner

noun
formal or social meal gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
a formal or social gathering where a group of people meet to have an evening meal together

Examples

Company dinner
He met his future business partner at a company dinner last year.
The annual charity dinner raised thousands for the hospital.
We were invited to a formal dinner at the ambassador’s residence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a large and elaborate formal meal for many people
  • synonym: reception - a formal social occasion, often with a meal or refreshments
