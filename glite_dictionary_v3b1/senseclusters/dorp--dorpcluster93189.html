dorp

noun
small village or town
Pronunciation: / dɔrp / (AmE), / dɔːp / (BrE)
a small town or village, especially in South Africa or southern Africa

Examples

A small dorp
They grew up in a quiet dorp far from the city.
The travelers stopped for lunch in a small dorp along the dusty road.
Life in the dorp was peaceful and everyone knew each other.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city - a large and important town
  • synonym: town - a place where people live that is larger than a village but smaller than a city
  • synonym: village - a small group of houses in the countryside, smaller than a town
