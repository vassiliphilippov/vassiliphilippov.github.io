Brahmanic

adjective
of Brahman in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / brɑːˈmænɪk / (AmE), / brɑːˈmænɪk / (BrE)
relating to Brahman, the ultimate, unchanging reality in Hindu philosophy.

Examples

Brahmanic philosophy
The scholar gave a lecture on Brahmanic traditions in ancient Indian philosophy.
Many rituals in the temple are rooted in Brahmanic beliefs about the nature of reality.
Her research explores the influence of Brahmanic thought on modern spiritual practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hindu - relating to Hinduism or its traditions
  • synonym: Vedic - relating to the Vedas or ancient Hindu scriptures
