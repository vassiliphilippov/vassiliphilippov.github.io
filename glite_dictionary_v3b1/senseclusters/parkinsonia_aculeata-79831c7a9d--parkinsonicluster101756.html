Parkinsonia aculeata

noun
spiny tropical shrub or tree with yellow flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑrkɪnˈsoʊniə ˌækjuˈleɪtə / (AmE), / ˌpɑːkɪnˈsəʊnɪə ˌækjuːˈleɪtə / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree with sharp spines, pinnate leaves, and yellow-orange flowers, grown as an ornamental or hedge, and sometimes used as emergency livestock food.
