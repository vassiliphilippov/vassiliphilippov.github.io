showy orchis

noun
North American orchid with purple and white flowers
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ.i ˈɔr.kɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈɔː.kɪs / (BrE)
a North American orchid with a spike of violet-purple and white flowers, whose sepals and petals form a hood
