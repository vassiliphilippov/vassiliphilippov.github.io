tsp

noun
abbreviation for teaspoon
Pronunciation: / ti ɛs pi / (AmE), / tiː ɛs piː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for teaspoon, a small spoon used for stirring or measuring ingredients

Examples

A tsp of sugar
The recipe calls for one tsp of salt and two of sugar.
Please add half a tsp of vanilla extract to the mixture.
He measured the medicine carefully using a tsp from the kitchen drawer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tbsp - an abbreviation for tablespoon, a larger spoon used for serving or measuring
  • synonym: teaspoon - a small spoon used for stirring or measuring
