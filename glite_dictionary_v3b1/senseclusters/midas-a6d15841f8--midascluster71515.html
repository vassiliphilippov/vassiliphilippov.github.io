Midas

proper noun
mythical king with golden touch
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪdəs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪdəs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a king of Phrygia who was given the power to turn everything he touched into gold, often associated with great wealth or greed.
