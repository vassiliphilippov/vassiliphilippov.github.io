need-to-know

adjective
restricted information; for those who require it
5.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /niːd tə noʊ/ /; / /niːd tuː noʊ/ (AmE), /niːd tə nəʊ/ /; / /niːd tuː nəʊ/ (BrE)
restricted to or intended only for those who require certain information for a specific purpose, especially for reasons of security or confidentiality.

Examples

A need-to-know basis
Access to these files is strictly need-to-know for security purposes.
The need-to-know policy ensures sensitive data is not widely disseminated.
They only provided need-to-know details about the project to the staff.
