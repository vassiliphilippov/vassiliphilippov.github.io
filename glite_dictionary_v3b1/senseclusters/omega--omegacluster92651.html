omega

noun
last letter of the Greek alphabet
Pronunciation: / oʊˈmeɪɡə / (AmE), / əʊˈmiːɡə / (BrE)
the last (24th) letter of the Greek alphabet, used symbolically in various contexts

Examples

Greek letter omega
He signed his letter with the Greek omega to symbolize the end of an era.
In biblical terms, Christ is sometimes referred to as the Alpha and the Omega.
The symbol omega is often used in scientific equations to represent angular velocity.
