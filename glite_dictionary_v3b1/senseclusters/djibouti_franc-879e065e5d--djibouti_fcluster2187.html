Djibouti franc

noun
currency unit of Djibouti
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈbuːti fræŋk / (AmE), / dʒɪˈbuːti fræŋk / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of Djibouti

Examples

The Djibouti franc
Businesses in Djibouti list their prices in both US dollars and Djibouti francs.
The Djibouti franc has been stable against the US dollar for many years.
Tourists can exchange US dollars for Djibouti francs at the airport upon arrival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: franc - a basic monetary unit in several countries, especially France before the euro
