strafe

verb
move sideways in video games
Pronunciation: / streɪf / (AmE), / streɪf / (BrE)
(in video games) to move quickly sideways (left or right), often to dodge attacks or to reposition while aiming.

Examples

To strafe sideways
During the match he learned to strafe left and right to avoid the enemy sniper's aim.
She strafed behind the cover while shooting, which kept her alive until reinforcements arrived.
The coach taught new players how to use strafing to control recoil and make themselves harder targets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dodge - to move quickly to avoid being hit or seen
  • synonym: sidestep - to step quickly to one side to avoid something
