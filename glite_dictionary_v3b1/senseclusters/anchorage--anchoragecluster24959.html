anchorage

noun
place where vessels anchor
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋ.kɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.kər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a place where ships or boats can anchor safely

Examples

Safe anchorage spot
The captain searched the coastline for a safe anchorage before nightfall.
The harbor was crowded, so we had to find an alternative anchorage.
We dropped anchor at a sheltered anchorage near the island.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harbor - a sheltered body of water where ships can anchor
  • synonym: mooring - a place where a boat or ship can be moored
