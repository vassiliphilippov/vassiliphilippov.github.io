chirper

noun
alert sound device
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɜːr.pər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɜː.pə/ (BrE)
a small device or component that emits short, sharp chirping sounds as a signal or alert, such as for a low battery

Examples

A smoke alarm chirper
The smoke detector's chirper kept beeping until we replaced the battery.
A small chirper in the device alerts users when maintenance is needed.
He was annoyed by the constant sound from the chirper in the hallway.
