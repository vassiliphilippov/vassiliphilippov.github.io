demimondaine

noun
female courtesan or prostitute
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmiˌmɒnˈdeɪn / (AmE), / ˌdɛmiˌmɒnˈdeɪn / (BrE)
a woman, especially in the 19th and early 20th centuries, who belonged to a class on the fringes of respectable society, often as a courtesan or prostitute.

Examples

A demimondaine woman
Rumors circulated in the city about the mysterious demimondaine seen at the opera.
She lived as a demimondaine in Paris, mingling with artists and aristocrats alike.
The novel tells the tragic story of a young woman who becomes a demimondaine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtesan - a mistress, especially one associated with wealthy or powerful men
  • synonym: prostitute - a woman who engages in sexual activity for payment
