plum sauce

noun
Chinese sweet sauce made from plums
1.6 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / pl åm s…îs / (AmE), / pl åm s…îÀês / (BrE)
A sweet and tangy sauce made from plums, sugar, vinegar, and spices, commonly served with Chinese dishes.

Examples
