faux queen

noun
woman falsely claiming to be a queen
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / fo ä ÀàkwiÀên / (AmE), / f…ô ä ÀàkwiÀên / (BrE)
A woman who pretends to be or is regarded as a queen without having a legitimate title or royal status.

Examples

A faux queen pretends
Many saw her as a faux queen, more interested in pageantry than leadership.
She was crowned the faux queen at the costume gala, wearing a dazzling tiara.
The tabloids called her a faux queen after she claimed royal descent.
