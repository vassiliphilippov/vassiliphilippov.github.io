graphomania

noun
compulsive writing urge
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræfoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌɡræfəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
a compulsive urge to write excessively, often without a clear purpose or need

Examples

Graphomania disorder
His graphomania filled dozens of notebooks with stories, poems, and random thoughts.
Some people with graphomania write constantly, even when they have nothing particular to say.
The psychologist diagnosed her excessive journaling as a mild form of graphomania.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypergraphia - an overwhelming urge to write, often associated with certain medical conditions
  • antonym: writer's block - an inability to write or produce new work
