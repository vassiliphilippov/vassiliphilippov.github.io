exotically

adverb
in an unusual way
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑːtɪkli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is unusual, striking, or suggestive of a foreign place or culture

Examples

Decorated exotically
The restaurant was decorated exotically, with vibrant colors and unique artifacts from around the world.
She danced exotically, captivating the audience with her mysterious movements.
The perfume smelled exotically of rare spices and tropical flowers.
The restaurant was decorated exotically with bamboo furniture and vibrant fabrics.
She dressed exotically for the party, wearing bright colors and unique jewelry.
The garden was planted exotically, featuring rare plants from distant countries.
