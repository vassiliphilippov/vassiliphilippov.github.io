pilled

adjective
fabric with fuzz balls
7.0
Pronunciation: /pɪld/ (AmE), /pɪld/ (BrE)
covered with small, fuzzy balls of tangled fibers formed on the surface of fabric by wear or friction

Examples

Pilled sweater
After several washes, the sweater became noticeably pilled and lost its original softness.
You can use a fabric shaver to remove the pilled areas from your favorite clothes.
The old couch was so pilled that it looked much older than it really was.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bobbling - having small balls of fiber on fabric due to wear
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, unblemished surface
