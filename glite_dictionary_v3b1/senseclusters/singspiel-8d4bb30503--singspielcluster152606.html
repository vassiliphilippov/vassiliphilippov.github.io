Singspiel

noun
German light opera
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪŋʃpiːl / (AmE), / ˈzɪŋʃpiːl / (BrE)
a form of German light opera that combines spoken dialogue with songs and musical numbers

Examples

A German Singspiel
Mozart's 'The Magic Flute' is a well-known Singspiel that features both spoken and sung parts.
The theater staged a Singspiel to introduce audiences to German musical traditions.
Many students enjoyed performing in the school's annual Singspiel because of its lively songs and dialogue.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: opera - a dramatic work set to music, usually entirely sung
  • synonym: operetta - a short, light opera with spoken dialogue
