stargate

noun
fictional travel portal
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌɡeɪt / (BrE)
a fictional portal or device that enables instant travel between distant locations, especially in science fiction stories

Examples

The stargate portal
The explorers activated the stargate to begin their journey to a distant galaxy.
In the movie, a mysterious stargate connects Earth to another planet.
Scientists in the story theorized that the stargate could link multiple worlds together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portal - a doorway or entrance, especially to another world in fiction
  • synonym: wormhole - a hypothetical tunnel in space-time connecting distant points
