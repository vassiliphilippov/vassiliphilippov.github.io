centeredness

noun
being at the center
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntədnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being placed or positioned at the center of something, especially in space or design

Examples

Visual centeredness
The centeredness of the sculpture made it the focal point of the entire room.
Good graphic design often relies on the centeredness of key elements to create balance.
She adjusted the painting to achieve perfect centeredness on the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrality - the quality of being central or in the center
  • antonym: off-centeredness - the state of not being at the center
