kneadable

adjective
able to be shaped
4.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàniÀêd…ôb…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàniÀêd…ôb…ôl / (BrE)
able to be shaped or worked by pressing, folding, or squeezing with the hands

Examples

Kneadable clay
The clay must be kneadable before you start sculpting your project.
She checked if the bread dough was still kneadable after resting for an hour.
A good eraser for artists is soft and kneadable for shaping.

Synonyms

  • moldable
    4.1 0.16/m
    moldable is easy to shape when soft, especially by pressing or forming, while kneadable is worked mainly by hand.
  • pliable
    4.2 0.73/m
    pliable bends or folds easily and often means adaptable, while kneadable is shaped by pressing, folding, or squeezing by hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malleable - able to be easily shaped or molded
  • synonym: pliable - easily bent or shaped
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed in shape
