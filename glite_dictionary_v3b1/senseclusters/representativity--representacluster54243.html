representativity

noun
quality of being representative
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntəˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of accurately reflecting or representing a larger group or population

Examples

The representativity of data
Researchers questioned the representativity of the data collected from only one city.
Ensuring the representativity of participants is essential for drawing valid conclusions.
The committee discussed how to improve the representativity of the survey sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bias - a tendency to favor one group or outcome over others, leading to unrepresentative results
  • synonym: representativeness - the degree to which something reflects a group or population
