boomerang

noun
curved throwing stick that returns to thrower
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːməˌræŋ / (AmE), / ˈbuːməræŋ / (BrE)
A curved, flat piece of wood or other material that, when thrown correctly, returns to the thrower; traditionally used by Indigenous Australians for hunting or sport.

Examples

Throw a boomerang
After practicing for weeks, she finally managed to make her boomerang return.
He skillfully threw the boomerang across the field and watched it curve back toward him.
The museum displayed an ancient Aboriginal boomerang among other traditional tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: throwing stick - a stick designed to be thrown as a weapon or for sport
