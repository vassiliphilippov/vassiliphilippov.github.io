flat tip screwdriver

noun
screwdriver with a flat, wedge-shaped tip
6.8 2.7
Pronunciation: /flæt tɪp ˈskruː.draɪ.vər/ (AmE), /flæt tɪp ˈskruː.draɪ.və/ (BrE)
