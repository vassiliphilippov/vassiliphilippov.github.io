soldierfish

noun
a large red marine fish, type of squirrelfish
5.1 9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊl.dʒɚˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl.dʒəˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A type of brightly colored marine fish in the family Holocentridae, typically reddish and larger than other squirrelfishes.
