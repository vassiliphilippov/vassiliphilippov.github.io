secondment

noun
temporary transfer to another position
8.0 6.6
Pronunciation: / səˈkɑndmənt / (AmE), / sɪˈkɒndmənt / (BrE)
the temporary transfer of an employee to another job, department, or organization, usually for a set period

Examples

A secondment period
After his secondment at the university, he returned with new skills and insights.
She accepted a secondment to the company's New York office for six months.
The manager arranged a secondment to another department to broaden her experience.
