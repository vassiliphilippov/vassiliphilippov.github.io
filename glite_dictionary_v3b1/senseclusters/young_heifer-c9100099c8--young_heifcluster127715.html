young heifer

noun
young female cow
5.3
Pronunciation: / jʌŋ ˈhɛfər / (AmE), / jʌŋ ˈhɛfə / (BrE)
A young female cow that has not yet borne a calf.

Examples

A young heifer calf
A young heifer requires special care and feeding during its first year of growth.
The farmer purchased a young heifer to add to his herd for breeding purposes.
Veterinarians examined the young heifer to ensure she was healthy before the livestock auction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heifer - a young female cow before she has had a calf
