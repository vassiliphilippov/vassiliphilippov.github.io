trip

noun
an accidental stumble or misstep
2.6
Pronunciation: / trɪp / (AmE), / trɪp / (BrE)
an accidental misstep that may cause someone to stumble or fall

Examples

A trip on stairs
His trip over the curb almost resulted in a fall.
She managed to recover her balance after a slight trip on the sidewalk.
The wet floor caused a sudden trip near the entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slip - an act of sliding unintentionally for a short distance
  • synonym: stumble - to trip or momentarily lose one's balance while walking or running
