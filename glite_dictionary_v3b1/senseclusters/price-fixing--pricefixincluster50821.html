price-fixing

noun
agreement among sellers to fix prices
1.2 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpraɪs.fɪk.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpraɪs ˈfɪk.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpraɪs.fɪks.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpraɪs.fɪk.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpraɪs ˈfɪk.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of competing sellers working together (directly or through algorithms) to keep prices at a certain level, often to limit competition
