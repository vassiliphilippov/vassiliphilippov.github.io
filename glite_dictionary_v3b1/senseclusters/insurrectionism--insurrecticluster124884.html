insurrectionism

noun
advocacy of revolt against authority
9.9 9.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.sə.ˈrek.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ɪn.sə.ˈrek.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)

Examples

Insurrectionism against government
