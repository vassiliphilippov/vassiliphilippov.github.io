multiwall

noun
layered sheet or bag
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌwɔl / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌwɔːl / (BrE)
a manufactured sheet or bag made with two or more layers, often used for packaging, insulation, or glazing

Examples

A multiwall sheet
The greenhouse was built using multiwall panels to provide better insulation.
They chose a multiwall bag for shipping to protect the contents from moisture.
Clear multiwall sheets are often used in construction for their strength and light weight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laminate - a material made by bonding layers together
  • antonym: single-wall - a sheet or bag made with only one layer
