whip

noun
quick blow with a whip
2.9
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
a quick, sharp blow delivered with a whip or a whip-like object

Examples

A whip mark
He flinched as the whip left a red mark on his skin.
She felt the sting of the whip on her bare arm.
The jockey gave the horse a gentle whip to urge it forward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lash - a sharp blow, especially with a whip
  • synonym: strike - a blow made by striking
