cosmonaut

noun
Russian or Soviet astronaut
8.1 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːz.mə.nɔːt/ /; / /ˈkɑːz.mə.nɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈkɒz.mə.nɔːt/ (BrE)
an astronaut from Russia or the former Soviet Union; a person trained and certified to travel in space, particularly as part of the Russian or Soviet space programs.

Examples

A cosmonaut in space
Many young Russians dream of becoming a cosmonaut one day.
The Russian cosmonaut completed a record-breaking spacewalk outside the International Space Station.
Yuri Gagarin was the first cosmonaut to journey into outer space.
