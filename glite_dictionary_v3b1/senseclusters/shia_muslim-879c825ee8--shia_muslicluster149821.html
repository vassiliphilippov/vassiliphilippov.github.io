Shia Muslim

noun
member of the Shia branch of Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːə ˈmʊzlɪm / (AmE), / ˈʃiːə ˈmʊzlɪm / (BrE)
a member of the branch of Islam that believes Ali was the rightful successor to Muhammad and does not accept the first three caliphs

Examples

Shia Muslim community
As a Shia Muslim, she participated in the rituals of Ashura each year.
He was a devout Shia Muslim and observed all the holy days of his community.
The region has a large population of Shia Muslims who celebrate different religious festivals.
