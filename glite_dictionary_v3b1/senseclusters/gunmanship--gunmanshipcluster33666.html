gunmanship

noun
skill with firearms
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnmənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill or expertise in handling and shooting firearms accurately and safely

Examples

Good gunmanship skills
His gunmanship impressed everyone at the shooting competition.
The officer's gunmanship was praised during the training session.
She practiced daily to improve her gunmanship before the big event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inaccuracy - lack of skill or precision in shooting
  • synonym: marksmanship - skill in shooting accurately at a target
