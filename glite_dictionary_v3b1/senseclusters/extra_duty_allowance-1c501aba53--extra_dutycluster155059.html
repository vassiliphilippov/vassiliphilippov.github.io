extra duty allowance

noun
payment for extra work duties
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.strə ˈduː.t̬i əˈlaʊ.əns / (AmE), / ˈɛk.strə ˈdjuː.ti əˈlaʊ.əns / (BrE)
an additional payment made to employees who undertake special assignments or perform duties beyond their normal responsibilities

Examples

An extra duty allowance payment
Employees assigned to cover night shifts received an extra duty allowance for their additional responsibilities.
She applied for an extra duty allowance after taking on several special projects last month.
The school introduced an extra duty allowance for teachers supervising after-school activities.
