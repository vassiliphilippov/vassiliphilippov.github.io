compositing

noun
combining materials
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑːzɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒzɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of combining two or more materials to create a composite material with new physical properties, often used in manufacturing or engineering

Examples

Compositing materials
Modern aircraft rely on compositing to produce lightweight yet strong structural components.
Advances in compositing have led to new materials with improved durability and flexibility.
The engineer explained how compositing different fibers could enhance the product's performance.
