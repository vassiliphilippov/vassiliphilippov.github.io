reraise

verb
to mention again
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈreɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈreɪz / (BrE)
to bring up, mention, or raise something again, such as a topic, question, or issue.

Examples

To reraise a question
If no one answers, you may need to reraise your concerns at the next meeting.
He decided to reraise the issue after it was overlooked the first time.
During the discussion, she reraised the question to ensure it was addressed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something
  • synonym: raise - to bring up a topic or issue for discussion
  • synonym: repeat - to say or do something again
