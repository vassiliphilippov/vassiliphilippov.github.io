spill-proof

adjective
secure against disclosure
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪlˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈspɪlˌpruːf / (BrE)
not likely to reveal secrets or confidential information; secure against accidental disclosure

Examples

Spill-proof friend
You can trust her with anything because she is completely spill-proof.
The manager needs to be spill-proof when handling sensitive company information.
He proved himself spill-proof by never sharing confidential details with anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discreet - careful not to reveal private or sensitive information
  • antonym: gossipy - inclined to reveal secrets or talk about others
  • synonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
