jetway

noun
airport boarding bridge
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛtˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛtˌweɪ / (BrE)
a movable enclosed bridge that connects an airport terminal to an airplane for boarding or disembarking

Examples

Airport jetway
The passengers walked down the jetway to board the plane on time.
After landing, we exited the aircraft directly into the terminal via a jetway.
A maintenance crew inspected the jetway before the next flight arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airbridge - a movable bridge at an airport for boarding or leaving an aircraft
  • synonym: boarding gate - the area where passengers wait to board an aircraft
