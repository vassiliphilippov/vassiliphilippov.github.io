reproductively

adverb
in relation to reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the ability to produce offspring or reproduction

Examples

Reproductively active animals
The animals are reproductively mature by the age of two years.
Some plants are reproductively active only during certain seasons.
The two populations remain reproductively isolated despite living in the same area.
