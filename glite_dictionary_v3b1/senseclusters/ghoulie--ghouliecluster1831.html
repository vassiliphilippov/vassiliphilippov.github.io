ghoulie

noun
playful small monster
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːli / (AmE), / ˈɡuːli / (BrE)
a playful or informal term for a small monster, ghost, or spooky creature, especially used with children

Examples

A little ghoulie under the bed
The children giggled as they pretended to be ghoulies hiding under the bed.
At the party, everyone wore costumes of witches, vampires, and ghoulies.
She decorated the classroom with paper ghoulies for Halloween.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often imagined as appearing to the living
  • synonym: monster - an imaginary creature that is large, ugly, and frightening
