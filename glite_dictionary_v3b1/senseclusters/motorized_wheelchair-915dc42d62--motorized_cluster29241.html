motorized wheelchair

noun
powered wheelchair with a motor
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtəˌraɪzd ˈwiːlˌtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌraɪzd ˈwiːltʃeə / (BrE)
a wheelchair fitted with a motor to be propelled electrically rather than by manual effort

Examples

His new motorized wheelchair allows him to navigate the city streets with ease.
She regained her independence after receiving a motorized wheelchair from the charity.
The hospital provides motorized wheelchairs for patients with limited mobility.
