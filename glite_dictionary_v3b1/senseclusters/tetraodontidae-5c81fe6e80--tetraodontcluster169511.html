Tetraodontidae

noun
family of fish known as puffers or pufferfish
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛtrəoʊˈdɑntɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˌtɛtrəʊˈdɒntɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of chiefly tropical marine fish known as puffers or pufferfish, characterized by their ability to inflate their bodies as a defense mechanism

Examples

The Tetraodontidae family
Many Tetraodontidae species are popular in aquariums despite their poisonous reputation.
Scientists are studying the toxins produced by members of the Tetraodontidae family.
The family Tetraodontidae includes more than 120 species of pufferfish found in warm seas.
