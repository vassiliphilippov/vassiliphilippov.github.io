nonreactive

adjective
not responsive
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnriˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnriˈæktɪv / (BrE)
not showing a response or change when exposed to another substance or stimulus

Examples

Nonreactive metal pan
Stainless steel is nonreactive with most foods, making it ideal for cooking.
The doctor explained that the test result was nonreactive, indicating no infection.
Glass is often chosen in laboratories because it is nonreactive with many chemicals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inert - not chemically reactive
  • antonym: reactive - showing a response or change when exposed to something
