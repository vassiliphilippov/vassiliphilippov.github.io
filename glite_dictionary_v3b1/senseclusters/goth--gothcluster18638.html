goth

noun
member of goth subculture
7.5
Pronunciation: /gɑːθ/ (AmE), /gɒθ/ (BrE)
a person who belongs to a subculture known for dark clothing, dramatic makeup, and an interest in gothic music and style

Examples

A goth style
She became a goth in high school and started listening to gothic rock bands.
Many goths express themselves through unique fashion and artistic makeup.
At the festival, a group of goths gathered to celebrate their favorite music.
She dressed as a goth for the concert, wearing black clothes and dramatic makeup.
Many goths enjoy listening to bands with a dark, atmospheric sound.
He became interested in the goth scene after attending a local club night.
