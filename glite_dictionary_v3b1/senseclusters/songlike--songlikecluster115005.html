songlike

adjective
having the qualities of a song
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a melody or qualities resembling a song, as opposed to speech or recitative

Examples

Songlike voice
Her songlike voice captivated everyone in the audience.
Many found his speech unusually songlike, almost as if he were singing his words.
The bird's songlike calls echoed through the forest at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melodic - having or characterized by melody
  • antonym: recitative - a style of delivery resembling speech, used in opera and oratorio
