epigraphical

adjective
about inscriptions
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to inscriptions or the study of inscriptions, especially those carved on monuments or buildings

Examples

Epigraphical inscription
The archaeologist published an epigraphical analysis of the ancient temple's stone carvings.
Her research focuses on epigraphical evidence from early Mediterranean civilizations.
Many epigraphical records have helped historians understand lost languages.
