Zn

noun
symbol for the element zinc (metal)
Pronunciation: / ziː ˈɛn / (AmE), / ziː ˈɛn / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for the metallic element zinc, a bluish-white metal used in alloys and in protecting iron from rusting

Examples
