plumb

noun
metal weight on a plumb line
7.4 0.84/m
Pronunciation: / plÊŒm / (AmE), / plÊŒm / (BrE)
a small, heavy metal weight attached to the end of a plumb line, used to determine vertical alignment

Examples

A plumb weight
A carpenter relies on a plumb to mark true vertical lines on the structure.
Please replace the old plumb at the end of the line before you start measuring.
The mason dropped the plumb to check if the wall was perfectly vertical.

Synonyms

  • plumb bob
    7.0 0.03/m

  • plummet
    7.5 0.17/m
    plummet is an older or less common word for a small metal weight used on a line, while plumb is the modern, everyday name.
