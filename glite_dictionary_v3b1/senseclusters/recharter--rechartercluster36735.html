recharter

noun
renewal of charter
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈtʃɑːrtər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtʃɑːtə / (BrE)
the act or process of granting, renewing, or extending an official charter to an organization or entity

Examples

Annual recharter process
The board approved the recharter of the local scout troop for another year.
After much debate, the committee voted in favor of the recharter of the historic society.
The university's recharter was celebrated with a special ceremony attended by alumni.

Related Terms

  • synonym: renewal - the act of extending the period of validity of something
  • antonym: revocation - the act of officially cancelling something
