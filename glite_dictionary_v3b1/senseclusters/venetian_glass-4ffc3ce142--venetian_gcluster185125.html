Venetian glass

noun
decorative glassware from Venice
6.5
Pronunciation: / vəˈniʃən ɡlæs / (AmE), / vɪˈniːʃən ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A type of fine and often decorative glassware made in or near Venice, especially on the island of Murano, known for its craftsmanship and artistic designs.

Examples

Venetian glass art
Collectors prize Venetian glass for its vibrant colors and intricate patterns.
She brought home a delicate Venetian glass vase as a souvenir from Italy.
The museum displayed exquisite pieces of Venetian glass from the 16th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Murano glass - glassware made on the island of Murano, part of Venice, famous for its quality and techniques
