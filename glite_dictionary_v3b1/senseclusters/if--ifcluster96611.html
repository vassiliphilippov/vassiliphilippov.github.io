if

conjunction
introducing indirect question
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪf / (AmE), / ɪf / (BrE)
used to introduce an indirect yes/no question, meaning 'whether' in reported or embedded speech

Examples

If he comes
He wondered if his friends would remember his birthday this year.
Can you tell me if the meeting has been rescheduled?
They asked if anyone had seen their lost dog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whether - used to introduce alternatives or indirect questions
