sixties

noun
the years of age from 60 to 70
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪk.stiz / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.stiːz / (BrE)
the period in a person's life between the ages of 60 and 70

Examples

In your sixties
Many people choose to retire in their sixties and pursue hobbies they never had time for before.
Regular exercise can help you stay healthy throughout your sixties and beyond.
She began painting seriously in her sixties, proving it's never too late to start something new.
