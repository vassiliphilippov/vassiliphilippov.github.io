huckster

verb
to bargain or wrangle over price or terms
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌkstɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌkstə / (BrE)
to bargain or wrangle, especially over the price or terms of a sale or agreement

Examples

To huckster over price
