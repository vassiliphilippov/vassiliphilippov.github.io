Horatian

noun
follower of Horace
9.9
Pronunciation: /hə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /hɒ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /hə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

A true Horatian

The literary club attracted several Horatians who appreciated wit and balance in poetry.
