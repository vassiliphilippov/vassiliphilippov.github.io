second power

noun
the square of a number
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ˈpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈsekənd ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
the result of multiplying a number or quantity by itself; the square of a number

Examples

The second power of two
Four is the second power of two, since 2 × 2 equals 4.
In scientific notation, the second power is often written as an exponent of two.
The area of a square is equal to the second power of the length of its side.
