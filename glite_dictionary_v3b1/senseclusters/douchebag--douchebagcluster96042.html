douchebag

noun
slang insult
Pronunciation: / ˈduːʃˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈduːʃˌbæɡ / (BrE)
an offensive slang term for a person who is rude, selfish, or unpleasant

Examples

A rude douchebag
He acted like a total douchebag at the party and upset everyone.
Some people use the word douchebag to insult others they do not like.
Calling someone a douchebag is considered very rude and offensive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asshole - an offensive term for an unpleasant person
  • antonym: gentleman - a polite and respectful man
  • synonym: jerk - a rude or mean person
