neurolinguist

noun
someone who studies brain processes in language
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies how the brain processes language, combining neuroscience and linguistics

Examples

A neurolinguist studies brain
A team of neurolinguists is analyzing language deficits in stroke patients.
After years of medical training, he became a leading neurolinguist at the research institute.
The neurolinguist presented her findings on how the brain processes grammar.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: linguist - a person skilled in languages or language science
  • synonym: neurolinguistics - the study of how the brain enables language
  • degree lower: neuroscientist - a scientist who studies the nervous system
