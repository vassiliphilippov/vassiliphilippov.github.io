rocketeer

noun
rocket operator
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑːkəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌrɒkəˈtɪə /; / ˌrɒkɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who designs, builds, or operates rockets

Examples

A rocketeer builds rockets
The young rocketeer presented his homemade rocket at the science fair.
Many rocketeers work for private companies developing new space technologies.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a rocketeer and traveling to Mars.
The young rocketeer dreamed of sending rockets to Mars one day.
Many rocketeers gathered at the annual space technology conference.
As a skilled rocketeer, she helped design the new launch system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronaut - a person trained to travel and work in space
  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs and builds machines or structures
