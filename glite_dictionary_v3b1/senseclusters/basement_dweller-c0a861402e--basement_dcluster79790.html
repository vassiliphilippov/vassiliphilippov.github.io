basement dweller

noun
person who spends much time indoors, often isolated
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsmənt ˈdwɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsmənt ˈdwɛlə / (BrE)
a person, often socially withdrawn, who spends a great deal of time indoors, especially in a basement, usually engaging in solitary activities such as gaming or internet use

Examples

A basement dweller gamer
After months of working remotely, she started to feel like a basement dweller.
His friends joked that he was a basement dweller because he preferred online games to socializing.
The stereotype of a basement dweller doesn't reflect everyone's real-life circumstances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shut-in - a person who rarely leaves home because of illness or emotional reasons
