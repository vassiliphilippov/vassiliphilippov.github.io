crybaby

noun
person who cries or complains excessively
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who cries or complains too easily or too often; someone who is overly sensitive

Examples

A crybaby child
His classmates called him a crybaby whenever he lost a game.
Nobody likes a crybaby who complains about everything.
Stop being such a crybaby and deal with the situation.
Don’t listen to the crybaby complaints; most people liked the decision.
His classmates called him a crybaby whenever he complained to the teacher.
Stop being such a crybaby and try to solve the problem calmly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complainer - a person who often complains
  • antonym: stoic - a person who does not show or complain about pain or hardship
  • synonym: whiner - a person who complains in an annoying way
