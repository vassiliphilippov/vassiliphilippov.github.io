crybaby

noun
person who cries or complains excessively
4.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkraɪ.beɪ.biː/ /; / /ˈkraɪ.beɪ.bɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkraɪ.beɪ.biː/ /; / /ˈkraɪ.beɪ.bɪ/ (BrE)
