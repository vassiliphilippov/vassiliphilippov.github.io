presentability

noun
neat or suitable appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzəntəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌprɛzəntəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being neat, tidy, or suitable for being seen in public

Examples

Presentability for an interview
He checked his presentability in the mirror before the interview.
Good presentability is important when meeting new clients for the first time.
Her presentability made a strong impression at the formal event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neatness - the quality of being clean and orderly
  • antonym: untidiness - the state of being messy or not neat
