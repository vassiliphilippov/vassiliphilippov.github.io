unmined

adjective
not extracted by mining
4.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
(of substances such as minerals or resources) not yet extracted from the earth by mining.

Examples

The unmined land
Geologists surveyed the unmined regions for valuable ores.
Many unmined coal seams remain untouched due to environmental regulations.
The country has vast reserves of unmined gold beneath its mountains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mined - extracted from the earth by mining
