cor

interjection
surprise exclamation
Pronunciation: / kɔː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, admiration, or mild shock, mainly in informal British English

Examples

Cor! That's amazing
When he saw the size of the cake, he exclaimed, 'cor! That’s enormous!'
‘Cor! I never thought I’d see the day,’ she said, shaking her head in disbelief.
The children shouted 'cor!' as the magician pulled a rabbit from his hat.
