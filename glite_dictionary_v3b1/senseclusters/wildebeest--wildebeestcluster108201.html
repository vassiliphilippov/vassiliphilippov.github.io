wildebeest

noun
large African antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪl.dəˌbist / (AmE), / ˈwɪl.dɪˌbiːst / (BrE)
a large African antelope with a head and horns resembling those of an ox and a long tufted tail

Examples

A wildebeest animal
A single wildebeest can weigh up to 270 kilograms when fully grown.
During our safari, we saw a massive herd of wildebeest crossing the river.
Every year, wildebeest migrate in search of fresh grass across the Serengeti.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gnu - another common name for the same animal
