Muslim

noun
a follower of Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊzləm, ˈmʌzləm / (AmE), / ˈmʊzlɪm, ˈmʌzlɪm / (BrE)
A person who believes in or follows the religion of Islam.

Examples

Muslims pray daily
Many Muslims around the world observe Ramadan each year.
She became a Muslim after years of personal reflection and study.
The community welcomed its new Muslim neighbors during the holiday celebration.
