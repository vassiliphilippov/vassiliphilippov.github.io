Examples

Synonyms

  • neglect of duty
    5.1 0.05/m
    neglect of duty means failing to carry out your job responsibilities, while dereliction can mean being abandoned or neglecting duties.
  • abandonment
    4.9 1.77/m
    abandonment is leaving someone or something and stopping support, while dereliction also covers both being abandoned and failing duties.
  • desertion
    6.2 1.15/m
    desertion is actively leaving when you should stay, while dereliction can mean either being abandoned or failing duty.
  • negligence
    6.5 4.32/m
    negligence is careless failure to take proper care, while dereliction can mean being abandoned or failing in duties.
  • breach of duty
    6.5 0.09/m
    breach of duty is breaking a legal or moral obligation, while dereliction is neglect or abandonment of duty or place.
  • nonfeasance
    9.9 0.04/m
    nonfeasance is the legal term for not acting when required, while dereliction is broader and can mean abandonment or duty neglect.
