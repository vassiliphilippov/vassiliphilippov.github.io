dereliction

noun
neglect of duty
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛrəˈlɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛrəˈlɪkʃən / (BrE)
the state of being abandoned or the act of neglecting one's duty or responsibility

Examples

Building in dereliction
The old hospital was left in a state of dereliction for decades before it was renovated.
His repeated dereliction of duty led to a formal warning from his supervisor.
The committee investigated the dereliction of safety standards at the construction site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandonment - the act of leaving something or someone completely
  • antonym: diligence - careful and persistent work or effort
  • synonym: neglect - failure to care for something properly
