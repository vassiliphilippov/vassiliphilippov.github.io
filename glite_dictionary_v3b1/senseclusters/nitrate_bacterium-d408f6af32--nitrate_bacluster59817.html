nitrate bacterium

noun
bacterium that converts nitrite to nitrate
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtreɪt ˌbækˈtɪriəm / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtreɪt ˌbækˈtɪəriəm / (BrE)
a bacterium that oxidizes nitrites into nitrates, playing a key role in the nitrogen cycle

Examples

nitrate bacterium in soil
Scientists isolated a new strain of nitrate bacterium from agricultural land.
The soil's health depends in part on the presence of nitrate bacterium in its ecosystem.
Without nitrate bacterium, nitrites would accumulate and disrupt plant growth.
