together

adverb
assembled in one place or group
Pronunciation: / təˈɡɛðər /; / təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
in or into one group, place, or company; with each other; in close proximity

Examples

The family spends every Sunday together at their grandmother's house.

We decided to work together on the group project to finish faster.
Her shoes were so wet that they clung together after drying.
The two magnets stuck together on the fridge door.
We pushed all the tables together to make a larger workspace.
