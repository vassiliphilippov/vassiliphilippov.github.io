Synonyms

  • all together
    1.0 3.08/m
    all together means everyone is in the same place or time, while together can also mean simple closeness or joining.
  • collectively
    3.6 10/m
    collectively is a formal word for acting as a whole, while together is more common in speech and everyday use.
  • as a group
    2.3 6.09/m
    as a group means people act or stand as one unit, while together can mean simply being near or joining informally.
  • side by side
    3.4 1.77/m
    side by side describes being next to each other physically, while together can also mean acting in one organized group.
  • in tandem
    3.5 1.54/m
    in tandem means working closely and in coordination, whereas together can mean simply being near or in the same group.
