quarter

noun
part of a shoe covering the heel
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
the part of a shoe or boot made from leather (or other material) that covers the heel and joins the vamp

Examples

A shoe quarter
A worn-out quarter can make a boot uncomfortable and unstable.
Modern sneakers often have padded quarters for extra heel support.
The shoemaker carefully stitched the quarter onto the rest of the shoe.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: vamp - the front part of a shoe's upper that covers the toes and instep
