ticklishly

adverb
in a sensitive way
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪklɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈtɪklɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that causes a tickling sensation or is easily affected by tickling

Examples

Laughs ticklishly
She giggled ticklishly when her friend touched her side during the game.
The kitten reacted ticklishly to the gentle brush of the feather on its belly.
He smiled ticklishly as the grass tickled his bare feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensitively - in a way that is easily affected or responsive
