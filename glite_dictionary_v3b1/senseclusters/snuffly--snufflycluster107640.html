snuffly

adjective
prone to sniffle; having a blocked nose
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌfli / (AmE), / ˈsnʌfli / (BrE)
Having a blocked or runny nose, often making sniffing noises; prone to sniffle.

Examples

A snuffly nose
Her snuffly voice made it clear she was suffering from allergies again.
I'm feeling snuffly this morning; I hope I’m not coming down with a cold.
The child sounded quite snuffly after playing outside in the cold all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congested - having a blocked nose, often due to a cold or allergies
  • synonym: sniffly - tending to sniffle or having a runny nose
