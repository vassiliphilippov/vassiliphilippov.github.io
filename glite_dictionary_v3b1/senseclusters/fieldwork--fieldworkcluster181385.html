fieldwork

noun
research outside lab
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːldˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈfiːldˌwɜːk / (BrE)
practical research or study carried out in a natural environment rather than in a laboratory or office

Examples

Do fieldwork outdoors
The anthropology students completed their fieldwork in a remote village over the summer.
Accurate data collection during fieldwork is essential for environmental research.
Her fieldwork involved observing animal behavior in their natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: field study - a study conducted outside the laboratory in a real-world setting
  • antonym: laboratory work - research or experiments conducted in a controlled indoor environment
