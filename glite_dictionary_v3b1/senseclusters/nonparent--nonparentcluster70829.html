nonparent

noun
person without children
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpɛrənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpeərənt / (BrE)
a person who does not have any children, either by choice or circumstance

Examples

A nonparent travels
As a nonparent, she often travels during school holidays when others stay home.
Some nonparents volunteer to mentor children in their communities.
The event was open to both parents and nonparents interested in family issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childless - not having any children
  • antonym: parent - a person who has one or more children
