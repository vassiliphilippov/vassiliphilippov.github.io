anxiolytic

adjective
reducing anxiety (medical term)
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋziəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌaŋziəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
(of a drug or other substance) reducing or relieving anxiety.

Examples

Anxiolytic effect
He was interested in studying the anxiolytic properties of the new compound.
Some herbal teas are said to have mild anxiolytic effects on the nervous system.
The doctor prescribed an anxiolytic medication to help manage her panic attacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiogenic - causing anxiety
  • synonym: tranquilizer - drug used to reduce tension or anxiety
