spinal accessory

noun
eleventh cranial nerve in the neck and shoulder
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnəl əkˈsɛsəri / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnəl əkˈsɛsəri / (BrE)
the eleventh cranial nerve, arising from both spinal and cranial roots, responsible for supplying certain muscles in the neck and shoulder

Examples

The spinal accessory nerve
Damage to the spinal accessory may result in weakness of the shoulder muscles.
Injury to the spinal accessory can cause difficulty in turning the head.
The doctor tested the patient's spinal accessory during the neurological exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cranial nerve - one of the 12 paired nerves emerging from the brain
