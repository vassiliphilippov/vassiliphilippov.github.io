Whitmanesque

adjective
like Walt Whitman
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪtmənˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌwɪtmənˈɛsk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of the poetry, style, or themes of Walt Whitman

Examples

Whitmanesque poetry style
The author's Whitmanesque descriptions of the city evoke a sense of wonder and freedom.
Her writing style is often called Whitmanesque because of its long lines and celebration of the individual.
Critics praised the Whitmanesque tone of the collection, noting its focus on nature and democracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: romantic - having qualities of the Romantic literary movement
  • antonym: unwhitmanesque - not resembling the style of Walt Whitman
