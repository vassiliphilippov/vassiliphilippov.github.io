Cephalotus

noun
genus of Australian pitcher plant
Pronunciation: / sɛfəˈloʊtəs / (AmE), / sɛfəˈləʊtəs / (BrE)
A genus of carnivorous plant native to southwestern Australia, commonly known as the Australian pitcher plant.

Examples

The Cephalotus plant
Botanists study the Cephalotus to understand the evolution of carnivory in plants.
The Cephalotus is prized by collectors for its unusual pitcher-shaped traps.
Unlike other pitcher plants, the Cephalotus grows only in a small region of Australia.
