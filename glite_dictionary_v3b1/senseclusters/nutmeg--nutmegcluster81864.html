nutmeg

noun
aromatic seed used as spice
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ /; / ˈnʌtmɛɡ / (BrE)
the hard, aromatic seed of a tropical tree, used as a spice when grated or ground

Examples

Ground nutmeg
Ground nutmeg is a common spice in holiday baking recipes.
She added a pinch of nutmeg to the custard for extra flavor.
The aroma of freshly grated nutmeg filled the kitchen.
Grate a little nutmeg over the top of the custard before serving.
She bought a small nutmeg tree for her tropical garden.
The islands are famous for their production of nutmeg and other exotic spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mace - a spice made from the outer covering of the nutmeg seed
