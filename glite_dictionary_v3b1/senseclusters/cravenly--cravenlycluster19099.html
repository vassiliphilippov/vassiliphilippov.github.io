cravenly

adverb
in a cowardly way
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪvənli / (AmE), / ˈkreɪvənli / (BrE)
in a cowardly or timid manner, showing a lack of courage

Examples

To cravenly refuse
He cravenly refused to stand up for his beliefs during the meeting.
The soldiers cravenly deserted their posts when the enemy approached.
She cravenly avoided confrontation, preferring to stay silent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bravely - in a courageous or bold way
  • synonym: cowardly - in a way that shows lack of courage
