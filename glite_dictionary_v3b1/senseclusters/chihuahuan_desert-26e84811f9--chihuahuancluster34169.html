Chihuahuan Desert

proper noun
desert in northern Mexico and U.S. Southwest
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈwɑwən ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɑːwən ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
A large desert region in northern Mexico and the southwestern United States, covering parts of Texas, New Mexico, and Arizona.

Examples
