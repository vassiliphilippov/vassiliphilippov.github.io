animal toxin

noun
toxin produced by animals
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl ˈtɑksɪn / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈtɒksɪn / (BrE)
a poisonous substance produced by an animal, often resembling bacterial toxins in its effects or properties

Examples

Animal toxin effects
Scientists studied the effects of the animal toxin found in jellyfish venom.
Some animal toxins are being investigated for their potential use in medicine.
The snake's animal toxin can cause paralysis in its prey.
