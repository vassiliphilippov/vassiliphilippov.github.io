the town bicycle

noun
person considered promiscuous (slang)
Pronunciation: / ðə taʊn ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ / (AmE), / ðə taʊn ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a slang, often disparaging term for a person regarded as promiscuous, especially someone whose sexual partners are widely known in a community

Examples

The town bicycle nickname
Ever since she started dating again, some people began to unfairly call her the town bicycle.
He resented the label of the town bicycle that the cruel gossip had given him.
Rumors and judgmental whispers referred to her as the town bicycle after her high school relationships.

Related Terms
