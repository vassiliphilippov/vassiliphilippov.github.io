unstratified

adjective
not arranged in layers
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrætɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrætɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not arranged, deposited, or formed in layers; lacking stratification.

Examples

Unstratified material
The sample contained unstratified material, indicating a lack of sedimentary processes.
The valley was filled with unstratified glacial debris after the ice receded.
Unlike sandstone, which forms layers, till is typically unstratified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stratified - arranged or deposited in layers
