bichir

noun
African freshwater fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃɪə / (BrE)
a long-bodied African freshwater fish with primitive features, belonging to the family Polypteridae

Examples

A bichir fish
The aquarium displayed a rare bichir in its tropical fish section.
A bichir can breathe air thanks to its primitive lungs.
Many hobbyists admire the unique appearance of the bichir in their home tanks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lungfish - a primitive freshwater fish capable of breathing air
  • synonym: polypterus - a genus of African freshwater fish with elongated bodies and lobed fins
