grilling

noun
cooking method using radiant heat
1.0 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgrɪ.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgrɪl.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgrɪ.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgrɪl.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
The method of cooking food by exposing it directly to radiant heat, such as over a fire or beneath a grill.

Examples

To grill meat
Many people enjoy grilling steaks and vegetables outdoors in the summer.
She prefers grilling her fish rather than frying it for a healthier meal.
The recipe recommends grilling the chicken for a smoky flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broiling - cooking by direct exposure to radiant heat, typically from above
