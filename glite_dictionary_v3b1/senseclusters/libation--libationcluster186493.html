libation

noun
act of pouring a drink as an offering
7.4 8.1
Pronunciation: /laɪ.ˈbeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /laɪ.ˈbeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of pouring a liquid, especially wine, as an offering to a god or in a religious ceremony

The priests performed a libation at the altar before the ceremony began.
