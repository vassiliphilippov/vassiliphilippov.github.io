blue point Siamese

noun
Siamese cat with bluish-grey points
9.9
Pronunciation: / bluː pɔɪnt saɪˈæmiz / (AmE), / bluː pɔɪnt saɪˈæmiːz / (BrE)
A Siamese cat variety with a pale bluish-cream body and dark bluish-grey points (ears, face, paws, and tail).

Examples

A blue point Siamese cat
At the cat show, the judge awarded first place to a beautiful blue point Siamese.
Her blue point Siamese has striking icy-blue eyes and elegant grey points.
The breeder specializes in rare blue point Siamese kittens with gentle temperaments.
