consolidationist

noun
supporter of mergers
8.9
Pronunciation: /kən.sɑː.lə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /kən.sɒl.ɪ.ˈdeɪ.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates merging organizations, companies, institutions, or political entities into a single larger unit
