antiparasitic

adjective
against parasites
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌpærəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˌpærəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
describing a substance or treatment that kills or inhibits parasites

Examples

Antiparasitic treatment
The veterinarian recommended an antiparasitic shampoo to treat the dog's fleas.
Farmers often use antiparasitic drugs to protect livestock from infections.
This antiparasitic cream is effective against several types of skin parasites.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthelmintic - a substance that destroys or expels parasitic worms
  • antonym: antifungal - a substance that kills or inhibits fungi
