halal food

noun
food permitted by Islamic law
Pronunciation: / hɑˈlɑl fuːd / (AmE), / həˈlɑːl fuːd / (BrE)
food that is permitted under Islamic law, especially as defined in the Qur'an

Examples

Halal food rules
He asked if the meal was halal food before placing his order.
Many airlines now offer halal food options on their international flights.
The new grocery store features a special section for halal food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kosher food - food that is permitted by Jewish dietary law
