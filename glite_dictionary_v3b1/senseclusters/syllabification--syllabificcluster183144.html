syllabification

noun
division of words into syllables
Pronunciation: / sɪˌlæbəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪˌlæbɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of dividing words into syllables, especially according to established rules

Examples

Correct syllabification rules
English teachers often emphasize correct syllabification when teaching spelling.
She used syllabification to help her students pronounce longer words.
The rules of syllabification can vary between different languages.
