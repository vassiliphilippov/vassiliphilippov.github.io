block

noun
section of a building with separate units
2.0 7.56/m
Pronunciation: / blÉ‘k / (AmE), / blÉ’k / (BrE)
a section of a large building, such as an apartment, school, or office, that is divided into separate rooms, units, or suites

Examples

An apartment block
Each block in the hospital has its own specialized department.
She moved into a new block of apartments downtown.
The science block is located on the east side of the campus.

Synonyms

  • apartment building
    1.2 4.60/m
    apartment building is a whole residential building with many separate flats, while block refers to a section inside a larger building.
  • apartment house
    2.0 0.97/m
    apartment house is an older term for a building with separate homes, while block refers to a part of a larger building.
  • office block
    2.8 0.10/m
    office block is a building that holds many offices for businesses, while block means a section within a larger complex of rooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wing - a part of a large building, often projecting from the main structure
