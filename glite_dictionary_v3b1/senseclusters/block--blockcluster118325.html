block

noun
section of a building with separate units
Pronunciation: / blɑk / (AmE), / blɒk / (BrE)
a section of a large building, such as an apartment, school, or office, that is divided into separate rooms, units, or suites

Examples

An apartment block
Each block in the hospital has its own specialized department.
She moved into a new block of apartments downtown.
The science block is located on the east side of the campus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wing - a part of a large building, often projecting from the main structure
