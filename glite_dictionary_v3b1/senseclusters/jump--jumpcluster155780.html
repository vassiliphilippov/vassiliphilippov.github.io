jump

noun
abrupt transition; sudden change
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an abrupt or sudden transition from one state, condition, or situation to another

Examples

A career jump
Her promotion was a big jump in her career.
Making the jump from amateur to professional can be challenging.
The company's sudden jump into the international market surprised many analysts.
