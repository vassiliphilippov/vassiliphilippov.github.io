Colonus

proper noun
ancient Athenian district
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊnəs / (AmE), / kəˈləʊnəs / (BrE)
an ancient district (deme) near Athens, famous as the setting of Sophocles' play Oedipus at Colonus

Examples

Oedipus at Colonus
The play Oedipus at Colonus is set in this historic district near Athens.
Archaeologists have uncovered ruins in the area once known as Colonus.
Many tourists visit Colonus to explore its connection to Greek tragedy.
