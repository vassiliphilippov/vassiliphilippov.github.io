pigeonhole

noun
an overly simple category
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒənˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒ.ən.həʊl / (BrE)
a category, especially an overly narrow or simplistic one, into which someone or something is placed or classified

Examples

A social pigeonhole
It's unfair to put people into a pigeonhole based on their appearance alone.
The committee refused to assign the proposal to a single pigeonhole and considered it from multiple angles.
Writers often dislike being placed in a particular pigeonhole because it limits creative freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: category - a class or division of people or things regarded as having particular shared characteristics
  • synonym: generalization - a general statement or concept obtained by inference from specific cases
  • synonym: stereotype - a widely held but oversimplified idea of a particular type of person or thing
