prosodically

adverb
relating to speech rhythm
8.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈsɑːdɪkli / (AmE), / prəˈsɒdɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the rhythm, stress, and intonation of speech

Examples

Read prosodically aloud
The poem was read prosodically to highlight its natural rhythm and flow.
Linguists often analyze sentences prosodically to understand patterns of stress and intonation.
She adjusted her speech prosodically to match the melody of the language.
