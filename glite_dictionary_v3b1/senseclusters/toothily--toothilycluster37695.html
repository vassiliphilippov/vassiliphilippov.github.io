toothily

adverb
showing teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːθɪli / (AmE), / ˈtuːθɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or exposes the teeth, especially when smiling or grinning

Examples

Smile toothily after loss
The child smiled toothily after losing his first baby tooth.
She waved toothily at her friends from across the playground.
The dog looked up toothily as it waited for a treat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadly - in a wide or open way, especially referring to a smile
  • antonym: grimly - in a serious or stern way, often without smiling
