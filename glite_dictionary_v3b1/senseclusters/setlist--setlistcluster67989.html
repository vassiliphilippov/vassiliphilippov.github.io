setlist

noun
concert song list
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛtˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɛtˌlɪst / (BrE)
a list of songs or pieces that a band or performer plans to play at a concert or show

Examples

Concert setlist
The band posted their setlist online after the concert for fans to see.
She carefully arranged the setlist to keep the audience excited throughout the show.
Every night, the setlist included a surprise song that was not announced in advance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playlist - a list of songs selected to be played in a particular order
  • synonym: program - a schedule of performances or events
