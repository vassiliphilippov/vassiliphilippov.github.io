regular tetrahedron

noun
tetrahedron with equilateral triangle faces
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr tɛtəˈhɪdrən / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə tɛtəˈhiːdrən / (BrE)
a tetrahedron whose four faces are all equilateral triangles and angles are all equal

Examples

A regular tetrahedron shape
Every face of a regular tetrahedron is an equilateral triangle of the same size.
In geometry class, we constructed a regular tetrahedron using toothpicks and clay.
The molecular shape of methane can be described as a regular tetrahedron.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regular polyhedron - a polyhedron whose faces are congruent regular polygons
  • degree lower: tetrahedron - a solid shape with four triangular faces
