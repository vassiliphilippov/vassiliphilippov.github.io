forwardly

adverb
toward the front
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərdli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwədli / (BrE)

Examples

Move forwardly

She stepped forwardly to address the audience with confidence.
The car moved forwardly after the traffic light turned green.
