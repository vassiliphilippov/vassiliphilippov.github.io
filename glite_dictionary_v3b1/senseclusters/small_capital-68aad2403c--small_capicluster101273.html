small capital

noun
capital-shaped letter sized like a lowercase
7.7 0.00/m

A letter or character that has the shape of a capital letter but is the same height as a lowercase letter, used for emphasis or stylistic effect in printing and typesetting.

Examples

Small capitals in printing
In this book, the author uses small capitals to highlight important terms.
Please convert these section headings to small capitals, not full-size capitals.
The title was printed in small capitals for a classic look.

Synonyms
