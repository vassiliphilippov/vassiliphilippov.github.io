antisymmetric

adjective
matrix property

Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌsɪˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌsɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
describing a square matrix that is equal to the negative of its transpose, so all diagonal entries are zero

Examples
