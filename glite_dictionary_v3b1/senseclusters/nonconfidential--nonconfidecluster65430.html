nonconfidential

adjective
not secret
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkɑnˈfɪdənʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkɒnˈfɪdənʃəl / (BrE)
not intended to be kept secret or private; open to general access or knowledge

Examples

Nonconfidential information
The company released a nonconfidential summary of the findings to the public.
All nonconfidential documents can be accessed by any employee without special permission.
He made sure the information he shared was strictly nonconfidential before sending the email.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidential - meant to be kept secret or private
  • synonym: public - open or accessible to everyone
