headlock

noun
a wrestling hold locking the head
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌlɒk / (BrE)
a wrestling hold in which an opponent's head is locked tightly under the attacker's arm and against their body

Examples

A headlock hold
During the match, he used a powerful headlock to gain the upper hand.
She broke free from the headlock and quickly countered with a move of her own.
The wrestler caught his opponent in a tight headlock and refused to let go.
