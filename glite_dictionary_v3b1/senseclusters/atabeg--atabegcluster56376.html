atabeg

noun
guardian of a prince
Pronunciation: / ˈætəˌbɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈætəˌbɛɡ / (BrE)
a noble appointed as the guardian, tutor, or regent of a young prince, often ruling on the prince's behalf

Examples

Prince's atabeg
The young prince was guided by his trusted atabeg during his early years on the throne.
In medieval times, an atabeg often held significant political power in the kingdom.
The sultan appointed an atabeg to oversee the education and protection of his heir.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regent - a person appointed to govern in place of a monarch
  • synonym: tutor - a person who teaches or supervises a young person
