bibliomaniac

noun
book lover
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniæk / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniæk / (BrE)
a person who is extremely passionate about books, especially reading or collecting them

Examples

A bibliomaniac collector
As a dedicated bibliomaniac, he spent every weekend browsing secondhand bookstores.
The bibliomaniac in our class always has a new novel to recommend.
Her friends joked that she was a bibliomaniac because she read three books a week.
The bibliomaniac spent hours searching for rare first editions at the book fair.
Her friends joked that she was a true bibliomaniac because of her overflowing shelves.
As a bibliomaniac, he could not resist buying every interesting book he found.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bibliophile - a person who loves or collects books
  • synonym: bookworm - a person who enjoys reading books very much
