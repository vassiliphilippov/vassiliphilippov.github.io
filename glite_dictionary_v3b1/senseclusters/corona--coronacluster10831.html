corona

noun
a type of long cigar with blunt ends
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊnə / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnə / (BrE)
a long, straight cigar with rounded or blunt ends, usually measuring about 5.5 to 6 inches in length and of medium thickness.

Examples

Corona cigar
A classic corona offers a balanced smoking experience for aficionados.
He selected a corona from the humidor to enjoy after dinner.
The shop specializes in premium corona cigars imported from Cuba.
