cinder pig

noun
pig iron with much slag
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndɚ pɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɪndə pɪɡ / (BrE)
Pig iron that contains a substantial amount of slag, making it of lower quality for steelmaking or casting purposes.

Examples

cinder pig iron
Due to the high slag content, cinder pig is less desirable for producing quality steel.
In older foundries, workers often encountered cinder pig during the initial smelting process.
The shipment included several cinder pigs that had to be separated out before refining.
