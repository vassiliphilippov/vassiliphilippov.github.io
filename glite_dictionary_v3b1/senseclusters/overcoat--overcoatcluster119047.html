overcoat

noun
final protective layer (paint, varnish)
4.7 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.koʊt/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.kəʊt/ (BrE)
A final or additional layer of paint, varnish, or similar substance applied for protection or finish.

Examples

A paint overcoat
After several base layers, a glossy overcoat was brushed on to complete the look.
For best results, let the primer dry before applying the final overcoat.
The furniture was finished with an overcoat of clear varnish for added durability.
