exhausted

adjective
completely used up or worn out
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔstɪd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd / (BrE)
extremely tired or completely used up (of energy, strength, or resources)
