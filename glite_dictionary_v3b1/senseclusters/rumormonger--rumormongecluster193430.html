rumormonger

noun
person who spreads rumors
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmərˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈruːməˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who habitually spreads rumors or gossip about others, often concerning their personal affairs.

Examples

The rumormonger spreads gossip
People quickly learned not to trust the rumormonger with private matters.
She avoided office politics because she didn't want to be labeled a rumormonger.
The rumormonger was responsible for much of the tension in the neighborhood.
It's difficult to trust information from a known rumourmonger in the office.
She earned a reputation as the village rumourmonger, always eager to share the latest gossip.
The article accused several rumourmongers of spreading false stories about the celebrity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busybody - a person who meddles in others' affairs
  • synonym: gossip - a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others
  • synonym: news hound - a person who eagerly seeks news or gossip
  • antonym: truth-teller - a person who tells the truth or facts
