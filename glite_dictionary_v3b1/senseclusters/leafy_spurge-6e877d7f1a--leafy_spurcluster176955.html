leafy spurge

noun
a perennial weedy plant (Euphorbia esula)
Pronunciation: / ˈliːfi spɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈliːfi spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a tall Eurasian perennial plant (Euphorbia esula) with greenish-yellow flowers, now a troublesome weed in parts of North America

Examples

Leafy spurge plant
Efforts to control leafy spurge include introducing natural insect predators to manage its spread.
Farmers are concerned about the rapid invasion of leafy spurge in their fields.
The pasture was overrun with leafy spurge, making it difficult for native grasses to grow.
