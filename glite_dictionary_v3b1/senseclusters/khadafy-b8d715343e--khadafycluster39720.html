Khadafy

proper noun
alternate spelling of Muammar Gaddafi
Pronunciation: / kəˈdɑːfi / (AmE), / kæˈdɑːfi / (BrE)
An alternate spelling of Muammar Gaddafi, the Libyan leader who ruled the country from 1969 to 2011 after overthrowing the monarchy.
