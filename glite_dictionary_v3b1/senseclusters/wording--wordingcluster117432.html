Synonyms

    3.4 1/m
    phrasing focuses on the specific choice and order of words to express an idea, while wording means the way something is expressed.
  • choice of words
    3.8 1/m
    choice of words names the actual words someone picks to say something, while wording describes the overall expression or phrasing used.
  • formulation
    5.5 0.2/m
    formulation sounds more formal and often means a carefully prepared sentence or plan, while wording means the way something is expressed.
  • phraseology
    8.2 0.2/m
