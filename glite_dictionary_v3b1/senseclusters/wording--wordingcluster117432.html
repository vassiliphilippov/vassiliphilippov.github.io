wording

noun
choice or arrangement of words
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the way in which something is expressed or phrased in words

Examples

The wording of a letter
He struggled to find the right wording for his apology letter.
The wording of the announcement was deliberately vague.
The lawyer advised her to be careful with her wording in the contract.
