glosser

noun
person who polishes
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈglɔː.sər/ (AmE), /ˈglɒ.sə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply a glossy finish or polish to surfaces to make them shiny

Examples

A glosser polishes surfaces
The company hired a skilled glosser to give the wooden floors a brilliant shine.
Each piece of furniture was carefully finished by the glosser before being displayed in the showroom.
After the paint dried, the glosser applied a final coat to make the surface gleam.
