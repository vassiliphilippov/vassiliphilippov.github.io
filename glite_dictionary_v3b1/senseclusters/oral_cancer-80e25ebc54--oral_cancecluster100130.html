oral cancer

noun
malignant tumor of the mouth
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.əl ˈkæn.sər/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.rəl ˈkæn.sə/ (BrE)
A malignant tumor that develops in the tissues of the mouth, most commonly affecting the lips, tongue, or inner lining of the cheeks.

Examples

Oral cancer diagnosis
Early diagnosis of oral cancer increases the chances of successful treatment.
Heavy smoking and drinking are major risk factors for oral cancer.
The dentist checked for signs of oral cancer during the routine exam.
