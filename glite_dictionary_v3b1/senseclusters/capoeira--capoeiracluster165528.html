capoeira

noun
scrub thicket
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpoʊˈeɪrə / (AmE), / ˌkapɔɪˈeərə / (BrE)
a patch of scrub or low thorny trees; a dense thicket, especially in regional or archaic usage

Examples

Dense capoeira thicket
They hacked a path through the dense capoeira to reach the riverbank before sunset.
The old map showed several areas of capoeira along the valley's edge.
Farmers cleared the capoeira to make room for new crops.

  • synonym: thicket - a dense group of bushes or trees
