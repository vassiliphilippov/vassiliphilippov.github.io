emblematically

adverb
symbolically
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmbləˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛmblɪˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that serves as a symbol or emblem of something

Examples

Used emblematically in speech
The national flag was raised emblematically to mark the start of the ceremony.
The artist placed the broken chain emblematically at the center of the painting.
The company’s logo emblematically represents its commitment to innovation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: literally - in a way that uses the exact meaning of words
  • synonym: symbolically - in a way that uses symbols to represent ideas
