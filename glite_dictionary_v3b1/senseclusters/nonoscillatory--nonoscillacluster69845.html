nonoscillatory

adjective
lacking periodic vibrations or oscillations
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɑsɪˈleɪtɔːri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɒsɪˈleɪtəri / (BrE)
Not having or characterized by periodic or repeating vibrations or oscillations.

Examples

A nonoscillatory solution
For certain values, the equation yields a nonoscillatory solution that smoothly approaches zero.
The engineer chose a nonoscillatory design to avoid unwanted vibrations in the machinery.
The system's nonoscillatory behavior indicated a rapid return to equilibrium without overshooting.
