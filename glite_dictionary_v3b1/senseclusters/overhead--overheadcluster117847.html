overhead

noun
tennis shot hit above the head
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəhɛd / (BrE)
(in tennis and similar sports) a stroke in which the ball is hit forcibly above the player's head, often in response to a lob.

Examples

Tennis overhead
Practicing your overhead can greatly improve your tennis game.
She executed a powerful overhead to win the point.
The player was caught out of position and missed the overhead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smash - a forceful overhead shot in tennis or badminton
