groper

noun
person feeling blindly
9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgroʊ.pər/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊ.pə/ (BrE)
a person who searches or feels about with the hands, especially blindly or uncertainly in the dark

Examples

As a groper, she carefully felt her way along the unfamiliar hallway.
The sudden darkness turned everyone into a groper looking for their belongings.
