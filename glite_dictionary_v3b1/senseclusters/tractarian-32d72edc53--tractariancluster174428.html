Tractarian

noun
follower of Tractarianism/Oxford Movement
9.9
Pronunciation: /træk.ˈter.iː.ən/ (AmE), /træk.ˈteə.riː.ən/ (BrE)
A follower of Tractarianism, especially one who supported the Oxford Movement in the Church of England, as expounded in the 'Tracts for the Times'.

Examples

A Tractarian follower
As a committed Tractarian, he championed reforms within the Church of England.
Many Victorian clergy were influenced by Tractarian ideas during that period.
The Tractarian movement had a profound impact on Anglican theology.
