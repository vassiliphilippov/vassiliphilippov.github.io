undeniable

adjective
impossible to deny; certain
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈnaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈnaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
so obviously true or certain that it cannot be disputed or denied

Examples

An undeniable fact
It is undeniable that climate change poses a significant threat to our future.
She made an undeniable impact on the success of the project.
The evidence against the suspect was undeniable and led to a quick conviction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indisputable - impossible to question or challenge
  • antonym: questionable - open to doubt or challenge
