centered

adjective
mentally/emotionally balanced
Pronunciation: / c8s5bn.t5ad / (AmE), / c8s5bn.t59d / (BrE)
Mentally or emotionally balanced, calm, and grounded, especially as a result of reflection, mindfulness, or self-control.

Examples

Centered mind
After years of meditation, she feels more centered and less reactive in stressful moments.
During the crisis meeting, his calm questions kept the team centered and focused on solutions.
A short breathing exercise in the morning helps me stay centered throughout a busy day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxious - feeling worried or uneasy; opposite of emotionally balanced
  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • synonym: grounded - emotionally stable and sensible; connected to reality
