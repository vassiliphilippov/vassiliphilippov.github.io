wrap a meeting

verb
conclude a meeting
Pronunciation: / ræp ə ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ræp ə ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
to bring a meeting to an end; to conclude the proceedings of a meeting

Examples

To wrap the meeting
After discussing all the points, the manager suggested they wrap the meeting.
Let's wrap the meeting so everyone can get to lunch on time.
We need to wrap the meeting in the next five minutes because another team needs the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjourn - to formally end or suspend a meeting or session
  • antonym: begin a meeting - to start a meeting
