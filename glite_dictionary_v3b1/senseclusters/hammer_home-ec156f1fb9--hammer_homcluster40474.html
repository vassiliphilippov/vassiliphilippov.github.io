hammer home

verb
to emphasize strongly, make clear
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmɚ hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈhamə həʊm / (BrE)
to emphasize something strongly or make it very clear, especially by repeating it often

Examples

To hammer home a point
News reports continue to hammer home the seriousness of the issue.
She hammered home her argument by providing clear and convincing evidence.
The coach tried to hammer home the need for teamwork during practice.
