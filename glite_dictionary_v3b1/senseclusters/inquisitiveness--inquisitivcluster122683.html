inquisitiveness

noun
the quality of being curious or eager to learn
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwɪzətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwɪzɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)

Examples

Teachers often encourage inquisitiveness in their students to promote active learning.
