exemplarily

adverb
in an exemplary way
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛmpləˌrɪli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛmplərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that serves as an example or demonstrates something clearly

Examples

Exemplarily explained steps
The teacher exemplarily explained the process by walking through each step with the class.
She handled the difficult situation exemplarily, setting a standard for others to follow.
The report was organized exemplarily, making it easy for everyone to understand the findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illustratively - in a way that explains or clarifies by example
  • antonym: poorly - in a way that is not a good example
