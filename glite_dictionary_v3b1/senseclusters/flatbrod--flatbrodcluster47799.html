flatbrod

noun
thin, crisp Scandinavian bread
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌbrɔd / (AmE), / ˈflætˌbrɒd / (BrE)
A thin, crisp, wafer-like bread traditionally made in Scandinavia, typically from rye or barley flour.

Examples

Crisp flatbrod bread
Homemade flatbrod can stay crisp for months if stored properly.
Traditional Norwegian meals often include flatbrod served alongside soups and stews.
We enjoyed slices of cheese on top of flatbrod during our Scandinavian holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crispbread - a dry, flat, and crisp bread or cracker, often made with rye flour
