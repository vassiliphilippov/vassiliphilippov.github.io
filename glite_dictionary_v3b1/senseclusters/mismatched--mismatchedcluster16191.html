mismatched

adjective
unevenly paired (contest/opponents)
2.4 3.7
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈmætʃt/ /; / /ˈmɪs.mætʃt/ (AmE), /ˈmɪs.mætʃt/ /; / /mɪs.ˈmætʃt/ (BrE)
(of a contest or opponents) not fairly matched in terms of strength, skill, or suitability; unevenly paired.

Examples

Mismatched teams
It was a mismatched contest from the start, as the experienced team faced complete novices.
Some critics argued that the final was disappointingly mismatched, with victory inevitable.
The boxing match was clearly mismatched, with one fighter much stronger than the other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uneven - not equal or level in quality or strength
  • antonym: well-matched - evenly matched or equally suited opponents
