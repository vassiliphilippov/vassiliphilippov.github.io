yearningly

adverb
with longing or desire
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈjɝː.nɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈjɜː.nɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a strong desire or longing for something.

Examples

To speak yearningly
He spoke yearningly of the life he might have had.
She stared yearningly at the photographs of distant cities she wished to visit.
The child looked yearningly at the last piece of cake on the table.

Related Terms
