chapbook

noun
small historical booklet
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtʃæpˌbʊk / (BrE)
a small, inexpensive booklet, especially one from the 16th to 19th centuries containing popular stories, ballads, or tracts

Examples

An old chapbook
The museum displayed a rare chapbook filled with folk tales from the 18th century.
Collectors often search for old chapbooks at antique fairs and book markets.
He published his poetry in a handmade chapbook to share with friends and family.

  • synonym: pamphlet - a small booklet or brochure containing information or arguments
