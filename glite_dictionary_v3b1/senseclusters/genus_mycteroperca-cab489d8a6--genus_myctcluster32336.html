genus Mycteroperca

noun
a genus of groupers (fish)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmaɪktəroʊˈpɜːrkə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmɪktərəˈpɜːkə / (BrE)
A genus of medium to large marine fishes in the grouper family, known for their stout bodies and importance in commercial and recreational fishing.
