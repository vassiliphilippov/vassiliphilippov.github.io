bewilderingly

adverb
in a confusing or perplexing way
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪldərɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldəɹɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes confusion or is difficult to understand.

Examples

A bewilderingly vague explanation
His explanation was bewilderingly vague, making it hard to understand his point.
The instructions were bewilderingly complex, leaving everyone uncertain about what to do.
The plot of the novel shifts bewilderingly from one character's perspective to another.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a clear manner, not confusingly
  • synonym: confusingly - in a confusing manner
