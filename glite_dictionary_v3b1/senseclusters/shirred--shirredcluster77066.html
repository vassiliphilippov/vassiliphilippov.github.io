shirred

adjective
baked in a dish
Pronunciation: / ʃɜrd / (AmE), / ʃɜːd / (BrE)
cooked by baking in a shallow dish in the oven, especially referring to eggs prepared this way rather than fried or poached

He learned to make shirred eggs as a new way to enjoy his favorite breakfast.
