Synonyms

  • evolutive
    7.7 0.01/m
    evolutive describes slow ongoing development and change, while evolutionary refers to change that results from the process of evolution.
  • phyletic
    9.9 0.03/m
    phyletic describes development and diversification of a species over a long time, while evolutionary is a broader term for change by evolution.
  • phylogenetic
    9.9 1.02/m
    phylogenetic describes the history and relationships of species through evolution, while evolutionary means relating to or caused by evolution.
