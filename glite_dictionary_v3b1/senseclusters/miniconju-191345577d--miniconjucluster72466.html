Miniconju

noun
a subgroup of the Teton Sioux people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnɪˈkoʊn(d)ʒuː / (AmE), / ˌmɪnɪˈkɒn(d)ʒuː / (BrE)
A member of a division of the Teton Sioux (Lakota) people who traditionally lived in western South Dakota, especially between the Black Hills and the Platte River.

Examples

Miniconju Sioux people
He is researching the traditions of the Miniconju in South Dakota.
Several families from the Miniconju tribe settled near the Cheyenne River.
The Miniconju were known for their skilled horsemanship and hunting practices.
