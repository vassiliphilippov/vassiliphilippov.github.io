boomlet

noun
brief surge
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːmlət / (AmE), / ˈbuːmlət / (BrE)
a small or brief period of rapid growth, activity, or popularity

Examples

A boomlet in startups
The tech industry experienced a boomlet in new startups after the policy change.
There was a boomlet of interest in gardening during the spring months.
Analysts noted a boomlet in electric car sales last quarter.
The city experienced a boomlet in tourism after the new museum opened.
A boomlet of interest in vinyl records surprised music store owners.
There was a brief boomlet in sales following the product's release.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: boom - a period of rapid growth or increase
  • antonym: decline - a gradual and continuous loss of strength or value
  • synonym: surge - a sudden and powerful forward or upward movement
