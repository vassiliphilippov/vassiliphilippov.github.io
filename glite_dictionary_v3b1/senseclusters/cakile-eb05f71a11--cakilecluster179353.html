Cakile

noun
genus of succulent coastal plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈkaɪli / (AmE), / kəˈkaɪli / (BrE)
A small genus of succulent annual herbs, commonly known as sea rocket, found on sandy shores of North America and Europe.

Examples

Cakile plant
Cakile is often recognized by its fleshy leaves and small purple flowers.
Some ecologists study Cakile to understand plant adaptation to harsh environments.
The Cakile plants thrive on sandy beaches along the Atlantic coast.
