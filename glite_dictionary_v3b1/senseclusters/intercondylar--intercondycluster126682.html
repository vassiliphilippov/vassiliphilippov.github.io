intercondylar

adjective
between condyles
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkɑndɪlər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈkɒndɪlə / (BrE)
located or occurring between two condyles, especially in bones or joints

Examples

The intercondylar fracture
The surgeon carefully repaired the intercondylar fracture in the patient's femur.
Damage to the intercondylar region of the knee can affect joint stability.
An MRI revealed swelling in the intercondylar area after the sports injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condylar - relating to a condyle, a rounded bone end
