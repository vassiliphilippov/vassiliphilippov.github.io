sameness

noun
lack of variety; monotony
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪmnəs / (AmE), / ˈseɪmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being the same as something else, with little or no variety; monotony or lack of difference

Examples

The sameness of routine
After several weeks, the sameness of the daily routine began to wear on the team.
Many people seek travel to escape the sameness of everyday life.
She was frustrated by the sameness of meals served in the cafeteria.
Many people find comfort in the sameness of their daily routines.
Some critics complained about the sameness of the TV show's episodes.
The sameness of their answers made it difficult to distinguish between the students.

  • synonym: monotony - lack of variety and interest; tedious repetition
  • synonym: similarity - the state of being similar or alike
  • antonym: variety - the quality of being different or diverse
