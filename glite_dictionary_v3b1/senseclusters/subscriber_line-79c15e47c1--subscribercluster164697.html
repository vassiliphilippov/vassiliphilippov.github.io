subscriber line

noun
telephone line to subscriber's premises
2.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈskraɪ.bər laɪn/ (AmE), /səb.ˈskraɪ.bə laɪn/ (BrE)
a telephone line that connects a subscriber's premises to the local telephone exchange

Examples

A subscriber line connection
Each apartment in the building has its own separate subscriber line for telephone service.
The technician came to repair the subscriber line to restore the homeowner's phone service.
With the installation of a digital subscriber line, customers can access high-speed internet.
