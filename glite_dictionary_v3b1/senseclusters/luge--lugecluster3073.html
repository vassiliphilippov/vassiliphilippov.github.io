luge

verb
to race on a luge sled
Pronunciation: / luːʒ / (AmE), / luːʒ / (BrE)
to travel or race while lying on a small sledge with the feet forward, typically at high speed down an ice track

Examples

To luge downhill
Athletes from around the world come to luge on this famous ice track.
She learned to luge during the winter Olympics training program.
The children dreamed of one day getting to luge down an Olympic course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toboggan - to ride on a long, flat-bottomed sled, usually downhill
