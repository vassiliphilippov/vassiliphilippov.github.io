dimension

noun
measurable aspect or distinguishing feature
1.0
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛnʃən /; / dɪˈmɛnʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈmɛnʃən /; / dɪˈmɛnʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a measurable aspect, feature, or quality of something; a way in which something can vary or be classified

Examples

Social dimension
In psychology, intelligence is often measured across different dimensions such as memory and reasoning.
Social status is just one dimension along which members of society can be classified.
The experiment examined each dimension of personality to understand how people differ.
The artist hoped to capture the emotional dimension of the landscape.
The box was impressive in every dimension, from its height to its depth.
The sheer dimension of the mountain range took my breath away.
