mud brick

noun
brick made from hardened mud
Pronunciation: / mʌd brɪk / (AmE), / mʌd brɪk / (BrE)
a brick made by shaping mud and allowing it to harden, used for building walls and structures

Examples

A mud brick wall
Many traditional houses in the region use mud brick walls for insulation.
The ancient city was built almost entirely from mud bricks.
The archeologists discovered a well-preserved mud brick structure at the site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adobe - a brick made from sun-dried earth and straw
