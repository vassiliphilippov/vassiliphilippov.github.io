sphinx

noun
an inscrutable or enigmatic person
8.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /sfɪŋks/ (AmE), /sfɪŋks/ (BrE)
a person who is difficult to read or who keeps their thoughts and intentions secret; an inscrutable person

Examples

To his friends, Mark was something of a sphinx, always keeping his feelings hidden.
