unfeelingly

adverb
without emotion or compassion
8.3 6.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfiː.lɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ən.ˈfiː.lɪŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfiː.lɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ən.ˈfiː.lɪŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that shows no emotion, sympathy, or compassion

Examples

Speak unfeelingly
He spoke unfeelingly about their difficulties, showing no sympathy at all.

The manager unfeelingly dismissed the employee without a second thought.
He unfeelingly ignored the cries for help from those in need.
Her manager unfeelingly dismissed her concerns about the workload.
The policy was unfeelingly enforced, regardless of individual circumstances.
