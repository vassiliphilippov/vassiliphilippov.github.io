remark

verb
make a brief comment or observation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
to say or write something as a comment or observation

Examples

To remark on changes
He remarked that the city had changed a lot since his last visit.
Many attendees remarked on the speaker's sense of humor.
She remarked that the meeting was running late.
During the meeting, he remarked that the project was ahead of schedule.
She remarked on how beautiful the sunset looked that evening.
The teacher remarked on the improvements in her students' essays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comment - express an observation or opinion
  • synonym: note - mention or observe briefly
