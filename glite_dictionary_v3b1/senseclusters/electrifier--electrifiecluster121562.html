electrifier

noun
one who excites
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person or thing that excites or energizes people emotionally, creating a strong sense of enthusiasm or excitement

Examples

Team electrifier
The new coach quickly became an electrifier for the team, inspiring them to play their best.
Her performance as the lead singer was a true electrifier for the entire audience.
He is known as an electrifier in the business world, always bringing energy to every meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dampener - someone or something that reduces excitement or enthusiasm
  • synonym: inspirer - a person who inspires others
  • synonym: motivator - someone who encourages others to take action
