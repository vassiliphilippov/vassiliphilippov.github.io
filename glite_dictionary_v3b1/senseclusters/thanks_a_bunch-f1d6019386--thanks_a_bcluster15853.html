thanks a bunch

interjection
informal expression of gratitude
Pronunciation: / ˈθæŋks ə bʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈθæŋks ə bʌntʃ / (BrE)
An informal way to express sincere or enthusiastic gratitude.

Examples

Thanks a bunch for help
Thanks a bunch for picking up my groceries when I couldn't leave the house.
Thanks a bunch for watching the kids while I ran my errands.
I forgot to say thanks a bunch for lending me your umbrella yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thank you - standard phrase to express gratitude
  • synonym: thanks a lot - an informal expression of thanks, sometimes used sarcastically
