solipsistic

adjective
self-centered attitude
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːlɪpˈsɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɒlɪpˈsɪstɪk / (BrE)
thinking only about oneself and disregarding the existence or needs of others

Examples

Solipsistic attitude
His solipsistic worldview made it difficult for him to form close relationships.
The novel explores the dangers of a solipsistic approach to life and society.
She was criticized for her solipsistic focus on her own ambitions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruistic - showing concern for the well-being of others
  • synonym: egocentric - thinking only of oneself, without regard for others
  • synonym: selfish - caring only about oneself and not others
