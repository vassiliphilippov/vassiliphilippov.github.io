capitalization

noun
sale of company stock to raise capital
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪtələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkæpɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of selling shares of a company's capital stock to raise funds

Examples

Initial capitalization round
After its initial capitalization, the startup attracted several major investors.
Strong capitalization is essential for the long-term stability of a business.
The company's capitalization allowed them to expand into new markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: financing - the act of providing funds for business activities
