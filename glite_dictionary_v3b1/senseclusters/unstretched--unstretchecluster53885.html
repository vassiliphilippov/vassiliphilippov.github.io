unstretched

adjective
not under strain
4.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrɛtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrɛtʃt / (BrE)
not under strain, pressure, or fully used; not extended to the limit in ability, resources, or physical state

Examples

Unstretched workers
With the new schedule, the workers felt unstretched and could handle their tasks easily.
The rope remained unstretched even after securing the tent tightly.
She enjoyed a job where she was unstretched and had time for her hobbies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relaxed - free from tension or stress
  • antonym: stretched - under strain or extended to the limit
