Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or other verbal or physical conduct of a sexual nature, especially by a person in authority toward a subordinate, creating a hostile or offensive working environment.

Examples

Sexual harassment at work
Employers have a legal obligation to address any reports of sexual harassment.

The company implemented strict policies to prevent sexual harassment in the office.
