full of shit

adjective
being dishonest or insincere (vulgar)
7.3
Pronunciation: / fʊl əv ʃɪt / (AmE), / fʊl əv ʃɪt / (BrE)
used to describe someone who is not telling the truth or is being insincere; dishonest or deceptive.

Examples

Be full of shit
Don't listen to his advice; the guy is completely full of shit.
I can't believe anything he says—he's always full of shit.
When she claimed she was a millionaire, everyone knew she was full of shit.

  • synonym: lying - not telling the truth
