DOB

proper noun
building agency abbreviation
7.6
Pronunciation: / di oʊ bi / (AmE), / diː əʊ biː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'Department of Buildings', a government agency responsible for enforcing building codes and issuing construction permits

Examples

DOB inspection
The DOB conducted an inspection before the new apartment building could open.
Contractors must submit their plans to the DOB for approval before starting construction.
The DOB issued a stop-work order due to safety violations at the site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: building department - a government office overseeing building regulations and permits
