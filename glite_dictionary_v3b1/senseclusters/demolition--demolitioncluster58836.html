Synonyms

  • razing
    7.8 0.10/m
    razing is destroying a building completely down to the ground, while demolition can mean planned removal that may be partial or total.
  • wrecking
    4.4 1.29/m
    wrecking is breaking or tearing a structure, often by machines, while demolition is the formal process of removing buildings.
  • leveling
    5.6 0.05/m
    leveling means knocking a building flat to the ground, while demolition can include careful or partial removal instead of full flattening.
  • teardown
    5.5 0.03/m
    teardown is removing a building usually to replace it with a new one, while demolition covers any planned destruction.
