depository

noun
a storage facility or place for safekeeping
5.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑzɪˌtɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈpɒzɪtri / (BrE)
a place or facility where things are stored for safekeeping, often referring to valuables, records, or documents

Examples

A depository for valuables
Investors often choose a trusted depository for storing important legal documents and certificates.
The museum uses a secure depository to keep rare artifacts safe from damage or theft.
The town built a new depository to house community records, books, and other valuable items.
