nodder

noun
person or device that nods
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑdər / (AmE), / ˈnɒdə / (BrE)
a person who nods frequently, especially in agreement, or a device that moves by nodding

Examples

An agreeing nodder
The manager surrounded himself with nodders who never challenged his ideas.
On her desk, she kept a small nodder that bobbed its head whenever the table shook.
During the lecture, several nodders in the audience agreed with every point the speaker made.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bobblehead - a toy with a head that nods or bobs when touched
  • synonym: yes-man - a person who always agrees with others, especially superiors
