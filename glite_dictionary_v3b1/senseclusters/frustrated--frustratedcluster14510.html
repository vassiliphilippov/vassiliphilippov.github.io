frustrated

adjective
unsuccessful; unable to achieve something
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌstreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfrʌstreɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling or showing disappointment because of being unable to achieve something; having failed to succeed despite effort

Examples

A frustrated attempt
Despite months of planning, their frustrated attempt to summit the mountain ended in retreat.
His frustrated efforts to fix the old car left him feeling disappointed and tired.
Many frustrated artists turn to teaching when their work fails to gain recognition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: successful - having achieved a desired outcome; having succeeded
  • synonym: thwarted - prevented from accomplishing something
