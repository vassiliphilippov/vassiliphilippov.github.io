border patroller

noun
person who patrols a country's borders
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdər pəˈtroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə pəˈtrəʊlə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to patrol and monitor the borders of a country to prevent unauthorized entry or exit

Examples

A border patroller on duty
Many border patrollers work long hours, especially in remote and challenging areas.
She aspired to become a border patroller to help improve national security.
The border patroller checked every vehicle crossing into the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border guard - a person or group enforcing the border security of a nation
  • synonym: customs officer - an official who inspects goods and people crossing a border
