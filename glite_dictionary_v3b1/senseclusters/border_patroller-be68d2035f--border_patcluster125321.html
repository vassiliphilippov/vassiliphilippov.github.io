border patroller

noun
person who patrols a country's borders
5.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɔːr.dər pə.ˈtroʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈbɔː.də pə.ˈtrəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
