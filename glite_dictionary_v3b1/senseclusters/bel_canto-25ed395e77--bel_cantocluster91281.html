bel canto

noun
style of operatic singing
9.8 7.2
Pronunciation: /bel ˈkæn.toʊ/ (AmE), /bel ˈkæn.təʊ/ (BrE)
A style of operatic singing characterized by smooth and lyrical phrasing, purity of tone, and emphasis on technique and agility.

Examples

Bel canto singing style
Many students study bel canto techniques to improve their vocal control.

The opera showcased the elegance and agility of bel canto singing.
