bearpaw

noun
succulent houseplant
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌpɔ / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌpɔː / (BrE)
a small succulent houseplant with thick, fuzzy leaves that resemble a bear's paw, especially Cotyledon tomentosa

Examples

Bearpaw plant leaves
The bearpaw is popular among plant lovers for its unique, paw-shaped leaves.
She placed her new bearpaw on the kitchen windowsill to get plenty of sunlight.
A healthy bearpaw needs well-draining soil and careful watering to thrive indoors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: succulent - a plant with thick, fleshy tissues for storing water
