prosecutable

adjective
can be tried in court
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːsɪˌkjuːtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈprɒsɪˌkjuːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be taken to court and tried as a crime under the law

Examples

Prosecutable crime
The theft was considered prosecutable under the new regulations.
Not every mistake at work is prosecutable as a criminal offense.
The lawyer explained which actions are prosecutable in a court of law.

Related Terms

  • antonym: legal - permitted by law
  • synonym: punishable - deserving or able to be punished by law
