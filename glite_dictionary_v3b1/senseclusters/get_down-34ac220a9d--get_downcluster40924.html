get down

verb
manage to swallow something
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
to manage to swallow or consume (food or drink), especially when difficult

Examples

To get down a meal
Despite the lump in his throat, he managed to get down a glass of water.
He could barely get down the medicine because it tasted so bad.
She tried to get down a few bites, even though she had no appetite.

Related Terms
