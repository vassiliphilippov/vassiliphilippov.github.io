Kevlar

noun
synthetic protective fiber
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkev.lər/ /; / /ˈkev.lɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈkev.lɑː/ (BrE)

Examples

Kevlar fiber

Many modern helmets are reinforced with Kevlar to provide extra protection.
The backpack has a layer of Kevlar to make it resistant to cuts and punctures.
