serac

noun
glacial ice block
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈse.ræk/ /; / /ˈse.rək/ (AmE), /ˈse.ræk/ /; / /ˈse.rək/ (BrE)
a large block or column of glacial ice found on crevasses, steep slopes, or icefalls of a glacier
