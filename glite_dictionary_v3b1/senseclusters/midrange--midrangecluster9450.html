midrange

noun
basketball court area
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪd.reɪndʒ/ /; / /mɪd.ˈreɪndʒ/ (AmE), /ˈmɪd.reɪndʒ/ /; / /mɪd.ˈreɪndʒ/ (BrE)
in basketball, the area on the court between the key (paint) and the three-point line, or shots taken from this area

Examples

The player is known for his accurate midrange shots, which make him hard to defend.
Coaches often encourage athletes to practice their midrange game to become more versatile scorers.
She hit a crucial midrange jumper in the final seconds to win the match.
