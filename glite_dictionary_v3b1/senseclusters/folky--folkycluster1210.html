folky

adjective
friendly and informal
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊki / (AmE), / ˈfəʊki / (BrE)
having a friendly, informal, or simple manner, often resembling the style or spirit of folk culture

Examples

Folky atmosphere
The restaurant has a folky atmosphere with wooden tables and live acoustic music.
Her folky way of speaking made everyone feel comfortable and welcome.
He wore a folky shirt with bright patterns to the outdoor festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: down-to-earth - practical, friendly, and unpretentious
  • antonym: formal - following strict rules or customs; not casual
