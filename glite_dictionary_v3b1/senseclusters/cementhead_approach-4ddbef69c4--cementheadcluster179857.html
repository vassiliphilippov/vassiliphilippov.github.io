cementhead approach

noun
stubborn, unyielding attitude or method
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛntˌhɛd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛnthɛd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
An attitude or method marked by stubbornness or refusal to change one's position.

Examples

A cementhead approach
Despite the advice of his colleagues, he stuck with a cementhead approach throughout the project.
His cementhead approach to negotiations made compromise impossible.
The team's progress was hindered by their manager's cementhead approach to every challenge.
