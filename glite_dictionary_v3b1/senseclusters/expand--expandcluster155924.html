expand

verb
write out an abbreviation
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspænd / (BrE)
to write or give the full form of an abbreviation or acronym.

Examples

To expand an acronym
Editors often expand acronyms in glossaries to improve clarity for readers.
When preparing the report, she expanded 'UN' to 'United Nations' in the footnotes.
The style guide instructs writers to expand abbreviations at first use in an article.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abbreviate - to make a word or phrase shorter, often by using initials or a shorter form.
  • synonym: spell out - to write or say something in full so that it is clear.
