unsymmetrical

adjective
not symmetrical; lacking symmetry
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛtrɪkəl /; / ˌʌn.sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛtrɪkəl /; / ˌʌn.sɪˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l / (BrE)
not having symmetry; lacking balance or evenness in form, design, or arrangement
