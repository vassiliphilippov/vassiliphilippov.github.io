dap

verb
to greet with a tap
Pronunciation: / dæp / (AmE), / dæp / (BrE)
to touch or tap someone lightly, especially by bumping fists or slapping hands, as a friendly greeting or sign of respect

Examples

To dap fists
When they met after a long time, they gave each other a quick dap and smiled.
He reached out to dap his friend before heading onto the basketball court.
At the end of the meeting, the teammates dapped to show their respect for each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handshake - a greeting or agreement gesture involving grasping hands
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to someone
