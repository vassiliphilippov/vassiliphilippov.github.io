sighingly

adverb
with a sigh
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsaɪɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or expresses sighing, often showing sadness or disappointment

Examples

She replied sighingly
She replied sighingly, unable to hide her disappointment.
He looked out the window sighingly, thinking about what could have been.
The student handed in his assignment sighingly, worried about his grade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive way
  • synonym: sadly - in a way that shows sadness
