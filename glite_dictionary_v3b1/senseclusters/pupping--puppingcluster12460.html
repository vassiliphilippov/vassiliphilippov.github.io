pupping

verb
to give birth (animals)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪŋ / (BrE)
to give birth to pups or young animals, especially used for mammals like seals, dogs, or otters

Examples

Otters are pupping
The female otter is pupping in the protected cove this week.
During spring, many seals can be seen pupping on the ice.
Wildlife researchers observed a dog pupping in the shelter last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whelping - to give birth to young, especially used for dogs
