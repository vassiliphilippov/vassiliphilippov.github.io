immunity

noun
state of not being susceptible
3.5 5.19/m
Pronunciation: / …™Ààmjun…ôti / (AmE), / …™ÀàmjuÀên…ôti / (BrE)
the state of being protected or resistant to a disease or harmful influence, especially because of the body's defenses

Examples

Natural immunity to disease
Certain plants have developed natural immunity to pests over time.
His immunity to criticism allowed him to persevere under pressure.
The vaccine provided lifelong immunity against the disease.
After recovering from the illness, she developed natural immunity to the virus.
Infants receive some immunity from their mothers in the first few months of life.
Vaccines are an effective way to build immunity against many infectious diseases.

Synonyms
