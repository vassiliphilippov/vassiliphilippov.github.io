intercommunal

adjective
between communities
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkəˈmjuːnəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkəˈmjuːnəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between different communities

Examples

Intercommunal dialogue
The government encouraged intercommunal dialogue to resolve the conflict peacefully.
An intercommunal sports event brought together teams from neighboring towns.
Efforts to improve intercommunal relations have led to greater cooperation in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercommunity - existing or happening between communities
  • antonym: intracommunal - occurring within a single community
