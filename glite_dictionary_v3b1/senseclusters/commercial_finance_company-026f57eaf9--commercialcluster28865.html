commercial finance company

noun
company that lends to businesses
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝː.ʃəl fəˈnaɪns ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / kəˈmɜː.ʃəl fəˈnaɪəns ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a financial institution that provides loans to businesses, especially manufacturers and wholesalers

Examples

A commercial finance company loan
After being turned down by several banks, the firm approached a commercial finance company for funding.
Many wholesalers rely on a commercial finance company to meet their short-term cash needs.
The commercial finance company approved a large loan for the manufacturer's expansion.
