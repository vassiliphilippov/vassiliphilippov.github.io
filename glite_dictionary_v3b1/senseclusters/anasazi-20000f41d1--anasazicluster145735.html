Anasazi

noun
ancient Native American people of the Southwest
9.9
Pronunciation: /æ.nə.ˈsæ.ziː/ /; / /æ.nə.ˈɑː.ziː/ (AmE), /æ.nə.ˈsɑː.ziː/ /; / /ɑː.nə.ˈsɑː.ziː/ (BrE)
a member of an ancient Native American people who lived in the southwestern United States and built cliff dwellings, especially in the regions now known as Colorado, Utah, Arizona, and New Mexico

Examples

Anasazi cliff dwellings
