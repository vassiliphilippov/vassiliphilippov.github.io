roselike

adjective
resembling a rose
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a color or appearance similar to that of a rose, especially pinkish or reddish in hue

Examples

Roselike glow
The sunset cast a roselike glow across the sky, filling the evening with soft colors.
She wore a dress in a roselike shade that complemented her complexion beautifully.
The painter mixed several colors to achieve a delicate roselike tone for the portrait.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinkish - somewhat pink in color
  • synonym: rosy - having a pink or reddish color like a rose
