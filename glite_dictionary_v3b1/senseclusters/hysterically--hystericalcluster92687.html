hysterically

adverb
in an uncontrolled, emotional way
2.6
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɛrɪkli / (AmE), / hɪˈstɛrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows uncontrolled emotion, especially laughter or crying, often to an extreme or irrational degree.

Examples

To laugh hysterically
He shouted hysterically after discovering the surprise party.
She burst out hysterically laughing at the comedian's joke.
The children screamed hysterically during the scary movie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a calm manner, without strong emotion
