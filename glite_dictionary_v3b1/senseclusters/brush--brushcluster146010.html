brush

noun
implement with bristles for cleaning or painting
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ / (AmE), / brʌʃ / (BrE)
An implement with bristles, wire, or other filaments, used for cleaning, painting, or arranging hair.

Examples

A paint brush
He used a brush to paint the fence.
Please clean your shoes with the brush near the door.
She always keeps a brush in her purse to tidy her hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comb - a device with a row of teeth for arranging hair
