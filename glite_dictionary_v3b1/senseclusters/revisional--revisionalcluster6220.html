revisional

adjective
involving a revision
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɪʒənəl / (AmE), / rɪˈvɪʒənəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving a revision, especially of a previous procedure, operation, or document

Examples

A revisional procedure
The doctor recommended a revisional procedure to address the complications from the first surgery.
After reviewing the report, the committee suggested several revisional changes to improve clarity.
A revisional operation may be necessary if the initial treatment does not succeed.

  • antonym: initial - occurring at the beginning; first
