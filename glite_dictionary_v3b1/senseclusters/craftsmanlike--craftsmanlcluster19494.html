craftsmanlike

adjective
skilled and sturdy
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkræft.smən.laɪk/ (AmE), /ˈkræft.smən.laɪk/ (BrE)
showing skill, care, and quality typical of a craftsman

Examples

Craftsmanlike skill
The furniture in the living room has a craftsmanlike quality, with clean lines and solid wood.
She admired the craftsmanlike details in the old house, especially the built-in shelves and exposed beams.
His approach to building the table was truly craftsmanlike, focusing on both function and beauty.
The furniture was built in a craftsmanlike manner, sturdy and precise but not especially innovative.
Her report was craftsmanlike, covering all the necessary details with accuracy and care.
Although the painting was craftsmanlike, it did not stand out for its creativity.
The artist's craftsmanlike approach was evident in every detail of the sculpture.
Her craftsmanlike repairs made the old chair look brand new.
The building was restored with craftsmanlike precision and respect for its history.
