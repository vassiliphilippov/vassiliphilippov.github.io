splatter

noun
a sharp, explosive noise of something hitting a surface
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsplætɚ / (AmE), / ˈsplætə / (BrE)
a sudden, sharp sound made when a liquid or small particles strike a surface explosively or forcefully

Examples

A loud splatter
A sudden splatter startled the crowd during the fireworks display.
She recognized the splatter of raindrops against the window pane.
The splatter from the kitchen made it clear dinner was being prepared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spatter - a light, scattered noise of small drops hitting a surface
