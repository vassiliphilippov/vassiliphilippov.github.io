inhospitably

adverb
in an unfriendly or unwelcoming way
5.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhɑːspɪtəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɒspɪtəbli / (BrE)
In an unfriendly or unwelcoming manner.

Examples

To act inhospitably
Due to previous experiences, she regarded the city as a place that greeted newcomers inhospitably.
He found himself received inhospitably in the cold mountain pass.
The villagers treated the strangers inhospitably, refusing to offer them shelter.
