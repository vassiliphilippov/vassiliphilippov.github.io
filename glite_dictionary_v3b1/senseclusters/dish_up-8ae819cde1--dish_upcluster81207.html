dish up

verb
serve food or provide something
3.6 1.9
Pronunciation: /dɪʃ ʌp/ /; / /dɪʃ əp/ (AmE), /dɪʃ ʌp/ /; / /dɪʃ əp/ (BrE)
to serve food onto plates or at the table, usually from a larger container

Examples
