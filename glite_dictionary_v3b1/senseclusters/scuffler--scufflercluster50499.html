scuffler

noun
soil-loosening tool
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌflər / (AmE), / ˈskʌflə / (BrE)
a tool or machine used for loosening soil or removing weeds from the ground

Examples

A scuffler for soil
The farmer attached the scuffler to his tractor to break up the soil before planting.
After a heavy rain, she used a scuffler to remove weeds from the vegetable garden.
A scuffler can make preparing large fields for crops much easier and faster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultivator - a machine or tool used to prepare soil for planting
  • synonym: hoe - a hand tool used for digging and removing weeds
