sound recording

noun
a recording of sounds or music
1.7
Pronunciation: / saʊnd rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / saʊnd rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A recording of acoustic signals, such as speech, music, or other sounds, preserved in any form for playback.
