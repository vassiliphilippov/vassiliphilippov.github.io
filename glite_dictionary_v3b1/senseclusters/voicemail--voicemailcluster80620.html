voicemail

noun
telephone message recording system
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊsˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊsˌmeɪl / (BrE)
a system that allows callers to leave recorded messages for someone who is unable to answer their phone

Examples

Voicemail system
He forgot to set up his voicemail, so callers couldn't leave messages.
I checked my voicemail and found three new messages from my boss.
Please leave a voicemail if I don't answer the phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: answering machine - a device for recording telephone messages
