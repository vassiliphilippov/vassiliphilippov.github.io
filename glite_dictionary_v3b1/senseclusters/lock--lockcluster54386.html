lock

verb
fasten with a lock
3.2
Pronunciation: / lɑk / (AmE), / lɒk / (BrE)
to fasten or secure something with a lock, or to confine someone or something in a place so it cannot be accessed or escape

Examples

To lock the door
Always lock your doors before going to bed.
He forgot to lock the suitcase before checking it in at the airport.
She locked her bike to the post outside the café.
He locked the documents in his desk drawer before going out to lunch.
Please lock the door when you leave the house for safety.
The zookeeper locks the animals in their enclosures every night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secure - to fasten or make safe
  • antonym: unlock - to open or release something by undoing a lock
