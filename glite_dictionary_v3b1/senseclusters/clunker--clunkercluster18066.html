clunker

noun
heavy dull sound
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈklʌŋ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈklʌŋ.kə/ (BrE)
a heavy object that makes a dull, loud sound when it is struck or dropped, or the sound itself.

Examples

A heavy clunker
The old engine made a loud clunker as it finally gave out.
When the metal box hit the floor, a heavy clunker echoed through the room.
He dropped the toolbox, and it landed with a sharp clunker on the concrete.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym: thud - a dull, heavy sound made by an object falling or hitting something
