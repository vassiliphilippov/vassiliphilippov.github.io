rustproof

verb
to prevent rust
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌst.pruːf/ (AmE), /ˈrʌst.pruːf/ (BrE)
to apply a protective coating or treatment to metal to prevent it from rusting

Examples

To rustproof a car
The mechanic is rustproofing the chassis to prevent corrosion during winter.
It is important to rustproof your car if you live in a snowy region.
They rustproofed the metal fence to make it last longer outdoors.

Related Terms
