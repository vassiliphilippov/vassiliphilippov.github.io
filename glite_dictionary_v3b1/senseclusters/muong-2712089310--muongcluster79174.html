Muong

noun
Vietnamese place name element
Pronunciation: / muːˈɒŋ / (AmE), / muːˈɒŋ / (BrE)
a word element in Vietnamese place names, originally from Tai languages, meaning 'valley' or 'district'

Examples

Muong valley name
Many Vietnamese towns include Muong in their names to indicate a valley or district.
The explorers traveled through the Muong region, known for its lush landscapes.
Local legends often mention the ancient Muong settlements in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: district - an area of a country or city, often with official boundaries
  • synonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
