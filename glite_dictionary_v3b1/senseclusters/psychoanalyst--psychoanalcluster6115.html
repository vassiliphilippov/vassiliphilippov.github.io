psychoanalyst

noun
therapist trained in psychoanalysis
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈænəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈanəlɪst / (BrE)
a medical doctor or therapist who is trained and licensed to practice psychoanalysis, using techniques to treat mental and emotional disorders

Examples

A psychoanalyst treats patients
After years of study, she became a respected psychoanalyst with her own private practice.
Sigmund Freud was a pioneer in the field and is considered the first major psychoanalyst.
The patient met with his psychoanalyst weekly to discuss his recurring dreams and anxieties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychoanalysis - a set of psychological theories and therapeutic techniques
  • synonym: therapist - a person trained to treat mental or psychological disorders
