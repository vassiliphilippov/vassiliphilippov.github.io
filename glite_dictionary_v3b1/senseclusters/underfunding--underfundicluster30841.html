Synonyms

  • underinvestment
    6.1 0.08/m
    underinvestment means not spending enough to maintain or grow something, while underfunding is a general lack of money for a project.
  • undercapitalization
    8.2 0.02/m
    undercapitalization is when a business lacks enough capital to operate or grow, while underfunding covers any project or service shortfall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: funding - the act of providing money for a particular purpose

  • synonym: shortfall - a lack of something needed or expected
  • antonym: surplus - an amount of something left over when requirements have been met
  • synonym: underinvestment - the act of investing less money than is needed
