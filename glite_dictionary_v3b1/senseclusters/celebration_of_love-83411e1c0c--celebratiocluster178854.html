celebration of love

noun
event honoring love and relationships
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən əv lʌv / (AmE), / ˌsɛlɪˈbreɪʃən əv lʌv / (BrE)
an event or ceremony that honors love and romantic relationships, often marking significant occasions such as weddings or anniversaries

Their anniversary party was a joyful celebration of love shared with friends and family.
They organized a celebration of love to mark their engagement.
