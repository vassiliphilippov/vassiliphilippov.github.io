blackberry

noun
edible dark purple or black fruit
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈblækˌbɛri / (BrE)
A large, sweet, black or very dark purple edible fruit made up of small drupelets, produced by various plants of the genus Rubus.

Examples

A ripe blackberry
In late summer, the blackberry harvest is at its peak.
She picked a ripe blackberry from the bush and ate it.
The pie was filled with sweet blackberry filling.
