forge

verb
create or validate a blockchain block
7.2 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / f…îrd í / (AmE), / f…îÀêd í / (BrE)
(in blockchain technology) to create or validate a new block of transactions as part of a distributed ledger's consensus process; often used for block production in proof-of-stake systems.

Examples

To forge a block
The validator began to forge a new block after collecting the latest transactions.
During the testnet run, her node forged several blocks without producing any forks.
By forging blocks in the PoS network, validators earn transaction fees and staking rewards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mine - to produce a block or obtain cryptocurrency rewards by performing proof-of-work computations (analogous technical role in different consensus systems).
  • synonym: validate - to check and confirm that transactions and blocks follow the protocol rules; validators may also produce (forge) blocks in some consensus mechanisms.
