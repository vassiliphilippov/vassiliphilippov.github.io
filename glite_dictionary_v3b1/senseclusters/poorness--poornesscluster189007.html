poorness

noun
state of being poor
2.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…îrn…ôs / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀên…ôs / (BrE)
the state of having little or no money or few material possessions; poverty

Examples

Poorness of the family
Despite his poorness, he remained generous to those in need.
Growing up in poorness taught her the value of hard work and perseverance.
The film depicted the poorness of the remote village in vivid detail.

Synonyms

  • poverty
    2.0 52/m
    poverty is the common term for long-term lack of money or goods, while poorness is a rarer, general name for the same state.
  • pennilessness
    6.8 0.01/m
    pennilessness means having no money at all, while poorness can include having little money or few possessions.
  • impoverishment
    6.8 1.19/m
    impoverishment describes the process of becoming poor, while poorness names the state of having little money or few possessions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • antonym: wealth - the state of being rich, having a lot of money and possessions
