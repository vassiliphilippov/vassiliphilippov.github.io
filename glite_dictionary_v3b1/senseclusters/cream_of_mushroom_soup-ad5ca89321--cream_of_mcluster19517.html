cream of mushroom soup

noun
a creamy soup with mushrooms
Pronunciation: / kriːm əv ˈmʌʃruːm suːp / (AmE), / kriːm əv ˈmʌʃrəm suːp / (BrE)
A soup made with a base of cream or milk, thickened and flavored with mushrooms and sometimes mushroom broth.

Examples

A cream of mushroom soup bowl
Many casseroles use cream of mushroom soup for added flavor and creaminess.
She prefers homemade cream of mushroom soup to the canned variety.
The recipe calls for one can of cream of mushroom soup as the base.
