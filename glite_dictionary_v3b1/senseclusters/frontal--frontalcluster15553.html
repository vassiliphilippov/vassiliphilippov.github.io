frontal

noun
altar cloth used in churches
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.təl / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.təl / (BrE)
a decorative drapery or cloth covering the front of an altar in a church

Examples

Altar frontal
A velvet frontal adorned with gold thread covered the front of the sanctuary's altar.
The church committee ordered a new frontal to match the recently restored altar.
The richly embroidered frontal was changed for each season of the church year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altar cloth - a cloth covering an altar, often used in religious ceremonies
