grownup

adjective
fully developed; adult (of animal/person)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊn.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊn.ʌp / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) fully developed; adult.

Examples

Grownup dog
After several years with the family, the puppy is finally a grownup dog.
Caterpillars turn into grownup butterflies after their transformation.
The lion is now a grownup male and leads his own pride.
