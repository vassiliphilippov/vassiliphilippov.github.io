tea bread

noun
sweetened bun served with tea
Pronunciation: / tiː brɛd / (AmE), / tiː brɛd / (BrE)
A sweetened bread or bun, often containing dried fruit, typically served with tea.

Examples

A tea bread with raisins
Her homemade tea bread is always packed with raisins and spices.
The café serves freshly baked tea bread alongside their tea selection.
We enjoyed slices of tea bread with butter during the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruitcake - a cake containing dried fruit, nuts, and spices
