holistic medicine

noun
medical care considering whole person

Pronunciation: /hoʊ.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmed.ə.sɪn/ /; / /hoʊ.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmed.ɪ.sɪn/ /; / /hə.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmed.ə.sɪn/ (AmE), /həʊ.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmed.ɪ.sɪn/ /; / /həʊ.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmed.sɪn/ (BrE)
