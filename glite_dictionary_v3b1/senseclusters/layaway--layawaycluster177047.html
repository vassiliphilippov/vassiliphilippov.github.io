layaway

noun
installment payment plan
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.ə.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.ə.weɪ/ (BrE)
a system in which a customer pays for an item in installments and receives it after the full amount is paid
