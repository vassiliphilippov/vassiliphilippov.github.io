Lockean

adjective
of John Locke's ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːkiən / (AmE), / ˈlɒkiən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas or philosophy of John Locke

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: Hobbesian - relating to the ideas of Thomas Hobbes, often emphasizing strong government authority
  • synonym: Locke - English philosopher known for his theories on government and property
  • synonym: empiricist - relating to the theory that knowledge comes from experience
  • antonym: rationalist - relating to the theory that reason is the main source of knowledge
