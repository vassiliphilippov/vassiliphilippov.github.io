Crescentia cujete

noun
tropical tree producing large round gourds
Pronunciation: / krəˈsɛnʃə kuˈheɪti / (AmE), / krɛˈsɛnʃiə kjuːˈjeɪtiː / (BrE)
A tropical American evergreen tree that produces large, round gourds; commonly known as the calabash tree.

Examples

The Crescentia cujete tree
A Crescentia cujete tree can be recognized by its distinctive round fruits hanging from its branches.
The Crescentia cujete is widely cultivated for its large, sturdy gourds used to make containers.
The bark and fruit of Crescentia cujete are valued in traditional medicine across many cultures.
