crucifix fish

noun
a Caribbean sea catfish with a cross-shaped bone
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːsəˌfɪks fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkruːsɪfɪks fɪʃ / (BrE)
a sea catfish of the Caribbean region, known for the bony cross-shaped structure found in its head
