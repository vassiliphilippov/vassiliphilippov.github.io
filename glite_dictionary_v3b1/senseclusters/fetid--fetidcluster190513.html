fetid

adjective
having a strong, unpleasant smell
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛtɪd /; / ˈfiːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪd / (BrE)
smelling extremely unpleasant; having a strong, offensive odor.

Examples

A fetid smell
Rotting food left in the sun soon becomes fetid and attracts flies.
The abandoned building was filled with a fetid odor that made everyone wrinkle their noses.
The swamp released a fetid stench in the heat of summer.
A foetid pool of stagnant water stood behind the barn.
His boots were covered with foetid mud from the swamp.
The air in the abandoned house was thick with a foetid stench.
