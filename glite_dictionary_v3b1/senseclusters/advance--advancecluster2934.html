advance

noun
increase in price or value
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
an increase in price, value, or amount, especially in the context of finance or commerce

Examples

Advance in prices
A sudden advance in oil prices impacted the entire economy.
Analysts predicted another advance in housing market prices this quarter.
The company reported a steady advance in their share value over the year.
