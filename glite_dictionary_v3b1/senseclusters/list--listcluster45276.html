Synonyms

  • tilt
    2.0 7/m
    tilt means move into a sloping position, often by force or action, while list implies an unplanned lean.
  • lean
    1.5 19/m
    lean means to rest at an angle or bend intentionally for support, while list implies an accidental side tilt.
  • slant
    3.7 1/m

  • tip
