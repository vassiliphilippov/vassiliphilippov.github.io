swarm

noun
group of cooperating devices or agents
7.1
Pronunciation: /swɔːrm/ (AmE), /swɔːm/ (BrE)
(in computing and robotics) a decentralized, self-organizing group of devices or autonomous agents that coordinate to share data, resources, or carry out tasks together.

Examples

A swarm of robots
A swarm of BitTorrent peers shared pieces of the file until everyone's download completed.
A swarm of drones circled the stadium, coordinating to monitor the crowd during the concert.
Engineers deployed a swarm of sensors that self-organized to track temperature across the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cluster - a group of computers or devices that work together or are linked for a common purpose
  • synonym: flock - a group moving or acting together; similar figurative sense for cooperating agents
