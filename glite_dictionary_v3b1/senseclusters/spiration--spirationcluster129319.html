spiration

noun
Holy Spirit procession
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / spaɪˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the theological concept of the Holy Spirit proceeding from the Father, or from the Father and the Son, in Christian doctrine

Examples

Spiration of the Holy Spirit
The doctrine of the spiration of the Holy Spirit is central to many theological debates.
He wrote his thesis on the concept of spiration in Western and Eastern Christianity.
The council discussed the differences in understanding spiration between various Christian traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procession - the act of coming forth, especially in a theological context
