bench clamp

noun
clamp for securing work on a workbench
5.9 4.3
Pronunciation: /bentʃ klæmp/ (AmE), /bentʃ klæmp/ (BrE)
a clamp designed to hold objects securely in place on a workbench during tasks such as sawing, drilling, or assembling

Examples

A bench clamp holds wood
Before drilling, make sure your workpiece is firmly held by a bench clamp.
She used a bench clamp to hold the metal sheet steady.
The carpenter tightened the bench clamp to secure the wood before cutting it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clamp - a device used to hold objects tightly together
