yellowish brown

noun
medium to dark brown with a yellow tint
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊɪʃ braʊn / (AmE), / ˈjɛlɪwɪʃ braʊn / (BrE)
a color that is a medium to dark shade of brown with a noticeable yellow tint

Examples

A yellowish brown color
As autumn arrived, the leaves turned a yellowish brown before falling to the ground.
The antique desk was finished in a deep yellowish brown that complemented the room's decor.
The artist mixed paints to achieve the perfect yellowish brown for the portrait's background.
