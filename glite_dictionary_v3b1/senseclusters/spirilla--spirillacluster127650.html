spirilla

noun
spiral-shaped bacteria (plural)
Pronunciation: / spɪˈrɪlə / (AmE), / spɪˈrɪlə / (BrE)
Plural of spirillum; any flagellated, aerobic bacteria with a spiral, rod-like shape.

Examples

The spirilla bacteria
Some spirilla are found in stagnant water and are known for their distinctive spiral shapes.
The presence of spirilla in a sample can indicate poor water sanitation.
Under the microscope, the microbiologist noticed several spirilla moving with their flagella.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirillum - a single spiral-shaped, flagellated bacterium
