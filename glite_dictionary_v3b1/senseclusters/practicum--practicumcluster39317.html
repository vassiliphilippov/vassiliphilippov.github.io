practicum

noun
supervised practical training
7.0
Pronunciation: /præk.ˈtɪ.kəm/ (AmE), /præk.ˈtɪ.kəm/ (BrE)
a supervised practical experience or course for students, usually as part of their training or education

Examples

A practicum course
During her final year, she completed a teaching practicum at a local elementary school.
The nursing program requires a clinical practicum before students can graduate.
He gained valuable experience through his practicum in a community health center.
As part of her education degree, she completed a teaching practicum at a local elementary school.
The psychology program requires every student to participate in a clinical practicum before graduation.
During his nursing practicum, he learned valuable skills by working directly with patients.
