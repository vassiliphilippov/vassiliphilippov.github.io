fussbudget

noun
person who complains a lot
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌsˌbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌsˌbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
a person who worries or complains a lot about small or unimportant things

Examples

A fussbudget about food
My little brother can be such a fussbudget about his food.
No one wants to travel with a fussbudget who complains about every little detail.
She was called a fussbudget because she always worried about the smallest things.
My aunt is a real fussbudget when it comes to cleaning the house.
Don't be such a fussbudget about the seating arrangements at the party.
He earned a reputation as a fussbudget because he always pointed out tiny mistakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easygoing - relaxed and not easily upset or worried
  • synonym: fusspot - a person who fusses or worries about unimportant things
  • synonym: worrywart - a person who worries excessively
