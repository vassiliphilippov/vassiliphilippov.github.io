closing off

noun
the act of isolating by blocking access
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzɪŋ ɔf / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ ɒf / (BrE)
the act or process of isolating something by preventing access or separating it from others

Examples

The closing off of areas
Construction workers are responsible for the closing off of the unsafe areas.
The closing off of the main road caused long delays for commuters.
The sudden closing off of communication made negotiations difficult.
