shopman

noun
male shop worker
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpmæn / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpmæn /; / ˈʃɒpmən / (BrE)
a man who works in or manages a shop, especially one who serves customers (often used in retail stores; can be old-fashioned)

Examples

Friendly shopman
The shopman greeted every customer with a friendly smile as they entered the store.
After years as a shopman, he knew exactly what his regular customers liked.
A helpful shopman quickly found the item I was looking for on the crowded shelf.
The shopman arranged the new products neatly on the shelves before opening.
A friendly shopman helped me find the right size for my shoes.
In Victorian times, a shopman was expected to be polite and attentive to every customer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services
  • synonym: salesman - a man whose job is to sell products
  • synonym: shop assistant - a person who serves customers in a shop
  • antonym: shopwoman - a woman who works in or manages a shop
