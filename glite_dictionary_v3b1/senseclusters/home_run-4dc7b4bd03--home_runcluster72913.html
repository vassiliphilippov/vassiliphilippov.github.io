home run

noun
outstanding success or achievement
5.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈrʌn / (AmE), / həʊm ˈrʌn / (BrE)
A result or action that is outstandingly successful or achieves its intended objective.

Examples

A home run idea
Her suggestion turned out to be a home run for the team, solving many problems at once.
The company's latest product launch was a home run with both critics and customers.
The new policy is a home run for improving workplace efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bull's eye - an achievement that precisely hits the goal; perfect success
  • synonym: hit the mark - to achieve exactly what was intended or aimed for
  • antonym: miss the mark - to fail to achieve the intended result
