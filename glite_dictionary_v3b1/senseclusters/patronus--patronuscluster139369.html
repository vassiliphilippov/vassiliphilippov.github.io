patronus

noun
magical protective charm
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊnəs / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊnəs / (BrE)
a magical protective charm in the Harry Potter universe that repels dark creatures such as Dementors

Examples

Cast a patronus charm
Harry was able to produce a powerful patronus during his third year at Hogwarts.
The patronus took the shape of a silver stag, shining brightly in the darkness.
Only skilled witches and wizards can cast a patronus to protect themselves from Dementors.
