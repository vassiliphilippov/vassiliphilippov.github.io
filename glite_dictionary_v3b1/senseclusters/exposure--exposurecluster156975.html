exposure

noun
vulnerability to weather conditions

Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈspoʊ.ʒər/ /; / /ɪk.ˈspəʊ.ʒər/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈspəʊ.ʒə/ (BrE)
the condition of being unprotected from severe weather or environmental conditions, such as wind, rain, cold, or heat
