spectrophotometrically

adverb
using spectrophotometry
Pronunciation: / ˌspɛktroʊˌfoʊtəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌspɛktrəʊˌfəʊtəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
by means of spectrophotometry, using a method that measures how much light a substance absorbs

Examples

Measured spectrophotometrically in lab
The concentration of the dye was measured spectrophotometrically to ensure accuracy in the experiment.
Researchers analyzed the protein samples spectrophotometrically to determine their purity.
The results were obtained spectrophotometrically after the solution was exposed to light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analytically - by means of analysis or using analytical methods
