phonorecord

noun
sound recording object
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊnoʊˌrɛkɔrd /; / ˈfoʊnoʊˌrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnəʊˌrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
