secretly

adverb
in a hidden or private manner
1.4 13/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀê.kr…ôt.li /; / ÀàsiÀêkr…ôtli / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀê.kr…™t.li /; / ÀàsiÀêkr…ôtli / (BrE)
in a way that is hidden from others; not meant to be seen or known

Examples

To secretly plan
He secretly planned a surprise party for his friend.
She secretly hoped to win the competition, though she didn't show it.
The two met secretly to discuss their future plans.
He secretly slipped the letter into her bag without anyone noticing.
She secretly dreamed of traveling the world someday.
The two leaders met secretly in a remote location.
