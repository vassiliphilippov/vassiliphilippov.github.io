underinsured

adjective
insufficiently insured
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərɪnˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ˌʌndərɪnˈʃʊəd / (BrE)
not having enough insurance to cover potential losses or needs

Examples

Underinsured property
Many homeowners are underinsured and risk large out-of-pocket expenses.
After the flood, they realized their business was underinsured and could not recover all the damages.
It is important to review your policy regularly to avoid being underinsured.
