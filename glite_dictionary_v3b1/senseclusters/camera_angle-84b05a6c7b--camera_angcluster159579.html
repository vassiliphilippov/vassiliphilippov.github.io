camera angle

noun
angle or position from which a camera captures a subject
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmərə ˌæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈkæmərə ˌæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
the position from which a camera views its subject, affecting what is seen and how it is perceived in a photograph or film

Examples

A camera angle change
Changing the camera angle can completely alter how the audience perceives a character.
She experimented with different camera angles to make her photographs more interesting.
The movie's dramatic effect was enhanced by the creative camera angle chosen for the action scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perspective - the angle or position from which something is considered or shown
  • synonym: viewpoint - a place from where something is viewed or considered
