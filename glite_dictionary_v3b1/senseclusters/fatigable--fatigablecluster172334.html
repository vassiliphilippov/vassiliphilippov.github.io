fatigable

adjective
easily weakened
9.1
Pronunciation: / fəˈtiːɡəbəl / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡəbəl / (BrE)
becoming weak or losing strength after repeated use or stimulation, especially in a medical context

Examples

Fatigable muscles
The patient's fatigable muscles became weaker after several minutes of exercise.
Doctors observed a fatigable response in the patient's arm during repeated strength tests.
A fatigable condition can make daily activities much more difficult for some individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatigued - tired or exhausted
  • antonym: resistant - not easily affected or weakened
