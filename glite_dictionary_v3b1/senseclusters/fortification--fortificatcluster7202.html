fortification

noun
defensive structure built for protection
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a defensive structure, such as walls or mounds, built to protect a place or strengthen its defenses

Examples

Stone fortification
During the tour, we walked along the old fortifications and learned about their history.
Engineers worked tirelessly to repair the damaged fortification before the enemy could attack again.
The ancient city was protected by massive fortifications that still stand today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bastion - a projecting part of a fortification
  • synonym: rampart - a defensive wall of a castle or walled city
  • antonym: siege - a military blockade of a fortified place
