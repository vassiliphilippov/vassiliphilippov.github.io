heeltip

noun
replaceable bottom of a shoe heel
1.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈhiːl.tɪp/ (AmE), /ˈhiːl.tɪp/ (BrE)
the bottom piece of a shoe or boot heel, typically made of plastic or metal, that comes into contact with the ground and can be replaced when worn
