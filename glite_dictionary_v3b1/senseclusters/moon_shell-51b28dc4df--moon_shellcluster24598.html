moon shell

noun
marine snail with a smooth, rounded shell
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmun ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈmuːn ʃɛl / (BrE)
a marine gastropod mollusc with a smooth, rounded shell that forms a short spire, typically found in sandy coastal waters

Examples

A moon shell on beach
Children marveled at the glossy blue-grey moon shell nestled among the rocks.
Many moon shells are prized for their polished, spiraled appearance in shell collections.
The collector found a beautiful moon shell on the sandy beach at low tide.
