nonpreferential

adjective
without preference
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəl / (BrE)
not giving preference or advantage to any particular person, group, or option

Examples

A nonpreferential process
The university adopted a nonpreferential admissions process to ensure fairness for all applicants.
A nonpreferential approach was used to allocate resources equally among the teams.
Their nonpreferential treatment of customers helped build a reputation for fairness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impartial - treating all people or groups equally; fair
  • synonym: neutral - not supporting or favoring any side
  • antonym: preferential - giving advantage or preference to some over others
