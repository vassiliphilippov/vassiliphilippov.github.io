keystone

noun
central, supporting element
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkiːstəʊn / (BrE)
the most important part of a system, organization, or plan, supporting or holding together all the other parts

Examples

Keystone of success
Education has always been the keystone of their community’s progress.
Her leadership became the keystone that held the project together.
Trust is the keystone of any successful partnership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cornerstone - an important quality or feature on which a particular thing depends or is based
  • synonym: linchpin - a person or thing vital to an enterprise or organization
