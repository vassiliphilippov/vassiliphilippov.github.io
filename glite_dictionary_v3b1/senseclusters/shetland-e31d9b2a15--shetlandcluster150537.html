Shetland

noun
breed of small sheepdog from Shetland Islands
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛt.lənd / (AmE), / ˈʃɛt.lənd / (BrE)
A small breed of sheepdog, resembling a collie, originally developed in the Shetland Islands of Scotland.

Examples

A Shetland dog
A Shetland makes a loyal family companion with its gentle and friendly nature.
The Shetland is known for its intelligence and agility in herding sheep.
They have owned several Shetlands over the years and compete in dog shows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Shetland Sheepdog - another name for Shetland, the breed
  • synonym: collie - a related breed of sheepdog, larger in size
