unbarreled

adjective
not in a barrel
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbærəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈbarəld /; / ʌnˈbærəld / (BrE)
Not contained in or transferred to a barrel.

Examples

Unbarreled olives
Olives can be packed unbarreled for direct sale at markets.
Some craft beers are sold unbarreled to preserve their fresh flavor.
The distiller offered both barreled and unbarreled versions of their whiskey.
Freshly squeezed juice is often sold unbarrelled for immediate consumption.
The unbarrelled oil proved more susceptible to contamination.
The unbarrelled wine was stored in glass containers instead of wooden barrels.
