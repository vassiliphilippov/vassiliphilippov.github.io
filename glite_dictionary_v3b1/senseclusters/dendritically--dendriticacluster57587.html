dendritically

adverb
in a branching manner
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈdrɪtɪkli / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles or relates to branching structures like dendrites.

Examples

Spread dendritically on glass
The minerals spread dendritically across the glass, creating intricate patterns.
Some neurons connect dendritically to form complex networks in the brain.
Frost formed dendritically on the window, resembling tiny tree branches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branchingly - in a way that forms branches
