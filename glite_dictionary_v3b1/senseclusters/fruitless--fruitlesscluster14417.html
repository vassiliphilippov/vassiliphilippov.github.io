fruitless

adjective
unsuccessful; unproductive
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfruːt.ləs/ /; / /ˈfrʊt.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈfruːt.ləs/ /; / /ˈfrʊt.ləs/ (BrE)

Examples

She made one last fruitless attempt to persuade him to stay.
Their efforts to improve the system proved fruitless despite months of discussion.
