depreciative

adjective
expressing criticism or making something seem less valuable
2.6 6.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpriː.ʃə.tɪv/ /; / /də.ˈpriː.ʃə.tɪv/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpriː.ʃə.tɪv/ /; / /də.ˈpriː.ʃə.tɪv/ (BrE)
