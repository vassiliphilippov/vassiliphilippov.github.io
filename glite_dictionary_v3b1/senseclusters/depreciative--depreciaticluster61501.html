depreciative

adjective
expressing criticism or making something seem less valuable
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpriʃətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈpriːʃətɪv / (BrE)
tending to diminish the value, worth, or importance of someone or something; expressing criticism or disapproval.

Examples

Depreciative comments
He was tired of her depreciative remarks about his achievements.
Her depreciative tone made the criticism sound harsher than it really was.
Many of the reviews took a depreciative view of the film's originality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approving - showing approval or positive regard
  • synonym: belittling - making something or someone seem unimportant
  • synonym: deprecatory - expressing disapproval
