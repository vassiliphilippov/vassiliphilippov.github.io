angwantibo

noun
West African primate with golden fur
9.9
Pronunciation: / æŋˈwɑntiboʊ / (AmE), / æŋˈwɒntɪbəʊ / (BrE)
A small, arboreal primate of the species Arctocebus calabarensis, native to West Africa, known for its yellow or golden fur.
