slip

noun
liquid clay for ceramics
4.3
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
A liquid mixture of clay and water used for coating, decorating, or joining ceramics.

Examples

Ceramic slip
Before firing, she brushed slip over the vase to give it a smooth finish.
Colored slip can be used to create intricate patterns on ceramic tiles.
The potter applied a thin layer of slip to decorate the bowl.
