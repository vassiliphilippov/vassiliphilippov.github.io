egocentrism

noun
self-centeredness; focus on oneself
8.8 0.26/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåiÀê…°o äÀàs…õntr…™z…ôm / (AmE), / ÀåiÀê…°…ô äÀàs…õntr…™z…ôm / (BrE)
The state of regarding oneself and one's own interests as the most important or central, often to the exclusion or neglect of others' perspectives or needs.

Examples

Egocentrism in thinking
His decisions were often driven by egocentrism rather than consideration for the team.
In psychological development, egocentrism often declines as empathy grows.
The child's egocentrism made it hard for her to understand her friend's feelings.

Synonyms

    5.8 0.25/m
    self-centeredness focuses on one's personal wants and needs in daily life, while egocentrism ignores others' perspectives.
  • self-absorption
    8.1 0.54/m
    self-absorption means being lost in your own thoughts and feelings, while egocentrism makes you ignore others' views.
  • selfishness
    3.0 3.85/m
    selfishness is acting for your own advantage without concern for others, while egocentrism centers on ignoring others' viewpoints.
  • egoism
    7.2 0.35/m
    egoism treats looking after your own interest as a guiding rule, while egocentrism is a mental habit of seeing yourself as central.
  • self-interest
    4.0 5.54/m
    self-interest means choosing what helps you most in a situation, while egocentrism makes you dismiss other people's needs or views.
