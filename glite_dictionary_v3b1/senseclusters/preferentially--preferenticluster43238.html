preferentially

adverb
in a preferential manner
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that gives preference or advantage to someone or something over others.

Examples

Considered preferentially for scholarships
Applicants from local schools will be considered preferentially for the scholarship.
In some societies, sons are preferentially treated over daughters.
The new system processes urgent requests preferentially to reduce wait times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: equally - in an equal manner, without preference
