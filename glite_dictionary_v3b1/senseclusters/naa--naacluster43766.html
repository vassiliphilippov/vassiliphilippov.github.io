naa

noun
female chief title
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑː / (AmE), / nɑː / (BrE)
a title given to a female chief or queen mother in certain West African cultures

Examples

The village naa
The new naa was honored with a traditional ceremony attended by the entire village.
As naa, she played an important role in resolving community disputes.
The elders consulted the naa before making any major decisions for the town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chief - the leader or head of a group or community
  • synonym: queen mother - a female leader or respected elder woman in some African societies
