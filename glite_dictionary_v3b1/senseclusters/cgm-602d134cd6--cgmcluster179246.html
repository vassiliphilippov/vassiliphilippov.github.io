CGM

noun
blood glucose monitor
Pronunciation: / si dʒi ɛm / (AmE), / siː dʒiː ɛm / (BrE)
a device that continuously monitors and records blood glucose levels in real time

Examples

Wear a CGM device
Her doctor recommended using a CGM to better manage her diabetes.
A CGM can alert users when their blood sugar is too high or too low.
Many people with type 1 diabetes rely on a CGM for daily monitoring.
