blesbok

noun
South African antelope
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛzˌbɑk / (AmE), / ˈblɛzˌbɒk / (BrE)
a South African antelope with a white face and a brown body, found in open grasslands

Examples

A blesbok antelope
A herd of blesbok moved gracefully across the South African plains.
The distinctive white face of the blesbok makes it easy to identify among other antelopes.
Wildlife photographers often travel to see the blesbok in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antelope - a grazing animal with slender legs and horns, found in Africa and Asia
  • synonym: springbok - a small, fast antelope native to southern Africa
