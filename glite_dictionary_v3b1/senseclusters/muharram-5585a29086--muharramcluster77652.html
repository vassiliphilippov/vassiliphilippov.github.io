Muharram

noun
first month of the Islamic calendar
7.6
Pronunciation: / muːˈhɑːrəm / (AmE), / mʊˈhærəm / (BrE)
the first month of the Islamic calendar, considered sacred by Muslims

Examples

The month Muharram
Ashura, a significant religious observance, takes place on the tenth day of Muharram.
During Muharram, some communities hold special gatherings and processions to commemorate historical events.
The Islamic New Year begins with Muharram, marking an important time for many Muslims around the world.
