harp

verb
play the harp (musical instrument)
Pronunciation: / hɑrp / (AmE), / hɑːp / (BrE)
to play the harp, a large musical instrument with strings stretched vertically in a frame

Examples

To harp a melody
During the recital, she harped a beautiful melody that enchanted the audience.
He harped at weddings for many years before joining the orchestra.
I watched as the musician skillfully harped on stage during the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perform - to present or carry out (music, a play, etc.) before an audience
  • synonym: strum - to play a stringed instrument by brushing the strings
