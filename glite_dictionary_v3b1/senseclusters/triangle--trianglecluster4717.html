triangle

noun
metal percussion instrument shaped like a triangle
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪæŋɡl / (BrE)
a percussion instrument made from a metal bar bent into an open triangle, which is struck with a metal beater to produce a clear, ringing tone

Examples

A metal triangle
Children often enjoy playing simple tunes on the triangle during music class.
The percussionist tapped the triangle at the start of the piece, adding a bright sound to the music.
When cued, she struck the triangle lightly and filled the hall with a shimmering note.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tambourine - a small percussion instrument with a frame and jingles, often played by shaking or hitting
