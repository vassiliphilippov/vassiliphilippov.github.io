bloody nose

noun
bleeding from the nose; figurative defeat
3.3 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈblʌd.iː noʊz/ /; / /ˈblʌd.ɪ noʊz/ (AmE), /ˈblʌd.iː nəʊz/ /; / /ˈblʌd.ɪ nəʊz/ (BrE)
