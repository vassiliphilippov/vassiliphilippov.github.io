battle-tested strategy

noun
method proven effective through experience
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɾlˌtɛstɪd ˈstrætədʒi / (AmE), / ˈbætlˌtɛstɪd ˈstrætədʒi / (BrE)
a method or plan that has been proven effective through experience, especially in challenging situations

Examples

A battle-tested strategy
During the negotiations, they used a battle-tested strategy to gain an advantage.
She trusts only battle-tested strategies when managing a crisis.
The company relied on its battle-tested strategy to navigate the competitive market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tried and tested strategy - a method that has been shown to work well after being used many times
  • antonym: unproven strategy - a strategy that has not been tested or demonstrated as effective
