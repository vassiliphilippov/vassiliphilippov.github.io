horse cart

noun
a cart drawn by a horse
Pronunciation: / hɔrs kɑrt / (AmE), / hɔːs kɑːt / (BrE)
a heavy cart pulled by a horse, typically used for farm work or transporting goods

Examples

A horse cart on farm
Before tractors became common, a horse cart was often used for heavy lifting on the farm.
Children watched as the old horse cart rattled down the country lane.
The farmer loaded the fresh vegetables into his horse cart for the trip to market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cart - a vehicle with either two or four wheels that is pushed or pulled and used for carrying things
