Quinisext Council

noun
692 church council
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɪnɪˈsɛkst ˈkaʊnsəl / (AmE), / ˌkwɪnɪˈsɛkst ˈkaʊnsəl / (BrE)
an ecumenical church council held in 692 AD in Constantinople that established important rules (canons) for the Eastern Orthodox Church
