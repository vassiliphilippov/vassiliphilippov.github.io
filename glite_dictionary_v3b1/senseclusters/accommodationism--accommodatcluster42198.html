accommodationism

noun
policy of compromise
9.3
Pronunciation: /ə.kə.mə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ə.kɒ.mə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a policy or attitude of making compromises to maintain peace or avoid conflict

Examples

Accommodationism in politics
The leader's accommodationism helped prevent a major dispute between the two groups.
Critics argued that accommodationism would only encourage further demands from the opposition.
Some praised his accommodationism as a way to keep the peace in a divided community.
