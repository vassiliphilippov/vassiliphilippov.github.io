sneeze

noun
involuntary expulsion of air from the nose
1.0 1.33/m
Pronunciation: / sniz / (AmE), / sniÀêz / (BrE)
An involuntary expulsion of air from the nose and mouth, usually caused by irritation of the nasal passages.

Examples

A loud sneeze
A sudden sneeze can spread germs in the air.
He let out a loud sneeze during the meeting, startling everyone.
If you have a cold, you may get a sneeze every few minutes.
