reinspection

noun
second inspection
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈspɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈspɛkʃən / (BrE)
a second or repeated examination or inspection of something to ensure standards are met

Examples

A reinspection of safety
After the repairs were completed, a reinspection was scheduled to confirm the building's safety.
The health department conducted a reinspection of the restaurant following the initial violations.
A reinspection is often required if a vehicle fails its first emissions test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspection - a careful examination or check
