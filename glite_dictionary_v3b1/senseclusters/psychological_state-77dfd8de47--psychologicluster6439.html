psychological state

noun
condition of a person's mind or emotions
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl steɪt / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl steɪt / (BrE)
a person's condition or situation in terms of thoughts, feelings, and mental processes, especially as stable over a period

Examples

A stable psychological state
After her recovery, her psychological state greatly improved.
Meditation can help you achieve a more balanced psychological state.
The therapist assessed his psychological state before recommending treatment.
