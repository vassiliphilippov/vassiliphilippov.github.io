barbacoa

noun
meat-cooking platform
9.2
Pronunciation: /bɑːr.bə.ˈkoʊ.ə/ /; / /bɑːr.bə.ˈkoʊ/ (AmE), /bɑː.bə.ˈkəʊ.ə/ /; / /bɑː.bə.ˈkəʊ/ (BrE)
