barbering

noun
hair cutting profession
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbərɪŋ / (BrE)
the job or activity of cutting hair and grooming beards, especially as a profession

Examples

Professional barbering

Modern barbering includes both traditional and contemporary hair styles.
She opened her own shop after years of studying barbering.
