Examples

Many citizens registered to vote for the first time this year.

Synonyms

  • enroll
    2.7 29/m
    enroll is used when people join a course or program officially, while register means to record or list someone with authority.
  • enter
    1.1 41/m
    enter is to put data or a name into a list or system, while register emphasizes official recording with an authority.
  • sign up
    1.3 20/m
    sign up is an informal way to agree to join an activity, while register is the formal act of recording membership with authorities.
