hurrying

adjective
moving quickly; in a rush
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜriɪŋ, ˈhʌriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌriɪŋ, ˈhɜːriɪŋ / (BrE)
moving quickly or with great haste

Examples

Hurrying waiter

She walked past the group of hurrying commuters, barely noticing the rush.
The hurrying footsteps in the hallway signaled that the meeting was about to start.
