Synonyms

  • shiver
    1.2 5/m
    shiver is an uncontrolled shaking from cold or fear, while quiver is a slight, rapid trembling often caused by strong feeling.
  • vibrate
    2.8 4/m
    vibrate moves quickly back and forth, often for objects or sounds, while quiver is a small, rapid tremble in living things.
  • flutter
    3.5 2/m
    flutter makes quick, light flapping movements like wings or eyelids, while quiver is a finer trembling of body parts.
