saffian

noun
fine goatskin leather
Pronunciation: / ˈsæfiən / (AmE), / ˈsæfiən / (BrE)
a fine, soft leather made from goatskin, often used for bookbinding or luxury items

Examples

Saffian leather cover
The antique book was covered in rich saffian that had aged beautifully over time.
She admired the wallet crafted from smooth saffian in the boutique window.
Collectors value notebooks bound in genuine saffian for their durability and elegance.
