rite

noun
formal or ceremonial act or practice
2.8 6.3
Pronunciation: / raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
a formal or ceremonial act, especially as part of a religious or solemn tradition

Examples

A rite of passage
Lighting candles is an important rite in many religious traditions.
The community gathered annually to perform the ancient harvest rite.
The graduation ceremony was conducted as a rite of passage for the students.
Many cultures have a coming-of-age rite to mark the transition to adulthood.
The church holds a special rite for new members each spring.
The wedding rite included several traditional blessings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremony - a formal religious or public occasion, typically one celebrating a particular event
  • synonym: ritual - a religious or solemn ceremony consisting of a series of actions performed according to a prescribed order
