Synonyms

  • two-piece
    3.2 0.04/m
    two-piece is the term for a musical act of two performers, while duo describes any pair who perform together.
  • twosome
    3.2 0.5/m
    twosome refers to any pair of people doing things together, while duo usually means a pair who perform or work as partners.
