triangle

noun
three-sided polygon
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌæŋɡəl /; / ˈtraɪ.æŋ.ɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.æŋ.ɡl̩ / (BrE)
a flat shape or object with three straight sides and three angles; anything shaped like a triangle

Examples

A red triangle

You can cut the sandwich into a triangle for easier eating.
The layout of the garden paths creates a rough triangle in the center.
The three mountain peaks formed a natural triangle on the horizon.
We arranged the tables in a triangle to fit more guests in the room.
