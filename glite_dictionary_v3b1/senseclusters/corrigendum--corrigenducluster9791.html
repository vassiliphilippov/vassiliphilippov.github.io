corrigendum

noun
a printer's error needing correction
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrɪˈdʒɛndəm /; / ˌkɔrɪˈdʒɛndəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒrɪˈdʒɛndəm / (BrE)
an error in a published work that is formally corrected, or the official notice of such a correction

Readers should check the corrigendum for updates to the scientific article.

  • synonym: erratum - an error in printing or writing, especially one noted in a published list
