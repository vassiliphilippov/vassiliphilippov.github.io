carbonate

verb
to make into a carbonate
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.bəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.bə.neɪt / (BrE)
to turn (a substance) into a carbonate by combining it with carbon dioxide or a carbonate compound

Examples

To carbonate a mineral
Certain minerals can carbonate when exposed to carbon dioxide over time.
Chemists often carbonate samples during laboratory experiments.
The factory will carbonate the water to produce sparkling beverages.
