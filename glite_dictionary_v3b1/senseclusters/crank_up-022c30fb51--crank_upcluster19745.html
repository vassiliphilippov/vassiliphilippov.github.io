crank up

verb
start an engine by cranking
6.5 3.6
Pronunciation: /kræŋk ʌp/ (AmE), /kræŋk ʌp/ (BrE)
to start a machine or engine by turning a handle or crank

Examples

To crank up the engine
He cranked up the old lawnmower and hoped it would run.
The mechanic cranked up the engine to check if the repairs worked.
We crank up the generator every morning during the power outage.
He had to crank up the old tractor before it would start.
She watched him crank up the generator during the power outage.
The mechanic cranked up the engine manually when the battery died.
