shotgun shack

noun
narrow, single-story house with rooms in a row
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑtˌɡʌn ʃæk / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtɡʌn ʃæk / (BrE)
a narrow, single-story house, typically with rooms arranged one behind another and no hallway, common in the southern United States

Examples
