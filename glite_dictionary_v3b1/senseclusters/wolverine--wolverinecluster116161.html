wolverine

noun
large, stocky carnivorous mammal
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊl.vɚˌin /; / ˈwʊlvəˌrin / (AmE), / ˈwʊl.vəriːn /; / ˈwʊlvəriːn / (BrE)
