smallness

noun
state of having little strength
3.7 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsm…îln…ôs / (AmE), / Ààsm…îÀêln…ôs / (BrE)
The quality or state of having little strength, force, or vigor.

Examples

Smallness of strength
He commented on the smallness of the engine, which limited the car's performance.
The smallness of her voice made it hard to hear her in the crowded room.
The smallness of his efforts did not go unnoticed by his team.
