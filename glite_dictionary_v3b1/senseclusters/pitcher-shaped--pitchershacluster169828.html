pitcher-shaped

adjective
in the shape of a pitcher
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃɚ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a pitcher, typically with a rounded body and a narrow neck or opening

Examples

A pitcher-shaped vase
Several antique teapots in the collection are pitcher-shaped and highly ornamented.
The pitcher-shaped leaves of the plant trap rainwater and insects.
The vase had a distinctively pitcher-shaped body that made it easy to pour liquids.
