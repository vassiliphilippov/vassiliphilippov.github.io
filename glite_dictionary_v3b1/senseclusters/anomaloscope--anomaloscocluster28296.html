anomaloscope

noun
color vision tester
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːmələˌskoʊp / (AmE), / əˈnɒmələˌskəʊp / (BrE)
a device used to test and diagnose color vision deficiencies by measuring how colors are perceived

Examples

An anomaloscope test
The optometrist used an anomaloscope to assess the patient's color vision.
An anomaloscope can help detect red-green color blindness in patients.
Researchers calibrated the anomaloscope before beginning the vision tests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colorimeter - an instrument for measuring the intensity or color of light
  • synonym: ophthalmoscope - an instrument for examining the interior of the eye
