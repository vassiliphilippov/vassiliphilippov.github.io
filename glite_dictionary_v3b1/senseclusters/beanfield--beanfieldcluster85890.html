beanfield

noun
field for beans
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːnˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈbiːnˌfiːld / (BrE)
a field where beans are cultivated as a crop

Examples

A beanfield of crops
The farmer walked through the beanfield to check the growth of the plants.
After the rain, the beanfield looked especially green and healthy.
They harvested beans from the large beanfield at the end of summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cornfield - a field where corn is grown
  • antonym: pasture - a field covered with grass for grazing animals
