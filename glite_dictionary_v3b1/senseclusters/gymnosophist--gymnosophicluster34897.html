gymnosophist

noun
member of ancient Hindu ascetic sect
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒɪm.nə.ˈsɑː.fɪst/ /; / /dʒɪm.nə.ˈsɔː.fɪst/ (AmE), /dʒɪm.nə.ˈsɒ.fɪst/ (BrE)
a member of an ancient Hindu philosophical sect that practiced asceticism and often nudism as part of their spiritual discipline

Examples

A gymnosophist ascetic
A gymnosophist lived a life of self-denial, often rejecting clothing and material possessions.
Ancient texts occasionally mention travelers who sought wisdom from a gymnosophist on the banks of the Ganges.
The Greek philosophers were fascinated by the practices of the gymnosophist sages of India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sage - a profoundly wise person, often from ancient times
