hirable

adjective
available for rent
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərəbl / (BrE)
available to be hired, rented, or leased for temporary use, especially referring to equipment or vehicles

Examples

Hirable car
