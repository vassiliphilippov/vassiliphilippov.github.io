overdecorated

adjective
excessively adorned
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdɛkəreɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Her apartment was overdecorated with trinkets and bright colors in every corner.
