overdecorated

adjective
excessively adorned
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdɛkəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
having too many decorations or being excessively adorned, often to the point of looking cluttered or gaudy

Examples

An overdecorated room
The hotel lobby was so overdecorated that it felt overwhelming rather than welcoming.
Some people find overdecorated cakes less appealing than simple, elegant ones.
Her apartment was overdecorated with trinkets and bright colors in every corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ornate - elaborately or highly decorated
  • antonym: understated - presented or decorated in a simple or restrained way
