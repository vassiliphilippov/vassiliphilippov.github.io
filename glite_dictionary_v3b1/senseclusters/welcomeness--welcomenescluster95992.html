welcomeness

noun
quality of being welcome
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəmnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being welcome or making others feel welcome

Examples

The welcomeness of community
The welcomeness of the community made new residents feel at home.
She appreciated the welcomeness shown by her colleagues on her first day.
His welcomeness was evident in the way he greeted every guest warmly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hospitality - friendly and generous reception of guests
  • antonym: unwelcomeness - the quality of not being welcome
