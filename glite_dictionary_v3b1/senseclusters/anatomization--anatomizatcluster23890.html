anatomization

noun
detailed analysis
Pronunciation: / əˌnætəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌnætəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of analyzing or dividing something into its parts to understand it in detail

Examples

The anatomization of parts
The anatomization of the company's financial records uncovered several irregularities.
Her anatomization of the novel helped students understand its deeper themes.
A careful anatomization of the argument revealed several logical flaws.
The anatomization of the report helped the team understand each section in detail.
Some critics argue that the anatomization of society leads to a loss of shared values.
After the anatomization of the company, communication between departments became more difficult.

  • synonym: analysis - the process of examining something in detail
  • synonym: dissection - the act of cutting apart or analyzing in detail
  • synonym: fragmentation - the process of breaking into smaller parts
  • antonym: synthesis - the combination of parts to form a whole
  • antonym: unification - the process of making things into a single unit
