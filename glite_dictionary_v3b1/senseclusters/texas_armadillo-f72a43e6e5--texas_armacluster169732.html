Texas armadillo

noun
species of armadillo with nine bands
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛksəs ˌɑːrməˈdɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs ˌɑːmədɪləʊ / (BrE)
a species of armadillo with nine hinged bands of bony plates, native to regions from Texas to Paraguay; also known as the nine-banded armadillo

Examples

Texas armadillos with nine bands
Texas armadillos are known for their distinctive armor-like bands.
The Texas armadillo can curl up into a ball as a defense mechanism.
We spotted a Texas armadillo crossing the road during our trip to southern Texas.
