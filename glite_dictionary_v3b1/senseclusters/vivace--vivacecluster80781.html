vivace

adjective, adverb
very fast and lively (musical tempo)
8.4
Pronunciation: / viˈvɑːtʃeɪ /; / viˈvɑː.tʃeɪ / (AmE), / vɪˈvɑːtʃeɪ /; / vɪˈvɑː.tʃeɪ / (BrE)
