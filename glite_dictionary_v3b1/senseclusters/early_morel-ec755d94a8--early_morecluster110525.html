early morel

noun
an edible spring mushroom with a wrinkled cap
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrli məˈrɛl / (AmE), / ˈɜːli məˈrɛl / (BrE)
A type of edible mushroom (Verpa bohemica) that appears in early spring, with a wrinkled, thimble-shaped cap extending down the stem.

Examples

The early morel mushroom
Foragers treasure the early morel for its delicate flavor and distinctive shape.
She was delighted to spot an early morel while walking in the woods in late March.
The early morel is often found along riverbanks before most trees have leafed out.
