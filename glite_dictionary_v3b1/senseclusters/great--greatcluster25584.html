great

adjective
excellent; of high quality
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt / (BrE)
Very good or excellent; of high quality or standard.

Examples

A great idea
She has a great sense of humor and always makes everyone laugh.
That was a great meal—thank you so much for cooking.
They had a great time exploring the city together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bad - of poor quality or a low standard
  • synonym: excellent - of the highest quality; extremely good
  • degree lower: good - to be desired or approved of; satisfactory
  • degree higher: outstanding - exceptionally good; standing out from others
