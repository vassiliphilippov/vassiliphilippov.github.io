gleaming

noun
a reflected bright or shining appearance
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡliːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡliːmɪŋ / (BrE)
a bright or shining appearance produced by reflected light

Examples

A gleaming of silver
A faint gleaming rose from the silver candlesticks in the corner.
He noticed a curious gleaming in her eyes as she told the story.
The gleaming of polished marble was visible even at dusk.
