knifing

noun
cutting with a knife
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪfɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or technique of cutting or moving with a knife, especially in a controlled or skillful way

Examples

Careful knifing of cake
Her careful knifing of the cake ensured each slice was perfectly even.
The chef demonstrated expert knifing skills while preparing the vegetables for the salad.
Accurate knifing is important when filleting fish to avoid wasting meat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chopping - the act of cutting something into pieces with repeated sharp blows
  • synonym: slicing - the act of cutting something into thin pieces
