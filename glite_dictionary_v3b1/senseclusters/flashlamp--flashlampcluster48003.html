flashlamp

noun
portable electric lamp
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃˌlæmp / (AmE), / ˈflæʃˌlæmp / (BrE)
a small portable electric lamp, usually battery-powered, used to provide continuous light

Examples

Handheld flashlamp
He grabbed the flashlamp and searched the dark cellar for the old toolbox.
During the power outage, she used a flashlamp to find her way around the house.
The campers packed a flashlamp in case they needed light at night.
