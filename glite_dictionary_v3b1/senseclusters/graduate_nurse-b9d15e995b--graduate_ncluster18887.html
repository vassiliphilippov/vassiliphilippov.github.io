graduate nurse

noun
nursing school graduate (not yet licensed)
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgrædʒ.juː.ət nɜːrs/ (AmE), /ˈgrædʒ.juː.ət nɜːs/ (BrE)
a person who has completed the required course of study and clinical training at a nursing school but may not yet be licensed as a registered nurse

Examples

A graduate nurse hired
After finishing her exams, she was hired as a graduate nurse at the city hospital.
Many graduate nurses are eager to sit for their licensing exam as soon as possible.
The hospital only allows graduate nurses to administer medication under supervision.
