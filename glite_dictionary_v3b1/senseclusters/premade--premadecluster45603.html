premade

noun
ready-made product
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmeɪd / (BrE)
a product that has been prepared or assembled in advance, ready for immediate use or consumption

Examples

A premade salad
She bought a premade salad from the grocery store for a quick lunch.
Many people choose a premade meal when they are too busy to cook.
The store offers a variety of premades for customers looking for convenience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homemade - made at home rather than bought or premade
  • synonym: ready-made - already made and available for use
