chewing

noun
the action of biting and grinding food
2.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃuː.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃuː.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
The action or process of biting and grinding food in the mouth to make it soft enough to swallow.

Examples

Good chewing action
Good chewing is important for proper digestion of food.
