pulse of the music

noun
the rhythm or beat in music
Pronunciation: / pʌls əv ðə ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / pʌls ɒv ðə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
the underlying rhythm or beat that drives a piece of music and can be physically or emotionally felt

Examples

The pulse of the music
As the lights dimmed, the pulse of the music grew stronger, filling the entire room.
She closed her eyes and let the pulse of the music guide her movements across the floor.
The crowd moved in unison, each person feeling the pulse of the music deep in their chest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beat - the basic rhythmic unit in music
  • synonym: rhythm - the pattern of sounds and silences in music
