tweaky

adjective
overly fine-tuned
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːki / (AmE), / ˈtwiːki / (BrE)
characterized by frequent small adjustments or excessive fine-tuning, especially of technical settings or performance.

Examples

Tweaky settings menu
The software's tweaky interface made it difficult for beginners to use without constant adjustments.
His guitar sound became too tweaky after he spent hours changing the amp settings.
Some people prefer a simple setup, but others enjoy a more tweaky approach to technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fussy - showing excessive attention to detail
  • synonym: refined - improved by making small changes
  • antonym: sloppy - lacking care or attention to detail
