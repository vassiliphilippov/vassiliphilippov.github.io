baffle

verb
hinder or thwart someone's efforts
Pronunciation: / ˈbæfəl / (AmE), / ˈbæfəl / (BrE)
to hinder or prevent the efforts, plans, or desires of someone; to frustrate or thwart

Examples

To baffle an opponent
Despite their best efforts, the team's strategies baffled their rivals throughout the competition.
He tried to baffle her ambitions by spreading false rumors.
The security measures were designed to baffle any attempts at unauthorized entry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frustrate - to prevent a plan or effort from succeeding
  • synonym: thwart - to stop something from happening or succeeding
