raker

noun
sloping support beam
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈreɪkə / (BrE)
a sloping structural member, such as a rafter or brace, that supports part of a roof or framework

Examples

Roof raker
The builders fitted a new raker to support the damaged section of the roof.
A raker is often used in timber framing to provide extra stability.
Without a strong raker, the framework could collapse under heavy loads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brace - a structural support that reinforces a framework
  • synonym: rafter - a sloping beam supporting a roof
