textbook

noun
book for study in schools or colleges
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɛkst.bʊk /; / ˈtɛkstbʊk / (BrE)
a book used as a standard source of information for studying a subject in school or college
