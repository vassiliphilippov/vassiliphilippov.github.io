tipu tree

noun
South American ornamental tree with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːpuː triː / (AmE), / ˈtiːpuː triː / (BrE)
A semi-evergreen tree native to South America, with odd-pinnate leaves and golden yellow flowers, often grown as an ornamental.
