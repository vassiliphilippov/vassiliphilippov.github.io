skinny-dipper

noun
person who swims naked
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪni ˌdɪpər / (AmE), / ˈskɪni ˌdɪpə / (BrE)
a person who swims naked, especially in a natural body of water

Examples

A skinny-dipper swims
He never imagined he'd become a skinny-dipper until his friends convinced him.
Late at night, the group of skinny-dippers laughed as they slipped into the lake.
Madeline admitted she had once been a skinny-dipper during her college years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skinny-dipping - swimming naked, usually for recreation
