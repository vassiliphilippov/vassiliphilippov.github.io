intercessional

noun
prayer for others
9.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)
a prayer or plea made on behalf of another person or group, asking for help or favor.

Examples

An intercessional for peace
During the ceremony, the priest led an intercessional for peace and unity.
She wrote an intercessional asking for her friend's recovery from illness.
The congregation joined together in an intercessional for those affected by the disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercession - the act of intervening or praying on behalf of others
  • synonym: supplication - a humble request or prayer for help
