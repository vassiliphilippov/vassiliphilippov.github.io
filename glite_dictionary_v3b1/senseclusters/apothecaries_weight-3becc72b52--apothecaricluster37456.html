apothecaries' weight

noun
pharmacy weight measurement system
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːθɪˌkeriz weɪt / (AmE), / əˈpɒθɪkəriz weɪt / (BrE)
a system of weights formerly used in pharmacy, in which an ounce is equal to 480 grains and a pound is 12 ounces
