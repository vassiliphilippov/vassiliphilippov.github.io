Synonyms

  • film
    1.0 193/m
    film is a single movie or artistic work shown on screens, while cinema refers to the medium and art of films.
  • motion picture
    3.0 3.45/m

  • moving picture
    3.5 0.60/m

    3.0 1.92/m
