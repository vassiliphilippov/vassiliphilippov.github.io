cinema

noun
motion pictures as a medium or art form
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəmə / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmə / (BrE)
a medium that uses moving pictures to tell stories or present information; motion pictures as an art or entertainment form

Examples

Cinema as art
Advancements in technology have transformed the way we experience cinema.
Documentaries have become an important genre within the art of cinema.
Many people consider French cinema to be among the finest in the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: film - a motion picture; the art or industry of making movies
  • antonym: television - a system for transmitting visual images and sound to screens
