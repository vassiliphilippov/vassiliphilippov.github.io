underbutler

noun
assistant household servant
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.bʌt.lər/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.bʌt.lə/ (BrE)
a servant in a large household who assists and is ranked below the main butler

Examples

The underbutler serves
The underbutler ensured that the silverware was polished before the formal dinner.
After the butler retired, the underbutler was promoted to the main position.
In grand estates, the underbutler managed the junior staff and assisted with daily operations.
