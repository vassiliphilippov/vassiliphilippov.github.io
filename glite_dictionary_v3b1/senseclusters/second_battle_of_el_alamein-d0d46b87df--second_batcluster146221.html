Second Battle of El Alamein

noun
key World War II battle in Egypt, 1942
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ˈbætəl əv ɛl ˌæləˈmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsekənd ˈbætəl əv ɛl ˌæləˈmeɪn / (BrE)
an important World War II battle in 1942 in which Allied forces, led by Montgomery, defeated German troops under Rommel in Egypt
