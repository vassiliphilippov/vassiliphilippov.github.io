veld

An open, grassy plain or elevated grassland, especially in southern Africa, characterized by few trees and a rich diversity of grasses.

Examples

The veld in Africa
Farmers graze sheep and cattle on the open veld of South Africa.
The wildflowers covered the veld in a brilliant display of color each spring.
We watched the sun set over the golden grasses of the veld.
A herd of antelope grazed peacefully across the sunlit veldt.
Lions could be seen roaming the veldt in search of prey.
The children played on the windy veldt, surrounded by tall grass and wildflowers.
