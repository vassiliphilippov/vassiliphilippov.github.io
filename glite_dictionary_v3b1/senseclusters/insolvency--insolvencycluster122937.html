insolvency

noun
inability to pay debts
7.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈsɑːl.vən.siː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsɑːl.vən.sɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsɒl.vən.sɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsɒl.vən.siː/ (BrE)
The state of being unable to pay debts owed; lack of financial resources to meet obligations.

Examples

Insolvency protection
Filing for insolvency protection allows businesses time to reorganize their debts.
Personal insolvency can have serious consequences for one's credit rating and financial future.
The company declared insolvency after failing to meet its financial obligations for several months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bankruptcy - legal status of being unable to repay debts
