varnishing

noun
layer of varnish
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑrnɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of applying varnish to a surface or the layer of varnish that gives a shiny or protective finish

Examples

Wood varnishing
The table received a fresh varnishing to restore its original shine.
After the varnishing, the wooden floor looked glossy and new.
A careful varnishing protects the artwork from dust and moisture.
The varnishing of the floor gave it a beautiful, glossy finish.
After careful varnishing, the artist's sculpture looked even more impressive.
Proper varnishing can help protect wooden furniture from moisture and scratches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coating - a layer of a substance covering a surface
  • synonym: paint - a colored substance spread over a surface for protection or decoration
  • synonym: painting - the act of applying paint to a surface
  • antonym: stripping - the act of removing a coating from a surface
