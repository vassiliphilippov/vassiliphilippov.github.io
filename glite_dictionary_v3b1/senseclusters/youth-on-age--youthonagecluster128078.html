youth-on-age

noun
a perennial herb that produces new plants on leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / juθ ɑn eɪdʒ / (AmE), / juːθ ɒn eɪdʒ / (BrE)
A vigorous perennial herb, known for its erect racemes of flowers and the development of young plants where the leaf blade meets the leafstalk; commonly used as a name for certain Tolmiea species.

Examples

The youth-on-age plant
The leaves of youth-on-age provide a striking appearance and are also practical for propagation.
The nursery specializes in unusual houseplants like youth-on-age and various succulents.
You can propagate youth-on-age easily, as it produces little plantlets on its leaves.
