softness

noun
quality of yielding easily to pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔft.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒft.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being easily shaped, cut, or compressed with little resistance to pressure

Examples

Softness of the ground
During spring, the softness of the ground makes planting much easier.
She admired the softness of the fabric between her fingers.
The softness of the dough made it easy to knead into shape.

  • synonym: pliability - the quality of being easily bent or shaped
