SCF

noun
supply chain finance
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɛs si ɛf / (AmE), / ɛs siː ɛf / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'Supply Chain Finance', the management of financial flows and funding in supply chains

Examples

SCF program setup
Many multinational companies implement SCF programs to optimize their payment terms with suppliers.
The adoption of SCF can help small suppliers receive payments faster and improve their cash flow.
Banks offer various SCF solutions to support efficient financial management in global supply chains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: working capital - the funds available for day-to-day business operations
