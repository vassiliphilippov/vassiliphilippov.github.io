lulz

noun
amusement for its own sake, internet slang
8.9
Pronunciation: / lʌlz / (AmE), / lʌlz / (BrE)
amusement or enjoyment derived from an activity, especially at the expense of others, usually in the context of internet culture

Examples

For the lulz
Some hackers do it just for the lulz, not for profit.
They spread the rumor online purely for the lulz.
Trolling the forum was all about getting some lulz.

Related Terms

  • synonym: LOL - acronym for 'laugh out loud', used to indicate laughter online
