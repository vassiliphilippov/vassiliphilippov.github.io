carper

noun
someone who constantly criticizes in a petty way
9.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêrp…ôr / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêp…ô / (BrE)
A person who habitually finds fault or criticizes in a petty or trivial way.

Examples

A carper at meetings
Instead of offering solutions, he became known as a carper at company meetings.
No one appreciated the constant interruptions from the carper during the presentation.
She grew tired of listening to the office carper nitpick every minor detail.
