quillwork

noun
decorative art with quills
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪlˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈkwɪlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a decorative art form in which porcupine quills are used to create patterns or decorate objects

Examples

Traditional quillwork art
The artist demonstrated traditional quillwork techniques at the cultural festival.
Many Native American garments feature intricate quillwork designs along the edges.
She admired the colorful quillwork on the old leather pouch in the museum.
The museum displayed a beautiful piece of quillwork made by Indigenous artists.
She learned the traditional techniques of quillwork from her grandmother.
Many ceremonial garments are decorated with intricate quillwork patterns.
