excess

noun
excessive indulgence
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛs /; / ɪkˈsɛs, ˈɛkˌsɛs / (AmE), / ˈɛksɛs /; / ɪkˈsɛs, ˈɛk.ses / (BrE)
excessive or immoderate behavior; doing something too much, especially something unhealthy or improper

Examples

A life of excess
After a weekend of excess, he decided to eat healthily for a while.
Her parents warned her about the dangers of excess in all things.
Their lifestyle was often marked by excess and extravagance.
He was criticized for his excess in spending during the trip.
The company took steps to curb the excess that had led to financial problems.
The doctor warned that excess of alcohol could damage your health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess or extremes
  • synonym: overindulgence - immoderate or excessive indulgence
  • antonym: restraint - self-control or moderation in behavior
