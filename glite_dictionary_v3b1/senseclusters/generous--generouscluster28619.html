generous

adjective
willing to give or share freely
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒen.ər.əs/ (AmE), /ˈdʒen.ər.əs/ (BrE)
willing to give or share more than is usual or expected; showing a readiness to give more of something, such as time or money, than is strictly necessary or expected.

Examples

Generous donation
He made a generous offer to donate money to the hospital.
Her generous spirit made her beloved by everyone in the community.
The company thanked its generous customers for their continued support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
