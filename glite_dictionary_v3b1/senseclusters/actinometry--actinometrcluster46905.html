actinometry

noun
measurement of electromagnetic radiation
Pronunciation: / ˌæktəˈnɑːmətri / (AmE), / ˌaktɪˈnɒmɪtri / (BrE)
the measurement of the intensity of electromagnetic radiation, especially sunlight

Examples

Actinometry of sunlight
Advances in actinometry have helped scientists understand the effects of sunlight on climate change.
Laboratories rely on actinometry to calibrate light sources with precise accuracy.
The meteorologist used actinometry to record the daily solar radiation levels.
