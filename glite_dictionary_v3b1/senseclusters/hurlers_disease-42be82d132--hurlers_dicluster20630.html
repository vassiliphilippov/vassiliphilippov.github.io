Hurler's disease

noun
rare hereditary metabolic disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrlərz dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈhɜːləz dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
A rare hereditary disorder characterized by abnormal development of bone and cartilage and intellectual disability, due to a metabolic error.

Examples

Hurler's disease symptoms
Hurler's disease is inherited in an autosomal recessive pattern.
Children affected by Hurler's disease often show delayed growth and coarse facial features.
Early diagnosis of Hurler's disease can improve the management of symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mucopolysaccharidosis - any of a group of metabolic disorders affecting mucopolysaccharide breakdown
