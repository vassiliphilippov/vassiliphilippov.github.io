artmobile

noun
mobile art studio
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtmoʊˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈɑːtməʊˌbiːl / (BrE)
a vehicle designed to bring art to people, used for making, teaching, displaying, or promoting art

Examples

The artmobile visits schools
The city sent the artmobile to local schools to encourage creativity among students.
Children lined up outside the artmobile to participate in free drawing classes.
The artmobile travels to different neighborhoods, bringing art supplies and lessons to the community.
The artmobile traveled to rural communities to make art accessible to everyone.
Children were excited when the artmobile arrived at their school with interactive exhibits.
Local artists displayed their work in the artmobile during the city festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: art studio - a room or building where artists work
  • synonym: gallery - a place where art is displayed for the public
  • synonym: mobile library - a vehicle equipped to lend books and promote reading in different locations
  • synonym: museum - an institution that collects and displays art or historical objects
