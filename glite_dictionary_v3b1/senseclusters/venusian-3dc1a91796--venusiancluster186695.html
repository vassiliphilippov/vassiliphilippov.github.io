Venusian

adjective
of planet Venus
Pronunciation: / vɪˈnuːʒən / (AmE), / vɪˈnjuːziən / (BrE)
relating to the planet Venus or its characteristics

Examples

Venusian atmosphere
The probe sent back detailed images of the Venusian surface.
Scientists are fascinated by the thick clouds in the Venusian atmosphere.
A Venusian day is longer than its year due to the planet's slow rotation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Martian - relating to the planet Mars or its characteristics
  • antonym: terrestrial - relating to the planet Earth
