schlub

noun
lazy or messy person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃlʌb / (AmE), / ʃlʌb / (BrE)
a person who is lazy, messy, or socially awkward, often appearing unkempt or unimpressive

Examples

A lazy schlub
He felt like a schlub sitting on the couch all day in his pajamas.
Despite being a bit of a schlub, he was always friendly and kind to everyone.
She worried that her messy apartment would make her look like a schlub to her guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat freak - a person who is extremely tidy and orderly
  • synonym: slob - a person who is messy or careless in appearance or habits
