wethead look

noun
wet and messy hairstyle
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛtˌhɛd lʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɛthɛd lʊk / (BrE)
A hairstyle that appears wet and messy, often as if just emerged from water.

Examples
