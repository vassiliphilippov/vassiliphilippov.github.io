Chinese date

noun
edible fruit, also called jujube
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪˌniz deɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz deɪt / (BrE)
the dark red, plumlike edible fruit of the jujube tree (Ziziphus jujuba), native to Asia and sometimes called jujube

Examples

A Chinese date fruit
She tasted a Chinese date for the first time and found it sweet and chewy.
The Chinese date is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and desserts.
You can find dried Chinese date in many Asian grocery stores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jujube - the fruit of the Ziziphus jujuba, also known as the Chinese date
