diminuendo

adjective
gradual decrease in volume (music)
7.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ / (AmE), / dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) a gradual decrease in loudness or intensity of sound.

Examples

Diminuendo ending
As the music moved towards the end, there was a noticeable diminuendo in the strings section.
The conductor signaled for a gradual diminuendo, lowering the energy of the piece.
The orchestra played the passage with a delicate diminuendo that faded to silence.
