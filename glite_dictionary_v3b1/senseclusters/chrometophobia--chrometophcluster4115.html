chrometophobia

noun
fear of money
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊmɪtəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌkrəʊmɪtəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
an irrational fear or strong aversion to money or handling money

Examples

Chrometophobia fear of money
People with chrometophobia may avoid dealing with their finances altogether.
Her chrometophobia caused her to feel anxious whenever she had to pay bills.
Therapy can help individuals overcome chrometophobia and manage their money more confidently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fear - an unpleasant emotion caused by the threat of danger or harm
  • synonym: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
  • antonym: wealth - an abundance of valuable possessions or money
