Chait

noun
first month of the Hindu calendar
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪt / (AmE), / tʃaɪt / (BrE)
the first month of the Hindu calendar, usually corresponding to March in the Gregorian calendar

Examples

Month of Chait
Holi is celebrated in the month of Chait according to the Hindu calendar.
Many traditional festivals take place during Chait every year.
The new fiscal year often begins with the arrival of Chait.
