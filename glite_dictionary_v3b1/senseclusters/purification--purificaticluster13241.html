purification

noun
moral or spiritual cleansing
Pronunciation: / ˌpjʊr.ə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpjʊə.rɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of becoming or making morally or spiritually clean, especially by removing sin or guilt

Examples

Spiritual purification
He sought purification through confession and sincere repentance.
Many traditions stress the importance of purification before participating in sacred rituals.
The ceremony included prayers and the purification of the community members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atonement - the act of making amends for sin or wrongdoing
  • antonym: defilement - the act of making impure, especially morally or spiritually
