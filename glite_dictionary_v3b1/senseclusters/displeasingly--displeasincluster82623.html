displeasingly

adverb
in an unpleasant or displeasing way
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈplizɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪsˈpliːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes displeasure or is unpleasant.

Examples

Displeasingly loud voice
His displeasingly loud laughter interrupted the quiet meeting.
She spoke displeasingly to her colleague, making the atmosphere tense.
The decorations were displeasingly arranged, making the room look cluttered.
