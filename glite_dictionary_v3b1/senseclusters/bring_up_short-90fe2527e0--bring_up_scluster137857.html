bring up short

verb
to stop or interrupt suddenly
4.9
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ ʌp ʃɔrt / (AmE), / brɪŋ ʌp ʃɔːt / (BrE)

Examples

To bring up short suddenly

I was brought up short by the sight of an old friend from school.
