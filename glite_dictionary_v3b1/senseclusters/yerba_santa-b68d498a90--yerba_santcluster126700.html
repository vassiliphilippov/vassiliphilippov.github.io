yerba santa

noun
aromatic shrub used medicinally
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌjɜrbə ˈsɑːntə / (AmE), / ˌjɜːbə ˈsæntə / (BrE)
a viscid evergreen shrub native to the western United States, with aromatic leaves used in treating bronchial and pulmonary illnesses

Examples

Yerba santa leaves
Many herbalists praise the medicinal properties of yerba santa for respiratory health.
The leaves of yerba santa are often brewed into teas to relieve coughs and chest congestion.
We found thick stands of yerba santa growing along the mountain trail.
