representation

noun
body of legislators acting for a constituency
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or right of speaking or acting on behalf of someone or a group, or being represented by delegates

Examples

Equal representation in Parliament is a fundamental principle of democracy.
She was chosen to provide representation for the workers at the board meeting.
The country's interests abroad are protected through diplomatic representation.
Citizens demand proper representation in government decisions.
She provided legal representation for her client in court.
The union offers representation for workers during contract negotiations.
