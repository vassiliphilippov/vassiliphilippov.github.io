nontheatrical

adjective
restrained, not dramatic
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnθiˈætrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnθiˈætrɪkəl / (BrE)
not dramatic, showy, or exaggerated in manner or expression; restrained and natural.
