bhat

noun
South Asian cooked rice
Pronunciation: / bɑːt / (AmE), / bɑːt / (BrE)
a South Asian dish consisting of cooked rice, commonly served as a staple food

Examples

Steamed bhat
She served spicy vegetables with bhat for dinner.
In many Indian households, bhat is eaten daily with lentils and curry.
The restaurant offered a traditional meal of bhat and assorted side dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rice - a cereal grain and staple food in many countries
