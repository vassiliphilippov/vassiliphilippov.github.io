Chinese lacquer tree

noun
tree producing lacquer sap in Asia
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪ.niz ˈlæk.ər triː / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌɪ.niːz ˈlak.ə triː / (BrE)
