thrombocytopenic

noun
person with low platelets
Pronunciation: / ˌθrɑːmboʊˌsaɪtəˈpiːnɪk / (AmE), / ˌθrɒmbəʊˌsaɪtəˈpiːnɪk / (BrE)
a person who has thrombocytopenia, meaning an abnormally low number of platelets in the blood

Examples

A thrombocytopenic patient
The doctor explained that a thrombocytopenic is at higher risk for bleeding after surgery.
Several thrombocytopenic were admitted with nosebleeds and severe bruising after the accident.
A thrombocytopenic may require special care to prevent excessive bleeding during dental procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - having normal or adequate platelet levels
  • synonym: thrombocytopenia - a condition of having an abnormally low number of platelets
