longevity

noun
long life or duration
Pronunciation: / lɑnˈdʒɛvəti / (AmE), / lɒnˈdʒɛvəti / (BrE)
The fact or quality of having a long life or existence; long duration of life or service.

Examples

Human longevity
She attributed her grandmother's longevity to a healthy diet and active lifestyle.
The longevity of that bridge is a testament to its solid construction.
The longevity of the tortoise is legendary, with some living more than 150 years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brevity - shortness of duration
  • synonym: lifespan - the length of time something lives or lasts
