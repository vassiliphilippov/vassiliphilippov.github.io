spindle

noun
rotating shaft in machinery
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪndl̩ / (AmE), / ˈspɪndəl / (BrE)
a rotating shaft, often serving as an axis for larger rotating parts in a machine

Examples

Machine spindle shaft
A broken spindle can bring the entire assembly line to a halt.
Each hard drive contains a spindle that spins the data disks.
The sewing machine's spindle turned rapidly as she stitched the fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axle - a shaft on which a wheel or pair of wheels rotates
  • synonym: shaft - a long, rotating rod used to transmit motion
