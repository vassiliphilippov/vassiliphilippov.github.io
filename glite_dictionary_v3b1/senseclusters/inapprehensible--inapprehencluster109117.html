inapprehensible

adjective
impossible to understand
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
impossible to understand or grasp; incomprehensible

Examples

Inapprehensible language
The technical jargon in the report was completely inapprehensible to most readers.
Her explanation became inapprehensible as she used more and more complex terms.
Some abstract art can seem inapprehensible to those unfamiliar with the style.

  • synonym: unintelligible - impossible to understand or make sense of
