significator

noun
indicator or sign
8.8
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪfɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
something that indicates, shows, or points to the existence, nature, or condition of something; an indicator or sign.

Examples

A clear significator
The sudden drop in website traffic was a clear significator that the campaign had failed.
In tarot readings, a card may serve as a significator to represent the person asking the question.
Rising unemployment rates are often seen as a significator of economic trouble ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicator - something that shows or points out
  • synonym: sign - an object or event that shows something exists or is true
  • synonym: symptom - a sign of the existence of something, especially something bad
