zopilote

noun
opportunist in misfortune
9.9
Pronunciation: / soʊpiˈloʊteɪ / (AmE), / zɒpɪˈləʊteɪ / (BrE)
a person who takes advantage of others' misfortune or exploits a bad situation for personal gain

He was called a zopilote for profiting from his neighbors' misfortune.
The community resented the zopilote who bought up homes at very low prices.
