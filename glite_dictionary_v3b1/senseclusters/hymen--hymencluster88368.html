hymen

noun
membrane partially covering vaginal opening
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪmən / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪmən / (BrE)
a thin membrane that partially covers the vaginal opening, often present at birth and sometimes associated with virginity

Examples

The hymen membrane
An intact hymen is not always an accurate indicator of virginity.
During the examination, she asked questions about the role of the hymen in female anatomy.
The doctor explained that the hymen can vary greatly in appearance and thickness among individuals.
