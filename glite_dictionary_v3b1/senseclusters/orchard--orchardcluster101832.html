orchard

verb
to grow fruit trees
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.tʃərd/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.tʃəd/ (BrE)
to plant, cultivate, or manage land with fruit trees, especially as part of an orchard.

Examples

To orchard the land
They spent the summer orcharding the valley, planting rows of apple and cherry trees.
Many families in the region have been orcharding for generations, passing down their knowledge.
After retiring, she decided to start orcharding on a small piece of land outside town.
