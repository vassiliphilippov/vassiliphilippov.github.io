piezo

noun
piezoelectric buzzer
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpiːɛtsəʊ / (BrE)
a small piezoelectric speaker or buzzer used to produce sound in electronic devices such as alarms and toys

Examples

Piezo buzzer alarm
The alarm uses a piezo to emit a loud beeping sound when triggered.
He replaced the broken piezo in his electronic project to restore the sound function.
Many modern toys include a small piezo for simple sound effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzzer - a small device that makes a buzzing sound
  • synonym: speaker - a device that produces sound from electrical signals
