crash

noun
serious vehicle accident
2.4 2.0
Pronunciation: /kræʃ/ (AmE), /kræʃ/ (BrE)
a violent collision or accident, especially involving vehicles or objects; or to hit, break, or move through something with great force and noise

Examples

A car crash
Investigators are examining the cause of the airplane crash.
She was injured in a crash on her way to work yesterday.
The highway was closed for hours after the crash involving several trucks.
The crash of bottles startled everyone in the kitchen.
The car made a loud crash when it hit the guardrail.
There was a sudden crash as the vase fell to the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident - an unexpected event causing damage or injury
  • synonym: collision - an accident in which two or more objects hit each other, especially vehicles
  • synonym: wreck - a serious accident destroying a vehicle or causing severe damage
