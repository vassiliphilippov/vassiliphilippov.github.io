Synonyms

  • collision
    3.8 7/m
    a collision is an accident where moving objects hit each other violently, while a crash often implies loud breaking and strong force.
  • collide
    3.1 3/m
    collide means two things move and strike each other, while crash suggests a louder, more violent impact that breaks or damages objects.
  • smash
    3.7 0.3/m
    smash is to hit with strong force that often breaks things, while crash also covers collisions that may not always break objects.
  • smashup
    4.4 0.00/m
    a smashup is an often severe vehicle accident that leaves wreckage, while a crash can mean any violent collision between objects or vehicles.
  • wreck
    2.7 5/m
    a wreck is a very serious accident or ruined vehicle, while a crash can be any violent collision that may be less severe.
  • hit
    1.0 20/m
    a hit is any act of striking or contact, while a crash is a larger, violent collision often causing loud noise and major damage.
