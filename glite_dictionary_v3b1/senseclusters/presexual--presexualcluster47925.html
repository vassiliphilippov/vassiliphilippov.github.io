presexual

adjective
before sexual reproduction
9.7
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈsek.ʃuː.əl/ /; / /priː.ˈsek.ʃəl/ (AmE), /priː.ˈsek.ʃəl/ /; / /priː.ˈsek.ʃuː.əl/ (BrE)
relating to a period or stage before the development of sexual reproduction, especially in evolutionary history or in organisms reproducing asexually.

Examples

Presexual organisms

The fossil record provides evidence of presexual life forms that reproduced by simple cell division.
Researchers are studying presexual stages in certain algae to understand the origins of sex.
