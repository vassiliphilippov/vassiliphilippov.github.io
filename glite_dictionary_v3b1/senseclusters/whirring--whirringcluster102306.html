whirring

noun
continuous low, rapid sound
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a continuous, low, rapid sound produced by something moving quickly or spinning

Examples

A whirring sound
A sudden whirring from the garden made them look up just in time to see a hummingbird fly past.
She was distracted by the whirring in the hallway as the vacuum cleaner passed by.
The whirring of the fan lulled him to sleep on the hot afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz - a continuous humming sound
  • synonym: hum - a low, steady, continuous sound
  • antonym: silence - absence of sound
