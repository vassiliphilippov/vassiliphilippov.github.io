abuse

noun
improper or excessive use
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbjus / (AmE), / əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
improper or excessive use of something, especially in a way that is harmful or incorrect

Examples

Drug abuse
Financial abuse occurs when someone improperly controls another person's assets.
The abuse of prescription drugs can lead to serious health problems.
There is widespread concern about the abuse of government power.
