halberd

noun
combined spear and ax weapon
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæl.bɚd / (AmE), / ˈhæl.bəd / (BrE)
a weapon consisting of a long staff with an ax blade topped with a spike, used chiefly in the 15th and 16th centuries

Examples

A halberd weapon
A halberd combines the features of a spear and an axe for versatile combat.
During the parade, several soldiers displayed ornate halberds from the royal armory.
The guard stood at attention, gripping his halberd beside the castle gate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poleaxe - an ax mounted on a long staff, used as a weapon
