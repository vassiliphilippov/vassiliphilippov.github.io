suprasegmental

adjective
pertaining to speech features like stress or intonation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəsɛgˈmɛn̩təl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˌsɛɡˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
Relating to a feature of speech, such as stress, tone, or intonation, that extends over more than one sound or segment.

Examples

Suprasegmental speech features
Intonation is a suprasegmental feature of spoken language.
Linguists study suprasegmental patterns to understand how emphasis changes communication.
The suprasegmental elements of speech, such as rhythm and pitch, influence meaning.
