habitant

noun
person who inhabits a place; Canadian settler
Pronunciation: / ˈhæbɪtənt / (AmE), / ˈhæbɪtənt / (BrE)
a person or animal that lives in a particular place

Examples

A habitant of town
Each habitant tended a small farm along the banks of the river.
Many of the early habitants of Quebec were of French descent.
The village habitants welcomed visitors with warm hospitality.
The forest is home to many habitant species.
Each habitant of the island has a unique story to tell.
The city’s oldest habitant remembers when the streets were unpaved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhabitant - a person who lives in a particular place
  • synonym: resident - a person who lives somewhere permanently or on a long-term basis
  • antonym: visitor - someone who comes to a place temporarily
