flush toilet

noun
toilet cleaned by water flow
1.4
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ ˈtɔɪlət / (AmE), / flʌʃ ˈtɔɪlət / (BrE)
A toilet that removes waste by washing it away with a flow of water.

Examples

A flush toilet
He heard the sound of the flush toilet from the bathroom after his guest left.
Most modern homes are equipped with a flush toilet for convenience and hygiene.
The invention of the flush toilet greatly improved public sanitation in urban areas.
