marsh trefoil

noun
a perennial aquatic plant with trifoliate leaves
9.5 7.9
Pronunciation: /mɑːrʃ ˈtriː.fɔɪl/ /; / /mɑːrʃ ˈtrɛ.fɔɪl/ (AmE), /mɑːʃ ˈtriː.fɔɪl/ /; / /mɑːʃ ˈtrɛ.fɔɪl/ (BrE)
a perennial plant native to Europe and America, with racemes of white or purplish flowers and intensely bitter, trifoliate leaves, often found rooting at the margins of water and spreading across surfaces
