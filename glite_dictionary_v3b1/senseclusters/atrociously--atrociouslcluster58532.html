atrociously

adverb
to a shocking or excessive degree
Pronunciation: / əˈtroʊʃəsli / (AmE), / əˈtrəʊʃəsli / (BrE)
in a very bad, shocking, or extremely unpleasant way

Examples

Behave atrociously
She was atrociously rude to the staff, which shocked everyone present.
The repair bills for the old car were atrociously high last month.
This restaurant is atrociously overpriced for the quality of food they serve.
She behaved atrociously towards her guests, showing no manners at all.
The food at the restaurant was atrociously prepared and almost inedible.
The team played atrociously and lost the game by a huge margin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreadfully - to a very bad or excessive degree
  • synonym: horribly - to a very bad or unpleasant degree
  • synonym: terribly - to an extreme or excessive degree
  • antonym: wonderfully - to a very pleasing or admirable degree (positive antonym)
