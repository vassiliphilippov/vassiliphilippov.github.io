phishing scam

noun
fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive data electronically
4.1 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪʃ.ɪŋ skæm/ (AmE), /ˈfɪʃ.ɪŋ skæm/ (BrE)
A fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, or credit card details, by impersonating a trustworthy entity in electronic communications.

Examples

A phishing scam email
After clicking the suspicious link, he realized it was a phishing scam targeting online banking users.
Many companies train employees to recognize a phishing scam to avoid data breaches.
She received an email that turned out to be a phishing scam designed to steal her login credentials.
