dentally

adverb
in a dental manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəli / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to the teeth or dental processes

Examples

Performed dentally
The dentist explained how the procedure would be performed dentally rather than surgically.
Some speech sounds are produced dentally, with the tongue touching the teeth.
Proper hygiene is important to stay healthy both dentally and overall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orally - in a manner relating to the mouth
