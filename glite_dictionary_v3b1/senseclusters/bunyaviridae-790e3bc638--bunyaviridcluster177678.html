A historical (now obsolete) taxonomic family name formerly used for a group of RNA viruses; after taxonomic revision by the ICTV (around 2019), members were reassigned to several separate families.

Examples

Historical Bunyaviridae
Historical literature often references Bunyaviridae, but modern taxonomy splits them into several distinct families.
A 2018 review listed viruses under Bunyaviridae, whereas 2020 papers use new family names instead.
When reading older case reports, clinicians should note that Bunyaviridae is now treated as historical.
