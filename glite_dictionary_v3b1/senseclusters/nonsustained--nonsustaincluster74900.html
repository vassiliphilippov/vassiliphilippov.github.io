nonsustained

adjective
brief medical episode
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səˈsteɪnd /; / ˌnɑnsəˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səˈsteɪnd /; / ˌnɒnsəˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not lasting or not maintained for a significant period of time; ending quickly

Examples

Nonsustained heartbeat

Doctors were relieved that the arrhythmia was nonsustained and did not require immediate intervention.
