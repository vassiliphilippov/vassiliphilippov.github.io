thickheaded

adjective
stupid or slow-witted (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˌθɪkˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌθɪkˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid or slow to understand; lacking intelligence or quickness of mind.

Examples

A thickheaded person
Don't be so thickheaded—the answer is right in front of you!
He is too thickheaded to grasp the simple instructions.
She called her brother thickheaded after he misunderstood the joke for the third time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clever - quick to understand, intelligent
  • synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
