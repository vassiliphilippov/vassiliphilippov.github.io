intermedia

noun
hybrid art forms
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
artworks or artistic practices that combine two or more different media or genres to create hybrid forms

Examples

Intermedia artworks
The museum's new exhibition features intermedia pieces that blend painting, sound, and digital technology.
She is known for her innovative intermedia works that challenge traditional boundaries between art forms.
Students in the course created intermedia projects combining video, sculpture, and live performance.
