beneficiate

verb
process ores to improve quality
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnəˈfɪʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃɪeɪt / (BrE)
to process ores or other raw materials to improve their quality, especially by removing impurities

Examples

To beneficiate ore
Before smelting, the company must beneficiate the ore to increase its mineral content.
Certain mining operations automatically beneficiate low-grade ores as part of their processing workflow.
The new plant is able to beneficiate raw materials more efficiently than older facilities.
