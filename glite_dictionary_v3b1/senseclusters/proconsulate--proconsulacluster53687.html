proconsulate

noun
office of a proconsul
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkɑːnsjʊlət / (AmE), / prəʊˈkɒnsjʊlət / (BrE)
The office or position of a proconsul, especially in ancient Rome.

Examples

The proconsulate of Asia
After his term as consul, he was rewarded with the proconsulate of Asia.
Historical records discuss the various duties associated with the proconsulate in different provinces.
The proconsulate was considered a prestigious assignment in Roman times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proconsul - a governor of a Roman province appointed with consular authority
