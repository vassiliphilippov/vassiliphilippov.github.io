watch glass

noun
protective cover for a watch face
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ ɡlæs / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a clear, protective cover over the face of a watch

Examples

A jeweler can replace your broken watch glass with a new one.
Many antique watches have domed watch glasses made of acrylic.
