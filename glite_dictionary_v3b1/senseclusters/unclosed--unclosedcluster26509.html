unclosed

adjective
not closed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkloʊzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkləʊzd / (BrE)
not closed or left open, especially referring to something that should be shut or sealed

Examples

Unclosed window

Please make sure no containers are unclosed before you leave the lab.
