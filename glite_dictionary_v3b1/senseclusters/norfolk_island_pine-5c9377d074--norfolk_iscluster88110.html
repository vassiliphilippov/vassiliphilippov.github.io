Norfolk Island pine

noun
an evergreen tree native to Norfolk Island
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔr.fək ˈaɪ.lənd paɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.fək ˈaɪ.lənd paɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen tree (Araucaria heterophylla) native to Norfolk Island and parts of Australia, often grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

A Norfolk Island pine tree
