consultation

noun
a professional meeting for discussion
4.1 18/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀên.s…ôlÀàte…™. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåk…ín.s…ôlÀàte…™. É…ôn / (BrE)

Examples

Medical consultation
