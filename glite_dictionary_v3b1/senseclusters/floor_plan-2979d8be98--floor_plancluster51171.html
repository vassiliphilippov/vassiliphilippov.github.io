floor plan

noun
architectural drawing of a building's layout
Pronunciation: / flɔɚ plæn / (AmE), / flɔː plæn / (BrE)
A scale drawing showing the arrangement of rooms and spaces in a building as viewed from above at a specific level.

Examples

A floor plan drawing
The architect showed us the floor plan for the new house before construction began.
The museum's floor plan is available at the entrance to help visitors navigate.
We studied the floor plan to decide where to place the furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elevation - a drawing showing a building from the side
