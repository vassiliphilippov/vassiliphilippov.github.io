biteable

adjective
can be bitten
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be bitten or suitable for biting, especially without causing harm

Examples

A biteable toy
The dog prefers toys that are soft and biteable for safe play.
Some fruits have skins that are not biteable and need to be peeled first.
The material is designed to be biteable for teething babies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edible - safe or suitable to eat
  • antonym: inedible - not suitable for eating
