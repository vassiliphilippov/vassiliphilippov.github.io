Martin Luther

proper noun
German theologian and Reformation leader
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtən ˈluθər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪn ˈluːθə / (BrE)
German theologian (1483–1546) who was a seminal figure in the Protestant Reformation, advocating justification by faith alone.
