self-torment

noun
self-inflicted mental or emotional suffering
Pronunciation: / sɛlf ˈtɔrmɛnt / (AmE), / sɛlf ˈtɔːmɛnt / (BrE)
Deliberate mental or emotional suffering inflicted on oneself; self-imposed distress.

Examples

Self-torment by thoughts
After the breakup, his constant dwelling on past mistakes became a form of self-torment.
She indulged in self-torment by replaying awkward conversations in her mind over and over.
Writing sad poetry was both an expression of her self-torment and a way to heal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masochism - enjoyment of, or desire for, one's own pain or humiliation
  • synonym: self-torture - the act of deliberately causing oneself pain or suffering
