guipure

noun
openwork lace
9.9
Pronunciation: / giˈpjʊr / (AmE), / giˈpjʊə / (BrE)
a type of lace with a heavy, open pattern and no net background, often used for decorative trimming

Examples

Guipure lace trim
The wedding gown featured beautiful guipure along the sleeves and neckline.
She admired the intricate guipure on the vintage tablecloth at the antique shop.
Designers often use guipure to add texture and elegance to evening dresses.
