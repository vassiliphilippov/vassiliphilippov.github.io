stem

verb
stop or control the flow of something
Pronunciation: / stɛm / (AmE), / stɛm / (BrE)
to stop or restrict the flow of something, especially a liquid; to prevent something from spreading or increasing

Examples

To stem the flow
Emergency workers managed to stem the flow of oil leaking into the river.
Rising interest rates are intended to stem inflation.
She used a cloth to stem the bleeding from his wound.

Related Terms
