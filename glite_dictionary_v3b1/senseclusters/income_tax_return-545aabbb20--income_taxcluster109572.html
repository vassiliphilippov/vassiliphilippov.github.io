income tax return

noun
tax form reporting income and taxes owed
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌkʌm ˈtæks rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɪn.kʌm ˈtæks rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an official document in which an individual or organization reports income, expenses, and other tax information to a tax authority

Examples

File an income tax return
Companies must file their income tax return by the end of the fiscal year.
Every year, I gather my financial documents to complete my income tax return.
The accountant explained how to claim deductions on your income tax return.
