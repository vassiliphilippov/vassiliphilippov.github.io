turret clock

noun
large clock with several dials in a tower
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.ət klɑk / (AmE), / ˈtʌr.ɪt klɒk / (BrE)
a large clock with multiple dials, usually mounted in a tower, designed to show the time in all directions
