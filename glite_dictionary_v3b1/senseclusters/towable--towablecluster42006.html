towable

noun
inflatable water toy
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊəbəl / (BrE)
a large inflatable object, such as a tube or mattress, designed to be pulled behind a boat for recreation

Examples

A towable tube
The kids spent hours riding the towable behind the boat on the lake.
We bought a new towable for our summer vacation at the beach.
After inflating the towable, they attached it to the speedboat for a fun afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raft - a flat inflatable structure used for floating on water
  • synonym: tube - an inflatable ring used for floating or being towed on water
