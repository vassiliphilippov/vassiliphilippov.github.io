scribble-scrabble

noun
messy writing or drawing
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪbəl ˌskræbəl / (AmE), / ˈskrɪbəl ˌskræbəl / (BrE)
messy or chaotic writing or drawing, consisting of a jumbled mixture of lines or colors
