Jerusalem cherry

noun
ornamental houseplant with poisonous red or yellow fruit
8.8
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈruːsələm ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / dʒəˈruːsələm ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a small shrub native to South America, grown as a houseplant for its ornamental but poisonous red or yellow berry-like fruit

Examples

The Jerusalem cherry plant
Children should be kept away from Jerusalem cherry because its berries are poisonous.

The Jerusalem cherry is popular as a winter houseplant despite its toxic berries.
