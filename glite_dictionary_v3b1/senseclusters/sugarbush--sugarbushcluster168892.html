sugarbush

noun
maple grove for syrup
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡɚˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡəbʊʃ / (BrE)
a grove or stand of sugar maple trees cultivated for producing maple syrup or sugar

Examples

A maple sugarbush
Every spring, families visit the sugarbush to see how maple syrup is made.
The old sugarbush on their property has produced syrup for generations.
Volunteers helped tap the trees in the sugarbush before the festival began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maple grove - a group of maple trees growing together
  • synonym: orchard - a piece of land planted with fruit or nut trees
