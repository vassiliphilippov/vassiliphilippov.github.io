Nynorsk

noun
Norwegian language standard based on rural dialects
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.nɔːrsk/ /; / /ˈnjuː.nɔːrsk/ (AmE), /ˈniː.nɔːsk/ /; / /ˈnjuː.nɔːsk/ (BrE)
