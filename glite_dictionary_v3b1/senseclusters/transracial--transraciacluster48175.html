transracial

adjective
involving different races
6.8
Pronunciation: / trænsˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / trænzˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
involving or relating to people of different racial backgrounds, especially within families or adoption

Examples

Transracial family
They adopted a child and became a transracial family.
The documentary explores the experiences of transracial adoptees growing up in different cultures.
A transracial friendship can offer valuable perspectives on identity and society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multiracial - involving or including people of several different races
