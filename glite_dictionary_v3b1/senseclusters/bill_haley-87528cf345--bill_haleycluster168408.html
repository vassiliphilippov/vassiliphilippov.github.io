Bill Haley

proper noun
American rock and roll singer (1925–1981)
Pronunciation: / bɪl ˈheɪli / (AmE), / bɪl ˈheɪli / (BrE)
(1925–1981) an American singer and musician who was one of the first to make rock and roll popular, known for hits such as 'Rock Around the Clock'.

Examples

Bill Haley rock singer
Bill Haley was a trailblazer of rock and roll in the 1950s.
Many historians credit Bill Haley with helping to usher in the modern era of popular music.
The band performed a cover of Bill Haley's signature song, 'Rock Around the Clock.'
