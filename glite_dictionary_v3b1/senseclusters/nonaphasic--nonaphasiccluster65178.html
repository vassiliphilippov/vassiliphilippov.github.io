nonaphasic

noun
person without aphasia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈfeɪzɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈfeɪzɪk / (BrE)
a person who does not have aphasia or any language impairment

Examples

A nonaphasic in tests
The control group included one nonaphasic who completed all the language assessments.
Researchers compared the brain scans of nonaphasic participants with those who had language impairments.
A nonaphasic was selected to match the age and education level of the patient group.
