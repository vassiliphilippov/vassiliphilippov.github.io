cat rig

noun
sailing rig with one forward mast and sail
8.0
Pronunciation: / kæt ɹɪɡ / (AmE), / kæt rɪɡ / (BrE)
A sailing rig with a single mast set well forward and carrying a single sail, as used on a catboat.

Examples

A cat rig sailboat
He chose a cat rig for his small lake sailboat because of its simplicity.
Sailors admire the ease of handling a cat rig in changing winds.
The traditional catboat is equipped with a simple cat rig.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sloop rig - a type of sailing rig with a single mast and two sails (a mainsail and a headsail)
