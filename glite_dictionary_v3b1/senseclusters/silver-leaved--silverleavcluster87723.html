silver-leaved

adjective
having silvery leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ liːvd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are silvery in color or appearance, often with a shiny or pale surface.

Examples

Silver-leaved plants
Many Mediterranean plants are silver-leaved to help reflect the intense sunlight and conserve water.
The silver-leaved lamb's ear is popular in cottage gardens for its soft, velvety foliage.
Their new landscaping featured several silver-leaved shrubs for a striking visual effect.
