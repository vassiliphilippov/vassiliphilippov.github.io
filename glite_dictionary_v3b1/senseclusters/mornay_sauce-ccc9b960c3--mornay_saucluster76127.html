Mornay sauce

noun
creamy cheese sauce with egg yolk and cheese
5.8
Pronunciation: / mɔːrˈneɪ sɔs / (AmE), / mɔːˈneɪ sɔːs / (BrE)
A rich cheese sauce made with butter, flour, milk, grated cheese (often Gruyère or Parmesan), and sometimes egg yolk, often flavored with onion.

Examples

Rich Mornay sauce
She learned how to make Mornay sauce in her French cooking class.
The recipe called for a rich Mornay sauce to complement the baked fish.
The vegetables were served with a generous helping of Mornay sauce on top.
