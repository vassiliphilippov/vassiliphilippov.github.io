Mornay sauce

noun
creamy cheese sauce with egg yolk and cheese
5.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /mɔːr.ˈneɪ sɔːs/ (AmE), /mɔː.ˈneɪ sɔːs/ (BrE)
A rich cheese sauce made with butter, flour, milk, grated cheese (often Gruyère or Parmesan), and sometimes egg yolk, often flavored with onion.

Examples

Rich Mornay sauce
