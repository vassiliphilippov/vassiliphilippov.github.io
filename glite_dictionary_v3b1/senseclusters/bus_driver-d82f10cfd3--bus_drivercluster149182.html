bus driver

noun
person who drives a bus
Pronunciation: / bʌs ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / bʌs ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive a bus, especially as a form of public transport

Examples

The bus driver stops
After the storm, the bus driver carefully navigated the icy roads.
Our local bus driver knows all the regular riders by name.
The bus driver greeted every passenger with a smile as they boarded.
