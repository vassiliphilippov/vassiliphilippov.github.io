milliped

noun
a segmented arthropod with many legs

Pronunciation: /ˈmɪl.ɪ.piːd/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ə.piːd/ (AmE), /ˈmɪl.ɪ.piːd/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ə.piːd/ (BrE)
An arthropod with a cylindrical, segmented body, most segments having two pairs of legs; commonly known as a millipede.

Examples

A milliped insect
Many people mistake a centipede for a milliped, but they are quite different creatures.

The scientist carefully observed the milliped as it crawled across the forest floor.
