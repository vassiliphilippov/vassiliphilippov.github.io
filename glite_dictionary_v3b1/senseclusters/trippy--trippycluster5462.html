trippy

adjective
like a hallucination
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtrɪ.piː/ (AmE), /ˈtrɪ.piː/ (BrE)
strange, surreal, or dreamlike, often like the effects of psychedelic drugs

Examples

A trippy experience
The movie's trippy visuals made the audience feel like they were dreaming.
She described the painting as trippy because of its swirling colors and strange shapes.
Listening to the band's new album was a trippy experience, full of unexpected sounds.
The artist's new painting is so trippy that it feels like a scene from a dream.
We listened to some trippy music that made the whole room seem to move.
The video game has trippy graphics that make it look completely unreal.
