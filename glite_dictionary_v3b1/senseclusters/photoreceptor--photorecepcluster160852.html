photoreceptor

noun
light-detecting sensor
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊrɪˈsɛptər / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊrɪˈsɛptə / (BrE)
a device or cell that detects light and converts it into an electrical signal, often used in machines or instruments

Examples

Camera photoreceptor sensor
The photoreceptor in the camera adjusts automatically to changes in light.
A faulty photoreceptor can cause the machine to stop working properly.
Engineers tested the new photoreceptor to ensure it could detect even low levels of light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detector - a device that discovers or identifies the presence of something
  • synonym: photosensor - a sensor that detects and responds to light
