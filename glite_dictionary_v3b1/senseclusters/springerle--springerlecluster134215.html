springerle

noun
decorative cookie mold
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋɚli / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋəli / (BrE)
a carved wooden or ceramic mold used to press decorative designs into the dough of springerle cookies

Examples

Wooden springerle mold
She carefully pressed the springerle into the dough to create beautiful patterns for the holiday cookies.
The antique springerle had intricate designs that had been passed down through generations.
Baking with a springerle is a cherished tradition in many German families during Christmas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cookie cutter - a tool used to cut shapes from cookie dough
  • synonym: mold - a hollow container used to give shape to something
