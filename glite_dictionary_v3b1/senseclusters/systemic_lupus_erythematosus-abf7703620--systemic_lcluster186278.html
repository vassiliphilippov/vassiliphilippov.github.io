systemic lupus erythematosus

noun
autoimmune disease causing inflammation
5.1
Pronunciation: /sɪs.ˈtem.ɪk ˈluː.pəs e.rɪ.θe.ˈmæt.ə.səs/ (AmE), /sɪs.ˈtem.ɪk ˈluː.pəs e.rɪ.θə.ˈmæt.ə.səs/ (BrE)
a chronic autoimmune disease causing inflammation of connective tissue, with symptoms such as fatigue, joint pain, and skin rash

Examples

Systemic lupus erythematosus symptoms
After months of unexplained fatigue and joint pain, she was diagnosed with systemic lupus erythematosus.
The patient's facial rash was a classic sign of systemic lupus erythematosus.
Treatments for systemic lupus erythematosus focus on controlling the body's immune response and managing symptoms.
