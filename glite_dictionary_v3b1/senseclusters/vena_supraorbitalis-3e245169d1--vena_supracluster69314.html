vena supraorbitalis

noun
vein in the scalp; forms part of the angular vein
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə suː.prə.ˈɔːr.bɪ.tə.lɪs/ /; / /ˈviː.nə sə.prə.ˈɔːr.bɪ.tə.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə suː.prə.ˈɔː.bɪ.tə.lɪs/ /; / /ˈviː.nə sə.prə.ˈɔː.bɪ.tə.lɪs/ (BrE)
A vein that drains blood from the front part of the scalp and unites with the supratrochlear vein to form the angular vein.

Examples

Vena supraorbitalis vein
An injury to the vena supraorbitalis can result in significant bruising above the eye.
During surgery on the forehead, care must be taken to avoid damaging the vena supraorbitalis.
The vena supraorbitalis is often visible in thin-skinned individuals.
