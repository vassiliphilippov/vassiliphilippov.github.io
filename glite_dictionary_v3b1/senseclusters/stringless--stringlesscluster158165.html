stringless

adjective
without fibrous string
3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstrɪŋ.ləs/ /; / /ˈstrɪŋ.les/ (AmE), /ˈstrɪŋ.ləs/ /; / /ˈstrɪŋ.les/ (BrE)
