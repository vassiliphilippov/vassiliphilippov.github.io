Utrecht

proper noun
city in the central Netherlands
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːtrɛkt / (AmE), / ˈjuːtrɛkt / (BrE)
A city in the central Netherlands, known for its historic center, canals, and as a major religious, educational, and cultural hub.

Examples

City of Utrecht
Many international conferences are held in the city of Utrecht.
The canals of Utrecht are famous for their unique wharf cellars.
The train from Amsterdam to Utrecht takes less than half an hour.
