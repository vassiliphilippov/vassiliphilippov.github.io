coffeemaker

noun
device for brewing coffee
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔfiˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a device or machine used to make coffee, either manually or automatically

Examples

Electric coffeemaker
She bought a new coffeemaker to prepare fresh coffee every morning.
The old coffeemaker still works perfectly after many years of use.
He prefers using a manual coffeemaker instead of an electric one.
Every morning, he uses his coffeemaker to prepare a fresh pot of coffee before work.
The new coffeemaker has a timer so you can wake up to hot coffee.
She cleaned the coffeemaker regularly to keep the coffee tasting good.
