ducting

noun
system of tubes
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌktɪŋ / (BrE)
a system or network of tubes used to carry air, gas, or other substances within a building or structure

Examples

Air ducting system
The ducting in the office was replaced to improve air circulation.
Engineers inspected the ducting for any signs of leaks or blockages.
Properly installed ducting helps maintain a comfortable temperature throughout the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pipework - a system of pipes for carrying liquids or gases
  • synonym: ventilation - the movement of air in and out of a space
