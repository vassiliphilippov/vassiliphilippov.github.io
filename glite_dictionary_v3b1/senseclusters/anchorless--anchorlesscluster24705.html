anchorless

adjective
without an anchor
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkərləs / (AmE), / ˈæŋkələs / (BrE)
not having an anchor or anything to keep something in place or secure

Examples

Anchorless boat
The small boat floated anchorless in the middle of the lake.
After losing his job, he felt anchorless and unsure of his next steps.
The balloon drifted anchorless into the sky, carried by the wind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secured - firmly fixed or fastened in place
  • synonym: unmoored - not fastened or secured in place
