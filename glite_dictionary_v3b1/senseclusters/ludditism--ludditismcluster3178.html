ludditism

noun
opposition to technology
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌdɪtɪzəm /; / ˈlʌdɪˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlʌdɪtɪzəm /; / ˈlʌdɪˌtɪzəm / (BrE)
opposition to new technology or industrial change, sometimes shown by protesting or destroying machines

Examples

Modern ludditism can be seen in movements that resist digital technology in schools.

Related Terms
