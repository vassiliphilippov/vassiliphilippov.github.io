effeminize

verb
make more feminine
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz /; / ɪˈfɛm.əˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnaɪz /; / ɪˈfɛm.ɪ.naɪz / (BrE)
to give a person or thing a more feminine or effeminate quality or appearance, especially in a way that makes them seem less masculine

Examples

To effeminize men's styles
Some critics argue that fashion trends have begun to effeminize traditional men's styles.
The character's gentle voice and delicate manners helped to effeminize his overall image.
The director sought to effeminize the hero's appearance for the new adaptation.
He felt his mannerisms were being unfairly effeminised by his peers.
Some critics argued that the movie effeminised the hero's character.
The new fashion trend seemed to effeminise traditional male attire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: masculinize - to make or become more masculine
