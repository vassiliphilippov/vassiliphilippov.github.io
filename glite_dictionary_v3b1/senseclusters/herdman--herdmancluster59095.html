herdman

noun
animal caretaker
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrdmən / (AmE), / ˈhɜːdmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to look after a group of animals, especially cattle or sheep

Examples

The herdman checks cattle
The herdman checked the fences every morning to keep the cattle safe.
As a skilled herdman, he could spot any sick animal in the group.
The village relied on the herdman to guide the sheep to fresh pastures.
