monopod

noun
single-legged support
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.nə.pɑːd/ (AmE), /ˈmɒn.ə.pɒd/ (BrE)
a single-legged support used to steady a camera or other device

Examples

Camera monopod support
The photographer used a monopod to stabilize his camera during the long exposure.
A monopod is lighter and easier to carry than a traditional tripod.
For sports photography, a monopod allows quick movement while keeping the camera steady.
