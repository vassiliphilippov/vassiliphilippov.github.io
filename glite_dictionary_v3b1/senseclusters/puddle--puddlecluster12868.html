puddle

noun
small pool of liquid on the ground
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdəl /; / ˈpʌd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpʌd.əl / (BrE)
a small pool of liquid, especially of water, on the ground (can also refer to something similar, like a puddle of light)

Examples

A puddle of water
A small puddle formed under the leaking pipe in the kitchen.
Children like to splash in a puddle after it rains.
He accidentally stepped into a puddle and soaked his shoes.
A puddle of light illuminated the center of the stage.
He sat in a puddle of confusion after reading the strange message.
Her jacket was left in a puddle of clothes on the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pool - a small body of still water
