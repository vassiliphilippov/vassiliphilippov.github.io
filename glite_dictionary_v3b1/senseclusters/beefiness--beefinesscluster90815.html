beefiness

noun
strong meaty flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːfinəs / (AmE), / ˈbiːfinəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a strong, meaty flavor or a thick, rich texture, especially in food

Examples

Soup beefiness
The beefiness of the soup made it especially satisfying on a cold evening.
Chefs often add mushrooms to increase the beefiness of vegetarian dishes.
He praised the beefiness of the sauce, saying it reminded him of home-cooked meals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blandness - the quality of lacking strong flavor
  • synonym: richness - the quality of having a deep, full flavor
