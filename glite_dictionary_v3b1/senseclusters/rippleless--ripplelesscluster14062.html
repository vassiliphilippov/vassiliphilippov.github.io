rippleless

adjective
smooth and calm
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpəlləs / (AmE), / ˈrɪpəlləs / (BrE)
completely smooth and calm, without any small waves or disturbances on the surface

Examples

Rippleless water surface
The pond remained rippleless even as the wind picked up around it.
She admired the rippleless surface of the glassy lake at dawn.
After the rain stopped, the street was covered in rippleless puddles reflecting the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not disturbed by waves or movement
  • antonym: choppy - rough with many small waves
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
