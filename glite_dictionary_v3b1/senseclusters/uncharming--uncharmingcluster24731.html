uncharming

adjective
not attractive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
not pleasant, attractive, or appealing in manner or appearance

Examples

An uncharming personality
Despite his skills, his uncharming personality made it hard for him to make friends.
The hotel room was clean but rather uncharming and plain.
Her uncharming response surprised everyone at the dinner table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charming - pleasant or attractive in manner or appearance
  • synonym: unattractive - not pleasing or appealing in appearance
