dead inside

adjective
emotionally numb
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɛd ɪnˈsaɪd / (AmE), / dɛd ɪnˈsaɪd / (BrE)
Lacking any feeling or emotion; emotionally numb or unresponsive.

Examples

Dead inside feeling
After years in a stressful job, many workers say they are dead inside.
Ever since the accident, he felt dead inside and could not connect with others.
She laughed at the joke, but admitted she was actually dead inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or emotion
  • synonym: numb - unable to feel, especially emotionally
