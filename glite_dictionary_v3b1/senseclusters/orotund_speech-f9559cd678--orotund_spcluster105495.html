orotund speech

noun
rich, resonant speech style
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɹəˌtʌnd spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒɹətʌnd spiːtʃ / (BrE)
speech that is rich, full, and resonant in sound, often perceived as impressive or pompous.
