nitta tree

noun
tropical Parkia tree with edible pods
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪtə tri / (AmE), / ˈnɪtə triː / (BrE)
any of several Old World tropical trees of the genus Parkia, having heads of red or yellow flowers and pods usually containing edible seeds and pulp

Examples

The nitta tree pods
Farmers harvested the pods from the nitta tree after the rainy season ended.
In the forest, the bright flowers of the nitta tree attract a variety of birds and insects.
The nitta tree provides nutritious seeds that are a food source in many tropical countries.
