Eucharistic liturgy

noun
Christian rite commemorating the Last Supper
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˌkærɪstɪk ˈlɪtərdʒi / (AmE), / juːˌkærɪstɪk ˈlɪtədʒi / (BrE)
The Christian ceremony commemorating the Last Supper, in which bread and wine are consecrated and consumed.

Examples

The Eucharistic liturgy ceremony
During the Eucharistic liturgy, participants receive consecrated bread and wine in memory of Jesus.
Many Christians gather on Sundays to celebrate the Eucharistic liturgy.
The priest led the Eucharistic liturgy in the beautifully decorated church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Holy Communion - the Christian sacrament of receiving consecrated bread and wine
  • synonym: Mass - the central act of worship in many Christian churches, especially in the Roman Catholic Church
