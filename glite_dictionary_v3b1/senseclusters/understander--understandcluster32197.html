understander

noun
person who comprehends
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈstændər / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændə / (BrE)
a person who understands or comprehends something

Examples

A understander of feelings
She is a thoughtful understander of other people's emotions.
As an understander of complex theories, he often helps his classmates.
A good teacher is also a patient understander of students' needs.
As an experienced understander of complex systems, she quickly grasped the new software's features.
He became the group's main understander, helping others make sense of the technical jargon.
A good understander listens carefully before offering advice or explanations.
