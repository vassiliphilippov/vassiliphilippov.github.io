in-service

adjective
in operation
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
currently in operation or being used as part of regular service.

Examples

In-service trains
The technician inspected the in-service equipment to ensure it was working properly.
Only in-service vehicles are allowed to use this parking area during business hours.
The company keeps a record of all in-service computers for maintenance purposes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: operational - functioning and ready for use
  • antonym: out of service - not in operation or not available for use
