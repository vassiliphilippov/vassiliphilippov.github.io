transgenerational

adjective
across generations
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌtrænzˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting multiple generations within a family or group

Examples

Transgenerational disease patterns
The study focused on transgenerational patterns of disease in the community.
Some traditions are passed down through transgenerational teaching and storytelling.
Scientists are investigating the transgenerational impact of environmental changes on wildlife.
