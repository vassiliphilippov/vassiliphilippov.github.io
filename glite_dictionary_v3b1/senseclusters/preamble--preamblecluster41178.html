preamble

verb
introduce formally or preliminarily
4.9 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌæmbəl / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌæmbəl / (BrE)
to make a preliminary introduction, especially to a formal statement or document

Examples

To preamble a speech
She decided to preamble her remarks with a quote from the Constitution.
The chairman chose to preamble the document with a short statement about its significance.
They often preamble official reports with background information for clarity.
