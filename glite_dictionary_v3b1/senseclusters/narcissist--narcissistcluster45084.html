narcissist

noun
person with excessive self-love
7.2 2.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…ëÀêr.s…ô.s…™st / (AmE), / Ààn…ëÀê.s…™.s…™st / (BrE)
a person who has an excessive interest in or admiration of themselves

Examples

A narcissist person
After years of dealing with a narcissist, she finally decided to focus on her own well-being.
It's difficult to work with a narcissist because they rarely listen to others' ideas.
Many people saw the celebrity as a narcissist who cared only about their own appearance.

  • egoist
