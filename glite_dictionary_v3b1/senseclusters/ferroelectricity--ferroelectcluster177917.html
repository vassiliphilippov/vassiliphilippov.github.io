ferroelectricity

noun
reversible electric polarization
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌfɛrəʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsəti / (BrE)
the property of certain materials to have a spontaneous electric polarization that can be reversed by an external electric field
