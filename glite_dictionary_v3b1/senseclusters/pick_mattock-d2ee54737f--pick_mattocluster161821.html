pick mattock

noun
tool combining pick and adze for digging
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪk ˈmætək / (AmE), / pɪk ˈmætək / (BrE)
A hand tool with a head that combines a pick and an adze, used for digging and breaking up hard soil or rock.

Examples

A pick mattock tool
A pick mattock is essential for grubbing out tree roots in hard soil.
He swung the pick mattock to loosen the packed clay along the path.
The gardener used a pick mattock to break through the rocky ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mattock - a hand tool for digging and chopping
  • synonym: pickaxe - a tool with a pointed end for breaking up earth and rock
