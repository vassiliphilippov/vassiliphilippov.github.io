triplet

noun
one of three siblings born together
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪplət / (AmE), / ˈtrɪplɪt / (BrE)
one of three children or animals born at the same birth to the same mother

Examples

A triplet baby
Each triplet had a distinctive personality, even though they looked alike.
She was delighted to learn she would be giving birth to triplet girls.
The hospital specializes in caring for triplet and other multiple births.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: quadruplet - one of four offspring born at the same time
  • degree lower: twin - one of two children born at the same birth
