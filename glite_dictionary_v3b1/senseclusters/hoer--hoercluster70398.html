hoer

noun
soil-cultivating tool
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊə / (BrE)
a tool or machine designed for hoeing or cultivating soil, especially between rows of crops

Examples

Mechanical hoer
The farmer used a hoer to break up the soil between the vegetable rows.
Modern hoers can be attached to tractors for faster cultivation.
After the rain, the hoer made it easier to remove weeds from the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultivator - a machine or tool used to loosen soil and remove weeds
  • synonym: hoe - a hand tool with a flat blade used for gardening or farming
