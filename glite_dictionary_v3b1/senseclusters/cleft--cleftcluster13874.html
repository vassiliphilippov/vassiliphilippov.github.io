cleft

adjective
split or divided (esp. anatomically)
5.9 5.1
Pronunciation: /kleft/ (AmE), /kleft/ (BrE)
divided or split into two or more parts, especially along a natural line (used for anatomical features, objects, or leaves)

Examples
