blooming-fool begonia

noun
hybrid winter-blooming begonia with large pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːmɪŋ fuːl bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈbluːmɪŋ fuːl bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
A hybrid variety of winter-blooming begonia cultivated for its numerous large pink flowers.
