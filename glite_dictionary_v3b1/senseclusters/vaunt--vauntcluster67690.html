vaunt

noun
extravagant self-praise
Pronunciation: / vɑnt / (AmE), / vɔːnt / (BrE)
extravagant self-praise or boasting

Examples

A vaunt of success
Her vaunt of her achievements soon wore thin with her colleagues.
In his speech, there was not a trace of vaunt or arrogance.
The leader's endless vaunt irritated those who preferred modesty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boast - an act of talking with excessive pride
  • antonym: humility - modest view of one's importance
