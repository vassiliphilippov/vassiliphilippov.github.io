adenomyosarcoma

noun
malignant tumor with glandular and muscle elements
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.əˌnoʊ.maɪ.oʊ.sɑːrˈkoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˌad.əʊ.maɪ.əʊ.sɑːˈkəʊ.mə / (BrE)
a malignant tumor composed of both glandular (adeno-) and muscular (myo-) tissue elements, often found in certain organs and characterized by aggressive growth
