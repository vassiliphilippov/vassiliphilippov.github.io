Meliphagidae

noun
family of nectar-feeding birds (honeyeaters)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛlɪˈfædʒɪdiː /; / ˌmɛləˈfægɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˌmɛlɪˈfædʒɪdiː /; / ˌmiːlɪˈfæɡɪdiː / (BrE)
