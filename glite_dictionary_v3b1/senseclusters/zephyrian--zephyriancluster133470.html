zephyrian

adjective
of the west wind
9.9
Pronunciation: / zəˈfɪriən / (AmE), / zɛˈfɪəriən /; / zɛˈfɪriən / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a gentle, mild breeze, often with a poetic or mythological reference to the west wind

Examples

A zephyrian breeze

Her dress fluttered in the zephyrian winds that swept across the hillside.
A zephyrian breeze drifted through the open window, bringing relief from the heat.
The poet described the spring morning as zephyrian and full of promise.
They enjoyed a picnic under the zephyrian sky, with soft winds rustling the leaves.
