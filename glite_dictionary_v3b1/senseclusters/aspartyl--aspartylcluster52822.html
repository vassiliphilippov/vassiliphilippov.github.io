aspartyl

noun
aspartic acid group
9.3
Pronunciation: / əˈspɑːrtɪl / (AmE), / əˈspɑːtɪl / (BrE)
a chemical group derived from aspartic acid, often found in proteins and peptides

Examples

Aspartyl group
The enzyme transfers an aspartyl group during the reaction.
Researchers studied the role of aspartyl residues in protein folding.
A mutation replaced the aspartyl group with another amino acid, altering the protein's function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspartic acid - an amino acid from which aspartyl is derived
  • synonym: peptidyl - a chemical group derived from a peptide
