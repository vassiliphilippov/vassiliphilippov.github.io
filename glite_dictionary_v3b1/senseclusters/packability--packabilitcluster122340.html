packability

noun
ability to be packed
7.0
Pronunciation: /pæk.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /pæk.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /pæk.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /pæk.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the degree to which a material, such as a powder or particles, can be compacted to occupy minimal space

Examples

Packability of powder
The packability of the new foam makes it ideal for shipping delicate items.
Researchers measured the packability of different powders to improve tablet production.
High packability is important when designing materials for limited storage space.
