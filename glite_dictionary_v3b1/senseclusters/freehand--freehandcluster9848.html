freehand

adjective
drawn or written by hand, not using tools
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈfriːhænd / (BrE)
(of drawing or writing) done by hand without the use of measuring instruments or mechanical devices.

Examples

Freehand drawing
He prefers to create portraits freehand rather than relying on rulers or compasses.
Her freehand lettering gave the invitation a personal touch.
The architect quickly sketched a freehand outline of the new building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manual - done or operated with the hands
  • antonym: mechanical - done with or by a machine or tool, not by hand
