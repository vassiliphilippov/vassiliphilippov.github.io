geebung

noun
an Australian shrub or small tree (Persoonia)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːˌbʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːˌbʌŋ / (BrE)
Any of various Australian shrubs or small trees of the genus Persoonia, with narrow leaves, yellow or white flowers, and small edible fruits.

Examples

The geebung fruit
Aboriginal Australians have traditionally eaten the fruit of the geebung.
Several species of geebung are native to the forests of New South Wales.
The geebung in the garden produces bright yellow flowers every spring.
