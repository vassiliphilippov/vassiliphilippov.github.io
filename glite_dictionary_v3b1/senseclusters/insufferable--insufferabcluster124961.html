insufferable

adjective
too extreme or unbearable
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsʌfərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsʌfərəbl /; / ɪnˈsʌfərəbəl / (BrE)
extremely unpleasant or impossible to endure; intolerable

Examples

An insufferable person
She found his arrogance truly insufferable and avoided him whenever she could.
The insufferable noise from the construction site made it impossible to concentrate.
The humidity was insufferable during the summer heatwave.
He found the constant boasting absolutely insufferable.
Her insufferable arrogance made it impossible to work with her.
The meeting dragged on, dominated by his insufferable condescension.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tolerable - able to be endured or tolerated
  • synonym: unbearable - so unpleasant or overwhelming as to be impossible to endure
