hackney cab

noun
taxi available for hire
Pronunciation: / ˈhækni kæb / (AmE), / ˈhakni kab / (BrE)
a taxi, especially one for public hire, traditionally a horse-drawn carriage and now commonly a motor vehicle, chiefly used in Britain

Examples

A hackney cab driver
He waved his hand and a hackney cab pulled up to the curb.
The streets of London were once filled with hackney cabs waiting for passengers.
Tourists enjoyed a ride through the city in a classic hackney cab.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taxi - a car licensed to transport passengers in return for payment of a fare
