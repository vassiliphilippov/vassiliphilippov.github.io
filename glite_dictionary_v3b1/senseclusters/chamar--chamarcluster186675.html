chamar

noun
leatherworker occupation
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈmɑːr / (AmE), / tʃəˈmɑː / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is working with leather, such as a tanner or leatherworker

Examples

Chamar leatherworker
The village chamar was known for making strong leather sandals for everyone.
A skilled chamar can turn raw hides into beautiful leather goods.
People brought their old shoes to the chamar for repair and polishing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tanner - a person who tans animal hides to make leather
