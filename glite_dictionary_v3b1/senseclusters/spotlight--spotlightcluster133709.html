spotlight

noun
strong lamp with focused beam, used on stage
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈspɑːt.laɪt/ /; / /ˈspɔːt.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈspɒt.laɪt/ (BrE)
