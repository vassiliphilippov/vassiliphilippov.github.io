divine

adjective
resulting from divine providence
2.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn / (BrE)
resulting from or seeming to be the result of divine providence; providential.

Examples

Divine intervention
Her escape from danger seemed almost divine in its timing.
Some believed the rainfall at last was a divine answer to their prayers.
The survivors considered their rescue a divine intervention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: providential - occurring at a favorable time as if by divine intervention
