unamenable

adjective
not responsive
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈmiːnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈmiːnəbəl / (BrE)
not willing to be influenced, persuaded, or affected by a person, method, or treatment

Examples

Unamenable to treatment
The patient was unamenable to the standard course of antibiotics, requiring a different approach.
Some children are unamenable to strict discipline and need a gentler method.
The proposal remained unamenable to any changes suggested by the committee.
Despite repeated requests, the manager remained unamenable to changing the company's policy.
Her views were so fixed that she was completely unamenable to new ideas.
The proposal failed because the committee was unamenable to compromise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amenable - willing to be influenced or controlled; responsive
  • synonym: resistant - not affected by or not willing to accept something
  • synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change one's mind or attitude
