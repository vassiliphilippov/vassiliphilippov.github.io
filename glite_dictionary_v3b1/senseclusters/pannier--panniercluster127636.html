pannier

noun
large basket carried by animal or person
Pronunciation: / ˈpæniər /; / ˈpæniɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæniə / (BrE)
a large basket or one of a pair of bags attached to the sides of an animal, bicycle, or motorcycle to carry goods

Examples

Bicycle pannier bag
She attached the pannier to her bicycle to carry groceries home.
The donkey was loaded with heavy panniers filled with vegetables for the market.
Traders used panniers to transport goods across the mountain paths.
He strapped a pannier onto each side of his bicycle before heading out for the trip.
The pannier was filled with groceries after her ride to the market.
Touring cyclists often use panniers to carry their camping equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basket - a container used to hold or carry things, often made from interwoven materials
  • synonym: saddlebag - a bag carried over the back of a saddle, especially on a horse or bicycle
