warrant

verb
justify or make necessary
5.5 9.50/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…îÀêr…ônt / (AmE), / Ààw…ír…ônt / (BrE)
to provide adequate grounds to justify or make something necessary or appropriate

Examples

Warrants further action

Synonyms
