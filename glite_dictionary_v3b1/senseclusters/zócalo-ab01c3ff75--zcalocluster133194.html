zócalo

noun
Mexican city plaza
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊkəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊkələʊ / (BrE)
a public square or plaza, especially the main one in the center of a Mexican city or town

Examples

Town zócalo plaza
Every evening, locals gather in the zócalo to enjoy music and conversation.
The festival parade passed through the crowded zócalo in the heart of the city.
Tourists often visit the zócalo to experience the vibrant atmosphere and historic buildings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plaza - a public open square in a city or town
  • synonym: square - an open area in a city where people gather
