gourami

noun
Southeast Asian fish
Pronunciation: / ɡuˈrɑːmi / (AmE), / ɡʊəˈrɑːmi / (BrE)
a freshwater fish from Asia, often brightly colored and kept in aquariums or sometimes raised for food

Examples
