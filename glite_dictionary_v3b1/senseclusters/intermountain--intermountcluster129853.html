intermountain

noun
region between mountains
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmaʊntən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmaʊntən / (BrE)
a region or area located between mountain ranges, especially referring to the Intermountain West of North America

Examples

Intermountain region
The intermountain region is known for its dry climate and unique wildlife.
Farmers in the intermountain area rely on irrigation from mountain streams.
Many species found in the intermountain are adapted to both high and low elevations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mountain range - a series of connected mountains
  • synonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
