laugh

noun
the sound of laughing
Pronunciation: / læf / (AmE), / lɑːf / (BrE)
the sound or facial expression people make when they are amused or find something funny

Examples

A loud laugh
He has a contagious laugh that always makes others smile.
Her loud laugh echoed through the empty hall.
The comedian's joke drew a hearty laugh from the audience.
His laugh was unmistakable, lighting up his entire face even before a sound escaped.
The child's laugh reflected pure joy and innocence.
There was a mischievous laugh on her face as she revealed the surprise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cry - the act of shedding tears, often as the opposite emotional expression to laugh
  • synonym: giggle - a light, silly laugh
  • synonym: smile - a pleased, kind, or amused facial expression
