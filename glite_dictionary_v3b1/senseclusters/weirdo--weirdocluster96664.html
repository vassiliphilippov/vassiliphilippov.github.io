weirdo

noun
an odd or eccentric person
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪrˌdoʊ /; / ˈwɪrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɪədəʊ / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a strange, eccentric, or unusual way

Examples

A weirdo person
If you keep acting like a weirdo, people might start to avoid you.
People at school called him a weirdo because of how he dressed.

Everyone thought the new student was a weirdo because he wore a cape to school.
She felt like a weirdo at the party because she didn't know anyone.
Stop acting like a weirdo and just join the group already.
