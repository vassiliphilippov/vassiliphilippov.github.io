designate

verb
to indicate or point out
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdezɪɡˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈdezɪɡneɪt / (BrE)
to indicate or point out a place, direction, person, or thing, either literally or figuratively

Examples

Signs designate exits
Signs at the airport designate the exits and directions for arriving passengers.
The manager will designate someone to take over the project during her absence.
The map uses a green circle to designate the picnic area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicate - to point out or show
  • synonym: specify - to state or name clearly and definitely
