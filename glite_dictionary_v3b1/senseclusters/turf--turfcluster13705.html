turf

noun
a group's claimed territory (esp. gang)
3.9 4.34/m
Pronunciation: / t…ùf / (AmE), / t…úÀêf / (BrE)
an area or territory that belongs to or is controlled by a person, group, or organization

Examples

Gang turf
After the new group moved in, disputes arose over whose turf this neighborhood was.
Police increased patrols in the area to prevent turf wars between rival gangs.
The two gangs fought over control of their turf and would not let outsiders in.
Political turf wars often slow down important reforms.
She fiercely defended her turf against any attempts to reduce her responsibilities.
The two departments are constantly fighting over turf within the company.

Synonyms

  • territory

    territory refers to land formally controlled by a country, ruler, or group, while turf is a smaller local area claimed by a person or group.
