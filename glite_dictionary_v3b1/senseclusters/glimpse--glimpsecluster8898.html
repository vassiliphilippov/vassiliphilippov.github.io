glimpse

noun
a brief or vague indication
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɡlɪmps / (AmE), / ɡlɪmps / (BrE)

Examples

A glimpse of hope

The speech gave only a glimpse of the plan's true intentions.
There was just a glimpse of understanding in his eyes.
