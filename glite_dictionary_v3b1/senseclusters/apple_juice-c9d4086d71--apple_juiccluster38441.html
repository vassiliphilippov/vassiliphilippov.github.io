apple juice

noun
juice made from apples
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəl dʒus / (AmE), / ˈapəl dʒuːs / (BrE)
the juice extracted from apples, often consumed as a beverage.

Examples

A glass of apple juice
Many children prefer apple juice to other soft drinks.
She poured a glass of apple juice for her son at breakfast.
The recipe calls for a cup of apple juice to add sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orange juice - juice made from oranges
