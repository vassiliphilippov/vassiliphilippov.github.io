coalescence

noun
process of coming together or uniting
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.əˈlɛs.əns / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.əˈlɛs.əns / (BrE)
the process of coming together to form one whole or group; the uniting of different elements or parts into a single entity

Examples

The coalescence of groups
His ideas evolved through the coalescence of different influences and cultures.
The coalescence of small companies created a powerful new corporation.
The scientist studied the coalescence of oil droplets in the solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusion - the process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity
  • synonym: merger - the combination of two or more things into one
  • antonym: separation - the process of moving things apart or making them distinct
