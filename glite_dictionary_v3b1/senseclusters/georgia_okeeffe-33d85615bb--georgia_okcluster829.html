Georgia O'Keeffe

proper noun
American painter (1887–1986), modernist artist
Pronunciation: / dʒɔr.dʒə oʊˈkiːf / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔː.dʒə əʊˈkiːf / (BrE)
(1887–1986) an American painter known for her large-format paintings of natural subjects, especially flowers, and for her contribution to modern art.
