chomping

noun
noisy chewing
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒmpɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of chewing food noisily or vigorously.

Examples

Loud chomping sounds
She was annoyed by his constant chomping at the dinner table.
The chomping of popcorn echoed through the quiet movie theater.
The horse's chomping on the carrots was surprisingly loud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chewing - the act of crushing or grinding food with the teeth
