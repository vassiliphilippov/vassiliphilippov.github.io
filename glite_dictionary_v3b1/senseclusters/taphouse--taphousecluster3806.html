taphouse

noun
tavern or pub with a bar and meals
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtæpˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A tavern or pub with a bar serving drinks, often offering light meals and public rooms for patrons.

Examples

Local taphouse bar
On Friday evenings, locals gather at the taphouse to socialize and listen to live music.
The old stone taphouse at the corner is famous for its wide selection of beers.
We stopped by a rustic taphouse for a sandwich and a pint after our hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - an establishment where alcoholic drinks are served
  • synonym: pub - a public house; a bar or tavern
  • antonym: restaurant - an establishment where meals are served to customers
