argal

noun
wild sheep of central Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrˌɡɑl / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡɑːl / (BrE)
A wild sheep found in the semidesert regions of central Asia, known for its large curved horns.

Examples

The argal sheep
Efforts are underway to protect the argal from habitat loss in central Asia.
Hunters once prized the large, spiraling horns of the male argal.
The argal is well-adapted to the harsh, rocky environments of Mongolia.
