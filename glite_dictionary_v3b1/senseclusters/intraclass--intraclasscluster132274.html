intraclass

adjective
within same class
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrəˌklæs / (AmE), / ˈɪntrəˌklɑːs / (BrE)
happening or existing within the same class or group

Examples

Intraclass variation
The researchers analyzed intraclass variation among the students in the same grade.
There was a notable intraclass difference in performance between the two teams.
The study focused on intraclass competition rather than comparing different groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: interclass - occurring between different classes or groups
