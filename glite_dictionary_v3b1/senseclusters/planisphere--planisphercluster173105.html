planisphere

noun
flat world map
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈplænɪˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈplænɪsfɪə / (BrE)
a map representing the entire surface of the Earth on a flat plane

Examples

A planisphere can help students visualize the Earth's geography on a single sheet.
