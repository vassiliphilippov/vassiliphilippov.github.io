sewer rat

noun
person regarded as dirty or degraded
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.ɚ ræt / (AmE), / ˈsjuː.ə ræt / (BrE)
an insulting or derogatory term for a person who is considered dirty, degraded, or contemptible

Examples

Called a sewer rat
After the scandal, he was treated like a sewer rat by his colleagues.
Feeling like a sewer rat, he left the party in shame.
She called him a sewer rat for betraying her trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rat - an unpleasant or despicable person (informal sense)
