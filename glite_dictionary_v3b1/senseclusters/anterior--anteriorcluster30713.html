anterior

noun
front tooth, especially incisor or canine
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriɚ / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
A tooth located at the front of the mouth, especially an incisor or canine.

Examples

Anterior tooth
Damage to the anterior can affect a patient's ability to bite into food.
In her dental records, several anteriors were noted as missing.
Orthodontists pay special attention to the alignment of the anteriors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posterior - a tooth situated toward the back of the mouth
