dully

adverb
in a dull, lifeless way
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌli / (AmE), / ˈdʌli / (BrE)

Examples

She stared out of the window dully, not reacting to anything around her.
The lights flickered dully in the foggy evening, barely illuminating the street.
