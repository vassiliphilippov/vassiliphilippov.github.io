ARP

verb
to play arpeggios
Pronunciation: / ɑːɹp / (AmE), / ɑːp / (BrE)
to play the notes of a chord quickly one after another, as in an arpeggio.

Examples

To ARP the chords
The composer instructed the pianist to ARP the left-hand chords for a softer effect.
During the rehearsal, she asked him to ARP the guitar part more clearly.
If you ARP the notes instead of playing them together, the melody will sound more flowing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arpeggiate - to play the notes of a chord in quick succession
  • antonym: strum - to play the strings of a musical instrument by sweeping the hand across them
