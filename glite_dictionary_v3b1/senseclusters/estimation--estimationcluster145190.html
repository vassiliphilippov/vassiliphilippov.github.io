estimation

noun
document appraising value
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛstəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a document that estimates or appraises the value of something, such as for insurance or tax purposes

Examples

Insurance estimation
He submitted an estimation to the tax office to determine the property's taxable value.
The insurance company requested an estimation of the house before issuing the policy.
We received a detailed estimation for the car repairs from the mechanic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appraisal - official assessment or evaluation of value
