Tragulus

noun
genus of small Southeast Asian mammals
Pronunciation: / ˈtræɡjələs /; / ˈtræɡjʊləs / (AmE), / ˈtræɡjʊləs / (BrE)
a genus of small hoofed mammals called mouse deer, found in South and Southeast Asia
