rounder

noun
a morally unrestrained, dissolute person
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndər / (AmE), / ˈraʊndə / (BrE)
a person, usually a man, who is morally unrestrained and often indulges in dissolute or debauched behavior

Examples

A rounder at bars
After years of living as a rounder, he finally decided to turn his life around.
He earned a reputation as a rounder who rarely refused a night of drinking.
The old saloon used to attract every rounder in town.
