protopathy

noun
basic emotional response
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈpæθi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈpæθɪ / (BrE)
a primitive or undifferentiated emotional response, often basic and not clearly defined

Examples

Therapists may help clients move from protopathy to more differentiated feelings.
