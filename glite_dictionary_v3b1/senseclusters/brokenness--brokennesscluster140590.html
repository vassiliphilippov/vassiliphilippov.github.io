brokenness

noun
fragmented state
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbroʊ.kən.nəs/ /; / /ˈbroʊk.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbrəʊ.kən.nəs/ /; / /ˈbrəʊk.nəs/ (BrE)
the state or quality of being fragmented, incomplete, or lacking smoothness, especially in speech or language

Examples

Brokenness in speech
The brokenness of his sentences made it difficult to follow his story.
Despite the brokenness in her speech, her message was heartfelt and clear.
Learning a new language often involves a period of brokenness before achieving fluency.
