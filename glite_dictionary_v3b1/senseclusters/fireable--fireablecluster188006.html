fireable

adjective
able to be dismissed
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərəbl / (BrE)
able to be dismissed from a job or position, usually for cause or poor performance

Examples

Fireable employee
Arriving late every day could make you fireable in a strict workplace.
The manager explained that violating the code of conduct was a fireable offense.
He worried that his mistake would be considered fireable by his employer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissible - able to be dismissed from employment
  • antonym: unfireable - not able to be dismissed from a job
