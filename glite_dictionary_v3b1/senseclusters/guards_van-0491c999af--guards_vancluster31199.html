guard's van

noun
railway car for the guard (British)
1.3 7.0
Pronunciation: /gɑːrdz væn/ (AmE), /gɑːdz væn/ (BrE)
a railway carriage at the end of a train, used by the guard and for storing luggage or parcels

Examples
