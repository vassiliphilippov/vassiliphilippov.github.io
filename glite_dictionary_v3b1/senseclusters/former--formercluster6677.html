former

noun
shaping tool or mold
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrmər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmə / (BrE)
a tool, mold, or device used to shape materials into a specific form or structure

Examples

Shoe former
The shoemaker used a former to shape the leather for the new pair of shoes.
Metal sheets are pressed over a former to create the curved panels for the car.
Each hat is shaped on a wooden former before being finished by hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mold - a hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material
  • synonym: template - a pattern or guide used to shape or cut materials
