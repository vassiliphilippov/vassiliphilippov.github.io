scrimmage

noun
football practice game between team squads
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a practice game or session in sports, especially football or rugby, used to prepare for real matches

Examples

A football scrimmage
During practice, the players took part in a heated scrimmage.
Many rookies get their first taste of real competition in a scrimmage against their teammates.
The coach organized a scrimmage to prepare the team for Saturday's game.
The coach organized a scrimmage to help the players prepare for the upcoming season.
Before the official game, the teams had a friendly scrimmage to test their strategies.
During yesterday's scrimmage, the new players got a chance to show their skills.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: match - an organized game or contest between teams or players
  • synonym: practice - repeated exercise in or performance of an activity
  • synonym: skirmish - a minor battle or conflict; the word from which 'scrimmage' derives
