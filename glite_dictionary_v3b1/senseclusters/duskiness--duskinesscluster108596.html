duskiness

noun
darkness of complexion
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌskinəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌskinəs / (BrE)
a dark or swarthy complexion, especially of the skin

Examples

Duskiness of skin
He wrote of the model's striking duskiness in his journal.
Her duskiness set her apart in the small village where most had pale skin.
The painter captured the unique duskiness of his subject's complexion in the portrait.
