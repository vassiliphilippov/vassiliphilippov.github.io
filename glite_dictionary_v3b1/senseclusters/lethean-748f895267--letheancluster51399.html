Lethean

noun
forgetfulness-inducing drink
Pronunciation: / ˈliːθiən / (AmE), / ˈliːθiən / (BrE)
a drink, potion, or substance that causes forgetfulness, especially one likened to the water of the river Lethe in Greek mythology

Examples

Lethean potion
The wizard offered her a Lethean potion to erase her painful memories.
In the story, the hero refused the Lethean drink, choosing to remember his past.
Some legends speak of a Lethean substance that can make you forget everything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elixir - a magical or medicinal potion
  • antonym: memory aid - something that helps you remember
