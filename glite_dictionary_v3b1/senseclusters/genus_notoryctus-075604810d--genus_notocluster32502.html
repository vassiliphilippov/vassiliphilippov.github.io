genus Notoryctus

noun
genus of the marsupial mole
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs noʊˈtɔːr.ɪk.təs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs nəʊˈtɒr.ɪk.təs / (BrE)
The genus of marsupial mammals comprising the marsupial mole, and the type genus of the family Notoryctidae.

Examples

Genus Notoryctus marsupial mole
Members of the genus Notoryctus are commonly known as marsupial moles.
Scientists discovered a new species within the genus Notoryctus in the deserts of central Australia.
The genus Notoryctus is unique among marsupials for its highly specialized burrowing adaptations.
