fanon

noun
unofficial fan stories
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈfæn.ɒn / (BrE)
ideas, stories, or interpretations about a fictional work created by fans and widely accepted in the fan community, but not part of the official story

Examples

Popular fanon theory
Many fans accept the fanon that the two characters are secretly related, even though it is not in the official story.
The online community debated whether a new theory should be considered fanon or just a personal idea.
Some fanon interpretations become so popular that new fans believe they are part of the original work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: canon - the official story or accepted body of work
  • synonym: fan fiction - stories written by fans using characters or settings from an existing work
  • synonym: headcanon - a personal interpretation or belief about a fictional work held by an individual fan
