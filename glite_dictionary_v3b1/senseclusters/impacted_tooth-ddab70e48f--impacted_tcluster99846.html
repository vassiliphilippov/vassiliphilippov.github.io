impacted tooth

noun
tooth unable to erupt due to crowding
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæktɪd tuθ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæktɪd tuːθ / (BrE)
a tooth that is prevented from erupting normally because it is crowded in its socket

Examples

An impacted tooth
An impacted tooth can cause pain and swelling if left untreated.
The dentist recommended removing the impacted tooth to prevent further complications.
Wisdom teeth are often the most common type of impacted tooth.
