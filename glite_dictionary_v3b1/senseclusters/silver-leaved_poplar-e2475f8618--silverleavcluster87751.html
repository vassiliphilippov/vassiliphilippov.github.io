silver-leaved poplar

noun
a white-barked poplar tree with silvery leaves
7.2 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪl.vər.liːvd ˈpɑː.plər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪl.və.liːvd ˈpɒ.plə/ (BrE)
a species of poplar tree (Populus alba) with distinctive white bark and leaves that have whitish undersides, widely cultivated for ornamental purposes

The silver-leaved poplar is often planted along city streets for its attractive foliage.
