flavored

verb
having added taste
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
having had a particular taste or seasoning added to it.

Examples

Soup flavored with herbs
The chef prepared a delicious soup flavored with fresh herbs and garlic.
She bought a box of strawberry flavored candies for the children.
This tea is naturally flavored with lemon and honey for a refreshing taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - without added flavor or seasoning
  • synonym: seasoned - having had spices or flavorings added
