rebirth

noun
spiritual renewal; starting a new life
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈbɝθ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbɜːθ / (BrE)
a process of spiritual enlightenment resulting in a person beginning a new or transformed life

Examples

Spiritual rebirth
After battling addiction, she experienced a spiritual rebirth and started helping others.
Many view meditation as a path to personal rebirth and inner peace.
The retreat led to a profound rebirth that changed his outlook on life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: renewal - the process of making something new or stronger
