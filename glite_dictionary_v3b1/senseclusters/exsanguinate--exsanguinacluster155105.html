exsanguinate

verb
to drain blood
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈsæŋ.gwɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ɪk.ˈsæŋ.gwə.neɪt/ (AmE), /ɛk.ˈsæŋ.gwɪ.neɪt/ /; / /ɪk.ˈsæŋ.gwɪ.neɪt/ (BrE)
