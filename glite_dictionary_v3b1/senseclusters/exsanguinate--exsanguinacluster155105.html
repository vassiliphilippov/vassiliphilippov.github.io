exsanguinate

verb
to drain blood
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈsæŋɡwɪˌneɪt /; / ɛkˈsæŋɡwəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɛkˈsaŋɡwɪneɪt / (BrE)
to drain or lose a large amount of blood from a person or animal, often resulting in death
