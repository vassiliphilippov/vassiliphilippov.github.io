unmanliness

noun
lack of traditionally masculine traits
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænli.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈmæn.lɪ.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking traditionally masculine traits or characteristics, especially in a manner considered unmanly or effeminate (often used derogatorily)

Examples

His unmanliness criticized
He was often criticized for his apparent unmanliness by those who valued traditional gender roles.
Some people wrongly equate sensitivity with unmanliness, overlooking the value of emotional openness.
The old tale shames unmanliness in warriors who show fear instead of bravery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminacy - the trait of being effeminate or having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman
  • antonym: masculinity - possession of the qualities traditionally associated with men
