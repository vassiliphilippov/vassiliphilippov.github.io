feather geranium

noun
aromatic Eurasian plant with yellow-green flowers
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðər dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
A Eurasian aromatic plant with oak-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow-green flowers, known for being naturalized in North America.

Examples

The feather geranium plant
Gardeners often grow the feather geranium for its clusters of yellow-green flowers.
The feather geranium has distinctive oak-like leaves and a pleasant aroma.
Wild patches of feather geranium can now be found throughout parts of North America.
