ebony

noun
hard dark wood from the ebony tree
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛbəni / (AmE), / ˈɛbəni / (BrE)
A dense, hard, dark-colored wood from the ebony tree, valued for use in furniture, musical instruments, and decorative items.

Examples

Ebony wood furniture
Her desk was an antique, made of polished ebony wood.
The craftsman carved a statue from a solid block of ebony.
The piano keys were originally made from ebony and ivory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - hard reddish-brown wood used for high-quality furniture
