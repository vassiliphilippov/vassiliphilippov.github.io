dig

noun
small indentation or gouge
6.5
Pronunciation: / dɪɡ / (AmE), / dɪɡ / (BrE)
a small indentation or gouge in a surface, or a quick poke with a finger or elbow

Examples

A small dig
Careless handling left a noticeable dig on the table's edge.
She was disappointed to find a dig in the glossy finish of her new phone.
The book looked almost new, except for a small dig in the back cover.
He couldn’t resist giving his friend a playful dig during the meeting.
She gave me a sharp dig in the ribs to get my attention.
With a discreet dig to her side, he reminded her to stay quiet.

  • synonym: scratch - a mark or wound made by scratching
