speakable

adjective
able to be spoken or uttered
4.5 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàspiÀêk…ôb…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàspiÀêk…ôbl / (BrE)
able to be spoken or expressed in words; capable of being uttered.

Examples

A speakable word
She put her feelings into speakable words for the first time.
Some topics are still not speakable in polite society.
The news was finally speakable after years of secrecy.

Synonyms

  • utterable
    6.7 0.01/m
    utterable describes words or thoughts that can be pronounced clearly, while speakable is a neutral word for things you can say.
  • sayable
    5.8 0.01/m
    sayable sounds more informal and fits casual speech, while speakable is broader and fits neutral written or spoken contexts.
  • expressible
    5.3 0.10/m
    expressible can mean shown in words or other ways, while speakable refers specifically to things that can be spoken aloud.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unspeakable - impossible to express in words; too bad or shocking to be spoken
