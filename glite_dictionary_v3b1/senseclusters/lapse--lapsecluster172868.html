lapse

verb
to end, cease, or expire
5.6 4.2
Pronunciation: /læps/ (AmE), /læps/ (BrE)
to come to an end, expire, or cease to be maintained

Examples

The contract lapses
Communication between the two friends had lapsed over the years.
Don't let your membership lapse—renew it before the deadline.
Their insurance policy will lapse if they forget to pay the premium.
If you don't pay the annual fee, you will lapse your gym membership.
She accidentally lapsed her subscription by forgetting to update her payment details.
Some insurance policies automatically lapse after a missed payment.

  • synonym: expire - to come to an end or cease to be valid
