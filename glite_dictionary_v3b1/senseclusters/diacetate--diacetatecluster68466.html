diacetate

adjective
containing two acetate groups
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæsɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / daɪˈæsɪteɪt / (BrE)
composed of or treated with a compound containing two acetate groups, especially in reference to films, fibers, or materials

Examples

Diacetate film material
The laboratory used diacetate fibers to produce lightweight, durable fabrics.
Many early color films were made with a diacetate base for improved stability.
The chemical process resulted in a diacetate compound with unique properties.
