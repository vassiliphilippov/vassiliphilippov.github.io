narco

noun
drug trafficker
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑrkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːkəʊ / (BrE)
a person involved in the illegal drug trade, especially as a trafficker or dealer

Examples

A narco boss
The authorities arrested a well-known narco near the border last night.
Many movies depict the dangerous lives of narcos in Latin America.
The journalist wrote an article about the influence of a powerful narco in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug dealer - a person who sells illegal drugs
  • antonym: police officer - a person who enforces the law
  • synonym: smuggler - a person who illegally transports goods, especially drugs
