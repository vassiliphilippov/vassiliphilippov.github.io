rotatably

adverb
in a rotating manner
Pronunciation: / roʊˈteɪtəbli / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that allows something to turn or rotate around an axis

Examples

Attached rotatably to base
The wheels are attached rotatably to the base, making the cart easy to maneuver.
Each mirror is mounted rotatably so it can be adjusted for the best angle.
The camera is fixed rotatably on the tripod for smooth panning shots.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixedly - in a way that does not allow movement or rotation
  • synonym: pivotally - in a way that allows turning around a central point
