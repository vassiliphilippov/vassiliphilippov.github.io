crocodilian reptile

noun
large predatory semi-aquatic reptile of Crocodylia
7.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /krɑː.kə.ˈdɪl.ɪ.ən ˈrep.taɪl/ /; / /krɑː.kə.ˈdɪl.ɪ.ən ˈrep.taɪ.əl/ (AmE), /krɒk.ə.ˈdɪl.ɪ.ən ˈrep.taɪl/ /; / /krɒk.ə.ˈdɪl.ɪ.ən ˈrep.taɪ.əl/ (BrE)
any large, predatory, semi-aquatic reptile of the order Crocodylia, such as crocodiles, alligators, caimans, and gharials.

Examples

A crocodilian reptile stalks prey

The Nile is home to several species of crocodilian reptile, including the fearsome Nile crocodile.
