styloid

adjective
slender and pointed
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɔɪd / (BrE)
having a slender, pointed, or pencil-like shape, especially referring to anatomical structures

Examples

Styloid shape

A styloid projection can be found at the base of the human skull.
The bone had a distinct styloid shape, making it easy to identify during the anatomy lesson.
