chalk

noun
stick for writing or drawing on blackboards
Pronunciation: / tʃɔk /; / tʃɑk / (AmE), / tʃɔːk / (BrE)
a soft, white or colored material (a type of rock) used for writing, drawing, or as a color

Related Terms

  • synonym: crayon - a stick of colored wax used for drawing
  • synonym: limestone - a sedimentary rock consisting mainly of calcium carbonate
  • degree lower: white - the color of fresh snow or milk; the lightest color
