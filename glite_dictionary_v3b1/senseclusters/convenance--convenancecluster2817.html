convenance

noun
suitability or propriety
Pronunciation: / kənˈviːnəns / (AmE), / kənˈviːnəns / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable, appropriate, or proper for a particular situation

Examples

Convenance of manners
The convenance of his behavior at the formal dinner was praised by everyone.
They debated the convenance of holding the event outdoors in unpredictable weather.
Questions arose about the convenance of her remarks during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriateness - the quality of being suitable or fitting
  • antonym: impropriety - the state of not being proper or suitable
  • synonym: propriety - the state of being proper or appropriate
