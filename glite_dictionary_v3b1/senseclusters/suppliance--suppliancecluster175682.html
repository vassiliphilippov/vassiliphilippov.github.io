suppliance

noun
submissive obedience
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / səˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
the act of yielding to another's authority or showing submissive obedience

Examples

Suppliance to authority
Her suppliance to company rules surprised her outspoken colleagues during the audit.
The manager expected complete suppliance from all team members when implementing new policies.
In some cultures, suppliance to elders is considered a sign of respect and good manners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliance - the act of obeying a rule or request
  • antonym: defiance - open resistance or refusal to obey
