section

noun
surgical cutting of tissues or organs
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of cutting into tissues or organs, especially by a surgeon during an operation

Examples

Surgical section
During the operation, a precise section allowed safe access to the underlying organ.
Microscopic examination of the tissue section revealed abnormal cells.
The surgeon performed a section of the liver to remove the tumor.
