Abies lowiana

noun
species of North American fir tree
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz loʊˈwaɪ.ə.nə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz ləʊˈwaɪ.ənə / (BrE)
