nonmeritorious

adjective
without merit
7.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.mə.rɪ.ˈtɔːr.iː.əs/ (AmE), /nɒn.mə.rɪ.ˈtɪə.riː.əs/ (BrE)
lacking merit, worthiness, or a valid basis; not deserving praise, reward, or approval

Examples
