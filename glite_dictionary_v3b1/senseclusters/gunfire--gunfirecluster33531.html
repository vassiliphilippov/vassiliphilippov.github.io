Synonyms

  • shooting
    2.3 16/m
    shooting is the act of firing guns as an event in a place, while gunfire often refers to the sound or a series of shots.
  • firing
    3.0 0.6/m
    firing is the act of discharging weapons in a technical or military sense, while gunfire refers to the sound or ongoing shooting.
  • gunshot
    2.3 11/m
    a gunshot is a single shot fired from a gun, while gunfire means multiple shots or the loud sound they make.
  • gunshot
    2.1 7/m
    gunshot is the loud sound of one gun being fired, while gunfire often describes the sound or act of many shots.
  • discharge
    5.5 0.5/m
    discharge is the formal act of firing a weapon once in technical contexts, while gunfire is the common word for shooting or its sound.
