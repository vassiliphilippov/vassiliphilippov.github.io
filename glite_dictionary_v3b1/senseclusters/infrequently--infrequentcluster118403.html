infrequently

adverb
not often; rarely
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfriːkwəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfriːkwəntli / (BrE)
not often; rarely or at intervals that are not regular or frequent

Examples

Used infrequently
Because the bridge is used infrequently, it is in need of repair.
He visits his hometown infrequently due to his busy work schedule.
Lightning storms occur infrequently in this region during winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frequently - often; at regular or short intervals
  • synonym: rarely - not often; seldom
