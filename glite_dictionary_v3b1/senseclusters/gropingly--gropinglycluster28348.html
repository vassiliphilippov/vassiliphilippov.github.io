gropingly

adverb
in an uncertain or searching manner
9.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgroʊ.pɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈgroʊ.pɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈgrəʊ.pɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈgrəʊ.pɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner that is uncertain, tentative, or seeking, as if searching or feeling one's way.

Examples

To grope gropingly in darkness
He reached out gropingly in the darkness, searching for the light switch.
She answered the question gropingly, unsure of the correct response.
The writer moves gropingly through complex ideas, slowly clarifying her thoughts.
