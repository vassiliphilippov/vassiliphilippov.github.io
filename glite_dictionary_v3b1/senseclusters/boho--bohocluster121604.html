boho

adjective
artistic, free-spirited
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊhəʊ / (BrE)
describing a style or lifestyle that is artistic, unconventional, and free-spirited, often inspired by bohemian culture

Examples

Boho style outfit
She wore a boho dress with flowing sleeves and colorful patterns to the festival.
The café's boho atmosphere attracted artists and musicians from all over the city.
He prefers a boho look, mixing vintage clothes with handmade accessories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bohemian - unconventional and artistic in style or lifestyle
  • antonym: conventional - following traditional or accepted standards
