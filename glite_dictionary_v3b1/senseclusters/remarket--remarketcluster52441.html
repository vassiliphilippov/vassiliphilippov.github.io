remarket

verb
to resell securities
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈmɑːrkɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
to sell or promote a product or security again, often using a new strategy or in a different market

Examples

To remarket a product
The company decided to remarket its unsold bonds to attract new investors.
After the initial offering, the bank will remarket the securities in the secondary market.
Financial institutions often remarket commercial paper to maintain liquidity.
After poor sales, the company decided to remarket the device with a new advertising campaign.
They plan to remarket the service to younger customers by highlighting its modern features.
The publisher chose to remarket the book with a different cover and title.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buy back - to purchase something again that was previously sold
  • synonym: launch - to introduce a product or service to the market
  • synonym: rebrand - to change the image or identity of a product or company
  • synonym: resell - to sell something again after buying or owning it
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove a product or service from the market
