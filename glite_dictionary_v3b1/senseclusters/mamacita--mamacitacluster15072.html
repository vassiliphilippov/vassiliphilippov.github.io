mamacita

noun
affectionate term for mother
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːməˈsiːtə / (AmE), / ˌmaməˈsiːtə / (BrE)
an affectionate or diminutive term for mother, often used by children or in Spanish-speaking families

Examples

My mamacita hugs me
The little girl ran to her mamacita for a comforting hug after she fell.
At the family party, the boy kissed his mamacita and asked for another story.
He called out to his mamacita from across the playground, waving excitedly.
