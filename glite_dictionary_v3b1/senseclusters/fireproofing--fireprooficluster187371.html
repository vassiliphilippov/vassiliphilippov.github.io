fireproofing

adjective
resistant to fire
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
describing a material, coating, or treatment that resists fire or protects structures from burning

Examples

Fireproofing paint
The building was treated with a fireproofing spray to meet safety regulations.
Engineers recommended a fireproofing solution for the wooden beams in the old house.
A fireproofing layer was applied to the walls during construction to prevent fire damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire-resistant - able to withstand fire or great heat
  • antonym: flammable - easily set on fire
