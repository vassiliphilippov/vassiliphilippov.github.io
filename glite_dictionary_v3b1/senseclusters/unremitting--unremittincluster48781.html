unremitting

adjective
never relaxing or stopping; continual
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈmɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈmɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(Of something unpleasant) never relaxing or slackening; continuing without interruption or weakening.

Examples

The unremitting rain
Despite unremitting rain, the construction crew finished the project on time.
She faced unremitting pressure at work throughout the entire year.
The patient's unremitting pain made it difficult for her to sleep at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceaseless - without stopping or pausing; unending
  • synonym: incessant - continuing without pause or interruption
  • antonym: intermittent - occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous or steady
