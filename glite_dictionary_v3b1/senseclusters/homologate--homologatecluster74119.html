homologate

verb
to officially approve
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ho äÀàm…ëÀêl…ôÀå…°e…™t / (AmE), / h…ôÀàm…íl…ôÀå…°e…™t / (BrE)
to officially approve or confirm something, especially for legal, technical, or regulatory purposes

Examples

To homologate a product
The new safety equipment was homologated by the international standards committee before being sold.
Before a car can compete in the championship, it must be homologated by the racing authority.
The government homologated the vaccine after a thorough review of the clinical trial data.
