cuspidated

adjective
having points or cusps
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌspɪˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌspɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)

The sculpture featured cuspidated edges extending from its crown.
