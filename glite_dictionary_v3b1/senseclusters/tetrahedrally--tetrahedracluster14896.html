tetrahedrally

adverb
in tetrahedron shape
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛtrəˈhiːdrəli / (AmE), / ˌtɛtrəˈhiːdrəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or has the shape of a tetrahedron, especially in geometry or chemistry

Examples

To tetrahedrally arrange molecules
The molecules are arranged tetrahedrally around the central atom in this compound.
In this crystal structure, each ion is surrounded tetrahedrally by four other ions.
The carbon atoms bond tetrahedrally in a diamond, creating its strong structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geometrically - in a manner relating to geometry or geometric shapes
