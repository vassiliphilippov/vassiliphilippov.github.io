illiberal

adjective
narrow-minded; intolerant of opposing views
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪbɚəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
Unwilling to accept or respect different opinions; narrow-minded or intolerant.

Examples

Illiberal views
An illiberal society often suppresses dissent and critical thinking.
His illiberal attitude prevented any genuine discussion during the meeting.
She found his illiberal refusal to consider opposing views frustrating.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liberal - willing to accept and respect different opinions or behaviors
  • synonym: narrow-minded - unwilling to accept or respect different opinions; intolerant
  • antonym: tolerant - willing to accept or endure ideas or behaviors different from one's own
