unblock

verb
play cards to allow a partner to win tricks
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblɑk / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɒk / (BrE)
(in cards) to play so as to remove lower cards from a suit, enabling a partner to win with higher cards in a combined holding.

Examples

To unblock a suit
Before leading the ace, remember to unblock any lower cards first.
In the last round, she expertly unblocked the diamond suit to help her partner win.
You should unblock your suit now to avoid trapping your partner's high cards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: block - to prevent the play of high cards in a suit by holding onto lower ones
