deer's-ears

noun
a western North American herb (Frasera) with narrow leaves
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrz ˌɪrz / (AmE), / ˈdɪəz ˌɪəz / (BrE)
a tall perennial herb of the genus Frasera, native to dry upland areas of western North America; known for its erect stems and narrow leaves resembling a deer's ears
