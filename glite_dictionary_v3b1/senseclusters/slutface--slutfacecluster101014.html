slutface

noun
offensive insult for a disrespectful person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌt.feɪs / (AmE), / ˈslʌt.feɪs / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar insult, used to describe someone regarded as disrespectful or contemptible.

Examples

A slutface insult
Calling someone a slutface is extremely rude and offensive.
He acted like a slutface and insulted everyone at the party.
Their arguments turned ugly when she called him a slutface in front of everyone.
