business sector

noun
part of the economy consisting of businesses
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪz.nəs ˈsɛk.tər/ /; / /ˈbɪz.nɪs ˈsɛk.tər/ (AmE), /ˈbɪz.nɪs ˈsɛk.tə/ /; / /ˈbɪz.nəs ˈsɛk.tə/ (BrE)
The part of a country's economy made up of companies, commercial enterprises, and organizations engaged in business activities, considered collectively.

Examples
