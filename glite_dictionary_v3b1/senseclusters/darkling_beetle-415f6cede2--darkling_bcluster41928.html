darkling beetle

noun
a black or dark-colored hard-bodied beetle
5.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːr.klɪŋ ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈdɑː.klɪŋ ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
a black or dark-colored, hard-bodied beetle, typically slow-moving, whose larvae often feed on decaying plant material or stored grain
