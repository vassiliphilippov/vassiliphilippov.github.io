lethally

adverb
in a deadly way
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈliːθəli / (AmE), / ˈliːθəli / (BrE)
in a way that is capable of causing death or is deadly

Examples

Lethally poisoned
The chemicals were mixed lethally, making the solution extremely dangerous.
He was lethally wounded in the accident and could not be saved.
Some diseases can spread lethally if not treated quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatally - in a way that causes death
  • antonym: harmlessly - in a way that does not cause harm or death
