lethally

adverb
in a deadly way
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.θə.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈliː.θə.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Lethally poisoned

He was lethally wounded in the accident and could not be saved.
Some diseases can spread lethally if not treated quickly.
