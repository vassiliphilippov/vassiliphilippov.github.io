Araucariaceae

noun
family of tall evergreen trees (botanical)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəwˌkɛriˈeɪsiːˌiː /; / əˌraʊ.kəˌriː.əˈsiː.iː / (AmE), / ˌarɔːˌkɛəriˈeɪsɪˌiː /; / əˌraʊ.kəˌrɪəˈsiː.iː / (BrE)
a family of tall evergreen, cone-bearing trees native to South America and Australia, with broad, leathery leaves (includes trees like the monkey puzzle tree and Norfolk Island pine)
