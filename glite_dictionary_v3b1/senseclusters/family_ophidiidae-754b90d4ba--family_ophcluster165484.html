family Ophidiidae

noun
a family of eellike marine fishes (cusk-eels)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfæməli oʊˌfɪdiˈaɪdi / (AmE), / ˌfæmɪli əʊˌfɪdɪˈaɪdiː / (BrE)
