reincarnate

verb
to be born again in another body
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɪnkɑrˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɪnkɑːˌneɪt / (BrE)
(of a soul or spirit) to be born in another body after death.

Examples

To reincarnate after death
According to some legends, the wise sage was destined to reincarnate as a great leader.
Many followers of Eastern religions believe that people can reincarnate after death.
She hoped that her beloved pet would reincarnate and find its way back to her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reincarnation - the rebirth of a soul in a new body
  • synonym: transmigrate - to pass into another body after death
