lb

noun
unit of weight (pound)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɛl biː / (AmE), / ɛl biː / (BrE)
A unit of weight equal to 16 ounces avoirdupois, used chiefly in the US and the UK; abbreviation for 'pound.'

Examples

lb of weight
She lost five lb on her new diet program.
The label states that each bag contains exactly one lb of coffee.
The package weighs just under 2 lb and will ship easily.
