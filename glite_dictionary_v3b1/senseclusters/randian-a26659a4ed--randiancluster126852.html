Randian

noun
follower of Ayn Rand
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈræn.dɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈræn.dɪ.ən/ (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the ideas and philosophy of Ayn Rand, especially Objectivism

Examples

A Randian thinker
As a proud Randian, she often quoted passages from Atlas Shrugged in debates.
The club attracted several Randians who discussed philosophy and economics every week.
He was labeled a Randian because of his strong advocacy for individual rights and capitalism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Objectivist - a supporter of Objectivism, Ayn Rand's philosophy
  • antonym: collectivist - a person who supports collective control or ownership, often opposed to Objectivist views
