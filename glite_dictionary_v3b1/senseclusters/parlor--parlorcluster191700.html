parlor

noun
reception room in an inn or club
2.3 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrlər /; / ˈpɑːrlər /; / ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
a room in a house, inn, or other building where people can sit, talk, and entertain guests
