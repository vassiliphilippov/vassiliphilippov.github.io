skeet shooting

noun
sport of shooting at flying clay targets
5.2
Pronunciation: / skit ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / skiːt ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
A sport in which participants shoot at clay targets that are launched into the air to simulate the flight of birds.

Examples

Competitive skeet shooting
Competitive skeet shooting requires precision, speed, and concentration.
He took up skeet shooting as a hobby last summer.
The club offers lessons in skeet shooting for beginners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trap shooting - a similar sport involving shooting at clay targets from different angles
