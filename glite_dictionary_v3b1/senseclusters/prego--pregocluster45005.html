prego

interjection
Italian polite invitation
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpreɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpreɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
an Italian interjection used to politely offer, invite, or give permission, similar to 'please' or 'go ahead' in English

Examples

Prego, after you
He held the door open and said prego as she entered the restaurant.
When offered a seat, the host gestured and said prego with a smile.
At the counter, the barista nodded and said prego to the next customer in line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go ahead - used to give someone permission to proceed
  • synonym: please - used to make a polite request or invitation
