subadult

adjective
not fully mature
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbəˈdʌlt / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈædʌlt / (BrE)
not yet fully adult; almost mature but not having reached full adulthood

Examples

Subadult wolf
The researchers observed the behavior of subadult wolves as they learned to hunt in packs.
A subadult elephant may look large but is not yet ready to lead the herd.
Many subadult fish are similar in appearance to adults but have not reached reproductive age.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: adult - fully grown or mature
  • degree lower: childish - typical of a child; immature
  • synonym: juvenile - not yet fully grown or mature
