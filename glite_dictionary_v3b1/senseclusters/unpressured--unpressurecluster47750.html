unpressured

adjective
not pressurized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɛʃərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɛʃəd / (BrE)
not containing or subjected to increased air or gas pressure; not pressurized

Examples

Unpressured cabin
The old aircraft had an unpressured cabin, so it could not fly at high altitudes.
Passengers noticed the difference when sitting in an unpressured section of the plane.
Because the room was unpressured, special equipment was not required for entry.
