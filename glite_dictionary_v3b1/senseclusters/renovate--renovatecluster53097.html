  • overhaul
    4.6 2.37/m

  • recondition
    5.4 0.32/m
    recondition restores something to a good working state, often machines or items, while renovate focuses on buildings and appearance.
  • rehabilitate
    4.9 0.76/m

  • restitute
    8.9 0.00/m
