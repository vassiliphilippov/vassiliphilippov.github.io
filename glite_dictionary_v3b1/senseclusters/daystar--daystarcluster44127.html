daystar

noun
the planet Venus as seen before sunrise
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌstɑː / (BrE)
a planet, especially Venus, seen just before sunrise and shining brightly in the eastern sky; the morning star

Examples

The daystar planet
Early risers could catch a glimpse of the daystar glowing low in the dawn sky.
Poets often compare hope to the arrival of the daystar after a long night.
Venus, known as the daystar, has inspired myths across many ancient cultures.
