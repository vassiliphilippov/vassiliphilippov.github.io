moneyball

noun
data-driven sports strategy
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌbɔːl / (BrE)
the use of advanced data analysis and statistics to improve the performance and management of a sports team

Examples

Moneyball strategy
The team's moneyball strategy helped them compete with much wealthier clubs.
Many franchises now use moneyball methods to scout undervalued players.
After reading about moneyball, the coach decided to focus more on player statistics.
