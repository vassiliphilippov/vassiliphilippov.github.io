wedding band

noun
a ring given at a wedding ceremony
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛdɪŋ bænd / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ band / (BrE)
a ring, usually made of precious metal, exchanged or worn during a marriage ceremony to show that someone is married
