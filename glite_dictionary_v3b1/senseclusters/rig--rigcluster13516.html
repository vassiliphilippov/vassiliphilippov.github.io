rig

noun
horse-drawn wheeled vehicle
Pronunciation: / rɪɡ / (AmE), / rɪɡ / (BrE)
a vehicle, especially a large truck or a horse-drawn carriage

Examples

A truck rig
He carefully loaded the luggage onto the back of the rig before their journey.
The old rancher hitched his horses to the rig and set out for town.
They arrived at the festival in a beautifully decorated rig.
Many long-haul drivers decorate the inside of their rig to make it feel like home.
She learned how to maneuver a rig through narrow city streets.
The driver parked his rig at the truck stop for the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - a horse-drawn vehicle for people
  • synonym: lorry - a large motor vehicle for transporting goods (British usage)
  • synonym: semi - a large truck with a semi-trailer
