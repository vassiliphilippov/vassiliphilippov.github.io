foiled

adjective
covered in metallic foil
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔɪld / (AmE), / ˈfɔɪld / (BrE)
covered with or having a thin layer of metallic foil (used for decoration, packaging, or a shiny finish).

Examples

Foiled chocolate
She admired the foiled invitation with shiny silver lettering and embossing.
The chocolate came in a foiled wrapper that crinkled when he opened it.
The designer used foiled accents on the poster to catch the light across the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gilded - covered with a thin layer of gold or a gold-like finish; similar decorative effect
  • synonym: wrapped - covered or enclosed, often for protection or presentation
