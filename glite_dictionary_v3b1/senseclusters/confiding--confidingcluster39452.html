confiding

adjective
willing to entrust personal matters
Pronunciation: / kənˈfaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈfaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
Willing to trust and share personal matters with others; showing readiness to confide.

Examples

A confiding child
As a confiding child, he often shared his secrets with his teachers.
His confiding manner made people want to open up to him in return.
She felt comfortable enough to be confiding with her close friends about her fears.
