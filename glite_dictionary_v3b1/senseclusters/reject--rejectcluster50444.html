reject

verb
fail to accept a transplanted organ or tissue
7.7 1.34/m

(of the body or immune system) to resist or fail to accept a transplanted organ or tissue, causing it to be attacked as foreign.

Examples

Body rejects an organ
After months without complications, her body began to reject the transplanted kidney.
Doctors were concerned that the patient's immune system would reject the new heart.
His body rejected the liver of the donor.
