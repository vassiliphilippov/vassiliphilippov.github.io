tailpipe

noun
the exhaust pipe at the back of a car
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌpaɪp / (BrE)
A pipe at the rear of a vehicle that carries exhaust gases from the muffler to the outside air.

Examples

A tailpipe on a car
The mechanic checked the tailpipe for signs of rust and corrosion.
Thick smoke poured from the car's tailpipe as it started up.
You should never block the tailpipe while the engine is running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhaust pipe - a pipe through which exhaust gases pass from an engine
