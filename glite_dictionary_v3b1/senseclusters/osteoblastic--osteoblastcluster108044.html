osteoblastic

adjective
bone-forming
8.8
Pronunciation: /ɑːs.tɪ.oʊ.ˈblæs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ɒs.tɪ.əʊ.ˈblæs.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to the formation of bone tissue

Examples

Osteoblastic bone growth
The X-ray revealed areas of increased osteoblastic activity in the healing bone.
Certain tumors can cause abnormal osteoblastic growth in affected bones.
Doctors monitor osteoblastic response to assess bone regeneration after injury.
The scan revealed an osteoblastic lesion in the patient's femur.
Doctors noted increased osteoblastic activity in the healing bone.
Certain cancers can cause osteoblastic metastases that make bones appear denser on X-rays.
