scurrilous

adjective
using coarse, abusive language
Pronunciation: / ˈskɝələs / (AmE), / ˈskʌrɪləs / (BrE)
using or expressed in coarse, abusive, or offensive language, especially to damage someone's reputation

Examples

A scurrilous attack
He launched into a scurrilous rant against his critics during the interview.
The article received many complaints for its scurrilous tone and unfounded accusations.
The candidate was angered by the scurrilous rumors being spread about her in the press.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulting - conveying disrespect or abuse
  • antonym: laudatory - expressing praise
