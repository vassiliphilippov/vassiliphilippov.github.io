genus Crotalus

noun
genus of large rattlesnakes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs kroʊˈteɪləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs krəʊˈteɪləs / (BrE)
A taxonomic genus of large rattlesnakes, primarily found in the Americas, which rarely bite unless startled or hunting prey.
