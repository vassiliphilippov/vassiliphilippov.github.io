sales force

noun
group selling a firm's products or services
5.6
Pronunciation: / seɪlz fɔrs / (AmE), / seɪlz fɔːs / (BrE)
the group of employees in a company who are responsible for selling the company's products or services

Examples

A strong sales force
A motivated sales force is crucial for increasing market share in a competitive industry.
She manages a sales force of over fifty people across three regions.
The company invested in training its entire sales force to improve their customer service skills.
