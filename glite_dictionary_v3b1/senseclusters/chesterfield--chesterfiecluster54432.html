chesterfield

noun
large, upholstered sofa with upright arms
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛstɚˌfild / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛstəfiːld / (BrE)
a large, overstuffed sofa with upright arms and a back of the same height, often upholstered in leather and with deep buttoning

Examples

A leather chesterfield
She lounged comfortably on the chesterfield while reading a book.
The antique chesterfield was the centerpiece of the drawing room.
They decided to buy a beautiful leather chesterfield for their living room.
