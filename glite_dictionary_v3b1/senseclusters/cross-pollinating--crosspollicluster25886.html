cross-pollinating

adjective
mixing ideas from different fields
5.6
Pronunciation: /krɔːs ˈpɑː.lə.neɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /krɒs ˈpɒl.ɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
Combining or exchanging ideas, methods, styles, or practices from different fields or disciplines in order to create new or improved outcomes.

The workshop included several cross-pollinating talks that mixed design thinking with scientific research methods.

The policy promoted cross-pollinating collaborations between universities and industry to speed innovation.
