intercom speaker

noun
loudspeaker on an intercom system
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌkɑm ˈspiːkər / (AmE), / ˈɪntəkɒm ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
the loudspeaker component of an intercom or public address system, used for transmitting sound or announcements
