pro-capitalist

noun
supporter of capitalism
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌprəʊˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates the economic system of capitalism

Examples

A pro-capitalist thinker
The pro-capitalist argued that free markets encourage innovation and competition.
As a known pro-capitalist, she often writes articles defending private enterprise.
During the discussion, the pro-capitalist viewpoint was challenged by several critics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-capitalist - a person who opposes the economic system of capitalism
  • synonym: capitalist - a person who supports or believes in capitalism
