custard-like

adjective
resembling custard in texture or consistency
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstɚd laɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd laɪk / (BrE)
having a texture or consistency similar to that of custard; soft, smooth, and thick.

Examples

Custard-like texture
A fungus with a custard-like texture covered parts of the damp wood.
She made a sauce with a custard-like consistency to pour over the cake.
The dessert had a custard-like filling that was both smooth and creamy.
