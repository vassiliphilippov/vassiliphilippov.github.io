tui

noun
New Zealand songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːi / (AmE), / ˈtuːi / (BrE)
a New Zealand songbird with iridescent feathers and a distinctive, melodious call

Examples

The tui bird
The tui perched on a branch, filling the morning air with its beautiful song.
We spotted a tui among the flowering trees during our walk in the park.
The iridescent feathers of the tui shimmered in the sunlight as it sang.
