hired woman

noun
female hired farm or ranch worker
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪərd ˈwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd ˈwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman employed as a laborer, especially on a farm or ranch

Examples

A hired woman worker
As a hired woman, she was responsible for milking the cows and collecting eggs every morning.
The hired woman rose before dawn to tend the animals on the ranch.
The family employed a hired woman during harvest season to help with the crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hired hand - a person employed to do manual labor, especially on a farm
