primy

adjective
overly proper
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪmi / (AmE), / ˈpraɪmi / (BrE)
excessively proper, neat, or formal in manner or appearance

Examples

Primy manners
Her primy attitude made her seem distant at the informal gathering.
He wore a primy suit that looked out of place at the beach party.
The teacher's primy manner intimidated some of the younger students.
