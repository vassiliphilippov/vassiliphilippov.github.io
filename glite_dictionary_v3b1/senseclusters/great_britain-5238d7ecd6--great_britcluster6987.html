Great Britain

proper noun
island comprising England, Scotland, and Wales
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈbrɪtən / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈbrɪtn̩ /; / ɡreɪt ˈbrɪt.ən / (BrE)
the island in northwestern Europe consisting of England, Scotland, and Wales (sometimes used loosely to refer to the United Kingdom)
