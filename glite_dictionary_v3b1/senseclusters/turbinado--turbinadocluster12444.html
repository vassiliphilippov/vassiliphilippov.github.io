turbinado

noun
coarse brown sugar
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜːrbɪˈnɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtɜːbɪˈnɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a type of minimally processed brown sugar with large, coarse crystals and a light molasses flavor

Examples

A turbinado topping
She sprinkled turbinado on top of the muffins for extra crunch.
The recipe calls for turbinado instead of regular white sugar to add texture.
A spoonful of turbinado gives coffee a subtle molasses flavor and a pleasant sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demerara - a type of light brown, coarse-grained sugar
  • antonym: white sugar - refined sugar with fine crystals and no molasses
