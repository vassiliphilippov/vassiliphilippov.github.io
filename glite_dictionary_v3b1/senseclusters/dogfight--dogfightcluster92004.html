dogfight

noun
fight between dogs (sometimes illegal)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔgˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɒgˌfaɪt / (BrE)
a violent fight between dogs, often organized illegally for entertainment or gambling

Examples

Illegal dogfight
He was horrified to learn that some people still attend illegal dogfights.
Laws have been passed to punish those who organize dogfights.
Police raided the warehouse where a secret dogfight was being held.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cockfight - a fight between roosters, often for entertainment or gambling
