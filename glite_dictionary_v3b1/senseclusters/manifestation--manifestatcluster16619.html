manifestation

noun
public demonstration, esp. political
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn.ə.fəˈsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæn.ɪ.fɛˈsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a public demonstration or display of the feelings or views of a group, especially concerning a political cause

Examples

A public manifestation
A large manifestation was held in the city square to protest government policies.
Each year, the workers organize a manifestation to demand better working conditions.
The authorities responded to the manifestation with a heavy police presence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstration - a public meeting or march expressing political views
