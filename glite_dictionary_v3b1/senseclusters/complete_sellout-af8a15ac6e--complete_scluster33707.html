complete sellout

noun
event with all tickets sold
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplit ˈsɛlˌaʊt / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt ˈsɛlˌaʊt / (BrE)
an event for which all available tickets or seats have been sold

Examples

A complete sellout concert
Every concert on the tour was a complete sellout, breaking previous attendance records.
Last night's show was a complete sellout and attracted fans from across the city.
The championship game became a complete sellout just minutes after tickets went on sale.
