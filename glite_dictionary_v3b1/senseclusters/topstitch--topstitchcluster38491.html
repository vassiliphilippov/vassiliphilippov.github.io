topstitch

verb
to sew visible stitches
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːp.stɪtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈtɒp.stɪtʃ/ (BrE)
to sew a visible line of stitches on the outside of a garment for decoration or added strength

Examples

To topstitch a pocket

The tailor will topstitch the collar to make it more durable.
After sewing the hem, he carefully topstitched along the seam for decoration.
