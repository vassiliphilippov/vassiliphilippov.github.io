truculent

adjective
fiercely aggressive or defiant
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌkjələnt / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkjʊlənt / (BrE)
aggressively defiant or eager to argue or fight; fierce and hostile

Examples

A truculent attitude
His truculent attitude made cooperation with the team nearly impossible.
She became truculent when questioned about her actions, refusing to back down.
The senator delivered a truculent reply to his critics in the press conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belligerent - hostile and aggressive
  • antonym: peaceful - not aggressive; calm and nonviolent
