Anna Mary Robertson Moses

proper noun
U.S. painter known as Grandma Moses
Pronunciation: / ˈænə ˈmɛri ˈrɑbərtsən ˈmoʊzɪs / (AmE), / ˈanə ˈmɛːri ˈrɒbətsən ˈməʊzɪs / (BrE)
