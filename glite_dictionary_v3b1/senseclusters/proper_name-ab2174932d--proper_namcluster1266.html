proper name

noun
noun denoting specific person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːpər neɪm / (AmE), / ˈprɒpə neɪm / (BrE)
a noun that designates a specific individual person, place, or thing, and is usually capitalized

Examples

A proper name
Company names, such as 'Apple,' are always written as a proper name.
The word 'London' is a proper name referring to a specific city.
We capitalize a proper name like 'Jupiter' because it names a planet.
