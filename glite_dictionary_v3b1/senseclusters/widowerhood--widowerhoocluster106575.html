widowerhood

noun
state of being a widower
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪdoʊərˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɪdəʊəˌhʊd / (BrE)
the state or condition of being a man whose spouse has died

Examples

A widowerhood period
After his wife passed away, he entered a long period of widowerhood.
Support groups can help men adjust to the challenges of widowerhood.
He found new friendships during his years of widowerhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marriage - the state of being married
  • synonym: widowhood - the state of having lost one's spouse by death
