permed

adjective
hair styled with permanent waves
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːmd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːmd / (BrE)
(of hair) treated to form permanent waves or curls, typically by using chemicals.

Examples

Newly permed hair
His mother appeared with newly permed curls at the family reunion.
Many women in the 1980s loved the look of permed hair.
She always keeps her hair permed and neat for special occasions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curly - having curls or tending to curl
  • antonym: straight - having no waves or curls (often describing hair)
