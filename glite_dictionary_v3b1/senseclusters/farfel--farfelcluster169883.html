farfel

noun
toasted wheat pellets
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrfəl / (AmE), / ˈfɑːfəl / (BrE)
small toasted pellets made from wheat or barley, eaten as a breakfast cereal or side dish

Examples

Breakfast farfel
For breakfast she served warm milk over a bowl of crunchy farfel with sliced bananas.
He prepared farfel as a side dish to accompany the roasted chicken.
Many families enjoy farfel during traditional holiday meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kasha - a dish of roasted buckwheat groats, often served as a side
