boop

verb
to tap playfully
8.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / buÀêp / (AmE), / buÀêp / (BrE)
to touch or tap someone or something lightly and playfully, often on the nose or cheek, sometimes making a short sound

Examples

To boop a puppy's nose
She reached out to boop the puppy's nose, making everyone laugh.
Whenever he walks by, his friends boop him on the cheek as a joke.
The cartoon character would boop the robot to turn it on.

Synonyms

  • tap
    1.0 4.23/m
    tap is a quick light touch used on objects or people, while boop is a playful touch often on the nose or cheek.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pat - to touch someone or something gently with your hand
  • synonym: poke - to jab or prod someone or something with a finger or object
