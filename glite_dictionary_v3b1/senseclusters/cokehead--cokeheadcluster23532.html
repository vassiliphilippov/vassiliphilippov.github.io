cokehead

noun
slang cocaine user
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkˌhɛd / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who habitually uses cocaine

Examples

A cokehead addict
The documentary followed the life of a young cokehead struggling to overcome addiction.
Rumors spread quickly that the new employee was a former cokehead trying to start fresh.
He lost many friends after becoming known as a cokehead in his community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - a person who is dependent on a substance
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from all intoxicating substances
