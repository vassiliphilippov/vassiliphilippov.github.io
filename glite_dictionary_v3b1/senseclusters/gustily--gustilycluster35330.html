gustily

adverb
energetically
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌstɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡʌstɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is full of energy, enthusiasm, or force

Examples

Laughs gustily
The children laughed gustily at the clown's antics.
He sang gustily along with the music, not caring who heard him.
The wind blew gustily through the open window, rattling the curtains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows strong excitement or interest
  • synonym: vigorously - in a strong and energetic way
  • antonym: weakly - in a way that lacks strength or energy
