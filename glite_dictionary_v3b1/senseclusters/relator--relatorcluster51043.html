relator

noun
storyteller; legal party
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who relates or tells stories, or in law, a person on whose behalf a legal action is brought

Examples

A relator tells stories
The relator described the events to the audience with great enthusiasm.
In the legal case, the relator provided crucial information to support the claim.
As a skilled relator, she captivated everyone with her storytelling abilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narrator - a person who tells a story
  • synonym: plaintiff - a person who brings a case against another in a court of law
