holy place

noun
sacred location for worship or pilgrimage
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli pleɪs / (BrE)
a place considered sacred, often where religious ceremonies or pilgrimages take place

Examples

A holy place of worship
He removed his shoes before entering the holy place out of respect.
Pilgrims from all over the world visited the holy place to offer their prayers.
The temple is regarded as a holy place by people of many faiths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sanctuary - a sacred or holy place
  • synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy due to its associations with a divinity or a sacred person
