Dolichos

noun
genus of tropical vine plants
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːlɪˌkoʊs /; / ˈdɑː.lɪˌkoʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɒlɪˌkɒs /; / ˈdɒ.lɪ.kɒs / (BrE)
