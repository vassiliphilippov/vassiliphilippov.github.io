uncharted territory

noun
new or unfamiliar situation
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɑːrtɪd ˈtɛrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑːtɪd ˈtɛrɪtəri / (BrE)
a situation or subject that is new, unfamiliar, or involves unknown risks

Examples

Uncharted territory for students
For many students, starting university is uncharted territory.
The pandemic forced scientists into uncharted territory as they searched for solutions.
When the company launched its first product overseas, it was entering uncharted territory.
