Welsh corgi

noun
a breed of small, short-legged Welsh dog
Pronunciation: / wɛlʃ ˈkɔrɡi / (AmE), / wɛlʃ ˈkɔːɡi / (BrE)
either of two Welsh breeds of small dogs with long bodies, short legs, erect ears, and fox-like heads, known for their herding ability

Examples

A Welsh corgi dog
Our neighbors just adopted two adorable Welsh corgis from a local rescue center.
The Welsh corgi is known for its intelligence and affectionate nature.
The Queen of England was famously fond of the Welsh corgi breed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Cardigan Welsh corgi - a variety of Welsh corgi breed with a longer tail and slightly different shape
  • synonym: Pembroke Welsh corgi - a variety of Welsh corgi breed with a shorter tail
