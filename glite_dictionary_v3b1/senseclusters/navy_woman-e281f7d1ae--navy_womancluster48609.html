navy woman

noun
female member of the navy
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvi ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is a member of a country's navy, especially as a uniformed service member.

Examples

The navy woman received an award for her outstanding service.
