booboo

noun
minor injury (child)
5.2 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀêÀåbuÀê / (AmE), / ÀàbuÀêÀåbuÀê / (BrE)
a minor injury, such as a small cut or bruise, especially as described by or to children

Examples

A booboo on the knee

Let me kiss your booboo and make it better, said the mother to her son.
Children often call small cuts or scrapes booboos when they are hurt.
