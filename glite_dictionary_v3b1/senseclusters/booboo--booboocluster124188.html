booboo

noun
minor injury (child)
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːˌbuː / (AmE), / ˈbuːˌbuː / (BrE)
a minor injury, such as a small cut or bruise, especially as described by or to children

Examples

A booboo on the knee
The little girl cried when she got a booboo on her knee after falling.
Let me kiss your booboo and make it better, said the mother to her son.
Children often call small cuts or scrapes booboos when they are hurt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bruise - an injury causing discoloration of the skin
  • synonym: scratch - a small cut or mark on the skin
  • degree higher: wound - an injury to the body, especially one in which the skin is cut or broken
