preinfection

adjective
before infection
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
happening, existing, or applied before an infection occurs, especially referring to conditions or treatments prior to infection

Examples

Preinfection treatment
Researchers collected preinfection samples to compare with those taken after the illness developed.
The preinfection status of the patients was carefully documented before the study began.
Doctors analyzed preinfection data to understand the patient's immune response.
