tasteful

adjective
having good taste or style
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪstfəl / (AmE), / ˈteɪstfəl / (BrE)
having or showing good aesthetic judgment or manners; exhibiting appropriate and attractive style

Examples

A tasteful design
Her speech was tasteful and appropriate for the occasion, impressing everyone in the audience.
The designer created a tasteful living room with elegant furniture and subtle colors.
They served lunch in a tasteful dining area decorated with fresh flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance or manner
  • antonym: tasteless - lacking good taste; in poor taste
