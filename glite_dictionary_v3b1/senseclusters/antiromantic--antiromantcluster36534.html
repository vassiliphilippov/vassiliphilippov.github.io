antiromantic

adjective
opposed to romance
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiɹoʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiɹəʊˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting romantic ideas, attitudes, or styles

Examples

Antiromantic view of love
The film presents an antiromantic perspective on traditional love stories.
Her antiromantic attitude surprised her friends, who expected her to enjoy romantic comedies.
Critics praised the novel's antiromantic approach to relationships and its realistic characters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cynical - distrustful of others' motives or sincerity
  • antonym: romantic - having or expressing strong feelings of love or idealism
