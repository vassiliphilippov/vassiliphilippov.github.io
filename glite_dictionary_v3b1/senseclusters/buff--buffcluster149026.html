buff

adjective
pale yellow-brown color
Pronunciation: / bʌf / (AmE), / bʌf / (BrE)
having the yellowish-beige color of buff leather; pale yellow-brown or light tan in color.

Examples

Buff colored jacket

The buff sand of the dunes stretched for miles along the coast.
The walls of the living room were painted a soft buff to create a warm atmosphere.
