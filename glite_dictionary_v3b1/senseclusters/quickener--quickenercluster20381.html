quickener

noun
one that gives life or energy
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪkənɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkənə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives or restores life, energy, or vigor

Examples

The quickener of life
In ancient texts, the spring sun is described as the quickener of the earth.
She hoped the song would serve as a quickener of their spirits after the long journey.
The new coach was a real quickener for the team, inspiring them to greater efforts.
