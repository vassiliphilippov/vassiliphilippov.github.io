personalization

noun
making unique for someone
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsənələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something unique or tailored for a particular person

Examples

Personalization of products
The personalization of online ads makes them more relevant to each user.
Many companies offer personalization options for their products, such as engraving your name.
With personalization, the website displays content based on your interests and preferences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customization - the process of modifying something to suit a particular individual
  • antonym: standardization - the process of making things conform to a standard
