plaster

verb
to coat with plaster
Pronunciation: / ˈplæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɑː.stə / (BrE)
to cover a surface with a pasty substance, especially plaster, for protection or decoration

Examples

To plaster a wall
After the repairs, they plastered the wall to create a smooth finish.
She carefully plasters the drywall to prepare it for wallpaper.
The workers plastered the ceiling before painting it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daub - to coat or smear (a surface) with a thick or sticky substance
