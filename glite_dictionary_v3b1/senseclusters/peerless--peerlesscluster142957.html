peerless

adjective
having no equal; unrivaled
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪrləs / (AmE), / ˈpɪələs / (BrE)
so good or outstanding as to have no equal; unmatched; without rival

Examples

A peerless talent
Her peerless talent as a violinist stunned audiences around the world.
Many consider Shakespeare's works to possess a peerless mastery of language.
The chef's peerless skill was recognized with three Michelin stars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matchless - having no equal; unrivaled
  • antonym: rivaled - matched or equaled by another
  • synonym: unequaled - better than anyone or anything else; unique
  • synonym: unparalleled - having no parallel or equal; exceptional
