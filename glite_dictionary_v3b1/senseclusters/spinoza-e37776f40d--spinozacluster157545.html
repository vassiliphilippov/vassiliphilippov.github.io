Spinoza

proper noun
Dutch philosopher (1632–1677)
Pronunciation: / spɪˈnoʊzə / (AmE), / spɪˈnəʊzə / (BrE)
(1632–1677) A Dutch philosopher known for developing a pantheistic system of philosophy.
