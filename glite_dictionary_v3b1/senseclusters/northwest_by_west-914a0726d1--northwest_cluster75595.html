northwest by west

noun
compass point, one point west of northwest
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˌwɛst baɪ ˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˌwɛst baɪ ˈwɛst / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point west of northwest, corresponding to a bearing of 292.5°.

Examples

Northwest by west direction
He checked the map and pointed northwest by west with his compass.
The ship set a course for northwest by west to avoid the storm.
We walked steadily in a northwest by west direction across the open field.
