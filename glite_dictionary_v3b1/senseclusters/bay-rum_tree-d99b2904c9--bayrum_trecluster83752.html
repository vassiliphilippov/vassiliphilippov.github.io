bay-rum tree

noun
West Indian tree used to make bay rum
Pronunciation: / beɪ ˈrʌm tri / (AmE), / beɪ ˈrʌm triː / (BrE)
A tropical West Indian tree (Pimenta racemosa) whose leaves are the source of the aromatic liquid known as bay rum.
