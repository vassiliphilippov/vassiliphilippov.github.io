Examples

Synonyms

  • show someone the door
    6.4 0.19/m
    show someone the door names a specific person and sounds direct and conversational, while show the door is a shorter idiom with same force.
  • send packing
    6.5 0.20/m
    send packing forces someone to leave quickly and makes it clear they are not wanted, while show the door is more neutral and simply dismisses.
  • dismiss
    2.7 3.25/m
    dismiss is a formal action to end someone's role or job, while show the door is a blunt personal way to tell someone to leave.
