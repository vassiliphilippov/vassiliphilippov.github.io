caenogenesis

noun
novel trait introduction during embryonic development
9.7 9.9
Pronunciation: /siː.nə.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ /; / /keɪ.nə.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ (AmE), /siː.nə.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ /; / /keɪ.nə.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ (BrE)
the introduction during embryonic development of characters or structures not present in the earlier evolutionary history of the strain or species; evolutionary addition of novel features, such as the development of the placenta in mammals
