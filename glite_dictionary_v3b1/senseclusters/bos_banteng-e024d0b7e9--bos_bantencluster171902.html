Bos banteng

noun
wild ox species of Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / boʊs ˈbɑːn.teɪŋ / (AmE), / bɒs ˈbæn.teŋ / (BrE)
A species of wild ox native to Southeast Asia, especially the Malay Archipelago, known for its sturdy build and distinctive markings.
