crosser

noun
breeding animal or person
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsər / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsə / (BrE)
an animal or plant used in breeding to produce hybrids, or a person who performs such breeding crosses

Examples

A plant crosser
The scientist chose a healthy crosser to improve the next generation of plants.
A skilled crosser can create new varieties with desirable traits.
The breeder introduced a new crosser to diversify the gene pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybridizer - a person who creates hybrids by cross-breeding
  • synonym: parent - an animal or plant that contributes genetic material in breeding
