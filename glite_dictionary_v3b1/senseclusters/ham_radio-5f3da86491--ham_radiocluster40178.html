ham radio

noun
amateur radio communication system
9.0 4.1
Pronunciation: / hæm ˈreɪ.di.oʊ / (AmE), / hæm ˈreɪ.di.əʊ / (BrE)
An amateur radio communication system used for non-commercial purposes, often as a hobby or for emergency communication.

Examples

Ham radio communication
After the hurricane, she used her ham radio to contact emergency services when other communications failed.
He earned his ham radio license so he could talk to people in distant countries.
Many enthusiasts build their own equipment to participate in ham radio activities around the world.
