positively

adverb
in a positive or affirmative way
2.6 13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêz.…ô.t…™v.li / (AmE), / Ààp…íz.…™.t…™v.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows confidence, approval, or affirmation; in a positive manner.

Examples

React positively
She responded positively to the suggestion and agreed to participate.
The news was positively received by investors, causing the stock price to rise.
The teacher praised him for contributing positively to the classroom discussion.

Synonyms

  • affirmatively
    4.8 0.84/m
    affirmatively states clear agreement or says yes, while positively shows confidence, approval, or a more general positive manner.
  • approvingly
    3.4 1.43/m
    approvingly shows praise or approval in a person's reaction, while positively expresses confidence, agreement, or a general positive tone.
  • favorably
    2.3 4.49/m
    favorably means with support or a good opinion, while positively conveys confidence, approval, or an overall positive manner.
  • confidently
    2.9 4.53/m
    confidently means with self-assurance or certainty in action, while positively covers confidence but also approval and general positivity.
