roving

adjective
wandering; moving from place to place
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊvɪŋ / (BrE)
moving from place to place without a fixed home; wandering or migratory.

Examples

Roving lifestyle
Many of the early settlers led a roving life, moving with the seasons.
She has a roving spirit, always eager to explore new countries and cultures.
The roving bands of shepherds traveled across the plains in search of pasture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stationary - not moving; fixed in one place
  • synonym: wandering - moving about without a fixed course or destination
