poached egg

noun
egg cooked by simmering in water
3.6
Pronunciation: / poʊtʃt ɛɡ / (AmE), / pəʊtʃt ɛɡ / (BrE)
An egg that has been cooked by gently simmering it in water without its shell.

Examples

A poached egg dish
He learned how to make a perfect poached egg in culinary school.
She ordered a poached egg on toast for breakfast.
The recipe calls for a poached egg placed on top of a salad.
