sheet-metal work

noun
craft of working with sheet metal
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʃiːt ˈmɛt̬l wɝːk / (AmE), / ʃiːt ˈmetl wɜːk / (BrE)
The craft or activity of shaping and assembling sheet metal, especially for use in building ventilation systems or other structures.

Examples

Sheet-metal work training
He learned sheet-metal work as part of his training in the construction trade.
Modern ventilation systems often require precise sheet-metal work for proper installation.
The college offers courses in sheet-metal work for aspiring tradespeople.
