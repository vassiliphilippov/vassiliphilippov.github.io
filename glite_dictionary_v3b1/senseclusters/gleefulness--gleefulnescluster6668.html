gleefulness

noun
great joy or merriment
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡliːfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡliːfəlnəs / (BrE)

Examples

His gleefulness at winning
He couldn't hide his gleefulness at winning the competition.
Her gleefulness was evident when she heard the good news.
The children's gleefulness filled the playground with laughter.
