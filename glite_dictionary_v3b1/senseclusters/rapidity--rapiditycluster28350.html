rapidity

noun
great speed; swiftness
Pronunciation: / rəˈpɪdəti / (AmE), / rəˈpɪdəti / (BrE)
The quality of moving, happening, or acting very quickly; great speed.

Examples

Great rapidity
She was amazed at the rapidity with which the disease spread.
The rapidity of technological change can be overwhelming.
The project's success depended on the rapidity of the response team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slowness - the quality of being slow; lack of speed
  • synonym: speed - the rate at which someone or something moves or operates
