stylomastoid vein

noun
vein draining the tympanic cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: / staɪloʊˌmæstɔɪd veɪn / (AmE), / staɪləʊˌmæstɔɪd veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the tympanic cavity and empties into the retromandibular vein
