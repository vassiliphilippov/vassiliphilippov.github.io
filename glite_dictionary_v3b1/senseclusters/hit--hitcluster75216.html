hit

noun
visit or access to a website
Pronunciation: / hɪt / (AmE), / hɪt / (BrE)
an instance of access to a website or online resource, usually counted to measure traffic or popularity

Examples

A hit on a website
Each image on the homepage generates a separate hit when loaded by a user.
The website received over a thousand hits in just one day.
Tracking the number of hits helps webmasters understand their traffic patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visit - an instance of browsing or viewing a website; similar to 'hit' but may refer to a different metric
