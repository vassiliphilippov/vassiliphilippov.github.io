nutcracker

noun
a type of songbird that feeds on nuts and insects
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌkrækə / (BrE)
a type of bird in the crow family (genus Nucifraga) that eats nuts and insects
