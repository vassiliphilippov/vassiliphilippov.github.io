millenary

adjective
relating to the doctrine of a millennium
6.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪləˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to the doctrine or belief in a coming millennium, especially a period of a thousand years of peace and righteousness foretold in some religious traditions

Examples

The book explores millenary expectations in various religious movements throughout history.
