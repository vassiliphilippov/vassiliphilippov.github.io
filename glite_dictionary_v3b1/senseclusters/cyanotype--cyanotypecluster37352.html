cyanotype

verb
to make blueprints
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.ə.noʊ.taɪp / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.ə.nə.taɪp / (BrE)
to create an image or print using the cyanotype photographic process, which produces a blue-colored print

Examples

To cyanotype leaves
Artists often cyanotype natural objects like leaves and flowers to create unique blue prints.
She learned how to cyanotype her photographs during a workshop at the local art center.
Many students cyanotyped their own designs as part of the photography class project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueprint - a photographic print with a blue background, often used for plans
  • synonym: photograph - to take a picture using a camera
