differentiate

verb
find the derivative (mathematics)
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪt / (BrE)
(mathematics) to calculate the derivative of a function with respect to a variable.

Examples

To differentiate a function
He showed us how to differentiate the equation step by step.
In calculus class, we learned how to differentiate a quadratic function.
You must differentiate the expression before you can solve for x.
