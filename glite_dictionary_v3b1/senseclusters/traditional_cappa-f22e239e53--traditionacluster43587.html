traditional cappa

noun
ceremonial cloak from Italy
1.8
Pronunciation: / trəˈdɪʃənl ˈkɑːpə / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənl ˈkæpə / (BrE)
A ceremonial cloak worn during special occasions in Italy, especially in traditional or religious ceremonies.

Examples

A traditional cappa cloak
