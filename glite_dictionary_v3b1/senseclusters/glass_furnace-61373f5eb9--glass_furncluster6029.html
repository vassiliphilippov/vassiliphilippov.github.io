glass furnace

noun
industrial furnace for melting glass
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs ˈfɝːnɪs / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
a furnace designed for melting and processing large quantities of glass, especially in industrial glassmaking.

Examples
