corrective facility

noun
institution for rehabilitating offenders
6.3 5.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈrek.tɪv fə.ˈsɪ.lɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /kə.ˈrek.tɪv fə.ˈsɪ.lɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
an institution where offenders are sent to undergo rehabilitation or correction of behavior, often as an alternative to standard incarceration

Examples

A corrective facility for offenders
After repeated offenses, she was finally sent to a corrective facility for young adults.
Rather than jail time, the judge ordered community service and a stay in a corrective facility.
The city announced plans to renovate its aging corrective facility to improve inmate rehabilitation.
