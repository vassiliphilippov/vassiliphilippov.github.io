lungyi

noun
traditional cloth garment from South and Southeast Asia
7.1 9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑːŋ.jiː/ /; / /ˈlɔːŋ.jiː/ (AmE), /ˈlɒŋ.jiː/ (BrE)
