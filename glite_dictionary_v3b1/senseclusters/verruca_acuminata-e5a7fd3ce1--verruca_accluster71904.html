verruca acuminata

noun
genital wart
Pronunciation: / vɛˈruːkə ˌækjəˈmɪnətə / (AmE), / vəˈruːkə ˌækjʊˈmɪnətə / (BrE)
A small benign wart occurring on or around the genitals and anus, caused by certain strains of the human papillomavirus (HPV).

Examples

A verruca acuminata wart
After the examination, the doctor diagnosed the small growths as verruca acuminata.
Because verruca acuminata can be contagious, safe practices are important to prevent their spread.
Effective treatment options exist for verruca acuminata caused by HPV infection.
