bruiser

noun
durable melee fighter role (gaming)
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbruː.zər/ (AmE), /ˈbruː.zə/ (BrE)
(in video games) a durable melee fighter role that soaks damage and deals steady close-range damage, often combining some offense and defense.

Examples

A bruiser character
In the MOBA, the bruiser pushed into the enemy team while shrugging off incoming spells.
Their strategy relied on a fed bruiser diving the backline to finish off squishy targets.
She prefers playing a top-lane bruiser who trades damage and builds both offense and defense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawler - a close-combat fighter who focuses on physical damage; often used interchangeably with bruiser.
  • synonym: tank - a character who primarily absorbs damage for the team; similar to a bruiser but usually less damage-focused.
