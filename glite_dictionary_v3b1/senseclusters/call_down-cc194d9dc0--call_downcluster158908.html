call down

verb
reprimand or scold severely
4.2
Pronunciation: / kɔl daʊn / (AmE), / kɔːl daʊn / (BrE)
to reprimand or criticize someone severely or angrily

Examples

To call down a worker
He calls down anyone who doesn’t follow the safety rules.
She was called down by her boss after missing the deadline.
The teacher called down the students for their disruptive behavior.
