trapshooting

noun
sport of shooting at flying clay targets
Pronunciation: / ˈtræpˌʃutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræpˌʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
A sport in which participants shoot at clay targets launched to simulate flying birds.

Examples

Trapshooting sport
He started trapshooting at a young age and quickly became an accomplished marksman.
Proper safety gear is essential when participating in trapshooting competitions.
The local club holds trapshooting tournaments every summer for enthusiasts of all skill levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skeet - A similar sport involving shooting at clay targets from different angles
