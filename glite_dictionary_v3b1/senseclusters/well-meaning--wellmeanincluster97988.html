well-meaning

adjective
having good intentions
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛlˈmiːnɪŋ /; / wɛlˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈmiːnɪŋ /; / wɛlˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
