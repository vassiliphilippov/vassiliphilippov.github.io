poco

adverb
slightly in music
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
in music, to a small degree or a little; used to indicate a slight change in tempo or dynamics

Examples

Play poco faster
The conductor asked the orchestra to play poco faster in the next section.
The pianist increased the volume poco to highlight the melody.
In this piece, the tempo should slow down poco before the final movement.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: molto - in music, very; to a great degree
  • synonym: un poco - in music, a little; slightly
