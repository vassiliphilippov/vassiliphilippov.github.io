Plinian

adjective
explosive volcanic eruption
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈplɪ.niː.ən/ /; / /ˈplɪn.iː.ən/ (AmE), /ˈplɪ.niː.ən/ /; / /ˈplɪn.iː.ən/ (BrE)
relating to or resembling a very explosive volcanic eruption characterized by a tall, sustained column of ash and gas

Examples

Plinian eruption
The volcano's Plinian eruption sent ash high into the atmosphere, disrupting air travel for days.
Scientists studied the Plinian deposits to understand the eruption's intensity and impact on the environment.
A Plinian event can cause widespread damage due to its powerful blast and ashfall.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: strombolian - relating to a less explosive type of volcanic eruption
  • synonym: volcanic - relating to or produced by a volcano
