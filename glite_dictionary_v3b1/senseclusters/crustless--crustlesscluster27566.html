crustless

adjective
without a crust
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌstləs / (AmE), / ˈkrʌstləs / (BrE)
without a crust or outer edge, especially referring to food such as bread or pies

Examples

Crustless sandwiches
She made a batch of crustless sandwiches for the children's lunch.
The bakery offers a delicious crustless quiche for people who prefer a lighter meal.
He requested his pie to be crustless because he did not like the edges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crusted - having a crust or outer layer
