unblunt

adjective
sharp, not dull
7.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblʌnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈblʌnt / (BrE)
having a sharp edge or point; not blunt or dull

Examples

An unblunt knife
The chef preferred to use an unblunt knife for slicing vegetables quickly and cleanly.
After sharpening, the scissors were completely unblunt and cut through paper with ease.
The archaeologist was surprised to find an unblunt arrowhead among the ancient tools.
