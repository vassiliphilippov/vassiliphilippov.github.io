nogada

noun
Mexican walnut sauce
9.9
Pronunciation: / noʊˈɡɑːdə / (AmE), / nəʊˈɡɑːdə / (BrE)
a traditional Mexican sauce made from walnuts, typically served with dishes like chiles en nogada

Examples
