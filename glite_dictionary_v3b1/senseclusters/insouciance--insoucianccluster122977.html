insouciance

noun
carefree lack of concern
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsuːsiəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈsuːsiəns / (BrE)
A relaxed and unconcerned state; lack of worry or anxiety.

Examples

His insouciance about deadlines
His insouciance about deadlines sometimes frustrated his more anxious colleagues.
She responded to the difficult question with an air of insouciance that baffled her teachers.
Wearing a straw hat and a smile, he strolled along the beach in perfect insouciance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • synonym: nonchalance - casual lack of concern or indifference
