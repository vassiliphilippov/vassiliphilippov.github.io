super-rich

noun
extremely wealthy people
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpər rɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpə rɪtʃ / (BrE)
people who are extremely wealthy, especially the very richest members of society

Examples

The super-rich live abroad
The new luxury resort was designed exclusively for the super-rich seeking privacy and comfort.
Many believe the super-rich should contribute more to society through higher taxes.
Charities often rely on donations from the super-rich to fund large projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billionaire - a person whose wealth is at least one billion units of currency
  • antonym: poor - having little or no money or possessions
  • synonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money, resources, or assets
