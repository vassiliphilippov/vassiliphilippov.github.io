bicarbonate buffer solution

noun
solution used to stabilize pH in biological experiments
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkɑːrbəˌneɪt ˈbʌfər səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / baɪˈkɑːbənət ˈbʌfə səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
a solution containing bicarbonate ions that resists changes in pH, commonly used as a physiological buffer in biological and chemical experiments

Examples

A bicarbonate buffer solution controls pH
Doctors often use a bicarbonate buffer solution to help regulate blood pH during certain medical procedures.
The experiment required careful preparation of the bicarbonate buffer solution to ensure accurate results.
To maintain a stable pH, we added a bicarbonate buffer solution to the culture medium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffer solution - a solution that resists pH change when acid or base is added
