third

noun
third gear in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / θɝd / (AmE), / θɜːd / (BrE)
the gear in a motor vehicle's gearbox that is third in order of increasing speed or gear ratio, used after second gear

Examples

Third gear
She shifted into third as the car picked up speed.
The engine runs smoothly when cruising in third.
You shouldn't try to start in third on a steep hill.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fourth - the gear after third in a vehicle's gearbox
  • degree lower: second - the gear before third in a vehicle's gearbox
