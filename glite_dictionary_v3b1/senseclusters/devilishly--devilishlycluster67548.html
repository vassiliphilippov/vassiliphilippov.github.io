devilishly

adverb
to a great or excessive degree
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈdɛvɪlɪʃli / (BrE)
To an extreme or excessive degree; very.

Examples

Devilishly hard puzzle
He was devilishly clever at solving puzzles that baffled everyone else.
The cake was devilishly rich and sweet, making it hard to stop at one piece.
The exam turned out to be devilishly difficult despite all her preparation.
