gaunt

adjective
extremely thin and haggard
Pronunciation: / ɡɔnt / (AmE), / ɡɔːnt / (BrE)
very thin, often as a result of illness, hunger, or hardship, and looking pale and worn

Examples

A gaunt face
After weeks in the wilderness, he appeared gaunt and exhausted.
Her gaunt cheeks and hollow eyes revealed the severity of her illness.
Stray dogs roam the streets, their gaunt bodies searching for scraps of food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emaciated - abnormally thin, especially because of illness or a lack of food
  • synonym: haggard - looking tired and unhealthy, often from worry or suffering
