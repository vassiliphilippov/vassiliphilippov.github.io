chimneysweeper

noun
a person who cleans chimneys
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪm.niˌswip.ər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪm.niˌswiː.pə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean soot and ash from chimneys

Examples

A chimneysweeper cleans chimneys
After the fire, they hired a chimneysweeper to ensure the flues were clear.
In Victorian England, becoming a chimneysweeper was often a job for young boys.
The old house required a chimneysweeper to remove decades of soot build-up.
