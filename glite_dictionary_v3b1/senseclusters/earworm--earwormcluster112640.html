earworm

noun
persistent mental image
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a thought, phrase, or image that repeatedly comes to mind and is difficult to forget, not necessarily musical

Examples

A repeating earworm thought
That strange phrase became an earworm and kept popping into my head all day.
After reading the slogan, it turned into an earworm that distracted him during meetings.
Sometimes a random image from a movie acts like an earworm and won't leave my mind.
