nonrecipient

noun
person not receiving
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈsɪpiənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɪpiənt / (BrE)
a person or entity that does not receive something, such as a benefit, award, or payment

Examples

A nonrecipient of aid
The scholarship was renewed for previous recipients, but nonrecipients had to reapply.
Only nonrecipients of the bonus will be considered for the new incentive program.
The survey compared the experiences of benefit recipients and nonrecipients in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recipient - a person or entity that receives something
