Ligustrum ibolium

noun
hybrid privet shrub used in hedges
9.5
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡʌstrəm aɪˈboʊliəm / (AmE), / lɪˈɡʌstrəm ɪˈbəʊliəm / (BrE)
A fast-growing, densely branched hybrid shrub of the privet genus, produced by crossing Ligustrum ovalifolium and Ligustrum obtusifolium, commonly used in hedges and landscaping.

Examples

Ligustrum ibolium shrub
Gardeners often plant Ligustrum ibolium along property lines due to its dense branches.
Native to East Asia, Ligustrum ibolium is valued for its hardiness and adaptability.
The Ligustrum ibolium hedge grew quickly and provided excellent privacy for the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: privet - any of various shrubs of the genus Ligustrum
