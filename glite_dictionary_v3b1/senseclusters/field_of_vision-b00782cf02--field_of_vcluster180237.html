Synonyms

  • visual field
    4.8 0.5/m
    visual field is the full area the eye sees at once, often used in medicine, while field of vision is everyday wording.
  • field of view
    4.2 2/m
    field of view is the area seen at a moment and often used for instruments like cameras, while field of vision refers to the eye.
  • field of regard
    8.7 0.00/m
    field of regard means the whole physical scene visible from one position, while field of vision describes what a single eye sees without moving.
