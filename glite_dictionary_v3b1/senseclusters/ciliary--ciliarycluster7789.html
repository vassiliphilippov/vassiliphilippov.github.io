ciliary

adjective
relating to cell cilia
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪliˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɪliəri / (BrE)
relating to cilia, the microscopic hair-like structures projecting from the surface of a cell

Examples

Ciliary hairs on cells
Scientists observed the synchronized beating of ciliary hairs under the microscope.
Some respiratory illnesses can damage the ciliary cells lining the airways.
The movement of the ciliary structures helps propel the cell through liquid environments.
