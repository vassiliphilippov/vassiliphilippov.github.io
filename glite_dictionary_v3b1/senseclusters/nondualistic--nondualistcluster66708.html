nondualistic

adjective
not divided; unified
9.5
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.duː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /nɑːn.djuː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.djuː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /nɒn.duː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
not based on the division into two opposing principles or concepts; viewing reality as a unified whole

Examples

A nondualistic view
Many Eastern philosophies are described as nondualistic in their worldview.
The teacher explained a nondualistic approach to understanding consciousness.
Some spiritual traditions promote nondualistic thinking to overcome feelings of separation.
