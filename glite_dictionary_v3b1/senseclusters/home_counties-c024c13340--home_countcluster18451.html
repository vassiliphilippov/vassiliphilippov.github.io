Home Counties

proper noun
counties surrounding London
7.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈkaʊntiz / (AmE), / həʊm ˈkaʊntiz / (BrE)
the counties of southern England that surround London, such as Surrey, Kent, and Hertfordshire, into which parts of Greater London have extended

Examples

The Home Counties near London
Many commuters live in the Home Counties and travel to jobs in central London.
Property prices in the Home Counties have increased rapidly over the past decade.
She grew up in the Home Counties, just a short train ride from the capital.
