count me in

x
include me; I want to participate
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt mi ɪn / (AmE), / kaʊnt miː ɪn / (BrE)
used to express a willingness to be included in something or to take part in an activity

Examples

Count me in for the hike
I'm planning to join the hike tomorrow, so count me in.
If you need more players for the game, count me in.
Whenever you decide to organize a trip, you can always count me in.

Related Terms

  • antonym: count me out - exclude me from something or not include me
