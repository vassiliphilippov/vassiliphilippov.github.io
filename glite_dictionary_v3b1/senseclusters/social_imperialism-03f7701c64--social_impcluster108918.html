social imperialism

noun
domination of one culture over others
4.8 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ɪm.ˈpɪr.iː.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ɪm.ˈpɪə.riː.əl.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the imposition of one culture's values, practices, or dominance over others, often leading to the control or erosion of local cultures

Examples

Social imperialism criticism
Many scholars criticize social imperialism for undermining traditional customs.
Policies encouraging one language can be seen as an example of social imperialism.
The spread of Western media is often cited as a form of social imperialism.

Related Terms
