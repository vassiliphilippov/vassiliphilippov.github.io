complexion

verb
give a particular color to
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplɛkʃən / (AmE), / kəmˈplɛkʃən / (BrE)
(rare) To give a particular color or hue to something.

Examples

To complexion the cheeks
His cheeks were complexioned by the heat of the fire.
The lanterns softly complexioned the room with a warm glow.
The last rays of sunlight complexioned the mountaintop in gold and pink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: color - to give color to; to paint or tint
