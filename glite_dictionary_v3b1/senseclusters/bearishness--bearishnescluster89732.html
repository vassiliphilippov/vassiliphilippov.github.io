bearishness

noun
pessimistic attitude
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈber.ɪʃ.nəs/ /; / /ˈbeə.rɪʃ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.rɪʃ.nəs/ (BrE)
a general tendency to expect negative outcomes or to have a pessimistic attitude, especially about the future

Examples

Bearishness about the future
His bearishness about the economy made him hesitant to invest in new ventures.
The team's bearishness affected their motivation and overall performance during the project.
Despite the positive news, her bearishness remained unchanged and she warned of possible risks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pessimism - the belief that bad things are more likely to happen
