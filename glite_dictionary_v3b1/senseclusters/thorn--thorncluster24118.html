thorn

noun
source of persistent irritation or annoyance
5.6
Pronunciation: / θɔrn / (AmE), / θɔːn / (BrE)
a person or thing that continually irritates, annoys, or troubles someone

Examples

Thorn in her side
His constant criticism became a real thorn in her side at work.
That loud alarm has been a thorn to everyone living nearby.
The new parking regulations are a thorn for local business owners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blessing - a welcome event or thing that brings happiness
  • synonym: nuisance - a person or thing causing inconvenience or annoyance
