common room

noun
shared sitting room at an institution
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ruːm / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ruːm / (BrE)
a room, typically at a school, university, or similar institution, where people gather to relax, socialize, or study together

Examples

The common room at school
After classes, she often relaxes with friends in the college common room.
Faculty members are welcome to use the staff common room during breaks.
The students met in the common room to discuss their project.
