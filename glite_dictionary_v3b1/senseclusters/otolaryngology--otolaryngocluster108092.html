otolaryngology

noun
medical specialty for ear, nose, and throat
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtəˌlærɪnˈɡɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəʊˌlærɪnˈɡɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.

Examples

Otolaryngology treatment
Advances in otolaryngology have greatly improved the treatment of hearing loss.
She decided to specialize in otolaryngology after medical school.
The hospital has a renowned otolaryngology department that treats both adults and children.
