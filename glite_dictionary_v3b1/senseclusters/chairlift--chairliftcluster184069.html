chairlift

noun
ski lift with seats on a cable
3.4 0.60/m

A type of ski lift in which passengers are seated on chairs attached to a moving overhead cable, used to carry people up or down a mountainside.

Examples

A chairlift ride
He watched the skiers descend as he rode the chairlift up the slope.
The chairlift carried us smoothly to the top of the mountain.
The resort installed a new high-speed chairlift for visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ski lift - a general term for any lift used to transport skiers up a slope
