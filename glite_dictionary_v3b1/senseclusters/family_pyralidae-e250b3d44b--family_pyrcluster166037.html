family Pyralidae

noun
a family of moths including bee and flour moths
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli paɪˈrælɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli paɪˈrælɪdiː / (BrE)
A large family of moths that includes several species of bee moths, corn borers, and flour moths, many of which are agricultural pests.
