notecard

noun
small note card
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a small card used for writing notes, reminders, or brief information

Examples

A notecard with notes
She wrote her main ideas on a notecard before giving her presentation.
Each notecard contained a single fact to help him study for the exam.
He organized his research using color-coded notecards for each topic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: index card - a small card used for organizing notes or data
  • synonym: sticky note - a small piece of paper with a re-adherable strip of glue for notes
