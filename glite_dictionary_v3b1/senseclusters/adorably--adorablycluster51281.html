adorably

adverb
in an adorable way
Pronunciation: / əˈdɔrəbli / (AmE), / əˈdɔːrəbli / (BrE)
In a way that inspires affection or delight; in an adorable manner.

Examples

Puppies adorably tilt heads
Puppies often tilt their heads adorably when they hear a new sound.
She smiled adorably at the camera during her school play.
The kitten meowed adorably as it played with a ball of yarn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adorable - inspiring great affection or delight
