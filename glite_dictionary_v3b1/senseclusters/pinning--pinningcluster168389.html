pinning

noun
attaching with pins
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of attaching or fastening something with pins

Examples

Fabric pinning
The tailor completed the pinning before starting to sew the fabric pieces together.
Careful pinning ensures that the pattern stays in place while you cut the material.
She spent the afternoon pinning the decorations to the bulletin board for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fastening - the act of securing something in place
  • antonym: removal - the act of taking something away or off
