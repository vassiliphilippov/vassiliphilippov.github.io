pappy

adjective
soft and mushy
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæpi / (AmE), / ˈpæpi / (BrE)
having a soft, mushy, or porridge-like texture, often lacking firmness or substance

Examples

Pappy oatmeal
The oatmeal turned pappy after sitting in the pot for too long.
She disliked the pappy consistency of the overcooked vegetables.
After adding too much water, the mashed potatoes became unpleasantly pappy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firm - solid and not easily pressed or changed in shape
  • synonym: mushy - soft and pulpy in texture
