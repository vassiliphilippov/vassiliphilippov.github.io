bring about

verb
turn in the opposite direction (nautical)
7.1
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / brɪŋ əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to turn something, especially a boat, in the opposite direction

Examples

To bring about the boat
As soon as the danger became clear, they managed to bring about the yacht quickly.
Experienced sailors know when to bring about a boat to avoid rougher waters.
The captain ordered the crew to bring about the vessel as the storm clouds gathered.
