straighten out

verb
resolve or fix a problem or confusion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪtən aʊt / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtən aʊt /; / ˈstreɪt(ə)n aʊt / (BrE)

Let me straighten out any misunderstandings about the new policy.
The report was full of errors, but she managed to straighten out the confusing parts.
