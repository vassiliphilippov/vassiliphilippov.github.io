phobe

x
suffix for fear of
9.1
Pronunciation: /foʊb/ (AmE), /fəʊb/ (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns meaning a person who fears or dislikes a particular thing

Examples

Arachnophobe word ending
A person who fears spiders is called an arachnophobe.
The term technophobe describes someone who dislikes or avoids new technology.
He was labeled a xenobphobe because of his fear of foreigners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: phile - a suffix meaning a person who loves or has a strong affinity for something
  • synonym: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
