duckbill

noun
large freshwater fish with a paddle-shaped snout
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌkˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʌkˌbɪl / (BrE)
a large, primitive freshwater fish of the Mississippi valley with a long, paddle-shaped snout, also called a paddlefish.
