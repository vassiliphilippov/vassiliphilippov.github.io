low

adjective
quiet or faint in volume
1.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /loʊ/ (AmE), /ləʊ/ (BrE)
(of sound or voice) quiet or faint in volume; not loud.

Examples

Low volume music
A low hum came from the engine, barely noticeable over the wind.
She spoke in a low voice so as not to wake the baby.
The music was too low to hear clearly from the next room.
