Kemalism

noun
Atatürk's principles
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈke.mə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈke.mə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the political and social principles based on the reforms of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk in Turkey, emphasizing secularism, nationalism, and modernization

Examples

Kemalism principles
The country's constitution was shaped by the principles of Kemalism after the fall of the Ottoman Empire.
Many schools in Turkey teach the values of Kemalism as part of their curriculum.
Debates about Kemalism continue to influence Turkish politics today.
