bollard

noun
short, strong post for security or mooring
7.9 5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑlərd / (AmE), / ˈbɒlɑːd / (BrE)
a short, sturdy post used to control or direct road traffic, or to secure mooring lines on a wharf, quay, or ship.
