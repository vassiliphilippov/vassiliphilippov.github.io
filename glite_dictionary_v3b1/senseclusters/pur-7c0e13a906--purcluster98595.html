PUR

noun
polyurethane polymer used in plastics/adhesives
Pronunciation: / pi ju ɑr / (AmE), / piː juː ɑː / (BrE)
Any of various polymers containing the urethane radical, widely used in the production of adhesives, plastics, paints, and rubber.

Examples

PUR foam
Many automotive interiors use PUR foam for added comfort and durability.
The adhesive based on PUR offers strong bonding for woodworking applications.
The soles of these running shoes are made from PUR to provide extra cushioning.
