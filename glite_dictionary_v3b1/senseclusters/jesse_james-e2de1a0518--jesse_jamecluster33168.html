Jesse James

proper noun
notorious American outlaw, 19th century
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛsi ˈdʒeɪmz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛsi ˈdʒeɪmz / (BrE)
(1847–1882) A famous American outlaw who fought as a Confederate soldier and later led a gang that robbed banks and trains in the Western United States.
