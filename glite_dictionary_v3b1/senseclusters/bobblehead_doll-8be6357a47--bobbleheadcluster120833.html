bobblehead doll

noun
a figurine with a wobbly head
9.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.bəl.hed dɑl/ /; / /ˈbɑː.bəl.hed dɔːl/ (AmE), /ˈbɒb.əl.hed dɒl/ (BrE)
A small figurine with an oversized head mounted on a spring, so that the head bobs or wobbles when lightly tapped.

Examples

A bobblehead doll figure
He proudly displayed his signed bobblehead doll on the shelf above his bed.
She bought a bobblehead doll as a souvenir from the baseball game.
The bobblehead doll nodded with every bump in the road on the dashboard.
