imaginary creature

noun
fictional or mythical being
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri ˈkritʃər / (AmE), / ɪˈmadʒɪnəri ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
a being that exists only in the imagination, legends, myths, or fiction; not a real animal or person

Examples

An imaginary creature
In his dream, he met an imaginary creature with wings and three eyes.
Many cultures have tales of at least one imaginary creature, such as dragons or unicorns.
The story features an imaginary creature that lives under the bed and scares children at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mythical creature - a being from myths or legends, not real
  • antonym: real animal - an animal that actually exists in the real world
