defacer

noun
website vandal
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfeɪsər / (AmE), / dɪˈfeɪsə / (BrE)
a person who illegally alters or damages the appearance or content of something, especially a website, often by hacking

Examples

Website defacer
The police arrested the defacer who had posted offensive images on the city website.
After the defacer attacked the online forum, administrators worked quickly to restore the original content.
A skilled defacer can change a website's appearance in just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hacker - a person who gains unauthorized access to computer systems
  • antonym: protector - a person who guards or defends something from harm
  • synonym: vandal - a person who deliberately destroys or damages property
