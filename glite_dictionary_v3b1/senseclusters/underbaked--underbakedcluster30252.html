underbaked

adjective
not fully cooked
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈbeɪkt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈbeɪkt / (BrE)
not cooked long enough in the oven or not fully baked

Examples

Underbaked cookies
The cookies were underbaked and still doughy in the center.
If the bread is underbaked, it will be heavy and sticky inside.
She realized the pie was underbaked when she cut into it and saw the filling was runny.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baked - cooked completely in the oven
  • antonym: overbaked - cooked too long in the oven
  • synonym: raw - not cooked at all or only partially cooked
