poly

noun
person in polyamory
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑli / (AmE), / ˈpɒli / (BrE)
a person who practices or believes in having more than one romantic partner at the same time, with the consent of all involved

Examples

A poly person
He explained that he is a poly and values honesty in all his relationships.
Many polys attend community events to meet like-minded people.
As a poly, she believes in open and ethical communication with her partners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monogamist - a person who has only one romantic partner at a time
  • synonym: polyamorous - having romantic relationships with more than one person at a time
