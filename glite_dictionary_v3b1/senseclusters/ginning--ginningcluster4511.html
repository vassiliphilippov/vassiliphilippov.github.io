ginning

noun
operating a hoist
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɪn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɪn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or process of operating a gin, such as a hoist or winch, to lift or move heavy objects

Examples

Ginning heavy boxes
The workers spent the morning ginning crates onto the upper floor of the warehouse.
Old photographs show men ginning heavy machinery into place with ropes and pulleys.
Safety precautions are important when ginning large loads in a construction area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowering - the act of moving something downwards
