neuropsychiatrist

noun
brain and mental health doctor
7.8
Pronunciation: /nʊr.oʊ.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.trɪst/ /; / /njʊr.oʊ.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.trɪst/ (AmE), /njʊə.rəʊ.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.trɪst/ /; / /njuː.rəʊ.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.trɪst/ (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in both neurology and psychiatry, treating disorders that involve both the brain and mental health

Examples

Neuropsychiatrist specialist
After several unexplained symptoms, she was referred to a neuropsychiatrist for a comprehensive evaluation.
The neuropsychiatrist worked closely with both neurologists and psychologists to treat complex cases.
A neuropsychiatrist can help patients whose conditions affect both their brain and mental health.
