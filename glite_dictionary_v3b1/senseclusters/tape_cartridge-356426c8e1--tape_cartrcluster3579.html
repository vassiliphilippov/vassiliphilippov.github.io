tape cartridge

noun
magnetic tape container for recorders or computers
7.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /teɪp ˈkɑːr.trɪdʒ/ /; / /teɪp ˈkɑːr.trədʒ/ (AmE), /teɪp ˈkɑː.trɪdʒ/ /; / /teɪp ˈkɑː.trədʒ/ (BrE)
