civil rights activist

noun
person advocating for equal rights
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl raɪts ˈæktɪvɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪv.əl raɪts ˈæk.tɪ.vɪst / (BrE)
a person who actively works to promote or secure equal rights and opportunities for members of minority groups, especially regarding social and political issues

Examples

Civil rights activist organizes protests
As a civil rights activist, she organized numerous protests advocating for equal treatment.
Martin Luther King Jr. was a renowned civil rights activist who shaped American history.
The documentary profiles a young civil rights activist fighting racial injustice in her community.
