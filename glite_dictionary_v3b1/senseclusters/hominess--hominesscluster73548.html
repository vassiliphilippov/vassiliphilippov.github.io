hominess

noun
comfort and coziness
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊminəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊminəs / (BrE)
the quality of being comfortable, cozy, and having the feeling of home

Examples

Warm hominess
The hominess of the cottage made it the perfect place for a relaxing weekend.
She decorated her apartment to add a sense of hominess for her guests.
Many people appreciate the hominess of a small, well-loved café.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coldness - a lack of warmth or friendliness
  • synonym: cosiness - a feeling of warmth and comfort
  • synonym: hospitality - friendly and welcoming behavior to guests
