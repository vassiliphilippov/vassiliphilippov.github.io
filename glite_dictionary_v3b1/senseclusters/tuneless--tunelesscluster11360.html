tuneless

adjective
lacking a musical sound or tune
5.3 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtunl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàtjuÀênl…ôs / (BrE)
not having a musical sound or lacking a pleasant melody; not tuneful.

Examples

A tuneless song
Despite his enthusiasm, the performance was flat and tuneless.
His tuneless singing echoed throughout the empty hall.
The old piano produced a harsh, tuneless sound when she pressed the keys.

Synonyms

  • unmelodic
    6.5 0.01/m
    unmelodic sounds without a clear, pleasant tune, while tuneless describes music or sound that simply lacks melody.
  • unmelodious
    8.0 0.03/m
    unmelodious sounds harsh or discordant to the ear, while tuneless means the music or voice lacks a pleasing melody.
  • unmusical
    6.0 0.05/m
    unmusical can mean lacking musical skill or harmony, while tuneless specifically describes a sound that has no pleasant tune.

Related Terms

  • antonym: melodious - pleasant-sounding; musical
  • antonym: tuneful - having a pleasant or musical sound
