mountable

adjective
able to be attached
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be attached, fixed, or placed onto another surface or object

Examples

Mountable wall bracket
The new television is mountable on the wall for a cleaner look in your living room.
These speakers are easily mountable under your desk to save space.
The camera is mountable on a tripod for stable photography.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachable - able to be fastened or joined to something
  • antonym: removable - able to be taken off or separated
