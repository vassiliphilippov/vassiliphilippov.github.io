ferula

noun
medical splint
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrjələ / (AmE), / ˈfɛrjʊlə / (BrE)
a splint or device used to support or immobilize a broken or injured limb

Examples

Medical ferula
The doctor applied a ferula to stabilize the patient's fractured wrist.
A temporary ferula was used until a permanent cast could be fitted.
In some cases, a ferula is necessary to prevent further injury during healing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cast - a hard protective shell for immobilizing broken bones
  • synonym: splint - a rigid device used to support and immobilize a limb
