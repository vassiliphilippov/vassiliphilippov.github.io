relative incidence

noun
the frequency of something compared to another
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛlətɪv ˈɪnsɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛlətɪv ˈɪnsɪdəns / (BrE)
the frequency of occurrence of something in comparison to another thing, often expressed as a ratio or proportion

Examples

The relative incidence rate
Environmental factors can affect the relative incidence of certain plant diseases across different regions.
Researchers examined the relative incidence of infections between vaccinated and unvaccinated groups.
The relative incidence of heart disease was higher in men than in women in the study.
