Saddam

proper noun
Iraqi president (1979–2003), led wars with Iran and Kuwait
Pronunciation: / səˈdɑːm / (AmE), / sæˈdæm / (BrE)
Saddam Hussein (1937–2006), Iraqi leader who was president from 1979 to 2003 and waged war against Iran and Kuwait.

Examples

Saddam Hussein
During the late 1980s, Saddam was frequently mentioned in international news for his conflict with Iran.
Many Iraqis lived in fear under the regime led by Saddam.
The capture of Saddam in 2003 marked a turning point in Iraq's history.
