battle cry

noun
rallying shout used in battle
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəl ˌkraɪ /; / ˈbæt̬l ˌkraɪ / (AmE), / ˈbætəl ˌkraɪ /; / ˈbæt.əl ˌkraɪ / (BrE)
a loud shout, phrase, or slogan used to encourage people or rally them to action, especially in battle or for a cause

Examples

'Freedom now!' served as a powerful battle cry for the movement.
During the protest, the crowd chanted their battle cry with passion and unity.
The slogan 'Save Our Planet' became the battle cry for environmental activists worldwide.
