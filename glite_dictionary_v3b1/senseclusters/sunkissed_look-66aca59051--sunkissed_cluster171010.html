sunkissed look

noun
tan or sunlit appearance
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌkɪst lʊk / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌkɪst lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance of having been tanned or brightened by exposure to the sun

Examples

A sunkissed look
After a week at the beach, she returned with a sunkissed look that everyone noticed.
He applied makeup to create a sunkissed look for the wedding.
Photographers often seek models with a natural sunkissed look for summer campaigns.
