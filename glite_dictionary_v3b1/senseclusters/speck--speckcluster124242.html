speck

verb
mark with small spots
5.7
Pronunciation: / spɛk / (AmE), / spɛk / (BrE)
to mark with small spots or specks; to cover or sprinkle with tiny dots or particles

Examples

To speck a shirt
After the rain, mud specked his clean white pants.
Sunlight specks the forest floor as it filters through the leaves.
Tiny drops of paint specked the surface of the old table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spot - to mark with small or round marks
  • synonym: stain - to mark or discolor with unwanted spots
