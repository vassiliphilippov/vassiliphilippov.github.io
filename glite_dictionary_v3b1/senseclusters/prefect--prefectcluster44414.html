prefect

noun
student with authority
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌfɛkt / (AmE), / ˈpriːfɛkt / (BrE)
a student in a school who is given special authority or responsibilities, often to help maintain order or assist staff

Examples

School prefect
The prefect helped organize the school assembly and made sure everyone was seated quietly.
As a prefect, she was responsible for supervising the younger students during lunch breaks.
He was proud to be chosen as a prefect in his final year at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monitor - a student appointed to help maintain order in a class or school
  • antonym: principal - the head of a school
