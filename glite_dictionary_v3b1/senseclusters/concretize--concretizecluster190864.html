concretize

verb
make something definite or specific
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋ.krəˌtaɪz /; / ˈkɑːn.krəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋ.krɪ.taɪz /; / ˈkɒŋ.krɪˌtaɪz / (BrE)
to make something definite, specific, or real; to give concrete form to an idea or concept
