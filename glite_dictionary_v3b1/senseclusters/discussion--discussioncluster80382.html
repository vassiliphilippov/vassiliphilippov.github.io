discussion

noun
exchange of views or conversation
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a conversation or talk between people where ideas, opinions, or arguments are exchanged, often to make a decision or understand something better

Examples

A discussion between friends
After a lengthy discussion, they reached a unanimous decision.
The team held a discussion to plan their next strategy for the project.
We had a lively discussion about climate change during dinner.
After reading the article, we had a thorough discussion on its main arguments.
The professor led a lively discussion about climate change with her students.
The report includes an in-depth discussion of the economic impacts of the policy.
After a lengthy discussion, they finally reached a compromise on the issue.
The team held a discussion about the upcoming project to clarify everyone's roles.
There will be a discussion following the lecture where students can ask questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debate - formal discussion on a particular topic
