ringlet

noun
a brown butterfly with eyespots
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a butterfly of the subfamily Satyrinae (family Nymphalidae), typically brown with eyespots on the wings, commonly found in grassland habitats

Examples

A ringlet butterfly
Many nature enthusiasts enjoy watching the ringlet among the tall grasses.
The ringlet fluttered gently above the summer meadow.
We spotted several ringlets near the edge of the woodland trail.
