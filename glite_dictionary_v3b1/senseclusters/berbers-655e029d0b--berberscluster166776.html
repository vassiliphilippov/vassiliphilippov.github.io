Berbers

noun
North African ethnic group
7.4 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…úrb…öz / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêb…ôz / (BrE)
A North African ethnic group indigenous to the Maghreb, descended from ancient Berbers and Arabs.

Examples

Many Berbers continue to live in Morocco and Algeria, maintaining their cultural traditions.
The Berbers have played a significant role in the history of North Africa.

Synonyms
