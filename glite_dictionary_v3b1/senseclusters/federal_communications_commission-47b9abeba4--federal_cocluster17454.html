Federal Communications Commission

proper noun
US government agency regulating communications
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛdərəl kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃənz kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɛdərəl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an independent US government agency responsible for regulating interstate and international communications by radio, television, wire, cable, and satellite

Examples
