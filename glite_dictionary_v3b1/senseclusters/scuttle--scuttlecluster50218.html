scuttle

noun
coal container with a sloping lip
8.2 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈskʌt.əl / (BrE)
A metal container with a sloping lid or lip, used for holding and pouring coal onto a fire.

Examples

A coal scuttle
He filled the scuttle in the cellar before carrying it upstairs.
She brought another scuttle of coal to keep the fire burning.
The ornate brass scuttle stood beside the old fireplace.
