bit

noun
metal piece in a horse's mouth for control
Pronunciation: / bɪt / (AmE), / bɪt / (BrE)
a piece of metal placed in a horse's mouth and attached to the reins, used by the rider to control the horse

Examples

Horse bit
Over time, the old bit became worn and needed replacement.
The new rider fitted the bit into the horse's mouth before setting off.
Without a properly adjusted bit, the horse refused to respond to the reins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridle - the headgear used to control a horse, to which a bit may be attached
