cedar

noun
coniferous tree resembling a cedar
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdɚ /; / ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a large, evergreen coniferous tree called cedar, valued for its aromatic wood

Examples

Tall cedar tree

Related Terms

  • synonym: cedarwood - aromatic wood from cedar trees
  • synonym: juniper - coniferous tree or shrub of the genus Juniperus, related to cedars
  • antonym: pine - another type of coniferous tree, different genus
  • antonym: true cedar - trees of the genus Cedrus, the typical or original cedars
