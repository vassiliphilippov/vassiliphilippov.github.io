jargon

noun
specialized language of a group

Examples

Technical jargon

Synonyms

    5.6 7.68/m

  • lingo
    7.8 1.62/m
    lingo is an informal, everyday set of words a group uses, while jargon is any specialist language outsiders may not follow.
  • argot
    9.9 0.25/m
    argot is a secretive group language used to hide meaning, while jargon is general specialist speech that is not always secret.
  • cant
    9.9 0.21/m
    cant is an insincere or exclusionary group speech that hides motives, while jargon is neutral technical language used by professions.
