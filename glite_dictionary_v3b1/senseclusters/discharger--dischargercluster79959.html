discharger

noun
one who fulfills obligations
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒər / (AmE), / dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a person or entity that pays off or fulfills a debt, obligation, or claim

Examples

Discharger of a debt
The discharger of the loan ensured all payments were made on time.
As the discharger of the obligation, the company settled all outstanding claims.
The court recognized the trustee as the discharger after all debts were paid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debtor - a person who owes money
  • synonym: payer - a person who pays money owed
