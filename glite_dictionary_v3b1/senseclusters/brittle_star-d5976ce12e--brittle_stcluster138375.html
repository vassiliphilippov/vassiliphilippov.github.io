brittle star

noun
marine animal with slender, fragile arms
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪt̬l̩ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtl stɑː / (BrE)
a marine animal resembling a starfish, with long, slender, fragile arms radiating from a small central disc
