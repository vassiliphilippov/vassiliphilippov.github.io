ablative case

noun
a grammatical case indicating movement away or means
9.6 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈæb.lə.tɪv keɪs/ (AmE), /ˈæb.lə.tɪv keɪs/ (BrE)
(in grammar) a case of nouns, pronouns, and adjectives found in some languages, used to show movement away from something, or the means or manner by which an action is performed.
