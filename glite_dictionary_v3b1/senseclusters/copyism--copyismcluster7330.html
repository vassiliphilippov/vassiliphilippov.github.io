copyism

noun
habit of imitation
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.pi.ɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈkɑː.pi.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈkɒp.i.ɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈkɒp.i.zəm/ (BrE)
