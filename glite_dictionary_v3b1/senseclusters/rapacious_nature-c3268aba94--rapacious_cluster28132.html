rapacious nature

noun
greediness or aggressive acquisitiveness
Pronunciation: / rəˈpeɪʃəs ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / rəˈpeɪʃəs ˈneɪ.tʃə / (BrE)
a tendency to be extremely greedy or aggressively acquisitive

Examples

Rapacious nature of companies
Environmentalists criticized the company for its rapacious nature towards natural resources.
His rapacious nature made him unpopular among his colleagues.
The rapacious nature of the industry shocked many.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous nature - a tendency to give or share generously
  • synonym: greed - an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power
