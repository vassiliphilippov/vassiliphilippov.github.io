mumbo jumbo

noun
meaningless or confusing language or rituals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌmboʊ ˈdʒʌmboʊ / (AmE), / ˌmʌmbəʊ ˈdʒʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
language, ideas, or rituals that are confusing or meaningless, often intended to obscure the truth or impress others

Examples
