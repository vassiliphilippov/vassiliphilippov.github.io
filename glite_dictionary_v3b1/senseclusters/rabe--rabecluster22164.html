rabe

noun
leafy green vegetable
Pronunciation: / rɑːb / (AmE), / rɑːb / (BrE)
a leafy green vegetable related to broccoli, commonly used in Italian cuisine

Examples

Rabe with garlic
We sautéed rabe with garlic and olive oil for dinner.
The chef added fresh rabe to the pasta for extra flavor.
You can steam rabe and serve it as a healthy side dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broccoli rabe - a bitter green vegetable also known as rapini
  • synonym: spinach - a leafy green vegetable often used in salads and cooking
