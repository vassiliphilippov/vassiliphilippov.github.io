fuck you

noun
vulgar rejection or insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / fʌk ju / (AmE), / fʌk juː / (BrE)
A vulgar expression used to strongly reject, insult, or show contempt for someone or their idea.

Examples

A loud 'fuck you'
She shouted 'fuck you' at the driver who cut her off in traffic.
The comment 'fuck you' was removed from the online discussion for violating community guidelines.
When he heard the proposal, he just said, 'fuck you' and walked out of the room.
