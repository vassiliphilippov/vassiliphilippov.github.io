beatless

adjective
undefeated in competition
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtləs / (AmE), / ˈbiːtləs / (BrE)
not having been defeated or beaten in a competition or contest

Examples

Beatless team

The boxer was proud of his beatless record throughout the season.
They celebrated their beatless run at the end of the tournament.
