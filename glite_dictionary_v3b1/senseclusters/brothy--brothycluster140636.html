brothy

adjective
like broth
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔθi / (AmE), / ˈbrɒθi / (BrE)
having the thin, liquid quality or flavor characteristic of broth

Examples

A brothy soup
The noodles were served in a brothy chicken soup that was light and flavorful.
I prefer a brothy base for my ramen rather than a thick, creamy one.
Her homemade vegetable stew turned out more brothy than she expected, but it was still delicious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soupy - having the thick or substantial quality of soup
  • synonym: watery - containing a lot of water; thin or diluted
