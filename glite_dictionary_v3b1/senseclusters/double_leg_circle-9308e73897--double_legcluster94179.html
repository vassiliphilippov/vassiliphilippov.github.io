double leg circle

noun
gymnastic move on the pommel horse
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌb.əl leg ˈsɜːr.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈdʌb.əl leg ˈsɜː.kəl/ (BrE)
a gymnastic exercise on the pommel horse in which the gymnast swings both legs together in a circle while alternating hands on the pommels

Examples

Double leg circle exercise
Coaches often emphasize the importance of rhythm and balance during the double leg circle.
His double leg circle on the pommel horse was executed with flawless precision.
Learning the double leg circle is a major milestone for young male gymnasts.
