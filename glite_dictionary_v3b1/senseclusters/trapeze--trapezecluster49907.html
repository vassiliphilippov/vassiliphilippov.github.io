trapeze

verb
to perform on trapeze
7.5
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiːz / (AmE), / trəˈpiːz / (BrE)
to perform acrobatic movements or swing on a trapeze, especially as part of a circus act

Examples

To trapeze in shows
She spent years trapezing in the circus before becoming a coach.
The children imagined they were trapezing high above the crowd like real acrobats.
He dreamed of trapezing across the big top under the bright lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perform - to present an act or skill in front of an audience
  • synonym: swing - to move back and forth or from side to side while suspended
