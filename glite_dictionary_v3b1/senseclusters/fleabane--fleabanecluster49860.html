fleabane

noun
a weed with small daisy-like flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈfliˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈfliːˌbeɪn /; / ˈfliːbeɪn / (BrE)
a plant of the daisy family with small yellow or white flowers, often considered a weed and once believed to repel fleas
