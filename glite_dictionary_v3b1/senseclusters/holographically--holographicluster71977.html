holographically

adverb
in author's handwriting
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːləˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɒləˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is written entirely in the handwriting of the author

Examples

Holographically written will
The will was written holographically, making it clear it was the testator's own work.
She prefers to draft her poems holographically before typing them up.
The document was signed holographically to prove its authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manually - by hand rather than by machine
  • antonym: typed - produced using a typewriter or computer keyboard
