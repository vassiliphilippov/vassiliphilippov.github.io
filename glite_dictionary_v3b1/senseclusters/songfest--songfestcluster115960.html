songfest

noun
group singing event
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔŋˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋˌfɛst / (BrE)
an event or program where people come together to sing songs or enjoy many songs, often as a celebration

Examples

A songfest event
The community center organized a songfest to bring neighbors together for an evening of music.
Every summer, the camp hosts a songfest where everyone sings around the campfire.
Our school’s annual songfest features performances from each grade level.
The school organized a songfest to celebrate the end of the year.
Fans tuned in to the radio station's summer songfest featuring classic hits from the 1980s.
Our family enjoyed a lively songfest around the campfire during our vacation.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: concert - a musical performance given in public
  • synonym: recital - a performance of music or poetry, usually by a soloist or small group
  • synonym: singalong - an informal event where people sing songs together
