flurried

verb
moved quickly and lightly
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflɜːrid / (AmE), / ˈflʌrɪd / (BrE)
moved quickly and lightly, often in a sudden or agitated way.

Examples

Birds flurried away
The sparrows flurried from the rooftop when the dog started barking loudly.
Papers flurried across the desk as the window was opened suddenly.
When the bell rang, students flurried out of the classroom in excitement.
