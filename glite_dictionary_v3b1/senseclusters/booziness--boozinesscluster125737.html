booziness

noun
alcoholic flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːzinəs / (AmE), / ˈbuːzinəs / (BrE)
the noticeable taste, smell, or strength of alcohol in a drink or food

Examples

Booziness of the wine
The booziness of the cocktail was too strong for her taste.
He could detect a hint of booziness in the chocolate cake.
Reducing the wine in the sauce lessened its booziness but kept the rich flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcoholic - containing or tasting of alcohol
  • antonym: sobriety - the state of not having alcohol present
