oxytetracycline

noun
antibiotic drug
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪˌtɛtrəˈsaɪklin / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪˌtɛtrəˈsaɪkliːn / (BrE)
a yellow crystalline antibiotic used to treat a range of bacterial and rickettsial infections

Examples

Oxytetracycline antibiotic
Due to bacterial resistance, oxytetracycline is sometimes replaced by newer antibiotics.
Farmers have used oxytetracycline to prevent infections in livestock.
The doctor prescribed oxytetracycline to treat the patient's respiratory infection.
