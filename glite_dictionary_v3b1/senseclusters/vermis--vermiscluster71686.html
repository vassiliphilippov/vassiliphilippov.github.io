vermis

noun
central part of the cerebellum
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜːrmɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmɪs / (BrE)
the narrow, central part of the cerebellum situated between its two hemispheres

Examples

The vermis of cerebellum
Damage to the vermis can affect a person's coordination and balance.
The vermis plays an important role in integrating sensory input for movement.
The MRI scan revealed an abnormality in the vermis of the cerebellum.
