milk cow

noun
cow kept for milk production

A cow that is bred and kept for producing milk, rather than meat or work.

Examples

A milk cow farm
After calving, the milk cow produces more milk to feed both her calf and for collection.
Each milk cow is inspected regularly to ensure the highest standards of hygiene.
The farmer owns a large milk cow that supplies the family with fresh milk every day.
