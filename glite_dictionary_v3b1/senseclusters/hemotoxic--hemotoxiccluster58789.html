hemotoxic

adjective
damaging blood cells
8.6
Pronunciation: /hem.ə.ˈtɑːk.sɪk/ /; / /hem.oʊ.ˈtɑːk.sɪk/ (AmE), /hem.ə.ˈtɒk.sɪk/ /; / /hiː.mə.ˈtɒk.sɪk/ (BrE)
