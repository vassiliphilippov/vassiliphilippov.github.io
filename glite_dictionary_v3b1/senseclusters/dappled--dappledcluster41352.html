dappled

adjective
marked with spots or patches of color
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdæpəld / (AmE), / ˈdæpəld / (BrE)
marked with spots or patches of different colors or shades, usually in an irregular pattern

Examples

Dappled light pattern

The horse had a beautiful dappled coat that shimmered in the sun.
The sunlight filtered through the trees, creating a dappled pattern on the forest floor.
