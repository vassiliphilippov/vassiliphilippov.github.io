Synonyms

  • state of mind
    3.4 5/m
    state of mind describes a person's mental or emotional condition at a time, while mood names a temporary feeling shown in behavior.
  • emotional state
    3.6 1/m
    emotional state focuses on the person's feelings at a particular time, while mood often refers to a temporary, visible way they act.
  • frame of mind
    3.7 1/m
    frame of mind is the mental attitude someone has in a moment, while mood is a general temporary feeling seen in behavior.
  • humor
    2.1 1/m
    humor means a temporary state, often used in old expressions like 'in a bad humor', while mood is the common modern word.
  • temper
    4.1 3/m
    temper often means a person's tendency to become angry or their usual strong reaction, while mood is a temporary feeling or state.
