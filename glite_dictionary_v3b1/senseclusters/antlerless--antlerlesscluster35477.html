antlerless

adjective
without antlers
Pronunciation: / ˈæntlərˌlɛs / (AmE), / ˈæntləˌlɛs / (BrE)
lacking antlers, especially referring to animals that normally have them

Examples

Antlerless deer
The antlerless deer wandered alone through the forest.
During the winter, many male deer become antlerless after shedding their antlers.
Hunters are only allowed to take antlerless elk during the special season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hornless - lacking horns
