Great Sandy Desert

proper noun
desert region in northwestern Australia
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈsændi ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈsændi ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a large desert region in northwestern Australia, located north of the Gibson Desert

Examples

The Great Sandy Desert
Driving across the Great Sandy Desert requires careful preparation due to its harsh conditions.
Several Aboriginal communities live within the boundaries of the Great Sandy Desert.
The Great Sandy Desert is one of the largest deserts in Australia, known for its remote and arid landscape.
