predetermination

noun
prior intention or mental resolve
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː.dɪˌtɝː.məˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriː.dɪˌtɜː.mɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of deciding or resolving to do something before it happens; an intention formed in advance

Examples

Personal predetermination
He approached the negotiations with a clear predetermination to reach a fair agreement.
Her predetermination to finish the marathon helped her push through the pain.
The predetermination of the committee members influenced the outcome of the vote.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decision - the act of making up one's mind
  • synonym: intent - determined resolve to do something
