heliothis moth

noun
medium-sized moth; larvae are corn earworms
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliˈoʊθɪs mɑθ / (AmE), / ˌhiːliˈɒθɪs mɒθ / (BrE)
A medium-sized moth of the genus Heliothis, whose larvae are known as corn earworms and are major agricultural pests.

Examples

The heliothis moth larvae
Farmers monitor fields closely for the presence of heliothis moth larvae to minimize crop damage.
The heliothis moth poses a serious threat to corn and tomato crops every year.
The scientist studied the life cycle of the heliothis moth in the laboratory.

Related Terms
