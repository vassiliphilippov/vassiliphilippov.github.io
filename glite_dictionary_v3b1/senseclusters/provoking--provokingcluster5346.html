provoking

adjective
causing strong reactions
4.2
Pronunciation: / prəˈvoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / prəˈvəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
causing strong feelings, reactions, or thoughts in someone

Examples

Provoking ideas
The professor gave a provoking lecture that challenged the students' assumptions.
Her provoking questions led to a lively debate among the panelists.
It was a provoking article that made many readers reconsider their opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspiring - causing someone to feel motivated or creative
  • synonym: stimulating - encouraging interest or enthusiasm
