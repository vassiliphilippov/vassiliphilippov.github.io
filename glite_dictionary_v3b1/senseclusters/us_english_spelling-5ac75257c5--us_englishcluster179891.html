US English spelling

noun
American conventions of English spelling
2.5
Pronunciation: / ju ɛs ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / juː ɛs ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the conventions of English spelling as used in the United States, often differing from British spelling in certain words

Examples

US English spelling rules
Some words such as 'color' follow the US English spelling, while others use British convention.
Teachers in the United States teach students US English spelling rules from an early age.
The software allows you to select either British or US English spelling when checking documents.
