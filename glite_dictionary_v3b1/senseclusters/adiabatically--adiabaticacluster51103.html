adiabatically

adverb
without heat exchange
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌædiəˈbætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌædɪəˈbætɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves no heat exchange with the environment

Examples

Expand adiabatically
The piston was moved adiabatically to prevent any heat from entering or leaving the system.
In this experiment, the gas expands adiabatically, so its temperature drops as it does work.
The process occurs adiabatically, ensuring that all energy changes are due to work, not heat transfer.

  • antonym: isothermally - in a way that keeps temperature constant
