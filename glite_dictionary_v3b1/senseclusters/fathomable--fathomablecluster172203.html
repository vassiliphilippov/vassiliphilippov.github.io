fathomable

adjective
able to be understood; comprehensible
Pronunciation: / ˈfæð.ə.mə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈfað.ə.mə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a concept or situation) able to be understood or comprehended; intelligible.

Examples

Fathomable explanation
She explained the complex theory in such a way that it became fathomable even to those unfamiliar with the subject.
The reason behind his sudden departure was completely fathomable to everyone in the office.
While some poems are mysterious, this one is quite fathomable and open to interpretation.

  • antonym: unfathomable - impossible to understand; incomprehensible
