gassy

adjective
resembling or full of gas
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsi / (AmE), / ˈɡasi / (BrE)
Having the characteristics or quality of gas; resembling or full of gas.

Examples

Gassy planet
The atmosphere on that planet is thick and gassy, mainly composed of hydrogen.
The swamp released a gassy odor as we walked by.
This drink is too gassy for my taste and makes me burp.
