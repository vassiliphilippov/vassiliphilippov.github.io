stutteringly

adverb
in a halting way
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌt̬ɚɪŋli /; / ˈstʌtərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstʌtərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a hesitant or interrupted way, with repeated pauses or stops (especially in speech)

Examples

To speak stutteringly
He answered the difficult question stutteringly, searching for the right words.
The lights flickered stutteringly before finally going out during the storm.
She began her speech stutteringly, but soon gained confidence and spoke clearly.
He answered the question stutteringly, clearly nervous in front of the audience.
The child read stutteringly from the book, pausing often between words.
She introduced herself stutteringly during her first day at the new school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fluently - in a smooth and effortless manner
  • synonym: haltingly - in a slow and hesitant manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a continuous and even manner
