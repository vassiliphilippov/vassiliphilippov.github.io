press home

verb
push the Home button to return to home screen
3.5
Pronunciation: /pres hoʊm/ (AmE), /pres həʊm/ (BrE)
To push the Home button or control on a device (phone, tablet, or other interface) to return to the home screen or exit the current app.

Examples

To press home button
To exit the app quickly, simply press home once and wait for the launcher to appear.
She accidentally closed the game when she tried to press home during the intense level.
After changing the settings, he pressed home and returned to the main screen to check the widgets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press a button - to push a physical or on-screen button to operate a device
  • synonym: return to home screen - to go back to the main screen or launcher of a device
