clayish

adjective
like clay
Pronunciation: / ˈkleɪɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkleɪɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or containing clay in appearance, texture, or composition

Examples

A clayish soil
The garden struggled to drain because the soil was too clayish after the rain.
Her hands were stained from working with the clayish mud by the riverbank.
The potter preferred a clayish mixture for shaping sturdy bowls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clayey - having the qualities or texture of clay
  • antonym: sandy - containing or resembling sand
