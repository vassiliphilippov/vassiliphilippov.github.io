sabine pine

noun
pine tree native to southwestern California

Pronunciation: /sə.ˈbiːn paɪn/ /; / /ˈseɪ.baɪn paɪn/ (AmE), /ˈsæ.baɪn paɪn/ /; / /sə.ˈbiːn paɪn/ (BrE)
