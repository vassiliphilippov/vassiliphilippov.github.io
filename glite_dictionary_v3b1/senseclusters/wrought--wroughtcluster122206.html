wrought

adjective
shaped or fashioned by work or effort
4.2
Pronunciation: / rɑt / (AmE), / rɔːt / (BrE)
(of metal or other material) shaped or fashioned by hammering, working, or effort.

Examples

A wrought silver bracelet
She wore a beautiful bracelet made from wrought silver.
The artisan skillfully crafted a wrought iron gate for the old mansion.
The museum displayed several wrought metal sculptures by local artists.
