hangdog

adjective
showing shame or guilt
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋˌdɒɡ /; / ˈhæŋ.dɒɡ / (BrE)
having an expression or appearance that shows guilt, embarrassment, or shame

Examples

Hangdog expression
He walked into the room with a hangdog expression after breaking the vase.
She gave him a hangdog look, clearly embarrassed by her mistake.
The children sat in hangdog silence after being caught cheating on the test.
He gave me a hangdog look when I asked about the broken window.
The child's hangdog face showed he knew he'd done something wrong.
With a hangdog expression, she admitted her mistake to the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiant - showing resistance or bold disobedience
  • synonym: downcast - looking downward or appearing sad or discouraged
  • synonym: guilty - feeling or showing guilt
  • synonym: shamefaced - showing embarrassment or shame
