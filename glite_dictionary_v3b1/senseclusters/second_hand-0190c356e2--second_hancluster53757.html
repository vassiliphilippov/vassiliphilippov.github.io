second hand

noun
by means of an intermediate source
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənd hænd / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd hand / (BrE)
by means of an intermediate person or source rather than directly; not firsthand.

Examples

Second hand information
He preferred not to judge people based on second hand accounts.
Much of the information was available second hand, not from those who witnessed the events.
She heard about the news only second hand through a mutual friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firsthand - coming directly from personal experience or source
