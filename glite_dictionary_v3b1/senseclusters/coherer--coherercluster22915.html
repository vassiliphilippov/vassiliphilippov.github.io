coherer

noun
unifying agent
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhɪrər / (AmE), / kəʊˈhɪərə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings separate parts, ideas, or elements together into a unified whole

Examples

The new manager served as a coherer for the previously fragmented team.
A good leader often acts as a coherer during times of organizational change.
The festival became a coherer for the diverse communities in the city.
