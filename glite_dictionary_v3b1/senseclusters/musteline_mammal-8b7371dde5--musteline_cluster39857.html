musteline mammal

noun
carnivorous mammal of the weasel family
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstəlaɪn ˈmæməl / (AmE), / ˈmʌstɪliːn ˈmaməl / (BrE)
a carnivorous mammal of the weasel family (Mustelidae), characterized by a slender body and often valued for its fur

Examples

A musteline mammal species
Many musteline mammals have long, slender bodies and sharp teeth adapted for hunting.
Otters, badgers, and weasels are all examples of musteline mammals.
