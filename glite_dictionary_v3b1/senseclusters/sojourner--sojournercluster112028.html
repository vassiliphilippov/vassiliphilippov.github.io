sojourner

noun
temporary resident
Pronunciation: / soʊˈdʒɝːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdʒɜːnə / (BrE)
a person who resides temporarily in a place

Examples

A sojourner in town
As a sojourner in the town, he tried to learn about local customs during his brief stay.
Many sojourners leave with fond memories of the places they visit.
The city welcomed every sojourner who came seeking new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resident - a person who lives somewhere permanently
