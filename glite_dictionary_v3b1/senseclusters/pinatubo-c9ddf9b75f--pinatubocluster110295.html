Pinatubo

proper noun
volcano in the Philippines
8.0
Pronunciation: / piːnəˈtuːboʊ / (AmE), / piːnəˈtuːbəʊ / (BrE)
a volcano in Luzon, northwest of Manila in the Philippines, known for its major eruption in 1991 after centuries of dormancy

Examples

Pinatubo volcano eruption
Ash from Pinatubo spread across the globe and affected the world’s climate.
The 1991 eruption of Pinatubo was one of the most powerful volcanic events of the 20th century.
Thousands of people were evacuated from the areas surrounding Pinatubo before the eruption.
