rooinek

noun
British newcomer (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈruːiˌnɛk /; / ˈrɔɪˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈruːiˌnɛk /; / ˈrɔɪˌnɛk / (BrE)
a South African slang or informal term for a British person or an English-speaking white South African of British descent, often used insultingly or for newcomers
