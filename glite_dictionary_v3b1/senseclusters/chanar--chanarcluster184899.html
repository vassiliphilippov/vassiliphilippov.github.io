chanar

noun
Argentinian thorny shrub with edible berries
Pronunciation: / tʃɑːˈnɑr / (AmE), / tʃɑːˈnɑː / (BrE)
A thorny shrub or small tree native to central Argentina, producing small orange or yellow flowers and edible berries.
