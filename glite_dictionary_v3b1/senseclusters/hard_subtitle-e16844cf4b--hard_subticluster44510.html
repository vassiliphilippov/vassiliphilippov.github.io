hard subtitle

noun
permanently embedded subtitle in video
7.6 4.1
Pronunciation: / hɑrd ˈsʌbˌtaɪ.tl̩ / (AmE), / hɑːd ˈsʌb.taɪ.tl̩ / (BrE)
a subtitle that is permanently embedded into the video frames and cannot be turned off or removed

Examples

Hard subtitles embedded permanently
Hard subtitles make it difficult to remove or edit the translated text from the video.
He prefers watching anime with hard subtitles because they're always visible regardless of the media player.
The old DVD release only included hard subtitles, so you can't turn them off.
