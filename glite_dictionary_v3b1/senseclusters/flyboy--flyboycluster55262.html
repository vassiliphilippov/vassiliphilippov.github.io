flyboy

noun
flashy young man
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a young man who dresses or acts in a flashy, fashionable, and attention-seeking way

Examples

Stylish flyboy outfit
Everyone turned to stare at the flyboy in his bright jacket and polished shoes as he entered.
The party was full of flyboys showing off their latest designer outfits.
He always wanted to be known as a flyboy with his stylish clothes and confident attitude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dandy - a man who places great importance on his appearance and clothes
  • synonym: show-off - a person who tries to attract attention by behaving in a conspicuous way
