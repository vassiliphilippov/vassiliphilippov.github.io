gloriosa

noun

A climber plant of the genus Gloriosa, native to tropical Africa and Asia, noted for its showy yellow, red, or purple flowers and poisonous parts.

Examples

A climbing gloriosa
A climbing gloriosa can quickly cover a trellis with its striking red and yellow blooms.
The gloriosa is admired for its vibrant, flame-like petals in gardens around the world.
Though beautiful, all parts of the gloriosa are highly toxic if ingested.
