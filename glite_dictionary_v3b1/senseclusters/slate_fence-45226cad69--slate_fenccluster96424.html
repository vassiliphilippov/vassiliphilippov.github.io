slate fence

noun
fence made of vertical slate slabs
3.9
Pronunciation: / sleɪt fɛns / (AmE), / sleɪt fɛns / (BrE)
a fence constructed from vertical slabs of slate wired together, typically used as a form of palisade

Examples

A slate fence panel
A skilled mason was hired to repair several loose slabs in the slate fence along the road.
Many gardens in the region use a traditional slate fence for decoration and privacy.
The old farmhouse was bordered by a sturdy slate fence that had stood the test of time.
