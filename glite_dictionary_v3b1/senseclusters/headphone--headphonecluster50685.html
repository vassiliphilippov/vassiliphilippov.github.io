headphone

noun
device for listening to audio privately
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.foʊn/ (AmE), /ˈhed.fəʊn/ (BrE)
A device worn over or in the ears to listen to audio signals such as music or speech, typically connected to a radio, computer, or other electronic device.

Examples

Headphones for music
He put on his headphones to listen to music without disturbing anyone.
New noise-cancelling headphones make it easier to concentrate in a busy office.
Please bring your headphones for the online meeting tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earphone - a small headphone worn inside the ear
  • antonym: loudspeaker - device for converting electrical signals into sound to fill a space
