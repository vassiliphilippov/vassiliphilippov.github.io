ibuprofen

noun
pain-relieving anti-inflammatory drug
Pronunciation: / aɪˈbroʊfjʊˌrɛn / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.bjuːˈprəʊ.fɛn / (BrE)
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and lower fever

Examples

Take ibuprofen medicine
After spraining his ankle, he took ibuprofen to reduce swelling and discomfort.
People often use ibuprofen to lower a fever or ease a headache.
The doctor prescribed ibuprofen to help with her back pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspirin - an NSAID used to relieve pain and reduce fever
  • synonym: naproxen - a similar NSAID used to relieve pain and inflammation
