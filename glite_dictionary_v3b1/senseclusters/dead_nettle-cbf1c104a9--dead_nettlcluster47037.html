dead nettle

noun
a perennial Eurasian herb with nettle-like leaves
Pronunciation: / dɛd ˈnɛtəl /; / dɛd ˈnɛt̬l̩ /; / dɛd ˈnɛtl̩ / (AmE), / dɛd ˈnɛtl /; / dɛd ˈnɛt.əl / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Lamium, with nettle-like leaves but no stinging hairs, often grown in gardens or found as a weed
