birtherism

noun
conspiracy about birthplace
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrθərɪzəm /; / ˈbɜːrðərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθərɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or political movement that claims a public figure was not born in the country and is therefore ineligible for office

Examples
