cascade penstemon

noun
a plant with blue-purple flowers native to the Pacific Northwest
6.9
Pronunciation: / kæˈskeɪd ˈpɛnstəmən / (AmE), / kæˈskeɪd ˈpɛnstɪmən / (BrE)
a perennial plant with whorls of deep blue to dark purple flowers at the tips of erect leafy stems, native to moist areas from British Columbia to Oregon

Examples

The cascade penstemon flower
Gardeners value the cascade penstemon for its vibrant blue-purple flowers and hardy nature.
She photographed a cluster of cascade penstemon blooming along the trail in July.
The cascade penstemon thrives in the moist meadows of the Pacific Northwest.
