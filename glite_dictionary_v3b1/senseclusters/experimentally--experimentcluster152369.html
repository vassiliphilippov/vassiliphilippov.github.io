experimentally

adverb
involving experiments
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspɛrəˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
in a way that is based on or involves experiments; by means of experiment.

Examples

To experimentally test
Scientists are experimentally testing the effects of the new drug on cell growth.
The hypothesis was experimentally verified through a series of controlled studies.
These results can only be obtained experimentally, not by theoretical calculation alone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: theoretically - in a way that is based on theory rather than experiment
