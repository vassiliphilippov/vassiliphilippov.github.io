magnetite

noun
magnetic iron oxide mineral
6.4 8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡnəˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmæɡnɪtaɪt / (BrE)
a black, magnetic mineral consisting of an oxide of iron, found in igneous and metamorphic rocks

Examples

Black magnetite mineral
Geologists discovered a rich deposit of magnetite near the volcanic ridge.
Magnets easily attract magnetite because of its high iron content.
