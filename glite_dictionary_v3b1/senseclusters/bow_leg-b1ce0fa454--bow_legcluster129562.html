bow leg

noun
outward curvature of the legs
6.0
Pronunciation: / boʊ lɛɡ / (AmE), / bəʊ lɛɡ / (BrE)
a condition where the legs curve outward at or below the knee, causing the knees to stay apart when standing with feet together

Examples

A bow leg condition
People with bow leg often notice a gap between their knees when standing with feet together.
She wore special braces as a child to correct her bow leg.
The doctor diagnosed him with bow leg after examining his stance.
Children with a bow leg often outgrow the condition as they get older.
The doctor explained that a bow leg could be corrected with special exercises.
When he stood straight, his bow leg was clearly visible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: knock-knee - a condition in which the knees angle in and touch each other when standing
