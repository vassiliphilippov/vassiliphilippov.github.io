pants presser

noun
device that presses wrinkles from trousers
Pronunciation: / pænts ˈprɛsər / (AmE), / pænts ˈprɛsə / (BrE)
a device for removing wrinkles from trousers by pressing them between heated surfaces

Examples

A pants presser device
After work, he put his trousers in the pants presser to smooth out the wrinkles.
She bought a new pants presser to keep her suits looking sharp.
The hotel room had a pants presser for guests' convenience.
