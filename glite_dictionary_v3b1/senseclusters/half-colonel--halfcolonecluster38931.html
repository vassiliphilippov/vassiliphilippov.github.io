half-colonel

noun
informal, partial or unofficial colonel
Pronunciation: / ˈhæf ˌkɝː.nəl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf ˌkɜː.nəl / (BrE)
an informal, humorous term for someone with partial authority or an unofficial status equivalent to a colonel

Examples

The half-colonel rank
After years as the half-colonel, he was finally promoted to full colonel.
Everyone teased him by calling him the half-colonel of the office since he was in charge of only one team.
She found the nickname half-colonel amusing after she was given temporary command.
