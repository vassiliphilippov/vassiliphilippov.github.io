requiem shark

noun
a type of shark in the family Carcharhinidae
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkwiəm ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈrɛkwɪəm ʃɑːk / (BrE)
any of various medium to large sharks of the family Carcharhinidae, including both harmless and dangerous species found in marine environments.
