detention

noun
school punishment requiring extra time
2.8 1.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈten.ʃən/ /; / /də.ˈten.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈten.ʃən/ /; / /də.ˈten.ʃən/ (BrE)
A punishment given to a student in which they are required to stay at school after normal hours.

Examples

After-school detention
He received detention for talking during class.
If you are late again, you'll be given detention.
Many students dread staying after school for detention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suspension - temporary removal of privilege to attend classes as punishment
