Tugela

proper noun
waterfall in South Africa
Pronunciation: / tuːˈheɪlə / (AmE), / tjuːˈheɪlə / (BrE)
a major waterfall in eastern South Africa, noted for its great height and multiple drops; also known as Tugela Falls.

Examples

Tugela Falls
