Tugela

proper noun
waterfall in South Africa
8.8
Pronunciation: / tuːˈheɪlə / (AmE), / tjuːˈheɪlə / (BrE)
a major waterfall in eastern South Africa, noted for its great height and multiple drops; also known as Tugela Falls.

Examples

Tugela Falls
Many tourists visit South Africa each year to see the spectacular Tugela Falls.
Mist from the Tugela can often be seen from miles away in the Drakensberg mountains.
The Tugela is considered one of the tallest waterfalls in the world.
