pathway

noun
bundle of nerve fibers in the brain
Pronunciation: / ˈpæθˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθweɪ / (BrE)
a bundle of myelinated nerve fibers in the brain that transmits signals along a specific route

Examples

A nerve pathway
Damage to the motor pathway can result in impaired movement.
Researchers discovered a new neural pathway involved in memory formation.
The optic pathway carries visual information from the retina to the brain.
