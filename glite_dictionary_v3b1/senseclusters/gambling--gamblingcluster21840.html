gambling

noun
playing games of chance for stakes
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæm.blɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡæm.blɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of playing games of chance for money or other valuable stakes, hoping to win a prize.

Examples

Casino gambling
He lost a lot of money through gambling at the casino.
She decided to quit gambling after realizing its negative impact on her life.
The government is trying to regulate online gambling.

  • synonym: gaming - the activity of playing games of chance for money
