player

noun
person who takes part in a game or sport
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in or is skilled at a sport, game, or other competitive activity

Examples

Soccer player
Every player must follow the rules during the match.
She is the youngest player on the basketball team.
The player scored a winning goal in the last minute.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitor - a person who takes part in a competition
  • antonym: spectator - a person who watches an event rather than taking part
