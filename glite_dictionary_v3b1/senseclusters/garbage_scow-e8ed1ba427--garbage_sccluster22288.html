garbage scow

noun
boat for transporting waste
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ skaʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ skaʊ / (BrE)
a boat or barge used for transporting waste or refuse, especially in harbors or rivers

Examples

A garbage scow boat
After the storm, a garbage scow was needed to remove the floating trash from the river.
From his apartment window, he could see the garbage scow making its rounds every morning.
The city contracted a garbage scow to clean debris from the harbor.
