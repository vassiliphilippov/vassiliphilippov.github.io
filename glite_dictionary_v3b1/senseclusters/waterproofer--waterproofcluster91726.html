waterproofer

noun
makes waterproof
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌprufər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːfə / (BrE)
a person or substance that makes something resistant to water

Examples

A waterproofer spray
Before the rainy season, I always apply a waterproofer to my leather shoes.
The waterproofer recommended a special spray for outdoor jackets.
This waterproofer works well on tents and backpacks to keep them dry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repellent - a substance that drives away or resists something, such as water
  • synonym: sealant - a substance used to block the passage of fluids
