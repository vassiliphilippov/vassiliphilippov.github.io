sweet

adjective
pleasing to the ear (sound/music)
1.0
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
(of sound, music, or voice) pleasing to the ear; having a pleasant, melodious quality.

Examples

Sweet singing voice
A sweet melody floated through the open window on the spring breeze.
The singer’s sweet voice drew in the entire audience.
The violin’s sweet tone complemented the gentle piano accompaniment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harsh - unpleasant or jarring to the senses, especially the ear
  • synonym: melodious - having a pleasant musical sound
