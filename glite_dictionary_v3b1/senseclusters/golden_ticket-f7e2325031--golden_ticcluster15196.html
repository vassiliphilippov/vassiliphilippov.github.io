golden ticket

noun
forged Kerberos TGT used in attacks
9.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0261o\u028ald\u0259n \u02c8t\u026ak\u026at / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0261\u0259\u028ild\u0259\u0303 \u02c8t\u026ak\u026at / (BrE)
(in cybersecurity) a forged Kerberos Ticket Granting Ticket (TGT) created by an attacker that allows impersonation of arbitrary users and widespread access to a Windows domain or network resources.

Examples

A hacker's golden ticket
The security team discovered a stolen golden ticket that allowed attackers full control of the domain.
After the breach they found several golden tickets created from a compromised domain controller image.
To prevent golden ticket attacks, admins regularly reset the KRBTGT account password and monitor tickets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forged Ticket Granting Ticket (TGT) - a fake Kerberos TGT created by an attacker to request service tickets and impersonate users
