Examples

To disconfirm a theory
The scientist tried to disconfirm the results by repeating the experiment with new methods.
Additional data may disconfirm the initial assumptions made by the research team.
If the facts disconfirm your theory, you should consider revising your hypothesis.
The scientist's findings disconfirm the widely accepted theory.

Synonyms

  • refute
    6.2 6.51/m
    refute proves an argument wrong with clear reasons or facts, while disconfirm points to missing supporting evidence.
  • confute
    9.9 0.07/m
    confute uses strong argument or evidence to show a claim false, while disconfirm emphasizes lack of supporting proof.
  • debunk
    5.7 4.02/m
    debunk exposes myths or false ideas publicly with proof, while disconfirm simply shows a claim lacks supporting evidence.
  • rebut
    7.0 1.23/m
    rebut gives counter-arguments to weaken a claim, while disconfirm shows the claim lacks evidence or is untrue.
  • contradict
    3.7 12/m

  • discredit
    5.6 1.01/m
    discredit attacks a source or idea to make it seem untrustworthy, while disconfirm focuses on showing lack of supporting evidence.
