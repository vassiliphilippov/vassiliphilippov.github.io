white rocket

noun
a Mediterranean plant with white flowers
7.1
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈrɑkɪt / (AmE), / waɪt ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant of the cabbage family (Diplotaxis erucoides), naturalized as a weed throughout southern Europe, with white flowers and deeply cut leaves.

Examples

The white rocket plant
Gardeners often mistake white rocket for other members of the cabbage family.
