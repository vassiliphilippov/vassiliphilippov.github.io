Cathar

adjective
of the Cathar sect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæθɑr / (AmE), / ˈkæθɑː / (BrE)
relating to the Cathars, a medieval Christian sect known for their dualist beliefs and opposition to the Catholic Church
