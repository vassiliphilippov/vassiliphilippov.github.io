cybersecurity

noun
protection from digital attacks
4.1
Pronunciation: /saɪ.bər.sɪ.ˈkjʊr.ə.ti/ (AmE), /saɪ.bə.sɪ.ˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti/ (BrE)
the practice or field of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, or damage

Examples

Strong cybersecurity measures
Strong cybersecurity measures are essential for protecting personal information online.
The company hired experts to improve its cybersecurity after a recent hacking attempt.
Governments around the world are investing more in cybersecurity to defend against cyber threats.
Many companies invest heavily in cybersecurity to prevent hackers from stealing sensitive information.
A career in cybersecurity requires constant learning to keep up with new threats.
The government announced new cybersecurity measures to protect national infrastructure.
The company invested heavily in cybersecurity to protect its customers' personal information.
Good cybersecurity practices include using strong passwords and updating software regularly.
After the attack, the government reviewed its cybersecurity policies to prevent future incidents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data protection - measures taken to safeguard personal or sensitive data
  • synonym: information security - the protection of information from unauthorized access or alteration
