cybersecurity

noun
protection from digital attacks
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪbərsɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˌsaɪbəsɪˈkjʊərɪti /; / ˌsaɪbəsɪˈkjʊərəti / (BrE)
the practice or field of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, or damage
