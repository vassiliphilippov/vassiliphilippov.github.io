acknowledgment

noun
electronic confirmation of receipt
3.9 0.22/m

(in computing and telecommunications) an automatic or explicit confirmation that a message, packet, or transmission has been received or delivered, often produced by a device or protocol.

Examples

Automatic acknowledgment message
The server sent an automatic acknowledgment as soon as it finished receiving the upload.
Please keep the email acknowledgment you received; it proves the message was delivered.
Network protocols often require an ACK acknowledgment to confirm each packet arrived correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ack (networking) - a short protocol message sent to confirm successful receipt of data
  • synonym: receipt (electronic) - a confirmation that an electronic message or payment was received
