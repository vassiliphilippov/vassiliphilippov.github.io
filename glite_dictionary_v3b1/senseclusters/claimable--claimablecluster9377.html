claimable

adjective
eligible to be claimed
Pronunciation: / ˈkleɪməbəl / (AmE), / ˈkleɪməbəl / (BrE)
able to be claimed or eligible to be requested as a right, benefit, or payment

Examples

Claimable refund
The refund is only claimable within thirty days of purchase.
Bonuses are claimable by employees who meet the annual targets.
The lost luggage compensation became claimable after the airline confirmed the delay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eligible - having the right to do or receive something
  • antonym: unclaimable - not able to be claimed or requested
