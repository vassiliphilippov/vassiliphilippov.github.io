family Palmae

noun
the palm family of plants
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˈpælmi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈpɑːlmiː / (BrE)
A large family of chiefly tropical trees, shrubs, and vines, usually with tall columnar trunks and a crown of very large leaves; also known as the palm family.
