horseback

noun
a narrow ridge of hills
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsbæk / (BrE)
A narrow ridge or crest of hills resembling the back of a horse.

Examples

A mountain horseback
A narrow horseback separated the two valleys below.
Clouds gathered above the horseback, casting shadows on the land.
The hikers followed the horseback until they reached the summit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ridge - a long, narrow elevated landform
