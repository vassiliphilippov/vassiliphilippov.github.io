cookshop

noun
large kitchen
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈkʊkʃɒp / (BrE)
a room or building where food is cooked, especially a large kitchen or one serving an institution or group

Examples

Large cookshop kitchen
The old manor had a spacious cookshop where meals for the entire staff were prepared.
During the festival, volunteers worked together in the cookshop to feed hundreds of visitors.
The school's cookshop was renovated to provide better meals for the students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cafeteria - a place where people get food, often in an institution
  • synonym: kitchen - a room where food is prepared and cooked
