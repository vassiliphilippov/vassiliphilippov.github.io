Australian terrier

noun
a small, wiry-haired Australian dog breed
Pronunciation: / əˈstreɪljən ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a small, hardy dog breed with a shaggy wire-haired coat, originating from Australia, similar to the cairn terrier

Examples

An Australian terrier dog
She entered her Australian terrier in the national dog show last weekend.
The Australian terrier is known for its lively personality and loyalty to its owners.
With its distinctive wiry coat, the Australian terrier is easy to recognize among other small breeds.
