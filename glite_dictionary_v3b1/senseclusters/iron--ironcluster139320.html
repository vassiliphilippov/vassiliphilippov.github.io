iron

adjective
extremely strong or robust (figurative)
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈaɪən / (BrE)
(of a quality) extremely strong, robust, or unyielding; not easily broken or weakened.

Examples

Iron will
Despite his age, he had an iron constitution and rarely fell ill.
Her iron willpower helped her overcome every obstacle.
It takes an iron resolve to complete such a demanding challenge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged; delicate
  • synonym: robust - strong and healthy; sturdy
