showboater

noun
attention-seeker
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌboʊtər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌbəʊtə / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a flashy or attention-seeking way, especially to impress others

Examples

A flashy showboater
The coach warned the showboater to focus on teamwork instead of showing off.
Fans criticized the showboater for celebrating every goal with an elaborate dance.
No one likes a showboater who always tries to steal the spotlight during meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not seek attention or show off
  • synonym: show-off - a person who tries to attract attention by displaying their abilities or possessions
