unrealistically

adverb
in an unrealistic manner
4.3 3.8
Pronunciation: /ən.riː.əl.ˈɪs.tɪk.liː/ (AmE), /ən.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
In a way that does not reflect what is practical or achievable; in an unrealistic manner.

Examples

Set unrealistically high goals
If you set your goals unrealistically high, you may end up feeling disappointed.
She unrealistically believed she would finish the marathon without any training.
The characters in the movie behaved unrealistically, making the plot hard to believe.
