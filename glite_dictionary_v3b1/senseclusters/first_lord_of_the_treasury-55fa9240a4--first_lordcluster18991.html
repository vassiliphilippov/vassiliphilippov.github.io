First Lord of the Treasury

noun
British cabinet minister overseeing the Treasury
7.7
Pronunciation: /fɜːrst lɔːrd əv ðə ˈtreʒ.ə.riː/ (AmE), /fɜːst lɔːd əv ðə ˈtreʒ.ər.iː/ (BrE)
