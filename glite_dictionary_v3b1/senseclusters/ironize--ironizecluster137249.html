ironize

verb
to act ironically
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaɪərənaɪz / (BrE)
to speak or act in an ironic or mocking manner

Examples

To ironize a comment
She tends to ironize when discussing topics she finds unimportant.
During the debate, he would often ironize instead of giving direct answers.
It is not always helpful to ironize serious issues in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mock - to make fun of or ridicule
  • antonym: sincerely - in a genuine or honest way
