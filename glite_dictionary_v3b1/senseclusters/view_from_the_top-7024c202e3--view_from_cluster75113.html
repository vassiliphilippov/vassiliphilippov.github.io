view from the top

noun
perspective from a high or advantaged position
Pronunciation: / vjuː frəm ðə tɑp / (AmE), / vjuː frəm ðə tɒp / (BrE)
a perspective or outlook from a high physical location or from a position of advantage

Examples

The view from the top
After years of hard work, he finally experienced the view from the top in his company.
From the view from the top, the city looked peaceful and orderly.
The view from the top of the mountain made the strenuous climb worthwhile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bird's-eye view - a view of something from above
