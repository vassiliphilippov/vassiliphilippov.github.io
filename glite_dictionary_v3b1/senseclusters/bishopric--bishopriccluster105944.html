bishopric

noun
bishop's area of jurisdiction
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪʃəprɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɪʃəprɪk / (BrE)
The district or area under the authority of a bishop; the territorial jurisdiction of a bishop.

Examples

The bishopric area
He was appointed to oversee the affairs of the bishopric after years of dedicated service.
The boundaries of the bishopric have changed several times throughout history.
The new bishopric was established to better serve the growing population in the region.
