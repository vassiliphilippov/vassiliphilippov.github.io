bogeyman

noun
imaginary monster used to scare children
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡiˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡimæn / (BrE)
an imaginary creature used to frighten children, often invoked by adults as a threat for misbehavior

Examples

The bogeyman under bed
Growing up, I was terrified of the bogeyman lurking under my bed.
Many parents told their children that the bogeyman would come if they misbehaved.
The old movie featured a mysterious bogeyman who haunted the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boogeyman - an alternative spelling for 'bogeyman'
  • synonym: bugbear - a cause of obsessive fear, irritation, or loathing; also an imaginary being invoked to frighten children
