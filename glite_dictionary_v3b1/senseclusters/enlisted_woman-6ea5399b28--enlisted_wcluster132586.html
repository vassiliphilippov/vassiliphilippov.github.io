enlisted woman

noun
female enlisted military member
1.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈlɪstɪd ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪstɪd ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who serves as an enlisted member of the armed forces, not as an officer.

Examples

An enlisted woman soldier
As an enlisted woman, she completed rigorous training alongside her male counterparts.
Benefits and opportunities for an enlisted woman have improved in recent years.
The army saw an increase in the number of enlisted women during the last decade.
