envelopment

noun
the act of enclosing or surrounding
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the act of enclosing or surrounding something completely

Examples

Thick envelopment of fog
Fog descended in a thick envelopment, making the streetlights barely visible.
The envelopment of the fragile object in layers of foam ensured its safe delivery.
The army's rapid envelopment of the city surprised the enemy forces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosure - the act of enclosing something
  • antonym: exposure - the act of leaving something unprotected
