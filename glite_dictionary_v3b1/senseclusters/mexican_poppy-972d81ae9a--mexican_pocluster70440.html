Mexican poppy

noun
annual herb with yellow flowers and prickly stems
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪkən ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
an annual herb with prickly stems and large yellow flowers, native to the southern United States, West Indies, and Mexico

Examples

The Mexican poppy flower
