outline

noun
general plan or summary
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.laɪn / (BrE)
a general description or summary giving the main points of something

Examples

A project outline
Could you prepare an outline of the project before the meeting?
The author presented an outline of her new book to the publisher.
We started with a rough outline before writing the full report.
Before writing the essay, I created an outline of the main ideas I wanted to cover.
His presentation followed the outline he had given to the class the day before.
The teacher asked us to submit an outline of our research paper topics by Friday.

  • synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
