co-director

noun
joint director
3.7
Pronunciation: /koʊ.də.ˈrek.tər/ /; / /koʊ.dɪ.ˈrek.tər/ (AmE), /kəʊ.də.ˈrek.tə/ /; / /kəʊ.dɪ.ˈrek.tə/ (BrE)

Examples

As co-director, she worked closely with her colleague to lead the project.
