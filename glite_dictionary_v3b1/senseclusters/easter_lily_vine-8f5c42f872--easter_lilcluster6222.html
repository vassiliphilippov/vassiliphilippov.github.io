Easter lily vine

noun
evergreen climber with white trumpet flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstər ˈlɪli vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈiːstə ˈlɪli vaɪn / (BrE)
An evergreen climbing plant with large glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white, trumpet-shaped flowers.

Examples

The Easter lily vine flowers
In spring, the Easter lily vine attracts bees with its abundant, sweet-scented blossoms.
She planted an Easter lily vine next to the fence for its beautiful, glossy leaves and trumpet blooms.
The Easter lily vine climbed the trellis, filling the garden with its fragrant white flowers.
