olive-colored

adjective
yellowish-green color like olives
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.lɪv ˌkʌl.ɚd /; / ˈɑːlɪv ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈɒl.ɪv ˌkʌl.əd /; / ˈɒlɪv ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
