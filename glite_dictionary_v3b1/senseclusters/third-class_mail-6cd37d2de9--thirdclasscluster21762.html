third-class mail

noun
mail category for printed matter at reduced rates
Pronunciation: / ˌθɝd ˌklæs ˈmeɪl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd ˌklɑːs ˈmeɪl / (BrE)
a category of mail consisting of printed matter that is eligible for reduced postal rates

Examples

Third-class mail delivery
Because it is sent as third-class mail, delivery may take several days longer than first-class.
Many charities use third-class mail to send out newsletters to their supporters.
The company shipped the promotional materials by third-class mail to save on postage costs.
