Equus kiang

noun
wild ass of Tibet and Mongolia
Pronunciation: / ˈekwəs kiˈaŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛkwəs kiˈaŋ / (BrE)
a species of wild ass native to the plains of Tibet and Mongolia

Examples

Equus kiang grazing
Researchers observed several Equus kiang grazing near a mountain stream.
The Equus kiang is well adapted to the harsh climate of the Tibetan Plateau.
Unlike domestic donkeys, the Equus kiang remains elusive in the wild.

  • synonym: wild ass - general term for any wild member of the genus Equus
