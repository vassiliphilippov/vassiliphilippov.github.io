claybank

noun
clay deposit or tan horse
Pronunciation: / ˈkleɪˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈkleɪˌbæŋk / (BrE)
a bank or deposit of clay, or a horse with a yellowish-tan coat

Examples

A river claybank
The river cut through a high claybank on its southern side.
They discovered fossils embedded in the old claybank near the farm.
Her favorite horse was a gentle claybank with a soft, tan coat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bank - the side of a river or a raised area of ground
  • synonym: clay - a natural earthy material used for making bricks and pottery
  • degree higher: palomino - a horse with a golden coat and white mane and tail
