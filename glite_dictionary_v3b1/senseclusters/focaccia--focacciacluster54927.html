focaccia

noun
Italian flatbread
3.5
Pronunciation: / foʊˈkɑːtʃə / (AmE), / fəˈkætʃə / (BrE)
a flat, soft Italian bread often baked with olive oil and herbs on top

Examples

A focaccia bread
We ordered a plate of focaccia with rosemary and sea salt as a starter.
The bakery sells fresh focaccia every morning, still warm from the oven.
She learned to bake focaccia during her trip to Italy last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ciabatta - an Italian white bread with a crisp crust and open texture
  • synonym: pizza - an Italian dish of flatbread with toppings such as cheese and tomato
