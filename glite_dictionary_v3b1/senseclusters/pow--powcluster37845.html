pow

noun
sound of impact
Pronunciation: / paʊ / (AmE), / paʊ / (BrE)
a word used to imitate the sound of a loud hit, explosion, or gunshot

Examples

Comic pow
The comic book showed the superhero punching the villain with a big pow.
We heard a sudden pow as the tire burst on the highway.
The fireworks ended with a final pow that echoed across the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise, especially from an impact or explosion
  • synonym: boom - a deep, loud, resonant sound, often from an explosion
