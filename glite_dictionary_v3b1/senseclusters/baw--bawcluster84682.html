baw

verb
to shout loudly
9.8
to shout or call out loudly, often to attract attention or be heard over a distance

Examples

To baw for help
The coach had to baw instructions across the noisy gym.
She bawed for help when she saw the fire in the kitchen.
During the parade, people would baw to get the attention of their friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bellow - to shout in a deep, loud voice
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very quietly
  • synonym: yell - to shout loudly, usually because you are angry, excited, or in pain
