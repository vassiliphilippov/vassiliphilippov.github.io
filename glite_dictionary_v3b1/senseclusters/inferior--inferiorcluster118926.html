inferior

adjective
situated lower than a reference point
1.3 4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪərɪə / (BrE)
(in scientific and anatomical contexts) situated lower down or further from a given reference point, especially in the body or a structure.

Examples

Inferior part of body
In anatomical diagrams, structures labeled as inferior are located below others.
The inferior alveolar artery supplies blood to the lower jaw and teeth.
The mouth is inferior to the nose in standard anatomical position.
