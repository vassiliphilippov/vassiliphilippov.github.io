lak

noun
insect for shellac
9.9
Pronunciation: / læk / (AmE), / læk / (BrE)
a small scale insect whose resinous secretion is used to produce shellac and natural dyes

Examples

Lak insect collection
The villagers collected lak from the trees to sell at the local market.
Traditional artisans use resin from lak to make natural red dyes for fabrics.
Harvesting lak is an important source of income in some rural communities.

Related Terms
