groak

verb
stare longingly (esp. at food)
3.4 9.7
Pronunciation: /groʊk/ (AmE), /grəʊk/ (BrE)
To look at or stare at someone longingly, especially while they are eating in the hope of being given food.

Examples

To groak at food
She couldn't help but groak as her friend enjoyed the delicious dessert.
The child sat quietly, groaking at his father's plate until he was offered a bite.
The dog would always groak at the table, hoping for a scrap of food.
