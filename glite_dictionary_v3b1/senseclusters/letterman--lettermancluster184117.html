letterman

noun
student athlete awarded varsity letter
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtɚmən / (AmE), / ˈlɛtəmən / (BrE)
a student who has earned a varsity letter in a sport at their school or college, signifying a significant contribution to the team

Examples

A letterman athlete
As a senior, he was proud to be named a letterman in both football and baseball.
Earning the title of letterman requires dedication and consistent athletic performance throughout the season.
She wore her letterman jacket to every game to show her team spirit.
