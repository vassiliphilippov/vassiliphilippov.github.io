florin

noun
former Dutch currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrɪn / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪn / (BrE)
the former basic monetary unit of the Netherlands, equal to 100 cents, replaced by the euro in 2002

Examples

Dutch florin coin
My grandfather still keeps a collection of old Dutch florin coins.
The Netherlands replaced the florin with the euro in 2002.
Travelers needed to exchange their dollars for florins when visiting Amsterdam in the 1990s.

Related Terms
