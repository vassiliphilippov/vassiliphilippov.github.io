noter

noun
music transcriber
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtə / (BrE)
a person who writes down or transcribes music into musical notation

Examples

Music noter
The band hired a noter to transcribe the improvised solo into sheet music for the rest.
As a skilled noter, she could quickly write down complex melodies by ear.
The orchestra relied on their noter to prepare accurate scores for every rehearsal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composer - a person who writes music
  • synonym: transcriber - a person who writes down spoken or performed material
