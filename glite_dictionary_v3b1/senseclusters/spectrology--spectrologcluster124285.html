spectrology

noun
study of ghosts
9.9
Pronunciation: / spɛkˈtrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / spɛkˈtrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of ghosts, apparitions, or other spectral phenomena, especially as a field of inquiry in the past

Examples

Spectrology of ghosts
In the nineteenth century, spectrology was sometimes considered a serious academic pursuit.
Books on spectrology often combined scientific language with supernatural claims.
Interest in spectrology declined as scientific understanding of the world increased.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parapsychology - the study of paranormal phenomena, such as telepathy and ghosts
  • synonym: spiritualism - belief in communication with spirits of the dead
