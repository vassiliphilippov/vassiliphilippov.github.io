fluke

noun
flat projection on anchor arm
6.6
Pronunciation: / fluːk / (AmE), / fluːk / (BrE)
The flat, bladelike part on the arm of an anchor that digs into the ground to hold the anchor in place.

Examples

Anchor fluke
Barnacles had collected on the fluke during the long voyage.
Inspect the fluke for damage before lowering the anchor.
The fluke of the anchor caught firmly in the sandy seabed.
