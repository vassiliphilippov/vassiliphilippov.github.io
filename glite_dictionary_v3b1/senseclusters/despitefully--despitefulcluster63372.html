despitefully

adverb
maliciously or spitefully
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspaɪtfəli / (AmE), / dɪˈspaɪtfəli / (BrE)
In a malicious or spiteful manner; with the intention to hurt or offend.

Examples

Act despitefully towards
He accused his enemies of acting despitefully towards him.
The king was despitefully mocked by his rivals in the court.
They treated her despitefully even though she had done nothing wrong.
