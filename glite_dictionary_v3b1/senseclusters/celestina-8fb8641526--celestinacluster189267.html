Celestina

proper noun
Spanish literary character
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛlɛsˈtiːnə / (AmE), / sɛlɛsˈtiːnə / (BrE)
the title and central character of a late-15th-century Spanish play by Fernando de Rojas, known as a cunning procuress
