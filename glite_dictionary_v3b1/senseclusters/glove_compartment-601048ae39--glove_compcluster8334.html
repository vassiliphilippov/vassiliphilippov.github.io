glove compartment

noun
dashboard storage compartment in a car
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌv kəmˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ɡlʌv kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a small storage compartment in the dashboard of a car, typically used for storing gloves, documents, and other small items

Examples
