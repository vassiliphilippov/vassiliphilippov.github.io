talk

verb
give a lecture or presentation
Pronunciation: / tɑk / (AmE), / tɔːk / (BrE)
to give a lecture or formal presentation on a particular subject, especially to an audience

Examples

To talk about science
Dr. Kim will talk about climate change at the university next Monday.
Each expert was invited to talk on their area of research.
She often talks at medical conferences around the world.
