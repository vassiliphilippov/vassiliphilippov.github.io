well-known

adjective
famous or widely recognized
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛlˈnoʊn /; / wɛl ˈnoʊn / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈnəʊn /; / wɛl ˈnəʊn / (BrE)
known or recognized by many people; familiar to a large number of people
