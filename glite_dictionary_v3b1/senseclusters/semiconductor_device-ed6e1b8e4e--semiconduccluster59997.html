semiconductor device

noun
electronic component made from semiconducting material
Pronunciation: / sɛmɪkənˈdʌktɚ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / sɛmɪkənˈdʌktə dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
An electronic component made from a material that has electrical conductivity between that of a conductor and an insulator, typically used in circuits.

Examples

A semiconductor device circuit
Every smartphone contains dozens of semiconductor devices like transistors and diodes.
Manufacturing a high-quality semiconductor device requires sophisticated technology and extremely clean environments.
The invention of the semiconductor device revolutionized modern electronics and communication technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diode - an electronic component allowing current to flow in one direction only
  • synonym: transistor - a semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electronic signals
