Synonyms

  • all in all
    3.2 5.07/m
    all in all is a casual speaker's phrase that sums up points quickly, while all things considered sounds slightly more formal and measured.
  • altogether
    3.0 13/m
    altogether means when everything is considered or in total and feels more neutral, while all things considered focuses on weighing each aspect.
  • in the final analysis
    4.1 1.01/m
    in the final analysis sounds formal and used in arguments to state a conclusion, while all things considered is more general and conversational.
