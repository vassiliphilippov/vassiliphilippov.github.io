precisionist

noun
person valuing accuracy
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪʒənɪst / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who insists on strict exactness and careful accuracy in details or performance

Examples

A careful precisionist
As a true precisionist, she checked every measurement twice before approving the drawing.
The team relied on a precisionist to ensure all calculations were flawless.
Being a precisionist helped him excel in fields that demand careful attention to detail.
