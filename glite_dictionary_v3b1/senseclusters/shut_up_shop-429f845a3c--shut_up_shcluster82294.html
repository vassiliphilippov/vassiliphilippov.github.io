shut up shop

verb
close a business for the day
Pronunciation: / ʃʌt ʌp ʃɑp / (AmE), / ʃʌt ʌp ʃɒp / (BrE)
to close a business for the day or stop trading, temporarily or permanently

Examples

To shut up shop early
After working late, she finally shut up shop and went home.
Because of the national holiday, they'll shut up shop earlier than usual today.
The café always shuts up shop at six o'clock on weekdays.
