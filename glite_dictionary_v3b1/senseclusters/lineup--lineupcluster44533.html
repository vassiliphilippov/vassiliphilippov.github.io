lineup

noun
order of batters in baseball
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnʌp / (BrE)
(Baseball) a list of batters on a team, arranged in the order in which they will bat during a game.

Examples

Baseball lineup
After checking the lineup, it was clear that the rookie would be batting third.
Each team submitted its lineup to the umpire before the tournament started.
The coach changed the lineup before the big game to improve their chances of winning.
