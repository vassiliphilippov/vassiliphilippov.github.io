substitution

noun
the act of replacing something or someone
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbstɪˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of replacing one thing or person with another

Examples

A substitution in sports
His sudden substitution surprised everyone at the meeting.
The team made a substitution in the second half of the match.
Vegetarian dishes are offered as a substitution for meat options.
The substitution of plastic for glass bottles has reduced shipping costs.
The coach made a substitution at halftime to strengthen the team's defense.
Vegetarian recipes often use tofu as a substitution for meat.
