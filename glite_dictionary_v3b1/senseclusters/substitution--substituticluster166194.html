Synonyms

  • replacement
    2.3 9.12/m
    replacement is the common, everyday act of putting a new item or person in place, while substitution sounds more formal.
  • commutation
    7.4 0.02/m
    commutation is a formal term for swapping one person or thing, while substitution is the everyday word for replacing.
  • switch
    3.2 9.62/m
    switch is a quick or informal change of one thing or person for another, while substitution is the act of replacing.
  • transposition
    7.3 0.11/m
    transposition is changing the order or positions by swapping items, while substitution concentrates on replacing one thing with another.
  • changeover
    4.1 0.67/m
    changeover is the process of moving from one system or activity to another, while substitution usually means one-to-one replacement.
