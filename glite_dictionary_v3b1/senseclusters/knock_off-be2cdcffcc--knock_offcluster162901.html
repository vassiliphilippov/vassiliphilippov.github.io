knock off

verb
kill or murder (often in a criminal context)
9.9 0.30/m
Pronunciation: / nÉ‘k É‘f / (AmE), / nÉ’k É’f / (BrE)
(informal, slang) to kill someone, especially as part of criminal activity or to remove a threat.

Examples

The gang planned to knock off the rival boss before the end of the week.
