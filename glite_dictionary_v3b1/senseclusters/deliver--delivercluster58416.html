deliver

verb
to throw the ball to the batter in baseball
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə / (BrE)
(of a pitcher) to throw or hurl the ball toward the batter in baseball.

Examples

To deliver a pitch

The pitcher delivered a fastball right down the middle.
