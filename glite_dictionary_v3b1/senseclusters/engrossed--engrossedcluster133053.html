engrossed

adjective
completely absorbed; giving full attention
2.6 3.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈɡroʊst/ /; / /ɛn.ˈɡroʊst/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈɡrəʊst/ /; / /ɛn.ˈɡrəʊst/ (BrE)
so interested or involved in something that you give it all your attention

Examples

Engrossed in a book
He became engrossed in his work and lost track of time.
She was so engrossed in her book that she didn't hear the phone ring.
The children sat engrossed in the movie, their eyes glued to the screen.
