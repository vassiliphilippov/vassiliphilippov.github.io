Synonyms

  • underbrush
    5.6 1/m
    underbrush is low growing plants and bushes beneath taller trees, while undergrowth names the whole dense growth under the canopy.
  • underwood
    8.0 1/m
    underwood is the low trees and bushes that grow under taller trees, while undergrowth describes that same dense plant layer.
  • understory
    5.1 0.7/m
    understory is the named forest layer of small trees and shrubs, while undergrowth refers to the actual dense plants in that layer.
  • brush
    2.2 8/m
    brush is a close group of bushes or shrubs in many places, while undergrowth means the dense plants found beneath taller trees.
  • thicket
    5.1 3/m
    thicket is a very dense group of bushes or small trees often standing alone, while undergrowth grows beneath taller trees.
