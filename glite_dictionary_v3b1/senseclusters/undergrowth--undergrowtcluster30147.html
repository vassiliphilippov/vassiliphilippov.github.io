undergrowth

noun
dense vegetation beneath trees
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
a dense growth of shrubs, small trees, and other plants beneath the taller trees in a wood or forest

Examples

Dense undergrowth
Foxes often hide in the undergrowth to avoid predators and rest during the day.
Much of the undergrowth was cleared to prevent wildfires from spreading rapidly.
The hikers struggled to make their way through the thick undergrowth of the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underbrush - shrubs and small trees beneath taller trees
