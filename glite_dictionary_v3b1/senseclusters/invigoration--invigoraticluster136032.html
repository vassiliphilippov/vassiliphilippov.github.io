invigoration

noun
the act of making something more lively or energetic
7.7 4.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.vɪ.gə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.vɪ.gə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or feeling of becoming more lively, energetic, or full of vitality
