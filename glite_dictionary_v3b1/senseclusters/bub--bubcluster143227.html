bub

noun
informal address for man
Pronunciation: / bʌb / (AmE), / bʌb / (BrE)
an informal and friendly way to address a boy or man, often used in casual conversation

Examples

Hey bub
Hey bub, could you give me a hand with this box?
Listen, bub, you need to be more careful next time.
The coach called out, "Great job, bub!" after the player scored.

  • synonym: pal - an informal word for a friend
