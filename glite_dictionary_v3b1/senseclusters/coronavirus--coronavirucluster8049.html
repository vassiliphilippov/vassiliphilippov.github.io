coronavirus

noun
a virus causing respiratory illnesses
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊ.nəˌvaɪ.rəs / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊ.nəˌvaɪ.rəs / (BrE)
A type of virus that can cause respiratory illnesses in humans and animals, including the disease COVID-19.

Examples

The coronavirus pandemic
Many countries took strict measures to slow the spread of the coronavirus.
Scientists quickly developed vaccines to combat the new coronavirus.
The coronavirus pandemic had a profound impact on global travel and economies.
