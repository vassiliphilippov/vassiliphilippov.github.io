hireable

adjective
available for rent
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərəbl / (BrE)
able to be hired or rented for use, especially referring to goods, vehicles, or services.

Examples

Hireable bikes
The bicycles are hireable for a small fee at the train station.
Is this equipment hireable for private events or only for company use?
Many boats on the river are hireable by the hour during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rentable - able to be rented or leased
  • antonym: unavailable - not able to be used or obtained
