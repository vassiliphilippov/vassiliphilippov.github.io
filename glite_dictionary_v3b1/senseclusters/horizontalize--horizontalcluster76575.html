horizontalize

verb
make horizontal in format or orientation
Pronunciation: / hɔːˌraɪzəntəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / hɒˌrɪzəntəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or convert (something, such as data, a chart, or a layout) into a horizontal format or orientation

Examples

To horizontalize a layout
By horizontalizing the layout, we can fit more columns on the screen.
She suggested we horizontalize the data to improve the report's appearance.
To make the information easier to compare, we decided to horizontalize the chart.
