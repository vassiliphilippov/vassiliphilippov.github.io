goldentop

noun
yellow-capped mushroom
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldənˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldənˌtɒp / (BrE)

Examples

Goldentop mushroom

Some people collect goldentop mushrooms for their distinctive appearance and properties.
It is important to correctly identify a goldentop to avoid confusing it with toxic species.
