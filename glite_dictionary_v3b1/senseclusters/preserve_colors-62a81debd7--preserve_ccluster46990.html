preserve colors

verb
maintain original colors (esp. in fabrics)
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝv ˈkʌlɚz / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːv ˈkʌləz / (BrE)
to maintain or prevent fading of the original colors, especially in fabrics or materials

Examples

To preserve colors in fabric
Curtains exposed to sunlight may require special care to preserve colors.
It is recommended to wash dark clothing separately to preserve colors.
Using a gentle detergent will help preserve colors in your favorite shirts.
