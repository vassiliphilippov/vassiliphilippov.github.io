Synonyms

  • interim
    5.4 1/m
    interim is a formal word for a temporary period used in plans or offices, while meantime is the general period between events.
  • in the meantime
    2.8 30/m
    in the meantime is a phrase people use when waiting for something to happen, while meantime names the period itself between events.
  • meanwhile
    3.2 0.2/m
    meanwhile links two events that happen at the same time and often joins sentences, while meantime names the period between events.
