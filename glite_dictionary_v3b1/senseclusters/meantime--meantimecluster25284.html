meantime

noun
the interval between two events
2.5 24/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀånta…™m / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀênta…™m / (BrE)
the period of time between two events or actions

Examples

A long meantime

Problems were being solved, but in the meantime, things were quite chaotic.
You can watch TV in the meantime while I finish cooking.

Synonyms

    5.4 2.10/m

  • in the meantime
    2.8 22/m

  • meanwhile
    3.2 0.14/m
    meanwhile links two events that happen at the same time and often joins sentences, while meantime names the period between events.
