infliction

noun
act of imposing something unwelcome
3.2 7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɪkʃən /; / ɪnˈflɪk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɪkʃən /; / ɪnˈflɪk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of causing or imposing something unpleasant, such as pain, punishment, or suffering

Examples
