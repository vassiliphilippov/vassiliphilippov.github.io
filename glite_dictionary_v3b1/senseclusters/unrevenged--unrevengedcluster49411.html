unrevenged

adjective
not avenged
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈvɛndʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈvɛndʒd / (BrE)

Examples

He could not rest knowing the crime was still unrevenged after all this time.
