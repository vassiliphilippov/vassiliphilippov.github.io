unconversant

adjective
awkward in conversation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɜrsənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɜːsənt / (BrE)
not skilled at or comfortable in informal conversation; awkward or reluctant to take part in social talk

Examples

Unconversant at parties
She was unconversant at the party and found it hard to join the group discussions.
Because he was unconversant with casual chat, he often felt left out during breaks at work.
His unconversant manner made social gatherings challenging for him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conversant - skilled or comfortable in conversation
  • synonym: taciturn - reserved or uncommunicative in speech
