conjuring trick

noun
magic trick to amuse or astonish
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.dʒɚ.ɪŋ trɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dʒə.rɪŋ trɪk / (BrE)
a trick performed by a magician that appears to defy natural laws, intended to amuse or astonish an audience

Examples

A conjuring trick show
Even though she knew it was just a conjuring trick, she was still impressed by the illusion.
He revealed the secret behind the classic conjuring trick that made the rabbit disappear.
The magician amazed the children with a simple conjuring trick involving a coin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magic trick - an illusion or feat performed by a magician
