unpleasing

adjective
unpleasant; disagreeable to the senses
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpliːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈpliːzɪŋ / (BrE)
Not giving pleasure; unpleasant or disagreeable to the senses.

Examples

An unpleasing color
His choice of colors was slightly unpleasing to most of the audience.
She found the bitter taste of the medicine rather unpleasing.
The harsh lighting gave the room an unpleasing atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pleasing - giving pleasure or satisfaction
  • synonym: unpleasant - not pleasant; causing discomfort or dislike
