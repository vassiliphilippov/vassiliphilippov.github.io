Hottentot bread vine

noun
South African plant with massive rootstock
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːtənˌtɑt brɛd vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɒt.ən.tɒt brɛd vaɪn / (BrE)
A South African climbing plant with a large, deeply fissured rootstock, known scientifically as Dioscorea elephantipes.
