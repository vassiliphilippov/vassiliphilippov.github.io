charley horse

noun
painful muscle cramp, esp. after exercise
7.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑːr.liː hɔːrs/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɑː.liː hɔːs/ (BrE)
a sudden, painful muscle cramp or stiffness, especially in the thigh or calf, often after exercise

Examples

A charley horse cramp
After the game, he was limping from a charley horse that wouldn't go away.

Stretching before exercise can help prevent a painful charley horse.

He stopped playing basketball because a charley horse made it hard to walk.
Waking up with a charley horse in the middle of the night can be very painful.
