PET

noun
medical scan using positron emission tomography
5.8 3/m
Pronunciation: / pi Àài ti / (AmE), / piÀê ÀàiÀê tiÀê / (BrE)
a medical imaging technique that uses positron emission tomography to examine metabolic activity in tissues, especially in the brain

Examples

PET scan
A PET scan can help identify cancerous tissues by detecting metabolic changes.
Advances in PET imaging have improved the early diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease.
The doctor recommended a PET scan to assess the patient's brain function.

    6.0 0.1/m
