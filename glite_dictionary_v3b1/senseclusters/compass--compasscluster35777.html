compass

noun
drawing instrument for circles/arcs
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌm.pəs/ (AmE), /ˈkʌm.pəs/ (BrE)
A drawing instrument with two arms, one with a point and the other with a pencil or pen, used for drawing circles or arcs and measuring distances on maps.

Examples

A compass draws circles
Make sure the point of the compass stays in one place while you rotate it.
She adjusted the width of her compass before drawing the arc.
The student used a compass to draw a perfect circle on the paper.
