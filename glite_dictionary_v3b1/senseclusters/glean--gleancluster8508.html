glean

verb
gather leftovers from harvest
Pronunciation: / ɡlin /; / ɡliːn / (AmE), / ɡliːn / (BrE)
to gather leftover crops or produce from a field after the main harvest

She spent the afternoon gleaning corn left behind by the machines.
