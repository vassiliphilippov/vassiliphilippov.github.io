mongoose

noun
small carnivorous mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnˌɡus / (AmE), / ˈmɒnˌɡuːs / (BrE)
A small, agile, carnivorous mammal found in Africa and southern Asia, known for its ability to prey on snakes and rodents.

Examples

A mongoose hunts snakes
Farmers often welcome the presence of a mongoose because it helps control the rodent population.
The mongoose is famous for its quick reflexes and ability to kill venomous snakes.
We saw a mongoose dart across the path during our safari in Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meerkat - a small southern African mongoose with a slender body and pointed face
  • synonym: weasel - small carnivorous mammal resembling a mongoose
