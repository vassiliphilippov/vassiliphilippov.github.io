retailer

noun
person or business selling goods to the public
2.4 19/m

A person or business that sells goods to the public, usually in small quantities for personal or household consumption.

Examples

An online retailer
Many small businesses struggle to compete with online retailers.
The large retailer offers discounts on electronics during the holiday season.
The local retailer stocks organic fruits and vegetables throughout the year.

Synonyms

  • retail merchant
    2.8 0.01/m
    retail merchant is used in formal business or legal writing for sellers who sell small quantities to the public, while retailer is common everyday term.
  • shopkeeper
    1.9 3/m
    shopkeeper runs or owns a small local shop and deals with customers in person, while retailer covers any business selling to the public.
  • storekeeper
    2.7 0.4/m
