litchi nut

noun
Chinese fruit with sweet pulp and a single seed
5.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.tʃiː nʌt/ (AmE), /ˈliː.tʃiː nʌt/ (BrE)
A small, round Chinese fruit with a thin brittle shell, sweet jellylike pulp, and a single seed, often eaten fresh or dried.
