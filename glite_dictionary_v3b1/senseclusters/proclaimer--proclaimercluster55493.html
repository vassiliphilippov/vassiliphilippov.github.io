proclaimer

noun
public announcer
5.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkleɪmər / (AmE), / prəˈkleɪmə / (BrE)
a person who publicly announces, preaches, or spreads beliefs, especially religious ones

Examples

Religious proclaimer
The proclaimer stood in the town square, sharing his message with anyone who would listen.
As a dedicated proclaimer, she traveled to many countries to spread her beliefs.
The community welcomed the visiting proclaimer who spoke about hope and unity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evangelist - a person who seeks to convert others to the Christian faith
  • antonym: listener - a person who listens to someone speaking
  • synonym: preacher - a person who delivers sermons or religious messages
