nonacute

adjective
not severe or intense
6.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ə.ˈkjuːt/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈkjuːt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ə.ˈkjuːt/ /; / /nɒn.ˈkjuːt/ (BrE)

Examples

Nonacute illness
