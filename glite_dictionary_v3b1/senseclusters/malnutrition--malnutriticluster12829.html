malnutrition

noun
condition of poor nutrition
Pronunciation: / ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmalnjuːˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
A condition resulting from an insufficient, excessive, or imbalanced diet, or from the body's inability to absorb nutrients properly.

Examples

Severe malnutrition effects
Children suffering from malnutrition may experience stunted growth and weakened immune systems.
Even in developed countries, malnutrition can occur due to poor dietary choices.
The organization provides food aid to communities affected by severe malnutrition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overnutrition - excessive intake of nutrients
  • synonym: undernutrition - insufficient intake of nutrients
