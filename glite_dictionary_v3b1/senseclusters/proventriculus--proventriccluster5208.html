proventriculus

noun
bird's glandular stomach
9.9
Pronunciation: /prə.ven.ˈtrɪ.kjʊ.ləs/ /; / /proʊ.ven.ˈtrɪ.kjʊ.ləs/ (AmE), /prə.ven.ˈtrɪ.kjə.ləs/ /; / /prə.ven.ˈtrɪ.kjʊ.ləs/ (BrE)
the glandular part of a bird's stomach that softens and begins digesting food before it reaches the gizzard

Examples

Bird proventriculus
The proventriculus produces digestive enzymes before food passes to the gizzard.
In birds, the proventriculus is located between the esophagus and the gizzard.
A healthy proventriculus is essential for proper digestion in poultry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gizzard - the muscular part of a bird's stomach that grinds food
