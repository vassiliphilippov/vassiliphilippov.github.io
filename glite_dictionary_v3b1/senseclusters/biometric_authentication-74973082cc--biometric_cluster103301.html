biometric authentication

noun
automatic individual identification using bodily traits
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈmɛ.trɪk ɔˌθɛn.tɪ.kəˈʃeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈmɛ.trɪk ɔˌθɛn.tɪ.kəˈʃeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of automatically identifying individuals by analyzing their unique physiological or behavioral characteristics, such as fingerprints or voice patterns

Examples

Biometric authentication system
Banks have started implementing biometric authentication to increase the security of online transactions.
He prefers biometric authentication over traditional passwords because it is more convenient and secure.
Many smartphones now use biometric authentication for unlocking the device, such as fingerprint or facial recognition.
