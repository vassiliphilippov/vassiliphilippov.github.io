twerk

verb
to dance by shaking hips and bottom
6.4
Pronunciation: / twɜrk / (AmE), / twɜːk / (BrE)
to dance by shaking the hips and bottom in a rhythmic, thrusting motion, often with a low squatting stance

Examples

To twerk at party
At the party, she began to twerk in the middle of the dance floor, drawing everyone's attention.
He learned how to twerk by watching online dance tutorials.
The music video features dancers who twerk energetically to the beat.
