meat safe

noun
ventilated cupboard for storing meat
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /miːt seɪf/ (AmE), /miːt seɪf/ (BrE)
a cupboard or container, often with mesh sides, used for storing meat to keep it cool and protect it from insects

Examples

A meat safe cupboard
Before refrigerators were common, every household had a meat safe to preserve fresh cuts.
The farmhouse kitchen still had an old-fashioned meat safe by the window.
They kept the bacon in the meat safe to protect it from flies.
