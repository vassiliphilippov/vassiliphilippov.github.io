striped racer

noun
a slender, striped whipsnake
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪpt ˈreɪsər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt ˈreɪsə / (BrE)
a whipsnake found in scrublands and rocky hillsides, known for its slender body and distinctive stripes

Examples

The striped racer snake
Because the striped racer moves so fast, it is difficult to photograph in the wild.
Birdwatchers sometimes spot a striped racer sunning itself on a flat stone in the afternoon.
The striped racer darted quickly across the rocky hillside, vanishing among the shrubs.
