bandolier

noun
shoulder belt for cartridges
Pronunciation: / ˌbændəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌbændəˈlɪə / (BrE)
A broad belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, fitted with loops or pockets for carrying cartridges by soldiers.

Examples

A bandolier with cartridges
He wore a bandolier filled with ammunition across his chest during the march.
In the old photograph, revolutionaries are seen with rifles and bandoliers draped across their chests.
The soldier slung his bandolier over his shoulder before heading out on patrol.
