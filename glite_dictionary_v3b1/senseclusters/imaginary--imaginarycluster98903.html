imaginary

noun
things imagined
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri / (AmE), / ɪˈmadʒɪnəri / (BrE)
things or concepts that exist only in the mind or imagination and are not real.

Examples

Strange imaginaries
The author filled his novel with imaginaries that fascinated young readers.
Children often create imaginaries to play with when they are alone.
Her paintings are inspired by the imaginaries of ancient myths and legends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantasy - something imagined or invented, not real
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
