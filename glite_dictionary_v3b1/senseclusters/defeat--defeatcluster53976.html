defeat

verb
prevent passage of a proposal or plan
7.1
Pronunciation: / dəˈfit / (AmE), / dɪˈfiːt / (BrE)
to prevent the success or passage of a proposal, plan, or motion, especially by vote or argument

Examples

To defeat a proposal

The city council will likely defeat the proposal if enough members oppose it.
The committee voted to defeat the controversial bill.
