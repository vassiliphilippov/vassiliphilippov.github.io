jewel orchid

noun
Asian orchid grown for ornamental leaves
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːəl ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəl ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
Any of several delicate Asian orchids cultivated mainly for their velvety leaves with metallic white or gold veins, rather than for their flowers.

Examples

A jewel orchid plant
In the greenhouse, she kept a rare variety of jewel orchid on the shaded shelf.
The jewel orchid is prized by collectors for its beautiful veined foliage rather than its blooms.
They admired the intricate patterns on the leaves of the jewel orchid during the botanic garden tour.
