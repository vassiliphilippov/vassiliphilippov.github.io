Examples

JHVH name
Many early English translations of the Bible used JHVH to refer to the divine name.

Synonyms

  • YHVH

  • YHWH
    9.9 0.4/m
    YHWH is a common scholarly spelling of the four Hebrew letters, while JHVH is an alternate English transliteration of that name.
  • Yahweh
    9.3 2/m
    Yahweh is a spoken vocal form used in study and speech, while JHVH is a letter-by-letter transliteration seen in texts.
  • Jehovah
    7.5 4/m
    Jehovah is an English vocal name common in churches and older books, while JHVH is a direct transliteration of the consonants.
  • Tetragrammaton
    9.9 0.06/m
    Tetragrammaton names the four sacred Hebrew letters, while JHVH is one specific transliteration of those letters used as God's name.
