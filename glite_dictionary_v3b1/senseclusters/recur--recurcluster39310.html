Synonyms

    2.7 2/m
    come around is used for regular events that return each season or year, while recur describes events that happen again repeatedly.
  • reoccur
    3.8 0.2/m
    reoccur means to happen again after a time, while recur suggests a repeated or regular return over intervals.
  • repeat
    1.0 100/m
    repeat is often an action done again by someone, while recur describes events that return on their own over time.
