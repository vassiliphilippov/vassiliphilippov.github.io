fuss

verb
worry unnecessarily or excessively
5.1 2.4
Pronunciation: /fʌs/ (AmE), /fʌs/ (BrE)
to worry or show concern unnecessarily or excessively, especially about something minor

Examples

To fuss about details
Please don't fuss about the weather; it's out of our control.
She tends to fuss over every tiny detail when planning a party.
Stop fussing—everything is going to work out just fine.
