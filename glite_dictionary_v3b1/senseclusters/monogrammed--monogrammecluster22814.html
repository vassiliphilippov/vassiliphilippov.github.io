monogrammed

adjective
with initials added
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnəˌɡræmd / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌɡræmd / (BrE)
having a design of initials, usually a person's, printed, sewn, or embossed on an item

Examples

A monogrammed bag
He carried a monogrammed briefcase to work every day.
The hotel provided guests with monogrammed bathrobes for extra comfort.
She loved her new monogrammed stationery set with her initials on every page.

Related Terms

  • synonym: personalized - made to include someone's name or initials
  • antonym: plain - without any decoration or added design
