scriber

noun
tool for marking lines on materials
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskraɪbər / (AmE), / ˈskraɪbə / (BrE)
a sharp-pointed tool used for marking lines on wood or metal before cutting

Examples

A scriber tool
A scriber is essential for detailed layout work in metal fabrication.
Before cutting the metal sheet, she carefully traced her measurements with a scriber.
The carpenter used a scriber to mark precise lines on the wooden plank.
