bowstring

noun
string of an archer's bow
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌstrɪŋ / (BrE)
the strong cord attached to the ends of a bow, used to launch arrows when drawn and released

Examples

The bowstring breaks
After years of use, the bowstring had begun to fray and needed replacing.
The archer carefully adjusted the bowstring before aiming at the target.
With a quick motion, she drew the bowstring back and released the arrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - a weapon for shooting arrows, typically made from a curved piece of wood
