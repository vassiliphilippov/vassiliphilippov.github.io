night lizard

noun
small nocturnal lizard of southwestern North America and Cuba
3.3 8.8
Pronunciation: / naɪt ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / naɪt ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a small, secretive, nocturnal lizard of southwestern North America and Cuba that gives birth to live young

Examples

A night lizard species
Some species of night lizard are found only in isolated parts of California.
The night lizard hides under rocks during the day and emerges at dusk to hunt insects.
Unlike most lizards, the night lizard gives birth to live young instead of laying eggs.
