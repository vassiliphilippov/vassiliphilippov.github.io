undistinctive

adjective
lacking unique features
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (BrE)
not having any unique, special, or easily noticed features; not different from others

Examples

Undistinctive design
The building's undistinctive design made it blend in with the rest of the street.
Her voice was so undistinctive that it was hard to tell her apart from others on the recording.
He wore an undistinctive gray suit that did not attract any attention at the meeting.
The restaurant's menu was undistinctive, offering the same dishes as many others nearby.
Her style was so undistinctive that no one remembered what she wore to the event.
The new logo is undistinctive and fails to stand out among competitors.
