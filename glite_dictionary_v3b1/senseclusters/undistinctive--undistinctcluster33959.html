undistinctive

adjective
lacking unique features
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (BrE)
not having any unique, special, or easily noticed features; not different from others

Examples
