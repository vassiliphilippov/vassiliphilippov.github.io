predevelopment

noun
early planning stage
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːdɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the stage, process, or condition before development begins, especially in planning, projects, or the state of land before construction

Examples

Wildlife surveys were conducted during the predevelopment phase to document existing species.
Restoring the wetland to its predevelopment condition is a key goal of the project.
