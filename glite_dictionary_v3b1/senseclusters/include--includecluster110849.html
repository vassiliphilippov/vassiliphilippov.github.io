include

verb
to have as part of a whole
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːd /; / ɪnˈklud / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːd / (BrE)
to have or make someone or something part of a group, set, or whole

Examples

To include all documents
Please include all relevant documents in your application.
The tour price includes accommodation and breakfast each day.

Her hobbies include painting, hiking, and reading.
Please include your contact information in the form.
The price of the meal includes drinks and dessert.
Children under 12 are not included in the price of the ticket.
Doctors who pass the exam will be included in the national medical registry.
The organization decided to include her as a full member of the committee.
