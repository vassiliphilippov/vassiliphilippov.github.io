self-destruction

noun
the act of killing oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf.dɪˈstrʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌself.dɪˈstrʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of intentionally causing one's own death; suicide

Examples

Tragic self-destruction

She overcame her tendency toward self-destruction with therapy and support.
The film explores the tragic consequences of self-destruction and addiction.
