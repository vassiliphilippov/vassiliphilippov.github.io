tympanic membrane

noun
membrane in the ear that vibrates with sound
8.0 4.8
Pronunciation: /tɪm.ˈpæn.ɪk ˈmem.breɪn/ (AmE), /tɪm.ˈpæn.ɪk ˈmem.breɪn/ (BrE)
a thin membrane separating the outer ear from the middle ear, which vibrates in response to sound waves
