accomplished fact

noun
an irrevocable achievement
5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑmplɪʃt ˈfækt / (AmE), / əˈkɒmplɪʃt ˈfækt / (BrE)
something that has already been done and cannot be changed; an irreversible accomplishment.

Examples

An accomplished fact
By the time we arrived, the decision was already an accomplished fact.
The merger became an accomplished fact before any objections could be raised.
The treaty is now an accomplished fact, despite earlier resistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fait accompli - a thing that has already happened or been decided before those affected hear about it
