black-necked stilt

noun
long-legged wading bird with black neck
9.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /blæk nekt stɪlt/ (AmE), /blæk nekt stɪlt/ (BrE)
A long-legged wading bird (Himantopus mexicanus) of the Americas, with black plumage extending from the head down the back of the neck.
