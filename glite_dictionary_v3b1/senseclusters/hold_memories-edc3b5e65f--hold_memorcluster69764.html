hold memories

verb
to preserve or evoke recollections
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ˈmɛm.əriz / (AmE), / həʊld ˈmɛm.əriz / (BrE)
to contain, preserve, or evoke recollections or feelings from the past, especially in reference to objects or places

Examples

Place holds memories
Every time I visit the café, it holds memories of my college days.
The old diary holds memories that I cherish deeply.
This house holds memories of our childhood summers.
