market research

noun
analysis of consumers and markets
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːr.kɪt ˈriː.sɜːrtʃ/ /; / /ˈmɑːr.kət ˈriː.sɜːrtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.kɪt ˈriː.sɜːtʃ/ /; / /ˈmɑː.kət ˈriː.sɜːtʃ/ (BrE)
