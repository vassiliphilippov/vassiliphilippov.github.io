sea squirt

noun
a small marine animal (ascidian)
6.4
Pronunciation: / si ˈskwɝt / (AmE), / si ˈskwɜːt / (BrE)
A small marine animal, an ascidian, that attaches to rocks and expels water when touched or contracted.

Examples

A sea squirt animal
Children often enjoy poking a sea squirt to watch it squirt water.
Marine biologists study the life cycle of the sea squirt as an indicator of water quality.
The rocky shore was covered in colorful sea squirts and sponges.
