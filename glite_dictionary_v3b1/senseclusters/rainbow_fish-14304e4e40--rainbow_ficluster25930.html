rainbow fish

noun
small, colorful freshwater aquarium fish
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌboʊ fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ fɪʃ / (BrE)
A small, brightly colored freshwater fish of South America and the West Indies, often kept in aquariums.

Examples

Bright rainbow fish
Aquarium enthusiasts often choose rainbow fish for their vibrant appearance and easy care.
The pet store had several brightly colored rainbow fish in its display tanks.
We watched the rainbow fish dart gracefully around the plants in their tank.
