cerebellar hemisphere

noun
lateral lobe of the cerebellum
Pronunciation: / sɛr.əˈbɛl.ɚ ˈhɛm.ɪ.sfɪr / (AmE), / ˌsɛr.əˈbɛl.ə ˈhɛm.ɪ.sfɪə / (BrE)
either of the two lateral lobes of the cerebellum, responsible for coordinating movement and maintaining balance

Examples

The cerebellar hemisphere
Damage to the cerebellar hemisphere can result in loss of coordination on one side of the body.
MRI scans revealed unusual activity in the right cerebellar hemisphere of the patient.
The surgeon carefully examined the left cerebellar hemisphere during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cerebellum - the part of the brain at the back of the skull that controls movement and balance
