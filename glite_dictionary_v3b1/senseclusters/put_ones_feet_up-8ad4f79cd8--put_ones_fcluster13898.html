put (one's) feet up

verb
relax or rest, often with feet elevated
Pronunciation: / pʊt ˈfiːt ʌp / (AmE), / pʊt ˈfiːt ʌp / (BrE)
to relax or rest, usually by sitting or lying down and elevating the feet

Examples

To put feet up
After finishing all her chores, Maria was ready to put her feet up and watch a movie.
On vacation, I love being able to put my feet up by the pool and read a book.
The doctor advised him to put his feet up for a few days to recover from the injury.
