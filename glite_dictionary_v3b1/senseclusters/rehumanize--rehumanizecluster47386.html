rehumanize

verb
to restore humanity
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈhjuːməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈhjuːmənaɪz / (BrE)
to restore human qualities, dignity, or compassion to someone or something that has been dehumanized

Examples

To rehumanize prisoners
The organization works to rehumanize prisoners by offering education and support programs.
Art can help rehumanize communities that have suffered from conflict and division.
Efforts to rehumanize the homeless include treating them with respect and understanding their stories.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dehumanize - to deprive of human qualities or dignity
  • synonym: humanize - to make more humane or compassionate
