pee-pee dance

noun
dance done when needing to urinate urgently
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌpi dæns / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌpiː dɑːns / (BrE)
a childish or informal dance-like movement done to indicate an urgent need to urinate

Examples

The pee-pee dance
During the long field trip, several children were seen doing the pee-pee dance.
She tried to hide the fact she had to go, but her pee-pee dance gave it away.
The little boy began to do the pee-pee dance while waiting for his turn in the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bathroom dance - a similar informal term for dancing due to needing the toilet
