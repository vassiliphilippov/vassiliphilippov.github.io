Alligator

noun
genus of large reptiles (alligators)
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈælɪɡeɪtə / (BrE)
A genus of large reptiles, including the American alligator and the Chinese alligator, belonging to the family Alligatoridae.

Examples

The Alligator is native to the southeastern United States and eastern China.
The American Alligator can grow to a length of over 4 meters.
