isobaric

adjective
equal pressure lines
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈbærɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈbærɪk / (BrE)
relating to or denoting lines on a map that connect points of equal atmospheric pressure.
