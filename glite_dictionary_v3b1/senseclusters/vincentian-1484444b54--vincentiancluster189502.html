Vincentian

noun
person from Saint Vincent
Pronunciation: / vɪnˈsɛnʃən / (AmE), / vɪnˈsɛnʃən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

Examples

Vincentian citizen
As a proud Vincentian, he enjoys sharing his culture with visitors.
Many Vincentians celebrate their independence day with music and parades.
The Vincentian community in New York gathers for traditional festivals every year.

Related Terms
