energetic disassembly

noun
active process of dismantling machinery or electronics
Pronunciation: / ˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk dɪsəˈsæmbli / (AmE), / ˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪk dɪsəˈsæmbli / (BrE)
the active or efficient process of dismantling equipment, machinery, or electronics into parts

Examples

Energetic disassembly of equipment
Automated systems now handle the energetic disassembly of equipment for rapid recycling.
Skilled workers carried out the energetic disassembly of large machinery in under an hour.
The company introduced a new technique for energetic disassembly of obsolete computers.

  • synonym: dismantle - to take apart a machine or structure
