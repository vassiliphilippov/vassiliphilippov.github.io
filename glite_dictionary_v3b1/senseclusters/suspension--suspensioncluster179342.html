suspension

noun
temporary stopping of activity
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛnʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act or period of temporarily stopping, interrupting, or delaying something

Examples

Suspension of service
Due to the storm, there was a suspension of all flights from the airport.
The suspension of service will last until further notice.
The school announced the temporary suspension of classes for the safety of students.
Production came to a suspension during the power outage.
The match was under suspension due to heavy rain.
There will be a temporary suspension of services this weekend for maintenance.
Negotiations resumed after a short suspension in talks.
The storm caused a suspension of all outdoor events for the weekend.
There was a brief suspension of service while the system was being upgraded.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuation - the act of continuing or resuming after stopping
  • synonym: interruption - the act of stopping something for a short period
  • synonym: pause - a temporary stop in an activity or process
  • antonym: resumption - the action of beginning something again after a pause
