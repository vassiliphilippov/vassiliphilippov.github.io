conserve

noun
fruit preserve cooked with sugar
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝːv / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːv / (BrE)
a type of preserve made by cooking fruit with sugar, often with whole pieces of fruit.

Examples

A jar of conserve
She spread homemade conserve on her toast every morning.
The gift basket included a jar of cherry conserve.
This apricot conserve pairs beautifully with cheese.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jam - a thick spread made by boiling fruit and sugar
  • synonym: marmalade - a preserve made from citrus fruit, especially oranges, with peel
  • synonym: preserve - a food made by preserving fruit with sugar
