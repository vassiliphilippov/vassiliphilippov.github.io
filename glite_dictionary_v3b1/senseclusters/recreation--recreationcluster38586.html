recreation

noun
activity for enjoyment and relaxation
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːkriˈeɪʃən /; / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
an activity done for enjoyment, relaxation, or amusement, rather than for work

Examples

Recreation activities
After a stressful week, she enjoys swimming as her main form of recreation.
Many cities invest in sports facilities to promote public recreation.
The park is designed for recreation and relaxation for local residents.
Outdoor recreation is encouraged to promote a healthy lifestyle.
Reading novels is her favorite form of recreation after a long day at work.
The new community center offers a variety of recreation activities for children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leisure - free time when one is not working
  • antonym: work - activity involving mental or physical effort for achieving a result rather than for enjoyment
