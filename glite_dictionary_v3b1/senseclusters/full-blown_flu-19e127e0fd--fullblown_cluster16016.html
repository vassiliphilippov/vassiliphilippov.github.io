full-blown flu

noun
severe, advanced influenza
3.7 2.5
Pronunciation: /fʊl.ˈbloʊn fluː/ /; / /fəl.ˈbloʊn fluː/ (AmE), /fʊl.ˈbləʊn fluː/ /; / /fəl.ˈbləʊn fluː/ (BrE)
