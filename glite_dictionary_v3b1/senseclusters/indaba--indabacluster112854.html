indaba

noun
council meeting of southern African peoples
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɑːbə / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɑːbə / (BrE)
a meeting or discussion, especially among indigenous peoples of southern Africa, often to share ideas or solve problems

Examples

A indaba meeting
Representatives from several tribes attended the indaba to resolve longstanding disputes.
The annual indaba focused on preserving cultural traditions and languages.
The community elders called an indaba to address the water crisis.
The community leaders organized an indaba to address local concerns.
During the annual indaba, experts from different fields shared their insights.
Everyone was invited to the indaba to contribute their ideas for the new project.

  • synonym: council - a formal meeting for discussion and decision-making
