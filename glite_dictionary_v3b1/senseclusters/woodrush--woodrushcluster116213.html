woodrush

noun
woodland grasslike plant
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊd.rʌʃ/ (AmE), /ˈwʊd.rʌʃ/ (BrE)
a plant of the genus Luzula, resembling grass and commonly found in woodlands

Examples

A woodrush plant
The forest floor was dotted with clusters of woodrush among the trees.
Botanists studied the woodrush to understand its role in the woodland ecosystem.
In early spring, the fresh green leaves of woodrush appear before many other plants.
