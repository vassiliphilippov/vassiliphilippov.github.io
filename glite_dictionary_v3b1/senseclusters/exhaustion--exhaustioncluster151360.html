Examples

Synonyms

  • frazzle
    7.4 0.09/m
    frazzle is an informal term for being completely worn out after stress, while exhaustion is the formal state of total fatigue.
  • enervation
    9.1 0.01/m
    enervation describes a deep loss of strength and energy in a formal or medical style, while exhaustion is common everyday complete fatigue.
  • overtiredness
    4.9 0.02/m
    overtiredness means being too tired, often restless and sleepless, while exhaustion means total weariness and inability to continue.
  • weariness
    3.1 2.41/m
    weariness is a tired feeling after hard work that fades with rest, while exhaustion is deep, long-lasting fatigue.
  • lethargy
    6.3 0.98/m
    lethargy is slow, low energy often linked to illness or lack of interest, while exhaustion is pure extreme tiredness.
  • tiredness
    1.0 1.02/m
    tiredness is a common, temporary lack of energy after activity, while exhaustion is complete, severe physical or mental fatigue.
