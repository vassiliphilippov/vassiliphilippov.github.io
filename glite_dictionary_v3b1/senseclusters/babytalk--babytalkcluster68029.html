babytalk

noun
simplified speech to or imitating young children
5.1 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.biː.tɑːk/ /; / /ˈbeɪ.biː.tɔːk/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪ.biː.tɔːk/ (BrE)
Speech characterized by simple words, short sentences, and exaggerated sounds, typically used by adults imitating or communicating with young children.

Examples
