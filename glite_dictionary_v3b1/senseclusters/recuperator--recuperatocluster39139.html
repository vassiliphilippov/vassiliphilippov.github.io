recuperator

noun
person recovering health
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkuːpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈkjuːpəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is recovering health or strength after an illness or medical treatment

Examples

A recuperator after surgery
The recuperator followed a strict diet and exercise plan to regain strength after his illness.
Family and friends visited the recuperator regularly to offer support during her recovery.
As a recuperator, he appreciated the quiet environment of the rehabilitation center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convalescent - a person recovering from illness or medical treatment
  • synonym: patient - a person receiving medical care or treatment
