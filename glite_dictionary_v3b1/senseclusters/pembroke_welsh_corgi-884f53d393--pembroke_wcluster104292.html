Pembroke Welsh corgi

noun
a small, short-tailed Welsh dog breed
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛm.broʊk ˈwɛlʃ ˈkɔːr.ɡi / (AmE), / ˈpɛm.brʊk ˈwɛlʃ ˈkɔː.ɡi / (BrE)
a small breed of dog, one of the two types of Welsh corgi, characterized by its pointed ears, short tail, and straight legs

Examples

The Pembroke Welsh corgi dog
Because of its friendly temperament, the Pembroke Welsh corgi makes a great family pet.
The Queen of England is famously fond of the Pembroke Welsh corgi breed.
Their distinctive short tails set Pembroke Welsh corgis apart from other corgis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Cardigan Welsh corgi - the other main breed of Welsh corgi, distinguished by having a long tail and rounded ears
