Ozark

proper noun
US mountain region
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊzɑːrk / (AmE), / ˈəʊzɑːk / (BrE)
a region of hills and mountains in the central United States, mainly in Missouri, Arkansas, and Oklahoma

Examples

The Ozark mountains

The Ozark landscape is famous for its rolling hills and clear rivers.
