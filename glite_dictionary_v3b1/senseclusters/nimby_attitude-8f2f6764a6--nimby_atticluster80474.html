NIMBY attitude

noun
opposition to local developments near oneself
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪm.bi ˈæt.ə.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈnɪm.bi ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude of opposing the development of undesirable projects or facilities near one's own home or community, while not necessarily objecting to such developments elsewhere.

Examples

A NIMBY attitude problem
A NIMBY attitude can hinder progress on important public projects such as wind farms or waste facilities.
Her persistent NIMBY attitude blocked plans for a new community center in the neighborhood.
The city faced strong opposition due to residents' NIMBY attitude toward the proposed homeless shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: NIMBY - someone who opposes local developments but accepts them elsewhere
  • antonym: YIMBY attitude - an attitude supporting local development projects
