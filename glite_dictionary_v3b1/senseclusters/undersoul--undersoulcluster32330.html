undersoul

noun
universal soul concept
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌsoʊl / (AmE), / ˈʌndəsəʊl / (BrE)
a supposed universal soul or spiritual force underlying and connecting all living things

Examples

Universal undersoul
The philosopher described the undersoul as the source of all life and consciousness.
Many spiritual traditions speak of an undersoul that links every living creature.
In her novel, the undersoul was a mysterious force guiding the characters' destinies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individual soul - the soul belonging to a single person or being
  • synonym: world soul - the idea of a single spirit or soul shared by all living things
