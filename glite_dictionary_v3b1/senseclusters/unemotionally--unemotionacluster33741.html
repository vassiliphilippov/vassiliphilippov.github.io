unemotionally

adverb
without emotion
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈmoʊʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈməʊʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no emotion or feeling; without displaying emotion.

Examples

To speak unemotionally
He spoke unemotionally about his experiences during the war.
She listened unemotionally as the doctor explained the diagnosis.
The judge read the verdict unemotionally, maintaining a neutral tone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionally - in a way relating to or expressing emotion
