paper clip

noun
attach-file icon in software
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.pər klɪp/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.pə klɪp/ (BrE)
an icon or button (often shaped like a paper clip) in an email, messaging app, or other software used to attach one or more files to a message.

Examples

Click the paper clip icon
To attach your resume, click the paper clip at the bottom of the email compose window.
On the messaging app, tap the paper clip and choose the photo you want to send.
She showed me how to press the paper clip button to add documents to the form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attach - to add or join a file to an email or message; the action represented by the paper clip icon
  • synonym: attachment icon - an icon used in software interfaces to add files to messages
