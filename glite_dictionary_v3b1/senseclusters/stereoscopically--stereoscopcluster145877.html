stereoscopically

adverb
in 3D manner
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriəˈskɑpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəˈskɒpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or uses stereoscopic vision or imaging to create a three-dimensional effect

Examples

To see stereoscopically
The movie was projected stereoscopically to give the audience a sense of depth.
Scientists examined the specimen stereoscopically to better understand its structure.
The photographs were combined stereoscopically to produce a realistic 3D image.

Related Terms

  • synonym: three-dimensionally - in a way that relates to three dimensions
