tentmaking

noun
making tents
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛntˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or occupation of making tents, especially as a trade or craft

Examples

Traditional tentmaking
He took a course in tentmaking to prepare for his expedition.
Traditional tentmaking requires skill in both sewing and working with sturdy materials.
The village was known for its high-quality tentmaking that supplied travelers and merchants.
