market strategist

noun
financial markets adviser
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːr.kət ˈstræt.ə.dʒɪst/ /; / /ˈmɑːr.kɪt ˈstræt.ə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.kɪt ˈstræt.ə.dʒɪst/ /; / /ˈmɑː.kət ˈstræt.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
(in finance) a professional who analyses capital markets and advises investors or institutions on market strategy, asset allocation, and trading positions.

Examples

A market strategist advises investors
The firm's market strategist recommended reducing exposure to emerging-market bonds this quarter.
As a market strategist, she prepared a briefing on interest-rate forecasts for institutional clients.
Our new market strategist argued that diversifying globally would protect the fund during volatility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investment strategist - a professional who develops investment strategy and asset allocation recommendations
  • synonym: market analyst - someone who studies market trends and data to inform investment decisions
