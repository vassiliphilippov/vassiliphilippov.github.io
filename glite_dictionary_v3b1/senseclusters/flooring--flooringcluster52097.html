flooring

noun
material covering a floor
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːrɪŋ /; / ˈflɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
material such as wood, tile, or carpet used to cover the surface of a floor, or the surface itself

Examples

Flooring material
The company offers a wide range of flooring options, including hardwood and laminate.
They chose bamboo flooring for its durability and modern look.
Water damage ruined the old flooring in the kitchen.
Before laying tiles, the builders inspected the wooden flooring for damage.
Rainwater had seeped through, soaking the tent's flooring.
The carpet covered the entire flooring of the living room.

Related Terms
