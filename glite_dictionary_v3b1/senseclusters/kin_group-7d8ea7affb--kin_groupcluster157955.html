kin group

noun
group related by blood or marriage
4.0
Pronunciation: / kɪn ɡruːp / (AmE), / kɪn ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of people who are related to each other by blood or marriage

Examples

A kin group family
Anthropologists study the structure and roles of a kin group to understand social relationships.
Membership in a kin group can influence inheritance, marriage, and social status.
The extended family formed a large kin group in their community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clan - a group of close-knit and interrelated families
  • synonym: family - a group consisting of parents and children living together
