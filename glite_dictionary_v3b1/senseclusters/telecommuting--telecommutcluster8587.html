telecommuting

noun
working from home via electronic communication

Pronunciation: /tel.ə.kə.ˈmjuː.tɪŋ/ /; / /tel.ə.kə.ˈmuː.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /tel.ə.kə.ˈmjuː.tɪŋ/ /; / /tel.ə.kə.ˈmuː.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
