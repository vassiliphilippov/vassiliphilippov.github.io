telecommuting

noun
working from home via electronic communication
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪkəˈmjutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkəˈmjuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of working from home, while communicating with one's employer or colleagues using telephone, email, or the internet

Examples

Telecommuting from home
Many employees now prefer telecommuting to avoid long commutes to the office.
Successful telecommuting requires reliable internet access and good communication skills.
The company introduced telecommuting options during the pandemic to keep staff safe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: office work - employment done at a traditional physical workplace
  • synonym: remote work - the practice of working outside a conventional office environment
