postintervention

noun
after treatment period
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˌɪntərˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˌɪntəˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
the period following a treatment or intervention, often used for observation or follow-up.

Examples

Postintervention phase
The study measured patient progress during the postintervention phase to assess the effectiveness of the therapy.
Doctors scheduled regular check-ups in the postintervention period to monitor for any complications.
Researchers compared preintervention and postintervention data to evaluate the impact of the new drug.
