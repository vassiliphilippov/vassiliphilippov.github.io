fir tree

noun
evergreen tree of the genus Abies
Pronunciation: / fɝ ˈtri / (AmE), / fɜː ˈtriː / (BrE)
An evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Abies, typically found in upland areas and known for its soft wood and needle-like leaves.

Examples

A tall fir tree
A fallen fir tree blocked the hiking trail after the storm.
During the holidays, many people decorate a fir tree in their living rooms.
The forest was full of ancient fir trees rising straight into the misty sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pine tree - evergreen conifer with needle-like leaves, genus Pinus
  • synonym: spruce - evergreen tree of the genus Picea
