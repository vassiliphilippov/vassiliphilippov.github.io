fruited

adjective
bearing fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfruːtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Fruited tree
The fruited vines covered the garden fence in late summer.
We admired the fruited branches heavy with ripe peaches.
After years of care, the orchard was finally full of fruited trees.
