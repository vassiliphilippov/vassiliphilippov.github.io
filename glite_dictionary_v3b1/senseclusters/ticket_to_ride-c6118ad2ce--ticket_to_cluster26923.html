ticket to ride

noun
opportunity for advancement or access
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪt tə raɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt tə raɪd / (BrE)
an opportunity that allows someone to achieve success, improve their situation, or gain access to something desirable

Examples

A ticket to ride opportunity
For many athletes, a college scholarship is their ticket to ride.
Graduating from a top university became his ticket to ride in the business world.
That invention was her ticket to ride out of poverty.
