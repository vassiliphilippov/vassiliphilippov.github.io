stormtrooper

noun
Star Wars soldier
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrmˌtruːpər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmˌtruːpə / (BrE)
a fictional soldier, especially one in the Star Wars universe, known for wearing white armor and serving the Empire

Examples

Stormtrooper costume
He dressed as a stormtrooper for Halloween and carried a toy blaster.
The parade featured dozens of people in stormtrooper costumes marching in formation.
Many fans collect stormtrooper action figures from the Star Wars movies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clone trooper - a fictional soldier in the Star Wars universe, predecessor to stormtroopers
  • antonym: rebel - a member of the opposing force fighting against the Empire in Star Wars
