stationarity

noun
absence of movement
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsteɪʃəˈnɛrəti / (AmE), / ˌsteɪʃəˈnærɪti / (BrE)
the state or condition of not moving or remaining unchanged over time

Examples

Stationarity of air
The scientist measured the stationarity of the air in the sealed chamber.
Economic models often assume the stationarity of certain variables over time.
The stationarity of the object made it easier to observe under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: change - the act or process of becoming different
  • synonym: motionlessness - the state of not moving
  • synonym: stability - the quality of being steady and not changing
