Styracosaurus

noun
genus of horned dinosaurs
9.9
Pronunciation: / staɪˌrækəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / staɪˌrækəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of horned dinosaurs from the Late Cretaceous period, known for its large frill and prominent nasal horn

Examples

The Styracosaurus dinosaur
A fossilized skull of Styracosaurus was displayed at the natural history museum.
Many paleontologists believe the Styracosaurus lived in what is now North America.
The Styracosaurus had a dramatic frill adorned with long spikes for defense.
