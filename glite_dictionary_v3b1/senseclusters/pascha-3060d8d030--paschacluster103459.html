Pascha

noun
Christian festival of Easter
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːskə / (AmE), / ˈpæskə / (BrE)
The Christian festival of Easter, especially as celebrated in Eastern Orthodox and some Eastern Christian traditions.

Examples

Orthodox Pascha celebration
Many traditions are observed during Pascha, including midnight liturgies and festive meals.
She baked a special bread for Pascha as part of her family's annual celebration.
The Orthodox Church celebrates Pascha according to the Julian calendar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Easter - Christian festival celebrating the resurrection of Jesus
