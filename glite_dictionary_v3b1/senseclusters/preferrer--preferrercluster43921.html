preferrer

noun
person submitting a charge
8.2
Pronunciation: / prɪˈfɜːrər / (AmE), / prɪˈfɛrər / (BrE)
a person who formally presents or submits a charge, complaint, petition, or claim

Examples

Preferrer of a complaint
The preferrer of the complaint provided detailed evidence to support the allegations.
As the preferrer of the petition, she was asked to address the council directly.
The committee listened carefully to the preferrer before making their decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accuser - a person who accuses someone of a crime or wrongdoing
  • synonym: complainant - a person who makes a formal complaint
  • antonym: respondent - a person who answers a charge or claim
