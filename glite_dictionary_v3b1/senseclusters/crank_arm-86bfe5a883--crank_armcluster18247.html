crank arm

noun
bicycle pedal lever
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkræŋk ɑːrm / (AmE), / ˈkræŋk ɑːm / (BrE)
a lever attached to a rotating shaft, especially on a bicycle, used to transfer force from the pedals to the drivetrain

Examples

The crank arm on bike
The crank arm on my bike broke during the ride.
He replaced the worn crank arm to improve his cycling performance.
A bent crank arm can make pedaling difficult and uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chainring - the toothed wheel attached to the crank arm on a bicycle
  • synonym: pedal - a foot-operated lever on a bicycle
