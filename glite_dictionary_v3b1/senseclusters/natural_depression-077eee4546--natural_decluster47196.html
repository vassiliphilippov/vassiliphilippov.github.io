natural depression

noun
sunken geological formation
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəl dɪˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl dɪˈprɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)

The lake formed in a natural depression left behind by a glacier thousands of years ago.
