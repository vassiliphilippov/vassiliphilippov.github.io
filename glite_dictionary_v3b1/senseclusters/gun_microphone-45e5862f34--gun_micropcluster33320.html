gun microphone

noun
directional microphone with a long barrel
9.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /gʌn ˈmaɪ.krə.foʊn/ (AmE), /gʌn ˈmaɪ.krə.fəʊn/ (BrE)
a highly directional microphone with a long, barrel-shaped design that allows for recording sounds from a distance by aiming it at the source

Examples

A gun microphone for recording

Due to its narrow pickup pattern, the gun microphone minimizes background noise effectively.
The sound engineer used a gun microphone to capture dialogue from across the street.
