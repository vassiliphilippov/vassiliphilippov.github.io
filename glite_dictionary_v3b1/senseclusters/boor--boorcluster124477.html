boor

noun
rude or uncultured person
9.7
Pronunciation: / bʊr / (AmE), / bɔː / (BrE)
an uncouth, rude, or ill-mannered person lacking culture or refinement

Examples

A rude boor
Don't be a boor; show some respect for your hosts.
He acted like a complete boor at the dinner party, shouting and interrupting others.
Many considered him a boor because of his crude jokes and lack of manners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a courteous or refined man
  • synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive man or boy
