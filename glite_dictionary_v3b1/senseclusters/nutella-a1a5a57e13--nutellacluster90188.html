Nutella

proper noun
chocolate hazelnut spread
Pronunciation: / nuˈtɛlə / (AmE), / njuˈtɛlə / (BrE)
a popular brand of sweet chocolate and hazelnut spread often used on bread or in desserts

Examples

Nutella spread
She spread Nutella on her toast for breakfast.
Many children love to eat Nutella sandwiches as a snack after school.
You can use Nutella as a filling for pancakes or pastries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spread - a soft food that is spread on bread or crackers
