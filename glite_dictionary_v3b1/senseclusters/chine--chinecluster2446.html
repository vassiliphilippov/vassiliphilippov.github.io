chine

noun
coastal river valley
9.6
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪn / (AmE), / tʃaɪn / (BrE)
a steep-sided river valley or gully, especially one found on a coastal area

Examples

A coastal chine
The hikers carefully descended the chine to reach the beach below.
A small stream ran through the bottom of the chine, carving it deeper over time.
The coastal path offered a stunning view down into the chine and out to the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gorge - a narrow valley between hills or mountains, often with a stream
  • synonym: ravine - a deep, narrow valley with steep sides
