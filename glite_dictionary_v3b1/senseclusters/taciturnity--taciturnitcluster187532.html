taciturnity

noun
quality of being reserved or uncommunicative
7.1 7.4
Pronunciation: /tæs.ɪ.ˈtɜːr.nɪ.tɪ/ /; / /tæs.ɪ.ˈtɜːr.nə.tɪ/ (AmE), /tæs.ɪ.ˈtɜː.nɪ.tɪ/ /; / /tæs.ɪ.ˈtɜː.nə.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being reserved and uncommunicative; reluctance to speak

Examples

His taciturnity in meetings

His taciturnity at the meeting was noted by everyone present.
She was known for her taciturnity, rarely joining in the lively conversations.
