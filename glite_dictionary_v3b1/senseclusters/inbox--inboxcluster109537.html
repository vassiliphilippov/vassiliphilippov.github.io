inbox

noun
tray for incoming papers
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌbɒks / (BrE)
a tray or container on a desk for holding incoming letters, documents, or papers to be dealt with.

Examples

Desk inbox
The assistant placed the new mail in the inbox for her boss to review.
Please check your inbox every morning for important documents that need your attention.
His inbox was overflowing with papers after a week of vacation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outbox - a tray or container for outgoing letters or documents
  • synonym: tray - a flat container used to hold or carry items
