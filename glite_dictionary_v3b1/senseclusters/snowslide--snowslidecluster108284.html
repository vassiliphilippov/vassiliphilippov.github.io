snowslide

noun
snow avalanche
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌslaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌslaɪd / (BrE)
a mass of snow that suddenly slides down a slope or surface

Examples

A snowslide on mountain
The sudden snowslide blocked the mountain road for several hours.
Rescue teams searched for skiers trapped by the snowslide after the storm.
Heavy snowfall increased the risk of a snowslide in the alpine region.
A sudden snowslide from the barn roof covered the path and blocked the gate early that morning.
After the storm, a snowslide fell from the hillside and lightly dusted the road.
The children watched as a snowslide slipped off the garage roof and piled up below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avalanche - a mass of snow, ice, and rocks falling rapidly down a mountainside
  • antonym: landslide - a mass of earth or rock moving down a slope
