aboundingly

adverb
in a plentiful manner
Pronunciation: / əˈbaʊndɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈbaʊndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is abundant or plentiful; to a great degree or in large amounts

Examples

Grow aboundingly well
The wildflowers grew aboundingly across the open fields after the heavy rains.
Her generosity was shown aboundingly during the holiday season.
Fruit trees in the orchard produced aboundingly this year, filling baskets to the brim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundantly - in large quantities; plentifully
  • antonym: scarcely - barely; in insufficient amounts
