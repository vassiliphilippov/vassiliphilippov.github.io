essayistic

adjective
like an essay
Pronunciation: / ˌɛseɪˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛseɪˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of essays or essay writing, especially in style or approach

Examples

Essayistic writing style
The author's essayistic approach allows for personal reflection within the novel.
Her essayistic style makes the book feel both informative and intimate.
Critics praised the essayistic tone that blended analysis with storytelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discursive - moving from topic to topic in a way similar to essays
  • antonym: narrative - relating to the telling of stories
