reclosable

adjective
can close again
3.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈkloʊ.zə.bəl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkloʊ.zə.bəl/ (AmE), /riː.ˈkləʊ.zə.bəl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkləʊ.zə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be closed again automatically after being opened, especially referring to devices like circuit breakers.

Examples

Reclosable circuit breaker
The engineer recommended installing a reclosable circuit breaker to improve system reliability.
Many modern electrical devices now feature reclosable safety mechanisms for added protection.
A reclosable switch can automatically restore power after a temporary fault is cleared.
