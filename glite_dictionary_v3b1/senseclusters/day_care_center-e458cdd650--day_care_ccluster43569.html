day care center

noun
facility for caring for children while parents work
2.0 1.6
Pronunciation: /deɪ keər ˈsen.tər/ /; / /deɪ kɪər ˈsen.tər/ (AmE), /deɪ keə ˈsen.tə/ /; / /deɪ kɪə ˈsen.tə/ (BrE)
