Synonyms

  • penalization
    7.3 0.03/m
    penalization is a formal legal process of applying a penalty for rule breaking, while punishment is the general act of penalizing someone.
  • penalty
    2.5 22/m
    penalty is a specific consequence like a fine or loss, while punishment is the broader act of imposing that consequence.
  • sanction
    6.1 28/m
    sanction is an official restriction often used by governments, while punishment is any act of imposing a penalty for wrongdoing.
