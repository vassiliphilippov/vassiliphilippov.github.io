school principal

noun
head administrator of a school
1.6 2.1
Pronunciation: / skuːl ˈprɪnsəpəl / (AmE), / skuːl ˈprɪnsəpəl / (BrE)
the head teacher or administrator in charge of a school, responsible for its overall management and leadership

Examples

The school principal meets parents
After the incident, the parents met with the school principal to discuss the school's policies.
The school principal implemented several changes to improve academic performance.
The school principal welcomed new students during the orientation ceremony.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: deputy principal - an assistant to the school principal, often second-in-command
  • synonym: headteacher - the person in charge of a school, especially in British English
  • degree higher: superintendent - the official in charge of several schools or an entire school district
