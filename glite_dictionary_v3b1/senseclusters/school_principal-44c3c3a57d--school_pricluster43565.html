school principal

noun
head administrator of a school
2.5 8.06/m
Pronunciation: / skuÀêl Ààpr…™ns…ôp…ôl / (AmE), / skuÀêl Ààpr…™ns…ôp…ôl / (BrE)
the head teacher or administrator in charge of a school, responsible for its overall management and leadership

Examples

The school principal meets parents
After the incident, the parents met with the school principal to discuss the school's policies.
The school principal implemented several changes to improve academic performance.
The school principal welcomed new students during the orientation ceremony.

Synonyms

  • head teacher
    3.2 0.11/m
    head teacher leads day-to-day teaching and staff at a school, while a school principal has wider administrative and leadership responsibility.
  • principal
    3.6 38/m
    principal is the main person in an organization or activity, while a school principal specifically runs and manages a school.
  • headmaster
    3.2 3.79/m
    headmaster is a traditional school leader often used in private or British schools, while a school principal is the general modern school head.
  • headmistress
    3.9 0.66/m
    headmistress is a woman who leads a school, often in older or girls' schools, while a school principal is a gender-neutral administrative head.
  • head
    2.2 87/m
    head is any leader of a group or activity, while a school principal is the specific leader responsible for running a school.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: deputy principal - an assistant to the school principal, often second-in-command
  • synonym: headteacher - the person in charge of a school, especially in British English
  • degree higher: superintendent - the official in charge of several schools or an entire school district
