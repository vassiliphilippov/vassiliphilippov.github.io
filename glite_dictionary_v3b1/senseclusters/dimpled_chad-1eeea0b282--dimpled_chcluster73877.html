dimpled chad

noun
chad on a ballot indented but not detached
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmpəld tʃæd / (AmE), / ˈdɪmpəld tʃad / (BrE)
a chad on a punch-card ballot that has been indented but not fully punched out, leaving all corners still attached

Examples

A dimpled chad ballot
A dimpled chad is not counted as a valid vote in many jurisdictions.
During the recount, election officials had trouble interpreting ballots with a dimpled chad.
They debated whether a dimpled chad indicated voter intent or was simply a mistake.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hanging chad - a chad on a punch-card ballot that is attached only at one corner
