postboy

noun
mail rider (historical)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man who delivers mail, especially in the past (often on horseback or guiding coaches), or a young male postal worker or messenger
