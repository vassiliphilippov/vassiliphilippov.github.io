waistband

noun
material band around garment waist
1.9 2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈweɪs(t)bænd / (BrE)
A band of material sewn around the waist of a garment, such as a skirt or trousers, to strengthen it or hold it up.

Examples

The waistband of trousers
His shirt was tucked neatly into the waistband of his trousers.
She adjusted the waistband of her jeans to make them more comfortable.
The elastic waistband makes these shorts easy to wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belt - a strip worn around the waist, often to hold up clothing
