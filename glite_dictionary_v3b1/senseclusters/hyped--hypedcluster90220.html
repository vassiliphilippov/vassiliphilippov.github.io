hyped

adjective
over-promoted
5.1
Pronunciation: / haɪpt / (AmE), / haɪpt / (BrE)
receiving excessive publicity or promotion, often making something seem more impressive or important than it really is

Examples

A hyped movie
The most hyped product of the year disappointed many customers after its release.
Critics argued that the hyped concert did not meet the audience's high expectations.
Despite being hyped by the media, the new restaurant received mixed reviews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overrated - considered better or more important than it really is
  • antonym: understated - presented as less important or impressive than it really is
