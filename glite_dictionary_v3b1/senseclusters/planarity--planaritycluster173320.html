planarity

noun
property of being planar
9.0
Pronunciation: / pləˈnærəti / (AmE), / pləˈnærəti / (BrE)
the property of a graph or shape being able to be drawn on a flat surface without any edges crossing

Examples

Planarity of a graph
The planarity of the network allows for simpler visualization and analysis.
Mathematicians study planarity to determine if a graph can be drawn without overlapping edges.
Checking the planarity of a circuit diagram is important for efficient design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonplanar - not able to be drawn on a plane without edges crossing
  • synonym: planar - lying in a single flat surface
