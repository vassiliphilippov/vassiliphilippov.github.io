percept

noun
AI/robot sensory data representation
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.sept/ /; / /pə.ˈsept/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.sept/ /; / /pə.ˈsept/ (BrE)
(in artificial intelligence and robotics) an instance of sensory data or an internal representation produced by a machine's sensors and used by an agent to model or act on its environment.

Examples

Robot percept data
The robot updated its internal map after the camera produced a new percept of the hallway.
During training the agent stored millions of visual percepts to learn common object patterns.
Researchers compared how quickly different sensors produced a useful percept for navigation.
