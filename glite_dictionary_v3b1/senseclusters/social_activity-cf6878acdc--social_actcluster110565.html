social activity

noun
an activity involving social interaction
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ækˈtɪv.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ækˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
an activity that involves social interaction or is appropriate for social occasions

Examples

A social activity with friends
Attending a dinner party is a common social activity enjoyed by many people.
Joining a book club is an easy way to participate in a regular social activity.
Volunteering at local events can be a rewarding social activity for those who like to meet new people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recreation - activity done for enjoyment in one's free time; leisurely activity
  • antonym: solitary activity - an activity done alone, without others
