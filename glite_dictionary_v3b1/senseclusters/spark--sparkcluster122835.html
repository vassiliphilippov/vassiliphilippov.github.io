spark

noun
lively or bright look in the eyes
Pronunciation: / spɑrk / (AmE), / spɑːk / (BrE)
a bright or lively expression in someone's eyes or face, showing merriment or animation

Examples

A spark of joy
Despite her age, a spark of joy lit up her face whenever she laughed.
The old man still had a spark of playfulness in his smile.
There was a mischievous spark in her eyes as she told the joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gleam - a brief or faint appearance of light, often in the eyes
  • synonym: twinkle - a bright, cheerful or lively glimmer in the eyes
