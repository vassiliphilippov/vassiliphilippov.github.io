gouge

noun
chisel with a curved blade
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊdʒ / (AmE), / ɡaʊdʒ / (BrE)
A chisel with a curved blade used for cutting grooves or channels in wood or other materials.

Examples

Woodworker's gouge
A gouge leaves a rounded groove, unlike a flat chisel.
She used a gouge to carve an intricate pattern into the wooden panel.
The craftsman carefully sharpened his gouge before starting the sculpture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chisel - a tool with a straight blade for cutting or shaping material
