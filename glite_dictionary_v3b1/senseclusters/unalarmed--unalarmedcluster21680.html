unalarmed

adjective
without an alarm system
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈlɑrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈlɑːmd / (BrE)
not equipped with an alarm device or system for protection or alerting

Examples

Unalarmed building
The warehouse was left unalarmed overnight, making it vulnerable to theft.
Many rural homes remain unalarmed because the crime rate is low in those areas.
After the break-in, they decided not to leave the garage unalarmed again.
