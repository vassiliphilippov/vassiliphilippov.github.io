family Xiphiidae

noun
swordfish family (marine fish)
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli zɪˈfaɪɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli zɪˈfɪɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, predatory marine fish known as swordfishes, characterized by their long, flat, sword-like bills.

Biologists have studied the migratory patterns of the family Xiphiidae for decades.
Members of the family Xiphiidae are known for their elongated, sword-like bills.
The family Xiphiidae contains only one living species, the swordfish.
