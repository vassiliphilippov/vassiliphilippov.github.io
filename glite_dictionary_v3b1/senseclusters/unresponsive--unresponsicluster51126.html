unresponsive

adjective
not reacting to influence or stimulus
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈspɑn.sɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈspɒn.sɪv / (BrE)
not reacting or responding to influence, stimulus, or communication.

Examples

Unresponsive patient
After the accident, the patient was unresponsive to all attempts to wake her.
His phone remained unresponsive despite several resets and updates.
The manager was unresponsive to employees' requests for flexible schedules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responsive - reacting quickly or positively to something
