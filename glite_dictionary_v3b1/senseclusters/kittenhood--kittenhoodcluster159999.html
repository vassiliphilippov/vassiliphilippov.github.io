kittenhood

noun
time as a kitten
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtn̩hʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɪtn̩hʊd / (BrE)
the period or state in which a cat is a kitten

Examples

Young kittenhood period
During her playful kittenhood, the cat loved to chase balls of yarn around the house.
Many important social skills are learned in a cat's kittenhood before it becomes an adult.
He often reminisced about his pet's mischievous kittenhood and the trouble she used to cause.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adulthood - the state of being an adult
  • synonym: childhood - the period of being a child
