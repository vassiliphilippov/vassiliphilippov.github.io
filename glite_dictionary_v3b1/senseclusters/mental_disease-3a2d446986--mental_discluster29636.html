A disorder that seriously affects a person's mind, resulting in emotional or behavioral problems that may require psychiatric treatment.

Examples

Mental disease treatment
Advancements in medicine have improved the treatment of many mental diseases.
Recognizing the early symptoms of a mental disease can lead to better outcomes.
The stigma surrounding mental disease often prevents people from seeking help.

Synonyms

  • mental disorder
    3.3 2.29/m
    mental disorder names a diagnosed condition in psychiatry, while mental disease sounds more medical and suggests a clear biological illness.
  • mental illness
    3.1 8.98/m
    mental illness describes conditions that affect mood or thinking, while mental disease focuses on a medical illness that damages mental health.
  • psychological disorder
    4.6 0.34/m
    psychological disorder refers to problems of thought or emotion studied in psychology, while mental disease implies a medical illness of the mind.
  • mental disturbance
    6.1 0.07/m
    mental disturbance describes a shorter or milder change in feeling or thinking, while mental disease suggests a serious, often long medical condition.
  • mental abnormality
    7.5 0.02/m
    mental abnormality names a deviation from normal mental function that sounds clinical and broad, while mental disease stresses a serious medical illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mental illness - a disorder that affects mood, thinking, and behavior
  • antonym: physical disease - a disorder affecting the body rather than the mind
