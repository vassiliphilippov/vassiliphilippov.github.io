mental disease

noun
disorder affecting the mind
4.3 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmen.təl dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /ˈmen.təl də.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /ˈmen.təl dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /ˈmen.təl də.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
A disorder that seriously affects a person's mind, resulting in emotional or behavioral problems that may require psychiatric treatment.

Examples

Mental disease treatment
Advancements in medicine have improved the treatment of many mental diseases.
Recognizing the early symptoms of a mental disease can lead to better outcomes.
The stigma surrounding mental disease often prevents people from seeking help.

  • antonym: physical disease - a disorder affecting the body rather than the mind
