lamestream

adjective
unoriginal mainstream
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪmstriːm / (AmE), / ˈleɪmstriːm / (BrE)
informal, derogatory: typical of mainstream culture, media, or products considered dull, unoriginal, or lacking credibility

Examples

Lamestream music
Some critics dismiss popular music as lamestream and lacking originality.
He refused to watch the lamestream news channels, claiming they never covered real issues.
The artist wanted to avoid the lamestream approach and create something truly unique.
Some bloggers criticize the lamestream media for not covering important stories.
He called the news report lamestream because it repeated the same old opinions.
Many people turn to independent sources when they feel the lamestream press is not trustworthy.
