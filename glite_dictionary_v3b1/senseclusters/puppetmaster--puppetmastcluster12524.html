puppetmaster

noun
secret manipulator
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpɪtˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪtˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who secretly controls or manipulates the actions of others, often from behind the scenes

Examples

Hidden puppetmaster
The media speculated that a powerful puppetmaster was influencing the election from behind the scenes.
She realized too late that she had been a pawn for a clever puppetmaster in the organization.
Rumors spread that the true puppetmaster never appeared in public but controlled everything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - someone who has power over others' actions or decisions
  • synonym: manipulator - a person who controls or influences others in a clever or unfair way
  • antonym: pawn - a person used by others for their own purposes
