fang-like

adjective
resembling a fang
Pronunciation: / ˈfæŋ laɪk / (AmE), / ˈfæŋ laɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or having the appearance of a fang.

Examples

Fang-like teeth
The sculpture featured fang-like projections jutting out from the main body.
The snake's fang-like teeth were clearly visible in its open mouth.
Thunderclouds cast fang-like shadows across the jagged mountain peaks.
