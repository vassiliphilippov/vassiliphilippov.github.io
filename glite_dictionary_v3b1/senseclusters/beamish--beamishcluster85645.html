beamish

adjective
smiling with happiness or optimism
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːmɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbiːmɪʃ / (BrE)
(literary) smiling with happiness or optimism; radiant with joy.

Examples

A beamish smile
Alice greeted the guests with a beamish smile that brightened the entire room.
He always wore a beamish expression, even on rainy days.
The beamish faces of the children showed their excitement for the holiday.
