Synonyms

  • ascribable
    6.9 0.03/m
    ascribable suggests direct assignment to a particular cause or person, while referable is a more general ability to be linked to an origin.
  • attributable
    5.4 4.62/m
    attributable often appears with 'to' to show something is caused by a person or event, while referable means able to be linked to an origin.
  • imputable
    8.6 0.01/m
    imputable is formal and often used to blame a person or action, while referable is a neutral term for being linked to a source.
  • traceable
    3.8 0.70/m
    traceable means you can follow steps to find an origin, while referable means simply able to be assigned to a cause or source.
