polycarbonate

noun
strong clear plastic
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈkɑrbəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈkɑːbənət / (BrE)
a strong, transparent plastic material used in products like eyewear, bottles, and electronic devices

Examples

Polycarbonate lenses
The safety goggles are made from polycarbonate to resist impact.
Many reusable water bottles are constructed using durable polycarbonate material.
The phone case is lightweight because it is made of polycarbonate instead of metal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acrylic - a clear plastic material often used as glass substitute
  • synonym: plastic - a synthetic material made from polymers
