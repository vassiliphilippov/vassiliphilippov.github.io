day

noun
period of waking or working hours
1.0
Pronunciation: / deɪ / (AmE), / deɪ / (BrE)
the time during each 24-hour period when you are awake and active, especially the part when you are working or occupied

Examples

A busy day
After a productive day, he relaxed with his family.
My day usually starts with a strong cup of coffee.
She had a long day at the office and came home exhausted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: night - the time when it is dark and you are usually sleeping
