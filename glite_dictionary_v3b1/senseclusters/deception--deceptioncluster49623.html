deception

noun
an illusory or magical feat
4.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
An act or feat that creates a false impression, often appearing magical or extraordinary, especially to someone easily impressed.

Examples

A magic deception
For those unfamiliar with the trick, his deception seemed nothing short of real magic.
Some stage artists create their own unique deception to separate themselves from traditional magicians.
The magician's deception left the audience completely mystified and in awe.
