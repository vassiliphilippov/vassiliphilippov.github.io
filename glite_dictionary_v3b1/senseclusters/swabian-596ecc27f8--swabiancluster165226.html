Swabian

noun
German dialect group
Pronunciation: / ˈsweɪbiən / (AmE), / ˈswɑːbiən / (BrE)
a group of Alemannic German dialects spoken in the Swabia region of southwestern Germany and nearby areas

Examples

Swabian dialect
He learned to speak Swabian after moving to Stuttgart for work.
The film features characters who converse in the local Swabian dialect.
Many older residents still use Swabian in their daily conversations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Alemannic - a group of High German dialects spoken in southwestern Germany, Switzerland, and Alsace
  • antonym: Bavarian - a group of Upper German dialects spoken in Bavaria and Austria
