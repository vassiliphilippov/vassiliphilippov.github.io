sleeper

noun
rail car with beds for sleeping
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpɚ / (AmE), / ˈsliːpə / (BrE)
a railway passenger car equipped with beds or compartments for sleeping during overnight travel

Examples

Sleeper train car
The sleeper was surprisingly comfortable, with fresh linens and privacy curtains.
Travelers often prefer the sleeper on long routes to get a good night's rest.
We booked a sleeper for the overnight journey from Paris to Venice.
