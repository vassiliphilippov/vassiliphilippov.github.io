tanner

noun
person who tans animal skins into leather
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtænər / (AmE), / ˈtanə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to tan animal skins and hides to make leather

Examples

Skilled tanner
After years as a tanner, he became very knowledgeable about different types of hides.
His grandfather was a skilled tanner who supplied leather to local shoemakers.
The smell from the tanner's workshop was strong but familiar in the village.
