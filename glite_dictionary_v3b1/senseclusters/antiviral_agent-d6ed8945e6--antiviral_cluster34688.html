antiviral agent

noun
drug that destroys or inhibits viruses
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.taɪˈvaɪ.rəl ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈvaɪ.rəl ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (BrE)
A drug or substance that inhibits the growth of viruses or destroys them.

Examples

A antiviral agent drug
HIV is treated with a combination of antiviral agents to limit the virus's growth.
New research focuses on developing more effective antiviral agent therapies.
The doctor prescribed an antiviral agent to help her recover from the infection.
