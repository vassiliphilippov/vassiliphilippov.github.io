incontrovertibly

adverb
undeniably; unquestionably
9.3 7.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.kən.trə.ˈvɜːr.tə.bliː/ (AmE), /ɪn.kɒn.trə.ˈvɜː.tɪ.bliː/ (BrE)
In a way that cannot be denied or disputed; undeniably or unquestionably.

Examples

Proven incontrovertibly
Her findings have been incontrovertibly proven through multiple independent studies.
The climate is incontrovertibly changing, according to scientific research.
The evidence presented was incontrovertibly in support of the defendant's innocence.
