choristoma

noun
normal tissue in wrong place
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈrɪstəˌmə / (AmE), / kɒˈrɪstəˌmə / (BrE)
a mass of normal tissue found in an abnormal location within the body

Examples

A choristoma mass
The surgeon removed a choristoma from the patient's eye during the procedure.
A choristoma can sometimes be mistaken for a tumor on medical scans.
After the biopsy, the growth was identified as a choristoma rather than a malignant mass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hamartoma - a benign growth made of an abnormal mixture of cells and tissues
  • synonym: neoplasm - an abnormal mass of tissue, such as a tumor
