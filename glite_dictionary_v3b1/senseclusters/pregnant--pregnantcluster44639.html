pregnant

adjective
having offspring developing inside the body
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛɡnənt / (AmE), / ˈprɛɡnənt / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) having a child or young developing inside the womb; carrying developing offspring within the body.

Examples

A pregnant woman
During her pregnant months, she made sure to eat a healthy diet.
She was delighted to learn that she was pregnant with her first child.
The veterinarian confirmed that the cat was pregnant and would soon give birth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barren - unable to produce offspring
  • synonym: expectant - (of a woman) expecting a baby; pregnant
