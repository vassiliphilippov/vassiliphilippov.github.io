pocketbook

noun
small handbag or purse (esp. for women)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑk.ɪtˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɒk.ɪtˌbʊk / (BrE)
a small bag or purse used for carrying money and small personal items, especially by women

Examples

A leather pocketbook
Emily's pocketbook was full of receipts and loose change.
He bought his mother a new pocketbook for her birthday.
She rummaged through her pocketbook searching for her car keys.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handbag - a bag carried by women to hold personal items
  • synonym: purse - a small bag for carrying money, especially by a woman
