Maker

noun
God as the creator
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
A name for God, especially as creator of the world, in Judeo-Christian belief.

Examples

The Maker of heaven
Many hymns refer to God as the Maker of heaven and earth.
She prayed and gave thanks to her Maker every morning.
The belief in a single Maker is central to many faiths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Creator - God regarded as the creator of the universe
  • synonym: Lord - Another title used for God in Christian and Judaic belief
