sago

noun
starch from palms, used in food
4.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪ.ɡoʊ/ /; / /ˈseɪ.ɡəʊ/ (AmE), /ˈseɪ.ɡəʊ/ (BrE)

Examples

Sago pearls

The textile industry sometimes uses sago as a natural stiffener.
