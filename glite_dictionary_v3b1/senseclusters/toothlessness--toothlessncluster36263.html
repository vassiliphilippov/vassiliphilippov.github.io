toothlessness

noun
the condition of having no teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈtuθˌlɪsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtuːθləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state of having no teeth; the condition of being toothless.

Examples

Complete toothlessness

Some animals adapt remarkably well to toothlessness by altering their diet.
The old man's toothlessness was evident every time he smiled.
