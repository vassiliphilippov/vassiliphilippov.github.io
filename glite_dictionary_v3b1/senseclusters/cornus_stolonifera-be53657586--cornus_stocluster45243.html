Cornus stolonifera

noun
North American shrub with reddish twigs, white flowers
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnəs stoʊˌlɒnəˈfɛrə / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnəs stɒləˈnɪfərə / (BrE)
A common North American shrub with reddish-purple twigs and white flowers, belonging to the dogwood family.

Examples

Cornus stolonifera shrub
For landscaping, Cornus stolonifera is valued for its vibrant twigs and attractive white blossoms.

Wildlife often seek shelter in the dense thickets formed by Cornus stolonifera.
