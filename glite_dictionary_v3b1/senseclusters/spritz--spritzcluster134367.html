spritz

noun
a quick squirt of liquid
Pronunciation: / sprɪts / (AmE), / sprɪts / (BrE)
A quick, light squirt or spray of liquid, especially carbonated water.

Examples

A spritz of perfume
A quick spritz of perfume filled the room with fragrance.
She gave the plants a spritz of water to keep them fresh.
The bartender finished the cocktail with a spritz of soda.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: mist - tiny droplets of liquid dispersed in the air
  • synonym: spray - liquid dispersed in fine drops
