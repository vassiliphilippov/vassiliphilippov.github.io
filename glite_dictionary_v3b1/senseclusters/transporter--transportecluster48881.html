transporter

noun
a conveyor belt for moving objects
Pronunciation: / trænˈspɔrtər / (AmE), / trænˈspɔːtə / (BrE)
a machine or device, such as a conveyor belt or crane, used for moving objects from one place to another

Examples

Conveyor transporter
Safety guards must be installed on every transporter to prevent accidents.
The boxes were carried down the assembly line by a transporter.
Workers loaded the packages onto the transporter for sorting.
A malfunction in the transporter delayed the unloading of cargo on the pier.
Modern transporters are capable of lifting much heavier loads than earlier models.
The dock workers operated the transporter to move heavy containers from the ship to the warehouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conveyor belt - a continuous moving band used for transporting objects from one place to another
  • synonym: crane - a machine for lifting and moving heavy objects
