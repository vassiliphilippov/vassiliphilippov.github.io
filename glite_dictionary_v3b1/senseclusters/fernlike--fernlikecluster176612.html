fernlike

adjective
resembling a fern, esp. in leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfəːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling a fern, especially in the shape or arrangement of leaves.

Examples

Fernlike leaves
Many houseplants have fernlike foliage, adding a touch of greenery to the room.
She admired the fernlike pattern etched into the old glass window.
The delicate, fernlike leaves create an airy canopy over the garden path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ferny - having characteristics similar to a fern
