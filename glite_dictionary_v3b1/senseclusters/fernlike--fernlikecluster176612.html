Examples

Fernlike leaves

Synonyms

  • fernleaf
    5.3 0.00/m
    fernleaf describes leaves that are finely divided and shaped like true fern leaves, while fernlike means a general resemblance to a fern.
  • ferny
    6.1 0.00/m
    ferny describes something that looks like or is covered with ferns, while fernlike stresses resemblance especially in leaf shape or arrangement.
