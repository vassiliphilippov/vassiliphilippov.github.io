charpoy

noun
South Asian woven bed
Pronunciation: / tʃɑːrˈpɔɪ /; / ˈtʃɑːrpɔɪ / (AmE), / tʃɑːˈpɔɪ /; / ˈtʃɑːpɔɪ / (BrE)
a traditional South Asian bed or bench with a wooden frame and woven surface, used for sitting or sleeping
