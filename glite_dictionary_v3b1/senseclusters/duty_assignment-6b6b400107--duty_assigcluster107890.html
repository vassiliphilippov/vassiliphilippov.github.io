duty assignment

noun
specific assigned task, often in the military
Pronunciation: / ˈduːti əˌsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˈdjuːti əˌsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a specific task or responsibility officially given to someone, especially in the armed forces

Examples

A duty assignment order
Each soldier received a duty assignment based on their experience and skills.
His new duty assignment required him to relocate to an overseas base.
She was nervous before her first duty assignment in the Marine Corps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignment - a task or piece of work allocated to someone as part of a job or course of study
