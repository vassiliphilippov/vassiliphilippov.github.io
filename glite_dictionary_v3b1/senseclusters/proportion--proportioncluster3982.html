proportion

verb
give harmonious proportions to
5.8 4.2
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈpɔːr.ʃən/ (AmE), /prə.ˈpɔː.ʃən/ (BrE)
to arrange or adjust the size, amount, or relationship of parts so they are balanced or harmonious

Examples

To proportion the parts
Every element in the sculpture was proportioned to create a sense of visual harmony.
She proportioned the garden paths to match the size of the surrounding lawns.
The architect skillfully proportioned the windows to complement the facade.
She proportioned her time between work, study, and relaxation.
The architect carefully proportioned each room to suit the overall design of the house.
The ingredients must be proportioned according to the number of servings needed.
The architect carefully proportioned the windows to let in just the right amount of light.
She proportioned the ingredients to ensure the cake would rise evenly.
The artist proportioned each figure in the painting to create a sense of harmony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance - to make or keep equal or in the correct proportions
  • synonym: equalize - to make equal or uniform
  • antonym: misalign - to arrange incorrectly or out of proper proportion
