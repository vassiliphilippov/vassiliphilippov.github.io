overzeal

noun
excessive zeal or enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈziːl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈziːl / (BrE)
Excessive zeal or enthusiasm; the state of being excessively eager or fervent about something.

Examples

Civic overzeal
Civic overzeal can be counterproductive when it ignores important regulations.
Her overzeal at work sometimes led to burnout by the end of the week.
The coach's overzeal annoyed some players who preferred a calmer approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overzealousness - the quality of being overzealous; excessive zeal
  • degree lower: zeal - great energy or enthusiasm for a cause or objective
