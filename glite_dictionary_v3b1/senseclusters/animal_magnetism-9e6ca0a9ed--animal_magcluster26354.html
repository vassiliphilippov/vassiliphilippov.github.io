animal magnetism

noun
personal charm; natural charisma
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl ˈmægnəˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈmægnətɪzəm / (BrE)
a natural ability to attract or charm people through one's personality or presence; personal charisma or allure

Examples

Animal magnetism power
Despite his lack of experience, his animal magnetism made him a natural leader.
She used her animal magnetism to win over the crowd during her speech.
The actor's animal magnetism drew everyone's attention the moment he entered the room.

  • synonym: charisma - compelling attractiveness or charm that can inspire devotion in others
  • antonym: repulsion - a feeling of intense distaste or disgust, the opposite of attraction
