Bullock's oriole

noun
western North American songbird species
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlərks ˈɔriˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʊləkts ˈɔːrɪəʊl / (BrE)
A brightly colored songbird (Icterus bullockii) found in western North America, recognized as a subspecies of the northern oriole.

Examples

Bright Bullock's oriole
During spring migration, many birdwatchers hope to catch a glimpse of a Bullock's oriole.
The Bullock's oriole builds its hanging nest in tall willow trees near streams.
We spotted a brilliant Bullock's oriole perched on the cottonwood branch.

  • synonym: northern oriole - A North American bird species including Bullock's oriole and Baltimore oriole as subspecies
