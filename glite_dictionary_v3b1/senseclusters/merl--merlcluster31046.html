merl

noun
European blackbird (species of thrush)
Pronunciation: / mɜrl / (AmE), / mɜːl / (BrE)
an old word for the common blackbird of Europe, a species of thrush with all-black plumage

Examples

The merl bird
Local legends celebrated the glossy plumage of the merl.
Once common in old poetry, the word merl now rarely appears in everyday conversation.
The merl sang sweetly in the early morning garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackbird - common European black thrush, Turdus merula
