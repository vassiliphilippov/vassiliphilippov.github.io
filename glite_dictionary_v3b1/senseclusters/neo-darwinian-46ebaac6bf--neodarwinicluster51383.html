neo-Darwinian

adjective
relating to modern Darwinian evolutionary theory
7.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.oʊ dɑːr.ˈwɪn.ɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈniː.əʊ dɑː.ˈwɪn.ɪ.ən/ (BrE)
Relating to the modern version of Darwin's theory of evolution, which incorporates genetics and population biology.

Examples

Neo-Darwinian theory
His research criticized some assumptions of neo-Darwinian theory.
Many scientists support the neo-Darwinian synthesis of evolution and genetics.
The neo-Darwinian perspective has influenced much of modern biology.
