weighty

adjective
excessively fat (person)
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪti / (AmE), / ˈweɪti / (BrE)
(of a person) having excessive weight; very fat.

Examples

Weighty person
Despite being weighty, he remained active and enjoyed playing soccer.
Several weighty children attended the clinic for nutritional advice.
The doctor advised the weighty man to consider a healthier diet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obese - very fat; overweight to a medically significant degree
  • antonym: slim - thin, especially in an attractive or healthy way (of a person)
