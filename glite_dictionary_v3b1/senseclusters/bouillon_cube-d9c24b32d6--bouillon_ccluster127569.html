bouillon cube

noun
cube of concentrated soup stock
3.0 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀêj…ën ÀåkjuÀêb / (AmE), / Ààb äj…ín ÀåkjuÀêb / (BrE)
a small cube of dehydrated concentrated meat or vegetable stock, used to make broth or add flavor to soups and sauces

Examples

A bouillon cube block
If you don't have fresh stock, you can dissolve a bouillon cube in hot water instead.
She added a bouillon cube to the pot to enhance the soup's flavor.
The recipe calls for one bouillon cube crumbled into the sauce.
