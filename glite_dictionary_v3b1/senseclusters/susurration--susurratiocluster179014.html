Synonyms

  • susurrus
    9.9 0.02/m
    susurrus is a soft, poetic murmuring of leaves or voices, while susurration is the more general name for that gentle sound.
  • whisper
    2.0 4/m
    whisper is a very quiet human voice or soft rustle used in speech, while susurration suggests many soft, continuous sounds.
  • murmuration
    9.6 0.00/m
    murmuration is a low collective murmur often from people speaking softly, while susurration often describes wind or leaf rustling.
  • mussitation
    9.9 0.00/m
