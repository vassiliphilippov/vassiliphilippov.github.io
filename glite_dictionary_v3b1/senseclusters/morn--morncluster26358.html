morn

noun
morning; time from dawn to noon (literary)
Pronunciation: / mɔrn / (AmE), / mɔːn / (BrE)
the period of time between dawn and noon; morning, especially in poetic or literary use

Examples

Early morn
A faint breeze greeted us on an early summer morn.
The dew sparkled in the soft light of morn.
They set out on their journey at first morn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evening - the period of the day from the end of the afternoon to nightfall
  • synonym: morning - the part of the day from dawn to noon
