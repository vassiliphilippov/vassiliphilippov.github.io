weatherwise

adverb
regarding weather
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌwaɪz / (BrE)
with regard to the weather or in terms of weather conditions

Examples

Weatherwise speaking
Weatherwise, it looks like we will have a perfect day for the picnic.
The trip was a success, weatherwise, since it did not rain at all.
Weatherwise, this region is known for its mild winters and cool summers.
