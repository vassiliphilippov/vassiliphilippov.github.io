miscasting

noun
failed spell casting
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
an incorrect or failed act of casting a spell or magical effect, often resulting in unintended or harmful outcomes

Examples

Dangerous miscasting
The wizard's miscasting caused the potion to explode instead of heal.
A single miscasting during the ritual led to unexpected chaos in the room.
Players must be careful, as miscasting can have dangerous consequences in the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casting - the act of performing a spell or magical effect
  • synonym: spell failure - an unsuccessful attempt to cast a spell
