timber

noun
land covered with trees; woodland
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbə / (BrE)
land that is covered with trees and shrubs, especially with reference to its suitability for use as wood; woodland or forested land.

Examples

Dense timber land
Much of the timber in this region has been logged over the past decade.
The company purchased several acres of timber for conservation purposes.
They hiked through dense timber before reaching the open meadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth
  • synonym: woodland - land covered with woods or trees
