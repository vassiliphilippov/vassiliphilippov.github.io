humanitarian

noun
person who promotes human welfare
2.2
Pronunciation: / hjuˌmænɪˈtɛriən /; / hjuˌmænəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / hjuːˌmænɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person who is concerned with or seeks to promote human welfare, especially through charitable actions or social reform

Examples

A humanitarian leader
Many humanitarians dedicate their lives to improving global health and education.
She was recognized as a leading humanitarian for her work with refugees.
The humanitarian organized food drives for families affected by the disaster.
As a humanitarian, she established several initiatives to aid refugees and impoverished children.
Many humanitarians risk their lives to provide food and medicine in conflict zones.
The award honors a humanitarian who has made a significant impact on society through charitable work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefactor - a person who gives money or other help to a person or cause
  • antonym: oppressor - a person who oppresses others, especially through unjust exercise of authority
  • synonym: philanthropist - a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others, especially by generous donation
