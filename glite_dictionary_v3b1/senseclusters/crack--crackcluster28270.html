crack

verb
make a sudden sharp sound
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: / kræk / (AmE), / krak /; / kræk / (BrE)
to make or cause to make a sudden sharp sound, as of breaking, snapping, or striking

Examples

To crack loudly
I heard a branch crack under the weight of the snow.
She likes to crack her knuckles when she's nervous.
Thunder cracked loudly overhead as the storm approached.
A branch cracked loudly under his weight as he walked through the woods.
She accidentally cracked a whip near the horse.
Thunder cracked in the distance, making everyone jump.
