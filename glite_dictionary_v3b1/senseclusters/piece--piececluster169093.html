piece

noun
artistic or literary work
3.0 82/m
Pronunciation: / pis / (AmE), / piÀês / (BrE)
a work of art, music, writing, or performance; a creative composition

Examples

A piece of music
He delivered a dramatic piece that captivated the entire audience.
She composed a stunning piano piece for the competition.
The magazine published a thought-provoking piece on climate change last month.
He received a generous piece of advice from his mentor during the meeting.
The artist produced another striking piece of work for the exhibition.
Winning the competition was truly a remarkable piece of luck for her.
She spent years perfecting her latest piece before exhibiting it to the public.
That piece in the corner is an original painting from the 19th century.
The gallery featured a stunning piece by a contemporary sculptor.

Synonyms

  • work of art
    3.5 8.90/m
    a work of art is a visible creation shown for its artistic value, while a piece is any creative work like music, writing, or performance.
  • composition
    3.5 6.03/m
    composition is a created work of music or writing with clear form, while a piece is a general creative work across many art types.
  • musical composition
    3.8 0.56/m
    musical composition is a written piece of music by a composer, while piece covers many creative forms beyond just music.
  • artwork
    3.0 8.51/m
    artwork is a visual object like a painting or sculpture seen in galleries, while piece can mean music, writing, or performance too.
  • art object
    4.2 1.17/m
    an art object is a physical item judged for artistic value in collections, while piece can also name music, writing, or live performance.
