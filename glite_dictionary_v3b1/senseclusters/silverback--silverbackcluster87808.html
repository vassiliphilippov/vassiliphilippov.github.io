silverback

noun
adult male gorilla with grey back hair
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəbæk / (BrE)
An adult male gorilla with a band of grey or silver hair across its back, typically the dominant member of a group.

Examples

A silverback gorilla
A silverback is responsible for protecting the troop from predators and rival males.
During our safari, we were thrilled to spot a resting silverback among the trees.
The powerful silverback led his family group through the dense rainforest.
