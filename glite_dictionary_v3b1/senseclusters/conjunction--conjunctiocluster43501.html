Synonyms

  • conjunct
    6.8 0.01/m
    conjunct is a word or phrase that links clauses in writing or speech, while conjunction is the common grammatical term for such linking words.
  • connective
    3.6 2/m
    connective is a general linking word used across grammar, while conjunction names the specific class of words that join phrases and clauses.
  • continuative
    8.8 0.00/m
    continuative is a rare term for a linking particle often without inflection, while conjunction is the usual name for linking words.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: coordinating conjunction - a conjunction that joins elements of equal grammatical rank
  • synonym: coordination - the linking of words, phrases, or clauses of equal importance
  • degree higher: subordinating conjunction - a conjunction that introduces a subordinate clause
  • antonym: subordination - the linking of clauses where one is dependent on the other
