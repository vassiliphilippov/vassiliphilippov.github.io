Sylviinae

noun
subfamily of Old World warblers (birds)
Pronunciation: / sɪlˈvaɪəˌni /; / sɪlˈviː.ɪˌniː / (AmE), / sɪlˈvɪɪniː /; / sɪlˈviː.ɪˌniː / (BrE)
a subfamily of small insect-eating birds, known as Old World warblers, found mainly in Eurasia and Africa
