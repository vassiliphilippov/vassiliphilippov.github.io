forte

noun
the strong part of a sword blade near the hilt
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrteɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːteɪ / (BrE)
the stronger part of a sword blade between the hilt and the weaker tip (foible)

Examples

Sword forte
In fencing, striking with the forte gives you greater control over your opponent's weapon.
The blacksmith explained how the forte must be strong to withstand repeated impacts.
The duelist relied on the forte of his blade to parry the heavy blow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foible - the weaker part of a sword blade near the tip
