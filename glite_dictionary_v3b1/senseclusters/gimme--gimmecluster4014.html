gimme

noun
easy win or task
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪmi / (AmE), / ˈɡɪmi / (BrE)
something that is very easy to get, achieve, or win, especially in sports or games

Examples

An easy gimme
The final question was a total gimme and everyone got it right.
That short putt was a gimme for any experienced golfer.
The team considered the first round a gimme because their opponents were much weaker.

Related Terms

  • antonym: challenge - something that is difficult to achieve
  • synonym: easy win - a victory that requires little effort
  • synonym: walkover - an easy or uncontested victory
