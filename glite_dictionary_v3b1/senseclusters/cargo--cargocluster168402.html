Synonyms

  • freight
    3.9 5/m
    freight refers to goods moved for business in large amounts, while cargo is any goods carried by a ship or vehicle.
  • lading
    8.8 0.2/m
    lading names the goods loaded on a ship or truck in legal documents, while cargo means goods carried by a vehicle.
  • shipment
    2.3 8/m
    shipment is a group of goods sent together as one consignment, while cargo is the goods carried by a ship or plane.
  • payload
    6.3 2/m
    payload is the load a vehicle can carry or the useful weight aboard, while cargo is the actual goods being transported.
