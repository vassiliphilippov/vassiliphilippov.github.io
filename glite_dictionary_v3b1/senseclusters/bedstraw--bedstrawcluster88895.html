bedstraw

noun
herbaceous plant of the genus Galium
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌstrɔ / (AmE), / ˈbɛdstrɔː / (BrE)
any of several herbaceous plants of the genus Galium, typically with slender stems and small white or greenish flowers, often found in hedgerows and meadows

Examples

Dried bedstraw plants
Dried bedstraw was once used to stuff mattresses in rural communities.
She identified the wildflower as a type of bedstraw growing near the hedgerow.
The fields were dotted with patches of bedstraw among the tall grasses.
