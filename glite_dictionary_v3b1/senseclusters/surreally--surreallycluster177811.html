surreally

adverb
in a dreamlike way
Pronunciation: / səˈriːəli /; / səˈrɪəli / (AmE), / səˈrɪəli / (BrE)
in a way that is strange, dreamlike, or unreal, as if in a surreal situation

Examples

Look surreally empty
The city streets were surreally empty during the early hours of the morning.
He felt surreally calm as he walked onto the stage for the first time.
The landscape was surreally beautiful, almost like a painting from a dream.
The landscape looked surreally beautiful under the pale moonlight.
He moved surreally through the silent, empty hallways after everyone had left.
The news was delivered so surreally that it took a moment to understand it was real.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bizarrely - in a very strange or unusual way
  • antonym: realistically - in a way that is true to life or reality
  • synonym: strangely - in a way that is unusual or unexpected
