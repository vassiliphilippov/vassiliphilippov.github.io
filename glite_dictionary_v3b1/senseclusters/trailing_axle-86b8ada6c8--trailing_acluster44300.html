trailing axle

noun
axle for unpowered rear wheels on a locomotive
5.1 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtreɪ.lɪŋ ˈæk.səl/ (AmE), /ˈtreɪ.lɪŋ ˈæk.səl/ (BrE)
an axle on a steam locomotive that connects a pair of unpowered wheels situated behind the driving wheels

Steam locomotives with a trailing axle can support a larger firebox for more efficient operation.
