powerlifting

noun
strength training sport
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊərˌlɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəˌlɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport or activity where people try to lift the most weight possible in the squat, bench press, and deadlift
