tunnel

noun
underground passageway
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʌn.əl/ (AmE), /ˈtʌn.əl/ (BrE)
an underground or underwater passageway through or under something, especially for trains, cars, or pedestrians

Examples

A train tunnel
Engineers are working to reinforce the walls of the old railway tunnel.
The new tunnel under the river makes commuting much faster.
We drove through a long tunnel carved into the mountain.
