icaco

noun
tropical American tree with edible fruit
6.3
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈkɑːkəʊ / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Chrysobalanus icaco) bearing edible fruit resembling a plum.

Examples

Wild icaco tree
Several birds were seen perching in the icaco near the riverbank.
She collected icacos to make a traditional jelly.
The icaco is often cultivated for its sweet, plumlike fruit.
