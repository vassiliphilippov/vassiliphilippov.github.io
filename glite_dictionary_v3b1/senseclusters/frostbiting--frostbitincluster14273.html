frostbiting

adjective
extremely cold
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstˌbaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstˌbaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely cold in a way that feels sharply painful, like being bitten by frost

Examples

Frostbiting wind
A frostbiting wind swept the valley, cutting through coats and chilling everyone to the bone.
The hikers struggled against the frostbiting air as they climbed higher up the mountain.
After hours outside in the frostbiting weather, her cheeks were red and numb.
