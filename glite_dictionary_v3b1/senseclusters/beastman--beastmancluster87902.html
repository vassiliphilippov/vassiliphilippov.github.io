beastman

noun
human-animal creature
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːstmæn / (AmE), / ˈbiːstmən / (BrE)
a creature that is part human and part animal, commonly appearing in fantasy stories or games

Examples

A beastman in fantasy
The villagers were terrified when a beastman was spotted near the river at dusk.
In the game, you can choose to play as a beastman with special animal abilities.
Legends tell of a powerful beastman who guards the ancient ruins deep in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centaur - a creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse
  • synonym: werewolf - a mythical creature that is part human and part wolf
