sullenly

adverb
in a sullen manner
4.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌl.ən.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsʌl.ən.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that shows irritation or ill humor by a gloomy silence or reserve.

Examples

To answer sullenly
He answered his teacher sullenly, avoiding eye contact.
She sullenly stirred her soup, refusing to join the conversation.
The child sullenly followed his parents after being scolded.
