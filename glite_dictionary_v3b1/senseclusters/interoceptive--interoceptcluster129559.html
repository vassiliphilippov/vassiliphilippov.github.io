interoceptive

adjective
relating to internal bodily sensations
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɛroʊˈsɛptɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɛrəˈsɛptɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving interoception, the perception of sensations from inside the body, such as heartbeat or hunger

Examples

Interoceptive awareness
Meditation practices often aim to increase interoceptive awareness, helping people notice internal bodily signals.
Patients with heightened interoceptive abilities may detect subtle changes in heart rate more easily than others.
Some researchers study interoceptive sensitivity to understand emotional regulation in anxiety disorders.
