euphonous

adjective
pleasant-sounding; harmonious
Pronunciation: / juˈfoʊnəs / (AmE), / juːˈfəʊnəs / (BrE)
(of a sound) pleasing to the ear; harmonious and agreeable in sound.

Examples

A euphonous sound
She prefers the euphonous tones of acoustic music over electronic sounds.
The piano's euphonous notes filled the hall with a sense of calm.
The poet admired the euphonous flow of the Italian language.

  • synonym: melodious - having a pleasant musical sound
