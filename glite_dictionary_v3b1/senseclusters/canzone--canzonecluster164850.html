canzone

noun
Italian lyric poem
Pronunciation: / kænˈzoʊni / (AmE), / kanˈtsəʊneɪ / (BrE)
a type of lyric poem or song, especially prominent in Italian literature, often with a formal structure

Examples

Italian canzone poem
The medieval poet wrote a beautiful canzone dedicated to his beloved.
In her literature class, she analyzed a famous canzone by Dante.
The composer set the words of a canzone to music for the choir.
