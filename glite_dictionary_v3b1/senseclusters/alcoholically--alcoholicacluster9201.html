alcoholically

adverb
in an alcohol-related way
Pronunciation: / ˌælkəˈhɑlɪkli / (AmE), / ˌælkəˈhɒlɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to alcohol or is characteristic of alcoholism

Examples

Laugh alcoholically
He laughed alcoholically, his words slurring after several drinks.
The room smelled alcoholically after the party ended.
She spoke alcoholically, revealing her struggle with addiction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcoholic - relating to or containing alcohol
  • antonym: soberly - in a way that is not affected by alcohol
