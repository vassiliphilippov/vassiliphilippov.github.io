turn over

verb
reverse or flip (an object)

Pronunciation: / t…ùn Àào äv…ö / (AmE), / t…úÀên Àà…ô äv…ô / (BrE)
to flip, rotate, or move something so that another side or surface is facing up or visible

Synonyms

    2.5 1.69/m
    flip over turns an object quickly so its other side faces down or up, while turn over means moving something to show another side.
  • flip
    1.8 23/m
    flip moves or tosses something quickly with a quick flick, while turn over means rotating an item so another surface becomes visible.
  • overturn
    4.1 2.52/m
    overturn turns something upside down or on its side, often by force, while turn over means moving an item so another side becomes visible.
  • roll over
    1.4 3.38/m
    roll over turns an object or body by rolling it along its length, while turn over means flipping or rotating to show another side.

  • synonym: roll over - to turn onto one's other side or from front to back, especially by rolling
  • synonym: tip over - to fall or make something fall onto its side or upside down
  • antonym: upright - in a vertical or straight-up position
