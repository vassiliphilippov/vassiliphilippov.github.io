turn over

verb
reverse or flip (an object)
1.6 2.2
Pronunciation: /tɜːrn ˈoʊ.vər/ (AmE), /tɜːn ˈəʊ.və/ (BrE)
to flip, rotate, or move something so that another side or surface is facing up or visible

Examples

To turn over a pancake
Carefully turn over the pancake to cook the other side.
Please turn over the document and sign the back page.
The gardener turned over the soil with a shovel.
He had to turn over the pancake to cook both sides evenly.
The turtle struggled to turn over after landing on its back.
When you lie in bed, remember to turn over every once in a while to stay comfortable.
Be careful not to turn over the frying pan while cooking.
She accidentally turned over the cup and spilled water everywhere.
The wind was so strong that it turned over several garden chairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flip - to turn something over quickly to show a different side
  • synonym: flip over - to turn something over quickly, especially with a short movement
  • synonym: overturn - to cause to turn upside down or fall over
  • synonym: roll over - to turn onto one's other side or from front to back, especially by rolling
  • synonym: tip over - to fall or make something fall onto its side or upside down
  • antonym: upright - in a vertical or straight-up position
