wise guy

noun
man who makes sarcastic or impudent remarks
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪz ɡaɪ /; / ˈwaɪz ˌɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz ɡaɪ /; / ˈwaɪz ˌɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who makes clever, sarcastic, or impudent remarks, often trying to appear smart or witty
