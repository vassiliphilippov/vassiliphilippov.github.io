emphysematous

(medicine) containing or characterized by gas produced within tissues or an organ, typically because of infection by gas-forming bacteria.

Examples

Emphysematous infection
The surgeon diagnosed emphysematous cholecystitis and scheduled emergency surgery to remove the gallbladder.
CT scans showed multiple emphysematous pockets in the kidney consistent with a gas-forming infection.
Because she was diabetic, doctors suspected an emphysematous urinary tract infection and started intravenous antibiotics.
