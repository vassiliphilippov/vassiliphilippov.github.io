Ryukyu

proper noun
Japanese island group
Pronunciation: / riˈjuːkjuː / (AmE), / riˈjuːkjuː / (BrE)
a group of islands in southern Japan known for their distinct culture, language, and history

Examples

Ryukyu Islands

Many tourists visit Ryukyu to experience its beautiful beaches and cultural festivals.
The traditional music of Ryukyu features distinctive instruments and melodies.
