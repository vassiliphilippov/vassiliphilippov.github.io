coolheaded

adjective
calm and self-controlled under pressure
5.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkuːl.hed.ɪd/ /; / /ˈkuːl.hed.əd/ (AmE), /ˈkuːl.hed.ɪd/ /; / /ˈkuːl.hed.əd/ (BrE)

Examples
