lose oneself

verb
become deeply absorbed
5.1 0.98/m
Pronunciation: / luÀêz Ààw ånÀås…õlf / (AmE), / luÀêz Ààw ånÀås…õlf / (BrE)
to become deeply absorbed in something so as to be unaware of anything else

Examples

To lose oneself in thought
On long walks in the forest, it's easy to lose oneself in thought.
She would often lose oneself in painting, forgetting about everything else.
Whenever he listens to classical music, he can lose oneself for hours at a time.
