bathtub

noun
large container for bathing
1.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbæθ.təb/ /; / /ˈbæθ.tʌb/ (AmE), /ˈbɑːθ.tʌb/ /; / /ˈbæθ.tʊb/ (BrE)
a large container for holding water in which a person may sit or lie to wash their body

Examples

A bathtub full
After a long hike, he soaked in the bathtub to relax his sore muscles.
She filled the bathtub with warm water and added some bubbles.
The children splashed happily in the bathtub during their evening bath.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shower - a device for bathing in which water falls on the body as a spray
