gruff

verb
to speak harshly
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌf / (AmE), / ɡrʌf / (BrE)
to speak in a rough, harsh, or unfriendly voice

Examples

To gruff a reply
He gruffed a reply without looking up from his newspaper.
When asked about the delay, she gruffed an answer that ended the conversation.
The coach gruffed instructions to the team during the tense final minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: growl - to speak in a low, rough voice
  • antonym: murmur - to speak in a soft, low voice
