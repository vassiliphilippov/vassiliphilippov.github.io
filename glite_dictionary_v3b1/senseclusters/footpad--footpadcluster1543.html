footpad

noun
robber who operates on foot
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌpad / (BrE)
a robber who operates on foot, especially one who assaults and robs travelers on public roads; a highwayman not mounted on horseback

Examples

A footpad robber
Caught by the authorities, the footpad faced severe punishment for his crimes.
Stories of the notorious footpad spread fear throughout the countryside.
The footpad lurked in the shadows, waiting for an unsuspecting traveler to pass by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: highwayman - a robber, typically on horseback, who holds up travelers on a road
