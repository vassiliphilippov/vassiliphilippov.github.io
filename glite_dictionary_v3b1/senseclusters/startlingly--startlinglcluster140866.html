startlingly

adverb
in a surprising or striking way
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrdlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is very surprising or striking

Examples

A startlingly different answer
His answer was startlingly different from everyone else's in the class.
She was startlingly honest during the interview, revealing more than expected.
The painting was startlingly realistic, capturing every detail with vivid clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surprisingly - in a way that causes surprise
