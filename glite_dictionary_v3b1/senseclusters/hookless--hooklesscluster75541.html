hookless

adjective
without hooks
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊkləs / (AmE), / ˈhʊkləs / (BrE)
not having a hook or hooks; designed without hooks.

Examples

Hookless design
The shower curtain uses a hookless design for easy installation and removal.
He preferred hookless zippers on his jackets for a smoother look.
Many modern bags now feature hookless closures for added convenience.
