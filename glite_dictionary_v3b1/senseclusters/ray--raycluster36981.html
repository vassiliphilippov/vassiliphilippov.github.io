ray

noun
straight line extending from a point (math)
8.8
Pronunciation: / reɪ / (AmE), / reɪ / (BrE)
(Mathematics) A straight line that starts at a given point and extends infinitely in one direction.

Examples

In geometry, a ray begins at an endpoint and extends infinitely in one direction.
