gross margin

noun
ratio of gross profit to sales
4.9 0.73/m
Pronunciation: / …°ro äs Ààm…ërd í…ôn / (AmE), / …°r…ô äs Ààm…ëÀêd í…™n / (BrE)
the difference between a company's sales and the cost of goods sold, expressed as a percentage of net sales

Examples

Company gross margin
Our gross margin remains healthy despite increased competition in the market.
Retailers often monitor gross margin to assess product profitability.
The company's gross margin improved significantly after reducing manufacturing costs.

Synonyms

  • gross profit margin
    4.1 0.04/m
    gross profit margin is the standard full term used in reports and textbooks, while gross margin is the shorter, common label.
  • GPM
    6.9 0.33/m
    GPM is an abbreviation used in tables and charts, while gross margin is the full phrase showing the percentage after goods costs.

  • synonym: profit margin - overall percentage of profit on sales
