arriba

adverb
exclamation of encouragement
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɑˈriːbɑ / (AmE), / əˈriːbə / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express excitement, encouragement, or support, especially in Spanish-speaking contexts

Examples

Arriba! Go team
The fans shouted arriba as their team entered the stadium.
With a loud arriba, the crowd encouraged the dancers to perform.
He raised his fist and yelled arriba to celebrate the victory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravo - an exclamation used to show approval or praise
  • synonym: hooray - an exclamation of joy or encouragement
