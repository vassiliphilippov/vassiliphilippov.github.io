holo

noun
holographic image
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a hologram or holographic projection, especially in science fiction or entertainment

Examples

A 3D holo
The sci-fi movie featured a realistic holo of the main character giving a speech.
During the virtual meeting, a holo of the presenter appeared in the center of the room.
Fans were amazed by the holo performance at the music festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hologram - a three-dimensional image created with lasers
  • synonym: projection - an image cast onto a surface
