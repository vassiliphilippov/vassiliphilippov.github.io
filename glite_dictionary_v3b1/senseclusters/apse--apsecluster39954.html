apse

noun
recess at end of church, often domed
9.7
Pronunciation: / æps / (AmE), / aps / (BrE)
a semicircular or polygonal recess, usually domed or vaulted, at the end of a church, typically housing the altar

Examples

Church apse area
During the renovation, the apse was carefully restored to its original design.
Light streamed through the stained glass windows of the apse, illuminating the altar.
The choir sang beautifully from the apse at the eastern end of the cathedral.
