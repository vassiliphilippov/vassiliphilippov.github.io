amicably

adverb
in a friendly or peaceful way
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæmɪkəbli / (AmE), / ˈamɪkəbli / (BrE)
In a friendly and peaceful manner; without anger or hostility.

Examples

To settle amicably
After months of negotiation, both companies resolved the dispute amicably.
Despite their differences, the siblings agreed to divide the inheritance amicably.
The meeting ended amicably, with everyone satisfied with the outcome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hostilely - in a hostile or antagonistic manner
  • synonym: peacefully - in a calm and non-violent manner
