sword fern

noun
a tropical fern with sword-shaped fronds
8.7 5.7
Pronunciation: /sɔːrd fɜːrn/ (AmE), /sɔːd fɜːn/ (BrE)
any of several tropical ferns, especially those with long, narrow, sword-shaped fronds, including the species from which the Boston fern developed
