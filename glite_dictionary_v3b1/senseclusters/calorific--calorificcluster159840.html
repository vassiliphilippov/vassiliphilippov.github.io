calorific

adjective
high in calories
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈrɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæl.əˈrɪf.ɪk / (BrE)
containing or producing a large amount of calories or energy

Examples

Calorific foods
Avocados are a calorific fruit, so eating them in moderation is recommended.
The nutritionist warned that many snacks are surprisingly calorific despite their small size.
He avoided calorific foods while trying to lose weight for the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low-calorie - containing few calories
  • synonym: nutritious - providing nourishment or energy
