disinfection

noun
destruction of harmful microorganisms
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of cleaning something, especially with a chemical, in order to destroy harmful microorganisms

Examples

Daily disinfection of classrooms
During the outbreak, daily disinfection of classrooms became mandatory.
Proper disinfection of medical equipment is essential to prevent the spread of disease.
The disinfection process includes using a bleach solution on all surfaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contamination - the process of making something impure by exposure to or addition of a poisonous or polluting substance
  • synonym: sterilization - process of making something free from all microorganisms
