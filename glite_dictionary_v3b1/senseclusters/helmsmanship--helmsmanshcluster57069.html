helmsmanship

noun
leadership skill
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlmzmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmzmənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill or ability to direct or guide an organization, group, or course of action, especially in a steady or effective way

Examples

Strong helmsmanship in business
Her steady helmsmanship guided the company through the merger and preserved employee morale.
The team's success was largely due to the coach's helmsmanship during difficult times.
Effective helmsmanship is essential when leading a group through major changes.
