Castilleja miniata

noun
wildflower with crimson or scarlet flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæˌstɪliˈeɪ mɪˈnaɪ.ə.tə / (AmE), / kæˌstɪliˈeɪ mɪˈnaɪ.ətə / (BrE)
A species of wildflower native to western North America, known for its ragged clusters of crimson or scarlet flowers; commonly called the giant red Indian paintbrush.
