Synonyms

  • modernity
    5.8 4.70/m

  • contemporariness
    7.7 0.00/m
    contemporariness describes being of the same time as now in a formal way, while modernness highlights present style and newness.
  • up-to-dateness
    6.7 0.01/m

  • currentness
