polydactyl

adjective
having extra fingers or toes
5.7 9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈdæktəl / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈdæktɪl / (BrE)
having more than the usual number of fingers or toes on a hand or foot.

Examples

A polydactyl hand

Doctors confirmed that the newborn baby was polydactyl, with an extra finger on his left hand.
The polydactyl cat had six toes on each paw, making her quite unique among her littermates.
