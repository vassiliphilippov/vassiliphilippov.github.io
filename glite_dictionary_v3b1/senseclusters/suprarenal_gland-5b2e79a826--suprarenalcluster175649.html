suprarenal gland

noun
adrenal gland above the kidney
9.9
Pronunciation: / suːprəˈriːnəl ɡlænd / (AmE), / sjuːprəˈriːnəl ɡlænd / (BrE)
either of a pair of complex endocrine glands located above each kidney, also known as the adrenal gland

Examples

The suprarenal gland
Damage to the suprarenal gland can result in serious endocrine disorders.
The suprarenal gland is responsible for producing several important hormones, including adrenaline.
The doctor explained that the suprarenal gland is also called the adrenal gland.
