Iris cristata

noun
species of low-growing iris with crested petals
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.rɪs krɪˈsteɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.rɪs krɪˈstɑː.tə / (BrE)
A low-growing iris species native to the northeastern United States, known for its summer flowers and distinctive crested petals.
