Piltdown

noun
scientific hoax
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɪlˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a deliberate hoax or fraudulent discovery, especially in science or archaeology, named after the Piltdown Man forgery

Examples

A scientific Piltdown
The scientist's claims were later exposed as a Piltdown that misled the entire field.
Some critics called the new fossil find a modern Piltdown due to lack of evidence.
After the scandal, the term Piltdown became shorthand for scientific fraud.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discovery - the act of finding or learning something for the first time
  • synonym: forgery - something falsely made or copied to deceive
  • synonym: hoax - a trick or deception, especially a false claim
