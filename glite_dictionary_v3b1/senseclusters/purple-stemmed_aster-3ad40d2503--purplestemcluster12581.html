purple-stemmed aster

noun
a North American wildflower with purple stems
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrpl ˌstɛmd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˌstɛmd ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
