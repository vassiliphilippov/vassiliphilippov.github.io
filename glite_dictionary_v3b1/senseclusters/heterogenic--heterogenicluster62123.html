heterogenic

adjective
genetically diverse
9.1
Pronunciation: /het.ə.rə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /het.ə.roʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (AmE), /het.ə.rəʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /het.ə.rə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (BrE)
composed of parts that differ in genetic origin or show genetic variation within a single organism or population

Examples

Heterogenic genes
The researchers found that the population was highly heterogenic at the genetic level.
A heterogenic tumor can be more difficult to treat due to its varied cell types.
Genetic testing revealed a heterogenic mixture of cells within the tissue sample.
