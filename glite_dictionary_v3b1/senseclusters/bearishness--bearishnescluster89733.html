bearishness

noun
expectation of falling prices
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈber.ɪʃ.nəs/ /; / /ˈbeə.rɪʃ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.rɪʃ.nəs/ (BrE)
a tendency to expect that prices in a financial market will fall or to act with pessimism about market trends

Examples

Market bearishness
His bearishness made him cautious about investing in the stock market this year.
Analysts noted a general sense of bearishness among traders after the disappointing earnings report.
Despite the recent rally, her bearishness remained unchanged due to global economic concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bullishness - a tendency to expect that prices in a market will rise
  • synonym: pessimism - a belief that bad things are likely to happen
