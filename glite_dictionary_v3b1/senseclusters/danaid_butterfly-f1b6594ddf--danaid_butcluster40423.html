danaid butterfly

noun
large tropical butterfly with degenerate forelegs
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ.nə.ɪd ˈbʌt.əˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.neɪ.ɪd ˈbʌt.əˌflaɪ / (BrE)
a large tropical butterfly of the Danaidae family, often having reduced forelegs and a distasteful quality that deters predators

Examples

A danaid butterfly
A danaid butterfly rested on the leaf, its brightly colored wings on display.
Many danaid butterflies have shortened forelegs, which differentiates them from other butterfly families.
The danaid butterfly is known for warning predators with its distasteful qualities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monarch butterfly - a well-known species of danaid butterfly with orange and black wings
