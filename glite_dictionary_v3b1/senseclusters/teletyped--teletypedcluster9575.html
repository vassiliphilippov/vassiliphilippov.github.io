teletyped

adjective
made by teletype
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlitaɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtɛlitaɪpt / (BrE)
produced, printed, or transmitted by a teletype (teleprinter) machine.

Examples

A teletyped message
The teletyped message was delivered to the newsroom within minutes.
He kept a stack of old teletyped reports in his office for reference.
A faded teletyped letter revealed details about the company's early days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handwritten - written by hand rather than by machine
  • synonym: typed - produced using a typewriter or keyboard
