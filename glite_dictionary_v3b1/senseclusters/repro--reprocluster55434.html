repro

noun
copying department
Pronunciation: / ˈriːproʊ / (AmE), / ˈriːprəʊ / (BrE)
a department, service, or process that produces printed or photographic copies of documents, images, or artwork

Examples

Repro department
The marketing team sent the posters to repro for high-quality printing before the event.
If you need extra copies of the report, just ask repro to handle it.
Our office relies on repro to produce all training materials quickly and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copy center - a place where documents are duplicated or printed
  • synonym: printing - the process of producing text and images on paper
