infotainment

noun
in‑car information and entertainment system
4.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.foʊ.ˈteɪn.mənt/ /; / /ɪn.fəʊ.ˈteɪn.mənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.fəʊ.ˈteɪn.mənt/ (BrE)
a vehicle or device system that combines information and entertainment functions, such as navigation, media playback, phone connectivity, and apps.

Examples

Car infotainment system
The rental car's infotainment system offers maps, Bluetooth calling, and music streaming.
Before the trip, she updated her car's infotainment software to enable new apps.
Drivers can connect smartphones to the infotainment display for directions and playlists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in-car entertainment system - a vehicle system for audio, video, and related media features; often overlaps with infotainment functionality
  • synonym: infotainment system - the dashboard hardware and software that provides navigation, media, and connected services in a vehicle
