a person, object, or thing that is observed, studied, or depicted in research, art, or law

Examples

Research subject
She struggled to keep the subject in focus as he moved around the studio.

The company must inform each subject about how their data will be collected and used.
When processing children's information, the organization must obtain consent from the subject or a guardian.
