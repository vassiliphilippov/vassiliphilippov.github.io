looker

noun
observer; attractive person
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkər / (AmE), / ˈlʊkə / (BrE)
a person who looks at or observes something, or informally, an attractive person.

Examples

A looker at art
Several lookers stopped to watch the artist paint in the park.
He is quite a looker, and many people notice him when he enters the room.
The looker at the museum spent hours studying the paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
  • synonym: onlooker - a person who watches an event without taking part
