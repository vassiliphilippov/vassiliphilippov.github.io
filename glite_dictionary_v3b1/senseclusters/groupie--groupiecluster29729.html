groupie

noun
an enthusiastic fan who follows celebrities or musicians
8.1 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgruː.piː/ (AmE), /ˈgruː.piː/ (BrE)
an enthusiastic fan, especially a young woman, who follows celebrities or musicians closely, often traveling to attend their performances

Examples

A groupie fan
He joked that his sister was a total groupie when it came to famous actors.
Many groupies waited backstage after the concert hoping to meet the musicians.
She spent the entire summer traveling as a groupie for her favorite band.
