ludicrous

adjective
ridiculously absurd or laughable
7.9 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈluː.dɪ.krəs/ /; / /ˈluː.də.krəs/ (AmE), /ˈluː.dɪ.krəs/ /; / /ˈljuː.dɪ.krəs/ (BrE)

Examples

A ludicrous idea
Her explanation for being late was so ludicrous that nobody believed her.
