ludicrous

Examples

A ludicrous idea
Her explanation for being late was so ludicrous that nobody believed her.
The idea of wearing a tuxedo to the beach seems utterly ludicrous to me.
They laughed at his ludicrous suggestion of building a bridge from paper.
