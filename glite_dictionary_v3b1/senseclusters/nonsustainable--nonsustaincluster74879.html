nonsustainable

adjective
not able to last
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsəˈsteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsəˈsteɪnəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be maintained or continued over time without causing harm or depletion of resources

Examples

Nonsustainable fishing
The current fishing methods are nonsustainable and could lead to the depletion of fish stocks.
Experts warn that nonsustainable energy use will have serious long-term consequences.
Switching from nonsustainable to sustainable practices is essential for protecting the environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sustainable - able to be maintained or continued over time
  • synonym: unsustainable - not able to be continued or maintained
