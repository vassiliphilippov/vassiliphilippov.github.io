Freon

noun
trade name for refrigerant CFC compounds
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːɑn / (AmE), / ˈfriːɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for any of a group of chlorofluorocarbon (CFC) compounds, or related chemicals, used as refrigerants, solvents, or aerosol propellants.

Examples

Freon gas
Leakage of Freon into the atmosphere has contributed to ozone layer depletion.
Technicians must handle Freon carefully due to its environmental impact and health risks.
The old air conditioner used Freon to cool the room, but newer models use more environmentally friendly substances.
