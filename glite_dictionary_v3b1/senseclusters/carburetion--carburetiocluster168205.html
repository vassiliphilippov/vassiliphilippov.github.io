carburetion

noun
fuel-air mixing process
8.2
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.bjə.ˈiː.ʃən/ (AmE), /kɑː.bjə.ˈiː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of mixing fuel with air in an internal combustion engine to prepare it for combustion

Examples

Proper carburetion process
Proper carburetion is essential for the engine to run smoothly.
Older cars often require adjustments to their carburetion for optimal performance.
Mechanics checked the carburetion system to diagnose the engine's starting problems.
