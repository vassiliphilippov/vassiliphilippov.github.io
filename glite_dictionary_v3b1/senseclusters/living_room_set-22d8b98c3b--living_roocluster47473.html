living room set

noun
set of furniture for a living room
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ruːm sɛt /; / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌruːm sɛt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ruːm sɛt /; / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌruːm sɛt / (BrE)
a matching or coordinated group of furniture pieces for a living room, usually including a sofa and chairs, and sometimes tables
