cow pasture

noun
field where cows graze
5.7
Pronunciation: / kaʊ ˈpæstʃɚ / (AmE), / kaʊ ˈpɑːstʃə / (BrE)
a field or area of grass where cows are kept for grazing

Examples

A cow pasture field
After a rainy spring, the cow pasture was lush and green with new grass.
The herd of cattle was grazing peacefully in the cow pasture behind the farmhouse.
They built a new fence around the cow pasture to keep the animals safe.
