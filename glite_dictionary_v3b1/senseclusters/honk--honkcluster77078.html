honk

noun
loud, harsh sound, like a goose's cry
Pronunciation: / hɑŋk / (AmE), / hɒŋk / (BrE)
a loud, harsh sound, especially the cry of a goose or a sound resembling it

Examples

A loud honk
Every morning, a honk outside the window signaled the arrival of the delivery truck.
The traffic jam was filled with the loud honk of car horns.
We could hear the distant honk of geese flying overhead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toot - a short, sharp sound, especially made by a horn or whistle
