rake

noun
long-handled garden tool with teeth
7.2
Pronunciation: / reɪk / (AmE), / reɪk / (BrE)
a long-handled tool with a row of pointed teeth at one end, used for gathering leaves, grass, or for loosening soil

Examples

A garden rake
After mowing the lawn, she grabbed a rake to clean up the grass clippings.
He used a rake to gather the fallen leaves into a pile.
The gardener loosened the soil around the flowers with a rake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoe - a tool with a thin, flat blade on a long handle, used for weeding or breaking up soil
