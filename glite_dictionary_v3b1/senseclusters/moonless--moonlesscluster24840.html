moonless

adjective
without a visible moon
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmuːn.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈmuːn.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a night or sky) having no moon visible; lacking the presence or light of the moon.

Examples

A moonless night
A moonless sky made the stars seem even brighter.
The campers found it hard to navigate on the moonless night.
The forest felt more mysterious and silent under the moonless sky.
