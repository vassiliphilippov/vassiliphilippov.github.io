-ist

x
person noun suffix
Pronunciation: / ɪst / (AmE), / ɪst / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns meaning a person who practices, is concerned with, or is a specialist in a subject or activity

Examples

Biologist expert
A biologist studies living organisms as part of their profession.
The famous artist displayed her paintings at the gallery.
He became a tourist when he traveled to several countries last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -er - suffix forming nouns for people who do an activity
