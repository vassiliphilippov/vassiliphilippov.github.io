tamp down

verb
press or pack down firmly
8.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /tæmp daʊn/ (AmE), /tæmp daʊn/ (BrE)
to press or pack something down firmly, usually to make it more compact or stable

Examples

To tamp down the soil
After adding the soil, he used his foot to tamp down the earth around the roots.
It's important to tamp down the coffee evenly before brewing an espresso.
She tamped down the trash in the bin to make room for more.
