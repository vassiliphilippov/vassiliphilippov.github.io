fork in the road

noun
a spot where a road splits or a decision point
8.3
Pronunciation: / fɔrk ɪn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / fɔːk ɪn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
a place where a road splits into two directions, or a point at which a critical decision must be made

Examples

A fork in the road
After traveling for miles, they reached a fork in the road and stopped to consult the map.
Graduating from college felt like coming to a fork in the road in her life.
He realized he was at a fork in the road and needed to decide which career to pursue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossroads - a place where two or more roads cross, or a point of decision
