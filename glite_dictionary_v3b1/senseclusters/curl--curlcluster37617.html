curl

noun
spiral or curved shape
Pronunciation: / kɝl / (AmE), / kɜːl / (BrE)
a spiral or curved form, especially one consisting of a series of concentric circles, as seen in leaves, petals, or hair

Examples

A curl in a leaf
Morning dew settled on the curl of the rose petal, making it shimmer in the sunlight.
She admired the delicate curl at the edge of each fern leaf.
The intricate curl of the shell fascinated the scientist studying its structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coil - a length wound in a spiral or sequence of loops
  • synonym: ringlet - a small curl or spiral, especially of hair
  • antonym: straight - having no curves or bends; direct
