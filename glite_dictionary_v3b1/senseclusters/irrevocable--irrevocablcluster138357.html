irrevocable

adjective
unable to be changed or reversed
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛvəkəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛvəkəbl / (BrE)
impossible to change or reverse; unable to be undone or revoked

Examples

An irrevocable decision
Her resignation was irrevocable and shocked her colleagues.
Once the contract is signed, the agreement becomes irrevocable.
The judge issued an irrevocable decision that could not be appealed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: final - not able to be altered or undone, conclusive
  • antonym: reversible - able to be changed back or reversed
