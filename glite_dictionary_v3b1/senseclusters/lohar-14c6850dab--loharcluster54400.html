Lohar

proper noun
South Asian blacksmith group
Pronunciation: / loʊˈhɑːr / (AmE), / ləʊˈhɑː / (BrE)
a member of a traditional blacksmith community found in South Asia, especially in India and Nepal

Examples

Lohar blacksmith community
The Lohar have a long history of crafting tools and weapons in their villages.
Many Lohar families continue to pass down metalworking skills through generations.
During the festival, the Lohar community displayed their traditional ironwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things made of iron
