filled with cant

adjective
full of insincere talk or jargon
Pronunciation: / fɪld wɪð kænt / (AmE), / fɪld wɪð kɑːnt / (BrE)
characterized by excessive insincere or hypocritical talk, especially using jargon or moralistic expressions

Examples

An essay filled with cant
Her essay was so filled with cant that it failed to persuade the readers.
The meeting became unbearable when the discussion was filled with cant and buzzwords.
The politician's promises were filled with cant and empty assurances.

Related Terms
