Satanist

noun
follower of Satanism
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪtənɪst / (AmE), / ˈseɪtənɪst / (BrE)
A person who follows or practices Satanism, or believes in or worships Satan.

Examples

Satanist follower
As a practicing Satanist, she follows the principles outlined by her faith.
Some people mistakenly believe that wearing all black makes someone a Satanist.
The group was accused of having Satanist beliefs, though no evidence was found.
