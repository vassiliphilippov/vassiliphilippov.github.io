neobaroque

noun
modern style inspired by Baroque art or architecture
9.9 9.1
Pronunciation: /niː.oʊ.bə.ˈroʊk/ (AmE), /niː.əʊ.bə.ˈrəʊk/ (BrE)
A style of art or architecture that draws inspiration from or imitates the 17th-century Baroque period, often characterized by elaborate ornamentation and dramatic effects.
