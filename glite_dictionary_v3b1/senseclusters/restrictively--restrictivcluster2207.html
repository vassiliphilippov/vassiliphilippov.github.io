restrictively

adverb
in a limiting or restrictive manner
5.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstrɪktɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈstrɪktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that limits or places restrictions on something; in a restrictive manner.

Examples

Used restrictively in sentences
Some adverbs are used restrictively to limit the meaning of the sentence.
The law was interpreted restrictively by the court, leaving little room for flexibility.
The permit is granted restrictively, so only certain activities are allowed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permissively - in a permissive manner; in a way that allows things rather than restricting
