Stephanotis

noun
floral fragrance note
9.8
Pronunciation: /stef.əˈnoʊ.tɪs/ /; / /stə.fəˈnoʊ.tɪs/ (AmE), /stef.əˈnəʊ.tɪs/ /; / /stə.fəˈnəʊ.tɪs/ (BrE)
(in perfumery) a floral fragrance note or ingredient that reproduces the sweet, white, jasmine-like scent of Stephanotis flowers, used in perfumes and scent compositions.
