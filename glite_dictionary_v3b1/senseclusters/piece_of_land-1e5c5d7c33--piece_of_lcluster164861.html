piece of land

noun
virtual real estate parcel
6.7
Pronunciation: /piːs əv lænd/ /; / /piːs ə lænd/ (AmE), /piːs əv lænd/ /; / /piːs ə lænd/ (BrE)
a unit of virtual real estate in an online world or blockchain-based metaverse platform, purchasable and tradable as a digital parcel and often representing ownership or usage rights within that virtual environment.

Examples

Virtual piece of land
Investors bought a piece of land in a popular virtual world to host events.
She sold her piece of land as an NFT and used the money to fund a startup.
Players competed to own scarce pieces of land near central hubs in the metaverse game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: virtual real estate - digital property in online platforms or metaverses that can be bought, sold, or developed.
