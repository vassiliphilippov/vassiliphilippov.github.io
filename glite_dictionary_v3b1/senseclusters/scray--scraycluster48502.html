scray

noun
stony hillside area
9.9
an old term for a stony or gravelly area, especially on a hillside

Examples

Rocky scray
The sheep grazed along the scray at the edge of the valley.
Loose stones made the scray difficult to cross after the rain.
Ancient paths wound their way up the scray toward the summit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scree - a slope covered with loose stones or rock debris
