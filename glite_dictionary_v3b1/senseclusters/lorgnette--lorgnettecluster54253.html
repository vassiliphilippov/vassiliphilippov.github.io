lorgnette

noun
eyeglasses with a handle
Pronunciation: / lɔrˈnjɛt / (AmE), / lɔːˈnjɛt / (BrE)
A pair of eyeglasses with a handle, held in front of the eyes rather than worn on the face.

Examples

A lorgnette with handle
At the theatre, the elegantly dressed woman raised her lorgnette to get a better view of the stage.
He inherited a silver-handled lorgnette from his great-grandmother.
With a graceful motion, she unfolded her lorgnette and examined the painting closely.
