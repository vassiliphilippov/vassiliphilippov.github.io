kabaka

noun
king of Buganda
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɑːkə / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːkə / (BrE)
the title for the king of the Buganda people in Uganda

Examples

The kabaka of Buganda
The kabaka plays an important role in Buganda's cultural traditions.
Many people gathered to celebrate the coronation of the new kabaka.
The kabaka is respected as a symbol of unity among the Buganda people.
