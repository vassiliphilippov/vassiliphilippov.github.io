unrestored

adjective
not reinstated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈstɔrd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈstɔːd / (BrE)
not returned to a previous official position, right, or legal status after being removed or lost

Examples

Unrestored rights
Despite the court ruling, he remained unrestored to his former office.
The property was left unrestored after years of neglect and legal disputes.
She felt frustrated being unrestored to her previous rights even after the investigation cleared her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinstated - given back a previous job or status
  • antonym: restored - returned to a previous position or condition
