Synonyms

  • cease
    3.6 23/m
    cease means to stop or bring something to an end, while desist often tells someone to stop a forbidden or unwanted action.
  • quit
    1.0 19/m
    quit means to stop a habit or activity in everyday speech, while desist is more formal and used to demand stopping.
  • halt
    2.4 14/m
    halt means to stop movement or progress suddenly, while desist is a formal verb asking someone to stop an action.
  • discontinue
    4.2 4.49/m
    discontinue means to stop providing or continuing something like a service, while desist refers to stopping a disliked or forbidden action.
