budget-zilla

noun
person who causes budget overruns
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌdʒ.ɪt.zɪl.ə/ /; / /bʌdʒ.ɪt.ˈzɪl.ə/ (AmE), /ˈbʌdʒ.ɪt.zɪl.ə/ /; / /bʌdʒ.ɪt.ˈzɪl.ə/ (BrE)
an informal slang term for a person (e.g., a manager or decision-maker) who causes or tolerates extreme budget overruns in a project or organization.

Examples

The budget-zilla manager
By the end of the meeting everyone blamed the budget-zilla for the project's rising costs.
She joked that the new director was a budget-zilla after approving costly design changes.
If we don't curb spending, the finance team warned we might hire another budget-zilla soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: budget-buster - a person or thing that causes a budget to be exceeded
  • antonym: cost-saver - a person or measure that reduces or controls spending; opposite in effect
  • synonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money recklessly or wastefully
