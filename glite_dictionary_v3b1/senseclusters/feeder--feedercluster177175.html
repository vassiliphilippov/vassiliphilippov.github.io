feeder

noun
branch stream flowing into a larger one
Pronunciation: / ˈfidər / (AmE), / ˈfiːdə / (BrE)
a small river or stream that flows into a larger one; a tributary

Examples

Feeder stream
After the storm, the feeder swelled and emptied more water into the main river.
The dam is built at the junction where the feeder joins the primary channel.
This small feeder helps sustain the wetland by supplying fresh water from the hills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tributary - a river or stream flowing into a larger river or lake
