electronic image

noun
digitally stored or processed image
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
an image stored or processed digitally as a two-dimensional array of pixel brightness values

Examples

A electronic image file
Modern cameras capture photographs as electronic images rather than on film.
She attached an electronic image of the document in her email.
The computer processed the electronic image to enhance its clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital image - an image in digital form, usually as pixels
