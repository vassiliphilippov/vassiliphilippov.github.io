oxymoronic

adjective
contradictory in terms
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪməˈrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪməˈrɒnɪk / (BrE)
describing something that combines two opposite or contradictory ideas or terms

Examples

Oxymoronic phrase
The phrase 'bittersweet' is often considered oxymoronic because it joins two opposite ideas.
His oxymoronic statement, 'seriously funny,' made everyone laugh and think at the same time.
Describing the meal as 'awfully good' is another example of an oxymoronic expression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paradoxical - seeming self-contradictory or absurd but possibly true
