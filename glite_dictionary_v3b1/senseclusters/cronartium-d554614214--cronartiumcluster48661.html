Cronartium

noun
a genus of rust fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑrtiəm /; / kroʊˈnɑːrtiəm / (AmE), / krəˈnɑːtiəm /; / krəˈnɑːtɪəm / (BrE)
a genus of rust fungi that produce aecia in raised or swollen sori and teliospores in waxy columns, some species causing diseases in pine trees
