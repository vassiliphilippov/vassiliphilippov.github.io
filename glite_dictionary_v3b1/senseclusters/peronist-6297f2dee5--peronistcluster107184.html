Peronist

noun
Argentine political member
Pronunciation: / pəˈroʊnɪst / (AmE), / pəˈrəʊnɪst / (BrE)
a member or supporter of the Peronist political party or movement in Argentina

Many Peronists gathered in the capital to celebrate the anniversary of Perón's presidency.
