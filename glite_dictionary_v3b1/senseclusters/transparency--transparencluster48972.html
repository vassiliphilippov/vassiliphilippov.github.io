Synonyms

  • transparence
    6.3 0.00/m
    transparence denotes clear, see-through material used in formal or older texts, while transparency is the common modern term.
  • pellucidity
    9.9 0.00/m
    pellucidity means perfect, undistorted clarity that lets you see details through a material, while transparency is general see-through quality.
  • lucency
    8.5 0.00/m
    lucency means letting light show through with a soft shine or translucence, while transparency stresses clear see-through vision.
  • diaphaneity
    9.9 0.00/m
    diaphaneity measures how much light passes through a material in science, while transparency is the everyday idea of see-through quality.
  • clearness
    2.2 0.00/m
    clearness refers to being free of dirt or confusion, often for liquids, while transparency means optical see-through ability.
