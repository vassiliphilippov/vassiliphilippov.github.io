imploring

adjective
expressing an urgent, pleading request
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
showing a strong, pleading, or begging quality; expressing an urgent request

Examples

An imploring look
He spoke in an imploring tone, hoping she would forgive him.
Her imploring eyes silently begged him to help her.
The child's imploring look was impossible to ignore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commanding - expressing authority or giving orders
  • synonym: pleading - expressing earnest entreaty or appeal
