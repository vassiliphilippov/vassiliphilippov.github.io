uncute

adjective
deliberately plain style
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkjuːt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkjuːt / (BrE)
intentionally plain or unfashionable in appearance, deliberately avoiding a cute or attractive style as an aesthetic choice.

Examples

Uncute haircut
Her uncute haircut made a bold statement against traditional beauty standards.
He chose an uncute backpack with a simple design instead of something colorful.
The artist's uncute style challenged the idea that everything must be pretty or decorative.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cute - attractive in a pretty or endearing way
  • synonym: plain - simple and not decorated or attractive
