Synonyms

  • rectifiable

    rectifiable is a formal word for problems that can be put right, while correctable is a general everyday word for the same idea.
  • restorable
    3.9 0.03/m
    restorable means something can be brought back to its original condition, while correctable covers fixing or improving in many ways.
  • reparable
    4.9 0.02/m
    reparable means something can be repaired or mended physically, while correctable also includes non-physical errors or faults.
  • fixable
    2.7 0.37/m
    fixable is a casual word for things you can repair, while correctable is more neutral and used in formal or technical contexts.
  • remediable
    6.6 0.09/m
    remediable describes problems that can be remedied or fixed, while correctable is a broader term for any correctable fault or error.
  • corrigible
    9.5 0.03/m
    corrigible applies to people or habits that can improve, while correctable usually refers to errors or things you can fix.
  • amendable
    6.0 0.03/m
    amendable is used for texts or laws that can be changed, while correctable covers fixing faults or returning things to right condition.
  • revisable
    4.4 0.01/m

  • redressable
