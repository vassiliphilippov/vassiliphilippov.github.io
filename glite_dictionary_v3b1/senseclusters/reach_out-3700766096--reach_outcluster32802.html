reach out

verb
attempt to contact or communicate
1.0
Pronunciation: / ritʃ aʊt / (AmE), / riːtʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to try to communicate with someone, offer help, or make a connection (often in a supportive or friendly way)

Examples

To reach out for help
If you need any assistance, please reach out to our customer service team.
She decided to reach out to her old friend after many years apart.
The organization encourages people to reach out if they are struggling with stress.
During the crisis, many volunteers reached out to help those affected.
She decided to reach out to her old friend after years of silence.
The organization encourages people to reach out if they need support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contact - to communicate with someone, especially by phone or letter
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately not pay attention or respond to someone
