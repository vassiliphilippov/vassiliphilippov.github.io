Perseid

noun
August meteor
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrsiɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsiɪd / (BrE)
a meteor that is part of an annual meteor shower visible in August, appearing to come from the constellation Perseus

Examples

Perseid meteor shower
Every summer, stargazers gather to watch the Perseid meteor shower light up the night sky.
We counted over twenty Perseids in just one hour during the peak of the shower.
The Perseid shower is famous for its bright and frequent meteors each August.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Leonid - a meteor from the Leonid meteor shower, which peaks in November
  • synonym: meteor - a small body from space that burns up upon entering Earth's atmosphere
