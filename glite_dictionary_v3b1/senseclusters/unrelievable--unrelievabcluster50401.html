unrelievable

adjective
cannot be replaced
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈliːvəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈliːvəbl̩ / (BrE)
unable to be relieved from a duty or replaced by someone else; impossible to provide a substitute.

Examples

Unrelievable duty
During the crisis the only surgeon was unrelievable, working non-stop for three days.
The guard's post was so remote that he was unrelievable for the entire shift.
Because of her unique skills, she was considered unrelievable in her position at the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreplaceable - impossible to replace or substitute
  • antonym: replaceable - able to be replaced or substituted
