Synonyms

  • reference
    3.2 37/m
    reference is a more formal act of pointing to a source or fact, while mention is a brief casual nod in speech.
  • allusion
    6.8 4.38/m
    allusion is an indirect, often literary hint at something, while mention is a direct, brief acknowledgment in ordinary speech.
  • passing comment
    3.3 0.07/m
    passing comment is a casual remark made without much thought, while mention is a short, simple reference to someone or something.
  • remark
    2.5 8.97/m
    remark is an explicit observation or opinion often meant to draw attention, while mention is a brief, neutral acknowledgment made in passing.
