mention

noun
brief reference or acknowledgment
2.6 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmen.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈmen.ʃən/ (BrE)
a brief reference or acknowledgment of someone or something, usually in passing

Examples

A brief mention
Each resource used for the project required a proper mention in the bibliography.

The article included a mention of earlier studies that addressed the same topic.
Her award received only a brief mention in the newspaper.
The teacher made special mention of Sarah's hard work during the ceremony.
There was no mention of yesterday's meeting in his report.
