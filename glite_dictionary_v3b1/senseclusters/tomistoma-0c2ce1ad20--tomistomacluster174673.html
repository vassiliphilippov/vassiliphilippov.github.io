Tomistoma

noun
genus of Malayan crocodiles
Pronunciation: / toʊˈmɪstəmə /; / toʊˈmɪstoʊmə / (AmE), / təˈmɪstəmə / (BrE)
a genus of large freshwater crocodilians with long, narrow snouts, native to Southeast Asia (also called the false gharial)
