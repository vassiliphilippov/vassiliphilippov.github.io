prevue

noun
film advertisement; movie trailer
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌvju / (AmE), / ˈpriːvjuː / (BrE)
an advertisement consisting of short scenes from a forthcoming motion picture; a movie trailer

Examples

A prevue trailer
Back in the 1950s, a prevue would excite audiences about the latest Hollywood releases.
The theater showed a prevue of the upcoming summer blockbuster before the main film started.
We arrived early and caught several prevues of movies that looked interesting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preview - an advance showing or advertisement of something, especially a film
  • synonym: trailer - an advertisement consisting of excerpts from a forthcoming film
