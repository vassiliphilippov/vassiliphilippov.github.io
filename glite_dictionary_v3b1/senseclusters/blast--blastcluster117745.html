blast

noun
long, powerful fly ball in baseball
Pronunciation: / blæst / (AmE), / blɑːst / (BrE)
(in baseball) a long high fly ball, typically one hit powerfully and often resulting in a home run.

Examples

The crowd cheered as the batter hit a blast over the center-field fence.
The pitch was caught for a blast deep into left field, nearly clearing the stadium.
