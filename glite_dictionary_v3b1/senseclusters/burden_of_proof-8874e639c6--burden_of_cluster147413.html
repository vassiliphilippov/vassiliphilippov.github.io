burden of proof

noun
legal duty to prove a claim
5.4 2.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ùÀêd…ôn …ôv ÀàpruÀêf / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêdn …ôv ÀàpruÀêf / (BrE)

Examples
