subcutaneous test

noun
a medical skin allergy test by injection
Pronunciation: / sʌb.kjuˈteɪ.ni.əs tɛst / (AmE), / sʌb.kjuˈteɪ.ni.əs tɛst / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which a suspected allergen is injected just beneath the skin to test for an allergic reaction

Examples

A subcutaneous test injection
A subcutaneous test is more likely to elicit a response than simple patch testing.
Patients with multiple allergies may undergo several subcutaneous tests during diagnosis.
The doctor performed a subcutaneous test to identify the cause of her allergic reaction.

  • synonym: skin test - a test in which a substance is applied to or injected into the skin to check for a reaction
