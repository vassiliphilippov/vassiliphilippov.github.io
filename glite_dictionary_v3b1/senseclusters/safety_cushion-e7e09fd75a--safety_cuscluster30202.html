safety cushion

noun
financial reserve for unexpected expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti ˌkʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti ˌkʊʃən / (BrE)
an amount of money kept in reserve as protection against unexpected expenses or financial emergencies

Examples

A safety cushion amount
Having a small safety cushion gave him peace of mind during uncertain times.
Many financial advisors recommend building a safety cushion to cover at least three months of living expenses.
She always keeps a safety cushion in her checking account for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safety net - a reserve or plan to protect against financial hardship
