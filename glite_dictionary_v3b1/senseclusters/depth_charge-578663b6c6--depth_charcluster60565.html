depth charge

noun
underwater bomb for destroying submarines
5.4
Pronunciation: / dɛpθ tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / dɛpθ tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a type of bomb designed to explode at a specific depth underwater, used especially to destroy submarines
