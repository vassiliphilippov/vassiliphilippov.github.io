in Oz

adverb
in a fantastical, limitless place
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɪn ɑz / (AmE), / ɪn ɒz / (BrE)
Existing in an imaginary or fantastical place, often used metaphorically to mean being in a situation where anything is possible or reality is suspended.

Examples

Living in Oz
Sometimes it feels like we're in Oz when everything goes our way.
The whole experience was so surreal that I thought I was in Oz.
When the rules don't apply, it's as if we're living in Oz.
