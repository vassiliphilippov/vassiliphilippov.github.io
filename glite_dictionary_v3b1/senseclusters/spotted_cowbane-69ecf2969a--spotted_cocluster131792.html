spotted cowbane

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈspɑː.tɪd ˈkaʊ.beɪn/ /; / /ˈspɑː.təd ˈkaʊ.beɪn/ (AmE), /ˈspɒ.tɪd ˈkaʊ.beɪn/ /; / /ˈspɒ.təd ˈkaʊ.beɪn/ (BrE)
