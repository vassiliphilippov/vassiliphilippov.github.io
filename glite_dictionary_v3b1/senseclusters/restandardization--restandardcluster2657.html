restandardization

noun
updating standards
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌstændərdaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌstændədaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of creating new or updated standards for something that already has standards

Examples

Restandardization of procedures
The restandardization of testing procedures ensured more accurate results across all laboratories.
After several years, the company began a restandardization of its quality control measures.
International organizations often call for the restandardization of safety guidelines to keep up with new technology.

  • synonym: standardization - the process of establishing standards
