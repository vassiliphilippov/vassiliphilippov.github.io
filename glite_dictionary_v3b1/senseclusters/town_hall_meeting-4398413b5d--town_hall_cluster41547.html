town hall meeting

noun
public community discussion meeting
4.3
Pronunciation: / taʊn hɔl ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / taʊn hɔːl ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting where members of a community gather to discuss issues, concerns, or policies, often involving local officials or public figures

Examples

A town hall meeting debate
A heated debate broke out during the town hall meeting about new development projects.
Community members used the town hall meeting to voice their concerns to local officials.
The mayor answered questions from residents at the town hall meeting last night.
