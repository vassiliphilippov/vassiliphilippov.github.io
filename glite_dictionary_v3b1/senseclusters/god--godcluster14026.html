god

noun
a man of extraordinary or divine qualities
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd / (AmE), / ɡɒd / (BrE)
A man who is greatly admired for his superior abilities or qualities, often compared to a deity by others.

Examples

A sports god
In the world of tech giants, some CEOs are treated like a god by their followers.
The fans worshipped the actor as a god for his incredible talent on stage.
To his teammates, he was a god on the basketball court, performing feats no one else could.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idol - a person who is greatly admired or loved
