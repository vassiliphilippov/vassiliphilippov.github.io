lensless

adjective
without a lens
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnsləs / (AmE), / ˈlɛnsləs / (BrE)
not using or not having a lens, especially in optical devices or imaging techniques

Examples

Lensless camera design
The new camera uses a lensless design to capture images in low light.
Researchers developed a lensless microscope for portable medical diagnostics.
A lensless projector can display images without traditional optical components.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lens - a curved piece of glass or plastic used to focus light
