pine

noun
a type of evergreen coniferous tree
Pronunciation: / paɪn / (AmE), / paɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen coniferous tree with long needle-shaped leaves, belonging to the genus Pinus, often valued for its wood and resin.

Examples

A tall pine tree
The forest was filled with tall, ancient pines that reached towards the sky.
The scent of pine needles reminded her of her childhood home in the mountains.
They used pine wood to build the new cabin because it is both strong and lightweight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fir - another type of coniferous evergreen tree
  • synonym: spruce - a coniferous evergreen tree similar to pine
