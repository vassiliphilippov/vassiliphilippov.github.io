sandy

adjective
pale yellowish-brown (hair color)
Pronunciation: / ˈsændi / (AmE), / ˈsandi / (BrE)
(of hair) pale yellowish to yellowish brown in color.

Examples

Sandy hair
He was known for his sandy locks and bright blue eyes.
Her sandy hair glowed in the afternoon sunlight.
Several young children with sandy hair played by the shoreline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blonde - fair or pale yellow hair color
  • synonym: flaxen - (of hair) pale yellow or golden in color
