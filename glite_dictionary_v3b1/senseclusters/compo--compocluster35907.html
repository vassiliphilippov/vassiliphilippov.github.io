compo

noun
informal compensation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɒmpəʊ / (BrE)
an informal British term for compensation, especially money paid by the government or an employer after injury or loss

Examples

Workplace compo payout
After his accident at work, he received a large sum of compo from his employer.
Many workers applied for compo after the factory closed unexpectedly.
She was relieved when her compo claim was finally approved by the government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compensation - money paid to someone for loss, injury, or work
