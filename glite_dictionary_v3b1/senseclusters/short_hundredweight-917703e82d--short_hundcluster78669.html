short hundredweight

noun
US unit of weight, 100 pounds
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt ˈhʌndrədˌweɪt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈhʌndrədˌweɪt / (BrE)
A unit of weight in the United States equal to 100 pounds (approximately 45.36 kilograms).

Examples

One short hundredweight
One short hundredweight of flour equals 100 pounds in America.
The shipment was measured in short hundredweights to comply with U.S. trade standards.
They ordered five short hundredweights of steel for the project.

Related Terms
