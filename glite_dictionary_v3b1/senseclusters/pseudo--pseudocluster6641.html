pseudo

adjective
not genuine; fake or false
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsjuːdəʊ / (BrE)
Not genuine; appearing to be something but not actually so, often used as a prefix in combination with other words.

Examples

Pseudo science
Many experts dismissed his theory as pseudo science with no real basis in fact.
She wore a pseudo diamond ring that sparkled just like the real thing.
The movie was filled with pseudo philosophical dialogue that sounded deep but meant little.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentic - of undisputed origin; genuine
  • synonym: fake - not genuine; a forgery or imitation
  • antonym: genuine - truly what it is said to be; authentic
