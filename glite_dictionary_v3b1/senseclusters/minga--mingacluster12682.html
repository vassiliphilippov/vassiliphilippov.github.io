minga

noun
communal work event
Pronunciation: / ˈmiŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈmiŋɡə / (BrE)
a communal event where people gather to work together on a shared task or project

Examples

A minga for building
The villagers organized a minga to repair the community center after the storm.
During the minga, everyone contributed food and tools to support the work.
A minga is a tradition that brings the whole community together for a common goal.
The villagers organized a minga to repair the local school after the storm.
During the annual minga, everyone in the community helps clean the public park.
A minga was held to build a new bridge across the river for the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barn raising - a community event where neighbors help build a barn together
  • synonym: volunteering - offering to help others without pay
