Synonyms

  • drop off
    3.0 19/m
    drop off is used for bringing someone or something and leaving them at a place, while put down means removing and leaving from a vehicle.
  • set down
    3.2 0.2/m
    set down is a more formal phrase for removing people or goods from a vehicle, while put down is more everyday.
  • drop
    2.7 30/m
    drop often means let something fall or leave quickly, while put down specifically means removing someone or something from a vehicle.
  • unload
    2.6 6/m
    unload is about taking goods or cargo off a vehicle, while put down also covers removing people as well as things.
