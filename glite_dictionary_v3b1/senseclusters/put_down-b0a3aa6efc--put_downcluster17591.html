put down

verb
remove and leave people or things from a vehicle
Pronunciation: / pʊt daʊn / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn / (BrE)
to remove people or things, especially from a vehicle, and leave them at a particular place

Examples

To put down passengers
Could you put down my suitcase here, please?
The bus will put down passengers at the next stop.
They put down the cargo at the port before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drop off - to leave someone or something at a particular place, especially by car
  • antonym: pick up - to collect someone or something from a particular place
