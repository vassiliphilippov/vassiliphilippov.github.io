Platymiscium trinitatis

noun
tropical South American tree species
Pronunciation: / ˌplætɪˌmɪs.siˈʌm trɪ.nɪˈteɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌplætɪˌmɪs.siˈʌm trɪ.nɪˈteɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A large tropical tree native to Trinidad and Guyana, with odd-pinnate leaves, violet-scented yellow flowers in racemes, and long smooth pods; often cultivated in parks and large gardens.
