basin plug

noun
device for sealing drains in washbasins or bathtubs
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsən plʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪn plʌɡ / (BrE)
A device used to seal the drain of a washbasin, bathtub, or similar container, preventing water from escaping.

Examples

A basin plug stopper
I can't wash the dishes because the basin plug is missing.
Please remember to put the basin plug back after cleaning the sink.
She pulled out the basin plug to let the soapy water drain away.
