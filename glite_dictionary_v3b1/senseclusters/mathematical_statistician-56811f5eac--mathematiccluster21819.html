mathematical statistician

noun
expert in the mathematics of statistics
Pronunciation: / ˌmæθəˈmætɪkəl stəˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæθəˈmætɪkəl stəˈtɪstɪən / (BrE)
a mathematician whose main area of expertise is statistics, especially in its theoretical or mathematical aspects

Examples

Mathematical statisticians study data
Many breakthroughs in probability theory come from mathematical statisticians working at the intersection of mathematics and statistics.
She consulted with a mathematical statistician to develop a robust statistical model for her research.
The university is seeking a mathematical statistician with expertise in theoretical statistics for its faculty.
