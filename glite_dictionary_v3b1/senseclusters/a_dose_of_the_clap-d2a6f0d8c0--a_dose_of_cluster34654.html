a dose of the clap

noun
something extremely unpleasant (esp. unfunny joke)
Pronunciation: / ə doʊs əv ðə klæp / (AmE), / ə dəʊs əv ðə klæp / (BrE)
used to describe something extremely unpleasant or undesirable, especially when referring to humor that is not funny at all

Examples

A dose of the clap joke
Hearing that tired old pun was like getting a dose of the clap—painful and best avoided.
His latest stand-up routine was about as funny as a dose of the clap.
She rolled her eyes and said his jokes always landed like a dose of the clap.
