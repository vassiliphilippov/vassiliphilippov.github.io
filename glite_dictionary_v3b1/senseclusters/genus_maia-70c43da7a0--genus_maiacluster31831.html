genus Maia

noun
type genus of the Majidae crabs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmaɪ.ə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Majidae, comprising a group of crabs found in nearly all parts of the world.

Examples

Genus Maia crabs
Marine biologists have studied the genus Maia to understand crab evolution.
