bedlamite

noun
archaic term for a madman
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdləˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdləmaɪt / (BrE)
(archaic) A madman; a person who is insane or acts in a wildly foolish manner.

Examples

A bedlamite man
In Shakespeare's play, Edgar disguises himself as a bedlamite to evade his enemies.
It was once common to see a bedlamite begging near the gates of old London.
The villagers whispered that the bedlamite wandered the streets at night, raving to the moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill (now offensive or old-fashioned)
  • synonym: madman - a man who is insane or behaves wildly
