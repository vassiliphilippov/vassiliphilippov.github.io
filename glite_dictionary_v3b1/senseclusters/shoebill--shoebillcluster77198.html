shoebill

noun
large African bird with a shoe-shaped bill
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈʃuːbɪl / (BrE)
a large, stork-like bird of central tropical Africa, notable for its broad, shoe-shaped bill

Examples

A shoebill bird
A shoebill can stand motionless for hours while waiting to catch fish.
Birdwatchers traveled to Uganda hoping to spot a rare shoebill in the wetlands.
The shoebill is famous for its massive beak and statuesque posture.
