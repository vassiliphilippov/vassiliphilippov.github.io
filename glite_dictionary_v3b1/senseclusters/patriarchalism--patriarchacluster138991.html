patriarchalism

noun
male-dominated system
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtriˈɑrkəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌpeɪtrɪˈɑːkəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a social system or belief in which men hold primary power and authority

Examples

Patriarchalism in society
The novel critiques the effects of patriarchalism on family life.
Many activists challenge patriarchalism in modern institutions and advocate for gender equality.
Historians have studied how patriarchalism shaped laws and customs in ancient societies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: matriarchalism - a social system or belief in which women hold primary power
  • synonym: patriarchy - a system of society in which men hold the power
