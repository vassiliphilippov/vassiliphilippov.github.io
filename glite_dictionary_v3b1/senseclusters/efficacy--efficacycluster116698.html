Synonyms

  • effectiveness
    3.5 33/m
    effectiveness is the common, everyday word for producing results, while efficacy is a more formal term often used in research or medicine.
  • effectivity
    8.2 0.01/m
    effectivity is a rare, formal synonym for producing a result, while efficacy is the standard formal word in scientific or medical contexts.
  • effectuality
    9.1 0.00/m
    effectuality feels more abstract or old-fashioned as a noun, while efficacy names a practical ability to produce intended results.
  • effectualness
    8.2 0.00/m
    effectualness is an uncommon form focusing on the quality of producing results, while efficacy is the standard formal term for effectiveness.
