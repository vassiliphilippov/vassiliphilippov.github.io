staff member

noun
employee who is part of a staff
2.0
Pronunciation: / stæf ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / stɑːf ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
an employee who belongs to a particular organization or group of workers; a member of the staff

Examples

Every staff member
Every staff member received an invitation to the annual meeting.
If you have any questions, please ask a staff member for assistance.
The new director met with each staff member individually during her first week.
