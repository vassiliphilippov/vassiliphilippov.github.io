bur marigold

noun
plant of the genus Bidens with sticky fruits
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɜr ˈmærɪˌɡoʊld / (AmE), / bɜː ˈmærɪˌɡəʊld / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Bidens, having yellow flowers and prickly fruits that stick to fur and clothing

Examples

Bur marigold plant
Gardeners often remove bur marigold to prevent its spread due to the sticky burs.
Seeds of the bur marigold clung stubbornly to the dog's fur.
The fields were dotted with bright bur marigold after the summer rains.
