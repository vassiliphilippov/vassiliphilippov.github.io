slalom

verb
move in a zigzag, especially on skis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːləm / (AmE), / ˈslɑːləm / (BrE)
to move or race in a winding, zigzag course around obstacles, especially while skiing

Examples

To slalom between poles
During the competition, he managed to slalom through the gates without missing any.
She expertly slalomed down the mountain, weaving between the flags.
The children loved to slalom around the cones on their rollerblades.
