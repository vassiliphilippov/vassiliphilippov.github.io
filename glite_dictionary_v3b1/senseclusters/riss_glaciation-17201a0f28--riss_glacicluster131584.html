Riss glaciation

noun
next-to-last Pleistocene glaciation in the Alps
9.9
Pronunciation: /rɪs ɡleɪ.si.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /rɪs ɡleɪ.si.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
