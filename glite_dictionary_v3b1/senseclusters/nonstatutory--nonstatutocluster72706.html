nonstatutory

adjective
not required by law
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstætʃuˌtɔri / (AmE), / nɒnˈstætjʊtəri / (BrE)
not established, required, or regulated by law or statute

Examples

Nonstatutory benefits
The company offered nonstatutory benefits to its employees, such as free gym memberships.
Participation in the training program is nonstatutory and completely optional.
These guidelines are nonstatutory, so organizations are not legally required to follow them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voluntary - done by choice or without legal obligation
