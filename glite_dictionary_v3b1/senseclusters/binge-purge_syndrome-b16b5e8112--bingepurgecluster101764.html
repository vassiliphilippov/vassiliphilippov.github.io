binge-purge syndrome

noun
an eating disorder involving bingeing and purging
7.1 5.9
Pronunciation: /bɪndʒ pɜːrdʒ ˈsɪn.droʊm/ /; / /ˈbɪndʒ.pɜːrdʒ ˈsɪn.droʊm/ (AmE), /bɪndʒ pɜːdʒ ˈsɪn.drəʊm/ /; / /ˈbɪndʒ.pɜːdʒ ˈsɪn.drəʊm/ (BrE)
an eating disorder characterized by episodes of excessive eating (bingeing) followed by fasting, self-induced vomiting, or other methods to avoid weight gain (purging)

Examples

Binge-purge syndrome treatment
Binge-purge syndrome often requires both medical treatment and psychological support for effective recovery.
After years of struggling with binge-purge syndrome, she finally sought help from a specialist.
Symptoms of binge-purge syndrome include repeated cycles of overeating followed by compensatory behaviors.

  • synonym: bulimia nervosa - an eating disorder involving binge eating and purging, typically by vomiting or using laxatives
