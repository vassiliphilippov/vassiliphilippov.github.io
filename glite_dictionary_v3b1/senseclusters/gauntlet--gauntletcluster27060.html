gauntlet

noun
a glove of armored leather or metal
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔnt.lət / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːnt.lɪt / (BrE)
a strong or protective glove, often made of leather or metal, that covers the hand and sometimes part of the arm

Examples

A metal gauntlet

The knight put on his gauntlet before riding into battle.
Heavy leather gauntlets protected the worker's hands from sparks and heat.
She picked up the gauntlet that had fallen from his armor.
