chlamydeous

adjective
having a calyx and/or corolla (botany)
Pronunciation: / kləˈmɪdiəs / (AmE), / kləˈmɪdiːəs / (BrE)
(botany) Having a perianth or floral envelope consisting of a calyx and/or corolla.
