Guy Fawkes

proper noun
English conspirator in the Gunpowder Plot
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪ ˈfɔks / (AmE), / ɡaɪ ˈfɔːks / (BrE)
(1570–1606) An English conspirator who participated in the Gunpowder Plot of 1605, an attempt to blow up King James I and the Houses of Parliament, and who was executed for his role.

The face of Guy Fawkes has become a symbol for protest movements around the world.
