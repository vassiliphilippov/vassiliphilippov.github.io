yearlong

adjective
lasting for a whole year
Pronunciation: / ˈjɪərˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɪəˌlɒŋ / (BrE)
lasting for a whole year; continuing throughout the year

Examples

A yearlong program
She is enrolled in a yearlong training program.
The museum held a yearlong exhibition on ancient art.
Their yearlong trip around the world inspired many stories.
