psychomotor

adjective
relating to mental and motor activity
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
relating to the relationship between mental activity and physical movement.

Examples

Psychomotor skills
A delay in psychomotor development may indicate a neurological disorder.
Children develop psychomotor abilities through activities like drawing and building blocks.
The doctor assessed her psychomotor skills as part of the neurological examination.
