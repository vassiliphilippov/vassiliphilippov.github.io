community

noun
residential district or neighborhood
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti /; / kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group of people living in the same place or sharing a particular characteristic, such as a neighborhood or people with a common interest
