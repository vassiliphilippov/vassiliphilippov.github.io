Synonyms

  • local community
    2.8 6.43/m
    local community is a group of people who live nearby and share local ties and interests, while community can mean any group with common traits.
  • social group
    2.7 2.91/m
    social group is people who interact and share a common identity, while community can also mean a place-based or interest-based group.
  • collectivity
    6.6 0.45/m
    collectivity is a group seen as a single unit acting together for a shared purpose, while community is a more everyday group of people.
  • social unit
    3.5 0.19/m
    social unit is a small organized part of society, while community is a broader group tied by place or shared interests.
