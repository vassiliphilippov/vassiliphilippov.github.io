unrounded

adjective
not rounded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
not changed by rounding; given or shown with full precision rather than as an approximate value.

Examples

Unrounded number value
The report included the unrounded values to ensure maximum accuracy in the analysis.
Please provide the unrounded total so we can check the exact figures.
For scientific calculations, it is important to use unrounded data whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approximate - close to the actual, but not completely accurate or exact
  • synonym: exact - completely correct and precise, not approximate
  • antonym: rounded - adjusted to the nearest whole number or a specified decimal place
