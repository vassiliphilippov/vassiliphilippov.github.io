Triticum

noun
genus of grasses including wheat
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪtɪkəm / (AmE), / ˈtrɪtɪkəm / (BrE)
a genus of annual cereal grasses, including wheat and its relatives, native to the Mediterranean and widely cultivated in temperate regions

Common wheat, known scientifically as Triticum aestivum, is a staple crop worldwide.
The Triticum genus includes several important cultivated species.
