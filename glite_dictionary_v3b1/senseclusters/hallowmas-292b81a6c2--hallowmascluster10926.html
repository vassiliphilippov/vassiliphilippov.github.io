Hallowmas

noun
Christian feast day, All Saints' Day
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæloʊˌmæs / (AmE), / ˈhæləʊˌmæs / (BrE)
a Christian feast day honoring all the saints, celebrated on November 1st; also called All Saints' Day

Examples

Hallowmas feast day

Many churches hold special services on Hallowmas to honor the saints.
The tradition of Hallowmas dates back to the early centuries of Christianity.
