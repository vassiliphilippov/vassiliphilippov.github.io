fuck up

verb
to mess up or ruin (vulgar)
9.9
Pronunciation: / fʌk ʌp / (AmE), / fʌk ʌp / (BrE)
to make a serious mistake or ruin something; to spoil or damage badly, often through carelessness or incompetence (vulgar, informal)
