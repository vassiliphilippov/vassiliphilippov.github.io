Nigerian

proper noun
person from Nigeria
Pronunciation: / naɪˈdʒɪriən / (AmE), / naɪˈdʒɪəriən / (BrE)
a person from Nigeria or of Nigerian descent

Examples

Famous Nigerian author
The famous writer is a proud Nigerian who often writes about her homeland.
Many Nigerian students attend universities abroad to further their education.
He enjoys cooking traditional Nigerian dishes for his friends and family.
