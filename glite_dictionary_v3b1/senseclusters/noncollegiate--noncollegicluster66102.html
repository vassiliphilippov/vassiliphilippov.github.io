noncollegiate

noun
student without college affiliation
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈlidʒiət / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈlɪdʒiət / (BrE)
a student at a university with a collegiate system who is not a member of any specific college

Examples

University noncollegiate
As a noncollegiate, she attended university events but was not part of any college community.
The university offers special support services for noncollegiates to help them feel included.
He chose to be a noncollegiate because he preferred more independence during his studies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collegiate - a student who is a member of a college within a university
