RSVP

verb
to reply to invitation
Pronunciation: / ˌɑr ɛs viː ˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌɑː ɛs viː ˈpiː / (BrE)
to respond to an invitation by indicating whether or not you will attend

Examples

To RSVP for a party
Please RSVP by Friday if you plan to attend the wedding.
I forgot to RSVP to the birthday party invitation last week.
They asked all guests to RSVP online to confirm their attendance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to not respond or pay attention
  • synonym: respond - to reply or give an answer
