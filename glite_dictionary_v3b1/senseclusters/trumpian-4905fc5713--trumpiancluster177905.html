Trumpian

noun
supporter of Donald Trump
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpiən / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpiən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows Donald Trump, or who displays characteristics associated with him

Examples

A Trumpian supporter
The crowd cheered as a proud Trumpian took the stage to speak.
Many Trumpians attended the event wearing red hats and waving banners.
She was labeled a Trumpian because of her outspoken political views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Trump supporter - a person who supports Donald Trump
  • antonym: opponent - a person who opposes or is against someone or something
