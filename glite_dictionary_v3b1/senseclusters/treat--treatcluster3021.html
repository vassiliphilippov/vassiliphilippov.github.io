treat

verb
give medical care
1.3
Pronunciation: / trit / (AmE), / triːt / (BrE)
to give medical care or attention to a person, animal, or illness, especially in order to cure it

Examples

To treat a patient
Paramedics must treat accident victims as quickly as possible to prevent complications.
She was treated in hospital for minor burns after the kitchen fire.
The physician decided to treat the infection with a new antibiotic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cure - to make a person or illness healthy again
  • synonym: heal - to make or become healthy again, especially after injury
