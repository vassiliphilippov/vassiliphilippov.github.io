Scandinavian Peninsula

noun
peninsula in northern Europe (Norway and Sweden)
3.9
Pronunciation: / skænˈdeɪviən pəˈnɪnsələ / (AmE), / skænˈdeɪviən pəˈnɪnsjələ / (BrE)
a large peninsula in northern Europe that is mainly occupied by Norway and Sweden

Examples

The Scandinavian Peninsula region
Geographically, the Scandinavian Peninsula covers most of Norway and Sweden, but not Denmark.
Many travelers dream of visiting the Scandinavian Peninsula to see its fjords, forests, and mountainous landscapes.
The Scandinavian Peninsula is surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean, the Baltic Sea, and the Barents Sea.
