noncardiac

adjective
not involving the heart
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkɑrdiæk / (AmE), / nɒnˈkɑːdiæk / (BrE)
not related to the heart or to heart disease

Examples

Noncardiac chest pain
The patient was relieved to learn his chest pain was noncardiac in nature.
Doctors ruled out any noncardiac causes before focusing on the heart.
She experienced noncardiac symptoms that were unrelated to her previous heart condition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cardiac - relating to the heart
