llama

noun
South American pack animal
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːmə /; / ˈlɑː.mə / (AmE), / ˈlɑːmə /; / ˈlɑː.mə / (BrE)
a domesticated long-necked mammal from South America, related to the camel, kept for carrying loads and for its wool

Examples

A llama herd
Many tourists were delighted to see a herd of llamas grazing in the Andes.
The farmer loaded the supplies onto the llama before heading up the mountain.
Wool from the llama is used to make warm clothing in Peru.
Farmers raise llamas for their wool and gentle temperament.
She saw a llama grazing peacefully in the field during her trip to Peru.
The llama is commonly used as a pack animal in the Andes Mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alpaca - A smaller South American mammal similar to the llama, valued for its wool
  • synonym: camel - large domesticated mammal with a hump, related to the llama
