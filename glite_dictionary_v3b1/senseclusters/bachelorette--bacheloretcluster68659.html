bachelorette

noun
young unmarried woman living alone
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbætʃəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌbatʃəˈlɛt / (BrE)
A young unmarried woman, especially one who lives on her own.

Examples

Independent bachelorette
After graduating, she enjoyed being a bachelorette in the city, exploring new places and meeting people.
She hosted a bachelorette party for her friends before her wedding.
The apartment complex is popular among bachelorettes who value their independence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bachelor - an unmarried man
  • synonym: spinster - an older unmarried woman
