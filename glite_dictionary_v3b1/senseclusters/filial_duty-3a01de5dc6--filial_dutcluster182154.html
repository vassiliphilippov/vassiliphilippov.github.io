filial duty

noun
child's duty to parents
7.2 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪl.jəl ˈduː.tiː/ /; / /ˈfɪl.iː.əl ˈduː.tiː/ /; / /ˈfɪl.jəl ˈdjuː.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈfɪl.iː.əl ˈdjuː.tiː/ /; / /ˈfɪl.jəl ˈdjuː.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples
