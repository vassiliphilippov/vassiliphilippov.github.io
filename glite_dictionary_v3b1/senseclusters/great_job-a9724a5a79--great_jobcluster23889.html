great job

noun
praise for excellent performance
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt dʒɑb / (AmE), / ɡreɪt dʒɒb / (BrE)
used to praise someone for performing a task or achieving something with excellent results

Examples

A great job done
The teacher told the student he did a great job on his science project.
They finished painting the house and really did a great job with it.
You did a great job organizing the event; everyone had a wonderful time.

  • synonym: well done - expression of praise for good performance
