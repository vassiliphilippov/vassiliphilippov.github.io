nonsaturated

adjective
not vivid in color
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not fully intense or vivid in color; having low chroma or purity.

Examples

Nonsaturated color tone
The artist preferred nonsaturated tones to create a gentle, peaceful mood in her paintings.
A nonsaturated green was used for the background to avoid overpowering the main subject.
Interior designers often recommend nonsaturated colors for a more relaxed and subtle look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muted - not bright or intense in color
  • antonym: saturated - having strong, pure, and intense color
  • antonym: vivid - very bright, clear, or intense in color
