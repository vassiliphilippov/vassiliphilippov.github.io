hemostasis

noun
stopping of bleeding (surgical/medical)
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈsteɪsɪs /; / hiːmoʊˈsteɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈsteɪsɪs /; / hɪˌmɒsˈteɪsɪs / (BrE)
The stopping of bleeding or the flow of blood, especially by surgical procedures or by natural processes.
