hemostasis

noun
stopping of bleeding (surgical/medical)
9.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /hiː.mə.ˈsteɪ.sɪs/ /; / /hiː.moʊ.ˈsteɪ.sɪs/ (AmE), /hiː.mə.ˈsteɪ.sɪs/ /; / /hiː.məʊ.ˈsteɪ.sɪs/ (BrE)
The stopping of bleeding or the flow of blood, especially by surgical procedures or by natural processes.

Examples

Medication can sometimes be used to aid hemostasis in wounds.
