heightener

noun
growth-promoting agent
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪtənər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪtənə / (BrE)
a substance or treatment that promotes increased growth in height, especially in plants or young animals

Examples

Plant heightener
The gardener applied a heightener to the seedlings to encourage stronger, taller stems this season.
Some farmers use a heightener to help their crops grow more quickly during the early stages.
The new formula acts as a heightener for young plants, resulting in noticeably taller growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to promote plant growth
  • synonym: growth promoter - an agent that encourages growth in living organisms
  • antonym: inhibitor - a substance that slows or prevents growth
