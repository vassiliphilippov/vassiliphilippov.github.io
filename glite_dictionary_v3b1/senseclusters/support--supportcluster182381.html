support

noun
provision of money or necessities
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
money or material help given to provide for someone's needs or to make something possible

Examples

Financial support
She relied on her brother's support after losing her job.
The government provides financial support to families in need.
Without parental support, many students struggle to pay for college.
After losing his job, he relied on government support to cover his expenses.
Many single parents struggle to provide adequate support for their families.
She sued her ex-husband for child support after the divorce.
Government support enabled the museum to host the new exhibition.
The charity relies on support from private donors to continue its work.
Without financial support, many talented students would not be able to attend university.
