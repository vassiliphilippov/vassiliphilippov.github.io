supernatural

noun
forces or phenomena beyond nature
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.rəl / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.rəl / (BrE)
the realm of forces, events, or beings that are believed to exist beyond or outside the laws of nature

Examples

The supernatural in stories
Many horror movies feature the supernatural as a central theme.
She has always been fascinated by stories involving the supernatural.
The locals believed the old mansion was touched by the supernatural.
