yesterday

adverb
recently; in the recent past
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛstərˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛstədeɪ / (BrE)
Used to refer to a time in the recent past; only a short time ago.

Examples

Just yesterday
He talked about his trip as if it had happened just yesterday.
People seem to forget the scandal that broke yesterday, as if it never happened.
The technology they used was cutting-edge only yesterday, but it's already outdated now.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recently - not long ago; in the recent past
