overjoy

verb
make someone extremely happy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel extremely happy or delighted

Examples

To overjoy someone
The entire family was overjoyed to hear the good news.
The teacher's praise overjoyed the young student.
Winning the award overjoyed the research team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delight - to give great pleasure or satisfaction to
  • antonym: sadden - to make someone feel sad
