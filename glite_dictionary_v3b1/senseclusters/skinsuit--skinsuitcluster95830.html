skinsuit

noun
compression garment
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪnˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈskɪnˌsuːt / (BrE)
a close-fitting compression garment worn to support body tissues and reduce swelling, especially after surgery or injury

Examples

Medical skinsuit
After her operation, she wore a skinsuit to help reduce swelling and support her recovery.
The doctor recommended a skinsuit to provide gentle compression after the injury.
Wearing a skinsuit can improve healing by supporting the tissues after surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to bind a wound or injury
  • synonym: compression garment - a tight-fitting piece of clothing used to apply pressure to the body
