stove poker

noun
metal rod for stirring fires
8.4
Pronunciation: / stoʊv ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / stəʊv ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
a metal rod with a handle used for stirring or rearranging burning material in a fire or stove

Examples

A stove poker tool
After dinner, she prodded the embers with a stove poker to keep the room warm.
He used the stove poker to move the logs and revive the fire.
The old iron stove poker hung beside the fireplace for easy access.
