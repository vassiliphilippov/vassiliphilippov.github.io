turnip bed

noun
garden bed for turnips
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.nɪp bɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.nɪp bed / (BrE)
an area of soil prepared and planted for growing turnips

Examples

A turnip bed in garden
