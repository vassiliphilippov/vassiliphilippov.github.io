humoring

noun
the act of indulging or gratifying a desire
4.7 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of indulging or gratifying a desire or inclination, especially to please someone; the act of accommodating another's wishes

Examples

To humor a request
He grew tired of humoring his demanding clients every day.
Her constant humoring of her son's whims made him quite spoiled.
Sometimes, a little humoring can go a long way in keeping the peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indulgence - the act of allowing oneself or another to enjoy pleasure
