repo

noun
short-term loan agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈriːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈriːpəʊ / (BrE)
a short-term loan in which securities are sold and later repurchased at an agreed price, known as a repurchase agreement

Examples

A repo loan
The central bank used a repo to inject liquidity into the financial system.
Many financial institutions rely on the repo market for short-term funding.
After the agreement expired, the securities were returned as part of the repo transaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loan - an amount of money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back
  • synonym: repurchase agreement - an agreement to sell and later repurchase securities at a set price
