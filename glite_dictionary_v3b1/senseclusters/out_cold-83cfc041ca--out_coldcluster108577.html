out cold

adjective
completely unconscious or deeply asleep
5.3
Pronunciation: / aʊt koʊld / (AmE), / aʊt kəʊld / (BrE)
completely unconscious or in a very deep sleep, often as a result of injury or exhaustion

Examples

He was out cold
After the accident, he was out cold and didn't wake up for several minutes.
By the time I checked on her, she was out cold from sheer exhaustion.
The sleeping pills worked so well that he was out cold all night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awake - not asleep; conscious
  • synonym: unconscious - not awake and aware of and responding to one's environment
