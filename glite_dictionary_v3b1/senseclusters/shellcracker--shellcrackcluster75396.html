shellcracker

noun
freshwater sunfish
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛlˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈʃɛlˌkrækə / (BrE)
a freshwater sunfish of the southeastern United States, also known as the redear sunfish, noted for eating snails and mollusks

Examples

A big shellcracker fish
The fisherman proudly displayed the large shellcracker he caught in the river.
Many anglers visit southern lakes hoping to catch a trophy-sized shellcracker during the spring.
A shellcracker can be recognized by the red edge on its gill cover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluegill - a common North American freshwater sunfish
  • synonym: redear sunfish - a species of freshwater sunfish with a red edge on its gill cover
