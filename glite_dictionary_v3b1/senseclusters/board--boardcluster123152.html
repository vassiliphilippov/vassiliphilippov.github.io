board

noun
table for serving meals (archaic)
Pronunciation: / bɔrd / (AmE), / bɔːd / (BrE)
(Archaic) A table at which meals are served.

Examples

Dinner board
A lavish dinner was arranged on the board to welcome the guests.
In the old manor, family stories were shared each night around the board.
The feast laid upon the board impressed everyone at the gathering.
