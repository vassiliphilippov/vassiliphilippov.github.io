fallow deer

noun
medium-sized Eurasian deer with spotted coat
9.1 6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfæloʊ dɪr / (AmE), / ˈfæləʊ dɪə / (BrE)
a medium-sized Eurasian deer (Dama dama), typically having a palmate antlers and a coat that is usually fawn-colored with white spots in summer

Examples

A fallow deer herd
A herd of fallow deer grazed peacefully in the clearing by the old oak tree.
The distinctive spotted coat of the fallow deer helps it blend into the dappled sunlight of the forest.
The park is famous for its large population of fallow deer that roam freely among the visitors.
