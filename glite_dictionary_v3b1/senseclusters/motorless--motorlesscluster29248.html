motorless

adjective
having no motor
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.tɚ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tə.ləs / (BrE)
Not equipped with or operated by a motor.

Examples

Motorless lawnmower
A motorless lawnmower can be more environmentally friendly than a gas-powered one.
Some children prefer motorless toys because they encourage creative play.
The motorless glider soared silently above the mountains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: motorized - equipped with or operated by a motor
