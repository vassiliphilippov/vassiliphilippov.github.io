decisive factor

noun
something that settles a decision or outcome
4.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.sɪv ˈfæk.tər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsaɪ.sɪv ˈfæk.tə/ (BrE)
a point, fact, or circumstance that conclusively determines the outcome of a situation or decision

Examples
