ring

verb
to call by telephone
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ / (BrE)
to contact or attempt to contact (someone) by telephone

Examples

To ring the office
Could you ring the office and ask if they're open late tonight?
He forgot to ring his mother on her birthday, and she was disappointed.
I'll ring you when I get home to let you know I'm safe.
