quincunx

noun
astrological aspect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪnˌkʌŋks / (AmE), / ˈkwɪnˌkʌŋks / (BrE)
an astrological aspect in which two planets or points are separated by 150 degrees

Examples

Astrological quincunx aspect
The astrologer explained that a quincunx can create tension between two planets in a birth chart.
A quincunx aspect often indicates the need for adjustment or compromise in astrology.
She noticed a quincunx between Venus and Jupiter, suggesting subtle challenges in relationships.
