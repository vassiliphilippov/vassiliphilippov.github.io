factory worker

noun
person employed in a factory
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ ˌwɝː.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæk.təri ˌwɜː.kə / (BrE)
a person who is employed to work in a factory, usually performing physical or manual tasks as part of the manufacturing process

Examples

A factory worker operates machines
After years as a factory worker, he became skilled at handling machinery.
As a factory worker, she puts in long shifts on the assembly line every day.
The company hired dozens of new factory workers to meet rising demand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
  • synonym: machine operator - a person who operates machinery, typically in a factory setting
