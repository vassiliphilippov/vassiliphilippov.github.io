subbing

noun
working as a substitute for someone
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of working as a substitute for someone, especially when the person is ill or on leave

Examples

Subbing for a teacher
Due to illness, he did a lot of subbing last year.
Many schools rely on subbing to cover unexpected teacher absences.
She will be subbing for the chemistry teacher next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: substitute - a person who takes the place of another
