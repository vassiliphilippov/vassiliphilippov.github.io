political leader

noun
important figure in politics or government
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈliːdər / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkl ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who holds a prominent position or plays a leading role in political party activities or government affairs
