purse strings

noun
financial control or resources
9.2
Pronunciation: / pɝs strɪŋz / (AmE), / pɜːs strɪŋz / (BrE)
the control of money or financial resources, especially in an organization or family

Examples

Tighten purse strings
After losing his job, he had to tighten the purse strings at home.
In most organizations, the finance director holds the purse strings.
The committee will decide who controls the purse strings for the new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: budget - the amount of money available for spending in a particular period
  • synonym: finances - money and financial resources held by an individual or organization
