mediocrely

adverb
in an average way
4.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåmiÀêdiÀào äk…ôrli / (AmE), / ÀåmiÀêdiÀà…ô äk…ôli / (BrE)
in a mediocre or average way; not very well or very badly

Examples

To do something mediocrely
She completed the assignment mediocrely, meeting only the basic requirements.
The team played mediocrely throughout the season, never standing out but not failing either.
He answered the interview questions mediocrely, giving responses that were neither impressive nor terrible.

Synonyms

    3.0 0.03/m

  • so-so
    3.8 0.2/m
    so-so feels informal and suggests mild quality or dislike, while mediocrely is neutral and means merely average.
  • passably
    4.6 0.2/m
    passably means doing something well enough to be accepted by others, while mediocrely means just average without praise.
  • tolerably
    4.9 0.3/m
    tolerably means done in an acceptable way you can live with, while mediocrely means plain average and not special.
