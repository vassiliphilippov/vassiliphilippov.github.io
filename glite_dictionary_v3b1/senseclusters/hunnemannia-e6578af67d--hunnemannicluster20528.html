Hunnemannia

noun
genus of bright yellow-flowered plant (golden cup)
Pronunciation: / hʌnəˈmɛniə / (AmE), / hʌnɪˈmiːnɪə / (BrE)
A genus of herbaceous flowering plants in the poppy family, with a single species known for its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers (commonly called golden cup).
