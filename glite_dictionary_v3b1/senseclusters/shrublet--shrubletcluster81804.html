shrublet

noun
a small or dwarf shrub
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrʌb.lət / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌb.lɪt / (BrE)
a small or dwarf shrub, typically a woody plant smaller than a typical shrub

The delicate shrublet grew close to the rocky hillside, barely reaching a foot in height.
