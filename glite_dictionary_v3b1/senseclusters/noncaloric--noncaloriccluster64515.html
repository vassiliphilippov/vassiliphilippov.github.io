noncaloric

adjective
containing no or few calories
2.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kə.ˈlɑːr.ɪk/ /; / /nɑːn.kə.ˈlɔːr.ɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.kə.ˈlɒr.ɪk/ /; / /nɒn.kə.ˈlɔːr.ɪk/ (BrE)
Containing no or very few calories; not providing energy in the form of calories.

Examples

Noncaloric sweetener
Many diet sodas are labeled as noncaloric to appeal to health-conscious consumers.
She switched to a noncaloric sweetener to reduce her sugar intake.
The beverage claims to be noncaloric and suitable for people watching their weight.
