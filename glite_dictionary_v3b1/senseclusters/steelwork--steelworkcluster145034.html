steelwork

noun
steel structural parts
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstiːl.wɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈstiːl.wɜːk/ (BrE)
the parts of a building or structure that are made from steel, especially the framework or supporting elements

Examples

Building steelwork
The steelwork for the skyscraper was completed ahead of schedule.
Inspectors checked the steelwork to ensure it met safety standards.
Much of the visible steelwork was painted to protect it from rust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framework - the supporting structure of a building
  • antonym: timberwork - the parts of a structure made of wood
