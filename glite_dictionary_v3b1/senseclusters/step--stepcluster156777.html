Synonyms

  • walk
    1.0 394/m
    walk is moving on foot at a steady pace for some time, while step is a single lift and put-down of the foot.
  • tread
    3.0 4.92/m
    tread means to press or walk with the foot, often heavily, while step is a neutral single lift and put-down of the foot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pace - to measure by walking a number of steps
  • antonym: stand - to be in an upright position on the feet
  • degree higher: stomp - to walk with heavy, forceful steps
  • degree higher: stride - to walk with long, decisive steps
  • degree lower: tiptoe - to walk quietly and carefully with one's heels raised
  • synonym: tread - to put your foot down while walking
  • synonym: walk - to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
