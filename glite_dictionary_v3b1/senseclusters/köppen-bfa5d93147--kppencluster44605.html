Köppen

proper noun
climatologist's name
Pronunciation: / ˈkœpən / (AmE), / ˈkœpən / (BrE)
Wladimir Köppen (1846–1940), a German–Russian climatologist known for creating the Köppen climate classification system.

Examples

Köppen climate scientist
The Köppen climate classification is still widely used by geographers and meteorologists today.
Wladimir Köppen made significant contributions to the study of world climates.
Many textbooks reference Köppen when discussing climate zones and their characteristics.
