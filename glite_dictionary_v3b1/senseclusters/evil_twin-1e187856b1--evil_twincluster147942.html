evil twin

noun
rogue Wi‑Fi access point
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.vəl twɪn/ /; / /ˈiː.vɪl twɪn/ (AmE), /ˈiː.vəl twɪn/ /; / /ˈiː.vɪl twɪn/ (BrE)
A rogue wireless access point that deliberately mimics the name and settings of a legitimate Wi‑Fi network to trick users into connecting so the attacker can intercept, monitor, or alter their data.

Examples

Evil twin Wi-Fi attack
The cafe warned customers about an evil twin that mimicked their Wi-Fi network and stole passwords.
Security researchers used a laptop to create an evil twin for testing the school's Wi-Fi defenses.
He unknowingly connected to an evil twin while traveling and later discovered his bank details were compromised.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rogue access point - an unauthorized or malicious wireless access point that can expose users to security risks
