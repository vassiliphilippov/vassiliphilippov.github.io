eternal

noun
immortal being
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtɜrnəl / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)

Examples

An eternal being
Legends tell of the eternals who have watched over humanity for centuries.
In the fantasy novel, the eternals possess wisdom beyond human understanding.
Some believe that the gods are eternals who exist outside of time.
