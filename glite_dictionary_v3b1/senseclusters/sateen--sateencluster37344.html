sateen

noun
a glossy cotton fabric
8.5
Pronunciation: / səˈtin / (AmE), / səˈtiːn / (BrE)
a smooth cotton fabric with a sheen resembling satin, produced by a particular weave

Examples

Smooth sateen fabric
Many quilters prefer using sateen for its smooth texture and subtle shine.
She wore a dress of blue sateen that shimmered in the light.
The luxurious bed sheets were made from pure sateen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: satin - a smooth, glossy fabric, typically of silk, with a characteristic sheen
