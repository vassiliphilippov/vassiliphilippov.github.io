teen

noun
person aged 13 to 19
2.6
Pronunciation: / tin / (AmE), / tiːn / (BrE)
a person in their teenage years, typically between the ages of 13 and 19

Examples

A teen girl
It's common for teens to develop new interests as they grow older.
Many teens enjoy spending time with their friends after school.
The school organized a workshop specifically for teens about internet safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adolescent - a young person developing into an adult
  • antonym: adult - a fully grown person or one who has reached legal age
