missionization

noun
spreading missions
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪʃənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪʃənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of establishing or spreading religious missions, especially by missionaries in new regions

Examples

The missionization process
The missionization of the region led to significant cultural changes among the local population.
Historians study the effects of missionization on indigenous communities in the Americas.
The process of missionization often involved building churches and schools in remote areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversion - the act of changing someone's religious beliefs
  • synonym: evangelization - the act of spreading the Christian gospel
