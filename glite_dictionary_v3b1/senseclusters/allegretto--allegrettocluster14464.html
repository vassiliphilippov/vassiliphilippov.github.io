allegretto

noun
moderately quick musical tempo
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈɡrɛtoʊ /; / ˌælɪˈɡrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌalɪˈɡrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a moderately quick tempo in music, faster than andante but slower than allegro; or a piece of music played at this tempo
