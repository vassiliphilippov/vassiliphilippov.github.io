re-create

verb
form again in the imagination
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈkreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkriːeɪt / (BrE)
to form or produce again in the imagination; to recall and reconstruct an image, scene, or experience in the mind

Examples

To re-create in memory
Artists often re-create the scenes they remember most vividly from their travels.
His mind re-creates lost moments from the past with remarkable detail.
She tried to re-create the joy of her childhood vacations in her writing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagine - form a mental image or concept
  • synonym: reconstruct - rebuild or form again, especially from memory
