hobbyhorse

noun
a favored or recurring topic
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːbiˌhɔːrs / (AmE), / ˈhɒbihɔːs / (BrE)
a subject, idea, or topic that a person talks about repeatedly or is particularly preoccupied with

Examples

His hobbyhorse topic
Climate change is his hobbyhorse, and he brings it up in every meeting.
He's always talking about recycling—it's become his hobbyhorse lately.
Try not to mention taxes around her, or she'll start riding her hobbyhorse again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsession - something that preoccupies a person excessively
  • synonym: pet topic - a favorite topic or subject someone repeatedly discusses
