nonlethally

adverb
without causing death
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈliːθəli / (AmE), / nɒnˈliːθəli / (BrE)
in a way that does not cause death

Examples

Act nonlethally
The officers acted nonlethally to subdue the suspect without causing serious harm.
Wildlife experts try to manage animal populations nonlethally whenever possible.
The security team responded nonlethally by using rubber bullets instead of live ammunition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lethally - in a way that causes death
