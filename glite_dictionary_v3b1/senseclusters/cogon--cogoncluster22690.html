cogon

noun
tropical coarse grass
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊɡɒn / (AmE), / ˈkəʊɡɒn / (BrE)
a tall, coarse grass native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in open fields and grasslands

Examples

Wild cogon grass
The hillside was covered in thick cogon that swayed in the wind.
Farmers often clear cogon from their fields before planting crops.
Wildfires can spread quickly through dry cogon during the hot season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grass - a common plant with narrow leaves, found in fields and lawns
