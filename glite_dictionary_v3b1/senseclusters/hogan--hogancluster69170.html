hogan

noun
traditional Navajo earth-covered dwelling
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈɡɑn / (AmE), / həʊˈɡɑːn / (BrE)
A traditional Navajo dwelling constructed of logs and earth, often with the entrance facing east.

Examples

A traditional hogan
His grandmother still lives in a traditional hogan near Monument Valley.
Many tourists visit the reservation to see an authentic hogan up close.
The family gathered in their hogan for the evening meal.
