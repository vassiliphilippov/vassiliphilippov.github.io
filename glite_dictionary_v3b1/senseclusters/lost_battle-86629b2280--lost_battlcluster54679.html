lost battle

noun
unsuccessful struggle or attempt
Pronunciation: / lɔst ˈbæt̬.əl / (AmE), / lɒst ˈbætl / (BrE)
an unsuccessful attempt to overcome a difficulty or challenge; a struggle in which one fails to win

Examples

Lost battle with illness
After years of trying to quit smoking, Maria finally accepted she had lost her battle.
Despite his optimism, he lost his battle with the disease.
The charity fought hard but ultimately lost the battle for funding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lose the battle - to fail to overcome a difficulty or challenge; often used interchangeably with 'lost battle'
  • antonym: win the battle - to be successful in overcoming a difficulty or challenge
