bar mask

noun
a catcher's mask with bars
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑr ˌmæsk / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ˌmɑːsk / (BrE)
a catcher's mask made with protecting bars instead of a solid faceplate, used in baseball or softball

Examples

A bar mask for catchers
A bar mask provides protection while still allowing clear visibility.
Some players prefer a bar mask over a solid mask for its lighter weight.
The catcher put on his bar mask before stepping onto the field.
