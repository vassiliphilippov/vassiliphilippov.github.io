shaping machine

noun
machine tool for shaping metal or wood
1.8 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈʃeɪ.pɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈʃeɪ.pɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
