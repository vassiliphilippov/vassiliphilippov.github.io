reniform

adjective
kidney-shaped (esp. leaves/organs)
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.ɪ.fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈriː.nɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of a kidney; kidney-shaped, especially describing leaves or organs.

Examples

A reniform leaf
A reniform mass was detected in the patient’s abdominal scan.
The plant’s leaves are distinctly reniform, making them easy to identify among other species.
The seed pods are reniform in shape, similar to small beans.
