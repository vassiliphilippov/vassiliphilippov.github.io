coup d'etat

noun
sudden illegal overthrow of a government
7.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /kuː deɪ.ˈtɑː/ /; / /kuː de.ˈtɑː/ (AmE), /kuː deɪ.ˈtɑː/ /; / /kuː de.ˈtɑː/ (BrE)
A sudden and decisive action in politics, especially one resulting in a change of government illegally or by force.

Examples

A coup d'etat attempt
After the coup d'etat, all political parties were banned and a curfew was imposed.
Several leaders condemned the coup d'etat and called for a return to democracy.
The military led a coup d'etat that toppled the civilian government overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: putsch - a violent attempt to overthrow a government, often on a small scale
  • synonym: revolution - a forcible overthrow of a government, usually with widespread support
  • degree lower: uprising - an act of resistance or rebellion; a revolt
