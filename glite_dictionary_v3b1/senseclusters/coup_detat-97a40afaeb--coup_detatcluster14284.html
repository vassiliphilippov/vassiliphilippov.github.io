coup d'etat

noun
sudden illegal overthrow of a government
8.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ku de…™Ààt…ë / (AmE), / kuÀê de…™Ààt…ëÀê / (BrE)
A sudden and decisive action in politics, especially one resulting in a change of government illegally or by force.

Examples

A coup d'etat attempt
After the coup d'etat, all political parties were banned and a curfew was imposed.
Several leaders condemned the coup d'etat and called for a return to democracy.
The military led a coup d'etat that toppled the civilian government overnight.

Synonyms

  • coup
    8.1 27/m
    a coup is a short, common word for a sudden illegal government takeover, while a coup d'etat is the formal phrase for the same action.
  • putsch
    9.9 0.34/m
    a putsch is a word from German often used in history for sudden government overthrow, while a coup d'etat is the general political term.
  • takeover
    5.4 4.99/m
    a takeover is any sudden seizure of control, often used for companies or states, while a coup d'etat is an illegal change of government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revolution - a forcible overthrow of a government, usually with widespread support
