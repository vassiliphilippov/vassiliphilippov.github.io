cantus

noun
song or chant
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæntəs / (AmE), / ˈkantʊs / (BrE)
a song or chant, especially one performed in a group or as part of a ceremony

Examples

Ceremonial cantus
The choir performed a beautiful cantus during the evening service.
Each year, the students gather to sing the traditional cantus at graduation.
The ancient cantus echoed through the hall, filling it with solemnity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chant - a repeated rhythmic phrase, often sung or spoken
  • synonym: hymn - a religious song or poem of praise
