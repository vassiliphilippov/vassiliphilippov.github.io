Bering Time

noun
a time zone west of Greenwich, used near the Bering Sea
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɛrɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
standard time in the 11th time zone west of Greenwich, reckoned at the 165th meridian west; formerly observed in the Midway Islands and other parts of the Bering Sea region.

Examples

Bering Time zone
Flights to the Midway Islands are scheduled on Bering Time.
Historically, Bering Time was used in regions around the Bering Sea.
The research team coordinated their activities according to Bering Time.
