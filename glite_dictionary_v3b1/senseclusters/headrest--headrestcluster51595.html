headrest

noun
support for the head on a seat
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌrɛst /; / ˈhɛd.rɛst / (BrE)
a padded support attached to a seat or chair for resting your head, providing comfort or protection (especially in vehicles)

Examples

Car headrest
Proper use of the headrest can help prevent whiplash injuries during an accident.
She leaned her head back against the headrest and closed her eyes for a moment.
The new car model comes with adjustable headrests for added comfort and safety.
For long flights, a good headrest can make a big difference in comfort.
I adjusted the headrest in my car to make the drive more comfortable.
The dentist’s chair had a soft headrest to help patients relax during treatment.
