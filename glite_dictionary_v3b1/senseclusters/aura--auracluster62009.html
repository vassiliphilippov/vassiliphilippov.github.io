aura

noun
distinctive atmosphere or quality
1.4 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrə / (AmE), / ˈɔːrə / (BrE)
A distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround a person, place, or thing and is often intangible or subtle.

Examples

An aura of mystery
She carried herself with an aura of confidence that was contagious to everyone around her.
The old library had an aura of mystery that captivated every visitor.
The painting gave the room an aura of elegance and tranquility.
