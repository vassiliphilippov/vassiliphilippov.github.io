inestimable

adjective
too great to be measured or calculated
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛstɪməbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛstɪməbəl / (BrE)
too great to be calculated or measured; impossible to estimate because of vastness or incalculable value.

Examples

An inestimable value
She made an inestimable contribution to scientific research over her lifetime.
The museum's collection contains works of inestimable value from ancient times.
Their kindness and support proved inestimable during our time of need.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immeasurable - too large, extensive, or extreme to measure
