Mapuche

proper noun
indigenous Chilean people
9.1
Pronunciation: / maˈputʃeɪ / (AmE), / maˈpuːtʃeɪ / (BrE)
the indigenous people of south-central Chile and southwestern Argentina, considered as a group

Examples

The Mapuche people
The Mapuche have preserved many of their traditions despite centuries of outside influence.
A Mapuche leader spoke at the international conference on indigenous rights.
Many tourists visit southern Chile to learn about Mapuche culture and crafts.
