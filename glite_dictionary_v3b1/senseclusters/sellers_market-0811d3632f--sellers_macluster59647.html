seller's market

noun
market favoring sellers due to high demand
3.9 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…õl…öz Ààm…ërk…ôt /; / Ààs…õl…öz Ààm…ërk…™t / (AmE), / Ààs…õl…ôz Ààm…ëÀêk…™t / (BrE)
a market situation where there are more buyers than goods or property for sale, so sellers have an advantage

Examples

A seller's market situation
During the boom, every real estate agent said it was clearly a seller's market.
In a seller's market, home prices tend to rise quickly due to competition.
With so few houses available, it is a seller's market right now.
During a seller's market, homeowners often receive multiple offers above the asking price.
It's harder for buyers to negotiate in a seller's market because competition is so high.
The current housing shortage has created a true seller's market in many cities.
