cosmopolitanism

noun
worldly sophistication
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪtənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənɪzəm / (BrE)
the quality of being familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures

Examples

Her cosmopolitanism shines
Her cosmopolitanism was evident in her appreciation for international cuisine and art.
The city's cosmopolitanism attracts people from all over the world.
He admired her cosmopolitanism and ability to adapt to any culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: provincialism - narrowness of outlook, limited to a local area
  • synonym: worldliness - experience and knowledge of the world
