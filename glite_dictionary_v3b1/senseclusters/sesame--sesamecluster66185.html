sesame

noun
an edible plant grown for seeds and oil
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛsəˌmi / (AmE), / ˈsɛsəmi / (BrE)
an annual plant (Sesamum indicum) grown for its small, edible seeds and oil, used in cooking and food products

Examples

Sesame seeds
Farmers in India grow sesame for its valuable oil-rich seeds.
I bought a bottle of sesame oil to use in my stir-fry recipes.
The chef sprinkled sesame on top of the salad for extra texture and flavor.
