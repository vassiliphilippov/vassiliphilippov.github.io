nonseasonal

adjective
year-round; not seasonal
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsiːzənl /; / ˌnɑnˈsiːzənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsiːzənl /; / ˌnɒnˈsiːzənl̩ / (BrE)
not limited to, affected by, or dependent on any particular season; available or continuing throughout the year

Examples

Nonseasonal work
The company offers nonseasonal employment, so workers have jobs all year round.
Farmers in this region are seeking more nonseasonal sources of income to support their families.
Tourism in the city has become nonseasonal thanks to new attractions open year-round.
The hotel offers nonseasonal discounts that are available throughout the entire year.
Farmers are looking for nonseasonal crops that can be grown regardless of the weather.
Many people prefer nonseasonal jobs because they provide steady work all year.
