still-fish

verb
fish with stationary bait and line
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪl fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈstɪl fɪʃ / (BrE)
to fish by allowing the bait and line to remain motionless or stationary in the water

Examples

To still-fish from dock
He still-fished from the dock all day and finally caught a large carp.
Many anglers prefer to still-fish for catfish using cut bait on the lake bottom.
When you still-fish, patience is key as you wait for the fish to find your stationary bait.
