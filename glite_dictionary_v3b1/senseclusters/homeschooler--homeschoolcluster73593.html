homeschooler

noun
student educated at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌskuːlər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌskuːlə / (BrE)
a student who receives their education at home instead of attending a traditional school

Examples

A young homeschooler
The local library offers special programs for homeschoolers every Wednesday afternoon.
As a homeschooler, she enjoyed the flexibility to study at her own pace.
Many homeschoolers join clubs to meet other students and make friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pupil - a young person who is learning at school
  • synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • antonym: teacher - a person who helps students learn
