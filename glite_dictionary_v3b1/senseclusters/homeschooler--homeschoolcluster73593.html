homeschooler

noun
student educated at home
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.skuː.lər/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.skuː.lə/ (BrE)

Examples

Many homeschoolers join clubs to meet other students and make friends.
