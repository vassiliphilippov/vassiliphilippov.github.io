Martin Luther King Jr's Birthday

noun
US holiday honoring Martin Luther King Jr.
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑɹ.tən ˈluː.θɚ ˈkɪŋ dʒɚz ˈbɝθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.tɪn ˈluː.θə ˈkɪŋ dʒuːnz ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
a United States federal holiday observed on the Monday closest to January 15 to honor the civil rights leader Martin Luther King Jr.
