recoupable

adjective
able to be recovered
6.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkuːpəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈkuːpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be recovered or regained, especially money, costs, or other valuable things after a loss

Examples

After a long illness, she was relieved to find her strength was still recoupable with therapy.
The lost data was not recoupable because there were no backups available.
