longe

noun
horse training exercise
9.9
Pronunciation: / lʌndʒ / (AmE), / lɒndʒ / (BrE)
an exercise in which a horse is made to move in a circle at the end of a long rein or line

Examples

A longe exercise
The trainer used a longe to help the young horse learn basic commands.
Before riding, she gave her horse a gentle longe to warm up its muscles.
A proper longe session can improve a horse's balance and obedience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunge - an alternative spelling for longe, also used for horse training
  • antonym: ride - to sit on and control the movement of a horse
