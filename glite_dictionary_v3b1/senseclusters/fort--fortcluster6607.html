fort

noun
military defensive structure
2.2
Pronunciation: / fɔrt / (AmE), / fɔːt / (BrE)
a strong or fortified building or place used for military defense

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: castle - a large fortified building or set of buildings, often the residence of a noble
  • synonym: fortress - a large and heavily protected fort or defensive building
