Synonyms

  • nerve tract
    6.2 0.01/m
    nerve tract is a bundle of axons that runs together in the brain or spinal cord, while tract names that same anatomical pathway.
  • nerve pathway
    5.3 0.04/m
    nerve pathway is used to describe a signal route between brain regions, while tract refers to a specific anatomical bundle of fibers.
  • pathway
    6.3 4.10/m
    pathway is a general route for signals in the nervous system, while tract is a defined bundle of nerve fibers in anatomy.
  • fiber bundle
    5.6 0.03/m
    fiber bundle is any group of fibers in the body, while tract names a nerve bundle in the brain or spinal cord.
