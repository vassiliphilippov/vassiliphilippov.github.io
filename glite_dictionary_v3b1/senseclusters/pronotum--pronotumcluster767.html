pronotum

noun
insect thorax plate
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈnoʊtəm / (AmE), / prəˈnəʊtəm / (BrE)
the upper surface of the first segment of an insect's thorax, often forming a shield-like plate

Examples

The pronotum shield
The beetle's pronotum was marked with bright red spots.
Scientists measured the width of the pronotum to help identify the insect species.
A grasshopper's pronotum often extends over part of its wings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abdomen - the rear section of an insect's body behind the thorax
  • synonym: thorax - the middle section of an insect's body between the head and abdomen
