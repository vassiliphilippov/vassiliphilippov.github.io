mood

noun
informal internet slang: 'I relate'
8.6
Pronunciation: / mud / (AmE), / muːd / (BrE)
Informal (internet slang). A brief label meaning 'I relate to this' or 'this captures a relatable feeling or vibe'; often used as a standalone reaction to content.

Examples

Internet mood
When I saw the messy desk photo, I thought 'that's a mood' and laughed.
Her tweet about surviving exams was a mood that hundreds of students shared.
If the song makes you want to lie down, simply type mood under the post.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relate - to feel that you understand or share another person's experience; what 'mood' often signals.
  • synonym: vibe - a general feeling, atmosphere, or emotional impression; often used to describe the feeling a piece of content gives.
