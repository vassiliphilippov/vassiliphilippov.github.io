reach out

verb
extend or stretch outward
Pronunciation: / ritʃ aʊt / (AmE), / riːtʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to extend or stretch outward in space or direction

Examples

To reach out a hand
A hand reached out from the darkness, startling everyone in the room.
He tried to reach out and grab the branch above his head.
The cat reached out its paw to touch the toy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extend - to stretch out or make longer
  • antonym: withdraw - to pull back or move away
