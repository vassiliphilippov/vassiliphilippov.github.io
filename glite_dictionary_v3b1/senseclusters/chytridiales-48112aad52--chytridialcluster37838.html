Chytridiales

noun
order of simple aquatic fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / kaɪˌtrɪdiˈeɪliz / (AmE), / kaɪˌtrɪdiˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of simple aquatic fungi, mostly saprophytic but some parasitic on higher plants or animals, sometimes placed in class Oomycetes

Examples

Chytridiales fungi
Researchers found that the spread of Chytridiales was linked to amphibian population declines.
Some species in the Chytridiales order are major aquatic decomposers.
The life cycle of Chytridiales includes both aquatic and terrestrial stages.
