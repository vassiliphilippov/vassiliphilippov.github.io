sasa

noun
broad-leaved bamboo
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈsɑːsə / (BrE)
a genus of small, broad-leaved bamboo plants native to East Asia, often used as ground cover

Examples

Sasa bamboo plant
The sasa plant is often used as ground cover in Japanese gardens.
Gardeners appreciate sasa for its ability to thrive in shady areas.
In some regions, sasa leaves are used for wrapping food.
