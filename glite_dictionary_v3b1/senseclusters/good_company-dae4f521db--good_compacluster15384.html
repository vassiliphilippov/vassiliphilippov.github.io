good company

noun
pleasant companionship
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
pleasant or enjoyable companionship; the state of being with agreeable or entertaining people

Examples

Enjoying good company
After a long week, I always look forward to some good company and a relaxing evening.
She is such good company; conversations with her are never dull.
Traveling with good company can turn any trip into a memorable adventure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bad company - the state of being with disreputable or unpleasant people
