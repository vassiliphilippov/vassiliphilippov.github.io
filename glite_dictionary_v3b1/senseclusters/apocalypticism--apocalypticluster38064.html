apocalypticism

noun
end-of-world genre
9.6
Pronunciation: /ə.pɑː.kə.ˈlɪp.tɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ə.pɒ.kə.ˈlɪp.tɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a style or genre in literature, film, or art that centers on large-scale destruction or the end of civilization.

Examples

Apocalypticism in fiction
Many science fiction novels explore apocalypticism through stories of global catastrophe.
The director is known for his use of apocalypticism to create a sense of urgency and despair.
Modern art often uses apocalypticism to comment on environmental and social issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dystopianism - a style or genre depicting a society characterized by suffering or injustice
  • antonym: utopianism - a style or genre depicting an ideal or perfect society
