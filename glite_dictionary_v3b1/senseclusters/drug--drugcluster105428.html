drug

verb
administer a drug to someone
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ / (AmE), / drʌɡ / (BrE)
to administer a drug, especially to someone, often to induce unconsciousness or alter behavior

Examples

To drug a drink
Authorities suspect someone drugged the drink at the party.
The criminals drugged their victims before robbing them.
The veterinarian drugged the lion so it could be safely examined.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medicate - to treat with medicine
  • antonym: poison - to administer a harmful substance
