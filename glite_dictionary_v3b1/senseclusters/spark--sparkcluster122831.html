spark

verb
emit or produce sparks
2.3
Pronunciation: / spɑrk / (AmE), / spɑːk / (BrE)
to emit or produce sparks, especially of electricity or fire

Examples

To spark a fire
Do not touch the cable, as it may still spark unexpectedly.
Old machinery can sometimes spark when it is powered on.
The faulty outlet began to spark during the rainstorm.
