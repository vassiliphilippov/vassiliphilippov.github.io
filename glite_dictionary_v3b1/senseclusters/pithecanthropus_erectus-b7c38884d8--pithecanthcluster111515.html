Pithecanthropus erectus

noun
historic genus for early human species
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪθiˈkænθrəpəs ɪˈrɛktəs / (AmE), / pɪθiˈkænθrəpəs ɪˈrɛktəs / (BrE)
a former genus name for an extinct species of primitive humans, now classified as Homo erectus

Examples

Pithecanthropus erectus fossils
Early textbooks in anthropology often referred to Pithecanthropus erectus rather than using the modern term.
Many fossils once called Pithecanthropus erectus are now identified as Homo erectus.
The discovery of Pithecanthropus erectus in Java was a major milestone in paleoanthropology.
