brushland

noun
area with bushes
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbrʌʃ.lænd/ (AmE), /ˈbrʌʃ.lænd/ (BrE)
land covered mainly with bushes, shrubs, and few or no trees

Examples

Dry brushland
The wildfire spread quickly through the dry brushland outside the city.
Many species of birds nest in the brushland where there is plenty of cover.
Farmers cleared sections of brushland to create new grazing areas for their cattle.
