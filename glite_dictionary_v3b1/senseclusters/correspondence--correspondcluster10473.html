correspondence

noun
communication by exchanging letters
Pronunciation: / kəˌrɑːspənˈdɛns / (AmE), / kɒˌrɒspənˈdɛns / (BrE)
the communication between people by exchanging letters, especially in written form

Examples

Business correspondence
All correspondence should be addressed to the head office.
She kept a box of old correspondence from her childhood pen pal.
The two authors maintained a close correspondence for over a decade.
