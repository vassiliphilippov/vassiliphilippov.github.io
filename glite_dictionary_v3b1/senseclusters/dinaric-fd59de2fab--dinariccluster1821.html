Dinaric

noun
person from Dinaric region
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnɑːrɪk / (AmE), / dɪˈnærɪk / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the Dinaric region of the Balkans, especially near the Dinaric Alps

Examples

A Dinaric person
A friendly Dinaric welcomed us to the mountain village.
The Dinaric shared stories about life in the highlands.
Many Dinarics are known for their traditional music and hospitality.
