pastellist

noun
artist using pastels
8.1
Pronunciation: / pæˈstɛlɪst / (AmE), / pæˈstɛlɪst / (BrE)
an artist who creates pictures using pastels as the primary medium

Examples

A pastellist artist
The renowned pastellist was invited to display her artwork at the museum.
As a skilled pastellist, he captures delicate light and color in his portraits.
Many art students admire the techniques used by a professional pastellist.
