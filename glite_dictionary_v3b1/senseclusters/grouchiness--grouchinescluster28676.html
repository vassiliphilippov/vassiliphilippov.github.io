grouchiness

noun
irritable mood
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊtʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊtʃɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being easily annoyed, irritable, or in a bad mood

Examples

His grouchiness today
His grouchiness made it hard for others to work with him.
She apologized for her grouchiness after a long and stressful day.
The rainy weather seemed to increase everyone's grouchiness in the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being happy and positive
  • synonym: grumpiness - the state of being bad-tempered or easily annoyed
  • synonym: irritability - the tendency to become easily annoyed or angered
