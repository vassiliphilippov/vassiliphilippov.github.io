global citizen

noun
person identifying with the global community
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊbəl ˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbəl ˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
a person who considers themselves part of a worldwide community beyond national boundaries, often with a sense of shared responsibility for global issues

Examples

A global citizen volunteer
As a global citizen, she volunteers for environmental causes on several continents.
He sees himself as a global citizen, caring deeply about issues that affect people everywhere.
The conference attracted global citizens from over fifty different countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: world citizen - a person who regards themselves as a citizen of the world
