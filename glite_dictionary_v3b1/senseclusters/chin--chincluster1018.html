chin

verb
raise body until chin reaches a bar
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɪn / (AmE), / tʃɪn / (BrE)
to raise oneself while hanging from one's hands until one's chin is level with the support bar, especially as an exercise

Examples

To chin the bar
After struggling for weeks, he finally chinned the high bar for the first time.
During her workout, she tried to chin herself on the playground equipment.
He can chin the bar ten times without taking a break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pull-up - an exercise where you pull yourself up until your chin is above a bar
