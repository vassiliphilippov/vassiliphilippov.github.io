citrus

noun
tropical fruit tree of the Citrus genus
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtrəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəs / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical evergreen tree of the genus Citrus, cultivated for its juicy, edible fruits with aromatic rinds, such as oranges, lemons, and limes

Examples

Citrus tree
A healthy diet often includes fresh citrus fruits rich in vitamin C.
Many citrus trees are in bloom, filling the air with a wonderful fragrance.
The climate in this region is ideal for growing citrus such as oranges and grapefruits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lime - a small, green citrus fruit with acidic juice
  • synonym: orange - a round, sweet citrus fruit with orange skin
