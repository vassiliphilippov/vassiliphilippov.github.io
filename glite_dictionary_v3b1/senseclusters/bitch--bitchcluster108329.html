bitch

noun
an offensive term for an unpleasant person
Pronunciation: / bɪtʃ / (AmE), / bɪtʃ / (BrE)
An offensive and vulgar term for a person (usually a woman) who is considered to be unpleasant, spiteful, or thoroughly disliked.

Examples

She is a bitch
He stormed out, muttering that his boss was such a bitch to him today.
She was tired of being called a bitch just for expressing her opinion.
Some people think she's a bitch, but others admire her confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jerk - an unpleasant or disagreeable person (informal, mostly male)
  • antonym: nice person - a person who is liked and pleasant
