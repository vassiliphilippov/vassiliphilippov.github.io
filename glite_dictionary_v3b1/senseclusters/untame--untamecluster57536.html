untame

adjective
wild or untrained
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪm / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪm / (BrE)
not tamed; wild or difficult to control or domesticate.

Examples

Untame wilderness
The untame forest stretched for miles, untouched by human hands.
Despite their efforts, the dog remained untame and refused to obey commands.
Legends spoke of an untame river that no one had ever crossed.
