voyage

noun
a long journey by sea
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪɪdʒ /; / ˈvɔɪədʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪɪdʒ / (BrE)
a long journey, especially by sea or sometimes in space

Examples

A long voyage
During his voyage, he encountered many storms and rough seas.
The captain prepared the crew for a long voyage to distant lands.
The first voyage across the Atlantic was a remarkable achievement.
Her dream was to take a voyage into outer space.
The novel describes the voyage of a young explorer in search of new lands.
The ship embarked on a voyage across the Atlantic Ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise - a journey by sea, usually for pleasure
  • synonym: expedition - a journey undertaken for a specific purpose, often exploration
  • synonym: journey - an act of traveling from one place to another
  • synonym: trip - a journey or excursion, especially for pleasure
