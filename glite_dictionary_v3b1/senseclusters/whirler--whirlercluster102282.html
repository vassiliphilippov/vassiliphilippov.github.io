whirler

noun
Sufi dancer who spins ecstatically
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːlər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlə / (BrE)
A member of a Sufi sect, especially a dervish, who performs ecstatic dances involving spinning or whirling as a form of spiritual practice.
