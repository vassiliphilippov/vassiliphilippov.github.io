mussel

noun
marine or freshwater bivalve mollusk
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəl / (BrE)
a marine or freshwater shellfish (bivalve mollusk) with a dark, elongated shell, often eaten as seafood
