hoster

noun
provider of hosting
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstə / (BrE)
a person or company that provides hosting services, especially for websites or online platforms

Examples

Web hoster
The hoster offers 24/7 customer support for all its web hosting clients.
After comparing prices, we chose a new hoster for our online store.
If your site is down, you should contact your hoster immediately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: client - a person or organization that uses the services of a hoster
  • synonym: host - a company or person that provides space for websites or services online
