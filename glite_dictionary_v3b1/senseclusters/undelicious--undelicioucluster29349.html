undelicious

adjective
unpleasant; not tasty
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
not pleasant or enjoyable in taste or flavor; tasting bad
