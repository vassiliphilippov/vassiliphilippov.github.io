Aqua-Lung

noun
early underwater breathing device
Pronunciation: / ˈækwə lʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈakwə lʌŋ / (BrE)
A trade name for an early type of portable breathing apparatus used by divers to breathe underwater; a predecessor to modern scuba equipment.

Examples

The Aqua-Lung device
Early divers relied on the Aqua-Lung before modern scuba gear became widespread.
Jacques Cousteau helped invent the Aqua-Lung, revolutionizing underwater exploration.
The documentary featured footage of an Aqua-Lung in use during the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scuba - a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus
