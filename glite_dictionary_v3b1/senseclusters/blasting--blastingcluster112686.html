blasting

adjective
unpleasantly loud and penetrating
Pronunciation: / ˈblæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) extremely loud, harsh, and penetrating, often causing discomfort.

Examples

Blasting music
I had to cover my ears to escape the blasting horns during rush hour.
She turned up the stereo, and the blasting music woke everyone in the house.
The festival was filled with blasting speakers that could be heard blocks away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blaring - very loud and harsh in sound
  • synonym: deafening - so loud as to make hearing difficult
