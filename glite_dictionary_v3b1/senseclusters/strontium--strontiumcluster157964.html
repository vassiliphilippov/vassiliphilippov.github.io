strontium

noun
a soft metallic chemical element
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɑn.ti.əm / (AmE), / ˈstrɒn.ti.əm / (BrE)
a soft, silver-white or yellowish metallic chemical element of the alkaline earth metal group, occurring naturally in minerals like celestite and strontianite

Examples
