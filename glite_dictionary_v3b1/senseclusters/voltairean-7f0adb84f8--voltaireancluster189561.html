Voltairean

adjective
witty or skeptical
Pronunciation: / voʊlˈtɛəriən / (AmE), / vɒlˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Voltaire, especially showing wit, skepticism, or critical thinking

Examples

Voltairean wit
His Voltairean remarks challenged the assumptions of the entire panel.
The novel is filled with Voltairean humor and sharp observations about society.
She admired his Voltairean approach to questioning authority and tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
  • synonym: skeptical - doubting or questioning accepted ideas
