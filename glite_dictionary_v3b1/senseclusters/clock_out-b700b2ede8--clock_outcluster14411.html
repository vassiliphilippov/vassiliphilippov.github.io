clock out

verb
register leaving work
3.4 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / klÉ‘k aÊŠt / (AmE), / klÉ’k aÊŠt / (BrE)
to record the time you leave work, especially by using a special machine

Examples

To clock out at work
After a long day, he was relieved to finally clock out and go home.
Please remember to clock out before you leave the building.
She accidentally forgot to clock out at the end of her shift yesterday.

Synonyms

  • punch out
    3.9 0.16/m
    punch out is often used for stamping your time on a work clock, while clock out names the general act of leaving.
  • clock off
    5.0 0.01/m
    clock off usually means recording the time you finish work with a machine, while clock out is a common way to say this.
