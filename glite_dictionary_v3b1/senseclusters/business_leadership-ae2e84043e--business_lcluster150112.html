business leadership

noun
directing and guiding a business
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznɪs ˌliːdɚʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˌliːdəʃɪp / (BrE)
the activity or ability of leading or directing a business or organization; the management and guidance of a company's strategies and people.

Examples

Effective business leadership
Effective business leadership is crucial for driving innovation and success.
She has shown exceptional business leadership throughout her career.
The company values strong business leadership to stay competitive in the market.
