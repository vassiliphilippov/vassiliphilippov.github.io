indigenist

noun
supporter of Indigenous rights
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪdʒənɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪdʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports, studies, or advocates for the rights and cultures of Indigenous peoples

Examples

An indigenist scholar
The museum hired an indigenist to help curate its new exhibit on native cultures.
As an indigenist, she works to protect the languages of Indigenous communities.
The panel included an indigenist who spoke about the importance of land rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social or political change
  • antonym: colonialist - a person who supports or practices control over another country and its people
