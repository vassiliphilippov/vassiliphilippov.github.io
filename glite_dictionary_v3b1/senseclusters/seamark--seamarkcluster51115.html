seamark

noun
navigation aid at sea
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an object or structure placed at sea or on the coast to guide ships and help with navigation

Examples

A seamark at sea
The captain spotted a seamark in the distance and adjusted the ship's course.
Old maps often show the location of every important seamark along the coast.
A new seamark was installed to warn sailors about the dangerous rocks nearby.

  • synonym: lighthouse - a tall structure with a light to guide ships at sea
