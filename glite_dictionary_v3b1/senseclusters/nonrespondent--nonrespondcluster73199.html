nonrespondent

noun
person not improving
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
a person who does not show improvement or benefit from a medical treatment or therapy

Examples

Treatment nonrespondent
The doctor noted that the nonrespondent required a different medication to see any improvement.
Researchers are studying why some patients remain nonrespondent to standard cancer therapies.
After several weeks of treatment, he was classified as a nonrespondent by the medical team.
