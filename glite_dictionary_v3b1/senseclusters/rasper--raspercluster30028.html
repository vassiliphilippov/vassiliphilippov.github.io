rasper

noun
kitchen grating tool
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈræspər / (AmE), / ˈrɑːspə / (BrE)
a kitchen tool with a rough surface used for grating or shredding food into small pieces

Examples

Kitchen rasper
She used a rasper to grate fresh nutmeg over the dessert.
A rasper is perfect for turning hard cheese into fine shreds for pasta.
The chef recommended a rasper for zesting lemons quickly and easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grater - a device used to grate food into small pieces
  • synonym: shredder - a tool or machine for cutting food into thin strips
