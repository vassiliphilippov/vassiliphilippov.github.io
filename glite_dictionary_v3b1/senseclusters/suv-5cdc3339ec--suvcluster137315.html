SUV

noun
sport utility vehicle; large car built like a truck
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /es.juː.ˈviː/ /; / /sʌv/ (AmE), /es.juː.ˈviː/ /; / /sʌv/ (BrE)
a large vehicle with four-wheel drive and raised ground clearance, designed for both regular roads and off-road use

Examples

A SUV vehicle
He bought a new SUV for family road trips and camping adventures.
Her SUV handled the snowy mountain roads with ease.
The parking lot was filled with large SUVs and pickup trucks.
He decided to buy an SUV for its spacious interior and higher driving position.
Many families prefer an SUV for long road trips due to its comfort and storage capacity.
The latest SUVs come equipped with advanced safety features and all-wheel drive.
