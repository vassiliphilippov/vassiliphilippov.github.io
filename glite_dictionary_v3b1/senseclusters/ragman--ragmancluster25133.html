ragman

noun
seller of old rags
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈræɡˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who collects and sells old rags, clothes, and discarded items, often from door to door

Examples

The ragman collects rags
The ragman visited our neighborhood every Saturday, calling out for old clothes and linens.
Children would watch the ragman sort through piles of discarded items on his cart.
In the early 1900s, a ragman was a common sight in many city streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junkman - a person who buys and sells discarded or secondhand items
  • synonym: peddler - a person who travels to sell small goods
