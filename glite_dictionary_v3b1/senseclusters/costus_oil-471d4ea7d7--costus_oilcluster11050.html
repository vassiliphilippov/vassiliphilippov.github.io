costus oil

noun
essential oil from costusroot, used in perfumes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑstəs ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒstəs ɔɪl / (BrE)
A yellow volatile essential oil extracted from the root of the costus plant, used chiefly in perfumes for its distinctive scent.

Examples

Costus oil perfume
Perfume makers value costus oil for its lasting aroma and blending properties.
Some luxury fragrances rely on costus oil for their distinctive earthy and musky undertones.
The extraction of costus oil from the plant’s root requires special distillation methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: essential oil - a concentrated, volatile oil obtained from plants, used in perfumes and aromatherapy
