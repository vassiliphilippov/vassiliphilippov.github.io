genus Erythronium

noun
a genus of bulbous perennial herbs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌɛr.ɪˈθroʊ.ni.əm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌɛr.ɪˈθrəʊ.nɪ.əm / (BrE)
a genus of perennial bulbous herbs found in most of northern United States, including species known as dogtooth violet, adder's tongue, trout lily, and fawn lily.
