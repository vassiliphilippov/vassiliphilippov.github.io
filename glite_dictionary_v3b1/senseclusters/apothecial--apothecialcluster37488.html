apothecial

adjective
relating to an apothecium in lichens/fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːˈθiːʃəl / (AmE), / əˌpɒˈθiːsɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an apothecium, a type of reproductive structure in certain lichens and fungi

Examples

apothecial structure

The apothecial discs on this lichen are clearly visible to the naked eye.
The specimen was identified based on its distinct apothecial structures.
