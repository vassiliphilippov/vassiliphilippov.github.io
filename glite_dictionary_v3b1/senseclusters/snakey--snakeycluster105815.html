snakey

adjective
winding or sneaky
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪki / (AmE), / ˈsneɪki / (BrE)
moving in a winding, twisting way like a snake, or behaving in a sly or sneaky manner

Examples

Snakey path
The snakey path wound through the dense forest, making it hard to see what was ahead.
He gave her a snakey look before slipping quietly out of the room.
The river followed a snakey course across the valley, curving back and forth.
