Examples

Synonyms

  • hitch
    3.9 1.66/m
    hitch means getting free roadside rides from passing cars by signaling, while hitchhike is the standard verb for this travel method.
  • thumb a ride
    8.2 0.04/m
    thumb a ride means asking a passing driver for a free lift by signaling with your thumb, while hitchhike is a broader roadside practice.
  • thumb
    6.2 0.30/m
    thumb means traveling by signaling with an outstretched thumb to get free rides, while hitchhike is the general, more common term.
