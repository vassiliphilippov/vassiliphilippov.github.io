sexing

noun
determining biological sex
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛksɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of determining the sex of an animal or plant, especially at an early stage of development

Examples

Sexing baby chicks
The hatchery uses experts for sexing chicks to separate males from females.
Accurate sexing of young plants can improve crop yields in agriculture.
Modern technology has made sexing animals faster and more reliable for farmers.
