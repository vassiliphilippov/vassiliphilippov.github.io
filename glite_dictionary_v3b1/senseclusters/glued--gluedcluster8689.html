glued

adjective
firmly fixed or unable to move
3.0 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / …°lud / (AmE), / …°luÀêd / (BrE)
firmly fixed in place, or unable to move or stop watching/listening, as if fastened with glue

Examples

To glued to a screen
She was so glued to her book that she didn't hear the phone ring.
The children sat glued to the television for hours.
The two broken pieces were carefully glued together to restore the vase.

Synonyms

  • glued to
    4.5 1.23/m
    glued to is used when someone watches or listens in total focus, while glued can mean firmly fixed physically.
  • fixed
    1.0 0.03/m
    fixed means firmly held or positioned so it does not move, while glued often means stuck or held fast.
  • affixed
    4.4 0.00/m
    affixed is a formal word for being fastened to something, while glued is common and can also mean fixed or absorbed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attached - joined or fastened to something
  • antonym: unstuck - not fixed or held in place by glue or other means
