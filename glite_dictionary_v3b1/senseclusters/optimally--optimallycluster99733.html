optimally

adverb
in the best or most effective way
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːp.tə.mə.li / (AmE), / ˈɒp.tɪ.mə.li / (BrE)
In the best or most effective way; to the greatest advantage.

Examples

Grow optimally in soil
Some plants grow optimally in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.
The system is designed to function optimally even under high demand.
To achieve the best results, resources must be allocated optimally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: suboptimally - in a less than optimal way
