SOPA

proper noun
US copyright bill
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpə / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Stop Online Piracy Act, a proposed 2011 United States bill aimed at reducing online copyright infringement

Examples

SOPA bill
Many technology companies protested against SOPA due to concerns about internet censorship.
The debate over SOPA sparked widespread online activism in 2012.
Congress ultimately decided not to pass SOPA after public outcry.
