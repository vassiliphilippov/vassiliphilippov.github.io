undercook

verb
to cook insufficiently
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈkʊk / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈkʊk / (BrE)
to cook food for too short a time so that it is not fully done or safe to eat

Examples

To undercook chicken
If you undercook chicken, it can be unsafe to eat.
The chef warned not to undercook the vegetables or they would be too hard.
She accidentally undercooked the pasta, so it was still a bit crunchy.
