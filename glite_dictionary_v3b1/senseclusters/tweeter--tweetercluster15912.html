tweeter

noun
high-frequency loudspeaker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtwitɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwiːtə / (BrE)
a small loudspeaker designed to produce high-frequency sounds in an audio system

Examples

Audio tweeter speaker
Many modern car audio systems feature separate woofers and tweeters for a fuller sound.
She replaced the broken tweeter in her home theater system.
The new speakers include a tweeter to enhance the sound quality of high notes.
