altissimo

adjective
very high musical register
Pronunciation: / ælˈtɪs.ɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ælˈtɪs.ɪ.məʊ / (BrE)
(in music) relating to or denoting notes higher than the normal range of an instrument or voice.

Examples

To play altissimo notes
Certain flute passages demand excellent breath support to produce clear altissimo tones.
Reaching the altissimo notes on the saxophone requires advanced technique and control.
The clarinet player performed a solo in the altissimo register that amazed the audience.
