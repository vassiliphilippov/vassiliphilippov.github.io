reconstructor

noun
data recovery tool
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkənˈstrʌktər / (AmE), / ˌriːkənˈstrʌktə / (BrE)
a device, program, or algorithm that creates or recovers an image, signal, or data set from incomplete or noisy measurements

Examples

Image reconstructor software
The new reconstructor software can generate high-quality images from very limited data.
Engineers tested the reconstructor to see how well it could recover lost audio signals.
A powerful reconstructor is essential for processing medical scans with missing information.
