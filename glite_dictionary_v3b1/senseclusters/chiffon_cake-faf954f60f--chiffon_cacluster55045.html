chiffon cake

noun
very light, airy cake
5.5 2.4
Pronunciation: /ʃɪ.ˈfɑːn keɪk/ /; / /ʃɪ.ˈfən keɪk/ (AmE), /ʃɪ.ˈfɒn keɪk/ /; / /ʃɪ.ˈfən keɪk/ (BrE)
