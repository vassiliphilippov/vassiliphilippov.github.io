haunter

noun
frequent visitor, ghost
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔntər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntə / (BrE)
a person, animal, or ghost that frequently visits or appears in a particular place, especially as a ghostly presence

Examples

A ghostly haunter
The villagers believed a mysterious haunter roamed the abandoned mansion at night.
As a regular haunter of the local library, she knew every corner of the building.
Legends say the forest has a haunter who appears whenever the moon is full.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often believed to appear to the living
  • synonym: visitor - someone who goes to a place for a short time
