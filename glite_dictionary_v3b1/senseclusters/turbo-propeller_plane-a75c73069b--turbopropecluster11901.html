turbo-propeller plane

noun
airplane with a turbojet-driven propeller
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtɝːboʊ prəˈpɛlər pleɪn / (AmE), / ˌtɜːbəʊ prəˈpɛlə pleɪn / (BrE)
An airplane powered by a turbojet engine that drives an external propeller.
