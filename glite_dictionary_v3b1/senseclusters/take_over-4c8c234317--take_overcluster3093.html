Synonyms

  • assume
    3.7 2/m
    assume means formally taking responsibility or ownership for another's debts, while take over stresses taking full control of their obligations.
  • accept
    2.5 4/m
    accept means agreeing to pay or share someone else's debts willingly, while take over implies legally taking their full obligations.
  • absorb
    4.9 3/m
    absorb means an organization covers or pays costs another caused, while take over implies assuming the debts and responsibility for them.
