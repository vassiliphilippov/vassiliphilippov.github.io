afforest

verb
create a forest on non-forested land
7.5 6.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈfɑː.rɪst/ /; / /ə.ˈfɔː.rɪst/ (AmE), /ə.ˈfɒ.rɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

Many countries are making efforts to afforest hillsides to combat soil erosion.
They hope to afforest the empty fields with native species of trees.
