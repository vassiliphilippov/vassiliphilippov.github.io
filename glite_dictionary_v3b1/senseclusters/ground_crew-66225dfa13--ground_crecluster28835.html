ground crew

noun
airport staff servicing aircraft on the ground
1.1 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊnd kru / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd kruː / (BrE)
the group of technicians and mechanics who service and maintain aircraft while they are on the ground at an airport

Examples

Without the skilled ground crew, flights would face serious delays and safety concerns.
