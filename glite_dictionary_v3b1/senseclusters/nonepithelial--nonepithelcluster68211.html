nonepithelial

adjective
not epithelial tissue
Pronunciation: / nʌnˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl / (AmE), / nʌnˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
not consisting of or relating to epithelial tissue.

Examples

Nonepithelial tissue type
The biopsy revealed a nonepithelial origin for the abnormal growth.
Doctors determined that the mass was nonepithelial after examining the tissue under a microscope.
Certain cancers are classified as nonepithelial because they do not arise from epithelial cells.
