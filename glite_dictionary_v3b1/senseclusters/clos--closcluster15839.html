clos

noun
narrow passageway
Pronunciation: / kloʊs / (AmE), / kləʊs / (BrE)
a narrow passage, alley, or shared entryway leading to residences, especially in Scottish towns and cities

Examples

A narrow clos
The old tenement building was reached by a narrow clos off the main street.
Children played in the clos while their parents chatted nearby.
Tourists admired the historic clos that led to hidden courtyards in Edinburgh.
