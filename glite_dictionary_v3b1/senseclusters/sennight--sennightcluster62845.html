sennight

noun
period of seven days
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛnaɪt / (BrE)
an old-fashioned word for a period of seven days or a week

Examples

A sennight later
They agreed to meet again in a sennight.
The letter promised a reply within a sennight of its receipt.
In old English texts, you may find the word sennight used instead of 'week'.
