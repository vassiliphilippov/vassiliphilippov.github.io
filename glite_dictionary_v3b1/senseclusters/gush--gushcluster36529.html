gush

noun
a sudden rapid flow of liquid
6.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /gʌʃ/ (AmE), /gʌʃ/ (BrE)
a sudden and rapid flow of liquid or other substance, especially in a large or forceful amount

Examples

A gush of water
A gush of blood followed the deep cut on his arm.
A gush of water burst from the broken pipe, flooding the floor within seconds.
He spoke with a gush of enthusiasm that surprised everyone in the room.

Related Terms
