sleek look

noun
smooth and glossy appearance
Pronunciation: / slik lʊk / (AmE), / sliːk lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance that is smooth, glossy, and stylish, often used to describe hair, clothing, or design

Examples

A sleek look
Modern cars are often designed with a sleek look to appeal to younger buyers.
She wore her hair in a sleek look for the party last night.
The designer aimed for a sleek look in the new office furniture collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glossy - shiny and smooth in appearance
  • antonym: shabby - in poor condition through long use or lack of care
