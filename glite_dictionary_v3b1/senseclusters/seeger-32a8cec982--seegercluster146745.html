Seeger

proper noun
American folk singer and songwriter
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːɡər / (AmE), / ˈsiːɡə / (BrE)
(Pete Seeger) United States folk singer and songwriter who played a major role in the American folk music revival of the 1960s.

Examples

Seeger folk singer
Many credit Seeger with popularizing folk music during the 1960s.
Pete Seeger's songs were influential in American civil rights movements.
The concert honored Seeger and his enduring legacy in folk music.
