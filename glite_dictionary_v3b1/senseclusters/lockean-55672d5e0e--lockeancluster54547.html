Lockean

noun
follower of John Locke
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːkiən / (AmE), / ˈlɒkiən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the ideas and philosophy of John Locke, especially in politics or knowledge

Examples

A Lockean thinker
As a dedicated Lockean, she argued for individual rights and limited government.
The professor explained how a Lockean would approach the concept of private property.
Many Enlightenment thinkers were influenced by Lockean principles in their writings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Locke - John Locke, English philosopher known for his theories on government and knowledge
  • synonym: Lockean - relating to the ideas of John Locke
