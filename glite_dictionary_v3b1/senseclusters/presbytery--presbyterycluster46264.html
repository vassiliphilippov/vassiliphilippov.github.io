presbytery

noun
clergy's residence or church area
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛz.bɪˌtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈprɛz.bɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
A building or part of a church reserved for the residence or use of the officiating clergy.

Examples

The presbytery building
Renovations to the presbytery revealed centuries-old manuscripts hidden in a wall.
The presbytery was open to visitors during the church's annual historical tour.
The old stone presbytery stood adjacent to the village church.

  • synonym: rectory - A house where the rector of a Christian church lives
