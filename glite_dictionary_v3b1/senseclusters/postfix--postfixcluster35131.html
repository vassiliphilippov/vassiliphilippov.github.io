postfix

verb
to add at end
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊst.fɪks/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊst.fɪks/ (BrE)
to add a letter, symbol, or element to the end of a word or item

Examples

To postfix a letter

In some programming languages, you can postfix operators to variables.
The editor instructed her to postfix the citation with the correct year.
