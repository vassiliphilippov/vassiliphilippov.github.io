thunder snake

noun
small reddish snake of eastern US
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndər sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈθʌndə sneɪk / (BrE)
A small reddish, wormlike snake found in the eastern United States, also known as the eastern worm snake.

Examples

A thunder snake in woods
A thunder snake is often found under logs and rocks in moist woodlands.
Many children mistake a thunder snake for an earthworm because of its size and color.
The thunder snake plays an important role in controlling insect populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: worm snake - small, slender snake resembling a worm
