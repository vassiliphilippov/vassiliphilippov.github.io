genus Elaphe

noun
genus of North American rat snakes
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ɪˈlæfi / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɪˈleɪfi / (BrE)
A genus of nonvenomous snakes known as rat snakes, native to North America and parts of Eurasia.
