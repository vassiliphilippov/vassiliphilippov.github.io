takedown

noun
formal notice to remove online content
6.3 0.4/m
Pronunciation: / c8te6ak.da8an / (AmE), / c8te6ak.da8an / (BrE)
a formal notice or request (often under copyright law) requiring a website or service to remove or disable access to specific online content.

Examples

A takedown notice
The platform received a takedown notice from the copyright holder.
After the takedown, the offending video was no longer available in several countries.
Creators sometimes dispute a takedown by filing a counter-notice to restore their content.
