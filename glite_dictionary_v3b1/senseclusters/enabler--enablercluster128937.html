enabler

noun
person who makes possible
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈneɪblər / (AmE), / ɪˈneɪblə / (BrE)
a person, thing, or factor that makes it possible for someone or something to succeed, develop, or function

Examples

A key enabler of change
A good mentor can be an important enabler of success.
Technology is often an enabler of innovation in modern businesses.
Strong communication skills are a key enabler for effective teamwork.
The new software update was a key enabler for the company's digital transformation.
Good communication is often an important enabler of teamwork and productivity.
The invention of the internet became an enabler for many modern technologies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facilitator - a person who helps make an action or process easier
  • antonym: obstacle - something that makes progress difficult
