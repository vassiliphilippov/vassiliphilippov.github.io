hormone production

noun
creation of hormones in the body
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrmoʊn prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːmoun prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process by which hormones are created and released in the body, especially by glands

Examples

Hormone production process
Certain medications can affect hormone production in both men and women.
Doctors monitor hormone production to diagnose endocrine disorders.
Stress is known to influence hormone production in the body.

Related Terms
