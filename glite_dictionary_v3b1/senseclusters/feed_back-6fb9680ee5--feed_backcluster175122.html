feed back

verb
give a response or reaction
1.7
Pronunciation: / fid ˈbæk / (AmE), / fiːd ˈbæk / (BrE)
to give a response or share information in reaction to a query, result, or process

Examples

To feed back comments
Please feed back any comments you have about the new website.
The system allows employees to feed back their suggestions directly to management.
We fed back our initial observations to the research team after the first round of testing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something or someone
  • synonym: respond - to say or do something as a reaction to something that has been said or done
