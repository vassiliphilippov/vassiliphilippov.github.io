surprise

noun
the act of surprising someone
1.0
Pronunciation: / səˈpraɪz /; / sərˈpraɪz / (AmE), / səˈprʌɪz / (BrE)
the feeling or act of being surprised; sudden wonder or astonishment caused by something unexpected

Examples

A big surprise
His sudden arrival was such a surprise that it took me a moment to react.
She planned a surprise for her friend's birthday.
The party was a complete surprise to her, and she couldn't stop smiling.
Her expression changed to one of surprise when she saw the unexpected gift.
Much to his surprise, the party was actually a celebration for his promotion.
The news came as a complete surprise to everyone in the office.

  • synonym: shock - a sudden unexpected event causing strong emotional impact
