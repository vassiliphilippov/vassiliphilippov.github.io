Wende

noun
East German political change
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛndə / (AmE), / ˈvɛndə / (BrE)
the period of major political change in East Germany around 1989–1990, leading to the end of communist rule and reunification

Examples

The German Wende
The Wende brought about the fall of the Berlin Wall and the end of East Germany.
Many families were reunited after the Wende changed the course of German history.
During the Wende, citizens organized peaceful protests demanding political reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reunification - the process of becoming unified again, especially of East and West Germany in 1990
  • synonym: revolution - a sudden and significant change in political power or organization
