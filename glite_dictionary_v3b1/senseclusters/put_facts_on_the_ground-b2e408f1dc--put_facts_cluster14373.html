put facts on the ground

verb
establish or confirm facts concretely
2.5 6.6
Pronunciation: /pʊt fæks ɑːn ðə graʊnd/ /; / /pʌt fækts ɑːn ðə graʊnd/ (AmE), /pʊt fæks ɒn ðə graʊnd/ /; / /pʌt fækts ɒn ðə graʊnd/ (BrE)
