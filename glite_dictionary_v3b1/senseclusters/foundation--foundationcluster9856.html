foundation

noun
the act of establishing something
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of establishing an institution, organization, or system for the first time

Examples

Foundation of a company
He took part in the foundation of a community project aimed at supporting local artists.
The foundation of the university marked a turning point in the city's history.
Their friendship was formed soon after the foundation of the club.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dissolution - the act of officially ending or dismissing an organization
  • synonym: establishment - the act of founding or beginning something new
