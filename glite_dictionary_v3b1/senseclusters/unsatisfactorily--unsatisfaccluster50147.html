unsatisfactorily

adverb
in an unsatisfactory manner
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌsætɪsˈfæktərəli / (AmE), / ʌnˌsætɪsˈfæktərɪli / (BrE)
In a way that does not meet expectations or requirements; in an unsatisfactory manner.

Examples

Answered unsatisfactorily
Her questions were answered unsatisfactorily by the customer service representative.
If a student performs unsatisfactorily, additional support should be provided.
The project was completed unsatisfactorily, requiring several revisions afterwards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: satisfactorily - in a way that meets expectations or requirements
