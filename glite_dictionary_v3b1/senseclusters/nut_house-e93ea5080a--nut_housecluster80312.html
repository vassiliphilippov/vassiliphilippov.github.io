nut house

noun
slang for psychiatric hospital
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌt ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈnʌt ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A slang and offensive term for a psychiatric hospital or insane asylum.

Examples

A nut house hospital
After his breakdown, he spent several months in a nut house.
People used to call any psychiatric facility a nut house, though that's considered offensive now.
She joked that her family was so dysfunctional they belonged in a nut house.
