discardable

adjective
able to be thrown away
4.0
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkɑrdəbəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɑːdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be thrown away or disposed of when no longer needed

Examples

Discardable cups
The discardable cups should be placed in the recycling bin after use.
Many products now come in discardable packaging to make cleanup easier.
He prefers to use items that are not discardable to reduce waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposable - intended to be used once or for a short time and then thrown away
  • antonym: reusable - able to be used again after being used
