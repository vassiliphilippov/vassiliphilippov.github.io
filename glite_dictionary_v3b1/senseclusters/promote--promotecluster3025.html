promote

verb
advertise or publicize to boost sales
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊt / (AmE), / prəˈməʊt / (BrE)
to advertise or publicize a product, idea, or event in order to increase sales or public awareness

Examples

To promote a product
She works in marketing and helps promote various products on social media.
The company hired a famous actor to promote its new line of smartphones.
They launched a campaign to promote awareness of the brand among young adults.
