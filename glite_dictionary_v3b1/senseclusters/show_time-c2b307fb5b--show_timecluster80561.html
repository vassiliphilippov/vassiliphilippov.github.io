show time

noun
scheduled start time of an entertainment event
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ ˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ ˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the scheduled start time of a movie, play, or other entertainment event

Examples

The show time starts

The show time for the movie is listed as 7:30 p.m. on the ticket.
We should get to the theater at least fifteen minutes before show time.
