field maple

noun
a small Eurasian maple tree
Pronunciation: / fiːld ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / fiːld ˈmeɪpəl / (BrE)
a small Eurasian maple tree (Acer campestre) with dense foliage, often used for hedges and ornamental planting

Examples

The field maple tree
In autumn, the leaves of the field maple turn golden yellow.
The field maple makes an attractive hedge in many English gardens.
We planted a row of field maple trees along the driveway for added privacy.
