misreport

noun
incorrect report
6.1
Pronunciation: /mɪs.rɪ.ˈpɔːrt/ (AmE), /mɪs.rɪ.ˈpɔːt/ (BrE)
a report or statement that contains incorrect or false information, often due to error or misunderstanding

Examples

A misreport in news
The newspaper published a misreport about the election results, causing confusion among readers.
After the misreport was discovered, the company quickly released an official statement to clarify the facts.
A single misreport can damage the reputation of a news organization if not corrected promptly.

  • synonym: falsehood - a statement that is not true
