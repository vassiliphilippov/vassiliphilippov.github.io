conformal

adjective
preserving angles
8.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɔrməl / (AmE), / kənˈfɔːməl / (BrE)
preserving angles and local shapes, especially in mathematics or geometry

Examples

Conformal map projection
A conformal map projection preserves the angles of small regions on the globe.
Engineers use conformal coatings to protect electronic circuits without altering their shape.
In mathematics, a conformal transformation keeps the angles between curves unchanged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distorted - changed in shape or form, not preserving angles
  • synonym: isometric - preserving distances and angles
