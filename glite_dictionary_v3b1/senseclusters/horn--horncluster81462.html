horn

noun
metal projection on Western saddle
Pronunciation: / hɔrn / (AmE), / hɔːn / (BrE)
the high, curved projection at the front of a Western saddle, used for securing a rope or holding onto, often metal and covered with leather

Examples

Saddle horn
She wrapped the rope around the horn of her saddle during the cattle drive.
The cowboy gripped the horn tightly as the horse bucked under him.
The leather covering on the horn was fraying from years of use.
