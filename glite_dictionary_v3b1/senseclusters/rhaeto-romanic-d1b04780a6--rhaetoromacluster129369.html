Rhaeto-Romanic

noun
Romance dialects of Switzerland and northern Italy
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪtoʊ roʊˈmænɪk / (AmE), / ˌraɪtəʊ rəʊˈmanɪk / (BrE)
A group of Romance dialects spoken in parts of southeastern Switzerland, northern Italy, and the Tyrol region.
