eclipse

noun
when one celestial body blocks another

an occasion when one celestial body moves into the shadow of another body, temporarily obscuring it from view

Examples

A solar eclipse

Many ancient cultures recorded their observations of a total eclipse in detailed writings.
We watched the solar eclipse through special glasses to protect our eyes.
