cuckoopint

noun
European woodland plant with purple spadix
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkoʊˌpaɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkʊk.uː.paɪnt / (BrE)
a European woodland plant (Arum maculatum) with a greenish or purple-mottled spathe and a short purple spadix, notable for its early spring appearance

Examples

The cuckoopint plant
Children are warned not to touch the berries of the cuckoopint plant as they are poisonous.
The glossy leaves of cuckoopint appear before most other woodland plants in spring.
The woodland floor was dotted with cuckoopint in early April.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lords-and-ladies - another common name for Arum maculatum
