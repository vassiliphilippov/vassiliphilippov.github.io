sedentariness

noun
settled lifestyle
8.5
Pronunciation: / sɛˈdɛntərinəs / (AmE), / sɛˈdɛntərinəs / (BrE)
the state of living in one place and not moving from place to place; a settled, non-migratory lifestyle

Examples

Sedentariness of villagers
The sedentariness of the village allowed its people to develop advanced farming techniques.
Modern sedentariness has led to new challenges in health and community life.
Anthropologists studied the shift from nomadism to sedentariness in ancient societies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nomadism - a lifestyle of moving from place to place
  • synonym: sedentary - living in one place and not moving around
