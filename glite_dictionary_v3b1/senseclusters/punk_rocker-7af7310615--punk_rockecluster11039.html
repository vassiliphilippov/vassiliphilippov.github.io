punk rocker

noun
a member of the punk rock subculture
Pronunciation: / pʌŋk ˈrɑːkər / (AmE), / pʌŋk ˈrɒkə / (BrE)
a teenager or young adult who performs or is enthusiastic about punk rock music and belongs to the punk subculture

Examples

A punk rocker band
He considers himself a true punk rocker and refuses to listen to anything but classic punk bands.
She became a punk rocker in the late 1970s, performing at underground clubs across the city.
The fashion choices of a punk rocker often include leather jackets and spiked hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punk - a member of a counterculture associated with loud music, distinctive fashion, and anti-establishment views
