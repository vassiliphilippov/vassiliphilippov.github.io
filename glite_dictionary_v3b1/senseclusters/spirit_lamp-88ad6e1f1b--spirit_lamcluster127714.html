spirit lamp

noun
lamp burning liquid fuel for heating
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt læmp / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt lamp / (BrE)
a small lamp that burns alcohol or another volatile liquid as fuel, typically used for heating in laboratories or for demonstrations.

Examples
