electronic database

noun
database stored and accessed electronically
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈdeɪtəˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈdeɪtəbeɪs / (BrE)
A database that is stored electronically and can be accessed and managed by computers.

Examples

An electronic database system
Researchers rely on an electronic database to access thousands of scientific studies.
The company stores all customer information in an electronic database for easy retrieval.
Updating the electronic database regularly ensures that the information remains accurate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: database - an organized collection of structured information or data, typically stored electronically
