milkiness

noun
cloudy white quality
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkinəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being milky, especially having a white, cloudy, or opaque appearance like milk

Examples

The milkiness of sky
The milkiness of the morning sky made the sunrise look soft and gentle.
She noticed the milkiness of the soup after adding cream to the pot.
The gemstone's milkiness reduced its transparency but gave it a unique charm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear or transparent
  • synonym: opacity - the quality of being opaque or not transparent
