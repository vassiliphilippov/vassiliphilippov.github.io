chicken-breasted

adjective
having a chest with a protruding breastbone
8.0 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪk.ən ˈbrest.ɪd/ /; / /ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˈbrest.ɪd/ /; / /ˈtʃɪk.ən ˈbrest.əd/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˈbrest.ɪd/ /; / /ˈtʃɪk.ən ˈbrest.ɪd/ /; / /ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˈbrest.əd/ (BrE)
