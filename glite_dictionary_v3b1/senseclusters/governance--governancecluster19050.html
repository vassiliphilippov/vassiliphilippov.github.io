governance

noun
rules and decision-making for decentralized systems
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvərnəns / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənəns / (BrE)
(in computing, especially blockchain and other decentralized networks) the rules, processes, and decision-making mechanisms—often implemented on-chain or via smart contracts—by which participants propose, vote on, and enact protocol changes or allocate shared resources.

Examples

Blockchain governance
The protocol relies on on-chain governance to decide upgrades and allocate resources.
Community members submitted proposals and voted using the platform's governance token to change parameters.
Experts warned that poor governance could allow a small group to control the decentralized protocol.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on-chain governance - decision-making mechanisms implemented directly on a blockchain, typically via tokens and smart contracts.
