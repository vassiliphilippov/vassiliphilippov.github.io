yum

noun
delicious food
7.5
Pronunciation: / jʌm / (AmE), / jʌm / (BrE)
an informal word for food or a dish that tastes very good

Examples

A chocolate yum
The chocolate cake was a real yum at the birthday party.
Everyone agreed that her homemade soup was a total yum on a cold day.
He brought a special yum for the picnic, and it disappeared quickly.
