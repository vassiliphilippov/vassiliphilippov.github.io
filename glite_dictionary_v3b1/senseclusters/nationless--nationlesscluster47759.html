nationless

adjective
without a nation
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈneɪʃənləs / (BrE)
not belonging to or associated with any nation or country

Examples

Nationless refugees
After the war, many people found themselves nationless and searching for a new home.
The artist described his identity as nationless, feeling no strong ties to any country.
Some refugees remain nationless for years while waiting for citizenship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: national - belonging to a particular nation
  • synonym: stateless - not recognized as a citizen by any country
