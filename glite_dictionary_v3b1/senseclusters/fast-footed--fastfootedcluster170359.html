fast-footed

adjective
having rapidly moving feet
5.0
Pronunciation: / fæst ˈfʊtɪd / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈfʊtɪd / (BrE)
able to move the feet quickly; swift or nimble in movement, especially of the feet.

Examples

A fast-footed player
Fans cheered as the fast-footed midfielder weaved through defenders to score a goal.
Her fast-footed reactions allowed her to dodge every obstacle on the course.
The fast-footed sprinter raced past all his competitors in the final stretch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleet-footed - able to move swiftly on one's feet; quick and nimble
