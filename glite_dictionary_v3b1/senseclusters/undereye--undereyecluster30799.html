undereye

noun
area below eye
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌaɪ / (BrE)
the area of skin located directly beneath the eye.

Examples

Undereye area
She applied a cooling gel to her undereye area to reduce puffiness.
Dark circles often appear in the undereye region after a sleepless night.
The makeup artist used a special concealer for the undereye skin.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cheekbone - the bone below the eye forming the prominence of the cheek
  • synonym: eyebag - swelling or puffiness under the eyes
