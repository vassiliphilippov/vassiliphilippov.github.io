unevolved

adjective
not developed
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌniˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌʌniˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
not having developed, changed, or advanced from an earlier or simpler form

Examples

An unevolved species
The island is home to several unevolved plant species found nowhere else.
Some critics argue that the technology remains largely unevolved despite recent updates.
His ideas seemed unevolved compared to the modern theories discussed at the conference.
