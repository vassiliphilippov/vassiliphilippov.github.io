bamboula

noun
festive drumming party
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbuːlə / (AmE), / bæmˈbuːlə / (BrE)
a lively party or gathering featuring African-inspired drumming and dancing, especially in Caribbean or Creole cultures

Examples

A lively bamboula party
The sound of drums filled the air as the bamboula brought the whole village together.
Tourists were invited to join the bamboula and learn traditional dance steps.
At the annual festival, a colorful bamboula was held in the main square.

  • synonym: soiree - an evening party or gathering
