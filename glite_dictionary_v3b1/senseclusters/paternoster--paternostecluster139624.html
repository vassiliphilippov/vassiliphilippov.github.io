paternoster

noun
a continuously moving open elevator
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtərˈnɑːstər / (AmE), / ˌpɑːtəˈnɒstə / (BrE)
a type of elevator consisting of a chain of open compartments which move continuously in a loop, allowing passengers to step on or off at each floor without the lift stopping

Examples

Paternoster elevator ride
Some people feel nervous using a paternoster for the first time because it never stops moving.
The old office building still has a working paternoster that fascinates visitors.
The university decided to keep its historic paternoster as a symbol of tradition and innovation.
