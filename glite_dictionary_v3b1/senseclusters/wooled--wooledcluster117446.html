wooled

verb
to cover with wool
8.4
Pronunciation: / wʊld / (AmE), / wʊld / (BrE)
to cover or line something with wool or a woolen material

Examples

To wooled a basket
The artisan wooled the basket to create a soft, insulated lining for winter use.
For extra warmth, she wooled the inside of her boots before going outside.
They wooled the doghouse to help the puppy stay warm during the cold months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: felt - to cover or line with felt material
  • antonym: uncover - to remove a covering from something
