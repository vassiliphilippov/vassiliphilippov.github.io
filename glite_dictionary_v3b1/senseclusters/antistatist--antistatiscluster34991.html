antistatist

noun
opponent of state control
8.9
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈstæt.ɪst/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈstæt.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who opposes state control or centralized government and supports minimal government intervention

Examples

An antistatist thinker
The new political party attracted many antistatists who wanted to reduce government spending.
As an antistatist, she argued against new regulations on small businesses.
Debates between antistatists and supporters of government programs are common in politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: libertarian - a person who advocates for individual freedom and minimal government
  • antonym: statist - a person who supports strong government control or intervention
