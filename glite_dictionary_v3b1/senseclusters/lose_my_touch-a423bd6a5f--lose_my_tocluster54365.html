lose my touch

verb
to lose a previously held skill
1.0 6.0
Pronunciation: /luːz maɪ tʌtʃ/ /; / /luːz mə tʌtʃ/ (AmE), /luːz maɪ tʌtʃ/ /; / /luːz mə tʌtʃ/ (BrE)
to no longer have the skill or ability that one once had, especially after being proficient at it

Examples

After a long break from painting, she worried she had lost her touch.
