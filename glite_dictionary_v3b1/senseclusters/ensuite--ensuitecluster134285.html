ensuite

adjective
with private bathroom
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈswit / (AmE), / ɒnˈswiːt / (BrE)
having a private bathroom directly connected to a bedroom or other room

Examples

Ensuite bathroom
We booked a hotel room with an ensuite so we would have our own bathroom.
The new apartment features an ensuite attached to the master bedroom.
She prefers to stay in places that offer ensuite facilities for added comfort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shared - used by more than one person
