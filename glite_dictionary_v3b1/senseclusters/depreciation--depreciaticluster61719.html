depreciation

noun
decrease in value or price
Pronunciation: / dəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a reduction in the value or price of something, especially over time or in relation to another currency

Examples

Depreciation of currency
Several factors led to the depreciation of property values in the neighborhood.
The company accounted for the annual depreciation of its equipment in its financial report.
The rapid depreciation of the local currency caused concern among investors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appreciation - an increase in value or price
  • synonym: devaluation - the reduction of the value of a currency
