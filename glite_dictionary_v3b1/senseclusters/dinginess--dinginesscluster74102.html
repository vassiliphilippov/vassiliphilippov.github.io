dinginess

noun
the state of being dirty or discolored
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪn.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdɪn.i.nəs / (BrE)
the state of being dirty, dull, or discolored; lack of brightness or cleanliness

Examples

The dinginess of walls

The dinginess of the curtains made the whole room look gloomy.
Years of smoke had added to the dinginess of the wallpaper.
