range

noun
variety of different things or activities
Pronunciation: / reɪndʒ / (AmE), / reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a number of different things of the same general kind; a variety or assortment

Examples

A range of products
The exhibition features a range of modern and classic paintings.
There is a diverse range of opinions on how to solve the problem.
This job requires you to handle a wide range of tasks every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: array - an impressive display or range of a particular type of thing
  • antonym: uniformity - the quality or state of being the same; lack of diversity
  • synonym: variety - the quality or state of being different or diverse; an assortment
