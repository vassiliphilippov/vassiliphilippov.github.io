nonpoetic

adjective
lacking poetic quality
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnpoʊˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnpəʊˈɛtɪk / (BrE)

Examples

A nonpoetic style

The report was written in a nonpoetic style, with no figurative language or imagery.
He preferred nonpoetic language when discussing scientific topics.
