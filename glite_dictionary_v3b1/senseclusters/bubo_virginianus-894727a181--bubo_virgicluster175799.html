Bubo virginianus

noun
great horned owl's scientific name
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːboʊ vɜːrdʒɪˈneɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈbjuːbəʊ vɜːdʒɪˈneɪnəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the great horned owl, a large brown owl native to North America, notable for its tufted ears and deep hooting call.
