fete

verb
to honor with a celebration or party
9.3
Pronunciation: / feɪt / (AmE), / feɪt / (BrE)
to honor or entertain (someone) lavishly, especially by holding a celebration or party

Examples

To fete a guest
Every year, the city fetes local heroes with parades and speeches.
The company decided to fete its retiring CEO with a grand dinner and awards ceremony.
They feted the Nobel laureate upon his return home from Stockholm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrate - to engage in festivities in honor of something
  • synonym: honor - to show respect or pay tribute to someone
