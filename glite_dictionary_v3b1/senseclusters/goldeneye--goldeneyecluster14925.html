goldeneye

noun
a green lacewing insect with golden eyes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldənˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldənˌaɪ / (BrE)
A green lacewing of the family Chrysopidae, known for its golden-colored eyes and delicate wings.

Examples

A goldeneye insect
At dusk, a goldeneye settled on the windowsill, its iridescent eyes glinting in the light.
Farmers value the goldeneye for its role in controlling pest populations naturally.
The goldeneye is often seen in gardens where it preys on aphids.
