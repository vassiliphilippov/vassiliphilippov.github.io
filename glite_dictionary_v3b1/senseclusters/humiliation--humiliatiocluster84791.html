Synonyms

  • mortification
    8.6 0.28/m
    mortification is a strong, crushing embarrassment that feels very personal and intense, while humiliation often means being shamed or made to feel inferior.
  • abasement
    8.9 0.23/m
    abasement is the act or state of being forced down in rank or dignity, while humiliation is the painful feeling of being made inferior.
  • embarrassment
    3.1 12/m
    embarrassment is an awkward, often mild shame felt in social moments, while humiliation is deeper and involves loss of self respect.
  • shame
    2.3 20/m
    shame is a painful feeling of guilt or disgrace people feel for wrong actions, while humiliation comes from being made to feel foolish or inferior.
  • degradation
    6.3 1.49/m
    degradation is a low, lasting loss of dignity or position often used for conditions, while humiliation is a painful, immediate feeling.
