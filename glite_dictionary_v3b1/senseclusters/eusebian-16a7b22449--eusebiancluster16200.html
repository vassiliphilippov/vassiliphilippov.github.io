Eusebian

adjective
of Eusebius or his works
Pronunciation: / juːˈsiːbiən / (AmE), / juːˈsiːbiən / (BrE)
relating to Eusebius, especially his writings, teachings, or followers in early Christian history

Examples

Eusebian writings
The Eusebian canons are often cited in studies of the early New Testament manuscripts.
Her research focused on Eusebian influence in the development of church history.
Many Eusebian texts provide valuable insight into early Christian theology.
