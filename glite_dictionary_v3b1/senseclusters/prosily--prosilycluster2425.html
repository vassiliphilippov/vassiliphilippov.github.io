prosily

adverb
in a dull or tedious manner
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.zɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.zɪ.li / (BrE)
In a dull, unimaginative, or tedious manner.

Examples

To speak prosily
She described the exciting adventure prosily, missing all the drama and suspense.
The lecturer spoke prosily for over an hour, causing many students to lose interest.
Though the subject was fascinating, the article was written remarkably prosily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prosaically - in a prosaic (unimaginative or straightforward) way
