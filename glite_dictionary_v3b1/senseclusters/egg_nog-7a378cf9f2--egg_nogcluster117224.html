egg nog

noun
traditional creamy holiday drink
7.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /eg nɑːg/ (AmE), /eg nɒg/ (BrE)
A traditional holiday drink made with milk, cream, sugar, beaten eggs, and often alcoholic spirits such as rum or brandy.

Examples

A glass of egg nog
During Christmas, my family always prepares egg nog with a sprinkle of nutmeg on top.
He offered his guests a choice between hot chocolate and egg nog at the party.
Many people add a splash of rum to their egg nog for extra holiday cheer.
