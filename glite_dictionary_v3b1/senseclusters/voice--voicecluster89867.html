voice

noun
computer-generated speaking sound
4.3
Pronunciation: / vɔɪs / (AmE), / vɔɪs / (BrE)
a synthetic or computer-generated speaking sound produced by text-to-speech systems, virtual assistants, or other speech-synthesis software.

Examples

Computer voice output

Developers tested a new voice for the app to improve accessibility for blind users.
