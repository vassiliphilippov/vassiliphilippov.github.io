hypochondriac

adjective
Affected by hypochondria
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈkɑndriˌæk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈkɒndrɪak / (BrE)
Suffering from or affected by hypochondria; excessively worried about one's health without medical reason.

Examples

Hypochondriac fears
Even as a child, he displayed hypochondriac tendencies, constantly fearing illness.
Her hypochondriac concerns led her to visit the doctor almost every week.
The novel's main character is portrayed as a hypochondriac patient obsessed with minor symptoms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - free from illness or anxiety about one's health
  • synonym: neurotic - suffering from excessive anxiety or obsession
