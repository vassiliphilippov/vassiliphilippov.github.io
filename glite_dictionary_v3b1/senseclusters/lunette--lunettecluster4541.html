lunette

noun
temporary detached field fortification
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / luˈnɛt /; / luːˈnɛt / (AmE), / ljuːˈnɛt /; / luːˈnɛt / (BrE)
a crescent-shaped or detached field fortification with two faces and a projecting angle, used in military defense
