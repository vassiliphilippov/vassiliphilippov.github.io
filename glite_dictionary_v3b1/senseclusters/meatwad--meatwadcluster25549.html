meatwad

noun
slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtwɑd / (AmE), / ˈmiːtwɒd / (BrE)
an insulting slang term for a stupid, clumsy, or contemptible person

Examples

He is a meatwad
He called his friend a meatwad after she tripped over the same step twice.
Stop acting like a meatwad and pay attention in class.
The coach was frustrated with the team's meatwad who kept missing easy passes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a person who is very stupid or foolish
