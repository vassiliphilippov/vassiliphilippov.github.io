extortive

adjective
using threats for gain
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɔrtɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɔːtɪv / (BrE)
using or involving threats or force to obtain something, especially money or favors

Examples

Extortive methods
The criminals used extortive methods to get money from local shop owners.
She was afraid of his extortive threats and decided to report him to the police.
The company faced criticism for its extortive business practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackmail - the act of demanding money or favors by threatening to reveal secrets
  • synonym: coercive - using force or threats to make someone do something
  • antonym: voluntary - done by choice, without force or pressure
