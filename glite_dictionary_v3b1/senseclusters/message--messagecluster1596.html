message

verb
send a message (especially electronically)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
to send a message to someone, especially by electronic means such as email, text, or instant messaging

Examples

He prefers to message rather than call his colleagues.

You can message me if you have any questions about the assignment.

She messaged her friends to let them know she would be late.
