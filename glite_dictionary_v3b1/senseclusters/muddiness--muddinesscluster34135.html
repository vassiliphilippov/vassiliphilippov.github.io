muddiness

noun
lack of mental clarity or order
6.2 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌd.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈmʌd.ə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈmʌd.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈmʌd.ə.nəs/ (BrE)
a mental state in which thoughts are unclear and disorganized; lack of mental clarity or order

Examples

Mental muddiness
After a sleepless night, she faced the meeting with mental muddiness.
The muddiness of his thoughts prevented him from making a firm decision.
The report was criticized for the muddiness of its logic and arguments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear, in particular mental sharpness and lucidity
  • synonym: confusion - a state of being unclear or disorganized in mind or understanding
