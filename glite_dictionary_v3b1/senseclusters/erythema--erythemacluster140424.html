erythema

noun

Abnormal redness of the skin caused by dilation of blood vessels, typically as a result of inflammation or sunburn.

Examples

Skin erythema
A rash with pronounced erythema can be an early indicator of an allergic reaction.
Persistent erythema may require further evaluation by a dermatologist.
The patient showed signs of erythema on her arms after spending too long in the sun.

Synonyms

  • rubor
    9.9 0.00/m
    rubor is the clinical term for skin redness related to inflammation, while erythema refers to redness from blood vessel dilation often after sunburn.
  • redness
    3.7 1.36/m
    redness means the skin turns red from injury or irritation, while erythema refers to redness caused specifically by blood vessel dilation.
