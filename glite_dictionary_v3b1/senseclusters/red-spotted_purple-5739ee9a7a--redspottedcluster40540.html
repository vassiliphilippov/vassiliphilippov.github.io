red-spotted purple

noun
North American butterfly with red spots under wings
8.2 8.4
Pronunciation: /red ˈspɑː.təd ˈpɜːr.pəl/ /; / /red ˈspɑː.tɪd ˈpɜːr.pəl/ (AmE), /red ˈspɒt.ɪd ˈpɜː.pəl/ /; / /red ˈspɒt.əd ˈpɜː.pəl/ (BrE)
