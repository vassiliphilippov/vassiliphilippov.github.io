praise

noun
worshipful words or acts
Pronunciation: / preɪz / (AmE), / preɪz / (BrE)
words or acts expressing reverence or homage, especially as an act of worship towards a deity

Examples

Morning praise in church
Every morning, the monks offer praise in the temple.
Her song was a beautiful praise to the divine.
The congregation raised their voices in praise during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: worship - show reverence and adoration for a deity
