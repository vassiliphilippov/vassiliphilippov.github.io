rood screen

noun
screen dividing nave and choir in a church
8.0 9.2
Pronunciation: /ruːd skriːn/ (AmE), /ruːd skriːn/ (BrE)
A decorative screen in a church that separates the nave from the choir or chancel, often supporting a cross or crucifix.

Examples

Medieval rood screen
During restoration, they discovered medieval paintings hidden behind the old rood screen.
The church's rood screen was intricately carved and adorned with gold leaf.
Visitors admired the imposing rood screen that marked the transition to the choir.
