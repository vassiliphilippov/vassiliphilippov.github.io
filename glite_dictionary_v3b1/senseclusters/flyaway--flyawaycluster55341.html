flyaway

noun
loose hair strand
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a small strand of hair that sticks out from the rest and does not stay in place

Examples

A flyaway strand
She used a little gel to smooth down the flyaway hairs around her forehead.
Humidity often causes more flyaway strands, making hair harder to manage.
After brushing, he noticed several flyaway hairs sticking out from his head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frizz - small, curly, or untidy hair strands
  • synonym: stray hair - a hair that is out of place
