carpus

noun
wrist joint; cluster of wrist bones
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pəs / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəs / (BrE)
the cluster of bones forming the joint between the forearm and the hand, commonly known as the wrist

Examples

The carpus bones
He fractured the carpus during the fall and required surgery.
The carpus allows for the flexible movement of the hand.
Veterinarians examined the horse's swollen carpus after the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrist - the joint connecting the hand with the forearm
