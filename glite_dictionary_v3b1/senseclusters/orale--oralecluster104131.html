orale

interjection
informal greeting
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɾɑleɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɑleɪ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to get someone's attention or to greet someone, especially in Mexican Spanish-influenced English

Examples

Orale! Hey there!
He spotted his friend across the street and shouted, "orale!" to get his attention.
At the start of the meeting, someone greeted the group with a cheerful "orale!".
When the team won the game, fans in the crowd yelled "orale!" in celebration.
