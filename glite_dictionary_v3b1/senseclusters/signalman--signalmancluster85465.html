signalman

noun
railway employee operating signals and switches
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəlmən / (BrE)
a person responsible for operating railway signals and switches, especially in a railroad yard

Examples

The signalman controls signals
Due to automation, the role of the traditional signalman has become increasingly rare.
She asked the signalman for directions to the station platform.
The signalman stood watchfully in the cabin, ready to change the points for the approaching train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signaller - person who manages signals, especially on railways (gender-neutral)
