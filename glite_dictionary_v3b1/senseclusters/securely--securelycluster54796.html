securely

adverb
in a safe or firm way
3.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrli / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊəli / (BrE)
in a safe, firm, or protected way; so that something is not likely to move, be lost, or be harmed

Examples

Securely fastened

She locked the documents securely in the safe each night.
The ladder was securely fastened to the wall to prevent accidents.
Please make sure the door is securely locked before you leave the house.
She held the baby securely in her arms while crossing the busy street.
The data was securely stored on an encrypted server to prevent unauthorized access.
Children should be securely fastened into their car seats before driving.
She felt securely protected in her new home.
The money was securely locked in the safe overnight.
