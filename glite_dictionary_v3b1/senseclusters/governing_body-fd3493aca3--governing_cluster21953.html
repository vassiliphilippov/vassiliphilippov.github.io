governing body

noun
an organization with authority to control or direct
6.1 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌv.ər.nɪŋ ˈbɑː.diː/ /; / /ˈgʌv.ər.nɪŋ ˈbɑː.dɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgʌv.ə.nɪŋ ˈbɒd.ɪ/ /; / /ˈgʌv.ə.nɪŋ ˈbɒd.iː/ (BrE)
a group of people or an organization that has the authority to direct, control, or manage an institution or activity

Examples

Sports governing body
Every sports league has a governing body that sets the official rules for competition.
She was recently elected to the university's governing body.
The school's governing body approved the new policy after much debate.
