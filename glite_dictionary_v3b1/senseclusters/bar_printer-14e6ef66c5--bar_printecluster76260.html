bar printer

noun
impact printer using a type-carrying bar
Pronunciation: / bɑr ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / bɑː ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
an impact printer in which a bar carries the type slugs to print characters on paper

Examples

A bar printer machine
The company used a bar printer to handle invoices in the accounting department.
The old bar printer in the warehouse was finally replaced by a modern laser printer.
Unlike dot-matrix printers, a bar printer strikes the entire line simultaneously with a bar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impact printer - a printer that prints by pressing inked type against paper
