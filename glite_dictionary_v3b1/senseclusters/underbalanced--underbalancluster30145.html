underbalanced

adjective
low-pressure drilling
8.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.ˈbæl.ənst/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.ˈbæl.ənst/ (BrE)
relating to a drilling method where the pressure in the wellbore is intentionally kept lower than the surrounding rock formations.

Examples

Underbalanced drilling

Using underbalanced techniques can help increase oil production in certain reservoirs.
Safety protocols are crucial when operating in underbalanced drilling conditions.
