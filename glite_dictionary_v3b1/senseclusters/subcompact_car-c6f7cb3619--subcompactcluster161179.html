subcompact car

noun
a car smaller than a compact car
7.8
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkɑmˌpækt kɑr / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒmˌpækt kɑː / (BrE)
A small car that is smaller than a compact car, typically with limited passenger and cargo space.

Examples

A subcompact car
Many students prefer driving a subcompact car to campus due to lower running costs.
She chose a subcompact car because it was easy to park and very fuel-efficient.
The rental agency offers a variety of vehicles, including several subcompact cars for city driving.
