Khalkha language

noun
standard form of Mongolian spoken in Mongolia
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlkə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkælkə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the standard form of the Mongolian language, spoken by the Khalkha people and serving as the official language of Mongolia
