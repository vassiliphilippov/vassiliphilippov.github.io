cattle boat

noun
cargo ship for transporting livestock
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬l ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈkætl ˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a cargo ship designed for the transport of cattle or other livestock

Examples

A cattle boat ship
A cattle boat needs special facilities to care for the animals during the voyage.
Many immigrants arrived in America on cattle boats converted for passenger use.
The old cattle boat sailed from Ireland to England twice a week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: livestock carrier - a ship designed to carry live animals
