mozzarella

noun
soft white Italian cheese
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /mɑːt.sə.ˈrel.ə/ /; / /mʌt.sə.ˈrel.ə/ (AmE), /mɒt.sə.ˈrel.ə/ (BrE)
