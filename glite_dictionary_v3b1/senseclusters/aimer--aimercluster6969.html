aimer

noun
person who aims
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪmər / (AmE), / ˈeɪmə / (BrE)
a person who aims a weapon, tool, or device at a target

Examples

Skilled aimer
The aimer carefully lined up the sights before pulling the trigger.
Each aimer must practice regularly to improve their accuracy.
During the competition, the aimer remained calm and focused on the target.
