marla

noun
Pakistani land unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrlə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlə / (BrE)
a unit of land area used in Pakistan, equal to about 25 square meters

Examples

A marla plot
The house was built on a three marla plot in Lahore.
He bought ten marla of land to start his new business.
Property prices per marla have increased significantly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acre - a unit of land area commonly used in the UK and US, equal to about 4,047 square meters
  • synonym: hectare - a metric unit of land area equal to 10,000 square meters
