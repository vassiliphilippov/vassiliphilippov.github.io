toothfish

noun
large deep-sea fish
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːθˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtuːθˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large fish found in cold southern or Antarctic waters, also known as Chilean sea bass, important for food and scientific research
