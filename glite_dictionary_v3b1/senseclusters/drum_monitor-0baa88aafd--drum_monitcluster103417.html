drum monitor

noun
stage speaker for drummers
1.0 4.5
Pronunciation: /drʌm ˈmɑːn.ɪ.tər/ /; / /drʌm ˈmɑːn.ə.tər/ (AmE), /drʌm ˈmɒn.ɪ.tə/ /; / /drʌm ˈmɒn.ə.tə/ (BrE)
a stage speaker designed specifically to allow drummers to hear themselves and the band during a live performance

Examples

A drum monitor speaker
A high-quality drum monitor can make a significant difference in live performances.
During the concert, the drum monitor had to be adjusted because it was too loud.
The band rented a drum monitor to ensure the drummer could hear the rest of the musicians clearly.
