predacious

adjective
seeking to exploit others; predatory
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdeɪʃəs / (AmE), / prɪˈdeɪʃəs / (BrE)
seeking to exploit or victimize others for personal gain; predatory.

Examples

Predacious business tactics
He warned investors to beware of predacious schemes offering unrealistic returns.
Some species exhibit predacious behavior, capturing and consuming other animals to survive.
The company was criticized for its predacious business practices that hurt small competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasitic - taking advantage of others without giving anything in return
  • synonym: predatory - seeking to exploit or oppress others
