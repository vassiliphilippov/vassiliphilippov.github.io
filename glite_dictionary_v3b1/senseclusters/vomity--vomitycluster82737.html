vomity

noun
matter from vomiting
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːmɪti / (AmE), / ˈvɒmɪti / (BrE)
the matter that has been vomited or the act of vomiting itself

Examples

A pool of vomity
The smell of vomity lingered in the hallway after the party.
He accidentally stepped in vomity on the sidewalk and had to clean his shoes.
After the roller coaster ride, there was vomity on several of the seats.

  • synonym: vomit - the matter ejected from the stomach through the mouth
