ibex

noun
wild mountain goat with curved horns
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.bɛks / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.bɛks / (BrE)
A wild goat found in the mountains of Eurasia and northern Africa, characterized by large, curved horns.

Examples

The ibex goat
Researchers studied the horns of the Alpine ibex to learn about their growth patterns.
The ibex nimbly leaped from rock to rock along the steep cliff.
We spotted a herd of ibexes during our hike in the Moroccan mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goat - any of various species of domesticated or wild ruminant mammals with backward curving horns and a beard
