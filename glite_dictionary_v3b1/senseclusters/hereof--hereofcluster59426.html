hereof

adverb
of or concerning this (formal/legal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɪrˈʌv / (AmE), / hɪərˈɒv / (BrE)
of or concerning this (document, matter, etc.), especially as used in formal or legal contexts.

Examples

The terms hereof apply
The obligations set forth hereof shall remain in effect until terminated in writing.
The parties to the agreement acknowledge the terms hereof as binding.
The rights granted hereof are subject to revocation at any time by the issuer.
