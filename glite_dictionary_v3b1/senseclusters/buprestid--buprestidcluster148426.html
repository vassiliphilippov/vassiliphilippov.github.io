buprestid

noun
jewel beetle
9.9
Pronunciation: / bjuˈprɛstɪd / (AmE), / bjuˈprɛstɪd / (BrE)
a beetle of the family Buprestidae, often called jewel beetles, known for their bright metallic colors

Many collectors prize the vivid colors of a buprestid in their insect displays.
A single fallen tree can attract dozens of buprestids searching for a place to lay eggs.
