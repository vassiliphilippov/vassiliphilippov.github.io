shrine

noun
holy place associated with veneration
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ʃraɪn/ (AmE), /ʃraɪn/ (BrE)
A place regarded as holy because of its association with a sacred person or relic, and used as a site of worship.

Examples

A shrine to a saint
Pilgrims traveled for miles to visit the ancient shrine on the hillside.
She lit a candle at the family shrine to honor her ancestors.
The cathedral's shrine houses the relics of a medieval saint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altar - a table or flat-topped block used for religious rituals
  • synonym: sanctuary - a sacred or holy place
  • synonym: temple - building devoted to religious worship, especially in ancient religions
