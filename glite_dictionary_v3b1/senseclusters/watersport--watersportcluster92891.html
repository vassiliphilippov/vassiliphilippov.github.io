watersport

noun
sport on water
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌspɔːt / (BrE)
a sport or physical activity that takes place on or in water

Examples

A watersport activity
Kayaking is a popular watersport during the summer.
Many tourists try at least one watersport when they visit the tropical island.
He prefers watersports like surfing and sailing to activities on land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boating - the activity of traveling in a boat for pleasure
  • antonym: land sport - a sport or activity that takes place on land
  • synonym: swimming - the sport or activity of moving through water using the limbs
