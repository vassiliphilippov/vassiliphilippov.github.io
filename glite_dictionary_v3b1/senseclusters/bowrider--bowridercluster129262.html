bowrider

noun
motorboat with bow seating
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊˌraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈbaʊˌraɪdə / (BrE)
a small motorboat with seating in the open front (bow) area for passengers

Examples

A bowrider boat
They bought a new bowrider to enjoy weekends on the river with friends.
The family loaded their picnic basket onto the bowrider before heading out on the lake.
A bowrider is perfect for water sports and relaxing in the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cabin cruiser - a motorboat with a cabin for sleeping
  • synonym: runabout - a small, open motorboat for short trips
