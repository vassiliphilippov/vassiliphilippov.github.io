stressfulness

noun
degree of emphasis
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛsfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈstrɛsfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or degree of being given prominence or emphasis, especially in speech or writing

Examples

Stressfulness of a syllable
The stressfulness of the first syllable changes the word's meaning.
Teachers often discuss the stressfulness of certain words to help students with pronunciation.
In poetry, the stressfulness of syllables can affect the rhythm of a line.
