sickie

noun
pretend sick day
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪki / (AmE), / ˈsɪki / (BrE)
a day off work taken by pretending to be sick, usually when not actually ill

Examples

Take a sickie
He decided to take a sickie and spend the day at the park instead of going to work.
Some employees are tempted to pull a sickie after a late night out.
She was caught taking a sickie when her boss saw her at the shopping mall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absenteeism - frequent absence from work or school
  • synonym: sick day - a day off work due to illness
