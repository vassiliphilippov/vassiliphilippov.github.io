mannequin

noun
female fashion model
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌkɪn / (AmE), / ˈmænɪkɪn / (BrE)
a woman who models clothes to display fashions, especially for designers or on catwalks

Examples

Fashion mannequin
Critics praised the mannequin for her elegant style and grace.
She started her career as a mannequin for a well-known Paris fashion house.
The aspiring mannequin walked confidently down the runway in the designer's latest collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: model - person employed to wear or display clothing and pose for photographs
