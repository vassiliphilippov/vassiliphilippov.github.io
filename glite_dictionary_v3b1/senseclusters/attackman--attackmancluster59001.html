attackman

noun
offensive player in lacrosse
Pronunciation: / əˈtækmən / (AmE), / əˈtækmən / (BrE)
a player whose main role is to score goals, especially in sports like lacrosse

Examples

Lacrosse attackman
The team's leading attackman scored the winning goal in the final seconds.
As an attackman, he is always looking for opportunities to break through the defense.
Coaches often rely on their best attackman to create scoring chances during close games.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defenseman - a player whose main role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring
  • synonym: forward - an attacking player in various team sports
