nonvoting

adjective
without voting rights
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈvoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈvəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
not having the right or ability to vote in a decision or election

Examples

Nonvoting member
She attended the meeting as a nonvoting member of the committee.
The company allows nonvoting shareholders to attend annual meetings but not to cast ballots.
As a nonvoting delegate, he could participate in discussions but not in the final decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: voting - having the right or ability to vote
