Hopkinsian

adjective
of Johns Hopkins University
Pronunciation: / hɑːpˈkɪnziən / (AmE), / hɒpˈkɪnziən / (BrE)
relating to Johns Hopkins University, its faculty, students, or characteristic institutional approaches.

Examples

Hopkinsian research methods
The Hopkinsian approach to research emphasizes interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation.
Many medical breakthroughs have come from Hopkinsian scientists working at the university.
Her teaching style was described as distinctly Hopkinsian by her colleagues.
