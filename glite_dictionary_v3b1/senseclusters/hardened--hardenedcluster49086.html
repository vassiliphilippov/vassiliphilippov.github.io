hardened

adjective
protected against attacks or explosions
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdənd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdənd / (BrE)
protected against attack, particularly by making something resistant to damage, especially from nuclear weapons or explosions

Examples

Hardened military bunker
Bunkers with hardened walls play a crucial role in national defense strategies.
The government invested in hardened shelters to protect vital infrastructure from attack.
The military base was hardened to withstand potential missile strikes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinforced - strengthened or made more resistant to attack
