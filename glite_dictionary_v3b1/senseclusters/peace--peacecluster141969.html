peace

noun
public order and safety
1.0 9.33/m
Pronunciation: / pis / (AmE), / piÀês / (BrE)
public order and security in society, especially as maintained by law; the condition of calm and safety in public places

Examples

Public peace
He was charged with breach of the peace after the protest.
Maintaining peace at public events is a top priority for the police.
The law aims to protect peace in crowded city areas.

Synonyms

    3.8 0.36/m
    public security is protection by authorities in public places to prevent crime and disorder, while peace is calm public order.
  • public safety
    2.5 2.98/m
    public safety is protection from crime and danger for people's well-being in communities, while peace is calm public order enforced by law.
  • security
    2.4 24/m
    security is the state or feeling of being safe from danger or worry, while peace is public calm kept by law.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorder - a state of confusion or lack of public order
  • synonym: order - a state in which laws and rules are obeyed
