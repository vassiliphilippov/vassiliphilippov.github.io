deworm

verb
to remove computer worms
7.2
Pronunciation: / diˈwɜrm / (AmE), / diˈwɜːm / (BrE)
to remove computer worms, which are malicious self-replicating programs, from a computer or network

Examples

To deworm a computer
The IT specialist was called in to deworm the office computers after a security alert.
It is important to regularly deworm your network to prevent data breaches.
After detecting the malware, the team quickly dewormed all affected systems.
