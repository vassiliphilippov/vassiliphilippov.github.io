eye up

verb
look at with interest or intent
5.4
Pronunciation: / aɪ ʌp / (AmE), / aɪ ʌp / (BrE)
to look at someone or something with interest, often showing desire or careful assessment

Examples

To eye up a car
He couldn't help but eye up the shiny new sports car in the dealership window.
She caught him eyeing up the last slice of cake.
The managers were eyeing up candidates for the new position during the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspect - to look at something carefully to assess it
  • synonym: ogle - to look at someone with obvious sexual interest
