asparagine

noun
an amino acid found in proteins and plants
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈspær.əˌdʒin / (AmE), / əˈspær.əˌdʒiːn / (BrE)
a crystalline amino acid found in proteins and many plants, especially asparagus

Examples

Asparagine amino acid
Asparagine is commonly found in asparagus, from which it was first isolated.
Researchers studied how levels of asparagine in the blood affect brain development.
The body can synthesize asparagine, so it is considered a non-essential amino acid.
