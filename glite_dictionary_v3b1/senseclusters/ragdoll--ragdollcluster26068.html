ragdoll

noun
floppy doll or cat breed
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡˌdɑl / (AmE), / ˈræɡˌdɒl / (BrE)
a soft toy made to look like a floppy doll, or a breed of domestic cat known for its relaxed nature

Examples

A soft ragdoll
The little girl hugged her favorite ragdoll tightly as she fell asleep.
A ragdoll cat is famous for its gentle and calm personality.
He bought a handmade ragdoll at the craft fair for his niece.
