spearmint oil

noun
essential oil from the spearmint plant
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrˌmɪnt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈspɪə.mɪnt ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic essential oil extracted from the spearmint plant, used in flavoring, perfumery, and aromatherapy
