pinery

noun
pine tree timber
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnəri / (BrE)
wood or timber that is obtained from pine trees and used for construction or other purposes

Examples

Pinery for building
The cabin was built entirely from pinery harvested in the nearby forest.
They admired the scent of fresh pinery as they entered the newly renovated room.
Using reclaimed pinery gives the furniture a rustic and natural appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardwood - wood from broad-leaved trees, not conifers
  • synonym: pinewood - wood from pine trees
