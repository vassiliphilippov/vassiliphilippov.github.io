meibomian

adjective
of eyelid oil glands
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈboʊmiən / (AmE), / maɪˈbəʊmiən / (BrE)
relating to the glands in the eyelids that produce an oily secretion to prevent evaporation of the eye's tear film
