case

noun
standard quantity in a container
5.1
Pronunciation: / keɪs / (AmE), / keɪs / (BrE)
a box, container, or covering used to protect, carry, or display something (including natural coverings)

Examples

A case of water

The supermarket sells soda by the case at a discount.
We ordered a case of wine for the party.
She opened the case to take out her violin.
The antique clock had a beautifully carved wooden case.
The phone case protects the device from scratches and drops.
He proudly displayed his baseball card collection in a special case in his room.
Jewelry sparkled behind the glass case at the store.
The museum kept the ancient coins in a secure case.
