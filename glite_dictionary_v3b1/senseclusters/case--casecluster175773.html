Synonyms

  • box
    1.0 103/m
    a box is a rectangular container with a lid used for storage, while a case is a broader protective container or cover.
  • container
    1.3 19/m
    a container is any object for holding or moving goods, often large for shipping, while a case is smaller or for display and protection.
  • casing
    3.0 1.12/m
    a casing is the outer housing that protects a device, often fixed and technical, while a case can be portable or used for display.
  • shell
    1.0 22/m
    a shell is a hard natural outer covering like on animals or eggs, while a case covers or stores objects and can be man-made.
