slurry

noun
semi-liquid suspension of fine particles
7.7 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆslÊŒri / (AmE), / ËˆslÊŒri / (BrE)

Examples

The construction workers poured the wet slurry into the molds to create the foundation.
The engineer tested the density of the cement slurry before it was pumped into the well.
