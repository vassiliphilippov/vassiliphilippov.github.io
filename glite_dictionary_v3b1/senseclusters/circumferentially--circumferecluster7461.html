circumferentially

adverb
around the circumference
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmfɚˈɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmfərˈenʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that goes around or along the circumference of something

Examples

Measured circumferentially around
The tree trunk was measured circumferentially to determine its size.
The band was placed circumferentially around the injured limb to stop the bleeding.
The artist painted a line circumferentially around the vase for decoration.
