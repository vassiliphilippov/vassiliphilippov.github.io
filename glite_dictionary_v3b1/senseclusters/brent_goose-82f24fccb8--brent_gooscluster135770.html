brent goose

noun
species of small dark migratory goose
6.2 8.8
Pronunciation: / brɛnt ɡus / (AmE), / brɛnt ɡuːs / (BrE)
a small, dark species of goose (Branta bernicla) that breeds in the Arctic and migrates south for the winter

Examples

The estuary is a vital stopover for migratory brent geese.
