sense of purpose

noun
feeling of having a clear aim or direction
2.3
Pronunciation: / sɛns əv ˈpɝːpəs / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
the feeling or quality of having a clear aim or direction in life or in what one does

Examples

A sense of purpose
During difficult times, maintaining a sense of purpose can help people persevere.
He lost his sense of purpose after retiring from his career.
Her new job gave her a strong sense of purpose and motivation.
