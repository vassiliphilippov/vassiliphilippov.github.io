elaborate

verb
add more detail or expand on an idea
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæbəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlæbəreɪt / (BrE)
to add more detail, develop, or explain something more thoroughly

Examples

To elaborate on a plan

He elaborated his theory with numerous examples and case studies.
The professor asked her to elaborate on her argument during the presentation.

The architect wants to elaborate on the initial design to create a unique structure.

The committee will elaborate a detailed proposal before submitting it to the board.
