lakeweed

noun
aquatic lake plant
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪkˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈleɪkˌwiːd / (BrE)
an aquatic plant or weed that grows in lakes or ponds, often considered undesirable or invasive

Examples

Lakeweed in water
Fishermen sometimes struggle to remove lakeweed from their nets after a long day on the water.
The growth of lakeweed can make swimming in the lake less enjoyable during the summer.
Local authorities organized a cleanup to control the spread of lakeweed in the popular recreation area.
