Chinese mustard

noun
very hot mustard used in Chinese cuisine
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪˌniz ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
a type of very hot mustard made from ground mustard seeds, commonly used in Chinese cuisine as a condiment

Examples

Spicy Chinese mustard sauce
