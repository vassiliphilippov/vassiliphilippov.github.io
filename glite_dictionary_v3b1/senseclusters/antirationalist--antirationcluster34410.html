antirationalist

adjective
opposed to rationalism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈræʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈræʃənəlɪst / (BrE)
opposed to rationalist philosophy, reasoning, or methods

Examples

Antirationalist ideas
The philosopher was known for his antirationalist arguments against scientific reasoning.
Her antirationalist stance led her to reject logical explanations in favor of intuition.
Some critics described the movement as antirationalist because it dismissed evidence-based thinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irrational - not based on reason or logic
  • antonym: rationalist - supporting reason and logic as the main source of knowledge
