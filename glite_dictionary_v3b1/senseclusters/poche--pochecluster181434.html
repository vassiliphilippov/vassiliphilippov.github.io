poche

noun
shaded area on plan
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / pɒˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
the shaded or filled area on an architectural drawing that represents solid elements such as walls or columns

Examples

Poche on a plan
The poche in the blueprint highlights the structural walls of the building.
Students learned to shade the poche accurately to distinguish solid from empty spaces.
On the section drawing, the poche clearly marks the thickness of the stone walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatch - a pattern of lines or shading used to indicate materials or areas on a drawing
