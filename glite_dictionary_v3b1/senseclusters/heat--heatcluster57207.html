heat

noun
intense emotional excitement
5.1 7.18/m
Pronunciation: / hit / (AmE), / hiÀêt / (BrE)
intense emotional excitement or intensity, especially in the context of strong feelings or arguments

Examples

Heat of the moment
In the heat of the argument, they both said things they later regretted.
She spoke with such heat during the debate that everyone paid attention.
The heat of his anger was obvious to everyone in the room.
