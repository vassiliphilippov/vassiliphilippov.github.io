premixed

adjective
fuel mixed before ignition
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmɪkst / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmɪkst / (BrE)
describing a flame, burner, or process in which fuel and oxidizer are combined before ignition

Examples

Premixed burner
The engineer adjusted the premixed burner to achieve a more efficient combustion process.
Safety procedures are important when handling premixed gases in the laboratory.
A premixed flame produces a different temperature profile compared to a diffusion flame.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diffusion - a process where substances mix as they move from areas of high to low concentration
