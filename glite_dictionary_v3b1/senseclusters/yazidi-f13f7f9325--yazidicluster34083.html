Yazidi

proper noun
religious minority group
Pronunciation: / jəˈziːdi / (AmE), / jəˈziːdi / (BrE)
a member of a Kurdish-speaking religious minority group mainly living in northern Iraq

Examples

Yazidi people
The Yazidi people have preserved their unique traditions for centuries.
Many Yazidi families live in villages in the Sinjar region of Iraq.
International organizations have provided aid to the Yazidi community after recent conflicts.
