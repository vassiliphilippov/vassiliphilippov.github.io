wind chime

noun
decorative object that tinkles in wind
Pronunciation: / wɪnd tʃaɪm / (AmE), / wɪnd tʃaɪm / (BrE)
A decorative object made of pieces of metal, glass, or pottery that hang loosely and produce tinkling sounds when moved by the wind.

Examples

A wind chime decoration
He bought a colorful wind chime at the craft fair for his garden.
She hung a wind chime outside her window to enjoy its gentle sounds.
The soft tinkling of the wind chime soothed him as he sat on the porch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a bell or set of bells tuned to a musical scale
