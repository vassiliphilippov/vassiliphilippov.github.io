note-taking

noun
writing important information
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtˌteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtˌteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or skill of writing down important information to help remember or review it later

  • antonym: transcribing - writing down spoken words exactly as heard
