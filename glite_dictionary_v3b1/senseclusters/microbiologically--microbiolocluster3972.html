microbiologically

adverb
in a microbial way
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the study or analysis of microscopic living organisms such as bacteria or fungi

Examples

Tested microbiologically
The laboratory analyzed the soil microbiologically to detect any harmful bacteria.
Food products are often tested microbiologically to ensure they meet safety standards.
The research team examined the samples microbiologically for signs of contamination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biologically - in a way that relates to biology or living organisms
  • antonym: chemically - in a way that relates to chemistry or chemicals
