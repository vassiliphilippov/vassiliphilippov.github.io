Perspex

noun
transparent acrylic plastic (trade name)
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrspɛks / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsˌpɛks / (BrE)
a brand name for a transparent thermoplastic acrylic material used as a lightweight or shatter-resistant alternative to glass

Examples

A sheet of Perspex
She cut a sheet of Perspex for her art project.
The display cases are covered with Perspex to protect the artifacts.
The window was made from Perspex to prevent accidental breakage.
