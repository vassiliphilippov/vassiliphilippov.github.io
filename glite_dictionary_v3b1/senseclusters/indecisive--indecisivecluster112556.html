indecisive

adjective
not clearly defined or fixed
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv / (BrE)
not clearly defined or having no distinct limits; vague or ambiguous.

Examples

Indecisive answer
The argument centered on indecisive definitions in the company's policy.
The outcome of the match was still indecisive until the very last minute.
The peace treaty was delayed by indecisive borders between the two nations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: definite - clearly defined or determined
  • synonym: vague - not clearly expressed, defined, or understood
