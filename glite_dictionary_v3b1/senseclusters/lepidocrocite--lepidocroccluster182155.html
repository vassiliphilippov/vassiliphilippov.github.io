lepidocrocite

noun
a red iron oxide mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: /lep.ɪ.də.ˈkrɑː.kaɪt/ (AmE), /lep.ɪ.də.ˈkrɒ.kaɪt/ (BrE)
a red to reddish-brown mineral consisting of iron oxide hydroxide, often found in iron ores together with goethite

Examples

Red lepidocrocite mineral
Collectors prize crystals of lepidocrocite for their distinctive reddish color.
Rust can sometimes form as lepidocrocite rather than the more common goethite.
The geologist identified lepidocrocite in the iron ore sample under the microscope.
