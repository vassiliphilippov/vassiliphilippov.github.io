diarist

noun
person who keeps a diary
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪərɪst / (AmE), / ˈdaɪərɪst / (BrE)
A person who regularly writes a diary or journal.

Examples

A diarist writes
As a dedicated diarist, she wrote faithfully in her journal each night.
Many historians value the work of a diarist for its personal perspective on historical events.
The famous diarist recorded every detail of her daily life during the war.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers or magazines or prepares news to be broadcast
  • synonym: memoirist - a person who writes memoirs
