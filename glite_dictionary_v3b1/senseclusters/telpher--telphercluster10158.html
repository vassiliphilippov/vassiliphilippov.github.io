telpher

noun
cable car in a telpherage system
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlfər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlfə / (BrE)
a small vehicle or car that runs on suspended cables, used in telpherage systems for carrying goods or passengers

A telpher car
