corn dog

noun
sausage deep-fried in cornmeal batter on a stick
4.9
Pronunciation: / kɔrn dɔg / (AmE), / kɔːn dɒg / (BrE)
A sausage, especially a hot dog, coated in a thick layer of cornmeal batter and deep-fried, typically served on a stick.

Examples

A corn dog on stick
She bought a corn dog at the fair for lunch.
The kids love eating corn dogs at birthday parties.
You can find corn dogs at most concession stands during sporting events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage served in a sliced bun
