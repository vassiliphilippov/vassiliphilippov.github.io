sleeper

noun
undercover agent awaiting activation
4.1 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsliː.pər/ (AmE), /ˈsliː.pə/ (BrE)
a spy, terrorist, or saboteur who is placed in an enemy country and lives as a normal citizen until activated by a prearranged signal

Examples

Sleeper agent spy
During the investigation, it was revealed that the sleeper received orders from a foreign intelligence agency.
In spy novels, a sleeper often blends seamlessly into society until activated.
The authorities uncovered a sleeper who had been living quietly in the suburbs for years.
