midsemester

noun
middle of semester
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪd.səˈmɛstər / (AmE), / ˌmɪd.sɪˈmɛstə / (BrE)
the middle period or point of an academic semester, often marked by exams or important assignments

Examples

A midsemester break
Many students feel stressed around midsemester due to exams and assignments.
The university scheduled a break at midsemester to help students recharge.
Professors often give feedback on major projects during midsemester.

Related Terms

  • synonym: midterm - an exam or period in the middle of a school term
  • degree lower: semester - a half-year term in a school or university
