skin doctor

noun
informal term for dermatologist
Pronunciation: / skɪn ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / skɪn ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
an informal term for a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating skin disorders

Examples

Skin doctor appointment
I made an appointment with the skin doctor to check out a rash on my arm.
My cousin is training to become a skin doctor after graduating from medical school.
The skin doctor prescribed a cream for my eczema.
