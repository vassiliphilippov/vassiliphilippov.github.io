horse

noun
large domesticated mammal for riding or work
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɔrs / (AmE), / hɔːs / (BrE)
A large, solid-hoofed four-legged mammal that has been domesticated since prehistoric times for riding, work, and racing.

Examples

Ride a horse
A horse can run at impressive speeds when galloping across open land.
Farmers often relied on a horse to pull heavy plows before the invention of tractors.
The children rode a horse along the beach during their summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: mare - an adult female horse
  • degree higher: stallion - an adult male horse, especially one not castrated
