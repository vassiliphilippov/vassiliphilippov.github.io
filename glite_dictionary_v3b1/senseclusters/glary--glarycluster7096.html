glary

adjective
unpleasantly bright
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɛri / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːri / (BrE)
unpleasantly bright or dazzling, especially because of strong or harsh light

Examples

Glary stadium lights
The glary lights in the stadium made it difficult to see the players clearly.
She shielded her eyes from the glary reflection on the water.
After stepping outside, the glary sunlight forced him to squint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzling - extremely bright or impressive
  • antonym: dim - not bright; lacking light
  • synonym: harsh - unpleasantly strong or intense
