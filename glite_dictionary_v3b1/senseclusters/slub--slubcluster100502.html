slub

noun
lump or unevenness in yarn or fabric
Pronunciation: / slʌb / (AmE), / slʌb / (BrE)
a soft lump or uneven thickness in yarn or fabric, either as an imperfection or intentionally created for texture

Examples

A slub in fabric
A slub in the thread gave the scarf a handcrafted appearance.
She chose a fabric with a pronounced slub to add visual interest to her design.
The unique texture of the linen came from the random slub in the yarn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knot - an imperfection or lump in yarn or thread
