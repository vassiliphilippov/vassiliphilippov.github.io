infectious energy

noun
enthusiasm that spreads to others
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛkʃəs ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛkʃəs ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
an enthusiasm or vitality that spreads quickly to others; an ability to make others feel energized or excited

Examples

Infectious energy spreads quickly
Her infectious energy made the whole team feel excited about the project.
The speaker's infectious energy kept the audience engaged throughout the event.
You can't help but smile when you're around his infectious energy.
