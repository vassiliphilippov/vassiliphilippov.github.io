Galla

proper noun
offensive term for Oromo
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈɡælə / (BrE)
an offensive historical term formerly used for the Oromo people of Ethiopia; now considered derogatory and not used

Examples

The term Galla
The word Galla is now widely recognized as offensive and should not be used.
Historians note that Galla was a term imposed by outsiders and is rejected by the Oromo people.
Modern texts use 'Oromo' instead of Galla to refer to the ethnic group in Ethiopia.
