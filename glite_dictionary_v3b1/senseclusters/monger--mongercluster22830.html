monger

noun
person who deals in a particular type of goods
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who buys and maintains a stock of goods for sale; a dealer or trader, especially in a particular commodity.

Examples

A fish monger
As a longtime book monger, she always knew where to find rare editions.
He was a fish monger who supplied restaurants across the city.
The cheese monger offered samples of her finest aged varieties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dealer - a person whose business is buying and selling a particular product
  • synonym: trader - a person who buys and sells goods, currency, or stocks
