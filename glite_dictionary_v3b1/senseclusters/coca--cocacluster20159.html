coca

noun
dried leaves of the coca plant, used as a stimulant
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkə / (BrE)
The dried leaves of the coca plant, especially those chewed by Andean people for their stimulant effects.

Examples

Chewing coca leaves
Farmers in Peru use coca as part of cultural rituals and local medicine.
The government regulates the cultivation of coca due to its connection with cocaine production.
Traditional healers in the Andes often chew coca leaves to combat fatigue and hunger.
