thirst

verb
to feel the need to drink
1.0
Pronunciation: / θɜrst / (AmE), / θɜːst / (BrE)
to feel the need to drink liquid; to experience thirst

Examples

To thirst for water
After running for an hour in the sun, I really thirst for a cold glass of water.
Hikers often thirst in the desert due to the dry heat.
She thirsted all day because she forgot her water bottle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hunger - to feel the need to eat
