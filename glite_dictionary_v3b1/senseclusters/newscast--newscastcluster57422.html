newscast

verb
to present news
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌkɑːst / (BrE)
to present or broadcast news on radio or television as a news program.

Examples

To newscast live
She began newscasting for the local station last summer and now anchors the evening show.
Many journalists dream of newscasting on national television one day.
He has been newscasting live events for over a decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to transmit a program or information by radio or television
  • synonym: report - to give a spoken or written account of something, especially news
