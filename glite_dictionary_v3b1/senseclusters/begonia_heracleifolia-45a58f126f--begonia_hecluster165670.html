Begonia heracleifolia

noun
species of begonia with star-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / bəˈɡoʊniə həˌrækliəˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə hɛˌrækliəˈfəʊliə / (BrE)
A species of rhizomatous begonia with pointed, star-shaped lobed leaves and pink flowers, native to Mexico.
