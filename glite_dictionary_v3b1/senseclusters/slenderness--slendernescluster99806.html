slenderness

noun
the quality of being thin or narrow
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛndɚnəs / (AmE), / ˈslɛndənəs /; / ˈslɛndənɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being thin, narrow, or gracefully slim in shape or appearance, especially in a positive or attractive way

Examples

The slenderness of her figure
She admired the slenderness of the model's waist.
The slenderness of the columns gave the pavilion an elegant appearance.
The slenderness of the tree trunks made them sway easily in the wind.
Grace admired the slenderness of the model as she walked down the runway.
Her slenderness is often complemented by her elegant posture.
The artist tried to capture the slenderness of the dancer in his sketch.

  • synonym: thinness - the quality or state of being thin
