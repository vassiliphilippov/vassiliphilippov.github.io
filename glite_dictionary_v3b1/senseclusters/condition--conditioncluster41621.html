condition

verb
make smooth/soft by applying conditioner
1.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
to apply a substance, especially a conditioner, to make hair or another material smooth, soft, and shiny

Examples

To condition hair
After shampooing, I always condition my hair to keep it soft and manageable.
She conditioned her hair with a deep treatment to restore its shine.
You should condition leather jackets regularly to prevent them from drying out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moisturize - to apply moisture, especially to skin or hair
