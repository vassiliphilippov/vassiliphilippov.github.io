gum elastic

noun
natural elastic material from latex
8.7 6.0
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm ɪˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ɡʌm ɪˈlæstɪk / (BrE)
a natural elastic material obtained from the latex of certain trees, especially Hevea and Ficus, and used in making rubber products
