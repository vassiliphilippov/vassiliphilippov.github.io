sweatshirt

noun
cotton athletic pullover garment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A loose, long-sleeved pullover made of thick cotton, worn especially for sport or casual wear.

Examples

A sweatshirt with hood
He wore a sweatshirt and jeans to the park on the chilly morning.
Her favorite sweatshirt is soft and comfortable after years of washing.
Many athletes warm up in a sweatshirt before their events.
