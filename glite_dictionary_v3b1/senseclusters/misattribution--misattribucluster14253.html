misattribution

noun
wrong assignment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌætrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌætrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
the act of wrongly assigning the origin, source, or cause of something to the wrong person or thing
