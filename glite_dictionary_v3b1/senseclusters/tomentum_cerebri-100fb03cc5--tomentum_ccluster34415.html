tomentum cerebri

noun
network of brain blood vessels
9.9
Pronunciation: / toʊˈmɛntəm səˈriːbraɪ / (AmE), / təʊˈmɛntəm səˈriːbri / (BrE)
a network of tiny blood vessels located between the cerebral surface of the pia mater and the cerebral cortex

Examples

The tomentum cerebri network
Detailed illustrations showed the position of the tomentum cerebri relative to the pia mater.
During the procedure, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the tomentum cerebri.
The integrity of the tomentum cerebri is vital for proper blood supply to the cerebral cortex.
