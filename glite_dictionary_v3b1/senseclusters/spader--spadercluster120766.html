spader

noun
soil-digging tool
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪdər / (AmE), / ˈspeɪdə / (BrE)
a tool or machine used for digging or turning soil, especially in gardening or farming

Examples

A garden spader
The farmer used a spader to break up the hard ground before planting seeds.
Modern gardeners often prefer a mechanical spader for preparing large vegetable plots.
After renting a spader, she finished turning the soil in half the usual time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shovel - a tool with a broad blade for lifting and moving material
  • synonym: spade - a hand tool with a flat blade for digging
