outta

preposition
informal 'out of'
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊɾə / (AmE), / ˈaʊtə / (BrE)
an informal contraction of 'out of', used in casual speech to indicate movement from inside to outside or lack of something

Examples

To be outta time
I am outta here as soon as the meeting ends.
She ran outta patience after waiting for an hour.
They were outta milk, so I went to the store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: out of - from inside to outside; lacking something
