squirrel

verb
to hide for later
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːəl / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
to hide or store something in a secret or safe place for future use

Examples

To squirrel away money
He squirreled away some money every month for emergencies.
She squirrels snacks in her backpack so she always has something to eat.
Over the years, they squirreled important documents in a locked drawer.
He squirrels away snacks in his desk drawer for late-night study sessions.
They squirrelled important documents in a hidden compartment for safekeeping.
She has been squirrelling money away to buy a new laptop.
He would squirrel away coins in a jar under his bed for emergencies.
Many animals squirrel food away to survive the winter months.
She tried to squirrel her favorite snacks in the back of the cupboard.

  • synonym: stash - to store something secretly for future use
