quintain

noun
jousting practice target
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪnˌteɪn / (AmE), / ˈkwɪnˌteɪn / (BrE)
a post or target used in medieval times for jousting practice by knights

Examples

Wooden quintain target
The knight aimed his lance at the quintain during training.
Young squires practiced their skills by striking the quintain in the castle yard.
A wooden quintain stood at the center of the field for the tournament practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joust - a medieval sport where two knights fight on horseback with lances
  • synonym: target - an object that is aimed at in practice or competition
