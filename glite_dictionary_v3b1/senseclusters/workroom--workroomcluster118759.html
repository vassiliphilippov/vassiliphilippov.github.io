workroom

noun
room for doing work
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkruːm / (BrE)
a room in which work, especially manual or artistic work, is done

Examples

A workroom for crafts
He converted the spare bedroom into a workroom for his woodworking hobby.
Natural light flooded the artist's workroom, illuminating her canvases as she painted.
The school provided a workroom where students could collaborate on science projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workspace - an area where work is done
