gamelan

noun
traditional Indonesian musical ensemble
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæməˈlɑn / (AmE), / ˈɡæməˌlæn / (BrE)
a traditional Indonesian musical ensemble featuring tuned percussion instruments such as metallophones, xylophones, drums, and gongs, often made from bronze or wood

Examples

Traditional gamelan music
At the cultural festival, the intricate rhythms of the gamelan captivated the audience.
He learned to play gongs and metallophones in a local gamelan during his trip to Bali.
The museum displayed several traditional instruments used in a Javanese gamelan ensemble.
