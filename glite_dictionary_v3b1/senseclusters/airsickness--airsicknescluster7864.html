airsickness

noun
nausea from flying in aircraft
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈeəˌsɪknɪs / (BrE)
a feeling of nausea and discomfort experienced while traveling by air, especially during turbulence or motion

Examples

airsickness during flights
Many passengers suffer from airsickness during turbulent flights.
She takes medication to prevent airsickness when she travels by plane.
Symptoms of airsickness include dizziness, nausea, and sweating.
