overdressed

adjective
dressed too elaborately
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdrɛst / (BrE)
wearing clothes that are too elaborate or formal for the occasion

Examples

Overdressed for a party
At the picnic, her suit and tie made her look overdressed compared to everyone else.
He realized too late that he was overdressed for the informal office party.
She felt overdressed at the casual barbecue in her fancy dress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: underdressed - wearing clothes that are too casual or not formal enough for the occasion
