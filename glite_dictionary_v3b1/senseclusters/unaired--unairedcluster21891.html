unaired

adjective
not made public
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈeəd / (BrE)
not expressed, discussed, or made public, especially regarding opinions, remarks, or grievances

Examples

Unaired opinions

The documentary included previously unaired interviews with the main witnesses.
She kept her doubts unaired to avoid causing any conflict in the group.
