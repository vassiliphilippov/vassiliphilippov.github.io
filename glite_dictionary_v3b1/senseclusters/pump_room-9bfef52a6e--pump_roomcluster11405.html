pump room

noun
room at a spa for dispensing mineral water
8.4
Pronunciation: / pʌmp ruːm / (AmE), / pʌmp ruːm / (BrE)
a room at a spa where medicinal waters are dispensed, often serving as a social gathering place for patrons

Examples

Spa pump room
Guests gathered in the pump room to sample the mineral-rich water and socialize.
In the 18th century, the pump room was the heart of fashionable society at the spa.
The famous Bath pump room still attracts visitors seeking the historic spa experience.
