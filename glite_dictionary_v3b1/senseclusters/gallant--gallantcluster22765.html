gallant

noun
fashion-conscious man
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlænt / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.ənt / (BrE)
a man who pays particular attention to his dress and appearance, often in a refined or showy manner

Examples

A gallant dresser
Among the city’s fashionable elite, a gallant was often recognized by his impeccable taste.
In the ballroom, every gallant vied for the admiration of the ladies with his elegant attire.
The young gallant adjusted his cravat before stepping outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fop - a man overly concerned with his clothes and appearance
