sector analysis

noun
assessment of an economic sector
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛktɚ əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛktə əˈnaləsɪs / (BrE)
A systematic review and evaluation of a particular sector of the economy, focusing on its current state and future outlook.

Examples

Sector analysis report
Banking institutions often conduct regular sector analysis to identify potential growth opportunities.
Effective sector analysis can help businesses anticipate market changes and plan strategic moves.
The investment team performed a thorough sector analysis before recommending technology stocks.
