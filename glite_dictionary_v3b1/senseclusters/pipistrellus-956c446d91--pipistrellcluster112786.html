Pipistrellus

noun
genus of small bats
Pronunciation: / pɪpɪˈstrɛləs / (AmE), / pɪpɪˈstrɛləs / (BrE)
A genus of very small bats found nearly worldwide, commonly known as pipistrelle bats.

Examples

Pipistrellus bats
