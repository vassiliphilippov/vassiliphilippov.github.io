cortical region

noun
area of the cerebral cortex
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtɪkəl ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtɪkəl ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
any area of the cerebral cortex distinguished by structure or function.

Examples

Cortical region of brain
Damage to a specific cortical region may result in the loss of certain cognitive abilities.
Researchers mapped the activity patterns across different cortical regions during memory tasks.
The visual cortical region processes information from the eyes to help us see.
