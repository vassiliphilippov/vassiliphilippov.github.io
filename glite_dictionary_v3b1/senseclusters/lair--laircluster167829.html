lair

verb
to shelter in a den
7.5
Pronunciation: / lɛr / (AmE), / leə / (BrE)
to provide or take shelter in a den or hiding place

Examples

To lair in a cave
The wolf laired deep in the forest during the harsh winter months.
After a long day, the animal laired in its cozy den to rest.
The bear laired in a cave to protect itself from predators.
