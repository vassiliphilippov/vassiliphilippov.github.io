hotkey

noun
keyboard shortcut
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌki / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌkiː / (BrE)
a key or combination of keys on a computer keyboard that quickly performs a specific function in software

Examples

Press the hotkey
You can use the hotkey Ctrl+C to quickly copy selected text.
She set up a custom hotkey to launch her favorite application instantly.
Learning the right hotkey combinations can make your work much faster.

  • synonym: shortcut - a quicker way to perform a task, especially on a computer
