strainer

noun
device for separating solids from liquids
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈstreɪnə / (BrE)
A device with a mesh or perforated surface used to separate solid matter from liquid, or coarse from fine solid matter.

Examples

A strainer for food
He held a strainer over the sink while rinsing the berries.
She poured the pasta into the strainer to drain the water.
Use a strainer to remove seeds from the juice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filter - a device to remove impurities or particles from a fluid
  • synonym: sieve - a device with a mesh for separating coarse from fine particles
