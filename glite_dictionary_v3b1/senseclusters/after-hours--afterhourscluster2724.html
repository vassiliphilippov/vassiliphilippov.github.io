after-hours

adjective
after the usual closing time
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.tɚ ˌaʊ.ɚz / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə ˌaʊ.əz / (BrE)
happening or open after the usual or legally established closing time, especially of businesses or clubs

Examples

An after-hours club
An after-hours club was operating in the basement of the restaurant.
Only members have access to the after-hours gym facilities.
The bar hosts an after-hours party for employees every Friday night.
