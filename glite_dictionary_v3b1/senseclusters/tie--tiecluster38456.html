tie

noun
fastener for joining or connecting
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
a cord, string, ribbon, or similar material used for fastening or tying things together

Examples

A plastic tie

Builders used a strong tie to join the beams together.
The cables were held in place with a metal tie.
He used a tie to secure the bundle of newspapers together.
She searched for a tie to keep her books from falling apart.
The bag was held closed with a simple piece of tie.

  • synonym: link - a connecting element or means of joining
  • synonym: ribbon - a long, narrow strip of fabric, often used for tying
