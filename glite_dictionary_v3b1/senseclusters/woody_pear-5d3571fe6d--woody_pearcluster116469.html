woody pear

noun
Australian tree with woody pear-shaped fruit
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdi ˈpɛr / (AmE), / ˈwʊdi ˈpɛə / (BrE)
a tree native to Australia that produces pear-shaped fruit with a thick woody outer layer

Examples

The woody pear tree
Gardeners often plant woody pear trees for their attractive foliage and unique fruit.
The woody pear thrives in nutrient-poor soils in parts of Australia.
The fruit of the woody pear is hard and valued for its unusual appearance.
