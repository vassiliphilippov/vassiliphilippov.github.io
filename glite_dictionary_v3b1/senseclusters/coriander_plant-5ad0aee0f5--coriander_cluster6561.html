coriander plant

noun
aromatic herb; coriander or cilantro
Pronunciation: / kɔˈriːəndər plænt / (AmE), / kɒˈriːəndə plɑːnt / (BrE)
An aromatic Old World herb with feathery leaves and seeds used as a spice, also known as coriander or cilantro.

Examples

Fresh coriander plant leaves
Many Indian recipes call for fresh leaves from the coriander plant.
She grew a coriander plant on her kitchen windowsill for easy access to its fragrant herbs.
The coriander plant thrives in well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cilantro - the leafy part of the coriander plant, especially in American English
  • synonym: coriander - the same herb; also refers to the seeds or leaves used in cooking
