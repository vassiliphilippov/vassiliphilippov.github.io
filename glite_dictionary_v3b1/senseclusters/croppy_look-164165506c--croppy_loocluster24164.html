croppy look

noun
style with cropped features
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑːpi lʊk / (AmE), / ˈkrɒpi lʊk / (BrE)
A style characterized by features that are cropped, often resulting in a shortened or truncated appearance, especially in visual art or fashion.

Examples

A croppy look style
Many modern advertisements favor a croppy look, highlighting just the central part of the image.
She wanted a croppy look for her new hairstyle, with short bangs and layers.
The artist gave her portraits a croppy look by focusing only on the subjects' faces.
