org

noun
informal for organization
Pronunciation: / ɔːrɡ / (AmE), / ɔːɡ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for 'organization', meaning a group of people working together for a common purpose

Examples

A local org
She volunteers at a local org that helps the homeless.
The student org organized a charity event on campus last weekend.
He started a new tech org to support young entrepreneurs.
Several local orgs are working together on the project.
She volunteers at a few different orgs in her community.
Many tech orgs offer remote work options for their employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: company - a business or association
  • synonym: organization - a group of people working together for a shared goal
