mist coat

verb
to prime with thin paint
6.4
Pronunciation: / mɪst koʊt / (AmE), / mɪst kəʊt / (BrE)
to apply a thin, diluted layer of paint to a surface as a primer before the main coat

Examples

To mist coat plaster
It is important to mist coat new plaster walls before applying the final color.
The decorator advised us to mist coat the ceiling to help the paint stick better.
After you mist coat the surface, let it dry completely before continuing with the next layer.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: paint - to cover a surface with paint
  • synonym: prime - to prepare a surface for painting by applying a base layer
