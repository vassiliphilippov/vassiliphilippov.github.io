snobbishness

noun
quality of being a snob; condescension
7.4 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsn…ëÀêb…™ Én…ôs / (AmE), / Ààsn…íb…™ Én…ôs / (BrE)
the attitude or quality of behaving as though one is superior to others, especially to people considered to be of lower social status

Examples

Snobbishness attitude
His snobbishness made it difficult for him to make friends among his new classmates.
She was criticized for her snobbishness at the charity event.
The novel explores themes of snobbishness within the upper classes of society.
