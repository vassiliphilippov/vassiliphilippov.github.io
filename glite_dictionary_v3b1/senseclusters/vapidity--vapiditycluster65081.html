vapidity

noun
dullness; lack of liveliness or interest
9.9
Pronunciation: / vəˈpɪdəti / (AmE), / vəˈpɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being dull, lacking in liveliness, or insipid

Examples

The vapidity of speech
Despite the actors’ best efforts, the play suffered from a certain vapidity that left the audience unmoved.
His conversation was marked by a vapidity that made it hard to stay interested for long.
The novel's vapidity became clear after just a few uninspired chapters.
