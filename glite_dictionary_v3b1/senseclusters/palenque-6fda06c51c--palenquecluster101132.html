Palenque

proper noun
Colombian village
Pronunciation: / paˈleŋkeɪ / (AmE), / paˈleŋkeɪ / (BrE)
a village in northern Colombia, officially San Basilio de Palenque, founded by escaped slaves and known for its Afro-Colombian culture
