misunderstand

verb
fail to interpret correctly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪs.ˌʌn.dɚˈstænd / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.ʌn.dəˈstænd / (BrE)
to fail to interpret or comprehend something correctly; to interpret in the wrong way

Examples

To misunderstand instructions
If you misunderstand the instructions, you might assemble the furniture incorrectly.
Mark clearly misunderstood the rules and made a mistake on the test.
Please don’t misunderstand my intentions; I only want to help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misinterpret - to interpret something incorrectly
  • antonym: understand - to perceive the intended meaning of something
