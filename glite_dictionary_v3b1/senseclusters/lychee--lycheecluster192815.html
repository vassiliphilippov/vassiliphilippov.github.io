lychee

noun
a sweet, round fruit native to China
7.9 4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.tʃi /; / ˈliːtʃi /; / ˈliː.tʃiː / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.tʃi /; / ˈliːtʃi /; / ˈlɪtʃ.i / (BrE)
