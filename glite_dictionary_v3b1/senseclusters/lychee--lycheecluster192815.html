lychee

noun
a sweet, round fruit native to China
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.tʃi /; / ˈliːtʃi /; / ˈliː.tʃiː / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.tʃi /; / ˈliːtʃi /; / ˈlɪtʃ.i / (BrE)
A small, round fruit with a thin, rough shell, sweet translucent flesh, and a single large seed, native to China and often eaten fresh or dried.
