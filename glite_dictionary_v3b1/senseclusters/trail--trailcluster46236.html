trail

noun
clues or evidence leading to a discovery
3.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /treɪl/ (AmE), /treɪl/ (BrE)
A series of clues or evidence that may lead to solving a problem or discovering something, especially in an investigation.

Examples

A trail of clues
Even after months, detectives kept searching for a new trail in the missing person's case.
The trail left by the hacker eventually led investigators to a foreign server.
The police followed the trail of evidence all the way to the suspect's hideout.
