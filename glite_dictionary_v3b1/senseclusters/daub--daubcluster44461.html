daub

verb
to coat or smear with a substance
9.9 6.4
Pronunciation: / dɑb /; / dɔb / (AmE), / dɔːb / (BrE)
to coat or smear a surface with a thick or sticky substance, especially in a rough or careless way

Examples

To daub paint on
People often daub mud onto huts to seal the walls.
She daubed some paint on the canvas without much care.
The workers began to daub the walls with fresh plaster.
Children daubed mud on each other’s faces while playing in the garden.
She took a brush and daubed paint onto the old wooden fence.
The workers began to daub the walls with a thick layer of whitewash.
He daubed a little sunscreen on his nose before heading outside.
She quickly daubed mud on her shoes to hide the scuff marks.
The children daubed bright colors across the canvas at art class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coat - to cover something with a layer of a substance
  • synonym: paint - to cover a surface with paint
  • synonym: smear - to coat or mark something messily with a substance
  • antonym: wipe - to rub a surface, especially with a cloth, for spreading or cleaning
