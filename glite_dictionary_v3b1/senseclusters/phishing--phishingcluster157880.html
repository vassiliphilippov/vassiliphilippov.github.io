phishing

noun
online information scam
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪ.ʃɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪ.ʃɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of tricking people online into revealing personal or financial information, often for fraudulent purposes

Examples

Phishing scam online
The company warned customers about recent phishing emails pretending to be from their support team.
He fell victim to a phishing attack and had to cancel his credit cards immediately.
Always check the sender's address to avoid phishing scams in your inbox.
