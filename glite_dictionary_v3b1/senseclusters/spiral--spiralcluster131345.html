Synonyms

  • coil
    3.8 0.86/m
    coil winds into tight loops like a spring or rope being wrapped, while spiral describes a single continuous curve around a center.
  • wind
    3.2 14/m
    wind wraps something around an object or into loops by hand or machine, while spiral means to move or form a continuous curved line.
  • twist
