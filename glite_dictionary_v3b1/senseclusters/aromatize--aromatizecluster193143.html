aromatize

verb
fill with a pleasant smell
4.2 5.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈroʊ.mə.taɪz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈrəʊ.mə.taɪz/ (BrE)

Examples

To aromatize a room
Herbs and flowers can aromatize a room naturally.
The baker likes to aromatize his kitchen with spices and citrus peels.
They aromatized the air at the spa using essential oils.
A gentle breeze aromatised the room with the scent of lavender.
The chef likes to aromatise his dishes with fresh herbs.
You can aromatise oils by infusing them with citrus peel or spices.
