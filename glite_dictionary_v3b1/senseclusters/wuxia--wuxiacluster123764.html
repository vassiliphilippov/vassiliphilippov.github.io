wuxia

adjective
Chinese martial arts genre
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːʃɑː / (AmE), / ˈwuːʃɑː / (BrE)
relating to the Chinese genre of fiction or film featuring martial arts, chivalry, and ancient heroes.

Examples

Wuxia film
The director made a visually stunning wuxia film that blended romance and swordplay beautifully.
She enjoys reading wuxia novels set in ancient China, full of heroic warriors and epic battles.
The costumes in the wuxia drama were inspired by traditional Chinese clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantasy - a genre involving magical or supernatural elements
  • synonym: martial arts - traditional fighting arts, especially as practiced in East Asia
