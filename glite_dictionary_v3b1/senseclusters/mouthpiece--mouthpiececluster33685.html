mouthpiece

noun
blown part of a wind instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊθˌpis / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθpiːs / (BrE)
the part of a wind instrument where the player blows air into the instrument

Examples

Trumpet mouthpiece
He replaced the old mouthpiece of his trumpet with a new one for better comfort.
Musicians often choose a mouthpiece that best suits their playing style.
The clarinet’s mouthpiece must be cleaned regularly to maintain good hygiene.
