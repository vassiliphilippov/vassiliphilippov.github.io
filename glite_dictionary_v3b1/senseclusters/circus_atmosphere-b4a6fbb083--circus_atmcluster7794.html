circus atmosphere

noun
lively and chaotic environment
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.kəs ˈætməˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kəs ˈæt.mə.sfɪə / (BrE)
A lively, noisy, and chaotic environment, often with a sense of excitement and disorder.

Examples

A circus atmosphere
Reporters added to the circus atmosphere as they crowded around the celebrity's car.
The fans created a circus atmosphere at the stadium, making it difficult for the players to concentrate.
The press conference dissolved into a circus atmosphere with people shouting and flashing cameras.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm atmosphere - an environment that is peaceful and tranquil
  • synonym: carnival atmosphere - a festive, exuberant, and lively environment
