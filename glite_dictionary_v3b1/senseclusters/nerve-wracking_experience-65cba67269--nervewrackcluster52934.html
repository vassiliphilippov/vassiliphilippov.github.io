nerve-wracking experience

noun
anxious, stressful situation
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrv ˌrækɪŋ ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈnɜːv ˌrækɪŋ ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)

Examples

Waiting for the test results was a nerve-wracking experience for everyone involved.
