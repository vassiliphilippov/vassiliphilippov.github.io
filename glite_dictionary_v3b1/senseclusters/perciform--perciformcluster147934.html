perciform

adjective
like perch fishes
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsɪfɔːm / (BrE)
relating to or resembling fishes of the order Perciformes, the largest order of vertebrates

Examples

Perciform fish species
The biologist studied several perciform species in the river ecosystem.
Many aquarium enthusiasts keep perciform fish because of their variety and colors.
The perciform order includes familiar fish such as bass, perch, and mackerel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: percoid - relating to or resembling perch-like fishes
