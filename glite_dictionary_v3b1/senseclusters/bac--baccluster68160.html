bac

noun
small ferry boat
Pronunciation: / bæk / (AmE), / bæk / (BrE)
a small ferry or flat-bottomed boat used to carry people or vehicles across a river or channel, especially in regional or historical contexts

Examples

River bac crossing
The villagers crossed the river on a wooden bac that could carry two cars at a time.
During the festival, tourists enjoyed a ride on the old bac across the peaceful channel.
Before the bridge was built, everyone relied on the bac to reach the other side.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raft - a flat structure for floating on water, often used for transport
