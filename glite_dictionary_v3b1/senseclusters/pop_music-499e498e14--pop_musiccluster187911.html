pop music

noun
popular, catchy music genre
Pronunciation: / pɑp ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / pɒp ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
a genre of popular music with a catchy melody and wide appeal, often focused on rhythmic elements and romantic themes

Examples

Catchy pop music
Many songs in pop music have simple, catchy choruses that people can easily sing along with.
She listens to pop music every morning to start her day in a good mood.
The concert featured famous artists from the world of pop music.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classical music - serious or traditional Western music with a long-established form
  • degree higher: rock music - a style of popular music with a strong beat, often featuring electric guitar
