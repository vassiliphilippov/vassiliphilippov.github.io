rectus abdominis

noun
abdominal muscle
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛktəs æbˈdɑmɪnɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɛktəs æbˈdɒmɪnɪs / (BrE)
a long, flat muscle running along the front of the abdomen, important for core strength and movement

Examples

The rectus abdominis muscle
Doing sit-ups regularly can help strengthen your rectus abdominis.
The rectus abdominis is often called the 'six-pack' muscle because of its appearance.
Injuries to the rectus abdominis can make it difficult to bend or twist your torso.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abdominal muscle - any muscle located in the abdomen
  • synonym: oblique - a muscle on the side of the abdomen
