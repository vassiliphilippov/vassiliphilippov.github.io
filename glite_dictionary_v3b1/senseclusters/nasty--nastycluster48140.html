nasty

adjective
obscene or indecent in content
Pronunciation: / ˈnæsti / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsti / (BrE)
offensive, indecent, mean, or likely to cause harm or distress; showing malice or hostility

Examples

Nasty comments

The movie was criticized for its nasty dialogue and crude humor.
He received a nasty email criticizing his work.
She can be quite nasty when she's angry.
The kitchen was left in a nasty state after the party.
