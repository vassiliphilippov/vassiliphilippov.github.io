go to pieces

verb
lose emotional or mental control
3.1 4.9
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə ˈpiːsɪz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ˈpiːsɪz / (BrE)
to lose emotional or mental control, often as a result of shock or distress.

Examples

To go to pieces suddenly

He goes to pieces whenever he has to speak in public.
It was clear he was going to pieces after the failed exam results.
