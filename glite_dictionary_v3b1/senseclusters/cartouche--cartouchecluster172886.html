cartouche

noun
decorative panel
9.5
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.ˈtuː.ʃ/ (AmE), /kɑː.ˈtuː.ʃ/ (BrE)
an ornamental tablet or panel, often oval, used to display a decoration, inscription, or coat of arms on buildings or artwork

Examples

Stone cartouche on buildings
The ancient temple's entrance was decorated with a large stone cartouche carved with hieroglyphs.
A golden cartouche above the fireplace displayed the family's crest in intricate detail.
The museum exhibit included a wooden cartouche that once adorned a royal palace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel - a flat, usually rectangular piece forming part of a surface
  • synonym: plaque - a flat, decorative plate or tablet, often inscribed
