dewberry

noun
a blackberry-like fruit from trailing brambles
8.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.ber.iː/ /; / /ˈdjuː.ber.iː/ (AmE), /ˈdjuː.bə.riː/ /; / /ˈdʒuː.bə.riː/ (BrE)
