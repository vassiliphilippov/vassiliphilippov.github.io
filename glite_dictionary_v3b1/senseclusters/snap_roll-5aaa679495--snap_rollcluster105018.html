snap roll

noun
rapid aerial rotation maneuver
Pronunciation: / snæp roʊl / (AmE), / snæp rəʊl / (BrE)
an aerobatic maneuver in which an aircraft makes a very rapid, complete rotation about its longitudinal axis while flying straight.

Examples

A snap roll maneuver
Learning to perform a snap roll safely requires extensive training and experience.
The pilot executed a perfect snap roll during the airshow, impressing the crowd.
The stunt plane's sudden snap roll barely missed the flagpole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrel roll - an aerobatic maneuver where an aircraft follows a helical path while rolling
