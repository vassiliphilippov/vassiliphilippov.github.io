flowering wintergreen

noun
North American plant with rosy-purple flowers
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈwɪntərˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈwɪntəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
A trailing perennial plant of eastern North America, having leaves resembling wintergreen and usually rosy-purple flowers with winged sepals; a milkwort species.

Examples

The flowering wintergreen plant
The rosy-purple blossoms of the flowering wintergreen made the trail especially beautiful in spring.
The woods were dotted with patches of flowering wintergreen blooming among the fallen leaves.
We spotted a rare flowering wintergreen while hiking in the eastern forest.
