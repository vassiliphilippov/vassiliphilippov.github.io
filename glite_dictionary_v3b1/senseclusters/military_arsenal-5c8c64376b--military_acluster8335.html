military arsenal

noun
collection of military weapons and equipment
4.3 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…™l…™Àåt…õri Àà…ërs…ôn…ôl / (AmE), / Ààm…™l…™t…ôri Àà…ëÀês…ôn…ôl / (BrE)
A collection of military weapons and equipment held by a country or organization for defense or warfare.

Examples

A large military arsenal
Intelligence reports indicated that the rebels had acquired a significant military arsenal.
Reducing the size of the military arsenal is a key component of the disarmament agreement.
The country expanded its military arsenal to strengthen its defense strategy.
