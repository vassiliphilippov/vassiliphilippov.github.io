chalk off

verb
mark as completed or remove from a list
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / t É…ëk …îf / (AmE), / t É…îÀêk …íf / (BrE)
to mark something as completed or to remove it from a list, typically by drawing a line or making a note

Examples

To chalk off a task
After finishing the report, she chalked off another task from her list.
At the end of the day, I like to chalk off everything I accomplished.
They chalked off the completed assignments on the classroom board.
