nutty

adjective
crazy or eccentric (informal)
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌti / (AmE), / ˈnʌti / (BrE)

Examples

Nutty behavior

My roommate's ideas can be nutty, but they often make me laugh.
Some people thought the inventor was nutty for trying such a risky project.
