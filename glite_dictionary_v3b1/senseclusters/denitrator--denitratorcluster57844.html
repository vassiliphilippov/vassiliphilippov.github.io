denitrator

noun
device for removing nitrates
9.1
Pronunciation: / diˈnaɪ.tɹeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / diˈnaɪ.tɹeɪ.tə / (BrE)
A device used to remove nitrates or other nitrogen compounds from water or other substances.

Examples

A denitrator device
Many aquarists use a denitrator in their filtration systems to maintain healthy water quality for fish.
Regular maintenance of the denitrator ensures it efficiently removes nitrogen compounds from wastewater.
The water treatment plant installed a new denitrator to reduce nitrate levels in the drinking supply.
