nonassociation

noun
lack of connection
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of not being connected, involved, or associated with someone or something

Examples

Strict nonassociation
The company required strict nonassociation with any rival organizations during the contract period.
His nonassociation with the group was noted in the official report.
A clause of nonassociation was included to prevent conflicts of interest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: association - the state of being connected or involved with someone or something
  • synonym: disconnection - the state of being separated or not joined
