unrecruited

adjective
not enlisted
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈkruːtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkruːtɪd / (BrE)
not having been recruited, enlisted, or brought into a group or organization

Examples

Several unrecruited candidates
Several unrecruited candidates were still waiting for a response from the company.
The coach was searching for talent among the unrecruited players at the local tournament.
Many positions remained unrecruited by the end of the hiring season.

  • synonym: unselected - not chosen or picked for a group or position
