furnace-like

adjective
extremely hot
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrnɪs laɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪs laɪk / (BrE)
extremely hot or resembling the intense heat of a furnace

Examples

A furnace-like heat
The desert wind was furnace-like and made it hard to breathe during the day.
After hours in the sun, the car's interior felt furnace-like when we opened the door.
The kitchen became furnace-like while the bread was baking in the oven.

  • synonym: scorching - very hot; burning
