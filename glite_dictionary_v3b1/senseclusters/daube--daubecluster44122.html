daube

verb
to smear roughly
9.9
Pronunciation: /dɔːb/ (AmE), /dɔːb/ (BrE)
to coat or smear something roughly with a soft or sticky substance, such as paint or plaster

Examples

To daube paint
The children daubed mud on the fence while playing in the yard.
She daubed paint onto the canvas without worrying about neatness.
Workers daube the walls with plaster to cover the cracks before painting.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: slather - to spread something thickly or liberally
  • synonym: smear - to spread a substance messily over a surface
