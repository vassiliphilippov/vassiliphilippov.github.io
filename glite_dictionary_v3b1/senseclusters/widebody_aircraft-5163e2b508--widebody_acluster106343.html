widebody aircraft

noun
commercial two-aisle aircraft
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdˌbɑdi ˈɛrˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdbɒdi ˈeəkrɑːft / (BrE)
a large commercial airplane with two passenger aisles, designed to carry more passengers than standard single-aisle aircraft

Examples

A widebody aircraft cabin
A widebody aircraft can accommodate hundreds of passengers across multiple seating classes.
Major international routes are typically served by modern widebody aircraft equipped with advanced cabin features.
The airline has added several new widebody aircraft to its fleet for long-haul flights.
