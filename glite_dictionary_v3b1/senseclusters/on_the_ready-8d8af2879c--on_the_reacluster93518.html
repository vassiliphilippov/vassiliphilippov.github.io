on the ready

adjective
prepared and alert for immediate use
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
prepared and alert for immediate use or action; ready and waiting to respond quickly

Examples

On the ready team
During hurricane season, relief teams are always on the ready to assist affected communities.
She kept her medical kit on the ready whenever she traveled with her children.
The security staff remained on the ready throughout the concert in case of emergencies.
