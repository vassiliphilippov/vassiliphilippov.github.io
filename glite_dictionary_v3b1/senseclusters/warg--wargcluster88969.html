warg

noun
person with animal-mind control
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɑːrɡ / (AmE), / wɑːɡ / (BrE)
a person in fantasy fiction who can enter the mind of an animal and control or share its senses

Examples

A warg in animal form
The young warg closed his eyes and listened to the fox's heartbeat in the grass.
In the story, a powerful warg could see through the eyes of wolves.
She discovered her abilities as a warg after dreaming she was flying with an eagle.

Related Terms
