whiff

verb
smoke and exhale strongly
Pronunciation: / wɪf / (AmE), / wɪf / (BrE)
to smoke (a cigar, pipe, etc.) and exhale strongly

Examples

To whiff a cigar
He took a deep whiff from his pipe and blew smoke into the air.
She liked to whiff cigars on the porch after dinner.
The old man slowly whiffed his favorite tobacco, savoring every puff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puff - to smoke and exhale a small amount from a cigarette, cigar, or pipe
