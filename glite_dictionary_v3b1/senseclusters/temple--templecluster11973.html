temple

noun
place of worship building
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpəl /; / ˈtɛm.pəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpəl /; / ˈtɛm.pəl / (BrE)
a building devoted to the worship of a god or gods, especially in religions other than Christianity

They visited the ancient temple perched on the hilltop.
