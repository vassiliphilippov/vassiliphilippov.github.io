economic condition

noun
the current state of the economy
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪk kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of an economy at a particular time, especially as measured by indicators such as growth, employment, and inflation

Examples

Current economic condition
Analysts are concerned about the current economic condition and predict slow growth.
Many businesses struggle during periods of poor economic condition.
The country's economic condition improved significantly after the government introduced new policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economic climate - the prevailing state of the economy at a given time
