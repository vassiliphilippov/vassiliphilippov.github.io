fedelini

noun
thin Italian pasta
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfeɪdəˈliːni / (AmE), / ˌfeɪdəˈliːni / (BrE)
a type of very thin Italian pasta, similar to but finer than spaghetti

Examples

Thin fedelini pasta
The chef recommended fedelini with a light tomato sauce for a delicate meal.
She prefers fedelini over spaghetti because it cooks faster and has a finer texture.
At the Italian market, I found fresh fedelini perfect for seafood pasta dishes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: capellini - very thin Italian pasta, also called angel hair
  • synonym: spaghetti - long, thin cylindrical pasta
