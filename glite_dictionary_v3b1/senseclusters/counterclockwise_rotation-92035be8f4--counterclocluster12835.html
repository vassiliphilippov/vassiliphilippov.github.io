counterclockwise rotation

noun
rotation to the left (opposite of clockwise)
3.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /kaʊn.tər.ˈklɔːk.waɪz roʊˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kaʊn.tər.ˈklɑːk.waɪz roʊˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kaʊn.tə.ˈklɒk.waɪz rəʊˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.tə.klɒk.waɪz rəʊˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a rotation or movement that turns to the left, opposite to the direction of a clock's hands

Examples

A counterclockwise rotation
Loosen the screw with a counterclockwise rotation of the screwdriver.
The planet's counterclockwise rotation causes the sun to rise in the east.
Turn the dial in a counterclockwise rotation to decrease the volume.
