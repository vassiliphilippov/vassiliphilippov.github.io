deerstalker

noun
hunting hat with dual brims, worn by Sherlock Holmes
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrˌstɔkər / (AmE), / ˈdɪəˌstɔːkə / (BrE)
a close-fitting hat with a brim at the front and back, traditionally worn by hunters and famously associated with the fictional detective Sherlock Holmes

Examples

A deerstalker hat
He wore his distinctive deerstalker as he strolled through the countryside.
The detective was instantly recognized by his deerstalker and magnifying glass.
Traditionally, hunters in Scotland wore a deerstalker during expeditions.
