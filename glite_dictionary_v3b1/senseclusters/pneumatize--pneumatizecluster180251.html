pneumatize

verb
to add compressed air system
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːməˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnjuːmətaɪz / (BrE)
to equip or convert a machine, system, or building to operate using compressed air or pneumatic components

Examples

To pneumatize a machine
The engineers decided to pneumatize the conveyor belt to improve efficiency.
Many modern factories pneumatize their equipment for faster and safer operation.
After they pneumatized the doors, they could be opened with the push of a button.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automate - to make a process operate automatically
  • synonym: mechanize - to equip with machinery
