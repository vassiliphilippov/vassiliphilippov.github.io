deutzia

noun
ornamental flowering shrub (Deutzia genus)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɪtsiə / (AmE), / ˈdjuːtsiə / (BrE)
A shrub of the genus Deutzia, typically having toothed opposite leaves, shredding bark, and loose clusters of white or pink flowers.
