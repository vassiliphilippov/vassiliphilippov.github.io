myocyte

noun
muscle cell
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.oʊ.saɪt/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.ə.saɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.əʊ.saɪt/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.ə.saɪt/ (BrE)
a muscle cell that forms muscle tissue and is responsible for contraction and movement in the body

Examples

Scientists study myocytes to better understand muscle diseases.
