jump on

verb
get up on the back of
1.0
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp ɑn / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ɒn / (BrE)
to get up onto the back of something, especially quickly or suddenly

Examples

To jump on a horse
He quickly jumped on his horse and rode away.
She laughed as she jumped on the back of her friend's bike.
The children tried to jump on the donkey for a ride.
