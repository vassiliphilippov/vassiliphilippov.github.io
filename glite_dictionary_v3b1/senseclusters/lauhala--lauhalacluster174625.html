lauhala

noun
pandanus leaves for weaving
9.9
Pronunciation: / laʊˈhɑːlə / (AmE), / laʊˈhɑːlə / (BrE)
the leaves of the pandanus tree, especially as used in Hawaiian weaving and crafts

Examples

Lauhala mat
The artisan skillfully wove a mat from dried lauhala collected near the beach.
Traditional Hawaiian hats are often made using strips of lauhala for their strength and flexibility.
She learned to prepare and soften lauhala before starting her weaving project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pandanus - a tropical tree whose leaves are used for weaving
