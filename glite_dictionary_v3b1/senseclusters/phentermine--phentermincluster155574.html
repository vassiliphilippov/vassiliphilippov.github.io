phentermine

noun
weight loss drug
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛntərˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˈfɛntəmiːn / (BrE)
a prescription drug used as an appetite suppressant to help with weight loss

Examples

Phentermine prescription drug
The doctor prescribed phentermine to help her lose weight safely.
Some patients experience side effects when taking phentermine for several weeks.
Before starting phentermine, you should discuss possible risks with your healthcare provider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amphetamine - a stimulant drug sometimes used to treat ADHD and narcolepsy
  • synonym: orlistat - a medication used to treat obesity by preventing fat absorption
