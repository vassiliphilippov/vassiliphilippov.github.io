MPC

noun
asteroid data organization
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɛm pi si / (AmE), / ɛm pi si / (BrE)
the international organization that collects and publishes data about asteroids and other small bodies in the Solar System

Examples

MPC asteroid data
The MPC received hundreds of asteroid reports from observatories around the world last year.
Researchers submitted their comet discovery to the MPC for official recognition.
The MPC database is a key resource for astronomers studying minor planets.
