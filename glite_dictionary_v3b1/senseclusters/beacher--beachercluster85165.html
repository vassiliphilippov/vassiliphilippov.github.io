beacher

noun
beach visitor or device
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who frequents beaches or a device used to bring something onto a beach

Examples

The beacher relaxes
Every summer, the beacher spends hours relaxing by the ocean with friends.
The old beacher was used to pull the fishing boat safely onto the shore.
As a regular beacher, she knows the best spots for swimming and sunbathing.
