teleprompter

noun
device displaying scripts for TV presenters
2.2 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.ə.prɑːmpt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtel.ə.prɒmpt.ə/ (BrE)
a device that displays a prepared script for a television performer or speaker to read while appearing to speak spontaneously

Examples

A teleprompter device
During the presidential debate, the candidates relied on a teleprompter to deliver their speeches.
If the teleprompter fails, presenters must be able to continue without it.
The news anchor read her report smoothly with the help of the teleprompter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prompter - a person or device providing cues or scripts for performers
