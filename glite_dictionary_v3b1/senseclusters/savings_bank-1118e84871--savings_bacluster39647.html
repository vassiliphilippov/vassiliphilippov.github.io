savings bank

noun
container for saving coins at home
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋz bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋz baŋk / (BrE)
A small container with a slot in the top, used to keep savings, especially coins, at home.

Examples

A coin savings bank
He deposited his loose change into his savings bank every night.
My children each have a savings bank shaped like a rocket.
She shook her savings bank to see how many coins were inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: money box - a box for saving coins, typically with a slot in the top
  • synonym: piggy bank - a small money box, often shaped like a pig
