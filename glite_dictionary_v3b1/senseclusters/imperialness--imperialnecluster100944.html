imperialness

noun
quality of being imperial
9.5
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəl.nəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪə.ri.əl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being imperial; of or relating to an empire, emperor, or empress.

Examples

The imperialness of empire
Debates raged over whether the architecture projected more republican simplicity or regal imperialness.
Many admired the imperialness she brought to her role as empress.
The imperialness of the ancient court was reflected in its grand ceremonies and strict hierarchy.
