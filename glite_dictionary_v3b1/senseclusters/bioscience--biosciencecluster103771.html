bioscience

noun
biological science; study of living organisms
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.oʊˌsaɪ.əns / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪ.əʊˌsʌɪ.əns / (BrE)
any of the branches of science that deal with the structure and behavior of living organisms, such as biology, biochemistry, or ecology.

Examples

Modern bioscience research
Recent advances in bioscience have led to breakthroughs in medicine and agriculture.
She decided to pursue a degree in bioscience at the university.
The conference brought together experts from many fields of bioscience to discuss climate change impacts.
