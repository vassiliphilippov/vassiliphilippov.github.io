engaging

adjective
charming; attractive and holding interest
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
charming or attractive in a way that holds your attention or interest.

Examples

Engaging personality
His engaging personality makes him very popular at parties.
She gave an engaging smile that put everyone at ease.
The documentary was so engaging that I watched it twice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charming - pleasantly attractive or delightful
  • antonym: unappealing - not attractive or interesting
