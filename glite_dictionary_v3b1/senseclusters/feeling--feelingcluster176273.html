feeling

noun
general atmosphere or mood
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the general atmosphere or mood of a place or situation and its effect on people

Examples

A warm feeling in the room
She sensed a tense feeling in the office after the announcement.
The restaurant's dim lighting created an intimate feeling that guests loved.
There was a festive feeling in the air during the holiday celebration.

  • synonym: ambience - the character and mood of a place
  • synonym: atmosphere - the pervading tone or mood of a place or situation
  • synonym: mood - the general feeling or atmosphere of a place or event
