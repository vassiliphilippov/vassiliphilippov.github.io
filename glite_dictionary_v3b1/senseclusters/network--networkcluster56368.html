network

noun
system of intersecting lines or channels
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːk / (BrE)
A system of lines, routes, or channels that cross or interconnect with each other.

Examples

A network of roads
A vast network of roads connects even the most remote villages.
The city's subway network makes it easy to travel from one district to another.
The irrigation network efficiently distributes water throughout the entire region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: web - a complicated system of connections or relationships, often resembling a network
