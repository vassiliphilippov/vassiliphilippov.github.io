untracked

adjective
not monitored or marked
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrækt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrækt / (BrE)
not monitored, followed, or recorded in any way; not marked by tracks or traces.

Examples

Untracked area
The hikers preferred to explore the untracked wilderness.
Her progress in the project remained untracked due to a lack of reporting.
Fresh snow covered the field, leaving it completely untracked by people or animals.
