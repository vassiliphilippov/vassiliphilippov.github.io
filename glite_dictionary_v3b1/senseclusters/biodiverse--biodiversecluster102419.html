biodiverse

adjective
rich in species
5.4
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.ˈdaɪ.vɜːrs/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.ˈdaɪ.vɜːs/ (BrE)

Examples

A biodiverse ecosystem is more resilient to environmental changes.
