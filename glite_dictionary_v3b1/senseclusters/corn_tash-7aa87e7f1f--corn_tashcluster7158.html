corn tash

noun
cornbread wrapped in cabbage leaves and baked in ashes
Pronunciation: / kɔrn tæʃ / (AmE), / kɔːn tæʃ / (BrE)
A type of cornbread dish wrapped in cabbage leaves and baked in hot ashes, traditionally prepared in the southern United States.

Examples

A corn tash dish
