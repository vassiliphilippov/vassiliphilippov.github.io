free-and-easy

adjective
relaxed and informal
Pronunciation: / ˌfri ən ˈizi / (AmE), / ˌfriː ən ˈiːzi / (BrE)

Examples

A free-and-easy style

The manager has a free-and-easy approach to supervising the team, making everyone feel comfortable.
