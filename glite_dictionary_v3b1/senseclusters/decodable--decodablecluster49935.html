decodable

adjective
able to be decoded
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkoʊdəbl / (AmE), / dɪˈkəʊdəbl / (BrE)
able to be converted from a coded, encrypted, or compressed form into an understandable or original form

Examples

Decodable message
The secret message was only decodable with the correct password.
Some audio files are not decodable by older media players.
The teacher provided decodable texts to help students practice reading skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indecipherable - impossible to decode or understand
  • synonym: readable - able to be read or understood
