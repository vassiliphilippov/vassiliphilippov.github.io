hectically

adverb
in a hurried or frenzied way
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛktɪkli / (AmE), / ˈhɛktɪkəli / (BrE)
In a hurried, busy, or frenzied manner.

Examples

To hectically rush
She rushed hectically around the kitchen preparing dinner for her guests.
Students worked hectically to meet the deadline for their final projects.
The week before the wedding passed hectically, with last-minute arrangements and endless phone calls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or unhurried manner
  • synonym: frenziedly - in a wild or frantic manner
