oho

interjection
triumph exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈhoʊ / (AmE), / əʊˈhəʊ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express triumph, satisfaction, or scorn when someone is proved right or catches another out

Examples

Oho, got you
When he found the missing piece, he exclaimed, 'Oho, so this is where it was hiding!'
She laughed and said 'Oho, I knew you would forget your keys again.'
The detective smiled, 'Oho, now the mystery makes sense at last.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: aha - an exclamation used when something is understood or discovered
  • synonym: gotcha - an exclamation used when catching someone in a mistake or trick
