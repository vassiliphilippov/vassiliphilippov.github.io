pointed

adjective
having a sharp or tapered end
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
having a sharp or tapered end or tip; coming to a point

Examples

Pointed shoes
She wore shoes with extremely pointed toes to the party.
The leaves of this plant are narrow and pointed.
The pencil has a pointed tip so you can write precisely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt - not having a sharp edge or point; dull
