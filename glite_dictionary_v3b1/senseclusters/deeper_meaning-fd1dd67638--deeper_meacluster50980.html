deeper meaning

noun
hidden or profound significance
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːpər ˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdiːpə ˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a hidden or more profound significance or interpretation beyond the surface level, especially in literature, art, or thought

Examples

A deeper meaning
Many readers believe that the novel contains a deeper meaning about the nature of society.
Sometimes, a simple story can hold a deeper meaning about life or morality.
The artist encouraged us to look for the deeper meaning behind his abstract paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hidden meaning - meaning that is implied or not immediately obvious
  • antonym: surface meaning - the obvious or literal meaning of something
