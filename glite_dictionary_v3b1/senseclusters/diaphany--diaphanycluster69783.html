diaphany

noun
thin translucent material
9.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈæf.ə.niː/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈæf.ə.niː/ (BrE)
a thin, translucent material or object that allows light to pass through, such as gauze or fine fabric

A silk diaphany

He admired the diaphany of the curtain as it gently filtered the morning light.
