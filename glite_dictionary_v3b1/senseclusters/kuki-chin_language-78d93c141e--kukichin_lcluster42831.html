Kuki-Chin language

noun
language group of western Myanmar and surrounding regions
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːki ˈtʃɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkuːki ˈtʃɪn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
Any of a group of Kamarupan languages spoken mainly in western Myanmar (Burma), Bangladesh, and easternmost India.

Examples

A Kuki-Chin language speaker
Many people in the region speak a Kuki-Chin language as their first language.
Several Kuki-Chin language communities can be found across the India-Myanmar border.
The study focused on the syntax of a particular Kuki-Chin language spoken in Bangladesh.
