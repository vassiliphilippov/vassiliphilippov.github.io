smoke grenade

noun
explosive device releasing smoke
1.0
Pronunciation: / smoʊk ɡrəˈneɪd / (AmE), / sməʊk ɡrəˈneɪd / (BrE)
A small explosive device designed to release thick smoke, used to create a smoke screen or mark a position.

Examples

A smoke grenade explosion
During the training exercise, a smoke grenade was deployed to simulate combat conditions.
He threw a smoke grenade to mark the landing zone for the rescue helicopter.
The soldiers used a smoke grenade to conceal their movement across the open field.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: flare - a device producing bright light, often for signaling
  • synonym: tear gas grenade - a grenade containing irritant gas
