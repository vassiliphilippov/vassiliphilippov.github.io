worldly possession

noun
material object or property owned by someone
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːldli pəˈzɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli pəˈzɛʃən / (BrE)
a material object or property owned by a person, as opposed to spiritual or non-material things

Examples

His worldly possessions
Despite losing most of his worldly possessions in the fire, he remained optimistic.
People sometimes realize too late that worldly possessions cannot bring lasting happiness.
She sold all her worldly possessions and traveled the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belonging - something owned; property
  • antonym: spiritual possession - non-material things such as beliefs, virtues, or spiritual attributes
