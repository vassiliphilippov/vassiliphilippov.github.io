scorepad

noun
pad for recording scores
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔːrˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpæd / (BrE)
a small pad of paper used to record scores during games or competitions

Examples

Game scorepad
After each round, he carefully wrote the points on the scorepad to keep track of the game.
We realized we had forgotten the scorepad just as the board game was about to start.
The scorepad was filled with numbers from their long night of playing cards.
