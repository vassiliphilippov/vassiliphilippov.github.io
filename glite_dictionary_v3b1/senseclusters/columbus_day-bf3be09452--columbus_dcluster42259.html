Columbus Day

proper noun
American public holiday honoring Columbus
Pronunciation: / kəˈlʌmbəs deɪ / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbəs deɪ / (BrE)
A public holiday in the United States commemorating the arrival of Christopher Columbus in the Americas, observed on the second Monday in October.
