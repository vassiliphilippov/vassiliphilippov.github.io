hopsack

noun
coarse, loosely woven fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑpˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈhɒpˌsæk / (BrE)
A loosely woven, coarse fabric made of cotton or linen, used especially in clothing and upholstery.

Examples

A hopsack fabric
He admired the texture of the hopsack suit on display in the shop window.
Lightweight hopsack can be a great choice for summer clothing.
The jacket was tailored from a durable hopsack that resisted wrinkles during travel.
