coarsely

adverb
in a rough or large-piece manner
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrsli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by large, rough, or thick pieces; not finely or delicately.

Examples

To coarsely grind
Sandpaper with a coarsely grained surface is best for removing old paint.
The bread was cut coarsely, giving it a rustic appearance.
The vegetables should be chopped coarsely for this recipe.
