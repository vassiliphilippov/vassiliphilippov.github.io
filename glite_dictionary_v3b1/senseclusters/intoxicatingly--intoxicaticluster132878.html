intoxicatingly

adverb
in an exciting way
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɑksɪˌkeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɒksɪˌkeɪtɪŋli /; / ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely exciting, appealing, or produces a feeling of euphoria, like being intoxicated
