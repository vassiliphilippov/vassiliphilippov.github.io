stereospecifically

adverb
in a spatially specific way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛrioʊspəˈsɪfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəʊspəˈsɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that affects or involves a specific three-dimensional arrangement of atoms in a molecule

Examples

React stereospecifically in chemistry
The catalyst reacts stereospecifically, ensuring only one enantiomer is produced in the process.
Some enzymes bind stereospecifically to their substrates, leading to highly selective reactions.
The drug was designed to interact stereospecifically with the target protein for greater effectiveness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stereoselectively - in a way that favors the formation of one stereoisomer over another
