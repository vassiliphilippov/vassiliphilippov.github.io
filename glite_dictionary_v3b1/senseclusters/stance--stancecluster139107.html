stance

noun
body posture or way of standing
4.3
Pronunciation: / stæns / (AmE), / stɑːns / (BrE)
The way in which someone stands, especially when deliberately adopted for a particular purpose.

Examples

A defensive stance
Her defensive stance helped her block the opponent's attack.
The coach corrected his stance to improve his balance.
You should adopt a wider stance when lifting heavy weights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: posture - the position in which someone holds their body
