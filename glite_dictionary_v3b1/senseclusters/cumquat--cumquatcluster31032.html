cumquat

noun
small citrus fruit; the tree or shrub bearing it
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmˌkwɑt / (AmE), / ˈkʌmˌkwɒt / (BrE)
a small citrus fruit with a thick sweet-flavored rind and sour pulp, or the shrub or tree that bears this fruit; also called kumquat

Examples

A cumquat fruit
A small cumquat tree grew beside the patio, heavy with bright orange fruit.
She made a batch of marmalade using fresh cumquats from the market.
The cumquat is prized for its tangy flavor and decorative value in gardens.
