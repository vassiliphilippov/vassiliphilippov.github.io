committable

adjective
able to be finalized
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪtəbəl / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be made permanent or final, especially in the context of computer systems or transactions.

Examples

Committable changes
The changes will not be committable until all errors are resolved.
After reviewing the code, the team agreed it was committable and ready for deployment.
A transaction becomes committable only when all required conditions are met.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finalizable - able to be made final or completed
  • antonym: reversible - able to be undone or changed back
