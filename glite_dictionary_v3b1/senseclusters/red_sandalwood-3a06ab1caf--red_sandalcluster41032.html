red sandalwood

noun
Indian tree with hard, red timber used for dye and furniture
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈsændlˌwʊd /; / rɛd ˈsændəlwʊd / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsændəlwʊd /; / rɛd ˈsændl̩wʊd / (BrE)
a tree of India and Southeast Asia (Pterocarpus santalinus) valued for its red wood and sometimes grown as an ornamental plant
