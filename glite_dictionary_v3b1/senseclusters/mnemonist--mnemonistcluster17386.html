mnemonist

noun
person with exceptional memory skills
Pronunciation: / nɪˈmoʊnɪst / (AmE), / nɪˈmiːnənɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in the use of mnemonic techniques or possesses extraordinary memory abilities

Examples

A mnemonist with skills
As a skilled mnemonist, she could recall every card in a shuffled deck.
Researchers studied the techniques used by a famous mnemonist to improve memory training programs.
The world champion mnemonist memorized hundreds of random numbers in under an hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mnemonic - a device or technique used to aid memory
