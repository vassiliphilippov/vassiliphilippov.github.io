negative light

noun
unfavorable or critical perspective
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪv laɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv laɪt / (BrE)
an unfavorable or critical way of looking at or presenting something or someone

Examples

Negative light on someone
Critics tend to view experimental films in a negative light until they gain popularity.
She was upset because her actions were shown in a negative light by the press.
The report presented the company's financial results in a negative light.
