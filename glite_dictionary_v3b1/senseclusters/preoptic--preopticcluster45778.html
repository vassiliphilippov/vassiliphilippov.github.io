preoptic

adjective
in front of optic region
9.8
Pronunciation: / priˈɑptɪk / (AmE), / priˈɒptɪk / (BrE)
located in front of or relating to the optic region, especially in the brain

Examples

Preoptic area of the brain
The preoptic area of the hypothalamus plays a key role in regulating body temperature.
Researchers studied the preoptic region to understand its influence on sleep patterns.
Damage to the preoptic nucleus can affect hormone release in the brain.
