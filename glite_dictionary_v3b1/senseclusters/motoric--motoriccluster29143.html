motoric

adjective
relating to movement
7.9
Pronunciation: / moʊˈtɔrɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈtɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to movement or muscular activity, especially as controlled by the nervous system

Examples

Motoric skills
The therapist designed exercises to improve the child's motoric coordination.
Researchers studied the motoric responses of patients after the treatment.
Early childhood education often focuses on developing motoric skills through play.
