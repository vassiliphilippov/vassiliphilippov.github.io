transactional

adjective
relating to exchanges
Pronunciation: / trænˈzækʃənəl / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to business, legal, or practical exchanges or transactions, often with a focus on business or practical matters

Examples

Transactional business deal
Their partnership was strictly transactional, with no personal connection.
The company uses a transactional approach to customer service, focusing on quick exchanges.
Some friendships are more transactional, based on mutual benefit rather than emotional closeness.
The lawyer provided transactional advice to help the company complete the merger smoothly.
Her job involves handling the transactional paperwork for real estate deals.
Banks must follow strict transactional procedures to ensure all financial records are accurate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contractual - relating to a contract or agreement
  • antonym: personal - relating to private or individual matters
