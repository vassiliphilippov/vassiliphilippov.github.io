nondoctrinaire

adjective
open-minded, flexible
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌdɑktrəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌdɒktrɪˈneə / (BrE)
not rigidly attached to a set of beliefs or doctrines; open-minded and flexible in thinking

Examples

Her nondoctrinaire attitude made her popular among colleagues from different backgrounds.
A nondoctrinaire leader can adapt to changing circumstances and ideas.
