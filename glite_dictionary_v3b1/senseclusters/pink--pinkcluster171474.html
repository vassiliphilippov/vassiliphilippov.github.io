pink

noun
a fragrant garden flower (Dianthus genus)
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk / (AmE), / pɪŋk / (BrE)
A garden plant of the genus Dianthus, grown for its fragrant, often pink or white flowers with fringed petals.

Examples

A pink flower
She picked a bouquet of pinks from the flowerbed for the kitchen table.
The delicate petals of the pink fluttered in the morning breeze.
We planted pinks along the border to add color and fragrance to the garden.
