jetliner

noun
large passenger jet airplane
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛtˌlaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛtˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
A large jet-powered airplane designed to carry passengers.

Examples

A large jetliner
She watched as the massive jetliner took off into the evening sky.
The new jetliner offers more comfortable seating and advanced safety features.
This jetliner can travel nonstop between New York and Tokyo.
