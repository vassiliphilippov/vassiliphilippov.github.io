Dominican

noun
native or inhabitant of the Dominican Republic
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɪnɪkən / (AmE), / dəˈmɪnɪkən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of the Dominican Republic

Examples

A proud Dominican
As a Dominican, she is proud of her country's rich culture and history.
Many Dominicans celebrate their independence each year with colorful festivals.
The Dominican greeted us with a warm smile as we arrived in Santo Domingo.
