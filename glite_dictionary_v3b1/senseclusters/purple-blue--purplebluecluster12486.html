purple-blue

adjective
bluish-purple color
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl bluː / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl bluː / (BrE)
Of a blue color tinged with purple; between blue and purple in hue.

Examples

A purple-blue dress
She wore a stunning purple-blue dress to the gala.
The artist chose a purple-blue shade for the night sky in the painting.
The petals of the wildflower were a delicate purple-blue color.
