pattle

verb
to stir by striking
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpæ.təl/ /; / /ˈpæd.əl/ (AmE), /ˈpæ.təl/ (BrE)
to beat, stir, or mix something by repeatedly striking it lightly with a flat tool or implement

Examples

To pattle the dough
The baker would pattle the dough to ensure it was evenly mixed.
She used a wooden tool to pattle the paint before applying it to the wall.
To make the batter fluffy, you should pattle it for several minutes.
