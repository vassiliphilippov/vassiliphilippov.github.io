rigor

noun
strictness; harshness; severity
5.4 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.gər/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.gə/ (BrE)
the quality of being extremely strict, severe, or harsh, especially in the application of rules or discipline

Examples

Parents sometimes criticized the school's rigour in enforcing its policies.
The army training program is known for the rigour of its discipline and routines.
