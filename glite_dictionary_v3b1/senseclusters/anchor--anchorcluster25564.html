anchor

noun
main presenter of a news broadcast
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkɚ / (AmE), / ˈæŋkə / (BrE)
a person, especially a television reporter, who presents and coordinates a broadcast involving contributions from several correspondents

Examples

TV anchor
After the retirement of the long-time anchor, the station saw a drop in ratings.
She worked for ten years as the anchor of the evening news program.
The anchor introduced live updates from reporters across the country.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: correspondent - a journalist who reports from a particular place or on a particular subject
  • synonym: newsreader - a person who reads the news on TV or radio
