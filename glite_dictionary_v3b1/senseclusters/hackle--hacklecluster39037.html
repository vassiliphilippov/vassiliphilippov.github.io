hackle

verb
comb fibers such as flax or hemp
Pronunciation: / ˈhæk.əl /; / ˈhækəl / (AmE), / ˈhæk.əl /; / ˈhækəl / (BrE)
to comb or separate plant fibers such as flax, hemp, or wool with a hackle to straighten and clean them
