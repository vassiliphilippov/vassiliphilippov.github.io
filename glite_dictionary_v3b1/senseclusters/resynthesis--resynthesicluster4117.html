resynthesis

noun
synthesizing again
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɪnθəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɪnθəsɪs / (BrE)
the process or result of synthesizing something again, especially in scientific or technical contexts, after it has been broken down or separated
