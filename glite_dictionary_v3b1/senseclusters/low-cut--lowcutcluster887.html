low-cut

adjective
having a low neckline (of clothes)
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ ˌkʌt /; / ˌloʊˈkʌt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌkʌt /; / ˌləʊˈkʌt / (BrE)
(of clothing) having a neckline, back, or edge cut low to show more skin

Examples

A low-cut blouse
He noticed her low-cut blouse as soon as she entered the room.
She wore a low-cut dress to the gala, turning many heads.
That top is too low-cut for school, according to the dress code.
Many summer dresses feature a low-cut neckline to keep cool in hot weather.
She wore a low-cut evening gown to the awards ceremony.
The swimsuit had a low-cut back, making it both stylish and daring.
