unenriched

adjective
not processed for purity
8.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪn.ˈrɪtʃt/ /; / /ən.ɪn.ˈrɪtʃt/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪn.ˈrɪtʃt/ /; / /ən.ɪn.ˈrɪtʃt/ (BrE)
