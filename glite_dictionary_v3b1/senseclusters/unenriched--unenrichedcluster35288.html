unenriched

adjective
not processed for purity
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈrɪtʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈrɪtʃt / (BrE)
not processed to increase the proportion of a particular component, especially an isotope such as U-235 in uranium.

Examples

Unenriched uranium
The power plant uses unenriched uranium as fuel for its reactors.
Because it is unenriched, the material poses less of a proliferation risk.
Scientists compared the properties of unenriched and enriched samples in their experiment.
