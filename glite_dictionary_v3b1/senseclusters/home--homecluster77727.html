home

noun
place where something originated or flourished
Pronunciation: / hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
The place where something originated, developed, or is most at its best or most typical.

Examples

Home of jazz
Chicago is often referred to as the home of the skyscraper.
Silicon Valley is considered the home of the tech industry.
The region is the home of several unique plant species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cradle - a place where something begins or is nurtured
  • synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins
