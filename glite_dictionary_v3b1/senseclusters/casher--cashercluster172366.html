casher

noun
person handling cash
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʃər / (AmE), / ˈkæʃə / (BrE)
a person who cashes checks or handles cash transactions, especially at a bank or store

Examples

The casher counts money
The casher counted the bills carefully before handing them to the customer.
At the end of her shift, the casher balanced the register and prepared the deposit.
He worked as a casher at the local check-cashing store for several years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cashier - a person responsible for receiving and paying out money in a store or bank
