superpatriot

noun
extreme patriot
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌpeɪtriət / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌpætriət / (BrE)
a person who displays extreme or excessive devotion to their country, often beyond what is considered reasonable

Examples

A superpatriot leader
The superpatriot criticized anyone who questioned the government's decisions.
Some people saw him as a superpatriot, while others thought he was too extreme.
Waving flags and singing anthems, the superpatriot led the parade with great enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their own nation
  • degree lower: patriot - a person who loves and supports their country
  • antonym: traitor - a person who betrays their country
