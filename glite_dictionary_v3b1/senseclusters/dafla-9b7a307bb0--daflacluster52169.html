Dafla

noun
a group of Kamarupan languages of India
Pronunciation: / ˈdæf.lə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːf.lə / (BrE)
a group of little-known Kamarupan languages spoken by the Nyishi people in northeastern India, especially in Arunachal Pradesh

Examples

Dafla language
Efforts to preserve Dafla have increased as speakers become more aware of language endangerment.
Linguists have documented the Dafla languages in various districts of Arunachal Pradesh.
The Dafla languages are classified under the Kamarupan group of the Tibeto-Burman family.
