rec

adjective
informal for recreational
Pronunciation: / rɛk / (AmE), / rɛk / (BrE)
informal abbreviation for 'recreational', relating to activities done for enjoyment rather than work or study

Examples

Rec soccer league
He joined a rec soccer league to meet new people in the city.
The community center offers several rec classes for adults in the evenings.
Many students participate in rec sports to stay active outside of school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: professional - relating to a job or paid occupation
  • synonym: recreational - relating to activities done for enjoyment
