cornmeal mush

noun
thick porridge from boiled cornmeal
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌmil mʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːn.miːl mʌʃ / (BrE)
a dish made by boiling cornmeal in water until it forms a thick, porridge-like consistency

Examples

Cornmeal mush breakfast
My grandmother used to serve cornmeal mush with honey for breakfast every Sunday.
On cold mornings, a hot bowl of cornmeal mush is comforting and filling.
Some people fry leftover cornmeal mush until it’s crispy on the outside.
