streamer

noun
media streaming device
3.5
Pronunciation: / c8strid0m5a / (AmE), / c8strid0m59 / (BrE)
A hardware device or software application that receives digital media from the internet and plays it on a television or other display.

Examples

A TV streamer
The new streamer lets the TV play movies directly from online services.
We connected the streamer to HDMI and logged into the subscription service quickly.
A cheap streamer can turn any ordinary television into a smart, internet-ready display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: media player - software or hardware used to play digital audio or video files
  • synonym: streaming device - a device that connects to the internet and plays streamed audio/video on a display
