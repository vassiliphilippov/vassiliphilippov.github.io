nigrescence

noun
becoming black or dark
Pronunciation: / naɪˈɡrɛsəns / (AmE), / nɪˈɡrɛsəns / (BrE)
the process or state of becoming black or dark in color

Examples

Nigrescence of the sky
The nigrescence of the sky warned sailors of the approaching storm.
Scientists studied the nigrescence of volcanic rocks after the eruption.
The artist captured the gradual nigrescence of twilight in her painting.
