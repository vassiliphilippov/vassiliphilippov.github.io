Capsicum annuum cerasiforme

noun
a type of cherry pepper plant
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkæp.sɪ.kəm əˈnuː.əm ˌsɛrəˈsɪfɔr.mi / (AmE), / ˌkap.sɪ.kəm əˈnjuː.əm ˌsɛərəˈsɪfɔː.mi / (BrE)
A variety of pepper plant bearing small, rounded, usually pungent fruits, commonly known as cherry peppers.
