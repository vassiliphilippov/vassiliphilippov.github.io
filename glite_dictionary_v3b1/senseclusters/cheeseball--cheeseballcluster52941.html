cheeseball

noun
silly or corny person
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzbɔl / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzbɔːl / (BrE)
a person or thing regarded as silly, corny, or lacking in style or sophistication

Examples

A cheeseball outfit
Everyone laughed when he wore that costume, calling him a total cheeseball.
The movie was such a cheeseball that we couldn't stop giggling at the clichés.
She thought his pickup lines were pure cheeseball and rolled her eyes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cool - fashionable and impressive
  • synonym: dork - a socially awkward or silly person
