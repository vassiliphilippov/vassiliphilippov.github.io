meagerly

adverb
in a scant or insufficient manner
7.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmiː.gər.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmiː.ɡə.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that is insufficient, scanty, or lacking in amount or quality

Examples

The school was meagerly funded, limiting the resources available to students.
