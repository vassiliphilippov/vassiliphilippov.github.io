kampung

adjective
village-like style
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmpuŋ / (AmE), / ˈkæmpʊŋ / (BrE)
informal, village-like or unsophisticated in style, behavior, or attitude, often used regionally

Examples

Kampung style decor
His decorating style is intentionally kampung, with handmade crafts and simple wooden furniture.
Some people see her kampung attitude as charming, while others think it is old-fashioned.
The restaurant's kampung atmosphere reminded me of my grandmother's village home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustic - simple and typical of the countryside
  • antonym: sophisticated - having refined tastes or manners
