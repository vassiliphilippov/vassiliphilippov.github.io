upswelling

noun
rising increase
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌswɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌswɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden or gradual increase in feeling, activity, or numbers, often rising in intensity or support

Examples

Upswelling of support
There was an upswelling of public support after the announcement.
An upswelling of emotion swept over her as she listened to the speech.
The city saw an upswelling in volunteer activity following the disaster.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - a gradual and continuous loss or decrease
  • synonym: surge - a sudden powerful forward or upward movement
