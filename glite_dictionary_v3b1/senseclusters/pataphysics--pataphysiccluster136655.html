pataphysics

noun
the study of what lies beyond metaphysics; science of imaginary solutions
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /pæ.tə.ˈfɪ.zɪks/ /; / /pə.tə.ˈfɪ.zɪks/ (AmE), /pæ.tə.ˈfɪ.zɪks/ /; / /pə.tə.ˈfɪ.zɪks/ (BrE)
