whoof

interjection
exclamation of surprise
Pronunciation: / wuːf / (AmE), / wuːf / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, relief, being impressed, or feeling overwhelmed

Examples

Whoof of surprise
Whoof, that was a close call on the highway this morning.
After running up the stairs, he stopped and said, 'Whoof, I need to get in better shape.'
When she saw the final exam questions, she whispered, 'Whoof, this is going to be tough.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: phew - an exclamation of relief
  • synonym: wow - an exclamation of surprise or admiration
