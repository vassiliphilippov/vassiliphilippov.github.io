nondestructively

adverb
without causing damage
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈstrʌktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈstrʌktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that does not cause damage or destruction to the object being examined or tested

Examples

Test nondestructively
The scientist examined the ancient artifact nondestructively to avoid any harm to its surface.
Engineers often test new materials nondestructively to ensure they remain intact for further analysis.
The painting was analyzed nondestructively using special imaging techniques to preserve its original state.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destructively - in a way that causes damage or destruction
  • synonym: safely - in a way that avoids harm or risk
