howbeit

adverb
however; nevertheless
Pronunciation: / haʊˈbiːɪt / (AmE), / haʊˈbiːɪt / (BrE)
however; nevertheless (used in old or literary English).

Examples

Howbeit he stayed
He was exhausted; howbeit, he pressed on until the work was done.
The journey was long and difficult, howbeit they reached their destination safely.
She disagreed with the plan, howbeit she agreed to support the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: however - used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or seems to contradict something that has been said previously
  • synonym: nevertheless - in spite of that; notwithstanding; all the same
