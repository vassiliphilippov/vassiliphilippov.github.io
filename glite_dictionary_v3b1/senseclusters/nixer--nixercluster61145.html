nixer

noun
casual extra job
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪksə / (BrE)
a casual or unofficial job done for extra money, often not declared to authorities

Many students take on a nixer during the summer holidays to save money.
She found a nixer helping out at a local café in the evenings.
