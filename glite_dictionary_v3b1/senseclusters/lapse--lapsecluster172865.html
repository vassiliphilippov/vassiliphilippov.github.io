lapse

noun
temporary pause or interruption
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæps / (AmE), / ˈlæps / (BrE)
a temporary pause, failure, or mistake, especially due to a loss of attention or concentration

Examples

A lapse in memory
After a brief lapse in communication, the negotiations resumed.
The lapse between updates frustrated many users.
There was a lapse of several hours before the police responded.
A momentary lapse of concentration led to a costly mistake.
After years of careful work, his sudden lapse surprised his colleagues.
She apologized for her lapse in judgment during the meeting.
He apologized for his lapse in concentration that led to the mistake.
One simple lapse can undermine a reputation built over years.
The jury overlooked her brief lapse in judgment during the trial.

  • synonym: slip - a small or accidental mistake
