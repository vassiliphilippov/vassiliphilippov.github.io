Shintoist

noun
believer or practitioner of Shintoism
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪn.toʊ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɪn.təʊ.ɪst / (BrE)
A person who believes in or practices Shintoism, the traditional religion of Japan.

Examples

A Shintoist believer
As a Shintoist, he observes various rituals to honor the kami.
Many Shintoists visit shrines during traditional Japanese festivals.
The community has several Shintoists who maintain the local shrine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Buddhist - a believer in Buddhism
  • synonym: Christian - a believer in Christianity
