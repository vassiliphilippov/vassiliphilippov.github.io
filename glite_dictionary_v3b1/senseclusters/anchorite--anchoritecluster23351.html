anchorite

noun
religious recluse; hermit
9.3 9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈæŋ.kə.raɪt/ (AmE), /ˈæŋ.kə.raɪt/ (BrE)
a person who withdraws from society, especially for religious reasons, and lives in seclusion; a religious recluse

The medieval church often honored the anchorite for his life of prayer and seclusion.
