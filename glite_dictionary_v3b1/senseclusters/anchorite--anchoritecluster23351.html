anchorite

noun
religious recluse; hermit
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈaŋkəraɪt / (BrE)
a person who withdraws from society, especially for religious reasons, and lives in seclusion; a religious recluse

Examples

An anchorite in seclusion
After years as an anchorite, she wrote a memoir describing her spiritual journey.
In literature, the image of the anchorite symbolizes devotion and withdrawal from worldly distractions.
The medieval church often honored the anchorite for his life of prayer and seclusion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hermit - a person living in solitude as a religious discipline
  • synonym: recluse - a person who lives a solitary life and tends to avoid other people
