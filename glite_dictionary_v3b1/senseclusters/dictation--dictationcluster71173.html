dictation

noun
authoritative order or instruction
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an authoritative order or instruction given by someone in authority

Examples

Follow a dictation
He acted under the dictation of his superior.
The project was halted by the dictation of government officials.
The rules are subject to the dictation of the company's board of directors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: command - an authoritative order
  • synonym: order - an instruction to do something, especially given by someone in authority
