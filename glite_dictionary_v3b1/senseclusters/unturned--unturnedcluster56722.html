unturned

adjective
not moved or flipped
4.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɜrnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɜːnd / (BrE)
not moved, flipped over, or changed from its original position

Examples

A stone unturned
The stone was left unturned in the garden for many years.
Several pages of the report remained unturned after the meeting ended.
Despite the search, some clues were still unturned in the investigation.
