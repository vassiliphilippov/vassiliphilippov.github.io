plant kingdom

noun
major biological category consisting of plants
1.0
Pronunciation: / plænt ˈkɪŋdəm / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈkɪŋdəm / (BrE)
the group of living organisms consisting of all plants, regarded as a major category in biological taxonomy; also known as the kingdom Plantae.

Examples

The plant kingdom
Recent discoveries have changed our understanding of the diversity within the plant kingdom.
The plant kingdom includes mosses, ferns, conifers, and flowering plants.
Unlike animals, members of the plant kingdom produce their own food through photosynthesis.
