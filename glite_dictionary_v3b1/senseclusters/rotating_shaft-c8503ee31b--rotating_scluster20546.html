rotating shaft

noun
a rod that transmits power by rotating
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌteɪtɪŋ ʃæft / (AmE), / ˈrəʊˌteɪtɪŋ ʃɑːft / (BrE)
a rod or bar that turns around its axis to transmit power or motion in a machine

Examples

A rotating shaft bar
