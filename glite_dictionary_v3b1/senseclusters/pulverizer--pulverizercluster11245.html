pulverizer

noun
overwhelming destroyer
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlvəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpʌlvəraɪzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that completely defeats, destroys, or overwhelms something or someone, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Market pulverizer
The new software was a pulverizer in the market, leaving competitors far behind.
In the debate, she acted as a pulverizer, dismantling every argument with ease.
Their team became known as the pulverizer after winning every game by a huge margin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conqueror - a person who defeats others completely
  • synonym: destroyer - someone or something that causes destruction
  • antonym: victim - a person who is defeated or harmed
