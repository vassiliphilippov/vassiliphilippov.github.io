scepter

noun
imperial authority symbolized by a scepter
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛptər /; / ˈsɛp.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛptə /; / ˈsɛp.tə / (BrE)
a decorated or ceremonial staff carried or held by a ruler as a symbol of royal, imperial, or other authority or power
