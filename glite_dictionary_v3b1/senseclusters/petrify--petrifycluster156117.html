petrify

verb
change (something) into stone
2.9 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtrɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to change or convert (organic matter) into stone or a stony substance, especially by a natural process

Examples

To petrify wood
Mineral-rich water can slowly petrify wood, preserving its external structure.
Scientists discovered a petrified log embedded in the cliff.
The ancient forest was gradually petrified over millions of years.
