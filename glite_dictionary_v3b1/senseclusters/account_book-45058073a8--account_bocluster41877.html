account book

noun
book for recording financial transactions
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊnt bʊk / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt bʊk / (BrE)
a book or digital record in which a business's financial transactions are recorded and summarized

Examples

The account book
After the audit, several errors were found in their account book.
She updated the account book every week to ensure accurate records.
The company kept a detailed account book to track all sales and expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ledger - a book or other collection of financial accounts
