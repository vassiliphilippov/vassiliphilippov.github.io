wooo

interjection
ghostly exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / wuː / (AmE), / wuː / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating a long, low moan or ghostly sound, often used to suggest eeriness or to playfully pretend to be a ghost

Examples

Wooo like a ghost
The children ran around the house shouting wooo to scare each other on Halloween night.
He crept up behind his friend and whispered wooo in a spooky voice.
A sudden wooo echoed through the empty hallway, making everyone jump.
