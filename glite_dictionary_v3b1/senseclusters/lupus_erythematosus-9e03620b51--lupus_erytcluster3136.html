lupus erythematosus

noun
chronic autoimmune disease affecting skin and joints
5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈluː.pəs ə.rɪ.θə.ˈmæt.ə.səs/ (AmE), /ˈluː.pəs ə.rɪ.θə.ˈmæt.ə.səs/ (BrE)
A chronic autoimmune disease causing inflammation and damage to skin, joints, and other connective tissue.
