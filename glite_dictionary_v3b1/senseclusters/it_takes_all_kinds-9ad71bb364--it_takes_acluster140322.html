it takes all kinds

x
different people have different preferences
Pronunciation: / ɪt teɪks ɔl kaɪndz / (AmE), / ɪt teɪks ɔːl kaɪndz / (BrE)
used to express the idea that people have many different personalities, tastes, or opinions, and that variety is a normal part of life

Examples

It takes all kinds of people
He collects stamps and she loves skydiving; what can I say, it takes all kinds.
Some people enjoy early mornings, while others prefer to stay up late—it takes all kinds.
You might not share her taste in music, but it takes all kinds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: variety is the spice of life - the idea that new and different experiences make life more interesting
