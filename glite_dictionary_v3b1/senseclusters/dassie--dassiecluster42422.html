dassie

noun
small African and Asian hoofed mammal (hyrax)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdæsi / (AmE), / ˈdasi / (BrE)
A small hoofed mammal native to Africa and Asia, resembling a rodent but more closely related to elephants and manatees; also known as a hyrax.

Examples

A dassie animal
Despite its rodent-like appearance, a dassie is actually related to elephants.
The dassie is a common sight in many parts of southern Africa.
We spotted a dassie sunning itself on the rocks near Table Mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyrax - another name for dassie; small herbivorous mammal of Africa and the Middle East
