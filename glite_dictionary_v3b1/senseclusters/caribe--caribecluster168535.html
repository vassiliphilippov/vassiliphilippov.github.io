caribe

noun
South American carnivorous freshwater fish
6.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈrib.eɪ / (AmE), / kəˈriː.beɪ / (BrE)
A small, fiercely carnivorous freshwater fish from South America, known for attacking and consuming living animals.
