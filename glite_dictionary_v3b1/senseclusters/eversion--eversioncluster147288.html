eversion

noun
the state of being turned outward
9.9
Pronunciation: / iˈvɜrʒən /; / iˈvɜr.ʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈvɜːʃən /; / ɪˈvɜː.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of turning something outward or inside out, especially a part of the body such as the foot or eyelid
