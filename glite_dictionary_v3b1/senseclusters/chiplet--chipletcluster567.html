chiplet

noun
small circuit component
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪplət / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪplət / (BrE)
a small integrated circuit designed to function as part of a larger computer chip

Examples

Small chiplets inside
The new graphics card uses several chiplets to increase its processing power.
