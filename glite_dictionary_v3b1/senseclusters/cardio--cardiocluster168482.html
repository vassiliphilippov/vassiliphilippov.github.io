cardio

noun
aerobic exercise
3.2 3.74/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêrdio ä / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêdi…ô ä / (BrE)
physical exercise that raises your heart rate and improves heart and lung health

Examples

To do cardio exercise
She does cardio every morning to stay healthy and energetic.
Many people combine cardio with weight training for a balanced workout routine.
Running, cycling, and swimming are all popular forms of cardio.

Synonyms

  • aerobic exercise
    2.4 1.40/m
    aerobic exercise increases the body's need for oxygen and builds endurance, while cardio is a general term for heart-raising workouts.
  • aerobics
    3.2 2.87/m
    aerobics is a class of rhythmic exercises often done with music, while cardio is any exercise that raises the heart rate.
  • cardiopulmonary exercise
    4.9 0.00/m
    cardiopulmonary exercise is a medical term for testing or training the heart and lungs, while cardio is everyday exercise for heart fitness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerobics - exercise that improves the efficiency of the body's cardiovascular system
  • antonym: strength training - exercise focused on building muscle strength
