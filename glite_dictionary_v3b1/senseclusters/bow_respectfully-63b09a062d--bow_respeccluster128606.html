bow respectfully

verb
bend forward as a sign of respect
Pronunciation: / baʊ rɪˈspɛktfəli / (AmE), / baʊ rɪˈspɛktfəli / (BrE)
to bend one's upper body forward from the waist as a sign of respect or deference

Examples

To bow respectfully to guests
As the ambassador entered, the guards bowed respectfully to greet him.
During the ceremony, each student bowed respectfully to the principal.
He paused at the entrance and bowed respectfully before taking a seat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - to bend forward at the waist, especially as a formal gesture
