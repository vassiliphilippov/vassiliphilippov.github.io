death rate

noun
number of deaths per population per year
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ ˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌreɪt / (BrE)
the number of deaths in a population during a specific period, usually shown as deaths per 1,000 people per year

Examples

The death rate increases
Many scientists study the death rate to understand the impact of diseases on communities.
The death rate among infants has dropped due to better nutrition and vaccination programs.
The death rate in the region decreased significantly after improvements in healthcare.
Policies aimed at reducing the infant death rate have proven effective.
Scientists were concerned about the high death rate in the region after the recent epidemic.
The country's death rate has declined significantly over the past decade thanks to improved healthcare.

Related Terms

  • antonym: birth rate - number of births per 1,000 people per year
  • synonym: mortality rate - the rate at which deaths occur in a population
