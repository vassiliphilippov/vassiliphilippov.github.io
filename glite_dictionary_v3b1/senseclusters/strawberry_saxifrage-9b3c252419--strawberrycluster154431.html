strawberry saxifrage

noun
an eastern Asian flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ˈsæksɪˌfrædʒ / (AmE), / ˈstrɒbəri ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a plant of the saxifrage family native to eastern Asia, having racemes of small red-and-white flowers and spreading by creeping stolons
