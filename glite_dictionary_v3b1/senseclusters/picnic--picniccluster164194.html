picnic

noun
something easy or pleasant
7.6 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.nɪk/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.nɪk/ (BrE)
an activity or situation that is very easy or pleasant to do or deal with, often used in negative phrases to mean something is difficult

Examples

Not a picnic
He thought the exam would be a picnic, but it turned out to be quite challenging.
Marketing this product will be no picnic in the current economy.
Raising three kids on your own is certainly not a picnic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: challenge - something that is difficult; the opposite of something easy
  • synonym: walk in the park - an activity that is very easy or pleasant to do
