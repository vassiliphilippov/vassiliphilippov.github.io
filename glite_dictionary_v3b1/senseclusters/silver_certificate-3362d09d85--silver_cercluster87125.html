silver certificate

noun
historic U.S. banknote exchangeable for silver
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ sərˌtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə səˌtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
a former U.S. banknote issued by the United States Treasury that could be exchanged for a specific amount of silver.
