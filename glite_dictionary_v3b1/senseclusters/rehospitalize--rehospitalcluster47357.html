rehospitalize

verb
to admit again
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈhɑːspɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to admit someone to a hospital again after they have already been discharged

Examples

To rehospitalize a patient
The doctors decided to rehospitalize her after her symptoms worsened at home.
If complications arise, the patient may need to be rehospitalized for further treatment.
He was rehospitalized just two weeks after his initial discharge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discharge - to officially allow someone to leave a hospital
  • synonym: readmit - to allow someone to enter again, especially to a hospital
