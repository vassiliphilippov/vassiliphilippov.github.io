at times

adverb
occasionally; sometimes
Pronunciation: / æt taɪmz / (AmE), / ət taɪmz / (BrE)
occasionally; sometimes, but not regularly or frequently

Examples

At times occasionally
At times, I find it difficult to concentrate when there is too much noise.
At times, the weather here can change very quickly.
She can be very talkative at times, but mostly she is quiet.
