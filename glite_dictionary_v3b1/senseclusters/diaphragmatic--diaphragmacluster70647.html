diaphragmatic

adjective
of the diaphragm
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəfrəgˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəfrəgˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to the diaphragm, the muscle that separates the chest and abdominal cavities and aids in breathing

Examples

Diaphragmatic breathing
The doctor explained that diaphragmatic movement is essential for proper breathing.
Singers often practice diaphragmatic breathing to improve their voice control.
A diaphragmatic hernia can cause serious health problems if not treated promptly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abdominal - relating to the abdomen
  • synonym: thoracic - relating to the chest or thorax
