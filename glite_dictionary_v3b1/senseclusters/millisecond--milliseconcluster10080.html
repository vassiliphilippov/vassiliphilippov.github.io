millisecond

noun
one thousandth of a second
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪˌsɛkənd / (BrE)
One thousandth of a second; a unit of time equal to 0.001 seconds.

Examples

A millisecond delay
A camera shutter can open and close in just a few milliseconds.
He responded to the signal within a millisecond delay, impressing the judges.
The computer processes each instruction in less than a millisecond.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: microsecond - one millionth (10^-6) of a second
  • degree higher: second - the base unit of time in the International System of Units (SI)
