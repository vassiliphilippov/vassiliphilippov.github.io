alt

noun
alternative account
Pronunciation: / ælt / (AmE), / ɒlt / (BrE)
an alternative account, character, or option, especially in online games or digital platforms

Examples

Online alt account
She uses an alt to play anonymously in the online game.
Many users create an alt to try out new strategies without affecting their main account.
He switched to his alt to join a different group of friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternative - a different option or choice
  • antonym: main - a primary account or character
