common winterberry holly

noun
North American holly shrub with red winter berries
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈwɪntərˌbɛri ˈhɑli / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈwɪntəbəri ˈhɒli / (BrE)
a deciduous holly shrub (Ilex verticillata) native to eastern North America, known for its bright red berries that persist through winter
