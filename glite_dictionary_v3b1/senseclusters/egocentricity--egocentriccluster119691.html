egocentricity

noun
self-centeredness
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡoʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being focused on oneself and one's own interests, often ignoring others

Examples

The manager's egocentricity was evident in every decision she made.
Overcoming egocentricity can help people build stronger relationships with others.
