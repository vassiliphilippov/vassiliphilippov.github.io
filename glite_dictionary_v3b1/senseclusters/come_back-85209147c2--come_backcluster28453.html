come back

verb
regain an equal score in sports
Pronunciation: / kʌm bæk / (AmE), / kʌm bæk / (BrE)
to reach an equal score after being behind in a sports contest; to make up a deficit in points or goals

Examples

Come back to tie
If we come back in the second half, we might still have a chance to win.
She was thrilled when her favorite player helped the team come back and draw the match.
The team managed to come back from a two-goal deficit to tie the game.
