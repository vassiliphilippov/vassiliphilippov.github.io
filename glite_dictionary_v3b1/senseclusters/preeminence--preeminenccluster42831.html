preeminence

noun
superiority or highest status
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriˈɛmɪnəns / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈɛmɪnəns / (BrE)
The fact of being superior to others in quality, importance, or achievement.

Examples

The preeminence of leaders
Few writers have matched the preeminence she attained in the 20th century.
Her preeminence in the field of genetics is recognized worldwide.
The orchestra achieved international preeminence under his leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eminence - high rank or superiority
  • antonym: inferiority - being lower in status or quality
