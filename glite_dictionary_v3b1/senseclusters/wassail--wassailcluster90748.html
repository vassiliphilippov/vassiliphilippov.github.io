wassail

verb
to sing carols door-to-door
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːseɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɒseɪl / (BrE)
to go from house to house singing and celebrating, especially during Christmas time

Examples

To wassail with songs
Every Christmas Eve, the children wassail through the neighborhood, singing joyful songs.
In some villages, people still wassail to bring good luck for the new year.
They planned to wassail after dinner, visiting friends and sharing holiday cheer.
