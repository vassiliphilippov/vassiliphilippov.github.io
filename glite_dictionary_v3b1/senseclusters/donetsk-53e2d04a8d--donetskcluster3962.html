Donetsk

proper noun
self‑proclaimed separatist entity/region
8.3
Pronunciation: /də.ˈnetsk/ (AmE), /də.ˈnetsk/ (BrE)
A self-proclaimed political entity and the authorities controlling parts of Donetsk Oblast in eastern Ukraine (the Donetsk People's Republic); widely unrecognized internationally.

Examples

Donetsk authorities
The Donetsk authorities announced new border controls after the latest clashes with government forces.
International observers reported that much of Donetsk now functions under the control of separatist administrators.
Sanctions were proposed against leaders in Donetsk accused of supporting armed groups.
