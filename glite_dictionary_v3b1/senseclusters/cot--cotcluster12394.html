cot

noun
folding portable bed
8.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /kɑːt/ /; / /kɔːt/ (AmE), /kɒt/ (BrE)
A small bed, often folding, used for sleeping, usually portable and easy to store or transport.

Examples

Camping cot
She quickly folded up the cot after the camping trip.
The guest slept comfortably on the cot they set up in the living room.
We keep a cot in the closet for when friends stay over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crib - a small bed with high sides for a baby or young child (US usage: crib, UK usage: cot)
