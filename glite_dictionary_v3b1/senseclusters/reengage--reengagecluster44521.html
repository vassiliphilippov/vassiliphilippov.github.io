reengage

verb
to hire again
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
to hire, appoint, or involve someone again, or to renew someone's services or contract

Examples

To reengage a worker
After a successful project, the company decided to reengage the consultant for another year.
The school board voted to reengage the principal after reviewing her performance.
They hope to reengage former employees for the upcoming busy season.
