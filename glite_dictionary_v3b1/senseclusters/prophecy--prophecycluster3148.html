prophecy

noun
divinely inspired prediction
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfəsi / (AmE), / ˈprɒfəsi / (BrE)
A statement that predicts what will happen in the future, especially one made under divine inspiration.

Examples

A prophecy comes true
According to the prophecy, a chosen one will unite the divided lands.
Her prophecy was recorded and studied for generations.
The ancient seer delivered a prophecy about the fall of the kingdom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prediction - statement about what will happen, not necessarily divinely inspired
  • synonym: prophet - a person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of the will of God
