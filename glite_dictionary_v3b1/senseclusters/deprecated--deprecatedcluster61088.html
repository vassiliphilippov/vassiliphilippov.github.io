deprecated

verb
to express disapproval
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛprəˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛprɪˌkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
expressed disapproval of something or criticized it, especially in the past.

Examples

She deprecated slang
The teacher deprecated the use of slang in formal essays.
Many experts deprecated the outdated technology in their reports.
She deprecated his decision to skip the meeting without notice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criticized - expressed disapproval or pointed out faults
  • antonym: praised - expressed approval or admiration
