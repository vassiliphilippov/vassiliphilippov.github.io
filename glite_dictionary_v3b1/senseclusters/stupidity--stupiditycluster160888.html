stupidity

noun
a stupid mistake or act
1.5
Pronunciation: / stuˈpɪdəti / (AmE), / stjuːˈpɪdəti / (BrE)
A stupid mistake or act; something done that shows a lack of good sense or judgment.

Examples

A big stupidity
His biggest stupidity was trusting someone he barely knew.
It was sheer stupidity to leave the keys in the car overnight.
We all laughed about the stupidity of his last-minute decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • synonym: folly - a lack of good sense; foolishness
  • antonym: wisdom - the quality of having experience, knowledge, and good judgment
