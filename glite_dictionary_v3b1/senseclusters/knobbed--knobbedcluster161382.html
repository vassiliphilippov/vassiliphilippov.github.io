knobbed

adjective
having knobs or rounded lumps
7.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / nÉ‘bd / (AmE), / nÉ’bd / (BrE)
having a surface covered with knobs or small rounded lumps; gnarled or knotted in appearance.

Examples

A knobbed walking stick
Her knobbed walking stick had clearly seen years of use over rocky paths.
The ancient gardener's hands were knobbed and strong from decades of hard labor.
The old oak tree had a thick, knobbed trunk that twisted toward the sky.

Synonyms

  • knobby
    5.8 0.29/m
    knobby is covered in small rounded bumps on a surface, while knobbed often looks gnarled or knotted.
  • knotty
    7.0 0.40/m
    knotty has many knots or tangles in wood or hair, while knobbed is bumpy and gnarled with small knobs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gnarled - rough, twisted, and knobby, especially of trees or hands
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; free from lumps or projections
