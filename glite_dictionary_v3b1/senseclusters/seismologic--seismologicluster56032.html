seismologic

adjective
relating to seismology
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪzməˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪzməˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with seismology, the scientific study of earthquakes and the movements of the Earth's crust.

Examples
