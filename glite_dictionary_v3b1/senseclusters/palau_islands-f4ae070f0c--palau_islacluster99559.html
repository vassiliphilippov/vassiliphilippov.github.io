Palau Islands

proper noun
chain of islands in the western Pacific Ocean
5.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈlaʊ ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / pəˈlaʊ ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
a chain of more than 200 islands, about 400 miles long, located in the western central Pacific Ocean, forming part of the country of Palau

Examples

The Palau Islands chain
