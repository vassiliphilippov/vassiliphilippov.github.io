underclass

noun
the lowest social class in society
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌklæs /; / ˈʌndərˌklæs / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌklɑːs / (BrE)
the group of people in a society who are at the lowest social level, often facing poverty and few opportunities
