antiracial

adjective
opposed to racism
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌæntaɪˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
opposed to racism, racial discrimination, or the use of race as a category

Examples

Antiracial policy
The school adopted an antiracial policy to ensure equal treatment for all students.
Many activists support antiracial initiatives to combat discrimination in the workplace.
The new law reflects the country's commitment to antiracial principles.
The organization promotes antiracial policies that avoid classifying people by race.
Her antiracial views challenge the use of racial categories in government statistics.
Many educators support an antiracial approach to curriculum development in schools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiracist - opposed to racism or racial prejudice
  • synonym: nonracial - not involving or based on race
  • antonym: racial - relating to race or races
  • antonym: racist - showing or believing in racial prejudice or discrimination
