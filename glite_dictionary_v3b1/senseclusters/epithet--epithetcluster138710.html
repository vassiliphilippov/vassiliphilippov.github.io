epithet

noun
abusive or insulting word/phrase
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpəˌθɛt / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪθɛt / (BrE)
a word or phrase used to insult or abuse someone

Examples

Rude epithet
The politician was offended by the epithet hurled at him during the debate.
Throughout the heated argument, angry epithets were exchanged between the two rivals.
Using a racial epithet is never acceptable in any context.
