Examples

Complete bafflement

Synonyms

  • perplexity
    7.5 0.80/m
    perplexity describes being puzzled by something complex or difficult, while bafflement is a more general feeling of confusion.
  • puzzlement
    5.5 1.04/m
    puzzlement means feeling confused because something is hard to understand, while bafflement is a broader kind of confusion.
  • bewilderment
    4.8 1.69/m
    bewilderment is strong, often sudden confusion that leaves people lost and unsure, while bafflement feels more steady puzzlement.
  • bemusement
    7.4 0.34/m
    bemusement often shows quiet, puzzled thought or mild amusement, while bafflement is plain confusion without the amused tone.
  • befuddlement
    8.1 0.20/m
    befuddlement describes muddled, mixed-up thinking and can sound informal, while bafflement is a straightforward feeling of puzzlement.
  • mystification
    8.1 0.51/m
    mystification can mean making something deliberately hard to understand or feeling puzzled, while bafflement is just the state of confusion.
  • incomprehension
    6.9 0.54/m
    incomprehension is an inability to understand something in a more neutral, often formal way, while bafflement shows emotional puzzlement.
