prefer over

verb
like one thing more than another
7.5
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɜr ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / prɪˈfɜː ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to choose or like one option more than another; to show a preference for one thing instead of another

Examples

To prefer over others

Many diners preferred over the vegetarian option to the meat dish.
Most people prefer over paying in cash when they shop online.
