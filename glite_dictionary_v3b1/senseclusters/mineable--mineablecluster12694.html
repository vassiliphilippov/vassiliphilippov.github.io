mineable

adjective
able to be mined
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be extracted, obtained, or processed by mining, especially using digital or computational methods

Examples

Mineable data sets
The data collected from the survey is highly mineable for marketing insights.
Only certain types of cryptocurrency are mineable using standard computer hardware.
Geologists determined that the mineral deposits were not economically mineable at current prices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extractable - able to be taken out or obtained
  • antonym: unmineable - not able to be mined or extracted
