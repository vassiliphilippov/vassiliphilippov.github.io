stakes

noun
tokens locked for staking
8.6
Pronunciation: /steɪks/ (AmE), /steɪks/ (BrE)
(in cryptocurrency and blockchain contexts) the total amount or value of tokens that users have locked or committed to a network or protocol in order to participate (for example in consensus or governance) and to earn rewards.

Examples

Crypto stakes rise
The platform reported rising stakes as more users locked tokens to earn network rewards.
She checked her wallet before increasing her stakes in the proof-of-stake pool.
Large institutional stakes can influence governance votes on many decentralized platforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stake - an amount of money, goods, or tokens invested in or risked on something
  • synonym: staking - the process of locking tokens to support a blockchain network and earn rewards
