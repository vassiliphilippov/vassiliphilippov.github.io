Nowrooz

noun
Persian New Year festival
8.5
Pronunciation: / naʊˈruːz / (AmE), / naʊˈruːz / (BrE)
the Persian New Year celebrated at the vernal equinox, observed in Iran, Azerbaijan, Afghanistan, Pakistan, parts of India, and among the Kurds

Examples

Nowrooz celebration
During Nowrooz, it is customary to visit relatives and exchange greetings for the new year.
Every spring, families come together to celebrate Nowrooz with traditional foods and music.
The school organized a special performance to honor Nowrooz and share its significance with students.
