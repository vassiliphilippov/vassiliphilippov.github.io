recontextualization

noun
placing in new context
9.8
Pronunciation: /riː.kən.tek.tʃuː.ə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.kən.tek.tʃuː.ə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.kən.tek.tʃuː.ə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.kən.tek.tʃuː.ə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of placing something in a new context to give it a different meaning or interpretation

Related Terms

  • antonym: decontextualization - the act of removing something from its original context
  • synonym: reinterpretation - the act of interpreting something in a new or different way
