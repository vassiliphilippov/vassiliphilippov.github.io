light-minded

adjective
frivolous; lacking seriousness
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
treating serious matters with a lack of seriousness or inappropriate levity; frivolous or careless.

Examples

A light-minded person
Being light-minded can sometimes be mistaken for being optimistic, but it may come across as insensitive.
His light-minded attitude towards the accident upset many of his colleagues.
She was criticized for her light-minded approach to work responsibilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frivolous - not serious or sensible; carefree
  • antonym: serious - thoughtful and solemn; not treating matters lightly
