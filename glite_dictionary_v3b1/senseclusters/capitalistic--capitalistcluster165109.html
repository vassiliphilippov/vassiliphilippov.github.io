capitalistic

adjective
relating to capitalism or capitalists
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪtəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkapɪtəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or supporting capitalism or capitalist values

Examples

A capitalistic society
Her views are strongly capitalistic, supporting free markets and private ownership.
Some critics argue that capitalistic societies increase inequality between rich and poor.
The company operates according to capitalistic principles, focusing on profit and competition.
His capitalistic ideals shaped the way he ran his business.
Many critics argue that capitalistic societies can increase economic inequality.
The company's capitalistic approach focuses on maximizing profits above all else.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anticapitalist - opposed to capitalism or capitalists
  • synonym: capitalist - a person who supports or benefits from capitalism
  • antonym: socialistic - favouring or practicing socialism
