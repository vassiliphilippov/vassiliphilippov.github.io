spellcheck

noun
spelling correction tool
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛlˌtʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈspɛlˌtʃɛk / (BrE)
a computer tool or process that checks and corrects spelling in written text

Examples

Run a spellcheck
Before submitting her essay, she ran the spellcheck to catch any mistakes.
The spellcheck highlighted several words that were spelled incorrectly in his document.
Many email programs include a built-in spellcheck to help users avoid typos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocorrect - a feature that automatically corrects spelling or typing errors
  • synonym: proofreader - a person or tool that checks written text for errors
