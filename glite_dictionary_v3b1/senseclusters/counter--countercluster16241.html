counter

noun
return blow in boxing or combat sports
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
a return action or reply, such as a return blow in sports or a quick response in conversation

Examples

Counter attack
A well-timed counter can quickly change the outcome of a match.
She trained hard to perfect her counter and catch her rivals off guard.
The boxer landed a swift counter after dodging his opponent's right hook.
He offered a clever counter to her sarcastic comment.
Her counter left everyone in the room speechless.
The politician's counter was both witty and disarming.

  • synonym: rejoinder - a quick reply, especially a sharp or witty one

  • synonym: riposte - a quick, clever reply to an insult or criticism
