almond tree

noun
tree producing edible almonds
2.7 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀêlm…ônd triÀê / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀêm…ônd triÀê / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree with pink or white blossoms, cultivated for its edible nuts (almonds).

Examples

A almond tree blossom
A row of almond trees stretched along the hillside, creating a beautiful landscape in bloom.
Farmers carefully tend each almond tree to ensure a good crop of nuts.
The almond tree in their garden was covered with delicate pink blossoms each spring.

Synonyms
