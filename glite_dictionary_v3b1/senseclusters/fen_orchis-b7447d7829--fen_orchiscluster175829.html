fen orchis

noun
a small orchid of North America and Europe
6.3
Pronunciation: / fɛn ˈɔrkɪs / (AmE), / fɛn ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
a small terrestrial orchid native to eastern North America and Europe, with two nearly basal leaves and dull yellow-green racemose flowers

Examples

A fen orchis flower
Botanists discovered a rare fen orchis while surveying the European wetlands.
The fen orchis thrives in wet, marshy meadows during the late spring.
The delicate flowers of the fen orchis are often overlooked due to their muted color.
