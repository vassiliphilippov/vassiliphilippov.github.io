blackfin

noun
fish with dark fins
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈblækˌfɪn / (BrE)
a type of fish characterized by dark or black fins, especially certain species of tuna or shark

Examples

A blackfin fish
The diver spotted a blackfin swimming near the coral reef.
Fishermen often seek the blackfin for its speed and strength.
A large blackfin was displayed at the local seafood market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shark - a large predatory fish with a cartilaginous skeleton
  • synonym: tuna - a large, fast-swimming fish found in warm seas
