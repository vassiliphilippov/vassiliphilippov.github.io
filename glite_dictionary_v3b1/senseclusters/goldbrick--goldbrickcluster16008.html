goldbrick

verb
to avoid work or duties (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbrɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbrɪk / (BrE)
(informal) To avoid work or one's assigned duties, especially by pretending to be busy or ill.

Examples

To goldbrick at work

Instead of finishing his assignment, he chose to goldbrick in the break room.
Some employees found ways to goldbrick by looking busy at their desks.
