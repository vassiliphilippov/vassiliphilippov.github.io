bumper guard

noun
vertical bar on a car's bumper for protection
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmpər ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpə ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a vertical bar or device attached to a vehicle's bumper to prevent it from locking with another vehicle or being damaged

Examples

Car bumper guards
He decided to install bumper guards after scratching his vehicle in a parking lot.
Old models often featured large chrome bumper guards for extra safety.
The car has sturdy bumper guards to protect it from minor collisions.
