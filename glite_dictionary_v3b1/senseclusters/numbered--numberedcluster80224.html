numbered

adjective
having a limited or soon-ending existence
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbəd / (BrE)
having a limited amount of time or existence remaining; likely to end soon

Examples

Numbered days

She knew her time as president was numbered once the scandal broke.
With resources numbered, the team had to act quickly before supplies ran out.
