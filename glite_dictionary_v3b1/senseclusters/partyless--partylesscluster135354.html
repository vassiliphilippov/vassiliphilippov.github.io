partyless

adjective
without political party
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtiləs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtiləs / (BrE)
not belonging to or associated with any political party

Examples

Partyless government
The new constitution established a partyless system of government.
He ran as a partyless candidate, appealing to voters tired of party politics.
Some believe a partyless democracy can reduce political division in society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not affiliated with any political party
  • antonym: partisan - strongly supporting a particular political party
