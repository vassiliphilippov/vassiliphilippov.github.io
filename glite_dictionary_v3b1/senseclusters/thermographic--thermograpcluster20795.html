thermographic

adjective
heat-based printing
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by a printing process that uses heat to create raised, glossy lettering or designs.

Examples

Thermographic printing process
The company used thermographic printing to make their business cards stand out with a glossy finish.
Invitations with thermographic lettering feel elegant and slightly raised to the touch.
She admired the thermographic details on the certificate, which shimmered under the light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embossed - having a raised design or lettering
