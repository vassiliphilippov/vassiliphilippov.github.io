blond

adjective
having pale or yellowish hair
1.0 14/m
Pronunciation: / blÉ‘nd / (AmE), / blÉ’nd / (BrE)
(of a person) having pale or yellowish hair and often fair skin and light-colored eyes.

Examples

Blond hair color
His blond hair shone in the sunlight as he walked down the street.
Many people associate Scandinavia with tall, blond individuals.
She admired the blond child playing in the park.
Her blonde hair shone brightly in the summer sunlight.
Many blonde people also have fair skin and light-colored eyes.
Several of the children in the photograph are blonde and have striking blue eyes.

Synonyms

  • light-haired
    2.6 0.06/m
    light-haired describes someone with any pale or light-colored hair, while blond refers to pale yellow hair often with fair skin and light eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blonde - female form; also fair-haired woman
  • antonym: brunette - having dark brown or black hair
