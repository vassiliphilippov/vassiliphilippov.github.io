tendentiousness

noun
deliberate and controversial bias
7.6
Pronunciation: / tɛnˈdɛnʃəsnəs / (AmE), / tɛnˈdɛnʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing or promoting a particular, often controversial, point of view; intentional bias

Examples

The tendentiousness of speech
Judges must avoid tendentiousness to maintain public confidence in their impartiality.
Some critics accused the documentary of tendentiousness in its portrayal of events.
The tendentiousness of the article was apparent to even casual readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bias - prejudice in favor or against one thing, person, or group compared with another
  • antonym: impartiality - the state of treating all rivals or disputants equally
  • antonym: objectivity - the state of being unbiased and impartial
