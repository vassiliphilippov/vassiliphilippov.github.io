tendentiousness

noun
deliberate and controversial bias
7.6 7.7
Pronunciation: /ten.ˈden.ʃəs.nəs/ /; / /tə.ˈden.ʃəs.nəs/ (AmE), /ten.ˈden.ʃəs.nəs/ /; / /tə.ˈden.ʃəs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of showing or promoting a particular, often controversial, point of view; intentional bias

Examples

The tendentiousness of the article was apparent to even casual readers.
