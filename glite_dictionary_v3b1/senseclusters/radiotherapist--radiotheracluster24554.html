radiotherapist

noun
medical specialist using radiation for treatment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
a medical specialist who treats diseases by using radioactive substances and X-rays

Examples

A radiotherapist treats cancer
Modern technology has greatly improved the tools available to the radiotherapist in targeting tumors.
She was referred to a radiotherapist after her cancer diagnosis for specialized care.
The radiotherapist explained the treatment plan to the patient before starting the first session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oncologist - a doctor who treats cancer
