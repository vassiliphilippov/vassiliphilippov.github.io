radiotherapist

noun
medical specialist using radiation for treatment
1.0 5.3
Pronunciation: /re.eɪ.di.oʊ.ˈθer.ə.pɪst/ /; / /re.eɪ.dɪ.oʊ.ˈθer.ə.pɪst/ (AmE), /re.eɪ.di.əʊ.ˈθer.ə.pɪst/ /; / /re.eɪ.dɪ.əʊ.ˈθer.ə.pɪst/ (BrE)
