intersessional

noun
course between terms
8.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)
a short meeting or academic course that takes place between regular sessions or terms

Examples

An intersessional course

The university offers an intersessional for students who want to catch up on missed courses.
Many students use the intersessional period to take intensive language classes.
