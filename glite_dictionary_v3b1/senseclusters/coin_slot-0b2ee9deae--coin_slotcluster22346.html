coin slot

noun
narrow opening for inserting coins in a machine
4.9
Pronunciation: / kɔɪn slɑt / (AmE), / kɔɪn slɒt / (BrE)
a narrow opening in a machine, such as a vending machine or payphone, through which coins are inserted
