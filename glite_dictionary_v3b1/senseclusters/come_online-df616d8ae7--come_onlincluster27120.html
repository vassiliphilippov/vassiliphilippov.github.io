come online

verb
to become operational or available (of a system, etc.)
1.0
Pronunciation: / kʌm ɑnˈlaɪn / (AmE), / kʌm ɒnˈlaɪn / (BrE)
(of a system, service, or feature) to become operational or available for use, especially after being installed, developed, or prepared.

Examples

To come online soon
The new product will come online by the end of the month.
The website finally came online after weeks of preparation.
We are waiting for the power plant to come online before starting operations.
