niff

noun
an unpleasant smell
Pronunciation: / nɪf / (AmE), / nɪf / (BrE)
an unpleasant smell or stink, especially of something stale or offensive

Examples

A niff in fridge
As soon as we opened the fridge, a nasty niff hit us.
She wrinkled her nose at the niff of damp clothes left in the laundry.
There was a strong niff coming from the garbage bin outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aroma - a pleasant, distinctive smell
  • synonym: stench - a strong and unpleasant smell
