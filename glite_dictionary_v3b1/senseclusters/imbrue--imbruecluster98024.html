imbrue

verb
to stain or permeate deeply
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbru / (AmE), / ɪmˈbruː / (BrE)
to permeate or stain thoroughly, especially with blood or a similar substance

Examples

To imbrue with blood
Centuries of conflict had imbrued the region with a legacy of violence.
His hands were imbrued in crime, leaving a stain upon his name.
The battle imbrued the fields with blood and sorrow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanse - to remove dirt, stains, or impurities from something
  • synonym: stain - to mark or discolor with something that is difficult to remove
