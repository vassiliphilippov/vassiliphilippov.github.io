hosier

noun
dealer in hosiery and knitwear
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊʒiər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊziə / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells stockings, socks, and other knitted goods, especially hosiery or knitwear

Examples

A hosier shop
In Victorian London, the hosier was a common sight in the bustling markets.
She visited her local hosier to buy new stockings and knitwear.
The hosier displayed an array of colorful socks in the shop window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haberdasher - a dealer in men's clothing or small articles for sewing
