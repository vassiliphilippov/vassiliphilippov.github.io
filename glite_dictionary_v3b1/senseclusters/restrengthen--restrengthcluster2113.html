restrengthen

verb
make strong again
Pronunciation: / riˈstrɛŋθən / (AmE), / riˈstrɛŋθən / (BrE)
to make strong again; to restore strength to something or someone

Examples

To restrengthen public trust
New policies were introduced to restrengthen public trust in the government.
Physical therapy helped him restrengthen his injured leg over the months.
The community worked together to restrengthen its economy after the storm.
