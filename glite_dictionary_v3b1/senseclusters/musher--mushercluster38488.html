musher

noun
driver of a dog team
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃər / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃə / (BrE)
a person who drives or travels with a dog team, especially in sled dog racing or travel across snow

Examples

A musher with dogs
As a young musher, she learned to read the signs of changing weather on the trail.
Every musher must train their dogs carefully for the long Iditarod race.
The experienced musher guided his team through the blizzard with confidence.
