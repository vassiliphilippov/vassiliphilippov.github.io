rosella

noun
hibiscus-based jam or drink
9.1
Pronunciation: / roʊˈzɛlə / (AmE), / rəʊˈzɛlə / (BrE)
a food or drink made from the calyces of the rosella plant, often used as a tart jam or floral beverage

Examples

Rosella tea drink
She enjoyed a cup of rosella tea with her breakfast every morning.
The homemade rosella jam added a tart flavor to the scones.
Vendors at the festival sold bottles of sweet and tangy rosella syrup.

  • synonym: jam - a sweet spread made from fruit and sugar
