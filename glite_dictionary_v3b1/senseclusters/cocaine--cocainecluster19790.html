cocaine

noun
addictive narcotic drug from coca leaves
2.1 5.0
Pronunciation: /kəʊ.ˈkeɪn/ /; / /koʊ.ˈkeɪn/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈkeɪn/ (BrE)
