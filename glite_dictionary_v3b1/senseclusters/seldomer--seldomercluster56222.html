seldomer

adverb
less frequently
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛldəmər / (AmE), / ˈsɛldəmə / (BrE)
less often; on fewer occasions than something else or than before

Examples

Call seldomer now
After moving to the city, she called her parents seldomer than she used to.
The tradition is observed even seldomer in modern times than in the past.
He attends the weekly meetings seldomer now that his schedule is busier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: less often - on fewer occasions; not as frequently
  • antonym: more often - on more occasions; more frequently
  • degree lower: seldom - not often; rarely
