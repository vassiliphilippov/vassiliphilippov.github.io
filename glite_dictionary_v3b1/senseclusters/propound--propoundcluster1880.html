propound

verb
put forward for consideration
5.4 7.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈpaʊnd / (AmE), / prəˈpaʊnd / (BrE)
to put forward (an idea, argument, or theory) for consideration by others

Examples

To propound an idea
In her paper, she propounds the idea that language shapes thought.
Many scientists propound innovative ideas that challenge established beliefs.
The philosopher chose to propound a new theory about the nature of reality.
