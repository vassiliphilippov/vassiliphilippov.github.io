Kardashian

adjective
celebrity style or image
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈdæʃiən / (AmE), / kɑːˈdæʃiən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the style, image, or publicity associated with the Kardashian family, especially in fashion or media.

Examples

Kardashian style outfit
The event had a Kardashian vibe, with luxurious decorations and plenty of media attention.
She wore a Kardashian outfit, complete with bold makeup and designer accessories.
His social media posts often feature a Kardashian level of glamour and self-promotion.
