electronic information service

noun
database accessible by computers
1.1 3.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˌɪnfəˈmeɪʃən ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
A system or database that provides information and can be accessed electronically by computers.

Examples

An electronic information service system
Accessing medical journals through an electronic information service has become standard practice in hospitals.
Many companies offer an electronic information service that provides real-time financial data.
The library subscribed to an electronic information service for research articles.
