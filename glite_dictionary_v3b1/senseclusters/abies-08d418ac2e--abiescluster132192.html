Abies

noun
genus of true fir trees
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz / (BrE)
A genus of coniferous trees known as the true firs, characterized by needle-like leaves and upright cones.

Abies tree species
Many Christmas trees are cultivated from Abies, particularly the noble and balsam firs.
