obduction

noun
covering or overlay
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑbˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ɒbˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
an archaic term for a covering or layer that overlays a surface

Examples

Obduction of wax
The ancient manuscript was protected by an obduction of wax that preserved its pages.
Restorers carefully removed the obduction of dust from the old painting.
Centuries of grime formed an obduction that hid the original colors of the fresco.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coating - a layer that covers a surface
  • synonym: overlay - something laid over or covering another thing
