ultracold

adjective
extremely cold
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌkoʊld / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəkəʊld / (BrE)
extremely cold; having a temperature much lower than usual in everyday situations such as weather, storage, or objects.

Examples

Ultracold storage
The laboratory uses ultracold storage units to preserve sensitive chemicals.
Scientists studied the behavior of materials at ultracold temperatures.
The vaccines must be kept in ultracold freezers to remain effective.
