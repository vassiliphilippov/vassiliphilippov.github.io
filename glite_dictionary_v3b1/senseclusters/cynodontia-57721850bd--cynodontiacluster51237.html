Cynodontia

noun
division of extinct mammal-like reptiles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪ.nəˈdɒn.ʃi.ə /; / saɪˈnɒntiə / (AmE), / ˌsɪ.nəˈdɒn.tɪ.ə /; / sɪˈnɒntiə / (BrE)
