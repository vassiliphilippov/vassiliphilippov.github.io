reprise

noun
musical repetition
Pronunciation: / rəˈpriːz / (AmE), / rəˈpriːz / (BrE)
a repeated section or passage in a musical or dramatic performance

Examples

A reprise section
The orchestra played a beautiful reprise of the opening melody at the end of the concert.
After the intermission, the actors performed a reprise of the show's most popular scene.
Fans cheered when the band included a reprise of their hit song in the encore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repeat - a part that is performed again
  • antonym: variation - a different version of a musical theme
