spring to life

verb
suddenly become active or lively
5.0
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ tə laɪf / (AmE), / sprɪŋ tə laɪf / (BrE)
to suddenly become active, energetic, or functional; to start working or moving briskly or with enthusiasm, often after a period of inactivity

Examples

To spring to life suddenly
After hours of silence, the classroom sprang to life when the bell rang for recess.
The dusty radio sprang to life as soon as he plugged it in.
With the arrival of spring, the entire forest springs to life with the sounds of birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: come to life - to become lively, active, or interesting
  • synonym: spring into action - to begin to act suddenly or energetically
