suprematist

noun
artist of the suprematism movement
6.4
Pronunciation: / suˈpriː.mə.tɪst / (AmE), / suˈpriː.mə.tɪst / (BrE)
an artist who belongs to the school of suprematism, a movement in abstract art that emphasizes basic geometric forms

Examples

A suprematist artist
As a suprematist, her art explored pure abstraction through simple shapes and colors.
Kazimir Malevich is widely regarded as a leading suprematist in modern art.
The exhibition features works by every significant suprematist from the early 20th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constructivist - an artist belonging to the Constructivism movement
  • synonym: futurist - an artist associated with the Futurism art movement
