suprematist

noun
artist of the suprematism movement
6.4 9.3
Pronunciation: /suː.ˈpre.mə.tɪst/ /; / /sjuː.ˈpre.mə.tɪst/ (AmE), /suː.ˈpre.mə.tɪst/ /; / /sjuː.ˈpre.mə.tɪst/ (BrE)
