ununiformed

adjective
not in uniform
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.juːˈnɪfɔːrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.juːˈnɪfɔːmd / (BrE)
not wearing a uniform, especially when a uniform is expected or required

Examples

Ununiformed staff
Only ununiformed visitors were asked to wait outside the restricted area.
The ununiformed employees attended the meeting in casual clothes.
Security quickly noticed the ununiformed person entering the staff-only section.
