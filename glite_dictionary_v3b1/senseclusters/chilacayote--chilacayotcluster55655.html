chilacayote

noun
candied squash dessert
Pronunciation: / tʃilaˈkaɪoʊteɪ / (AmE), / tʃɪlakaɪˈəʊteɪ / (BrE)
a sweet preserve or candied dessert made from the flesh of the chilacayote squash, often glazed with sugar and spices

Examples

Sweet chilacayote dessert
For dessert they offered thick slices of chilacayote glazed with sugar and cinnamon.
My grandmother taught me how to make traditional chilacayote for family celebrations.
The market stall sold jars of homemade chilacayote alongside other sweet preserves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candied fruit - fruit preserved or glazed with sugar
  • synonym: marmalade - a sweet preserve made from citrus fruit
