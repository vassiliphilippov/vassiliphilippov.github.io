gonna help

verb
informal: going to help
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑnə hɛlp / (AmE), / ˈɡɒnə hɛlp / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone intends to help in the future; informal contraction of 'going to help'

Examples

To gonna help someone
Are you gonna help me with my homework or not?
He's gonna help us move into our new apartment this weekend.
She promised she was gonna help organize the event.
