hamburger steak

noun
ground beef patty served as a dish
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmˌbɝɡɚ steɪk / (AmE), / ˈhæmˌbɜːɡə steɪk / (BrE)
a dish consisting of a patty made from ground beef, typically cooked by frying or grilling and served without a bread bun

Examples

A hamburger steak dinner
For dinner, she made a hamburger steak instead of the usual burgers with buns.
I ordered a hamburger steak with mashed potatoes and gravy at the diner.
The restaurant's signature dish is a grilled hamburger steak served with onions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Salisbury steak - a similar dish made from ground beef and other ingredients, often served with gravy
