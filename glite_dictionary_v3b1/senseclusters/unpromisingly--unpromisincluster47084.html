unpromisingly

adverb
without hope of success
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɑːmɪsɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɒmɪsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not give hope of success or improvement

Examples

To start unpromisingly
The negotiations started unpromisingly, but both sides eventually reached an agreement.
Although the weather looked unpromisingly gray, the sun came out later in the day.
He began his new job unpromisingly, making several mistakes in the first week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopelessly - in a way that shows no hope
  • antonym: promisingly - in a way that gives hope of success
