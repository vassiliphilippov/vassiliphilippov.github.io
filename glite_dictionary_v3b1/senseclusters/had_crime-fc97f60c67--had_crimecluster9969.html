Had crime

noun
serious crime under Islamic law
Pronunciation: / hɑd kraɪm / (AmE), / hɑːd kraɪm / (BrE)
In Islamic law, a serious crime punishable by mandatory penalties prescribed in the Qur'an, such as theft, adultery, apostasy, and murder.

Examples

Had crime in Sharia
Apostasy is considered a Had crime in some interpretations of Sharia law.
The judge explained that the punishment for a Had crime is outlined in the Qur'an.
Under Islamic law, a Had crime such as theft carries a predetermined penalty.
