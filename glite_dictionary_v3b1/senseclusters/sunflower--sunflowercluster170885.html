sunflower

noun
tall plant with large yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A tall plant of the genus Helianthus with large golden-yellow flower heads and edible seeds.

Examples

A sunflower plant
A field of sunflowers stretched as far as the eye could see.
She planted a sunflower in her garden to attract birds and bees.
The sunflower turned its head to follow the bright afternoon sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daisy - a type of plant with a similar flower structure
