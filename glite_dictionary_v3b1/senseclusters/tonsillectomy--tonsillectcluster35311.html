tonsillectomy

noun
surgical removal of the tonsils
7.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /tɑːn.sɪl.ˈek.tə.miː/ (AmE), /tɒn.sɪl.ˈek.tə.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove the palatine tonsils, usually performed to treat recurrent infection or airway obstruction

Examples

A tonsillectomy operation
My son had a tonsillectomy last year after suffering from repeated throat infections.
Recovery from a tonsillectomy can take about a week for most children.
The doctor recommended a tonsillectomy due to chronic tonsillitis.
