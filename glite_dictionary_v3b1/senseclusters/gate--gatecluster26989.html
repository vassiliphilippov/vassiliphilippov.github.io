gate

noun
airport boarding/disembarking passage
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪt / (AmE), / ɡeɪt / (BrE)
a passage or area in an airport terminal where passengers board or leave an aircraft

Examples

Airport gate
After landing, passengers exited the plane through the jet bridge at the gate.
The flight to Paris will depart from gate B15 at 9:30.
We waited near the gate for our boarding group to be called.

Related Terms

  • synonym: door - a hinged barrier used to close an entrance to a room, building, or vehicle
