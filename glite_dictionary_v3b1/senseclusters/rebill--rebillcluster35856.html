rebill

noun
corrected invoice
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbɪl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbɪl / (BrE)
a revised or replacement invoice sent to correct or update a previous bill

Examples

A rebill notice
After noticing a mistake in the original bill, the company sent a rebill to the customer.
The rebill included the correct charges for the additional services provided last month.
If you receive a rebill, check it carefully to ensure all corrections are accurate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: credit note - a document issued to correct an invoice by reducing the amount owed
  • synonym: invoice - a document requesting payment for goods or services
