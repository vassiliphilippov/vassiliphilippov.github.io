lawbreaking

noun
illegal activity
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of violating or not obeying the law

Examples

A lawbreaking incident
The city has seen a rise in lawbreaking over the past year.
Strict penalties are meant to discourage lawbreaking among young people.
Repeated lawbreaking can lead to serious legal consequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crime - an act that breaks the law
  • antonym: law-abiding - obeying the law
  • synonym: offense - a breach of a law or rule
