Neosho

proper noun
river in Kansas and Oklahoma, USA
Pronunciation: / niˈoʊʃoʊ / (AmE), / niˈəʊʃəʊ / (BrE)
a river that rises in eastern Kansas and flows eastward into Oklahoma, where it becomes a tributary of the Arkansas River

Examples

The Neosho River
Several towns are located along the Neosho as it flows into Oklahoma.
The Neosho River provides water resources for several Kansas communities.
The flooding of the Neosho has been an issue in recent years.
