weta

noun
New Zealand insect
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛtə / (AmE), / ˈwiːtə / (BrE)
a large, cricket-like insect native to New Zealand, known for its heavy body and long legs

Examples

The giant weta
The giant weta is considered one of the heaviest insects in the world.
Scientists traveled to New Zealand to study the rare weta in its natural habitat.
A weta crawled across the forest floor as we hiked through the reserve.

Related Terms
