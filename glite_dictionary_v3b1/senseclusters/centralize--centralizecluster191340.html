centralize

verb
bring under central control
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəlaɪz / (BrE)
bring (activities, power, or organization) under a single central authority or location.

Examples

To centralize power
After the merger, the company centralized all administrative functions in New York.
Efforts to centralize data collection have met with both support and resistance.
The government decided to centralize health services to improve efficiency.
He argued that it was unwise to centralise all decision-making power in one office.
Many governments have sought to centralise healthcare systems for better regulation.
The company decided to centralise its operations to improve efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decentralise - to distribute (activities, power, or organization) away from a central authority
  • antonym: decentralize - distribute power or functions away from a central authority
