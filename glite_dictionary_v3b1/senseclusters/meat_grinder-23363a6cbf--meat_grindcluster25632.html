meat grinder

noun
situation causing major harm or destruction
Pronunciation: / ˈmit ˌɡraɪndər / (AmE), / ˈmiːt ˌɡraɪndə / (BrE)
A situation or process that causes severe destruction, injury, or suffering, especially in a relentless or indiscriminate way.

Examples

War meat grinder
Many soldiers were lost in the meat grinder of trench warfare during World War I.
Surviving the corporate meat grinder can take a significant toll on your health and spirit.
The lawsuit became a legal meat grinder, dragging on for years and exhausting everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slaughterhouse - a place where animals are killed for meat; metaphorically, a scene of mass killing or suffering
