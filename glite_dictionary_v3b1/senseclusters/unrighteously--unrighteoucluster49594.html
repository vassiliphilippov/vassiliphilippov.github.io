unrighteously

adverb
in a morally wrong or unjust manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraɪtʃəsli / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪtʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is morally wrong or unjust.

Examples

Act unrighteously
He felt guilty for having acted unrighteously toward his friends.
The politician was criticized for behaving unrighteously during the campaign.
Throughout history, people have suffered because their leaders ruled unrighteously.

Related Terms

  • antonym: righteously - in a morally right or justifiable manner
