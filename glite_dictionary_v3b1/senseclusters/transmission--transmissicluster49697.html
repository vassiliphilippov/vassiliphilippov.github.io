transmission

noun
the act of sending something, especially a message
Pronunciation: / trænˈsmɪʃən / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of sending, passing on, or conveying something, especially a message or information, from one place or person to another

Examples

Radio transmission signal
Radio transmission was interrupted due to severe weather conditions.
Rapid transmission of news helped people prepare for the approaching storm.
The transmission of confidential information over email must be done carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reception - the act of receiving something sent or communicated
  • synonym: transfer - the act of moving something from one place to another
