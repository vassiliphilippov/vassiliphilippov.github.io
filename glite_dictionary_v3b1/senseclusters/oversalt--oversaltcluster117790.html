oversalt

verb
to add too much salt
3.7
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsɑːlt/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈsɔːlt/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsɒlt/ (BrE)
to add too much salt to food, making it taste excessively salty

Examples

To oversalt the soup
If you oversalt the stew, it can be difficult to fix the flavor.
She accidentally oversalted the pasta water, making the noodles too salty.
Be sure not to oversalt your food when cooking for guests.
