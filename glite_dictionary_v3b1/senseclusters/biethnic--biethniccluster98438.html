biethnic

adjective
involving two ethnicities
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɛθnɪk / (AmE), / baɪˈɛθnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving two different ethnic groups

Examples

Biethnic communities
The research examined biethnic communities and how they maintain their cultural traditions.
She grew up in a biethnic household, celebrating customs from both her parents' backgrounds.
The school organized a biethnic festival to honor the two largest cultural groups in the area.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: monoethnic - involving only one ethnic group
  • degree higher: multiethnic - involving several or many ethnic groups
