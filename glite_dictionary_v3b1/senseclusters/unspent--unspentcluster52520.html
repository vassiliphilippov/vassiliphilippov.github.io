unspent

adjective
not used or spent (esp. of money)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɛnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɛnt / (BrE)
(of money or resources) not yet used or spent.

Examples

The unspent money
After the project was completed, there was a significant amount of money still unspent in the budget.
He was surprised by how much of his allowance remained unspent at the end of the month.
The charity reported several million dollars in unspent funds after the campaign.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spent - already used or consumed, especially of resources
