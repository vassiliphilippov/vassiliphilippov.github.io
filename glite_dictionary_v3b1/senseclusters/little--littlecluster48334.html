Synonyms

  • slightly
    1.0 87/m
    slightly describes a small change or amount in formal or neutral speech, while little can mean almost none or very small.
  • a bit
    1.0 144/m
    a bit is an informal way to say a small amount or degree in speech, while little can sound stricter or weaker.
  • somewhat
    2.0 67/m
    somewhat means to a small or moderate degree and appears in formal speech, while little often implies a smaller, closer to none amount.
  • scarcely
