keratoderma blennorrhagica

noun
scaly rash on palms/soles; seen in reactive arthritis
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrətoʊˌdɜrmə blɛnəˈrædʒɪkə / (AmE), / ˌkɛrətəʊˌdɜːmə blɛnəˈrædʒɪkə / (BrE)
A rare skin condition marked by scaly rashes on the palms and soles, commonly seen in association with reactive arthritis (Reiter's syndrome).

Examples

Keratoderma blennorrhagica rash
Doctors noted characteristic lesions of keratoderma blennorrhagica during the physical examination.
The patient developed keratoderma blennorrhagica on both feet following a recent episode of reactive arthritis.
Treatment for keratoderma blennorrhagica often focuses on managing the underlying arthritis.
