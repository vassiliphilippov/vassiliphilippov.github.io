Leuctra

proper noun
site of major ancient Greek battle
Pronunciation: / ˈluːk.trə / (AmE), / ˈljuːk.trə / (BrE)
the site in ancient Greece where, in 371 BC, Thebes defeated Sparta, ending Sparta's military dominance

Examples

Battle of Leuctra
At Leuctra, the Theban army defeated the Spartans using innovative tactics.
Historians often highlight Leuctra as the event that ended Spartan dominance.
The Battle of Leuctra marked a turning point in Greek history.
