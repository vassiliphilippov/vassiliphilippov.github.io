company

noun
group of people gathered for an activity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a group of people who are temporarily gathered or associated for a particular activity or purpose

Examples

A company of hikers
A company of hikers gathered at the trailhead before sunrise.
She joined a company of volunteers cleaning up the beach for the day.
The company of musicians moved from one street corner to another, playing lively tunes.
