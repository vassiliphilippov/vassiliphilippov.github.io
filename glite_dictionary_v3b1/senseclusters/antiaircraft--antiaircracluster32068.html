antiaircraft

noun
weapons against aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɛrkræft / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈeəkraːft / (BrE)
artillery or weapons designed to attack or defend against aircraft in flight

Examples

Antiaircraft gun fire
During the war, antiaircraft fire filled the sky as bombers approached the city.
Modern antiaircraft systems use radar to track and destroy hostile aircraft.
The ship was equipped with powerful antiaircraft guns to defend against enemy planes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-aircraft gun - a gun designed to shoot at aircraft
