hogger

noun
greedy person; train engineer
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡər / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡə / (BrE)
a person who takes more than their share, or in US railroading, a locomotive engineer

Examples

Greedy hogger
Don't be a hogger—let everyone have a slice of cake.
The experienced hogger drove the freight train across the country.
At the buffet, he was called a hogger for piling food on his plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - a person who operates a train
  • synonym: hog - to take more than one's share
  • antonym: sharer - a person who divides things fairly with others
