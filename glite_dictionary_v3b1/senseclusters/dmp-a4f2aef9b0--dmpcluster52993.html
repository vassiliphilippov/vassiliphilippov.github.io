DMP

noun
data management platform
Pronunciation: / diː ɛm piː / (AmE), / diː ɛm piː / (BrE)
a software platform used to collect, organize, and manage large sets of data, especially for marketing or advertising purposes

Examples

Marketing DMP platform
The marketing team relies on a DMP to segment and target their audience more effectively.
By integrating a DMP with other tools, companies can improve their data-driven strategies.
A DMP helps businesses collect and analyze information from multiple sources in one place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CRM - a system for managing a company's interactions with customers
  • synonym: database - an organized collection of data stored electronically
