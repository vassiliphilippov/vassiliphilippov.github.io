unactivated

adjective
not made reactive
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈæktɪˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈæktɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been made reactive or not containing activating groups, especially in a chemical context.

Examples

Unactivated compound
The unactivated compound did not participate in the reaction as expected.
Researchers found that unactivated substrates require harsher conditions to react.
Because the molecule was unactivated, it remained stable under normal laboratory conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: activated - made active or reactive, especially in chemistry
  • synonym: inactive - not active or not participating in a process
