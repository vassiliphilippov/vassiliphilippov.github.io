beastman

noun
cruel or savage person
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːstmæn / (AmE), / ˈbiːstmən / (BrE)
a cruel, violent, or savage person who behaves like an animal, often used as an insult

Examples

A violent beastman
The angry mob called the criminal a beastman after witnessing his violent behavior.
In the story, the villain was described as a beastman feared by everyone in the village.
No one wanted to befriend the beastman because of his cruel and savage actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brute - a person who is cruel, violent, or lacking in reason
  • antonym: gentleman - a man who is polite and behaves well
  • synonym: monster - a person who is extremely cruel or wicked
