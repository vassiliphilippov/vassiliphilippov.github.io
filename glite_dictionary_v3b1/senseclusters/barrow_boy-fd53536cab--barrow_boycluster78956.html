barrow boy

noun
young street vendor with a cart
Pronunciation: / ˈbæroʊ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbærəʊ bɔɪ / (BrE)
A young person or street vendor who sells goods from a handcart, especially in a market setting.

Examples

A barrow boy sells goods
His grandfather was a barrow boy on the busy city streets.
Many successful businessmen started out as a barrow boy in the local market.
She bought apples from a cheerful barrow boy every Saturday.
