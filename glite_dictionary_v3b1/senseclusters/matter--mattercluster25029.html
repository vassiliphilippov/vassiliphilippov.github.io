matter

noun
subject or situation under consideration
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmætər /; / ˈmæt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈmatə /; / ˈmætə / (BrE)
a subject, topic, or situation that is being considered, discussed, or dealt with

Examples

Matter of concern

This is a private matter and should be handled discreetly.
It's a private matter and doesn't concern anyone else.
She tried to change the subject whenever the matter of money arose.
We have an urgent matter to discuss at tomorrow's meeting.
