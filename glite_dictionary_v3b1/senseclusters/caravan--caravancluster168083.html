Examples

A holiday caravan

Synonyms

  • camper trailer
    3.4 0.02/m

  • trailer
    2.6 12/m
    trailer is a wheeled vehicle pulled by a car and sometimes fitted for people to sleep in, while caravan is used for holiday travel.
  • house trailer
    2.8 0.13/m
    house trailer is a vehicle meant for living in long-term, often as a mobile home, while caravan is smaller and used for short holidays.
