caravan

noun
vehicle for living/traveling, typically towed
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌvæn / (AmE), / ˈkærəvan / (BrE)
(British) A vehicle equipped for living in, typically towed by a car and used for holidays or travel; a camper with living quarters.

Examples

A holiday caravan
She decorated the inside of the caravan with colorful cushions and fairy lights.
The family bought a new caravan to take on their summer trips.
They spent their holidays touring the countryside in a caravan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camper - motor vehicle or trailer equipped with living space
  • synonym: motorhome - motor vehicle equipped as a self-contained living area
