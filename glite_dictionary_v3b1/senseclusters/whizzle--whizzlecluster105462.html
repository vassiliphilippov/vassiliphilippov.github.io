whizzle

verb
to make a whizzing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪzəl / (BrE)
to make a whizzing or hissing sound, especially while moving quickly through the air

Examples

To whizzle past
The arrow began to whizzle through the air as it left the bow.
We heard the bullet whizzle past our heads before it struck the tree.
A stone whizzled across the frozen lake, making a sharp, high-pitched sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiss - to make a sharp sibilant sound
  • synonym: whiz - to move quickly with a buzzing or hissing sound
