dunno

interjection
informal uncertainty
9.0
Pronunciation: / dəˈnoʊ / (AmE), / dəˈnəʊ / (BrE)
an informal way to say that you do not know or are unsure about something

Examples

She replied dunno
When asked about the test results, she just replied, "dunno."
"Dunno if I'll make it to the party tonight," he said with a shrug.
I dunno what time the movie starts, so let's check online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certain - sure or confident about something
  • synonym: don't know - to not have the answer or information
  • synonym: unsure - not certain or confident about something
