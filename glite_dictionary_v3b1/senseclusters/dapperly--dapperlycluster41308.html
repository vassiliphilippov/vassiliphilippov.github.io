dapperly

adverb
in a stylish way
Pronunciation: / ˈdæpərli / (AmE), / ˈdæpəli / (BrE)
in a neat, stylish, or smart manner, especially in appearance or dress

Examples

To dress dapperly
He always arrives dapperly dressed, no matter the occasion.
The guests admired how dapperly she presented herself at the gala.
Even on casual Fridays, he manages to look dapperly put together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shabbily - in a worn or untidy way
  • synonym: smartly - in a neat and fashionable way
