unbathed

adjective
not recently washed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbeɪðd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbeɪðd / (BrE)
not having washed or bathed recently; dirty from lack of bathing

Examples

Unbathed for days
After a week in the wilderness, they returned home unbathed and exhausted.
The unbathed dog left muddy paw prints all over the kitchen floor.
She felt self-conscious about being unbathed after the long journey.
