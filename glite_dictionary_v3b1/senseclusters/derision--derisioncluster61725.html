Synonyms

    mockery is making fun of someone by copying or joking about them, while derision is broader scornful ridicule.
  • ridicule
    4.8 4/m
    ridicule means making someone the object of jokes or scorn, while derision is similar scornful treatment.
  • belittlement
    7.1 0.05/m
    belittlement is making someone or their work seem small or unimportant, while derision adds mocking contempt.
  • disparagement
    8.1 0.4/m
    disparagement is speaking badly to show lack of respect or value, while derision focuses on mocking with contempt.
  • denigration
    8.4 0.6/m
    denigration is unfairly criticizing to damage reputation, while derision is open mocking meant to show contempt.
  • derogation
    8.6 0.08/m
    derogation is a formal act or remark that belittles someone, while derision is more everyday mocking and scorn.
  • revilement
    9.3 0.01/m
