Tosk dialect

noun
dialect of Albanian spoken in southern Albania
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɒsk ˈdaɪəˌlɛkt / (AmE), / tɒsk ˈdaɪəlekt / (BrE)
the dialect of Albanian spoken mainly in southern Albania and certain regions of Greece and Italy

Examples

Tosk dialect of Albanian
Linguists study differences between the Tosk dialect and the Gheg dialect of Albanian.
Many immigrants carried the Tosk dialect with them to new communities in Italy.
The official standard of Albanian is largely based on the Tosk dialect.
