wavelet

noun
a small wave on a liquid surface
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvlət / (AmE), / ˈweɪvlət / (BrE)
a small wave, especially one that appears on the surface of a liquid

Examples

A tiny wavelet
A single stone sent wavelets spreading outwards in perfect circles.
In the moonlight, wavelets shimmered on the lake's surface.
Tiny wavelets rippled across the pond as the wind picked up.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: wave - a moving ridge or swell on the surface of a liquid
