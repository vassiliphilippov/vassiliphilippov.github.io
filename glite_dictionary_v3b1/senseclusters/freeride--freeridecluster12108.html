freeride

noun
trick-focused riding style
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌraɪd / (BrE)
a style of riding, especially on a bike or snowboard, that focuses on freedom, tricks, and creativity rather than racing or strict rules

Examples

A freeride session
He loves the excitement of a challenging freeride through the forest trails.
The competition featured impressive freeride performances on both snowboards and mountain bikes.
Many athletes prefer freeride because it allows them to express their own style and creativity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downhill - a style of riding focused on speed and descending slopes
  • synonym: freestyle - a style of sport emphasizing tricks and creativity
