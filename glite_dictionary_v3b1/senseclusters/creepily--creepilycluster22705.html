creepily

adverb
in a scary way
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːpɪli / (AmE), / ˈkriːpɪli / (BrE)
in a way that causes fear or makes someone feel uneasy or uncomfortable

Examples

To creepily move
The wind howled creepily through the empty streets after midnight.
He smiled creepily at the children, making them want to leave the room.
The dolls in the attic stared creepily from the shadows, unsettling everyone who entered.
The old house looked creepily quiet as the sun set behind it.
He smiled creepily at the children, making them feel uncomfortable.
The cat moved creepily through the shadows, barely making a sound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortably - in a way that makes someone feel relaxed and at ease
  • synonym: eerily - in a strange and frightening way
  • antonym: openly - in a way that is not secretive or hidden
  • synonym: stealthily - in a secretive or sneaky manner
