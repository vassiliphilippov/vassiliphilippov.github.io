peaberry

adjective
containing peaberry beans
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌbɛri / (BrE)
made from or containing peaberry coffee beans, which are single, rounded beans found in some coffee cherries

Examples

Peaberry coffee blend
She prefers peaberry coffee for its unique flavor and smoothness.
The roaster specializes in peaberry blends sourced from East Africa.
A cup of peaberry espresso often has a brighter, more delicate taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: single-origin - made from coffee beans from one specific location
