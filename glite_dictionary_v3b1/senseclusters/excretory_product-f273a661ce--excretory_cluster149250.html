excretory product

noun
waste substance discharged from the body
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskriːtəˌri ˈprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / ɪkˈskriːtəri ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
a waste substance (such as urine, sweat, or especially feces) that is discharged from the body

Examples

The excretory product sweat
Sweat is an excretory product that helps regulate body temperature.
The buildup of any excretory product in the body can lead to health problems.
The kidneys remove urea, an excretory product, from the blood.
