scholarly

adjective
relating to scholars or academic study
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑː.lɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈskɒl.ə.li / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of scholars or academic study; showing knowledge, learning, or academic rigor.

Examples

Scholarly article
He is known for his scholarly approach to literary criticism.
Her scholarly work has been widely cited in academic journals.
The debate took on a scholarly tone, with participants referencing historical texts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unacademic - not scholarly or academic
