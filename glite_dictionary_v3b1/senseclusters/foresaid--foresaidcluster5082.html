foresaid

noun
previously mentioned person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌsɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌsɛd / (BrE)
a person or thing that has been mentioned earlier in the same document or statement, especially in legal or formal contexts

Examples

The foresaid person
The foresaid shall be liable for all costs incurred during the process.
According to the contract, the foresaid must provide written notice before vacating the premises.
The judge referred to the foresaid in her final statement to clarify the parties involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aforementioned - mentioned previously in the same context
  • synonym: latter - the second of two things mentioned
