cater

verb
provide food for events
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪtɚ /; / ˈkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
to provide and serve food and drinks for an event or group, often as a professional service

Examples

To cater a party
She has decided to cater private parties on weekends.
The company will cater the wedding for 150 guests.
They often cater corporate events and business meetings.
The restaurant can cater for large parties and corporate events.
They hired a company to cater their wedding reception.
We always try to cater to the needs of our customers.
If you want us to cater your event, please contact us two weeks in advance.
Many families choose to cater big celebrations rather than cook themselves.
The hotel will cater the conference with breakfast and lunch each day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provide - to supply something that is needed or wanted
  • synonym: serve - to bring food or drink to people
  • synonym: supply - to make something available to someone as needed
