remodel

verb
alter the structure of a building
1.8 3.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈmɑː.dəl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈmɑː.dəl/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈmɒ.dəl/ /; / /riː.ˈmɒ.dəl/ (BrE)
to change the structure, shape, or appearance of something

Examples

To remodel a house
The company spent millions to remodel its headquarters after the merger.
