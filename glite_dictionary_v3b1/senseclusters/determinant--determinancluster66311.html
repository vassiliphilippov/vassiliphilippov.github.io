determinant

adjective
deciding or determining factor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɝː.mə.nənt / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜː.mɪ.nənt / (BrE)
(formal) having the power or quality to decide something; serving to determine the final outcome or result.

Examples

Determinant role
A determinant issue in the case was the reliability of the witness.
Her determinant role in the negotiations secured the final agreement.
The determinant factor in the election was the turnout of young voters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decisive - having the power to decide or determine an outcome
  • synonym: determinative - having the power to determine or decide; decisive
