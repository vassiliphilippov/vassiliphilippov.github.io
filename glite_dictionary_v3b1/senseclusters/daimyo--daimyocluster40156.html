daimyo

noun
Japanese feudal lord
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪmjoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪmjəʊ / (BrE)
a powerful feudal lord in pre-modern Japan who controlled large areas of land and had significant political and military influence
