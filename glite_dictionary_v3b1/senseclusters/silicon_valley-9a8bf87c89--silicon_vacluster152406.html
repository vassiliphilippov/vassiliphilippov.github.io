Silicon Valley

proper noun
high-tech region in California
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪkən ˈvæli /; / ˌsɪlɪkən ˈvæli / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪkən ˈvæli /; / ˌsɪlɪkən ˈvæli / (BrE)
