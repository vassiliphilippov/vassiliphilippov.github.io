Atlantic manta

noun
large manta ray species found worldwide
7.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈlæn.tɪk ˈmæn.tə/ /; / /ˈæt.læn.tɪk ˈmæn.tə/ (AmE), /ə.ˈlæn.tɪk ˈmæn.tə/ /; / /ˈæt.læn.tɪk ˈmæn.tə/ (BrE)
a large species of manta ray found in warm oceanic waters worldwide, especially common in the Gulf of Mexico and along the southern United States; noted for its vast wingspan, reaching up to 22 feet
