ill-scented

adjective
having an unpleasant smell
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪl ˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ɪl ˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having an unpleasant or offensive smell; malodorous.

Examples

An ill-scented breeze

She wrinkled her nose at the ill-scented garbage bin.
The pond became ill-scented during the hot summer months.
