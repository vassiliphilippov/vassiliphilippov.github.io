contrarious

adjective
tending to oppose or contradict
Pronunciation: / kənˈtreəriəs / (AmE), / kənˈtreəriəs / (BrE)
tending to oppose or contradict; marked by willful opposition or perversity

Examples

Contrarious attitude
Her contrarious attitude made every group decision an uphill battle.
His contrarious nature often led him to take unpopular stances merely for the sake of opposition.
The child grew even more contrarious whenever he was told what to do.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrary - opposed in nature, direction, or meaning
  • synonym: obstinate - stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or action
