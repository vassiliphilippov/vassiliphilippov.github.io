count

noun
total number counted
1.0 35/m
Pronunciation: / kaÊŠnt / (AmE), / kaÊŠnt / (BrE)
the act of counting or the total number of things or people that have been counted

Examples

A blood count
The doctor ordered a blood count to check for infection.
The official count of votes showed a clear winner.
