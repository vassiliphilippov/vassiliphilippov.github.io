count

noun
total number counted
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt / (AmE), / kaʊnt / (BrE)
the act of counting or the total number of things or people that have been counted

Examples

A blood count
The doctor ordered a blood count to check for infection.
The official count of votes showed a clear winner.
We took a quick count of the students present before leaving.
After the ballots were collected, the count revealed a clear winner.
The official count took place behind closed doors.
The teacher asked the children to start a count from one to ten.

  • synonym: total - the whole amount or sum of something
