brassed

verb
past of brass (cover with brass)
9.1
Pronunciation: / bræst / (AmE), / brɑːst / (BrE)
past tense and past participle of 'brass': to cover or plate something with brass or add brass fittings

Examples

They brassed the handles
They brassed the cabinet handles to match the antique hinges before selling it.
The craftsman brassed the lamp to give it a shiny, golden appearance.
After they brassed the old doorknobs, the house looked much more elegant.
