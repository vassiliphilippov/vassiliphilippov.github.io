arb

noun
someone who practices arbitrage in finance
9.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / …ërb / (AmE), / …ëÀêb / (BrE)
a person who engages in arbitrage, buying and selling securities in different markets to profit from price differences

Examples

A skilled arb
Many hedge funds employ at least one experienced arb on their trading desk.
The arb made a quick profit by exploiting price gaps between stock exchanges.
With his sharp eye for opportunities, he soon became known as the best arb in the city.

Synonyms

  • arbitrageur
    9.9 0.09/m
    arbitrageur trades the same asset across markets to earn small, quick profits, while arb is an informal short form for a person who does arbitrage.
  • arbitrager
    8.8 0.06/m
    arbitrager makes quick cross-market trades to gain small price differences, whereas arb is a less common or variant term for that trader.
