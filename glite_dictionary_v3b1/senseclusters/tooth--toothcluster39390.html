tooth

noun
hard structure in the jaw for biting and chewing
2.3
Pronunciation: / tuːθ / (AmE), / tuːθ / (BrE)
Each of the hard, white structures in the mouth of people and animals, used for biting and chewing food.

Examples

A tooth falls out
A shark can lose a tooth and grow a new one in its place.
She lost her first baby tooth when she was six years old.
The dentist examined each tooth for signs of decay.
