striker

noun
part of a device that strikes
6.9 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkə / (BrE)
the part of a mechanical device that delivers a blow to make something happen, such as firing a gun or ringing a bell

Examples

Striker of a gun
If the striker of the gun malfunctions, it may not fire correctly.
The striker in the clock hit the bell precisely at noon.
Watchmakers must carefully fit the striker to ensure the chime rings at the right time.
