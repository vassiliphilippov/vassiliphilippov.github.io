decorate

verb
to add decorations or make attractive
1.0 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈde.kə.reɪt/ (AmE), /ˈde.kə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to make something look more attractive or beautiful by adding ornaments, color, or other decorative elements

Examples

To decorate a room
Every year, we decorate the office for the holidays.
She loves to decorate her cakes with fresh flowers and colorful sprinkles.
They spent the afternoon decorating their new apartment with paintings and plants.
He asked his friends to help decorate the gymnasium for the school dance.
Many people decorate their homes with lights during the holiday season.
She spent all morning trying to decorate the living room for her daughter's birthday party.
Fresh flowers decorate the lobby of the hotel every morning.
She loves to decorate her home with colorful artwork and unique pieces.
The students decorated the classroom walls with their drawings before the big celebration.
