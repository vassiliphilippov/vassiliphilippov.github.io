pan

noun
shallow metal vessel for cooking
Pronunciation: / pæn / (AmE), / pæn / (BrE)
a shallow, wide container, usually metal, used for cooking food or holding liquids

Examples

A frying pan
After dinner, he washed the dirty pan and put it away.
She fried the eggs in a pan on the stove.
The recipe calls for a nonstick pan to prevent sticking.
After breakfast, he washed all the dirty pans.
She heated the soup in a large pan on the stove.
The recipe says to use a shallow pan for frying the vegetables.
