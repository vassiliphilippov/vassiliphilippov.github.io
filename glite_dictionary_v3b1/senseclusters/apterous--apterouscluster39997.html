apterous

adjective
wingless (especially of insects)
Pronunciation: / ˈæptəɹəs / (AmE), / ˈæpt(ə)ɹəs / (BrE)
(of insects) lacking wings.

Examples

Apterous insects
Some ants have apterous workers while their queens possess wings.
The collection included both winged and apterous beetles for comparison.
The scientist identified the species as apterous due to its lack of visible wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wingless - lacking wings
