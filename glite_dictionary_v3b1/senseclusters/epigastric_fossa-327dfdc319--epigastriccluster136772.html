epigastric fossa

noun
shallow depression below the sternum
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk ˈfɑsə / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk ˈfɒsə / (BrE)
a slight depression in the midline of the upper abdomen just below the sternum, where a blow can affect the solar plexus

Examples

The epigastric fossa area
A sharp blow to the epigastric fossa can temporarily wind a person.
Pain in the epigastric fossa may be a sign of digestive issues.
The doctor pressed lightly on the epigastric fossa to check for tenderness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epigastrium - the upper central region of the abdomen
  • synonym: solar plexus - a complex of nerves located in the epigastric region, affected by blows to this area
