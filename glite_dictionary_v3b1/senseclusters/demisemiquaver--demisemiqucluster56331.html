demisemiquaver

noun
thirty-second musical note
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛm.iˈsɛm.iˌkweɪ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˌdɛm.iˈsɛm.iˌkweɪ.və / (BrE)
a musical note with the time value of one thirty-second of a whole note; also called a thirty-second note

In British notation, a demisemiquaver lasts for one-thirty-second the duration of a whole note.
