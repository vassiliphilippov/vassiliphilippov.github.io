keep an eye on

verb
watch or monitor closely
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /kiːp ən aɪ ɑːn/ /; / /kiːp ən aɪ ɔːn/ (AmE), /kiːp ən aɪ ɒn/ (BrE)
