flamer

noun
slang for flamboyant person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmər / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmə / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who behaves in a flamboyant, provocative, or exaggerated way, sometimes used offensively.

Examples

A real flamer
Some people called him a flamer because of his bold fashion choices and dramatic gestures.
The online community does not tolerate users who insult others by calling them a flamer.
She disliked being labeled a flamer just for expressing herself openly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: show-off - a person who tries to attract attention by behaving flamboyantly
  • antonym: wallflower - a shy person who does not draw attention
