breakbone

noun
bone-breaker
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌbəʊn / (BrE)
a person or thing that breaks bones, or is known for causing bone-breaking injuries, often used figuratively or in older texts

Examples

A breakbone fighter
