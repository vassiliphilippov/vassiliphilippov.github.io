Taoist

adjective
relating to Taoism or its philosophy
6.3 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊɪst /; / ˈtaʊ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈtaʊɪst /; / ˈtaʊ.ɪst / (BrE)
relating to Taoism, a Chinese philosophy and religion that teaches living simply and in harmony with nature

Examples

Taoist philosophy
He attends a Taoist meditation retreat every spring.

Many Taoist rituals involve music, incense, and ornate costumes.
She studied Taoist philosophy during her stay in China.
The temple is decorated in traditional Taoist style.
