cricket ball

noun
ball used in the game of cricket
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪkɪt bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪt bɔːl / (BrE)
A hard, solid ball used in the game of cricket, traditionally made of cork and leather.

Examples

A cricket ball
It's important for a batsman to watch the cricket ball closely after it's bowled.
She bought a new cricket ball for her brother's practice session.
The bowler polished the cricket ball before his over began.
