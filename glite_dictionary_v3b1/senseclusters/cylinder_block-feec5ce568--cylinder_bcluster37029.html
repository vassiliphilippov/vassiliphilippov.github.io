cylinder block

noun
main casing for engine cylinders
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪndər blɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪndə blɒk / (BrE)
the solid metal casting in an internal combustion engine that contains the cylinders and cooling ducts

Examples

The cylinder block metal
High temperatures can cause the cylinder block to warp or fail.
The mechanic discovered a crack in the cylinder block during the inspection.
We had to order a new cylinder block to rebuild the engine properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engine block - the main part of an engine containing the cylinders
