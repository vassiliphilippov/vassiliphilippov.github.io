unchivalric

adjective
lacking courtesy or honor
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnʃɪˈvælrɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnʃɪˈvælrɪk / (BrE)
not showing the honor, courtesy, or bravery expected of a knight or a gentleman

Examples

An unchivalric act
It was considered unchivalric to leave the elderly man standing while others sat.
His unchivalric behavior disappointed everyone at the formal dinner.
Ignoring someone in need is often seen as unchivalric in many cultures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chivalrous - showing honor, courtesy, and bravery, especially toward women
  • synonym: discourteous - rude or lacking in politeness
