Gutierrezia microcephala

noun
a species of North American flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˌɡuːtiəˈrɛziə ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛfələ / (AmE), / ˌɡjuːtɪəˈrɛzɪə ˌmɪkrəʊˈsɛfələ / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant in the daisy family native to North America, similar to Gutierrezia sarothrae but distinguished by smaller flower heads with fewer ray and disk flowers.

Examples

Gutierrezia microcephala plant species
Gutierrezia microcephala thrives in arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
The botanist collected specimens of Gutierrezia microcephala for further study in the laboratory.
Unlike its relative, Gutierrezia microcephala has smaller flower heads with fewer rays.
