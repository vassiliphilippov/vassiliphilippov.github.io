maid

noun
female domestic servant
1.0 11/m
Pronunciation: / meÉªd / (AmE), / meÉªd / (BrE)
a female servant whose job is to clean, cook, or otherwise take care of housework in someone else's home

Examples

Hotel maid
She worked as a maid in a hotel during the summer holidays.
The old mansion has several maids who help with daily chores.
The wealthy family hired a new maid to keep the house clean.

Synonyms

    7.1 0.33/m
    maidservant is an older, more formal word for a female domestic worker, while maid is the common everyday term.
  • housemaid
    2.4 0.39/m
    housemaid is a female worker who cleans and cares for a home, while maid often refers to any female household servant.
  • domestic help
    2.6 0.14/m
    domestic help is a paid person who helps with household chores, while maid usually means a resident female house servant.
  • domestic
    5.8 0.52/m

  • housekeeper
    1.1 4.21/m

  • house servant
    2.3 0.06/m
