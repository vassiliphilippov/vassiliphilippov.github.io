road surface

noun
top layer of a paved road
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /roʊd ˈsɜːr.fəs/ /; / /roʊd ˈsɜːr.fɪs/ (AmE), /rəʊd ˈsɜː.fɪs/ /; / /rəʊd ˈsɜː.fəs/ (BrE)
the top layer of a road, usually paved, on which vehicles travel

Examples

A smooth road surface
A smooth road surface helps reduce vehicle wear and improves safety for drivers.
The city repaired the road surface after last winter’s harsh weather caused several potholes.
They applied a new layer of asphalt to the old road surface to extend its lifespan.
