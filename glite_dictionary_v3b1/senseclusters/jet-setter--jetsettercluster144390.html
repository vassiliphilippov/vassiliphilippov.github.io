jet-setter

noun
a wealthy frequent traveler
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛtˌsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛtˌsɛtə / (BrE)
A wealthy or fashionable person who travels frequently, especially to glamorous or exotic destinations.

Examples

A jet-setter lifestyle
Despite appearing to be a jet-setter, he actually preferred quiet weekends at home.
She lived the life of a jet-setter, flying from Paris to New York and Tokyo in a single week.
The magazine featured interviews with several jet-setters who frequented luxury resorts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society and is frequently seen at social events
