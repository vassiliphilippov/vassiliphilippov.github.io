signal caller

noun
(football) the team's directing player, usually quarterback
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nəl ˌkɔ.lɚ /; / ˈsɪɡ.nəl ˌkɔl.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nəl ˌkɔː.lə / (BrE)
in American football, the player (usually the quarterback) who directs the team's offense by calling out plays or instructions
