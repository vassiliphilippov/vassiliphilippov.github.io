strong suit

noun
a suit with many high cards (cards)
5.6
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ suːt / (AmE), / strɒŋ suːt / (BrE)
A suit in a card game in which a player holds an unusually large number of high cards.

Examples

Strong suit in cards
Clubs turned out to be her strong suit, giving her an advantage in the game.
He bid hearts because it was his strong suit in the hand.
If your strong suit is spades, consider leading with one in your first trick.

Related Terms

  • antonym: weak suit - a suit in a hand with few or no high cards (cards)
