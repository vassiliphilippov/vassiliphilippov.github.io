thermoform

noun
plastic forming machine
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːrmoʊˌfɔːrm / (AmE), / ˈθɜːməʊˌfɔːm / (BrE)
a machine or device that heats and shapes plastic sheets into specific forms or products

Examples

Thermoform machine
The technician loaded plastic sheets into the thermoform to create custom packaging trays.
A new thermoform allowed the company to produce food containers more efficiently.
Operators must monitor the thermoform closely to ensure the plastic is heated evenly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: injection molder - a machine that shapes plastic by injecting it into a mold
  • synonym: vacuum former - a machine that shapes plastic using heat and vacuum
