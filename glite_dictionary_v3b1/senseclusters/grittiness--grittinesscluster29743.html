grittiness

noun
raw or harsh quality
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪtinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being raw, harsh, or unpolished, often giving a realistic or tough impression

Examples

Grittiness of the story
The grittiness of the novel made the characters' struggles feel authentic and powerful.
Some viewers were surprised by the grittiness of the documentary's portrayal of city life.
The artist used dark colors to add a sense of grittiness to the painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harshness - the quality of being unpleasantly rough or severe
  • synonym: realism - the quality of representing things as they really are
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even, polished, or lacking roughness
