rester

noun
person who stays
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛstər / (AmE), / ˈrɛstə / (BrE)
a person who remains in a place or stays behind when others leave

Examples

The last rester
After the movie ended, only one rester remained in the theater to watch the credits.
During the evacuation, the rester chose to stay behind and help others.
At the end of the party, the last rester helped clean up the mess.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leaver - a person who departs or goes away
  • synonym: stayer - a person who continues to stay or endure in a situation
