caddisfly

noun
aquatic case-building insect
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkædɪsˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkædɪsˌflaɪ / (BrE)
a small moth-like insect whose aquatic larvae build protective cases from materials like sand, stones, or plants
