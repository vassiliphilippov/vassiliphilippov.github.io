hahahaha

interjection
written laughter
6.0
Pronunciation: / hɑːhɑːˈhɑːhɑː / (AmE), / hɑːhɑːˈhɑːhɑː / (BrE)
an interjection used in writing to show laughter, amusement, sarcasm, or mockery

Examples

Hahahaha at a joke
She replied with hahahaha when her friend sent a funny meme.
After hearing the joke, he typed hahahaha in the group chat.
The comment was so unexpected that everyone responded with hahahaha.
When he saw the ridiculous excuse, she replied with a simple hahahaha to show her disbelief.
He texted hahahaha after reading the obviously fake news story.
Her comment, followed by a long hahahaha, made it clear she was mocking him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haha - an interjection used to express laughter, sometimes sarcastically
  • synonym: hehe - a written representation of soft or sly laughter
  • synonym: lol - an abbreviation for 'laugh out loud', used to show amusement
