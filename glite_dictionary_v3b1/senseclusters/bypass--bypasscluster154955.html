bypass

noun
road around a town or city
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌpæs / (AmE), / ˈbaɪpɑːs / (BrE)
a road built around a town or city to allow traffic to avoid its center

Examples

City bypass road
It takes about five minutes longer if you don't use the bypass.
The new bypass has reduced traffic congestion in the city center.
They built a bypass to divert trucks away from the residential area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ring road - a road that goes around the edge of a town or city
