bypass

noun
road around a town or city
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌpæs / (AmE), / ˈbaɪpɑːs / (BrE)

Examples

City bypass road

The new bypass has reduced traffic congestion in the city center.
They built a bypass to divert trucks away from the residential area.
