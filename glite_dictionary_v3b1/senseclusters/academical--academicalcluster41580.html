academical

adjective
theoretical, not practical
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌækəˈdɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌækəˈdɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
theoretical or related to formal study, rather than practical or applied use

Examples

Academical debate
The debate became too academical for most of the audience to follow.
Her research was criticized as being overly academical and lacking real-world relevance.
Some students prefer hands-on learning to academical discussions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academic - relating to education or scholarly study
  • antonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice
