kempt appearance

noun
neatly groomed look
Pronunciation: / kɛmpt əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / kɛmpt əˈpɪə.rəns / (BrE)
a neatly combed or well-groomed look, especially in relation to a person's hair or overall presentation

Examples

A kempt appearance
A kempt appearance is valued in many professional settings.
Even after a long flight, her kempt appearance impressed her colleagues.
His kempt appearance made a great first impression at the job interview.
