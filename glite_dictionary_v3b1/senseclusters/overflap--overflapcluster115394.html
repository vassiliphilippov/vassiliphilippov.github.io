overflap

noun
covering flap
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərflæp / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəflæp / (BrE)
a flap that extends over another part to cover or secure it, especially in packaging or construction

Examples

Envelope overflap
The envelope has an overflap that folds down to keep the contents safe.
Make sure the overflap is properly sealed to prevent the box from opening during shipping.
The designer added an overflap to the package for extra protection against dust.
