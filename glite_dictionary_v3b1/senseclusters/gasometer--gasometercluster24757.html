gasometer

noun
large container for storing gas
3.6
Pronunciation: / ɡæˈsɑːmətər / (AmE), / ɡəˈsɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a large, airtight container, often spherical or cylindrical, used for storing gas to be used as fuel
