dramatically

adverb
in a dramatic or theatrical manner
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /drə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kliː/ /; / /drə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kə.liː/ (AmE), /drə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kə.liː/ /; / /drə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
in a way that is very noticeable, striking, or intended to create a strong effect (sometimes relating to drama or excitement)
