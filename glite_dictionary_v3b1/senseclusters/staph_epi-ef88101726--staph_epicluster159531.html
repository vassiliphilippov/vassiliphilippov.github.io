Staph epi

noun
abbreviation for Staphylococcus epidermidis bacterium
7.4
Pronunciation: / stæf ˈɛpi / (AmE), / stæf ˈɛpi / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for Staphylococcus epidermidis, a type of bacteria commonly found on human skin and a frequent cause of infections, especially in hospitals

Examples

Staph epi infection
Doctors routinely monitor catheters for signs of Staph epi contamination.
Some antibiotics are less effective against Staph epi infections.
The hospital lab identified Staph epi in the blood culture.
