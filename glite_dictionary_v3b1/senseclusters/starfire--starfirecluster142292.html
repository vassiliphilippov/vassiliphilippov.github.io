starfire

noun
sparkling starlight
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a bright or sparkling light that resembles the light of stars, especially seen in the night sky

Examples

Night starfire
The children watched the starfire twinkle above the quiet countryside.
A gentle starfire illuminated the path as they walked home late at night.
Poets often write about the beauty of starfire in the midnight sky.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darkness - the absence of light
  • synonym: glow - a steady light, often soft or shining
  • synonym: starlight - the light that comes from stars
