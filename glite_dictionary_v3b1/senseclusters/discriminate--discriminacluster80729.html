Synonyms

  • distinguish
    3.1 20/m
    distinguish means to notice clear differences in general situations, while discriminate means to recognize or separate things more carefully.
  • tell apart
    3.0 1/m
    tell apart is an informal phrasal verb used to recognize similar things, while discriminate is a more formal verb for noticing differences.
  • differentiate
    4.4 9/m
    differentiate often means to show or make differences in study or science, while discriminate focuses on noticing differences.
  • discern
    5.8 9/m
    discern suggests noticing something with difficulty or careful thought, while discriminate means simply to tell differences.
  • separate
    2.3 14/m
    separate can mean to divide or sort things physically, while discriminate mainly means to notice or tell differences.
