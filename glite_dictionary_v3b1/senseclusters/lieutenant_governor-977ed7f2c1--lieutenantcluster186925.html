lieutenant governor

noun
deputy to a U.S. state governor
6.3 1.36/m
Pronunciation: / luÀêÀàt…õn…ônt Àà…° åv…ôrn…ôr / (AmE), / l…õfÀàt…õn…ônt Àà…° åv…ôn…ô / (BrE)
