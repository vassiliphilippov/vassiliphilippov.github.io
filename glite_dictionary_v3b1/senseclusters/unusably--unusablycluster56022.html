unusably

adverb
not fit for use
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjuːzəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈjuːzəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be used or is not fit for use

Examples

Road unusably damaged
After the storm, the road was unusably damaged and had to be closed.
The instructions were unusably complicated for most people to follow.
His handwriting was unusably messy, making the note impossible to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inoperably - in a way that cannot function or be operated
  • antonym: usefully - in a way that is fit for use or helpful
