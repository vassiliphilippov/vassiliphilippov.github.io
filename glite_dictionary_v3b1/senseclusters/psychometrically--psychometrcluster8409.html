psychometrically

adverb
in a measurement-related way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the measurement of mental abilities, traits, or psychological functions

Examples

Psychometrically tested questionnaire
The questionnaire was psychometrically tested to ensure its reliability and validity.
Researchers analyzed the data psychometrically to assess the accuracy of the new scale.
The results were evaluated psychometrically before being published in the journal.
