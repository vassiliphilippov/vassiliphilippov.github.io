cakra

noun
spiritual energy center
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkrə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkrə / (BrE)
one of the centers of spiritual energy in the human body, according to some Indian traditions, believed to influence health and emotions

Examples

Balance the cakra
Many yoga practitioners focus on balancing each cakra to improve their well-being.
The teacher explained how energy flows through every cakra in the body.
Meditation techniques often aim to open a blocked cakra for emotional healing.

Related Terms
