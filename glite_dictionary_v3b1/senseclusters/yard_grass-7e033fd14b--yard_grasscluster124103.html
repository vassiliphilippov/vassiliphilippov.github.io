yard grass

noun
coarse tropical grass; common weed
4.0
Pronunciation: / jɑrd ɡræs / (AmE), / jɑːd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A coarse annual grass with fingerlike spikes of flowers, originally from the Old World tropics and now a common weed in many other regions.

Examples

Wild yard grass
In summer, patches of yard grass can be seen growing along the roadside.
She learned that the tough weed in her backyard was actually a type of yard grass.
The yard grass quickly spread across the garden, crowding out the flowers.
