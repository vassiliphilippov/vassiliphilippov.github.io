training

noun
process of learning skills
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of learning the skills needed to do a particular job or activity

Examples

Job training
All new employees must complete intensive training before starting their jobs.
Physical training is required for all firefighters.
She is attending a training course to improve her computer skills.
