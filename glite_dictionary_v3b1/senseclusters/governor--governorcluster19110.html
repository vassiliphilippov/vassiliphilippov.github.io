governor

noun
device to regulate machine speed
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvɚnɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənə / (BrE)
a device that automatically regulates the speed of a machine by controlling the supply of fuel, power, or another input.

Examples

Engine governor
A malfunctioning governor can cause fluctuations in engine speed.
Old windmills often relied on a mechanical governor to control the rotation of their sails.
The steam engine's governor keeps it from running too fast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regulator - a device for controlling the operation of a machine, process, or system
