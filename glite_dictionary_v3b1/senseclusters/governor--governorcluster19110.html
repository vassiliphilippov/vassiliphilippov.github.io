a device that automatically regulates the speed of a machine by controlling the supply of fuel, power, or another input.

Examples

Engine governor
A malfunctioning governor can cause fluctuations in engine speed.
Old windmills often relied on a mechanical governor to control the rotation of their sails.
The steam engine's governor keeps it from running too fast.

Synonyms

  • regulator
    3.9 1.75/m
    a regulator keeps pressure, flow, or temperature steady in a system, while a governor specifically and automatically adjusts input to control speed.
  • controller
    3.8 7.29/m
    a controller directs or adjusts a machine's overall operation in many ways, while a governor specifically changes fuel or power to keep speed steady.
