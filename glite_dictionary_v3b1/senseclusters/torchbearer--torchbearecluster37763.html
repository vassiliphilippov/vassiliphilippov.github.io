torchbearer

noun
leader or inspiration in a cause
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtʃˌbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃˌbɛərə / (BrE)
a person who leads or inspires others in a campaign, movement, or cause

Examples

A torchbearer of change
As a torchbearer of civil rights, he inspired millions to stand up for justice.
Many looked to her as the torchbearer of a new generation of artists.
She became the torchbearer for environmental reform in her country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
  • synonym: pioneer - a person who is among the first to lead in a field or movement
