line

noun
method or direction of reasoning
5.7
Pronunciation: / laɪn / (AmE), / laɪn / (BrE)
a way of thinking about something, especially as part of an argument or reasoning

Examples

A line of thinking
He developed a compelling line of reasoning to support his proposal.
The lawyer's line of questioning suggested a new theory about the case.
Your line of argument doesn't address the main issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argument - a reason or set of reasons given in support of an idea
  • synonym: reasoning - the process of thinking about something in a logical way
