Conversational language used chiefly to establish or maintain social relationships rather than to convey information.

Examples

Phatic communication greetings
In many cultures, greetings and farewells are rituals of phatic communication.
Much of the morning conversation was pure phatic communication to ease social interactions.
Saying "How are you?" as part of phatic communication doesn't require an honest answer.

Synonyms

  • phatic speech
