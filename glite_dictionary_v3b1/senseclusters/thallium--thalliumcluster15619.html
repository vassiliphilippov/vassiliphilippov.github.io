thallium

noun
a soft, toxic metallic element (Tl)
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθæliəm / (AmE), / ˈθaliəm / (BrE)
a soft, grey, highly toxic metallic element, resembling tin, used in electronic devices and some insect and rodent poisons

Examples

Soft thallium metal
Thallium was once commonly used in rat poison but is now strictly regulated.
Exposure to thallium can be extremely dangerous and require immediate medical attention.
Scientists detected trace amounts of thallium in the contaminated soil sample.
