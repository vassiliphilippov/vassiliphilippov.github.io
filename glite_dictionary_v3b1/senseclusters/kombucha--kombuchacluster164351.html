kombucha

noun
fermented tea drink
Pronunciation: / kɑmˈbuːtʃə / (AmE), / kɒmˈbuːtʃə / (BrE)
a fizzy, slightly sour drink made by fermenting sweetened tea with bacteria and yeast

Examples

A glass of kombucha
She brews her own kombucha at home every week.
Many people enjoy drinking kombucha for its unique taste and possible health benefits.
The café offers several flavors of kombucha, including ginger and berry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fermented tea - tea that has undergone fermentation, often resulting in a tangy flavor
  • degree lower: soda - a carbonated soft drink
