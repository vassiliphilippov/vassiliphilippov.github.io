ritual killing

noun
killing as part of a religious ritual
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəl ˈkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃu.əl ˈkɪl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the killing of an animal or person as a religious or ceremonial act to appease or honor a deity or supernatural power

Examples

Ritual killing ceremony
Anthropologists have studied the history and meaning of ritual killing in ancient societies.
Debates continue over whether the remains point to a ritual killing or a different form of sacrifice.
The evidence suggested that the victim had been part of a ritual killing meant to ensure a good harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: human sacrifice - the killing of a person as an offering to a deity
