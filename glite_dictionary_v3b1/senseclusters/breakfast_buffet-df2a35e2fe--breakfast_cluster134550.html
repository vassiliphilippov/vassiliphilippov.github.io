breakfast buffet

noun
self-service breakfast meal
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛkfəst bəˈfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst bəˈfeɪ / (BrE)
A meal served in a self-service style, especially in the morning, where a variety of breakfast dishes are offered.

Examples

A breakfast buffet meal
Many guests arrived early to enjoy the extensive breakfast buffet.
She filled her plate with pastries and fruit from the breakfast buffet.
The hotel offers a delightful breakfast buffet every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffet - a meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes
