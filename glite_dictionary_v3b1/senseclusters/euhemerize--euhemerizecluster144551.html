euhemerize

verb
interpret myths as history
9.3 9.9
Pronunciation: /juː.ˈhiː.mə.raɪz/ (AmE), /juː.ˈhiː.mə.raɪz/ (BrE)
to interpret myths as derived from real historical events or personages.

Examples

To euhemerize myths
Many scholars have tried to euhemerize the legend of King Arthur, seeking evidence of a real leader beneath the myth.
Some historians argue that attempts to euhemerize every myth risk oversimplifying complex narratives.
The professor encouraged students to euhemerize the Greek gods as ancient rulers who were later deified.

  • synonym: historicize - interpret or represent as historical or based in history
