backbreaker

noun
hard manual worker
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a person who does extremely hard, physically demanding manual work

Examples

A backbreaker in the mines
The job of a backbreaker in the mines was both dangerous and exhausting.
He was known as a backbreaker who could outwork anyone on the construction site.
Years as a backbreaker left him with a strong body but aching joints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled physical work
  • synonym: toiler - someone who works very hard, especially at difficult tasks
