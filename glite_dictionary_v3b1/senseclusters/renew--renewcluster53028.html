renew

verb
to make valid again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnu / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuː / (BrE)
to make something valid, fresh, or effective again after it has ended or become inactive

Examples

To renew a license
You must renew your library card every year to keep borrowing books.
The couple decided to renew their wedding vows on their anniversary.
He forgot to renew his driver's license before it expired.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expire - to come to an end or no longer be valid
  • synonym: restore - to bring back to a former condition
