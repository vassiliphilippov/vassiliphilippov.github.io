Sunday

proper noun
day of rest
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
the day of the week between Saturday and Monday, often considered a day of rest or worship in many cultures

Examples

Sunday lunch
Many families gather for a special meal on Sunday afternoons.
Shops in some countries close early on Sunday for a day of rest.
He enjoys going for a long walk every Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Monday - the day after Sunday
  • antonym: Saturday - the day before Sunday
