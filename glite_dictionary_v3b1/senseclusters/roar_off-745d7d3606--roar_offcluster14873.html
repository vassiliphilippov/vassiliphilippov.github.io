roar off

verb
leave quickly with a loud noise
6.3 0.31/m
Pronunciation: / r…îr …ëf / (AmE), / r…îÀêr …íf / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or person) to leave or depart quickly, making a loud, roaring noise.

Examples

To roar off quickly
After saying goodbye, she roared off in her sports car.
The bus roared off before I could reach the stop.
The motorcycle roared off down the empty highway, leaving a cloud of dust behind.
