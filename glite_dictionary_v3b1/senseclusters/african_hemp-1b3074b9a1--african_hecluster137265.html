African hemp

noun
fibrous South African plant (Sansevieria)
Pronunciation: / ˈæfrɪkən hɛmp / (AmE), / ˈæfrɪkən hɛmp / (BrE)
A strong, fibrous plant of the genus Sansevieria native to South Africa, used historically for making bowstrings and other fibers.

Examples

African hemp fiber plant
Gardeners often grow African hemp as an ornamental plant for its striking leaves.
Researchers are studying the potential uses of African hemp in sustainable materials.
The fibers of African hemp were traditionally used to make strong ropes and twine.
