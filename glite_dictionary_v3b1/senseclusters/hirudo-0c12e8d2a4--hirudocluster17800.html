Hirudo

noun
genus of leeches (type of Hirudinidae)
Pronunciation: / hɪˈruːdoʊ / (AmE), / hɪˈruːdəʊ / (BrE)
the genus of leeches that includes the medicinal leech, and is the type genus of the family Hirudinidae
