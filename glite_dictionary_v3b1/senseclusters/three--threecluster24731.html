three

noun
playing card with three pips
9.9
Pronunciation: / θri / (AmE), / θriː / (BrE)
A playing card with three pips, being the card of the third rank in each suit in a standard deck.

Examples

Three of hearts
He drew the three of hearts from the deck and placed it on the table.
She needed a three to complete her straight in the card game.
There are only two threes left in the stack after this round.
