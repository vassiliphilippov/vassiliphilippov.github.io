promotion

noun
advancement in rank or position
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
the act of raising someone to a higher rank or position

Examples

Promotion to manager
His recent promotion to manager surprised many of his colleagues.
Opportunities for promotion are limited in smaller organizations.
She received a promotion after years of hard work at the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advancement - the process of moving forward or upward in rank
  • antonym: demotion - the act of lowering someone to a lower rank or position
