whoo

noun
owl hooting sound
9.3
Pronunciation: / wuː / (AmE), / wuː / (BrE)
a hooting sound made by an owl, typically a single call or note

Examples

An owl's whoo
The forest was silent except for the occasional whoo echoing through the night.
We listened to the soft whoo of an owl as we camped under the stars.
A sudden whoo startled the small animals hiding in the underbrush.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoot - the loud call or cry of an owl
