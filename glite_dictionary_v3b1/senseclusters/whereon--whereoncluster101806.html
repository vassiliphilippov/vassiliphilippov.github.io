whereon

conjunction
as a result of which
9.3
Pronunciation: /wer.ˈɑːn/ /; / /wər.ˈɑːn/ (AmE), /weə.ˈɒn/ /; / /wə.ˈɒn/ (BrE)
immediately after which or as a result of which; introducing a clause that describes what happened next or followed

Examples

A statement whereon applause follows

The contract was signed, whereon the project officially began.
She declined the invitation, whereon he decided not to attend the event either.
