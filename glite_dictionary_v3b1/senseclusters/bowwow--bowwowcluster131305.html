bowwow

verb
to bark like a dog
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊˌwaʊ / (AmE), / ˈbaʊˌwaʊ / (BrE)
to make a barking sound like a dog, or to imitate a dog's bark.

Examples

To bowwow loudly
The children loved to bowwow during their pretend play as puppies.
When he heard the dog, the baby tried to bowwow in response.
She could bowwow so realistically that even the neighbor's dog barked back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bark - to make the sharp, loud sound of a dog
