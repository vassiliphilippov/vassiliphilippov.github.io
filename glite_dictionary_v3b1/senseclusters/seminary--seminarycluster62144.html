seminary

noun
religious training school
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪˌnɛri /; / ˈsɛm.əˌnɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪnəri /; / ˈsɛm.ɪ.nər.i / (BrE)
