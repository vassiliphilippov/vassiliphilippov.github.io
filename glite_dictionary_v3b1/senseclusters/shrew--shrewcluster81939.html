shrew

noun
small, mouse-like mammal with a pointed snout
5.1 0.43/m
Pronunciation: /  Éru / (AmE), /  ÉruÀê / (BrE)
A small, mouse-like mammal with a pointed snout, belonging to the family Soricidae and related to moles.

Examples

A tiny shrew
A shrew scurried through the undergrowth, searching for insects to eat.
Despite its small size, the shrew is known for its voracious appetite.
The field biologist trapped several shrews to study their dietary habits.
