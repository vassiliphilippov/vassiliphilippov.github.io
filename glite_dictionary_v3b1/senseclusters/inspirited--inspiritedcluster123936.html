inspirited

adjective
spiritually inspired
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspɪrɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɪrɪtɪd / (BrE)
filled with spiritual or supernatural inspiration, especially as if guided by a divine influence (archaic or literary)

Examples

Inspirited by a vision
The poet seemed inspirited by a force beyond ordinary understanding as she recited her verses.
In ancient texts, prophets are often described as inspirited when delivering their messages.
The leader's inspirited words gave hope to the troubled community.
