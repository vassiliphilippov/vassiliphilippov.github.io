cultivated plant

noun
plant intentionally grown by humans
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltəˌveɪtɪd plænt / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that is grown intentionally by humans, usually for food, ornamental purposes, or other economic uses.

Examples

A cultivated plant species
Many cultivated plants require regular watering and soil care to thrive.
The exhibition featured hundreds of rare cultivated plants from around the world.
Tomatoes are one of the most popular cultivated plants in home gardens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wild plant - a plant that grows naturally without human intervention
