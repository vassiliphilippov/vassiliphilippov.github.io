puckle

noun
mischievous spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈpʌkəl / (BrE)
an archaic term for a mischievous or troublesome spirit or goblin in folklore

Examples

A puckle spirit
In the old village legends, a puckle was blamed for missing tools and strange noises.
Travelers were warned to avoid the woods at night, lest a puckle play tricks on them.
The children listened wide-eyed to stories of a puckle who haunted the crossroads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - a benevolent spiritual being
  • synonym: goblin - a small, mischievous creature from folklore
  • synonym: sprite - a small, playful supernatural being
