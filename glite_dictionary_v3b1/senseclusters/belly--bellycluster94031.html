belly

noun
bulging or rounded part of something
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli / (AmE), / ˈbɛli / (BrE)
the rounded, bulging, or central part of an object or structure, or the hollow inside of something large

Examples

Belly of the plane
Engineers reinforced the belly of the pipeline to withstand higher pressure.
The belly of the sail caught the wind and pushed the boat forward.
The artist painted the belly of the vase with a decorative pattern.
Many supplies were stored in the belly of the ship for the long voyage.
The whale's huge belly held more than a ton of fish.
Workers inspected the belly of the airplane for damage after the flight.
