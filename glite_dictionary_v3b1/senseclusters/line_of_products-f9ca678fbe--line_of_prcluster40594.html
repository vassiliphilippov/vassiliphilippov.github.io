line of products

noun
range of similar products by a company
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪn əv ˈprɑːdəkts / (AmE), / laɪn əv ˈprɒdʌkts / (BrE)
A range or category of similar products or merchandise sold by a particular company.

Examples

A line of products
Our new line of products includes both skincare and haircare items.
The company decided to expand its line of products to attract new customers.
They introduced an eco-friendly line of products last year.
