husklike

adjective
resembling a husk
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌskˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʌskˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a husk, especially in appearance or texture

Examples

Husklike outer layer
The seeds were wrapped in a husklike layer that kept them dry.
She noticed a husklike texture on the outside of the fruit.
The insect's cocoon had a tough, husklike exterior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papery - thin and resembling paper in texture
  • synonym: shell - the hard outer covering of something
