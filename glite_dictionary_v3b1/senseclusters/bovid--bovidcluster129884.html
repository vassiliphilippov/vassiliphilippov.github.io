bovid

noun
member of the Bovidae family (hollow-horned ruminant)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊ.vɪd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.vɪd / (BrE)
a mammal of the family Bovidae, which includes cattle, sheep, goats, antelopes, and similar hollow-horned ruminants

Examples

A bovid animal
Domesticated cattle are the most widely known bovid animals.
Scientists discovered a fossilized skull belonging to a prehistoric bovid in the cave.
The African savannah is home to many species of bovid, including antelopes and buffalo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ruminant - an animal that chews cud, such as a cow or sheep
