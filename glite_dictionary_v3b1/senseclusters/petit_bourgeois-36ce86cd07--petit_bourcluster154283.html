petit bourgeois

noun
member of the lower middle class
Pronunciation: / pəˌti bʊrˈʒwɑ /; / pəˌtiː bʊrˈʒwɑː / (AmE), / pəˌti bʊəˈʒwɑː /; / ˌpeti bʊəˈʒwɑː / (BrE)
a member of the lower middle class, often seen as conventional or materialistic

Examples

The petit bourgeois lifestyle
Critics described his tastes as petit bourgeois rather than truly refined.
He was proud of his petit bourgeois background and careful with his money.
The novel depicts the lives of a petit bourgeois family struggling to maintain their status.
He rose from a petit bourgeois background to become a leading intellectual.
Many revolutions viewed the petit bourgeois as wavering between the working class and the elite.
The petit bourgeois of the town tended to vote conservatively in local elections.
