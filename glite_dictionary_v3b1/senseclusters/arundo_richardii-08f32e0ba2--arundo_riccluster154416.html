Arundo richardii

noun
tall New Zealand grass with plumelike heads
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈrʊndoʊ rɪˈtʃɑrdi.aɪ / (AmE), / əˈrʊndəʊ rɪˈtʃɑːdi.aɪ / (BrE)
A tall grass native to New Zealand, cultivated for its plumelike flower heads.

Examples

Arundo richardii tall grass
In its natural habitat, Arundo richardii can grow over two meters tall.
Many gardeners in New Zealand use Arundo richardii to create ornamental borders.
The plumelike heads of Arundo richardii make it popular for floral arrangements.
