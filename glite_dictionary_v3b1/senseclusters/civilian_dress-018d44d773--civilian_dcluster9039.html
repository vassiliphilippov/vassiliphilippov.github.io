civilian dress

noun
ordinary clothing, not a uniform
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪliən drɛs / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪliən drɛs / (BrE)
ordinary clothing worn by people who are not members of the armed forces, police, or clergy, as opposed to uniforms or special attire

Examples

Civilian dress clothes
After retiring from the army, he was glad to wear civilian dress again.
Members of the secret police often operated in civilian dress to avoid detection.
The ceremony required officers to change out of uniform and into civilian dress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uniform - the distinctive clothing worn by members of the same organization
