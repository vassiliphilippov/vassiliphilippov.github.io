permit

noun
official authorization document
Pronunciation: / pərˈmɪt / (AmE), / pəˈmɪt / (BrE)
an official document that gives someone permission to do something

Examples

A building permit
She applied for a building permit before starting construction.
Without a parking permit, you may get a ticket.
You need a permit to camp in the national park.
She applied for a work permit before starting her new job.
The construction project was delayed until the permit was approved.
You need a permit to park in this area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certificate - an official document attesting a fact, especially completion of requirements or qualification
  • synonym: license - an official permission to do something, often driving or operating something
  • antonym: prohibit - to formally forbid by law, rule, or other authority
