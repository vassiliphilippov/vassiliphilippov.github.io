permit

noun
official authorization document
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.mɪt/ /; / /ˈpər.mɪt/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.mɪt/ (BrE)
an official document that gives someone permission to do something

Examples

The construction project was delayed until the permit was approved.
