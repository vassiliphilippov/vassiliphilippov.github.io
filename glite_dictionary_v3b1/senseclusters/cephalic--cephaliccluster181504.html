cephalic

adjective
relating to the head
6.6
Pronunciation: / sɪˈfælɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈfælɪk / (BrE)
relating to the head or located near the head, especially in medical or anatomical contexts.

Examples

Cephalic end
In anatomy, the cephalic end refers to the part closest to the head.
Some fish display a distinctive cephalic hump above their eyes.
The doctor examined the patient for signs of cephalic trauma.
