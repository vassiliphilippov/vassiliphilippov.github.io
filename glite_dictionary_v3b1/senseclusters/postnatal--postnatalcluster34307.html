postnatal

adjective
after birth; relating to period after birth
5.3 3.4
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈneɪtəl / (AmE), / pəʊstˈneɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the period immediately after birth, especially with reference to the mother or infant.

Examples

Postnatal depression
Postnatal depression can affect both mothers and fathers after childbirth.
She attended several postnatal checkups to ensure the baby's health.
The clinic offers postnatal care for new mothers and infants.
