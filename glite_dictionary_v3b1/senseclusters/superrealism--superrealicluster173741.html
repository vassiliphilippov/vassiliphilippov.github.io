superrealism

noun
highly realistic art style
8.2
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈriː.ə.lɪz.əm/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈriː.ə.lɪz.əm/ /; / /suː.pə.ˈrɪə.lɪz.əm/ (BrE)
an artistic style in which objects are depicted with extremely detailed and lifelike accuracy, often resembling photographs

Examples

Superrealism art style
The museum's new exhibit showcases superrealism with paintings that appear almost photographic.
Many artists who practice superrealism spend hours perfecting tiny details in their work.
She was fascinated by superrealism and tried to capture every shadow and reflection in her drawings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstraction - an art style that does not attempt to represent reality accurately
  • synonym: photorealism - a style of painting that looks as realistic as a photograph
  • degree lower: realism - an art style focused on representing subjects truthfully and accurately
