windigo

noun
mythical evil spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndɪˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndɪˌɡəʊ / (BrE)
a mythical evil spirit or monster from Algonquian folklore, said to possess people and drive them to cannibalism

Examples

A windigo spirit
The villagers feared the windigo that haunted the forests in winter.
Stories of the windigo warn against greed and isolation in Algonquian culture.
Some believe that seeing a windigo is a sign of great misfortune.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monster - a large, frightening imaginary creature
  • synonym: wendigo - a variant spelling of windigo, a mythical evil spirit in Algonquian folklore
