ensiform leaf

noun
long, sword-shaped leaf
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈen.sɪ.fɔːrm liːf/ /; / /ˈen.sə.fɔːrm liːf/ (AmE), /ˈen.sɪ.fɔːm liːf/ /; / /ˈen.sə.fɔːm liːf/ (BrE)
A long, narrow leaf shaped like a sword, as seen in plants like the iris.

Examples

An ensiform leaf shape
Botanists often identify species by the presence of an ensiform leaf among other foliage types.
The iris is well known for its distinctive ensiform leaf which rises gracefully from the base.
The sword-like shape of an ensiform leaf helps distinguish it from broader, oval leaves.
