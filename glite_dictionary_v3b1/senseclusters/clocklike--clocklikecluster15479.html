clocklike

adjective
regular and precise
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɒkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
happening with regularity or precision, similar to the way a clock operates

Examples

Clocklike precision
Her clocklike arrival at work impressed her manager every morning.
The machine operated in a clocklike manner, never missing a beat.
His clocklike habits made his daily schedule easy to predict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irregular - not happening at regular intervals; lacking consistency
  • synonym: punctual - happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time
