Lake Huron

proper noun
the second largest Great Lake in North America
Pronunciation: / leɪk ˈhʊrɑn / (AmE), / leɪk ˈhjʊərɒn / (BrE)
the second largest of the Great Lakes of North America, situated between the state of Michigan and the province of Ontario

Examples

Lake Huron waves
A powerful storm swept across Lake Huron, causing large waves and dangerous conditions for boaters.
Many small towns along Lake Huron rely on tourism and fishing as their primary industries.
The shores of Lake Huron are known for their beautiful sandy beaches and clear water.
