lumbodorsal

adjective
of lower back and back
Pronunciation: / ˌlʌmboʊˈdɔrsəl / (AmE), / ˌlʌmbəʊˈdɔːsəl / (BrE)
relating to both the lumbar (lower back) and dorsal (back) regions of the body

Examples

Lumbodorsal fascia
The lumbodorsal fascia is an important structure in supporting the lower back muscles.
Doctors examined the patient's lumbodorsal region for signs of injury after the accident.
Physical therapy focused on strengthening the lumbodorsal muscles to reduce back pain.
