oversaturate

verb
to make colors too intense
6.8
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsætʃ.ə.reɪt/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈsæt.ə.reɪt/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsætʃ.ə.reɪt/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈsæt.ə.reɪt/ (BrE)
to increase the color, tone, or intensity of an image or visual element so much that it appears excessively strong or unnatural

Examples

To oversaturate a photo
If you oversaturate the picture, the colors will look unrealistic and harsh.
Graphic designers try not to oversaturate images to maintain a natural appearance.
The advertisement was criticized for its oversaturated visuals that distracted from the message.

  • degree lower: saturate - to increase the intensity of color in an image
