fly off the handle

verb
become suddenly very angry
7.6
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ɑf ðə ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / flaɪ ɒf ðə ˈhændl /; / flaɪ ɒf ðə ˈhændl̩ / (BrE)
to suddenly become very angry or lose your temper

Examples
