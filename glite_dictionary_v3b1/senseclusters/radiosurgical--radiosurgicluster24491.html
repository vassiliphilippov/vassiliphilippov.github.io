radiosurgical

adjective
using radiation in surgery
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊˈsɜrdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊˈsɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the use of targeted radiation in surgery, especially for treating tumors

Examples

Radiosurgical treatment
The hospital offers advanced radiosurgical treatments for patients with brain tumors.
A radiosurgical approach can minimize damage to healthy tissue during cancer therapy.
Doctors recommended a radiosurgical procedure instead of traditional surgery for the spinal lesion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiotherapeutic - relating to the treatment of disease with radiation
  • synonym: surgical - relating to surgery or operations
