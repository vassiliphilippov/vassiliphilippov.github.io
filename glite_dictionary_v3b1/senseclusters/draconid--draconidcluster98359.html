draconid

noun
Draconids meteor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdrækənɪd / (AmE), / ˈdrækənɪd / (BrE)
a meteor that is part of the Draconids meteor shower, which appears to radiate from the constellation Draco

Examples

A draconid meteor
Every October, skywatchers hope to see a bright draconid streaking across the night sky.
The draconids are best viewed from the Northern Hemisphere after sunset.
Last night, we counted five draconids during the peak of the meteor shower.
