honeydew honey

noun
dark, strong honey from plant honeydew
8.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌn.ɪ.duː ˈhʌn.ɪ/ /; / /ˈhʌn.ɪ.djuː ˈhʌn.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhʌn.ɪ.djuː ˈhʌn.ɪ/ /; / /ˈhʌn.ɪ.duː ˈhʌn.ɪ/ (BrE)
A dark, strongly flavored honey produced by bees from honeydew excreted by plant-sucking insects, typically with a rich aroma and less sweetness than floral honey.
