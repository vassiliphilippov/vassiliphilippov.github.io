philanthropy

noun
desire to promote human welfare
Pronunciation: / fəˈlænθrəpi / (AmE), / fɪˈlanθrəpi /; / fɪˈlænθrəpi / (BrE)
the desire or practice of helping others, especially by giving money or resources to good causes

Examples

Philanthropy work
Her lifelong commitment to philanthropy inspired others to give back to their communities.
Many business leaders engage in philanthropy by donating large sums to charitable organizations.
The foundation is known for its philanthropy in supporting education and healthcare initiatives.
The foundation is known for its philanthropy in supporting education and health programs worldwide.
Her lifelong philanthropy has improved the lives of thousands of children.
Many companies encourage philanthropy by matching employee donations to charities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charity - generous actions or donations to aid the poor, ill, or helpless
  • synonym: generosity - the quality of being kind and willing to give more than expected
  • antonym: selfishness - lack of consideration for others; concern for one's own advantage
