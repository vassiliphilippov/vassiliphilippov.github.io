frag

verb
to break or destroy
9.1
Pronunciation: / fræɡ / (AmE), / fræɡ / (BrE)
to break, damage, or destroy something, especially into pieces, in slang use

Examples

To frag a phone
He accidentally fragged his phone when he dropped it.
The kids fragged the old computer just to see what was inside.
If you keep tossing your bag like that, you might frag your laptop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destroy - to damage something so badly that it no longer exists or cannot be used
  • antonym: fix - to repair or restore something that is broken
  • synonym: smash - to break something into pieces with force
