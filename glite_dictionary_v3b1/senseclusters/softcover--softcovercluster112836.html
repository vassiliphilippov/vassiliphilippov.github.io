softcover

noun
book with soft cover
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌkʌvər / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌkʌvə / (BrE)
a book with a flexible, soft cover, usually made of paper or thin cardboard, instead of a hard cover

Examples

A softcover book
She prefers to read novels in softcover because they are lighter to carry.
The new edition is available in both softcover and hardcover formats.
I found a rare softcover version of the book at the used bookstore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardcover - a book with a rigid, hard cover
  • synonym: paperback - a book bound in a flexible paper cover
