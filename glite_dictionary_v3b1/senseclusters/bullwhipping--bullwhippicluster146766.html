bullwhipping

noun
crack of a whip
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌwɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌwɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the loud cracking sound or action produced when a bullwhip is snapped through the air

Examples

Bullwhipping sound
The bullwhipping echoed across the field, startling the nearby horses.
He demonstrated his skill with the whip, the bullwhipping ringing out sharply.
Spectators covered their ears at the loud bullwhipping during the rodeo show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crack - a sharp, sudden sound like that of a whip
  • synonym: snap - a sudden, sharp sound, especially made by something flexible
