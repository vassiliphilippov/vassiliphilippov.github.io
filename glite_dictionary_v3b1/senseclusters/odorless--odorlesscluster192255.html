odorless

adjective
having no smell
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdɚləs / (AmE), / ˈəʊdələs / (BrE)
Having no smell; not producing any odor.

Examples

Odorless gas
Helium is an odorless and colorless gas used in balloons.
She preferred odorless flowers in her bouquet to avoid triggering allergies.
The cleaning solution is completely odorless, making it ideal for enclosed spaces.
Air is typically odourless and invisible to the naked eye.
She prefers her cleaning products to be odourless so they don't irritate her allergies.
The lab technician released an odourless gas into the chamber for the experiment.
