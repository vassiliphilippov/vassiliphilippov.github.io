odorless

adjective
having no smell
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.dər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.də.ləs/ (BrE)
Having no smell; not producing any odor.

Examples

Odorless gas
Helium is an odorless and colorless gas used in balloons.
