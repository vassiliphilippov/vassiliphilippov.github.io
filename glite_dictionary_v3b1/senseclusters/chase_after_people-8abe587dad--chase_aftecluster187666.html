chase after people

verb
pursue others' attention or affection
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪs ˈæftɚ ˈpipəl / (AmE), / tʃeɪs ˈɑːftə ˈpiːpəl / (BrE)
to actively pursue the attention, affection, or approval of other people, often persistently or eagerly

Examples

To chase after people eagerly

You shouldn't chase after people just to feel accepted.
