citronella

noun
insect-repelling plant
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlə / (BrE)
a tropical grass whose fragrant oil is used to repel insects, especially mosquitoes

Examples

Citronella grass
We burned a citronella candle to keep mosquitoes away during the barbecue.
The garden was planted with citronella to help reduce the number of insects.
Many natural sprays use citronella oil as their main ingredient to repel bugs.
