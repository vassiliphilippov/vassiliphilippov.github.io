Zetland

proper noun
former name for the Shetland Islands
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛtlənd / (AmE), / ˈzɛtlənd / (BrE)
a former name for the Shetland Islands, an archipelago of about 100 islands in the North Atlantic off the north coast of Scotland
