psychometrician

noun
test design specialist
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊməˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊməˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
a specialist who designs, administers, or interprets psychological tests and measurements

Examples

A psychometrician designs tests
The school hired a psychometrician to develop fair and reliable entrance exams.
A psychometrician ensures that psychological tests are both valid and accurate.
After years of study, she became a respected psychometrician in the field of educational assessment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statistician - a person skilled in the use of statistics
