brown hickory

noun
American hickory tree with bitter nuts
Pronunciation: / ˌbraʊn ˈhɪkəri / (AmE), / ˌbraʊn ˈhɪkəri / (BrE)
An American hickory tree (Carya glabra) known for its smooth bark and bitter nuts.

Examples

The brown hickory tree
He used wood from the brown hickory to build a sturdy axe handle.
Many animals avoid eating the bitter nuts of the brown hickory.
The woods were filled with brown hickory and oak trees.
