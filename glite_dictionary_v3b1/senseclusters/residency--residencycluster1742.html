residency

noun
temporary work placement
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈre.zɪ.dən.siː/ /; / /ˈre.zɪ.dən.sɪ/ (AmE), /ˈre.zɪ.dən.siː/ /; / /ˈre.zɪ.dən.sɪ/ (BrE)
a period of time spent living and working in a particular place, often for artists, students, or professionals to develop their work or skills

Examples

Artist residency
The artist completed a year-long residency at a studio in Berlin.
Medical students must finish a hospital residency before becoming licensed doctors.
She applied for several writing residencies to focus on her novel.
