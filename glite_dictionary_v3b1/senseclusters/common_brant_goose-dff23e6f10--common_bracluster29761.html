common brant goose

noun
variety of brant goose
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən brænt ɡus / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən brænt ɡuːs / (BrE)
a widespread variety of brant goose (Branta bernicla), commonly found in northern coastal regions

Examples

The common brant goose
Birdwatchers gathered at the estuary to spot the rare common brant goose among other waterfowl.
During the spring migration, thousands of common brant geese can be seen along the coast.
The common brant goose feeds mainly on eelgrass and sea lettuce in tidal flats.
