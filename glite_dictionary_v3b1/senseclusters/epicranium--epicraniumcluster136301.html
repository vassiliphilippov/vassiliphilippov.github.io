epicranium

noun
soft tissues covering the skull
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈkreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkreɪniəm / (BrE)
the muscle, aponeurosis, and skin covering the upper part of the skull (cranium)

Examples

The epicranium muscle
During the surgery, an incision was made through the epicranium to access the skull.
Injuries to the epicranium can result in swelling and bruising of the forehead.
The epicranium includes the scalp and its associated muscles and connective tissue.
