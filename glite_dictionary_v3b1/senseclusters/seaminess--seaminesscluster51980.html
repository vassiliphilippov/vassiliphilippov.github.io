seaminess

noun
visible joins or seams
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːminəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːminɪs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of having visible seams, joins, or stitching where parts are connected

Examples

Seaminess of the fabric
The seaminess of the handmade quilt gave it a unique and rustic charm.
After years of use, the seaminess of the sofa became more noticeable along the edges.
The tailor tried to hide the seaminess of the repaired jacket, but the lines were still visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roughness - the quality of being uneven or not smooth
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even, regular surface without seams or joins
