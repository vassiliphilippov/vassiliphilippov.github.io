bear grass

noun
a perennial plant with grasslike leaves and tall white flower panicle
6.9
Pronunciation: / bɛr ɡræs /; / ˈbɛr ˌɡræs / (AmE), / beə ɡrɑːs /; / ˈbɛə ˌɡrɑːs /; / ˈbeə ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
