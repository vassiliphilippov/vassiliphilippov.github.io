slip my mind

verb
to be unintentionally forgotten
Pronunciation: / slɪp maɪ maɪnd / (AmE), / slɪp maɪ maɪnd / (BrE)
(of something) to be unintentionally forgotten; to be overlooked because of forgetfulness.

Examples

If it ever slips your mind, just set a reminder for next time.
Paying the bill completely slipped his mind, so the service was cancelled.
