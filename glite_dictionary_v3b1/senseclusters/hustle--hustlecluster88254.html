hustle

noun
busy or energetic activity; commotion
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈhʌsəl / (BrE)
a state of busy or energetic activity and movement, often involving hurry or commotion

Examples

Festival hustle
After the hustle of the festival, the town felt unusually quiet.
She missed the daily hustle of working in a busy office.
The morning hustle in the city streets made it hard to walk quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bustle - energetic, often noisy, activity and movement
  • antonym: calm - a state of peacefulness and lack of activity or commotion
