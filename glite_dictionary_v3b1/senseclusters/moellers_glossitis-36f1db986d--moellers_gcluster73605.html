Moeller's glossitis

noun
oral condition with red tongue patches
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊlərz ɡlɑˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈməʊləz ɡlɒˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A superficial form of glossitis characterized by irregular red patches on the tongue and sensitivity to hot or spicy foods.

Examples

Moeller's glossitis red patches
People with Moeller's glossitis often experience discomfort when eating spicy foods.
Proper oral hygiene can help manage the symptoms of Moeller's glossitis.
The dentist diagnosed her with Moeller's glossitis after examining the red patches on her tongue.
