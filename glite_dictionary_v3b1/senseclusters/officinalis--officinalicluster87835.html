officinalis

adjective
medicinal plant (scientific)
8.7
Pronunciation: /ɑːf.ɪ.ˈsɪn.ə.lɪs/ /; / /ɔːf.ɪ.ˈsɪn.ə.lɪs/ (AmE), /ɒf.ɪ.ˈsɪn.ə.lɪs/ (BrE)
used in scientific names to indicate that a plant has recognized medicinal or healing properties

Examples

officinalis plant name
The species name officinalis is often given to plants with traditional medicinal uses.
Lavandula officinalis is valued for its calming and healing properties.
When you see officinalis in a plant's scientific name, it usually means it was used in medicine.
