red maple

noun
a North American maple tree with red autumn leaves
3.4 2.3
Pronunciation: /red ˈmeɪ.pəl/ (AmE), /red ˈmeɪ.pəl/ (BrE)
a deciduous tree of eastern and central North America with five-lobed leaves that turn scarlet and yellow in autumn
