myotactic reflex

noun
muscle contraction in response to tendon stretch
9.5 7.0
Pronunciation: /maɪ.oʊ.ˈtæk.tɪk ˈriː.fleks/ /; / /maɪ.ə.ˈtæk.tɪk ˈriː.fleks/ (AmE), /maɪ.ə.ˈtæk.tɪk ˈriː.fleks/ /; / /maɪ.əʊ.ˈtæk.tɪk ˈriː.fleks/ (BrE)
a reflex contraction of a muscle in response to its tendon being stretched, important in maintaining posture

Examples

The myotactic reflex test
A healthy myotactic reflex is crucial for maintaining an upright posture.
Damage to certain nerves can impair the myotactic reflex, leading to muscle weakness.
The doctor tested the patient's myotactic reflex by tapping just below the kneecap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tendon reflex - reflex contraction of a muscle when its tendon is tapped or stretched
