stigma

noun
diagnostic skin lesion
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪɡmə / (AmE), / ˈstɪɡmə / (BrE)
a mark or lesion on the skin that is a diagnostic sign of a particular disease

Examples

Skin stigma
Certain diseases produce a characteristic stigma that aids in their diagnosis.
The doctor identified a stigma on the patient's hand that indicated the presence of the infection.
The sudden appearance of a stigma prompted further medical testing.
