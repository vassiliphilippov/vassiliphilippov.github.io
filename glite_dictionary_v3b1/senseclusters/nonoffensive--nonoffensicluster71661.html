nonoffensive

adjective
not causing offense
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈfɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
not likely to cause offense or upset anyone

Examples

Nonoffensive language
The teacher used nonoffensive language to make sure everyone felt comfortable in class.
They chose a nonoffensive color scheme for the office to appeal to all employees.
His comments were completely nonoffensive and did not upset anyone in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inoffensive - not likely to offend or upset anyone
  • antonym: offensive - causing someone to feel hurt, upset, or angry
