salt-free diet

noun
diet without salt for health reasons
Pronunciation: / sɔlt fri ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / sɒlt fri ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that restricts or eliminates the consumption of salt, often prescribed for health reasons such as hypertension or fluid retention

Examples

A salt-free diet
After his diagnosis, he switched to a salt-free diet to manage his blood pressure.
Her doctor recommended a salt-free diet to help reduce fluid retention.
Many packaged foods are not suitable for a salt-free diet due to their high sodium content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: low-sodium diet - a diet that limits the intake of sodium
