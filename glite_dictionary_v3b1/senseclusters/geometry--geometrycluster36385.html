geometry

noun
branch of mathematics dealing with shapes and space
Pronunciation: / dʒiˈɑːmətri / (AmE), / dʒiˈɒmətri / (BrE)
The branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relationships of points, lines, shapes, and surfaces.

Examples

Basic geometry concepts
In eighth grade, we started learning about the basics of geometry in math class.
She has always been fascinated by geometry and enjoys solving complex problems with shapes.
The architect used his knowledge of geometry to design the impressive building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trigonometry - the branch of mathematics dealing with the relations of the sides and angles of triangles
