amorphophallus

noun
a plant genus with large, pungent flowers
9.2
Pronunciation: / əˌmɔrfəˈfæləs / (AmE), / əˌmɔːfəˈfaləs / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Amorphophallus, known for its large inflorescence and, in some species, a strong odor resembling rotting flesh.
