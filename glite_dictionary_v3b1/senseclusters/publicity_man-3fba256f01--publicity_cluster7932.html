publicity man

noun
man employed to arrange publicity
Pronunciation: / pəˈblɪsəti mæn / (AmE), / pʌbˈlɪsəti mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to arrange publicity for a company, public figure, or event

Examples

Publicity man manages image
As a publicity man, he skillfully managed the company's image during the crisis.
The publicity man arranged several interviews to boost the candidate's visibility.
The film studio hired a new publicity man to generate buzz for their upcoming release.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press agent - a person employed to promote or give publicity to a person, organization, or event
  • synonym: publicist - a person responsible for generating publicity for a person, product, or company
