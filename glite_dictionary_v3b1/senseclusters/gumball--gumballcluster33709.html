gumball

noun
small rubber toy ball
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small, round, rubber ball used as a toy, often dispensed from machines or given as a prize to children

Examples

A bouncing gumball
The vending machine released a colorful gumball that bounced across the floor.
Each child received a gumball as a prize after the game.
He collected every gumball he could find from different machines at the arcade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bouncy ball - a small rubber ball that bounces when thrown
  • antonym: marble - a small glass ball used as a toy
