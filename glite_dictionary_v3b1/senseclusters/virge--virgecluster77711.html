virge

noun
thin rod for punishment
Pronunciation: / vɜrdʒ / (AmE), / vɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a thin, flexible rod or switch used for corporal punishment or discipline, especially in the past

Examples

Punishment virge
In old schools, a virge was sometimes used to discipline students who misbehaved.
The teacher placed the virge on the desk as a warning to the class.
Historical accounts mention the use of a virge in maintaining order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rod - a straight, thin stick or bar
  • synonym: switch - a slender, flexible shoot or rod used for whipping
