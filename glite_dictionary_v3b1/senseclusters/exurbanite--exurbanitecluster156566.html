exurbanite

noun
suburban commuter
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˈzɜrbəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ɛɡˈzɜːbənaɪt / (BrE)
a person who lives in an area outside a city, especially in a commuter suburb or exurb

Examples

Exurbanites commute daily
Many exurbanites choose to live far from the city but commute daily for work.
The new train line made it easier for exurbanites to reach downtown quickly.
As housing prices rose, more families became exurbanites seeking affordable homes outside the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suburbanite - a person who lives in the suburbs of a city
  • antonym: urbanite - a person who lives in a city
