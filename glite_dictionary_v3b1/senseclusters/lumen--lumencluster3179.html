lumen

noun
inside space of a tubular organ
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈluː.mən / (AmE), / ˈluː.mɛn / (BrE)
the inside space of a tubular structure, such as an artery or intestine, in an organism

Examples

Lumen of an artery
Food passes through the lumen of the digestive tract during digestion.
Inflammation can cause narrowing of the intestinal lumen.
The surgeon examined the lumen of the artery for blockages.
