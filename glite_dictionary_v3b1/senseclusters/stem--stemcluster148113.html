stem

noun
skiing turn by pushing one ski outward
Pronunciation: / stɛm / (AmE), / stɛm / (BrE)
a turn in skiing in which the tail of one ski is pushed outward while the other ski is brought parallel to it, used to control speed or direction

Examples

Stem turn in skiing
Before attempting parallel turns, beginners often rely on the stem technique.
