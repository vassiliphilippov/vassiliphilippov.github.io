thyrse

noun
type of dense flower cluster
9.4 9.9
Pronunciation: /θɜːrs/ (AmE), /θɜːs/ (BrE)
a dense flower cluster, such as that of the lilac or horse chestnut, in which the main axis has a racemose structure and the branches are cymose
