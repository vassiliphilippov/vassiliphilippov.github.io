psychopath

noun
person with antisocial personality disorder
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkəˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkəpæθ / (BrE)
a person suffering from a severe antisocial personality disorder, marked by lack of empathy and remorse, often displaying violent or manipulative behavior

Examples

A psychopath person
Experts warn that a skilled psychopath can be very charming and difficult to detect.
She read a book about the mind of a psychopath and its impact on society.
The court ruled that the psychopath was unfit to stand trial due to his mental condition.
