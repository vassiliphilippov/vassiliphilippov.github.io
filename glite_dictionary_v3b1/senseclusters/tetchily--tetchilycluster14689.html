tetchily

adverb
in an irritable or touchy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈtɛtʃɪli / (BrE)
In a touchy, irritable, or easily annoyed manner.

Examples

To tetchily refuse
He tetchily refused to answer any more questions.
She responded tetchily when asked about the incident.
The manager tetchily dismissed their concerns, clearly in a bad mood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or untroubled manner
  • synonym: irritably - in an annoyed or easily angered manner
