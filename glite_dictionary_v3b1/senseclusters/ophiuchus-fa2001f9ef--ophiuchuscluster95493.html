Ophiuchus

proper noun
proposed thirteenth zodiac sign

Pronunciation: /oʊ.fɪ.ˈjuː.kəs/ /; / /ɑː.fɪ.ˈjuː.kəs/ (AmE), /ɒ.fɪ.ˈjuː.kəs/ /; / /ə.fɪ.ˈjuː.kəs/ (BrE)
(in astrology, informal) a proposed thirteenth sign of the zodiac, based on the Sun's passage through the constellation Ophiuchus and sometimes used to assign birthdates to this sign.

Examples

Ophiuchus zodiac sign
Some horoscopes claim that people born between November 29 and December 17 fall under Ophiuchus.
