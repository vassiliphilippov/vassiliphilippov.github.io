hostly

adjective
welcoming like a host
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstli / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a host, especially in being welcoming or hospitable

Examples

A hostly welcome
She offered a hostly reception to everyone who entered her home.
His hostly manner made the guests feel comfortable and appreciated.
The hostly atmosphere at the inn encouraged travelers to return year after year.
