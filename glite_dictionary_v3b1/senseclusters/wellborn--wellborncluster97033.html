Examples

Synonyms

  • highborn
    7.5 0.11/m
    highborn describes someone born into an old aristocratic family, while wellborn means born into the upper class.
  • highbred
    7.4 0.01/m
    highbred implies noble birth and often refined manners, while wellborn focuses on being born into an upper-class family.
  • noble
    4.4 4.75/m
    noble often names people with hereditary title or high rank, while wellborn simply means born into the upper social classes.
  • aristocratic
    7.2 3.38/m
    aristocratic describes social position and manners linked to the aristocracy, while wellborn refers specifically to being of upper-class birth.
  • blue-blooded
    8.2 0.10/m
    blue-blooded sounds informal and paints a family of old noble blood, while wellborn is a neutral term for upper-class birth.
