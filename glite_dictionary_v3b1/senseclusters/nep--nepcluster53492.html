nep

verb
to form fabric knots
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɛp / (AmE), / nɛp / (BrE)
to form small tangled knots or lumps on wool, yarn, or fabric, or to cause material to become covered with such knots

Examples

To nep the wool
If you wash this delicate scarf too roughly, it may nep and lose its smooth texture.
The old wool blanket began to nep after years of use and washing.
Manufacturers try to prevent fabric from nepping during the production process.
