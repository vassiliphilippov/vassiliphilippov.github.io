absorbent cotton

noun
cotton treated to increase absorbency
1.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrbənt ˈkɑtn̩ / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbənt ˈkɒtn̩ / (BrE)
cotton that has been made absorbent by removing its natural wax, commonly used for medical or hygiene purposes

Examples

Absorbent cotton roll
He purchased a roll of absorbent cotton from the pharmacy for first aid at home.
Make sure to use only absorbent cotton to soak up the excess liquid.
The nurse cleaned the wound with absorbent cotton before applying a bandage.

Related Terms
