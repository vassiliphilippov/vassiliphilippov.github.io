undevelopable

adjective
not able to develop
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpəbəl /; / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpəbl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpəbəl /; / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpəbl / (BrE)
not able to be developed, improved, or built upon (especially land or property)

Examples

Undevelopable land
The land was considered undevelopable due to its steep slopes and unstable soil.
After several tests, the scientist declared the cell sample undevelopable for further research.
Some regions remain undevelopable because of environmental protection laws.
Due to environmental regulations, the wetland area is considered undevelopable.
The rocky terrain made the plot of land completely undevelopable for housing projects.
Investors avoided the undevelopable property because it could not be built on.
