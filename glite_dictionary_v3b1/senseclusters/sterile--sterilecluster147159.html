sterile

adjective
unable to reproduce
5.3 1.03/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆstÉ›rÉ™l / (AmE), / ËˆstÉ›rÉªl / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) unable to produce offspring; incapable of reproducing.

Examples

Sterile animal
After years of trying, the couple learned that one of them was sterile.
Some plants are bred to be sterile, so they cannot produce seeds.
The vet confirmed that the cat was sterile and could not have kittens.

Synonyms

  • infertile
    5.2 1.62/m
    infertile is a medical word used for people or animals who cannot have babies, while sterile is used more widely, including lab items.
  • barren
    6.8 0.51/m
    barren is often used for women, female animals, or land that cannot produce crops or young, while sterile means unable to reproduce.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fertile - able to produce offspring or support reproduction
  • synonym: infertile - unable to reproduce or conceive offspring
