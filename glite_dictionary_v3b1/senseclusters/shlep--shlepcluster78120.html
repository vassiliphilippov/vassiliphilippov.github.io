to carry, drag, or move something (or yourself) with effort, especially if it is heavy or awkward

Examples

  • lumber with
    7.7 0.00/m

  • tote
    6.0 2.34/m

  • pull along
    3.0 0.46/m
    pull along means to move something by dragging it with effort, while shlep focuses on carrying or moving awkwardly.
  • haul
    2.5 9.16/m

  • drag
