Borg

proper noun
Star Trek cyborg collective
Pronunciation: / bɔːrɡ / (AmE), / bɔːɡ / (BrE)
a fictional collective of cyborgs in the Star Trek universe known for assimilating other species and technology

Examples

Borg collective
The Borg are one of the most feared enemies in the Star Trek series.
Captain Picard was once assimilated by the Borg and became Locutus.
The Borg believe that resistance is futile and seek to add all life to their collective.

  • synonym: cyborg - a being with both organic and biomechatronic body parts
