incense

verb
to perfume with incense
7.8 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsɛns / (AmE), / ˈɪnsɛns / (BrE)
to perfume or make fragrant with incense, especially as part of a religious ceremony

Examples

To incense a temple

The priest began to incense the altar with slow, deliberate movements.
They traditionally incense the temple before the evening prayers.
