kittiwake

noun
a small northern gull species
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtiˌweɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪtɪweɪk / (BrE)
a small gull of northern coastal regions, with pearl-grey plumage, that nests on cliffs

Examples

A kittiwake gull
Large colonies of kittiwakes can be found nesting on sea cliffs each spring.
The distinctive call of the kittiwake echoed along the rocky coastline.
We watched a kittiwake swooping gracefully above the churning waves.
