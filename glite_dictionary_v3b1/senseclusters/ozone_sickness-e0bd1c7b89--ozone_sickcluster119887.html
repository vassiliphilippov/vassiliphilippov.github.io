ozone sickness

noun
illness from ozone exposure, esp. at high altitude
6.1 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊzoʊn ˈsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈəʊzəʊn ˈsɪknɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition caused by exposure to ozone, especially during high-altitude flight, marked by symptoms such as sleepiness, headache, chest pain, and itchiness

Examples

Ozone sickness symptoms
Pilots in high-altitude jets have sometimes experienced ozone sickness during extended flights.
Precautions are taken on long flights to minimize the risk of ozone sickness among crew members.
Symptoms of ozone sickness include headache, chest discomfort, and drowsiness.
