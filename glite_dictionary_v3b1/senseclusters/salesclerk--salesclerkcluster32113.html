salesclerk

noun
salesperson working in a store
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlzˌklɝk / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzˌklɑːk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sell goods to customers in a store

Examples

The salesclerk helps customers
After asking the salesclerk for advice, he decided on the more expensive headphones.
The salesclerk helped me find the perfect dress for the party.
The salesclerk was restocking shelves when the customer walked in.
