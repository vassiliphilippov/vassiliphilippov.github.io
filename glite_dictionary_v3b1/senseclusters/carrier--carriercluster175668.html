Examples

Disease carrier

Genetic counseling can help identify if someone is a carrier of inherited disorders.

Synonyms

  • transmitter
    6.2 0.23/m
    a transmitter spreads disease between people directly, while a carrier often has the infection without showing any symptoms and passes it on.
  • vector
    6.2 2.21/m

  • harborer
    8.1 0.00/m
    a harborer carries parasites or pests on their body and spreads them, while a carrier often has an infection inside without clear symptoms.
