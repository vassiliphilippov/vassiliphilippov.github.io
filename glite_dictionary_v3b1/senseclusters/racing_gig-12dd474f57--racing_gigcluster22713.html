racing gig

noun
light narrow racing boat for rowing
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsɪŋ ɡɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ ɡɪɡ / (BrE)
A light, narrow rowing boat designed for racing, typically crewed by two or more oarsmen.

Examples

A racing gig boat
He rowed in a traditional wooden racing gig during the annual competition.
Several racing gigs lined up at the estuary, their crews preparing for the challenge ahead.
The crew swiftly launched the racing gig into the water before the start of the regatta.

  • synonym: shell - a long, narrow, competitive rowing boat
