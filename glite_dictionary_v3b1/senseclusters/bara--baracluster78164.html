bara

noun
absolute pressure unit
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.rə/ /; / /ˈbæ.rə/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.rə/ /; / /ˈbæ.rə/ (BrE)
a unit of pressure equal to one bar measured relative to a perfect vacuum, used in technical contexts

Examples

Bara pressure reading
The sensor displayed a reading of 2.5 bara after the system was pressurized.
In technical documents, pressure is often specified in bara to avoid confusion.
The vessel must withstand at least 10 bara during normal operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a metric unit of pressure equal to 100,000 pascals
  • antonym: barg - a unit of pressure equal to one bar above atmospheric pressure
