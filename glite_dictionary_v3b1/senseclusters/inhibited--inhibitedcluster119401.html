inhibited

adjective
held back or restrained
6.0 0.00/m

Unable to act in a relaxed and natural way because of self-consciousness or restraint; prevented from developing or expressing fully.

Examples

To feel inhibited socially
Alcohol can make people less inhibited, leading them to act more openly in social situations.
She felt inhibited in the presence of her boss and could not express her ideas freely.
Some children are inhibited by shyness and have trouble making friends at school.
