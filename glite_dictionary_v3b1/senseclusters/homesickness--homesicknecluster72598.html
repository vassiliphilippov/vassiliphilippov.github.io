homesickness

noun
feeling of missing home
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌsɪknɪs / (BrE)
a feeling of sadness and longing for home when away from it

Examples

Homesickness feeling
His homesickness faded as he grew more comfortable with his surroundings.
Many travelers experience homesickness during their first weeks abroad.
She felt a deep homesickness after moving to a new city for college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nostalgia - a sentimental longing for the past or for a period of time in one's life
