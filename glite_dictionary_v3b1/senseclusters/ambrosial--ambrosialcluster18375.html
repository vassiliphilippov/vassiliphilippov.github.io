ambrosial

adjective
extremely pleasing; delicious and fragrant
Pronunciation: / æmˈbroʊʒiəl / (AmE), / æmˈbrəʊziəl / (BrE)
extremely pleasing to the senses (especially taste or smell); delicious, fragrant, or heavenly
