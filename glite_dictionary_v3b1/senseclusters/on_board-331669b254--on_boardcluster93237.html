on board

adverb
aboard a ship, plane, or vehicle

Examples

Synonyms

  • aboard
    1.0 8.59/m

  • onboard
    3.3 2.14/m
    onboard is often an adjective meaning part of the vehicle or its equipment, while on board is the phrase for being physically on it.
