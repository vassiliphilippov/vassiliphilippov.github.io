imperative form

noun

the verb form or mood used to give commands, requests, or instructions

Examples

The imperative form of a verb
In the sentence "Please close the door," "close" is in the imperative form.
Teachers often use the imperative form when instructing students to do homework.
The imperative form is used in English to issue direct commands such as "Sit down!".
