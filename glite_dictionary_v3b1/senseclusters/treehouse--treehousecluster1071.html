treehouse

noun
children's play structure
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtriˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtriːhaʊs / (BrE)
a small house or shelter built in the branches of a tree, used for play, relaxation, or as a unique place to stay

Examples

They rented a cozy treehouse for a weekend escape in the woods.
