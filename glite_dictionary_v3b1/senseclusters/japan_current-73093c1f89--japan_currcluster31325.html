Japan current

noun
warm ocean current near Japan
8.5
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæn ˈkɝːənt / (AmE), / dʒəˈpan ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a warm ocean current that flows northeastward off the coast of Japan into the northern Pacific Ocean

Examples

The Japan current
As the Japan current mixes with colder waters, it creates unique weather patterns.
Fishermen depend on the Japan current for its abundant marine life.
The Japan current influences the climate along the eastern coast of Japan.
