remelt

noun
re-melted material
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌmɛlt / (AmE), / ˈriːˌmɛlt / (BrE)
material, especially metal or glass, that has been melted again for reuse or further processing

Examples

Molten remelt
The factory blended the old scrap with the remelt to produce uniform metal ingots.
After the initial casting, any leftover metal was collected as remelt for future use.
Glass manufacturers often use remelt to reduce waste and save on production costs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: raw material - basic material used in manufacturing processes
  • synonym: scrap - waste material, especially metal, suitable for recycling
