polygonally

adverb
in a polygonal manner
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪɡənəli / (AmE), / pəˈlɪɡənəli / (BrE)
In a manner resembling or relating to a polygon; having the characteristics or form of a polygon.

Examples

Cluster polygonally
In the experiment, cells began to multiply and cluster polygonally across the surface.
The tiles were arranged polygonally to create an intricate geometric pattern.
The wall was decorated polygonally, giving the room a unique architectural style.
