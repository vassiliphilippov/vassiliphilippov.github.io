graphic design

noun
art of visual communication using text and images
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪk dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪk dɪˈzaɪn /; / ˈɡrafɪk dɪˈzʌɪn / (BrE)
the art and practice of creating visual content by combining text and images to communicate ideas or information

Examples

Good graphic design
Good graphic design can make information much easier to understand.
He studied graphic design at university and now works for an advertising agency.
The new magazine has a fresh graphic design that appeals to younger readers.
Many companies hire experts in graphic design to improve their brand identity.
She decided to pursue a career in graphic design after high school.
The website's graphic design makes it easy to navigate and visually appealing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graphic artist - an artist who specializes in graphic design
  • synonym: illustration - a picture illustrating a book, newspaper, etc.
  • degree lower: industrial design - the design of mass-produced products
  • synonym: visual communication - conveying ideas and information through visual elements
