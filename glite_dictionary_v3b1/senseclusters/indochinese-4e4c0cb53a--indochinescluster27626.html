Indochinese

noun
person from Indochina
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndoʊˈtʃaɪniz / (AmE), / ˌɪndəʊˈtʃaɪniːz / (BrE)
a person from Indochina or of Indochinese descent, especially from Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia, or nearby regions
