andante

adverb
moderately slow tempo in music
6.4
Pronunciation: / ænˈdɑn.teɪ / (AmE), / ænˈdæn.teɪ / (BrE)
(in music) at a moderately slow tempo, typically used as a direction to play a passage at a walking pace.

Examples

Play andante
Her piano teacher instructed her to play the piece andante.
The orchestra played the introduction andante, creating a calm and steady atmosphere.
The second movement should be performed andante to convey its gentle character.
