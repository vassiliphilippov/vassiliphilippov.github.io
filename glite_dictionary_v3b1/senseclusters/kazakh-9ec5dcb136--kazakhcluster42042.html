Kazakh

proper noun
person or language of Kazakhstan
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈzɑːk / (AmE), / ˈkæzæk / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Kazakhstan, or the Turkic language spoken in Kazakhstan

The Kazakh language uses a modified Cyrillic alphabet in official documents.
