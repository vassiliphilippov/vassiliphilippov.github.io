third party

noun
person/group not directly involved in a transaction
1.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /θɜːrd ˈpɑːr.tiː/ (AmE), /θɜːd ˈpɑː.tiː/ (BrE)
a person or group besides the two primarily involved in a situation, especially in a transaction or dispute.

Examples

A third party company
If you share your data with a third party, you must have consent from the user.
The contract specifies what happens if a third party becomes involved in the dispute.
The insurance policy will compensate a third party in case of an accident.
