shuffler

noun
device/software that randomizes playback
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈʃʌf.lər/ (AmE), /ˈʃʌf.lə/ (BrE)
a device or software feature that plays items (for example, songs) in a random order rather than a fixed sequence.

Examples

Music shuffler
The streaming service added a shuffler feature to randomize tracks during playback.
My portable speaker's shuffler broke, so songs now play in album order on trips.
During the party they used several shufflers to create unpredictable music mixes for dancing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: randomizer - a tool or function that produces a random or unpredictable order
  • synonym: shuffle - a mode or action that randomizes the order of items in a list or playlist
