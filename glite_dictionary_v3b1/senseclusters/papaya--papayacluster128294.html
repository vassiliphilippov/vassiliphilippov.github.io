papaya

noun
tropical fruit-bearing tree or its fruit
Pronunciation: / pəˈpaɪə / (AmE), / pəˈpɑːjə / (BrE)
A tropical American tree or shrub with large deeply lobed leaves, grown for its large oblong yellow or orange fruit with sweet flesh and black seeds.

Examples

Papaya tree leaves
Adding papaya to smoothies gives them a tropical taste and vibrant color.
The papaya tree in our backyard produces fruit all year round.
We sliced a ripe papaya for breakfast and enjoyed its sweet, juicy flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pawpaw - another name for papaya (the tropical fruit)
