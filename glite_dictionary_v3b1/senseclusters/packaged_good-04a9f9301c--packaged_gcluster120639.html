packaged good

noun
pre-packaged consumer product
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpækɪdʒd ɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈpakɪdʒd ɡʊd / (BrE)
A product, especially food or consumer item, that is sold in a pre-packaged form for convenience and distribution.

Examples

A packaged good item
Many packaged goods are labeled with nutritional information to help consumers make healthy choices.
Some shoppers prefer fresh ingredients over packaged goods because they contain fewer additives.
The company expanded its line of packaged goods to meet growing demand for convenience foods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh produce - fruit and vegetables not processed or packaged
  • synonym: processed food - food that has been altered from its natural state for safety or convenience
