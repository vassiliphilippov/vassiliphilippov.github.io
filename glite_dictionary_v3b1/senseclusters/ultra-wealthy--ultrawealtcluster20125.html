ultra-wealthy

noun
extremely rich people
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈwɛlθi / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈwɛlθi / (BrE)
people who are extremely rich and possess very large amounts of wealth

Examples

The ultra-wealthy invest
The new tax law is expected to affect only the ultra-wealthy in the country.
Many ultra-wealthy individuals invest in art and luxury real estate around the world.
Charities often seek donations from the ultra-wealthy to fund large projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billionaire - a person whose wealth is at least one billion units of currency
  • degree lower: millionaire - a person whose wealth is at least one million units of currency
  • antonym: poor - people who have little or no money or possessions
