bacopa

noun
aquatic plant genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈkoʊpə / (AmE), / bəˈkəʊpə / (BrE)
a genus of aquatic or semi-aquatic plants often grown in aquariums, ponds, or gardens for their small flowers and foliage

Examples

Aquarium bacopa plant
Many aquarium enthusiasts add bacopa to their tanks for its attractive green leaves.
The gardener chose bacopa to border the pond because it thrives in wet soil.
Small white flowers appeared on the bacopa after several weeks of warm weather.
