development

noun
biological growth process
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the process of an individual organism growing and becoming more complex from a simpler form, especially as part of biological growth

Examples

Child development
Genetic mutations can sometimes interfere with normal development in plants and animals.
Researchers studied brain development in infants to understand early learning processes.
The embryo undergoes a remarkable development during the first weeks after fertilization.
