lymphogranuloma venereum

noun
sexually transmitted infectious disease
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪm.foʊ.ɡræˈnjuː.loʊ.ma vəˈnɛri.əm / (AmE), / ˌlɪm.fəʊ.ɡræˈnjuː.lə.mə vɪˈnɪəri.əm / (BrE)
a sexually transmitted infectious disease caused by Chlamydia trachomatis, characterized by genital ulcers and swelling of lymph nodes in the groin
