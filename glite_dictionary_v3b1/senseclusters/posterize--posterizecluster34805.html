posterize

verb
to reduce image colors
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to convert an image so that it has only a few distinct tones or colors, often for artistic effect

Examples

To posterize a photo
The designer decided to posterize the image to create a bold, graphic look for the advertisement.
You can posterize your photos using many free editing apps on your phone.
After you posterize the picture, the details become less realistic but more striking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enhance - to improve or increase the quality of something
  • synonym: simplify - to make something less complex or detailed
