in their right minds

adjective
sane or behaving sensibly
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðɛr raɪt maɪndz / (AmE), / ɪn ðə raɪt maɪndz / (BrE)
behaving in a sensible or sane way; thinking and acting rationally and responsibly.

Examples

People in their right minds
Most people in their right minds would have walked away from the deal.
No one in their right minds would invest in such a risky business venture.
Were you in your right mind when you agreed to do that?

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of their minds - not sane, acting irrationally
