Synonyms

  • interrelated
    4.3 0.8/m
    interrelated is used when parts affect one another directly and depend on each other, while interconnected means being linked.
  • interwoven
    5.5 0.5/m
    interwoven shows parts mixed together in a way that is hard to separate, while interconnected focuses on mutual dependence.
  • intermeshed
    7.5 0.00/m
    intermeshed describes parts tangled tightly like a net, while interconnected means parts are linked and affect each other.
  • interlocking
    4.5 0.05/m
    interlocking refers to parts that fit together closely like gears, while interconnected emphasizes mutual effects and dependence.
  • connected
    2.5 0.00/m
    connected means joined or related in any way, while interconnected stresses that parts affect or depend on one another.
