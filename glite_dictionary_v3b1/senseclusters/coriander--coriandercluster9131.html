Synonyms

  • cilantro
    3.5 5.29/m
    cilantro is the common American name for the plant's fresh, fragrant leaves used in cooking, while coriander is the aromatic plant whose leaves are used as seasoning.
  • Chinese parsley
    2.8 0.01/m
    Chinese parsley acts as an alternate common name in some regions for the same fresh leaves, while coriander names the aromatic cooking herb.
  • coriander plant
    3.5 0.00/m
    coriander plant refers to the whole herb, leaves and seeds included, while coriander often names the same aromatic plant used for fresh leaves.
  • Coriandrum sativum
    5.2 0.00/m
    Coriandrum sativum is the plant's formal scientific name used in gardens and labels, while coriander is the common cooking name for its leaves.
