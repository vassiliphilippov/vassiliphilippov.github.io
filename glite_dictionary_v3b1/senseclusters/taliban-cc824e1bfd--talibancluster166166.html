Taliban

proper noun
Islamic fundamentalist group in Afghanistan
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtæ.lɪˌbɑn / (AmE), / ˈtæ.lɪ.bæn / (BrE)
A fundamentalist Islamic movement that emerged in Afghanistan in the 1990s, taking control of the country and establishing an Islamic government.

Examples

The Taliban movement
Talks between the government and the Taliban have repeatedly stalled over the years.
The Taliban imposed strict rules on society after seizing power in Afghanistan.
The international community closely monitored the return of the Taliban to power.
