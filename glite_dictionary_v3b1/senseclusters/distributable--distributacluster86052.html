distributable

adjective
able to be paid out
5.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈstrɪ.bjə.tə.bəl/ /; / /dɪ.ˈstrɪ.bjuː.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈstrɪ.bjə.tə.bəl/ /; / /dɪ.ˈstrɪ.bjuː.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be divided and paid out, especially referring to profits, assets, or reserves available to shareholders or beneficiaries

Examples

Distributable profits
The company announced that its distributable profits would be shared among all shareholders.
Only distributable reserves can be used to pay dividends to investors.
The accountant explained which assets were distributable under the current regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: non-distributable - not able to be divided or paid out
