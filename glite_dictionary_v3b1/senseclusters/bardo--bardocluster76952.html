bardo

noun
state between lives
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
in Tibetan Buddhism, the intermediate state between death and rebirth

Examples

The bardo state
According to Tibetan tradition, the soul passes through the bardo before its next life.
The concept of bardo is central to many Buddhist teachings about death and rebirth.
She read about the bardo to better understand the journey of the soul after death.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limbo - a state of neglect or an intermediate state between two conditions
