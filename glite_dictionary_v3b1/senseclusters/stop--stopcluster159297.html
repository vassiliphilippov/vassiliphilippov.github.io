stop

noun
a place where something pauses or halts
1.0 25/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘p / (AmE), / stÉ’p / (BrE)
a place where a vehicle, person, or process pauses or halts, especially along a route or journey

Examples

A bus stop
The bus made an unexpected stop in the middle of the journey.
Their last stop before reaching the coast was a small fishing village.
We only have time for a quick stop at the grocery store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: station - a designated place where trains or buses regularly stop
  • synonym: terminal - a place where a journey ends or begins, especially for public transport
