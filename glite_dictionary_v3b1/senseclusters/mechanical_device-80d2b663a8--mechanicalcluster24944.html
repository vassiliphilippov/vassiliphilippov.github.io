mechanical device

noun
machine or apparatus with moving parts
Pronunciation: / məˈkænɪkəl dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / məˈkænɪkəl dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A machine or apparatus that operates using mechanical principles, often involving moving parts to perform a specific function.

Examples

A mechanical device invention
He invented a clever mechanical device for opening bottles quickly.
Routine maintenance ensures that each mechanical device in the factory operates smoothly.
The clock is a classic example of a mechanical device used to measure time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machine - an apparatus using mechanical power to accomplish work
  • synonym: mechanism - a system of parts working together in a machine
