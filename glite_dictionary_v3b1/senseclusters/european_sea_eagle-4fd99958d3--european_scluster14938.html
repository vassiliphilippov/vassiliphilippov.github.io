European sea eagle

noun
large European and Greenlandic eagle species
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən si ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən siː ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large greyish-brown eagle with a short wedge-shaped white tail, found in Europe and Greenland

Examples

The European sea eagle
Conservation efforts have helped increase the European sea eagle population in several countries.
Fishermen sometimes spot a European sea eagle swooping low over the water in search of fish.
The European sea eagle is one of the largest birds of prey in Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: white-tailed eagle - another name for the European sea eagle, Haliaeetus albicilla
