parceling

noun
distribution by dividing into parts or shares
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrsəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of dividing or distributing something into parts or shares, typically according to an arrangement or plan
