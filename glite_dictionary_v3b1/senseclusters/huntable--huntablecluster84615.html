huntable

adjective
able to be hunted
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhʌntəbəl / (BrE)
able or permitted to be hunted, especially according to law or regulation

Examples

A huntable animal

The law clearly states which animals are huntable and which are protected.
