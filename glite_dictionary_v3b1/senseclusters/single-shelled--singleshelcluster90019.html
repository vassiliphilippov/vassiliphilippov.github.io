single-shelled

adjective
having a single shell (univalve)
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl ˌʃɛld / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˌʃɛld / (BrE)
Having a single shell; univalve, especially in reference to mollusks or similar organisms.

Examples

Single-shelled mollusks
Marine biologists studied the single-shelled gastropods found in the tidal pools.
Most snails are single-shelled creatures known as univalves.
The museum display included both single-shelled and bivalve mollusks for comparison.
