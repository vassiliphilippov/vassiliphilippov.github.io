A group of single-celled, microscopic organisms that are typically motile and feed on organic matter, including flagellates, ciliates, sporozoans, and amoebas.

Examples

Scientists observed the protozoa moving rapidly under the microscope.
The pond water sample contained several species of protozoa and bacteria.
