nincompoop

noun
foolish or silly person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪn.kəmˌpup / (AmE), / ˈnɪn.kəmˌpuːp / (BrE)
a person who is silly, foolish, or lacking in sense

Examples

A silly nincompoop
He called his brother a nincompoop for forgetting the keys again.
Only a nincompoop would try to fix an electrical problem without turning off the power.
The plan was so flawed that even a nincompoop could see it wouldn't work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking intelligence or common sense
