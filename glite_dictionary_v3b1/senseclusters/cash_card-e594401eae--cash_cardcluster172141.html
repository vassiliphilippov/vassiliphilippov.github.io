cash card

noun
card for withdrawing cash or making payments
4.2 2.2
Pronunciation: /kæʃ kɑːrd/ (AmE), /kæʃ kɑːd/ (BrE)
a payment card that allows the holder to withdraw cash or make electronic payments, typically from an ATM or at point-of-sale terminals

Examples

Most banks now issue a cash card to new account holders.
She used her cash card to withdraw money from the ATM.
