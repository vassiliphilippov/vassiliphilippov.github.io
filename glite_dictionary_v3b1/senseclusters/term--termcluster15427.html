term

noun
statue or bust on a square pillar (architecture)
6.0 8.0
Pronunciation: /tɜːrm/ (AmE), /tɜːm/ (BrE)
(Architecture) A statue or human bust, or sometimes an animal, carved from the top part of a square pillar, originally used as a boundary marker in ancient Rome.
