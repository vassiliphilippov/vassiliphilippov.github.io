miscreant

noun
person behaving badly or illegally
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌkriənt / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.krɪ.ənt / (BrE)
a person who behaves badly, breaks the law, or has no moral principles

Examples

A miscreant breaks laws
Every society must find ways to deal with miscreants who threaten the peace.
The judge delivered a harsh sentence to the young miscreant.
The police quickly apprehended the miscreant who had vandalized the school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: saint - a person of great holiness, virtue, or benevolence
  • synonym: villain - a wicked or criminal person
