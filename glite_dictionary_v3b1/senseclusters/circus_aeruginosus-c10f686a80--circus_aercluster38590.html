Circus aeruginosus

noun
Old World marsh harrier species
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkəs ɛˌruːdʒɪˈnoʊsəs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kəs ɪˌruː.dʒɪˈnəʊ.səs / (BrE)
A large Old World harrier (western marsh harrier) that inhabits marshy regions of Europe, Asia, and northern Africa.
