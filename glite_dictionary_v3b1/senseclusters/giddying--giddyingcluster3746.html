giddying

adjective
causing dizziness
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪdiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪdiɪŋ / (BrE)
causing dizziness, excitement, or a feeling of being overwhelmed (often because of height, speed, or strong emotion)
