frizzen

noun
flintlock gun part
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪzən / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzən / (BrE)
the metal part of a flintlock gun that produces sparks when struck by the flint

Examples

The frizzen of gun
The gunsmith carefully polished the frizzen to ensure the musket would fire reliably.
Without a working frizzen, the flintlock rifle could not produce the necessary spark.
He replaced the worn frizzen before heading out on the hunting trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flint - a hard stone used to produce sparks in a flintlock gun
  • synonym: hammer - the part of a gun that strikes the firing mechanism
