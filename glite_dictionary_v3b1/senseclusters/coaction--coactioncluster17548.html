coaction

noun
joint action; collaboration
Pronunciation: / koʊˈækʃən / (AmE), / kəʊˈækʃən / (BrE)
the act of working together with others to achieve a common goal

Examples

Effective coaction among groups
Community improvement is possible through effective coaction among local organizations.
Successful research often requires the coaction of scientists from different disciplines.
The two companies entered into coaction to develop a new software platform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the action of working with someone to produce something
  • synonym: cooperation - the process of working together for a common purpose
