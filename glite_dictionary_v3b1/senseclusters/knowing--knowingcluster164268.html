knowing

adjective
intentional; done deliberately
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
done with full awareness, intention, or deliberate purpose; intentional

Examples

Knowing action
He gave a knowing nod, fully aware of the consequences.
Her knowing actions showed clear intent to break the rules.
The company made a knowing decision to ignore the complaint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned or intended
  • synonym: intentional - done on purpose or with intent
