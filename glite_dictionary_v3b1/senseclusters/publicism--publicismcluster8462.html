publicism

noun
study of public affairs
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the study or practice of public affairs, journalism, or public policy

Examples

Publicism studies
She decided to study publicism because she was interested in media and government.
A background in publicism can lead to careers in journalism or public policy.
The university offers a new course in publicism focusing on modern communication strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalism - the activity of gathering, assessing, and presenting news and information
  • synonym: public relations - the practice of managing communication between an organization and the public
