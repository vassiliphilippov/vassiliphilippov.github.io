cashless

adjective
without physical money
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkæʃ.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈkæʃ.ləs/ (BrE)
not having any physical cash or money in the form of coins or bills available.

Examples

Cashless after shopping

Many modern stores now accept only cashless payments like credit cards or mobile apps.
Travelers often find themselves cashless in countries where digital payments are common.
