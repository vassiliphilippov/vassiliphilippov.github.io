ult

noun
gaming ultimate ability
9.6
Pronunciation: /ʌlt/ (AmE), /ʌlt/ (BrE)
In online gaming, an abbreviation of 'ultimate': a character's most powerful ability or move, typically with limited uses or a long cooldown.
