room

noun
opportunity or scope for
1.0 56/m
Pronunciation: / ruÀêm /; / rum / (AmE), / ruÀêm / (BrE)
space that is available for movement, action, or opportunity for something to happen or be done

Examples

Room for improvement
The report showed room for improvement in several areas.
There is still room for negotiation in the contract.
We need to leave some room for flexibility in the plan.
In the tiny kitchen, there was barely room to turn around.
Please move your bags so I have room to sit down.
There isn't enough room in the car for five people.

Synonyms

  • space
    1.0 136/m
    space is a general empty area used for many purposes, while room is the available area for movement or action.
  • elbow room
    4.1 0.26/m
    elbow room means enough space to move or act freely, while room simply means space available to do something.
  • breathing room
    3.8 0.57/m
    breathing room gives time or freedom to relax and plan, while room mainly names the space where actions can happen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capacity - the ability to hold or contain something
  • antonym: crowded - having little space for movement due to too many people or things
  • synonym: scope - opportunity or possibility to do or achieve something
  • synonym: space - the opportunity for something to exist or happen
