filmy fern

noun
tropical fern with translucent leaves
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmi fɝn / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmi fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical fern with very thin, translucent leaves or fronds, often found growing on trees or mossy banks
