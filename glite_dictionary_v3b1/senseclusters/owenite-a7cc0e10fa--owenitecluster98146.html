Owenite

adjective
of Robert Owen's ideas
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊənaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊənaɪt / (BrE)
relating to Robert Owen or his social and economic ideas, especially cooperative communities and social reform

Examples

Owenite ideas
The Owenite movement inspired many early experiments in communal living.
Her Owenite ideals shaped her approach to education and social welfare.
Several Owenite communities were established in the 19th century to promote cooperation and equality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialist - relating to the political or economic theory of socialism
  • synonym: utopian - relating to idealistic or visionary social reform
