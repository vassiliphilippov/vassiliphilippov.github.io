stang

noun
long carrying pole
Pronunciation: / stæŋ / (AmE), / stæŋ / (BrE)
an old term for a long pole or bar, especially one used for carrying loads across the shoulders

Examples

A stang for buckets
The villagers carried water buckets on a stang across their shoulders.
He balanced the heavy load using a wooden stang during the festival.
In olden times, a stang was often used to transport goods over rough terrain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pole - a long, slender piece of wood or metal
  • synonym: yoke - a wooden bar used to carry loads or harness animals
