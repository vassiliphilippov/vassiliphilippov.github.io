tooth

noun
projection on a gear or similar part
3.4
Pronunciation: / tuθ / (AmE), / tuːθ / (BrE)
a part or projection on an object, such as a gear or saw, that resembles a tooth in shape or function

Examples

Gear tooth
A broken tooth in a clockwork mechanism can stop the entire system from working.
Over time, the teeth on the sprocket can become worn and affect performance.
The tooth of the gear must be properly shaped to ensure smooth motion.
Each tooth on the gear must align perfectly for smooth operation.
The tooth of the saw needs to be replaced after heavy use.
The zipper was missing a tooth, making it difficult to close.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prong - a pointed projection, often on a fork or other tool
