paste

noun
lead glass used for jewelry imitation
Pronunciation: / peɪst / (AmE), / peɪst / (BrE)
a hard, glassy material made from lead glass, used especially to imitate gemstones in artificial jewelry

Examples

Paste jewelry
Collectors often seek antique paste jewelry for its historical craftsmanship.
The necklace sparkled with paste jewels that closely resembled real diamonds.
This vintage brooch features beautifully cut paste stones set in silver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhinestone - imitation diamond made from rock crystal or glass
