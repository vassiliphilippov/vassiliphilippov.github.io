centennially

adverb
every hundred years
6.6
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɛniəli / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛniəli / (BrE)
Once every hundred years; at intervals of a century.

Examples

Performed centennially once
The ancient ritual is performed centennially by local villagers.
The archives are opened centennially for public viewing during special ceremonies.
The parade is held centennially to mark the city's historic founding.
