AstroTurf

verb
to install artificial grass
Pronunciation: / ˈæstroʊˌtɜrf / (AmE), / ˈæstrəʊˌtɜːf / (BrE)
to cover a surface, such as a field or playground, with artificial grass or synthetic turf.

Examples

To AstroTurf a playground
The school decided to AstroTurf the football field to reduce water usage.
They will AstroTurf the backyard so the children can play outside year-round.
Many sports facilities choose to AstroTurf their grounds for easier maintenance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pave - to cover a surface with a hard material
  • synonym: turf - to cover with turf or grass
