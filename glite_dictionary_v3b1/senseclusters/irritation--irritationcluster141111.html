irritation

noun
a sudden outburst of anger
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈteɪʃən /; / ɪrɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈteɪʃən /; / ɪrɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of annoyance, anger, or discomfort, or the process of becoming annoyed or angry

Waiting in long queues can be a source of irritation for many people.
