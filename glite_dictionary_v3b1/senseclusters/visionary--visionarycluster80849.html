visionary

noun
person with fanciful or unrealistic ideas
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒənəri / (BrE)
a person who is prone to fanciful ideas or unrealistic plans, with little concern for what is achievable

Examples

A visionary dreamer
He was dismissed as a visionary whose ideas could never work in the real world.
Some critics accused her of being a visionary detached from practical concerns.
The project was abandoned, for even its main supporter was known to be something of a visionary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamer - a person who dreams or has unrealistic ideas
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts things as they are and deals with them practically
