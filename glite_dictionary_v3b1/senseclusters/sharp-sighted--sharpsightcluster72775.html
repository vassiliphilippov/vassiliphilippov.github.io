sharp-sighted

adjective
keenly perceptive or alert
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpˌsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpˌsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
having an unusually keen perception or alertness; able to notice or understand things quickly and clearly

Examples

Sharp-sighted thinker
His sharp-sighted analysis helped the company spot a flaw in the plan.
She proved to be incredibly sharp-sighted when it came to understanding people's motives.
The detective was sharp-sighted, instantly noticing small details others missed.
