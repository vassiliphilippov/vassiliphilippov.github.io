fairness commission

noun
commission ensuring balanced viewpoints
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrnɪs kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeənɪs kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a commission established to ensure that different viewpoints are given equal opportunity for expression

Examples

A fairness commission role
A fairness commission ensures that all parties have access to the media during elections.
Concerns about bias led to the formation of an independent fairness commission.
The government appointed a fairness commission to oversee political debates.
