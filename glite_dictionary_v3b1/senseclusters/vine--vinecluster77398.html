vine

noun
a plant with a climbing or creeping stem
1.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /vaɪn/ (AmE), /vaɪn/ (BrE)
a plant with a long, weak stem that grows along the ground or climbs by twining around other objects for support

Examples

A grape vine
The old house was covered in thick vine that stretched across every wall.
The pumpkin vine crept across the garden, sending out new leaves each day.
We planted a grape vine along the fence to provide shade in the summer.
