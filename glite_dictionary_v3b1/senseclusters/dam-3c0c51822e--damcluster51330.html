DAM

noun
digital asset management system
Pronunciation: / diː eɪ ɛm / (AmE), / diː eɪ ɛm / (BrE)
(initialism) software or a system for storing, organising, managing, and retrieving digital media assets such as images, videos, and design files.

Examples

A DAM system
The marketing team relies on a DAM to manage its digital media library.
Before publishing, designers must upload images into the DAM with accurate metadata.
Our company adopted a cloud-based DAM so teams can quickly find approved logos and videos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: content management system - software that helps create, manage, and publish digital content; sometimes overlaps with DAM for media assets
  • synonym: digital asset management system - software for organising, storing, and retrieving digital media files
