Shoshonean language

noun
Uto-Aztecan language subfamily
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊˈʃoʊniən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ʃəˈʃəʊniːən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A subfamily of Uto-Aztecan languages spoken mainly in the southwestern United States.

Examples
