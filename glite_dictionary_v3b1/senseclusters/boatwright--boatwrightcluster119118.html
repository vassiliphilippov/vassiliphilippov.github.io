boatwright

noun
boat builder
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtˌraɪt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to build or repair boats

Examples

The boatwright builds boats
The boatwright spent months crafting a traditional fishing boat for the village.
As a skilled boatwright, she could repair almost any wooden vessel.
The old harbor still has a working boatwright who keeps the local boats in good shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person who makes and repairs wooden objects
  • synonym: shipwright - a person who builds or repairs ships
