Jatropha curcus

noun
tropical tree with poisonous seeds and dye-producing bark
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈtroʊfə ˈkɜːrkəs / (AmE), / dʒəˈtrəʊfə ˈkɜːkəs / (BrE)
a small tropical American tree whose seeds yield a poisonous purgative oil and whose bark produces a purple dye and tanning extract
