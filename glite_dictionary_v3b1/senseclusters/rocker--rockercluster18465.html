rocker

noun
device for separating gold by rocking
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkər / (AmE), / ˈrɒkə / (BrE)
a device consisting of a trough mounted on rockers, used by gold miners to separate gold from earth by rocking it back and forth in water
