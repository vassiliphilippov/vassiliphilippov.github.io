undoable

adjective
impossible to do
4.9 0.04/m

impossible to accomplish or carry out; not able to be done.

Examples

An undoable task
The project seemed undoable with the limited time and resources available.
Climbing the mountain in one day was simply undoable for most hikers.
They realized the task was undoable without additional help from the team.
