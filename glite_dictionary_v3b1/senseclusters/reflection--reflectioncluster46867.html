reflection

noun
image in a mirror with left and right reversed
2.6 1.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛk.ʃən /; / rəˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛk.ʃən /; / rɪˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
an image you see in a mirror, glass, water, or other shiny surface

Examples

Mirror reflection
A left-handed person appears right-handed in their reflection.

The reflection in the mirror showed the writing backwards, making it hard to read.
He adjusted his tie while looking at his reflection in the bathroom mirror.
She caught her reflection in the shop window as she walked by.
The lake was so calm that the mountains' reflection was almost perfect.
