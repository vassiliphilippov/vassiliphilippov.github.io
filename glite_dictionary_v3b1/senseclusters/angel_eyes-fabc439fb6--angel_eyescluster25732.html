angel eyes

noun
beautiful, innocent eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl aɪz / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl aɪz / (BrE)
eyes that look especially beautiful, innocent, or captivating, often suggesting purity or kindness

Examples

Her angel eyes shine
Everyone noticed her angel eyes when she entered the room, shining with warmth and kindness.
He fell in love with her angel eyes the moment they met at the café.
The child looked up with angel eyes, asking for another bedtime story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doe eyes - large, gentle, and innocent-looking eyes
  • antonym: piercing eyes - eyes that seem to look deeply or intensely at someone
