barebacking

noun
riding without saddle
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌbækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌbækɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of riding a horse without using a saddle

Examples

Barebacking a horse
She enjoyed barebacking through the fields on a sunny afternoon.
Many riders find barebacking helps them improve their balance and connection with the horse.
After years of lessons, he finally tried barebacking for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bareback - without a saddle
  • antonym: saddling - the act of putting a saddle on a horse
