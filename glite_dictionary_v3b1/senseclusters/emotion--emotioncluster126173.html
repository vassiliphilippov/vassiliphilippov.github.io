emotion

noun
strong feeling (e.g., love, anger)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
a strong feeling such as love, anger, joy, hate, or fear

Examples

Strong emotion
He tried to hide his emotion, but everyone could see he was upset.
Her voice was full of emotion as she recounted the story.
Music has the power to stir deep emotion in people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of feeling or emotion
  • synonym: feeling - an emotional state or reaction
