Dengoid infection

noun
infection resembling dengue fever
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛŋɡɔɪd ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɛŋɡɔɪd ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
An infection that resembles dengue fever in its clinical presentation but is not caused by the dengue virus.

Examples

Dengoid infection symptoms
Her persistent high fever led the team to consider the possibility of a Dengoid infection.
Several patients presented symptoms of Dengoid infection during the outbreak last summer.
The doctors diagnosed him with a Dengoid infection after ruling out dengue fever.
