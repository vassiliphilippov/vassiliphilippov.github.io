synthesis

noun
process of creating a chemical compound
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnθəsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪnθəsɪs / (BrE)
the process of producing a chemical compound by combining simpler substances

Examples

Chemical synthesis
