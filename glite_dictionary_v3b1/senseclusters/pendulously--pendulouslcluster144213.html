pendulously

adverb
hanging and swinging
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛndʒələsli / (AmE), / ˈpɛndjʊləsli / (BrE)
in a way that hangs down loosely and swings or moves back and forth like a pendulum

Examples

Sways pendulously
The chandelier swayed pendulously during the earthquake, making everyone nervous.
Long vines hung pendulously from the ancient trees in the jungle.
The old sign creaked as it moved pendulously in the wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dangling - hanging or swinging loosely
  • antonym: rigidly - in a stiff or unmoving way
