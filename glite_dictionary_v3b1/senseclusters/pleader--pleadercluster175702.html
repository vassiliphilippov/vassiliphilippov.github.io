pleader

noun
lawyer who argues cases in court
5.1 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpliːdər / (AmE), / ˈpliːdə / (BrE)
a lawyer who presents and argues cases in a court of law

Examples

A pleader in court
As a skilled pleader, she was known for her persuasive courtroom speeches.
Each pleader must be prepared with strong evidence to support their client's case.
The pleader presented a convincing argument before the judge.
