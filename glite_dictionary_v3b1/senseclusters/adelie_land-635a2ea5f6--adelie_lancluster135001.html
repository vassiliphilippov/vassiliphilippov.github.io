Adelie Land

proper noun
Antarctic coastal region south of Australia
Pronunciation: / əˈdeɪli lænd / (AmE), / əˈdeɪli lænd / (BrE)
a coastal region of Antarctica south of Australia, known for its large colonies of penguins

Examples

Adelie Land coast
A French expedition first claimed Adelie Land in the early twentieth century.
Large colonies of emperor penguins can be found along the coast of Adelie Land.
Researchers set up a temporary base in Adelie Land to study climate change.
