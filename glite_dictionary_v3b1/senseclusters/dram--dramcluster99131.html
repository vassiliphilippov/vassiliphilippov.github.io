dram

noun
small amount of liquid
Pronunciation: / dræm / (AmE), / dræm / (BrE)
a small amount of a liquid, especially a measure of alcoholic spirits

Examples

A dram of whiskey
He poured a dram of whiskey into the glass after dinner.
Would you like a dram of brandy to warm you up?
She savored a dram of fine Scotch by the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nip - a small quantity of strong alcoholic drink
  • synonym: shot - a small serving of spirits
