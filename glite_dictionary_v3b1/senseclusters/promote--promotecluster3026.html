promote

verb
to support growth or development
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊt / (AmE), / prəˈməʊt / (BrE)
to help something develop, increase, or become more successful; to support or actively encourage the progress of something

Examples

To promote good health
Regular exercise can promote better mental health and overall well-being.
The campaign was launched to promote recycling in the community.
The organization aims to promote literacy among children in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encourage - to give support, confidence, or hope to someone or something
  • antonym: hinder - to create difficulties that slow down progress or growth
