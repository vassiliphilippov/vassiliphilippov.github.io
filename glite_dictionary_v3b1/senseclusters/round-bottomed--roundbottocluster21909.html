round-bottomed

adjective
having a rounded bottom
Pronunciation: / ˌraʊndˈbɑməld / (AmE), / ˌraʊndˈbɒmənd / (BrE)
having a bottom that is rounded rather than flat

Examples

A round-bottomed bowl
I bought a round-bottomed mixing bowl for baking bread.
The laboratory used a round-bottomed flask to heat the chemicals evenly.
The vase was round-bottomed, making it easy to roll but hard to stand upright.
