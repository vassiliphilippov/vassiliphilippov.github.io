sealskin tent

noun
tent made of sealskin used by Inuit
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlˌskɪn tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsiːlˌskɪn tɛnt / (BrE)
a tent traditionally made of sealskin, used by Inuit people as a summer dwelling

Examples

A sealskin tent
A sealskin tent provides excellent protection from wind and rain during the milder months.
For centuries, the Inuit constructed sealskin tents as practical summer homes.
The explorers came across a traditional sealskin tent while traveling through the Arctic region.
