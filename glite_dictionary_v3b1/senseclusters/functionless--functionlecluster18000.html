functionless

adjective
without grammatical role
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənləs / (BrE)
having no grammatical or syntactic role; not serving any grammatical function

Examples

Functionless words
In some languages, certain words are completely functionless and do not affect the sentence structure.
The old code contained several functionless commands that could be safely removed.
Linguists debated whether the particle was truly functionless or had a subtle effect on meaning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: functional - having a specific role or purpose
  • synonym: meaningless - having no meaning or significance
