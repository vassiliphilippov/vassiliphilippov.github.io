flavored

adjective
with added taste
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
having a particular taste or flavor added, often by artificial or natural means

Examples

Flavored tea
He bought a box of flavored tea with hints of lemon and ginger.
Many children enjoy flavored milk as a sweet treat after school.
The restaurant offers several flavored syrups to add to your coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seasoned - having spices or flavorings added
