dandle board

noun
playground seesaw board
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdændl bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdændl bɔːd / (BrE)
a board balanced on a central support (fulcrum), ridden up and down as a playground toy by children at either end; also known as a seesaw or teeter-totter

Examples

A dandle board ride
At the old school playground, there was a brightly painted dandle board next to the swings.
Grandpa made a wooden dandle board for the backyard.
The children laughed as they played on the dandle board in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seesaw - a long board balanced in the middle, used as a playground apparatus
  • synonym: teeter-totter - American term for a playground seesaw
