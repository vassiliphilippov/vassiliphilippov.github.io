serpent star

noun
marine animal with slender arms; brittle star
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrpənt stɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɜːpənt stɑː / (BrE)
a marine animal related to starfish, with slender, flexible arms extending from a small central disc; also called a brittle star

Examples

A serpent star animal
During the dive, the marine biologist discovered a serpent star hiding under the rock.
Many aquariums display the serpent star because of its unusual appearance and movement.
The fragile arms of a serpent star can easily break off when touched.
