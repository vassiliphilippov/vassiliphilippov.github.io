cordierite

noun
a blue mineral used as a gemstone
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrdiəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɪəraɪt / (BrE)
a blue mineral composed of magnesium, iron, aluminum, silicon, and oxygen, often used as a gemstone
