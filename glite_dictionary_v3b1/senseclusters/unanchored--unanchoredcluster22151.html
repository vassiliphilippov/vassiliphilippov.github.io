unanchored

adjective
without stability
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈæŋkərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈæŋkəd / (BrE)
not having a firm basis, support, or sense of stability, either physically or emotionally

Examples

Feeling unanchored
After moving to a new city, he felt unanchored and struggled to adjust.
The theory seemed unanchored in any real scientific evidence.
Without her family nearby, she sometimes felt unanchored during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anchored - firmly fixed or supported; having stability
  • synonym: rootless - having no permanent home or sense of belonging
  • synonym: unstable - not steady or secure; likely to change or fall
