spiritualization

noun
making something spiritual
Pronunciation: / spɪrɪtʃuələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌspɪrɪtʃuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / spɪrɪtʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌspɪrɪtjʊəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something spiritual or infusing it with spiritual content

Examples

The spiritualization of life
His poetry is noted for the spiritualization of ordinary experiences.
Many ancient rituals were preserved through a process of spiritualization by later generations.
The spiritualization of their daily routine brought them a sense of peace and purpose.
His teachings focus on the spiritualisation of human relationships and society.
Some religious movements seek the spiritualisation of everyday life.
The spiritualisation of art is a recurring theme in many philosophical discussions.
