pawnable

adjective
can be pawned
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːnəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be given as security for a loan at a pawnshop

Examples

Pawnable items
The shop only accepts pawnable items such as gold, watches, and electronics.
He checked if his old guitar was pawnable before taking it to the store.
Not every valuable object is pawnable at a typical pawnshop.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unpawnable - not able to be given as security at a pawnshop
