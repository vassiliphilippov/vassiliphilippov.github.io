log

noun
segment of a tree trunk or branch
5.8 1.0
Pronunciation: /lɔːg/ /; / /lɑːg/ (AmE), /lɒg/ (BrE)
a section of the trunk or a large branch of a tree, typically after being cut and stripped of its smaller branches

Examples

A log of wood
He split the log into firewood using an axe.
The fireplace was stacked with a large log ready to burn.
They sat on a fallen log by the river and had lunch.
