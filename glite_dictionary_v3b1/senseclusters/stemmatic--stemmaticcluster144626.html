stemmatic

adjective
of or relating to a textual stemma
9.4
Pronunciation: / stɛmˈmætɪk / (AmE), / stɛmˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerning a textual stemma, especially in the study of the relationships among manuscripts or textual versions

Examples

A stemmatic study
A stemmatic approach is essential in reconstructing the original text from fragmented sources.
Her stemmatic research revealed that several copies shared a common ancestor.
The scholar conducted a stemmatic analysis of the medieval manuscripts to determine their relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stemma - a diagram showing the genealogy of manuscripts or texts
