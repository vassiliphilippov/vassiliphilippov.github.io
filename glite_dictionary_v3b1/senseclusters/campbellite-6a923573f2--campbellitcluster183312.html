Campbellite

adjective
Restoration movement
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæmpəlaɪt / (BrE)
relating to the beliefs, practices, or history of the 19th-century Campbellite (Restoration) movement in Christianity

Examples

Campbellite church history
The church's Campbellite traditions emphasize simple worship and congregational autonomy.
Historians studied the Campbellite influence on religious communities in 19th-century America.
A Campbellite approach to scripture focuses on returning to early Christian practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Restorationist - relating to the Christian Restoration movement
