chimichurri

noun
Argentinian green sauce
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɪmiˈtʃʊri / (AmE), / ˌtʃɪmiˈtʃʊəri / (BrE)
a green, herby sauce from Argentina, typically made with parsley, garlic, vinegar, and oil, served with grilled meat

Examples

Green chimichurri sauce
The chef drizzled chimichurri over the grilled steak for extra flavor.
I learned to make authentic chimichurri during my trip to Argentina.
Fresh herbs give chimichurri its vibrant color and distinctive taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pesto - an Italian green sauce made with basil, garlic, pine nuts, and cheese
  • synonym: salsa verde - a green sauce made with herbs, common in Italian and Mexican cuisine
