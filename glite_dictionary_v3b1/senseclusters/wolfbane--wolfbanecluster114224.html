wolfbane

noun
a poisonous Eurasian plant (aconite)
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a poisonous Eurasian perennial plant with broad, rounded leaves, yellow flowers, and a fibrous rootstock, also known as aconite or monkshood

Examples

The wolfbane plant
Researchers studied the toxic compounds found in the root of wolfbane for potential medical applications.
The medieval villagers believed that wolfbane planted around their homes would ward off dangerous animals.
Touching wolfbane can cause skin irritation, so it should always be handled with gloves.
