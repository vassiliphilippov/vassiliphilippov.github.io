wolfbane

noun
a poisonous Eurasian plant (aconite)
9.2 9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊlf.beɪn/ (AmE), /ˈwʊlf.beɪn/ (BrE)
a poisonous Eurasian perennial plant with broad, rounded leaves, yellow flowers, and a fibrous rootstock, also known as aconite or monkshood
