nonirrigation

noun
absence of irrigation
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪrɪˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪrɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
the absence or lack of artificial watering of land or crops

Examples

Nonirrigation farming
Farmers in the area practice nonirrigation agriculture because of scarce water supplies.
The success of nonirrigation farming depends heavily on seasonal rainfall.
Switching to nonirrigation methods can help conserve water in drought-prone regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrigation - the artificial application of water to land or crops
  • synonym: rainfed - relying on natural rainfall for water
