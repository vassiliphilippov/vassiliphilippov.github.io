mill

noun
the act of grinding to powder or dust
Pronunciation: / mɪl / (AmE), / mɪl / (BrE)
the act of grinding something into a powder or fine dust, especially using a mill

Examples

The mill of spices
During the mill of the spices, the aroma filled the room.
The chemist performed a mill to obtain a fine powder for analysis.
The wheat underwent a mill before being used in bread production.
