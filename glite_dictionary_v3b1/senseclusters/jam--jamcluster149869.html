jam

verb
become stuck and unable to move
2.9
Pronunciation: / dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
(of a device or mechanism) to become stuck and unable to move or function properly.

Examples

The door jams
Be careful not to let your fingers get jammed in the door.
Her zipper jammed just before she was about to leave the house.
The printer will jam if you load too much paper at once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free - to release from being stuck or immobilized
  • synonym: stick - to become fixed in one position and unable to move
