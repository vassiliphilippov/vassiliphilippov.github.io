masticatory

adjective
of chewing
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈmæstɪkətəri / (BrE)

Examples

Masticatory muscles
The dentist examined the patient's masticatory muscles for signs of strain.
Certain animals have highly developed masticatory teeth for grinding tough plant material.
Problems with the jaw can affect masticatory function and make eating difficult.
