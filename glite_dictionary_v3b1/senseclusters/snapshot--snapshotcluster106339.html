snapshot

noun
an informal photograph
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈsnæpʃɒt / (BrE)
an informal photograph taken quickly, especially with a hand-held camera

Examples

A snapshot photo
He kept a snapshot of his dog in his wallet.
My favorite snapshot from vacation shows us laughing on the beach.
She took a snapshot of her friends at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photograph - a picture made with a camera
  • antonym: portrait - a painted or photographed representation of a person, usually posed
