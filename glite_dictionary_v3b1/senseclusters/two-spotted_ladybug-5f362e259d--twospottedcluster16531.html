two-spotted ladybug

noun
a red ladybug with a spot on each wing
Pronunciation: / tuˈspɑtɪd ˈleɪdiˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / tuˈspɒtɪd ˈleɪdiˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a species of red ladybug distinguished by a black spot on each of its wing covers

Examples

The two-spotted ladybug insect
The two-spotted ladybug is often seen in gardens feeding on aphids.
Unlike other species, the two-spotted ladybug has only one black spot on each wing cover.
We found a two-spotted ladybug crawling on the rose petals.
