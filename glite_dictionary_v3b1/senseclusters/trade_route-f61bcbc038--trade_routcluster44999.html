trade route

noun
regularly used route for commercial shipping
2.8 3.0
Pronunciation: / treɪd ruːt / (AmE), / treɪd ruːt / (BrE)
a route that is regularly used by traders or merchants to transport and exchange goods, often between regions or countries

Examples
