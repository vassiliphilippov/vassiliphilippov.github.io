winter rat

noun
person who stays indoors in winter
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˌræt / (BrE)
a person who spends most of the winter indoors to avoid the cold

Examples

A winter rat indoors
As a winter rat, he stocks up on books and snacks for the long snowy months.
During January, Jenna admits she turns into a true winter rat and barely goes outside.
My neighbors jokingly call me a winter rat because I refuse to leave the fireplace in cold weather.
