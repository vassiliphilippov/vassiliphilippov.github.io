broker

verb
arrange or negotiate as a broker
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkər / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkə / (BrE)
to arrange or negotiate a deal, agreement, or settlement between parties as a broker

Examples

To broker a deal
He has brokered several major real estate deals over his career.
She was able to broker a peace agreement between the two countries.
The consultant will broker the merger of the two companies.
