manic depression

noun
mental disorder with mania and depression
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A mental disorder marked by alternating episodes of mania and depression; now usually called bipolar disorder.

Examples

Manic depression disorder
He struggled for years with manic depression before finding effective treatment.
Her diagnosis of manic depression explained her unpredictable mood swings.
Medical researchers now often refer to manic depression as bipolar disorder.
