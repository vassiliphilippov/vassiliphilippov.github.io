witchman

noun
male practitioner of magic
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃmæn / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃmən / (BrE)
a man who practices witchcraft or is believed to have magical powers

Examples

The witchman casts spells
The villagers feared the mysterious witchman who lived at the edge of the forest.
Legends spoke of a powerful witchman who could control the weather.
People traveled from distant towns to seek the help of the witchman in times of trouble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorcerer - a person who claims or is believed to have magical powers
  • synonym: witch - a person, especially a woman, who practices magic
  • synonym: wizard - a man with magical powers
