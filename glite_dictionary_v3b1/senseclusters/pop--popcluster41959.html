pop

verb
hit or strike quickly
3.4 0.99/m
Pronunciation: / pÉ‘p / (AmE), / pÉ’p / (BrE)
to hit or strike someone or something quickly and usually lightly

Examples

To pop someone lightly
He popped me on the arm as a joke.
She popped him lightly on the shoulder to get his attention.
The player popped the ball into the air with his glove.
