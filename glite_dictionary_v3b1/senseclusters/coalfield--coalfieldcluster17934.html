coalfield

noun
region with underground coal deposits
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌfiːld / (BrE)
an area of land where large amounts of coal are found underground and can be extracted

Examples

A coalfield area

The vast coalfield in the region fueled the local economy for decades.
