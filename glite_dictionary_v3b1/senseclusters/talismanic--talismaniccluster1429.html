talismanic

adjective
having magical or protective power
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtælɪzˈmænɪk / (AmE), / ˌtalɪzˈmanɪk / (BrE)

Examples

The villagers believed the talismanic stone would keep their homes safe from harm.
