abscess

noun
swollen area with pus caused by infection
4.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈæb.ses/ /; / /ˈæb.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈæb.ses/ /; / /ˈæb.sɪs/ (BrE)
A swollen area within body tissue, containing pus, caused by an infection.

Examples

Dental abscess
A dental abscess can cause severe toothache and swelling in the jaw.
If an abscess is left untreated, the infection may spread to other parts of the body.
The doctor lanced the abscess to relieve the pressure and remove the pus.
