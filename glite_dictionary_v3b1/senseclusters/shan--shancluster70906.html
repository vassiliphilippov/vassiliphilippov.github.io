shan

noun
Chinese mountain or hill
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːn / (AmE), / ʃɑːn / (BrE)
a mountain or hill, especially as used in Chinese place names

Examples

A shan mountain
The Great Wall winds over the shan for hundreds of miles.
Many temples in China are built on top of a shan.
We hiked up the shan to watch the sunrise over the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hill - a naturally raised area of land, not as high as a mountain
  • synonym: mountain - a large natural elevation of the earth's surface
