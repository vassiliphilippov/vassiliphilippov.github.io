automotive engineer

noun
engineer specializing in automobiles
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˌmoʊtɪv ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌɔː.təˌməʊ.tɪv ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
