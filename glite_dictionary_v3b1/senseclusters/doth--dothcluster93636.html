doth

verb
archaic 'does'
Pronunciation: / dʌθ / (AmE), / dʌθ / (BrE)
an archaic third person singular present form of 'do', used in older English

Examples

He doth speak
What doth the law require of us in this matter?
He doth protest too much, methinks.
The sun doth shine brightly upon the fields today.
