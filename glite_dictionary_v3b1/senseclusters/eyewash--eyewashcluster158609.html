eyewash

noun
solution for rinsing the eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌwɒʃ / (BrE)
a liquid solution used to cleanse or rinse the eyes, typically to remove irritants or foreign particles

Examples

Eyewash solution
Always check the expiration date on the eyewash before using it.
She used an eyewash to soothe her irritated eyes after swimming in the pool.
The laboratory kept bottles of eyewash near every workstation for emergencies.
