nose

noun
prominent facial organ for breathing and smelling
2.4
Pronunciation: / noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊz / (BrE)
The part of the face, usually prominent in humans and most animals, through which they breathe and smell.

Examples

A big nose
Dogs have a much more sensitive nose than humans.
She touched her nose absentmindedly while thinking.
The doctor examined his nose for signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nostril - either of the two external openings of the nose
