fish tank

noun
container for keeping live fish
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ tæŋk / (AmE), / fɪʃ taŋk / (BrE)
a glass container filled with water for keeping live fish and underwater animals, often used in homes or public displays

Examples

A fish tank at home
After buying a new fish tank, he researched how to set up the ideal environment for his pets.
She spent hours cleaning the fish tank to make sure her tropical fish stayed healthy.
The fish tank in the waiting room was filled with colorful plants and small fish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aquarium - a transparent tank of water in which fish and other water creatures and plants are kept
