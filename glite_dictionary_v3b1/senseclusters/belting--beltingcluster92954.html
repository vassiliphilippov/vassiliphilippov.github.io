belting

noun
material used for making belts
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛltɪŋ / (BrE)
a strong and flexible material, typically leather, fabric, or synthetic, used for making belts and mechanical belts

Examples

Strong belting material
