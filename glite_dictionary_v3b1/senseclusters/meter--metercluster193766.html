Synonyms

  • rhythmic pattern
    5.6 0.25/m
    a rhythmic pattern is a regular arrangement of stressed and unstressed syllables in verse, while meter names the structured beat system in poetry or music.
  • poetic rhythm
    4.9 0.01/m
    poetic rhythm is the pattern of stressed and unstressed syllables that makes verse flow, while meter is the formal rhythmic structure used.
  • rhythm
    3.9 3.13/m
    rhythm is the general pattern of sound or beats in speech and music, while meter is the fixed poetic or musical beat structure.
  • musical rhythm
    3.2 0.02/m
    musical rhythm is the regular pattern of beats for music and dance, while meter is the named poetic or musical structural pattern.
