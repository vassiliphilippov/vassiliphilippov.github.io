Damgalnunna

proper noun
Babylonian earth goddess; consort of Ea
9.9
Pronunciation: / dæmˌɡɑːlˈnʊnə / (AmE), / damˌɡɑːlˈnʊnə / (BrE)
In Babylonian mythology, the earth goddess who is the consort of Ea and the mother of Marduk.

Examples

Damgalnunna earth goddess
According to mythology, Damgalnunna was revered as the mother of Marduk.
Scholars study the role of Damgalnunna in Babylonian religious rituals.
The ancient texts describe Damgalnunna as the consort of the god Ea.
