unconvicted

adjective
not found guilty
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɪktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɪktɪd / (BrE)
not having been found guilty of a crime in a court of law

Examples

Unconvicted defendant
The unconvicted defendant was allowed to remain free until the trial.
Journalists reported on the rights of unconvicted prisoners awaiting their court dates.
He was still unconvicted after months of investigation and legal proceedings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acquitted - declared not guilty by a court
  • antonym: convicted - found guilty of a crime by a court
