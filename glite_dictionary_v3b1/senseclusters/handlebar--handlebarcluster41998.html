handlebar

noun
bar for steering a bicycle or motorcycle
5.6 1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhændəlˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈhændlˌbɑː / (BrE)
the curved or straight bar attached to the front of a bicycle, motorcycle, or similar vehicle, used for steering

Examples

Bicycle handlebar
He gripped the handlebar tightly as he rode down the hill.
She adjusted the handlebar on her bike for a more comfortable ride.
The bicycle's handlebar was bent after the crash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steering wheel - a wheel used for steering, especially in vehicles like cars
