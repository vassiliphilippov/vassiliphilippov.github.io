sprue

noun
casting channel
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / spruÀê / (AmE), / spruÀê / (BrE)
a channel through which molten metal or plastic is poured into a mold during casting

Examples

Metal sprue channel
The technician poured the molten metal into the mold through the sprue.
After casting, the sprue was removed from the finished part.
A properly designed sprue helps prevent air bubbles in the final product.
After the metal cooled, the worker cut off the sprue from the finished part.
The mold design includes a central sprue to ensure even filling.
Excess material often remains attached to the sprue after casting is complete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gate - an opening in a mold that allows molten material to enter
  • synonym: runner - a channel in a mold that distributes molten material
