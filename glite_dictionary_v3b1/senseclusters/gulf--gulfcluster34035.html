gulf

noun
large sea area partly enclosed by land
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf / (AmE), / ɡʌlf / (BrE)
A large area of sea or ocean that is partly enclosed by land, typically larger than a bay.

Examples

The Gulf of Mexico
A powerful storm swept across the Gulf of Mexico last night.
Many species of fish thrive in the warm waters of this gulf.
The Persian Gulf has been a major trade route for centuries.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bay - a broad inlet of the sea where the land curves inward, typically smaller than a gulf
