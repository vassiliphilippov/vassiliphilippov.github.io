diatonic

adjective
using standard major or minor scale notes
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪ.əˈtɑn.ɪk /; / ˌdaɪ.əˈtɑ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.əˈtɒn.ɪk /; / ˌdaɪ.əˈtɒ.nɪk / (BrE)
relating to the standard major or minor musical scale, using only the seven notes without chromatic alterations
