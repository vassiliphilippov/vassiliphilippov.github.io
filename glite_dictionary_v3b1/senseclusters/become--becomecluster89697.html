Synonyms

  • change
    1.0 500/m
    change means to make or become different often in many ways, while become means to start to be a new state.
  • turn into
    2.2 40/m
    turn into means to change into something different often by a clear transformation, while become is a neutral verb for changing state.
  • grow
    1.0 50/m
    grow means to change slowly over time, often naturally, while become can describe any start of a new state or role.
  • turn
    1.0 300/m
    turn means to change often suddenly or in appearance, while become is more general and suits many kinds of change.
  • go
    1.8 500/m
    go means to move into a usually unwanted state, often with bad result, while become is neutral for starting any new state.
