perirhinal

adjective
situated near the nose (anatomy)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪˈraɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈraɪnəl / (BrE)
Situated near or around the nose, especially referring to a region of the brain associated with the rhinencephalon.

Examples

Perirhinal brain area
Damage to the perirhinal area can impair sensory processing.
Researchers observed increased activity in the perirhinal region during the experiment.
The perirhinal cortex plays a crucial role in object recognition memory.
