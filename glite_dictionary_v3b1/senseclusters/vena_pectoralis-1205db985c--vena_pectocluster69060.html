vena pectoralis

noun
vein draining the pectoral muscles
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə pekˈtɑːr.ə.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə pekˈtɔːr.ə.lɪs/ (BrE)
Any of several veins that drain blood from the pectoral muscles and empty into the subclavian vein.

Examples

Vena pectoralis vein
Damage to the vena pectoralis can lead to significant bleeding in surgeries involving the upper chest.
Knowledge of the vena pectoralis is essential for performing safe pectoral muscle biopsies.
The surgeon identified the vena pectoralis during the chest procedure to avoid accidental injury.
