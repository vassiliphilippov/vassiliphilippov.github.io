albatross around one's neck

noun
burdensome responsibility or problem
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.bəˌtrɑs əˈraʊnd wʌnz nɛk / (AmE), / ˈæl.bə.trɒs əˈraʊnd wʌnz nɛk / (BrE)
A constant and burdensome problem, guilt, or responsibility that is difficult to get rid of.

Examples

Albatross around his neck
For years, the company's debt was an albatross around his neck, affecting every decision he made.
His reputation as unreliable became an albatross around his neck in the job market.
She considered the old house an albatross around her neck, as its maintenance drained her finances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millstone - a heavy and inescapable burden
