frailness

noun
state of being weak or delicate
3.8 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfreɪl.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈfreɪl.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of being weak or delicate in health or body, especially as a result of old age

Examples

Old frailness

The frailness of her grandfather became more evident during the cold winter months.
The doctor was concerned about the frailness of the elderly patient's bones.
