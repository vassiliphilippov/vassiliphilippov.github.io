shingling

noun
the laying of shingles
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of laying shingles, especially on a roof or wall

Examples

Roof shingling
He learned the art of shingling from his grandfather, who was a skilled roofer.
Proper shingling ensures that the house is protected from leaks and weather damage.
The shingling of the new roof was completed in just two days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roofing - the act or process of putting a roof on a building
