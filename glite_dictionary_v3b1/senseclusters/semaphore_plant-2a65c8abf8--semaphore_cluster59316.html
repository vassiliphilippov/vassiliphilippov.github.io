semaphore plant

noun
a tropical Asian shrub with moving leaflets
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛməˌfɔr plænt / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪˌfɔː plɑːnt / (BrE)
an erect tropical Asian shrub (Desmodium gyrans) whose small lateral leaflets move or rotate, especially in response to sunshine

Examples

The semaphore plant moves leaves
Botanists observed the semaphore plant responding quickly to changes in light conditions.
Gardeners are fascinated by the semaphore plant because of its unusual leaf movements.
The semaphore plant is famous for its leaflets that appear to dance in bright sunlight.
