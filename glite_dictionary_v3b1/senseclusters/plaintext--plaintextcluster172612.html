plaintext

adjective
not encrypted
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪn.tekst/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪn.tekst/ (BrE)
not encrypted or encoded; stored or presented as ordinary readable text rather than in a coded or formatted form

Examples

Plaintext password
Never send your password in plaintext over the internet, as it can be easily intercepted.
The document was stored in plaintext format, making it accessible to anyone with access.
For security reasons, sensitive data should never be kept as plaintext on a server.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ciphertext - text that has been encrypted and is unreadable without a key
  • synonym: unencrypted - not protected by encryption; readable as is
