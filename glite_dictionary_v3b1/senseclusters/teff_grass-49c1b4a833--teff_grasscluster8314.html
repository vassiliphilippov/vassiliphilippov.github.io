teff grass

noun
African cereal grass; source of teff flour
Pronunciation: / tɛf ɡræs / (AmE), / tɛf ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
an African cereal grass cultivated for its small edible seeds, used for flour and forage

Examples

Teff grass seeds
Demand for teff grass has increased worldwide due to its gluten-free properties.
Ethiopians traditionally use teff grass to make injera, a type of spongy flatbread.
Farmers value teff grass both for its nutritious grain and as high-quality hay for livestock.
