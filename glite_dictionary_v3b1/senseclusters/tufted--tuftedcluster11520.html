tufted

adjective
having an ornamental bunch or cluster (tuft)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌftɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd / (BrE)
having tufts or decorated with tufts; featuring bunches or clusters of soft threads, hair, feathers, or similar parts

Examples

Tufted cushion
A tufted capuchin monkey has a noticeable patch of hair above its ears.

The tufted duck is easily recognized by the tuft of feathers on its head.
A tufted headboard brings texture and sophistication to any bedroom décor.
The fields were covered with tufted grasses swaying in the gentle breeze.
The new living room features a luxurious tufted velvet sofa that adds elegance to the space.
