Synonyms

  • synchrony
    6.7 0.47/m
    synchrony is when events match in precise timing and coordination, while synchronization is the process of making them happen together.
  • synchronism
    8.2 0.03/m
    synchronism describes events occurring at the same time, while synchronization refers to actively arranging them to happen together.
  • simultaneity
    7.9 0.56/m
    simultaneity is the state of two or more events happening at once, while synchronization is arranging those events to match in time.
  • concurrency
    8.1 0.46/m
    concurrency means actions occur at the same time, often by chance, while synchronization implies deliberate timing or control to match them.
  • concurrence
    7.2 0.03/m
    concurrence is two or more events happening together, often simply by overlap, while synchronization is actively coordinating their timing.
  • contemporaneousness
    9.9 0.00/m
    contemporaneousness names the quality of existing at the same time, often in formal writing, while synchronization is the process of making timings match.
