synchronization

noun
things occurring at the same time
4.6 3.8
Pronunciation: /sɪŋ.krə.nə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /sɪŋ.krə.nə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the state or process of things happening or being made to happen at the same time

Modern computers rely on the synchronization of multiple processes to function efficiently.
