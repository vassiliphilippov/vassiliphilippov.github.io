blazon

verb
to display prominently
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪzən / (AmE), / ˈbleɪzən / (BrE)
to display or announce something very prominently or vividly, especially so it attracts attention

Examples

To blazon the news
The news of the merger was blazoned on every major website within hours.
Her achievements were blazoned across the walls of the school for all to see.
The company blazoned its new logo on billboards throughout the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conceal - to hide or keep something secret
  • synonym: proclaim - to announce something publicly or officially
