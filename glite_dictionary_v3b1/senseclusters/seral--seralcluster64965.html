seral

adjective
ecological succession stage
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪərəl / (BrE)
relating to a stage in the ecological succession of a plant or animal community

Examples

Seral stage
A seral community is an important step before the ecosystem reaches its climax stage.
Scientists studied the seral stages to understand how the forest developed over time.
Grasslands often represent a seral phase in the succession from bare soil to mature woodland.

Related Terms

  • antonym: climax - the final stable stage in ecological succession
  • synonym: successional - relating to the process of ecological succession
