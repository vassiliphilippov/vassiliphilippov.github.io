popcorn ball

noun
sweet snack of syrup-coated popcorn shaped in a ball
7.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpˌkɔrn bɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɒpˌkɔːn bɔːl / (BrE)
A sweet snack made by combining popcorn with a thick sugar, molasses, or caramel syrup and shaping the mixture into a ball.

Examples

Sweet popcorn balls
Every Halloween, my grandmother makes homemade popcorn balls for the neighborhood children.
She wrapped each popcorn ball in colorful cellophane to give as party favors.
The popcorn ball was sticky and sweet, with a hint of caramel flavor.
