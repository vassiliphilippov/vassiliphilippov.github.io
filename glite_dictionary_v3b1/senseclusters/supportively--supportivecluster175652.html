supportively

adverb
in a helpful manner
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtɪvli /; / səˈpɔːrtɪvli / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that gives support, encouragement, or approval to someone or something

Examples

He nodded supportively as his colleague explained the new idea to the team.
The teacher listened supportively while the student shared her concerns about the project.
Her family stood supportively by her side during the difficult times.
