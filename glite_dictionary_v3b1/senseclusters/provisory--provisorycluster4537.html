provisory

adjective
conditional; depending on a proviso
2.8
Pronunciation: / prəˈvaɪzəri / (AmE), / prəˈvʌɪzəri / (BrE)
subject to or depending on a proviso or condition; provisional.

Examples

A provisory position
His position at the company was provisory pending the outcome of the investigation.
The contract includes a provisory clause that requires yearly review.
They accepted the offer on a provisory basis, with final details to be negotiated later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provisional - temporary; not permanent
  • antonym: unconditional - not subject to any conditions or provisos
