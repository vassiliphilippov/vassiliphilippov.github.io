teethy

adjective
showing prominent teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːθi / (AmE), / ˈtiːθi / (BrE)
having prominent or noticeable teeth, especially when smiling or grinning

Examples

Teethy grin
The child gave a teethy smile after losing his first tooth.
Her teethy grin made everyone in the room laugh.
The dog looked up with a teethy expression, eager for a treat.
