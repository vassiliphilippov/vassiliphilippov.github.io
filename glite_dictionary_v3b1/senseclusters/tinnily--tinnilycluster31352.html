tinnily

adverb
in a metallic manner
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪnɪli / (AmE), / ˈtɪnɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is thin, metallic, or artificial in sound or quality

Examples

Sounds tinnily played
The old radio played music tinnily, lacking the warmth of modern speakers.
Her laughter echoed tinnily through the empty metal hallway.
The decorations sparkled tinnily under the harsh fluorescent lights.
The cheap alarm clock rang tinnily in the quiet room, startling everyone.
Music played tinnily from the old speaker, making it hard to enjoy the song.
Her voice echoed tinnily through the empty metal hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant way
  • synonym: metallically - in a way that is like metal, especially in sound or appearance
  • antonym: richly - in a way that is deep, full, or strong in quality
