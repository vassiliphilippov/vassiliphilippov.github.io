blessed

adjective
used informally for emphasis
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛsɪd / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Used for emphasis to express annoyance or surprise, often replacing stronger expletives.

Examples

Not a blessed thing
He didn’t say a blessed word the entire evening.
I haven't got a blessed thing to wear to the party.
Not a blessed soul was in sight that night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darned - used informally to replace a stronger curse word
