Synonyms

  • healthful
    3.2 3.42/m
    healthful means food or habits that help the body's health, while good is more general and less specific.
  • healthy
    1.0 33/m
    healthy describes someone or food that keeps you well, while good is broader and can mean simply positive.
  • good for you
    1.0 4.44/m
    good for you is a casual phrase meaning helpful to your health, while good can be very general praise.
  • beneficial
    3.4 20/m
    beneficial means giving a helpful or useful result in many areas, while good in this sense focuses on health.
  • wholesome
    3.1 3.93/m
    wholesome suggests health and moral goodness together for people, while good here refers mainly to health benefits only.
  • salutary
    7.6 1.17/m
    salutary often means a useful lesson or health benefit after something bad, while good is more common and less formal.
