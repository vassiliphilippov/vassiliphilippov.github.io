extravaganza

noun
lavish or spectacular entertainment
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkˌstrævəˈɡænzə / (AmE), / ɪkˌstrævəˈɡænzə / (BrE)
a lavishly staged or spectacular entertainment or event, often featuring grand performances, elaborate sets, and impressive effects

Examples

A extravaganza event
Every year, the city hosts a winter extravaganza with fireworks, ice sculptures, and live performances.
The musical was a true extravaganza, featuring dazzling costumes and a live orchestra.
Tickets to the charity extravaganza sold out within hours due to its star-studded lineup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spectacle - an impressive or eye-catching display or performance
