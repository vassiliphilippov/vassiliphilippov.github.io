pessimal

adjective
least favorable condition
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛsɪməl / (AmE), / ˈpɛsɪməl / (BrE)
(of an environment or condition) least favorable or most disadvantageous for survival or functioning.

Examples

Rapid environmental change can push organisms into a pessimal state for survival.
