potter's field

noun
cemetery for unknown or poor people
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːdərz ˌfiːld /; / ˈpɑːtərz fiːld / (AmE), / ˈpɒtəz ˌfiːld /; / ˈpɒtəz fiːld / (BrE)
a burial ground for people who are unknown, poor, or unclaimed by relatives
