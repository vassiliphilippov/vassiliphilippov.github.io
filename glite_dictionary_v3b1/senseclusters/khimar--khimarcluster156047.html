khimar

noun
Muslim women's headscarf covering to the waist
8.6 7.8
Pronunciation: /kɪ.ˈmɑːr/ /; / /hɪ.ˈmɑːr/ (AmE), /kɪ.ˈmɑː/ /; / /hɪ.ˈmɑː/ (BrE)
A type of headscarf worn by some Muslim women that covers the hair, neck, and shoulders, hanging down to just above the waist.
