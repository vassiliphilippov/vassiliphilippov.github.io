pancake-style

adjective
cooked like a pancake
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌkeɪk staɪl / (AmE), / ˈpæn.keɪk staɪl / (BrE)
cooked or prepared in the same way as pancakes, typically by frying batter or mixture on a flat surface in a round, flat shape

Examples

Pancake-style cooking
The chef served a delicious pancake-style omelet with vegetables for breakfast.
They prefer their potatoes prepared in a simple pancake-style manner, crisp and golden.
We tried making tofu pancake-style, and it was a hit with the whole family.
