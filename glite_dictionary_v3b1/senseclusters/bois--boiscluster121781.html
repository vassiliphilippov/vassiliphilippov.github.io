bois

noun
French word for woods
9.9 2/m
Pronunciation: / bwa / (AmE), / bwa / (BrE)
a French word meaning 'wood' or 'woods', sometimes used in English to refer to a wooded area, especially in place names or literary contexts

Examples

A shady bois
They picnicked in the cool bois that bordered the manor's flower gardens.
The estate was named after the ancient bois that surrounded it.
Many French place names include the word bois to indicate nearby woods.
