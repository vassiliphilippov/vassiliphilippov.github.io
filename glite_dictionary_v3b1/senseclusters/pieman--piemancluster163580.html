pieman

noun
pie seller
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who makes or sells pies, especially as a street vendor in the past

Examples

The pieman sells pies
The pieman called out to customers as he walked through the market with his basket.
In old London, a pieman would often be found on busy street corners selling hot pies.
Children gathered around the pieman to buy a treat after school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baker - a person who makes and sells bread, cakes, and pastries
  • synonym: vendor - a person who sells goods, especially in the street
