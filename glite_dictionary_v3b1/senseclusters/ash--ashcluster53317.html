ash

verb
to remove ash
6.5
Pronunciation: / æʃ / (AmE), / æʃ / (BrE)
to drop or remove ash from something, especially by tapping a cigarette so the ash falls off

Examples

To ash a cigarette
He paused to ash his cigarette into the tray before continuing the conversation.
Please ash your cigarette in the provided ashtray, not on the ground.
She carefully ashed her cigarette so that no ash would fall on her clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - to strike lightly, often to remove something
