transportive

adjective
able to move things
Pronunciation: / trænˈspɔrtɪv / (AmE), / trænˈspɔːtɪv / (BrE)
able to carry or move people or goods from one place to another.

Examples

Transportive vehicle system
The transportive conveyor belt moves packages quickly through the warehouse.
They designed a transportive container to ship delicate equipment overseas.
A transportive vehicle is essential for delivering supplies to remote areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved
  • synonym: transportable - able to be moved or carried easily
