mediative

adjective
relating to mediation
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiˌeɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to mediation or serving to mediate between parties or sides

Examples

Mediative approach
The counselor adopted a mediative stance to help the two students resolve their disagreement.
Her mediative skills were essential in bringing both sides to the negotiation table.
A mediative approach can often prevent conflicts from escalating further.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conciliatory - intended to make peace or reduce conflict
  • antonym: divisive - causing disagreement or hostility between people
  • synonym: mediatory - serving to mediate or promote reconciliation
