assimilationist

adjective
favoring cultural integration
8.2
Pronunciation: /ə.sɪ.mɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /ə.sɪ.mɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
favoring or promoting the integration of minority groups into a dominant culture, often by encouraging cultural assimilation

Examples

Assimilationist policy
The new law was criticized as being assimilationist and ignoring the value of cultural diversity.
Some educators hold assimilationist views, believing students should adopt the dominant language quickly.
Her assimilationist approach led to debates about preserving minority traditions in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integrationist - supporting the inclusion of different groups into a unified society
  • antonym: multicultural - supporting the coexistence of diverse cultures
