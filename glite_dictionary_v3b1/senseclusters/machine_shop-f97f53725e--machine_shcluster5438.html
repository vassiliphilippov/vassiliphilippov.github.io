machine shop

noun
workshop for machining metal or materials
1.5
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin ʃɑp / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn ʃɒp / (BrE)
a workshop where metal or other materials are cut, shaped, or machined using machine tools

Examples

A machine shop workshop
He works in a machine shop specializing in precision automotive parts.
Many factories have their own machine shop for making custom parts.
The college offers courses in operating equipment found in a machine shop.
