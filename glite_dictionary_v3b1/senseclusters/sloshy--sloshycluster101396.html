sloshy

adjective
full of moving liquid
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑʃi / (AmE), / ˈslɒʃi / (BrE)
containing liquid that moves around and makes a splashing sound when shaken or moved

Examples

Sloshy boots
After the rain, his boots felt sloshy with water inside.
She shook the cup and heard the sloshy sound of juice moving around.
The soup was so thin and sloshy that it spilled easily from the bowl.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - not containing or covered with liquid
  • synonym: watery - containing or resembling water
