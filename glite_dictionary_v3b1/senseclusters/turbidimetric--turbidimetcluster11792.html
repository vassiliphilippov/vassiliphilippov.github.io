turbidimetric

adjective
measuring liquid turbidity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜːrbɪdəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɜːbɪdɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of the cloudiness or turbidity of a liquid

Examples

Turbidimetric analysis
A turbidimetric analysis can quickly determine the purity of drinking water.
The laboratory used a turbidimetric method to assess the level of bacteria in the sample.
Researchers rely on turbidimetric measurements to monitor changes in river water quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colorimetric - relating to the measurement of color
  • synonym: turbidity - the cloudiness or haziness of a fluid
