spur

noun
device on heel to urge horse
5.9
Pronunciation: / spɝː / (AmE), / spɜː / (BrE)
a small, sharp device attached to the heel of a rider's boot, used to urge a horse forward

Examples

A rider's spur
After the parade, she cleaned the dirt off her spurs.
He fastened the spur to his boot before saddling the horse.
The rider pressed his spurs gently, signaling the horse to move faster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rowel - the small, revolving wheel at the end of a spur
