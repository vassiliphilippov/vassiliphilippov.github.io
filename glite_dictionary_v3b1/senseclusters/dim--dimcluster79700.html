Examples

Synonyms

  • dark
    1.0 105/m
    dark shows almost no light in a room or at night, while dim means the light is low but still visible.
  • underlit
    5.3 0.01/m
    underlit describes a room with too little lighting for cameras or people, while dim simply means not bright.
  • subdued
    4.2 0.96/m
    subdued means soft, muted light used to create a gentle mood, while dim means simply low brightness.
  • dull
    1.0 4.17/m
    dull describes colors or light that lack shine or vividness and can seem boring, while dim means only low brightness.
