hero ball

noun
individualistic play style in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪəroʊ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəʊ bɔːl / (BrE)
a style of play in sports where a player prioritizes individual effort, often attempting to win or change the outcome single-handedly, typically in high-pressure moments

Examples

Hero ball style
Analysts often debate whether hero ball helps or hurts a team in close games.
The star player was criticized for playing hero ball instead of trusting his teammates.
When the score was tied, she tried to win with a hero ball move and missed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: teamwork - cooperative effort by a group
