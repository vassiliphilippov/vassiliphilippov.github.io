juggernaut

noun
an overwhelming, crushing force
6.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌ.gər.nɔːt/ /; / /ˈdʒʌ.gər.nɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌ.gə.nɔːt/ (BrE)
