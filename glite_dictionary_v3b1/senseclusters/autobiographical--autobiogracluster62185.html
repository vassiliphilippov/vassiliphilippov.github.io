autobiographical

adjective
relating to one's own life story
4.9 4.7
Pronunciation: /ɔː.tə.baɪ.ə.ˈgræf.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ɔː.tə.baɪ.ə.ˈgræf.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to a person's own life or written in the style of an autobiography

Examples

An autobiographical story
He published an autobiographical account of his years at sea.
Her new novel is highly autobiographical, drawing on her own childhood experiences.
The artist's paintings have a deeply autobiographical quality, reflecting his personal struggles.
He gave an autobiographical speech about overcoming adversity at the conference.
Her novel has strong autobiographical elements that reflect her early childhood experiences.
The filmmaker's latest documentary offers an autobiographical account of his travels around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autobiography - an account of a person's life written by that person
  • antonym: biographical - relating to someone's life (written by someone else)
