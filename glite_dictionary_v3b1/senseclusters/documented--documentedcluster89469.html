documented

adjective
supported by documents; recorded
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːkjəˌmentɪd /; / ˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɒkjʊˌmentɪd /; / ˈdɒkjʊˌmɛntɪd / (BrE)
supported or proven by written evidence or official documents

Examples

Documented evidence
Scientists examined the documented evidence before drawing conclusions.
The museum displayed documented artifacts dating back several centuries.
This is the first documented instance of this disease in the region.
His achievements are well documented in the historical records.
Only documented citizens are eligible for these government benefits.
The archaeological find is a documented artifact, proven authentic by several independent experts.
