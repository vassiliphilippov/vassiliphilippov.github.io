venomous

adjective
filled with hatred; deliberately harmful
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnəməs / (AmE), / ˈvɛnəməs / (BrE)
full of deep hatred or intended to harm someone; showing or expressing strong ill will or malice

Examples

Venomous words
He delivered a venomous attack on his critics during the press conference.
Her venomous remarks left everyone in the room feeling hurt and insulted.
The online comments about the politician were unusually venomous and personal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benevolent - well meaning and kindly
  • synonym: malevolent - showing a wish to do evil to others
  • synonym: malicious - intending or intended to do harm
