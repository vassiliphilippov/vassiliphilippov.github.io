grooviness

noun
stylish rhythmic quality
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːvinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡruːvinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being stylish, enjoyable, or having a strong, pleasing rhythmic feel, especially in music

Examples

Their music is known for its unique grooviness and catchy rhythms.
