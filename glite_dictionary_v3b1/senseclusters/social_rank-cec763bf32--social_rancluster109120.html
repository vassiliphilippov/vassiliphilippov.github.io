social rank

noun
position in a social hierarchy
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ræŋk / (BrE)
a person's position or level in a society's hierarchy, based on factors such as wealth, family, or occupation

Examples

Higher social rank
Advancing in one's career can lead to a higher social rank within certain circles.
Discrimination based on social rank remains an issue in many cultures around the world.
In traditional societies, a person's social rank was often determined by birth or ancestry.
