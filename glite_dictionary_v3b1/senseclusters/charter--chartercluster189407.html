charter

noun
contract for exclusive hire of a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑr.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑː.tə / (BrE)
a contract for the exclusive hire or lease of a vehicle, such as a boat, aircraft, or bus, usually for a specified period

Examples

A charter flight
The company arranged a charter for a private jet to fly the executives to the meeting.
They organized a charter with the bus company for the school field trip.
We signed a charter to hire the yacht for a weeklong cruise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lease - a contract granting use of property during a specified time for a specified payment
  • synonym: rent - the act of paying for the use of something (as an apartment or car)
