Amiga

proper noun
Commodore computer line
8.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmiː.gə/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmiː.gə/ (BrE)
a family of personal computers made by Commodore in the 1980s and 1990s, known for advanced graphics and sound

Examples

Amiga personal computer
He spent hours playing classic games on his old Amiga in the basement.
The Amiga was famous for its impressive graphics and sound capabilities in the 1980s.
Many artists used the Amiga to create digital art and animations before modern computers became common.
