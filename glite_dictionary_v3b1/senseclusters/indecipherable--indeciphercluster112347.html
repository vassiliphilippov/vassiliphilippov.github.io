indecipherable

adjective
impossible to understand or read
9.2 7.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.dɪ.ˈsaɪ.fər.ə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.dɪ.ˈsaɪ.frə.bəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.dɪ.ˈsaɪ.fə.rə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.dɪ.ˈsaɪ.frə.bəl/ (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to read, understand, or interpret

Examples

An indecipherable message
His mumbling was so low and fast that it became indecipherable to the entire audience.
She received an indecipherable note scribbled hastily on a scrap of paper.
The writing on the old document was indecipherable due to water damage.
Her signature was indecipherable on the form, making it hard to verify her identity.
Historians struggled to decode the indecipherable manuscript found in the old library.
The directions he left behind were so indecipherable that no one could follow them.
