single-minded

adjective
firmly focused on one goal or purpose
7.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /sɪŋ.ɡəl.ˈmaɪn.dɪd/ /; / /sɪŋ.ɡəl.ˈmaɪn.dəd/ (AmE), /sɪŋ.ɡəl.ˈmaɪn.dɪd/ /; / /sɪŋ.ɡəl.ˈmaɪn.dəd/ (BrE)
focusing all your attention and efforts on one particular purpose or goal; determined and unwavering

Examples

A single-minded person
Despite the obstacles, he remained single-minded in achieving his goals.

She approached every challenge with a single-minded determination to succeed.
Despite criticism, he remained single-minded in his pursuit of his dreams.
Her single-minded dedication to environmental causes has inspired many people.
It takes a single-minded approach to train for a marathon successfully.
