artificial joint

noun
surgically implanted replacement joint
3.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.tə.ˈfɪʃ.əl dʒɔɪnt/ /; / /ɑːr.tɪ.ˈfɪʃ.əl dʒɔɪnt/ (AmE), /ɑː.tə.ˈfɪʃ.əl dʒɔɪnt/ /; / /ɑː.tɪ.ˈfɪʃ.əl dʒɔɪnt/ (BrE)
