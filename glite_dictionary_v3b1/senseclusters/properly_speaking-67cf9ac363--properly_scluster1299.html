properly speaking

adverb
strictly or technically correct
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.pɚli ˈspiː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.pəli ˈspiː.kɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate what is technically or strictly correct, even if common usage differs; in strict accuracy.

Examples

Properly speaking strictly
Properly speaking, that isn't a loan but a gift.
Properly speaking, this building is a museum, not an art gallery.
Properly speaking, whales are mammals, not fish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strictly speaking - introducing a precise or technical clarification
