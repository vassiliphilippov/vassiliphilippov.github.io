Mount Communism

proper noun
former name of highest Pamir mountain
8.8
Pronunciation: /maʊnt ˈkɑː.mjə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /maʊnt ˈkɑː.mjuː.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /maʊnt ˈkɒm.juː.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /maʊnt ˈkɒm.jʊ.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the former official name of the highest mountain peak in the Pamir Mountains, located near the Chinese border in northeastern Tajikistan (24,590 feet/7,495 meters high); now called Ismoil Somoni Peak.

Examples

Mount Communism peak
In Soviet times, the highest summit in Tajikistan was known as Mount Communism.
Many climbers attempted to reach the top of Mount Communism before its name was changed.
The expedition set their sights on Mount Communism, the tallest peak in the Pamirs.
