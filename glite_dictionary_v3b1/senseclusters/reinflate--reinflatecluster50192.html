reinflate

verb
to restore confidence
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈfleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈfleɪt / (BrE)
to restore someone's pride, confidence, or self-importance after it has been reduced or damaged

Examples

To reinflate confidence
A few kind words helped to reinflate her self-esteem after the criticism.
The coach tried to reinflate the team's confidence following their unexpected loss.
Compliments from friends can quickly reinflate your sense of self-worth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflate - to make someone lose confidence or pride
  • synonym: encourage - to give someone support or confidence
