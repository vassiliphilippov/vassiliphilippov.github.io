harrumph

interjection
annoyed exclamation
Pronunciation: / həˈrʌmf / (AmE), / həˈrʌmf / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express annoyance, disapproval, or mild contempt

Examples

Harrumph of disapproval
When the meeting ran late, he let out a loud harrumph to show his impatience.
She gave a quick harrumph when she heard the unfair decision.
With a dismissive harrumph, the professor turned away from the question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hmph - an exclamation expressing annoyance or doubt
  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation expressing disgust or strong disapproval
