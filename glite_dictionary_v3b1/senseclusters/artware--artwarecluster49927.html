artware

noun
decorative art objects
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈɑːtwɛə / (BrE)
objects created mainly for artistic display rather than for practical or functional use

Examples

Ceramic artware
The museum's new collection features stunning artware from contemporary ceramicists.
She prefers to buy artware that adds a unique touch to her living room decor.
Many visitors admired the glass artware displayed in the front window of the shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craft - objects made by hand, often with artistic skill
  • synonym: decorative art - artworks made to be decorative rather than functional
  • antonym: utilityware - objects made primarily for practical use
