admin

noun
system manager
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌmɪn / (AmE), / ˈæd.mɪn / (BrE)
a person who manages or oversees a system, group, or organization, especially online

Several admins work together to maintain the online community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person responsible for managing a system or organization
  • synonym: moderator - a person who oversees discussions in an online group
  • antonym: user - a person who uses a system but does not manage it
