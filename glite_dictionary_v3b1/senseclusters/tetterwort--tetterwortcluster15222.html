tetterwort

noun
North American woodland plant with a red root
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtərˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtɛtəwɔːt / (BrE)
A perennial woodland plant native to North America with a red root and red sap, bearing a single lobed leaf and a white flower in early spring; its rootstock has been used as a stimulant and expectorant.

Examples

The tetterwort plant
Red sap exudes from the cut root of tetterwort.
Some herbalists use the root of tetterwort in traditional remedies.
The tetterwort blooms early in the spring before the forest canopy thickens.
