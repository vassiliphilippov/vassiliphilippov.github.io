dislodgment

noun
forced removal from a place or position

Pronunciation: / d…™sÀàl…ëÀêd ím…ônt /; / d…™sÀàl…ëd ím…ônt / (AmE), / d…™sÀàl…íd ím…ônt /; / d…™sÀàl…íd ím(…ô)nt / (BrE)
the act of forcing someone or something out of a place or position, especially one of advantage.

Examples

The dislodgment of boulders

His sudden dislodgment from power surprised both his allies and opponents.
The dislodgment of the occupying forces was celebrated as a major victory.

Synonyms
