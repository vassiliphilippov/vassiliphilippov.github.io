impactite

noun
rock from meteorite impact
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpæktaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪmpæktaɪt / (BrE)
a type of rock that forms when a meteorite strikes the Earth and melts or alters existing rocks

Examples

A impactite rock
Geologists collected samples of impactite from the edge of the crater.
The museum displayed a piece of impactite formed millions of years ago.
Scientists study impactite to learn about ancient meteorite collisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breccia - a rock composed of broken fragments cemented together
  • synonym: tektite - a small glassy object formed by meteorite impacts
