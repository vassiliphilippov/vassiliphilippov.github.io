fruit-eating

adjective
feeding mainly on fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːt ˌiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfruːt ˌiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal) feeding on fruit as a main part of its diet.

Examples

Fruit-eating animals
Bats are well-known fruit-eating mammals found in tropical regions.
Many fruit-eating birds play an important role in seed dispersal.
Researchers observed several fruit-eating insects damaging the crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frugivorous - feeding primarily on fruit (scientific/technical term)
