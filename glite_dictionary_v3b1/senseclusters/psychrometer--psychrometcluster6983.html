psychrometer

noun
instrument for measuring humidity
9.7 7.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.ˈkrɑː.mə.tər/ (AmE), /saɪ.ˈkrɒ.mə.tə/ (BrE)
an instrument that measures the humidity of the air, consisting of a dry-bulb and a wet-bulb thermometer

Examples

A psychrometer instrument
A psychrometer can provide accurate readings of how much moisture is in the air.
