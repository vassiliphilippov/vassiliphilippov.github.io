psychrometer

noun
instrument for measuring humidity
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / saɪˈkrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument that measures the humidity of the air, consisting of a dry-bulb and a wet-bulb thermometer

Examples

A psychrometer instrument
A psychrometer can provide accurate readings of how much moisture is in the air.
Students learned how to operate a psychrometer during their science lesson on weather instruments.
The meteorologist used a psychrometer to determine the relative humidity before the storm.
