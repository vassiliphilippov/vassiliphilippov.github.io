curator

noun
person managing a museum or collection
Pronunciation: / kjʊˈreɪtər / (AmE), / kjʊəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a person in charge of a museum, library, or other collection, responsible for its care and management

Examples

Museum curator
As a curator, she oversees the preservation of rare manuscripts in the library.
He became the curator after years of studying art history and conservation.
The curator organized a new exhibit on ancient civilizations at the museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custodian - a person who has responsibility for or looks after something; caretaker
