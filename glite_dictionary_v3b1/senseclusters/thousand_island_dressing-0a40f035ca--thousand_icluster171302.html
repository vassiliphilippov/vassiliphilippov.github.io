Thousand Island dressing

noun
a creamy salad dressing with chopped ingredients
Pronunciation: / ˈθaʊzənd ˈaɪlənd ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθaʊz(ə)nd ˈaɪlənd ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
a creamy salad dressing made with mayonnaise, ketchup or chili sauce, and various finely chopped ingredients such as pickles, olives, peppers, and hard-boiled eggs.
