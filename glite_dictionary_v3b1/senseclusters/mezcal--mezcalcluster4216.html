mezcal

noun
Mexican agave spirit
Pronunciation: / mɛsˈkɑl / (AmE), / mɛsˈkɑːl /; / mɛzˈkæl / (BrE)
a Mexican alcoholic drink distilled from the agave plant

Many bars now offer cocktails featuring mezcal as a main ingredient.
