voluntarism

noun
acting by free will
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːlənˌtɛrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈvɒlənˌtɛərɪzəm / (BrE)
the principle or practice of acting by free will, especially by offering help or services voluntarily

Examples

Community voluntarism
The charity relies on voluntarism to provide support for the local community.
Many social programs are successful because of the spirit of voluntarism among citizens.
Her commitment to voluntarism inspired others to join the cause.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compulsion - the act of being forced to do something
  • synonym: volunteerism - the use or involvement of volunteers, especially in community service
