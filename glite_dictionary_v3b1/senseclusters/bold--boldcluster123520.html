bold

verb
to emphasize text
2.7
Pronunciation: / boʊld / (AmE), / bəʊld / (BrE)
to make text appear darker and thicker, usually to emphasize it

Examples

To bold a word
Please bold the headings in your report to make them stand out.
You can bold important terms in your notes for easier review.
To emphasize the deadline, she bolded the date in the email.
To make the title stand out, she bolded it in the document.
You should bold important keywords in your presentation slides.
The instructions said to bold all section headings for clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: highlight - to make something stand out or more noticeable
  • synonym: italicize - to make text slanted for emphasis
  • synonym: underline - to draw a line under text for emphasis
