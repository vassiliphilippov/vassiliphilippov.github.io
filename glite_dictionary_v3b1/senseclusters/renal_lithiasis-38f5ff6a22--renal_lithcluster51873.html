renal lithiasis

noun
presence of kidney stones in the kidney
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈriːnəl lɪˈθaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ˈriːnəl lɪˈθaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
the presence of kidney stones (calculi) in the kidney

Examples

Renal lithiasis disease
Doctors often recommend increased fluid intake to help prevent renal lithiasis from recurring.
The patient was diagnosed with renal lithiasis after experiencing severe pain in the lower back.
Treatment options for renal lithiasis depend on the size and location of the kidney stones.
