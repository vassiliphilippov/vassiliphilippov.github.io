Synonyms

  • booklet
    1.9 2/m
    a booklet provides several pages of detailed information or instructions, while a brochure is usually shorter and focused on advertising.
  • pamphlet
    3.1 4/m
    a pamphlet gives short information or argues a single topic, while a brochure often promotes products or events with pictures.
  • leaflet
    2.7 1/m
    a leaflet is usually one sheet folded and handed out on the street, while a brochure is a small bound piece used for advertising.
