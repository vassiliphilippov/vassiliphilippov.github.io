brochure

noun
small informational booklet/pamphlet
3.3
Pronunciation: / broʊˈʃʊr / (AmE), / brəʊˈʃʊə / (BrE)
a small, thin book or magazine with a paper cover, containing information or advertising about something

Examples

A brochure about travel
Please read the safety brochure before your flight departs.
The travel agency sent me a colorful brochure about their summer tour packages.
We picked up an art exhibition brochure at the museum entrance.
