Examples

A kind nature

You can't change someone's nature no matter how hard you try.

Synonyms

  • personality

  • temperament
    4.3 5.62/m
    temperament describes a person's usual mood and emotional tone, whereas nature covers broader emotional and intellectual traits guiding behavior.
