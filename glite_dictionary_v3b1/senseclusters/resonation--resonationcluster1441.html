resonation

noun
echoing or vibrating
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of echoing, vibrating, or producing resonance, especially in response to sound or vibration

Examples

Engineers measured the resonation of the structure to ensure its safety under stress.
Audio equipment can suffer from resonation if not properly designed to dampen vibrations.
