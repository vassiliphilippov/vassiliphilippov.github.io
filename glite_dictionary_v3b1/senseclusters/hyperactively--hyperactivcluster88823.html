hyperactively

adverb
with excessive energy
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that shows excessive activity, energy, or restlessness

Examples

To act hyperactively
The puppy jumped hyperactively from one end of the room to the other.
After eating too much sugar, he behaved hyperactively during the class.
She spoke hyperactively about her new project, barely pausing for breath.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a relaxed or quiet way
  • synonym: energetically - in a lively or active way
