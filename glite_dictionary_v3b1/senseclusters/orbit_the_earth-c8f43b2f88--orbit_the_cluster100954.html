orbit the Earth

verb
travel in a path around the Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrbɪt ði ɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈɔːbɪt ðiː ɜːθ / (BrE)
to move or travel in a curved path around the planet Earth, typically as a satellite or spacecraft

Examples

To orbit the Earth slowly
Astronauts orbit the Earth aboard the International Space Station.
She dreamed of building a spacecraft that could orbit the Earth multiple times.
The first human-made satellite to orbit the Earth was Sputnik 1.
