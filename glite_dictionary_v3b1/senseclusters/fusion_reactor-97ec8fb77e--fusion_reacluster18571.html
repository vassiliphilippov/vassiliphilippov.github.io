fusion reactor

noun
device generating energy by nuclear fusion
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːʒən riˈæktər / (AmE), / ˈfjuːʒən riˈæktə / (BrE)
a nuclear reactor in which energy is produced through controlled nuclear fusion reactions

Examples

The experimental fusion reactor achieved temperatures hotter than the sun's core.
