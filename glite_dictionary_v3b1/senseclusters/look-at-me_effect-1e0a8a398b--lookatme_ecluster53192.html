look-at-me effect

noun
attention-seeking style or feature
4.0
Pronunciation: / lʊk æt mi ɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / lʊk æt mi ɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
A style or feature intended to attract attention or make the wearer noticeable.

Examples

A look-at-me effect style
For a subtle outfit, avoid accessories with too much of a look-at-me effect.
Her sparkling dress gave her a real look-at-me effect at the party.
The designer's new collection has a strong look-at-me effect with bold colors and dramatic shapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: understated - Not seeking to attract attention; subtle
