bundled-up

adjective
dressed in warm clothes
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌndəld ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˈbʌndld ˈʌp / (BrE)
Wearing a lot of warm clothing, especially because of cold weather.

Examples

Bundled-up in warm clothes
In winter, the streets are filled with bundled-up commuters rushing to work.
The children were bundled-up in thick scarves, hats, and gloves before heading outside.
You could barely recognize her because she was so bundled-up against the freezing wind.
