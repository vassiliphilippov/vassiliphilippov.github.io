courgette

noun
long green vegetable (zucchini)
Pronunciation: / kʊrˈʒɛt / (AmE), / kɔːˈʒɛt / (BrE)
a long, thin green vegetable with smooth skin, eaten cooked or raw; called zucchini in American English

Examples

Fresh courgettes
Many gardeners grow courgettes during the summer months for their mild flavor and versatility.
She sliced the courgette thinly before adding it to the stir-fry.
The recipe calls for one chopped courgette and a handful of cherry tomatoes.
Grilled courgette slices make a delicious addition to salads in summer.

This recipe calls for a large courgette and plenty of fresh herbs.
