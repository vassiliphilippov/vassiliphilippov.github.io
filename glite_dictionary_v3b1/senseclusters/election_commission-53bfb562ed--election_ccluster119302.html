election commission

noun
official body supervising elections
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛk.ʃən kəˌmɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛk.ʃən kəˌmɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an official body appointed to organize, supervise, and ensure the fairness of elections

Examples

The election commission office
A new election commission has been established to oversee the upcoming national vote.
Each candidate must register with the election commission before the campaign begins.
The election commission announced the official results after counting was completed.
