ki

noun
life force in Chinese philosophy
9.9 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / kiÀê / (AmE), / kiÀê / (BrE)
In Chinese philosophy and traditional medicine, the circulating life force or energy believed to be inherent in all things, the balance of which is considered essential for health.

Examples

Control ki energy
Martial artists are trained to develop and control their internal ki energy.
She practices meditation to balance her ki and maintain good health.
Traditional acupuncture is based on the belief that ki flows through channels in the body.

Synonyms
