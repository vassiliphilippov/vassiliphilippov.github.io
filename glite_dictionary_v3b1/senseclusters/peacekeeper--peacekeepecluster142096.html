peacekeeper

noun
law officer's pistol (Old West)
Pronunciation: / ˈpisˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌkiːpə / (BrE)
(historical, US) A pistol carried by a law officer in the Old West, particularly the Colt Single Action Army revolver.

Examples

A lawman's peacekeeper
He polished his peacekeeper every night, ready for whatever trouble might come.
Legendary lawmen in the Old West relied on their trusty peacekeeper to maintain order.
The sheriff always wore his peacekeeper at his hip when patrolling the dusty streets.
