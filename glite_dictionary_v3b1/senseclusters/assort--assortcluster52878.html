assort

verb
arrange by classes or categories
2.5
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔrt / (AmE), / əˈsɔːt / (BrE)
to arrange or group things into classes or categories based on similarities or differences

Examples

To assort items carefully
She is assorting the documents before filing them in the cabinet.
The librarian will assort the books by genre for easier browsing.
They assorted the candies into different jars according to color and flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mix - to combine or put together different things
  • synonym: organize - to arrange in a systematic way
