virginals

noun
archaic young woman
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdʒɪnəlz / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪnəlz / (BrE)
an archaic term for a young unmarried woman, especially one who is a virgin

Examples

A group of virginals
In the old tale, the group of virginals gathered by the river to sing and dance.
The poet wrote verses about the beauty and innocence of the virginals in the village.
During the festival, only virginals were allowed to participate in the ancient ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maiden - an unmarried young woman
  • synonym: spinster - an older unmarried woman
  • antonym: wife - a married woman
