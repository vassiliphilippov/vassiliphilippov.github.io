zombified

adjective
like a zombie
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑːmbiˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbiˌfaɪd / (BrE)
looking or acting like a zombie, especially appearing slow, lifeless, or mechanical

Examples

Zombified student
After staying up all night, she felt completely zombified during her morning class.
The workers looked zombified after the long and exhausting shift.
He wandered through the crowded station with a zombified expression on his face.
After staying up all night, he felt completely zombified at work the next day.
The movie showed a city overrun by zombified citizens wandering aimlessly.
She stared at the screen with a zombified expression, barely blinking.
