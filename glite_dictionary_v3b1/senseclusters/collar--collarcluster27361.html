collar

noun
rim of a shoe or boot
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlər /; / ˈkɑːlər / (AmE), / ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
the band of material around the neck of a shirt, coat, dress, or shoe/boot

Examples

Shirt collar
The collar of my boot was torn after a long hike through the forest.
This model has a padded collar to provide extra ankle support.
When repairing shoes, a cobbler will often reinforce the collar for durability.
He adjusted the collar of his shirt before the interview.
She wore a necklace just above the collar of her dress.
The new coat has a fur-lined collar to keep your neck warm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuff - the end part of a sleeve, where the material of the sleeve is turned back or a separate band is sewn on
