thumbnut

noun
a nut with thumb-turning wings
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌmˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈθʌm.nʌt /; / ˈθʌmˌnʌt / (BrE)
a small nut or fastener with winglike or ridged projections, designed to be easily turned by hand without tools
