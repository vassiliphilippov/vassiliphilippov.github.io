Synonyms

  • custom-made
    3.9 0.31/m
    custom-made is made exactly for a customer's specifications, while made-to-order describes items produced on request rather than kept in stock.
  • bespoke
    6.9 0.55/m
    bespoke is a British word for things made to a customer's exact wishes, while made-to-order is a more general term for custom items.
  • tailor-made
    4.0 0.61/m
    tailor-made fits a person or need very closely, while made-to-order simply means produced specially for a customer's order.
  • tailored
    3.7 0.05/m
    tailored is normally used for clothing that is cut and sewn to fit, while made-to-order covers many kinds of custom goods.
  • custom-built
    4.1 0.63/m
    custom-built refers to things specially built, often machines or houses, while made-to-order applies to any item produced to a request.
  • to order
    3.8 1.30/m
    to order means made or prepared only after a customer requests it, while made-to-order is the usual adjective describing such items.
  • personalized
    4.1 0.04/m
    personalized is changed to include a person's name or preference, while made-to-order is built to meet full customer specifications.
  • custom
    4.4 14/m
    custom is a broad adjective for things changed to suit someone, while made-to-order emphasizes items produced specifically on a customer's request.
