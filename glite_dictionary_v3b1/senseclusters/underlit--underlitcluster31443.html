underlit

adjective
dimly illuminated
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈlɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈlɪt / (BrE)
not having enough light, so that things appear dark or dim

Examples

Underlit room
The restaurant was so underlit that it was hard to read the menu.
She walked through the underlit alley, feeling a little uneasy.
The painting looked dull in the underlit gallery room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bright - full of light; well lit
  • synonym: dim - not bright or well lit
