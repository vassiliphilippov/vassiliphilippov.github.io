Synonyms

  • dim
    1.0 8.08/m
    dim gives a low, soft light that still shows shapes, while underlit means there is not enough light overall.
  • dark
    1.0 105/m
    dark describes a place with little or no light and deep shadow, while underlit means simply too little light.
  • unlit
    2.7 1.03/m
    unlit means having no light or illumination in a place, while underlit means there is some light but not enough.
  • unilluminated
    7.3 0.02/m
    unilluminated sounds formal and says something lacks light or illumination, while underlit implies it has too little light to see well.
