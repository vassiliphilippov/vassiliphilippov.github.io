yellowish-white

adjective
white with a yellowish tint
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊɪʃ waɪt / (BrE)
(of a color) white with a slight tinge of yellow.

Examples

Yellowish-white color
A flock of yellowish-white birds gathered in the fields at sunset.
Her teeth were a natural yellowish-white rather than perfect white.
The fabric was a yellowish-white, soft and delicate to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: off-white - a white color with a slight hue, such as yellow or gray
