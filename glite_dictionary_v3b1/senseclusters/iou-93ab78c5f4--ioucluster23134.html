IOU

noun
informal written acknowledgment of debt
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪ oʊ ˈju / (AmE), / aɪ əʊ ˈjuː / (BrE)
A written acknowledgment of debt; a note stating that one owes a specified sum of money to another person.

Examples

A IOU note
He handed me an IOU for the $50 he borrowed last week.
Instead of paying cash, she left an IOU on the kitchen table.
The bank refused to accept an IOU as collateral for the loan.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: promissory note - a formal written promise to pay a certain sum of money
