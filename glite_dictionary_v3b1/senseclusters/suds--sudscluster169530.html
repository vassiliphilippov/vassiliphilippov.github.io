suds

verb
to make bubbles or foam
7.1
Pronunciation: / sʌdz / (AmE), / sʌdz / (BrE)
to produce froth or foam and become bubbly

Examples

Suds up quickly
The soap began to suds when she added water and started scrubbing.
Watch how the mixture will suds up if you shake the bottle vigorously.
When detergent is poured into the tub, the water will quickly suds and fill with bubbles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bubble - to produce bubbles; to become covered with bubbles
  • synonym: foam - to produce a mass of small bubbles on the surface of a liquid
