hobbit

noun
small, fictional creature created by J.R.R. Tolkien
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.bɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɒb.ɪt / (BrE)
an imaginary, small humanoid creature with hairy feet, invented by J.R.R. Tolkien in his novels

Examples

A hobbit character
According to legend, a hobbit's feet are covered in thick hair.
Bilbo Baggins is a famous hobbit from Tolkien's stories.
The hobbit preferred a quiet life in the Shire to adventure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dwarf - a member of a mythical race of short, stocky beings in Tolkien's fiction
  • synonym: elf - a supernatural being in folklore and Tolkien's works
  • antonym: orc - an evil creature from Tolkien's works
