metronome marking

noun
tempo indication in beats per minute
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrəˌnoʊm ˈmɑrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrənəʊm ˈmɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
an indication of the tempo of a piece of music, given as the number of beats per minute
