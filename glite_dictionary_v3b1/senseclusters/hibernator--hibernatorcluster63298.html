hibernator

noun
person avoiding winter socializing
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.bər.neɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.bə.neɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who spends the winter in seclusion or remains mostly inactive, avoiding social activities during the colder months

Examples

A social hibernator
During the long, cold winter, he became a true hibernator and rarely left his apartment.
Some people jokingly call themselves hibernators because they avoid going out in winter.
After moving north, she turned into a hibernator, preferring cozy nights at home to social gatherings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recluse - a person who lives in seclusion and avoids others
  • antonym: socialite - a person who is very active in social life
