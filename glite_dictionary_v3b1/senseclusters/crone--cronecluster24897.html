crone

noun
an ugly old woman, often sinister
9.9
Pronunciation: / kroʊn / (AmE), / krəʊn / (BrE)
an old woman, especially in stories, who is often depicted as ugly, sinister, wise, or magical

Examples

The children were afraid to approach the mysterious crone who lived at the edge of the forest.
In the story, a wise crone gave the hero advice that changed his fate.
Legends say the crone could heal the sick with her ancient remedies.
