jabiru

noun
large tropical stork
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒæbɪˈruː /; / ˌdʒæbəˈru / (AmE), / ˌdʒabɪˈruː / (BrE)
a large, mostly white stork with a black head and neck, found in the Americas and northern Australia
