twicer

noun
person who repeats
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtwaɪsər / (AmE), / ˈtwaɪsə / (BrE)
a person who does something twice or repeats an action, especially when it is unusual or frowned upon

Examples

A twicer at dinner
He was called a twicer for going back for a second helping at dinner.
Nobody likes a twicer who tries to win the same prize twice.
She became known as a twicer after entering the contest again.
