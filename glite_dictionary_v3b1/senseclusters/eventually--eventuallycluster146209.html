eventually

adverb
in the end, after some time
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɛn.tʃu.ə.li / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛn.tʃu.əli / (BrE)
after a period of time, especially after a long delay or series of events; in the end

Examples

It may take some time, but you will eventually get used to your new routine.
The negotiations were difficult, but both sides eventually reached an agreement.
