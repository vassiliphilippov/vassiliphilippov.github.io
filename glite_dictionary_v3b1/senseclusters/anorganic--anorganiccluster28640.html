anorganic

adjective
not from living matter
Pronunciation: / ænɔːrˈɡænɪk / (AmE), / ænɔːˈɡænɪk / (BrE)
not consisting of or derived from living matter; inorganic

Examples

Anorganic materials
The geologist explained that the rock was formed from anorganic materials.
Unlike organic fertilizers, anorganic substances do not come from plants or animals.
The laboratory tested the water for anorganic compounds to ensure its purity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: organic - derived from living matter
