hare's-foot bristle fern

noun
fern with hairy rhizomes
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrz ˌfʊt ˈbrɪsəl fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈheəz ˌfʊt ˈbrɪsəl fɜːn / (BrE)
a species of bristle fern with soft, hairy rhizomes resembling a hare's foot, belonging to the genus Trichomanes or Davallia.
