wireline

adjective
using physical cables
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəlaɪn / (BrE)
relating to telecommunications or data services transmitted through physical cables or wires rather than wireless methods

Examples

Wireline internet service
Many rural areas still depend on wireline internet connections for reliable service.
The company invested in upgrading its wireline infrastructure to support faster data speeds.
Unlike wireless networks, wireline systems are less affected by weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wired - using physical cables for transmission
  • antonym: wireless - using radio waves or other methods without physical cables
