private eye

noun
private detective
7.7 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət aɪ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət aɪ / (BrE)
a private detective; a person who is hired to investigate and collect information, often for criminal cases

Examples

A private eye detective
Classic films often feature a private eye with a sharp wit and a knack for solving mysteries.

The private eye lingered outside the restaurant, hoping to catch a glimpse of the target.
The novel is about a cynical private eye who unravels a complex mystery.
