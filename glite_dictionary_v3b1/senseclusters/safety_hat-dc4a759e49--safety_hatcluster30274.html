safety hat

noun
protective helmet for construction workers
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti hæt / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti hæt / (BrE)
a light, protective helmet made of plastic or metal, worn to prevent head injuries on construction sites or other hazardous workplaces

Examples

Wear a safety hat
All workers must wear a safety hat while on the construction site.
He forgot his safety hat in the break room and was not allowed to enter the restricted area.
The bright yellow safety hat made him easy to spot among the crowd.
