crabbing

noun
sideways movement
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkræbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving sideways, especially in a manner similar to a crab

Examples

Crabbing sideways
The dancers practiced crabbing across the stage for the performance.
During the hike, we had to start crabbing along the narrow ledge to avoid falling.
The pilot used a technique called crabbing to land the plane safely in strong crosswinds.
