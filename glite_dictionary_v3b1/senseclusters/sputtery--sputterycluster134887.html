sputtery

adjective
making uneven sounds
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌtəri / (AmE), / ˈspʌtəri / (BrE)
making short, uneven, or popping sounds, often because of irregular or faulty operation

Examples

Sputtery engine
The old car made a sputtery sound as it struggled up the hill.
Her voice was sputtery after she caught a cold and lost her breath.
The candle burned with a sputtery flame in the drafty room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choppy - irregular or interrupted in flow or sound
  • antonym: smooth - even and without interruptions or roughness
