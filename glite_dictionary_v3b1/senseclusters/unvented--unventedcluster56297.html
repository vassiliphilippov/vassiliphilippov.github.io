unvented

adjective
lacking vents or ventilation
5.8 6.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈven.tɪd/ /; / /ən.ˈven.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈven.tɪd/ /; / /ən.ˈven.tɪd/ (BrE)
Not equipped with vents, or lacking any opening for the escape of air, gas, or fumes.

Examples

An unvented gas fireplace
Certain types of unvented gas fireplaces are prohibited in some states due to safety concerns.
The unvented water heater should only be installed by a qualified technician.
This room becomes stuffy quickly because it is completely unvented.
