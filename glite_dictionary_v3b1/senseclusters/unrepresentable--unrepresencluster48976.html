unrepresentable

adjective
impossible to express
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to be shown, described, or expressed in a particular form or system

Examples

Unrepresentable in numbers
Certain mathematical concepts are unrepresentable using only whole numbers.
The artist felt that her emotions were unrepresentable on canvas.
Some data values are unrepresentable in this computer system due to its limitations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexpressible - impossible to express or describe in words
  • antonym: representable - able to be shown, described, or expressed in a particular form
