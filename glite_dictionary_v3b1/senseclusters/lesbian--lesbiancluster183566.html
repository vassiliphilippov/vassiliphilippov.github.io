lesbian

noun
homosexual woman
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (BrE)
A woman who is sexually or romantically attracted to other women.

Examples

A lesbian woman
Several lesbians spoke at the event about their experiences.
She came out as a lesbian in college and found support among her friends.
The film features a lesbian couple as the main characters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gay - a person who is sexually attracted to people of the same sex; often refers to men
  • antonym: heterosexual - a person sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex
