triatomic

adjective
three-atom molecule
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtraɪ.əˈtɑːmɪk / (AmE), / ˌtraɪ.əˈtɒmɪk / (BrE)
consisting of or relating to molecules made up of three atoms

Examples

Triatomic molecule
Ozone is a triatomic molecule made of three oxygen atoms.
Scientists studied the properties of a triatomic gas in the laboratory.
A triatomic structure can behave differently from diatomic or polyatomic molecules.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: diatomic - consisting of two atoms in a molecule
  • degree higher: polyatomic - consisting of more than two atoms in a molecule
