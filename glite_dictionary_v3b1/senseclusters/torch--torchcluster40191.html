torch

noun
portable handheld light source
Pronunciation: / tɔrtʃ / (AmE), / tɔːtʃ / (BrE)
A portable light usually carried in the hand, typically made of a stick with a flammable material at one end that is lit to provide illumination.

Examples

A burning torch
A ceremonial torch was carried through the streets during the festival.
The explorer raised his torch to light the ancient cave walls.
The villagers gathered around the burning torch as night fell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lantern - portable lamp with a protective enclosure for the flame
