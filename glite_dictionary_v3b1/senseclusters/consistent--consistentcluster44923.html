consistent

adjective
in agreement or compatible with
1.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsɪs.tənt/ (AmE), /kən.ˈsɪs.tənt/ (BrE)
(often followed by 'with') in agreement with or compatible with something else; not contradictory.

Examples

Consistent with facts
Her actions were consistent with her words, demonstrating her honesty.
His explanation was not consistent with the facts already established.
The results of the experiment are consistent with earlier studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compatible - able to exist or work together without conflict
