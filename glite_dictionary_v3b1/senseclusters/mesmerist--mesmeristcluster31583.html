mesmerist

noun
a person who induces hypnosis
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛzmərɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛzmərɪst / (BrE)
a person who induces hypnosis, especially by using mesmerism

Examples

The mesmerist hypnotizes people
During the performance, the mesmerist claimed to control the volunteer's movements with his gaze.
In Victorian England, people often visited a mesmerist seeking cures for nervous conditions.
The famous mesmerist was able to put the entire audience into a trance.
