youth

noun
early stage of development
Pronunciation: / juθ / (AmE), / juːθ / (BrE)
The early or initial period in the existence or development of something.

Examples

Youth of the movement
During the youth of the movement, support came mostly from students.
The youth of the internet was characterized by rapid innovation and experimentation.
The company was still in its youth when the first major breakthrough occurred.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maturity - the state of being fully developed
