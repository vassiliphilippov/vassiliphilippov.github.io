Synonyms

  • apologia
    9.9 0.30/m
    apologia is a formal written defense often used in books or speeches, while defense is a general argument to respond to criticism.
  • apology
    8.6 1.35/m
    apology is a formal written defense presented by someone to explain actions, while defense is a broader argument to justify or answer critics.
  • rebuttal
    6.7 3.10/m
    rebuttal is a direct statement that opposes an accusation or claim, while defense can be broader support or justification.
  • counterargument
    5.1 0.99/m
    counterargument is an argument offered to oppose another point, while defense may instead support or explain one's own position.
  • refutation
    6.8 1.38/m
    refutation is the act or evidence that proves a claim wrong, while defense aims to support or justify something under criticism.

  • synonym: defense - alternative (American English) spelling of 'defence'
  • synonym: justification - the action of showing something to be right or reasonable
  • synonym: refutation - the act of proving a statement or charge to be false
