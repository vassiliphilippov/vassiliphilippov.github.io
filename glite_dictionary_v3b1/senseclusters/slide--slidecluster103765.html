slide

noun
playground chute for sliding
Pronunciation: / slaɪd / (AmE), / slaɪd / (BrE)
a structure with a smooth sloping surface for people or objects to slide down, especially in playgrounds

Examples

Playground slide
After the rain stopped, the slide was still too wet to use.
She climbed the ladder and zipped down the slide with a big smile.
The children raced to the playground to take turns on the slide.
He built a wooden slide to help transport the logs down the hill.
The children took turns going down the slide at the playground.
The factory uses a metal slide to move products from one floor to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chute - an inclined channel or passage down which people or things can slide
  • synonym: swing - a seat suspended by ropes or chains for swinging, typically found in playgrounds
