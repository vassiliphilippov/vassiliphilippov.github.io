fly orchid

noun
a European orchid with insect-like flowers
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / flaɪ ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a small European orchid (Ophrys insectifera) with flowers that look like flies
