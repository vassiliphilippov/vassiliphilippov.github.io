Portulaca oleracea

noun
edible herb with yellow flowers, known as purslane
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔːrtʃəˈleɪkə ˌɑːlərˈeɪsiə / (AmE), / ˌpɔːtjʊˈlɑːkə ˌɒlərˈeɪsiə / (BrE)
A mat-forming herb with small yellow flowers, cultivated for its edible, mildly sour leaves, commonly used in Indian, Greek, and Middle Eastern cuisine.
