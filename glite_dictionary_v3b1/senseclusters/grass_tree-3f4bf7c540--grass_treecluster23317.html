grass tree

noun
New Zealand tree with long leaves and fragrant flowers
4.7 6.8
Pronunciation: /græs triː/ /; / /græs tʃriː/ (AmE), /grɑːs triː/ /; / /grɑːs tʃriː/ (BrE)
An elegant tree native to New Zealand, having either a single or branching trunk, each with terminal clusters of long, narrow leaves and large panicles of fragrant white, yellow, or red flowers.
