disinterment

noun
act of digging up something buried

the act of digging something up out of the ground, especially a buried body

Examples

The disinterment of a body
After the disinterment, the archaeologists carefully examined the ancient coffin.
The disinterment of the remains was ordered by the court for further investigation.
The family requested a disinterment so new medical evidence could be considered.

Synonyms

  • exhumation
    9.4 0.98/m
    exhumation is the formal act of digging up and removing a buried body, while disinterment is a general term for digging up buried things.
  • digging up
    4.8 0.27/m
    digging up is an informal phrase for uncovering buried objects or corpses by digging, while disinterment is a formal legal or medical term.
