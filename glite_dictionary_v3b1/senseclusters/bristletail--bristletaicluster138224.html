bristletail

noun
small wingless insect with bristle-like tail
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪsəlˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsəlteɪl / (BrE)
a small, wingless insect with a long, bristle-like tail, belonging to certain primitive insect groups such as Archaeognatha and Thysanura

Examples

A bristletail insect
A bristletail scurried quickly across the stone surface in search of shelter.
The scientist collected several bristletails as part of his field research on primitive insects.
You can often find a bristletail hiding under rocks in moist, shady areas.
