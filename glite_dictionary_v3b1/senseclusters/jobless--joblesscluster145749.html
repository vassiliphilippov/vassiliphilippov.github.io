jobless

adjective
unemployed; not having a job
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑb.ləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒb.ləs / (BrE)

Examples

Jobless person

Many jobless workers attended the job fair hoping to find new opportunities.
