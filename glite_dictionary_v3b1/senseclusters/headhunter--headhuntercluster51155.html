headhunter

noun
recruiter for high-level jobs
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person or company that identifies and recruits suitable candidates for employment, especially for high-level positions in organizations

Examples

A headhunter calls
After being contacted by a headhunter, she decided to consider new job opportunities.
Several candidates were referred to us by a professional headhunter specializing in finance.
The company hired a headhunter to find a new chief executive officer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recruiter - a person who encourages people to join a company, organization, or the armed forces
