social policy

noun
policy relating to social welfare issues
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃl ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
a government's or organization's policy relating to social welfare, health, education, housing, or other issues affecting society

Examples

Social policy issues
Many political debates focus on social policy issues such as healthcare and housing.
The new government introduced a social policy to reduce child poverty.
Universities often offer courses in social policy and welfare studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public policy - policy made by governments that affects the public
