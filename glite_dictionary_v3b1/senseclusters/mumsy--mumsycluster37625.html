mumsy

adjective
old-fashioned motherly
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmzi / (AmE), / ˈmʌmzi / (BrE)
having qualities considered typical of a mother, especially in an old-fashioned or unfashionable way

Examples

Mumsy clothes
Her friends teased her for choosing such a mumsy dress for the party.
He thought the curtains looked a bit too mumsy for his modern apartment.
The actress played a mumsy character who always worried about her children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matronly - having the characteristics of an older, motherly woman
  • antonym: stylish - fashionable and elegant
