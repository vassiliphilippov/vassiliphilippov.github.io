clutch

noun
a critical or high-pressure situation
Pronunciation: / klʌtʃ / (AmE), / klʌtʃ / (BrE)
a crucial or tense situation in which decisive action is needed, often under pressure

Examples

Clutch moment
She's known for staying calm and performing well in the clutch.
The team delivered a victory in the clutch to reach the finals.
You can always rely on her in the clutch when things get tough.
