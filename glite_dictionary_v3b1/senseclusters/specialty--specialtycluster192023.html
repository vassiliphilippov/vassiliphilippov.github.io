specialty

noun
main area of work or expertise
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəlti /; / ˌspɛʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlti /; / ˌspɛʃiˈælɪti / (BrE)
an area of work, study, or skill, or a particular feature that someone or something is especially known for

Examples

Medical specialty
Her specialty is environmental law, and she has practiced it for over a decade.
Pediatric medicine is the specialty he chose after medical school.
The chef's specialty is French pastries, which are highly acclaimed.
Baking is my specialty, especially when it comes to making bread.
Her specialty is solving difficult math problems quickly.
Negotiation has always been his specialty in the business world.
After years of training, neurology became his speciality.
Her speciality is pediatric medicine, and she loves working with children.
The chef's speciality was creating innovative vegan desserts.
