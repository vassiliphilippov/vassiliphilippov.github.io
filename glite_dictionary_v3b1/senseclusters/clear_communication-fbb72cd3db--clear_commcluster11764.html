clear communication

noun
effective and understandable exchange of ideas
1.0
Pronunciation: / klɪr kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / klɪə kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the exchange of information or ideas in a way that is easily understood and unambiguous

Examples

Effective clear communication
Effective leaders always strive for clear communication when presenting new ideas.
Good managers know that clear communication is key to a productive team.
Workshops on clear communication are becoming more popular in schools and offices.
