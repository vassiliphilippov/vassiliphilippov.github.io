sunrose

noun
plant of the genus Helianthemum
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌrəʊz / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Helianthemum, typically found in rocky or dry regions, known for their vigorous growth and bright flowers.

Examples

The sunrose plant
Many gardeners appreciate the low-maintenance beauty of the sunrose.
The sunrose blooms in early summer with vibrant yellow petals.
We planted a sunrose in the rock garden because of its tolerance for dry soil.
