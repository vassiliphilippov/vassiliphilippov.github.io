MSL

noun
mean sea level
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛs ɛl / (AmE), / ɛm ɛs ɛl / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'mean sea level', the average level of the sea used as a standard for measuring land elevation and aircraft altitude

Examples

Altitude in MSL
The pilot reported the plane was flying at 10,000 feet MSL.
Maps often show elevation in meters above MSL to help hikers plan their routes.
The city is situated 200 meters above MSL, making it less prone to flooding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: AGL - abbreviation for 'above ground level', used in aviation
  • synonym: altitude - height above a reference point, usually sea level
