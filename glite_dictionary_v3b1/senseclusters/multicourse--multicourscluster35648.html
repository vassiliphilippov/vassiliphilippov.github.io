multicourse

noun
meal with several courses
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌkɔːs / (BrE)
a meal that consists of several separate courses served one after another

Examples

A multicourse meal
They enjoyed a luxurious multicourse at the new French restaurant downtown.
The wedding reception featured a traditional multicourse with appetizers, soup, main dishes, and dessert.
Preparing a multicourse requires careful planning and timing in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a large formal meal with many dishes
  • antonym: buffet - a meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes
