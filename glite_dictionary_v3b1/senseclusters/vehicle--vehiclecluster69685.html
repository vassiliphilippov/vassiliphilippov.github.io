vehicle

noun
means for expressing or achieving something
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈviːɪkəl / (BrE)
a means or medium by which something is expressed, achieved, or conveyed

Examples

Vehicle for ideas
Music can be a powerful vehicle for emotional expression.
The film became a vehicle to showcase the actor's comedic talents.
The novel served as a vehicle for the author's social commentary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: channel - a medium through which something is conveyed or transmitted
  • synonym: means - an action or system by which a result is brought about
