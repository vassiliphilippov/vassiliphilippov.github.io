retirement savings plan

noun
plan for saving money for retirement
2.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪərmənt ˈseɪvɪŋz plæn / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt ˈseɪvɪŋz plan / (BrE)
