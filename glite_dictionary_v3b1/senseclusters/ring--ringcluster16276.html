ring

noun
a characteristic sound, often suggesting authenticity
3.5
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ / (BrE)
a characteristic sound, especially one suggesting a particular quality such as truth or genuineness

Examples

Ring of truth
Her explanation had the unmistakable ring of truth.
I like the ring of that idea; it sounds promising.
There was a hopeful ring to his voice during the announcement.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: clang - loud resonant metallic sound
  • degree lower: echo - a sound that is reflected or has a suggestive quality
  • synonym: tone - the general character or quality of a sound
