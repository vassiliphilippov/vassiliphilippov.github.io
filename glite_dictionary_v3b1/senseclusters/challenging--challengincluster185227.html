challenging

adjective
demanding significant effort or skill
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
requiring great effort or determination; testing your abilities or resources.

Examples

Challenging task
Completing the puzzle proved far more challenging than I had expected.
She enjoys working on challenging projects that push her skills to the limit.
The hike up the mountain was both beautiful and challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arduous - involving or requiring strenuous effort
  • synonym: demanding - requiring much effort or attention
  • antonym: easy - not difficult; needing little effort
