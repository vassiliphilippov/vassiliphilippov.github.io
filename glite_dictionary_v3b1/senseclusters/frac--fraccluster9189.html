frac

noun
hydraulic fracturing treatment
9.6
Pronunciation: / fræk / (AmE), / fræk / (BrE)
a hydraulic fracturing operation or treatment used to increase the extraction of oil or gas from rock

Examples

A frac job
The engineers scheduled a frac to boost production from the aging well.
After the frac, the oil company reported a significant increase in output.
A successful frac can make previously unproductive rock layers yield oil or gas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drilling - the act of making a hole in the ground to extract oil or gas
  • synonym: fracking - the process of injecting liquid at high pressure to extract oil or gas from rock
