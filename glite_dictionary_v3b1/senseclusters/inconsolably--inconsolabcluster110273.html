inconsolably

adverb
unable to be comforted
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkənˈsoʊləbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkənˈsəʊləbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be comforted or made less sad

Examples

To cry inconsolably
After losing his pet, he cried inconsolably for hours.
The child sobbed inconsolably when her favorite toy broke.
She was inconsolably upset after hearing the disappointing news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortably - in a way that is free from distress or pain
  • synonym: despairingly - in a way that shows a loss of hope
