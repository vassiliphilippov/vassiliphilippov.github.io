hypersonic

noun
very high-speed vehicle
7.5
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.ˈsɑː.nɪk/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.ˈsɒn.ɪk/ (BrE)
a missile, aircraft, or vehicle that travels at speeds greater than five times the speed of sound

Examples

A hypersonic missile
The military unveiled a new hypersonic capable aircraft that can reach distant targets in minutes.
Scientists are studying the effects of extreme heat on hypersonic vehicles during atmospheric reentry.
A hypersonic missile can travel much faster than traditional ballistic missiles, making it harder to intercept.
