home screen

noun
main interface page
Pronunciation: / hoʊm skriːn / (AmE), / həʊm skriːn / (BrE)
the main screen or page that appears first on a device, app, or website, usually showing apps, widgets, or shortcuts

Examples

The home screen page
When you unlock your phone, the home screen displays your favorite apps and widgets.
She customized her home screen to make it easier to access important tools.
After installing the update, the home screen looked completely different.
I keep my most-used apps on the home screen for quick access.
You can customize your home screen by adding widgets and changing the wallpaper.
After unlocking her phone, the home screen appeared with all her favorite shortcuts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dashboard - a main screen showing key information and controls in an app or system
  • synonym: desktop - the main screen of a computer where icons and files are shown
  • synonym: home page - the main page of a website, usually the starting point for navigation
  • antonym: lock screen - the screen shown when a device is locked, before access is granted
