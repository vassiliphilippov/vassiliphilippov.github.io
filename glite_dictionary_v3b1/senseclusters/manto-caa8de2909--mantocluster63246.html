Manto

proper noun
Greek mythological prophetess
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈmæn.təʊ / (BrE)
a prophetess in Greek mythology, daughter of the seer Tiresias, known for her gift of prophecy

Examples

Manto the prophetess

Many temples in Greece were believed to have been founded by Manto.
