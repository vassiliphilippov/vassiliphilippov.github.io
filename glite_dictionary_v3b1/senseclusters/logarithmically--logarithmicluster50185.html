logarithmically

adverb
in a logarithmic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌlɑːɡəˈrɪðmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌlɒɡəˈrɪðmɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is expressed using logarithms.

Examples

Increase logarithmically
Population growth in some bacteria increases logarithmically over short periods.
The chart was scaled logarithmically to better display the range of data.
The sound intensity decreases logarithmically as you move away from the source.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arithmetically - in an arithmetic manner, using addition or subtraction
  • degree higher: exponentially - in a manner involving an exponent or rapid growth
