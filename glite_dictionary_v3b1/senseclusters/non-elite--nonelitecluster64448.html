non-elite

noun
ordinary person
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn ɪˈlit / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ɪˈliːt / (BrE)
a person who is not part of the social, economic, or political elite; an ordinary or common person, often considered as a group

Examples

Support the non-elite
The new education program aims to support the non-elite in gaining better opportunities.
Many politicians promise to represent the interests of the non-elite during elections.
Social changes often begin with movements among the non-elite in society.
