per year

adverb
for each year; annually
1.2
Pronunciation: / pɚ ˈjɪr / (AmE), / pə ˈjɪə / (BrE)
for each year; annually, often referring to an amount of money paid or received every year

Examples

Per year cost
Membership to the club costs $200 per year.
The company increased salaries by 5% per year for their long-term employees.
The insurance premium is paid per year rather than monthly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annually - once every year; yearly
  • synonym: per annum - for each year (especially for payments); Latin equivalent of 'per year'
