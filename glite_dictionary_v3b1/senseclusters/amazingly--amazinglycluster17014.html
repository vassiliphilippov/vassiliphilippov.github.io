In a way that causes surprise or wonder; in an astonishing manner.

Examples

An amazingly fast recovery
Amazingly, the small boat survived the violent storm unscathed.
She learned to speak Japanese amazingly quickly for someone with no prior experience.
The cake turned out amazingly well, considering it was her first attempt.
