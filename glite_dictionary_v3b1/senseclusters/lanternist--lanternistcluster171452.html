lanternist

noun
person using lantern
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlæntərnɪst / (AmE), / ˈlæntənɪst / (BrE)
a person who carries, operates, or uses a lantern to provide light, especially at events or in public settings

Examples

Parade lanternist
The lanternist led the parade, illuminating the path with a bright lantern.
At the festival, a skilled lanternist demonstrated how to use traditional oil lanterns.
During the night watch, the lanternist checked each corner of the grounds for safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lamplighter - a person who lights and maintains street lamps
