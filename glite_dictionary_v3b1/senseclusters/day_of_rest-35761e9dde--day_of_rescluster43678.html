day of rest

noun
a day set aside for rest
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪ əv ˈrɛst / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ əv ˈrɛst / (BrE)
a day set aside for relaxation or cessation from work, often for religious observance or recuperation

Examples

A day of rest
After a long week at work, she looked forward to her day of rest.
Different cultures observe a different day of rest according to tradition.
Many people consider Sunday their day of rest and spend it with family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sabbath - the religious day of rest observed in Judaism and Christianity
