the apple doesn't fall far from the tree

x

Pronunciation: / ði ˈæpəl ˈdʌzənt fɔl fɑr frəm ðə tri / (AmE), / ði ˈæpəl ˈdʌznt fɔːl fɑː frəm ðə triː / (BrE)
