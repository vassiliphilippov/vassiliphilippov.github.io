hand-to-hand struggle

noun
close physical fight
Pronunciation: / ˌhænd tə ˈhænd ˈstrʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˌhænd tə ˈhænd ˈstrʌɡl / (BrE)
a fight in which people engage in close physical combat, especially without weapons or at very close range

Examples

A hand-to-hand struggle fight
Surviving in the wild sometimes means being ready for a hand-to-hand struggle with a predator.
The movie's climax featured a dramatic hand-to-hand struggle between the hero and the villain.
The two soldiers engaged in a fierce hand-to-hand struggle during the battle.
