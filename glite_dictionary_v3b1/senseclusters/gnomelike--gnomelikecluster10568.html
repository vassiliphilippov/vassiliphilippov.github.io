gnomelike

adjective
aphoristic or enigmatic
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊmlaɪk / (BrE)
expressing short, wise, or enigmatic sayings in a manner similar to a proverb or aphorism

Examples

Gnomelike wisdom

The teacher's gnomelike advice was brief but full of meaning.
She answered the question in a gnomelike way, leaving everyone to interpret her words.
