marsh pink

noun
marsh plant with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / mɑrʃ pɪŋk / (AmE), / mɑːʃ pɪŋk / (BrE)
A marsh plant of the eastern United States with pink flowers, resembling a true centaury.

Examples

The marsh pink flower

Gardeners value the marsh pink for its vibrant color and adaptability to moist soils.
