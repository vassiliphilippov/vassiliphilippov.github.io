wheelspin

noun
unproductive effort
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌspɪn / (AmE), / ˈwiːlˌspɪn / (BrE)
unproductive activity or effort that results in no real progress or achievement

Examples

Team wheelspin at work
After hours of discussion, the team realized their wheelspin had led to no concrete decisions.
The project suffered from constant wheelspin as members debated without taking action.
He grew frustrated with the wheelspin at work, feeling that nothing was actually getting done.

Related Terms

  • synonym: futility - pointless or useless action
  • antonym: progress - forward or onward movement toward a goal
