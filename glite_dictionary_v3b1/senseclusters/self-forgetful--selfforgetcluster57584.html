self-forgetful

adjective
not concerned with oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf.fɚˈɡɛt.fəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.fəˈɡɛt.fəl / (BrE)
not concerned with oneself; showing a lack of self-interest or self-awareness, often from being absorbed in something else or in the welfare of others.

Examples

Self-forgetful devotion
His self-forgetful devotion to helping others was admired by everyone in the community.
Immersed in the performance, she became so self-forgetful that she lost all sense of time.
In moments of true joy, children are often self-forgetful and completely present.

Related Terms

  • antonym: self-centered - preoccupied with oneself and one's affairs
  • synonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs of others than with one's own
