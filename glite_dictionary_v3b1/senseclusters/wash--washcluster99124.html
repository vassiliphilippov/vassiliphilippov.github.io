Examples

Synonyms

  • slipstream
    8.0 0.45/m
    slipstream is the narrow air flow pulled behind a moving plane, while wash means the broader backward flow from a propeller or wing.
  • airstream
    6.3 0.19/m
    airstream is the steady air pushed back by engines or propellers, while wash is the flow driven backward by a propeller or wing.
  • prop wash
    8.4 0.05/m
    prop wash is the rough, turbulent air pushed back by spinning propeller blades or rotors, while wash can mean any backward aircraft air flow.
