ride tandem

verb
to ride one behind another, esp. on a tandem bicycle
Pronunciation: / raɪd ˈtændəm / (AmE), / raɪd ˈtændəm / (BrE)
to ride a bicycle or other vehicle with one person seated directly behind another, often on a tandem bicycle

Examples

To ride tandem bicycle
It takes good coordination for two people to ride tandem up a steep hill.
They decided to ride tandem through the park on a sunny afternoon.
We watched a father and son riding tandem along the lakeside path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ride double - to ride a horse or bicycle with two people
