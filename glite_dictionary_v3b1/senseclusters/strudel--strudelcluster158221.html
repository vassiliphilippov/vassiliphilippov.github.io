strudel

noun
type of filled rolled pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈstruːdəl / (AmE), / ˈstruː.dəl / (BrE)
A baked pastry made of thin sheets of dough rolled around a sweet or savory filling.

Examples

A strudel pastry
He ordered a slice of strudel with apples and cinnamon for dessert.
The bakery is famous for its homemade cherry strudel.
We watched her carefully roll the dough to make the perfect strudel.
