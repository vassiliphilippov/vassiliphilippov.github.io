frogsicle treat

noun
frozen treat shaped like a frog
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑɡˌsɪkəl ˈtrit / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡˌsɪkəl ˈtriːt / (BrE)
A frozen dessert shaped like or resembling a frog, often intended for children.

Examples

A frogsicle treat dessert
After a hot day, the children cooled off with a frogsicle treat from the ice cream truck.
She handed out a frogsicle treat to every child at the party.
The grocery store advertised a new frogsicle treat in the frozen section.
