nib

verb
to add a nib
9.3
Pronunciation: / nɪb / (AmE), / nɪb / (BrE)
to provide with a nib or to shape something so that it has a nib-like point

Examples

To nib a pen
The craftsman nibbed the end of the pen to prepare it for writing.
After sharpening the stick, she carefully nibbed one end to make it suitable for drawing.
He nibs each quill by hand to ensure a precise writing point.
