Synonyms

  • tiredness
    1.0 1.0/m
    tiredness is the everyday word for low energy after work, while weariness suggests deeper tiredness from heavy physical effort.
  • fatigue
    4.2 9/m
    fatigue is used for temporary loss of strength after hard work or study, while weariness names strong tiredness from exertion.
  • exhaustion
    3.6 7/m
    exhaustion is extreme, complete tiredness that can leave you unable to work, while weariness is less total and often temporary.
  • lassitude
    9.3 0.4/m
    lassitude is a formal, slow lack of energy and interest, while weariness is a common feeling after hard physical effort.
  • lethargy
    6.3 1/m
    lethargy is a heavy, sluggish state often linked to illness or low mood, while weariness follows exertion and is temporary.
