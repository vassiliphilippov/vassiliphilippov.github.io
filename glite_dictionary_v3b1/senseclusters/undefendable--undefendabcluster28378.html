undefendable

adjective
unable to be defended
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈfɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈfɛndəb(ə)l / (BrE)
unable to be protected or defended from attack, criticism, or challenge.

Examples

An undefendable position
His position in the debate became undefendable when new facts emerged.
The ancient castle was left undefendable after its walls collapsed.
The troops withdrew, considering the town undefendable against the advancing army.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indefensible - impossible to defend or justify
