apologizer

noun
defender of controversy
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːlədʒaɪzər / (AmE), / əˈpɒlədʒaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who defends or makes excuses for someone or something that is controversial or criticized

Examples

Apologizer for bad actions
He became known as an outspoken apologizer for the company's controversial decisions.
Some historians are seen as apologizers for past government actions.
The journalist refused to be labeled an apologizer for the political leader.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • synonym: defender - a person who supports or protects someone or something
