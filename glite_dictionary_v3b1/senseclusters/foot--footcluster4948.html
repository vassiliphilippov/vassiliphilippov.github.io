foot

verb
walk (informal)
Pronunciation: / fʊt / (AmE), / fʊt / (BrE)
(informal) To walk, especially when there is an alternate means of transportation.

Examples

To foot it home
After the bus broke down, we had to foot it all the way home.
They decided to foot it through the city rather than take a taxi.
We can foot it across the park to get there faster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoof it - to walk, especially quickly or by foot; informal expression
