intestine

noun
part of the digestive tract
3.2 4.61/m
Pronunciation: / ÉªnËˆtÉ›stÉªn / (AmE), / ÉªnËˆtÉ›stÉªn / (BrE)
The part of the digestive system that extends from the stomach to the anus, where absorption of nutrients and water takes place and waste is formed.

Examples

The intestine absorbs nutrients
Doctors performed surgery to remove a blockage in his intestine.
She suffered an infection in her intestine after surgery.
The small intestine absorbs most of the nutrients from our food.

Synonyms

  • bowel
    3.5 6.78/m
    bowel refers to the small and large intestines in medical contexts, while intestine names that digestive tube more generally.
  • gut
    3.3 13/m
    gut is an informal word for the digestive tract or belly, while intestine is the formal name for that part of the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowel - another term for intestine or gut
