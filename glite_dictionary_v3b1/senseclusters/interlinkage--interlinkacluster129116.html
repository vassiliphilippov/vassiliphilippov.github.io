interlinkage

noun
connection between systems
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlɪŋkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɪŋkɪdʒ / (BrE)
the connection or linking together of things, systems, or parts so that they affect each other
