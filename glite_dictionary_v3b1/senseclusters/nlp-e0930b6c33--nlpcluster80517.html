NLP

noun
natural language processing (computing)
6.1
Pronunciation: /en el piː/ (AmE), /en el piː/ (BrE)
the field of computer science and linguistics concerned with the interaction between computers and human (natural) languages, especially the programming of computers to process and analyze large amounts of natural language data

Examples

NLP research field
He is pursuing a master's degree in NLP to improve machine translation.
Many modern AI systems rely on NLP techniques to understand and generate text.
Thanks to advances in NLP, virtual assistants can now respond to complex voice commands.
