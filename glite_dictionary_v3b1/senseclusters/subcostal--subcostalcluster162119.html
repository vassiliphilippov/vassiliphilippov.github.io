subcostal

adjective
below a rib
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkɑstəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒstəl / (BrE)
located below or near a rib, especially in anatomical or zoological contexts

Examples

Subcostal margin
The surgeon made an incision along the subcostal margin to access the liver.
Pain in the subcostal area can indicate issues with the gallbladder or liver.
The ultrasound revealed a mass in the right subcostal region of the abdomen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercostal - situated between the ribs
