fan-out

noun
distribution reach
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfæn aʊt / (BrE)
the extent to which information, messages, or products are distributed from a single source to multiple recipients or branches

Examples

Fan-out of information
The fan-out of the company's new software was impressive, reaching users in over 50 countries.
A wider fan-out allows emergency alerts to reach more people in less time.
The marketing team measured the fan-out of their latest campaign to assess its effectiveness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concentration - the act of focusing or gathering in one place
  • synonym: distribution - the process of delivering or spreading something to various recipients
