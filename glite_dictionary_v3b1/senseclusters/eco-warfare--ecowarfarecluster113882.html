eco-warfare

noun
environmentalist violence for political goals
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkoʊ ˌwɔrˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈiːkəʊ ˌwɔː.feə / (BrE)
Violent action undertaken to advance the political or social goals of environmental activists or organizations.

Examples

Eco-warfare attacks
Authorities are concerned about a rise in eco-warfare targeted at energy infrastructure.
Some extremist groups have resorted to eco-warfare to stop the construction of oil pipelines.
The film explores the consequences of eco-warfare in a future threatened by climate change.

Related Terms
