reprehensible

adjective
deserving blame or criticism
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsəbl / (BrE)
Deserving strong criticism or disapproval; blameworthy.

Examples

Reprehensible behavior
His dishonest behavior was completely reprehensible and shocked everyone in the office.
Many consider cruelty to animals to be a reprehensible act.
The company's reprehensible actions led to widespread public outrage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blameworthy - deserving blame
  • antonym: praiseworthy - deserving praise or approval
