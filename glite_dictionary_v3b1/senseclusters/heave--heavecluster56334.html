Synonyms

    hoist uses ropes or machines to raise heavy objects, while heave means to lift, pull, or throw by strong human effort.
  • haul
    2.5 9/m
    haul means to pull or drag something slowly and with effort, while heave focuses on lifting, pulling, or throwing heavy things.
  • hurl
    3.6 5/m
    hurl means to throw something with great force and speed, while heave often means lifting or moving heavy things with effort.
  • fling
    3.2 0.3/m
