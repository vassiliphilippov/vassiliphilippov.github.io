concerning

adjective
causing worry
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
causing worry, anxiety, or unease

Examples

Concerning news
The doctor said the test results were concerning and needed further investigation.
It is concerning that so many students failed the exam this year.
The rise in crime rates is concerning for local residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarming - causing fear or anxiety
  • antonym: reassuring - making someone feel less worried
  • synonym: worrying - causing anxiety or concern
