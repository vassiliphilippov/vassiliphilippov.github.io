podding

noun
formation of pods
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɒdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or period when a plant forms or produces pods

Examples

Podding stage of beans
The podding stage is crucial for determining the final yield of the crop.
Farmers monitor podding to decide the best time for harvesting.
Weather conditions during podding can greatly affect the quality of the beans.
