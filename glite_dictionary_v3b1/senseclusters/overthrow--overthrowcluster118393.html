Synonyms

  • overturn
    6.3 0.79/m
    overturn is used for removing a government or ruler often by force, while overthrow usually suggests a violent or sudden removal of power.
  • depose
    7.4 1.85/m
    depose removes a leader by force or legal act, while overthrow focuses on toppling a government or ruler, often in revolt.
  • oust
    6.2 4.18/m
    oust removes someone suddenly or by force from office, while overthrow means removing a ruler or government, often through violent action.
  • bring down
    4.6 5.05/m
    bring down causes the defeat or fall of a government or leader, while overthrow usually means removing power often by force.
  • dethrone
    6.7 0.21/m
    dethrone removes a monarch from the throne, while overthrow covers removing any ruler or government, often by force.
  • unseat
    6.2 1.27/m
    unseat removes someone from office, often by vote or defeat, while overthrow implies forcing a ruler or government from power.
