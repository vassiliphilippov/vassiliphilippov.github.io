immunoblot

noun
protein detection method
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmjunəˌblɑt / (AmE), / ɪˈmjuːnəˌblɒt / (BrE)
a laboratory technique used to detect specific proteins in a sample using antibodies

Examples

To perform an immunoblot
The researchers performed an immunoblot to identify the presence of the target protein in the sample.
After running the gel, the technician used an immunoblot to confirm the protein's identity.
An immunoblot can help diagnose certain diseases by detecting specific antibodies or proteins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ELISA - a laboratory test to detect and measure antibodies or antigens
  • synonym: western blot - a common type of immunoblot used to detect proteins
