slow

adjective
showing a time earlier than correct (clock, watch)
Pronunciation: / sloʊ / (AmE), / sləʊ / (BrE)
(of a clock or watch) showing a time that is earlier than the actual or correct time

Examples

Slow clock
If your alarm clock is slow, you might miss important appointments.
My watch is ten minutes slow, so I was almost late.
The wall clock in the kitchen is always slow.
If the wall clock is slow, please adjust it to the correct time.
My watch is always slow, so I am often late for meetings.
The old grandfather clock tends to run slow by a few minutes each day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fast - (of a clock or watch) showing a time that is later than the actual time
