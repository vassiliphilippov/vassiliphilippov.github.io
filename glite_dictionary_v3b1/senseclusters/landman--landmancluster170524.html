landman

noun
estate manager
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.mən/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.mən/ (BrE)
a person employed to manage a large estate, handling rents, farms, and tenants

Examples

Estate landman
The wealthy family hired a landman to oversee their farms and collect rent from tenants.
As a landman, she was responsible for managing several properties across the region.
The duties of a landman included negotiating leases and ensuring the smooth running of the estate.
