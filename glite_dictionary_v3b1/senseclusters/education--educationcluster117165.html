education

noun
knowledge gained by learning
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of learning and gaining knowledge and skills, especially in schools, colleges, or universities
