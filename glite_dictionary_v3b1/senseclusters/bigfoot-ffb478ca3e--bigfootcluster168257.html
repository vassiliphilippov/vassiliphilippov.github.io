Bigfoot

noun
legendary wild humanoid creature
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡ.fʊt / (BrE)
an alleged large, hairy, human-like creature said to inhabit remote forests, especially in the northwestern United States and western Canada

Examples

The Bigfoot legend
Many people claim to have seen Bigfoot in the forests of the Pacific Northwest.
Photographs of large footprints are often said to be evidence of Bigfoot.
Scientists remain skeptical about the actual existence of Bigfoot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sasquatch - another name for Bigfoot, especially in Canadian English
