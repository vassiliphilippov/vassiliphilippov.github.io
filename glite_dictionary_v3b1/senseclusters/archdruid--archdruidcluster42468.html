archdruid

noun
chief druid leader
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtʃˌdruːɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃˌdruːɪd / (BrE)
the chief or highest-ranking druid in a group, often serving as a spiritual leader

Examples

The archdruid leads rituals
The archdruid led the ceremony at the sacred stone circle.
Many people traveled from distant villages to hear the wisdom of the archdruid.
During the festival, the archdruid wore elaborate robes and a crown of oak leaves.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: druid - a member of an ancient Celtic priestly class
  • synonym: high priest - the chief priest in a religious group
