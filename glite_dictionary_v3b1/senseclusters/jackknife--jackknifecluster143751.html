jackknife

verb
to fold sharply
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌnaɪf / (BrE)
to bend or fold sharply, especially when a vehicle with a trailer folds so the cab and trailer form a V-shape

Examples

If you brake too hard while towing a trailer, your vehicle might jackknife unexpectedly.
The driver lost control and the bus jackknifed at the intersection, causing a major accident.
