serum

noun
clear medical liquid
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪərəm / (BrE)
a clear liquid, especially one used in medical treatments or skin care, often containing active ingredients or antibodies

Examples

Medical serum injection
The nurse injected the serum to help the patient recover from the infection.
She uses a vitamin C serum every morning to improve her skin.
Scientists developed a new serum to treat the rare disease.
