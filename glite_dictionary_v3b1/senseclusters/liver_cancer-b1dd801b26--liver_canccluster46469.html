Synonyms

  • cancer of the liver
    3.3 0.01/m
    cancer of the liver can mean any tumor in the liver, while liver cancer in general can be primary or spread from elsewhere.
  • hepatocarcinoma
    9.9 0.00/m
    hepatocarcinoma is the medical name for a primary liver cell tumor, while liver cancer also includes tumors that spread to the liver.
  • malignant hepatoma
    7.4 0.00/m
    malignant hepatoma refers to a cancer starting in liver cells, while liver cancer can mean either primary or metastatic disease.
  • hepatoma
