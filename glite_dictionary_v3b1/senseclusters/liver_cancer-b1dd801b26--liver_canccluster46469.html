liver cancer

noun
malignant tumor of the liver
1.2 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪv.ər ˈkæn.sər/ (AmE), /ˈlɪv.ə ˈkæn.sə/ (BrE)
A malignant tumor that originates in or spreads to the liver, often causing symptoms such as loss of appetite, weakness, and jaundice.

Examples

liver cancer symptoms
Early diagnosis of liver cancer can significantly improve the chances of survival.
He was diagnosed with liver cancer after experiencing persistent abdominal pain and jaundice.
Recent advances in medicine have led to new treatments for liver cancer.
