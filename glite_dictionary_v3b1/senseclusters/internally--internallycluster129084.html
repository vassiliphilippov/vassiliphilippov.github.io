internally

adverb
on or from the inside
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɝːnəli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːnəli / (BrE)
In or by the inside; in an inner manner or internally.

Examples

Solved internally
Problems must be solved internally before they escalate into bigger issues.
The company promotes employees internally rather than hiring from outside.
The new policy will be reviewed internally before being announced to the public.
