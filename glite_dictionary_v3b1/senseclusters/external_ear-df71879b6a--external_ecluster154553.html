external ear

noun
the visible outer part of the ear
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɝːnl ɪr / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnl ɪə / (BrE)
the part of the ear that is visible on the outside of the head, consisting of the auricle and ear canal

Examples

A slight injury to the external ear can cause noticeable discomfort.
