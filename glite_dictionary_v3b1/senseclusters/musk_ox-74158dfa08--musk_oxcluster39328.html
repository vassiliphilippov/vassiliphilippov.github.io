musk ox

noun
large, shaggy mammal of Arctic regions
8.3
Pronunciation: / mʌsk ɑks / (AmE), / mʌsk ɒks / (BrE)
a large, shaggy-coated mammal of northern Canada and Greenland, related to cattle and sheep, known for its musky odor and curved horns

Examples

A musk ox animal
A musk ox can weigh up to 400 kilograms when fully grown.
During our trip to Greenland, we spotted a herd of musk oxen grazing on the tundra.
The musk ox survives harsh Arctic winters thanks to its thick undercoat.
