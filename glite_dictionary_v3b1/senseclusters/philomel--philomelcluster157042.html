philomel

noun
poetic nightingale
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪləmɛl / (AmE), / ˈfɪləmɛl / (BrE)
a poetic or literary word for the nightingale, a small bird known for its beautiful song

In the quiet dusk, the philomel filled the air with its enchanting melody.
Poets often wrote about the philomel to evoke feelings of longing and beauty.
The gentle song of the philomel drifted through the moonlit forest.
