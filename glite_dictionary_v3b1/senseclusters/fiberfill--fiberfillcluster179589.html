fiberfill

noun
synthetic filling material
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.bər.fɪl/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.bə.fɪl/ (BrE)
a lightweight synthetic material made of fine fibers, used as filling in clothing, bedding, pillows, or upholstery to provide insulation and softness

Examples

Fiberfill stuffing
The comforter is stuffed with fiberfill to keep you warm on cold nights.
Many winter coats use fiberfill instead of natural down for insulation.
She chose a pillow with fiberfill because it is hypoallergenic and easy to wash.
The new comforter is filled with fiberfill to keep you warm on cold nights.
Many people prefer fiberfill pillows because they are hypoallergenic and easy to wash.
She replaced the old stuffing in her teddy bear with fresh fiberfill to make it softer.
