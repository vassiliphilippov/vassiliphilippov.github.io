review

verb
critically assess or evaluate
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
to examine or assess something critically, especially in order to give an opinion or evaluation about its quality, value, or significance

Examples

To review a movie
Before publication, experts review scientific papers for accuracy.
He will review my performance and provide constructive feedback.
She was asked to review the latest novel for the magazine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critique - to evaluate or analyze critically
  • antonym: praise - to express approval of (the opposite of criticizing in a review)
