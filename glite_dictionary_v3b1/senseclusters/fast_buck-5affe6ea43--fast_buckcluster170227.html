fast buck

noun
quick or easy profit, often dubious
4.2
Pronunciation: / fæst bʌk / (AmE), / fɑːst bʌk / (BrE)
money that is earned quickly and often dishonestly or with little effort

Examples

A fast buck
He’s always looking for a way to earn a fast buck rather than working a steady job.
Many people are attracted to gambling in hopes of making a fast buck.
Some companies will do almost anything for a fast buck, even if it's unethical.
