skin

noun
customizable software appearance or theme
5.1
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
a visual appearance or theme applied to software, a game, a media player, or a website that changes how the interface looks without altering its functionality.

Examples

Game skin
You can download a dark skin for the music player to match the rest of your system.
The game offers dozens of character skins, letting players change looks without affecting gameplay.
After updating, she switched the app's skin to a high-contrast design for easier reading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the way that something looks to users; the visible design of an interface
  • synonym: theme - a set of visual design elements that change the look of a user interface
