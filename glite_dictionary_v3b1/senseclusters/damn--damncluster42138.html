damn

interjection
expletive to show anger or surprise
Pronunciation: / dæm / (AmE), / dam / (BrE)
Used to express anger, annoyance, or surprise, often considered vulgar.

Examples

Damn! That hurt
Damn! I forgot my keys at home again.
Damn, that was a close call!
She whispered, 'damn, this is harder than I thought.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: darn - a milder expletive sometimes used in place of 'damn'
