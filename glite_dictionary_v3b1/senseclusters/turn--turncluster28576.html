turn

noun
short walk out and back
1.7
Pronunciation: / tɝn / (AmE), / tɜːn / (BrE)
a short, usually leisurely walk taken for pleasure or exercise, especially one that is out and back

Examples

An evening turn
After dinner, she suggested they go for a turn through the gardens.
He stretched his legs with a quick turn about the block before returning to work.
On sunny afternoons, elderly couples would often take a turn around the village green.
