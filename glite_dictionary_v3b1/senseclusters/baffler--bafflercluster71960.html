baffler

noun
flow-reducing device
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæflər / (AmE), / ˈbæflə / (BrE)
a plate, screen, or device used to slow down, direct, or reduce the flow of air, liquid, sound, or light in a machine or structure

Examples

Ventilation baffler
The engineer installed a baffler in the ventilation system to control airflow.
A baffler can help reduce noise in industrial machines by redirecting sound waves.
They replaced the old baffler to improve the efficiency of the water heater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baffle - a device that regulates or obstructs flow in a system
  • synonym: filter - a device that removes unwanted substances from a fluid or gas
