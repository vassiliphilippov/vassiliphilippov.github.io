praiser

noun
person giving praise
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpreɪzə / (BrE)
a person who expresses praise, especially in a religious context by singing or proclaiming devotion

Examples

A praiser in church
The praiser led the congregation in singing joyful hymns during the ceremony.
As a devoted praiser, she often volunteered to sing at church services.
Each praiser in the choir brought their own unique energy to the worship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • synonym: worshipper - a person who takes part in religious worship
