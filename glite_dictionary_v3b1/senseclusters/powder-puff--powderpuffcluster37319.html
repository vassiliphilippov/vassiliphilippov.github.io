powder-puff

adjective
competition for female participants only
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdər ˌpʌf / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə ˌpʌf / (BrE)
(of a sporting event or competition) involving only female participants, often in reference to teams or matches traditionally played by men.

Examples

Powder-puff sports match
A powder-puff volleyball match was the highlight of the homecoming celebrations.
Every year, they organize a powder-puff basketball tournament to encourage female participation in sports.
The school hosted a powder-puff football game with senior girls playing against junior girls.
