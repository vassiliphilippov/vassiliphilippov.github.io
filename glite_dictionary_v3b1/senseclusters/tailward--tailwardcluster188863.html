tailward

adverb
toward the rear
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlwərd / (AmE), / ˈteɪlwəd / (BrE)
toward the tail or rear end of an animal or object

Examples

To tailward direction
The stripes on the fish run tailward from its gills to its fin.
As the animal moved, its fur seemed to ripple tailward in the breeze.
The scientist noted that the pattern extended tailward along the lizard's back.

Related Terms

  • antonym: headward - toward the head or front end
  • synonym: rearward - toward the back or rear
