clarifier

noun
water treatment tank
Pronunciation: / ˈklærəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈklærɪfaɪə / (BrE)
a tank or device in a water treatment plant where solids settle out, making the liquid clearer

Examples

Water clarifier
The city installed a new clarifier to improve the quality of its drinking water.
Engineers inspected the clarifier to ensure it was removing enough solids from the wastewater.
Regular maintenance of the clarifier is essential for efficient water treatment operations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: filter - a device that removes impurities from liquids or gases
  • synonym: settling tank - a tank where solids settle out of liquids
