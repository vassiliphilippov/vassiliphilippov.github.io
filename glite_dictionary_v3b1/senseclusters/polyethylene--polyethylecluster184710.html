polyethylene

noun
lightweight thermoplastic material
1.5 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈɛθəˌlin / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɛθəliːn / (BrE)
A lightweight, flexible, and durable thermoplastic material, especially used in packaging, containers, and insulation.

Examples

A polyethylene bag
Most shopping bags are made of polyethylene, which makes them lightweight and durable.
Researchers are developing new forms of polyethylene that are easier to recycle.
The wires were coated with polyethylene to provide extra insulation.
