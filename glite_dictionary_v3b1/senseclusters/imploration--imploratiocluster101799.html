imploration

noun
desperate plea
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪmpləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an earnest or desperate request or plea for something

Examples

An imploration for help
Her imploration for help moved everyone in the room.
Despite his imploration, the decision remained unchanged.
The child's imploration for another story was hard to resist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demand - an insistent and firm request
  • synonym: entreaty - an earnest or humble request
  • synonym: plea - an urgent or emotional request
