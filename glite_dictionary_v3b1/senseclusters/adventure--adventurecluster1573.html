adventure

verb
to risk or undertake boldly
2.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
to risk something with the hope of gaining a positive result, especially by taking a chance or trying something hazardous

Examples

To adventure money
He was not afraid to adventure his fortune on the high seas in search of riches.
Many entrepreneurs adventure their savings in hopes of building a successful business.
She adventured into unfamiliar territory, trusting her instincts despite the risks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: risk - to expose to danger or loss
