briquet

noun
flame-producing device
Pronunciation: / briːˈkeɪ / (AmE), / briːˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a small device used to produce a flame for lighting cigarettes, candles, or fires

Examples

A pocket briquet
He reached into his pocket for a briquet to light the candle.
She borrowed a briquet from a friend to start the campfire.
The old briquet still worked after many years of use.
