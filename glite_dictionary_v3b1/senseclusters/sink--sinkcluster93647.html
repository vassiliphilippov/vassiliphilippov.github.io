Synonyms

  • descend
    2.7 15/m
    descend is a formal word for going down in space or level, while sink often means moving down physically or feeling lower emotionally.
  • subside
    4.8 1.59/m
    subside means to settle or calm down after disturbance, while sink is broader and can mean moving down physically or emotionally.
  • come down
    2.8 7.58/m
    come down means to move or fall to a lower level or value, while sink often implies going lower physically or in feeling.
  • drop down
    1.8 2.73/m
    drop down means to fall from a higher place suddenly or quickly, while sink often describes a slower or deeper descent.
  • fall
    1.0 164/m
    fall means to drop quickly by gravity, while sink often suggests a slower, gradual move down or emotional lowering.
  • drop
    1.0 60/m
    drop means to let or cause something to fall often by accident, while sink usually means moving down by weight or settling.
