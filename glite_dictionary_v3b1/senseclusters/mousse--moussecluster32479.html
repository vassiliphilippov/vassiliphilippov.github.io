mousse

noun
light creamy dish set with gelatin
3.5
Pronunciation: / mus / (AmE), / muːs / (BrE)
A light, creamy dish made from fish or meat, set with gelatin and often served cold.

Examples

Chicken mousse
Chicken mousse pairs well with a crisp, green salad and fresh bread.
For special occasions, she often serves a savory seafood mousse in elegant molds.
The chef prepared a delicate salmon mousse for the appetizer course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soufflé - a light, fluffy baked dish made with egg whites
