throwaway

noun
single-use item
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊəˌweɪ / (BrE)
an item designed to be used once and then discarded after use

Examples

A throwaway cup
Many fast food restaurants provide throwaway cups and utensils for convenience.
The festival encouraged visitors to avoid throwaway products and bring their own containers.
After the party, the kitchen was filled with throwaway plates and napkins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposable - intended to be used once or for a short time and then thrown away
  • antonym: reusable - able to be used more than once
