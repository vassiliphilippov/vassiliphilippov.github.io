hedge garlic

noun
European herb with garlic scent
8.0
Pronunciation: / hɛdʒ ˈɡɑrlɪk / (AmE), / hɛdʒ ˈɡɑːlɪk / (BrE)
A European herb (Alliaria petiolata), also called garlic mustard, with leaves that smell like garlic when crushed.

Examples

Hedge garlic leaves
Many gardeners remove hedge garlic because it spreads rapidly in woodlands.
She added some fresh hedge garlic to the salad for extra flavor.
The leaves of hedge garlic release a strong garlic aroma when crushed.
