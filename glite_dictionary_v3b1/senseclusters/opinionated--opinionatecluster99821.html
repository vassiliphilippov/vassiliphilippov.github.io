opinionated

adjective
having strong, inflexible opinions
4.4
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪnjəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or showing strong, often unreasonable, beliefs or opinions and not willing to change them

Examples

An opinionated person
His opinionated stance often led to arguments with colleagues.
She can be very opinionated and rarely listens to others' suggestions.
The discussion was challenging because both participants were equally opinionated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dogmatic - inclined to lay down principles as undeniably true
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas
