broad

adjective
wide from side to side
Pronunciation: / brɔd /; / brɑd / (AmE), / brɔːd / (BrE)
having a great extent or distance from side to side; wide

Examples

The river was over a mile broad at this point, making the crossing difficult.
Her research has a broad scope, encompassing several scientific disciplines.
The garden features a broad lawn that stretches nearly to the river.
We admired the broad plains from the top of the hill.
