fuzzy

noun
soft comfort object
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌzi / (AmE), / ˈfʌzi / (BrE)
a soft, cuddly object such as a stuffed animal or blanket, often providing comfort.

Examples

A fuzzy toy

She brought her fuzzy to summer camp to help her feel at home.
Many children find comfort in holding a fuzzy when they are upset.
