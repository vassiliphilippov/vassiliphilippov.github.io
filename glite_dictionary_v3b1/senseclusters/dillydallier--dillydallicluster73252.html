dillydallier

noun
person who wastes time or dawdles
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪliˌdæliər / (AmE), / ˈdɪliˌdaliə / (BrE)
a person who wastes time by being slow or indecisive; someone who dawdles

Examples

A dillydallier wastes time
Stop being such a dillydallier and finish your work on time.
The team lost patience with the dillydallier who was always late for meetings.
There's no room for a dillydallier in this fast-paced environment.
