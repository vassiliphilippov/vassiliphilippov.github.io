flinter

noun
flint worker
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪntər / (AmE), / ˈflɪntə / (BrE)
a person who works with or collects flint, especially for making tools or artifacts

Examples

A skilled flinter
The experienced flinter carefully selected the best stones for toolmaking.
At the archaeological site, a flinter explained ancient techniques for shaping flint.
Local museums sometimes invite a flinter to demonstrate traditional stone tool production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flintknapper - a person who shapes flint into tools or weapons
  • synonym: miner - a person who extracts minerals from the earth
