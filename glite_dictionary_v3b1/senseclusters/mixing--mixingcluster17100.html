mixing

noun
the act of combining things
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪksɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of combining different substances or elements so that they become a single entity

Examples

Audio mixing process
Audio engineers spend hours perfecting the mixing of tracks in the studio.
Proper mixing of the ingredients is essential for a smooth cake batter.
The mixing of colors allowed the artist to create a unique shade for the painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix things so that their parts combine completely
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving things apart or away from each other
