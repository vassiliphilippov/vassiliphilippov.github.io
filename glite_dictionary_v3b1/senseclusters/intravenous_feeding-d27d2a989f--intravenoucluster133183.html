Synonyms

  • total parenteral nutrition
    4.9 0.01/m
    total parenteral nutrition is the technical hospital term for supplying all nutrients through a vein, while intravenous feeding is the general description.
  • hyperalimentation
    9.9 0.01/m
    hyperalimentation is an older medical term for supplying full nutrition through a large vein, while intravenous feeding is a simpler, general phrase.
