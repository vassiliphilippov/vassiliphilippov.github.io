sanatorium

noun
dated term for an insane asylum
Pronunciation: / ˌsænəˈtɔriəm / (AmE), / ˌsan.əˈtɔː.ri.əm / (BrE)
A dated and sometimes pejorative term for a hospital for people with mental illness, especially in the past; an insane asylum.

Examples

A mental sanatorium
In the novel, the character was sent to a sanatorium after suffering a breakdown.
Some communities resisted having a sanatorium nearby, fearing the stigma associated with mental illness.
The old sanatorium on the outskirts of town was later converted into a museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asylum - an institution offering shelter and support to people with mental illness
  • synonym: psychiatric hospital - a hospital specializing in the treatment of mental illness
