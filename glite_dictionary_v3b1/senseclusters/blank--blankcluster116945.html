blank

verb
to ignore or forget
Pronunciation: / blæŋk / (AmE), / blæŋk / (BrE)
to deliberately ignore someone or suddenly forget something

Examples

To blank a friend
She blanked him at the party and walked away without saying a word.
I completely blanked on his name during the introduction.
He waved, but she just blanked him and kept walking.
I completely blanked during the test and could not remember any answers.
She sometimes blanks on her friends' names when she is nervous.
He blanked for a moment before answering the interview question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forget - to fail to remember something
  • antonym: recall - to remember something
  • antonym: remember - to recall or keep in mind
  • synonym: snub - to ignore or treat someone coldly
