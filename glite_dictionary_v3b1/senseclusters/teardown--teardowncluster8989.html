Synonyms

  • disassembly
    5.2 0.30/m
    disassembly is taking a machine into parts to repair or inspect, while teardown focuses on analyzing or removing parts to study a device.
  • dismantling
    4.4 1.26/m
    dismantling means removing parts or systems often for transport or demolition, while teardown normally means taking apart to inspect or analyze.
  • dissection
    6.3 1.13/m
    dissection is cutting something open to study its insides in science or medicine, while teardown is taking things apart to inspect or remove parts.
  • deconstruction
    7.6 0.25/m
    deconstruction analyzes ideas or texts by breaking them into parts to show meaning, while teardown is a physical act of taking objects apart to inspect.
