teardown

noun
dismantling for analysis
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈtɛəˌdaʊn / (BrE)
the act of taking something apart, especially to analyze, inspect, or remove it

Examples

Device teardown
The engineers performed a teardown of the device to study its components.
Before recycling the old building, the crew completed a full teardown of its interior.
A detailed teardown revealed the hidden features inside the new smartphone model.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assembly - the act of putting parts together to make something
  • synonym: dismantling - the process of taking something apart
