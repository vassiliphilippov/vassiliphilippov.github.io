crabwood

noun
tropical timber tree
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈkræbˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tropical tree with hard wood, often used for timber or medicinal purposes

Examples

A crabwood tree

Local communities use crabwood not only for building but also for traditional medicine.
Because of its strength, crabwood is a popular choice for making durable furniture.
