check

verb
decline to bet (in card games)
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
In card games, to decline to make a bet but remain in the game.

Examples

To check in poker
If all the players check, the round moves to the next card.
It's your turn; you can check or place a bet.
She decided to check instead of raising the stakes.
