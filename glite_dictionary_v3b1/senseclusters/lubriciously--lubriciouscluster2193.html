lubriciously

adverb
in a slippery way
8.6
Pronunciation: / luːˈbrɪʃəsli / (AmE), / luːˈbrɪʃəsli / (BrE)
in a slippery or smooth manner, especially in a way that causes slipperiness (literal, non-sexual)

Examples

Water flows lubriciously
The rainwater ran lubriciously down the newly waxed car hood.
After being polished, the marble floor gleamed and felt lubriciously smooth underfoot.
The snake moved lubriciously across the wet grass, barely making a sound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roughly - in a rough or uneven manner
  • synonym: slipperily - in a slippery manner
  • synonym: smoothly - in a smooth or even manner
