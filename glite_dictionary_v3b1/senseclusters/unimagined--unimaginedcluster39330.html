unimagined

adjective
not imagined or conceived
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈmædʒɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈmædʒɪnd / (BrE)
not conceived or thought of, even in dreams; never imagined or expected.

Examples

The unimagined future
Her success opened up unimagined opportunities for people in her community.
The technology brought changes that were previously unimagined by scientists.
They faced unimagined challenges in the aftermath of the disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unforeseen - not anticipated or expected
  • synonym: unthinkable - impossible to imagine or accept
