compassionateness

noun
quality of showing deep sympathy
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpæʃənətnəs / (AmE), / kəmˈpæʃənətnəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing deep awareness of and sympathy for the suffering of others

Examples

Deep compassionateness
He was admired for his compassionateness in difficult situations.
Her compassionateness made her a trusted friend to those in need.
The compassionateness of the volunteers brought comfort to the refugees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for others' suffering
