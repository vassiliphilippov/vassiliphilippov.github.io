corn sugar

noun
dextrose from cornstarch
3.9
Pronunciation: / kɔrn ˈʃʌɡɚ /; / kɔrn ˈʃʊɡɚ / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈʃʌɡə /; / kɔːn ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
dextrose (a type of sugar) made from cornstarch, used as a sweetener in food and drinks
