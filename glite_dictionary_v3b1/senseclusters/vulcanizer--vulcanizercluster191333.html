vulcanizer

noun
device for vulcanizing rubber
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlkəˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈvʌl.kə.naɪ.zə /; / ˈvʌlkənaɪzə / (BrE)
