civil engineer

noun
engineer of public works (roads, bridges, etc.)
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪv.əl en.dʒə.ˈnɪr/ /; / /ˈsɪv.əl en.dʒɪ.ˈnɪr/ (AmE), /ˈsɪv.əl en.dʒə.ˈnɪə/ /; / /ˈsɪv.əl en.dʒɪ.ˈnɪə/ (BrE)
