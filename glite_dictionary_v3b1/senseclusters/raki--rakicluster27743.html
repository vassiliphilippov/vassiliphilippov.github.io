raki

noun
Balkan fruit brandy
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːki / (AmE), / ˈræki / (BrE)
a strong distilled alcoholic drink from the Balkans, made from grapes, plums, or other fruit

Examples

Homemade raki toast
They offered guests a glass of homemade raki at the start of the meal.
In many Balkan countries, raki is a traditional drink served at celebrations.
He learned how to distill raki from his grandfather using ripe plums.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brandy - a strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice
  • synonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage, typically made from grains or potatoes
