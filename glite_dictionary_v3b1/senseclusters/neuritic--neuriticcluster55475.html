neuritic

adjective
relating to neurites
9.9
Pronunciation: / nʊˈraɪtɪk / (AmE), / njʊəˈraɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to neurites, which are the axons or dendrites of nerve cells, or to tissue showing neurite formation.

Examples

Neuritic plaques
The pathologist noted several neuritic plaques in the brain tissue sample.
Researchers observed neuritic growth in the cultured nerve cells after treatment.
Microscopic examination revealed numerous neuritic processes extending from injured neurons toward the lesion.
