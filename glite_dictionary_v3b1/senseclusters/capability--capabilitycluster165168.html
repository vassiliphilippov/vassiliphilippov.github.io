Synonyms

  • ability

    ability shows a person's current skill or power to do tasks well, while capability can mean present skill or future potential.
  • capacity
    3.5 24/m
    capacity refers to the amount or power someone or something can hold or do, while capability stresses skill or possible development.
  • potential
    3.2 64/m
    potential is the possible ability that may appear in the future, while capability can mean actual skill or a future possibility.
  • potentiality
    7.2 1.05/m

  • competence
