bubinga

noun
African hardwood tree
Pronunciation: / buˈbɪŋɡə / (AmE), / buˈbɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a tropical African tree (of the genus Guibourtia) or its strong, attractive hardwood, used in furniture and musical instruments
