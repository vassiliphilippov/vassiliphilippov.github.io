fetch

noun
the action of going to get something
Pronunciation: / fɛtʃ / (AmE), / fɛtʃ / (BrE)
the act of going to get something and bringing it back

Examples

Our puppy is learning to play fetch with a tennis ball.
The dog loves a good game of fetch in the park.
