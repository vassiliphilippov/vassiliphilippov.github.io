Marian

adjective
relating to the Virgin Mary
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛriən / (AmE), / ˈmɛəriən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Virgin Mary, or showing veneration for her, especially in religious contexts.

Examples
