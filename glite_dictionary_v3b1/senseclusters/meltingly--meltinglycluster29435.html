meltingly

adverb
in a soft, melting way
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛltɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɛltɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that becomes soft or liquid, often describing food that melts or dissolves easily.

Examples

Meltingly soft cheese
The cheese was meltingly soft on top of the freshly baked bread.
She described the dessert as meltingly delicious, with every bite dissolving on her tongue.
The butter spread meltingly over the hot pancakes, making them even more tempting.
