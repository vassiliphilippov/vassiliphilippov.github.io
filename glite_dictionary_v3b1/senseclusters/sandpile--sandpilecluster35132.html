sandpile

noun
pile of sand for children's play
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌpaɪl / (AmE), / ˈsændˌpaɪl / (BrE)
A pile of sand, especially one used for children to play in such as in a sandbox or play area.

Examples

A sandpile in playground
After a heavy rain, the sandpile in the playground needed to be refilled.
Every summer, the neighborhood kids gather around the big sandpile behind the school.
The children spent hours building castles in the sandpile at the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandbox - a container filled with sand for children to play in
