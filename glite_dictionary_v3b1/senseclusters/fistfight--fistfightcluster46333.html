fistfight

noun
fight using bare fists
2.3 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪst.faɪt/ (AmE), /ˈfɪst.faɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

He walked away with a black eye after losing the fistfight.
The two men got into a fistfight outside the bar last night.
