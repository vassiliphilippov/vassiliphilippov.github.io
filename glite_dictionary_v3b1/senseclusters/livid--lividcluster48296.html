livid

adjective
discolored by bruising
5.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪ.vɪd/ (AmE), /ˈlɪ.vɪd/ (BrE)
(Of the skin) discolored by bruising, especially with a bluish or dark mark caused by blood beneath the skin.

Examples

Livid bruise
Doctors examined the livid marks left by the accident.
He had a livid bruise on his arm after falling off his bike.
Her leg was livid where she had bumped it against the table edge.
