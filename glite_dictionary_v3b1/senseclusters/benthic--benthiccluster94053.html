benthic

adjective
relating to the bottom of a body of water
9.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈben.θɪk/ (AmE), /ˈben.θɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or occurring on the bottom of a sea, lake, or other body of water.

Examples

Benthic sea life
Certain fish species are adapted to benthic life, feeding primarily along the ocean floor.
Environmental scientists study benthic habitats to assess the health of aquatic ecosystems.
The benthic zone supports a diverse range of organisms, including many types of crustaceans and worms.
