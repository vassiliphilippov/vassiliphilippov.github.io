unreflecting

adjective
not shiny or reflective
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
not reflecting light, heat, or sound; having a dull or non-shiny surface or appearance

Examples

Unreflecting surface
The unreflecting surface of the blackboard made it easier for students to see the writing.
He preferred unreflecting sunglasses because they did not produce glare in bright sunlight.
The artist chose an unreflecting finish for the sculpture to give it a more natural look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - not shiny or bright
  • synonym: matte - having a dull, non-shiny surface
  • antonym: reflective - capable of reflecting light or other energy
