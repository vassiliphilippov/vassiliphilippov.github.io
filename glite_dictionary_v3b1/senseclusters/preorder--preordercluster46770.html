preorder

noun
advance product order
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌɔːdə / (BrE)
an order placed for a product before it is officially available for sale or release

Examples

A preorder bonus
Many fans placed a preorder for the limited edition game to ensure they received it on release day.
The store offered a special discount for customers who made a preorder before the launch date.
Her preorder was confirmed by email, and she looked forward to getting the new book first.

Related Terms

  • synonym: order - a request to purchase a product or service
  • synonym: purchase - the act of buying something
  • synonym: reservation - an arrangement to secure something in advance
