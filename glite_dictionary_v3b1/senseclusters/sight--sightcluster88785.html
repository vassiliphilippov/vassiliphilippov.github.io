sight

noun
range within which something can be seen
4.2
Pronunciation: / saɪt / (AmE), / saɪt / (BrE)
the distance or area within which something can be seen by the eye

Examples

Out of sight
Several ships sailed out of sight as the fog thickened.
The lighthouse was barely within sight from the shore.
We waited until the mountain came into sight before starting our climb.
