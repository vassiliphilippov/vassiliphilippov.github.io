wicketkeeper

noun
cricket fielding position
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkɪtˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪtˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a player in cricket who stands behind the stumps to catch the ball and attempt to dismiss the batsman

Examples

The wicketkeeper catches ball
The wicketkeeper made a brilliant catch to dismiss the batsman.
Every cricket team relies on a skilled wicketkeeper to prevent runs and take catches.
After the bowler delivered the ball, the wicketkeeper was ready to react quickly behind the stumps.
