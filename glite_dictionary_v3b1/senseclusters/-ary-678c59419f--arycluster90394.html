-ary

x
adjective or noun suffix
7.1
Pronunciation: / ɛri / (AmE), / əri / (BrE)
a suffix used to form adjectives meaning 'related to' and nouns meaning 'a place for' or 'a person connected with'

Examples

Adjectives with -ary ending
The word 'stationary' uses the suffix -ary to mean 'related to staying in one place'.
In 'secretary', the suffix -ary indicates a person connected with a particular job.
A 'dormitory' is similar to a 'library', where the suffix -ary means 'a place for'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -ory - suffix forming adjectives and nouns, often meaning 'related to' or 'place for'
