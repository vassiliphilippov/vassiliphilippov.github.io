beating

noun
the act of defeating or outdoing
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of defeating or outdoing someone, especially in a competition or contest

Examples

A heavy beating
After last week's beating, they trained even harder for the next game.
Our team suffered a heavy beating in the final match.
The chess champion gave his opponent a thorough beating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defeat - an instance of beating someone in a contest or battle
  • antonym: loss - being beaten in a competition
  • synonym: victory - the act of winning or prevailing
