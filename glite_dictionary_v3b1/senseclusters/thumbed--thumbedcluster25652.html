thumbed

adjective
worn from frequent handling
Pronunciation: / θʌmd / (AmE), / θʌmd / (BrE)
(of pages or books) showing signs of wear or being soiled from frequent handling or turning by thumb and fingers.

Examples

A thumbed book
Library books are often thumbed from years of being borrowed.
She handed me a thumbed guidebook filled with handwritten notes.
The novel was so popular that its pages became thumbed and worn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: well-thumbed - (of books or pages) showing signs of frequent handling
