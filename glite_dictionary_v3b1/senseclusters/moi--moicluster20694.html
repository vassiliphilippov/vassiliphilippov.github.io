moi

pronoun
French 'me' pronoun
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / mwa / (AmE), / mwa / (BrE)
the French first-person singular object pronoun meaning 'me', used after prepositions or for emphasis

Examples

French moi pronoun
In the sentence 'C'est moi', the word means 'It's me' in English.
French learners often confuse 'je' and moi when forming sentences.
After a preposition in French, you must use moi instead of 'je'.
