monkeytail

noun
curved decorative mark
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkiˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌteɪl / (BrE)
a curved mark or projection resembling a monkey's tail, especially in typography or architecture

Examples

A railing monkeytail
The architect added a monkeytail to the railing for a playful touch.
Some old fonts include a monkeytail on the capital Q for style.
He admired the elegant monkeytail at the end of the signature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swash - a decorative flourish or extension on a letter in typography
