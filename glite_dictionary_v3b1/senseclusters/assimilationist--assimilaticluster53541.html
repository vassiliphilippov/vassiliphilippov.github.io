assimilationist

noun
integration supporter
Pronunciation: / əˌsɪməˈleɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / əˌsɪmɪˈleɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes the integration of different groups into a common culture or society

Examples

An assimilationist thinker
The assimilationist argued that immigrants should adopt the language and customs of their new country.
As an assimilationist, she believed in blending cultural differences to create unity.
Critics of the assimilationist approach say it can erase important cultural identities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integrationist - a person who supports the mixing of different groups into one society
  • antonym: segregationist - a person who supports keeping different groups separate
