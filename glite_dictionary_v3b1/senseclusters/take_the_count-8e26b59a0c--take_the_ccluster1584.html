take the count

verb
be counted out in boxing
9.8 7.0
Pronunciation: /teɪk ðə kaʊnt/ (AmE), /teɪk ðə kaʊnt/ (BrE)
(of a boxer) to remain down on the floor during the referee's ten count, resulting in a knockout defeat.
