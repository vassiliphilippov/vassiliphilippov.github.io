skivvy

noun
female servant for menial tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪvi / (AmE), / ˈskɪvi / (BrE)
(chiefly British) a female domestic servant who performs menial and unpleasant tasks.

Examples

A skivvy girl
No one appreciates being made to feel like a skivvy at work.
She worked as a skivvy in a large Victorian house for several years.
The young girl was treated like a skivvy by her wealthy employers.
