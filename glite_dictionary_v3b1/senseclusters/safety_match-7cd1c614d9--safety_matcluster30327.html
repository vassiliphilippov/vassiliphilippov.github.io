safety match

noun
match that lights only on special surfaces
5.7 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪf.tiː mætʃ/ /; / /ˈseɪf.tɪ mætʃ/ (AmE), /ˈseɪf.tiː mætʃ/ /; / /ˈseɪf.tɪ mætʃ/ (BrE)
a match that can only be ignited by striking it against a specially prepared surface, reducing the risk of accidental ignition

Examples
