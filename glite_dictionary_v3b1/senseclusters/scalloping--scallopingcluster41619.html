scalloping

noun
catching scallops
Pronunciation: / ˈskæləpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskæləpɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of catching scallops for food, usually by dredging or diving in the sea

Examples

Scalloping for shellfish
They spent the afternoon scalloping in the shallow waters near the island.
Local restaurants serve fresh dishes thanks to the region's active scalloping industry.
During the summer season, many tourists try scalloping for the first time.
