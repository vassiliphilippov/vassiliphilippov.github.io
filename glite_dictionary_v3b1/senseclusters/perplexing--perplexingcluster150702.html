perplexing

adjective
causing confusion or puzzlement
3.2
Pronunciation: / pərˈplɛksɪŋ / (AmE), / pəˈplɛksɪŋ / (BrE)
causing confusion or difficulty in understanding; hard to explain or comprehend

Examples

A perplexing problem
Her sudden change in attitude was perplexing to everyone at the office.
The directions were so perplexing that many students got lost on their way.
The scientist encountered a perplexing problem while conducting his experiment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to understand; not confusing
  • synonym: confusing - difficult to understand; not clear
  • synonym: puzzling - causing one to be puzzled; hard to explain
