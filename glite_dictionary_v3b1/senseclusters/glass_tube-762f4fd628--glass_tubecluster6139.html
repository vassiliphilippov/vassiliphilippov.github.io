glass tube

noun
cylindrical glass laboratory container
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs tuːb / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs tjuːb / (BrE)
a cylindrical container or piece of equipment made of glass, typically used in laboratories for holding, mixing, or conducting substances.
