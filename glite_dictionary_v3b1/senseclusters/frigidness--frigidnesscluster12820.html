frigidness

noun
sexual unresponsiveness, esp. in women
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪdʒɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The condition of being sexually unresponsive or unable to achieve orgasm, especially in women.

Examples

Frigidness in intimacy
Open communication may help couples address issues of frigidness in their relationship.
Some older medical texts attribute frigidness to psychological factors rather than physical causes.
The therapist discussed her client's frigidness and its impact on her marriage.
