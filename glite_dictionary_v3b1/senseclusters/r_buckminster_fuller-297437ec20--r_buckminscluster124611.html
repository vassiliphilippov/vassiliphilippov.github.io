R. Buckminster Fuller

proper noun
U.S. architect and inventor, geodesic dome creator
Pronunciation: / ˌɑr ˌbʌkmɪnstər ˈfʊlər / (AmE), / ˌɑː ˌbʌkmɪnstə ˈfʊlə / (BrE)
(1895–1983) United States architect, inventor, and visionary best known for inventing the geodesic dome and his influential ideas on design and sustainability.
