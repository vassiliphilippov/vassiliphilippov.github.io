glacier lily

noun
mountain plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪʃər ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪsɪə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A perennial plant of western North America, noted for its bright yellow, nodding flowers, commonly found near receding snow in alpine regions.

Examples

Bright glacier lilies
Hikers spotted several glacier lilies near the edge of the retreating glacier.
The glacier lily blooms soon after the snow melts in the high mountain meadows.
The bright yellow flowers of the glacier lily add color to the alpine landscape in spring.
