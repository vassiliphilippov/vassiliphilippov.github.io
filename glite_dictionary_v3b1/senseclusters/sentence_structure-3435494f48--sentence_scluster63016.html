sentence structure

noun
grammatical arrangement of words in sentences
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntəns ˌstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntəns ˌstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the way in which words, phrases, and clauses are arranged to form sentences in a language
