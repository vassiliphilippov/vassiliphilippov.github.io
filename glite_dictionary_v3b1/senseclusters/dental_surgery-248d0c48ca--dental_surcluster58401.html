dental surgery

noun
branch of dentistry involving surgery
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl ˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry that involves surgical procedures for the treatment of diseases, injuries, or defects of the teeth, jaws, and mouth

Examples

Dental surgery treatment
Advances in dental surgery have made many procedures less invasive and more comfortable for patients.
After years of pain, he finally underwent dental surgery to correct his jaw alignment.
She was referred to a specialist for dental surgery to remove her impacted wisdom teeth.
