Grossulariaceae

A family of flowering plants, including currants and gooseberries, whose fruit is a berry; sometimes considered part of Saxifragaceae in certain classifications.

Examples

Grossulariaceae plant family
Botanists sometimes classify currants under Grossulariaceae instead of Saxifragaceae.
The Grossulariaceae family is well known for producing edible berries such as gooseberries and currants.
The leaves and fruiting habits of Grossulariaceae species are studied in horticulture.
