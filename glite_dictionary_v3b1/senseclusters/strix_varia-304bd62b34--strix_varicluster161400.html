Strix varia

noun
large North American owl (barred owl)
Pronunciation: / ˌstraɪks ˈvɛəriə / (AmE), / ˌstrɪks ˈveəriə / (BrE)
A large owl of eastern North America with a streaked brown breast and abdomen, commonly known as the barred owl.
