coal face

noun
active front line of work
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊl feɪs / (AmE), / kəʊl feɪs / (BrE)
The most active or direct part of a job, organization, or activity, especially where work is actually being done rather than planned or managed.

Examples

At the coal face
As a teacher, she feels she is right at the coal face of education reform.
Policy decisions often seem disconnected from the realities faced by staff at the coal face.
Working at the coal face of customer service can be both challenging and rewarding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: front line - the most advanced, direct, or active position in an activity or organization
