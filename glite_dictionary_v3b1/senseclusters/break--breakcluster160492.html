break

noun
billiards/pool opening shot
3.0
Pronunciation: / breɪk / (AmE), / breɪk / (BrE)
the initial shot in billiards or pool that disperses the balls from their starting position

Examples

A pool break
After a disappointing break, the balls remained tightly clustered near the center.
He lined up for the break, hoping to scatter the balls widely across the table.
Her powerful break set her up for an easy first shot.
