untypical

adjective
not typical; not representative
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɪpɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪpɪkəl / (BrE)
not representative of a group, class, or type; not typical

Examples

An untypical reaction
Her reaction was quite untypical of her usual calm demeanor.
It's untypical for him to be late to meetings.
The weather this week has been very untypical for June in this region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atypical - not typical; not usual or representative
  • antonym: typical - having the distinctive qualities of a particular type of person or thing
