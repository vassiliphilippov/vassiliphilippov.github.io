severalfold

adverb
by several times
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛvrəlˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈsɛvərəlˌfəʊld / (BrE)
by several times as much or as many; multiplied by several times.

Examples

Grew severalfold
Profits have grown severalfold since the company launched its new product line.
The number of tourists visiting the city increased severalfold after the festival was advertised internationally.
Her responsibilities at work expanded severalfold after her recent promotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multiple - by more than one time; several times as much
  • antonym: single - only one; not multiplied
