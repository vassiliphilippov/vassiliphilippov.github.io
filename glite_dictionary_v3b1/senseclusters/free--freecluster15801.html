free

adverb
not restrained; at liberty
Pronunciation: / fri / (AmE), / friː / (BrE)
Without being confined, restrained, or controlled; at liberty.

Examples

Fly free
After years in captivity, the eagle flew free again over the mountains.
Children ran free in the open fields, laughing and shouting.
The wild horses roamed free across the plains.

  • synonym: loose - not fastened, restrained, or confined
