pudden-head

noun
foolish or unintelligent person
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdənˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpʌdənˌhɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is not very intelligent; a fool

Examples

A pudden-head person
Don't be a pudden-head—think before you act!
Everyone called him a pudden-head after he forgot his own birthday.
Only a pudden-head would believe such an obvious lie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: genius - a person of extraordinary intellectual power
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense
