war

verb
to engage in war
5.9 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / w…îr / (AmE), / w…îÀê / (BrE)
to make or wage war; to engage in active conflict or hostilities

Examples

To war with rivals
For generations, the rival tribes have warred with each other.
The two neighboring countries threatened to war over the disputed border.
While some sought peace, others chose to war against their enemies.

Synonyms

  • go to war

    go to war means to begin fighting between nations or groups, while war means to wage active hostilities more broadly.
  • fight
    1.0 153/m
    fight is a direct physical or personal clash, while war means organized large-scale conflict between countries or groups.
