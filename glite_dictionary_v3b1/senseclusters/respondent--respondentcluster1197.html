respondent

noun
person who replies or answers
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑːndənt / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
a person who replies or answers, especially to a survey or in a court case

Examples

The respondent answers
During the interview, the respondent answered all of the questions candidly.
Each respondent was asked to rate their satisfaction with the service.
The survey received feedback from over one hundred respondents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defendant - person against whom a legal claim is brought
  • antonym: plaintiff - person who brings a case against another in court
  • synonym: respondent - defendant in a legal proceeding
