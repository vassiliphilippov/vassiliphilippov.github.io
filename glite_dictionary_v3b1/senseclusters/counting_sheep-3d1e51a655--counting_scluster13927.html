counting sheep

noun
imaginary counting of sheep to induce sleep
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntɪŋ ʃip / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntɪŋ ʃiːp / (BrE)
a mental exercise in which one imagines counting sheep, used as a way to help fall asleep

Examples

To count sheep to sleep
As a child, my grandmother often told me to start counting sheep if I woke up in the night.
I tried counting sheep when I couldn't fall asleep, but it didn't really work.
She recommends counting sheep to anyone who struggles with insomnia.
