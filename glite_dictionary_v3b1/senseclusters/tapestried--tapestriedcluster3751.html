tapestried

adjective
decorated with tapestry
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpəstraɪd / (AmE), / ˈtæpɪstrid / (BrE)
hung or decorated with tapestry; covered in or adorned with woven designs

Examples

Tapestried walls
Guests marveled at the tapestried ceilings, which gave the ballroom a magnificent and historic atmosphere.
She admired the tapestried halls as she passed, each telling a different story of the estate's history.
The walls of the old manor were richly tapestried, casting a sense of opulence throughout the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tapestry - a piece of thick textile fabric with woven designs
