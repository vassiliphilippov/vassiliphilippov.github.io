in the countryside

preposition
in a rural area
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə ˈkʌntriˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈkʌntrɪsaɪd / (BrE)
in a rural area, away from towns and cities.

Examples

Living in the countryside
Living in the countryside offers a quieter lifestyle compared to the bustle of the city.
Many people dream of owning a small cottage in the countryside after they retire.
They spent their summer vacation in the countryside, enjoying the fresh air and beautiful scenery.
