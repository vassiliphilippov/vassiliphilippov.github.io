phenylhydrazine

noun
chemical reagent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛnəlhaɪˈdræziːn /; / ˌfɛnəlˈhaɪdrəˌziːn / (AmE), / ˌfiːnɪlhaɪˈdræziːn /; / ˌfiːnɪlˈhaɪdrəziːn / (BrE)
a chemical compound used in laboratory analysis, organic synthesis, and research (can be toxic and used to destroy red blood cells in experiments)
