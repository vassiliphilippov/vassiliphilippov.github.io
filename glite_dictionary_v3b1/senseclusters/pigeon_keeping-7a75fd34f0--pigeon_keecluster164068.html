pigeon keeping

noun
breeding and caring for domestic pigeons
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒən ˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn ˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of breeding and caring for domestic pigeons, often as a hobby or for sport

There are clubs and competitions dedicated to the art of pigeon keeping worldwide.
