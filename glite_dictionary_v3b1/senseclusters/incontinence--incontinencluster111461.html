incontinence

noun
lack of self-restraint (esp. sensual pleasures)
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑntənəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒntɪnəns / (BrE)
lack of self-restraint or control, especially with regard to indulgence in sensuous pleasures.

Examples

Moral incontinence
Ancient philosophers debated the consequences of incontinence for moral character.
The play portrays a tragic hero whose incontinence with respect to desire brings ruin.
Unable to resist temptation, his incontinence ultimately led to his downfall.
