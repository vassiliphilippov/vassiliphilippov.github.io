repair

noun
the act of fixing something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛr / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛə / (BrE)
the act of restoring something to good or working condition

Examples

A repair job
After the storm, the roof required immediate repair.
She took her watch to the shop for repair.
The car was out of service for a week while it was undergoing repair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: damage - physical harm that impairs value or usefulness
  • synonym: fix - the act of repairing something
