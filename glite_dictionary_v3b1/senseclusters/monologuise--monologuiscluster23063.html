monologuise

verb
talk to oneself at length
7.4
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑl əˌɡaɪz / (AmE), / məˈnɒl əˌɡaɪz / (BrE)
to talk to oneself, especially at length or in the form of a monologue

Examples

To monologuise alone
He began to monologuise about his day's events as he walked alone.
She would monologuise in an empty room, rehearsing her speech.
Writers sometimes monologuise their characters to explore their inner thoughts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soliloquize - to speak one's thoughts aloud when by oneself
