refinement

noun
subtle distinction or nuance
6.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a subtle or fine distinction in meaning, opinion, or attitude; a nuance

Examples

Refinement of meaning
His argument contains a refinement of the traditional theory.
She appreciates every refinement of language and expression.
The final proposal included an important refinement that made it more acceptable to everyone.
