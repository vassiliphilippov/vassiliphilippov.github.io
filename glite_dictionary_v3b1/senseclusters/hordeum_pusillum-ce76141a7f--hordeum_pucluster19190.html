Hordeum pusillum

noun
a species of annual barley
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɔrˈdiːəm pjuˈsɪləm / (AmE), / hɔːˈdiːəm pjuːˈsɪləm / (BrE)
A species of annual barley native to western North America and widespread in the southern United States and tropical America.
