overcast sky

noun
sky covered with clouds
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkæst skaɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkɑːst skaɪ / (BrE)
A sky that is completely covered with clouds, with no direct sunlight visible.

Examples

An overcast sky
An overcast sky made the entire landscape look dull and gray.
Photographers often prefer an overcast sky because the lighting is soft and even.
The children played outside under the overcast sky, hoping it wouldn't rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear sky - a sky with few or no clouds
  • synonym: cloudy sky - a sky partially or fully covered with clouds
