Fortunella margarita

noun
shrub producing oval kumquats
9.0
Pronunciation: / fɔrˌtuːnɛlə mɑːrˈɡɛrɪtə / (AmE), / fɔːˌtjuːnɛlə mɑːˈɡærɪtə / (BrE)
A shrub native to East Asia, bearing oval kumquat fruits with an edible rind and sweet-tart flavor.

Examples

Fortunella margarita shrub
Botanists classify the oval kumquat plant as Fortunella margarita due to its distinctive fruit shape.
Ornamental Fortunella margarita shrubs are popular in subtropical landscapes for their fruit and beauty.
The Fortunella margarita in the garden is heavy with bright orange kumquats.
