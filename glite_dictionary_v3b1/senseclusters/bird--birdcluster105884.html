bird

noun
sound/gesture of derision or contempt
Pronunciation: / bɝd / (AmE), / bɜːd / (BrE)
a sound or gesture of derision or contempt, especially made by booing or hissing at a performer

Examples

Give the bird
Several players gave the umpire the bird after he made a controversial call.
She was so bad on stage that the crowd gave her the bird before she finished her song.
The comedian received the bird from the audience after his offensive joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boo - an exclamation or sound expressing disapproval or contempt
  • antonym: cheer - a shout of approval or encouragement
