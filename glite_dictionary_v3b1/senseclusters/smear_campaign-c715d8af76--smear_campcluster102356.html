smear campaign

noun
coordinated attack on reputation
Pronunciation: / smɪr kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / smɪə kæmˈpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized effort to harm someone's reputation, especially by making false or unfair accusations

Examples

A smear campaign online
After the smear campaign in the tabloids, her public image suffered greatly.
He denied all allegations, claiming they were part of a deliberate smear campaign.
The opposition party orchestrated a smear campaign to undermine the government's credibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: character assassination - the malicious and unjustified harming of a person's good reputation
  • synonym: defamation - the act of damaging someone's good reputation; slander or libel
