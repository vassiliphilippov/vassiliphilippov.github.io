hutch

verb
to cage an animal
7.9
Pronunciation: / hʌtʃt / (AmE), / hʌtʃt / (BrE)
to put an animal into a hutch or small enclosed cage; to confine in a hutch

Examples

To hutch a rabbit

The farmer hutched the rabbits each night to keep them safe from foxes.
After cleaning the cages, she hutched the guinea pigs with fresh bedding and food.
