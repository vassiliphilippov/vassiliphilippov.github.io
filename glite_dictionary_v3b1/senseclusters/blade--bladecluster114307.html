blade

noun
cut of beef from the shoulder
6.0
Pronunciation: / bleɪd / (AmE), / bleɪd / (BrE)
A cut of beef taken from the shoulder blade area of a cow, often used for braising or slow-cooking.

Examples

Blade steak
Chefs often recommend the blade because it is both affordable and tasty.
The recipe calls for a blade steak, which becomes tender after slow cooking.
You can ask the butcher for a blade cut if you want a flavorful roast.
