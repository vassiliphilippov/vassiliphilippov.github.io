ppm

noun
printing speed unit
4.9
Pronunciation: / pi pi ɛm / (AmE), / piː piː em / (BrE)
a measure of printing speed indicating the number of pages a printer can produce in one minute

Examples

Printer ppm speed
The new printer can reach up to 40 ppm, making it ideal for busy offices.
When comparing printers, check the ppm to see which one is faster.
A higher ppm means the printer can handle large print jobs more efficiently.
