Synonyms

  • put out
    3.6 9.74/m
    put out is a casual phrasal verb for issuing books or magazines, while publish is the neutral term for preparing and distributing them.
  • bring out
    2.7 0.56/m
    bring out is often used for launching a new book or product with publicity, while publish focuses on preparing and making material public.
  • release
    1.8 59/m
    release is used for making films, software, or statements available at a set time, while publish mainly refers to books or articles.
  • issue
    2.5 63/m
    issue is a formal act of giving documents, statements, or editions, while publish is about preparing and distributing written works.

  • antonym: withhold - refuse to publish or make public
