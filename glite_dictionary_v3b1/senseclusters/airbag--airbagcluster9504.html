airbag

noun
inflatable safety device
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a protective inflatable device built into clothing or equipment that inflates on impact to reduce injury

Examples

Jacket airbag protection
The cyclist's jacket contained an airbag that inflated instantly during the accident.
Modern motorcycle vests often include an airbag for extra protection in a crash.
After the fall, the airbag in his protective suit helped prevent serious injuries.
