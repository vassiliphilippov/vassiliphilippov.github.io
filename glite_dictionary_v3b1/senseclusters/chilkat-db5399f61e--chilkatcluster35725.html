Chilkat

proper noun
Tlingit subgroup member
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪlkæt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlkæt / (BrE)
a member of the Chilkat, a subgroup of the Tlingit Indigenous people of southeastern Alaska and northwestern British Columbia

Examples

The Chilkat people
The Chilkat are renowned for their intricate weaving and traditional art forms.
A delegation of Chilkat elders attended the cultural heritage conference in Juneau.
Many Chilkat continue to preserve their language and customs in modern Alaska.
