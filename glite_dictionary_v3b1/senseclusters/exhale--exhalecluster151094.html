exhale

verb
to breathe out
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /eks.ˈheɪl/ /; / /ɪks.ˈheɪl/ (AmE), /eks.ˈheɪl/ /; / /ɪks.ˈheɪl/ (BrE)
