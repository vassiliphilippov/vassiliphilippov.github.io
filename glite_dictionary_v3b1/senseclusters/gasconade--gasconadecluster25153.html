gasconade

verb
to boast or show off extravagantly
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæs.kəˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌɡæs.kəˈneɪd / (BrE)
to boast or brag in a showy or extravagant way; to show off

Examples

To gasconade loudly
He would often gasconade about his exploits in the most exaggerated fashion.
She gasconaded endlessly at the dinner table, hoping to impress everyone.
Tired of listening to him gasconade, his friends left early.
