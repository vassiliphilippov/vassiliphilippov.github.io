subdermal

adjective
beneath the skin
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈdɜrməl / (AmE), / sʌbˈdɜːməl / (BrE)
located or placed beneath the skin

Examples

Subdermal implant
The nurse checked the subdermal implant to ensure it was working properly.
A subdermal injection delivers medication just below the surface of the skin.
Scientists are developing new subdermal sensors to monitor blood sugar levels.
