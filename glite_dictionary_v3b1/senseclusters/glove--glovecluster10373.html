glove

verb
to catch with glove
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ɡlʌv / (BrE)
to catch or stop a ball using a glove, especially in sports like baseball or softball

Examples

To glove a ball
The outfielder quickly gloved the ball before it could hit the ground.
She expertly gloved the fast-moving line drive during practice.
The player gloved the ball and threw it to first base for the out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catch - to capture or seize something that is moving
  • antonym: miss - to fail to catch or reach something
