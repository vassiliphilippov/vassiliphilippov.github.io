dustpanful

noun
amount held by a dustpan
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌpænˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈdʌstpænˌfʊl / (BrE)
The amount that a dustpan can hold.

Examples

A dustpanful of dirt
After sweeping, he was surprised by the dustpanful of dirt he collected.
It took three dustpanfuls to clean up the broken glass.
She emptied a dustpanful of crumbs into the bin.
