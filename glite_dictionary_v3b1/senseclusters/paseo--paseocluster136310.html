paseo

noun
bullfighters' parade
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈseɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / pæˈseɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
the ceremonial parade of bullfighters entering the ring at the beginning of a bullfight

Examples

Bullfighters' paseo
The paseo marked the official start of the bullfight as the matadors entered the arena.
Spectators applauded during the paseo, admiring the colorful costumes of the bullfighters.
Before the action began, the paseo set a solemn and traditional tone for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procession - a formal parade or march, often ceremonial
