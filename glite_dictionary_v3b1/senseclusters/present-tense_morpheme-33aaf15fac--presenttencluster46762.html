present-tense morpheme

noun
morpheme marking present tense
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzənt tɛns ˈmɔrfiːm / (AmE), / ˈprɛznt tɛns ˈmɔːfiːm / (BrE)
a linguistic morpheme added to a verb to indicate present tense

Examples

The present-tense morpheme -s
In English, the -s in 'runs' is the third-person singular present-tense morpheme.
Many languages use a specific present-tense morpheme to distinguish habitual actions.
The absence of a present-tense morpheme in some verbs can make tense ambiguous.
