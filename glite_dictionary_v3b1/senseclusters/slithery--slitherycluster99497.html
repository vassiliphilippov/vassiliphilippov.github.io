slithery

adjective
smooth, slippery quality
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪðəri / (AmE), / ˈslɪðəri / (BrE)
having a smooth and slippery surface or quality that makes it easy for something to slide or move smoothly

Examples

Slithery snake skin
Snakes have slithery skins that help them move smoothly across the ground.
The slithery fish slipped through my fingers and fell back into the water.
Walking on the slithery rocks by the riverbank was quite dangerous after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slippery - difficult to hold or stand on because it is smooth, wet, or slimy
