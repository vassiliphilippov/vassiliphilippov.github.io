flea-brained

adjective
having nonsensical or silly ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈfliˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈfliːˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
Having silly, foolish, or nonsensical ideas; lacking seriousness or sense.

Examples

Flea-brained idea
Calling that plan flea-brained is putting it mildly, if you ask me.
His flea-brained suggestions at the meeting left everyone confused.
She came up with another flea-brained scheme to get rich overnight.
