cushat

noun
Eurasian wood pigeon with white markings
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊʃæt / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃat / (BrE)
A Eurasian pigeon, specifically the wood pigeon (Columba palumbus), known for its distinctive white patches on the wings and neck.
