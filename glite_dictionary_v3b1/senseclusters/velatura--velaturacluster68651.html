velatura

noun
thin paint layer technique
9.9
Pronunciation: /ve.lə.ˈtuː.rə/ /; / /və.lə.ˈtuː.rə/ (AmE), /ve.lə.ˈtʊə.rə/ /; / /ve.lə.ˈtuː.rə/ (BrE)
a painting technique where a thin, semi-transparent layer of paint or glaze is applied to change the color, tone, or finish of a painting
