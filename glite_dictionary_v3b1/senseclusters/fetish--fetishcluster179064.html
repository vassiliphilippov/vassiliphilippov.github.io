fetish

noun
sexual desire focused on a specific object or body part
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪʃ / (BrE)
a form of sexual desire in which gratification depends to an abnormal degree on a particular object, item of clothing, or part of the body
