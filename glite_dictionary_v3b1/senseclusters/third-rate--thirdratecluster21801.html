third-rate

adjective
of very poor or inferior quality
Pronunciation: / θɝd reɪt / (AmE), / θɜːd reɪt / (BrE)
of very poor quality; inferior to what is considered average or acceptable

Examples

A third-rate production
Critics described the play as a third-rate production with poor acting and direction.
She was disappointed to receive a third-rate product that didn't match the advertisement.
The hotel offered third-rate service and outdated amenities.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: first-rate - of the highest quality; excellent
  • degree lower: fourth-rate - of worse quality than third-rate; extremely poor
  • degree higher: second-rate - of mediocre or average quality
