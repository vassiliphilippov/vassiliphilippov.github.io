Examples

Synonyms

  • despoilment
    9.8 0.01/m
    despoilment is the forceful stripping of valuables from land or people, while spoliation is the legal term for wrongful taking.
  • plundering
    6.0 0.21/m
    plundering is stealing valuable things during war or disorder, while spoliation stresses wrongful forceful taking of property.
  • pillaging
    7.7 0.40/m
    pillaging is violent stealing and destruction during war, while spoliation covers broader wrongful seizure of goods.
  • looting
    4.9 3.66/m
    looting is stealing from shops or buildings during riots or war, while spoliation implies formal wrongful plundering.
