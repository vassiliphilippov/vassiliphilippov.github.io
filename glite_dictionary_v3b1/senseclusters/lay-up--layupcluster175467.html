lay-up

noun
basketball shot near the basket
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈleɪ ˌʌp / (BrE)
In basketball, a shot made with one hand close to the basket, typically banked off the backboard.

Examples

A lay-up shot
He missed a crucial lay-up in the final seconds of the game.
She scored an easy lay-up after stealing the ball.
The coach taught the players how to perfect their lay-up technique.
