Digitaria

proper noun
genus of grasses
Pronunciation: / dɪˈdʒɪtəriə / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɪtəriə / (BrE)
a genus of grasses commonly known as crabgrass, often found as weeds in lawns and gardens

Examples

Digitaria in lawns
Digitaria can quickly spread across lawns if not controlled early in the season.
Many gardeners struggle to remove Digitaria from their flower beds each summer.
Some species of Digitaria are used as forage crops in certain regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crabgrass - a common name for several species in the Digitaria genus
