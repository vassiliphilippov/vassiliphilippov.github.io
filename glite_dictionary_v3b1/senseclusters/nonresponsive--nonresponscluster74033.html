nonresponsive

adjective
not reacting to treatment
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒnsɪv / (BrE)
not reacting or showing improvement after treatment, therapy, or intervention as expected.

Examples

Nonresponsive to treatment
The infection was nonresponsive to the antibiotics prescribed by the doctor.
Despite several attempts at therapy, the patient remained nonresponsive to all treatments.
Doctors considered alternative options when the tumor proved nonresponsive to chemotherapy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively to something
  • synonym: unresponsive - not reacting or responding to something
