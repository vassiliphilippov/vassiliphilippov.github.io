commit

verb
transfer to memory or writing
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt / (BrE)
to transfer (words, information, or thoughts) to a medium such as memory or writing so that they can be preserved or remembered

Examples

To commit words to memory
He committed the entire speech to memory over the weekend.
It can be difficult to commit scientific terms to memory without frequent review.
She commits her thoughts to paper every night before bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memorize - to learn by heart
  • synonym: record - to set down in writing for preservation
