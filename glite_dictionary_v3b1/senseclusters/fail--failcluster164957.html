fail

verb
become bankrupt; cease operations
Pronunciation: / feɪl / (AmE), / feɪl / (BrE)
(of a business or financial institution) to become bankrupt or insolvent and cease operations.

Examples

Fail in business
Several small banks failed during the economic crisis.
The company failed after a series of poor investments and mounting debts.
When the market crashed, many businesses failed within months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go bankrupt - to become unable to pay debts and be declared bankrupt
