navigational chart

noun
map for navigation purposes
Pronunciation: / ˌnævəˈɡeɪʃənəl tʃɑrt / (AmE), / ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃ(ə)nəl tʃɑːt / (BrE)
A map specifically designed for navigation, showing meteorological, hydrographic, and navigational conditions to assist navigators.

Examples

Modern navigational charts
Modern navigational charts include electronic updates to reflect real-time conditions.
Pilots rely on a navigational chart to avoid hazardous weather along their route.
The captain studied the navigational chart before setting a course across the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aeronautical chart - a chart specifically for air navigation
  • synonym: nautical chart - a chart specifically for marine navigation
