Jezebel

noun
shameless or scheming woman
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛzəˌbɛl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛzəbɛl / (BrE)
a woman who is regarded as shameless, wicked, or scheming (often used as an insult, based on the biblical queen Jezebel)
