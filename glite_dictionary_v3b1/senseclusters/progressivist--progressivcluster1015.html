progressivist

noun
supporter of progress
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪvɪst / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or believes in progressive ideas, reforms, or policies, especially in politics or education

Examples

A progressivist leader
The progressivist campaigned for new laws to improve public education.
As a known progressivist, she often spoke about the need for social reform.
Many progressivists attended the rally to support environmental policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - a person who resists change and prefers traditional values
  • synonym: progressive - a person who advocates for social or political reform
