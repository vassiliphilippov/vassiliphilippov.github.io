tuxedo

noun
semiformal evening suit for men
3.0 3.26/m
Pronunciation: / t åkÀàsiÀêdo ä / (AmE), / t åkÀàsiÀêd…ô ä / (BrE)
A man's semiformal evening jacket, usually black, worn with matching trousers and typically accompanied by a bow tie; also refers to the complete suit.

Examples

A tuxedo jacket
He looked very handsome in his tuxedo at the wedding.
Renting a tuxedo is common for formal events like proms or galas.
She spilled wine on his white tuxedo jacket during the reception.

Synonyms

  • tux
    3.7 2.11/m
    tux is the short, informal name often used in speech for the same evening suit, while tuxedo is the formal term.
  • dinner jacket
    3.1 0.29/m
    dinner jacket is the single jacket worn for evening dress, whereas tuxedo often names the whole matched suit including trousers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dinner jacket - Formal jacket for men, British term for tuxedo
