spotted weakfish

noun
fish of the US Atlantic and Gulf Coasts
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːtɪd ˈwiːkfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈwiːkfɪʃ / (BrE)
A weakfish found along the southern Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of the United States, distinguished by its spotted appearance.

Examples

The spotted weakfish fish
In the summer, large schools of spotted weakfish migrate closer to the shore.
The spotted weakfish is a popular catch among anglers along the Gulf Coast.
You can recognize a spotted weakfish by the distinct spots along its back and sides.
