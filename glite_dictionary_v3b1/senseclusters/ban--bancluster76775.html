ban

noun
logarithmic unit of information (base 10)
9.9
Pronunciation: /bæn/ (AmE), /bæn/ (BrE)
a logarithmic unit of information or entropy, equal to the amount conveyed by an event of probability 0.1, using base 10 logarithms

Examples

A ban of information
A result of 0.5 ban means the event has a probability of about 0.32.
In some fields, the ban is used instead of the bit to express information content.
