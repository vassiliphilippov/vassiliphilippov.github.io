aftermost

adjective
nearest the stern or tail
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæftɚˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəməʊst / (BrE)
Situated nearest to the stern (rear) or tail of a vessel or vehicle.

Examples

The aftermost part
He inspected the aftermost part of the ship for any damage.
The aftermost cabin is often quieter than those closer to the bow.
The crew stored the supplies in the aftermost hold for easy access.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foremost - situated closest to the front or bow
