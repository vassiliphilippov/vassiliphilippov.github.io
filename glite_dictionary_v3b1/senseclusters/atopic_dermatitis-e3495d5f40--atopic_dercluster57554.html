atopic dermatitis

noun
chronic allergic skin condition
Pronunciation: / əˈtoʊpɪk ˌdɜːrməˈtaɪtɪs / (AmE), / əˈtɒpɪk ˌdɜːməˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A chronic inflammatory skin condition characterized by itchy, red, and swollen skin, often associated with allergies.

Examples

Atopic dermatitis symptoms
Children with atopic dermatitis may experience intense itching, especially at night.
Managing atopic dermatitis often involves moisturizing the skin and avoiding known allergens.
The doctor diagnosed her with atopic dermatitis after examining the persistent rash on her arms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eczema - a general term for inflammatory skin conditions, often synonymous with atopic dermatitis
