ml

noun
metric unit of liquid volume (milliliter)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛl / (AmE), / ɛm ɛl / (BrE)
a metric unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a liter, commonly used to measure liquids

Examples

100 ml of water
