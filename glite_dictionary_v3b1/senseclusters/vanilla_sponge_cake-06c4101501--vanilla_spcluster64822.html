vanilla sponge cake

noun
light vanilla-flavored cake
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪl.ə spʌndʒ keɪk / (AmE), / vəˈnɪl.ə spʌndʒ keɪk / (BrE)
A light, airy cake flavored with vanilla, made by whisking eggs with sugar and flour.

Examples

A slice of vanilla sponge cake
After dinner, we shared a slice of homemade vanilla sponge cake.
For her birthday, she requested a vanilla sponge cake with fresh strawberries and cream.
The bakery is known for its soft and fluffy vanilla sponge cake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chocolate sponge cake - a light, airy cake with chocolate flavor
  • synonym: sponge cake - a light, airy cake made with beaten eggs, sugar, and flour
