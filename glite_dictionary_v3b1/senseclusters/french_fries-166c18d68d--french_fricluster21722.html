French fries

noun
deep-fried potato strips
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ fraɪz / (AmE), / frɛntʃ fraɪz / (BrE)
strips of potato that are deep-fried and eaten as a snack or side dish, typically with salt or other seasonings

Examples

A plate of French fries
He ordered a large serving of French fries at the cafe.
Kids often dip French fries in ketchup.
Would you like some French fries with your burger?

Related Terms

  • synonym: chips - slices of potato fried and served as food (British English)
