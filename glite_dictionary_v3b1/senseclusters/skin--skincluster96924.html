skin

noun
thin outer covering of an object
6.1 1.0
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
a thin outer layer or covering of an object, often serving as protection or a surface finish

Examples

Plastic skin
A thin plastic skin covered the device to protect it from dust.
The skin of the aircraft was made from lightweight aluminum alloy.
When the skin of the drum is damaged, the instrument loses its sound quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shell - the hard outer covering of something, especially in technical contexts
