escaper

noun
one who escapes
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskeɪpər / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪpə / (BrE)
a person or animal that escapes from a place or situation where they were confined or trapped

Examples

The escaper fled
The police searched the area for the escaper after the alarm was triggered.
One escaper from the wildlife reserve was found hiding in a nearby field.
The movie tells the story of an escaper who outsmarts his captors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fugitive - a person who is running away, especially from the law
  • antonym: prisoner - a person who is kept in prison or captivity
