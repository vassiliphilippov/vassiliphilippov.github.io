well-formed

adjective
grammatically correct; properly structured
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛlˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
(of a word, phrase, sentence, etc.) correctly constructed according to the rules of grammar or accepted usage.

Examples

A well-formed sentence
Computer languages require commands to be well-formed for the code to run properly.
He struggled to write a well-formed question in English.
Her essay was praised for its well-formed sentences and clear structure.
