go-kart

noun
small motor racing vehicle
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ ˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ ˌkɑːt / (BrE)
a small, low motor vehicle with four wheels and an open frame, designed for racing or recreation

Examples

A go-kart race
Children and adults alike enjoy competitive go-kart tracks at amusement parks.
He built his own go-kart using a lawnmower engine and old bicycle parts.
Racing a go-kart can be an exciting way to spend a Saturday afternoon.
