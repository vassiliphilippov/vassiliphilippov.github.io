electrical conduction

noun
passage of electricity through a conductor
1.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktrɪkəl kənˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrɪkəl kənˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the passage of an electric current through a conductor, such as a metal wire or electrolyte
