sannyasi

noun
Hindu religious ascetic
9.9
Pronunciation: / sænˈjɑːsi / (AmE), / sænˈjɑːsi / (BrE)
a Hindu religious ascetic who has renounced worldly life to pursue spiritual liberation

Examples

A sannyasi monk
He dedicated his life to meditation after becoming a sannyasi in his early thirties.
Many people seek guidance from a sannyasi for advice on leading a spiritual life.
The sannyasi wandered from village to village, surviving on alms and teaching spiritual lessons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetic - a person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention
