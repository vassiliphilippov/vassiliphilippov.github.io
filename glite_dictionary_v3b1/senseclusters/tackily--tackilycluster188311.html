tackily

adverb
cheaply or tastelessly
Pronunciation: / ˈtækɪli / (AmE), / ˈtækɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is cheap, tasteless, or lacking in style or good taste

Examples

Tackily decorated room
The living room was tackily decorated with plastic flowers and bright neon lights.
He dressed tackily for the formal event, wearing mismatched colors and flashy jewelry.
The advertisement was tackily designed, using too many clashing fonts and colors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elegantly - in a stylish and graceful way
  • synonym: gaudily - in a showy or overly bright way, lacking good taste
