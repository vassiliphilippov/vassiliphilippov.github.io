Emperor Napoleon III

proper noun
French emperor (1852–1871), nephew of Napoleon I
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈpɪrər nəˈpoʊliən ðə θɜrd / (AmE), / ɛmˈpɪərə nəˈpəʊliən ðə θɜːd / (BrE)
(1808–1873) Nephew of Napoleon I, he was emperor of the French from 1852 to 1871.
