advocator

noun
person who supports a cause or idea
Pronunciation: / ˈædvəˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈadvəkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who supports or argues for a cause or idea

Examples

An advocator of rights
As an advocator of equal rights, he often spoke at public rallies.
She became a passionate advocator for environmental protection in her community.
The conference featured several leading advocators of renewable energy solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports or recommends a cause or policy
  • antonym: opponent - a person who opposes or is against a cause or idea
