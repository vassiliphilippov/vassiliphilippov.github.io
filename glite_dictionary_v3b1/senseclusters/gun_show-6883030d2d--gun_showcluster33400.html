gun show

noun
firearm exhibition and sales event
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ɡʌn ʃəʊ / (BrE)
an event where firearms are bought, sold, and displayed, often to the public

Examples

The gun show event
Collectors and enthusiasts travel across the state to attend the annual gun show.
No one under 18 was admitted without an adult to the gun show.
She bought a rare pistol at the gun show last weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firearms fair - an event for buying, selling, and displaying firearms
