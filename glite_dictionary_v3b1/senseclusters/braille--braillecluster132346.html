braille

noun
writing system for the blind using raised dots
Pronunciation: / breɪl / (AmE), / breɪl / (BrE)
a system of writing and printing for the blind, in which combinations of raised dots represent letters, numerals, and punctuation

Examples

Braille alphabet
All elevator buttons in the building are labeled with braille.
Books written in braille help blind students access information.
She learned to read braille after losing her sight.
