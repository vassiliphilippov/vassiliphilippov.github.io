excoriates

verb
to scrape skin
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskɔriˌeɪts / (AmE), / ɪkˈskɔːrɪeɪts / (BrE)
to damage or remove the surface layer of skin or material by rubbing or scraping.

Examples

Rope excoriates skin
The rough rope excoriates his hands after hours of hauling the nets along the deck.
Repeatedly falling on the pavement excoriates the skin on her knees.
If you scrub too hard, the brush may excoriates the surface of the table.
