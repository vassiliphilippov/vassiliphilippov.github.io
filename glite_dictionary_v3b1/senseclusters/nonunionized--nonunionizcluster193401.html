nonunionized

adjective
not belonging to a trade union
7.5 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / n…ënÀàjuÀêni…ôÀåza…™zd /; / Àån…ënÀàjunj…ôÀåna…™zd / (AmE), / n…ínÀàjuÀêni…ôÀåza…™zd /; / Àån…ínÀàjuÀêni…ôn å…™zd / (BrE)
(of workers, workplaces, or organizations) not belonging to or represented by a trade union.

Examples

Nonunionized workers
Most of the labor force at the new factory is nonunionized, which has led to debates about workers' rights.
The company prefers to hire nonunionized employees to retain more direct control over its workforce.
There are significant wage differences between unionized and nonunionized teachers in the district.
Management faced little resistance from the nonunionised staff during the transition.
Many nonunionised workers have fewer benefits compared to their union counterparts.
The company preferred hiring nonunionised employees to avoid collective bargaining agreements.
