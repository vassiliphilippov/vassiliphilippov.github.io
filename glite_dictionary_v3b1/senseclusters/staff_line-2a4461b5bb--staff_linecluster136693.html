staff line

noun
one horizontal line of a musical staff
8.0
Pronunciation: / stæf laɪn / (AmE), / stɑːf laɪn / (BrE)
any of the five horizontal lines on a musical staff, used to indicate the pitch of musical notes

Examples

A staff line in music
Make sure each note head sits exactly on a staff line or in a space.
The melody was written clearly above the top staff line in the sheet music.
The third staff line from the bottom usually represents the note B in treble clef.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staff - a set of five horizontal lines and four spaces in musical notation
