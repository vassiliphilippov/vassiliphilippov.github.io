Examples

To last for years
Let's hope the sunny weather lasts until the weekend so we can go hiking.
The celebrations lasted all night and brought the whole community together.
Their friendship has lasted for over twenty years, despite living in different countries.

Synonyms

  • continue
    1.1 15/m
    continue is used to say something goes on over a period or without stopping, while last means to persist or remain.
  • persist
    3.5 9.78/m
    persist means to keep existing longer than expected, while last is a general word for continuing over time.
  • endure
    3.6 9.53/m
    endure means to continue despite trouble or pain over time, while last simply means to continue or remain.
  • remain
    1.0 316/m
    remain means to stay in the same place or condition, while last focuses on how long something continues.
  • survive
    2.7 56/m
    survive means to keep living after danger or disaster, while last is broader and means to continue over time.
  • live on
    3.2 2.08/m
    live on means to keep living after a danger or to continue being remembered, while last means to continue in time.
  • live
    1.0 175/m
    live means to be alive or exist now in a general way, while last means to continue for a period of time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perish - to die, especially in a sudden or untimely way
