demand note

noun
promissory note payable on demand
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmænd noʊt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːnd nəʊt / (BrE)
a written promise to pay a specified amount of money on demand, rather than at a fixed time

Examples

A demand note payment
A demand note allows the lender to request repayment at any time.
After reviewing the contract, she realized it was a demand note rather than a term loan.
The bank required him to sign a demand note for the short-term loan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promissory note - a written promise to pay a specified sum of money
