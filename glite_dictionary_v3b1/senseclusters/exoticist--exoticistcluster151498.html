exoticist

noun
one who favors the exotic
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑːtɪsɪst / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates, studies, or favors things considered exotic, especially from foreign cultures

Examples

An exoticist studies cultures
The museum hired an exoticist to curate its collection of rare artifacts from distant lands.
As an exoticist, she often incorporated elements from various world cultures into her artwork.
Critics accused the writer of being an exoticist for his romanticized portrayals of foreign places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orientalist - a person who studies or represents Asian cultures, often in a Western context
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who supports traditional ideas or customs
