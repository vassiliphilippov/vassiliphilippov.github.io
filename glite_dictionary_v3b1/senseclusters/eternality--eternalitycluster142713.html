eternality

noun
state of being eternal
Pronunciation: / ɪˌtɜːrˈnæləti / (AmE), / ɪˌtɜːˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being eternal or lasting forever

Examples

The eternality of time
Philosophers have long debated the eternality of the universe and whether it had a beginning.
The poem reflects on the eternality of love and the passage of time.
Many religions discuss the eternality of the soul in their teachings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perpetuity - the state of lasting forever or for an indefinite period
  • antonym: temporality - the state of being temporary or related to time
