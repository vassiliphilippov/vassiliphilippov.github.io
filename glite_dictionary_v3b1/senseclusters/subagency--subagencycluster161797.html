subagency

noun
legal relationship of subagent
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a legal relationship in which an agent appoints another person to act on behalf of the principal

Examples
