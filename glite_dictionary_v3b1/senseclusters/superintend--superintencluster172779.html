Synonyms

  • oversee
    3.9 10/m
    oversee means watching and directing work generally from above, while superintend implies formal responsibility for the whole process or group.
  • supervise
    3.5 9/m
    supervise is to watch and guide people closely to ensure correct or safe work, while superintend suggests overall charge of that operation.
  • manage
    2.7 30/m
    manage means to run or control daily tasks and resources, while superintend focuses on directing people and overseeing processes.
  • administer
    4.7 4/m
    administer is to carry out or apply policies and systems formally, while superintend is to lead and oversee a team's work.
