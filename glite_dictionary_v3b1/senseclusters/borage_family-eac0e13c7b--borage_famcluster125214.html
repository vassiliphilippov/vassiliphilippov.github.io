borage family

noun
family of flowering plants, Boraginaceae
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːrɪdʒ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbɒrɪdʒ ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of widely distributed flowering plants (Boraginaceae) distinguished by coiled (circinate) flower clusters and nutlike fruit
