mobile chicane

noun
movable barrier to slow traffic
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.bəl ʃɪˈkeɪn / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.baɪl ʃɪˈkeɪn / (BrE)
a movable barrier or temporary obstacle placed on a roadway to control traffic flow and reduce vehicle speed

Examples

A mobile chicane barrier
During the festival, a mobile chicane was set up to manage the heavy traffic.
The introduction of a mobile chicane made the track safer for the drivers.
Workers installed a mobile chicane to slow vehicles near the construction zone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicane - an artificial feature creating a bend or curve in a road or race track to slow vehicles
