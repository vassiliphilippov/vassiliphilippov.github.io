gastrogavage

noun
feeding via tube into the stomach
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstroʊɡəˈvɑːvɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊɡəˈvɑːvɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process of feeding a nutrient solution directly into the stomach through a tube inserted via a surgically created opening

Examples

Gastrogavage feeding tube
After the surgery, the doctor prescribed gastrogavage to ensure the patient received adequate nutrition.
Because of severe swallowing difficulties, gastrogavage was necessary for her continued care.
The medical team monitored the patient closely during gastrogavage to prevent any complications.
