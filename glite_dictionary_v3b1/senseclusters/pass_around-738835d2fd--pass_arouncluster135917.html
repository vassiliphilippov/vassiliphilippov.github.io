pass around

verb
distribute to each person in a group

to distribute something to each person in a group, usually by having each person hand it to the next

Examples

To pass around a basket
At the meeting, someone passed around a basket for donations.
Please pass around the signup sheet so everyone gets a chance to write their name.
The teacher passed around the exam papers before starting the test.
