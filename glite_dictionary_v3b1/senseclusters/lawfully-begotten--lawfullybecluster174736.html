lawfully-begotten

adjective
born to married parents (legitimate)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːfəli bɪˈɡɛtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːfəli bɪˈɡɛtn̩ / (BrE)
(of a child) born to parents who are legally married; legitimate.

Examples

A lawfully-begotten child
He was recognized as the lawfully-begotten son of the nobleman.
In Victorian society, great importance was placed on being lawfully-begotten.
Under the law, only lawfully-begotten children could inherit the estate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: illegitimate - born to parents not legally married
