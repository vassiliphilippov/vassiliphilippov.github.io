funster

noun
person who entertains others
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌn.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈfʌn.stə / (BrE)
a person who amuses or entertains others, especially in a group

Examples

The funster at work
As the funster at work, Maria organizes the office parties and keeps spirits high.
Every social circle appreciates a good funster to lighten the mood.
He soon became the funster of the group, always telling stories that made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joker - a person who tells jokes or amuses others
  • antonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
