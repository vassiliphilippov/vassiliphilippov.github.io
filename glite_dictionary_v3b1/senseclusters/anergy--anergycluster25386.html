anergy

noun
reduced or absent immune response
Pronunciation: / ˈænərdʒi / (AmE), / ˈænəːdʒi / (BrE)
a condition in which the body shows a reduced or absent immune response to a particular antigen or stimulus

Examples

Immune anergy
A lack of immune reaction in the test was attributed to anergy, not the absence of the disease.
Researchers are exploring how anergy contributes to chronic infections and autoimmune diseases.
The patient's anergy made it difficult to diagnose tuberculosis using the skin test.
