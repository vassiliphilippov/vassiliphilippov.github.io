freckly

adjective
covered with spots
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛkli / (AmE), / ˈfrɛkli / (BrE)
having many small spots on the skin, especially light brown ones like freckles

Examples

A freckly face
Her freckly face always reminded me of summer days at the beach.
The bird's freckly eggs were well camouflaged among the pebbles.
He chose a freckly ceramic mug with brown and cream spots for his morning coffee.
Her freckly cheeks turned red when she laughed at the joke.
The boy had a freckly face from playing outside all summer.
People often compliment her freckly skin, especially in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear-skinned - having skin without spots or blemishes
  • synonym: freckled - having freckles on the skin
  • antonym: plain - without any marks or spots
  • synonym: speckled - marked with many small spots or patches
  • synonym: spotted - having small round marks of a different color
