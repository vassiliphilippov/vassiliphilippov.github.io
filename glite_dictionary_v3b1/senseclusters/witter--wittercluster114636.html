witter

noun
chatterer
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtər / (AmE), / ˈwɪtə / (BrE)
a person who talks at length about trivial or unimportant matters, often in a tiresome way

Examples

A boring witter
Everyone at the party knew him as a witter who never let anyone get a word in.
She tried to avoid the office witter during lunch breaks to enjoy some peace and quiet.
The meeting dragged on because the witter kept sharing irrelevant stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bore - a person who talks too much in a dull or uninteresting way
  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially about unimportant things
  • antonym: listener - a person who listens rather than talks
