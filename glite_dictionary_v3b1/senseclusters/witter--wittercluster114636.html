witter

noun
chatterer
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈwɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who talks at length about trivial or unimportant matters, often in a tiresome way

Examples

A boring witter

The meeting dragged on because the witter kept sharing irrelevant stories.
