breeding ground

noun
place where animals breed
6.6 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːdɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbriːdɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a place where animals gather to mate and produce offspring; also used figuratively for a place that fosters the development of something

Examples

A breeding ground for birds
The marsh is a vital breeding ground for migratory birds each spring.
These shallow ponds serve as a breeding ground for mosquitoes during the summer.
Unchecked garbage can quickly turn into a breeding ground for disease.
