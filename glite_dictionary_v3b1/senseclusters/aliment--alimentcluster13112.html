aliment

verb
to nourish or feed
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈælɪm(ə)nt / (BrE)
to provide nourishment or sustenance to; to feed

Examples

To aliment the body
He aliments his plants with a homemade fertilizer mix.
In times of famine, it was difficult to aliment the entire population.
The caretaker did her best to aliment the injured bird until it recovered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nourish - to provide with food necessary for life and growth
  • antonym: starve - to suffer or die from lack of food
