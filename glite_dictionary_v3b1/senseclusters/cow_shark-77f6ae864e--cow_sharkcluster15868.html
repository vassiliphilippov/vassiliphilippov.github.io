cow shark

noun
a large primitive shark species
7.7
Pronunciation: / kaʊ ʃɑɹk / (AmE), / kaʊ ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large, primitive shark found in warm seas, notable for its broad head and more than five gill slits

Examples

A large cow shark
Divers occasionally encounter a cow shark in deep coastal waters.
Scientists are studying the unique anatomy of the cow shark to learn more about shark evolution.
The cow shark is recognized by its extra set of gill slits compared to most other sharks.
