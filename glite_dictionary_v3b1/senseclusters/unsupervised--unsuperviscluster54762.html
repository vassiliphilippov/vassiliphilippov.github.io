unsupervised

adjective
machine-learning: uses unlabeled data
6.4
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈsuː.pɜːr.vaɪzd/ (AmE), /ən.ˈsuː.pə.vaɪzd/ (BrE)
(in machine learning) describing a method or model that learns patterns, clusters, or structure from unlabeled data without human-provided labels.

Examples

Unsupervised learning
The research team developed an unsupervised model to discover hidden customer segments from millions of purchase records.
In contrast to labeled methods, the course used unsupervised algorithms like clustering to explore the dataset.
When the file lacked annotations, she trained an unsupervised system that grouped similar images automatically.

Related Terms

  • antonym: supervised - in machine learning, methods that learn from labeled data provided by humans (opposite of unsupervised).
