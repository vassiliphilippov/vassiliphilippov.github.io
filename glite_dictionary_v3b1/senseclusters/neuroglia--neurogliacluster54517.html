neuroglia

noun
supportive tissue of the central nervous system
7.2 9.8
Pronunciation: / nʊˈroʊɡliə / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒɡlɪə / (BrE)
Supportive tissue in the central nervous system, composed of various types of glial cells that surround and support neurons.

Examples

Neuroglia cells
Astrocytes are a type of neuroglia that help maintain the blood-brain barrier.
Damage to the neuroglia can impair the support function of the central nervous system.
Research on neuroglia has led to new insights into brain health and disease.
