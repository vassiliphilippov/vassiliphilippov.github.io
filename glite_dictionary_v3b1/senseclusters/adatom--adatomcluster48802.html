adatom

noun
atom on surface
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌætəm / (AmE), / ˈædˌætəm / (BrE)
a single atom that lies on the surface of a material, often studied in surface science

Examples

A adatom on surface
A single adatom can significantly change the properties of a material's surface.
Scientists used a microscope to track the movement of an adatom on the metal surface.
The presence of an adatom can influence chemical reactions at the nanoscale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adsorbate - a substance that is adsorbed on a surface
  • antonym: substrate - the surface on which an adatom rests
