mosaicist

noun
mosaic artist
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzæɪsɪst / (AmE), / məʊˈzæɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who designs or creates pictures or patterns using small pieces of colored material such as stone, glass, or tile

Examples

The mosaicist creates patterns
The famous mosaicist was commissioned to decorate the cathedral's walls with colorful patterns.
As a skilled mosaicist, she carefully selected each piece of glass for the artwork.
The ancient city employed a mosaicist to restore the damaged floor designs in the palace.
