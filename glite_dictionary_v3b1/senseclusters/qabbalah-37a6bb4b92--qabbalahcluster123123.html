Qabbalah

noun
Jewish mystical and esoteric theology
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / kəˈbælə / (BrE)
A system of Jewish mystical and esoteric theology based on interpreting the Hebrew scriptures, developed from the 7th to 18th centuries.
