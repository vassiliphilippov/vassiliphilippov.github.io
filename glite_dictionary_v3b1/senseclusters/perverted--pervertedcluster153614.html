perverted

adjective
abnormal or deviant sexual behavior
1.8
Pronunciation: / pərˈvɜrtɪd / (AmE), / pəˈvɜːtɪd / (BrE)
(of sexual behavior or tastes) regarded as abnormal, deviant, or showing an attraction to unusual practices, especially those considered morally or socially unacceptable.
