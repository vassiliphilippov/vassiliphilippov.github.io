fibered

adjective
structured as a fibration
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbərd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbəd / (BrE)
in mathematics, arranged as a fibration or fiber bundle, with a structure decomposed into fibers over another space

Examples
