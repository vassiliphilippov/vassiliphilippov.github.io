floor

verb
knock to the ground, especially by force
Pronunciation: / flɔɹ / (AmE), / flɔː / (BrE)
to knock someone down to the ground with force, especially in a fight or competition

Examples

To floor an opponent
A sudden blow floored him before he could react.
She was floored by the unexpected tackle during the game.
The boxer floored his opponent with a single punch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deck - to knock someone down, especially with a punch
