harebrained

adjective
very foolish or reckless
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛr.breɪnd / (AmE), / ˈheə.breɪnd / (BrE)
(of ideas or plans) very foolish, reckless, or ill-considered.

Examples

Harebrained scheme
He dismissed the suggestion as just another harebrained scheme.
Her harebrained plan to travel across the country without any money didn't last long.
They invested in a harebrained invention that never worked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment; unwise
  • antonym: sensible - having or showing good sense or judgment
