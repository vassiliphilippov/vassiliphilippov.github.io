caring

adjective
showing empathy and concern
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeərɪŋ / (BrE)
displaying concern and empathy for the well-being of others

Examples

A caring person
He is known as a caring father who always puts his children first.
Her caring attitude towards her patients made her an excellent nurse.
The community came together in a caring response after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • antonym: uncaring - not showing care, concern, or sympathy
