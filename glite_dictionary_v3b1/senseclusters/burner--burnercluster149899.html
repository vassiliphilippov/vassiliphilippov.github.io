burner

noun
disposable mobile phone
Pronunciation: / c8b5dd0n59r / (AmE), / c8b5cd0n59 / (BrE)
informal slang: a prepaid or disposable mobile phone bought or used for short-term, private, or anonymous communications.

Examples

Burner phone
He bought a burner phone to protect his privacy on short trips.
The detective left his burner switched off until the arranged meeting time.
They brought two burners to the meeting in case phones were confiscated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposable phone - a mobile phone intended for short-term use, often prepaid and not tied to the user's identity
  • synonym: prepaid phone - a mobile phone service paid for in advance rather than by monthly contract
