warmouth

noun
freshwater sunfish
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
a North American freshwater fish of the sunfish family, known for its large mouth and found in lakes and streams

Examples

A warmouth fish
The fisherman proudly displayed the warmouth he caught in the shallow part of the river.
Many anglers enjoy catching warmouth because they put up a strong fight.
A warmouth can be identified by its large mouth and dark, mottled coloring.
