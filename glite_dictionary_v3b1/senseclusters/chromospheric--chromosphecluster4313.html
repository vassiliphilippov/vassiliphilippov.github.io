chromospheric

adjective
of the chromosphere
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊməˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrəʊməˈsfɪərɪk / (BrE)
relating to the chromosphere, the layer of a star's atmosphere above the photosphere

Examples

Chromospheric activity
Astronomers study chromospheric activity to understand solar flares and other phenomena.
The telescope captured images of chromospheric eruptions on the sun's surface.
Researchers analyzed chromospheric lines to determine the temperature of the star.

Related Terms

  • antonym: photospheric - relating to the photosphere, the visible surface of a star
