arabica

noun
mild coffee bean
Pronunciation: / əˈræbɪkə / (AmE), / əˈræbɪkə / (BrE)
a type of coffee bean from the Coffea arabica plant, known for its mild flavor and aroma

Examples

Arabica coffee beans
Many specialty cafes serve only arabica beans for their smoother taste.
The price of arabica coffee has increased due to poor harvests this year.
She prefers arabica over other types because of its delicate aroma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coffee - a beverage made from roasted coffee beans
  • antonym: robusta - a type of coffee bean with a stronger, more bitter flavor
