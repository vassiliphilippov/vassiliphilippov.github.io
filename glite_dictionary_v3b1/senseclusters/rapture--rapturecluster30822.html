rapture

verb
to ascend to heaven
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræptʃərd / (AmE), / ˈrap.tʃəd / (BrE)
to take someone up to heaven, especially as believed to occur during the Christian event called the Rapture.

Examples

To rapture to heaven
Many believers hoped they would be raptured before the chaos and destruction began.
Some people believe only the faithful will be raptured at the end of days.
The story describes how the characters were suddenly raptured into the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascend - to go up or rise, especially to a higher or spiritual place
  • antonym: descend - to go down or move to a lower place
