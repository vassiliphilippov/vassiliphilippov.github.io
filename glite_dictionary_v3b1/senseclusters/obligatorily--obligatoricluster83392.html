Synonyms

  • compulsorily
    7.2 0.05/m
    compulsorily means people must obey a law or rule that forces action, while obligatorily simply says the action is required.
  • mandatorily
    7.0 0.06/m
    mandatorily is used when authorities or rules set a duty that cannot be avoided, while obligatorily means something is required.
