halogenate

verb
to add halogen
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhæ.lə.dʒə.neɪt/ (AmE), /ˈhæ.lə.dʒə.neɪt/ (BrE)
to treat or combine a substance with a halogen element, such as chlorine or fluorine

Examples

To halogenate compounds
Scientists often halogenate organic compounds to change their chemical behavior.
The process to halogenate water can help disinfect it for safe drinking.
Industries may halogenate plastics to improve their resistance to fire.
