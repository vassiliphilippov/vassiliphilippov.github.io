balkanization

noun
fragmentation into parts
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːlkənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɔːlkənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the division of a larger region, organization, or group into smaller, often hostile or uncooperative, parts

Examples

The balkanization of regions
The balkanization of the internet has led to separate online communities with little interaction.
Political disagreements resulted in the balkanization of the organization into competing groups.
Some experts warn that balkanization of the market could harm innovation and cooperation.
The balkanization of the company led to several independent departments competing against each other.
Political analysts warned that balkanization could threaten the stability of the region.
After years of unity, the country faced balkanization due to rising internal conflicts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fragmentation - the process of breaking into smaller parts
  • antonym: unification - the process of joining parts into a whole
