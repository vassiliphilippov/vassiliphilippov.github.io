classist

noun
person with class prejudice
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈklæ.sɪst/ (AmE), /ˈklæ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who discriminates against or is prejudiced toward people based on their social class

Examples

A classist person
Many activists spoke out against the classist attitudes present in the hiring process.
He was labeled a classist after making comments about people from poorer backgrounds.
The novel explores the life of a classist who struggles to understand those outside his social circle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - a person who believes in equality for all social classes
  • synonym: elitist - a person who believes certain people are superior due to their social status
