Philadelphia fleabane

noun
North American wildflower with fringed white or pink rays
9.6
Pronunciation: / fɪˌlædəlˈfiə ˈfliːˌbeɪn / (AmE), / fɪˌlædəlˈfɪə ˈfliːˌbeɪn / (BrE)
