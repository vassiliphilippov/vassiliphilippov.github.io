kabbalist

noun
expert in Kabbalah
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkabəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who is an expert in Kabbalah, especially one skilled in interpreting its esoteric doctrines.

Examples

A kabbalist expert
She consulted a renowned kabbalist for spiritual guidance.
Some consider Isaac Luria the most influential kabbalist in Jewish history.
The kabbalist spent years studying ancient manuscripts to uncover hidden meanings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mystic - a person who seeks by contemplation and self-surrender to obtain unity with the divine
