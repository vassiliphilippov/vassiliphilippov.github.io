lunar day

noun
period of moon's rotation on its axis
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnɚ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈluːnə deɪ / (BrE)
The period of time it takes the moon to rotate once on its axis, about 27.3 sidereal days.

Examples

A lunar day period
A lunar day is much longer than an Earth day, lasting about 27.3 days.
Scientists measure one lunar day as the time it takes for the moon to complete a full rotation.
The temperature on the moon varies greatly over the course of a lunar day.
