virtual workspace

noun
digital space for remote work collaboration
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝː.tʃu.əl ˈwɝːk.speɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tʃu.əl ˈwɜːk.speɪs / (BrE)
a digital environment in which people can collaborate, communicate, and perform work tasks remotely

Examples

A virtual workspace online
Many businesses now rely on a virtual workspace for meetings and document sharing.
The company adopted a virtual workspace to allow employees to collaborate from different locations.
Using a virtual workspace has improved our team's productivity and communication.
