unmasterable

adjective
impossible to master
9.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæstərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːstərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to fully control, learn, or become skilled at

Examples

An unmasterable skill
For many, the complexity of quantum physics feels unmasterable despite years of study.
The wild river was considered unmasterable by even the most experienced kayakers.
Some languages seem unmasterable because of their difficult grammar and pronunciation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manageable - able to be controlled or handled
  • synonym: uncontrollable - impossible to control or manage
