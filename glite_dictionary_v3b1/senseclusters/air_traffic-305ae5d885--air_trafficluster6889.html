air traffic

noun
movement of aircraft
Pronunciation: / ɛr ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / eə ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the movement of aircraft through the air, especially in a particular region or at a specific time

Examples

Air traffic control
Controllers monitor air traffic to ensure the safety of all flights.
During the holidays, air traffic increases significantly around major cities.
Heavy air traffic caused delays at the airport this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air travel - travel by aircraft
  • antonym: ground traffic - vehicles moving on roads
