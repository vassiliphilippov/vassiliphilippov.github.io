viga

noun
wooden roof beam
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɡə / (AmE), / ˈviːɡə / (BrE)
a large wooden beam, often exposed, used in traditional Southwestern or adobe architecture to support roofs or ceilings

Examples

Exposed viga beams
The living room featured exposed vigas that added a rustic charm to the house.
Traditional Southwestern homes often use viga beams to support their flat roofs.
The architect chose to highlight the original viga in the renovated adobe building.
The adobe house featured exposed vigas supporting the ceiling in every room.
Restorers carefully replaced each viga to preserve the building's historic character.
Sunlight filtered through the gaps between the vigas in the old mission church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of wood or metal used to support a structure
  • synonym: joist - a horizontal supporting member running between foundations or walls
