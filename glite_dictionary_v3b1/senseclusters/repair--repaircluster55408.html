repair

verb
go or proceed to a place (formal)
Pronunciation: / ɹɪˈpɛɚ / (AmE), / ɹɪˈpeə / (BrE)
(formal) To go or proceed, especially deliberately, to a particular place.

Examples

To repair to the lounge
After dinner, the gentlemen repaired to the drawing room for conversation.
She repaired to her study to continue working on her novel.
The villagers repair to the square every Sunday for the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depart - to leave, especially to start a journey
  • synonym: go - to move or travel from one place to another
