wale

noun
raised fabric ridge
Pronunciation: / weɪl / (AmE), / weɪl / (BrE)
a raised ridge or line on fabric or another surface, often forming part of its texture

Examples

Corduroy wale
Corduroy pants are known for their distinctive wale pattern.
The fabric's thick wales made the jacket warm and durable.
You can feel each wale when you run your hand across the velvet cushion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rib - a raised line or ridge in fabric or material
