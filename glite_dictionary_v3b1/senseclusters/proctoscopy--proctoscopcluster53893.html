proctoscopy

noun
rectal examination with a proctoscope
6.4
Pronunciation: / prɑkˈtɑskəpi / (AmE), / prɒkˈtɒskəpi / (BrE)
A medical procedure in which the rectum and the end of the colon are visually examined using a proctoscope.

Examples

Proctoscopy medical procedure
Proctoscopy is commonly performed as a diagnostic procedure in gastroenterology clinics.
After the proctoscopy, the patient experienced only mild discomfort.
The doctor scheduled a proctoscopy to investigate the source of the patient's rectal bleeding.
