nonmimetic

adjective
not imitative; abstract
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnmɪˈmɛtɪk /; / ˌnɑnmaɪˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmɪˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)
not intended to imitate or represent reality; abstract or nonrepresentational, especially in art or appearance
