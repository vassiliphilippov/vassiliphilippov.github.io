astringently

adverb
in a drying way
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɪndʒəntli / (AmE), / əˈstrɪndʒəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that causes a dry, puckering, or tightening sensation, especially in the mouth or on the skin.

Examples

Astringently dry taste
The herbal tea tasted astringently, leaving a dry feeling on my tongue.
Some facial cleansers work astringently to tighten the skin and reduce oil.
The sauce was seasoned astringently, making the dish feel sharp and dry in the mouth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is soft or pleasant to the senses
  • synonym: tartly - in a sharp or acidic manner
