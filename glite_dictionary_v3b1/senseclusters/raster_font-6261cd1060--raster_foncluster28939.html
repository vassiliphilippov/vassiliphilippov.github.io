raster font

noun

A computer font that is stored and displayed as a collection of pixels or bitmaps, rather than as scalable outlines.

Examples

Raster font display
Older computer systems often used raster fonts because they were easier to render on low-resolution displays.
The graphics software allows users to switch between raster font and vector font options.
You may notice that raster fonts can appear pixelated when enlarged beyond their intended size.
