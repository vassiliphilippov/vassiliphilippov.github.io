precautional

adjective
preventive; serving as a precaution
Pronunciation: / prɪˈkɔʃənəl / (AmE), / prɪˈkɔːʃənəl / (BrE)
(formal) Taken in advance to prevent possible danger, harm, or failure; serving as a precaution.

Examples

A precautional action
Before the storm arrived, the city took precautional actions to protect residents.
Researchers recommended a precautional approach when handling hazardous chemicals.
The company implemented several precautional measures to avoid data breaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precautionary - taken in advance to prevent harm or danger
