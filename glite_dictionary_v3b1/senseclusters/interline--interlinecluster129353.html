interline

verb
to add line spacing
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlaɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlaɪn / (BrE)
to insert extra space between lines of printed or written text

Examples

To interline the text
The teacher asked the students to interline their essays for easier grading.
To improve readability, the designer chose to interline the entire document.
You should interline your notes so you have space to add comments later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: double-space - to leave a full blank line between each line of text
  • antonym: single-space - to write or print with no extra space between lines
