wronger

noun
person at greater fault
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔŋər / (AmE), / ˈrɒŋə / (BrE)
a person who does wrong or is more at fault than another in a situation

Examples

The real wronger

She refused to apologize, insisting that her friend was the bigger wronger in the argument.
The judge had to decide who was the wronger in the complicated case.
