accessory cephalic vein

noun
superficial forearm vein joining the cephalic vein
7.0
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛsəri səˈfælɪk veɪn / (AmE), / əkˈsɛsəri sɪˈfælɪk veɪn / (BrE)
a superficial vein running along the radial side of the forearm, which joins the cephalic vein near the elbow

Examples

The accessory cephalic vein in forearm
Anatomy students often use models to trace the path of the accessory cephalic vein along the forearm.
During the procedure, the accessory cephalic vein was carefully preserved to maintain effective blood flow in the arm.
The surgeon identified the accessory cephalic vein before making an incision near the patient’s elbow.
