atheromatic

adjective
relating to or resembling atheroma
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæθ.əˈroʊ.mətɪk / (AmE), / ˌaθ.əˈrəʊ.mətɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling atheroma; affected by or characteristic of atheromatous degeneration, especially in arteries.

Examples

Atheromatic artery changes
Atheromatic changes increase the risk of cardiovascular diseases.
Advanced imaging techniques can reveal atheromatic deposits before symptoms appear.
The surgeon noted significant atheromatic plaques in the patient's coronary arteries.
