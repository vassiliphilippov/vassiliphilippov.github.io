lay eyes on

verb
to see or notice someone or something
Pronunciation: / leɪ aɪz ɑn / (AmE), / leɪ aɪz ɒn / (BrE)
to see or notice someone or something, especially for the first time or after a long time

Examples

To lay eyes on a car
