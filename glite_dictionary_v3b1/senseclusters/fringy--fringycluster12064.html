fringy

adjective
at a border or edge; marginal
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪndʒi / (AmE), / ˈfrɪndʒi / (BrE)
Situated at or constituting a border or edge; marginal.

Examples

A fringy border
A thin, fringy line of trees grew along the riverbank, marking the boundary between field and water.
The artist preferred to live in a fringy neighborhood just at the city's edge.
The house had a fringy stretch of land covered in wildflowers where the lawn met the forest.

  • synonym: marginal - at or near the edge or margin
