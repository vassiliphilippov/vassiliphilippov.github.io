beast of burden

noun
an animal used for carrying loads or heavy work
Pronunciation: / bist əv ˈbɝdən / (AmE), / biːst əv ˈbɜːdn / (BrE)
an animal, such as a donkey, ox, or elephant, that is kept for carrying loads or doing heavy work

Examples

A beast of burden animal
In some remote villages, the camel remains a vital beast of burden.
The donkey is an example of a beast of burden used by farmers for centuries.
With the invention of machinery, the use of the ox as a beast of burden has declined.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draught animal - an animal used for pulling heavy loads
  • synonym: pack animal - an animal used to carry loads
