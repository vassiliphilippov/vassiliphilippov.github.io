convocant

noun
summoned attendee
Pronunciation: / kənˈvoʊkənt / (AmE), / kənˈvəʊkənt / (BrE)
a person who has been summoned or invited to attend a meeting or assembly

Examples

A convocant attends assembly
Each convocant arrived before noon to participate in the special assembly called by the dean.
The list of convocants was posted on the notice board outside the conference room.
As a convocant, she was expected to contribute her ideas during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attendee - a person who is present at an event or meeting
  • synonym: invitee - a person who has been invited to an event
  • antonym: organizer - a person who arranges or manages an event
