jam-packed

adjective
extremely crowded or full
Pronunciation: / dʒæm ˈpækt / (AmE), / dʒæm ˈpækt / (BrE)
Extremely full or crowded; filled to capacity.

Examples

Jam-packed suitcase
Her suitcase was jam-packed with clothes and souvenirs from her trip.
The stadium was jam-packed with cheering supporters.
The train was so jam-packed that there was barely room to stand.
