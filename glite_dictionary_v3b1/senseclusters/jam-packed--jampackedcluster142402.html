Synonyms

  • packed
    1.3 2/m
    packed is a common word for places that are very full or crowded, while jam-packed sounds slightly more forceful.
  • jammed
    2.3 0.01/m
    jammed is often used for crowded places or traffic, while jam-packed stresses being tightly full and completely crowded.
  • chock-full
    4.1 1/m
    chock-full sounds informal and strong, while jam-packed is the common phrase to say something is extremely full.
  • wall-to-wall
    4.2 0.6/m
    wall-to-wall suggests something fills every part of a room, while jam-packed focuses on crowding and no free space.
