fireside

noun
area near a fireplace
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the area near or in front of a fireplace, often paved and extending into a room

Examples

By the fireside
On cold winter evenings, they like to gather by the fireside with a good book.
She placed a rug near the fireside to make the room feel warmer and cozier.
The family spent hours talking and laughing around the old stone fireside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hearth - the floor of a fireplace, often extending into the room
