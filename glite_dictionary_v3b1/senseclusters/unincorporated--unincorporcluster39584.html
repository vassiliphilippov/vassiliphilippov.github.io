unincorporated

adjective
not organized as a legal corporation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪnˈkɔːr.pəˌreɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈkɔː.pər.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Not formed into or maintained as a legal corporation; not constituted as a corporation.

Examples

An unincorporated area
In an unincorporated region, local services are often provided by the county government.
Many small towns remain unincorporated due to limited population and resources.
The area is an unincorporated community outside city limits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incorporated - formed into a legal corporation
