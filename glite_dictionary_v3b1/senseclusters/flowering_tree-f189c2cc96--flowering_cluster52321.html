flowering tree

noun
tree that produces flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tree that produces flowers and whose seeds and ovules are contained within an ovary

Examples

A flowering tree blooms
Many city streets are lined with flowering trees to enhance the urban landscape.
She planted a flowering tree in her backyard to attract birds and bees.
The magnolia is a beautiful flowering tree that blooms in early spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiosperm - a plant having seeds enclosed in an ovary; flowering plant
  • antonym: conifer - a tree that produces cones and does not produce flowers
