resting potential

noun
membrane potential of inactive nerve cell
1.6 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈre.stɪŋ pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ /; / /ˈre.stɪŋ poʊ.ˈten.ʃəl/ (AmE), /ˈre.stɪŋ pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ (BrE)
the electrical potential difference across the membrane of a nerve cell when it is not transmitting a nerve impulse

Examples
