turn around

verb
cause dramatic improvement

Examples

To turn around a business
It took a lot of effort to turn around his failing grades at school.
Strong leadership helped turn around the team's poor performance this season.
The new CEO managed to turn around the struggling company within a year.
After a slow start, the team was able to turn around the game and win.
Her attitude has really turned around since she changed schools.
The company managed to turn around its finances and avoid bankruptcy.

Government intervention is needed to turn around the declining economy.
She worked hard to turn around the struggling company and make it profitable again.
