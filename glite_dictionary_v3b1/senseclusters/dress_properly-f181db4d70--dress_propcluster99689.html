dress properly

verb
wear suitable attire
Pronunciation: / drɛs ˈprɑpɚli / (AmE), / drɛs ˈprɒpəli / (BrE)
to wear clothing that is appropriate or suitable for a particular occasion or situation

Examples

To dress properly for winter
Children are taught to dress properly for cold weather to stay warm.
Interviewers expect candidates to dress properly and look professional.
It is important to dress properly when attending a formal dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress appropriately - to wear clothing that is appropriate
  • antonym: dress casually - to wear informal or everyday clothes
