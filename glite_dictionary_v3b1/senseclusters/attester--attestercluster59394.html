attester

noun
person who affirms or vouches for something
8.3
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛstər / (AmE), / əˈtɛstə / (BrE)
a person who affirms or vouches for the correctness, truth, or authenticity of something

Examples

Legal attester
A notary public often serves as an attester for legal documents.
The scientist acted as an attester to the accuracy of the research findings.
We require an attester to verify all signatures on this form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certifier - someone who certifies or officially confirms something
  • synonym: witness - someone who gives testimony or evidence, especially in court
