free weight

noun
unattached exercise equipment for lifting
7.6 2.8
Pronunciation: / fri weɪt / (AmE), / friː weɪt / (BrE)
a piece of exercise equipment, such as a dumbbell or barbell, that is not attached to a machine and is lifted and moved freely by the user

Examples

She prefers using free weights at the gym instead of weight machines.
