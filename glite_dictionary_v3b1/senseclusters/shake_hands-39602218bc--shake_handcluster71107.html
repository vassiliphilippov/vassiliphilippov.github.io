shake hands

verb
perform a protocol handshake
5.8 0.03/m

(in computing, networking, and security) to perform a protocol handshake; to exchange messages between systems to establish a connection or to agree on session parameters (for example, encryption keys).

Examples

Systems shake hands digitally
During the TLS negotiation, the client and server shake hands to agree on encryption keys.
After the initial packets timed out, the devices finally shook hands and resumed secure communication.
When diagnosing connection failures, confirm the endpoints are shaking hands before checking application layers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handshake - an exchange of messages used to establish a network connection or negotiate parameters
