freeze off

verb
dismiss or reject contemptuously
8.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / friz …îf / (AmE), / friÀêz …íf / (BrE)
to reject someone in a cold or contemptuous manner; to deliberately ignore or dismiss someone with disdain

Examples

To freeze off a person
After his rude comment, she completely froze off any further conversation with him.
Every time I approached her at work, she would freeze me off without a second thought.
He tried to join their circle, but they froze him off with icy indifference.
