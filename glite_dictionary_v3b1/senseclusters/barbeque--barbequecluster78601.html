barbeque

noun
outdoor meal with food cooked over an open fire
1.0 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêr.b…™.kjuÀê / (AmE), / Ààb…ëÀê.b…™.kjuÀê / (BrE)
a meal or gathering at which meat and other food are cooked out of doors on a rack over an open fire or hot coals, especially a whole animal roasted on a spit

Examples

A barbeque party
At the beach, they set up a barbeque and enjoyed grilled chicken and vegetables.
The annual barbeque featured a whole pig roasted over an open fire.
We invited our neighbors to a barbeque in the backyard last Saturday.

Synonyms

  • barbecue
    1.2 4.95/m
    barbecue is the standard spelling for an outdoor meal with grilled or roasted meat, while barbeque is an alternative spelling.
  • cookout
    1.9 0.76/m
    cookout is an informal outdoor meal where food is grilled or eaten casually, while barbeque often describes larger, meat-focused gatherings.

Related Terms
