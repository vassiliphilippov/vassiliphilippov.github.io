dicey

adjective
risky, unpredictable
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪsi / (AmE), / ˈdaɪsi / (BrE)
involving potential danger or risk; uncertain and unpredictable

Examples

A dicey situation
Investing in that startup seems a bit dicey to me.
The weather looks dicey for our picnic tomorrow, so we might need a backup plan.
We drove down a dicey mountain road during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: risky - involving risk or danger
  • antonym: safe - free from danger or risk
