Bambara

proper noun
West African people or language
9.6
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbɑːrə / (AmE), / bæmˈbɑːrə / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group mainly found in Mali, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

The Bambara are known for their rich cultural traditions and music.
