roughness

noun
lack of polish or refinement
4.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌf.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈrʌf.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being unpolished or lacking refinement; crudeness or lack of sophistication in manner, appearance, or style

Examples

Roughness of his manners
Despite the roughness of his manners, he was genuinely kind at heart.
Her writing style has a certain roughness that makes it feel authentic and direct.
The roughness of the handmade furniture gave it a rustic charm.
