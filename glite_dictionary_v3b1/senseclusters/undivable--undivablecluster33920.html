undivable

adjective
not safe for diving
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdaɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdaɪvəbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or possible to dive into or through, often due to safety or physical conditions

Examples

Undivable water
The lake was too shallow and rocky, making it completely undivable for anyone.
Due to strong currents, the underwater cave was declared undivable by the rescue team.
After the storm, debris made the pool undivable until it could be cleaned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dangerous - likely to cause harm or injury
  • antonym: divable - suitable or possible to dive into
