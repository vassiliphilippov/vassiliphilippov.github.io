nonnuclear

adjective
not a nuclear family
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈnuːkliər / (AmE), / nɒnˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
relating to a family or household that does not consist only of parents and their children

Examples

Nonnuclear family structure
She grew up in a nonnuclear household where her grandparents also lived.
Many cultures have nonnuclear families that include aunts, uncles, and cousins living together.
The study compared the experiences of children in nonnuclear and nuclear family settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extended - including relatives beyond the immediate family
  • antonym: nuclear - consisting only of parents and their children
