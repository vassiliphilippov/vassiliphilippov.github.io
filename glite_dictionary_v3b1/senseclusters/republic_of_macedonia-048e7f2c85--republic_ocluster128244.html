Republic of Macedonia

proper noun
country in the Balkans, now North Macedonia
Pronunciation: / rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmæsəˈdoʊniə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmæsɪˈdəʊniə / (BrE)
A landlocked country on the Balkan Peninsula in southeastern Europe, which achieved independence from Yugoslavia in 1991; officially renamed North Macedonia in 2019.
