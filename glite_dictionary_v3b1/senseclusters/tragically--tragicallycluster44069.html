tragically

adverb
in a tragic or disastrous manner
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtrædʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˈtrædʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that causes or involves great sadness or suffering, or with disastrous results.

Examples

To tragically lose
Tragically, the fire destroyed most of the historic building.
He died tragically in a car accident just days before graduation.
Their story tragically ended in heartbreak and loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comically - in a humorous or amusing manner
  • synonym: tragic - causing or characterized by extreme distress or sorrow
