Anishinaabe

proper noun
Indigenous North American group
Pronunciation: / əˌnɪʃɪˈnɑːbeɪ / (AmE), / əˌnɪʃɪˈnɑːbeɪ / (BrE)
a member of a group of Indigenous peoples native to Canada and the United States, including the Ojibwe, Odawa, and Algonquin peoples
