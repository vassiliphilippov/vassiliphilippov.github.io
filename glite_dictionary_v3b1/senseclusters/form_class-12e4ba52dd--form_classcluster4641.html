form class

noun
grammatical word category
6.3
Pronunciation: / fɔrm klæs / (AmE), / fɔːm klɑːs / (BrE)
a category of words grouped according to their grammatical function, such as nouns, verbs, adjectives, or adverbs

Examples

English form class
Nouns and verbs each belong to a different form class in English grammar.
Some words can switch their form class depending on context, such as 'run' as a noun or verb.
When analyzing a sentence, it is important to identify the form class of each word.
