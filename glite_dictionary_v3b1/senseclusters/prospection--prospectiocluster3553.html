prospection

noun
search for resources
Pronunciation: / prɑːˈspɛkʃən / (AmE), / prɒˈspɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of searching or surveying an area to discover mineral deposits, oil, or other natural resources

Examples

Mineral prospection
The team began prospection in the remote mountains to locate new mineral deposits.
Modern technology has made prospection for oil more efficient and accurate.
After months of prospection, the company finally discovered a promising gold vein.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploration - the act of searching for resources or new areas
  • synonym: survey - an examination of an area to gather information
