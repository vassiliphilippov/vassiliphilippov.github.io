Synonyms

  • rebellion
    4.1 7.73/m
    a rebellion is an organized armed attempt to overthrow authority, while resistance is the act or group fighting against an enemy or government.
  • revolt
    4.6 6.71/m
    a revolt is a sudden, forceful attempt to remove rulers, while resistance is broader organized opposition, often long or secret.
  • uprising
    5.1 11/m
