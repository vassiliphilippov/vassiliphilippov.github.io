resistance

noun
military or organized opposition to an enemy
3.4 4.4
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈzɪs.təns/ /; / /riː.ˈzɪs.təns/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈzɪs.təns/ (BrE)
