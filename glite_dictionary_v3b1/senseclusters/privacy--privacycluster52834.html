privacy

noun
state of being concealed or free from intrusion
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpraɪ.və.siː/ (AmE), /ˈprɪ.və.siː/ /; / /ˈpraɪ.və.siː/ (BrE)
the state of being free from the observation or intrusion of others; being hidden or not seen by the public
