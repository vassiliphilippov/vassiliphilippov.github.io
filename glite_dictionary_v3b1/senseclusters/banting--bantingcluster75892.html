banting

noun
wild ox of Southeast Asia
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a wild ox native to the Malay Archipelago, especially found in Indonesia and Malaysia

Examples

The banting ox
Conservationists worry that the habitat of the banting is shrinking due to deforestation.
Farmers in Borneo sometimes spot herds of wild bantings near the edge of their fields.
The banting is a shy animal rarely seen by visitors to the remote forests.
