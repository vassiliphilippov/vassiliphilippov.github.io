housedog

noun
dog kept for guarding a house
2.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆhaÊŠsËŒdÉ”g / (AmE), / ËˆhaÊŠs.dÉ’g / (BrE)
A dog kept or trained to guard a house and live indoors with people.

Examples

A housedog guards
The family relies on their housedog to alert them to any unusual noises at night.
They trained their housedog to recognize and bark at strangers approaching the front door.
Unlike farm dogs, a housedog spends most of its time indoors with its owners.
