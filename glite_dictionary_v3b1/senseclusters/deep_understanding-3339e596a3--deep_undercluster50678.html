deep understanding

noun
profound comprehension
Pronunciation: / diːp ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / diːp ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
a profound or thorough comprehension of a subject, situation, or concept

Examples

A deep understanding of language
Gaining a deep understanding of a new language takes years of study and immersion.
Her deep understanding of cultural differences helps her connect with people from around the world.
The professor has a deep understanding of molecular biology, which makes his lectures fascinating.
