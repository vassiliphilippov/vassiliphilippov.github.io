antipasto

noun
Italian starter of assorted appetizers
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈpɑː.stoʊ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈpæ.stəʊ / (BrE)
An Italian starter course consisting of a variety of appetizers, such as cured meats, cheeses, and vegetables.

Examples

Italian antipasto plate
The waiter recommended the house antipasto to start our meal.
Their signature antipasto includes marinated olives, salami, and roasted peppers.
We ordered a delicious antipasto before our main courses arrived.
