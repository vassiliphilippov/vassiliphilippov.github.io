baguette

noun
long, thin French bread loaf
Pronunciation: / bæˈɡɛt / (AmE), / baˈɡɛt / (BrE)
A long, thin loaf of French bread with a crisp crust and soft interior.

Examples

A baguette loaf
A warm baguette is perfect for making delicious sandwiches.
He bought a fresh baguette from the bakery this morning.
She sliced the baguette and served it with cheese and olives.
