submind

noun
unconscious mental part
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌmaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌmaɪnd / (BrE)
a part of the mind that functions below the level of conscious awareness

Examples

The human submind
Some psychologists believe the submind can influence our choices without us realizing it.
Dreams are sometimes thought to be messages from the submind.
Meditation techniques aim to connect the conscious mind with the submind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conscious mind - the part of the mind that is aware of thoughts and surroundings
  • synonym: subconscious - the part of the mind not fully aware but influencing actions
