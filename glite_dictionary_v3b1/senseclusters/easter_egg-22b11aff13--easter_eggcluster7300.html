Easter egg

noun
egg-shaped candy for Easter
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈistɚ ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈiːstə ɛɡ / (BrE)
an egg-shaped candy (often chocolate) or a decorated real egg, given or used as part of Easter celebrations
