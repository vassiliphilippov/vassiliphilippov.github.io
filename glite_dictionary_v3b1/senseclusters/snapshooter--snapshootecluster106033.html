snapshooter

noun
quick, accurate shooter
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈsnæpˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who shoots quickly and accurately, especially at suddenly appearing targets

Examples

Expert snapshooter
The police trainer praised the new recruit as a snapshooter for his quick, accurate response under pressure.
In the competition, the best snapshooter was able to hit every moving target within seconds.
Hunters value a skilled snapshooter when game appears suddenly in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marksman - a person skilled at shooting accurately
  • synonym: sharpshooter - an expert at shooting, especially with firearms
