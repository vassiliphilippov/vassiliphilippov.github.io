unconfident

adjective
lacking self-assurance
4.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɑnfɪdənt /; / ʌnˈkɑːnfɪdənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒnfɪdənt / (BrE)
not feeling sure about yourself or your abilities; lacking confidence

Examples

Unconfident about answers
She felt unconfident about her answers during the job interview.
Many students are unconfident when speaking a new language for the first time.
He appeared unconfident in his ability to lead the team.
He felt unconfident about giving his first presentation to the team.
The student appeared unconfident when answering questions in class.
Her unconfident tone made it clear she was unsure about her decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confident - feeling or showing certainty and self-assurance
  • synonym: hesitant - unsure or slow in acting or speaking
  • synonym: insecure - not confident or assured; uncertain and anxious
  • synonym: unsure - not certain or confident about something
