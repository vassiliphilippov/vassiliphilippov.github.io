assets

noun
items of value owned
Pronunciation: / ˈæsɛts / (AmE), / ˈæsɛts / (BrE)
things of value, especially property or resources, owned by a person, company, or organization

Examples

Valuable assets
Real estate is one of the most valuable assets a person can own.
She listed her assets and liabilities for the loan application.
The company's assets include factories, vehicles, and cash.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liabilities - debts or obligations owed by a person or company
