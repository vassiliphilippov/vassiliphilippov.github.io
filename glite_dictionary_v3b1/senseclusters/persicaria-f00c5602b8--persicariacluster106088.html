Persicaria

proper noun
plant genus, smartweeds
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsɪˈkɛəriə / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsɪˈkeəriə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants in the buckwheat family, commonly known as smartweeds

Examples

Persicaria plants
Many wetlands are home to several species of Persicaria.
The leaves of Persicaria are often used to identify different species in the field.
Gardeners sometimes plant Persicaria for its attractive flowers and foliage.
