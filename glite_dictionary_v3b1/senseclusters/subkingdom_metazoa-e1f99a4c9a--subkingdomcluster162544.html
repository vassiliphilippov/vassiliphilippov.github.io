subkingdom Metazoa

noun
taxonomic group of multicellular animals
9.6
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈkɪŋ.dəm me.tə.ˈzoʊ.ə/ (AmE), /səb.ˈkɪŋ.dəm me.tə.ˈzəʊ.ə/ (BrE)
A major taxonomic group comprising all multicellular animals with cells differentiated into tissues and organs, typically with a digestive cavity and nervous system.
