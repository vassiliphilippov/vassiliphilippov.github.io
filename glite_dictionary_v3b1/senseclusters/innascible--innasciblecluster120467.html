innascible

adjective
cannot be produced
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnæsɪbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnæsɪbəl / (BrE)
not able to be born, produced, or generated; incapable of being created.

Examples

An innascible force
The ancient text refers to an innascible force that has always existed.
Some philosophers argue that the universe is not innascible but had a definite beginning.
He described the divine as innascible, having no origin or creator.

Related Terms

  • antonym: created - brought into existence
  • synonym: eternal - existing forever; without beginning or end
