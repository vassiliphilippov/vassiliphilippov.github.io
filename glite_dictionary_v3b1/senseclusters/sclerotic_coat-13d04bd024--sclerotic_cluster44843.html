sclerotic coat

noun
outer fibrous layer of the eyeball
8.1
Pronunciation: /sklɪ.ˈrɑː.tɪk koʊt/ (AmE), /sklɪ.ˈrɒ.tɪk kəʊt/ (BrE)
the dense, white, fibrous outer layer of the eyeball that together with the cornea forms its outer covering
