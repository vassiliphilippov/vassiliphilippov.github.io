waah

verb
to cry loudly
Pronunciation: / wɑː / (AmE), / wɑː / (BrE)
to cry or complain loudly in a high-pitched, whining way, often imitating the sound of a baby crying

Examples

To waah loudly
The baby began to waah as soon as his toy was taken away.
He tends to waah whenever things do not go his way.
You could hear her waah from the other room after she scraped her knee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheer - to shout with joy or encouragement
  • synonym: wail - to make a long, loud cry of pain or sadness
  • synonym: whine - to complain in a high-pitched, annoying voice
