sectional

noun
modular sofa or furniture unit
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˈsek.ʃə.nəl / (BrE)
a large sofa or other piece of furniture made up of separate sections that can be arranged in different configurations

Examples

A sectional sofa
She placed a blanket over one side of the sectional to make it more inviting.
The sectional can be rearranged to fit into smaller spaces.
We just bought a new sectional for the living room to maximize seating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sofa - a long, comfortable seat for more than one person
