rogue nation

noun
country acting outside international norms
5.1
Pronunciation: / roʊɡ ˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / rəʊɡ ˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a country that acts independently and irresponsibly, often disregarding international laws and norms

Examples

Rogue nation with weapons
Diplomats argued that no rogue nation should possess nuclear weapons.
Several countries accused the regime of being a rogue nation after its latest missile test.
The international community condemned the actions of the rogue nation in violating sanctions.
