sealer

noun
official who affixes a seal
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlər / (AmE), / ˈsiːlə / (BrE)
an official whose duty is to affix a seal to documents, often to authenticate or certify them

Examples

Official sealer of documents
A sealer must ensure that all certificates are properly authenticated.
After the contract was signed, it was handed to the sealer for official approval.
The sealer stamped the document to make it legally valid.
