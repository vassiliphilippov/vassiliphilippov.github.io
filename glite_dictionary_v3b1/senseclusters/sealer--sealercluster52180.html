sealer

noun
official who affixes a seal
7.6 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêl…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêl…ô / (BrE)
an official whose duty is to affix a seal to documents, often to authenticate or certify them

Examples

Official sealer of documents
A sealer must ensure that all certificates are properly authenticated.
After the contract was signed, it was handed to the sealer for official approval.
The sealer stamped the document to make it legally valid.

Synonyms

  • attestor
    8.3 0.00/m
    an attestor formally signs to confirm a document's authenticity, while a sealer physically affixes an official seal to authenticate papers.
  • certifier
    4.8 0.13/m
    a certifier officially confirms that something meets required standards, while a sealer specifically affixes a seal to validate documents.
