sympathy card

noun
a card expressing sympathy
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəθi kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəθi kɑːd / (BrE)
a card sent to someone to express sympathy, especially after a bereavement or loss

Examples

A sympathy card message
A heartfelt message inside a sympathy card can bring comfort during difficult times.
She sent a sympathy card to her friend after hearing about the loss.
We received dozens of sympathy cards following my grandfather's funeral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condolence card - a card expressing condolence or comfort, often interchangeable with sympathy card
