brainstorm

verb
to think intensely to solve a problem or generate ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnˌstɔrm / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌstɔːm / (BrE)
to try to solve a problem or generate ideas by thinking intensely or freely, often as a group

Examples

To brainstorm ideas
Let’s brainstorm ideas before we start writing the report.
The team decided to brainstorm solutions for the upcoming project.
We spent an hour brainstorming ways to increase productivity.

  • synonym: ideate - to form an idea or concept
