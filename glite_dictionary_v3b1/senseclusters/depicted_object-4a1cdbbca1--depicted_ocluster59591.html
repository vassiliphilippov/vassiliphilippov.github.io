depicted object

noun
subject chosen for artistic representation
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɪktɪd ˈɑbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪktɪd ˈɒbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
the person, object, or scene that an artist or photographer selects to represent in an artwork or image

Examples

The depicted object in art
Photographers often choose a depicted object that evokes strong emotions.
The depicted object in the painting is a serene mountain landscape.
The artist spent hours studying the depicted object before beginning her sketch.
