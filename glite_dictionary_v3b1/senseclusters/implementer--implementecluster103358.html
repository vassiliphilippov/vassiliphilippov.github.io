implementer

noun
maker or seller of tools
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpləˌmɛntər / (AmE), / ˈɪmplɪˌmɛntə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes or sells implements, especially tools or equipment for agriculture.

Examples

Agricultural implementer
The implementer delivered new farming tools to the village before the planting season began.
As an experienced implementer, she advised customers on the best equipment for their needs.
Many farmers rely on a trusted implementer for high-quality agricultural machinery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes goods for sale
  • synonym: supplier - a person or company that provides goods or equipment
