content

adjective
satisfied; at ease with circumstances
2.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛnt / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnt / (BrE)
feeling or showing satisfaction with what one has or the way things are

Examples

Content with life
After the long walk, she was content to relax in the shade with a book.
He seemed perfectly content living a simple life in the countryside.
The child gave a content sigh as he settled into bed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discontent - feeling dissatisfaction or unhappiness with one's situation
  • synonym: satisfied - pleased or content with what has been experienced or received
