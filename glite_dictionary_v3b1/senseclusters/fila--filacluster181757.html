fila

noun
Brazilian working dog
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlə / (AmE), / ˈfiːlə / (BrE)
a large Brazilian breed of working dog known for its loyalty and protective instincts

Examples

The fila dog
The fila is famous in Brazil for its courage and devotion to its family.
Many farmers rely on a fila to protect their livestock from intruders.
A well-trained fila can be both a gentle companion and a fierce guardian.
