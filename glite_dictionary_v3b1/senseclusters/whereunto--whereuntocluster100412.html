whereunto

adverb
to which; to what
Pronunciation: / wɛrˈʌnˌtu / (AmE), / wɛərˈʌnˌtuː / (BrE)
to which place, thing, or situation; to what or which.

Examples

The place whereunto
He signed the document, whereunto he affixed his seal.
The agreement, whereunto both parties consented, was finalized last week.
She pondered the path whereunto her choices might lead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: where - in or to what place or position
  • synonym: whither - to what place
