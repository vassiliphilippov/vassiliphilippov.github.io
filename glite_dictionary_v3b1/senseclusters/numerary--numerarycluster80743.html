numerary

noun
celibate lay member
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːməˌrɛri / (AmE), / ˈnjuːməˌrɛəri / (BrE)
a full lay member of certain Catholic organizations, especially Opus Dei, who usually lives celibately and follows strict rules

Examples

Numerary member of Opus Dei
As a numerary, she dedicated her life to the community and lived according to its rules.
He became a numerary after years of involvement with the organization.
The responsibilities of a numerary include daily prayer and communal living.

Related Terms

  • antonym: associate - a member with a less strict commitment in a religious organization
  • antonym: supernumerary - a member with a less central or less committed role in an organization
