running

adjective
relating to advancing the ball by running (sports)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(in sports) relating to the action of advancing the ball by running rather than passing or kicking.

Examples

Running attack
During practice, they focused on improving running plays instead of passing ones.
Our team's running game was stronger than their defense expected.
The coach praised their running offense for its effectiveness in the final quarter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passing - relating to advancing the ball by throwing or kicking (in team sports)
