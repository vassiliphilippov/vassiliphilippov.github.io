hardtail

noun
guitar with fixed bridge
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːrd.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːd.teɪl/ (BrE)
an electric guitar with a fixed bridge that does not allow pitch bending, providing stable tuning and sustain

Examples

Hardtail electric guitar
Many rock guitarists prefer a hardtail for its reliable tuning during performances.
He replaced his tremolo guitar with a hardtail to get longer sustain on his notes.
The new model features a hardtail design, making it easier to keep in tune.
