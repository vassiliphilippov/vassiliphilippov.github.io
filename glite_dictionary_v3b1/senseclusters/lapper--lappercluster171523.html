lapper

noun
surface polisher
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpər / (AmE), / ˈlæpə / (BrE)
a person or machine that smooths or polishes surfaces by rubbing them with an abrasive material or lap

Examples

Metal lapper tool
The technician used a lapper to achieve a perfectly smooth surface on the metal part.
Modern factories often rely on an automated lapper for precision polishing tasks.
After hours of work, the skilled lapper produced a flawless finish on the glass panel.
