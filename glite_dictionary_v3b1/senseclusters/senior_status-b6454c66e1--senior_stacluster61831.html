senior status

noun
higher rank due to longer service
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.njɚ ˈsteɪ.təs / (AmE), / ˈsiː.ni.ə ˈsteɪ.təs / (BrE)
a position of higher rank or privilege granted due to longer service or greater experience, often used in professional, organizational, or judicial contexts
