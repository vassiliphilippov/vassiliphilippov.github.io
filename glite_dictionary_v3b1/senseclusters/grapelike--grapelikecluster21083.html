grapelike

adjective
resembling a grape
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪpˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪpˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance or qualities of a grape, especially in shape or texture.

Examples

Grapelike bunches
Some fungi grow in grapelike bunches on decaying wood.
The chandelier's grapelike crystals glinted in the sunlight.
The tumor appeared as a cluster of grapelike masses on the scan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grape - a small, round, smooth-skinned fruit
