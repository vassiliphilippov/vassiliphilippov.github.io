Milo

proper noun
chocolate malt drink
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪləʊ / (BrE)
a brand of chocolate and malt powder used to make a sweet, hot or cold beverage

Examples

Milo chocolate drink

Many children enjoy Milo as a breakfast drink with toast.
