just

adverb
possibly, with a slight chance
1.0
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst / (AmE), / dʒʌst / (BrE)
used to indicate a slight chance that something might happen or be true.

Examples

Just might work
It might just work if we hurry.
She could just be telling the truth, but I'm not sure.
The team just might win if they're lucky.

Related Terms

  • antonym: definitely - without doubt, for sure
  • synonym: perhaps - maybe, indicating uncertainty or possibility
  • synonym: possibly - with a possibility, not definitely
