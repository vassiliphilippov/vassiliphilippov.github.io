prosciutto

noun
Italian salt-cured ham, sliced thin
7.6
Pronunciation: / proʊˈʃuːtoʊ / (AmE), / prɒˈʃuːtəʊ / (BrE)
An Italian dry-cured ham, typically sliced very thin and served uncooked.

Examples

Sliced prosciutto ham
He wrapped slices of prosciutto around fresh melon for an appetizer.
She ordered a pizza topped with prosciutto and mushrooms.
The sandwich was filled with prosciutto, cheese, and arugula.
