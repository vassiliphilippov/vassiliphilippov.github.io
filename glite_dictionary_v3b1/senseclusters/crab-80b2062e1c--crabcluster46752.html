Crab

noun
fourth sign of the zodiac
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
The fourth sign of the zodiac, associated with the period when the sun is in Cancer, from about June 21 to July 22.

Examples

The Crab zodiac sign
In astrology, Crab is considered a water sign.
People born under the sign of Crab are said to be intuitive and loyal.
The sun moves into Crab around the summer solstice.
