wieldy

adjective
easy to handle or manage
8.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàwiÀêl.di / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêl.di / (BrE)
easy to handle, use, or manage; not awkward or cumbersome

Examples

A wieldy tool
Despite its size, the toolbox was surprisingly wieldy and easy to carry.
He preferred a wieldy rod for fishing in the narrow stream.
The new design is much more wieldy than the previous bulky model.

Synonyms

  • handleable
    3.8 0.00/m
    handleable describes things that are easy to control or steer, like tools or animals, while wieldy means not awkward to hold or use.
  • handy
    1.7 0.57/m
    handy describes things or places kept within easy reach for quick use, while wieldy means easy to hold and not awkward.
