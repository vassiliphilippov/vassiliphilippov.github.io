Paul Joseph Goebbels

proper noun
Nazi German propaganda minister (1897–1945)
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊl ˈdʒoʊzəf ˈɡɜrbɛlz / (AmE), / ˈpaʊl ˈdʒəʊzɛf ˈɡɜːbɛlz / (BrE)
(1897–1945) German politician who served as the Minister of Propaganda in Nazi Germany, known for orchestrating propaganda campaigns and persecuting Jews.
