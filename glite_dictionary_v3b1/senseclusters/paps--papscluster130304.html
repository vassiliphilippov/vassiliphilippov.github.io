paps

noun
soft food for infants
6.8
Pronunciation: / pæps / (AmE), / pæps / (BrE)
soft, semiliquid food such as porridge or mush, especially prepared for babies or sick people

Examples

Baby paps
The nurse prepared some paps for the elderly patient who had trouble swallowing solid food.
Babies are often fed paps before they are able to eat regular meals.
After his illness, he could only eat paps until he regained his strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mush - a thick, soft, wet mass of food
  • synonym: porridge - a soft food made by boiling grains in milk or water
