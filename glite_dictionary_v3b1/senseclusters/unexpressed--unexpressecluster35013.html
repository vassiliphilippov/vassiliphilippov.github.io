unexpressed

adjective
not made explicit or expressed
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈsprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsprɛst / (BrE)
not stated or made explicit; not put into words or directly communicated

Examples

An unexpressed feeling
His dissatisfaction with the proposal was unexpressed, but everyone sensed it.
Many contracts contain unexpressed expectations that can later cause disputes.
The unexpressed sorrow in her eyes was more powerful than any words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: explicit - stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion or doubt
  • synonym: unspoken - not expressed in speech or writing; tacit
