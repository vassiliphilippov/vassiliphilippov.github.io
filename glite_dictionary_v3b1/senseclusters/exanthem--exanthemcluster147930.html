exanthem

noun
disease-related skin rash or eruption
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˈzænθəm, ɪɡˈzænθəm / (AmE), / ɛɡˈzænθəm, ɪɡˈzænθəm / (BrE)
a widespread skin rash or eruption that appears as a symptom of a disease, especially in children

Examples

A exanthem rash
A viral infection can often cause an exanthem on the trunk and limbs.
Doctors noted a widespread exanthem that prompted further investigation.
The patient's exanthem was a key sign of measles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rash - red spots on the skin, often from an illness
