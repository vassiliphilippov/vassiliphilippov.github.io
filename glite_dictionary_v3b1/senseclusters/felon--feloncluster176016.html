felon

noun
person convicted of a serious crime
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛlən / (AmE), / ˈfɛlən / (BrE)
a person who has been found guilty of a serious crime, especially a felony

Examples

Convicted felon
After serving his sentence, the felon struggled to find employment.
She was labeled a felon after her conviction for grand theft.
The law prohibits felons from possessing firearms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - a person who has committed a crime
  • antonym: innocent - a person not guilty of a crime
