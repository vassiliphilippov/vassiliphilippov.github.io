rhyme

noun
matching ending sound
Pronunciation: / raɪm / (AmE), / raɪm / (BrE)
a word or phrase that has the same ending sound as another word, especially in poetry or songs

Examples

A rhyme for 'cat'
The poet searched for a perfect rhyme to end her verse.
Children enjoy songs with a simple rhyme at the end of each line.
Can you think of a rhyme for the word 'blue'?

Related Terms

  • antonym: prose - written or spoken language in its ordinary form, without metrical structure
  • synonym: verse - a line of poetry
