left-footer

noun
footballer who mainly uses left foot
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛftˌfʊtər / (AmE), / ˈlɛftˌfʊtə / (BrE)
a player, especially in football, who predominantly uses their left foot to kick the ball

Examples

A left-footer player
As a natural left-footer, he often takes their corner kicks and free kicks.
She was the only left-footer on the team, which gave her a unique advantage.
The coach praised the new left-footer for his accurate crosses from the wing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: right-footer - a player who predominantly uses their right foot
