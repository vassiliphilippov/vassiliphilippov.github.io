worktable

noun
table for a specific task
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
A table designed and used for a specific task, such as sewing, writing, or manual work.

Examples

A worktable for sewing
A sturdy worktable is essential for any serious craftsperson.
He spread blueprints across the worktable in his studio before starting the design.
The seamstress kept her sewing machine and fabric neatly organized on her worktable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workbench - a strong, sturdy table for manual work, especially in workshops
