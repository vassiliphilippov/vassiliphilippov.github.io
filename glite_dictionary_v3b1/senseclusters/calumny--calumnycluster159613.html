Examples

Synonyms

  • defamation
    7.1 2.50/m
    defamation is the legal word for harming someone's reputation with false statements, while calumny sounds more literary and older.
  • calumniation
    9.9 0.01/m
    calumniation is a formal, rare noun for making false, damaging statements, while calumny is the standard literary word used in writing.
  • malignment
    9.9 0.00/m
    malignment names slanderous defamation in formal speech, while calumny is a broader older word for false damaging statements.
  • traducement
    9.9 0.00/m
    traducement is a formal, often old-fashioned word for a false damaging statement, while calumny appears in similar literary use.
  • slander
    6.6 2.34/m
    slander is a false spoken statement that harms a reputation, while calumny can refer to spoken or written attacks.
  • smear
    5.7 2.31/m
    smear is an informal, often short attack that hurts someone's name, while calumny is a more formal false accusation.
  • vilification
    8.4 0.56/m
    vilification is harsh, abusive speech meant to show someone as bad, while calumny focuses on false statements that damage reputation.
  • character assassination
    7.3 0.62/m
    character assassination is a planned campaign to ruin someone's public image, while calumny is any single false damaging statement.
