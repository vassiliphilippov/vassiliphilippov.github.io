remontant

noun
plant with repeat blooms
Pronunciation: / riˈmɑntənt / (AmE), / rɪˈmɒntənt / (BrE)
a plant, especially a rose, that produces flowers more than once in a single growing season

Examples

A remontant plant
The gardener planted a remontant to enjoy fresh blooms throughout the summer.
Unlike traditional roses, a remontant will flower several times before autumn arrives.
Many people prefer a remontant for its ability to brighten the garden more than once a year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ever-blooming - producing flowers repeatedly throughout the season
  • antonym: once-blooming - producing flowers only once per season
