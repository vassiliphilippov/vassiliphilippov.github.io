palmature

noun
webbing of the fingers (medical)
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpælmətʃər / (AmE), / ˈpælmətjʊə / (BrE)
A congenital abnormality in which the fingers are joined by webbing.

Examples

Palmature fingers
Inherited genetic factors can sometimes result in palmature in more than one family member.
Surgical correction is often recommended for children with palmature to improve hand function.
The newborn was diagnosed with palmature between the second and third fingers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: syndactyly - the condition of having fused or webbed fingers or toes
