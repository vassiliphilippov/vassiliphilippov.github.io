three-wheel

adjective
having three wheels (vehicle)
Pronunciation: / ˈθriːˌwil / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌwiːl / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having three wheels.

Examples

A three-wheel vehicle
He prefers the stability of a three-wheel motorcycle over a two-wheeled one.
She learned to ride a three-wheel bike when she was a child.
The company developed a new three-wheel electric scooter for urban transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tricycle - a vehicle with three wheels, especially one for children
