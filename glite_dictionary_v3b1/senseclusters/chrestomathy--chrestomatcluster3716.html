chrestomathy

noun
collection of selected literary passages
9.7
Pronunciation: / krɛsˈtɑməθi / (AmE), / krɛsˈtɒməθi / (BrE)
a collection of selected literary passages, especially from different authors, used to aid in learning a language or for literary study

This chrestomathy includes excerpts from prominent Russian authors to support language learners.
