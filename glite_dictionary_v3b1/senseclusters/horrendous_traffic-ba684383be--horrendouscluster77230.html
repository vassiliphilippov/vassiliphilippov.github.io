horrendous traffic

noun
extremely bad traffic congestion
Pronunciation: / həˈrɛndəs ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / hɒˈrɛndəs ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
very severe or extremely bad traffic congestion.

Examples

Horrendous traffic jam
