fond regard

noun
affectionate feeling
Pronunciation: / fɑnd rɪˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / fɒnd rɪˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
Affectionate or warm feelings toward a person or institution.

Examples

Fond regard for someone
He spoke of his mentor with great fond regard and gratitude.
She has a fond regard for her hometown even after moving away.
The students remember their teacher with fond regard years after graduation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affection - a gentle feeling of fondness or liking
  • synonym: esteem - respect and admiration
