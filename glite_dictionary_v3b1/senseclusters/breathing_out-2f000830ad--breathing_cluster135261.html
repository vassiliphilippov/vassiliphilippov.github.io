breathing out

noun
exhaling air from the lungs
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːðɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
the act of expelling air from the lungs during respiration

Examples

Slow breathing out
After finishing her run, she focused on breathing out slowly to calm herself.
He let go of his anxiety by breathing out deeply and relaxing his shoulders.
The yoga instructor emphasized the importance of breathing out during each stretch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhalation - the act of breathing out air from the lungs
  • antonym: inhalation - the act of breathing in air to the lungs
