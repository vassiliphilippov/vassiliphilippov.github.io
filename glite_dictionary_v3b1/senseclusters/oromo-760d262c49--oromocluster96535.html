Oromo

proper noun
Ethiopian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / oʊˈroʊmoʊ / (AmE), / əˈrəʊməʊ / (BrE)
a member of a large ethnic group in Ethiopia, or the Cushitic language spoken by this group

Examples

An Oromo person
The Oromo have a rich cultural heritage and play a significant role in Ethiopian history.
She is fluent in both Amharic and Oromo, the two most widely spoken languages in Ethiopia.
Many Oromo communities celebrate traditional festivals with music and dance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Amhara - another major ethnic group in Ethiopia
  • synonym: Somali - a Cushitic-speaking ethnic group in the Horn of Africa
