among

preposition
shared within a group
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmʌŋ / (AmE), / əˈmʌŋ / (BrE)
used to show that something is shared, given, or divided between three or more people or things in a group

Examples

Shared among students
The teacher distributed the books among the students in the classroom.
There was a sense of excitement among the crowd as the concert began.
The inheritance was divided among the three siblings after their parents passed away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: between - used to show something shared or divided by two people or things
