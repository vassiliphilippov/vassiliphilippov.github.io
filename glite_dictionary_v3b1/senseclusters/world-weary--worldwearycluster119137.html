world-weary

adjective
tired or bored with life and the world
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldˌwɪri / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldˌwɪəri / (BrE)
feeling or showing fatigue, disillusionment, or boredom with life as a result of long experience or having seen too much of the world

Examples

World-weary feeling
After years of reporting on wars, the journalist felt increasingly world-weary and disillusioned.
His world-weary attitude made it hard to excite him about anything new.
She gave a world-weary sigh as she looked out at the crowded city below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cynical - believing that people are motivated by self-interest; distrustful of human sincerity
  • antonym: enthusiastic - having or showing intense and eager enjoyment
  • synonym: jaded - tired, bored, or lacking enthusiasm after having had too much of something
