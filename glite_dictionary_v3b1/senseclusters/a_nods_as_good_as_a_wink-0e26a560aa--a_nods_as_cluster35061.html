a nod's as good as a wink

x
a hint or gesture is enough
Pronunciation: / ə nɑdz əz ɡʊd əz ə wɪŋk / (AmE), / ə nɒdz əz ɡʊd əz ə wɪŋk / (BrE)
Used to say that a hint or suggestion is enough to convey a message or agreement without the need for explicit words.

Examples

A nod's as good as a wink phrase
He didn’t say anything, but you know, a nod's as good as a wink.
He glanced at his friend, and a nod's as good as a wink told him to act.
