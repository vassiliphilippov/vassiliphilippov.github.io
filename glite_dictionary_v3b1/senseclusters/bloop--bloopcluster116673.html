bloop

noun
funny popping sound
7.6
Pronunciation: / bluːp / (AmE), / bluːp / (BrE)
a short, soft, or funny popping or bubbling sound, like a drop of water or a bubble bursting

Examples

A soft bloop
We heard a bloop from the kitchen sink as the last drop fell.
The cartoon used a bloop to make the scene more playful.
A sudden bloop came from the aquarium when the fish surfaced.
The phone made a quiet bloop when the message arrived.
He heard a bloop as the fish jumped out of the water.
The batter hit a bloop that landed just beyond the infield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blip - a short, high-pitched sound or a brief interruption
  • antonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym: plop - a soft sound made by something dropping into liquid
  • synonym: splash - the sound of something hitting or moving in water
