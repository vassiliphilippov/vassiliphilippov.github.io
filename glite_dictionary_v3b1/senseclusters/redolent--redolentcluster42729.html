redolent

adjective
having a strong, pleasant smell
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdələnt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdələnt /; / ˈrɛdəl(ə)nt / (BrE)
having a strong, pleasant, or distinctive smell (often used with 'of' or 'with' to specify the source)

Examples

Redolent of flowers
Her kitchen was always redolent of fresh baking and spices.
The air inside the cabin was redolent with the smell of pinewood and smoke.
The garden was redolent with the scent of blooming roses on summer evenings.
Her kitchen was always redolent with the aroma of freshly baked bread.
The old library was redolent of leather-bound books and polished wood.
The summer air became redolent of blooming jasmine as the evening approached.
