tyrannic

adjective
relating to a tyrant or tyranny
6.1
Pronunciation: / tɪˈrænɪk / (AmE), / tɪˈranɪk / (BrE)
Characteristic of a tyrant or tyranny; exercising or relating to absolute and oppressive power.

Examples

Tyrannic rule
Historians described the king's decade-long reign as both tyrannic and cruel.
Many people lived in fear under the tyrannic rule of the monarch.
The tyrannic measures imposed by the regime led to widespread unrest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tyrannical - characteristic of a tyrant; oppressive and unjustly severe
