takeout

noun

Examples

Order takeout food

Synonyms

  • carryout
    2.7 0.15/m
    carryout refers to prepared food you take away and can mean the shop that sells it, while takeout names the food to eat elsewhere.
  • takeaway
    1.5 0.17/m
    takeaway is the British term for food bought to eat elsewhere and often comes in a paper box, while takeout is the American form.
