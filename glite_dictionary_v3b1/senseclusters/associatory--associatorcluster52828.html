associatory

adjective
relating to mental associations
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsiəˌtɔri / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiətəri / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from the process of forming mental connections between ideas or events

Examples

An associatory process
His dreams showed a highly associatory pattern, connecting unrelated events and images.
Researchers studied the associatory mechanisms behind learning new languages.
The psychologist described how associatory processes can link memories from different times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: associative - relating to or involving association, especially of ideas in the mind
