stiff aster

noun
a wiry perennial plant with violet flowers
5.4
Pronunciation: / stɪf ˈæstər / (AmE), / stɪf ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a wiry perennial plant native to the eastern United States, with stiff erect rough stems, narrow leaves, and large violet flowers

Examples

The stiff aster plant
Gardeners often choose stiff aster for its hardy nature and beautiful violet blooms.
The stiff aster flourishes in dry, sandy soils throughout the eastern United States.
We spotted a cluster of stiff asters growing at the edge of the forest.
