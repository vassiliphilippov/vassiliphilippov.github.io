Plattensee

proper noun
German name for Lake Balaton, a Hungarian lake
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑːtənseɪ / (AmE), / ˈplatənzeɪ / (BrE)
the German name for Lake Balaton, a large shallow lake in western Hungary, popular as a tourist destination.
