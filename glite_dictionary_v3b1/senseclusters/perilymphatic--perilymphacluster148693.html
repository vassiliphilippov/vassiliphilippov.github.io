perilymphatic

adjective
around inner ear fluid
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪlɪmˈfætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪlɪmˈfætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or located around the perilymph, the fluid found in the inner ear

Examples

Perilymphatic fluid space
The surgeon carefully avoided the perilymphatic space during the procedure.
Damage to the perilymphatic area can affect hearing and balance.
Researchers studied the perilymphatic tissues to understand inner ear disorders.
