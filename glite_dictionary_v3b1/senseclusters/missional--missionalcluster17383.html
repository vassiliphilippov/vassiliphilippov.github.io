missional

adjective
mission-driven (secular)
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /ˈmɪʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by a clear mission or purpose, especially used for organizations, strategies, or activities that prioritise social impact rather than profit; used in secular, non-religious contexts.

Examples

Missional business strategy
The social enterprise adopted a missional strategy to maximize community impact and long-term change.
Her startup's missional focus on affordable housing attracted investors who cared about social outcomes.
Boards now ask whether programs are missional enough to justify continued funding and organizational priorities.

Related Terms
