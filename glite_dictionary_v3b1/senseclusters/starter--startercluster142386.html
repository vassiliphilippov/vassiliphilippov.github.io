starter

noun
official who starts a race
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
an official who signals the start of a race or competition, typically by using a starting gun or flag

Examples

Race starter
All runners must wait for the starter to give the signal before they can move.
The starter made sure everyone was ready before starting the marathon.
The starter raised his pistol and fired, signaling the beginning of the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referee - an official who enforces the rules in a competition
