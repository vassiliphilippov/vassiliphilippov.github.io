article

noun
a nonfictional work in a publication
2.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈɑːr.tə.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈɑː.tə.kəl/ (BrE)
a piece of nonfictional writing or self-contained content, such as in a newspaper, magazine, or on a website

Examples

A news article

  • antonym: story - narrative, typically fictional; distinguished from article by nonfictional focus
