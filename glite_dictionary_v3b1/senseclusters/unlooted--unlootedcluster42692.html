unlooted

adjective
not stolen from
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈluːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈluːtɪd / (BrE)
not having been looted or stolen from; remaining untouched by thieves or robbers

Examples

An unlooted temple
The ancient temple remained unlooted despite centuries of war and unrest.
They were amazed to discover an unlooted tomb filled with priceless artifacts.
The museum displayed treasures from an unlooted shipwreck found off the coast.

  • antonym: looted - having been stolen from or plundered
