  • indeterminate
    7.0 1.75/m

  • undetermined
    4.6 0.97/m
    undetermined is used when a fact or result is not fixed yet, while uncertain covers a general lack of certainty.
  • indefinite
    4.0 3.50/m

  • unsettled
    4.2 1.67/m

  • undecided
