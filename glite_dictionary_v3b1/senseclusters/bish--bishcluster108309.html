bish

noun
slang for person
Pronunciation: / bɪʃ / (AmE), / bɪʃ / (BrE)
a playful slang term for a person, often used among friends as a lighthearted or teasing address

Examples

Hey, bish!
She greeted her best friend with a cheerful 'Hey, bish!'
He laughed and said, 'You are such a bish sometimes.'
Among close friends, calling someone bish can be a sign of affection.
