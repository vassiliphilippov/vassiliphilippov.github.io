toolroom

noun
room for tools
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtuːl.ruːm/ (AmE), /ˈtuːl.ruːm/ (BrE)
a room or area in a factory or workshop where tools, dies, or molds are stored, made, maintained, or repaired

Examples

The toolroom area
The mechanic went to the toolroom to fetch a wrench.
All the equipment is carefully organized in the toolroom for easy access.
Only authorized staff are allowed to enter the toolroom during working hours.
The mechanic went to the toolroom to get a new wrench for the repair.
All specialized equipment is kept in the toolroom for easy access by the staff.
After every shift, workers must return borrowed tools to the toolroom.
The new toolroom is equipped with advanced machines for making custom dies.
Engineers visited the toolroom to check the progress on the specialized molds.
A skilled technician works in the toolroom to maintain the factory's cutting tools.
