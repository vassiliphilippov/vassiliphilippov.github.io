Synonyms

  • gravesite
    3.8 0.67/m
    gravesite is the exact spot where someone lies in the ground, while resting place is a polite general term.
  • grave
    1.0 19/m
    grave is a single burial hole often marked by a stone, while resting place is a softer, general phrase for burial spot.
  • burial site
    3.7 0.59/m
    burial site is a specific area used for burials or mass graves, while resting place usually means an individual's grave.
  • burial ground
    3.9 0.80/m
    burial ground is a piece of land used for burials and community plots, while resting place points to one person's grave.
  • graveyard
    1.8 5.46/m
    graveyard is a churchyard or small burial area often near a church, while resting place means any single grave.
  • burying ground
    6.1 0.04/m
    burying ground is an older or regional word for a cemetery, while resting place is a modern, gentle term for a grave.
  • cemetery
    2.8 17/m
    cemetery is a larger place set aside for many burials, while resting place refers to a single person's grave or burial spot.
