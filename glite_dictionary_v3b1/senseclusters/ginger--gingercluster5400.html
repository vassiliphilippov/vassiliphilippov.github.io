ginger

noun
aromatic perennial plant; source of spicy root
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
A perennial plant (Zingiber officinale) with thick, branching, aromatic rhizomes and leafy, reedlike stems, widely cultivated for its pungent, spicy root used in cooking and medicine.

Examples

Ginger plant leaves
Fresh ginger is often used in Asian cuisine to add flavor and aroma to dishes.
She planted ginger in her garden for both culinary and medicinal uses.
The tea was made with slices of ginger and a touch of honey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ginger root - the rhizome of the ginger plant used as a spice
