portlight

noun
ship window
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrtˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a small, usually round window in the side of a ship or boat

Examples

A portlight window
She gazed through the portlight at the endless expanse of ocean outside.
A sudden flash of lightning illuminated the cabin through the portlight.
Salt spray collected on the portlight, making it hard to see the horizon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: porthole - a small, round window in the side of a ship
  • degree higher: window - an opening in a wall or vehicle to let in light and air
