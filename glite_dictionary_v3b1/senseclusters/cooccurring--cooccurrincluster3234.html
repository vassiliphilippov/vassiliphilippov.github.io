cooccurring

adjective
happening at the same time
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.əˈkɜr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.əˈkɜː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or existing at the same time as something else, especially by chance or in parallel
