military attache

noun
embassy official specializing in military matters
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˌætəˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˌætæˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
an official attached to an embassy or diplomatic mission who is a specialist in military matters and represents their country's armed forces

Examples

Military attache at embassy
As military attache, she attended international arms control negotiations on behalf of her government.
Reports of troop movements were shared with the ambassador by the military attache.
The embassy's military attache organized a meeting between defense officials from both countries.
