post horn

noun
historic brass wind instrument for signaling
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊst hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst hɔːn / (BrE)
a simple brass wind instrument, formerly used by postilions or mail coaches to signal arrival or departure

Examples

A post horn instrument
A gleaming post horn was displayed in the museum's mail coach exhibit.
In the 19th century, a post horn was essential for every long-distance stagecoach.
The coach driver blew his post horn as they approached the village.
