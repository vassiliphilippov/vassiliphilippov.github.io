feel

noun
intuitive awareness or instinct
1.0
Pronunciation: / fil / (AmE), / fiːl / (BrE)
an intuitive awareness or natural ability, especially that which comes from experience or instinct rather than explicit knowledge

Examples

A feel for music
His feel for music impressed everyone at the audition.
It took me a while to get the feel of the new software.
She has a real feel for languages and learns them quickly.

Related Terms
