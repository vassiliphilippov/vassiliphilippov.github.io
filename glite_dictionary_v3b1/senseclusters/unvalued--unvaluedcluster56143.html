unvalued

adjective
not valued or appreciated
4.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvæljud / (AmE), / ʌnˈvaljuːd / (BrE)
not acknowledged as valuable or appreciated; not given due recognition or worth

Examples

An unvalued worker
Her contributions often went unvalued by those around her.
Many artists feel their work is unvalued by society until long after their death.
The old books sat unvalued on the dusty shelves for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appreciated - recognized and valued
  • antonym: valued - regarded as important or having worth
