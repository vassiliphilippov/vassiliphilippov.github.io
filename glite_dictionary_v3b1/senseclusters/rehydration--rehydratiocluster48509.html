rehydration

noun
restoring body water
3.7
Pronunciation: /riː.haɪ.ˈdreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.haɪ.ˈdreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.haɪ.ˈdreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.haɪ.ˈdreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of restoring lost water to the body, especially after dehydration

Examples

Rehydration after exercise
After running a marathon, rehydration is essential to help the body recover.
Doctors recommend rehydration solutions for patients suffering from severe diarrhea.
Proper rehydration can prevent headaches and fatigue after exercise or illness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dehydration - a condition caused by excessive loss of water from the body
  • synonym: hydration - the process of providing adequate water to the body
