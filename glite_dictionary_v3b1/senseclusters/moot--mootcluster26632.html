moot

verb
to suggest for discussion
Pronunciation: / muːt / (AmE), / muːt / (BrE)
to suggest or bring up a topic or idea for discussion or debate

Examples

To moot an idea
The committee mooted several possible solutions to the ongoing problem.
A new policy was mooted during the annual conference last week.
He mooted the idea of extending the deadline to give everyone more time.
She decided to moot the idea of a new project during the team meeting.
The committee will moot several possible solutions before making a decision.
It is important to moot any concerns early in the planning process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately not consider or discuss something
  • synonym: propose - to put forward an idea or plan for consideration
  • synonym: raise - to bring up a topic or issue for discussion
