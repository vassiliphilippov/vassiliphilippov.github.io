fumarate

noun
salt of fumaric acid
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːməˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈfjuːməˌreɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of fumaric acid, often used in chemistry and medicine

Examples

Iron fumarate
The doctor prescribed iron fumarate to treat her anemia.
In biochemistry, fumarate is an important intermediate in the Krebs cycle.
Some medications contain fumarate as an active ingredient to improve absorption.

Related Terms

  • synonym: succinate - a salt or ester of succinic acid
