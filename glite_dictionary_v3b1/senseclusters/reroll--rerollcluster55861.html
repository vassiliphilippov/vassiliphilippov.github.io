reroll

noun
extra dice roll
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈroʊl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈrəʊl / (BrE)
an extra chance to roll dice or generate a new random result in a game, often to improve an outcome

Examples

A lucky reroll
He used his only reroll to try for a better score in the board game.
The game allows one reroll per turn if you are not satisfied with your result.
After a bad first throw, she was relieved to have a reroll available.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chance - an opportunity to do something again
  • synonym: retry - an additional attempt to achieve a better result
