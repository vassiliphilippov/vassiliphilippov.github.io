Gaius Octavianus

proper noun
first Roman emperor, also called Augustus
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgaɪ.əs ɑːk.ˈteɪ.vɪ.ə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈgaɪ.əs ɒk.ˈteɪ.vɪ.ə.nəs/ (BrE)
