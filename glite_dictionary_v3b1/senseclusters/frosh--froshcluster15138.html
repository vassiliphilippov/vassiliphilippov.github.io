frosh

noun
first-year student
Pronunciation: / frɑʃ / (AmE), / frɒʃ / (BrE)
an informal term for a first-year student at a high school or college, especially in North America

Examples

A college frosh
The university organized a special welcome event for all incoming frosh this semester.
As a frosh, she was excited but also nervous about starting college life.
Many frosh join clubs to make new friends during their first year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freshman - a first-year student at a high school or college
  • antonym: senior - a student in their final year of high school or college
