Synonyms

  • sparkle
    1.0 2.17/m
    sparkle gives bright, lively flashes across a surface, while twinkle is a small, often softer flashing of light.
  • flicker
    3.6 7.32/m
    flicker means light moves or burns unsteadily and quickly, while twinkle describes short bright flashes or lively eyes.
  • scintillate
    8.5 0.07/m
    scintillate sends sharp, bright flashes and sounds formal, while twinkle is more gentle and common for eyes or stars.
  • shimmer
    4.3 6.31/m
    shimmer means a soft, wavering shine across a surface, while twinkle is a quick sparkling flash from a point source.
  • glimmer
    3.9 1.71/m
    glimmer gives a faint, weak light that barely shows, while twinkle makes distinct small flashes or lively bright eyes.
