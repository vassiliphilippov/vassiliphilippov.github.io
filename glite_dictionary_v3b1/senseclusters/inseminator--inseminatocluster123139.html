inseminator

noun
sperm donor
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛmɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person who donates sperm for use in reproduction or fertility treatment

Examples

A sperm inseminator
The clinic required the inseminator to undergo several health screenings before donating.
An anonymous inseminator helped the couple achieve their dream of having a child.
Some people choose to meet the inseminator before starting fertility treatment.

  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something, such as sperm or an organ
