homeliness

noun
lack of stylishness or elegance
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm.li.nəs /; / ˈhəʊmli.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of not being attractive or beautiful; plainness or drabness in appearance

Examples

Simple homeliness
Despite her homeliness, her kindness attracted many friends.
He preferred the homeliness of simple clothes over flashy designer outfits.
The homeliness of the room made it feel uninviting and dull.
Despite her homeliness, she had an infectious smile that drew people to her.
He was painfully aware of his own homeliness when he looked in the mirror.
The critics noted the deliberate homeliness of the main character’s costume design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beauty - a combination of qualities that pleases the senses, especially the sight
  • antonym: elegance - the quality of being stylish or graceful
  • synonym: plainness - the quality of being simple or lacking decoration
