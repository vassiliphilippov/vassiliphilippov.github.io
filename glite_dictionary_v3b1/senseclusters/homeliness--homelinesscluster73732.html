homeliness

noun
lack of stylishness or elegance
7.6 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.lɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.lɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of not being attractive or beautiful; plainness or drabness in appearance
