struvite

noun
urinary mineral crystal
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstruːvaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstruːvaɪt / (BrE)
a crystalline mineral (magnesium ammonium phosphate) that can form in kidney stones, wastewater systems, or be used as a fertilizer
