ship

verb
to transport goods commercially
1.0 1.1
Pronunciation: /ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ʃɪp/ (BrE)
to send or transport goods, especially commercially, by sea, air, or land

Examples

To ship goods
Due to high demand, we are shipping new products every week.
The company will ship your order within two business days.
They shipped the furniture overseas last month.
Please make sure all orders are shipped by the end of the day.
The workers will ship the furniture to Europe next week.
They shipped the machinery overseas by cargo vessel.

  • antonym: receive - to be given or delivered something that was sent
  • synonym: send - to cause to go or be taken to a particular destination
