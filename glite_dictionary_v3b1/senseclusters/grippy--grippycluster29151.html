grippy

adjective
exciting or suspenseful
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪpi / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpi / (BrE)
exciting, interesting, or suspenseful in a way that strongly holds your attention

Examples

Grippy story
The movie was so grippy that I couldn't look away for a second.
Her grippy storytelling style keeps readers turning the pages late into the night.
Everyone agreed that the series finale was the most grippy episode of the season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boring - not interesting; dull and unexciting
  • synonym: gripping - very exciting or interesting; holding your attention completely
