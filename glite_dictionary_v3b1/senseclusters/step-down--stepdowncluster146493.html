step-down

noun
device that reduces voltage
Pronunciation: / st5bp da8an / (AmE), / st5bp da8an / (BrE)
A device or circuit (such as a transformer or voltage regulator) that reduces electrical voltage or current to a lower level.

Examples

Step-down transformer
The lab installed a step-down transformer to power the sensitive sensor from the main supply.
An electrician recommended a compact step-down unit to lower the voltage for the new equipment.
If your device needs less current, select a certified step-down regulator with the correct output rating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transformer - an electrical device that transfers energy between circuits and can change voltage levels
  • synonym: voltage regulator - an electronic device or circuit that maintains a constant output voltage
