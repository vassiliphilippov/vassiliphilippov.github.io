greensickness

noun
archaic term for anemia in young women
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.sɪk.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.sɪk.nəs/ (BrE)
an old term for a form of anemia in young women, marked by weakness, menstrual problems, and a pale or greenish skin tint
