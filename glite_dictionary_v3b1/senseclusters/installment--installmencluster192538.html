installment

noun
a part of a serial publication
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɑlmənt /; / ɪnˈstɔlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɔːlmənt /; / ɪnˈstɑːlmənt / (BrE)
one of the parts or episodes of a story, series, or broadcast that is released separately at intervals

Examples

Next installment of the series
Fans eagerly awaited the next installment of the popular crime drama.
The author released the latest installment of the novel online each Monday.
This magazine will feature a special installment of the investigative series next month.
Fans eagerly awaited each installment of the podcast to see how the story unfolded.
The detective show released a new installment every week.
The next installment of the drama series will air on Monday night.
Each week brought a new instalment in the thrilling detective series.
Fans eagerly awaited the final instalment to discover the fate of their favorite characters.
The first instalment of the drama aired last night to excellent reviews.
