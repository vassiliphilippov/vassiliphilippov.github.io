ambitious

adjective
requiring great skill or effort
Pronunciation: / æmˈbɪʃ.əs / (AmE), / æmˈbɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
(of a plan, idea, or task) requiring a great deal of skill, effort, or resources to achieve.

Examples

An ambitious plan
Completing the ambitious project within a month required the whole team's dedication.
He took on an ambitious schedule that challenged everyone involved.
The company announced an ambitious expansion plan covering three new countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demanding - requiring much effort or attention
  • antonym: unambitious - not requiring much effort or skill; modest in scope
