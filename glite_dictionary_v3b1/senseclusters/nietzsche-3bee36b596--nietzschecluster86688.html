Nietzsche

proper noun
German philosopher (1844–1900)
Pronunciation: / ˈniːtʃə / (AmE), / ˈniːtʃə / (BrE)
Friedrich Nietzsche (1844–1900), a German philosopher known for his ideas on the 'superman,' the critique of Christian values, and contributions to existentialism.

Examples

Nietzsche philosophy
Nietzsche argued that traditional moral values were outdated and needed to be re-evaluated.
Nietzsche's philosophy has had a lasting impact on modern existential and postmodern thought.
Many students are introduced to the concept of the 'will to power' through Nietzsche's writings.
