salar

noun
salt flat
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑr / (AmE), / səˈlɑː / (BrE)
a large, flat expanse of land covered with salt, typically found in desert regions

Examples

The salar in Bolivia
The famous salar in Bolivia becomes a giant mirror after it rains.
Scientists study the unique ecosystem of the salar to learn about extreme environments.
Tourists often visit the salar to take stunning photographs of the endless white landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desert - a dry, barren area of land with little rainfall
  • synonym: salt flat - a flat area of land covered with salt deposits
