puppeteer

noun
person who operates puppets
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌp.əˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌpʌp.ɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who operates and manipulates puppets, especially as a performer in a show

Examples

A puppeteer controls puppets
As a skilled puppeteer, she brings each character to life with subtle hand movements.
Many television programs hire a puppeteer to create engaging shows for young audiences.
The famous puppeteer captivated the children with his lively marionettes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puppet - a movable model of a person or animal used in entertainment
