distractedly

adverb
in a distracted manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstræktɪdli / (AmE), / dɪsˈtræktɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of concentration or is preoccupied by something else.

Examples

To speak distractedly
He spoke distractedly, clearly thinking about something else.
She glanced distractedly at her phone during the meeting.
While stirring the soup, she hummed distractedly to herself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absentmindedly - in an absentminded manner; lacking attention to surroundings
