gel

noun
jelly-like hair styling substance
Pronunciation: / dʒɛl / (AmE), / dʒɛl / (BrE)
a jelly-like substance, especially one used for styling and holding hair in place

Examples

Hair gel
He applied a generous amount of gel for a slicked-back look.
I use gel every morning to keep my hair in place.
This gel holds even the thickest hair styles all day.
Hair styling products often come in the form of a gel that holds shapes in place.
The enzyme moved slowly through the gel during electrophoresis.
The scientist poured the solution into a mold, and soon it formed a gel within minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hair mousse - a foam product used to style hair
  • antonym: sol - a colloidal solution, more liquid than a gel
