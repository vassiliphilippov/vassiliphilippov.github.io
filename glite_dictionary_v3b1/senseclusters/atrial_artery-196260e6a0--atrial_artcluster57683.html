atrial artery

noun
artery supplying the heart's atria
6.4 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.tri.əl ˈɑːr.tər.iː/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.tri.əl ˈɑː.tə.riː/ (BrE)
