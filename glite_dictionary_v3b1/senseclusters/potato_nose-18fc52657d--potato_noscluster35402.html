potato nose

noun
enlarged, bulbous, red nose
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪɾoʊ noʊz / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ nəʊz / (BrE)
A nose that appears enlarged, red, and bulbous, often with visible blood vessels, sometimes associated with chronic alcohol consumption or certain skin conditions.

Examples

A potato nose
His potato nose was instantly noticeable at parties.
Over the years, the man's potato nose became more pronounced.
Some people develop a potato nose due to underlying skin conditions or lifestyle choices.
