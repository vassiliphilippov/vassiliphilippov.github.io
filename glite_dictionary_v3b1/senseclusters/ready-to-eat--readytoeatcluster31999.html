ready-to-eat

adjective
food that can be eaten as sold
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈre.dɪ tə iːt/ /; / /ˈre.dɪ tuː iːt/ (AmE), /ˈre.dɪ tə iːt/ /; / /ˈre.dɪ tuː iːt/ (BrE)
(of food) prepared in advance and able to be eaten without further cooking or preparation.

Examples

Ready-to-eat food
