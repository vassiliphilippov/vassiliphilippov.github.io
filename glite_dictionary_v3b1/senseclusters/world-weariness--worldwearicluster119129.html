world-weariness

noun
sadness caused by reflecting on the world's troubles
9.1 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ùÀêld Àåw…™r.i.n…ôs / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀêld Àåw…™…ô.ri.n…ôs / (BrE)
a feeling of sadness or disillusionment caused by reflecting on the troubles or evils of the world

Examples

A feeling of world-weariness
A sense of world-weariness crept over her after reading the news every morning.
After years in politics, he showed clear signs of world-weariness and disillusionment.
His poems often express world-weariness and disappointment in humanity.

Synonyms

  • Weltschmerz
    9.9 0.07/m
    Weltschmerz is a literary word for deep sadness about the world's suffering, while world-weariness is sadness at seeing the world's evils.
  • melancholy
    4.9 2.12/m
    melancholy is a deep, long-lasting sadness in a person, while world-weariness is sadness tied to seeing the world's troubles and evils.
