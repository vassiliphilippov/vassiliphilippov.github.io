go up

verb
be posted or published online
3.2
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈʌp / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of content) to be posted, uploaded, or published on a website or social media, becoming available to readers or viewers.

Examples

A post goes up online
The article will go up on the site later today, drawing more readers.
Several photos went up on the band's Instagram while they were on tour.
The review is going up on the site tonight, so check back after nine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: post - to publish content on a website or social media platform
  • synonym: publish - to make content available to the public, especially online or in print
