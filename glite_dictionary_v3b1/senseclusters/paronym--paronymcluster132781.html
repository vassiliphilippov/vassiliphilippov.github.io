paronym

noun
word similar in form to another
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpæɹ.əˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈpa.rə.nɪm / (BrE)
a word that is similar in form and spelling to another word but differs in meaning and origin

Examples

Paronyms in English
"Accept" and "except" are paronyms that are often confused by English learners.
Care should be taken not to mix up paronyms like "elicit" and "illicit" in writing.
In her essay, she discussed several paronyms and explained their different meanings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homonym - a word pronounced or spelled the same as another but with different meaning
