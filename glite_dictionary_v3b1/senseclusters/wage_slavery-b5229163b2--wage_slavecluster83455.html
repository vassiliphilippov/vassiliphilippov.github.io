wage slavery

noun

4.2 6.4
Pronunciation: /weɪdʒ ˈsleɪ.vər.iː/ /; / /weɪdʒ ˈsleɪv.riː/ /; / /weɪdʒ ˈslæv.ə.riː/ (AmE), /weɪdʒ ˈsleɪ.və.riː/ /; / /weɪdʒ ˈsleɪv.riː/ /; / /weɪdʒ ˈslæv.ə.riː/ (BrE)
A situation in which a person is compelled to work for very low pay under exploitative conditions, often with little control or alternative options.

Examples
