commenter

noun
person giving commentary
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmɛntər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɛntə / (BrE)
a person who provides spoken or written commentary, especially during an event or on a topic

Examples

Sports commenter
The commenter provided insightful analysis throughout the football match.
Each commenter on the panel offered a different perspective on the news story.
During the live stream, a popular commenter kept the audience engaged with witty remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commentator - a person who describes events as they happen, especially on TV or radio
  • synonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
