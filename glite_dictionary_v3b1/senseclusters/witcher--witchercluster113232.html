witcher

noun
fantasy monster hunter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃə / (BrE)
a fictional monster hunter with supernatural abilities, especially in fantasy literature and games

Examples

A witcher hunts monsters
The witcher tracked the beast through the dark forest.
In the story, a skilled witcher is hired to protect the village from monsters.
Fans of fantasy novels often admire the bravery of a witcher facing dangerous creatures.

Related Terms
