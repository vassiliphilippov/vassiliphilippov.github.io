tower

verb
appear dominant or very large
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊ.ɚ /; / ˈtaʊər / (AmE), / ˈtaʊ.ə /; / ˈtaʊə / (BrE)
to be much taller or more dominant than things around

Examples

To tower over others
During the presentation, her confidence towered above that of her colleagues.
Mount Everest towers impressively above the surrounding peaks.
The new skyscraper towers over all the other buildings in the city center.
The ancient castle towers above the small village, visible from miles away.
A huge oak tree towered over the playground, providing shade for the children.
The new office building will tower over all the other structures in the area.
