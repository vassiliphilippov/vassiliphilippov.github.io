bloop

interjection
comic popping sound
Pronunciation: / bluːp / (AmE), / bluːp / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating a small popping or bubbling sound, often used to mark a light or comic action

Examples

Bloop of surprise
When the bubble burst, she giggled and said bloop to make her friend laugh.
He pressed the button and a little light flashed with a cheerful bloop.
Every time the cartoon character jumped, the show added a funny bloop sound effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boop - an exclamation imitating a light, playful sound
  • synonym: pop - an exclamation imitating a sudden, small explosion or sound
