nonactive

adjective
without activity
6.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈæktɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈæktɪv / (BrE)
having no chemical, pharmacological, or biological activity; not producing any physiological or therapeutic effect.

Examples

Nonactive ingredient
The medication contains both nonactive and active ingredients to help with absorption.
Scientists confirmed that the compound was completely nonactive in their laboratory tests.
Some tablets use nonactive substances as fillers to give them shape.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - having an effect or producing activity
  • synonym: inactive - not active or not functioning
