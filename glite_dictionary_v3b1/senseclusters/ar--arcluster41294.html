ar

noun
metric unit of area (100 square meters)
Pronunciation: / ɑr / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
a metric unit of area equal to 100 square meters, chiefly used for measuring land.

Examples

Land price per ar
Prices are listed per ar for buyers interested in purchasing farmland.
The farmer owns a plot measuring fifteen ar.
The property covers just one ar of land, making it relatively small.
