smallmouth

noun
a type of freshwater black bass
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
a species of freshwater black bass, distinguished by its relatively small mouth, commonly found in North American rivers and lakes

Examples

A smallmouth bass
He caught a large smallmouth near the rocky shoreline last summer.
Many anglers consider the smallmouth to be one of the best fighting fish in freshwater.
The lake is known for its abundant smallmouth population.

Related Terms

  • antonym: largemouth - a species of black bass with a larger mouth
