untangle

verb
separate things that are tangled
4.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ /; / /ʌn.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈtæŋ.ɡəl/ /; / /ən.ˈtæŋ.ɡəl/ (BrE)
to separate things that are twisted together or caught in one another, especially fibers or threads, so that they are no longer tangled

Examples

To untangle a knot
It took him an hour to untangle the knots in his fishing line.
She carefully began to untangle the string that held the package together.
The cat had to be rescued after getting completely untangled from the cords.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tangle - to become twisted together in a confused mass
  • synonym: unravel - to undo twisted, knitted, or woven threads
