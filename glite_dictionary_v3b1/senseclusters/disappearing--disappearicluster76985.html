disappearing

noun
the act of vanishing secretly or suddenly
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈpɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈpɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of leaving a place secretly or without explanation

Examples

Secret disappearing
His disappearing left everyone puzzled and concerned for his safety.
The magician's greatest trick involved the disappearing of a rabbit from the hat.
There was a sudden disappearing of evidence from the crime scene.
