delicate

noun
fragile clothing items
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪkət / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkət / (BrE)
items of clothing made from fine or fragile materials that need gentle care, especially when washing

Examples

Wash delicates gently
Always wash your delicates in cold water to prevent them from getting damaged.
She keeps her delicates in a separate drawer lined with soft fabric.
The laundry label recommends using a mesh bag for delicates in the washing machine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lingerie - women's underwear and nightclothes, often made of delicate fabric
  • synonym: undergarment - a piece of clothing worn under outer clothes
