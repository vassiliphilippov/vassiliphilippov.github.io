cinnabar

adjective
vivid red to reddish-orange in color
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.əˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.əˌbɑː / (BrE)
of a vivid red to reddish-orange color, resembling the mineral cinnabar.

Examples

Cinnabar color
A scarf of cinnabar silk drew every eye in the room.
Her lips were painted a striking cinnabar shade for the evening gala.
The artist used a cinnabar hue to suggest intensity and warmth in his painting.

  • synonym: vermilion - bright red to reddish-orange color
