becloud

verb
make less visible or obscure
6.5 7.1
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈklaʊd/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈklaʊd/ (BrE)
to make less visible, distinct, or clear; to obscure or cloud over

Examples

Smoke beclouds the sky
Heavy smoke beclouded the skyline after the fire broke out.
His judgment was beclouded by anger and resentment.
The morning fog began to becloud the harbor, hiding the ships from view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obscure - to make unclear or less visible
