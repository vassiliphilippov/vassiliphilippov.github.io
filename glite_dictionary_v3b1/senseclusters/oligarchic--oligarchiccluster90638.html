oligarchic

adjective
relating to an oligarchy
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːlɪˈɡɑːrkɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, supporting, or characteristic of an oligarchy, especially in reference to a society or government controlled by a small group of people.

Examples

Oligarchic government
Critics argue that the reforms only strengthened the oligarchic control of the elite.
Despite the appearance of democracy, the government remained essentially oligarchic.
The country's oligarchic structure has led to widespread social inequality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democratic - relating to democracy, rule by the people
  • synonym: oligarchical - alternative form meaning the same as 'oligarchic'
