trichomoniasis

noun
parasitic sexually transmitted infection
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪkəˌmoʊˈnaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌtrɪkəˌməʊˈnaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
A sexually transmitted infection caused by the parasite Trichomonas vaginalis, often affecting the vagina but may also affect the urinary tract.

Examples

Trichomoniasis infection
Effective treatment for trichomoniasis usually involves a course of antibiotics.
Symptoms of trichomoniasis include vaginal itching and unusual discharge.
The doctor diagnosed her with trichomoniasis after reviewing her test results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chlamydia - a common sexually transmitted infection
  • synonym: gonorrhea - a sexually transmitted bacterial infection
