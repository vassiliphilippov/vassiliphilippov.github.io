coracoid

adjective
shoulder bone area
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəˌkɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəkɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to the coracoid, a bone or bony process in the shoulder region of vertebrates.

Examples

Coracoid process injury
The surgeon carefully avoided the coracoid process during the shoulder operation.
Pain near the coracoid area can indicate an injury to the shoulder joint.
The anatomy class studied the coracoid structure in both birds and mammals.
