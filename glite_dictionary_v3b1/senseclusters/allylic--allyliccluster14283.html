allylic

adjective
relating to the allyl group in chemistry
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlɪlɪk / (AmE), / əˈlɪlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing the allyl group, especially in reference to a position or structure adjacent to an alkene in organic compounds.

Examples
