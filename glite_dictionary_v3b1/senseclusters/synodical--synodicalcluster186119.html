synodical

adjective
of a church synod
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɑːdɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒdɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a synod or a formal meeting of church officials

Examples

Synodical meeting
The bishop attended a synodical conference to address important issues in the diocese.
New rules were established during the synodical meeting last spring.
The synodical decisions were published for all church members to review.
