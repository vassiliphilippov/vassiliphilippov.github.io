windedly

adverb
in a roundabout way
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndɪdli / (AmE), / ˈwɪndɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner that is winding, twisting, or indirect; in a roundabout way

Examples

Explained windedly
She described the route so windedly that I got confused halfway through.
The instructions were given windedly, making the process seem more complicated than it was.
He answered the question windedly, never getting straight to the point.

  • synonym: indirectly - not in a straight or direct way
