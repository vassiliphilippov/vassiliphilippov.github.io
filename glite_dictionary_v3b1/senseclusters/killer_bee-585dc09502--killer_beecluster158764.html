killer bee

noun
aggressive hybrid bee species
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlər biː / (AmE), / ˈkɪlə biː / (BrE)
a hybrid strain of bee, originally from Brazil, known for its aggressive behavior, produced by crossing African bees with European honeybees

Examples

A swarm of killer bees
After being attacked by a swarm of killer bees, the farmer needed medical attention.
Many people are frightened when they hear reports of killer bees moving into new areas.
Researchers are studying how killer bees impact local ecosystems and native bee populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Africanized bee - another name for the killer bee, especially in scientific contexts
