Moon

noun
Earth's natural satellite
Pronunciation: / muːn / (AmE), / muːn / (BrE)
The natural satellite of the Earth, visible at night by reflected sunlight.

Examples

The Moon at night
The astronauts admired the bright Moon during their historic mission.
The full Moon illuminated the countryside with a silvery glow.
We studied the phases of the Moon in our science class.
