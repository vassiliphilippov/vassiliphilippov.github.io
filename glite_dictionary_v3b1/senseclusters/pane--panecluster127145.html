pane

noun
panel or section of a wall or door
6.7
Pronunciation: / peɪn / (AmE), / peɪn / (BrE)
a panel or distinct section of a surface, especially one forming part of a wall or door

Examples

A door pane
Each pane in the wall was painted a different color to brighten the hallway.
Please replace the damaged pane before installing the new lock.
The wooden pane in the old door had been carefully carved by hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel - a distinct section or piece set into a surface, such as in a door or wall
