hemacytometer

noun
cell counting device
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməsaɪˈtɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌhiːməsaɪˈtɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device (usually a special glass slide with a grid) used under a microscope to count blood cells or other microscopic particles in a liquid sample
