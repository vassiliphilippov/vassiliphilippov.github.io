weatherproofing

noun
protection from weather
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or materials used to protect something from damage caused by weather conditions such as rain, wind, or sun

Examples

Weatherproofing the roof
The contractor recommended weatherproofing the roof to prevent leaks during heavy storms.
Good weatherproofing can help lower your energy bills by keeping your home insulated.
They applied new weatherproofing to the windows before winter arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulation - material used to prevent heat, sound, or electricity from escaping
  • synonym: waterproofing - the process of making something resistant to water
