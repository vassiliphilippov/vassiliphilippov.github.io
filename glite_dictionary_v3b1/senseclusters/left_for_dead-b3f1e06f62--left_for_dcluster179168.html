left for dead

adjective
abandoned as dead in danger
Pronunciation: / lɛft fər dɛd / (AmE), / lɛft fə dɛd / (BrE)
abandoned or presumed dead in a dangerous or life-threatening situation, especially without help or rescue

Examples

Left for dead after shipwreck
After the shipwreck, several survivors were left for dead by the rescuers.
He was badly injured in the attack and left for dead on the roadside.
The explorers were left for dead when help failed to arrive during the blizzard.
