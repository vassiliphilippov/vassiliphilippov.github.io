Malaya

proper noun
region of Malaysia on Malay Peninsula
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪə / (AmE), / məˈleɪə / (BrE)
the part of Malaysia located on the Malay Peninsula, historically known as Malaya and sharing a northern border with Thailand
