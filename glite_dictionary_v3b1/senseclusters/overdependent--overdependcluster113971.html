overdependent

adjective
excessively reliant
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərdɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvədɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
relying too much on someone or something for support, help, or guidance

Examples

Overdependent on others
Some children become overdependent on their teachers for answers instead of thinking for themselves.
The company grew overdependent on a single supplier, which caused problems when deliveries were delayed.
She realized she was overdependent on her friends for emotional support.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dependent - needing someone or something for support
  • antonym: independent - not needing help or support from others
