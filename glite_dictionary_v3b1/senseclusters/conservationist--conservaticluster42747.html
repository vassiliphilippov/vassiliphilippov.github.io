conservationist

noun
person who protects the environment
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates or works to protect the environment from destruction, pollution, or excessive exploitation

Examples

A conservationist protects nature
As a conservationist, he believes everyone should take action to reduce pollution.
Local conservationists protested the plans to build a highway through the forest.
The conservationist devoted her life to preserving endangered species and habitats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environmentalist - person concerned with protecting the environment
  • antonym: polluter - person or company that pollutes the environment
