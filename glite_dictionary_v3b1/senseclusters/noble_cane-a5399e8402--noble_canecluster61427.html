noble cane

noun
highly developed variety of sugarcane
9.6 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈnoʊ.bəl keɪn/ (AmE), /ˈnəʊ.bəl keɪn/ (BrE)
A type of sugarcane known for its highly developed qualities, characterized by large, juicy stalks, soft rinds, and high sugar content.
