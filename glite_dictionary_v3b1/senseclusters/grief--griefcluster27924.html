grief

verb
harass/sabotage players in online games
Pronunciation: / ɡriːf / (AmE), / ɡriːf / (BrE)
(informal, gaming) to deliberately annoy, harass, or sabotage other players in an online game, often by breaking rules or behaving disruptively.

Examples

To grief other players
During the match, strangers began to grief new players by stealing all their supplies.
He was banned after he griefed teammates and repeatedly ruined game objectives.
After the update, clans coordinated griefing tactics to force opponents to quit matches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harass - to subject someone to aggressive pressure or intimidation
  • synonym: troll - to provoke or upset others online, often for attention or amusement
