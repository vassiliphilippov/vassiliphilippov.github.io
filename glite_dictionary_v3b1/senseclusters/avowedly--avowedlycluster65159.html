avowedly

adverb
openly or admittedly
Pronunciation: / əˈvaʊ.ɪd.li / (AmE), / əˈvaʊ.ɪd.li / (BrE)
in an open or admitted way; as stated or acknowledged openly

Examples

He is avowedly opposed
He is avowedly opposed to the new policy, making his position clear to everyone.
She avowedly supports environmental causes and often speaks at related events.
The publication was avowedly independent, operating without political influence.
He is avowedly committed to environmental causes.
The group is avowedly against any form of censorship.
The magazine has always been avowedly apolitical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admittedly - as acknowledged, confessing a point
  • synonym: openly - in an open manner; without concealment
  • antonym: secretly - in a secret or covert manner
