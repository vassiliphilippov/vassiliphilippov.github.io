glandulae cervicales uteri

noun
glands in the uterine cervix
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡlænˈdjuːli ˌsɜːrˈvaɪkəliːz juːˈtɪraɪ / (AmE), / ɡlanˈdjuːliː ˌsɜːvɪˈkaːliːz juːˈtɪraɪ / (BrE)
mucus-secreting glands located in the mucous membrane of the uterine cervix.

Examples

Glandulae cervicales uteri mucus glands

The glandulae cervicales uteri play an essential role in maintaining the cervical mucus.
