democratization

noun
making accessible to all
5.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˌmɑːkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something accessible or available to everyone, often by removing barriers or restrictions

Examples

Democratization of technology
The democratization of technology has allowed more people to access powerful tools once reserved for experts.
Social media platforms have contributed to the democratization of information worldwide.
Efforts toward the democratization of education aim to make learning opportunities available to everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberalization - the process of making something less restricted or more open
  • antonym: restriction - the act of limiting or controlling something
