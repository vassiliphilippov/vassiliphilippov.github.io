leadoff

adjective
first in sequence
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈliːdˌɒf / (BrE)
coming first in a sequence, especially as the initial participant or item in an event or activity

Examples

Leadoff position

The leadoff speaker set the tone for the entire conference.
Their leadoff product quickly became a bestseller after its launch.
