hairstyle

noun
the arrangement or style of hair
1.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /ˈher.staɪl/ (AmE), /ˈheə.staɪl/ (BrE)
The way in which a person's hair is cut and arranged, especially as a distinctive or fashionable style.

Examples

A new hairstyle
After years of long hair, he decided to try a short hairstyle.
She changed her hairstyle before the big event to something more sophisticated.
The trendy new hairstyle quickly became popular among teenagers.
