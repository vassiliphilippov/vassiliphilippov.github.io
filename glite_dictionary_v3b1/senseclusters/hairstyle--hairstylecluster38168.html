The way in which a person's hair is cut and arranged, especially as a distinctive or fashionable style.

Examples

A new hairstyle
After years of long hair, he decided to try a short hairstyle.
She changed her hairstyle before the big event to something more sophisticated.
The trendy new hairstyle quickly became popular among teenagers.

Synonyms

  • hairdo
    2.9 1.86/m
    hairdo is an often fancy, arranged look people choose for events, while hairstyle is the general way hair is cut and styled.
  • haircut
    1.0 3.90/m
    haircut is the result of cutting hair at a barber or salon, while hairstyle focuses on the overall cut and arranged look.
  • coiffure
    8.1 0.62/m
    coiffure is a formal word for an elegant hair arrangement made by a hairdresser, while hairstyle is the common term for hair shape.
