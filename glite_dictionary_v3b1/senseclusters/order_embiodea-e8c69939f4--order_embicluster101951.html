order Embiodea

noun
an order of insects known as web spinners
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrdər ˌɛmbiˈoʊdiə / (AmE), / ˌɔːdə ˌɛmbɪˈəʊdiə / (BrE)
a small order of insects, commonly known as web spinners, characterized by their ability to spin silk to form tunnels or galleries.
