sob story

noun
story meant to gain sympathy
1.0 4.0
Pronunciation: /sɑːb ˈstɔː.ri/ /; / /sɑːb ˈstoʊ.ri/ (AmE), /sɒb ˈstɔː.ri/ /; / /sɒb ˈstəʊ.ri/ (BrE)
a story or account, often exaggerated, designed to arouse sympathy by focusing on personal distress or hardship

Examples

A sob story
After hearing his sob story, many people donated to his cause.
He always has a sob story ready when he's late with his rent.
The journalist dismissed her account as just another sob story designed to attract viewers.
