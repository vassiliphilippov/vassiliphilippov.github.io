stewing

noun
a state of worry or agitation
2.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstuː.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈstjuː.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈstjuː.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈstuː.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a state of intense worry or agitation, especially when someone is anxiously thinking about something for a prolonged period

Examples

A night of stewing
A night of stewing left him exhausted, but no closer to resolving his concerns.
After the argument, her stewing kept her busy for hours, replaying every word in her mind.
His constant stewing over the missed opportunity made it hard to focus on anything else.
