attack submarine

noun
military submarine for attacking enemy ships
1.5
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk səˌbməːrin / (AmE), / əˈtæk səˌbmɑːriːn / (BrE)
a military submarine equipped and designed to seek out and destroy enemy vessels, especially other submarines and warships

Examples

An attack submarine
An attack submarine is designed to hunt enemy vessels and protect a nation's fleet.
During the exercise, the attack submarine successfully tracked and shadowed a hostile warship.
The navy recently commissioned a new attack submarine to strengthen its underwater capabilities.
