Furcraea

noun
fiber for rope
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɜːrˈkriːə / (AmE), / fɜːˈkriːə / (BrE)
a strong natural fiber obtained from the leaves of plants of the genus Furcraea, used for making rope, twine, and coarse textiles

Examples

Furcraea fiber rope

Rope made from Furcraea is valued for its strength and resistance to saltwater.
Farmers harvested Furcraea fibers to sell at the local market for textile production.
