everglade

noun
confusing situation
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈev.ər.gleɪd/ (AmE), /ˈev.ə.gleɪd/ (BrE)
a complicated, confusing, or entangling situation that is difficult to escape or resolve (figurative use).

Examples

Trying to fix the old system became an everglade of technical problems and unexpected setbacks.
