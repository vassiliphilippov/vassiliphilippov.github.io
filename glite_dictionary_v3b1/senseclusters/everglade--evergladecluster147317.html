everglade

noun
confusing situation
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌɡleɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡleɪd / (BrE)
a complicated, confusing, or entangling situation that is difficult to escape or resolve (figurative use).

Examples

An everglade of problems
The legal process turned into an everglade of forms and procedures that confused everyone involved.
She found herself lost in an everglade of conflicting advice and unclear instructions.
Trying to fix the old system became an everglade of technical problems and unexpected setbacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: morass - a complicated or confusing situation
  • synonym: quagmire - a difficult or precarious situation
