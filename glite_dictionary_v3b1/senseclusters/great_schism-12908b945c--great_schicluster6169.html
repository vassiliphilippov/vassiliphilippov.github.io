Great Schism

noun
division in the Roman Catholic Church, 1378–1417
9.9
Pronunciation: /greɪt ˈskɪz.əm/ /; / /greɪt ˈsɪz.əm/ /; / /greɪt ˈʃɪz.əm/ (AmE), /greɪt ˈskɪz.əm/ /; / /greɪt ˈsɪz.əm/ /; / /greɪt ˈʃɪz.əm/ (BrE)
