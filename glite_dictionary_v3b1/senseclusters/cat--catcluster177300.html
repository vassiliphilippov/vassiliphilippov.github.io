cat

noun
a whip with nine knotted cords
7.8
Pronunciation: / kæt / (AmE), / kæt / (BrE)
a whip made of nine knotted cords, used especially for punishing offenders at sea or in the military (short for 'cat-o'-nine-tails')

Examples

The cat whip
Old naval records describe how the cat was dreaded by all aboard ship.
The captain ordered the use of the cat to punish the mutineer.
The deck was silent as the cat was brought out for punishment.
