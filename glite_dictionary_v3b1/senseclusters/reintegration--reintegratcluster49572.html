reintegration

noun
joining body part
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɪntəˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which a repaired, reattached, or implanted body part becomes functionally joined to the body again

Examples

Reintegration of a limb
The doctors were pleased with the reintegration of the transplanted kidney after the operation.
Successful reintegration of the severed finger was confirmed by restored movement and sensation.
Careful monitoring is required to ensure proper reintegration of implanted tissues in the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integration - the process of combining parts into a whole
  • antonym: rejection - the failure of a body to accept a transplanted organ or tissue
