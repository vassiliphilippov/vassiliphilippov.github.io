goon

noun
an aggressive young criminal or thug
Pronunciation: / ɡun / (AmE), / ɡuːn / (BrE)
a violent or aggressive young criminal, often used to refer to a hired thug or gangster

Examples

A goon attacks
Police arrested a goon involved in last night's robbery.
That part of the city is run by goons working for various gangs.
The goon threatened anyone who dared to cross his boss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gangster - a member of an organized group of violent criminals
  • synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
