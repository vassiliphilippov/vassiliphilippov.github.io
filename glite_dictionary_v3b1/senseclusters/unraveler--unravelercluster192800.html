unraveler

noun
person who untangles or solves problems
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrævələr / (AmE), / ʌnˈravələ / (BrE)
a person who untangles something or solves a complicated situation

Examples

The unraveler solves problems

She became the chief unraveller of the family conflicts, always smoothing things over.
