plastic

adjective
capable of being easily shaped or molded
8.5 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance) capable of being easily shaped or molded, especially by physical force.

Examples

Plastic clay
Clay is highly plastic when wet but hardens as it dries.
Some types of wax become plastic when warmed and can be molded into different forms.
The sculptor chose a plastic material for her latest statue because it could be easily shaped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malleable - capable of being shaped or bent
  • antonym: rigid - not flexible or easily shaped
