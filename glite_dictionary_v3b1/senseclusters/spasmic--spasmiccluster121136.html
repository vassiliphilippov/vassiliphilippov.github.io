spasmic

adjective
sudden and irregular
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˈspæzmɪk / (BrE)
occurring in sudden, brief, or irregular bursts or spasms

Examples

A spasmic pain
He experienced spasmic pain in his lower back after lifting the heavy box.
The patient's spasmic movements made it difficult for the doctor to examine him.
Sudden, spasmic coughing fits interrupted her speech several times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spasmodic - happening in sudden, short bursts
  • antonym: steady - regular and continuous, not changing suddenly
