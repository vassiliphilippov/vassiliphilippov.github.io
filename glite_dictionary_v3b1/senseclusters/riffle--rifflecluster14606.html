riffle

verb
shuffle cards by interleaving them
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪfəl / (BrE)
To shuffle (playing cards) by dividing the deck into two parts and interleaving them together, usually using the thumbs.

Examples

To riffle the deck
After several rounds, Mike could riffle the cards almost without looking.
He expertly riffled the deck before dealing the cards.
She asked her friend to riffle the cards for a fair game.
