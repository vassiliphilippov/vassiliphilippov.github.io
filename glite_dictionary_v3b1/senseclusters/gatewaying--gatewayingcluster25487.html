gatewaying

noun
network connection process
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgeɪt.weɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgeɪt.weɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgeɪt.weɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgeɪt.weɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of connecting different computer networks or systems through a gateway to enable communication

Examples

Network gatewaying
The IT department implemented gatewaying to link the company's internal and external networks.
Proper gatewaying ensures secure and efficient data transfer between different systems.
Without gatewaying, the two networks would not be able to communicate with each other.
