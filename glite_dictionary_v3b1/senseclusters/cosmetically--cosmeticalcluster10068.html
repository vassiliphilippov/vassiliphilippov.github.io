cosmetically

adverb
in a manner to improve appearance
8.3 4.3
Pronunciation: /kɔːz.ˈme.tɪ.kə.liː/ /; / /kɑːz.ˈme.tɪ.kə.liː/ (AmE), /kɒz.ˈme.tɪ.kə.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that relates to improving or altering the appearance, especially for beauty rather than for functional reasons.

Examples

Used cosmetically to improve
Many products are used cosmetically to enhance one's looks.
The scar was treated cosmetically to minimize its appearance.
While the car was damaged, it was repaired cosmetically with new paint and trim.
