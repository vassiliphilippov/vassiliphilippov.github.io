impudently

adverb
in an impertinent or disrespectful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpjədəntli / (AmE), / ˈɪmpjʊdəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of respect or shameless boldness; in an impertinent manner

Examples

To impudently refuse
He impudently refused to apologize for his rude comments.
She impudently questioned the manager's decision in front of everyone.
The child impudently interrupted the teacher during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impertinently - in an impertinent or insolent manner
  • antonym: respectfully - in a manner showing respect or deference
