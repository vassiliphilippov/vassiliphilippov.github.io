bronchoscope

noun
instrument for examining bronchial tubes
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑŋ.kəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋ.kəˌskəʊp / (BrE)
a slender, tubular instrument used to examine the inside of the bronchial tubes

Examples

A bronchoscope tool
A flexible bronchoscope allows for easier navigation through the bronchial passages.
Sterilizing the bronchoscope after each procedure is essential to prevent infection.
The doctor used a bronchoscope to inspect the patient's airways for blockages.
