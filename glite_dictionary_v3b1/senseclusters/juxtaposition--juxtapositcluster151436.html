juxtaposition

noun
placing things side by side for comparison
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /dʒʌk.stə.pə.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dʒʌk.stə.pə.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
