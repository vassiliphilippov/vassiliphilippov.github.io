bursting explosive

noun
high explosive used to damage a target
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrstɪŋ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv / (AmE), / ˈbɜːstɪŋ ɪkˈspləʊsɪv / (BrE)
