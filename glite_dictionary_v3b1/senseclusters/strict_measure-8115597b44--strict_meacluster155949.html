strict measure

noun
rigid rule or action to enforce control
4.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /strɪkt ˈmeʒ.ər/ (AmE), /strɪkt ˈmeʒ.ə/ (BrE)
a rule or action that is enforced very rigidly and allows little or no flexibility, often to maintain order or safety

Examples

Strict measures in place
Due to the security threat, the organizers put strict measures in place at the event.
Some employees complained that the company’s strict measures limited their flexibility at work.
The government enforced strict measures to control the spread of disease.
