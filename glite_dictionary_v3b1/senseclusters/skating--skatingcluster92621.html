skating

noun
the sport of moving on skates
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskeɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of moving smoothly and swiftly over a surface on skates

Examples

Figure skating
Figure skating requires both athletic skill and artistic expression.
He spent his Saturday morning skating at the local rink with friends.
Many people enjoy skating outdoors when the lakes freeze in winter.
