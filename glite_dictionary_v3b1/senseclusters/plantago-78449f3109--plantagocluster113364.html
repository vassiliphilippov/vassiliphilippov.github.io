Plantago

proper noun
genus of herbaceous plants (plantains)
8.4
Pronunciation: / plænˈteɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / planˈtɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of small, mostly herbaceous plants in the family Plantaginaceae, found worldwide and commonly known as plantains.

Examples

Plantago major plant
Common plantain, or Plantago major, can often be found growing alongside roads and in lawns.
Several species in the genus Plantago are used in herbal medicine for their soothing properties.
The botanist collected samples of Plantago from different regions for her study.
