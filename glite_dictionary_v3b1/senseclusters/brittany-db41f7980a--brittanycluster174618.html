Brittany

proper noun
former province in northwestern France
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪt̬əni / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtəni / (BrE)
a former province in northwestern France, located on a peninsula between the English Channel and the Bay of Biscay, known for its distinct cultural identity and Celtic heritage.
