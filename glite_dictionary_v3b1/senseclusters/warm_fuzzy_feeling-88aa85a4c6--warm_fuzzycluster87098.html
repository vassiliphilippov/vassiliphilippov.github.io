warm fuzzy feeling

noun
a feeling of comfort or happiness
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ˈfʌzi ˈfilɪŋ / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈfʌzi ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a feeling of comfort, happiness, or affectionate contentment; an emotional sense of warmth and well-being often elicited by kindness or something endearing

Examples

A warm fuzzy feeling
Donating to charity always leaves me with a warm fuzzy feeling inside.
His kind words at the meeting produced a warm fuzzy feeling among his colleagues.
Seeing the children play together gave her a warm fuzzy feeling she hadn't experienced in years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contentment - a state of being happy and satisfied
  • synonym: delight - great pleasure or satisfaction
