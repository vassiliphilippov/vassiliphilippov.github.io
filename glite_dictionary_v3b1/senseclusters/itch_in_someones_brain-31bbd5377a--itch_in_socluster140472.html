itch in someone's brain

noun
persistent, nagging thought
5.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /ɪtʃ ɪn ˈsʌm.ənz breɪn/ /; / /ɪtʃ ɪn ˈsʌm.wʌnz breɪn/ (AmE), /ɪtʃ ɪn ˈsʌm.ənz breɪn/ /; / /ɪtʃ ɪn ˈsʌm.wʌnz breɪn/ (BrE)
