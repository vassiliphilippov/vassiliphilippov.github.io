itch in someone's brain

noun
persistent, nagging thought
5.9
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz breɪn / (AmE), / ɪtʃ ɪn ˈsʌmwənz breɪn / (BrE)
a persistent thought or idea that continually occupies one's mind and is difficult to ignore or forget

Examples

An itch in my brain
Ever since I heard that melody, it has been like an itch in my brain that won’t go away.
She couldn’t shake the itch in her brain about whether she locked the door.
That mysterious riddle became an itch in his brain, distracting him all day.
