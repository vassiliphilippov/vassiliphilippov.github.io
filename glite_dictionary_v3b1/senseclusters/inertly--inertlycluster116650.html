Synonyms

  • inactively
    3.9 0.01/m
    inactively is used when someone or something shows little activity or movement, while inertly stresses a lack of movement and response.
  • motionlessly
    4.0 0.09/m
    motionlessly describes being completely still without moving, while inertly adds lack of energy or any response to sound or touch.
  • passively
    3.7 2.49/m
    passively means accepting or not acting in a situation, while inertly suggests being motionless and not reacting at all.
