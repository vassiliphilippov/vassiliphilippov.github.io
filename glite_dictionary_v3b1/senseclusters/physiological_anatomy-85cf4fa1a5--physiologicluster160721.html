physiological anatomy

noun
anatomy relating structure to function
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪkəl əˈnætəmi / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkəl əˈnætəmi / (BrE)
the branch of anatomy that studies body structure in relation to its function

Examples

Study of physiological anatomy
Her thesis explored comparative physiological anatomy in marine mammals.
Modern research in physiological anatomy has advanced our understanding of organ function.
The course on physiological anatomy bridges the gap between structure and biology.
