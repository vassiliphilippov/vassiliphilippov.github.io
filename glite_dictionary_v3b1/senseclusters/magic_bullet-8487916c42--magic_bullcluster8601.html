magic bullet

noun
a simple solution to a complex problem
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪk ˈbʊlɪt / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
a simple and highly effective solution to a difficult or complex problem, especially one that is hoped for but unlikely to exist

Examples

A magic bullet solution
Many experts warn there is no magic bullet for improving education systems.
The company is still searching for a magic bullet to boost productivity across all departments.
Unfortunately, the new policy wasn't the magic bullet that everyone had hoped it would be.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panacea - a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases
