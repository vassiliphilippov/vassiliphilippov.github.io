coralwood

noun
East Indian ornamental tree
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəlˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəlˌwʊd / (BrE)
An East Indian tree with racemes of yellow-white flowers, cultivated as an ornamental.
