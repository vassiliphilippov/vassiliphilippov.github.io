car battery

noun
high‑capacity EV battery pack
3.4
Pronunciation: /kɑːr ˈbæt.ər.ɪ/ (AmE), /kɑː ˈbæt.ə.rɪ/ (BrE)
(in electric vehicles) a high‑capacity rechargeable battery pack, typically lithium‑ion, that supplies power to the vehicle's electric drivetrain and determines driving range.

Examples

Electric car battery
Electric vehicles rely on a high-capacity car battery for propulsion and driving range.
The manufacturer offers an eight-year warranty on the car battery and its capacity retention.
Replacing the heavy car battery improved acceleration but added significant weight to the vehicle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battery pack - an assembly of individual cells combined to form a larger rechargeable unit used in vehicles or devices
  • synonym: traction battery - a battery designed to provide energy for an electric vehicle's propulsion system
