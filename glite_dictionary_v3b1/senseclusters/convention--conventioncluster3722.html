convention

noun
large formal meeting or assembly
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛn.ʃən /; / kənˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / kənˈvɛn.ʃən /; / kənˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
a large meeting or formal assembly, especially for a particular profession, group, or activity

Examples

Annual convention
Most major comic artists will attend the annual convention in San Diego this summer.
She spoke at a medical convention where leading doctors gathered from around the world.
The political parties held their convention to nominate their presidential candidates.
After the formal convention, the members discussed plans for the upcoming term.
Proper procedures are followed during the convention of an international conference.
The convention of the committee took place every year in January.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion, often academic or professional
  • antonym: dispersal - the process of distributing things or people over a wide area
