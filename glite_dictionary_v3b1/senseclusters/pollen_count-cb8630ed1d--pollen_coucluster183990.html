pollen count

noun
measurement of airborne pollen concentration
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlən kaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈpɒlən kaʊnt / (BrE)
the measurement of the number of pollen grains present in a specified volume of air over a set period, typically used to indicate allergy risk

Examples

Daily pollen count
Many news channels provide a daily pollen count during the spring and summer months.
She checked the pollen count before deciding whether to spend the day outdoors.
The pollen count is especially high today, so allergy sufferers should take precautions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allergen - a substance that causes an allergic reaction
