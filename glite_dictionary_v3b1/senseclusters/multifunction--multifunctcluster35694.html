multifunction

noun
device with many uses
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
a device or tool designed to perform several different functions or tasks

Examples

A multifunction tool
The company introduced a new multifunction kitchen appliance that can blend, chop, and juice.
A multifunction tool is useful for camping because it saves space and weight.
He prefers using a multifunction printer that can handle scanning and faxing as well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all-in-one - a device that combines multiple functions in one unit
  • antonym: single-function - a device designed to perform only one specific task
