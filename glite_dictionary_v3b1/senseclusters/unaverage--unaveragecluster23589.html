unaverage

verb
to reverse averaging
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ʌnˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
to reverse the process of averaging or to separate values that have been combined into an average

Examples

To unaverage data
Once the numbers are merged, it is hard to unaverage them to see the original values.
The software cannot unaverage the results after the data has been processed.
Researchers tried to unaverage the statistics to analyze each group separately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combine - to join or merge together
  • synonym: separate - to divide into individual parts
