stationary

adjective
not moving; standing still
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪʃəˌneri / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃənəri / (BrE)
not moving or fixed in one place; standing still

Examples

A stationary object
During the storm, the boat stayed stationary to avoid accidents.
Please remain stationary until the ride comes to a complete stop.
The train was stationary at the platform for several minutes.
During the experiment, the platform remained completely stationary despite the wind.
The car collided with a stationary object on the side of the road.
The heavy safe is stationary and cannot be pushed without special equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immobile - not moving; unable to move
  • synonym: motionless - not moving; without motion
  • antonym: moving - in motion; not stationary
  • synonym: static - lacking movement, action, or change
