surgical strike

noun
precise military attack on a specific target
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːdʒɪkəl straɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkəl straɪk / (BrE)
a military attack aimed precisely at a specific target, with the intention of limiting collateral damage

Examples

A surgical strike attack
Intelligence agencies coordinated closely to ensure the surgical strike would avoid civilian casualties.
News outlets reported that a surgical strike destroyed the weapons cache with minimal damage to surrounding areas.
The government authorized a surgical strike to neutralize the enemy's command center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airstrike - an attack by military aircraft, especially on a specific target
  • degree higher: blitz - a sudden military attack
