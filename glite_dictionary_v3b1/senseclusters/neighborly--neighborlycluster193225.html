neighborly

adjective
friendly and helpful to neighbors
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪ.bər.liː/ (AmE), /ˈneɪ.bə.liː/ (BrE)
(of a person or action) friendly, helpful, and considerate toward people living nearby.

Examples

Neighborly help
He believes in maintaining neighborly relations with everyone on his street.
It was neighborly of her to help shovel the snow from our driveway.
Our new neighbors are very neighborly and always greet us with a smile.
Everyone on our street is very neighbourly and always willing to lend a hand.
It's important to be neighbourly, especially when someone is in need.
She made a neighbourly gesture by bringing soup to the new residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant in manner or behavior
  • antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic in attitude
