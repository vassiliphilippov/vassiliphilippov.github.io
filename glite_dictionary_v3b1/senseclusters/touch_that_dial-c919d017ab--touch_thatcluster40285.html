touch that dial

verb
do not change the channel or station
6.1
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ ðæt daɪəl / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ðæt daɪəl / (BrE)
used to tell someone not to change the radio station or television channel, especially before a program resumes

Examples

Don't touch that dial
Before commercials, announcers often said, "Don’t touch that dial!" to keep people from tuning away.
The host reminded listeners to touch that dial as their favorite show would return soon.
We'll be right back with more after this break—so don't touch that dial!
