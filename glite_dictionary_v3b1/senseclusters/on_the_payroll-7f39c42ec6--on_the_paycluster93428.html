on the payroll

adjective
employed and being paid
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə ˈpeɪˌroʊl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈpeɪˌrəʊl / (BrE)
employed and currently receiving a regular salary from an organization or company

Examples

On the payroll list
After three months of probation, she was finally on the payroll and receiving benefits.
He’s not officially on the payroll, but he does freelance work for them regularly.
The company has over two hundred staff members on the payroll this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: employed - having a paid job
  • antonym: off the payroll - no longer employed or being paid by an organization
