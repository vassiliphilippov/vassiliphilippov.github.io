western spadefoot

noun
species of spadefoot toad native to California
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn ˈspeɪdˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈspeɪdfʊt / (BrE)
a species of spadefoot toad (Spea hammondii) native to California, known for its burrowing habits and distinctive spade-like projections on its feet

The western spadefoot toad
