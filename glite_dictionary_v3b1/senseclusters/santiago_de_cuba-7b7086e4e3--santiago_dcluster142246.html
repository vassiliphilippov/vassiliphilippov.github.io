Santiago de Cuba

proper noun
1898 naval battle in the Spanish-American War
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsæn.tiˈɑːɡoʊ də ˈkuːbə / (AmE), / ˌsan.tiˈɑː.ɡəʊ də ˈkuː.bə / (BrE)
a major naval battle of the Spanish-American War in 1898, where the United States fleet trapped and destroyed the Spanish fleet in the harbor of Santiago de Cuba.
