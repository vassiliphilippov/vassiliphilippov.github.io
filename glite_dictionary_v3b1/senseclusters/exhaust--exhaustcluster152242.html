exhaust

noun
waste gases from an engine
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔst / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst / (BrE)
the waste gases released from an engine, especially a car engine, after fuel has been burned

Examples

Exhaust fumes
Modern vehicles are designed to minimize harmful exhaust emissions.
The mechanic checked the color of the exhaust coming from the car's tailpipe.
When the exhaust started to smell unusual, she took her car to the garage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emission - the act or process of releasing substances such as gases
