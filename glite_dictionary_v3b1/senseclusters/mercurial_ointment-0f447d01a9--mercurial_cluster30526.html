mercurial ointment

noun
ointment containing mercury
8.6
Pronunciation: / mərˈkjʊriəl ˈɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / məˈkjʊəriəl ˈɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
an ointment containing mercury, formerly used to treat skin diseases such as syphilis and lice infestations

Examples

Mercurial ointment for skin
Due to mercury's toxicity, modern medicine no longer recommends mercurial ointment.
In the nineteenth century, mercurial ointment was commonly used to treat syphilis.
The doctor prescribed mercurial ointment for his skin condition, following the traditional treatment.
