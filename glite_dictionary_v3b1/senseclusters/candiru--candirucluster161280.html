candiru

noun
Amazon parasitic catfish
Pronunciation: / ˌkændiˈruː / (AmE), / ˌkændɪˈruː / (BrE)
a small parasitic catfish from the Amazon River, known for entering the body orifices of animals and humans

Examples

The candiru fish
The candiru is feared by swimmers in the Amazon for its parasitic behavior.
Researchers study the candiru to understand how it locates its hosts in murky waters.
Local guides warn tourists about the dangers of the candiru in certain parts of the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catfish - a type of freshwater fish with whisker-like barbels
  • synonym: parasite - an organism that lives on or in another and benefits at its host's expense
