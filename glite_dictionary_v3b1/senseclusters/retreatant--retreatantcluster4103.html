retreatant

noun
participant in a religious retreat
8.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtriːtənt / (AmE), / rɪˈtriːtənt / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a religious retreat, often for spiritual reflection or renewal

Examples

A retreatant in prayer
Each retreatant is encouraged to reflect on their life's journey during the program.
Meals were taken together, but the retreatant maintained silence throughout.
The retreatant spent three days in silent meditation at the abbey.
