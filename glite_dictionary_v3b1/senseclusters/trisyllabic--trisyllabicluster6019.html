trisyllabic

noun
three-syllable word
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtraɪsɪˈlæbɪk / (AmE), / ˌtraɪsɪˈlæbɪk / (BrE)
a word that has three syllables

Examples

A trisyllabic in speech
The teacher asked the class to find a trisyllabic in the reading passage.
Can you think of a trisyllabic that rhymes with 'banana'?
Many English adjectives, like 'beautiful', are trisyllabic.
