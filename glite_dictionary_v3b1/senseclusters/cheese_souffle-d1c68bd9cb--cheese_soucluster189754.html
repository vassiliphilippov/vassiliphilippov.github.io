cheese souffle

noun
light, puffy baked cheese dish
4.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /tʃiːz suː.ˈfleɪ/ /; / /tʃiːz ˈsuː.fleɪ/ (AmE), /tʃiːz ˈsuː.fleɪ/ /; / /tʃiːz suː.ˈfleɪ/ (BrE)
