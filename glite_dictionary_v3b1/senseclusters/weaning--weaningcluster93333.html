weaning

noun
the introduction of food other than mother's milk
Pronunciation: / ˈwiniŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of gradually introducing an infant or young mammal to food other than its mother's milk

Examples

Early weaning process
Early weaning can have long-term effects on a child's nutrition and development.
She documented the weaning process in her parenting blog.
The weaning of the puppies began when they were about four weeks old.
