unevenly

adverb
in an unequal or irregular way
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈivənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvənli / (BrE)
in a way that is not equal, regular, or smooth; irregularly or inconsistently

Examples

The paint was applied unevenly, leaving some patches darker than others.
The teachers distributed the supplies unevenly among the students, causing complaints.
Her efforts were distributed unevenly across the different projects.
The cake rose unevenly in the oven, making one side higher than the other.
The paint was applied unevenly, leaving streaks all over the wall.
After the earthquake, the ground had shifted unevenly under the houses.
He breathed unevenly as he ran up the steep hill.
The paint was applied unevenly, leaving streaks across the wall.
