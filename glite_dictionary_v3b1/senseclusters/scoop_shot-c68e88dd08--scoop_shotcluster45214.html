scoop shot

noun
basketball shot with underhand scooping motion
1.8 4.0
Pronunciation: / skuːp ʃɑt / (AmE), / skuːp ʃɒt / (BrE)
(in basketball) a shot made with an underhand scooping motion, often used to shoot over defenders at close range.

Examples

A scoop shot in basketball
A perfectly timed scoop shot can catch the defense off guard.
She practiced her scoop shot to get around taller opponents in the paint.
The guard drove to the basket and finished with a clever scoop shot over the defender.
