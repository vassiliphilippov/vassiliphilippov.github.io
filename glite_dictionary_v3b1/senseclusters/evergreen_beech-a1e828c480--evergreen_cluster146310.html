evergreen beech

noun
tree with small evergreen leaves, genus Nothofagus
7.5 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈev.ər.griːn biːtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈev.ə.griːn biːtʃ/ (BrE)
any of several trees of the Southern Hemisphere belonging to the genus Nothofagus, having small, usually evergreen leaves, and resembling true beeches.

Examples

The evergreen beech tree
Gardeners admire the glossy foliage of the evergreen beech in mild climates.
The evergreen beech is native to temperate forests of South America and Australasia.
Unlike the deciduous European beech, the evergreen beech retains its leaves all year round.
