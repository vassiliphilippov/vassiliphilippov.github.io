bobsleigh

noun
long racing sled for 2 or more people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbz.leɪ /; / ˈbɑb.sleɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒb.sleɪ / (BrE)
a long, narrow sled for two or more people, used for racing down an ice track in winter sports

Examples

A bobsleigh race
Four athletes carefully climbed into the bobsleigh before the race began.
The bobsleigh hurtled down the icy track at incredible speed.
The Jamaican team famously competed in the bobsleigh event at the Winter Olympics.
After the bobsleigh event, the athletes gathered to celebrate their victory.
In its early days, the bobsleigh consisted of two smaller sleds joined together.
The four-man bobsleigh team prepared for their race at the top of the icy track.
