fawnlike

adjective
gentle or innocent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a gentle, timid, or innocent quality, resembling a young deer

Examples

Fawnlike eyes
The child gave her a fawnlike look, full of trust and curiosity.
Her fawnlike manner made everyone want to protect her.
He noticed her fawnlike eyes as she entered the quiet room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bold - showing courage or confidence
  • synonym: innocent - pure and lacking experience or guilt
  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence
