unobserved

adjective
not noticed or seen
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈzɜrvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
not seen, noticed, or watched by anyone; happening without being detected

Examples

Go unobserved
Many important details went unobserved during the hurried inspection.
The animal lived its life mostly unobserved by humans in the dense forest.
The thief managed to escape unobserved through a side door.
