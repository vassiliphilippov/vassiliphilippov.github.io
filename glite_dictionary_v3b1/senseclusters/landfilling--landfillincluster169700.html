landfilling

noun
land creation process
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlændˌfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of creating or raising land by depositing large amounts of earth, rock, or other fill material

Examples

Landfilling the bay
The city expanded its waterfront by extensive landfilling along the bay.
Environmental regulations now require careful planning before any landfilling can take place.
The airport was built on new ground created through landfilling over marshland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthworks - large-scale movement or shaping of earth for construction or engineering
  • synonym: land reclamation - the process of making land usable by filling or draining
