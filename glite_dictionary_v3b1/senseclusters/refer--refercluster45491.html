refer

verb
to send for help or information
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfɜr / (AmE), / rɪˈfɜː / (BrE)
to send or direct someone or something to another person or place for help, information, or a decision

Examples

To refer a patient
If the issue cannot be resolved internally, we may need to refer it to upper management.
The doctor decided to refer the patient to a cardiologist for further tests.
The teacher will refer students with learning difficulties to a specialist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transfer - to move from one place or person to another
