cup of tea

noun
something one likes or is good at
Pronunciation: / kʌp əv tiː /; / kʌp əv ti / (AmE), / kʌp əv tiː / (BrE)
something that you like, enjoy, or are good at; something that suits your tastes or interests (used especially in the phrase 'not my cup of tea')

Examples

Not my cup of tea
Classical music has always been her cup of tea.
Mathematics was never his cup of tea at school.
Traveling alone is not really my cup of tea, but I’ll do it if I have to.
If adventure sports are your cup of tea, you'll love bungee jumping.
Jogging at sunrise isn't really my cup of tea.
Reading science fiction novels has always been my cup of tea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bag - an activity or subject that someone is interested in or good at (slang)
  • synonym: dish - something that someone likes or excels at (chiefly in negative contexts, dated)
  • antonym: not one's cup of tea - something a person does not enjoy or prefer
