ayuntamiento

noun
town hall building
Pronunciation: / aɪˌjuntəˈmɛntoʊ /; / aɪˌjʊn.tɑːnˈteɪ.toʊ /; / aɪˌjʊn.tɑːnˈtɛɪ.toʊ / (AmE), / aɪˌjʊntəˈmɛntəʊ /; / aɪˌjʊn.tæmˈteɪ.təʊ /; / aɪˌjʊn.tæmˈtɛəntəʊ / (BrE)
the local government or town council, or the building where it meets, especially in Spanish-speaking countries
