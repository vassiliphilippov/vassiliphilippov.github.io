Synonyms

  • woof
    9.9 0.1/m
    woof is the yarn woven across the warp in a fabric, while weft is the standard technical name used by weavers.
  • filling
    6.8 0.00/m
    filling is the thread woven across warp yarns to form cloth, while weft is the simple weaving term for that crosswise thread.
  • pick
    9.6 0.00/m
    pick is the yarn inserted across the warp in each pass, while weft names the same crosswise yarn in general weaving.
