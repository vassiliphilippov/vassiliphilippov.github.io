wildcard

noun
unpredictable factor
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a person or thing whose actions or influence are unpredictable and may affect the outcome in unexpected ways

Examples

A wildcard person
The new player was a complete wildcard and surprised everyone with his skills.
In the negotiations, her unpredictable behavior made her a real wildcard.
Weather is always a wildcard that can affect outdoor events unexpectedly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: predictable - able to be foreseen or expected
  • synonym: unknown quantity - a person or thing whose abilities or effects are not yet known
