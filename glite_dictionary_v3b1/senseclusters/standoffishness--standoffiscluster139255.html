standoffishness

noun
quality of being distant or unfriendly
Pronunciation: / ˌstændˈɑːfɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˌstandˈɒfɪʃnəs / (BrE)
a quality of being distant, unfriendly, or reserved in manner

Examples
