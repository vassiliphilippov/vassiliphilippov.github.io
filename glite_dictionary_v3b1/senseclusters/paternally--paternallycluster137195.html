paternally

adverb
in a fatherly manner
3.2
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɝːnəli / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of or relating to a father; in a paternal way.

Examples

To speak paternally
He always spoke paternally to his students, offering advice and support.
She nodded paternally, expressing concern for the younger members of the group.
The manager treated his employees paternally, looking out for their well-being.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maternally - in a maternal manner
  • synonym: paternal - of or relating to a father
