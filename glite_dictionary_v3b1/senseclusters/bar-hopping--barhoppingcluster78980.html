bar-hopping

adjective
visiting many bars
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑr ˌhɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ˌhɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of visiting several bars or pubs in one outing, often for socializing or drinking

Examples

Bar-hopping tour
They organized a bar-hopping tour for tourists interested in the city's nightlife.
The bar-hopping crowd filled the streets on Friday night.
She wore comfortable shoes for their bar-hopping adventure downtown.
