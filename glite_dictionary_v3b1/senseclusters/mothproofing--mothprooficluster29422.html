mothproofing

noun
protection against moths
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːθˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒθˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
a treatment or substance used to protect materials from being damaged by moths

Examples

Mothproofing wool clothes
The tailor recommended mothproofing for all wool garments before storing them for the season.

The company specializes in mothproofing wool carpets for hotels and offices.
Regular mothproofing can help protect your clothes during long-term storage.
Many people choose mothproofing treatments to keep their wardrobes safe from damage.
