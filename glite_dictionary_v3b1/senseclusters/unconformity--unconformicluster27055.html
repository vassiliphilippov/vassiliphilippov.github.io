unconformity

noun
lack of conformity
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈfɔːrməti / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfɔːməti / (BrE)
the state of not following accepted customs, practices, or standards; nonconformity

Examples

Artistic unconformity
Her artistic unconformity challenged the gallery's expectations and attracted critical attention.
The team's unconformity with industry standards led to innovative solutions.
He admired her unconformity in always speaking her mind, even when it was unpopular.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conformity - compliance with accepted rules or standards
  • synonym: nonconformity - refusal to follow established customs or standards
