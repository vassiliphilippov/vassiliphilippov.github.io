touchily

adverb
in an easily offended or irritable manner
8.1 0.01/m

In a manner that shows sensitivity to offense or annoys easily; in an irritable or easily offended way.

Examples

To reply touchily to questions
After the argument, he replied touchily to the simplest of questions.
She touchily dismissed any criticism of her work, unwilling to discuss the matter further.
The author touchily responded to readers who misunderstood her intentions.
