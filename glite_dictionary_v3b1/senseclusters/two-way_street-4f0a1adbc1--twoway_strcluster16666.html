two-way street

noun
street allowing two-way traffic
2.5 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtuː weɪ ˈstriːt / (AmE), / ˌtuː weɪ ˈstriːt / (BrE)
A street on which vehicles are allowed to travel in both directions.

Examples

A two-way street sign
