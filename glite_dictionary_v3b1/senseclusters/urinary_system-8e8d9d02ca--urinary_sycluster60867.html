urinary system

noun
body system for urine production and elimination
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrənɛri ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪnəri ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs in the body responsible for the production, storage, and elimination of urine

Examples

The urinary system organs
Doctors examine the urinary system to diagnose kidney disorders and related conditions.
Proper hydration helps maintain a healthy urinary system.
The urinary system includes the kidneys, ureters, bladder, and urethra.
