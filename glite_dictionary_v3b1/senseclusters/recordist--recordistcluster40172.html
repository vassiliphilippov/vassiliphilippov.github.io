recordist

noun
official transcriber
6.1
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈkɔːr.dɪst/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈkɔː.dɪst/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to make official written or audio records of spoken words, especially during legal or formal proceedings

Examples

Court recordist work
The recordist carefully documented every statement made during the meeting.
As a recordist, she ensures that all legal proceedings are accurately transcribed.
The court relied on the recordist to provide an official transcript of the trial.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies or writes documents by hand
  • synonym: stenographer - a person who transcribes speech using shorthand
