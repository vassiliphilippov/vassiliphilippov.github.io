amaryllis

noun
a flowering bulbous plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈrɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌaməˈrɪlɪs / (BrE)
a bulbous plant with showy funnel-shaped flowers, typically white, pink, red, or purple, often grown as a houseplant

Examples

A amaryllis bulb
She purchased a pink amaryllis bulb to brighten up her living room.
The amaryllis bloomed beautifully in the kitchen window throughout the winter months.
Their garden features several varieties of amaryllis with large, colorful flowers.
