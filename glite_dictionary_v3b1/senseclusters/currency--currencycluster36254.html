currency

noun
system of money in use
1.2 36/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ùÀê…ônsi / (AmE), / Ààk år…ônsi / (BrE)
The system of money in general use in a particular country, especially in the form of coins and paper notes.

Examples

Foreign currency exchange
Due to inflation, the value of the national currency dropped significantly in recent years.
The country's official currency is the euro, which replaced the previous national money.
Travelers must exchange their dollars for local currency before making purchases.
