Indocin

noun
trade name for anti-inflammatory drug
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndoʊsɪn / (AmE), / ˈɪndəsɪn / (BrE)
Trade name for indomethacin, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to reduce pain and inflammation.
