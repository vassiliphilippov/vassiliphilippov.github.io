unathletic

adjective
lacking athletic ability
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnæθˈlɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnæθˈlɛtɪk / (BrE)
not having the physical qualities, skills, or abilities typical of an athlete

Examples

An unathletic person
Although he was unathletic, he still enjoyed hiking with his friends on weekends.
Her unathletic build made gym class challenging, but she always tried her best.
Despite being unathletic as a child, she later became interested in yoga and fitness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: athletic - physically strong, fit, or active like an athlete
  • synonym: clumsy - moving or doing things in a way that is not smooth or graceful
