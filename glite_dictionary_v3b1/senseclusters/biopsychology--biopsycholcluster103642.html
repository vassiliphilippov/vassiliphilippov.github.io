biopsychology

noun
study of brain and behavior
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊsaɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊsaɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how biological processes, especially those in the brain, influence the mind and behavior

Study of biopsychology

The professor gave a lecture on how hormones influence emotions in his biopsychology class.
