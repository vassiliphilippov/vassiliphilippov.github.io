overexaggerate

verb
to exaggerate too much
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt / (BrE)
to exaggerate something to an excessive or unnecessary degree

Examples

To overexaggerate problems
She tends to overexaggerate the difficulties she faces at work.
The movie overexaggerates the dangers of technology for dramatic effect.
Try not to overexaggerate your symptoms when talking to the doctor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggerate - to make something seem larger, better, or worse than it really is
  • antonym: understate - to describe something as less important than it really is
