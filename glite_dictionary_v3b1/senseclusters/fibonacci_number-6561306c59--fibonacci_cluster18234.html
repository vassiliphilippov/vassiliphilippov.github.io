Fibonacci number

noun
number in the Fibonacci sequence
5.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /fɪ.bə.ˈnɑː.tʃiː ˈnʌm.bər/ (AmE), /fɪ.bə.ˈnɑː.tʃiː ˈnʌm.bə/ /; / /fiː.bəʊ.ˈnɑː.tʃiː ˈnʌm.bə/ (BrE)
