wooing

noun
the act of courting or seeking affection
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwuɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwuːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of courting someone or attempting to gain their affection, often with the intention of marriage

Examples

Persistent wooing
His persistent wooing eventually convinced her to accept his proposal.
Much of traditional poetry centers around the art of wooing and romantic courtship.
The novel describes the wooing of a reluctant heiress by a charming suitor.
