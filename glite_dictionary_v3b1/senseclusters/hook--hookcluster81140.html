hook

noun
basketball shot made in an arc over the head
9.9
Pronunciation: / hʊk / (AmE), / hʊk / (BrE)
(basketball) a shot made by swinging the arm in an arc over the head, typically with the hand farthest from the basket.

Examples

A hook shot
He scored two points with a beautiful hook from the left side of the court.
Kareem Abdul-Jabbar was famous for his unstoppable hook.
She practiced her hook every day to improve her basketball skills.

Related Terms
