gas-turbine ship

noun
ship powered by a gas turbine
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæs ˌtɝː.bin ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡæs ˌtɜː.bɪn ʃɪp / (BrE)
A ship that is powered by a gas turbine engine instead of traditional diesel or steam engines.

Examples

A gas-turbine ship
Due to its powerful engine, the gas-turbine ship can reach higher speeds than traditional vessels.
Engineers are developing environmentally friendly fuels for the next generation of gas-turbine ships.
The navy commissioned a new gas-turbine ship to enhance its fleet's speed and efficiency.
