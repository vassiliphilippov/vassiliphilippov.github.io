exhibit

verb
show or display publicly
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɪbɪt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɪbɪt / (BrE)
to show or display something publicly, such as an object, quality, or feeling

Examples

To exhibit a painting
