sedan chair

noun
enclosed chair carried by bearers
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdæn tʃɛr / (AmE), / sɪˈdæn tʃɛə / (BrE)
a type of enclosed chair for one person, carried on poles by bearers, used especially in the past for transport

Examples

A sedan chair carried
During the eighteenth century, it was common for nobles to travel through the city in a sedan chair.
The wealthy merchant arrived at the party in a luxurious sedan chair carried by four attendants.
Visitors to the museum admired the ornately decorated sedan chair displayed in the hall.
