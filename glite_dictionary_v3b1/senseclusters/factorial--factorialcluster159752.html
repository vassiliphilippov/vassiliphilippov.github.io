factorial

adjective
relating to a factorial
Pronunciation: / fækˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / fækˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving a factorial (the product of all positive integers up to a given number)

Examples

Factorial numbers
Each row in the table corresponds to a factorial value.
The factorial growth of the sequence quickly produces very large numbers.
The algorithm has factorial time complexity, making it inefficient for large inputs.
