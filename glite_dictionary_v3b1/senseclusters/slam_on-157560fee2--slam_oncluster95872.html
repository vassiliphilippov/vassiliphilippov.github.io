slam on

noun
harsh criticism or attack
Pronunciation: / slæm ɑn / (AmE), / slæm ɒn / (BrE)
a severe or harsh criticism or attack (especially in speech or writing) directed at someone or something

Examples

A slam on character
His comments were seen as a slam on her character.
Many took the review as a slam on the entire industry.
The article was a clear slam on the government's policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - a strong or forceful criticism
  • antonym: compliment - an expression of praise or admiration
