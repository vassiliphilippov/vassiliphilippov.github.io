nonbillable

adjective
not charged to client
5.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈbɪləbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈbɪləbəl / (BrE)
not eligible to be charged to a client or customer for payment

Examples

Nonbillable hours
The hours spent on administrative tasks were marked as nonbillable in the report.
Training sessions are usually considered nonbillable time by most consulting firms.
She tracked her nonbillable activities separately from her client work.
