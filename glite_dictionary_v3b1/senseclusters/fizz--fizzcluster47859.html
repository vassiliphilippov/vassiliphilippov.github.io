fizz

noun
bubbles in drinks
1.5
Pronunciation: / fɪz / (AmE), / fɪz / (BrE)
the bubbles and hissing sound produced by a carbonated drink or similar effervescent liquid

Examples

The fizz in soda
The fizz in the soda made it taste refreshing on a hot day.
She opened the bottle and heard the familiar fizz escape into the air.
Without its fizz, the sparkling water tasted flat and dull.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flatness - the state of lacking bubbles or liveliness in a drink
