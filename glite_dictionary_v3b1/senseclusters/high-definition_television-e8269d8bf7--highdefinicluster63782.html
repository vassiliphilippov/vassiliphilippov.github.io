high-definition television

noun
TV system with superior picture quality
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ dɛfəˈnɪʃən ˈtɛləvɪʒ(ə)n / (BrE)
A television system with a higher resolution than standard television, producing pictures with greater detail and clarity.

Examples

High-definition television picture
Many sports fans prefer to watch games on high-definition television for the enhanced clarity and vibrant colors.
Sales of high-definition television sets have increased dramatically in recent years.
The new movie looks stunning when viewed on a high-definition television.
