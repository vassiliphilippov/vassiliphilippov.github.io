wingman

noun
social ally who helps with dating
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋmæn / (BrE)
a person who accompanies and helps a friend in social situations, especially to introduce them to or support them in meeting potential romantic partners.

Examples

Party wingman
At the party, he asked his wingman to start a conversation with the person he liked.
Good friends act as wingmen when you want support approaching someone at a bar.
She thanked her reliable wingman for introducing her to colleagues at the networking event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wingperson - a gender-neutral term for someone who assists a friend in meeting people socially
  • synonym: wingwoman - a woman who accompanies and helps a friend in social situations, especially to meet potential romantic partners
