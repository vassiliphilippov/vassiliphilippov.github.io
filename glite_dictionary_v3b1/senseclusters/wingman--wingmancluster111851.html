wingman

noun
social ally who helps with dating
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪŋ.mən/ /; / /ˈwɪŋ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈwɪŋ.mən/ /; / /ˈwɪŋ.mæn/ (BrE)
a person who accompanies and helps a friend in social situations, especially to introduce them to or support them in meeting potential romantic partners.

Examples

Party wingman

Good friends act as wingmen when you want support approaching someone at a bar.
She thanked her reliable wingman for introducing her to colleagues at the networking event.
