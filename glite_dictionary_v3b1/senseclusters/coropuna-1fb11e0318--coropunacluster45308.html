Coropuna

proper noun
Andean mountain peak in southern Peru
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrəˈpunə / (AmE), / ˌkɒrəˈpuːnə / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes of southern Peru, rising to 21,083 feet (6,425 meters) and known for its volcanic origin and glaciated summit.
