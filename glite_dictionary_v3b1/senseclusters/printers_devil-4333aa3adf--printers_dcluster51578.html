printer's devil

noun
apprentice in a printing shop
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪn.tərz ˌdɛv.əl / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.təz ˌdɛv.əl / (BrE)
An apprentice who assists in the work of a printing establishment, often performing menial tasks.

Examples

A printer's devil apprentice
As a young man, he worked as a printer's devil before becoming a journalist.
Many famous authors began their careers as a printer's devil in their local print shops.
The printer's devil swept the floor and mixed the ink for the typesetters.
