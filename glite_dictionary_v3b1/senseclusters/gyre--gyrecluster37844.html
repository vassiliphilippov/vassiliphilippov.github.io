gyre

verb
to spiral or circle
9.2
Pronunciation: /dʒaɪr/ /; / /ˈdʒaɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdʒaɪ.ə/ (BrE)
to move in a circle or spiral, especially in a whirling or revolving way

Examples

Leaves gyre in wind

Dust began to gyre in the sunlight streaming through the open window.
The dancers gyred gracefully across the stage in perfect circles.
The leaves gyred in the wind before settling on the ground.
In the poem, strange creatures gyre and dance beneath the moonlight.
The dancers gyred across the stage in a mesmerizing pattern.
