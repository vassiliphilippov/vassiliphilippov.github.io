bookmaking

noun
craft of making books
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or business of creating books, including writing, designing, printing, or binding them

Examples

Handmade bookmaking
She took a course in bookmaking to learn how to design and bind her own journals.
Traditional bookmaking requires patience and attention to detail at every stage.
The museum displayed ancient tools used in the art of bookmaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookbinding - the process of physically assembling a book from sheets of paper
  • synonym: publishing - the business of producing and distributing books or other printed material
