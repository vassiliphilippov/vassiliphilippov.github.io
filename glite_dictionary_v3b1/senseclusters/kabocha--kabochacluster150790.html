kabocha

noun
Japanese winter squash
Pronunciation: / kəˈboʊtʃə / (AmE), / kəˈbɒtʃə / (BrE)
a type of Japanese winter squash with green skin and sweet orange flesh, often used in cooking

Examples

Roasted kabocha squash
She made a delicious soup using roasted kabocha and ginger.
At the market, I found a fresh kabocha to use in my curry recipe.
The chef prepared tempura with slices of kabocha for a seasonal appetizer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butternut squash - a type of winter squash with tan skin and sweet orange flesh
  • synonym: pumpkin - a large round orange squash
