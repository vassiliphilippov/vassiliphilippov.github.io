effeminateness

noun
trait of being effeminate
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmənətnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmɪnətnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having characteristics regarded as typical of women, especially when considered unbecoming in a man

Examples

The effeminateness of men
Older texts sometimes use effeminateness as a criticism of men who don't conform to traditional gender roles.
Some critics wrongly equate artistic sensitivity with effeminateness.
The king was often mocked for his perceived effeminateness and gentle manners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminacy - the state or quality of being effeminate
  • antonym: masculinity - qualities regarded as typical of men
