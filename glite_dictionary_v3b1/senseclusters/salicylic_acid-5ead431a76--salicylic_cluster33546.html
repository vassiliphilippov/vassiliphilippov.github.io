salicylic acid

noun
compound used in aspirin and skincare
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsælɪˈsɪlɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌsælɪˈsɪlɪk ˈasɪd /; / ˌsælɪˈsɪlɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a chemical compound used in making aspirin, dyes, perfumes, fungicides, and in skincare products to treat acne and other skin conditions
