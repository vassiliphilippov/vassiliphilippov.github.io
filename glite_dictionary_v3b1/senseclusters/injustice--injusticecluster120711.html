injustice

noun
an unjust act or instance
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
an act or situation that is unfair or not just; lack of fairness or justice

Examples

He could not tolerate even the slightest injustice against his friends.
Many people protested against the injustice they witnessed in the legal system.
She dedicated her life to fighting social injustice around the world.
