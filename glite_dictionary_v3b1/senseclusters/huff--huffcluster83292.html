huff

verb
inhale fumes for intoxication
9.4
Pronunciation: / hʌf / (AmE), / hʌf / (BrE)
to inhale fumes or vapors from substances such as glue or paint, typically for the purpose of getting high

Examples

To huff glue
Many inhalants can severely harm those who huff them even just once.
She was worried her son might be huffing glue after finding empty tubes in his room.
The teenagers were caught huffing spray paint behind the old warehouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sniff - to inhale something through the nose, sometimes for intoxication
