thigh

noun
upper part of the leg
Pronunciation: / θaɪ / (AmE), / θaɪ / (BrE)
the part of the leg between the hip and the knee

Examples

Human thigh muscle
A doctor examined the patient's injured thigh after the accident.
She bruised her thigh when she bumped into the table.
The thigh muscles are important for running and jumping.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: calf - the back part of the lower leg below the knee
  • degree higher: hip - the joint connecting the thigh with the trunk
