mammonic attitude

noun
attitude focused on wealth and materialism
8.3
Pronunciation: / mæˈmɑnɪk ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / mæˈmɒnɪk ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude or mindset that is obsessed with acquiring wealth and material possessions.

Examples

Mammonic attitude to money
Growing up in an environment with a mammonic attitude shaped his views on success and happiness.
His mammonic attitude often led to conflicts with friends.
The company's policies reflect a distinctly mammonic attitude toward profit over ethics.

  • synonym: materialistic - overly concerned with material possessions
