Borana

proper noun
East African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / bɔːˈrɑːnə / (AmE), / bɔːˈrɑːnə / (BrE)
an ethnic group living in southern Ethiopia and northern Kenya, or the Cushitic language they speak

Examples

Borana people
The Borana have a long tradition of cattle herding in the arid regions of Kenya.
Many Borana people continue to speak their native Cushitic language at home.
Researchers studied the social structure of the Borana community in southern Ethiopia.

Related Terms
