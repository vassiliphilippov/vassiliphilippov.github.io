chérie

noun
term of endearment
9.8
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪˈri / (AmE), / ʃeˈriː / (BrE)
a term of endearment meaning 'darling' or 'dear', borrowed from French and sometimes used in English

Examples

My chérie
He whispered 'Goodnight, chérie' before leaving.
She smiled and called her daughter chérie as she tucked her into bed.
At the café, he greeted his friend with a cheerful 'Bonjour, chérie!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: darling - a person who is very dear or loved
  • synonym: dear - a loved or cherished person
