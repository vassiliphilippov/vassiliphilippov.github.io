cutty

noun
short undergarment
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌti / (AmE), / ˈkʌti / (BrE)
a short undergarment or chemise, especially used in Scottish or historical contexts

Examples

A cutty chemise
In the old cottage, she found a faded cutty among her grandmother's belongings.
The character in the novel wore a simple cutty beneath her heavy dress.
During the festival, traditional dancers dressed in a cutty and tartan skirts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemise - a loose-fitting undergarment or dress
  • synonym: petticoat - a woman's light, loose undergarment worn under a skirt or dress
