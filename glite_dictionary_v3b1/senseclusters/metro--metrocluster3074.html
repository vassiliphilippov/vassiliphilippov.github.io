metro

noun
underground urban railway system
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtroʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrəʊ / (BrE)
an underground electric railway system, especially in a city

Examples

The metro system
Many commuters use the metro to avoid traffic jams during rush hour.
The city is expanding its metro network to serve more neighborhoods.
We took the metro from the airport to the center of Paris.

  • synonym: underground - the automated underground railway in London; also used generally for subway systems
