devocalization

noun
removal of vocal cords
Pronunciation: / diˌvoʊkələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌvəʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the surgical removal or alteration of the vocal cords to reduce or eliminate an animal's ability to make sounds

Examples

Pet devocalization

Some veterinarians refuse to perform devocalization because of ethical concerns.
After the devocalization surgery, the dog could only make faint noises.
