technically

adverb
with regard to technique or skill
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛknɪkli / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪkli / (BrE)
With regard to technique; in a way that relates to the skills or methods used in a particular activity or field.

Examples

Technically perfect performance
He was technically correct, although his answer missed the main point.
She is technically skilled but lacks emotional expressiveness in her music.
The architect’s design is technically impressive, featuring innovative construction methods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: practically - in a way that relates to actual practice rather than theory or technique
