fluttery

adjective
light, flapping movement
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌtəri / (AmE), / ˈflʌtəri / (BrE)
making or characterized by quick, light, and repeated flapping or flickering movements, like wings or small objects.

Examples

Fluttery curtains
The curtains had a fluttery motion as the breeze came through the open window.
She felt a fluttery sensation in her stomach before giving her speech.
A fluttery butterfly landed gently on the flower in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flickering - shining or moving with quick, unsteady movements
  • antonym: steady - not shaking or moving; stable
