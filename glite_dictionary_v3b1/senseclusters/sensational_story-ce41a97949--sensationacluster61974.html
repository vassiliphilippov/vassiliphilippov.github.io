sensational story

noun
shocking or exciting news article
1.9
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈseɪʃənəl ˈstɔri / (AmE), / sɛnˈseɪʃnəl ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a news article designed to excite, shock, or arouse strong interest, often by exaggerating or emphasizing unusual events

Examples

A sensational story about a scandal
Readers flocked to the website after a sensational story about a celebrity scandal went viral.
The newspaper was criticized for featuring a sensational story on its front page.
They accused the media of fabricating a sensational story to boost ratings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tabloid - a newspaper focusing on sensational news and gossip
