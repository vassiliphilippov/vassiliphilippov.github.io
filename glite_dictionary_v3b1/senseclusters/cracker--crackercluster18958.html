cracker

noun
malicious computer intruder
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrækə / (BrE)
a person who gains unauthorized access to computer systems, often with malicious intent

Examples

A computer cracker
After the cracker defaced the website, administrators quickly restored the original content.
Some experts distinguish between a hacker and a cracker based on their intentions.
The cracker was able to bypass all the security measures and steal sensitive data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hacker - a person skilled in computer programming, sometimes used for those who break into systems
