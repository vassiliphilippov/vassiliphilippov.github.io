designator

noun
target-marking device
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛzɪɡneɪtə / (BrE)
a device or person that indicates, marks, or identifies a target or location, especially using a laser or other signal.

Examples

Laser designator
The soldier used a designator to guide the missile to its target.
A laser designator is essential for precision airstrikes in modern warfare.
The technician checked the designator before the operation to ensure it was working correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: identifier - something that identifies or distinguishes
  • synonym: marker - a device or person that marks a position or object
