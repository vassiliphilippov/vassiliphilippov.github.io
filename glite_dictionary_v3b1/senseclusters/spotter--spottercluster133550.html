spotter

noun
safety assistant
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑtər / (AmE), / ˈspɒtə / (BrE)
a person who watches and assists someone for safety, especially during exercise or weightlifting

Examples

Gym spotter
It is important to have a spotter when you are bench pressing heavy weights.
The gymnast trusted her spotter to help her land safely during practice.
Before attempting a new lift, he asked his friend to be his spotter at the gym.

  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees an activity or people
