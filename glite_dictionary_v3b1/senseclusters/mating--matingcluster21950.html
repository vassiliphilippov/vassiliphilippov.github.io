mating

noun
pairing for reproduction
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pairing a male and female for reproduction

Examples

Animal mating behavior
Scientists observed the mating behavior of wolves in the wild.
Spring is the season when mating occurs for many bird species.
The zoo carefully monitors the mating of endangered animals to increase their population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeding - the process of producing offspring by sexual reproduction
  • synonym: copulation - sexual intercourse, especially in animals
