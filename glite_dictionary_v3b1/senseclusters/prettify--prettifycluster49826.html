prettify

verb
make more beautiful or attractive
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛtiˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈprɛtɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make (something) look more attractive or beautiful, often in a superficial or decorative way

Examples

To prettify a room
Don't prettify the facts; tell them exactly as they happened.
She tried to prettify the old living room with fresh paint and new curtains.
The web designer used animations to prettify the site's homepage.
