coppa

noun
Italian cured meat
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊpə / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə / (BrE)
a traditional Italian cured pork cold cut made from the neck or shoulder, similar to capicola

Examples

Coppa sandwich
He ordered a sandwich with coppa and provolone cheese.
The chef arranged thin slices of coppa on the charcuterie board for the guests.
Authentic Italian coppa is often served with bread and olives as an appetizer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capicola - an Italian cured meat made from pork neck or shoulder
  • synonym: prosciutto - an Italian dry-cured ham, usually thinly sliced
