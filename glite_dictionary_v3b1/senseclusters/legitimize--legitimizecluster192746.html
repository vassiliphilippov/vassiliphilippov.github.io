Synonyms

  • legitimise
    5.0 0.00/m
    legitimise is the British verb used for making something legal or officially acceptable, while legitimize is the American form used the same way.
  • legitimate
    5.6 1.01/m
    legitimate often appears as a verb or adjective meaning to make something lawful or justified, while legitimize stresses official legal acceptance.
  • legalize
    3.7 7.94/m
    legalize means to make something lawful by law or government action, while legitimize can mean making it officially accepted more widely.
  • validate
    3.9 10/m
