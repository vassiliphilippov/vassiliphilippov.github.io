cholangiographic

adjective
bile duct imaging
Pronunciation: / koʊˌlændʒiəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / kəʊˌlændʒiəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the radiographic examination or imaging of the bile ducts

Examples

Cholangiographic imaging
The doctor ordered a cholangiographic study to check for any blockages in the bile ducts.
A cholangiographic procedure can help diagnose gallstones or tumors in the biliary system.
The surgeon reviewed the cholangiographic images before planning the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cholangiography - the process of taking X-ray images of the bile ducts
  • synonym: radiographic - relating to the use of X-rays or other radiation for imaging
