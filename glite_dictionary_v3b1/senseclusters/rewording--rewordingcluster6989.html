rewording

noun
expressing something in different words
4.1
Pronunciation: / riˈwɝːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈwɜːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of expressing something in different words, or the words resulting from this change

Examples

A simple rewording
A simple rewording can sometimes avoid misunderstandings.
Her rewording of the instructions helped everyone understand the process.
The editor suggested a rewording of the title to make it clearer.
