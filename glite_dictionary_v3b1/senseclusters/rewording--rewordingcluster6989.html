noun

Examples

A simple rewording

Her rewording of the instructions helped everyone understand the process.
The editor suggested a rewording of the title to make it clearer.

Synonyms

  • rephrasing
    3.5 0.11/m
    rephrasing uses different words to make meaning clearer or different, while rewording is the general act of changing any wording.
  • paraphrasis
    9.9 0.00/m
    paraphrasis restates a text to clarify its sense for readers, while rewording covers any change of wording or form.
  • recasting
    5.6 0.14/m
    recasting alters the form or structure as well as wording, while rewording often means simply changing words.
  • restatement
    4.1 1.13/m
    restatement repeats or presents an idea again, sometimes with small edits, while rewording focuses on changing words.
  • reexpression
    6.5 0.00/m
    reexpression expresses an idea again, possibly in another form like tone or medium, while rewording changes words specifically.
  • phrasing
    3.4 2.01/m
    phrasing refers to the choice and order of words to express something, while rewording means replacing those words.
