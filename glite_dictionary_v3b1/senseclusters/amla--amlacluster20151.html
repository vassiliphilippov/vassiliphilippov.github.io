amla

noun
Indian fruit tree
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmlə / (AmE), / ˈæmlə / (BrE)
a small deciduous tree native to India and Southeast Asia, known for its edible sour fruit rich in vitamin C

Examples

Amla tree
The amla is often used in traditional Indian medicine for its health benefits.
She added dried amla to her tea for a tangy flavor and extra vitamin C.
Farmers in the region grow amla trees for both their fruit and medicinal value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gooseberry - a small, tart fruit from the Ribes genus
