bedazzle

verb
to impress or decorate brightly
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdæzəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdæzəl / (BrE)
to impress greatly or decorate with bright, shiny objects that attract attention

Examples

To bedazzle a jacket
The designer bedazzled the jacket with colorful rhinestones for the fashion show.
Her performance bedazzled the audience, leaving everyone in awe of her talent.
He loves to bedazzle his phone case with glitter and shiny stickers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzle - to impress or amaze with brightness or skill
  • synonym: decorate - to make something look more attractive by adding items to it
  • antonym: plain - not decorated or impressive
