uncuttable

adjective
cannot be removed
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be removed, dismissed, or dropped, especially from a team or position; indispensable or guaranteed to remain.

Examples

Uncuttable player
After her promotion, she became almost uncuttable from the management team.
The star player's contract made him uncuttable for the next three seasons.
Some traditions are considered uncuttable parts of the annual festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indispensable - absolutely necessary or essential
  • antonym: replaceable - able to be replaced or removed
