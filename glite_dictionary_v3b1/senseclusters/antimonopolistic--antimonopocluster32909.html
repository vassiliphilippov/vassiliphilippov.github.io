antimonopolistic

adjective
against monopolies
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌmɑːnəpəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌmɒnəpəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
opposing or designed to prevent the formation or existence of monopolies

Examples

Antimonopolistic laws
The new regulations are antimonopolistic and aim to increase market competition.
Many economists support antimonopolistic policies to protect consumers from unfair practices.
The commission reviewed the merger for possible violations of antimonopolistic laws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antitrust - opposing or regulating monopolies and promoting competition
  • antonym: monopolistic - relating to or characteristic of a monopoly
