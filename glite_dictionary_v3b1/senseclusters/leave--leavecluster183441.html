Synonyms

  • bequeath
    8.9 2.06/m
    bequeath is a formal legal verb that gives property by will, while leave is a more general term for passing on belongings or duties.
  • pass on
    2.7 6.49/m
    pass on means transfer or tell something to another person, while leave often means giving property, knowledge, or responsibility, especially after death.
  • give
    1.0 783/m
    give means hand or offer something to someone now, while leave usually means transferring property or responsibility, often after a person's death.
