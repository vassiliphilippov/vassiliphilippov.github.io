ruby

adjective
deep red color
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈruːbi / (AmE), / ˈruːbi / (BrE)
Having a deep red color, resembling that of a ruby, blood, cherries, or tomatoes.

Examples

Ruby lips
His lips were stained a ruby shade after eating the cherries.
Sunset cast a ruby glow over the quiet city skyline.
The bride wore a stunning dress in ruby silk that caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scarlet - bright red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • synonym: vermilion - a brilliant red or scarlet
