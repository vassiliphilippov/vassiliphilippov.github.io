control tower

noun
tower for airport aircraft observation and control
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊl ˌtaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl ˌtaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a tall structure at an airport with a glass-enclosed workspace for visually monitoring and directing aircraft movements

Examples

Airport control tower
From the control tower, you have a clear view of all the runways.
She works in the control tower, coordinating arrivals and departures at the airport.
The control tower cleared the plane for takeoff despite the heavy traffic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air traffic control - the ground-based service directing aircraft movements
