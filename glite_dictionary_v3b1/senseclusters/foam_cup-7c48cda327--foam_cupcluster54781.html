foam cup

noun
disposable cup made of foam
Pronunciation: / foʊm kʌp / (AmE), / fəʊm kʌp / (BrE)
a lightweight, disposable drinking container made from expanded polystyrene foam

Examples

A foam cup
Please recycle your foam cup after use.
She poured orange juice into a foam cup for her son.
The cafeteria only had foam cups for hot drinks that day.
