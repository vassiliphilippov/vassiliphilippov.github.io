Synonyms

  • circle around
    2.3 1.92/m
    circle around is a phrasal verb used to describe moving in a circular course around something, while circle is the simple verb.
  • round
    3.0 5.57/m
    round is used to mean travel along a curved path, often in speech, while circle is the neutral verb for moving around.
  • go around
    2.0 2.50/m
    go around is an informal phrase for moving or turning in a circle in speech, while circle is the neutral verb for circular movement.
  • revolve
    3.2 3.03/m
    revolve is more technical and means turning around an axis continuously, while circle means moving around something.
