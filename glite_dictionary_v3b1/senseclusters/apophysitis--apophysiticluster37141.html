apophysitis

noun
growth plate inflammation
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːfəˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / əˌpɒfɪˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of a growth plate in a bone, commonly affecting children and adolescents during periods of rapid growth

Examples

Apophysitis in children
The young soccer player was sidelined due to apophysitis in his knee.
Doctors often see cases of apophysitis in teenagers who play sports regularly.
Rest and physical therapy helped her recover from apophysitis in her heel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: osteomyelitis - inflammation of bone caused by infection
  • synonym: tendinitis - inflammation of a tendon
