slanting

adjective
at an angle; not vertical or horizontal
Pronunciation: / ˈslæntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɑːntɪŋ / (BrE)
at an angle or incline that is not vertical or horizontal; having a slanted or oblique direction

Examples

Slanting lines
She admired the painter's use of slanting lines to create a sense of movement in the artwork.
Sunlight streamed through the slanting blinds, casting stripes on the wall.
The old roof had a slanting design that helped rainwater drain away quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: horizontal - parallel to the plane of the horizon; flat
  • synonym: oblique - not parallel or at a right angle; slanted
  • antonym: vertical - at a right angle to the horizontal; upright
