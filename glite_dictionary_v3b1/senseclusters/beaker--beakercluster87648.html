beaker

noun
cylindrical cup or container, usually without handle
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːkər / (AmE), / ˈbiːkə / (BrE)
A cup or container, usually cylindrical and without a handle, often used in laboratories or for drinking.

Examples

Plastic beaker for drinks
At the picnic, each child received a colorful beaker for their drink.
She drank her orange juice from a plastic beaker with no handle.
The laboratory technician poured the solution into a glass beaker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cup - a small open container for drinking
  • synonym: goblet - a bowl-shaped drinking vessel without handles
  • antonym: mug - a sturdy cup with a handle
