Synonyms

  • propagate
    5.9 2/m
    propagate spreads ideas and beliefs actively, often by supporters or groups, while disseminate is a general formal way to spread information.
  • spread
    1.6 50/m
    spread becomes known or moves among people naturally or quickly, while disseminate implies planned wide sharing of information.
  • broadcast
    3.0 4/m
    broadcast makes information public through media to many people at once, while disseminate covers many ways of spreading information.
  • publicize
    4.0 9/m
    publicize makes something known to attract attention or customers, while disseminate simply spreads information widely without promotion.
  • circulate
    3.9 9/m
    circulate passes information or documents among a limited group, while disseminate aims to reach many people widely and publicly.
  • distribute
    2.0 9/m
    distribute gives out items or information to people or places in an organized way, while disseminate focuses on wide public spread.
  • diffuse
    5.5 4/m
    diffuse spreads something thinly over an area or among people slowly, while disseminate suggests active wide sharing of information.
