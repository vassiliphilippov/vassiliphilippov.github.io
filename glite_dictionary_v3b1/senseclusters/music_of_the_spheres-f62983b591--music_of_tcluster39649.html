music of the spheres

noun
ancient idea of celestial harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuː.zɪk əv ðə sfɪrz /; / ˈmjuːzɪk əv ðə sfɪrz / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪk ɒv ðə sfɪəz /; / ˈmjuːzɪk əv ðə sfɪəz / (BrE)
an ancient philosophical idea that the movements or proportions of planets and stars create a kind of perfect, inaudible music, symbolizing harmony in the universe
