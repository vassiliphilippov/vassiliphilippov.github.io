Pachycephala

noun
genus of whistling birds
Pronunciation: / ˌpækiˈsɛfələ / (AmE), / ˌpakɪˈsɛfələ /; / ˌpakɪˈkɛfələ / (BrE)
a genus of arboreal insect-eating birds, called whistlers, found in Australasia and Southeast Asia
