mark

noun
target for aiming in shooting or archery
1.0
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
a point or target at which something is aimed, especially in shooting or archery

Examples

The archer steadied his bow, focusing on the distant mark.
You must keep your eyes on the mark if you want to hit the bullseye.
