compere

noun
British show or TV host
9.7 6.2
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛr / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pɛə / (BrE)
A person who introduces performers at a show, on television, or at a public event, especially in British English.

Examples

A TV compere
At the awards show, the compere introduced each celebrity with great enthusiasm.
She worked as a compere for several popular TV talent competitions.
The compere kept the audience entertained between acts with his clever jokes.
