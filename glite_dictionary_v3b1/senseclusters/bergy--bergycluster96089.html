bergy

noun
small iceberg
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrɡi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡi / (BrE)
a small iceberg or a large floating piece of ice that has broken off from a glacier or ice shelf

Examples

A bergy floating nearby
The ship carefully navigated around a bergy floating near the coast.
A single bergy drifted past the research station during the night.
Fishermen spotted a bergy blocking their usual route to the open sea.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: growler - a very small iceberg or piece of floating ice
  • degree higher: iceberg - a large mass of ice floating in the sea
