trolley

noun
wheeled cart
1.0 0.74/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtr…ëÀêli / (AmE), / Ààtr…íli / (BrE)
a cart with wheels used for carrying items, especially in supermarkets, airports, or stations

Examples
