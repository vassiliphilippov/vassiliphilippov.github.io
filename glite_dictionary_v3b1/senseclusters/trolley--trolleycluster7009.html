trolley

noun
wheeled cart
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːli / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli / (BrE)
a cart with wheels used for carrying items, especially in supermarkets, airports, or stations

Examples

Supermarket trolley
She loaded her groceries onto the trolley at the supermarket.
At the airport, he pushed a heavy trolley full of luggage toward the exit.
The children enjoyed riding on the trolley while their parents shopped.
She pushed a trolley full of groceries through the supermarket aisles.
At the airport, he loaded his suitcases onto a trolley to make them easier to move.
The store provides a trolley for customers to carry heavy items to their cars.
