contravention

noun
violation of a law or rule
8.9 0.53/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀêntr…ôÀàv…õn É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåk…íntr…ôÀàv…õn É…ôn / (BrE)
the act of doing something that is not allowed by a law or rule; violation or breach

Examples

A contravention of rules
Acting without a license is a contravention of the city's regulations.
Any contravention of these safety rules may result in immediate dismissal.
The company was fined for contravention of environmental laws.

Synonyms

  • violation
    4.1 29/m
    a violation is the act of breaking a rule or law in many contexts, while contravention is a formal breach of law or rule.
  • infringement
    6.7 16/m
    infringement often refers to breaking rights or laws in a legal case, while contravention is any act that breaks a law or rule.
  • offense
    3.3 16/m
    an offense is any act that people or courts treat as wrong or illegal, while contravention focuses on breaking a specific rule or law.
  • transgression
    8.1 3.77/m
    a transgression is an act that breaks a moral rule or law and often feels personal, while contravention is a legal or formal breach.
