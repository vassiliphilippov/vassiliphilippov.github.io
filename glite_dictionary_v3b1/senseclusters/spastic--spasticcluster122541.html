spastic

noun
offensive disability slur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspæstɪk / (AmE), / ˈspæstɪk / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person with a disability, especially one affecting muscle control; strongly discouraged and considered insulting

Examples

Called a spastic
The word spastic is now widely recognized as an offensive insult and should not be used.
He was hurt by being called a spastic at school, which is an unacceptable slur.
Many organizations campaign against the use of spastic due to its derogatory meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cripple - an offensive term for a person with a physical disability
  • antonym: disabled person - a person with a physical or mental disability (neutral term)
