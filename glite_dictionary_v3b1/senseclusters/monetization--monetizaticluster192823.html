monetization

noun
turning content into revenue

the process of converting content, data, services, or other assets into revenue, typically through advertising, subscriptions, sales, or licensing.

Examples

Monetization of content
Many platforms rely on monetization of user data or content to generate revenue.
Independent journalists struggle to balance quality reporting with monetization strategies like paywalls and sponsorship.
App developers planned the monetization model carefully, choosing subscriptions instead of intrusive ads.
The startup's monetisation plan combined ads, premium features, and a small in-app store to fund development.
After the blogger reached a large audience, she focused on monetisation through sponsored posts and memberships.
Platform designers consider user experience before aggressive monetisation to avoid driving people away from the service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercialize - to manage or exploit something for profit
  • synonym: monetize - to convert something (for example, a service or content) into a source of revenue
