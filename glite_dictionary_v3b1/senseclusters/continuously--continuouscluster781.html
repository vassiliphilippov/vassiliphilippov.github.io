Examples

Continuously without pause
She spoke continuously during the entire meeting, barely pausing for breath.
The river flowed continuously through the valley for miles.
The security cameras record continuously to ensure the building is always monitored.
Rain fell continuously for three days, causing widespread flooding.
She talked continuously for over an hour without taking a break.
The machines in the factory operate continuously throughout the night.

  • uninterruptedly
    6.3 0.08/m

  • constantly
