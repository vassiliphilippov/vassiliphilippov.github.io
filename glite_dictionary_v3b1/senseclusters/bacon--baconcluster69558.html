bacon

noun
salted and smoked pig meat
8.5 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.kən / (BrE)
Salted and dried or smoked meat from the back and sides of a pig, usually sliced thin and cooked by frying or grilling.

Examples

Crispy bacon strips
He likes to eat bacon and eggs for breakfast every morning.
She could smell the sizzling bacon from the kitchen.
Vegetarians do not eat bacon or other pork products.
