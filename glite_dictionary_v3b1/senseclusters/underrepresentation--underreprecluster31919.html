underrepresentation

noun
insufficient representation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of a group or category being represented in smaller numbers or less fairly than expected or needed
