dieldrin

noun
banned insecticide
Pronunciation: / daɪˈɛldrɪn / (AmE), / daɪˈɛldrɪn / (BrE)
a toxic chemical compound formerly used as an insecticide, now banned in many countries due to environmental and health risks

Examples

Toxic dieldrin chemical

Traces of dieldrin can still be found in some soils decades after its use was stopped.
