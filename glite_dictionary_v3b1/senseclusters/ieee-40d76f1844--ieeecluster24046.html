IEEE

noun
engineering citation style
Pronunciation: / aɪ iː iː iː / (AmE), / aɪ iː iː iː / (BrE)
a widely used citation and reference style for engineering and computer science publications, based on the standards of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

Examples

IEEE citation style
The professor asked us to use the IEEE format for our research papers.
Many engineering journals require submissions to follow the IEEE citation guidelines.
She learned how to properly reference sources using the IEEE style in her technical writing class.

Related Terms
