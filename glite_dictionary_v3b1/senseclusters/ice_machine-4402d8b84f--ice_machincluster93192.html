ice machine

noun
machine that makes ice cubes
Pronunciation: / aɪs məˈʃin / (AmE), / aɪs məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine, typically electrically powered, that produces and dispenses ice cubes

Examples

The ice machine produces cubes
The bartender filled the bucket using the ice machine behind the counter.
The hotel lobby had an ice machine for guests on every floor.
We had to fix the broken ice machine before the party started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice maker - a device for making ice in cubes
