Lymantria dispar

noun
species of moth pest
9.9
Pronunciation: / laɪˈmæntriə ˈdɪspɑr / (AmE), / lɪˈmantrɪə ˈdɪspɑː / (BrE)
A species of European moth, commonly known as the spongy moth, that is a significant pest of shade trees in North America.

Examples

Lymantria dispar moth
Lymantria dispar has caused extensive damage to oak forests in North America.
Efforts to control Lymantria dispar have included biological and chemical methods.
The spread of Lymantria dispar poses a threat to urban and rural trees alike.
