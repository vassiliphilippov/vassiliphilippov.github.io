flighting

noun
spiral blade on auger
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a helical blade or spiral fin attached to an auger or screw conveyor to move material along a shaft or trough

Examples

Auger flighting

Regular maintenance of the flighting ensures smooth movement of grain through the conveyor.
Worn flighting can cause blockages and reduce the performance of the machine.
