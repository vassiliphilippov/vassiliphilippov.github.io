Examples

Synonyms

  • loose
    1.0 10/m
    loose is a general adjective for not tight and can describe many things, while loose-fitting names clothing that hangs away from the body.
  • baggy
    1.0 3.29/m
    baggy fits very loosely and often looks casual, while loose-fitting simply describes clothing that does not sit close to the body.
  • blousy
    7.1 0.03/m
    blousy is fuller and often looks loose or untidy, while loose-fitting is neutral clothing that hangs away from the body.
  • flowy
    3.8 0.16/m
    flowy hangs or moves softly and feels light and graceful, while loose-fitting only means clothing that does not fit closely.
