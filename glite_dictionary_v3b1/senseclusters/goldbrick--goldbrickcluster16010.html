goldbrick

verb
cheat or swindle by deceit
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbrɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbrɪk / (BrE)
to cheat or swindle someone, especially by tricking them out of money or something valuable

Examples

To goldbrick someone
He tried to goldbrick his friend out of their concert tickets.
She warned them not to goldbrick others if they wanted to keep their reputation.
The company was accused of attempting to goldbrick customers with fake investment schemes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defraud - to illegally obtain money from someone by deception
  • synonym: swindle - to cheat someone out of money or property by fraud
