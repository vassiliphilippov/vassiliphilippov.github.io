nipple

noun
part of a baby's bottle or pacifier for sucking
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪpəl / (BrE)
a flexible rubber or silicone cap with a small hole, attached to a baby's feeding bottle or pacifier for sucking and feeding

Examples

Baby bottle nipple
He replaced the worn-out nipple on the baby's pacifier.
Please make sure the nipple is clean before using the bottle.
The flow of milk depends on the size of the holes in the nipple.
