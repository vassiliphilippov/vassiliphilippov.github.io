metallicity

noun
metal-like quality of sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈlɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈlɪsəti / (BrE)
The quality or state of having a sound resembling that of metal, especially a bright, resonant, or metallic timbre.

Examples

Some audio equipment can enhance the metallicity of cymbals, making them sound sharper.
