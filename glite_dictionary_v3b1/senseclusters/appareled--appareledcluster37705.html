appareled

adjective
dressed or clothed, often finely
Pronunciation: / əˈpɛrəld / (AmE), / əˈparəld / (BrE)
dressed or clothed, especially in fine or distinctive attire; often used in combination or literary contexts.

Examples

Appareled in robes
Ancient statues are often depicted as appareled in elaborate robes.
She was splendidly appareled in a silk gown and pearls.
The guests arrived appareled in their finest evening wear for the gala.

  • synonym: garbed - dressed in distinctive or specific clothing
