metaphyton

noun
algae community in water
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈfaɪtɑn / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈfaɪtɒn / (BrE)
a community of algae and microorganisms that grow attached to submerged surfaces, such as plants, in aquatic environments
