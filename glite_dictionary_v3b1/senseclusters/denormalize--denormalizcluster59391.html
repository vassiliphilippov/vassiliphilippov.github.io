denormalize

verb
to make data less structured
8.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈnɔːr.mə.laɪz/ /; / /də.ˈnɔːr.mə.laɪz/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈnɔː.məl.aɪz/ /; / /də.ˈnɔː.məl.aɪz/ (BrE)
to convert data from a normalized structure to a less organized or more redundant form, often for performance reasons

Examples

To denormalize a database
The engineer chose to denormalize the database to speed up reporting queries.
Sometimes it is necessary to denormalize data to improve application performance.
After several changes, the team decided to denormalize some tables for easier access.
