English primrose

noun
a European primrose with pale yellow flowers
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈprɪmˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈprɪm.rəʊz / (BrE)
a plant native to western and southern Europe, widely cultivated for its pale yellow flowers; a variety of primrose (Primula vulgaris)
