print

noun
picture/design made from an engraving
3.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /prɪnt/ (AmE), /prɪnt/ (BrE)
A picture or design made by pressing paper against an engraved or etched surface covered with ink.

Examples

An art print
Limited edition prints are often signed and numbered by the artist.
She bought a beautiful print of a landscape by a famous artist.
The museum displayed a rare print from the eighteenth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engraving - a print made from an engraved plate, block, or other surface
  • synonym: etching - a print produced by the process of etching
  • antonym: painting - an image made with paint, not printing
