mothproof

verb
treat to protect from moths
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔθˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈmɒθˌpruːf / (BrE)
to treat or protect (an article, especially of clothing or fabric) so that it is safe from damage by moths

Examples

To mothproof clothes
It is important to mothproof wool carpets so they last longer.
She spent the afternoon mothproofing blankets and coats in the attic.
We always mothproof our sweaters before packing them away for summer.
