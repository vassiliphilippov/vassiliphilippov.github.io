junkyard

noun
place for collecting and storing scrap or junk
3.5 1.89/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í å≈ãk.j…ërd / (AmE), / Ààd í å≈ãk.j…ëÀêd / (BrE)
a place where discarded or scrap materials, especially metal or vehicles, are collected and stored for resale or recycling

Examples

A junkyard full of cars
Children love to explore the junkyard in search of hidden treasures.
The old car sat in the junkyard for years before it was finally recycled.
We found a replacement part for the engine at a local junkyard.

Synonyms

  • scrapyard
    2.8 0.14/m
    a scrapyard is a place that dismantles and sells parts from old vehicles and metal, while a junkyard stores discarded items for resale or recycling.
  • scrapheap
    5.4 0.04/m
    a scrapheap is often a loose pile of waste metal for disposal or recycling, while a junkyard is an organized site for resale.
  • boneyard
    8.0 0.09/m
    a boneyard stores old vehicles and equipment left to decay or spare parts, while a junkyard focuses on collecting items for resale or recycling.
