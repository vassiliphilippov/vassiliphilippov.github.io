quest after

verb
search or hunt for something
8.6
Pronunciation: / kwɛst ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / kwɛst ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
to go in search of or hunt for something, especially something difficult to obtain or achieve.

Examples

To quest after happiness
For years, she would quest after the meaning of true happiness.
Many adventurers quest after legendary treasures hidden in distant lands.
Poets and philosophers often quest after universal truths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pursue - follow or chase after something in order to catch or achieve it
  • synonym: search for - try to find something by looking or seeking
