sunfish

noun
flesh of American perch-like fish
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.fɪʃ / (BrE)
The flesh of any of various American perch-like fish of the family Centrarchidae, used as food.
