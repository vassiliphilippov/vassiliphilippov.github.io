gong buoy

noun
buoy with a bell as a navigational aid
Pronunciation: / ɡɑŋ ˈbuːi / (AmE), / ɡɒŋ ˈbɔɪ / (BrE)
a buoy equipped with a bell that rings as a signal, typically used as a navigational aid at sea

Examples

A gong buoy rings
A gong buoy marks the entrance to the harbor, helping ships navigate safely in low visibility.
Sailors relied on the sound of the gong buoy to avoid dangerous rocks near the shore.
The lighthouse keeper checked the gong buoy to ensure its signal could be heard in heavy fog.
