trackless

adjective
without tracks or paths
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækləs / (AmE), / ˈtræklɪs / (BrE)
without tracks, paths, or roads; not marked or traveled by people or vehicles

Examples

A trackless field
After the snowstorm, the field was a vast, trackless expanse of white.
The explorers journeyed deep into the trackless forest, unsure of what lay ahead.
They relied on a compass to cross the trackless desert.
Satellite images showed only mountains and trackless wilderness stretching across the region.
The expedition ventured deep into the trackless jungle, relying only on old maps and a compass.
They camped for days in the trackless desert, far from any sign of civilization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pathless - having no path
  • synonym: roadless - (especially of an area) not having roads
  • synonym: unmarked - not having signs or marks of a path or track
