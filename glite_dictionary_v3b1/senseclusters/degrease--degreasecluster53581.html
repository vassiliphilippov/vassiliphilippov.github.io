degrease

verb
remove grease or oil
2.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈɡris / (AmE), / dɪˈɡriːz / (BrE)
to remove grease or oil from something, especially a pan or surface

Examples

To degrease a pan
After frying, she carefully degreased the tray to keep it clean.
He used soap and hot water to degrease the kitchen counter.
Please degrease the pan before adding the new ingredients.
