Examples

Synonyms

  • medicative
    7.9 0.00/m
    medicative is described as having medicine-like healing qualities, while medicinal relates more broadly to medicine or treating illness.
  • curative
    6.2 0.89/m
    curative directly heals disease and restores health, while medicinal mainly describes substances or properties related to medicine.
  • therapeutic
    5.2 11/m
    therapeutic refers to treatment and has beneficial effects, while medicinal more generally means relating to medicine or healing.
