medicinal

adjective
having healing properties; relating to medicine
2.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈdɪ.sə.nəl/ /; / /mə.ˈdɪ.sɪ.nəl/ (AmE), /mə.ˈdɪ.sɪ.nəl/ /; / /mə.ˈdɪ.sə.nəl/ (BrE)
(of a substance or property) having the ability to heal or treat illness; relating to medicine.

Examples

Medicinal tea
He drinks a medicinal tea every evening to help his digestion.
Many plants have medicinal uses, such as treating minor cuts and bruises.
Some mushrooms are prized for their medicinal properties in traditional medicine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curative - able to cure disease or heal
  • antonym: harmful - causing damage or injury; not beneficial to health
  • synonym: therapeutic - having a healing effect; relating to the treatment of illness
