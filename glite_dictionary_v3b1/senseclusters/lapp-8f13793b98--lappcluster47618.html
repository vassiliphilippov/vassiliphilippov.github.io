Lapp

noun
old term for Sámi language
9.9
Pronunciation: / læp / (AmE), / læp / (BrE)
an old term for the language spoken by the Sámi people in northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula; now usually called Sámi

Examples

Lapp language
Many linguists prefer the term 'Sámi' to 'Lapp' when referring to the language.
She studied traditional folklore written in Lapp while living in Finland.
The Lapp language is still taught in some schools in northern Scandinavia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sámi - language spoken by the indigenous Sámi people of northern Europe
