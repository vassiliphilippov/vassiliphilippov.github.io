articulate

adjective
made of jointed segments
4.1 6.2
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪk.jə.leɪt/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪk.jə.lət/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪk.jʊ.leɪt/ /; / /ɑː.ˈtɪk.jʊ.lət/ (BrE)
(of an animal or structure) consisting of segments joined together by joints.

Examples

An articulate insect body
A worm’s body is not articulate, unlike that of an ant with clearly defined joints.
Many insects possess an articulate exoskeleton that allows for complex movement.
The crab’s articulate limbs help it scuttle quickly along the ocean floor.
