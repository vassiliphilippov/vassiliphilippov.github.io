taramasalata

noun
Greek fish roe dip
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːrəˌmɑːsəˈlɑːtə / (AmE), / ˌtærəməˌsælˈɑːtə / (BrE)
a Greek dish made from fish roe blended with olive oil, lemon juice, and bread or potatoes, served as a dip or spread
