buster

noun
a person who breaks horses
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbʌstə / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who breaks and trains wild horses for riding

Examples

Horse buster
A good buster needs patience and a steady hand to earn a horse's trust.
As a young man, he worked as a buster out west, breaking horses for the rodeo.
The ranch hired a skilled buster to tame their wild mustangs.
