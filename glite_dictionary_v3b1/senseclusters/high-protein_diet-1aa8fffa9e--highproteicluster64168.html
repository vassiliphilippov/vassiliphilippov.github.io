high-protein diet

noun
diet with high protein content
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈproʊtin ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / haɪ ˈprəʊtiːn ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that contains a large proportion of protein-rich foods, often used to promote muscle growth or treat malnutrition

Examples

A high-protein diet plan
A high-protein diet can be beneficial for people recovering from surgery or illness.
Athletes often follow a high-protein diet to help build muscle and speed recovery after workouts.
Some weight-loss plans recommend a high-protein diet to reduce appetite and preserve lean muscle mass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low-protein diet - a diet with a reduced amount of protein
