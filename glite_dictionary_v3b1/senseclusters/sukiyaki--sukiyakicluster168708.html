sukiyaki

noun
Japanese meat and vegetable hot pot dish
9.1 6.8
Pronunciation: /suː.kiː.ˈɑː.kiː/ /; / /suː.kiː.ˈjɑː.kiː/ (AmE), /suː.kiː.ˈɑː.kiː/ /; / /suː.kiː.ˈjɑː.kiː/ (BrE)
a Japanese dish of thinly sliced beef (or other meat) cooked quickly with vegetables and soy sauce, usually at the dining table

Examples

Japanese sukiyaki dish
At the party, a chef prepared sukiyaki right at the table, impressing everyone.
Her favorite meal to cook for guests is homemade sukiyaki with plenty of fresh vegetables.
We enjoyed authentic sukiyaki at a traditional Japanese restaurant in Tokyo.
