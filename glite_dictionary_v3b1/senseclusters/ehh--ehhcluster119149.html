ehh

interjection
request for repetition
Pronunciation: / ɛ / (AmE), / ɛ / (BrE)
used to ask someone to repeat what they said or to show that you did not hear or understand them

Examples

Ehh? What
Sorry, I didn't catch that—ehh?
When the music was too loud, she leaned in and said ehh to her friend.
He looked confused and replied with ehh when asked a difficult question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: huh - used to express lack of understanding or to request repetition
  • synonym: pardon - used to politely ask someone to repeat what they said
