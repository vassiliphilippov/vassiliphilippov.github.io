noncharismatic

adjective
not Charismatic
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkærɪzˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkærɪzˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not relating to or emphasizing the Charismatic movement or spiritual gifts in Christian worship

Examples

Noncharismatic church
The church is known for its noncharismatic services that focus on scripture and tradition.
Many older congregations remain noncharismatic, preferring hymns and formal prayers over spontaneous worship.
He grew up in a noncharismatic denomination that did not practice speaking in tongues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charismatic - relating to the Charismatic movement or spiritual gifts in Christianity
  • synonym: traditional - following established customs or practices, especially in worship
