looby

noun
clumsy person
Pronunciation: / ˈluːbi / (AmE), / ˈluːbi / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a clumsy or foolish person

Examples

A clumsy looby
He stumbled into the room like a looby, knocking over a chair.
They laughed at the looby who spilled soup all over himself.
In the old story, the looby always made silly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: oaf - a clumsy or unintelligent person
