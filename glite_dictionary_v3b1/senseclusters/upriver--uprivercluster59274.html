upriver

adverb
toward the source of a river
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ʌpˈrɪvə / (BrE)
in the direction toward the source of a river or against its current

Examples

Travel upriver
The boat traveled upriver for several miles before stopping to rest.
They paddled upriver against the strong current.
We spotted fishermen working upriver early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downriver - in the direction toward the mouth of a river or with its current
