water line

noun
mark showing ship's water level
3.2 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər laɪn/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər laɪn/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə laɪn/ (BrE)
a line showing the level reached by water, especially on a ship, wall, or other object

Examples

The water line on ship
After a week at sea, a clear water line appeared along the ship's hull.
Before each voyage, the crew checked the water line to ensure the vessel was not overloaded.

The old boathouse still displays the water line from last year's storm.
