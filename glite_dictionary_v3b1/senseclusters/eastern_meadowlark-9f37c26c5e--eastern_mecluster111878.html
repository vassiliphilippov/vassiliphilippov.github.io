eastern meadowlark

noun
a North American songbird species
Pronunciation: / ˈistərn ˈmɛdoʊˌlɑrk / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈmɛdəʊˌlɑːk / (BrE)
a medium-sized songbird of eastern North America, with bright yellow underparts and a distinctive black 'V' on its chest

Examples

The eastern meadowlark bird
Conservationists are concerned about the declining population of eastern meadowlarks due to habitat loss.
The eastern meadowlark is known for its cheerful, flute-like song.
We spotted an eastern meadowlark singing in the grassy field early this morning.
