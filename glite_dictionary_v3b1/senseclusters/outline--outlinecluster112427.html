outline

noun
outer edge or shape of an object

a line or shape that marks the outer edge of an object or figure

Examples

The outline of a tree
Only the faint outline of the boat could be seen in the fog.
She traced the outline of her hand on the paper.
The mountain's outline was visible against the evening sky.

Synonyms

  • contour
    4.5 6.65/m
    contour traces smooth curves that show an object's outer form in detail, while outline names the simple outer edge shape.
  • profile
    3.4 8.67/m
    profile shows a side view or cross section of something, often a face, while outline marks the general outer line.
  • silhouette
    4.2 6.70/m
    silhouette appears as a dark shape against light, showing a filled outer form, while outline names the drawn edge alone.
  • edge
    1.0 122/m
    edge is the physical border or pointy limit you touch on an object, while outline is the visible drawn outer line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contour - the outline representing or bounding the shape or form of something
  • synonym: silhouette - the dark shape and outline of someone or something visible against a lighter background
