Synonyms

  • contour
    4.5 1.0/m
    contour traces smooth curves that show an object's outer form in detail, while outline names the simple outer edge shape.
  • profile
    3.4 4/m
    profile shows a side view or cross section of something, often a face, while outline marks the general outer line.
  • silhouette
    4.2 3/m
    silhouette appears as a dark shape against light, showing a filled outer form, while outline names the drawn edge alone.
  • edge
    1.0 50/m
    edge is the physical border or pointy limit you touch on an object, while outline is the visible drawn outer line.
