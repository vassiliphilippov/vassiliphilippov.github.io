outline

noun
outer edge or shape of an object
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.laɪn / (BrE)
a line or shape that marks the outer edge of an object or figure

Examples

The outline of a tree
Only the faint outline of the boat could be seen in the fog.
She traced the outline of her hand on the paper.
The mountain's outline was visible against the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contour - the outline representing or bounding the shape or form of something
  • synonym: silhouette - the dark shape and outline of someone or something visible against a lighter background
