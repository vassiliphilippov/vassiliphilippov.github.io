carpal

adjective
of or relating to the wrist
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.pəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəl / (BrE)
relating to the wrist or the bones forming the wrist

Examples

Carpal tunnel syndrome
Carpal tunnel syndrome is a common condition affecting the hands and wrists.
The doctor explained that the pain was due to inflammation of the carpal bones.
X-rays revealed a fracture in one of the carpal bones after the accident.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tarsal - relating to the ankle or bones forming the ankle
