kyr

noun
thousand-year period
9.6
Pronunciation: / kiː waɪ ɑːr / (AmE), / kiː waɪ ɑː / (BrE)
a unit of time equal to one thousand years, commonly used in geology and astronomy

Examples

Over 20 kyr
The ice core samples date back over 20 kyr.
Scientists estimate the eruption occurred around 75 kyr ago.
This cave painting is believed to be at least 30 kyr old.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millennium - a period of one thousand years
  • degree higher: myr - a unit of time equal to one million years
