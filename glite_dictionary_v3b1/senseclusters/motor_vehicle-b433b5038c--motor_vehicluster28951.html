motor vehicle

noun
road vehicle powered by an engine
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊt̬ɚ ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ˈviːɪkəl / (BrE)
A road vehicle powered by an engine, especially one that does not run on rails, such as a car, bus, or truck.

Examples

A motor vehicle engine
Electric cars are a type of motor vehicle that helps reduce pollution.
It is illegal to operate a motor vehicle without a valid driver's license.
The city has seen a sharp rise in motor vehicle thefts this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automobile - a passenger vehicle powered by an engine, typically with four wheels
