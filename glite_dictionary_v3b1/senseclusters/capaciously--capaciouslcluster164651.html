capaciously

adverb
broadly or inclusively
7.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈpeɪ.ʃəs.liː/ (AmE), /kə.ˈpeɪ.ʃəs.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows broad or comprehensive mental or emotional capacity; with openness or inclusiveness.

Examples

Capaciously considers ideas
She approached the complex issue capaciously, considering every possible perspective before making a decision.
The committee listened capaciously to all suggestions, ensuring everyone felt heard.
He responded capaciously to criticism, showing understanding and acceptance of different viewpoints.
