gastroplasty

noun
stomach reduction surgery
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæstroʊˌplæsti / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrəʊˌplæsti / (BrE)
a surgical procedure that reduces the size of the stomach to help with weight loss

Examples

Weight loss gastroplasty
After her gastroplasty, she had to follow a special diet.
The doctor recommended gastroplasty for patients who had not succeeded with other weight loss methods.
Recovery from gastroplasty usually requires careful monitoring and lifestyle changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bariatric surgery - surgery performed to help with weight loss by altering the digestive system
  • synonym: gastric bypass - a surgical procedure that creates a small stomach pouch and reroutes the intestines
