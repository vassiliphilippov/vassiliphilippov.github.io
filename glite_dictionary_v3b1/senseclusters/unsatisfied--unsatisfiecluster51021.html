unsatisfied

adjective
worried or uneasy due to unmet needs
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsætɪsfaɪd /; / ˌʌnˈsætɪsˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsætɪsfaɪd /; / ˌʌnˈsætɪsfaɪd / (BrE)
not having your needs, desires, or expectations fulfilled; feeling uneasy or restless because of this

Examples

An unsatisfied mind
An unsatisfied mind is often troubled by doubt and worry.
Despite his success, he felt unsatisfied and restless, always looking for something more.
She was unsatisfied with the explanation and remained uneasy about the outcome.
Despite all his achievements, he still felt unsatisfied with his career.
Many customers remained unsatisfied after the issue was not resolved.
The children were unsatisfied with how little time they had to play.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contented - feeling satisfied and at ease
  • synonym: dissatisfied - feeling discontented or displeased
  • antonym: satisfied - having one's needs or desires fulfilled
