stalklike

adjective
resembling a stalk
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstɔːkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the form or characteristics of a stalk

Examples

Stalklike shape
The flower's stalklike base helped it stand tall above the grass.
Some mushrooms have a stalklike structure supporting the cap.
The insect's legs were thin and stalklike, perfect for moving through tall plants.
