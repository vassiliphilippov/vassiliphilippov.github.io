garment cutter

noun
person who cuts fabric for clothing
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrmənt ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːmənt ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to cut fabric according to measurements for making garments.

Examples

A garment cutter cuts fabric
A good garment cutter is essential for ensuring that suits fit perfectly.
Many aspiring designers start their careers as a garment cutter in clothing factories.
The garment cutter skillfully laid out the fabric before making precise cuts.
