gesticulation

noun
deliberate expressive gesture
Pronunciation: / dʒəˌstɪkjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒɛˌstɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a deliberate and expressive gesture, especially when speaking or to emphasize a point

Examples

Expressive gesticulation
His excited gesticulation made it clear he was passionate about the topic.
She punctuated her speech with expressive gesticulation to capture the audience's attention.
The actor's animated gesticulation brought the character to life on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gesture - a movement of part of the body, especially a hand or the head, to express an idea or meaning
