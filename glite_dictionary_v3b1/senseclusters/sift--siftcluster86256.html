sift

verb
separate with a sieve
8.1
Pronunciation: / sɪft / (AmE), / sɪft / (BrE)
to put a substance through a sieve or similar device to separate finer parts from coarser ones

Examples

To sift flour
Please sift the flour before adding it to the batter.
She sifts powdered sugar over the cake as a finishing touch.
The archaeologists carefully sifted the soil to find ancient artifacts.

Related Terms
