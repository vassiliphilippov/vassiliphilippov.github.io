Synonyms

  • fall back

    fall back is a planned withdrawal to a safer or lower position in tactics or sport, while drop back can be deliberate or caused by losing ground.
  • fall behind
    3.5 0.08/m
    fall behind is to lose progress when others move ahead, while drop back can mean choosing a lower position or simply losing ground.
