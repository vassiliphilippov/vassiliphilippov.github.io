proprietarian

noun
private property advocate
9.9
Pronunciation: / prəˌpraɪəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / prəˌpraɪəˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
a person who believes that private property rights are fundamental to the structure of society

Examples

A proprietarian thinker
As a committed proprietarian, she believed that property rights should be protected above all else.
The debate featured a proprietarian and a collectivist discussing the role of ownership in society.
Many proprietarians argue that economic freedom depends on secure property rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collectivist - a person who prioritizes group ownership or collective control over property
  • synonym: libertarian - a person who advocates for individual freedom and minimal government intervention
