Duluth

proper noun
city in Minnesota on Lake Superior
Pronunciation: / dəˈluːθ / (AmE), / dəˈluːθ / (BrE)
a city in northeast Minnesota, United States, located on the western shore of Lake Superior

Examples

Duluth city Minnesota
