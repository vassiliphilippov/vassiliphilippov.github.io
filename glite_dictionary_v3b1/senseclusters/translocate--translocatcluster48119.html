translocate

verb
to move within a system
7.5
Pronunciation: /træn.ˈsloʊ.keɪt/ (AmE), /træn.ˈsləʊ.keɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

Certain diseases can cause nutrients to translocate abnormally within the body.
Researchers are studying how bacteria translocate across cell membranes.
