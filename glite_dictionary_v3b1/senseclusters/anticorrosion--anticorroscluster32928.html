anticorrosion

adjective
prevents corrosion
Pronunciation: / ˌæntikəˈroʊʒən / (AmE), / ˌæntikəˈrəʊʒən / (BrE)
designed to prevent or reduce damage caused by rust or corrosion

Examples

Anticorrosion coating
The metal beams were treated with an anticorrosion coating to ensure durability.
Engineers recommend using anticorrosion paint on outdoor structures exposed to moisture.
This anticorrosion technology helps protect vehicles from rust in harsh climates.
