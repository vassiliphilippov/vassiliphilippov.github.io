Dormition

noun
Eastern Orthodox celebration of Mary's death and assumption
9.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / d…îÀêrÀàm…™ É…ôn / (AmE), / d…îÀêÀàm…™ É…ôn / (BrE)
The celebration in the Eastern Orthodox Church of the Virgin Mary's passing from earthly life and her being taken up into heaven, commemorated on August 15th and corresponding to the Assumption in the Roman Catholic Church.

Examples

The Dormition feast
Icons depicting the Dormition often show the apostles gathered around the Virgin Mary.
Many pilgrims travel to Jerusalem for the Feast of the Dormition on August 15th.
The Dormition is celebrated every year with special liturgies in Orthodox churches.

Synonyms

  • Feast of Dormition
    9.9 0.00/m
    Feast of Dormition is the common church name for the August 15 celebration, while Dormition is the shorter term for that event.
