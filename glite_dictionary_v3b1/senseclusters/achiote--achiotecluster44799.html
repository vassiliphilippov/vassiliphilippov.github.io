achiote

noun
tropical dye plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːtʃiˈoʊteɪ / (AmE), / ˌætʃiˈəʊti / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree (Bixa orellana) whose seeds are used to produce annatto, a red pigment and spice

Examples

Achiote plant seeds
The bright red seeds of the achiote are often used to color and flavor traditional dishes.
Farmers tended the achiote plants carefully to produce high-quality seeds.
In some regions, achiote is also used as a natural dye for fabrics and cosmetics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annatto - a red-orange food coloring and flavoring made from achiote seeds
