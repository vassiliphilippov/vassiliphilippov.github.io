happy hour

noun
time with reduced drink prices in bars
4.4 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈhæp.iː aʊr/ /; / /ˈhæp.iː ˈaʊ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈhæp.iː ˈaʊ.ə/ (BrE)
a period of time, usually in the late afternoon or early evening, when a bar or restaurant offers drinks (and sometimes food) at reduced prices

Examples

We often go to the pub for happy hour on Fridays after work.
