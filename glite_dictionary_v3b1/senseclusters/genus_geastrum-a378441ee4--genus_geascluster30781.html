genus Geastrum

noun
type genus of Geastraceae fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈʤiː.æstrəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈdʒiː.ɑːstrəm / (BrE)
the type genus of the family Geastraceae, consisting of fungi whose outer layer splits into star-shaped segments when dry

Examples

Genus Geastrum fungi
Members of the genus Geastrum are often found in woodland areas on decaying wood.
