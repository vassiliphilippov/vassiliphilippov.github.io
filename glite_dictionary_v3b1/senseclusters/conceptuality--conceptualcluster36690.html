conceptuality

noun
quality of being conceptual
1.0 7.1
Pronunciation: /kən.sep.tʃuː.ˈæl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /kən.sep.tʃuː.ˈæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
