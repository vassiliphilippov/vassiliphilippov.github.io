newsprint

noun
cheap paper used for newspapers
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌprɪnt / (BrE)
cheap, relatively low-quality paper made chiefly from wood pulp, used for printing newspapers.

Examples

Cheap newsprint paper
Old newspapers printed on newsprint tend to yellow and become brittle over time.
She wrapped the fragile vase carefully with sheets of newsprint before packing it.
The presses were running low on newsprint during the strike.
