neutral

noun
someone not taking sides in a conflict
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtrəl / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəl / (BrE)
a person, country, or group that does not take sides in a conflict or dispute

Examples

A neutral in a conflict
As a neutral, she was asked to mediate the disagreement between the two teams.
Switzerland remained a neutral during the world wars.
The conference included representatives from all sides, including several neutrals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: belligerent - a person, country, or group engaged in conflict
  • synonym: pacifist - someone who opposes war or violence
