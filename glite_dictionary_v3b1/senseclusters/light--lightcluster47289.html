light

adjective
sexually unrestrained; promiscuous
6.9
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a woman) behaving in a sexually unrestrained or promiscuous way.

Examples

Light company
He warned his son not to be led astray by light company at the tavern.
In Victorian novels, a light woman was often portrayed as a cautionary figure.
She was unfairly judged by the townspeople as a light character because of her friendships.
