dysmorphia

noun
body shape abnormality
9.6
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈmɔrfiə / (AmE), / dɪsˈmɔːfiə / (BrE)
a physical malformation or abnormality in the shape or structure of a part of the body, often present from birth

Examples

Facial dysmorphia
The doctor noticed signs of dysmorphia in the patient's facial features during the examination.
Genetic disorders can sometimes result in dysmorphia affecting the limbs or skull.
Early detection of dysmorphia is important for planning appropriate medical care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deformity - a permanent structural deviation from the normal shape
  • synonym: malformation - an abnormal or faulty formation of a body part
