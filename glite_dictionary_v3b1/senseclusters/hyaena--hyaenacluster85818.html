hyaena

noun
alternative spelling of hyena (carnivorous mammal)
Pronunciation: / haɪˈinə / (AmE), / haɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
An alternative spelling of hyena, a doglike nocturnal mammal of Africa and southern Asia that feeds chiefly on carrion.

Examples

A hyaena pack
Researchers observed the hyaena pack feeding at dusk near the riverbank.
The spotted hyaena is one of the most common large carnivores in Africa.
Unlike lions, hyaenas are primarily scavengers but can also hunt in groups.
