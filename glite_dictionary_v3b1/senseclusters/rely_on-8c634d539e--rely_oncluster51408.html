rely on

verb
depend on for support or help
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlaɪ ɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪ ɒn / (BrE)
to depend on or trust someone or something for help, support, or to do what is expected

Examples

To rely on help
Farmers often rely on rain to grow their crops.
Many people rely on their smartphones for navigation every day.
You can always rely on her to keep your secrets.
Many students rely on scholarships to help pay for their education.
When faced with difficulties, I know I can rely on my closest friends.
You shouldn't rely on public transport if you need to arrive on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trust - to believe in the reliability or honesty of someone or something
