Synonyms

  • depend on
    1.0 24/m
    depend on means needing something for support or survival, while rely on means trusting someone to help or do what is expected.
  • count on
    1.8 20/m
    count on is a casual phrase meaning you expect someone to help, while rely on is more neutral and often used widely.
  • trust
    1.0 109/m
    trust means believing in someone's honesty or ability, while rely on focuses on depending on them for help or specific action.
  • look to
    3.1 9.16/m

  • rest on
    3.7 9.41/m
