Endymion

proper noun
mythical sleeping youth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈdɪmiən / (AmE), / ɛnˈdɪmiən / (BrE)
a figure in Greek mythology famous for his eternal sleep and extraordinary beauty

Examples

Sleeping Endymion
The poet described the sleeping youth as another Endymion lost in dreams.
In the myth, Selene fell in love with Endymion and watched over him as he slept.
Artists often depict Endymion lying peacefully beneath the moonlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aurora - the Roman goddess of dawn, associated with love of a mortal youth
  • synonym: Narcissus - a beautiful youth in Greek mythology who fell in love with his own reflection
