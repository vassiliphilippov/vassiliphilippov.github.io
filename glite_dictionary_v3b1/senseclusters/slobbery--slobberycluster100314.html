slobbery

adjective
overly sentimental
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑbəri / (AmE), / ˈslɒbəri / (BrE)
excessively sentimental or emotional in a way that seems insincere or overly dramatic

Examples

Slobbery love scene
The movie's slobbery ending made some viewers roll their eyes at the exaggerated emotions.
He wrote a slobbery letter filled with dramatic declarations of love.
Critics found the speech too slobbery to be taken seriously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mawkish - excessively sentimental or emotional
  • antonym: restrained - showing control and not expressing strong emotions
  • synonym: sentimental - showing or causing a lot of emotion, especially in an exaggerated way
