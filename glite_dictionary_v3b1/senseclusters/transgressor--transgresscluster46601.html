transgressor

noun
person who violates rules or laws
Pronunciation: / trænzˈɡrɛsər / (AmE), / trænzˈɡresə / (BrE)
a person who violates a law, command, or moral code

Examples

A transgressor of rules
As a first-time transgressor, he received a lighter sentence than a repeat offender.
Religious texts often warn of the consequences awaiting the transgressor in the afterlife.
The transgressor was judged harshly by the community for breaking the sacred rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - person who has committed a crime
  • antonym: law-abider - person who obeys the law
  • synonym: offender - person who commits an illegal act
