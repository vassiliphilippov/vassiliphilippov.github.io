subcritical

adjective
below critical threshold
7.4
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /sʌb.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /səb.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /sʌb.ˈkrɪ.tɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
below the level or threshold required to sustain a chain reaction, especially in nuclear physics

Examples

Subcritical reactor
The nuclear reactor was kept subcritical to ensure there was no risk of an uncontrolled reaction.
Engineers monitor the system closely to maintain a subcritical state during testing.
A subcritical mass of uranium will not start a self-sustaining chain reaction.
