ohmic

adjective
relating to ohms or Ohm's law
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.mɪk / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to or measured in ohms, or behaving according to Ohm's law.

Examples

Ohmic resistance
Careful measurement revealed a change in the ohmic resistance of the sample.
Only ohmic contacts ensure accurate current readings in electronic circuits.
The material shows ohmic behavior over a wide range of voltages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resistive - having electrical resistance; relating to resistance
