polluter

noun
person or group causing environmental pollution
5.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈluː.tər/ (AmE), /pə.ˈluː.tə/ (BrE)
a person or organization that causes pollution of the environment, especially by releasing harmful substances into air, water, or soil

Examples

A polluter of air
As an individual polluter, everyone has a role to play in protecting the environment.
Governments are enacting stricter laws to penalize corporate polluters.
The factory was identified as the biggest polluter in the region by the environmental agency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleaner - a person or thing that makes something clean, opposite of polluter
  • synonym: pollute - to make air, water, or soil dirty or harmful
