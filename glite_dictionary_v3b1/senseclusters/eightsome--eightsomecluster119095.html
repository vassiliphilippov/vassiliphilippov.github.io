eightsome

noun
Scottish dance for eight people
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪtsəm / (AmE), / ˈeɪtsəm / (BrE)
a Scottish reel performed by eight dancers, often as a traditional group dance

Examples

Scottish eightsome dance
During the wedding, the guests joined in a lively eightsome that lasted almost half an hour.
He practiced the eightsome steps in preparation for the upcoming folk festival.
The band started playing an eightsome at the ceilidh, inviting everyone to the dance floor.
