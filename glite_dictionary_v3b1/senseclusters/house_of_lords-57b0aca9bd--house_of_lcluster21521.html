House of Lords

noun
upper chamber of UK Parliament
4.8 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àåha äs …ôv Ààl…îrdz / (AmE), / Àåha äs …ôv Ààl…îÀêdz / (BrE)
the upper chamber of the British Parliament, composed of appointed, hereditary, and clergy members, responsible for reviewing and revising legislation passed by the House of Commons

Examples
