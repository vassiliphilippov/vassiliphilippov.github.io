metafictive

adjective
self-referential fiction
9.9
Pronunciation: /me.tə.ˈfɪk.tɪv/ (AmE), /me.tə.ˈfɪk.tɪv/ (BrE)
describing a work of fiction that is self-aware and draws attention to its own fictional nature or the process of storytelling
