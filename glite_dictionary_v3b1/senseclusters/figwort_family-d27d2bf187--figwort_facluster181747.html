figwort family

noun
family of flowering plants (includes figwort, snapdragon, foxglove, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡˌwɜrt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡwɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (formerly Scrophulariaceae) that includes figworts, snapdragons, foxgloves, toadflaxes, speedwells, and mulleins; classification may vary in some taxonomies
