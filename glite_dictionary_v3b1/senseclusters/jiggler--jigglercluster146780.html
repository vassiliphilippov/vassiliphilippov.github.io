jiggler

noun
lock-opening tool
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪɡlər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡlə / (BrE)
a small tool or key used to open a lock by moving its internal parts, often by wiggling or jiggling it inside the lock

Examples

He kept a jiggler in his toolkit for emergency lockouts.
A skilled thief might carry a jiggler to bypass simple locks.
