droppage

noun
discarded debris
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpɪdʒ / (BrE)
items or debris that have been dropped or discarded, especially as waste left behind in a place

Examples

Festival droppage
After the festival, volunteers picked up all the droppage left on the field.
The park was much cleaner once the droppage from the picnic was removed.
Please dispose of your trash properly to avoid creating unnecessary droppage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanliness - the state of being clean and free from waste
  • synonym: debris - scattered pieces of waste or remains
  • synonym: litter - waste or trash left in public places
