rag

noun
a (tabloid) newspaper, often low in quality
Pronunciation: / ræɡ / (AmE), / ræɡ / (BrE)
a newspaper, especially one regarded as low in quality or with half-size pages; often used disparagingly

Examples

A gossip rag
He reads the rag every morning on his commute, despite its sensational headlines.
Many people prefer a reputable paper over a cheap rag filled with flashy stories.
That celebrity gossip rag is notorious for spreading rumors and scandals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tabloid - a newspaper with pages half the size of those of a standard newspaper
