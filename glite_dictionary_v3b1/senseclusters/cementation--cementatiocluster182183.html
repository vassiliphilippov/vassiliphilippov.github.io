cementation

noun
bonding with cement
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːmɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsiːmɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of bonding building materials together using cement, mortar, or another adhesive substance

Examples

Cementation of tiles
The cementation of the tiles ensured the floor would last for many years.
Proper cementation is essential for the stability of brick walls in construction.
After the cementation process, the stones were firmly held together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adhesion - the action of sticking or holding together
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
