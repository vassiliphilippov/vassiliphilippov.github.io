junta

noun
military group ruling after a coup
8.1 1.71/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆhÊŠn.tÉ™ / (AmE), / ËˆdÊ’ÊŒn.tÉ™ / (BrE)
A group of military officers who take control of a government, especially after seizing power by force.

Examples

Military junta
After the coup, the junta imposed strict martial law throughout the country.
Many citizens fled the country when the junta came to power.
The military junta was condemned internationally for its human rights violations.

Synonyms

  • military junta
    8.5 0.34/m
    military junta describes armed officers who seize and rule a country, while junta is the shorter word used in news and speech.
  • military government
    5.6 1.58/m
    military government is a state run by the armed forces, often as an official administration, while junta emphasizes a small group seizing power.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democracy - a system of government by the people
  • synonym: regime - a government, especially an authoritarian one
