genotyping

noun
analyzing genetic makeup
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəˌtaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəʊˌtaɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of determining the genetic makeup of an individual or sample by examining its DNA
