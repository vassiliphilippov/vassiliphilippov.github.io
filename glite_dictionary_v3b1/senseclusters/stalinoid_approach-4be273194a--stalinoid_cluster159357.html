Stalinoid approach

noun
authoritarian leadership style like Stalin's
7.3 9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑː.lɪ.nɔɪd ə.ˈproʊtʃ/ /; / /ˈstæl.ɪ.nɔɪd ə.ˈproʊtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.lɪ.nɔɪd ə.ˈprəʊtʃ/ /; / /ˈstæl.ɪ.nɔɪd ə.ˈprəʊtʃ/ (BrE)
