whoever

pronoun
any person who
1.0
Pronunciation: / huːˈɛvər / (AmE), / huːˈɛvə / (BrE)
any person who; no matter who is involved or does something

Examples

Whoever wants to join
Whoever wants to join the club should sign up by Friday.
You can give this ticket to whoever needs it most.
Whoever called last night did not leave a message.
