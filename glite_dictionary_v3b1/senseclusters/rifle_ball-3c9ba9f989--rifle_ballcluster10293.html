rifle ball

noun
bullet for a rifle (historically spherical)
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪfəl bɔl / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl bɔːl / (BrE)
a bullet designed to be fired from a rifle, originally spherical but later made elongated
