jumpiness

noun
nervousness or being easily startled
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪnɪs / (BrE)
a state of nervousness or being easily startled, often with anxious feelings

Examples

His jumpiness increased
After hearing about the earthquake, his jumpiness increased every time the floor creaked.
Her jumpiness made it hard for her to concentrate during the exam.
The doctor's calm voice helped to reduce the patient's jumpiness before the operation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being calm and relaxed
  • synonym: nervousness - the quality of being nervous or anxious
