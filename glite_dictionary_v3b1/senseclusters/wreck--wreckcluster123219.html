wreck

noun
serious vehicle accident
1.9
Pronunciation: / rɛk / (AmE), / rɛk / (BrE)
a serious accident, especially one involving vehicles such as cars, trains, or planes

Examples

A car wreck
He suffered minor injuries in yesterday's train wreck but is expected to recover fully.
Investigators arrived at the scene of the plane wreck early this morning.
The highway was closed for hours after the massive wreck involving five cars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collision - an event in which two or more objects hit each other violently
  • synonym: crash - a sudden and violent accident, especially between vehicles
