wrapped up

adjective
deeply absorbed in something
2.2
Pronunciation: / ræpt ʌp / (AmE), / ræpt ʌp / (BrE)
deeply involved or absorbed in something, often with strong interest or devotion

Examples

To be wrapped up in work
He's so wrapped up in his work that he barely notices the time passing.
It's easy to get wrapped up in daily routines and forget to relax.
She became wrapped up in her studies and stopped going out with friends.
