Bignonia

noun
a genus of tropical climbing plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪɡˈnoʊniə / (AmE), / bɪɡˈnəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical American climbing plants in the family Bignoniaceae, often known for their large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers; sometimes used specifically for the cross vine
