A nuclear weapon that releases a massive amount of energy through the process of nuclear fission, usually involving uranium-235 or plutonium-239.

Examples

The atom bomb explosion
Scientists debated the ethical implications of using the atom bomb in warfare.
The city was destroyed when an atom bomb was dropped in 1945.
The film explored the aftermath of an atom bomb explosion.

Synonyms

  • atomic bomb
    2.3 2.88/m
    atomic bomb is the usual technical term for a fission weapon in books and news, while atom bomb sounds like a variant.
  • fission bomb
    6.7 0.10/m
    fission bomb highlights splitting nuclei and the technical process used, while atom bomb refers to the same fission weapon more generally.
  • A-bomb
    3.0 1.08/m
    A-bomb is an informal short name often used in speech and headlines, while atom bomb is the full formal term.
