rollingly

adverb
with rolling motion
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.lɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ˈroʊ.lɪŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.lɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ˈrəʊ.lɪŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples
