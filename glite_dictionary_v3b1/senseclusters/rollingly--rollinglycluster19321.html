rollingly

adverb
with rolling motion
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a rolling, smooth, or undulating manner (of movement, landscape, sound, or speech)

Examples

Hills rise rollingly
The green hills rose rollingly toward the distant mountains.
Clouds drifted rollingly across the sky as the wind picked up.
The river flowed rollingly through the valley, curving around the trees.
The river flowed rollingly through the valley, creating a peaceful atmosphere.
She spoke rollingly, her voice rising and falling like gentle waves.
The music played rollingly, filling the hall with a soothing rhythm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jerkily - in a way that moves with sudden, uneven motions
  • antonym: monotonously - in a dull, unvarying way
  • synonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without sudden changes
