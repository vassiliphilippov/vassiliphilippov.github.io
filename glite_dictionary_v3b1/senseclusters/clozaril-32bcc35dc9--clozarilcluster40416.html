Clozaril

noun
antipsychotic drug (trade name)
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzəˌrɪl / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzərɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked antipsychotic drug (clozapine) used as a sedative and for treatment-resistant schizophrenia.

The psychiatrist prescribed Clozaril to help control his schizophrenia symptoms.
