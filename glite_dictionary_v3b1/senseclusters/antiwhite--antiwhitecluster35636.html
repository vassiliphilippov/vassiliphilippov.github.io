antiwhite

noun
person hostile to whites
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈwaɪt / (BrE)
a person who is hostile to or prejudiced against white people

Examples

An antiwhite activist
He was labeled an antiwhite after making several derogatory remarks about white people.
The article accused certain activists of being antiwhite in their rhetoric.
Some online communities ban users for posting antiwhite comments or hate speech.
