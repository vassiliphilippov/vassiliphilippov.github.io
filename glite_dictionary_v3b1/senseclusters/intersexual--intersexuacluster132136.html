intersexual

adjective
having intermediate male and female sexual characteristics
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
Having sexual characteristics that are intermediate between those typically associated with male and female.

Examples

Intersexual traits
Medical practitioners are trained to understand intersexual conditions and provide appropriate care.
Some plants are classified as intersexual because they display both male and female traits.
The researchers studied the genetics of intersexual fish in polluted rivers.
