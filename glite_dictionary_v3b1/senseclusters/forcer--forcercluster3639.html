forcer

noun
person or device applying force
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrsər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːsə / (BrE)
a person or device that applies force to make something happen or to move or open something

Examples

A forcer tool
The mechanic used a forcer to open the stuck valve.
A forcer can help when a door is jammed and will not budge.
The technician reached for a forcer to loosen the tight fitting.
The hydraulic forcer was used to open the stuck door.
As a forcer of change, she never accepted the status quo.
The machine acts as a forcer to push the material through the mold.

  • synonym: opener - a tool or device used to open something
  • antonym: preventer - a person or thing that stops something from happening
  • synonym: tool - a device used to carry out a particular function
