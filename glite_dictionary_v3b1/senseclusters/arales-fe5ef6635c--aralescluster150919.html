Arales

noun
an order of flowering plants
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈreɪ.liːz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈreɪ.liːz/ (BrE)
An order of flowering plants that includes families such as Araceae (the arum family) and Lemnaceae (the duckweed family).

Examples

The order Arales
Duckweed, a small floating aquatic plant, belongs to the order Arales.
Recent studies have revised the classification of some genera within the Arales.
The family Araceae, which contains many popular houseplants, is classified under Arales.
