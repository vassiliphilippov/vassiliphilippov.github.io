mgr

noun
Catholic clerical title
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛm dʒi ɑr / (AmE), / ɛm dʒi ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'Monseigneur' or 'Monsignor', a title used before the name of certain Catholic bishops or high-ranking priests

Examples

Mgr Dupont
The letter was addressed to mgr Dupont at the cathedral.
During the ceremony, mgr Rossi delivered a moving sermon to the congregation.
Everyone stood as mgr Smith entered the church to begin the service.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bishop - a senior member of the Christian clergy, usually in charge of a diocese
  • degree lower: father - a title used for Catholic priests
  • synonym: monsignor - a title of honor for certain high-ranking Catholic priests
