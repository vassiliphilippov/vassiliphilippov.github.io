speedwalk

noun
fast-paced walking
3.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàspiÀêdw…îk / (AmE), / ÀàspiÀêdw…îÀêk / (BrE)
a form of walking for exercise or sport at a pace faster than normal walking but slower than running

Examples

A daily speedwalk
He goes for a speedwalk every evening to improve his fitness.
During her lunch break, she enjoys a quick speedwalk around the park.
A daily speedwalk can help reduce stress and boost energy levels.

Synonyms

  • power walking
    3.2 0.10/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: power walk - brisk walking for exercise with vigorous arm movement
  • degree higher: run - to move at a speed faster than walking by lifting both feet off the ground
  • degree lower: walk - to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
