bok

noun
male goat
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑk / (AmE), / bɒk / (BrE)
a male goat, especially as used in South African English and derived from Dutch and Afrikaans

Examples

A bok leads goats
The farmer kept a large bok to lead the herd in the pasture.
During the festival, children watched the bok climb the rocky hillside.
In some regions, a bok is prized for its strong horns and leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billy goat - an adult male goat
  • antonym: nanny goat - an adult female goat
