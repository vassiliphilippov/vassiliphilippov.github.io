homecoming

noun
the act of returning home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of returning to one's home or to a place where one previously lived or belonged, especially after being away for a period of time.

Examples

A soldier's homecoming
After graduating, his homecoming to his hometown was filled with mixed feelings.
She organized a small party to celebrate her brother's homecoming after a year abroad.
The soldier's homecoming was an emotional event for the whole family.
