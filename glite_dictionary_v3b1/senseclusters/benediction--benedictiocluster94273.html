benediction

noun
prayer asking for divine blessing
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnəˈdɪkʃən /; / ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
a prayer or blessing, especially at the end of a religious service, asking for God's help or protection

Examples

A benediction prayer
After the wedding ceremony, the minister offered a benediction to the couple.
The priest ended the service with a heartfelt benediction.
We bowed our heads as the benediction was pronounced.
At the conclusion of the ceremony, the priest gave a benediction to all attendees.
She bowed her head during the benediction, feeling a deep sense of peace.
The bishop delivered a special benediction before everyone left the church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blessing - a prayer or words asking for divine favor
  • antonym: malediction - a curse, a prayer for harm
