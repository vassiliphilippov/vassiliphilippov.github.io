supersensuous

adjective
beyond sensory experience
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåsuÀêp…ôrÀàs…õn Éu…ôs / (AmE), / ÀåsuÀêp…ôÀàs…õn Éu…ôs / (BrE)
beyond or exceeding what can be perceived by the senses; not accessible to ordinary physical experience

Examples

The supersensuous world
The philosopher discussed the possibility of supersensuous realities that cannot be seen or touched.
Some spiritual traditions believe in supersensuous knowledge that comes from intuition rather than the senses.
The novel describes a supersensuous world that exists beyond human perception.
