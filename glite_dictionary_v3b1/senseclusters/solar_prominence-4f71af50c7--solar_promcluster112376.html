solar prominence

noun
eruption of gas above the Sun's surface
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˈprɑːmənəns / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈprɒmɪnəns / (BrE)
a large, bright feature extending outward from the Sun's surface, consisting of luminous hydrogen gas that can rise thousands of kilometers above the chromosphere.

Examples

A solar prominence rises
A spectacular solar prominence could be seen during the recent solar eclipse.
Scientists study solar prominences to better understand the dynamics of the sun's magnetic field.
The astronomer observed a massive solar prominence arching above the sun's surface through the telescope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solar flare - a sudden, intense burst of radiation from the Sun's surface
