glazer

noun
person who cuts and fits glass
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪzə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut and fit flat glass for doors, windows, and other installations

Examples

A glazer replaces glass
After the storm, we had to call a glazer to replace the broken pane.
As a skilled glazer, she can shape and install all types of architectural glass.
The glazer carefully measured the window before cutting the glass to fit.

Related Terms
