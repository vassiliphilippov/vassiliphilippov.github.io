pseudarthrosis

noun
false bone joint
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdɑːrˈθroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdɑːˈθrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a condition in which a broken bone does not heal properly and forms a false joint

Examples

Bone pseudarthrosis
After the accident, the patient developed pseudarthrosis in his leg, requiring further surgery.
Doctors diagnosed pseudarthrosis when the bone failed to heal and formed a movable false joint.
Treatment for pseudarthrosis often involves bone grafting to encourage proper healing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fracture - a break or crack in a bone
  • synonym: nonunion - failure of a fractured bone to heal
