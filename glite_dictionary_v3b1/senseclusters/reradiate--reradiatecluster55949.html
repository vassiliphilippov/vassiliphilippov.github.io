reradiate

verb
to retransmit energy
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈreɪdieɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈreɪdieɪt / (BrE)
to receive a signal, energy, or radiation and transmit it again, often to extend its range or effect

Examples

To reradiate a signal
The satellite will reradiate the television signal to remote areas.
Certain materials can reradiate heat absorbed from the sun during the day.
The relay tower is designed to reradiate emergency broadcasts across the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absorb - to take in energy or a signal without sending it out again
  • synonym: retransmit - to send a signal or message again
