pine for

verb
long for someone or something
9.3
Pronunciation: / paɪn fɔr / (AmE), / paɪn fə / (BrE)
to feel a deep longing or desire for someone or something, often with sadness

Examples

To pine for a person
After her friend moved away, she couldn't help but pine for their time together.
He would often pine for his hometown when he felt lonely in the big city.
In the winter, many people pine for the warmth and sunshine of summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miss - to feel sad due to the absence of someone or something
  • synonym: yearn for - to have an intense feeling of longing for something
