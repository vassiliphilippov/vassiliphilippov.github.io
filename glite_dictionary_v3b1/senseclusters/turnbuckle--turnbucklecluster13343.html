turnbuckle

noun
adjustable device for tensioning rods or cables
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnˌbʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌbʌkəl / (BrE)
a device consisting of a metal sleeve with internal threads, used to adjust the tension or length of rods, cables, or ropes by rotating the sleeve.

Examples

A turnbuckle device
Before raising the mast, check that every turnbuckle is properly set and locked.
Each suspension cable was fitted with a turnbuckle to allow for easy adjustments.
The contractor tightened the wire using a turnbuckle to ensure it was secure.
