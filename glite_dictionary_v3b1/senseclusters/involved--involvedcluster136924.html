involved

adjective
emotionally connected or affected
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
emotionally connected or affected; having a close personal interest or relationship with something or someone.

Examples

It's best not to become involved if you want to avoid getting hurt.
She tries not to get too involved with her clients to stay objective.
