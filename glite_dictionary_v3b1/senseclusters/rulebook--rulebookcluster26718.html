rulebook

noun
book of rules
3.3 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàruÀêlÀåb äk / (AmE), / ÀàruÀêlÀåb äk / (BrE)
a book or document that contains the official rules for a game, activity, or organization

Examples

The rulebook of games
Before joining the club, you should read the rulebook to understand all the requirements.
The referee checked the rulebook to resolve the dispute during the match.
Every new employee receives a rulebook outlining the company's policies and procedures.
Before starting the game, everyone read the rulebook to understand how to play.
The new manager decided to follow the rulebook strictly to avoid any mistakes.
Sometimes, innovation means ignoring the traditional rulebook and trying something new.
