magnetism

noun
science of magnetic phenomena
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡ.nəˌtɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.nə.tɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The branch of science concerned with the properties and effects of magnets and magnetic fields.

Examples

Magnetism research
He devoted his career to the study of magnetism and its effects on electrical devices.
Many basic physics courses cover electricity and magnetism as fundamental topics.
The field of magnetism saw rapid advances in the 19th century.
