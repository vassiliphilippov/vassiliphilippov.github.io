irregularity

noun
infrequent or difficult bowel evacuation (medical)
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærəti / (AmE), / ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarɪti / (BrE)
An infrequent or difficult evacuation of the bowels; a medical symptom suggestive of intestinal obstruction or related conditions.

Examples

Digestive irregularity
A high-fiber diet is recommended to reduce irregularity and promote regular bowel function.
Chronic irregularity can sometimes point to a more serious digestive issue.
The doctor asked if she had experienced any irregularity in her bowel movements lately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constipation - difficulty in emptying the bowels, often associated with hardened stool
