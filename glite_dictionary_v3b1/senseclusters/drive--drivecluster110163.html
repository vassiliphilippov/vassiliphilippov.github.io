drive

noun
journey in a car or vehicle
Pronunciation: / draɪv / (AmE), / draɪv / (BrE)
a journey in a vehicle, especially in a car, for pleasure or as transportation

Examples

A long drive
The drive to her parents' house takes about forty-five minutes.
We decided to go for a drive along the coast after lunch.
Yesterday's drive through the countryside was very relaxing.
