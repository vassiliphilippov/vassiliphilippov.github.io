minor

adjective
secondary academic specialization
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
relating to or designating a secondary field of academic concentration or specialization, as opposed to a major

Examples

A minor in history
He completed his degree with a minor in psychology.
Many students select a minor that complements their main area of study.
She chose history as her minor while majoring in biology.
