Examples

Unforgivably rude behavior
His actions were unforgivably rude and hurt many people at the meeting.
She arrived unforgivably late to the critical presentation, missing the main discussion.
The restaurant staff was unforgivably slow in bringing our food on a busy night.

Synonyms

  • unpardonably
    8.0 0.01/m
    unpardonably is a stronger, more formal word for actions beyond forgiveness, while unforgivably is more common in everyday speech.
  • inexcusably
    6.9 0.11/m
    inexcusably describes behavior that cannot be justified or excused, while unforgivably focuses on acts people cannot forgive.
