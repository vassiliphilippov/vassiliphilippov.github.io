schlieren

verb
to show density bands
Pronunciation: / ˈʃlɪrən / (AmE), / ˈʃlɪərən / (BrE)
to form or display streaks or bands caused by differences in density or refractive index, especially in fluids or gases.

Examples

Air schlieren above flame
The hot air above the candle began to schlieren, making the invisible flow visible in the experiment.
Engineers observed the exhaust gases schlieren as they passed through the test chamber.
When the liquid was heated unevenly, it started to schlieren and form visible bands.
