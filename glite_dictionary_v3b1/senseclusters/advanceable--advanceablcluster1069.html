advanceable

adjective
can be moved forward
6.1
Pronunciation: / ədˈvænsəbəl / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be moved forward in position or time

Examples

Advanceable meeting date
The meeting date is advanceable if everyone agrees to change it.
Some holidays are advanceable depending on the calendar year.
The payment is advanceable upon request from the client.

Related Terms

  • synonym: movable - able to be moved from one place to another
  • antonym: postponable - able to be delayed or moved to a later time
