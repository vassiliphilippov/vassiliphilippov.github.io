Bruxelles

proper noun
French name for Brussels, Belgium's capital
5.9
Pronunciation: / bʁyˈsɛl / (AmE), / bʁyˈsɛl / (BrE)
The French name for Brussels, the capital and largest city of Belgium; seat of the European Union and NATO.

Examples
