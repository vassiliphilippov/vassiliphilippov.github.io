icebound

adjective
trapped or surrounded by ice
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
Completely surrounded, trapped, or blocked by ice, especially referring to ships, harbors, or areas in cold regions.

Examples

An icebound ship
During winter, the harbor becomes icebound and inaccessible to most boats.
Explorers struggled to survive in the icebound wilderness of the Arctic.
The icebound ship waited desperately for a thaw to release it from the frozen sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frozen - made into ice or very cold, unable to move because of extreme cold
