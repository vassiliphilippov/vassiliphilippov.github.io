appointive

adjective
relating to appointment to a position
8.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpɔɪn.tɪv/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpɔɪn.tɪv/ (BrE)
relating to a position or office that is filled by appointment, not by election or hiring

The governor holds several appointive offices in addition to his elected position.
