Synonyms

  • embarrassedly
    4.6 0.05/m
    embarrassedly is used to show clear embarrassment or self-consciousness, while abashedly suggests a more shy or startled awkwardness.
  • shamefacedly
    7.9 0.06/m
    shamefacedly is old-fashioned and shows shame or embarrassment, while abashedly is a more general word for feeling awkward.
  • ashamedly
    6.8 0.03/m
    ashamedly expresses a stronger sense of moral shame or guilt, while abashedly refers to feeling embarrassed or uncomfortable.
  • sheepishly
    5.1 1.40/m
    sheepishly means shy and awkward after a mistake, while abashedly simply shows embarrassment or shame without that foolish tone.
  • bashfully
    3.5 0.18/m
    bashfully is shy and timid, often in a gentle or flirtatious way, while abashedly means embarrassed or ashamed.
  • blushingly
    5.8 0.03/m
    blushingly shows a red-faced reaction to embarrassment, while abashedly describes feeling embarrassed or ashamed more generally.
