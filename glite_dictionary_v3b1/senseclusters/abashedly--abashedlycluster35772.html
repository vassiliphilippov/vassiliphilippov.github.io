abashedly

adverb
embarrassed manner
Pronunciation: / əˈbæʃɪdli / (AmE), / əˈbæʃɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows embarrassment or shame

Examples

Speak abashedly
He spoke abashedly after realizing his mistake in front of the group.
She abashedly admitted that she had forgotten the meeting time.
The student looked abashedly at the floor when the teacher called his name.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidently - in a self-assured or bold way
  • synonym: sheepishly - in a shy or embarrassed way
