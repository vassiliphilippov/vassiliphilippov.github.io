embittered

adjective
having bitter taste
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɪtɚd / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɪtəd / (BrE)
having a bitter taste or made physically bitter, especially in reference to food or drink

Examples

Embittered flavor
The burnt herbs left the stew with an unpleasant, embittered taste that nobody enjoyed.
After simmering too long, the sauce became embittered and lost its original flavor.
The chef warned that overcooking the greens could make the dish embittered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitter - having a sharp, unpleasant taste
  • antonym: sweet - having a pleasant, sugary taste
