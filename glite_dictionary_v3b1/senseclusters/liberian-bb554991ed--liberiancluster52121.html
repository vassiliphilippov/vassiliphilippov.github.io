Liberian

proper noun
person from Liberia
Pronunciation: / laɪˈbɪriən / (AmE), / laɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
a person from Liberia or relating to Liberia, a country in West Africa

Examples

Liberian ambassador
The Liberian ambassador gave a speech at the United Nations.
Many Liberian families celebrate Independence Day with music and food.
A Liberian athlete won a medal at the international competition.
