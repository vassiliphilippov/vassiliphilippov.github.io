stylebook

noun
branding guidelines manual
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlˌbʊk / (BrE)
a manual that sets out the rules for an organization's visual identity, including logos, colors, and typography

Examples

Company stylebook
The marketing team referred to the stylebook to ensure all materials matched the company's visual identity.
Before designing the new website, she carefully reviewed the organization's stylebook for logo and color requirements.
Every department must follow the stylebook when creating official documents and presentations.
