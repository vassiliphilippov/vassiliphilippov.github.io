lancet

noun
small surgical knife
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlæn.sət / (AmE), / ˈlɑːn.sɪt / (BrE)
a small, sharp surgical knife with a pointed double-edged blade, used for making punctures or small incisions

Examples

A lancet blade
Before checking her blood sugar, she prepared a new lancet for the test.
Sterilizing the lancet is important to prevent infection during minor procedures.
The doctor used a lancet to make a small incision in the patient's skin.
