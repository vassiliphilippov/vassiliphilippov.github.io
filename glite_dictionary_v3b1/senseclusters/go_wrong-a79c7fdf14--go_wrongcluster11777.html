go wrong

verb
to fail or not succeed
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ rɔŋ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ rɒŋ / (BrE)
to not succeed or to fail; to develop or happen in an undesirable way

Examples

Plans go wrong
If our plans go wrong, we’ll have to find another solution.
No one knew exactly when things had gone wrong, but the project was failing.
Something seemed to go wrong with the computer during the presentation.
