candidness

noun
quality of being honest and open
Pronunciation: / ˈkændɪdnɪs / (AmE), / ˈkandɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being honest, open, and straightforward in speech or attitude

Examples

Her candidness in speech
Her candidness during the interview impressed the hiring panel.
His candidness sometimes surprises people who expect more guarded responses.
I appreciate your candidness about the challenges we might face.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candor - the quality of being open and honest; frankness
  • antonym: deceitfulness - the quality of being dishonest or misleading
