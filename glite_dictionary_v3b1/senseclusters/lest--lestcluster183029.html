lest

conjunction
to prevent something
6.8
Pronunciation: / lɛst / (AmE), / lɛst / (BrE)
so that something undesirable will not happen; for fear that

Examples

Leave early lest miss
She left early lest she miss the last train home.
Take your umbrella lest it rain while you are out.
He whispered lest anyone overhear their secret conversation.
