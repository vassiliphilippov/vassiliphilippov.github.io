noticer

noun
someone who notices things
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtɪsər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtɪsə / (BrE)
a person who notices or pays attention to something, especially details

Examples

A careful noticer
A good editor must be a keen noticer of inconsistencies in writing.
It takes a skilled noticer to appreciate the subtle changes in the artwork.
She is a noticer of the little things that others often miss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: observer - a person who watches or pays close attention to something
