chainring

noun
bicycle gear wheel
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪnˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪnˌrɪŋ / (BrE)
a round metal part or gear on a bicycle that the chain moves around to drive the wheel

Examples

A chainring gear
After replacing the old chainring, the bike rode much more smoothly.
He noticed the chainring was bent after the accident and needed to be fixed.
Dirt and grease can build up on the chainring if you do not clean your bike regularly.
He replaced the worn chainring on his bike to improve performance.
A larger chainring can make it harder to pedal uphill but faster on flat roads.
The mechanic checked the chainring for any bent teeth before the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sprocket - a toothed wheel that engages with a chain
