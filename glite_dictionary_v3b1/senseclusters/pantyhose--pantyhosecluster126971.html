pantyhose

noun
one-piece women's tights garment
Pronunciation: / ˈpæntiˌhoʊz / (AmE), / ˈpantiˌhəʊz / (BrE)
A close-fitting one-piece garment for women that covers the body from the waist to the toes, combining underwear and stockings.

Examples

A pair of pantyhose
In colder weather, pantyhose can provide extra warmth under skirts or dresses.
She wore a new pair of pantyhose to the office meeting.
The store sells pantyhose in a variety of colors and sizes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tights - a similar close-fitting garment covering the legs and sometimes the waist, often used interchangeably
