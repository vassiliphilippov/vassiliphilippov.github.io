mouth

noun
person to be provided for, consumer of food
Pronunciation: / maʊθ / (AmE), / maʊθ / (BrE)
a person regarded as a consumer of food, especially as a dependent to be provided for

Examples

Another mouth to feed
In times of famine, every mouth counts when dividing up what little food remains.
Once the war ended, there were fewer mouths to worry about at every meal.
With another baby on the way, that's one more mouth to feed.
