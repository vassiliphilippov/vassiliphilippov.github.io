Chinese goose

noun
large domestic goose breed from China
3.8 4.5
Pronunciation: /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz guːs/ /; / /tʃaɪ.ˈnɪz guːs/ (AmE), /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz guːs/ (BrE)
