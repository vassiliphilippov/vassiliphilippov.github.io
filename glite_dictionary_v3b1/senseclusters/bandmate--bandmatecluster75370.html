bandmate

noun
fellow band member
Pronunciation: / ˈbændˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈbændˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who is a member of the same musical band as you

Examples

Bandmate in a band
After the concert, her bandmate congratulated her on a great performance.
He has played guitar with the same bandmate for over ten years.
Each bandmate brings a unique style to their music.
After the concert, every bandmate celebrated their successful performance together.
She became close friends with her bandmate after joining the group last year.
Each bandmate contributed ideas when they wrote their new album.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bandleader - the leader of a musical band
  • synonym: colleague - a person you work with, especially in a profession
  • synonym: teammate - a person who is on the same team as you
