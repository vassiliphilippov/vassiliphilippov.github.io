bull thistle

noun
a spiny European thistle plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / bʊl ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / bʊl ˈθɪsəl / (BrE)
A European thistle with large, spiny flower heads and prickly leaves, commonly found as a weed in North America.

Examples

The bull thistle plant
Gardeners often remove bull thistle to prevent it from spreading in their yards.
The bull thistle has become a common weed in many parts of the United States.
The bull thistle is easy to recognize by its purple flower heads and spiny leaves.
