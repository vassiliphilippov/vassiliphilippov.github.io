soft

adjective
not demanding or burdensome; easy
Pronunciation: / sɔft / (AmE), / sɒft / (BrE)
(of a job, life, etc.) not demanding, difficult, or burdensome; easy to do or bear.

Examples

Soft job
After years of hard labor, she finally found a soft position at the company.
He landed a soft job with very little responsibility.
Many people dream of a soft life with no stress or worries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demanding - requiring much effort or attention
  • synonym: easy - not difficult; requiring little effort
