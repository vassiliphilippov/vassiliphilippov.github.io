genus Bertholletia

noun
genus of trees including the Brazil nut
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs bɛrˌθɑːˈliː.ti.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs bɛˌθɒlˈliː.tɪ.ə / (BrE)
