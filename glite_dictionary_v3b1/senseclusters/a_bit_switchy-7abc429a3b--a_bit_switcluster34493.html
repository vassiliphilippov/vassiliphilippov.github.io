a bit switchy

adjective
somewhat unpredictable or changeable
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt ˈswɪtʃi / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈswɪtʃi / (BrE)
Somewhat unpredictable or prone to sudden changes, especially in mood, attitude, or behavior.

Examples

A bit switchy mood
He's usually calm, but lately he's been a bit switchy in his reactions.
Her moods are a bit switchy, depending on the day.
The weather this week has been a bit switchy, with sunshine and rain within hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - not changing or fluctuating; stable
  • synonym: unpredictable - not able to be predicted; changeable
