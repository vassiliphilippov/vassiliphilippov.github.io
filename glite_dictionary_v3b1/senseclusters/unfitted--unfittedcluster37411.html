unfitted

adjective
not suitable or qualified
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtɪd / (BrE)
not suitable, qualified, or capable for a particular purpose or role

Examples

Unfitted for the job
He was considered unfitted for the demanding position due to his lack of experience.
The equipment was unfitted for use in extreme weather conditions.
She felt unfitted to handle the responsibilities of leadership at such a young age.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fit - suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose
  • synonym: unqualified - not having the necessary skills or knowledge
  • synonym: unsuitable - not appropriate or right for a particular purpose
