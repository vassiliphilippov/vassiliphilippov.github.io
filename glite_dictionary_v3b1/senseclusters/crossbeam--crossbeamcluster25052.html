crossbeam

noun
horizontal support beam
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbim / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌbiːm / (BrE)
A horizontal beam that spans or connects across a structure, providing support or stability.

Examples

A crossbeam support
A fallen crossbeam blocked the entrance to the warehouse after the storm.
He secured the roof by nailing each rafter to a sturdy crossbeam.
The old bridge was strengthened by adding a new crossbeam beneath the main deck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of wood or metal used to support weight in a building or other structure
