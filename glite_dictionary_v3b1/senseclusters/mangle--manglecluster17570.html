mangle

noun
machine for ironing laundry
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmæŋɡəl / (BrE)
a machine for drying and ironing laundry by passing it between two heavy heated rollers

Examples

A laundry mangle
She used a mangle to get the sheets perfectly smooth.
The old-fashioned mangle stood in the corner of the laundry room.
Vintage collectors sometimes seek out a mangle as a decorative piece.
