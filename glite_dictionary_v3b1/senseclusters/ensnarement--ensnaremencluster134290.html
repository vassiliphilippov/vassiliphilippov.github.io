ensnarement

noun
act of trapping
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsnɛrmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsnɛəmənt / (BrE)
the act or state of being trapped or caught, often by trickery or with a device

Examples

The ensnarement of animal
The ensnarement of the animal was achieved using a hidden net.
He warned his friends about the ensnarement set by the con artist.
The story describes the ensnarement of the hero in a complicated trap.
The ensnarement of the animal in the hunter's trap was swift and unexpected.
He struggled to escape the ensnarement of debt that had built up over the years.
Her ensnarement in the complicated scheme left her feeling helpless and anxious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capture - the act of catching or taking control of someone or something
  • synonym: entrapment - the act of catching or trapping someone, especially by trickery
  • antonym: freedom - the state of being free and not trapped
  • antonym: release - the act of setting someone or something free
