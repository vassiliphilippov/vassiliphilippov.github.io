URS

noun
user requirements document
8.7
Pronunciation: / juː ɑr ɛs / (AmE), / juː ɑː ɛs / (BrE)
a written User Requirements Specification document that details what a system or product must do to meet users' needs

Examples

URS document
The project manager asked everyone to read the URS before the kickoff meeting.
A clear URS helps developers understand exactly what the client expects from the software.
Changes to the URS must be approved by both the client and the engineering team.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: design document - a document outlining the design of a system or product
  • synonym: requirement - something that is needed or demanded
  • synonym: specification - a detailed description of requirements or standards
