yellowish green

noun
a shade between green and yellow
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.ɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green that is tinged with yellow; a color between green and yellow

The walls were painted a soft yellowish green that made the room feel fresh and lively.
