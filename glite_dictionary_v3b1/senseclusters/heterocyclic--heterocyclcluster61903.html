heterocyclic

adjective
containing a non-carbon atom in a ring (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬.ə.roʊˈsaɪ.klɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛt.ər.əˈsɪk.lɪk / (BrE)
(of a compound or ring) containing a closed ring of atoms in which at least one atom is not carbon.

Examples

Heterocyclic ring structure
Chemists synthesized a new heterocyclic molecule to study its potential as an antibiotic.
Many pharmaceuticals are based on heterocyclic compounds for their biological activity.
Pyridine is a classic example of a heterocyclic aromatic compound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homocyclic - containing a ring made only of carbon atoms (chemistry)
