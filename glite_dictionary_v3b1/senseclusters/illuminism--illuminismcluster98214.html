illuminism

noun
spiritual enlightenment belief
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːmɪˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪˌnɪzəm / (BrE)
a belief in spiritual enlightenment or the pursuit of knowledge through inner illumination, often associated with mystical or esoteric movements

Examples

Mystical illuminism
The philosophy of illuminism attracted many followers seeking personal spiritual growth.
Some secret societies in the 18th century were founded on the principles of illuminism.
He studied illuminism to better understand the connection between knowledge and inner light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mysticism - belief in direct spiritual experience or insight
  • antonym: rationalism - belief that reason is the main source of knowledge
