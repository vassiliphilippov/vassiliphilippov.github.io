parsley-leaved thorn

noun
southern U.S. hawthorn with lobed leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrsli livd θɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsli liːvd θɔːn / (BrE)
a southern United States hawthorn tree or shrub with pinnately lobed leaves, resembling parsley

The parsley-leaved thorn thrives in the warm and humid climate of the southern United States.
