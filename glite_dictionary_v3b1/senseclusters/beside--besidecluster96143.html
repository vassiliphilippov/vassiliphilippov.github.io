Examples

Beside the manager

There was nothing beside a few old books left in the room.
She had no one beside her family to rely on during difficult times.

Synonyms

  • other than
    1.0 17/m
    other than is used to mean 'except for' when pointing out one thing, while beside means apart from or except for someone.
  • except
    1.0 137/m
    except is used to leave something out of a list or group, while beside means to set something apart or not include it.
  • apart
    1.0 17/m
    apart is used mainly in 'apart from' to exclude or separate things, while beside means apart from or except for someone.
