US Naval Academy

proper noun
U.S. Navy officer training college
3.8
Pronunciation: / ju ɛs ˈneɪvəl əˌkædəmi / (AmE), / juː ɛs ˈneɪvəl əˌkædəmi / (BrE)
The official undergraduate college of the United States Navy, where men and women are trained to become naval officers.
