indecent exposure

noun
showing nakedness in public (offensive/illegal)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdisənt ɪkˈspoʊʒər / (AmE), / ɪnˈdiːsnt ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
the act of deliberately showing one's naked body or genitals in public, considered offensive or illegal in most societies

Examples

Indecent exposure in public
He was arrested for indecent exposure after running naked through the park.
Parents were concerned about incidents of indecent exposure near the school.
The law defines indecent exposure as deliberately displaying private parts in public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modesty - behavior, manner, or appearance intended to avoid impropriety or indecency
  • synonym: public nudity - appearing nude in public; state of being nude in public places
