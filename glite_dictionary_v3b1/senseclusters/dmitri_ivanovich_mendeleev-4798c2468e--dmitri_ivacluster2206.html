Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev

proper noun
Russian chemist, created periodic table
8.2
Pronunciation: / dəˈmitri ˌivəˈnoʊvɪtʃ ˌmɛndəˈleɪəf / (AmE), / dmɪˈtri ˌiːvənəˈvɪtʃ ˌmɛndɪˈleɪɛf / (BrE)
(1834–1907) a Russian chemist best known for formulating the periodic table of chemical elements and predicting the discovery of several new elements.

Examples

Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev periodic table
