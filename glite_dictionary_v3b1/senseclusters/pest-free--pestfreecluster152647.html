pest-free

adjective
free from pests or insects
Pronunciation: / pɛst ˈfri / (AmE), / pɛst ˈfriː / (BrE)
Not containing or affected by pests such as insects or rodents.

Examples

A pest-free garden
It took extra effort to maintain a completely pest-free greenhouse.
Local regulations require all exported produce to be pest-free.
The storage container is designed to keep your grains pest-free for months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infested - contaminated with pests or insects
