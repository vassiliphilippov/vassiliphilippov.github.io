potbelly stove

noun
rounded cast-iron wood or coal stove
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌbɛli stoʊv / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌbɛli stəʊv / (BrE)
a stove with a rounded, bulging body, typically made of cast iron, used for burning wood or coal, especially in old-fashioned settings

Examples

A potbelly stove
Children huddled around the potbelly stove while their clothes dried after playing in the snow.
He restored a vintage potbelly stove to heat his workshop during the winter.
The old cabin was kept warm by a potbelly stove in the corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wood stove - a stove that burns wood for heat
