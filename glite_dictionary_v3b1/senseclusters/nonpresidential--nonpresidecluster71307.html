nonpresidential

adjective
lacking presidential qualities
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl / (BrE)
not having the qualities, behavior, or dignity expected of a president.

Examples

Nonpresidential behavior
Many critics found the leader's remarks to be nonpresidential during the important press conference.
His nonpresidential attitude surprised those who expected a more formal approach.
Voters debated whether her actions were nonpresidential or simply modern and relatable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: presidential - having the qualities or dignity of a president
  • synonym: unpresidential - not suitable for a president
