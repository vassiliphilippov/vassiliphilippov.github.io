candlepower unit

noun
unit of luminous intensity
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkæn.dəl.paʊ.ər ˈjuː.nɪt/ /; / /ˈkæn.dəl.paʊ.ər ˈjuː.nət/ (AmE), /ˈkæn.dəl.paʊ.ə ˈjuː.nɪt/ /; / /ˈkæn.dəl.paʊ.ə ˈjuː.nət/ (BrE)
a unit of measurement for luminous intensity, originally based on the light produced by a standard candle.

Examples

A candlepower unit measure
Early headlights often specified their output in candlepower units to describe their intensity.
Scientists replaced candlepower units with the candela as a more precise standard for measuring luminous intensity.
The brightness of the new lamp was measured in candlepower units to ensure it met safety standards.
