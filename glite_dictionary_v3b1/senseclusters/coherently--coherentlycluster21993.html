coherently

adverb
in a logical and clear way
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhɪr.ənt.li / (AmE), / kəʊˈhɪə.rənt.li / (BrE)
in a logical, clear, and consistent way so that separate parts connect well

Examples

To speak coherently
After getting some rest, she was able to speak more coherently about the incident.
It is important to present your arguments coherently in an academic essay.
The professor explained the complex topic very coherently, making it easy for students to understand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incoherently - in an unclear or disjointed manner
