Synonyms

  • amazement
    1.2 6.80/m
    amazement is a strong, sudden feeling of surprise, while wonderment is a quieter mix of surprise and admiration.
  • astonishment
    3.4 4.64/m
    astonishment is a powerful, often shocked surprise, while wonderment is a gentler, admiring surprise at something new or beautiful.
  • admiration
    2.5 0.24/m
    admiration is respect and warm praise for someone's qualities, while wonderment is surprise mixed with admiration at something unfamiliar.
  • awe
    3.4 9.39/m
    awe is a deep, often reverent feeling that may include fear before something vast, while wonderment is gentler admiration and surprise.
