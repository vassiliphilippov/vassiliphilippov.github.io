birth-control campaigner

noun
person advocating birth control
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝθ kənˌtroʊl kəmˈpeɪnər / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ kənˌtrəʊl kəmˈpeɪnə / (BrE)
