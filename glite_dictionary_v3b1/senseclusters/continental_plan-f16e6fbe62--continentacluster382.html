continental plan

noun
hotel rate including continental breakfast
3.6 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.tənˈen.t̬əl plæn / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.təl plan / (BrE)
a hotel rate or arrangement that includes a daily continental breakfast with the accommodation, but does not include other meals

Examples

Our travel package comes with a continental plan, featuring breakfast but no additional meals.
The hotel offers a continental plan, which includes coffee and pastries each morning.
