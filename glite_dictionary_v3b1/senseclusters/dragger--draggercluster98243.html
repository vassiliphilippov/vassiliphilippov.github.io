dragger

noun
person who pulls or drags something
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡər / (AmE), / ˈdræɡə / (BrE)
a person who pulls, tugs, or drags something, especially with effort

Examples

A parade dragger
During the parade, each float required at least one dragger to keep it moving.
He became the unofficial dragger of suitcases for the entire family on their trips.
The dragger pulled the heavy boat ashore with great effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puller - a person or thing that pulls something
  • antonym: pusher - a person or thing that pushes something
