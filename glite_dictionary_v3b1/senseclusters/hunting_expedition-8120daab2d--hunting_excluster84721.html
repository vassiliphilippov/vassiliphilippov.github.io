hunting expedition

noun
organized hunting journey or trip
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a journey or organized trip undertaken for the purpose of hunting animals, especially in remote or foreign regions

Examples

A hunting expedition in Africa
During the 19th century, wealthy Europeans often embarked on a hunting expedition in Africa.
He wrote a book about his adventures on a hunting expedition in the Amazon.
The group planned a hunting expedition to the remote forests of Tanzania.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safari - an expedition to observe or hunt animals, especially in East Africa
