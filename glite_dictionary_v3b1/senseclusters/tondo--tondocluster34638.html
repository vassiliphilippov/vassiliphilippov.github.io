tondo

noun
circular artwork
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɒndəʊ / (BrE)
a circular painting or sculpture, especially one created during the Renaissance period

Examples

A tondo painting
The gallery featured a Renaissance tondo with vibrant colors and intricate details.
She admired the unique shape of the tondo hanging above the fireplace.
Artists often chose the tondo format to create a sense of harmony and unity in their work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medallion - a large, round decorative piece of art or jewelry
  • antonym: panel - a flat, usually rectangular piece of artwork
