tone language

noun
language where tone affects meaning
8.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /toʊn ˈlæŋ.gwɪdʒ/ /; / /toʊn ˈlæŋ.gwədʒ/ (AmE), /təʊn ˈlæŋ.gwɪdʒ/ /; / /təʊn ˈlæŋ.gwədʒ/ (BrE)
A language in which differences in pitch (tone) are used to distinguish word meanings.

Examples

Tone language pronunciation
It's often challenging for English speakers to master pronunciation in a tone language.
Mandarin Chinese is a tone language, so a word's meaning can change based on its pitch pattern.
Many African languages, such as Yoruba, are classified as tone languages.
