lectionary

noun
church reading book
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɛkʃənəri / (BrE)
a book or list of scripture readings chosen to be read at church services throughout the year, usually arranged by the church calendar
