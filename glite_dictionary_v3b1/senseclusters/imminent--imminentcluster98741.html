imminent

adjective
about to happen
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪnənt / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪnənt / (BrE)
likely to happen very soon; about to occur.

Examples

An imminent event
His resignation from the company seemed imminent after weeks of rumors.
Scientists warn that an earthquake in the region is imminent.
The dark clouds signaled that a storm was imminent.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: immediate - occurring or done at once; instant
  • synonym: impending - about to happen; likely to occur soon
  • antonym: remote - unlikely to happen soon; distant in time
