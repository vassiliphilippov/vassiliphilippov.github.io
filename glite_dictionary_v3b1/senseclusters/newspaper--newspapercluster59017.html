newspaper

noun
cheap paper for printing newspapers
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
A cheap paper, usually made from wood pulp, used for printing newspapers and sometimes for wrapping or packing.

Examples

Newspaper strips
Artists sometimes use strips of newspaper to create papier-mâché sculptures.
The floor was covered with newspaper to catch any paint drips.
We wrapped the fragile dishes in newspaper before shipping them.
