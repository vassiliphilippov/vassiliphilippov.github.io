Alice B. Toklas

proper noun
American writer, companion of Gertrude Stein
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪs bi ˈtoʊkləs / (AmE), / ˈælɪs bi ˈtəʊkləs / (BrE)
(1877–1967) American writer and lifelong companion of Gertrude Stein, known for her memoir and literary influence.
