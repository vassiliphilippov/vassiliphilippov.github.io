incense wood

noun
fragrant wood used for incense
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sɛns wʊd / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sɛns wʊd / (BrE)
the fragrant wood from certain trees of the genus Protium, used for incense

Examples

A piece of incense wood
She bought a piece of incense wood as a souvenir from her trip to the tropics.
The villagers harvest incense wood from the forest to use in religious ceremonies.
When burned, incense wood produces a sweet, aromatic smoke that fills the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incense - a substance that is burned for its pleasant smell
