bitte

noun
polite request
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtə / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə / (BrE)
a polite request or plea, especially one made in a gentle or formal manner

Examples

A gentle bitte
She made a quiet bitte for understanding during the meeting.
His bitte for assistance was met with immediate support from the team.
The letter included a heartfelt bitte for forgiveness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demand - an insistent and forceful request
  • synonym: plea - an earnest request or appeal
