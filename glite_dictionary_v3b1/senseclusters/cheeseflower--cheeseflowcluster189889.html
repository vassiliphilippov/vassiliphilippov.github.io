cheeseflower

noun
perennial plant with rosy-purple flowers
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzflaʊə / (BrE)
a perennial plant (Malva sylvestris) with rosy-purple flowers native to Europe and Asia, naturalized in North America

Examples

The cheeseflower plant
The cheeseflower blooms in late spring, adding vibrant color to gardens.
Traditional herbalists often used cheeseflower for its soothing properties.
We found several cheeseflowers growing wild along the countryside path.
