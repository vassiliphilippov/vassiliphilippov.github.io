lima

noun
edible flat bean
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪmə / (AmE), / ˈliːmə / (BrE)
a type of edible bean that is flat and pale green or white, commonly used in cooking

Examples

Lima beans
She added chopped lima beans to the vegetable stew for extra flavor.
The salad was topped with fresh lima beans and a light vinaigrette.
Farmers harvested a large crop of lima beans this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butter bean - another name for the lima bean, especially in the southern United States
  • antonym: kidney bean - a type of common bean with a kidney shape and red color
