deep middle cerebral vein

noun
a vein in the brain near the Sylvian fissure
Pronunciation: / dip ˈmɪdəl səˈbrəl veɪn / (AmE), / diːp ˈmɪdəl səhˈbriːəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein in the brain that accompanies the middle cerebral artery deep in the Sylvian fissure and empties into the basal vein

Examples

The deep middle cerebral vein
During surgery, care must be taken not to injure the deep middle cerebral vein.
Imaging studies revealed a blockage in the patient's deep middle cerebral vein.
The deep middle cerebral vein is important in the venous drainage of the lateral surface of the brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: middle cerebral vein - a vein accompanying the middle cerebral artery
  • degree lower: superficial middle cerebral vein - a related vein located more superficially along the Sylvian fissure
