unposted

adjective
not made public
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpoʊstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊstɪd / (BrE)
not sent, published, or made available to the public.

Examples

Unposted announcement
The announcement remained unposted until the official approval was received.
Several important updates were still unposted on the website by the end of the day.
He realized his comment was unposted and quickly shared it with the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posted - sent, published, or made public
  • synonym: unpublished - not made available to the public in print or online
