web-toed

adjective
having toes joined by skin
7.1
Pronunciation: / wɛb toʊd / (AmE), / wɛb təʊd / (BrE)
having feet in which the toes are joined by a web of skin

Examples

Web-toed feet
Some species of ducks are web-toed, which aids in swimming.
The web-toed frog can swim efficiently in the pond.
Their web-toed feet make these birds excellent divers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: webbed - having a membranous web between the toes
