unaesthetic

adjective
not attractive or tasteful
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɛsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
not attractive or pleasing to the senses; lacking a sense of beauty or tastefulness.

Examples

An unaesthetic design
He replaced the unaesthetic curtains with elegant drapes that matched the room's decor.
Many found the colors of the brochure to be unaesthetic and jarring to the eye.
The new monument is widely considered unaesthetic and out of place in the historic square.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aesthetic - concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty
  • synonym: unattractive - not pleasing or appealing to look at
