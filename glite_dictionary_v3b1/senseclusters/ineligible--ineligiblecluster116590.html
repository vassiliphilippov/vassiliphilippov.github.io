ineligible

adjective
not qualified or not permitted
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛlədʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛlɪdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
not allowed or qualified to do or receive something, usually because of rules or requirements

Examples

Ineligible for a prize
Because he missed the deadline, he is ineligible to enter the contest.
Foreign nationals are ineligible for jury duty in this country.
She was ineligible to receive the scholarship due to her grades.
An ineligible pass receiver resulted in a penalty for the team.
Several applicants were found ineligible due to missing documents.
The player was declared ineligible for the match after breaking the rules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eligible - having the right to do or obtain something; qualified
