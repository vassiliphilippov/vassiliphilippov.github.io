fullback

noun
defensive sports player
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfʊlˌbæk / (BrE)
(in soccer or football) a defensive player who plays near the goal area and helps prevent the opposing team from scoring, often supporting both defense and attack
