beached whale

noun
person feeling exhausted or helpless
Pronunciation: / biːt͡ʃt weɪl / (AmE), / biːtʃt weɪl / (BrE)
a person who feels out of place, exhausted, or helpless, especially due to physical or emotional fatigue

Examples

Like a beached whale
After hours of yard work in the sun, I collapsed on the couch like a beached whale.
I was so exhausted after the marathon that I lay on the grass, a beached whale among the finishers.
Whenever Mark tries to dance, he feels like a beached whale among the graceful performers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish out of water - a person who feels uncomfortable or out of place in a certain situation
