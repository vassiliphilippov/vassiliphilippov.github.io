rimrock

noun
rocky edge of plateau
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪmˌrɑk / (AmE), / ˈrɪmˌrɒk / (BrE)
a layer of hard rock or a cliff that forms the edge of a plateau, canyon, or other elevated landform
