scaleless

adjective
without scales
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈskeɪlɪləs / (BrE)
having no scales; lacking scales on the skin or surface.

Examples

Scaleless fish species
Many species of catfish are scaleless and rely on other forms of protection.
The scaleless fish appeared smooth and shiny under the laboratory light.
Their bodies were completely scaleless, making them vulnerable to certain parasites.
