bund

noun
raised embankment
8.2
Pronunciation: /bʌnd/ /; / /bʊnd/ (AmE), /bʊnd/ /; / /bʌnd/ (BrE)
a raised bank or embankment built to control water flow or protect land from flooding

Examples

River bund
The villagers built a bund along the river to protect their crops from flooding.
Heavy rains caused the old bund to break, leading to water entering the fields.
Engineers inspected the bund regularly to ensure it remained strong and effective.
Farmers built a bund around the field to prevent flooding during the rainy season.
The village was protected by a sturdy bund along the riverbank.
After heavy rains, the bund held firm and kept the water away from the houses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dam - a barrier built to hold back water and raise its level
  • synonym: levee - an embankment built to prevent the overflow of a river
