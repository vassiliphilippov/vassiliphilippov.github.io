greenstuff

noun
modeling epoxy putty
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.stʌf/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.stʌf/ (BrE)
a two-part green epoxy putty used by model makers and hobbyists to fill, sculpt, or repair miniatures and models

Examples

Greenstuff for models
He filled the miniature's gaps with greenstuff before smoothing the surface for painting.
Many hobbyists use greenstuff to sculpt custom details on their models.
After mixing the two parts of greenstuff, she quickly shaped it before it hardened.
