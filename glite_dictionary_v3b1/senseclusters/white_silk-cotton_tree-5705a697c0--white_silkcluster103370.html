white silk-cotton tree

noun
large tropical tree, source of kapok fiber
7.8 7.4
Pronunciation: /waɪt ˈsɪlk.kɑː.tən triː/ /; / /hwaɪt ˈsɪlk.kɑː.tən triː/ /; / /waɪt ˈsɪlk.kɔː.tən triː/ (AmE), /waɪt ˈsɪlk.kɒt.ən triː/ /; / /waɪt ˈsɪlk.kɒt.ən tʃriː/ (BrE)
