manless

adjective
without men
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ləs / (BrE)
without a man or men present or involved

Examples

Manless home
After her husband left, she adjusted to a manless household.
The club remained manless for years, accepting only female members.
She enjoyed her independence and the freedom of a manless lifestyle.
