Haitian centime

noun
monetary unit of Haiti
9.9
Pronunciation: / heɪˈʃən sɛnˈtiːm / (AmE), / heɪˈʃən sɒ̃ˈtim / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Haiti, equal to one hundredth of a gourde

Examples

A Haitian centime coin

The coin is worth only one Haitian centime, making it nearly obsolete in daily transactions.
