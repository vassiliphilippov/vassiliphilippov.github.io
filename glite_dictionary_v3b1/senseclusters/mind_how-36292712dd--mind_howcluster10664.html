mind how

verb
be careful about how you do something
Pronunciation: / maɪnd haʊ / (AmE), / maɪnd haʊ / (BrE)
Be careful about the way you do something; pay attention to how you act or behave.

Examples

To mind how you cross
Mind how you cross the street, especially at this busy intersection.
Please mind how you speak to your grandmother; she's not feeling well today.
You should mind how you handle these fragile items, or they might break.
