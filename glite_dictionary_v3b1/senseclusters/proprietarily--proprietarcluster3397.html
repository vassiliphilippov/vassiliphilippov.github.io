proprietarily

adverb
relating to ownership
8.1
Pronunciation: / prəˌpraɪəˈtɛrəli / (AmE), / prəˌprʌɪəˈtɛərɪli /; / prəˌpraɪəˈtɛərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or involves ownership or exclusive control

Examples

Distribute proprietarily
The company distributes its products proprietarily, restricting access to licensed users only.
Certain file formats are managed proprietarily and require special permission to use.
The data is held proprietarily and is not available to the public.
She glanced proprietarily at the new painting, making it clear it belonged to her.
He acted proprietarily toward the project, rarely letting others contribute ideas.
The cat sat proprietarily on the windowsill, as if it owned the entire house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusively - in a way that limits access to only certain people or groups
  • antonym: generously - in a way that is giving and not controlling
  • synonym: possessively - in a way that shows a desire to own or control
  • antonym: publicly - in a way that is open or available to everyone
