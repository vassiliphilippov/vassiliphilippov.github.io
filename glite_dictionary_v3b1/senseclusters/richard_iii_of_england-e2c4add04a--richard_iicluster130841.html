Richard III of England

proper noun
King of England (1483–1485), last Plantagenet ruler
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪtʃ.ərd ðə θɜːrd əv ˈɪŋ.glənd/ (AmE), /ˈrɪtʃ.əd ðə θɜːd əv ˈɪŋ.glənd/ (BrE)
(1452–1485) King of England from 1483 to 1485; he seized the throne from his nephew Edward V and was defeated by Henry Tudor at the Battle of Bosworth Field, marking the end of the Plantagenet dynasty.
