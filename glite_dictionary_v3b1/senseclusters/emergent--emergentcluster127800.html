emergent

adjective
requiring urgent action; unexpected (esp. medical)
4.7 5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
(of a situation or event) occurring unexpectedly and requiring urgent action, especially in a medical or emergency context.

Examples

Emergent situation
An emergent situation in the ward required all doctors to respond quickly.
The hospital staff is trained to handle emergent cases efficiently and calmly.
The patient was taken to surgery for an emergent procedure after a sudden collapse.

Related Terms
