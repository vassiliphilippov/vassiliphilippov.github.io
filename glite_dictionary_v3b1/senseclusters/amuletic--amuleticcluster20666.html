amuletic

adjective
relating to amulets
Pronunciation: / ˌæmjəˈlɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæmjʊˈlɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an amulet, especially believed to offer protection or bring good luck

Examples

An amuletic necklace
She wore an amuletic necklace for good luck.
The ancient coin was believed to have amuletic properties that protected its owner.
Many cultures use amuletic objects to ward off evil spirits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magical - having mysterious or supernatural powers
  • synonym: protective - intended to protect or guard
