foulard

noun
light silk or silk-like fabric
Pronunciation: / fuːˈlɑrd /; / fuˈlɑrd / (AmE), / fuːˈlɑːd /; / fuˈlɑːd / (BrE)
a light, soft fabric (usually silk or similar) with a printed pattern, or a scarf made from this material
