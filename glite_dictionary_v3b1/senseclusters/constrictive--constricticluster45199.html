constrictive

adjective
causing tightness
6.2
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈstrɪk.tɪv/ (AmE), /kən.ˈstrɪk.tɪv/ (BrE)

Examples

Constrictive bandage

Some people find turtlenecks too constrictive to wear comfortably all day.
The new law was criticized for its constrictive effect on personal freedoms.
