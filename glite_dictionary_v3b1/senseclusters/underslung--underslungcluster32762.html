underslung

adjective
having a lower part projecting below the upper
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈslʌŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈslʌŋ / (BrE)
having a lower part that projects or is suspended below the upper part, often giving a hanging or drooping appearance.

Examples

Underslung jaw
He noticed the bulldog’s distinctive underslung jaw as it played with the children.
The new model features an underslung exhaust system for improved aerodynamics.
The old railway carriage had an underslung frame beneath the passenger compartment.
