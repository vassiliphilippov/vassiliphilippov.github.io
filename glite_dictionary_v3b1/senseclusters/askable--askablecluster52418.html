askable

adjective
open to questions
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæskəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːskəbəl / (BrE)
willing or ready to receive questions or requests; approachable and open to being asked.

Examples

Askable teacher
The teacher made herself askable by encouraging students to share their concerns after class.
It is important for managers to be askable so employees feel comfortable raising issues.
Even during busy times, he remained askable and willing to help anyone who needed advice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unapproachable - not friendly or easy to talk to
