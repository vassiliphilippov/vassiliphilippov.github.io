set up

verb
to frame or ensnare someone
2.2 5.0
Pronunciation: /set ʌp/ /; / /set əp/ (AmE), /set ʌp/ /; / /set əp/ (BrE)
to trick someone or make them appear guilty of something they did not do, especially by deliberate deception
