city desk

noun
newspaper's local news department
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈsɪti dɛsk / (BrE)
the editorial department of a newspaper responsible for editing and managing local news coverage

Examples

The city desk team
After the accident, the city desk received dozens of tips from eyewitnesses.
She worked as a reporter on the city desk covering local politics.
The editor assigned the developing story to the city desk for further investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newsroom - the main office of a newspaper or broadcasting organization where news is received and processed
