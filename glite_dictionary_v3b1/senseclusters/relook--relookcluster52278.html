relook

noun
review or re-examination
6.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàl äk / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàl äk / (BrE)
an act of looking at something again, especially to review or re-examine it

Examples

A relook at plans
The manager called for a relook at the project timeline after new information emerged.
A relook at the evidence changed the outcome of the investigation.
We should have a relook at our marketing strategy before launching the campaign.
