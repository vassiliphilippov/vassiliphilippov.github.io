plastic surgery

noun
surgery for reconstructing or repairing body tissue
3.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈplæ.stɪk ˈsɜːr.dʒə.riː/ (AmE), /ˈplæ.stɪk ˈsɜː.dʒə.riː/ (BrE)
the branch of surgery concerned with the repair or reconstruction of body tissue, either for medical or cosmetic reasons

Examples

Plastic surgery repair
Advances in plastic surgery have made it possible to correct many birth defects.

Some people opt for plastic surgery to enhance their appearance rather than for medical reasons.
