Siamese

noun
the Thai language of central Thailand
8.6
Pronunciation: / saɪ.əˈmiz / (AmE), / saɪ.əˈmiːz / (BrE)
the standard language of Thailand, belonging to the Tai language family, spoken mainly in central Thailand and Bangkok
