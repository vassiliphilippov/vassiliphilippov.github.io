Jeremiah

proper noun
Israelite prophet in the Old Testament
Pronunciation: / dʒɛr.əˈmaɪ.ə / (AmE), / dʒɛr.ɪˈmaɪ.ə / (BrE)
(in the Bible) an Israelite prophet of the 7th–6th century BC, known for his prophecies and lamentations about the wickedness of his people.

Examples

Prophet Jeremiah
Jeremiah warned the people of Jerusalem about the coming destruction if they did not change their ways.
Many scholars study Jeremiah for insights into ancient Israelite society and religion.
The Book of Jeremiah contains many vivid accounts of his struggles and prophecies.
