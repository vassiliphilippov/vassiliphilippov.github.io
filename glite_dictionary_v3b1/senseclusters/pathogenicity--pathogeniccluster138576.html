pathogenicity

noun
ability to cause disease
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθəˌdʒɛnɪˈsɪɾi /; / ˌpæθəˌdʒɛnɪˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌpæθəˌdʒɛnɪˈsɪti / (BrE)
