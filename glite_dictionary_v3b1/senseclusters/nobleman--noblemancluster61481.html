nobleman

noun
man of noble rank or title
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊbəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈnəʊbəlmən / (BrE)
A man who belongs to the nobility, especially one who holds a hereditary or honorary title.

Examples

A nobleman attends court
As a nobleman, he was expected to attend royal court regularly.
The castle once belonged to a wealthy nobleman who ruled the land.
The story features a young nobleman who falls in love with a commoner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aristocrat - a member of the aristocracy; a person of high social rank
  • antonym: commoner - a person who is not of noble rank
