acuminate

verb
make sharp; taper to a point
9.0
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / əˈkjuːmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to make sharp or taper to a point; to shape something into a pointed form

Examples

To acuminate a stick
Many leaves acuminate at the tip, giving them a distinctive sharp appearance.
The craftsman skillfully acuminated the wooden stick until it formed a perfect spear point.
Using the knife, she carefully acuminated the end of the pencil for easier writing.
