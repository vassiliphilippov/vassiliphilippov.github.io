redecoration

noun
decorating again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of decorating a room or building again to make it look new or different

Examples

Room redecoration
After moving into the old house, they started the redecoration of every room.
The hotel will close for a month to allow for complete redecoration of its lobby and guest rooms.
She saved money for the redecoration of her kitchen, hoping to give it a modern look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoration - the act of making something look more attractive by adding items or color
  • synonym: renovation - the process of repairing and improving a building
