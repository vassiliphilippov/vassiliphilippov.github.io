underbred

adjective
lacking refinement or good manners
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈbrɛd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈbrɛd / (BrE)
Lacking in refinement or good manners; not properly brought up or cultivated.

Examples

Underbred behavior
Despite his wealth, his manners revealed a distinctly underbred character.
His underbred behavior embarrassed everyone at the dinner table.
She was dismissed as underbred and unsophisticated by the elite social club.
