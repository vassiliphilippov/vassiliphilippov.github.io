clanging

adjective
making a loud metallic sound
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
making or characterized by a loud, resonant, metallic sound.

Examples

A clanging alarm
A clanging alarm filled the building, signaling an emergency.
The clanging of the church bells echoed across the valley.
We were startled by the sudden clanging of metal as the gate slammed shut.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clangorous - making a continuous loud, resonant sound
  • synonym: ringing - producing a clear, resonant sound
