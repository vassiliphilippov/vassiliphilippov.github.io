rusticate

verb
live in the countryside; adopt a rustic life
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌstəˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrʌstɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to live in the countryside, often adopting a simple, rustic lifestyle

Examples

To rusticate in the country
After decades in the city, she decided to rusticate on her family's countryside estate.
Some artists rusticate during the summer to find inspiration for their work.
They rusticated for a year to escape the chaos of urban life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retire - to withdraw from active life, especially to the country
  • antonym: urbanize - to make or become urban; adopt urban ways
