criminal

adjective
extremely bad; deserving strong criticism
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪm.ə.nəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nəl / (BrE)
extremely bad or deserving strong criticism; reprehensible

Examples

Criminal waste

Some consider food waste on this scale to be absolutely criminal.
