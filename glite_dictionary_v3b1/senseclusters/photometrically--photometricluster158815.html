photometrically

adverb
by means of photometry
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊ.təˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
By means of photometry; using the measurement of light intensity or illumination.

Examples

Measured photometrically
The brightness of the star was photometrically determined using advanced instruments.
The researchers evaluated the sample photometrically to obtain precise results.
These minerals can be identified photometrically based on their reflectance characteristics.
