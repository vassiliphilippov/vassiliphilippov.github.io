bird's nest

noun
structure for birds' eggs and young
Pronunciation: / bɝdz nɛst / (AmE), / bɜːdz nɛst / (BrE)
a structure built by a bird for laying eggs and raising its young

Examples

A bird's nest in tree
It's important not to disturb a bird's nest during the breeding season.

We found an abandoned bird's nest after the storm had passed.
