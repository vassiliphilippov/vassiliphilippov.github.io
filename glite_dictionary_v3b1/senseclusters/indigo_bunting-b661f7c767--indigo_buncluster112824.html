indigo bunting

noun
small blue North American songbird
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.dɪˌɡoʊ ˈbʌn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪˌɡəʊ ˈbʌn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a small North American songbird, the male of which has bright deep blue plumage during the breeding season

Examples
