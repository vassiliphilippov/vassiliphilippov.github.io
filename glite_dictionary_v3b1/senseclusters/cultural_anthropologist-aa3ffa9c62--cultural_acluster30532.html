Examples

Cultural anthropologist studies customs
As a cultural anthropologist, she spent years researching wedding rituals in rural villages.
Many cultural anthropologists study the ways in which communities adapt to globalization.
The university invited a cultural anthropologist to lecture on kinship systems in Southeast Asia.

Synonyms

  • social anthropologist
    6.9 0.05/m
    a social anthropologist studies social structures, kinship, and institutions in communities, while a cultural anthropologist focuses on cultural beliefs and everyday customs.
  • ethnographer
    8.6 2.50/m
    an ethnographer records daily life and customs through close fieldwork and observation, while a cultural anthropologist studies culture broadly across beliefs and practices.
  • ethnologist
    8.9 0.29/m
    an ethnologist compares cultures and traces their development in scholarly studies, while a cultural anthropologist studies living culture and everyday practices.
