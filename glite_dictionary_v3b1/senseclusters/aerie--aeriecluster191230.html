aerie

noun
high-altitude dwelling
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛri /; / ˈɪri, ˈɛri / (AmE), / ˈɪəri /; / ˈɪəri, ˈɛəri / (BrE)
a house, room, or other habitation situated at a high altitude, often on a mountain or high building
