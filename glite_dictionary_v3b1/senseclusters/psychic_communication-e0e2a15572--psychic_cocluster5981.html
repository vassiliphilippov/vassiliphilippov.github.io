psychic communication

noun
communication by paranormal means
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkɪk kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪkɪk kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
Communication that occurs by means of paranormal or supernatural abilities rather than the usual physical or sensory channels.

Examples

Psychic communication abilities
He studies cases of alleged psychic communication between twins in scientific research.
Some people claim to have experienced psychic communication with loved ones who have passed away.
The medium demonstrated what she described as psychic communication during the séance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telepathy - the supposed communication of thoughts or ideas by means other than the known senses
