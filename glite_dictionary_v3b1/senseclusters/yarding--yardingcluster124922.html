yarding

noun
moving felled logs
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of moving felled logs to a central collection point during logging operations

Examples

Yarding logs
The team spent hours yarding logs from the forest to the collection site.
Modern machinery has made yarding much faster and safer for workers.
After yarding was complete, the logs were loaded onto trucks for transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hauling - transporting something, especially heavy loads, from one place to another
  • synonym: skidding - dragging logs from where they are cut to a landing area
