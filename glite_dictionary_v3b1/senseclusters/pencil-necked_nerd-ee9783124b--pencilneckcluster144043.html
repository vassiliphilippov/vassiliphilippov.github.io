pencil-necked nerd

noun
derogatory term for a socially awkward intellectual
9.1 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpen.səl.nekt nɜːrd/ /; / /ˈpent.səl.nekt nɜːrd/ (AmE), /ˈpen.səl.nekt nɜːd/ /; / /ˈpent.səl.nekt nɜːd/ (BrE)
a derogatory term for a socially awkward and physically unimposing person, often perceived as highly intellectual or bookish

Examples

A pencil-necked nerd
Everyone in the office thought he was just a pencil-necked nerd until he won the trivia contest.
Growing up as a pencil-necked nerd, he was always more interested in books than sports.
The bully shoved the pencil-necked nerd into a locker and walked away laughing.

  • antonym: jock - a physically active and athletic person, especially in sports; often contrasted with nerds
