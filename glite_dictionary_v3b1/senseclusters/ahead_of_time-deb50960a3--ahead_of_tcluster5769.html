ahead of time

adverb
before expected or scheduled time
Pronunciation: / əˈhɛd əv taɪm / (AmE), / əˈhɛd əv taɪm / (BrE)
earlier than planned or expected; before the scheduled or usual time

Examples

Plan ahead of time
Please let me know ahead of time if you are unable to attend the meeting.
The project was finished ahead of time thanks to the team's hard work.
Tickets for the event sold out, so I'm glad I bought mine ahead of time.
