ruffian

noun
violent or brutal person
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfiən / (AmE), / ˈrʌfɪən / (BrE)
a violent, cruel, or brutal person, especially one involved in crime or causing trouble

Examples

A ruffian in crime
Police arrested the ruffian for his involvement in the street brawl.
She refused to be intimidated by the ruffian standing at her door.
The ruffian threatened everyone in the bar with a broken bottle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a polite or refined man
  • synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
