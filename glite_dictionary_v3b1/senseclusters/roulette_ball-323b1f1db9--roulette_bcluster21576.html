roulette ball

noun
ball used in roulette game
1.2
Pronunciation: / ruˈlɛt bɔl / (AmE), / ruːˈlɛt bɔːl / (BrE)
A small, hard ball used in the game of roulette, spun around the edge of the roulette wheel to determine the winning number or color.

Examples
