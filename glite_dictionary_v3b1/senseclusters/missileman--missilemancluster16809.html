missileman

noun
missile system operator
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪsaɪlˌmæn / (BrE)
a person trained to operate, maintain, or launch missile systems, especially in the military

Examples

Military missileman

Every missileman must complete rigorous training before handling live weapons.
