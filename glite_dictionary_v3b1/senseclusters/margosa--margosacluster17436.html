margosa

noun
East Indian neem tree
Pronunciation: / mɑːrˈgoʊsə / (AmE), / mɑːˈgəʊsə / (BrE)
a large, semi-evergreen tree of the East Indies (Azadirachta indica), known for its bitter bark, aromatic seed oil, and medicinal properties; also called the neem tree.

Examples

The margosa tree
Farmers plant margosa along their fields as a natural pesticide.
Oil extracted from margosa seeds is used in a variety of cosmetic products.
The bitter bark of the margosa tree is often used in traditional medicine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neem - the neem tree, Azadirachta indica, known for medicinal uses
