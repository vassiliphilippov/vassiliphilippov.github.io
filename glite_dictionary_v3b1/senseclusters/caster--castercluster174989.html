caster

noun
pivoting wheel for moving furniture or equipment
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstə / (BrE)
a small wheel, usually mounted on a swivel, attached to the bottom of furniture, equipment, or machinery to make it easier to move.

Examples

Furniture caster
After replacing the broken caster, the cart moved without any trouble.
Many suitcases today come with swivel casters for easy transport.
The office chair rolled smoothly across the floor on its casters.

  • synonym: wheel - circular object that rotates and helps something move
