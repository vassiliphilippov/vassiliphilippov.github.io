inquietude

noun
anxiety; uneasiness
6.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwaɪəˌtuːd / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪ.ə.tjuːd / (BrE)
A feeling of anxiety, restlessness, or uneasiness.

Examples

A feeling of inquietude
Despite his outward calm, a sense of inquietude lingered in his mind before the big exam.
Rumors of layoffs created inquietude among the employees, causing a tense atmosphere in the office.
The late-night storm filled her with inquietude as she listened to the wind howl outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - A feeling of worry or unease
  • antonym: serenity - The state of being calm and peaceful
