bat

verb
wink or flutter the eyelids
9.9
Pronunciation: / bæt / (AmE), / bæt / (BrE)
to wink briefly or flutter the eyelids quickly, especially as a reaction or gesture

Examples

To bat your eyes
He tried not to bat his eyelashes during the serious conversation.
She didn't bat an eyelid when she heard the shocking news.
The actress batted her eyelids playfully at the camera.
