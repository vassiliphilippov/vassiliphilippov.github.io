Matterhorn

proper noun
mountain on the Swiss-Italian border in the Alps
Pronunciation: / ˈmætərˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈmætəhɔːn / (BrE)
a famous mountain in the Alps on the border between Switzerland and Italy, notable for its distinctive pyramid shape and peak at 14,780 feet (4,478 meters)

Examples

The Matterhorn mountain
A postcard showing the snow-capped Matterhorn is a popular souvenir from Switzerland.
From the summit, they enjoyed breathtaking views of the Matterhorn towering above the Alps.
The climbers prepared for their ascent of the Matterhorn, one of the most iconic peaks in the world.
