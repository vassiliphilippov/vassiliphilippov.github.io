prime of life

noun
peak period of one's life
Pronunciation: / praɪm əv laɪf / (AmE), / praɪm əv laɪf / (BrE)
the period in a person's life when they are at their peak in terms of strength, energy, and ability

Examples

In the prime of life
Even in his prime of life, he never neglected his family and friends.
Illness struck him suddenly while he was in the very prime of life.
She felt that she was in the prime of life when she started her own company at thirty.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - the period when someone is decreasing in power, health, or vigor
  • synonym: heyday - the period of a person's or thing's greatest success, popularity, or vigor
