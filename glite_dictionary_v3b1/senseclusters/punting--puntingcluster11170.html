punting

noun
dropping and kicking the ball in football
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(in football) the act of kicking the ball after dropping it from the hands and before it touches the ground.

Examples

Football punting practice
During practice, the coach praised his punting technique and distance.
Effective punting can help a team control field position during a game.
He specialized in both punting and place-kicking for his college football team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punt - a kick in which the ball is dropped and kicked before touching the ground (football)
