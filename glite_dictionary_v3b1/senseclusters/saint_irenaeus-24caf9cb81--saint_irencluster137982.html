Saint Irenaeus

proper noun
early Christian theologian and bishop of Lyons
9.3
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ɪrəˈneɪəs / (AmE), / seɪnt ɪrəˈneɪəs / (BrE)
A Greek theologian and bishop of Lyons, noted for his antiheretical writings and recognized as a saint and Doctor of the Church (circa 130–200).

Examples

Saint Irenaeus bishop
Saint Irenaeus is often cited for his influential role in shaping early Christian theology.
Many scholars regard Saint Irenaeus as a foundational figure in combating heresy.
The writings of Saint Irenaeus are important sources for understanding early Church debates.
