obsessive-compulsive disorder

noun
anxiety disorder with obsessions and compulsions
Pronunciation: / əbˌsɛsɪv kəmˈpʌlsɪv dɪsˌɔrdɚ / (AmE), / əbˌsɛsɪv kəmˈpʌlsɪv dɪsˌɔːdə / (BrE)
A mental health disorder characterized by recurrent, unwanted thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions) performed to reduce anxiety.

Examples

Obsessive-compulsive disorder symptoms
Doctors often use therapy and medication to help manage obsessive-compulsive disorder.
Her obsessive-compulsive disorder made daily routines extremely challenging.
People with obsessive-compulsive disorder may feel compelled to wash their hands repeatedly.
