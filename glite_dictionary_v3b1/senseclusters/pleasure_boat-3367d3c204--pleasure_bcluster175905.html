Synonyms

  • pleasure craft
    5.1 0.06/m
    pleasure craft is a broad term for recreational boats with cabins and facilities, while pleasure boat focuses on large motorized leisure vessels.
  • cruiser
    5.4 4.37/m
    cruiser is a motorboat built for living aboard during travel, while pleasure boat emphasizes use for leisure rather than long trips.
  • cabin cruiser
    5.3 0.14/m
    cabin cruiser is a motorboat with a small cabin and facilities for short stays, while pleasure boat covers larger recreational craft.
  • motor yacht
    3.5 0.10/m
    motor yacht is a larger, often luxurious engine-powered vessel for cruising, while pleasure boat is a general term for recreational motorboats.
  • yacht
    5.3 7.92/m
    yacht is usually an expensive boat for racing or pleasure, often sail-powered, while pleasure boat stresses motorized leisure use.
