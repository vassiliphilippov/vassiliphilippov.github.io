shorthand typist

noun
person who transcribes speech in shorthand and types it
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtˌhænd ˈtaɪpɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːthænd ˈtʌɪpɪst / (BrE)
a person skilled in transcribing spoken words, especially dictation, using shorthand methods and typing.

Examples

A shorthand typist at work
As a skilled shorthand typist, she handled all of the executive’s dictation tasks efficiently.
He trained to become a shorthand typist in order to work at a busy law firm.
The shorthand typist quickly transcribed the manager’s speech during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stenographer - a person whose job is to transcribe speech, often using shorthand; synonym for shorthand typist
