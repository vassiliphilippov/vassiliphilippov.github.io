automobility

noun
self-propulsion ability
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəmoʊˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the ability of a machine or vehicle to move by its own power without external assistance

Examples

Robot automobility
The robot's automobility makes it ideal for exploring hazardous environments without human intervention.
Advances in automobility have led to the development of self-driving cars.
Engineers tested the automobility of the new drone in various weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immobility - the state of being unable to move
  • synonym: self-propulsion - the ability to move using one's own power
