seatback

noun
back of a seat
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtbæk / (AmE), / ˈsiːtbæk / (BrE)
the back part of a seat that supports your back, especially in vehicles or airplanes (sometimes with a pocket for storage)

Examples

The seatback pocket
The tray table folded down from the seatback in front of her on the plane.

There was a magazine pocket attached to the seatback in the theater.
She placed her water bottle in the seatback pocket before the flight began.
The safety instructions were stored in the seatback in front of each passenger.
He reclined the seatback to rest during the long bus ride.
