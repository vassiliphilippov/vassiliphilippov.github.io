sanctioner

noun
approver or penalizer
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋkʃənər / (AmE), / ˈsaŋkʃənə / (BrE)
a person or authority that gives official approval or imposes a penalty

Examples

Sanctioner of penalties
The city council acted as the sanctioner of the new environmental regulations.
As the official sanctioner, she had the authority to approve or reject the proposal.
The sanctioner imposed strict penalties on companies that violated the rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approver - a person who gives approval
  • synonym: punisher - a person who imposes a penalty
