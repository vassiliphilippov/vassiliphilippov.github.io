orecchiette

noun
ear-shaped pasta
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɔːˌrɛkiˈɛteɪ / (AmE), / ɒˌrɛkiˈɛteɪ / (BrE)
a small, round Italian pasta shaped like little ears, traditionally made from durum wheat

In Puglia, orecchiette is often paired with broccoli rabe and anchovies.
The chef demonstrated how to shape orecchiette by hand from fresh dough.
