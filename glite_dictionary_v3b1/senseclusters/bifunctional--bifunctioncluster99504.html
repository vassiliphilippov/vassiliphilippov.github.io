bifunctional

adjective
two functions
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (BrE)

Examples

Bifunctional tool

Scientists developed a bifunctional catalyst that speeds up two different chemical reactions.
This bifunctional tool works as both a screwdriver and a wrench.
