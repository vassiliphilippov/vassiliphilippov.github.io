bifunctional

adjective
two functions
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (BrE)
having two distinct functions or uses

Examples

Bifunctional tool
The bifunctional protein can both repair DNA and regulate cell growth.
Scientists developed a bifunctional catalyst that speeds up two different chemical reactions.
This bifunctional tool works as both a screwdriver and a wrench.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: monofunctional - having only one function or use
  • degree higher: multifunctional - having many functions or uses
