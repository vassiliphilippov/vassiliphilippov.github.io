flunk

verb
fail to pass a test or class
3.8
Pronunciation: / flʌŋk / (AmE), / flʌŋk / (BrE)
to fail to achieve a passing grade in an examination or course

Examples

To flunk a test
He didn't study for the test and ended up flunking it.
If you flunk the exam, you might have to repeat the class.
She was so worried she would flunk chemistry, but she passed in the end.
