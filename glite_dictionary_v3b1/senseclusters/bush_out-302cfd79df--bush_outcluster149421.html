bush out

verb
grow outward with many branches
5.4 3.6
Pronunciation: /bʊʃ aʊt/ (AmE), /bʊʃ aʊt/ (BrE)
(of a plant) to grow outward and develop many branches, giving a fuller appearance.

Examples

To bush out branches
If you want your rosemary to bush out, pinch off the top shoots regularly.
The shrubs in the garden began to bush out after heavy spring rains.
These tomato plants will bush out if they get enough sun and nutrients.

Related Terms
