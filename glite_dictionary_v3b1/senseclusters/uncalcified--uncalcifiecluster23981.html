uncalcified

adjective
not hardened by calcium
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkælsɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkælsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not hardened or stiffened by the deposit of calcium salts

Examples

The uncalcified cartilage
The doctor noted that the cartilage was still uncalcified in the young patient.
Scientists studied the uncalcified shells to understand the effects of ocean acidification.
After the injury, the bone remained uncalcified for several weeks.
