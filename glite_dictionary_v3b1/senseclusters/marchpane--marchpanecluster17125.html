marchpane

noun
almond paste confection
9.2 9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːrtʃ.peɪn/ (AmE), /ˈmɑːtʃ.peɪn/ (BrE)
A sweet confection made of almond paste and sugar, sometimes with egg whites, popular in historical English cooking.
