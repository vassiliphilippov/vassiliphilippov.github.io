chemical engineering

noun
engineering of industrial chemical processes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˌɛnʤəˈnɪrɪŋ /; / ˌkɛmɪkəl ɛn.dʒəˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˌɛnʤɪˈnɪərɪŋ /; / ˌkɛmɪkəl ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the branch of engineering that designs and operates industrial processes and machinery for making chemicals and other products, using chemistry to solve practical problems
