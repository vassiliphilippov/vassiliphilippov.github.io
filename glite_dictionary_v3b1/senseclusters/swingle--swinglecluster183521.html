swingle

noun
harness crossbar
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
a wooden crossbar in a harness that connects and balances the traces of draught animals, also called a singletree

Examples

Wagon swingle bar
The blacksmith crafted a new swingle for the farmer's wagon.
Without a sturdy swingle, the harness would not balance properly between the two horses.
He checked the swingle before setting out to plow the field.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: doubletree - a bar used to connect two singletrees for a team of animals
  • synonym: singletree - a crossbar in a harness for draught animals
