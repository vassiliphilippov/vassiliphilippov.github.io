metallization

noun
metal deposit process
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the natural geological process by which metals are deposited in rocks, forming ore bodies

Examples

Natural metallization in rocks
Geologists studied the ancient metallization that created rich copper veins in the mountain.
The process of metallization is essential for the formation of valuable ore deposits.
Understanding metallization helps mining companies locate new sources of metals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mineralization - the process by which minerals are deposited in rocks
