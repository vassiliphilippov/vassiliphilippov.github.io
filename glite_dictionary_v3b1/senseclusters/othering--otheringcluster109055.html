othering

noun
treating as outsiders
Pronunciation: / ˈʌðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌðərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of perceiving or treating people as fundamentally different, alien, or outside one's own group

Examples

Acts of othering
The documentary explores how othering can lead to prejudice and misunderstanding between communities.
Teachers are encouraged to avoid othering students from different backgrounds in the classroom.
Social media sometimes amplifies othering by highlighting differences rather than commonalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discrimination - unfair treatment of people based on group differences
  • synonym: exclusion - the act of keeping someone out or denying them participation
  • antonym: inclusion - the act of making people part of a group or community
