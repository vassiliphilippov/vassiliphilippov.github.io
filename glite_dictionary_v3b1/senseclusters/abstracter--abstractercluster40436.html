abstracter

noun
person who makes abstracts or summaries
Pronunciation: / æbˈstræktər / (AmE), / əbˈstræktə / (BrE)

Examples

As an abstracter, she reads and summarizes scientific papers for publication.
The abstracter prepared a concise summary of the lengthy legal document.
