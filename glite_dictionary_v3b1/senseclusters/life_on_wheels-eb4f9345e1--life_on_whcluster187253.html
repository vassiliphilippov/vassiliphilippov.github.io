life on wheels

noun
lifestyle of constant travel in vehicles
Pronunciation: / laɪf ɑn wilz / (AmE), / laɪf ɒn wiːlz / (BrE)
a lifestyle characterized by continual travel in a vehicle, such as a van, camper, or RV, rather than living in a fixed home

Examples

Life on wheels lifestyle
After retiring, they decided to embrace life on wheels and explore new places every month.
Many dream of life on wheels, working remotely while traveling in a van.
Social media inspires younger people to try a life on wheels, seeking adventure and freedom.
