ELT

noun
English teaching field
Pronunciation: / iː ɛl tiː / (AmE), / iː ɛl tiː / (BrE)
the field or practice of teaching English to people whose first language is not English

Examples

ELT teacher
She attended a conference on ELT methods for young learners.
Many universities offer degrees in ELT for aspiring teachers.
Recent advances in technology have changed the landscape of ELT worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ESL - English as a Second Language
  • synonym: TESOL - Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages
