portray

verb
represent in art
5.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /pɔːr.ˈtreɪ/ (AmE), /pɔː.ˈtreɪ/ (BrE)
to show or represent someone or something in a work of art, such as a painting, drawing, sculpture, or photograph

Examples

Many ancient sculptures portray gods and heroes from mythology.

The artist was commissioned to portray the king in his ceremonial robes.
