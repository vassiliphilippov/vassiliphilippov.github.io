portray

verb
represent in art
Pronunciation: / pɔˈtreɪ /; / pɔːrˈtreɪ / (AmE), / pɔˈtreɪ /; / pɔːˈtreɪ / (BrE)
to show or represent someone or something in a work of art, such as a painting, drawing, sculpture, or photograph

Examples

To portray a scene in art
Many ancient sculptures portray gods and heroes from mythology.
The artist chose to portray the queen as both regal and approachable.
This mural portrays the struggles and hopes of the local community.
She chose to portray her sister in a soft, natural light.
The artist was commissioned to portray the king in his ceremonial robes.
Throughout his career, the photographer portrayed many famous authors.
