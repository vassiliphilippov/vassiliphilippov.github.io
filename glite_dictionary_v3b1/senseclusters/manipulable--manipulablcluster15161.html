manipulable

adjective
easily managed or influenced
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪpjuləbl̩ / (AmE), / məˈnɪpjʊləbl̩ / (BrE)
easily managed, controlled, or shaped; capable of being influenced or molded

Examples

Easily manipulable objects
Children's opinions can be quite manipulable at a young age.
He found the situation manipulable and quickly gained control over the negotiations.
The material is soft and manipulable, making it ideal for crafting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intractable - hard to manage or control
  • synonym: manageable - able to be managed or controlled without difficulty
  • synonym: tractable - easily managed or controlled
