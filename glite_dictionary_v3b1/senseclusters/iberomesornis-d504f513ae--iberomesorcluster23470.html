Iberomesornis

noun
extinct Cretaceous bird genus from Spain
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪbəroʊmɛˈsɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˌaɪbərəʊmɛˈsɔːnɪs / (BrE)
An extinct genus of small, sparrow-sized bird from the Early Cretaceous period, with a vestigial tail, known from fossils found in Spain and considered one of the most primitive birds.
