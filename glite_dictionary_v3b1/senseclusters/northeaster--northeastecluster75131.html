northeaster

noun
storm or wind from the northeast
6.6
Pronunciation: / nɔrˈθistər / (AmE), / nɔːˈθiːstə / (BrE)
A storm or wind blowing from the northeast, often bringing heavy rain or snow, especially along the eastern coast of North America.

Examples

A northeaster storm
Last winter, a severe northeaster dumped two feet of snow on the city.
Sailors prepared their boats for the approaching northeaster.
The powerful northeaster caused widespread flooding along the coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nor'easter - a storm or wind from the northeast (alternate form)
