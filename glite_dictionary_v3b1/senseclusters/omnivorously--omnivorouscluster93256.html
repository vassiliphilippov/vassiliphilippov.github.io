omnivorously

adverb
indiscriminately; widely
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈnɪvərəsli / (AmE), / ɒmˈnɪvərəsli / (BrE)
in a way that takes in, uses, or consumes many different things without discrimination or limitation

Examples

Read omnivorously every day
She reads omnivorously, devouring books from every genre and subject she can find.
The young artist omnivorously absorbed influences from both classical and modern styles.
He listened omnivorously to music, never limiting himself to one type or era.

Related Terms

  • antonym: selectively - in a way that chooses carefully among options
  • synonym: voraciously - in an eager and hungry manner
