Broca's center

noun
area of the brain controlling speech production
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkəz ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkəz ˈsentə / (BrE)
the part of the brain, usually in the left hemisphere, responsible for the production of speech
