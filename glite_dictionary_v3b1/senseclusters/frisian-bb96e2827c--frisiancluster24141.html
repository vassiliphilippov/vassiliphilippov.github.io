Frisian

noun
native or inhabitant of Friesland or Frisia
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfriʒ.i.ən / (AmE), / ˈfrɪz.i.ən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Friesland or Frisia, a region in the northern Netherlands and northwestern Germany

Examples

The Frisian people have a history dating back over a thousand years.
