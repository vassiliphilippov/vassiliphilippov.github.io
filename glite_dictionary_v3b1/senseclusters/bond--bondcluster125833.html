bond

noun
connection or fastener
2.9 30/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘nd / (AmE), / bÉ’nd / (BrE)
a strong connection or tie that holds things or people together, either physically or emotionally; also, something that restrains or confines

Examples

A strong bond
Glue acts as a bond that holds the pieces of wood together.
The strong bond between the bricks ensures the wall remains stable.
Years of teamwork created a bond between the two colleagues.
Marriage created a strong bond between the two families.
The shared experience of hardship forged a powerful bond among the survivors.
Their close friendship formed a lasting bond that survived many challenges.
Historical accounts often describe escaped slaves breaking their bonds under the cover of darkness.
She felt emotionally trapped, as if invisible bonds kept her from moving forward.
The prisoner struggled against the bond that held his wrists tightly together.
