accordion fold

noun
series of zig-zag parallel folds

Synonyms

  • zig-zag fold
    6.7 0.00/m
    zig-zag fold has sharp alternating creases for patterns or paper maps, while accordion fold is the bellows-like back-and-forth fold.
  • concertina fold
    6.7 0.00/m
    concertina fold folds material back and forth in parallel like musical bellows, while accordion fold refers to the same zigzag pattern.
  • z-fold
    8.8 0.00/m
    z-fold creates a continuous Z-shaped series of panels for brochures or maps, while accordion fold describes a more general bellows-like zigzag.
  • fanfold
    6.0 0.00/m
    fanfold is paper folded in a long stack for continuous printers and feeders, while accordion fold is a general zigzag fold.
