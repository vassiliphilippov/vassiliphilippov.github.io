separation

noun
the act or process of becoming apart
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of moving apart, dividing, or being apart; the state of being separated or disconnected
