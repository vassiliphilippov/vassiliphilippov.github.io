bumpity

noun
state of bumpiness
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmpɪti / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being bumpy, especially referring to a rough or uneven surface or journey

Examples

The bumpity of the road
The bumpity of the old country road made the ride feel like an adventure.
After the earthquake, the bumpity of the playground surface was obvious to everyone.
We laughed at the bumpity of the roller coaster as it rattled along the tracks.
