violation

noun
disrespectful or unlawful act
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌvaɪ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of breaking or disregarding a law, rule, agreement, or someone's rights

Examples

Violation of rights
She was upset by the violation of her privacy when her diary was read without permission.
Such a violation of trust can permanently damage a friendship.
The athlete's outburst was viewed as a serious violation of the team's code of conduct.
Any violation of the contract may result in legal action.
She reported a violation of her privacy after the incident.
The protesters argued that the law was a violation of their freedom of speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breach - an act of breaking a law or rule
  • antonym: compliance - the act of obeying a law or rule
  • synonym: infringement - the act of breaking a law, agreement, or right
  • antonym: respect - polite regard for someone or something
