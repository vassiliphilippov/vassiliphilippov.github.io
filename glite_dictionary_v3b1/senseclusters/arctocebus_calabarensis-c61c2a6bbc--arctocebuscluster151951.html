Arctocebus calabarensis

noun
species of nocturnal African primate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑrkˈtoʊsɛbəs kəˌlɑbəˈrɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ɑːkˈtəʊsɪbəs kəˌlɑːbəˈrensɪs / (BrE)
