concealment

noun
means of hiding or sheltering
7.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈsiːlmənt / (AmE), / kənˈsiːlmənt / (BrE)
something that covers or shelters and thus hides or protects another thing from view; a means of hiding.

Examples

Natural concealment
Hunters waited quietly behind natural concealment provided by fallen logs.
In nature, many animals rely on color patterns as a form of concealment from predators.
The army unit relied on thick bushes for concealment during the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover - something that covers and protects another thing
  • synonym: shelter - something that provides protection or refuge
