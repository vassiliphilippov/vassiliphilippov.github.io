seasonable

adjective
appropriate for the season
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənəbl / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənəbl / (BrE)
appropriate for or typical of a particular season; suitable or timely for the season

Examples

Seasonable weather
A seasonable frost covered the fields early in the morning.
She wore seasonable clothing to stay comfortable during the chilly autumn day.
The chef prepared seasonable dishes using fresh summer vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timely - happening at the right or suitable time; appropriate
  • antonym: unseasonable - not usual or appropriate for the season
