data leak

noun
unauthorized disclosure of sensitive information
4.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààde…™.t…ô lik / (AmE), / Ààde…™.t…ô liÀêk / (BrE)
an incident in which confidential or sensitive information is disclosed to unauthorized parties, often unintentionally or due to a security failure

Examples

A data leak incident
Due to a data leak, the company had to notify thousands of affected users.
Implementing stronger security protocols can help prevent a data leak in the future.
The investigation revealed that a data leak had exposed financial information to the public.
