Tambocor

noun
antiarrhythmic heart medication (brand name)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtæm.bəʊ.kɔːr/ /; / /ˈtæm.boʊ.kɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈtæm.bəʊ.kɔː/ (BrE)
A trademarked oral antiarrhythmic medication used to treat abnormal heart rhythms, typically as a last resort due to increased risk of sudden death in heart attack patients.

Examples

Tambocor medication
After his heart attack, he was advised not to take Tambocor because of the associated risks.
Patients taking Tambocor require regular monitoring due to the potential for severe side effects.
The doctor prescribed Tambocor to control her irregular heartbeat when other medications failed.
