nonanswer

noun
irrelevant reply
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌænsər / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌɑːnsə / (BrE)
a reply or response that does not address the question or issue asked

Examples

A nonanswer reply
When asked about the budget cuts, the spokesperson gave a typical nonanswer that avoided the main issue.
Her nonanswer frustrated the interviewer, who was looking for a clear explanation.
The student's nonanswer showed he had not prepared for the question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evasion - an act of avoiding a direct answer or responsibility
  • degree higher: response - an answer or reply to a question or situation
