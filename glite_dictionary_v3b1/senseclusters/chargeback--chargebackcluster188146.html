chargeback

noun
internal cost allocation
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑːrdʒ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɑːdʒ.bæk/ (BrE)
a system in which costs or expenses are billed to the department or unit that used a service or resource within an organization

Examples

Chargeback system
The finance team introduced a chargeback system to track IT expenses by department.
Each division received a monthly chargeback for the resources they used.
Implementing chargebacks helped the company control unnecessary spending on shared services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cost allocation - the process of assigning costs to different departments or units
  • synonym: reimbursement - repayment for expenses already paid
