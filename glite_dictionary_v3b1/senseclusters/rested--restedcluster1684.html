rested

adjective
not tired; refreshed
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) not tired; feeling refreshed after sleeping or relaxing.

Examples

Feeling rested after sleep
After a full night's sleep, she felt completely rested and ready for the day.
He came back rested and energized from his vacation.
It is important for athletes to remain rested before a big competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refreshed - feeling energetic or revitalized after rest
  • antonym: tired - feeling in need of sleep or rest
