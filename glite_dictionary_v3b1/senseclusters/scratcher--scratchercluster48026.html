scratcher

noun
lottery ticket to scratch
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃər / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃə / (BrE)
a lottery ticket with a surface that you scratch off to see if you have won a prize

Examples

A lottery scratcher
He bought a scratcher at the gas station and hoped for a big win.
Many people enjoy the excitement of revealing a scratcher to see if they are lucky.
She gave her friend a scratcher as a small birthday gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lottery ticket - a ticket bought for a chance to win a prize in a lottery
  • synonym: scratch card - a card with a surface to scratch off to reveal information or prizes
