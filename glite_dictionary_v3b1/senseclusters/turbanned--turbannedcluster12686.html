turbanned

adjective
wrapped like a turban
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtɜːr.bənd/ (AmE), /ˈtɜː.bənd/ (BrE)
covered, topped, or wrapped with a turban or with cloth arranged in a turban-like way, especially of objects or buildings.

Examples

Turbanned tower
The ancient tower was turbanned with a ring of blue ceramic tiles at its peak.
From a distance, the hill appeared turbanned by a band of mist circling its summit.
The ornate vase was turbanned with a strip of golden fabric around its neck.
