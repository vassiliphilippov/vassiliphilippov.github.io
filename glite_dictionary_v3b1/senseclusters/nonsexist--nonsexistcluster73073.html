nonsexist

adjective
avoiding gender bias
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsɛksɪst /; / nɑnˈsɛksɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsɛksɪst /; / nɒnˈsɛksɪst / (BrE)
not showing gender bias or discrimination; avoiding language or actions that reinforce gender stereotypes

Examples

Nonsexist language
The company adopted a nonsexist policy to ensure fairness in all communications.
Teachers are encouraged to use nonsexist language in the classroom.
She edited the article to make it more nonsexist and inclusive.
The school uses nonsexist language in all its official documents.
They created a nonsexist environment where everyone feels respected regardless of gender.
A nonsexist approach to hiring helps ensure equal opportunities for all applicants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gender-neutral - not specific to any gender
  • antonym: sexist - showing prejudice or discrimination based on gender
  • synonym: unbiased - not showing prejudice for or against something
