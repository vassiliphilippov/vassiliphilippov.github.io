blue bloater

noun
person with chronic bronchitis and oxygen deficiency
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈbloʊtər / (AmE), / bluː ˈbləʊtə / (BrE)
a person, typically with chronic bronchitis, who is overweight and shows bluish skin coloration due to lack of oxygen
