hooter

noun
vehicle warning noise device
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːtər / (AmE), / ˈhuːtə / (BrE)
a device on a vehicle that makes a loud noise to warn or attract attention, especially known as a car horn

Examples

Car hooter sound
Before pulling out, he gave a quick blast on the hooter to warn cyclists.
In Britain, drivers are advised not to use their hooter late at night.
The hooter startled the pedestrians as the car approached the crosswalk.
