lanjiao gesture

noun
Chinese gesture expressing annoyance
Pronunciation: / lænˈdʒjaʊ ˈdʒɛstʃər / (AmE), / lanˈdʒaʊ ˈdʒɛstə / (BrE)
A Chinese slang gesture, typically considered offensive, used to signify annoyance or frustration.
