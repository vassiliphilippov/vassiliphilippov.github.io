Madeira cake

noun
firm sponge cake, often eaten with Madeira wine
Pronunciation: / məˈdɪrə keɪk / (AmE), / məˈdɪərə keɪk / (BrE)
a rich, firm sponge cake with a close texture, traditionally served with a glass of Madeira wine

Examples

A slice of Madeira cake
She served homemade Madeira cake with tea in the afternoon.
The Madeira cake was moist and had a delightful buttery flavor.
Traditional recipes suggest pairing Madeira cake with a glass of sweet wine.
