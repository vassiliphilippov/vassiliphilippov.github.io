mugginess

noun
uncomfortably warm and humid air
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌɡinəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌɡinəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being uncomfortably warm and humid.

Examples

High mugginess
High mugginess can make even moderate temperatures feel oppressive.
I could feel the mugginess as soon as I stepped outside after the rain.
The mugginess made it hard to sleep through the summer night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humidity - the amount of water vapor in the air
