fold

verb
mix gently in cooking (culinary use)
Pronunciation: / foʊld / (AmE), / fəʊld / (BrE)
to gently mix an ingredient, such as whipped egg whites or cream, into another mixture by repeatedly turning it over with a spoon or spatula, without stirring or beating, so as to retain its lightness

Examples

To fold cream
Carefully fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture to keep it light.
He learned how to properly fold the egg whites into the batter without deflating them.
The recipe instructs you to fold the dry ingredients into the wet ones slowly.
