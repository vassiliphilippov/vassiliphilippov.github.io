ghast

noun
frightening ghost
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡæst / (AmE), / ɡɑːst / (BrE)
an apparition or ghost, especially one that is frightening or spectral (archaic)

Examples

A ghast apparition
The villagers whispered about a ghast that wandered the cemetery at midnight.
In the old legend, a ghast appeared to warn travelers of danger ahead.
Some believed the eerie sounds at night were made by a restless ghast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often thought to appear to the living
  • synonym: phantom - a ghostly figure or illusion
  • synonym: spectre - a visible spirit, especially one of a terrifying nature
